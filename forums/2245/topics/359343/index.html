    19-year-old student Celestina Tiew wins FHM Girl Next Door 2009 contest
    By Patwant Singh, Channel NewsAsia |     Posted: 30 May 2009 2329 hrs

    SINGAPORE: The FHM Girl Next Door 2009 contest started with some 300 contestants then short-listed to 100 and finally 10.

    And on Friday night, the winner was selected.

    19-year-old student Celestina Tiew was named the eventual winner at a glitzy affair at Zirca.

    The girls strutted their stuff much to the delight of the shutterbugs.

    The annual contest started some six months ago with readers and fans voting for their choice.

    Celestina walked away with S$800 cash and will be featured in the FHM July issue cover shoot.

    As part of the campaign promise, the winner will also be washing cars in her lingerie at Orchard Road. - CNA/vm
