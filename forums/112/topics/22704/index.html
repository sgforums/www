    Fourteen year-old Welsh Schoolboy Chaz Davies will compete in this year's 125cc World Motorcycle Grand Prix Championships. Davies, who celebrates his 15th birthday next month, will be pitted against the best riders in the world in the 16-round world title chase that starts at Suzuka, Japan in April.

    Davies, who will ride an Aprilia machine for the Italian Matteoni racing team, has been granted special permission by his school to miss lessons to compete in motorcycle racing's premier World Championship that visits four separate continents between April and November.

    'I've seen the World Championship riders on television and they are very fast but they also ride very competitive bikes,' explained the 14-year-old, who last season gained valuable experience in both the British and Spanish Championships. 'I've been to the Aprilia factory in Italy to see the new bike, which was great. Most of the tracks will be new to me, including the first one at Suzuka in Japan but at least I know which way to go, because it's on my Playstation.'
