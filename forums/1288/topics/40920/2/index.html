Consider also how you know the people your see in your dreams and whether they not acting like themselves.

    Faceless:
    -- To see a faceless figure or person in your dream, indicates that you are still searching for your own identity and finding out who you are. Perhaps you are unsure of how to read people and their emotions. And therefore are expressing a desire to know and understand these people in a deeper level.

    Being Stab:
    -- To dream that you have been stabbed, signifies your struggle with power. You may be experiencing feelings of inadequacy and defensiveness. Alternatively, you may be feeling betrayed as the popular phrase goes, "being stabbed in the back".

    Fighting:
    -- It usually symbolizes anger and confusion that comes about in times of change. If nothing is changing in your life, it may be a clue that a change is needed or that you want to change internally. Dreaming about fighting reflects the difficulties you have in balancing your conflicting personality traits. If you are watching a fight: you could help other people with your conciliatory words to diffuse the tension between them. But however, for yourself, to see others fighting in your dream, suggests that you are unwilling to acknowledge your own problems and turmoil. You are not taking any responsibility or initiative in trying to resolve issues in your waking life.

    Stabbed Someone:
    -- To dream that you stab someone, indicates your fear of betrayal and your untrusting nature. You may be too much on the defensive.

    Blood:
    -- Seeing no blood indicates that situations in life hasn't been solved. The worst is yet to come.
