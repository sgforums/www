    Tell you more about the facts of nightmares... Why do people sometimes have nightmares??

    A nightmare is a disturbing dream that causes the dreamer to wake up feeling anxious and frightened. Nightmares may a response to real like trauma and situations. These type of nightmares fall under a special category called Post-Traumatic Stress Nightmares (PSN).

    Nightmares may also occur becos we have ignored or refused to accept a particular life situation. Research shows that most people who had regular nightmares have had a family history of psychiatric problems, bad drug experiences, people who have contemplated suicide, and/or rocky relationships. Nightmares are indication of fear that need to be acknowledged and confronted. It is a way for our sub-conscious to make up take notice.

    To get rid of recurring nightmares requires understanding of your problems and resolving what causes this tension.

Some believe that when you see blood in your dream, the distressing situation in your life which is at the root of the dream has come to an end, and the worst is over. Consider the details and the relationships between of all the symbols in your dream before making an interpretation.
