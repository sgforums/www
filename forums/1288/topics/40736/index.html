    Hi *evanesence*,

    Many people at some point in their lives dream about their teeth falling out.

    This type of dream denotes serious troubles for your children or relations, as your teeth are said to represent friends and relatives.

    As with all dreams the situation will denote a truer meaning such as the taking on of responsibility or the fear of waning power.

    Look and see if you are losing or abusing power and control in any area of your life (especially if you are losing teeth in your dream).

    Locked Doors:
    To dream that of locked doors, signifies opportunities that are denied and not available to you or that you have missed out on.

    Shut Windows:
    To see shut windows in your dream, signifies desertion and abandonment.

    Some dreams, however, are simply scary. A frightening dream is called a Nightmare. Severe anxiety dreams, where you wake up in a cold sweat feeling very afraid, occur because what is happening in the dream is too overwhelming to be contained while asleep. True nightmares occur in the non-dream phase of sleep, and have little remembered content.

    Causes of nightmares -

    1) Childhood and Family
    2) Life
    3) Relationships
    4) Stress
    5) Work

    Cheers!
