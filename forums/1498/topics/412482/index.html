    SINGAPORE: Some 20 people were evacuated from Orchard Towers after a fire broke out early Wednesday morning.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force said it received a call about the fire at a pub on the ground floor of the building at about 5.40am.

    The management of the building evacuated people from the first four floors of the building as a precaution.

    The fire has since been put out.

    SCDF said the cause of the fire is under investigation.
