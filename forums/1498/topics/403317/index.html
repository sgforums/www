    SINGAPORE: The body of a Malay man believed to be in his 20s was recovered from the Singapore River Thursday morning.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force received a call about a man falling into the river near Coleman Bridge at about 7am this morning.

    A search was conducted by divers from SCDF's Disaster Assistance and Rescue Team.

    The body was found about 45 minutes later.

    Paramedics pronounced the man dead at the scene.
