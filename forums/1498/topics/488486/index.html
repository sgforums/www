    Fire breaks out in Clementi flat, no one injured

    SINGAPORE- A fire broke out in the master bedroom of a flat in Blk 376, Clementi Ave 4 on Sunday.

    No one was injured. The Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) said that the residents of the unit on the ninth floor evacuated themselves. About 50 other people from the block were also evacuated. The incident happened at about 1.20pm.

    The SCDF put out the fire using one water jet.
