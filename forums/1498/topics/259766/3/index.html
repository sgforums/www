    Originally posted by sockjordan:
    Juf! looking forward to the uniform!! whats BRTC stands for? How long is it? whats the usual routine from first day to posting of vocation ands how longs is the period before we go to our posted vocation? Can we select our vocation?? than whats the duration of the on and off shift?
    BRTC = Basic Rescue Training Centre whereby you'll be there for around 2-3 months for your recruit period...first day nothing much actually, just the normal admin stuff (e.g issuing of uniforms, stuffs, haircut, etc)

    Vocation can be selected but however, the final choice still lies with the upper personnel & don't place hopes to high as you might not get into the desired vocation that you'll want so play safe by choosing another as a stand-by...the duration for the posting to your confirmed vocation as well as the duration for the on & off shift depends really on which vocation you'll get but rest assured they'll inform you once you're in the confirmed vocation so don't worry
