    Small fire at Battery Rd office building forces tenants to evacuate

    SINGAPORE: A small fire broke out at No.6, Battery Road late Friday morning, forcing all tenants to evacuate.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) said it received a call at about 11.40am.

    The fire, which originated from a storeroom located at the office building's Basement 3, was put out in five minutes using an internal fire hose reel.

    A 44-year-old woman, who complained of discomfort, was sent to the Singapore General Hospital for smoke inhalation.

    Tenants were let back into the building about an hour after the fire.
