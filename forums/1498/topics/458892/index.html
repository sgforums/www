SCDF Operational Excercise at the F1 Race Circuit

  • QX179R
    19 Sep 12, 20:37

    SCDF will be conducting an operational exercise at the F1 Race Circuit area on Wednesday, 19 Sept 2012.  The exercise will begin at 9.30pm and end at about 12am.

    Such exercises are part of SCDF’s on-going efforts to validate and update operational plans and procedures.  In total, about 170 SCDF personnel and some 25 emergency and supporting vehicles will be participating in this exercise.

    As the exercise will be conducted within the confines of the F1 race circuit area, there will not be any road lane closures or traffic disruptions.
