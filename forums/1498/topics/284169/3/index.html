  • dumbdumb!
    7 Apr 08, 00:15
    Originally posted by sir_peanuts:

    packing… besides the list given to us and some additional advice from other threads, such as white rubber tape, key lock, permanent marker, what else should i bring?

    how will the clothes arrangement be like?

    also, are training hours from morning to night? thanks…

    PTP stage? quite slack la.

    morning wake up around 5? do morning exercises. that one quite sian, because i'm not a morning person, then if everyone don't count together, start from 0 again. lol.

    then breakfast, food usually quite tasteless and bland, the best is bread and hotdog and hard boiled eggs with jam and butter. the nasi lemak sometimes can get diarrhoea.

    then after breakfast is, area cleaning, last time old camp alot of leaves, then must sweep them up. now the new CDA should be ok.

    the first 2 days alot of talks at the air-conditioned hall. talk about suicide, if you feel troubled, who to talk to, why is NS important etc. given by various company's OC.

    then will have various exercises throughout the day. sometimes gym, conditioning training, stamina training, and reflexes training - psychomotor training. you will also have footdrill lessons

    then lunch. the first day the lunch confirm is nasi bryani. then for the rest of your NS life is mix vege rice la etc.

    then after lunch, can be area cleaning abit. SRB will be the one cleaning the sink and emptying the rubbish bins (you lucky bastards), so please don't mess up the area..

    after that can be rest abit, polish boots whatever..

    then resume exercises - should be gym la.

    then dinner - again, mix vege rice.

    then rest - watch everyone else book out. and you spend the time, washing clothes, resting, doing watever you want. 10pm, lights out.

     

    anyone can re-confirm? i am not sure if i remember correctly
