    Wow, you're in pes A then you sure kena all those "fit" type of vocations like fire fighting for example & you won't get to dig rubbles of hotel new world coz there's none to start with...

    During recruit time, depends on which company you are in so you really can't say that discipline is lax & just relax, go with the flow & remember if you expect others to be friendly/respect you then you must take the first step...

    Regarding booking out times, for SCDF the good thing is that there's no confinement so you'll most likely book in on a Tuesday & book out on the Friday of the same week...

    As for what is learnt during BRT stage is all the basic rescue techniques as well as the basic first aid & CPR...

    Hope the above mentioned will clear some of your doubts & do feel free to ask anything & I'll try my very best to get them answered
