Fire at Blk 612 Ang Mo Kio Ave 4, Level 2 unit

  • QX179R
    31 May 12, 08:41

    SINGAPORE: Two elderly women were rescued after fire broke out in a flat at Block 612 Ang Mo Kio Avenue 4 on Wednesday afternoon.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force said they received a call just before 5pm and reached the site within five minutes.

    They forced their way into the four-room flat and extinguished the fire in the kitchen.

    SCDF said one of the women was found crouching in the corner of one bedroom.

    The other was found in another bedroom nearest to the kitchen.

    Both were conscious and brought to hospital for smoke inhalation.

    Thirty people were evacuated from the 3rd, 4th and 5th levels of the block as a precautionary measure.

    It's not known what caused the fire.
