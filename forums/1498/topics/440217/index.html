SCDF to hold drills at Century Square Mall

  • QX179R
    23 Oct 11, 16:07

    SINGAPORE: The Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) will be conducting a ground deployment exercise at Century Square Mall in Tampines on Tuesday morning, from 1am to 4am.

    SCDF said such exercises are part of its efforts to validate and update operational plans and procedures.

    About 150 personnel and 20 emergency and supporting vehicles will be participating in this exercise.

    The SCDF vehicles involved will have their beacon lights turned on to alert motorists.

    Signs will also be placed at prominent locations to keep the public informed about the exercise.

    The public is advised not to be alarmed.
