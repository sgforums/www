Fire at Blk 52 Toa Payoh

  • QX179R
    6 May 12, 21:15

    SINGAPORE: Another fire has broken out on Sunday and this time at Blk 52 Toa Payoh Lorong 5, the second one this week involving HDB flats.

    A unit on the eighth floor was damaged in the fire. The fire has been put out.

    A hotline caller, who is a resident at the block, told Channel NewsAsia that explosions were heard.

    He added that the block residents ran downstairs after hearing the explosions.

    SINGAPORE: A fire broke out at Blk 52 Toa Payoh Lorong 6 on Sunday evening, the second case this week involving discarded items at a lift landing.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force was alerted to the fire at 7.45pm.

    The incident took place at the lift lobby on the eighth floor of the block.

    There were bicycles at the lift landing too.

    A hotline caller, who is a resident at the block, told Channel NewsAsia that explosions were heard.

    SCDF said the fire was put out within fifteen minutes.

    There were no casualties.
