    haha once u break through the first pull up, it'll become really easy to increase.

    as for standing broad jump, my pti friend taught me this method. u dun rest on your entire foot. but u rest on the front part of the foot. the heel u raise abit.

    u dun need to keep swinging ur arms and try to get momentum. then u imagine there is a big hole in front of you, and you need to jump across it, and u look far away at the end of the mat.

    then this is the most impt part, u know u swing ur arms right, when u swing them up, ur knees are bent and u're straightening ur knees as if u're gonna jump.

    so when u swing down, u bend ur knees down, while still resting on the front part of your feet,

    u swing a few times,

    then the final swing, when ur arms swing up till its parallel to the ground, u jump, while ur arms continue to swing up. cannot hesitate.

    confirm can get silver.
