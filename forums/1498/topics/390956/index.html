    A151 is now using QX451D.

     

    Few points to note on new Alpha:

     

    1-Top front cover no longer has "Ambulance Lighted Sign" . But has blue Paramedic instead.

     

    2- Red cheatlines along top side body gone & red cheatline across engine to side door windows also gone

     

    3- No blue Paramedic logos at back doors

     

    4- New LED Red & Blue lights now on top side body!!!
