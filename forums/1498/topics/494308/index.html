Chemical Leak Incident At 166 Kallang Way

  • QX179R
    18 Jul 17, 20:47

    11 people taken to hospital after Kallang Way chemical leak

    SCDF was alerted to the incident at about 1.40pm.

    2 fire engines, 1 fire bike, 3 support vehicles and 2 ambulances were despatched to a chemical leak incident.

    The leak was shut off prior to SCDF arrival by the in-built safety system on the premises.

    9 workers from the production room were conveyed to Tan Tock Seng Hospital by the SCDF in a conscious and stable condition.

    SCDF HazMat detectors currently show no reading of the chemical vapour in the production room. The surrounding environment is also safe with no traces of the chemical in the air.

    Update: Two more workers from the production room were conveyed to Tan Tock Seng Hospital in a conscious and stable condition.
