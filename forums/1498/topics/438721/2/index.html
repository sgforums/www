    Sunday, 2 October 2011
    Fire at 1, Pulau Bukom: Update 9

    As of 1145 hrs this morning, 16 SCDF Vehicles (5 Fire Engines, 1 Red Rhino and 10 Support Vehicles) and about 50 SCDF personnel returned to the mainland. 18 SCDF Vehicles and about 50 SCDF personnel remain on the island to support Shell.

    There are still traces of fuel vapour at the affected area.

    Fire investigation is ongoing.

    Wedneday, 5 October 2011
    Fire at 1, Pulau Bukom: Update 10

    SCDF has withdrawn all their remaining operational resources and personnel from Pulau Bukom, as SCDF’s assistance and support operations on Pulau Bukom are no longer required.

    As at 2150 hrs, all of SCDF remaining operational resources and personnel have returned to the mainland.

    Fire investigation is still ongoing.

    This is the final update to the incident which occurred on Pulau Bukom, on Wednesday, 28 September at 1318 hrs.
