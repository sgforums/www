Off-duty SCDF personnel helps injured motorcyclist

  • QX179R
    9 Mar 11, 13:56

    THE husband of a Stomp reader named LydiaZara attended to an injured motorcyclist involved in an accident on Bedok Reservoir Road last Sunday.

    The SCDF regular revived the man and helped him maintain consciousness till the ambulance arrived.

    'My husband and I were driving back when we saw a bike being flung out of nowhere right in front of our eyes after a collision with another car.

    'He quickly stopped and attended to one of the riders who was more seriously injured,' she said.

    'Some passers-by suggested to remove the victim's helmet and carry him off the road.'

    LydiaZara said that her husband told them that in doing so it would only injure the victim more.

    'He managed to revive the victim and gain his attention till the ambulance arrived.'

    -- ST
