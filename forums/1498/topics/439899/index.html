Fire Incident at Tampines Street 93

  • QX179R
    18 Oct 11, 19:02

    Monday, 17 October 2011
    Fire at Tampines Street 93

    At around 12.31pm, The SCDF was alerted to a fire incident at Block 9002 inside Tampines Industrial Estate at Tampines Street 93.

    One fire engine, one Red Rhino, two fire bikes and three support vehicles were then dispatched to the scene.

    Upon arrival, personnel from the SCDF proceeded to the switch room at the first level where the fire occurred.

    The fire was then extinguished using a 2.5kg dry-powder extinguisher.

    The cause of fire is still under investigation.
