Rescue incident at 80 Raffles Place

  • QX179R
    21 Aug 12, 20:37

    Civil defence personnel were involved in rescuing two men who were trapped in a gondola at Raffles Place on Tuesday.

    At about 5pm, the Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) received a call from a member of the public, who said that a gondola was stuck midway at a building at 80 Raffles Place.

    Upon SCDF's arrival, they found a gondola stalled on the 8th floor of the building's exterior. Two men were in the gondola.

    SCDF rescuers first secured the two men with safety lines. A ladder was then extended from a window on the 8th floor into the gondola.
