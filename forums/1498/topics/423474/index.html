SCDF operational exercise at Causeway Point

  • QX179R
    22 Feb 11, 17:10

    THE Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) will be conducting an operational exercise at Causeway Point on Wednesday (Feb 23).

    The exercise will begin at 1.30am and end at 4am.

    The exercise area will also include the Woodlands Regional Bus Interchange and the Woodlands MRT station.

    These exercises are part of SCDF's ongoing efforts to validate and update operational plans and procedures. In total, about 100 personnel and about 30 emergency and support vehicles will be participating in this exercise.

    All SCDF vehicles participating in this exercise will have their beacon lights turned on to alert motorists. Exercise signages will also be placed at prominent locations to keep members of the public informed of the exercise.

    The SCDF has advised the public not to be alarmed.

    -- ST
