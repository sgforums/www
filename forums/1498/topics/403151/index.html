2 men trapped in accident between two lorries

  • QX179R
    22 Jun 10, 13:49

    SINGAPORE: Two men were trapped in a head-on collision between two lorries on the Bukit Timah Expressway Tuesday morning.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force said they received a call about the accident about 9.45 am.

    The two men were freed and sent to hospital.
