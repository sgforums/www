Fire at Rowell Road pre-war shophouse

  • QX179R
    12 Jul 08, 21:01

    A midday blaze on the second storey
    of a pre-war shophouse at Rowell
    Road on Friday caused traffic along
    Serangoon Road to slow to a crawl for about 30 minutes.

    Passers-by helped three Indian men
    in the unit escape using a three-step
    ladder before fire-fighters arrived at the
    scene. The trio were unhurt.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force
    received a call at 12.39pm and sent two
    fire engines, two fire-bikes and two
    ambulances to the conserved building
    within eight minutes.

    The blaze was put out within
    10 minutes, but by then, the living and
    bedroom of the unit were completely
    destroyed.

    The authorities are investigating the cause of the
    fire. - TODAY
