    Cleaner hit by taxi at Budget Terminal, dies

    Published on Mar 17, 2012

     

    A 34-year-old man died on Saturday morning after he was hit by a taxi at the driveway in front of the Budget Terminal. The Indian man, who was a cleaner on duty at the airport, was pronounced dead at the scene by the paramedics at 7.13am.

    At about 6.50am, the police were informed of an assault by a passenger on a 57-year-old taxi driver along the stretch of Airport Boulevard in front of SATS In-flight Catering Centre 1.

    According to a statement released by Comfort Delgro, the passenger who was headed for Changi Airport started getting violent towards the taxi driver during the journey. The taxi driver later activated the distress button and got out of the cab. Police said that the passenger was later seen re-entering the taxi and driving towards the direction of the Budget Terminal.

    The passenger who drove the taxi is a 30-year-old Chinese National. He was detained at the scene.

    A Citycab taxi slammed into the entrance next to MacDonald's at Budget Terminal and killed a cleaner on Saturday, March 17. -- ST PHOTO: WONG KWAI CHOW

    Chinese national in Budget Terminal tragedy faces 3 new traffic charges

    Published on Mar 23, 2012

    A Chinese national facing a robbery with hurt charge was charged on Friday with causing the death of a cleaning supervisor through dangerous driving at Budget Terminal.

    Yuan Zhenghua, 30, a technician, was charged last Sunday with punching cabby Yeow Chuwee Lam on the back and strangling the 57-year-old while robbing him of the Citicab taxi at Airport Boulevard at about 6.50am last Saturday.

    The three fresh charges state that he drove dangerously by causing the taxi to veer from the road onto the pavement at a fast speed before hitting cleaning supervisor Chandra Mogan Panjanathan, 34, and a traffic light pole.

    The incident resulted in the death of the Malaysian father of four, who was operating a cleaning machine in front of Budget Terminal departure hall at the time.
