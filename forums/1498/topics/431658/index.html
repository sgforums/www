Boy falls into large storm drain at Balestier area

  • QX179R
    1 Jun 11, 20:39

    SCDF rescuers were spotted combing this large storm drain near Mandalay Road after a boy was reported to have fallen into the water there.

    A spokesperson for the SCDF told STOMP at 7.30pm on June 1:

    "SCDF received the call at 4pm.

    "A boy fell into the drain near Mandalay Road and Minbu Road.

    "SCDF personnel are conducting a search following the drain towards Towner Road.

    "The rescue operation is currently ongoing."

    STOMPer Mark G, who was at the scene of the accident, wrote:

    "At approximately 5pm today, there was a team of SCDF officers waist deep in this large drain.

    "I overheard from the people gathered there that someone had fallen into the river."
