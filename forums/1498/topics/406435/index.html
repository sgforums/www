SCDF operational exercise

  • QX179R
    27 Jul 10, 11:08

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force, SCDF, will be conducting an operational exercise at the Marina Bay Floating Platform early tomorrow morning.

    It says the exercise is part of its on-going efforts to validate and update operational plans and procedures. 

    The Police and Health Ministry will also be involved in the exercise. 

    Some 500 personnel and about 50 emergency and supporting vehicles will be participating in this exercise. 

    SCDF says up to 3 lanes along Raffles Avenue between the junction of Temasek Avenue and Raffles Link would be closed between 1am and 4am tomorrow. 

    The public is advised not be alarmed.
