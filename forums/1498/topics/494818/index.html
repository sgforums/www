    Four sent to hospital after sofas at condo void deck catch fire

    SINGAPORE - A fire broke out at the void deck of a condominium on Saturday (Oct 28) afternoon, resulting in four people being taken to Tan Tock Seng Hospital.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) said it received a call about the fire at Bartley Ridge, a condominium at 42 Mount Vernon Rd, at 1.10pm.

    The blaze, which involved several sofas that were at the void deck, was extinguished by the SCDF with two water jets.

    "Due to the fire, units at the second floor sustained smoke and heat damage," said an SCDF spokesman.

    SCDF is investigating the cause of fire.
