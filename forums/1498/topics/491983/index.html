    Never mind,for those pre enlistees interested in transfering from scdf to spf to saf, found out the only thing that can be done is to sign on. Do so at least 4-5 months before your possible enlistment date , or you would have to still serve bmt while they process your application before they can choose to absorb you to saf, in which case you join the next closes intake and serve saf bmt.
