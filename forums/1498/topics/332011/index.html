    SCDF vehicle in Bedok accident

    Guess who came to this Singapore Civil Defence Force's vehicle's rescue after it was involved in an accident? Why, another SCDF vehicle, of course!

    Isabelle told STOMP:

    "I saw this scene at around 11am on 19th Sept.
     
    "This happened around 10am, when I was teaching tuition.
     
    "After tuition, I was walking from Bedok North St 3 to interchange when I saw this.
     
    "The Civil Defence helps us to get out of traffic accidents. Who helps them then? Their own people.
     
    "Another SCDF truck was dispatched to the scene to remove the bags from the truck.
     
    "Think they must be going too fast and when doing a left turn, they hit the sign flat."
