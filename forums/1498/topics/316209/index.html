    1) Yes, there's no confinement (book in on a Tues & book out on the Fri)

     

    2) For friday prayers, during my intake they did provide a shuttle bus for those who wishes to go for their Fri prayers but I'm not sure if they still have it now.

     

    3) For your specs frame, its either black or blue with no fanciful type of designs or whatsoever & yes, it will definately affect your chances of getting a vocation of a fire fighter.

     

    4) A very rewarding experience is all that I can say.

     

    5) Do whatever you want but don't get caught..
