    Wisma Atria shopping mall evacuated after 'minor fire'

    SINGAPORE: Wisma Atria shopping mall was evacuated on Monday (Oct 17) afternoon, after a small fire broke out at a shop.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) said in a Facebook post that it was alerted to a fire at about 5.20pm, at a shop on the second floor. In an update at 6.30pm, SCDF said the fire has been put out.  

    The "minor fire" was confined to a small area of the shop, said SCDF, adding that the building was evacuated as a precautionary measure. Shoppers said on social media that entrances to the mall, including underpasses, were also closed.

    SCDF said the cause of the fire is being investigated.
