  • surferpunk
    21 Apr 07, 20:55
    Originally posted by joeacid:
    Hmm... what are the Do's and Don'ts?? The Must bring and Mustn't bring things?
    Let me tell u from my experience. Cos I just entered 2 weeks ago.

    First day, better to cut real short. No 1 will be best a few days before.(The shortest possible) This is because if the barber think it's long enough, he will just ask for $3 and cut a bit a bit. And if you cut beforehand, at least your hair will be handled properly. Cos for my hair, some are longer than others due to their incompetence. That's all about hair. But don't worry. After your 7 weeks of training, you are allowed to grow some hair. But not too long la.

    Bring photocopy version of your bank book, highest cert and perhaps NRIC. That's what I did and I'm safe. And remember to ask your Next-Of-Kin(one of your parents or your guardian) his/her IC number, phone number. You gonna need it to fill up forms. Also, try to remember your bank account number.

    Your first day is super slack. Just fill up forms and other declarations. Wayang for the parents who came. So confirm no tekan on first day. Hair cut for all after taking the pledge. Then stock taking. You will be issued your stuffs and all. Remember not to lose it. Perhaps bring permanent marker and white rubber tape to mark your utensils given to you.

    As for handphones, you can bring them in. But MUST be worth less than $100. Highly recommended due to the high frequency of handphones 'running away'. But some PCs will force you to bring worthless HPs instead to guard against theft. As for camera phones, they will close one eye. But you must be smart enough not to take pics openly.

    And bring key padlock. NEVER bring combi padlock. Many are masters of unlocking them.

    And yeah. Don't bring ciggies, lighters. If you wear specs, make sure it's black and plastic frame.

    A few words of advice. Just listen to their instructions. Follow them closely. Just obey them for 7 weeks and you'll be safe. For bunk inspection, clean really everything and standardise your room(eg, the way you put the mass tray. even the direction of your fork and spoon.) Cos they sometimes psycho la. Purposely find fault in your bunk. As long they find dirt, they happy already and will pump you guys.

    Simple rule to survive. Be quick and fast. NEVER walk, be slow, sleep, be blur. If you're blur, good luck. You will be their main target. Some say CD slack. But it depends on those who is in charge of you. For me, i was from the 'FIT' squad and my platoon was tekan much more than others due to us being the 'FIT' squad which we never ask for anyway. Just be good and they'll be good to you. Anyway, practice drinking 500ml of water. You gonna need that skill for water parade. ERS even worse. 1 litre for water parade.

    Since you're PES C, lucky you. You won't be exposed to much physical torture. Just get through your 7 weeks and it's an easy life for the rest of your 2 years. Make sure you learn fast. If you used to be from a uniformed group, you won't have much problem adapting to CD life. Just be on your guard always and remember to greet everyone else except for you recruits. 1st week basically is orientation. They will somehow make you guys on your tip toes and if they think you're disciplined enough, your 7 weeks will pass off smoothly.
