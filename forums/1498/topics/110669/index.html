    don need to bring too much stuffs as u will be booking out friday evening...

    Things to bring

    1. Perm Marker (Label ur stuffs)
    2. Pen & Pencil
    3. Clothes Hangers (Bring arnd
    4. Toiletries (Soap/Shower Foam, Shampoo etc...)
    5. Toothbrush / Toothpaste
    6. Shaver
    7. Nail Cutter
    8. Slippers
    9. Key Lock
    10. Washing Powder
    11. Alarm Clock
    12. Hp Battries (No charger allowed)
    13. Starch (For ironing ur uniform)
    14. Spectacle Hook / Strap (If u wear glasses)
    15. Underwear (Bring more unless u wanna wash everyday)
    16. Some Cash
    17. Phonecard (Only accept phone cards)
    18. Socks (Bring some long, thick black socks. Only 2 pairs are given)
    19. Medication (Couterpain etc.)
    20. Own clothes (If u need for slping)
    21. Swimming Goggles
    22. Towel (For bathing. The towel provided not very gd.)
