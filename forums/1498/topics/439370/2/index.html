    A body was found in the Bedok Reservoir on Tuesday morning. This is the fifth since June.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF), who responded to a call from the public at 7.11am, said the body was found floating about two metres away from shore when they arrived on the scene.

    A section of the reservoir bank, directly opposite housing block 717, was cordoned off on Monday morning before the body was removed at around 10.30am.

    The body belonged to a Chinese female, an SCDF spokesman added. Police believe the woman to be in her late 50s. They said she was pronounced dead at the scene by paramedics at 7.20am. An eyewitness, Mr Yeo Kin Kiat, 53, said the woman was wearing a red top.
