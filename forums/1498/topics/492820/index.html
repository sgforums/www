Man injured in RTA involving taxi & van

  • QX179R

    RTA involving taxi & van at  junction of North Buona Vista Road & Ayer Rajah Avenue

    SINGAPORE: A man in his 20s suffered minor injuries after a van flipped to its side at the junction of North Buona Vista Road and Ayer Rajah Avenue on Wednesday (Oct 12). 

    According to the Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF), it was informed of the incident at 10.37am and dispatched two fire bikes, two fire engines, a support vehicle and an ambulance to the scene. 

    Upon arrival, SCDF said it assisted the man who was inside a van which was lying on its side. The man who suffered from minor injuries refused to be sent to the hospital, it added. 

    A photograph of the incident posted by Twitter user @rachbass appeared to show that the yellow van had collided with a blue Comfort DelGro taxi.
