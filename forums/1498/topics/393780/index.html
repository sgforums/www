    SINGAPORE: The new Banyan Fire Station at Jurong Island was officially opened by Minister of Law and Second Minister for Home Affairs K Shanmugam on Tuesday.

    Strategically located at the centre of the Jurong Island extension, the fire station manages a fleet of 12 emergency vehicles, including fire engines and Hazardous Materials (HazMat) vehicles, with some 80 personnel who will respond to HazMat as well as fire and rescue incidents round-the-clock.
