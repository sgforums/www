    SINGAPORE: A collision between a trailer and a private bus left 28 people injured and two with head injuries.

    The accident happened at about 8.20 on Friday morning at the junction of Tuas South Ave 3 and Tuas South Ave 5.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) despatched five ambulances, two mini vans and a fire engine to the scene.

    No one was trapped when SCDF officers arrived.

    13 casualties were sent to the National University Hospital and the rest to Alexandra Hospital.
