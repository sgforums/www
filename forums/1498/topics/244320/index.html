SCDF to conduct emergency exercise at Senoko Power Station

  • SMRT Buses
    23 Mar 07, 21:30
    The Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) will be conducting a routine emergency exercise at Senoko Power Station on Monday, 26 March 2007.

    It will be held from 2.30pm to 5.30pm.

    SCDF said roads leading to the Senoko Power Station would not be affected by the exercise.

    But the power station will activate its in-house siren and there will be movement of emergency appliances and equipment in the vicinity.
