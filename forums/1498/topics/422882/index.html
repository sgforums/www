RTA at bus stop along Loyang Avenue

  • QX179R
    15 Feb 11, 14:51

    Three people were trapped in the front cabin after their lorry crashed into a bus stop along Loyang Avenue this morning.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force said the front cabin had flipped and was impacted against a bus stop bollard.

    SCDF rescuers used hydraulic cutters, spreaders and an extension ram to rescue the trapped casualties. 

    The casualties were conveyed to Changi General Hospital.
