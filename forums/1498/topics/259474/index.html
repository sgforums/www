    Fire at wooden factory in Sungei Kadut, no casualties

    SINGAPORE: Fire broke out at a wooden factory in Sungei Kadut on Thursday morning.

    It destroyed some wooden furniture, wooden pallets and cushions in a single-storey zinc structure.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force says the fire, which began at around 10.40am, was contained within 20 minutes.

    All the workers from the factory were evacuated and there were no casualties.

    Investigations into the cause are in progress. - CNA/yy
