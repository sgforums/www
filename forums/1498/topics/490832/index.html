Four people injured after roof of driveway at Hilton Hotel

  • FireIce
    13 Dec 15, 16:42

    Four people were injured after part of the ceiling over the main entrance of the Hilton Hotel collapsed on Sunday (Dec 13) afternoon.

    A passerby, full time tutor Patrick Loh, 64, told The New Paper that he saw a woman, whom he said was a staff member, walking into an ambulance after the collapse.

    He added that another woman, believed to be her colleague, was seen helping her into the vehicle.

    According to the Singapore Civil Defence Force, one man and three women were conscious when they were taken to Tan Tock Seng Hospital.
