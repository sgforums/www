    SCDF officers put out blaze at Specialists Shopping Centre

    SINGAPORE : A fire broke out at the old Specialists Shopping Centre on Orchard Road on Thursday morning.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force officers successfully put out the fire.

    Officers said the blaze was caused by welding work on the seventh floor rooftop where workers were dismantling some metal structures.
