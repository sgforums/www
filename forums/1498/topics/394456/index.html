    SCDF DART team search canal for person believed to have drowned

    SINGAPORE : Officers from the Singapore Civil Defence Force's Disaster Assistance and Rescue Team are combing a canal in Potong Pasir for a person believed to have fallen into the water on Wednesday evening.

    The canal is located next to St Andrew's School.

    SCDF said it received a distress call at about 5pm.

    One fire engine, a rescue vehicle and an ambulance have been deployed to the scene.

    The search is ongoing.
