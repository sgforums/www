    Motorbikes up in flames at Yishun car park

    SINGAPORE: Some Yishun residents woke up to a blazing fire in their backyard early Sunday morning.

    Six motorcycles parked at the car park near block 736, Yishun Street 72, went up in flames.

    The Civil Defence Force deployed a fire engine and a Red Rhino to the scene after receiving a call from the public around 7am.

    The fire was put out within minutes and the SCDF are investigating the cause of the blaze.

    No one was injured in the incident but a total of 14 motorcycles were damaged. - CNA/so
