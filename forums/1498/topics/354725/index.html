SCDF holds open house to show shelter facilities at MRT stns

  • QX179R
    4 Apr 09, 21:06

    SINGAPORE: The Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) conducted a Civil Defence Shelter Open House at three Circle Line MRT stations - Bishan, Lorong Chuan and Bartley - on Saturday.

    This is part of the SCDF's continuous efforts to build up Singapore's civil defence preparedness under the umbrella of total defence.

    The aim is to sustain public awareness on civil defence shelters and emergency preparedness measures.

    The SCDF also hopes to familiarise members of public on the use of shelters during an emergency.

    Mayor for Central Singapore District, Zainudin Nordin, was given a tour of the shelter at Bishan MRT station.

    Mr Zainudin said: "Having such an exercise, opening the places for our residents to see for themselves and understand their role, at the same time knowing there's such a facility, will give Singaporeans more comfort and awareness of what they can do and how they can do it in an emergency."

    Among the facilities available in the shelter was a decontamination chamber to cope with chemical threats. The SCDF also showed that even train carriages can double up as shelter areas.

    An estimated 10,000 visitors had a chance to experience the tour at the stations. The visitors were mostly organised groups from the various constituencies, schools and institutions.
