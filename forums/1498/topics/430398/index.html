Ambulance on 995 call but drivers honk & refuse to give way

  • QX179R
    14 May 11, 21:10

    STOMPer JD was appalled at the lack of patience drivers displayed at an accident site in Novena, as they honked and refused to give way to an ambulance. Yes, it is peak hour, but somebody was hurt and needed help urgently, she reasons.

    JD commented:

    "This picture was taken earlier this evening (May 13).

    "An accident occurred at the cross junction at Novena. Although there were no fatalities, the motorist, a lady, was hurt from the impact of the accident, even though she immediately got up after the crash.

    "Also, the driver of the other car involved was nice and did not hit-and-run despite the peak hour traffic.

    "Here's the best part however. The ambulance and police were stuck in the heavy traffic because nobody wanted to give way! Instead, everybody honked at the ambulance which approached the accident site thinking the injured person was still there, when she had already been escorted to a seating area nearby.

    "When the ambulance tried to make its way nearer to where the injured woman was, all the drivers and motorists started honked again!

    "Goodness! It's an ambulance for crying out loud and the very fact that its making its way to the pavement shows that someone needs help!

    "Come on Singaporeans! Please learn to be patient and cultured!!!"
