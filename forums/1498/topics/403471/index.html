    Teenager drowns at Sentosa's Siloso Beach

    SINGAPORE: An 18-year-old teenager was found dead at Sentosa's Siloso beach early Saturday morning.

    Singapore Civil Defence Force said it received a call at 8.04 pm last night about a swimmer who had gone missing off Sentosa.

    SCDF personnel began a search with divers, an amphibious vehicle and a dinghy boat.

    After searching for about four hours, they called the naval diving unit to help in the rescue operations.

    Rescuers managed to recover the body at 4am.

    The teenager was pronounced dead by paramedics.

    Police are investigating the incident. - CNA/jy
