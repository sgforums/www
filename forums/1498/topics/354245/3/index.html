    P1 cases - no pulse, no breathing, CPR required & massive bleeding, etc

    P2 cases - dislocation, fractures, fainted, etc

    P3 cases - able to walk, patient is mostly alert (mostly non emergency cases)

    PAOs should have this matra (aka walkie talkie) to communicate to OCC..
