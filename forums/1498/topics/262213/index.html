    Fire damages Kallang factory's roofing, no one hurt

    SINGAPORE : A fire broke out at a paint factory in Kallang Way on Tuesday evening.

    It damaged the factory's rear roofing and part of its fence, but no one was injured.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) said it deployed two fire engines, two Red Rhinos and four support vehicles to the scene.

    When they reached the location, they found the back of the two-storey factory ablaze.

    The fire, which involved paints as well as wooden and plastics products, was brought under control within 15 minutes.

    SCDF is investigating. - CNA/de
