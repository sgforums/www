    Acid leak in Tampines factory, affected part shut down

    SINGAPORE: Fourteen factory workers were taken to Changi General Hospital on Tuesday evening following a chemical leak at a factory in Tampines.

    All of them have since been allowed to go home.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force received a call about the incident just after 7pm and attended to it shortly.

    SCDF officers found diluted acid dripping from a delivery pipe at AFPD Private Limited, located at Tampines Industrial Avenue 3.

    The company subsequently isolated the affected part of the pipe and shut it down. - CNA/so
