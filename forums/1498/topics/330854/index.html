Balestier fire - Kudos to SCDF for quick action

  • QX179R
    17 Sep 08, 12:20

    STOMPer Woofair was having dinner at Balestier when he witnessed a fire that was later put out by quick thinking SCDF fire fighters.

    In an email to STOMP yesterday (16 Sept), the impressed STOMPer says:

    “The fire broke out around 7pm. apparently, it was caused by an electric short circuit.”

    “10 min later, a Red Rhino arrived at the scene followed by a police car.

    "Then, there were at least four more fire engines, one ambulance and one police car assisting to put out the fire.

    “They took about 10mins to get the fire under control. Good job to the fire fighters! Without their swift action, there may be more houses along the stretch swallowed by the fire.

    "By the time we left the scene, there were no casualties or injuries. I hope not anyway.”
