    PES BP will be going to Delta Company. don't worry, the training here is not damn teruk.. mostly will do Physical Training in BRTC but some training will go to CDA too. 

    first two weeks no training because these two weeks is enlistment week. training starts 3rd week onwards.

    If you have medical History cases, bring the doc. letter and go to Medical Centre on the enlistment day. 

    CD bookout timing..
    every Friday at 5pm (even on enlistment week)

    CD Bookin timing..
    every Sunday at 8pm (must be at your company line by 7.30pm)

    Your company line will be at outside Detention Barrack and  Dormitory block staircase
