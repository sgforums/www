Fire at Block 221 Choa Chu Kang Central

  • QX179R
    25 Dec 11, 13:00

    The SCDF got a call around 9.32am this morning (Dec 25) for an incident at Block 221 Choa Chu Kang Central.

    This was a case of minor fire.

    The SCDF dispatched one fire engine, one Red Rhino and one ambulance to the incident location and arrived in slightly over three minutes.

    The fire was from the cooking area of a wedding function at the ground floor of Block 221.

    It was extinguished by SCDF using one domestic hose.

    Two workers suffered minor burns but were conscious and got conveyed to Singapore General Hospital via the SCDF ambulance."
