    Civil defence officers put out fire at Jalan Suka, no one injured

    SINGAPORE : Singapore Civil Defence officers put out a fire which engulfed a two-storey semi-detached house along Jalan Suka on Tuesday morning.

    When they arrived, they found part of the living room on the first floor engulfed in flames.

    With the help of one fire engine, two Red Rhinos and two support appliances, firemen extinguished the blaze in five minutes.

    There was no casualty and the cause of the fire is being investigated. - CNA /ls
