Motorbikes set on fire in Woodlands Circle

  • SMRT Buses
    26 Aug 07, 21:54
    Motorbikes set on fire at multiple locations in Woodlands Circle

    SINGAPORE: Some 24 motorcycles went up in flames on Sunday morning at several multi-storey car parks in Woodlands Circle.

    Eye-witnesses said the fire started in one of the multi-storey car parks around 4.10am and within 12 minutes, fire broke out at nearby areas as well.

    In all, the arsonists lit up motorbikes in three car parks - Blocks 766A, 768A and 748A as well as the car park at the back of Block 748.

    Civil Defence officers were at the scene to put out the fire.

    No one was hurt in the incident and investigations are still ongoing. - CNA/so
