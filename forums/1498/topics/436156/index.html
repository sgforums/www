Fire at 29 Lembu Road

  • QX179R
    14 Aug 11, 22:29

    SINGAPORE: Eleven people were brought to the Singapore General Hospital after they sustained burns on Sunday, in a fire at Lembu Road.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) said it was alerted to a fire at 29 Lembu Road on Sunday evening.

    It said its officers arrived at the incident site within eight minutes.

    The fire involved contents of a kitchen area in a restaurant.

    It was extinguished using one water jet by SCDF officers, within 10 minutes of their arrival.

    The cause of the fire is being investigated.
