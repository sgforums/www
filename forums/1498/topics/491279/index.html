    1 injured in Whitley Road accident

    SINGAPORE: A 47-year-old female driver was injured in an accident involving two cars at the junction of Whitley Road and Dyson Road on Thursday morning (Feb 11). 

     

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) said it received a call about the accident at about 8.10am and deployed an ambulance to the scene. 

     

    The paramedics then conveyed the driver of one of the vehicles to Tan Tock Seng Hospital. She was in a conscious state when the responders arrived, SCDF said. 

     

    The Singapore Police Force said investigations are ongoing regarding the cause of the accident.
