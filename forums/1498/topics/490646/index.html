    Fire at 78 Redhill Lane on 19 Nov 2015



    SCDF was alerted to a case of fire at the above mentioned location. SCDF resources were immediately dispatched to the incident site.

     

    SCDF firefighters had to force open the main door of a unit on the 4th floor. The fire involved a bed in the bedroom and was extinguished within 5 minutes using 1 water jet. A body was found in the bedroom and was pronounced dead by an SCDF Paramedic.

     

    The cause of the fire is under investigation.
