Crouch happy with Reds

  • bryanseb
    3 Feb 07, 23:05
    Liverpool striker Peter Crouch has admitted it was never in his plans to exit Anfield during the January transfer window.

    Crouch's future with Liverpool had some uncertainty hanging over it a few weeks ago, leading to rumours that the England forward was about to leave the club.

    The 26-year-old was frustrated at manager Rafa Benitez's rotation policy and at being subbed in the Boxing Day defeat at Blackburn.

    Newcastle reportedly tabled a bid for the forward but Crouch was reassured by Benitez that his future is with The Reds.

    "There was interest but the manager reiterated that I had a future at Liverpool and that was good enough for me," Crouch told the Daily Mail.

    "It can be frustrating. I wasn't playing as much as I'd have liked in that period just before January.

    "I was frustrated to be taken off at Blackburn when I thought I could have influenced the game.

    "I wasn't surprised about the speculation but I had no intention of leaving."
