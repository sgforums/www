    I have watched the entire ASEAN football championship, and IMHO Singapore was very lucky to win the championship. They only won two games, the one against Laos and the Kellong game against Thailand. Skill wise and speed wise they pale in comparision to the other team, what they had over them was discipine (they never lose their cool and have superior teamwork and organisation) and a lot of luck. To be honest I though that the standards of Singapore, Thailand, Vietnam, Malaysia, Indonesia and Myanmar don't differ by a lot. Singapore was a lot better during the last tiger cup where I could say for sure that they deserved to win and was the best in ASEAN. I guess the standards of our neighbours has improved significantly?
