Asean Football Championship: Ticketing Details

  • bryanseb
    11 Jan 07, 20:26
    Defending champions Singapore, grouped in Group B of the Asean Football Championship, will play their first match against 1998 runners-up Vietnam on Saturday, 13 January 2007 at the National Stadium.

    The other teams in Group B are 2004 runners-up Indonesia and Laos. Singapore are the tournament's co-hosts, along with Thailand. All group matches would be played at Singapore's National Stadium and Jalan Besar Stadium, and Thailand's Suphachalasai Stadium and Army Stadium.

    Following the fixture against Vietnam, Singapore will next play Laos on Monday, 15 January 2007 and Indonesia on Wednesday, 17 January 2007.

    Tickets are available at the FAS Office, Jalan Besar Stadium from Mondays to Friday, during office hours (9am to 5pm).

    Tickets are also available on Friday, 12 January 2007, from 10am to 7pm, at the East Entrance of the National Stadium.

    On Match Days, tickets can be purchased at the National Stadium (on 13, 15 and 17 Jan), East & West Entrances, and Jalan Besar Stadium (on 17 January), North & South Entrances, two hours before kickoff time.
