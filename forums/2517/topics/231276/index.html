Fergie: No change on Henrik deal

  • bryanseb
    13 Jan 07, 11:28
    Sir Alex Ferguson claims Manchester United are not currently looking into extending Henrik Larsson's loan stay at Old Trafford.

    The Swedish forward made an instant impact as he scored in his first game at the weekend and it was immediately suggested that United would look to extend his deal until the end of the season.

    Helsingborgs have suggested that they would be prepared to let Larsson stay if a compensation package can be agreed but Ferguson claims that, at present, the forward will leave as planned on 12th March.

    "Henrik is here until March 12 and we are not looking beyond that," stated the Scot.

    "I do not think it is pertinent at this moment anyway.

    "It would not be fair to start putting Helsingborg under that type of pressure.

    "Henrik is enjoying playing here and that is the way we are going to leave it."
