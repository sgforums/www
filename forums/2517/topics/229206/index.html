Giggs aiming for double

  • bryanseb
    3 Jan 07, 09:13
    Manchester United winger Ryan Giggs sees no reason why The Red Devils cannot conquer the Premiership and Europe this season.

    The Old Trafford side are currently seven points clear of the summit of England's elite league having gone three years without claiming the coveted crown.

    Sir Alex Ferguson's troops are also through to the last 16 of the UEFA Champions League and Giggs is confident the club can repeat their stunning 1999 success in Barcelona.

    "It is something we will certainly try for," stated Giggs. "Our aim is to win the Premiership and Champions League.

    "We are confident if we keep our form up we will have a great chance.

    "There are some real top teams left in Europe and it is a very tough competition to win.

    "You need everything going for you. You need to play well and need that little bit of luck."
