    It a failureand shame for Singapore soccer league as the league stand to lost most of the national players or best foreigner players to other Asian league due to this 3+2 ruling of AFC. But who to blame when Singapore has such a poor soccer league compare to our neighbours? $15000 prize money for S-league winner is too little and no demotion to lower rank league make the league lost it color as it is boring to see the same bottom team year in year out. There is little or no motivation for the players and clubs to perform up to standard. And we have a lot of soccer talent players and there is no need to depend on foreigner clubs to make the Singapore soccer league attractive to gain public attention.  

    What we need is some change of rule, marketing and management on the S-league and it organizer.
