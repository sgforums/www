    There was a famous soccer-club in Austria, which were nearly bankcrupt because of lack of money. Its soccer team had lost nearly every match, also against weak soccer-clubs. Therefore no spectators came to the soccer-stadium and less ticket were sold. The manager of the club gathered then all leading club-members and said to them: "We have now only a small amount of money in oure club-cash. I want to take it and use it for beting on oure loss in oure next soccer-match." The club-members were very glad about this offer by their manager, because this was a way - they thought right- to come out of their financial crisis. The goalkeeper of the soccer-club did not defend his goal against the ball-shootings of the opponent team and so his club were defeated in the next match. Every shooted ball against his goal aimed it. Therefore the manager's bet was won and he could save his club for the inability of paying. Afterwards they made this manoevre to often and the referee on the soccer-field recognized this cheating -the sleeping, inactive goalkeeper - and broke up the match and the soccer club was accused for this criminal acting in court.
