Larsson hails 'frightening' duo

  • bryanseb
    2 Feb 07, 20:39
    Henrik Larsson has paid tribute to the 'frightening' abilities of Manchester United team-mates Wayne Rooney and Cristiano Ronaldo.

    The Swedish striker, who lined up alongside the likes of Ronaldinho at Barcelona, has been amazed by what he has seen from the pair since joining United on loan from Helsingborgs.

    Rooney and Ronaldo were in devastating form in the 4-0 victory over Watford on Wednesday and Larsson admits it is a joy to play in the same team as them.

    Larsson feels they are already among the best players in the world and will continue to go from strength to strength.

    "They're already up there. They've got all the attributes and are still only young," he praised.

    "It's frightening to think what they could be.

    "I've always said it's easy to play with great players, and Wayne is one of the great players.

    "He has got the vision and he can score goals. Cristiano is the same. They are both fantastic."
