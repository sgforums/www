Crossley's Owls loan extended

  • bryanseb
    7 Jan 07, 17:38
    Sheffield Wednesday have extended the loan of goalkeeper Mark Crossley.

    Crossley will now be eligible to play in Sunday's FA Cup tie against Premiership side Manchester City.

    The 37-year-old's initial spell at Hillsborough expired on New Year's Day after the defeat to Hull City.

    But the Owls have since come to an agreement with Fulham to extend Crossley's loan until the end of the season.

    The former Nottingham Forest keeper has featured 11 times for Wednesday this campaign and scored an unforgettable injury-time equaliser in the 3-3 draw with Southampton.
