Saha: Drogba is no Ron

  • bryanseb
    30 Dec 06, 20:36
    Louis Saha insists Cristiano Ronaldo, not Didier Drogba, is the best player in the Premiership.

    The Manchester United star has responded to claims by Drogba, who has 12 league goals, that he is the best player at the moment, after his stunning winning goal against Everton.

    But Saha believes that Ronaldo, who has 10 Premiership goals, is the more complete player and the person most feared by the opposition.

    He told the Daily Star: "Today the best player in the Premiership is Ronaldo, without doubt. Ronaldo terrorises every defence he comes up against and you can see that each team tries to adopt a special tactical system in an attempt to stop him.

    "This is very positive for the other forwards because his action opens up the pitch for us and gives us greater opportunities to score.

    "The other thing about Ronaldo is that he is always smiling on the pitch or on the street or whether he is in training."

    Saha was also quick to insist that the pressure is all on Chelsea in the fight for the Premiership title.

    "Now the pressure in the title race is all on Chelsea. We are the leaders and I do not believe we will fail.

    "I am ambitious and I am desperate to break Chelsea's dominance in the Premiership and reconfirm that this Man United team is one of the best in the world."
