Xabi: I'm no diver

  • bryanseb
    8 Jan 07, 21:24
    Xabi Alonso has dismissed suggestions that he dived against Arsenal on Saturday, with the Spanish midfielder of the opinion that he should have won a penalty during Liverpool's FA Cup defeat at Anfield.

    With the tie evenly poised at 0-0 after 25 minutes, Alonso tumbled to the floor under challenge from Gunners midfielder Gilberto.

    Referee Steve Bennett felt Alonso had dived as he brandished a yellow card to the Spaniard, but the midfielder is adamant he was sinned against rather than sinner.

    "I felt the contact, that is for sure," said Alonso. "People who have watched it on TV have said it was a clear penalty and I felt that on the pitch.

    "I was talking to the referee and Gilberto and I said to them there had been contact, that I didn't cheat.

    "I was trying to play with the ball and I fell down because of that contact - I was not cheating."

    The Reds, meanwhile, have offered a testimonial to long-serving defender Jamie Carragher.

    The Bootle-born England international has risen through the ranks at Anfield and has established himself as a firm favourite on Merseyside since making his debut against Middlesbrough in 1997.
