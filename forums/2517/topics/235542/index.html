No Real return for Beckham

  • bryanseb
    4 Feb 07, 00:06
    Fabio Capello has again left David Beckham out of his Real Madrid squad to face Levante.

    Beckham has not featured for the Spanish giants since it was announced he will be joining Major League Soccer side LA Galaxy in the summer.

    Capello later declared that the former England captain would not play for Real again, although the Italian has since backtracked on that statement.

    Beckham missed last weekend's game against Villarreal after an ankle injury forced him to sit out training in the build up to the game.

    The ex-Manchester United man was fit enough to resume training this week, but has not been recalled to the Real squad.

    Sunday's home fixture against Levante will be the sixth successive Real game that Beckham has not been involved in.
