Fergie hands Ron Real warning

  • bryanseb

    Manchester United star Cristiano Ronaldo admits he is aware of Real Madrid's desire to sign him, but has been ordered by Sir Alex Ferguson not to discuss the Spanish giants' interest.

    The Portuguese international has enjoyed a fantastic season for the Premiership leaders and is one of the leading contenders for the PFA Player of the Year award.

    However, his form has not gone unnoticed across Europe and reports in Spain suggest Real are now keener than ever to land a player who has made little secret of his wish to play in Spain at some point in the future.

    The Primera titans attempted to lure the 21-year-old to La Liga last summer but were flatly rebuffed by Ferguson and the uncompromising Scot wants to nip renewed interest in the bud having informed his young charge not to publicly acknowledge Real.

    Ronaldo was tracked down by Spanish journalists over the weekend who were keen to discuss a possible switch to Spain, but was reticent to discuss his future having revealed his manager's instructions.

    "I know of the interest of Real Madrid, but I can not say anything," he told reporters from Spanish sports newspaper AS.

    "Yesterday (Saturday), I had two chats with Carlos Queiroz and Alex Ferguson and they have forbidden me to speak about Real Madrid.

    "You have to understand that because I don't want any problems."
