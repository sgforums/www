Agent rejects Jose Real rumours

  • bryanseb
    3 Feb 07, 12:42
    Jose Mourinho's agent has rejected reports linking the Chelsea boss with a possible move to Real Madrid.

    Reports emanating from Spain suggest Real have targeted Mourinho for the manager's job at El Santiago Bernabeu for next season.

    Mourinho's long-term future at Stamford Bridge has been questioned in the last few weeks amid reports of a breakdown in his relationship with club owner Roman Abramovich.

    The former Porto chief insists he has no plans to leave Chelsea and will honour his contract to 2010.

    However, the rumours in Spain continue to claim Real will try to lure Mourinho to Madrid.

    Mourinho's representative Jorge Mendes has moved to quell the speculation insisting it is only rumours.

    "It is only rumours, but nothing is true," said Mendes.

    Real Madrid director of sport Predrag Mijatovic refuted the rumours regarding Mourinho earlier this week saying the club were happy with current incumbent Fabio Capello.
