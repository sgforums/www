Music by the Beach: Electrify Your Saturday Nights At Siloso

  • BanguIzai
    1 Jul 10, 23:33


     

    Music by the Beach

    		 Every Sat in July
    		6pm – 10.30pm
    		WaveHouse Sentosa, Siloso Beach
    		Free*

    WaveHouseSentosa presents Simply Electrifying

    Turn up the voltage, as sultry Saturday nights on Sentosa turn electrifying! Jam into the wee hours with heart-throbbing and digitally creative music by renowned artistes in the music scene.

    Sonofa - 3 July

    A dynamic musical sensation hits town! Sonofa, awarded MTV Asia’s Cutting Edge Award, captivates audiences with a new “LIVE” take on electronic dance music married with a powerful presence and a myriad of visual spectacles. A revolution and an evolution of percussive and electronic music, conceptualized, directed and produced in Singapore, SONOFA brings the human element, drums and electronic together organically, producing a raw, creative energy which will pump up audiences for times to come and drive them wild.

    Reverie - 10 July

    Go wild over Reverie as the boys heat up the night with their incredible stage chemistry and musical technicality. Playing since 2003, Reverie has performed gigs at hot clubbing spots in Singapore and their very own concerts at Esplanade. This band is not to be missed!

    Sixx - 17 July

    Vibrant and edgy, Sixx rapidly woos crowd and fans alike with their gritty urban music. From rock to classical, funk and jazz, Sixx has spent the last year merging their musical influences to bring a different sound to the stage; a niche in today’s thriving music scene. Sixx brings together the good old sounds of the 60s, 70s and 80s with the vibrancy and energy of the present hip hop era. Catch this 6-men band grow into a 10-piece band, only for Music by the Beach!

    Magnetico - 24 July

    Enjoy a fresh new twist to regular pop-rock tunes, and dance to electro rap, drums & bass jungle, jazzy hip & hop, and whatnot. Headed by Christine Sham, Magnetico features sessionist drummer Mohd Noor, multi-instrumentalist bassist Casey Subramaniam, woodwind specialist Leonardo, rock guitar legend Paul Daniel and “Live The Dream” champion Affendi on vocals

    Jack & Rai with Paul Danial - 31 July

    Rock the night with the dynamic duo Jack & Rai, as they team up with multi-genre studio and live sessionist Paul Danial, to deliver an eclectic mix of old and new favourites, from blues and rock to country, funk and metal. Let this unique combination blow you away!
