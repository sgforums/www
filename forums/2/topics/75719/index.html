    Whee!

    that was awesome. 4 days of intense wheel to wheel racing, 140km/h battles, damaged bodywork scraping all over the place, the chaos, the noise, the passion! what an experience!

    Day 1: Wednesday

    8pm: left singapore.
    drove up to kota bahru overnight, 10 hours straight without sleep.

    Day 2: Thursday

    6am: reached kota bahru.
    7am: reached the racing circuit.
    8am: preparations all done, testing started.
    10.30am: registration done
    1pm: testing went fine
    2pm: scrutineered.
    3.30pm: water leak detected around the radiator piping area, troubleshooting begains
    3.45pm: testing on track, spewing water all over the track
    4pm: problem fixed, but engine running hot.
    4.20pm: engine temp goes up to 110 degrees C after only 2 laps on track.
    4.45pm: engine still running hot.
    5.15pm: engine removed. replacement engine installed.
    5.20pm: replacement engine refuses to start.

    6.30pm problem still not solved. by now, short on sleep, tired and very cranky, me and my coach desperately tries to fix the problem, but fails to remedy it. Team boss calls from singapore and demands we work on the problem overnight, argument ensures. finally decided that the team should return to hotel for rest, food and a good shower.
  • HENG@
    13 Apr 04, 00:13
    Day 3: Friday
    7am: left hotel
    8am: arrived at circuit
    9am: engine started. problem seems solved.
    9.15am: rescrutineered due to change of engine.
    10.10am: Official free practice starts
    2.30pm: driver's briefing
    3.35pm: time trial 1. qualified 12 out of 24, timing 51.08s
    5.05pm: time trial 2. qualified 10 out of 21 timing 50.64s.

    overall starting position for Saturday heats 1: 13 out of 27

    Day 4: Saturday

    8am: reach circuit.
    9.30am: official warm up and systems check
    11.15am: heats 1. non start due to fouled spark plug
    2.50pm: heats 2. started 23. fought up to 15
    starting position for Sunday Pre-Finals: aggregate for heats 1 & 2: (23+15)/2 = 19
  • HENG@
    13 Apr 04, 12:15
    Day 5: Sunday

    8am: reached the circuit. started testing on threadbare tyres, managed to stay on circuit.
    11.20am: Pre-finals. started 19, finished 18
    3pm: waiting for the arrival of the Tengku, guest of honour
    4pm: welcome ceremony
    5.15pm: Finals. started 18, finished 16.

    And that concludes my 1st official overseas race.
