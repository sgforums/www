    =========================================

    Dear All,

    We all had our fair share of those darned cabbies.... and with their reckless stunts on the roads. Most of the time, it is just cutting in without signalling or checking blind spots, etc... today, I had to be honoured with a YELLOW TOP Cabbie who tried something so stupid and so dangerous.

    Date: 10 Jan 2006
    Time of incident: 1455hrs
    Place: Junction of Bt Batok East Ave 5 / Ave 2, where Masjid Ar-Raudhah is located.

    Description:
    It was raining, as usual for the past week or so, and I was on Bt Batok East Ave 5 making a left turn into Bt Batok East Ave 2.

    The green arrow lit up for my (left) lane to turn. The two cars in front of me turned left and I followed.

    As I turned into the 2 lane road, there were cars parked by the side of the road along the mosque due to Hari Raya Haji celebrations/ceremonies in the mosque. I signalled and moved to the right lane just after turning left.

    I had to swerve back into whatever space I could find on the left!

    This yellow top cab driver from the opposite direction (without signalling) cut into the space between the car in front of me and was due for a head-on collision with me! He was on the right most lane for turning right/ going straight. There were two cars ahead of him going straight and they stopped at the (red) lights eventhough the green turn right arrow was on.

    The cabbie 'overtook' these two cars in front by coming into the opposing side of the road and in my path, forcing me to swerve. He then proceeded to make the right turn into Bt Batok East Ave 5. I only managed to blast my horn as he passed me and I thought I caught a glimpse of a passenger in the rear.

    The sketch drawing below shows my path and the path of the taxi.



    The junction was full of vehicles on every side of the road and there were a few pedestrians around as well.

    Now, from driver to driver, I am humbly appealing to any driver who was there at the scene and caught the YELLOW TOP CAB's licence plate number. I did not get it as he was going the opposite direction and I had to evade asap to avoid a head-on collision (in my lane!). Better still, if you are the passenger...

    Please forward all information to madscienc@yahoo.com as I am seriously contemplating shooting a letter to Confort Delgro [(65) 6550 8588 / prfeedback@ctpl.com.sg] as well as LTA, and relevant authorities.

    This behaviour was way out of line and the cab driver, for whatever reasons he had, should be taken off the streets as soon as possible.

    Thank you for your understanding and help on this matter.
