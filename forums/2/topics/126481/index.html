    This is how u can calcualate the 1st payment.

    Let take this example
    Car Cost : $70,000
    Let say: Downpayment 10%=$7,000
    Road Tax is usually free for 1/2 year, therefore no cost.
    Insurance estimated to be $1,000.
    Interest rate is 2.5% for 10 year.
    Deposit of $5,000 when u book the car.

    What u have to pay up usually is as follows:

    Downpayment : $7,000
    1st Installment: $ 657
    Insurance : $1,000

    Total : $8,657

    Deposit : $5,.000

    U will then have to pay up $8,657 - $5,000 = $3,657 before the car company will register the car with LTA.

    U will have to check with the seller if there is any other cost, eg GST, registrations fees, admin, coffee $$, haha.
