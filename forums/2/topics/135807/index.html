    no probs unless u rev ur engine to high rpms. the oil will never rise high enuff to flood ur combustion chambers. even at high rpms, there is a bleeder tube in ur valve cover for excess engine oil to be directed back into ur intake for it to be combusted along with the a/f mix. typical symtoms of oil burning will occur but it does no damage to the engine except make ur engine feel sluggish.
