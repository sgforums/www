    Thanks for the honest feedback. CTR and WRX are seen as 2 different types of "sports cars" Wrx is more of a rally car and CTR has always been more track focused.  So it depends of what kinda of look you want, although honestly the WRX has been labelled ah beng qia in singapore whereas the new CTR is seen as more grown up and not beng.

    Its all down to personal preferance. Some people think that CTR is sporty and others dont. Personally, i think the sedan WRX looks sportier than the hatchback one and the new CTR is sporty.

    Price is usually determined by performance in the sports car sector.

    The CTR has a different engine from the other civics on the road. and has a different ECU, Suspension, brakes, a whole redone chassis and different exhaust. Tyres are Potenza RE070 vs Turanzas on other civics. Interior is also different

    In layman terms: you are paying for performance items .

    So why does CTR cost as much as WRX? Because you probably are comparing the normal civic to a WRX w/o knowing the difference between a CTR and a normal civic. No offence =)
