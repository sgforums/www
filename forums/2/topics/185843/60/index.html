    Originally posted by wannamarchy:
    May i know when we go for our 10k service(which is the last free service), will they ask you whether want to sign up their service plan for future?
    The main question they might ask you is whether you want synthetic oil. Mineral oil is free (part of the free service) but synthetic must top $60 (I think). Synthetic oil is preferred as it does a better job of lubrication, and can even save petrol because your engine has less power loss due to friction. Also it lasts longer so your next service can be 20k. So it's worth it to take synthetic oil.

    Even if they ask you to sign up to their service plan, it's really up to you whether you want. Personally I did the calculations and there is actually not much savings, but it locks you in so you can't go somewhere else like Autosaver or your nearby petrol station for servicing. I decided not to sign up so that I have the flexibility.
