  • Shooting_Cloud
    16 Jul 07, 18:01
    I have not fixed the dents or scratches on any of my cars before, because I think the cost is not worth the benefit. Dents I usually try to knock out myself. The March steel is thin and so you'll be surprised how easy it is to knock/push out the dent yourself. It won't be perfect but you should be able to get it out more than 50%.

    For big scratches, you should get a small can of paint from the dealer. Toyota used to give them free (if you bring your own can). Now they sell a small can for $10. TC should also be able to provide some paint if you bring your own small can. As it is paint, the can must be made of metal, not plastic.

    My wife caused some scratches, and went to the Ubi service center. The nice people over there touched up the scratches FOC. So you can try this too. Have your gf/wife do it - may get better response...
