    7 Nov 06, 13:40
    Just watched the Amazing Race clips on YouTube. Quite cool that the show decided to use the March instead of so many other cars. It's a real looker for the money!

    Looks like there are lots of new Marchies around. Must organize another mega meetup so everybody can meet & greet.

    Got my car on Sat. Already lost my key ring with the key code metal plate. Oh well. Good thing March doesn't have immobilizer so can duplicate key anywhere. Just did for $4. Nissan price for spare key with remote - $240!

    The car is very impressive and super refined for its price class. Fit and finish is top notch. No rattling and jiggles. My Vios had rattles and funny noises from the first day. The Japs really do put in a lot more effort when they put something together.

    The interior is a lot more quiet than other small cars (Vios, Getz, City, Sunny). I can hear the sound system so much better. By the way, I opted out of the Kenwood (so $250 off), and changed to the JVC 2-DIN with 12CD changer setup which comes with the Latio premium class ($530, so net topup is $280). Heard from the in-house Nissan installer that speakers are the same for March, Latio, and Cefiro. The sound is awesome, especially since the car is so quiet.

    Sitting position is higher than other cars, more like SUV height. So for those who like higher seating position, this will be a nice plus.

    So when we organize March Mega Meetup?
