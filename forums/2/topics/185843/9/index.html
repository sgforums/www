    Well Autosaver "specialises" with Nissan cars and many of the members here go to them for servicing. I've also heard of one other workshop which is based on the 2nd floor of Ang Mo Kio Industrial park (in fact there are many car workshops there as well ). Cannot remember the name of the shop but the fella also sells Hurricane filters. Check for you when i'm back.

    Anyway I am into my 70k servicing but has stuck to Tan Chong all these time. I am into my second the NPMP (Nissan Preventive Maintenance Program) which gives you 4 servicing per plan within a 2 years window (something like that) and i'm quite satisfied. In mind opinion if you drive frequent enough (covering at least 40k within 2 years), you might want to consider Tan Chong's NPMP as it will not be too much difference in cost when compared to outside servicing for 4 times. And afterall it gives me a peace of mind with regards to going back to Tan Chong for warranty claims. After 100k or 3 years maybe I will go for outside workshop for servicing as warranty would have lapse. But if you intend to do piece meal servicing with Tan Chong, then forget it as it is relatively expensive. Again there will be some other skools of thoughts here between servicing with Tan Chong and 3rd party workshops and some will point them out very eagerly.
