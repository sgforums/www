    hi, this is four_eggs and just to share my exp as i have just gotten my new nissan march. 1K servicing (free from TC) is to drain the old engine oil since manufactured and transferred to our port for LTA clearance, easily the oil is 6 mths old or longer. must change and also free. no other parts require to change at this time. it is more for the engine to run in since brand new.

    5K servicing (free from TC); no need to change to synthetic old as the serviceman may want you to top up some $$ as fully synthetic old is capable for 10K running so waste of $$ if you only drive it for 5000 km. have not heard about semi-synthetic.

    10K servicing (free from TC) is worthwhile to top up some $$ for synthetic oil and it can last for another 10000 km till 20km for next major servicing.

    parts at service centre is more expensive than outside as they factor labour charges.

    cheers, four_eggs
