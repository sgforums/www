    Originally posted by dreamingaboutmarch:
    thanks! it's crystal clear! $200-$250 seems like a lot of driving to me. u must enjoy it very much!

    what's your opinion on CVT on fuel consumption? higher?
    The March is on a traditional 4-speed auto transmission, not CVT which has no gears but rather 2 pulleys with variable lengths. CVT gives smoother (but slower) gear changes but is designed for slow easy driving. Acceleration is slower than standard transmissions. I much prefer the standard auto trans for better performance.

    Get the March if you're looking for a fun city car with lots of window area. It is a very easy car to drive and park, and so is great for less confident drivers. It stands out for different reasons than WRX with huge spoilers. However be prepared to get bullied a lot on the road, i.e. other cars will always assume you are slower, and so will overtake you aggressively, and won't give you way.

    Have fun choosing your color!
