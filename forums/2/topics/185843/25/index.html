    The stock tyres that come with my car is from Goodyear GT3. Been on the Falken ST115 for almost 40k and also looking to change mine again. IMO the tyres did well for me. Good grip, initially quiet (but i am not too bothered by the noise), wet weather also ok. Thread still there after 40k. GR80 should be around $108 or so for 195/50/R15. Generally from asking around the tyres could range between $85 to $100 plus for size 15. There is this shop in Ang Mo Kio industrial park which is opposite the Bishan MRT depot called Poh Heng who is a good help for rims and tyres. Many of the Marchies got our rims and tyres from them.
