    Originally posted by wannamarchy:
    Hi,
    my next service is also 10K...but why for 10k service they got write by when must go for service regardless whether you reach the 10K mark?
    Technically service intervals are by distance travelled and time used, so that cars that are rarely used are still serviced regularly. For Jap cars, it's usually 3months or 5000km for mineral oil, and double that for synthetic oil (6mos / 10,000km), whichever comes first.

    The Germans are now on 15,000km / 1 year service intervals (BMW, Porsche, Audi) but I still think 10,000km/6mos is the best. The Germans don't even have the run-in periods i.e. 1000km and 5000km! First service recommended by the German makers is 15000km...
