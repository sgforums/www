    Your Service Advisor is the person who took the car from you, walked you to your car to find out what's wrong if anything, and got you to sign the service form. His name is also on the detailed receipt you got when you picked up your car. If you still don't know who your advisor is, just call up TC and they can tell you from the computer, along with his handphone number.

    The only thing that changes when you have passengers at the back is the back springs are compressed more. The sound likely means that the suspension is faulty and needs to be replaced. The warranty will take care of the replacement FOC. If it gets worse, you might have stability problems (car wobbly at high speed and while cornering).
