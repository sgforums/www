    The price for soundproofing done by hifi workshop varies greatly.
    You will have to ask them yourself to understand and believe the pricing.

    A basic two doors isolation will cost about ~$200-$400 (depends on the brand of the material and the number of layers applied.)

    Of course if you intend to isolate the road noise coming from the floorboard/seats area and the spare tyre area, the cost would be about $1000-$1500 (again depends on the material quality/layer)

    Some people may also want to isolate the wheel 'hump' areas, or maybe the roof and the dashboard area.
    Everything counts to large amount!
