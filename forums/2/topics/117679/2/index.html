    Originally posted by Lancer2005:
    i drive a new lancer 1.6 manual. just got it few days back. the FC for 1/4 tank is around 125-135km...Is this high???
    New car's FC tends to be lousy. Anyway, since you should be running-in your car, might want to find out how heavy your foot should be during this period.

    After run-in (after 5000km servicing), pump a full tank, then clock your milage and the next pump amount. Do the necessary maths to find your FC. Maybe get a few to average out.

    Anyway, if you fell a pull whenever you accelerate, then you are heavy-footed. And if you change gears after 3000rpm, your FC will be high. Trying hard to accelerate your car when it is below 2000rpm is going to cost you fuel too.

    All in are generally speaking case.
