    Hi folks,

    After a long search, wait, decisions between decisions, I've finally booked a Lancer (M) Sports Edition! Delivery should be early March, or so the SE says.

    Booked it in Jan when COE was about $18k +. Costs $62.5k. Funny thing is, latest COE is also about $18k +, but the price of the exact same model dropped to just below $60k! So I "lost" about $2k, $3k on savings. *pengz* Guess buying a car is pretty much like playing the stock market or punting football.

    As for freebies, managed to get free Rike Cool film. Costs about $800+ in the market, or so I was told. Can't think of anything more to squeeze the SE, becos its the Sports version and comes with pretty much everything.

    Got the Manual version becos the auto CVT doesn't cut it for me. Smooth quiet ride yes if you're light-footed and all, but it doesn't feel anything like driving a car at all. Manual, although a bit more troublesome esp. in city driving like SG, gives more power and control. That's just me opinion of course, as I'm sure other auto CVT drivers have their own valid reasons for sticking to auto. Test out BOTH to see what works for you.

    I intend to hit the NS highway once in awhile after the initial run-in period.

    Wo-ho!
