Shell petrol & diesel prices down 3 cents from 8.30am today

  • ^tamago^
    24 Oct 05, 11:26


    Shell press release

    "Retail pump prices at Shell stations will be reduced by three (3) cents for petrol and diesel with effect from 8.30am today.

    Since the previous pump price reduction on 26 September 2005, which was also led by Shell, internationally traded finished product prices have eased. Shell is pleased to be the first to pass the savings from this drop in product costs to our customers for the second consecutive time in less than a month."

    Shell's advertised prices (after discount) with effect from 8.30am today:
    Shell 95 1.606
    Shell 98 1.680
    Shell V-Power 1.742
    Shell Diesel 1.044

    Both Caltex and ExxonMobil have not revised their prices yet, while SPC has followed suit this morning from 10am.
