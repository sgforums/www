    Hi all,

    just a quick summary

    History
    1) Vtec: Variable cam profile: technology pioneered by honda in 1980s
    2) Mivec: Variable cam profile; same as Vtec
    3) VVTi: Variable cam phasing: technology pioneered by Ford in 1960s

    Characteristics
    1) Vtec and Mivec: Power optimization, complicated to mass manufacture; activation at pre-determined rpm. agressive high cams at high rev

    2) VVTi: Fuel Consumption optmization, easy to manufacture and cheap; Continuous variable (always "on") so do not associate with sudden increase in "power", it not suppose to do that.
