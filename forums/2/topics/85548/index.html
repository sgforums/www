    yes the Jazz/Fit is different from the rest. But I think there is some good issues to think abt here.

    a. Fuel Consumption: JAZZ wins hands down. The iDsi engine is one of the most fuel saving engines in the market now and depending on how u drive, an average of 16km/llitre is possible......some higher, some lower but generally it has to be one of the highest in fuel savings. In terms of car price, I believe Jazz would be one of the cheapest amongst all ur choices.

    b. To me, when I buy a car, I dun look at how expensive it is. I ask myself whether I am emotionally and financially stable to accept that price. If yes I go ahead. A first hand car need not has high downpayment, in fact the downpayment part can be considered non issue in first hand or 2nd hand becos both can be almost the same anyway. Therefore I declare that buying first hand is always better unless the price difference is so great or you are getting a damn good deal (my fren's fren sold him the Nissan Sunny 6 mths old at just 55k which he bought at 68K, to me thats a good deal lah.). WHY? becos u never know what problems u have in a 2nd hand car. I have experience that. Buying a 2nd hand car can be good or can be bad. If you can live with that risk, then sure! If not, u better buy a first hand to ease ur worries.

    c. I am not sure abt warranties with parallel importers. But just a food for thought in buying from parallel importers.

    Will they stock parts for you? Even so, will they be cheap?
    Anything happen to ur car, you think servicing will be easy?

    The thing abt PI is that when ur car spoils or anything happens, it gonna get expensive and the authorised dealer are not going to help u man cos this is not their car.
