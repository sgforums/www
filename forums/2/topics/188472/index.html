    Originally posted by Tayysj:
    whats a VIN
    VIN is Vehicle Identification Number. Can say it's your car's serial number.

    its a 16 digit number that you can decode. Note that vehicles meant fro Japanese market do not follow this system. Other than that, the world mostly stick to 16 digit.

    But North America's 16 digit will contain different info than other parts of the world 16 digit.

    first digit/alphabet (some cases 2) is the Country it is made. Numerals 1 to 5 are reserved for North America, generally, 1 = USA, 2 = Canada, 3 = Mexico. 4 and 5 are shared between these countries

    J = japan, A = australia. W = Germany, most asian countries start with P or M and are usually 2 alphabets.

    So if you ordered a supposed Japanese Camry, but your VIN is MHXXXXXXX
    it means you just got a Thai Camry.
