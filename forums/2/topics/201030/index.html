  • sir_boris
    20 Jul 06, 15:58
    instead of chasing by brands, should look out for

    1. specification -- as in how much IR rejection, and transmittance. most will have 99% UV rejection. the highest priced ranges a vendor give are those with high IR rejection. this is to reject heat.

    2. workmanship -- this is utmost important.

    3. guarantee on LTA compliance for light transmittance (min 70% front, 25% rear)-- this has to be when installed, not the film itsef. some cars already slightly tinted.

    4. no interference with radio signals - as in HP, IU.

    so do shop around, see which shop you like... and do it there.
