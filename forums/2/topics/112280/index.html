    SSDC PDL FAQs
    You can apply for PDL at the Traffic Police Counter @ the Driving Centres..It costs $25 and is valid for 6 months from the date of issue. As far as the CENTRE state that you shd apply for PDL when u start to learn driving, it is most likely that you will be learning your 1st few lessons within the circuit. So, best is to ask the instructor too...

    As for how to book the practical lessons, you should check with the driving school or Private Driving Instructor that you have engaged..Normally, school have quite a comprehensive online booking system.

    I get what you are thinking...The PDL is not exactly a license for you to drive just like that...You can start to LEARN how to drive after BTT. The person sitting next to you must be a TP approved and registered Driving Instructor or School Instructor and you must put an "L" plate in front and behind the vehicle..
