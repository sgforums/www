    Actually the instructor should teach you how to do them bf the test. U need to pratice more to gain confidence.

    I use to remember the method by hard.
    " In( right), out(left), out in"

    1. In - Turn the steering fully to the right. Once u see the pole at the centre of the car, stop.

    2. Out - Turn the steering 1 time out . Reverse until car is in the centre of the line

    3. Out - Turning the steering 1 time out. Reverse until car is into the parallel parking lot.

    4. In - to straighten the car.

    To bring the car back,
    " 1/2 In, 2 out"

    1. 1/2 In - turn steering and reverse a little.

    2. 2 Out - Turn steering and move forward. U should be on the road now.

    U have to take note of the environment around such as road conditions.
