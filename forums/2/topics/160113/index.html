    If you want to buy a new car, first thing, see how much are you willing to fork out. What will be your budget be.
    next, choose a few model in the price range
    think how long are you gona drive the car
    With tight budget, you can either go for looks or durability but not both unless you willing to fork out much more.

    First day u make payment for the car u already losing money.

    Normally for first 2 yrs, will lose 15k on average. Which is excluding road tax and insurance u are going to pay.

    Those second hand car which is around 1-2 yrs old are mainly, the owner cannot tahan liao or they wan to get rid of car fast(lose more $$)
    Normally, if you are taking a 70% car value loan. After 3 yrs u go and scrap the car will still get around 10k back after you pay off the mortage balance which you can use to invest in new cars. (for 7 yr loan there is a 3 yr bond which cannot break or pay $$ or lose benefits)

    For a normal 1.6L car, get ready to spend around 15k-18k a year including tax, installment,petrol, season parking etc etc. If you are earning 4k amonth with not liabilities, you will be safe. LOLZ

    As for car choice, it would be best for u to go down and test drive it yourself. Individual got their own preference.
