    galvanising is the base coat on bare metals. its the last line of defence, to prevent rust in case the paint and the top few coats fail. however quality differs between makes, some coat it thicker some thinner.

    on top of galvanising are a few more coats like primer, paints etc etc. some good cars like volvo has a total of like 12 coats, dunno what the march has, need to find out.

    toughseal and some other brands are sacrificial paint protection, it protects the acrylic coat of finished paint so that the gloss look of your new paint can stay longer. however it will diminish over time, different brands claim different duration and performance, some can last like 18 months (!). check them out in their websites or ask the marchies who have tried it.

    also can see www.mycarforum.com to read about protection and detailing, very informative.

    as for reverse sensor, if you are a new driver and no so confident then pay the $200 lor. for me i think its not necessary, the march is a small car[/url]
