    ESPLANADE THEATRE AND CONCERT HALL

    PARKING

    Esplanade - Theatres On The Bay
    810 lots

    Marina Square
    1,500 parking lots
    Six to eight minutes' walk to the Esplanade

    One Fullerton
    400 lots
    Six to eight minutes' walk to the Esplanade

    Suntec City
    3,002 lots
    10 to 15 minutes' walk to the Esplanade

    Millenia Walk
    About 1,200 lots
    10 to 15 minutes' walk to the Esplanade

    EATING

    Colours By The Bay
    #01-13 Esplanade Mall
    Enjoy 15 per cent off your bill at Al Dente Trattoria, Local Bite and Embassy Bar and Restaurant when you present your Arts Festival ticket stub.Beijing La Mian Kitchen and Shima Aji are offering a 10 per cent discount off their a la carte menus, and you get a two-for-two deal on lunch and dinner main courses from Mondays to Thursdays at The Garlic Restaurant.

    Harry's@The Esplanade
    #01-05/07 Esplanade Mall
    You can wine, dine and enjoy jazz at the bar. Open till 2am on Fridays and Saturdays, and 1am during the rest of the week.

    California Bistro
    #01-11 Esplanade Mall
    Situated on Level 1 of the Esplanade, this is a convenient place for pizza, cakes and pastries. Happy hours from 4 to 7pm daily - a mug of Tiger beer costs $5, and a glass of housepour costs $5.50. Open till 1am on Fridays and Saturdays.

    Max Brenner Chocolate Bar
    #01-06/08 Esplanade Mall
    Indulge in a wide range of chocolate treats. Open till midnight on Fridays and Saturdays, and 11pm during the rest of the week.

    VICTORIA THEATRE, VICTORIA CONCERT HALL AND THE ARTS HOUSE

    PARKING

    New Parliament House
    260 lots
    Five minutes' walk to The Arts House, Victoria Theatre and Victoria Concert Hall.

    EATING

    Indochine Waterfront
    1 Empress Place, Asian Civilisations
    MuseumIndochinese and Western cuisines are served at this restaurant facing the Singapore River. Dinner hours are from 6 to 11pm.

    Jade
    1 Fullerton Square #01-02 The Fullerton
    Those with a generous budget can dine at this modern Chinese restaurant. Dinner is served from 6.30 to 11pm.

    Cafe Society, Stage Door and Restaurant 1827 Thai
    The Arts House
    1 Old Parliament Lane
    Show your Arts Festival ticket stubs on the day of the performance to enjoy 15 per cent off food items at these three outlets. Singapore Arts Festival artists enjoy 15 per cent off food and beverage items. Restaurant 1827 Thai and Stage Door open till 11pm daily, while the last order for food at Cafe Society is at 10.30pm.

    Coffee Club
    Present your Arts Festival club membership card, ticket stub or artist pass to get a 10 per cent discount. Available at all branches, including the ones at Millenia Walk and Raffles City.

    UNIVERSITY CULTURAL CENTRE THEATRE

    PARKING

    The UCC carpark is under renovation, but up to 1,000 spaces are available at the Kent Vale, Sports Complex and University Hall carparks nearby.

    EATING

    Dome Kent Ridge
    University Cultural Centre
    10 Kent Ridge Crescent
    Open for another hour after shows end, so you can grab a coffee or a cake snack.

    DBS ARTS CENTRE

    PARKING

    Robertson Walk
    While DBS Arts Centre does not have a carpark, its neighbour, Robertson Walk, has 356 spaces.

    UE Square
    Also a stone's throw away are the 900 parking lots at UE Square.

    EATING

    Robertson Walk
    Almost all dining outlets at Robertson Walk, including BarCelona, Bistro Chameleon and Cappadocia, offer 10 per cent off your bill when you present a ticket stub for any performance at DBS Arts centre.

    En Japanese Dining Bar
    207 River Valley Road #01-57 UE Square
    Open till 3am on Fridays and Saturdays, and midnight during the rest of the week, you can have a leisurely drink to go with your meal after a show.

    SINGAPORE CONFERENCE HALL

    PARKING

    184 spots are available at the Singapore Conference Hall's carpark.

    EATING

    Starbucks Coffee
    8 Shenton Way B1-12/13 Temasek Tower
    Grab a frappucino or a muffin before heading across the road for a night of music at the home of the Singapore Chinese Orchestra.

    Hawker Centres
    Killiney Kopitiam at Singapore Conference Hall is open till 8pm on weekdays.Maxwell Food Centre, Amoy Street Food Centre and Lau Pa Sat are also within walking distance from Singapore Conference Hall.

    JUBILEE HALL

    PARKING

    Raffles Hotel
    About 300 lots are available at the hotel's carpark.

    EATING

    Seah Street Deli
    Raffles Hotel
    Hearty American fare comes complete with jukebox music in this eatery. Open till 11pm on Fridays, Saturdays and eve of public holidays. Open till 10pm during the rest of the week.

    Long Bar
    Raffles Hotel
    An extensive drinks menu awaits at this bar. Open till 2.30am on Fridays and Saturdays, and 12.30am on other days.

    Bar & Billiard Room
    Raffles Hotel
    You can choose to have tasty seafood or cocktails at this dining outlet. Open till 12.30am daily.
