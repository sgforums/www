Was driving towards CBD area yesterday in the afternoon and the weekend traffic was pretty quiet then. The male biker struggled to get up on his two feet, holding his hand, seems that he was hurt and was using one hand to press on to the other hand which was bleeding.

    He had lost control by himself and the whole bike just topple over.
