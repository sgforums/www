    Originally posted by KamenRider:
    think somebody asked this ques before. the march's wing mirrors are fully convex and thus can distort distance estimation and hamper your safety assessment. so when you can see the cars on your side in the wing mirrors, DONT change lanes cos they are already very very near your sides.

    safe bet is change lane ONLY when you can see the cars in your REAR view mirror.

    some better wing mirrors have 2/3 flat and the most outside 1/3 is convex. this lets you know there are cars on your side and yet can judge distance properly.

    i also almost kanna accidents few times due to this problem.
