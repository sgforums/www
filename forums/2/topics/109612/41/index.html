RoyCindy, WOF & KKT left us as they had some other businesses going on. this time the convoy planning was pretty fast, without much deliberation, we left for Changi at 5 plus, everyone was like experienced convoy old bird already.

Cpt Goh continued her oohing and arrhing in my car as she saw so many heads turned to look at our colourful convoy along nicoll highway and ECP. while i was pretending "aiyah-this-is-nothing-lar-its-common-for-a-march-to-turn-heads" but in my heart i was quietly shouting "well what are you waiting for?!!"
