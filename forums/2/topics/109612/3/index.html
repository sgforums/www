    the "wooing" sound is the operating of the DC brushless motor when you steer the steering wheel. its an electrical steering and not a hydraulic steering. it should be very very soft when you are steering under low speed at least the tyre noise should be loud enough to cover the steering noise. but you can hear it when you steer under stationary speed. if it is irritatingly loud enough then you shd see the mechanics.
