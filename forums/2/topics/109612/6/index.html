    Hallo all.........

    Juz want to share my experience with my recent accident....so that you will not have to waste unnecessary time travelling around like mad man..... juz like me (spending 3-4hrs go round and round)

    If your car is insured by AIG....you not necessary to go to idac to report......actually it will charge you as AIG does not belong to idac members....provided the other party is insured by NTUC.....(there are other 3 company also not in idac list...one is AXA and can't remember the others)
    In fact....you could directly go to any TC workshop and report it, they will able to help you to take pictures and submit report also......and quote you directly the price for repair............. much faster and easier......

    If the person decided to have personal settlement.......it will be best that the $$ is collected first before the car is repair......otherwise after your car is done....you can't do anything if the person declare he has no money to pay.......(good people is easy to be ..... ) this is the advise given from TC workshop as they had seem many such cases..........

    Hope this information could help if any (touch wood) but really not
