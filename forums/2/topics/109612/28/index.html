    So it depends on what you want. I wanted clear visibility all round but with maximum UV protection. This is the UV Protect Clear. The price is standard in all outlets at $500 but I have got a 10% off because of AA membership. This package use the JS83 film and include a 'sunvisor' film (much darker shade) for the front windscreen which I declined because I want clarity. JS83 film gies 99% UV protection and about 31% heat rejection and 83% light transmission. If you pay more, you will get higher heat rejection but darker tint. Practically, I can't feel the difference between films that reject 31% and the top of the range 65%. This is because the difference between my package and other higher price packages is the film on the rear screen only. Check this out at www.vkool.com.sg.
