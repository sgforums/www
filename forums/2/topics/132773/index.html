    I feel it's really a preference. As in, if you prefer to learn it systematically then school would be good. The one big advantage about school is that, you can book your practical test date early and the chances of you passing your theory is much higher, also, retest waiting period is shorter.

    Hm, as for the standards of instructors, oh well, the chances of school to pass TP test seem higher than private learners.

    Hm, overall, i think if you're not concerned too much with costs, as in you're not tight on budget or what, go for school. People go for private cos it's cheaper, they place cost as like the top 1 factor.

    So yeah, it's all a preference, an experience. To each of its own.
