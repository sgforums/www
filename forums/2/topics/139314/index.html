    Exceeding speed limit of the road by more than 20 km/h up to 30 km/h.
    DIPS: 6 Points.
    Fine: Light vehicles - $150, Heavy vehicles - $180.
    Court fine if you go to court: $500 -$1000

    The waiver of demerit points and reduction of the fine amount is fixed and not negotiable.

    If payment is not made by 12 noon on the court date, no payment of any form will be accepted and court attendance will be compulsory.

    Decide if you want to go to court and explain to the magistrate, or pay the fine and close the matter.

    Don't whine, please.
