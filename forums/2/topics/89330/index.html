    Bugfest
    28 August 2004 - 30 August 2004

    Venue | Danga Bay, Johor Bahru

    If you are a fan of the Beetle, the car not the insect, make your way to Danga Bay for the ever-popular Bugfest.

    Volkswagen lovers from Singapore, Thailand and Malaysia congregate at this festival to appreciate and recognise fellow Beetle owners. Competition for the signature title, Battle of the Beetle is stiff. Witness the inspiring and painstaking efforts initiated by car owners; adding creative themes to decorations and paintwork, bedecking their cars with VW memorabilia as well as superb maintenance of the aged cars. Also up for grabs are titles such as Beetle of the Year? Best Modified VW, Best Maintained VW and others.

    2004 marks the 10th anniversary of the event and this year the organisers have upped their notch of expectation and are targeting the participation of 600 cars.

    Accompanying friends and family have a whole host of activities to choose from for entertainment. Among them Tug of War VW style, Teh Tarik Competition, Karaoke Singing Competition, Arm-Wrestling Competition, Children Colouring Contest to name a few.

    The enthralling activities, the carnival atmosphere and the camaraderie amongst the Beetle lovers, are destined to leave you with cherished memories. So mark this date on your calendar and head on to Johor Bahru.

    The production lines may have ceased but the car lives on.
