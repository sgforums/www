    i assume that u are telling the truth.

    the letter of fine from court should also indicate a court date. if not, make your way to subordinate courts located at Havelock road and lodge a complaint with the magistrate on duty. there is only one way to get outta the situation now. fight the case in court. go to the judge that day and tell him the truth on this matter (u have to swear to tell the truth before you begin speaking anyway).

    if he buys your story (which i think holds some water knowing that there are indeed some unscrupulous IOs out there), than he'll fix dates for your case to go on trial for the IO's inappropriate actions in forcing a confession outta you.

    pros: if you win, u and ur bf dun have to live with the fine and a record with the tp which will prevent u from driving for some time.

    cons: u'll have to spend money to engage a counsel to fight your case for you in court or die die use ur words against the officer of the case in court (not advisable since you mentioned that u are timid). u may still lose the case and end up paying more for the lawyers fees.

    its a mistake on your part as much as the police IO's to have succumbed to the threats. it would have been much easier to prove in court that the car wasn't moving at time of the alleged offence in the first place than to prove now that the IO had forced u into signing the statement. nobody should be coerced into signing a statement which they do not believe to be the truth.
