    Your monthly costs is usually based on how much loan you took on your car.

    The rest are basically:

    >> petrol
    >> season parking (home & office)
    >> monthly savings for anual roadtax & insurance
    >> car servicing every 7,000-10,000km (app. $200 or $400 for branded ones)

    On the whole, you should expect a basic amount of $1,500 covering your loan, petrol, and parking. To be on the safe side, $2,000.

    But it would be a lot lesser if you have bought the car in full cash payment.
