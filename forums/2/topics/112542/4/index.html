    Actualli wat is the noticeable difference between a type s and a type r

    lots of tell tale signs, but knowing singaporeans, they'd go to incredible lengths to make their type s look like the real mccoy.

    brake: R has brembos up front. but S owners can purchase caliper "covers" to "bremborize" their brakes too.

    seats/ steering and shifter: the R comes equipped with factory recaro reclinable bucket seats and leather MOMO SRS steering wheel, the S does not. most S are auto, so thats a sure giveaway. the R comes with a 6 speed manual transmission with LSD.

    exhaust: R has a intricate exhaust system with a significantly larger tip than the S which utilizes a closed port to create low end back pressure and open port during high end vtec operation.

    rear wiper: with the exception of R with the factory spec C option (PIs usually import non spec C as the additonal parts that come with the car incurs taxes, thereby raising their cost), all S comes with a rear wiper.

    body kit: all too easy to imitate, so i shouldn't even list it as one of the distinctive factors.

    other signs are more subtle, and would take a trained eye to really tell the diff.
