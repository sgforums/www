    GSR is the export version of Type S in Japan. They are the same except for the emblems. Engine is K20A3 with vtec cross over at 2200rpm and short ram exhaust cross-over at 4800rpm; with VTC on the exhaust side. FC is good for a 2L at about 11km/L. Century timing of 8.7s is for the five-speed steptronic gearbox which is a lightened design; of coz the manual version will be much better; good launch will be below 8.2s. Celica only the SSII version is good but already discountinued. Apple to apple; Type S creams SSI, Type R creams SSII. The celica SSII represents toyota first foray into VTEC technology but as it seems, its too complicated for them to be profitable. (Honda's VTEC patent has already ended; but only BMW and Porsche able to adopt them successfully). The 1.8L SSII engine represents the best in class with VTEC and VTC both on the intake and exhaust side; Honda's K20C and K20A2 lacks VTEC on the exhaust; but still one hell of an engine.
