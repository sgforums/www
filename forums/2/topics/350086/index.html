    Car insurance dun insured ur life. Get a accident or life policy.

    Most car insurance insure third party drivers nowadays. Meaning u can drive her car. In case u got into any accidents, insurance will still cover the car. However, the excess for third party drivers are usually very high. For your friend, the excess may be $500. But if it's 3rd party, excess coulb be as high as $2000.

    Note: Excess are amount of cash the driver have to pay before the insurance pays. Example, if repair cost is $1200, and ur car insurance policy excess is $500, U need to pay $500 and insurance company pays the reminding $700. Hence if your excess is high, (example $2000) the insurance company wun pay for any repair amount below $2k.


    Another point to note is that in doing what u are thinking of doing, you will not be able to accumulate your own No Claims Discount (NCD) and Safe Driver Discount. Your NCD will increase usually every year by 10% when u do not have any accidents. Iff u have a NCD of 50%, your car insurance premium will be cut by half. Thus in future when u do get your own car, you'll have to start from scratch.
