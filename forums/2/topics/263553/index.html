  • DcR
    26 Jun 07, 11:12
    1. look around the car, make sure no dents, scratches etc. check the seats for any marks / discolouration.

    2. if sales person was supposed to include free stuff or you ordered extra stuff like reverse sensors, make sure they are installed, if not, make a note.

    3. open the bonnet, check everything looks ok. of course, we arent mechanics but just see if anything looks amiss. i've heard sometimes where mechanic leaves say a cloth lying in the engine bay or something by accident before ...

    4. test all your systems - radio, air conditioning, headlights etc

    lastly, please remember to bring a cashcard and some parking coupons with you, just in case! (thats for ERP or parking etc ... dont be caught w/o it)
