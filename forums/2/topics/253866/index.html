    Basically,

    Standard Clutch ---> Sports Clutch ---> Racing Clutch.

    The racing clutch, my advice is not to use it for daily driving. The design is not for half-clutch. Not a good idea if you need to stop half way on a slope frequently.

    Just upgrade to a heavy duty one, usually we call it Sports Clutch.

    It can withstand more abuse and will not slip easily compare to a Standard Clutch after some spirited driving.

    So the available power can be transfered more effectively.

    About less than $1.2K (depends you need the clutch cover etc).
