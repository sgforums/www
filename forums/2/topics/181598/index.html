    Dude, it's a cycle... now COE rise, all the car companies raise their car prices by $2k. So that in the next bidding, they will all bid $2k higher for their COEs, so the next COE will be another $2k higher than the current one. The car companies will respond to that by raising the price a further $2k more, and COE will rise by another $2k in the next next bidding... and so on.
