    hmm....out of the small hatch class...like:
    Honda Jazz/Fit
    Mitsubishi Colt
    Nissan March
    Renault Clio
    Toyota IST
    Mazda2

    Verdict:
    Nice look go for: Honda Jazz/Fit
    Fierce look go for: Renault Clio, Mazda 2
    Performance go for: Non
    Driveability go for: All but Nissan March
    Reliability go for: Toyota IST, Mazda 2 and Honda Jazz/Fit
    Economy: All are around the same although Renault Clio parts are more expensive
    Comfort ride go for: Renault Clio, Toyota IST and Honda Jazz/Fit

    My Comment:
    Honda Jazz/Fit tend to understeer very early.
    Renault Clio and Mazda2 provide the best road holding or traction.
    Never drifted any of those so i skip handling.
    For pure torque for overtaking, Renault Clio is the one.
    For the best driveability, Mazda2 is a good choice follow by Renault Clio.
    Nissan March is a toy-car.(my personal opinion, no offence to March owners)
    Don't know much about Mitsubishi Colt.(never tested & never liked Mitsubishi cars. maybe someone else can post about the car.)
    Renault Clio provide the best ride, the car is heavy compare to the rest and it has the continental feel.
    Toyota IST suspension is damn soft and comfy, but road holding is sacrifice.
