    For the same make and model, the manual ones definately goes faster. You can make the car reach a certain speed faster by revving hard before you change to the next gear.

    For auto, the gear box decide when it wants to change gear to reach a higher speed. So sometimes, you may have stepped hard on the accelerator, but the engine does not respond as fast as you want.

    For Manual
    Pros - Can reach a certain speed faster
    - Consumes lesser petrol
    - Can push start (usually in 3rd gear) when battery is flat
    - Cheaper maintennace because you dun need transmission fluid (needed
    for automatic transmission
    - Dun have to worry about transmission problems (esp with electronic
    parts)
    Cons - YOu left leg must be in good condition (no injuries) in clutch in
    - YOu left hand have to change gear
    - Tiring at traffic jams (very common in Spore!)
    - YOu must control biting piint when at slopes (esp in carparks)

    For Auto
    Pros - Comfort (No.1 reason for automatic cars)
    - Your left leg can rest (You bai kah left leg aso can drive!)
    - No worries about roll back
    - Ladies love automatic transmission
    - Its better for resale

    Cons - Consumes a little more petrol
    - You can't push start when battery flat
    - One more item to change during servicing (transmission fluid)
