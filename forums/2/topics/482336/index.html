ERP not in operation yet still deducts money from cashcard

  • FireIce
    4 Apr 14, 16:12

    Stomper Chiang, who specifically times his commute to avoid ERP gantries was still charged even though it was before 7 am.

     

    The Stomper was driving along the CTE and passed the ERP gantry before Braddell Rd at around 6.55 am with no deduction, but two minutes later, he reached another gantry.

     

    Even though it was before ERP operating hours, and the gantry was not in operation, $1.00 was still deducted from his cash card.

     

    Chiang expressed his concerns on how well the ERP gantries are monitored, as if it happens to even more people, it could be more than just one dollar in question.

     

    He later called the LTA about this, and says that they are now investigating the matter.

     

    The Stomper said:

     

    "Firstly it's a bit questionable, how can you be sure that they are deducting the right amount each time you go through the ERP?

     

    "The trouble you have to go through to get the money back is ridiculous.

     

    "Even if they just deduct 50 cents, the principle of it is still not right."

     

    STOMP
