    tamiya enzo is not suitable for beginners. however, so far tamiya enzo is of the best quality and details. i cant describe to you how hard to build a enzo, but for sure you will need lots of patience and steady hands especially when the doors and engine hood can be opened using fragile hinges etc. 1 mistake may affect other parts of the car which may result in fitting problems or worse the car may sit on 3 wheels only.
