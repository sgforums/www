    1) SG: Zhng
    Meaning: Tune or modify
    Usage:" Today I went to Johnny's garage to Zhng my car! Put spoilers, install sports Rims, etc ...

    2) SG: Chie
    Meaning: Challenge someone to a race
    Usage:" Today an Ah Beng in a WRX tailgated me at the PIE and zoomed passed me quickly! It seems like he wants to Chie me, so I chased after him!"

    3) SG: Orh-Kong
    Meaning: Fine/ summon
    Usage:" I got Orh-Kong today for Speeding

    4) SG: Sup
    Meaning: Taking a turn at a high speed
    Usage:*at the Kallang Carpark Rally*" Wah! Look at that Evo Sup that tight corner!"

    5) SG: Cheong (pronounced as Chee-yong, not Chiong)
    Meaning: Imitation
    Usage:" what! $80 for a Carbon Fibre Hood?! Confirm is Cheong one!"

    6) SG: Langar/Buang
    Meaning: crash
    Usage:" I heard Ah Beng Buang his civic today at Orchard Road..he was busy staring at Ah Lians while driving!"

    7) SG: Zhum
    Meaning: Slam your foot onto/kick
    Usage:" As I was driving my car outside a school, a stupid kid suddenly dashed across the road. Luckilly I zhum my brakes in time and avoided an accident!"

    8 ) SG: Tuah
    Meaning: Drag
    Usage:" Dun Tuah your engine and faster shift to a higher gear la! U wanna spoil your car is it? "

    9) SG: Boh Cheng Hu
    Meaning: Total disregard of the rules/laws
    Usage:" Look at that car speeding through the zebra crossing when the AH Mah was trying to cross it! Some drivers really Boh Cheng Hu!"

    Add on to the list, people!
