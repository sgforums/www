    Hello!

    Nowadays....

    - It usually takes 2 weeks to receive the TP's thank you letter (sometimes a bit sooner, sometimes a bit later). Sometimes never arrive also, if you're very lucky (did you see the flash?). Understand, however, that its considered a serious offence - 12 points + possibility to be charged in court (although I think hardly anyone actually goes to court for it unless an accident happens).

    - I heard of people getting warning letter for speeding recently, but only if they were over the speed limit by a bit (1-20 kmh), probably closer to the 1 kmh side). I believe the letter also mentions that if the person gets another warning, they may decide to serve a summons instead.

    - Having demerit points does not affect road tax. If you don't have demerit points, you may get additional 5% off insurance price. Otherwise, it doesn't affect NCD or insurance at all.
