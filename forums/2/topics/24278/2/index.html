    Challenge (ver.2)
    During the race, you can press the Start button to switch between accepting or declining any challenges. If you switch to decline, nobody can challenge you.

    Music selection
    When you are at the level select screen on time attack or in story mode, press the view chage button and in the bottom left corner you will see the track name of the song.

    Race in the Dark
    To race in the complete dark, simply hold the start button when you are in a night race for about 5 seconds and your car's light will be turned off! Really cool effects!

    Special matches
    After you win all the 16 racers you will have to fight Takumi Fujiwara on the last course, if you win him, you will have a free match to race against Bunta (Takumi's Father) in Akina.

    Unlock the GC8V in Ver. 2
    When on the screen with the various makes ( Toyota, Honda, etc. ) enter the following code:

    Move the shifter up, up, down, down, up, down, up, down, then hold the brake and press view change.

    If entered correctly, there should be an engine sound. Select Subaru, and the GC8V is now a selectable car.

    Note! The GC8V has NO tune.
