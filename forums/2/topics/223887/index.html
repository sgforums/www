    YES! SAVE THE EARTH!!!

    Most pple hv the wrong concept about turning off the air con. There are many components to the air con.
    1)the blower (the one that is blowing out the cool air)
    2)the compressor (the one that is producing the cool air)

    the compressor can be controlled by the A/C button on all cars. hence instead of turning up the temperature when the interior is too cold, you could just turn off the compressor (which is the component that uses the MOST electricity and hence increases fuel consumption)

    You could even do so at traffic lights!

    Dont worry, you wont suffocate inside as long as you turn it back on!

    **by not using the compressor, you are saving up to 10% of your fuel consumption.

    We could also save the environment by NOT leaving the engine RUNNING while you wait for pple!!!

    Let us all pls do our part to save the environment!
