    Engine wise Civic bit better, pick up slightly faster (if both cars compared are manual geared). But sounds a little noisier at high revs. Fuel consumption similar.
    In terms of handling Civic is more enjoyable to drive, being a little more responsive at the wheel.
    Space is pretty similar, tho the Civic as a flat floor behind, bit better for middle passenger.
    As for looks i think Civic looks better stock, but Lancer looks better with the visual upgrades.
    But in terms of cost, Lancer is a much better option.

    17 Feb 05, 19:56
    Displacement same, Lancer 1584cc Civic 1590cc.

    Not sure abt gear ratio, but the 2004 auto Civic is a normal auto transmission (not CVT like earlier 2001-2003 models) so step-down response bit better than Lancer's CVT (which some ppl don't like...sluggish, slipping clutch sensation etc). 0-100 abt even, Civic 11.5 sec to Lancer 12 sec.

    But if considering manual, Civic more pleasant to drive, gears shift more accurately. Stiffer clutch than Lancer, tho not like Rex one (like doing weights).
    However for performance better to take the EK series, the current model like a kitten compared to a tiger.
