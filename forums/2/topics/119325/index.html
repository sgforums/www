    It isn't 276kW. It's 276hp or 280ps, which is the Japanese agreement for AWD cars. It is 250ps for 2WD cars. And torque figures are off too. Max torque is 40.8 for the regular titanium turbo, and 41.3 for the new mag titanium turbo, both reaching max torque at 3000rpm. The MR titanium turbo reached 40.8 at 3500rpm. 4500rpm would be very bad turbo lag.

    So it looks like the agreement is still in effect, if only on paper. Technically, though, it is just a simple ECU reflash to regain the lost horsepower since all of the new cars (STI, Spec C, Evo 8/MR etc) are all capable of much more.

    And no, I have seen some dealers quoting less than S$150k for the 9 GSR, which is considerably lower than what the 8 and MR initially listed at.
