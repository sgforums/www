    Discs brakes were invented long after drum brakes.

    They were invented as a result of research for formula one racing.

    All the expensives cars have disc brakes on all 4 wheels.



    The big round shining part is the disc. It has holes for ventilation to dissipate heat.

    That block at the back of the pic is where the brake pads are housed. Using hydraulic action, the pads are pressed against the disc. The pads are like the bread in sandwiches. The disc is like the ham inside the sandwiches.

    When the pads press against the dics, the spinning discs stop and that stops the car.
