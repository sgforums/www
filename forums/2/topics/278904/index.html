    The all now Pagani Zonda is out, and this time it's called the Zonda R. Guessed Tommie Goh bought his Zonda F too early.

    This new Zonda R has 750bhp from the same V12 AMG engine. A big increase over the Zonda F's 594bhp. It's nearly entirely made of carbonfibre and it has a rubber fuel tank, roll cage and a fueler cap (much like a GT car) that can refuel faster than normal fueler cap. This is to reduce pit stop time when racing at a track. YES, it's track-ready yet it's also road-legal.

    It also has a longer wheelbase than the Zonda F and also a wider track, so centre of gravity is lower. There's also a HUGE carbonfibre wing at the back to generate tons of downforce at high speed.

    It'll lap the famous Nurburgring in 7 minutes and 32 seconds. It's priced at a cool Â£700,000. That translates to S$2.1million without ANY tax yet. Plus tax, expect it to cost at least S$5-6million locally I guess.
