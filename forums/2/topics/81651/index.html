    When come to buying new or old, some year back I also got this headache.... eventually I chose to buy new....

    few pointers.... coz new car.....

    (a) reliability and maintaenance headache free (at least for 3 years)....
    (b) since new and u are the first owner, u have the control over how u want to "treat" it.... u will know the car inside out.....
    (c) u will have the new car smell for a few months.....

    Older car....
    (a) unknow history.... maintenance history....accident history....
    (b) reliability... if u are new to car and no knowledge of car mechanism, u are in dead trouble if the car breakdown on u.... the mechanics will "keto" u.... u will spent alot....
    (c) confidence.... u may lose confidence if the car frequently fail u....
    (d) the 2nd car dealers are like "tai-yee-long".... unless u are good, many of us are prone to "con" by them..... higher sellling price... additional cost which u din anticipate when u pen the paper.... many horror stories....

    Of course, new car.... higher cost...higher depreciation.... but u can be assure of reliability....

    so it depend on wat u want! Cheers!
