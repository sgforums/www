    A suggestion... most people dun calculate "reality checks" like that. you might want to try for:

    price of car - parf value (not omv value) divide by 10.

    this gives you depreciation per year.

    add insurance per year + multiply fuel costs by 12 + $1200 for servicing, small repairs, etc etc, + road tax + car radio fees + parking + ERP + fines.

    this one you vary vary yourself. you know what kind of driver you are and can adjust accordingly. Fierce and crap = more on servicing and accident bills not including fines. Be honest.

    add the depreciation by year to yearly maintenance and then divide by 12 lor.

    monthly cost. ta-da
