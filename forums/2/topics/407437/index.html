    Hi brothers,

    After I got ERP fines due to cash card not insert or cash card value not enough, I begin my search for a better way. ERP timing and rate keep changing quarterly. It depends on whether it is Saturaday or Monday, day or night, from $0.50 to as high as $3. Singaporeans are getting more fines as more ERP gantries are built. Finally, I spend 7 days and create a free app for every phone ( Nokia, iphone and Android etc) to live in "harmony" with ERP.

    By pressing a button, you can know your "favourite" ERP rate at the moment. Green means free. Yellow mean about $1. Orange equals $2. Red is the highest. Another button, you can locate all ERP nearby with your phone GPS. I am making it out of my free time and will make it free for anyone to use.
