    On E3:

    Tg Kupang Toll Plaza
    Gelang Patah (Route J4)
    Lima Kedia Toll Plaza
    Senai South Exit (Route 16)
    then turn left and up to E2 liao.

    On E2, just go all the way for 298km, 2-lane road, keep left unless overtaking. At the end of it you will reach Sg Besi Toll Plaza and you will be ushered right into KL proper.

    On your way back, try to get any signs that say KL-Seremban Expressway or E2.
