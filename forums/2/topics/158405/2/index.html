    It has been there for a long time (as long as the 18 yrs I have been staying in Yishun). Used to cycle there before the roads were constructed to that place to catch crabs in the swamps. Canoed once onto the reclaimed land located next to the private airport.

    Can't remember when they opened up the road from Yishun Ave 1 to that place, but now with the opening of Seletar Camp to link Jalan Kayu to Yishun Dam, it has become a short cut for many people who are travelling from North to Northeast.

    A nice place to BBQ if there's a slight breeze, minus the couple of mishaps (drowning) that happened a couple of months and years back.
