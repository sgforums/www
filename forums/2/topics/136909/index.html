  • nakata10
    16 Jun 05, 01:37
    Myth 1: All car SEs earn big bucks!

    Truth: Only those who work for the best-selling Japanese/Korean brands or German brands earn big bucks. Just check out the number of car SEs working for other brand dealers or parallel importers that don't even own a car.

    Myth 2: Car SEs are overpaid!

    Truth: Most car SEs only have a miserably low basic (<$500) and depend on the finance/insurance/vehicle/used car/accessories commissions to earn a living. If every customer were to squeeze their SEs to return their commissions in the form of giving freebies, then what will the SEs earn? The actual ones making big bucks are the car distributors/dealers, not their SEs. Moreover, car SEs are wearing multiple hats: car salesman, insurance agent, used car dealer, bank officer etc.
