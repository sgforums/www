    The grounding and the voltage stabilizer actually distribute the electricity evenly around the car. Meaning if there suddenly any one of the equpiment (eg, air-con, speaker bass, etc) need a peak in electricity, the stabilizer supply it without cutting down on other equipment needs.

    Without grounding and the voltage stabilizer, you may see a sudden dimmer in your lights or speakers.

    Another advantage is that you will have clearer & stronger speaker tone as well as brighter headlights....

    It will not affect the warranty of the vehicles.
