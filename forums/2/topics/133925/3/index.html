    So how does it handle? Wanted to test drive it when I went looking for the Altis.
    I used to drive an Alpha 156 selespeed. In comparison, the IS's pickup is not so hot, doing 11+ sec from 0-100kph compared with the 156's 8+ sec. The steering is also not as sharp nor as well weighted but still quite good.

    However, the IS's weight is better balanced due to the rear wheel drive and cornering is a real joy, without any sign of understeering which is clearly noticed in the 156. The ride is also softer on the IS but still quite sporty.

    I must say on the whole, I missed my 156 but I sold it because it was starting to give me some problems after some 4 yrs on the road. I am hoping the built quality will be much better in the IS......
