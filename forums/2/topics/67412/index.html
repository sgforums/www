    ha, me another '99 sunny(m) driver. yeah, I think sunny tends to be seen as a 'slow' acceleration due to the heavier body, but the positive side is the stability.

    as for consumption, i think it's decent. i average about 14km/litre... and if you asked me to trade in my car, i'll go for a second nissan, coz it's damn cheap. a 3 yr old model typically sells close to paper value, a price which you can't get for a honda/toyota/mitsu.

    personally, this is how i sum it up:
    toyota - superb reliability, durability, solid fuel saving BUT boring
    honda - solid performance but fragile body
    mitsu - good looks, ok fuel consumption, light body
    nissan - lousy acceleration, average consumption but very good stability and strong in a collision
    mazda - ok performance, stable body, boring interior (except the newest range of mazda 2, 3 and 6)
