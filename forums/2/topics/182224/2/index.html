    In my shopping list, there were three cars (Golf2.0, Sonata2.4 and Civic2.)
    - Price: Golf>Sonata>Civic
    - Exterior Design(my wife's consideration): I like all of these cars
    - Ponny and Torque(This is minor factor though): Sonata>Civic>Golf
    - Build Quality (based on other's opinion): Golf > Civic >Sonata (however sonata also as good as the others)
    - Show off factor: Golf>Civic>Sonata
    - Value for money: Sonata>Civic>Golf
    - Dimension of car: Sonata>Civic>Golf
    - All of these cars are FF

    Anyway civic was one of the 1st car i do not feel strange/odd at first with premium car's feeling.

    My purchase experience:
    - Booking: the 1st week of this year
    - Delivery: 17March
    - Cost factors (Interest rate 3.25pa)
    . List price: 85,000
    . Financial Rebate by Kah -4,000
    . COE rebate(contract amount: 12,000) -2,400
    . GE Money (banking business sector of GE, 3month free installment) -1,800

    There were three available models (1.8vti, 1.8vti-s and 2.0Si)
    - 1.8vti seems best buy (Cost and Fuel Economy with same functionality) in Sg
    - but my wife liked factory built in audio of 1.8vtis and 2.0Si
    - there was small price difference between vtis and si (I found this is tricky)
    - Moreover i do not need cruise control, fog light, silver finish of doors (but side airbags and Vehicle Stability Assist (VSA) seems good option)
    this is the reason I've chosen si auto if i were sole decision maker, i might have chosen 1.8vti (for your reference). Anyway it's kinda sad thing that we can't configurate optional parts, which eventually we have to pay more to have unnecessary things.

    I may update my driving experience in a few months time
