    17 Mar 05, 01:33
    Originally posted by geniez:
    i always come across the word "excess" when checking e rates for car rental... wat izzit actually? do u pay in advance?
    Excess is the amount you have to pay before the insurance starts to cover.

    E.g. Excess is $1000, means that for the 1st $1000 damage you have to bear the repair cost, insurance will not pay.
    Insurance will only cover and applicable if damage is more than $1000. For instance, damage cost $3000, insurance pay $2000, you pay $1000.
