    Incident happened on : 1/3/2004 @ around 2045hrs in Jurong Point Carpark.

    I was to meet my friends in Jurong Point so I decided to drive there. I drove there, found the Jurong Point Carpark, went into the 3rd lane from the left and stopped at the barrier.

    I wind down my window and was going to press the "ticket issuing machine", well, I never expect it to be a cashcard reader. I was like... WTF!?!? Realising I have no cashcard with me, I quickly reverse up the slope and enter the 1st lane from the left, thinking that at least 1 lane should be accepting cash payment.

    But ALAS!! I was wrong. I stopped at the barrier, switched on the hazard lights and called my dad, hoping that he'll tell me he's got an extra cashcard somewhere in the car, but no.

    Feeling anxious, I got out of the car(hazards still on), was trying to get a fellow driver to lend me his cashcard, but feels too pai seh to do so. Luckily, I remember a security guard by the carpark entrance and quickly seek his help. I paid him $2 and he opened the barrier. He also informed me tat I dun have to get out of the car to get him, just talk to him via the comm(@ the cashcard reader) and he'll get to me.

    Sooo pai seh... I was like a country pumpkin, dunno anything. Hell, when did this "cashcard carpark entry" applied?? I didn't know a thing bout it. *slaps forehead*
