    Originally posted by HENG@:
    EVO7 has "EVOLUTION VII" badging across the boot. EVO 8 has none
    Depends. In the UK non-FQ models there is the "EVOLUTION VIII" lettering. On JDM models there is the word "Evolution" on the bottom right of the trunk lid, while on the USDM models it's on the bottom left.

    Other differences between 7 and 8: front bumper, hood, spoiler, clear tail lights, Enkei rims. And different color schemes.
