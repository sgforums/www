    im not accusing anybody here nor have intention doin so, im juz bringin the fact that trying to install a supercharger/turbocharger is not allowed in singapore or rather impractical. ur uncle's Vito came with a turbocharger from its OEM thus it is LTA approved. its not aftermarket installation of turbocharger.

    read this line:[b]The addition of superchargers or turbochargers to a vehicle may adversely affect the safe operation and its exhaust emission characteristics.
    so do u really think our vehicle installed with turbocharger or supercharger can easily pass the test? it'll probably need to go through so much paperwork and $$$ that it'll make it impractical to install and probably with that money, people might as well buy a TC car.[/b]
    Superchargers are allowed, if they are OEM but not aftermarket installation. Even when they are OEM approved, you have to go through rigorous tests by LTA to make sure that the other components in the car are compatible for the addition of the supercharger, meaning they can support the extra load etc. It's not easy and it's very troublesome, most likely you will need the advice of a professional.

    But whether it is impractical due to the paperwork, money and trouble involved, it is really up to the owner to decide, since they may feel it's worth everything It's their car after all.
