  • Koon King
    11 Sep 04, 12:25
    2nd hand car prices usually include COE.

    OMV = Open Market Value (i.e. the value the manufacturer quotes from the factory). All sorts of taxes are than based on this value. Go check out the LTA website on the policies. You should have a understanding of all these before buying a car, especially for 2nd hand cars because the tax structure changed in the last few years.
