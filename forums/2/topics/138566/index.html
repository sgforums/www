    Main thing to look out for is the expiry of COE and the condition of the car (any accident previously etc...). Go for a test drive of the car at low & high speed to feel if the engine is ok.

    Dun even consider buying this car... not worth it...

    - no scrap value as > 10 years old
    - no parts warranty as > 10 years old
    - interior components probably needs to be replaced ...
    - low mileage indicative of accidents or spoilt engine that need overhaul
    - modified... sure a lot of problem....
    - often get stop by TP.... check exhaust sound level... sure exceed....
    - COE of $18,444... pro-rated means for the remaining 6 yrs is ~$10.8K... but asking price min $30K.... definitely not worth....
    - road tax much higher for > 10 yr old car...
    - insurance higher because race-type / not sedan car
    - not 3rd party insurance because > 10 yrs old

    DO NOT buy... throwing $ away......
