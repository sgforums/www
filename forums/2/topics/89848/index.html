    Nevertheless, Rumie, I believe with your budget, you can only go for a SS1 instead of SS2. But then, how much are you downpaying? 0% is quite impossible. Maybe lets take it as 10k downpayment, excluding insurance and rd tax etc.

    To work out your sum, lets say a 3 yrs old Celica SS1 cost 70k (est. from the car in the web), so after 10k downpayment, you loan abt 60k for 7 yrs. So your interest will be S$1620 a yr (based on 2.7% for 2nd hand car), and a total of S$11340. Your total loan is 11340+60000 = 71340 ........ yrly will be 71340/7 = S$10191, and a mnthly of 10191/12 = S$850/- .

    So thats roughly what you pay per mnth for installment only. Of cause, after 7 yrs, when you scrap your car, you will get back abt 20k. So your depreciation per yr is abt S$7.1-7.2k.

    But I have to remind you that your insurance will be very very expensive. Hope my calculation helps.
