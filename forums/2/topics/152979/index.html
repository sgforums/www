    Considered Kelisa. It have similar FC compared to Picanto, but a slightly more power in the low-end (low speed) range.

    Back to your question. I would go for Picanto if I am down to these 2 choices.

    Pros:
    5 door vs 3 doors.
    Manual option available.
    Great FC if you know your footing.
    Much cheaper to maintain. (road tax, fuel, up-front price)

    Cons:
    A little smaller cabin and boot space than Getz.
    Slightly weaker in the quality side.
    Smaller wheel base, stablity compromise.
