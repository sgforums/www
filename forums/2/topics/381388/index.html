    You will have to drive more. Focus & feel the movement of the car rather than focusing on the techniques. Techniques are dead.

    If the speed is too slow the car will surely jerk because the car is already at a stopping speed. You can free wheel the car at such speed but make sure your foot is at the brake pedal.

    Once the clutch engage you will have to accelerate if not the slow speed will stall the engine.

    At the end of the day, feel the movement of the vehicle. Do not put all your focus on the techniques.
