    relax dude...nothing to worry...

    there are only 2 yellow lights on the indicator...

    one is for the fuel level

    the other one that i'm sure is showing right now is the engine sensor. if u turn off the engine and on again the light goes off??i experienced that not to long ago. if that is the symptom, then continue reading...

    if u open ur hood/bonnet...somewhere to the left of the oil dipstick area there is a sensor attached to the engine...looks like a sparkplug with a wire attached to the tail. that's ur problem.

    at first i thought it was serious...took my car to Kah Motors...thought they were going to do some complicated, computer board programming, sophisticated repairs...later...found out that they only changed the sensor...

    so my suggestion would be to take ur car to an ordinary workshop...or ur favourite workshop...tell them u suspect that that sensor is faulty...but do let them check for other things also, just in case i'm wrong...

    hope that solves the mystery of the yellow light...
