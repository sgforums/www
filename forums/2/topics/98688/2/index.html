    Yes, the VIII was launched last year.

    Differences (off the top of my head..)

      Aluminium roof (reduce weight and lower CG)
      Aluminium side impact beams (reduce weight)
      Bilstein suspension
      tweaked SAYCII (vs SAYC for VIII vs AYC for VII)
      Dual solenoid controllers to the actuator for faster boost
      Turbo dimensions different 10.5T (used in MR and TME) vs 9.8T (in VIII)
      different cams (not sure of the exact differences)
      improved torque at 40.8 kgm at 3500rpm
      same bhp

      Other frills
      Darkened (CF effect) trim for interior (vs blue trim)
      Darkened/brushed steel Momo steering wheel (vs metallic steel in VIII)
      Headlights and Tail lights have darkened inserts (vs chrome in VIII)
      Black Recaros (vs black wif blue trim in VIII)
      lightened BBS rims option


    No, MR is still using 4G63 block first used in the original Evolution 1 (and the prev Galant VR4)
