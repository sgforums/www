    XinYi,

    80% of the singapore cars on the road are having COMPREHENSIVE insurance, which I believe is a must get insurance cos you never know what's gonna happen on the road when driving.

    Yes, the insurance is under your father's name but there'll always be 2 slots for NAMED DRIVERS. So, you can put your name as one of the named driver and you'll get the same benefits as your father.

    Authorized driver without putting the exact name will have to pay a slightly higher excess if they were to claim insurance.

    So, when buying the insurance, just asked the insurance agent to add the name in for you on the paper. As long as your name reflected in black and white, you are safe.
