    with the exception of small parts that are not subjected to any aerodynamically induced stress during travelling, almost all parts in a bodykit would require you to use some screws or brackets to attach it to the main frame of the vehicle. the only difference would be the accuracy of the fittings. OEM kits tend to fit better. Fake or imitation kits would depend alot on the manufacturing process and may need modifications (drill extra holes, sand and cut) in order to fit properly.
