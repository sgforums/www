    Improved aerodynamics (new front and rear bumper design)

    The MIVEC in the 4G63 seems to be only cam-phasing (similar to Toyota's VVTi) and it is only on the intake side.

    VTEC is cam-changing.

    Honda's iVTEC and Toyota's VVTL-i has both cam phasing and changing.

    Cam phasing isn't as manic as VTEC but the MIVEC-enhanced 4G63 does deliver better low end and mid end torque, traditionally a bane for turbo cars.

    You get the peak torque at 3500rpm.

    With the 9, you can see some noticeable gains from 2000rpm, and peak torque is at 3000rpm

    You need a way to release the boost when you go off throttle. Stock turbocharged cars usually use a recirculating diverter valve. So you don't get a loud pssshhh sound.

    Most guys change to a vent-to-air VTA blow off valve (BOV) which gives that sound. It mucks up the MAF sensor on stock Evos tho. And there are no gains... unlike what the other guy says.. it does not reduce turbo lag.
