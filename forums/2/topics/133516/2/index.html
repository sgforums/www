    Originally posted by D16Z6:
    is there a diff between SMG n manual???
    tot SMG also manual mah...
    the M3 is 6 speed manual right???
    SG M3s come with SMG-II standard as the transmission unless you specifically say so in your order form. In fact, the M3s in SG come with almost option possible as spec by PML. The only other option that i have chosen for my 05 M3 was navigation.

    SMG-II is Sequential Manual Gearbox. It can be put in FULL-auto and driven exactly like an auto car, or it can be put into SMG mode. SMG mode is whereby gear changes are done like in an F1 car with the paddle shifts behind the steering wheel. The gearstick can also be used to "+" or "-" gears.

    6 Speed manual comes as standard in the US and in Europe i believe. This is the normal manual gearbox that will come on any normal car.

    The E36 M3 was using SMG-I, the E46 M3 SMG-II and the new E90 M3 and current E60 M3 are using SMG-III which is the new 7 speed Sequential Manual Gearbox system.

    Hope that i have answered your question.

    Cheers!
