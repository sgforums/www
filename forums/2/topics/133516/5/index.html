    Originally posted by LH83:
    How do you spot a fake M3?
    1) Powerdome on the hood (Can easily replace the hood)

    2) Side gills (Will have to cut a hole in the front fenders to put gills)

    3) Super flared wheel arches (Unless bodykit is modified to "widebody", very hard to copy)

    4) Quad exhaust pipes (Can be copied but expensive)

    5) Black window trimming (Not a very good indicator)

    6) ///M3 badge at the back (Badge can be bought easily)

    So from the above i'd say that the easiest way to spot a M3/Wanna-be M3 would be to look @ the flared wheel arches and also if it has side gills. But if the owner is willing to go ALL OUT to make his car look like a ///M3, anything is possible.
