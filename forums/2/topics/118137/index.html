    Not taking price factor into consideration, the standing are as follows (Stock):-

    1) Honda Integra Type R - 2000cc , 220bhp
    2) Toyota Celica SS2 - 1800cc, 180bhp
    3) Honda Integra GSR - 2000cc, 160bhp
    4) Toyota Celica SS1 - 1800cc, 145bhp

    Believe price is in order too............ Celica will have cheaper road tax by ard $300 a yr i believe, and have more after market body kit then the GSR. Of cos, performance wise Type R is still the king among the 4 but believe the price diff between the Type R and SS2 is more than 10k.

    Insurance wise, the top three belong to the PERFORMANCE CAR category and the SS1 is under coupe, so definitely cheaper, which you might want to consider.

    Hope this information helps. Lastly, head that the new model of Celica will be out later this year.
