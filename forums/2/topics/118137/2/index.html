    Celica GT-S is USDM (US domestic market, engine is detuned for lower octane rated fuel). JDM celicas howver have 190bhp on tap..as quoted from the australian toyota site which imports JDM celicas.
    /////

    As you'd expect, the Celica has an impressive engine. The powerful 4 cylinder, 1.8L twin cam engine with intelligent Variable Valve Timing and Lift (VVTL-i)is nothing less than Toyota's latest technology.
    This is not so much an engine, more a rocket. It's bred for driving excitement, with an explosive 141KW of power and a hefty 180 Nm of torque.

    2ZZ-GE engine
    A 1.8 litre Alloy twin cam multi-valve 2ZZ-GE multi-point sequential EFI engine with VVTL-i, making this sophisticated engine powerful, yet fuel efficient.

    The 2ZZ-GE engine is an in-line, 4 cylinder engine with two intake and two exhaust valves per cylinder.

    VVTL-i makes the Celica a car with brains as well as brawn, a car which instinctively drives better. It lets you fully assert all Celica's power, and it even gives you better fuel economy.

    So how does VVTL-i work?
    Toyota's advanced technology optimises efficiency and air flow by varying open and close timing of the intake valves, giving you more power in every gear.

    And it does more:
    By increasing the intake valve lift when the engine speed is high, VVTL-i allows greater volumes of fuel air mixture into the combustion chamber for increased power.

    In addition, Celica is fitted with Toyota's Direct Ignition System which provides a powerful and clean spark while lowering vehicle emissions.

    Celica has a highly developed 6-speed manual transmission, to give the car a sporty, belongs-on-a-racetrack feel. For better shift feel and control, this transmission adopts double-cone syncro for the second gear.

    And the four speed automatic transmission employs the latest technology.

    The automatic transmission, features Electronic-shift (E-shift), allowing you to choose between the standard shifter in the centre console or twin switches located conveniently on the steering wheel.

    Celica's suspension adds to its sheer drivability. There are Independent MacPherson struts at the front, and at the rear a double wishbone type suspension glues you tightly onto the road. A toe control function helps both operability and stability. Everything adds up to a car which is easier to handle, more stable and more comfortable than ever.
