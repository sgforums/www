    There are some things regarding COE cars you should know (if you still don't):

    - Many COE cars have almost no resale value, so you're probably stuck with it.
    - COE cars have no scrap value (except for the remaining COE rebate).
    - Condition is unlikely to be that good (there are exceptions, if you're lucky).
    - Parts may be a difficulty (depending on model and part).
    - Road tax is 10% more expensive per year, capped at 150%.
    - Fuel consumption is usually high due to old technology and wear and tear.
    - You need to bring it for inspection every year (S$100-200 each time).
