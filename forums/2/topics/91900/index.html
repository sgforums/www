    Most important thing is to think safety. I can give you a few scenarios, hope it helps.

    1. Cars behind you are tailgating u.
    - Dun attempt to speed up, keep to your right speed and keep left
    - instructors can fail you for driving too slow

    2. In situation where you just turn right at a junction and wanted to keep left, but the cars on the left are not letting into the left most lane.
    - Stay in your lane then.
    - Speed accordingly to avoid behind vehicles from horning u

    3. In conjunction with (2). You need to go straight and the intructor has intructor had instructed u to go straight too. But in the right lane, u can't keep left and the lane u are in needs to turn right.
    - follow the road and turn right
    - even u drive out of the intended course, the instructor won't fail u.
