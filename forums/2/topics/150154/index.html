    SINGAPORE, 29 August 2005. Effective from 6:00pm today, retail pump prices for Synergy petrol at all 77 Esso and Mobil service stations in Singapore will increase by nine cents a litre.

    The following prices will apply to our various grades of petrol:

    Old Pump Price New Pump Price
    Inclusive of 5% GST Inclusive of 5% GST
    Product ($/Litre) ($/Litre)

    Synergy 8000 1.837 1.927

    Synergy 5000 1.754 1.844

    Synergy 2000 1.722 1.812

    Synergy diesel remains unchanged at $1.17 per litre.

    This increase in petrol prices is primarily due to high crude oil and wholesale petrol prices. The price of crude oil is at record high levels. In the past year, retail prices have not kept pace with the sharp increase in wholesale product prices. We have tried, for as long as we can, to shield our customers from this increase.

    We continue to offer a 6% petrol discount at all 77 Esso and Mobil service stations with an additional 4% discount available to all Smiles Members. Furthermore, Esso MasterCard, Esso Mobil Privilege Card, Esso Mobil Easipay Card and Mobil Easipay Card and Esso Mobil Fleet Card customers enjoy a supplementary 1% petrol discount.

    Source: http://www.exxonmobil.com/AP-English/News/sg_news_pump_prices_290805.asp
