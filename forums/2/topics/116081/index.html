    That guy only provides spraying...he DOES NOT mend your scratches.

    if your rims are scratched and it is NOT originally chrome, you can try Yew Lip at Sin Ming Industrial Park. They will use putty to mend you scratches, smoothen them out and then spray them again.

    They can do for chrome too..just that the result is that there is no paint like chrome so end up that scratched part will be painted silver..

    barely noticable but if you have a lot of scratches...then your chrome will be replaced with silver.
