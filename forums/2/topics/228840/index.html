    First, find the radiator drain plug.

    -drain off all water in the radiator.
    -open the radiator cap.
    -flash with water using a 1.5L bottle.
    -see the color of the water flowing out of the drain hole.

    Next
    -buy a small bottle of coolant.
    -Mix with water, read the instruction label if not sure.

    For me, I pour at least half into the radiator. After topping up with coolant, close the cap. You can also get a new cap at any petrol shop ( very cheap nia ).

    Starts engine and observe the fan. Must be runnning. Also check the engine oil level ( before u starts the engine ). Check the temp level go up or not.
