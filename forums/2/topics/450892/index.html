    SINGAPORE: Certificate of Entitlement (COE) prices for cars rose sharply in the latest bidding exercise on Wednesday.

    The Open and big car categories saw the biggest rise in premiums, rising S$7,420 and S$7,300 respectively. The Open category closed at S$92,010 while the big car category closed at S$91,000.

    The small cars COE went up S$5,700 to S$64,201.

    Premiums for commercial vehicles rose S$3,600 to S$57,589.

    The COE for motorcycles edged up S$28 to S$1,924.

    -CNA/ac
