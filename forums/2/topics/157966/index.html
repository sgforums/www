    Easi-ERP

    Currently, when drivers receive an ERP CashCard violation notification, it is likely due to negligence or their CashCard has insufficient store value. The ERP CashCard violation will be issued immediately the next day by LTA and payment must be made via SAM or cheque.

    Such payment methods are time consuming and troublesome.

    Easi-ERP, a joint service brought to you by SingTel and LTA, allows SingTel Mobile customers to pay for their ERP CashCard violation charges through their monthly bill.

    With "Easi-ERP", a SingTel mobile customer who owns the vehicle will receive an SMS alert to inform him/her of the ERP CashCard violation. Instead of the $10 administrative fee PLUS the respective ERP charges, Easi-ERP customers will enjoy a discounted rate of only $4 administrative fee PLUS the ERP charges. A one-time processing fee of $1 will be charged to your SingTel Mobile bill upon successful registration.
