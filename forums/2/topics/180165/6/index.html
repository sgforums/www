    so, now that u know some of the technicalities and tips about driving, u reckon u can give it another try the next time u go there?

    Just remember these 10 points:

    1) learn the correct line

    2) slowly find a braking point that allows u a good entry speed and is not too garang.

    3) experiment to see which gears each corner should be taken in

    4) always get your shifting down, done before u enter a corner

    5) While in a corner(unless its a gentle curve), do not excecute a gear shift even if u discover u're not using the optimum gear for the corner.

    6) shift up only upon exit of the corner

    7) learn the apexes of the corners, especially in relation to any corner before or after it if it belongs to a series of corners close together

    start straightening the steering as much as u can once u've passed the apex

    9) if u are going to oversteer, oversteer on entry. oversteer on exit just wastes time

    10) if u're understeering, lift off and tuck the nose in before u step on the gas again
