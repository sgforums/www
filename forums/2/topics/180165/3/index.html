    Hi,

    Basically u need someone frm SG trackies to recomend U into their group, before u can join them for the trip up to sepang.

    The fee for whole day, 9am to 4pm is abt $230 sing. U need also around $70 to pay "gate fee", from spore up to sepang and vice versa.

    Its an expensive hobby, as i spent close to $500, not including doing servicing of the car, like changing engine oil, gear oil, coolent, tyres, alignment,etc before going up.

    Its a long drive up there, abt 3hrs, at and average speed of 150 - 170km/h.
    Very shack.

    A general rule of thumb, always check your engine oil (better to change it before u go up), and prevent wracking your car more than 3 rounds in sepang. Best to keep it 3 rounds, unless u got oil cooler. my friend had his engine overheat, left 1/4 of engine oil and had to tow his car back frm sepang, spenting alot of $ to repair his engine.
