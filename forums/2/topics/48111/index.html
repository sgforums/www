    Okie, I already check and the procedure is as such, just to share with all.

    1) You have to go to one of the authorised motorcar workshop to file a report and accessment.
    2) This particular workshop will then feedback to your insurance company.
    3) After that the insurance company will open tenders to all their authorised workshop.
    4) After a few days, they'll then review the tenders, see who lowest and ask you to go there.

    The bad thing is if the tender of the lowest is in Tuas area, and you live in Pasir Ris, you'll still have to go!!! Wow piang, sianz man. Hopefully it won't be that far off. Somemore needs a few more days. Jia lat.
