    Let's say a driver drives 6 days to and from work, x 52 weeks = 312 days. And let's say this driver drives 30km per way (e.g., Woodlands to town, Changi to town, Jurong to town). Thus, annually, the driver travels 312 x 30 x 2 = 18720km. Thus, I would say that annual mileage in the range 15000-18000km is reasonable, whereas anything more than 18000km would be considered excessive.
