    From my observation, there ae 2 types of org / individuals which do car renting services.

    1. Car rental companies
    2. Individual which rent out the car (we call them non-companies here)

    Car rental companies are more strict to their policies min 22 yrs and at least 1 year driving experience. Most of the things will be stated in b&w including the charges, excess (amt u have to pay shd u get into an accident), etc
    Non companies are usually more flexible (some dun check for age/ min driving license- just have a driving licence will do). The problem comes with it is usually the excess which the non-companies might ask you to cover most / all the damage if insurance companies don't pay. So you really have to check the contract (if there is any) carefully.

    I have rented car from car rental companies before and average price for a 1.6 car is about $70 - $90+ / day

    Non companies can be found at newspaper classified (vehicle for hire) and probably on online auctions too. Average price from what I observed is around $50 - $70 / day. Slightly cheaper. I have not tried non-companies yet, the above was told to me by my friend who experienced it.

    Willing to take the risk? it's all up to you
