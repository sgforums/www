    Info provided by Nick34 :

    Class 4 and 5 Driving License
    1.Where can I apply for Class 4 and 5 driving license?
    You can apply for Class 4 and 5 driving license at Pan Country Driving Centre.

    2. Where is Pan Country Driving Centre?
    The driving centre is just by the side of Jurong Stadium. Road name is Fourth Chin Bee Road.

    3.How much will is cost?
    There is a package that allows you to enroll for class 4 and 5 together at the cost of about $750. If you are just going for the class 4 driving license, it will cost you about $450 just for one license.

    4.What is the difference between class 3 from class 4 and 5 ?
    The difference is mainly the turning radius and the braking. The turning radius is wider then a normal class 3 vehicle as it is longer. Braking system used on Class 4 and 5 is different from class 3 as they are using air brakes. You must get used to the air brake if not you gonna jerk your way throughout on a class 4 or 5 vehicle.

    5.What isthe procedure to getting a class 4 licence and the duration? <-- Asked by williamtan
    The procedure is the same as you enrol for a class 3 driving license and the duration is about 1 - 2 months time before you actually have your first practical lesson. So alone class 4 will take about 3 months and the same for class 5.

    6.Can I book for all practicals one shot?
    Yes you can. Even if you wish to change the practical date, just give them a call. Forms of contact can be found in the Links section below.

    7.What is the min. requirements for class 4 and 5 ?
    You must be 21 of age and must not have more then 12 points in your Driver Improvement Points System(DIPS)
