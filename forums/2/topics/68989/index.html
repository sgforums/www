    I used to cycle there at various hours of the day when i was doing my NS in Nee Soon Camp. Very secluded stretch with a slip road that leads to the reservoir service facilities. After rounding one of the bends the light disappeared, i still think of it as a ah pek who stopped and parked his bike to go durian hunting .... after all there are lots of durian tress there ....
