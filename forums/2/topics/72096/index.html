    The prescribed legal limit for blood alcohol content is 80 miligramms (mg) of alcohol per 100millilitres (ml) of blood while that for breath is 35 microgramms (mg) of alcohol per 100 millilitres (ml) of breath. Any person caught driving with blood or breath alcohol content wich exceeds the prescribed limit will be charged in court for drinking. The penalty for drink driving is a fine between 1000S$ and 5000S$ or imprisonment not exceeding 6 months. there is also a mandatory 1-year isqualification in respect of the offender's driving licene. A repeat offender will face mandatory jail sentence not exceeding 1 year.
