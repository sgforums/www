    Hi Jack,

    Sorry to inform you that you may face problems trying to sell this car in Singapore, unless there are people who wish to keep their cars in Malaysia.

    1. LTA states that no car above the age of 3 years can be imported into Singapore for registration (Unless special conditions)

    2. Singapore Citizen or PR (Malaysians) are not allowed to drive foreign registered vehicles into Singapore (ie. Malaysia Plates).

    These two concerns may be the biggest barrier for you. Nevertheless, you can try calling LTA hotline or check out http://www.onemotoring.com.sg
