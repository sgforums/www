    If $50k is your absolute max, then I would say the best value cars are:-

    1. Kia Rio
    2. Hyundai Getz
    3. Toyota Vios

    Depends on where your priorities lie but my personal choice would be the Getz as I find the Vios a bit boring shape-wise and I have doubts over the Rio's build quality.

    I have test driven the new Getz 1.4A and found it to be reasonably responsive. Downside was the ride was a bit bouncy. Went over some undulations on Alexandra Road ata round 80km/h and had to slow down coz the car started to bounce too much.

    If you want a bit more performance then the 1.6M is a bargain at around $47k.

    I'm also in the market for a new car but as my budget is $60k, I'm planning to get the Swift 1.5A at $53,500. Excellent ride/handling; good refinement and nice interior. Only downside is the Auto box is a bit sluggish.

    Yes, FC is important but if you consider that you save about $10k by buying the Getz vs the City, that will buy you quite a lot of petrol!

    If you assume:

    1. City's FC is 15km/L and the Getz is 10km/L
    2. You do 15,000 km per year
    3. Petrol costs $1.55 per litre

    Then the Getz would cost you $775 a year more in petrol costs. But over 10 years you still end up saving $2,250 by buying the Getz.
