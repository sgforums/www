    That's a turbo timer. Since most turbos share the same oil as the engine, the purpose of the turbo timer is to keep the oil circulating in the engine and around the turbo bearings for some time, allowing it to cool more, especially if the car had been driven hard. Otherwise the oil, especially if it's mineral or semi-synthetic form, will coagulate and coke, leading to premature turbo failure.
