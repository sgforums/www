  • critter33
    27 Jan 06, 11:10
    I used to drive a '90 Starlet, it was one of the more memorable cars I've driven.

    FC is good, abt 12 - 13km/litre, mine had a 'taxi' gear, super loose and easy to slot around,... ... like jacky chan in thunderbolt.... hahaha

    But most memorably, my starlet saved my wife, my father in law, my sister in law and my life once... we survived a side-on crash by a kuku lady driver at Pasir Panjang abt 4 years ago, the starlet was sturdy enough to withstand the bulk of the impact without causing any major injuries in us, phew...

    I always belive that if it was any other car of the same size, we would have been dead...the kuku was driving really fast, trying to beat the red light...

    dynamics wise, it is fun to chuck around corners but remember that you will have to fork out money for a few items... Air-con, suspension dampers and check the engine bay for signs of previous damage (esp radiator).

    I will never forget driving the starlet at 100km/h and feeling as excited as driving my galant at 140km/h...

    Drive safe, get well ok.. and stay clear of kuku female drivers...
