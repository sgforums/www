    i'm currently driving a 1 mth old Lancer Sports Manual. Dunno how you guys managed to get FC around 15km/l. Must have some super secret fuel saving driving method. For me, the best I've managed is 14.3, but on the average, I hit about 13.4km/l, which I'm quite happy with.

    The following are just MY opinion:

    LANCER (Pros)
    1) Spacious interior
    2) Good FC

    LANCER (Cons)
    1) Recent PR hoo-ha (i dun really care)
    2) uses 4-year-old technology (GLX)

    VIOS (Pros)
    1) Speedy pickup/acceleration
    2) New modern technology (VVTi)
    3) Probably higher resale value

    VIOS (Cons)
    1) Cramped back space (i mean, very cramped)

    Vios is good if you're driving alone, or with 1 front passenger most of the time. Quick pick-up, sporty, lots of aftermarket stuff etc. But if you need to ferry friends, family etc and use the back seats very often, you'll have to seriously consider the mild "discomfort" of the cramped rear.

    Eventually, I picked the Lancer.
