    Originally posted by digicharat:
    then what is consider a clean record?
    tio carpark summon, forgot to put cashcard when passing erp summon still will have clean record?
    Clean record for NCD refers to no one claimed your insurance policy (Third party claim) for 5 years

    After 5 years of clean record ... you will be granted 10% discount on your insurance premiums on the 6th year and 20% on the 7th till you get 50% d/c ( meaning 10 years of clean record)
