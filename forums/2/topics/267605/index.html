    Truck fell into Lim Chu Kang sea

    Police have found a body of a man in the sea of Lim Chu Kang.

    It's believed to be that of the man whose truck slipped into the waters near the scout campsite earlier today.

    The deceased is Goh Chin Went, a Malaysian man who worked for supermarket chain, Seng Siong, said Goh Eng Lai who's a supervisor with the company.

    He added that the 2 survivors are Yong Chee Seng and Chua Kim Swee.

    "Three of my men were working at the back of the truck when 1 of them noticed that it was moving into the water. He sensed some danger and this prompted 2 of the men to climb onto the roof of the vehicle. One was left behind. The 2 survivors shouted at him to get out but he panicked and didn't. People from the nearby fish farm came over and rescued the 2 survivors."

    Mr Goh said that all the men involved are in their twenties

    Police say that they will be contacting the victim's next of kin.
