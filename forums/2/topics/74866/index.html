    If you want to avoid door dents, don't park next to any other vehicles, farthest away from any. Maybe spare one side by looking for the last space in any row, so one side always spared. Otherwise always park on the side of the road along the footpath and not in side by side lots. This will save both sides all the time unless some idiot bangs their bicycle or watch or diamond ring against your door! Also avoid parking next to work vehicles like lorries, delivery vans , station wagons, 4 wheel drives or taxis. These can cause some really nasty door dents and scratches. Don't forget also the very close parking lots in HDB estates and multi-level carparks and some stupid idiots who cannot stand admiring your newly bought or often polished, shiny car and are bent on damaging it in jealousy.
