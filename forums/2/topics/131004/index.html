    Come 14th May, join us for an afternoon of adrenaline pumping, heart-stopping action when we launch the new Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution IX.     Action Super Star in Town!
    Meet Jackie Chan, the legendary action movie hero in town as he introduces his limited edition of Evolution IX.     Judging of Best Dressed Car Contest
    A total of at least 15 cars will be on grand display. These cars are the finalists from the internet voting contest held on The Evolution IX. See what these owners have done to make their car super stylish.

    Meet the babelious Race Queens in their seductive outfits and cat poses. Watch them sashay around all the turbo-charged cars on display and parade in the latest Ralliart collection apparels at the fashion walk.
