    From Straits Times Interactive

    CHECK SUMMONS STATUS ONLINE

    FROM next Monday, the 1.2 million people here who hold a driving licence will be able to go on the Net to check the status of outstanding summonses against them as well as any demerit points they have.

    Anyone applying for a photocard licence can check the status of his application, while foreign drivers can find out if they have committed any traffic offences here.

    About 300,000 summonses are issued to Singaporean and foreign-registered vehicles every year, said a police spokesman. About 5,000 issued last year have yet to be settled.

    The public can access the Electronic Driver Data Information and Enquiry System at www.ecitizen. gov.sg or www.spf.gov.sg
