    Answer to:

    1. The ECU is always learning and relearning, adjusting its parameters to the conditions. It is not a ONE OFF thingy. U cant train it to keep fuel consumption low. The Air Fuel Ratio and Ignition timings are more or less standard and is made to run rich in stock ecu.

    2. 98 RON monimum for Scooby, not less.

    3. While running in, vary your speed and do not drive at constant speed. Change gear regularly to put your engine under different loads. Do not exceed 3K rpm in first 1K and 4K rpm in the next 2K. Use mineral oil up to 3K. After that, synthetic.

    Hope this helps. See u in Rex Factor.
