    Hey, so sorry to everyone who has been waiting for my reply, especially Lambo. I've been really busy and out of town lately.

    Most Motormax diecast models can be bought at all Toys R' Us stores, selling at $24.95 each. But depending on the store, there might only be certain models available for sale. As far as I can remember, I've seen (in 1:18 Scale) BMW 7-Series, BMW Z4 Roadster, BMW X5, Ford Focus ZX3, Volkswagen Nardo W12, Mercedes-Benz SL500, Saleen S7, Nissan Skyline GTR, Lexus LS430, Porsche 911, Acura NSX, Land Rover Discovery and of course, the Pagani Zonda C12 on the shelves. Maybe there are a few other models I've left out. I last spotted the Pagani Zonda C12 (blue) earlier this year, think around February. Guess it's sold out by now. But you can always check with the staff at Toys R' Us to see when is the next available stock. Personally I feel that the quality of Motormax diecast models is not very good because of the crude interior. But in terms of opening features (though not as detailed, e.g. engine bay), it is definately better than Maisto and Hotwheels.

    I personally have the Acura NSX 2003 model (I think this is the best model Motormax has produced based on its quality), Land Rover Discovery and BMW 7-Series (not willing to spend more $$ to buy the Kyosho model ). I bought those 3 models because based on my experience, I know that Motormax is the only company to produce the Acura NSX 2003 model and the Land Rover Discovery. I don't have much interest in the Pagani Zonda C12 model though but kinda regret that I didn't buy it when I saw it earlier now that its so difficult to find such a sought-after model car....
