    This particular model is manufactuer by CMC - designed in Germany and produced in China. It is in 1:18 scale and, as mentioned is very high in detail. The interior contains seats and steering wheel covered in genuine leather. However the wheels on the model are a little under-scaled as complained by several people who have bought the model, looking more like 17 to 17 1/2 inch to scale rather than the actual 18 inch items.

    Its a very nice model, but to tell you the truth the 500$ price is way above what it should retail for locally. They are selling in the states for 185$ US, which is around 300$ Singapore. The mark up, as you might note, is 200$ - I would say shipping makes up only 15% of that so you do the math to see if its worth that price Nikko tends to drive up the prices for these so called exclusive models, I guess its a matter of supply and demand!
