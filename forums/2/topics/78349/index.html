    Hi folks, just wanna share with u guys a freak accident along TPE sometimes 2 / 3 weeks ago along TPE.

    There are about 3 lanes along TPE toward SLE before CTE exit.

    It was morning about 7:15am, as I emerged from Punggol into TPE toward CTE, I noticed there was a Nissan Sunny SC# plate tailgating a Honda Civic SFF plate on the extreme right lane.

    I soon cut into the Honda Civic's path from the middle lane and was soon way ahead of the Honda Civic. After a while of cruising, I realised the Honda Civic was behind me. I then pulled back to the middle lane. Guess what was the next thing that happened?

    The Nissan Sunny behind the Honda Civic (still tailgating closely) pulled away from the Honda Civic and cut across my path diagonally to the left into the extreme left lane.

    There was a tipper truck 2 - 3 cars' length away from me! The next crazy thing the Nissan Sunny did was it then cut across the path of the tipper truck diagonally to the right and attempted to squeeze into the 3rd lane. That Honda Civic was at the 3rd lane!!!

    It side swiped with the Honda Civic causing the Honda Civic to brushed against the side of divider railing sending lots of shrubs' leaves flying.

    It side-swiped the Honda Civic from the left and stayed close in front of the Honda Civic.

    The Honda Civic immediately overtook the Nissan Sunny fiercely from the left and managed to send the signal to the Nissan Sunny to pull over to the side.

    What happened next did not appear in the papers...guess nobody fought.

    Crazy right? Can't believe my eyes such crazy drivers are on our expressways...I kept seeing SFF in my head....WOW SFF new car siaaaaaa.......kena brushed against that way by such crazy driver.....
