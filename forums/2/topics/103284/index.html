  • Troy437
    18 Nov 04, 22:06
    Last night, I was attending a function along Bras Basah Road and I was directed to park at NTUC Income Building carpark. There were road construction covering the NTUC building and I had to make one round to the back of the building. I thought it was the carpark for both NTUC building. But I had unknowingly went into the NTUC Trade Union Building, which is directly connected and next to NTUC Income Building. It was 7:38 pm then.

    At the function, I was given an complimentary ticket where no value will be deducted from my cash card. the function ended at 11 pm.

    At 11:05 pm on the same night, I went to collect my car. But the ticket machine did not accept the complimentary ticket. There were other cars who held the same ticket like me and we were all stuck for a while. We pressed the help button and the guard came.
    He then told us the ticket is for NTUC Income carpark which is the next building. I thought, OK, we were mistaken. Just pay with my cash card lor.

    I then got a shock. The machine states the parking fee is $22!!

    The guard then told us that there is a $20/- charge for parking after 11 pm!! He showed us a signboard where thw words are small. He says that he cannot help us waive the charge as the parking system is fully electronic. Most of us got down and went to the POSB ATM at level 1 to top up our cash card. SHIT !! And the carpark have a big sign that says "No overparking allowed". DAMN, why the $20/- charge?!

    Just a word of advice....take note and read any sign at any private carpark entrance before you drive in.
