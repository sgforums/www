    Any nice driving location to recommend?

    These are my current list :-

    Kranji Way
    Pros: Scenic drive, kampung like. Stop at Turut track to relax and enjoy the view.
    Cons: Coz there's farms around, there might be a lot of lorries.

    South Buona Vista Rd
    Pros: Challenging corners, mildly forested. Drive up the super steep hill of Kent Ridge park to enjoy a nice coastal view.
    Cons: Very short road. Alot of bikers like to 'play' on that road.

    Seletar Airport
    Pros: Scenic drive. Near TPE.
    Cons: Biker gangs like to hangout and 'play' at the nearby seletar dam (Yishun Ave 1).
