    Hi Ponders
    In a real situation, this will happen. Eg. dealer bids for you 11k but result was 14k. Before COE closes, if the dealer did not revise your bid to 14k, you are out bid, i.e. you cannot get a COE with the original bid of 11k.

    A sincere dealer will revise your bid to 14k if he wants to release the car to you. So if you bought the car at a COE of 14k, that is the COE price that will stick to your car for the next 10 years. If the dealer revise your 11k bid to 14k without sucking 3k from you, you win 3k , dealer loses 3k.
