    Other costs: (for my case Picanto)
    Downpayment $10k
    Instalment: $400 per month
    Insurance: $1.1K
    Road Tax: $500 - $600+ per year
    Season Parking: $60+ per month
    Petrol: $300+
    Servicing: Depends (1K, 5K, and 10K all definitely needs $$$), and let's not 4get about accidents, etc...
    Miscellaneous: Car wash ($6), Grooming accessories ($$$), etc.....

    Also for your info, with your current expectations, maybe you can try a COE car (>10 years old car). BUT the servicing will kill you. Plus, all the replacement parts is going to cost a bomb too! (Sometimes as much as $300-$400 for faulty parts such as air con compressor spoil, alternator spoil, etc) Speaking from experience, as my first car is a COE car. Sometimes, you may be better off buying a new car, really!

    Currently, earning $4k +, so I can say that it is still well within my ability to afford. But try not to be a slave to the car. All of us here geniunely hope that you know what is the sensible solution. What for you buy a car now, and end up no money for your own insurance(very very important!), no money to save for your wedding (who wants to marry a poor man? ), and no money to live in a HDB (unless your parents going to support you even after your marriage). You see my point?
