    The Ford Focus.Very gd build and steering.The car made in germany,assembled in Spain, with the exception of the white color which is build and assembled in Germany. The feel and build is solid.Dials and knobs have expensive touch and feel.More driver oriented console as compared to the Maz3. Its handling and steering capabilities has been vastly improved and for $78,900, u get ABS and ESP. Not to mention Regent Motors are pushing for a promotion where they will absorb $800 worth of installment every mth for 6 mths. So therefore u will save up another $4,800 on the focus.

    Megane Hatch.Very daring concept. But you might want to consider the lack of boot space if u need to lug huge stuff.The megane has a whole lot of features and sensors mostly found on high end cars. Refridgerated glove comparment,parking sensors,climate senors.keyless entry,card ignition,6 airbags steering wheel mounted radio and CD buttons and much more.
    For the price of the megane. its a whole bargain. the workmanship gd,quality materials used, and the very unique areoplane inspired handbrake.

    But i had got frens who bought the megane sedan,and within 2-3 mths. the keyless entry fails, engine fails to start,loss of power during reversing.So the megane is still trying to sort of these problems.I might be blasting the megane,but the ppl i noe who had bought the megane are facing such problems.
