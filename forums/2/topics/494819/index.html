    Electronic Road Pricing rates to increase by $1 at two gantries from Nov 6

    The Electronic Road Pricing rates at two locations will increase by $1 with effect from next Monday (Nov 6), said the Land Transport Authority on Monday (Oct 30).

    The two locations are: Kallang-Paya Lebar Expressway (East Coast Parkway) after Defu Flyover and westbound Pan-Island Expressway before Eunos Link, with the rates increasing from $2 to $3 between 8am and 9am, and from $0 to $1 from 8am to 8.30am respectively.

    The revision comes after the Land Transport Authority's completion of its quarterly review of traffic conditions on roads and expressways.

    The next review will take place next month for the December school holiday rates.
