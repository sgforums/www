    oso, how did u adjust the reverse sensor beeper?
    Not as easy as it sounds...

    You need to remove the instrument cluster, If you are removing it.. be careful as it is attached by a plastic screw plug.
    Unscrew the back panel of the cluster and you will find a buzzer(beeper).
    Use a piece of rubber foam to cover the buzzer. The sound will lessen.

    I use those air-con pipe insulation (round) to wrap round the buzzer and another smaller piece to cover the opening.

    I hope this helps....
