    Ok, here is how it works for reverse sensor on altis.

    1) Engage reverse gear 'beep beep' comes from reverse gear warning indicator under your steering wheel compartment.

    2) Car reversing near big object or wall 'beep beep' comes from the rear, near boot area.

    PS: Do not depend too much on reverse sensor, sometimes smaller object or metal pole cannot be detected

    Correct me if i'm wrong abt those sensors.
