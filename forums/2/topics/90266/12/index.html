    Hi..overdrive is by default On.. and everytime when you switch on the engine it'll go to On.. even if you off it previously.

    To save petrol.. leave it On.

    OD means the top gear... so leaving it On will allow the automatic transmission to enter top gear and thus saving petrol.

    Pressing the button once on the gear lever will turn OD Off which means telling the automatic transmission not to enter top gear.

    If the OD Off is lighted up on the panel, it's telling you Overdrive is switched off.. So you better press the button again to turn it back on.
