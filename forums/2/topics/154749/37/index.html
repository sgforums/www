    Heyo!

    Did a small mod for my Marchie Dash board for my Video Ipod. (it plays thru the stock radio unit thru the AUX and also charges at the same time)

    DId it because:

    1) all my music collection is in MP3
    2) I don't listen to local radio
    3) Catch my favourite podcast like digg, dl.tv, insomnia while driving.

    Been having this mod for quite a while now, so i tot i'd share pics of it

    Basically I shifted the stock radio to the bottom DIN, then did a paper mockup of what i wanted it to be, went to a workshop, they fabricated it with wood backing , then got a car upholstery shop to wrap it in cream coloured pvc leather for me to give that pudgy feeling.

    To connect to the RCA jack, i went to simlim to custom make a very short mini stereo to RCA (red and white connector) . I also got a mechanic to route out in parallel , the cigarette lighter jack to the back of the dash board. There, I used a 12v DC jack plug into a car ipod charger ( u can find this in funan or simlim)

    Finally all is wired up to an apple ipod universal dock ( the small back dot is actually the infrared red port just in case I wanted to used an ipod remote)

    So there's the short of it
