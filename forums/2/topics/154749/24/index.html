    Originally posted by silver marchie:
    I am planning to drive up to kukup via woodlands. It's my first time to drive across the borders.

    Anyone here can provide info. Esp maps.
    i drove up there twice. quite short and easy journey.

    stayed overnight in the kelong, surprisingly quite pleasant. you can see thru the toilet bowl into the sea underneath though.

    the kelongs are generally houses where the family let out for rent for a duration while they move temporarily to their siblings.

    someone will come to your house and BBQ seafood for you during meal time.

    they also ferry you around to the real kelongs (at sea) to buy seafood and look around.

    nice food, getaway from city life for a change.

    make sure you secure your march very tightly cos you will be parking your car far far away from where you stay.
