    13 Dec 05, 20:47
    Hi Vellerina

    $190 per panel sounds expensive. Does TC have an in-house spray paint bay, or are paint jobs outsourced? If it is outsourced, you will be better off going straight to the workshops that do the job.

    There are two workshops that I have good experience with for my car. The first is Hup Seng at Blk 1003 Bt Merah Lane 3 (Tel: 62719909). My Volvo agent outsources paintjobs to this workshop. The 2nd one is Yew Lip Spray Painting at Blk 15 Sin Ming Industrial Estate (Tel: 64536600). The owner is a lady called Angie and the workshop is very popular among car clubs. She's very well-known for repairing curb rashes at $25 per rim. Her charges are not expensive and workmanship's not bad.

    Hope this helps. I'm not linked to both businesses. Anyway, no harm calling them up to get an indicative quote and if you are happy, make a trip down and bargain.

    If you hang around car clubs, a handful of common workshops for body panel touch-up jobs keep coming up. They are Lye Design at AMK Industrial Pk 2 and ADSM at Kim Chuan Drive. I don't have any experiences with them so can't comment.
