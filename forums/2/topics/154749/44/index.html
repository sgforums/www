  • KingMariot
    13 Mar 06, 08:41


    A summary for those who did not manage to join us yesterday... we had about 20 Marchies that turned up yesterday. And this time we have couple new faces, I would like to welcome Alvin, Byron & Johnny to the club.

    The weather was great for this activity. We had the usual mingle around session at the Seah Im carpark with registration, selling of decals/T-Shirts and giving away of goodie bags.

    Had a convoy around Sentosa and we assembled at Gateway carpark for the start of the treasure hunt around the island. Thought it was good fun and there were one or two challenging "pit stops". Time catered to complete the course was 1 hour but some manage to finish in slightly less than 30 minutes. You can literally see "ants" of vibrant Marchies going around Sentosa in all directions during the treasure hunt. Red marchie came in first and Tedz came in 5th for the team event but "first" for the "individual event"

    Had a photo taking session with Tedz conducting the formation and a few doing parttime Valet parking duties. Did our prize presentation and finally we had 2 beautiful cakes baked by our resident chef Faded Mango to wrap up our 1st Anniversary celebrations. Some of us adjorned to Lau Pa Sat for dinner.

    So for those we missed the activity this time, hope to see you in our next meetup. Cheers.
