    The main reason oil goes bad is because it's viscosity breaks down. In other words it gets thinner. Heat cycles break down the viscosity. The more the oil goes from room temperature to operating temperature to room temperature again the faster it looses viscosity. The limiting factor would be the oil filters ability to keep the oil free of dirt/deposites. If a car is taken on alot of very short trips the limiting factor will be the viscosity because of the heat cycles. Your engine manual will give you recomended changing intervals. ( The handbook is just a guide)

    Synthetic oil is not always desirable because of it's inter action with some material. Do some reaserch on your particular application and always consult the owners manual.
