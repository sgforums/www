    I don't think it would be good for my Extol to run speed-tests. It is NOT BUILT for fast acceleration. It has high torque for hauling load, but not pick up.

    On a hot afternoon/day, the engine is really sluggish due to heat build up and hot ambient air. But feels really good on rainy days and early mornings or late at night, when air is cooler.
