    That is because after May 2002, the PARF value is based on ARF as per table below:

    Not exceeding 5 years 75% of ARF paid
    Above 5 years but not exceeding 6 years 70% of ARF paid
    Above 6 years but not exceeding 7 years 65% of ARF paid
    Above 7 years but not exceeding 8 years 60% of ARF paid
    Above 8 years but not exceeding 9 years 55% of ARF paid
    Above 9 years but not exceeding 10 years 50% of ARF paid

    And ARF between May 2002 to March 2004 is 130%. After March 2004, the ARF is 110% (I.e. Tax is reduced).

    So, for a car less than 5 years old, you get 75%*130% = 97.5% before March 2004 and 75%*110 = 82.5% after March 2004.

    Essentially, it is just lower upfront tax, lower tax rebate on scrapping.
