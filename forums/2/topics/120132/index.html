    The iDSI on offer now comes only with driver's side airbag. No passenger airbag, ABS, EBD and so forth. All these extra offering are found on the vtec model only. Earlier iDSI model do offer such features though.

    ABS will give you steering control when you apply e-brake and not locked your brake, causing uncontrollable, skid. It will not shorten your braking distance.

    I believe you still can get the iDSI with the extra safety fetaures but need to pay a few k more. The price would come up same as vetc.

    vtec model FC fares slightly poorer than iDSI as vetc employs conventional 4 spark plugs while idsi 8 plugs. iDSI will normally gives you about 17km/litre, auto transmission.
