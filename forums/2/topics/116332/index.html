    the YRV for both NA and Turbo is 1.3 liter.

    The NA manual can hit >500km on 23L depending on driving habits. the T can hit ~400km on 32L

    Handling, wise, not as good as Ignis sports, but put in some aftermarket parts, it is much improve and can do high speed conering without feeling of toppling. been there and done that.

    space wise, good enough for 3 adults at the back. Ride is abit bouncy due to the stiff springs at the back for the T. The NA is softer.

    Best thing is, it is 4 doors and can be real quick off the line.
