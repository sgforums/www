    What you had mention above. Is true abt how to drive the march like a manual car....! using foot works ..! Normally I will use my left leg to press on my brakes, while the right foot will be controlling the acceleration on normal roads and on slop use Gear -2 and OD on. !

    The ECU of the car is tune by factory that the driver have to floor alittle more on the pedals to gain the right power to pull yr car forward. After a few drives the ECU will capture yr driving habits, and will change according to what the ECU had capture...!
    Chances of you car having excess fuel is when you floor hard and try to pick up speed..! To push yr car forward, but it feels that the car is under power..! It will be becos the ECU can't read the sudden hard flooring .... again waste fuel .. and can damage yr engine.. No way the car is under power..! with 98bhp.. .. In combustion will happen when you try to floor hard where the RPM is still below the charging gear ratio, and very soon yr car will have a lot of carbon and etc...! try to drive normally with hard flooring is healthy for your engine...! slow does not = lower F.C or reducing damage to yr engine.. The car needs a lot of air intake...! drive slow ... air in take low.. lesser power...!!! For a new car and running in with mineral oil will consume more Fuel, after yr 10k... can change drop in filter, Denzo sparks, change to 5w-30 or 0w 40 engine oil..! You can feel the difference..! Is a trade off...! lower FC = other problems..!
    By the way all Japanese car pre run in abt 5k, before they install the engine onto the body..!
