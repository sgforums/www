    Do anyone of u have the same encounter and what do you do to solve it?
    Hi silver marchie,

    Are you referring to:
    1) the board on the base of the boot which covers the spare tyre?
    Or
    2) the board which is attached to the boot door by two strings?

    If its (1), you can use 3M or some other adhesive tape to create a cushion between the board and the sides so that they don't come in direct contact.
    If its (2), I suspect the board may not be fitted properly. If I remember correctly it can be detached for added storage space, so make sure its sitting properly on its hinges.

    Hope this helps solve your problem. If not, just pop down to Tan Chong and ask one of the technicians to have a look at it.
