    Hi, TAL, nice to hear from you.

    According to some reviews from foreign websites, there are only a few differences between the 2003 and 2004 versions. Most noticeably are the front grille and bumper.

    The 2004 version has a curve bar bending towards the Honda logo, a sharper tail-end for the head-lamp, and outward "teeth" on the bumper next to the fog-lamp pockets, giving it a shark-like appearance. The 2003 one has a horiz bar instead, orange-tinted signal lamp cover with a rounded tail-end for the head-lamp, and inward "teeth". Putting them side-by-side you will know what I mean. Other than some other minor upgrades or changes (not too sure about the details), both are practically the same car.

    So, after some deliberation, I decided to go for the 2003 version when its price was tagged at 74.5K in early April. Considered quite a steal Dun have too much bargaining power as the SE said he "barely earn much" from clearing these old stocks. Only got the leather seats. Hmm... wonder if I was too "kind-hearted",.... or simply stupid?
