    For long term protection, you'll need a PPS as wax will wear off. If you wish to maintain your ride, you have to be consistant (immediately rub off bird stains, wash after driving through rain, etc etc) and park under shelter. Waxing regularly will also help to maintain paintwork - but it's not cheap if you do outside, and if you do it yourself, without a buffing machine, you'll be sweating buckets.

    And I think you are referring to Meguiars products, yes they seem to be quite good. A few professional groomers also using their products.
