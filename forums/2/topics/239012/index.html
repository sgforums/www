    The question is space vs money !
    For stream you get the extra space for cary more people and stuff when you need, which does comes in very handly. But the disadvantage is the cost, as a bigger capacity car - insurance will be more expensive as they have to insure you for carry more people.

    My experience with Stream.
    For me when I convert from a salon car to Stream, it was great, as nowaday, all colleagues can be in, when last time a salon car, sometime we have to leave a person out. So the space is great !!! Get the 2.0L Stream - forget about 1.8. The 2.0L will be more economic and efficient engine in the long run then the 1.8L. So far my 2.0l Stream give me all the power I need, I can easily outrun anything if i have to, it drive more like a car then a MPV, infact it drive better then most salon cars .
