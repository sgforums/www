    Hi,sirlee here.Perhaps i can share something wif u regarding ignis sports,

    1.Roadtax:$868 per year
    2.Issurance:$1600(under my dad's name n its under coupe's class)
    3.Fuel consumption with minium grade of fuel - 98 (abt 12.8-1.3.5km/L)
    If u pump V power, the FC is higher,ard 12.4-12.6km/l.

    Cons:
    1. Only 2 drs
    2.Hard suspension-basically u feel most of the things on the road.
    3.small boot space.
    4.No auto lock features.
    5.Not much after markets products...

    Pros:
    1.Gd acceleration, 0-100km/h in 8.9sec.
    2.Very responsive,Close gear ratio.Though @ 100kmlh the rpm is abt 3400k,n 120km/h is abt 4000k the FC is still quite ok.
    3.Comes with Enkei sports rims,sports tuned suspension n exhaust.High profile cams..
    4.Quite stable @ the spd of 150km/h, wont feel 'Floaty" as the car is quite light 935kg.

    I thought tis is a pretty cool car to play wif.Hope this help u...
