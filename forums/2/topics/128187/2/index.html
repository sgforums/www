    Nowadays, all accident have to be note down in some kinda form with signature from both driver. If without that still can claim ? What is the consequence if claim without that form ?
    That's a SAS form, which is a must for IDAC to attach in the e-filing. If you did not complete the required form. IDAC cannot proceed with the reporting of your case to GIA and to your respective insurance company.
