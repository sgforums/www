    Been driving a kangoo for 3 yrs now. Am self-employed & use it to see customers and deliver stuff. Diesel cost about $27 for a full tank and gets you about 600 km (BP 18% off if you pay cash) so it's really economical. Road tax is approx $250 for 6 months. Insurance is a killer, at least $1200 for compre cover annually.

    The downside is the engine is noisy, which is expected of diesel vans. Every year must go inspection and Renault servicing & parts are not cheap.

    But it's comfortable, aircon is good, comes with power steering, ABS, reverse sensor and handles like a normal car.

    Overall, practical for small biz owner... cheers! hope it helps. PM me if any other questions.
