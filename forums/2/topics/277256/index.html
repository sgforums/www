  • AndrewPKYap
    5 Sep 07, 12:08
    Go to the petrol station and reset the odometer to zero. Then pump to full tank.

    After maybe using half a tank, go to the petrol station and pump to full. Record down the mileage shown in the odometer. Record down the amount of petrol pumped. Divide the mileage by the number of liters and you get km/liter.

    Reset the odometer back to zero, pump to full, use about half and repeat this a few times and take the average.
