    Cheers,
    Keyless entry is a system in which u are being made able to open the doors without sticking a key in a keyhole. as long as ur key fob is within range of your car, the car doors unlock automatically and u can just open the door. immobiliser is a chip in your remote key fob. this chip, which is in ur keyfob has to be in the car for you to start the engine. this is a anti-theft mechanism. lets say you are in your car in a parking lot and some theif who is heavily inspired by the game GTA comes and jacks your car, he will not be able to start the engine if the keyfob with the immobiliser chip in it is with you. as long as the keyfob is out of the proximity of your car, ignition will be impossible, even by means of hotwiring.

    hope that lowers ur PSI a bit.
