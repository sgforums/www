    The problem with the Chery QQ headlight switch is that it is located on the dashboard itself next to the electric mirror controls, not on the turning indicator stalk like normal cars. Instead, the light controls on the turning indicator stalk control the foglamps.

    My guess is that these drivers are too used to conventional cars, they turned on the foglamps by habit since they have a habit of turning the indicator stalk to turn on the headlights.
