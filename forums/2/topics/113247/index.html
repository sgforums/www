    Many had asked the question between getting a new or old. And the most logical answer I find is, first to ask yourselve, how long do you see yourselve driving the car? If you have intention to change 2-3 years down the road, Old car is better. Reason is someone has already suffered the steepest 1st year depreciation of that car. But if you will be driving it for at least 5 years, get a new one.

    Just remember, 40k for 8 years is not going to be a easier load than 50k for 10. Also consider that old car resale comes with high interest rate. So strictly speaking, 40k for a about 8 years old Getz isn't cheap at all.
