    Please let me share my experience:

    I have a 4 yr old Sunny (Auto N16, Pre-FL). Already exceed 100K km.

    Over the years, I have felt dissatisfied with the FC of this model, as I have previously driven the Pulsar. I felt like I visited the petrol station so often. But, I never really calculated the FC. It was based on "feeling".

    Recently, I had the inspiration to do so and these are the results:

    No. of trials: 2
    Petrol: Mobil 92.
    Method: Top up to full tank, drive till half tank and record mileage.
    Road condition: Working weekdays, Morning and Evening traffic jams through CTE + CBD + near Orchard areas + HDB areas.
    No. of passengers: 75% 1 driver + 1 passenger, and 25% 1 driver only.
    Driving Style: Quite light footed. Avoid revving beyond 3000 RPM.

    Result: 9.0km/L.

    I was somewhat surprised! I compared it with other postings in various forums and found this is pretty normal for 1.6L cars after all. (Of course, there are much better FCs around)

    Hope it helps.
