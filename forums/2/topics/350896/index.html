    The problem with parallel importers is there are just too many of them and difficult for the consumer to filter out the unscrupulous ones. Some of the PI companies are just one man show and can easily just wind up and start a company with another name the next day. So that's why people say, better to find a PI with a good reputation and history. Nowadays, PI warranties may be more than AD. Some offering 5 yrs/100,000km warranties for JDM cars. Do not believe everything you see in car forums as many PI are just lurking around and sabotaging other PI companies. For peace of mind, my advice is go with the ADs.
