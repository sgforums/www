    Hi guys,
    I just bought a colt too! I had shortlist both Jazz and colt and decided on the colt due to :-

    1) Lower cost : Colt (77k) and Jazz(82K)
    2) Higher power rating & torque performance.
    3) Stylisy internal (i.e. bench seat, nice dashpot, etc)
    4) Many sensors such as auto temp control, headight sensors, window glass sensors, etc)

    However, the Jazz had its advantage too such as:-
    1) Sligthly higher OMV
    2) Bigger internal space
    3) better fuel consumption rate.
