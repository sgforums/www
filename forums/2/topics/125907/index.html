    Hi,

    I'm currently driving a Honda Civic ES8 (2001). Thinking of selling it away though, and might just be doing that.

    The new civic are not know for power. In fact, they are rather weak, esp the 1.5 and 1.6l auto. Not too sure about the manual one though. If you could afford, I'll suggest you go for the Vti-S manual. At least much more powerful. Of cos, the insurance will be a bit high.

    Below is the personal view on my own civic.

    Pros:-
    1) Good passenger seating space behind, esp the flooring is flat
    2) A big boot
    3) Smooth engine
    4) Reasonable petrol consumption
    5) Stable (1100kg+)

    Cons:-
    1) Engine lack power
    2) Encounter problem with aircon compressor twice
    3) Getting kinda common nowsaday, esp black one for the new civic (just my personal view).
    4) Automatic gearbox tends to virbrate in gear one if ATF is dirty.
    5) Expensive insurance from some insurance company.

    Hope this helps..........
