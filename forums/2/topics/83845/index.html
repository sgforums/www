    please check the One motoring site before making claims that the tinted film can be darker now. You'll regret it if the TP ask you to change or strip the film that is no according to LTA requirements.

    Personally Im using Nanolux, satisfied with the reduction in heat, you can actually feel it. Of course this is subjective.

    Do note to get those films that reflect heat not those that absorb. The recommended would be V-cool, Nanolux and Huper Optik. I chose Nanloux becuase of price and Heat rejection performance. check out at www.nanolux.com.
