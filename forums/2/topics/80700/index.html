    PICTURES of Picanto http://www.pbase.com/caravincent_feb04/picanto_pics

    I bought it last Sunday at Century Square at Tampine. Initially took up the AUTO Picanto, but later (monday) changed it to the MANUAL one.

    After a test drive on both cars, I was furthered convinced by my desicion.

    On the AUTO, I have to floor the pedal each time i want to over-take but it's not responsive enough.

    On the MAUNAL, you can actually feel the torque!

    I believe both cars will have pretty much the same fuel consumption, BUT... that means, you will have to drive the AUTO very conservatively (no flooring). A good MANUAL driver will be able to do very good mileage.

    Anyway, I am surprise the shifting of the gears on the MANUAL are so easy on this budget car.

    Enough said. Anyone else getting this little car?
