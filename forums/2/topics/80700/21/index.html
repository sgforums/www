    formula for calculating car price....

    Car price : A
    Downpayment : B
    Amount loan : (A - B) = C
    Interest : D (2.2% is taken as 0.022)
    No. of yrs loan : E
    Mths : E * 12 = F

    Calculation : ((C*D*E)+(C)) / F
