  • Vinsanne
    15 Aug 04, 01:36
    We collected our car at Pandan Gardens today (saturday). The handover was very successful. Mel, the KIA sales rep. that handed the car over to us was very informative and of all, friendly. We had a very enjoyable time going thru this proceduce.

    Our experience:
    From a distance, to our surprise, we can see both side mirrors are white, as the same of the car's body (very well done paint work and finishing). As you can see in the pictures, even the door knots are already white, except the door straps. From this alone, we were delighted. (side mirrors also have knots to adjust from inside of car)

    Mel went thru every possible check with us; from every single light in the car to the components in the bonnet. Some documents were signed and we were on our way out of the centre with our very own car, Picanto.

    Mel later revealed to us that, this is her first Picanto delivery and she was not expecting the colored door knots and colored side mirrors, not ever the adjustable level for the side windows. Guess we must have at least saved $200 to $300.

    Our Picanto has a mere mileage of 15m as we drove out of the collection centre. Another surprise was, the petrol gauge was 3/4 full (have done another 56km and the petol guage is still more than half, probably due to the low rev driving).

    The car, I must say was a head turner as we drove in town and stop at the lights. I supposed most people were going "is that a new car?". All in all, we are just glad we finally got our Picanto.

    Will post more pictures of our Picanto on our pbase website soon.

    Vincent
