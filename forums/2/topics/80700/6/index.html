    This was the information given to me on the Picanto. Hope is useful to those interested. Cheers

    KIA PICANTO. Price is at S$56,999 include 1st year insurance with installment susidise up to a total of S$5000(subject 3mth bidding).Downpayment of S$2900 can be used on DBS or Citibank credit card, spread into 24mth interest free, which mthly at S$130. Mthly instalment for the car will be at S$562( 10yrs). Loan will be taken from Maybank at a 2.45% interest(per annum). Here's an example:

    Car Price S$56 999
    Downpayment S$2 900 (Divided by 24mths for credit card installment = S$130)

    Loan amount S$54 099
    Monthly S$ 562

    MayBank will required the customer to pay a min of S$150 every month.

    Therefore if your insurance is S$1500 we will take S$5000-S$1500=S$3500

    So these S$3500 will be used to offset for your monthly installment.
    If you pay S$150 the rest of the S$412 will be susidise by C&C
    Therefore if your insurance is at S$1500, the 1st 8mth of your installment will be at S$150 then from the 9th mth onwards will resume back to your usual monthly installment at S$562.

    On top of the susidise monthly installment S$150,you will need to pay for the S$130 for the downpayment installment and thats make up to the amount of S$280per month
