    Gentlemen, remember one thing, don't ever be persuaded to pay an extra S$1,500 for a "guarantee COE" or to top up any difference. Why do I say so? I booked my blue diamond picanto end May 2004 and successfully got my COE today! Mind you, this is the first bid and I got it!!! Furthermore, my car is supposed to come in September. There are altogether 6 bids and you are sure to get the COE sooner or later. I told myself not to be too anxious or desperate in getting the car. If I get it, fine, if not, forget it. I told my SA plainly and firmly that I will rather give up the car than pay anything additional. That's why I think he did his best in assisting me in getting the COE. Perhaps a previous posting by my friend Vit4WD will explain everything clearly, read on the copied posting:

    You are being mislead that C&C does not make much from the Picanto sales.

    I did my sums and calculated, that based on COE prices at S$28,000, C&C still makes between S$5,000 to S$7,000 profit for every Picanto sold.

    Please remember that the OMV of the Picanto is very low. Only S$6,000

    Whatever the case, please DO NOT top up the extra S$1,500 for guaranteed COE. After top up, the annual dep will be in excess of S$5K per annum, which is ridiculous for a 1.1litre Korean car.

    Don't worry too much about not getting your car.

    C&C is not stupid. They have been in this busines for decades. They have the margin to secure your COE, even if it rises above S$30K

    The only issue is that it eats into their profit levels, so they wil try some tricks to get you to top up.

    At the end of the day, if C&C does not secure your COE, they make no money out of you, but if they secure your COE, they can still make some money out of this sale.

    And also, if C&C cannot hit KIA Korea's sales targets, they wil be taken off the KIA dealership in S'pore.

    So please relax. You can get your car without any top ups.

    Everyone got the picture here? The more you gian (desire) it, all the more the SA will play on that aspect and you may get your COE later. Unite and you all will happily get your car soon.
