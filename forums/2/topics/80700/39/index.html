    I think you can work out the figure yourself. I dunno whether you can get full loan amount or you need to pay at least an percentage upfront. My upfront amount is less than 10%. Here is an example.

    KIA Picanto Manual $49,999

    Upfront amount = 5% = $2500

    Loan amount = $49,999 - $2500 = $ 47,499

    For 10 years loan interest = $47,499 x 2.45% x 10 years = $11,637

    Monthly installment = ($47,499+$11,637)/10years /12mth = $493

    Monthly about $493, excluding pertrol, insurance, road tax etc
