    OK lah, just to let all know, the people at Subaru Club SG listed more pros than cons

    Pros:
    - Better handling (cos of wider tyres)
    - Better stability
    - Better cornering
    - Higher top speed (cos of larger wheel rolling radius)

    Cons:
    - Higher FC (cos of wider tires)
    - Higher likelihood to scratch the rims
    - Cost (for good rims for comfort and minimal increase in FC)

    Neutral:
    - If blowout, rims are more likely to be damaged, unless run-flats, but can be safer to have a blowout with bigger rims
    - Higher stress to axles (unlikely to damage axles)
    - Higher FC (only for low quality / heavy rims)
    - Cannot take big potholes (not necessarily as larger tyres can overcome potholes)
