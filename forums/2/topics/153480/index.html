    Originally posted by Troy437:
    cars above 1.6 L?

    I seldom see cars above 1.6L got manual version.
    I am looking for 2.0L SUV / saloon car with manual transmission. Is there any?
    Most cars (regardless of engine capacity) have both manual and auto transmission variations. But due to manufacturer's corporate policies, distributor's marketing/stocking strategies, etc etc.....mostly only auto models are imported into Singapore (logical move, as auto tranny is more popular in the start/stop traffic conditions here, they can't risk tying up their capital stocking something which doesn't move).

    If not already available locally, you can ask the distributors to specially import a manual version for you, subject to the following limitations:

    - That manual tranny is actually available for that particular model.

    - That you are willing to pay more for it.

    - That you are willing to wait for it (soonest would be months).

    - That the distributor agrees to import it (if its a model they haven't imported before, the testing/documentation/declaration procedures to import the first one is really hell).

    - That even if successful, you'd be willing to wait for spare parts, and probably pay more for them, if you should need them (eg. tranny parts).
