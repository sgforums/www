    Trade in value for used rim and tyres is only around $300 to $400. If you are thinking of upgrading the stock rims of the above cars, you are likely to be looking at a 17 or 18". That will set you back by around $2 to 3K and above

    If you are on a tight budget, just drive with the stock rim and upgrade it after 1 to 2 years. You might want to consider buying used rims, if you are lucky you might get a good buy.
