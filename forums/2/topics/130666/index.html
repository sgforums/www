    f.c. does not depends on run in dates. the run in is approx 1000km. but dun expect miracles. usually they hv special additives in the 1st engine oil to handle engine seizure fr high temperature fluctuation like extreme low temp, etc

    but otherwise, they probably hv used the car, like driving it fr workshop to onboard the ship as well.

    to improve ur f.c. interim measure, check ur tyre pressure against the recommended pressure marked at the b-pillar or door (b-pillar is the mid section betw front & back door.) up ur pressure by 10%.

    lastly, try not to be too harsh on urself abt FC and just enjoy ur ride.

    By the way, if you are concerned about FC, you shouldn't have gotten an auto car.
