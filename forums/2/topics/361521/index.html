    Hi bro,

    No cheap insurance in the market.. Many companies will not want to cover you because you are too young.. <21 years old and less than 3 years driving experience.. Most likely, only NTUC and AIG may consider covering you, and most of the plans quoted to you will have an excess in it..

    Usually 1k excess, or up to 3k excess.

    What I mean by excess is that in the case of any accident, you want to claim your insurance, you have to bear the first 1K or 3K SGD repair cost. Let's say your repair as quoted is 6k, you pay 3k SGD and the insurance company pay the other 3K for you.

     

    At my age (24 and 4 years driving exp), they still consider me as young driver and my the plan i took up has $500 excess in it. Heard that some companies consider 30 and above as "Not young drivers"

    Just for your info, I paid 3k for my insurance premium. 10% NCD.

    Not Recommended: NTUC (Slow claims, lousy service)
