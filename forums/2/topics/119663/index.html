    ok. i test drove the latio today.
    to be fair, this car score points in the following area.

    1) suspension & control - I cornered at high speed (30 -40km/h) my rear passenger indicated to me that he does not felt being thrown off the seat.
    2) CVT - excellently smooth
    3) Extremely spacious cabin and boot for its size.
    4) Very quiet. Excellent sound insulation.
    5) Steering - Good feel, not too light, good feedback

    The car did not score in the following

    1) looks - subjective, I find it generally OK. at least better than the City
    2) pick up from standstill - the initial pickup is bad, does not fell the surge forward even when I accelerate hard, but once above 30kmh, can feel the power coming in, when engine is in the power zone. (me driving a YRVT, does tends to be more pick-up hungry)
    3) Price slightly steeper than a Civic.

    Overall - i would go for the latio. I would say it is in its own class, compared to current models available in the market.
