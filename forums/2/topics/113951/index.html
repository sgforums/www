    This is my recent experience with the Opel distributor.

    I went to their Leng Kee showroom on Saturday (26Feb) morning expecting to view and test the Astra Turbo. I was told by the salesman that the car was only available for viewing and testing at their Ubi centre. Moreover a $5,000 deposit was also required prior to the test drive.

    Well, I went to Ubi on Sunday (27Feb) around lunch time. I spoke to the sales lady (as recommended by the salesman at Leng Kee). She told me the test drive car was sold. All she offered was an exterior view of the car. What a disappointment! Can't believe the company has only 1 Astra Turbo test unit. Even harder to believe they sold off their test unit.

    One observation I made while at both showrooms is the sales staff were not particularly enthusiastic. Seems they are not hungry for sales at all. If this is the attitude of the sales force (the prime revenue unit of the company), I dread to think what their technical/mechanical team is like. Would you entrust your precious car to a "boh chap" lot?
