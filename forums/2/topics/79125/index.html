  • ditzy

    In short, horsepower is how much power the engine can develop. The higher, the more power. It is usually addressed as BHP-brake horsepower.

    CC denotes a short form of cubic centimetres. It represents the displacement taken up by an engine. The higher the cc, the larger the engine. for example, 1997cc engine might represent an engine displacement of 2.0L.

    Hope that helps abit.
