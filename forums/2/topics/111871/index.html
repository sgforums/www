    Lexus RX300 is a Harrier 3.0 in Japan since they dun have lexus brand there yet. Lexus is a branding exercise for export market, which will end sometime this year. The local Harrier is just a lower-spec car with a smaller engine in the harrier line-up. Badge aside, there are also slight difference in interior trim.

    Most people who bought a harrier rebadged it as a Lexus. Easy way to up the snob appeal. Other than the logcard, the only dead give-away that it's a Harrier is the third side mirror on the front fender.
