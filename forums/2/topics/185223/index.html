    Picanto is good for a budget car. May be you can also consider kelisa.

    Though budget is one of the main deciding factors but be reminded to get a car that can meet your daily needs.

    For example, if you have infant or you need to ferry kids, parents, parents-in-law or maid, picanto may not be a good option though it's cheap.

    For old cars, be prepared to fork out more for road tax, maintenance and repair cost and also, it's quite hard to tell if the particular old car has any accident before or not.

    I drove an old car before but not now anymore since new car now is pretty affordable and also, peace of mind to own a new than an old car.
