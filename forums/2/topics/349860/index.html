    cost + tax (omv, etc) + profit margin = selling price mah. From personal experience, I do know that when it comes to pricing, KM is more strict and it's not easy to get further discounts from the listed pricing.

    Another reason why Honda cars are generally more expensive - Options are generally not avaliable. Things like Leather seats, sports rims, solar flim, ICE, etc etc, are usually offered as options/freebies by other ADs. KM on the other hand, anything and everything that can factory fit, they will factory fit - that's why all Civics come highly-spec-ed, and in the end you don't have to ask for this and that because anything that you want and which can be fitted into the Civic, will already be fitted AND included in the selling price.
