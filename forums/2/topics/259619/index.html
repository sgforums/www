    Unfortunately I was there at the scene - me just came back from JB, and were supposed to meet my friend for coffee at Yishun. I arrived about 15 mins after it had happened. Total of two ambulances & three police cars.

    The woman (dressed in black t-shirt & shorts) was lying on a stretcher - from my observation, she looked like she had a a broken right leg, huge amount of abrasions, & her face was wounded very very badly. She was grasping for air, and was in shock (body twitched uncontrollably).

    The driver was pulled from under the back bumper....looked like a goner to me there & then. The entire car was disintegrated. The only part that resembled a car-part was the back bumper. The debris of this car was spread over a wide area.

    From what I observed, he should have been driving at high speed taking a steep corner; had lost control; ran over the centre kerb; smashed into a truck parked at opposite side. The tire skid marks were unquestionable. His final stop was by the side of a monstrous truck.

    Yes, it''s the long stretch of road that u exit YD towards Orchid Country Club.
