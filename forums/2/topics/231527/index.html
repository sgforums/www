    You are private candidate right?

    Ok, I list out some common mistakes here ranging from 2 to 4 points

    Fail to check blindspot - 4 points
    Fail to check mirror before slowing down or turning - 2 points

    In some case other than below-4 points mistake, you will get 6 to 10 points for each mistake made like:

    Fail to slow down when approaching road hazard - 6 points
    Cause other vehicles to take evasive action - 8 points
    Stop on pedestrian crossing - 10 points

    There are 64 mistakes in total. About 5 or 10 of them will give Immediate Failure if you make one of these mistakes.

    So my advice to you is to check blindspot and also mirror everytime before you doing the turning whether it is left or right. It's not worthy of being penalised for failing to check blindspot.

    For safety check when doing circuit course, you must always look at the mirror and then check your blindspot before making left or right turn when driving into S course, Crank course or Directory Change course.

    For vertical and parallel parking, before you reverse the car into the lot or to move out of the lot, you must be more exaggerated to turn your head to keep your lookout and also blindspot too on both side, not just one side. And while you are reversing your car into the lot, be sure to turn your head back and look through rearwindow every 5 seconds, and at the same time, keep your lookout on your both side until your car is in the lot.

    This also go to the same thing for directory change course. Hope this help you
