  • ^spidee^
    13 Aug 06, 01:16
    You can practise feeling the biting point on a road without alot of traffic. Maybe you can feel it better if you wear shoes with thinner soles. As you improve, move on to busier roads and slopes.

    Once you've gotten the biting point, depress the accelerator gradually, whilst slowly moving your left leg towards yourself.

    Relax, the more you tense up, the harder it is to feel the biting point and the more frequent the car stalls.
