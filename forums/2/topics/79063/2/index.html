    I've installed my car a month ago, with the Nanolux Ultimax package for $605 (incl of gst), can feel the difference during hotter days. I have actually contemplate on V-Cool and Huper Optik as well but very expensive. Note that the technology behind the manufacture of the films, these three are the same i.e. sputtering of nano-sized metallic or ceramic to special plastics film. All of which has one similar characteristics, reflective! it reflects the heat. Of course, depending on the grade you chose, it would provide different "shades". BTW all 3, Nanolux, V-Cool and Huper Optik are from the same distributor, GMX trading Pte Ltd. The question is why the price is so different, my personal deduction is the workmanship cost. Nanolux allows you to buy the films and install in your car or homes and sells the installation kit for $7, DIY mode.
