    Primera is 2.0litres 4 cylinder, 150bhp, 200Nm of torque. 10.9 sec century sprint.

    I have seen interior of it and to me it looks rather futuristic. Quality wise, nissan should be quite alright. You might want to take a look in their showrooms to get a feel of it. I like the centre console.

    The reason why there are such few primera might the singaporean mentality of "biggest car possible with the smallest engine cc possible"(read: Cefiro, S280, S80 2.0T, 730i, etc). Also, before the current model, cefiro 2000cc V6 engine kinda make primera an "inferior" car. However, now, a Primera seems logical with the dismisal of Cefiro 2000cc V6 engine.

    To me (and probably nissan too), it actually capture a different niche of market, targeting at younger buyers who do not want to be labelled driving father's cars or driving physically big cars. It is also more powerful too 150bhp vs Cefiro 145bhp and probably lighter too, characteristics more desirable to younger buyer. It is also slightly cheaper than the current 200JK Cefiro

    If you like it, why bother what other think, go down to nissan showroom, test drive and see how it goes.
