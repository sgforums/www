    Like you I haven't found a forum for Chevy yet. I am shopping around for an effortable car and came across AVEO. The design is great and value for money with many choice of colour. It is true that most people are put off by the ex-DAEWOO brand including myself. Come to think about it, people are also put off by Suzuki for the sound of its name. As for Toyota, it has been on the road for a long time with a significant market share. That is why most still consider VIOS. Compare to AVEO, VIOS has sport kits, airbags and Japanese engine&gearbox, these are the good stuff. However, there are no 60:40 split rear seats in VIOS ( as with ALTIS) and we cannot run away from the fact that it is assembled in Thailand. I have seen a VIOS with the wording 'VIOS' removed for apparent reason. You must be looking for a low-budget car if you happen to shortlist AVEO and VIOS. Hyundai ACCENT is also around this price range.

    Will be looking around for AVEO owners on the road to obtain their genuine feedback. At the meantime, you can check up reviews from AAS: http://www.aas.com.sg/features/feature.htm
