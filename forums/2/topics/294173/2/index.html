    Slope test

    Go up slope
    step foot brake and clutch and engage 1st gear
    pull handbrake
    step acc till 1.5k - 2k rpm
    slowly release clutch (you will see your bonnet rising up and thats the biting point)
    release handbrake and step more on acc

    From bbdc long long time ago
