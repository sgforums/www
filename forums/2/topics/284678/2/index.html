    Hi there,

    Congrats on your new license. I just had my license exactly 6 days back and had my driving license sent to me today.

    I went to test drive Vios and Latio yesterday and these are my comments.

    Vios: The car has good acceleration and is very light. That helps in FC, however, too light for my comfort as I did a fast turn (for a try) and my passengers behind (mum, brother n gf) felt they were flying. The engine sounds a little loud from the inside of the car.

    Latio: The exterior doesn't look appealing, however the premium version of latio caused me to drool with their luring interior. The seats are exremely comfortable and I like the 'hand'brake which is at the acceleration/brake padels. It removes the usual brake which is by your left. Similar design vis-a-vis Mercedes/ Lexus etc. The car is not so light and when I made a similar sharp turn, my passengers felt okay. I cannot comment on the FC as I'd not physically witnessed it. However, I was told that it is between 13 - 15 on road.

    My conclusion, if you were to get Nissan Latio, go for the Premium version. An additional $3000 is worth the money as it uses CVT, which makes your automatic gear shifting smoother.

    My 2 cents worth.

    By the way, I'd booked for a Latio Premium. Just note that the insurance is pretty high. I paid $2,900 for the insurance at the age of 25.
