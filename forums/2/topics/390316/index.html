  • lianamaster
    16 Feb 10, 10:15

    A 1 year old Swift 1.5(Auto) costs about $48,000 ($31,000 for OPC), while a new one, as at 3rd Feb, costs about $58,000.

    Instalments will depend on how much u are willing to pay as downpayment. Typically for a 7 year loan, it's around $500+.

    Petrol is around $200 per month.

    Road Tax: $678 per year

    Insurance: Around $2000 per year depending on age, driving experience etc...

    Servicing: Every 30,000km I suppose.

    Radio Tax: To be paid together with road tax. $27 per year I suppose.

    Maintenance: Variable cost. Can balloon to a few hundreds if opt for thorough servicing especially at higher mileage.

    For OPC cars, the instalments, road tax and insurance will be lower.
