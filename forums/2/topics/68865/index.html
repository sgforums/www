    The Cyclone system (from what learn in my mechanical enginnering days) is actually to improve air-fuel mixture and with a system that has better mixture the combustion will be more complete and giving the total energy that the amount of explosive mixture is in the cylinder at that time. The cyclone as i read allows the swirling of air into the cylinder, this swriling effect cause the air and fuel to be properly mixed, thus producing better combustion and hence performance as a result. This is why today the petrol engine design has Fuel injection, which was found in desiel engine in the early days (I think Mecedes was the pioneer), to improve mixture and also a more positive response throttle.
