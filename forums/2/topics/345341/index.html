    Honda Siel Cars India rolled out the all new Honda City in India to replace the Honda City ZX. The new City sedan offers great performance, stylish features, and advanced technology. The Honda City has always remained one of the most popular sedans in the midsize segment and the new model will further garnish Honda sales.

    The Honda City is a wonderful blend of splendid styling, cutting edge technology, and advanced safety. The car is passionately crafted with a unique 'Arrow Shot Form' design that presents vibrant and path-breaking style. The interior is spacious and is adorned with plenty of comfort and convenience features to give total comfort.

    The Honda City competes with the Maruti Suzuki SX4, Hyundai Verna, and Ford Fiesta.
