    Originally posted by macTea:

    so what about used car of less than 3 years? would it be "safe" to buy? really tempting..:D

    Well, in my opinion, cars younger than 3 years will be a 'safer' bet in the sense they might still be covered under warranty; so there's still some allowance for maintenance by the main agent should anything crop up. The engine and mechanics of the car should still be working fine as compared to a 10-year old car. But still, you never know the driving habits of the previous owner. Some unscrupulous dealers will make the car look brand new although it kena accident the day before, hence there's always some doubts raised when buying a second-hand car. Just be cautious of cheapo offers that are deemed too good to be true.
