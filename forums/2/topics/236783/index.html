    Yes you can. Which means, the car will be under your name but the finance side would be your dad or sis name to be your guarantor.

    But rememeber, if total income declared under the combine name is less than 3k, they will only give you a 90% loan.

    But if you are just going to buy the car at full payment, just walk in and go ahead.

    Remember to ask the agent to check up your insurance premiums. AIG is one of the highest, there are cheaper options like NTUC or Aviva.

    It would be better to get someone experience to accompany you when purchasing the car, so as to make sure the agent does not cheats you and get better deals.
