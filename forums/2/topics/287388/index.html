    "This forum section has been quite dead so rather than leaving it like that I would like to talk about Singapore. By far there is no proper member from Singapore on this site, but I often go there for holidays and would like to talk about experiences and stuff there.

    Singapore is a very tiny country with no capital city, it is just like a small island, but filled with business districts. Rich men in Malaysia, who can not stand the terrible road conditions, reckless motorcycles, traffic offenders and corrupt cops, go to Singapore to chill and have a break.
    And of course, there are lots of rich men in Singapore as well, driving fancy cars. On occasions, you can even find exotics with plates from Thailand as well. Singapore is walking distance from the South Malaysia. It's just connected by a short bridge.

    Singapore's law is very strict, particularly laws related to automobiles.
    Highway speed limits are only 110km/h, road limits are only 80km/h. Seatbelts, signal use, are all under watchful eyes by the police, who usually are in completely random unmarked vehicles.

    So anyway, here is a preview of what you can see there.

    2 Lamborghini LP640
    5 Lamborghini Murcielago
    15~20 Lamborghini Gallardo Coupes (no SE's sighted)
    10 BFS
    3 Arnage

    1 997 GT3 RS
    3 997 T
    3 360 Sp'dr
    1 360 Modena
    2 360 CS (1 yellow no stripes, 1 black no stripes)

    1 V8 Vantage (my first; funny how I seen Superleggeras much earlier than some of you)
    3 Elise (or maybe 4)
    1 Esprit
    2 Continental GT

    Impressive, no? All those great exotics from Singapore and Malaysia combine together in a beautiful organized place, isn't it just heaven?

    Singapore is the second best place after Ginza District of Tokyo, Japan."
