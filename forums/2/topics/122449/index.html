    Well for starters..here is a tip to see if the distributor is supplying electricity to your spark plug cables.

    1. Plug out one spark plug cable.(engine off)

    2. Attach a metal piece(like a long screw driver) inside the cable and touch the metal piece to a metal part (like the engine block)

    3. Crank the engine. (dont hold on to the metal piece)

    4. If you see a spark along the metal piece then the distributor is working fine and supplying electricity to the spark plug cables.
