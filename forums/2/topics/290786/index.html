    Think of the various factors that affect FC.

    Driving techniques: Heavy or light accelaration, warm up engine, braking
    Road condition: Traffic lights, jams, slope, highway
    Engine Condition: Regular maintenance, proper tuning, tyre pressure
    Load: Weight the vehicle carry around
    Usage: looking for parking space, waiting '5 minutes', poor route knowledge.

    No point having a great milage when most of the milage is not productive. Like getting lost and circling the destination. Looking/waiting for parking space is also not productive. Parking in the upper decks of MSCP daily adds to the milage and FC, and you're going nowhere.
    A 10Km congestion-free route that takes 15 minutes, consumes less fuel than a 7Km congested route, that takes 25-30 minutes.
