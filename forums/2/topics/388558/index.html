    d)if i have a class 3 Licence,which vehicle is the cheapest to buy?how about pickup,minivan,lorry(class 3 license rite)?..hw much 1st n 2nd hand price?

    Which vehicle is the cheapest to buy? It all depends on whether you buying 1st hand / 2nd hand, China make / korea make / japan make / continental make.

    A China car can cost you anything from 30 - 60k 1st hand. A Japanese car can cost you from 45k - 300k. Korean car from 35k - 90k. Continental car from 55k to 2M.

    A japanese van (Hiace) can cost you $54k first hand, and about 39 - 40k 2nd hand. A China Van (Minz) can cost you about 20k first hand, 2nd hand about 10k+.            A Korean Van can cost you 30 - 40k first hand, 2nd hand about 10-20k.A Continental Combi (Opel Combo, Fiat Doblo etc) can cost you up to 55k first hand, 2nd hand about 30k..

    Pickup = Class 3.

    Goods Minivan = Class 3

    Passenger Minivan = Class 3 + Class 3 Vocational License.

    Lorry = Class 3 or Class 4, depending on length and unladen weight.

     

    Hope this answers your queries !
