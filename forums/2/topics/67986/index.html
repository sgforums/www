    If you had prior driving experience and have passed your final theory test,I suggest learning through private.It's cheaper and faster.

    However,if you are intent on learning through school,from what i know,only CDC in ubi has no minimum lessons to go through before you're elilgible to book for your Practical test.Other schools implement a minimum 25 lessons.

    In the case which you have not passed your final theory test,it'll be better to go through school.They have very useful theory tutorials that will help you a great deal in your final theory test.

    In the case of you having taken the practical test once but failed as a private student.There are express courses in schools that cater just 8 lessons for you before you take the Practical test again.This is for private students who don't wish to wait the long duration.
