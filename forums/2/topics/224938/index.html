    Hi all,

    For those looking for some excitment, please check out the following and take part.

    This is the second one which I'm helping out for this year, and personally I can say that the first ADC (Advance Driving Course) got a lot of positive comments.

    You will learn to do hand brake turns and reverse flicks like in the movies. Something you really cannot do on public roads. So come down and have a good 2 days of fun and drive like a mad man if you want.

    What is $200 to upgrade your car handling skills compared to the amount of money you spent on the car?

    This is at Turf City and there's no kerbs to worry about. Come with your forum buddy/friend. Or buy this package as a christmas gift for your loved one!

    Last chance for registration is on 14th Dec Thurs 2006 from 8pm onwards. 10pm will be a short briefing for the Sat and Sun events. Don't miss it!
