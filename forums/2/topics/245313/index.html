    Diesel Engine
    Advantages=more torque than similar size petrol engine,better fuel economy,strong components to withstand high compression(can be turbocharged w/o changing internal parts).
    Disadvantages=heavy and bulky,noisier than petrol engine,longer time to start for cold season and engine knocks until warms up.produce black soot

    Petrol Engine
    Advantages=lightweight,smooth idling when cold start(no knock),better acceleration(due to higher rpm den diesel engine),easy starting when cold.
    Disadvantages=may overheat if left idling too long,need more maintainence(spark plug,distributor..)

    Turbocharger
    Advantages=strong boost,use waste to convert to energy(exhaust gas),easier to install than supercharger.
    Disadvantages=lag(need higher rpm to get boost than supercharger),need to change components(bigger injectors,ecu,stronger piston,crankshaft)

    Supercharger
    Advantages=boost at low rpm,almost no lag
    Disadvantages=uses engines energy(belt driven),troublesome to install,troublesome maintainence

    Smaller rim diameter
    Advantages=better acceleration
    Disadvantages=loses top speed

    Removing spare tire
    Advantages=improves power to weight ratio(better acceleration)
    Disadvantages=call tow truck if have flat tire

    Racing clutch
    Advantages=No clutch slip if engine have too much power
    Disadvantages=heavy to step
