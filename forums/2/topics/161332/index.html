  • Kuali Baba

    The trick to good drifting is the ability to coax the car into controllable oversteer. The suspension can be tuned to assist this, like in the not-too-distant days of F1 when drivers had different set-ups for qualifying and the race, and in rallying where it's the fastest way to shed speed on loose surfaces.

    But please, don't practise it on public roads!
