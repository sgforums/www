    yup. same procedures.

    warm up is just before the test itself. no need to book. just report according to your designated test-time (which includes warm up time already). warm up means 1 round before the actual test.

    revisions are just normal lessons. after u have completed all requirements and passed all subjects. the subsequent lessons will be called "revisions". can do as many as u wan if u r rich. if not, i would recommend you to book for at least 2.

    1. the day before your test-date.
    2. the day day before your test-date.
