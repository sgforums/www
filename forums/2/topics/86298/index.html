    Then the pole appear infront of the car, full lock to the LEFT!!! Tis v impt coz always forget...

    The car will now swerve in... Stop...

    Adjust ur car by movin forward abit... n straighten ur car at the same time u move forward... straghten by turnin 2 rounds to the right.

    FREE GEAR HAND BRAKE!!! If tester keep quiet, tell him u r ready... If he ask u move off as soon as u park finish, jus go ahead, no need free gear hand brake... Tis applies to vertical parkin also.

    Ur clutch technique very impt... Do it very slowly, esp when u seein ur side mirror tat part... U miss the pt if u go to fast... usually use 1/2 clutch, tis use 1/4 clutch tat kind of power or slowness... Hmm... N hor, mak sure when u turnin the steerin wheel tat time the car is movin... else will deduct marks....

    Relax relax, dun gan giong, do carefully... don knock down poles if possible...

    Hmm... jUs mak sure u check ur blindspot before u go in... Safety check...

    Then hor, jus follow the pointer ur inst teach u... U first go in, half clutch technique... do v slowly... hmm, keep the car in the center... then u reach the most curvy part, whereby the car cannot keep to the center liao rite, u adjust ur car to suits the curvage can liao lor... Usually u will steer to the left side if i not wrong....

    Crank course:

    The same, check blindspot b4 u go in...

    Do slow, half clutch...

    The turnin pt mus be somewhere around ur body tat area then u turn... don turn too early or too late...

    UR postion of the car is that the ERP thingy sittin on the center line or white line...

    check front, right n back...

    Continue reversing, u see big traingle form by the pole, u 2 round to the right, small triangle, 2 rounds to the left... u will then go inside the parkin space...

    Then u see ur side mirror, adjust 2 rounds to right to straighten... then u go in n stop can liao lor!
