    Currently driving a 1.6 small sedan - unfortunately, quite noisy at high speeds. Assessment that most 1.6 cars unable to achieve the sufficient balance of drive comfort, size and power.

    Thinking of upgrading to 2.0l mid-sized vehicle. (probably sedan but open to other types / options)

    Frustrated by the lack of technical details on the various dealer websites, (especially when providing Singapore-specifications) but no time to visit showroom after showroom...

    As such, keen to tap on collective wisdom of forum.

    Selection Criteria:

    Value-for-money - Cost is of primary consideration. So 'premier' European models are out. Not going to mention budget as I'm looking for something that has most/all of the required functionalities for the least amount of dollars;

    Reliability - Good reliability, in particular major components such as engine, transmission, etc. Not brand conscious.

    Silence / Quietness - Very low NVH at high speeds of 110-140km/h;

    Fuel Consumption - Good FC. Prefer VVT engine for low FC;

    Safety - ABS, EBD, Brake Assist must-have. ESP - good to have. Driver airbag must have. Additional airbags good to have;

    Others - Have cruise control as part of package to help in long trips up north.

    Thanks in advance for your input.
