    With so much info and feedback, you'll have to rank whats more important - Price, Resale value/demand after couple of years, perceived image/status, fuel consumption, 0-100 acceleration, low-end torque, fuel economy, etc
    Today, I went to test-drive the Sonata 2.4 at $77k (OMV$17k) and the Camry 2.4 at $95k (OMV28 to 29k)
    Sonata feels smooth and very quiet for this price And quite a good looker too. Very good acceleration and road noise insulation. Very spacious.
    AA highway review sounds good too esp. its fuel economy, around 10km/l with 10.4sec. for 0-100
    What worries me about the Sonata is :Resale value, reliability

    The Camry 2.4 at $95k gives a little more peace of mind but at a price.

    My friend who came along to test drive owns a Cefiro2.0 V6- he says the FC is around 8 to 9km/L and the pick up is a little sluggish.

    Hope you'll find this info useful.Cheers
