    Okay that was the last photo taken on the way back, after which my phone battery DIED cause i didn't fully charge it the night before. oh well. haha.

    A few things that I learnt.. firstly, if you indent a porsche with those gloss wood trims or carbon fibre printing trimming around the door handle there.. 85% chance the door handle that u pull will scratch the material. -_-

    secondly.. heated seats are so not necessary in singapore. but it was 'cool'.

    thirdly.. the drinks holders can double up as walkie talkie holders..

    fourthly.. i have no idea how to start the stopwatch on the centre of the dashboard of the porshe. i've tried EVERY button on the console and steering wheel but none of them start the stopwatch damn. (well every button except PSM OFF)

    fifthly.. i love the fact that the rear spoiler can be raised and lowered but the stupid button only has one light to indicate on = up, off = down but yet it's so complicated to operate.. with a series of different pressing and holding the button.. (press once the thing come up, press again nothing happen, press and hold it go down. press again nothing happen. etc. so confusing bleah)

    sixthly, the seats quite comfortable la

    seventhly, the navigation system seems quite alright. although the map loaded was for europe and thus was useless for all intents and purposes in singapore (of cos u can change the disc to singapore)

    eighthly, telephone function says "insert sim card". I say "insert to where?"

    ninthly, the SOS button on the console is very tempting. i wanna press it. will it launch a red hand flare to the sky and alert the police? =D i wish =(

    ok that's a brief review.
