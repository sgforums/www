    you are willing to spend $1500/mth.

    less petrol of $300 = $1200

    assuming you are staying at HDB, season parking = $65
    plus, parking here and there, ERP, say = $55
    Total for parking and misc = $120
    less this cost = $1200 - $120 = $1080

    here comes the fun part:

    A. assume your insurance is $1000/yr, this amount will allow you to get most saloon car.
    per month will be $1000/12 = $85

    and road tax, depending on the size of the engine:
    <1600 cc = $1000/yr (approx), = $85/mth (approx)
    1600cc < size < 2000cc = $1500/yr, = $130/mth

    So, amount left for installment
    = $1080 - $85 - $85
    = $900+ for a car <1600 cc

    OR,
    = $1080 - $130 - $85
    = $800+ for a 1600cc < size < 2000cc

    B. assume you want to get a 2 door sports car, your insurance is $1800/yr.
    per month will be $2000/12 = $150

    and road tax, depending on the size of the engine:
    <1600 cc = $1000/yr (approx), = $85/mth (approx)
    1600cc < size < 2000cc = $1500/yr, = $130/mth

    So, amount left for installment
    = $1080 - $150 - $85
    = $800+ for a car <1600 cc
    (the only car i can think of for this amount of installment, for sports car is PROBABLY a mazda mx-5...)

    OR,
    = $1080 - $130 - $150
    = $750+ for a 1600cc < size < 2000cc
    (but usually, for this amount of installment, you won't be able to get any 2 doors sports car, maybe the hyundai tuscani...)

    So, this is my estimation for you. calculation is based on 10years loan, with a bit of downayment (apporx 10%)... of course, talking about purchasing a new car.
