    Hi everyone, i don't know how should i start this or where i should put this but the sad fact is that i lost my cousin due to a traffic accident last night. It was around 1am when my family heard the news that my cousin with 3 of his friends, one of them the driver, was involved in a traffic accident. On route to hospital, he died and his heart stopped while the front passenger died immediately and the other back passenger(which is also my friend)* just passed away only, the driver's status is unknown at this point of time. I lost a dearest cousin and a friend whom i only knew for a few months, both of them were close and it devastates me to see them just pass away like that, but why am i writing this for?

    The main reason for this thread is that i hope to pass a message to all drivers(regardless just qualified or driving for a long time). When u want to speed, think about the lives u are carrying inside the car. Especially to youngster who love the thrill of speeding late in the night with your friends, think about whether u can live through the burden when you have caused your friend's death through your reckless driving. Advice to one and all, you want to speed, think twice.

    Thank you for taking the time to read this.

    *edit* updated at 11.40am.
