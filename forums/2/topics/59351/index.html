    S15 is 250hp and Skyline is 280hp or 185kw and 206kw
    Personally I would get a Skyline of course because it has more power, more torque. For Skyline, there are a few models, 25GT-T, 25GT and GTR these ranges are R34s. 25GT=non turbo and rwd, 25gtt=single turbo and rwd, gtr=twin turbo and 4wd. Although the GTR and 25GT-t have the same power, gtr has more torque than the 25gt that makes it faster. The 25gt model is a nonturbo, and the power is around 265hp. But there is no point owning a GTR in sg coz u have no place to run and most probably only can showoff with your car; lastly the price tag would probably turn people off.
