While they stay at home and happily shake their legs when they send in their vehicles in for repair. Not only are they endangering the lives of other road users especially motorcyclists, they are a pest to other road users as well.

We all work for a living and dealing with stress are part and parcel of life. I just hope that taxi companies can really take serious action against those errant taxi drivers who think they are the kings of the road.
