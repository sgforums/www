    Channel NewsAsia - Friday, January 22
    ERP rates up at 3 gantries

    SINGAPORE: The ERP rate will go up at three gantries from next month.

    The gantries at East Coast Parkway and on the KPE Slip Road onto ECP are now at S$3, up by a dollar, between 7.30am and 8am.

    The rate at the Thomson Road gantry is now at S$2, up by 50 cents, between 8am and 8.30am.

    The revised rates will kick in on February 1.
