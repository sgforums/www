    Let me try to expand on those answers...

    Different sized rims do somewhat affect your ride... Larger rims are nicer, but do somewhat affect handling of the car... not to mention fuel consumption... Not too sure about this, remember reading about this quite a while back...
    In a way, yes, bigger LOOKS better... but sometimes smaller rims are better for fuel consumption...

    HIDs stand for High Intensity Discharge lights... these are those very bright lights you see on some cars these days. Some cars come stock with them, others come as additional options... HIDs are not just chio... they do give off a more intense light than normal conventional headlights, but the danger is the glare they give off to oncoming traffic that could blind drivers if the lights are not directed to the ground. In such situations, HIDs are a danger to other drivers as they may temporarily blind oncoming drivers, causing them to lose their night vision and they might lose control of the car.

    This is especially so when you're driving in areas that are not lit by any overhead lamps, where it is totally dark. If you get blinded by HID lights, there is a risk that you might not be able to see where you are going on the road... and might lose control of the car...

    what is the difference between dohc & sohc ? Double and single overhead camshaft. DOHC means there are 2 camshafts controlling the opening and closing of valves. More expensive to manufacture though.

    what is cylinders / value means ? No. of cylinders used in the engine. Cylinders are where air and fuel are combusted to push pistons.

    what is max power bph means ? why some rpm low some high, got purpose ? Maximum power in Brake Horsepower. If developed at lower engine speeds it's more accessible, but after that the output decreases.

    what is max torque ? Maximum twisting force developed by engine

    how to see tire size and rim size ? E.G. 195/55R14: 195 denotes tyre tread width in mm, 55 denotes height of sidewall as % of tyre width, R denotes radial construction, 14 is wheel rm diameter in inches

    what is 2.0 DOHC i-VTEC / PGM-FI ? good? total capacity of cylinders in engine is 2000cc/2.0 litres, i-VTEC and PGM-FI are a form of valve timing and fuel injection respectively. "Good" depends on more variables than these.

    disc brakes vs drum brakes why is pro n con ? Disc brakes are more powerful for lighter vehicles but more expensive to manufacture.

    why rims got so many sizes ? got differences ? Weight and appearance are considerations. Bigger rims mean tyre sidewalls have to be shorter and thus stiffer (aids cornering but makes ride more jittery)

    hid means what ? High Intensity Discharge

    what is vvt or vvt-i ? Variable Valve Timing

    what is cvt or cvt-i ? Continuously Variable Transmission

    what is abs with ebd ? Anti-lock Brakes with Electronic Brake-force Distribution

    what is smart key? A keypod which causes the doors to unlock on their own when you approach the vehicle, and lock when you leave.

    what is afs hid lights ? any good and purpose ? As above. Betterr illumination, more expensive

    Before you ask more questions, go read up 'How Stuff Works'. Link has been provided above.
