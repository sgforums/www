To maximise Auto CVT driving, you should always accelerate to hit 2,500 (thatÂ’s the point when Nissan Latio or many other Auto CVT car changes gear).     So what I did was to quickly (that take skills because it is a fine line between speed driving) cross 2,500 whenever it is possible to do so and not holding it to below 2,000.
