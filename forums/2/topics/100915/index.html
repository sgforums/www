    PIs nowadays are regarded to have lower margins than agents. Not sure how true.

    Agree with TOD that, in terms of packaging, most PIs cannot match agents. That's for obvious reasons such as preferential treatment from manufacturers and economy of scale. Packaging aside, some PI cars come with higher performance specs. Eg, JDM cars from PIs have higher specs than equivalent export models that are carried by agents

    Suggest that if you are looking at PI, it should ideally be for a car that is not available from a local agent. That or you prefer higher performance specs (eg. jdm) for the same car.
