    Originally posted by elindra:

    To me, generally a car is an SUV if the platform is raised so that it can or (looks like it can ) go offroad - a sporty activity so an SUV - Sport Utility Vehicle.

    A MPV is one that multi purpose in nature, like having a big boot, extra rows of seat...

    Citing examples for same manufacturer,
    Mitsubishi Grandis, Colt, Colt Plus can be considered MPV - difference being the size while Mitsubishi Airtrek Turbo, Outlander are considered SUV

    Just my opinions

    Best SUV:
    Offroad Capabilities: Hummer H1/H2, Land Rovers
    Performance: Cayanne Turbo, ATR,
    Versatility: XC90 (and looks too)
    Low Price: Tucson, Vitara, Sportage
    Value-for money: CR-V, RX300
