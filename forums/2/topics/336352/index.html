    Well... my choice is Wheels Asia!

    Its comprehensive coverage of latest automobile news, exclusive car reviews and annual car driveaways; at an affordable price of $3 per issue are their strengths.  

    They even publish an annual issue of Wheels Asia Buyers' Guide at $10 that features available cars from all authorised dealers in Singapore.. their specs and brief review of them.

    I have been buying the magazine every month for 8 years since November 2000.

    You should be able to get them pretty easily at 7-Eleven, petrol kiosks and all major newsstands.
