    Yes, you read it correctly. It is LP550-2

    Lamborghini is rumoured to be testing a Gallardo with two wheel drive. With the deletion of the front differential, it's rumoured to have slimmed down by 30kg. The 0-100kmh time now is 3.9 seconds. Top speed is yet unknown.

    Engine is retuned to cope with two wheel drive and, hence, lost 10 Italian horses. But you won't consider this a big loss if you consider the 30kg weight loss. Externally there's no difference as you can see from the leaked pictures. Only that added racing stripe down the center and maybe a few special colours for it.

    Be ready to see it unveiled real soon at the next motorshow.
    Added: And instead of marketing as a higher performance Gallardo selling at a higher price, it's actually going to be 10% cheaper than the LP560-4. That's gonna cost roughly S$800,000. Surprise surprise.
