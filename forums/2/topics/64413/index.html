Ok here's my take on this.... i've been playing wif TAMIYA 1:24 scale models for a while now have 3 in my possession and one more skyline to built.... always shop from this place in funan last time... i haven't done any model shopping for the past 2 yrs but i think it closed down...

    2 popular locations to buy plastic kits and paints and other stuff for buildign up one is either bugis..or Specialist shopping centre 3 floor.... bugis has more tank kits and japanese anime stuff... but for a wide variety die die have to go tai seng... and they are onli open during office hrs on weekdays and half days on sat.......

    As for building models, my advice.. dun buy your dream car or yoru fav car model to build 1st... coz usually 1st try will always be disaster... like when i 1st got mine i wrecked the bumper paint job and numerous decals... try on something less fanciful... i.e. no F1 or WRC cars wif lots of decals...... u might consider investing in airbrush and other paint tools like mask tape and paint pens.. ....
