    $3+ avg per day is not practical. we need to include 2 bus + 1 mrt per trip on avg. $2.50 per trip. $6 per day is more reasonable, averaging over weekends and nights out.

    To own a basic car + all stuffs would require abt $1.5k a month. (dont forget insurance and servicing and repair and fines!).

    So even for a family of 5, public tpt is still abt half owning a car.

    Tat's from the cost point of view. Quality of life is totally another story.

    OPC are meant for a niche segment who in my opinion are not meant for tight income folks.

    My conclusion is
    1. public tpt is much lower then owning a car
    2. if single, and job does not require it, is best take public tpt
    3. for family, and can afford it, best own a car.
