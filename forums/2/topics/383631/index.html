No more poles for TP Driving Test wef March 2010

  • Rednano
    3 Dec 09, 09:35

    SINGAPORE: From March 2010, poles will not be used during the TP driving test.

    The poles are used to help learner drivers park their cars.

    The Traffic Police have alerted driving centres and instructors about the new requirement for those taking Class 3 and 4 practical tests.

    Responding to media queries, a police spokesperson said the change is to enhance the standard of the driving tests as well as the competency level of the learner drivers.
    The method of teaching and testing learner drivers to park their vehicles without poles is also practised in countries such as the UK and Australia.
