    Firstly here is the list of type of engine configarations and the advantages and the disavantages. Can the expert add more information in there please ?

    In line engine

    Adv - smooth, easy to service
    Dis- high nose increase aerodynamic drag

    V engine

    Adv - Shorter crankshaft increases stiffness and strength, shortens length of engine block, lower centre of gravity than in-line
    Dis - Not as simple to manufacture and access for servicing

    Box engine

    Adv - lower centre of gravity, low nose good for aerodynamics
    Dis - hard to service, expansive to make, difficult to lubricate properly

    Wankel Rotory engine

    Adv - light , smooth , compact, high pwer-to-size ratio
    Dis - high exhaust emissions due to insufficient control of airflow, high heat losses

    Secondly , since Rearwheel (FR) drive car is easier to steer why is it Frontwheel (FF) Drive car still widely use. Is it due to the cost and the complexity of the design or ease of car design. It's the more space-efficient design for family cars.
