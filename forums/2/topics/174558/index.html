    Light scratches and hairline scratches are able to be covered up by doing a polish job.
    Touching up with those marker pen is not recommended unless the area is not obvious.
    For those deep scrathes the area will be mended and the whole panel will be respray so as there will not be any differences in colour between the new and old coat of paint.
