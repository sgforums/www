    Hi Shadez.

    I wouldn't dare to say I'm a good/experience driver, but is only 3 days since I got my driving licence.

    But my parents willing to let me drive their valuable Honda Civic. I hope my would be tips and might able to help you in your future driving.

    All these started on my revision lesson, whereby my instructor allows me to drive like a 'maniac'. Is all about judgement, speed and how daring you are, that's what I learn and what my instructor told me.

    The first day I got my driving licence, I drove home, I got horn by a car, when I cut into his lane, despite I signal my intention for more than 3 sec. That car did allow me to enter, but I wasn't daring enough to cut in, so it speed up to close the gap, which at that time I was about to lane change.

    With a P-plate, there's road driver bound to be nicer and mean to you. So far, I have met a balance of both.

    Is okay to be horn by other road driver. They are there to warn you their presence, don't let this lower your morale. Cos one of these days, you will able to get your chance to horn them back.

    Like the another day, a qualified driver about to cut into my lane without signaling intention, I horned that driver.

    My confident was built during my revision lesson and recent public road driving. But despite me able to show my confident towards my parents, they still deem that I'm still not able to drive alone. Probably I just got my driving licence only a couple of days ago.

    All I can say is, judgement (take note of surrounding), speed (for overtaking or slow down when approaching hazzard), daring (on overtaking, on how calm you be).

    Hope these helps.
