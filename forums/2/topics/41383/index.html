    Vehicle with number "RD XXXX" are rental vehicle under the scheme, Honda Intelligent Community Vehicle System.

    The Petrol & Electric Hybrid model of HONDA CIVIC, which was launched in Japan on Dec 13, 2001, is used as the ICVS car in Singapore. As an ICVS member, you will be among the first to drive this latest environmentally friendly car in Singapore.

    The most distinctive benefit Honda ICVS offers is that it allows members to access the car even without booking. Secondly, it allows members to use the car without having to commit to a return time in advance. Thirdly, it allows members to pick up and return the car at any port that is convenient to them. Fourthly, it uses environmentally friendly cars for saving energy and reducing level of pollution.

    All trips are charged $2.00 on the first 20 minutes block and $0.20/minute thereafter. You can now save on short trips during the Overnight use and WEPH use. For Overnight use, there is a time charge cap of $20, daily between 7 pm and 8 am. For WEPH use, the time charge cap is $70 per day. This applies between 7 pm on Friday evenings and 8 am on Monday mornings or from 7 pm on the public holiday eve to 8 am the morning after the holiday. In addition to the time charge, there is also a distance charge of $0.30/km.

    SERVICE TYPE SERVICE HOUR

    8:00am - 7:00pm
    Overnight Service Mondays to Sundays
    7:00pm - 8:00am
    Weekend/Public Holiday (1-Day) Saturdays, Sundays & Public Holidays
    7:00pm on eve - 8:00am the following day

    At the end of each trip, the on-board touch screen will display the usage charges to the member. All usage charges will be processed by the system and billed to the member on a monthly basis.

    Bismarck,

    Diplomatic or Foreign Armed Forces Vehicles are exempted (under Vienna Conventions) from Singapore road tax, COE and Petrol tax. Meaning they only have to pay OMV and pay about 50-70cents/litre on petrol.

    Humvees are not meant for speeding but they are damn good for transporting a heavy load (try towing an artillery piece with a 1.5 litre Soluna). The have a low horsepower rating but high torque. Vehicles that runs on diesel has these charateristics.

    Also, there are petrol (gasoline) versions of Humvees meant for consumer use.

    Humvees are designed according to military specs, so they dun tend to be very comfortable to sit in. Hummer 2 is more for the consumer use, that's why they have a smaller engine, higher power output and more spacious.
