    Yes, absolutely possible.

    BTT results are PERMANENT. Once you pass it, you will NEVER have to take it again.

    FTT is slightly different. If you took FTT with BBDC, and you quit BBDC, results will still stick with you because BTT and FTT results are kept by TP. BBDC or any other schools have nothing to do with the BTT and FTT. But FTT's results don't last forever like BTT. I think FTT's results is only valid for a year or two. So you must pass everything before you previous FTT's results is void and nullified.
