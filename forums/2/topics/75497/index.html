    simply put ..... turning on OD is enabling the final gear for your car's auto transmission

    turn it off and your auto transmission will not use the final gear

    you will understand the concept if you understand a manual transmission car. why higher fuel consumption? try using only up to 4th gear for a manual transmission car (even on the highway) and you will understand ...

    or, if you have the luxury of a 6th gear, use only the 1st 5 gears ....
