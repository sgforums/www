    I used to roll back on the slope during my lessons, more worse is sometimes can and sometimes cannot, so I can't be sure of whether I can or not.

    Then slowly realise just have to do synchronised movements when reached half clutch and releasing hand brake. This is what I do

    1. Rev up to at least 2.5k rpm or 3k if wan to make sure
    2. Release clutch to till u feel the car "wanting" to move ahead (if can't feel, then can judge by when rpm start dropping)
    3. Then do 3 movements at the same time - release clutch, step accelerator and release handbrake and off u go.

    Most impt to remember when "taking off" is hand push down, left leg up, right leg down.

    Hope my experience is useful to you
