    hi all,
    me new member...!
    i drive an altezza...the 4-inline (3s-ge) version) 210 hp...not the 1g-fe (6-inline 160hp)...
    i love the FR handling characteristic, and matched with the 6-speed manual gearbox...it can easily do 0-100km/h in 7.5 secs....you'll feel attached to the tyres and know what the footworks are doing...
    the brakes are commanding...the car is very stable at high speed..
    the block (3sge) is very reliable and strong...(the fact that this block has been around and tested for 15 years...you don't have to worry to rev the engine up to 7600rpm (the max power point)...
    best car i've ever had...many other good features...like interiors and so on....but the above is what i like the most....
