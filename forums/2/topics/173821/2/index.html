    The new model is extremely cool-looking. The only part I don't like is the front intake grill. Looks like a last-minute job and they didn't know what to do so they just dumped a plain grill on it.

    The interior looks great, they have followed Honda's 3 button look. Almost all car manufacturers have really gone up a notch in terms of interior styling (look at the Mazda 3, Honda Accord Euro) and Toyota has finally caught on, albeit a little late.

    I just purchased a Nissan Maxima (branded as Cefiro in Singapore) and I chose it over the Camry because although the latter was stable and performed alright on the road, it didn't excite me and I felt it was too conservative (hence some people calling it the "Ah Pek" car). The Maxima was more comfortable, better interior and ran like a rocket thanks to its 3.6L V6 VQ engine.

    It's still a good car though, and with the new model I'd expect Toyota to give the other models even greater run for their money.
