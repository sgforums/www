    The Fit and the Jazz are exactly the same in terms of outlook, size and engine capacity.

    Both have 1339cc engine. It is just that Kah Motor unshamelessly market their Jazz as 1.4L(which is obviously untrue). The Fit is bought in by Parallel importers.

    The OMV for both are almost the same, though prices are vastly different. That is the reason why most pple go for the Fit rather than the Jazz.
