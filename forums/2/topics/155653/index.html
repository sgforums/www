  • calfskin
    5 Oct 05, 15:31
    In cityspeed, there are some "plans" that charges by the hour, there are other "plans" that charge as a package, e.g. overnighter, weekend pakages etc. All these come with "free km".

    there's this thing somewhere behind the steering wheel called a tripmeter. some digital, some analog, but they serve the same purpose, which is to measure the distance you've travelled. Once you clocked the allocated "free km", you start paying additional km charges.

    For the hard facts and figures, how it works, charges, why not you call Cityspeed directly and get all your questions answered?
