    I don't know about other cars, but for kelisa, auto transmission only have 4 gear. Manual have 5 gear. More gear (with correctly calculated gear ratio) should be more efficient in builting speed. Also the last gear's ratio can afford to be bigger, compared to lesser gear. Thus at say 100kph, manual's rpm would be around 2800 at the fifth gear, while auto would be over over 3000. And thus the FC is always better for manual cars compared to auto.

    Secondly, the more complex the system is (manual, tritonic......) the heavier the car, the more expensive the car, the more maintainene needed.
