  • jiayong
    30 Jul 06, 17:30
    To scrap a car.

    1. Chassis with correct chassis number
    2. Engine Block minimum with engine number. You can keep the head and intake and exhaust manifold all that.

    When u scrap a car with LTA, they will need verfication from the scrap yard after a certain period of time. The scrap yard must be in possession of the car else if discovered, they will be fined.

    You cannot keep a scrap car. LTA doesnt allow that.

    What u can do is strip out all the parts u want and find a place to store them.

    If you want to keep it. You have to deregister the car, register your car with LTA as a de-registered car.

    They will want you to provide information on where the car will be stored.(they will do spot checks) And should you want to move it, you need to ask permission from LTA and tow it around.

    Have I answered all your queries?
