    You have only one driving licence for various classes of vehicles. If you accumulate enough points for revocation that driving licence as a whole is revoked. You get the accumulative demerit points. Not the Classes of vehicles.

    Therefore riding motocycle you get 3 points, driving car you get 6 points, and driving lorry you get 3 points you are in danger if  you are on probation, even 2B probation. One more point and you will be called up to give reason why your licence (for all classes) should not be revoked.
