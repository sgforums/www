    26 May 03, 14:12
    ARF - Additional Registration Fee
    PARF - Preferential Additional Registration Fee
    OMV - Open Market Value

    OMV is basically In short, cost of the car minus tax/fees and dealer's profit.

    PARF is the amount you get after you de-register your car.

    ARF is 130% of your OMV value.

    Thus the basic cost of a car is OMV + Custom Duty (20% OMV) + ARF + Reg. Fees + COE.

    The selling price that you get from the dealer is the "basic cost" + their profit.

    Hope its helpful....
