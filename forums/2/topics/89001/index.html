I was left in a total shock as I had never thought that this type of thing can happen as I thought that by placing a cash deposit, I will definitely get a car somehow from the shop. But no! The guy just reply me that the previous hirer got the higher priority!!! Thus my weekend was left with no car to drive on... I frantically tried to call the guy whom I had SMS-ed all these while.. and he persistently refused to pick up my call. He just give me a lame excuse that his battery is going to run out and can only SMS. I then SMS to him about my complaints and reasoning and he tried to reply me several times trying to ask for my account number to give me back my money and rejecting my appeal to help me to get a replacement. The funny thing is that he can continue to SMS me for numerous times during the past 3 hours and yet his HP battery haven't run out yet!

    I went on to search on other car rental websites and found that they have policy that states clearly that if the hirer place a deposit on a car, he will get a car replacement if that particular model got extended by the previous hirer!

    My cash deposit was returned back to me though but it left me with great disappointment and a feeling of betrayal. I learnt this lesson through the hard way. So people up there.. be extra careful when you want to rent a car. Check whether if the company got such policy to protect the hirer. I obtained the advertisement thru Singnet classifeds.
