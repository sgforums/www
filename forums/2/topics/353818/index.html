    $20 TP test fee = paid to traffic police

    $100-$200 fee = rental of test vehicle + "warm up" fee = paid to the driving centre

    "warm up" fee means for about 45 mins before the actual TP test, u will get to familarise with the test vehicle for use during the TP test.
