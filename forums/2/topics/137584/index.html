    Originally posted by scotts07:
    I want to hear frm u guys,hw u think abt the new NISSAN LATIO..

    Cheers,
    Pros:
    - Solidly fast after 5 seconds, smooth ride
    - Cooling alcantara and leather seats (even park under sun)
    - FC is avearge good 13-14KM/L (TC said 15KM/L); some only reached 12KM depending on driving technique
    - For hatchback, adjustable rear seats fwd/backwd (less passenger seats means more cargo space) , 45degree/120degree.
    - 6 speakers, solid kenwood radio/mp3 player
    - quiet ride, can't hear engine when driving
    - Foot-break instead of hand-break (I love it!)
    - keyless ignition and door lock/unlock (everyone love it!)
    - if fetch wife, MIL, baby, good space, only loose to 7-seaters MPV

    Cons:
    - slow pick up at complete stop (about 5 econds); that how CVT engine is
    - personally I feel wheels a little bumpy, should change to a better ones if $ not a problem
    - a bit to EX compare to VIOS/ALTIS/CIVIC, shd get ALTIS or VIOS is $ is a big concern
    - No alarm and SE said can't install one as it may affect other electricals; can't find car sometimes in Suntec and Great World

    For any car, test drive at leat twice before deciding.
