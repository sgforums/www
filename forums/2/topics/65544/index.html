    dun mind i give some comment.

    i have friends who tried out boquet and told me it's a waste of money. for the kind of money, how many tank of petrol are you going to save to cover the cost of that thing. and it doesn't really work well too. i personally didn't try it so i dunno.

    other things you can do to your civic is to replace your intake filter to open port type. personally i prefer K&N or apexi. both has got good reviews. but bear in mind it'll reduce some lower end torque but it give more powerful high end.

    for the lower end side, you can throw in 1 or 2 E68. change to denso irdium plug and get the spark plug cable changed. you might want to consider power boost valve but it'll increase a bit of your fuel comsuption. to the little more extreme side, you can consider ADM air charger($600 w/o installation). the air charger gives light turbo charged effect. personally i didn't try this too. cos my car comes turbo charged already.
