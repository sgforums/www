    this is for parallel on left

    1.move up alongside the front car and stop when you're slightly in front.

    2.start reversing slowly, when the back of your car is in line with the back of the front car, achieve full left lock quickly while still reversing slowly.

    3.when your left side mirror is parallel with the back of the front car, turn to get full right lock while keeping your car on the move...if you dry steer, you'll end up too far out. how fast you turn the wheel will depend on how much more u need to move in...practice makes perfect

    4. when you're in position, straighten the wheel and do any minor adjustments
