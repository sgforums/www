    Want to share with my experience with the Geely CK 1.5L automatic car and hope some one else could share his/hers.

     

    I just bought Geely CK a month ago. Run-in for about 2 weeks without modification. There are three huge problems with the design of the car that I was wondering how the car is going to sell well.

     

    1. The engine noise was unbearable - like a sawing machine next to your ear each time you press against the accelerator. The noise is not those that you like to hear as compare to those sporting noise.

     

    2. The low end torque is terribly bad. The dragging of engine makes you feel that the engine has run out of breathes.

     

    3. The fuel consumption per liter of fuel is about 10km. Which is quite bad for a 1.5L car? Found that the car's auto transmission uses hydraulic system and the gear switch from 2nd gear to third gear change at more than 40kmh and 3rd to 4th gear switch at about 70kmh. These are pretty high speed; no wonder the fuel consumption is so high.

     

    After the 2-weeks run-in at a mileage of greater than 1,500km, I decided to modify the car to solve the three issues.

     

    1. I designed and built a resonator box and fixed it in front of the air-filter box. Result is very good. It solves the noise problem and makes the car more like a family car. Those interested could email me at ss7259@gmail.com.

     

    2. I fixed the E-Power System (an automobile enhancement manufacturing company in Singapore) to improve the low end torque and more smooth driving and acceleration. It also improves on the fuel consumption to 10.5km per liter. I'm quite satisfy with the performance except the car's hydraulic auto transmission system which I could not do anything without void the warranty.

    Any one has any advice or has done anything to improve the car?

    SS
