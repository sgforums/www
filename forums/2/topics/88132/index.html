    I used to rent cars in JB frequently many years back. Two of my fav are Pacific and Hawk. Of the two, I prefer the former.

    The rates would be lower if you choose their package, for example, weekend package. I remember paying around RM230 -240 for a weekend package. The best is, they are very flexible when it comes to the time you return your car.
    If you are late for a few hours, they normally won't charge you extra.

    Their cars are (or should I say were?) parked at the Shell station. Not the one near the police HQ, but the one further down. It's about 10mins walk from Citi Square.

    When your return time is after office hours, you just leave the car at the Shell station and dump the key into a box (something like the NTUC car co-op thing). When you return the car after office hours, they won't be there to note your return time, so returning the car late a few hours is OK!

    Get their contact number from Singapore Yellowpages directory.

    Do note that my info could be outdated as it has been a long time since I last rented their cars.
