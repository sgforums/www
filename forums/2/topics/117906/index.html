    Reverse Parking

    For me when I 1st got my car ... i would really observe my car (As in how big) and bear that in mind

    I think this topic is a repeated topic but nvm ... I would suggest you stop at a 45 degree angle to the parking lot (think you know this) ... haha ...

    If you are not confident in getting the car into the lot, GO SLOW and i mean real slow ... Don't worry in blocking others while you are parking (Remember there are experience drivers that took a god damn long time to get into a lot and this might help relieve you alittle)...

    A impt note : Once your rear wheel is in the lot , you are 90% into the lot ... so just go slowly

    *Rear wheel as in if you are reversing into a left lot = Left rear wheel and same for the other direction.

    Please do not try the open door technique and try to just use your mirrors

    Summary : Know the size of your car and go slow if you are unsure
