  • SBS2601D
    20 Sep 09, 21:33

    Put half-clutch at stationary when you are about to move off, but put the brakes on at the same time.

    You will hear the engine rpm getting lower. If it starts to vibrate, you're gonna stall.

    So once you hear the rpm getting lower, just hold it there, and then lift your foot off the brakes and step on the accelerator and release the clutch somemore to the point of vibration but not anymore than that.

    You'll cut your reaction time that way and still achieve a smoother move-off that way.
