    vti is just a variant of civic.. vvti is corolla's variable valve timing technology.. which acts on the exhaust valves.. closing and opening then at different times at different revs to maximise efficiency..

    as for vtec, it modulates both the valves' timing and lift.. in both the exhaust and intake valves.. in honda's normal vtec this is optimised for fuel efficiency and low end torque.. as for sports models its optimise for maximum performance.. while not sacrificing low end torque.. only in the i-vtec engines.. found in accords, odysseys, s2000.. except civics and jazzs and city
