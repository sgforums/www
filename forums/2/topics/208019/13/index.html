    Hiya,

    As i've mentioned in my previous post, its cheaper to brake harder than to engine brake more often cause:

    1) Brake pads are cheaper compared to the wear on your gearbox

    2) Engine brake with the intermittent jerk consumes more petrol cause its a sudden downforce.

    3) unless u know the proper techinique of heel & toe to keep the rpm constant or minimal drop, up if not ur gearings will wear in no time as well u will have to visit the petrol kiosks more often.

    4) If you constantly engine brake, that will reduce the life span of the gearbox to 1/2. Wat could be a good practice would be to tap on the brakes hard once to reduce the speed of the car then slowly downshift.
