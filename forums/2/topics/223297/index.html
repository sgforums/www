    Don't lose heart. It happens to all new drivers. All you need to do is to keep practising your driving skill at different road conditions - during the day, night, rainy days etc.

    Don't rely on your mirror all the time, turn your head when necessary as some mirrors don't show the actual distance between the car and the object. Also, the object may have been in your blind spot. The same applies when changing lane - always remember to turn your head to see if the lane you are going to get into is free. Looking at the side mirror alone won't help.

    Just drive carefully and slowly (don't speed) and I am sure after much practice it'd build up your confidence.

    Good luck!
