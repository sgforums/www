    Actually it is a GT-R. The '2 dr', which is actually referred to as a coupe, comes in GT, GT-T, 25GT and GT-R spec for the R34 Skyline. The 'GTS' is a specification only available on the R32 and R33 models, through the GTS-T and GTS-M models for example.
