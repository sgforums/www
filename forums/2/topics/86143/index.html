    24 Jul 04, 00:27
    Originally posted by LLukeShen:
    "scrap value minus 5%"? Then, why the owner don't scrap the car directly?
    If the owner scrap the car himself, LTA will only give him back a piece of PARF paper which can be used to offset when buying a new car. This piece of PARF paper some more got 6 months expiry date.

    If the owner sells the car to the dealer, the dealer will pay him cash, and of course will deduct 4%-5% off the scrap value as a kind of processing fee. Dealer will not do free business. They sure want to earn $$$. They give you back hard cash and do you expect them to service you for free.
