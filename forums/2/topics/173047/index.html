    The current colt has a revised engine with better engine output and torque. It also sounds more refine. Interior is of reasonable build quality. Some may miss the front bench seats though since the new colt has done away with the original bench seats which can be quite good for couples. Few consumption is not too bad, but it is really nothing fantastic.

    The honda jazz has undergone a facelift. Unless you are some jazz fanatic, the changes to the exterior is minimal. Interior feels much better in this facelift edition. One thing about honda cars is the ability to squeeze space out of nowhere and the jazz has done it very well. Entrance into the rear seats of the car is easier compared to the colt. Try getting into both cars and you will know what I mean. However, is it important to note that rear seats of the jazz do not slide. This is one aspect of the colt that is quite pleasing. In terms of fuel consumption, I dare say the the jazz is really quite good.

    Hope the info is useful.
