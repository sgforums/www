    Originally posted by siaokao:
    Hey.. can tell us how the new WRX is like?
    hmm... ok as u can see(or maybe not) from the pics, it was a very wet and cold and foggy day. the new STi had plenty of grip despite the conditions, and was able to accelerate hard, although I did get wheelspin shifting up from 3rd into 4th while blasting out of the last corner onto the main straight. Nothing the car's systems couldn't control though. I saw something light up on the instrument panel almost instantly and the wheelspin was gone. In 4th, charging into the 1st corner which is a 90 degree right hander, all i needed was to stand on the brakes hard and it scrubbed off speed nicely, turn in, get on the throttle, and then nail it. Excellent car. It did shimmy a bit when it hit standing water, but nothing the car's AWD couldn't handle. I'd say, in fact, in these conditions, the STi was the faster car than the Ferrari.
