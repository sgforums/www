    many auto cars actually have 3 physical gears only (mechanically). the overdrive is a mechanical system that allow the 3rd gear to spin off a "4th gear" thereby making it a 4 gears auto system.

    so in normal use, the overdrive should be turned "on", ie the orange light "off". if you drive with O/D off long distance, not only it will waste fuel it will also damage your gear system esp you go high speed.

    "O/D off" means max you can go is 3rd gear and is for going up/down hill or when you tow a caravan when 4th gear is not appreciated or even dangerous to use. some pple use it for overtaking, i think not necessary cos when you "kick down" the accelerator during an overtaking it will shift down from 4th to third or from 3rd to second automatically, as and when the engine requires.
