    Hello Fellow Marchies,

    I am glad that some of you have enjoyed yourselves in the gathering on Saturday.... Today is a mixed and confused day for me. I was supposed to see my SE and sign the contract but somehow, the meeting didn't turn out the way I expected.

    I test-drive the March for one final time. I did it once two weeks ago. This time round, I was rather disappointed with the March cos the engine sound was far too noisy and the entire trip was rather bumpy. My SE did comment that I stepped on very hard at the accelerator for too long period of time unlike my first test-drive. I did remember that I didn't drive too fast in the first test-drive. She commented that I stepped up on my accelerator too hard even before the gear was changed from first to second. No matter what, the noise from the 6-months old March was really uncomfortable for me. My friend who happened to sit in the back seat commented that the suspension of the car was not too good and the entire ride was very bumpy.

    To make a better comparison, I test-drive the Sunny thereafter and I did the same, slamming my accelerator hard and similarly, there are some noise from the engine but it was not too loud as compared to the March. My friend commented that the ride was much more smoother than March.

    I did not intend to use my new car to travel at high speed for long period of time but I cannot imagine myself driving it at the speed of 60km/hr most of the time cos it seems fine at this speed.

    Nevertheless, I still like the March very very much though I notice that the car may not have good suspension. Do you all have problem driving through road humps. The bottom of the car seems to come very close to the road surface as compared to Sunny.

    I have proceed to sign up for the March though I still have strong reservations about this car. I have up to Tuesday noon to decide if I want to change to Sunny instead. I am still confused cos my heart tells me that I should go with the March cos I still have very strong feelings for it though my brain tells me that I should go for the Sunny which is more value for money.

    Do you all have any negative feedback on the engines and suspension of the March? Care to share? Thank you to you All.

    hi tweetie, agree with tedz, dun get influenced by pples enthusiasm, its afterall a very personal decision.

    let you know first, the march indeed cannot handle bumps or humps very well with the stock suspension. it is quite "tight" as it is designed for city driving which usually has good roads. if you go country sides, you will have sore arse, and may even need a barf bag.

    this is most obvious if you make a turn and manouevres a hump at the same time, when you have one wheel (eg. front Left) over the hump before the other wheel (front Right) comes in. you really can get thrown around in the car, depending on your speed.

    also high speed turning, the handling is quite challenging.

    the above shortcomings however, can be improved thru some mods. our boys here will be able to help.

    as for the engine noise, i wonder. mine was very quiet, especially when you are seated in the interior.

    however it is only noisy on the outside when the cooling fan kicks in, when you idle for too long. but all these cannot be heard when you are inside. at speed the engine noise can only get obvious when you go above 120km/h, but like kingmariot said, the engine sounds most pleasing when you go 90-100km/h.

    my 1 cent worth.
