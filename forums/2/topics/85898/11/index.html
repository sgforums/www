  • BoringSilver
    16 Oct 04, 09:12
    TEIN, HKS are all coilovers which are height adjustable. If you set it to a decent height of say 30mm down, it is unlikely to scrape the undercarriage. But please know that coilovers are pretty hard, they might re-wire your neuro-connections.

    There is the Nismo or Impul suspension which is just a normal upgraded set.

    Lowering springs are still quite comfortable, handling would be 7/10 tenths of a full suspension change, but still a lot more sporty with minimal body roll than the standard suspension.

    If anyone decides to upgrade your car's suspension. This is my advice.

    If you are not into having a lowered car but just want something substantial in improving car handling.
    Shock Absorber Upgrade

    If you do not drive hard but want the K12 to look gorgeous without a major financial outlay.
    Lowering Springs

    If you want the full monty cause you want a looker plus improved handling. Money is no object and who cares if i get a headache.
    Suspension Upgrade
