    The tires are stock, then it is better to inflate it to a higher tire pressure like 240 and 230 for the front and rear. As the side walls are not as stiff because these are comfort tires and the profile is quite high. Aspect ratio of 70 for comfort version and 60 for elegance. The ride might be a tad harder than usual but a small price to pay for having slightly better steering feel. This is one way to alleviate the costs of having to change your tyres.

    The CR series of engine require an octane level of 95 for optimum function. That is to say that the engine can run on 92 octane but with a slightly retarded engine timing meaning less power. This also means that running on 98 octane does NOT offer better performance than running on the stipulated octane grade of 95.
