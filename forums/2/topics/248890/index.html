Pump prices up by 10 cents a litre

  • SMRT Buses
    16 Apr 07, 22:26
    Petrol companies across Singapore have increased their pump prices.

    Shell, ExxonMobil, SPC and Caltex have raised both petrol and diesel prices by 10 cents a litre.

    They say a combination of factors have led to the rise.

    These include increases in the price of oil and market conditions.

    Petrol prices now range between $1.63 and $1.97 a litre, depending on the grade.

    For diesel, it is $1.21 a litre.
