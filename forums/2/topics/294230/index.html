    The law you broke is S120(1) (B) CAP 276, Road Traffic Act.

    The penalty comes under these broad categories

    Parking within a Demerit Points No Parking Zone
    Stopping within a Demerit Points No Stopping Zone
    Parking abreas5t of another vehicle
    Parking within a pedestrian crossing
    Stopping at a zebra-crossing
    3 pt and $120.

    In Singapore this is considered lucky, because it is to compound the offence, so that no need to turn out a judge and book a court-room for you to say the summon only says disobeying no waiting sign.. nv say within derimet points lehz.. different ritre?
