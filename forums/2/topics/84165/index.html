    read. the . signs.

    learn routes by road names first rather than landmarks
    although landmarks are useful, it's easy to get them confused because of poor explanation
    road names are much more precise

    really sit up and take notice of your surroundings because observation is the best way of learning roads
    and practice your familiar routes first, like home to work, home to town
    once you're sure of them, then learn another route to somewhere further
    learn to read a map - it's not that difficult really
