    Double yellow lines = no parking, no waiting, you may alight or pick up passengers, and go off. Fine=S$70, no demerit points.
    Double zig-zag yellow lines = no stopping, no parking, no waiting, no alighting or picking up, in short no stopping is allowed. Fine=S$120 + 3 demerit points.
    Example, if you just stop at double zig zag yellow lines just few seconds, alight or pick up friends...etc, if spotted by TP, they might record your vehicle no, and send a notice of parking offence to register owner of the vehicle.

    Anyway, in HK or some other countries, if vehicles are stopped at even double yellow lines, for picking up or alighting, it is an offence. I experienced once in HK, Taxi stop and pick me up (less than 5 secs), police man was waiting in front, driver was booked. Actually double yellow lines means 24 hours no stopping, parking or waiting. But Singapore, since most of drivers ignore this, LTA then introduce single / double zig zag yellow line(s).....funny....
