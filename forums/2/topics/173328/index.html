    The COE you want to re-new would be the PQP (prevailing Quota Premium), i.e.moving average of the past 3 months COE. 5 years COE pay half.

    If you re-new 5 years COE, after the 5 years, you would not be able to re-new any longer. Thus if you still want to keep the car or be able to sell it on the market, it would be advisable to re-new with the 10 years COE..... till u renew it for the second time, i.e. car is now registered for 30 years then it would be classified as an antique car already.....dats another story altogether....
