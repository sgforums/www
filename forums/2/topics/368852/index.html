    hmm, the rate of fees for you to pay largely depend on how fast you learn or how quick you can master the skill of it.

    im learning at bbdc currently, the range is around 1.7k to 2.+k depending on how many times you use to pass tp (traffic police test).

    theory wise damn cheap, for bbdc students, if both btt and ftt pass on the first time, $100 enough alr. i, myself pass both tests on the first attempt

    for bbdc theory student, you need to attend 2 btt lessons, and 2 ftt lessons, which cost around $17+ per lesson
