    Try not to get a COE car.

    Road Tax goes up every year by 10% up to a maximum of 150% on the 5th yr of the extension. A 1.6l COE car would easily cost you ard $1.7-1.8k rd tax.

    Additional concern is the Loan for COE Car usually interest it at least 3.8% up to 4.6% depending on Banks / Financial Inst etc.

    Also the maintenance for such cars are usually more expensive as wear & tear would be significant. Expect to have to fork out additional $$$ every time you go for your routine svs as things starts to spoil(wear & tear). Fuel Consumption would also not be good as most of these cars are on carbruettor. You might not get more than 10km/l even for a Light-Footer.

    Rgds
