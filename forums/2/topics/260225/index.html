    To find out whether your car has been booked for traffic offences in Malaysia, call up JB's Traffic Police Info-line at 0207-225-4466 from 8am to 3pm on weekdays and from 8am to 11am on Saturdays and cite your vehicle's registration number. It does not matter where in Malaysia or how long ago the offence was committed. No penalty will be imposed on overdue fines. A motorist can either call personally at JB's Traffic Police headquarters in Jalan Tebrau or in Kulai, and pay in cash or with a bank draft, or send someone on his behalf. (The Straits Times 6 Jul 2001) (4)[/url]
