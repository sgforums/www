    PARF is some additional tax that you pay besides COE.
    The value has changed over time, sometime ago it was 80% of the OMV, then it dropped, and dropped again.

    This is also the money that you get back when you scrap the car at the end of 10 years (if scrap before 10, you get remaining of COE back too).

    Anyway, the COE of tha car is $28~k. That means $28~k was paid to use the car for ten years.
    Right now the COE is less than $20~k for 10 years.
