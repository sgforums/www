    So far ive got 32 or so 1:18 models. As for 1:24, they arent really the way to go if you want real accuracy and detail in your models. Some are however very detailed, but these are most likely models of older cars and are very very expensive (ie. diecast from Frankin Mint).

    The best manufacturers for 1:18 diecast currently are Kyosho, Minichamps, Autoart, UT (although now they are out of business and fairly hard to obtain) as well as more exotic (ie. much more expensiive) makes such as Exoto, CMC, Biante, Classic Carlectables and Lane models.

    Usually Autoarts sell for between 100$ - 160$ depending on model and editions.

    Kyosho 1:18s sell for 130$ or so

    If you can find UT models, they range from 50$ or so to more than 200$ for the older, more hard to find models (such as the UT Mclaren F1 LM roadcar i recently obtained).
