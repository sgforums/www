    Hi, the 2.7 V6 model Tuscani, is not very powerful, i drove the 2002 Integra Type R and 2005 Integra GSR.

    GSR(A) vs Tuscani both A (real drag tests)
    2.0 Tuscani; Totally CMI; GSR can outrun it easily
    2.7 Tuscani, Almost equal GSR, short distance win GSR abit, long distance and high speed GSR can chiet it...

    The Integra GSR has very good low to mid end torque, its almost flat;it can bring out >16kgm of torque to bear from as low as 2500rpm. and reach maz torque at only 4000rpm. The Type R on the other hand is a high rev performer. At below 4000rpm, the GSR actually feels more torquey. In local context; <120km/h, the GSR is a tough opponent;discounting turbo equipped vehicles.

    Regarding the back seat; it is the largest in coupe class; plus the rear flat floor; disgarding roof height, its is very spacious...no problem taking 2/3 adults comfortably....

    Regarding Mazda 3, its a looker, but there is no performance to speak off...actually it is more powerful at high speed...due to its variable manifold...

    Oh yes, due to its 12/16v configurations, the GSR is super frugal on fuel, no problem getting ~11km/L
