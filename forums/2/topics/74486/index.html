    Originally posted by Theatre Of Dreams:
    so gotta get a car of racing capabilities yet is road legal? hmmm then how can the licence broaden up my scope? able to take part in various professional races? thanks.
    ok if u do not want to take up go karting, the alternative is to start in cars. a good place to start is in autotests. an autotest is like a sort of rally, where u need to drive a fixed course around cones placed on the ground. autotests are good starting grounds because there are many diff classifications of cars, which include classes for stock, showroom spec cars. If u have a car already, it should be ok to use it for autotests. do get the 2004 rule book from SMSA and read thru it, noting what is allowed for stock classes and what is not.
    the licence does not broaden your scope. having one does not make u skillful suddenly overnight. in fact, the only reason that u get one is because it is already determined that u are skillful enough to own a racing licence, which is why it has been issued to you.
    so with a official FIA licence(issued thru SMSA), you will be able to take part in events, either national or international, depending on your licence class. eg. my licence class is International Senior C. that allows me to race in the advanced class races worldwide.
