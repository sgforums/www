    Bring a experience driver, best if he does how to drive aggressively. Get big family members or friends along to test space and power during test-driving. Don't be afraid to ask for a test-drive. Don't sit down with them to discuss payments and plans..... only waste your time. Get their namecard enough, so later you can call for an update of the price. Spend you time browsing the cars' interior and test-driving. Plan your road, so you save time, they only allow test-drive until 7pm.

    If you are considering Picanto, you can also consider Kelisa. Both are Cheap nice car that can easily hit above 16km/L FC. Do check out Swift, very quiet cabin when I last sit in 1. Oh ya, if you have time, check out those cars above your budget. So you want what you are missing while saving those few thousand. Manual Lancer is a nice car to test. Will let you experience decent power and good handling.
