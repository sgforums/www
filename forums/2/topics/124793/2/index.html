    Reasons for chosing City:
    -> Good FC... 1 litre about 14 - 18 (depends on driving style). Important because you are burning away cash in the long run
    -> Reasonable leg room space for passengers
    -> Good value for money.
    -> Very modable
    -> Big boot very useful, especially if you are a family man doing your grocery shopping once a week

    Reasons for not chosing Vios
    -> Stuck up SE....
    -> Small leg room for passengers
    -> FC lose out to City....although not by a lot.
