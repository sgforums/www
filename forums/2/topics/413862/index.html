    About Lions Befrienders 
    Lions Befrienders Service Association (Singapore) was formed in 1995, providing befriending and outreach programmes to more than 4,000 lonely and vulnerable seniors island wide and at three Seniors Activity Centres – Ang Mo Kio, Bendemeer and Mei Ling Street - which aim to 
    help relieve the loneliness and isolation of the seniors. 

    About Lions Home for the Elders 
    Established in May 1980, Lions Home for the Elders has been providing holistic nursing care, long term residential care and other eldercare services to about 354 needy elderly staying in our two aged-care facilities - one 200-bed Toa Payoh Home and the other 154-bed Bedok Home. About 95% of our elderly residents come from low-income families or are destitute.
