    Anyone ever encounter poseur cars on the road? They are so nicely bodykitted with mean sounding exhaust systems, they can have even seasoned car enthusiasts fooled. Personally, I have been fooled by some impreza 1.6 TS....everything including the interior was done up as per STI spec, only thing was the red brake calipers.....not the golden colour normally found on STI...but still I almost bought it until the fella floored the car. Engine note rises NOWHERE as fast as an STI and no PSST or BOV leh. Of course not fast also

    While I can appreciate the work and effort put into detailing a car, I think drivers of 'replica' cars should not race other cars, you will remove all doubt about the true credentials of your cars.

    Today I had my first encounter with a poseur M3, yep it had quad exhausts, M3 styled side sills, M3 badge, M3 styled bumpers, skirts....anyway I was convinced it was an M3....and that bronze yellow colour so unmistakedly M3! Driver revving engine....engine note very nice, until lights went green, engine very loud....but how come cannot even keep pace with me and a passat ABT beside....we have not even floored our pedals...just a brief step-off Initially, I did not even press the accelerator much as M3 is way above my league (no use to challenge) but the pseudo M3 was so sluggish, it was HARD not to leave him behind.

    Actually, immediately I knew the car was a dud....have seen how fast a M3 can really go. Think the car was a nicely done up 318 CI....not even a 320CI as a 320 definitely has stronger performance.
