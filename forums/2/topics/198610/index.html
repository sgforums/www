    I am helping a friend to ask a question with regards to his DIPS record. As I am unable to find an answer in the FAQ of the SPF website, I hope someone in this forum can assist me in this. Many thanks!

    It is stated that "A new driver's licence will be revoked upon accumulation of 13 or more demerit points within a period of 12 months from the date of the grant of the licence."

    SCENARIO

    If Driver A passed his driving on 02 Sep 05. That means Driver A is on probation till 01 Sep 06. During this period, Driver A committed an offence e.g. Failing to conform to red light signal which carries 12 demerit points on 05 Jun 06.

    That leaves Driver A with no more room for errors before Driver A's licence gets revoked.

    The questions are

    A. When Driver A approaches 01 Sep 06, will Driver A get the other half of 12 points allowance since Driver A has passed his/her probation period? (Points Calculation = 24 - 12 = 12 as on 01 Sep 06)

    B. The 12 demerit points which Driver A accumulated on 05 Jun 06 will only be cancelled on 04 Jun 07 thus getting back his/her 24 points allowance only on 04 Jun 07? (Points Calculation = 24 - 00 = 24 as on 04 Jun 07)
