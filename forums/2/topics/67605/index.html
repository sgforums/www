    I've just got the 1.6AT H/B in Dec 03. Faceligt looks great ! After two months of serious urban driving (I only touch the expressways once or twice a week), the average consumption is 9.8km/L. My friend who owns the Mar 03 version clocked 13km/L (80% highway driving).

    The interior width is slightly narrower than the corrolla. About a half-a-shoulder size. headroom is terrific. Height of seats is great for mothers carrying baby and old folks when entering and alighting. Engine gives quite a purr when engine is rev 3000rpm. PIckup is quite sluggish (considering its 88bhp/tonne compared to Corrolla ALTIS 92bhp/tonne). The car is great for family and also for those who don't have the $$$ for MPVs like PICNIC.

    My previous car Swift 1.0 sedan clocks 15.6km/L on highway and 12.8km/L on urban driving.
