    For a car license, have to go through 2 tests, practical lessons and finally the TP test.

    Basic Theory Test (BTT)
    Final Theory Test (FTT)

    Once you reach 18, go apply for your BTT.
    Pass liao then can apply for Provisional Driving Licence (PDL).
    After you get your PDL can start taking driving lessons le.
    Private or school up to you.
    Usually about 20 - 24 lessons, depending on student.
    But if take school lessons, be prepared to fork out about $1.7k - $2.0k
    FTT must pass before your TP test date can liao.
