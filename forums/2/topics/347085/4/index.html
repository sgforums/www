    Hi everyone, I'm new here.  I took the plunge and ordered a Cerato Forte pre launch and got the 1.6A SX at 45K, COE inclusive, 6 months road tax and Rikekool solar films thrown in.  Solar films comes with 10 yr warranty!  Any time the film starts to peel, send the car back and they will have it replaced, quite cool.  My friend is using this brand of solar film and he mentions that his 'transition glasses' does not darken when placed in the car, but it darkens after he winds down the windows, so it can't be that bad.

     

    The interior is really spacious while the exterior is sporty.  What I did not like is the 1.6 versions did not offer you leather seats, although you can get it done yourselves outside.  Exterior design was by Kia's Chief Design Officer, Peter Schreyer, who designed the Audi TT also.

     

    The feel when test driving is good, however it is noticeably noisier than the Hyundai Avante.  Possibly because of the 17 inch rims they use, suspension is also harder than the Avante which makes it a little bumpy when going over little pot holes.  Not sure if this is to compensate the height since it is 30mm lower than the Avante.

     

    The sales person also mentioned that the engine is the same as the Cerato Variant 2, they sup-ed up the Forte's engine so that it is performing almost at its limit, they also gave it an aluminium block, making it lighter than previously.

     

    I checked with my collegue who owned a Cerato variant 2, he mentioned that FC is about 11-12KM?  Hence I am not optimistic that the Forte will be any better, specially with those 17" sports rims offered.  I won't be driving it for long distances, hence not too bothered by the FC.
