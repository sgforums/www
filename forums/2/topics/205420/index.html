Bidding Of Car Registration Numbers For The SGM Series

  • SMRT Buses
    17 Aug 06, 14:34
    Bidding for car registration numbers 1 to 9999 in the SGM series will start from 8.00am on 19 August 2006 (Saturday) and end at 12.00 noon on 26 August 2006 (Saturday). Interested members of the public are invited to submit their bids.

    The minimum bid amount is $1,000/-. Offer higher than $1,000/- shall be in multiples of $1/-. All bids must be made on prescribed forms and envelopes that can be obtained from:

    Information Counter

    Land Transport Authority

    10 Sin Ming Drive

    Level 1

    Singapore 575701
