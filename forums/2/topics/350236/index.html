    I had the same questions early this year when the COE price started coming down.

    I've been a Honda person for more than 10 years.  In March this year, I traded in my 6 year old Honda Civic for a Golf GT Sport.  One of the reason's I didn't like the new civic was because the seating position was quite low even with the seat all the way up.  Could be a silly reason for most people :).  And the fact that there are a dime a dozen Civics on the road.


    The Golf is my first conti car... and I absolutely love it.  The Golf IS DIFFERENT.  It's kind of hard to compare it to a Civic.  It is so easy to think of it as a sports car when in fact it is not.  It picks up to 100 km/h so easily when you still think it's could be doing 60.  A lot of power when you need it but with as much fuel economy as my old Civic.  Road tax is also cheaper because of the 1.4L engine.

    It's gonna be very difficult for me to go back to a Japanese car after this.  The Golf is expensive though.  I was lucky to get it during the sale at 82.8k from VW.  Very happy with the Golf.
