    Depends on yr style of driving

    Like with all cars, you can't expect to mash yr revs to redline at every gear and still expect to have good FC.

    At its best, my MR was doing abt 8+ km/l. Not the best I have seen but considering the power and tune compared to my old rides, it was acceptable.

    In higher states of tune and the occasional revving, I am getting 7+

    AFAIK, the redlines are the same for 8 and MR. Should be same for 7 as well. 9 is anybody's guess since it isn't out here yet.

    Fuel tank is 45l for E7, and 55l for 8/MR. Expect 9 should have same 55l

    Traditionally Evos come with 2 specs: RS and GSR.

    GSR has all the nice frills for daily driving: aircon, Sports ABS, power windows, sports rims, Recaro seats. Comes with SAYC and 6 speed gearbox. Standard turbo for 9 GSR is titanium turbo.

    RS is the full stripped down version meant for track/racing. You don't get aircon, no ABS, no power windows, no sports rims, no Recaro seats, no AYC, 5 speed gearbox (supposedly stronger). Also coems with the new magnesium titanium turbo as standard. This is optional for the GSR

    Most teams go for these and then quickly replace them with far better components anyway, eg 6 pot brakes, racing LSD, even lighter forged rims, full bucket seats, clutch etc

    GT is something new for E9. It is an intermediate spec. Think of it as an RS with some GSR amenities.. aircon, Brembos, ABS, Recaros etc.

    Best part of it is that it is Group N homologated already. Also available in full range of colors like the GSR. RS is limited tow hite.
