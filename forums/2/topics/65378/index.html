    Hi. I would like to add a few more facts. Hope it will help you.

    The earilest Honda Civic SIR registered in S'pore is Dec 95 and the last ones should end around early 2001. The Manual SIR had a Horsepower of 170BHP and 155BHP for the automatic model. Depreciation per year is around $7k to $7.5k for the Auto model and $7.5k to $8k for the manual ones. The estimation is base on dealers price. Do not pay anything higher than this amount. Look for those with a higher COE & Parf value. So in case you need to sell your car earlier, you will not lose too much money.

    If you are under age 25 or your license are less than 2 years, it is not advisable to buy a SIR as the premium cost about 3k to 5k per policy year.
