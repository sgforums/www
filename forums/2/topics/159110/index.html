    Your rear got banged huh? Just one bang, or gangbang?

    Bumpers are made of some kind of fibreglass which can "bounce" in and out. This buckling feature is what protects you (and the rest of the car) from full impact.

    Just shake your bumper...if its still tight, then everything's OK lah. If not, just to go the workshop and re-secure the bumper.

    The only crucial thing at the back of the car I can think of its the fuel tank, but thats very very well-protected (takes a lot more than a small bang to affect it).
