    well.. my logical explaination is that...

    HB cars rear windscreen is more tilted.. the wind resistance from the front cutting through the car will not come into contact or hardly come into contact with the water droplets.. so when driving in the rain, visibility is poorer because the water will not be pushed away.. hence, a wiper is needed to clear the water..

    on the other hand.. sedan's rear windscreen is more towards "horizontal". i believe since of the angle difference, the wind resistance coming from the front have more chance to come into contact with the droplets at the rear end.. pushing away water.. therefore no wiper is needed..
