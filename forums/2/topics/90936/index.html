    I sifted through the replies and not many really answered your question.

    PIs are a force to be reckoned with these days. The cars they sell, most models are not sold by the agent here. If they are, they offer quite a discount. Anyway, the worry here is aftersales. Not many are credible PIs. The only ones that are good are Hua Yang, Ricardo Motors to name a few. The latter has its own workshop and I was servicing my Celica there for as long as I owned it. When u decide to buy a PI car, please check how many of teh same makes are running on the road. If there are many, the stockist will bring in the parts and u will have no difficulty.

    More often than not, small PIs are tied up with some lousy workshop who knows no sh*t about the car and are good for changing engine oil.

    Cheers.

The car tires that comes with the JDM car may also be different class as what our weather and environment permits here.
