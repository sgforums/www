    Title : ERP rates to increase by 50 cents for Fort Road gantry along ECP
    By :
    Date : 30 October 2006 1404 hrs (SST)
    URL : http://www.channelnewsasia.com/stories/singaporelocalnews/view/238357/1/.html

    SINGAPORE: The Land Transport Authority will be revising the Electronic Road Pricing rates for a gantry along the East Coast Parkway from next week.

    Rates will go up by 50 cents from Monday the 6th of November for vehicles passing the Fort Road gantry between 7.30am and 8.30am.

    The revision in ERP rates are a result of LTA's quarterly review of traffic conditions.

    Rates are adjusted to achieve optimal traffic flow on expressways and roads, maintaining a speed range of 45 to 65 kilometres per hour for expressways and 20 to 30 kilometres per hour for arterial roads.

    The next revision of ERP rates will take place at the end of next month for the December 2006 School Holidays Rate Review. - CNA /dt
