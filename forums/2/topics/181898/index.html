    I am replying with personal knowledge and if other ppl thinks i am wrong correct me by all means...

    To get license, the fastest time frame is 3 months. It will cost about $1200.
    I did not get mine locally, so this is as much as i know from my friends.

    You can buy your car even before you get the license. But their will be alot of insurance issues after you get your license. You will need to change the police schedule, etc. So i suggest get the car after the license.

    However, being new licensed driver, be prepared to pay hefty insurance of at least $1800. Depending on type/make of car.

    To buy a car you must be able to afford the downpayment. Latest changes have made quite a big mess but i heard you can pay as low as 15% of the value of the car. The rest payable by loan.

    To sustain a car comfortably, you may need a monthly income of $2500-3000. But you have to make some personal sacrifices.

    Montly expenses, $200-400 on fuel (depending how you drive)
    Monthly debt payment $500-3000 (depending on car and loan term)

    COE is just a government's way of curbing car population. You have to get one thur bidding. Succesful bidders will get to buy cars. Bidding process, don't worry salesman will do that for you.

    AS for "litres" it is just the engine capacity. The volume of the combustion chamber (cylinder) of the car. In general, the bigger the volume, the more powerful the car but the more fuel uneconomical.

    Recent technologies have made these volumes very hard to gauge.

    firstly, where do i sign up to take my license? and is it after a test den ill be able to get a license? theory test and 'physically driving a car'?

    You can sign up at any driving centre, CDC, SSDC, BBDC. Once you pass your Basic Theory Test you can start your practical lessons with a Provisional Driving License.

    normally, how long does it takes to get a license?

    Depending on your performance and the time you have, it can range between 3 months to a year.

    if i have the money, can i get a car straight away after i got my license?

    If you have money you can get a car anytime. Haha!

    how much do i roughly need to buy a decent car? how much savings to maintain it?

    About 50-60k. Maintanence varies from car to car.

    what the hell is COE? and how much do i've to pay for the COE and than for the car? (i heard from time to time, COE are more expensive den a car? )

    Certificate of Entitlement. No, COE are not more expensive than a car, if that is the case toyotas would cost 100k. These are measures by the govt to increase the price of buying a car so as to discourage singaporeans from buying a car.

    what does the difference in litre means? i read some cars are 1.5l some 2l? able to go futher with more petrol?

    Engine capacity. Higher engine capacity means more power, and generally cars with higher engine capacity will be bigger, more spacious. FC varies from car to car.

    hmm... is car really only for those with ample and stable $$ in their bank??

    That is generally true. Cars in singapore is a luxury, not a neccesity. Unless you are rich, it is not practical to own a car unless you need it for purposes such as work. Owning a car is a big financial burden, so be sure you have the means to undertake it first.
