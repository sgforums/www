    No More Picnic

    Toyota's Picnic 7-seater, once the best-selling full-size MPV here, will be retired this year.

    The trustworthy workhorse, introduced here back in 1997, will not have an immediate replacement. Borneo Motors is now sourcing for another multi-seater to take its place.

    For those who want something conservative but tried and tested, the 2-litre Picnic is now going under $76,000, down from around $155,000 back in 1997.

    You could, of course, opt for the more compact (and modern) Toyota Wish, which is substantially cheaper. A new Wish will arrive towards the end of the year.

    Source: ST Life! Motoring section (14/03/2009)
