    Date: 12th & 13th June 2004
    Venue: Police Academy at Thomson Road (opp Europa Country Club)
    Time: 10am til 6pm

    I would like to emphasize to you our main highlight in this
    Carnival, it is the Monster Truck Show. First ever in Singapore and
    the programme will also include Stunt Show and Defensive Driving & Riding Performance by 3 Driving Centres. More great news for all of you!
    Normal charges for Monster Truck Show is usually $50 to $60 SDG in other
    countries, as this is the first time to have this show in Singapore,
    the entrance fees will be heavily subsidise by us and we are charging
    only FIVE DOLLARS ONLY($5/-), YES!! FIVE DOLLARS ONLY. The whole performance will take 2 1/2hrs.

    Besides the Monster Truck Show there are other fun and
    activities, kindly refer to the write up of some of the activities.

    Write up on individual activity in Police Week Carnival 2004

    Bubble Fantasia

    A bubbly good time in the bubble courtyard of Bubble Fantasia with
    giant bubble workshop conducted by professionals.

    Sandcastle Playland

    A fun filled sandcastle workshop that will occupy children endlessly.
    Participate in the Road safety sand building competition to win
    attractive prizes.

    Monster Truck & Stunt Show

    As the name implies, the Monster Truck is one of the awesome machines
    mankind has ever made. It stands more than 10 feet tall and develops
    more than 1,500 horsepower. It's supercharged, oversized, and
    extraordinary.

    The show is full of none stop spectacular actions with awesome Monster
    Truck with its monstrous motor producing thousands of horsepower. It
    drives right over nearly every obstacle in its path and splits nearly every
    eardrum in its vicinity.

    Stuntmen performing superb freestyle jumps with their motor bike
    flying extremely high in the air long enough to leave the bike free then
    grab it again to land smoothly.

    Euro Bungee

    The Euro Bungee is one of the most exiting attractions. It fascinates
    adults and children alike; it allows everyone (from 20 to 90 kg), even
    those with no experience, unlimited jumps up to 10 meters high (33
    feet), with no risk, which is not possible with an ordinary trampoline. Even
    people with certain physical disabilities are able to use it.

    Car Display

    Sponsors showcase some of their finest merchandise and that is their
    latest model of cars.

    Defensive Driving & Riding

    Demonstration with live commentary by experienced instructors from 3
    driving centres will be demonstrating on the following:
    1. Emergency Braking

    a) 3 different types of braking

    The purpose of practising the emergency braking is to stop the
    motorcycle in the shortest possible distance without losing control of the
    machine, by applying the front and rear brakes effectively. The techniques are to apply the brakes hard without locking the wheels, which may cause skidding.
    When applying the brakes, the rider should look straight, grip the fuel
    tank tightly with the knees and both hands holding the handlebar firmly.
    As the motorcycle is about to stop, pull in the clutch lever to prevent the
    engine from stalling.

    2. Pylon Shalom

    By practising riding through shalom course, its develops your sense of
    balance at slow speed and at a higher speed when taking corners. It
    also helps the rider to understand and to acquire the correct banking
    skills to ride round a bend safely.

    3. Off-set pylons - "Slow In, Fast Out"

    To understand the relationship between man and machine when going
    round a bend. The technique to go round a bend is call "slow in, fast
    out" technique. With this riding technique, he has to reduce speed, use the
    correct gearshift and thus reducing the effect of centrifugal force
    acting on the bike, which caused it to drift outwards. Half way through the
    bend, the rider may accelerate as to set the bike upright hence with
    achieving the Â“Fast OutÂ” effect. This is the best technique to handle
    corners and bends in a safe manner.

    Charity Corner

    Foot Reflexology is the holistic approach to wholeness through the
    feet. With the use of energy and manipulation of the foot reflexes, the
    client assisted by the therapist, goes into a state of relaxation, allowing
    the Spirit, Mind and Body to heal.

    Handicap masseurs from charitable organisation will offer foot
    reflexology for a charitable cause.

    Static Exhibition

    Showcase Singapore Police Force finest vehicles and equipment in
    combating crime.

    Transportation

    SBS shuttle service will be provided from Toa Payoh Interchange to
    Police Academy and back during the 2 days event.
