    how tall are u? anyways, when parking, use the side mirrors to judge where the rear of your car ends. as for the front, that u will really have to just get used to having a feel for the length. My mom's short, im short, but we both have no problems parking the Merc E class. my mom taught me where the critical spots are so I know which parts of the car to use as a reference point. I think u can ask your dad where he usually gets his reference points from.

    when all else fails, u can try opening the door or window to look out from. while i usually would reverse the car in even if i have my head out the door or window, i would actually advise u not to. back it in a little bit, handbrakes on, look out the window or door, and decide how much more u need to reverse, pull your head back into the car, and then disengage the brakes n continue reversing.

    3 Dec 04, 11:36
    Try to angle ur car more before entering the lot.. check ur side mirrors to see if ur rear bumper 'clears' the other cars front, then slowly back in and watch your other side mirror to confirm.

    Slowly angle in and straighten at the earliest possible time. Then now u muz worry about how deep to go cuz as u said, the car is long. Agar gauge the car model next to you. If it happens to be a big car too, can more or less follow him, while checking ur rear and side mirrors too, or popping ur head out.

    Or even going as deep as u dare, then coming out of the car to physically check behind. Mite be 'embarassing', but less than knocking the wall.
