    I said this before about such incidents; it is HOW and WHERE you park that contributes to such heartaches.

    Some people, park their beloved shiny new car so close to the neighboring parking lot. This pisses the next motorist who intends to park next to it, and if the person is an Ah Beng, good luck to you.

    Some people park in crowded places where there is high human traffic, because it is very near to where they want to go. Obviously, accidents do happen - someone pushing a trolly of goods passes by, misjudged the clearance from your car, and the goods he is trollying scratches your beloved car.

    Vandalism is harder to deal with, because it isn't accidental - it is intentional. Park in well lit lots, in clear view of public eyes, or better still, where you can see it when you sit down to a cup of coffee. Always give the neighboring lot plenty of room, and park in corner lots even if it means walking a distance back to where you are going. Sometimes, convenience may have a hidden price tag!

    But in general, DON'T park in a manner that will piss other motorists off! Be CONSIDERATE, your car will be scratch and dent-free.
