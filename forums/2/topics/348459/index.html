COE prices up across all categories

  • QX179R
    18 Feb 09, 20:41

    COE prices have gone up across all categories after news of a smaller COE supply was announced last week.

    The new COE quota will only kick in from May this year.

    In February's latest tender, the biggest jump was seen in the big cars category where the premium rose by $4,200 to nearly $4,900.

    COE for small cars went up by more than $3,400 to nearly $4,500.

    There was also a similar increase in the goods, vehicles and buses category, where the premium went up $1,600 to nearly $4,200.

    COE for the open category went up by nearly $3,000 to around $6,000.

    The premium for motorcycles went up $100 to around $800.
