    Hi,

    I do not have much experience with AIG insurance.
    But I can share with you what I know about NTUC Car Insurance.

    I have a car that is happily insured with NTUC Income.
    I can see that most of the complains and frustrations with Income are about having to report to IDAC after an accident which people felt is time consuming. I guess the reason why there are so many complains is because Income is the largest motor insurer in Singapore so naturally whenever there is a complain about motor claims, Income will "ganna" the complain.

    But to think of it in a positive light, if you report at the IDAC centre after an accident, people at the centre will be able to guide you on the claim processes (rather than being totally at loss what to do after an accident). At the same time, the claim will be treated legitimately and there will be lesser disputes about your vehivce damages. IDAC will also conduct a quality check after the repairs.

    That's what I feel. Feel free to share your views.
