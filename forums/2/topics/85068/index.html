IMPORTANT:- TIPS TO WASH NEW CARS

  • 2004Lancer
    8 Jul 04, 16:25
    Never Never ever let ah neh neh or others to wash your car...

    soon you will find minute and fine circular scratches on the surface...

    new car has a 'plastic-like' protective coating.

    when u let ah neh neh or others wash your cars, chances are they r using the same old piece of cloth and same pail of soiled sabun water to clean your car...

    SO WHAT TO DO leh?

    1) BUY ONLY [WASH AND WAX] shampoo diluted in a pail of water
    2) ONLY USE SPONGE (coz sponge got holes to house dirt and soil)
    3) WORK / WASH in ONE DIRECTION ONLY (like pushing the dirt and water away in a single direction)
    4) POUR or SPRAY clean water to rinse the soapy or bubbled water away from those hidden constrictions / drains...

    lastly:- DON'T WASTE WATER!!! i only use 2 pails...one pail diluted with shampoo, one pail for rinsing
