    ok let me ans u one by one.
    For the best cars in the jap cat, personally I think the top 2 cars are the Skyline GT-R and the EVO.

    The Skyline is AWD, with a electronically controlled torque split between its front wheel and rear wheels, which means torque is directed to the set of wheels which need it most. The chassis is good and stiff, and it makes for an awesome touring car. This year it almost won the Nurburing 24 hours enduro race, and certainly is the only jap car monster enough to mix it with the Vipers, Opel DTMs, Audi DTMs and Porsche GTs to fight for top honours. Looks-wise, its very brutal, very menancing and muscular.

    The EVO is another AWD car, with similar electronic trickery to the Skyline. It is simply awesome because of the way the car feels so taut and so precise thru corners. A test done by Autocar(i think) in UK with the WRX STi and the EVO had the EVO coming 4 seconds faster than the STi, even beating the time set by a Lamborghini Murcielago set earlier. Also, winning 4 straight WRC champoinships speaks volumes about the car's ability. It has a very purposeful look about it, and screams business when viewed from all angles.

    Mazda is good, especially their rotary engines, but those are the engines that were previously unreliable once the mileage starts piling on. The latest rotary offering in the RX-8, has addressed this problem by moving the interior exhaust port inlet, so most of the reliability probs are solved. This engine was just awarded the best engine by an international panel of 22 automobile journalists, which certainly shows how good it is. as for the modded mazdas at S11, well, that stretch at night is part of an illegal racing circuit so its not very suprising.
