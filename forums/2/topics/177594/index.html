  • tailslide
    10 Feb 06, 09:29
    wheelspin occurs when torque transmitted to the wheels outstrips the grip available from ur tires. therefore, the 2 deciding factors are power and grip.

    a more torquey engine will require a lower rpm to coz wheelspin. but a less grippy tire will also be able to wheelspin even if the engine is not torquey at all.

    but why do it in the first place? waste tire, waste fuel, waste ur clutch and most importantly, waste time moving off (u lose time when ur wheels slip).
