    Are you talking about cleaning the carbies/injectors, or are you just talking about chucking octane booster into the fuel to clean carbon out of the combustion chambers?

    If you're talking about the latter, it's a case of carbon deposits forming on top of the piston. These deposits get red-hot in the chambers, and ignite the fuel-air mixture before the spark plug goes off, causing detonation that not only wastes fuel but causes damage to the pistons, crankshaft bearings and other parts that are exposed to the force of the detonation.
