    Hi. Let me explain to you. Hope you will benefit from it.

    Production Year End 1992 to End 1995
    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
    Honda LSI : Lower horsepower of the 3 models. Not a VTEC engine. 1500cc SOHC (Single Cams) engine.

    Honda ESI : Honda 2 Stage 1600cc VTEC SOHC Engine. Comsumption about 12km/Litre for manual (I drove this model before)

    Honda VTI : Honda 2 Stage 1600cc VTEC DOHC (Twin Cams) Engine.

    Production Year End 1995 to Early 2001
    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
    Honda EXI : Replace the previous model of LSI. 1500cc SOHC engine with a horsepower of 105BHP.

    Honda VTI : Replace the previous model of ESI. 1500cc SOHC engine with a horsepower of 130BHP. Auto gear box came with economy, normal & sport mode. Fuel comsumption about 11-12km/Litre (I drive this model before)

    Honda SIR : Replace the previous model of VTI. 1600cc DOHC engine with a horsepower of 155BHP for Auto & 170BHP for manual.

    NB : I had drove 3 of the generation model for Honda. Namely 1991 Honda Civic 3dr 1.3GL(Manual) Modified, 1993 Honda Civic 4dr 1.6ESI(Manual) & 1996 Honda Civic 4dr 1.5VTI(Auto), If you need more info, you can drop me a mail at ray_mondsg@yahoo.com.sg, I will try to provides you any info that I know.
