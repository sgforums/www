    Kelisa: fuel consumption is around 15km/litre. Boot space is more than Picanto. Rear passenager leg space is more than Picanto. DOHC vs Picanto's SOHC (subjective). 1litre vs 1.1litre. Price is the same (Picanto manual is not 1k more expensive). 53hp vs Picanto's 63hp. Kelisa has very poor and plastic like interior finishing. Kelisa, being a Malaysian car has cheap servicing over the causeway. Kelisa is so called made for asian climate (that's what the sales rep. told me).

    Picanto: fuel consumption is around 18km/litre (claimed to be able to do 20km/litre in London test drive). Very European look alike, thus the nice finishing, interior and exterior. ENOUGH SAID.
