    If you get the leather seats done outside, of course you can choose the quality and colour. Borneo Motors (BM) outsources the leather seats to different vendors depending on who can meet the price and deadline. The colour of the leather from BM is also quite light, might get dirty easily. My father-in-law also notices that the way they sew the 2 front leather seats are different within the same car!! Usually, he will use one piece to complete the certain area. Neck area of the cow is where the leather is hard and the body is soft. They should use the soft area to build the portion where people sits and the hard area for the places where you can't see. Try not to expose the leather to direct sunlight too often as it causes the leather to harden and have crack lines, sometimes loses the original colour as well.
