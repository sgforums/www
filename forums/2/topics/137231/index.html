    Conincidentally two of my close fren bought a 1991 corolla and sunny together ... 3 years ago.... they have since sold them off and upgrade to new kia and Hyundai currently.... I have also test driven their old car... must say its quite a pain to drive after been driving and driven in brand new cars all these while. But of cos it still quite acceptable if you are not so particular about comfort , refinment

    They have of cos given my fren lots of problem such as timing belt replacement, shocker absorber, radiator, alternator, leaking window seal , worn clutch just to name a few....infact my fren called it a timebomb

    However in my opinion...if you can bargain down the cars to paper value its may be a good buy... you have to be prepared ..... if the engine really conk out big time and need a major repair.. you should just send it to scrap yard and recover the paper value then repairing them...

    The depreciation should be less than 3 K per year anything higher... you are better off driving a brand new hyundai or kia
