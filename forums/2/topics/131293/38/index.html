    i checked the manual it didnt explain your queries. i have worked on some RFID projects earlier on, so this is my theory/assumption:

    all the ikeys have different ID, but when you buy a spare it will be programmed with the same ID as the first.

    when you activate the door or the ignition with the first ikey, the car and the ikey are thereon in constant communication. the car will always be asking "are you there Mr. ABC?", and the ikey will reply "yes, i am Mr. ABC and i am still here" and it goes on and on.

    if other ikey comes in and announce "i am Mr. XYZ", the car will ignore.

    if ikey Mr. ABC goes away or out of range, the car will know and starts shouting "are you still there Mr. ABC?" (meanwhile sounding and displaying the red "key" alarm)

    if the spare ikey who has the same ID as Mr. ABC comes in or within range, the car will know and operation will continue as normal again.

    there are different types of RFID systems, some are active and some passive. many applications. quite cool stuff. dunno what else have been invented lately.
