    Performance: well, it depends on what kind of performance you are after, ideally, its better to mod if not you feel "jack-shit" power on the mid and high rev..
    BUT it is a rather "torque" car, meanin you will definately be the first to "zoom" off once the lights turn green cos of its weight to power ratio but i can't say thereafter, so mod will be ideal....what kind of mod? exhaust, airfilter, spark plugs,ecu tunning.
    FC: stock is good but after mod, i am clocking in at 8-9k a litre and of cos subject to whether you are "heavy footer" as usual.
    Handling: this car feels like its going to "turtle" when its running on stock suspension and wheels(165/70/14) so ideal you should lower your ride and upgrade the wheel size before you play corner etc etc, and if running on stock, i will say good luck to your arms when you are planning to drive to KL cos the handling sucks cos the steering is light as well.I hope you know what i mean.
    Honestly: if you are just a point "A" to point "B" person and not care about interior looks blah blah blah; and if you can afford the cash then go get a Honda Jazz.
