    Vandagal, Perhaps next time when you go to Kuantan, you might want to take the route I suggested, since you are now familiar with Kuantan. Ie take the N/S expressway from JB to Yong Peng (Can have a break there), then on to Segamat (for some durians perhaps) and take the road to Kuantan. The road after you leave the N/S expressway to Yong Peng is not good (same as it was in the days before the N/S expressway, ie about 30+ years ago), but the road after Segamat to Kuantan, even though it is not an expressway (single lane only) has less traffic. On the return journey you can take your same route back from Kuantan-Nenasi-Rompin-Mersing-Kota Tinggi-JB as you may want to break your journey since you will probably be travelling in the afternoon. You can take a day trip from Mersing to one of the islands, eg P Rawa, not too far.

    Somebody just came back from KL and suggested that all KL trips be postponed if possible.
