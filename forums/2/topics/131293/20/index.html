    When you wash your car :

    1. Cloths
    - After washing when drying 1pc for 1 side of the car ( x2 )
    - 1 pc for bonnet, 1 pc for roof and back. ! Buy a big pail put all your cloths, shampoo and etc..! set aside abt $100 to purchase all these items..! and you can start wetting your car..!

    2. Always shampoo one part of the car first after which wash over quickly..

    3. Do not shampoo you whole car and after which wash it... the shampoo will damage your paint work...!

    4. always wash your can from head to toe... dry your car from head to toe...! ( roof to the bottom) start with circular motion...!

    Have fun washing your car..! abt 2 hrs - 4 hrs (my record)
    wash, dry, polish and detailing.. only external !
    Internal, is every 3weeks..!

    GOOG LUCK !
