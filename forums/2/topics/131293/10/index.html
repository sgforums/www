    Hi Madagascar,

    The car comes with 98bhp stock, if you are talking abt full load of 4 taking on a Benjamin S. bridge is not a problem. It depends on what you mean by weight to power ratio....maintaining at 90 km/h with full load and climbing without compressing the paddle and not accelerate is not going to happen... for 1.4 car.

    1. 975 kg / 98 ps = 9.95kg per horse. ( just the car itself )
    2. 975 kg + 4 person x 60kg each = 1215 kg / 98ps which gives you 12.5 kg + -per horse pull.

    If you are going up a carpark slop like taka, shift to D2 and step on it you can climb the slop with full load with not much stress on your engine...! For a 1.4L car is good enough... !! can't expect the sky..!

    It also depends very much on your personal driving habits..... after all the K12 is a 1.4L, is not a 1.6L or a 2L... !! just my 2cents worth....!

    If you are comparing other cars vs the K12, is different and you can't compare it with a sunny or latio...! different skools..!
