    Hey Toadie,

    I have installed the 3M film, and tried leaving the car out in the sun for 1 hour.. (i didn't have a choice anyway!). I still feel the heat, but later, i realised that it is because in front, in the driver's seat, the front film of course has lesser IR reduction compared to the back, which was much cooler.

    But, it is definitely better, as the heat dissipated much faster then before, after the engine & aircon is switched on! So, all in all, quite happy with it and the customised mats.

    48 hours after installation, the rear window film makes everything looks distorted, so i had to drive carefully (cars behind me looked like many lambourghinis & ferraris!!), but, now, it is fine, though the demister lines are now more prominent then before..
