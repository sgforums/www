    its true that cats leave scratches on the car... i've seen it with my own eyes.

    i know it was the cat because while the car was being washed in the morning, there were no scratches noted. but in the afternoon, i saw a cat jump off the bonnet and hey presto... scratch marks at the same spot. these are fine but deep scratch marks that can't be polished away... unlike the shallow ones caused by wiping the car with a dirty cloth.

    unfortunately, the cat kept coming back over a period of 3 mths or so, sometimes it left new scratches, sometimes it didn't. i finally called AVA, they loaned me a cat trap and 2 days later, the culprit was caught and collected by AVA. since then, no new scratches have appeared on the car.

    it's not an uncommon problem. i'm a partner in a car grooming business and we see plenty of customers with similar scratches and stories to share. Unfortunately, there's nothing you can do once the damage is done, short of a respray.
