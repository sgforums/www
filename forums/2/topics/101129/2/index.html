    As regards to what car u wan to buy, u have to think of the following

    1) what you need

    eg, a big car to drive yr family around, a sport car to show off, a small car to move yrself around.

    Type of car will also affect the fuel consumpation, insurance and road tax.

    2) what you can afford

    it is easiler for the back to approve yr loan if yr mth installment is 3.5 time yr mth salary. Lower than that, u will need to find a guarnator.

    3) Agent or Parallel Import car

    Agents can provide better servicing package for their cars but they can earn it back from the higher profit margins.

    Parallel Import have a highter OMV than agent cos agent have bulk discount, therefore the depreciation per year is lower and have a higher scape value.

    So maybe u can use the above to think about what car u want and need, then come back here to see how can we help u.
