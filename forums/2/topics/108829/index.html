    Originally posted by MCY:

    It depends on how long you are waiting.
    If it is less than 5 mins, you can put to "N" and pull the handbrake.
    If it is more than that, you can put to "P" and pull the handbrake. It can also be used when you want to rest your right leg during the start and stop traffic, or at some traffic lights after a long drive.
    Also, you car can only be switched on in "P" or "N", so it is never redundant, otherwise it would not be there in the first place.
