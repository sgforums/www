    assuming that you are driving a rear wheel drive car.
    part 1-you are into a right turn, well, smash in the throttle, power overcoming the reARS, car stars to slide left, apply oppo lock( to the left), balancing the gas pedal, so the slide holds.

    part 2 - transferring to the right - clutch in to abruptly cut the power to the rear wheels. keep the oppo lock on. this causes the rear wheels to suddenly regain grip,this throws the car sliding in the other direction (top the right) as the suspension rebounds. at this point,smash the gas again and let out the clutch. this causes the rear to lose grip again. you continue sliding right,remember to apply oppo steering lock to hold the slide, in combo with gas pedal moderation.
