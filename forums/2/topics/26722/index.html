    Hi,

    I'm driving a Nissan March/M (09/95 version) which I bought in April 2002. Overall, I think the car itself is good e.g. powered steering, powered windows (front 2 only), 4 cylinder fuel injection engine, single disc CD player etc.

    I heard of those notorious 2nd-hand car dealers.
    They keep telling you that its good value for money and that its a good car. And when you really decided to buy the car, they will tell you some sorts of additional charges that you will need to pay for (hidden costs). Luckily, I bought it from my cousin who is selling 2nd hand cars. He tested and chose the car for me without any hidden costs.

    Like what Ah Wei said, the engine is good. I tried 110km in S'pore and 120km in JB Malaysia. Its a good 1000cc car with a good engine with good fuel consumption. But if you're into power, then it might not be what you're looking for.

    I'm thinking of selling this car since I don't really drive it that often nowadays. I'm living in Bedok but i'm attached to customer's site at Chai Chee. Its only a 15min bus ride away.
