    I do not really know the Latio but I think I can provide some information about the Colt Plus.

    Basically, the Colt Plus has slightly more space in the boot compared to the Colt or the Latio. The plus point would be the electric tailgate which is unheard of in the Colt Plus's category. The tailgate can be opened via the remote key of a switch in the car. After loading the stuffs, a press of a button on the tailgate will close it.

    The Coltl Plus runs on a more refined engine than when the Colt first made a comeback into the local motoring scene. The Colt Plus also comes with stuffs like auto-headlights, speed-variant intermittent wipers, 4 auto power window controls on driver side, power wing mirror that will unfold when the car reaches a speed of 30km/h.

    In my opinion, the Colt Plus is definitely value for money cos you don't get all those stuffs mentioned for the same price. Also this Mitsubishi comes from Japan.
