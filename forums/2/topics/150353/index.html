    I'm not saying FC of all Mazda3 sucks, mine is really good (compare to negative feedback in all forums), while some are really bad. I've long suspected that cars, irregardless of brands always have variation in quality. Lousy QC in Japanese production line is really unthinkable, but it may be happening...Human, petrol factors, no matter how well you control seems to improve the FC very little. Even conception Altis has good FC is also a misconception really.....FC from my 2 friends reported at <11km/L compare to 13-14 you'll always hear.

    seems like taboo to write anything negative in any specific car forum, only get ppl to encourage you to spend more dough (no offence for any car forum ppl, but it's true for me)...most ppls in the forums are businessppl anyway.

    what i gather from there, other good forums and my own experience to keep good FC (and if you are only any normal drivers like me..no racing, no tailgating, no chionging...:

    1. dun modify unnecesarily...especially tyres
    2. air intakes / chargers dun improve FC, friend tried one $400 on sunny, but no improvement
    3. dun add additives
    4. petrol whether v-power or 95 makes little difference in FC
    5. plan your route properly, with petrol price now higher, it's wise to avoid jams..FC really sucks at long jams, <9km/L
    6. pump at the station which is busier, then you'll get fresher petrol

    Hope to hear of more frank contributions from ppl in this forum.
