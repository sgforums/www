    Have patience, your car has not "run in" yet. Follow Tan Cheong maintenance package and change engine oil to synthetic only after run in.

    My Sunny FL(M) is coming to 2 years old and I would like to share with you the performance of my car so far:

    I get an average FC of 14-15km/l. 70% highway 30% urban driving. Aircon low usuage and speed try to keep 80- 90km/hr.Petrol using Shell 95ron. Engine oil Castrol RS.
    I have done the following so far:

    1) Install Splitfire spark plug.
    2) Gounding. (took the combo package with item 1 @ $156)

    After a few months,I get a simple Cold Air intake done.

    After doing item 1) n 2), FC and performance reach what I have now. I personnaly think gounding is good for older car only.

    As for the CAI, I realise it doesn't help much when I keep to 80km/hr. But when I try to go faster @ 90km+, it doesn't have high impact on my FC.

    Everything else is original stock. Even my wheels are original steel rim type.

    Hope that helps.
