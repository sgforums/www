    It actually something call heat shield. Its to prevent the engine heat or hot air from entering into the airfilther. As what they say, Cold air give better engine response. You might as well do a direct pipping from the front bonnet to allow the air to flow in directly to the filther. but again, it dun really make much difference. Just minor.

    Secondly, i thot of changing the spark plug .... to NGK Iridium IX spark plug .. as understand it gives betta acceleration, better fuel consumption, faster starting and smoother running .... any opinion you guys will like to share ?

    A better plug only give better sparks. It only smooth the acceleration and the iginition part.

    Also, how much cost will it involve? Besides that, any economical price for other modifications you think i should do? put aside those turbo charge etc ...

    Things you can do to improve accleration. Take out spare tyres, dun put sound system. change the rim to forge or super light weight. Dun take passenger. Sure will increase by 0.5secs
    Other things to do is bore throttle, change piston, re-programe the original ECU ( if you know who can do it in singapore let me know ). After re-programe, sure can increase the pickup + speedcut remove + rev limit remove.
    Pump premium petrol. Forget about 95 and below grade.

    Lastly, dun forget, a faster car = a faster drinker + more servicing + faster wear and tear = more $$
