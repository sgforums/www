    I got my license 4 months back & finally got the chance to drive out on my own yesterday. I rented a Proton Gen-2 from a rental company for 1 day. I was so excited, so ready to take on the roads myself.

    This experience ended up costing me $440 ($280 repair fee, $160 rental extension fee).

    You see, the vehicle had a bodykit which means the bumper is very low, almost touching the ground. While I was making a turn out of a multi-story carpark, I turned too wide an angle & went up the kerb. Suffice to say, the low-hanging bumper was beat up. Then, it happened again. While I was sending my friend home, he suggested that I do a U-turn on a narrow road. Stupidly, I attempted to do it & once again, went up a kerb. By now, the damage to the carkit bumper was very obvious. 

    So I end up having to pay close to $500+ just for 1 night of driving. Worse of all, after this experience, I feel that I don't have the confidence anymore.
