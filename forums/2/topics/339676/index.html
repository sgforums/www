    TS, from what I read, I think u are not ready for your test yet.

    " My parking not very good, for parallel is okay but always not straight "

    to solve this problem, make sure what u see at the front dashboard and side door of your view are parallel with whatever things u can find to compare (kerb, walls, anything outside your car) And of course, lots of practise.

    "except I tend to lag behind the car in front of me "

    Its very normal to lag behind other cars because you are not going speed of excess 60kmh. Your speed for the test (on road) should be below 60kmh.

    "my car usually is closer to the centre line"

    To solve this, try to vision yourself slightly off centre of the lane. Or, in another words, your steering wheel slightly off the centre imaginary line of a lane.
