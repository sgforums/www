    For vertical parking, there are 2 main techniques.

    1) Turning your head around throughout the reversing process or

    2) Using only both the side mirrors during the reversing process.

    Which technique that you be taught, will depend on your instructor. And really, technique (2) is the better one. Once you've learnt it correctly, vertical parking is really very simple. Be it any angle, any distance, you can park in one go. Of course, sometimes you will adjust a little to ensure that your car is sui-sui (exact equal distance)  from both adjacent cars. This is to minimise your own car being scatched by the opening door of a car parked too near you.
