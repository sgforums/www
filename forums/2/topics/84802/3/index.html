    Actually, the 3 Litre Toyota Crown Diesel is a very capable car.     From 0-100, a crown takes less than 12 seconds, and from there on, it will beat most 1.6L saloons (especially the automatics). I've had several taxis "play" with me.... from 100kmh up to 160kmh.... the 1.5 tonne taxis are almost as quick as my 1 tonne saloon, mostly because their torque is so high.

    The reason why most taxis "SEEM" slow is because the drivers do not know their car well enough. OR... they don't have/don't know how to use their tachometer.

    Just because most taxi drivers try to limit their fuel consumption by upshifting at lower rpms doesn't mean that their cars are slow. And if a taxi ever hit you.... you'd know that they cause more damage to your car then yours would to them (unless you drive a German marque)
