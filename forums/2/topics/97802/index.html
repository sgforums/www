    Ironically, CDC's passing rate is actually the highest among all 3 driving centres, hmm.

    Since the traffic at Ubi is heavier, try to clear your circuit without any demerit points. That way, half the battle is already won, and it will give you that extra boost of confidence when you go onto the busy roads.

    Also, testers are EXTREMELY particular about blind spots, so make sure you check them conscientiously. The other thing they focus on is pedestrians' safety, so at all pedestrian crossings, be extra careful.

    Ultimately, no matter what the traffic conditions, if your driving skills are there, the tester will have no reason to fail you. Drive with confidence, all the best!
