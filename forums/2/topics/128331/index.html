    any1 heard be4 of the places bottle tree village and ubin seafood? i've been there both and would like to recommend these places to u folks out there wif cars and finding for good places to eat (good food and tok cock).

    Firstly is bottle tree village, it is at sembawang area, and is strictly for ppl who are driving only as the road leading to the place is very secluded and long. upon reaching, u can park for free in a wide and open carpark. the ambience i will rate it as 8/10. you will be dining nearby a sea in a dimly lit open space (romantic atmosphere). the food there are mostly "chi char" wif a few speciality of their own. the prices are reasonable and u will definetly want to go back again. i myself had been there thrice liao. u may wonder where does the place gets its name from. it is becoz the owen of the place had bought trees which look like bottle from austraila costing a few thousands a tree. these tress are located at the entrance and are really cute looking (good to show to gals). taken a few pics myself.

    2nd is ubin seafood. located at keppel road. cars are recommended but not a must.but be prepared to walk for a good 20-30mins if u not going by car. the ambience i will say is more romantic than bottle tree, i rate it 9/10. i myself have brought my galfrien over there a couple of times as she really like the place alot. even to the extend ta she makes it a policy to go there once a month! ($$$$)....haha....the pirces are in the same range as bottle tree, reasonable.the food not bad too, "chi char" style oso. there is a seaside view and eatig there u'll feel relaxe, that's for sure.
