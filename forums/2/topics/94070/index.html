    Tailgaters can tailgate me all they want. When traffic is heavy in front of me, I will keep 3 to 4 car lengths. Or slow down until tailgater over-takes.

    Before braking, I would always turn ON hazard lights for 2 to 3 seconds, indicating slowdown in front so that tailgaters or traffic behind me is alerted. To shake off tailers, I would purposely switch into a lane or get behind large and slow moving trailer trucks or lorries. This usually spoils tailers "fun", and they go away. Or, if there is NO traffic in the middle lane, pull out to the center lane suddenly and jam brake. This causes the tailer to over shoot me, and I cut back into the left lane directly behind the tailer. Of course keeping good distance.
