  • Kuali Baba
    31 Dec 05, 20:28
    The unmodified TS does not have a honeycomb grille - instead it has a black plastic strip holding the Subaru emblem in the grille. The gearknob, seats and steering wheel are also different, and the voltmeter and charge meter are absent.

    Open the bonnet and you'll see that the TS doesn't have a turbocharger and intercooler. The latter is placed on top of the engine, under the air sccop in the WRX and STi.

    Only the STi comes with xenon headlights as standard, and as the others have pointed out, the TS has rear drum brakes, and the STi has all-round Brembo disc brakes.
