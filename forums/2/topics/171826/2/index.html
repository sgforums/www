    Hi guys

    The only way to differentiate between a TS to a WRX or STI is to follow behind and look out for the 3rd brake light at the rear wind screen (As pointed out by one guy already).

    No matter how much much they do up the exterior or de-badge their TS, the moment you see a 3rd brake light in the car, it is a TS. Cos WRX and STI does not have 3rd brake light in the car. Their 3rd brake lights are on their rear spoilers.

    So while owners may buy brembo brake covers, install scoops and STI wing, the 3rd brake light in the car is a straight giveaway of the TS's humble roots.

    And there is no need to worry that you can't spot the 3rd brake light when the TS pulls away from you. Cos the heavily cladded TS with its under-powered engine will not be able to pull away from you.

    Happy spotting WRX/STI wannabes and their 4th brake light!!

    Cheers!!!
