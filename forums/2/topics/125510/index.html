    Shinka is the special edtion. Spec S refers to the 6 speed manual. Mazda motors brings in the euro spec which comes with the sunroof, 18 inch rims, and 231 PS. The JDM spec is about 245 PS. Interior for the type S is generally black as standard.
    Fuel consumption is about same as the WRX/STI. But cheaper insurance and road tax. Handling is great. Not as fast as WRX but definitely fun to drive and eye catching too. If your budget is limited and you want speed, buy a WRX. If you're feeling generous and like the RX8 looks, then buy it lor. You can also consider the Intergra Type R....which is a little faster than the RX8. Cool VTec engine but 2 doors and high insurance.
    If you really want a fast car, forget the RX8. Go for the STI instead. Good bang for bucks. Or Evo9 if you got more cash.
