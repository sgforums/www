    If the car is stationery, you can just read the road-tax disc. Ts and WRX have different road tax values. Also, most Ts, even modified, will not have the bonnet scoop (its not cheap to change the bonnet - the scoop is a few hundred bucks!).

    BUT.....there are some modified Ts which look very tame. But if you look carefully, you can see a front-mounted intercooler. Be careful of those - they can fly!
