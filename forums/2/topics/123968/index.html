    My prev car was a Kia Rio 1.3M

    As a basic pt A to pt B car, its ok.
    Pretty nice looking car (subjective), the exterior body shell is rather soft. You will get dents and dings easily.

    Fuel consumption so so only, i'm getting about 11-12km/l.

    Go for the 1.5 model if you are considering this car. The 1.3 feels pretty weak and is getting about same consumption figure as a 1.5
