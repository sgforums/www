    technically, u cannot remove the limitor. unlike the speed limitor, there is no such thing as a "limitor" function built into the ecu for power. power is usually limited by way of the ecu mapping, keeping ignition, fuel injection cycle in check such that the engine does not become "over efficient" to make more than 280 hps. of course, we all know that some manufacturers will simply "quote" the power at 280 hps for some models. actual fact is that they are actually producing much more.

    the only way u can "unlimit" this is to reprogram the whole ecu map in order to tune for max power. of course, with more mods to compliment the tuning (more boost, xhaust, intake etc) u can tune for even greater gains.
