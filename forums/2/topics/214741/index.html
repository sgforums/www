    Are you seeking legal advice or general opinion as to whether or not to cancel the contract?

    With regards the latter >>> Yes.

    With regards the former:

    1) Your vehicle purchase contract should have a clause that includes a "cooling-off" period, i.e. during this period taking effect immediately from the purchase, you can rescind the contract without incurring liability.

    alternatively,

    2) As a bona fide purchaser, having acted to your detriment, on the misrepresentation of facts by the vendor, you are entitled to seek a refund of your deposit/purchase price. However, it should be noted that as you have already taken notice of this misrepresentation (with the vendor acknowledging and admitting to this), any delay would act against your claim (acquiescence). I have not mentioned specific performance because I am doubtful the vendor would have a model, exactly/similarly close to what was promised.

    Please do not regret your purchase and never let circumstantial pressure get the better of you.

    FYI, the TV, is not cheap; 3rd party installations might give rise to subsequent warranty issues, and it wouldn't be cheap either.

    Which BMW dealer did you go to? The one on Kingsway?

    EDIT: The views above represent that of an individual, and is not intended to be/represent any form of professional legal opinion.
