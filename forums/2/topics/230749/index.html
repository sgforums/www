    Here's an estimate of my normal car expenses:

    Insurance = $900 (annual 50% NCD)
    Road Tax = $1400 (annual)
    -> $192 per month over 12 months
    Fuel = $210 ($70 per refill; 3 times a month)
    Instalment = $800 (per month)
    Servicing = $360 (4 times a year @$90 per 3 months) -> $30 per month over 12 months
    HDB Car park = $75 per month
    Coupons = $30 per month

    This works out to be $192 + $210 + $800 + $30 + $75 + $30 = $1,337 per month
