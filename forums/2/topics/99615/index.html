  • elfhunter

    Timing is as follows:

    Mondays to Fridays: from 7.00pm to 7.00am
    Saturdays & Eve of the 5 major Public Holidays*: 3.00pm onwards
    *New Year, Lunar New Year, Hari Raya Puasa, Deepavali and Christmas.

    If you need to use your OPC during the restricted hours, you will need to display a $20 day licence which can be brought at any singpost outlets
