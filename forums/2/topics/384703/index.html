    2011 Honda Civic

     

    According to the latest reports, the next-generation of the Honda Civic will be officially unveiled in 2010 and expected by the dealers in 2011. Like the current version, the new vehicle will be available in two versions, with 3 or 5 doors. The new generation will use petrol and diesel engines but will also offer a hybrid version, powered by a 1.5-liter petrol engine with 150 hp. Later, the Type-R version will come showing more than 200 hp.

    The American will beexpected to get their choice of sedan and coupe body styles while the other countries will be getting their different versions of the hatchback theme. As expected, the 2012 Civic will be coming with a hybrid variant which will come equipped with a 1.5-liter gasoline unit coupled to the Integrated Motor Assist technology of the carmaker. This engine has been speculated to be shared with the forthcoming CR-Z hybrid hatch which will come with either an automatic CVT transmission or a six-speed manual transmission. All of these reports will be confirmed where production prototypes of the 2011 CR-Z will be showcased as has been long expected.
