    Me is a taxi driver now after being forced to resign 6 months ago..........

    b 4 that I was just like any of you swearing and cursing the taxi drivers

    Let me tell you our story..

    Our rental is $92 (before GST) per day that is for the lousiet cab nissan or toyota........... Mercz is $126 per day,

    Our cost a day are as follow

    $96 rental
    $30 diesel
    $3 parking

    Total $130 per day

    Theoritcally, After deducting rest time, meal time etc the hours we are able to make money are only..16 hours ..... and then there are peak and off peak hours..some time suay vehicle breakdown , company dun reimburse is its not more than 4 hours

    during peak hour the max you can made is around $25 per hour

    during off peak you can ply around for 2 hours and cannot get a single fare.

    Say you drive the full 16 hours the max takign you can get is only $200

    after deduct cost you made $70 that is for 16 hours of works

    $70/ 16 hrs = $4.375 per hour

    That is assuming you can tahan 16 hours of driving...........

    You need to drive at least 8 hours just to breakeven on your cost

    In addition, when you fall sick or have some urgent matter to attend to you dun get any MC or leave you still pay your rental..

    Our typical nissan cedric and toyota crown is not like your sunny or corrolla that is comfortable to drive .. the driving position sucks , interior is dark and gloomy , engine is noisy and vibrates badly. After just driving for 2 hours I feel giddy and start to loose concentration. At the end of the day my leg cramp after controlling the clutch pedal for 16 hours..

    After 3 months of driving I take home less than $1000 per month..

    All I can say now is Taxi Driver is the worse job I ever had ... Goverment are introducing more taxis to generate more income for the coffer and to lower the umemployement rate . New taxi are introduce at a rate of 600 per month. all these take its toil on us....... the current driver. WE have to spend more time on the road competing with other driver for fare .. just go and look at the taxi queue at the taxi stand you know what I mean

    Sometimes you dunno the way passenger scold you etc etc.. all the traffic jam add to your stress also.. some time unable to cover the cost stress again..

    I have found another job recently ... and I tell myself I will cherish my new job and I will never want to be a taxi driver again !!

    For the rest of you , try being a taxi driver for a day you will be more symphatetic and emphatic towards them they really lead a very hard life!
