    keyless ignition works on a proximity card system. You get a credit sized car which you put in your wallet. When you are within proximity of your car, the car unlocks. Engine ignition is activated through a button, but it'll only work when the card is within proximity.

    Mercedes Benz is generally the marque credited for popularizing this system. Perhaps you can just waltz into their showroom and ask for a demonstration on the S Class? I'm sure they'll be quite happy to oblige.

    On a side note, they're available as aftermarket parts. I saw their booth at some autoshow few years back, but haven't been able to find them since.
