    Actually I think both the Cerato Forte and Avante are pwned by another Korean. But it's a mixed blood Korean. Introducing *drumroll pls* the Hyundai i30!

    I think it's extremely underrated here in Singapore mainly because of a few reasons. Firstly, it's price. It's more expensive than the Cerato Forte or even their own Avante when it's only a hatchback. Blame manufacturing in Slovakia for that.

    And this leads me to my second reason, it's a hatchback. You know Singaporeans and Asians. We just love cars with butts (eg Ford Focus sedan, VW Jetta). Maybe because with the extra butt, they feel that the moolah they shelled out for is more worth it.

    But if you think of the i30 in another way, it's extremely bang for your bucks. It's around the same size as your Opel Astra, Ford Focus, VW Golf, blah blah blah. All your premium hatchbacks. And the i30 has a very similar looking spec sheet compared to its competitors too. Multlink independent rear suspension, wow. And reviewers also thought that although not as entertaining to drive as compared to a Focus, it's good enough. Quality inside is on par with its European competitors too.

    Heck, if I am ever considering a premium hatchback, I won't only be looking at Focus or Golf. I'll make a trip down to Komoco Motors too.
