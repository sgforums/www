    I have installed a surbo and it works fine for me. Definately can feel the thing working and the car feels more powerful. Fuel comsumption wise I am recording a 10 % improvement after 2 tanks of trials.

    I think the Surbo does function as explained in their website, but for it to work in your car, you need to ensure 2 things :

    1) The engine has to be air-tight .... so get it installed by a authorized mechanic.

    2) You need to "activate" the "turbo" using the throttle control during acceleration .... once you get the technique right, can feel the power surge.

    Finally I think this device works best if you are the type of drivers who have a heavy right foot. btw, there's a refund policy and the installation can be reversed easily.
