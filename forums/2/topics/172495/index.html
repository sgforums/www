    so zhun saw was this tp rex stopped far ahead at the road shoulder. my bro immediately slowed down. apparently the carry driver reacted late, as he slowed down at the same time as us, but he was already far ahead nearer to the tp.

    by now i was already waiting (or rather, hoping), for the rex to take off for the carry. but it just stayed there! when our car went past the rex, i saw the tp head down towards the left. either he was busy copying down the van's number, or he'd missed the van while doing business with a female officer down there
