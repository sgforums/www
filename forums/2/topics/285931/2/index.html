    There are some legal complications to raising the Age to 21.

    Singapore observes 1949 and 1968 treaties on international driving, part of which is to recognise International driving permits issued overseas. As part of the treaty, 18 year old can obtain IDPs for use in Singapore.

    So if singapore is to raise the age to 21, there would be a scenario where foreigners between 18-21 can drive but Singaporeans can only drive after 21.

    So for now, I don't foresee the age being raised to 21.

    The "best" Singapore can do to raise it is that ppl between 18-21 MUST get parent's approval.
