    Minimum age for the theory/practical for Class 3, 3A, 2B is 18!

    Class 3: Manual & Auto cars
    Class 3A: Auto cars ONLY (no clutch pedal)
    Class 2B: Motorcycles not exceeding 200cc

    NOTE: AGE is based on your BIRTHDATE, not the year. You cannot apply for learning before your birthDATE, only after.

    And no you cannot take the theory before 18 first unless you can make a fake I/C with a fake birthdate.

    So stop asking already! -.-

    It's 18 NO MATTER WHAT! (Unless you're not talking about Singapore or unless you're the next prime minister then maybe)

    P.S. Full definition for Class 3 and Class 3A as follows:

    Class 3: Motorcars Of Unladen Weight Not Exceeding 3000KG With Not More Than 7 Passengers, Exclusive Of The Driver And Motor Tractors And Other Motor Vehicles Of Unladen Weight Not Exceeding 2500KG

    Class 3A: Automatic Motorcars Of Unladen Weight Not Exceeding 3000KG With Not More Than 7 Passengers, Exclusive Of The Driver And Motor Tractors And Other Motor Vehicles Of Unladen Weight Not Exceeding 2500KG (Auto Car)

    References:
    BBDC-

    http://www.bbdc.sg/bbdc/xl/XL200_lite/faq_course.asp
