    25 Dec 04, 10:20
    u will get back portions of both PARF and COE values.
    ie. PARF Rebate and COE Rebate.

    PARF Rebate is based on how old the car is, up to 5 yrs old, will get back 75% of PARF value.

    COE Rebate is based on the unused COE period. So a 4 yrs old car, will get back 6 yrs COE value. ie. 60%.
