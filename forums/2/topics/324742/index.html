    If you are talking about halogen bulbs that produces a bluish white light, that's legal but useless. It's not any brighter than conventional halogen bulbs. Xenon bulbs, also known as HID, produce a very bright bluish white light. But it's only legal if it came factory fitted on your car. LTA prohibit aftermarket HID bulbs to be fitted to your car.
