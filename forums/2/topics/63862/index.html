  • terrkia
    4 Jan 04, 23:16
    LTA guideline is that fron windscreen and front 2 windows must have 70% visibility, means 70% of visible light must be able to pass through.
    rear windscreen and rear 2 windows must have 50% visibility, means can be darker.
    mirror type of window tinting also cannot, must the the traditional ones.
    Go malaysia, it's very cheap, did mine last year for less than RM300 i think, also depend on how big your car is, mine's a colt, so use less material i think, i bargained with the guy.
    can go to taman sentosa that side, got quite a few shops doing, usually the banner will write the price, somemore got 5yr guarantee.
    **Remember to tell them that it has to be approved by LTA, the people there know about it some of them even have previous certs of vehicles that passed the inspection to show you.
    ***Don't do inspection at VICOM, they are VERY strict, go to the other inspection centres, they are more lenient.
