    You need to use one of yr trip computers or odometer.

    Fill up to full tank. I usually use the Auto Stop feature.

    Reset the trip computer to zero after the fill up.

    Now drive as you would normally till the next time you top up.

    When you pump to full tank as before (using the Auto Stop), check how much fuel was needed to be topped up. This should be a good approximation of how much fuel you used since the last time you topped up. Call it X litres.

    Now check your trip computer. That is the total number of kilometers travelled. Call it Y km.

    Your fuel consumption is thus Y/X km/l.

    If you don't have a trip computer just note the odometer (total mileage of the car) readings after the first time you fill up, call it Y1 and the odometer reading when you top up, call it Y2.

    Kilometers travelled Y = Y2 - Y1
