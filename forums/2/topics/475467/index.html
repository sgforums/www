ERP rates at four gantries to increase by S$1 From 4 Nov

  • FireIce
    28 Oct 13, 18:36

    SINGAPORE — From Monday (Nov 4), the Electronic Road Pricing (ERP) rates at four gantries along the Southbound CTE after Braddell Road and PIE slip road into the Southbound CTE will increase by S$1.

    The increase will take place during the 8.30am to 9am time period, where rates are currently S$5.

    The rates for the other gantries will remain unchanged.

    The Land Transport Authority (LTA) announced the revision today, following its completed its quarterly review of traffic conditions on the ERP-priced roads and expressways.

    The next ERP review will take place next month for the December school holiday period.
