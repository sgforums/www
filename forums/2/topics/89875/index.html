    After getting my licence, I had a go with my dad's manual trannie car for about a year, then stopped driving for close to 10 years. My first car was a company issue with auto trannie and I loved the convenience.

    Honest. When you are unfamiliar with the terrain, the routes etc, having an auto trannie gives you one less thing to worry about.

    When I got back to Singapore, I got my first car, an Auto 1.6. Man, the power loss was just horrendous. Upgraded to a 1.8, Auto gearbox with Manual mode cos I didn't know any better then. Still underpowered. Driving the manual equivalent of the same car showed so much more power on tap.

    I had a lot of trepidation about going back to a manual but I guess it was inevitable. And I am surprised at how much more fun and easy it is to drive even on a daily basis.

    I now find myself taking scenic routes, heck even at the beck and call of the missus just to get myself behind the wheel. My old auto car never got this kind of miles
