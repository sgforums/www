    2. suzuki swift(MT/AT) - european design, classic cabin & pretty decent power as well. not too much of performance parts tat i noe of at the moment. the new 1.6L swift sports might be a better catch but it comes at a higher price too though. damage: ard $53k

    3. daihatsu yrv turbo - probably the cheapest turbo u can find in the market now. very gd power for the 1.3L engine. pretty fast in straights but kinda of suspecting on the corners due to its height. comes in only AT but got steer-shift function which works like a MT. think there are some bros in this forum driving the yrv turbo..it reminds me of the old toyota starlet turbo series. damage: ard $55k

    4. mazda 2sp - this is another car u can consider. but the price is a tad more expensive then the abovementioned. however it has great cabin built, roomy interior and spacious legroom for rear passenger compared to all the above. agian it comes in only AT but its also the 1 & only in its class tat offers a "sports-activematic" function tat operates like a MT. has decent power. not too sure abt the handling though. damage: likely to be ard $60k
