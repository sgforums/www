    WHAT to bring

    Enrolment

    1. What are the minimum requirements for enrolment?

    * At least 18 years old upon enrolment
    * Physically and mentally fit
    * Passed eye-sight and colour blindness tests
    * Have not accumulated more than 12 demerit points under the Driver Improvement Point System (DIPS)

    2. What documents should I bring along for my enrolment?

    * Identity card or passport
    * 2 recent passport-size photographs
    * Provisional / Qualified driving licence (if any)
    * Student Pass, Work Permit or Employment Pass (for foreigners only)

    3. What are the modes of payment accepted by CDC?

    * Cash
    * Cheque (Must be crossed and made payable to Comfort Driving Centre Pte Ltd)
    * Nets
    * Cashcard
