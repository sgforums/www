    no worries with boost on the supercharger when the turbo comes on song at higher rpms. new aftermarket SCs come with a clutch system much like those found on ur air con compressors which can be electronically engaged (boost on) and disengaged (boost off). with a suitably wired ecu, the computer can be made to set a "switchover" point, when the SC switches off/ disengage its connection with the pulley and starts to read the boost from the TC to moderate the a/f mix. nothing is impossible with a bit of electronic trickery, provided u have the expertise and the rite hardware/software 2 do it.
