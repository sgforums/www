    Use this map from the official Genting web site :

    http://www.genting.com.my/en/getting_there/road_map.htm

     

    You can park at the multi-story carpark at the Skyway Station at the foot of the mountain and take the cable car up the mountain.  Very safe as the place has security guards from the resort manning the place and ensuring safety to the cars parked there to ensure & encourage visitors.

    You can also drive all the way up to the mountain top.  Parking lots are always filled, so be prepared to wait a long time for somebody to vacate a space.  Along the way up, you'll be stressing your clutch to the max.  You'll see cars parked on the roadside cooling down or broken down.  Very interesting.
