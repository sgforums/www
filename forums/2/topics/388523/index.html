FORD ASEAN in Thailand

  • Uncle4edgar
    19 Jan 10, 14:11

    Ford Motor Company's all-new Ford Fiesta makes its ASEAN debut at the Thailand International Motor Expo at Impact Arena, Muang Thong Thani from December 2-13, giving the Thai public their first look at this wildly popular car that has sold more than a half million units in its first 12 months.

    The dynamic all-new Fiesta will go on sale in Thailand in just a few short months and will be locally produced at AutoAlliance Thailand's new $500 million small car production facility, which will supply the entire ASEAN region, as well as the Australia, New Zealand and South Africa markets.
