    5 Jul 05, 18:15
    Well, the Quattroporte was on display at ngee ann city not too long ago with other Italian makes. I must say that this car is rather special and it does not appeal to the masses like how the BMW 7 series and Mercedes S class does. If you were to ask a car noob whether he/she knows what is a Maserati, chances are that they won't know. Therefore in this brand conscious society like what Singapore is, Maseratis will always be rare and only for the exclusive who are able to appreciate Italian cars. If you were to take a closer look at the gear shift of the Quattroporte, you will realise it is not like your conventional auto. Known as the duo-select transmission which is also found on Maserati Spyders and Coupes but known as Cambiocorsa. The Quattroporte is also fitted with Brembo brakes if I am not wrong
