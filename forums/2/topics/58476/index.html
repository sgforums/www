    when the light is at green, use the 3 arrows on the road as a guide

    the first arrow, release accelerator
    second arrow, if its still green, accelerate.
    if its amber, get ready to brake...
    by the 3rd arrow which is nearest the stop line, u should be stopping already IF there's amber or red light

    to see if the light is going to change to amber or remain as green...
    take a look at the pedestrian crossing
    if its green light, and the green man is on or blinking, the light will remain green, so u can carry on accelerating.

    but it the man is red, just be prepared to stop.

    but just remember to take ur foot of accelerator on the 1st arrow.
