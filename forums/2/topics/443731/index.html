    Junctions without a standby box: you can turn right at any junction with right-turn arrows in the lane. Where there isn't a standby box, you have to stay behind the line until you make the turn.

    Bus lanes: if you're turning on to or off a road with a bus lane, you can only enter the bus lane where the line is broken - - - - -

    U-turns are not allowed at junctions unless positively indicated with a "U-turn" sign. Penalties apply for U-turns at junctions without this sign, and are higher at junctions with a "no U-turn" sign.

    And if you have held your licence for less than a year, you'll need to display a florescent triangle on front and rear wind screens.
