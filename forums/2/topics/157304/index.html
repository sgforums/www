    Wheelbase is different from the length of the car... The length of a car can remain the same but yet the wheelbase expands... Wheelbase is actually the distance between the front and rear wheels, so the car length can remain the same but the length of the engine bay or boot can shorten to allow the wheelbase to expand... Hence the wheelbase of the new civic may be long but the length of the car isnt...
