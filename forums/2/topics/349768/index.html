    cost to get licence varies greatly....cos some ppl learn faster...all in all...should be around 800 to 1.2K bah...

    cost for bike varies even more...first is the bike's machine price...which will change with bike model...length of COE left...condition of the bike...personal preference...etc...

    2ndly is the cost of insurance...which varies according to age,experience(P-plate of course more expensive).

    3rdly is inspection and transfer fee(if getting 2nd hand) for the bike...these 2 are relatively cheap...should be around 50 bucks.

    4thly is the misc items like road tax(should be around 100+ a year)...season parking(17 bucks per month)...petrol....2T if applicable....general maintenance cost of the bike...

    last but not least...if U take over a 2nd-hand bike...U are likely to need to send it for a "check-up"...look look see see if there is anything that needs to be changed...or maybe even overhaul....so a few hundred goes into this as well...
