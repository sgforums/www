    Hey all,

    NUS Motoring Club is organising a Advanced Driving Course. This course is especially cheap if you compare with other courses and it will be conducted by Sean Khoo.

    The Advance Driving Course is very useful for those who drive as it increases their awareness of motoring. For those motorsports enthusiasts, the course will allow them to have better control and knowledge of the dynamics of the car . Advance driving techniques such as reverse flick, hand-brake turn and drifting will be taught . Learn more about high speed cornering and how to better control your speed as the instructor demonstrates to participants various performance driving techniques that will enhance one's confidence and competency.

    Details are as follows:

    Date: 28th Oct (Theory) AND 29th Oct (Prac)
    Cost: $180 (Inclusive of car) OR drive your own car U.P: $280!
    Venue: Sentosa

    If you would like more info, visit www.nusmotoring.org or email kelvin@nusmotoring.org

    Cheers!
