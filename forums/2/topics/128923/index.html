    The articles I have come across says yes.
    If normal radio, still ok. But if you wanna play music with amp whiel engine is off, dun do that . You may have difficulty starting the engine later.
    Dun you notice that if you on your radio and start the engine thereafter, the radio is off by itself for a few seconds while the engine is starting up. That's bcoz starting a car requires near full batt strength.

    Air-con on while engine not running is a No No.

    Dun try to save petrol in such a way. You are draining your batt and putting yourself at risk of engine not being able to start later. When batt is low and you require to turn the ignition longer to get the engine started, you are also shortening your starter's life span.
