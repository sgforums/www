    hehe, nice try. but fact is, u gotta change quite a number of things to get the 160 hp engine 2 perform up to 220 hp.

    1) Change the head to a euro r head that has i-vtec on both the inlet and exhaust cams. while you are at it, u also need to get a type-R intake manifold.

    2) Change the exhaust system to support the new head.

    3) Change the pistons to those of an euro r with higher compression.

    4) Change the ecu to manage the new i-vtec and exhaust plus the higher compression (normal ecu will make ur engine go crazy).

    5) Change the gearbox to manual 6 speed. an auto gear box will never get you the 220 hps down to the ground.

    lots of things to change. by the time you spend all the time and money, other ppl driving a real euro r probably luffing liao.
