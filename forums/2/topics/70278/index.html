    Ok, I'll start out since I'm the topic starter.

    So far been smoked once on the expressway, ECP. Since I got my class 3 of cos.

    Been trying to max out my dad's car(unmodded 1000cc Suzuki Swift) potential. (I know itz not much, but keepin a car in SG needs "mu-lah".)

    While trying out, mostly during the wee hours, 2am - 3am, cos few cars around, some taxi drivers for some reason challenges me at traffic lights(dun understand them, I tot they too tired until cannot think).

    Scenario(my first challenge, or so I've considered it to be) : I stopped at traffic light, 2 taxis(1 yellow, 1 blue) stops at either side. Green light, I floored, they floored, I lead(never challenge them in the first place), I overtake them take right most lane, yellow taxi flashing high beam and horning(I dun understand y he did tat). Reached another traffic light. Yellow taxi stop beside me, green light, both floored, I take inner corner and overtake taxi again, taxi repeat action(flashing & horning), reached home safely.

    Scenario(my defeat) : travelling at 100km/h, 5th gear(save fuel) at ECP exiting Fort road, feeling kinky, floored, reached 120km/h. A Saab(I dunno much bout this car, very bright lights) tailgates me, both overtakes a taxi in front at either side(me left Saab right), the Saab is leading after we passed the taxi, I give a chase, speed 135km/h @ 4500rpm, car vibrates violently, both of us brakes hard at the bend(I drop to 115km/h@4th gear), was able to close up the distance very quickly at the bend, my car vibrates violently again, I slowed down, the distance between me and the phantom Saab lenghtens.

    The experience was exciting and thrilling, I was totally concentrating on the road and corner at the highest possible speed. But the defeat was argonizing, at least I've learnt 1 thing. Skill isn't everything, and never try to take on a bigger car, and I sucked.. Tatz 3 things... hmm...

    My dad's car is going to be scrapped soon, I gave it a chance to prove its maximum potential(on the roads). Its not much but I'm satisfied with the result.
