  • Ali_Khl
    25 Jul 04, 21:37
    Often an automatic gearbox contains a torque converter, which means that if you spin the engine to in Neutral then shift to Drive there will be quite a delay in transmiting the power. My quickest method is with 'power braking' - with my left foot tight on the brake pedal and right foot on the gas pedal, launching at just over 1700 RPM, using much of the low-down torque of the engine. Really pushes you back into your seat!
