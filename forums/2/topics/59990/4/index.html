    Passed on the fifth attempt. Not to discourage anyone or anything, just be presistent and never give up. You give up = you waste time + money.

    Like what the other people said, drive carefully and practice alot until you are confident. What testers want to see :

    1. Confidence(not driving recklessly mind you)
    2. Try to complete circuit without demerits
    3. Check the mirrors(Just check the mirrors, be kia-su a bit)
    4. Chose a good time where there are less cars on the road
    5. Be very alert and watch for dangers or hazards
