  • soleachip
    19 May 08, 21:39

    Guess my version has a few flaws, for TS, check out a useful step by step procedure from another forumer from somewhere else.

    Quote megaweb:

    Position your car so that it is 1 car length ahead and about 1m away of the side.
    Turn your head to check the rear for clearance
    Signal left
    Turn your head to check left, right and then the rear
    reverse straight slowly
    Reverse until see the first pole from the rear, full lock to left
    occassionally turn head to check left and right
    Reverse until see second pole appear on right mirror, 2 rounds to the right...
    See first pole in the front, full lock to right...
    Turn head to check the rear
    Make sure I don't hit the rear poles while reversing...
    Then 1st gear turn left to straighten the wheels...
    Reverse move back to center of parking lot...
    Neutral and hand brake.
    Raise both arms surrender to tester.
    Then 1st gear move to the front again...
    Turn your head to check right and the rear for clearance
    Reverse slowly with full lock to the left
    Signal right and engage 1st gear
    Turn your head to check right for blind spot and move off

    btw, I am still using the same techniques to park my car but not using pole. Checking safety is important. There is reason to check safety for each step. Check left, right and the rear is not only checking the car, you also need to take note of the pedestrian esp. kids.

    Additional tips:
    - move off and reverse slowly <-- you need to master the half clutch to get smooth and ready
    - do not turn the steering wheel when car is stationary
    - always know what you suppose to do next , thus drive confidently
    - do not panic when encounter a hazard. If you can proof that you can handle any hazard, there is no reason TP will fail you. __________________
