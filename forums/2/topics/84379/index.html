    I think if one has the spare cash then the wiser option would certainly be to buy a brand new car. But if one is a little bit tight on the budget, a used car would suffice.

    Maintenance-wise, many is of the opinion that one would be spending a bit more on the servicing of used cars. However, I don't really agree with that.

    Vehicles are like humans, we need regular exercise to keep ourselves healthy. Likewise for cars, they need regular servicing to ensure they function to their optimal level.
    Please don't have the misconception that new cars don't need regular servicing because their lifespan would decrease tremendously if not properly maintained.

    But in the end, it is your money - so think carefully.
