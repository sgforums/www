    During a non-injury accident or accident not involving foreign vehicles and government vehicles, you do not need to make a police report. If you are not able to produce your licence or identity at that point of time, you are required under the law to maka a police report within 24 hours of the accident.

    Don't care about what the other party says, be it no licence or not. Just note down his vehicle number and make a police report and you will be alright.
