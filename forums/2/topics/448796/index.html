OUTRAGEOUS Carpark fee hike at Millenia Walk during IT fair

  • Dahoth
    10 Mar 12, 19:44

    Who here had a similar experience?

    I am outraged by the carpark fee hike that I experienced at the Millenia Walk basement carpark today. While normal charges are $1 an hour on Saturdays. For a period of 6 to 7(max) hours, I was today charged $19.80!!! I should have only been charged $6-$7!

    The reason claimed, was due to the IT fair next door at Suntec, and the ONLY notification of this hike I found, AFTER I WAS GIVEN THE SHOCK of $19.80 when I then went to look for the management, was a small A4 size notice put on the sistic and information counter on the ground floor of Millenia Walk!!

    There were NO signs at the car park entrances nor were there signs at the escalators leading up from the carpark to the shopping area about the fee hike!! How often  do consumers walk at the FRONT of the sistic counter and take notice of a very plain looking A4 size notice displaying the sudden carpark fee hike for this period??? Is this truly sufficient notice to drivers???

    As a consumer, I was given no CHANCE to decide whether to accept this fee hike due to a lack of sufficient signages, so I am APPALLED by this! I realised this had happened before and others have complained but back then the management claimed sufficient notice was put up. How is an A4 size notice on a counter at an information desk sufficient for this year???

    I am not the only one taken aback by this. Other drivers coming out of the carpark were similarly shocked by this hike, they were too were not properly informed.

    The reason of managing traffic congestion is ludicrous, so their management should not not even try justifying the hike with this reason. Entering the carpark, electronic signboards displayed hundreds of empty lots for one. There were NO signs at the carpark entraces (i reiterate) to inform drivers of this fee hike. In fact, the carpark entrances displayed the USUAL rates. So this is inconsistent messaging to carpark drivers. No, it's FALSE notification in fact. Effectively, luring the driver with a cheaper rate and charging them with a HIGHER rate!! Even Suntec, where the IT fair itself, did they raise rates? so what VALID reason did millenia walk have?

    This is a BLATANT abuse of power and a perfect example of opportunistic capitalism for short term gain! Truly, is such short term gain worth it? Because the price for it is now my boycott Millenia Walk and I now become an advocate in informing the world of such blatant abuse of power and will no doubt convince anyone I meet not to patron such an opportunistic establishment.
