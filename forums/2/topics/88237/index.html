    I tried go karting for the first time back in June. Absolutely loved it! It was such an exhilarating experience that I rounded up a couple of Initial D-watching friends for another session back in Jurong.

    No regrets for the money spent at all. In fact, I'm itching to get back into the go kart seat.

    The sense of speed on the go kart, even though it doesn't look fast, definitely feels fast - much faster than anything I've felt on 4 wheels. But of course, I only drive a normal 1.6 l car, so I can't compare to what the turbo or VTEC guys experience. But to me, the sensation of being squished to the side of your seat when taking corners in a go kart is dangerously addictive.

    All car forum guys should give go karting a try!
