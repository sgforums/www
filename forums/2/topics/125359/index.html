    S$40k with high FC, Picanto. Average at 17km/L and can go to 19-20km/L if done correctly. I guess that is your only choice. The next better one would be kelisa with similar FC. But it is usually priced S$2-3k above Picanto.

    The next budget car with remarkable FC would be Vios, which S$15k above your stated budget. Then there is the City with super FC, at S$20k above you stated budget. Then the all-time favour, Jazz with un-beatable FC, but I guess you are S$26k short.

    All model stated above are manual cars, cause manual cars are cheaper and produce better FC.
