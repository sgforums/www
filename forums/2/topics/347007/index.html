    I choose to spend abit more on petrol rather than having a faulty air com compressor.

    When you on your aircon, you can actually feel a 'tak tak' sound to show that your aircon compressor is working to pump cool air into the cabin. With the constant on and off of aircon, you are actually stressing the compressor.

    How compressor works ?

    It will work to pump cool air into the cabin till the the thermostat temp and it will deactivate itself and only activate when the cabin temp is below that temp.

    And the belt you mentioned is either the fan belt or timing belt.
