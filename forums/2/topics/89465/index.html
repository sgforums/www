    Class 4 alone is $500 inclusive of 1 theory lesson and 5 practical lessons.

    Class 5 alone is $500 inclusive of 3 practical lessons.

    Class 4 and 5 together is $750 inclusive of 1 theory lesson and 5 practicals lesson for class 4 and 3 practical lessons for class 5.

    All fees does not include booking TP date,PDL and GST.
