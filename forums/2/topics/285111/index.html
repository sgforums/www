    actually sat in one today. even shifted the gear around for awhile internally, build quality has improved the most over the ct9a (finally, an updated dash over the old boring one since evo 7) and features even larger leg room (thanks to longer wheelbase). door closes with a more solid feeling now. engine bay looks more compact (read: neat) now even though the SE was telling me the engine is a bit larger. the intercooler pipings run more directly now dat the inlet and exos manifold has switched directions from the old 4G63 (the 4B11 now has the inlet manifold facing the front).

    dun really like the exterior though. despite the significantly wider fenders, vents on bonnet and high mount spoiler, appearance still very much like the lancer we have now. but give me 130k to blow on a car, i'd still place my money on the evo x. but advice to all contemplating: get the SST option. both GSR and RS feature the 5 speed manual tranny if u do not specify the SST option (which will set u back another 8k or so). given the heavier kerb weight albeit, a slight improvement of 22 nm over the IX, my guess is the only way the X can outrun the IX (stock to stock) is the faster shifting time of the SST.
