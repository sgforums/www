    N can, for instance, be used in situation when you want to rev the engine for a fast getaway from a standstill or create a wheelspin. Can't be shift from P to R to N then to D right?

    Also, for emergency freeing of gear in case your accelerator get stuck, notice you dont have to release the catch (press the button) to shift fr D to N and vice versa. Shifiting to P can be dangerous as it will lock the wheels.

    If you car breaks down, try pushing the car wit the gears in P or D, the car probaby will not move. N is also useful here.

    Although there is not much application in daily driving, since much of P function overlaps / or is superior to N purpose, N is still designed to be there for some reasons.

    And one more thing, shifting frequently will cause wear and tear but that does not mean shifting into N will do damage the gearbox. Example when you are waiting for someone, shifting to N might be a good idea.
