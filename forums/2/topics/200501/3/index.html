    The Evo in F&F:TD is actually an IX. Note the black chrome headlights that come standard with IXs. VIIIs come with chrome headlights and rear lights.

    The reason why there is the centre-piece on the front bumper is because the Evo was fitted with a APR widebody kit based off the standard VIII front bumper.

    It was also installed with a kit supplied by RMR allowing it to switch from AWD mode to RWD mode on the fly, hence making it more driftable. Although Evo purists would cry foul at the conversion, given that the Evo's roots are in 4WD rally events.
