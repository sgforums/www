    1) It depends on what package you got for yourself at SSDC, if you only enrol strictly for BTT and FTT at SSDC, then you can just complete your BTT and FTT at SSDC.

    2) Yes, you can complete your BTT and FTT at a driving school and look for a private instructor for practical lesson and go back to school for TP final test.

    3) On average, it takes about 4-6 months to get a driving licence. 3 months is rather impossible, unless you always keep a lookout for people who cancel their BTT and FTT and TP final test date booking and get their earlier date. But all these is about luck.
