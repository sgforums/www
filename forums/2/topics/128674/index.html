    It is rather clear that the RX-8 is not an "replacement" of the lamet RX-7 coz it isnt, but rather just another rotary interpation.Initally when it was 1st launched,almost wanted to get the JDM MT from MM but had a change of plans.
    Although the RX-8 has the look of a "race-tuned sports car",it requires high engine speeds to make its peak power.
    Anyway,the feel of the grip for corners through the steering wheel is not bad,noise level are well kept,the delivery is also quite smooth,not forgetting its "4dr" concept.It has been seen to be more like a family "coupe" to many,no wonder quite a number of the older age group individuals are driving this car. Overall,quite a fun car to drive while still remaining livable on a day-to-day basis.
