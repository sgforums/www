    My Review

    1) Novena Square - I've checked it out personally. The carpark entrance & exit are actually ok, not as narrow as claimed.. I wouldn't classify it as difficult.

    2) Bukit Batok - one multiple-storey carpark, which I believe is the same one mentioned by sOnic (I also dunno where, can't remember). That one is probably the narrowest that you can find in S'pore (your car will be only a few inches away from both sides of the wall, leaving you with almost no allowance for any margin of error). I remember that when I had my refresher back then, my instructor got me there to practise (very honourable of him to trust me with his car). He did tell me then, that if I could drive thru' the entrance & exit a couple of times, I could go any carparks safely. =)
