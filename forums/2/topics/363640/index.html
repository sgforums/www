    Here is another list that i saw from some other places.....

     

    1. Novena Square (brings new meaning to tight spots)
    2. Allson Hotel (opposite National Library: The entrance and exit kills!)
    3. Pearl's Centre (Slopes are a killer and the turning sigh... I go there for vegeterian food okay! Not for Yangtze!!!)
    4. Burlington Square.. Lots of pillar, very small parking lot, sharp corners, blind spots..Should be used for driving test by TP
    5. Great World City Basement Carpark 90 degree turn.
    6. Parkway Parade.. Killer slope, narrow turns to go up/dn, small lots, long queues... there are 2 car parks (depend on where u enter) and they are not linked.
    7. Shaw towers... Cheapest parking in town, most narrow exit in town too! (the marks on the wall proves it!)
    8. Gleneagles carpark (damn narrow)
    9. HDB carpark opp jurong point (narrow slope, alot of mark on wall indicating casualty)
    10. Tan Tock Seng Basement carpark (the spirals are really tight)
    11. Liang Court (Very tight and long killer slope spirals) Had being to most of the above car park and this is the worst.
    12. Sheraton Hotel (Scotts) basement carpark (narrow turns, small lots n drabby looking)
    13. Specialist Shopping Centre - narrow slopes, small slots, low ceilings (even the management warned before you enter the carpark   )
    14. Marriott - low wall beside the lots - potential hits on rear bumper cos these walls are blind spots for reverse sensors
    15. Fortune Centre (Beside Si-Ma Lu Guan Yin Temple) The entrance will turn you off, with very narrow slopes along and all the way at least to four-five stories before can reach carpark lot. Super challenging for the beginners and veri expensive to park there too!
    16. Orchard Building (acute angle for up-ramp, must always do THREE-POINT-TURNS to go up from B3 to B2 and from B2 to B1 else bang walls)
    17. West Mall (check out the grooves in the walls....)
    18. Paradize center ( the slope, no matter how slow u are sure hit out bumper leap)
    19. Cineleisure (steep Slope, and tight S turn)
    20. Wisma Atria (Going Up the slope and sudden tight turn)
    21. IMM (on Sundays..and Sats evenings.....carparks always full…)
    22. Bugis Junction (only applicable to those who lowered their suspension  )
    23. HDB at CCK central (Go into basement carpark easy but be prepared to look at left and right walls when u exit, esp right after the exit gantry.
    24. Robinson Centre (steep & narrow with sharp turns... so many langa lines at each turns..)
    25. Delfi Orchard carpark( veryvery cramp need to do 3 point turn)
    26. Rochor center ( alot of pillars can easily hit pillars and other cars)
    27. Marine Parade Multi-storey (Super tight, Kanna twice at the pillars when started driving.. sigh.. must have technique)
    28. Opp Bishan Junction - Public multi-storey carpark. Not tight but instead very wide. But Going down the spiral exit fast will make u giddy
    29. Takashimaya basement carparks (very narrow, spiral exit)
    30. Tampines Mall carpark exit ( Don;t step too hard on acc else you will hit the pillar right in front of the exit !!! and look out for taxi exiting from you right)
    31. Parco Bugis [Nvr exit @ Intercontinental hotel, down slope to main rd is so steep that any front bumper can grind the rd, unless u driving SUV]
    32. Roberson Quay [not so bad for small cars. but like Burlington Square, hell alot of pillars w/ sharp bends]
    33. Central opp Clarke Quay [beware going up/down with a full stomach .. killer spiral slope]
    34. White Sands Shopping Centre - undergoing some reno, very tight U-turn to get to B2, another super tight one at exit (need to shift right before hard-left at end of ramp)
    35. Far East plaza (turning is 90 degree.. sian diao.. my car just got a scratched by the side when i going down the slope...   maybe I’m not experience in driving.. sianz...
    36. Sembawang Shopping Centre...Very Narrow to Turn Down for Exit
    37. Haw Par Technopark downslope.....narrow with wall on both side
    38. Novena Square 2 Spiral carpark narrow and steep (spiral until headache)
    39. The Cathay (near PS)... alot of very tight turnings.
    40. LIDO (Shaw Center) - SO SO SO SO NARROW with the PARKING LOT - RISK getting scratch by others
    41. M Hotel - watchout as you drive down the carpark. Protruding pillar .. scratched my car last month..
    42. Funan the IT Mall - Very narrow slopes when moving up and down between levels, I banged my car against the metal poles here when turning down the slopes.
    43. United Square, narrow and winding slopes
    44. Excelsior hotel, beware of reverse parked vehicles at both sides of the corners.
    45. Hougang mall, small tight turn everywhere.
    46. T2 Carpark coming down - can be champion racer but passengers will be very giddy..prepare some plastic bags.
    47. OCBC centre -- very tight turns. Lots are not deep enough to fit car!
    48. Tong Eng Building - miniature carpark with improper placing of exit gantry
    59. Tiong Bahru Plaza Basement carpark - Watch out for errant pedestrians crossing the road when you rev your car park up the slope to exit the carpark.
