    I think change old Pi to new Pi is ok. I got a friend intend to change old Sunny to new Sunny. but do consider when you want to sell off the new Pi. Logic is by the time you want to sell new Pi, the scrap value can be lower than your old Pi even though it's a newer car.

    e.g. COE was around 25k 1 year ago. So assuming you get new Pi with same OMV at lower COE of 12.5k and want to sell 3 years later, you old Pi can still get back COE rebate of 15k (6yrs left) while your new Pi will only get back COE rebate of 8.75k (7yrs left). Parf rebate for both old and new Pi the same, so you will be worse off at that time unless you can sell off your old Pi at 4k premium (not including body value).
