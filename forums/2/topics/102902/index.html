    Open-ended Gameplay
    Ok, the first thing tat really struck me while playing NFSU2 is how much more open-ended and robust it is. This time, instead of playing thru a sequence of races (as in the 1st game), you can choose which race to contend in simply by finding ur way ard the city. Yes, u heard me rite.. It's an ENTIRE city for u to explore and cruise around. Think Grand Theft Auto thrown into NFSU, stir around a little, and NFSU2 is wad u get! Finding ur way ard the city is not as hard as u might tink as a neat GPS system will guide u where to go.


    The city is all yours... But other racers are out there waiting...


    Your car's GPS navigator will keep u updated on the lo'down happenings in the city

    The Cars!
    Of cos, wad is NFSU without cars?! Along wif most of the vehicles from the previous game, NFSU2 throws in a a neat selection of new cars including the much-wanted Evo (Evo VIII in this case) and even SUV's!! No, I'm not kidding! Tere's even a Hummer H2!! Imagine a giant racing on the streets!


    My current car in the game... Toyota Celica

    Variety of Races
    Nothing much tat is new here.. Circuit races, drag, sprint and drifting r as fun as ever. But tere is a new mode of race called Street-X where u race wif ur opponents on a really small track (sth like drift track) and the onlie way to win in such tight tracks is to be savage and slam ur opponents into railings and walls...

    Custom/Dyno Tuning
    This is the reason y u car fanatics shld play it. NFSU2 features a really sophisticated dyno-tuning system which allows u to tweak wif juz abt every component of ur car.. Found out ur car is a little too slow in the drag race? Just tweak ur car's aerodynamics to allow it to cut thru the air like a knife! Engine not satisfying? Adjust ur RPM! I am not so much of a mechanical expert myself, but I can tell EA really put their heart and soul in this part.. The numbers & figures juz look like indian language to me though..


    Tune everything right down to ur suspension ratio!

    !!Verdict!!
    Every gamer or car fanatic MUST play tis game.. You shld play it, ur brother shld play it, ur grandma shld play it!!


    Hit the NOS & enjoy NFSU2!!
