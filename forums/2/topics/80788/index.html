    you will need to complete 5 practicals and 1 theory lesson .
    All this will takes one week to complete .
    They will let you choose the days of practical as near to the test date as possible .

    Class 4 : Three point turn , rope tying , parallel parking and U turn will be tested

    Class 5 : Three point turn and U turn will be tested

    As for the theory : a verbal test will be tested after your practical test . *Hint for theory test : Remember the height limit and what kind of vehicles canNOT enter CTE . Height limit as in how high your vehicle need to inform Traffic police and how high your vehicle then you will need to call TP for escort . The rest of their question is just question like asking you to identify logos on road like 60 km/h and height limit logos etc

    For practical : Just drive at a speed below 45km/h , Safe and smooth driving will pass you from the test but do note that if you are taking class 5 , you got to be careful with the crane infront while making a turn , three point turn or a U turn .

    Just taking class 4 alone cost you about $450 while if you enrol for their package of Class 4 & 5 together , you will only need to pay $750 .

    Hope this helps
