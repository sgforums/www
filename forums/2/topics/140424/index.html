    in any case, my point is that anti-roll bars, as the name implies, prevents body roll (sideways movement). and this occurs only in cornering and lane switching at high speed. whether ur car floats or not (vertical oscillations) when traveling in a straightline at hi speeds depends on ur spring/damping rates and how they counteract with the ground at those speeds. not ur lateral roll resistence (via anti roll bars).
