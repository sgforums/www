  • Forhod
    3 Oct 04, 04:20
    If you find yourself stepping more on the accelerator when accelerating and increase in FC, it could be time for you to change your clutch.

    Run a simple test to determine if there is clutch slip.

    -In a stationary position, pull the handbrake.
    -Slowly release the clutch pedal and at the same time increase the accelerator.

    Result:
    -If the engine stalls, your clutch is fine.

    -If the engine is still running, time to change your clutch.
