    they have A, B, C, E, G, M, R, S classes. under S and C class, we have SL/CL, SLK/CLK, SLR and CLS.

    Class ranking: S, E, C, A.

    A are super minis entry-level cars.
    C are compact (mid-sized) entry-level luxury cars.
    E and CLS are mid-sized cars.
    S are full-sized (limousine) cars.
    SL and SLK are roadsters.
    CL and CLK are coupÃ©s.
    SLR is McLaren.
    G are SUVs.
    GL, M and MLK are Crossover SUVs, or XUVs.
    B and R are MPVs.

    the first 2 number is an indication of the engine displacement, e.g. E220's have a displacement of roughly 2.2 litres. but this comes with exceptions, e.g. C-class C180, C200 CDI and C240.

    Non-class (not for first alphabet) designations:
    L = Leicht (light). used to be long wheelbase.
    R = Racing
    S = Sportlich (sporty)
    K = Kompakt (compact)
    D = Diesel
    CDi = Common-rail Diesel Injection
