    Originally posted by narz:
    hmm... how much should one be earning to afford cheaper cars like Hyundai Getz? any advice from anyone out there?
    It's actually a simple equation:
    Instalment - $500-800, depending on your downpayment, no. of years loan, etc, but i believe most car buyers now pay the least and spread the loan over, say 7 years. This is likely to result in about $600-700 for a normal Jap or Korean car..

    Petrol - depends on usage, but typically $100-400

    Road tax - about $50-100 a month

    Insurance - about $1500-2500 a year

    Parking - fixed HDB about $65-90, variable for your other trips

    Just take an average of each of the above components, and you are likely to end up with about $1000/month.
