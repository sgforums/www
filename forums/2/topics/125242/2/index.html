    After looking through all the pervious discussion about whether to buy a new or 2nd-hand car, I drew 2 conclusion.

    Why get a 2nd-hand car,
    1. If you can downpaid a huge sum so as not to suffer from it super high interest.
    2. If you have not intention to drive it for more 5 years.

    If you get loans that extends all the way to the death date of the COE, the car will always be a liability to you. Even if you want to sell it off 2-3 years before the end of its COE, you might still have to top-up for the loan you own the bank.
