    I just booked one, so obviously I like it.

    At the end of the day, a lot depends on what your priorities are and what you intend to you the car for.

    Personally, I was looking for a small hatchback for mostly City driving. My priorities were:-

    1. Good ride/handling balance
    2. Reasonable performance
    3. Good build quality
    4. Economical to run.

    I chose the Swift over the Getz as it just felt nicer to drive (although the Getz was cheaper and it's pick up was slightly better in Auto form). Also the Swift interior looked and felt more classy than the Getz, which was too plasticky for my liking.
