    i personally used polygaze before and dont really recommend it..

    it gets the job done.. but just dont find satisfaction in using it..

    there are others.. but they are quite pricey for shampoo..

    i used autoglym b4 and i am satisfied with the shampoo.. after washing, the car feels relatively smooth and nice shine.. no, its not a 3in1 product..

    3in1 product = products with wax...

    i personally dont like 3in1 products.. but u can try..

    u can always go to carrefour to look at their wide range of car care products or u can always go to autobacs at bukit batok to take a look too..

    getting an average priced microfiber cloth is good as it has less tendancy to scratch ur car surface. but no need to get those cloth that costs $20-$40.. get those below $10 is good enough...

    proper way to wash a car..

    1. use water hose or pail to rinse dirt off the car.
    - there are small fine dirt particles on the car that might scratch the car if u do not use this step..

    2. mix ur shampoo with water and give the car a really good scrub..

    3. repeat step 1.

    4. use the MF cloth to dry ur car.

    for rims.. those cars with many spokes are relatively tough to clean because of braking dusts.. 5-6 spokes are much easier to clean..

    i personally like those foam sprays to clean rims because its just spray on and wash off..
