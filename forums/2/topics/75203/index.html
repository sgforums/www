  • hocky
    10 Apr 04, 09:36
    That they start with a clean bucket of water all the time, clean sponges and clean drying cloths. That they also use proper car shampoo that doesn't eat away at your expensive paintwork and also proper car polishes that are not too abrasive to do more damage. That they spend the required amount of time doing the work instead of doing a speed wash leaving some of the abrasive dirt still on the paintwork and rubbing this all into the surface causing more surface damage and spiderweb fine scratches visible in direct sunlight especially on the darker colours like black, midnight blue and so on. Best is to do it yourself like me and you will only be pleased with what you have done and not be pushed to complete in the allocated work time. Cheers.
