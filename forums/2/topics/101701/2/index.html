    When I see the title, I though we are comparing between the normal lancer and the city-idst. Since they are of about the same price of 61K. Anyway, my bro test-driven both (Low end ones, not V-tec) and he made some comments about them.

    Firstly, engine. City actually feels a little sluggish, its power kicks-in slowly maybe because of its SOHC engine. Lancer however does give you the rush when Rev.

    Secondly, handling. My bro didn't say anything about city's handling. But he was quite please with Lancer's. It gives him the confidence to speed toward a corner and made a high speed turning during his test-drive. I was in the rear passenger seat and I could really feel the G-force. I can tell my bro really likes driving that car.

    Thirdly, space. Lancer's cabin is decent, usual for cars of its range. City's cabin is also about that, but wait til you open its boot. You can easily place 2 super-sized lugguge in there and still have room to spare. You will never run out of room for this car.

    In all, Lancer would be the choice if my bro were to choose between these 2 cars. But the decision wouldn't be clear with Vios in the picture.

    Both Lancer and city he tested are Manual, but Toyota doesn't have manual Vios for testing, so my bro went for the auto one. For enginer and power, Vios wins. But I think most people are quite concern about its small cabin space and thin car walls(safety).

    Anyway, do test-drive all and you will be able to see the different.
