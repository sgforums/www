    biting point is easy once u get the hang of it. for me, i sorta know my biting when my thigh sorta in line with the air con switch, the car is always moving forward.

    slope. once u stepped to 2500 rpm, stay thr. but b4 that, u slowly step the accelerator to get the feel. to feel how much to step to rch certain rpm. listen to the noise too. that's what i did.

    once u get that, stay n lift up ur clutch handbrake dwn n go. u will know when ur car from q noisy to quieter smoother engine noise.
