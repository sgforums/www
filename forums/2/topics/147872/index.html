    if you looking for solar film, the established ones are Vkool, huper optik and 3M

    Vkool and huper optik are from the same parent company but apparently their products are different. vkool's film are metalic base, and they have to cut out a piece from your frontwindscreen film to enable your IU to work properly.

    Huper optik on the other hand uses ceramic base,no problem with your IU.

    From the best of my knowledge 3M is neither ceramic or metal base. so i have no idea what it is.

    but my recommendation is not to go malaysia for the films. they are usually films dyed sold cheaply without much UV/heat resistant.

    you get what your money worth, a cheap one no help in uv/heat resistant and may not pass LTA specs.
