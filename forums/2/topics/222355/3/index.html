    Originally posted by phoenix114:
    my dad just got his Axio.
    good stuff i must say for such a price. very value for money.
    the build quality is tip top and the door closes with a secure thud, not a 'clank'. the meters glow amber at night and there is a reverse camera such that you would not hit the curbs. the stock console also plays dvds and vcds. there are also enough storage spaces and the attention to detail is just impressive. for example, the in-car lights light up awhen you unlock the car doors (not only when you open it). and dim after you lock the doors. very classy touch. noise insulation is also very good.
    the engine is 1.5L however, there is sufficient power to pull the car out of sticky situations. shift the knob in to sports mode and one would feel a little surge from the engine. however, the ride is a little high with 2 hand spacing from the wheel arch to the tyres. rear end styling is not very attractive...abit bland imo. and unlike the G8 and G9 corolla, the G10 has rear drum brakes.
    sounds great. let me tell you my experience when I get my car mid apr.... btw how much did you pay for the axio?
