    My father's Picanto had this creaking sound too. Kia gave him a explanation which I think is reasonable and it isn't a cause for alarm.

    When there is people sitting at the back of your car, the rear suspension is compressed and the rear ride height is slightly lower than usual.

    Then when you pull the handbrake up and the rear passengers alight, the suspension will rebound back to its original height and at the same time the rear wheels will rotate A BIT. And this SLIGHT rotation work against the handbrake and cause this creaking noise. Kia assured us it is normal.
