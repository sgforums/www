  • zorro82
    1 Apr 05, 22:10
    Tips from me:

    1) Take your time in the circuit. The faster you go, the more kan chiong you are and thats where all the unwanted mistakes come along. Dun rush, there's no time limit in the circuit except for the parellel and vertical parking.

    2) Parking: make good use of half clutch, slow and steady and you would be able to park it nicely

    3) Blind spot: Juz keep checking for every turn and changing of lane. Blind spot is free of charge so check more also nv mind. (If you can clear your blind spot, most likely you will passed) Tester are extreme typical of blind spot

    4) On the road: If the road in front is clear/ not much cars, change to higher gear liao. When approaching traffic light or cars, better change to lower gear at least before 3 car lengths. You can stop at gear 3 which is the minimum. Tester want to see how confidence you are at the road.

    5) Changing lanes: First check front mirror, then check rear mirror. If it is clear to change, do a quick blind spot then speed up to change lane.

    6) Go and pray to gods/ancestor for good luck and no cars/motorcycle to disturb you in your test. Believe it or not, is up to you

    7) Go and have a good rest.

    All the best to your test. Regardless the result, hope you can post it here and let us know. Good luck
