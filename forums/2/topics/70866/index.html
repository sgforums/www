    At first I thought of going for the 7 yrs loan, i.e. monthly is about $760, but i changed my mind, i put in more downpayment and took the 10yrs loan to have my monthly reduce to $560.

    If you are looking for the auto version, is only the 1.6. My friend has been driving the 1.3 manual for about two months, fuel consumption is good I believe its about 15-16km/L.

    The chain drive is positive, ie slippage is almost eliminated as compared to belt drive. This translate to accurate timing of the valves etc. if it is tuned correctly in the 1st place. BUT the con is that if the chain breaks, it would be very costly to replace. The dealer did not commit but he say that chain drive can last very long i.e. 5 yrs minimum without replacement. Still I think it depends on your driving habits.
