    Hi guys,

    My name is Jasper. I don't know if you guys have heard the news but The Boys' Brigade is holding it's annual sharity gift box car rally at ACS Barker.

    The Sharity Gift Box is an annual Boys' Brigade initiative which is a national movement spearheaded by The Boys' Brigade (BB) in Singapore which aims to spread cheer to the less fortunate during the Christmas season each year.

    The car rally is basically an event where drivers from all over Singapore both brigade and non-brigade members come together to send out food hampers to the less fortunate individuals of our community.

    The event will be held at ACS Barker on the 15 Dec 2007 (Sat) from 11am to 2pm.
    Feel free to come down anywhere during the time period.

    If any one of you guys are interested and would like to register (name, contact number and e-mail) or require more information, feel free to e-mail me at bbsgb@bb.org.sg or you can call me at 67370377 (ext 125). Feel free to walk in on the event day to help out. We do have a walk in registration counter.

    Take this opportunity to spread the spirit of caring and sharing to the less fortunate this christmas. Have a heart, share a gift.
