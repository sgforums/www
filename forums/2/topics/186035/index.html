  • Chin Eng
    13 Apr 06, 08:07
    Drive into petrol kiosk. Head for the car wash section.

    Go through tunnel (most have some kind of enclosure). The folks there will spray water first, soap second, and water to wash off the soap.

    They will ask you to move out of the enclosure.

    They will wipe down you car, including opening the doors to clean the side of the doors.

    Most of them will end at driver's side and with the door open someone will tell you that the job is done.

    Price: Between $5 to $8 depending on car. Usually hatchback and sedan is one price, MPV and SUV another. Taxi is usually cheaper.

    Don't pay till the job is almost done.
