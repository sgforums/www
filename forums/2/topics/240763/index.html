    You can find out more on vehicle transfer here :-
    http://www.onemotoring.com.sg/publish/onemotoring/en/lta_information_guidelines/buy___sell_a_used/Ownership_Transfer.html


    They tell you the calculations and also provide a calculator if you have the specific car no.

    As for your question, yes, if you buy a 2nd hand car, if its 2 yrs old or whatever, you can own it for the rest of its COE life (ie in this case, another 8 years). All 2nd hand cars will already have a COE that is paid for. Only when you buy a car that is at the end of its life (e.g 9.9 yrs old car due for scrap), then you will need to get a renewed COE for the car to continue driving it for another 10 yrs.

    But usually dealer will include a new COE in the sale.
