Tried once on a really full tank pump from jb, last me almost 700 before i go top up ( combination of city and highway drive from F to E).

    For space wise, altis would be bigger coz lancer front seat is ok but if the driver has long legs, the rear passenger would feel discomfort.

    As for sunny, although the fc is high, at least the body feels tougher than a lancer as in feel more solid.

    The fc for TS, I would say its on average but when raving, it is higher than a lancer. lancer when really whacked, fc around 8km/liter but ts is around 6-7km. (when i say whack...i mean really whack hard)

    Appearance wise, lancer and TS would be better choice as there is quite alot of room for body kits and design. More choices and it will still look very sporty.

    But one bad thing about the new lancer, is the front grill. Instead of the moustache look, it now look so squarish.

    Things to know about lancer
    1) The insurance is higher compare to the other cars like sunny,altis.(around 3-5% higher depending)

    2) The parts are more expensive.( On average around 20%-50% depending on which parts e.g front bumper $350, altis around $200-250)

    3) You would be unlikely to find choices for parts in JB as the people there dun really like to get lancer.The choices are very limited.(very hard to find parts and accessories like e.g strut bar, proton for less den 150ringgit can get, lancer need 285 ringgit)

    4) Mantainace if go back to CC, get ready to get your blood suck up.( Imagine 20K servicing+ change pad+change filther = $500)If go outside get, I think can get around 350-400.
