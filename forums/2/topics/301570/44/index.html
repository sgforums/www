    Rather easy to differentiate real JDM Type-Rs from fake Type-Rs. The fake ones usually don't have the same white alloys of the real Type-R. I believe the white alloys are unique to the JDM Type-R.

     

    And fake ones either have the stock brakes, which is smaller than the Type-R ones. And their calipers may not be red in colour. Even if it's red, it might just be a stuck on fake "Brembo" calipers. Even if the fake Type-Rs have bigger brakes, it's usually aftermarket and it's maybe grooved or cross-drilled. Rear bumpers will usually be different also.

     

    That's all I can think of for now.
