    In case you don't know, a trade plate, which is the proper term... is a form of insurance to cover whoever is driving whatever vehicle the plate is attached to. Should there be an accident in any case, the company who owns that trade plate will make the report to the insurance company. The plate basically allows a driver authorized by the company to drive whatever vehicle that plate covers, an unregistered vehicle (new car), a special forces Ford Explorer or for this case, TMJ's Bentley. A Singaporean is not allowed to drive a Malayisan registered vehicle here, which is another point to take note. Whoever is driving that TMJ Bentley is definately not from the palace :)

    Assuming you already know how to use the pen tool and create an outline of something -

     

    - Open picture in photoshop

    - Duplicate the layer
    - In the duplicated layer/picture, outline whatever you want to keep in colour using the pen tool

    -When you are finished, right click in the area and 'make selection'

    - Press CTRL + SHIFT + I to inverse it, and then press delete - this will delete everything except what you have outlined

    -Select original layer, play around with it as much as you want, then go IMAGE>ADJUSTMENTS>DESATURATE

     

    That should work if you play around with it
