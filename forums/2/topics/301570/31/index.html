    18 Feb 08, 22:09

    On Sunday I went to Funan with my parents, and we parked there, forced to park on the lowest level B3 though because it was full everywhere else. I noticed a special lot next to the lift which was reserved for 3 number plates, was curious so I noted them down and checked them when I got home. I was quite shocked because turns out to be reserved for a Porsche Turbo, a Challenge Stradale and a Gallardo! I was shocked not by the cars, but specifically how the heck the owner could park the cars in there. Basically not only would you have to be extremely careful about the slopes and 3 humps just after the ERP barrier, but if any of you have ever parked there before then you'd know how tight the turns are, especially the ones to go down to another level. I was already nervous enough going down there in the CR-V, all the turns had marks all over the walls and railings where others had scraped... Must be one damn good driver whoever owns those car's
