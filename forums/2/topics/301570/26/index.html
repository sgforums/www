    Originally posted by alleggerita:
    No way..it had titanium tipped exhausts and the rims are unique to John Cooper Works model if I'm not wrong..
    JCW Minis are not produced straight from factory, in actual fact they are still Mini Cooper S, with JCW tuning kit retrofitted. The picture you took had aftermarket wheels. JCW wheels exists in 3 forms, all 18inch. One sure way to tell if a Cooper S has really got the JCW kit is by bending down and looking at the exhaust system. Stock exhaust has the pipe going through the 2 ugly mufflers in series, the JCW one splits into 2 going to individual chrome oval mufflers on both sides. Oh and the exhaust tips are not made from titanium.

    A rare Mini would be the Cooper S JCW GP Edition. 2000 made, 3 made it here. Here's one example being put up for sale:
    http://www.sgcarmart.com/main/info-2E0L1Bx-1000.html
