    I believe the most important thing is that you must show the tester that you're confident in your driving. If not, then they'll just use all the blind spot / safety checks as excuse to fail you.

    Peak hour is tough cos there're lotsa cars and they're all going faster than you (e.g. hard to change lanes).

    When I took my test, I had to go a little over the speed limit in order to change lanes. The tester pointed this out after we came in, I apologised, but he still passed me.

    Since you're a driver at SCDF, I suppose you're already reasonably good at driving. Just be polite, humble, but show that you're confident
