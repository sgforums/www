  • YiFengz
    9 May 06, 22:34
    ALS = Anti Lag System.

    This system can be enabled with the use of aftermarket ECU provided they have such an option.

    This involves rough idling as the engine has to keep the turbo spooling so that when throttle is applied, full boost is delivered and hence eliminate of turbo lag.

    Not recommended because ALS will kill your turbo, engine, and exhaust pretty quick. However wrc/race/track cars can afford to rebuild and change their turbo several stages.
