    12 Apr 06, 07:30
    If you're going for the cefiro, go for the V6 versions... 230jm (or 350jm if you can afford). The cefiro's V6 engines are award-winning, definitely leagues ahead of the camry's, just go test drive it and you'll know. You'll love the nice muted low pitch growl from the engine whenever you rev. Otherwise at low revs, the engine is practically silent. And if you compare the specs between the cefiro 230jm and the camry 2.4 you'll find that the cefiro has almost 20 more horses than the camry. But however the difference does not translate onto real-world straight line acceleration gains as the cefiro has a much heavier body than the camry.. which also probably explains the higher FC for the cefiro compared to the camry.

    So far my cefiro is doing around 7.2-7.8 km/L, but i hope it will improve as it has yet to reach the 1000km mark.

    The interior of the cefiro is much nicer than the camry's lor... get into one and you'll know. The furniture-quality wood veneer beats that shiny plastic 'wood' on the camry anytime. And frankly, the zen design of the cefiro's interior looks more pleasing to the eye than the plain design of the camry's. But as Fondue pointed out, the radio of the cefiro looks terribly out-of-place. But no worries... if you get the 350jm, you get the nice, integrated digital aircon and radio instead.

    As i've read in a car review before, the Cefiro was described as a 'true limousine with a sports car engine'. And i can't agree more.
