  • slacker001
    29 May 06, 21:20
    S40:
    Exterior: looks a odd initially, like a truncated version of S60 (its a Ford Focums/Mazda 3 platform, dimensions similiar). But looks grow on you.
    Interior: the rear's a little cramped, leg and headroom-wise. But the materials are quite decent (fascia's a little rough tho, compared to luxury makes). But well equipped and ergonomics is good. I like the floating console, classy. Seats worth mentioning: superb for long dist driving, little driver fatigue with good support. Pretty refined, except for wind noise from side mirrors.
    Engine: 2.4l NA (my dad's car) or 2.5l turbo (test drove too). Pretty strong mid range torque. Off beat 5 cylinder warble interesting. Needs a little determination to rev up, but once up and steaming you can bully a 320i out of your way (show-off deserved it). For city driving, 2.4l is sufficient.
    Handling: body movement well controlled. Predisposed to understeer thanks to hefty nose. But otherwise quite pleasing to drive (Focus' heavier sister). Not as deft as a BMW, but steady enough in corners, throttle controllable. Ride a little jarring over rough roads (esp lowered T5) but acceptable. Brake pedal spongy, modulate with care or scrub off too much speed (coz the brakes bite fiercely when stomped on).
    Overall: a good car for an affordable price. If you're not looking for too much space and drive longer distances, a great cruiser. But for more fun with less money, the Focus is good.
