    More research findings from Sweden:

    For drivers who tend to comply with speed limits in any case, ISA makes little difference. For ISA to be effective against the group of drivers most likely to flaunt speed limits, a mandatory limiting system is the key:

    "The results of this study suggests that the AAP has the effect of reducing the speeds of
    drivers who want to comply with the speed limit, but for drivers who deliberately exceed
    the speed limit the effect is much less, which suggests that a limiting system is necessary
    to make these drivers comply with the speed limit."

    (See:http://www.ectri.org/liens/yrs05/Session%201/hjamdahl.pdf)
