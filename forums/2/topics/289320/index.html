'Cos you must book Basic Theory and that roughly must wait 1 month,then if you pass you get your PDL ( Provisional Driving License ) that you need to start practicing driving with a qualified instructor. Then after you have passed your basic theory, you must book your Final Theory Test and after you pass that, then only can you book your practical test,in other words your TP test. Same as Basic Theory, you gotta wait around a month for your Final Theory Test. Then wait 5 months for your TP.

    There is no way you can get your licensoe in 3 weeks. Even if you know how to drive and you just wanna get 3 or 4 lessons with a private instructor, you must wait 5 months for your test.
