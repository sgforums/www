    The higher the octane number, the better the fuel's resistance to knocking (when fuel in the cylinder burns spontaneously, wasting fuel and causing damage to your engine).

    RON 92 is safe for older Japanese cars....minimum recommended number is RON 95 for newer cars. RON 98 is just a little more expensive.
