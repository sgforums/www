    Here are some tips:

    Optra Sedan
    - Heavier than Vios ,contributing to stability at high speed / cornering but slower acceleration
    - Consumes more fuel than Vios but compare well with other 1.6 sedans
    - Definitely more spacious than Vios
    - Quality of material a bit better than Vios . Take note that both are made in Thailand
    - Availability of parts not clear but seems cheaper. Eg. original fog lamp with installation is $150
    - Handle humps comfortably, no sweat
    - Latest version includes optra station wagon
    - Can bargain for freebies

    Vios
    - Lighter than Optra. You can feel it when cornering. Acceleration is a bit faster.
    - Brake more sensitive than Optra
    - Rear passenger space a bit cramp. At 1.69m, my head is about 4cm from the roof of the car. Squeezing 3 adults may be a problem.
    - Rear seat cannot be folded to increase storage space or to access the boot compartment
    - Availability of parts confirmed ok but you must get parts from parallel importers and not BM. Eg. original fog lamp with installation is $280 but parallel importer charged $145
    - Handle humps at ok level
    - The 2005 version includes a spoiler
    - Cannot bargain for freebies
