    You can't expand your fuel tank size. Your fuel tank was designed by the manufacturer with all the safety specifications and calculations in place. It is located in the most optimal space in your car there is, to prevent leaks in the event of an accident. It's not so easy to modify your original fuel tank (as there might not be enough space) and it will be highly dangerous to add on a piggyback tank (for example, mounted in the boot.)

    A better alternative to increasing your fuel carriage capacity would be the installation of a CNG tank, properly installed by a authorised installer. I hear that a full CNG tank can run about 200++km for some cars.
