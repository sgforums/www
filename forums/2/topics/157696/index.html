    Originally posted by pdriver:
    Hi everyone, just wana ask will the red light camera from opposite street snap at your car if you beat the red light?
    Red light camera will only shoot your car back number plate.. How the camera is activated is this way:

    At the junction of where there is a camera, there will be a pressure pad on the ground before the stop line. This pressure pad acts as a timer for both the traffic light and the camera.

    For the camera:

    When a car beats the red light, 3 secs after the car shoots past the pressure pad, the camera will shoot. 3 sec is because of the angle needed..

    For the Traffic light:

    The pressure pad will send a signal to the traffic light to have change of waiting time to be shorter. This is usually the case on heavily congested roads.
