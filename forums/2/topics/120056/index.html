    Ceramic Smart Films are unlike any other film in the industry. They provide superior aesthetics, performance and protection through their patented German ceramic technology that is unique only to Huper Optik. Ceramic window films have reset the standards in the window film industry in heat control, visibility, and fade control.

    Ceramic Advantages:

    -Ceramics utilize advanced nano-technology similar to that used on the NASA space shuttle to dissipate heat.
    -Ceramics, unlike metallic films, will not appear shiny and have very low reflectivity.
    -Ceramics, unlike dyed films, will not fade, turn purple or bubble while giving you maximum heat rejection.
    -Ceramics have lasting superior durability and are non-corrosive in sea air conditions.
    -Ceramics reflect the heat while allowing the visible light to enter permitting a clear view both day and night.
    -Ceramics, unlike metallic films, deter wavelength interference with GPS systems and cell phones.

    Ceramics Superior Aesthetics:

    -Made with 100% metal free materials. Ceramic films will not have a shiny metallic appearance.
    -Made with 100% dyed free materials. Ceramic films will not fade, bubble or turn purple.

    Huper Optik uses a uses a unique German technology that creates a ceramic coating, which can spectrally select the rays to transmit. Alternate layers of ceramic coatings are deposited onto a continuous reel of ultra clear film to form the super heat rejection Huper Optik polyester shield. The end result is a high performing product that has its claims to being the first and the premier in the advanced window film industry.

    Scratch Resistant Hard Coat

    -All Huper Optik Smart Films come with an acrylic based, pure hydrocarbon molecules hard coat. This protective top layer acts as a scratch resistant barrier to any minor abrasion through the normal care of the windows. All Huper Optik films are tested under ECE 43 abrasion test with the result of 1.3% haze measurement after test.
