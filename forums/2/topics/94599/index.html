    try 2 `ride' the clutch.

    from neutral to 1st, release the clutch slowly without pressing the accelerator. once u reach the `biting' point, the car will move slowly forward (make sure u're not on an upward slope). then realease the clutch fully and the car will maintain the forward movement without having to press the accelerator pedal.

    practice this untill u have a feel for when or where the `biting' point is.

    for normal driving...when u reach the biting point, press the accelerator while continuing to release the clutch. happy trying.
