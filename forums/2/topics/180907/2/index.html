    Finally, unable to resist the constant sms from my dealer that Coe is going to rise etc..
    Hi guys , dealers have all kind of reasons to tempt u to book a car. So u need to monitor the prices of your favourite model and strike when the prices is low.

    At the current 12k MAR COE, Sunny (A) is 52800 (after discount).
    I got my auto at 49000 in the last 12K COE. So new buyers are actually paying $3800 more than my car price at the same COE. The same goes for Toyota and Honda etc. The increase in car prices is never the same as the increase in COE's price.

    Having paid more, new buyers will have a better chance to secure a COE that is higher than the COE when they booked. Technically, you should get your car faster. Take note that the estimated time to get your car include the period after securing a COE...which is from 10 days to 30 days (depending on dealers).

    But it is true that salesman only secure sales. The COE bidding is left to the main dealer Tan Chong (as with other brands). If you want some trill, log on the COE online bidding website on Wednesday afternoon.
