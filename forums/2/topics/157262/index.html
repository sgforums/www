    Your car depreciates $3133 annually. You have to decide how much depreciation you are comfortable with. Even if you keep car until scrap, will a similar car by then be cheap enough to depreciate at this rate after subtracting its scrap rebate? Whatever you pay now will extend another 4yrs of driving. Next year, whatever you pay will extend driving for 5yrs and so forth... So every year the calculation changes as scrap value of your current car gets lower and you will pay more to extend driving for longer period.[/b]
