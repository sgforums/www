    Warranty wise, if you have free servicing by MI, then they will most probably not be sticky about an intake here, a strut bar there. To be honest your GD rex is not uncommon, there are many workshops, with some specially catering to imprezas, and your GD rex does not have a plenthora of electronics which may potentially go wrong as well, and yes, at external workshops you have the option of using your own higher spec-ed engine oil, albeit at a higher cost.

    For your intake, parts wise, the price is more or less the same depending on the markup, the discrepency is most likely due to the different charges for installation. Depending on the complexity of the installation, such as exhaust systems, decat, demuff etc, is still better to go to a better known workshop. For intake wise its more a matter of plug and play so smaller workshops should be able to install for you at cheaper labour rates.

    zerocool is right, yes the workshops mentioned above may charge higher rates, but at least their parts and labour is proven, and even if something does go wrong with their workmanship you can be assured they have a reputation to protect and will rectify, unlike unknown workshops that can open and close in ubi only to pop up at sin ming area, for example.

    For your GD chasis rex, you are in luck, there are lots of exhaust systems for it, such as fujitsubo and supersprint, HKS silent hipower and Apexi N1.
