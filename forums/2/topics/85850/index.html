SiR is also DOHC and puts out 170bhp. The confusion is that you can say the Esi evolved into the newer (1996 onwards) Vti which is also SOHC. The older Vti ('92 - '95) evolved into the SiR.

    forgot to add - as far as power is concern hard fight between older Vti (esp JDM version) and SiR. The older Vti is only about 10bhp less (non JDM version) than the SiR, but weighs less and more streamline.
