    welcome to the forums!

    the acura brand only exists in the states as far as i know. the RSX is known as the integra locally as it is in JDM.

    in sg, the integra comes in 2 specs. the base IS version pumping out 160 ps and the type-R (aka ITR) putting out 220 ps.

    the RSX type-S puts out 200 ps and it uses the same block as the IS and the ITR (albeit, in a different tune). the diff btw the ITR and the RSX-S is that the S comes without the factory recaros, LSD and brembo brakes as found on the ITR.

    hope that answers your question.
