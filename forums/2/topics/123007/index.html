    Answers to the frequent asked questions.

    1. Yes lower graded oil or higher graded oil can be used. However the use of undergraded oil may cause the performance of the car to loss its peak and result in more carbon build up that will be left after combustion has taken place.

    2. Yes difference company petrol can be mixed in one single tank. Even if 95 RON with 98 RON. Although it is not recommended as the reason being of what that is stated above.

    3. Pumping of diesel into a petrol tank , will result in the car totally un runable and cost will be incurred draining diesel out from the tank. To make it simple for understanding, Petrol running engine need a purer form of fuel for combustion.
