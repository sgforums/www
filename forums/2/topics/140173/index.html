    1969,
    I used to be an owner of the A190L which I traded in for the new Evo 8 MR.

    I used to use the A-Class on race tracks at Sepang and PG and even some carpark rally courses. Contrary to popular belief, the A-Class handles very well at corners.

    In terms of fuel consumption, it was awesome. The record was 680kms on one full tank of, I think, 52 or 54 litres!

    That was when my whole family of 5 with our luggage bags drove to KL then to Genting Highlands and back to Sin! Even the service engineer at C&C could not believe my claim.

    My family and I still miss the A190L!

    I saw the new A170. In terms of cabin space, it is shorter than the previous model becos the trunk has become bigger. Too bad.

    Are you hungry for power? If yes, why not wait for the A200 Turbo which will be launched at the end of this year? 193hp, 6-speeder!
