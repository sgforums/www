  • zorro82
    29 Apr 05, 21:35
    Good luck to your test

    - Check all blind spots

    - Do not do wide turning, turn early.

    - Take your time in circuit. No time limit except in parellel and vertical parking

    - S course, crank course, directional change, parking use half clutch is the best. Remember your safety check in your parking. Check blind spots.

    - On the road, drive confidently. How? Look below

    - If it is a all clear road, shift up your gears

    - Got car in front, slow down to gear 3 and anticipate

    - Can stop at gear 3. If got time and distance, better gear 2.

    - Look out for traffic light, if green light still on, apply the arrow sign. Once reach the third arrow, dun step accelerator, slow down if speed is fast. If near the line liao, speed up.

    - On the road, always check your front, rear mirror every 5 seconds. Even it is unneccessary, show to your tester that you always check. *Wayang a bit lah

    Most importantly, stay calm and awake. If tired, go SSDC buy redbull to drink, very effective. If you check all your blind spots, 70% likely to pass.

    Thats all, good luck. Hope to see your good result posting in this forum tmr. All the best
