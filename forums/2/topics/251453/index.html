    Registering a company is done via internet!
    Its as easy as registering a e-mail account.
    You will need to pay the registering fees via credit card online.
    You DON"T need to actually do any business for this company, you can report $1 profit each year for this company and no one will care.
    Just make sure you pay the yearly business licence renewal fees to the gov, about $5 per year!!

    You chose your company name "Ah-Kow Sell Cow"
    You chose the CEO name "Yourself"
    You chose the business address "Your HDB flat"
    You chose the business type "Chose anything you like, eg transport co"

    All its takes is 10mins.
    INSTANTLY you will get a business registration number.

    Print out this number and show it to your van/pickup saleman, you will be able to buy the van of your choice. Use the van like a car as you wish.

    ITS THAT EASY...even easier that bidding for you COE!
