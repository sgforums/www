    Depends which ABS you are asking,

    Albino Blacksheep.
    Anti-lock braking system
    Abdomen Muscles
    Association of Banks in Singapore

    OR Acrylonitrile, Butadiene and Styrene.

    ABS is a versatile polymer because it eliminates most of the disadvantages found in the more common plastics like PS, PC, PU, PE.

    Only disadvantage is ABS is considerably more expensive but prices have been falling but i guess its rising again.

    The factors you need to consider for a bumper,

    1) High impact resistant
    2) non-deteoration
    3) easily mouldability
    4) heat resistant
    5) strong

    however you should consider that the bumpers are just a shell, most cars will have a PS block under it to absorb impact when collided.
