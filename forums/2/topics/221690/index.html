    COE prices for most categories down in latest bidding exercise

    COE prices for all categories except big cars and the open category have gone down.

    The COE for cars below 1,600ccs (Category A) is $12,689, down by $290.

    But for big cars (Category B), COE prices rose by some $700 to $14,509.

    The latest COE prices for motorcycles have dropped by more than 40 percent, standing now at $702.

    COE prices for the goods vehicles and buses category went down by more than $2,000 to $1,003.

    For the Open Category, prices rose from $14,156 to $14,501.

    The Land Transport Authority received 8,265 bids at the end of the November.

    Of these, 6,228 were successful. - CNA/ch
