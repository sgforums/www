    ERP rates at 6 gantries to be revised from May 3

    SINGAPORE : The ERP rates for six gantries will be revised from Monday, May 3, following a quarterly review by the Land Transport Authority.

    The ERP gantry rate on the Central Expressway (CTE) north of Braddell Road will go down by 50 cents between 7.30am and 8am.

    The rates for four gantries — Fort Road, Kallang—Paya Lebar Expressway (KPE) slip road onto East Coast Parkway (ECP), Adam and Mount Pleasant along the Pan—Island Expressway (PIE) — will go up by between 50 cents and a dollar for the morning peak hours.

    And there will no ERP charges for the westbound gantry along the PIE before Eunos Link between 7am and 7.30am.

    The rates for the other gantries remain unchanged.
