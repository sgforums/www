    You already say your not financially stable. So why are you not smart enough to figure out that by owning a new car in singapore you are more likely to get yourself into alot of debts.
    IMHO, you want to start out by owning a car i suggest you forget taking a walk along Leng Kee or Ubi becos you'll end up paying your loans over 10 years. Start out by getting those Toyota Starlets 91 -93 models. They are cheap, easy to maintain and FC is very good at 13km/l if i'm not wrong. But get a mechanic to check out the cars before you get one just to make sure they're still in reasonable condition.
    Bottomline, try the 2nd hand car market or the COE cars.
