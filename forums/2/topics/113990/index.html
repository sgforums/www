    Everyone is unskilled at the beginning. The question is "do you want to drive manual or auto if skill is not a issue?" Just remember the pain of starting off from a stationary position on the slope, car engine stopping when you didn't down gear in time or clutch in too fast, or the occasional peak JAMs. So willing to expereince all that for that little amount of fuel?

    Strictly speaking, if FC is a concern, then you shouldn't go for Chevrolet in the first place. So you decide. Auto Vios can easily win Chevrolet manual anytime, especially since you are unskill.

    Anyway, I am also an unskilled driver. But once you get used to it, things becomes natural. Of course Auto is also easier to sell off.
