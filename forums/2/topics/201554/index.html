    Got a chance to drive an Evo 9 GT briefly yesterday.

    All I can say is: what a waste to own an Evo on the Singapore roads! Before I can fully grasp the feel, I have already hit the next traffic light! (meebee in 3 seconds or less?) The Evo had really taught me the true definition of the term POWER.

    On the second gear, there was some kind of lag for a split second. But when the turbo kicks in on the 2nd gear, I went "Whoooooaa!". But the best is yet to be. After moving onto the 3rd gear, there was absolutely no turbo lag, and on throttle I went like "WAHLAN EH!!!! My sh-it coming out liao!!!"

    Hahaha ok la, frankly I haven't had many chances to drive performance cars, so this may just be sua-koo on my part. But really, the drive is so exhilerating that I almost pee on my pants. I thought I was driving a supersonic jet!

    I just gotta post this - cos the ride of the car just keep recurring in my mind! No wonder people are so crazy about Evo. I heard that the stock Evo 9 GT is 320bhp. Is that true?

    Just a question: How does the WRX Sti matches up to the Evo? I am talking about the euro spec Sti.
