    Originally posted by mini-sized:
    Nice pict, if plus fog light would be better....start saving now...
    I think fog lights, rear spoiler, body kits... all will be great if they arrive... however, I do not want to spend anymore, for now...

    So far... the three major things I have done to my car are Ultrashield, CoolnLite and changing of rims/tyres.

    Ultrashield being the most satisfying... I can bring my car out any time to show you guys... it's sparkling shining... all with just rinsing and very light shampooing if there are oil stains....

    As for solar film... cos I did 25% on all four windows... the heat has been significantly reduced; gone are the days when I say, "hot" on a sunny day. The rear allows 50% light and the windscreen allows 30% light... Everything seems legal except the two front windows.

    The 14"inch sports rim have completely change the cute Picanto to something more than 'it' (refer back to the pictures if you need more convincing). As for the tyres... what more can I say... they are great... I drive with more confident (call it mental if you want to) on wet road and I do feel the car's grip has improved. Only downside... I am losing about 1km per litre since the change (for city driving).

    That's all for now...
    Vincent
