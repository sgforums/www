    1) Self manage and categorise discussions.
    That is to say, we shall have up to maximum 15 folders ....... a folder only used for announcement, a folder strictly for meetup location, date and time, a folder for good place for makan, a folder for jokes, a folder for cheap and good parts, a folder to complain stupid driver and................you name it!

    2) More piracies and authorisation (Security).
    We can create one or two folders or even levels strictly for Key or Confirmed members. Some sensitive issues can be discussed as long as they are not related to religious issues, sexual, violence and etc. Once a New member exceed maybe say posting more than 1000 messages, we can upgrade them to Confirmed member.

    3) Own chat rooms
    It is a Live chat room.

    4) Create polling


    5) Link up with more car clubs in Singapore (join activities).
    Delphiforums has already becomes a centralise forums that most Singaporean car owners/ drivers where they like to hang around to discuss and exchange ideas.

    So as to say, we shall be migrating from SGforums to our own one whenever it is ready to operate. And on top of that, whatever yanghwee as done for us shall be the homepage of kelisa club in Singapore and it will be cross linked back to our future forum in delphi and emailing address (kelisasgclub@yahoo.com.sg), and further extend our link to Malaysia, the K2OC (Kenari 'N' Kelisa Owners Club).

    By having our own club name, logo, slogan, flyers and linkage, we hope to achieve or rather attract more Kelisa owners to join us and benefits everyone here. With that, thanks for your kindness and patience of reading this message.
