    I have been bullied before, but by a 10 tonne tipper, who was trying to get to the left lane as he need to turn off up ahead. That is not unusual.

    The lady drivers were of the late 20 or 30 age group. Alone in their cars.
    I was begining to profile them as frustrated, yuppies who can't quite afford a sedan. But then it might just be co-incidents.
