    Hey dudes,

    I finally did it!!! I procured the Evo VIII. I sourced and scanned the newspapers, took endless trips up and down AML, visited Sin Ming... finally... after the depressing CPF cuts - I finally got the guts to place my order for a yellow Evo. I must say that it was scary, my first car and everything. I sorted everyone's views... from my MD to my colleague sitting to my right. Conclusion was to get the Evo while you are young.

    I must say that I think I got a reasonably good deal. Titanium turbo for S$148k immed delivery with sunroof. Now I just have to find a good service centre.

    Somethings that I found out: GSR has a intercooler and the titanium turbo model - the latter has a better turbo system compared to the former. Models manufactured betw end-Jan to mid-July had a recall to check for a transmission issue involving the 6th gear. Do not change the rims - they were specially made for the Evo.

    Thats all I can remember - hope this helps anyone who is looking to get an Evo.

STi more raw and its more fun on the track where you can afford to whip your tail out (oversteer). Such driving not advisable on public roads. Net-net, EVO VIII more intelligent car and nicer car to drive. STi - raw and more unpredictable.
