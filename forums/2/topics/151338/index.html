    I owned one for almost 3 yrs and sold it off just when it hit 60,000 km
    Very nice car to drive, very powerful, smooth revving engine, very good FC.
    Feels very safe with so many airbags
    Downside is the French technology on the fuzzy logic gearbox. When new, the gearbox is very intelligent - can rememberyour driving pattern, going round bend, downslope, etc, it can tell and shifts accordingly. that is when everything is fine and IF everything runs normally
    BUT I feel theres too many sensors that gives way, and when things go wrong, it jerks when taking off, occassionally, when changing driving style, it gets confused and hesitates in changing up or down,

    So I sold it when the warranty expired. Didnt want to risk paying for the very expensive gearbox which only EXclusive Auto has the parts.

    Great car when new
