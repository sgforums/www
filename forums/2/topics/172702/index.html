    To turbo-charge it isn't that cheap/easy....It's a loop....not just fitting on a turbo

    After fixing on a turbo u'll need a intercooler, bigger injectors, ECU and fuel pump....later realised exhaust not pushing out the power from the engine...exhaust system....

    Soon the handling won't be up to par, hence coilovers + strut bars etc.....

    After that, u'll need to forged low comp pistons to sustain the level of boost...

    That's before factoring in the loss of a reliable engine though output power is enhanced and not to forget.... the fear of White Ants....

    Anyway, a vios isn't that under-powered anyway. If u're looking at performance, I think other cars would fit the bill better
