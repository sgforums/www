    No worry..

    Yes, if you own the car, you have to pay an insurance premium yearly, and a road tax (6 monthly or yearly, depending on your budget). Your insurance and road tax starts from the day your car gets registered at LTA.

    Right, Let's say excess is 1.5k. You kena accident (touchwood) and repair cost is 2k. You have to come up with the first 1.5k and the rest covered by insurance company.

    For your family car, you have to check with your dad. They usually cover the main driver, 2nd driver and "all the drivers authorized by the owner of the car". But there is a catch here:

    "Additional Excess of $1000 - $3000 applies to young and inexperienced drivers: i.e. 26 years old and below, driving experience of less than 2 or 3 years"

    Means if you are the one driving the car at the time of accident. the excess will be

    $1500 + $3000 = $4,500

    If your repair cost is only $2k, means you cannot claim insurance..
