    Friday, 4th December 2009, 2.45pm - 3.30pm, CDC

    Score: -8

    I put my concentration on the test, because I wanted to pass and get a license quickly.

    I was nervous, so I tried redbull+7up mix. Damn high lah. LOL. It is common for people to be nervous in the waiting room. But after the tester called me, i relaxed a lot and drove him around following his instructions. He wasn't fierce, but he instructed me to drive route 6 (the one you need to cut lanes from bus lane to last lane along Jln EUnos and make u-turn). I lost 4 points to the u-turn. LOL.

    I passed, very happy and excited. Went home, put my P-plate inside the car, lazy to drive, go and sleep. LOL.

    1st time driving out after I passed, I wasn't quite used to it. I brought my brother out for joyriding, as well as to look after my car when i get something. Haha! Driving auto car is as easy as driving a go-cart.

    Comparing manual vehicles, I prefer driving a van to a car, even though there's more blind spots. There's a slight difference.

    Don't be nervous when the tester is beside you! :P
