    U go in and approach the person-in-charge.

    They will have different packages based on type of car and period of rental you want.

    Usually for 1 day, they will recommend cars like Vios, Lancer etc.

    U fill in application form, he will explain your excess amount (amount u need to pay in the event of accident) based on your driving experience and sometimes, age.

    Once u fully understood the terms and conditions, he will bring u to the car.

    He will take down any scratches/dents on the car so that nextÂ round of checking upon return, he will not find fault on you. He will also take down the fuel amount so that next time u top up to the same amount or more. Preferably, keep the car clean before returining.

    After all these are completed, he will pass u the keys and u are ready to drive off.
