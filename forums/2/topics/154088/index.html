    can't see your pic but maybe u can try to DIY?

    assuming that your car's panels are removable, remove the panel either by screws or most likely plastic pop rivets... (just use a flathead to 'pry' it out)

    get it cleaned with methanol or a solvent, then buy a can of auto paint (~$13?) and then spray the panel in your backyard or something..

    when it dries just pop it back to place... may not be the best job in the world..

    or you can use touchup marker paints to cover the area... if not a small paintbrush, but there'll most likely be a colour tone difference..

    hope this helps... if not for your current problem then for future (touchwood) 'accidents'...
