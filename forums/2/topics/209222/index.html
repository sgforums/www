    Unless you can afford the hefty insurance premium, you wont be able to afford the expensive premium, dont forget you must renew your insurance every year...

    I am 26 this year, and I have driven 9 cars from the day I passed my TP, my insurance has been ranging at least $3000. I owned Lancer, Civic VTi, BMW coupe, MR-S, Rav4 2-dr, Silvia S14, RX-8, Renault Cabrio-coup and currently, Mini Cooper S. So hope this will give you a rough guide...

    From the cars you want to buy, it's going to be expensive. Trust me, somemore thanks to the recent accidents, premium will surely go up again. And based on your age, you falls into the youngest age group which will be the most expensive as well...

    Hope this helps.
