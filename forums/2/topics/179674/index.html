    it is an offence to drive without a license. Even u forgot to bring it, it is also considered as "without".

    u need your "xing shi zheng" also, which is no matter who's driving, this pass should be matched with the car. A valid driving license allow u to drive a car on the road. A valid "xing shi zheng" allows a speicfic car (every vehicle will be issued one pass after the car is sold) to be driven on the road. If u dun have one of this 2 when a TP ask for it, he can charge u.

    in singapore, the law is strict, but the officers are reasonable.

    in China, the officers love to catch drivers, cos if they hit thier quota, they get extra bonus at the end of the yr.

    in Cambodia, under most situations, a USD 5 dollar note is a one time driving licence.
