    For normal 1.6 cars

    4th gear @ 3k rpm is normally around 80km/h

    5th gear @ 3k rpm is around 100km/h

    For 2.0 and above

    4th gear @ 3k is around 90 and above

    5th gear @ 3k is around 110 and above

    But this really depends on the horsepower and throttle level.

    As for run-in during the first 1K before servicing.. try to maintain around 2.5k to 3 k rpm for all the gears not juzz only to smooth run the engine, its oso to save your life. Coz during the first servicing, they will oso help you to tighten some of the screws so its better to go slow first if u dun really wanna see your trye running faster than your car. A new engine is juzz like you juzz started to light your ciggie, need to inhale a bit first before u continure for a deep in hale to smoke.

    After your first servicing, den its time to slowly build up the rpm, go to 3.5 den 4 den 4.5 and so on. It is oso to let your engine get accustom to the power lvl .Dun go for a full blow immediately after your first servicing if u wanna have a longer engine life. There are some idiots i kno who juzz ramp all the way and less den 1 yr problems comes out for eg. How come the FC like increase. Engine like dying for more power etc etc...

    So far i have tried on my lancer and its all ok.. Highest reach is 180km/h on 5th gear @ 5.5k rpm and its working fine. Take your time to build up the cars stamina.. dun b too hasty about it. I waited till 10K service den go for the 180. after the first servicing, i only go to 120km/h.

    Have fun running in the engine of your car
