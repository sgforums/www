    If u really compare up between 2nd hand rx7 and mazda 6, first thing.. think of the mantainance follow by the insurance and condition.
    Mantainance wise.. getting a 2.0 mazda 6 brand new with 3 yrs warrenty labour free. RX7...2nd hand, condtion depends, any side effects or problem oso hard to say and some more rotary engine is really hard to find good workshops unless u r willing to spend big bucks back at mazda agents if anything goes wrong and for the spare parts, dun forget old models some times they may phase out some spare parts and that will be the starting of your headache. Insurance wise depends on how old u r and whether u got any NCD to discount it. But if i not wrong, rx7 is slightly higher den M6.

    Yes, rx 7 may look much more cool and stylo but dun forget all these may end up with heavy price. And for rx 7... 3 yrs or so u r gona renew the coe...thats gonna cost u another bomb. if your budget is around 75k n u r going to spread it over 5-7 yrs installment.. go for the more economical type which may save your wallet from becoming flat fast.

    But even if you r looking @ M6... dun expect those nice body kits that u see out side on other M6.. its gonna cost u additional or maybe if the agent is nice may throw in sport rims or a normal spoiler for u.

    Unless you r really rich and dun care about the cost for mantainance and upkeep... go for the rx7 if not... get a M6 or other model which is not so bad.

    Ford Focus ... toyota altis full kit .. or maybe a celica may fit more on your budget.
