    The basic requirement for loan approval is an income statement from your company you are working.

    If you don't have income statement, you need a guarantor....The guarantor must have an income statement irregardless whether she/he has a licence or not.

    If you are working and you can afford the instalments, then you don't need a guarantor.

    Preferably take no more than 70% loan for no longer than 7 years.
