    $68,000 only


    The Copen is a combination of a roadster and the economy kei car size. It features a 16 valve, 659 cc engine with 4 cylinders and is turbocharged to provide strong response from as low as 2,000 rpm.

    The Copen comes equipped with an electronically operated aluminium hard-top that can convert the car from hard-top to open-top with a single push of a button. With the top down, all-round visibility is excellent and parking is easy, with visual reference points at every corner.

    The Copen weighs only 840kg, and offers excellent fuel economy. It is claimed to achieve 17km/l. Though a small and minimalist car, the Copen comes with standard ABS and dual front airbags.
