    For those who need to know before you speed, here's the run down on the standard of fines here in Singapore.

    40km/h below set speed limit - Summon ranging S$100 to S$500
    40km/h above set speed limit - YOU GO TO COURT 21!!!
    Double the set speed limit - YOU GO TO COURT 21 and Guarantee 4 to 6 months Suspension plus fines of S$900 to S$1500.

    I was doing 127 over set limit of 80. My fine was S$800 payable on the spot! Standard was about S$900 for most of the 80 of so people that was haul up today at court 21.

    So before you speed, check your wallet if you have enough to pay up and get haul up to court 21.
