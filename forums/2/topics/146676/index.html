    I was at the Leng Kee showroom the last weekend and I must say the 407 looks nice. Apart from the sporty styling, the cabin had adequate room to fit 5 for a longer journey. It has also a 5 star EuroNCAP rating which is excellent and also to highlight the ESP which has ASR traction control, VSC, EBA and ABS included. It was a pity that the boot was very small compared to other cars in that category.

    I believe residuals for Peugeot in general are not favourable although the salesperson was trying real hard to convince me otherwise. I was also told that there might be a possibility that the Ubi Centre might shut down and move its operations to Leng Kee instead to better serve customers. However, that would be a real problem to people living in the east like myself if I were to have a Peugeot sent for servicing.
