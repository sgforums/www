    Originally posted by joeacid:

    1)Is there a private instructor for motorcycle like car?

    No.

    2)Do i still have to apply for a PDL?

    Yes, after you pass RTT and also Lesson 5 internal assessment ie. circuit with bike.

    3)Do i still have to take the BTT?

    Only need RTT. No BTT whatsoever.

    4)I have to take the RTT right? is it difficult compared to FTT?

    Slightly more difficult. You have to pass internal RTT first before they even allow you to take the REAL RTT. Chicken feed if you have Class 3.

    5)Can i take a RTT privately? that means dun hav to go thru evaluation 1st?(School) just like what i did for my FTT?

    No. But revisions and lessons can take online for SSDC.

    6)How many sessions estimated do I have to attend before i can take the Riding Test? (Both Circuit and Road)

    14 to 15 if you are good. Else be prepared to got for at least 30.
