    U have to 1st be involved in some form of motorsports. not competitively.... but lets say u have to have a machine u can race and get some decent practice in it.
    Then u go to SMSA clubhouse cum office which is at lock road.
    The address is:
    Singapore Motor Sports Association, Blk 9 Lock Rd #03-01 S(108937)

    They will give u some forms to fill out, and a medical card. 1stly u need to be a member of a motorsports organisation in SG, so i would recomend u join SMSA as a member.
    Next... the med card. U need to get a doctor to fill out your card, which means a medical checkup. A GP is fine. u also have to provide details of any road accidents u've been involved in as well as any old injuries. Whether u r fit for motorsports is up to the discretion of your doctor. When u go n collect your racing licence, u have to bring the medical card along and 2 passport sized photos(or was it 4? bring 4 to be safe).
    What grade of competition licence u get depends on what the competition commitee thinks u r capable of. I got a International Senior 'C' n my 1st application. but then, b4 that i had been practicing for over a year and some of the commitee members have seen me during practice so it helps.
    Thats all there is to getting one. Gd luck!
