    I tend to agree with Yo2020 that you will be OK.

    There are usually slight delay in the detection circuit and the turning of the red light.

    Most of the time, the delay is about 1 sec or less and not 2 sec. ie when you cross the white line and the light just turn red, then you are OK. It is only when the light turn red for about 1 sec or more, then you cross the line, then the camera will activate.

    The timer delay if any is meant to sort out those who intentionally beat the red light from those just pass it when it turns red. Of course sometime the timer not working, and the camera will then work without delay.

    Anyway, do drive carefully, within speed limit, and obey the traffic light signal and you will be allright. Slow down and be extra alert when near traffic junction.

    From my experience, if following 3 conditions are met, then you will get summon for beating the red lights.

    (1) Cross the stop-line after 0.2 sec , or more, when light turns red.
