    I presume you're learning under school?

    If so, you must attend the 4 theory lessons, it's compulsory and you must pass final evaluation for both theory and practical..

    The minimum time to complete the whole course before you can book practical test is about 2 months, assuming you attend practical lessons almost everyday and you're quite a fast learner, and your theory everything must be cleared within 1 month. It also depends alot on the availability of slots...

    Lastly, the waiting period for the prac test is about 1 month, subjective to individual school's schedule... You can check out the schedule on each driving centre's website.

    If you're asking for private learners, I ain't so sure anymore.

    There is no short cut at schools, everything is systematic and structured.
