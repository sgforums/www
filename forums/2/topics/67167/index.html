    Hi,

    Haha, I was bo liao so went down wif a colleague to Mazda in MacPherson just now, and tested drive the Mazda 3.

    Okie, admit this is the 1st time I'm seriously impressed with the Mazda. I like the HATCH BACK MAZDA 3 with full kit. Very very chio.

    The car is new so the engine is very smooth and quiet. Pick up also not too bad, and got the 'manual' shift gear to play with. Played with it as well, and the mid-range surge is rather good for a 1.6l. Rather suprise.

    Overall designs and interior will get 5 stars out of 5 from me. And performance 4 star (Not too sure if it gets older). The only draw back is that the Hatch Back Mazda 3 Sports edition cost 85,888! That's a lot of money for a Mazda 1.6l!!!

    The saloon is nice as well but not as nice as the hatch back. The basic model for the Mazda 3 saloon is 77,888..... pretty okie, but there's only one color to choose from! Sad............... Heard from the sales man the saloon is selling better than the hatch back cos of the price.

    Ermmmm overall the car is nice, esp the HATCHBACK SPORT! But for that price, most probably won't buy it, as I rather drive my current 01 Civic. In fact, a 1.6 Civic now only cost ard 80k flat.

    Sometime, its the brand that counts.
