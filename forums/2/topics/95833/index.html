    Module 1 means still in circuit right?

    Having problems at the crank and s courses?

    For crank course,judge with the bottom of the side mirror.Once the entire bottom of the side mirror passes the curb edge of the turning corner,you can turn.Of course,your speed should be slow,preferably 2nd gear throughout.

    For S course,judge by aligning the centre of the bonnet and the tip of the curve edges.Adjust your steering wheels accordingly.And go slow but steady.

    Practice makes Perfect.Good Luck!
