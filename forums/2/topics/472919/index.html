    From www.cdc.com.sg/faqs.htm

    How many lessons do I have to attend before I can take the practical test?
    On average, a learner will need an average of 24 - 27 lessons before they can take the practical test. The final number of lessons required will be advised by the instructors.

     

    That means either your instructor is taking his own sweet time to teach you slowly  or he see that you are a slow learner who is just another "cannot make it" learner-driver.

    Change instructor, and write to info@cdc.com.sg (cdc.com.sg/feedback.htm) to complain.
