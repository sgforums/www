    Originally posted by bcdggk:

    So comfortable, so quiet, so smooth, first 2 seconds pick-up maybe a bit slow, but ok after 3 seconds (I'm not chasing a police car, so it's ok with me). Audio is supurply enjoyable (this is my 1st "expensive" car, so unable to compare other car's audio). I purposely drive a few rounds near my home (even I already reach home) just to enjoy the audio ... well, just own it for a few days mah.

    Look is BEAUTIFUL, my wife love the color she chose. Only thing not use to it, I can't see the front part of the car from the driver seat, thus I cannot estimate parking head-in (I cannot tell whether side of the car touches the pillar or not). I used to drive a cheaper car and I always park head-forward because I can see the front part of the car from the driver seat. Any other Latio-Owner has this problem???

    Suspension is Wow, wow, and wow: Never feel a bump over many humps, speed-regulatory-strips, I told my wife this car is best for pregnant ladies.

    Keyless, so convenience, never have to put hand in pocket to take out key and aim at the door hole/ignition anymore.

    Air-con, full blast feature (TC called it "AUTO" button) if parked under hot sun and it gets colddddd in seconds, wow , wow, wow.

    Alcantara/leather seats - so comfy; except neck no rest (unlike sunny - I like the neck rest in sunny when test-drive).

    Spacious front and back (rear passenger seats): it's a well-known fact!

    There are more wonderful things to say, will add next time ...

    I got my new Latio on Tues. There are certain things that really impressed me.

    1) keyless entry is something new. Convenient needless to say. I don't have to even remove the key from my pocket throughout the entire day

    2) The newly improved CVT engine is fuel economical and very smooth and quiet. You can even feel the engine braking when you are coming to an halt. Commendable for an auto car

    3) Spacious in the inside, by making use of a more compact engine compartment

    4) However, it's still considered a chep car, hence dun be surprised to see cheap plastics and ugly lights in the interior. The reading lights look like some modificaion bought from Autobac lor

    5) On the whole, it's value for $$, relatively better performance as compared to sunny and a smoother ride. (in comparison with a jan 2003 sunny)
