    Lancer. Spacious, sporty look. Made in Japan, but doesn't mean it has not quality issue. Slightly more responsive SEs. Power isn't that bad, should be far from Vios. Handling quite good due to its steering and long wheel base. If FC for Vios is normal, FC for lancer would be normal, about 12-13kpl for manual.

Of course if you are lazy by natural, I have nothing to say. Vios auto is quite decent too, power still among the better ones around.
