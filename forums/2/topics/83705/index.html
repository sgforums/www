    some pros and cons of the city(iDSL manual):

    pros:

    fc
    : very good fc - average 14.8 km per l at 60 percent highway

    space
    : very good space - fold flat seats means a 1.7 m guy can lie
    fully straightened(even 2 persons side by side)
    dual glove box

    gear level
    : manual gearbox short throw and very smooth

    cornering
    : cornering is good with little bodyroll

    steering
    : accurate, fast and weighted at high speeds, good lock-to-lock

    boot
    : 500 litre boot

    engine
    : very quiet when idle, almost vibrationless at standstill (best in
    class), very willing to rev, overall a refined engine (for iDSL)

    pick up
    : good pick up as long as you are willing to rev at least 3000 rpm

    cons:

    looks
    : futuristic tadpole morphing into frog look. can be remedied using
    bigger wheels, rear spoiler and side rain visor. (IMHO, body kit
    makes city look fatter especially side skirt)

    vanity mirror
    : on front driver side

    ride
    : on the hard side

    no clock
    : only when cd player is playing you can select display to show the
    clock

    power
    : initial power upslope a little lacking especially in second gear
