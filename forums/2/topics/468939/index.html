    SINGAPORE: Revised ERP rates for certain roads and expressways will kick in on 6 May.

    For BKE, the ERP rates will go up by 50 cents and S$1 for different periods in the morning.

    From 7:30am to 9am, the ERP rate will be S$2; and from 9am to 9:30am, it will be S$1.

    For the four gantries at the Southbound CTE after Braddell Road and PIE Slip Road into Southbound CTE, the ERP rate will be S$5 from 8:30am to 9am.

    From 9am to 9:30am, the ERP rate will be S$2.

    Along the Nicoll Highway, there will not be any ERP charge from 7:30am to 8am.

    From 8am to 8:30am, motorists will pay S$2, down 50 cents from the current ERP rate.

    The Land Transport Authority said the rates for other gantries will remain unchanged.
