    Not neccesarily true. The price gap has closed considerably, and no, the specs of the car is not the same. Do take note that PI can give you lower price not because they cut into their profit margin, but because of the tricks they have to lower the OMV of the car, hence less tax = lower paper value for you, the owner.

    Things like solar flim, sound system, leather seats, etc are all installed locally. Quality wise, unless you're getting from high end PIs such as Richburg who specialises in Toyota, quality wise will not be on par with the real MIJ - Factoy Fitted deal that KM ships in directly. PI cars are shipped in from Japan are all 'bare minumum' to lower OMV as low as possible. Sure when you buy from KM the price is a bit higher, but if there are problems/product recalls, at least you know you're safely covered.

    Then again, its your money and hence your choice, but I do agree that KM sales have very rigid price packages, then again most of the things you'll ask for (floor mat, solar, leather) are already included in the price, not like other places where the base price is really bare minimum and you have to work your way up through expensive options.
