    5. Lotus 340R

    The 340R is a special edition of the Lotus Elise. Just 340 models were built, and all were sold before they were manufactured. It uses a custom built bodyshell with no roof and no doors. All 340 cars came with a silver and black colour scheme. Special A038R tyres were developed for the 340R in collaboration with Yokohama. (from wiki) Last saw it in the Lotus showroom garage. cant find my pic, so got it from else whr way back then.

    6. Fab Design AM1000
    V12 engine taken out from SL600, and modified to pump out 745bhp and 1000 Nm of torque (hence it's name AM1000). Added Gull Wing doors with hydraulic pumps and 20inch Fab Design wheels. 60 were manufactured. 0-60mp/h: 3.8secs.

    The AM800 Yellow colour E-class mercedes owned by the yellow zonda owner is known to have 630 BHP. An E55 AMG engine with 476 BHP modified to pump out that about of power...

    7. Mercedes Benz CLK GTR Roadster

    A total of five CLK GTR Roadsters were built by the company. And this one i have here is one of the 5 built, and it's the only RHD available in the world (it's in SG). yes i admit, the interior is ugly.

    8. Mercedes Benz 300SL

    Yes, this is the very famous classic model by Mercedes with the Wing doors. And no, the heart of this car isn't wat it was used to be. Sry but i totally forgot wat they do to this car, i will just state down wat i can recall. The swap the engine for an AMG engine, not sure particularly which one. AMG rims added. Tuned to performed like any other supercars out there, and it has CD player installed if i rmb correctly, it's selling for SGD 3m.

    9. Pontiac GTO

    Nothing much, all can be found on the net, but a Pontiac is SG? That's rare. It's the 06 Model, whether AM has done any tuning to it,  wasn't sure. But it exists in sg, that's for sure.

    10. 40th Anniversary Lamborghini Murcielago

    In late 2003, Lamborghini announced a special 40th Anniversary Edition of the Murciélago, following in the footsteps of their Countach and Diablo Anniversary Editions. The limited production run of 50 vehicles featured enhancements including a limited-edition body color, carbon-fiber exterior detailing, upgraded wheels , a new revamped exhaust system, and a 40th Anniversary numbered plaque displayed along the side. The interior featured a new leather trim and additional electronic features. (from wiki) It's situated in Lamborghini Singapore Showroom, wrapped up in plastic, only displayed every now and then.
    Pics credit to DcR

    You must be thinking... A Koenig? Yes it exists in sg. Not very well known, but it is situated in some owner's garage. He was friendly enough to entertain me. And there it is, a yellow Koenig F48. Rare shit... No pics, sry... Camera phone wasn't a common gadget during that time.

Looks like the Ferrari F40 from a side view. Highly tuned engine - goes way beyond 200mph packs 550bhp.  $300,000US+ new, probably more now. A collector's car.
