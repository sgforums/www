    For more family fun, u can go to the "Animal Resort" at jalan kayu there. Just turn in at the road where there's an old folk's home and follow the signs. For a small donation(a few dollars, its up to you) to maintain the resort, you can get some small food to feed the animals there. Horses, goats, chickens, AFRO-Head chickens, geese, fishes, flamingoes, emus, guinea pigs, rabbits. They sell puppies there also. Sometimes they have dog obedience classes there. Its quite a lot of fun.

    Note: no entrance feees. If you wanna feed the animals, just make a donation at the feed-counter and take whatever you want to feed them. Anyway, I am not working there. Just got to know it from my friend.
