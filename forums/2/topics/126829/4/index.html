    For Food:-
    Jalan Kayu - not for prata, but for barbecued stingray

    Katong - Laksa (The one nearer to katong shoppg ctr, @ a corner coffee shop, best amongst its competitors in that vicinity)

    Sin Ming Rd - bak kut tea(Rong City)

    Casuarina Rd - yong tau foo (but abit ex)

    Joo Chiat - wanton mee (Fei Fei)

    Geylang Lor 29??? - Hokkein Mee (U wldnt want to eat hokkien mee from elsewhr agn once u hv tried this stall)

    Hollang Rd - XO Fish soup (Great n at a reasonable price)

    Geylang Lor 3- Frog Legs Congee & other ala carte dishes

    Serangoon Circle
    - NY cheese cake (Brown Box)
    - cafe cartel (void of the claustrophobic feel of other outlets)

    AMK Blk 632 - I think they serve the best prata, beat jalan kayu n casuarina handsdown.

    Carlton Hotel - Wah Lok Restaurant for tim sum thou its abit ex, but worth it. Great ambience n great food. (6 stars rating)

    SAF yatchclub (Changi) - Serves both chinese n western delights. Great ambience, but food is not as great. Dinner n drinking by the beach, can see planes flying past. Parking is ex but u already paid tat much for the food, wat's the few more dollar.

    Lavender foodcourt @ eminent plaza - the most assorted array of local hawker food all under one roof. Can't find elsewhr in sg. The wanton mee is alwys long Q, but i perosnally think its jus passable.

    For shopping :-
    Harbour Front Shopping Ctr - void of the claustrophobic crowd, while most of the 'popular shops and brands' are within one building.

    Turf City - void of the claustrophobic crowd. Free parking all day long.

    Sembawang Shoppg Ctr - void of the claustrophobic crowd.

    'Exotic' places:-
    - Sembawang Park - can see JB across
    - Yishun Dam - getting more n more crowded thou
    - Labordor Park
    - Kent Ridge Park
    - Pasir Ris Park- Fisherman Village (for the ambience)
    - Punggol End
    - Mount Faber Hill
    - Telok Blangah Hill Park
    - Cosy Bay (Parking of $5 is redeemable by spending $15 or more at the restaurant. Ambience is great, but i think the food is below avg.)
    - Upper Pierce Reserviour (Can see monkeys. Closed at 7pm thou)
    - Sungei Buloh - Nature Reserve
    - Sungei Tenggah - Farmart
