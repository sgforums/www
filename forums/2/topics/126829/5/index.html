    Scenary:

    1) Mt Faber (theres a bistro on the top of the hill close around MN, Nice view of Tajong pagar and PSA)
    2) Labrador Park ( for peacefull and nice view of the sea)
    3) West Coast Park Macdonald (Nice place to chill out at the MAC)
    4) East Coast Park Hawker center (Food to eat a place for gatherings)
    5) Changi Beach/ Changi Village ( see ah gua+food+sea breeze)
    6) Punggol End / Track 17 (If you r looking for a quiet place)
    7) Kent Ridge Park/Car park (see PSA areas+peep at couples..lolz)
    Jurong Kecil Hill ( Peep @ couples)
    9 Yishun Dam ( Night illegal fishing and catch couples in action)
    10) Sembawang Park (Quiet spot+some sea breeze+ mosquitoes)

    Food for thought:

    1) Lau pat sa
    2) Geylang Lor 7 (for beef hor fan & frog porridge)
    3) Geylang Lor 27 (dim sum Hoe Kee)
    4) Macpherson Kopitiam ( large variety )
    5) Lavenda Food court ( large variety )
    6) Y C K ( prata )
    7) Upper Thomson- Long Beach ( 1 of the best big prawn noodle in sg )
    Baliestier ( Bak ku Teh )
    9) Behind bugis/sunshine plaza ( Nice fried wanton mee + duck rice + alot more)
    10) Nee soon / Chong Pang ( large variety )
    11) Jalan kayu ( Prata area )
    12) Serangoon Garden / Chomp Chomp (large variety but remember to put coupon..uncle come very fast 1 )
    13) Marina South (BBQ steamboat,big enough for gatherings )
    14) Upper Serangoon Rd/ Hougang (Punggol nasi lemak + selegie bean curd+ curry rice etc etc )
    15) Newton circle
    16) Beside Tang dynasty ( lots of fast food resturant to chill out )
    17) Bedok Blk 28 ( large variety )
    20) Bedok village ( large variety but go during day time )
    21) Beach Rd / Near shaw Tower ( quite alot of buffet steamboat )
    22) Upper East Coast Rd
    23) Siglap Rd
    24) River Vally ( small pubs & cafe to chill out )

    Nice place to drive around:
    1) Coastal Rd next to changi Airport
    2) West Coast Highway
    3) Old Woodland Rd
    4) Tampanise Ave 10
    5) Mandai Rd
    6) Upper Thomson towards Sembawang
    7) Neo Teo ( quite ulu if from kranji to Gedong )
    International Rd from clementi to JE
    9) Clementi Rd ( From NYP to Clementi)
    10) Kent Ridge Rd
    11) From harbour front to west coast thru pasir panjang ( Alternative rd to go to west coast)
    12) Bedok Reservoir Rd
    13) YCK to Yishun

    Theres toooooo many places where u can drive irregardless finding food scenary or juzz wanna drive around... If u r driving, Drive around.. explore... Theres still some more places but can't really remember the exact location.

    But so far i still tink Changi area is one of the best.. After makan, go see ah guas then drive down Coastal Rd to ECP to town... Especially driving along Coastal rd and see the aeroplanes taking off or landing.. If u wan.. theres only 2 spot which u can stop and view the plane.. but sometimes the police or airport police car will come and even ask u to leave.
