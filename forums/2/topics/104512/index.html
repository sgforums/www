    Just some general advice
    Don't wash the car immediately after hard driving when the wheel and brakes
    are hot.
    Rinse the car first to remove big particles, else you may end of scratching the paintwork
    At the rinsing stage, you may want to feel the surfaces with you bare hands
    to locate and remove any such particles harmlessly.
    After that, you can shampoo the car.

    try not to use any kind of soap on a newly waxed car. u will end up thinning the layer of wax n other protective layers applied on your paint. Your paint also gets kindof rough if you happen to apply the liquid wax directly onto the car and do not wash it off immediately. Wash off all bird dropping as soon as possible, or it will eat into ur paint. Wash ur car after it has rained also as the water will dry up n leave spots . For the rims, you can use normal soap powder( the one u use for washing clothes)..very effective.. check ur tires for stuck bits of stones etc..

    Aviod engine wash by companies unless ur going to sell your car. The chemical they use eats ur cables n metal after a period of time.
