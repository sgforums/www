    there's 2 reasons to fitting a rollcage,

    a) for protection. as jonathan has pointed out, it prevents the chassis from getting crumpled in and crushing the occupants in the event of a serious crash.

    b) for additional chassis rigidity. the structure of the roll cage reinforces the chassis and prevents the chassis from flexing during hard acceleration, braking and cornering. this aids greatly in improving the overall handling of a car.

    u dun find road cars installed with roll cages simply coz its illegal. its illegal coz most roll cages can only be installed after extensive modifications have been made to the existing chassis to accomodate the roll cage. moreover, as the purpose of the roll cage implies, its like telling everyone ur car is used for racing. a taboo in local road terms.
