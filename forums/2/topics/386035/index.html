    It really depends on your diligence and ability to learn. I got my 3A license in 3 months starting from the date I enrolled to the date I passed my driving test. 

    I went for driving lessons twice a week after getting my PDL. Went for about 20 lessons and booked my driving test. You gotta keep going for lessons so that you won't forget what you've learned during the last lesson lor. You must also be lucky lah. I was lucky enough to  find a driving test which was 2 weeks away after my last lesson. 

    Also, try to block-book. Don't book lessons one by one. It's pretty hard to find lessons that are like 2-3 days away from your previous lesson.
