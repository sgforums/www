    White GC8V and GT-T are along a different stretch of Bukit Timah, near Beauty World, where Courts was (is still?). There is still a second GC8V, gun-metal in colour, I don't know whether it is the same one that was driven by someone from Pasir Laba SISPEC camp around 2000. But I saw that one recently as well.

    MotorImage did sell Impreza Coupes at some point in time, so they could either be parallel import or official imports.

    I guess I've seen as many wannabe R34 GT-R as I've seen real GT-Rs. There's a "M-Spec NuR" parking sometimes at the Gateway West basement, it is a very good replica, has most of the bits, down to the Brembo brakes, 6 spoke 18 inch wheels, but almost nothing to give it away on the first glance except it is narrower than a GT-R. But you look into the interior and voila, no digital dash display.

    That white GT-T trying to look like a GT-R has an automatic transmission as well.

    Other rare cars I have seen and haven't got (a good) picture of include the Lexus/Toyota IS200/Altezza station wagon, NSX, Starlet Glanza, EK9 Civic Type R, Impreza WRX Wagon, MA70 Supra, DC5 Integra Type R (both 2 and 4 door models).
