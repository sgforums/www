    GUYS GUYS GUYS!!!! UP CLOSE AND PERSONAL WITH AN M5!!!! YOU MAY THINK I'M CRAZY BUT WHO CARES, IT'S STILL AN M5!!!!

    It was in the carpark in Holland Village opposite KFC. I saw the number plate... SFA * T and it was quite familiar, plus I saw the M5 styled rims. Thought it was another poser at first, but soon realised that I had seen it zoom around on the road before. This was the real thing!!!!

    I ran up to it and took out my phone and took lots and lots of photos immediately, but not before inspecting its interior to see if it was a real one, because I've seen another M5 which was so well done up on the outside, but had an auto tranny when I looked in. I seriously cannot describe the feeling but I'd say it's quite close to how you guys must have felt when you were really little and got like a brand new toy or something. Anyway, I took at least 20 photos, but unfortunately couldn't get much of its interior cause it was too dark and camera phones aren't really the best to take photos either.

    I hung around the car for about half an hour; I had nothing to do and was actually on my way home. And finally, the owner came, with his son who was about 16 years old. True Blue Diehard Driver. Told me he wasn't realy keen on the E60 M5 cause it was only available with SMG. LUCKY SON. Very nice people they were too, I think he might be from Hong Kong.

    Anyway, he let me take a photo of his interior and told me that there're about 5 E39 M5s on the road, and that his was the last one to be bought in Singapore before they stopped production, and there's 1 E34 M5 too. My God I am sooooo lucky!

    Anyway, the pics are still in my phone and I need to upload them to my laptop before I can have them posted here. Does anyone know where I can get a free and simple webby to host them? Pleaaase let me know I'm sure there're some of you hardcores who might love the M5 as much as I do =)
