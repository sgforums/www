    Powerful S Class continues to grow stronger

    Lorinser is the first choice in perfection. The Swabians have been refining the noble
    Mercedes-Benz vehicles since 1977. Traditionally, the upper class models of the star
    brand have been the special highlights. The current S class as well is being perfectly
    refined by Lorinser down to the finish.

    Matching the marked, sportive and, at the same time, dignified look of the Lorsinser S
    06, the engines are also optimized. More performance and a torque band with up to 30
    percent improvement Â– the extra power always makes itself felt. Three versions are
    available: Lorinser enhances the V6 with 3,498 cc from the standard 272 HP (200 kW) to
    298 HP (219 kW). The V8 with 5,461 is also undergoing a performance treatment: Instead
    of 388 HP (285 kW), the mighty 5-liter engine now transfers 420 HP (309 kW) to the
    asphalt. A Vmax increase to 280 km/h is available both for the six and eight-cylinder
    version. The extra output is achieved through a modified exhaust manifold/catalytic
    converter unit in conjunction with altered engine maps. This results in clearly improved
    combustion in the upper rotational speed range.

    The additional power is particularly impressive with the compressor version of the
    Lorinser S 06: The engine achieves 416 HP (306 kW) thus 110 HP (81 kW) more than the
    basic model. The top speed of 330 km/h turns the compressor version of the Lorinser S 06
    into a high-class super performer. The altered compressor sets new standards also in the
    area of acceleration Â– its max. torque lies in the range of powerful 550 Nm (series: 460
    Nm).

    Optically refined and with significantly higher performance: the elegant and sportive
    Lorinser S 06 is a vehicle of the noble class. Being sporty and luxurious at the same
    time, it satisfies even highest demands. Design and power of the Lorinser S 06 truly
