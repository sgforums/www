    14 Jul 04, 11:56
    There is limited reviews on the new cefiro on the net. Seems like only Asia has it....

    I've upgrade from the old cefiro2.0 to a black 230. Overall the driving expereince has been excellent. Stability has improved vastly! I need to be very careful when driving over speed bumps with my old cefiro. The newer version glides over bumps nicely.

    Noise insulation has improved. Other drivers cant seem to hear my expletives on the road now....

    I like the smaller steering wheel. The bigger steering in the old cefiro feels like a lorry. But its a bit too sensitive and twitchy. Need to be more focused on the road. Turning radius of the car is smaller and that is a plus.

    They have cleaned up the interior. Spartan and zen like. Very good on the eyes. No excessive electronics and every feature has a sensible function. Well done on that part Nissan!!

    The gearbox is a beauty. very smooth transitions compared to the old one.

    The keyless entry is cool too. But i worry what if i forget to lock the door. someone can just drive it away...

    Bad points:
    Its a fuel guzzler!!! My gut feel its about 20% higher than my old car???
