    On behalf of EuroVanClub,kindly allow me to post the below write-up.Many thanks and regards to the admin and moderator team:

    Tulip Hearts Day is [http://www.ccf.org.sg/] Children's Cancer Foundation's annual fund-raising EVent to be held on 3rd September 2006.

    In true Tulip Hearts Day tradition, volunteers will be out in unity islandwide, raising funds through the sales of more than 100,000 handmade tulips to create awareness of childhood cancer. To help raise further awareness among the public, and in the absence of major sponsors towards CCF, we will be organising our THD2006 Drive-Thru for the 3rd year running.

    And in true EuroVanClub(EVC) [http://www.eurovanclub.com/] tradition, we will be adopting the EVent for the 5th consecutive year - in an attempt to reach out to more kind-hearted donors. The purpose of the Drive-Thru is to make it more accessible to reach out to donors in the driving community whom might be driving past on the EVent day and to have a permanent location for drivers to make their doantion. We will have volunteers stationed at Kallang carpark for the sales of the tulips. Our road marshalls will be there to provide assistance for donors.

    Silk cocooned tulips will be sold at $2 each to help raise fund CCF. There will be approximately 14 colours to choose from. An exhibition area will also be set up inside Take 5 Kopitiam to help raise awareness on childhood cancer, "Boy Girl" handphone strap cum bookmark at $5 each will also be sold at the counter next to the exhibition area.

    We sincerely hope like-minded people who wish to make this year's EVent a more successful one then the last, can set aside an hour of your precious time on that Sunday to make a trip down to Kallang. Purchase the tulips from our volunteers, making it ours, and your day a truly fruitful and eventful day out of 365.

    See you there!

    To find out more about the Drive-Thru Donation, please visit:

    For more information about Tulip Hearts Day '06, please visit:
    http://www.ccf.org.sg/thd/
