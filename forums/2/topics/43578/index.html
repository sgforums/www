    Hi again,

    We'll 1st compare the 3 Honda Civic (ES) spec 1st:-

    1.5 Vti (112 horsepower)
    Auto 0-100km/h - 11.2 sec
    Manual 0-100km/h - 10.2 sec

    1.6 Vti (110 horsepower)
    Auto 0-100km/h - 11.7 sec
    Manual 0-100km/h - 10.2 sec

    1.7 Vti (128 horsepower)
    Auto 0-100km/h - 9.5 sec
    Manual 0-100km/h - 8.6 sec

    As you can see, the 1.7 Vti, though SOHC, the manual version is still quite fast, though u can't compare with SiR. And with accessories like sun-roof etc, I guess that's why they put the price 10k different from the 1.6. Anyway, the 1.6 and the 1.7 has a different of close to 20 horsepower, which is quite a hugh different, I mean, can actually feel it.

    Like I mention b4, I don't understand why the 1.6 Vti is having slightly less horsepower than the 1.5, doesn't make sense.

    As for the corolla Vvti 1.6, the spec are below, hope it helps you to choose your car better.
    Horsepower - 108
    Auto 0-100km/h - 11.7sec
    Manual 0-100km/h - 10.3sec
