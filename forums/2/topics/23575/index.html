    Ok yesterday I was at Orchard Road, the HMV road junction if you knew what I mean. After crossing the road and heading for Cineleisure, I heard the police siren ... seconds later, a traffic police sped to the junction, stopped his bike beside the road and ran to the centre of the junction. Seconds later, 1 or 2 more bikes sped past the junction. Should have seen the faces of the drivers, they were like totally in shock! Then, the traffic police stopped cars from cutting across the horizontal main road i.e. the stretch from Borders -> Paragon -> Heeren -> Emerald didn't require stopping at all.

NO ALPABHETS, only NUMBERS! The traffic police were actually there to 'kai lu' so that the VIPs would not be stuck in Orchard Road ...
