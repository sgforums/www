    here's some tips for you :

    you can't feel the speed. so watch it.

    check your blindspot, the frame of your car is blocking your view, so turn your head and save a life.

    check your mirrors constantly so that you know every vehicle that's around you.

    judge the speed of an oncoming car before changing lanes or overtaking. do not force the oncoming car to do any hard braking.

    just because your car is big, it doesn't give you the right of way, be a courteous driver.

    Motorcycles are vehicles too, they want to squeeze, let them squeeze. If they fall, its their loss. Do not do stupid things like block them, they want to take the risk, let them take responsibility. On your part, check your mirrors, drive normally.

    Since you're still learning to park, do not rush. Next thing you know, the BMW will have to go for a paint job. If possible, get a friend with enough experience to guide you through.

    Make a mental note of the size of your car. You don't want to knock into anything when parking.

    Finally, if its just too big for you, drive the sunny. How do you know if its too big? You have to reach for the accelerator or you have to stretch your neck to look ahead... you don't wanna knock into something and realised that you couldn't see it due to the size of the vehicle. Do everyone a favour, don't drive just because its a BMW. Drive something your comfortable with and save lives on the road.
