New record for World's Fastest Production Car

  • yxTay
    13 Oct 07, 10:16

    Not exactly new news, but the SSC Ultimate Aero TT has unofficially overtaken the Bugatti Veyron as the World's Fastest Production Car, with an average top speed of 256.15 mph. That's just a little faster than the previous record. The record run is said to be currently being verified by the Guiness Book of Records. With 1183 bhp, it is also the World's Most Powerful Production Car. The Ultimate Aero achieved this record despite having half the number of cylinders and superchargers. I guess Bugatti really has to make more effort.
