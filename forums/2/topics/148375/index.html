    yes my sister in law also has a 4 months old Nissan Sunny and it is Auto yet the FC is very low, average only 10km/l so your manual sounds quite right in the range. It is a characteristic of Nissan's high revving engine (notice the gears change down too fast each time and revs pick up too quickly, hence FC is higher , instead of the better low-end torque from VVTi engines from other makes like Honda and Toyotas.

    fill up full t0 the brim. Drive to whatever distance, doesnt really matter. Record the distance, Go fill up full to the brim again and see how many litres you put in . Then take the travelled km $ divide by the litres used
    and there you have it.
    Some cars have varying FC with different octane grade, some cars make no difference as long as it meets its min.octane requirement.
    Some cars have on-board on-the spot read out of km per litre consumption, so this is more accurate.
    But age of car, driving condition, tyre pressure, oil viscocity, air-filter, plugs, your right foot, weight of the car, passengers carried, etc also pay an important part to get optimum FC,
    I'm averaging 13.8km per L on my new (5000km driven-in)1.8vvti MPV , using 95Octane, with an occasional heavy-footed driving and carry 2 passengers 30% of the time on 60% highway, 40%urban traffic

    My Sunny FL(M) is coming to 2 years old and I would like to share with you the performance of my car so far:

    I get an average FC of 14-15km/l. 70% highway 30% urban driving. Aircon low usuage and speed try to keep 80- 90km/hr.Petrol using Shell 95ron. Engine oil Castrol RS.
    I have done the following so far:

    1) Install Splitfire spark plug.
    2) Gounding. (took the combo package with item 1 @ $156)

    After a few months,I get a simple Cold Air intake done.

    After doing item 1) n 2), FC and performance reach what I have now. I personnaly think gounding is good for older car only.

    As for the CAI, I realise it doesn't help much when I keep to 80km/hr. But when I try to go faster @ 90km+, it doesn't have high impact on my FC.

    Everything else is original stock. Even my wheels are original steel rim type.

    Hope that helps.
