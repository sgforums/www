    Of course the TP will not charge anyone caught driving with only one hand on the steering wheel. However, as someone mentioned, it is easy to veer off course if you are driving with only one hand on the steering wheel, or to make an overly wide turn and cross the center white line. Under such circumstances, the TP can charge you for dangerous driving, etc. Thus, in conclusion, you will not be caught for driving with only one hand on the steering wheel, but you may be charged for the consequences of driving with only one hand on the steering wheel.
