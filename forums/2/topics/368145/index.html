    Honda has officially confirmed that they will release the Honda CR-Z hybrid and Honda Fit Hybrid by the end of 2010. 

    Honda will bring the CR-Z to market in Japan beginning in February 2010, with sales of the Fit starting towards the end of that year. The 1.3 liter SOHC aluminium alloy combustion engine with i-VTEC, along with the electric motor, is able to crank out 98HP. The auto maker also revealed that it is developing a new hybrid system for mid to large vehicles. Honda also announced that the Fit Hybrid (also known as the Jazz in certain markets) will hit showrooms by the end of 2010.


    Japanese car manufactureHonda Fit

    The Fit hybrid will hit the market in October next year. But Mr Ito declined to reveal a price for the two cars, saying only that customers would find them affordable.
