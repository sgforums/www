    hi, just passed my 2nd attempt at SSDC on 14 jan. hope the following tips help :-

    1. try to maintain 0 pts inside the circuit.
    2. take your time when doing all the circuit tests ie, don't be too kancheong to complete each test in a hurry coz you'll make mistakes.
    3. listen to instructions given by tp carefully. they get irritated when you ask them to repeat again.
    4. never ever engage in conversations with tp during test. you are suppose to concentrate in your driving! even if he talks to you, don't look at him. if he nags at you or ticks you off inside the car, just don't look at him but simply answer politely "ok, i will improve on it".
    5. never over-check yr blindspots. i got ticked off for over-checking. simply tilt yr head either to left or right in split second when checking and then look straight ahead.
    6. one good tip the tp himself gave me was, always check yr rear mirror constantly and especially when you are contemplating overtaking and switching lanes.
    7. they are always on the look out for gear-changing and accelerating on the road. so you need to really work on perfecting in those areas.
    8. 2.40pm is a good time coz by the time you complete yr circuit, you shud be out on the road by 3pm which means lesser traffic but look out for hazards like stationary cars parked on the roadside especially those damned taxis. remember to signal right and switch lane than signal left and return back to your rightful lane after passing them.
    9. be extra-extra careful if you are given test routes involving ang mo kio street 61. maintain yr speed at not more than 40km and max 3rd gear. there are lots of pedestrains dashing across that street.
    10. lastly, pray that the weather is fine and also tp in good mood.

    best of luck to you coz you wud need it for yr test tomorrow too! remember don't be too kancheong inside the circuit!!
