    Hi,

    I think you dont quite understand yet.

    The amount of money you pay to renew or extend your COE is based on the average COE price over 3 months for the category your vehicle falls in.

    Imagine:

    Month 1: 7000

    Month 2: 6500

    Month 3: 6000

    So average: (7000 + 6500 + 6000)/3 = 6500

    So amount you have to pay to extend or renew your COE for another 10 years will be 6500.

    Usually the amount is already calculated and posted in the onemotoring website..
