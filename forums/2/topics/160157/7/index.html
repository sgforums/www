The question is, how much speed do you need in order to lift the car up like that in the first place?

    The car appeared to be travelling on the overtaking lane, and the MX-5 is a car with very immediate response. Without judging his forward speed, I would feel that he had very possibly steered the car much too quickly to snap the tail out like that - the car *should* have responded with a directional change and began turning into the slow lane way before the rear wheel had a chance of tagging the right hand side road kerb.
