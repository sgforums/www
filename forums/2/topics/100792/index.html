  • Ali_Khl
    2 Nov 04, 22:47
    I took a picture of a Celica GT-4 ST185 that i spotted in Singapore in the "Sightings 2" topic. The GT-Four model of the Celica comes in ST165, 185 and 205 guise. The ST165 GT-4 was released in 1987 or so as a homologation special (as at the time manufacturers had to produce a road-going version of cars they wished to enter World Rally championships). The one pictured above in red is the ST185, and so far I have spotted two here in Singapore. The blue one pictured with the quad-round headlights is the latest Celica GT-4 model, the ST205. All feature turbocharged engines and four wheel drive, hence the name. You likely wont see any new versions as Toyota is no longer competitng in the WRC and is unlikely to add a turbocharged engine and four-wheel drive platfrom to the SS2 Celica.
