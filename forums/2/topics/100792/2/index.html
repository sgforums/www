    There's no ST205 Celicas in Singapore, the ST205 denotes the 4WD 3S-GTE turbocharged version that was produced to homologate the Group A rallycar. Those in Singapore are the ST204 series, FF engine layout, I believe.

    There are however a scant few examples of the ST185 Celica GT4, though, they are the late model Carloz Sainz versions with updated bodywork and most obviously the large bonnet radiator grille (older versions used bulged scoop and weren't seen in Singapore). Most were registered with late series SBx plates, probably around 1991 and '92. Even rarer are the cabriolet versions though there were not turbocharged.
