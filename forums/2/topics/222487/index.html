    LSD is Limited Slip Differential.. something which distributes power to the wheels differently... when cornering at high speeds, the car may tend to want to roll, which means the wheels on the "inside" has less traction or weight on them, causing them to want to spin..

    this wheelspin doesn't add to the traction and with LSD, this wheelspin is diminished...

    how it does so, you have to go into the gearbox to figure it out...
