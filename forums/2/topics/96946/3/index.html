    in terms of handling, the ford focus is the unparalleled handler in its class.. it uses a traditional hrdraulic steering rack.. which gives much better feedback than an electric-power steering, used in the altis..handling wise.. the altis lags behind due to its more comfort-biased springs.. and due to the fact tht it has a torsion beam at the rear.. in contrast to the focus's multilink.. it doesnt' give as much variations in suspension geometry.. thus handling is not as good.. however, the altis's handling isnt as bad as most ppl think.. in fact, its turn-in response is quite good.. so lowering the car and fixing stiffer springs will help a bit..

    performance-wise, the altis beats the focus.. as it has variable valve timing.. which means optimised torque and power..

    the focus is going to be replaced soon... which explains the lower price.. but i still think its a better buy... since its built in europe.. so its quality is better than tht of the altis.. foci dont sell tht cheap usually.. so if u cant wait.. just go for it..
