    You must be 18 before u can even start going to any driving centre to enrol the whole thing.

    2. How long does the theory course last for? What are the things we learn in it?

    Own time own target, but u must complete the whole theory before u can be on outside road. Things learnt, the markings on the road what they meant for, the sign shown alongside a road what they stand for,right of way etc...

    3. Can we choose our own driving instructor, from his company?

    By default u can't unless, u join the fixed group scheme,where a same bunch of instructors stick with you.

    4. What will we do in a practical test? What if I crash while doing it?

    The standard 6 circuit courses, and outside road.

    You wun need to pay for the damages, and most likely u wun need to cos,TP tester will jam the brake once he senses something dead wrong.

    5. How long will this entire course last for?

    From 3 months to half year.

    Everything not covered in the police link above:

    3 and 4. The instructor's insurance policy will pay for accidents, and he is there to help you avoid getting into them. Which is why it's illegal to engage unlicensed instructors.

    If you go to a driving centre and request a specific instructor, the driving centre will charge extra.
