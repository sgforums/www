    So I assume that you either own or have driven an E-Class for a sustained (ie more than just a day) period of time as to judge it as such? The braking performance of the W211 E-Class is actually top notch, namely considering its a brake-by-wire system (known as the SBC in Mercedes terms) and I would say that the braking is far from "having problems" stopping a vehicle weighing 1560kg (less in the case of the E200K).

    Just for comparision a Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution 8 weighs 1480kg, and considering just the amount of finishing and features that goes into producing an E, it is not "damn heavy" either. The steering feels like it needs more effort to use as it has more feedback and hence it is not artificially light as with many Japanese compacts, which are 'easier' to manouver in traffic and such but infact severly lacking from needed feedback and proper 'feel' around the bends.
