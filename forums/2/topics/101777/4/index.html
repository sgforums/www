    OPC COUPON & OPC RESTRICTED ON SATURDAYS
    FEEDBACK NUMBERS: 219256 & 219257

    Thank you for your feedback of 25-FEB-2005 on the above-mentioned issues.

    We regret to inform you that we are unable to accede to your suggestions. The OPC scheme was designed to provide car owners with the option to own cars at significantly lower costs if they are prepared to use them sparingly. Under the scheme, OPC owners are given substantial upfront tax rebates and road tax discounts in return for restricted usage of OPCs during certain time periods.

    The quantum of tax benefits provided to the owners of OPCs is determined based on the periods of their usage restriction. Any change to the restricted time periods, whether on weekdays or Saturdays, will require a review of the tax benefits given. We have recently reviewed and decided that owners of OPCs should continue to enjoy the tax benefits at present.

    Once again, we thank you for your feedback.
