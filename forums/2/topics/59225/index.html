    well, for me. When getting a second hand car, don't go for the cheapest, a 93 esi can cost from 20k to 40k, depending on quality.

    Things to check for: General body condition, paint condition, visible dents/scratches, Interior seats condition, speaker/stereo/HU condition, If possible, try to get someone to check out the engine and handling condition.
