    My torque starts at 1300rpm all the way till abt 6300rpm before it tapers down. Maximum rev is 8000 but limited to 7000.

    Its actually not really dangerous unless you dont knwo what you are doing. BMW has too many checks and sensors in the car so every tune has to include the fail-safe parameters.
