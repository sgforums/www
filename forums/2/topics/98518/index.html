    after 2011, the car cannot be used unless u cont to renew the COE.

    There is no scrap values for cars after 10 years.

    U must check what is the mileage for this car. Most parts of the car must be changed after certain mileage, such as the timing belt, spark plug etc.

    U should also check the serving records; what parts has been changed and what has not.

    Lastly, send to VICOM or AAS to have it certified. If the certification is within 3 months of the sales date, then should be ok. If not, have it send to VICOM or AAS again. They charge about 80 ~ $120.
