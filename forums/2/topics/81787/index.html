    If you buy a normal scheme car and convert it into Weekend car, u get nothing in rebate and pay $100 for the conversion. If u buy a weekend scheme car and convert it into a normal scheme car u pay the prorated amount of 17k rebate. E.g. If u buy a 8yr old weekend scheme car then u got to pay LTA 2/10*17K = $3,400 for the car to be used for the remaining 2 years. On top of this u pay the usual $100 for the conversion as well. Convert only if you are sure. The only tangible benefit is the amount of road tax saved. If you buy and convert a 1.5L car, u pay only roughly $70-80 for your road tax all year round. The savings is $800 off the usual tax amount.
