  • darrenosaurus_rex
    11 Nov 04, 18:09
    Originally posted by bismarck:
    nice pics
    hey darren, care to share with me how u take the pics ?
    using a similar lumix...fz1 on monday...when i go with a few friends.

    how do u avoid the noise and underexposure problems ?
    thx lots i used the ISO-200 setting on my camera instead of auto according to my photog fren's advice. also, if u haf battery life to spare, turn on ur histogram on ur display tt will give u a spiky graph. try to keep most of the graph in the middle instead of at the 2 ends cos if tt happens ur pic is over/underexposed. also, try to manually set the white balance instead of using auto since ur pictures wont look yellowed or anyth. pics shdnt be underexposed cos its pretty bright. most of the booths have a lot of white in them. u can try the flash+ambient light setting if the pic is still too dark but make sure ur hand is steady for tt. but i din use flash more the pics i posted here.

    oh and i turned my lcd brightness all the way to minimum. save battery plus if u look thru lcd it wont be so bright cos lcds tend to make the pics seem brighter than they rly are so when u transfer to com its all messed up. hope this helps i'll ask him for more advice for u n i'll tell u asap. haf fun at the show.


    i took the first pic without flash but it was too dark. took the 2nd pic with flash + ambient light, much better but had a bit of the flash bounce off the perspex. btw tts the renesis rotary engine from the mazda rx8
