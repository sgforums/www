    Because the stock B series blocks are aluminium, they are not ideal for boosting, thats why its so essential to rebuild the motor with forged pistons and valves, and sleves too.
    Have to reduce compression alittle, so cams must change. Since cams and valves are upgraded, stiffer valve springs must also be added.
    All this is done for reliability issues only.

    Cars like the supra and the '93 eclipse GRX come with stock cast iron engine blocks, so little or nothing needs to be changed before adding a snail to them.

    Hondas will be great cars to TC, but they won't be cheap!
