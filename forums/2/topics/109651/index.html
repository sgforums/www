    Yes. qp = your "coe rebate" (actually this term is radically different in meaning in the showrooms but i guess we all know what you mean...) they mean the same thing. Government purposely did it to curb "wasteage" on those cars scrapped b4 the 3rd year cos' owners used to get 130% of the OMV rebate if they scrap early under the old PARF system. If your car is pretty new the perhaps on top of the COE rebate, scrap value(PARF) u may also receive an additional amount from the scrap dealer for the car body cos' they will eventually export the car to regional countries if the age of the car is still acceptable...
