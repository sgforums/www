    wah very good thread started by free4ever. Actually I'm a very frequent car rental guy (dun wanna spend so much on car by owning one, but has a passion in driving around). Here are my recommendation:

    Date of rental: Just last weekend
    From: Kent / Carol
    Rate of rental: $60 / $80 (everyday, wkday and wkend same rate. min 2 days)
    Car driven: Hyundai Getz Auto 1.3 / Toyota Vios Auto 1.5
    Collection area: edgedale plains blk 131 muti-storey carpark
    Contact number: 81257822 / 90115179
    Experience: Very detailed owner who takes very good care of both cars. Both cars are very new (Getz less than a yr, Vios less than 3 months). NO DEPOSIT required. I've tried only the Getz. It has bodykit and remus exhaust. Overall, very good and smooth. I've seem the Vios, condition is superb.

    Date of rental: one month ago
    From: Rose
    Rate of rental: $75 (wkday) $80 (wkend)
    Car driven: 2002 Nissan Sunny Auto 1.5
    Collection area: blk 326 ubi ave 1 #06-695
    Contact number: 90025105
    Experience: I'm actually quite a regular with this owner. She owns more than one car. Mit Lancer, Trajet, Corolla, Sonata are also under her. She works from home. So far, I've been renting only the Sunny from her. Only until recently I realised something ugly about the way she handles rental. As I always rent short term like 1-2 days, initially she will reserved the car for me when i book a slot. And ALWAYS until the last min, one day or on the day itself, she will call and tell me the car is out. She claimed she "forgot" or sometimes she claimed "overlook" the booking. Then, finally one day, I found out its because she rent the car out to drivers who rent longer term from her!! Come'on man, she gave her word to me!! I understand that those car rental guys wanna earn more by renting out for a longer period, but its a matter of trust and business. You cant be going around giving people your word and then back out last min?!?!? Anyway, i'm just sharing out this bad experience to you people. Overall the car is good.

    Date of rental: many months ago
    From: Yusri
    Rate of rental: $100/day. ($250/wkend package)
    Car driven: Kai Caren 7-seater Auto 1.8
    Collection area: Woodlands Mrt
    Contact number: 93692195
    Experience: Good budget 7-seater car. I drove this to malaysia and has no problem at all. It even has an extra large box on the top of the car. Just beware of the height limit when you drive into a multi-storey carpark. Owner is very "anything" one. Just return the car in one piece and top up the tank accordingly can liao.

    There are many more which i have tried but most sold their car away already. I hope my experience help you people in one way or another.

    Cheers!!
