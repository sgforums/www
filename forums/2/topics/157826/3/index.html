    Originally posted by alberteinstein:
    if scoop is so bad, why subary dun use the evo type, no scoop to cause air resistance?
    because they have a top mounted intercooler, which is due to the engine bay packaging they decided on during the car's conception and design phase. and with a top mounted intercooler, u need a scoop to force air in. The evo bonnet is not actually a scoop, rather, its a vent, more for letting hot air out of the bay than for air intake. The evo has its intercooler at the front, thus precluding the need for a bonnet scoop. My airtrek Turbo R has a bonnet scoop as well, but as the intercooler of the ATR is smaller than the WRX intercooler, the bonnet scoop is smaller as well.
