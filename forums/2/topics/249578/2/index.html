    Hey guys,

    Just test drove(or my dad rather,I'm still a P plate ) the VW golf gti today at the showroom near leng kee there.

    And boy did it leave a deep impression on me. First thing that greeted me while awaiting the car at the main entrance was hearing the bassy note of the exhaust. The test car was a 5 door gti. so i hopped on to the back while hearing the SE go on and on about the safety aspects of the car and boy was it power packed.

    Acceleration in the car was extremely linear for a turbo engine. no feeling of turbo lag at all. i think its due to the fact that a smaller turbo unit was fitted. okay anyways my as the car has the ability to be semi automatic, my dad decided to be abit more adventurous and try out its handling. gear changes out of bends was extremely smooth, lol felt almost as though the car was invincible enough to beat a subaru WRX(of course its not ). Wind noise at speeds is quite low. haha the only noise that enters the cabin is that of the 2.0l engine and the turbo roar during hard acceleration which was pure bliss. The car could do with a better exhaust system though, and the sound would be even more perfect.

    I think i give this car a 7.3/10.
