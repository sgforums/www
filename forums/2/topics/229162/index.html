    Correct ..

    Open pod = power comes in only after 3k RPM for most cars .. Sound of sucking in the air is very loud and bassy

    Drop in Filter = Not much increase in power .. just a smoother move off .. recommended for auto as auto already lose out in pick up compared to manual ..

    Cold Air Intake (CAI) = A piping from your air intake inlet to somewhere infront of your front grill or somewhere in your engine bay that is cooler .. Recommended for all vehicles as the more cold air is being feed into your engine, the better combustion you have hence a more responsive acceleration respond ...
