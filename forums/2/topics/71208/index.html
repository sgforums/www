    A normal good system consists of

    1. Head unit
    2. 2 sets of component speakers ( Front and Rear )
    3. 1 Woofer
    4. 2 amps ( 1 X 4 channels amp for component speakers and a 2 channels amp for your woofer

    If you have more cold hard cash ... then you can consider adding

    5. pre-amp
    6. capacitor ( spelling error )

    Fuse and wiring is a must even when your car is a new car cause the stock wiring of your car just cant transmit the quality of sound anywhere ...

    Malaysia pricing might be cheap but their stuffs cant really be used to compare to singapore stuffs ...
    The crossover they use for their component speakers is junk ...

    As for brands of speakers :

    High end
    kicker
    DSL
    CDT

    Mid end
    Dyno quest
    Lanzer
    Lighting audio

    The brands above is purely based on my personal point of views

    My car setup is as below (for your referance)

    1. Pioneer head unit 50w X 4 single disc
    2. DSL 6' component speakers
    3. 1 X midend pre-amp ( forget the brand cos din notice the brand )
    4. Lanzer 1000watts 4 channels amp
    5. Dyno quest 600watts 2 channels amp (bridged)
    6. Dynoquest 12" basstube

    Hope this helps and in any case you want an introduction of audio specialist that i can get discount at .... feel free to PM me
