    well BOVs are more relavent for vehicles with high pressure turbos, because these turbos need more exhaust pressure to start spinning. when they are not spinning they lag, and when u release the accelerator pedal abruptly, the exhaust gases backflow. This backflow exerts a back pressure on the turbo which will cause it to slow down or stop. This translates to turbo lag, which is undesirable, so a BOV prevents backflowing exhaust gases from having an effect on the turbo turbine by venting it out of a valve.
