    that is the turbo boost being 'dumped' when the throttle is closed. the turbo compresses air into the cylinders, so when throttle closed, the compressed air is not needed, so it has to be rid of, so it goes out thru a valve. so it comes out making that hissing sound. most turboed vehicles make that sound, including buses and trucks.

    so i wonder y zzit so cool on a car, when that sound has been around for eons.
