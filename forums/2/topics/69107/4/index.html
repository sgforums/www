    Hi all Altis owners,
    Here are 2 pics which I took for my car for the Singtel Charity Drive to Penang. The journey is damn long and tiring but it was fun.
    Some experiences to share with you all abt driving the Altis.
    1) I clocked 462km at half tank at 120km/h.
    2) Upon driving up to penang, the car doesn't have that stable feeling when I'm driving at 120-130 km/h. The steering starts to vibrates. After 1 hr or so, it starts to get stable and smoother.
    3) The max speed I tried was 155km/h (at abt 3600rpm). My wife said that it's too fast, so I reduced to 120-130km/h.
    4) On my way back from Penang, I was doing at 130-140km/h. The car just gets better, the engine noise isn't to loud but the wind noise is. The handling is good. Glad that I bought the Altis.
    5) Overall, I clocked abt 1600km for the whole trip. Now can go for the 5000km servicing.
