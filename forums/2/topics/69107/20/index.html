    the polymer wax comes in two size packing. 300mls or 1 litre. I usually buy the 300 ml packing as it could last easily a year. I paid $14.50. (list $16.50 - must bargain with the sales people). The 1 litre packing is about $31 and its more economical if you have someone to share with.
