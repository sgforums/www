    well OD = ON is equivalent to driving with all your active gears (4 Forward Gears)

    when OD = OFF, the maximum gear your automatic transmission will engage is 3rd Gear.

    it is usually used when you wan to do Overtaking when you are cruising or gradual gradient climb when the not so intelligent automatic transmission does not kick down from 4th gear to 3rd gear. thus it does not make a difference if you drive off with OD=OFF as it only affects the final gear.

    instead you should try driving with ECT in POWER mode. allows you to drag all 4 gears....shiok
