    Hi Speedy,
    initially I thought it would show up the dirt easily but as it turns out, it is quite ok and manageable.
    For a start, I recommend coating the surface with polymer wax (e.g Hi-glaze 88 ). This liquid wax gives a protective seal to the paint surface and allows easy wiping of dirt with just a damp cloth. This will free you from much maintenance washing. I am not advocating any particular brand as any polymer base wax should do the job. I stick to this brand for the last 20+ years and all my previous cars (average 6 yrs) need no re-spray job. Avoid using soapy foam frequently as the detergent base removes the wax protective layer.
