    The Altis is as heavy as many Conti cars, if not heavier.
    Selection of good tyres may depend more on:
    - quality of rubber used (some say tyres of same brand made in Europe better than Thai or M'sia in terms road noise. Not so sure abt this)

    - wet grip vs road noise (new technology may produce tyres of good grips on wet condition yet with minimal low noise. I read in some forums that Yokohama Aspec DB or AVS is one of them. There may be others)

    - durability of tyres (some tyres 'run out' faster than others. Yokohama is also one of them)

    - lifespan of tyres (must not exceed 5 years storage. Need to check production date of tyres)

    - Road condition (can say Sin roads are generally 'softer' than Malaysian roads)

    - of course, price of tyres

    Comments from others most welcomed.

    Hi

    I just got my Altis 1.6 last week. Previously, I was driving G9 XLi.
    Here are some differences between these two rides:
    1. Altis is more powerful. Probably this is due to the difference in engines of 1.6 and 1.5
    2. Altis is a smoother ride than the G9.
    3. Altis is quieter than the G9. My colleague who is driving the a.6 G9 can feel and hear the difference in the quietness.
    4. Altis does not give me the "floating" feeling when turning corners.
    5. Altis has ECT which G9 does not have.

    Exterior:
    1. Altis looks longer probably due to the sleek exterior as compared to G9. Some said corolla has a successful diet regime
    2. Both Altis and G9's boots are difficult to close. I have to give an extra force in order to close the boots. But this has been resolved when I sent the rides for servicing.
    3. The headlights are different
    4. The front grilles are different
    5. The tail lights are different

    Interior:
    1. The interior is not much difference between Altis and G9 1.6 models.
    2. Both have climate control (G9 1.6 model and Altis)
    3. Both have the same leather seats and woodgrain
    4. The dashboard lights are different.
    5. The gear box of Altis 1.6 and G9 is the same.
    6. Altis is slightly more spacious than G9.

    In a nutshell, both rides are really good cars and i have no regrets driving them around.
