    Originally posted by y720:
    deepak, i thot the 185/195 is the tyre width (as in seeing it from the front of the car), how can it affect the tyre circumference?
    Hi, i will try my best to explain to you...here goes (may bore the rest to death )

    185 or 195: The tyre section width (W) in millimetres
    70: The aspect ratio in % (The height of the sidewall divided by the tyre's width) [H/W x 100%] = Aspect Ration %

    If you understand the above, say the 185/70 R14 vs a 195/70 R14 based on the above theory, you will realise the height-H will be different for both, thus if the Rim size stays constant (i.e. 14") the overall radius and circumference will change.

    I hope I have answered and cleared your doubts.....
