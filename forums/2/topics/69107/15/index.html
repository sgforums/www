    Hi,
    I don't own a toyota, but below is what was advised to reduce car theft, just to share

    - park head in first
    - turn your steering wheel all the way to one side
    - used a steering wheel lock or any similar device to lock your steering wheel

    This would prevent the towing truck car-theft method, ie. the truck needs to lift either the front or back wheels, and the turned front wheels will prevent them from the pull out, IF you only parked head in.
