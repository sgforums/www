    REVIEW 1.

    Ok ok my turn to contribute some reviews. I just got my car last week. Its the Deluxe version. Interior is quite elegant and classy. Nice steering wheel. But the leather color only comes in light bedge. will have to take extra care...sigh. Having the MP3 is great. Long playing hours with one single disc. Can forget the CD Changer.

    Altis is heavier than my trade-in nissan sunny 99. It's firm and steady when big buses speed pass you. Engine wise is very responsive and powerful, no doubt about that. Guess i won't be activating the ECT mode. Sound level is acceptable to me. Steering and brakes might be too sensitive. Will need time to get used to it. and i still not used to the drive seat. can't really rest my head. And there's a level on left side to lower and raise spine support. Anyone used that? Feels funny.

    Compare to Nissan, Altis has no Auto-Lock. Must constantly remind myself to lock all doors while driving. And it's higher, can slide in and out easily. It's also slightly longer, i think. No sure about the roofing yet. Will see how when it rains.

    Great pleasure driving Altis. I think it's a reasonable quality family car build in Toyota Thailand. So far i can't find any traces of "build in thailand" quality in the car.....except for the price...$70K.
