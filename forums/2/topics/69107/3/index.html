    Harlow all,

    Another new Altis owner. Colected 1.6 in early Feb. Met Silver, factory sepc, no mods.

    Overall, a very good ride. Door closes very very well. I suggest you compare with other makes, esp Mit, to compare. Dun flame, this is personal preference. Finishing is good. The feel is not so bare / basic.

    Currently FC for 1st tank is 10.6, 2nd is 12.4, 3rd is 12.1 km/L. No complaints except that there is a very very very slight squeaking noise coming from dashboard when going over bumps at higher speeds. very very slight, but still bothers me ..

    Also believe they did not tighten something for passenger car seat when they changed to leather .. coz sometimes can hear the rattling noise when going over bumps again.

    Overall, very luxuious ride .... happy so far, except maybe could have more power, except that I always all my cars always had more power ...

    so far happy altis corolla customer
