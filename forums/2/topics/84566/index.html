    Okie one thing about NTUC's insurance. I believe all of us have the right to know. It is that they have this 'lowest bidder' system.

    Basically, when you kena accident, NTUC will send your vehicle to IDAC to assess all the repairs needed. After that IDAC will send out this list to all their workshops and the repair will be awarded to the lowest bidder. My concern, is such a system good? Will quality be compromised?

    Secondly, if you decided to send back to KAH MOTOR (say if you buy a HONDA) or your authorised dealer for repair and the cost is $2000. NTUC will pay only up to the lowest bidder. So if NTUC's got a lowest bid for your car at $1500, you will have to top up the $500 yourself.

    Overall, I would agree that as long you drive safely, NTUC is the best becos the cheapest but think abt it, in the first place, you buy insurance to have a peace of mind in case of accident, so why put urself thru problem should accidents arise?
