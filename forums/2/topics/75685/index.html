    I've test driven both:
    Forget the Altis. It looks like an out-of-production Corolla. No character, the rear lamps looks like a cheap Korean car. Inside looks big, except for the huge floor hump. Rear seats cannot be folded, unless you buy a 1.8 litre model. The interior is cleverly camouflage with real "plastic" wood panel, MP3 player and nice leather seats to boost its perceived value.

    You should consider a Vios instead.

    But as for the City,
    1. Ride comfort is a little stiff, but sound proofing is quite acceptable.
    2. Petrol consumption is definitely better than Altis, about 14 to 15 km/litre
    3. Back seats very spacious. No floor hump. Seats back rest and pan are foldable
    4. Accelerate better than the Altis, because it is lighter.
