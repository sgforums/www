COE prices fall across the board

  • QX179R
    23 May 12, 17:55

    SINGAPORE: Certificate of Entitlement (COE) prices for vehicles fell across the board in the latest bidding exercise on Wednesday.

    The biggest drop was in the big car category, where the premium fell S$6,834 to S$85,216.

    The COE price for small cars also ended lower, falling S$4,599 to S$58,001.

    Open category COE, which can be used for any vehicle type but ends up mainly for big cars, retreated S$2,101 to close at S$86,889.

    The COE price for commercial vehicles also dropped. It declined S$1,447 to S$57,106.

    Meanwhile, the premium for motorcycles dipped S$131 to S$1,890.

    -CNA/ac
