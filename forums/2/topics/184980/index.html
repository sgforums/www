    I used v-kool for both my existing and previous car. I think it is the different grades that you have to compare.

    When I used the premier package (that's what they called it then) on my previous car, the heat reduction was excellent. However my new car that comes with v-kool Mirage Package (with compliments from the salesperson) is not as good in terms of heat reduction.

    Do compare the light transmission, IR & UV rejection as well as the overall heat reduction for the different packages offered by each brand. Do check with the companies on the light transmission as some cars windows might fall below LTA's requirement after installing the film.
