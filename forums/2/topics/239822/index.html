    Standardise height of road kerbs, please

    I read with interest 'Standardise road humps for better ride' (ST, Feb 2).

    I would like to suggest that all roadside as well as carpark kerbs be standardised.

    Recently I had a very nasty shock when I reverse-parked my new car in a public car park in Lorong Ah Soo next to Block 128. My back bumper was scratched underneath and when I drove forward it became dislodged.

    Can we have some form of uniform height for kerbs? It is quite painful to see my new car being damaged in such circumstances. I drive a family saloon car for that matter.

    Lee Yoke Lan (Mdm)
