    15 Mar 06, 21:50
    The most important thing is to be calm and confident when driving.

    Be a safe driver, but don't drive too slowly or accelerate slowly either( can get points for that, in case some of you didn't know)

    Before changing lane, turning right or left, always check your mirrors and blindspot.

    Don't drag your gear, change gear at right moment.

    Listen instructions carefully. Avoid asking them to repeat.

    I think thats about all. I passed my TP the 2nd time. Thinking back now, its not difficult to pass at all.

    These guys ( testers) are paid to assess your driving skills, if you fail, blame yourself, don't blame them. Most importantly, learn from your mistakes. Even after passing, you will encounter new situations on the roads. Everyday is a learning process, remember all your mistakes and learn from them. Thats what am doing now.

    Happy driving!
