    D16Z6,

    The GTO Mitsubishi is virtually identical to the 3000GT - The 3000GT is known as the GTO in Japan, the car's central market and everywhere outside (if imported offically, at least) as the 3000GT. Hence due to this fact it can be said that the car that I spotted is a late-Japanese Domestic Market model imported here privately. As for more pics of 3000GTs, ive got a few more as well as an engine picture i took, i will most likely post these up another time.

    The Ford pictured there is a Cosworth - It is based on the Sierra sedan (never officially imported into Singapore) and sports a cosworth tuned turbocharged engine. It is known as the Ford Sierra RS Turbo Cosworth, or a 'Cossie' Apparently i ve heard from a friend that this is the sole Sierra in Singapore and sports more than 500hp! I recently spotted a older Ford Escort RS Cosworth in Singapore too, which was quite a site.
