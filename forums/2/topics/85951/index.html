    Uh.. Port and polish also brings negligible gains for lower bhp engines... You're only removing some slight imperfections inherent from casting techniques used to forge the cylinder heads.

    We're talking a couple of % only.. So for a 100bhp car, you're going to only squeeze about 2 to 5 bhp. Maybe something higher like a 300bhp engine, then maybe you could squeeze 6 to 15 bhp more.

    Considering the costs associated, you could prob get better bang-for-buck with the grounding system. Just my 2 cents.
