    Your FC will not be as accurate if you use this method. To better clock your mileage, follow this:

    1. Top up till full (till the nozzle stops filling). Don't pump in anymore fuel to make it really full tank (This is important for accuracte reading)

    2. Reset ODO and drive as usual for about 100 to 200 Km.

    3. Repeat step 1 and note down the amount of litres pumped.

    4. Check your Km travelled and divide it by the amout of litres pumped e.g. 150/9.5 = 15.8 km/litre.

    Hope this helps.
