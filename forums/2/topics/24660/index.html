The New Driving Licence

  • FireIce
    7 Aug 02, 16:30
    The Traffic Police will be introducing a new photocard driving licence from
    16 December 2002.

    Features:
    credit-card sized; durable; enhanced security features; colour photograph of licence holder

    Cost:
    One-off payment of $50

    Validity:
    Lifetime for Singaporeans and PRs; up to 5 years for others

    How to apply:
    If you current paper licence expires after 16 Dec 2002, wait for a photocard application form that will be mailed to you about six(6) weeks before your paper licence expires.

    If your current paper licence expires before 16 Dec 2002, you will receive a renewal notice for a paper licence. Renew the paper licence for a year and wait for a photocard application form to be mailed to you.

    Where to apply:
    Submit completed application form, in person, along with your NRIC/passport and 1 colour photo, to any SingPost branch.

    How will it be delivered:
    Mailed by registered post to address indicated on your application form about two(2) weeks from date of application.

    Visit www.spinet.gov.sg to find out more or call Traffic Police @ 65470000
