    Dear Friend , well all the nice ppl here offer advice are very gd and true.
    well in singapore context, moddig turbo is rather troublesome esp with LTA.
    okay i have done once with a toyota Rav4 , automatic transmission.
    u have to modify many things as well. And u cannot go for a boost more that 1 bar as your gear box will slip due to the increased horsepower.
    the brakes and absorber also have to be modify to cater the increased hp.
    secondly you fc will also increase.
    thirdly beside adding a turbo charger to ur car, u also have to change your piston to turbo types and also metal gasket to drecreas the compression ratio of your engine as turbo charged engine runs on a lower compression than NA.
    the cooling of the car also need to be considered.
    intercooler etc.... most importantly the nozzle also has to change to a bigger cc to accomdate the increase flow of fuel into the engine.
    if any of the factors are over look the engine will blow up easily
