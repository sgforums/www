    HDB to introduce family season parking ticket from Oct 1

    SINGAPORE: The Housing and Development Board is introducing a family season parking ticket starting next month, making family visits easier.

    HDB residents who require season parking tickets to two different car parks due to family arrangements can get family season parking tickets, which will cost half the price of regular season parking tickets.

    For example, regular monthly season parking ticket charges for passenger cars are S$65 for surface car parks and S$90 for multiÂ—storey car parks. The charges for family season parking ticket will be S$32.50 for surface car parks and S$45 for multiÂ—storey carparks.

    The scheme will be introduced at car parks where there are sufficient parking lots for residents.
