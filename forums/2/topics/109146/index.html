    Confirm with your SE when you'll be getting your car.

    Normally the car should already be physically here when the registration process is done.

    However, I've heard that they need only the engine and chasis number to register the car to your name, but I don't think the SE will dare to do that before the car arrives in Sgp. What if the car is registered, and something cocks up during the journey to Sgp?

    If you are getting the car only in Feb and it is registered now, you lose two months of usage time, not to mention depreciation.

    Also, find out from the SE what fees you'd be paying. It's strange how easily people part with their money these days.
