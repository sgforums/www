    Hi pple!! I was originally doing some extensive search through the internet for automotive interior neon lights today but then found the location for the new Autobacs Bukit Batok branch instead.

    I was asking around for the extract location in the past few days but to no prevail.
    I recce around the given location earlier that evening and found it. But they were closing up when I reached there. heh heh... So I have to visit them the next day.

    Here's the address : 30 Bukit Batok East Avenue 6 Singapore 659761
    It's very big, located beside the Shell petrol station.

    Follow this link for the bird's eye view of the location : http://www.streetdirectory.com.sg/mapsize2.jsp?x=20330.0&y=36627.24&level=7&star=1&oldX=20330.0&postal=659761&oldY=36627.24&heading=30+BUKIT+BATOK+EAST+AVENUE+6+%28659761%29

    Opening hours : 10am - 8pm
    They open everday even on public holidays.

    1st floor selling rims, types, automotive accessories, etc.
    2nd floor selling automotive audio.

    I got the address from this website : http://www.ip-atlas.com/pub/cars/
    It features links to other automotive care and repair garages. It also provide some of the important links to automotive need-to-know website too.

    I hope this helps a bit for you Automobile lovers out there.
