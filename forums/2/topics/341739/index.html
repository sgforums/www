    What Carrerista Jamboree 2008

    When 27th December 2008 – 31st December 2008

    Time

    • 11am - 10pm
    • 11am - 1am (only on 31st December)

    Entry Fee

    • S$20.00 for Adults
    • S$10.00 for Children
    • No charge for those aged 3 and below

    Where Changi Exhibition Centre

    What to expect

    • Drifting Competition
    • Drifting Demonstrations
    • ¼ mile Drag Race
    • Karting

      • Fun Prix Sound Challenge
      • Race Princess and Prince Search
    • Exhibitions
    • Auctions (in aid of the President Challenge 2008)
    • Countdown Party with Fireworks Display
    • … and more!

    I went there on monday. The $20 spend is a little bit disappointing. The static display in the hall had so little cars, most of the area was empty. It was like a ghost town with no people around. But what I like is the drift demo. I manage to meet Taniguchi san up close. He sign his autograph on my HKS calendar and magazine. It was nice to see him drift in his IS-220Z. Lots of angle and smoke, just like those in video options. The drag race on the other hand had lots of delays, although there were many cars participating. There were evos, rexes, new GT-Rs and a lone RX-7 FC.
