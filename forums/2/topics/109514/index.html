    Hi,

    I got the following in my car:-
    1) RPM 6 points grounding cables.
    2) Pivot Voltage stabilizer
    3) NGK Iridium spark plugs
    4) Air Max 2 (E68 equivalent)
    5) K&N air filter

    And to be frank, the only major different I have is my car FC. It used to be around 10.5km/l, but has risen to 11.5-12km/l now.

    As for power, do feel a little (slightly more responsive) initially but once you get use to it, its nothing.
