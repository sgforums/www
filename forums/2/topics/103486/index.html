    Hi free4ever and TheaterOfDreams

    Yep, it's loads of fun to drive, great at corners.
    And well, for a sub-70K car, it's really decent.
    No regrets gettin it.

    Guess there's enough pros about the car out there.
    So i'll share some of what i feel are the minuses, at least on my car,

    1) Built quality isn't really top notch, there are minor rattles here and there. Esp when closing the doors.

    2) Tranmission really noisy. And the clutch quite clunky too.

    3) Not really suited for high speed cruisin. The rpm's 3800 at approx 120 kmh, though this may be a trade-off for the close ratio gears (?). And the engine's noisy at this rpm.

    So, if you guys are gettin it eventually, so check the cons with the test cars.
    Hope you guys get it, then i'll know at least 2 other owners to exchange all kinds info / lobang with on the car.

    Cheers!
