    To Jurong Hill: Board service 194 from Boon Lay interchange, alight along Jln Ahmad Ibrahim near Jurong Bird Park, walk straight and turn left into Bird Park Drive and turn again, walk up this road called "Jurong Hill".

    To Mount Faber: You can take cable car from Cable Car Tower. Or you can board the following services to the following roads and walk up the hill via the stair near the bus stop.
    Henderson Rd: services 131, 145, 176 and 273
    Telok Blangah Way: services 124, 195, 272 and 605

    Lower Delta Rd (walk via Mount Faber Rd): 65, 85, 124 and 855
