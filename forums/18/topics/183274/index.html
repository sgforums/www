    This was announced 2 months before, but the confirmation just hit, so I'd like to share this with everyone with the taste for the Dark side of electro. I'm thoroughly excited because this represents the first major industrial act to come to Singapore, totally live - we're talking all keys + synth; if anyone saw pseudo echo @ Sembawang festival, the performance would be akin to that level of cohesion. There will also be live visuals

    Rockstar Collective & Home Parties Present:
    INTERLACE (SWEDEN)
    Live in Singapore

    When: 30th March, Thursday, doors open 8pm

    Where: Home Club
    Certainly home of the underground music in singapore:
    B01-01/16 River Walk Galleria Singapore
    (right across from the big white bridge in Boat Quay, the former Mad Monks club)

    How Much: $20

    Support: Saito Nagasaki (Divine9 / AU) - yours Truly

    Embarking on their Phoenix Tour throughout south-east asia, INTERLACE is the first European Electro / Industrial band to conduct a full live tour in this region! Divine 9 from australia was picked to be the first choice for the opening act, with Saito on the decks for both the Singapore and Malaysian shows (April 1st, SAX club, KL)!



    "Interlace have finished working on their new full-length album Imago. It is descibed as a one hour journey of progress and evolution that reflects both inner struggle and harsh reality, culminating in the birth of the clone -the beginning of the second evolutionary phase. This process is interpreted by artists Dave McKean and Teiaiel who collaborated on the cover design."
