    Pom=I(man tone) Chan=I(lady tone)
    Koon=you(man tone) Ter=she/him(lady tone)
    Kau=he/she

    lerng=one Sorng=two Samm=three Si=four Ha=five Hob=six Z=seven
    pai=eight Gau=nine Seep=ten

    Sa wa dee KRUP(man tone)
    Sa Wa Dee KA (lady tone)

    Ser By Dee Mai KRUP=how are you
    Ser By dee Mai Ka=how are you

    As for KRUP is for man n KA is for lady as u see above.
