    If a woman is married, she would wear a ring on the ring finger of her left hand. It is also known as the "heart finger" for it was traditionally believed that there was a vein called vena amoris that directly ran from that finger to the heart, and that a ring worn on that finger is closer to the heart than a ring worn on any other finger.

    The significance of marriage (or engagement) is a vow of lifelong commitment to the partner, and the ring is a symbol of that vow. If she is married, but not to you, it is best you left her alone and not force her to break that vow.
