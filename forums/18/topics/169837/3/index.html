    I think that they most likely used the sense of smell to triangulate the Indians location.

    Given that its night and the original poster said that sight was not one of the senses employed, we have to assume that its pitch black and no one can see anything.

    So how could they smell out the Indians? It's not because they as a race 'smell', rather it was because there were so many of them and they were in a huddle that they sweated (when you huddle in a closed group, you heat each other up faster than if you were just standing alone).     The Indians couldn't smell the Chinese because they weren't huddled and/or there were not enough people to generate that kind of heat to make them sweat.
