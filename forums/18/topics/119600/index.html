    U will need to obtain a permit from Singapore Police Force. Upon your return, you will need to show the permit to the Immigration & Checkpoints Authority officers (more commonly known by public as Customs) stationed at the Airport for their clearance. If you do not have clearance from SPF and is found to bring in such items, you might be detained and hand over to SPF for investigations. Anyway you will have to inform the airlines when you bring those items on board anyway.
