    One day, there was this man who spoke to the media.

    A day in the last quarter of year 2003,
    CT: Year 2004 will be a better year for all singaporeans, given the
    rise in export and manufacturing indexes in our last 3 months of
    2003.....
    HL: Yeah, yeah. That's look promising. Year 2003 is a worst year with
    SARS and wars spoiling the world's market.....

    Another day in the first quarter of year 2004,
    HL: I'm so sorry. What I had said last year dos not look good this
    year. Blame it in the bird flu. We have no control over such an
    epidemic.....

    it will be a good 2003 but again due to SARS, it crippled.....right?
