    THE BEACH HOUSE PARTY!
    Two years on from the party that even Volcano Eyjafjallajokull couldn’t stop, Hed Kandi is back in Singapore!

    16th July 2010, be prepared for an experience like never before. Set in the cool evenings at Azzura (Sentosa), we bring you an Ibiza style beach party featuring Hed Kandi specially flown in from the UK.

    Expect the unexpected when Hed Kandi returns to Singapore!
    Starting at 4pm in the afternoon the globally renowned luxury house music brand will be showcased poolside at Singapore's largest beach resort, AZZURA BEACH CLUB.

    Think of sipping rejuvenating cocktails while watching the sunset, and relaxing in the poolside sun beds all culminating into heaps of fun as day turn to night!
    The talented and flamboyant Hed Kandi performers will be sure to give this party its groove by playing only the sexiest house music from around the world.

    The glitz of Miami, the extravagance of Vegas and the vibe of Ibiza are fused together through soulful and uplifting house music, a flamboyant saxophonist and percussionist plus spectacular performers. Come and join the glitterati of Singapore at Sentosa Beach, as Hed Kandi at AZZURA BEACH CLUB is surely the place to be seen, poolside, this summer.

    Hed Kandi get set to transform the stunning surroundings of Azzura into the ultimate hands-in-the-air house music experience.
    The music induces so many hands in the air moments you’d think everyone’s shoulder joints had seized up. It's wicked fun and you're guaranteed a great evening! Get ready to groove Kandi style.
