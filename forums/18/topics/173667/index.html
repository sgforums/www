Lust of the Succubus

  • Ashiene
    11 Jan 06, 18:29
    Hi I just finished writing this poem:

    Black was the color of her long straight hair
    Red was the glow of her eyes
    Lust was what controlled her instincts
    As the Succubus prowled the dark streets in the night

    She wished for neither gold nor fame
    But for the man who slept in the small house
    Quietly she moved
    Yet heavily she breathed

    In the dim light of the full moon
    Her slim and slender shape was revealed
    In the semi-darkness she crawled up the walls
    As the man in the room stirred

    She reached the side of his bed
    And the man moved a little
    His eyes still closed
    His mouth open

    Undressing the sleeping man
    The succubus breathed in his scent
    Ah how handsome he was
    She thought

    Lust overcoming caution she reached down
    And as she straddled the man he seemed to groan
    Her hand caressed his muscled body
    But the man only snored

    As the dawn broke the man stirred once more
    And she leaned forward to kiss him
    The succubus turned to leave
    And gave the sleeping man one final glance
