Agree?

    2) A good number of FI's posts do spark off some unwanted and unrelated posts by other forumites, in the sense that the topic in duscussion turn into crap threads, talking about something totally different altogether, deviating from the subject originally intended, such that it almost becomes like a chit chat session between several regular forumites. I have seen this in so many threads. Should that happen? I would think not. I particularly like to read Aunt Agony, where even though the posts are cheeky and sometimes really outrageous, THEY ARE STILL RELEVANT TO THE TOPIC. There are of course some totally irrelevant posts, but it is not as numerous as in BAR. IMHO, what makes BAR so difficult and unpleasant to read is because whole pages can contain totally irrelevant and unmeaningful posts. Such posts should IMHO go into CHIT CHAT, and not BAR. I think CHIT CHAT or KPKB is the perfect place to crap and not BAR I feel, since BAR has a more sensitive, and probably more serious theme.

    3) I really appreciate some of the forums though, especially Singapore Buses, Speakers corner, where the moderators really ensure that whatever is posted is related. There can be personal flames, but the subject matter is hardly deviated, as I believe most people who post want serious and constructive discussion. I see no flaming in Singapore Buses, maybe because of the nature of the topic, where everything that is posted is factual. As for Speakers Corner, everything is on the other hand is more on opinion, and thus the common flames. In BAR, there is a little of both, so the mod can actually exercise some flexibility in deciding what is suitable and what is not.

    4) I have to ask this: Why is there such an obsession with forum ratings and number of posts generated? This I completely do not understand: hope someone will enlighten me.

    5) Coming back to the review of FireIce, I feel without her, this BAR forum would not be as lively as it is. But well, is it lively for the right reason? (right reason being that sexual topics are discussed with maturity, since BAR is the established forum to talk about sex issues). The fact that there are some forumites who post only similes, +1, X1, etc....how do such posts contribute to the relevancy of the topic?

Because by saying that, it defeats the purpose of having a discussion - you are not welcoming alternative views. Then what is the use of having a forum? Stand up to scrutiny by counterarguing with relevant and rational points, if one does not agree with a certain argument. Make responsible posts, one that does not incite flaming.

For example, sgboy2004 wants war with Ah Ma. I feel something like this would be perfectly acceptable in CHIT CHAT or KPKB, but not BAR.
