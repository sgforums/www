    A good place to chill out and meetup place for Car Clubs
    Try Swinging by for weekly gatherings or even for some good meals too.

    Here's the Address...
    RedLine Cafe, Lounge & Gallery
    200 Upper Thomson Road #01-04 Thomson Imperial Court
    Singapore 574424
    Tel: 6253 1321

    We are located diagonally opposite Thomson Road Long House or next to Thomson Community Centre

    Opening hours are as follow:-

    Sunday to Thursday - 12pm to 12am
    Friday to Saturday - 12pm to 3am

    We serve finger food, coffee, icy cold drinks and main course.
    We screen movies on cars like F&F, Initial D etc.
    We screen Formula One races and World Cup soccer matches
    We provide Fashion magazines, Motherhood magazines and car magazines for your reading pleasure.

    *Free Parking at the Public Carpark after 5pm.
