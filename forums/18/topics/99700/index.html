    Henna tattoo is a tattoo made by crushing (with a little water), the leaves of the henna plant. The crushed mixture is then placed (in elaborate designs) on the body and left untouched for at least an hour. When the mixture is washed off the body part, the stain remains on the body part and acts like a tatto. Usually can last up to a week, maybe more - depending on the quality of henna leaves used.
