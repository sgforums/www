    where you guys go after pontang from work, lets swap ideas

    The problem is not how to pontang but after you pontang what to do and how to keep out of sight.

    Personally if I sometimes have time in the afternoons and feel sleepy or dun feel like working I will go to Badminton Hall Health Center at Geylang, not many ppl know this place, The facilities + massage(non-private open concept) is abt 60-80.

    But you don't have get the massage which sux anyway. Personally I prefer just to use facilities abt $30+.

    For $30 you can order all you want to eat from the daily menu like hokkien mee, bee hoon, char kuay teow, fried rice or drink all the fresh fruit juice or coffee you want, eat all the snacks like kuey chicken pie or curry puff they sometimes have, they also provide all languages daily news papers & business times, magazines, her world etc ... TV room where you can sleep all day long or watch movies from their movie library all day long, they have Hot Pool, Cold Pool, Jacuzzi, Whirl Pool, Steam and Sauna.

    They even have Facial, foot massage, even Professional turkish back rub(guy) but have to pay extra for these services.

    I normally get in before lunch check in my wallet into their safe, stash my clothes into personal locker change to bath robe and slippers provided.

    Then I'll take a dip into the hot pool, cold pool, jacuzzi, steam, sauna.

    Then after I soak enuf I get out change again to a new bath robe and go to the canteen order makan, after dat go to the living room read newpapers or magazines check out news on TV. Then after a while soak again, then makan again ard 2pm, then go to TV room watch movies or sleep by ard this time the place will be packed with fellow pontangers & many will be snoring already, ard 6 feel a bit famished makan again then soak after dat bath properly change clothes and leave, rch home ard 7:30pm just nice
