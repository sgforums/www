    12/22/2005 08:00 PM - Home Club
    B01-01/16 River Walk Galleria, Singapore, ,SG - FREE! email info@divine9.net for invites

    This December promises a barrage of cutting-edge electro rock with the introduction of groundbreaking Australian act Divine 9 alongside local Drum and Bass Veterans Vortex and the Exitmusik Crew. The latter collective have been responsible for an entire plethora of sharp sets in defining local events such as Subversive and even playing alongside Goldie during his recent sojourn. This will certainly be no exception on the 22nd of December, when our junglist comrades rally to bring you the fanatics fusing dance and live music to a higher state!
    Support: Vortex, Dimbob ,S-tactics
    Presented by Exitmusik, Home Events and Outbreed
