Apart from the corrupted cops you see today in TVs, esp HK shows, they made a impression in my head after hearing the stories. Just one of them to share. Back in the 70s or 80s. It's told that several gang fights in SG exist back then, laws were there but still fights exist where bloodbath is often shed whereby the common "Parang" is used.     Infact i could share the exp in DB. Those who went thru NS would somehow have a disliking to MPs( Military Po****). But they're not that bad actually except they "book" (the term used when they take out their handy red books)you when you're performing disorderly. Back in DB, you see another face which is hidden to most army personnel, their compassionate side. Known to many offenders who sat behind bars, those who guard the cells are quite amicable. They chat with you, sometimes entertain "ah bengs" nonsense to an extend... but only some not all.

    Life in the cell (which is abt twice the size of a 5room HDB flat toilet), was simple. Writing letters which is once a month (can't really recall), exercising, reading books (in cell), sleeping and carrying sandbags(it's a term whereby all inmates fall-in and do quick march around the basketball court with Helmet, Full Backpack, t-shirt & shorts, army boots and carrying sandbags). Breakfast would be hefty (a loaf of 7 piece white bread), egg, sausage/chicken patty. It's kinda funny when i used to this. Everytime after the loafs of bread i would get too bloated and saved the eggs for later snacks. Put it up on the window (which is what we call "slit" cos it's a small horizontal slit to view the outside world). Oh and of course in the wee hours of the morning... you could smell the pungent smell of fertilisers from the chicken farms away from there. Gosh, it's darn pungent i tell you, esp in the early morning when u r half awake.

    What i heard now is that the exercising part which is inside the cell now is forbidden and less of the quick march. They get less opportunity to expose their eyes to the outside world now. For reasons i can't disclose. Lol. Well don't have to get me PM you, i really can't... i'm putting myself in a situation to be arrested for disclosing military info lol. Well that's for DB.
