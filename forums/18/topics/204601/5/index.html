The man claimed that it was his third letter, but the woman said she has received only two. The Malay Mail reported that the police have classified the case as molestation as there was no evidence of rape.

    Kajang district police chief Mohd Nor Hakim told Malaysia's Sun newspaper that he advised the woman to lodge another report with the original copies
    of the letters she received.

    He explained that when the woman lodged her initial report on Tuesday, she had handed over only photocopies of the letters to the district police
    headquarters. The investigating officer was also not in at the time. The woman's husband told reporters that he was disappointed that the
