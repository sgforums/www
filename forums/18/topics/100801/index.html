Judge bans sexy party sweets

  • kiri
    2 Nov 04, 18:21
    A judge has banned a Brazilian teenager from having snacks in the shape of sexual organs at her birthday party.

    The sweets, especially made for the 15-year-old's party, were in the shape of penises, vaginas, bottoms and breasts.

    The teenager's mother decided to consult the local court in Mossoro after a friend warned her that she could get into trouble.

    And Judge Fatima Maria Lima ruled that the sweets should not to be served at the party, saying they would embarrass guests.

    She said the teenager's mother could face two years in prison if she served the sweets and told Terra Noticias Populares: "Those sweets would even embarrass adults!"
