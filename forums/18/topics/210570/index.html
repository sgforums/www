    -- says upset neighbour of 3 women moving around bra-less in HDB flat in opposite block
    CAN you believe it? Every morning not one, not two, but three women have been strutting around topless in an HDB flat.
    16 September 2006

    CAN you believe it? Every morning not one, not two, but three women have been strutting around topless in an HDB flat.

    Their antics have upset some of their neighbours in a block at York Hill, off Chin Swee Road.

    A Shin Min Daily News reader said the women looked like they are in their 20s.

    Two are slim, while the third is well-built.

    They are believed to be foreigners.

    The female resident, living in the opposite block, said she was shocked at what she saw when she looked out of her window one morning.

    'Through their kitchen window, I saw one of them walking around topless,' she said.

    'I just could not believe my eyes!'

    A few seconds later, another two topless women joined the first woman.

    'All three had nothing on and went about their activities as if it was the most natural thing to do - from chatting to brushing their hair in front of the mirror, and brushing their teeth and so on.'

    This went on for almost three hours.

    The reader said she was not certain if the women were totally naked.

    'But how can anyone do something like that?'

    ROUTINE

    Since that first incident, walking around the flat topless has become a routine for the three women, said the reader.

    'Often it happens on Sundays between 10am and 1pm.

    'Sometimes it is one woman, sometimes two,' she said.

    Acting on the tip-off, the Shin Min Daily News team staked out the unit and witnessed one woman undressing before walking around the flat.

    The woman, who was wearing a bathrobe, had walked into the kitchen to put out a pair of panties to dry outside the window.

    She then left the kitchen and when she returned, she was topless.

    She proceeded to brush her teeth, and then raised both hands to tie her hair, all this while her breasts were in full view.

    Another neighbour said the women had moved into the flat early this year.

    It was believed that they had rented the unit from a male odd-job labourer.

    'I am not sure what they are working as, but they usually return home at about 2am,' said the neighbour.

    A third neighbour said she had once seen a male driver waiting for one of the women at the carpark.

    Last night, the New Paper spoke to some residents in the block opposite the one in which the three women lived.

    None of them seemed to be aware of the women's antics.[/b]
