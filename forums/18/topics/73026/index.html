    I and my gal have been loyal Hotel81 customers. Have been patronising their branches since last year..

    If u feel that there's a stigma bringing ur gal to budget hotels in Geylang, there are a number of Hotel81 branches in other parts of Singapore.

    My personal favourite is Hotel81 @ Bencoolen. Its the newest branch and its quite high class. Been there a number of times. Used it for 2 hours quickie or overnight rendezvous. Another good one is at Chinatown. Very convenient as well. Other Hotel81 branches are at Balestier and Lavender.

    Charges are as follows:

    Hotel81 @ Bencoolen and Chinatown: $30 for the 1st 2 hours and $10 for the next two hours. Need to pay $60 upon checking in. Will get back your $30 upon checking out after 2 hours.

    Hotel81@ Balestier and Lavender: $20 for 1st 2 hours and $10 for the next one hour. Need to pay $40 upon checking in.

    For day/night stay, its $69 for the four branches at Hotel81 that I've been mentioning. I think the rest shd be ard $49 - $69 range. There's a peak period as well during public holiday eve and on Saturday.

    Disclaimer: I am not a staff of Hotel81. All the above rates are through my experience. I've only tried Hotel81 so far. Perhaps Fragrance Hotel might be better. Will be trying out soon!
