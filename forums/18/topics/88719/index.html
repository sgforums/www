    THERE was a nip in the air as two policemen in plain clothes rode up to an abandoned factory on the outskirts of Penang.

    But it was certainly not cold enough to make a car shiver.

    But that was what met their eyes, as they parked their motorcycle not far from a Kancil. The car appeared to shake vigorously.

    Curious, the policemen inched their way towards the car.

    They deduced the reason for the car's movements as they peered through its windows. A young couple was having a romp.

    Startled, a girl leapt from the back seat and drove off in all her naked glory.

    She had not noticed the parked motorcycle in front of the car. She rammed into it and the motorcycle got stuck to the car. But she just drove on.

    The policemen had, however, managed to note down the registration number of the car, reported the Star.

    For them their morning walk last Saturday began a bit early. They located their bike and the car about 6km away.

    Not much is known about what happened to her boyfriend. Well, he probably got a free ride.

    Using the car's number, the police tracked down the woman and summoned her to the police station.

    Sure enough, she was given a dressing down when she appeared at the Bukit Mertajam police district headquarters the next day.

    The woman, believed to be waiting for admission into a local university, was released on Monday after her and her boyfriend's statements were recorded.
