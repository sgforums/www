    Boy falls ill during investigation into porn VCD at school
    By Derek Cher, Channel NewsAsia

    SINGAPORE : An investigation into pornography at Kent Ridge Secondary School has taken an unfortunate twist -- one of the 17 boys hauled up by the principal has ended up in hospital.

    The drama began after assembly on Monday, when 17 boys from a secondary 2 class were questioned about a pornographic VCD that had been confiscated from one of them.

    It is believed they were passing the disc around.

    The boys were made to sit along the corridor outside the school office and write down their side of the story. They all missed their recess and did not eat for seven hours.

    As a result one student had to be hospitalised.

    He suffers from a rare disease in which the walls of his intestines start to fuse together if he goes without food or water.

    It is understood the boy is still under observation at the hospital.

    The principal of the school, who declined to be interviewed on camera, said in an e-mail to Channel NewsAsia, "It was an oversight on our part that we had neglected to let the boys go for a break during the period of investigation. We are sorry that a boy has taken ill as a result. We have visited the boy at hospital to check on his condition."

    The school has also involved the parents of the boys and the police in its investigation.

    Three pornographic VCDs were handed over to the police, who detained and questioned a 14-year-old student.

    He was later released on bail of S$2,000. - CNA
