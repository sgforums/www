    A dad at 88 - and he wants more kids

    AN 88-YEAR-OLD Indian farmer, who has never heard of Viagra, has become the father of a baby boy. He has sex daily and wants more kids, The Times of India reported yesterday.

    'I don't want to live to 100 but, as long as I live, I should be able to enjoy sex,' Mr Virmaram Jat, who lives in a village in the Barmer district in the western desert state of Rajasthan, said.

    The prosperous farmer with a flowing white beard said he takes long walks daily and has been drinking fresh camel milk since childhood.

    His latest wife - his third - is 45 years younger than him and delivered male twins last month, but only one boy survived.

    He celebrated the birth of the surviving son with a feast for villagers and said he will try for more children.
