    Location : Bintan, Kelong Resturant (Inside Nirwana Garden Resort)

    How to get there : Take 45min boat ride from Tanah Merah Ferry Terminal to Bintan. Take bus to Nirwana Garden Resort. Have a stroll around the white sandy beaches of ManaMana. Then take an internal shuttle bus to Kelong Resturant.

    The view : Kelong linked to the main land, so you can to enjoy nice sea breeze, good sea view as well as a very nice sunset if you time it right. Service is very good, as well as the food. Price is reasonable.

    Total cost : Boat Trip + Dinner : Less then $200. And a very happy gf/spouse.
