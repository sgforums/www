    A Japanese adult video (AV) production house was reported to have filmed a pornographic film -- starring actress Aoki Misora -- at various locations across Singapore, including the Marina Bay Sands (MBS) hotel.

    In the film, they can be seen taking a dip in the swimming pool at the Sands SkyPark.

    The reel-life couple was also filmed touring around Singapore and visiting places such as Bugis, Shenton Way and Chinatown and savouring local Chinese delicacies such as Hainanese chicken rice.

    These scenes are punctuated by the couple engaging in sexual romps inside what appears to be a hotel room at the Marina Bay Sands.

    The report also mentioned that this film sparked a debate among local netizens who had seen the film, as some expressed concerns of this video being mistaken for a promotional video for tourism in Singapore.
