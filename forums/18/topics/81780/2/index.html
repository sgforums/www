    This happened some time back during my BMT at Pulau Tekong.

    This coincidently (or not?) happened during the period of Ching Ming (a lot of incence was burned on the island during this period). My bunk was directly opposite the back of the canteen. There was a big Chinese altar there, presumably used by the canteen vendors on the island.

    It was one of those weary nights after our training. We were all lying in our bed as it was already time for lights off. As i was trying to fall asleep, I noticed a dark shadow by the window of my bunk. All the lights have already been switched off, so I could not really see whether it was actually just one of my instructors checking on us. However, I found it strange that the shadow was there for quite some time before it just disappeared. I also did not hear any of my instructors' usual footsteps. I did not want to think too much, so I just ignored it and soon fell asleep.

    ......to be continued

    Continuation...

    That night I had the eeriest and weirdest nightmare (or encounter?) of my entire life. I had fallen asleep after the 'shadow' incident but somehow felt the strange presence of a white, lady figure looking into my bunk from the very same window. What spooked me was the fact that my body suddenly felt so heavy and numbed. However, I managed to somehow turned my body away from the window. I dare not face the figure! But I could sense that this 'figure' was drawing nearer and nearer to me!

    Suddenly, I sensed as if something heavy had landed on the blind side of my bed as I can clearly feel that my bed had sunk deeper on my blind side. Deep inside me knows that this was actually that 'figure'! My body just froze as I felt that this figure was somehow drawing nearer to my neck! By then, I could feel that it was sucking away at my neck!
    My mind and body were numbed and I could only manage to say whatever prayers that I could still remember. After awhile, my bed felt 'lighter' as i realised/concluded that the 'figure' must have gone away.
    I soon fell back to sleep.
