    Hi, my name is Beng Huat and here is my story. After graduating with a diploma in Civil Engineering, I went on to a full-time private university During my University days, I took Japanese as a foreign language subject as part of my syllabus. Finally I graduated with a degree finance. After that I went on to work at a Japanese builders and developers company evaluating and procuring of builders materials. Finally with a stable job and a good income, I married my polythenic sweetheart of 8 years and is going to be a father in 10 months time. But here was where all the problems begin………

     

    Because of my hardwork and my knowledge in Japanese/English translations , I was selected by the Head Office in Japan to become the personal assistant to our new incoming GM who was from Japan. I was very happy that head office noticed me. On top of that, there was a big salary jump and  I was also given a company car. My first assignment was to make all the necessary arrangement and accommodations for my new boss and his family in Singapore. Finally, the day arrived and I drove my new company car to the airport to pick up my boss and his family.
