    The general behaviour of a forum depends heavily on the moderators.

    Some forums have very strict moderators who have no tolerance for such nonsense. Though they diligently keep the posts clean, politically-correct and newbie-friendly, the atmosphere in such forums tends to be rather sombre, subdued and posters often practise self-censorship on the articles they post.

    I'd say the moderators here are more open-minded and "laissez-faire " in style. While it encourages active posting, it also inadvertently invites the dingbats, along with the ubiquitous clones and trolls as well. That may not necessarily be a bad thing considering the society that Singaporeans are already living in. Some of the acid remarks can be witty as well, so if you can take it, just laugh them off as mindless entertainment.

    All in all, it is a two-edged blade and there is no forum that pleases everybody. There are so many forums and message boards out there, just pick whichever is more suited to your tastes.
