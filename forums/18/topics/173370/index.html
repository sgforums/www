    its a must watch 2.... its a weird triller movie.

    Unlike the usual, everyday slasher, this doesn't contain a psycho killer, running around killing the teenagers. Instead, what causes death is a force of nature, like earthquakes, for example. Imagine the grim reaper does exist, only that he doesn't appear as a human shape, but as a dark shadow. And imagine 'death' killing people in a certain order, by a design. This movie is based on that thought. And the thought that you may be able to cheat death, by figuring out the design, and keeping it from happening. I know a lot of people see this as a B movie, but I think you just need to suspend disbelief to enjoy this. Of course, some of the death scenes are a little unlikely, but most of them are stuff that happens every day. It's interesting to think about, and this movie does inspire some further thought, if you can suspend disbelief for the duration of the this movies run time.
