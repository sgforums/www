    I MISS TECHNO CLUBS LIKE RUSH / ANGELS REBORN / SPARKS.

    it was so much more fun than the r&b hiphop clubs i go to nowadays like MOS n butter factory etc

    1. nice melody fast eurodance techno is the real dance music. some songs the beats and melody n singing so fast and nice to listen n dance! techno clubs all the guys dance n move more than the girls, footwork and the hands dancing para style damm nice. r&b clubs almost all the guys sway or moving only and no hand moves wan

    2. techno clubs had a lot of hokkien chants and gang poems, nice to listen. the dj will also start and alternate lines with the crowd this one for eg: fire fire burn so high. kaninabeichaoche.ebye! fire fire burn so low. kaninabeichaoche.ebye! old macdonald had a farm. e i e i o! popeye the sailor man. poo poo! the dif groups of gangs will also shout their poems and show their hand signs in the air.

    3. younger and more fun and interesting crowd of 99% bengs and lians. 80% of friday nights at sparks will have FIGHT the last time i went rush also fight cos somebody look at a gangsters gf. then whole group punching and when the bouncer came then they punched him ALSO until his mouth bloody and the floor bloody. then police come in n on lights then just nice 3am we go home!

    4. techno clubs so many 16-20 yr old chio lians and yandao bengs nice to see! not like r&b clubs crowd older n more boring

    funny to hear chio lians shouting at ppl ai sio kan mai? wo si qin cai! ai si ai! ta bi bo sor zai! toh bai ki! toh bo bai ki sio hunki! or kaninahbeichaoche.ebye!
