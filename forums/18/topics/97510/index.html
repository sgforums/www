    Susan Sarandon has been asked to pose naked by Playboy - at the age of 58.

    But the mother-of-three turned the magazine down because she says it would be embarrassing for her sons.

    Susan told Access Hollywood, "They have been talking to me for years. I got a lot of lunches out of Playboy.

    The Oscar winning actress has two boys, John, 15, and Miles, 12, from her marriage to actor Tim Robbins as well as a 19-year-old daughter.
