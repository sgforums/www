    # Two Norwegians set up sute showing them having sex
    # Plan to donate part of proceeds to 'green group'

    WITH so many people surfing the Net for pornography these days, two young Norwegian environmentalists thought it would be a good, albeit controversial, idea to exploit this for a good cause.

    They have created a pornographic website in an attempt to raise money for various organisations working to protect the environment.

    'In light of the turn things are taking in the world today, I think that this is a very honourable way of raising money for environmental causes,' said the 27-year-old creator of the website.

    Visitors to the site pay US$15 ($25.50) a month to view explicit videos of the creator making love to his 21-year-old girlfriend.

    The two plan to donate most proceeds from the site to different environmental organisations.

    HESITANT TO TAKE PART

    However, they admit their organisations of choice are still hesitant to participate in the project.

    Meanwhile, the Norwegian Employment Service unwittingly supported the initiative when it allowed him to take a break from his job-hunt while he developed his website project, which the agency believed was for selling T-shirts online.

    With only about 200 subscribers, the site has not yet made a profit, the website's creator admitted.

    But he said he hoped that more publicity would help attract enough web-surfers to allow him to donate US$10 from each subscription to environmental organisations. - Wire services.
