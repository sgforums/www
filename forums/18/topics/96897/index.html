    The story is basically about the mother and teenage daughter died together in a bus accident. However, the mother's soul was being transferred into the daughter's body; while the daughter's soul has disappear.
    Thus, because the body is the daughter's body and the soul's the mother, the father and mother won't be able to lead a normal husband and wife life. at first, the mother is trying to be a good wife and housewife and meanwhile trying to live the life of her daughter's by attending to school etc.
    But, eventually, the father and mother just can't cope with this kind of life anymore.
    In the end, the mother told the father that she is going to disappear and saying that their daughter's soul is coming back to her own body.
    Thus, for the next few years, the mother has continued to live as the teenage daughter without the father knowing.
    In the ending, the "daughter" get married to a guy and on the day of marriage, only then the father realised that the "daughter" is actually still his wife all the time.
    After watching the show, I have a mixed feeling. One feeling is that the "mother"
