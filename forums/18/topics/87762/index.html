    Red wine read meat... white wine white meat? True, but I don't care.

    How to know which year it is by tasting? No such thing lah!! Only possible if you are familiar with the produce from all region every year. Which means you have keep track of the wine industry and tasted all the produce each year. Not sure even if the pro is doing that.

    Longer it's kept... better it is? Not true. Only that in some years where the harvest is very good quality, people keep it until there is a good event to want to drink it. So, the wines from the good years are consider as vintage wine and are collectibles.
