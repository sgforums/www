    past participle after 'is' and 'are', 'was' and 'were'.

    present tense - bear
    past tense - bore
    past participle - born/ borne
    simply means to carry, give birth to
    related sentence style
    "she bears him a child"
    "the virus is air-borne"
    "we were born under the same blood line"

    "children are...." children is the plural of child (i.e son/ daughter)
    "a child is...." child is the singular of children
