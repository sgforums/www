    The teenager was on home leave from his detention when he raped his sister in their flat in 2006.

    He did it while his mother was sleeping in the bedroom and his mentally challenged younger brother was playing in the living room.

    But should he commit any further offences, he will receive a longer jail term.

    "You will come out an old man," said Justice Choo.

    He also said that while behind bars the teenager should attend offence specific rehabilitative programmes, "not only for his own sake but for the sake of others."
