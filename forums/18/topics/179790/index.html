    World: Vascerii Gladius
    Star System: Ana-Tacticulus
    Galaxy: Milky way or Yaj'Kul'har
    People: Mas'Ka
    Emperor: Sik'Tahu

    Formed 9.2 million years ago, we have studied much about life on our planet. I have documented a few of our most intelligent plant and animal life here.

    Sarkitira sliders:
    Strange insect-like creatures that lurk beneath the surface of the water on our planet. One must always blow at the water to frighten away a Sarkitira slider before one drinks the water that so gives us life on Vascerii Gladius.

    Hava'Bora Plant:
    A very tall tree-like plant, this semi-intelligent behemoth towers above all else. Its red and yellow leaves are very nutritious and can be made into a paste we call Fogeri'Ha, a yellowish-white semi-solid. Also, this paste is mixed with water to form the Gonv'Bohul, or the Liquid of Life that is carefully placed in sacks for newborns on our planet to soak the fluid into their skin to last an entire lifetime without food.

    Yol'Pukar:
    A small species of animal-men who live near the water, eating the sarkitira sliders and Maha-Gori'tol worms.

    Maha-Gori'tol:
    Worms that live in the sand at the edge of the water. They dig deep and feast on the roots of the Hava'Bora Plant.

    Social Life:
    Toga (Sperm) and Yohi (Egg) are fertilized in a Birth Machine in the center of the city. Then, the newborn matures rapidly and when ready to be born, is warped into existance at in a pod. Newborns are then put in metallic sacks with the nutritious Gonv'Bohul that gives them life and enough nutrition for them rest of their infant life. Once out of the sack, he never eats again. He is brought to the Bio-mechatronics engineering facility where cybernetic implants are fused into his body. He has become a biomechanism.
