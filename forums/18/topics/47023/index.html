    some ppl prefer not to use artificial contraception but choose instead to have sex only at the times in the menstrual cycle when a woman is least likely to become pregnant. this means avoiding intercourse for about 4 or 5 days before and after ovulation (see below). but since its impossible to know in advance precisly when ovulation will take place, these so-called 'safe' periods are not totally safe, only slightly less risky than other times of the month.

    day 1 - 5: menstruation ('safe' days)
    day 6 - 9: 'safe' days
    day 10 - 14: unsafe days
    day 15: ovulation
    day 16 - 20: unsafe days
    day 21 - 28: 'safe days'
