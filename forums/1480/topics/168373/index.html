    Pls do not mix STAR and SOF up.

    STAR is part of the Singapore Police Force. The personnel in STAR are Police Officers.
    SOF are part of Singapore Armed Forces. They are military men.

    STAR is essentially the SWAT team of Singapore. If there is war. The commandos will of cos fight the war. Not STAR. STAR is used to enforce the law in high risk situations. For example, Hijackings of bus, airliners. Hostage situations. So on and so forth. Star is heli-ops capable. And can also handle ops at sea. They are cross trained.

    Ok, some may wonder why have STAR when we have SOF? THis is because. STAR members are police officers. The main difference between a STAR member and a SOF member is, a STAR member has arresting powers. SOF members do not.
    STAR is formed to enforce the law. SOF is to protect our country.
