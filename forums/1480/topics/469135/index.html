    Hi there emilangry,

    Congratulations, you are becoming as PNSF officer.

    Here are some tips for enlisting,

    On Enlistment Day, you will be taking oath of allegiance to become as a Full time NS men in 152 intake as the 150 intake will passing out on 21 May 2013. Other than that, there will be Field Instructor to train you on drills and PT to prepare you 3 IPPTs aft 8 wk of physical training.

    14 Weeks Police Officer Basic Course

    Within 14 wks of POBC, you will learn new things such as

    1) Shooting  - Using of Revolver
    2) PDT (Police Defensive Tactics) - Using of T-Baton, Handcuff, Verbal Commands & Unarmed Tactics.
    3) Life Saving
    4) Criminal Laws
    5) Drills - Turnout & Bearing
    6) Scenario Based Training - Role Play
    7) First Aid Course
    8) PSOC - Police Standard Obstacle Course.

    All these will cover you in 14 weeks time. Life as a trainee is very fast in there. Once you are trainee, don't forget your 3 important items must be with you all the way. You will know it later.

    Every 2 weeks, you have to shave your heads and exchange linens.

    Cheers....
