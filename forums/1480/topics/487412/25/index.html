  • Chawi
    27 Oct 15, 19:20

    @hello0605 I think the most up to date forum discussion on JPO is JPO 2014 forum. Not sure whether are there any discussion on your question but as far as I know, if you are talking about from one org to another under home team, it could be done. To most people, its like changing uniform but in actual fact, your roles and responsibilities also changes and even your appointment. I heard that some people does that but the catch is that, if you do this, you cannot undo and you have to stick to what you have changed.

    The redeploment to another org is not as simple as you think, and it requires an extensive amount of approval. So think carefully whether making a switch to home team service is worthwhile because its not just about exceeding challenges everytime. As a PO, the job scope could get routinal and monotonous after awhile and exceeding challenges might not be an antidote everytime because of the complexity of the things PO do and in extreme case, it could be overwhelming. I think it would be more important to have a keen interest in policing but I do not want to scrutinise your passion in becoming PO as everyone is different and hold different motivation and interest towards pursuing a career in SPF.

    All the best in your application!
