    Thanks!

    My dialogue to psych consultation was really fast. I think it was 3 days after the dialogue, I got a call for the psych consultation for the next week.

    After the psych consultation, it took about 2 weeks for the call to schedule me for the panel interview.

    I recall my dialogue interview was rather unpredictable. No matter what I answered, the SUPT keep frowning at me. I was rather worried but I did not regret my answers. The questions asked were why do you want to join SPF, tell me about yourself, what is one challenge you foresee in the SPF.

    I actually answered 'I was inspired by the TV shows and movies' when she asked me why do I want to join SPF. But of course, I gave a more elaborate answer, a more convincing answer rather than it being a shallow cause for me to join the police.

    I think the best is, be yourself and prepare for any of the interviews. Be confident of your answers :)
