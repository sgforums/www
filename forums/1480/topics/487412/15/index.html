    Originally posted by fren22:

    Hi, I have just received email notification to attend the selection assessment. It mentioned to be a full day thing may i know for this SA , there is no interview?

    Hi fren22, yeah there isn't any interview per se done during the SA. Basically the day is split into 2 parts where the morning part is doing paper tests (VRT etc) and questionnaire. The afternoon is where they will brief you in the documents you are supposed to hand up (according to what they mentioned in the email and some others not mentioned like bank account statements) and the physical body check.

    Hope this helps you and anyone else who is attending.
