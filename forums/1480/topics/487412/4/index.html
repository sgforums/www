    In general, the process to be offered as Senior Police Officer is a lengthly one.

    The entire duration from online application to being offered the job and contract signing with vital will take approximately 5-6 months long. This may or may not include time waiting to slot you for the next slot in attachment/ orientation/ C Course. Hence it will even be longer than 6 months.

    It is definitely not a speedy process. If you cannot wait too long, you might want to seek for other job alternatives.

    The waiting time between each stage may last from 3-6 weeks as SPF always receive high volume of applicants each year. The stages to expect are:

    1. Online Application
    2. Selection Assessment
    3. Dialogue with Asst Dir Manpower
    4. Psychologist Clinical Interview
    5. Panel Interview
    6. Medical
    7. Contract signing

    If you are confused about the abbrevations used in this thread, it might be useful to refer to past few posts or even read up the thread for 2014/13. You may even get useful tips and advices from past applicants. And thats if you are truely keen on this job.

    Good luck!
