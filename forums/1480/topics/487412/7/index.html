    Hi @Exvinix, it depends on the order which you would be called in. After you report at the stipulated time, they will get you guys to fill up some forms and also to submit any outstanding documents needed. After which they will  brief you about what to expect if you get the job. (the 9 months stay, rotation after come out from HTA etc). After that, your batch of people will be seated outside the room to wait for your interview. I was from the 1025am batch, some from the 845am batch was still outside when we came out. I came out before 1230. So I believe you should be safe to be able to leave before 2pm.
