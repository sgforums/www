  • ugene87
    13 Jan 15, 18:25

    @Fazfazwuz dialogue basically just chit chat. very relaxed setting. Find out more about your background and why you want to join the force.

    Just done with board interview today. board interview will be more in depth than the dialogue. whatever you say, they probably will ask why/elaborate or ask you to give examples.  
    e.g. if you say you relish opportunity to lead, they will ask you to give example of your experience in any leadership role. 

    I figure the questions they ask can be based on your previous answers as well. So have to be careful not to answer without thinking it through. because you may face even more difficult questions subsequently. example, when i mentioned that i was not able to see any goals in my previous job, they proceeded to asking me what goals do i have in mind in SPF that i want to achieve. 

    Just answer all questions confidently i guess. because i think they want to see whether you really thought through everything about this career and how motivated you are.  =)
