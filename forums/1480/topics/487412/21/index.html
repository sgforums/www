    29 Sep 15, 15:57

    @Zainasaid You can try to look at the previous year thread. They talked about this. I didnt received any request for full body photo. Not sure what is it for.

    @Kunduno Hey, firstly all the best to your upcoming interview. From my experience, they can asked you ANY questions. So, be prepared for that. Try to prepare yourself for any questions by discerning your strengths, motivation and weaknesses. It would be important for you to understand yourself before you reply them. There's no shortcut to this. Also, read up on the current affairs of SPF like its challenges, and what are the future prospect in terms of technological and the current crimes eg. low robberies, but high crimes involving online scams, and this pattern suggest internet is a big driving factor (try to go beyond those observation and point out the underlying factor and what could be done to tackle these issues) My two cents of opinion.

    In general, I would recommend you break down your interview preparation in two parts, yourself (strengths, weakness, motivation) and your understanding about SPF (challenges, technological advances and etc). I hope my experiences might aid you in your preparation. FYI, many people left the force for better career opportunity or for better pay and SPF is aware of that. Most important, you must show that you are very passionate about this career. Show them that you know what you are signning up for and not for the sake of just getting a job. All the best :)

    It feels like a freaking long wait since medical. Days felt like month. Patience game so strong. Jiayou everyone!
