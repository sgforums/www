An amazing but true story of service excellence!

  • Deunamist
    10 Mar 07, 20:52
    Ok, here's an amazing incident which happened to me yesterday....and every part of it is 100% true

    I lost my wallet (containing IC, POSB ATM card, Adult ezlink and Poly ezlink, with $24 in notes and $1.10 in coins) at around 1.30 - 2pm when I took a cab to my secondary school for the Sec 1 camp.

    I didn't realise it until my OC indicated that I would nid identification proof to enter the Home Team Academy (he was driving there with me and one of the NCOs to collect rations at NPCC HQ for the camp). I assumed I either dropped it in the cab or outside school.

    Tried calling Comfort's lost & found hotline throughout the entire ride across the country (school's in Bedok and HQ's in Old CCK). managed to get thru after the car turned into KJE, and made my report. Heng I managed to enter the Academy by sharing pass with my OC, luckily I was wearing my NPCC CI shirt.

    Back to school, after dropping the ration boxes off at the NPCC Room, I walked 500m to the nearest NPP which was Kaki Bukit NPP.

    Here's the rough conversation when I entered the NPP
    *pushes open the door*
    Me: Hi, I'd like to report a lost wallet containing my IC..
    Officer: Are you... *says out my name*
    Me: eh, yah. how did you..
    *Officer (SSgt Foo) opens drawer and takes out my wallet*
    SSgt Foo: oh, my colleague found your wallet somewhere outside and dropped it off here.
    Me: oh...ok... *shocked*
    SSgt Foo: did you receive any call from POSB?
    Me: regarding?
    SSgt Foo: I searched your wallet for a contact number but didn't come across one so I contacted POSB and got them to notify you
    Me: oh no, they didn't (POSB !!!)
    SSgt Foo: Anyway there is $24 in notes, $1.10 in coins, your IC..etc etc

    This went on and I filled in a form and signed on an incident report before SSgt Foo handed back the wallet to a very elated owner

    after grabbing my wallet I quickly zhao and caught the earliest bus back to my unit just in time for dinner and the campfire.

    Here's the most amazing part of the story...

    after campfire we CIs played a prank on the cadets. after the prank was done, I got sms-es from an unknown number and my mum.

    Unknown number: Hi, I m the officer tat return yr wallet 2 u but again had forgotten yr IC. anyway i had went to yr residence and passed it to yr parent. i m sorry about tat

    My mum: Yr IC is returned by SSG Foo pls call him to say thanks urgent!

    of cos I call SSgt Foo to thank him. I didn't expect him to go all the way to my place to return my IC, and I didn't even realise he didn't return my IC after filling in the particulars.

    what an amazing story of service excellence sia.

    I would like to thank SSgt Foo for his help, to the extent of returning my IC at my place!! and also his unnamed colleague who spotted my wallet along the street and brought it to the NPP.
