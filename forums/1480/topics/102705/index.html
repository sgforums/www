    K-9 can be said to be dog unit but they are refining the training now. STAR, dog-unit are sub-units of SOC.

    STAR is a 1 day screening, if u pass then u go for a week or 2 of training cum 1-2 interview, if u pass the interview and the training then u got into STAR to go for a 16-20 week training to learn the basics. then u can/may learn sniping, diving etc etc during your tour of duty.

    once u finish the 16-20 week u are eligible for the few hundred bucks of allowance bonus that will go ontop of your current pay so ur pay is x+bonus. u will need to earn the bonus as there are 3-4 physical training u must pass per year, if u fail then u will not get ur bonus until u pass.

    u must not have colour deficiency or night blindness. degree also cannot be too high per eye. be able to don contact lens for 10-16 hrs without taking it off. cannot have phobia of heights. ability to work as a team and follow orders. gd physical fitness and mental alertness. ability to work under pressure and stressful surroundings. be able to swim rather well and fast.

    note dat u will go for Basic airborne course to earn dat airborne badge
