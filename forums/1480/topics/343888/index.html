    Just a heads-up for some of you who might be interested....

    The SRA (Singapore Rifle Association) has tentatively confirmed a Basic Pistol Course for civilians on Sat & Sun, the 8th & 9th of March 2009 at the National Shooting Center which is located at the end of Old Choa Chu Kang Road.

    Course fee - $500 plus cost of ammo which you buy from the range and firearm rental. Yup, you can actually rent one there. I will post additional details including cost of ammo and pistol, etc soon. Minimum age limit is 18 as per SRA.

    I will be one of the two instructors for the course and the course is pretty intensive even for a basic course. It is geared towards those who might be in interested in getting into the shooting sports, whether casual plinking or dynamic like IPSC (www.ipsc.org) or IDPA (www.idpa.com).

    It is open to the public (there might be some folks from the SAF) BUT I will provide additional information (as soon as I get them) on any additional requirements by SRA for those interested. I expect them to open this to their members first but I know that non-members are allowed.

    As you know, courses like this do not come around very often and the interest for dynamic shooting is on the rise; I have noticed new faces in the shooting sports over the last year or so.

    Please keep in mind that I am currently travelling so any replies to Qs might be delayed due to the difference in time zones.

    Thanks all.

    Hey guys,

    Thanks very much for the support and the interest I am seeing. Here are some additional info that is also now available online at this LINK .

    1. Rental pistols at NSC/SRA - see link for a list of what's available. Rental of pistol is $40 per day.

    2. It is 2 days only, Sat & Sun BUT it's going to be a pretty demanding 2 days. It is a pretty intensive course. The idea is to get your money's worth on such a course.

    3. Registration is on-going. More info at link provided and below.

    4. SAFRA members? Sorry brother. Don't know about that one. Contact info is provided at the link and below. Please contact Marcus from SRA who is in-charge of the course. His email and phone are both given.

    5. Will it be inline with IPSC or IDPA? It will be the necessary basics that you would require to shoot booth. FYI, I am the Area Coordinator for IDPA, the current regional IDPA Certified Safety Officer Instructor and a 2-gun Master by US standards. That said, we shoot all 3 disciplines; IPSC, IDPA and Tactical 3-gun. Ths course will get you started in the right direction especially when it comes to weapons handling and safety. You are guaranteed to see the difference before and after the course.

    5. For all registration and for any other questions about NSC or SRA, please call Marcus at SRA/NSC at 6790-7284 or you can also email him at SRA1862 (AT) singnet.com.sg

    FYI, the second instructor is an associate of mine who was a former senior firearms and tactics instructor for STAR and the Asst Match Director/Chief Range SO for last year's IDPA at the 2007 SOSC. You are guaranteed to be in good hands.

    Feel free should you have any other Qs and mods, thanks for letting us do this.
