    Originally posted by RaTtY8l:

    Hi all

    i hav a qn here. is sending lewd sms or msn from a guy to a gal considered outrage of modesty to a gal? can the gal take the sms or print the msn dialogue and show to police for proof of outrage of modesty?

    The answer is.... No...
    Outraging of modesty (1) doesn't restrict to only women... (2) is defined as a physical outrage of modesty i.e. there must be physical contact for there to be outrage of modesty... even your long fingernail touch the other person's hair and if the person feels his/her modesty was outrage still counts...

    What you're looking for is Chapter 223 section 502 of the penal code, Word or gesture intended to insult the modesty of a woman.

    Whoever, intending to insult the modesty of any woman, utters any word, makes any sound or gesture, or exhibits any object, intending that such word or sound shall be heard, or that such gesture or object shall be seen by such woman, or intrudes upon the privacy of such woman, shall be punished with imprisonment for a term which may extend to one year, or with fine, or with both.

    I find this act too general... This means if you go up to a girl and say Hi, she can claim that you insulted her modesty... luckly this is not a seizable offence...

     

    So in conclusion, Outrage of modesty applies to both men and women and but have the presence of physical contact. Insult of modesty of women applies only to women and must by word, gestures or any other form of messages.
