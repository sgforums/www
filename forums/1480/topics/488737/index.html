Police seek info on missing person Tan Soo Chai

  • QX179R
    14 Jun 15, 14:22

    SINGAPORE - Police are appealing for information on the whereabouts of 58-year-old Tan Soo Chai.

    He was was last seen at Block 409 Ang Mo Kio Avenue 10 on Saturday (June 13).

    Mr Tan was wearing a red polo T-shirt with black pants, grey and black sandals.

    Anyone with information is requested to call the police hotline on 1800-255-0000 or submit information online at www.police.gov.sg/CrimeStopper.
