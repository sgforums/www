    usually, about 3 months [from the date you apply] for them to send u the first letter. this one is for preliminary assessment, ie color-blindness test, iq tests, etc.

    after the initial assessment, then they will send u a letter inviting you for a panel interview, about 1-2 weeks later. after the interview, if you're successful, you'll get a letter arranging for ur medcal checkup within days.

    about 3 weeks later, the results of your medical checkup will be sent to you, and if you're successful the details of the bond-signing session will be sent to you.
