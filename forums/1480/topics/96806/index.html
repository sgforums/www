    I suppose most of you have heard of this right? For those who dosent know...here's the press release from the SPF:

    MEDIA RELEASE 03 OCTOBER 2004

    Fighting At Haig Road

    At 8.30pm, police received a call of a fight at a hawker centre at Haig Road. Upon police arrival, 2 men among a group of 30 foreign workers were seen to be fighting. Police immediately stopped the fight and proceeded to make arrests. Some members of the groups started assaulting the four police officers at the scene using their fists and dustbin.

    One troop of Special Operations Command and the Police K-9 unit were activated to control the situation. A total of 22 men believed to be foreign workers comprising of Sarawakians and Sabahans, in their 20s were arrested on the spot. They were all believed to be involved in the assault of police officers.

    Two officers injured were treated as outpatient. Police are investigating.

    Commander Bedok Police Division, Superintendent Hoong Wee Teck said that assaulting police officer is a serious offence and police will not tolerate such acts. Members of public are advised not to take the law into their own hands.

    MEDIA INFORMATION CENTRE
    PUBLIC AFFAIRS DEPARTMENT
    SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
    3 October 2004 @ 11pm
