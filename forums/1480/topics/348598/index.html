    just to add on... most loans that people undertake, e.g. study loans, housing loans, car loans, are secure loans and do not contribute to your being financially embarassed. the main problem is unsecured loans. examples of these include credit card bills, buying of stuff [e.g. furniture, appliances] on instalments, renovation loans, ah longs aka Unlicensed Money Lenders [UML]...

    in essence, you're doing alright. banks will ensure that you keep up your instalments, so you have nothing to worry about. And yes, do enquire if need be, just to be sure.
