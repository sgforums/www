Stopped & checked by plain clothes police officers

  • Undiscoveredsoul98
    4 Feb 12, 23:36

    Yesterday, after i had bought a pack of cigarette i walked past behind my block towards my bus stop. Suddenly, a black plain car stopped by and a man approached me.

    He asks if Im a local, so i said yes. I thought he was just a passerby asking for directions around that area. Then he showed me his warrant card, confirmed a police officer. The second man did the same thing. The third guy just looked on.

    Then one of them asked me where Im going, i said Im meeting a friend. He checked my bag. My bag only contains the cigarette I bought, my umbrella, my bills and a restaurant vouchers. The other one asked for my IC. I gave it to him, then he talked to someone on the phone, screening me I guess. After that they returned me my IC & they said I can go as they are doing some checks around that area. I asked them if I had done anything wrong. So one of them said no, otherwise I would have been taken away.

    This leave me wondering why a plain clothes officers checked on me. I wouldnt be too worried if its a uniformed ones in police patrol car. Im not sure if they thought Im a underage buying cigarettes but Im 30 years old.
