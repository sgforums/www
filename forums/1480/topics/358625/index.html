      There are significant changes to Certis Cisco and other Auxillary Police Forces (APFs) uniforms. Some noteable changes are

    Belt (while SPF still have their black leather belt, Cisco have changed theirs to brown)
    Cap bagdes and collar lapels ( SPF ones are embroidered while Cisco have metallic ones)
    Uniform, (different shade of blue)
    Peak caps (one brown band on Cisco peak caps)

    They are still special because of their APF status, meaning that they are authorized to provide armed security officers to government organizations as well as private companies. Another know APF is Aetos. And there should be another 1 or 2 more APFs ...

    If I am not wrong, Cisco guards are only a supplement to the police officers whom are also guarding the premises ...
