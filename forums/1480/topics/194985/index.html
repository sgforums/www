    Hi.

    Having been personally involved in several parades of this magnitude in the past where NPCC was the main organiser, I would just like to state that there are specific SOPs pertaining to the handling of firearms and pyrotechnics in place. As is probably the case for all other youth organisations, safety has always taken top priority, and this parade is no different.

    If you would like further details on this, I would suggest that you visit the official NPCC website or directly contact our staff officers (see http://www.npcc.gov.sg/downloads/circulars/npcchq_contact_numbers.pdf)?
