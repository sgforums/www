    for your info, there is direct entry for departments like, Police Coast Guard, Dog Unit, Commercial Affairs Department, Central Narcotic Bureau... and more going for the direct entry thing......

    abt the difference for the 6 mth basic training at TRACOM(Training Command) as compared to army, its diff to explain to u unless u experience army life before....

    basically regardless wat time the dae starts, it will end ard 5.30pm.. and after that will be dinner and OTOT(own time own target) for most of the nights, u will be having every wed off(which is known as lights off, where u book out ard 530-6 and come back abt 9-1030pm the same dae), u will also be able to book out on alternate fridays at abt 530-7pm till 9-1030pm on Sun....

    the training will mostly be split into two halfs, the morning and the afternoon.... so its either u training(which includes arms training, defence tactics, swimming etc) in the 1st half and lessons(law, police procedures etc) in the other half or vice versa....

    so after 6mths training will depends on where u choose to go when u are given a choice to opt during training(thou not guarantee)... so wat happens after that regarding the shift work or working pattern perhaps when u decided to opt for which particular dept or before u are posted u can post another thread and i will explain to u again?
