    i remember the 'kan cheong' feeling i had b4 my first time. u'll find that pulling the trigger is quite hard. to make that simpler, u gotta train ur grip strength of ur shooting hand. another thing that may come as a shock is the loud sounds from the shooting. very easy to get used to it. to get good scores, make sure u use ur non-shooting hand to grip ur shooting hand tightly so that u have a steady aim. if using laser, dont close one eye to aim. leave both eyes open to see where the laser is at cos thats where the bullet is supposed to land. once u master this, getting marksman is simple
