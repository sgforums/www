    VC = vigilante corps

    VCs are those in white uniformed shirts and dark blue pants and blue beret whilst SCs are just like any regular policeman. VCs do not carry firearms with them whilst on patrol, neither are they trained in their use. The training course for VCs is only 12 weeks, as compared to the 18 weeks for SCs.
