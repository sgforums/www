    Police ask for information on baby found at Teck Whye Lane

    SINGAPORE: Police are appealing for information about a newborn baby found at the entrance of a rubbish centre at Block 26, Teck Whye Lane on Sunday, October 12.

    There were no visible injuries on the baby boy, who has since been sent to Kandang Kerbau Hospital for care and observation.

    Anyone with information on the case can call the police hotline at 1800-255-0000.
