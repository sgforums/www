    No, no, no, no, no. You have gotten it horribly, horribly, wrong. Who fed you this information? Because it is misleading, not to mention totally wrong.

    Chances for promotion to SPO exist, true. But they are few and far between, and do not come around 2-3 years later. Heck, even one's first promotion will not come around 2-3 years. Not generally, anyway.

    Anyway, for what it's worth, the chance to convert from PO to SPO will only come after an average of 10-12 years. Yes, it is that long. And even then, conversion is not a certainty.

    This is why people will always ask you to get your degree first and come to SPF, so you can start off as an SPO [if approved] straight.

    Just thought I would clear this up for the benefit of this thread's readers.

    Originally posted by 2wheels:

    2) The sms indicated casual wear,...wad do you guys wear for this selection assessment? Don want to overdress or underdress ah...


    You cant go wrong with smart casual.

    a) Try covered shoes, from sneakers to loafers to your leather shoes & heels.  Ladies to avoid sandals, cos you are not going to a market. 

    Preferably one you wear socks with and when you take them off, they wont stink to high heaven and kill off people around you. (If it does, you should apply to be a assasin instead.)

    b) Socks. preferably without holes at the toes.  And ladies.....fishnet stockings with short shorts are kinda frowned upon.  You are not expected to perform pole dancing, so kinda leave that outfit at home please.

    c) Long Jeans or denim dress/skirt is okay, just make sure its not torn at places and preferably a dark colour.

    d) Wear a collar shirt aka a polo shirt at the very least. For females, a blouse, a polo shirt or a outfit that looks presentable to your b/f's parents.

    4) Some cologne or at least a deodarant will be nice. (Look to (a) for my reasonings.

    5) Some grooming will be nice too. Clean tidy looking hair & face.  Females please do have some colours on your cheeks and your lips.  NOT 3 LAYER ICI PAINT!

    6) Bring your original documents plus some photocopies to give to the officers. Maybe bring along a A4 plastic folder hold your originals certs and so on and to keep out the rain.

    7) Did they mention you need to bring along a PT kit to change into?  If not, then forget number 7.
