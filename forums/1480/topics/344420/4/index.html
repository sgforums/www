    Hi Envisage, it is okay if its PO, you can start as PO first, then if you perform well, there is always a chance for promotion to SPO around 2-3 years later. The reason for maybe putting you on a waiting list is perhaps the degree you have does not come with Honors. Is it an engineering degree? Cuz which course you took could also be related.

    Anyway, wish you good luck! When going for interview, prepare all your stuff, try to sell yourself not only in academic. If you are some leader in any organization or internationally recognized organization, you should put them in CV and also try to mention it in interview. Eg. sports leader/captain, some society president, SAF Sergeant/Officer, received some awards or been a volunteer in charity etc. These will show that you have leadership, initiative, public service attitude, etc. And think about situations in school or work when you need to multi-task and team work. Lastly, try to get powerful people to act as your referees.

    And, try to meet up those in the forum that has already gone for the interview, e.g. Anshinkan, etc and obtain advice from them.

    Wish you all the best!
