  • Tiggerific
    23 Feb 09, 22:14
    Originally posted by skythewood:

    OBS trip.

    no idea.

    OBS trip is for Officer Cadet Trainees [OCTs], NSFs who will graduate as National Service Probationary Inspectors [NSPIs]. Training has since been reduced for this lot, from the previous 9 months to 8 months currently.

    The Nepal Trip is for Senior Officer Trainees [SOTs], regulars who will either graduate from their 9-month course as Inspectors of Police or Assistant Superintendents of Police.

    Big difference in training, pay, job scope, et al.
