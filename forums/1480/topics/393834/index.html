    Punishment for Section 380 of the Penal Code, Chapter 224, is as follows:

    Theft in dwelling-house, etc.
    380.     Whoever commits theft in any building, tent or vessel, which building, tent or vessel is used as a human dwelling, or for the custody of property, shall be punished with imprisonment for a term which may extend to 7 years, and shall also be liable to fine.

    [retrieved from http://statutes.agc.gov.sg/]

    However, with your friend being a first-time offender and all, the punishment might not be the maximum punishment lah. This will depend entirely on the judge.
