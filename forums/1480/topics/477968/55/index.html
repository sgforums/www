    Hello bros, i also applied for the diploma sponsorship back in october last year, they contacted me about two weeks after i applied where they scheduled for my psychometric test. 

    After the psychometric test they scheduled for the panel interview which is once again two weeks later. The interview in my case was a 9 interviewers to 1 interviewee. 

    A month later i was scheduled for medical on 8 December. Few weeks ago i emailed them to ask when the results would be out and they say the assesment and interviews are still ongoing for the other sponsorship applicants so they would notify us about the outcome when concluded.

    Today, around 3 pm i finally recieved an email congratulating the sponsorship applicants for recieving the award. The contents of the email besides congralutating was to give us the forms and instruction to find two sureties for bond signing.
