    Application timeline:

    15/10/2014 - Submitted online application via Career.gov
    17/10/2014 - Received notification that they are reviewing my application
    21/10/2014 - Received invitation for Selection Assessment
    18/12/2014 - Medical assessment at Shenton Medical
    08/05/2015 - Formalities Session
    12/05/2015 - Enlistment & Training

    I can't remember when is my actual bond signing but its within a week from my formalities if I recall correctly. I understand the wait is excruiating but unless you can think of a reason why you could be rejected and there is no rejection letter/email, you should eventually get in. Don't give up!

    Originally posted by Mary sanchez:

    Hi guys any help for me? where can or do i check my application status for my SA interview? or will they email it to me?

     

    Hey Mary! As suggested by darklighter, you really have to be patient. The average wait for results after the first SA is about 1 month. Depending what your next outcome will be.

    Usually for rejection, you will receive it within 2 weeks after your SA.

    For second interview, you will receive it about  1 month after your SA

    and if medical straight after, you will receive it about 2 months after your SA

    when I meant receive, it would mean somebody will call you up, and an email will be sent to you thereafter. There is no way for you to check the status yourself. And calling them will just give you a very standard reply, which will be "application is still processing". Unless you're lucky enough to get something more than that. 

     

    PS. my answers to you are basically rough estimation from what I had observed, definitely there are also cases that vary/differ much from these suggestions.
