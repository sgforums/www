    Hi All

     

    If you are really passionate about policing or want to find out more, please join the VSC.

     

    https://www.hometeamvolunteers.gov.sg/htvms/web/volunteerspecialconstabulary-individualpoliceofficer

     

    We have many VSCs who joined and understood about the demands of the job and applied to be a Direct Entry Inspector. Puts you in better stead for the interviews too!

     

    To apply to be a VSC

     

    Stage 1 - Psychometric Assessment

     

    Stage 2 - Interview

     

    Stage 3 - Medical Examination

     

    Classes are usually on Monday & Thursday 6.30pm to 10pm at Home Team Academy, where you will learn Criminal Law, Shooting and Police Defensive Tactics. :)

    For my batch's SA, the english proficiency test was actually more like an essay/compo writing. You will get a random topic like, "Why do you want to join the SPF (i guess it mainly revolves around this topic). Don't stress too much about it, mainly use it as a platform to freely express your thoughts, at least that's what I did. I can't remember if there was an actual vocabulary/grammar test, but even if there was, it shouldn't be too hard. Neither of this should be a problem lah, will be quite simple/straightforward, it won't be like super hard, so really don't worry about it too much.

    I would say prepare more for the interview, you can predict on some qns they will ask maybe let's say, based on your background. They will definitely sure ask the same question as ever "Why you wanna join SPF, etc etc". All the best!
