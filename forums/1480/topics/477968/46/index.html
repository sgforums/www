  • NotThomas

    Originally posted by risyfozy:

    Hi everyone!

    What's the difference between Community Policing Officer and Police Officer? What about the training and salary etc?

    Hi risyfozy, a community policing officer is more focused on interacting with the community such as go to schools hosts talks, attend Resident Committee meetings etc. For a regular police officer they are the ones that respond to 999 calls and conduct either foot patrols or cruise around in the police car and patrol. Training wise, I believe CPO and PO both go through Police Officer Basic Course which is 3 months followed by Ground Response Force which is also 3 months in HTA.
