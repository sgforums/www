    Hi brothers and sisters, I just got my confirmation of appointment today and I would like to share my timeline with you guys. Hope it would be of help and all the best for those who are applying or still waiting for results in whichever phase(:

    My total timeline is about 3-4 months, considered pretty fast as I am a pre enlistee and I already received letter asking me to report to tekong, therefore I think they processed my application faster so that I an enlist to HTA directly. Once again all te best to all(: 

     

    26/12/2015 - Applied for post of junior police officer at career@gov.

     

    29/12/2015 - Received email regarding Selection Assessment and interview.

     

    11/01/2016 - Selection Assessment.

     

    01/02/2016 - Received call and email from Parkway Shenton medical to go down for body check up.

     

    05/02/2016 - Received call from police recruitment telling me to inform them if I received my enlistment letter so that they can defer me from SAF.

     

    07/03/2016 - Medical check up.

     

    31/03/2016 - Received email from police recruitment to send them the pending documents(bank acc and diploma transcript).

     

    13/04/2016 - Received email asking me to start looking for 2 sureties.

     

    15/04/2016 - Confirmation email of successful application.

     

    19/04/2016 - Bond signing session.

     

    16/05/2016 - Formalities session.

     

    17/05/2016 - Report to HTA for training.
