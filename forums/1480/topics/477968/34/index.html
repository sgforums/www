    12 Apr 15, 08:38

    Hi guys, first time posting here! I have been following this thread ever since I applied to be a JPO in Jan this year haha, so anyway, here is my timeline for  the whole application process.

    9 January 2015 - Applied through Careers@gov for JPO position

    26 January - Went for selection assessment and invterview

    -waited 6 weeks-

    10 March  - Got a call to book my medical appointment at Mt E Novena

    17 March - Did my medical checkup

    -waited 3 weeks-

    10 April - Call form my recruitment officer that my bond signing is next week!!!!

    I guess the reason why my application process is alot faster than some of you guys is because I'm a pre-enlistee? And I am actually supposed to serve my NS in Tekong, but I really have a deep interest in joining the SPF so I chose to sign on with the SPF instead and they helped me do the admin work quickly so I would not have to spend 3 months doing BMT (although I would not mind going for the experience and people I would meet)

    Lastly, my advice if you are going to call them and ask about your application status, DON'T call the recruitment hotline that is shown everywhere, you are better off calling the recruitment officer's on phone (Not mobile phone ah, like their work phone at their own table, get it right? haha) But do bear in mind the recruitment officers handle alot of applicants and that is why they might be slow in processing your application. Well thats all I have to say, thanks for reading this long ass post!
