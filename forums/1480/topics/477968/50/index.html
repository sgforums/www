    @Mulberry Janice Hi! I have replied you :)

    @aims The medical is just a basic checkup. Maybe 2.5 to 3 hours?

    @sivahopeful, hi there, normally for Assessment u will go through submitting of ur documents and some test such as what is stated in ur email like example English profession test, physical test etc. U can read through my previous reply around page 47 onwards.
