    31 May 15, 14:35
    Originally posted by Jofy94:

    Hi all! 

    Any kind soul willing to give me some advice? Im 21yo female graduating from poly soon! Keen on joining the force upon graduation. Would like to know how is the training and work scope after admittance and the pace of promotion within 5 years! 

    For the first 3 months of the training it will be called the Police Officer Basic Course. Main focus here will be clearing your ippt, marksman, police defence tactics and learning basic law. The last 3 months of the training will be more focused on scenario based training. There will be classroom lessons almost everyday.

    After graduation most of the trainees would be posted to various land divisions as an NPCO unless you are being posted to specialised unit like PCG. Promotion wise it will take about 3-4 years to promote to the next rank, provided you pass your IPPT.

    Piece of advise for all those who are joining, train up on your physical before enlisting and take some time reading various penal codes online so you will have an easy time learning law. Good luck to all of you!
