    Originally posted by Dreamrainbow:

    Hi, coming from someones who has been inside for 3 months already, I suggest you bring in a smartphone with its' camera removed. This is so you can still use whatsapp and other apps. But do note that you need to physically remove the camera and not just "disable" the camera app and don't try to stick black tape over the camera hole.. they'll check!

    Furthermore in each squad they will only let 1 or 2 people (the Squad IC & Communications IC) bring in a mobile phone charger. No extra batteries/ phone cables/ power banks are allowed inside. So by right only the person allowed to bring in the charger can charge his/her phone.. But by left..... hehe
