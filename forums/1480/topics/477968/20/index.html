    Hi all! Okay frankly speaking, I have been stalking this forum for quite some time and I gained quite a lot of information. So now, I will take this opportunity to share w u guys about my personal application timeline for those who are still waiting in whichever stage of application.

    12 Mar 2014 – Send online application and resume

    24 Mar 2014 – first interview

    07 Apr 2014 – received rejected letter

    15 Apr 2014 – Sent in appeal letter

    Sometime a week or two before 27 May 2014, I received a call from Home Team Career Centre (HTCC) to be invited for the second interview.

    27 May 2014 – second interview

    1 Jul 2014 – HTCC requested full length photo

    2 Jul 2014 – Sent full length photo

    9 Jul 2014 – Received a call from Shenton Medical for medical check up

    15 Jul 2014 – went for medical check up

    23 Jul 2014 – went for 2nd medical check up because of platelet count

    15 Sept 2014 – called HTCC for result but was asked to wait as the application is under processing

    22 Sept 2014 – HTCC requested a copy of dip cert n transcript

    23 Sept 2014 – Sent the copies over

    3 Oct 2014 – Was informed by HTCC that the application is at the last stage of processing and was asked to source for sureties.

    10 Oct 2014 – Received email from HTCC on bond signing, appointent formalities and reporting date

    03 Nov 2014 – Appointment formalities

    4 Nov 2014 – Reporting day to TRACOM

    In short, I waited for 8 months for my application to be a success. So to those who are waiting, I will like to say all the best for your application and I hope that the above timeline is of some help to you guys. Cheers! :)
