    Originally posted by mhcampboy:
    hmmm...so interesting...

    can relive my npcc days!
    NPCC and VSC training different wan.

    Discipline is the same, uniforms, marching & shooting training. Except its a higher calibre revolver.

    Timing wise, classes are conducted in the evening, so it wont disrupt your normal working/studying hours. Twice a week. Usually Monday & Thursday nite.

    New intakes are now conducted at HTA and it takes about 8-9 months to complete the course. While in training, you are given an allowance for the hours you actually are training.

    Can submit application forms, but note that there is now a aptitiude test. This test will determine your suitability for any SPF/Enforcement positions for the next 3 years.

    Then there is physical examinations to make sure you are of reasonable health.

    Ex-POs might not have to go thru the whole 8-9months course. Depending on how long ago they left the force.
