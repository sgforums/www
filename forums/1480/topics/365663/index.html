    hey vitasoys,

    since u jus ord, u must be applying for PO right? First they will ask u to submit all ur documents, cert, transcripts etc. den u wait and wait den u do physchometric test which is mcq and english proficiency test(write short compo) den they will ask u go inside a room strip to underwear check if u got tattoo den spread ur hands out, blah blah blah. nothing physical, simple marching, see ur hand and leg coordination. den u wait again. usually after u complete all that next is the panel interview usually by 3 person or more. My was 3 person which consists of a station inspector and 2 management executive (1 quite sexy. lol) den they will ask u questions like "why u want join police?", "u intend to study further?", blah blah blah.

    Sometimes they will split into separate days, the first day i went there i did the english proficiency test and physchometric test den they ask me go home. I received a call few days later to go back for panel interview. If ur the lucky few, they squeeze everything in

    In panel interview, this is where they access u whether u are some fuckedup person or police-calibre person. lol. There will be a chair where u will sit down and bout 3m away thats where the interviewers sit. so they can see ur whole body, best not to shiver just be confident!

    After the panel interview, den wait for their medical appt letter. For some people, they receive the medical and appointment letter together, some received the medical letter only like me. Usually when u receive the medical letter, it means u pass the interviews and meet the basic requirements otherwise they wouldnt bother to ask u to go checkup. (medical checkup isn't cheap!) I went for my medical checkup, am still waiting for my appointment letter, hope it comes soon! and good luck to u too!
