  • intelligenceman

    You have to sign on before you receive your enlistment notice.

    Changing of service (SPF/SAF/SCDF) is not allowed upon receiving enlistment letter, signing on with a different service will be rejected. 

    The only cross-service transfer possible is for a very rare few who are transferred after BMT to become NSPI (NS Police Inspectors).

    So in short - you must sign on before enlistment letter reach you, otherwise too late.

    Yes, you need to confirm admission into Poly/ITE before you can apply.
