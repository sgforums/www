    in-service is about once a month. rc meetings also. cssp stuff... depends on your precinct/npc/rc chairman really. depends on a lot of factors. and once your 2nd off-day has been burnt, it will be back to morning shift again the next day...

    usually the day taken away is the 2nd off-day because the 1st off-day is for you to sleep after coming back from night shift mah. but in extreme circumstances, you do get called back on your first off-day. but it's usally for large-scale, important deployments lah.
