    Police appealing for information on missing teenager

    SINGAPORE: Police are appealing for information on a missing 15—year—old girl.

    She’s known as 'Alexia’ and was last seen by her parents on 20 May at about 10pm along Eastwood Terrace in the East Coast area.

    The teenager is about 1.69 metres tall, slim built, and has blonde shoulder—length hair.

    If you have any information on the girl, please call the Police Hotline at 1800—255—0000. — CNA/vm
