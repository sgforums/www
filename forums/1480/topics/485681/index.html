    1) what makes you and why do you want to join the spf? 

    2) how and what are you able to contribute to the force?

    3) if you are in the force. How well can you see yourself in 5 or 10 years time? 

    4) your background check. Eg: whats your parent and siblings occupation? 

    5) what do you do normally during your free time?

    6) will you go for further studies?
