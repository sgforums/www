    Briefing - Is just a briefing... same as all other briefings that you have attended in your life...

    The usual who, what, where, when, how of VSC. And yes, there's Q & A.

    Aptitude Test - Is the psychometric test that you will do. It takes 3 hours. It determines your suitability for VSC. The tester will tell you more.

    Document verification - Verify information about you. Copies of your birth cert and IC will be taken from you. Your educational qualifications will also be verified. Various questions will be asked, in particular, specialization/major if you have taken any.

    You will also be asked to bring your bank statement or anything that verifies that you have a bank account. SPF will pay your VSC allowance to this bank account.

    I don't know what sessions you are referring to, please elaborate.

    Trainings will be conducted on Tuesdays and Thursdays at the new Home Team Academy (that is if they have not changed, pay attention during briefing to find out).
