    Sometimes VSCs are treated better because of the VSC's efforts that has helped out the regs. And because of the advantages the VSC brings.

    ie. information of the private sectors jobs, a different approach to problems that the regs are used to/know of; the VSC that make the efford to integrate among the regs and ns boys alike.

    Its more of a person, then the title.

    And even VSCs do fight/sabotage among themselves, much like regs and the NS.

    The only common grounds that anyone can look to is possibly where we value our vows.

    To protect, to enforce, to balance and to make an effort to make our own Singapore the place where our future generations can walk safe.

    On, and to better ourselves, mentally, emotionally and physically.

    Originally posted by Shakespeare_1982:
    .....By the way , I see from another thread that they will post you to the places where you wan, does that includs the division ? Or do they post you to the nearest divison near your house ?
    Except for some specialised units.

    You can be posted to the land division that you want. Does not necessary be near your home.

    As as and when you need a change, you can opt for transfer/posting to another divisions depending on the headcount at the other divisions.

    ie. You choose and was then posted to tanglin police division upon your graduation.

    2 years later, you figure you want to experience the works of Traffic Police. You can apply for transfer there.

    And a year later, you want to try out Police Coast Guard Work. You can request for a transfer again.

    Female patrol officers are rare and is always in big demand. So you wont be that easily shunted to one side just because you are female.
