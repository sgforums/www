    Hi pple,

    to Kurol-Kaze: 

    for each squad of 18 - 25, there are roughly one trainee fresh from SIM. Most of the squad is made up of rank and file, NUS and NTU grads. Overseas grads are also in the minority. But not to worry, its easy to gel up inside when we train together. The recruitment side don't really look at which uni you come from. As long as you are proficient in the various application stages, they will accept you. They just want people with the right mindset.

    when you are inside, everyone is equal. (not to say scholars i guess, hah)

     

    to Vic Lee:

    Applicants do not need to pass IPPT in the application stage. Don't worry about the physical  side as there are physical trainings in the course.

    Although passing IPPT is not a criteria for trainees to pass out from the course, I seriously think trainees should at least get a pass inside as it is part of the baseline/KPI for your assessment. The training inside will toughen you up. If you have difficulties passing, just have the right attitude and be persistent when it comes to training.

    The physical stage in the application is just to take your height and weight, test your psychomotor skills such as basic marching and bending of your joints. Unlikely that you will fail this stage unless you cant march well.


    to Rigaku:

    for a grad with 3rd class honours, your salary will roughly be $3,150 if I am not wrong. You can also refer to http://www.spf.gov.sg/career/so_salary.htm#top  as the salary scale is very transparent. Whether you have driving license or not will not affect your pay, it will merely increase your bond amount. Don't have to worry about unless you intend to break bond.

    There won't be a pay raise when you pass out but upon completing your 1st year of service, there will be an increase of ~ $216.

    From my knowledge, I don't think you will get extra allowance if you work as an IO or in the division. However, if you join specialized units such as SecCom and SOC, there will be additional allowances depending on your capabilities.

     

     

    Hope I cleared some doubts you guys have.
