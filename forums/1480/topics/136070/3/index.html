  • viciouskitty74
    26 Jul 05, 00:48
    Okies.....just so that everyone here has a chance to know.

    There is VSC in the CNB.

    However, no one is allowed direct entry to CNB.

    You first have to get thru the application to join VSC process. Namely, find the guts to fill in and submit the form.

    Am selected to attend the interview.

    Cleared the psychometric tests, medical examinations and are selected for the Orientation/Training.

    Training is for a minimum of 6 months, twice a week at TRACOM (1830 hours to 2200 hours) on Monday & Thursday Night. Not stay in.

    Pass the written test as well as the IPPT & Weapon Handling Training.

    Serve at least 6 months on the grounds as a VSC. ie. do active patrols, plain clothes raids & ambush as well as clocked impressive hours proving your commitment and meattle to function as a Officer.

    Then.......you can be considered for posting to the CNB.

    The TP VSC is fairly erractic on their acceptance of VSC officers into their division, so its a matter of whether your luck is there or not.
