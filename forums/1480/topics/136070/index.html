  • chowwaikein
    12 Jun 05, 11:15
    Just to clarify some questions and answers given:

    1. PES C and below will no longer be recruited into VSC.

    2. Whether poly or even ITE students, as long as lady is above 18 and gentleman is after NS, they can join VSC.

    3. VSC is given an allowance of $2.80 an hour.

    4. Police officer need to clock 16 hours a month and Senior Officers need to clock 24 hours a month.

    5. Yes, we do anti-crime round, normal proactive patrol, plainclothes operations, security coverage etc.

    http://www.spf.gov.sg/about_spf/vsc/index.htm#top
