    For the newly minted officers.

    Do take note:

    Make copies of your Warrant Card & Arm card.

    Both cards contains serial numbers you will never be borthered to remember. But in the event of loss, these number will turn up crucial.

    Make copies of your Security Pass, Driving Permits, Licences and your NRIC. They serve a record purpose which you can refer to whenever its needed.

    Those integrated into the Arms System. Your NRIC number is the key and its not barcoded on your arm card. So what you can do is to use the barcode of your copied NRIC, paste it on your arm card for easier scanning.
  • viciouskitty74
    10 Nov 05, 10:11
    Its coming to the end of a calendar year.

    VSC officers are allowed a set of new uniforms a year.

    Do be reminded to exchange any torn uniform and accessories with your Division LO.

    Ladies & senior officers will require tailoring. Do drop by to the tailors, have yourself measured and inform the LO the reference numbers so the LO can order the tailors to start work and complete your new uniform.



    Also on the end of any calendar year, officers are required to resubmit their new attachment forms early.

    ie, if you are from HQ, TRACOM or specialised units which can be attached to normal land division.

    All attachments are valid for only 1 calendar year. So this would be the right time to fill in the forms, chase your heads for approval, have it submited to HQ and have it published in the Force Orders in time for the New Year Holidays.

    Take note, if your attachment is not approved. Its nothing personal. It just meant that either the units you are planning to attached to is full or is currently under re-structuring.
