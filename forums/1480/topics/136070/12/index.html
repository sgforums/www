    20 Feb 06, 08:47
    Hmmm....I just answered a question on email. Might as well post it here for everyone to know.

    VSC accepts applications from males that have completed their NS obligation.

    However

    Let say you are 21, signed up with SAF for 5 years, serving your NS at the same time. 1st 2 years you are considered as doing your NS (full pay) and the next 3 years as actual SAF.

    You will require approvals from SAF if you intend to apply and also volunteer as VSC.

    And

    If you are already serving SAF/SCDF/CISCO/PSA/ICA/ as an regular officer, you are required to have the approval of your immediate supervisors from SAF/SCDF/CISCO/PSA/ICA to allow you serving as VSC concurrently.
