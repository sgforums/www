  • red_amoeba
    7 Nov 08, 15:33
    got friend who is SPO - physical requirement like normal PES B requirements. Need to sign bond, need a guarantor to sign on the contract. Training for 6 mths at Home Academy before being posted. Initially got to work shifts and once or twice a month have to do night duty (as duty officer). Otherwise if you are SPO, you usually do day shift - but they start early in the day and end around 3 plus. If you are single, can choose to take up the company's dorm at nominal rental, like that you save on travelling esp if you will be posted to Changi Complex. As SPO you dun really interact with the inmates but rather take more admin role and supervise the Junior Officers (wardens)...at least that is what I know from my friend.
