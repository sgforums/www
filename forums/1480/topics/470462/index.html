    SINGAPORE - Motorcycle riders in a wedding convoy was caught on camera posing as traffic police officers to escort a pair of newly-weds to their destination.
    In the video, some of the motorcyclists are dressed in fluorescent green jackets while others are dressed in grey long-sleeved shirts. Others wear silver riding jackets.
    Their motorcycles are also big-engine bikes resembling those ridden by traffic police officers. The bikes have blue and orange safety stripes painted on them.
    Their attire and motorcycles have led netizens to question whether their actions are legal.
    When contacted, the police said that anyone with information about the incident is encouraged to come forward and lodge a police report so that the police can look into the matter and determine if an offence has been committed.
