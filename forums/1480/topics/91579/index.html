    No. 3 is what you normally see your neighbourhood police officers in. A slight variation though, for ranks Station Inspector (SI) and above , they are also issued with bush jackets.

    Bush jackets are those that are tucked out, with 2 breast pockets and another 2 at the waist level.

    No 4 is what you see the Police Coast Guards (PCG) wearing. No metal buttons.

    No 5. Both 5(J) and 5(T).

    Long sleeve shirt and tie-- 5 (T)
    5(T) plus the jacket/ blazer on , then it's 5 (J)
