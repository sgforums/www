    Just to share my opinions.

    Most commandos are directly selected even before they have held a M-16 or more recently SAR 21 ( Not too sure if they have changed all M16 to SAR21 yet or not, old man liao lah... so not sure what SAF is going through now). Of course once selected doesn't mean that you will become one. But in my opinion, Commandos are not exactly specialised units. The real elite and specialised one is the SOF. A better comparision would be between STAR and SOF, although I don't see why should we compare as to which one is better, both are different and unique in their own rights. For these 2 units, you must be a regular , not NSF, haved served in the force for a min period, before you can qualify for selection.

    Anyway on a more general tone, in the SAF you have some 350,000 "trained" soldiers ( including old reservists like me) that chiong here and there, but at the end of the day, you are still training. You do not really see real ops. We can't really afford that too.

    For the SPF, every single day itself is operation, even off duty. When you see a snatch thief off-duty, you are very much required and obliged to tend to it. In SAF, other than live firing exercises, you fire blanks or maybe even universal rounds most of the times. In SPF, once you are on the roads, you have to carry live rounds and you better do. You won't know what's going to happen next.

    In any case, I don't think it's quite possible to compare the two forces because of the different types of operations.

    In my opinion, if you really wanna compare, although I don't see the point maybe you can compare the Military Police and SPF lah. Afterall their job scopes are much more similar.
