    Hi , I also serving my NS in police force but I can't reveal which Intake i am from but I can share with you my life in PA.
    When you go to the Police academy , you will go to a hall where you can see a lot of NS men waiting there. Then following you will see your FI & your OC leading your squad to your barrack (which is neither Alpha , Bravo , Delta or Charlie Coy) If you are a smoker , be smart & keep your cigarettes well. You can bring your HP battery along. Don't ever declare this two items to your FI. You can almost buy instant noodles in after book out... Advice: Keep well too
    Your FI will be very demanding & strict at the first few months but after that , he will be a close friend to you

    Anymore questions to ask
