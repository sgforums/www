    The working hours is like work 2 days rest 2 days...

    1st shift (Day)   _ 8am to 7.30pm...

    2nd shift (Night) _ 7.30pm to 8am...

    1st, 2nd, OFF, OFF, 1st, 2nd, OFF, OFF, ....

    But timing may extent because some time got last mins case... OFF doesn't mean nothing to do...

    If everything goes smoothly, after shift still need to stay and finish up all the incident reports. But this may depend on the NPC and Team...

    Don't think so much, once go there you will know... Good Luck...
