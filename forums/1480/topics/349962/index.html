  • mancha
    3 Mar 09, 00:02

    There are some dreamers who really really want to become police officers. These are usually weeded out during the interview.

    When they call you up for the interview, it only means you met the qualifying requirements.

    During the interview they assess you. This is where you show yourself.

    You must be well groomed for the interview.

    Your entry into the interview room is crucial. Your confidence shown in those first few seconds will carry a lot of marks. The rest of the interview will be routine for the interviewers, you only need to answer carefully and intelligently. Saying things like your previous employer sucks, (even if it is true) is not good for you. Say they don't provide what you are looking for, i.e advancement in life.

    What do you think of rapist? Don't say you hate them. Say the crime is terrible, and that society needs to be protected against them. Think first, don't answer straight from the cuff.
