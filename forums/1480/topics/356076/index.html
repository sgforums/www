    hope this will help.

    the test will consist of a mainly 2 parts: 1) psychometric test and 2) personality

    the psychometric test is mainly verbal critical reasoning. u will be given a short paragraph to read. there will be a few qns, the options will be a) true, b) false,c)can't tell. u need to understand the paragraph fully cus the qns r quite tricky sometimes.

    for the personality test there's no way to prepare so just answer the questions truthfully.

    after the test if u make the grade they will probably call u up for interview in about 1-2 weeks time (at least 2 rounds), but subjected to availability to slot u in for training at the Home Team Academy.
