    VSC...there is going to be a recruitment booth at Westmall soon, but I cant remember the date. But you can just go to the website and join anytime wat.

    Assuming you are in the land divisions, you will most likely be patrolling with the regular officers, although in some NPCs, they let a whole bunch of VSCs take a vehicle out. In the past 10 months I have been at my NPC, I participated in one special ops so far, with a second one coming up soon. Other then that, its usually normal patrols loh. They usually wont put you at the counter unless you ask for it lah (which I had before...can be rather hectic too).

    Depending on how "proactive" you are, you may get a chance to update log sheets and man the radio. You attend to cases with the regulars, and if you wish, you can also participate in the handling of the case. However, your jobscope ends once you leave the scene, compared to the POs who need to do plenty of follow-up work. Of coz you can also choose to be a sleeping man in uniform, just sitting there and not doing anything. No one could fault you for that.
