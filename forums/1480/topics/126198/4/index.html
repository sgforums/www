    Okay guys, it's a no end to all these. So just make a summary:

    It really depends on the resources available at the time of incident. The police can be free now but may not be free after 5 minutes. Any cases can happen anytime of the day. Maybe at 12 noon, there is not a single case. But at 1230, there are more than 5 cases at different locations. So, all these cannot be predicated.

    Of course, at the time of incident such as robbery, theft, etc, the victim wishes the police to reach asap. But we really got to think of constraints. There may be traffic congestion, some motorists does not give way. Location of scene not given clearly (informant say is along Hougang Street 21, and no further details given), the police have to search for the whole stretch of the road.

    For ambulance & fire engine, they only cover medical and fire-fighting and resuce incidents, so they can reach faster and with more resources. For police, they do almost all other things, direct traffic during blackout of traffic lights, accident, neighbours quarrelling, etc etc etc.

    So for all out there, 999 or 995, do not abuse it so that the authorities can attend to the emergency cases asap.
