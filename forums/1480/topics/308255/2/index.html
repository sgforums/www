    it's quite fun to learn, and you will get to know interesting stuff.

    for any volunteer services, like volunteer to help at old folks home, the army won't stop you. but you will still need to notify your superiors. the army will of course take priority, so you will never be able to use the excuse of vsc to avoid doing your part in the army.

    if your army training requires you to stay in, and you can't do vsc work for the month, than you will have to apply for leave from vsc, and tell them you are not available.
