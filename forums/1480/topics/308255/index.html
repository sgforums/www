  • skythewood
    28 Mar 08, 17:01

    The raffles medical should be the one nearbugis area? don't really remember, but it is definitely near town... You will be briefed by  the recruitment officer.

     

    The training should end by 10.30pm, sometimes earlier, but never later... for me anyways...

     

    The public transport to HTA usually involves getting to Choa chu kang MRT and taking a bus from there...  How convenient is that depends on where you live bah, haha.

     

    Or you can use the VSC allowance you get to pay for the cab to HTA. $3.60 times 4 hours = $14.40. You get a allowance during training too.
