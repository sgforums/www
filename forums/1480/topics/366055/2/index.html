    i believe when u are going to ord, SAF will conduct a e-prep roadshow to introduce their soldiers about the e-prep and together will it will be a recruitment and career fair to allow those going to ORD and might wan to enter the work force to find their future employer there. and agency will be involve. SCDF,SPF, DSTA, and maybe some foreign uni. if you wan to study private uni.

    i think you can submit your application maybe 2 months before you ord or maybe submit during the roadshow. cus they will still have to hold and wait till u ord then they will call u up for the first psyhometric test.

    I went for my psychometric test about 2 weeks before i ORD. then after i ORD then i receive letter for first interview. so i think you can only go through your interview after you ord. from what i know, there is no shortcut like do all the check first while in SAF and go to VSC right after you ord.

    So after my first interview, i go for medical check up in march. then i receive my first training date on Aug. so you really have to wait. my whole process from application to first training is about 8 months.

    I do understand your excitement cus that time i also thought thing can be done that way. =)

    I think we are also quite similar in situation, i am also from combat engineers. haha. if you do need any help or wan to ask more you can email me at klmw_kevin@hotmail.com maybe i can share with u my experience. especially on how to mangae schoolwork and VSC together. it is not easy but can be done.
