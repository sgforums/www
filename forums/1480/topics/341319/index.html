    err..

    1st qns. Promotion frm Corporal to Sergeant. Promotion is much based on performance and your ability. The average period of promotion is 3-4years.

    2nd qns. Prison Officers Power. As said from their position, they are Prison Officers, their authority is different from Police Officers.

    3rd qns. CNB. As said from their position, They are Narcotics Officers, their Authority is different from Police Officers and General Policing.

    4th qns. Private Diploma. SGT Entry Officers should have a A lvl, Local Polytechnic Diploma for NAFA Diploma. According to what i know, other qualifications will be looked on at a case by case basis and may be recognised as long as the modules/time spent is same as Local Polytechnics(I may be wrong)

    5th. You signed on as a Police Officer and will be on the Police Officer Scheme and will be graded based on your rank and Scheme rather than your subsequent education qualifications. Should you be converted to Senior Police Officer, then you will be graded under the Senior Officer Scheme.

    6th. If you have further queries, call up SPF straight.
