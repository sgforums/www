  • shad0wprince
    25 Dec 13, 00:30

    Hey Alvin1988

    For the selection assessment, you will be asked to do a psychometric test, a physical assessment and verification of documents. Physical assessment include checking of body tattoos(if any), short march, height & weight etc....

    As for the panel interview, you will be interviewed by 3 senior officers. The questions they will be asking are more or less the same, such as "why do you want to join the police force", "how can you contribute to the force?", "are there any plans to upgrade yourself?". Just appear confident and relax and everything will be fine.

    After the panel interview, they will inform you to go for your medical check up in about 2-3 weeks. If you got the medical appointment, it means you have passed both the selection assessment and panel interview. I hope this will help, cheers!
