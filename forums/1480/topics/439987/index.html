    Hi there street93 If you are a poly or A Levels graduate and you pass your ippt during your basic training, you will pass out as a SC/CPL If you do not fulfil the 2 conditions, you pass out as a SC Postings after your basic training can vary from anything like frontline roles such as NPC Officers and Transcom Officers to instructional roles as Fitness Instructors and staff roles. I think you get to indicate your preferences before passing out but as always, its subjected to manpower needs. If OCS is a target for you, just do your best and try to be the best trainee in your cohort. You have a chance of making it as you happen to be in 1 of the 2 intakes per year that upgraders (people who join OCT from the police mono intake) can join OCT. The other intake will be that in Jan. If I am not wrong, there should be 4 positions up for grab since the OCT squad intake has increased after my cohort.
