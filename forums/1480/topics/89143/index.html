    hi there,

    here are the answer which is similiar to houndy's reply....

    1) there is no difference between guys and gals, but i believe you can use excuse like muscle cramp to avoid training when you are not feeling well during training.... some jobs like carrying heavy(and i mean really heavy) stuffs will be excused as a gal.... etc Of cos you will not be sleeping with the guys.... you have a company of your own with a female Coy commander...

    2) as for the pay... as long you are appointed as a CPL/SGT/INSP or ASP.... you will be getting the pay stated in http://www.spf.gov.sg/career/index.html..... anytime, anywhere... unless you are promoted of cos...

    3) as for the intake, its all lies with the HQ.... usually is monthly... but sometimes one month might have more people, sometimes will have less or even nil.....

    4) as for how long you will have to wait.... it should be seven working days... but i will always advise those who are keen to join the SPF to send in application about twice a month to "bug" them.... =P
