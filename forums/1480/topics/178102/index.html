    Ermmmmm yep.

    It was a MOE recommendations to put up Hon Vs.

    Hon Vs are mostly professional in their own right. Or are DMs and teachers recommended by the MOE.

    They are introduced to provide the DM and teachers police powers to handle difficult students as well to provide a deterrance against triad/secret societies/drugs & any criminal activities.

    Hon V goes thru a much condensed version of training then normal VSCs and has their training tailored to suit their roles in the schools.

    They however arent your run of the mill POs or NS Man.

    Their academic capabilities and rank should tell you so. NO matter whether they are Hon Vs or not, their rank still means business in the disciplined organisation and count for no small responsibilities with our country. So do give them their dues.

    Any DM/teachers who are Hon V and left MOE, are automatically removed of their Hon V status unless they made other arrangements.

    Of the recent intake of VSC. There was one female teacher Hon V. She later quit Hon V and re-take normal VSC training and becomes a full fledged VSC.

    Who knows, she might quit VSC and later join SPF? It happens, and will happen again. We aften have VSC that quit and join SPF as regs. And the emerging trend shows a lot more professionals doing that. Resulting in a lot more Inspectors & ASPs that was previously VSC.
