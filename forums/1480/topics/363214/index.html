  • FuMo
    8 Jun 09, 16:33

    Haha thanks :) Posted to 'E' Tanglin Division atm =)

    Yes they have vegeterian food at Mess,all u have to do is go to the front counter tell those staff,write the book and they will give u the food in a green plate with a green cup,u have to go to the side to fill up the drinks yourself tho,but bare with it,HTA mess food isnt good,u wont like it,especially the vegetables..i once spotted a worm in the soup,so check ><

    Basically,u lights out at 2230 sharp and wake up at 0530,theres waterparade at 0600,a FI (field instructor) will come and conduct it,after that if u got PT you can go back to ur bunk and take a lil rest until 0620,ur squad will jog to the parade square for the flag raising.

    Flag raising should end ard 0730 and the FI will come take ur squad,go for jog and etc,the toughness of ur daily PT depends on ur FI(every squad will be assign to a FI),how relax or how cocky he can be,theres quite a lot of assholes FI in HTA that cant wait to showoff their power,but base on what i see in HTA,regular always dont get scolding and way,way more relaxing,trust me =) they wont make you run like a dog until u die or etc etc lol. Anyways after PT finish at 0830 u go for ur breakfast,20mins for you to eat. Then u jog with ur squad back to ur bunk,change to Full-U,go for law class until ard 11.30am or 12++ depend on ur OC,OC is those oldbird regular aka so called teacher lol,every squad will have their own OC also,they'r ur 'Angel' in HTA,will stand up for u and etc etc,and FI are the 'devil' :) you will see that once u been there for weeks xD at 12++ go for lunch,ard 12.20pm u go for ur afternoon break,freetime in bunk until 2pm i think,then go for law again -_-" until 4.30-5,ur FI will come take u for drills or PT again,depend on what they wan or whats on ur timetable,until 6.20pm,go for dinner,after dinner u can go back to ur bunk and relax till lightsoff. BUT! there are times where some retards go smoke and etc kana caught,u will spend the night doing PT at ur coy ground sweating like mad by some FIs. Anyways i can give u some advice/tips,Ippt for female not realli important,BUT,when u pass out and wanna grow in ranks u gotto PASS ur IPPT. Regular life in HTA is relaxing,so u wont expect kana fuck by FI like Us lol =)

    Oh and,every week will have 1 or 2 PDT(Police Defensive tatics) and swimming class,base on what i went thru,its damn lame,swimming 1hr= 55min PT,5min go into water and out u go,finish :) PDT will be fking tiring and u will get damn dirty and sweaty,the PDT Mat is extremely dirty..PDT u will learn if i'm correct..err,130 stances? Theres also PSOC,Police Standard Obsticle Course test after a 1st and 2nd tryout,u will wear fake rubber gun,T-baton etc etc of the same weight and run a series of LAME obsticle course -.-" but u will be damn sweaty and tiried as if u jus finished 2.4km after that xD

    A few test in the 3rd month.take note and rmb how to say the drills,quite alot lol,u will have to SHOUT at the test.

    Law***(70% and below=Fail),Shooting***(pass/2nd/1st/marksman),Drill,3rd/4th IPPT*(fail 1 station=Fail),PDT(fail 3 stance = Fail),Swimming(swim 50m),PSOC(5min and above=Fail)

     

    Anything more you wanna ask feel free ;)
