    Actually its the same course.

    Does not mean you are automatically qualified to pilot the police boat.

    For that, you have to acquire a certain number of seatime and attend/pass the Steersman's course.

    For Power Pleasure Craft Licence, you can pilot as the licence name suggest.

    Well, even if you have not attended the MPC. You can still go out for patrol, except that you have to be extra careful and try to pick up as much as you can. Let your fellow crewmates on the boat knows also, so they can also guide and watch out for you.

    Like all divisions POs, you pick up the skills and procedure via 'on the job training' of sorts.

    Oh.....and the 12 hour means the actual shift hours. My time, we usually can stretch easily into 14 hours. So always bring lots of foods to share and lots of chit chat topics.
