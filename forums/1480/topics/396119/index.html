    You are posted to: Police OCT Inspector at home team academy

    I'm sure that quite a number of ppl are unsure of what is this posting and so I will try to answer some question I had when i got this posting after my BMT


    questions:

    1) Is there confinement 3 weeks? if no, we can go back home on the first week?

    Ans: There will be no confinement. You are able to book out on every friday if nothing bad happens.

    2) What OCT stand for?

    Ans: Officer cadet trainee. The lowest rank as you are only a trainee but with highest responsibility and discipline.


    3) whats the pay like during training and after the 9 months course is completed?

    Ans: Pay will be $700 on the 12th of every month


    4) Any SOC?

    Ans: We do have SOC, but dont worry la. Its not that imp compared to the army and its easier too.

    5) Any smoking area?

    Ans: There is no such thing as 'yellow-box' in HTA. Strictly no smoking!

    6) Bunk

    Ans: It will be two person to a room, Four person to a toilet

    7) Items to bring on the first day when you report

    Bring your running shoe ( Usually out of stock), hanger and all basic stuff that you need. No need to bring any SAF stuff as you will be given new uniform and stuff on the first day.

     

    You are free to post more questions. I will try to answer them if possible.

    To be posted to Police force after BMT, the only way is posted to POLICE OCT as all commission officer in SAF, SPF & SCDF have to go through BMT first. All police enlistment are mono intake. If you are enlisted into tekong, there are no chance for you to get into police force beside posted to OCT.

    There are only 2 intake per year for Police OCT. In April and September every year, only 60 cadets per batch. So you can see the rate to be selected is very slim. You cant have any tatoo, pending offence and you need to own a dip/A' lvl cert.

    To sum up : Do your best in BMT, your PC/ peer appraisal will know who are the leader born-to be and who are the wayang ones :D
