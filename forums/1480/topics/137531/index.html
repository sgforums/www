    Guys... this is a very common mistake by many to regard SOC as an individual unit, SOC actually stands for Special Operations Command and it encompass STAR, Police Task Force (PTF) and Police Dog Unit.

    All these units undertake specialised roles in supporting the various land division and units.

    E.g, STAR is capable of arresting of armed/dangerous criminals, providing VIP escorts, hostage rescue operations etc... They are technically different from the SAF SOF as they are responsible for more home based criminal threat, while SOF is more of terrorist threats... whereas PTF is used when there is a need for large scale crowd control, riot control and lower risk criminal incidents. If I'm not wrong, they are the ones who wore the red berets and took part in the Tekong search.... lastly, K9 unit is used to support CNB operations, customs checks and some prison searches...

    Hope these information helps
