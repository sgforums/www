    Hello everyone, for panel they will most likely ask about

    1. Leadership, reflect on your past experience in school, CCA's, and if any, your BMT experience to see how these have developed your professional and command abilities. 

    2. Motivation. You may have many motivations but there are a specific few that made you choose to pursue a career as a SO. Reflect on your train of thought and discover which core driving factors will enable you to push on in the face of adversity.

    3. Different people get different questions, some get situation questions some get questions pretaining to your eassy.

    4. Update yourself on current affairs etc.

    MOST IMPOTANTLY when speaking to the board members, you are doing yourself a disservice if you state what you think they "want to hear." Don't pre-form your answers but internally re-affirm your commitment because it is what's best for you. Be true to yourself.

    To those who are interested,

    I actually submitted my application in March and I only received the email and was told to go for the psychometric tests 1 month later. Try to go for the dates that the admin has scheduled you to go for, if not the wait is around 1 month if you were to miss any. I couldn't make it for 2 consecutive scheduled dates and I finally went for my psychometric tests in early June. After, which, the interval for the next few rounds of interviews are also around a month each. i.e. I went for Dialogue session in early July, met the psychologist in late July, had the Panel interview in August and finally went for the Medical in early September. They called me a few days ago to check my notice period. No confirmation so far but to be honest, the wait has been so long that I have already forgotten the anticipation I had in the past. No choice, just wait lor.

    For those who needs advices, I can try to help cos I'm so free as I'm currently doing casual work and coaching. For those in the same plight as me, let's just hope that all these will end soon haha.
