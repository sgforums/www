    Hi All, I am not sure how much it has changed since the time I went for my interview but I guess most of the aspects mgmt is looking out for are: Composure, ability to relate and converse well on your thinking as well as keen interest. The usual qns include: Tell me more about yourself Why are you interested in SPF What can SPF improve on Do some ground work to know about the force before going to the final interview. I know it is a daunting task to speak up in front of many senior officers but we are also very keen to get to know you better. As for psychometric test and interview with phychologists, be honest and tell them who you reallt are. Don't give cock and bull stories or answers on who you think you are cos your answers will not be congruent. All the best and good luck! Klv
