  • SeeWhyGee
    23 Nov 14, 06:06

    Hi everyone,

    Was taking a look around to find out what's the typical process like and found out that everyone's generally going through the same procedure (and the extremely long wait), so I decided to share the details of my applicaton so far.

    26 April Application received by SPF (email notification from careers.gov.sg)

    28 May SPO Selection Assessment (Round 1)

    10 June Dialogue with Assistant Director Manpower - Recruitment (HR interview, Round 2)

    21 July Psychologist Interview (Round 3, "No fail stage", was initially scheduled for an earlier date but was overseas)

    31 July SPO Board Interview (Panel interview, Round 4)

    14 Aug Submission and Verification of Graduation Certificate from NTU (Convocation was in early AUG 2014, this was self-arranged with the recruitment division personnel as they requested us to inform them once we received our graduation certificate)

    3 Sept Medical Examination (Round 5)

    15 Oct Submitted updated documents - 15 mths CPF transaction history, updates PES status (got a call couple of days earlier to do so)

    20 Nov Called SPF recruitment division to check status of application. Was informed that my application is still "In Process". Requested for more specific details but was told that they are unable to disclose this information.

    I actually also applied for SCDF senior officer and was shortlisted to go for their medical check up (passed the panel interview), but I decided to pull myself out of the SCDF application and wait for SPF instead. Heard that there's an intake in Dec but I doubt I'll be getting into that intake. Got this info from a guy I met during my Board Interview. He got into the Dec intake.

    Hope this information is useful. Cheers! =)
