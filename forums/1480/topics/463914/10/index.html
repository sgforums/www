    10 Jun 13, 12:50

    @haha2006: the medical checkup consists of 3 stages. The first is to measure ur your body statistics like height, weight, blood pressure, urine test and drawing of blood for hiv test. Second u will be interview by the doctor asking u various related or random questions to check for listening or speech defects and some check on breathing and heart beats. Last of all u will do ur chest xray. The entire process took around 1.5hrs for me. But I went during non peak and it's raffles medical for us.
