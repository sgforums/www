    16 Jul 13, 09:29
    Originally posted by Whyzee:

    Hi weymas,

     

    how can I prepare for board interview? Any advise on this? Thanks!!

    Well just be confident when u r answering their qns.. it can be quite daunting especially when the senior commanders ask u qns. They look very serious n will not smile, but dont be affected. They're just testing ur composure under stressful situations.

    U dont have to rush, take a bit of time to think through their qns n come up with ur answer. As long as ur answers are logical and u r confident, u should b fine.

     

    Good luck!
