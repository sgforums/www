    There will be about 6-7 ppl during the panel interview, but only 3 will be asking the questions. these 3 are senior commanders (at least DAC rank). the other few will be a psychologist and probably the same 2 ppl from the AD dialogue session.

    Originally posted by shoubuliao_111:

    The interview is 15 mins right?

    How do I prepare for the interview? Any tips for the dialogue session? Example of qns asked??

    the interview should be longer than 15mins. during my session each person took at least 20-30mins. i feel that it's quite casual compared to the Panel interview (which had some mind-challenging qns). the dialogue qns are mainly to know more about u and why u've chosen this path. i think everyone's qns should be slightly different as they will ask on ur background. eg. i was selected for wushu national team but in the end i backed out. so they asked what was the reason, how u felt about it, why u made the decision...

    eg. i was in quite a number of leadership posts so they asked, how did u lead ur team, what are some problems u faced and how u dealt with it...

    so it depends on what background u have to determine the qns they will ask. my friend who's working in MHA gave me this advice: share ur learning points from each experience. hope its helpful to u (:
