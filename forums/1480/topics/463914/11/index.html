    15 Jun 13, 19:02
    Originally posted by geckoian:

    Btw I hate my own long posts. Paiseh

    weymas any advice or suggestion for people who are joining training soon? How is a typical day in training like?

    well u can start increasing the intensity of your physical training cos sometimes you can be quite bogged down with tests and exams and you won't have much time for physical activities. you can also start revising your footdrills and make sure you know at least the basic ones which are crucial for the first few days.

    a typical day would start with a water parade, followed by either breakfast or flag raising depending on whether your first lesson is PT or not. the rest of the day will be either classroom lessons, PT, or other activities which you'll find out more about when u come in.

    don't worry, when you come in, you can look for me and my squad and we'll be more than glad to help you all.
