    Originally posted by mjlee:

    SPO retirement age has been risen from 55 to 60? Anyone heard about it?

    Source: SPF Recruitment Facebook Page, comments section.

     

     

    Yes it has been increased till 60, but it's not mandatory. U can still choose to retire at 55. If u want to work till 60, it is subjected to several conditions such as work performance, health/fitness, and whether the organisation still needs u.

    Also, u can work till 60 but ur SPO INVEST scheme (I.e. retirement fund) stops at 55, and u cannot withdraw it until u reach 60 or choose to retire.
