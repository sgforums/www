    Both options are opened to VSC. Depending on capability, availability and necessity.

    Hard to fail IPPT or Shooting with the VSC. Fail IPPT, still can serve, fail shooting, cannot draw arms only. But still can serve.

    VSC requires minimum 16 hours per month commitment, if unable to clock hours for that month, officers are required to inform and apply for leave for that month.
