    Non swimmers are allowed,and for your eyesight,you have to be 6/20 for each eye. One of my bunk mates have 800 degrees for his eyesight and he is coping well. There are obese enlistees and they will try to trim your BMI to a healthy range. As for your IPPT or NAPFA you mean,they will accept NAPFA Silver or Gold.Regular trainees are to pass their IPPT before graduation or else they may get terminated from service. To my opinion,see if your NITEC results qualify for poly admisssion.If it qualifies,enter a polytechnic and enter with a diploma as you will be graduated from Home Team Academy (HTA) as a Sergeant but Higher NITEC graduates and below will graduate from HTA with the rank of Corporal.
