    The VSC duties are interesting..

    At NPC level, you'll get to work with with the Regulars.
    Everything under the sun. Round and Round in the police car.

    If you are experienced, you'll get to join the plain clothes duties.
    You do what the regulars do as a team. (exciting!)

    You are the co-partners with Regulars.
    The experiences, it's "money cannot buy"!

    In VSC, You'll get the joys and thrill...
    But on a serious note, it is also huge responsibilites.
    You will be seen/judged by the public as the Regulars when you are out in the field, once you don the blue uniform or in plain clothes.

    So have a good thought and whether you are willing to take up the challenges.

    Don't let it be a 1 - 2 year thingy. Yes, some will quit after a while (which is discouraged). The time and efforts have been wasted.

    On the brighter side, most of them have stayed for a long time, some even retire only after their 25 years of service. (applaud loudly)

    26 Oct 06, 19:46
    Anyway, the interview usually comes with makan.

    So take the initiative. dress in decent clothes and help yourself to the catered food and chit chat with the other officers there. Bring a jacket cos it can get cold waiting your turn.

    Interviewers also do tend to look and like to see confident folks they will meet at the rooms. Bring along a water bottle, cos the drinks always arent enough for lots of thirsty nervous interviewees.

    Bring the following:

    1) Birth Cert
    2) NRIC
    3) Educational Cert (Preferably your O, A & Dip or Degree Transcript if you have them)
    4) POSB/DBS book/card
    5) Bring copies of above also so it save the officers trouble to photocopy them.

    And Lotus is rite, as long as you can roughly say the Singapore Pledge, it will work enough for the interviewers.
