    Erm....the SPF used to have the LCP rank until it was removed in a shake-up of the rank system a few years ago. LCP isnt the only casualty. The basic Constable rank was removed too (for regulars).

    PNSFs have a different rank structure compared to their SAF peers. They go from SC to SC/CPL and then to SC/SGT before ORDing, assuming they possess a diploma/A level and passes promotion requirements (such as IPPT). That said, I have seen some jumping straight to SC/SGT.

    The minimum rank of a regular is indeed a Corporal. Ditto for VSCs.
