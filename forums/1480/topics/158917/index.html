  • oranges
    8 Nov 05, 23:22
    in prison service, spo means senior prison officer. as long as u have a degree, u are eligible to apply. there is an 18-week residential training (academic, ippt, some self defence & restraint techniques). after that, u will pass out and be posted to one of the many prisons in singapore.

    there are generally two types of work u can do: one is staff work (paper work either in hq or one of the prisons) and the other is operations work (direct supervision of prison inmates). every year, u are required to pass your ippt. u may also likely be required to work shifts & on phs, but u get time off for that.

    the job is challenging, with the many initiatives and programmes being rolled out to help inmates in their rehabilitation, and would especially be suitable if u like helping people.
