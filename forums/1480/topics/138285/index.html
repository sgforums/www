    Totally agreed with the statement. Focus on your studies now and get some fantastic grades. Preferably a First Class Hons in Engrg, Sci, Law, Med, etc. Check with PSD for recognised degrees as well.

    It is not easy to be put up for SO conversion from JO cos it takes time. Fundamentally, how long are you willing to wait for this opportunity as it may never happens in the entire career in SPF. You will need to put up as Acting Inspector (AI) and subsequently been selected to attend the "C" course to move on as Probationary Inspector (PI) .. and eventually be confirmed as Inspector ... and graded as Inspector of Police (I).

    Go get a First Class Hons and be a ASP.
