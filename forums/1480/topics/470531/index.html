    Welcome,I am waiting as my application is undergoing its final processing. For Pokish, take your time as even if you call the Recruitment Officer,he or she is unable to tell you the inside details. They will normally process on what they can and if you have outstanding documents,they will ask you to submit the outstanding documents like your educational certificates/transcripts etc as if you owe them your educational cert (For example,you apply  with a Diploma but  you are yet to get your cert), they are unable to process it further. Normally if you have went through the medical checkup and they ask you for your outstanding documents,you should be more or less accepted into the Force.For the medical checkup requested by SPF,it is not early to do so as they do have a date if you noticed on the letter.The medical checkup is the final checkpoint for the process.Cheers.
