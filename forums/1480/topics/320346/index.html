    Woman stabbed to death at Causeway Point food court

    SINGAPORE : A 45—year—old woman was stabbed to death at a shopping centre in Woodlands on Sunday morning.

    Police were called in to the Causeway Point food court at 10.35 am, where the victim was found with multiple stab wounds. She was pronounced dead about five minutes later.

    Police have classified the case murder, and are looking for a 60—year—old man to assist in investigations.

    The man was last seen wearing a blue polo T—shirt, with shorts and black shoes. — CNA/ms

    Man wanted by police to assist in murder investigations found at foot of block

    A 60-year-old man, who was wanted for the murder of a woman dishwasher at Causeway Point last Sunday, jumped to his death after three days on the run.

    He was found dead at the foot of Block 660, Jalan Tenaga, at about 9 pm last night.

    Police was looking for the Chinese man in connection with the murder of 45-year-old Ms Khoo Quee Kee, who was said to be his former tenant.

    The man had brutally attacked and stabbed her to death with a 15cm-long knife, while she was at work at the Causeway Point foodcourt on Sunday morning.

    The man then left the scene and the knife was later found in a rubbish bin on the sixth floor of the Woodlands mall, a floor above the foodcourt.
