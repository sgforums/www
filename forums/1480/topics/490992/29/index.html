    There seems to be some changes:

    December 2016: Re-applied

    March 2017: Selection Asssessment - half day needed, just checking of documents, writing down your particulars, essay writing.

    9 May 2017: Dialogue session: Check documents, test uniform, psychometric test and questionnaires will be after lunch, no more dialogue session interview this time

    24 May 2017: Psychology interview - a total of 3 hours session for me, including a 2 hour interview with the psychologist (this interview will not affect your advancement to panel interview). I have a friend who was interviewed by 2 psychologists. So experience might vary. 

    2 June 2017: Panel Interview, my friends who were at the same Dialogue as me, received the psychology interview but yet to receive a date for panel yet,

    Got my psychology interview a week after the dialogue session and at that same week, received my Panel interview date as well. 

    Also, i also have friends who got the rejection email 3 weeks after dialogue session, I don't understand why, cause there is no interview for this dialogue :(
