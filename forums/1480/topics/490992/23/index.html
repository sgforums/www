    1 more week and my 6 months wait since my last rejection date will come to an end.

    This should be my 2nd and last application as at 28 yr old, I do not think I have a lot more 6 months to wait.

    I was rejected on 31 May this year, 4 days after my birthday (what a splendid birthday gift) after my Dialogue Session.

    I remember in my Dialogue, the questions asked were, "tell me more about yourselves", "what are the things you know about SPF", "why do I want to change a different career after being in my current job for so long". We were then into the subject of terrorism, and I was caught off guard when the question was: "what are your point of view regarding Minister K Shamugan's speech recently?". It was something that I never watch or read. Then came the 2nd off guard question, "What do you know about SGSecure?". At that time SGSecure was only just launched, and I did not took notice of that. I almost thought it was a mobile application, or something to prevent hackers at that time.  Of course I was honest and I did not smoke. Then they asked about my point of views on terrorism. 

    I guess I got rejected because I did very badly for my interview. Is not that I did not go unprepared, but I focused on other kind of news, e.g. Benjamin Lim's case, etc.
