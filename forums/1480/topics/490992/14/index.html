    Originally posted by h9:

    Anyone applied for Senior prison officer? care to share the outome?

     

    Went to Senior Prison Officer Selection Assessment just few days ago. So here's a summary of my experience (hope I did not left out anything)

    - Document check

    - Take height and weight

    - Color Blindness test

    - And everyone will be gathered into a conference or meeting room for questionaires (situational based multiple choice questions like what are the actions you will take for certain situations)

    - Then is a video screening to more about the job of Senior Prison Officer and how their training is like

    - Then a mass Dialogue Session with everyone included

    - Intro and why you want to join SPS. And base on your job, the interviewer may ask you things regarding to your job and how it is related to being a Senior Prison Officer. The interviewer is very good at asking questions base on what you say, so be careful with that.

    - Open question with no right or wrong answer for everyone to answer to see your opinion. Interviewer will take note of who answered.

    Report time is 8.30am and should be finished between 12pm to 12.30pm.
