    Originally posted by Anyhoow:

    How's your AD Interview?

    I applied on 2 Dec 2016 and till now I receive no reply. The wait is so long!

    It went ok i guess? Interviewers looks tired and bored. Probably cos I was the last few for the batch that day.

    Psychometric test was different from the one i did 4 years ago. Instead of shading, now it is all computerised. No more IQ style questions like patterns/numbers. Now is more of situational on how you choose to handle/logical questions from reading passages. Have to write and elaborate on your past experiences as well. Like what was stressful, your best and worst qualities etc. The whole thing was so draining i slept from 6pm when i got home until this morning. 100+ mcq as well as 15 or so questions you need to elaborate on. Yesterday i received an email on full body pic. Felt so demoralised cos of overweight issue? Am kind of chubby but definitely not obese. Fingers crossed that they are lenient if not i might be rejected just based on that. Sighhh. Ill lose weight if they give me the green light to next round. XD

    If you noticed my timeline, it was 3 months from online application to first round then another 1 month+ to second round. It has been Oct until now and I'm only on round 2 so I can only can ask you to be patient and hang on there. :)
