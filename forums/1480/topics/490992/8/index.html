    Good day guys, 

    Only just bothered to create an account today to provide some help for newer applicants. Just went for my Dialogue Session today..

     

    Anyways, for new applicants, Selection Assessment process goes like this.

    Attendance taking

    Filling up of several pages of personal particulars forms, including immediate family particulars, occupation and office location as well, so get those ready.

    Administrative instructions for Psychometric Test

    Several Time-sensitive tests which requires full attention to the statements and answers, followed by another test which is OTOT..

    Lunch

    Physical assessment (height n weight, declaration of tattoo, etc), no fitness test.

    Submission of documents.

    For the documents, apart from what was listed, you could also prepare your passbook, driver's license, necessary certifications, all original and photocopied. Don't worry if you missed out anything, they'll verify again during the dialogue session if you're shortlisted.

    Pardon me if missed out some details and also if i'm not that precise cuz i dunno if these are meant to be in this forum..

    SA should end before 5pm..

    For the dialogue session, i'm sure many of the above posts alr mentioned. Just be yourself i guess.. Eat some breakfast before hand cuz there might be some waiting..

     

    Cheers and good luck to all.. Hope to see some of you in future..
