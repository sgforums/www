    Hi! I just received the offer call today. Here is my timeline, hope it helps. 

    Nov 2016: Applied

    Feb 2017: Selection Assessment (verification of documents, essay writing, physical - height, weight, check arms) 

    April 2017: AD Interview (briefing, uniform measurement, psychometric test, interview with 2 officers from manpower) 

    April 2017: Psychologist Interview (1-on-1 chat with psychologist)

    April 2017: Board Interview (5 interviewers, generic questions similar to AD interview)

    May 2017: Medical (blood, urine, hearing, colour vision tests, chest x-ray) 

    June 2017: Medical Follow-Up (my hearing test had problems earlier)

    June 2017: Offer Call

    I had some very long periods of waiting, and got super disheartened at times. But the offer call finally came! To those waiting, don't give up yet :)
