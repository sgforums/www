    @Mulberry and Naddy

    Hi to the both of you.. As you may not know, this is a senior police officer intake post (direct entry-inspector), hence the interview process is very different from JPO(direct entry-sergeant). Most of the post that you read here is for SPO which include 5 stages, 

    1) Selection Assessment

    2) Dialogue Session

    3) Clincial Interview

    4) Board Interview

    5) Medical

    All this may take up to 6 months or more to process. I believe for JPO's selection process is shorter than SPO, thus you may not need to wait for 6 months to know your result. Again you may want to find a post that is related to your direct entry-sergeant which will be more beneficial to you guys. They will have more knowledges and comments to advise you. 

    For the rest who are actually applying for SPO, some advises from me are really show them why u choose police as a career, from what i know they are short of JPO than SPO.

    When i went for SA there are at least 40 applicants, at Dialogue we are left with 20, lastly at board interview (clincial interview is one to one, so i do not know the number of people left) only 7 of us left. I know most of them at board interview because we see each other since SA, so the cut off rate is very high. What the few of us have in common is we are all doing part time job and waiting to get into SO. 

    All the best to those who are still applying as a SO!! will try to come online and answer some of your question if i can.. Cheers!!
