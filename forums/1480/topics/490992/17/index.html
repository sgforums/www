    Just finished my Dialogue Session.

    I fumbled a little but managed to answer most all the questions fairly well I hope.

    One point to note for those who are going for the dialogue session, the answers you give for the questions they ask will determine the type of questions they will ask subsequently. So there's no template set of questions they will ask you.

    They are quite daunting but try not to get affected by their stance. Be steady and be confident. All the best!
