    Dengue fever(Non life threatening)
    -High fever(bingo)
    -Bone and muscle aches
    -Burning sensation and redness in the eyes
    -Cough and runny nose
    -Diarrhoea
    -Rash, skin flush

    Now this one, this one kills...its a complication

    Dengue Haemorrhagic fever
    -Abdominal pain and vomitting lasting 2-4days
    -Severe bleeding-nosebleeds, bleeding from the gums and under the skin, vomitting of blood, rectal bleeding, tarry stools indicative of GI bleeding
    -red pinprick-sized spots on skin due to spontaneous bruising.

    If you strike on 3 or more of the dengue symptoms...GO SEE A DOCTOR...yes, you will need a blood test, but think of it...since it sux now, might as well get it treated and over with right?
