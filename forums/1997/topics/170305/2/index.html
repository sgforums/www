  • Rigante
    23 Dec 05, 01:53
    You can leave Pulau Tekong now. Don't hesitate. In Temasek (or Winter wonderland), you have many options to level up faster. Visit the ice rinks and gazebo inside to get gold when you answer correctly. Visit Seth in the Hotel (only when you have used up your turns) to get additonal fights or gems or gold. Talk to Cedrik in the hotel and exchange gems for vitality to up your hitpoints. Have extra gold? Then visit Saucy's kitchen and enjoy a hot meal there - results vary. With little gold, you can visit Audrey's kittens when she comes - either lose charm or gain forest fights. The rest I'll leave you to explore

    Ps: You can get killed in Winter Wonderland, so advisable not to thrill-seek and to heal yourself often.

    Have fun

    Cloud
