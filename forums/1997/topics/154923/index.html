    The recent reports abt schools acting swifting to discipline students who blog about their teachers with profanities has been well covered.

    The thing abt blogs is that people tend to reveal their aggressive nature. Anger is usually the first thing they sprout. Biased impressions of people are next and that merely adds fuel to the fire. In a school where one teacher may be ostricised because of over-exaggerated rumours, rumour mills will start going and stories become more far-fetched.

    The result is when they hit the blogs, the stories and comments become outright fantasy.

    People have been given the right and freedom to express themselves. But they also have to be responsible and mature about it. Stay objective and focus more on mistakes by teachers and how they can better address them.

    No student is going to be penalised for that.
