    Katong Shopping Centre, Basement food court, Western food / Chicken Rice

    the western food stall gives their servings in large quantities and it's cheap. $3.80 for chicken chop rice and the chicken chop is thick and large. you will feeel its really worth $3.80. one recommendation is their lamb chops. it's not as bony as those sold in other western food stalls. meaty and worth the money

    chicken rice stall on the other hand is very expensive. unless you tell specifically that you one person serving, if not it's ard $4 per person if i remember correctly. but the rice is fragrant and the steamed chicken is nice and tender. only try if you have spare cash
