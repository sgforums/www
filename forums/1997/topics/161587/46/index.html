    Antidepressant - Prozac or Luvox
    These medications will increase her serotonin chemicals in her brain reducing her sadness and anxiety altogether...

    On a more psychological basis, an insight oriented dynamic psychotherapy and a hypnosis or behavioral therapy may be needed...

    The insight oriented dynamic psychotherapy is aimed at helping her to understand why she believes that she has an illness and tries to make some sense into it by explaining to her how it happens... Since it is dynamic, the doctor will play an active role by participating in the treatment more...

    Behavior therapy is to induce relaxation, as her recurrent thoughts about the illness will bring up anxiety symptoms...

    It has to be known that this is a chronic condition, and relapses will be caused mostly by stress from work or family or life...
