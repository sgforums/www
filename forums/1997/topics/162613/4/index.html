    Anyway, the BM part:

    how much does this cost?
    Berapakah harga untuk barang ini? (or you can substitute it with the name of the thing)

    what is this? (pointing to something)
    Apa ini?

    what is the time now?
    Pukul berapa? (informal way of asking for time)

    excuse me
    Tumpang lalu (if you want to pass by someone)

    thank you
    Terima kasih

    it's my pleasure/you're welcome
    Sama-sama

    sorry
    Maafkan saya

    do you know where (this person) is??
    Kamu tahu (person's name) di mana?

    how do i get to (this place)?
    Boleh beritahu saya bagaimana saya boleh pergi ke tempat ini (or place name here)?
