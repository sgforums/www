    Lemon Lime Sorbet

    Difficulty Level: Very easy

    1 cup sugar (250grams)
    3/4 cup lemon juice (around 200ml )
    3/4 cup lime juice
    2 egg whites beaten

    1.) Dissolve sugar with water in pant. Bring to boil, reduce low heat and simmer 5 minutes at least. Let it cool
    2.) Add lemon n live juice to syrup. Pour onto metal tray, cover and freeze 2 hours. Transfer icy mixture to food processor bowl and beat to a slush.
    3.) Repeat step beating and freezing process twice more.
    4.) Transfer to bowl or food processor. When beating, add the egg whites. Freeze until firm. Voila~!
