    http://www.dbs.nus.edu.sg/programmes/grad/index.htm

    GRADUATION CAP REQUIREMENTS

     

    Student Matters - Graduate Research Students

    (i) Continuation Requirements

    Master's : CAP should not fall below 2.50 or equivalent for 2 consecutive semesters.

    Ph.D: CAP should not fall below 3.00 or equivalent for 2 consecutive semesters.

     

    Students who CAP fall below the minimum continuation requirement in the first semester will be placed on academic probation and will have their candidature terminated should their CAP remain below the continuation requirement in the next semester.

     

    (ii) Graduation Requirements

    Master's:            (1) Minimum CAP of 3.00 for all required modules;

                            (2) Passed Master's thesis; &

                            (3) Satisfactory grade* in English course (intermediate level), where applicable.

     

    Ph.D:                (1) Minimum CAP of 3.50 for all required modules;

                            (2) Satisfactory grade* in English course (advanced level), where applicable;

                            (3) Passed Qualifying Examination;

                            (4) Passed Ph.D Thesis; &

                            (5) Passed Oral Examinations.

     

    *Note: Satisfactory grade for English module has been raised from grade "D" to "C" wef July 2003 intake (BGS minutes 24 Jan 2003)

     

     

     

    Note:  Update from Ms Yvonne Seah of DRGS (email dd 20 May 2005 refers).
