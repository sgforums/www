    Krobeen thus began his involvement with the Order of the Phoenix from that day onwards. On orders from Joe Albuquerque, he was to be taught the Book of Shadows by Harvey. There were three other students under his tutelage. Their names were Jack, Rachel and Yan-xin. The families of the other three youths were resistance fighters and did undercover work for the Order. From conversations with the trio, Krobeen learnt that one needed to be well-versed in the Book os Shadows before he could be commissioned as a full member of the Order. As the last surviving descendant of Lionel MacLaggan the Half-blood Prince, it was all the more important for him to be leaned in the sacred tome of Wicca. Many of Rainee's government buildings had protective magic around them and one had to be able to break curses in order to infiltrate them.

    From Jack, Rachel and Yan-xin, Krobeen gathered that Rainee had put a price on the Raven's head following his desertion of the Black Knives. Her Interior Ministry forces managed to track him down to the tower of Orthanc where Starblue was imprisoned. They were ordered to kill the pair, but when they arrived, the bird had flown and the traitor was already dead. Immediately, "outside involvement" was suspected, and resistance groups placed under scrutiny. At the moment, there was no choice but to conceal Kobeen and Starblue at their respective locations.

    From his interaction with the three, Krobeen noticed that both Jack and Yan-xin were vying for Rachel's heart, but strangely that did not affect their firendship. In fact, the trio were virtually inseparable. They showed up for lessons together and sat side by side in the dining hall. he himself wondered how Starblue was doing. Nobody could tell him the location of the safe house where Starblue was held. He only knew it was in Puddlemere. But Puddlemere was a large city.
