    Well you don't want to get hit by something doing 700 feet per sec (fps) at less than 100ft. Trust me on that. Actually its a bit inaccurate to say muzzle velocity is restricted. Its more about the energy of the projectile fired. Because the weight of the projectiles we fire varies from 0.12g to around 0.5g, muzzle velocity is dependent on the weight of the projectile but the energy is consistant. A lighter projectile will be fired with a higher initial muzzle velocity, but have shorter range due to lower momentum, while a heavier projectile fires with an initially lower muzzle velocity but flys further due to a higher momentum. But both will fire with the same energy, and the energy limit in UK is 1 joule.
