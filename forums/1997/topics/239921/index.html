    BIRTH TEST

    Once you have discovered your Birth Number, send this email to your friends, so they can enjoy the fun too.

    Your birth date describes who you are, what you are good at and what your inborn abilities are.

    It also points to what you have to learn and the challenges you may face. To figure out your Birth Number, add all the numbers in the birth date together, like in the example below, until there is only one digit.

    Example: March 20, 1950

    (March) 03 + 20 + 1950 = 1973

    03

    20

    1950

    1973

    1 + 9 + 7 + 3 = 20

    2 + 0 = 2

    Keep going until you end up with a single digit number.

    2 is the Birth Number to read for the birth date in the example.
