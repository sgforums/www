Let's talk about L.O.V.E.

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    13 Feb 08, 17:08

    .....as Valentine's day is around the corner, lovers are busying planning their candle light dinner, buying of flowers, gifts, saying those lovey dovey words......

    Whether you are single, attached or married, what are your thoughts when the word "love" comes into your mind?

    As for myself, love is the most important element for living besides air and water. Devoid of love, nothing you posess is ever enough. To know that you're love unconditionally is the most precious emotional possession you can ever have. To be loved is easy, to love is hard. Love knows no boundary, it cuts through race, age,religions and nationality. A chinese can love an african, buddhist married a christian, a 60 years old man married a 30 years old woman...etc etc.

    Love is a simple thing but turned complicated by human beings. Love had caused many hearts to be broken, inflicted endless pain on luckless souls. And why still so many people want to pursue this crazy little thing called "love"? Love can be summed up like a boat in a stormy sea, if you can sail it out, the rewards are abundant. Calm sea, clear blue water and beautiful sunset.

    Love is hope. With hope, it gives you a reason to live, reason to fight for another day, reason to say "Thanks God, I'm alive". To have love in your heart is not always easy to achieve. Love can leaves you, love can walk out on you and love can kill you. Treat love with tender loving care, it is fragile and delicate. Once love leaves you, it might never come back.

    With love in your life, the food taste nicer, the jokes are funnier, the day is brighter and the night is never too dark. Love shines through from your heart and glow in your face. You smile more and laugh much. Love not only brings joy to yourself but to others. Love is a wonderful thing that money cannot buy. So always remember, when love walks in, hold on tight.
