    Originally posted by starblue:

    so.... for longer distances it's safer to take cab. but if it's a short dist, say from first hotel to siam, can take tuk tuk right? why wud tuk tuks cost more?

    Eh...cos tuk tuk will just charge you more cos it is up to them to name the price. For cabs, must make sure the driver turns the meter on before you let them drive off. Otherwise you might be paying for double the price. If they refuse, just get out and get another cab.

    Another thing about tuk tuk that I have read about is that some of them get commissions from certain places/restaurants, so if you ask them to bring you to a certain place, they might bring you to sumwhere where they can get commission from the shop owner. Happened before to one of my friends who got driven to a "fake" restaurant instead of the real one she wanted to go to...

    As for Patpong, yea it is in a red light district area...most recommended if you want to have a taste of night life. I am avoiding it cos I have no interest to experience gay bars, etc lolz.

    Suam Lum market, heard from my friends the price of things there are ridiculously high. You can get things cheaper at wholesale markets such as Platinum Mall.
