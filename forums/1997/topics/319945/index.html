    Not sure about Bangkok.... I've been to Chiang Mai and Kanchanaburi, though.

    You can get around using tuk-tuks and the red vans. Tuk-tuks are more expensive, but more fun to ride in. Make sure you haggle the price with the driver before boarding.

    For the most part, they speak English, but if you're gonna do a lot of bargaining, I suggest bringing a small calculator to indicate your price to them.

    Otherwise, Thailand is a generally safe place to be in, just exercise the same level of discretion and safety as you would when visiting any foreign country.
