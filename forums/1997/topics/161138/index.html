    TITLE: NECK TWISTER

    This happened in Genting Highlands 4 years back when my cousin decided to take his parents, his wife and his two kids up to GH to have fun at the theme park and at the casino...

    They had so much fun travelling up to that place, and when they reach there, they booked a room in one of the hotels, and quickly, the kids rushed into one of the rooms and reserved their own beds screaming, yelling and jumping up and down the beds like never before...

    They threw the blankets and started a pillow fight between each other...

    It was approaching evening soon, and after a quick dinner and a nice long stroll, they decided to pull in for the night. The two kids shared the room with their parents, while my cousin's parents slept at the other room next door.

    Halfway through the night, one of the kid (the youngest) started to have a stiff neck, and found that he couldn't turn his head to both directions... he started to freak out and walked out from the bed like a zombie, since he could only see in one direction... he started to shout and scream and cried out loud waking up his parents and brother...

    My cousin brother was shocked to see his son's condition and called for his parents to come over. There was physically nothing wrong with him during the whole trip today, but he was unable to move his head now... His brother was fine and he already started to freak out when suddenly he also gave out a loud cry... The grandmother asked him why are you crying? Is everything okay with you?

    He pointed and shivered as his fingers directed towards the right of his grandmother... he said, ah ma! there is a lady with long hair standing behind you....

    At that point in time... all rushed out from the room, still in their night gown and prowled all the way with their luggage into the lobby and related the whole incident to the manager...

    Of course, the manager denied the fact that it was real...

    The truth behind it was that this lady had attempted suicide after her boyfriend had allegedly dumped her for another girl during their vacation in the resort itself... She was found hanged in the room about 5 years back...

    TITLE: HANDS AND AXES...

    Deep in the reserve forest of Pahang, about 50 years ago during the Japanese occupation, life was tough for Mr A as he could not work to earn enough money after the impose of curfew throughout the country. He had to sneak out at night to the jungle at times to avoid the Japanese soldiers who believed that he is giving out information of military positions of the Japanese base to the enemy.

    Sneaky as he was, he was finally caught by one of the soldiers. In an attempt to run off from the soldier, he tripped and fell off from a tree root. The Japanese soldier cut off his right hand with his bayonet and stab him on his leg. He managed to fend off by throwing his personal axe towards the Japanese's head.

    As it was dark, he could not police a search for his hand as there were more soldiers lurking nearby. He grabbed a first aid kit from the soldier and quickly wrapped his hand with a bandage. He ran deeper towards the jungle but never did survived long enough for the severe shock he suffered from the blood loss. However, his body was never seen, not up till today when the forest has declined in its area and now preserved as a forest reserve.

    Students who carry out their research or study in that jungle would often encounter Mr A. He would appear as a normal person, explained one of the students. He would appear to be looking for something that is missing, and at the same time holding up his right arm still wrapped with the bandage. Some of the students did mention that they were not afraid of him as he appeared normal until he pop the question to them in Hokkien...

    Ah ba, ah ba... lu oo kua tio wa eh chiu boh? (Have you seen my hand?)

    Once he complete the question, he would disappear in thin air.
