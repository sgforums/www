    Angina does not result from reduced blood volume...

    Angina results when there is a disproportion between supply and demand...

    Your coronary arteries get clogged up because of atherosclerosis and hence the distal (after the blockage) portion of the arteries collapse and stop supplying the parts they used to supply, hence leading to infarction and necrosis (myocardial infarction).
