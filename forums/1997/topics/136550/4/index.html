    Ever heard of this phrase,"Self-Fulfilling Prophecy" ? It is also called the Pygmalion Effect.

    The concept of the self-fulfilling prophecy can be summarized in these key principles:
    - We form certain expectations of people or events
    - We communicate those expectations with various cues.
    - We tend to respond to these cues by adjusting their behavior to match them.
    - The result is that the original expectation becomes true.
    This creates a circle of self-fulfilling prophecies.

    So if you think you will succeed, you will adjust your behavior to move you towards achieving success. If you keep thinking about failure, you will adjust your behavior towards achieving failure.
