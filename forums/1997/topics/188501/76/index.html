    go watch something.. out of the ordinary.. go to the Picturehouse, go watch a few arthouse movies... go to a museum, slowly look at and admire the paintings.. go look around and see everything as if you're looking at it for the first time.. go look at art and photograph exhibitions...

    visual....

    then, listen to other things instead of the stuff you usually listen to.. try some Pearl Jam, think about the words and why they write and sing those words.. listen to some classical music... think about the emotions the musicians and composers were trying to convey... listen to rock, heavy metal, grunge, punk.. and imagine the primal need to scream, for loudness.. listen to sarah mclachlan and think about that beautiful voice she has... and the words she sings.. and again, as if music is totally new to you.. do not resist it.. let it envelope you...

    music...

    and then, dance.. watch some performances.. if you cannot go see them, go to the shops and borrow "Lord of the Dance", and other things like that... watch it... enjoy it... think about what drives people to dance... and plays.. watch some shakespeare and enjoy the words they use...

    then all over again and figure out which one you enjoy most.. which one moves you the most...

    you'll be fine
