    MRT - Mass Rapid Transit 24 July 1996

    MRT, a transport i take
    to and fro everyday for convenience's sake
    have established a relation
    a loyal commuter since it's creation
    it is equipped with bright coloured plastic seats
    enclosed in an air-conditioned environment away from the Sun's heat
    big see-through panels are placed on both sides of the train all round
    to let commuters have a panaromic view of the beautiful scenery especially
    when it's travelling above ground
    there are railings and handles for standing commuters
    so more of them could be accommodated without any fluster
    safety measures are implemented
    fire extinguishers and emergency alarms fitted
    to add on to it's facilities, there is music
    though i think it's gotta do with physics
    listen intently as the train moves along the tracks
    those rhythms from the rattling of the train on the tracks rise and fall,
    to and back
    and the chorus starts as the train enters underground
    melodic screeching and roaring inside the tunnels echo around
    so all non-MRT commuters, go and take a trip
    guarantee to make you flip!
