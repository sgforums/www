    My Club - Nottingham Forest 19 Apr 1996

    i support Nottingham Forest Football Club
    an activity for me to indulge
    amidst the city's hub
    now here's their history
    a fairy-tale story

    1865 it was formed
    Forest Football Club it was called
    'Forest' referred in the club name was Forest Racecourse or Recreation Ground
    moved to present home in 1898 named City Ground
    honours came few and far
    2 FA Cup victories in 1898, 1959, coupled with 2nd division titles
    in 1907, 1922 so far

    1975 heralded of great achievements to come
    indeed Brian Clough and Peter Taylor did come
    under their partnership,
    Forest finally won their first league Championship
    it was in 1978
    and gave all their sceptics a heartache
    followed which winners of other titles come up ...
    1978, 1979, 1989, 1990 Football League Cup
    1977 Anglo-Scottish Cup
    1989 Simod Cup
    1992 Zenith Data Systems Cup

    Forest's foray into foreign compeitions brought them fame
    sending their local counterparts to shame
    here are winners of those Cups ...
    1962, 1968 Fairs Cup
    1979, 1980 European Cup
    1984, 1985 UEFA Cup
    1980 Super Cup

    all in all, Forest a good fighter
    but i like to term them hunter
    come hell or high water
    Forest will fight water with fire
    if need to be, fire with fire
    Hail Forest !
    eliminate your oppositions i dub them pest
    co's you are simply the best !

    now as the fairy-tale ends
    my undying support for you never ends
    Long Live Nottingham Forest !!!
