    The subject is IPPTMADNESS

    "Nominal role oh nominal role
    how many lines must I scroll,
    nric, ranks, names and units
    pen and paper, a tick and a ruler"

    "YOU HAVE NOT BEEN UPDATING IT
    says my officer, cowardly as I am
    to speak of an arguement is to
    bring forth tragedy into my arms"

    "Comparing comparing, letters alphabets
    ranks and numbers non-stop,
    how I wish I can do them all
    in a single day"

    "Thanks to a virus, I am scared
    of using Marcros, write my own VB macro
    to sort out this mess of comparing"

    "Thanks to faulty installation, I cannot
    use Access to compare databases,
    all thanks to heaven that made the rain
    fall on my skin."

    "Vulgarities, expletives, not-withstanding,
    how I wish I can get out of this NS mess,
    how I wish I could buy myself out of NS
    so that I can climb forward and get out of this hole, a hole that someone digged and said that WAR is an ILLUSION"

    "It trapped my head."

    This poem is dedicated to TerrePanda, Stingray and myself.

    TerrePanda, are you confused with
    black and white?
    Right and wrong?
    Just follow the book
    the snake is shining its light
    on its words, an open book
    a crest of Ngee Ann Polytechnic

    Stingray, you are like an arrowhead
    that points nowhere, useful but not used,
    dont you know that yellow and red
    are the colours of the mastery of tools?
    tools get outdated over time, but
    at least you felt useful, the applause
    and the glory, you will never forget
    see the crest of Singapore Polytechnic
    , its lion ontop of a compass, the indicator
    of mastery of tools, strive to be like them to be a master of tools

    Have you seen the crest of NTU?
    a lion motiff on a castle wall
    hidden behind are the gears and energies
    do you want to study there?
    and be like a lion motiff, because anywhere you work, you are like a motiff for them, you make sure you keep things going,

    Learn to be independent like the lion
    on NUS's crest, the books and circles on
    one platform, and yourself on another
    platform, they can never fall ontop of you, you can choose to do anything you want with them.

    JUST A CRAZY POEM!
