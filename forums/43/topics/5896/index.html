After realizing that the damage he had caused with his battle against the Devil, he was filled with remorse that he had caused the life of his loved one in danger. It was the love of the mortal woman, and in the end, both of them lost the mortal woman to Death, because it was fated that the Endless could never love a mortal. If the love was consummated, cities would burn and the Earth as we know it, would suffer from hellfire. He would knew that if he would beg the god of destruction for help to seal the anomaly, he would be utterly humiliated. But there is no choice. The fate of the cosmos is by far, much higher in magnitude than personal pride, whether the Prodigal could rationalize all these out.

    He was in pain, only deeply in pain, as he stepped out of the door. He wished that he could kiss his love goodbye, but he did not want her to know that he would seek destruction on his own path. Such was his way of living. He was walking down the corridor, towards the exit of Death's palace. As he was about to leave the Nexus, a feminine voice spoke,"Going somewhere, my dear brother."

    "Yes," Prodigal said,"I have to go to Savana."

    "You are leaving Runoa here," Death said,"don't you love her?"

    "Yes, I do," Prodigal nodded his head as he could not face Death in the eye."You must understand that to love someone is not to let someone suffer with you."

    "Yes," Death shared the Prodigal's sentiments,"But if I would love that someone, I would go with him in all my heart, even to die with him. Isn't that the mortal way, how it should be, my brother? Isn't that love? Are you not hurting her by leaving her here, and face the danger against Devestation alone?"

    "But, I cannot let her to suffer, " the Prodigal said,"It is between Devestation and me."

    "Yes, yes, you and him, both of you are always at loggerheads," Death sighed,"So, you are seeking him out on your own."

    "Yes." the Prodigal said, "Death, I would like to go now, take care of Runoa for me, and I would be back soon." He turned his head and was about to leave.

    "Please stop here, my love," a voice had suddenly stopped the Prodigal, as he was about to open the door and leave the place. It was the voice of his love. "Even if you have to face the enemy, I would like to be with you, till death do we part." Those words touched the wanderer, but he was at a loss of words. The wanderer walked towards his love and hugged her, muttering words of apology as he embraced her with his arms.

    Death was shaking her head, and realized that it was going to be a difficult situation for the two of them... "Why are they behaving like mortals?" Death said to herself as she shook her head. "Sometimes, I really wonder why these two Endless are turning so much mortal. We have a different purpose in our existence."

    "S.A. Tan," the Prodigal gasped, and he was in surprised why the Devil had come to the domain of Death. "Why are you here in the Nexus?"

    "Of course, I am not here to pick another battle with you," said the Devil, as he shot a glance at Death, who was not duly impressed with his entrance into her realm. "I am here to negotiate a deal with you."

    "A deal?" Prodigal could not believe his ears,"After all that humiliation I have made you, I am sure your heart is consumed with the fire to destroy me now."

    "Well, always remember that the Devil may not be a forgiving person, but there are no forever enemies or friends in this world. I am here on the pretext as a businessman."

    "Yes, so what is your deal?" the Prodigal said, as he clenched tightly to Runoa's hand.

    "Before that, let me show you something," the Devil conjured a sphere and the image in the sphere was a little gal trapped inside a cage in hell. "This is your daughter, Valentine, the product of 2 Endless, you and Runoa who holds the profile of True Love in the future of our Universe, and she is in my hands now."

    Prodigal and Runoa was shocked as they stared into the sphere, and found an uneasiness in them, that the gal was somehow related in blood with them. Especially the blue crystal eyes in the little gal's eyes, both Prodigal and Runoa could not help but to believe what the Devil had said.

    "S.A. Tan, you are so despicable to use a little gal to trick my brother," Death attacked the Devil with her words.

    "No, my sister, he is right, that is our daughter, both Runoa and I can feel it," the Prodigal said. "Let's cut the long story short, what is the deal?"

    "Well, Prodigal, I am aware that our battle had caused an anomaly in our Universe, and this anomaly had not affected you, but me as well. I would get you the thing you need from Devestation in Savana. U need his flail so that you can effectively use your powers to seal the anomaly. If you could do something for me, I would return your daughter in the deal which I proposed as she is suffering from extermination of her own existence because Runoa has somehow passed away in her timeline."

    "Don't trust him, Brother," Death said, as she was pondering how to help her brother out fo the situation.

    "Well, the Devil came with a deal which has no choice," the Prodigal said, as he made a short glance from his love that they had to save that little gal. "S.A. Tan, tell me what you want from me, but if you do not return my daughter and Devestation's flail after I have done the thing for you, you would expect my fury in hell."

    "U have my promise, Prodigal," said the Devil. "Here is the thing which I want you to do : you must go to the Seven heavens and see the Creator, and inform him of a plot by the Archangel Gabriel to revolt against him. I want this to end as fast as possible, as it was caused by the anomaly which our battle had somehow affected Heaven. I have news from my associates that Gabriel is going to revolt against the Creator, and lead the angels to a war against humanity. However, he is met by resistance in the community of Angels in Heaven. Hence to secure his power, he has plotted against the Creator. Your job is to warn the Creator i.e. that stupid old man who is trapped in his own shell after leaving the world to me and the angels to play with."

    "That is strange, S.A. Tan, why would you want to help the resistance and the Creator to deal with Gabriel? Afterall, it does not benefit you at all. The Creator would just quell the rebellion by reversing the effect caused by the anomaly to Gabriel."

    "Although I hate the old man for what he done to me, nevertheless, my purpose to eliminate Gabriel and assist the resistance rests on a fundamental assumption here : There can only be one Hell. If Gabriel successfully lead the revolution, heaven and earth would change, and revelation would seriously need a new editor. But never trust the bible, since it was written by Man and not by the old man."

    "Well, to secure your throne in Hell, you have to stop Gabriel before a great war would lead to him holding a similar realm like you. Good thought," the Prodigal said,"Despite that I am not interested between your feud with the Creator, nevertheless, I would fulfil your part of the deal by going to Heaven."

    "Then after you fulfil your part of the deal, we would meet to seal the anomaly, before it is going to cause more damage to our existence." S.A. Tan said, as he disappeared into thin air.

    "Deal," the Prodigal said as he saw the road ahead filled with obstacles and difficulties.

    "Are you sure you are going to do this?" Death said,"It is not easy to gain audience with the Creator, even if you are an Endless. Besides, you would have to deal against Gabriel."

    "I have to, since S.A. Tan wants to seal the anomaly as well," said the Prodigal,"Well, my sister, please do me a favour, please let Runoa stay in the Nexus and protect her from harm."

    "Prodigal," Runoa looked at him with the notion that she wished to follow him.

    "No, my dear love, I would want to have your company very much with me," said the Prodigal,"But this is far too dangerous. I want nothing to harm you, and you must be protected at all costs, otherwise, our daughter would die."

    "Alright, come back safely," Runoa said, as she kissed her knight deeply with her lips sealed to his. There was not a moment that both of them knew that it was an ordeal to them, and they had to support each other to carry through the test.
