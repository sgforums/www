    A poem, that describe my process of soul searching.

    Soul search

    My soul wanders in the void,
    searching for it's destination,
    without a compass, it travels,
    without a purpose, it thinks.

    Out of life's clutters,
    it tries to find simplicity.
    Admist chaos,
    it looks for harmony,

    Was a mistake made, yesterday?
    It does not know,
    for the mistake was only an action,
    that was not meant to be.

    My soul sees light,
    a beautiful sight.
    Enlightenment lies beyond,
    and wisdom is the prize.

    With morale high,
    and "Onward Bound" it shouts,
    towards the destination,
    it has just found.

    I SAW U STANDING THERE

    I saw you standing there.
    I didn't dare go near.
    I knew what I had done.
    Of you, I was most scared.

    I watched from a hill.
    I watched you wait.
    Wait for someone.
    Someone who would never show.

    Who could it be?
    You waited so long,
    so intently.
    It did not seem to bother you.
    The hours passed and you waited.
    For who?

    Who was it that you wanted so badly?
    Or was it that you just wanted to stand alone?
    But that's not you.

    I get enough courage to go see you.
    To ask who you are waiting for.
    I confront you.
    But dont ask who it is you wait for.
    Instead I run away.
    Back to the spot where I watched you.

    Only when I had returned you were gone.
    I only saw a single rose.
    I walked back down and saw a note attached.
    I read it curiosly.

    It was to your only love.
    I could only hope that was me.
    I read on.

    It said that you were leaving.
    Leaveing permently.
    A tear fell from my left eye.
    I sat down on the bench.

    I thought:
    I never meant to say those mean words.
    I would take it all back.

    I read further,
    you wrote that you were sorry.
    I didnt know why you would be sorry.
    For it was me who wronged you.

    The note continued with,
    a simple I love you,
    and good bye forever.
    More tears began to fall.

    As I weeped it started raining.
    A man walking by asked if I was ok.
    I simply replied "good bye sir"
    I went back to where I had watched you,
    the rose and note in my hand.
    I smelled the sweet scent.
    My favorite flower.

    I sat on this hill side,
    where I had seen you for the last time.
    I cried more.
    The rain blended with my tears.
    The roses scent grew stronger.
    I placed the note in my pocket.

    I left this place after many hours.
    I walked everywhere in the rain.
    I finally arrived home.
    But it wasn't right.
    You weren't there,
    to tell me that everything would be ok.

    I began to cry again.
    I layed the rose on a table.
    I reached in my pocket for the note,
    but it had gotten too wet and fell apart.
    I fell to the floor,
    and folded my arms around my knees,
    where I rested my head as I cried.

    The phone rang.
    I gatherd my myself,
    and answered the phone.
    They said you were dying in the street.
    I dropped the phone.

    I ran to you,
    held you in my arms.
    I told you how I was watching you the whole time.
    And that I was scared to see you
    You said that you knew.

    More tears came.
    You told me not to cry,
    that you would be in heaven soon.

    I told you that I love you.
    You told me you knew that too.
    You then closed you eyes,
    and said "good-bye my love."
    I kissed you one last time,
    and said"good bye,for now."
    For I knew that I would soon see you again.

    The Field where I died
    ======================

    I stood by the field...
    only to feel the whistling wind....
    flow with the running waters....
    dance with the swaying grasses...
    experience the call of nature....

    Too much beauty passed me by......
    Too much uglyness swept me across...
    Too much hate that could not seduce me to evil...
    and too much love which I don't know whether it was true or not...

    I gasped at the last breath of my life,
    the beat of my heart almost come to a standstill,
    My senses began to wither and slow
    for the blood in my heart running low...

    I want to let her go,
    but all those love and hate which pervaded my mind,
    all those sorrows and joy which injure my heart,
    all those pain and tears which scarred my soul...

    If there was a beginning,
    there will be an end..

    Perhaps, the field where I died...

    that this phase of my life's over
    and it's time to go into the next cycle.....
