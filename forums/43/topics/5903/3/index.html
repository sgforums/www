    Here's the untitled poem. Dedicated to a group of friends who graduated one year before me

    I sat there thinking
    Of an opening
    For a poem with meaning
    Dedicate to you people who are graduating.

    Sadness wells
    I rather not dwell
    Thinking of happy moments that were really swell
    It always rings a bell.

    The times we spent
    Often till our very last cent
    Our curfews we reluctantly bent
    Chatting and laughing to our hearts content

    Our 'sim su' sessions
    Was a time for confession
    To get rid of the depression
    It never fails to leave an impression.

    Do remember this, it is a must
    Do not let our friendship turn into dust
    Something so beautiful, 'coz it's build on trust
    Unlike iron, it's not meant to rust.

    And so, forget me not
    Don't let your memory rot
    Although on the map our country is just a dot
    It's here that i found you precious lot!

    Fate

    The day I first saw light,
    I knew someone was there.
    One who makes me whole,
    One who fulfilled my life.

    The moment I knew it was you,
    My destiny was found.
    My life thus have a purpose,
    to find you, now my mission.

    We were once united,
    but now physically divided.
    With fate as my compass,
    I sought our union.

    When fate brought me to you,
    I found it could only do so much,
    for you were held captive,
    and a compass don't help much.

    Thus I use my key,
    for I knew it to open all.
    One so compassionate,
    this key I call love.

    With love I found your freedom,
    but you were alone and bare.
    Thus I became your armor,
    and we are whole once more.

    I thank fate for finding thee,
    but my love did just as much.
    And with me as your armor,
    Your heart will hurt no more.

    ------------------

    I fight this war in Honour of my princess. LIBERATION!
