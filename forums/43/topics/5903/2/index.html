    Dream by the passing lane
    -------------------------
    Nothing but a cold and chilling nite
    upon the soft bed there I lie,
    Tired and exhausted almost to die,
    closed and forgotten are my eyes.

    My senses gone and slumber dominates,
    Was I asleep or was I awake?
    Only when the world changed colour,
    Remaining only fascinating images
    which eternally invaded me
    in body, heart and soul.

    Was it a calm wind by the passing lane,
    Beyond all, a mansion seen above the hills,
    prompting me to cross the mill,
    In curosity, I followed,
    with an inner voice telling me
    that someone was waiting there for me.

    In the mansion, there I search,
    through endless dimensions and doors,
    finally, I heard a familiar voice,
    SheÂ’s there, my heart beat rapidly,
    That cannot be, my mind debated clearly,
    I must enter, my soul coerced every part of me.

    only a simple remark that surprised me :
    Â“Why have you come so late to pick me?Â”
    Puzzled and shocked,
    I still held her hand
    and then we left.

    Was it her voice which comfort me,
    or was it that feeling of love in me.
    But I thought she was to be forgotten,
    to be faded off my mind forever
    in order to ease my pain
    Why is there a hundred and eighty degrees change?
    Why was the dream so seductive
    as to surpass and dominate
    every whim of my thoughts.

    Simple is the Reason of my Heart
    --------------------------------
    All but a quiet afternoon in the Grantchester Orchard,
    Ponder upon a relaxing and sagging seat,
    Pervaded by chilly winds, yet I see :
    So powerful is the sun,
    who stares with an intense glow of brilliance,
    So deep is the blue sky,
    with a long streaming trail of dispersed smoke and dust,
    So strong are the apple trees in Autumn,
    with the arriving winter stripping half her branches bare,
    So tattered and messy is the bed of grass,
    with brown leaves and apples strewn down under.

    The soul seeks redemption from the heart
    with many feelings lost and found.
    The individual search for the meaning of existence
    with many thoughts provocative and yet profound.

    Simple is the reason of my heart,
    Complexity proceeds with the evolution of thought,
    Subtle is the mystery of life,
    Contradiction unravels the riddle with irony,
    Vivid rattles the persistence of memory,
    Clarity redeems the sense of direction,
    Truth begins from the undaunted spirit,
    And Beauty, from all eyes, subjective and strange.

    Everything becomes meaningless
    upon the setting glimpse of light.
    And one moves on bravely
    with the next destination on sight..

    Written on 31 Oct 1999
    At the Orchard in Grantchester
