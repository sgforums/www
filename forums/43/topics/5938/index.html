    I'm fine after all these years...
    just a bit down...
    so,let's hit the bar tonite...
    drinks' on me...
    no problem...
    Teach me then,my friend...
    How do I tell her,
    Tell her that I still misses her...
    Misses her much more than I ever do...
    No,my friend...i'm not drunk...
    just a bit down...
    just a bit down,I say...
    Dun ever feel sad for a loner like me...
    It's just the way I live...
    Just a bit down...remember,my friend,
    I'm just a bit down...tat's all.

    Just a bit down>>>by Steiner S.Scott
