    Just a passing prodigal

    Love Story
    ----------
    Tell me a love story
    and offer me a happy one
    Tell me another love story
    and refrain from a sad one
    So I heard many tales of love,
    where the best moved me
    from one end of the Universe to the another
    where the worst that we learned
    threw us into the treacherous pits of Hell.

    How much time do we know of love?
    Is love a mere reaction for completion
    where one seeks the other by purely superficial whims
    or one finds the other thru the passing of time :
    a brief second to have a crush,
    Ten minutes to be infatuated
    and perhaps a day to fall in love
    yet a lifetime to really forget a person you love.

    Are we not actors merely
    playing our roles on the stage?
    Are we not jesters surely
    dancing with the twists of irony?
    Does love really end when lovers part?
    I beg not and I wish not,
    like a string that does not break
    when one could never see another again.
    yet sometimes plagued by a rhetorical question :
    Am I that fragment in your memory
    that vanished without a trace
    when you decided to leave for another?

    Such is nostalgia like a grammar lesson,
    where one finds the present tense and past perfect.
    Such is love like a double edged sword,
    which one could either be healed or hurt by Her.
    Such is passion as a desire,
    when Disappointment laughs mercilessly on our follies.
    Such are the uncertainties of Destiny
    in the weaving of a fragile fabric tinged with love and hate
    Such are my lamentations
    grown weary of the strange twists and turns
    by Fate's mysterious wheel.

    And yesterday, I was the Prince charming
    of your elaborated fairy tale
    where today, my time was up and another appeared,
    and tomorrow I would be totally forgotten
    stripped of my royalty which I once held
    The cycle continue, in a beginning with an end.

    Love is a dream, life is a dream,
    Half our lives wasted, just for one dream.
    Yet, torn between the edges of hope and despair,
    in the spirit of a sentimentalist,
    Hear me, and hear me again,
    It is journey in the love story
    that made it all worthwhile to seek
    whether the ending is beautiful or ugly.

    Written on
    25 May 2000
    Inspired by a song of Sammi Cheng entitled "Love Story"

    here's a nice poem.

    You throw away the items
    as if they are things that tune
    the world around you,
    tuning and changing yourself
    your stance and response
    towards everybody else

    Jim look at the book of lies..
    lies so glamourised by people
    who are paid to make them
    His heart held on to them in the past
    it made him hold on to the illusions
    and these Jim show them in his stance,
    it betrayed the illusions he held

    Jim threw away the book
    It is worthless, he thought
    He had allowed its colours
    to capture his heart

    He will give them up
    because the conditions want him
    to make a leap of faith,
    a conceptual leap into the life
    of engaging the present;
    ever engaging the reality that
    says nothing lasts forever
