Good Command of the Language

  • Small Boy
    1 Apr 01, 08:01
    In the name of creativity, please do not compromise on the standard of the language.

    These days, anyone who can pen, doodle, scrawl, type, talk seems to produce things for people to read. So long as they are for his private reading, standards as regards the use of language are not that important. However, if the writings are meant for the world, then, the writer must take pains to write grammatically and idiomatically.

    That is the unwritten covenant between the writer and the reader.
