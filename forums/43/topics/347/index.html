    ~~~ JUST ME ~~~
    (a poem especially wrote for my "friends" in Temasek Poly who likes to joke about me without a bf. Ha!)

    Accept me as I am-I have no guarantee.
    A claim to perfection I have not,
    Perfect I cannot be.
    I, like you.....am human,
    Prone to make mistakes.
    Failure is not a character flaw,
    Just a part of the God's plan.

    I live, I laugh and I also learn,
    My knowledge is incomplete.
    I am searching all the time,
    in waking hours as well as sleep.
    I have a long road to travel,
    as well as you do.
    We learn our lessons on the way,
    Wisdom we shall use.

    Accept me as I am
    be kind in your words and
    think of my feeling.
    Whether I am single or lonely,
    married or happy,
    just promised to be with me.
    Don't laugh at my singleness,
    I am just not as lucky,
    a man is hard to come by
    and I don't want to take the risk.

    Don't force me into losing my esteem,
    help me when I fall.
    If you are in love, I'm happy for you.
    Just don't laugh at my misery.
    Because I am ....me.
    And You are you.

    Take me as I am,
    just as my feeling for you is true.
    That is my only guarantee.
    that my friendship for you is timeless
    and to me your friendship is priceless.

    ** any comments? feel free to post it here **
