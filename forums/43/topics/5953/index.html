    softly within u sleep...
    taking a picture of u just to keep
    eyes closed with serenity,

    pale yet with a slight pink base

    ur tender skin, ur sweet smile
    ur long hair, cascading down
    the grace with which u walk
    the love with which u talk.
    memories i can never forget,
    as u sleep silently on....

    just to be drawn back with wretch
    afraid to touch u was i
    still as a mouse i quietly sat
    just watching over u i did,
    but who was i to kid?

    painfully aware of being alone
    there was nothing i could do to atone
    too late to tell u "i love u"
    and too late to say "i care"
    these u never knew,
    for it was always kept within...
