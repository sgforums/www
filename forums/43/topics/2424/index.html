    MY BUTTERFLY

    Beautiful girls are like butterflies
    Their thought are always mingle with lies
    Man are like judges
    Sober but full of gauges

    Choose wisely and not your next of kin
    Choose not for beauty nor the fairness of skin
    For beauty may fade and skin grow old
    But the heart that is loyal shall nerver turn cold

    Life is not to look for a good buy
    If life is a thing that money can buy
    The rich will live and the poor will die
    And there will never be a tie
    But God in his wisdom had make it so
    That the rich and the poor together must go

    Ashes to ashes, dust to dust
    There is no girl a poor man can trust
    So have no girl and have no sorrow
    Cause we are here today and gone tomorrow

    In this modern world the girls are wise
    To date a man she always think twice
    When we reach the age of maturity
    We say there is no LOVE without security

    Althought distance hold us far apart
    Friendship bind us dear at heart
    May these verse convey you cheer
    I wish you a successful career
