    The Background of the Story :

    The year is the beginning of 2000, after the end of the so-called millenium, when the devil has failed to find his wife, and the world did not end. The infinite cyberspace has just begun to continue to expand into the big universe by the use of world wide web (WWW).

    It is the year of fire, the year of sorrow, the year where there is a great war in the world of Sgforumsia. Yet it is a new age. From the entities to the humans and to the little creatures, Sgforumsia was a small continent in the midst of the World Wide Web. Chef is the current emperor of the empire and have the mandate of the gods of cyberspace. His imperial troops are powerful enough to quell any rebellion.

    Of course, near Sgforumsia, there is a small island city called Eropagins, where it hosts a 3 million population. However, the recent years, Sgforumsia were filtered by many factions of power, ranging from the self-proclaimed high brow intellectuals, to the cult leaders and to the other people who wander to give their peace of mind.

    The first conflict dated from the war unleashed by the cult leader Karhole who antagonized the orthodox religions and the unorthodox religions. The various cult factions whom he declared as morons raised against him, namely the 2 androids Paiser I and II, Jane Wang (the grandmother or father if you consider sex change of 2 cyber punks by the name of bad beng marks and Kao Lin gal), Duck, and etc.... The arena was about to be infested with blood and mayhem. However, the rebellion was quelled by Chef in a decisive action to use the weapon bestowed by the gods of cyberspace, the thread killer!!!! With the Thread Killer, the imperial troops sent all the cult factions to Liaj with the exception of Karhole to New Yoke, and Jane Wang to Woodbridge hospital.

    The war was witnessed by 5 of the characters of the story, namely prodigal (whose origin is unknown), Torrants, Bish the god of war, Ack and Dakar. At the end of the first war, Torrants was granted the manifestation of Time, where he sought to use his powers to rescue his lover who was cursed by an evil demoness from Hell... Bish was called to duty as there were unrest in the island city of Eropagins, where people are fighting over some dolls sold by some famous fast food restaurant called MacKaralds.. It was soon realized that it was the plot of a evil demoness called Hello Kathy, and she is hatching a plot to summon the God of War and the new God of Time, for reasons unknown. The prodigal was caught in the intrigue of the war. However, his role was yet to be determined. So, what is this big cosmic game which everyone is involved?

    Characters of the Story : the Heroes

    Prodigal
    --------
    The first protagonist of the story. A mysterious person in Sgforumsia where no one knows where he came from and who he was. Just know that he appears in a black trench coat, and wear a pair of sunshades, and exhibit powers that are beyond man. He is the most secretive of all. The only thing we know so far is that he has a soulmate called Ruona.

    Torrants
    --------
    The other protagonist of the story, who is a handsome dude, whom all the gals would cat-call or yearn for in life. He was a powerful harpist, who could charm many with his soothing music, better than the popstars... However his heart is destined for a woman called Mayar, who was cursed by a demoness from Hell because of jealousy over a sad story competition he started years back. He was undoubtedly a good thinker and writer, with a few offensive insults to morons most of the time. His lover had disappeared before the day of the wedding. Ever since then, he searched high and low for her... He was awarded the manifestation of Time, and now he was going to be involved in a new conflict against the demoness plaguing the island city of Eropagins with the God of War.

    Bish, the God of War
    --------------------
    Otherwise, the manifestation of war. Known to be a brute warrior in shining armour, but with his array of weapons ranging from simple halberds to machine guns. His duty is to preside over the wars, where his counterpart Peace would take over after the destruction caused by him. He was the god of conflicts, and a good friend of Torrants, and got him the job as the New Time... His powers are known to be destructive, just below the Continuum, the Creator (or God), Devil, and the Endless.... Nevertheless, he is the man with a good sense of humor and a taste for beers and wines...

    Dakar
    -----
    The observer of the story. It is rumoured that he is the god of parties. He presides over drinks and food such that he is always seen everywhere, watching others' happiness and misery with his good popcorn and good beer... He is nevertheless a important character in time to come. However, he needs to get more involved.

    Ack
    ---
    The highpriest of Karhole cult, did not get involved in the first war. His motivations are unknown, but it would be reveal in time to come.....

    Mayar
    -----
    The beloved bride to be for Torrants, but was cursed by the Evil demoness called Hello Kathy by an act of jealously, her whereabouts are unknown presently, and it is known that she is hiding somewhere so that her curse would cause famine and destruction to the people around her. Rumours that the Devil Lucifer S.A.Tan had interest in taking her as the bride....

    Runoa
    -----
    The soulmate of Prodigal, but have not appeared in the story... However, it is known that she is the manifestation of Love....
