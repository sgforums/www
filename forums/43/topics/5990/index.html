    Scenario:
    I need a banner printed, have got 5 correctly sized images (180dpi and correct dimension), all they need to do is put the images together in 1 file and get it printed. Clarified with the boss there, he said ok.. no problem.. no need for extra charges.

    Problem arises:
    Called up to inform me that the images were too big to fit on the banner. I asked if they could just shrink the entire image down proportionally till it fits, they said ok.

    Fax from Art Sign:
    They faxed the image of the banner over asking for signing off, and scribbled on the paper that there will be a $30 charge for artwork.

    The telephone call:
    Asked why the $30 charge... boss said the designer spent 2 hrs to shrink the images down to size.... CAN YOU BELIEVE THIS? It takes 2 whole hours to click on (for those familiar with Photoshop) Image>Image size and scale it down by a little.

    I then asked why wasn't I informed of the additional charge during the phone call, and he said, "Oh, you mean my designer didn't tell you?"

    "I think $30 just to resize is too unreasonable, I'll do it myself and get a courier to send it down for $8"

    "In that case, how are you going to compensate me for the 2 hrs of work?"

    Hello!!!!??! Can you believe this boss?? First he tries to con me into thinking it takes 2 hrs to resize an image, even when I told him that I'm proficient with Photoshop and knows it can't take 2 hrs.... (for the record... I did the changes on my own within 5 mins)..... he still insisted that it takes 2 hrs.. and began rattling off all the things that need to be done.. manz...

    That place is a con shop..... don't ever go there...
