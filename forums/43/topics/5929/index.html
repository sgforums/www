    Here is my 1st contribution and my very first sonnet :

    Sonnet 1
    --------
    Oh my lady, be happy for my sake,
    and beware of your emotions flow,
    yet let not Fate's problems bestow,
    Because there is so much at stake,
    so much deceit, entanglement and fake,
    Be contented for our lives are well-endowed,
    and see our changing world thru a wide window,
    and by sunshine, please be wide awake.
    As Disaster seeks to reduce you
    do not be fettered nor battered
    and break you outright,
    As Desire tries to seduces you,
    do not let your heart bewildered
    and forget what is morally right.

    Written on 6 June 2000
