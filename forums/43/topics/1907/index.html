    Hi Tired :

    This piece of poetry is written by me last year, entitled "What you leave behind."

    What You Leave Behind
    ---------------------
    What you leave behind,
    is nothing but a hollow shell,
    that I struggle with pain and difficulty
    and the days become nights.

    What you leave behind,
    is a knight who have lost his battle,
    without honour he can proclaim
    without a cause to fight for
    and without the reason to carry on.

    What you leave behind,
    is a spirit of a man broken
    distraught by betrayal and destruction
    that only the wine could drown his sorrows
    and nothing else in the world matters.

    What you leave behind,
    is a man who lost his convictions in life
    who can no longer face himself
    not even his family, friends and everything else.

    So what have you left behind?
    a soul that is awaiting to be defeated even more,
    a body that is impatiently seeking for death
    a heart that is shattered to pieces where nothing could mend it
    Nothing but a dying wish to seek death.
    What you leave behind is me,
    a person who loved you and now be alone again.

    Written on 18 July 99
    Dedicated to those who have been hurt by love
    and have to live with pain...

    The Field where I died
    ----------------------
    I stood by the field...
    only to feel the whistling wind....
    flow with the running waters....
    dance with the swaying grasses...
    experience the call of nature....

    Too much beauty passed me by......
    Too much uglyness swept me across...
    Too much hate that could not seduce me to evil...
    and too much love which I don't know whether it was true or not...

    I gasped at the last breath of my life,
    the beat of my heart almost come to a standstill,
    My senses began to wither and slow
    for the blood in my heart running low...

    I want to let her go,
    but all those love and hate which pervaded my mind,
    all those sorrows and joy which injure my heart,
    all those pain and tears which scarred my soul...

    If there was a beginning,
    there will be an end..
    "Our lives entangled in woven threads of destiny"

    If it was not meant to be now,
    perhaps it shall be in the next destiny to come.
    "Our love and hate trapped in this changing cycle"

    If it was only a debt I need to pay,
    it has definitely be over by now...
    "for you will always remain a special place in my heart"

    Perhaps, the field where I died...
    was only to tell me
    that this phase of my life's over
    and it's time to go into the next cycle.....

    Written on
    14 Feb 1997

    Hi all,

    This is another of my contribution to the forum. It is written in 1994, where it is the year where I wrote the most poetry.

    The Night
    =========
    The Night,
    A mystical period of the day
    with the fiery sun obscured,
    A periodic timing of half the twenty four segments
    with saturated darkness and minimal light points.

    Of Legend, it is said,
    that an Entity in Heaven,
    threw the cloak over half of Gaia,
    but the shroud was imperfect,
    which was why there are orbs of illumination,
    For the concealed is overwhelming
    and thus the birth of night.

    Of Science, it is postulated,
    By Oblers' paradox,
    that it cannot exist,
    as all points of starlight make the day,
    even if the sun sets in the ecliptic.
    But Edgar Allan Poe forged the golden walls with poetry,
    Lord Kelvin conjectured the calculations with mathematics,
    Hubble built the Expansion Law with physics,
    to challenge the difficult riddle of the night,
    Despite the efforts to solve the myth are made
    the Night's mystery is still in shades.

    Of Arts, it is fabricated,
    by many poets that personified the night,
    by many writers that it was Hades' incarnate,
    by many artists that characterized the art innate,
    by many musicians that composed soothing nocturnes,
    with all music, arts,literature and philosophy built,
    none can match a mortal's thought under the dark sky.

    Of Mortal's thoughts,
    Adore, cherish and love the night,
    Fearful, hateful and afraid of no light.

    As it is a time
    to cease all work in day,
    to consolidate all events in diary,
    to enjoy that silent and soft flow of thought,
    and to just idle and rest,

    As it is a moment
    of remembering someone or something dear,
    of starry spheres to sight in heaven sear,
    of not afraid the next day without fear,

    As it is a whim
    to feel a sense of care and comfort,
    to be inspired by the surroundings,
    to self reflect and cultivate,
    and to feel peace and harmony,

    The night, of beauty,
    to enjoy her essence, as it's said,
    till the next passing day awaits.

    Written on
    11 Aug 94
