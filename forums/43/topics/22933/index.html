    Another Jobless Singaporean

    On A Hot Day, On A MRT Bound For Nowhere
    I Met Up With A Middle Age Friend

    As we talk about life, My Friend say I've Made My Living
    From understanding other People's Actions,
    And Knowing What Their Game Was
    By The Way They Work In The Job
    Making Use of them to achieve what I need.

    So If You Don't Mind My Saying,
    I Can See You're Out Of Job Just Like Me.

    He Said Let Me Give You Some Free Advice,
    The MRT Turn Quiet As His Face Turn Sad & Haggard

    He Said My Friend If You're Going To Work In A Job,
    You Got To Learn To Work It Right

    You Got To Know
    When To Endure & Stay,
    Know When To Give Up & Walk Away,
    Most Importantly, You Must Know When To Run away.

    You Can Never Highlight Your Achievements
    When You're Working There.
    Because That Is Always Your Responsibility.

    There'll Be Time For People To Comment On
    Your Failures When You Are No Longer There.

    Now, Every " Successfully" Career Man Knows
    The Secret To a "Successful " Job Is
    Knowing When To Stay
    Knowing When To Leave

    Because Every Job's A Winner
    And Every Job's A Loser
    And The Best You Can Hope For
    Is To Collect Your CPF When You Reach 55.

    So When As He has Finished Speaking
    He Walk Out Of The Train As It Reach The Station.

    Hopefully, Somehow & Somewhere
    My Middle -Age Friend Will Find His Cozy Corner
    To Past His Time Away
    Until He have enough $$ for Him to Stop Working.

    By Thomas Su
    23rd June 2002
