A Poem : Of the 3 types of Women I meet

  • prodigal
    22 Feb 00, 07:27
    Of three kinds of women
    I meet and know till now,
    Of none become my companion,
    which I seemed to be helpless somehow,
    Perhaps I lack charisma and chemistry
    or just equals and opposites simply don't attract.

    The first and foremost prototype,
    are the ladies who fail to understand
    and refuse to accept you,
    of arrogance and haughty thoughts,
    as they are materialistic
    and vain in nature
    or simply think
    they are just too high class for you
    But there is always the fact,
    they are always attractive, affluent and appealing,
    though these seemed to be their good points
    but they still lacked the vision
    to see the good nature in average men
    and always presume that their boyfriends
    must be handsome dukes.

    The second and next to come,
    are the maidens who can support you
    and contemplate your stand,
    however there are only the refusal and rejection
    to accept your love,
    such ladies are simple, virtuous and practical,
    but seemed emotionless at times or
    they just always have somebody in mind.
    They are the most ideal people
    that may become a good companion or lover for you
    only if she accepts you,
    But if she turns around and avoid you,
    she may become your greatest enemy.

    The third and last to arrive
    are the females who
    believe that they can see your point
    which they usually don't
    and are able to accept you,
    Such ladies are compassionate
    and amiable at times,
    but they lacked the time
    to comprehend your thoughts,
    If both of you are to form a couple,
    it may be the greatest fallacy of all,
    as she may someday claim that
    she fail to know you
    and that's the end of the relationship with you.

    To feel discouraged and dejected,
    of all these three types of women I met,
    Not driven to desperation there I sought,
    of the fourth type of woman
    to come to my life as soon as possible,
    for she will perceive my thoughts
    and support my stand in life,
    as she bears the power of confidence and love
    I am searching and longing for in life

    *This poem is dedicated to my best friend* For those who know who I am after reading this poem, I do appreciate that you would keep it secret...Thank you and have a good day...
