    I must say that the Protestant denominations do not have different teachings. All doctrines and teachings from churches of various denominations are uniform and should be based on the Bible which we held as authoritative and has the final say in all matters of the Christian faith.

    Denominational differences are mainly found in the way the churches are governed and the form of worship. Some denominations uses liturgical manner of worship while others freedom of expression. Other than that, the teachings are the same.

    In such cases, it is possible for Christians from different churches to interact and this can be seen in Para-church organisations such as Campus Crusade for Christ which consists of believers from different denominations.
