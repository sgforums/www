By the very fact of having a particular religion, or idealogy, you are standing by a certain truth claim on the nature of reality, that by default, and the law of non-contradiction, states that any other truth claim in direct contradiction is false and wrong.

    All religions, even Buddhism cannot get by without making truth claims on the nature of reality. Buddhism is hardly all-accepting, it pushes a particular perspective of reality that by default contradicts the truth claims put forward by other idealogies and religions and by logical extension... are already claiming that they are false.
