    4 May 06, 01:16
    a good hotel to stay at is the pathumwan princess, it is linked to MBK and is very near siam square...

    the hotel has a full brekky spread so you can have "brunch" and then head out... make sure you go over a week end and go to chatuchak you can get there by taking the trains or maybe by tuk tuk... my advice is not to use a taxi otherwise they will take you to all the must stop tourist place to look at "tourist" products...

    also you have to go to siam square to eat the steamboat and to eat the kobe beef.

    don't forget also that the pathumwan princess has a really good japanese restaurant and a korean restaurant too... if you are fortunate enough, they will have their buffet promotion where you can have all you can eat jap and korean food for about $30 sing... the jap food includes tempura, teppanyaki, sushi etc... if you want a nice cosy dinner in the room, their room service food is very good. their buffet spread for dinner and supper is open till 1 am i think.

    the food court at the top of the MBK centre also has very good food.

    chinatown is a must to eat the birds nest, and for the really good food too... also very very cheap...

    late night shopping at pat pong.. but be good ya, no tiger shows...

    hope you have your accommodation booked and your transfers arranged, otherwise, best to take either a taxi or a merc limo from the airport as they can arrange for two way transfers as well. if you want to get into the city quick, then pay the extra 70 baht of thereabouts and take the highway.

    At the airport, you can also arrange for free tours of thailand as part of their tourist promo, but if you are not into buildings and culture, not so fun.

    before I forget, visit the mandrin hotel...

    other places to shop are the world trade centre and the other place for computer stuff... but then walking around MBK can occupy you for a couple of days..

    and get your fill of foot massages and massages if you want, the pathumwan princess are arrange them in your room for you as they have a full service in house. They also have a very good gym and a nice swimming pool.
