    The Sacraments were instituted by Christ to supply grace to the faithful through visible means. Jesus promised he would not leave us orphans (John 14:18 ) but would send the Holy Spirit to guide and protect us (John 15:26). He gave the sacraments to heal, feed, and strengthen us. They are visible signs in which God imparts grace and love to us. In other words, God uses matter to impart grace.

    There are seven Sacraments, sorted into 3 categories.

    The Sacraments of Christian Initiation:
    Baptism
    Confirmation
    Eucharist

    The Sacraments of Healing
    Penance and Reconcilliation (Confession)
    Anointing of the sick (Extreme Unction)

    The Sacraments at the service of Communion
    Holy Orders
    Matrimony (Marriage)
