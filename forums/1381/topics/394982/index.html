    My Prayers Were Answered

     

    After reading around 20 threads, I thought I would start one and I hope it would be interesting.

    I’m born catholic and am as skeptical about religion as some of you are. As humans, we only believe what we see or experience. All we seek for is evidence and this evidence does not come to us easily so we choose not to believe.

    Here’s something I would like to share. I’ve been force to pray when I was young. My mom would scold me if I did not pray. Despite being forced, I often pray sincerely when I’m doing so and not knowing what to pray for, I prayed for height and the prettiest girl in class to love me.

    In sec1 I’ve an average class height and there were like 10 people taller than me in class. Miraculously, my height shot up to 188cm when I was in sec 4. Even though I’m not the tallest person in the world, I’m sure that prayer was answered. Oh… and the girl that I had a crush on didn’t really love me, even if she did I would never know as I didn’t confess to her. But one rare incident did happened then involved us. Being a short and unsociable guy in my early years, I would be bullied occasionally, thank god there were nerds that took the rap most of the time. There was once this bully tried to do something and the girl actually stood up for me and shouted at the bully.

    “STOP BULLYING *MY NAME*....(i forgot what she said after that)” she said.

    The bully replied “WHY? U LIKE *MY NAME* AR?” the bully rebutted.

    The girl ignored and went back doing her thing.

    That was the first time the girl actually stood up for someone being bullied and I was really touched. Good or bad, I was never bullied again and when my height shot up I was the one bullying others -.-

     

    This thread is created for those people who pray to share their answered prayers.
