    braised seacucumber...

    seacucumber is usually sold dehydrated... you need to soak them in water for a few hours... taking time to take them out to feel else they become too waterlogged and lose all texture..

    need dried chinese mushrooms... again, soak in water.. do not throw out the water they were soaked in.. adds to the taste...

    other types of vegetables, may add as you like.. i like the stuff (i dunno what it is in english.."dang orh"

    sauce (if i remember rightly)

    soy sauce
    sugar
    about 2 huge ladles of stock
    chinese mushrooms
    bamboo shoots (diced)
    rice wine
    cornstarch
    water
    sesame oil
    green onions
    ginger

    stirfry the fragrant stuff like sliced ginger and onions..

    add stuff like the bamboo shoots, whatever vegetables, mushrooms and qq

    allow liquid to reduce by simmering it for a while...

    add cornstarch, sesame oil and cover for a while..
