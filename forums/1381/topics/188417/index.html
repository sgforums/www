    ....and not by any works or keeping any commandments.

    Justification is once and binding. Our salvation is guaranteed thru Christ's perfect work on the cross.

    Gospel Justification:

    1) What is justification?

    Justification is an act of God's free grace, where he pardons all our sins.

    2) Free Grace - based on Christ's merits, not men's

    3) Our pardon - our sins

    4) Our acceptance - of what our lord did for us on the cross

    5) By faith apart from the law (Romans 3:21, 22)

    God has given us his grace freely. Let's not take his grace in vain by wanting to please him by observing the law.

    Let us not rely on works but by his grace - and not rob god of his glory (salvation in saving us thru grace)

    c) gossip/confess the sin of others

    Prayer

    Fellowship
