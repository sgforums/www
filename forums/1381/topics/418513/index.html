    Do you, fellow christian pray often to God? Are you disappointed when sometimes God does not seem to answer your prayers? I will encourage you to continue praying, even when God does not seem to answer prayers the way you want to?

    Let me share a personal experience of mine with you.

    I once had a toothache. I was afraid to go to the dentist, so I prayed for God to heal me miraculously. Well, the toothache worsen and I did go to the dentist. That day, while I was surfing online, I clicked onto a picture and it was a picture of Jesus guiding the dentist's hand to operate a person's tooth.

    God spoke to me. God had answered my prayer. He had healed me. He had healed me through a dentist. And that was His answer to my prayer.

    Sometimes, in christian life, what we wanted may differ from what God had intended for us. Netherless, do not give up on prayers, because sometimes, just through prayers, you may be changed by God.

    In my case, I realise that science had a role to play in God's plan and God can heal us through dentists, doctors and scientific inventions.
