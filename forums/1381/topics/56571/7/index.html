    Popiah recipe

    Shred:
    1 bangkwang (90c - $1)
    half carrot (for color)
    optional: leek

    Bangkwang cooking method:
    heat oil in wok
    fry minced garlic
    fry minced dried prawn (soak first. 1 table spoon prawn for 1 bangkwang)
    put in bangkwang, fry
    add light sauce 1 table spoon, fry
    add alittle water, boil (about 1 cup, note: bangkwang produces water)
    add 1 teaspoon brown sugar
    when bangkwang almost cooked, add carrot (last item, else too soft)

    Total cooking time abt 15-20 min
    Taste better overnight / morning cook, evening eat/ reheated
    Makes 1 big plate, can make about 14 popiahs.

    Other additions for popiah:
    popiah skin
    hard boil egg
    chopped taukua
    ground peanuts
    sweet sauce
    prawns
    minced garlic in oil
    beansprouts
    lettuce
    chilli paste
