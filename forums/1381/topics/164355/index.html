    23 Nov 05, 12:44
    i believe in Conservative Evangelical theology:

    a) Salvation by grace through faith alone in Christ [sola fide]

    b) Trinity- three persons in the Godhead but one God

    c) The Bible as innerrant from Genesis to Revelation and therefore authoritative and the only source for faith and practice [sola scriptura]

    As to Calvinism and Arminianism:

    I am a four-point Calvinist, i do not accept all the five tenets of Calvinism.

    I accept only: a) Total Depravity b) Unconditional Election c) Irrisistible Grace and d) Preservation and perseverance of the Saints.

    I reject Calvinism's doctrine of Limited Atonement; that Christ died only for the elect and not for all men.

    I believe in Universal Atonement. The Atonement is universal and sufficient for all but efficacious only for those who believe. The atonement is universal but limited only because of man's unbelief.

    As to my system of interpretation:

    I am a Normative Dispensationalist like Scofield, Chafer, Swindoll, Ryrie, Pentecost, Barnhouse, Gray, Henry Allan Ironside, Woodrow Kroll and etc.

    Essenstials of Dispensationalism:

    a) Normal and plain interpretation of the Bible from GEnesis to Refelation, recognizing figures of speech but looking for the plain meaning behind the figure.

    b) We Believe that the Church is not Israel [unlike covenant theology]. The Church is a distinct entity from Israel. A new creation.

    c) We believe that God's goal of History is to glorify Himself.

    d) We believe in one method of salvation in all of time [unlike Ultra Dispensationalist].
