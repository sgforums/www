    Allow me to use this metaphor which i best describe what language God understands best.

    God doesn't understand spoken languages as much as He understands the language of the heart.
    Because that's what he does best.

    I cannot help but to bring this to a real setting of a father and his toddler.
    This toddler is still does not know the figure of speech and only goes 'geeee gee, gaah gaah.'
    Every action, every smile attracts the father attention. Though father doesn't know how the other fathers handle their child. But he sure know what his kid needs even without little toddler saying anything. There is a certain connection which only he knows.
    When toddler crys, he's afraid, or he falls, he's hungry, he's lost.
    This father always knows.

    Though this earthly father doesn't know what's the best for little toddler.
    He doesn't guarantee to know exactly what his baby is thinking.
    But he knows enough to provide for the toddler.
