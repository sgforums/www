All the best wishes and blessings to everyone here and may you find better purposes and meaning in life. Real life. Not virtual reality nor even hyper-reality. Truth is absolute, not defined by the person who wants it to be nor by social norms.

    For those of you who are believers in Jesus Christ. Don settle for second best. God has a better calling, a higher ground for you to walk on. Based and ground your entire life firmly upon God and His infallible word. That is the only source of certain hope in this uncertain world.

    The Lord bless and keep you always.
