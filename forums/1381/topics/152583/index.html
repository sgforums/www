    "Josh McDowell was born in Union City (Battle Creek), Michigan in 1939 with the given name Joslin. He was one of five children born to Wilmot McDowell (senior). His biographer, Joe Musser, indicates that McDowell struggled with self-esteem issues in his youth, as his father was an alcoholic and abusive. As an atheist at college he decided to prepare a paper that would examine the historical evidence of the Christian faith in order to disprove it.
