    Matthew 5:20 NIV

    1) Who are those people who can exceed the righteousness of the Pharisees?

    Those who have Christ indwelling in them. Christ indwelling through the Holy Spirit makes us capable of being filled with the fruits of righteousness to the glory of God.

    Php 1:11 being filled with the fruits of righteousness which are by Jesus Christ, to the glory and praise of God.

    It is only BY Jesus Christ that true righteousness is possible. All other righteousness will not do with God. God does not accept filthy rags righteousness.

    How can we have Christ in us?

    By trusting alone in Christ alone.

    When we have trusted in Christ, we are sealed by the Holy Spirit, the Holy Spirit indwells us.

    When we have the Holy Spirit in us, what are some of the fruits that will be manifested through us as we walk in the Spirit?

    Ga 5:22 But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, longsuffering, kindness, goodness, faithfulness,

    We are only capable of giving true love when we have the Holy Spirit in us and we walking in HIm. AGain we recieved the Holy Spirit through faith alone in Christ alone.

    Example: Was Paul a Pharisee? Yes

    Php 3:5 circumcised the eighth day, of the stock of Israel, of the tribe of Benjamin, a Hebrew of the Hebrews; concerning the law, a Pharisee;

    How did Paul surpass the righteousness of the Pharisee? by faith alone in Christ alone

    Php 3:9 and be found in Him, not having my own righteousness, which is from the law, but that which is through faith in Christ, the righteousness which is from God by faith;

    2) the Bible does not command us to have faith?

    Mr 11:22 So Jesus answered and said to them, "Have faith in God.

    Joh 14:1 "Let not your heart be troubled; you believe in God, believe also in Me.

    Paul told the Philippian jailer: "Believe on the Lord Jesus Christ and you will be saved.........."

    Of course Paul was exhorting him to believe in Christ truly, to have faith in Christ truly. When that happens, our hearts are purified through faith in Jesus Christ.

    Ac 15:9 "and made no distinction between us and them, purifying their hearts by faith.

    When can a heart be purified and be capable of loving? When it has been cleansed by faith in Christ. Paul knew that trusting in Christ will transformed the sinner from the inside out, giving him a new heart capable of true love, therefore Paul [or God through Paul] commanded the jailer to believe [trust] in the Lord Jesus Christ.

    3) faith is the result of loving God?

    Love is the result of a renewed and purified heart by faith alone in Christ alone

    We love Him because we have appropriated the truth by faith that He first loved us, that He gave Himself for us.

    4) The whole idea that some christians subscribe to - salvation by faith not works/deeds - is a lie or at best a half truth.

    Please notice the word "by" in verse 8 and the word "for" in verse 10 below. We are saved BY grace through faith......not of works but FOR good works.

    Paul said that:

    Eph 2:8 For by grace you have been saved through faith, and that not of yourselves; it is the gift of God,

    Eph 2:9 not of works, lest anyone should boast.

    Eph 2:10 For we are His workmanship, created in Christ Jesus for good works, which God prepared beforehand that we should walk in them.
