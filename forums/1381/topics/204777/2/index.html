    I just want to add one more point....

    ... the attitude of servanthood. I have seen to many well-intentioned individuals wanting to serve God only to be let down by the attitude.

    What attitude? the attitude of not receiving anything in return. A servant is someone who exist in the background, many-a-times, the things that are done are humble tasks that no one will even thank you for. Sometimes, you can blamed for things that are perceived to be wrongly done.

    Another salient point is the ability to let go. Lots of us has the talent and energy to go the whole nine yards, yet at the end, when it's time to pass over the baton, we'd end out having withdrawal symptoms and may end out overscutinising the new leadership and compare against what you may have done.

    The most important of all is your heart of servanthood. The objective is not to glorify oneself but to glorify the one whom we call Lord.
