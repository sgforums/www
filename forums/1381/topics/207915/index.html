    The following are my personal opinions on additional points:

    - Remember and practice the greatest commandments of Love (Matt 22:37-40, 1 Cor 13:13). For we all ought to know that God is Love, and the devil is God's 180-degree opposition, which means being evil is having absence of Love. Evil shall not completely overcome us if we have even a slight trace of Love within our heart and spirit.

    - If you know how to pray in tongues, use it when facing difficulties (Rom 8:26-27). In this way, your spirit will intercede for yourself in praying to the Holy Spirit Himself, Who will strengthen your spirit. But, if you do not know how to pray in tongues, do NOT even try, for the devil can give devilish tongues as well.

    - Christ died for mankind once and for all, by replacing the blood of a sacrificial lamb with His own precious Blood. Have faith and always call on the Blood of Jesus to cover us when facing difficulties or fear.

    - Remember the promises of God for eternal life (Jn 10:10) in any circumstances. Do not treat challenges in life as a means of forsaking God, for hardships will eventually leads us to hope on God (Rom 5:3-6). Once we forsake God, the devil will work overtime to claim our souls.
