    gong1 has a few meanings. In this case, it means public.

    According to this wiki entry, gong1 is taken from tian1 xia4 wei2 gong1 for the equivalent meaning of being universal.

    gong1 jiao4 hui4 was originally used on Roman Catholic churches. Later, tian1 zhu3 jiao4 was for Roman Catholic churches instead. (I think both terms are interchangeable currently).

    The word gong1 remains as a word to describe universal in the creed's context.
