  • SingaporeMacross

    An ordained priest is addressed as 'Father'.

    A deacon (Priest's assistant, but doesn't have his powers), seminarian (someone who study to become priest), religious brother, are called 'Brother'.

    A nun or religious sister, is called 'Sister'.

    The Mother Superior of a convent is sometimes called 'Mother'. I'm not too sure about that.

    Of course, brother and sister are sometimes used to address friends or people from church. Usually in a formal occasion.
