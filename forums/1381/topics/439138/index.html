    Let us define faith as belief in a god or religion in the absence of proof. I don't show myself, you just believe I am real.

    Although man has been free to conjure up dozens of religions, faith is coincidentally an ingredient in most or all belief systems.

    Who else finds it unconvincing that despite the diversity of these religions, the eons and the civilizations that evolved them, not a single one of these god has lifted a finger to show that he exists?

    This despite stiff competition between faiths, anguish and violence caused by faith and various benefits touted, such as avoiding divine punishment or an unpleasant afterlife.
