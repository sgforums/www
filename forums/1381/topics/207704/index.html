    I consider myself an in-between because I believe in taking the goods from each and discarding the bad.

    The problem with most charismatic christians nowadays is that they based their faith in God too much of the "seeing" factor. It is always about signs, wonders, healings, miracles, people jumping or people getting slained or delivered. They feel that if God is present, ALL THESE must be present. So my question to them is always, if all these are not present at a meeting, God is not present? I explain to them that healing is not always in the physical but in many ways can be emotional or spiritual. So, if you do SEE it, does not mean God is not present. I find charismatics a post-modernistic bunch. They must see to believe. While it is not wrong, it is not always right. I belong to a rather pro-charismatic church right now where they start to practice prophetic worship, healings, word faith etc.... but i am very clear about it to my leaders, I practice what I am convicted about, not just what the church officially stands for.

    The problem with most traditionals then is that they sometimes cannot accept that there is a need to be relevant, for changes and for things to be taken in current context. Before I came to my current church, I have actually in a Brethen church which was super conservative. They speak out against the charismatic movement and would go as far as saying they are not christians. Too me, that is a generalisation. Again, the generalisation is not wrong but it it is not always right. I believe I am called to use my passion and talent for rock music to reach out to an unique group of people, people on the edge. People who may not walk into a church playing hymns or hillsongs. These are punks, skins or just rebellious teens u see around you. We believe that our on stage ministry which is the music can DRAW them to us and our off stage ministry which is our friendship can DRAW them to GOD. However, again my traditional church frens or pastors will waved my passion off as trends, as not in "the scheme of God". They feel that God does not appreciate rock music and will never use it to reach people. I disagree of cos but well again I work according to my convictions. I never try too hard to convince people of what I do.
