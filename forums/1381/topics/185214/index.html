    it is good that you ask this question. i presume the backslidden Christian who ask this question is concerned about his or her present condition.

    First, Satan will make every effort to discourage the backsliding one to return.

    However God promises the backsliding one this:

    " Jer 3:22 "Return, you backsliding children, And I will heal your backslidings."

    The backsliden person has a part to do and that is to return. And when you do that God will do His part.Confess your sins, Return and do the first works.

    Second, find a mature Christian whom you can open up your situation. One who will pray for you and be your keeper. One who will help you up again on your feet. REach out and find help from others.

    Ga 6:1 'Brethren, if a man is overtaken in any trespass, you who are spiritual restore such a one in a spirit of gentleness, considering yourself lest you also be tempted.'

    Third, stay away from the causes of your backsliding.

    a) bad company
    b) lack of fellowship. no part of a body will stay healty if he remains seperated from the body.
    c) bad relations. fixed it.
    d) poor foundation. the person who is not getting enough sound teaching from the Word is weak and therefore susceptible to backsliding.
    e) prayer. never underestimate the value of prayer in our daily life.
    f) compromise with the world. maintain a healthy seperation.
    g) playing with temptation. WATCH and pray that you will not enter into temptation.

    P.S. it is my responsiblity as a Christian to pray for my brother or sister who is backsliding. I will certainly pray for that person my friend whoever she is.

    PM me if you need to talk confidentially to someone.

    Originally posted by latterrain:
    Seriously. what is the real definition of a backslider?
    A backslider is a genuine believer in Christ who has let his sinful nature get the upperhand of him. One need not do out and out sins to be a backslider. A 'faithful' church attender, gospel tract distributer and etc. could do Christian things and yet be a backslider at heart.

    an example of a backslider though still doing religious things are the Galatians. See the one posted by vince.

    It is different from an apostatizer. this is a person who is not really a believer from the start but just looks like it.

    1Jo 2:19 They went out from us, but they were not of us; for if they had been of us, they would have continued with us; but they went out that they might be made manifest, that none of them were of us.
