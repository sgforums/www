    No. All those who died before Jesus and still believed in the one True God went to Paradise, a sort of holding area. It took the Blood of God's own Son to open the gates and cleanse all sinful people, so that all who believe would go to heaven and be with God. Luke 16 describes this in detail.

    In a way, the Blood of Christ cleansed the Old Testament saints as well; they looked forward to the prophecies of the cross, while we today look back to what happened on the cross.
