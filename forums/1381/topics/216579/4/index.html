    EH will continue to be a place for discussing Christian topics in sgForums. It will be a gathering for Christians so that the Christian views can be heard. It will be a place for non-believers to ask questions they want an answer to.

    I thank HB for this thread. In the past, I have approached a few members to take up the moderating role, without success however. The invitation to them is still open.

    EH will continue to be moderated by born-again Christians only. And the election of moderators will only be decided by the moderators. Having said that, the role of a moderator here is minimal. As SIS have pointed out, our role here is to remove/lock threads/posts that are degrading/offensive. However, since this is a forum for Christianity-based topics, and judgment-calls to moderate threads need to be made from a Christian point-of-view (mostly), moderators must be Christians only.

    We may try to steer discussions in a general direction. But I think that can be done by all members or the threadstarter. We can take up the role of councillor, but I think that can be done by all compassionate Christians.

    Since I cannot be checking this forum consistently, and my co-mod is absent, I have asked a few people to assist me to watch over the forum. They will contact me when there is action that needs to be done by me. I will continue to look our for new moderators.

    I will continue to come in regularly to check for PMs. Please write to me if there are any concerns. However, I will not be able to participate actively in discussions. I will try my best.
