    We should not think of this thread as imposing the practise of one church to the others. But rather, a good opportunity for Christians from all denominations to learn from one another other each denomination reinforce what they learn from the bible.

    As for observing Sabbath on Saturday, the Seventh Day Adventists do practise that and have their services on Saturdays only. If you look at the church history, the early Christians did that too.

    So it's neither right or wrong for Christians to go to church on Sat or Sun.
