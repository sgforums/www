His parents had brought him to see a number of priests & mediums. Some of them said that the 3rd eye was given to him for a divine reason. Maybe to help those who passed on but still have some uncompleted wishes in the mortal world.

When all resources seem drained & he felt hopeless, his girlfriend (his present wife) who was a devout Christian, persuaded him to attend church with her & get a Pastor to pray over this matter. He was skeptical but since he had nothing to lose,he went.

Of course, he was overjoyed. Since then he had been also a devout Christian although he had changed a few churches & now we attend the same church. Years later, he was blessed with promotions (because he was blessed with the patience to deal with our demanding boss), as well as the financial wisdom to save up for a wedding which he could'nt wait to testify the Lord's goodness to us.

His uncle also has the 3rd eye & he is a Chinese medium or "Tang Ki", where he uses his 3rd eye to help people who seek him.

At the end, it was not known whether this person's uncle received Christ but they did enjoy the service.
