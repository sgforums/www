    This is something I constantly asked myself in my christian walk.

    "To do or not to do, why? is the question."

    If salvation/justification can only be through Christ, and can only be received by the grace of God.

    to do/not to do good works, to obey/follow or not to obey/follow the commandments, to observe/celebrate or not to observe/celebrate a feast day.

    Why? is the question. What is the reason/motivition for us to do them, to observe/celebrate them, to obey/follow them?
    If the answer to the Why? question is that I (lets each person make a personal decision on this) want to do these because of the great love I have received from God; Because I love God who had first loved me;...etc then by all means do them.
    If the answer to this Why?question is I am trying to work out my salvation; because I fear that if I don't do them, God will condemn me; ...etc then my take is there is no point doing them, reason being, when God sees us, He only sees us in Christ, His perfection, His righteousness (not our works, not out faults).

    conclusion: Good works have to be done out of a willing and sincere heart, with an attitude of thanksgiving, resting in the beauty and perfection of Christ.

    God bless.
