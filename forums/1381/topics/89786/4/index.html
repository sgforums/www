    Shanghai feels like Singapore. Modern bustling city. No feeling of displacement whatsoever.

    If your friend is working during the day you could just get recommendations of places to visit, directions on how to get there, and travel via public transport. It's safe and easy.

    Within SH itself there's the few highlights like the Bund, their shopping district nan jing lu (hello DVDs!), and some museums.

    You could travel to the outskirts and visit one of those "water villages" like zhou jia zhuang. They call themselves the Venice of the East.

    Did you do some homework like checking out where to visit on the web?
    How about a guide book?
