    Dear All

    What is Project Type R.

    Project Type-R (www.project-typer.com) is a global development program that aims to create a series of animated film depicting the content and message of the bible of Christ.

    The series will feature modern animated characters in an urban environment, grouping together in various sections of this urban environment and encountering situations where they are taken back into selected moments during history as recorded in the bible.

    The overall direction and concept centers on these characters and their interactions within their own urban setting that is similar or identical to contemporary real-world scenarios.

    Individual characters are constructed with a diverse racial make-up, and corresponding street and urban areas will also reflect various racial and cultural tones, such as architecture (Chinatown, Hispanic-American strips, African urban plains etc), language accents, and music.

    The integrity and preservation of the original message of the bible is the highest priority and importance in this project, in order to ensure an exact version of the bible to be relevant to youths around the world today.

    I need some volunteers to identify and develop sponsors for Project Type-R, co-ordinate promotions and funding campaigns to raise targeted funds for the operational requirements of Project Type-R.

    Develop and formulate financial budgets within inter-divisions for planning & control.

    I also need volunteers to help in script writing and animation portion.

    It will not be a forever volunteer for free thingy but when funds are available, we will reimburse you back.

    PLEASE HELP!!!
