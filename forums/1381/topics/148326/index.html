    And peace to His people on earth.
    Lord God, Heavenly King
    Almighty God and Father

    Lord Jesus Christ, only Son of the Highest.
    Lord God, Lamb of God. You take away the sins of the world.
    Have mercy on us.

    You are seated at the right hand of the Father
    Receive our prayer.

    For you alone are the Holy One. You alone are the Lord. For You alone are the Most High, Jesus Christ.

    With the Holy Spirit in the Glory of God, the Highest.
