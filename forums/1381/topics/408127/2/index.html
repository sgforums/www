Teachings of Jesus christ from his time with the apostles.. handed till today. from 10 commandments, sacred tradition (matters regarding faith not recorded in the bible handed from apostles' time or early church  till today). The magesterium is the official teachings of the catholic church.. Honor the saints. models of christian living.. eg.. st teresa of avila.. st pio.. just to name a few..

    CC: based on bible .. or sola scripture.. almost literally..

    Sacraments: they are of visible sign of God's grace on the person.. eg baptism..

    RC: has 7 sacraments: baptism, confirmation, Holy Eucharist, Holy orders (for becoming priest), Holy matrimony (marriage), confession of sins or reconciliation of sins, and anointing of the sick or extreme unction in old days.

    CC: mostly 2 .. namely baptism and matrimony..

    Holy communion: RC: real body and bread of Jesus christ when it is consecrated by the priest during mass i.e service.

    CC: holy communion is symbolic..
