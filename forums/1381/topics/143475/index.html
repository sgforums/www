    It is not wat u do, but wat GOD has freely given to u that assured ur salvation.

    No other faith in the world have this grace freely given to u unconditionally, except thru Christ.

    It is sad that Christians, even those long in the faith and who once walked closely with GOD, still see themselves living under legalism/morality instead of GOD's grace. If one do not believe that his salvation is assured by faith thru Christ alone, then what Christ has done on the cross has been taken in vain.

    A Christian who is not assured of his own salvation is incapable of preaching effectively to non-believers. How can he preach that others are saved when he himself is not?

    The Church is not perfect, but if one is going to let the failures of men affect his decision concerning his own salvation, I can say unfortunately he has been misled. As Christians, our role is to preach and to plant the seed of faith. The rest is up to GOD and the person. We cannot be blamed for that particular individual losing his salvation, after all it is a relationship between GOD and that individual; Nobody can decide for him/her whether they want to receive Christ in their lives.
