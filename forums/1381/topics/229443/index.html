    Personally, I feel that it ok to attend 2 church service on the same day, and even have communion twice (after its to bring to remembrance what the Lord had done for us).

    First of all... welcome to Eternal Hope.

    There is no reason why we cannot attend multiple worship services.

    Your questions:
    1. The method of worship is absolutely different (so is it ok?) and
    2. Is it ok to have 2 holy communions on the same day?(To drink the red wine and eat the bread)

    My opinion:
    1. The method of worship thing should be something you are comfortable with. Some people have certain inclination while others do not. So it's all up to you.
    2. Multiple holy communion? My personal take is: while there is really no reason why you cannot take, I don't see the need to take more than one.

    Having said all that, I think you need to make a choice as to what you want to do. Going along with a friend for the sake of friendship is really not a good reason why one goes to church. Worshipping in church goes beyond sitting in the sanctuary and going through the motions of the rites and rituals. A worshipper should go towards committing oneself to the attitude of servanthood within the chosen church.

    Hope this helps....

    I think since you were baptised in the lutheran church, you have become a member of the church right?

    Personally, i think it is ok to visit another church for another service, but as you become a member of the church, it is recommended that you commit more of your time to fellowship and encourage other believers or speaking to visitors.
