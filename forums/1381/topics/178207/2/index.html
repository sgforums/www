    Hello there photo seeker.........there is a big difference between our Lord's coming FOR HIS saints and our Lord's coming WITH HIS saints.

    The former speaks about the Rapture while the latter speaks about the second coming proper.

    Second Coming verse:

    1Th 3:13 so that He may establish your hearts blameless in holiness before our God and Father at the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ with all His saints.

    Some believe 'saints' here refer to angels. We pre-trib deny deny this based on exegisis of the Scriptures. Yes, angels will come together with all His previously raptured saints. but this verse here speaks about saved saints. Paul makes it clear if he talks about angels coming together with the Lord:

    "2Th 1:7 and to give you who are troubled rest with us when the Lord Jesus is revealed from heaven with His mighty angels."

    rapture verse:

    "1 Th 4:17 Then we who are alive and remain shall be caught up together with them in the clouds to meet the Lord in the air. And thus we shall always be with the Lord."

    Some godly Christians deny the Rapture saying its not in the Bible. Actually when they say this they are not denying the Rapture per se. What they are saying is that there is no PRE-TRIBULATIONAL RAPTURE.

    Rapture means being caught up. To deny the Rapture is to say that Paul is wrong when he said:

    "1 Th 4:17 Then we who are alive and remain shall be caught up together with them in the clouds to meet the Lord in the air. And thus we shall always be with the Lord."

    the issue is not whether the Bible teaches Rapture doctrine or not, No, for the Bible sets forth clearly this doctrine. The issue is WHEN will 1 Thessalonians 4:17 take place.

    I personally believe our Lord will come to take His Church to Himself BEFORE the Tribulation period. At the second coming, He will judge all the nations.

    who will be included in the Rapture? Those who have repented of their sins and trusted in Christ's saving work alone for salvation.
