    and soup? sure-fire way of cooking great soup...

    necessary: slow cooker...

    stuff: radishes, dried cuttlefish, lots of pork ribs...

    boil pork ribs in water.. once it starts to boil, remove and pour away water.. fill pot with cold water (basically to "wash" the pork ribs)

    transfer pork ribs into slow cooker

    wash 1 whole dried cuttlefish and cut into 2 or 3 pieces.. throw into slow cooker..

    wash and cut up radishes.. throw into slow cooker..

    cook for about 12 to 24 hours...
