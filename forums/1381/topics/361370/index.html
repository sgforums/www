    It would be best if you write the whole passage then ask for discussion on that specific verse. Anyway I read from verse 1 to 6 and here's what I think.

    I dissect this to two points:

    Jesus: "I am THE WAY and THE TRUTH and THE LIFE"

    Jesus: "NO ONE comes to the Father EXCEPT through Me"

    Jesus: "I am THE WAY and THE TRUTH and THE LIFE" Jesus said He is the way, the truth and the life. To me Jesus was emphasizing that whoever choose to live(THE LIFE) that is to live in eternity should believe and follow Him in His teaching thus He said it that He is the way and the truth. Jesus was saying BELIEVE IN ME, FOLLOW MY TEACHING and YOU SHALL HAVE LIFE. Worthy of another note is that Jesus didn't say that He was merely way, truth and life but He was saying that He is THE WAY, THE TRUTH and THE LIFE. This is emphasizing that Jesus is the ONLY way, the HIGHEST or SUPREME truth [to that way/to life] and that He was offering the HIGHEST life.

    Jesus: "NO ONE comes to the Father EXCEPT through Me" Thus the second point wraps it up, backing the first point by saying that "No one comes to the Father except through Me". So we can be assured that No one that is no one can really know God and have a true relationship with God, the father except through putting faith in Jesus and following Jesus teaching because Jesus is the ONLY way, the SUPREME truth to life everlasting(life with God, the highest life or the best of life).

    In other words Jesus was saying by speaking those words that "He[Jesus] is the only way, the supreme truth to the highest life"

     

    I other words:

    Follow my teaching(My way),

    believe in Me(as I am the Truth, believe in what I've said and promised)

    and you will have the best of life(The life)
