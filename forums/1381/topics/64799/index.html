    FIRST LOVE
    by Bill Bright

    This book talks about renewing your passion for Christ, how through the business of our lives and carelessness we have abandoned our 'first love' for God.

    Using the Biblical reference of Revelation 2:1-7 where our Lord Jesus commended the Ephesian church for her many hard work and endurance, yet they have forsaken/abandoned their first love which they experience when they first come to the Lord.

    Have you felt that God is no longer with you? Is your heart for God cold and less zealous as compared to those days when u first believed in Him? Are you, like the Prodigal Son has forsaken your Father and left him only to realise all you get is a messed up and empty life, no meaning at all. Is God nothing more than a supernatural figure that you have not felt His sweet comforting presence for ages.

    This book is like an instruction manual to rekindle that flame of love. It gives practical steps we should take to return to the God who loves us so much. It is a life story of the founder of Campus Crusade for Christ International and how he came to know the Lord and how for the past 50 years have clinged on to this first love he have for Christ. A thought-provoking book that calls us to rethink our present spiritual condition and once again run to the Altar to be ignited by His flame.
