    2) To Adam he said, "Because you listened to your wife and ate from the tree about which I commanded you, 'You must not eat of it,' "Cursed is the ground because of you; through painful toil you will eat of it all the days of your life.     => Work was intended to be enjoyable and fulfilling. Now it would include drudgery and difficulty.
