    But when God sees ADAM as PERFECT, His idea of PERFECTION was not that. Adam was perfect not by our own standards but BY HIS STANDARDS. And His standard include giving ADAM a free will. A free will is not complete if it doesnt include the ability to choose the wrong thing.

    Who is to blame for Adam choosing to eat the wrong apple?

    Was God wrong? Nope. God did not force him to eat the apple.
    Was Eve wrong? Nope, she merely gave the apple to Adam.
    Was the serpent wrong? Nope, it merely gave Eve another perspective to the apple

    When EVE ate the apple, it is becos "the woman saw that the fruit of the tree was good for food and pleasing to the eye, and also desirable for gaining wisdom, she took some and ate it" (Gen 3:6) She then gave it to Adam who ate it.

    From the beginning till end, no one forced Adam to eat the apple. Thoughts and Desires came into him as with free will. All I can say and I believe is that Adam and Eve sinned becos they entertained the wrong desires.

    James 1:13-15 tells us this " When tempted, no one should say, "God is tempting me."
