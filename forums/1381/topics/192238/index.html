    Originally posted by laoda99:
    when we die, we go heaven/hell direct....or await judgement first?
    There are five judgments Laoda

    1) The judgement of our sins in Calvary. All who trust in Christ alone for salvation have already been judged in Calvary. When you die you don't have to be judged again for your sins. Christ bore it all.

    2) Self-judgement. This happens when we sin. This judgement of ourselves restores the fellowship lost because of sin in our life. Fellowship is lost but not the relationship.

    3) Judgement seat of Christ. This happens after the Rapture of the Church. Here rewards will be given based on our service here on earth.

    4) WE have the judgement of the nations. This takes place in the Second Coming of our Lord Jesus Christ where the gentile nations will be judged according to how they treat the restored nation of Israel during the great Tribulation

    5) The great white throne judgement. all the unsaved in all generations will be judged according to their works. And they will be cast into the lake of fire.

    When you die [if your saved] you will go direct to heaven and await the resurrection of your body [the final stage of salvation] at the resurrection you will face the Judgement seat of Christ [ number 3] together with all Christians from Pentecost to the Rapture.

    if you are an unbeliever: you are already condemned because you did not believe on the Son of God. You will go to Hades, the temporary place of the dead who are unsaved. there your soul will be tormented. At the Great White throne judgement you will be resurrected and face the Judge [Jesus Himself] and you will be thrown to your permanent abode: The lake of fire to be punished body and soul forever.
