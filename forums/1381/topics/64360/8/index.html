    Dear Members and Friends of the Brigade

    Calling all members and friends of the BB to join us in a time of shopping for the less fortunate. A concerted effort is put in place by the organising committee of BBSGB to gather all to purchase food items for the project.

    Till date, we have only collected 43% of the food items. With only 6 days to the close of the project, we still have 57% which amounts to about 172,000 food items to collect.

    We appeal for your participation in the "Buy a Gift for Sharity" by members of The Boys' Brigade. The BB would like to take this opportunity to take the lead in appealing to Singaporeans to come forward and make their contribution. Let's rally as a Brigade to show that WE CARE!

    Your attendance for this event would be a great encouragement to the less fortunate benefiting from the BB Sharity Gift Box. Details of the mobilisation alert are as follows:-

    Venue : Marine Parade NTUC FairPrice
    Date : Tuesday, 16 Dec 2003 / 7.30p.m.

    See the smiles on the beneficiaries faces when they receive the gifts from the BB Boys and you play a part to bring cheer to them this Christmas!
