    My church service usually starts off with worship songs. Followed tithes & offerings collection. A short 30min - 60min sermon after collection. Some annoucement if there's any. End off with some song and beneniction by the Pastor.
    For NCC, regular Sunday service start of with praise and worship, follwed by Holy Communion conducted by pastor, then followed by senior pastor's preaching/teaching/sharing. Normally we call it feeding of word, which is breaking the bible knowledge into easy-to-understand language to feed the congregation. After that is salvation call, for those who wants to receive Christ, then end with offering and announcement.
