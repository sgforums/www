    SONNET FOR THE SON OF GOD

    Jesus, You are my Lord, God and Saviour.
    To You, I am eternally grateful.
    You have shown me great mercy and favour,
    Despite me being stubborn as a mule.

    You have called me forth in Your awesomeness.
    You have beckoned me with Your still, soft voice.
    And when, Your commandments, I do transgress,
    You disciplined me; I left You no choice.

    Of all Your blessings, I am unworthy.
    Your love and pardon, I do not merit.
    Yet, my inheritence You guarantee.
    My falling away, You will not permit.

    Lord, thank you.  I'm eternally secure.
    In Your arms, I will rest forevermore.
