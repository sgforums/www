  • SingaporeMacross
    11 Feb 07, 23:04
    There is emphasis on a 'state of grace'. It describes one's communion with God. Committing venial sins will make one drift away from God. Mortal sins cut the communion for good, until the person is penitent and makes sacramental confession.

    It is not always possible to determine one's state of grace. However, examining one's conscience is often the best guide.
