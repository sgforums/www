    The Dubliner Irish Pub as the name suggest is a truly Irish pub complete with the decorations and ambience. Serving authentic Irish drinks such as Kilkenny, Strongbow and Guinness, The Dubliner Pub is you one stop spot to wind down after a long day at the office.

    3. Half Grilled Lobster with Cafe de Paris Butter

    4. Crispy Pork with Ponzu Vinaigrette

    5. Roasted Chicken Sandwich with Bacon & Cranberries

    These are merely some of the selections available at the Dubliner Irish Pub. A comprehensive main course menu is also available at the Dubliner which is just next door to Lanson Place.
