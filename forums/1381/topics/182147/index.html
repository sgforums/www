    1) Now, when you say "Dear God", are you referring to God the Father?

    2) Why does one need to say "Amen"?
    1) "Dear God" can refer to as the Holy Trinity (God the Father, Son and Holy Spirit).

    2) "Amen" refers to "and so be it" or "and let it be so".

    There are different ways of praying. Most important it has to come from a sincere heart. It also depends on whats your conviction. Some start with "Almighty Father", "Dear Heavenly Father", Dear Lord" etc etc.... and some end it as "In your most precious name" etc etc...

    Start by addressing God, followed by Thanking him, asking for forgiveness, then your thoughts (request) and end once again thank him then your closing.

    Whenever i am stuck and do not know what to pray and yet i know there is something that i need to pray for. Just say the most comprehensive prayer that Jesus taught us which is "The Lord's Prayer"

    Our Father In Heaven,
    Hallowed be your Name,
    Your Kingdom Come,
    Your Will be done on earth as it is in Heaven,
    Give us today our daily bread,
    and Forgive our sins as we Forgive those who sin against us,
    Do not put us to the test,
    But deliver us from evil
    For Thine is the Kingdom, and the Power and the Glory,
    Now and Forever,
    Amen!
