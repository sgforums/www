    We must recognise that in life we cannot avoid adversity. We can grow stronger through adversity. We will never figure out the right way to do things until we learn how not to do it in the first place. Adversity or 'sufferings' exists will be here to stay.

    The key to the problem is our attitude towards it. Many times we are quick to put the blame others for the situation we find ourselves in. We blame others for the misfortunes in life we face. The first step to our problems is to take responsibility of our own actions. We must learn to bear the consequences of our decisions.

    We should also look at 'sufferings' as an opportunity to grow. But we must be careful to handle adversity well. Anne Morrow Lindbergh said of suffering.
    I do not believe that sheer suffering teaches. If suffering alone taught, all the world would be wise, since everyone suffers. To suffering must be added mourning, understanding, patience, love, openness and the willingness to remain vulnerable. - Anne Morrow Lindbergh
    It is natural to react in anger to the frustrations in life. But once we change our attitudes towards problems, then we can be free of unneccesary anger.
