    Hepatitis slowly destroys your liver cells leading to cirrhosis - liver hardening which in turn blocks the flow of blood.

    Silymarin is a liver anti-oxidant and it prevents cirrhosis from developing protecting your liver cells from dying from the deadly toxins. It is best used in the early stages of a diseased liver.

    Note that some milk thistle are sold as mere powdered form of the herb. It's efficacy is very low because the active ingredient, silymarin is very low.
