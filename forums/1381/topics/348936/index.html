    Independent churches are churches that do not belong to any denominations like AG, Methodist, Presbyterian, Anglican, Baptist, Brethren etc

    They are usually Charismatic churches.

    The pastors usually came out from some traditions eg Brethren, but now they have a slightly different orientation, so they came out and form their own Charismatic, Independent church. 

    Charismatic churches don't have a denomination. So each one is independent.

    Unless they join the Assembly of God denomination and set up an AG church, then they will belong to the AG denomintaion, which is Pentecostal.
