    Maudy Thursday or Holy Thursday, commemorates the Institution of the Eucharist during the Last Supper. After the evening Mass, there will be a Benediction of the Blessed Sacrament. Adoration of the Blessed Sacrament will continue until midnight in all churches.

    Of particular notice is that in the morning a Chrism Mass will be celebrated. All the priests will assemble and celebrate Mass together with the archbishop. There will be the blessing of Holy Oils, or Chrism. The Holy Oils are used for Baptism, Confirmation and Holy Orders.
