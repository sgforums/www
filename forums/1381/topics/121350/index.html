    Christian life......

    1) Eternal life with God through Christ

    2) Forgiven from all of our sins

    3) Journey of faith

    4) Lots of trials and difficulties, never meant to be easy

    5) Spiritual warfare and wrestling against spiritual darkness

    6) Need to feed upon His Word daily

    7) Dependent on the Holy Spirit is a must

    8 ) Get ready to be mocked and persecuted for you bear His name

    9) Press on, forgetting what is behind but looking forward for the prize He has called you heavenward to obtain

    10) Yes, christian life is hard...but take courage for Christ has overcome the world.
