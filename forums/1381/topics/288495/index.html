Closing of Eternal Hope

  • sillyme
    29 Oct 07, 09:47
    Dear friends,

    It is with great sadness that I am writing this. I have proposed to Chin Eng and ben1xy on closing this forum. We have agreed that we will no longer keep this forum active.

    I am no longer able to actively read posts in this forum and be an effective moderator because of family and work priorities. As I consider about lessening my activities here, it will not be fair to let the other moderators bear too much of the load. Hence, we will close this forum.

    The secondary reason in closing this forum is because of the recent activities in EH has made it unpleasant to me to visit this forum. I love God and would love to share my joy with whoever is interested to know more. But I feel it is no longer sensible to share when our views are repeated put down just because someone is not agreeable to our faith.

    Instead of appearing to be a thorn in the flesh, it is best that we remove our presence here.

    Eternal Hope was started as a gathering place for fellow Christians in sgForums. I have made many good friends through this forum and my stay here was mostly enjoyable.

    To the "old gang", Choco B, the Bear, skinnybeanie, Inzaghi, M, s|nned, tare, NT2 etc., my beginnings in here was made wonderful by all of you.

    To Chin Eng, ben1xy, Icemoon, laurence82, vince69, laoda99, thank you. Thank you for the knowedge I have learnt from you. Thank you for the friendship I have with you. (though I did not have the opportunity to meet some of you)

    From this day, Eternal Hope will no longer moderated. All old and new posts will be deleted.

    I will still be in sgForums.
