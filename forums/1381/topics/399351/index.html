    Just to share an extract of an inspiring talk delivered by Dr Mark Conner. Take time to reflect on the questions and be blessed.

    1. What are the qualities or attributes of the people you like to be around? Imagine who you would invite for a Friday night out. Here are some of the answers provided by the audience. Feel free to add to the list.

    Fun
    Humorous
    Generous
    Friendly
    Polite
    Respectful
    Punctual
    Positive
    Patient
    Caring
    Loving
    Listen
    Dress Well
    ...

    2. What are the qualities or attributes of the people you don't like to be around? Think of the people whom you would not invite for a Friday night out. Here are some of the answers provided by the audience. Feel free to add to the list.

    Grumpy
    Irritable
    Rude
    Loud
    Demanding
    Impatient
    Stingy
    Negative
    Self-centred
    Late
    Bad Dress Sense
    Bad Breath
    Body Odour
    Talk too much
    Eat too much
    ...

    3. How do you want to be treated?

    1. You want others to accept you.
    2. You want others to encourage you.
    3. You want others to listen to you.
    4. You want others to understand you.
    5. You want others to forgive you.


    In everything, do to others what you would have them do to you, for this sums up the Law and the Prophets. - Mathew 7:12
