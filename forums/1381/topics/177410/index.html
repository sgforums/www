    Siamese Twins

    It is a freak of nature. Twin babies born, joined together. One cannot live with out the other. Repentance and faith are like that. They are spiritual Siamese twins. Repentance is turning away from our sinful ways. While faith is turning to Christ.

    The turning away from sin is repentance. The turning to Christ is faith. Repentance leaves sin. Faith embraces the free offer of salvation by Christ. They are like two sides of a coin.

    Repentance is not the payment for sin, rather it is the condition to forgiveness. The payment for sin is the work of the Lord Jesus Christ alone in Calvary and must be embraced by faith alone. Sinners are not to rely on their repentance so that their sins may be forgiven, rather they are to rely on the saving work of Jesus Christ alone for the forgiveness of sins

    The fountain of forgiveness, the fountain of salvation is free in Calvary. For a sinner to get his stains washed and cleaned, he must leave the dark alleys of sin and go to Calvary. The leaving is repentance. The goint to mount Calvary is faith.

    His leaving sin did not wash it away, it was when he got to Calvary that it got washed away, it was there he got saved.
