    The Yellow Ribbon Project seeks to help ex-offenders who take responsibility for their own actions and are trying their best to persevere and earn back the trust from their family, friends, colleagues and the community. The Yellow Ribbon Project engages the community to offer second chances to ex-offenders who sincerely want to change.

    The activity seeks to mobilise the community, through the help of CARE Network, aftercare agencies, the Home Team, VWOs and community partners, employers, religious organisations, schools and grassroots organisations, to wear the yellow ribbon during the week from 2 Â– 9 Oct.     Yellow ribbons packs at $1 each will be distributed and proceeds will go towards the Yellow Ribbon Fund, to be set up to fund aftercare programmes as well as prisoners' family and children support programmes.

    Reading and Understanding your Bible
    Workshop for church groups aimed to help individuals understand the Bible for themselves.

    The Reading and Understanding Your Bible (RUB) series of workshops is a specially tailored programme intended to help ordinary church members understand the Bible for themselves. Usually only Bible college students receive training in exegetical and hermeneutical skills, while the laity in many churches have little exposure to such disciplines.

    These workshops are structured for the typical church goer, providing Christians with techniques of understanding the Bible and comprehending seemingly difficult portion of Scripture, such as the Old Testament prophets.

    In addition, the Bible Society is also offering a four-session workshop to train facilitators who would then be equipped to conduct RUB sessions for their own members and small groups in their respective churches.

    This FacilitatorsÂ’ Training workshop would be ideally suited for leaders of small groups and ministries, who regularly lead in Bible studies. To qualify for the Facilitators' Training workshop, participants must first complete the RUB Workshop. Upon completion of the Facilitators' Training workshop, each participant would be awarded a certificate that would qualify them to teach the RUB Workshop, if they were to purchase the workbooks."
