    Let's testify and glorify God

    By writing what God has done for each one of us, small or big, but it has got to be genuine of course.Don't quote from the bible we all could read from the bible but write from what you know by your own experience of being Christian. There is no need to be detailed but please be sincere and truthful. And I've been accused of some sort of bragging in other forum when I didn't even try. I mean why would I want to brag while everyone doesn't know me. Just be sincere and I hope this could strengthened your faith. I go first I hpe this thread will never end. God bless.

    The God whom I worship set me free from the bondage of evil(spirit). My cry of help from the heart was not in vain.

    The God whom I worship knows what lies in the heart of men.

    The God whom I worship could change the hearts of men in an instant. Giving love of Jesus, changing anger and hate to love and changing the decision of men that was thought to be final

    The God whom I worship hears my silent prayer and could give me what I asked in an instant. He showed me the glory of Jesus and He moved the pillars of the earth just to answer you are my chosen. And He freed me from evil because I cried out to Him.

    The God whom I worship isn't bound by time. He knows what is going to happen to anyone or to any nation. He knows my future like it already happened.

    The God whom I worship makes plans before doing anything and executes them in detail. His plans are well thought of and even considerate to humans and that shows that  He cares and loves us.

    The God whom I worship could tell the secret of this life. Secret of life that no man could ever know by himself. Secrets that could not be revealed by our effort alone. He let me know because He wanted His people to know the truth and be wiser in life.

    God whom I worship could give [and take away] a sense of overwhelming peace that put a smile on your face every second of it and makes you heart so gentle.
