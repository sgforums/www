"GOD/others are cruel to me" When we met with a problem, it is always others who are at fault. When we point the accusing finger at others/GOD, we forgot about our own mistakes.     My question is: Can one really bear the consequences?

    6) For Christians, do we often focus on doing our own will and not GOD's?
