    Actaully, there are two kinds of faith:

    1) Natural faith and 2) Divine faith

    natural faith --everybody has it. We hail a taxi cab and we board in trusting, having faith that the driver would take us to our destination. We are diagnosed with an ailment that entails surgery........we submit ourselves to the surgeon's skillful hand trusting that he will do well.

    We take medications prescribed by the doctor believing and trusting in him that he means well and wants us to get well.

    Divine faith-- on the other hand is a faith not found in unregenerated man. It is a gift from God. True faith means that we have seen our helplessness with regards to salvation and convicts us of our sin and makes us embrace what Christ has done.

    True faith is just an enlightened eye. 1) it sees our helplessnes 2) it sees our depravity 3) it sees Christ as Savior. The reason why true faith endures to the end and keeps on seeing Christ [number 3] is because it keeps on seeing 1 and 2.

    True faith says: "i will never let go of Christ because ever before me i see my helplessness with regards to salvation and the sin that is in me."
