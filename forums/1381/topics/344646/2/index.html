Whether it hits the ground or lands on someone's head, again depends on the position of the two at that particular time. If both item and person are at the same place at the same time, a collision happens. When that happens again it depends on the item and the person, what the results will be. Everything will be precisely accordingly. Whether we like the results or not does not affect the precision of the results. Maybe someone is killed, severly injured, or just shocked, maybe the item just hit the ground without anyone knowing about it. There cannot be a wrong result. Like a pot falling on a person's head and that person turn into ice. This can never happen because that would be a wrong result. So everything happens accordingly, whether we like it or not. But no, things will happen accordingly correct. That person will die accordingly, or be cured accordingly. Correct results meaning being successful, having a pleasant life, wealth, health, strength, and all things good. The answer is simple, you think. You search for information, direction, clues, help, suggestions, from any source that is available to you, in order to do the correct actions for the correct result. And the simplest available source is to think of God. All of us has an inherent God within us. We can think, we know what is right and what is wrong, what is good and what is bad. It is just a matter of strengthing the God within us, or letting it wither. So God is for guidance as how to act for a particular result, but then we need the wisdom to know that things don't always go the way we want it to go. There are too many influences that are outside our control, that affect our plans. Think of God, and you will know how to work, to live, to react, how to overcome troubles.
