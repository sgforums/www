    I must say that this is a bold venture on my part because I have not done anything of such nature before in my life. I set up this blog partly out of impulse and partly as a result of the moving of the Holy Spirit.

    I see my blog as a mosaic of theological articles. Actually to be very honest, most of the writings will not exactly be original per se, that is the ideas and arguments are mostly based on the books that I read. There are other much more competent Apologetics websites out there in the Internet such as carm.org but I realise that people I will like to evangelise will only bother to read anything written by me.....

    Yeah, fundamentally the intent and engine of the blog will be evangelism-based but I will be writing entries out of my own personal reflections so occasionally there will be so-called inconsistencies in content and theme.
