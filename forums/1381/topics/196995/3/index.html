    To those who have quoted me regarding the Bible.

    God uses man. God gives man free will. This free will includes writing what man wants in the Bible.

    Does God intervenes in man's life directly? Yes but only for those justified by faith. Even then God intevenes in a natural rather than supernatural manner.

    Only when it is impossible for man to do it, does God intevene. And only in times of great need. These are so few and far in between that we can safely say that God does not intervene directly and chose to depend on man and all other incidences are exceptions to the rule.
