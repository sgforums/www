    Sorry, my previous introduction was too brief.

    Ok... I was a cradle Catholic from Queen of Peace. For years, my family has stopped going to church. They have become what we called the lapsed Catholic. So when I reached the age that I should be attending Catechism classes, nobody registered for me, nobody brought me to church.

    Then slowly, I was introduced to a Protestant church, under the denormination of Presbyterian. Many years later when I reached 18, I decided to go back to the Catholic Church, for the confirmation. As I was a special case (most catholics got confirmed at the age of 14 or 16 ... can't remember), the church arranged for a one-to-one crash course on the sacrements.

    After some months of preparations, I was finally confirmed at the church where I was baptised .

    This year, I have just moved to Bukit Batok and have since cut myself away from the church activities in the East district, due to the distance and commitment. I have yet to meet anybody in SMOA or take part in anything.

    End of story....
