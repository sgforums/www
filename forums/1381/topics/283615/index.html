    It depends what one means by "loving yourself".

    If "loving yourself" is a self-centred kind of love, then it has negative consequences.

    In the Christian context, it means knowing that you are made in the image of God; you are wonderfully created in Him. You are to be good stewards of your life and what you have been given. You are called to have a relationship with Him, and by that you are to called to love others.
