    Palm Sunday. To me, such celebrations, including normal day activities / events can always be well used by our Lord to refresh us, or give us a good message - messages to bring us closer to Him.

    Like this Palm Sunday. i believe God used a Pastor to talk about the "Palms" of God. A true story was shared - which i thought is appropriate to share here.

    There was this girl from Africa, whos parents died when she was at a young age. She was left with a big group of younger brothers to take care of. There was practically no money to take care of the brothers - and so she considered some options - including prostitution. But finally, she thought that prostitution is no go - because her parents are good Christians and would not have allow it to happen when they are still alive. But then what can she do ? Her options are very limited indeed.

    So she has to do little tasks which no one wants to do. One of those is the washing of clothes. She had to do many of those day in, day out - just to feed her brothers and giving them an education.

    At the end of it, she managed to succeed - her brothers today received good education and are good men of faith. But all these are given at a cost - the cost of her health. Very soon, her health sccumbed to pure exhaustion.

    At her deathbed, the girl ask to see a Pastor. Upon reflecting on her life, she cried to the Pastor, saying that all her life, all she did was to wash other people's clothes. And if ever she gets a chance to go to Heaven, she is scared. She does not know what to say to God when she meet Him in Heaven. What can she say ? What has she done ? All she has done is to wash clothes !

    The Pastor says: "My child, perhaps you dont have to say a word. Just show God your palms, He knows all about you through those callouses of your roughen hands".

    Indeed, when Jesus comes riding in on a donkey as King of kings, the Love of God for His people is revealed.
