    Simple explanation:

    Blessed are the poor in spirit,
    for theirs is the kingdom of heaven.

    Poor in spirit = humble

    Blessed are they who mourn,
    for they shall be comforted.

    Mourn = sad that the world we live in is inperfect, with sins.

    Blessed are the meek,
    for they shall inherit the earth.

    meek = be gentle and kind. not prone to anger

    Blessed are they who hunger and thirst for righteousness,
    for they shall be satisfied.

    hunger... righteousness = seeking for justice + people who are honest

    Blessed are the merciful,
    for they shall obtain mercy.

    as aboved. Love, compassion, and forgiveness.

    Blessed are the pure of heart,
    for they shall see God.

    Pure = not selfish and being selfless. It does not mean being naive.

    Blessed are the peacemakers,
    for they shall be called children of God.

    peacemaker = bring peace to oneself and others.

    Blessed are they who are persecuted for the sake of righteousness,
    for theirs is the kingdom of heaven."

    People who are persecuted for the sake of Christianity.

     

    Kingdom of Heaven = The place given to the people, aka chosen ones, after they die.

    "Your kingdom comes, your will be done on earth as it is in heaven".

    Final place of Kingdom of Heaven = Earth.
