    About Sola Scriptura
    I think a clarification is in order for the benefit of everyone. Sola scriptura ("bible alone") is the protestant doctrine that asserts that God's word is revealed to us through the bible alone and no other means. The Catholic Church agrees 100% that God's word comes to us through the bible which the Church cherishes and encourages all Catholics to read.

    What the Catholic Church rejects is the belief that it is only through the bible that God's word is revealed to us. This is false and unbiblical. God's word comes to us through the bible and his Church, which existed even before the bible was completed. The bible is a collection of writings by various persons, including the Apostles. These writings were all inspired by God most certainly, but the inspired authors (and God) never intended for these writings to be the be all and the end all of the Christian faith.
