    actually I listen to Nirvana, Slipknot, Creed, Radiohead, Marilyn Manson even....though I am a christian.

    Now, people will rush to demean my spirituality or faith because of what I listen to. They say that good christians do not listen to such music. I say nonsense.

    In my church, I use to be 'counselled' left, right, centre because of what I listen to. But I had my reasons. I was serving in another ministry where they reach out to kids who are not keen on church or church music, they simply love "destructive" loud music. And to reach out to them, I must know my stuff, i cannot just go up to them and ask them to listen hillsongs or preach Jesus right away. They would simply ask u to buzz off if u did that. In their words, they said "Dun talk sh*t about me, if you aint know sh*t about me.". I had to be their fren, so I listen to what they listen to identify with them (actually i listen to all these since secondary school lah hehehe).

    But beyond that, I wish to correct christians' perceptions that rock music is evil. it is not. The music itself has nothing. The people who plays it puts life into it. If we were to say God has no control of Rock music, then He is not LORD of ALL. God can use rock music to reach out too. The key to rock music evangelism is to get people to come, play songs (never mind if they cant hear the lyrics), get off stage, mingle with the people and be a fren.

    So, people, there are a group of people who will turn and walk away when they hear hillsongs or hymns (sometimes i wanted to hahaha), so why not let God use their kind of music reach out to them.

    In that aspect, recently my church allowed me to do a small concert with my kind of music in church. People came and heard about Christ! Cool!!
