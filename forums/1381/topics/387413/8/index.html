    What are the reasons for me to believe in God?

    Well, it depends on which God. Although I excepted Christ during my teens, I grew up with in a family where my father is a Tang Ki, a Chinese Medium. He had bloodless scars when he used parangs to slash his own belly. He also kept Kun Man Thong (Those Thai statues of little children believed to be spirits of children).

    I stopped attending church after my O'levels I also became a professed Thai Buddhist where I consulted a Bomoh & I set up an altar at my own home, I did'nt even know what I was worshipping. I worshipped anything which promises me wealth. It is a Phra Na Prok statue, a Buddha sheltered by a Giant Cobra but for mine, the statue sitting there was Khum Paeng, a general in Thailand during the medievel days, the one who believed to be the Father of all the Kun Man Thong. In the Thai mythology, he sweared before the devils from Hell to make him the most powerful warrior on earth, he killed his wife & roasted his unborn foetus over fire, his foetus spirit made him a powerful warrior, but his story also ended up in a tragedy.

    The Phra Na Prok statue was one of the kind, with seven monkey heads on the Cobra's crown, with Khum Paeng sitting on it.In the Cobra's coiled body there were those pearl like bone relics(believed to be the monk's unburnt ashes) sealed by a Tan Gah. Those copper plates with the symbolic signs.

    I also kept a Phra Som Deq pendant where I will always put on faithfully. I also chanted faithfully on every Thurs morning. I will also visit Thai Temples to worship Phra Pong, the four face Buddha & often get the monks to bless me.

    But a twist of fate happened to me. Although my dad did strike 4D oftenly, my parents were divorced. Both my parent were down with schizophrenia. My Dad was also down with stroke & every single organ was failing him. He almost lost his life. For me, I lost my job, lost my house, lost my car & what I only have is my wife & kid. I looked for a shelter to for my Dad to live in. At last, it was a Pastor who lended the 1st helping hand. That was my turning point. I returned to Christ. Now my Dad is resting in a Methodist nursing home.

    Through this twist of fate, I am now attending church. Although life is not smooth & I faced some of my toughest times in my life even when I'm a Christian. Me & my wife is on the brink of bankruptcy. But deep inside I know that this is the God that I want to worship. The God of Abraham, Jacob & Moses. The same God which parted the Red Sea & brought His people out of Eygpt. The strength which the Lord gave me to overcome the few of my most toughest times made me knew that it was'nt me. I was broke but not broken. It was my God who strengthened me. I grew in character & learnt how to enjoy my family life although we are not financially well off, as well as the wisdom to handle money which do not seem to be much. I was given the Grace to repent for my wrong doings.

    Although I am not saint, but I am looking forward to changing myself everyday, from the inside out. I regained my fitness, I have a stable job despite the economical madness & I am expecting a pay rise next month. No one forced me to accept Jesus as my Lord & Saviour. I chose Him because I believe in His goodness, His mercy especially His acceptance of who I am.
