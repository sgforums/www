    The event described here between Jesus and Nicodemus (a short background on him, he is a Pharisees, not just any Pharisees, he is a member of the Jewish ruling council; ie. a high ranking Pharisees)
    Here, Jesus was telling Nicodemus, that one need to be born again to enter into the Kingdom of God. Nicodemus at the first moment was confused, hence he asked the Lord, "how to be born again?"
    Here is Jesus' answer to Nicodemus;
    No one can enter the kingdom of God unless he is born of water and the Spirit.
    at this, Nicodemus obviously lost, asked how can this be? as in what is it to be born of water and the Spirit?
    At this, Jesus reminded him about the bronze snake that Moses lifted up in the desert.
    (background to this event: pls read Numbers 21:4-9 - 8 The LORD said to Moses, "Make a snake and put it up on a pole; anyone who is bitten can look at it and live." 9 So Moses made a bronze snake and put it up on a pole. Then when anyone was bitten by a snake and looked at the bronze snake, he lived.

    Key note to this is that whoever bitten by the snake (consequence of their sin), will to look at the bronze serpent (not fight the snake, not suck out the vernom..etc) will live. )

    contrast this to our Lord Jesus, He was hanged up on the cross (pole) and whoever will to put their faith in Him (not on works or deeds), will not be condemn (consequence of Sin) but have eternal life (live).

    Hence: to be born again is to change our mindset
    From
    trying to work it out with God through our works or deeds
    To
    believing/depending in the finished works of Christ on the cross.
