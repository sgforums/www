    Here are the wines to be tasted:
    #1 BlackWood Fishbone Semillon Chenin Blanc 2002
    Brilliant pale straw colour with a tinge of green. A
    good intensive nose with passion fruit and grapefruit
    dominating. A clean crisp mouth feel with moderate
    palate intensity. The passion fruit on the nose
    carries through the palate, while zingy acid carries
    throughout (Alc. 12.5%.).

    #2 BlackWood Fishbone Cabernet Shiraz 2001
    A brick red coloured Shiraz with a moderate nose
    intensity. Has hints of violets, and plum overtones.
    Good mouth feel, clean and long, with chocolate and
    coffee flavours leading to chewy finish (Alc. 14.0%).

    #3 Rivendell Cabernet Merlot 2001
    A smooth, soft red style, with good intensity of
    flavour. 60% Cabernet Sauvignon, 30% Merlot and 10%
    Cabernet Franc. This wine was matured for 12 months in
    French barriques (Alc 13.5% ).

    #4 Scotts Brook Cabernet Sauvignon 2000
    A rich, beautifully balanced wine with blackberry and
    almond characters as well as hints of dark chocolate
    and mint. A stunning wine which thoroughly deserves
    the GOLD medal the wine won at the 2002 Australian
    Small Winemakers Show (Alc. 14.5).

    #5 BlackWood Verdelho 2001
    A true late harvest wine from handpicked fruit grown
    in Australia's South West, this 2001 Verdelho exhibits
    all the intense and luscious sweet fruit
    characteristics typical of this style. This is matched
    with a fine mouth watering acidity giving the wine a
    lingering finish (Alc .10.2%).
