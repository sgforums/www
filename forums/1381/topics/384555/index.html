    Dear Siblings in Christ,

    In your walk with the Lord, have you ever came to a point in life where life seems so hopeless & you really feel like ending your life & join the Lord in His kingdom?

    Honestly speaking although we know the Word of the Lord & His Word says Jesus died on the cross to save us. I also heard the testimonies & I wonder when will be my turn to give one. Even in the toughest times of my life, I still confess that Yahweh is the True God & I won't bow down to other gods.

    I am bonded in a lowly paying job with no time for church, my wife lost her job, we have a pile of debts to clear. My marriage is falling apart. But I insisted on being strong because I love my 2 lovely sons which the Father gave me. My absence from church made me so condemned that I did'nt dare to face my care group.

    But life can be so painful & I worked myself crazy during work to forget all the pains. I read the Word, I confessed, I prayed but I can't bring out my faith to believe. The only consolation which I have is to enjoy my occasional worship music & worship Yahweh the True God & give thanks to Jesus who died on the cross for me who took the worst of me to give me His best.

    Dear believer, have you ever came to this stage in life & how do you overcome it & came out victorious? Pls share.
