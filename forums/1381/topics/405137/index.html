    A play by a new theatre company called The Mulberry People (TMP). Who is showcasing their debut production 'RELATE' on from July 29 to 31  with 4 shows! The play comprises a series of 10 sketches stringed together into one play; all revolving relationships in realism that we see or personally experience everyday.

     Why 'RELATE'?

    Since time immemorial, so many successful movies, music and shows out there are about relationships - stories between people. We cannot deny the innate human hunger to connect with people around them. And that is what we aim to do, showcase relationships that will relate in a personal way to the audience.
