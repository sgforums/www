    Originally posted by gasband:
    This place needs some loving....
    And also this (taken from other forum, written by other forumer):

    Greetings
    I have been reading these posts with great interest.
    I love ministering on the internet, been doing it for about 15 years. I also love spending time with my non-Catholic Brothers and Sisters.
    Some of the things I have observed are the following.

    1. Non Catholics spend more time thinking about Mary than most Catholics do. It
    is non-Catholics who usually start threads about her. We don't even spend a lot
    of time chatting about her, even with each other. In a Catholic forum, most
    often when there is a Mary thread, it has been started by a non-Catholic who is
    visiting.

    2. Very little time is spent discovering what we agree on. Most discussions are
    extremely negative. Does anyone ever spend any time TRYING to understand
    what the other person is feeling, thinking, why they believe what they do?
    3. I see a lot of Spiritual Pride. I am so sorry to say this but it is true. We. that
    goes for me and for you, believe that we are right in everything. Some of you
    think it is your duty to charge right in, when YOU think something is bad
    doctrine. What makes you right and another person wrong? You say it is
    scripture but so does the other person. You say it is the Holy Spirit, but then,
    so does the other person. Does the Scripture lie to one of you? Does the Holy
    Spirit? I always get very suspicious of that person who stubbornly digs in his/her
    heels and closes the ears and eyes to what is being said.
    4. How often, when you are having a difficult time with someone, do you stop and
    pray together? This probably should be first. The most important things
    Christians can do. You and I continually try to do it alone.

    All to often we talk about loving each other, talk about our being Christian Brother and Sisters, but what would most of the world think if they were lurking and watching? Jesus in his prayer for Unity, Prayed that WE would be ONE as HE and the FATHER are one, so that the world will know that the Father sent Him.

    We are not doing a good job and I agree this is not good at all.
