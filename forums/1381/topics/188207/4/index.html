    We who have trusted in Christ alone for salvation are not under the law. We are under grace. But being under grace does not mean that we are lawless [antinomianism, cheap grace].     1) You must keep on knowing that you [the old man] enslaved by sin died with Christ and therefore sin shall not have dominion over you.
