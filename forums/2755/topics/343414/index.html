    Try these canapes for starters. Buy frozen filo pastries, bake them til brown & top up with prawns or tuna. Garnish with dill.

     

    Tuna with green apple mayo recipe:

    - Drain away water in tuna completely.

    - Mix tuna with mayo, french onions, green apples & dash of lemon.

    - Top the pastries with tuna mayo & garnish with dill.

     

    Prawns with avocado cream receipe

    - Stir fry prawns with olive oil, garlic, black pepper & dash of salt.

    - After cool down, drain away excess water from prawns.

    - Stir in small chunks of cucumber, mashed avocado into salad cream.

    - Top the pastries with cream, then prawns, garnish with dill.

     

    Presentation

    - Lay all the canapes on a big tray.

    - Line the balance dill around the tray.
