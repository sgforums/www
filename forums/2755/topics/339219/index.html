    Spells of the Darkling Lords

    ======================

    1) Rot  ( Darkstorm, leader of the darkling lords)

    by what creeps, what crawls,

    by what does not,

    let all that grows, recede and rot.

     

    2) counter-spell for Rot ( Darkstorm)

    Power of rot, obscuring truth,

    what once was old, return to youth.

     

    3) Fear

    O mist-filled pits,

    dark, damp, unclear,

    touch all the foremen with frost-fingered fear.

     

    4) Destruction

    by nature's hands,

    by craft, by art,

    what once was once, now flys apart.

     

    5) Force-shield/Protection

    the arrows turn, the swords repel,

    may nothing pierce this mortal shell.

     

     

    Spells of the Spectrum Knights

    ========================

    1) Wisdom ( Leoric, leader of the spectrum knights)

    whispered secrets of a shattered age,

    i summon you, renew this sage.

     

    2) Knowledge

    a wind, a thought,

    and more is sought,

    awake my mind, thy will be wrought.

     

    3) Speed

    shift these feet in the driving gale,

    make swift these legs on land i sail.

     

    4) Strength

    three suns align,

    pour forth their light,

    and fill the archer's bow with might.
