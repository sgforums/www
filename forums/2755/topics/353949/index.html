    Rough GPS overview of the path taken...

    Distance: 12.74 Km

    Time start around:1020 Hrs

    Time End around : 1315 Hrs (not inclusive of eating)

    Duration: 3 Hrs

    Type : Forest walk

    Difficulties : Pathways are dirt road with occasional slopes with some hilly area. Some danger as some higher walk path are eroded. Fast moving bicycles were encountered in forested area which increase risk as well.

    Tips : Bring around 1.5 to 2.5 litres of water, travel light and wear light clothing.
