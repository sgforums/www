    DS = Direct Shot! Come to say that, in soccer direct shot is not that easy, you would rather take a shot yourself! But in this virtual world of street soccer, its totally the opposite! When you are starting out with low levels, getting a solo shot on for goal is highly impossible(cept in training). So the only/most probable way to score is DIRECT SHOT. Everyones calling it DS! Look at the picture! http://i3.photobucket.com/albums/y58/AlgrenFrancis/DS1.jpg See the red circle? The ball is within the circle. You can proceed to training or practice server now to try it out. Pass the ball to the "forward" at the corners. Wait for them to cross over. When the ball is similarly in the air,(meaning timing) press "D" or "F" for a direct shot. You learnt this in tutorial.(I rec you guys do it coz of the free 4k points) http://i3.photobucket.com/albums/y58/AlgrenFrancis/DS2.jpg Keep trying untill you get the flow of it. If you think you can score for all the crosses that are made to you. You can move on! Next stage of DS: Pass the ball to the "forward" thats back court. But you stay a little in front of them. They will Pass you the ball, before the ball reaches you, press "D" or "F" for a DS. it takes time to get use to the ball speed. If your sprinting, they players pass a faster ball. Just keep passing the ball to the "forward" and wait for the 1-2 return ball. TIPS: Usually, i would just keep spamming on my "SHOOT" keys when they are passing. Direction and so isnt important so yet coz its just practice. You will know which direction to aim or face during a actual game. TIPS: During a game, the most important criteria for a goal is "No Mark".
