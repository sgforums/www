    4th - Air Suspension Controller

    8th - Glass Full Snoke ( Use to radiate sunlight and etc... )

    10th - Neon Tube ( AWESOME! you can see lights under your car chassis )

    This car is definitely not for hardcore battle or something, unless playing handicap/boost on might gives you chances of winning...

    Have played the Celsior till 640Hp and here are my thoughts.

    a) 4 gears Manual gear shifting.
    - Because its only 4 gears, in order to maintain top speed, you really have to try to stay in 4th. If you need to slow down, perhaps tapping the brake lightly would be better than shifting down to 3rd. Not totally mastered it yet though.

    b) Battles
    - Quite hard. You can be playing flawlessly but to overtake opponents is still quite hard regardless boost is on or off. Not sure if everthing changes at 800hp but it is quite hard to accelerate fast. perhaps because of its body weight.

    c) Opponents crashing or nudging you
    - This is where the celsior excels. Opponents cant nudge you out of the way as easy as it would other cars. maybe becuase this car is heavier and nudging your opponents is easier.

    d) Handling
    - Handling is good and very stable.

    Overall, its a lovely car to drive as its very beautiful with the metallic purple, 4 exhauste pipes, neon blue lights at the bottom of the car. Can only post a proper battle feedback after i hit 800hp. People say at 800hp, its a totally different car.
