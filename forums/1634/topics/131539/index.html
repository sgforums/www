    HG - High Grip (good for Hakone as tires are grip type, good for a lot of courses with lots of corners)
    G - Grip (a lower version of HG)
    B - Balance (just nice)
    D - Drift (slightly unbalance but still alright)
    DG - Dangerous (no grip at all)

    All cars are equal so no fast or slow, it all depends on skills and the familiarity of the maps...good luck and enjoy the game...
