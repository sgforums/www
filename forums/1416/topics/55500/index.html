History of Serangoon Garden Secondary School.

  • foxtrout8
    24 Aug 03, 01:00
    1959 - Started as Lichfield Secondary with English Steam intake.

    1960 - The School renamed Serangoon Garden Government High School , taking in English and Chinese stream pupils.

    1969 - The School absorbed Hwi Yoh Vocational School becoming Serangoon Garden Secondary with facilities for academic , technical and commercial subjects.

    1965 to 1974 - The School had Pre-University classes which offer 2 year courses in Arts and Sciences.

    1976 - Enrolment peaked with a total of 3000 in 78 classes. On top of that , a few hundred of pupils enroled in technical and economics classes.

    1980 - Pre-University Classes restarted with 3 year couse in commerce.

    1981 - First batch of Normal Academic Courses.

    1984 - Last two Chinese stream pupil graduated.

    1985 - School becomes purely English Stream.

    1991 - Last Pre-University Classes graduated.

    1994 - First batch of Normal Technical Courses.

    1997 - Together with Bartley sec , Deyi Sec , Xinmin Sec , Nayang JC , Holy Innocents sec and Sengkang Sec , we formed the North One Cluster of schools.

    2000 - The School function as a single session school.

    2003 - Intake of our friends from Serangoon Garden Technical School.
