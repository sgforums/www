    If you are 40 to 60 years old:

    Participate in a speech research project for I2r (A-Star) and earn $40

     All you have to do is read aloud from an English script which will be provided by us (2 persons taking turns to read alternately).

    Please check out "BBC Learning English - 6 minute lessons" for the type of script you will be asked to read. 

    Limited participants hence please email us asap to book your time slots. Time slots are available on weekdays (Monday to Friday) between 8 am to 8 pm. In case you are free only on weekends, a mutually agreeable time slot can be arranged. 

    To apply for participation in this project kindly email the following information to hr@tvcpl.com :

                                    - Email Subject: SGFORUMS - Participate in a speech research                                     project for I2r (A-Star)

                                    - Your Details: Nationality, Name, Age, Gender, Mobile, Highest                                     Educational Qualification

                                    - Person who will be partnering you: Nationality, Name, Age,                                           Gender, Mobile, Highest - Educational Qualification

                                    - Your preferred days/timings for the recording session

     

                                    Please ensure that you satisfy the following requirements:

                                    - Native Singaporean

                                    - Between 40 and 60 years of age

                                    - Able to read English well (please refer to BBC Learning English                                   for script sample)

                                    - Willing to travel to A-Star's office at Fusionopolis (just above the                                   One-North MRT Station on Circle Line)

                                    - The session is expected to take around 1.5 hours (or shorter,                                       depending on your reading speed)

                                    - Bring along a friend who satisfies the above criteria (as 2                                             persons need to take turns to read from a script which will be                                       recorded on a computer)

                                    - If you are unable to bring a friend along, we will try to pair you                                       up with other participants

    You are required to fill up a simple participation form and agree to letting A-Star use your voice for Speech Research project and to make your voice publicly available without disclosing your identity

    Each person participating in this project will be paid S$40.

    If you bring a friend, each of you will be paid S$40 (total $80 per speaker pair).

    Hurry up and email us !!!
