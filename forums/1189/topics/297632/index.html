    This poem was composed by poet Li Zhiyi.

    Basically it's about this girl pining for her beloved, who lives on one end of River Yangtze, while she lives on the other end of the same river. Despite drinking from the same river, they never meet.

    "Jun1" does not necessarily have to refer to a husband. It can also be a more intimate way for a lady to address a man.

    Just sharing.
