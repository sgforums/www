    . . . A Sad Love Story . . .

    few years ago
    two friends fell in love with each other
    they were very happy together
    and felt at ease with each other's company

    they shared good moments
    shared personal experiences
    conveyed through many sms and e-mails

    but all the while
    they had been aware
    of the innocent feeling of love
    that existed between them
    before friendship began

    still, they are willing
    to allow the friendship
    to grow into something deeper
    in a natural way

    then one day
    the man asked the woman out
    and she agreed.
    it was a very beautiful evening
    though a light rain was showering the place

    however...
    some days later
    the woman began to think
    about a past relationship......
    and the one between she and this man she knew

    she felt confused
    fearing that this man she knew
    might subconsciously became a replacement
    for the recovery of the breakup with her ex
    who still kept contact with her as casual friends

    she did not want to lose the second man
    yet she felt if she told him that she
    needed time to recover from her breakup with ex
    this man might gave her up and moved on
    she knew this was a selfish thought

    at the same time
    the woman did not want
    to hurt the second man
    who used to be her best friend

    she also was afraid
    that the second man might not
    be able to fully accept her in the future
    as she had a physical birth defect
    whose pain she hardly spoke about
    which she suffered in silence

    in the end
    she did something she would not forget:
    she gave the second man up

    this decision she made
    not only hurt this man
    but it hurt herself as well

    the woman did this
    only because she wanted to be fair to the second man
    she felt he deserved a "better" partner

    till this day
    she had never been able
    to tell the truth to the second man

    and the truth is,
    the reason why she had chosen to give him up in the beginning
    was because she just wanted him
    to be really happy with the one
    who would deserve his love better
    with someone who would truly make him happy

    it was a painful thing for her to do
    but the happy memories they once had
    would forever stay in her heart

    ( from e-mails )
