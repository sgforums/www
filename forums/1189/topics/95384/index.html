My heart is in flight

  • InnoHippo
    27 Sep 04, 13:41
    Just to share a nice read from the internet

    =====================================

    I open my eyes to see I am alone,

    The mirror tells me I am old and grown,

    Yet in my heart what do I see?

    A soul that has yet, to be set free.

    I remember many years ago,

    The reason why I am alone.

    I sat and looked at a door once closed,

    So much I didn't see the new one exposed.

    I recall when my heart was awake,

    And I closed it down for it's sake.

    Little did I know, little did I try,

    Now it's my heart that let's out this cry,

    Open your door let me in,

    I have a life I want to begin.

    I want to give, not to take,

    I am your heart, I need this break.

    That day I opened my door,

    Now I have love and so much more.

    I see everything sunny and bright,

    For now my heart is in flight.
