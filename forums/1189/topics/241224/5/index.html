    Now, from how I see it, here is the paradox: for the sake of his/her happiness and security, one would do many things to ensure his/her partner gets that. It may even go to such an extreme that it requires a leg, arm, eyes, liver or even a life.

    Then when one gladly gives such sacrifice, with or without the partner's acknowledgement, the partner will feel hurt and asked, "What happened?" in the case of his/her acknowledgement. Without his/her acknowledgement, aka one left quietly, he/she will ask, "Where did he/she go? Why he/she left without a word?".

    In both cases, leaving quietly or letting him/her know, one will still feel hurt and sad BECAUSE he/she loves the person. Love comes in a package: sweet, cuddly, fragrant, fuzzy, bitter, tough, hurting, etc, etc. You get what I mean.

    Unless the couple has something to hold on to or have faith in a common thing, ie love for each other, then will they overcome the many obstacles they will face together and grow stronger in their relationship. Its also a testament of how deep and strong their love for each other is.
