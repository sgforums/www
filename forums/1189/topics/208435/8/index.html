    we just came back from ABC.
    went straight for the claypot rice,satay and bbq chicken wings.
    surprisingly they were all the same stall we had before.

    the claypot rice :

    $10.00 for 2. still as delicious and filed with soft and wholsome chunks of chicken, loads of lup chiong ( normal and liver ones ), waxed sam-zham bah, and just enough salted fish. Simply umami. but the rice like lesser this time. Quality is still there thou.

    the satay was great! Fog shu said it was one of the better ones we had and i couldn't agree more

    give the wings a miss thou, they were a little undercook and the skin was crispy to being with.

    All in all, a terrific makan experience for both of us. and ABC makan has indeed transform into more spacious and well ventilated makan place
