Wenger hails ruthless Gunners

  • turbo_drift
    23 Sep 07, 16:42
    Arsenal have scored 15 goals in six league games

    Arsene Wenger has pinpointed Arsenal's cutting edge as the defining factor that has led them to an unbeaten start to the season.

    Last campaign The Gunners were guilty of creating countless chances but failing to reward them with the finish they so often deserved.

    But with 15 goals in six games, and players chipping in from all over the pitch, Arsenal seem to have founding their ruthlessness in front of goal.

    Belief

    Speaking after the 5-0 thumping of Derby County, the Frenchman hailed his side's quality.

    "You can see that there is something more in there that is called ruthless and belief. I believe in the team," he said.

    Wenger also praised the manner at which his men focused on the game following their midweek UEFA Champions League victory over Sevilla.

    And also spoke of his delight at his side's confidence to express themselves on the pitch.

    Exceptional

    "It was very enjoyable. We kept our focus following the Champions League game - we were serious from first to last minute," he added.

    "Overall we were lucky with some of the goals, but it was a good team performance.

    "One thing that keeps me very happy is it is enjoyable to watch the team, the togetherness, the kind of football they enjoy to play together - the life coming out of the team is exceptional."
