Walcott: We will get it right

  • felirox
    19 Mar 08, 01:42

    Theo Walcott admits Arsenal will need to sharpen up their shooting if they are to win The Premier League this season.

    Arsenal have been in patchy form of late with only four draws out of their last four league games.

    These dropped points have allowed Manchester United to overtake them in table, yet Walcott insists their troubles in front of goal will soon be eradicated.

    “We have been playing some brilliant football but just cannot get that penetration to finish it.

    “But hopefully if we keep on doing the right things then we will get the results we want.

    “The finishing is the main issue at the moment but we just have to take the positives out of games, and continue to train hard,” said the 19-year-old.

    With the Red Devils and Chelsea closely challenging, The Gunners will have to capitalise on any slip-ups by their rivals but Walcott believes Arsenal must focus on their own plight.

    “We have just got to concentrate on ourselves now, take each game as it comes and not worry about anyone else.”
