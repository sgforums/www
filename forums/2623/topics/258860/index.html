Atletico eye Gunners youngster

  • turbo_drift
    4 Jun 07, 20:08


    Atletico Madrid are reportedly looking into taking Arsenal midfielder Fran Merida back to Spain on loan next season.

    Merida joined The Gunners last summer after opting to leave Barcelona and signed his first professional contract at Emirates Stadium in March.

    Arsene Wenger is often willing to let Arsenal's top youngsters go out on loan to gain vital experience in preparation for a career in the Premiership.

    According to Spanish paper Sport, Wenger met with Atletico officials recently to discuss the possibility of Merida moving to Madrid.

    Arsenal are expected to make a decision on whether to let the 17-year-old go out on loan to the Vicente Calderon sometime this month.
