Reyes focused on Real

  • turbo_drift
    6 Jun 07, 12:54
    Jose Antonio Reyes has reiterated his desire to stay in Spain, but is adamant he will only think about his future at the end of the season.

    Reyes is currently on a season-long loan at Real Madrid but is not looking to return to Arsenal this summer.

    Atletico Madrid have been linked with a move for Reyes but he insists he is fully focused on Real's last two games of the season.

    "I am only thinking about the two games I have left and about winning the league, which is what I joined this team for in the first place.

    "I've said many times that I don't want to return to London, but I won't decide anything regarding my future until the league ends.

    "There are teams interested in me but I don't really want to know too much about the whole issue until the very end because I want to stay focused.

    "My agent's doing his work and I'm doing mine. I only wish I can stay in Spain."

    Robinho, meanwhile, has declared that David Beckham's form has been a major factor in Real's upturn.

    The Brazilian also feels Beckham was England's outstanding player in the 1-1 draw at Wembley last Friday.

    "He is in excellent form and was the best in his team," said Robinho.

    "At Real Madrid he has been playing at this level so for that reason it did not surprise me as much, but he is a very important piece to winning the league."
