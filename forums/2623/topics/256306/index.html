Togo lift Adebayor suspension

  • turbo_drift
    25 May 07, 08:32
    Togo's football federation has lifted its suspension of Arsenal striker Emmanuel Adebayor.

    The TFT suspended Adebayor and two other players in March following a row over unpaid World Cup bonuses.

    Adebayor, forward Mohamed Kader Coubadja and defender Nibombe Dare claim payments are outstanding after they helped Togo qualify for their first World Cup finals last summer.

    The federation said in a statement that it had reinstated the trio 'due to demand from the population, the sporting public and the senior sporting authorities of the country'.

    Adebayor revealed recently that he was considering his international career after reportedly receiving death threats for his reluctance to commit to the team's African Cup of Nations qualifiers.
