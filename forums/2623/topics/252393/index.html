    Arsenal's Tomas Rosicky is unsure if he will be fit to face Chelsea on Sunday

    The Czech Republic international sat out last weekend's win over Fulham with a hamstring injury.

    Rosicky is still struggling with the strain and is unsure if he can prove his fitness before Sunday's clash at Emirates Stadium.

    "Personally, I hope I'll return soon," he told his personal website. "But if it is for Sunday's derby, I cannot say if I'll be fit.

    "There are still a few days left in which I'll step up my training and then we will see if I am ready to return to action.

    "For now I can only reveal that starting on Sunday is very uncertain. The good news is that I have started with running in the last few days.

    "However, they have just been light trainings in which I've had no problems. Only a sharp session can show if I'm ready.

    "Of course I'd like to play against Chelsea and not only because I would meet Petr Cech.

    "Liverpool are one point ahead of us but we want to erase their lead with two games left."
