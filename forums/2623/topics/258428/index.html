Reyes targets Spanish stay

  • turbo_drift
    2 Jun 07, 18:33


    Arsenal misfit Jose Antonio Reyes is desperate to secure a permanent return to Spain at the end of his season-long loan at Real Madrid.

    The 23-year-old former Sevilla forward has struggled to make his mark at the Bernabeu this term and looks unlikely to remain with Real beyond the summer.

    However, Reyes is equally certain that he is not returning to England, leaving him looking around for another Primera Liga club.

    He said: "I am only thinking about the two games I have left and about winning the league, which is what I joined this team for in the first place.

    "I've said many times that I don't want to return to London, but I won't decide anything regarding my future until the league ends.

    "There are teams interested in me, but I don't really want to know much about the whole issue until the very end because I want to stay focused.

    "My agent's doing his work and I'm doing mine. I only wish I can stay in Spain."
