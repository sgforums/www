Arsenal to face full-strength Seville

  • felirox
    18 Sep 07, 00:35
    Seville coach Juande Ramos has a full-strength squad to chose from ahead of their Champions League opener against Arsenal.

    In their first league match since the tragic death of full-back Antonio Puerta, Sevilla beat Recreativo Huerta 4-1 with two goals each from strikers Frederic Kanoute and Alexander Kerzhakov.

    But more pleasing for Ramos was his team's clean bill of health ahead of the trip to the Emirates.

    "We are going away satisfied because we have picked up three points and not had any injuries," he said.

    "Arsenal know that Sevilla are a good team and they will respect us, the same as Sevilla respect them. In Europe, every game is tough, which is why my prediction is of a very close game," he added.

    Arsenal will go into the game full of confidence after a 3-1 derby win in the Premier League away at rivals Tottenham.
