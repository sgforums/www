    Arsene Wenger has committed his future to Arsenal.

    He told the News of the World: "I have a contract for one more year here and I will complete that contract.

    "I have never broken a contract in my entire career and I will not break a contract now. So I can tell you categorically that I will be at the club next season.

    "And next summer I will sit down with the board and see."

    And he insists he is convinced Arsenal can again be a force in the Premiership this term - despite a desperately disappointing effort last time out.

    Wenger stressed: "There's absolutely no need to panic. We finished fourth in the table - level on points with Liverpool - and we have a very young side which, I believe, can improve again this season."
