Ljungberg upbeat for Gunners

  • turbo_drift
    7 Mar 07, 21:27
    Freddie Ljungberg is confident Arsenal can see off PSV Eindhoven and reach the UEFA Champions League quarter-finals.

    The Gunners lost 1-0 in Holland in the first leg a fortnight ago, with Edison Mendez's low shot proving the difference.

    The second meeting is at Emirates Stadium on Wednesday night and Arsenal hope to have captain Thierry Henry available for the game.

    His return would boost the club's prospects of progressing and Ljungberg is upbeat about the team's chances, despite being wary of PSV's threat on the break.

    "It's too bad we conceded a goal there," Ljungberg told Expressen.

    "It makes it a bit harder for us here at Emirates.

    "But I believe we will go through.

    "They will probably defend quite deeply and hope for counter attacks."
