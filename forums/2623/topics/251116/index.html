Rosicky admits injury annoyance

  • turbo_drift
    28 Apr 07, 12:02
    Arsenal star Tomas Rosicky admits he is annoyed at his recent spate of injuries.

    Rosicky and Freddie Ljungberg have both picked up hamstring injuries which will mean neither are available for the Super Sunday clash with Fulham.

    "Rosicky and Ljungberg have hamstring injuries," said Wenger.

    "Rosicky might be available for Chelsea. I don't think Freddie will be.

    "It is a recurrence, so you have to be a bit more careful. It is not a big [problem]. But he has pain where he had it before so he will be out for a few games."

    Rosicky admits he is becoming increasingly agitated with his injuries.

    "It is nothing serious as it has been always in my case. I will not be able to play for a week or ten days. It is hard, I have never been so much injured like in this season," Rosicky told CTK..

    "It is annoying. Every time I got into good shape and felt good, some injury slowed me down."
