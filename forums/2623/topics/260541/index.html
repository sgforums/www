Spaniards linked with Senderos

  • turbo_drift

    Real Zaragoza are reportedly showing an interest in Arsenal defender Philippe Senderos.

    The Swiss international is likely to spend next season as a backup at North London with Kolo Toure and William Gallas occupying the two central defensive roles in Arsene Wenger's starting XI.

    La Liga club Zaragoza are believed to be pondering a move for the 22-year-old as they look to strengthen their squad for a European push next season.

    Sporting director Miguel Pardeza has told The Mirror, though, that: "Nothing is decided yet."

    Senderos's club and international team-mate Johan Djourou recently admitted he was considering leaving the club on loan next season, and The Gunners are unlikely to let both of their Swiss defenders depart the club in the summer.
