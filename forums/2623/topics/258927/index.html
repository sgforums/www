    The votes are in and the counting has taken place. Now it is just a matter of revealing the winners. As usual the results of the Arsenal.com Player of the Year poll will be made known over a matter of weeks, in reverse order, with each of the top four receiving a week of dedicated content.

    Arsenal.com editor Richard Clarke kicks off this process with the player who finished fourth with five per cent of the vote.

    For all sorts of reasons, it is a real pleasure to announce that Gael Clichy has finished fourth in the Arsenal.com Player of the Year poll.
