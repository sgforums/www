Vieira: Wenger may leave

  • turbo_drift
    2 May 07, 20:06
    Patrick Vieira feels David Dein's departure from Arsenal could lead to Arsene Wenger leaving Emirates Stadium.

    Dein left his post as vice-chairman of The Gunners last month after a row with the board, reportedly regarding the input of American billionaire Stan Kroenke.

    His exit prompted speculation that Wenger could also be on his way due to the pair's close working relationship in North London.

    And former Arsenal captain Vieira concedes the situation could eventually lead to his old manager vacating his own position.

    "It was a big shock when I found out, I didn't really believe it," Vieira told the website.

    "At the start, Dein leaving Arsenal is really difficult to believe. I think he has been there quite a long time and just the work he has done for the club has been fantastic, so it's really difficult to understand how he left the club so quick."

    When asked if Wenger would also leave, Vieira replied: "At the long term, maybe yes. I really believe they have been close together.

    "They started the job together and I don't see Arsene finishing the job by himself, but you never know.

    "I really believe that Arsenal still has some work to do at the club, so now he will finish by himself, or he will leave because David Dein left."
