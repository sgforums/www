Wenger focused on title

    Arsene Wenger says that winning the Premier League is Arsenal's main challenge this season.

    The Frenchman insists that the domestic title is more important than success in the UEFA Champions League.

    Wenger is less than convinced by the credibility of the Champions League, claiming the Premier League is harder to achieve.

    Over-rated

    He said: "Is that bigger than the championship? I am not sure. How many games do you need to win it?

    "It's a cup game and is not a championship. The team who wins over the 38 games is the best team. Was AC Milan the best team in Europe last year? I am not convinced.

    "It becomes so difficult in the championship and a lot of teams do not turn up to the Champions League."

    Achievements

    Wenger's decade in charge at Arsenal has seen them win both the Premier League title and FA Cup, but he would love to add the Champions League to his achievements.

    He said: "I want this club to win the Champions League, but for my personal ego it is less important.

    "I was interested in it 10 years ago, but a lot less now. You want to win it for different reasons.

    "I feel a manager should be judged more on his overall career than just winning one trophy."
