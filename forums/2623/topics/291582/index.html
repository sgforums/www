Ancelotti questions Gunners

  • turbo_drift
    15 Nov 07, 08:08
    Milan boss Carlo Ancelotti doubts whether Arsenal's young squad has the experience to succeed in this season's UEFA Champions League.

    The Gunners have hit the ground running in their domestic campaign and currently top the Premier League with an unbeaten record.

    Arsene Wenger's men have also secured qualification to the knock-out stages of the Champions League with two games to spare.

    However, Ancelotti is unsure if the 2005/2006 runners-up can cut it in Europe's elite competition.

    Crucial

    "Arsenal play well and they are getting excellent results, but the Champions League is decided in spring," he told Gazzetta dello Sport.

    "Arsenal have lots of young, enthusiastic players but experience was crucial when the competition headed into its latter stages.

    "It's necessary to know how to handle the pressure and tension in the best way. I don't know if they are trained for this."
