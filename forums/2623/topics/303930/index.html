Russian denies Arsenal deal

  • turbo_drift
    19 Jan 08, 22:15
    Vyacheslav Dmitriev has denied that he has agreed a deal with Arsenal.

    The 17-year-old is rated as one of the best young defenders in Europe and has also attracted interest from Juventus.

    However, both Dmitriev and his club Torpedo Moscow have played down claims that a transfer in the offing.

    "I haven't signed a contract with Arsenal as some newspapers have wrote," said Dmitriev, who has been nicknamed the Elephant, in the Russian media.

    "If the interest is true, such a deal would only be possible anyway if the clubs have negotiated."

    No deal

    Reports claim that Arsenal have agreed a deal with Torpedo to take Dmitriev in the summer - but their president Alexandr Tukmatov has denied this.

    "Nobody from England has contacted us enquiring about Dmitriev," he said.

    Dmitriev's agent Alexei Skoroed admitted there was previously an interest from Arsenal, but it was from their youth department.

    "At the moment we are talking about a prior interest from Arsenal," he said.

    "I got information from one European agent, but there have not been anymore phone calls from England at the moment.

    "Their interest was from the junior side.

    "Earlier Italians were also interested in signing him, but he got injured and only came back last October."
