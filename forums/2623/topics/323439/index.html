Hleb & Ade To Decide Arshavin's Future

    The future of Zenit St. Petersburg star Andrei Arshavin will depend on whether Emmanuel Adebayor and Aliaksandr Hleb are at Arsenal next season, according to his agent…

    Arsenal completes this curious transfer triangle, as Arsene Wenger has been quite vocal with both his admiration and criticism of the 27-year-old, which many believe is a deliberate tactic.

    The Gunners could potentially lose both Hleb and Adebayor to Barca this summer, meaning the Blaugrana’s interest in Arshavin would dissipate and Wenger’s would increase significantly.

    And the player’s agent, Dennis Lacheter, said: “The push for Andrei Arshavin has only just got going.

    “The clubs who are following him are only just warming their engines.

    “Arshavin’s future depends on what happens with Hleb and Adebayor.”
