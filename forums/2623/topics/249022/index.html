Gilberto: Cesc has great future

  • turbo_drift
    17 Apr 07, 18:36
    Arsenal vice-captain Gilberto says Cesc Fabregas can go on to become an all-time great.

    The 19-year-old Spain midfielder has been a consistent performer in a disappointing season for The Gunners.

    His performances have resulted in him been shortlisted for the PFA Players' Player and the PFA Young Player award.

    Gilberto has played alongside the youngster for most of the campaign and believes the midfielder will only get better.

    "Cesc can be one of the best in his position. It is just down to him to continue the way he is playing and try to learn more," said the Brazilian World Cup winner.

    "Sometimes you need to play more with your brain than with your power, to read the game all of the time.

    "But if he can learn that, quickly, then he can be one of the best.

    "I speak to him, especially on the pitch when we play together. I try to position him in the right place. When you are young, you want to run more than position yourself in the right place.

    "Cesc is learning. He is young, it takes time. Next season he can develop himself more than he has done."
