Henry eyes Euro glory

  • turbo_drift
    19 Feb 07, 21:25
    Thierry Henry thinks it would be amazing if Arsenal could go one better this season and win the UEFA Champions League.

    The Gunners made it all the way to last season's final in Paris before going down 2-1 to Barcelona in an epic encounter.

    Henry and co. tackle PSV Eindhoven on Tuesday and the French ace wants this season to be the first time Arsenal collect the coveted trophy.

    "It would be amazing if we won the trophy because I've never won it and the club has never won it," Henry said on Sky Sports News. "So it will be a great feeling."

    Meanwhile, the former Juventus and Monaco ace believes Arsenal are beginning to reap the rewards of blooding a number of youngsters last August.

    The Gunners began the Premiership season slowly - securing just two points from their opening three games - and Henry thinks the side have answered their critics.

    "We started the season poorly, but we had a lot of young kids in the team. Now they are maturing so they're improving and now you can see the team we can be," he added.

    "We got a lot of stick by a lot of people who said we did not have a lot of character and when we're 1-0 down we often don't come back and win or even come into the game.

    "But we've shown that even with 10 men that we can win away and come back into the game and have a late finish too. So I think it's a plus for the end of the season."
