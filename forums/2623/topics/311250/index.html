Bobby not interested in Spurs offer

  • felirox

    Former Arsenal winger Robért Píres has said that he is not interested in playing for Tottenham should the London club make an offer for him.

    Píres' contract with Villareal is up at the end of the season.

    There have been rumours that Spurs would attempt an approach for the Frenchman, but Píres categorically said that he was not interested in such a thing.

    Píres said: "Is it a joke? They must have forgotten where I come from.

    "They can make the offer, but I won't play for Tottenham. Seeing myself wearing their shirt would be weird, after the six years I spent at Arsenal.

    "If they are interested that's encouraging regarding my age, they can make the offer.

    "I understand Villarreal's position because the end of the season is fantastic to experience because we are third and my situation is secondary.

    "I don't worry and I think they'll propose me something because they haven't told me that they don't want to continue, unlike Pascal Cygan, who has been told he'll not stay.

    "There are priorities in life and I'm relaxed, waiting for them to tell me good news. I'm just trying to play well until the season ends and we'll discuss at some point.

    "I can sign wherever I want right now and of course I have been contacted (by other clubs), but Villarreal remain my priority and I'll take a decision when I'll know what they want to do.

    "I don't forget the financial effort they made when they signed me and the fact they waited for me to return from a seven-month absence due to injury."
