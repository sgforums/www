    Lawrie Sanchez has said he was wrong to accuse Arsenal players of simulation during his Fulham side's 2-1 away defeat on Sunday.

    Sanchez said Arsenal players went to ground too easily on three occasions but after reviewing the incidents he said: "I have to hold my hand up and say I was probably wrong to a certain extent.

    "You sometimes shoot from the hip and in that instance perhaps I should have waited."

    Opposite number Arsene Wenger had said: "I saw only one real dive and that was not from an Arsenal player, it was from Steven Davis."
