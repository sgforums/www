Clichy - Villa will drop points

  • turbo_drift

    Arsenal defender Gael Clichy has insisted his team-mates are not worried by the threat of Aston Villa.

    The Gunners currently find themselves fifth in the Premier League table, three points behind Martin O'Neill's Villa.

    French full-back Clichy, though, is confident Arsenal can usurp their top-flight rivals, so long as they focus on their own game.

    "We are not worried," he said. "We made mistakes and we have to carry on now, try to win all of our games.

    "It is just down to us to be better in the next game; we just have to carry on working."

    Of Villa, he added: "Of course they are a good side, but we are sure they are going to drop points.

    "We are going to fight until the end because we have the quality to do something this season.

    "We just have to stay focused game after game."
