Henry: We need more width

  • turbo_drift
    21 May 07, 17:49
    Thierry Henry has said that his team need more width in order to break down teams who come to the Emirates Stadium and for 90 minutes.

    The Gunners have had difficulty scoring goals last season and many blamed the width of the Emirates pitch, which is one of the largest in the Premiership.

    However, the Arsenal captain acknowledged that the team didn't have this problem at Highbury.

    Henry said: "It's a paradox. The Highbury pitch was smaller but we were finding more space.

    "Now nobody wants to stay on the wing to stretch a team and that makes a great difference."
