    Spurs Target Makoun Wants Champs League




    Reports from France suggest that Lille's Jean Makoun could snub Tottenham Hotspur in his pursuit of Champions League football.

    Makoun is almost certain to leave and has been hotly tipped to join Juande Ramos' Spurs revolution, but Arsenal and Juventus are reportedly waiting to hijack the deal.

    In fact, sources suggest that Spurs have already made an initial offer to Lille for the Cameroon international. However, the London side can only offer Uefa Cup football, despite their widely accepted potential.

    Furthermore, Juve are said to be very interested in the midfielder, while Arsenal, too, have him on low priority as they seek to replace the departed Mathieu Flamini.

    Ligue 1 champions Lyon have also been tipped to enter the race, but Makoun could look for a fresh challenge outside Ligue 1.
