    Manchester United star Cristiano Ronaldo has confessed that he came close to joining Arsenal before moving to Old Trafford.

    Ronaldo signed for United in a £12million move from Sporting Lisbon in 2003, but the Portugal international was 'within a whisker' of a move to North London instead.

    The recently-crowned 2008 Ballon d'Or winner met Arsene Wenger on three occasions and he admits he holds a great deal of respect for the Gunners boss.

    "I came within a whisker of doing it," Ronaldo told France Football when asked about rumours that he was close to agreeing a deal at Arsenal five years ago.

    "Wenger is someone I respect a lot. He is very intelligent."

    Jorge Mendes, Ronaldo's agent, added: "That is what happened. Everyone wanted Cristiano; Real (Madrid), Juventus, Barcelona.

    "But only Arsenal and Manchester United attempted to take him from there (Sporting Lisbon) that season. The others wanted to leave him at Sporting at least a year."
