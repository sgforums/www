Arsenal face Eboue injury lay-off

  • turbo_drift

    Arsenal could be without Emmanuel Eboue for several weeks after he was stretchered off with a knee ligament injury in the 1-0 win over Chelsea.

    Eboue faces a spell on the sidelines after he came off worse in a 69th-minute collision with Joe Cole.

    "It's a medial ligament injury," said Arsenal boss Arsene Wenger. "We will see in the next 48 hours but it looks as if he will be out for a while."

    Eboue is set to miss Tuesday's Carling Cup quarter-final at Blackburn.
