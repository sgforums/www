    ARSENAL are rumoured to be considering a move for Bolton Wanderers goalkeeper Jussi Jaaskelainen.

    The Finnish goalkeeper has not agreed a new deal at the Reebok Stadium and has previously hinted at leaving his contract expire, which would allow him to join another Premiership club next summer.

    Gunners boss Arsene Wenger, however, is rumoured to be ready to test Bolton's resolve with a bid during the January transfer window with Jens Lehmann likely to exit the Premiership leaders.

    Bolton boss Gary Megson will be looking to hold onto the goalkeeper, but faces a battle on his hands.
