Adebayor dulls title talk

  • felirox
    18 Sep 07, 00:29


    Emmanuel Adebayor is concentrating on maintaining Arsenal's form rather than thinking about silverware come the end of the season.

    The Gunners were not expected to mount a serious challenge for the Premier League title, but Saturday's win over Spurs sees them top the table.

    Although some pundits are tipping Arsenal to go all the way this term, Adebayor is simply looking for Arsene Wenger's troops to maintain their form and see where they end up.

    "We are very happy and we are very confident at the moment, but it is now about keeping it going," he told the club's official website.

    "Every game we play they call it a test. We feel that as well, but the most important thing is taking it one game at a time.

    "We are showing maturity now, every player knows how to play for 90 minutes. But we don't want to speak about the title, we just want to keep on going.

    "We are learning and we are playing good football. If we carry on like that we'll see where we are at the end of the season."
