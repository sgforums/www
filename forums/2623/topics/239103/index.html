    Le Havre goalkeeper Steve Mandanda has played down reports linking him with a move to Arsenal.

    Arsene Wenger is reported to have kept an eye on the Le Havre number one who first shot to prominence during the French team's Under 21 European Championship campaign.

    Rangers and Marseille have also registered an interest in the African-born Mandanda.

    However the player himself admits he is none the wiser as to the speculation concerning his future.

    "I have heard the rumours but frankly no one has contacted me," said Mandanda.
