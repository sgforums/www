Diarra happy with Emirates debut

  • felirox
    26 Sep 07, 23:00


    Arsenal's 11th-hour signing Lassana Diarra has expressed his delight after having made his first start at Emirates Stadium on Tuesday.

    22-year-old Diarra joined The Gunners from Chelsea in a bid to try to play more regularly after failing to impose himself at Stamford Bridge.

    The France international was started for the first time by Arsene Wenger in the 2-0 Carling Cup win against Newcastle and impressed with his Claude Makelele-style football.

    "I am not at 100 per cent of my potential yet," he told AFP.

    "Everything was fine on the collective and individual aspects.

    "I am happy with my first performance as a starter. I have rhythm problems but I will be okay next month.

    "I have finished now with Chelsea and I am totally focused on Arsenal. I still speak to Chelsea players on the phone but it is the past for me.

    "I am happy at Arsenal, where I have a lot of fun. I hope it will continue. "
