Gervinho eyes summer exit

  • turbo_drift
    21 Jan 09, 22:15

    Gervinho is eyeing a summer move away from Le Mans, with the Premier League his favoured destination.

    The highly-rated forward, who has netted five goals so far this term, has regularly been linked with North London rivals Arsenal and Tottenham Hotspur.

    Gervinho, 21, has made no secret of his desire to play in England's top flight, and is eyeing a switch at the end of the season.

    He told skysports.com: "My club have not received an approach for me, and I don't think I can leave in January.

    "For me, the ideal time to move would be in the summer. I have a contract with Le Mans and am happy here at the moment."
