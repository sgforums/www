    how much you should bring depends on what you can afford.  if you want to stay in hotel it is not cheap, try hostel or guesthouse (ryokan). 4 days is not a lot of time to try different accommodation and waste time checking in and out.

    from tokyo to osaka/kyoto you have to go by train unless you can afford to fly. try arranging to fly to tokyo and return from osaka or vice versa. don't buy rail pass for single journey.

    everything is expensive in japan, so plan well if you are on budget.
