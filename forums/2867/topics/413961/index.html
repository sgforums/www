    Its a very expensive country given with the rise in YEN. I am a sucker for japan so everything for me over there is nice. Food, people, fashion, weather, transport... etc.  For tourist, it might not be very convenient for you if you do not understand japanese and travel alone if you are outside tokyo as most of them do not speak english. I would recommend you do a research on your itinerary before you go. Try to go those "onsen" if u have a chance, u will not regret. Tokyo and kyoto do not speak the same japanese dialect, fyi, but I do not think its a concern for you if u do not speak japanese at all :)

    Take note to find out the time when the last train leaves from the station cos if you miss the last train, most likely u have to sleep on the streets. So plan well. And avoid taking the train in the morning during starting of the office hours in tokyo city area cos u might squeeze to death hehe. Hope you have fun there! :)
