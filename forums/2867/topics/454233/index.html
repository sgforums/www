    Be prepared for a very expensive trip for Norway.

    For starters, you can travel to both Bergen and Oslo, save some cost by booking the train ride and get mini-pris tix. Take a detour to enjoy the scenary at Flam.

    Take a coastal cruise on the Hurtigruten to visit many fjords and coastal cities.

    Or simply fly North to Tromso and find local guided tour packages for  riding dog-sleds, snow shoe trekking (only when there's snow) or go Aurora-chasing.

    Everything WILL be expensive by our standards but 5k should be able to cover a week's worth if you know how to scrimp and save. You have been warned.
