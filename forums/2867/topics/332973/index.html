    1. Have cash, & different currencies in your wallet.  I trust you are changing flights in Japan; so try to have minimum 1,000 Yen & another S$30/- ready.  they are for when you are hungry or thirsty during the stopover.

    2. Have medications clearly available in your carry on backpack,

       2A) and a muscle relaxant for you to sleep thru the 2nd lag of your flight so your body can adjust to the time difference in USA quickly.  Sleep as much as you can.

      2B) Stuff a small water bottle in that side pocket of the backpack so you can use it to take drinking water at airports & parks. A lightweight torchlight in there is also good.

       2C) have at least 1 big roll of blanket/towel at easy reach of your backpack so they can be used to support your back when you sleep or can use to prop your head during the flight.

       2D) have also at least one set of your colthing rolled into your backpack; in case the luggage was lost.

       2E) Have a change of underwear there with small bottles of talcum powder, face wash, face towels, comb & wet wipes.

    3) In a luggage, try to get one that is about 30 inch big and with about it about half empty for stuffs you might buy and bring back to sg.  The army market kit bag is also good.

     3A)  You need about 4 set of clothings, One you are already wearing with socks and preferably crocs (fake also can so you wont need to bring it home). a pair of sports shoes & a pair of open toe slippers for wearing with socks into the toliet/bath.  Forget about belts if possible.

  • viciouskitty74
    8 Oct 08, 03:23

    3B) In that luggage, bring at least a bottle of anti dandruff shampoo.  They are good for when your body readjust to different temperature and humidity.

    3C) for coldwear, you can rely on a simple windbreaker & thick socks for when you sleep.

    3D) Bring swimwear and a beach towel & at least a swim goggles.  the swimming pools there use a lot of chorine.

    3E) if there is a function, you should bring along some formal wear, like decent shoes/heels, your school pants/skirts, shirts/blouse & jacket.

    8 Oct 08, 03:30

    these are for your considerations:

    1. buy anti static wrist bands at the airport.  Cos flights will give you static shocks & the weather in LA is dry and gives static.

    2. Bring a fanny pack along with the backpack.  The fanny bag lets you keeps stuffs yet wont get in the way much when you are taking rides.  Make sure the packs has ready to use lipbalms, water bottles, tissues, wet wipes, sunblock & even vaseline tube (vaseline tube can find in Mustafa Centre, not jar ones.)

    3. Bring extra sets of contacts and your glasses if you need them.

    4. Dont bring your handphone, the power sockets there are not suitable.

    5. Have a jockey cap ready at hand, so you can cap down during flight & sleeping and it helps to prevent the sun from burning your eyes.  A decent sunglass helps too.

    6. leave most of your acne treatment in sg, they might not work in LA or is too dry for you.  Check with your doc for skincare that is richer for use in LA or you can simply use the vaseline gel & sunblock.
