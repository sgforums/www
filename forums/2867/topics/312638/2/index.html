    Frankly, i still think tai zi area requires quite a bit walk from the main heart of activities.  Prince edward is one stop after mongkok.  You want to stay somewhere between tau ma tei and mongkok.

    Tell you how convenient is Dorsett seaview.  I walk to mongkok and look for my dinner there, usually the rickshaw noodles or guo qiao mian xian at women's street, whatever it is as long as there is a cup of hot milk tea.  Then go to xian da centre and buying the latest handphone at cheapest price, grab a shirt at broadway shopping centre, etc. Walk back to Dorsett seaview hotel, put my stuffs in the room and head towards tsimshatsui.  You will pass thru a lane lined with sex toys vendors and a street adjacent lined with fortune tellers.  You head toward jordan and walk thru the night market on the way to tsimshatsui.  you can actually walk all the way to xing guang da dao, while you are there, you will see a YMCA and wonder why you didn't stay there with spectacular view overlooking central.

    Anyway, first time just trial and err lah.  Next time you know what to do.
