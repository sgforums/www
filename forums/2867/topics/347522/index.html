    And like always, here's the following to pack for any given trip that is more then 4 days:

     

    1) anti-dandruff shampoo, conditioner, hair gel/serum & a comb

    2) facewash, toner & moisturisers

    3) Bodywash, lotion & deodarant stick with a wash cloth/puff

    4) Toothbrush, toothpaste, floss & mouthwash

    5) 4 pieces of sleep singlets & boxers. (Or whatever rock your sleep.)

    6) 4 pieces of polo shirts/t-shirts (One to wear on body, the rest in bag.)

    7) 4 pieces of Jeans/bermudas (One to wear on body, the rest in bag.)

    8) 4 pairs of socks. (One to wear on body, the rest in bag.)

    9) One backpack that holds your laptop.

    10) A waist pouch to maybe hold your barang barangs like cameras and so on when you are there.

    11) 4 sets of undergarments for day, and 4 for nights (Of if you wear boxers, ignore the 2nd part of this.)

    12) a sunblock like Neutrogena dry touch & at least a lip balm with spf15

    13) A pair of crocs/slippers & a pair of sports/hiking shoes

    14) A tube of Redoxon fizzy orange vitamin c.  Throw one tablet into your orange juice every morning should keep you healthy for the duration of the trip.

    15) your mp3 or walkman loaded with hokkien, malay or cantonese songs (the feeling of listening to asian music in a angmoh environment will rock you. Serious.)

    16) a real neck pillow, so you can nap on a plane, a bus or just on a bed with unfamiliar smells. (Dont get hte inflatable one, they say it may pop in mid air, and you dont want it to pop near your ears where all pillows will be at.)

    17) a beach towel and a normal towel.  Also throw in a small face towel in a ziplock bag for easier access in the plane or just when you are outdoors.

    18) Some wet tissues and plenty of dry tissue packs.

    19) a empty waterbottle, (if you can get a filter for it, by all means get it so you can easily filter water from water taps around the country.)

    20) a deep heating rub.  It really is useful for when you wake up from a strange bed, and aching all over.  also good for massaging your feet after a long day sightseeing.

    2) Small Comb

    3) Black ink pen

    4) Sleeping pills

    5) portable vicks

    6) lip balms

    7) small tube of face moisturiser

    9) 1 pack of dry tissues

    10) 1 pack of ricola sweets

    11) Some airborne tablets/vitamin c fizzy tablets.
