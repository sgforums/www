Beat The Queue with Jetstar's self check-in

  • FireIce
    28 May 15, 13:19

    Convenient and superbly easy-to-use, Jetstar’s new self-service kiosk and bag drop allows passengers travelling on selected Jetstar flights (departing from Changi Airport) to beat the queues, giving you more time to swan around the airport. Best part? You only need 60-seconds — yes we actually timed it — to check yourself in. Simply scan your passport, add bags to booking, print your boarding pass and bag tags, and off you go! This nifty service is truly a godsend for last-minute packers like ourselves (you won’t want to know the number of times we nearly miss our flights due to long check-in queues).

    Those with checked baggage need not go through the counters, too. Just stick your bag tags on, scan your boarding pass, place bags on the designated baggage belt, inject the bag and, voila, you’re all set to tear apart the duty-free stores in the terminal.
