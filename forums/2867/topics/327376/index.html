    You have decide what is your focus of your trip.

    If you like the urban area more, you can stay in Taipei more.

    Or else you can travel southern for more local stuff.

    For Taipei, try to stay near Xi Men Ding and MRT station.

    The MRT station is good enough to bring you to major attraction, followed by the local buses.

    Please remember to bring some one that can READ chinese or else you need to ask a lot.

    The only damper for Taiwan is the weather.

    You have to check the weather prediction before you go.

    Or else you end up stuck in your hotel watching TV.

    Remember to take the lift at the 101 building.

    You have to pay but it is worth it.
