    Miss_Happy,

     

    You are right, change S$50 to rupiah is more than enough. In fact, the things there are not really that cheap, a normal t-shirt costs about S$20-30. Might well as buy in SG haha.

     

    Overall my stay at batam view beach resort is not bad. The scenery (we were given a sea view room) is nice and the room is cosy. The staff are friendly too! But, the only thing was there were not too few ppl (maybe we went at the wrong time?)

     

    Initally, I thought the beach would be filled with ppl playing sea sports or water activities, but it was empty when we went there...really, no ppl at all, very very quiet. The cafe where we had our dinner was also very quiet, only a few people in a big cafe.

     

    Maybe this resort is too isolated from city area, that's why not many ppl stay there. All our activities were confined within the resort. If you like a quiet place to relax or to be with your love ones, then this resort is a recommended choice. If not, I think you should consider other hotels/resorts that are near the city.
