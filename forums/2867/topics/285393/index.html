    15 Oct 07, 09:37
    I just returned from Hong Kong. 4D3N free and easy.
    If anyone intends to go to ladies street, you got to be brave at bargaining.
    My advice, whatever the opening quote, slash the price by more than half.
    My friend got a handbag for HK$90 (opening quote was HK$230)
    If they won't lower the price, just walk away, there will be another store down the street selling the same thing.
    Most of the time, they'll stop you from walking away by giving in to you.
