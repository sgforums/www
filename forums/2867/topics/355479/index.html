    Switzerland
    Nicest country I ever been. Seriously, if you do not have money, save it up for just one trip there. Beats everywhere else in terms of landscape. And you do not have to visit anywhere this whole life. =) You can see snowy mountains everywhere and its like you are in a winter wonderland. =) People are also more friendly, compared to other European countries. They try their best to communicate in English with you. Best time to visit is in December.

    Recommended: Mount Pilatus or Titilis, Interlaken, Zermatt, Berne

    Australia
    Nice holiday resort. People there are generally friendly. Wind is strong and you can see magnificent sea views. Totally unique experience. Beautiful flowers and also you can expect to see some fruit trees. Get to see exotic creatures like Koala Bears, Kangaroos, Wombat and Tasmanian Devils. =)
     
    Recommended Cities: Sunshine Coast, Gold Coast, Outskirts of Sydney, Melbourne, Adelaide.

    Not recommended: Perth (Very "Singaporean")
