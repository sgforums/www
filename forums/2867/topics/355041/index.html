    JY, repeat of PM - also for benefit of rest

    Historical sites - A Famosa fort, Christ church, the art museum, the merdeka memorial, dutch buildings etc.. all in the same area..

    Cultural place - the fri/sat night market at Jonker's street..

    Restaurants:

    1) chicken rice ball - chinese coffee shop cornerlot at the famous clock tower/bridge site, dont know the name.. try the popiah stall stationed outside the shop too..

    2) satay celup - Ban Lee Hsiang, Jln Ong Kim Wee

    3) nyonya food - Makko or Jeta Groves, Melaka Raya, must try the cendol..

    4) mee rebus - a malay stall at Padang Temu

    5) rojak - a stall by Bukit Cina

    6) nyonya kuih - a stall by Taman Nam Yang housing area, Ujong Pasir

    4-6 is difficult to locate, unless there's a local around... [maybe try google map or gps?]
