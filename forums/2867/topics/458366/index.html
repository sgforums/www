    Hi

    I was there in October last year and i had a good time there. The MTR there like Singapore MRT are very good easily accessible to most Hong Kong tourist attractions like Ngong Ping 360, Disneyland and so on. the taxis are good too because there use meter and sometimes better than taking train.

    Tips on public transport in Hong Kong

    1) when you planning to take train, be sure to buy their tourist pass, it is much cheaper

    2) Hong Kong is separated by a waterway connected with a tunnel, don't take taxi through the tunnel cause you will be charge additional HK$60 (i can't really remembr how much) just to cross the tunnel. Better to take the train accross then take taxi.

    3) Take the ferry across the waterway, cheap and fast

    Kowloon is definitely the best place to stay because it is near to most attractions and shopping
