Where to staycation in Singapore this December 2015

  • FireIce
    15 Dec 15, 12:11

    The Amoy

    This gorgeous little 37-room, shophouse-turned-hotel on Telok Ayer has a pretty sweet package. The Amoy Weekend Experience involves a one-night stay in a Chinese heritage-themed room, free set dinner at fine sushi place Jin, breakfast and free access to the mini bar and Wifi—all for $268. 

    Valid until Dec 31 (on Fridays, Saturdays, Sundays and public holidays only). 76 Telok Ayer St., 6580-2888.

    Carlton Hotel

    You’ll be a stone’s through from Orchard Road festivities, and the Christmas Staycation package is a reasonable $268 a night for a deluxe room and includes breakfast, lunch and a festive cocktail at Gravity bar. Valid until Jan 17. 

    76 Bras Basah Rd., 6338-8333.

    The Club

    The gorgeous revamped boutique hotel by the Harry’s Group has a special staycation rate for Christmas (Dec 24-25) and New Year (Dec 31-Jan 1): three days, two nights in The Club room for $860, with $200 F&B credit, breakfast and one day’s free use of the minibar. Don’t forget to visit the sexy basement jazz bar, B28.

    28 Ann Siang Rd., 6808-2188.

    Hotel Vagabond

    This sexy, boudoir-chic new opening in Kampong Glam is doing a 20% discount off their best available price ($198-398), provided you stay over the weekend (Fri, Sat, Sun). Among other perks, you get a complimentary bottle of wine per stay and a later check-out until 3pm.

    Valid until Dec 30. 39 Syed Alwi Rd., 6291-6677.

    Mandarin Orchard

    Located right in the thick of the Christmas light-up action, this hotel offers a Christmas-New Year package that lets you stand a chance to win a free weekend stay in their Presidential suite. Book your staycation here and get a $50 voucher for ME@OUE. Room rates range from $256-570.

    Valid until Feb 8, 2016. 333 Orchard Rd., 6737-4411.

    Pan Pacific

    Get close to the fireworks (and visit the ArtScience Museum and other attractions nearby) with the Pacific Escape package gives you free breakfast, free internet, 15% off spa services and food (except their special festive dinners and brunches). Valid until Feb 28 2016. There’s also the Escape Staycation, which also throws in 20% off dining outlets, late check-out, $40 credit per day and other perks. 

    Valid until Dec 29. 7 Raffles Blvd, 6336-8111.

    Park Hotel Alexandra

    The new hotel next to IKEA is doing the Festive Staycation Package, with a $50 food and beverage credit per room every night. The highlight is Aqua Luna, a poolside bar with 16 swim-up bar seats. Room rates start at $220 per night.

    Valid until Dec 31. 323 Alexandra Rd., 6828 8888.

    Raffles Hotel

    A staycation at the prettiest heritage hotel in town comes with breakfast at Tiffin Room, a bottle of bubbly, a Christmas sock full of treats and a free Singapore Sling. Room rates start from a cool $980.

    Valid until Dec 25. 1 Beach Rd., 6337-1886.

    The Parkroyal on Pickering

    This hotel's SG50 staycation package lasts till the end of the year, so you still have some time to reserve a room here. Prices start from $236 per night, and comes with SGD50 daily in-house credit on the spa purchases, as well as from Lime restaurant, its bar, and the lobby bar.

    The Scarlet

    You’ll be in the heart of the Club Street action. Their Escape with Me package includes use of their Flaunt gym and Soda Jacuzzi, on top of a complimentary breakfast for two. Rates start at $190. Fancier is the Suite Attraction package. Stay at one of their suites and also get free bubbly, use of the minibar, breakfast for two. Suites prices starts from $528.

    Valid until Dec 31. 33 Erskine Rd., 6511-3333.

    The South Beach

    The city’s hot new boutique opening has an opening package, where you stay (a minimum of) three nights and pay for just two at $490 per night, in their Showcase Me (Deluxe) rooms. Not many additional perks, except the all-important
    complimentary WiFi and minibar. 

    Valid until Dec 29. 30 Beach Rd., 6818-1888.
