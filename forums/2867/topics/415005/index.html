My Bangkok Trip

  • kanamoocorb
    9 Nov 10, 22:52

    i having trip to Bangkok with my boyfriend for 4 days and it’s been perfect!! I love Bangkok XD!!

    We stay in Aspen Suites which is recommended by my friend. It is a nice hotel with friendly staffs where they always give a warm greeting and the service was good. The room is big and comfortable enough with the facilities provided.

     After put our stuff and take some rest, we go for lunch in the hotel’s cafe. We ordered some thai food and i like the chicken basil so much but not for my boyfriend bcoz he didn’t like basil leaf hehehe.. The food is not expensive and very tasty ;). But the restaurant quite dark and not much lightning, maybe it is good for dinner. After lunch the hotel send us out to the main road by tuk-tuk and it’s free of charges.

    Our first trip is going to four faces Buddha which is just 5 minutes walk from the hotel located next to Erawan shopping mall. People believe that the four faces Buddha can make our wish come true and a lot of tourists from different places purposely come to Bangkok for this temple. After that we continue to Siam Paragon, Siam Center, and MBK. The best thing is all shopping mall is in walking distance areas.

    The second day we go to Wat Pho and Wat Arun temple in the morning, and we continue to China Town. It is really tiring becoz the china town market is really big hahaha..

    The next day we went to chatuchak weekend market. It is really amazing market, they have almost sell everything from cloths, plant, accessorises, dog, furnish and many others thing. At the afternoon continue to Platinum shopping center, and Pratunam market. This place is really shopping paradise for girls, where the price is extremely cheap, and the fashion is really up to date ;).

     At the last day we check out quite early because our flight is at the morning so after take breakfast, we just take some picture in the hotel and also with some staffs. Thai people are very friendly just that they not really can communicate in English. And i miss thai food a lot especially the chicken bail from the hotel’s cafe.
