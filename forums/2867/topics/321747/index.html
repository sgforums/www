    Did you guys got any confirmation if it's the Executive Class or the Super VIP coach?

    Anyway, from Genting, you can hop on to the yellow Genting Shuttle bus, which departs every 30 mins from the First World Bus Terminal to Pekeliling Bus Hub (Titiwangsa LRT/Monorail Station) in KL. RM5.50 per person.

    Alternatively, head down to the Skyway Cable Car Station (via the Skyway Cable Car of course) and take the red Genting Express buses to either Puduraya (RM3.50), Pasarakyat (RM3.50) or KL Sentral (RM3.30).
