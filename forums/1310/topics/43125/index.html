    My comments:

    a. Man-of-the-match Not a bad idea as it is definitely a fairer assessment of performance than goals scored, but difficult to implement. With everybody supposedly concentrating on the game, who to decide? It should be decided by neutrals anyway...

    b. Top scorer list I personally feel that a top 5 list is sufficient as that's what it's supposed to be... a list of top scorers!!

    c. Score chart Let's not confuse this with the above. We could have a seperate page to show the number of goals scored by each player. Question is... do we also include other stats (i.e. tackles, passes, assists) to ensure that other defensive players are not left out? I personally feel that a chart with only goals scored is not a balanced chart and is far from being fair, while if we were to include other stats, it would be too time-consuming... and we would have to change the website name to Opta.

    d. Motivation With regards to the motivational factor in the game, I would like to state that I have only looked at goals as one of the benchmarks of my performance since I moved into the forward position. However, even if I scored a goal but didn't contribute otherwise, I still would not feel happy with my performance. If I am not playing upfront, my benchmarks would not include the goals I scored and would actually depend on the position I played in (e.g. crosses, tackles, assists, etc). I would be particularly worried if every player in the team actually viewed goals as recognition of their performance.

    To sum up what I have written above, I basically feel that while it would be good to have a seperate page to include indicators of our players' performances, it would be too time-consuming for a social team like ours. So let's move on man....
