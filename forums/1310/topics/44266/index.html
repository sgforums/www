    I was actually deliberating whether to proceed with this posting for hours. I really needed to get this out of my head before I can sit down and concentrate on my studies. Furthermore, with the break, we will have time to assess ourselves and the team and look forward to better performance when we the team regroup again. I have over the months made some observations of our team and players. I have also gathered opinions from others in the team and even from Yean, the shaggy looking guy whom we played against twice. Please do not begrudge me for the comments and hopefully everyone can take them in the right spirit. I may not be the best person to be doing it, as I am definitely not one of the more outstanding players in the team. In fact after writing the following comments, I have come to the conclusion that I am in fact, the weakest player in the team.

    The following comments are made with the understanding that we are playing 4 - 4 - 2 with wingers.

    Russell - There is really not much you can say when the guy is our most dependable striker, scoring 14 goals in 19 games. Lately, he has started to play from a deeper position and that has somehow reduced his scoring rate. Excellent during one-on-one situations and beats the off - side trap well. Still lacks the ability to take shots earlier.
    Personal view: He can be good in midfield with his aerial strength plus ball winning abilities but we really need him up front. Should remain as the target man.

    Robin Goh - Mostly as the anchor man in Midfield and recently as a back -up for central back. Not winning enough headers in the midfield (mostly due to his eye injury) and can be too eager to go for the ball at times. Has a commanding presence on the field and is one of our better free-kick and corner takers.
    Personal view: Feel that he may be better playing as a defender or right - winger. Would be good as a stopper if we were to explore that option in the future. His naturally long strides and crossing abilities will make him a good winger.

    Jimmy - Hard working midfielder and has the ability and presence of mind to take shots from outside the penalty box. Tends to play the ball too long and is not a good passer in midfield.
    Personal view: Should remain in central mid but must work on his distribution and refrain from over - dribbling.

    Damien - One of our best central mids with good distribution and passes. Has the ability to dribble past opponents when its is needed. Can be a bit too laid - back at times. It is a wonder why he has not scored so far.
    Personal view: Should stay at his current position.

    Khairul - Was quite frustrated with him when he was playing in midfield. However, he seems to have fit right into his new position as a striker. May not be effective at times due to his lack of stature.
    Personal view: Too similar to Russell in terms of playing style. Should separate the two when we are playing 4 - 4 - 2. Must curb his tantrums and learn to be a better loser.

    Jeremy - Dependable defender with good last ditch tackles. Has improved in his defensive headers but can work on his clearance. Still likes to play the ball in his penalty box. Anyone will tell you it is suicide to do that.
    Personal view: Should remain as central back. Has a good head, should come up for corners more often if the situation permits.

    Charles - Excellent marshall in defence and is always missed when he is not around. Has the ability to jump start the offence with his passes. Slow at times but overall solid.
    Personal view: Can be a good stopper if we do decide to play one.

    Ian - Very fit left back and can be a back - up for left mid. Incredible pace but lack close control and not a good passer of balls. Good tackler but sometimes commits too early.
    Personal view: It would be good if he can improve on his passes down the flanks. Should improve on his crosses as it will come in handy when he does on of his runs down the flanks.

    Patrick - Similar to Ian but not as fast. Used to have the tendency to commit too early but has improved tremendously. Likes to have the occasional foray up front.
    Personal view: Can be bit soft, should learn to use his body more.

    Alfred - How should I place him? Crunching tackles from the back is his forte. Need I say more?
    Personal view: We should put him on any opponent we don?t like.

    Kelvin - A very pleasant surprise. Able to switch from left to right with ease. Efficient player who does his primary job well.
    Personal view: Has a very good attitude apart from his tardiness.

    Lawrence - One of the few who knows how to play as a winger. Skillful player but hindered by his fitness. Often missing due to injury and work commitments.
    Personal view: Can be a fox in the box kind of striker.

    Alvin Chia - Good striker with some good skills. Poor reading of the off-side trap and should learn to position himself better to meet crosses. Not very good when under pressure. One of our better takers of free - kicks and corners.
    Personal view: Would like to see him fulfill his potential.

    Alvin Ang - Uses his size to great effect. Has good skills. Lack pace and fitness.
    Personal view: Out and out striker.

    Derrick - An enigma. Definitely has the skills and possesses a powerful shot but all these are seldom seen during the game. Lacks fitness and determination. Not a natural winger.
    Personal view: Not effective as a winger, should explore other positions.

    Boon - Another enigma. Very skill-full player but seems to have taken a back seat when playing for our team. Tends to over dribble the balls at times.
    Personal view: Seems to be comfortable playing in center mid.

    Bernard - A welcomed inclusion to the team but sadly, another short central mid. Utility player who can cover up defensive positions too.
    Personal view: Full potential may only show after a while as laid off for quite long.

    Colin - Honestly, his reputation precedes him but has yet to live up to it. An excellent shot-stopper. Not very good when cutting out crossers and corners. Lack confidence in one - on - one situations. Slow in moving off his line. Marshalls his defence well and with much authority.
    Personal view: Perhaps he is not yet giving his all to the team. Definitely, can do much better.

    Robin Yap - Gaining in confidence everyday as a striker. Rather soft at times and gives people the impression that he is not trying his best.
    Personal view: Due to his willingness to accept comments and the occasional scolding from the manager, I am sure he will continue to improve.

    I left myself out as I am not about to screw myself in public. However, I would like you guys to screw me (this doesn't sounds right). Please do give me your comments.
