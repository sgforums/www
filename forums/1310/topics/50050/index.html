    Looks like I'll be the first to contribute.

    Although we have more or less settled for a 4-4-2 formation, we have to agree that there are several shortfalls. They are as follows:

    1. Lack of pacy wingers - our current wingers are unfortunately not fast at all. Even players ike jimmy, who is often thought to be fast is not really fast enough due to his stature. He may look like he is running fast but he is simply not covering enough ground with his strides to be effective.

    2. Poor quality of crosses - This is evident from the fact that although we have played 4-4-2 for so long, there simply aren't many goals resulting from a cross. I have more mis-kicks than actual crosses, most of my services to the strikers are mainly long passes from deep or square passes.

    3. Poor positioning of our strikers - Our strikers with the exception of Russell, are often poorly positioned to meet any crosses. Most of our strikers thrived on through balls or direct passes. They are also often caught off-side.

    4. Players not suited for formation - The 4-4-2 formation requires much discipline from all the players and there is seldom room for individual play. The play is simple, release the ball early to the flanks. This obviously will not work well as we have several players who enjoys running with the ball. The only players who should be running with the ball in this formation is the wingers.

    As such I am proposing that we try out a new formation which allows our players to play to their strengths.

    SC

    AML AMC AMR

    DMC DMC

    LB DC DC RB

    SC - We will play a lone striker up front. likely candidates will be Alvin Ang and Russell.

    AMC/R/L - In this formation, the three attacking midfielders can indulge in their dribbling, short passes, beating of players etc. Likely candidates are Damien, Jimmy, Boon, Alvin Chia, Robin Yap and Derrick.

    DM - The two defensive mids will have to be very discipline and refrain from any movement further up-field. They will have to cut out any counter attacks from the middle and provide cover for the defence if need to. Likely candidates, Robin Goh, Alvin Chua, Bernard.

    The defenders should see no changes.
