    My frens all said I am one of the most motivated and determined person they have ever known.

    I have goals. Some are long term goals and some are short term ones.

    I used to be a person who are not so motivated. I just tell myself "try your best". And that is what i did all the time. I scored pretty well for my PSLE, at least I am qualified for Special Course. So I thought I can get into XXX school since I am already a Special Course Student. However, I was taken aback when I knew that I was actually rejected by that school and was hence posted to my 2nd choice.

    That was kind of a turning point of my life, yes, at a young age of 12.

    From that lesson, I learnt that if I really want something, I must really work HARD for it. Don't take success for granted. I then became a very determined and motivated person and had never failed to achieve what I wanted from then on.

    Today, I don't believe in "trying my best" anymore because I still dun know what is my "best". I am still trying hard to know what i am capable of.

    I see myself as a rubber band. I know i can be stretched, but how far ? I am stretching myself whenever i have the opportunity. One day that rubber band will snap, and that is when i can truly say "that's my best".

    How to be motivated ?? It is hard, but not impossible. But these things keep me going :

    a. Keep all promises you made, no matter how small and to whom do you made those promises (even young children).

    b. Plan your goals. Make sure you have planned your route well to achieve what you want to achieve. Don't only think of the end results, think of *how* you are going to achieve these results. Then work them out.

    c. Never give up. Plan well, understand your plan, believe in it and then work it out. But be prepared to change your plans. The World is dynamic, you should not be static.

    d. Set datelines for yourself. And MAKE SURE you meet the datelines.

    e. Not only do well in the things you do, you MUST be the BEST.

    f. Do not allow yourself to fail. Every failure will be a blow to your confidence. But if you do fail, be PROUD of it.

    g. Passion. Have passion in the things you do.

    h. Nothing is more impossible than the creation of life itself, which has already happened millions of years ago.

    That's about all i can think of now.

    Just keep stretching yourself, one day you'll know your limits but most of us are too young to have reached that.

    "In order to accelerate, you'll need a force. Without a force, you'll stay where you are" -- Sir Isaac Newton on Newton's 2nd Law.

    And this force must come from WITHIN you.

    Start young. Don't believe the "never too late" concept.
