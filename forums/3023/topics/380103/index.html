    One at the Ballet

    Ever wondered how long does it take to train a professional dancer from being a practice student to a professional dancer, where they trained for more than 30 hours weekly?  Hard work, perseverance and determination plays a big part but what exactly defines a successful talent and what are the rewards in a dancing career?  Have your questions answered and join Janek Schergen at this session as we examine the arc of a professional dancer’s career and the challenges that entails the career.

    What is ONE @ The Ballet?

    Initiated in 2009, One @ The Ballet is a dance presentation focusing on topics such as “The ballet behind the fairytale”, “what defines a dancer’s career”, to “how a choreographer creates a ballet”.  Within the format of each session, audiences will also get to watch excerpts from main performance seasons as well as to gain a deeper understanding of the topics covered, at this bi-monthly session on Saturdays from 1.00 to 2.00 pm at Singapore Dance Theatre.

    Who are the audiences?

    Be it for individuals, family outings or a part of school’s outreach programmes, One@The Ballet aims to engage with audiences through this interactive platform as well as to create an opportunity for us to meet new audiences including youth and senior citizens who have yet a chance to catch SDT’s performances.

     

    Where is it held?

     

     It will be held at Singapore Dance Theatre’s Founder’s Studio, located at Fort Canning Centre, 2nd Storey Cox Terrace.

    How to register?
