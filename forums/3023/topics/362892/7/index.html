    Ok my this Euro Trip is confirmed.

     

    Flying to ZRH Zurich on the morning of the 28th of March. Reach ZRH at 8a.m local time.

     

    Depending on how I clear immigration(time), I will either head for the 9.28a.m train to Bregenz where I can swiftly connect to an onward train to Vienna. However, I would need to buy the ticket and this takes time, potentially skewering this journey.

    Should this fail, I will take my time at the airport to take some photos of planes at Zurich airport, then buy an S-bahn ticket to Zurich Hauptbahnhof by 1p.m. Then buy a sector and reserve from Zurich HB to Feldkirch(the Austrian border station), after which I can use my Eurail East Europe Pass to access 1st class onboard the train(with an optional upgrade to Premier Class for €25).

     

    Should reach Vienna West train station by night. First night gao dim.

     

    Second day, day trip to Graz from Vienna Melding station, and back to Vienna by night. Also gao dim liao.

     

    Third day, go to the South train station of Vienna, and take 2nd class to Bratislava. Day trip, back to Vienna by night. This day I won't use the Pass(due to date restriction).

     

    Fourth morning, go to Vienna West station, take the Railjet(with compulsory reservation) to Budapest Keleti-pu. Check in. Spend two nights there.

     

    On the sixth night, proceed to Keleti-pu train station where I will board the night train directly to Warsaw Central(Warszawa Centralna) station. Arrive Warsaw on the seventh morning. Check into hostel. Should time allow, will do day trip of either Torun or Lodz. Night in Warsaw regardless.

     

    Eighth morning, tour Warsaw. Night Warsaw hostel.

     

    Ninth morning, train from Warszawa Centralna to Krakow Glowny. Day tour of Krakow commences. Night in Krakow.

     

    Tenth morning, 2nd class train from Krakow to Oswiecim, where the Auschwitz camps are near there. Tour of the concentration camps. 2nd class rail back to Krakow. Night in Krakow.

     

    Eleventh morning, train to Katowice, and connect to train that goes to Olomouc, Czech Republic. Time in Katowice if possible. Night in Olomouc.

     

    Twelfth morning, reservation-made SuperCity Pendolino Czech train to Czech capital, Prague(Praha). Check in straight into hostel. Tour Prague if possible. Night in Prague.

     

    Thirteenth morning, continue tour where left off the previous day. Night also in Prague.

     

    Fourteenth morning, should I finish my tours in Prague, I shall take a 2nd class train to Karlstejn Castle just out of Prague for sightseeing... Back to night in Prague.

     

    Fifteenth morning, plan is to take an early morning train to southern Czech town of Ceske Budejovice, home to the Budweiser beer and BATA! Tour and night in Ceske Budejovice.

     

    Sixteenth morning, 2nd class train and back to/from Ceske Krumlov, just out of Ceske Budejovice. Tour Ceske Krumlov and it being a UNESCO World Heritage Site, experience the countryside... Back to night in Ceske Budejovice.

     

    Seventeenth morning. 9+a.m train cross the border into Austria where I will end up at Linz Haupbahnhof. Tour Linz for a short while and take an evening train to Innsbruck for night. Hostel not booked yet.

     

    Eighteenth day. Tour Innsbruck and surrounding areas. Night in Innsbruck. Hostel not booked yet.

     

    Nineteenth day, take an early morning train to Salzburg and tour Salzburg till night. Night in Salzburg. Hostel not booked yet.

     

    Twentieth early morning, 2nd class train to Munich Hauptbahnhof and thereafter continuing 2nd class to Munich Airport where my flight departs at 1+p.m. Have to reach Munich Airport by 11.30a.m.
