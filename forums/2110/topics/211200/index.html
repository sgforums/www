Chelsea chief checks on CSKA's Brazilians

  • ghimpheng
    20 Sep 06, 16:45
    tribalfooball.com - September 20, 2006

    CSKA Moscow's Brazilian contingent were watched last week by Chelsea football chief Frank Arnesen.
    Arnesen attended CSKA's Champions League clash with Porto and while the Portuguese club's teenage Brazilian midfielder Anderson has been linked with the Blues, it's his countrymen at the Russian club which have also sparked serious interest.

    Attacking midfielder Daniel Carvalho has been likened to AC Milan star Kaka and like teammate Vagner Love has long been linked with Chelsea. Left-sided winger Dudu was also watched by Arnesen and could come under consideration as a replacement for Damien Duff.
