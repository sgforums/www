    Andriy Shevchenko has revealed his relationship with Chelsea manager Jose Mourinho is on the upturn.

    His relationship with Mourinho has improved in recent weeks and alongside Didier Drogba this evening, Chelsea's strike-force will have the chance to make another favourable impressions.
