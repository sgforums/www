    SYNOPSIS

    The 'Sad Sonata' is love story about two men and one woman. Joon Young (KSW), raised by mother who sold liquor to American soldiers, met Hei In (KHS), who was blind, and became childhood sweethearts. Then, Hei In immigrated to US following her American adoptive father and met Gun Woo (SSH), who was Joon Young's friend and son of a very rich man. The story picks up again when composer Joon Young, singer Hei In, and music producer Gun Woo all meet.



    Cast:

    Kwon Sang Woo : Soe JunYoung

    Yun JungHoon : Lee GunWoo
