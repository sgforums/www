    Chelsea defender Khalid Boulahrouz admits he is unhappy with his tag of 'Mr Versatile'.

    Boulahrouz, who expects to return to the bench or the right-back spot against Arsenal, said: "Everybody knows what my best position is.

    "I prefer to play in the middle and it's where I feel more comfortable, but I always give everything I have. For me, I prefer to play centre-back.

    "Arsenal is a good team and it won't be easy. Of course I would like to play but it is not easy to play John out of his place.

    "I like to play football but I do not agree that I am very versatile."
