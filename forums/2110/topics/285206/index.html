    Football: Chelsea's star Lampard up for sale
    Posted: 11 October 2007 2321 hrs

    MADRID : Chelsea's England star Frank Lampard is up for sale, a Spanish newspaper said on Thursday, quoting sources in the London club and close to the midfielder.

    Lampard would cost around 18 million euros (US$25.5 million, 12.5 million pounds), the AS sports newspaper said.

    Three clubs are already in contact with the player's agent, Steve Kutner: Juventus, AC Milan and "a few days ago with a representative to Real Madrid," it said.

    Lampard, who has scored 13 goals in 56 England appearances, has won two Premier League championships with Chelsea, in 2005 and 2006. He joined the west London club in 2001 from West Ham. - AFP/de
