    LONDON, April 28 (Reuters) - Portugal defender Ricardo Carvalho will miss Chelsea's Champions League semi-final second leg against Liverpool on Tuesday after injuring his knee, manager Jose Mourinho said.

    Carvalho went down after a tackle by Bolton's Kevin Davies in the first half of Chelsea's 2-2 Premier League home draw with on Saturday and was substituted by Frank Lampard. Davies was booked.

    'It's bad...it's his knee,' Mourinho said. 'He's been our best defender the whole season. It's as simple as that,' the Portuguese coach said. 'We lost in Liverpool in the Premiership without him.'

    Chelsea's last Premier League defeat was by Liverpool in January.
