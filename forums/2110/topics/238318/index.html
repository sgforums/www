Norwich's Etuhu: Chelsea dressing rooms bigger than my house

  • ghimpheng
    19 Feb 07, 20:51
    tribalfooball.com - February 18, 2007

    Norwich City midfielder Dickson Etuhu has revealed the huge the gulf that exists between Carrow Road and Saturday's FA Cup opponents Chelsea.
    Etuhu said: "It was great to play against the best players in the world and Mourinho didn't have to tell us that we didn't deserve to lose 4-0. We all know that.

    "What was incredible was going into the Chelsea dressing-room to have a chat and swap shirts after the game. It's bigger than my house!

    "I'm not joking. They've got about 55 staff in there. It's huge."
