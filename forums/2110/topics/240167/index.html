    Chelsea are in talks with Bournemouth and Plymouth Argyle over partnership arrangements.
    Blues boss Jose Mourinho is pushing through a deal for Plymouth fullback Dan Gosling, part of which will include an informal link-up with Ian Holloway's club.

    Gosling could return to Home Park for the whole of next season as part of the arrangement - with other promising Chelsea kids also going to Plymouth on-loan.

    The Mirror says Mourinho and his backroom team also plan to hook up with Bournemouth to send some of their up-and-coming players to Dean Court on loan spells.

    They feel reserve football is of little use in their development.
