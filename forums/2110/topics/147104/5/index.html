    SAMGYUPSAL

    Samgyupsal, or grilled pork belly, is a popular Korean dish. It consists of pork bacon cut from the belly, served like Kalbi, sometimes cooked on a grill with kimchi troughs on either side. It is commonly grilled with garlic and onions, dipped in ssamjang and wrapped in ssangchu.

    * ssamjang - a korean dip or sauce
    * ssangchu - lettuce
