    Chelsea trials for 'new Cech'
    tribalfooball.com - December 14, 2006

    Chelsea have handed a trial to Czech goalkeeper Jan Sebek - described as the 'new' Petr Cech.
    The 15-year-old is known to idolise the Blues number one, currently recovering from a fractured skull.

    The youngster is currently on loan with Viktoria Plzen - coincidentally Cech's first club.

    Sebek is contracted to Tabor from the sixth tier of Czech football, but he is already a youth international and was moved to Viktoria to play in their youth side.

    "Sebek is currently on loan at Plzen, where he takes part in the youth league," his agent Pavel Zika explained to Idnes.

    "He will undergo a trial in the middle of January," Zika remarked.
