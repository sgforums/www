    Chelsea's Robben determined to prove he's hard enough
    tribalfooball.com - February 12, 2007

    Arjen Robben is determined to prove he's tough enough for the Premiership.

    Robben, 22, has started just 48 out of a possible 103 Premiership matches for Chelsea since his arrival from PSV Eindhoven in July 2004.

    He said: "It feels like I've had no luck at all. I do get fed up and I can imagine people thinking I'm always injured. But I can assure them nobody is more frustrated by the situation than myself.

    "The fans must be getting tired of this as well. But I always work very hard to come back as quickly as possible."

    He added: "I can do a lot more for the team when I play a lot of consecutive games. I need a lot of matches under my belt and to get on the scoresheet more often.

    "I've looked back at what I could have done differently. I've even watched the Liverpool injury again in slow motion but there was nothing I could have done to prevent it."
