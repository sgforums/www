    Some of us here are getting confused.

    Revenue is not profit.

    Revenue is all the money collected from all source of income.

    Expenses are all money paid out when you buy things, pay salary and upkeep etc.

    Very very simple calculation : Revenue - Expenses = Gross Profit (before tax)

    Of course there are other factors such a purchase of assets, depreciation, acruals etc which I will not dwell into.
