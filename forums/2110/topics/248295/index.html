    Blackburn's McCarthy: No reason for Chelsea fear
    tribalfooball.com - April 12, 2007

    Benni McCarthy says there's no reason why Blackburn Rovers should fear Chelsea ahead of their FA Cup semifinal this weekend.

    The Rovers' top scorer said: "We have knocked out Everton and Arsenal.

    "When you have beaten teams like that you feel you might have a great chance of reaching Wembley.

    "You get a feel for the games and you can smell Wembley is not so far away and it drives you on.

    "None of our team has played at Wembley so this is a great opportunity for us all."

    Even though McCarthy has won the Champions League with Jose Mourinho's Porto, he believes the FA Cup has something special about it.

    The 19-goal hitman said: "The FA Cup has a great atmosphere and a great vibe. I play every game as if it's my last. It has been like that since I came to England."
