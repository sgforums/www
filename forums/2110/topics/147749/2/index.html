    Surpassing even the legendary success of Korean mega hit series Winter Sonata, Stairway To Heaven not only broke TV ratings records when it was aired in Korea in Dec 2003 but also created a viewership following in the region.

    Premiering on 15 August, this drama is setting records again locally by being the first Korean drama to make its foray into the 10pm prime time belt on Channel U.

    Boasting a cast that includes Korean superstars Cho Ji Woo (Winter Sonata) and Kwon Sang Woo (Love, So Divine, My Tutor Friend and Volcano High), this hit series also stars familiar face Shin Hyun Joon (Guns and Talks and Bichunmoo) and the sweet-looking Kim Tae Hee.

    A drama depicting the struggles that young adults encounter in the face of their destinies, Stairway to Heaven is a heart-wrenching love story that reveals unique values that the characters have about love. Four different people with four different perspectives and ways of showing their love - a purist, a sentimentalist, a selfless admirer and an opportunist, just what type of fate beholds them?

    Stairway to Heaven garnered a tremendous following after its airing in Korea such that Hong Kong, Taiwan and Japan followed suit with similar waves of viewership interest that cemented Cho Ji Woo and Kwon Sang Woo as one of the most popular screen couples in Asian television.
