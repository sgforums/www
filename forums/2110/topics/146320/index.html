    Did You Know Â…

    Chosun Royal Cuisine     or gungjung-eumsik has long been recognised as an important part of Korean cultural heritage. The expert that supervises the culinary aspects of Dae Jang Geum is Han Bok-ryeo, the eldest daughter of Hwang Hye-seong, the government designated Human Cultural Property of Royal Cuisine.

    Although Hwang has never been a royal cook herself, she spent decades learning from Han Hui-sun, the last surviving royal chef. Ever since Han passed away in 1972, Hwang has been the highest authority and the ambassador of Korean Royal Cuisine at home and abroad.

    Han Bok-ryeo, who was responsible for the cooking scenes in Dae Jang Geum supervised the royal banquet given by Kim Dae-jung in honour of Kim Jong-il during their historical meeting in June 2000.

    - excerpt from article by Katarzyna J.Cwiertka, Dpt of Jap & Kor Studies
