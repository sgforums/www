    Chelsea Football Club will be greeting Londoners, tourists and commuters on one of the key gateways into the capital from today. A huge adidas poster featuring many of Chelsea FC's stars was unveiled on one of London's biggest poster sites next to the M4 elevated section in west London.

    The giant banner, called Team Move, is 600 sq m and was put up by official sponsors adidas. It is the first banner visitors will see as they drive into London and features Frank Lampard, Michael Ballack, Arjen Robben, Andriy Schevchenko, Didier Drogba, Petr Cech and Joe Cole.

    The players are shown performing one continuous move culminating in a goal celebration around the words 'Welcome to London - Home of Chelsea FC' An adidas spokesman said :'We're extremely proud to have signed a long term partnership with Chelsea and wanted to celebrate it in a big way. It doesn't come much bigger than this and who better to welcome people to London than the Premiership Champions"
