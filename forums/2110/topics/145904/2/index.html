    Rain replaces Jay Chow with HK$4 million

    Source : Entertainment News from Oriental Daily News, 26 August 2005

    Korean most popular idol-singer Rain who has acted in the serial drama Full House, had become more popular after the serial has broadcasted in Hong Kong. He had become one of the most exploded overseas singers in Hong Kong, With his rising popularity, he had also become the most wanted artistes by Commercial/Advertisement businessmen.

    Next month's visit to Hong Kong

    Oriental Daily News has received a reliable news that shortly after Rain had been invited to join PEPSI to be its No.1 spokesman, he is now replacing Taiwan idol Jay Chow, to be DHC's new spokesman, and will visit Hong Kong as the "mysterious" special guest on 4th September to attend opening ceremony of DHC's new store at TsimShaTsui Harbour City. As DHC has been very concerned and anxious to endorse Rain as their new spokesman, the trip to Hong Kong next month is kept confidential; in order to give his fans a surprise.

    In addition, despite Rain being a popular Korean idol and very marketable in Hong Kong, he is actually quite amiable, he does not demand high price for the endorsements. According to records, Jay earned HK$3 million as DHC spokesman, and Rain has only asked for HK$1 million more i.e. HK$4 million plus lots of commercial promotions.

    Clarification with Hong Kong Manager
    The reporter called up Rain's Hong Kong manager Ms Chan Ka Ying to clarify this matter. First the report asked if Rain is coming to Hong Kong? She happily replied "Yes, later he has something to do in Hong Kong". Reporter added "Has Rain accepted to shoot DHC's commercial?" Ms Chan reacted quickly, "How do you know? There's no way you could know." After that she admitted "Yes!" and quickly ended the telephone conversation.
