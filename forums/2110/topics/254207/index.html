    Fergie fed-up as Man Utd's Saha ruled out of Cup final

    Manchester United boss Sir Alex Ferguson has ruled Louis Saha out of next week's FA Cup final against Chelsea.

    The Guardian says Ferguson has become increasingly exasperated by Saha's susceptibility to injury and it has led to angry words behind the scenes at the training ground. Saha has started only four matches in 2007 and there have been three occasions in the past six weeks when Ferguson has thought the player was fit only for him to pull out at the last minute.

    "He has picked up an injury in his knee, believe it or not," said a frustrated Ferguson. "He was pencilled in to play against Manchester City last Saturday but he got an injury the day before in training. He felt it on the Saturday morning. We were ready to pick him for the team but unfortunately this knee injury surfaced. I would say he has no chance [for the final]."
