    On paper, they seem to have a easier fixture list running in, they have done the tough away games (except to Arsenal) whereas Man U have to visit Liverpool away, Chelsea away.

    Think the crucial factor is the lack of able players to double up when their keyplayers are injured or not performing. I don't think Chelsea can use injuries to Terry & Cech as excuse for poor performance. If you want to be a big time team, these are things that you must counter and live with. But looking at Chelsea's lineup, the Dutch defender - Balh...something has not been good. Same goes for Ferrira (or something like that) - hence essentially, you rely solely on Terry & Cavarlho - either one gets injured / suspended, you are in trouble. Playing Essien in centre of defense is not exactly a bright idea.

    Man U is facing a defensive crisis at the moment - all their defenders are out. But the saving grace is that their goalie is around although in the recent games, his form is questionable as well.
