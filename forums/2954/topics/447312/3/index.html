    10 benefits you can gain by reciting “Nanmo Amituofo" :

    1. The protection of all heavenly gods, day and night.

    2. The protection of twenty-five great Bodhisattvas, including Guan Yin.

    3. The protection and blessings of all Buddhas. Particularly, Amitabha Buddha will shed light on you and protect you.

    4. No evil spirits or poisonous snakes can harm you.

    5. Flood, fire, and weapons will not harm you. Accidents will not happen to you.

    6. The karma of your past wrongdoings, mischievous deeds, and misbehaviors will all be eliminated.

    7. You will have no nightmares – only peaceful dreams. In them you may see Amitabha Buddha’s radiant golden image.

    8. You will be happy, good-looking, energetic, healthy, and successful.

    9. You will have the respect of other people.

    10. As the end of your life approaches, you will not be afraid and you will be of sound mind.

    Relying on our own (self) power to liberate from the cycle of life and death is difficult. Relying on Buddha’s power to liberate from the cycle of life and death is easy. Everyone who has a heart (willing mind), would be able to chant the Buddha’s name and surely be reborn in the world of Utmost Bliss. Buddha’s power refers to Amituofo great compassionate vows to bless, to receive and to guide all sentient beings to His PureLand. Own (self) power has two meanings. One is not relying on Buddha’s power but base on one’s cultivation in the precepts, concentration and wisdom. However in this Dharma ending period, it is impossible for us as lay people to cultivate to attain the result by relying on our own power. The other one is the ability to rely the power of the Buddha Dharma, that is base on our own power in having a true deep faith, make sincere vow and single-heartedly chanting the ( Amituofo) Buddha’s name. – Great Master YinGuang
