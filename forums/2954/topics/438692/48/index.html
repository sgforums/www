    This autumn you are taken for a sleep –

    close your eyes now

     

    a feel of its original strangeness

    with time easily turning on the gush

     

    of passion

     

    when you were drifted away in its name

    is my meditative loneliness, my memorabilia

     

    forever

     

    My usual life becomes but a compromise

    whilst time let me in and learn habitually

     

    this hail of sadness –

    let this love returns its warmth

     

    ever

     

    fcukapap
