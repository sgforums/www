    The tidal waves have broken these heart walls
    While on their journey to the sea.
    Great shivers

    of the heart flows are but
    Living life like an eternal game.
    Each ripple is a voice from above,

    watching over in love,

    Gentle and true to the waves as it spreads.
    One day the ripples will speak for itself,

    and one day the waves will break these heart walls.

    For one has loved with deepest innocence,
    and hoped for the one with the highest love.

    - Fcukpap
