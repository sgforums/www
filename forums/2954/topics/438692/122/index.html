    Dear Rahula (Only son before enlightened)..

    Understand that any material in your body or out, you don’t own it, you cannot have control of it, that is not you and it has its own way of existence.

    Understand that any liquid in your body or out, you don’t own it, you cannot have control of it, that is not you and it has its own way of existence.


    Understand that any air in your body or out, you don’t own it, you cannot have control of it, that is not you and it has its own way of existence.

    Understand that any warmth in your body or out, you don’t own it, you cannot have control of it, that is not you and it has its own way of existence.

    When you contemplate the true nature of these 4 building materials of universe, you lose the attachment for them..

    --------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Like a sailor strives day and night with courage to reach his destination, monk should contemplate about Materials, sense, Identifications, Thoughts and mind, with intelligence day and night.

    ~Enlightened Nagasena thero~
