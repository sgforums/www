    Attention is our navigator, meaning "acceptance". If we have faith in accepting Buddha's teaching, then it will act like a ship to ferry us over the land of Nirvana.

    ----------------------------------------------------------------

    Three mindsets are required for practicing Buddhism:
    decency, mindfulness, and compassion.

    ----------------------------------------------------------------

    In life, the most ugly is hatred, and the most beautiful is compassion. Only true, everlasting compassion can pacify hatred.


    To achieve immeasurable merits is to eliminate inexhaustible vexation. A person is truly "rescued" for giving up vexation of his side to reach nirvana of the other side.
