    Give up your constant need to complain about anything and everything. 
    From the bad service at the restaurant, to the person who knocked you on the bus; the people, situations, events that make you unhappy, sad and depressed, stop with the negative comments. 
    Nobody can make you unhappy, no situation can make you sad or miserable unless you allow it to. It's not the situation that triggers those feelings in you, but how you choose to look at it.
