    Guard your thoughts. They are like the bandits who will destroy your mind.They are like robbers who will destroy your virtues.They are like thieves who will steal your sanity. They are like unguarded wild animals which will destroy you. Unwholesome thoughts will result in unwholesome actions.So guard all your doors well.Be mindful of what you see.Be mindful of what you taste.Be mindful of what you hear.Be mindful of what you touch.Be mindful of what you feel.Be mindful of what you perceive.Be mindful of your desires.Be mindful of your cravings.Be mindful of your afflictions. Guard them well. Purify and filter your thoughts. The Mind is the forerunner of all your actions. Good thoughts produce a good mind. Good mind produces wholesome actions..
