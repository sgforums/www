    “The moon may be appearing half or full, yet it is still a complete moon”. When we can only see a half moon, does it mean that there is only half a moon left? The moon is always full. It is just that we can only see half of it. Although we are drifting in the rounds of rebirths, our true Buddha nature has never been lost. It is always with us.
