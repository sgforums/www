    and let her nature’s beauty shines
    through the mellowing days and solemn nights

    and let time halts between
    the void of her alluring glance and mine -

    no words or deeds could pry through heart’s passage
    nor the tolling of the bells

    as they chime through the chimera of the heart...
    and dwell, dwell, dwell in passion's euphoria

    what reasons impel the rippling tinkers of the nights
    but a heart swells in passionate intensity tells

    the hallucinatory tales of clanging remembrances
    like rapturing waves that clamour for her archangel’s smile?
