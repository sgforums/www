    stopping by with a heart that refuses to go,

    stopping by each faltered well of memories,

    furry comes soon with next page of departure

    nothing more I can do to delude exit of a long dream:

    we had walked miles upon miles, in rushes,

    those moments we held dear and green

    and sighed together at life’s uncertainties

    but timelessness of time forbids turning back the unreal

    desires we once had, leaving 

    the remains of illusions and moments

    that forever beckon the return of old follies

    follies you will never know, ever know.

    fcukpap
