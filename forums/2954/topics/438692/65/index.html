    Lord Buddha: Don’t say so Thambaruka…

    Thambaruka:Then, is it created by god…?


    Lord Buddha: Don’t say so Thambaruka..

    Thambaruka:Then is it created by itself..?

    Lord Buddha : Don’t say so Thambaruka..

    Thambaruka: So then, aren’t there things called happiness & suffering..?

    Lord Buddha: There are happiness & suffering Thambaruka..

    Thambaruka: Do you know them well Lord Buddha..?

    Lord Buddha: I see & know them very well Thambaruka..

    Thambaruka: I cant understand,…please explain Lord Buddha..?

    Lord Buddha: Happiness & suffering, are thoughts..Thoughts are identifications of senses..Senses are created by connecting,..

    l)Things, ll)Sensory organs and lll)Mind..
    (Things + Sensory Organs + Mind = Sense)
