    Novice, yes indeed.  If you cultivate in the Pureland, sooner or later you will be able to help deliver all those who ever were your relatives in many, many lives past...  not just this life. 

    The compassion that one will develop while treading the path towards bodhisattva-hood or Buddhahood is a larger, more all-encompassing type of compassion than mere concern about earthly matters.  We will want to help deliver sentient beings from suffering, not just put a band-aid (or handyplast) on a minor wound... only to have the wound fester again, or appear on another part of the body.  Suffering will keep assailing us unless we become awakened.

    That's not to say that we shouldn't love those around us, but that we should realise that the only way all suffering can stop is to get enlightened.  Hence, we get enlightened so that we may help others along the path.  Along the way too, we should help friends along; who are also working towards the same goal of liberation.
