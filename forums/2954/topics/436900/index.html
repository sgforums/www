    Originally posted by likeyou:

    If you vow to reborn to pureland (that is no suffering anymore), will you still remember your family members? Can you help to bring them together with you to pureland?

    I am sorry if I am too attached to my family members.

    What I understand is yes. You will still remember your family members and you will also be grateful to those who had helped you to know about Pureland. However, you can't bring them with you as they must be willing to go with you, they  must also practice and have faith in Pureland. When they are still with you now, let them have the same faith in Pureland as you. Encourage them to vow and practice.

    What I understand is if you are in Pureland and unfortunately your family members and your benefactors are being reborn, you can help them by dedicating merits to them. By doing so, they will have affinities with Amituofo and  find their way to Pure Land by themselves.

    I always dedicate merits to my loved ones who had passed away. They did not know about Amituofo when they were alive. By doing so, they will also have affinities with Amituofo and find their way to Pureland by themselves. The power of dedicating merits is very strong.
