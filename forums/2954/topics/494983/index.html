    Some Feedback From Previous Runs:

    Respected Bro Shi’an, all these years, I’d been attending many of your courses, which strongly heightened the progress of my cultivation toward the path to liberation. Very often, when I’d completed each course, I developed a positive energy that purifies my tainted mind, feeling joyful and increase in my faith to be born in Amituofo’s Pure Land. May Bro Shi’an live long and healthy in Samsara, to benefit all sentient beings. Many thanks with gratefulness. Amituofo. Warmest regards, Geok Choo

    Looking forward to more of your courses. True Dharma teachers are hard to find nowadays, especially one who teaches in English. Thank you for sharing the true Dharma… Not many Dharma teachers will share such true Pure Land teachings. Of course, only those who are sincere, knowledgeable and willing to help sentient beings do. Again, thank you. Amituofo, Sally

    Thank you for being a good teacher, sharing your abundant knowledge about Buddhism with the students. This is the first time I am attending a Buddhist class, so it was an eye-opener for me… Many thanks for your time. I appreciate this opportunity to learn about Buddhism… Regards, Anne

    Thank you, teacher Shi’an, for sharing the Dharma with us. Amituofo, Serene

    I enjoyed your Buddhist lessons and they really gave me very fresh perspectives on the Pure Land tradition. Your efforts in propagating the Dharma are indeed commendable! A sincere ‘thank you’ to you! Amituofo, Zhengjie

    Hi Shi’an teacher, thank you for conducting the Amitabha Sutra class. You are extraordinary, so profound in the teaching of this course, putting in so so so… much efforts. I have good karma to meet a teacher like you. Homage to you. Amituofo, Sheila
