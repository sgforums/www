    Originally posted by Beautiful951:

    I am intending to be a full vegetarian but jusy not in one go.

     

    I do not understand about the relief of suffering part. Can you explain further?

    In the lower realm (say hell or ghost realm), the hell beings suffer all the time. If one is reborn in the lower realm (let's say hell or ghost realm) due to some reasons, one will be relieved of sufferings on those days or time when he/she was a vegetarian as a human in his/her past life.

    Let's say the person is a vegetarian on 1st and 15th of every month, he will be relieved of sufferings on 1st and 15th if he is reborn in hell or ghost realm due to some reasons. If the person is a vegetarian at lunch time, he will be relieved of suffering in hell or ghost realm during lunch time if he is reborn in hell or ghost realm due to some reasons.

  • Dawnfirstlight
    27 Aug 11, 07:24
    Originally posted by Beautiful951:

    For the eight percepts, about not eating in the afternoon, is it that we cannot eat dinner?

    Yes. Followings are the 8 precepts. In fact, it is not difficult to observe just for 24 hours because sleeping already takes up at least 8 hours.

    1) No stealing

    2) No telling lies

    3) No sexual activities (including husband and wife relationship)

    4) No drinking of alcohol (including drugs that make one feels high)

    5) No killing

    6) No sleeping or sitting on high beds or high chairs, no watching TV programme or hearing music or songs ( sometimes these entertainments will make us emotional)

    7) No cosmetics or jewelleries

    8) No eating after 12 noon but allow to drink (don't worry that you will go hungry. I'm the type who can't skip a meal on normal days but during observing 8 precepts, strangely I don't feel hungry at all. Not only I say that, many devotees say that too. It must be the blessings from the Bodhisattva).
