    Okay sorry. I misunderstood your question.

    My earlier post was a response to apologetic arguments that the bible predicted that the earth was a sphere.

    You asked if the bible stated the earth was flat.

    The bible does not explicitly say the earth is flat. That's not surprising as the bible is not an exposition on cosmology. The bible does not cover the structure of the universe any more than it describes gravity. However, even though gravity is not explicitly mentioned, you can tell from the writings that the people in the bible are living in a world where gravity exists.

    Similarly, there are numerous examples where you can see that biblical authors followed the flat earth paradigm.

    A more through treatment can be found at http://www.lhup.edu/~dsimanek/febible.htm
