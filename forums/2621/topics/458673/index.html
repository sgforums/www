    Heyo,

    I'm an atheist in Singapore and I chanced upon this forum section discussing atheism!

    Just FYI, there are quite a number of such groups in Singapore already, springing up across the island over the past 4 years. If you are interested, you can join some of their events. I have already attended a few myself.

    One of them, the Humanist Society (Singapore), was registered with the Registrar of Societies in 2010. They have compiled a directory of local atheist/skeptic/humanist groups so you don't have to google endlessly anymore..

    http://www.humanist.org.sg/information-on-hss/local-groups-directory

    Humanist Society (Singapore) (http://humanist.org.sg/)

    The Humanist Society (Singapore) is a registered society in Singapore for humanists, freethinkers, atheists, agnostics and other like-minded people. 

    Drinking Skeptically SG 

    Drinking Skeptically is an informal social event designed to promote fellowship and networking among skeptics, critical-thinkers, and like-minded individuals.

    Singaporean Atheists 

    Singaporean Atheists is an informal network of Singaporean atheists from all walks of life. Founded in 2008, they are based primarily on their Facebook group, which serves a platform for discussions and also hosting events.

    Singapore Philosophy Group

    The Singapore Philosophy Group meets regularly to discuss life and its fundamental questions. 

    NUS Students for Freethought

    This group was set up in Feb 2012 to provide a social and support network for students in NUS with no religious affiliations.

    Murtads of Singapore and Malaysia

    The Apostates in Malaysia and Singapore is a group of former Muslims in Malaysia and Singapore.

     

    There is a even a wikipedia entry about the non-religious community in Singapore: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Irreligion_in_Singapore
