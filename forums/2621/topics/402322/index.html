    Hey everyone!

    Just to inform you, a freethinker group already exists in Singapore since 2008. It is the Singapore Humanism Meetup. You can check out our blog here: http://sg-humanism-meetup.blogspot.com/

    To join us, all you need is sign up here: http://www.meetup.com/Singapore-Humanism/ and you will receive email notifications of our gatherings. 

    We also have a facebook group: http://www.facebook.com/#!/group.php?gid=41667702434&ref=ts and you will messages from there too. 

    Combining both websites we have about 500 members online. Even the Straits Times wrote about us twice:

    1) http://sg-humanism-meetup.blogspot.com/2010/02/shm-in-news-again.html

    2) http://sg-humanism-meetup.blogspot.com/2009/09/aware-saga-and-pink-dot.html

    We currently do not have a fixed venue, nor do we need one. We meet at cafes, private rooms and community centres once or twice a month. 

    During our meetings, one key feature is discussion where the free marketplace of ideas exist. Among our hottest topics are science, philosophy, religion, politics, and everything that has to do with human nature. As we are freethinkers, no 'dogma' exists. Instead, members sharpen their mind through debate, learn about the world through each other, and practice tolerance and respect of different ideas. 

    If you have any more questions, feel free to email us at sg.humanism.meetup@gmail.com
