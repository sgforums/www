Supermodelme Season 3

  • FGW
    16 Feb 12, 17:44

    Multi-platform production, Supermodelme features 12 aspiring Asian models going head-to-head through a series of physically and mentally grueling challenges – each vying for a chance to kick-start her modeling career. The SUPERMODELME winner will receive $20,000 along with attractive prizes from key sponsors and get to grace the cover of local leading fashion magazines.

    Season 3 will push the contestants to their limits, as the girls undergo tougher-than-ever challenges – braving the elements, getting into uncomfortable situations and confronting their own fears, all while maintaining their allure of a model.

    Containing 12 episodes, Season 3 premieres 22 March on Mediacorp Channel 5, Every Thursday, 22:00 SIN. and 13 March on Celestial Tiger Entertainment- Channel KIX, Every Tuesday, 21:30 SIN/ HK, 20:30 WIB.
