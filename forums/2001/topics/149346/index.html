    Born in England, raised in Australia, Watts is a beautiful talented actress who has been working actively in the film industry for more than 10 years. Unlike her good friend, fellow Australian Nicole Kidman who made it big early in Hollywood, Watts languished in many small-time roles before getting her belated big break in David Lynch's "Mulholland Dr." It was a challenging role that made people sit up and appreciate the talent beneath her intellectual beauty.

    Watts has since starred in more bigger-budget movies, like "The Ring" movies, "Le Divorce", "I Heart Huckabees", "21 Grams", "Stay" and more recently Peter Jackson's "King Kong". She has also produced and starred in a personal semi-autobiographical indie comedy "Ellie Parker" about an actress' struggle to pursue a career in Hollywood.
