    The Korean period drama serial "Da Chang Jin" or "Daejanggeum (Jewel in the Palace)" currently showing on Channel U has a huge female cast, some of whom are relatively new faces. The serial not only revived the acting career of Lee Young-Ae, but also served as a professional stepping stone for these new actresses.

    One of them, Park Eun-Hye or Lian Sheng in the series, has already been introduced:

    http://www.sgforums.com/?action=thread_display&thread_id=187069

    Another young actress may not have appeared yet, but viewers would probably be interested to know who is the sweet medical student with Chang Jin in the later part of the drama serial.

    The character Xin Fei is played by 24-year-old Han Ji-Min, whose acting debut was in the 2003 Korean drama serial "All In". She has also recently appeared in "Blue Swallow", a movie on one of the first female pilots in Korea.

    Just in case the question pops up again, one has no clue whether she has gone under the knife.
