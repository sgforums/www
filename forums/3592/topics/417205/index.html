     The Singapore Transportation challenge 2010(STC 2010)

    (inspired by SMRT Challenge 2008)

    Introduction:

    Up to 6 teams of two will be participating in this event. Each participant will be given a race pack on the race day provided they paid for the race.

    Proceed to all challenge point located island-wide where there will be member of the EXCO awaiting for your arrival.

    Prizes: 

    ~C751A model or $75 in cash for 1st team

    ~5V OC bell or $50 IN CASH for 2nd team

    ~Alstom goodies or one day unlimited food at McDonald's for 3rd team

    Things to bring:

    - Ezlink card with at least $10 (student) / $15 (adult) stored value

    - Hand phone to contact staff members if necessary

    - Small light weight bag

    Rules:

    - You are only allowed to use public buses, trains and SMRT Taxis, up to a maximum or $8. 

    - Ensure there are sufficient value in your CEPAS-compliant card before the race.

    - More updates will be given as the race day closes in.

    Participation Fee: $7

    Criteria:

    You must have an active Facebook account.
