    Singapore Fireworks Festival 2004

    Date(s): 31 Jul 2004 to 15 Aug 2004

    Venue: Marina Bay
    Prices: Free Admission
    Description
    The Marina Bay area will be transformed into the most happening carnival the nation has ever seen.

    Held in conjunction with Singapore's 39th National Day Celebrations, the carnival will include an International Fireworks Festival, Outdoor Mega Concerts and a 16-day Food and Fun Fair Event.

    This is the first time that the International Fireworks Festival is being held in Singapore. The Festival will be held over 3 weekends, with a different country firing every Sunday night. The fireworks firing teams that will grace the occasion would be the teams from Hong Kong, Singapore and USA. Each fireworks performance would be 15 minutes long depending on the individual teams choreography.

    Agenda

    1 Aug 04, 8.00pm - USA Team

    8 Aug 04, 8.30pm - Singapore Team

    15 Aug 04, 8.00pm - Hong Kong Team
