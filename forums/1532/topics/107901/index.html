POEM: Prisoner of Circumstances

  • nienie
    18 Dec 04, 11:14
    Are you happy with your location?
    Are you strictly restricted to limitations?
    Are you living right in all these circumstances?
    Are you a prisoner of your own life not because you want to...
    ....but because you have to.

    A cold rush sends a shiver down my spine
    Sniffing the air, eyes closed like I'm blind
    Hands numb and relieved of feelings
    Heart strangled and hardly beating

    Body laying twisted on the freezing hard floor
    Legs only with strength, nudged myself across
    Eyes with time opened to see flickers of light
    Nose bleeding and yet smells the moisture

    Hands with time regains feelings and uses
    I stand up and trudged across empty space
    Through the darkness and flickers of light
    I felt the metal grills on the other side

    Realizations of cold hard truth strikes
    I'm but a prisoner of circumstances
