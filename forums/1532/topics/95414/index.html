    Just a minor bug for you all to notice (some of you may have noticed)

    I spotted a 'bug' in the ez-link system. My card contains a value of -$1.31 and I am aware of this all along. However, I am using Concession Pass ($52 bus only) all this while. When I tap the ez-link card, it shows me "Welcome and have a nice day".

    When I tap out before exiting the bus, it will show me my value, which is -$1.31. However, I usually don't tap out when I leave the bus, as I am using concession pass, it's not compulsory for me to tap out. This gives my card a even higher negative value. On one occassion, I did not tap out and when I top-up my card with $10, the value is shown as $7.16. This means that my card has a negative value of $2.84. From $1.31 to $2.84?

    However, after tapping the card again, my value will be restored to $8.69. This does not affect my real value in the card, but just a defect.

    The system works in a way that if you do not tap out, the system will minus a full fare on your card (it seemed to minus even for concession pass but will restore once the user tap in or out again).

    Works only for Tertiary/Sec/JC/Primary ez-link cards. Go try it.
