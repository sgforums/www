  • ChingAlvin

    Try the soft method.

    When i just got my Corgi, my sister kept on telling me, if she does bad things, cane her so that she will learn. Never did she learn after we caned her but instead, she seems defiant and peed everywhere she loved if given the chance.

    Then i started doing scold, then praise thing..

    Take for example, if she peed at some places she is not supposed to, i would take her pee or poo to the litter, with her along. Then i would take the pee soaked tissue paper and put it in the litter while she smell at it.. then i let her stayed there for some time and kept on letting her smell it, then feed her. 

    do that many times and once she starts peeing in the litter, praise her and feed her until she absolutely does it there..

     

    Barking at people part..my dog only barks at my malay neighbours too.. partly because they have got so many kids whom stomp their feet on purpose when they walk past..
