    A few years ago, maybe 5-6 years ago, when i was little a kid, a stray dog (Pomeranian cross breed i think ) jumped up onto my dad's car.     Therefore, my dad decided to keep it.
    However, my dad did not acquire any kind of license for it, nor did he went through any required procedures to officially keep a dog. Heck, i dont even know if my dog went through any sterilization or received any vaccines before we kept them.
