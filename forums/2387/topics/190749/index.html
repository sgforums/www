    These are some of the food that are harmful to our puppy:
    1. Chocolate,
    2. Grapes,
    3. Raisin,
    4. Onions,
    5. Macadamia Nuts,
    6. Xylitol (a common artificial sugar substitute used in many sugar-free candies and other recipes).

    Avoid serving Turkey and Ham as they can be serious problems for them. Turkey has high fat content and can cause acute pancreatitis while ham is high in sodium content.
