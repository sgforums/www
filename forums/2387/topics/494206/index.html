    We can all agree that as cute as they are, our loyal furry friends require a lot of work to look after. Training and grooming can be an arduous task. Even choosing the right type of food can be overwhelming. Here is a list of unique dog services that might be able to make providing quality care for your fur kids a little more convenient.

    1. The Grateful Dog

    5 Unique Dog Services in Singapore
    More

    A dog’s health is largely affected by his diet – this was the belief that this company was built upon. Rachel and Sandee wanted to provide the best care for their dogs. To combat the harmful effects of overly processed kibble and canned food, they have created their own line of home cooked dog food with high nutritional value and zero preservatives. The Grateful Dog offers different meal subscription plans that you can pick from, even providing bi-weekly home delivery services for those with small freezers.

    2. Likeable Pets

    Pet grooming is an essential part of proper pet care. But with each grooming session lasting a couple of hours, sometimes it is difficult to find time in the day for it. Likeable pets provide mobile pet services island wide, saving you time for your busy schedules by bringing the groomers, dog walkers and pet sitting right to your door step.

    3. Pawshake

    Source for a pet sitter through Paw Share just like how you search for an apartment on Air Bnb. Your preference for price and type of care required for your fur kids can be easily accounted for with the different filters. Get to know the pet sitters through their profiles and feedback given by their previous clients.

    4. Happy-Dog Training

    Singapore’s very own professional dog trainer – Mark who is certified under the association of professional dog trainers is originally from Scotland. He has brought this unique set of skills to Singapore, offering a variety of dog training classes. From basic dog training to K9 nose work scent classes, Happy Dog also does one on one home training that is fully customised to your dog’s personality.

    5. Sunny Heights

    Spend some quality time with your dogs at the swimming pool in Sunny Heights. Humans are encouraged to join the dogs in the pool! They even have a one stop dog wash machine that is fully equipped with shampoo and conditioner for your convenience. Have a taste at their dog cafes and bakeries there as well!
