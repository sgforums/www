    convert to MP3:
    if you want it fast, audiocatalyst is the fastest and easiest to use.

    If u are concerned about quality however, use EAC to extract to wav. And then encode to MP3 using the "LAME" codec.

    ----------

    MP3 -> CD: just use winamp to convert to wav, then burn to cd. Some software enables you to directly burn to cd from mp3 without manual conversion to wav, but thats not recommended if quality is of highest concern.
