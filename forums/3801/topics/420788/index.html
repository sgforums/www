Getamped 2 Review

  • Blame-on-lag
    20 Jan 11, 11:25

    GetAmped 2 is a fighting MMO set within the city of Wingdom.  Players choose from five classes, and are given a chance to try out their selection in the game’s tutorial before committing to it.  After a mock battle against their trainer, players are free to decorate their personal room, explore the various areas of the city and, most important, fight.  There are two general match types in GetAmped 2.  Those that take place in the GAT Stadium (which involve players fighting one another), and co-op missions initiated within Mega Force HQ.  GetAmped 2 is a huge improvement over its predecessor, which many may know under the name ‘Splash Fighters.’

     

    Publisher: CyberStep

    Playerbase: High

    Graphics: Low

    Type: mmo

    Exp Rate: Medium

    File size: 242MB

     

    This is a F2P (free to play mmorpg) but there's a cash shop mall for higher and advanced items

     

    Pros: +RPG elements, including player housing & story. +5 classes. +Tons of appearance customization options. +Short, rewarding tutorial.

    Cons: -Limited graphic options, low resolution. -Dated visuals. -Repetitive gameplay.

     

    The Classes for this game are:

    Soldier: Fairly good in strength but more focus on techical which is faster reloading of weapons and good in counter. 

    Fighter: The most balanced out of all classes on the stats.

    Spy: Although weak in strength, they are very swift.

    Hero: Slow but have high strength.

    Knight: Weak in attack but have high defence

     

    This game is direct installation. No other files are needed for this game to install.
