    (Based on a true and factual story in my life)

    Dear Mum,

    I just wanna say that i miss you very very much. 18 years you left abruptly, leaving me,dad and brother, yearning for your love and concern. Though i never remembered what my first words are or to who they are to, i can still remember the feeling of your hug as you took me under your wing. I remembered the vanishing glimpse of your face under your cascading tresses. 18 years may not be long for a bankrupt to rebuild his empire but it is very long for a person who longed for his mum.

    The only treasure you left me are the distant memories i had as a toddler and photographs of you carrying me around the house. You never showed me expressively how much you loved me. I never experienced motherly love before because you never gave me the chance to. I hid myself in envy during mother's day for i had no reason in the world to celebrate one. I ran away in despair during your birthday for there is no reason a birthday cake is to be bought. I hung my head in sadness during your death anniversary for now i am stepping on you instead of looking up to you in high esteem.

    You are sorely missed by me and me alone. Auntie Edlynn appeared now in the picture and she will never replace you in my heart. You are the reason why i am now sitting here trying to succeed in my life. Please come back. I really need you Mum. I love you

    Love,
    Andy
