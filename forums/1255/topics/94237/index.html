    YEAH!!!! Went for Lasik surgery on wed & fri.. and so please to know that I got back my perfect eyesight when nurse tell me during checkup...

    would like to share some info since i guess some ppl would be interested in doing it.. btw, both my eyes : myopia 300 each, astigmatism less than 100...

    I did it at SNEC (Singapore National Eye Center).. There would be a medical checkup ($160) for ur eyes to confirm the degree and thickness of cornea... my cornea kind of thinner ..

    I went for senior consultant that charge $1830 per eye that have 10 years experience min.. normal consultant charge $1350 (3~9 years)... can pay via 12 months credit card interest free installment for major credit cards...

    The doc will remove ur cornea and do the laser treatment, later put the cornea back, no pain or heat during the surgery.. heheheh, got burning smell, like having BBQ.. within 2~3 minutes, the surgery is done..

    I felt irritation and pain for both eyes last 1~2 hours after the anesthetic gone .. only some ppl will suffered it, some of those ppl that went together did not feel any pain.. depending on individual..

    Note that the eyesight will get corrected, but not to perfect.. very much depend on how high ur myopia.. got a quite cute gal having myopia 1000 each for both eyes... she get corrected to 9/6.. another one 500 each, on eye is 6/6 and the other is 9/6...

    Well.. I guess I am the lucky one that get 6/4.5 for both eyes...

    Post surgery checkup will charge abt $50 each and need to see the doc for 6 times... A bit waste of time, doc will check ur eye and said "veri good, veri good" that last 5 mins... KNS, charge $50 for that ....

    The first 2 weeks will be the most important as you cannot rub ur eyes, no soap or tap water, no swimming, avoid contact sports and glaring.. 1 week no eye makeup for gal ....

    Overall, I should spend abt $4200 to get back my perfect eyesight.. now its up to me to takecare and maintain my eyes...
