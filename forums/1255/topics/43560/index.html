    I started my first job in a small film, and I was given the opportunity for a big project~ I made a small mistake and lost the deal~~
    it was you who gave me a hand and help me up~"

    "After I lost my job and found another~, although I have never been promoted, and my bosses never ever praise me for my hard work~~ you were still so supportive and stood by my side~~"
