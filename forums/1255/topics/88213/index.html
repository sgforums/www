    My 'lazy' teacher

    I remember the days back when I was in secondary school... I was quite good in Maths (for my school's standard) and was in of the few chosen ones to represent the school for the Olympics Maths competition.. When I was there, I realised that alot of things have not been taught by our teacher yet... The paper was testing things way ahead of our schedule... In the end, most of us just left many of the questions (almost half?) BLANK...

    Discussing.. We were saying why didn't our teacher taught us beforehand or even told us that we would be tested on such..? No preparations.. Nothings... I thought.. Was our teacher was lazy or busy..? And to think that I actually respected him so much... He's always SO KEEN in teaching us MATHS... SO MANY ADDITIONAL stuffs he taught us... And his PASSION for MATHS... Why was he like that?

    Years gone by... I thought... Perhaps our teacher know that we probably won't stand a good chance in winning... Meaning he looked down on us? Why kill our chance..? At least he could have told us..??

    But well.. He didn't... I think he fully understand that we're already very busy trying to cope with our subjects... Which are mostly examinable... I remember him telling us to just go and have an 'experience' of it... He's always smiling.. So kind..

    I guess it's really for our good? We probably won't win anyway... If we're to try and take up all the last min studying, we probably won't be doing any better... Might even affect our other subjects... Might even lose our passion for Maths...

    I guess he's always him... His PASSION... His commitment... Conviction... He made me understand... 'Winning' is not everything... Yes... HE - I STILL RESPECT...
