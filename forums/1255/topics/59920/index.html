    The Asakusa Temple in Tokyo is kind of similar to the Guan Yin Temple (Goddess of Mercy) which we have in Bugis.

    Consists of 3 parts - The first, a shopping street with shops selling all sorts of japanese merchandise. The second part, a huge gate which is the entrance to the main temple and the last part, the main temple itself.

    Filled with worshippers (both japanese and foreigners) everyday, it is the most famous Buddhist temple in Tokyo.
