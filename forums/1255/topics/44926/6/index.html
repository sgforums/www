    Originally posted by Devon:
    ar..got one silly question here..wat functions must a camera have to be defined as manual, semi-auto, and auto?
    a full manual camera u have to adjust focus, aperture and shutter urself.

    semi-auto u can choose to change one or more of those settings, and the cam will choose the best settings for the rest that u didn't for you.

    full auto u dun choose anything, the cam does it for u.

    most semi-auto cams of course, have a full auto mode. the converse is not necessarily true.

    also, most semi-autos have have a full manual mode (technically), since in a semi-auto, u can set it to manual more for exposure settings (shutter and aperture) and change the lens to use MF (manual focus) mode.
