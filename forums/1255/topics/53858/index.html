    Originally posted by singtel88:
    hi i would like to ask about 28-300mm lens, is for take photo in far or long distance? now maket is selling for $300+ and is it suit for canon EOS 500N?
    Yup, 28-300 will just about cover everything from you holiday wide-angle landscape shots to your close up subjects like sports action or aircraft (my forte).

    As for whether its suitable for the EOS 500N, you'll have to check. If its made by Canon, it will most probably be suitable, but if it comes from 3rd-party lens manufacturers like Tokina, Tamron or Sigma, you'll have to check which mount its for as most SLR camera brands have their own mounts (These 3rd party guys manufacture lenses for the mounts most camera brands). Ask the seller or sales assistant at the shop if its suitable for your camera.

    If its manufactured by camera companies like Nikon, Pentax etc, then its most likely not suited for your Canon.
