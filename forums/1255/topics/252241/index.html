    A few situations that causes blurr images in pictures taken

    (1) shaky hands, not stabilized camera (camera shake)
    (2) image/ objects not in focus
    (3) slow shutter (which may lead to point #1 since its prone to have camera shake)
    (4) subject is in motion

    In night photography it is always a challenge to freeze a moving subject since the shutter will be slower as compaired today photography. To allow a faster shutter means the image will be underexposed and appear dark but you will be able to freeze it. Now newer compact digital cameras comes with better ISO settings to capture sharper images in the dark. Noise at any level can still be removed with photo editing software.
