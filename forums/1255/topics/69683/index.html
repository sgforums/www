    My Last Breath

    I stare into the dark corner of my cage,
    Drawn into it, I start to wonder
    Bracing myself against the mighty carnage,
    Finally beaten, I bleed like water

    I ran as hard as I could,
    Away from the fangs of the Gargoyle
    Only to be stopped by walls covered with rotten roots
    I was helpless and cornered
    Fight, am I able?
    Its' teeth sunk into my flesh, with the last breath I took

    I lay on the crimson pool,
    as I float away from the living
    Who will be the next fool,
    to be born into this mortal witching...

    ~ Aeon
