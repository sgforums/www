    i start mine~~~!!! It's a dream poem...

    You say I am an unreachable creature-
    Mind in the cosmos
    Impulsively dreamy...
    You say I talk too much
    About things that are unearthly
    About anecdotal accounts of my astral actions
    That has nothing to do with your wants and desired.
    You say when I am grunded
    At least you can approach me
    You can arouse and control me;
    Saving me from my madness
    That gets worked up over visions and voices.
    You call to tell me I've become too elusive-
    And though my words are uplifting
    You need a break from my muse.
    I can only reply with a gesture,
    A momentary truce from my madcap nature,
    And offer you an insight to all that is holy
    For you do have a smile I want to taste-
    So I will suspend all my madness
    And become incredibly sane
    Just for the light and the motion
    Of your unbelievable eyes.

    Cheers!

    Oh yah.. Mother's Day is coming..

    This is for my mum.. and for all the mummies in the world!!!

    Another Mother's Day is here,
    Bringing joy and pleasures new,
    On this special day, Mother dear,
    I want to remember you.
    I cannot give you costly gifts,
    And I've told you this before,
    No matter what I give to you,
    You give back much, much more.
    I'm giving you a pure, sweet rose,
    Gathered in the early morn,
    This rose you planted in my heart,
    The day that I was born.
    In kindly, loving thoughts of you,
    And with the faith you still impart,
    The rose I give to you today,
    Is the love that's in my heart.
