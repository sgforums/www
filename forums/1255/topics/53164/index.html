    as in over reducing the size of the pic?
    1280*1024 has a ratio of 5:4 whille 640*480 has a ratio of 4:3, so yes if u resize from the bigger to the smaller the picture will look slight squashed sideways.

    4R size is 6" by 4" which is a ratio of 3:2, so NONE of the above resolutions are good ahahah, if u give them the pics in those resolutions, they will either crop off to 3:2 ratio, or print until there'll be white gaps, where they'll fit the longest side onto the paper, then u crap the white parts off urself.

    so, if i were to print 4R i will resize to a 3:2 ratio and make the size 6"x4". hence i will make the picture 1800x1200 becos i want/like 300dpi. those figures will gimme exactly 6"x4"

    however u don't have to use such a high resolution, anything around 150dpi is still very good. just give them the highest then u can afford. that should be ok.
