St. Andrew's Cathedral Extension Project-Ground Breaking

  • NeonTetra
    23 Nov 03, 18:09


    The piece of land facing City Hall MRT exit and Capitol. In 15 months time, a 3 storey(one above ground, two basements) extension church building will be at this site.



    Photo taken from the North Lawn. Singapore Archaelogy Team for the past one month has excavated several historical artefacts underneath this field. Items ranging from Song Dynasty Porcelain s, 1800s coins and some head badges and decors of the colonial volunteer corps and many more are found. They are displayed for exhibition today.



    Nave of the Cathedral. The new extension will hold about 800 seats in its worship hall as well as a prayer hall, a chapel, a visitors' centre, sunken courtyard, garden and sunday school classrooms and other facilities.



    Site of Ground breaking. The Bishop,The Rt Rev Dr John Chew, Dean The Very Rev Dr John Tay and Canon Rev Ong King Ming graced the occasion and ground breaking service. 23 Nov 2003, 4pm.



    The Dean.



    On the left with blue and white vestment is Canon Ong, on the right is Bishop John Chew. Other clergy and pastoral staffs are in the background.
