    I think for tricky situations like this, nearly everyone will face.
    What I heard is, you can use program mode to gauge the metering of light, say if u point ur camera at the darker side and it reads F4, and if u point ur camera at the brighter side and it reads F8, then, u can switch to Manual mode, take the in btw. Set to F6

    This is just an example lah.

    Normally to minimise camera shake, pple tend to push the ISO to quite high when taking pictures under low light condition. If u have something to lean on or u got a tripod with u, u can set the ISO to 50. That should solve the noise problem.
    Other than that, it is not easy to over come things like that. It is not ur camera, situations like that happen all the time. It is a matter of how the photographer overcome it
