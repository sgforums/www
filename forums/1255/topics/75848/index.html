  • bismarck

    upgrade of fz1
    addition of hotshoe, manual functions and 4 MP

    other than that...it's a fz1
    problems with fz1
    digital noise even at ISO100 and ISO200
    low sensitivity to light although F2.8
    AF is slow and inaccurate unless in very good lighting
    pic quality suffers at 12x zoom
    funny pic quality when taking pics at 12x zoom under cloudy skies
    lack of an eye cup makes it a pain when taking pics under direct sunlight

    positive points
    burst mode sibei fast
    shot to shot lag minimal,except pics taken on night scene mode
    pretty good macro performance ( a contradictory to the 12x zoom)
    includes a lens hood, effective...
