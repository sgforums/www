    One Night Stood

    It was the night i met you face to face,
    an online love in a sleepless island.
    The warmth and length of your sweet embrace,
    followed by minutes of eerie silence.

    You have just known me and you are holding hands,
    in fact it has been only 4 months so far.
    Suddenly, you were overwhelmed by a moment of intense,
    The next thing i saw was the colour of your bra.

    We started making love from dusk till dawn,
    The glitter of the morning and you were gone.
    Were these sparks or ripples of my dream,
    A night of pleasure or so it seems.

    I hope you wouldn't treat me like a fool,
    I tried calling you but you weren't around.
    But there you lie on the bottom of my pool,
    Naked and dead, just totally drowned.
