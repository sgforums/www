    Rambling III

    How can someone miss someone whom they've only known for less than a month??? It seems quite ridiculous, doesn't it? Would you know when you meet the right one for you?? Would you feel that 'zing!' feeling or you only realise later??

    I went for a movie with my best friend, watched 'Anger management', after that we were walking from Cineleisure to Tanglin Mall, all the way talking... We realised that we didn't really know much guys, and even the guys we knew, none were the kind who would just come out and meet us at 12 am to just hang... I'm sure if we were overseas, our friends would be more than willing to jump out of their houses to meet us on a Friday night!! Doesn't that just show how boring guys in Singapore are?? Or is it not the people but the place? Does Singapore make us boring?

    We're both 21, or rather both going to be in July, so we are allowed to feel like going out every night to party right? Or is it just that we know the wrong kind of friends? How do you know what kind of friend is right for you? Is it if they don't smoke, don't drink, don't give you bad advise? But if that's so, won't they be angels? Are friends meant to be our guardian angels??

    I think I'm not making sense, must be the popcorn...
