    The Lumix Award 2004
    The unique features of digital cameras allow you to throw off the restrictions of conventional film and introduce you to a whole new way of taking photographs.

    It's easier to get the right shot or capture that moment because you can repeat your shots, keep them or delete them. Add to that the photo manipulation possibilities when you transfer your shots to a PC and you have more control and more expressive photography.

    The Lumix Award aims to promote creativity in digital photography.
