    Hi NeonTetra. Could you please tell this old fogey here what entails Life Sciences at NUS? Is it under the Science Fac? Do you get a degree in Life Sciences or just Science (Life Sciences). What kind of modules do you study? TIA.

    2. Molecular cell biology

    3. Biology
