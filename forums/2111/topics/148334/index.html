    There are 2 phases of RT, Phase 1 is 4 weeks with 2 sessions each week (1 weekday and 1 weekend), if you pass IPPT at the end of Phase 1 (Session , you will be dischaged from RT, and the IPPT for that year will be considered as cleared.

    If you fail, you will proceed to Phase 2, 4 weeks with 3 sessions each week. There will be another IPPT test at the end of Phase 2 2nd week (Session 14), if you pass, you be discharged, IPPT for that year will be considered as cleared; if fail, continue with another 2 weeks.

    The last IPPT test will be at the end of Phase 2 4th week (Session 20), if you pass, IPPT for that year will be considered as cleared; if fail, you will need to book for another IPPT test before your next birthday, if you fail to book, your unit can charge you.

    You cannot miss ANY session, you will not be allowed to take IPPT if you do not attend the required number of sessions. If you missed one, you will need to arrange for a make-up session. And you can only do up to 2 make-up sessions (Phase 1 and 2 combine). Your RT will be defered if you request to do the 3rd make-up session, and you'll need to start from Phase 1 again even you are already in your Phase 2.
