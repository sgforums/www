    This is very common. Esp famous retail shops like See Seng, Heng Choon, etc. Many shops at Sim Lim do that, too. Pearl centre, as well. It's best to check out the prices before you decide which shop to buy from. A particular shop at Pearl Centre, name "Dr. Mobile", offers pretty attractive prices for certain models, but for some not so high demand models, the salesperson there actually charges alot more, like $100 more than the other shops. I kana once there, as I was outside when my mobile phone was spoilt, and I urgently needed a phone. That one lesson there taught me to be cool and cautious. Also, other than checking out the price, check what comes with the package, as well. Better to be safe than sorry.
