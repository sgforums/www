    SINGAPORE - Samsung has announced that the registration of interest for its Galaxy Note 3 will begin on Sept 7, at 12am.

    You can register with the local telcos here:-

    M1: www.m1.com.sg/galaxynote3 
    SingTel: www.singtel.com/galaxynote3 
    StarHub: www.starhub.com/galaxynote3

    Alternatively, you can also visit the following Samsung Experience stores on the same day during opening hours to pre-order a unit of the Galaxy Note 3 (without contract) by placing a $50 deposit:

    313@ Somerset #B2-24/25/26 • AMK Hub #B1-37/38 • Bedok Point #03-21 • Bugis Junction #01-54 • Century Square #04-02A• Causeway Point #03-12 • Chinatown Point #01-48 • Great World City #02-39 • Jurong Point #B1-09/10/11 • Marina Bay Sands #B2-61• nex Mall #04-42 • The Paragon #B1-27/27A • Plaza Singapura #B2-23 • Parkway Parade #02-32 • Tampines Mall #02-29 • Vivo City #02-28/29

    Customers will receive a Galaxy Note 3 limited edition accessory pack (worth $146) consisting of a Desktop Dock and Extra Battery Kit, while stocks last, when they collect their Samsung Galaxy Note 3. They will also enjoy $100 off the retail price of Galaxy Gear when they purchase the mobile device.

    More information will be shared on the Samsung Mobile Facebook page from Sept 7.
