    8. Shake your mobile phone to make a call
    Reason for selecting: from identifying texts to knowing sign language, the IQ of mobile phones has achieved great leap


    By shaking a mobile phone up and down twice, spam calls and messages can be deleted; and speed dial can be fulfilled by finger painting numbers on the monitor. In early 2005, South Korea’s Samsung Electronics announced that they have developed the world’s first mobile phone that can identify continuous movement.
    It is said that this mobile phone had adopted the up-to-date technology of identifying continuous movements. The user only has to shake the cell phone to delete spam messages, to hang off spam calls, to achieve speed dial and generate sound effects automatically.
