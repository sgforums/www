    For a moment there, I thought this was another thread dedicated to people who have problems with the n80 battery life. I have a few suggestions to help alleviate the battery drain.

    1) Don't switch on 3G.
    2) Don't use the wifi.
    3) Don't leave the phone on when you sleep at night.
    4) Don't switch on the display to the brightest setting.
    5) Don't use the power save mode, the blinking blue light consumes more energy contrary to your thinking.
    6) Don't use the n80 on a warm day, your phone feels laggier. Laggier phones use more energy.
    7) Don't leave the phone uncharged at all times.
    Don't be stingy and buy a spare battery.
    9) Don't forget to bring the spare battery out, if possible, bring all 3.
