    Samsung Galaxy Note 8 – You can start registering interest at all 3 telcos now

    Plus get a FREE DeX Station

    The Samsung Galaxy Note 8 is finally here and the things Samsung squeezed into this phone is just quite impressive.

    Some say this might just be the perfect Note smartphone the company ever created, but one thing is for sure – no more explosion jokes on this one.

    For those who will be waiting to get your hands on one, you better start registering your interest with your telco. The reason is that all 3 of them, Singtel, Starhub and M1, will be giving away a free DeX station (worth $198) when you do so now.

    Samsung DeX station converts your smartphone to a desktop computer

    What you will be able to do with the Samsung DeX station is the ability to convert your smartphone into a desktop computer, somewhat like Google’s Chrome OS found on Chromebooks.

    Although it won’t be Samsung’s most expensive accessory, it’s still a nifty gadget to have around or sell it away if you absolutely don’t need it.

    The Samsung Galaxy Note 8 is expected to arrive on 15 September later next month. You can register with Singtel here, Starhub here and M1 here.
