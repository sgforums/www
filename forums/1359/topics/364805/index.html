    Learn Symbian

     

    Want to be a mobile application developer?

     

    Learn Symbian, the most widely used mobile operating system in handsets worldwide.

     

    Mobile apps are increasingly being used to educate, inform & entertain us on the move. Mobile app-stores are being built by telecom operators & handset manufacturers. Many Startups & SMEs are addressing the growing demand for innovative mobile apps, not just in Singapore, but all over the world.

    Here is your chance to ride this big wave of opportunity!


    Join us on a 3-day 'Boot-camp' to cut your teeth on Symbian, and stand apart in this fascinating business.

    What you will learn?

    Symbian OS basics
    Developing with Carbide C++
    Memory management
    Application Structure
    Resource & localization
    Client/server & Active Object framework
    50% theory & 50% hands-on lab-work

    Who can join?

    Besides a passionate interest in developing mobile apps, you should have basic C++ programming experience, a good grasp of object-oriented programming principles, and a familiarity with UI & usability principles of developer platforms.

    This boot-camp will be facilitated by an experienced Nokia/Symbian Certified Professional with extensive hands-on developing & training experience.

    Dates : 23rd- 25th July (3 full days). 9am to 6pm

    Venue : In Singapore. Will be confirmed upon registration.

    Training manual & course-ware will be provided. But please come with your laptop, as you’d want to practice on your very own gear.

    Course Fees : S$ 1,750 (incl. GST). 10% discount on group registrations (2 or more).
