    Why am I unable to send SMS?

    You will not be able to send SMS if you have not set up the Message Centre number. This set-up process is a one-time effort.  Generally, the steps are as follows. Please use them together with your hand phone manual if necessary:

    STEPS:

    1. Enter Menu Mode on your hand phone
    2. Select "Messages" or "Mail"
    3. Select "Message Setting"
    4. Select "SMS Centre", "Message Centre Number", "Route Centre" or it equivalent
    5. Enter Message Centre Number:
      +65 9619 7777 (GSM user)
      +65 9650 0001 (GSM1800 user)
      Please refer to your handphone manual on how to enter the international prefix '+'.
    6. Confirm selection
    7. Select "Exit"/"Quit"

    ** Depending on your handphone model, "Message Setting" could be "Mail Setting" or "Message Editor".
