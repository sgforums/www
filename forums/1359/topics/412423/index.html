    To answer your questions

    Android is an Operating System. Just like Windows XP, Mac OS or Windows Vista is for PC, Android is an OS for phone. The latest Android version is Froyo 2.2.1.

    I would not say android is better than an iphone because each has it own pros and cons.

    If you buy an Android phone, there is an app called "Market" in it. When u enter, there are tons of free apps inside for you to download. The paid version is coming soon to Sg. But there are always ways to get free paid apps without the need to root your phone.

    A few of the awesome apps that are free are
    Google Map, Google Latitude (you get to see where your freinds are on the map), Voice dialer, Gesture Search, Barcode scanner, Starhub TV (you get to watch free History channel and National Geographic if you are starhub user), Ringdroid and more.

    Hope this help clarify.
