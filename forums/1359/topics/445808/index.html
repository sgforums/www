    SINGAPORE: SingTel is offering customers free mobile calls, SMS and MMS as part of Lunar New Year celebrations.

    It'll give 48 hours of free local mobile calls, SMS and MMS between 12.00 am on January 23 and 11.59 pm on January 24.

    SingTel said these can be enjoyed by postpaid mobile customers.

    They'll not be deducted from the free bundled minutes and SMS included in their current service plans.
