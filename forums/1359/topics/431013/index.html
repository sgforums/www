Free Calls For M1 Subscribers Tomorrow

  • granslime

    SINGAPORE - MOBILE service provider M1 will be providing free local mobile calls, SMS and MMS to all customers on 25 May 2011.

    The company will be extending this free service as a form of goodwill, in light of the recent unexpected technical failure on a card in one of their customer management servers.

    A number of M1 customers were unable to connect to their network on 18 May 2011 due to this disruption.

    This offer will commence from 12am to 11.59pm of 25 May 2011.

    The free calls, SMS and MMS will not be deducted from the free minutes and messages in customers' existing mobile service plans.

    M1 also indicated that they will work to further improve the robustness of their network.
