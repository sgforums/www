Singtel FREE Unlimited Mobile Data SG50 Promo 7-10 Aug

  • FireIce
    16 Jul 15, 17:02

     

    This benefit is offered to all Singtel Mobile Postpaid customers, including Corporate Individual Scheme (CIS) and corporate mobile customers, all of whom must use the APNs listed below (“Eligible Customers”).

    Eligible Customers can enjoy free unlimited local data on 7 Aug 2015 to 10 Aug 2015. This benefit starts from 00:00hrs on 7 Aug 2015 and ends at 23:59hrs (Singapore Time) on 10 August 2015 (“Benefit Period”).

    If your mobile price plan comes with a local data bundle, this means that your data usage during the Benefit Period will not be deducted from your bundle.

    If you are not on a data-bundled plan, you will still get to enjoy this benefit during the Benefit Period by using the APNs mentioned below.

    For Postpaid mobile customers, you will need to access local mobile data (2G/3G/4G) through e-ideas/ Internet/ BlackBerry APNs. These are the default APNs on your device if you have not changed your settings.

    If you are not on a data-bundled plan, you will still get to enjoy this benefit during the Benefit Period by using the APNs mentioned above.

    As your local data usage is waived during the Benefit Period, you will not see any clocked usage during that period on your My Singtel App and on your monthly bill.
