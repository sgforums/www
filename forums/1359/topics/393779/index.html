    1. Most likely if you turn off your phone, you will have to jailbreak to activate it again.  This is called "teathered jailbreak".  The only way around this is if you get a 2nd hand or a refurbished iphone 3GS where it was produced before a certain date.  Search teathered jailbreak in google to find out more.  Also, if you get an iphone now, most likely it already comes with firmware 3.1.3 - those can't be jailbreak'd as for now.

    2. You can have two sim cards, but I believe you are charged on a monthly basis for this.

    3. Never tried, but call diverting should be available.
