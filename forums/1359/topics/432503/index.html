    HK Racing Infocast

             

    ** Real-time odds from HKJC together with detailed racing information and live race broadcast!** (Eng version is available)

    1. Odds table: W/P, Q/QP, Trio & Double etc and odds change indicators in familiar HKJC presentation

    2. Exclusive functions:
    -Real-time updates of Jockey and Trainer Standings with horse exit, replacement and result
    -Keep track of Q & QP odds changes to find out the best time of place your bet with brown indicators
    -Replay video database: Previous race replay and parade ring replay (Both in Chi only)
    -Real-time updates of race status (from parade ring, before the gate, entering the gate, race starts , race finished to weighted in)

    3. HD Live Race Broadcast & Audio Broadcast. Exclusive commentary

    4. Support iPhone, Android handsets.

    5. All information transmitted via Wi-Fi or any 3G mobile networks whether you are in HK or overseas
