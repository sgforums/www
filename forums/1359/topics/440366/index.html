    SINGAPORE: iPhone 4S will be available at SingTel stores in Singapore from Friday.

    SingTel announced on Tuesday that it is making the latest iPhone model available at its retail stores here starting at S$0 for the 16GB and 32GB models and S$108 for the 64GB model with monthly subscription plans.

    Customers can also go online at www.singtelshop.com/iphone to make an appointment from Wednesday to pick up their iPhone 4S.

    A special launch event for the iPhone 4S will also be held at Suntec Convention Hall 402 on Friday.
