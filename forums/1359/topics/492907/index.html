StarHub is offering 6GB mobile plan trial to youths for free

  • FireIce
    2 Nov 16, 16:09

    Youths between 18 to 24 years of age have a new way to save on their mobile plans with StarHub’s 4G 3 plan catered for the data-hungry.

    Now the important question is what will it give you? It’s got 6GB of data for all your Snapchat-ing and Instagram-ing needs, 150 minutes of talktime for when you’re not dialing over data, and unlimited SMS (if you’re still into that). The best thing is you get to see if it fits your needs by trying it out for three months free as long as you have a valid student or 11B ID.

    And if you’re not feeling it at any point of time, you can cancel your subscription or choose to continue at S$21.45 a month (SIM only) or S$42.90 a month with a subsidised phone of your choice. Both are for the period of 24 months.

    If you’re between plans at the moment, there's no harm in climbing onboard. 

    image: http://images.cdn.stuff.tv/sites/stuff.tv/files/styles/big-image/public/brands/starhub_youth_plan.png?itok=tU_4QWp3

    On top of that, you also get access to free classes ranging from dance to fitness and even coffee appreciation. That BBC Journalism workshop might even net you a free trip to London to witness the newsroom action. There are 800 slots opened, which are not a lot, so if you want to get in on the activities over your holidays, you can sign up here.
