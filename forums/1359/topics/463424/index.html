    Hi,

    regarding the layout of your homescreen, maybe you can try the various launchers available on the play store. the Xperia "skin" is designed in that way and the only way to change it to how you were used to on your iPhone is to download a launcher. you can take a look at the reviews of some of the best launchers in the play store now: http://www.android.gs/best-android-launchers-of-2012/

    for grouping of apps, the only way you can do it is by tapping on the "four mini square" on the top right hand corner of the app menu (right next to "arrange" app order). you will see the apps jiggle and you can manually shift them one by one to the page that you want to.

    regarding the charger problem. the "charging" light will go away when you get a notification. instead it will blink a light based on the notification type (this can be set, i.e. whatsapp allows you to choose colors)

    hope this helped! and if you want to know more about your Xperia, you can check out our site at http://knowyourxperia.wordpress.com. Cheers! =)
