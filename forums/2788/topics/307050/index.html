Depression mainly come from 2 causes: "Not knowing your place in society and having too much choice causes stress." - Professor Ronald Duman of Yale University Brain and Depression New research discover that stress can cause a reduction in size of up to 15 percent in a brain region called hippocampus (which produce new brain cells). Amygdala, which controls fear and anxiety is founded to be enlarged in some depressed patients. Abnormally small nerve cells are found in Prefrontal Cortex and disturbed thought patterns. Dream and Depression Excessive dreaming used up a lot of energy in the brain and can disturb sleep patterns, which in turn can lead to depression. We need deep sleep (slow-wave stage 4 sleep) which is the part that physically restores us. Wealth and Depression Research seems to suggest that once we have lifted ourselves out of poverty, further rises in income make no difference to our sense of well-being, maybe even less happy. Darkness and Depression Cold dark winter is well known for causing depression. Sunlight and bright lights can cure depression, but too much of it will excite our body from having sufficient rest.
