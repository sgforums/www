ZoukOut on 10 Dec - Any Party Goers?

  • Turbulent Times
    28 Nov 11, 23:39

    Zoukout is on the 10th December at Siloso Beach, and thanks to my boss's persistent, fluctuating, demands and amnesia, add that with clients nail-biting as the globe slowly falls into chaos, it completely slipped my mind. Yeah, the crisis is probably going to hit our bank accounts soon, so before that happens and we starting cutting up our credit cards, we should be heading over to the beach, pour a wide range of colorful poisons down our throat, and bump bodies with the other youthful maniacs.

    I realise it's late and wondering if we can still get the tickets in. Been there last year and the year before, and it's the one time, I am away from my group of regulars, that allows me to drink till I puke by the side of the beach and cheer my glasses to new strangers, ending up in random groups of faces I had never seen before.

    But I don't even have a group, wait, I don't even have a buddy. I am opening this up until next Sunday and once we have confirmed participants to our group, I will buy the tickets for all of us. I don't have a maximum number yet but the more the merrier?

    Yeah, the above may come across as somewhat immature and youthful. All things aside, I am actually one of em yuppies who works harder than I ever play, can clock in at the office up to 4 am, 7 days straight, and at the right places and time I am the 'Mr Congenial Professional'. But this is Zoukout and it is one of those nights, we should really forget who we are, where we are from and what we do,
    and just party and go wild, like there is no tomorrow, cause when tomorrow comes in we are back to our normal, daily, routine.

    Email me with details and I will start registering.

    Because I will buy the tickets for all of you beforehand, please please please be serious and sincere. Either you are coming or you are not. No last minute stuff 'Oh hey my cat caught on fire'

    Caveat: If you are wondering, hey maybe this is a Caucasian with a deep accent and Spartan bod. Nope I am not. I am your Average Joe in looks, but hey, I am nice and love parties, :)
