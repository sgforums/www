  • Kyndred

    Take the effort to look good....don't go over the top with it....just look presentable....

    Take the effort to plan the date out....no need for every single detail to be picture perfect....but just to show that thought was put in to the planning....

    Take the effort to LISTEN when conversing.....show an interest....if the topic gets a little boring, spice it up a little...but never put her/him down even if it was meant to be a joke....

    I repeat....make the effort.....cuz thats what really shows on a first date.

    Remember that the other person is as nervous as you are.....take him/her off the pedestal you have them on in your mind and interact with them at eye level....Be yourself....Make them like you as you are....

    Above all....have fun....a person who knows how to have fun is fun to be with.
