    GREAT THINGS YOU CANNOT FIND IN REAL LIFE:

    LET’S BE A BLOGGER

                                                                by annisa

     

    If you feel your real life is so miserable, you perhaps can give a try to live a life as a blogger on internet. Why? There are so many good things that you impossibly have it in your real life.

    Do you wanna some easy to find proofs? First of all, the facilities on internet allow you to keep the person you despise from entering your site. If  you  happen to despise a certain person on internet, perhaps  the person is so annoying, or always get your nerve, all you have to do just click the word block and the person cannot visit your blog anymore. That’s very simple just like snapping your fingers. But in real life, you cannot just send away any person you resent just like that. Very often, you have to put up with the person you don’t like since there are things that do not allow you to escape or, on the other hand, make the person away from your life. It’s too bad actually that, in real life, you do not have that kind of tool on a sidebar ready to click J So… try to be patience to everybody and be nice to everyone....

    The last but not the least, when things tend to get worse because something you said, or something you did not mean to offend but someone gets hurt, or because you were just broken hearted, or your life romance does not work well on internet (since you expected a person on internet to be the one), all you have to do just lose you life on internet. It’s painless and no hustle. How come? Because all you have to do just permanently delete your site by clicking the icon on the tool bar and taraaa…. Uncle Google will automatically tell everyone who tries to find you that your site does not exist anymore. Case is closed and you can start a new life with a new site under new name. What a beautiful life, right? 

    You can start your new life on internet as soon as you feel much better about things you’ve been upset about.  You can always reincarnate yourself under any other name completed with your new home and new concept. That’s definitely the thing you impossibly do in your real life since life teaches you to be tough with everything you go through. Life also teaches you to be responsible with everything you do. Life makes your character mature. Hardship in life very often can turn people into wisdom. Life is certainly the best teacher ever. As good “teacher”, they will never ground you for good or give you rewards all the time. Life will certainly give you ups and downs. So…never impulsively grab your bag and leave your “teacher” irresponsibly by taking your life just because sometimes in our life things are not too favorable to you..
