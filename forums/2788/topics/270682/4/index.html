    Having a crush and constantly having crushes is good. It helps you feel alive and give life meaning (not only of course, just one of the ways to help give life meaning).

    Having a crush is bad if you do not handle it well.

    Not handling it well includes:

    1. Imagining a long term outcome from short term crushes
    2. Letting your short term crushes interfere with your long term commitments

    Crushes are fun and create lots of positive feelings in you as long as you understand that they are fleeting and "for fun"; not for long term relationships.
