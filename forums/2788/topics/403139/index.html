    NEVER invest your heart, time, money or energy with a woman who is not interested in you. Although this might seem like common sense, in reality we always see the exact opposite scenario. Love is a two way street. A one sided affair will only be just that, one sided.

    Men have been brainwashed by mainstream media into thinking that he should have perseverance/determination when pursuing a woman he is interested in. We see it all the time in cinemas and on TV. The usual story goes like this. A guy goes after a lady he is interested in. After being rejected for the first time, he refuses to give up. He thinks of ways and means to win her heart. He starts to do various nice things for her in a bid to win her over. And after a while, the lady finally falls for him due to all his efforts. This is classic brainwashing because this is not how it works in reality. And every time men watch these scenarios in movies and drama serials, we begin to think that this is how it’s supposed to be. It can’t be further from the truth. This bullshit out there is seriously ruining our chances with women and is counter-productive. If the lady is interested in the guy, she will display certain signs. If she is not, then she is not. No matter what you do, she will never be interested in you. Period.

    Men need to learn to read signs of interest. When you see the red man on the traffic light, you do not cross the road. When you see the green man, you cross. Men are crossing even when the red man is on. It’s suicide, my fellow brothers. Here are some green signs you can rely on to gauge her interest in you.

    Before you approach her, you notice that she is repeatedly looking at your direction and checking you out. Some girls who are shy or nervous might resort to stealing glances instead. When you approach her to initiate small talk, her eyes seem extra bright and she’s all smiles. Some of them will try very hard to contain their high interest in you but it will show through their nervousness. When talking to her, the conversation is flowing freely. Her responses are NOT short or one word answers or utter silence. After 5-8 minutes of small talk, you ask her for her number. She gives it to you WITHOUT hesitation. She doesn’t come up with some stupid excuse like “Why don’t you give me your number instead”, “I don’t have a phone”, “I don’t even know you well”, “We’ve only spoken for 5 minutes”. The list goes on, but I think you guys get the drift here. As long she does not give you her number, she has absolutely no interest in you. It’s time to move on to the next girl! On very rare occasions there might be exceptions, but she will still offer some other contact alternative such as email address with enthusiasm and without you asking for it.

    When you ask her out for a specific date, such as Tuesday 8pm Marina Square Starbucks, she agrees or counters with another specific date and time if she cannot make it on your proposed date. If she does not counter with another specific date and time, it’s a sign of lack of interest in you. It’s time to delete her number and hustle other girls. If she agrees and subsequently breaks the date on the actual day few hours before with some stupid excuse like her sister is giving birth and she needs to be at the hospital, it means lack of interest. It’s time to delete her number and hustle other girls. The exception is when she breaks the date DAYS IN ADVANCE and counter offers another specific date and time to make up for it.

    When you finally meet her on the actual date, her eyes are still bright and she’s still all smiles. Conversation is still flowing freely. She will tend to ask you a lot questions that are too personal for a friend. Do note that she should not be asking you questions in an interrogative manner. You will know because it will feel like you are being grilled by the police at Criminal Investigation Department. She laughs at your corny jokes. She does NOT offer to pay for her own share of drinks/food. She will create chances to accidentally touch or bump into you. She mentions the future with you included. Like “I had fun, hope to see you again” or “My favorite band is playing next Saturday, would you like to come with me to watch them perform”.

    If you are getting none of the green signs and full of red signs, you better get out of there and start hustling other women. NEVER invest your heart, time, money or energy with a woman who is not interested in you.
