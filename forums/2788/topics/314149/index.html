    Singles Club and House Rules

    About us~
    First of all, welcome to Singles Club.
    This is a place which is made up of mostly Singles out there and even those attach and married.
    This club is open for all...

    This club is a place where we plan outing and have funs...
    This is not a match making club.
    Despo are NOT welcome here.

    Now...

    The House Rules...

    First Rule
    Threads that are not allowed.
    Simple, basic sgForums rules.
    All moderator have the rights to close any threads they find it unsuitable.

    Second rule.
    All moderators here have the rights to edit any post if they find it unsuitable.
    Posts with intention to flame will be edited.
    Posts which moderators find unsuitable will be edited.
    If you don't want your post to be edited, think before you post.
    All moderators here have the rights to edit any post if they find it unsuitable.

    Third.
    Contacts of other forumies
    Giving out contacts of other forumies without their permission is NOT ALLOWED and regardless in msn, other forum, this is not allowed.
    Also, do not post your own contact number as you might recieve a lot of spam.
    Contacts that have been given to moderator for outing purpose or some other reason will not be given out to anyone else.
    If you want someone contact number/msn email/friendster address/blog or whatever, PM the person. Don't ask around.

    Fourth
    Outing's photographs.
    Any photographs of fellow forumnies should not be posted in the forum.
    If you want to see how other forumnies look like, go for the outings.
    This is just to be fair for all.

    Fifth
    Thank you thread.
    Strictly only to be open by ORGANISER or else will be close without warning.

    Sixth
    Only the moderator are allowed to organise outings
    Unless you are appointed by the moderator, you are not suppose to organise an outing.
    Unless you are given the permission to organise an outing, you are not suppose to organise one.
    Failing to tell the moderator before having an outing will result in closing or total deleting of thread without warning.
    The more you join our outings, the more the moderator trust you in organising an outing...





    Outing and gatherings...

    We will go out if we are free...
    There will be, Official Outings or just a small gathering...

    Outings. (Official)
    All official outings will have a thread made and information on the outings can be found there. It will be sticky.
    Outing can be of any types...
    A few hours outing, a full day outing, overnight outings, or even travelling outside the country...
    Again, new or old, all is welcome to our outing.
    *Only Moderator are allowed to organise an outing or otherwise appointed someone else.
    *Thank you thread is strictly ONLY to be started by organiser. Otherwise would be appointed or thread close.
    If you have a suggestion for an outing, feel free to contact the moderators. The moderator may let you be the organiser only after they feel that the particular outing will work out and details are out.

    Gatherings. (Unofficial)
    Gathering is just like an outing but we will keep in small or we will keep it only within those who frequent Singles club.
    They may and may not have a thread.
    If we do not have a thread, most likely the moderator will contacts those who normally frequent Singles Club and will contact via handphone/msn...
    There will not be a Thank you thread for this.


    Enjoy your stay with Singles club. ;)



    NOTE: While meeting new friends in this forum, please remember that the person may not be what she/he describe himself as and may have motive in that meeting. Please excercise caution when meeting someone new. Our outings and gathering will NEVER have only two person.
