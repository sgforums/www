    Hello Spanish person! I've been here since 2001, and have very few local friends.  Meeting locals is extremely difficult and they are VERY reserved and parochial. It's just the way it is. You need about 5 years of something in common- such as a classroom, platoon or a undergraduate programme; where you see them on a regular basis (though without talking most of the time) and that's how friendship with locals develops.

    As an immigrant from the UK, i've also found it hard to make friends here but not entirely impossible. Good luck.
