    Hi guys and gals,

    I will be posting the rules and guidelines for this forum very shortly.

    As there were unpleasantries that happened before in SGforums, I sincerely hoped that it won't happen in Singles Club.

    Do bear with me for awhile, as I am new in this position. So I need some time to come up with the rules and guidelines for everyone.

    Let's make this forum a place where Singles come together and mingle as friends in the healthy way.

    Thanks.

    Rules

    First rule
    Forum will not take the blame if anything happen during meet up with forumies.
    While meeting new friends in this forum, please remember that the person may not be what she/he describe himself as and may have motive in that meeting. Please excercise caution when meeting someone new.
    Forum official outing will not have just 2 person involved.

    Second rule.
    All moderators here have to right to edit any post if they find it unsuitable.
    Things like flames and post with Motive here will be deal with.
    If you find that your post is unfairly edited, please feel free to PM the moderator.

    Third.
    All is welcome whether single or not to this forum.
    Everyone will be welcome as this forum is meant to makes friends for those lonely singles out there!

    Fourth.
    Threads that is not allowed.
    Simple, basic sgForums rules.
    No cross posting, Go correct forum to post.
    Anything Adult or Sexual, should be posted in Bar and not here.
    Jokes allowed ^^

    Fifth.
    Contacts of other forumies
    Giving out contacts of other forumies without their permission is NOT ALLOWED and regardless in msn, other forum, this is not allowed.
    Also, do not post your own contact number as you might recieve a lot of spam.
    Contacts that have been given to moderator for outing purpose or some other reason will not be given out to anyone else.
    If you want someone contact number/msn email/friendster address/blog or whatever, PM the person. Don't ask around

    Sixth
    Photographs
    Any photographs of fellow forumnies shld not be posted in the forum without approval. Please observe that some people like to have their privacy.

    7th
    Thank you thread.
    Strictly only to be open by ORGANISER or else will be close without warning.

    8th
    Outings, organiser please ask the Moderator before organising...
    This is to prevent clash of outings.
    Outing thread will be close or deleted if without approval of Mod.
