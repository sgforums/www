    Little lecture on hamachi for people who never heard it before...

    Hamachi, something that let you connect to your friends over the internet( sometimes state as LAN).

    Download it, install it...
    Where to download? google it yourself, above got link liao...

    Hamachi works when a person creates a network, then other will join the network.
    Your need both the network name and network password to join.

    For singles club network,
    Network name: singlesclub
    Password : 1234

    Then you will see all those who is added the or online...

    To play CS together with your friends, both parties must be in a same network and online in the network.
    Open CS and go to the 'LAN' and you should be able to see your friend if he has created a game in CS.
    Due to technical problems, a number of people can't see their friends room..
    But there is another way...

    Copy the IP address of your friend who has created the room. It can be found in hamachi, just right click and press 'copy address'
    The go to CS and paste the address on 'favorite' and you should be able to see the room.

    Others function of Hamachi,
    Chat> Just right click on the name and press chat.
    Block> To block the user you want.
    Evict> to kick the person in the network ( Only the person who create the network can used this.)
    Go offline> to go offline totally in a network.

    well, those are the basic...

    Happy CS-ing...
