    Softwares that make you Sleep

    Posted by boon hong on Feb 24, '08 9:21 PM for everyone

    Am I getting old? I have difficulty keeping myself awake, especially after an heavy meal. I'm often restless yet unable to get to sleep. Chronic fatigue syndrome? Can computer help? Well, I came across this interesting article: http://www.wired.com/software/softwarereviews/news/2007/07/sleepsoftware

    It introduced 3 software to generate sound that will help us relax and sleep:

    1. http://www.pzizz.com/ This is a paid software but there is 5min free trial.
    2. http://uazu.net/sbagen/ This is free and I love the water soundtrack, very calming.
    3. http://gnaural.sourceforge.net/ This is also free and it can generate noise that make you sleepy. You can export it and burn into AudioCD

    As I read up more, I found a website with really interesting articles based on buddhism philosophy: http://www.centerpointe.com/articles/

    It talks about how our mind tried to make sense of our world by filtering, organizing and interpreting what we see, hear etc, and how meditation helps to quiet our mind so that we can bypass our mind to see reality as it is, instead of a worldview that is created by our mind.

    There's also another interesting site, the faq: http://www.monroeinstitute.com/content.php?content_id=35
