    Assuming you are just talking about happiness with respect to singlehood, how happy you are as a single will depend on how strong your desires to attachement/relationship is.

    Thus,

    1. If you have no desire to be attached, then you will always be happy.

    2. If you have a small desire to be attached, then you will be happy most of the time.

    3. If you have a strong desire to be attached, then you will be unhappy most of the time.

    4. If you have a very strong desire to be attached, then you will be depress all the time.

    All of us starts at level 1 (like when we are just a baby, no attachment, but always happy), but as we grow older, our desires will start to increase depending what kind of environment we grew up in.

    Controling ones desire for attachment is difficult. Although I underdstand that no desire for attchement is the best , I still have desire to get attached.
