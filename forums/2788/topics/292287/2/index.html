    It depends on which stage of your life you are in.

    If you almost or around my age, meaning 30, I will go for the overseas opportunity but that is provided, it is a value adding opportunity. In Dubai, there are plenty of good jobs for people from countries like Singapore because there are plenty of developments esp in their infrastructure and business framework sector. B2B sector is booming. I would have moved there if there is an opportunity for FMCG sector for expat packages. But are these jobs value adding for you? It is not just a matter of moving overseas for the sake of getting the overseas experience. Many people jumped at the chance of working overseas and end up worse there. Are you ready for it? Will that experience add value to yourself, not just to look good in your resume but the actual experience you will get?

    If not, you are better off, working and studying in Singapore. Overseas work is not something you want to consider as just "One of the options". It involves too much.
