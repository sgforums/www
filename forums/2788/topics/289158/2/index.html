    ... Seriously speaking. If you only know your Sun sign and not all the rest (rising sign, moon sign and planets signs), you can never see the "big picture".

    Sun signs are just the beginning of astrology. Every one of the signs appears somewhere on your birth chart, and every sign is a part of who you are in some way. You may want to see the "big picture" because there is a whole lot more to you than just your Sun sign.

    Your ascendant, or rising sign, is the sign that was rising over the horizon at the moment of your birth. This sign is your outward manifestation, the "you" that the outside world perceives. Usually, your Sun sign and your rising sign are different, which is why your rising sign is perceived as a "mask" most of the time, hiding your true self, your Sun sign.

    The Moon is the third part of of the most important triangle of astrology: the combination of Sun sign, ascendant and Moon sign. Moon, the closest to Earth in the sky, represents what could be called your feminine side. Your moon sign reveals your emotional nature, in other words, shows how you feel.

    Anyway, my Sun sign is Gemini.
