    Objectives

    1. To promote inter-cultural/racial/religious interaction.

    2. To encourage mutual understanding and respect among one another

    3. To eventually reach out to the rest of the world in understanding and accepting common values of being a human, regardless of language, race or religion.

    Rules and Regulations

    1. You can disagree, but do not challenge over-aggressively or persistently ask questions which only furthers your own real intentions to mock or insinuate that the religions of others are superstitions, and/or its followers or members are delusional or mindless.

    2. If you are not able to come here with the sole intention to learn about other races/cultures/religions with an open mind and humble attitude, please refrain (or best totally avoid) from making comments that disturb or provoke others unnecessarily. The best option is still to keep quiet if you have nothing positive or constructive to contribute.

    Doubts are ok to express, but remember to do so sensitively, tactfully, respectfully.

    3. Moderators reserve the right to edit or delete totally any threads/comments deemed unsuitable without prior notice or explanation. But generally,warnings (max two) would first be given should it come to such a stage.

    The above are still works-in-progress. Will be continuously reviewed as and when necessary by the moderators, who will try to consider the feedback of the community. But decisions by moderators are final.
