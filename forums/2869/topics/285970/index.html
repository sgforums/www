    According to Trust Central, Harmony Circle of the Central Singapore Community Development Council (CDC), there are 10 official religions in Singapore.

    Extracted from the website:

    Singapore recognises 10 faiths as its official religions : Baha'i faith, Buddhism, Christianity, Hinduism, Jainism, Judaism, Islam, Sikhism, Taoism and Zoroastrianism.

    For information on the 10 religions, please visit www.iro.org.sg

    I really didn't know that at all. Call me a frog in the well or whatever, but it's really interesting and good to know about the official religions in my own country.

    10, quite a lot, in my opinion. Of the ten, I only know Christianity, Hinduism, Islam, Taoist, Buddhism, Sikhism and Judaism.

    Guess I have lots to learn from here.

    How about the rest of you? How many among the official religions do you know or have at least heard about?
