    MapleSEA Best Guild SS Contest

    Dear Maplers,

    Time to show off your guild! Here's the MapleSEA Best Guild SS Contest! Take the best screenshots (SS) and be the winning guild! Get the EXCLUSIVE in-game item that lets your guild stand out from the crowd!

    Prizes
    Leader: 1x Cargo Plushie + 1x Newspaper Sword (in-game item)
    Members: 1x Newspaper Sword each (in-game item)

    Rules and Regulations
    Postings that do not follow the rules and regulations will not qualify for this event:
    1. Get as many guild members of your guild to be included in the SS as possible.
    2. SS should contain members of only ONE guild. Sub guilds are consider as separate guild.
    3. Character names of all guild members captured in the SS must be clearly shown.
    4. Guild leader must stand in the center of the SS.
    5. All characters in the SS to keep 'quiet' (no speech).
    6. No editing is allow. Resizing is not advisable.
    7. ONE SS to be submitted per guild. So, send in your BEST SS!

    Hints and tips for good Guild SS

    - Make the most tidy and neat presentation of guild members in SS.
    - Clear, original sized SS is good for viewing.
    - Express your guild's characteristic as much as possible through any means (e.g. fashion, expressions, IGN, class, weapons etc.).

    Closing Date
    10 Feb 2006.

    Availability
    Contest is open to all Singapore, Malaysia and Thailand players. Winners will collect the prizes from AsiaSoft's office in the respective country.

    Terms and Conditions
    To take part in the event, you must agree with the terms and conditions hence stated:

    1. All personal details stated in your AsiaSoft Passport must be valid.
    (e.g. national ID and address)
    2. Winners will be informed with details of the prize collection. Proof of identity will be required during the prize collection.
    3. AsiaSoft Online reserves all rights to use the submitted posts for the purposes of marketing promotions by Asiasoft Online. Art works may be used according to the absolute discretion of the organizers for the said purpose.

    Panel of judges includes:
    + MapleSEA Event Management Team
    + Forum Moderators
