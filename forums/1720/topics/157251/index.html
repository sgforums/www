    Fixing Black Bull's House (Level 1)
    Item's Needed :
    Tree Branches x 30
    Firewood x 50

    Procedure:

    1. Talk Black Bull in Perion.
    2. Hunt 30 Tree Branches from Stumps.
    3. Hunt 50 Firewood from Axe Stumps and Dark Axe Stumps.
    4. Talk to Black Bull again.

    Reward
    Either a Level 15 Steel Shield or a Level 25 Red Triangular Shield.

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Maya And The Weird Medicine (Level 15)
    Item's Needed :
    Charm of the Undead x 40
    Squishy Liquid x 50
    Leaf x 50
    Octopus's Legs x 20

    Procedure:

    1. Talk to Maya in a house in Henesys.
    2. Talk to Teo in Lith Harbor.
    3. Talk to Sophia in the potion shop of Perion.
    4. Talk to Manji at the top left platform of Perion.
    5. Hunt 40 Charm of the Undead from Zombie Mushrooms in Ant Tunnel.
    6. Talk to Manji again to trade 40 Charm of the Undead for 1 Arcon's Blood.
    7. Hunt 50 Squishy Liquid from Slimes around Henesys or Ellinia.
    8. Hunt 50 Leaf from Dark Stump around Perion.
    9. Hunt 20 Octopus's Legs from Octopus around Kerning City.
    10. Return to Sophia again for the Glittering Stone.
    11 .Return to Teo in Lith Harbor.
    12. Give Maya the Medicine.

    Reward
    Level 25 Brown Bamboo Hat + 5000 mesos + 200 experience

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Luke The Security Guy (Level 15)
    Item's Needed :
    Stirge's Wings x 10
    Salad x 1
    Jr. Necki's Skin x 100

    Procedure:

    1. From Henesys, walk towards the Dungeon. Luke is on the right exit. Talk to him.
    2. Go deep into the Ant Tunnel and hunt 10 Stirge's Wings from the bats.
    3. Go deeper in until you see the 24 hour mobile shop or from the store at Florina Beach.
    4. Buy 1 Bowl of Salad. (Do not eat it.)
    5. Hunt 100 Jr. Necki's Skins from little snakes in the swamp near Kerning City.
    6. Return to Luke.

    Reward
    A random refined jewel + 300 experience

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    John's Pink Flower Basket (Level 15)

    Item's Needed :
    Pink Violas x 10

    Procedure:

    1. Talk to John in Lith Harbor.
    2. Go to Sleepywood and talk to the Mysterious Statue.
    3. Climb step 1 and 2 of the Deep Forest of Patience. You get a random number of Pink Violas.
    4. Go back to John and give him the Pink Violas. (Note: you cannot trade or put the pink violas into bank)
    5. Repeat Step 3-4 if you have not pick 10 Pink Violas.

    Reward
    Screw x 30

    Rewards for rejumping after completing the quest:
    Random mineral ores

    Item's Needed :
    Pig's Ribbon x 100
    Evil Eye's Tail x 50

    Procedure:

    1. Talk to Alex in Kerning City (above the Pharmacy).
    2. Hunt 100 Pig's Ribbons from Ribbon Pigs in Pig Beach.
    3. Hunt 50 Evil Eye's Tails from Evil Eyes in the Ant Tunnel.
    4. Talk to Chief Stan of Henesys (outside Mushroom Park).
    5. Choose the following answers:

    (a) Well, I'm here at the request of Alex to see you. Please give me 5 minutes.
    (b) Stop it now! Alex is really sorry for running away.
    (c) Why don't you just ask Alex how he's doing?
    (d) I think you are a great chief of this town.

    A random pair of earrings + 300 experience + 550 experience

    Item's Needed :
    Diamond x 1

    Lupin's Bananas x 50

    1. Talk to Mr. Wetbottom in the VIP Sauna in Sleepywood.

    9. Return to Rina to get Unagi Special.

    Cursed Doll x 200
    Cursed Doll x 400
    Cursed Doll x 600
    Cursed Doll x 1000

    Procedure:

    1. Talk to Rowen the Fairy in Ellinia.
    2. Collect the correct amount of Cursed Dolls from Zombie Lupin for each round.
    3. Return to Rowen of Ellinia and give her the item.

    Looking for Book of Ancient (Level 55)

    Items Needed :
    Book of Ancient

    Procedure:

    1. Meet Alcaster in El Nath, and he'll direct you to find Hella in Orbis, which will trigger a series of related quests. Complete the quests Missing Hella, Spiruna and the Black Crystal, Scadur the Hunter and The Four Wisemen of Victoria Island and you'll obtain the Book of Ancient.
    2. After completing the aforementioned quests, head back to El Nath and talk to Alcaster.
    3. Go to Orbis and find Corporal Easy. Provide him 3 Werewolf's Toenails
    to obtain the Memory Powder.
    4. Go back to Alcaster and talk to him.
    5. Go down to Orbis Tower <9th Floor> and inspect the unknown sculpture.
    6. Return the Book of Ancient to Alcaster and this long series of quests finally ends, and the Justice Cape is yours.

    Reward
    - Get 6340 experience points after returning 3 Werewolf's Toenails to Corporal Easy.
    - Returning the Memory Powder to Alcaster will net you 7850 experience points.
    - Finally, after returning the Book of Ancient to Alcaster, you'll get a Level 55 Justice Cape of either 4 colours: , and 16170 experience points.

    -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Missing Hella (Level 55)

    Items Needed:
    None.

    Procedure:

    1. This quest is initiated after starting the Looking for Book of Ancient quest.
    2. Head over to Orbis Park to Hella's house, but the only person you'll encounter is Elma. Talk to her.
    3. Go to El Nath (you might want to get some Statue-Teleporting Scrolls to make your life easier) and seek out Jade. Talk to Jade repeatedly till he talk about Hella.
    4. Go back to Hella's house in Orbis Park and talk to Elma again.
    5. Go back to El Nath again and talk to Jade.
    6. Go back to Orbis Park and seek out Spiruna (located in Old Man's House in Cloud Park IV). Talk to her repeatedly.
    7. You will initiate the next quest, Spiruna and the Black Crystal.

    Reward
    None.

    Old Ring

    Procedure:

    1. This quest is a continuation from Spiruna and the Black Crystal. Meet Alcaster in El Nath.

    3. Return the items to Scadur, and he'll return you the Old Ring. He'll tell you something about the four wisemen in Victoria Island. Your quest ends here, and the next one, The Four Wisemen of Victoria Island, begins.

    The Four Wisemen of Victoria Island (Level 55)

    Items Needed :
    Power Crystal x 2
    Wisdom Crystal x 2
    DEX Crystal x 2
    LUK Crystal x 2

    Procedure:

    1. This quest starts off directly after Scadur the Hunter. Visit Dances with Balrog first in Perion in Victoria Island , and show him the Old Ring. He wants 2 Power Crystals in exchange for a piece of an Old Piece of Map.
    2. Go to Ellinia and talk to Grendel the Really Old, where he now wants 2 Wisdom Crystals. Give him those and you'll obtain your 2nd piece of map.
    3. Next, go to Henesys and talk to Athena Pierce. You'll need to provide her 2 DEX Crystals to obtain the 3rd piece of map.
    4. Now head over to Kerning City to meet the final wiseman, Dark Lord, and give him 2 LUK Crystals for the final piece of map.
    5. Your quest ends here, and your next final task begins in Looking for Book of Ancient.

    Reward
    Gain 8320 experience points and an Old Piece of Map each time you return the different types of crystals.
