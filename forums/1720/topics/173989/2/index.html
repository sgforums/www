    hmmm

    1st) dragon knights
    why?
    Bascially dk have dragon roars which can do 4k to 5k damage at lvl 100. Partying with 1 dk can own the entire map like dmt or macis, and save alot of health pots for the dk too, also, since the priest can give additional 150% and heal.

    2nd) fire mages, ice mages
    the same as dks, they can do aoe damage at high damage but a long casting time, they will most likely party together at macis or high lvl monsters like cere and they need extra armour and get healed while they cast the spell.

    3rd) rangers/snipers
    they can do aoe damage too, but since they cant hit at close range, it is wise if the priest go forward to tank the monsters with his invincible and heal the rangers/snipers at the same time.

    4th) white knights/crusader
    since they can kill high lvl monsters easily, the exp gained is high and can be improved further by the priest's 150% exp.

    5th) bandits/sins
    basically i feel that aoe damage is the most important in training fast, bandits and sins aoe damage is either (high but expensive-meso explosion) or (medium damage and medium aoe-sin's aoe damage)
