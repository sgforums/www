    Hunting Tips:

    *If you do not pick up your items within the designated time (~10 secs) other people can pick them up and will do so.

    *If you're being ksed (had your kill stolen) it is wise to simply shrug off and change channels. it is a waste of time and energy if you were to retaliate because sometimes it's unintentional; sometimes it is intentional. Just move on.

    *Fighting lots of creatures which are easy to kill is sometimes a lot better for experience than fighting one big creature.

    *If you do decide to trade fame with someone, be careful that they may be a cheater and whatsnot and not fame you, only trade if you trust someone.

    *To create keyboard shortcuts, have your hotkeys open (the box with shift, ins, del, ctrl, pgup, pgdown, end, home listed) and either open your inventory (for potion/item hotkeys) or skill box (for putting skills/spells at your hotkeys). Click and drag whatever item/skill icon you want into the box on which key you'd like to be the hotkey. It'll stay there and every time you hit that key, it'll use that item or skill. The same can be done with the [I]key settings [/I] (you can move sit/attack/etc and designate (for example) 'F' for one of your skills/items.)    [/quote]
