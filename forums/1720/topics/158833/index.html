    Spearmans are overall strong and flexible, with skills that is really helpful in parties.

    To make a good warrior, you will require the right stats. As you will see, there are four stats to choose from: STR (Strength, your ability to dish out physical damage), DEX (Dexterity, which increases your accuracy and avoidability), INT (Intelligence, a useless stat to the warrior), and LUK (Luck, which isn't bad but you do not want it for a warrior). The two stats that you want most are STR and DEX. The two stats you want to keep to a minimum are INT and LUK. So roll the dice and watch the numbers roll. While rolling, watch the INT and LUK numbers. You will want to have [B]4's[/b] on them, which will be explained later. 4 is the lowest number that you can go on the random number generator, thus having 4's on the stats will exploit your power as a warrior and increase your damage.
