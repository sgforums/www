    Ossyria (Ludibrium and Omega Sector)
    This part of Ossyria is currently released on the South Korean, Japanese, Chinese, and Taiwanese versions of MapleStory. To get to Ludibrium, you must ride a flying train from Orbis. The ship departs every 15-30 minutes depends on what version but you need to get into the waiting room of the ship 1 minute before departure time (which is shown at the dock). Unlike the flying boat from Ellinia to Orbis, no monsters will show up during the ride to attack you.

    There are two towns in this part of Ossyria: Ludibrium and Omega Sector (also known as Earth Defense Base). Similarly to Ossyria, you can go down to the Earth Defense Base by going down a tower called Eos/Dibrium Tower (prounounced Eh-ohs in Korean)in Ludibrium. However, this tower is 101 floors tall, not only 20. The monsters range from level 23-50. There are many quests in Eos/Dibrium Tower, like "The Lost Guard". If you complete this quest, you get 20 Eos/Dibrium Tower scrolls. You need three or them to fully go down Eos/Dibrium Tower.

    In Ludibrium the castle monsters aren't that stong but if you go deeper in to the Time Trail and Toy Graveyard the monster are a levels of 50-108. In ther deepest part there is a Boss call Phantom Plant, and unsurprising it is one of the strongest monsters in Ludibrium.

    Including Eos Tower, there are other maps like the Factory, the Time trail, the Cloud Terrace, Sky Terrace and Terrace Hall.

    On the 101st floor, there is a second party quest. It is for characters of level range 35-50, requires 6 people, and has 9 stages in total. At the end of the quest, there is a bonus stage with many boxes. You have only one minute to break open as many boxes as you can. These boxes release lots of meso and many different types of items. As a final prize, an NPC will reward you with an item. There also a boss in towner some where.

    In the Omega Sector aka Earth Defense Base, there are monsters from level 39-60. All of these monsters (except one) possesses magic capabilities, and they are very strong. The monsters are aliens, some big, some small. There are many quests, especially from the Mesorangers, the MapleStory version of the Power Rangers. If you go deeper you might end up with two bosses.
