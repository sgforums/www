    there are two types of attributes that affects damage - i call them the major and the minor.

    regular attacks
    major - str
    minor - dex

    major stat affects max damage more than minor stat, but minor stat affects min damage more than major

    for regular weapons, that is to say, normal "poking", str affects the max damage most, dex affects a little. however, dex affects min damage more than str. this applies to warriors since they all use regular attacks, as well as all other classes (magician attacking with mace/wand/staff, bowman swinging bow/xbow, sin poking enemies with claw), but warriors with lvl 19 mastery will find that str affects min damage more than dex.

    thief attacks
    major - luk
    minor - dex, str

    for throwing stars and daggers' stabs, replace the "str" of regular attacks with "luk", and "dex" with "dex and str"

    bowmen arrow shootings
    major - dex
    minor - str

    opposite of regular attacks! swap the places of "dex" and "str" in regular attacks.

    magicians' spells
    major - int
    minor - luk

    this is a special case - according to hidden-street.net, int affects max spell damage more than luk for both max and min damage, regardless of mastery lvl. but there are reports that spell damage in hidden-street is not accurate.

    for more information, try out the damage calculator in hidden-street.net
