    Originally posted by shinta:
    ah ditzy likes simon road hor?

    where is it ar?
    1 ) Take NEL heading to punggol and alight at kovan station.
    2 ) Immediately out the train, turn to the right.
    3 ) Head to the escalator and go up.
    4 ) On top to your immediate right is the control station.
    5 ) Ignore the control station and head out the barriers.
    6 ) To your immediate front are the ticketing machines.
    7 ) Ignore the ticketing machines and turn right.
    8 ) Walk straight untill you hit a T junction.
    9 ) Turn right again at the T junction.
    10 ) Walk towards the escalators and go up.
    11 ) Once up, you'll see a sheltered walkway heading towards a bus stop on your immediate right.
    12 ) Walk along this sheltered walkway to the bus stop.
    13 ) On the opposite side of the road is heartland mall.
    14 ) On your left is one of those small stalls that sell magazines and tidbits.
    15 ) Ignore what is on your right and left.
    16 ) Carry on walking from the bus stop along the narrow pavement.
    17 ) Walk straight and make sure heartland mall is on your behind right side.
    18 ) The road directly in front of you now is simon road.
    19 ) You will see a very old coffeshop in front of you.
    20 ) The fried hokkien mee stall wok is facing you.
    21 ) The orh lua stall is hidden behind the pillar on the right.
    22 ) The satay stall is left of the fried hokkien mee stall.
    23 ) Find a table, preferably inside, coz the smoke blows out.
    24 ) Make sure its an old man cooking the hokkien mee, not his son.
    25 ) Ask him what days he not open.
    26 ) He'll tell you sundays and mondays.
