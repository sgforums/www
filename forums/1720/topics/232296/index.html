    Stats of Limited Edition Super Snowboard:



    The Limited Edition Super Snowboard is the newest snowboard that have been genetically created from 6 snowboards Â— Sky Snowboard, Aqua Snowboard, Silver Snowboard, Golden Snowboard, Dark Snowboard and Blood Snowboard.

    With the combination of these 6 snowboards, the Super Snowboard has the ability to grant anyone who uses it with additional speed and greater attack power which the 6 snowboards were lacking of.

    Here is your chance to grab this Limited Edition Super Snowboard which is only available for one month!

    How to get Super Snowboard

    1. Gather 6 different Snowboards (dropped by various monsters)



    2. Find and talk to any Maple Administrator located in any town.



    3. Exchange all 6 snowboards with the Maple Administrator for the Limited Edition Super Snowboard.

    4. Start your adventure by adding the latest snowboard to your collection!

    Note:
    Super Snowboard event is available to all Maplers from 17th January 2007 to 21st February 2007.
    Super Snowboard is transferable and can be traded between players.
