    The Guide on Zakum

    Level of Zakum and its 8 arms: 140
    Minimum recommened level to face Zakum: 100-120++(best if same lv as Zakum)
    It's also recommended that u can deal 5,000++ damage to Zakum per hit in order to damage Zakum significantly.

    now, let's go on to the 8 arms that Zakum have:

    The 8 arms of Zakum

    Zakum Arm 1 & 2 (left side & right side of Zakum: first arm from top)
    HP: 20,000,000
    MP: 18,000
    EXP: 1,680,000

    Attacks
    1) mp drain
    Effects: hp-1, mp-300

    *the 1000+ damage in this pic is due to a hp drain casted just before the mp drain
    if you are realli that worried about the mp loss, just use a mana elixir :p

    2) hp drain
    Effects: hp-1000 or even more, it depends on how much your magic defence is, and whether u have any other skills, such as magic resistance.


    Zakum Arm 3 (left side of Zakum, 2nd arm)
    HP: 20,000,000
    MP: 18,000
    EXP: 1,120,000

    Attacks:
    1) fire storm
    Effects: hp-1000+, depends on other factors again


    2)poison mist
    Effects: hp-800 every 1 sec (needs confirmation)


    Zakum Arm 4 (right side of Zakum, 2nd arm)
    HP: 20,000,000
    MP: 18,000
    EXP: 1,120,000

    Attacks:
    1) ice strike
    Effects: hp-1000-2000++, comes out from ground


    2) thunder strike
    Effects: hp-1,000-2,000++, targets all around Zakum


    Zakum Arm 5 (left side of Zakum, 3rd arm)
    HP: 25,000,000
    MP: 18,000
    EXP: 1,400,000

    Attacks:
    1) status infliction
    Effects: maplers gets a status ailment
    *a different spell is casted for a different status infliction

    the pic shows the arm casting one status infliction


    Zakum Arm 6 (right side of Zakum, 3rd arm)
    HP: 25,000,000
    MP: 18,000
    EXP: 1,400,000

    Attacks: none

    Specials: Heal
    Effect: heals main zakum body and the other 8 arms for 50,000-55,000 HP every 30 seconds


    *latest: apparantly, this arm can also cast physical attack up, physical defense up, magic attack up and magic defence up on Zakum and the 8 arms! all the more u shld take out this arm first!

    Zakum Arm 7 & 8 (left & right side of Zakum, last arm)
    HP: 23,000,000
    MP: 18,000
    EXP: 1,288,000

    Attacks: Earth Fist
    Effect: a fist comes out from the ground, dealing damage to those around the fist.


    Total HP of Zakum's 8 arms: 176,000,000

    let's go on to...

    Zakum's Main Body
    After all of Zakum's 8 arms have been defeated, the only one that remains is Zakum itself.
    Zakum only starts attacking after all its 8 arms have been defeated

    statistics
    NOTE: Zakum's HP bar shows up when all of Zakum's 8 arms have been defeated.
    A different colour is used to represent Zakum's 3 forms

    1st form (HP bar: red)
    HP: 66,000,000 (60 million)
    MP: 30,000

    2nd form (HP bar: yellow/yellowish-green)
    HP: 88,000,000 (80 million)
    MP: 35,000

    Final form (HP bar: green)
    HP: 110,000,000 (100 million)
    MP: 40,000
    EXP: 8,750,000

    Zakum's attacks can be classified into two kinds:
    1) offensive skills
    2) defensive skills

    offensive skills
    1) Apocalypse
    Zakum's ultiamte attack!
    Effects: Reduces all players' HP and MP to 1!
    the spell is very large, so u'll know that the attack is coming

    after being hit by this attack, it's best that u use pots to heal yourself
    a recommended potion would be the mushroom miso ramen.

    2) Molten slab strike
    Effects: use flaming slabs to damage multiple maplers
    (i)Zakum first casts a red-coloured spell:


    (ii)small stone slabs pop out from the ground
    they show the points on which the molten stone slabs will fall on


    (iii)then the molten stone slabs will come down on the maplers, dealing 2500++ damage


    3) Mega Thunderstorm
    Effects: whole map affected, targets multiple maplers
    (i)Zakum casts a huge yellowish-green spell


    (ii)the lightning strikes! and it deals 1000-2000+ damage


    4) Monster summoning
    Effect: summons monsters (namely Boogie, Puco, Pachu, Punco, Cuzco, and Opachu)
    *zakum can only summon 1 kind of monster at any one time


    here's another pic of monster summoning:


    Total HP of Zakum's 3 forms: 264,000,000 (264 million!)
    Total HP of Zakum's 3 forms+Zakum's 8 hands: 440,000,000 (440 million)!

    defensive skills

    1) defensive summoning
    (i)Zakum lifts and slams his tombstone once, then it will cast a spell (yellowish-white in colour)

    (ii) Zakum lifts and slams the tombstone a second time, his whole body gives off a blinding flash of light
    after this, either one of two super-defensive skills will be activated.
    the seal representing the skill will appear on Zakum's tombstone.
    the two defence skills are:

    a) physical damage nullify
    Effect: all physical damage dealt to Zakum is reduced to 1
    The seal looks like a pole arm and spear crossing over one another

    ***note that some other strong monsters can also use this skill (exploding goby house, doom flounder)

    *Suggestions:
    ->Rangers and Snipers should use their Fire Shot and Ice Shot respectively, as they deal magic damage
    ->White Knights can use their Elemental Charges to their advantage
    ->All mages except Priests (those healing) do full-scale attack
    ->other jobs should focus on Zakums' monsters

    b)magical damage nullify
    Effect: all magical damage dealt to Zakum is reduced to 1!

    mages are advised to stop attacking Zakum and concentrate on the summoned monsters
    the other jobs shld focus their attacks on Zakum.

    2) Heal
    Effect: Zakum heals himself and his summoned monsters for 30,000++ HP.
    This spell is the largest i've seen so far o.o


    now we plan a strategy to defeat Zakum...

    Zakum Strategy Guide
    *if u realli want to defeat Zakum, u must plan first!!!
    1) get the Eye of Zakum ready "-_-
    2) get a party of 6 (more parties if u wan)(get pple from all kinds of jobs)
    3) charge through the Door to Zakum!
    4) inside, get ur support skills ready (magic guard, bless, holy symbol etc)
    5) throw out the Eye of Zakum in the center of the altar, and get away from there. In just a few seconds, Zakum will be summoned!
    6) ATTACK! (recommended way of killing Zakum, up to u to decide how u shld destroy Zakum
    a) destroy Zakum Arm 6 aka Healing Arm
    b) destroy Zakum Arm 5, then 3&4, 1&2, and finally 7&8
    c) after this, deal as much damage as u can on Zakum!
    (i) dun use multi-target skills; use single-target skills, most single-target skills deal more damage
    besides, there's only 1 target left: Zakum. use multi-target skills only when Zakum summons
    monsters.
    (ii) mages: if your HP and MP have turned into 1 due to Zakum, quickly use a Ramen, or u'll die!
    (same for priests, since they can't heal themselves if their Mp is 1 )
    for other jobs: heal your HP first, then ur MP, or use Ramen too if u managed to get them.

    Lastly, good luck! :p
