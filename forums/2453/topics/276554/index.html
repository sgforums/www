    About Laydio:

    Laydio was introduced on 09 July 2006, by Sophia.

    The Name:

    "Laydio" is most likely to be pronounced by people who doesn't know how to pronounce the word, "radio". "Laydio" was also chosen because of its unique pronounciation.

    Language

    Currently, our songs played are mostly in English or Mandarin, we'll be broadcasting more Cantonese, Hokkien and other languages POP soon.

    We're also looking forward to DJs/VJs who can speak at least 3 languages fluently.

    The Crew
    *Recruitment drive has just ended, but do feel free to send us your application and you'd be the first to be reviewed during the next recruitment drive.

    Currently, we have DJs aged 15~20+/-, most of them are from Singapore, the rest are from Malaysia and Australia.

    If your interest is to be a DJ, sharing your thoughts on air, and you're older than 17, please feel free to send us an application email to laydio.online@gmail.com with the following information:
    [*]Brief introduction of yourself,
    [*]A voice clip-interview if possible,
    [*]Tell us why you're interested,
    [*]Show us your passion,
    [*]Do include any other information that you think we may want to know.

    *Common sense tells you that you're not 100% guarenteed to be recruited into the crew if you don't meet what we're looking for. Sorry about that, but BloodVanille had shown an clear example for not knowing this little common sense thing. :] --
