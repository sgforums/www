Making a Difference

  • Workaholic1509
    8 Aug 06, 11:04
    Okay, so here I am typing this topic for the first time. Well, I pretty sure it'll welcome 2 groups of people. Those who share the same vision of making a positive influence and impact on the society we are living in. And also those who are so sceptical and anti-positive people that will try to put us down. But really, if we can stand together, we can make a difference. A positive one!

    I grew up in a pretty well-to-do family once, but when the 1997 financial crisis came, it swept us away. Presently, my family is struggling even to survive, although the government is claiming that the economy is much better.

    Pls do not get me wrong, I think our government is doing quite a good job so far, just that sometimes, it too hard to look at the individual needs of every single family.

    I believe there are too some families out there that is experiencing the same circumstances. People struggling just to make it through the day.

    Maybe at the first look, people will not be able to tell that I'm struggling through. Maybe because of what I have on me. I do not dress like I'm broke. But yes, the truth is, I'm struggling financially.

    I'll be lying to say that I never had thoughts of taking loans. Time and again, I hoped to do so, but yet, I hang on to the principles of life, and I believe that "Tough time don't Last, Only Tough Man Last"!

    It has not been any easy, trust me. But if there are people out there going through the same scenario, hey, I would like to invite you to share about it. Share about your personal struggles. And maybe we can come together, and start encouraging each day, and helping each other. And thus, in the near future, use our lives as testimonies to encourage others as well.
