Soccer...The Bare Facts

    19 May 05, 22:02
    One for all football fans out there....

    - Soccer - Football - as it is known in Europe and other countries other than North America, is played on every continent.

    - FIFA (The Federation of World Football) counts 204 member countries.

    - Soccer is a bigger sport than Baseball, Basketball and Football (American) combined.

    - Organised soccer was born in Britain. England and Scotland being co-founders of the beautiful game.

    - The World's First Football Club - Sheffield Football Club - was founded in 1857 by Colonel Nathaniel Cresswick and Major William Priest, two British Army officers.

    - The oldest Football Associations are:

    England - founded 1863.
    Scotland - founded 1873.
    Wales - founded 1875.
    Ireland - founded 1880.

    - The English League was founded in 1888.

    - The founding clubs were:

    Accrington (Old Reds) : Aston Villa : Blackburn Rovers : Bolton Wanderers : Burnley : DerbyCounty : Everton : Notts County : Preston North End : Stoke City : West Bromwich Albion : Wolverhampton Wanderers.

    - The First League Goal - ever - was scored on 8th. September 1888, by Jack Gordon a Preston North End player.

    - The First International Match was played in Glasgow, Scotland on 30th. November 1872 when Scotland played England. The Scottish FA did not then exist - it was not founded until three months later. The team England played that day was actually the oldest Scottish club team, Queen's Park - founded 1863.

    - The First Professional Players were reputedly two Scots -John Love, and Fergus Suter, who played for a north of England club called Darwen in the 1879 season - and were paid for their soccer talent.
