If pressed for a more specific answer, always give a range, rather than a specific number.

    Got back from the interview two days ago...

    Interview only lasted 10 minutes At first I thought it was only that way for me, turned up it was that way for everyone else as well...they only asked 3-5 questions

    First question they asked was about what I used to teach/am teaching now. Then asked me about the challenges of teaching in Malaysia.
    Then the differences between Malaysia education and Singapore education. This one quite hard, as I have to come out with a difference that doesn't give a bad impression of Malaysia, and yet at the same time relevant.
    After that asked me about the challenges I think I would face when teaching primary school students, and how would I go about to overcome those.
    Lastly, asked if I am willing to relocate to Spore if selected.

    That's all. Will know the result in 2 to 3 weeks time
