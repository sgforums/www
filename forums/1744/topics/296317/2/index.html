    Are we talking about stress or workload? They're related but entirely separate things. With workload it's pretty much a matter of managing client expectations - I under-promise so that I can over-deliver, and if the client makes noise about it, I play the quality card: you want a quality job, it takes time.

    With stress, the only way I find I can actually get rid of stress is to finish the job off. Doing anything else doesn't help me much because my mind's still on the job anyway.
