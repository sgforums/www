    Big Company
    ===========
    Pros
    1) Higher numeration (debatable?)
    2) Greater opportunities for overseas advancement
    3) Better fringe benefits
    4) Multi-cultural, multi-national working environment (yes its a con too)
    5) Defined job scope (as in more defined then a small company where you have to do everything)
    6) More opportunities for career advancement + looks better on your resume (e.g. software engineer from microsoft)

    Cons
    1) Prone to constant restructuring or even downsizing
    2) Multi-cultural, multi-national working environment (yes its a con too)
    3) Less friendlier environment, greater degrees of office politics

    Thats my personal experience with the company which I am in right now..
