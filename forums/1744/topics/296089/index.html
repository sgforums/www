    i feel that there are more cons than pro.

    the relative might find it hard to give a difficult job to u and ended up giving to someone else. sooner or later other colleagues will say u have the backing of the boss.

    or it could be the other way round, the relative give u a difficult job not within your job scope. he thinks that since u and him are related, u can help eachother out. u on the other hand will also find it hard to reject him since both of u are related.

    and if u screwed up the job, the boss also won't blame u since both of u are related. sooner or later the other colleagues will say u have the backing of the boss.

    conclusion is, i don't recommend relatives working together unless it's a family business.
