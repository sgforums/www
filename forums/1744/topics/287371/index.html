    surround myself with people who are supportive.

    think about what i'd achieved in the past, encourage myself.

    then when can think clearly again:

    -think objectively about what the boss said, and try to extract learning value from it. if die die disagree with what he said or the fella was just being difficult, try to shrug it off. Cos nobody can please everyone all the time la...

    -think objectively about why kena makan, kena arrowed for sai kang, or whatnot. and try to identify what can be done differently next time. may have something to do with human relationships. sometimes sai kang may not be a bad thing, and i volunteer my help to my colleagues all the time. u gain their gratitude and build trust over time.

    but at the end of the day, you have to find meaning and purpose in your job. can be for money, for learning opportunities, and for some people, their job is a calling. so u have to see for yourself and think whether u want to stay on in this job
