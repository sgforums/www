    actually it is not good to resign when u are still in probation. it gives people an impression u are not serious about the job.

    what u should do is before sending resume, u should think beforehand carefully on what kind of job u wanna do. do research on the company u wanna join as well.

    if u get selected for interview, take this opportunity to see the company, understand how they works, the working environment etc.

    upon confirmation that they want to hire u, rise all your questions like job scope, welfare, salary etc.

    don't wait until few months into the job then regret it.
