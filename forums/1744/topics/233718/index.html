    in the concrete jungle, one must be careful of what u say to each other, especially your colleagues.

    many will say the wrong thing at the wrong time without knowing.

    take for instance your colleague next to u is thinking of quiting and is taking a half day leave to go for a job interview. he wants to keep this a secret but since u are his best pal, he decided to let you now.

    since u are the only one who knows, u must be careful not to mention anything regarding the interview during everyday conversation.

    take for examples u shouldn't say "Hey so what time are u going to XXX company later?" when other colleagues are around. people will get suspicious.

    feel free to discuss and share your experiences.
