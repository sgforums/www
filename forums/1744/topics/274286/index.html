    aiyo, dun tell me u never did any project in school? Dun have to base on ur work experience. It is the skill that u acquired during ur life that can be invaluable to the company. Juz learn to package and sell urself during an interview.

    When answering question, think like a salesman. What does the customer want and how u can fulfil their needs. Relate their need to ur ability. Learn to speak confidently and phrase your answer in your mind before u speak. Normally a delay in answering is actually a good thing. It give ppl the impression that u think b4 u speak. Practise talking to a mirror and see uself in action.

    If you have no idea what to answer or how to relate it to urself, try google the most commonly ask question and answer in an interview. Dun memorise it, instead try relating them to u and rephrasing them, juz like the way you would normally answer. That way, it sound nature and believable.

    Half the battle is won depending on how u carry urself during the interview. If u can do that, the chances of getting the job isn't that hard.
