NUS Motoring Club AGM/EXCO [positions open]

  • NUSMotoring
    10 Sep 06, 11:50
    We are pleased to announce that the Motoring Club 11th Executive Committee is now open for nominations!

    Due to a need to make the Club even more successful than before, a whole NEW structure has been established this year. I sincerely welcome all of you to be part of a dynamic team of directors and leaders to fulfil the new vision & direction of the Club. This is a great chance for anyone who wishes to contribute and at the same time, gain valuable and fulfilling experiences in leadership roles.

    Under the new structure, the Motoring Club will focus itself to rally all enthusiasts to share the passion and knowledge about motoring. We will infuse a greater variety of exciting events and projects to our members, NUS staff and alumni, so as to enhance the profile and standing of the Club in NUS.

    Various projects will also be launched to bring our Club closer to the NUS community. For example, we were proud to be the organisers of the 1st Motorsports race held in the school carpark in Singapore- NUS Go-Karting Charity Challenge 2005 and the NUS Centennial Charity Car Rally 2006. These events received tremendous support and participation by the NUS students, staff and alumni members. With this success, we hope to bring on other interesting events like the upcoming NUS Motorshow cum Bazzar, Drive-Trip to Malaysia and Go-Karting Challenge. We strongly encourage you to be part of family and enjoy the many rewards and satisfaction you will get from working in a closely-knitted group.

    Below are the positions available for nominations,

    1) Honorary General Secretary

    2) Honorary Treasurer

    3) Motorsports & Events Comm.: 3 positions

    2) Publication Comm.: 2 positions (people with a flair in writing and events coverage in the local motorsports scene)

    3) Publicity Comm.: 2 positions

    4) Marketing Comm.: 4 positions (2 guys and 2 girls most preferred)

    5) IT Comm: 2 positions (Visual Design and simple website programming/maintenance)

    for more information please email us at info@nusmotoring.org.

    thanks.
