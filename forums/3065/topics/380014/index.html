Impresario 2010! Sing, Dance or Compose!

  • huip
    27 Oct 09, 14:25

    Have a burning passion for the performing arts? Can’t find any avenue to express your talents?

     

    Be it singing, dancing or composing, wait no more! Impresario is back for its 18th year running.             

     

    The time is here now; it’s Your Turn to Shine.

     

    Impresario, a nationwide talent search organised by Nanyang Technological University (NTU) Cultural Activities Club (CAC) since 1992, has always been an annual highlight in NTU’s calendar of events. It has since gained national recognition as a main channel for discovering fresh talents in Singapore.

     

    With Music Forest and Touch Music Publishing as the official music/dance school and music publisher respectively, be sure to get some valuable pointers from them!

     

    Who knows, you could even join the Impresario’s alumnus in taking on bigger stages like Project Superstar (Kelly Poon), Singapore Idol (Candice Foo & Maia Lee) and even Taiwan’s highly rated One Million Star (Tay Kewei)!

     

    With prizes worth as much as $10,000 to be won, what are you waiting for?

     

    Registration is now OPEN!

     

    For more information and registration, visit http://www.cacimpresario.com/index.php
