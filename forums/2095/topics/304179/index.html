Changes To Roads

  • TWS4793
    21 Jan 08, 00:31
    If you spot any major changes to roads, please post your sightings here.

    Here are a few I would like to highlight.

    1. Previously, the carpark for Blocks 204 to 209 at Bedok North have two entrances and exits: one at Bedok North Interchange (from Bedok North Street 1) and another at New Upper Changi Road.

    There is a slight change in the road system two weeks ago. The entrance at Bedok North Interchange has been converted to an all exit road and only buses are allowed to turn into Bedok North Interchange (from Bedok North Street 1).

    Please take note of this admenment to the road system. Expect jams at New Upper Changi Road leading up to the entrance of the carkpark.

    2. Formerly a carpark for residents living in Blocks 210 to 218 (later downgraded to Blocks 210 to 215 due to MSCP Block 215A), the area has now been converted to a HDB ground and the stretch of road from New Upper Changi Road to Bedok North Avenue 3 (within the former carpark area) is now upgraded to a one way road named 'Bedok Central'. Motorists who wish to go to Bedok North Street 3 may use this road as an alternative.
