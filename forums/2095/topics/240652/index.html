    Closure Of Albert Street And Opening Of New Access Road

    Albert Street will be closed from 25 February 2007 and will be converted into a pedestrian mall.

    A new two-way access road joining Short Street and Prinsep Street will be opened on the same day. The portion of Short Street between Albert Street and the new access road will be converted to a two-way road.

    The Bras Basah/Bugis district is envisaged as the Arts, Culture, Learning and Entertainment hub in the city. It is a vibrant district focused around arts and entertainment where developments are planned around a pedestrian-oriented environment. This new pedestrian mall is an extension to the existing Albert Mall and forms part of the overall pedestrian network in the district.

    This portion of Albert Street pedestrian mall is expected to be opened in June 2007.
