Road/buses: Alternaltive way to Geylang/Kallang Bahru

  • sbskidz

    Last time, the only way to enter and exit Geylang/kallang Bahru is from PIE (now changed to only enter), Bendemeer Road, and Boon Keng Road.

    Now there is another faster alternative way to Geylang/Kallang Bahru.
    Drivers can now enter to Geylang/kallang Bahru from Upper Boon Keng Road.

    From Lor 1 Geylang (the road where you turn right from Kallang MRT at Sims Ave), normally, road vehicles will turn left as turning right leds them nowhere as it ends up at the car park.

    Cars last time have to travel down to Boon keng road and turn right and then left again when travelling to Geylang/Kallang Bahru. Now, drivers at Lor 1 Geylang can right turn and you go straight and you will reach Geylang/Kallang Bahru!! A very fast journey!

    Bus stop have been built, but no bus have been functioned yet ,so look out for more informations on buses here at roads.sgforums.com
