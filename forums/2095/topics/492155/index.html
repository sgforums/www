    Truck overturns on Tampines Expressway, causes tailback to Seletar Expressway

    SINGAPORE: A tipper truck travelling on the Tampines Expressway (TPE) overturned on Monday (Jun 20).

     

    The flipped truck occupied three lanes on TPE and caused congestion until the Seletar Expressway (SLE) entrance, said the Land Transport Authority in a tweet at 6.15pm. It added that motorists should avoid lanes 2, 3 and 4

     

    According to the Singapore Civil Defence Force, assistance was not required as no one was injured.

     

    Police added that motorists should avoid travelling on the expressway and use alternative routes, if possible.
