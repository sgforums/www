    Permanent Closure of U-Turning Facilities along Upper Paya Lebar Road

    To facilitate the completion of works for a vehicular underpass and the implementation of the final traffic scheme, the U-turning facilities along Upper Paya Lebar Road near Kim Chuan Road / Little Road will be closed permanently from 9pm, 13 November 2008.

    Information signs will be placed along the affected roads to inform motorists of the works ahead.

    Please refer to the attached map for the alternative U-turns along Upper Paya Lebar Road.

    Should motorists have any queries, they can contact the LTA hotline at 1800-CALL LTA.

    Conversion of a Stretch of Lincoln Road into One-way Road

    The Land Transport Authority will be converting the stretch of Lincoln Road between Surrey Road and Wee Nam Road from the existing two-way road to a one-way road in the direction towards Keng Lee Road from 14 November 2008, 10am.

    This conversion is undertaken as part of improvement works to enhance the safety of motorists along this narrow stretch of road.

    With the conversion, motorists are advised to use Wee Nam Road to connect to Surrey Road. Please refer to the attached map for more information.

    Should motorists have any queries, they can contact the LTA hotline at 1800-CALL LTA.
