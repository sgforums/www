    The Journey - Singapore's Land Transport Story tells the story of Singapore's transformation , "mosquito" buses and "pirate" taxis, to the modern and efficient system Singapore has now.

    The book features Ilsa Sharp who speaks to pioneers and ministers about the involvements in decisions behind major transportation construction like the MRT. Personal accounts of Dr Yeo Ning Hong, the late Lim Leong Geok, Mah Bow Tan, Yeo Cheow Tong and others are in the book.

    Commuters' impressions can also be found inside with pictures taken by award-winning photographers.

    People who has the interest in Singapore history and those who observe the transport developments of Singapore developement will like and enjoy this book very much.

    The Journey - Singapore's Land Transport Story are now sold at leading bookstores at $45.00.
