Roads cordoned off due to war relic found

  • QX179R
    18 Sep 12, 06:11

    SINGAPORE: A war relic was found at about 12.40pm on Monday at the junction of Lorong 22 Geylang and Guillemard Road.

    Police have cordoned the affected area and a Chemical, Biological, Radiological and Explosive (CBRE) team is at the site.

    During this period, Lorong 22 Geylang and Guillemard Road, between Mountbatten Road and Cassia Crescent, will be closed to traffic.

    Traffic Police officers have been deployed to divert and regulate traffic at the affected areas.

    Police have advised the public not to be alarmed and to tune in to the radio for the latest update on the traffic situation.
