    Braddell Road/Thomson Road/Lornie Road Flyover to open in July

    SINGAPORE: The Land Transport Authority (LTA) said the Braddell Road/Thomson Road/Lornie Road Flyover will open by July.

    The 1.2km flyover should help ease the traffic congestion on Lornie Road during the morning peak hours.

    The morning jam used to dissipate by 9am, but in the past few weeks, it sometimes stretched past 10am.
