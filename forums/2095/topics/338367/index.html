Opening of New PIE Connection

  • QX179R

    Opening of New PIE Connection at Bedok North Avenue 3

    Motorists will be able to enjoy a direct and faster connection between Bedok North avenue 3 and the PIE from Saturday afternoon.

    That's because the new vehicular interchange between the Pan-Island Expressway and Bedok North Avenue 3 will be opened to traffic from 4pm on Saturday.

    This follows the completion of an upgrading project which costs $20.5 million.

    Works for the latest road interchange with the PIE began in June 2006 and involved the construction of four slip roads to connect Bedok North Avenue 3 to the PIE.

    It also involved works to widen Bedok North Avenue 3 from two lanes to three lanes in both directions.

    The new interchange will also serve as an alternative to the two existing interchanges with the PIE at Bedok North and Simei.
