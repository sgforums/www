Roads: Saturday ERP At Orchard Road Area

  • sbskidz
    10 Oct 05, 17:23
    Starting from 8 October 2005, there will be Electronic Road Pricing (ERP) charging for motorists travelling to the Orchard Road area from 12.00 noon to 8.00pm on Saturdays.

    Motorists who drive into the Orchard Road area will have to pay an ERP charge of $0.50 during the operating hours.

    Motorists are encouraged to use alternative routes, other than Orchard Road, to get to their destinations that is to travel outside the Orchard Road area, ERP will be charged at the Handy Road gantry to promote motorists not to travel in the Orchard road area.

    Motorists are also encouraged to take public transport as the bus lane along Orchard Road, from Paterson Road to Dhoby Ghaut, will be in operation from 7.30am to 8.00pm to prevent the delayed of buses by the traffic congestions.
