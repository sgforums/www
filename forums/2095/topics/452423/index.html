Traffic arrangements during Cold Storage Kids Run

  • QX179R
    16 May 12, 19:04

    SINGAPORE: Several roads will be closed to vehicular traffic on May 20 to facilitate the Cold Storage Kids Run which will be held at the Padang.

    The affected roads are Connaught Drive from 3am to 11am; St Andrew's Road from 4.00am to 10.30am; Fullerton Road, Parliament Place and the carriageway of Fullerton Road in the direction of Esplanade Drive (between Empress Place and Esplanade Drive) from 5.00am to 10.30am.

    The police says parking restrictions along the peripheral roads will be strictly enforced. Vehicles found parked and causing obstruction will be towed away.

    SMRT bus services 75, 167 and 961 will also be temporarily diverted during the period of 4.00am to 11.00am.

    These bus services will not stop at the following bus stops at Victoria Concert Hall, opposite Singapore Cricket Club, and the Supreme Court.
