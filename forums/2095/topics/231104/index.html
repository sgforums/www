    Opening of West Coast Highway Extension And Upgraded Roads

    LTA has just completed a number of projects to improve the road network from Ayer Rajah Expressway (AYE)/ Keppel Interchange, Telok Blangah Road, Pasir Panjang Road, West Coast Highway to Jalan Buroh.

    The improvement works were carried out in stages from 1998 and the completed sections were progressively opened to traffic since 2002. With the completion of this West Coast Highway Extension (viaduct at Pasir Panjang Road), LTA completes the road network improvement projects for Singapore's south-western sector. Overall, the projects will help to improve the traffic flow along the south-western corridor, benefiting the residents, businesses and motorists.

    To mark the completion of these road projects, the Land Transport Authority (LTA) will be organising an opening ceremony cum 2.4 km Fun Walk on Saturday, 25 March 2006 at 9.15 am. The event is open to the public.

    If you would like to participate and be amongst the first to walk on the West Coast Highway extension (viaduct at Pasir Panjang Road) before it is opened to traffic, please download the registration form and submit it to:

    Land Transport Authority
    (Corporate Communications Department)
    No.1 Hampshire Road, Blk 5 Level 4,
    Singapore 219428
    Fax: 6396 1148 (Attn: Ms Jelita or Mr Hadi)

    Pre-registration is required for participation in this event and the closing date for registration is Wednesday, 15 March 2006.

    Here are some tips if you are participating in the fun walk:

    1) Dress appropriately, preferably in sports attire and sports shoes.

    2) Have a light breakfast before coming. You need the energy for the walk.

    3) Although there will be medics on standby, please ensure that you are feeling well before coming to the walk.

    4) Report early at the pick-up points which you have selected in your registration form. (Note: Due to inaccessibility of the event location and for safety reasons, participants are NOT allowed to drive or walk to the event ground).

    5) Goodie bags will be given to registered participants only. Each participant is entitled to one goodie bag.

    6) We seek your co-operation to comply with the organisers' instructions at the event ground to ensure safety for all.

    7) Please note that in the event of rain, the organizers may postpone or cancel the walk. Please wait for instructions from the organizers.
