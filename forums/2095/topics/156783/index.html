    Ministry of Transport
    -- The Ministry of Transport (MOT) oversees air Transport , sea transport and land transport.
    The main focus is to bring about efficient and cost-effective transportation.

    Land transport Authority:
    -- The Land Transport Authority (LTA) is under the Ministry of Transport and was established on 1 September 1995. LTA takes care of the transportation needs of Singapore, taking care of people who take public transport or drive on roads.
    www.lta.gov.sg

    EZ- Link Pte Ltd
    -- EZ-Link Pte Ltd was formed on 8 January 2002and responsible for the sale, distribution and management of ez-link Cards. EZ-Link cards are now selling at all Transitlink offices at bus interchanges or Mrt trains for taking MRT, LRT and buses.

    http://www.ezlink.com.sg/index.html
    Transit Link Pte Ltd
    -- TransitLink is a company set up by SBS Transit Ltd and SMRT Corporation Ltd to provide an integrated public transport system by combinding the MRT, LRT and buses together as a network.
    www.transitlink.com.sg

    SBS Transit Ltd
    -- SBS Transit was formed in 1973 through the merger of three private bus companies. In November 1997, Singapore Bus Service (1978 ) Limited was renamed DelGro Corporation Limited (Now ComfortDelGro Corporation Limited). Singapore Bus Services Limited changed its name to SBS Transit Ltd in November 2001 to reflect its bi-modal status as a bus and rail operator.
    www.sbstransit.com.sg

    SMRT Coporation Ltd
    -- SMRT Corporation Ltd (SMRT) is a company with MRT, LRT, buses and taxis as its business. It offers public transport services in Singapore through its subsidiaries. SMRT buses and SMRT Taxis were taken over bt the former Trans-Island Bus Services Ltd.
    www.smrt.com.sg
