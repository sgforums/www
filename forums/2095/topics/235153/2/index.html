    Relocation of ERP Gantry at Orchard Turn

    1. To facilitate works for ION Orchard, the current ERP gantry at Orchard Turn will be relocated about 20 metres further down the road towards Wisma Atria Shopping Centre.

    2. The columns for the new gantry will be installed on Monday, 13 October 2008 and the gantry installation will be completed on Tuesday, 21 October 2008.

    3. To facilitate the installation, the left lane of Orchard Turn towards Orchard Boulevard will be closed from 10pm, 13 October to 1am, 14 October. The opposite flow single lane road on Orchard Turn towards Orchard Link will be closed on 14 October from 0100hrs to 0500hrs.

    4. During the temporary road closures, motorists affected will be diverted to Orchard Boulevard as shown in the attached temporary road diversion plan. Traffic wardens will be deployed to allow access by residences / tenants of Orchard Park Suites and Wisma Atria.

    5. Please refer to the attached map for more details on the diversion.

    6. The switch over of the ERP gantry operation will be carried out in December 2008, when construction of Ion Orchard will require the dismantling of the existing gantry. The dismantling will occur two weeks after the switch over whereby the new gantry will remain in operation.

    7. Existing ERP rates and operating hours apply. Informational signboards will be displayed at the junctions of Orchard Link / Orchard Boulevard and Orchard Turn / Orchard Boulevard and on Orchard Turn towards Orchard Boulevard to inform motorists of the relocation of the ERP gantry.

    8. Should motorists have any queries, they can contact the LTA hotline at 1800-CALL LTA.

    New ERP gantry at Eunos Link; two gantries up charges

    SINGAPORE: It will cost motorists more to pass through the Electronic Road Pricing (ERP) gantries at Marina South and on the slip road from Ophir Road during evening peak hours from next month.

    The charges will go up by 50 cents to S$1.50 between 6.30pm and 7.30pm from November 3.

    This follows a quarterly review by Singapore's Land Transport Authority (LTA).

    LTA said it will also implement ERP at a new gantry before Eunos Link for westbound traffic between 7am and 8am.

    LTA said this is because traffic speeds have fallen below the threshold.

    Motorists will be charged S$2 between 7am and 7.30am, and S$1 between 7.30am and 8am.

    ERP rates to go down for December holidays

    Electronic Road Pricing charges for selected gantries will come down by between 50 cents and a dollar due to the December school holidays.

    Some of these gantry points are:

    - along the CET south of Braddell Road;

    - along Thomson Road; and

    - the gantries in the Shenton Way -China town cordon.

    The revised rates will apply from this Saturday to 31 December.

    The rates will be revised back from 2 January onwards.

    The next ERP review will take place in February 2009.
