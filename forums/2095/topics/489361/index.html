ERP rates to remain unchanged in third quarter

  • QX179R
    27 Jul 15, 22:29

    SINGAPORE: There will be no changes to Electronic Road Pricing (ERP) rates for the third quarter of 2015, the Land Transport Authority (LTA) said on Monday (Jul 27).

    The rates were lowered from May 30 to Jun 28 for the school holidays, but reverted to the usual rates on Jun 29.

    The LTA conducts a review of traffic conditions on ERP-priced roads and expressways every quarter, and decides whether to raise or lower rates based on the review.

    The next ERP review will take place in November for the fourth quarter.
