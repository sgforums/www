Several roads, lanes to be closed for National Day Rally

  • QX179R
    25 Aug 12, 11:49

    SINGAPORE: Several roads and lanes will be closed on Sunday when the National Day Rally is held at the National University of Singapore's University Cultural Centre.

    The affected roads are:

    1) The extreme left lane of Clementi Road in the direction of West Coast Ferry Road

    2) The extreme right lane of Clementi Road in the direction of Bukit Timah Road Kent Ridge Crescent (between Clementi Road and College Link)

    3) Engineering Drive 1 (between Kent Ridge Crescent and Engineering Drive 3)

    The closure is from 2.30 pm to midnight on Sunday.

    Auxiliary police officers and police officers will be deployed at the affected junctions to assist and direct motorists.

    Public bus services affected by the road closures will be diverted accordingly.

    About 1,600 guests are expected to attend the event.
