    Widening of Eunos Link and Jalan Eunos from Airport Road to Sims Avenue

    The Land Transport Authority (LTA) has awarded a $28.5 million contract to Or Kim Peow Contractors Pte Ltd for the widening of Eunos Link and Jalan Eunos from Airport Road to Sims Avenue.

    The contract involves the widening of Eunos Link and Jalan Eunos from a dual three-lane carriageway to a dual four-lane carriageway. It also includes the construction of drainage works, upgrading of street furniture and landscaping works.

    Two existing pedestrian overhead bridges across Eunos Link near Bedok Reservoir Road will be re-constructed with new ramps. A new covered pedestrian overhead bridge with ramps will also be constructed at Eunos Link in front of Eunos Technolink to facilitate pedestrian crossing in the area.

    When the contract is completed around 4th Quarter 2010, the public will enjoy smoother traffic flow during peak hours when travelling between Hougang Ave 3 and Still Road.
