    Soil subsidence at Marina Boulevard

    A depression measuring 5 metres wide and 2.5 metres deep, formed at Marina Boulevard at about 9 this morning.

    The Urban Redevelopment Authority says it happened next to the slip road towards East Coast Parkway, where construction works for the Common Services Tunnel is being carried out.

    It says no one was hurt in the incident.

    URA and professional engineers appointed for the Common Services Tunnel have made assessments and established that there is no risk to drivers or the general public.

    Marina Boulevard which was temporarily closed and traffic diverted as a precautionary measure has since been opened to the public.

    URA says works to backfill the hole is in progress. - 938Live
