Power failure affects street lamps in Jurong West

  • QX179R
    28 Aug 12, 20:01

    SINGAPORE: Jurong West has been hit by a power failure affecting most street lamps in the area on Tuesday morning.

    Several calls were made to the MediaCorp Hotline in the early hours.

    Among them was Mr Tan, a taxi driver, who said he was driving from Jurong Central to Boon Lay when he noticed that all the street lamps were not working, and the area was in total darkness.

    However, Mr Tan said the power failure did not affect the traffic lights, and he did not encounter any driving problems.

    Mr Kuek, a Jurong resident, also called about the same incident.

    He also reported about an accident between an SBS bus and a van at Jurong West Pioneer Road, near Gek Poh Shopping Centre.

    Mr Kuek said he saw a recovery truck at the scene.

    A spokeswoman from the Land Transport Authority (LTA) said their contractors had been informed and they are working to restore power to the affected area.

    She said the cause of the power breakdown is unknown and it is being investigated.

    - CNA/de
