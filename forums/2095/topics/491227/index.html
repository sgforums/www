    Lane closed on Kampong Bahru Rd due to oil spill

    SINGAPORE: An oil spill forced the closure of one lane on Kampong Bahru Road during Tuesday evening (Feb 2) rush hour. 

     

    A Land Transport Authority spokesperson said in a statement at 6.40pm that one lane for traffic in the direction of Jalan Bukit Merah was closed for resurfacing.

     

    "LTA will reopen the affected lanes when resurfacing works are completed,” the spokesperson added.

     

    On Monday, another oil spill saw two lanes of the Pan Island Expressway sealed off, after an accident between a tipper truck and an oil tanker. And on Friday, an oil tanker leaked palm oil on the Bukit Timah Expressway for 10 hours, causing traffic gridlock in the morning.
