    ERP rates at 2 CTE gantries to be raised next month

    SINGAPORE: Electronic Road Pricing (ERP) rates at two gantries on the Central Expressway (CTE) will be increased from Aug 1, the Land Transport Authority (LTA) said on Monday (Jul 25). 

    The two gantries on northbound CTE, after the Pan Island Expressway (PIE), will see an increase of S$0.50, according to LTA after its quarterly review of traffic conditions on ERP-priced roads and expressways.

    Another two gantries on westbound Marina Coastal Expressway (MCE) before the exit to Maxwell Road and on the slip road to Marina Coastal Drive will see ERP rates reduced by S$1 between 7.30am and 8am, it added.  

    The next ERP review will take place in November 2016 for the fourth quarterly rate, said LTA.
