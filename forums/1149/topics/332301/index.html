    Japanese airsoft are the most solid. But if you watching your budget can go for some China airsoft. They are getting better at replicating the Japanese airsoft AEGs. Like Real Sword for instance.

    It depends heavily on what type of role you want to play, your taste of weapons and what is the skirmish ground like. If it is CQB you should get a GBB pistol or a CQB AEG like the MP5A5.

    CQB - TM Hi capas are very solid. Either the 5.1, 4.3 or the 1911 models. Newer models like the TM G17 I never tried yet but should be as good since they using the same blowback system as the Hi capas.

    Outdoor - Alot of choices here: TM AEGs, like the TM M4 or the AK is very popular. You can also get sniper rifles. Lots of those to choose from.

    China made MP5s like the one you mentioned above are probably Double Elephant or CYMA or some lower end AEG. The main difference is the gearbox and motor are of inferior quaility than the jap one. But Im very surprised to see the china AEG is 1/6 the cost of the japanese model (most probably TM?).

    If I were you I would just buy the China MP5, open it up and replace the internals (just fly to HK and buy the motor and gearbox, then bring back swap; get a thai gunsmith to do it for you): More powerful and will last much longer.
