    This is a 3 day course to be held for 3 full days on 24 Feb, 1 & 2 Mar, conducted by the Singapore Red Cross.

    The course is structured to provide the necessary knowledge and hands-on experience on Standard First Aid and CPR and includes both theory and practical sessions.

    There will be both theory and practical assessments at the end of the course.
    A certificate (3 year validity) will be issued.

    Cost of $90 / 95 (for non passion card holders) includes:
    - Training Notes
    - 2 bandages
    - 1 face shield

    * 100% attendance is necessary in order for you to take your assessment.
    * Please bring an NRIC-sized photograph (2.5cm by 3.5cm) for card-sized First Aid Certificate
    * Some stationery for notes taking.

    If you're decided, please send your names and IC no to Josephine at josephinly @ gmail.com to indicate your interest. (Payment details will be made known at a later date)
