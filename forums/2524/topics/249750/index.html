Tom Cruise & Holmes Host 9/11 Charity Dinner Costing $6K Per

  • turbo_drift

    Scientologist Tom Cruise and his wife Katie Holmes hosted a Scientology detox program/ 9/11 charity dinner in New York City yesterday. The dinner tickets for the benefit were $6,250 each.

    The dinner was dedicated to the New York Rescue Workers Detoxification Project, a rehabilitation program based on Scientology founder L. Ron Hubbard's teachings.

    Each dinner ticket was $6,250, but the proceeds went to a great cause. The money went towards assisting the workers who suffered breathing problems after 9/11.

    Even if you don't agree with Scientology, at least the profits went towards the men and women who are still suffering after helping to clean and save lives following the tragic 9/11 attacks.
