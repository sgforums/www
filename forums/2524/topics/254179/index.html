    Paparazzo Claims Actress Struck Him With A Car

    (CBS) NEW YORK Only days after joking about rumors she drove over a photographer's foot, Lindsay Lohan is being sued by the paparazzo in question.

    In court papers obtained by "Access Hollywood," Giovanni Arnold says he "sustained severe and permanent personal injuries" and "economic loss" from the March 13, 2007 incident in New York's trendy meatpacking district.

    The lawsuit, filed on Thursday in New York State Supreme Court, Bronx county, also names Lohan's mom Dina as co-defendant.

    Arnold is suing for unspecified damages and "exceeding the jurisdictional limits of all lower courts" which would normally handle this type of case.

    On Monday night's interview on Late Show With David Letterman, the talk show host had some fun with Lohan, asking her which of the many rumors about her were actually true.

    "Drove over a photographers foot?" asked Letterman.

    "No, but I should," she replied, laughing.

    "Not a bad idea," Letterman agreed.

    "The guy completely faked it," Lohan added. "It was amazing."
