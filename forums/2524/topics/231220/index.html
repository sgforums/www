Spears Dating Male Model

  • bryanseb
    12 Jan 07, 23:49
    HOLLYWOOD - Pop star Britney Spears is dating 25-year-old male model and actor Isaac Cohen, according to media reports.
    The "Toxic" star was spotted sailing around Marina del Rey, California, over the weekend on a private powerboat with her new companion.

    Cohen bears a striking resemblance to Spears' estranged husband Kevin Federline and dresses in a similar style, favoring low-slung jeans and a kerchief on his head.

    The bikini-clad mother of two seemed to be enjoying herself during the excursion, and the two shared several intimate moments.

    Cohen's agent at L.A. Models, Brandi Lord, has confirmed the romance to People magazine saying the couple's relationship began "recently...within the last month. He's got a great heart and a good family, and he was raised well. He's a gentleman."

    Lord insists Cohen isn't after the singer for her fame, adding, "(He) is not a player. (He) is not out to get a name for himself."
