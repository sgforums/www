Michael Jackson faces new custody lawsuit

  • mcfadden
    1 Apr 07, 14:25
    Nona Paris Lola Jackson, who claims to be the biological mother of Michael Jackson's three children, filed legal papers against him for a third time in the Los Angeles Superior Court to request visiting rights and child support.

    She filed the petition on March 27, reports www.hollywood.tv.

    In addition to a motion detailing how easily a scheduling arrangement could work, she further demanded that she should be given Jackson's Neverland ranch and another one of his property, even though her demands mean that Jackson retains full custody of the children.

    Lola is adamant in her claim that she gave birth to 10-year-old Prince Michael, nine-year-old Paris and five-year-old Prince Michael II, despite the fact that Jackson's ex-wife Deborah Rowe is named on the birth certificate as the mother of both Prince Michael and Paris.

    Meanwhile, the identity of Prince Michael II's mother remains unknown. She bolsters her claim by insisting that Jackson never consummated his marriage.

    'Debbie's hospital records will prove that my kids are not hers because of DNA,' she said.

    Because the court has already dismissed two previous custody attempts by Lola, Raymone Bain, Michael Jackson's representative, described her actions as 'ludicrous'.

    Court hearings on her motion have been scheduled for May 2 and May 30.
