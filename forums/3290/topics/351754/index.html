    Halo.... I also from Ngee Ann and the went to NUS..

    Firstly, do as well in ya poly as possible, if can try going for diploma with merit.

    English at O level fail or pass does not matter. As long as D7 you would be safe (dont tell me you F9, if you F9 then you have to retake O level Eng). I have friends failing O level english also able to go into NUS.

    Ya, it is true that before you enter NUS year 1, you need to take some sort of english test. If you perform well, you dont need to take any english modules. If not you have to take either 1 or 2 english modules.
