    Malaysian girl, 17, alleges:
    I had sex with 4 teachers
    July 30, 2005 Print Ready Email Article

    SHE was video-taped and photographed having sex with teachers from her school.
    Click to see larger image
    The worker and teachers from the teen's school. One of the teachers allegedly invited other teachers to join them in having sex. -- GUANG MING DAILY

    Then, the 17-year-old found her nude photos being sold in her village in Kuching, Sarawak.

    They were being sold for between RM2 and RM10 reported Sin Chew Daily.

    Now, four teachers and a school worker have been arrested in connection with the sex scandal.

    Two of the teachers, aged 34 and 39, and the school worker, 40, were from her secondary school.

    It is alleged that they first had sex with her in April last year. The teenager was allegedly paid RM200 ($89).

    The girl alleged the three men forced her to have sex with them in a hotel and taped the lewd acts on video in the first incident, reported the New Straits Times.

    The police found nude photographs of her in the men's handphones after they were arrested. A few VCDs containing her pictures were also found in the home of one of the suspects.

    On 5 Jul this year, the three men allegedly had sex with the student again in a hotel in Kuching.

    One of the teachers then allegedly invited his two friends - who were teachers from two other schools - to join them.

    The teachers were attending a course at the hotel then. The teenager was later allegedly given RM10 for transport.

    Her father had wanted to settle the matter with the teachers privately so she wouldn't be embarrassed further.

    The two teachers and the school worker were arrested last Wednesday after the girl made a police report.

    The police applied to the court to have the three men remanded for five days as investigations have not been completed. The men have yet to be charged.

    The two other teachers, aged 38 and 40, turned themselves in on Monday.

    The teenager, who was dressed in traditional Malay attire, was accompanied by her parents when she went to the police station to formally identify the two men.

    Sin Chew Daily said that the police are looking into the possibility that more teachers and students were involved.

    MARRIED MEN

    The teachers from the girl's school were married with children. The school worker was engaged to be married.

    Sarawak's Deputy Commissioner of Police, Abang Wahap bin Abang Haji Julai told The New Paper that the police began investigating the incidents after the student lodged a report last week.

    He added: 'There were video tapes and photographs of the girl in the nude.'

    He would not say if more people were involved as investigations were ongoing.

    The girl went to the hospital for a medical checkup last Thursday.

    When asked how the student was coping, DCP Abang Wahap said she was still attending school. 'But we are giving her the necessary protection to keep her from public view,' said DCP Abang Wahap.

    Malaysian newspapers reported that the men could be charged under the Child Act, which protects those under the age of 18 from being used for prostitution.

    If convicted, they could be fined up to RM50,000 and jailed up to 15 years.
