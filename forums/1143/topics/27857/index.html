    Okies, for drive-by shootings, you can ONLY use the Uzi. Hope you remember which is your fire button coz you'll need that obviously !!! You also have to know which keys you set for "Look left" and "Look right" when in the car. That's the only way you can see what you're shooting when doing a drive-by. Press the key for "Look left" and it'll show the side profile of your car and people passing by on the left side as you drive. Press the shoot button and you're shooting people, effectively doing a drive-by !!! Ditto with the right side when you click the "Look right" button and press your fire key. As for aiming, you can't. Your uzi will fire in a straight line, where you're sitting from the car, so drive up to a person, get your door roughly in line with the guy you want to shoot, then press fire. Takes a bit of practise, but you'll get the hang of it. =)

    Yes, you're supposed to HOLD DOWN the target button to target people. Or, if you're using your mouse, you can use it like a regular counterstrike crosshair and shoot people by. This isn't very accurate though, and it's not very effective when you've got a SWAT team chasing your ass from behind you and you're not paying attention. Holding down the target button effectively locks onto the nearest threat to you. See which is your preference. =)
    Personally, when I'm being chased by cops, I like to hold the target button, so I can kill some po-lice !!! If you have the shotgun, two shots hitting a police car will cause it to catch fire and blow up. Don't hold the target button down if you intend to do that.
