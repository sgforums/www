    Cheer 2003 is here!

    MALAYSIA'S national cheerleading competition is back! The Cheer 2003
    cheerleading competition will fall on June 29 and once again, it
    will be at Stadium Malawati in Shah Alam.

    We have some exciting additions to this year's competition, namely a
    college division - which means there will be a separate category,
    trophies and medals for secondary school and college teams - and a
    Best Newcomer award, in addition to the Best Dressed and Best
    Showmanship categories.

    For more information and for registration forms, contact The Star's
    Marketing Service Department at 03-7967 1244.
