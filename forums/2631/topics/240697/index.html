    For advice on what to do after A levels, the folks at the Chit Chat forum at this place will be more helpful.

    This place is a Support Group for

    - People who are intending to retake exams as private candidates in the GCE N/O/A Level examinations, and also for people who are unfortunately stuck in JC for the third year.

    - Pri/Sec/Pre-U/JC/ITE/Poly/Uni students struggling with results and gradings at school.

    - People who are having difficulties dealing with <> exam results they have, be it major or normal school examinations and tests.

    - People who are having trouble with exams (exam stress/anxiety), and need to find others to talk with about it.

    - People with any other problems that are related to exams.

    Last of all, let's all work hard to aim to do better! Remember, no one is denied the right to succeed.
