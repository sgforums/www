    I am proud of the REDS revelation under GH. However I still think that our team is not really world class compared to our rival like Arsenal or Chelsea. I personally think that Mr.Houllier really needs to bring in 2-3 real world class players if REDS is to challenge for the very top position next season. The current team is good actually, but I somehow feel that we really lack of the 'magical touch' as a team which we can identify with teams like Barcelona or even Manchester United. They always look like they are going to score. I think there are a few players that might help to transform the REDS to becom even more invincible. Top of my list is Pavel Nedved (Lazio), the Czech gifted left winger. With him in the team, Patrik Berger can roam freely behind the 2 strikers and Berger is most dangerous in that position (He scored a hat-tricks against Chelsea a few seasons ago). Back to Nedved, he possessed the skills to bring the ball to the box, also the accuracy and power to blast the ball into the goal from outside the box. Second in my list is Jari Litmanen of Barcelona. He is not a regular in the team last season and with the departure of Van Gaal he may just want to leave. His scoring ability has long been proven and he is definitely a better cover for Owen or Fowler than Meijer and Camara. His vision which helped Ajax to European success is really what we need. Lastly, we really ought to fight for the signature of Sol Campbell as he simply is the best English defender around now. He can provide the aerial threat during corners or freekicks which Henchoz can't. Campbell is definitely a more able partner for Hyypia. As for left back, I think D.Matteo really deserves a place in the team with his excellent performance for the 2nd half of last season. The right midfield role, I think Houllier may want to bring in Kieron Dyer (though he just signed for Newcastle). He is a really exciting prospect. To summarise, my dream team will be (4-4-2) Westerveld, Babbel(right back), Hyypia, S.Campbell(central defenders), Dominic Matteo(left back), Pavel Nedved(left winger), P.Berger(attacking midfielder), Redknapp/Gerrard(central midfielder), K.Dyer(right winger), Fowler, Owen(strikers) with Litmanen and Heskey as covers. What do you Reds faithful think?
