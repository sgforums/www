    Okonomiyaki is a famous delicacy in Japan, often referred to as a Japanese pancake or Japanese pizza. And almost every province has its own style of cooking it.

    Drizzling mayonnaise on food is not as easy as you think because it requires great control to intricately draw designs. One mistake and the masterpiece is ruined.
