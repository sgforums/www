    You know what we should all do more? Eat at themed restaurants.

    The Prison of Fire eatery has just opened in China and it certainly makes Pizza Express look like an absolute bore.

    Whilst the food is above jail-standard, diners can look on at the confinement rooms, water torture devices and in-restaurant entertainment – music bands who play from inside a giant birdcage contraption – whilst they eat.

    The restaurant in Tianjin City, which is a half hour bullet train ride from Beijing, opened on Saturday, and the owner said that he wants diners to ‘cherish their freedom and stay away from crimes’.
