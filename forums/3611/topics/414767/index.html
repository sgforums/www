    Up in smoke: Furious fans burn effigy of Wayne Rooney

    Last updated at 9:18 AM on 7th November 2010

    Irate football fans have burned an effigy of England striker Wayne Rooney as the furious backlash over his poor form and threats to quit continued.

    Supporters, furious at a poor World Cup performance and incendiary comments about the lack of ambition at his current club Manchester United, set fire to the figure at a bonfire night celebration in Edenbridge.

    Wearing a Manchester United shirt, clutching his new contract and a bag full of money, the effigy is the latest controversy to hit Rooney after ill-tempered negotiations with his club last week.
