    Photographer Joanna Lemanska aka Ms Cool Pic captures Paris in a whole new light. Or should we say reflection. Shooting naturally-occurring mirrored images in cities, Lemanska’s collection of photos from Paris captures unexpected moments in the Capital of France in a unique, and very wet, way. But best of all, her talent is all in the technique not the equipment–she takes some of these photos with her iPhone 4s.
