Bolivia Carnival

  • NeverSayGoodBye

    La Paz, Bolivia celebrates the Carnival before Ash Wednesday. Bolivians dressed as Carnival characters Chuta, Pepino and Chola compete in their local communities to be chosen as one of the three beloved Carnival characters. This trio of Carnival royalty, who represent gaiety, must be adept at spreading happiness and never tire of dancing, as those chosen attend all official Carnival events.
