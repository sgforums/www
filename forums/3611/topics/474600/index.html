    TV reporter Sabrina Rodriguez was left red-faced after a smiling baboon got up to some monkey business during a live broadcast

    Sabrina, 29, was reporting on a Californian grape festival – which featured a monkey show – when the primate known as Mickey clamped one hand on her right breast.

    Determined to stay professional, the presenter carried on her broadcast for the Fox 40 channel in Sacramento.

    Despite her best efforts, Mickey, who has appeared alongside Tom Cruise in movie musical Rock Of Ages, refused to remove his wandering mitt.

    Asked later if the baboon was showing his teeth because he was mad or smiling, Sabrina said: “I’m going to go with smiling.”
