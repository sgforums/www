    San Francisco's Lusty Lady, United States' first and only unionized, employee-owned strip club shuts its doors on September  after its landlord refused to negotiate on a lower rent. The club had operated in the North Beach neighborhood for more than 30 years. According to report, landlord Roger Forbes, an owner of much real estate in North Beach, gave the dancers two weeks to vacate the property.
