    A German dominatrix has been fined €200 after being accused of hurting and robbing a client.

    The unnamed plaintiff, a 49-year-old undertaker, says the woman he hired held a kitchen knife to his throat and asked for his debit card and PIN number, reports Reuters.

    The 35-year-old dominatrix, a mother of four, denied the accusation. She says her client even asked if a transsexual friend could join them.

    But according to Reuters, the court decided it was impossible uncover what really happened, as both the dominatrix and her client had "consumed too much cocaine" at the time.

    The dominatrix has been in custody for five weeks, but declined compensation.
