    Ayyoub Useila, 12, was murdered in Gaza by an Israeli missile on 11 March 2012. His body torn to small pieces even flew over trees. His cousin (another child) was injured seriously in the same criminal Israeli attack.


    The mother of little Ayyoub had earlier lost two of her children during 2 separate Israeli air-strikes.


    “Ayyoub packed his bag and went to school carrying his breakfast in his hand. A few minutes after he went. I heard the Israelis firing a missile on the road which the students use when going to school. I ran to search for my little son but he had been killed. Small pieces of his body were spread all over the place and on branches of trees. He was murdered before he could even eat his breakfast”.


    With this just one of numerous attacks, Israel is pursuing a policy of premeditated murder against Palestinians. All the Palestinians children, women and the elderly are targets of Israel.


    This is the story of all mothers in Gaza, whose children go to school in the morning without guarantee that their children will return home safely, walking on their own legs and in one piece. Some children in Gaza get murdered by missiles and others are injured with shrapnel and return to hospitals instead of home or going to school. Many others suffer pain, trauma and fears which last a lifetime.
