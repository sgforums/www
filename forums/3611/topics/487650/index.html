    Bettas are anabantoids, which means they can breathe atmospheric air using a unique organ called the labyrinth. There are up to 73 recognized species and various grouping by complexes. Usually kept in small tanks for breeding.

    Feeds mostly on insects and other small invertebrates in nature. Captive fish will normally accept dried products once they are recognised as edible. Offer Daphnia, Artemia or bloodworm for optimal color and condition

    Some build bubble nest at water surface while some mouth broods. Parents usually do not care for fries once they being to swim freely.

    Males are more colorful and develop more extended fins than females
