Inside the new World Trade Center: 13 years after 9/11

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    9 Nov 14, 13:02

     

    One World Trade Center, America's tallest building, on Monday welcomed its first tenants, publishing group Conde Nast, in a symbolic moment 13 years after the 9/11 attacks that brought down the original Twin Towers.

    Publishing company Conde Nast began moving on Monday into One World Trade Centre, a 104-storey skyscraper that dominates the Manhattan skyline.

    The skyscraper - at 1,776ft (541m), America's tallest building - is the centrepiece of the site where the twin towers once stood.

    New beginning: A security guard stands inside One World Trade Center

    Only about 170 of the company's 3,400 employees are moving in now, with another 3,000 expected to arrive by early 2015.
