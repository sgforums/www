Chill Out! the 7 Most Amazing Ice Hotels Around the World

  • NeverSayGoodBye

    Curl up in the world's first ice hotel in Sweden and gawk at the stunning ice art that surrounds you. Stay a night under the Northern Lights in the Aurora Ice Museum that is open all year round. Most of these chill hotels are only seasonal, so you might want to book soon before they melt away. You can have the experience of a lifetime with a room unlike any other in these 7 amazing ice hotels. 

     

    ICEHOTEL in Sweden 
    Want to start at the beginning? Look no further than chilly Sweden! Stay the night on a bed built from ice blocks in the world's very first ice hotel. 

    Sorrisniva Igloo Hotel in Alta, Norway 
    Don't forget, these structures can't stay up year-round! Rebuilt for the 15th time this winter, the Igloo Hotel is the only one of its kind.

    Ice Hotel in Lake Balea, Romania 
    Surely you didn't expect to be able to pull of at the next exit and find another ice hotel - This magical ice hotel stands 2000 meters above sea level and can only be accessed via cable car. 

    Snow Village in Kittilä, Finland 
    This isn't your little brother's igloo. Every year in November, one thousand trucks of snow are used to create Finland's Snow Village. That's a lot of the cold stuff! 

    Igloo Village in Engelberg, Switzerland 
    Stay a night or two in the sustainable Igloo Village. This hotel is built every season from 3000 tons of snow. Time to crawl in and get cozy! 

    Ice Hotel in Hokkaido, Japan 
    This hotel is only open seasonally. A room averages $900 per night and is the only one of its kind in Japan, so get ready to splurge! 

    Aurora Ice Hotel in Fairbanks, Alaska, USA 
    Owned by Chena Hot Springs Resort, the Aurora Ice Museum is open year round for guests to come and look around. It's not advertised, but some guests can even stay the night in one of the rooms.
