Inside a gun manufacturing factory in Kashmir (India)

  • NeverSayGoodBye

    Workers are seen in a gun manufacturing factory in Srinagar, the summer capital of Indian Kashmir, 25 October 2013. After the outbreak of militancy in Indian Kashmir in early 1990's the government cut down the quota of licensed guns manufactured at the factory, hitting the livelihood of workers and the factory. Presently only two gun factories are functioning in Kashmir.
