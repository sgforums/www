Roppongi Zombie Walk (Japan)

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    7 Apr 13, 19:10

    About 50 people dressed up as zombies early evening on Sunday, catching the attention of pedestrians on the streets of Tokyo's downtown Roppongi district.

    If dressing up as a zombie and roaming the streets of Roppongi along hundreds of other zombies sounds appealing to you, then you shouldn’t miss this event.

    Organized by a performance unit aptly named “Zombiena”, this is becoming somewhat of a regular event in Tokyo .

    No gory makeup in your bathroom cabinet? No worries, for a fee of ¥2,000 you can benefit from a makeover from a zombie makeup artist and, apparently a free “zombie energy drink”… You may need this to keep you going through the walk and the after party.
