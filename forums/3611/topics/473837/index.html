Whopping 167 kg dessert made in London

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    4 Sep 13, 20:27

    The taste of the British summer was brought to the capital today after chefs created the world’s largest Eton mess in Soho Square.

    Weighing in at 167kg (368lbs), it fed 2,000 commuters as part of Tesco’s Love Every Mouthful campaign.

    Those stepping off overcrowded tube trains were treated to a portion of the gargantuan dessert, placed in an oversized martini glass.

    The sweet treat was made using 50kg of fresh British strawberries, raspberries and blueberries, plus a generous serving of double British cream.

    Tesco fruit buyer, Paul Jones, explained: ‘It’s great seeing so many strawberries in one gigantic bowl whipped together with cream to create this British classic.

    ‘It looks like Wimbledon, the arrival of Prince George and this spell of great weather have reignited our passion for this iconic British summer fruit.

    ‘We’re selling 20 per cent more than this time last year and the giant Eton mess was finished in half the time expected.’
