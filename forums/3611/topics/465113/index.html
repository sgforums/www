Hundreds of sheep in UK marked with smiley faces

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    28 Jan 13, 21:53

     

    Smiley faces have appeared on the sides of hundreds of sheep across the UK. The image was first spotted on 20 animals grazing in a field in Melton Mowbray, Leicestershire. There have been sightings in five other fields since from Perthshire in Scotland to Exeter, 500 miles away in Devon.

    Ros Turner, whose sheep were “tagged” in Melton Mowbray says, “Passing motorists and walkers have had to double take. They can’t quite believe what they are seeing and I’ve had a few people stop me and ask what is going on. It’s like crop circle - but on sheep.

    "The animals do not seem to have been affected in any way. They are still grazing and going about their day to day lives in the usual way."

    Pransters are being blamed, but some Internet users are convinced aliens are responsible.

    Meanwhile, farmers scratch their heads and wonder why their flocks have been targeted.
