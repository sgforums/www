    The Oktoberfest in Munich is the largest people's fair worldwide. In 2012 almost seven million people attended the festival. As you can see it is all about beer. Cheers!

     

    The 179th Oktoberfest started on Saturday 22nd at 12 p.m. when Munich's mayor Christian Ude tapped the first keg.

     

    And then the beer started flowing...

     

    Oktoberfest is a bit like carnival: Everyone dresses up. This woman is indeed an eyecatcher

     

    Especially during the first weekend a lot of VIPs show up. Here we have the German actresses Christine Neubauer and Anja Kruse wearing fancy Dirndls.

     

    But there is also room for tradition

     

    Finally an occasion to wear the traditional Bavarian hat.

     

    Even the youngest wear 'Dirndls' and 'Lederhosen'

    Not everything at the Oktoberfest is about beer. There's also cotton candy, hearts made of gingerbread and other sweet stuff

     

    Not to mention the amusement park. We do not recommend the rollercoaster after drinking more than one glass of beer

     

    One of the beer-tent-owners even recruits his very own yodeller to attract more guests

     

    The Oktoberfest is the perfect place to find new friends. But you might not recognize them the next day

     

    Since the tents are very crowded a lot of people prefer to stay outside and relax on a meadow

     

    Especially if they had too much beer. You have to keep in mind that Oktoberfest-beer is quite strong containing 5.8 to 6.3 percent alcohol

     

    This year's Oktoberfest will end on October 7th.
