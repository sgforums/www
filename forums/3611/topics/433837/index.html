    Bicyclists in Pendleton, Oregon, said they are getting whacked on the head by an angry bird because they are too close to a nest he is guarding in an old cottonwood tree.

    Bird-watchers said the large bird is a Swainson's hawk and will ease up once his brood learns to fly.
