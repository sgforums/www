School made out of shipping containers

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    26 Feb 14, 15:08

    Children who can't normally afford an education have a place to learn at this Indonesian school made almost entirely of shipping containers. The school is known as Masjid Terminal (Terminal's Mosque) school or MASTER since it is located near the bus terminal mosque in Depok, West Java.
