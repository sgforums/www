Revellers gather for Indonesia's Jember Fashion Carnival

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    24 Aug 14, 14:14

    At the 13th Jember Fashion Carnival (JFC) in Indonesia, the theme for 2014 is "Triangle, Dynamic in Harmony" and consists of ten parades, which include Mahabharata, Tambora, Phoenix, Pine Forest, Apache, Borobudur, Flying Kite, Wild Deers, Stalagmite, and Chemistry. This street carnival is claimed to be one of the biggest in the world and comprises more than 850 performers parading along a 3.6 km road used as the catwalk.
