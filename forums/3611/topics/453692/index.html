    Inspired by FarmVille-type computer games on social networking sites, a small town in Jiangsu province has built more than 600 villas for its pigs.
     
    According to Chinese reports, the well-equipped villas are cool in summer and warm in winter.
     
    CCTV monitors are installed inside the villas and around the farm.
     
    These happy pigs go out for walks at 8am and return to their villas at 4pm.
     
    The manager of the villas said he believes pigs are spiritual creatures and if they are happy, then their meat will be more delicious.
     
    Public reaction appeared to suggest that pigs have better lives than Chinese citizens.
