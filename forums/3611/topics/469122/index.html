Save our bees!

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    5 May 13, 19:33

    Bees all over the world are dying off and killer pesticides are responsible. Europe's leaders could ban these toxic chemicals but we only have days left to make our voices heard.

    Quietly, globally, billions of bees are dying, threatening our crops and food. But in days the European Union could ban the most poisonous pesticides, and pave the way to a global prohibition that would save bees from extinction.

    Four EU countries have begun banning these poisons, and some bee populations are already recovering. Weeks ago the official European food safety watchdog stated for the first time that certain pesticides are fatally harming bees. Now legal experts and European politicians are calling for an immediate ban. But, Bayer and other giant pesticide producers are lobbying hard to keep them on the market.
