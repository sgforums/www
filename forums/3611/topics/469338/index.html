Tulipmania hits Singapore

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    10 May 13, 21:31

    Be wowed by some 20,000 colourful tulips at Gardens by the Bay

    Tulips have taken over the Flower Dome at Gardens by the Bay.

    Tulipmania, Singapore’s largest tulip-themed floral display, features some 20,000 multi-coloured tulip bulbs in shades of red, pink, yellow, white and purple from Amsterdam. Also in full bloom are other spring flowering bulbs such as hyacinths, muscari and narcissus.

    Along with other features such as Dutch windmills and customised wooden clogs at Tulipmania, visitors can expect to enjoy a little piece of the Netherlands right in the heart of the Gardens.

    In conjunction with Tulipmania, 50 3D giant tulips, each measuring over one metre in height, have also popped up Raffles Place. The tulips will be on display till May 20.
