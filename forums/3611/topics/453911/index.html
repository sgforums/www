Japan sprinter strips to raise funds for London paralympics

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    12 Jun 12, 22:31

    She’s lost a leg when she was 21 years old but that is not stopping her from dreaming big. Japan’s disabled sprinter Maya Nakanishi poses nude with her prosthetic leg to fund her trip to the London Paralympic Games.

    Maya Nakanishi, who is planning to compete in the 2012 London Paralympic Games, published a calendar featuring her posing semi-nude with her prosthetic leg to help fund her training and trip to London Paralympic Games.

    But with little financial assistance, she made up her mind to pose herself partially nude in her calendar to fund her trip to compete in London. The calendar of 7,000 copies is priced at 1,200 yen ($15) in Japan.
