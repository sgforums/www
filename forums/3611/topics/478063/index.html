Want To Live In This Tiny House?

    If you have ever wanted to visit the Alps in Austria, then you should do it in style. This 45 square meter house, designed by Peter Junnmann, is available for rent and will give you the most spectacular view. The best part about it, though, is its unique design… oh, and did I mention it’s only 45 square meters (484 square feet)?

    The house is called Uvogel, which is a combination of “UFO” and “Vogel,” which means bird in German.

    It was originally commissioned by a family as a holiday house.

    The minimalistic design lets you focus on the beauty that’s all around the house.

    Even though the house is simple, its contemporary design is absolutely breathtaking.

    The views of the surrounding countryside are unbelievable from inside

    And the panoramic windows let in an amazing amount of natural light.

    Anyone can rent this house, year-round.

    Although the inside is very modern, there is still a sense of warmth from all of the exposed wood.

    What this place lacks in size, it makes up for in spectacular design and breathtaking natural beauty surrounding it.
