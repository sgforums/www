Buccoo Goat and Crab Race Festival (Tobago&Trinidad)

  • NeverSayGoodBye

    This is a unique, unusual and to some, a seriously competitive sport on the island of Tobago. Every year, on the Tuesday after Easter, the Buccoo village prepares for the exciting sporting event and renovates itself into a sporting arena.

    Complete with a 100-yard track and a growing number of spectators from all over the world, the Buccoo Goat Race Festival combines excitement, entertainment and sportsmanship from an out of the ordinary sporting event.
