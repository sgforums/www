    The skill and time invested in each of these pieces was to an extremely high standard and the attention to detail on each of the cakes stunningly beautiful. From an actual working water fountain in the Athens piece to elehpants holding up the Taaj Mahal, the Collosseum made out of rice paper to precariously stacked parisen dessert and glow in the dark edible gelatine to depict the Maldives, these really are some of the best cakes we have seen as a collaborative effort for some time!
