    THIS goat isn't afraid to do things its own way - walking around using only its front legs.

    The baby animal, whose rear legs do not appear to work, doesn't let that stop it from making its way around its enclosure just like its pals.

    Leaning forward with its hind legs hanging high up in the air, the determined critter looks surprisingly steady as it walks about.

    But it does cut an odd figure as it carefully picks its way between its pals on just two hooves, with the back half of its body carefully balanced.

    After making its way up to some other goats, it cheerfully nuzzles them before heading off.

    And while it briefly brings its hind legs down on to the floor, it quickly raises them up again before walking away.


    At one point it looks as though the baby goat will totally overbalance, as its legs start to lean over its head.

    But it soon manages to correct itself before going on its way.
