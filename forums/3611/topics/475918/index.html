World's biggest chicken nugget unveiled in the US

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    11 Nov 13, 20:15

    THIS chicken nugget is an absolute monster.

    It's bigger than 720 regular-sized nuggets combined, measuring a metre long and 61cm wide. It weighs in at a whopping 23 kilos.

    That officially makes it the largest chicken nugget in the world.

    The beast was unveiled during the Kosherfest trade show in the US state of New Jersey on Tuesday. Representatives from Guinness World Records were there to weigh and measure it.

    It was created to celebrate the 75th anniversary of Empire Kosher, America's largest producer of kosher poultry.

    Unsurprisingly, no one has been quite mad enough to make a chicken nugget this huge before. A team of six people took more than three hours to cook it up, using a 1892-litre fryer and more than a kilo of breading, The Jersey Journal reports.

    The nugget doesn't look particularly appetising to us, but America is famous for supersized food. Perhaps we're just unaccustomed to their portion sizes.
