    The land-locked Himalayan kingdom of Bhutan remains shrouded in mystery till today. In recent times, the nation made headlines for the young King’s marriage and the country’s move from an absolute monarchy to a constitutional one. But not many have treaded the country’s tranquil roads and gorgeous vistas. Here a peek at what one can expect at this oft over-looked kingdom.
