    The frigid air and "polar vortex" hitting the eastern US and Canada this week has once again turned Niagara Falls into an icy wonderland.

    This week's massive storm brought cold air, snow and freezing rain down on the US East Coast, causing federal and local offices in Washington to close on Monday after it pummelled the central United States over the weekend.
