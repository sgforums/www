House decorated with over 450,000 Christmas lights

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    2 Dec 13, 13:19

    (Germany) 

    More than 450,000 Christmas Lights illuminates the House. Rolf Vogt started the Decoration of his House in 2000. Since then, thousands of People come to the House every year. 75 Percent of the lights are LED lights, to save energy and money, Vogt says.
