    Headism is a collaborative project and exhibition with artist Philip Levine and body artist Kat Sinclair.

    London born Philip started using his head as a canvas for creativity back in 2006 when he began to go bald. He did not want to conform to shaving his head like everyone else so started using it as an art form to express.


    An exhibition showcasing ten of Philip's head designs, photographed by myself, were on show at the NL Dutch Cultural Pop Up Space between 4th - 9th May 2011. The exhibition was supported by brand Gillette and gained recognition as one of Time Out's top 5 exhibitions to see in London. Headism has since gained a great deal of features and praise in both national and international press.
