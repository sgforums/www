Colourful creatures take over Orchard Road

    It's the end of the year, and you know what that means: Sentosa's epic Siloso Beach Party is just days away! To mix things up this year, SBC will introduce a "paint party" concept that promises to splash buckets of neon-coloured paint on partygoers as they ring in the new year.

    In keeping with the paint party theme, Siloso Beach Party unleashed five colourful figures onto Orchard Road last Saturday, turning an otherwise ordinary day of holiday shopping into a festive photo-taking session.

    The Siloso Beach countdown paint party will take place on 31 December 2013 from 6 pm to 6 am the next morning.
