One night in Bangkok

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    23 Sep 13, 20:03

    “One night in Bangkok makes a hard man humble,” sang Murray Head for the musical Chess in an iconic track that sums up, pithily and unapologetically, everything that represents the capital of Thailand. Made of equal parts spirituality and sleaze, commoditization and nirvana, Bangkok continues to hold tourists in thrall.
