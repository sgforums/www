Remarkable Rocks on Kangaroo Island

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    18 Jul 10, 16:41

    Remarkable rocks are one of the best known icons of Kangaroo island. They are located in the Flinders Chase National Park, over on the western side of Kangaroo Island.
    The huge rocks look like a creation of the crazy sculpturer, of should I say - abstractionist sculpturer? It's hard to believe the Mother Nature has created those rocks.

    Admirals Arch is an open cave located not far from the Remarkable rocks. This place is well known for its breathtaking views as well as the colony of New Zealand fur seals.
