Meet Cherry, a dog who can wash hair & provide massage

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    3 Aug 12, 22:12

    A dog in Taiwan has gained celebrity status in the cyberworld after netizens found out it could wash hair and provide massages, reported Sin Chew Daily.
     
    The dog called Cherry earned praises from netizens after its photograph was posted on a social networking site.
     
    Cherry's owner Liao Zheng Hui even created a special Facebook page for the celebrity dog, which had pictures of it providing massages to people.
     
    According to the daily, the celebrity dog even appeared in various Taiwanese series and variety shows.
