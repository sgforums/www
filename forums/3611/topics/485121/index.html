    The 2014 Chelsea Flower Show on Monday gave the media and some invited guests a sneak peek of this year's show.


    The prestigious gardening show, held annually in May by the Royal Horticultural Society in London, is the most famous of its kind worldwide and opens to the general public at Royal Hospital Chelsea on Tuesday, featuring hundreds of stands and exhibition gardens.



    Last year, a team of Australian gardeners won the top prize, the first victory at the show in 100 years for a team from that country, with their display of sustainable design, showing off the biodiversity of Australian flora.
