  • NeverSayGoodBye

    Over 200,000 sunflowers welcomed guests at an annual summer festival

    A girl on her father's shoulders looks through a maze of sunflowers growing in a field during a three-day sunflower festival in the town of Nogi in Japan.

    A total of 200,000 sunflowers welcomed guests for the summer festival. This is a big event for the small town, as 20,000 visitors are expected to attend.
