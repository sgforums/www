Tattoo Week In Sao Paulo 2015

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    27 Jul 15, 14:26

    This weekend the biggest Latin American tattoo convention takes place right here in São Paulo. This fifth edition takes place on days 24, 25 and 26th of july and is bigger than ever before. There will be around 460 stands where you can find tattoo and body piercing artist doing their magic on visitors.

    The artists are mostly from the whole of Brazil, but also internationals are to be found from USA, England, Japan Argentina, Mexico and Uruguay, to name a few. The winner of the Miss Tattoo election will be anounced on the 25th. Going to the convention is not only cool because of the arts, but also because you'll be supporting some social projects. And this again will help you. Because if you take 1 kilo with you to the convention (dry food, like rice or flour) you only pay half the price of the ticket!
