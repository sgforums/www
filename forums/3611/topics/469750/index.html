    21 Drag queens in Berlin come together to compete in a show casting, and it's a riot of big hair and eyelashes

    The casting of drag queens for a show was held in Friedrichstadt-Palast revue theater in Berlin, May 10, 2013. Contestants put their makeup skills and fashion know-how to the test, where a jury  awarded the overall-winner. Some of the 21 participants where also chosen to take part at the Christopher Street Day Gala Show of Friedrichstadt-Palast.
