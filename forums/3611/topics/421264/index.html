Revolting festival food: Insect pizza!

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    26 Jan 11, 00:51

    Dubiously-sourced burgers and falafel wraps that taste as if they could sear a hole through cast iron are staple festival sustenance - but is there any more disgusting a snack than insect pizza?

    Festival-goers are able to bug out on the grubby grub at the Paleo festival in  Nyon, Switzerland, which runs until Sunday.

    Want to make your own slice of insect pizza? All you need is a pizza base, mozzarella, tomato, cumin, mealworms and zophobas worms. Sprinkle invertebrates according to taste.
