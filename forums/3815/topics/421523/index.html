    FASHION TRENDS IN JAPAN? 
    FROM COURT DRESS TO DESIGNER BRANDS 
    1.Japanese Dress In Former Times

    • wrapped themselves in lengths of cloths that hung on the shoulder
    • Formal Dress
    2.From Japanese to Western Dress
    • Japanese clothing with a mix of western style
    3.Japan’s Often-Changing Modern Fashion
    • 1940s-American Style(eg. narrow-waisted long skirts flaring out at the bottom & wide belts)
    • 1950s-Red shoes, men -t-shirts, aloha shirts & sunglasses, women- colourfully patterned short pants
    • 1960s-miniskirt,ivy style
    • 1970s-basically the female equivalent of the traditional American Ivy League fashion for men;”surfer fashion”
    • 1980s-designer & character brand(brands of clothing with insignia or identified specific fashion designers’ design concept) ,bodikon(Body conscious
      • style & shibukaji (shibuya casual),concepts of ‘plain’ & ‘sober’
    • 1990s-”Bubble economy”-elements of orientalism or romanticism (eg loose socks, darkly tanned skins)
    • 2000s-Brand boom;”Tokyo Girls Collection”(targeting youths)


    4.The Future Of Traditional Japanese Dress

    • kimono commonly worn by the elders
    • Worn for formal functions among the young (eg.wedding) 

    LATEST FASHION!!!
    ”Wa” 

    •Used Japanese motifs & Traditional techniques –Newfound Attention
    •Modern products
    •Appeal to a contemporary sensibility while incorporating traditional elements—
    Japanese materials, decorations or fabrication techniques.
    •Range far & wide
    •Dresses & Shawls featuring indigo dyeing(Kyo yuzen)
    •Kimono-inspired wrap cardigans
    •Stickers — decorating cell phones featuring maki ,Japanese art(Nara period 710-794)
    •Prints motifs — sho-chiku-bai( pine. bamboo & Japanese apricot—auspicious)/tsuru-kame(crane & tortoise –longevity)

    Berry-Tinted Winter Fruity colours dominate Season’s Fashions 

    •Colours--Berry Purple
    •Emerge as colour of choice for knit tops, blouses,
    •Began prominence in Harajuku & Shibuya
    • Season’s top trendsetter



    Animal Prints
    Fauna-inspired fashion 

    •Latest undercurrent in Japan

    Cute & Punk

     

    •Not a full-fledged,
    •Head-to-toe hardcore punk but an eclectic mix of cute & punk with pretty miniskirts & dresses coordinated with riders jackets ,engineer boots, skull motif accessories , stud belts & other hardcore punk items.
    • Black hair
    •Black colour– trendy shade in make up(eye shadow & eyeliner) for a cool “dark” effect
    •Juxtaposition of hardcore-punk style clothing with frills and ribbons
    •Preconceive notions, mixing styles as they(youths) see fit
    •Like anime-inspired cosplay (costume play)
    •Now attracting to overseas
