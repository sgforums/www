    Mid Project Presentation for Path A2
    Please click here to view your project assessment schedule. All Path A2 students are expected to present their projects to a group of assessors on 29th March 2003 and 5th April 2003.

    Please take note of the following:

    1. Dress Code : Smart Casual

    2. Report to assessors 10 minutes before start of presentation session. Those who arrive late will be reschedued and marks will be deducted.

    3. All presentation format will be in POWERPOINT Format. Power point slides to be limited to approximately 5 slides, containing the project title, objective, description & work done to date, including their AES. Students are to bring along design & other available documents, if any, to aid in their explanation.

    4. The presentation session is allocated with 5 minutes for setup, 15 minutes for presentation and 5 minutes for Q&A. Please do not exceed the given time (Those with 2 person in the project group, each person will be given 10 minutes for presentation.)
