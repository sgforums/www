    Yes, internet is a Killer App that many claimed to rendered Michael Porter's Industry 5 Forces useless. You shld know tat the 5 forces are Porter's reasons to consider when entering an industry. THere are 2 schoosl of thoughts here, let me talk about the school that teaches that internet overrides 5 forces briefly

    1st Force - Threat Of New Entrants

    With the internet, this threat becomes infintely high, cos anyone, and i mean anyone can have a business on the internet, there is nothing much to stop a person entering the industry.

    2nd Force - Bargaining Power Of Buyers

    Naturally, as more people go onto internet to set up businesses and able to reach more consumers, the bargaining power of buyers become higher.

    3rd Force - Bargaining Power Of Suppliers

    Dont even have to consider this as on the internet, you go straight to your consumers.

    4th Force - Threat Of Substitute Products

    With the internet, there will be countless substitute products.

    5th Force - Degree Of rivalry

    WIth anyone able to get into the industry with internet, the rivalry naturally is a foregone conclusion.

    Basically just remember that Internet has broken the traditional brick and mortar way of doing business. It is now anytime anywhere 24/7 available to anyone with internet access. Thus, to consider 5 forces in whether to enter an industry is no longer useful, instead there is this theory called the New 5 Forces. Look up in internet marketing websites or books, you can find it.
