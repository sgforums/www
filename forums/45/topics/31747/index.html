    Um, Wicca is a religion that revers nature (not worship, just deeply respects), believes in both Goddess and God (instead of just one aspect of divinity, that says God had to be male/female). Practioners, or believers (if you would), casts spells and believes in magic (often spelled as magick to seperate it from a magician's sleigh-of-hand/ illusions) which is what distinguish Wicca from the other religions. They do believe in the three-fold law, or karma that states that whatever they sent out would be returned to them times three. That is why wiccans don't do hexing/cursing.

    Wiccans, often called witches, (<-- the term used is widely debated) are very basically (while not very accurately), what you see on the popular TV series: 'Charmed' without the special effects.

    It is ,however, NOT satanism or anything to do with the devil. Wiccans DO NOT believe in the devil. If interested, you can get more info from:
