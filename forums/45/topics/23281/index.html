Xin Lang the concert: to all local chinese song writers

  • vincentataux
    5 Jul 02, 16:42
    Hi everyone,

    This coming August, Youthopia and Aux Entertainment will be organising a series of local Chinese music concert, "XIN LANG" at Youthpark (diagonally opposite Orchard Cineleisure).

    The aim of this series of mini concert is to promote local Chinese music and song writings. Aux Entertainment is now urgently looking for local Chinese composers and singers to show off their talent in this series of mini-concert. If possible, we would like to revamp the good old days of Xinyao, where local Chinese scene extremely popular.

    If you are interested in local Chinese music and is neither a composer nor a singer yourself, we would also like to welcome you as an audience. Admission is FREE as the concert is fully sponsor by National Youth Council.

    The first concert will be held on the 10 August at 6pm and on every second Saturday of each following month. The venue again is at YouthPark.

    For more information please drop us an email at:
    auxevent@singnet.com,sg
    Or fax us at 65 6275 8168
    So dun hesitate, SEE YOU @ YOUTHPARK.

    Yours truly,
    Vincent Toh
    Event Organiser
    Aux Entertainment

    If you are wondering, what is Youthopia? Well, Youthopia is actually a new subsidiary of Singapore National Youth Council whom is always willing to support the local music scene. And Aux Entertainment is the official event company that is engaged by Youthopia to coordinate these series of concerts.
