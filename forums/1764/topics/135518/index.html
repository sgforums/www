    Sun, 11 Sep 2005
    Start Point: Esplanade Drive
    End Point: St AndrewÂ’s Road

    Gear up for the most scenic run in town. Participants of SAFRA Sheares Bridge Run & Army Half Marathon will flag off from the Esplanade Bridge, running past the Central Business District before pounding across the Benjamin Sheares Bridge and ending the run at the Padang.

    This year, corporations can also look forward to the competitive Corporate Team Marathon category as another exciting bonding activity for their employees. Registration package includes a quick-dry event t-shirt, number tag, goodie bag and certificate of participation. The competitive 21km half marathoners will also receive a finisher medal and personalised certificate of participation. Participants can also look foward to more performances, merchandise discounts and other surprises.

    SAFRA members take note! You will enjoy $2 off your registration fee when you sign up over the counter at any SAFRA Clubs.

    Closing date for competitive 21km and online registration is Sun, 21 Aug while non-competitive (6km, 12km) runs is Sat, 10 Sep.
