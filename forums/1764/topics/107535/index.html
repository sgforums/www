    But to answer some of the questions ..
    Medical teams for KLIM is.... ... NO GOOD. Also not enought of them to be distributed on the race route. Their strategy is to have an ambulance doing loops around the race route and wait for a call. Then the ambulance will do the pick up and drop off the victim back at the starting venue where a bunch of the medics are located.

    So gentelman .. its really like one of those Navy Seals mission. "You are on your own" once you hit the road in KL. For me I always bring some CASH along ... so that I can call my own ambulance or TAXI.

    KLIM marathon chicks are really scarce .. not many run the 42.195km as there are no mobile toilets provided. SERIOUSLY .. folks .. I am not kidding on this. Only way is to mark your own toilets base on the petrol stations or HOTELS en route. Normall .. I just piss on the road but as this marathon is 70% city .. there is going to be lots of spectators. Ha ha ..ha..

    Hello Fuqua, 8-)
    Thanks for visiting my site and reading up.
    Yeah.. the rules on taking stuff like gels or deep heat ? Forget the rules .. NO RULES .. just survive and get ass accross the finish line.     Hello kops21 and everybody,


    Does that means, if i get my malaysian friend to register(in order to pay lesser entry fees), he needs to go personally to collect the items?
    Yes! your malaysian friend should be the one collecting for the "group". Best tell your friend to use fiticious IC numbers that correspond to your age category. Seems this is the only thing race official check.

    How about returning of the chip? Just return the chip after the run at Dataran Merdeka, & i can get my RM$20 back on the spot? Do i need to use my malaysian friend identification to do that? Same goes for collection of certification.
    Nope! Just return the chip on the spot and get back your money. No checking. Only rule is got chip .. means got money. As for the cert. make sure your name is printed correct. Again no checking. They will see your BIB number to confirm before they pass the cert to you.

    Is it necessary to run in the Marathon Vest provide?
    Not necessary. The vest quality normally not good as the sponsors all put the LOGO there. So the race organizors have to stress all must wear the vest but who can enforce this rule ?? Normally I only wear it after the race.

    Looking at the route, is Refreshment station = water point. Sponging station = wet sponging point(no water provided). There is no mention of any isotonic drink or gel station.
    Last year .. sponge stations got water. This year I am not sure. But assume no water. Last year got isotoinic drinks after 10km or 15km into the marathon. Last year .. gel station was at the 30km mark. I am not sure about this year.

    As for the water stations .. got lots of water. But better watch out on the last 3 water stations towards the finish. Normally no more water .. dry as the sahara. Also no 7-11 shops to buy... real killer stretch.

    8-)
