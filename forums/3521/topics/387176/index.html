    Jet Charter

    This network also make us the most dependable and in the world of private travel that is critical.

    SavvyJet is managed and operated by industry experts with many years of experience in the private travel industry. We understand that traditional sources of private travel offer more disadvantages than advantages.

    SavvyJet has created a business model that works for everyone.

    Air Charter

    Our mission is to change the private travel industry for the best. We want to give travelers the opportunity to fly private without having to deal with excessive costs and without laying out large sums of cash and even risking money with jet card programs. 

    We want to help private travelers obtain the best service and safest aircraft hassle-free and at the best price on each and every trip..

    At SavvyJet, safety is our number one priority. We follow an aggressive safety program that ensures that all operators and pilots who have been selected to participate in our program have been rigorously screened and meet our stringent safety standards. All aicraft sourced by SavvyJet are operated under FAA part 135 certificates.

    In addition to the safety requirements met under under FAA, SavvyJet  requires many aircraft to be ARG/US and Wyvern rated.
