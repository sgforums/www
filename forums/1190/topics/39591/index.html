    I do have a copy of the Tales of the Sea conductor score. The size is of the original size as well(A3). Can help you but was reminded of copyright laws? Anyway, I myself do not know how to put it to .pdf format.

    All SLs SHOULD have a copy of the conductor score 'cause you will know exactly what your section is playing. Like me, who was a Percussion SL, my section always have so many parts to play. It will be crazy to take the individual part score and conduct sectionals with that. It is so much better to do sectionals with a conductor score to refer to.

    Not only that, you will know what the band is playing. This way, you can work on the balance, the dynamics and etc of your section for the different parts of the song.

    When you create a PDF file, you are actually "printing" to a digital paper, or eBook. So, what you see on the screen will be what you see on the paper. No fonts to worry about.

    You will need Adobe Distiller to "print" PDF files. Of course, you can print from any software which allows printing.
