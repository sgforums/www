Formation of Sembawang Symphonic Winds

  • psalms
    8 Feb 05, 10:05
    Dear all,

    In view of the move to a five-day academic school week with effect from 2005, the Sembawang Community Club and the Sembawang Wind Orchestra are pleased to announce the formation of the Sembawang Symphonic Winds.

    The Sembawang Symphonic Winds caters mainly to youths from the ages of 13 onwards. The symphonic winds will function on Saturday mornings from 9am to 12.30pm at the Sembawang Community Club. Practice will commence on 19th February 2005.

    The formation of the Sembawang Symphonic Winds is to encourage community service through music making. The Sembawang Symphonic Winds envisions to create an environment fostering music appreciation and awareness, in the process enhancing creativity and thinking skills. In line with the Ministry of EducationÂ’s call for the young to be more actively involved in community service, the Sembawang Symphonic Winds will participate in various community activities like charity fund raisings, visits to old folk homes and community outreach programs and performances.

    Workshops and master classes will be organized for members to better musical skills and techniques.

    Through the formation of the symphonic winds, the Sembawang Community Club and the Sembawang Wind Orchestra hope to cross cultural boundaries, to appreciate and encourage music culture heritage.

    Interested parties are advised to contact our Administrator at 96621229 or email kapella_swo@hotmail.com on further details and arrangements.

    Thank you for your attention and we look forward to your kind support!
