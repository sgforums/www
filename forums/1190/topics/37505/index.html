    Go here http://www.geocities.com/hscsab/SYF2003.html for SYF 2003 list of the order of bands + medals attained in previous SYFs.

    I had emailed the CCA branch regarding the tickets to the SYF. Read below(their reply) for more info:

    This year, we have issued 25 admission passes to each school for the day
    that they are participating in the central judging. These 25 tickets are
    meant for the parents and supporters of the band. The band members in
    their band uniforms will be granted automatic admission on the day of their
    central judging. Teachers, members of Band Directors' Association and
    WASBE will admit using their CSC cards, BDA and WASBE cards respectively.
    To better control the crowd, we will not be admitting students in school
    uniforms unless they can produce the admission passes. If you are an adult
    member of the public interested to attend the central judging and do not
    have the passes or cards, you will need to identify yourself to our ushers
    and we will try our best to get you a seat. We are expecting a huge turn
    out just like in previous years hence we have put in place this system.
