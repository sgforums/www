This concert also serves as a valuable opportunity for Mr Luk to showcase his contributions to the local wind band scene. The finale to the concert will see the participants from all 3 bands together in a combined piece - 1812 Overture Sollenelle, by P.I. Tchaikovsky.

    Through advertisements, sponsorships, and ticket sales, the band hopes to raise $10,000.00 for the Community Chest. The Band hopes that their gesture will help spread the message on the need to continuously show care and concern for the less fortunate despite the times of uncertainty and economic downturns.
