    Dear musicians,

    A few of my friends and myself have set up this new forum to cater to the needs for most bandsmen in Singapore. It is actually a continuation of our previous band forum of which was shut down due to financial problems. Now we have found a complimentary forum which can serve as well.
    It consists of categories like:

    What's Your Opinion?
    In this place, band-related topics are brought up, hoping to see some of the people's view regarding the issue discussed.

    Home of the Critics
    A new category specially put up for you people to have your criticism (positive or negative) regarding news or events. If you want to be Simon Cowell, here's your place. Take note that for this section, all entries will be validated by moderators so that it will be controlled and not go out of hand. This is very essential.

    Latest Events Coming Up
    For members to put up their latest concerts or performances. Free advertisement!

    Do You Know??
    For all to get to know new things that they might not know or realise in their band life or "music life", so to speak. A very informative category!

    Band Music Recommendation
    This is another brand new category whereby certain band music are brought up for recommendation weekly. Information regarding the piece of music will be found there. A remark will also be included to help people understand more about the particular piece as well as to know some techniques in the performance. Check it out!

    Music Clinic
    This is where you can ask anything that you've never seen before in your entire life on your part/score (e.g., Music Theory) as a bandsmen! We will try to get your doubts cleared as soon as possible!

    Join us to be part of Bandzonian family. Sign up and register as a member now! It's free!
