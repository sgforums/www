    SAF CENTRAL BAND PRESENTS
    "IN HARMONY 18: A DECADE OF MUSIC MAKING"

    The Singapore Armed Forces Bands celebrates its tenth year in music making on Friday, 5th March 2004, 7.30pm at the Concert Hall, Esplanade - Theatres on the Bay.

    Presented by the premiere band of the SAF Bands, the SAF Central Band will perform a variety of orchestral and winds selection which include John Williams' Star Wars Trilogy and Ottorino Respighi's Belkis, Regina di Saba.

    The band is delighted to have its guest Ms Katryna Tan to perform on her harp a transcription of Joaquin Rodrigo's famous Concierto de Aranjuez, originally written for guitar and orchestra.

    Katryna graduated from the University of Michigan, USA with a Master's in Harp Performance under Professor Lynne Aspnes. She has performed in many solo and chamber recitals and with major symphony orchestras to high acclaim in Australia, Japan and USA.

    Tickets are available at $15.00 each (excluding $1.00 SISTIC charge) through SISTIC. Please purchase your tickets early as they are sold on the best available seat basis.

    Thank you and best musical regards.
