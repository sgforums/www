    SINGER: FREYA LIN(LIN FAN)

    WHO IS SHE: THIS IS HER SOPHOMORE EFFORT...A VERY VERY GOOD SINGER.

    TITLE OF ALBUM: DOU SHI TA (ALL ABOUT HIM)

    RELEASED BY: SHANG HUA (WHAT'S MUSIC)

    Just bought Freya's sophomore effort on Saturday, and I am pleasantly surprised by her good voice.

    Let's talk about Freya. Li Zongsheng wanted to sign her on, only to realise he was too late. She had signed with What's. She made a name for herself by doing a rendition of Sandy Lam's YE TAI HEI. Freya is currently having her examinations in Seattle and for this album, they put a photo of a guy instead of Freya on the cover. So don't think you bought the wrong album. On the album, the company put that Freya songs have a power of soothing your wounded soul.

    Let's talk about the music...she is collaborating with mainly two producers Ling Weiwen (wrote her YI GE REN SHENG HUO) and Lin Mingyang...

    Let's talk abt the flaws...she is doing so so many covers of songs...She is singing the chinese version of the theme song Shrek...she sounds a little odd...can give this song a miss...And she did a cover of Sarah Mclachlan's Angel...for those of you who love this song...u might be a little aghast at freya for doing a cover of it again...but honestly, she does a good job with this song...and her pronunciation is perfect...as with the other english song Make Believe (Also a cover)(did a chinese version called SAN GE XIN YUAN). People who adore the song Angel, Freya's version is good too. She also did a cover of Sammi Cheng's SHE BU DE NI...so so alrite...and she did a cover of a Japanese song(ZEN ME LI KAI NI CAI NENG GOU BI SHANG XIN).

    That leaves us with 5 original songs...U probably heard the first song DOU SHI TA. It's a light R&B number...need some time getting used to. My preference is however for track 2, NAN DAO. Composed by Liu Qing (of BU ZHI BU JUE, XIN SHI JIE), it is a very slow moving song...coupled with kinda sad lyrics by Li Zhuoxiong...a slow moving song u would like after a while...excellent arrangement from Tu Yin. Freya's voice is excellent in this number...but i think ah mei could sing this voice just as well too...if only ah mei got this song.

    Wo De Gan Jing Tai Fen Fu is composed by Ling Weiwen (of YI Ge Ren Sheng Huo)...lyrics by Lin Xi...a nice ballad...WO XIANG YAO and Now I See are also more of pop in nature.

    In fact if you can ignore the fact that Freya is doing too many direct covers...this is an excellent album for those who like vocals.

    OVERALL RATING: B+

    BEST LYRICS: DOU SHI TA

    NAME OF ALBUM: REN SHENG HAI HAI (PEOPLE LIFE OCEAN WILD)

    WHO ARE THEY: CONSISTING OF 5 BOYS, ONE OF THE BIGGEST RECORD SELLERS OVER THE LAST 2 YEARS.

    As usual there are the usual 5 hokkien songs in the album...i persoanlly like the song HAO BU HAO...the kinda sentimental hokkien ballad tat is kinda touching...the rest are quite fast...

    ARTISTE: STEFANIE SUN YANZI

    NAME OF ALBUM: FENG ZHENG (KITE)

    WHO IS SHE: SINGAPORE'S PRIDE AND ONE OF THE FOUR SMALL HEAVENLY QUEENS WITH IMPRESSIVE RESULTS...BOTH CRITICALLY ACCLAIMED AND SALES RESULTS.

    Firstly, I am not a huge fan of Stefanie. But nevertheless, I was anticipating her third outing, and the fact is, she never stops to impress, and this third album is quite well, impressive.

    With the great sale record of her first two albums, Stefanie must be under immensed pressure for this album to sell. And I'm sure it does.

    She kept the producers Bao Xiaobo, Si Song, Wei Song and added Chen Wei and Chen Zihong to the list of producers. As for music wise, 8 out of 10 songs are by local Singaporeans. Hurrah!

    Her title track FENG ZHENG starts off with her just singing without any music, and it brings out the quality of her voice. She sounds a little like Alanis when she is doing this song. 20 lyricists were asked to fill in the lyrics to this song, but Calvin Yi Jiayang emerged as the winner. This song is really touching, and somehow I could feel that Yanzi is crying at the same time. Touching song, but needs more time to appreciate.

    Her first song LU GUANG (Green Light) is a fast number...sounds pretty enya like, and yanzi does a rap in it (she wrote the rap) herself. On the whole, the music sounds pretty enya like, but i enjoyed this song, a difference from Ling Que Dian and Chao Kuai Gan...

    Her two compositions REN XING (Track 3) is the best song in the album. It's also very touching and easy on the ears especially when she gets to the Bridge...wow...her technique is really good. Her other song ZHEN DE (Track 6) is the demo ON THE ROAD (from Album 2)...this is yet another nice song and easy with well written lyrics by Xu Shi Zhen. A bit slow, but u need time to catch on it.

    There are some uptempo/fast numbers like TAO WANG, LIAN XI, SUI TANG CE YAN and WO SHI WO. WO SHI WO is a duet with Sun Jing...has this kinda band sound and Sun Yanzi really let herself go...my preference is SUI TANG CE YAN...with cute/creative lyrics written by Li Zhuo Xiong. However a small part of the tune sounds like Sammi's MEI LI RAN SHAO.

    Other karaoke friendly numbers include BU SHI ZHEN DE AI WO...and AI QING ZI DIAN...these songs cater to the market needs, but then they dun fail to please the listener as they r easy on the listeners and yet touching.

    I hope Yanzi would be able to do more slow numbers though. This album is a really decent and good effort. So do support Yanzi.

    OVERALL RATING: A-

    BEST LYRICS: FENG ZHENG, SUI TANG CE YAN, REN XING.

    SINGER: HARLEM YU (HA LIN) (YU CHENG QING)

    WHO IS HE: SUPER HOST OF THE VARIETY PROGRAMME SUPER SUNDAY/ HUBBY TO ANNIE YI/ LOSER AT TAIWAN'S GOLDEN MELODY AWARDS

    TITLE OF ALBUM: HAI XIAO (TIDAL WAVES)

    RELEASED BY: SONY MUSIC

    At this time when I am writing my review, according to reports, Harlem's album has passed 250,000 and is the saviour of the bad record sales market.

    Frankly, I never wanted to get this album. It was not really on my list of must get albums...and it was not becoz of the 250,000 that I decided to get this album...it was partly due to the concert.

    It is no doubt Harlem is a capable host...he needs no host to create that kinda atmosphere...but i dislike him for doing covers...

    Back to this album, I find that it is one of the best for the first half of the year...in terms of of lyrics, music, and singing style...yah...it's really good...a wide variety...it's as good as Elva's if not better...Harlem produced this album, and wrote 8 out of 10 tracks...the other 2 (HAI XIAO and QING FEI DE YI) is by a local composer Tang Xiao Kang (no idea who he is)...anyway, lyrics maestro Adam Hsu/Xu Chang De wrote 9 out of 10 songs in this album. Reportedly, Xu Changde had some differences with Harlem at first, but it worked out nicely in the end. Although, I still like Lin Xi and Yao Qian, Xu Changde did a good job with the lyrics thanks to Harlem's constant haranguing until Xu Changde got so scared...Harlem made calls to Xu and even made Xu convince him that the lyrics to Hai Xiao is touching. Xu took abt 9 months to a year for this album, normal cases 2 to 3 months. So u should be able to see the quality of the lyrics...2nd to elva's Ming Tian album.

    Was kinda upset over HAI XIAO...it's a nice song...but Harlem's singing sounds so bitter and sad...very sa gou xue...that kinda feeling...okie...but still a nice song...can show off some vocal prowess...

    As the album progresses, you can realise that Harlem's forte is in his zhuan yin. Track 2 is very R&B and Harlem laps it up with confidence. Track 3, and Track 7 (QING FEI DE YI, my favourite, happens to be the theme song of the tv serial LIU XING HUA YUAN) is very folksy, very Harlem like, and this is the two tracks which he really excels in.

    There are other ballads and my of my favourites is Track 4, YING HUA L.A. TOKYO...beautiful music...as well as TRACK 8 GOODBYE PETER PAN'S WORLD. No matter whether it's ballads or folksy or even Fast numbers, personal favourite is HUAN YING GUANG LIN DISCO...with a disco kinda arrangement...COOL!!! GROOVY! REMINDS ME OF ABBA.

    Don't miss out on Track 6 AI DAO DI...kinda touching...is this track meant for Annie Yi?

    Harlem has been losing for the last few years at the Taiwan's Golden Melody Awards...certainly...I hope with this album, he would be able to get the crown for best male vocalist. This is a totally fresh album, and without strong competition...i would wanna him to win.

    OVERALL RATING: A-

    BEST LYRICS: ALL OF THEM ARE GOOD...
