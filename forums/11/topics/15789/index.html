    I think some people will think this is a boring topic to discuss on, but I am a believer of good music. I personally recommend a few albums which I believe are really good lor. You want to listen or not, up to you.

    1stly, I want to recommend Dreamz FM self-titled album. It is a great local album which I think those who support good music should give it a listen. Jim, Cavin & Ric are not only great singers, their compositions are just as awesome. Besides the radio-friendly "Ni Ai Ta, Wo Ai Ta", other gems on the album such as "Qing Di", "Nan Ban Qiu, Bei Ban Qiu", "Ao Ye", "Cheng Nuo", etc. are really good songs as well.

    2ndly, Panda Xiong's debut album "Ai Qing Duo Nao He". Although this might be an old album, it still remains as one of my personal faves. There are many gems in there which the radio didn't pick up, which was actually a pity. Like "Bai Tou", "Bo Li Wa Wa" & "Ye Ye Ye Ye".

    3rdly, Jeff Chang's "Kuan Rong" album. Almost all the songs were highly recommended. "Bu Hui Shou" is one of the best. It was actually an old song by Su Rui, but later Jeff covered it. Both old & new versions have their own charms.

    Er..well, that is all I can think of at the moment. All the above are purely personal opinions. Perhaps you all can add your fave albums to the list. I would like to know if there are any good albums which have slipped my ears

    Good Music Rules!!
