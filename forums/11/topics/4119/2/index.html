    Review of Elva's album

    ELVA HSIAO'S 4U

    After a long 10 months, Elva is finally back with her long awaited 4th album.

    Four You + For You + 4 You = 4 U.

    This album strives to give us 4 sides of Elva...white, red, blue...hehe...forgot about the last...

    Anyway...image wise, yes, she decides to return to a simple image.

    Yes, she has been dropped by Mcdonalds from singing the advertisement song...and yanzi is taking over...but heck...let's ignore that to focus more on Elva's music.

    In this album, Elva has sort of dropped quite a bit of R&B for some refreshing change of songs. In fact, she is able to find her own voice and walk out of Coco Lee's shadow...

    She has taken vocal lessons and yes, her techniques and voice have improve tremendously.

    Keeping Jim Lee, Jae Chong and Chen Wei and adding Chen Zihong as producers in addition to the wonderful lyrics by Lin Xi and Yao Qian, this is definitely a good buy album.

    Now let me recommend some songs from this album.

    Track No 3 WO XI HUAN NI KUAI LE

    Lyrics by Yao Qian and composed by Chen Wei. This song's MTV is way cool, music wise, it's very refreshing, and unique...this is a feel good song in this kinda turbulent days...find that Elva's singing is improving, seen from this song.

    Track 4 Kuai Ge

    Lyrics by lyrics maestro Lin Xi...he compares love to a fast song...very good intelligent lyrics...this song is rather short, and is a cover, it has a spanish-latin arrangement and it would make you wanna dance...Elva keeps her voice a little down to attempt this song...yet another song to prove her vocal prowess.

    Track 6 Zi Ji Ren

    Another lyrics masterpiece by Lin Xi...this song is rather a rock song...although it is not one of the zhu da this is a great song with the lyrics together.

    Track 9 Yin Wei Ni

    This track is composed by Elva herself. It has the sunny feel of California...it's a medium beat track and it is kinda like cappucino...when it comes to this song, Elva is at her best

    Track 8 Xiao Zhe Ai Ni

    Another piece of work by lyrics maestro Lin Xi...the music is more to zhong ban...in fact this is quite a sad song...that's what i feel...the lyrics can be quite sad...and yes...elva is once again at her best.

    Track 7 Wo Men De Ji Mo

    A slow song combining the finest qualities of Yao Qian and Chen Wei.

    Track 10 Cha Qu.

    A touching song...which uses orchestra music...a slow movin ballad...listen out to the chorus...lyrics by Yao Qian

    Track 2 Wen Zi Ji

    I've seen the MTV. It's great...composed by Shunza and lyrics by Yao Qian, this is one of the best songs in the album...feel very touched by the song and elva's delivery...a slow ballad...it would easily be one of the best songs in the year.

    Don't miss out on this great album. Support Elva.

    RATING: B+
