    wat i know abt slip disc is that u can undergo go a major operation to correct it. it is costly, but most importantly very painful. require abt 3 surgeons to perfrom, a orthopedic doc to do the spine structure, one neurologist to make sure the nerve are not affect or damage and another i forget do wat .......

    the implication is that there is risk involved like another other operation. that is certain percentage that the nerve might be affect and result in permanant paralysis.

    due to the operation risk, if the slip disc is minor, the doctor will not advise an operation. and there is an excerise that can control the slip disc from worsen.

    it is a kinda physiotherapy ....... do only after discharge from hospital, and has had rest for abt 3 months before starting this excerise. it is swimming, BUT not anyhow swim, only the following style. clump a float, can buy from sports shop, at the lower thigh region, slightly above the knee, and swim like free style using the hand BUT the leg do not kick !!!!! keep the body a straight line.

    this excerise will help to "lengthen" the body and spine becoz it is like the upper body "dragging" the lower body. kicking with the leg will stress the spine and worsen the situation. so just use the upper body free style method. the upper body breast stroke (frog style) method will also hurt the spine as the body is force to curve slight like U shape.

    another advice, change the matress to hard matress, no more soft and springy type. try getting sea horse brand (not advertising here, but theirs are hard, firm and good). hard matress is good for the back.
