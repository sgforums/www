    Think his real name is Leong Wen/Wern Fook. He has no "Christian" name, as far as I know. You can find his CDs under Asian Pop/compilations in many CD shops. A CD shop in Outram (same bldg as Chang Jiang ) sells his albums as well as many local artistes' old albums, eg. Jiu Jian, Wu Qing Kang, Edmund Chen , Wang De Yuan , etc...

    His latest book includes a CD of his music compilation. There are 2 versions of the book though, one with CD and the other without, so don't get the wrong one. His books are highly recommended, if you are the "wen2 xue2" type, but most of them are no longer selling. Recalled seeing only "Zi Ran Tong Chuang" in bookshops...
