  • jaslwc
    12 Oct 02, 08:45
    well, minyao is more to songs from taiwan, like "lan hua cao", "ye bai he ye you chun tian", "gan lan shu"... etc
    xinyao are songs from singapore, during around 80's to 90's, sang by singers like eric moo, shui cao san chong chang, li fei hui, liang wen fu, yan li ming... etc
    in min ge can ting, different groups of performers tend to perform different sets of songs. Some only perform xinyao songs, with very little pop songs. Some perform more pop songs with a little mix of xinyao songs. finally, some groups have a good balance of xinyao & pop songs! u have to see what suits your preference lor!
