    Pardon me, but I think the term "yan chang hui" just means a (singing) concert.

    The term "yan chang" is not to be split into two words as "act" and "sing" but rather as "yan yi" (express) and "ge chang" (sing). Hence, "yan chang" will mean "to sing expressively".

    But how an artiste wants to "yan yi" his/her "ge chang" is up to him/her lor. Some may choose to perform stunts whilst singing (Jay playing basketball whilst singing), play piano whilst singing (YanZi's forte), "strip" whilst singing (Karen Mok's talented in this), tease a guy whilst singing (A*Mei's fave act)...
