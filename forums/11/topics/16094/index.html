Dreamz FM 3 --- A Review

  • Fei_Chuan

    Album: Dreamz FM 3 -- Hear it, Like it, Play it...
    Singer: Dreamz and Flying Machine (Dreamz FM)
    Company: Hype Records
    Released Date: 301001

    Comments: After one year of "secret training", Dreamz FM finally released their third album. You may be wondering wat to expect from it, but believe me, this time, they are sure to give you something very different.

    Recommended song #1: Shou Hu Xin
    This is a song with simple lyrics and yet it touches your heart. The light melody with great lyrics is similar to what Bu Zhi De has, that is, it leaves deep impression.

    Recommended song #2: Love at Seven- Eleven
    The song title sound so odd but the way of starting the song and ending the song is sure to make u like it. Want to koe how special it is?

    Recommended song #3: Stay with you
    Especially for those who wish to propose to their counterpart. One of the song that you cannot miss listening.

    Recommended song #4: Bad Habit and Tactics Expert
    Dun reali koe how to translate the song correctly from chinese to english, but guess the meaning is there. In this two songs, Dreamz FM use a different way of singing style, more "rock" and more 'jia yin'. A new Dreamz with different style. You can expect a different Dreamz.

    Other than that, there is 3 english songs. "Should I stay?" shows the difficulty of giving up an relationship. Well, they do really well singing this. This shows that they can oso launch an english album. Reali so good? Well, hear it and you will know. Bonus track like "The V in You" and "Live it Up" are songs that most of us have heard it. Now included in this album as bonus track, we should haf a good time enjoying it.

    And sumthing more special. The album include three track that not mani album will haf and that is a demo- kind track and two 'personal talk'. From the former, u will hear the "raw" demo of how some of the songs are recorded and hoe Dreamz finally decide on the style of singing. This is certainly new. The later two is about their personal feeling on the album and how it is made possible. Hmm.. i can sae.. it is reali true from their heart and touching.

    In conclusion, tis album is reali worth to listen. All their own production and effort..... If you haf sum money to spare, why not take a walk to the nearest CD shop and buy it? U will not regret it... They touched u... tis time..

    From this album, i haf found the song that represent me. how abt u?

    Rating: Wat do u all think?

    I think this album is pretty experimental by local standards - experimental as in they were actually trying out new formulas...a la Jacky Cheung in Xue You Re. But it is a very different kind of experiment from that of Jacky, of course.

    To be frank, I never regret forking out money for Dreamz FM music, all 3 albums are good in their own way, and I know for sure that Dreamz FM = quality music, so my money is well worth it. Just a few spins had already justified my words.

    Bu Zhi De may be the song that made them better known to listeners, but for sure this is not the only song which will endear to you. In this 3rd album, I like Shou Hu Xing and Ai Yao Zen Me Jie best. But these are only the current faves. I realise that everytime I listen to Dreamz FM albums, I never fail to like increasingly like some songs which I previously thought were a little plain, eg. Ao Ye in the 1st album, and Ni Zou Ye Hao in the 2nd. Some of their songs need a little warming up, but what is for sure is that all Dreamz FM songs are sincere works which have the word QUALITY GOOD MUSIC written all over. Moreover, if more listens make you like a song more, it definitely means the song is of lasting quality, not like certain songs which will just fade out of your memory within minutes.

    We don't have to buy certain albums just because every other person in the streets is buying it. I just buy albums which are worth buying, and support music which deserves long-due credit, and all these translate to...

    "DREAMZ FM 3 - Hear It, Like It, Play It"

    Rating: 4/5 stars ( Shou Hu Xing )
