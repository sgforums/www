    The Butterfly Lovers Concerto (Liangzhu) is one of my favourite pieces of symphonic music. You can actually 'hear' their love story unfold. Through this piece, one can really understand how powerful music can be, even without words.

    For those who are new to symphonic music or the Chinese orchestra, this is a good concert to try. Liangzhu is a very captivating piece. You should enjoy it. And Chinese orch is not just for the middle-aged and above.

    Some of my friends shun classical/symphonic/orchrestra music without even trying. They just say it is too 'cheem'. Well, how do you know that you will not like it if you have not given it a chance?
