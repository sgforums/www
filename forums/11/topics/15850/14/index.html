    Yerp... there is lots of gloom but hey, there's music too. Not sure about the others but whenever I am down, all I need is a nice piece of music (be it contemporary pop or classical), and I can feel my worries melt away, replaced by a certain cold, detached calmness that can see the situation in the proper light. And more often than not, hope is born and solutions stumbled upon in time.

    And... SM Lee Kuan Yew also mentioned in his memoirs about music being able to uplift the mood, the spirit. Can't help but agree.

    There are some songs with pretty encouraging lyrics, like Ah Mei's Lu3 Cheng2 and Wang2 Jie2's Lu4. But well, somehow, sad love songs will work for me too! So Jieqi can play all the sad sad love songs and I won't complain!

    In short, music does wonders. And the job of musicians and singers and deejays becomes pretty important if you see music in that way.

    To sum it all, here's an excerpt :

    For do but note a wild and wanton herd,

    And his affections dark as Erebus:
    Let no such man be trusted. Mark the music.
