    And... my understanding about outings (especially irc channel outings) is that once there is an outing, chances are, the channel collapses. It's like the people will evaporate together with the sense of mystery. Also, outings are often debased by desperados as fishing trips to get to know gals, or insurance agents who want to cut a quick deal. It would really pain me to see that this thread of ours be reduced to just another avenue for exploitation. I suppose there have been too many bad expereinces with IRC channels that were painstakingly built only to be exploited.

    Just hope that people with not so honourable intent will stay clear or leave on their own accord when they understand the objective and spirit of this thread.

    Honestly, I would prefer that this thread of ours would perpetuate and remain "totally different though...cool-headed..mature...focused...a joy to read".

    [color=indigo]Chance or Choice

    When we meet the right person to love, when we're at the right place at the right time, that's chance.

    When you meet someone you're attracted to, that's not a choice. That's chance. Being caught up in a moment (and there's a lot of couples who get together because of this) is not a choice. That's also a chance.

    The difference is what happens afterwards. When will you take that
    infatuation, that crush, that mind-blowing attraction to the next
    level?

    That's when all sanity goes back, you sit down and contemplate whether you want to make this into a concrete relationship or just a fling.

    If you decide to love a person, even with his faults, that's not a
    chance. That's choice. When you choose to be with a person, no matter what, that's choice.

    Even if you know there are many people out there who are more
    attractive, smarter, and richer than your mate, and yet, you decide to love your mate just the same, that's choice. Infatuation, crushes, attraction comes to us by chance. But true love that lasts is truly a choice. A choice that we make.

    Regarding soul mates, there's a beautiful movie quote that is so true about this: Fate brings you together, but it's still up to you to make it happen.

    Soul mates do exist. That there is truly someone made for you.

    But it's still up to you to make the choice if you're going to do
    something about it or not. We may meet our soul mates by chance, but loving and staying with our soul mate is still a choice we have to make.

    We came to the world not by finding someone perfect to love...BUT to learn how to love an imperfect person perfectly..
