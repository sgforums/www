    Noticed a lot of singers nowadays also tout themselves to be composers. Some of them started out just singing, and after a couple of years, foray into the composing arena. However, are all of them competent composers? Or are they just pretenders who write a couple of mediocre pieces so as to pass themselves off as "multi-talented stars"?

    For one, I was quite impressed by Gigi's composing talent. I have always thought of her to be just a pretty thing, nothing else. But after listening to "Hua Huo" and "Shen Shuo", I was utterly dumbfounded by her song-writing skills. This girl can really write!

    However, I can't say the same for many others, eg. Andy Lau. I quite like him as an artiste, but his song-writing (esp. Ma Tong, I really can't stomach that)...DUH...To me, his song-writing was "born" more because he was a willing learner of many things, hence he gave a try at song-writing too. But his attempts make me wish that he will just stick to what he does best, which definitely does not include song-writing.

    Just a word to all wannabes out there: Just do what you do best, and don't undermine your ability or success by foraying into areas which you don't have any clue about.
