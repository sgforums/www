    Dear All

    Whilst we rejoice in this festive season, we can perhaps also spare a thought for the less fortunate.
    There are many ways you can help to bring a smile to the less fortunate during this joyous season.

    * Donating general food items such as rice, sugar, biscuits, canned food and other daily necessities for distribution to the beneficiaries. You can bring these food items to BB Sharity Gift Boxes nearest to you (see attached list at end of message).
    * Purchasing a $20 FairPrice-BB Sharity Gift Card at all FairPrice outlets and food hamper will be delivered to the beneficiaries for you.
    * Making someone's dream come true by fulfilling specific Wishes. You can adopt Children, Elderly or Family Wishes at any Wish trees
    * Do charity online at http://sharity.e-mart.com.sg/ to purchase Sharity food hampers or fulfill Wishes and they will be delivered to the beneficiaries for you
    For further enquiries, you can call them at 1800-SHARITY (1800-7427489) or email at bbsgb@bb.org.sg.
    You can also find out more at http://www.bbsgb.org.sg/index.html
    Please help to spread the word. Every bit helps.
    Let's care and share with the needy and make this festive season a happier one for all !
