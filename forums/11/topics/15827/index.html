    Not a bad show...though the movie title is quite a mouthful Leon Lai & Maggie Cheung do have quite a bit of chemistry between them, they looked quite natural as a couple. dunno why but I felt that Maggie looked a little haggard in this show...maybe it is due to her minimal makeup, since her role as a taxi driver needed little of that.

    Quite a good romance comedy, according to HK's standards. The style is quite un-Wong Jing...though some parts of the movie were not very suitable for kids Ge Ming Hui & Wu Yao Han were the ones who provided the laughs, & really livened up the show.

    Rating: 3.75/5
