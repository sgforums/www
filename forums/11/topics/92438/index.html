    Singapore Hit Awards 2004

    MediaCorp Radio's Y.E.S. 93.3FM and the Recording Industry of Singapore (RIAS) are proud to present the Singapore Hit Awards 2004! The star-studded Awards will take place on Friday, 10 September, 7pm at Suntec Halls 601 & 602.

    There are a total of 21 award categories this year and fans can expect stellar performances from veteran and promising artistes in the local and regional Chinese music scene. We are also working with radio stations in Mainland China , Taiwan , Hong Kong and Malaysia to present the "Most Popular Regional Artiste" awards. Listeners from these four countries will be able to show their support for their favourite artistes by casting votes online at the official Singapore Hit Awards 2004 website. Fans can also vote for their favourite music video in a new category - "Courts My Favourite MV" at all Courts stores.

    The Singapore Hit Awards 2004 tickets are on sale exclusively for RADIOCard members from 24 July to 31 July.

    Tickets will be available at all SISTIC outlets from 1 August onwards at $28, $38, $58, $88 (exclusive of $2 booking fee).

    For more information on the Singapore Hit Awards 2004, log on to http://www.singaporehitawards.com.sg.
