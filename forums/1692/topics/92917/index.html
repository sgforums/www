    Yah, MTB=Mountain bike

    GT is quite a reputable company when it first came out with its mountain bike series in 1991. That was when they had that unique "U" brake for the rear, and that patented "triple triangle" construction. I had the GT Karakoram model. It was still made in Japan, but subsequently the rest of GT bikes were made in Taiwan.

    GT itself underwent several changes I think. They nearly went bankrupt at one point, and their market share gradually dwindled as new competitors entered the MTB market...Specialized, Trek, Kona, etc...
