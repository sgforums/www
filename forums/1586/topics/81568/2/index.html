    Originally posted by dumbdumb!:
    just curious.. what is the temperature limit thats the safest? my CPU is 40 degrees and my motherboard is 36 degrees
    Your CPU will shut itself down (AMD) or throttle itself down (Intel) if it gets too hot. This won't happen until at least 75c.

    Generally, the lower the temp, the better. The amount of electrical resistance increases proportionally to the amount of heat there is in a chip, the lower the resistance, the better.

    anything below 50 is OK. When ur running at 50+ degrees (centigrade), thats when i would look for a better cooling solution.
