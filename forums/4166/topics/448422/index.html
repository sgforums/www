    Hey guys!

    Anyone interested in Adventure Races?

    If you are, X-Physique is a must-join!

    X-Physique is an urban adventure race proudly organised by Nanyang Technological University (NTU) Sports Club. It combines a plethora of sporting elements such as kayaking, abseiling and cycling, with intriguing puzzle stations to uncover their route. Each year, we surprise participants with new sporting elements that were only revealed during the event. In X-Physique 2011, we had tree climbing!

     

    This year, X-Physique will be held on 20th May 2012 @ ARC (Alexandra Retail Centre)
