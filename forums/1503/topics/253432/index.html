Slumber

  • LazerLordz
    9 May 07, 17:49
    He lies, arms akimbo
    Face deep in slumber
    A waft of wind, unlike in the snow
    blows around the little toddler.

    His mother carries
    a bag of wisdom
    bound in color
    dressed like black berries

    For she has not seen
    her husband
    in Yangzhou
    for half a decade, since.

    The books pile up
    as she walks in
    she reads to him aloud
    so that a good school
    may take him in.
