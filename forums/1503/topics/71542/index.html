    A little bit of light
    Brings hope to a world of darkness

    A little bit of compassion
    Brings joy to a life of sadness

    A little bit of money
    Brings food for a hungry man

    A little bit of kindness
    Brings smiles to the faces of the needy

    A little bit of words
    Brings a 2nd chance to a suicidal boy

    A little bit of tragedy
    Brings a new life to a diseased patient

    A little bit of greed
    Brings strife to a happy time

    A little bit of insanity
    Brings escape to a lost soul in our chaotic world

    A little bit of darkness
    Brings fear to a time of uncertainty

    A little bit of gloom
    Brings a tear to a young girl's eyes

    A little bit of war
    Brings suffering to the innocent people

    A little bit of violence
    Brings relief for a pent-up teenaager

    A little bit of death
    Brings a closure to my life
