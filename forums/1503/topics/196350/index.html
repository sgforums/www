Gullible

  • HENG@
    20 Jun 06, 10:21
    Sweet honey-ed sugar-coated words,
    They sway me so effortlessly, my achilles heel.
    I don't even know it, and suddenly I am,
    Plunging,
    Plunging.

    Deep into this dark lonely well,
    This hole in the ground of trust misplaced.
    Surprise, shock, a double blow,
    A precious gift I give so easy,
    My nature so fatally susceptible
    .
    Perhaps when all is said and done,
    I'm just a deluded fool in a padded cell
