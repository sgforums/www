They Remind Me Of You...

  • pipi & poot-poot
    10 Jul 04, 00:17
    The dewdrop from a petal,
    A snowflake from the sky,
    That trickle on my window pane,
    The teardrop from my eye...

    The giggles of a baby,
    A hug from friends I know,
    The fragrant smell of Autumn leaves,
    And the way the sunset glows...

    The beauty of a rainbow,
    And that gentle evening breeze,
    The waves on our shoreline,
    And the way you blew a kiss...

    The friendly smile of strangers,
    A chirp from birds on trees,
    The orange from a candle-flame,
    That scar above your knees...

    The melody you yearned to learn,
    With its lyrics cut me deep,
    Or that moment when you said good-bye,
    Which was the day I start to bleed...



    Penned by: Pipi
