    Current Voluntary Groups in DPS

    Moderators (Upon invitation only)
    Friends of DPS (All are welcome to join)
    Working Committee (Subject to approval)
    Judges, for Judges' Choice Category (Upon invitation only)

    # You might be required to join our newly form working committee. (subject to approval) You might also be invited for an informal coffee session in order for us to get to know you better.

    DPS Poetry Writing Competition

    For Friends of DPS

    You might be required to assist in the designing of poster.
    We need you to publicize the competition to your friends who might be interested to participate in the competition.
    We need you to vote for your favourite poems in the Open Category.
    If you write poems, we need your support by participating in the competition.

    For Working Committee

    You will be in charge of organizing the competition from deciding on the theme, invitation letters to the judges, monitoring the votes, organizing the prize presentation coffee session, etc
    In the future we might need you to source for sponsors for our prizes.
    We might also near you to publicize the competition to schools.

    Poetry Reading and Book Launching Session

    For Friends of DPS

    In time when none of the moderators are free to attend the above-mentioned events, we might need you to coordinate and attend them on behalf of DPS. We also need you to share your experience with the rest of us in DPS after the event.

    DPS Coffee Sessions and Activities

    For Friends of DPS

    We need you to source for ideal locations for our coffee sessions & poetry reading sessions and of course to attend them if you are free.

    For Working Committee

    You will be in charge of organizing DPS Coffee Session and Activities such as poetry reading sessions. DonÂ’t worry we will take turns.

    Other ways you can help us

    For Friends of DPS

    If you have a blog, we need your help to publicize our activities such as our poetry writing competition.
    We need you to assist in monitoring the forum and alert the Moderators if you notice any inappropriate materials being posted in DPS.
    We need you to publicize DPS to your friends especially those who are interested in poetry.
    If possible, we need you to create creative poetry writing exercises and or other interesting threads in DPS

    For Working Committee

    As above
    You are required to attend our online meetings on a regular basis.
    You are required to work closely with the Moderators.
    You are required to source for other platforms to promote DPS.
    You are also required to establish and maintain good rapport with poets. (both local and overseas)

    For those who are interested in joining the Working Committee you need to have interest in organizing/planning activities and meeting people.
