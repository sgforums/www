An Old man's Rain...

  • Devil1976
    19 May 04, 17:32
    Morning... A wet weather... I stood by the door as the train was about to stop... Bringing me to work... Just another day...

    Then the floor... It's wet... It's insignificant... I traced the water to its source... Its owner...

    An old man.. In a jacket... In a trousers obviously too small for him... Stood by the side of the door... Expressionless... All wet...

    The air-con blew on me... I felt the chill. I looked at him.. He didn't move a thing... His thoughts seemed stagnant. I looked at the floor.. As if it's drawn out of him... Together with it... I saw his hope... I saw his energy...

    He's not dead. Could be worse than that? What's the coldness compared to the 'emptiness' inside of him? Hollow... As that's what he seemed...

    He don't seems much affected by the way I looked at him... I wanted to take a step closer... The train came to a halt. I walked.. Off to work...

    It's just strange... That this old man and his rain kept coming to my mind....
