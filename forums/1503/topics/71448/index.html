This poem's for my 2 best friends.

  • archdoinky
    2 Mar 04, 10:54
    You should have listened before you even dare,
    Now look at the stir and the people who stare,
    Don't start saying that your decisions are final
    And that your own emotions is the only vital.

    How could you say force when there isn't resistance
    what they've said they've spoken needs no persistence
    Cause deep within you is the fire to win
    and that losing or failing is as good as sin.

    In times to come, I hope it is now
    You'll learn that no one will be happy, no one will bow
    to such whims and fancies that it takes 'em by surprise
    when they see your farce stripped by disguise.

    Think again and think it through
    There's so much solutions, ways that will do
    Apart from this road, you've taken thus far
    Take off that mask, that face smeared with tar.

    Sometimes it's wise to look at what animals seem,
    Of minute ants, so tireless working as a team
    And when one man down, could the rest survive?
    Will they just wither and cease to thrive.

    So don't blame them for their anger, resentment or fear
    They once depended on your talent, your creations, your tear.
    The tears that will drip to the floor so bare
    When you see them laugh without a care.

    Yet, I understand your cause of emotion
    It's so hard to work without much of a caution
    And when things all come to a bout
    I will find it hard if someone scream and shout.

    His intention is precise, it's very clear
    His desire to win, in his heart he holds dear
    So when ideals clash and conflicts arise
    He can't just stand and watch with angered eyes.

    He chose to boss and push us humans around
    It was then you knew that argument abound
    So what did you do? Did you play the game?
    Did you went along like a tiger, tame?

    And as for you, I've mentioned much
    With words I've adviced with elbows I've nudged
    That someday you will feel the bout of your words
    Then you'll know how it feels to be dirt.

    No one likes to be pushed aside,
    Ordered with harshness, demands with vice
    And that's the one lesson you should learn
    To always always look back and turn

    I'm siding neither side in this
    Cause I see the silver admist the riot of peace
    It's hard I know, to compromise
    It's easier to fight and say your goodbyes.

    But now I'm telling this trio, so listen good,
    Life is not without it's up and down mood
    It's the variation that matters, the way it is seen
    That determines your attitude from the places you've been

    Take a sit on this ride of life
    And stop treating this like a fatal dive
    Where one solution stops and refuses to bother
    Always remember that we all need one another

    When it comes to playing this game of chance,
    This shall not be your last dance
    [/b]
