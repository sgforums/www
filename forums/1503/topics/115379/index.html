  • droopyeyes

    if tears can speak my thoughts,
    i am never a quiet person by nature.

    times and times i see you there,
    but have you ever look up and acknowledge my presence?

    i yearn for the care you've showered for others,
    no doubt you will not do the same for me.

    forever i will not wait,
    eternity is what i'll do.
