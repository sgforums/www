Sleep, dear heart,

  • thehappybunny
    27 Sep 08, 01:57

    Light, burns through this skin,
    A morning, which aids my dying.
    Sorrow that brims in me,
    I'm trying hard to fall asleep.

    World, that I hate to face,
    I need a place, a sanctuary
    where walls, will cage me in,
    and it's there, my soul will drip.

    I could see you, smiling,
    but you never looked at me,
    Discarding, those old feelings.
    Go now, he'll set you free.

    Left alone, hell in limbo,
    no goals, no hopes, no dreams.
    Without reason to live, sleep, dear heart,
    it's time you stopped bleeding.
