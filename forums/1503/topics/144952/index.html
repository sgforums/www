    This poem i wrote two years when i just started writing poetry, it's the first poem i really like myself at that time so i thought it's appropriate to put it here. I think this could also be my worse poem here ever.

    What she loves

    She loves her dark straight hair
    She loves her long deep stare
    She loves her dark black eye
    What she loves is not I

    She loves her pagan art
    She loves her cold dark heart
    She loves her painted nails
    She loves her frightening wails
    What she loves, I fail

    Beside the grave she kneels
    Hollow whispers as she feels
    Lonely heart she longs to kill
    What she loves is so few

    I love her dark straight hair
    I love her smell and fair
    I love her weary blur
    What I love is her

    Her look is sad
    Her smile is glad
    About her, i'm mad
    I want her so bad

    She knows what she loves
    I know she doesn't love
    What I love is love
    What she loves is not love

    I had a crush on my teacher when i wrote this and i turned her into a gothic girl here not that she's gothic looking or anything
