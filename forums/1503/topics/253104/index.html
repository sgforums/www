    Roleplaying

    He's sitting here but when you talk
    to him, he does not answer; his eyes
    hardly blink, his mouth hangs open;
    on our television, the warrior
    that he named after himself slays
    dragons, trolls and evil wizards
    in our living room; only his hands
    twitch, they become his brains,
    the controller becomes his nerve
    center; he is here and not here
    because he's in there, in that world
    someone else created for those
    who prefer fantasy over chores.

    ****
