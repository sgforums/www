    diff between nydc and big o....price...ambience...crowd....nydc food is more to the pasta and italian styles...they are more known for their desserts...at the O, u get a more complete meal....abit more ex tho...but the most ex is 18.90 which is the lamb and the steak...try the big o platter for 2....32.90 i tink....damn filling...want cheap good and alot.....fish n chips....bacon wrap chicken leg is my favourite....for cakes....indecent obsession and g-spot(warm choc fudge cake) but a must try is the spike d cup.....durian choc cake....famous for that....hope it helps...personally all cakes are good...except the mocha cheesecake and tiramisu....mocha is too heavy and very jelat...tira is quite small and not worth the price....and theres an ice-cream and cake combo from 3-6 every weekday...oso applicable to public hols which falls on weekdays....
