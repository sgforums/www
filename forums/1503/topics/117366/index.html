    Clubbing

    feeling estacsy high,
    letting my soul fly high.
    moving lights,
    flashing bright,
    shaking to the sound of the night.
    legs swing wild,
    hands high up,
    enjoying the sensation,
    cloud nine.
    gulping down,
    concoctions of desire,
    in tune with spirits of the night.
    Fantasy?
    or reality?
    sober mind we are not in.
    savouring the excitment,
    grew,
    and grew it did.
    never cease,
    never fade.
    and when it did,
    so did the rhytmn.
    rhytmn of the night.

    In the game of love

    selfish is what i am known to you,
    but it never cross my mind before.
    you placed your importance above all,including the me,
    you claimed you loved the most.
    The words i said is not what i meant,
    yet took it to heart you did.
    temptation is luring me,
    in times i felt defeated,
    in my heart u soared,
    making it difficult to forsake you for it.
    i longed u so,
    but is that true?
    or loneliness make me do.
    am i the one?
    or are you the one?
    pondering in my mind it did.
    struggling against how i really felt.
    in time i hope,
    winter will turn spring.
    melting my doubts as ice turns river.
    for that time to come,
    i really yearned

    Promises

    "Gods may die",

    "Sea shall dry",

    "My love for you,"

    "Will never die."

    Streamed my blood,
    Warmed my heart.

    All is heard.
    None is done.

    Words like this,
    Doubt my heart.

    Scribbled on sand,
    it is not?
    Carved from rocks,
    i think not.

    Gone with the waves.
    Gone with the wind.

    Perish to void...
    I whispered: "Jerk"
