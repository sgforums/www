    The Winged Mind

    Why is it?
    People cherish broken vases
    Like they weep over
    A lost love

    Whither the lines that stay
    Glaringly visible
    After momentous patching.
    Love is not loctite.

    The soft sheen of care
    Protects the storm inside
    From eating up the innards
    And the blanket of life.

    Have we ever stopped?

    Sacred
    Breath.
