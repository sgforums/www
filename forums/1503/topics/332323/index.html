A Dream's Scion

    2 Oct 08, 00:30

    (I'd love feedbacks/ reviews/ critiques please.)

     

    Trifle through drops of Morning Dew

    My Swedish Emir upon a Unicorn’s faith

    His heart, it yearns for eyes of Truth

    Dashing across swarthy valleys of Wraith

     

    Flowing silvery, this River of Honey

    Ample with Milk, for all Sacral Seeds

    Come gavotte with me, deep through Prairies

    Where our Thoughts, shall it be palsied

     

    Prance, through and through, bridged so trew

    Take me away amidst trees of Noble Depth

    To cottages, warmth-filled with Banshee’s Virtue

    To meadows of Oasis, where Mermen have delved

     

    Carry me away to where leaves crackle naught!

    To where Orchestras of Dagon holds Concertos

    Landscapes of Pure wails, Lover of Tales besought

    To riddles of crafts, enrapturing Limbo’s Crows

     

    Carry on my Swedish Emir, afloat all peccant Bloom

    Cover me with spectrums of radial Nacres

    Silken Royalty, exudes halting fume

    Nevermore, this Dream be of sanguine Nectar....

     

    -Leah
