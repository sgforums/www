    Dialect-ic

    Mai hiam.
    Johnny told you
    about the gold Skyline
    but he did not
    think I would steal
    his thunder.

    Even the Sunny hor
    will be faster
    than your silly little
    Tuscany.

    Or Tuscan Sun.
    of lazy Italian summers
    and wised-up women.
