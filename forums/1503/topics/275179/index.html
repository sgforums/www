    Sing to me, your song, my song, our song.
    Sing to me, Song for a free world,
    like an image,

    Everybody needs somebody.

    I promise you nothing gonna change.
    Lady Madonna, don't give me

    Your lips, rounded like a letter Â“OÂ”.
    Kiss me! Kiss me mama!
    I will feel at ease if you shoot

    So life goes on,
    Nothing is free from nothing.
    Hundred times I fall,
    Life goes on.
    Guess this is my last fight.
    What did I see in you?

    I am just standing there, like a dumb
    bird. Standing there forever,
    killing the time.

    Remember the silver dust lane?
    It still lies in the bottom of the sky.
    Those sweet and beautiful days.
    Frankly I never look back again,
    Miss you so much, Miss your last smile.
    I wish we could see the light of heaven,
    Just the two of us like spring leaves, green,
    in neverland.

    Hold on
    Hold on
    Hold on
    Hold on

    So long my memories.
    Today I dream on the kitchen floor.
    Today is gonna be a day for you.
    It is time to set you free,
    free you all!
    Everyone, everyone!
    But let me play back the music one last time.
    Please, one last time,
    one last song -

    Rosy.
    Oh sweet little music.

    You still make me smile,
    Love Psychedelico.
    You are the best.
