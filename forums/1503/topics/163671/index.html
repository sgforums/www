    An Appointment With Eternity

    Your eyes meet mine
    In a place where
    Language keeps a mistress
    To bear false tongues
    And paid witnesses.
    you look beyond me
    But there are parts of me
    Even well-paid spies
    Could not even swim
    In decadence.

    The captain smiles faraway
    As he fondles the trigger
    "A smoke?"
    "Why not?"
    I threw him a salute
    And the seas of the Med
    Engulf me.
    I saw the ages
    And Granny Smith
    Fall like toys
    Strewn in haste.

    I meet you
    In a grove.
    Of olive husks and a rusty sky
    And we embrace
    With eyes aglow
    In a purifying blindness
