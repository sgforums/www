    If God were an architect and Life,
    A denominator of base products --
    Are we then mere jellies in a vat,
    Existing for the next stabbing impulse?

    I am Superman with an invisible gun,
    My body is a rough of ones and zeros,
    With entrails gutted out to make room
    for pure, unadulterated vice.

    Save this peccadillo I am anybody:
    I could even be the one who gives you
    your new b_reasts or a next-to-discrete lift.
    Tear sheets off your face until there is none left.

    Will that be all, lovely Galatea?
    I'll sculpt you a brand new face,
    throw darts at your wild black heart.
    Strangle, silently, this Pygmalion constuct.
