    its another ugly day
    laughs
    thinking how close tears are
    to happiness and pain
    another ugly day
    (hahahaha)
    reaches for the phone and turns away
    (blah ahahaha and its nothing to talk about)

    it is a nice place
    and full of good people
    but i'm just cursed...

    i love you all Bye Bye.

    another ugly day
    (hahahaha)
    sits up on the chair and goes out the door

    starts the car
    hits the highway
    and hesitates...
    a nice car
    because it is a very nice car,
    and a shame to waste it.
