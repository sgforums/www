    Welcome to MPC Angelll!

    Regarding your questions here are a few recommendations,

    Word Forward

    IMO, this is the most active poetry group in Singapore. They organized regular reading sessions, competitions, etc. Do check out their website. And yes, they have a forum for aspiring poets to share their poems.

    Hetero Poetry Club

    If you are interested in Chinese poetry this is the group for you. I think they do organize outings occasionally.

    And if you are interested in meeting poets subtext will be a good choice. And not forgetting our friends from BooksActually. Do take a trip down if you are free, have a chat with Kenny and Karen, they are very friendly people.
