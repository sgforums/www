    Chew Bakar

    The naked king in transparent robe
    The jokers and paupers along the road
    Expousing the wisedom base on gold
    Only innocences have been sold

    Ancient scrolls and recent blogs
    Various schools of different thoughts
    Any attempt that try to block
    The forbidden will be most well sought

    Another day another page
    Music tunes of an ancient sage
    Some come to hear but are deaf
    Dispute it and there are death

    Fruits and rewards have odd taste
    Sweet or sour greeds have no grace
    Pets are kept to entertain
    Grow too big will be hard to tame

    Sacred creeds and hidden files
    Fancy notions twist and bend
    Rumours frown and anger smiles
    Fiery jokes that never end

    Half truths and lies are all the same
    Plans and schemes are games of pain
    Statistics is a number game
    Win or loss nobody gain

    Justice serves lies remain
    Joy, anger, fear and pain
    Planet revolved on slanted plane
    More degrees more insane

    Crime and passion glory and fame
    Start to boil for stakes to claim
    Gentleman rules and ruthless mean
    Cunnings win for cheating clean

    Dreams and ideas beg steal or borrow
    Weak and naive love to follow
    Fakes and half-bakes line in a row
    Highest level all time low

    Rotating chairs up for grab
    Only liars will win this trap
    Another joker try his bet
    Childish game the mortals get

    Trust and hope bought and sold
    Prejudice views from young and old
    Lengthy debates nothing told
    Enlightment and illusion all unfold

    Virtue shrinks fools propose
    Knowledge grows odds dipose
    Passive hassles discords grow
    Power of deceits steal the show

    Freedom and logics always shock
    Sway the verses in the common flock
    Laziness upholds instincts kill
    Luck ran out fate to seal

    Truths embedded in transparency
    Every soul will want to see
    One by one the people fall
    Truths and lies are same afterall

    Strong and soft all will decay
    Desire stalls sciences delay  
    Memories and physics postulate
    One more round the planet rotate

    No more left in my regime
    I am now the naked king
    Lonely and silence fill my realm
    Ancient sage no longer sing
