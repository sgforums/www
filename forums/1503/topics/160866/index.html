    I Love You

    Hey baby won't you take a ride with me,
    It'll just be us two, not three.
    Let's go see new places,
    meet new faces.
    I'll bring you to see new sights,
    rainbow arcs and fairy lights.
    It'll be really fun,
    to sit and watch the setting sun.
    And then on the beach we'll run,
    into the distant horizon, our hearts merged as one.

    Hey honey won't you cuddle up with me on the sofa so warm,
    won't you let me shield you from the tide and storm?
    And then you look up at me and gaze into my eyes,
    it's just pure magic, like watching the sun rise.
    There's just this electric feeling,
    it takes away all my pain, like some emotional healing.
    And we're just sitting there saying everything without saying anything,
    cos we both know without each other we'll both be nothing.
    And in the day we'll live and miss one another,
    while in the night we'll sleep and dream of each other.

    Hey darling won't you throw away all your worries,
    just leave them all behind and come dance with me.
    Just the two of us under the starry night sky,
    nobody else around except for you and I.
    And at the end my lips will gently meet yours,
    I'll never get enough of your love, I'll always want more.
    Cos you're not somebody I can live with, but somebody I can't live without,
    and in the arithmetic of love, two minus one is equal to nought.
