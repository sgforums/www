    Probably referring to the iambic rhythm. - chortles-

    Poetry Is Making Love?

    We were limericking when we first met -
    You were flirting with your puns
    I was tripping over my metre and rhyme
    Gosh, were we having fun!

    It wasnt long before we moved on to
    the game of serious foreplay -
    Terse Tense Haikus - a feverish pitch
    and forgot about the wordplay

    You swore to me your love
    with Shakespearean sonnets
    While you moved in rhythm
    Coupling to iambic couplets

    Ah! The Metaphysics of love
    The mysteries one can't shun
    A gasp, A sigh, A gentle touch
    and then, my love, we're Donne.
