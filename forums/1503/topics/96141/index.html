    Eternity

    Love transgresses all matter and ages for eternity and beyond
    It speaks with the Soul of The World
    Through the Language of The World
    An unspoken tongue that seeks to bind us as one
    With Love as the gel that cements us together
    That is the rigidness of my affections for Her
