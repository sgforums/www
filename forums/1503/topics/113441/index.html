Never meant to be

  • Daemon's Quill
    20 Jan 05, 15:11
    We were never meant to be
    When in your heart there is no space for me
    You were everything but a million miles away
    Will you beckon me to stay?

    So I cried and tears eventually followed
    To take you out and have that space in my heart hollowed
    Melting every memory of you from the day we part
    To leave myself with an empty heart

    Yet a shard of ice still pricks my soul
    That lies deep in my flesh leaving me cold
    I shy away from your shadow to keep myself sane
    But fate keep bringing me back to this narrow lane

    Though I saw you again after such a long time
    Our friendship was already past our prime
    I bidded goodbye but you couldn't hear
    We are so far yet so near

    Someone had already replaced you on my mind
    Yet I still hope our friendship will rewind
    Even though we were never meant for each other
    We can still be friends together
