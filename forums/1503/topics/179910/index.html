    Too tired to study
    Too awake to sleep
    Here I am writing words
    Which make sense
    Not to you but only to me

    I've had enough of ribosomes
    And transfer functions
    And eigenvectors
    But my mind doesn't seem to have enough
    Of this state known as wakefulness

    Hazy my mind is
    Drugged with a shot of caffeine
    And a surge of adrenaline
    Which comes from preparing
    For a 10am test in the morning.

    How I wish I could be
    In one state or the other
    Wide awake or fast asleep
    Mutually exclusive
    And not in a mess of both.
