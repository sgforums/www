    Cardio.Flux

    Sometimes, you're never there
    but my heart still beats.
    It beats like a life
    once lost, now in limbo
    and you see it my eyes
    that it only seeks
    your caress,your wonder
    and is that something
    you can give ?

    Our smiles do tick
    like a clock, full of batteries
    hah.
    Did you change the time
    so abruptly
    that I no longer know
    if its day or
    night?

    And like a whispering stream
    your gentle essence doth are
    I would jump in,
    to be touched
    like a dull carbon piece
    on the verge of becoming
    a diamond.

    So, if you find your heaven
    before I do, do this for me
    keep that door open
    and the candle lit
    light the fires
    and warm up the soup
    as I will be there
    but only when...
