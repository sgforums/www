    Untitled

    Falling
    and rising as waves under mute skies, we
    open, expand and unfold,
    thudding hearts suspended
    in protracted
    semi-flight as we clasp and unclasp
    with the evening breeze
    brushing
    upon uncaring
    dry leaves.
