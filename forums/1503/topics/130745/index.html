    Take care of yourself! A gentle poem to remind ladies out there to look after themselves

    Tragedy is her

    Take heed, my fair lady
    For the moon paints you vulnerable and pale
    Take cover, my fair lady
    For the sun burns your pretty skin

    Wolves lives not in wild but in darkness
    Foxes are cunning and leaves you in sadness
    Beasts lusts for your beauty
    Do not let their paws stain your chastity

    Lead astray the gazers
    The further up your seducing skin
    In the bed of roses
    Every rose has its thorn
    Like every night has its dawn

    A sin for him, a tragedy fot you
    Come pleasure haunted by fear
    Come closure haunted by tear
    Beware of desires within
    Falling in love with their deep dark sin
