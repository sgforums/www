[Poem] Tracks

  • LazerLordz
    29 Jan 07, 09:54
    Will you race with me
    Even when the road
    Splits up
    Narrows down
    Splices our vision
    and makes us belch?

    Did you turn down
    a path of uncertainty?
    A road leading nowhere
    or somewhere
    perfectly random
    like the timekeeper's bell?

    Have you gone
    from my sight
    down a road
    I could never follow?

    I'm stuck in
    a pit-lane.
    Waiting.
    For a crackle
    on the radio
    that will never
    come.
