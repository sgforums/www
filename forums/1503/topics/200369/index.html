    Confused randomisations.

    Stare,
    Staring,
    Stared.
    You live in blissful ignorance whilst you forget I used to tear and bleed.
    You, you, you.
    I am out for your blood, and you are still blissfully ignorant.
    Stay, blissfully ignorant, and you ensure you fall even harder.

    Blood,
    Bloody,
    Bled.
    Dreams of you, us, them, me,
    Dreams of knives and blood.
    I wake up in the midst of a murder,
    And wonder about the intentions.

    Dreaming,
    Dreamy,
    Dreamt.
    I dream about my birth,
    And my self-constructed death.

    Love,
    Loving,
    Loved.
    Loving you more than each day passes,
    And each day I feel closer,
    Blood ties and sisterhood.
