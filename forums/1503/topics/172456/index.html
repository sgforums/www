Thinking of you..

  • LazerLordz

    He sits alone in thought,
    hands chattering across the void
    the screen flickers
    his phone, snoozing and calm.

    He pours his heart out
    to a friend who rows
    the same boat of woe
    and they muse in misery.

    He thinks back fondly
    and merrily aye.
    For the lad has not
    stopped smiling since
    his lass to be
    nearly stomped on a bee.

    He takes one last look
    at the dark and silent phone
    and crawls into bed.
    His only glee,
    a prayer for his magical lass to be.
