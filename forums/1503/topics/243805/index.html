[Poem] Suppose

  • LazerLordz
    21 Mar 07, 01:39
    Suppose.

    Suppose that we are
    more like cats
    than a mere pair
    of geckos on the wall.

    Rather that we crawl,
    paw, maul the fence
    in wanton uncertainty
    but in clear true-type sight
    than a day without
    needy visibility.

    Suppose love talks
    and we take the walks
    in thy garden
    where Geronimo wept
    for the sunset of the Red.

    Rather you dream?
    And garrulously tousle
    about in a day long
    lost.
    In a loop of sweetness.

    Suppose you love me
    but I do not know
    nor confirm with chop la!
    And we merely kick
    our feet in bliss.
    My toes, and yours
    they grin in the night air.
