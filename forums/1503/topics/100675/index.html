November Shadows

  • Master -_-
    1 Nov 04, 22:47
    The time has come once again,
    For her presence to appear.
    For i'll be reliving all the pain,
    The moment the day comes near.

    Though i never remembered her face,
    Neither did i called her Mum.
    But i listen what she says,
    whenever she was around.

    November shadows creeping in,
    How i want them to be gone.
    The doldrums of death are yet to be seen,
    For her passing the days i mourned.

    Now i know she wun be here,
    For she has gone to weave silk clouds.
    Someone i hold so dear,
    November shadows i keep out
