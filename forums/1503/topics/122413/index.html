To see my home again.

  • xking

    How much more time do i need
    How much more further
    Wat else do i seek
    Wat kind of an answer

    As i'm laying down to sleep
    A thought came upon me
    Was it me that i'm dealing with
    or just plain uncertainty

    One day when it's half way there
    On a one way trip
    Maybe it's me i found again
    Its me and the road i lead.

    I move further, deeper still
    until i lost my way
    if i can't found the right door to you
    Nothing gonna makes me stay

    And so i'm moving on
    No time there is to waste
    Each step i took is a long way home
    But soon i'll find my way

    And i'll walk right through the day.
    To see my home again.
