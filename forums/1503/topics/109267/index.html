This Christmas

  • ironoxideman
    26 Dec 04, 03:49
    Christmas to me ,
    is just a day in every single year
    When everything I lack,
    Is transformed into yearn

    When all the outside world is laughing
    and beyond my door is song,
    Silence becomes soothing
    And every hour is long

    Her presence I sought,
    and the presents I almost bought
    If only I weren't so meek
    Perhaps we again can meet

    Told me you did,
    That if we met,
    Then coldfeet
    would be all I'd get

    But believe me
    When I say I already know
    About the realities of a white christmas
    and its awful bitter cold

    My feelings for her
    Know do I,that they are unsure.

    But Try I did,
    to take back Christmas
    and to quench this hunger
    and that insatiable thirst

    All I wanted ,
    was to see Christmas
    as something other than apocalypse
    For approach does January the first

    and the holiday is blowing its final kiss

    Must all release come to a screeching halt
    Must all good days and all good cease?

    Wait,wait...slow down

    stop that one man sleigh!
    I havn't enjoyed my Christmas feast
    the loads on my legs,tonnes they weigh
    Havn't crossed out anything on my wishlist
    Havn't said hello to the people I missed

    I havn't smoothened out this crease

    In santa's suit.
