    the technical parts like language, vocab, metaphors etc can be learnt... but one thing you must explore on your own is to be more sensitive to your emotions, feelings... happy is not just happy... think of why you're happy, how you can describe your state of happiness... once you come up with ways to describe your emotions instead of just saying happy... you're on your way to build images into your expressions and put them into poems...

    like DP said..read, write and practice... and start with simple things first... when you are more confident then go into things like rhythm, pace, rhyme... tats where the technical details like iambic pentameter comes in... kekeke
