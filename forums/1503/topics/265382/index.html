Cupid communing with the Sun

  • peebrain
    3 Jul 07, 11:38
    A divine comedy

    Good morning, old Sun
    who's never taken a vacation
    just for fun --
    Good morning, how has the day
    treated you thus far?
    Oh, haven't you heard?
    Madame Nimbus
    has just tendered her resignation,
    said she is tired of raging all day
    and weeping until her insides sagged.
    Wrung out like a ratty kerchief,
    her note read,
    and strung up like a well-trussed ham! --

    Not again! Not the evil eye!
    Thanks to you, we're still trying
    to spin Icarus's fall
    into something positive for the books.
    So far,
    Pride cometh before the fall
    is the best strapline we've got.
    Have you any ideas?
    Write in!
    The Muses are manning the grapevines.
    I've heard they're fresh out of juices
    so play nice,
    don't blow if they filch
    your idea and pass it off as theirs.
