    what dark force
    ............... turned my gaze back?
    .......................... i saw

    ......................... watching?

    Then a dog barked,
    ............... distant
    ................ its howl primordial
    ............................. frightened

    and the sky turned a shade of rusty grey

    ...... nerves frayed as a used sack
    ...... i unlocked the door
    ...... walked into a warm room
    ...... to a hot bath
    ...... to wash away
    ...... the dark.
