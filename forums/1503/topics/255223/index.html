    Golden Point Award 2007.

    The Golden Point Award competition is now seeking the best, most original short stories and poems by unpublished local writers. First Prize winners will receive up to $10,000 in prize money and educational training grants, and may also be offered the opportunity to be represented by a well-known, international publisher and / or by an established literary agent.

    If you are a Singaporean or Permanent Resident, download the application form now and submit your works before 31 July 2007. Contact gpa@toph.com.sg or call 8183 6034 for more information.
