    Im conveying exactly what the title implies. Simplicity on primal flow. I have feminized the assault on my spirit, and "she" is none other than my thesis paper that I have to do.

    The first verse entails the apprehension and subsequent daunting task of starting the essay as its way overdue.

    To combat such stress I have resolved to heading to the bar with some friends. And I saw "her" in the hands of people walking by, on the posters of the streets, in the toilet signs. She was everywhere.

    The third verse is about her wholeness and composition. Im drawing on ancient imagery to further describe the difficulty of the challenge as well as her cruel disregard for my feelings. That paper tyrant!

    4th verse. In a fit of resilience and diligence, I started to delve into her inner workings to solve the question put before me. "I entered her between lines. her body paper soft transcribed" Which I may sadly report, not to much success. Its been 8000 words since last night and about 2000 words to go. Woop de doo!

    Desperation, thou art in words
    Oh... now it is beginning to make sense. Heh.
