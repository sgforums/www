    I wish to share with you the 12 statements that have been guiding me for the past few years. Of course there are more but currently these stand out from the rest.

    To the weak, difficulty is a closed door. To the strong it is a door waiting to be opened.

    Keep on trying. No matter how hard it seems, it will get better.

    Everything is determined by our attitude, by our resolve.

    You never know how much you can do until you try.

    Consider things from every angle.

    It is not work that kills men; it is worry.

    The starting point of all achievement is faith in self.

    To be free, one must have command over himself.

    The secret of getting things done is in doing them now.

    Difficulties are the things that show what men are.

    When I go to bed, I leave my troubles in my clothes.

    Seek first to understand, then to be understood.
