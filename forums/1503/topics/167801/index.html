Island State

  • peebrain
    8 Dec 05, 13:43
    I could never keep track of all the names
    the critics claim for my beloved,
    a once-colony of sapphire isles squeezed
    on a pinhead of a city state.
    More than just Safire, the caning
    of Michael Fay, Singapore is
    the apostrohpe before a stop --

    An iron tree raised above a colonial crop,
    roots unseen, it blooms in waterless shade.

    ---------------------------------------------

    Something I shrugged out over lunch.
