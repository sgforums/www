    Episode #1

    I cant believe that it was sunday again. The thought of

    meeting Janice made my heart skipped a beat. As i smacked

    myself think how silly i must be looking, i heard this clump-clump of

    heavy footsteps behind me.

    Turning back, i saw a bearded jumber-jack man - complete

    with bedraggled tartan shirt, scuffed jeans and a menacing grin -

    loping towards me with a brown paper bag in hand.

    Oh man, I turned left. He turned left, closing in on me so quickly

    that i could almost feel the spittle from his urgent huffs and puffs. I

    crossed the road; so did he.

    i was breaking into a run for the last 100m to the church

    when , suddenly, he veered to the right towards an old man with a

    balaclava and begging bowl, who was crouching outside the church.

    Now safely within the church grounds, i saw my apparent stalker

    pull two buns out of the bag and hand one to the man. 'Fresh from

    the oven, Lenny. Walked real fast so they're still hot,' he boomed

    Did i actually ran from this kind guy?

    I turn about looking Janice dear. There she was, as if glowing

    in the warm sunlight. I suddenly started to feel warm and fuzzy all
    over.
