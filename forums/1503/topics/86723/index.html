    Elysium Brick

    Your face surmounted by
    stately palms that
    bear stoic witness to that
    infamy of men and coins,
    brought about by society's bit*h
    and her faceless pals.

    I see the builders.
    they rip and they tear,
    as the desecration goes on.
    One pillar is no more
    and my psyche, it swings anew.
    Wither it not a strange deed
    for a hall of lore,
    shall be crafted
    but it hath no foundation.

    Thus your framework is but
    a ghost.
    A wraith which deigns to honor us,
    with the adulation of the world,
    but with the backalley snippets,
    of one who seeks water
    but dies trying.
