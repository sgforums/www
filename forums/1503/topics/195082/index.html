    Being told to act natural

    A handshake; a smile, not too wide;
    say a few kind words to the camera;
    remember to mean what you say,
    and hold back the tide of vomit
    rising in your disemboweled insides.

    If somehow, something slips out,
    play it like that plastic 9mm
    in the top drawer of your desk,
    the one with the rubber bullets;
    they wonÂ’t hurt, or kill anyone.
    They bounce off balding heads,
    just like the things you wanted to say.
