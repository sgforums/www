    THE MAGIC OF LOVE.

    Love like a Rose,
    lights us up in its beauty.
    immerse in the beauty of the rose whereby failing to see beyond the beauty of the rose, its hidden and ugly thorns which cuts deeply into the hearts of young maidens...leaving fear and pains.

    Yet just the thoughts of it beauty,
    ones who was once hurt yearns for the beauty of rose to embross upon them once again.

    Hence, the power of love remains undefeated again due to the very much feared feeling of loneliness.
