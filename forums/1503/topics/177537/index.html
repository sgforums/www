Missing

  • Master -_-
    8 Feb 06, 20:12
    There's no one who knows what i'm feeling right now,
    A bit of despair and abit of happiness.
    All i did the whole day was just to frown,
    To those who came and pissed me, i swear and curse.

    I need someone to love me
    But she is just far away
    All i need her is to be
    someone to brighten up my day

    There's no denying she's all i ever got,
    the gear in my life which made me move on.
    The times i spent with her were very very short,
    compared to the years i yearned whenever she was gone.

    Where are you dear, as i am writing this?
    Your warm and tight hug i sorely missed.
    Meet me right now and share a fragile kiss.
    For you have showed me the wonders of this union bliss.

    When dragons start flying and fish start walking,
    when deserts start snowing and ice caps start melting.
    When hate starts to set and people start mocking,
    Those will be the days when our love starts ending.

    The end of my life,
    just depends on one small thing.
    The sharpness of this knife,
    into my eyes i will swing
