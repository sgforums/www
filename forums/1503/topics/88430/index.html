    i may not wanna be
    who u want me to be.
    the one person i wanna really be,
    is just me.

    i may not be able to
    reach the sky for you
    tho' i try to stretch as far
    still i cant be where you are

    i cannot be as tall
    or as strong,fast,brave
    or perfect
    just like you.
    i know u do your best
    and want me to.

    i m sorry but i really
    do not wanna be,
    what you have installed for me......
