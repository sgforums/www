I come in peace...

  • S-wordsman
    11 May 06, 00:16
    Today I enter into a new dimension of space and time,
    It is a strange feeling dealing with firsts,
    A new brand of poison that quenches new thirsts,
    I say poison because words are dangerous,
    Words can kill,
    Still...
    I continue to write and brave this virgin soil of fertile thought and emotion,
    I accept your invitation, Deadpoet, and serve this blood-stained quill with devotion
    And in modest motions i move myself to move yourselves in omnidirections
    To serve your quills, to cure all ills, regardless of race, language or religious faction.

    I am your humble servant,
    S-wordsman

    I welcome you dear swordsman,
    into this harem of knowledge.
    Where newbies like me learn,
    our poets' tips and adage.

    Here I await with glee,
    your significant and valuable advice.
    Generous albeit free,
    where poems i seek as prize.

    A swordsman of the King's court,
    versus a peasant with a USB cord.
    The twist is where the keyboard,
    Is indeed mightier than the sword.
