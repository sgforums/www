    Episode #17

    The seafood dinner was sumptuous. Especially when we had it by the sea, the view simply enhanced the entire eating experience. Although I was awkward having dinner with Jason's parents at first, but soon the sea, the breeze and the food soon got all of us relaxed and chatting like a family. Plus his parents were really nice and friendly, not imposing or anything.

    After dinner, his parents went back to their room. Well not after coaxing us to have a stroll to help digestion. Oh well, what else can I say? The thought of a leisurely stroll does seems like a great idea, especially after all the food we had eaten. I can feel my waist expanded already....

    I must have the weirdest way of walking. To avoid him holding my hand, I keep walking left and right in 'S' shape. Of course I tried to cover up by looking here and there, but I guess he ain't that stupid, right?

    Being as clumsy as I was, I accidentally stepped on a rock and lost my balance. Jason grabbed me in time and there in his arms I can't help but looked into his eyes. His eyes seemed to be smiling and sparkling like the stars above. Were they really so, or my eyes were playing a trick on me?

    Jason helped me to stand up and held me close. Standing there, with his arms around my waist and our foreheads adjoining, I could feel my heart pounding away. A part of me was moved, it was after all a really romantic moment. Yet another part of me wanted to move away, accusing myself of "betraying" Vince.

    I have no idea how long have we been standing there by the sea in that position. Between us, there were total silence, except for the sound of the wind and the waves hitting the shores. Suddenly....

    "Ah~~~~ choo~~~~~" I let out a sneeze.
    Jason broke out in laughter. "Let's go before your mucus flow out. There won't be at all romantic!"
    I gave him a light punch for teasing.

    We made our way back to the resort before we both freeze to death. And this time Jason held my hand and I just let him. Having mucus ain't a pretty sight, so is falling down along the way.

    Was it my imagination? Or his hand really so warm and nice? This ain't the first time he hold me, but why his hand felt so different? Was it the bali air?! I shook my head, trying not to think so much.
