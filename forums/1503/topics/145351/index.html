For Her...

  • Othello_Red
    1 Aug 05, 02:21
    Wrote this while on duty....something thats been on my mind for quite awhile...see what you make of it...

    As the days while away
    So too my service to the nation
    Perpetually feeling take for granted
    Chronically overworked, severely underpaid

    Why this resentment?
    Isn't it supposed to be patriotism?
    Don't we owe Her our all?
    Because of Her, we stand tall?

    Just what do we lack?
    Belonging or pride?
    Or do we even want to stand for Her,
    Up against the tide?
