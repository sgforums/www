    Inspired by our fellow poet Master -_- poem, November Shadows. Let us try our hand in writing a similar poem.

    First choose a month or if you want maybe a season.

    Then using a Â‘motionÂ’ words (For example, stagger, limp, dance, glide, creep, crawl, etc) show how the month/season arrives.

    Next in a few sentences describe what the month/season does while it is here. Follow by what how it leaves and

    finally how you feel about it.

    You can write in past or present tense, up to you.

    For a start this is my version.

    September sneaked
    among the unwary
    stabbing deep
    into the heart
    of Innocence
    spreading pains and terrors
    then stormed out of sight
    dragging thousands
    of unwilling anguish souls
    leaving behind
    silent memories
    I was stunned
