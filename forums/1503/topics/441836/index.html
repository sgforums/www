    LDV

    If someone could redouble
    Your efforts in another way,
    And place your shadow today
    How would you greet
    this alien exchange?

    Jot down from right to left,
    coded cursive as always,
    taking in a new world
    With little regard to hymns
    And praises that you never knew
    Or choose hermitage, in the courts
    and markets, in bewilderment that
    the courts still preen like petty kings
    but act like filth, and
    the markets trading for nothing.

    Or perhaps do away the excesses of
    A past life spent in study
    To explore the riches of
    What your childer have become,
    Nourishing the genius, that others
    Would claim you were, only for you
    to deliver in coded, archaic, speech.

    Would you live to see yourself in our world?
    Or continue to live within?
