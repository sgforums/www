    Okay..this may not be poetry but don't mind if I post this here..I wrote this down in one of my darkest days..So there.

    -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Hello world

    From the day I was born, Im very sure that I've never done something regrettable.

    Whimsical, naughty,silly,slightly evil..Yes.Regerettable....erm..No.

    Therefore I shall keep my heart open.Never can I close my heart to her.

    Sometimes, I feel like a fool, what if there are others out there who love me?

    I ask myself, is she worth the pain and agony?Yes.

    But if others want me to love them, prove to me that you are more worthy of the stinging acid burning through my
    innocent heart.

    Maybe then, things might change?For Rachel, she need not prove anything to me.

    She has proven.My sorrow and baleful mourns have passed her with flying colours.

    Rach, you told me you wanted to fly away to Japan or even to the Big Apple with me.We wanted to go to the rooftop of the Empire State Building and yell anything, like two schoolkids on summer vacation.

    Its not too late.

    Im not selfish.Just confident that you and I will not regret this in the long run.

    No offence.You were meant to fly amongst the stars.Your heart tells me that.Believe it or not,
    I see that in your eyes, the same ones that blessed my life one year ago in Changi Airport.

    You'll be happy.

    I promise.Seldom do i really do that.The best I can give, I will give.True love watches and waits.

    Love rules.
