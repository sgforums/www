    Today

    The sunlight streamed through curtained panes,
    the shrill alarm drives me insane.

    Eyes still closed, I reached over
    grabbed the clock and brought it closer

    Another day, another morning.
    will be be better, or just as boring

    Stifling a yawn I stared at the ceiling
    and tried to gather what I am feeling

    I rub my eyes, with thumb and fingers
    tried to banish the sleep that lingers.

    stumbling across the cold hard floor
    I walked and stumbled to the bathroom's door

    turned on the tap, the ice cold water
    flows down my face and through my fingers

    looking up into the mirror
    saw me staring back and started to whisper

    "who are we today, who will we be?
    which face to put on,for the world to see?
