    What I love most at Books Actually are the leather bound books.
    Does anyone else love that???

    I love love love the musty smell of leatherbound books and the yellowing pages inside. It's a pity that it's so hard to get cheap beautiful books in singapore.

    My dad bought my sister and I, when were were 8 & 5... a book of collected children's fairy tales. It was a hardcover with this amazing ruby-red and sapphire-blue diamond print. It came with a matching blue cloth-marker and the page edges were dipped in shimmering gold paint!
    When you open the book, there are beautiful paintings of the fairytale characters interspersing each story. Up till today, I still feel quite transported when I see and open that book
