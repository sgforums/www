    Some of you have mentioned that it is very difficult to write a poem. A poem is just a form of expression. You do not need to use very difficult words or must rhyme for every sentence. One good example is the following poem by William Blake, a famous poet. He used simple words that a little child can understand.

    Spring

    Sound the Flute!
    Now itÂ’s mute.
    Birds delight
    Day and Night
    Nightingale
    In the dale
    Lark in sky,
    Merrily,
    Merrily, merrily to welcome in the year.

    Little Boy,
    Full of joy,
    Little girl,
    Sweet and small,
    Cock does crow,
    So do you
    Merry voice
    Infant noise
    Merrily, merrily, to welcome in the year.

    Little Lamb
    Here I am,
    Come and lick
    My white neck,
    Let me pull
    Your soft wool,
    Let me kiss
    Your soft face,
    Merrily, merrily, we welcome in the Year.

    William Blake
