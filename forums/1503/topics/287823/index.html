There is a piece of argon stuck in my teeth

  • Mirumoto
    25 Oct 07, 22:24
    I am an inert gas ,
    Caught between the fringes
    Of a unhealthy obsession
    born of my charges
    and a solitary neutron

    I sought the attention of a friend
    Across the table,
    Hoping , in her volatility ,
    She would reach out
    For my lonesome engagment
    and bear witness
    to my silent anatomy
    Of the stoic silence
    I bear to others instead.

    She exploded still
    For there was too little of myself,
    And too much of herself to bear.
