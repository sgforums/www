    Crazy little maze

    There is so much i can say,
    if only you believe.
    I don't wish to stay that way,
    if you only know what it means.

    I'm trying to convince myself,
    but its harder than it seems.
    Don't even know what else,
    to do or even think.

    I'm looking for the light,
    i'm looking for a guide.
    Bring me to where you are,
    take me to somewhere bright.

    Yes i've lost my way,
    here's not where i want to stay.
    Get me out of here,
    To see another day.

    Slowly and carefully,
    as my guide led me.
    To you i hope he brings,
    it's you i hope to meet

    Yes i've lost the way,
    i've lost my way today.
    Wanted to get out of here,
    this crazy little maze.
