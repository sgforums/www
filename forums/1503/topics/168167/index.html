    21 November 2005
    Title: Death of an Angel

    White feathers scattered,
    Drifting in the wind.
    Drops of black blood,
    Stains sparking wings.

    Crumpled beauty lies,
    Shattered visage of peace.
    Darkness glowing brighter,
    Light of being cease.

    Unspoiled love divine,
    Adoring pollution of hate.
    Lost innocence of heaven,
    Hell's dawn mankind's fate.

    26 November 2005
    Title: Do You Believe in Magic?

    Do you believe in magic?
    White rabbits in top hats?
    Pretty girls sawn in half?
    Tricks and shows and parlour acts?

    Do you believe in magic?
    Charms and chants all kept in sacks?
    Mighty warlocks in darkest armour?
    Ladies in scarlet and cats in black?

    Do you believe in magic?
    Diamonds of power and enchanted pearls?
    Runes and symbols that heal or kill?
    Boys who sleep and wake up as girls?

    Do you believe in magic?
    Dolls who come alive at night?
    Pretty toadstools in a ring?
    Faeries dancing in moonlight?

    Do you believe in magic?
    In Ragwitch Lilim and evil spells?
    Doorways to another place?
    Portals unto nineteen hells?

    Do you believe in magic?
    Ancient ones with dark visions?
    Nyarlathotep and the end of the world?
    Elder Gods on death missions?

    Do you believe in magic?
    In strange things unexplainable?
    If it happened before your eyes,
    Would you run and tumble?

    Do you believe in magic?
    I must say I do.
    I started believing in magic,
    When I saw the great Cthulhu.
