    GEEK TRAGEDY

    stood I,

    silent by the window

    the stars above,now

    swing into view

    they burn so bright

    but how, they are few

    my tread was silent

    no shadow behind me fell

    visage of no concern

    you would not know me well

    doors all silent on a hinge

    unbidden was how i came

    but hear,this i would confess,

    that mine heart is clear of sin.

    So stood I before her bed,

    a pale neck exposed,

    covers over skin,so spied

    a damning alluring sheen.

    her hair is splayed out over

    a pillow like a river in darkness,

    the wind tugs at those strands

    twirled about an unseen finger

    what form of creature should know

    how i am suffered to linger.

    I watch those sealed eyelids

    and imagine the sights within

    something gnaws at my heart

    as I stand mere inches apart

    from my living breathing dream

    Pulse quickened

    I reach out a hand

    for the flower i would

    with my life and hopes to tend.

    I reach out a hand

    it slips through my fingers

    like grains of fine white sand
