    When is the last time you want to ask your boss/client/customer/your colleague who can’t keep his bloody mouth shut to fuck off!

    Well I have always wanted to hang a huge “FUCK OFF!” sign in my workstation but my colleague told me that it would be a career suicide to do so. Common sense lah, I also know. So what to do?

    Well this is what the challenge is all about.

    You are to write a short poem using the first letter of the phrase “FUCK OFF”. Too easy a challenge for you?

    It must not be an angry or vulgar poem. Instead you must disguise it, beautify it, etc. It must be so well disguised that any one who read the poem will says, “Wow! That is really a beautiful love/sad/nice/beautiful/touching poem!”  Or “This poem is so sweet, can I use it for my wife’s birthday?” If he/she starts to cry after reading it, even better.

    I will personally select the best poem. You can submit as many you like but must post them here by 30 April 2009. A prize will be given to the author of the selected poem.

    I am counting on all you creative poets out there! Help me fulfill my twisted dream!
