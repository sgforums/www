Crave

  • HENG@
    15 Nov 04, 13:32
    Crave

    Looks of longing,
    Of unsaid lust.
    Piercing my soul,
    As an arrow does.
    I miss it,
    I need it,
    I want it,
    I crave it.

    Soft whispers,
    In my ear.
    Sweet murmurings,
    Of love unending.
    I miss it,
    I need it,
    I want it,
    I crave it.

    The feeling of hands,
    Upon my body.
    Gliding down my back,
    Caressing my neck.
    I miss it,
    I need it,
    I want it,
    I crave it.

    A lithe body,
    So good to hold.
    A hug from her,
    It warms me so.
    I miss it,
    I need it,
    I want it,
    I crave it.

    Soft moist lips,
    Oh so sensual.
    With hot passion,
    They consume mine.
    I miss it,
    I need it.
    I want it,
    I crave it.

    Someone to touch,
    Someone to love.
    Someone to kiss,
    Someone to hold.
    I miss it,
    I need it,
    I want it,
    I crave it.
