    Self- mockery

    I laughed, mockery of myself.
    In the shadows I danced, I hid in a shell.
    The castle I wanted to build, I stopped.
    I looked at the blue prints.... My castle?
    The bricks, the shovels... Nothing was mine.
    I lied.... Mockery of myself....
