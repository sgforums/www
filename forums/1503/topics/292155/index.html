    Tomorrow

    Contemptuous stares,
    hostile glares.
    I never cared.
    I was never scared

    The howling of an east wind,
    screaming so loudly
    Trapped in a thunderstorm,
    whole world against me.

    Tired, bloodshot eyes.
    resting on a distant tree.
    so far away, so far away,
    and then I would be free.

    Time stopped, time passed,
    no big matter.
    Living on dead hopes,
    my life torn asunder.

    The old tree stood,
    resisting the tests of time.
    I stood and looked,
    this would be my shrine

    My neck rested on the noose.
    Soon my body would hang loose.
    Today, a reality,
    tommorow, a memory.

    quite short, but i had a burst of creativity.
