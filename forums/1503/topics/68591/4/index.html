  • oceanic
    4 Apr 04, 10:03
    What is hope?

    Have you ever wake up one day full of hope and dreams and the next moment, something drastic happen and all your hopes are shattered?

    Everyday we lived in fear, fear of bad things that will befall us. Fear of failures, fear of rejection, fear of getting hurt and perhaps, even fear of death.

    So, where can we really find hope? Nothing in this world promises anything. A box of vit C from GNC said it will boost ur immune system if you take one capsule each day.. Well, it might help but it does not guarantee tat u'll never fall sick. Cos there is no guarantee in anything in life. No one in this world, I repeat, NO ONE can promise you or guarantee anything. Life seemed like a solitary journey in this world. Now, dun get me wrong. I dun mean living alone, eating alone.. You can be surrounded by many good frens and your loved ones. But the only one person who really cares and loves you the most is the one who created you from above. He's none other than God.

    He was and He is, will be the beginning and the ending. We can find hope in Him. We can trust Him cos we know He will gives us the best that we can endure. He never forsake us even if we do forsake Him. His mercy and love is boundless. He saved us all with his blood. Anguish it seemed to be, the flesh torn for us, the blood shed to save us. He did it all. He saved us from the world of SINS.

    My point is, no matter how grevious your SINS are, turn unto Jesus Christ, repent and He will forgive you. Have hope in Him in whatever you do, trust him and have faith. Take heart and be courageous. He will sustain you with your faith in Him.

    I didn't know why I began writing this. I've not been close with e Lord for a long time. I believe He's trying to bring a message to everyone through me. I hope someday, someone will come to know Him.

    Au revoir
