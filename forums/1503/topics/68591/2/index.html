    Still, you're gone

    Wait

    That night
    A rainy and cooling night
    I waited and waited
    I simply waited

    I waited till I fell asleep
    I waited till the tears came
    I waited till dawn broke
    The call never came

    I waited
    Too long in my life
    I waited for you too much
    I waited for you, all my life

    I still persists in waiting
    I knew I could have given up
    But still,
    I waited and waited

    I waited and stop waiting
    Waiting should end
    I can't wait any longer

    The tears must stop
    The pain must heal
    The love must end
    The broken heart should let it go

    I stop waiting
    I know it hurts so much
    I can't wait anymore
    Cos I dun wish to face disappointment again once more..
