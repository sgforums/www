    In memory of my beloved Grandfather whom I missed and love and always will

    A black umbrella lying at a corner
    Unused,untouched for a decade
    Unknown mysteries behind
    Only we'll know why

    The day you went away
    Tears never stopped flowing then
    Like a trampled rose on the ground
    You lost the battle, somehow

    Anniversary is here again
    The years passed by simply too fast
    Our longing for you each day
    Never cease to pause even for a while
