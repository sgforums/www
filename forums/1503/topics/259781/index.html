    Been a while since I last wrote a poem of sorts... here I go!

    ___________________

    There...

    It's always been there; the menacing leers from those in bloodstained white,
    It's always been there; those rusty chains around my ankles,
    It's always been there; the remains of the last resident in the cubicle;
    It's always been there; the howling cries echoing through the chamber.

    I've been here; I used to reside here,
    I've been here; the place where my life was ripped apart,
    I've been here; no wonder it felt so nostalgic,
    However; it's as if I've been there, but not exactly here.

    I see you; very so often in the operating table,
    But I can't hear you; as if a wall separated us,
    I cried out for you; you can't hear me,
    I try to hear you; I've been disturbed.

    There; I met you before,
    There; the place where peace never fails,
    There; our foothold friendship of five,
    Here; we're shattered entities of the past.

    Our blood; we shall mix,
    Merged; for what reason, we don't know,
    Mindless as we are; survival is the top priority,
    From this hell; we call them the Neo-Nazis.

    A pact we made; we will fulfill,
    Until then; we shall not die,
    Our battle; we shall assault,
    Our lives; we no longer cherish.

    The Neo-Nazis; we loathed them,
    For what they've done; especially to our innocent lives,
    We maybe tools to their horror,
    But we shall deceive them, back stab them.

    A Mauser C96; I clenched,
    A Walther P-38; I grasped,
    Hands; we hold each other's,
    We will fight; in search...

    ...And escape this vicious cycle...

    Raquel Schmidt and Tessa Schneider
