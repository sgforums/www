    Distractions

    Memos, schedules and paper files
    Each day passes as I sit in the pile
    Not lifting my eyes, not resting a while
    Enslaved in toil till my heart turned vile

    Scores, wealth and pride in fame
    Each time it grows my ego gains
    Not humbling my actions, boasting without restraint
    Engulfed in arrogance I live a life in vain

    Lies, flattery and critiques that hurt
    Each wound deepens as my words grow curt
    Not caring a soul, treating others as dirt
    Engrossed in biases my ill judgement I assert

    Blind, deaf and drawn away
    Each momentÂ’s too late, far from You I stray
    Not seeing Your light, not hearing what You say
    Enshrouded in denial, I made excuses for not walking Your way

    Lord, Saviour, Eternal Friend
    Each sin I committed I now repent
    No more excuses, no distractions by any man
    Embracing Your Word, my old life I end

    Father, Son and the Holy Ghost,
    Each night I pray to do my utmost
    To heed Your Word, to love You most
    Endeavouring to serve You, to do as You chose.

    Powerful engines rumbling,
    Overtaking, swerving recklessly.
