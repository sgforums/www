    Roses Red

    Roses are red,
    Violets are blue
    Dread filled my head
    as I thought of you

    Snow is white,
    Blood is red,
    on this pale wight,
    your hunger fed.

    Night is dark
    Day is bright
    Death slowly struck
    as you took your bite.

    Roses are dead
    So are violets too
    On my blood you fed
    The vampire, you.

    Going Home

    Pitched dark.
    A chilling cold,
    Around my heart
    it tightened its hold.

    Deadly silent,
    a stilled air.
    Pain raked through veins,
    as did great despair.

    Peacefully numb.
    An endless night.
    Memories faded,
    as life took flight.

    Forgotten words,
    carved in stone.
    Your loving tears flowed
    as Death took me home.

    True Love

    Your large bright eyes, intelligent and clear,
    sees through my shield of insecurity and fear.

    Your soothing words, truthful and sincere
    melts away my defences that guards my heart dear.

    Your steady arms, comforting and strong
    assures me that with you around, nothing goes wrong

    Your gentle touch, caring and tender
    mends the scars that past hurts did render

    Your loving heart, enduring and forgiving
    awaits the love of a heart that's long been grieving

    O wretched heart,
    discard nightmares of ages past
    Start anew in hope and trust
    for true love to grow at last.
