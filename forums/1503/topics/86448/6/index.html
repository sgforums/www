    Hope of the Stars

    Peacefully, dusk settles upon me.
    Orange hue, I bathe in and paints the sky.
    Easing, thy weary, dull eyes up,
    Towards the sparkling heavens.
    Incandescence flows,
    Cascading light upon the darkness.

    Velvety hues with a warming glow,
    Erstwhile gloom begins to go.
    Regally like a returning King,
    Sweet new hope begins to bloom.
    Enchanted, as new awakening descends.
