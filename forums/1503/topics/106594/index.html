Writer's Block

  • fudgester
    10 Dec 04, 21:00
    One day I was lamenting over my writer's block..... my sudden inability to churn out any more poems.
    In the end, I came up with the following:

    Writer's Block

    I've reached it.
    The horror
    The bane of every writer
    Novelist
    Poet
    In the pantheon
    Of the warriors of the written word
    It has a name:

    Writer's Block.

    Frustrating it can be
    For one hardly has the time

    To pen down anything
    And when the time is free
    For the written word to take shape
    None ever seems
    To manifest

    It is someone akin
    To looking into the horizon
    Hoping for
    Vast fields of cheery sunflowers
    Of an endless carpeting
    Of green grass
    Only to encounter
    A barren land of waste.

    Darn it, man!
    What has gotten into you?
    Are you distracted
    To the point
    Where nothing can be said

    Wait - hold.... Contemplate this.

    You begin by complaining
    About this inescapable malady

    Only to complete
    Another entry
    In this anthology.

    Irony could never have been
    Described in any better way.

    Haha - you the man.
