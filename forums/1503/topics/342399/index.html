    Baby, please don't be sad,
    about the tomorrows we never had.
    Instead, please be glad,
    about the beautiful past we had shared.
    We could have been the perfect lover,
    with once upon a time and happily ever after.
    But reality hit, robbing me of words to use,
    so this may be the last time I say I love you.

    Baby, I'm truly sorry,
    for all that pain I had caused,
    and the years I made you lost.
    With those eyes brimming with tears,
    I chose to harden my heart and close my ears.
    Like rain in the winter,
    my heart is now cold and bitter.
    I used to be your superwoman,
    but now I became your extra burden.

    So, now listen to me dear,
    listen carefully and wipe that tear,
    because I have to tell you a love so real.

    'Though my love has seemed so surreal,
    with shadows, dreams and now to burial,
    please know that you are not hallucinating,
    even though it do resembles a boy day-dreaming.'

    Baby, for the last time, I'm sincerely sorry,
    No matter what, I still want to thank you for a love story.
