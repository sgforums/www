    Shoelaces , and a belgian morning

    I rub my eyes and stretch
    Wishing I had slept earlier
    Breathed alot easier
    Feel alot better, and sing
    Like a certain canary
    Out of merciful captivity
    And into this haze
    Of flutter and stutter
    Dicing my heart's gaze
    Into mindless, symbolic bits of chatter.
    So that myself , i can digest, that i matter

    Wake me up. Please. From this little spell.
    As i tie these shoelaces, while riding the carousel

    Christmas at the Mall

    When we were just children,
    we used to marvel and covet
    the piles of presents hoarded under
    the lofty dressed Christmas tree
    in the center of the mall;
    we thought they were real,
    ....................................we thought
    they were free for the taking.
    When we got our hands on one
    and tore it open, and saw that
    it was just an empty cardboard box
    wrapped up in gold shiny paper,
    .....................................we wondered
    who would be so cruel
    as to falsely decorate Christmas.

    Ho Ho Ho! (A Song?)

    There will be lots of dirt about
    So get ready an eraser or just scribble onward
    Young and Old alike will throw words at one another
    Dead Poets Society or Mad Pencils Club
    Hohoho...

    Dead Poets Society for Past Years
    or is it DPS
    But Mad Pencils Club for the Future
    or is it MPC
    Closed and Dark
    or
    Mad and Creative
    Hohoho...

    Madness lies ahead
    Joy abound in Abundance
    Smiles brighten up the day
    Pencils is set by our side
    Gifts of Words are exchanged
    Hohoho...

    Paper and Pencil in hand
    I frown
    I smile
    I write
    I inspire
    All with a small thin stick in my hand
    And a sheet below
    Plus the small but musing thingy up there.
    Hohoho...

    Poems
    Songs
    Stories
    There they are floating around
    Waiting for someone to catch hold
    To show them to the world
    Hohoho...

    Tears of sadness
    Tears of joy
    Cracks of Smiles
    Sound of Laughter
    Twinkles Twinkles Twinkles
    Hohoho...

    Premier Membership

    Oh, the vile Premier Membership
    Its nothing but a pile of shit

    Pardon my crass
    For i am brass

    And not one to mince my words

    For twenty bucks a year
    Could feed many you hear?

    Puny avatar and dwarvish signature
    What a lousy overture

    Give me more, I'll consider
    If its worth any dear

    At my pleasure I will sign
    Not a knife to make me sigh

    Lure me, seduce me
    But never throttle me

    Darn FireIce for all she is worth
    To make me lose my Perth

    Down with tyranny
    Down with gluttony

    Come all say, No Premier Membership!
