No One World...

  • Devil1976
    21 Feb 04, 12:29
    No world gives a damn about us...
    No one cares....

    No one has the time to be spared...
    No one like....

    No one who can understand... Underestimated...
    No one can understand us... Not even the ones who have understood....

    No one stand under the sun... And notice the moon is there..
    No one who really cares... Have the life to share...

    No one could hear me... No one would wanna...
    No one could beat me.... No one bothers....

    No one thing, time, spirit can truly understand no one....

    - Devil1976
