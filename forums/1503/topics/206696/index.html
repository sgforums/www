    Originally posted by prayingbudda:
    what exactly do people in book clubs do?
    Basically, they decide on what book they are going to read, which means that everyone joining in must have the same book, then they come together and discuss its contents and what they think about it.

    If they don't do it like that, then it's more like a reading and review club.

    IMHO, book clubs are like having reading homework, inevitably you'll have a few who never follow through or can't finish the book, or some people just bowing out because they don't like the choice of the book in focus.

    Usually sustainable if you have a like-minded group, like a group of people who all like the same genre.
