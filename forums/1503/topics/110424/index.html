    a delicious encounter with a strawberry

    as i was walking home one summer night

    a nutty fruit

    sprang out of nowhere

    and gave me a fright

    it yelled

    it kicked

    and tried to grab my shoes

    but I won the fight

    and baked myself

    a delicious pie

    i walked around
    minding my business
    not doing anything
    nor testing my fitness

    then the old feeling
    the intense pain!
    damn my back
    that disc again!

    so on painkillers
    all zonked out
    can't even get up
    or move about

    total rest
    the quack said
    you're lucky
    you aren't dead

    so i thank fate
    and take care
    coz it could be worse..
    me in a wheelchair!

    so to the ortho
    i soon will visit
    not for the world
    would i miss it

    and pray that
    he can put right
    the damage
    which caused such a fright

    now i gotta
    go lie down once more
    rest the back
    and flat on the floor

    this silly rhyme
    is to remind everybody
    look after your back
    don't let it get too shoddy
