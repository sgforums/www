    FOR LETTER OR WORDS
    Spoken Words
    with Dato' Dr M Shanmughalingam

    Fri, 25 August, 7.30pm
    NX Gallery, NUS Museum, UCC

    Be treated to an hour of solo dramatised reading in an informal setting, which breaks down any boundary between the poet and his audience. With the poet's natural wit and humour, he gives life to the text in a way not many writers can. On this evening of infectious entertainment, Dato' Shan delivers social issues in a comic vignette, using extracts from his short stories and poems.

    FOR LETTER OR WORDS (F.L.O.W.) is a dedicated space for literary and theatre works.

    F.L.O.W. happens every last Friday of the month.
