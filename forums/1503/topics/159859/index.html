    1. The more you take, the more you leave behind. What are they?
    Ans: footsteps (othello red)

    2.What is it that you can keep after giving it to someone else?
    Ans: promise or your word (othello red)

    3. What walks all day on its head?
    Ans: A shoenail (KittyNmeow!)

    4. What gets wet when it's drying?
    Ans: towel (mystiv)

    5. There were five men going to church and it started to rain. The four men who ran got wet and the one that remained still stayed dry. What is happening? (hint: think of an event)
    Ans: A funeral (princess cutie)

    6. I am taken from a mine, and shut up in a wooden case, from which I am never released, and yet I am used by almost all children. What am I?
    Ans: pencil (ShrodingersCat)

    7. Though it is not an ox, it has horns; though it is not an donkey, it has a pack-saddle; and wherever it goes it leaves a trail behind. What is it?
    Ans: snail (ShrodingersCat)

    8. I soar without wings. I see without eyes. I've traveled the universe to and fro. I've conquered the world, yet I've never been anywhere but home. What am I?
    Ans: dream (freestyle)

    9. There are four brothers in this world. The first runs and never gets tired. The second eats and is never full. The third drinks and is always thirsty. The fourth sings a song that is never finished.

    Ans: Water, Fire, Earth and Wind (Othello Red & Rainee)

    10. A cloud was my mother, the wind is my father, my son is the cool stream, and my daughter is the fruit of the land. A rainbow is my bed, the earth my final resting place. Some people hate me, others cannot do without me. What am I?

    Ans: rain (othello red)
