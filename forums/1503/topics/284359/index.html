    do you remember the day i met you
    lying on the grass under the shade of the raintree
    watching the clouds pass by
    the cool breeze made me slept

    i awoke to a beautiful voice
    more melodious than the nightingale singing
    i looked around for the source
    a sweet soothing sound mere mortals could not make

    that was when i noticed you
    standing against the sun
    you were heaven sent
    i called out to you
    and just like an angel
    you floated down to me

    days weeks months years passed
    we spent our time together
    be it on the gentle slopes of a hill
    or in the soft warm hay
    sleepin together in each other's arms
    the earth was our bed
    and the star studded sky our blanket

    till one fine day
    you had to return home you said
    i was befuddled
    we were meant to be together
    always and forever
    i cried out in anger and cursed the gods
    for this cruel twist of fate
    and the illness they had given you

    a pile of keepsakes remind me everyday
    your words are like the gentle kisses you pepper on my cheek
    eyes more beautiful than the moon
    beautiful hair billowing in the spring breeze
    your melodious voice echoing within the depths of my heart
    my memories of you clear as a lake

    i miss you

    not exactly a poem. apologies if i posted in the wrong place.

    just some thoughts of mine influenced from my so far single life.

    so what do you guys think. any suggestions. how to improve etc. like what is this exactly? more of a story i suppose with poor paragraphing

    and im just 17 year old engineering student. not a professional poet or author so dun be too harsh.
