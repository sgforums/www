    The Handphone Device

    The handphone device in my hand. In my pocket, and times in my bag. I look at it. So silent. So strange? It feeds on battery. The energy I charged it with. It seems so full of 'strength', yet it does basically nothing much. It doesn't smile at me. It doesn't talk to me.

    "Hey, there's a call for you." Press the button. "Whey?"

    Sometimes it tells me silent stories. Sms. I stare it. It stares back at me as I read. It seems to have seen so much more of me than others do. It know my secrets. It know my lies. It know the world in which I hide.

    I always carry it with me. Occasionally check if it's okie (got message for me or not?). Sometimes I wonder what if it has a mind of its own? What if it can tell me lies? Would it be as tired as I am? Well... At least it doesn't have to walk?

    Could it be a deaf? The way I speak so closely to it. Sometimes yell at it... Must be a real poor thing...? "Hey, your saliva's spilling on my face..." That's what it seems?

    I wish it has hair, so that I can at least comb for it? But all I can do is put on the 'danglings' and expect it to look neat. The day it lies by my side. The night it lies by my bed. The moment it lights up for me. It often has a different story to tell.

    "Are you okay? You look upset?" It doesn't reply me back. Maybe it's just tired? Maybe sleepy? Motionless. Wonder if it shares my happiness when I encounter them? If it can talk of its own, wonder what would it say to me...
