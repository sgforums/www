    Who am I?

    Contemplative, forlorn, my naked mind,
    Phony front, deception, even oneself,
    As mesmerizing roses, drawing minds,
    Gorgeous deception, dwelling thorns reside.

    Chameleon, environs alteration,
    Oneself, situation alteration,
    Changes when changes occur,
    Traits within traits concurred.

    Persona, creation, a play,
    Situations, a set, the men,
    Scenes change, along I play,
    Elements, creations, in hand

    Who am I, IÂ’m just a coy!
    Dummies fit, for different plays.
