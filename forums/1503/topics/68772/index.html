    Death~

    "He sleeps at night and wake at light,
    his distal fallen charms...
    Each step he fights, he stretched his mights,
    his heart beats weakly lit....

    The day he stares, the nights he slept,
    what emptiness might come?
    The wind of dawn, last breath was drawn,
    he slept... Smiling for this day which has finally arrives..."

    - Devil1976
