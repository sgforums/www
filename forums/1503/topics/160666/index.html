    To The Rope That Binds..

    Sometimes I just
    Want to break out of this jail
    That shackles me.
    This yellowed cell
    Of uncertainty
    Where the warden holds the cards
    And the chief
    The ace
    Of hearts.
    A bell tolls
    A man falls
    To the rope
    And do you and I
    Know?
    What lies beyond that 6 feet
    Of Italian fabric
    Is it God?

    A mother laments
    To the weeds
    So that her son
    May be resurrected in the waste land
    Of her youthful Elysium
    Where the grey exists
    As sovereign.
