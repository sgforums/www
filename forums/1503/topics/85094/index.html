Dedicated to Velvet

  • pipi & poot-poot
    8 Jul 04, 22:06
    Sometimes I dream of summer,
    Sometimes I wish for rain,
    Sometimes a second's all I need,
    To remember all the pain...

    Sometimes my friends surround me,
    Sometimes I make them leave,
    Sometimes a word is all it takes,
    To discard the smiles they give...

    Sometimes I choose to remember,
    Sometimes I wish to forget,
    Sometimes the wind bring whispers,
    And the promises we shared...

    The man that stands before you,
    That same boy from yesterday,
    Lived through all the broken hopes and dreams,
    And learnt to live life.....my way.

    Penned by: Pipi
    Dedicated to: ........
    Message: When Dreams End, Life Begins
