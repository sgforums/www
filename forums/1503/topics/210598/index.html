    Haze

    The smell of ash:
    molecules freed
    from a burning
    rainforest
    somewhere south,
    assail our noses,
    claw at our throats
    and dry our eyes.

    Somewhere,
    a farmer holds
    a flaming torch
    ...............to set
    more trees on fire.

    HeÂ’ll have a smoking
    patch of fertile soil
    and for the rest of us,
    a choking aerosphere.
