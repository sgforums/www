    when daylight comes, M. H. Heng, 13 October 2004, 5 pm

    When daylight comes,
    I long for you.
    You leave me at dawn,
    When I still want you around.

    At night in my dream,
    We are together.
    My arms around you,
    Your arms around me.

    Ah, what ecstasy!
    When we are together.
    A preview of life,
    When we are in holy union.

    I beg you to stay,
    but night grows old.
    You are Cinderella.
    One who leaves at dawn!

    The glass slipper she leaves,
    Is an awful mess.
    A mixture of spilt seed and spilt angst.

    When daylight comes,
    I long for you.
    Waiting for night,
    Waiting for another dream.

    Kindly presented for your critique. Oh, M. H. Heng are my initials.
