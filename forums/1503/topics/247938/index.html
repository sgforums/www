    Farewell I shall bid,
    The heart which fails to beat.
    Fallen to defeat,
    Feelings come with it.

    Feelings of defeat,
    Ice rains on skin.
    Then all began to melt,
    Cold rain on the skin

    Torment, anguish and cold,
    Manifests, unable to hold.
    Like soldiers invading a hold,
    Battling till kings fall!

    First they flee their fleet,
    Then fight back from below.
    Farewells, why I bid,
    To climb up from down low.

    From the lessons of defeat,
    I shall be reborn.
    When the day comes to me,
    Return the smiles once worn.
