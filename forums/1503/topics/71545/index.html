    "All empty and bare like a paper white
    That represents his heart, excite
    Waiting to paint that canvas blue
    As a prologue to a romance due

    Take red and yellow and strike it bold
    Colour it bright and wild with gold
    Then on the canvas, draw a picture of birds
    No need for sketches, no need for words.

    With a frame in hand, all wooden and brown
    Now the canvas is painted, like a king just-crowned
    It's beautiful and a wonder to see and awe
    Yet something is missing, a grievous flaw.

    "You call this art? An impossible claim!
    Such works, so careless would never know fame
    For what this drawing, this canvas needs
    Is emotions and feelings that far exceeds"

    And so the artist, now bowed in regret
    Walked away in silence, choosing to forget
    Never again would he paint in blue
    Nor red, nor gold in any other hues

    Now, 2 years on he chanced to glance
    At the tattered canvas, he sees a chance
    To make correct what once was wrong
    He knows how to beautify, twas in him all along

    His heart once cold, and hard as stone
    Has now found love in instances unknown
    With the courage to paint that canvas again
    He picks up his brush with strokes refrain

    So the canvas once blue, with red and gold
    Is now a piece of art to see and behold
    Yet something's still amiss, it don't quite make sense
    For it now has more worth than dollars and cents

    Thus locked in a closet, the painting sat still
    For a day when its master has his wish fulfilled
    Only then will it know the power of love
    And the meaning to the drawing of the two white doves"

    The purpose of this stranger's poem and prose
    Is simple, and true like a song compose
    That somewhere in the crowd there is a "he"
    Whose love for you spans ocean and sea

    And here's what he needs to say to you
    A short indication of what is true
    So don't be surprised as it takes you away
    Cause truly love is, and this what he has to say.

    "Sorry for not doing what I should do
    To an angel like you sent from above
    You're special and unique, in my heart you'll stay
    Cause you're the she I fell in like for

    And now IÂ’m in love"
