    Growing up

     

    When I was just a little boy

    My mum didn’t ask me what I want to be

    When I get poor results for my exam

    She would bring out the cane to hit me

     

    I would play soccer on the field below

    With a lot of kids which I don’t know

    Slowly we began to play more regularly

    And we all start to feel like a small family

     

    Life was so much simpler than it is now

    We don’t have things like laptops and mobiles

    All we had was a small pager

    Which mum will expect me to quickly answer

     

    But good things don’t last forever

    And now I am just an ordinary worker

    If I could go back fourteen years earlier

    I wish I didn’t make that terrible blunder

     

    It is too late now for me to ponder

    Fourteen years too late and it’s all over
