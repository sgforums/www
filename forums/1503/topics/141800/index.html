    No one has solved this:

    #7
    It comes
    on little cat feet.

    It sits looking
    over harbor and city
    on silent haunches
    and then moves on.

    And some of my own:

    Mysteriously I appeared,
    Nothing serious when I am explained.
    I am something, if you don't know me,
    I am nothing if you know.

    Above I remain dancing proudly
    Ministers, generals, world leaders all will respect me
    Play music while I rise to the top
    Conquests and great events require my presence
    Yet sometimes all I do is dance with my brothers
    What am I?
