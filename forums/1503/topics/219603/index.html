    Some information regarding The Rhodia No. 12 Exhibition.

    The Rhodia No. 12 Exhibition

    An exhibition showcasing the creations of 50 individuals from various disciplines: poets, designers, sound-artists, photographers and architects... Where they are each given the same medium, the Rhodia No. 12 notepad, and asked to create art.

    (Also aptly called an event for the nerds - after all, who really bothers making a notebook beautiful?)

    Dates: 6 November 2006 - 5 December 2006
    Venue: BooksActually. 125A Telok Ayer Street

    Call: (65) 6 221 1170
