FIRSTFRUITS Joint Book Launch

  • DeadPoet
    25 Aug 06, 18:10
    Featuring 6 new titles by Eleanor Wong, Boey Kim Cheng, Paul Tan, Yeow Kai Chai, Cyril Wong and Ng Yi-Sheng, this firstfruits annual launch once again spotlights a dazzling chorus of incisive voices.

    Date: 9 September
    Time: 7.00pm - 9.00pm
    Venue: SAM Glass Hall

    Singapore Art Museum(SAM) - 71 Bras Basah Road, Singapore 189555.

    Guest of Honour: Dr K K Seet

    "Earlier" by Eleanor Wong
    "After the fire" by Boey Kim Cheng
    "Pretend I'm not here" by Yeow Kai Chai
    "Like a seed with its singular purpose" by Cyril Wong
    "First meeting of hands' by Paul Tan
    "Last boy" by Ng Yi-Sheng

    For queries, kindly contact Enoch Ng via email firstfruitsoikos@ftnetwork.biz

    Organised by Singapore Art Museum and FIRSTFRUITS publications.
