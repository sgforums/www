    REFRESH BUTTON



    Am I mad, or does the Muse call out to me?
    Don't you hear her? Don't you hear her
    in the rustling leaves, in the wind's soft voice,
    in the water's laughter? Don't you hear her?

    If this is madness, let me go insane!
    Let me go in search of her, calling out
    to the bright sky: descend, singing goddess,
    descend! Bring us your immortal music:

    the sound of flutes playing like wind
    over bending grass, the sound of strings
    plucked like sudden sunshine, the clear bell
    of your voice thrilling through the world.

    from The Odes of Horace

    2005

    Two thousands and five ones,
    There are many things that everyone wants.
    The fifth year of the twenty first century,
    And two thousand and four became history.

    Yet the nightmares of yesterday was still fresh,
    Like a thorn in everyone's flesh.
    The rumbling of the tectonic plates,
    And the destructive waves that wrecked the coastal states.

    Sorrows and pains that many had shared,
    Donations and charity to victims by people who cared.
    Too little time to celebrate the joys but too many tears,
    Too little enjoyment to take away our fears.

    Let's hope for a little miracle for all,
    If there was a god that he will heed our call.
    A better year in two thousand and five,
    And also for everyone, a better life.

    Here Lies a Lion...Dead

    Inky nights sleep like dust
    gathered on the mantelpiece of
    my youth, my former playpen
    which seemed a dream
    but now is a cage
    of padded spikes
    and unfeeling caregivers.

    The immaculate roads snake with
    a purpose known
    only to those who see the plans;
    which wizened warlocks
    have crafted in their
    exceptional cranial
    spaces.
    You never really leave
    for it merely takes you from
    one place to another
    while the tar snake
    grows unsatisfied
    crushing the little
    unyielding wildflowers.

    When I turned on that box
    of packaged information,
    all dolled up and sugared,
    my soul's liver took a jolt
    of emotional diabetes.
    Somehow the masquerade
    was a wafting smell
    of dead ideas and
    dying lions,
    all embalmed with the garish
    pink and wanton gold
    of funerals.
    But this was not for any human,
    but a lion whose collar has begun
    its deadly silk
    strangulation.

    Alienation

    Swirls of water, cold and blue,
    Rush against weary battered hull.
    Lone ship in the vast vast sea,
    A speck of light in the dark loneliness.

    Ahoy, what is there, a pinprick of light,
    Could it be another ship alone like us?
    We've not had another soul in sight,
    It would be nice to meet one tonight.

    A signal is flashed, a reply is given,
    The other ship draws near.
    As she pulls up alongside,
    Men on both sides heartily cheer.

    It's been a boon, we've had no company for months,
    We can sail together, the loneliness is not fun.
    Both Captains have some rum and a friendship is sealed,
    Both crews feasts into the night, nobody knew.

    For days and months, both ships sailed together,
    Glad for the friendship on the cold dark seas.
    It provided a warmth to the soul of the lonely,
    And comforted men in times of crisis.

    But slowly, a terrible change begin to happen,
    An alien change came over men on the other ship.
    Their eyes glazed over and they became pale,
    It looked like death himself had marked those men.

    The men on the healthy ship looked on in horror,
    As their newfound friend turned into a zombie ship.
    Their Captain, with a heavy heart,
    Let out the order to alter course and separate.

    I only wanted to be friends, more if I could,
    If not, happy to remain friends if I should.
    Yet I have not drifted away without reason,
    It is your alienation that has made me gone.

    Never yield before the wind

    When the wind is cold and dark
    and the beasts surround you
    like the ravenous feasting of vultures
    and the sky is dark like the evil
    of our world unsurpassed..
    Fear not my brave paladin,
    my standard bearer of yore
    now affixed with the armor
    of the new, and the soul of the few.

    There will come a time
    where your burnished gun
    will be raised in defence of the few
    who chose to stand with you
    against the tide of unspeakable acts
    all unhindered and unheralded.
    As Durendal was the right hand of Roland
    now a plasma powered weapon of mystical might
    shares your soul.

    Wield this into battle,
    and when the night falls
    around you and covers you
    God be with you brave Marine,
    for you bleed for freedom
    and for humanity unclothed
    like a fragile baby.

    Stand tall upon the wall
    and may your diamond lance scythe
    the ones who would ruin this world
    into the despondancy of defeat.
    Be strong,never yield before the wind,
    And if you find yourself alone,
    riding through green fields
    with the sun on your face,
    O not be troubled,
    for you are in
    Heaven,
    and are already dead.

    Let the last bolt fly,
    the last warhead explode
    into a flower of a million stars
    and fear not,
    for what you have done in life
    will be echoed throughout eternity.

    My Advocates' Complains

    Tell me something my man
    what is it with you these days;
    you have doubts more than the grains of sand,
    more than the hellish scorching glints of ray,
    more than the mortals on this rotting land
    or the autumn leaves that withers away;
    more than all the crows in my orchestra band,
    more than the elves in the Enchanted Woods far, faraway...

    Doubts? On what?
    Have I not let you into my abyss-deep cellars,
    or peer into my darkest chamber?
    Have I not let you take the reins on my mind's pillars
    and cast all the others asunder?
    Have I not jolly well fool around with you in your parlor,
    or ride with you on the highways of meander?
    Doubts? I doubt so...

    Well, you see not what everyone here observes;
    Your train of thoughts've gone real askew,
    very much getting on our nerves;
    Really, your misdeeds are not few,
    and such treatment is not what we deserve;
    think of how we refresh you daily like morning dew,
    how you have had divinely exciting times in our turf
    where everyday's joyous thrills are new...

    So I see what you mean my dear advocate;
    it's not that I'm shunning you or anything of that nature.
    Just that things have somewhat complicate,
    or rather simplify this mystifying, abstract picture
    which I have painstakingly perused for so long my mate...
    for in it lies the exceedingly crucial juncture
    where I can find the trident key to abate
    the raging storms that my mind's agendas so perpetually feature...

    Well then, however disappointed we may be,
    keep in mind we're always there,
    faithfully standing by in the deepest, coldest valley
    ever ready with our concern and care;
    with lanterns that never burns out easily
    to light your path and expose the craftiest snare;
    for whatever you desire, we aim to fulfill readily,
    for whatever you want destroyed, there's nothing we won't dare...

    The Muahaha Song

    There's this little imp called Tralala
    bouncing here and there singing a looney song
    this song he named Muahaha
    yes, the looney Muahaha song...

    So here's how it goes...

    "Muahaha i go, as i see the guy in the Hugo Boss suit
    swaggering down Shenton Way,
    his haughty eyes hint of his pride in his pursuit,
    that meaningless pursuit of a so-called Elite Way;

    Muahaha i go, as i bump into this chick so voluptuous,
    all glitters of Tiffany's, her ivory arm clutching the renowned checkered bag;
    but like a supposed-to-be masterpiece ruined by an error so preposterous
    her other arm's around a confounded, lascivious old hag;

    Muahaha i go, as i overhear these four single ladies bantering
    of the hunks they drool over and dream about
    of men they need not, they concur though, so firmly

    Muahaha i go, as i see the pompous yuppie
    lavishly spending on designer brands and classy dinners
    so wanting of respect with his cheapened money
    bah, those cheapened money from his forefathers.

    So there goes Tralala,
    bouncing along
    happily singing his Muahaha song...

    Hunting the Devil

    Sometimes I look back
    and I see the fleeing man
    running his lungs out
    barely cognizant of his climate
    as if Lucifer was at his tail,
    demanding payment wrought
    of greed and humanity.

    Sometimes, I see you in Imperial Rome
    Alexandria and Constantinople
    a Hyksos princess or a Byzantine harem-girl
    who is not who she seems.
    Everytime my feet step near
    the dwelling place where
    you have made holy
    you vanish.

    I see you in the shells
    that bury my feet in My Lai,
    the metal that sears my black buddy
    into a pale ghost
    and a Jell-O eternity of his mind.
    You have been there
    everytime those rockers saluted
    your name
    yet you did not care
    for you had a grander design

    And I saw your masterpiece
    when the concrete fell like bullets
    and fire consumed above itself
    your cackling heard for eons
    but as I stood there
    mottled in the colours of
    my earthly army
    i swore that you
    will
    not
    have
    my soul
    nor the one I have chased
    for years.

    She was killed
    the day the Sun shone
    on the fields of Auchswitz.
    and little poppies grew
    as rebels against your cause.

    Desire

    Saw your picture just the other day
    and
    it spoke to me in a voice
    like the familiar warmth of granny's
    apple pie and ice cold
    lemonade.

    and still I stand
    deep in the shadows
    only reaching the light
    which you hold in your hands;
    so slightly, that even a flower
    would not survive this winter
    inside.

    So many have said
    or preached of love unbending
    like a reed in the wind.
    Say not but act.
    For the reed is tiny
    and his chances reflect his size
    but when the day comes
    they stand together in a bond
    fierce as people might
    cannot put asunder.
    The reed in the storm
    and the calm before my heart

    Four years in passing,
    even forty
    is not enough
    to embrace
    the light within
    your hands.

    Love.
