    Chasing Paradise

    Behind the wall
    lay a man, silent.
    He used to smile
    but the grins
    have recently shrunk.
    Intimidated by uncertainty,
    stretched far by hope
    unsung.

    He drew his heart
    perhaps she didn't see
    he walked in front,
    she walked the other way.
    But the sky failed
    the azure blue blotted
    his sight, assumed.

    Could it be,
    I was only
    a little boy
    in yesterday's
    wild sheep chase?
    ---

    This is probably one of my random musings, uncolorful and not as reflective.
