    Finger Food War Blurbs

    Another siren.
    Body parts covering
    The sandy lawns of a hopeful people.
    Freedom is on the march.
    Always.

    Swarthy men
    Led as dogs.
    Fed by teenage girls.
    Freedom of expression
    Unbridled.

    Pimply faces greeted
    The Reaper
    As he walked in the dust
    The Right to Life
    Raped.

    Two boots
    One rifle
    25 years returned
    to God.
    Freedom from evil.

    One President
    One coalition
    Unwilling in deeds
    Willing in voice
    Freedom of patronage.

    Fifteen years
    Same sandy dunes
    One family
    One choice
    God bless America Finger Food War Blurbs
