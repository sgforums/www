Never yield before the wind

  • LazerLordz
    24 Jan 05, 23:41
    When the wind is cold and dark
    and the beasts surround you
    like the ravenous feasting of vultures
    and the sky is dark like the evil
    of our world unsurpassed..
    Fear not my brave paladin,
    my standard bearer of yore
    now affixed with the armor
    of the new, and the soul of the few.

    There will come a time
    where your burnished gun
    will be raised in defence of the few
    who chose to stand with you
    against the tide of unspeakable acts
    all unhindered and unheralded.
    As Durendal was the right hand of Roland
    now a plasma powered weapon of mystical might
    shares your soul.

    Wield this into battle,
    and when the night falls
    around you and covers you
    God be with you brave Marine,
    for you bleed for freedom
    and for humanity unclothed
    like a fragile baby.

    Stand tall upon the wall
    and may your diamond lance scythe
    the ones who would ruin this world
    into the despondancy of defeat.
    Be strong,never yield before the wind,
    And if you find yourself alone,
    riding through green fields
    with the sun on your face,
    O not be troubled,
    for you are in
    Heaven,
    and are already dead.

    Let the last bolt fly,
    the last warhead explode
    into a flower of a million stars
    and fear not,
    for what you have done in life
    will be echoed throughout eternity.
