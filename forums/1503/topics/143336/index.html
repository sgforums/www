  • mistyblue
    12 Sep 05, 18:48

    dark melancholy rain
    awash this world of dust
    and the rush of winds
    bend the tress as such

    Rolling thunders sound
    rumble with the lightning sparks
    and the quiet patter of the rain drops
    upon my windows cast

    there the people run
    Drentch by the sudden pour
    Bright colored umbrellas sprung
    like mushrooms in the grass

    the skies churn as it pours
    sleets of water comes
    refresh this dry earth gladly
    and this my soul shall calm
