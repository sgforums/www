[Poetry] You

  • LazerLordz
    11 Jun 07, 00:37
    I know you.
    A glance, a steal
    of a head.
    That familiar voice.
    Those brown eyes.
    They sing to me
    and dance with me.

    Little by little you slip
    into my head.
    Where catatonia keeps
    me bound, not asunder.

    You looked
    and I gazed.
    The tango of fates;
    that chiaroscuro
    between you and I.

    Your presence graces
    my unfailing soul.
    And the sunset lives on
    to speak of our silence
    and coded existence.

    It's you.
    Just you.
