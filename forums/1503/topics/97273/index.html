    Fallen poet

    The fallen poet gives a long sigh
    He sits under an apple tree
    Beneath the azure blue sky
    Steams of tears well in his eyes
    The fallen poet begins to cry

    He mourns the lost of a love gone by
    He sits in silence wondering why
    Endless thoughts that fill his mind
    How did the love they had suddenly die
    The fallen poet wonders why

    The fallen poet thinks of her
    Every moment of his broken life
    He regrets the day he said goodbye
    All he hopes is for one more try

    The fallen poet takes his life
    He makes his mistake one more time
    He canÂ’t take his lost in his stride
    He slashes his throat with cold sharp knife
    He breathes his last and says good night
    The fallen poet finally dies

    Hello fallin, since I am not you, it is difficult for me to know exactly how you felt when you were writing this poem. So for this poem I choose not to comment on it. Instead I rewrote it.

    Here is my version. I try to come as close as possible to the original meaning. Hope you like it.

    When I look into the starless night
    The moon shines alone with an eerie light
    Beyond the swirls of the sapphire blue sea
    Waves of reality crashes down on me

    Like tides of ocean ebbs and flows
    People around me come and go
    Those that arrive will eventually part
    Never to settle down in my heart

    Not wanting to stay around for long
    Joys of company seems so far off
    Those precious moments I have lost,
    Have taught me well to treasure their worth...
