    Perched on the bowl,
    I sit and watch
    The ant that crawleth,
    Toward my crotch

    At how he fares

    It covers muscle,
    After rippling muscle
    Its aim apparently,
    Would be my castle

    Tired of watching
    I flick it away
    For since I am done,
    I can no longer stay

    But as I get up,
    To pull up my pants
