    Keeping Hungry

    To write when one is famished,
    is a reminder, a connection
    to a savage past where our present
    was just a dream.

    We must keep ourselves hungry,
    if only to drive the inspiration to hunt.

    .................................We hunt
    herds of rumor that wander the space
    between mouths and ears; we'll trap
    stumbling words, bend them into
    metaphors, so that they can catch
    the sun and reflect on a canvas of lines,
    a rainbow of overlapping memories.
