    Hi folks, here's 10 riddles to work those idle brains of yours.
    If u give up and need clues, tell ok?
    Me thinks it's not too easy, these riddles...

    Here it is :

    #1
    From end to end I span the air
    so you can go from here to there
    I land in land, though I've no wings
    I am made of much more solid things.
    What am I ?

    a bridge -- Othello_Red

    ------------------------

    #2
    The beginning of eternity
    the end of time and space
    The beginning of every end
    and the end of every place

    The letter "E" -- SBS9828X

    --------------------------------

    #3
    Screaming, soaring
    seeking sky
    Flowers of fire
    flying high
    Eastern art
    from ancient time
    Name me now
    and solve this rhyme

    Fireworks -- Othello_Red

    -------------------------

    #4
    We are very little creatures;
    all of us have different features.
    One of us in glass is set;
    one of us you'll find in jet.
    Another you may see in tin,
    and a fourth is boxed within.
    If the fifth you should pursue,
    it can never fly from you.
    What are we?

    Vowels -- heavenscloud

    -------------------------

    #5
    Walk on the living, they don't even mumble.
    Walk on the dead, they mutter and grumble.

    Leaves --- dibilo

    ---------------------------------
    #6
    You saw me where I never was,
    And where I could not be;
    And yet within that very place.
    My face you often see.

    Reflection in a mirror -- Othello_Red & dibilo

    ------------------------------------

    #7
    It comes
    on little cat feet.

    It sits looking
    over harbor and city
    on silent haunches
    and then moves on.

    Fog --- OldAhPek

    --------------------------------------

    #8
    It cannot be seen, cannot be felt
    Cannot be heard, cannot be smelt.
    It lies behind stars and under hills,
    And empty holes it fills.
    It comes first and follows after,
    Ends life, kills laughter.

    Darkness -- dibilo
    -----------------------------------

    #9
    I have hands that wave at you,
    Though I never say goodbye.
    It's cool for you to be with me,
    Especially when I say, "HI."
    What am I?

    Electric Fan -- DriftingGuy & morbid

    -------------------------------

    #10
    This thing all things devours:
    Birds, trees, beasts, flowers;
    Gnaws iron, bites steel;
    Grinds hard stones to meal;
    Slays king, ruins town,
    And beats high mountain down.

    Time --dibilo

    That's all guys, happy solving!

    Bright and beautiful I am,
    Touch me and pain you feel.
    Racing through a forest unstoppable,

    What am I?

    What am I?
