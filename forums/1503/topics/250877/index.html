Your porcelain mask

  • binarynwitz
    27 Apr 07, 02:37
    Slowly, your mysteries unfold.
    Yet already I was told,
    Do not be easily sold.

    The plot thickens,
    Your mists hinders,
    But they do not deter.

    For I'm hell-bound to know the truth!
    Hell-bound to break your porcelain mask.

    Do not hide,
    You have no where to run.
    Sit back, and enjoy the fun.

    I pick your mask,
    Bit by bit.
    And slowly it shall crumble.

    For you know I'll be there!
    There to break your porcelain mask.
