    and the beauties of the flowery field?
    Can you see the golden sun, the silvery stream,
    and the virgin forest that is evergreen?

    Can you see the children having fun,
    and the innocence looks, upon their blissful faces?

    and the songs of the nightingale, upon the rustling tree?

    Can you hear the children having fun,
