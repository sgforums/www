    Penned by a stranger, written with care
    As the words unfold, like rubies rare
    Poetic by nature, itÂ’s meaning discreet
    Intentions as pure as honey is sweet

    In love or out, as passion it stands,
    Reads like a play, a monologue planned,
    As such that happens takes you by surprise,
    Neither prepared, neither wise.

    The feeling that comes from a heart so true,
    Bear witness to this love renew,
    From a boy of charm, of intelligence and romance
    Who prays day by day to be given a chance

    To appreciate how special you truly are
    Like a diamond in the rough, like an amorous star
    Or like a flower in bloom, like a rainbow arc
    Like rain upon sand, like a shimmer in the dark.

    Yet, doubts still remain, as stubborn as it seems
    Oh, could it be just an impossible dream?
    Then do choose to awake your soul at rest
    For truly, with eyes opened, you are much blessed

    Showered with a love that only friends could possess
    From friendship to courtship and words to impress
    And hence will spark a relationship so real,
    Only then do the intentions really reveal.

    The smile upon your faces says much than you both know
    So why stop at expressions? Why not just let it show?
    ItÂ’s only when you accept this gift thatÂ’s sent from up above
    That you realize beyond doubts,

    Without question,
    That yes, youÂ’re in love.
