    and into the depths i sail, with hand over heart.
    i plunge deep, pouring forth my agony.

    to this day i hurt,
    to this day i heal.
    to the day i reveal,
    the one for real.

    i yearn,
    i cry,
    i bitch,
    i die,
    i call,
    i wish,
    i dream,
    for a world so perfect
    in an imperfect world.

    what overcame me?
    one can only ask.
    to choose the route
    that leads to the end.

    into the depths i sail.
    ah, ahead of me
    a neverending quest.

    a quest of love,
    a quest for hate.
    a quest of one,
    a quest for one.
