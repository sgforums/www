silence

  • preya
    31 Jan 06, 19:35

    i can hear you
    loud and clear
    but you don't know

    when thunder crashes
    a million times today
    words too loud
    wanting no answer

    so i stay silent

    when lightning burns
    spotlighting, highlighting imaginary flaws
    a look of fury
    too far for an answer

    so i stay silent

    when it ices over
    to trap and drown and freeze
    cold anger within walls
    ignoring the answer

    words tear at my throat
    but i stay silent

    and the rain is soft and
    unoticed
