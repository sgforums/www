    The unwilling stalker

    I watch you from darkness,
    In deafening silent.
    Dare not make a sound,
    Just in case you look around.

    In dark corners I hide,
    Like rats in broad daylight.
    Hidden from your views,
    Watching your move day and night.

    At least in you eyes,
    I am an invisible man.
    Not worthy of your attention,
    Though I am truly your humble servant.

    My feelings for you,
    I buried deep inside.
    Bringing them to my grave,
    With my memories and dreams.

    I fell in love with a lady,
    a real beauty in my eyes.
    But sadly she loved another,
    and her heart will never be mine.

    and have her by my side.
    How I long to hold her,
    the lady of my delight.

    under the cold distant sky.
    Though I knew she will never come,
    but I knew I had to try.

    as I waited alone in vain,
    A song about a sad story,
    a song of love and pain.

    But I was foolish a young man then,
    and that was so long ago.
