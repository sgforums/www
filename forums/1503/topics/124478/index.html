    Dust in the wind

    All we are is dust in the wind landed on another brick in the wall
    Through all and all, in the end we have to fall
    Life is like a tunnel we all have to crawl
    Until dead leaves and friends we saw
    That's when the wind answers the call
    And we all have to fall on the floor
    Now, we're dust in the wind landed on another crack in the floor

    Blades of grass

    Falling leaves of wind's mercy
    Covering grasses of no one's fancy
    Thickening fields with every howl
    Dragging my feet through crispy tiles
    Trampling on blades for many miles

    The blades never seems to rust
    Which only its cover does
    Forest's fury seems to melt
    But a simple breeze to strike more

    Winds may fan the flame
    Cools the edges at the same
    Little and weak grass may be
    But it's immortality makes us green
