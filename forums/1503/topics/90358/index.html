Bored, I was. Bored, I am. Bored, I will be.

  • ratslen
    21 Aug 04, 14:09
    Bored, I was. Bored, I am. Bored, I will be.

    Time, why are you moving so slowly?
    And it torments to watch the second hand tick away.
    Oh, the lizard on the wall!
    Are you waiting for dinner?

    Bored, I am and even more, lonely.
    And I am giving many comments on this day.
    Oh, the people in the shopping mall!
    Your wallets are getting thinner.

    Work, does it have to be so troublesome?
    And my butt is stucked to the chair.
    Oh, the music in the background!
    I am weary of your repetitions.

    Money, I believe I can earn a tidy sum,
    But why, do I have to wait for the white on my hair?
    Oh, the stomach moaning sound.
    And it isn't even time for refections!

    Time, are you still with me?
    If yes, why do you leave me in the lurch?
    Oh, the words on the screen,
    They are all so boringly familiar.

    Bored, I will be,
    If love is something I must search.
    Oh, the wedding bells is keen,
    But there is no one for me here.

    This is devoted to a friend of mine. Name not disclosed.

    I am not crying, I am not crying.

    Where are you, the person I call friend?
    I am not crying, I am not shouting.
    You told me today is within our grasp but you let it go.
    To let the sands of time slip past between the fingers.

    You said that the past is a faded glimpse of our shadows,
    But why you let that darkness consume you?
    To let hurt roll over your conscience,
    And take you to a place you'll never return.

    You asked me to believe in the future,
    That there is still tomorrow to be had.
    Yet bitterly, you flunged yourself over the top.
    You wouldn't even leave the answers why.

    I lost you but I am not crying.
    Because you will not hear me anymore.
    I tell myself I will not weep.
    Even in the face of your fall.

    I remember our encounters so vividly it felt like yesterday.
    And I could not believe you were gone.
    I trudge alone now because you left,
    But there will be others along the way.

    I am not crying because time has no place for tears,
    Because you had forgotten about me,
    To take that flight,
    Of doom.
