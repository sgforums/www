    The Deserted Stage

    Take a bow.
    The music plays on.

    Curtains unwinded.
    Lots to see.

    Strings of puppets.
    Dangling.
    Barely make it.

    Weak as it seems,
    of superficial orders...
    Controls the whole show.

    Dance. Music.
    Drum beats. Cockroaches which flee out of it.
    Dance steps. Steps.
    Dead cockroaches on scene.

    Laughters. Excitements.
    Muffle the dead ends.
    Children.
    Time for bed.

    Curtains. Unrolled of rusty frames.
    Revealed. The rotten footworks.
    Welcome to maggots' home.
    Termites join in the funeral's wepts.
    Hardly visible to the audience's eyes.

    Come laugh with us.
    Come cry with us.
    Come dine with us. On the stage.

    Where the voices are no longer there.
    The spirits are no longer there.
    The meats are no longer.
    Except for the rottings.
