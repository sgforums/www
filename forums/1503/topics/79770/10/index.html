    My Quiet Space

    When I see them falling back on the white sand,
    I see my foot prints everywhere.

    Every single gesture I made,
    every thoughts I had,
    captured before me as if
    I can see them blink right in my very eyes.

    Upon closer inspection,
    they seems unbearable.
    They have no order.
    They hold no superiority.
    Like some fanatics waving at me.

    Still,
    I can't help but to touch them.
    To be touched by them as they reach out for me.
    The signatures they hold,
    their mysteries so bold.
    But with one they're so completed.

    Days and nights,
    I can look into my dark sky.
    Not a single word spoken by me.

    And yet,
    it is them who have brought me upon this journey...
    Of a new creation and as me,
    their creator.

    Rain or shine,
    when my mood do not fail me.

    Sharp or blunt,
    they're cut up as pieces of me....

    When one fails to impress others....
    Mostly,
    indulgence is within me......
