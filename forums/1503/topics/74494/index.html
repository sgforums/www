    True_Xerion: 81

    As forlorn moon in a wayward sky falls,
    a solitary shadow across unlit park cuts.
    in shabby garb, bag in hand.
    anguish in his drifting orbs.
    sanctuary a cold husk,
    family shuns infirmity.
    dismal thoughts,
    castaway.

    AdAptAlis: Two Worlds

    A normal boy,
    A good - looking girl
    Girl brought him joy
    And colours to his world

    A bubbly girl,
    An ultra shy boy
    Puts him in a downward swirl
    Plays him similar to an old boring toy

    Joy or toy
    World or swirl
    Two distinct thoughts
    From both a boy and a girl

    tare: Last Kiss

    in your bossom I lay,
    with nothing in our way,
    looking into your soul,
    and finds a gal untold,
    that gal whom i'm not,
    holds you in a knot,
    I hold back my sorrow,
    and kiss you with no tomorrow.

    oceanic: My Own World

    A wish unfufill
    A thought unknown
    My own world had long vanish
    Anguish fill my mind

    Forlorn for how long?
    Waiting isn't a solution
    Can't I just walk away?
    And simply stop this 'play'?

    Crying will not prolong my wish
    Giving in will not satisfy my soul
    Walking away without turning back
    My own world is still a solitary trip in this world

    An hour which controls within us... Blink..."

    MoonIce: You are

    My only half
    How I long for you
    How you stay in my mind for long
    I ask my soul

    That day as I look at you
    I know
    I will always think of you
    For you am it

    Why you I ask
    You I do not know
    But thus far
    I sing your words with all my soul and spirit of the mind
