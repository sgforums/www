    Poem #4

    The Two of Us

    Something was there, that only they could feel,
    the love for each other, so strong and real.

    They were always together, never apart,
    sharing almost everything, even their hearts.

    Their hands met, clasped so firm and tight,
    they were never out of each other's sight.

    He was there when she needed him the most,
    when everything felt seemingly hopeless and lost.

    The day soon came when she had to go,
    when they exchanged good-byes, he told her so.

    "I'll wait for you, no matter what,
    for you'll always be in my heart."

    "With all my heart, I solemnly pledge,
    with all my love, I solemnly swear."

    Their lips locked in a passionate kiss,
    she soon left, leaving him thinking of what he'll miss.
