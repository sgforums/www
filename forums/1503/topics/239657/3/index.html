    [b]Poem #3

    Such A Vulgarian

    Boys I have loved and who loved
    come up to me
    'ah, you're ugly,' they say
    I smile my monsterous teeth and bite them in two.
    I smile shiningly and kiss them on the head.
    I tell them I am going to Tibet
    through a medical miracle
    I will suckle 300 children there,
    a mother of rushdie proportions.

    Sweet Shiva! They exclaim.
    I am out of my mind, I am given to schizophrenia
    they give me their cards to their specialists
    Dr. V, Dr.D, Dr. S
    My hugeness has made them kind and relieved,
    I am jolly as a horse with bells on the street
    they pat my hand with utmost pleasure,
    I crush them like chocolate figures
    into a bear hug, my monsterous dangs.
    [/b]

    Originally posted by DeadPoet:

    Image taken from www.gastro.hr

    [b]Poem #8

    Food For Thought

    Food for Thought
    who lurks around the edges and shadows
    Parsing
    The etheral and incorporeal given life
    and thus
    death

    Food for Thought
    who hunts panther-slow-and-stealthy
    And consumes
    the Illusions and the Dreams
    by dissection
    You cannot see the starlight for the dust motes
    You cannot see the starlight at all

    Food for Thought
    who prowls unseen
    by Inspiration
    who grazes in wide-spread, plentiful herds
    The young, the wise, the fruitful ---
    they are the first to die

    And even the fiery will grow ripe someday

    The predator is patient

    Food for Thought
    who feeds
    and feeds
    and feeds...

    Food for Thought
    whose vicious articulateness
    burns me out
    The hunt goes on forever
    And I die a little inside
    everytime
    [/b]
