    Has Love Been Forgotten ?

    The day I fall in Love
    The World seems to be turning the way I wanted
    Everything so Beautiful
    So Much Happiness to be shared
    Even faced with the toughest difficulty
    There bound to be a way to solve it
    So Happy was that day
    I almost forget who I am

    But as time goes by
    We both became weary
    So tired and so sad
    Everything don't seems to turn out right
    But have we forgotten
    How happy we used to be
    The common love we shared
    The happiness this union brought

    Remember when we first met
    Something sparkled in our eyes
    It's not something physical
    Where we can see or feel
    It is a feeling deep inside
    Inside the heart of two people in love
    Something we both cherish
    Where joy and happiness is abundance

    For every words that was spoken
    Is fill with endless love
    The love that is so great
    It seems it will never run out
    The sight of each other
    Always bring on a smile
    With it was a glimpse of light
    Tiny bit that can outshine the sun

    Remember the tenderness we felt
    The promises we made to each other
    With so much to hold on and cherish
    We must persevere on
    Even in times of trouble
    We have each other to rely on
    To Love, to care and to trust
    To have someone there for you

    Then why let this love dies ?
    Why let it starve in hunger ?
    Hunger for Love and care
    To be part of each other forever
    Must it wait till love is gone
    Then we understand it
    Or have you lose the love you once felt
    Or has Love been forgotten
