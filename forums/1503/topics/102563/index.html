    Originally posted by LazerLordz:
    Hell.Global

    -------------------------------------------
    Listless in the murky
    depths of a lost being
    The rage ebbs and flows
    like a curious veneer of
    loathing and bloodlust
    of idolatry and hubris,
    whose stained power flows
    from the
    scimitar.

    The hands are drenched
    with the stark glee of
    the Ripper and a whisky glass
    of Sade's shot, 100% distilled
    to devious perfection.
    Like a drug designed
    to inure the hapless soul
    and drive him through spikes
    of devilish delight
    turning his back
    on the God he
    once served,
    now a slave to a
    godless Clone
    who dreams of
    retaking the Kingdom
    and dressing it in crimson
    waves.
