    sorry but i think i'm not feeling what you hope the reader would feel with this poem...

    first of all, a lot of the images used here are cliches... basically images that we see all over the place, used all the time and sometimes to death by other writers, and even in daily use of the language. I think what would help your piece would be if you could conjure your own images, describe something unique that shows your personality, yet conveys the same kind of feeling you want to express. Not saying that you cannot use cliches... but i think it applies for all kinds of writing whether poetic or not -- use cliches sparingly and effectively.

    Second, you were trying to make the even lines in each stanza rhyme. The result is that some sentence structure and grammar were thrown out the window because you were trying to force it to rhyme. It makes the reading awkward. I'd rather you didn't force a rhyme... let the words come naturally. would have been better.

    Keep practising yah??
