    It just seem harder to write anything after stopping for a while... Anywayz, me most recent work, just thought to share...

    Twin souls hoping to share a heart,
    One of insecurity, another- ever more scarred...
    So very close, yet never futher apart
    Ever so near, yet ever afar.

    Stars that glide across the sky
    Angels fell from paradise
    Emotions calm as monsoon tides
    A fairy tale night of sweet surprise.

    One night of dreams and wonders galore,
    Illusions enchanted, Reality lost...
    Dreams fade with the breaking of dawn
    False hope was distributed... but not for long...

    Kindred spirits hoping to complete their half
    When one started seeking, the other gave up.
    Ever so close, and yet so far
    So very near, and yet never more apart...
