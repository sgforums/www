    to anything that is you

    i hate you and all thoughts of you,
    that lick and suck my mind;
    that rape and burn my soul;
    that steal, that kill, that ever destroy.
    And yet,
    you,
    with your sweet fake smile;
    with your moist sweaty palms;
    and your thick ugly-looking glasses,
    do touch
    me,
    in a place
    where even angels miss.

    the room

    1.02am
    she smells its orange,
    shadowed in pouring caresses;
    of others' flooding presence,
    of many memories whitened into yellow.

    2.01am
    she feels the yellow,
    rusting in its throbbing gushes;
    crushing every breath,
    writhing each limb,
    she twitches and trembles.
    There,
    on her sealed lips where her urges seal,
    she erects
    dreams within walls,
    cemented.
