    Since everyone suggesting their version, here's mine... kekeke

    Her Smile

    That smile of hers,
    Like a candy melting in the mouth.
    Diffusing sweetness
    that fills the air.

    That smile of hers,
    Guileless like a newborn
    With innocence and truth
    that shines so fair

    That smile of hers,
    Like light in the darkness.
    Showers me with warmth
    and tender loving care

    That smile of hers,
    So pure and magical.
    Leaves an imprint
    deep down My heart, it's forever there.
