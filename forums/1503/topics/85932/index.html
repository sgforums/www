    Regrets
    To whom I may have love;
    To whom I may have hurt;
    My life would have been incomplete
    if you have not appear;
    So I'm stating to you truthfully
    its impossible for both of us to be with;
    Harsh it might be to thee;
    But its the only way I could make thee leave me;
    My love for you shall remain the same
    but more of friendship than of tender;
    My feeling is hurt;
    Like you, my life is of miserable;
    Hoping for your pain to end soonest;
    To thee, I dare not seek for forgivness
    hence I shall feel remorseful forever....
    ....To someone whom I have hurt "Sorry for all the hurt that I have cause & I shall never forgive myself for causing you so much sadness"
