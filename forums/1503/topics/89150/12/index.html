    But yet not all are perfect

    But there is a way to solve them

    But is it true you can always support the family

    Which you never notice before
    When help is wanted, surely there be a hand
    Helping out you to pull you up
    Have you met them

    Wondering why wonders is like a dream
    Where people think it's so common
    Might be day-dream or an illusion
    Where you always never know
