    Let go

    My heart, I thought it has stopped... But it keeps sending me thumps of pain...
    For it runs on the tears you've sent me... Creation of my destiny...

    If the sky is really blue,
    and the words you've once told me are really true...
    What color should I be using to describe my feelings...?

    The sky... If it's really dark...
    Where should I start to search for the mark?
    For the scar that you have left me...
    Upon my shoulders it has carried me...
    Through an endless route that always leads me back to the start...

    That day... If you had told me so...
    All this is but a beautiful dream...
    At least I could have been less confused...
    Holding onto this grip...
    Watching it as my heart breaks...
    When all I see is only of it slipping away...
    Slowly... Dreams...?

    For Carey

    All the time when I'm looking at you,
    I just can't help this feeling...
    For the feeling is so real...
    If I were to take it away from me...
    I can't comprehense what this world might be...

    All the while as I was sitting beside you...
    I can just see your heart looking at me...
    Smiling all its sweetness...
    By a name of Carey,
    that's you...

    All the troubles I've caused you...
    Make me realise that troubles mean nothing to you...
    For in your eyes I'm like a star...
    Shining a light in every corner of your dreams...

    Of all the things I've wanted to tell you...
    I just couldn't put them down in words to express myself...
    Sometimes they would just send me tears...
    So much I've felt, I just can't rationalise them...
    For you to understand of all the above...
    Everytime I speak the 3 words, it's so true to me and you...

    Togetherness

    Distance, it's driving me insane...
    While you stood right next to me...
    All I see is but just a face...
    Emptiness it filled both of us...

    Memories, what it might be...
    Even as I see you...
    Your eyes, they just speak of nothing...
    Not a word from you...

    Tenderness, a yesterday's dream..
    Laying in my arms, eternity it may seems...
    But today has just woke us up...
    You looked at me... How could it have been easy?

    I stood by the sea...
    The laughters...
    The chatters...
    I stood by you but you just isn't here anymore..
    It used to be a lonely beach...
    Now the loneliness is just all inside of me....

  • pipi & poot-poot
    19 Aug 04, 23:45
    The words that I yearn to find,
    Are often beyond my reach,
    To describe the tears I hold,
    To allow my soul to preach....

    I have a sudden urging,
    To see things I've never known,
    The maiden flight of a Firefly,
    Or when a field is sown.....

    I hope to feel the summer,
    Transformed to drops of Summer Rain,
    I like to see the colours,
    On the wick of a dancing flame.....

    Without you by my side,
    All truth has turned to lies,
    Just like the way your back is turned,
    Beyond the comprehension of my cries......

    This hand that felt your warmth,
    Now feels the coldness of a pen,
    In translating the things I feel,
    Into the writings from my hand.....

    I spend my life persuing,
    This dream that was supposed to last,
    But no man has ever won the wind,
    No matter how quick and fast.....

    I try to live life better,
    With the memories I have in store,
    But every moment bears your name,
    And in my dreams I have lots more....

    So I gave up on the struggles,
    Gave in to all the fights,
    As I tried hard to go and slumber,
    To live through another night.....

    Penned By: Pipi
