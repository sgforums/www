    Waiting
    Waiting...
    I waiting...for the truthful one
    waiting for one who yearns for me as much as i do.

    Waiting...
    Waiting for the rightful one
    he whom hold the key to my deepest thoughts
    waiting for the one to unlock my heart
    waiting to held in his arm
    with confirmation that we be together till old ages

    Waiting..seems ages..
    when will i be able to see the light ahead

    Remember clearly the moment when he left
    Remember the tear that significant the pain
    the pain that cuts deep into me

    Tears was the only form of expression
    i could use.
    Teardrop that drips down my face and mark the end of our relations

    Months had pass and tears has disappear
    yet the tears within my heart still flows.
