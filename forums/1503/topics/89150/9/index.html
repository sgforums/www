    28 Aug 04, 12:44
    Hi I am new here. Entering my work titled 'A passionate kiss'.

    His face was forgotten but the touch is lingering so long
    It is soft on the lips yet the feelings were so strong
    His name was washed away by the sands of time
    But why do I still feel it after a long time?

    I burnt away all the photos he left behind
    Threw away the memories in my mind
    Yet whenever I put my fingers on my lips
    A part of him remains on my lips.

    He was not inside the phone album on my shelf
    When or how we met, I'm not sure myself
    However the tears wouldn't hold back
    Haunted by the kiss he gave back.

    It was a passionate kiss
    A part of him I couldn't miss
    Why does it remain on my lips for so long
    When he is already gone?
