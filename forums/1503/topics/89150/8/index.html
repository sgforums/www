    Life

    Life is full of laughter
    But can even taste bitter
    Why must we live on earth
    Even though we know that life is a burden

    Life is a way of challenge
    That you want things to succeed
    But do you think this is life
    That you are battling...

    Life is nothing towards me
    As I can eventually find happiness
    Having friends beside you
    Is indeed nothing that be compared with

    Life is like time
    Where we will grow each day
    Never will the time stop
    Till the last breath takes you away

    Life is actually a treasure
    Where the value no one knows
    Treasure yourself now
    Like god treasures you

    Stopping TIME

    if only i could freeze Time...
    I will choose to freeze the moments of happiness we shared.

    If only i could stop Time
    I will choose to stop the moment u left me..

    if only i could reverse time.
    I reverse it to where I first met u

    Might as I wish,
    Might as I dream I could,
    I can't.

    I can't stop the time i spent thinking of us
    I can't stop the time we match like a jigsaw puzzle
    I can't stop the heartbreak i had when we together

    Last but not the least....i like to stop you from leaving me.
    'Cos I can't stop myself from missing you
