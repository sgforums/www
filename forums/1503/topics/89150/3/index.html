    My entry.

    Title: I Hate You... Not

    I hate you not,
    I swear I don't.
    You infuse me with,
    A warm fuzzy glow.

    When I see you,
    I close my eyes.
    For to open them,
    Means my heart will sigh.

    And yet I cannot,
    Tell you what I know.
    And what I know,
    Is that I adore you so.

    It is my cowardice,
    That drives me from you.
    That I appear mean,
    And melancholy isn't true.

    But I cannot allow,
    Myself to get near you.
    Because I hate you not,
    But I hate myself so.
