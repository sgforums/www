  • ratslen
    14 Aug 04, 16:25
    Without You (It all begins with you and ends with me)

    The girl walked into my life and gave me breath,
    Was a gentle breeze that lifted me on my feet.
    That tender arms and soothing voice,
    Charmed and mesmerized me.

    She is the one I am missing bare,
    Like right missing left.
    Oh dear, her eyes so pretty,
    To render me with no wit.

    Her magic kept me in her spell,
    Yet so my heart will rejoice.
    To know her name,
    And for her to know he.

    If God have a name it will not be fair,
    For she is made so dainty.
    If there is a place she shall dwell,
    Even heaven cannot be the same.

    And so he had a chance and walked into her life,
    Like a blazing flame meeting ice.
    The feeling of rejection stirred strong,
    Wrecking all the nerves.

    He is the one like the dozen,
    In that week that wanted a wife.
    Oh dear, he was so foolish,
    Yet he still tried thrice.

    His incessant kept her in the bleak,
    As straightforward rejection is wrong.
    Yet leaving his demands not answered,
    Is not giving him what he deserves.

    If we want answers God will not reason,
    For reasons are not what we relish.
    If reasons are what we seek,
    Then answers had already been served.

    So tell me where do I go,
    If my breath is taken away?
    Alone and I will die,
    If she do not belong to me.

    --------------------------------------------------

    Author's note: Just for fun.

  • Master -_-
    15 Aug 04, 16:34
    "Getting Over"

    I'll never seem to understand
    why I need you so much
    Why I stay up night after night
    longing for your touch.

    I'll never be able to explain
    why I want you so bad
    I'd give up practically everything
    just to get back what we had.

    If I could just have one more night
    to have you whispering in my ear
    To feel your arms around me
    listening to the things I like to hear.

    I can't help the fact that I miss you
    I can't really tell you why
    after everything you've said and done
    and how much you've made me cry.

    If I could just have the courage
    to really try to make things right
    I want so bad for you to be
    pulling me close and holding me tight.

    I don't really understand
    why you had to say goodbye
    you told me that you'd always need me
    I should have known it was a lie.

    You let me fall over you
    and you knew it at the time
    and still you insisted on telling me
    that you would always be mine.

    Now the only thing I think about
    is everything that you do
    and the question I keep asking myself
    is how can I get over you?

    *If its familiar, its a poem i used on a thread donkey years ago*
