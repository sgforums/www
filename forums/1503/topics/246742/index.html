    The Cashier

    She returned after getting the change she needed
    and the customers-in-line slashed her with glares
    of where have you been for the past five minutes
    of my precious time     ;as she counted through
    the heavy stack of fifties: change for the thousand
    dollars the asshole paid for his three-bag $40.55
    groceries, the silent knives broke into softer sighs.
