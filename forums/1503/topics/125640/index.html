    #001: What can I do to draw you closer?

    What can I do to draw you closer?

    Catch your eye and hold your gaze?
    And as we get mesmerized,
    Drawn into each other's world
    Lose our focus in all else
    The sea of people around us
    Fades, becoming insignificant
    Obscure backdrop
    I see only you, and you alone...

    What can I do to draw you closer?

    After the first flush of attraction
    Comes the romancing
    Can't have enough of you
    I just need to see you everyday
    Weave our lives together
    Do little everyday things together
    Declare to the world that we're one
    Comfortable in our unity, seemingly inseparable...

    What can I do to draw you closer?

    Things start to fall apart
    And can it be that I find someone else
    Attractive, magnetically drawn to him
    So it's true then
    There's no such thing as 'The One'
    I've learnt it's possible to love another
    At different times of our lives
    We let in other people...

    What can I do to draw you closer?

    Truth is a double-edged sword
    Wield it and see what gives
    Whilst truth can bring one couple closer
    The very same truth can also draw another couple apart
    Can you handle the truth?
    I want to be truthful but I fear
    How will you respond?
    It was never supposed to be this hard...

    What can I do to draw you closer?

    Keep you alive in my memories
    Retrace our steps in the places we used to go
    You and me, timeless, in that place in time
    Revisiting our special moments again and again
    How can it be, that the truth can create a ravine between us
    So deep, so dark, so final
    I see you once more, walking away
    Drawing yet another one closer to you...

    #002: Starcrossed

    Starcrossed lovers they were
    Seperated by more than a mere
    Forces they couldn't defy
    Neither could it petrify
    For they were young and bold
    Strength with the love they hold

    Bitter sweet and sorrow
    Death, pain and misery soon follows
    The consequences takes it's toll
    Always out of their control
    Banished by the plague
    The walls that are opaque
    Just when everything turns to despair
    Always their love was there

    A rose by any other name still has thorn
    Still it didn't stop the beginning of a new dawn
    The death she did fake
    The poison he did take
    A fatal stab
    One giant step

    Fear the reaper they never did
    Families' words they never heed
    Capulet, Montague united in one bow
    Kinship formed by lovers' vow

    #004: the lure of dark passion

    my voice reaches to you,
    soaring through the night,
    though my face unseen,
    you hear it clear as daylight.

    my soul longs for you,
    the beauty in my mind,
    to see your face,
    and hold your body near mine.

    with my voice i lure you,
    into the darkness of the night,
    captivating and arousing,
    you came under my might.

    our spirit unite,
    as passion ignites,
    no one knows me but you,
    this ghost of the night.

    from you my face withheld,
    scarred by hatred hidden in disguise,
    i could not bear to see myself,
    in the reflection of your eyes.

    though i desire to be with you,
    spirit soul and mind,
    yet destiny has its voice,
    our bodies will be forever divide.

    #006: love in a strange land...

    across the oceans i came,
    to a land of elephants so tamed,
    exotic arrays of spices,
    filled with beauty and vices.

    being brought into your presence,
    it is almost hate at first instance,
    your arrogance infuriates me,
    and my openness provokes thee.

    strong and powerful you may be,
    yet stubborn and proud as can be,
    and the occasional cruelty,
    is what triggers me to leave.

    i threaten never to return,
    yet i saw your children as i turn,
    with wide eyes so enchanting,
    i was refrained from departing.

    patiently i teach your children,
    so eager to listen and learn,
    even your many wives are curious,
    of this lady from another custom.

    daily with your family i venture,
    into the differences and similarities in culture,
    through them i know more of you,
    your strengths and courage unyield.

    both fascinating and captivating,
    we draw near yet withholding,
    as we dance at the glamourous banquet,
    our deepest passion finally uncage...

    #008: the room

    1.02am
    she smells its orange,
    shadowed in pouring caresses;
    of others' flooding presence,
    of many memories whitened into yellow.

    2.01am
    she feels the yellow,
    rusting in its throbbing gushes;
    crushing every breath,
    writhing each limb,
    she twitches and trembles.
    There,
    on her sealed lips where her urges seal,
    she erects
    dreams within walls,
    cemented.
