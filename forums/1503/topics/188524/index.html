    POEM #002 : Stone Destiny


    Taken from Internet

    Stone Destiny

    Here am I...
    Under the sea of atrocity
    Surrounded by dark braziers
    Trapped in despair

    Evil's omnipotence
    Summoning abominable calamity
    Took the lifestreams
    Of my beloved family

    The messenger of fiend
    Depravity of an angel
    Mercilessly assassinated
    Eviscerated my devotion

    Droplets of crimson
    Pool of blood
    Pure pearl plunges
    Down my cheek

    Standing in front
    A huge tombstone
    Sepulchral dirge
    Cleaving my heart

    Stop!
    Stop... No more...
    This excruciating pain
    Too unbearable

    Enough...
    Spare me...
    Let me go...
    From this sphere of darkness

    Sitting there,
    Crying, alienated
    40 days and 40 nights
    Until I am petrified
    Into a stone statue
    "The grief-stricken maiden"

    POEM #004 : Catching Up


    Contributed by Bluesky_Liz

    Catching Up

    So it turns out that you're
    Seeing him and Joyce and Yit are
    Together
    And this isn't supposed to be news anymore -

    Or so you say.

    Watching our shadows stretch
    Over the rock face
    I remember that it is you who instigated this
    Meet-up. This Gathering.

    To commemorate? To celebrate?
    But what to - three years of
    Friends Forever?

    A stranger trails from behind
    Not a tourist but a man who walks
    Like he owns the mountain the same way
    He owns his camera -

    I can see from his smile he's reuniting
    With the mountain
    But not in the same way
    Friends (Forever) reunite
    He knows something I don't

    And in the brief moments
    You smile back at him and Joyce
    holds on tighter to Yit's hand

    I would rather I got to know this mountain instead.

    POEM #006 : Memorable Scenery


    Contributed by Bluesky_Liz

    Memorable Scenery

    The sun shines brightly
    Athwart the neverending firmament
    Piercing even the densest forest
    The allmighty burnished sun
    Shines like a coruscating gem

    As the time flows
    Thick clouds agglomerate
    Lovely evening is coming
    The sky wears a cummerbund rainbow
    Seen as an adorable tapestry

    Relaxing...
    Gazing at the beautiful sky
    Seeing the last remnants of azure blue
    The sky is brushed with golden hue
    Slowly dims into a velvet oblivion

    As night falls
    The moon waxes and wanes
    Illuminating the dark sky
    Accompanied by scintillating stars
    Sending a soft glow
    Radiating like a halo

    Enjoying the night breeze
    The cosy ambience
    With a cup of ambrosial, piping hot soup
    On my right hand

    I am enamoured
    By the beauty of this place
    God's omnipotence
    Has created such a wonderful scenery

    When the sky
    Wears the moon as its pendant
    I shall be at the quay
    Waiting for a luxurious ship
    To take me home
    Back to my own country

    May I be able to witness
    That panorama once again?

    All these memories
    Will be etched forever
    On my memory

    I hope...
    I can get another bite of cherry
    To visit that Arcadia once again

    POEM #007 : Have You Seen My Shoes?


    Contributed by LazerLordz

    Have You Seen My Shoes?

    Have you seen my shoes, my friend?
    I swear! I left them here!
    Modern black with flashy whites
    Stylish - I am sure they were

    Why are they not on my feet, my friend?
    This is a good question you ask
    You see, the weather was hot
    and my feet sweats a lot
    And one, really doesn't feel I should put to task

    our friendship if I have to walk around
    your house with my odourous feet
    So i decided to air
    my two unwholesome pair
    Which was quite a backbreaking feat

    I tried to scrub a bit between the toes
    Did I mention there was a whole lot of grime?
    and some unindentified species of moss?
    Come on, being a little dirty is not a crime!

    Oh there were specks of little creatures too
    From my shoes they fell
    About a hundred of them - kind of cute they were
    But harmless - one could tell

    Allow me to confess though, my friend
    The emanation was an abomination
    If I wax lyrical - the smell was almost physical
    and rather Alive - as if it has awaken
    and taken on a life of its own
    kind of a volatile, hostile tang it had
    One could almost taste the stench
    It was really quite so bad

    Are you feeling alright, my friend?
    Why have you turned so white?
    Stop stuttering and stammering
    and start worrying about my plight

    What did you say? Shoes? Walking past?
    Without feet attached you say?
    You are making me more than a little frustrated
    If you continue this jesting play
    What did you mean, from just behind me
    my shoes have walked away?
    by themselves down the lane
    in the bright light of the day?

    Enough! If you do not wish to help
    Just be honest and say so
    Wipe that horrified look off your face
    And speedily I would go
    Hey! I have not finished yet
    Where are you going so hastily?
    What? Run? In what, my friend?
    My feet are naked you can see!

    Ah! What is this! My Shoes!
    I must have missed it, though I swear
    it is really kind of a little curious
    for I remember ... looking there...

    POEM #009 : Oblivion


    Contributed by LazerLordz

    Oblivion

    He sits on a seat
    not meant for him,
    he tilts his head
    and rests.

    The world goes by
    without his noticing,
    and oblivion becomes
    his name.

    Not only is oblivion
    his name now, but his
    being, his nature and
    his soul.

    A temporary respite
    for the tired man,
    oblivion visited
    and claimed.

    But when oblivion
    leaves the man, he
    looks around him
    and sees

    A world going by
    without noticing him,
    oblivion already theirs
    as claimed.
