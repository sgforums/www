    The Living Dead

    Never it was so silent before,
    Never it was so gloomy till
    A pin drop can be so distinct,
    Disrupting the emotions that
    A tear trickles down his weary face
    Its like ice uncontrollably melting to nature's warmth
    Its like time, unfeeling, running towards infinity
    But no words now can ever describe his feelings now
    Because he has turned into a living dead.
    Heaven had been very cruel to him,
    Had he left with her that very day
    Maybe his life will not be in tatters or if
    Medical genius could find a cure for love, loss, grief
    Time is the only cure for this illness my friend,
    Time will wash away your pain slowly
    But as now Life still have to go on
    Because she always wanted you to be brave so
    Be a man and live life to the fullest
    That faithful day perhaps its her last wish to god,
    What could be the reason that you survive and lived
    Such is a good reason to not let her good intention down
    So she will not weep when she saw you again
    Let her memories be kept in a corner of your heart,
    Lasting till the end of your life
    So long the emotions are kept controlled
    Such that you can be a living person once again!

    Eric

    Loneliness

    When the future is a distant vague,
    the sentiments is often stirred
    adjacent objects are still as tarnished
    without essence or feelings attached
    The shadow is then so creeped
    along by my dragging loud feet
    that the thought of it echoes
    unbelievably; the call of dire loneliness
    like the thorns to a rare budding tangerine rose
    and the latter do not just dematerialize
    as when the rose do decease,
    the phenomenon does not just falter away. Now,
    slowly, my heart breathes to you
    while the roses dance in the light showers.
    Eric

    Story Of A Tear

    A tear so trivial it is,
    Yet withholding so many emotions,
    Unlocking so many untold deep secrets.
    The measure of one soul,
    Either the Devil or the Angel,
    Sadness or Happiness.
    May it be a broken heart, or
    May it be a blissful romance,
    The tear means so much.
    Words can't define many feelings,
    Be it overhelming pain or joy,
    But a single tear can tell the whole story.
    So no matter how little it may seems,
    Do not let it deceive you,
    A single tear is a key to a human's heart.

    Eric

    Eric's Wish

    I often wanted to speak what's on my mind,
