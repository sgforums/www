    ~Blue

    Monday. Life. Used to be all blues. Now. The sea. The ocean. The sky. We shall soar. We shall swim. We shall fight and struggle. Sink no more? Blue. One of our favourite colours. Seems like an eternal stretch...

    Red

    Blood. Hatre. Anger. SINS we've all commited. Red. Drops of blood falling from the sky. When it lands on us, would we cry? RED. BURNING. Flesh. CHAOS in its ACTIVE STATE. RED. ALLURING.

    BLACK

    PAIN. NUMBNESS. SILENCE. ETERNITY. The FORBIDDEN and UNQUESTIONABLE. The MYSTERY. CHAOS is ITS ETERNAL STATE. So 'PLAIN', yet so 'ATTRACTIVE'. BLACK. WHAT can you SEE?

    WHITE

    WIDE. BRIGHT. JUSTICE. BLINDNESS. EMPTINESS. Beauty in its simple form. WHITE. SPOTLESS. Nothing to see. Makes you tired....

    Green

    TREES. NATURE. FREEDOM on EARTH. LIFE. How far can you run? How long can you live? GREEN. LIFE. How SILENT can you be?

    GREY

    MIXED. NOT PURE, but that's LIFE. Variation to plain life, but not much. Not much, but yet so much... GREY. Shades. Whole range of it. Celebration of life, in its plain forms.
