    The night was cool and blue,
    Painted by streaks of clouds, white and pale.
    The air was icy and eerie,
    with a tingling bite at my flesh.
    My head was dizzy, my heart heavy,
    eager in anticipation.

    The radiance and brightness of her smile,
    somehow didn't calm my spirit.
    I had a burning question to ask,
    Yet my courage seemed to fail me.
    O Such moments drive me wild!

    Fear, excitement, hope, joy, sadness -
    All these feelings swirling in me.
    I just couldn't control,
    Emotions got the better of me and I slipped,
    - I like to take you out for Valentines -

    She flashed that smile again,
    yet i felt it hid a bearer of grave news
    - I like to be alone for that day -
    I smiled, but behind that smile lurked,
    disappointment, despair, rejection.
    Alas! I was not to have her

    - I understand! Good night -
    My body turned, but my heart and soul longed to linger
    I felt afraid, scared, rejected,
    feelings I couldn't comprehend.
    I walked bravely back into the night,
    But my heart cracked that night.
