The clock strucked thirteen tonight

  • Nelstar
    20 Dec 04, 00:59
    The clock strucked thirteen tonight,
    Oh, what a time for me to have my plight?
    To listen to people grumbling about others,
    When these people had once regarded brothers?
    The clock strucked thirteen tonight,
    For the rain lost her red moonlight.

    The clock strucked thirteen tonight,
    Oh, who is to judge who is wrong or right?
    To listen to people grumbling about friend,
    Turned on each other into strangers in the end?
    The clock strucked thirteen tonight,
    For the joy had lost the cloud from her sight.

    The clock strucked thirteen tonight,
    Oh, forgive me for I could not shred any light.
    To tell on others when I'm as blind as the rest,
    For them I had never fought for with any zest.
    The clock strucked thirteen tonight,
    For the star has lost his mind in the blight.

    The clock had strucked thirteen tonight,
    Oh, all the zeros gone from here with flight.
    To one's demise again is a good way out,
    Dear friend, do I have any doubt?
    The clocked had strucked thirteen tonight.
    I shall disappear to everyone's delight.
