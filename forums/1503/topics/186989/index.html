    part-timer.

    sixty percent of me
    still thinks you're
    a girl, interrupted
    and here I am
    trying to fix
    that cable
    which connects
    you
    to
    this world.

    am I trying
    or is this
    a dream
    a waking smile
    which I recall
    vaguely, in a way
    like simplicity
    raises a question
    about life.

    who's?
