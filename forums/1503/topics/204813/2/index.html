    The title of the book published by Penguin is "India Smiles". You can find the details at their website:
    http://www.penguinbooksindia.com/Books/BookDetail.asp?ID=6323

    It was actually based on a global online contest organised by a very popular Indian portal, called Sulekha in 2005. There were as many as five thousand entries from all over the world. The first shortlist of sixty was formed based on feedback from over a million readers around the world. The final list of twenty six that made it to the publication was chosen by a distinguished panel of judges. My story, "The Man Who Married a Tree" was one among the 26. Last week I received a copy of the book. It is not out in Singapore yet, but I believe it would soon, since Penguin has the World Rights.

    You can find the list of selected entries at :
    http://www.sulekha.com/penguin/finallist.htm

    And thanks to you too, Bluesky

    Cheers and happy writing,

    Hari
