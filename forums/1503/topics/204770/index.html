[Photo-Poetics] Forty One : Skyscape Esplanade

  • LazerLordz
    13 Aug 06, 20:23


    41 years of flight,
    has your oxygen
    run out?

    Or are you still
    living in a world
    with hot air.

    Eating air,
    dreaming of Toto,
    and bickering
    over ter kwa.

    Kindred spirits
    wander in McDonald-land,
    lost in a sea;
    drowning in static,
    failing to find their
    plastic chairs
    in crimson light.

    Even in eternity,
    these good brothers
    face a media
    far worse than life.

    But I still gaze
    skywards
    to dream.

    Singapore.

    Ter kwa = pork liver. If you heard of the bak chor mee podcast by mrbrown, you'd get what I meant..

    Well, I chose the Esplanade because it is a symbol of what we have taken to be our own symbol of how artificial terms and names, can be made so familiar and local, i.e "Durians".

    The Esplanade was the State's idea of progress into the arts scene, yet, we have a long way to go before it is a real incubator of talent, not just a fancy place to hangout with.. something like Singapore itself, trying to look funky but we all know that the substance isn't there yet, and hopefully, it appears one day.
