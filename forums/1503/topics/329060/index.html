    Cyborg She

     

    Cyborg She

    A robot in disguise

    A beautiful lady

    With a pair of sparkling blue eyes

     

    Her heart is made of stone

    With no mortal feelings

    She is just like a clone

    With no emotion, sentiment and sympathy

     

    She’s an angel from the heavens

    Sent to protect her master

    Just like a guardian

    Shielding him from all danger

     

    Soon disaster struck

    And buildings are all falling apart

    Lovers separated by a catastrophe

    And even Cyborg She can’t do anything much

     

    Just when heart of ice seems to melt

    Everything starts to fell apart

    Heaven seems to make sport of man

    And they can’t do anything much

     

    Just when all seems to be over

    A miracle happens

    Cyborg She came from future

    And they live happily ever after
