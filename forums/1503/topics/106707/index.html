To The Living

  • fudgester

    To The Living

    Sitting
    Thinking

    Things might have been
    Different
    From what
    They currently are

    Thinking
    Feeling

    The organ of fire
    An everlasting emptiness
    Burning passions
    Lingering desires

    Feeling
    Crying

    Tears
    The stream of the soul
    Wherever
    It may wish to lead

    Crying
    Kneeling

    Forgiveness
    For weakness is
    A human attribute
    A flaw

    Kneeling
    Hoping

    Granted
    It shall be
    To the deserving
    To the living

    To be frank when I first read this poem, I don't really like it.

    But after reading it the second time, hey, not too bad, actually very good.

    This is a very creative write.

    At first you are (Sitting) than you begin to think (Thinking), when you think you tend to feel (Feeling), something trigger you off, you get emotional and you start to cry (Crying) and you kneel (Kneeling) down and pray to seek forgiveness and hope (Hoping). Cool.

    And the other thing I like is that each action is followed by a short description of the action. It is like a series of Haikus linking together.

    Well done!
