    Seraphic

    As days go by; I see a shadow of you
    Fading into the blinding light.
    I need someone to share;
    my life and everything that I've got.
    Don't let my heart break,
    and turn my love to dust; dead.

    The feeling's stronger,
    but does it matter?
    I need an answer;
    I want to decipher.
    Won't you be my angel?
    My lovely angel.
    So seraphic, so pure,
    my lovely girl.

    This world ain't perfect,
    no one is.
    It's full of payback,
    agonising; so sad.
    It wasn't meant to end this way,
    as nostalgia sets in.
    By what thou come what may,
    This world I had enough of what I've seen.

    The stage is set for us;
    in heightened atmosphere.
    The scenes flew thick and fast;
    they were never here.
    Each page I flipped were empty sheets,
    not even a blotch, nothing meets.
    It's all a dream; or so it seemed.
    Delusional and what you may deem.

    The feeling's stronger,
    but does it matter?
    I need an answer;
    I want to decipher.
    Won't you be my angel?
    My lovely angel.
    So seraphic, so true,
    my lovely girl.

    My heart's serrated;
    so easily torn from the core.
    Sometimes; it's just so serene.
    where us deux; so flawless, so real.
    My heart is bleeding,
    my tears are flowing.
    As I lay back; staring
    at the empty ceiling.
    They lied to us, they had us fooled.
    The truth hurts;
    it hit us hard.. so hard.

    The feeling's stronger,
    but does it matter?
    I need an answer;
    I want to decipher.
    Won't you be my angel?
    My lovely angel.
    So seraphic, so true,
    my lovely girl.
