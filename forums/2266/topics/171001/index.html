    Hi 2 all!

    This might be your first time visiting ABC Tuition Place Forum. Well, first and foremost, I am Angelia, one of the moderators of the forum.

    Tutors, how to you get about on this forum? Well, you should know you must register with us as a tutor first before you can bid or be contacted about any assignment. So, please head over to our website at ABC Tuition Place to register yourself first.

    Okay, you have been through the registration process. What should you do next then? Well, you will have 2 options. Firstly, you can wait for us to contact you on any new assignments either through email or phone calls. The second way is to check for any new assignments on the forum. (either this forum or the other)

    The format of any assignments will be in this way. Subject being: [new] p3 EMSC at Pasir Ris st 12

    Basically, it simply means that the words in the brackets simply shows the status of the assignment.

    [new] - this is a new assignment and all registered tutors can bid for it.
    [pending] - bidding is closed and parent is considering.
    [closed] - this means that the assignment had been confirmed and is now closed.

    E - English
    M - Maths
    S - Science
    C - Chinese
    MLY - Malay
    TA - Tamil

    Pasir Ris st 12 simply means the location at which the assignment is located at. Further information on the fees and frequency of the lessons will be in the message content.

    Do remember to contact the relevant co-ordinators on the assignments that you are interested in.

    Well, happy bidding and enjoy tutoring! Ciao
