    Welcome

    here we play according to handicaps (meaning give or take balls)

    Example 1

    Milan vs Juventus 0 : 1/2

    This can be read like: "Milan giving 1/2 ball to Juventus"
    Said in another way: Juventus have a "a half goal" (ball) advantage when the match starts.

    Bet on Milan:
    If Milan win the game you will win, otherwise you will lose.

    Bet on Juventus:
    If the game ends in a tie or Juventus win you will win.

    Example 2

    Leeds vs Arsenal 3/4 : 0

    This can be read like: "Arsenal giving 3/4 ball to Leeds".

    Bet on Arsenal:
    If Arsenal win the game by two goals or more you will win full stake.
    If Arsenal win the game by one goal you will win half your stake. (overall arsenal win by 1/4 ball => implying onli win half stake)

    Bet on Leeds:
    If the game ends in a tie or Leeds win, you will win stake
    If Arsenal win the game by one goal you will lose half your stake.

    1/4, 3/4, 1 1/4 etc also mean giving or taking 1/4, 3/4, 1 1/4 ball advantage.

    Note

    Here the accuracy is calculated based on win, lose or draw

    win full or half stake => consider win
    lose full or half stake => consider lose
    draw => match will be excluded for calculation
