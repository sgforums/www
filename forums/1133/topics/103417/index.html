    Coppa Italia Preview: Roma-Siena

    With the Coppa Italia being the only winnable competition left for Roma this season, they are expected to play a strong side in front of their home crowd in this first leg meeting against Siena on Friday night.

    Background

    The Giallorossi know that this is their last chance for a trophy this season. With the Coppa Italia being their only real shot at silverware with it only being the middle of November shows how poor this Roma side has been up until now.

    During the week it has got even worse in the capital with both players and staff pointing fingers at each other. Cufre, Aquilani, Montella and Del Neri have all had their say among others on the reason why Roma have been playing poorly when the real fact is they all have to look at themselves. A couple of players being the exception, such as Cufre and Montella, RomaÂ’s squad has been lacking the spirit and desire the Romanisti demand.

    With this now being an important match for Roma, they have called up a strong squad for the match. Totti and Cassano are likely to be involved in this match while De Rossi and Pelizzoli despite playing for Italy on Wednesday may have to play for Roma just two days later.
    Siena on the other hand will feel they have a real shot at coming away from Roma with a good enough result heading into the second leg at their stadium. Despite themselves not being in a comfortable position in Serie A, they have a good spirit at the club, but their will be some bad blood between these two sides after the Taddei transfer, or rather non-transfer this summer which has left Taddei rotting away in the Siena squad, but never playing.
