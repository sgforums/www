    Warriors one game away from semi-final berth

    Friday at the Tampines Stadium brings fans the second leg of the RHB Singapore Cup quarter-final clash between the Indonesian Under-23s and the Singapore Armed Forces FC.

    While many feel that SAFFC must surely be more than quietly confident as to their chances of progressing through to the final four, rest assured coach Kim Poulsen will not let his guard down.

    Much of the burden of leading a comeback for the Indonesians fall on the shoulders of midfielder Syamsul Bachri. Smart and combative, the 22-year-old was impressive during the first-leg, purposefully snuffing out the opposition while setting up the attacking rhythm for his own team.
