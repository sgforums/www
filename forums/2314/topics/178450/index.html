    Hi I've decided to start a forum that is dedicated to video arcade games. This forum is only for video games that can be found in video arcade centers.

    I realised that the local video arcade industry seem to be fading out, as compared to a few years ago. More people are turning to internet or console gaming. I for one, also stopped visiting video arcade centers for several years until recently.

    I hope that this forum will be a source of latest news for all video arcade gamers. Do feel free to discuss about your favourite games here, or bring us news about the latest games found in the arcade!
