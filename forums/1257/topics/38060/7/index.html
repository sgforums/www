It would take some time before the scarred landscapes of Prussia, 440mhz and Malaya could be healed of their wounds of war, and already the people were working to get back to the humdrum of peace.

Most of the Jaeger high commanders committed suicide while the lower ranked Jaegers tried to resist, but it was futile. They were killed, pounded to dust wherever they posed a security threat by overwhelming firepower which was overkill for their strength.
