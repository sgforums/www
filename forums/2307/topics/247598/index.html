    to Unique,

    "most of the time the mind is willing but not the hand"

    Do you know why? in fact you already know the answer.

    Our thoughs is faster than our motion, we need to synchronise them.

    Many time, Think & Think & Think, but we seldom FEEL enough.

    Feel what you may ask...

    Feel the Grip of our Ball,
    Feel the Breathing b4 we atke off,
    Feel the Balance at the Foul Line(Footwork)
    Feel the Release of the ball
    Feel the Landing of each ball onto the lane,
    Feel that we are in control of the path of the ball to the Target and the Pocket

    We need to Think & We need to Feel.

    Are they consistently the same?
