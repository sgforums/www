    The correc practice is to use a polyester ball for spares. The reason is that they are very predictable, whereas low end reactive are still not that predictable. Spare ball should be something you can always depend on, no matter what kind of lane condition.

    The higher spare you can get with your low end reactive is most likely becauseyou have not get used to and master sparing with polyester. Give it some time and you will see results.

    But of cos, it stil voice down to self preference. I am just advising on what professionals will do usually
