    Long time no regular bowling liao since our league ended, at least few months lor....sigh.

    Juz to share my experience to the noobies.......no completely right, but not completely wrong too....juz preferrence

    I use my tip of my shoe to take my position, these cut all the confusions i read from the book.

    Use the 2 roles of dots as starting position, some have 5, some have seven, so, its always safe to start using the center dot(20 board). slowly adjust it to align with the target(Arrow/Board) I wanna hit.

    As for board adjustment, remember, "Unless "All" Else Remains" minor adjustment can be quite confusing.

    You can develop your own "Where to Stand(20 board), Where to Aim/Hit(10 board/2nd arrow)" lists, it takes some time to understand our own method.

    Important things is Control , I must be able to go where i wanna the ball to go, which is not that easy for newbies. The only answers is Practise, Take Notes and make adjustments, till getting it rights.

    But if really wants to learn to bowl better fast, best is to take up bowling lessons from proper coaches, they will guide you through and correct the wrong habbits.

    Trial and errors take a long time......years .
