    step to clear first stage

    1. click the branch to drop

    2. click the branch again .. so the creep will jump onto it

    3. click the frog to eat that creep .. *beep*

    4 click the tree hole .. the bird will fly there

    5. click the small tree trunk

    6. click the fishing rod

    7.click the meteor

    8. click the creep .. it will jump onto the fishing rod

    9. click on the winding part of the rod a few time .... off the creep go ..
