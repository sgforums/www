    Poco Parcois an extraordinarily quirky Japanese title that works much like the Grow series of games. Yes, you may get excited now. Click the cute little icons at the bottom of the screen to start an event. The longer an item is active, the more it will evolve as the game progresses. The order in which you click determines how the game plays out. For example, some things won't be able to level up without another object present. The fun and exploration comes from trying things in a different order just to see what happens. It's entertaining even when you don't find the "right" order.
