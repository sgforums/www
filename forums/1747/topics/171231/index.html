    The Blue Chamber

    Escape from The Blue Chamber

    1.Click on the table drawer twice. Click on pencil to remove it

    2.Click to turn left twice

    3.Click on top right of picture once

    4.Click on top left of picture once

    5.Click on the bottom edge of picture in the centre

    6.Click on Money to remove it

    7.Click to turn left twice

    8.Click on cushion of sofa. Click on Paper to remove it

    9.Click on Paper. Click on pencil repeatedly to reveal writing on Paper.

    10.Click to turn left twice

    11.Click on base of pipe twice. Click on capsule to remove it

    12.Open capsule. Put paper inside.

    13.Place capsule in pipe. Press Red Button.

    14.Click to turn left twice

    15.Click on phone receiver. Click HELP*ME.

    16.Click to turn right twice

    17.Remove capsule from Pipe. Remove paper n replace with Money

    18.Send Capsule up.

    19.Capsule returns with key.

    20.Click on Door handle

    21.You Have escaped from the Blue Chamber
