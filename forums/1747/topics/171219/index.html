    The Viridian Room

    Escape From the Veridian Room

    1. Click on white dot on black screen

    2. Game begins

    3. Click on red shelf

    4. Click on shoes twice. Click on Paper to remove it

    5. Click on base of red shelf. Click on Photo Frame to remove it

    6. Click left of screen

    7. You are now facing the olive colour pillows

    8. Click on the pillows twice. Click on paper to remove it

    9. Click left of screen.

    10. You are now facing the cupboard

    11. Click on the bottom doors twice. Click on the photo album

    12. Click on the 2 pictures to remove them

    13. Click on the top shelf.

    14. Click right of screen. Click on the incense to remove it

    15. Click left of screen twice. Click on the paper to remove it

    16. Go back to facing the cupboard. Click left of screen.

    17. Click on Lamp. Click on papers to remove it. (1 on the lamp shade another on the wall.

    18. Click left of screen.

    19. Click on the fridge till u get it open. Click on the meat. Click on paper to remove it (note do not remove can of beer)

    20. Click on dustbin. Click on the paper w Black Hair inside to remove it

    21. Click on dustbin. Click on Lighter to remove it.

    22. Click right of screen. Click on the red Blanket.

    23. Click on blanket. Click on Skull. Click left of screen.

    24. Click repeatedly on left hand. Click on key to remove it.

    25. Click on medicine container to remove it. (hip area)

    26. Return to cupboard. Use key to open red diary.

    27. Read diary. Note the dates. Flip to last page. Click on CD to remove it.

    28. Click on the Black Hair. Click on lighter n click repeatedly on the black hair paper.

    29. Click CD case. Open it. Place CD inside

    30. Open medicine bottle. Read note inside.

    31. Go back to the skeleton. Unlock his feet with the dates.

    32. Click on the Red Blanket repeatedly. Click on green dice to remove it.

    33. Using the code from the medicine bottle. Place the papers on the green dice in order. U will get a soul box.

    34. Return to fridge and remove the Chilled Beer.

    35. Return to altar

    36. Place CD Case, Incense and Chilled Beer on the altar.

    37. Light the incense. Click no the Stick to ring the bowl.

    38. You have escaped the Viridian Room.
