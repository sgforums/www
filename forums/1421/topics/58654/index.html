    The birth date describes who we are, what we are good
    at and what our inborn abilities are. It also points to what we have to learn and the challenges we are facing. To figure out your Birth Number, add all the numbers in the birth date together like in the example until there is only one digit. The Birth Number does
    not prevent you from being anything you want, it will
    just color your choice differently.

    Example: March 20, 1950
    3 + 20 + 1950 = 1973 = 1 + 9 + 7 + 3 = 20 = 2 + 0 = 2. 2 is the Birth Number to read for the birth date in the example.
