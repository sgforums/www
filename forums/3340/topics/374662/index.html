FUEL Festival -- 24th, 25th, 26th, 27th Sept

  • Sound Formula
    7 Sep 09, 17:32

    [B][U]FUEL Festival Day 1[/U][/B]
    Brace yourself for THE wicked experience of the year as we kick off our series of parties with [B]UNDERWORLD Live[/B], their first tour in Singapore at the [B]Suntec Convention Centre, Level 6![/B]

    Where no other clubs have managed to get this renowned group to perform in town, FUEL has achieved the impossible Karl Hyde and Rick Smith of [B]UNDERWORLD[/B] to rock the halls out with their immaculate fusion of techno, dub, trance, Krautrock, drum n bass, ambient house and even the blues.

    [B]UNDERWORLD[/B] has distinguished themselves in the music scene with their ability to instill empathetic humanity and deep soulfulness into club hangers, while tying sounds and emotions to Hyde’s strikingly poetic lyrics, churning out soundtracks like those in “Trainspotting” and “The Beach”.

    Check out our website to learn more about [B]UNDERWORLD[/B]
    [URL="http://www.fuel.com.sg/underworld.php"]http://www.fuel.com.sg/underworld.php[/URL]

    [B][U]FUEL Festival Day 2[/U][/B]

    FUEL on with our second day, we shift over to the National Museum of Singapore and convert the building to an abode of music and lights at nightfall, where we promise you three days of intense music that will keep you on your feet till Sunrise.

    [B]HYBRID[/B] will start the beats on the first night. The music duo has graced international music events with the likes of Fuji Rock Festival in Japan, Millenium NYE in Melbourne and worked on various film score’s such as Tony Scott’s “Man of Fire” and Ridley Scott’s “Kingdom of Heaven”.
    [URL="http://www.hybridsoundsystem.com"]http://www.hybridsoundsystem.com[/URL]
    [URL="http://fuel.com.sg/hybrid.php"]http://fuel.com.sg/hybrid.php[/URL]


    Following this gig, [B]DARREN EMERSON[/B] of Global Underground fame requires no introduction as he belts out his famous tracks including “Crash Jack” and “Bouncer”.
    [URL="http://www.darrenemerson.com"]http://www.darrenemerson.com[/URL]
    [URL="http://fuel.com.sg/darrenemerson.php"]http://fuel.com.sg/darrenemerson.php[/URL]


    With the success of her gig at the previous FUEL party, crowd pleaser DJ SAMANTHA RONSON will be back in town to spin, with pre-party stints along Orchard Road for the whole week leading up to FUEL party. Samantha Ronson will be headlining the VVIP and VIP area for all three days at the National Museum.
    [URL="http://samantharonson.com/"]http://samantharonson.com/[/URL]
    [URL="http://fuel.com.sg/samantharonson.php"]http://fuel.com.sg/samantharonson.php[/URL]

    [B][U]FUEL Festival Day 3[/U][/B]

    FUEL brings the non-stop action even to the third night without compromising on the music.

    We bring up the tempo with Indie rock chart topper, [B]BLOC PARTY (DJ set)[/B]. Strongly influenced by modern electric sounds, the inclusion of synthesis and vocal manipulation intertwining with heavy instrumentation is what gives this group its one-of-a-kind reputation. The group has also collaborated with DJ Armand Van Helden who remixed “Signs” from their recent album.
    [URL="http://blocparty.com"]http://blocparty.com[/URL]
    [URL="http://fuel.com.sg/blocparty.php"]http://fuel.com.sg/blocparty.php[/URL]

    To mix things up a little, we present Peter Hook of New Order to take us back in time for some of that much loved disco fever!
    [URL="http://fuel.com.sg/neworder.php"]http://fuel.com.sg/neworder.php[/URL]

    The night progresses on to up and coming artistes “[B]FREAKS[/B]”, with their chart topping hit “The Creeps”. With a mind-blowing line-up like this, you will only be left wanting for more.
    [URL="http://www.myspace.com/freaksspace"]http://www.myspace.com/freaksspace[/URL]
    [URL="http://fuel.com.sg/freaks.php"]http://fuel.com.sg/freaks.php[/URL]

    [B]Boxhead[/B] will also be featured on this night to bring some Acid House to the party. Casey's first gig was with 808 state. He moved down south and formed a DJ partnership with Justin Harris (Freaks). They reigned supreme for many years running clubs, breaking new ground, spinning with the world's finest DJs.
    [URL="http://www.onelove-music.com."]http://www.onelove-music.com.[/URL]

    [B]WILL.I.AM of the BLACK EYE PEAS[/B] is a highly revered, sought-after musician, songwriter and producer. Come party along with one of the industry's top producer, as he rocks the house down with some boom boom pow beats!
    [URL="http://fuel.com.sg/william.php"]http://fuel.com.sg/william.php[/URL]

    [B][U]FUEL Festival Day 4[/U][/B]

    On the final day of [B]FUEL’s[/B] series, we end our stint at the grandeur of the National Museum of Singapore with exclusive artistes Johnny Vicious and Samantha Ronson, as well as guest celebrities.

    [B]JOHNNY VICIOUS[/B] starts off the night with his eclectic remixes. Johnny is widely known in NYC clubs such as Mars and Paladium with his DJ stint there in the 1980s. His label “Vicious Musik” with Jeffrey Rodman as a huge hit in the early 90s and subsequently became a massive name in the music scene for his remixes with hits “Kiss You All Over” by No Mercy, “Let’s Go All The Way” by React, as well as “Its Not Right But It’s Okay” by Whitney Houston.
    [URL="http://fuel.com.sg/johnnyvicious.php"]http://fuel.com.sg/johnnyvicious.php[/URL]


    Adding to this line up, [B]GURU JOSH[/B] will be at the decks dishing out dance anthems charted all over the world. Rave to ‘Freaky Dreamer’, ‘Holographic Dreams’ ‘Infinity 2008’ and his new materials with him at the National Museum, before he jets off for his summer festival tour.
    [URL="http://fuel.com.sg/gurujosh.php"]http://fuel.com.sg/gurujosh.php[/URL]

    [B]NORTH AND SOUTH[/B], the infamous duo on the free party circuit throughout UK, make their way here for the night, bringing to the audience Acid House, Techno and Hard House tunes.
    [URL="http://www.myspace.com/akaboxhead"]http://www.myspace.com/akaboxhead[/URL]


    With a magnetic stage presence and recently voted as one of San Francisco’s top ten DJs in the “Top 10 DJ Dreamteam” Poll, [B]DJ ICON[/B] brings on her energetic DJ sets and dancefloor-moving blend of Nu-School Breaks, Tech Funk, Electro House and Breakbeat to the night.
    [URL="http://www.myspace.com/djicon"]http://www.myspace.com/djicon[/URL]
    [URL="http://fuel.com.sg/icon.php"]http://fuel.com.sg/icon.php[/URL]
