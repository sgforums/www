    As much as the scout report card comes quickly by the next turn, send out your scouts to see the player for say 1 or 2 matches instead. If u are unsure still, send another scout to see the player's matches.

    Take note also what scouting knowledge the scout is strong at. The higher the scout's knowledge is at his best country, the better reports there is.

    Sometimes, a player is probably gng thru a lean spell, just recovered from injuries and such... so his ratings may not be favourable. So, take note too.
