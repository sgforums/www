  • CoolMyth

    i)Appeal decision. The game allows you to appeal once before settling the transfer.

    ii)Send him out to feeder club. For Belgium/Poland, 3 years is enough to let him get an EU (European Union) passport. Thereafter, he can play for your club.

    iii)Hope he gets called up by his country and play quite a number of fixtures in a year, that is, first team lineup. Also, not really sure about this, but his country must be in Fifa rank 70 and above. Then when you're confident, offer to give him a new contract. In the process, the game will also automatically apply a new work permit for him. If it goes through, the best lor.

    Originally posted by glacer:
    the problem is i cannot even buy the player, how to send my player to feeder club when he cannot attain work permit?
    You can buy the player, unless special conditions such as Brazilian youngsters that are under 18 and stuff.

    The sequence goes like this:

    Offer to buy player --> Discuss contract --> Work permit fail --> Appeal for new work permit --> Fail again --> Choose to let the player to still join your club (but he can't play for you)

    After the last one, just send him on loan (Find "Move to Affiliates") to your feeder club. And also, you can send him only during the transfer windows (ie, July 1 - Aug 31 or Jan 1 - Jan 31 for the EPL).
