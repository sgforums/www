    I downloaded FM08 Demo a few days ago and my thoughts?

    Its about the same with FM07, with several tweaks..

    Skin wise, its looking nicer and easier layouts of buttons.

    Match engine wise, the AI is reasonably 'down-to-earth'; you are able to beat the AI if you tweak your tactics accordingly.

    Player wise, some players are given too high attributes as compared in real-live.

    Training-wise, no difference..

    Negotiations wise, they are more reasonable and more 'real'. We're able to get the players we want easily.

    One good new feature is the "make player available for reserves game for the next 5 days options". So, when a player isn't called-up for international duty or when he just recovered from injury, you can put him as that status, thus he is able to maintain his match-fitness.

    Another good feature is the Confidence. Supporters are able to voice out their opinions on the outcome of matches and new signings.

    And also the Match Analysis is a good add too!.

    I'm awaiting the release of the full version here.
