    Feeder club have 3 main benefits:

    - First refusal on their players. If there are any bids accepted for any of their players, you can bid at the same amount which they must accept.

    - Financial benefits. Sales of merchandise. Get clubs in USA, Japan, China, South Korea, you get the idea.

    - A nursery for your young players who cannot get a work permit. Let's use the case of Dong Fangzhuo for Man U. He was bought by Man U but he cannot get work permit cause China's FIFA ranking less than 70 that time over a time period, so Man U sent him to some Belgium club for 3 years. So now, he has a European Union passport, which bypasses the need for him to get a work permit.

    For teams in Europe, if you want to do this, Poland and Belgium are the best choices cause they need just 3 years for the person to get a European Union passport. This is the shortest you can get, compared to either England or Spain where you need 5 years.

    For Parent club, you have also 2 main benefits:

    - Parent club may send their players to you on loan, where you dun have to pay their wages.

    - The Parent Club will typically play a pre-season game with you, with you at home ground and you get to keep all proceeds. Plus, your Parent club will give a small amount of money every year.
