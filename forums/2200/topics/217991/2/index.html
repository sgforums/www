    Allright, for those who are not sure about the "show onto foot" during the pre-match talks, it simply means forcing the opposition to use his weaker foot. SO for example, you have a opposition WInger who prefers to use his Right foot and his stronger foot is his right; so set him 'onto left foot' and your defenders will try to force him to use his left foot more often.

    And well, it doesn't matter how strong the team is; as long as you have a good tactic and counter-tactic, then I believe any team can beat any other team.
