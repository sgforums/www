    The more you send out your scouts, the more knowledge they will gain from that particular Region/Nation that they been to and thus the more players will come when you do a 'Player Search' of the country.

    Another thing, there's a few glitches here and there, since hte game's being released earlier then planned; things like a gk bringing the ball to the edge of penalty box before bringing back to his goalkick-line to make a goalkick. You can see that EVERYTIME the keeper gets hold of the ball; go ahead and see for yourself.

    There's also glithces like players being sold too cheaply/expensively to rival clubs , there's also the glitch regarding the Germany National Team. All in all, my advice is to look out for SI's latest updates and patches.

    Oh yes one more thing, the Eng Prem Lge teams are all 'logoless'; which means, you cannot see the actual logos or jersey colours. But the rest of the major leagues like Spanish, Italian etc etc, all the logos and jersey colours are exact replicas of the team.

    I must also urge those playing to read the Hints & Tips as they do provide essential information regarding gameplay. So do take time and read the Tips carefully. (ps. once installed, you can always read the actual Notepad file on Hints & Tips which can be accessed from the directory of the game in your computer)

    Last but not least (for now), do enjoy the game as it has truely blossomed from the olden days of CM. And to those of you who intend to get CM 07, may I urge you not to do so as there has been lots of bad reviews about it and well, FM rocks anyway!
