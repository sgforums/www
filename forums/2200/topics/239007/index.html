    Originally posted by Ben_Ng69:
    so just send him to feeder club n wait for w/p to approve?
    Nope. Send him to Belgium or Poland on loan for 2.5 years and he'll get an EU passport.

    The player will come back on loan once a year. After the next transfer window start, send him there again. Though the game does not notify you, the player will have his EU passport the third season you send him there and you can immediately recall him back to your first team.

    Real life example: Dong Fangzhuo of Man Utd.
