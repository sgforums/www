    Synopsis :

    Makiko Esumi stars as a talented 32-year-old doctor whom everyone thinks has no need for a man in her life as she is more than capable to take care of herself. She however, thinks otherwise. Once, she and her friend pray for love at the church of St. Valentine in Rome. There, they met a strange woman outside the church who predicted that Makiko will be getting married soon. Back in Tokyo, the subplots get more complicated. After saving the chubby owner of an exclusive Italian restaurant from a nasty gastritis attack on the plane, he introduces Makiko to the secretary of a Diet member and a struggling actor-cum-bartender. Then there is the crafty news reporter who is after the inside scandal scoop on the Diet member. He is determined to get it anyway he can, even if it means passionately kissing a stranger (Makiko) in the hospital hallway.
