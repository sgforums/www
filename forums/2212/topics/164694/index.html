    It's about Palace politics coupled with two triangle love stories.

    The initial episodes showed the process which ladies who entered the palace as potential concubines undergo. Also interesting to note the different "titles" or levels of the potential concubines.

    Most junior level : ju tsi
    Next level: tup ying
    Next : kuai yan
    Next : fei

    Sheren Tang acted very well as yu fei. Her facial expressions does all the "acting". However, Gigi Lai won the Best Actress instead and having watched the series twice, I do believe Gigi deserves the win. She had to portray more facets of her character and she did it brilliantly. But I must admit, I don't really like her crying face. She is beautiful but not when crying.

    In my humble opinion, the best-looking crying face is Myolie Wu.
