    Plot Summary:

    Lu Rui Xi is watching TV with her friend, Julia, and is struck with an idea after seeing Hi-Jumper Zuo Yi Quan. She wants to transfer to his school and become his friend, but the particular school he attends is strictly boys-only. After a shaky start, including rudely colliding with Quan outside of the classroom, Rui Xi is able to settle in at the school. By a stroke of luck, she is also assigned his room mate.

    Soon she is accepted as a normal student, but after sustaining an injury to the head, her gender is discovered by Quan and Mei Tian, the school health official. Mei Tian becomes her confidant, but Quan keeps his knowledge to himself, protecting Rui Xi's identity. Jin Xui Yi begins to question his own identity when he finds himself attracted to this new student, whom he believes is male.

    JJ Lin cameos as Rui Xi's elder brother
