    its been a long time, but i remembered the storyline to be something like this:

    starting of vampire1 : tianyou & tall jap guy aka final boss having their showdown in the 1960s, both of them dying, then jiang chen (first vampy) came and turn them both to vampires

    ending of vampire1 : tianyou gets to make a wish after beating final boss (lol) , he wished to turn back time + change history so final boss and tian you will not get bitten by that jiang chen (first vampy) and hence all the conquer-the-world & pple dying stuff would not have happened. his wish was granted

    starting of vampire2 : using the wish from ending 1, the show 'restarted' the start of vampire1. tianyou & tall jap guy fought without interference from jiang chen. i forgot who won (iirc tianyou killed jap guy) but later, ma xiao ling's mother requested for tianyou's help in dealing with first vampy. so even though history was changed, tianyou still end up as a vampire, tall jap guy dead but his son (who looked exactly like him) eventually became one too, but on the good side.

     

    also, since history was changed, vampire2's tianyou do not know anything about the events in vampire1. but vampire2 reference the history in vampire1 sometimes iirc, mentioning how it's tianyou's fate to become vampire etc
