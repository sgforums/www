    Casting: Steven Ma, Fala Chen, Yoyo Mung, Wong Tak Bun, Lee Sze Chit, Hung Tin Ming, Wong Chi Yin, Lau Tan

    The subtle relationship between Barrister SHING WAI-SHUN (Steven Ma) and pole dancer HUNG LONG-KIU (Fala Chen) begins with a cell phone. KIU has been treating SHUN hot and cold. On top of his he also finds her somewhat unpredictable so he decides to check her out secretly. His good friend KWAN HO-CHING (Yoyo Mung) discovers that KIU is going out with rich businessman KO WING-TAI (Lau Tan). Under SHUN’s questioning, KIU confesses she is coveting TAI’s money, telling him to give up his hope. It turns out that a mysterious man who has been close at hand is controlling KIU. Laying bare the darkest side of human nature, Links to Temptation is a thriller about morality and money, and greed and arrogance.
