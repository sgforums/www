    Wycombe 3-8 VILLA

    Villa survived an almighty scare before eventually cruising into the third round of the Carling Cup.

    Trailing 3-1 at half-time, David O'Leary's men responded by hitting seven second-half goals with no reply.

    Milner (2), Davis (2), Barry (2), Baros and an own-goal from Easton did the damage on a free-scoring nightat the Causeway Stadium.

    A slow start to the game saw neither side pres in the opening five minutes.

    The hosts had the lead on six though as Tyson met a Senda cross from the right and powered a header past Sorensen.

    Angel headed narowly past the post three minutes later as he met a Milner cros from the right.

    Johnson almost deflected a Baros cross past keeper Talia on nine. Milner then volleyed inches wide from the subsequent corner.

    With Villa pressing, Angel fired a right foot free kick straight at the Wycombe wall on 13.

    Davis levelled matters on 14 as Baros raced down the right, cut the ball back to the edge of the box and the young midfielder slotted home.

    Burnell fired wide from the edge of the box at the other end on 16.

    Two minutes later Wycombe were back in fromt as Johnson met an Oakes corner from the left and looped a header past Sorensen.

    Baros headed wide from Milner's right wing corner on 27 as, for the second time in the game, Villa chased an equaliser.

    Williamson headed over the bar from another Oakes corner on the half hour mark.

    As Villa continued to make hard work of things, Barry saw a left foot drive deflected behind for a corner on 36.

    Wycombe made it 3-1 on 39 as Mooney powered a low header past Sorensen from another Oakes cross from the left.

    Talia acrobatically denied Bary in stoppage time, palming a left foot curler past the post.

    Mooney fired high over the bar two minutes after the interval as Wycombe looked to kill off the tie.

    Baros pulled one back a minute later, slotting home as Hughes raced in from the right and cut a low cross back for the Czech international.

    Not surprisingly, Villa looked much livelier after the break as O'Leary opted not to ring the changes.

    Cahill headed straight at Talia on 58 as Wycombe only half cleared a Berger free kick from the left.

    Milner levelled matters on 64, making space for himself on the edge of the box as he met a Davis back-heel and firing a right foot drive past Talia.

    Bloomfield fired straight at Sorensen on 67 as Wycombe looked to restore the lead.

    Villa had the lead on 69 as Wycombe's Easton redirected Berger's pass to Milner down the right past his own keeper.

    Baros almost made it 5-3 with a 20-yard drive two minutes later, but was denied by Talia's impressive block.

    Villa had a penalty on 72 as Senda felled Baros in the box - Barry made no mistake from the spot, slotting straight down the middle.

    Barry grabbed Villa's sixth on 78, half-volleying past Talia as he met a Baros cut-back from the right.

    Torres struck the Villa post on 82 as he created space for himself on the edge of the Villa box.

    Milner made it 7-3 on 87, capping off the move of the match - a flowing six-man move - with a low drive past Talia.

    Moore might have made it eight on 90, but was denied by Talia as he met a Milner flick on the edge of the box.

    Davis did grab goal number eight in stoppage time, tapping home from close range.
