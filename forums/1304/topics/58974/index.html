    It's been 2 months since you left me.
    But then I always feel that you are still by my side.
    Every moment, Now and then
    Giving me all your love and care that you can shower me with.

    I'm eating a cake right now.
    It may sounds funny, but to me, it's all bits and pieces of heart break and pain.
    Every munch I take, reminds me the picture when I bought it for you on the day that you wish for this tiny little piece of cake.
    I know it means a lot to you..

    I just wanna say how sorry I am if I ever treat you bad
    How sorry I am if I ever let you down
    How sorry I am not to be able to protect you from harm

    Only till then when you left,
    those who did not treasure you regretted...
    But because of your departure, they then begin to realise that they had expressed their love to you in the wrong way.

    I hate to recall the day when I saw you lying on the bed, hoping that you could go home soon,
    I hate to recall the day when I saw you crying helplessly..
    But at least I'm relieve that I'm with you the moment you need me most..

    I enjoy the most when you started chatting with me,
    abt me, abt him, abt her and abt we.
    How you brush through my hair, how you encourage me, how u praise me to your friends...

    But my heart breaks terribly when I think of you.
    I really hope you come back or even still here and let me compensate you with all the things you have lost..
    But I know I can't....

    I always love you grandma..
    I always do....

    Always in my remembrance
    Love Angeline
