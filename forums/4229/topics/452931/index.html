The balanced life of Libras

  • Kumarmanoj533
    25 May 12, 20:20

    Libra (September 23 - October 23) is represented by the Scales. Thus people belonging to Libra horoscope sign is all about balance in life. They are very judicious, truthful and have a good conscience. They want the world to be a place where everything is perfectly balanced and everyone is considerate and compassionate. They are extremely diplomatic and are always looking to find a middle path, so that no one gets offended or hurt. They dislike conflicts and avoid them as much as they can. They find it very hard to say no to someone, lest it might hurt them. Hence they lack the courage to make strong decisions and are always looking for ways to reach mutual understanding.
