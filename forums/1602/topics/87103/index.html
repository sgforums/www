    Calculations for Men.

    Subtract the last 2 number of your year of birth from 100 and divide by 9
    The remainder is the number of your compass.

    If there is no remainder you should take the number 9.

    Example Born in 1962.
    100 - 62 = 38
    38/9 = 4.2 ie your number is 2.
