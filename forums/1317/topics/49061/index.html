MRT Sembawang - Changi Airport Line

  • tranquilice
    17 Jun 03, 17:44
    This is currently an imaginary MRT line, but may be possible reality in the future.

    The line is called Sembawang - Changi Airport Line (also known as the NorthEast Coast Line as it runs very near to the coast). The line will pass by future Simpang town, existing Seletar Camp, Punggol town and Pasir Ris town.

    The line is divided into 2 stages. Stage 1 between Punggol and Changi Airport will be bulid first.

    The imaginary station names and locations of stage 1 are as follows:

    1. Punggol station (Integrate with Punggol station on the NorthEast Line)
    2. Halus station (At Lor Halus, will be build later than other stations, when the area around it is more developed)
    3. Elias station (Along Pasir Ris Drive 1, near Pasir Ris West Plaza)
    4. Pasir Ris station (Integrate with Pasir Ris station on the East-West Line)
    5. Loyang station (At junction of Pasir Ris Drives 1 and 2)
    6.Changi Airport station (Integrate with Changi Airport station on the East-West Line)
