    Thot I'd post it here, so my darling Lingos can "discover" my secrets

    I've used SKII's Miracle Water for abt a mth (or was it shorter? ) & it has improved my complexion lots!!! How to use: After cleansing & toner ev. nite, apply a few drops onto hands & gently pat all over face.

    For an instant glow to the skin (perfect for the day after a late nite), try Liz Arden's "Let There Be Light". How to use: Apply a small amt to fingers & apply after toner & moisturiser in the morning. Follow wif makeup if u wan.

    Eyegel: I like TWO MAC's Line Eraser & Estee Lauder's Advanced Nite Repair. How to use: Apply after moisturiser & follow up wif makeup if necessary. At nite, apply after toner & SKII.

    Moisturiser: My fave is Clinique's Moisture Surge.

    Masks: I like using Estee Lauder's Deep Pore Cleansing Mask. Apply all over face, avoiding eye area. Leave until dry, then wash off. Another I use is Shiseido's Deep Pore Cleansing Mask, but tt's only for the T Zone. Apply once or twice a wk.

    Foundation: I alt. amongst 3 types. MAC's Studio Fix (I recommended u this, Lingos! ) is a 2-way cake tt's really simple to apply. Gives a nice matte look. MAC's Studio Finish is nice for a dewy finish. For best coverage, use Shiseido's UV White.

    RP
