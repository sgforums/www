    I oso swear by SKII miracle water. As u age, ur skin cell renewal is less often, so the miracle water will boost up the renewal process. After using for 1 week, skin looks more radient and pores appears to have minimise.
    But the rest of the SKII prdts, results so-so.

    1. SKII cleaner - smells good, like the good old hazeline, removes make up quite well. But the price doesnt justify.

    2. SKII solution - so-so. Very diluted and odorless. Feels cheated cos it feels exactly like water.

    3. SKII Mask - tried both treatment and whitening mask. The effect is fantastic after treatment. Face immed. glows and look flawless. But effect is gone the next day. Good to put on b4 ur party. Make-up glides easily. I stock up a few pcs to standby for occasions.

    4. SKII whitening source - A totally overstated product! Claim to work like laser to remove / lighten pigmentation. No effect for me at all! Sunblock is even better than this to prevent darkening of the pigmentation. Very fed up cos i paid 120 plus for a miserable 30ml !

    5. SKII signs treatment - Not bad result, but extremely expensive.

    Want to try the skin refining treatment cream and SKII lotion (moisturiser). Have anyone tried? Pls feedback.
