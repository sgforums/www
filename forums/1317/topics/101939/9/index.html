    The Doll Master

    There's no escape from The Doll Master!

    Writer/director Jeong Yong-gi has woven a captivating thriller that revolves around a terrible secret that began a century ago and has extended its influence to present day Korea. Five very different people find their way to a doll museum that holds a mystery that may be too terrible for any of them to survive ...

    Among the visitor's of the strange museum are the hard working photographer Jeong-gi (Im Hyeong-jun), Hae-mi (Kim Yu-mi), a sly sculptress and wild high school student Seon-yeong (Lee Ga-yeong). However, fate does not seem to hold anything good for any of the guests in store, as evil occurrences start to take their unstoppable turn ...
