  • tranquilice

    From Sunday 01 May 2005, movie ticket prices of Shaw, Eng Wah and Golden Village will be increased as follows:

    Monday - Wednesday

    $6.50 $7.00

    Thursday

    $7.50 $8.00

    Friday before 5pm, eve of public holiday before 5pm

    $7.50 $8.00

    Friday after 5pm, eve of public holiday after 5pm

    $8.50 $9.50

    Saturday, Sunday and public holiday

    $8.50 $9.50

    If eve of public holiday falls on Saturday or Sunday, the price will be $9.50 the whole day.

    Cathay cinemas (Orchard and Causeway Point) are excluded from the price increase, but there is plan to increase in the near future.
