Clementi to get proper library at long last

  • iveco
    31 Mar 05, 14:27
    Finally, Clementi folks will get a proper community library in 2010. This is part of the town centre upgrading that will also mean an end to getting wet while waiting for the bus at Clementi Interchange.

    After years of looking on in envy at Jurong West, Bukit Batok and Choa Chu Kang residents get their very own libraries and pondering the absurdity of the reasoning that Jurong East Regional Library (previously a community library) is big enough to serve the West, there is finally some reason to smile if you are a resident of Clementi (like myself). The CLECL is long overdue. Some stupid government official shot down this proposal when it was floated more than 10 years ago.

    Honestly, if Tampines Regional Library is large enough to cater to those in the East, why has the Bedok library not closed down and also why has another one opened in Paris Ris? The officials knew this and opened libraries all over the West except my beloved Clementi.

    What's next? It is rumoured that Serangoon town centre is next on the list for a revamp (due to Circle Line and such). Will the residents over there be clamouring for a new library or are they content with those at Ang Mo Kio and Hougang?
