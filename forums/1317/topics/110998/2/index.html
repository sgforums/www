    Shinta i am not sure which u are suffering from, but its advisable to see a specialist in NSC.If u are seuffring from Male Pattern Baldness where the hair loss slowly starts off the sides of your forehead and gradually to the top, its called Androgenetic Alopecia , in short layman terms is Male Pattern Baldness (MPB)..its usually caused by DHT in your body affecting hair follicles who are genetically preprogrammed to be sensitive to it...every guy produces DHT but it needs genetically programmed hair to trigger it...its usually inherited but u need to inherit the genes from yr ancestors first..some people who have parents with balding problem seems not to suffer from it cos they have not inherited it even though their parents and grandparents suffer from it..as u can see there a lot of factors involved...the extent of Balding, whether partial or full depends on these factors and are usually difficult to predict..if u are suffering from this, eating nutritional or vitamin pills wont help much.,.it needs treatment

    Well there 's hope for this kind of hair loss....its a pill called Propecia and its a proven treatment...its the most widely presribed drug for this problem and one of the two which is commonly used..the other is called dustaride which is more recent and is supposedly better but more costly....Both work in the same manner though..Note that Dustaride has not yet been approved for hasirloss bcause there s not much difference between propecia and dustaride to warrant its release on the market...
