    Today i was called.. an insecure person who wants a prestigious career to get a meaning of life. Some people's addictions is alcohol, some drugs, some cigarettes, some a combination of several. I like a high flying life? Why? Why is it an addiction? Because drugs, ciggies and (too much darn Abba on Eurovision) tv is an escapism. My high flying life allows me to escape reality. I don't face reality because it hurts. It's too much for me.. too harsh for me. Too much sorrow in mine. As far as I can remember, i used to talk to myself.. i used to look up to 'sucessful' and want to be like them. Power suits, skyscraper heels. Hhaha.. man.. charcoal, black, brown suits.. They are my favourite. Not having something inside you to look up to.. you look outside the square.

    You lose yourself in the meantime, i do not know what i do anymore. I do not know what i want. I want to be happy. I don't know what makes me happy. Hence i keep plugging along. Plugging along.
    Well.. that is sad.. but what is your vice? Legit ones.. chocolat (it is not a spelling mistake!), work.. legal drugs (bulbs comes to mind) or the frigging movies? Everyone needs their own escapism. Their own life to run to.. their own obliviation.. some sort of world where they do not have to deal with themselves.

    This is ... my vice.
