    EXTENSION OF PUBLIC TRANSPORT SERVICES ON
    CHRISTMAS' EVE AND NEW YEAR'S EVE

    SINGAPORE - Commuters can count on the MRT and LRT as well as selected SBS Transit and SMRT buses to be at their service beyond the usual operating hours on Christmas' Eve (Friday, 24 December 2004) and New Year's Eve (Friday, 31 December 2004).

    With the extension of service operating hours, revellers can rely on public transport to get around on the eves of both holidays.

    Transit Link Pte Ltd, on behalf of the public transport operators, is pleased to announce the following extended operating hours:

    TRAIN SERVICES

    SMRT Services
    The last Northbound (to Jurong East Station) and Southbound (to Marina Bay Station) trains will leave Orchard Station at 1.00am (on 25 December 2004 and 1 January 2005). Departure times for the last Eastbound (to Pasir Ris Station) and Westbound trains (to Boon Lay Station) will be adjusted correspondingly to ensure that passengers can transfer to connecting trains at City Hall and Raffles Place Interchanges.

    Train service to Expo and Changi Airport stations will not be extended. The last train timings are as follows:

    Bukit Panjang LRT Services
    The last Bukit Panjang LRT train will be extended to match the last SMRT train service at Choa Chu Kang on both Christmas and New Year's eves. Notices have been put up on the station notice boards to inform the passengers about the extended operating hours.

    North East Line Services
    Northbound: The last train will depart HarbourFront Station at 1.05am.
    Southbound: The last train will depart Punggol Station at 12.52am.
    Northbound towards Punggol Station and southbound towards HarbourFront Station: Both last trains will depart Dhoby Ghaut Station at 1.15am.

    Sengkang LRT Services
    The last Sengkang LRT train will be extended to depart Sengkang Town Centre Station on the East Inner Loop at 1.45am.

These Special Services will serve residents in the major housing estates from 12:45am onwards. Feeder fare applies and valid concession cards can be used. The current Nite Owl Services 65M and 174M will continue to operate as usual - on Fridays, Saturdays and eve of Public Holidays.

    Operating hours : 12 midnight - 4.00am (from city)
    Frequency : 20 minutes
    Fare : $2.50 (ez-link card) or $3.00 (cash)

    Concessionary travel (includes concession cardholders with valid Bus Concession Pass) and transfer rebates are not applicable.
