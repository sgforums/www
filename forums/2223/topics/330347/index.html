    It's Not Our Fault That We Fail Our Exams

    There are 52 Sundays in a year. You know Sundays are for students to rest.

    365 Days - 52 Days = 313 Days

    There are 70 days of School Vacation in a year (exclude Sundays / Public Holidays). School Vacations are for students to relax.
    313 Days - 70 Days = 243 Days 

     

     

     

    Students need 8~10 hours of sleep everyday. In a year, they sleep about 142 days.

    243 Days - 142 Days = 101 Days


    Two hour for daily playing. That means 30 days. 
    101 Days - 30 Days = 71 Days

     

     

     


    Students must be at least sick for 10 days.

    11 Days - 10 Days = 1 Day

    That 1 Day is the student’s birthday! How can he/she study on that day?

     


    Three Hours for daily eating. In a year, they take 45 days to eat. 
    71 Days - 45 Days = 26 Days

    Public Holidays takes up 15 days.

    26 Days - 15 Days = 11 Days
