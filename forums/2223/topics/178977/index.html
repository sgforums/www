where's God!

  • whereismyrib
    19 Feb 06, 03:00
    there were two brothers that were so bad their parents could not control them. one evening their mother suggested that maybe the pastor of their church could talk to the boys. the father agreed and called the pastor. the pastor told the father to send the boys one at a time the following day. the next day, the mother sent the youngest boy first to see the pastor. the boy walked down to the church wondering why the pastor wanting to talk to him. when the boy meet the pastor in his office the pastor just stared at him, after about a minute the pastor said, "where is God!" the boy got nervous and wondered what the pastor was talking about. then the pastor said, "where is God!!" the boy still did not answer so the pastor screamed " son, where is God!!!" at this time, the boy panicked and ran out of the church all the way home. he ran up to his brother's bedroom and locked the door. his brother asked him what was wrong. the younger boy then said, "we're in big trouble like never before." the older brother then said, "what did we do!" the younger brother then replied, "God is missing and they think we had something to do with it!"
