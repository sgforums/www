How to Take Elevator More Effectively

  • pierre^^
    9 Aug 07, 18:01
    Taking the elevator plays a big part of our lifes but sharing a space with a stranger inside an evelator is not exactly a very pleasent feeling. There is nothing to do except looking at the display counter.

    Here is one way to shave a few extra seconds off from the elevator ride.

    Upon entering the elevator, press the "close" button first before pressing the choosen floor.

    The floor selection will be made as the doors are closing, instead of choosing the floor before the door closes. This may not save ample of time but every seconds counts.

    12) Ask, "Did you feel that?"
