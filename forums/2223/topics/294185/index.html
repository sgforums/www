    Dear Ms. Pretty,
    I have read your post with great interest. Guess there are lots of girls out there who have similar questions like yours. Please

    for any clues to marry a rich guy. And by the way, you could make yourself to become a rich person with $500k annual income. This
    has better chance than finding a rich fool.
