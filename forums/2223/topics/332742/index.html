    Well so Tom and Harry are 2 12-year olds who went to see the doctor. Since Tom was ahead of Harry in the queue, he went into the doctor's room first. 10 minutes later, he came out crying.

    Harry asked Tom: "Hey, why are you crying? What went on in there?

    Tom, through his tears, replied: "Well I went for a blood test, and the doctor cut my finger.."

    Upon hearing that, Harry burst out crying even louder than Tom.

    Bewildered, Tom asked Harry why he started crying.

    Harry replied, through his tears, "Well, I'm going in for a urine test now..!"
