    John was an avid antique collector. One day, his friend Sam went to visit his house.

    "This is my Picasso painting collection centuries old." John introduced as they entered the hallway.

    "And this coin dates back 5000 years ago." John said as they passed a small glass case.

    Sam was really impressed with all the ancient collections. After a 30 minute tour around the house, John decided to treat Sam with wine and cake.

    "This is a 3000 year old english wine, please enjoy it" John said as he poured Sam a glass of top-notch wine.

    "Everything in this house is antique, and this wine is great. Pity the cake is fresh." Sam joked.

    John said "I bought that 2 years ago"
