     Guts or Balls?


     There is a medical distinction ... We've all heard about people
     having guts or balls, but do you really know the difference between them?
     In an effort to keep you informed, and to alleviate further confusion, the
     following definitions are listed below:


     GUTS - Is arriving home late after a night out with your guy friends, being met by
     your wife with a broom, and having the guts to ask:
     'Are you still cleaning, or are you flying somewhere?'

     BALLS - Is coming home late after a night out with your guy friends, smelling of
     perfume and beer, lipstick on your collar, slapping your  wife on her backside and  having the balls to say: 'You're next,  Chubby.'

     I hope this clears up any confusion on the definitions.

     Medically speaking, there is no difference in the outcome, since both
     ultimately result in death.
