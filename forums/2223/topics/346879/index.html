3 blondes

  • PassiveIncome1

    Three blondes who work in the same office noticed that their female boss
    has started leaving work early every day, so one day they decided that
    after she leaves, they'd take off early, too. After all, she never
    called or came back, so how was she to know?

    Fran is thrilled to get home early. She did a little gardening, watched
    a movie and then went to bed early. Maryann was elated to be able to
    get in a quick workout at her health club before meeting a dinner date.

    Linda was also very happy to be home early, but as she went upstairs
    she heard noises coming from her bedroom. She quietly opened the door a
    crack and was mortified to see her husband in bed with her Boss!

    Ever so gently, she closed the door and crept out of her house. The
    next day Fran and Maryann talk about leaving early again, but when they
    ask the Linda if she wanted to leave early also, she exclaimed, "NO
    WAY! Yesterday I almost got caught!"
