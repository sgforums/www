    In SG, there is a Fort Canning Tunnel with an ERP in front.

    To cope with this, she would need to direct the water to the Marina Barrage which is 'removable'. Sometimes it may overflow and that is a really gross thing....

    The T junction leads to 2 roads. Connaught Dr and St Andrews Rd.
    On this 10 days that the toll booth opens, a Malaysian Petrol Station (Which has Caltex With Techron) opens on 1 end of a road.

    On MY, there is a car factory called Old Ford Factory.
    Old Ford Factory produces ALOT of cars every single day.
    These cars are pumped with Diesel to prepare them for the long drive ahead.
    The cars line up in Johor Bahru and it is waiting to cross the Causeway.

    The customs at Johor Bahru opened and all the cars fly through the causeway into the Fort Canning Tunnel. Many cars run out of fuel and died in the tunnel while most of them were killed by the Police of SG in the tunnel.
    Only a few hundred cars make it to the Padang.
    They then split, some went to Connaught Dr while the rest went to St Andrews Rd.
    This time, the petrol station was at St Andrews Rd.
    The cars on Connaught Dr eventually die and some cars died at St Andrews Rd.
    Eventually one lone car found the petrol station and pumped the fuel inside.
    The car then moved to the Padang where it stayed there for 9 months....
