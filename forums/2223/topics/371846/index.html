    They started it.

    1. Call.
    2. Don't lie.
    3. Two words: clean socks.
    4. You're wrong.
    5. You're sorry.
    6. Burping is not sexy.
    7. Think boxers.
    8. Silk boxers.
    9. Shave - no matter how cool you think it looks, we hate it.
    10. Never tape any of her body parts together.
    11. If guys' night out is going to be fun, invite the girls.
    12. The correct answer to "Do I look fat?" is never, ever "Yes."
    13. None of your ex-girlfriends were ever nicer, prettier, or better in bed.
    14. Ditto for "Is she prettier than me?"
    15. A grunt is seldom an acceptable answer to any question.
    16. Answering "Who was that on the phone?" with "Nobody" is never going to end that conversation.
    17. Ditto for "Whose lipstick is this?"
    18. Buying her dinner does not equal foreplay.
    19. Don't assume PMS is the cause for every bad mood.
    20. You don't have PMS. Don't act like you know what it's like.
    21. Don't try to change the way she dresses.
    22. Her haircut is never bad.
    23. She is a natural born drama queen.
    24. No means No. Yes means Yes. Silence could mean anything she feels like at that particular moment in time, and it could change without notice.
    25. Remember Valentine's Day, and any cheesy "anniversary" she so-names.
    26. Never let her walk anywhere alone after 11pm.
    27. Don't tell her you love her if you don't.
    28. Tell her you love her if you do. Often.
    29. If you want to break up with her, break up with her. Don't act like a complete jerk until she does it for you.
    30. The rules are never fair. Accept this without question.
