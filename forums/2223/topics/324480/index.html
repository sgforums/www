Tourist Attractions

  • MS
    17 Jul 08, 16:29

    A Singaporean Tourist travelled to his neighbouring countries on a tour. When he got off the flight at a Philippines air port, he was ushered into a cab at the air port. While on the way to the hotel, he saw many beggars on the streets who dug into rubbish bin for food. He also saw many little children rushing to knock onto the doors of every car to beg the passengers to buy something from them.

     

    The Singaporean tourist quickly snapped down all these sights on his camera. Unable to withhold his curiosity, the Filipino cabbie asked, "Sir, why are you taking photos of the beggars? They are all over the streets, dirty and unwanted. Nothing special about them."

     

    The Singaporean tourist replied, "But these are tourist attractions to me! Back in Singapore, it is very difficult to find a beggar. And I want to find a real beggar. So this is how a real beggar looks like. I've never seen one like that in Singapore. Beggars in Singapore are not really authentic."
