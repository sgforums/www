    In the final round of the Miss Universe Pageant, the 3 finalists namely Miss USA, Miss Malaysia & Miss Singapore are being asked 3 simple questions:

    MC: the 1st question is name me an electrical appliance starting with 'L'.

    Miss USA: Lamp, Miss Malaysia: Light bulb, Miss Singapore: Ladio.  Judge: No no!  Radio does start with the letter 'L'.

    MC: The next question is name me an animal starting with the letter 'L' - Miss USA: Lion, Miss Malaysia: Leopard, Miss Singapore: Labbit.  Judge: No no no!

    MC: Your next chance - name a famous car that starts with 'L' - Miss USA: Lexus, Miss Malaysia: Lamborghini, Miss Singapore: Lolls Loyce.  Judge: Oh my God!.

    MC: Next question, name me a fruit starting with the letter 'L' - USA: Lemon, Malaysia: Lychee, Singapore: Liew Lian.

    The Judge consulted the board of judges to determine if Miss Singapore should really be disqualifed & they decided that since Miss Singapore was having so much problem with the letter 'L', they decided to give her another chance.

    Judge: Ok the final question: name me a human anatomy starting with the letter 'L' - Miss USA: Lung (applause), Miss Malaysia: Liver (even more applause), Miss Singapore: Lan Jiao!!
