When I was your age......

  • SBS3625D

    A man was playing golf when the ball hit a tall tree and rolled to a halt at the base of the tree.

    He tried to whack the ball over the tree, but failed and the ball rolled back.

    An old man walked over and said: When I was your age, I could have easily whacked the ball over this tree.

    The man took it as a challenge and tried.......and failed again.

    Then the old man said: Of course when I was your age, the tree was only half-a-metre tall.
