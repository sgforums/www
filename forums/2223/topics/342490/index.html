    Ah Beng,Ah Seng and Ah Meng were construction workers building a skyscraper.One afternoon they break for lunch.Ah Beng opens his lunchbox to see Char Kway Teow in it.

    "Wah lau eh!Everyday eat Char Kway Teow eat until sian liao.If tomorrow i kenna Char Kway Teow again i will jump of the building."

    Then Ah Seng opens his lunch box to find Chicken Rice in it.

    ""Wah lau eh!Everyday eat Chicken Rice eat until sian liao.If tomorrow i kenna Chicken Rice again i will jump of the building."

    Then Ah Meng opens his lunch box to discover a couple of Bread with Peanut Butter.

    "Wah lau eh!Everyday eat Bread eat until sian liao.If tomorrow i kenna Bread again i will jump of the building."

    Then the next afternoon,they break again for lunch.

    Ah Beng opened his lunchbox to find Char Kway Teow and true to his words jumped off the building.

    Ah Seng opened his lunchbox to find Chicken Rice and too killed himself by leaping off the building.

    Ah Meng opened his lunchbox to find the same bread with peanut butter and commited suicide in the same fashion as the first 2.

    The next day,their wives arrived at the place where their bodies were found to pay their respects.

    On reaching,Ah Beng's Wife cried on the spot :"Ah Beng ar!If i knew you didnt like Char Kway Teow,i would not have made it everyday for you!"

    Watching Ah Beng's Wife sorrow brought tears to Ah Seng's wife too.

    Ah Seng's Wife:"Ah Seng ar!If i knew you didnt like Chicken Rice,i would not have made it everyday for you!"

    When the 2 crying woman turned to look at Ah Meng's wife,they found out that she was not crying and Ah Meng's wife said to them :"Hey,dont look at me.He makes his own lunch."
