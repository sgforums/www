    One day Little Pauly's teacher, decided to play a spelling game. She
    gave a letter of the alphabet and the kids have to spell a word
    starting with that letter, and then use the same word in a sentence. Starting with
    "A" Little Pauly's hand was continually in the air, but the teacher
    ignored him. Little Pauly always made lewd remarks and could
    turn the simplest of statements into sexual statements, so the teacher
    was afraid to let Pauly use any letter that he could turn into an
    obscene remark.

    "All right now, Susan, you first?" said the teacher. "A is for Ape, A-P-
    E, An ape likes bananas" answered Susan. "Excellent " said the teacher.
    She continues on through the alphabet. Finally she reaches F. Now she
    will NOT let Little Pauly answer this under any circumstances so she
    asks Mary. "F is for Fairy F-A-I-R-Y, a FAIRY is a little girl who lives
    among the flowers", Mary replies.

    "Great," says the teacher. "Now we get to G." Only Little Pauly has his
    hand up so the teacher thinks about this and decides "G" is a safe one.

    "Yes Pauly?" She asks. "G is for Gnome G-N-O-M-E. A Gnome lives among
    the flowers too."

    "Pauly! That's Excellent!" Exclaims the teacher, very happy that for
    once he wasn't out of line.

    Little Pauly grins broadly and goes on to say, "Yes, teacher, he's the
    one who fucks the fairies."
