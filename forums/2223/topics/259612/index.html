The 3 good friends

    There were 3 good friends... They were stranded on an island...
    They wanted to get out from that island...
    So everyday, they tried building fire hoping that they would get rescued...
    But nobody went to rescue them...
    One day, a genie pop-ed out in front of them and said that she would grant what ever wish they wished for...
    The first guy, who is poor and has a family at home...
    He wished that he could return home with lots of money...
    *POOF* He returned home with lots of money... Starting to enjoy his life...
    The second guy, who is quite rich, has a girlfriend...
    He wished that he could return home and marry his girlfriend...
    *POOF* He returned and they got married at a church...
    The third guy, who is poor, no girlfriend, no family member.. The only friends he had was those 2 guys...
    So he wished that those 2 guys would be back on this island with him...
    And *POOF* everything those 2 guys had was gone in just a few minutes time...
