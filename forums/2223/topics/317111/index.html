    I became confused when I heard these terms which reference the word "service".

    Internal Revenue "Service", U.S. Postal "Service", Telephone "Service", T.V. "Service", Civil "Service", City & County Public "Service", Customer "Service" and "Service" Stations.

    This is not what I thought "service" meant.

    THEN last weekend, I overheard two ranchers talking after church, and one of them said he had hired a bull to "service" a few cows.

    BAM!!!

    It all came into perspective. I now understand what all those "service" agencies are doing to us.

    I hope you are as enlightened as I am.
