    A father walks into a toy shop looking for a toy to buy for her daughter's birthday.

    He asks the salesgirl how much does a Barbie doll cost.

    The salesgirl replied, "That depends, Bikini Barbie is $69, Schoolgirl Barbie is $59, Office Lady Barbie is $55 and Divorced Barbie $1290."

    "Why is the Divorced Barbie so expensive as compared to the rest?", asked the father.

    The salesgirl replied, "That's because Sir, Divorced Barbie comes with Ken's house, Ken's car, Ken's computer and all of Ken's friends."
