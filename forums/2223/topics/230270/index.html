    Two drug convicts were on death row but the judge decided to give them another chance, "I will drop all charges if you can persuade enough people to give up drugs over the weekend!"

    On monday, the two men faced the judge again. The first man managed to persuade 10 people to give up drugs.

    Judge: "Very good, how you managed to do that?"

    1st man: "Well, I drew two circles, one bigger, one smaller. I told them the bigger one is their brain before drugs and the smaller one is their brain after drugs."

    The second man said, "I managed to persuade 100 people to give up drugs!"

    Judge: "Oh My God, Excelent, How you managed to do that?"

    The second man: "Well, I drew the same circles and I told them that the smaller circle is their a$$ hole before prison..."
