    Don't worry, for worry is the most unproductive of all human
    activities.

    Don't be fearful, for most of the things we fear never come to
    pass.

    Don't carry grudges, for they are the heaviest of all life's
    burdens.

    Face each problem as it comes. You can only handle one at a time
    anyway.

    Don't take problems to bed with you, for they make very poor
    bedfellows.

    Don't borrow other people's problems. They can better care for
    them than you.

    Don't try to relive yesterday for good or ill, it is forever gone.
    Concentrate on what is happening in your life and be happy now!

    Be a good listener, for only when you listen do you hear ideas
    different from your own.

    Don't become bogged down by frustration, for 90% of it is rooted
    in self-pity and will only interfere with positive action.

    Count your blessings, never overlooking the small ones, for a lot
    of small blessings add up to a big one.
