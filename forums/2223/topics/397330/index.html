    • "The Sleeper has awakened."
    • "My path is clear."
    • "I'm awake... i'm awake."

    • "Ah, the great outdoors!"
    • "Making Trails"

    • "Fear the fearsome fury of the forest fawn!"
    • "Fall! Like leaves… in fall!"
