Courage

    Tom had a English Composition test one day.

    The title was 'Courage'.

    He thought for 5 minutes and could not decide on what to write on.

    For the next hour, he saw many of his friends finishing off their essays and began panicking.

    Suddenly, he knew what he was going to write.

    He wrote for 10seconds and putted down his pen, with a large grin.

     

    After the test, Tom's friend, Ali, who sat behind him in the test asked curiously.

    'Tom, i saw that you didn't start writing till the last few seconds, but your still smiling, why?'

    Tom replied ' I wrote something which was without a doubt linked with courage and i am confident of getting high marks '.

    Ali looked at him and asked, ' What did you write '?

    Tom said ' This is courage'.
