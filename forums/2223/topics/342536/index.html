    Once, Lee Hsien Loong received a delegation of workers from NTUC.

    When the workers left, LHL looked around for his sunglasses but did not see it. He
    called Lim Swee Say and said, "Swee Say, my sunglasses disappeared after the visit of those workers."

    "Yes, Boss!, I'll immediately take proper measures."

    Ten minutes later, LHL pulled out a drawer in his desk and saw his sunglasses neatly tucked away inside the drawer.

    He let out a sigh, and dialed Swee Say's number.

    "Listen... Swee Say, my sunglasses has been found."

    "What a pity," Swee Say said. "All of them have already confessed."
