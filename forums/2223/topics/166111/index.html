Yet another Genie joke

  • Sleeping Forest
    30 Nov 05, 03:22
    There were 4 guys John, Franky, Harry and Tom who found a small bottle. When they rubbed the bottle, a genie appeared. Thankful that they had released him , the genie said, "Infront of you all are four swimming pools, I will give each of you a wish. When you run towards the pool and jump, you should shout what you want the pool of water to become, and then your wish will come true."

    John ran towards the pool, jumped and shouted "Wine". The pool immediately turned into a pool of wine. John was ecstatic. Next came Franky. He did the same and shouted, "Vodka" and immersed himself into a pool of vodka. Harry ran and shouted, "Beer" and ditto, he was swimming in beer. The last of them was Tom. He was running towards the pool when suddenly he stepped on a banana peel. He slipped towards the pool while shouting "Shi tt tttt!!!!!!!" ......
