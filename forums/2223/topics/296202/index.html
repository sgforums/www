    A little girl was watching her mum boil chicken. She was told that this was an ancient family recipe and she should watch carefully.

    As the little girl watched, she noticed her mum cut off the chicken's drumstick and wings. Thus, she questioned her mum.

    Her mum looked at the excited little girl's face and thought for a moment.
    Finally she gave a wise look to the little girl and said, "I don't know".

    Her mum went to the little girl's grandmother, whom she learnt the chicken recipe from, and asked the same question. The little girl's grandmother said, "I have no idea."

    Months later, they stumbled accross an old photo and found out the answer of the question.

    This is what the picture is.

    The little girl's great grandmother, who taught the grandmother the recipe, was trying to sqeeze the chicken into a tiny pot, with its wings and drumsitck sticking out.
