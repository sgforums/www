The English girl

    A woman goes to England to attend a 2-week, company training session.
    Her husband drives her to the airport and wishes her to have a good trip.
    The wife answers: "Thank you honey, what would you like me to bring for you?"
    The husband laughs and says: "An English girl!" The woman kept quiet
    and left. Two weeks later he picks her up in the airport and asks:
    "So,honey,how was the trip?"
    "Very good, thank you"
    "And, what happened to my present?"
    "Which present?"
    "what I asked for.... the English girl?!"
    "Oh, that? Well, I did what I could, now we have to wait a few months
    to see if it is a girl!!!"
