    A girl was left alone at her ranch house one day when her neighbour showed up at the door.

    Neighbour: Where's your father, I want to see him, quick.

    Girl: He's out. You want our strongest bull to service your cow? My father charges $150 for that.

    Neighbour (agitated): Just get me your father!

    Girl: Is it too expensive? We have a veteran bull here and my dad charges $50 to service your cow.

    Neighbour: I don't care! I want to see your father! Your brother made my daughter pregnant!

    The girl faltered.

    Girl: I don't know how much my father charges for that one....
