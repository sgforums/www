    One day, Mr Chee was going to work. He saw some dumplings on sale and he bought it for his colleagues and boss. When he reached his office, all his colleagues were busy asking him what did he buy for them.

    Mr Chee said, "I bought it for you guys and Boss". 

    Mr Chee went to the pantry to have a coffee. At the same time, his boss came.

    Boss : Who bought these dumplings?

    Colleagues : Mr Chee Buy Boss! (Mr Jibai Boss)

    Boss : Why Mr Lum Never Eat?

    Colleagues : Mr Lum Pa. (Pa = Scared in Chinese, Mr Lampa)  

    Boss : Who Pay?

    Colleagues : Mrs Kao Pay. (Mrs Kaopei)

    Boss : Who is Booing?

    Colleagues : Ms Kao Boo. (Ms Kaobu)
