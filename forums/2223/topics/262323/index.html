Monk and a Room

  • sand king
    20 Jun 07, 20:09
    There was once a traveler, he traveled to the top of a mountain to visit a monastery.

    When he was at the monastery, he studied every detail of it, wowing at how it looks and how it was built.

    He walk through the hallways till he saw a door, at the conner of the building.
    Being curious he walked up to it, then knocked on it 3 times. No one responded.

    Suddenly the head monk tapped the man on his back. He got a fright!

    After calming down, he asked the head monk what was behind the door.
    The head monk replayed: I cannot tell you, but the only way you can find out is to become one of us monks.

    The traveler thought of it, then told the head monk to let him stay alittle while longer so that he could think.

    Few weeks past, the man told the head monk: i want to join.

    The monk said: you have to go tru a though training.

    *Few years past*

    The head monk died, before he died, he handed a key to the man. The key to the door he said with his last ounce of breath.

    The man, dressed in brown monk's robe, walked up to the mysterious door.
    He fitted the key into the key hole then twist it. The door creeked open.

    He walked in, closing the door behind him.
    In that room, there was another door, he opened it.

    Then he stepped into the next room....

    *guess what he saw in the next room?*

    Cant tell you! You got to be a monk first!
