    A Major Discovery in sgforum.

    I found that u can make clickable images here.

    Example:

     <---clickable image

    just surround your image by the url tag.

    it is like this:

    [ url= url of the picture ] [ img] url of the image [/img] [/url]

    So for those who picture is too big, can use a small version first and then click on it to get the larger version.
