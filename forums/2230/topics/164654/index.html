    ONLY FOR SIT STUDENTS!!!!

    Date: 8 February 2006

    Time: 4pm-6pm

    Venue: Wireless Lab, Belvedere B Level 3

    Activity 1:

    ========

    The objective of this activity is to introduce students theoretically to wireless ad hoc networks, the inherent properties, and challenges faced in popularizing ad hoc networks in the computing context. The format will be a mini-tutorial, after which participants will be required to do a short quiz.

    1 CE DA point.

    Activity 2:

    ========

    Participants will be grouped into teams, and each team will compete to set up an ad hoc network connecting 3 computers in a mesh configuration. Participants will have to provide proof that their network works, e.g. by showing file sharing/transfer abilities, etc.

    1 CE DA point for completion of task.

    Activity 3:

    ========

    Participants will be grouped into teams, and each team will compete to enhance the capability of their ad hoc network (set up in activity 2) to perform internet sharing, where 1 computer will act as a wireless gateway connecting a local LAN with a WAN uplink into a hypothetical ISP. A mesh configuration must still be maintained.

    1 CE DA point for completion of task.

    The team who completed activity 2 and 3 the fastest will be awarded 1 extra DA point.

    If you are interested, please register to emanuel_yudanto@rp.sg or richard_gwee@rp.sg by February 1st.
