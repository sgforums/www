    well it's just a term... prosumer is in between consumer and professional.

    consumer is more a point and shoot... auto everything. professional cams are mainly SLRs and have full manual functions and millions of settings. prosumers are in between, usually fixed lens (not SLR) but with the flexibility of manual settings for aperture and shutter speeds and other things like white balance and exposure compensation.
