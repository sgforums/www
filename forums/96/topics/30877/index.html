    A dedication to all the netizens. Isn't this what goes on in your mind...when you meet a special someone online? Let's bathe in it's glamour.

    Cyber Imagination

    Deep in the tunnels of my mind,
    images abound,
    Silky Hair and Sparkling eyes,
    tempt me like the devil.
    Was that pair of eyes yours?
    I sure hope it is,
    for none charmed me like it did.

    Words flow like streams of clouds,
    into my mind they went.
    fogging my thoughts no end.
    A vision is finally form,
    and how beautiful you look.

    I pondered, if this was you,
    but why the heck I care?
    This is how I want you,
    and how you will to me.

    Till we meet shall it perish,
    or maybe it will stay.
    "Nonetheless" I told myself,
    you were beautiful this way.

    Inspired by a chinese pharse that I have seen, thereafter....I extended it into 5 paragraphs

    To know you...

    The Greatest distance in this world,
    is not the distance that spans across the universe,
    nor is it the distances that separates life and death.
    it is when I am standing right in front of you,
    but I do not dare to tell you I love you.

    The greastest yearning in the world,
    is not the yearn to be the among the brightest stars,
    nor is it the yearn to be the undying immortal,
    it is when I am thinking of you,
    but can be nowhere near you.

    The greatest pride in this world
    is not the pride of landing on the moon,
    nor is it the pride of being a parent,
    it is when I have nothing,
    but I see you all around me..

    The greatest joy in this world,
    is not the joy of owning the unpossesable,
    nor is it the joy of feeling alive,
    it is when I am born into this world,,
    when you would be too.

    You must be the northstar in the sky,
    shining ever bright,
    for that is the only star I see.
    Guiding me in my life,
    the silver trail you leave behind,
    leads me on my journey.

    A poem describing the journey one experience in a truly fulfilling and beautiful relationship...though not all relationship moves ideally...that's why this is very much a dream isn't it?

    Fate

    The day I first saw light,
    I knew someone was there.
    One who makes me whole,
    One who fulfilled my life.

    The moment I knew it was you,
    My destiny was found.
    My life thus have a purpose,
    to find you, now my mission.

    We were once united,
    but now physically divided.
    With fate as my compass,
    I sought our union.

    When fate brought me to you,
    I found it could only do so much,
    for you were held captive,
    and a compass don't help much.

    Thus I use my key,
    for I knew it to open all.
    One so compassionate,
    this key I call love.

    With love I found your freedom,
    but you were alone and bare.
    Thus I became your armor,
    and we are whole once more.

    I thank fate for finding thee,
    but my love did just as much.
    And with me as your armor,
    Your heart will hurt no more.
