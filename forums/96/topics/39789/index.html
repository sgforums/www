    For some people, it comes naturally. If you purposely try to keep a conversation going, most of the time the mood is very artificial.

    Just relax and let it be a easy and pleasant conversation. Slowly gather any common topics, interests, or viewpoints that the two of you may share. Expand a bit on it. But try not to 'out-talk' the other person or bore her with your own life experiences. Most people like being listened to more than being the listener.
