    The Straits Times - 4 Mar 08

     

    A Chinese national who videotaped a woman in the shower with his mobile phone was jailed for two weeks on 3 Mar 08.

     

    Jin Dong Long, 20, an electrical and electronic component assembler, admitted to insulting the modesty of the 25-year-old woman in the kitchen toilet of a flat in Simei on 14 Feb 08.

     

    A court heard that the woman noticed the mobile phone stuck on the toilet wall after she had a shower that evening.

     

    The phone was on video mode.  She found a clip of herself in the process of showering.

     

    Pleading for leniency, Jin asked the court to give him a chance and said he wished to continue working here.

     

    He could have been jailed for up to one year or fined or both
