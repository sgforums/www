    Dear Maggie,

    It was with poignant sorrow as I hugged you for the first and possibly the last time.I looked forlonly as you walked away...I had so many things to tell you,but Fate did not give me the chance to...Why must it always be like this?

    I want to take this opportunity to share with you what I wanted to tell you this morning.

    Maggie,you were the reason why my stint as an exchange student here was so memorable.I had some of the happiest moments in my life while with you.Whenever I was with you,I had this very nice feeling inside.It was such a heart warming feeling.It was like having something to look forward to everyday.Life seemed to be so Beautiful when you were by my side.

    Maggie,remember that time when we went to High Park which happened to be my second last day in Canada? We had such a great time negotiating the fallen logs.How about the frozen little pond that we came across? You asked whether it was frozen,so I threw the empty film container into it...and it bounced right off! After that,we went to the beach and there was where the fun really began!We became children again and we had such a wonderful time at the playground with the see saw,the slide and the swing.It was arguably the happiest moment of my Life.

    Maggie,now I want to apologize to you for what happened thereafterat MacDonald's.I was too emotional then,I know I should not had done that.Sorry,ok?

    Maggie,Now I am looking at the photographs of us which were taken during your birthday and while we were at High Park.Looking at them gives me a bittersweet feeling.On the negative side,I am sad that seeing you again is uncertain.On a positive note,I am glad that Fate brought you into my Life,albeit for an extremely short duration of less than three months...

    I vehemently hope that Fate will let our Paths in Life cross again.

    Warmest Regards,
    Bennie
