    This is a tactic which I'm practising now for perfection sake.

    Firstly, get in eye contact with the pretty gal.
    Make sure she really leng (under the dark surrounding, clouding visiblity).
    Look at her to let her know u're actually looking at her, but don't stare.

    Note that many times pretty gal will be/dance with an ugly gal. (maybe in a group)

    Secondly, approach the UGLY gal first. This might set the pretty gal thinking towards 'why this guy nvr approach me?'. Then, DANCE along n try to have fun, MORE with the UGLY gal. This might make the pretty gal even more confused.

    After some time, try to grab opportunities to communicate with them. (eg at songs interchange, sitting down, drinking along). Soon enough, TALK less and less with the ugly gal n start to give the pretty gal attention.

    What this technique aims is to use the ugly gal as the bridge to the pretty gal. (mostly, the objective is to get the pretty gal's phone no)

    So far, I tried the high level style; that is making the pretty gal telling me her phone no herself. But, = unsuccessful.
    Most probably my too young looking face, but handsome.

    The high level style of getting girl's phone no, IMO, has to be done in long conversations. Pubs are not a good place to use this technique i guess.

    But u'll never know. I once got a girl noticing me(when i'm dancing, n i had not looked at her yet), n I gave my eyes' attention to her. The next thing i know, when i popped up my mobile phone towards her, the 1st word she said 2 me was her phone no! She is pretty though.

    Most probably, in pubs, ya have to get the phone no by you asking her.

    Other players out there, share a technique?
