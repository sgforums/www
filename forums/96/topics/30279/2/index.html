    some more info from disneyland website

    When is the best time to visit the Disneyland Resort?
    A: Selecting the "best" time to visit the Disneyland Resort depends upon each individual's interests. If you are looking to visit when the attendance will be fairly low, you will want to plan your stay during the mid-week (Tuesdays-Thursdays) during:

    mid September - mid November
    January - mid March
    mid April - mid May

    However, during these times of year, the Theme Parks typically have reduced operating hours and entertainment events, and attractions closed for refurbishment. Although the hours and entertainment schedules vary during these times of year, the attendance is low and Guests are able to experience more attractions as opposed to holiday/summer periods.

    If you are interested in visiting while the Park has extended hours, entertainment events and all attractions operating, you can plan your stay for selected weekends (Saturdays/Sundays), extended holiday weekends, Christmas and Spring Break, or the summer months June-August. Please be aware however that attendance is usually increased during these time frames.
