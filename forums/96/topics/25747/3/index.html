    nonexistence writes "Have you ever noticed how many people allow their relationships to go stale and fall into a rut? Once you are there, it can be very difficult to break out. Listed below are 5 things you can do to avoid the rut, and keep the romance alive in your relationship.

    1. Express Your Love
    Always tell your partner that you love them. Saying, "I love you" is not just about saying the words, it is also about the way you say it. Be creative and remember that actions speak louder than words.

    2.Communicate
    One of the most talked about areas of a relationship is communication. The point, however, cannot be stressed too strongly: "Lack of communication kills relationships." So, it is extremely important to spend at least a portion of each day talking to your partner. It really doesn't even matter too much what you talk about, as long as you talk about something. Soon you will find that you have a lot to say.

    3.Be True To Your Relationship
    This one may seem a little strange, as the benefits are not immediately obvious. When you do something wrong against your partner, it doesn't matter if they ever find out about it. The fact that you did something changes the way you feel about your partner and creates a distance between the two of you. Keep doing things against your partner, and you will soon find yourself in a downward spiral.

    4.Don't Be Too Judgmental Of Your Partner
    On the reverse side to the above, realize that everybody makes mistakes. If your partner has done something wrong, sit down calmly and see if it is something you can work out. Too often we are too quick to condemn every wrongdoing on gut reaction alone. That gut reaction could lead to many regrets later on.

    5.Add A Little Romance Everyday
    Being romantic is not that difficult and can take many forms. It is very important that you at least perform one act of romance everyday. It does not even have to be spontaneous or incredibly creative. It just has to be romantic.
