  • Chief

    sgGirls.com aims to be a clean site. That is why we have a decency system and we choose to relegate photos that are not suitable for children away from the main content of the site.

    The purpose of the decency ratings system is so that girls who post their own photos up will not have their photos shown next to a bikini clad model in a seductive pose. As we would rather err on the safe side, as long as ONE person profiles a photo as 'R' it would be relegated to the 'R' section of the site.

    Recently the moderators and some users have brought to my attention people rating perfectly decent pictures 'R'. This defeats the purpose of the decency system in the first place because these girls' photos appear next to even more 'R' photos.

    I have since tweaked the system to allow fairer classification of photos. Also, moderators will bring to my attention any user who abuses the system and I will ban him and remove all his profiles (at the click of a button no more ).

    Let's hope this works out!
