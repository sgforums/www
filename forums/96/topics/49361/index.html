    Hi bus,

    Analysis
    When your fish keeps going to the surface for air, this usually means that it is being affected by gill disease. When a fish gets affected by gill disease, it is unable to gather oxygen from the water, which explains why it has to keep going to the surface for air.

    Unecessary Action
    When you add yellow/blackwater to the water, it only makes the water 'softer', and does not help solve gill disease. In Singapore, yellow/blackwater is pretty much unecessary because Singapore's water is already 'soft' enough.

    Solution
    What you should have done is:
    - Don't change water
    - Put one/two drops of PP Medicine (Potassium Permaganate, or otherwise known as a medicine for gill disease) daily *edit: available at Rainbow and probably other aquarium shops
    - Change 10% of water daily
    - Keep doing the above until the fish's condition gets better

    Hope this helps if your other fishes have the same problem again![/b]
