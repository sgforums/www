    Sorry people amendments here :
    Pls Bring :
    1)footwear - Slippers prefered here but due to dinner & other activities that might follow u can bring your shoes along. Or u wear your shoes & bring slippers along. Or if u find slippers troublesome then forget it, coz u dont need it in the game..but u will have trouble walking round the beach

    2)suntan lotion - This's important coz it's in the bright afternoon. You wouldnt want to fall sick or get sunburn.

    3)water bottle - can always refill at the tap there

    4)sun glasses - protect your eyes but not compulsory

    5)attire - For guys best is T-shirt & bermuda shorts. For girls i'm not too sure. Can wear t shirt & shorts( but shorter la), or bikini ?.. ofcoz the lesser the better la

    6)extra clothing, undergarment, towel & shampoo - those staying for dinner & not going off straight after game can have a shower there

    7)food - some sandwiches is enough to last you the whole afternoon( it's not too late to buy it at Tiong Bahru)

    Hope that the above points are complete..
