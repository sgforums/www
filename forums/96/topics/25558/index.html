Coming Soon: Decency Feature!

  • Chief
    30 Aug 02, 00:30
    Girls have been complaining that having their photos up on this site is not good for their reputation because of all the 'indecent' photos like japanese upskirt shots being contributed to the website.

    One solution is to ban 'indecent' photos but then again these photos do not contain pornography and it is very hard to enforce the ruling because 'decency' is very subjective, people will be complaining 'how come that picture can but my one cannot?'.

    So I decided to implement the 'decency' feature. If you 'profile' a photo now, there is a new 'decency' option. There are 2 selections, G for all ages and R for not so decent photos.

    Soon, the G and R photos will be seperated and visitors will not be able to see the G and R rated photos together. They will have to click on a different page to see the 'R' photos. This will ensure that 'decent' shots never appear next to a 'not so decent' one.

    I'm sure many of our female contributors will appreciate this feature.
