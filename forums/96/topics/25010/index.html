[b]Are You A Romantic?[/b]

    Do you consider yourself a romantic? Why? What characteristics do you feel makes you that way?

    Chances are that it was quite awhile ago or even never. To be romantic, or be considered a romantic, you have to ACT like a romantic. You know the things to do. What is your idea of a perfect date? Most of us sit around and complain that their partner isn't romantic or understanding of their needs and desires. Let me ask you this. When was the last time you did something like that for them?

    Some of us have gotten so caught up in our lives that we forget what it's like to be a romantic. We have this notion of what it is... but because we are so caught up in the acting out of our lives we forget how do to it. Or even how much we enjoy being romantic! Do you find yourself longing for something more meaningful in your life? It could be your just not acting on what you feel. The solution is do and be who you want to be. Don't sit around and talk about it... be it!
