    Originally posted by Ah^ken:
    Mi gtg to start my practical lessons for car next tues, a bit nervous.. how does it feel like to drive the car for the 1st time??
    Seeing that in are in the school, your 'first time' will prolly be more dull. You will be brought to an area of the circuit marked specially for first-timers and from there you will be explained by the instructor some technical details of the car, and taught to 'feel-press' the accelerator pedal and sense the 'biting point' of the clutch before practising moving foward and back using that technique. Once the instructor feels u are proficient enough, u will then proceed to do numerous rounds around the circuit where u can practice your changing of the first 2 gears and controlling your speed around bends. That basically summarises what u will experience in your first lesson.
