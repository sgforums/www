    In relationships, I guess ...

    RESPECT would mean respecting the other person's privacy and respecting the other person's right to spend time alone or with his/her own family and friends;

    TRUST would mean trusting that the other person would be faithful, trusting that the other person would not do anything to hurt yourself, and trusting that the other person would be frank and open to you.
