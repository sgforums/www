    *pokes guy* haha, here's an old one since i haven't written anything new

    A cold wind stirs the eternal remains
    Of a world once warm with love and grace
    Amidst this world of hurts and pains
    A new life comes, a pure white rose

    A message of hope, this life brings
    A song of love, this soul sings
    A loan from God, an angel from heaven
    A second chance, to make things even

    From such beauty, the darkness flees
    Seeking refuge in the dark valleys
    For this rose, the gry clouds part
    Returning to this world, it's fiery heart.

    Throbbing life, creation awakes
    To a world of warmth, hope and light
    No longer rule the dark Forsakens
    Gone are the days of the eternal night

    A distant rumble shakes the earth
    A silent roar trembles the heavens above
    The mighty mountains sadly bend their knee
    As darkness rejoices in silent glee

    For the end of the age of light's at hand
    As the load from God, the heaven sent
    Returns to him on angel's wings
    A song, for her, the heavenly host sings

    Heaven's gate open to receive this soul
    That has made this world, for an instant, whole
    Out of the valleys, the shadows peek
    Scorning creation as it weeps

    The black clouds pierce this world's heart
    The returning darkness shreds creation apart
    Upon the Voice of the ancient winds
    The name of this rose forever rings

    Walking down the corridor,

    Sometimes I smile and laugh,
    Hearing happiness of the past.

    Reminising to those who hear,
    Embarking on journey of memoirs.
    Here I am riding alone,
    Enduring the cold bitter night,
    Racing through the cars and lights,
    Embracing what future lies...

    >instead of assuming you would KNOW I do.
