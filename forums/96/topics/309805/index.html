    The Straits Times - 4 Mar 08

     

    A young man paid S$850 to have sex with a 15-year old girl he got to know on the Internet.

     

    On 3 Mar 08, Chen Wei Jian, 22, was poorer by S$7,000 – the amount he was fined after pleading guilty to having sex with a girl under the age of 16 last May.

     

    A district court heard that sometime in the beginning of May, the two were surfing the Internet when they chatted on MSN and exchanged phone numbers.

     

    About two weeks later, she sent him an SMS message.  In a subsequent phone call, she revealed her age and the school she was studying in.

     

    Chen, then 21, told her he was 20 and was working in a logistic company.

     

    Later that month, he invited her to his home to have sex after agreeing to her request to pay her S$850.

     

    The girl went to Chen’s home and they had consensual sex in a bedroom.  He paid her the money.

     

    The crime came to light only when the girl quarrelled with her boyfriend on 21 May and told him she was seeing Chen and that he had raped her.

     

    The boyfriend them told the girls mother and they called Chen to meet them.

     

    When Chen arrived at Hougang Green with a friend, the girl’s boyfriend identified himself and called the police.  Chen was then arrested.

     

    A second similar charge of having sex with the victim in mid-May at a hotel was taken into consideration.

     

    Chen, who pleaded for leniency, could have been jailed for up to five years and fined up to S$10,000 for the offence.
