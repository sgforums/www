    Hi. I'm from Malaysia, and when I was in primary school, I've seen many canings on boys and girls (I often get caned too). That time, I don't really have any feelings on it, but now, when I'm big, I do feel happy when I think of girls getting caned in school. Sometimes, when I return to my primary school, and looked into the class and saw teachers caning girls, I do feel very 'excited' (I'm not heartless... The canings are not cruel... They just give u pain for half an hour... and of course making u embarrassed... but not to hurt the student).

    I enjoy the most (didn't enjoy that time, enjoy when thinking back) the way my primary 3 chinese teacher cane students. She will ask the student to show his/her hand first, and cane him/her many times. But if the student after a few strokes refuse to show his/her hand (coz he/she cant stand the pain), and the teacher is still not finished, she will order them to show their hand for a few times, and if he/she doesn't show his/her hand, my teacher will cane them on their bottoms many times. When girls get caned at the bottom, they will run about a little, and the sound produced when the cane land on their skirt (pinafore) is loud, but nice. And the way they cry after that, is really cute........

    In secondary school, I rarely got the chance to see girls get caned. If they do, they are caned privately in the principal's room. There is some exceptions though, for example, when a lot of students are to be caned, for example, all the students who went to assembly late, we will line up in front of the principal's office, and every student will walk into the room, and the principal cane his/her bottom 2 strokes. During this time, both boys and gals are caned together, but not other times. I get caned this way too... But during that time, I already started like these canings... So I quite enjoy it, although it's painful. Rarely got chances to see girls caned in secondary school.The loud sound when the cane hit the girl's pinafore, and the way the gals feel shy and embarassed, or sob... is nice...

    Any one got the same feelings? Wanna share some? I'm sure in singapore, there's also public canings of sch students rite?
