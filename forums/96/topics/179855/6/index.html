    Girl, recommend u a book by Charlie Young (Yang Cai Ni). Can't remember title tho. I happened to see it in library and borrowed it. A good book, teaching u the steps for make ups, what colours to use, the right way of applying, how to clean etc. Also teach u what to eat/drink for good health/body etc.

    I duno much about make ups but can share with u what i know..

    There are press powder, foundations and 2-way cake.

    I have been using foundation..actually I dinoe the difference between the 3 by looking at the packaging. My sis told me press powder usually dun have a mirror, foundation comes with mirror and for 2-way cake, usually it will be written on the package. 2-way cake is foundation with extra protection against sun or UV rays.

    Avoid using liquid foundation. It will clog ur pores and trap the dirts inside.
    My fren ever let me use her Body Shop foundation.. it's shaped like a long fat tube. Looks natural and even after applying.

    I will probably change to press powder after I have finished using my foundation.

    For blusher, choose a light colour that will give u natural blush can liao. Use a thick blush for blusher, coz it will help to spread out evenly.

    For eyeshadows, somebody recommended u liao la. I use pink or purple.

    I usually dun like to draw around my eyes. I have never used eyeliner and mascara before. I often see my girl frens getting sore eye bcos they nv clean their mascara and stuff ard their eyes properly.

    If u are juz putting a light make up, use lip gloss can liao. Lip gloss will protect ur lips and leave a bit of colour and outline on ur lips even if the colour has run off.

    eyebrow pencil.. i duno how to recommend. I'm not expert in drawing eyebrows. Many of my gfs dun dare to see people without their eyebrows drawn, even tho they are not wearing make up.

    U can make up watever u like.. but hor, pls remember to clean properly hor.

    Dun be vain and put on make up so often but anyhow clean. Sooner or later ur complexion will become very jialat. Use make up removal and remove ur make up thoroughly. Wash face then use toner and moisturiser.
