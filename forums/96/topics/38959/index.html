    If price is not a problem, the best I have been to so far is Billiard Rooms at China Town. It is along Trengannu Street to be exact and has a personal lift up to the place on the 3rd floor. I will normally only go there should I need to talk business with my clients over some pool.

    It is an exclusive place with individual cubicles for every table. There are also VIP Rooms availiable. The Tables, Cues, and balls are all maintained in tip top condition. The staff are very friendly and service oriented. And the food there are not so bad.

    Pool lessons are also availiable there by this guy who is really pretty good call Chalres Poolz. But the lessons are really quite expensive too.

    Like I said, if you really want the best, this is the place with all these, but the price ain't really cheap. It is over $11 per hour if I did not recall wrongly.
