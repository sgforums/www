    There are 10 bottles of pill where each bottle contains 100 pills in it. Each pill weighs 1g however there is one defect bottle whereby all the pills weighs only 0.9g.
    By using an Electronic weighing machine, how to detect the defective bottle.

    Split into two groups of five bottles.

    Weigh both groups. Take the group that has the smaller total mass.

    Then weigh the five bottles one by one. The bottle with the smallest mass is the defective bottle.

    Of course, you can just weigh all ten bottles one at a time, but this method is faster.
