    Try as we might, we are unable to regulate the ratings and comments given by the members in sgGirls. However, if any offensive comment or degrading remark is made in particular to the girl in the picture, the girl may request for her picture to be removed.

    See it this way. sgGirls is a place for us to showcase the beauty of the Singapore Girl, as well as to pay tribute to the Oriental beauty. Nevertheless, we welcome any photo contribution from all members in the sgGirls family. Beauty lies in the eye of the beholder. What is average-looking to one person may be good-looking to another.

    I'd like to highlight that the photo section is only one part of the sgGirls.com website. Another interesting segment would be the sgGirls forum. Through various events, many members (both guys and girls) have gotten to know each other better. The friendship that the members found here is one reason why these members keep coming back to the sgGirls website, and not the photo section.

    If you have observed, most members who post here in the forum hardly comment in the photo section at all. I myself have gone on 7 outings with the regular members here and I can vouch for their characters that they are one friendly lovable bunch of people to hang out with. If you don't mind, you are always welcomed to join us for any events that we may hold in the future. See the truth for yourself. =] Best regards.
