Adler Hostel Is Hiring

  • Adler Intl
    20 Sep 12, 16:15

    Adler Hostel, an up-and-coming luxury hostel, is looking for young, upbeat receptionists that can:

    ·         Do simple housekeeping (washing/changing bed linens, making the beds, clearing the bins, sweeping/mopping the areas once daily and generally just keeping the lounge area neat and tidy, cleaning the washrooms)

    ·         Do check-ins/check-outs of guests 

    ·         Simple chit-chat with guests if/when you see them

    ·         Aid guests with their questions about touring Singapore( we will have a booklet ready for you to refer to)

    Employees on the 7am shift are also required to prepare a simple breakfast for the guests.

    Training is provided and the pay is S$6/hour. We’re looking for ladies for the morning (7am-3pm) and afternoon (3pm-11pm) shifts, and guys for the night shift which is from 11pm to 7am. The night shift would be more relaxing as there shouldn’t be much unless a guest requires help during the night!

    We’re located at Chinatown. If interested, or know anyone who is genuinely interested to work with us, please email contact@adlerintl.com or drop by 259 South Bridge Road if you’re near the area!
