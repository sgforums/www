Assistant IT Manager opening

  • An ProTeam
    11 Oct 10, 16:07

    Job Role

    The selected candidate will be reporting to the CIO and be groomed to take on a managerial role and be responsible for the various activities associated with the systems development and technical support of business applications and networks in the company.

    Responsibilities

    • Conduct system feasibility studies covering technical, social and economic feasibility
    • Advise Users and Project Team on project scope and terms of reference
    • Identify and assess the impact of changes to systems and processes
    • Analyze and revise system logic and documentation
    • Provide analysis and recommendations on various systems options
    • Perform detailed data analysis and modelling
    • Develop detailed specifications for use in Programming
    • Participate in system testing and manage UAT
    • Lead small to medium size projects
    • Develop project cost estimates

    Requirements

    • Degree in Information Systems, Computer Science or equivalent
    • 4 to 5 years of systems analysis and data modelling experience
    • Strong skills in SDLC management, OOAD, Enterprise System Architecture design
    • Hands-on development experience in Microsoft .NET technology with VS 2005 and SQL Server Stored Procedures
    • Familiar with virtualization setup in Microsoft Windows Server 2008
    • Experience working with IBM Blade Systems and Cisco networks
    • Excellent interpersonal, communication and documentation skills
    • Ability to keep abreast with new technology as it applies to project assignments.

    Working Hours:

    • 5.5 days work week with alternate Saturday off
    • Company location at North
