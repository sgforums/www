    Buyer (6months Contract)

    Dear All,

    Our company, Maxsphere Solution is looking for people for available Positions below.

    Buyer (6months Contract)
    Location : Tuas
    Working hours : Monday - Friday (8.25am - 5.30pm)

    Job Scope :
    1.Update Packaging Approved Vendor List and Price List.
    2.Monitor delivery performance of chemical & packaging
    suppliers.
    3.Source for packaging materials & liaise with QA for
    evaluation and approval.
    4.File claims against suppliers for defective packaging
    materials & downtime.
    5.Verify supplier and forwarder invoices.
    6.Ensure that replacement of rejected goods is delivered
    on time.
    7. Generate POs, disseminate shipping documents, filing,
    typing & other clerical duties.
    8. Ensure that QA receives their C.O.As and Inspection
    Reports on all deliveries.
    9.Adhere to QEHS standards and requirements.
    10.Co-ordinate with shipping and forwarders on local
    clearance of imports.
    11.Follow up with suppliers for Corrective and Preventive
    Action.
    12.Liaise with suppliers for UN qualification of packaging
    materials.
    13.Co-ordinate with Corporate Communication and suppliers
    for artwork process.
    14.Generate & evaluate Statistical Purchasing Reports.
    15.Conduct stock check @ suppliers' warehouse.
    16.Perform other duties as and when required.

    Salary : $1,000 (increment up to $500 upon confirmation)

    Requirements
    - G.C.E. 'A' Level or Diploma in Supply Chain Management
    - 3 to 5 years' relevant experience
    - Computer literate.
    - Good interpersonal skills

    Interested applicants please email your comprehensive resume in words formats to profile@maxsphere.com.sg
    Please also indicate which job/position you are applying for as title header in your application. Thank you.

    We regret that only short-listed candidates will be notified.

    Only Singaporean and PR need to apply.

    * Please help to inform your families, relatives and friends of the availability of the position above and also their opportunity of earning referral fees by forward this email to them. Thank you.
    * Refer to us a successful candidate and earn up to $70 as a referral fee!
