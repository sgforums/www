    Recruitment Drive for Aljunied MRT Station

    Positions Available: 4 to 5 F&B Service Personnel (on different shifts)

    Duty:

    -       Responsible for the preparation of food & beverages

    -       Responsible for cash register transaction

    -       Maintain the standard of quality services product and cleanliness

    -       Food supply maintenance, liasing with logistics

     

    The staffs needs to be savvy and supporting of the Western culture design of the food store, and be responsible for the generic preparation of food & beverages.  As economical multi-tasking personnel, being honest with money is important for the confidential security of cash register transaction.  A good knowledge of different languages is a bonus incentive for rapport between staff and customer, which definitely enhances reputation and brand loyalty for popular business maintenance.  Cleanliness and order is also a parameter of maintenance to upkeep the standard of quality services product.  Being typically in charge of the food station, knowledge of logistic support is needed to maintain food supply lines for service.  The working hours are based on shifts and flexible working hours is questioned for work, which include working on Saturday, Sunday and Public Holiday.

     

    Requirement:

    -       No experience is required as training is provided

    -       Able to converse in English, Chinese and Malay

    -       Able to perform the shift duties (including Saturday, Sunday and Public Holiday)

    -       Student are welcome

    -       Flexible working hours

    -       Only Singaporean and PR may apply

    Salary is negotiable
