Looking for IT Customer Support Engineer (Japanese Speaking)

  • Clara.hts
    28 Feb 13, 09:16

    Job Responsibilities:

    • Communicate with Japanese channel-partners concerning incoming incidents and register these findings in trouble tickets.
    • Resolve technical issues pertaining clients’ network connections by analyzing and troubleshooting the incident.
    • Coordinate the repair process with third party providers / suppliers
    • Manage the channel-partner relation proactively throughout the entire project and ensure the client experiences the professionalism and high level of service standard.
    • Perform technical acceptance testing to newly provisioned services to ensure the quality of delivery in accordance to Expereo standards
    • Administrate the process of technical support accurately and share findings with colleagues
    • Participate in project teams as necessary to support the business.
    Requirements:
    • Degree in Computer Science/ Information Technology.
    • Minimum 2 years of experience in IT help desk Support (Hardware and Network).
    • Able to work independently and proactively.
    • Possess strong communication, interpersonal skills and good attitude towards work.
    • Fluent in English, Mandarin & Japanese (At least JLPT Level 2, Oral & Written) to liaise with multi-lingual speaking customers.
