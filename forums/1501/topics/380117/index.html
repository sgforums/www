    Hi Farid,

    Please do not give up so easily, for you have 5 more semesters to bring up your results.

    I do not believe that you are a lazy person, for if you are truly one, it will be highly unlikely for you to get merit even in ITE.

    I sincerely apologise for saying this, but it is highly likely that you are a slower learner as compared to others, as we can see from the fact that you take a longer route to enter poly.

    Thus, you cannot work at the same rate as others. Anything which others put in an hour of effort in, you must be prepared to put in up to as much as five hours in. I encourage you to study together with your peers. Thus, if you have any problems, you can seek them for help.

    Do not have the mindset that just because they do better than you, you shouldn't seek them for help, as it will be embarrassing. Learning is about humility, and thus one should seek help when necessary.

    As for the pay, I will say that a diploma holder's pay will definitely be higher than a nitec holder's pay. The amount of pay difference is dependent on which sector is your job in though.
