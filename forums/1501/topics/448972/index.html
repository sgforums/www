Creative Designer Wanted Urgently!

  • Global Water Solutions
    13 Mar 12, 16:14

    Global Water Solutions Pte. Ltd. has been delivering quality drinking water into homes & corporate offices throughout Singapore. We produce only the highest quality drinking water, with the highest quality labels and bottles. We strongly believe in the power of branding and value every of our clients’ brand image. All our projects must have great design aesthetic and also encapsulate the quality of our clients’ product/s and organization.

    As we are expanding rapidly, we are looking for a new creative designer who is quick to adapt in a fast paced environment, eager to learn and bold to exploring ingenious ways to tackle design obstacles.

    Candidates must be:

    • •        Proficient in Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Microsoft Office, Basic HTML/CSS Scripting.
    • •        Skilled with layout, typography, and color theory in at least one graphic program.
    • •        A natural attention to detail.
    • •        Able to conceptualize, plan & execute design works independently.
    • •        Active in brainstorming ideas for marketing campaigns.
    • •        Able to rationalize design works and communicate ideas clearly to clients.
    • •        Preferably with 1-2 years of training experience.
    • •        Fresh graduates/Entry level applicants are encouraged to apply.
    • •        Bilingual (English/Chinese).
    • •        Able to work 5.5 day work week.

    Interested applicants should include a link to your portfolio or provide work samples as part of the application process. Please indicate your role and contribution for each project submitted. Please e-mail your CV/Portfolio to Joshua@watersolutions.com.sg or call: 6463 8230. Only shortlisted candidates will be notified.

    Thank you!
