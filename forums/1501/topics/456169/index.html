Linguistic Checks of Brand Names

  • The Brand Checker
    25 Jul 12, 10:07

     

    Job type:Online survey completion, proofreading

    Title:Linguistic Checks of Brand Names and Taglines

    Target Language: English (Singapore), Mandarin (Singapore), Malay (Singapore), Tamil (Singapore) 

     

     

    Overview: We are looking for native speakers of local languages and dialects to complete simple online surveys to assess the pronunciation, sound, meaning, and connotations of brand names and taglines. Ideally candidates should be linguists, cultural experts, academic or commercial professionals.

    Upon our approval of your application, you will regularly receive email invitations to join surveys in your language/dialect that can be accessed using your username and password. Surveys must be completed in a timely manner and provide quality feedback. All information is confidential and cannot be disclosed or used for any other purpose.

    You will be paid per survey according to a specified rate and payment schedule via bank transfer.

    Description:

    The Brand Checker was developed by a team of brand consultants with years of experience and hundreds of satisfied international brand naming clients to allow for fast and meaningful linguistic screening worldwide.

    The online platform connects brands, agencies, and linguists to assist in building strong and valuable global brands, whose verbal identities give consideration to local language and cultural characteristics.

    Requirement:

    Native speakers of the target languages

    Job description:

    -Respond to survey invitations received via. email

    -Read and consider brand name/tagline candidates carefully in your mother language/dialect and answer the survey questions online in a timely manner. This may include, but is not limited to:

             -Writing down words/brand names with similar pronunciation

          - Indicating the evocations (good or bad) of the name/tagline candidates according to your cultural context

            - Rating and commenting on the name/tagline candidates in terms of pronunciation, sound, overall evaluation, etc.

    If you are interested in becoming a linguistic checker of the Brand Checker, please send you CV to info@thebrandchecker.com
