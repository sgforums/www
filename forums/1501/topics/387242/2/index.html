    The problem with Singapore is that the IT line is floored by big Government-linked IT companies hiring FTs. Only SME may hire local Singaporeans, but they usually only hire staff with experience. Thus, Singaporeans with little or no experience face strong competition from experienced FTs with higher qualifications.

    And those application software engineer in Banking Industries and Financial services will tend to recruit Indian nationals due to the nature that they had been supporting Finance/Banking related software for many years.

    Thus, Singaporeans will end up looking for other jobs, secure a degree in IT management and return to end up going in to manage these FTs.

    Moreover, Singaporeans in IT industry do not really like to do programming, or do not really enjoy mopping up someone's programming error. Thus, they tend to switch to other fields.
