    "Tell me about yourself" is a must you need to answer in the interview.

    And don't only read the job scope. From the scope, know what is expected (eg: team environment, customer-oriented/sales driven, and skills required : mutlitask/ detailed/ able to prioritize/ flexible)

    Don't just say, I'm detailed person. Say something like : In my previous company, whenever I'm given a task, I will always double-check all the documents and make sure it's error free. I also like to keep a record of all tasks so I am able to keep track on my schedule as well as to ensure everything runs smoothly without rushing for deadlines.
