Flexi-based part time job

  • Walnutpaste

    Job Description:

    Love to eat? Love to take pictures? Want a flexible job that needs you to do just that? Best thing is that you get to keep the food you buy and earn money at the same time!

    Buy, Snap, Upload, Eat and Get Paid - That’s all you have to do.

    Job Scope:

    A Singapore-based company seeks to understand the food market in Malaysia.

    This flexi-based job requires you to buy food (basically all kinds ranging from snacks, canned food, packaged food etc) that has an ingredient list behind and upload the taken pictures into the company’s website.

    Hassle- free - No need to type product description, price or anything at all!

    Requirements:

    1. 18 years and above
    2. Love to shop for food, possess a digital camera (at least 3.2 megapixels and above) and IT-savvy
    3. Hardworking, responsible and has good time management
    Hardworking – The more you hardworking you are, the more you earn
    Responsible – Contact is via email/phone only
    Good Time Management – Able to manage a 2nd stream of income without compromising on day job
    4. Only Malaysians need apply

    Interested applicants please send in a photocopied Identity Card/Papers & a picture of yourself to walnutpaste@yahoo.com.sg.

    Subject header: I love to eat!

    Benefits :

    No limits on the type of food as it is a new market.
