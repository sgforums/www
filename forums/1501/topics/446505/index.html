    Suiting Up for the interview

    Like it or not, until you've been given an opportunity to prove yourself, your appearance speaks a thousand words. That 's why it is always important to dress professionally for a job interview regardless of job nature and seniority.

    * Look Clean & Sharp

    * Nothing kills an interview faster than a gush of bad breath or body odour, so keep your mouth and underarms fresh and clean at all times.If you intend to wear cologne, make sure it's not too overpowering.

    * Mens are expected to dress in a pair of pants and a long-sleeve shirt. A matching tie can be used to complement your look.For more senior executives, you will definitely look more credible in a well-tailored suit.
