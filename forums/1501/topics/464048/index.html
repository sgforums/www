    Multidisciplinary Job Scope

     

    ·         Develop go-to-market plan, execute sales, build distribution channels and formulate management processes

    ·         Structured training content that deliver multidisciplinary skill covering sales, marketing, product management and business management

    ·         Be a Singapore Citizen/PR

    ·         Graduate from University or Polytechnics

    ·         Interested to become Technopreneur in the dynamic technology startup environment
