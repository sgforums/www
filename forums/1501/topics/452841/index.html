    • Remains flexible to changing priorities, open to new ideas
    • Fast learner, with high energy levels, who can roll up their sleeves and are comfortable in fast-paced start-up environments.
    • Detail-oriented and precise; strong verbal and written communication skills.
    • Tech savvy and some Photoshop skills
    • Prior experience in guerilla and online marketing, social media sites, events management, restaurant industry will be of great advantage.
    • At least 2 years of marketing

    As with any start-ups, flexibility will be the key. The position can be highly rewarding in terms of participating in the evolution of a start-up. A reasonable base pay is available. The bigger pay off will be in the form of growth in role and responsibilities in an early stage company and bonuses.
