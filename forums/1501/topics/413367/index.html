    Job Opening - Temasek Polytechnic, Singapore

    Job Code: Tech Spec/ENG
    Job Title: Technical Specialist (Technical Support) / Interactive Digital Centre - Asia

    Job Description:     JOB SUMMARY
    The successful candidate will be expected to provide technical, operational and maintenance support to the centre and laboratories.


    JOB RESPONSIBILITES
    1. To provide technical support and maintenance for the centre and laboratories
    2. To provide support in the daily operations of the centre
    3. To setup and maintain all PCs in the centre as well as plan for hardware and software upgrades
    4. To administer and support student project work areas
    5. To be involved in budget planning, monitoring and administration
    6. To be involved in purchasing of equipment and software
    7. To organize and support events e.g. school events, centre events and exhibitions
    8. To be in-charge of computer render farm and liaising with vendor on maintenance
    9. To issue, maintain and keep track of assets, and perform assets reporting
    10. To setup showcase demos for visitors, and provide support during the visits
    11. To liaise with vendors and various departments on technical support and maintenance of equipment and facility
    12. To maintain website and manage website content


    JOB REQUIREMENTS
    1. A good diploma or degree in Computer Engineering/Information Technology or related field with a minimum of 2 years working experience in the related area
    2. Candidates should have strong fundamental skills in computer technical support, servers and network maintenance, and website maintenance
    3. Familiar with computer installations setup, display systems setup and projector systems setup
    4. Knowledge of networks, switches, firewalls, NAS and network security software
    5. Knowledge of website maintenance and content management
    6. Good presentation and communication skills, strong interpersonal skills
    7. Resourceful, ability to work independently and in a team on events and activities
    8. Interest in interactive and digital media technologies would be an advantage


    Closing Date :     12/11/2010

    For application of the position, please visit Temasek Polytechnic website (Career link) for details.
