    Hi all!

    World Vision Singapore Youth Ambassador Programme (YAP) is currently holding its Recruitment Drive for the next batch of Youth Ambassadors! We are looking for passionate, committed, as well as enthusiastic post-secondary students, who are willing to join us in our cause.

    Here's some information about YAP:

    Aims and Objectives
    YAP seeks to:
    Serve as a bridge, connecting World Vision to the youths in Singapore
    To instill a spirit of volunteerism and a heart of compassion in youths
    To expose youths to the realities of poverty, thereby encouraging them to partake in or spearhead meaningful projects that will benefit the community
    To empower youths to reach their full potential and to live life in all its fullness

    Roles and Responsibilities
    As a Youth Ambassador, you will
    Promote active involvement in community service among your peers
    Plan and fundraise for an overseas community work trip to one of World Vision Singapore's supported communities and projects
    Conduct talks in schools to share about the work of World Vision and YAP
    Participate actively in activities organised by World Vision
    Spearhead new initiatives
    Uphold the values of World Vision

    Commitment
    As a Youth Ambassador, you will be required to 
    Attend month General Meetings, currently set on the last Friday of every month
    Attend Event and Department meetings (fortnightly basis)
    Participate in World Vision and YAP activities
    Plan and participate in the overseas community work trip
    Commit to the term of service from March 2010 to June 2011

    If you would like to apply, please visit our website athttp://www.worldvision.org.sg/st_yap.php .You will be able to find more information, as well as details on the selection process. Please send your application form in to yapexco@gmail.com by 28th February 2010.

    Also, we will be having a booth at the upcoming Youth Got Heart 2010. Do feel free to drop by to find out more about us! Alternatively, you can email your queries toyapexco@gmail.com.

    Thank you very much for your kind attention and we look forward to receiving your application!


    Sincerely
    World Vision Singapore
