    Here's a Casting Call for ‘Stories of the National Museum of Singapore’, a project directed by award-winning Singaporean Director, Anthony Chen. 


    Shoot Details :
    Shoot Dates: Either on the 20th/ 21st January 2014 (Monday/Tuesday)

    Examples of people we are looking for...
    Young Singaporeans 
    Have you visited the National Museum of Singapore when you were a child or as part of a school field trip? Or have you recently visited the NMS? Let us know your story! 
    - Aged between 16 - late 20s. 


    The Working Class 
    Do you remember taking your graduation photos or having a class gathering at the NMS? Are you still in contact with most of them? We would love to hear from you! 
    - If you're in your 30s - 40s. 

    Grandma & Grandpa 
    Did you have your wedding photos taken at the NMS years ago? Do you remember how long ago it was and how the NMS looked like back then? 

    Could you have been...
    A long time staff/librarian/conserver or even a security guard who has worked/is still working at the NMS? 

    How did it feel to gaze upon the towering Elephant Skeleton at the National Museum for the very first time? Do you remember the gigantic whalebone hovering within the museum’s majestic passageway? Let us know your thoughts! 


    ____________________________________________________________
    Casting Details : 
    Day/Dates: 4 - 5 Jan 2014 (Sat - Sun), 10am - 6.30pm
    You may bring items or tokens related to your experience. For example, you may want to bring photos that you took at the musuem. If the photo include other people, we would love it if you can bring them along to share with us that moment when the photo was taken.

    ____________________________________________________________

    If you or anyone you know fit the description above, please drop us an email at casting@hellocreatives.sg with recent clear photographs/head shots (if any), contact details, availability for casting audition and we'll get in touch with you shortly to schedule an appointment. 
    Please feel free to share this open call with your friends and family! 

    Thank you and have a great new year ahead!
