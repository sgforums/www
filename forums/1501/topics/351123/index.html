    Temp positions refer to relief officers, sometimes on an ad-hoc basis.

    For those without NSRS/WSQ, if you are from

    PERSONS WHO MAY BE EXEMPTED FROM TRAINING REQUIREMENT FOR SECURITY OFFICER
    S/No Criteria for Exemption wef 7 March 2006
    1 - Former Auxiliary Police Officer who has completed and passed the Basic Auxiliary Police Officer Course conducted by CISCO or AETOS APF Training School.
      - Former regular uniformed officer/other ranks from the Singapore Police Force (SPF), the Singapore Armed Forces (SAF), the Central Narcotics Bureau (CNB), the Singapore Prison Service (Prisons), the Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF), the Corrupt Practices Investigations Bureau (CPIB)
      - Reserve officers from SPF and SCDF only.
      - Volunteer Special Constabulary (VSC) officers from SPF.
      - SAF full-time National Servicemen who had served full-time NS in the vocation of Military Police or Provost Unit. Please note that Regimental Police Officers (RP) do not fall in this criteria.
    2 Guard who has passed the Essential Security Guarding at Group 4 CISCO Training before 1 Jan 96 .
    3 Guard who has passed the Specialised Guarding Skill at Group 4 CISCO Training before 1 Jan 96 .
    4 Guard who has passed the Advance Security Course at APRO Asian Protection Pte Ltd before 1 Jan 96 .
    5 Guard who has passed the Basic Protection Officer Course at APRO Asian Protection Pte Ltd before 1 Nov 2002.
    6 Guard who has passed the Basic Security Training Course at APRO Asian Protection Pte Ltd before 1 Nov 2002 .
    7 Guard who has passed the 2-day Basic Security Course Examination organised by ACSA or SAS and certified by SHRI or RELC before 1 Nov 2002 .

    Quoted from spf website.
