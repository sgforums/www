    Art at Work is a young and enterprising creative agency startup that specializes in printed media. Due to expansion, we are on a lookout for a Business Development Executive and Graphic Designer to join us in driving the company to greater heights.

     

    Business Development Executive

    Role Summary

    To prospect and build a diverse range of clientele and strengthen business relationship with existing clients 

    -Assist clients in their evaluation and execution of marketing and advertising programs

    -Work closely with creative team to ensure creative development is in sync with clients’ needs and expectation

    -Manage budget and account profitability of projects

    -Proactively identify strategic projects and issues within to grow the account

    -Participate in new business efforts

    -Potential to grow, mentor and lead a team

    -Able to draw up a creative brief, the act of writing ensures you capture what you need to convey to the creative team.

     

    Requirements

    -Minimum 1-3 years of proven sales experience in the creative or advertising industry

    -Tertiary education (GCE ‘O’/’A’/Diploma) with sales and business background

    -Pleasant disposition, good interpersonal and communication skills is a prerequisite

    -Able to understand Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator or In-design / free is a plus

    -Experience in web design will be an advantage

    -Positive attitude and good interpersonal skills

    -Able to handle and work within tight schedules

    -Work closely with the Account Executive and internal team on job progress and completion

    -Knowledge of pre-press and printing production process
