    Wanted: Sales Manager – Internet Services

     

     

     

    Like what you hear about Shownearby?

     

    We would like to extend an invitation to you to come aboard our ship that will sail to the ends of the earth and beyond.

     

    Role:

    You will convince businesses of and sell ShowNearby internet services. In addition, you will also have to manage these clients by providing good after sales support and customer service. Main product – ShowNearby.com advertising space, map integration into business websites, paid listings/profiling.

     

    Responsibilities:

    • Promotion and selling of online advertisement spaces, applications and listings/profile

    • Corporate Sales

    • Finding new corporate customers to increase ShowNearby’s market share

    • Maintaining good rapport and building relationships with existing corporate customers

    • Managing a sales team and meeting team sales quota

     

    Requirements:

    • Minimum of ‘O’ Level for formal education

    • 3 years or more work experience in media (preferably internet media) or advertising sales (preferably online) preferred

    • Interest in Information Technology and Internet industry

    • Must be independent, team-player and customer orientated

    • Must be well-groomed with a cheerful personality

    • Most importantly must KNOW HOW TO HAVE FUN!

     

    What Do We Offer?

    An attractive package that emphasizes on high variable income (overriding sales commission) with a basic salary.
