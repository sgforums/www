    We are recruiting marketing volunteers for our thrift shop in close proximity to Holland Village.

     

    This shop is run by Fei Yue's Community Services, an organization which is registered with the Registry of Charities and partially aided by the National Council of Social Services.

     

    Goods on display are donated by Singaporeans and expatriates. It is manned by volunteers and the proceeds from its transactions go to fund the Fei Yue's Holland Close Senior Activity Centre (HCSAC) and its many community programmes and services.

     

    Although we are partially funded by the NCSS, we have to raise fund on our own to support community activites for the less priviledge and the disadvantaged.

    We have marketing programmes lined up to raise fund for the community and we need volunteers with min 2 to 3 years experience and skills in the following areas:

     

    1. Retail Merchandizing
    2. Web Design
    3. Sales Promotion
    4. Graphic Designing
    5. Copywriting
    6. Coordination
    7. Press/Public Relation
    8. Photography
    9. Fashion Show Production

    There will be 2 to 3 events a year requiring your assistance.

    Please write to me at feiyuethriftshop@gmail.com for more details if you want to make a positive contribution. We welcome all enquiries.

     

    Please help me circulate this notice to your friends.

     

    Thank you.

     

    H P Loh
