    Are you currently a part-timer?

    Simply participate in our surveys and receive $5! Alternatively, you can opt to donate the money to the Straits Times Pocket Money Fund.

    Survey details:
    - This survey is for our Business Course Final Year Project.
    To be eligible for the survey, you must be working for a company/organisation/business on a non-full-time or non-permanent basis now.
    - The online survey will be administered twice: once now and another in 2 months time. The link for the 2nd part of the survey will be sent to your email.
    - A $5 incentive will be given to you after completion of the 2nd part of the survey. The money will be deposited directly into your bank account or donated to the Straits Times Pocket Money Fund based on your indicated preference (in the 2nd part of the survey).
    - Disclaimer: Engaging in any freelance work, e.g. teaching private tuition, is not considered part-time in our study and will not be included in our collected data. We reserve the right to reject any unsuitable survey entries.
