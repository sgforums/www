    Sales/Service/Electrical/Mechanical/C&S Engineers

     

    Requirements:

    • At least a Diploma or Degree in Mechanical / Electrical / Mechatronics / Civil & Structural Engineering
    • At least 2 year(s) of working experience in the related field
    • A Valid Class 3 license (preferably owns a car )
    • Able to converse well both in English & Mandarin
    • Basic salary : $2.2k above

    Applicants should be Singaporean citizens or hold relevant residence status.

    Interested candidates, please email a detailed resume to jobs@careerservices.com.sg, stating

    . employment history
    . personal details
    . availability
    . present and expected salaries
