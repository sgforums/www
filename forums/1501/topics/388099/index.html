    Pioneering start-up business to-be requires three candidates to host enrichment workshop and tutoring lesson, and assist in running business operation. This job is suitable for candidate who has strong ability in team playing, and leadership.

     

    Suitable for
    - Educator who wants to make a difference in student’s life.
    - Individuals who are willing to invest time and effort
    - Graduates who wish to gain hands-on experience
    - People with entrepreneur minds

     

    Job Requirement
    - Strong Command in English
    - Good Interpersonal Skills
    - Creative, Analytical, Humorous, and Extrovert
    - 1 to 3 years in tutoring/ teaching /facilitation experience in N/O/A Chemistry, Biology, Physics, Mathematics
    - Poly/Uni undergraduates ('O' level grad with good results are also welcome)
    - Good team player

    Job Scope
    - Course planning and execution
    - Administrative work for the day-to-day running operation

    - Per assignment basis (between $100 to $200); negotiable.
