    BUILD-A-BEAR WORKSHOP - SINGAPORE

    Looking for A Honey of A Career?

    If you like kids, are a child at heart, and have a beary high level of enthusiasm, come join our team.

    1. Associate Managers / Supervisors (Full-Time)

    Reports to: Chief Workshop Manager & Assistant Workshop Manager

    Principle Responsibilities: 

    • Help to deliver and promote an atmosphere of excellent Guest Service.
    • Assist Chief Workshop Manager & Assistant Workshop Manager in managing store operations.
    • Floor supervision to ensure all Associates are in place, perform roles, meet dress standards and deliver excellent Guest Service.
    • Support Associates in performing their Guest Service roles.
    • Accurately perform all opening and closing procedures.
    • Other tasks and assignments as may be requested.

    Prerequisite Qualifications and Experience

     

    Education:

    •  ‘N’ level/'O' level or Polytechnic certified.

    Experience:

    • Min 1-2 yr experience as a supervisor in retail environment

    Personal attributes: 

    • Excellent Guest Service, organizational, planning, and problem solving skills
    • Work well with people of all ages with an emphasis on children of all ages
    • Proficient abilities in computers, written, and oral communication
    • Neat, clean appearance
    • Self-starter, highly motivated, and effective in a fast-paced environment
    • Positive attitude with an outgoing and friendly personality
    • Honest and forthright
    • Team player
