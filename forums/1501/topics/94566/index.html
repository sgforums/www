    we are looking for a office admin/HR who is available starting October 04.

    Below is the job description.

    Office Administration:-

    Handle all incoming and outgoing mails (ie. faxes, general emails,
    letters, courier)

    Administer office telephone system and photocopying machine and maintain
    speed-dial programming

    Coordinate Sales and Technical Trainings and other business meetings

    Evaluate and review travel agency and contract

    Stationery and pantry supplies

    Establish and negotiate for hotel corporate rates

    Oversee office internal cleanliness and to ensure the cleaner upkeeps the office

    General Data entries in SAP

    Company product trademark registration/renewal matters

    Make travel and related arrangements (ie flight and hotel bookings, visa
    applications)

    Human Resources:-

    Administer Annual/Medical/Child Sick Leave

    Application for Employment Pass, Work Permit for foreign staff

    Office Insurance (GHS/SC, Personal Accident, Fire/theft, motor, etc.)

    Recruit Advertisement and interview appointments

    Maintain SAP personnel records

    Update and issue Office Handbook

    Take care of personnel welfare (gifts for new born, birthday cards,
    flower wreath, etc)

    Though the above list of JD seems long but we are a small set-up of
    sales and service office here with 22 personnels. The workload is
    filling about 70% of her time that is also the reason she is expected to
    assist in other departments data entries. Generally, most of the time
    there are only about 10 personnels in office as the service and sales
    personnels are out in the field.

    Our company practices 5 days work week with company transport pick-up
    available at Chua Chu Kang and Jurong East MRT.

    We are looking for a mature and contented chinese lady, preferably staying in the west of Singapore with at least 3 years working experience in admin position of age 27-32. Singapore PR with good command of english is acceptable.

    Salary expected S$1,600-1,800/mth, other fringe benefits (personal Insurance, medical, dental, leave, etc) will be made known when we have
    the right candidate.

    Please do not hesitate to contact me should you require further information. We are looking to interview some candidates next week, therefore would appreciate you arrange for the suitable candidates resumes to be emailed to us soon.
