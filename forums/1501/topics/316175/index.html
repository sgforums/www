    Looking For Fresh Graduates!!!

     

    Building Surveyors

    Attractive Pay + 5 days work week

    Work location: Shenton Way

    Job Scope
    You are appointed by contractors to carry out photographic survey of the properties surrounding the site before the commencemement of work. This is to confirm the pre-existing condition.

    In the event of a claim, you are appointed by insurance companies to ascertain the nature and extent of the damage and whether this can be attributed to the contractors' work. A reasonable amount of office work and site work is required.

    Personal Qualities
    - Tactful and able to communicate
    - Good written and oral communication skills
    - quick learner

    Entry Requirements
    - ITC, Diploma in engineering disciplines
    - fresh graduates are welcome to apply

    Educational and training
    - On the job training under an Engineer and a Chartered Loss Adjuster
    - Further education developments on insurance and chartered loss adjusters examinations

    Career Opportunities
    - working in a Loss-adjusting company with opportunities to become a full fledge loss adjuster.

    Only Singaporean or PR need apply.

    To apply, please send your CV+cover letter to recruit@inspireglobal.com.sg
