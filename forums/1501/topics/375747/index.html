    That's the way it operates - people who want to work at fast food joints just ask to see the manager.

     

    Time wise, usually it is flexible and depending on your schedule - check with the manager.  There are some 24 hour fast food joints like Macs if you are willing to work odd hours.

     

    Because of your age, they will likely give you some simple stuff to do like clearing trays, mopping floors etc.

     

    Try to work at some place near your home where transport is accessible.  No point in spending all your hard earned money on transport or if it takes 1 hour to travel to and from your house.  Fast food joints don't really pay too much for part time workers anyway.

     

    I recommend that you try it out if you really want to work.  Other jobs I don't recommend because there is a chance you might get scammed or cheated.  With fast food joints, at least if anything goes wrong, you can still get your parent to come down to the joint and complain.
