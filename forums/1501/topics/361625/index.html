    Hi forummers,

    I like to share a real story about a job situation in singapore.

    Seng Kang West Ave has a chicken rice hawker stall which has a job ad printed on a A4 paper for people to see. It says hiring singaporean / pr .

    It shows the mobile number of the boss. After calling him, this is what I gathered about the job :

    0. Place of Work : Jurong stall. Place advertised, Seng Kang. Target audience Singaporeans / PRs.

    1. PART Time : $5 / hour , for 8 (EIGHT) hours per partime day , work EVERY DAY a month, 2 days off per month only.  $5 tpt allowance.

    2. FULL Time : $1500 / month , for 12 (T W E L V E ) hours per full time day, work EVERY DAY a month , 2 days off per month only.

    Ask yourselves, how can singaporean survive on this salary when :

    1. got dependents to feed, pay medical bills, pay and pay expensive flat, high cost of living etc. Insurance to pay, CPF min sum to meet. Electric and Water conservation tax to pay and pay, every month leh. Some more got G.S.T leh. hope wont rise further? TC bills to pay and pay leh. TV license to pay and pay leh. 

    2. foreigners many come here to just 1 motive - earn and go back to their lower cost environment. Usually no kids and dependents to support here. No house to pay loans here.

    There is no min wage to protect singaporeans who are to compete head on with FTs given that the playing field is uneven.

    Taking the Partime example above to illustrate:

    1. $5 income per day minus whatever expense per day necessary , say $2.50 (consider gst), the income is a mere mortal $2.50 per day and $5 tpt allowance eaten up basically completely assuming the case of singaporean living in Seng Kang and travelling to work in Jurong.

    2. Taking the case of Full time working, a singaporean has to work 12 hours Every Day for a month with 2 day offs. Include travelling time of say 1.5 hours including preparation to go to work and waiting time, easily a singaporean worker shall use up 15 hours A DAY for this $2.50 nett income.

    3. In both cases, Full and part time, singaporean has to work EVERY DAY with 2 day offs. Tell me, how many can last even 1 week like this just on the working hours alone?

    4. Is there MIN Wage ? Why singaporean who is stuck in a high cost environment is being paid this kind of salary? Is it sufficient? For how long? Min Sum can meet? Child can get educated?  Will there be problems created for society?

     I must say employers are greatful for the accessibility to FTs and the lower cost they incur.  But I do NOT  think this model is practical for singaporeans.

     

    suzy wang

     

    edited: added 'NOT' in last statement.
