    •         Provide administrative support to principal consultant 

    •         To organize and assist in carrying out internal process audits for our clients. 

    •         To draft meeting minutes and management reports 

    •         To maintain and manage quality-related documents for our clients 

    Requirements: 

    •         Diploma / Degree holders (any field) 

    •         Fresh graduates can be considered 

    •         Process-oriented with an eye for details. 

    •         Good interpersonal, verbal and written communication skills. 

    •         Able to organise, multi-task and time manage under pressure. 

    •         Proficient in using Mircrosoft Office 2010 

    •         Chinese speaking, Singaporean / PR only
