    SEMINAR- Attaining PERSUASIVE POWER in Business Writing

    Attaining PERSUASIVE POWER in Business Writing
    ==============================================
    17 & 18 July 2002 (Wednesday & Thursday)
    9.30am - 5.00pm, Grand Hyatt Singapore

    We can have the best idea, the best product or even the
    best strategy in the world. However, if we fall short of
    the power to communicate what we have to offer in a way to
    enable decisionmakers to understand, accept or buy it,
    our great idea amounts to nothing. As writing is a central
    function in today's business communication (especially
    with the use of email and other electronic messages), the
    POWER of persuasive writing is a key asset in an
    organisation. The ability to present our ideas, products
    or services in writing with persuasive impact is crucial
    in winning goodwill, gaining approval and clinching
    deals in today's competitive business environment.

    This two-day seminar has been developed for business
    executives who are required to write with PERSUASIVE
    POWER. It develops our ability in using language to
    "woo and win" in business writing. It explains how to
    target key decisionmakers with language that is clear,
    effective, and credible. It also provides special writing
    suggestions, emphasising word choice, and sentence
    structure. This seminar helps us to improve our
    PERSUASIVE POWER in writing by employing "Argument and
    Negotiation Strategies" to win the heart of our audience.

    If you are responsible for business writing in your
    organisation, and would like to see how you can attain and
    be empowered with persuasive writing skills, this is an
    opportunity you should not miss. Confirm your participation
    now by filling in and returning the registration form below.

    TOPICS TO BE COVERED:
    --------------------
    * Hitting Our Targets
    - Audience and Purpose

    * Business English is Plain English
    - Today's business writing styles and conventions

    * Being Clear and Convincing
    - Using language to woo and win in business writing
    - Argument strategy and negotiation skills in writing

    * Shaping and Structuring
    - Topic, sentence and paragraph development
    - Editing techniques
    - Strategies for presenting and formatting information

    This seminar will enable participants to:
    ----------------------------------------
    * Sharpen their persuasive writing skills by focusing
    on Argument and Negotiation Strategies.
    * Improve the fundamentals of contemporary business
    writing in correspondence and reports.

    Who should attend:
    -----------------
    Managers, executives and professionals who require
    PERSUASIVE POWER in writing to convey information,
    messages, make decisions, persuade and convince others.

    Your Speaker - DR. ARLENE BASTION (BA DipEd MEd EdD)
    ---------------------------------
    Dr Bastion holds a Doctorate from the University of
    British Columbia and a Masters Degree from University
    of Malaya. She has lectured and taught programmes in
    the areas of Communication Skills, Technical Report
    Writing and Professional Ethics as Assistant
    Professor with leading university and educational
    establishments. Her previous work experience and
    contacts had also enabled her to understand the modes,
    techniques and issues of communication in different work
    settings. Dr Bastion has developed a variety of executives
    development programmes in "Persuasive Writing",
    "Negotiation Skills" and "Interpersonal Relationships".
    Her other research and teaching interests also include
    Moral Philosophy, Ethics, Education and Critical Thinking.
