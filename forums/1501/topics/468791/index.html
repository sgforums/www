    Financial Consultant
    (Singapore - Tanjong Pagar)

     

    • We are an established financial services company located in the Central Business District and we are currently recruiting Financial Consultants into our family.

     

    • Foreseeing a surge in our company growth, we are seeking high caliber candidates to join our team as financial consultants to assist us in bringing our company to greater heights. If you are keen on becoming an entrepreneur, and yearns a job that has extraordinary earning potential, yet promises flexibility in working hours, this is the job for you.

     

    • We recognize that our employees are our ultimate competitive advantage. Therefore, short-listed candidates will receive high quality training by our experienced and successful managers, who would impart essentially useful knowledge and skills to develop them into effective financial consultants. Career advancement to the management level will also be presented to competent individuals.

     

     

    Responsibilities

    • Expand and retain the clientele base
    • Provide support to clients and offer excellent after sales services
    • Assets management such as shares bonds and investments

     

     



    Benefits

    • High basic monthly salary + High Commission & Performance Bonuses


    • 3 Overseas achievement incentive trips per year for high achievers


    • Free professional training will be provided 


    • Outstanding consultants will be promoted to a senior or executive position depending on merit, where these consultants will be managing portfolios of clients with higher net worth.


     

    Who we are looking for

    •  Positive and hardworking attitude
    •  Passion in the financial services industry
    •  Determined to earn high source of income

     

     

    Requirements

    •  Final year studies/Fresh graduate/Minimum GCE O level/Diploma/Degree (other      
       disciplines will be considered as well)
    •  21 - 30 years old preferred
    •  Only Singaporeans and Singapore PR may apply for this position
    •  Full-time commitment required
    •  Ability to take initiative and function independently
    •  Strong drive and dedication

     

     

    • If you are interested in joining us, feel free to send us your resume to sprecruitment@hotmail.com . We regret that only short-listed candidates will be notified.
