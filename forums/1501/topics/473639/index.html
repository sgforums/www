    ·         Support the Executive Housekeeper in the daily administrative work

    ·         Responsible for sorting & filing all daily activity reports in housekeeping office

    ·         Attend to internal and guests’ requests

    ·         Coordinate between room attendants and supervisors

    ·         Check on the cleanliness of rooms before releasing to the guests

    • Greet and register incoming guests.

    • Supervise and co-ordinate activities of the Maintenance Supervisor and Technician.
    • Plan and implement preventive maintenance schedule with regards to all plants, equipment & facilities.

    • Manage and lead a team of technicians.

    • 2-3 years electrical/ plumbing/ air-conditioning experience is needed.

    • Welcome guest at the lobby.
    • Handle and deliver guests’ luggage.
    • Buggy of guests to guest room.
    • Delivering of newspaper.

    • Conduct the hotel site inspection.
    • Service existing customers and develop new market territories.

    • Convert sales lead into sales platform.
    • To achieve revenue goals, guest satisfaction and oversee the financial performance of the department.
    •  2-3 years working experience in Hospitality sales is a MUST. 

     Guest Service Manager

    • Assist with guests’ check-in and check-out.
    • Process guest folios and collect payment.
    • Respond to guest requests and complaints in a timely and professional manner.
    • Overall in-charge in the absent of the FOM & AFOM.
    • Monitor and ensure all operations are being carried out to standard and smooth operations.
    • Train and guide junior staffs to ensure competencies are met and excellent service standard is provided to the guests.
    • Ensuring lobby and all activities in order.

    • 3-4 years front office working experience is needed.
