    Join our team of Private Tutors

    PROTUTORS Tuition is a registered entity, providing supporting services to education in Singapore. Our mission is to match committed and experienced tutors who can provide high quality tuition at affordable prices. We want our students to get the most out of their tuition budget.

    We are looking for
    +  MOE Current & Ex school teachers
    +  NIE trainees
    +  Part & Full-time tutors
    +  Business and Computer specialists
    +  Music and Language teachers
    +  Other Certified tutors

    Requirements
    +  Committed and Responsible
    +  Min. University level or full Diploma holder
    +  Min. 2 years of relevant tutoring experience
    +  Excellent interpersonal & communication skills in English and/or Mandarin
    +  Must possess the relevant academic certificates.

    If you have the right experience and attitude to help our students achieve their most challenging academic achievement,
    please submit your profile confidentially at www.protutors.sg/about/careers.

    (Please do not call or SMS your profile to us. Thank you.)
