    Mystery Shopper

    GET PAID TO SHOP - Mystery Shopping is the process of visiting a store and posing as a normal customer to measure the store appearance and customer service provided.

    As a Field agent for Market Pulse International you will be responsible for providing accurate and objective feedback on the service received from each assignment/store you visit.

    You can see all assignments available in your area online and will have the flexibility to select as much or as little work in your local area as you can do. The work is 100% flexible around your timetable and ideal for students, parents, FT/PT workers looking to increase their earning potential.

    You are required to report your shopping experience back on the same day as the visit via an online survey. You will need to be honest, reliable and have great observation skills. As all results are submitted online the same day as the visit is completed you will need internet access.

    You will be paid per assignment and all payments are processed monthly.
