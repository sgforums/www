Interior Designer position

  • parapara
    20 Apr 08, 16:45

    To perform interior design work and ensure that the design development phases meet the company’s objectives. 

    To manage and oversee the production works and ensure that standard, quality and schedule of the projects are met. 

    To report progress and key design issues to executives and ensure seamless coordination with owner, consultants, sub-contractors and suppliers.

    Please note that the roles of Design Coordinators are to assist the Design Managers on the day to day running of the project.

    • 3-8 years of experience in handling commercial interiors projects with an Interior Design / Architecture Consulting firms
    • Strong aptitude, flair and creativity in space planning and design conceptualising
    • Good communication and presentation skills
    • Strong leadership qualities to lead and oversee the interior design works
    • Excellent people management and organisational skills
    • Able to use AutoCAD, and good knowledge of other relevant software will be an added advantage
    • Native Japanese or Japanese speaking is required for the Design Manager position
    • Japanese or Singaporean or PR is preferred

     

    Renumeration will be consummerated with qualification and experience.
