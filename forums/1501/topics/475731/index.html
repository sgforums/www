    EDUCATION MANAGER (FULL-TIME POSITION) @ Innovating minds Pte Ltd

    About Us

    Our program provides innovative and holistic development for children. We are committed to discovering the unlimited potential of each and every child. If you have a passion for teaching and learning on the way and if you like to work with young minds, we would love to have you on our team !

    Summary of Job Responsibilities

    As an Education Manager, you will own the operations and execution at Innovating minds, and be responsible for driving awareness and usage.

    You will have a strong understanding of marketplace dynamics, work with the team to launch new programs, liaise with partner(s) on marketing initiatives, promote campaigns and track results.

    You will also work with external agencies and vendors to ensure successful operational activities in Singapore.

    You will work closely with the team to build the business in a fast, start-up like environment. As the scope of the role grows, you will also support in launching the program across the region. 

    Requirements

    • The candidate preference is for a Singapore citizen/ PR
    • A degree in Education or related disciplines of Teaching, Counselling, Psychology, etc. and at least 4 years of experience in teaching or working with children of different ages. 
    • Experience in planning, scheduling, or coordinating is preferred.
    • Fluent in English, and excellent interpersonal and communications skills.
    • Ability to multi-task and handle various tasks and functions. 

     A competitive and attractive package will commensurate with the academic qualifications and work experience of successful applicant. 

    If you are interested, please send your resume to asmaa@innovating-minds.com. Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.
