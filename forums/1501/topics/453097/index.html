    So many foreigners these days, is your job safe? These are some interesting qns that you may want to ask yourself:

    1) Can a cheapo foreigner do your job?

    4) What happens if one day your salary reaches 10k? What makes you think a younger and hungrier grad will not replace you? Unless you are in professions where old is gold, for eg: pilot

    5) To invalidate all of the above, join the civil service. You are free from foreign competition, or if not, most of it.
