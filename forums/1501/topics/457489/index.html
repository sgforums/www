    The Big Sheila - Company Brief

    My offering is simple. I'll cook for you so you can enjoy my food at your table or at my table.

    I've created a gourmet online food ordering system so you, my people, can order lunch, dinner fill the fridge items in 2-3 clicks and have it delivered twice a day. Monday to Friday.

    Or, you can come into my place and have my scrumptious food at my table in Swan Lake Avenue, Siglap. Every day of the week.

    The love and pleasure of feeding people is what inspires me. Made fresh daily, and all on display, my food has been inherited from the love of three generations of women.

    Making simple pleasures enjoyable, all created for a life made easier.

     

    Responsibilities:

    • Timely deliveries of food boxes, drinks from the food store to offices, residential and business parks
    • Any other ad-hoc duties/ projects as assigned
    • Able to use computer and web applications to process transactions efficiently
    • Proper housekeeping and maintenance for the bikes

    Requirements:

    • Preferably resides in the East (Working location at Swan Lake Ave near Siglap)
    • 5 weekdays delivery 10.30am to 8.30pm (breaks from 2.30pm to 4pm)

    • Possess Class 2a / 2b driving license for Dispatch Riders
    • Diligent with good attitude and well presented
    • Pleasant personality and cheerful disposition
    • Clean driving records, diligent with good working attitude
    • A good team player and customer oriented
    • Physically Fit
    • Able to communicate in English proficiently
    • Delivery bikes provided. Bikes are not allowed to be brought home
    • Able to multi-task while working
    • Able to commence immediately
    • 2 full time positions available
