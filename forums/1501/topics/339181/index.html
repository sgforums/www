    Due to expansion, an established and leading marine electronics company seeking self motivated individual to join the continuous growing marine industry.

    Job Description
    * Assist sales team in handling, coordinate and follow-up on all sales enquiries
    * Provide liaison work between customer and equipment manufacturer
    * Project status reporting, monitoring and follow-up
    * Ensure proper filling and perform necessary administrative work on sales transaction and enquiries
    * To communicate and negotiate with relevant party concerning project/sales
    * Able to meet tight deadlines and prioritize tasks
    * Provide administrative support to any other assignments or tasks that may be assigned from time to time by Manager

    Requirement
    * Minimum GCE 'O'/'A' Level, Higher Nitec or Diploma in Electronics/Electrical/Mechanical
    * At least 1 year relevant experience in sales or sales support
    * Strong communication and interpersonal skill
    * Pleasant and honest personality
    * Highly resourceful, and have an extraordinary eye for details
    * Responsible, well organised and a good team player
    * Self starter and can work independently
    * Able to multi-task
    * Fresh graduate willing to learn or fast learner are welcome to apply

    Others
    * Only Singaporean or Singapore PR may apply
    * Applicant able to start immediately or within short notice will be an advantage
    * Working location @ Bukit Merah
    * 5 1/2 days work week with alternate Saturdays off

    * Female applicant preferred

    Interested applicant please email detailed resume with attached color photograph to bethtech@pacific.net.sg
