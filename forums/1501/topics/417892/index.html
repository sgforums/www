    Tuition Coordinator for Ace Tutors

     

    We are looking for honest and sociable people who enjoy helping others. We need people who put passion into what they do, whether is it in terms of interacting with our clients and tutors or is it in terms of education. We welcome prospective employees from all walks of life, including gap-year students.

     

     

    Resource Requirement (Qualifications & Experiences)

     

    • Minimum of ‘O’ level qualification
    • No working experience needed
    • Eloquent in English
    • Well organized and disciplined
    • Able to meet monthly sales target
    • Singaporeans and SPRs only
    • 2 positions available

     

     

    Job Description

     

    You are required to match our clients to suitable tutors from our database, primarily through the conduct of phone calls. Perform various basic administrative tasks. Training and guidance will be provided.

     

    • Work hours: Tuesday-Saturday (5-days), 9am-6pm
    • Basic salary of $7.50/hr weekday, $10.00/hr weekend
    • Sales target bonuses
    • Location: Shophouse along Macpherson Road (accessible by buses 8, 64, 65, 66, 90, 151, 154, 561)
    • Commitment: 5 months or longer, starting in Jan 2011

     

     

    Contact Us

     

    Should you be interested in joining us, please send your resume to our email: HR@acetutors.com.sg

    We regret to inform that only shortlisted applicants will be called up for an interview.
