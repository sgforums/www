    This is call artifically inflating the salary of employee. There's a few types of work permit in Singapore.

    R pass is for foreign worker who earns less than $1800,

    S pass is for those who must earn a minimum of $1800.

    In order to bring in these foreign workers, the employer have to artifically inflate the salary to $1800 so that the foreign worker will be able to enter singapore to work. However, the employee does not get the full sum of $1800 because of under table deals. In fact, the employer would only hire the worker for mere $800 a month. The trasaction of $1800 just reflect on paper that the employee is paid $1800 every month. But that is not the case because $1000 is transfered by to employer.
