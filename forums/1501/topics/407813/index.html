Looking for Country Club Customer Care Officer(full-time)

  • Alteva
    11 Aug 10, 13:53

    Responsibilities

    •  Sales of cash card and souvenirs and perform the necessary sales documentation work.
    • Effective management of the reception area.
    • Take reservations for the restaurants.
    • Sales of tickets for social events organized by the Club.
    • Updating members’ databank.
    • Perform mailroom function for Committee Members and Sports conveners.
    • Call sports players reminding them of matches.
    • Taking bookings for sports courts and meeting rooms.
    • Issuing of temporary membership cards for reciprocal clubs' members.
    • Tendering cheques and documents to Committee members for their signatures.
    • Attending to members’ queries and needs.
    • Issuing carpark labels.
    • Filing of notices on clubs' notice boards.
    • Undertake any other duties and projects as designated by Management.

     

    Requirements

    • GCE 'O' level.
    •  Previous experience in frontline customer service.
    •  Fluent in English.
