    I'm looking for mystery shoppers for a new cafe at Katong serving international food for quality control purposes.

    What do I have to do?
    You will be given a review checklist to fill up. You need to read the checklist thoroughly before going on your
    assignment so you know what to look out for. You will be given a date (between 1 Jun - 30 Jun) and time range for your assignment.
    You are expected to fill up the review form completely after your visit. Then, submit the review form and receipt
    via email and your meal will be reimbursed to you. You may bring up to ONE friend along with you.

    Ideal shoppers are detail-oriented, responsive and alert.

    Interested parties, please email me to register.

    What is Mystery shopping?
    Mystery shopping is a tool used by market research companies to measure quality of retail service or gather
    specific information about products and services. Mystery shoppers posing as normal customers perform specific
    tasks -- such as purchasing a product, asking questions, registering complaints or behaving in a certain way --
    and then provide detailed reports or feedback about their experiences.
