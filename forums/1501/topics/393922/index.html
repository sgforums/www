Recruitment for Web Master

  • Crescendo Publishing
    18 Mar 10, 18:45

    WEB MASTER REQUIRED FOR EXCITING MEDIA FIRM

     

     We are an integrated media company located in Singapore but with operations across the Asia-Pacific region, including Hong Kong, Australia, the Philippines, South Korea and Taiwan. Our products include print magazines as well as web-based digital media platforms.

    We are currently looking to recruit a web master to build and maintain a collection of websites.

     

    REQUIREMENTS

     

    - Only those with legal permission to work in Singapore need apply

    - Experience in website development and building required, freelance work included

    - Students looking for an internship will also be considered

     

    BENEFITS

     

    - Flexible schedule, late morning starts

    - 4 day work week initially (though this may increase later on depending on work load)

    - Competitive remuneration with CPF

    - Opportunities to develop yourself with exposure and on-job training

    - Dynamic, young and motivated team, open working culture and flat organisational hierarchy

     

    APPLICATION

     

    To apply for this position, kindly submit your CV/resume and a covering letter to crescendo.publishing@gmail.com, with the subject header WEB MASTER JOB APPLICATION

     

    Your CV should include the following information:

    - Full name

    - Date of Birth

    - Contact details including address, e-mail and mobile number

    - Nationality

    - Recent colour photo of yourself

    - Full educational qualifications including grades received in English/GP from 'O' Level onwards

     

    We will contact all successful applicants for an interview.

     

    Please note that this vacancy is available for immediate start.
