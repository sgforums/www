TAFEP -- what you need to know

  • FireIce
    14 Aug 15, 15:25

    The Tripartite Alliance for Fair and Progressive Employment Practices (TAFEP) promotes the adoption of fair, responsible and progressive employment practices among employers, employees and the general public. TAFEP is co-chaired by Mr Bob Tan, Vice President, Singapore National Employers Federation (SNEF) and Mr Heng Chee How, Deputy Secretary-General, National Trades Union Congress (NTUC). Members include employer representatives, union leaders and government officials.

    Leveraging its unique tripartite identity, TAFEP works in partnership with employer organisations, unions and the government to create awareness and facilitate the adoption of fair, responsible and progressive employment practices. TAFEP provides tools and resources, including training workshops, advisory services, and educational materials, to help organisations implement fair employment practices.

    Those who have encountered workplace discrimination are welcome to contact TAFEP for advice and assistance or to provide their feedback.
