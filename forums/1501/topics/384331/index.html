    Asking is always good, it saves a lot.

    However asking for people to match your request works only when YOU are the one in demand, in which case the negotiation ball is in your court.

    Remember, to ask the right person, ie ask to talk to the boss or whoever it is that has the authority to decide if you get the job or not.

    Oh, and before you ask, inquire if your skillsets are required and that you are able to meet their expectations.
