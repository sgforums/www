    Temasek Polytechnic

    Singapore

     

    Job Code: Tech Spec/ENG
    Job Title: Creative Specialist / Interactive Digital Centre-Asia

    Job Description:     Applicant must have a relevant degree and with a minimum of 3 years working experience in the digital interactive media related area. Candidates should have strong fundamental skills in design, possess good conceptual design, graphics design, graphical user interface design, and storyboard design skills.

    Job Responsibilities:
    1. To undertake conceptual design, graphics design, graphical user interface design, and storyboard design of a digital media production

    2. To undertake and be involved in art direction, textures, lighting, rendering and post production

    3. To be involved in creating interactive 3D visualization applications/content

    4. Knowledge of scripting would be an advantage

    Job Specifications & Minimum Qualifications:
    1. A good degree in Arts/Design/Media or related field

    2. Possess good aesthetic skill and conceptual design skill

    3. Familiar with creative graphics design and image editing software

    4. Familiar with the use of 3D animation software

    5. Ability to collaborate and work in a team on projects

    6. Good presentation and communication skills

    7. Candidate must be a hands-on person who enjoys design and digital media creation work. It will be advantageous for candidates to show a portfolio of their creative work.

    Closing Date :     29/09/2010
