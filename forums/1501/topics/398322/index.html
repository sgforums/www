    Freelance Graphic Designer x 1

    -Knowledge in output and printer production.

    -Knowledge in photography and photo retouching is preferred.

    -Strong creative flair and good art sense.

    -Strong communication skills with different parties, able to manage tight deadlines and work well under pressure.

    -Proficiency in English & Mandarin (both spoken & written) an advantage.

    -Applicants should be Singaporeans or hold relevant PR status.
