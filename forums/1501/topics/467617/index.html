    Financial Planner/Manager

     

    About Us

    Grace Chua & Associates is an agency representing Great Eastern Life. We are an agency with dedicated advisors recognized for our excellence. We build the financial futures of people by guiding them in core life areas such as protection, investment, education and retirement.

     

    Our agency is currently expanding and we are interested in working with dynamic individuals who have a strong will to succeed and are passionate in building their own business.

     

    Our experienced planners will provide mentor-ship and guidance to our new associates in fostering personal, professional and financial growth.

     

    If you are ambitious, self-motivated and are willing to work hard, a rewarding career within the financial industry awaits you.

    Responsibilities

    • Provide financial advise to corporate / individual clients in various areas
      (Training Provided)
    • Manage and develop Agent - Customer Relationship
    • Perform the role of a Life Planner, assisting in providing coverage protection & financial analysis

    Requirements

    • Self motivated and service-oriented personality
    • Strong determination to succeed
    • Candidate must possess at least GCE A level / Diploma or advance diploma and above in any field
    • Fresh Graduates/Entry level applicants as well as applicants with experience in relevant fields alike are welcomed to apply
    • Candidate must be 21 years old and above, Singaporean or PR
    • As long as you are confident and willing to learn, you are welcomed

    Job Benefit

    • Flexible Working Hours
    • Academic Training provided
    • Comprehensive Training/ Upgrade Program
    • Performance driven income & Allowance option scheme
