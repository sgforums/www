Customer Service Coordinator in Jurong East

    Happy Fish Swim School Pte Ltd

    Happy Fish is one of the fastest growing swimming school in Singapore since 2007. Today with network of over 250 passionate and certified swimming instructors, Happy Fish provides group and private swimming lessons for all ages all across the island. Happy Fish is committed in helping customers to achieve water confidence and equip them with excellent swimming skills, fast and easy.

    Customer Service Coordinator

    Responsibilities:
    • Responsible for processing of customer enquiry through emails and phone calls.
    • Liaise with customers on their sales enquiries
    • Able to generate sales from customers’ enquiries and achieve a certain target
    • Liaise with customers on fulfilment discrepancies
    • Attend to telephone calls
    • Other ad-hoc admin and simple accounting duties

    Pre-requisites:
    • Fast learner, motivated, deadline and customer oriented.
    • Team player with good integrity.
    • Bilinguals have an advantage.
    • Proficient in Microsoft Office.

    Working Location:
    • Jurong East (Walking Distance from MRT Station)

    Salary:
    • Starting Salary: SGD1200 + Bonus

    Interested applicants,
    Please send your detailed resume to:

    hr@swimminglessons.com.sg
