How to calculate overtime?

  • eien-kisu
    23 Nov 10, 21:51

    Assuming...

    • Overtime is calculated if I have worked 44 hours/week.
    • Working hours: 0930 HRS to 1915 HRS
    • 5-day work week (Mon-Fri)

     

    1) Is this correct?

    9.30am to 7.15pm, minus 1 hour for lunch, is a total of 8.75 worked hours per day.

    44 working hours/week divided by 8.75 hours per day = I have to work 5.0286 days/week to cummulate 44 worked hrs/week.

    So, I need to work the extra 0.0286 days/week then overtime will be calculated?

    0.0286 work days is 2.5025 hours.= 30.03 minutes per day.

    So I need to work 30.03 minutes extra a day, if not overtime will not be calculated.

     

    2) OR is this correct?

    Worked hours 8.75 hours per day

    8.75 * 5  working days / week = 43.75 total working hours

    44 - 43.75 = 0.25 extra hour / week

    That's 15 minutes extra per week. So to get overtime paid, leave office after 7.18pm every day?
