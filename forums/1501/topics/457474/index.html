    We're giving out a SGD$40 cash token of appreciation for involvement in a two hour social networks web-based survey pilot test. The session includes the pre-survey briefing, actual survey and post-survey feedback.

    This is applicable to Singaporeans, aged 35 to 65 years old. Do help spread the word to your family and friends who are eligible.

    Participants are invited to come by our laboratory in CREATE @ NUS University Town, during August or September.

    Questions and registration are best conducted through email (teresa.tan@nus.edu.sg OR teresa.tan@ivt.baug.ethz.ch). Thank you and have a great day!

    More details:

    The Future Cities Laboratory (FCL), funded by the National Research Foundation, is interested to conduct a study of Singaporean social network geographies. This study will be undertaken by a graduateresearcher at the National University of Singapore (NUS), supervised by a NUS professor with the support of a professor at ETH Zurich.

    The purpose of this study is to understand the social networks and social capital of Singaporeans. To this end, we will ask you about your life situation and your social contacts. To place this in a larger context, we will also ask about events in your life, your life satisfaction, work life, family life, leisure activities, national service experience, participation in voluntary organizations, identity, mobility and communication patterns. This will contribute towards academic research and could play a role in influencing public policy.

    You are invited to participate voluntarily in this study as we value your feedback as a Singaporean. We would appreciate it if you could spare us some of your precious time. We sincerely thank you for your contribution.
