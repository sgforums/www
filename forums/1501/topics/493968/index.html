    Laundry Operations Team Leader

    Laundry collection from customer premises

    Check and count quantity of laundry

    Advise customer the price of the laundry items

    Perform dismantle and installation of curtain if required

    Delivery the items back to office

    Call customer when washing is completed

    Return the laundered items back to customer after washing

    Min Secondary Education and must be able to communicate and write simple English

    Class 3 driving license from the Republic of Singapore and good driving record

    Non-smoker, hardworking and good working attitude

    Must be able to work on Weekend and public holidays

    Basic $1200 + commission + attendance allowance up to $2600 per month

    Working hours 10 am to 8 pm

    10 days annual leave

    Singaporeans only
