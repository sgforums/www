Hiring: Full-Time Research Administrator

  • CogNeuroLab
    9 Jul 13, 17:11

    Housed in Duke-NUS Graduate Medical School, the Cognitive Neuroscience Laboratory conducts scientific research on: What happens when we don't get enough sleep? How do people decide what they want to eat? How do our brains change as we age, and can we observe differences in the brains of people at risk of schizophrenia?

     

    Join our team!
    We are looking for a full-time research administrator to support our work.

    Responsibilities include:
    • Recruiting participants (e.g., through phone-calls and emails)
    • Managing the schedule of research appointments
    • Reminding participants prior to scheduled appointments
    • Interacting with participants (e.g., by meeting participants, briefing
    participants)
    • Managing participant databases and project administration
    • Ad-hoc administrative and person-interaction duties

    On the job training will be provided for these duties.

    Requirements:
    • Diploma (training in psychology will be highly favoured)
    • Excellent organisational skills
    • Excellent people skills
    • Able to commit for at least 2 years

    Expected salary:
    $1800-$2100

    How to apply:
    Candidates should send their resume and two reference reports to Dr. Ong Ju Lynn atjulynn.ong@duke-nus.edu.sg. Only short-listed candidates will be contacted.

    For more information, please visit our website: http://www.cogneuro-lab.org or our
    Facebook page: https://www.facebook.com/cogneuro.
