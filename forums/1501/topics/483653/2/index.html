    My advice to people looking for job is not to be too rush into getting a new job.

    Always consider the following:

    1) Will the job scope be what you like to do for that amount of working hours

    2) Will the location of your office be suitable for you to travel during peak hours

    3) Will the pay be enough for you to feed not only yourself but to have enough savings for your future.

    4) Will the company offer you good career advancement for the next few years

    5) Will you be confident to perform or exceed the expectation from the company

    If you rush into a job, more often than not you will find yourself dreading to work sooner than later.
