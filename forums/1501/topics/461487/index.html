    Armed Security Officer/Auxiliary Police Officers

    The Auxiliary Police plays an important role in securing our nation's assets and people. They safeguard government and commercial buildings, and maintaining law and order at major point of entry and events. 

    Job Requirements:
    • Min. 3 N levels credit (Singaporean)
    • Min. 3 SPM (Malaysian)
    • Singaporeans / Singapore PR/Malaysian
    • Physically and medically fit
    • Completed National Service for Singaporean Males
    • No criminal records
    • Normal colour vision
    • Able to perform 12 hrs rotating shift duty

    Benefits:
    • Joining bonus up to $8,200* for all Singaporean**
    • Comprehensive & extensive medical coverage for staff and dependents
    • Full training provided
    • Career Advancement


    ** Terms and conditions apply
