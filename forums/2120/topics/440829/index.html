    Cold Storage – More imported food types and specialty items from Australia, USA, Europe etc  . Will also bring in seasonal items like Egg Nog, Mince pies, Brandy Fruit Cake etc. Overall better quality even for the fresh items. Vegetables are much fresher compared to the other supermarkets (I’m using lettuce as a yardstick here). I also think that Cold Storage have a much better fruit selection compared to the other supermarkets. Overall, prices are similar to others or higher priced depending on the item.

    NTUC Fairprice – Average quality for fresh fruits and vegetables and the selection of fruits are really pathetic. Main grip is how they package their Chilled Pork. It’s usually a little bit of meat in a packet. Would rather they increase the amount by another 1-3 pces instead of paying more for the foam tray. Selection of brands are limited to their Housebrand and popular brands. To be honest if it’s not coz there is only NTUC nearest to my place, I would rather shop at Giant for fruits and vegetables

    Sheng Siong – More of air-conditioned wet market concept to me with supermarket attachment. The fresh section is large especially for seafood and live seafood. My perception is that the things are cheaper but as I don’t shop there often I can’t comment. The quality looks ok to me.
