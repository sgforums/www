    Help needy students. Donate used textbooks at NTUC supermarkets

    Here is your chance to help the needy -- simply by donating your used textbooks at any NTUC FairPrice supermarkets.

    The NTUC FairPrice Used Textbooks Programme is back in its 26th year, and has since collected 1.5 million textbooks that have gone towards helping over 110,000 needy students.

    Last year alone, NTUC FairPRice collected about 100,000 used textbooks and distributed them to about 8000 needy students.

    This year, the supermarket will be working directly with schools to help collect, sort, store and distribute the textbooks.

    The textbooks will be distributed in December.

    From now till 30 November, the public can donate make their donations of textbooks at all NTUC FairPrice supermarkets and hypermarkets situated across the island.
