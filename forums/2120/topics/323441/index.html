    NTUC Fairprice raised more than $155,000 for Sichuan earthquake

    NTUC FairPrice has raised more than $155,000 for the Sichuan earthquake which struck in May through a donation drive held at more than 200 outlets across Singapore.

    The funds raised will go towards the Singapore Red Cross China Earthquake Appeal to help victims rebuild their homes and lives.

    Red Cross donation tins/boxes were available for the public to make donations from 23 May to 14 June this year across all FairPrice outlets and Cheers convenience stores. - 938Live
