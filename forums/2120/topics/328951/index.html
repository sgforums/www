    Shop-and-Save always overcharges my 84-yr-old mum


    A STOMPer's 84-year-old mother was overcharged on several occasions and made to pay for items she didn't purchase at this store, and he wishes to remind members of the public to be careful and check their receipts before leaving.

    Taiwandonald was fuming mad the last time this happened.

    He recounted, in an email to STOMP:

    "I am writing on behalf of my aged mother, 84 years old.

    "She is a frequent customer of Shop and Save at Pasir Ris Drive 4.

    "Many a times, she told us that she was overcharged for things that she did not buy.

    "On two occasions it was just $2 and another occasion $3 over dollars.

    "those times, when she told us, it was late evening when we, family members were home from work.

    "The lapse between her claims was more than 8 hours, so we did not bother to approach Shop and Save for the difference.

    "However, on National Day, she was again charged for 5 kg of Rice that she did not buy.

    "This time we were at home. I was very angry because 5kg and all the items she bought in the pictures are too heavy for an old lady to carry and walk more than 1 km home.

    "We went back to Shop and Save , and asked for the store manager. A lady came but did not identify herself.

    "We showed her the bag of items we bought, asked her three times why my mother was overcharged but she remained silent and walked away and murmuring, " it's only S$7.95 for rice."

    "I lost my cool and shouted and her, asking her what she meant by that.

    "Just then a man came out and identified himself as the store manager. He had witnessed his staff attitude and apologised.

    "Then he said he will investigate the matter of overcharging.

    "We were refunded the difference but I did not hear any more from the management, except receiving just a call from store manager that it was just a mistake.

    "I have a concern here-- how can the staff just say "It's only $7.95" when they charged an old lady who can't see or read English well for something she didn't buy?

    "I wonder how many similar incidents have gone unnoticed.

    "Shoppers , check your receipt when you make purchases or you will be the losers."

    STOMP is contacting the store for a response. Watch this space for updates.
