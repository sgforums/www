    How do I Download Mp3s from #Mp3Junction?

    i. If you are looking for Zhou Jie Lun - An Hao, type this in the main channel window: @find Songname
    eg. @find Zhou Jie Lun An hao

    ii. To widen your search, try to use as liitle words as possible.
    eg. @find An hao

    iii. When your search is successful, the Mp3 File sharers will message you telling you the available files they have.

    iv. Copy the whole line & paste it into channel main.
    eg. !mJ-Server S.H.E - Mei Li Xin Shi Jie - 02 - Ai Ne.mp3

    v. you will get a msg saying file is on it's way or you are on which queue

    vi. To check your queue status, type @servers' nick-que
    eg. @mJ-Server-que
