    As my Camp is located near MINDEF, i often take service170 from Dunearn Road. Here are some of my findings:

    1. Most MK3 for Service170 has faulty Speedometers! Most drivers are able to speed more than 60km.

    2. The drivers of service170 is able to out run other vehicles such as Buses, taxis & vans especially in the early mornings & nite. Usual HOTSPOTS for them to do these stunts are Bukit Timah Rd near HCJC, Hillview.

    3. The drivers does CHANGE Shift outside St Francis Methodist School @Bukit Timah Rd where the S'pore Driver will take over the Bus from Malaysia Driver. The Malaysian driver will then cross over to wait for another bus to drive back to JB.

    4. Bus Conductors seems to like to check on this service. I saw 4 Bus conductors boarded the BUS at the same time and only one conducted the check.

    Above are my findings which i would like to share with the rest...
