    LEYLAND OLYMPIAN 3-AXLE/ Walter Alexander Royale
    SBS9000S-SBS9012H, SBS9014C-SBS9199C (also known as "LO3x" )

    All LO3x's have a window at the rear of the upper saloon.
    The main difference between a LO3x and a VO3x is that LO's use 2 square radiator grilles at the back while VO's use a single radiator grille.


    VOLVO OLYMPIAN 2-AXLE/ Walter Alexander Royale
    SBS7198T-SBS7203L, SBS7205G-SBS7226X, SBS7228R-SBS7250A, SBS7252U-SBS7297R (also known as "VO2x" )
    All VO2x's are non air-conditioned.


    VOLVO OLYMPIAN 3-AXLE/ Walter Alexander Royale
    SBS9200G-SBS9244E, SBS9246A-SBS9670H (also known as "VO3x" )

    There are no windows at the rear of the upper saloon (as shown) for SBS9401H to SBS9670H, while SBS9200R-SBS9400K has a window at the rear.


    VOLVO B10TL 3-AXLE/ Volgren Cr222LD
    SBS9800A-SBS9849K, SBS9888Y, SBS9889U (also known as "Volvo Super Olympian" or "VSO" )


    The newest member of SBST, SBS9889U, currently serves 147 on weekday peak hours only.
