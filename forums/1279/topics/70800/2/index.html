    The only pre-2000 buses in Singapore not fitted with TV Mobile are the Scania N113CRBs and the Volvo Mark 2s, abundant over at Pasir Ris, Tampines, Bedok, Toa Payoh, Bishan, Boon Lay, Hougang, Buona Vista, Clementi and Bt Merah. In other words, services which have 100% fleet of these buses do not have TV Mobile. However, these so-called services will have buses with TV Mobile installed deployed from time-to-time.

    Conclusively, there is no particular SBST service in Singapore which do not have TV Mobile at all times.
