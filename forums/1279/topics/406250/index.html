    Kindly let me clarify first of all, I am not working in /own any bus services. Just would like to share following info with everyone who are interested.

     

    Only untill recently I realised the convenience of taking private premium buses to work. These premium buses are operated by private bus company, and only at a low rate of $3/ $3.50 (depending which line u take) they take you to places in CBD.

    They pick up commuters in Heartland Areas and after a couple of stops they will go onto expressway and towards CBD. With fewer stop means I can reach work on time & $3 is cheaper than when i take a bus to the nearest mrt station and transit to a train.

    As many had known driving to work places in CBD is actually quite "xiong". Parking and ERPs can actually burn a hole in my pocket.

    I am not sure if they operate at all places, but for me (Hougang/serangoon nrth) I have a few buses that provide such services.

    =) hope this info benefits all ppl working in CBD areas.
