    Routes 160, 181, 372, 361, 365, and 367 are now routes 980, 961, 903, 941, 945, and 947 respectively. (Yes, then-SBS started a feeder route, 372, in Woodlands before then-TIBS came in)

    Routes 204 and 208 became, or was the basis of, route 925, I believe.

    Routes 214E and 214W later bcame 820 and 821 (not sure which became which) in the TIBS days. When they were handed back to now-SBS Transit, they got the numbers 378 and 379 (also not sure which is which). Later intergrated with a shortened route 103 (also a SBS-TIBS-SBS Transit route) and became routes 103 and 103W.

    Route 175 was shortened to Bukit Panjang and renumbered 975.

    Route 185 was intergrated with route 989 and became route 985.

    Route 59 was handed to TIBS when they took over the north-east sector. TIBS extended it to Sengkang and renumbered it to 866. When SBS Transit took over, it became route 86. The current route 59 is a reused number, and is not related to this route. Also, the current routes 180, 181, and 182 are reused numbers, and are not related to the current routes.

    You missed out on the Bukit Panjang feeders: routes 343, 344, and 345. At least one of them survives as either route 920 or 921 (not sure which), I believe.
