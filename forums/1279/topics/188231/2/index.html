    188 use to ply between Jurong Int to Jurong West area in 1980's
    History of service number 188:-

    In 1980s, this SBS service ply from Ulu Pandan to Shenton Way. Soon Chow models were used. I used to take this bus with my mum when i was young . Withdrawn in 1986-1987.

    In 1989, service 188 was introduced by SBS to ply along Jurong West area. Withdrawn again in 1990-1991.

    In March 1999, service 188 was introduced to ply between CCK and WTC. Service 10 was shortened to Kent Ridge from Clementi on tat day. Service 188 by then was a PAC service by SBS, MB0405s and LO2x were used.

    Service 188 was then transferred to Tibs in Dec 1999. Majority Hino Volgrens and 2 or 3 NAC MB0405s were used by Tibs upon it's handover. Till then, Tibs had replaced the whole fleet of service 188 with MB0405, MB0405G and Habits.

    Seems tat service 188 was improved by Tibs upon its handover...SBS had more NAC buses on service 188 than Tibs had
