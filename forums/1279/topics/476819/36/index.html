    Just to clarify on the history about the Transbus/Alexander Dennis Enviro 500 buses.Â 

    When Transbus International was formed after the merger in 2001, the old Dennis Trident 3 was still sold under the Dennis brand. In 2002, Transbus annouced the development of a new chassis based on the Trident 3, which is the Enviro 500.

    This is the Transbus Enviro 500 (which is still badged a Dennis):

    In 2004, Transbus became Alexander Dennis Limited, and the Enviro 500 received a new facelift and the Dennis badge was changed to Alexander Dennis:

    In 2012, Alexander Dennis unveiled a new generation of the Enviro 500 chassis and a new facelift design based on the Enviro 400. This chassis is known as the Enviro 500 NG (New generation) or the Enviro 500 MMC (Major model change):

    All photo credits to the original authors.

    What SMRT ordered could be known as the Enviro 500 NG or the Enviro 500 MMC, both mean the same.
