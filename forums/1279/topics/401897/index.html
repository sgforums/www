      It is what I would consider a fact. I have seen lots of busfans on facebook and i have to say, busfans are getting younger. We now have lots of primary school students who are interested in this hobby of buses. These kids socialise online on facebook and MSN. They rely on the primary source of information like BusInfoHub.com for their fix on buses.

     

      With these young bus enthusiasts around, I believe it is time that we, as a community, should tell their parents about the hobby so that the parents are aware of what their son/daughter is indulging in is not awkward because they are not the only bus enthusiast around.

     

      For me, I first went on this forum in 2007. Before that, I rely on BIH and SgWiki for my primary source of information. When CSS announced it's withdrawal in April that year, I red an article of the events during the last day for CSS. I see pictures of the bus enthusiasts, whom i managed to meet and interact with thanks to various outings and gatherings either public or private and I know that I was not alone.

     

      Then, I assumed that I was one of the youngest of the bus enthusiasts, at 14 years of age. How wrong was I. I was exposed to others who started the hobby from as back as they were young. I too started the hobby back in Year 1998, the heyday of SBS red-white liveries and TIB buses. I was 5 years old. I don't know any single thing about them in detail like model name or whatever. As I grew up and new buses emerged, I happily remembered the times where I had to beg my grandmom to let me board this bus because it is new. There was once I told my grandparents to skip a Strider on 168 in 2003 for a VSO that was behind it. During that period, the Volvo Super Olympians were new to SBST and i never ridden on one before.

     

     Ok, that's my story. What I'm saying is that there are still many young boys and girls out there who are interested in this hobby. So, I would suggest that we keep this forum free from trolls and conflict and make it a more comfortable place for them to gain information. Who knows? Our knowledge now can be useful for the future.

    In 2001, Hispano Bendy started to ply on 911. At that time, I still love to take Bendy bus. I also manage to ride it and even took picture of it. Time passes, Volgren rigid on 911 was also my favourite and I took TIB964B once on 911. 

    The best was my F1rst dream came true, A B9TL on 161. Before B9TL came to 161, VSO on 161/168 are common. I took the VSO on both the service before, and both do have the smell of the fuel. When I took 168 to bedok, I manage to spot the SBS Transit Bendy bus. Back to the topic, my first B9TL ride was on 143, where I was so happy and surprise of the look. I always hope that it would cameo on 161. 

    During the CNY, while I was going to Sengkang, instead of taking the long ride of 965, I took svc. 161. And I was lucky. A B9TL appeared infront of me, heading to the bus park. At the bus park, the only 161 bus was the B9TL. And I manage to take it! 

    Volgren on 911 also attracted me. Last time, TIB1188T & TIB1139K was the famous bus I have known. For TIB1188T slot, it sometimes replaced my TIB1190K, TIB1193C and TIB1191H.
