    Originally posted by soblur1:

    Hi there,

    Does anyone know where is the boarding point and what time the bus arrive in sembawang?

    Boarding points are along Admiralty Road West, Canberra Road and Sembawang Road. Fare ranges from $0.60 to $1.40, depending on distance. Try to be at the bus stop by 8 if you are taking from Sembawang Road, 7.30am if around Canberra Road. There are multiple buses operating 640, so there isn't just 1 time of arrival.

    Usually these buses will stop upon flagging it down. A good way is to, for a day, take 167, 856, 859 or 980 down to Sembawang Rd, flag the bus down, and ask the driver about the route.

    Another way is to wait at International Plaza and Cecil St in the evening for the bus, and enquire about its route.
