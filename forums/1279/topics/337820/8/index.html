    Hello guys, I going jb later. I not quite sure how the new bus route. Anyone can advise where to alight if going to city square and how do I board the bus from city square to get back to Singapore? Thanks.

    After chopping your passport at Iskandar complex, you may follow the road signs to JB Sentral/City Square and  walk to City Square via the covered walkway but it is quite a long distance.

     

    As for returning from City Square back to Singapore, there is an exit (yes that is the same door when you walked from iskandar complex to City Sq) in 3rd or 4th floor of city square stating 'to JB sentral' and walk through that exit and you will see some policeman blocking the way who want to see your passport before letting you advance, then followed the sign 'to woodlands' and you will see the departure hall. after chopping your passports you may go down to the bus bay and take whatever buses back to sg.
