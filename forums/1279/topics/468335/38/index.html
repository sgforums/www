    Hi there both. I believe it is not due to this... It was due to the history of bus service of Sengkang...

    If I remember correctly,
    59 - Ang Mo Kio to Jalan Kayu convert to 866
    866 - Ang Mo Kio - Sengkang
    865 - Sengkang - World Trade Centre
    864 - Sengkang - Marina Centre
    860 - Sengkang - Hougang St 21 (Loop) (Correct or not?)

    Then 82 was operated from SBS to TIBS then back to SBS Transit.
    Only service 84 was under SBS operate from Hougang Central - World Trade Centre.

    After tender of North East Line (NEL), 864, 865, 866 came under SBS.. I remember boarding 866 always from Interchange.. The bus they using also was the Volvo Olympian buses. SBS 9644 J, SBS 9645 G, SBS 9646 D & SBS 9647 B was part of the fleet (IIRC)

    Subsequently, I think it was because of the duplication under the NEL, there were merger of service and renumbering.

    864 + 84 = 80
    865 = 85 (Sengkang - World Trade Centre)
    866 = 86

    82 remains travelling from Punggol - Shenton Way. After NEL open, more rationalizatio happened.

    82 - shorten to Serangoon
    83 - I think it was introduced
    84 - haven't introduced
    85 - Withdrawn
    80, 86 - Remained
    87 - Newly introduced also
    88 - I dun remember when was introduced
    89 - I dun remember when was introduced

    81 - It was also shorten from Tampines - New Bridge Road to Tampines - Serangoon
