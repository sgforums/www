    F1 2013 edition route amendments:

    sv14,14e,16 has new route diversions this year to minimise inconvenience to commuters.
    After the Raffles Hotel bus stop at Bras Basah Rd, the bus will turn left to Beach Rd, keep right and turn right to Middle Rd, then left again to Nicoll Highway to continue its journey.

    This is a special provision(right turn to Middle Rd) during F1 period to reduce bus bunching up for rts.14,16 and the traffic snarls at Beach Rd/ Java Rd.
