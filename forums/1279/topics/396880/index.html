Why do I have to wait 1 hour for Service 502?

  • TWE
    18 Apr 10, 10:54

       A friend yesterday told me that he had to wait for 1 hr for bus service 502 to reach his bus stop at B04 jurong east avenue 1 . He said that 502 were scheduled to reach at interval of 19 to 26 mins as stated in the noticeboard . But yet on multiple evening this week esp thursday and friday night he had to wait for 502 from 45 mins on thursday to 1 hr on friday . And from what he observed , he said that between 20:35 to 21:35 he saw multiple 502 buses turning left to PIE at the junction before bus stop B04 jurong east ave 1skipping a total of 6 bus stop from bus stop B04 jurong east avenue1 to B06 at boon lay way . Resulting in a few of the passengers including my friend , an elderly malay lady and 2 teenage girls having to wait very long for the bus service 502 at bos stop B04 jurong east ave 1 . After waiting for 45 mins , the 2 teenage girls had to take a cab to their destination while my friend and the elderly malay lady had to continue to wait for the 502 to come as they had to go to work using this bus service very often not to mention the remaining passenger at the remaining 5 bus stop down the road .

      And finally when the bus 502 finally came at 21:35 my friend question the bus driver what was the interval of the 502 coming out for service , the bus driver replied that the PIE was severely jammed  . Being very familiar with the 502 route , he know that the 502 actual route never uses or even enter the PIE at any part of its journey nevertheless he ask the driver why your bus take so long to come ? The bus driver replied that " they "  ask him to come here to pick them up while other 502 driver just travel " halfway " then they hit PIE . Then trying to make the conversation short , he told the driver that he had waited at the bus stop from 20:25 to 21:35 . This the driver said he don't know . And my friend observed that when the driver reached one of celementi bus stop , the bus driver will deliberately remove the bus number plate from the front and back of the bus and whenmy friend alighted from the bus , he noticed that even the side number plate was also removed making it to be a out of service bus which proves that what the bus driver earlier said about purposely sent to fetch them were true . This happen both on thursday and friday .

     As a result of the delay of the bus , both the elderly malay lady and my friend were terribly late for work .

      Can anyone explain why this is happening ? Someone from SBST care to answer this ? My friend was fuming mad yesterday when he told me . He said that everyone are paying money for all bus service trusting it to come at usual time to send them to their destination , but this is way too much . Everyone knows that supervisor don't really entertain the excuse of bus coming late as a valid excuse for being late . How can bus driver skip bus stops as and when they like to ? Or is it because they are following instructions from someone " higher up " there whom feel that they can just " modify " any bus route to suit their own pleasures ?

      I really hope someone can take action to stop this nonsence immediately and give all of the passenger who had to wait 1 hr for the bus service a good reason as to why this is happening ? Particularly the elderly malay lady whom had to wait at a bus stop for 1 hr after that having to rush like hell to her work place after alighting from the bus .
