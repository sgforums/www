    A number of things were missed.

    33 - Not only was it just moved to Bedok, there was a time when 33 didn't ply Old Airport Rd. In 1986, there were 2 versions. One via Geylang Rd/Sims Avenue (original) and the other via Dunman Rd/Old Airport Rd. Eventually, the latter is retained.
