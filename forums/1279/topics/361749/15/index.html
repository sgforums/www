    S52
    It was mentioned earlier by mod that the buses usually off service from the opposite terminating point. This is true.

    1st bus from Bishan is 0545hrs. If its deployed from slbp the bus will depart slbp at 0458hrs. Last bus reaches Bishan at 0042hrs. Back to slbp will be 0129hrs. In both cases its possible to go full slbp.

    But look at the amdep split shift. By rule of thumb all split shifts have to return to their own depot by 2030hrs. the amdep split shift arrives at Bishan at 2008hrs. Tell me if its possible for that split shift to be under slbp?
