    Originally posted by Yongjunzer:

    does it means that now the past amdep161's bc will have to drive the other hgdep bus  service and  the amdep bus service?


    Let me clarify with you again:-

    Service 161 when introduced in 2001 was dual AMDEP and HGDEP controlled. AMDEP drivers change duties either at AMDEP or Woodlands interchange, whereas HGDEP drivers will change at HGDEP or Hougang interchange.

    Starting this week, made service 161 a full HGDEP control. I believe that AMDEP management beforehand had given service 161 drivers a choice of whether to cross over to HGDEP to continue on with service 161 or to stay on AMDEP to do other services. For planning purposes.

    After service 161 becomes full HGDEP, all service 161 drivers regardless of whether they used to come from AMDEP side or not will change shift at Hougang side. Note that the drivers whom were used to be from AMDEP side are now under HGDEP side. There's no reason that a HGDEP driver can drive either an AMDEP bus or AMDEP controlled service anymore. Those ex-AMDEP 161 drivers whom are still under AMDEP can be doing any other AMDEP controlled services but not service 161.
