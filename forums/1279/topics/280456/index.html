  • tintinspartan

    From 1 October, 859A will be introduced from 1215 am till 1250 am. This new shortroute will ply from Sembawang Int till Sembawang Vista (Bus Stop outside Sembawang Bus Int) in Yishun Direction.

    This may come from commuters complaining about 859 last bus timing being too early and this disobeys one PTC guideline: Last Bus for feeder services must bring all passengers from the last MRT Train. Sembawang MRT last train leaves at 12:35 am to terminate at Kranji. Sembawang to Yishun 859 last bus leaves at 12:00 or 12:15 am.
