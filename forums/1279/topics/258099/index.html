    Most ulu, and at the same time weird, place that is reachable by bus is probably the area around Seletar Airport, beyond the tiny passenger terminal building along route 103W's route in the direction in. The true looping point of route 103W is in the service road/carpark area of some squat and lonely single-story building housing some aviation companies; beyond the area is the airport apron and a track that seems to lead to nowhere. I had cycled there before, and I still remember seeing SBS Transit buses (including a Strider once) going through that road.

    Very interesting surroundings to capture an on-route SBS Transit bus on photograph.
