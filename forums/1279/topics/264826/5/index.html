    Lorry smashes into bus in Loyang accident



    STOMPer Yusf got the shock of his life when a lorry smashed head-on into the back of the bus he was travelling in. He was commuting in SBS bus 19 along Loyang Avenue at about 3.30 pm on Sunday (15 July) when it happened. This impact resulted in the lorry being dragged a few metres by the bus.

    This accident was described as 'traumatic' and 'shocking' by Yusf. He shares with STOMP, "I'm not sure how it happened. I just suddenly heard a loud bang, then this lorry was dragged a few metres together with the bus."

    The 35-year-old operation assistant says that even though no one from the bus was hurt, he saw that the lorry passenger had his leg stuck under the dashboard.

    The police told STOMP that they received a call at about 3.30 pm that day. They confirmed that the lorry passenger suffered leg injuries and was conveyed immediately to Changi General Hospital.

    The cause of the accident is under investigation.
