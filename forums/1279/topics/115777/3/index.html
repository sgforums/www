    Originally posted by iveco:
    By the way, are there any more DDs serving on SLBP feeders? I remember LO2Xes were predominant on 240 and 334 in years gone by.
    There are DDs still serving on SLBP feeders but none of them are perm on those services like in the past with the LO2x. They are cameos which complements the services during certain periods. 242 gets LO3x from either 199 or 179 during the weekends and peak hours. Sometimes a VO2x is used on peak hours but this is rare. Also rare is a VO2x on 243. I never saw 240 with a DD for a long time. As for 334, well, there is the occasional appearance of a DD. So, in short, 242 is the one which gets DDs regularly.
