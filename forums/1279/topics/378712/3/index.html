    The best way for you to get home is to take the Service 2 that departs at 2309hrs from Changi Village terminal. However it could not connect you to the train at Tanah Merah unless you take the bus at 2257hrs.

    If you take the 2257hrs trip, you alight at Tanah Merah and take the train all the way home. You will reach Tanah Merah at 2323hrs and the last train departs at 2331hrs.

    If you are on the 2309hrs trip, alight at Clarke Quay Central shopping Mall, cross the road and wait for NR3. You should reach Clarke Quay at around 0017hrs.
