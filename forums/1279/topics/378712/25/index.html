    Try taking 22>5 or 22>59 much faster.

    22>29 - you go all the way to tampines, and then 29 roams around in Tampines streets for quite some time before it gets to Old Tampines Road.

    22>5 - change at Kaki Bukit or further stops on Bedok Reservoir Road. Get down at Old Tampines Rd / Upper Changi Rd Junction, and walk for 10 mins. Total travel time: (not more than 90 mins)

    22>59 - change at Kaki Bukit or further stops on Bedok Reservoir Road. Get down at junction of Loyang Ave and Upper Changi road, and walk for 10 mins. Total travel time: Less than 90 mins.

    You also have the option of changing into 2 buses at Kaki Bukit and that will make the transfer faster too. Again, both have a good # of DDs deployed, so you should get a seat.
