    Originally posted by TIB1182J:

    Chinatown to Marina Bay Sands? (does MBS have free shuttle to MRT like RWS?)


    Yes.

    Pickup Points at bus stops at:
    1. People's Park Complex (05013)
    2. Central (Clarke Quay Stn) (04222)
    3. Opp Bugis Junction (01112)
    4. Suntec Convention Ctr (02151)

    Departures are at half-hourly intervals from MBS Coach Park, but its prone not to be in operation unlike the Orchard Route.

    The third shuttle plies HarbourFront calling at 14121 and 14129. (Seah Im FC & HarbourFront Ctr)
