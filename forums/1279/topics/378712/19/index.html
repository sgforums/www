    Originally posted by Bonk:

    Yes, I need some advise.

    My medical screening is on the 19th of September but I would like to know beforehand how to get to CMPB from Bishan Interchange/MRT.


    From Bishan interchange, take service 57 direct to Depot Rd.

    For a faster journey,

    1. take service 54 opposite Bishan MRT, alight at Scotts Rd Far East Plaza, from here, you can transfer to service 5, alight at Bukit Merah interchange, lastly transfer to service 57 to Henderson Rd or Depot Rd OR transfer to service 132, alight outside Redhill MRT, transfer to service 145 to Henderson Rd.

     2. take service 156 outside Bishan MRT to Whitley Rd, transfer to service 153 to Bukit Merah interchange, lastly transfer to service 57 to Henderson Rd or Depot Rd.
