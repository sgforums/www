    Originally posted by ahnoob:

    what's the fastest / cheapest way from toa payoh lor 1 to chevrons in jurong east?

    If you live near Trellis Towers then walk to Jln Toa Payoh take 105 direct (45-50min). Costs $1.31. Taking 985 (12min) xfer 52 (20min) costs $1.62. () indicates in-vehicle journey time.

    If you live near Braddell Rd then take 157 from Braddell Rd (near Bishan Rd) to Bt Timah 1st stop (12min), xfer 154 to Clementi MRT (20-25min), xfer 52/105 (6-8min). Total cost $1.71.

    Or take a bus to Toa Payoh Int change 105 direct simply add $0.19 to $1.31 = $1.50. Or 231 to Oleander Tower walk to Trellis Towers take 985 then 52 will cost you $1.81.
