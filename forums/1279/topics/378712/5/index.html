  • tanjun
    14 Jan 10, 22:32

    136 goes one big round along AMK Ave 1 plus another big round at Serangoon Gardens. So, 136 is probably not the choice for you if you want the fastest route.

    86 is rather fast but its freq is lower than 88 coupled with the fact that you need a 10 min walk.

    So, if you want a direct bus, take 88. If you want the fastest route, you can try taking 74 and transfer to 88 along Hougang Ave 8 (along Montfort sch) if service 74 bus comes first

    Or 132 to Upper Serangoon Rd which is 2 stops after Hougang Stadium and transfer to 136.
