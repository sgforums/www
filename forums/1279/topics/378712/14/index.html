    Originally posted by StressSia:

    What is the shortest(least travelling time) route from Toa Payoh to MINDEF?

    Be it trains or buses or a combination of both.

    U got posted to MINDEF?

    Here's the route I took for 1.5 years.

    Morning:

    Go to bus stop opp trellis towers, take 966 or 985, whichever comes 1st. If 966, go to pending stn, change 184/700 to BPJ int, then 177. If 985, go to bt timah road outside the food centre, change 67, 170, 171, 961. Walk in to pass office and then take shuttle bus up the hill.

    Alternatively, take 105, 151, 153, 154 from opp Trellis Towers to Tanglin CC, cross the road and change 190/700 to BPJ, then 177.

    Evening:
    Wait for 177 at the bus stop on top of the hill. Take whichever direction comes 1st.

    If towards BPJ, alight 1 stop before interchange and change 190 to whitley road, cross road and take 105/153. (Faster)

    If towards bukit batok, alight outside The Petals along Hillview Ave, walk to bus stop Bt Batok Ave 3 and take 985 (Slower)

    Fastest record is about 40 mins. Usually takes 50-60 mins (incl waiting time)

    After some time, you should be able to guess the 177/985/966 timings so just adjust the bus combination accordingly.
