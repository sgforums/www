    The 25 cents is a rebate given to reduce the cost of making transfers (changing from one bus to another bus, or changing in between bus/MRT/LRT).

    Taking buses of the same service number consecutively is not considered transfer (you don't transfer to the same bus number right?).

    If you are running errands and spend only a short time at the destination, you may choose to another service number back to enjoy the 25 cents rebate (note that the time lapse between alighting and boarding should be within 45 mins, after which transfers rebate are not applicable). Of course, this can only work if you have alternative services to choose from.
