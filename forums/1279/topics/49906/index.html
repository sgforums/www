3 NR services to operate daily

  • off_service
    24 Jun 03, 23:27
    From 1 July 2003, NR services NR1, NR5 and NR7 will operate daily.

    Sundays to Thursdays (11.30PM - 3.30AM, 1 directional)

    NR1 - Marina Centre Terminal to Woodlands Road

    NR1 will be extended to ply:

    Yishun Ave 5
    Sembawang Road
    Canberra Road
    Admiralty Drive
    Woodlands Ave 7,6,5,4
    Marsiling Drive, Road
    Woodlands Road

    NR5, NR7

    *NR5 - Marina Centre to Jurong West

    The route of NR7 remains unchanged.

    Fridays, Saturdays and Eves of Public Holiday (11.30PM - 4.30AM, 2 directional)

    All NR services will operate as usual.

    *NR1 will ply between Yishun Interchange and Suntec City.

    *NR8 will ply between Marina Centre and Bukit Batok.

    SMRT SN services

    SN10, SN20 and SN30 have been re-branded as NightRider services.

    For more information, please visit Tibs website.
