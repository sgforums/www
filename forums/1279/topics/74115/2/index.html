  • The_Bus_Guide
    12 Apr 04, 12:24
    Originally posted by SBS9818A:
    How long did Sv. 147 terminate at Potong Pasir and where exactly was the terminal?
    147 was amended to Potong Pasir around 1984. Potong Pasir Terminal had 2 services, the other being 142 which went to Toa Payoh (142's route back then was the reverse of today. It looped at Toa Payoh Interchange). Not sure when 142 was brought over to start from Toa Payoh. Anyway, the terminal ceased operations when 147 was extended to Upp S'goon Ter in the early 1990s.

    Both 147 and 142 used the green plate and white plate system. Although the terminal was located at Ave 1, inbound buses went round the estate via Ave 1, Ave 3 and Ave 2. Ditto for outbound buses. I have a chance to see the terminal, albeit only once. It was a simple roadside one with just a small control room and a typical bus shelter.
