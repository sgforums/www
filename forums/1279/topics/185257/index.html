    So, do you actually dream about buses? If so, then what are they like?

    I confess: I do dream about buses, and since young too . Usually, I dream about SBS (and later, SBS Transit) buses serving the Yishun and Sembawang areas. And it's not just like the case of route 39 or 85; the buses actually detour into those two places - and the route numbers sometimes are also numbers of existing routes . I once dreamed about Volvo Olympians running along Yishun Ring Road, and Volvo MK4 DM3500 buses along Sembawang Drive .

    There is this particular one though, which I still remember quite well and quite freaked me out when I recalled about it the other day. The night after returning from a walk in the Marina South area several months or about a year ago (can't remember exactly when), I had a dream about a new bus route in the Marina Bay area that served a new development (I did not know the nature of that development though) in Marina South. The route number the bus had in my dream was 402.

    ...

    Bells ringing yet?

    And this was way before I knew about the new pier at Marina South, or it's opening date, and definitely way before anything about the bus route.
