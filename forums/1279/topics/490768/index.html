    13 Jan 16, 19:46

    I know it is quite a waste of time to catch buses.

    I would like to appeal to everyone to update this thread, as and when you board a bus that is NOT the common ones (Scania KUB single decker, MAN single decker, Volvo B9TL, ADL Enviro 500).

     

    It should be coincidental that you happen to catch these buses. I do not want to waste your time.

     

    As and when you happen to board a bus model that is unique, do update this thread with the following information:

    - date.

    - bus service number.

    - bus license plate number.

    - time when the bus is at a landmark (eg. MRT Station, Shopping Mall, Community Centre, etcetera.)

    - name of landmark.

     

    Likewise, when you happen to board a bus (any bus model) that has special interior design (e.g. interior filled with Hello Kitty wallpaper), do update this thread about which bus service it is on, what time it was near (or next to) a landmark and the name of the landmark.

    After all, this thread was created with the objective of sharing details on where and when to catch buses. If details are updated here, it would make it easier for one to catch buses and try them out.

    Thank you.
