    Let's list all the free shuttle buses (available to the Public) in Singapore so that one knows when he can use it

     

    • The most famous one: IKEA Shuttle

     

    • Bedok MRT<>Ikea Tampines
    • Tampines MRT <>Ikea Tampines
    • Pasir Ris MRT <> <>Ikea Tampines (Weekend & PH only)
    • Seng Kang / Punggol <> <>Ikea Tampines (Weekend & PH only)]

     

    • Parkway Parade Shuttle (Sun & PH only)
    • PP<>Bedok
    • PP<>Sengkang
    • Dhoby Gaut MRT <> Marine Cove (discontinued) (by far the most useful shuttle for me but too bad I could only go on it once and it got discontinued soon after  )

     

    • Costa Sands Resort <> Downtown East <> Pasir Ris MRT

    -> Parkway Parade is not only sundays, infact is Weekends and PH.

    PP<-> Bedok via:Marine Parade CentraI, Marine Parade Road

    then may spilt: Method 1)Amber Road, then one small road, then go on ECP, then         

                                               Bedok South Ave 1, then normal route back to Bedok
                                            2)Roundabout then Marine Parade Road then Bdk Sth Ave 1 

    PP<-> Sengkang via:Marine Parade Central, Marine Parade Road, Amber Road, one small road, then ECP, KPE, TPE, svc 27 route to Sengkang Interchange, but will turn right before the bus stop near SKG Interchange. Then got one bus stop and stop there.
