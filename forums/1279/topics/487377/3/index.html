    SMRT will be introducing three new Wheelchair Accessible Bus (WAB) Services - bus services 176, 188 and 990 - to its existing service routes from 22 March 2015. This brings the total number of WAB services to 45.

    Please refer to the attached poster or visit http://www.smrt.com.sg/Journey-with-Us/Travel-Information/Accessibility for more details on WAB services.

    Passengers may contact SMRT Customer Relations at 1800-336-8900 from 7.30am to 6.30pm on weekdays (excluding public holidays) or visit www.smrt.com.sg for more information.
