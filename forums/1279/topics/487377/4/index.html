    SMRT extends some train, bus services on eve of Labour Day

    SINGAPORE: SMRT train services on the North-South, East-West and the Circle Line, together with the Bukit Panjang LRT, will be extended on the eve of Labour Day, Apr 30, SMRT announced on Friday (Apr 24). Selected bus services will also be extended.

    On the North-South and East-West Lines, last trains towards Jurong East, Marina Bay, Pasir Ris and Joo Koon stations will depart City Hall MRT station at 12.30am. The last train towards Marina South Pier MRT station departs City Hall station at 12.14am.

    On the Circle Line, the last train towards HarbourFront MRT station will depart Dhoby Ghaut MRT station at 11.55pm. The last train towards Dhoby Ghaut MRT station will depart HarbourFront MRT station at 11.30pm.

    As for the Bukit Panjang LRT, passengers on the last northbound train can connect to the last Bukit Panjang LRT train at 1.31am.

    Bus service hours have also been extended at Bukit Batok, Choa Chu Kang, Sembawang, Woodlands and Yishun Interchange. Passengers on the last trains can connect to the following bus services on May 1:

    • Bukit Batok Interchange: 173A, 189A, 941, 945 and 947 at 1.40am
    • Choa Chu Kang Interchange: 300, 301, 302 and 307 at 1.30am
    • Sembawang Interchange: 859A at 1.35am
    • Woodlands Interchange: 901, 911, 912 and 913 at 1.30am
    • Yishun Interchange: 804, 812 at 1.45am

    -- CNA
