    18 Dec 09, 23:37

    Some other popular redeployments of the year 2009:

    CNGs redeployed to operate on short haul services

    So no more CNGs on 66, 93 and 143. However, there was a silver lining, and in this case, 198 received CNGs (x-over from 243).

    Changing Hands: DM3500 and Strider swaps between HG, BN, BB and AR

    To prepare for the removal of the Mark 3s from revenue service, in particular those on cross-border routes  160 ,  170  and  170X .

    More Scanias, less non-WAB buses

    The Scania K230UBs, especially the newer EEV batch (or I call it the 3rd batch), removed typically any non-WAB bus. Merc O405s were especially the subjects of these redeployments as their deregistration dates are drawing near.

    Looking back in the year of 2009,

    May 2009: Dereg of all the Scania N113CRBs.

    May 2009: Introduction of 7 WAB services for SMRT.

    May and Oct 2009: Mass transfer of Batch 1 Scania K230UBs from HGDEP to SLBP.

    Jul 2009: Abolishment of CityShoppers' services

    Jul - Oct 2009: Arrival of Batch 2 Merc OC500LEs for SMRT.

    Jul - Dec 2009: Mass lay-up and dereg of Merc 0405s with the introduction of Euro 5 Scania K230UBs.

    Aug 2009: Removal of perm Volvo MK3 fleets from services 100, 109 and 163 with the introduction of Euro 5s into HGDEP.

    Aug 2009: Introduction of 1st China made Yutong bus (SMB135E) into SMRT's fleet.

    Aug - Sept 2009: Introduction of overnite parking for SBST

    Oct 2009: Mass dereg of laid-up SMRT's Nissan UDs and ELBO Scanias.

    Oct 2009: 1st OC500LE to be deployed to WLDEP.

    Oct 2009: Introduction of ADDs for service 89e

    Nov - Dec 2009: 16 more WAB services for SBST.

    Nov - Dec 2009: Mass transfer/swop of Volvo MK4s between HG, BN with AR/BB.
