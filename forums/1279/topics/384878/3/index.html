    Most memorable New Introduction: SMB 0135E on Service 854 (20 Aug)

    Fortunately for me, I ended my only Prelim paper early on that day, so when I came home and checked the forums for updates, indeed I received the notification early. That was despite the fact that no one had informed me prior to this.

    I took this bus twice on the same day; the first one was from Hougang Ave 3 to Bedok and the next was from Yishun to Bedok. I took my photos outside Yishun and that was the only time I also managed to snap my only EB88 photo (SBS 0466R).

    Most Memorable Event: Withdrawal of Remaining Cityshoppers (26 Jul)

    Having a little bit of time to spare, I quickly rushed down to Orchard Road (at the junction with Handy Road) to take some final shots of the Cityshoppers on their final day. Unfortunately though, I only managed to snap them with their 'A' plates on given the time limit that I had.
