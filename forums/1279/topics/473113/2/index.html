    New Bus Service 904 From 18 August 2013

    Press Release  •  Aug 12, 2013 15:00 SGT

    12 August 2013 – A new bus service – Service 904 – will be launched on Sunday, 18 August 2013. The new service will provide better connectivity for commuters travelling between the Woodlands Regional Centre to Woodlands Drive 73 and Woodlands Crescent. This is the 13th bus service rolled out under the Bus Service Enhancement Programme (BSEP).

    The new loop service will ply along Woodlands Avenue 7, Avenue 6, Drive 62, Drive 73, and Woodlands Crescent before returning to Woodlands Regional Bus Interchange. It will enhance connectivity to key transport nodes such as Woodlands and Admiralty MRT stations, and Woodlands Regional Bus Interchange where commuters can transfer to other bus services or rail lines to reach their destinations. Service 904 will also enhance local links by providing connections to key amenities such as the Woodlands Galaxy Community Centre and Admiralty wet market, and also serve schools such as Riverside Primary School, Admiralty Secondary School and Greenwood Primary School.

    Vice President for SMRT Buses Ltd, Tan Kian Heong said: “We are pleased to roll out Service 904 which presents an additional travel option for Woodlands residents. This addition to our existing fleet will bring us closer towards enhancing commuters’ travelling experience.”

    Bus Service 904 will operate daily from 5.30am to 1.00am.

    Passengers may contact SMRT Customer Relations Centre at 1800-336-8900 from 7.30am to 6.30pm on weekdays (excluding public holidays) or visit www.smrt.com.sg for more information on Service 904.
