    Hi everyone,

    My website had been updated.

    For the first time, SSB buses are featured. As I didn't have enough time, I did the page in a shortcut way, by not including descriptions by model. Only an introduction on SSB, followed by photos with captions.

    For Tibs, I added a fleet summary page and a nostalgia page. The summary page contains photos of all the different models of buses, the range of registration numbers, the original fleet size, and the year of introduction. The nostalgia page contains photos of all known bus models which Tibs had operated, other than the OF1417 leased from SBS. Even though TIB302L is not confirmed a R87 yet, I'll assume it is for the time being. I'll send the page to Mr Tan for editing.

    Now that I know all Tibs Scanias are L113CRLs, I had "integrated" the former Scania L113CRB page into the L113CRL page, with no additions of photos - just some changes to the text. I had also followed Mr Tan's advice PARTIALLY to change part of the commentaries and captions for the O405 and O405G pages.

    Under the "special" section, I added a photo of TIB700X with its original front and at the same time, removed two shots of the bus after the accident. The number of photos for the PA Lances had been increased from 3 to 8.

    I had also finished with moving my pages and photos around. Now all htm files are under sg_bus, while full size photos are under sg_bus_2 or sg_bus_3. I think this is more organized and I also hope that this will reduce the occurance of exceeding the data transfer limit.

    I won't have any more time to pursue my interest and this will very likely be my last update until November.

    Last but not least, I would like to thank Team 9600K for allowing me to use some of their photos and to find photos of old Tibs buses for me.
