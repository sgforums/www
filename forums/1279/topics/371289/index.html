    The fare cap of $1.90 was OFFICIALLY withdrawn effective the working day of 1st October 2008.

     

    Unofficially, the farecap still exists. The difference between this and the unofficial farecap is that you cannot claim the trips that begin two hours after the first trip, nor trips after the 4th.

     

    The old system allowed you to claim the non-automated fare cap trips. So in the old days, if you took 20 trips (with valid transfers within 45 minutes) and it cost you $13, you could claim $11.10 back from SBSTransit.
