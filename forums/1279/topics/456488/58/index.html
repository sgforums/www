    Hi,

    SBS2000M - SBS2004C and SG1001 - SG1010 is part of SBS Transit's 3rd batch order of 250 Mercedes-Benz Facelift Citaros, paid for by the Land Transport Authority (LTA).

    Here is a breakdown of the current registered batch 3 buses:

    SBS6866E - SBS6893B: Fully owned and operated by SBS Transit
    SBS6550R - SBS6599B, SBS2000M - SBS2004C: Owned by LTA, operated by SBS Transit
    SG1001D - SG1010C: Owned by LTA, operated by Tower Transit under the Government Contracting Model (GCM).

    As these buses were ordered by SBS Transit, they feature SBS Transit specifications for the interior, despite some of them going under LTA's ownership.
