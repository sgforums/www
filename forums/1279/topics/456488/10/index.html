    I'm not sure if anyone noticed, but some of the Citaros are suffering from turbo boost issues already..

    It's nothing to do with the amount of pressure the BC use to floor, cause in case I've seen, it's relatively the same. It's the way the turbo boost propels the Citaro that is different, especially significant from 2nd gear to 3rd gear & 4th. 1st gear to 2nd gear turbo boost doesn't do anything much in any Citaro (that change is like Mk4 ZF shifting from 1st to 2nd).

    I sat on 6127E (HG 151) & 6132P (HG 158) lately on 109. Driven by the same BC IIRC, both times he was late, but the way 6127E moved even with turbo boost (by that sound) was really lethargic compared to 6132P. It's like 6127E had the turbo sound, but the speed gain is much slower compared to 6132P. Hmm.
