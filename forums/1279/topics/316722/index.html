    Hi all! (Updated as of 7/5/2008, 10am)

    Following the gathering & the activity for the month of May on Labour Day, there will be a very exciting Amazing Race for all of you here to take part in and enjoy the race, and at the same time, getting to know one another in this big family of Bus Enthusiasts more better and foster stronger bondings among one another.

    For this coming Amazing Race during the June holidays, it will be adopted based on the series of Amazing Race shows as shown on Channel 5, instead of the usual way that we had for our schools or external parties like SMRT (Bus.MRT.Walk) and SBST (Buzz My Day). Promise you guys here that it will be a great and enjoyable day for people who join in for this race.

    The details that you guys need to know are as follow:-

    1. Meeting venue: Youth Park @ Orchard (the place where the LO2x red bus is)
      (in case of wet weather, it will be at Somerset MRT Station, near the control room area there.
    2. Meeting time: 8:30am
    3. Tentative time it will end for this activity: 7:00pm
    4. Items that you need to bring:
      • Handphone (for contacting)
      • Water Bottle (for hydration)
      • Unbrella (for hot or wet weather)
      • Camera (required for race)
      • Ez-link with concession (BCP) or a lot of money stored inside the card.
      • Money for lunch and dinner
    5. Programmes for the day:
      • Ice-breaker
      • Allocation of teams
      • Rules and Safety briefing
      • Race start! (with lunch break in between, so DO not worry about not having lunch and OTOT settle your lunch yeah! we have arrange a period of time for you guys, so just race with the flow.)
      • Debrief & Announcing of winning team
      • Dinner (for those who are NOT going back home or going out with your own outside friends for dinner)

    Anyone interested in this activity, please PM or e-mail your particulars (i.e. your full name, e-mail address if you are not using your own e-mail account, and contact number) to XY (ghoby107) ASAP. Your particulars will be kept P&C (Private and Confidential). Please also indicate whether you have any medicial history (you have to declare it if you have, for safety issue) and also whether you have digital camera/DSLR for the race. (bring it if you have for the race)

    So guys (and gals if there's any gals who are interested as well), what are you waiting for? An exciting activity not to be missed!
