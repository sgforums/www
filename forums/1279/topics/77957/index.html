    Deserve but do not have:

    Sv 19 - Packed to the brim during morning peak. Quite popular with residents along Tampines Ave 7 / Loyang Ave as it is the most direct link to Tampines Int. Plus its frequency beats Sv 81 anytime. Sometimes loads are even better than Sv 9.

    Sv 24 - Loads are good even though its off peak, they should cut it from the airport so they can put ADDs on it. Maybe Ang Mo Kio - Pasir Ris. Even if they do not cut it, it deserves better buses than O405s only.

    Sv 27 - Excellent loads the whole day especially from Tampines - Sengkang, they should really cut it Hougang Ctrl - Tampines so they can put ADDs on it.

    Sv 36 - Really needs ADDs, but too bad because of Changi Airport. They should make a TPY II at the airport!

    Sv 38 - It gets overloaded when sec school lets out, esp on Fridays when both pri & sec are released at the same time. Other services never get overloaded.

    Sv 131 - Popular among Telok Blangah residents, St Michael's residents, and the people at Owen/Rangoon/Kent Rds...but got height restriction at Henderson Rd?

    Got but do not deserve -

    Sv 89 - Does not deserve Tridents, and loading does not shoot up too much, plus theres already Sv 88 from the Sengkang - Pasir Ris market (where it makes most of its $$$).

    Sv 165 - Heard that loading is not too good.
