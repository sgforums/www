    History Of Double Deckers:

    The double-deckers first began in 1977 when 20 LA were shipped in to Singapore. Some of them were bodied by BACO and they were put on route 86, a semi express route connecting from Tampines to the CBD area.

    35 Years of Leyland Double deck history, 35 years of double deck history.

    We have the new Wright Eclipse Gemini 2 on the roads and we'll be seeing another 550 of them by 2015.
