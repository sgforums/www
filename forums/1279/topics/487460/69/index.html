    Anyway, I don't really understand why there are certain people who like to revert here & there with regards to putting in the SP BSEP buses under the SP Buses fleet. 

    Some kids have been pointing out whatever Section 6 Guidlines blah blah on Wiki that says that BSEP buses cannot be listed as Spare buses. But with depots like BNDEP & HGDEP hoarding over 20+ BSEP buses as Spares & typically deploying them on non-BSEP slots, is it high time to reconsider that guideline? 

    And the best part is, I didn't change those Spare BSEP buses' statuses to something like 'BNDEP/HGDEP SP' etc on their respective BSEP Wrights/Citaro pages; I left them as the 'BNDEP/HGDEP' only. Because to respect that guideline on Wiki.
    But i placed them under the Spare Buses page, to reflect the sheer amount of BSEP buses used typically as Spares nowadays. Just look at BNDEP's SP fleet size on Wiki change from 78 buses to only 63 buses when you take away the SP BSEP buses.

    It's becoming massively annoying to see something that I've done in hope to benefit the whole community with regards to clearer info on Wiki, & then get pinpointed out every now & then by certain kids who have lesser years in this hobby. 

    Would be glad if some wise person can advise on what to do to solve this issue. Either create a separate page to account for those BSEP SP buses, or to re-look at those guidelines on Wiki to keep up with the times. Time to look at things to benefit the entire community instead of dishing out whatever guidelines.
