    Originally posted by k230ubmoderator:

    can anyone tell the difference between batch 1 ,2,3  vo3x


    Those batches of VO3xs r produced at different years.

    Batch 1: SBS9200G to SBS9400X earliest VO3x

    Batch 2: SBS9401T to SBS9600K (produced in 1996)

    Batch 3: SBS9601H to SBS9670H (produced in 1999 to 2000)

    Physically, Batch 3 model is same as batch 2 but they dun hv the old advertisement holders on both sides of the bus. However, some of batch 2 buses had removed advertisements alr except some of BNDEP buses.

    Furthermore, the total capacity 4 batch 2 is: 130

    Batch 3 is: 139

    They were meant 2 replace old NAC buses (SBS70**)
