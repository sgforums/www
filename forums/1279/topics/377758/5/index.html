    I am still waiting for answers from LTA...LTA is the agency who designed the fare stages system as well as the EZlink readers. The Ezlink card reader displays fare stages correctly if the VLS (Vehicle Location System) is in Auto mode.

    Fare stages would then be updated accurately and hence there will not be error in fare deductions. The problems now are, that the fare stages shown could be inaccurate if the VLS sub-components malfunctions intermittently, or when the bus driver switches it to manual update mode. So..it is only fair that the correct fare stags are made known to passengers at the bus stop prior to boarding the buses.
