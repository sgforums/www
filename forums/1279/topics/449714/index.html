    BUS CRASHES AT JOHOR BAHARU CIQ, KILLING TRAFFIC COP ON DUTY

    Bernama Media – Fri, Mar 23, 2012 8:46 PM MYT

    JOHOR BAHARU, March 23 (Bernama) -- A policeman was killed when he was hit

    by a tourist bus from Singapore while manning the traffic at the Customs,

    Immigration and Quarantine (CIQ) Complex here last night.

    Corporal Ismayuddin Ismail, 50, died on the spot in the 11.55pm incident.

    Johor police chief Datuk Mohd Mokhtar Mohd Sharif said the bus with a

    Singapore plate number was believed to have lost control and crashed into a

    lorry and a car.

    "It then rammed into the road barrier before hitting our personnel," he told

    reporters after visiting the family of the deceased policeman at Felda Taib

    Andak Kulai here today.

    It was learnt that after the incident, the bus driver, a 49-year-old local

    woman, tried to flee but was arrested 150 metres from the spot.

    -- BERNAMA
