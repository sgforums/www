    In 2014, RapidKL announced a placing of order for 80 Alexander Dennis Enviro 200s for fleet expansion and upgrading.

    A year later, the first of these orders came into service on Tempatan (Local Feeder) routes under the Cheras Depot route group.

    These Enviro 200s are manufactured as Complete Knock Down (CKD) kits shipped from UK, with assembling done up by DefTech Malaysia at it's plant in Pekan, Pahang. 

    As of posting of video, these buses can be found running on T433, T424 as well as occasional appearances on T417.

    Specification

    Length: 10.4 m

    Engine: Cummins ISBe engine

    Gearbox: Voith 4-speed

    Seats: Vogelsitze

    Doors: Ventura

    EDS (Display Signs): Lumiplan

    Total Order: 80 units
