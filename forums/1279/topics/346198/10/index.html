    Originally posted by lifelikedrama:

    Double posting!!! But anyway, why is there a Crown Relief Duty? Was there an event? Or because of weekend?


    Crowd Relief to Dolphin Lagoon on Weekends. People would always go Dolphin Lagoon to see the pink dolphins. There's also crowd relief from Dolphin Lagoon to Siloso Beach/Imbiah Lookout and Beach Station after the pink dolphins show. Buses that usually does this relief service are B7R Coaches, B7RLE Open Tops (additional for Red Line) and some crossover from Woodlands Transport during Public Holidays.
