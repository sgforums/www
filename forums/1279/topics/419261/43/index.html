    I just took my first long-haul ride on 7888K a week ago on 25, the previous time was a short 2 bus-stop ride, and I must say I love this bus as much as the WEG B9TL.......

    Will SBST consider Scania DD? I hope so.

    The interior is decidedly Gemilang style, very simple brown interior, but it feels more solid than the flexing K230UB body. Very little vibrations, nice aircon, seat is wider, but not necessarily more comfy, and smoother and more powerful than the WEG B9TL and aesthetically from outside looks more fanciful. It feels like the super smooth K230UB EEV minus the jerks from the engine braking....probably because as a DD, it is less affected by the jerks. Suspension is alright.
