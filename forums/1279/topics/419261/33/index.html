    Based on my observation,

    (a) Buses doing Premium 588 will xover to RWS8 STAFF after completion of Premium 588

    (b) Two buses doing Premium 531 will xover to RWS8 & RWS8 STAFF; one each respectively after morning trip. As for evening, both buses will do RWS8 STAFF from 1600-1800 hours and went to do Premium 531 before OFF.

    For (a), random bus will do that Premium 588 slot. Most of the time is TIB907S/ random OAC Merc.

    For (b), Perm bus for RWS8 STAFF/Premium 531 is SMB147X. Bus for RWS8/Premium 531 can be any random bus.
