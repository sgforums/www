    A quick summary for those who are interested to know more about the issue,

    There are 3 services (and 2 short trips) into and out of NTU.

    179 - serves boonlay mrt, west st 63 pioneer mrt, pioneer north rd, ntu north & south spine.

    179A (AM) - express from boonlay mrt only to ntu north & south spine direct. last bus at 9.30am

    179A (PM) - express from ntu north & south spine to boonlay mrt only, additional downroute trips from bus stop after north spine to cater to those who cant board the bus as the people from north spine has filled the bus to the doors.

    199 - serves boonlay mrt, jln bahar, nie, ntu north spine only.

    Pioneer shuttle - serves pioneer mrt only and somewhere in between the two spines.

    The issue as i understand from sbst275 is that sbst is reducing 179A PM trips from this system and putting those buses to 179 instead.

    And why this might end up being a problem (correct me if im wrong) is because 179 takes longer time to travel from ntu to boon lay due to additional stops and large amount of pax getting off at pioneer, which thus reduces the trips each bus can make overall. Lesser trips = lesser people moved = more people waiting for buses.

    And, since theres lesser people moved, the buses are full to the doors, and thus those who really need the services (living along pioneer nth rd - only served by 179) is being deprived of buses to go home.

    However, i would like to add my point that although this is generally true, theres a missing factor in this analysis.

    Going to school, most people would alight at boonlay mrt, since theres 3 services going to ntu (179, 179A, 199) and because 179A is fast.

    However, the reverse does not apply for going home! Not many people care if the bus brings you to boonlay mrt 5min faster.

    The important thing is if they live somewhere along the ewl (lets say tampines, pasir ris, etc), they will always want to alight at pioneer mrt as they are guaranteed a seat for a 40-50mins train ride home.

    This is probably why if theres a 179 and 179A, many would still take the 179 and thus those at south spine cant board. And also probably why sbst decided to increase the amount of 179.

    However, there are valid issues to consider such as crowding up the bus stop at pioneer mrt and turnaround time as mentioned by sbst275.

    Ideas?
