    Trying out having a new fan club, after the Volvo Single Deckers Fan Club thread expired... Anyway, some info on (Volvo) DDs below:

    My favourite models:

    1. Volvo B9TL (Wright, ZF gearbox)

    2. Volvo Olympian (Batch 2)

    3. Volvo B9TL (CDGE)

    Reasons for my favourites:

    1. I like the engine sounds of the Wright B9TLs with ZF gearbox, as it is very musical.

    2. I like the pink and purple colour scheme for the interior and prefer it's appearance without EDS.

    3. I like the engine sounds of the CDGE B9TLs too, but prefer the ZF Wrights...

    Anyway, my favourite buses for the bus models above (except Volvo B10TLs):

    1. Volvo B9TL (ZF Wrights): SBS7528A, SBS7537Z, SBS7592M, SBS7671T

    2. Volvo Olympian (Batch 2): SBS9408A, SBS9512G

    3. Volvo B9TL (CDGE): SBS7395R, SBS7477M

    Note: You can also include Scania K310UD and Dennis Tridents into this thread!
