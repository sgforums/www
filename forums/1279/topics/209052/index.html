    SBS Transit has put up large display screens at Suntec Singapore where the IMF/World Bank meetings will be held.

    The screens will track the 16 chartered shuttle services that ferry the delegates between their hotels and the convention centre.

    Arrival information will be updated every 30 seconds, showing a countdown of bus arrival times as the buses near Suntec Singapore.

    More than 10,000 delegates are expected to ride on the shuttle bus services which are provided free. - CNA/ir
