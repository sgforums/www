    Took it just now around 10pm. Taking it at night is even better than daytime, although just now was the first time taking it. The lightings everything was so cool, very stylish and neat. The moment I stepped inside the bus, "WOW". 

    Nothing could describe it as it feels more like an A Class coach. Saw the black Entrance door button just below the windscreen wiper hidden in the black coloured portion.   

    The button is in a very good place actually, whereby in case of accident causing infront, once the button is touched, the Entrance doors open immediately for the sake of passengers safety.

    Overall, its smooth, sleek designs, comfortable and very classic indeed.

    Oh, one more thing, on top of the last row of seats, theres actually aircon flowing just above our head. Good job!!!

     

    Only found 2 cons:

    1) "STOP" sign can mean anything, so passengers may not know its actually 
    "BUS STOPPING", and just now, the sign never lights up a few times.
    Wire connection problem?

    2) When BC wanted to close the exit doors, the Entrance doors have to be closed first, then open again, in order for all the doors to close. So with this, it wasted abit of the time whenever all doors are open. Close-open-close!
