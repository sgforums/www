  • TIB1051D
    21 Mar 10, 12:33
    Originally posted by Scania96:

    I konow this question is stupid but, there is so many annoucement and the annoucement controller only have 4 buttons, 3 blue 1 red. Then how do they use it. There is already more than 4 annoucements uploaded in the bus. 

    there are 5 announcements loaded in the system, of which 2 are automated and 3 others are manually operated by the 3 blue buttons.

    Automated Announcement 1: "Thank you for travelling with SMRT Buses."
    Automated Announcement 2: "Please hold onto the grabpoles or handgrips when standing in the bus."

    now to the blue buttons. starting with the button on the most left.

    Announcement Button 1: "Please move to the rear for the benefit of boarding passengers."
    Announcement Button 2: "Please do not leave your belongings behind."
    Announcement Button 3: "Attention please. This bus is affected by a service diversion ahead. Please check with our Service Leader for more details."

    the red button is to activate/deactivate the mic function. a 3.5mm input for mic is available on the console.
