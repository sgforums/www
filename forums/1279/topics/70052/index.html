    Bus companies have a group of dedicated planners to work on the planning of bus routes, arrival times etc. Due to the long frequency and usage of lesser buses, the buses will easily fall behind schedule, especially if there are heavy jams or huge number of passengers.

    It is known to many of the bus fans that some bus captains do leave the stops earlier than scheduled, especially if it is the last bus.

    If this do happen frequently to you, you can do the following:

    1. Pay cash to get a ticket (just for once).
    2. Feedback to SBST using the details on the tickets and your experiences.

    Tickets are the best proof as sometimes the mentioned buses in service are denied by companies, saying that they did not deployed the mentioned bus on the service.

    Hope this will be of some help to you. Have a nice day
