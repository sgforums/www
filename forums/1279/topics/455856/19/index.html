    This qns tests all on your knowledge of the different speed that different svcs' BC may tend to drive on TPE/SLE towards Woodlands. I noe some of u may say this is subjective or whatever, but I have really done spotting on this.

    So here goes...

    You are given SBST/SMRT svc A-E.

    Question: Identify svc A-E based on the information given below:

    Svc A is driving on the expressway at a certain speed. Then came svc B which overtook svc A. As the two svcs continue on the expressway, svc C, D and E joined in.

    Upon reaching the expressway, svc E changed lanes and overtook every svc.

    Svc D did not change lane immediately. It overtook svc A, but did not overtake the other svcs, and it continued in this speed until it exits the expressway. 

    For svc C, it overtook every svc except for svc E upon reaching the expressway. It changed lanes and allowed svc B behind. Svc B then changed lanes and overtook svc C. But as Svc C reaches it's exit it broke speed and overtook svc B before chiong ing out of the expressway.

    Svc A and B still did not manage to overtake svc E. thus, svc A and B exits the expressway at the same exit, with svc B exiting first, leaving svc E speeding on.

    Clue: for those who are very smart, you dun even have to judge their speed, the route details give everything away :)
