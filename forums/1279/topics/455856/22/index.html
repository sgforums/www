    Hope I get some guesses... :

    You are given four SBST svcs, svcs A-D. 

    Svc A is a svc in the shape of a flipped "L" if you plot the route on the map of Singapore. It passes through* two Xin Wang Cafés. It also passes through the only MRT station which has the barriers and metal platforms at one of its exits (on street level).
    This svc meets with svc B at one of its interchanges, interchange E, and after the interchange these two svcs do not meet at any other bus stops. Svc B passes through two Wendy's outlets. It also stops at the same stops with two svcs, svc C and D, which both terminates at a same interchange, interchange F near a famous island of Singapore. 
    Three of these svcs share a same depot, depot G. Coincidentally the svc which does not share the depot Is the only non-WAB svc among all the four svcs. 

    Question: 
    1. Identify the four svc.
    2. Identify the MRT station. 
    3. Identify the two interchanges.
    4. Identify the two Xin Wang Cafés.
    5. Identify the two Wendy's outlets.
    6a. Identify which three svc share the same depot, and
    6b. hence, identify the non-WAB svc.
    7. Identify the road and avenue that svc B meet with svc C and D respectively.
    8. Identify the famous island.

    *For the Xin Wang Cafés, passing through them need not be stopping at/passing through them, but instead be able to see them (or the shopping mall(s) that they are in) from the bus along the journey.
