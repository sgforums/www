    • 6) Service 166 have SBS7716Z as a Batch 2A wright permer, whereas Service 132 and 165 does not.

    • 7) Service 132 and 165 terminates at Hougang Int together and is controlled by HGDEP together.

    • 8) Service 165 and 166 terminates at Clementi Int together and is controlled by BBDEP together.

    • 9) Service 132 is formally AMDEP controlled(explained in criteria 3), and one of its current depot, ARBP, has no spare buses.

    • 10) Service 132,165 and 166 piles the same road from St. Theresa's Home to AMK hub when Service 166 terminates at AMK Int.
