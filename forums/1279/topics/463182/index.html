    MY OBESRVATIONS

    147- HL on most parts of the route, esp. from Chinatown towards Clementi.

    196- HL between Marine Parade-Shenton Way, Jln Bt Merah-Clementi quite ulu... unless it's an SD...

    197- Mostly L(Seating) throughout the journey on Striders or DDs.

    167- Packed from Jln Bt Merah-Cecil St during 8am+

    961- Can vary. Sometimes ML[25% chance], L[60% chance] or HL[15% chance]. ML and HL is uncommon, while L is common. The sector with this pattern is from Jln Bt Merah-Bt Timah Rd. It is packed from 6AM until 9AM, where loading drops significantly. MANs packed to the entrance door, O405s high loading only.

    175- Can be packed sometimes only, probably from West Coast Rd-Clementi, Depot Rd-Orchard Rd(pax drops significantly after Lucky Plaza).

    65- HL on most parts of the route, esp. at Orchard Rd- Little India(Not sure from MacPherson onwards though...)

    123- Can vary. From Queensway- Bt Merah Ctrl is ulu, while from Orchard-Outram Rd is ML. Sometimes, it is packed from Tiong Bahru Plaza to Bt Purmei Ave(only direct svc.)

    131- Not too sure abt the loading throughout the entire route, but it can vary from ML-HL. During 2PM++, although rare, you can expect to see the bus filling up at BKM Int(High Loading)- Henderson Rd, not sure from Harbourfront onwards.

    195- Super high loading from Tiong Bahru- Telok Blangah Way, Seating loaded(MK4) from Depot Rd- Queenstown MRT(this svc. is kind of the only direct svc that links many places together)... AND I MEAN SUPER HIGH LOADING, WORSE THAN 855 DURING PEAK.

    275- NL all the way, NL all the way, but very rare to see ML on 275, probably if there's no 131 infront.

    855- HL between Farrer Rd and Harbourfront during both AM/PM Peak(towards HBF), other direction seating(L)[50% chance] or ML[50% chance] only. Non peak in both directions is usually ML.
