    Originally posted by BusAnalayzer:

    My personal observation on svc 116 since its launch on 18-Nov-2012

    It has been 5 months since 116 was introduced. I feel somewhere LTA & SBST failed to capture a good loading route for this service. During off-peak, the loading is a mere 15-20 pax (90% times) and during morning / evening peak as well the bus hardly gets more than 40 pax (90% times).

    If at all 116 has taken away some load from 72 from Hougang 1 to HG MRT. With increase in 72 frequency from Jan, that too has taken away some of the load from 116 itself in Nov/Dec between Hougang 1 and HG MRT.

    315 still has better loading than 116. At Serangoon Nth Ave 4, people just take whichever bus comes first. If it is 315 they take it and don't really wait for 116. 116 takes away some load from 43/70 on YCK Road towards NEX is what I observed. The real load from Serangoon Nth Ave 4 to NEX will be just about 15 pax during peak hours.

    In the evening hours, 116 does not help relieve load on 43/70 on YCK road till AMK Ave 3 junction, as its bus stop is opposite side of NEX. In fact, people prefer to take 315 at Interchange or NEX rather than walk to bus stop opp NEX. 116 should be re-reouted via Boundary road to NEX bus stop to have common synergies with 43/70/315. I see that svc 4 and svc 50 that were introduced later are doing way better than 116. Just my thoughts :-)

    Maybe LTA AND SBST should make this bus service go inside serangoon interchange to loop and then go out by the same way and call at the bus-stop at nex,not referring to the opposite side
