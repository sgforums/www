    As for your topic, NONE of Tibs buses from 1982 till present are locally built.

    The old Japanese buses are built in Japan.

    The Mercs are built in Hispano factory.

    The Volgrens are built in Volgren factory.

    Allow me to share the following.

    Hinos, Nissans - Built in Japan
    Hispano DAFs, O405s, O405Gs, Habits - Built in Spain
    Volgren O405s, O405Gs - Built in Johor Bahru
    Volgren Lance 245s - Built in Johor Bahru
    Volgren Hino HS3KRKA - Built in Johor Bahru
    Volgren Hino HS3KRKA demonstrator - Built in Australia
    WA Scania L113CRLs (Strider) - Built in UK
    ELBO Scania L113CRLs - Built in Greece
    DM-UMW Lance 245s - Built in KL
    DM Lance 211s - Built in UK

    The TIBS manager I spoke to during one of the depot trips said that TIBS will continue to buy CBU buses, which is known for their high quality workmanship.
