    The following is my predictions (repeated) to service changes after NEL opens.

    97 (Jurong East to Little India stretch) will be integrated with 23 (Tampines to Little India) to become 68 (Tampines to Jurong East)

    65 (Tampines to Orchard stretch) will be integrated with 111 (Ghim Moh to Orchard stretch) to become 65 (Tampines to Ghim Moh). As for 65 Orchard to HarbourFront stretch, can take alternative 14 (to Lower Delta Rd) or 85.

    103 (Jln Kayu to Upp Serangoon stretch) will be integrated with 107 (Serangoon Int to Shenton Way/Marina Centre) to become 107 (Jln Kayu to Marina Centre only, skipping Shenton Way)

    81 will be withdrawn, 53 will be amended to via Upp Serangoon Rd to serve Kovan station, skipping Lor Ah Soo and Hougang Ave 1. Affected passengers can transfer to 62 outside Kovan station.

    321 will be withdrawn, 62 will be extended to Hougang Ctrl, via Upp Serangoon Rd, Hougang St 21, Hougang Ave 1 and continue with its present route.

    501, 511 & 512 will be withdrawn, 502 (Boon Lay to Orchard stretch) will be integrated with 518 to become 510 from Pasir Ris to Boon Lay, via ECP, Marina Centre and Orchard in both directions, skipping PIE, CTE, Moulmein Rd, Newton Rd & Scotts Rd.

    131 to serve Farrer Park station in both direction, skipping Dorset Rd.

    All services in Hougang South Int will be shifted to Hougang Ctrl Int.
