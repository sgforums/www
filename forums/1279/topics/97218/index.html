    The following shift is one of the shifts on a special gas roster at Connex Southern Coast Transit:

    ----------

    Shift No#: 1201
    Operator: Connex Southern Coast Transit, Fremantle
    Buses used: 1234-1245+

    *** FIRST HALF ***

    TRIP 1: 740 to EAST PERTH
    Dep. Hamilton Hill Hall (740/940 outer terminus): 0721h
    Arr. Witenoon St., East Perth: 0832h

    ... then display '000', run empty via Freeway to do:

    TRIP 2: 146 to FREMANTLE
    Dep. Booragoon Bus Station, Stand C1: 0855h
    Arr. Fremantle Interchange: 0929h

    * REST BREAK *

    TRIP 3: 146 to BOORAGOON
    Dep. Fremantle Interchange, Stand F10: 1005h
    Arr. Booragoon Bus Station: 1034h

    TRIP 4: 154 to FREMANTLE
    Dep. Booragoon Bus Station, Stand C1: 1040h
    Arr. Fremantle Interchange: 1105h

    ... then display '000', run empty to Fremantle depot for conclusion of 1st half.

    *** END OF FIRST HALF ***

    ----------

    *** SECOND HALF ***

    Display '000', run empty to PATH Transit Morley depot for gas bus fuelling.

    ... then display '000', run empty to Morley Bus Station (Galleria Shops) to do:

    TRIP 5: 99 CIRCLEROUTE short journey to FREMANTLE
    Dep. Morley Bus Station, 99 Stand: 1540h
    Stirling Interchange: 1607h
    Edith Cowan University: 1616h
    University of WA: 1634h
    Arr. Fremantle Interchange: 1702h

    TRIP 6: 99 CIRCLEROUTE short journey to OATS STREET
    Dep. Fremantle Interchange, Stand F7: 1707h
    Murdoch University: 1730h
    Murdoch Park & Ride: 1735h
    Southlands Boulevarde Shops: 1747h
    Curtin University: 1804h
    Arr. Oats Street Train Station: 1814

    ... then display '000', run empty to Fremantle depot for conclusion of 2nd half.

    *** END OF SECOND HALF ***

    ----------

    Other known new bus shifts: 1021, 1036

    1036:
    Route 004 Red CAT from Wellington Street Bus Station @ 0752h and 0822h.

    Known new bus run:
    Be at the Esplanade Busport (on the 940 stand on A platform) by 1415h and you will find an OC 500 LE CNG on a route 940 to Hamilton Hill Hall. Hop off this bus at Canning Bridge Interchange and you will find that the next route 881 back into the City is also an OC 500 LE CNG.

    Hope that helps!

    Regards,
    Dave
