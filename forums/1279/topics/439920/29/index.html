  • SBS 9631X
    1 Mar 12, 21:00

    01 Mar 12, Thurs

    Sv 2 SBS 6116L (BN 109) 

    Is this a debut? I think there was another Citaro on 2 before?
    Anyway, took this bus on Service 2 today. BC drove 6116L like a race car, swerving left and right (quite smoothly though in my opinion) to make the stops. The bus was able to be floored without the gears getting stuck all the way from Bugis to Tanah Merah, not an easy task to floor a Citaro given that BC probably doesn't drive a Citaro often on Service 2, and certain Citaros would get stuck in gears at full boost since they are without a tachometer. And it climed the hill at Kembangan towards Bedok effortlessly with ease with the turbo boost. 
    Or probably because 6116L that I have on 109 is one of the better-maintained ones. 

    As for the crush loading problem, it is actually viable for Citaros to be put on Service 2 somehow from what I can see, just that they have to be on specific timings during peak hours (i.e not the super packed periods). The bus was packed today when I boarded at around 1740 at Bugis, but not too packed till everyone was packed like sardines all the way till Tanah Merah, but the DD behind about 10 mins was super packed.
