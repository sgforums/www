    Originally posted by Lsk138:

    Well, the story went like this:-

    1.Selected MK4 Striders from AR/BB were mass transferred to HGDEP and BNDEP in exchange for the MK4 DM3500s. This took place in 2008 as well as 2011.

    2. AR/BB services that have Striders affected by the depot swop got the DM3500s perms for a short period of time.

    3. At the same time BBDEP began to register most of their DM3500s with the JB government in preperation for the mass replacement of JB service 160/170 fleets.

    4. When 160/170 were ready for the fleet replacement, mass redeployments for MK3s and MK4 DM3500s took place. Services that have newly JB registered DM3500s redeployed to 160/170 got the former 160/170 JB MK3s in return.

    5. Also at that time Citaros started to roll into SBST fleet and BBDEP intake Citaros. Some services like 16 and 175 got Citaros directly to replace their affected DM3500s. On the other hand, some JB MK3s were directly laid up when 160/170 got their fleets replaced.

    6. As more MK3s in AR/BP (including the JB ones) were deregistered, the new Citaros gradually replaced those services with MK3s. Services like 92, 191 and 200 got indirect replacements.
