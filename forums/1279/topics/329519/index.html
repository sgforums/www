    Overnight parking at Bukit Batok Interchange - BBBP
    Overnight parking at Yishun Interchange - YIBP or YIDEP

    Services that have some, if not all, buses parking inside Yishun INT @ night are 800, 804, 806, 811, 812, 853, 854, 855. However they have to make 1 trip down to AMDEP to refuel and wash their buses and back before they can end their duty (If it's an S1, S2, TQ or PM). Timeframe for such timesheets that require an extra trip and back to park their buses is around 26 ~ 34 minutes.

    851 buses park @ AMDEP.
