    The perm bus TIB 0784D also parks overnight @ Yishun INT, and it's off service runtime from Sembawang - AMDEP - Yishun INT is somewhere close to 40 mins IIRC.

    An interesting thing to note is that all buses that park at YIBP overnight have to display 969 route details on their EDS. The reason why so is that they want to make sure all EDS are working properly and they choose 969 route details because a small handful of unknown buses do not have 969 route details programmed and so it's better to standardise. Don't be surprised when you enter Yishun INT in the wee hours of the morning and find so many Sv969 buses.
