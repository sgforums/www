    Withdrawl of Shortworking Service 131B

    Service 131B plies between Serangoon Road (Block 663) to Norfolk Road (bef Carlisle Road) on Weekdays, from 5.00pm to 7.00pm, will be withdrawn from 01st June 2006.

    Hence, these buses will serve Service 131 to improve service frequency.

    We seek your understanding on this matter.
    131B was introduced on 23 January 2006.

    Traffic Diversion To Realigned Tanjong Kling Road

    The Land Transport Authority (LTA) will be diverting traffic from the existing Tanjong Kling Road, between the junction of Pioneer Road/Jalan Buroh and Shipyard Road, to the realigned Tanjong Kling Road from 12.30 am on Saturday, 27 May 2006.

    The realigned Tanjong Kling Road is also wider than the existing Tanjong Kling Road by 1 lane in each direction. The widening and realignment works are part of the project at Pioneer Road, Tanjong Kling Road and part of Jalan Buroh, which aims to tie in with JTC en-bloc redevelopment proposal at Tanjong Kling area.

    Motorists are advised to drive carefully and follow the directional signs on display.
