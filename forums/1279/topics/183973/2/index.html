    SMRT Launches Bus Service 922

    On 23 April 2006, SMRT will launch 922, a new bus service from Bukit Panjang Interchange which loops at Segar Road via Jelebu Road, Bukit Panjang Ring Road, Jelapang Road, Saujana Road and Fajar Road.

    Service 922, which operates from 5.30am to 12.00am daily at a frequency of 30 minutes, will provide residents with increased accessibility within Bukit Panjang Town.

    Fares are $0.64 for ez-link card, and $0.90 for cash payment. Transfer rebate and concessionary travel are applicable. Commuters may contact Transitlink Hotline 1800- 2255663 or SMRT Customer Relations Centre at Tel: 1800-3368900 for more information.

    Route amendment to Service 985 & 985A with effect from Sunday 30 April 2006

    To better serve the residents of Bukit Batok Town, Service 985 & 985A will be amended to ply along Bukit Batok West Ave 5 and Ave 7 with effect from 30 April 2006 (Sunday). As such, the services will stop calling at the existing 4 bus stops along Bukit Batok Road and Bukit Batok West Ave 7.
