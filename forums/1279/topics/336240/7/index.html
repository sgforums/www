    Let me add mine as well !

    Good old services

    Service 41

    Bedok to Bukit Merah, this service was merged with the old 197 to become Bedok 197 Jurong East (current). The fleet was a mixture of Mercedes O305 from Hougang and Leyland Alanteans from Ayer Rajah! I often take this service , it was thrilling to be on the speedy O305 ! Shiok man.

    Service 149

    Changi Airport to Toa Payoh

    Service 31 was extended and 36 was introduced an as a result, 149 was withdrawn. I was delighted when I saw mercedes O405 new x2 on the service but when 149 became partially aircon, I was dismayed and extremely disappointed that the NAC n113crbs replaced the mercedes NAC. I remembered 149 had the rego range from 232 all the way to 238 if I am not wrong.

    Service 196

    Marine Parade to Jurong

    I cannot forget the O305 on the services ! Of course at that time they also had the LA buses from BNdepot , I wanted the LAs to be totally replaced by the O305s but when the Boon Lay interchange was newly opened at that point of time, 196 was one of the services that was transferred to there and as a result , LO 2 axles took over the O305s . That is ok but later on was shortened to Clementi and it became a full fleet LA ! But later on the service became partial aircon , the aircon perm buses that 196 had before were, 35B, 38T, 43C, 44A,50G,75K, 78C ,227P,228L,229J and a few others I think I had missed out.

    Service 36 was not introduced as a result of this. Service 36 was actually re-numbered from service 16E, which was a peak-hour service introduced in Jan 1994, plying express between Stamford Rd/Bras Basah Rd and Changi Airport. In Dec 1997, service 16E was re-numbered service 36. The route was amended in Sep 1998 to become the present route (except for the slight amendment to skip Siglap Link).
