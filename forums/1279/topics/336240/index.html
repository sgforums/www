    6 Nov 08, 22:04

    I shall share my pleasant memories.

    In 2000, I used to take Service 9 to my kindergarden almost everyday. That time Service 9 was under HGDEP control so there were lots of Mark IV DMs which I could remember.

    Somewhere around that year, I remember taking OAC O405 on 31 a few times with blue unrefurb seats. Soon after that I discovered that all O405s already had pink refurbished seats. Also, I remember taking an LO2x on 28 to Tampines for just once and no more. Was such a nice ride on the lower deck. I still remember the days when Service 220 was around and took it a number of times mostly CAC Scanias and a few times NAC Scania. There were also VO2x on 35 those days which I took too.

    In 2001, I remember my first ride on a DM3500 which made me instantly fall in love with this model.

    Between 2001 and 2003 I started to fall in love with the O405s when I started taking them frequently on Services 13, 24 and 31. Nevertheless, during those days I also took a number of VOs and LOs which I enjoyed too. I really missed the days when Service 2 had lots of VOs and now replaced by horribly vibrating B9TLs and thankfully I don't take this service very often now. I especially miss the O405s on Service 24.

    I can also remember those beautiful yellow top bus stops before they were replaced with boring grey bus stops. Thankfully we still have some unique orange top bus stops now. It would be nice to have some old things around us rather then keep replacing them.

    Everything was so nice especially during the late 1990s and the very early 2000s with SBS and TIBS. Those were the days with much more unique and nice buses (not to forget better service standards).  must say that the merging of TIBS and SMRT was a BIG BIG mistake. If they did not merge, we would still be enjoying the good services by TIBS and there would be lesser complaints in general. Things would be nicer if SMRT operates the NEL, SBS remains SBS and TIBS remains TIBS.
