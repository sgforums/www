    Originally posted by Apex-LW'21:

    In 1985...

    Service 3 was Bedok to New Bridge Rd (Loop) at start.. and later the route was extended to Jurong East in 1987

    Original Route of Service 3 (Bedok to New Bridge Rd)
    Bedok North Ave 1, New Upp Changi Rd, Changi Rd, Geylang Rd, Kallang Rd, Crawford St, North Bridge Rd, South Bridge Rd, Neil Rd, New Bridge Rd, Hill St, Victoria St, Kallang Rd, Sims Ave, Sims Ave East, New Upp Changi Rd, Bedok North Ave 1

    In 1987, Service 3 was extended to Jurong East via Jln Bt Merah, Queensway, C'wealth Ave, C'wealth Ave West, Boon Lay Way & Jurong East Central, with a slight amendment via Upp Cross St & New Bridge Rd.

    Nissan Diesel U31SCN OAC buses were used.

     

    Service 3 was withdrawn in Dec 1990 due to MRT rationalisation.
