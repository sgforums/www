    For those guides collector who want to know what guides are available at Buona Vista Bus Terminal. I was there at 9:15 PM, 6 November 2012.

    Here are the results:

    32 - Accurate as at 18 Jun 2012

    48 - Accurate as at 20 Jan 2012 - Only 1 piece left. It is hidden in between 200++pieces of 191 guides

    74 - Accurate as at 19 July 2012

    191 - Accurate as at 26 Nov 2010

    200 - Accurate as at 26 Nov 2010

    All these guides are found on the table outside the Terminal. I did not went inside the office to check.

    I ask for permission from the office staff to check the WHOLE stack of guides on tthe table to see if any guides are hidden. Which, of course, I found 48 guides.

    For 48 guides (Accurate as at 20 Jan 2012), they are hidden in between 200++pieces of 191 guides. There are 3 pieces and I took 2 pieces.

    The last piece is still hidden in between 200++pieces of 191 guides.

    Buona Vista Bus Terminal do not have 91, 145, 185 guides.
