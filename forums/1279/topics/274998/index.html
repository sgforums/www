    Take Service 196

    Alight at the bus stop AFTER Lau Pa Sat. jaywalk across the road and walk towards Robinson Rd, turn right and walk along the road till you reach Robinson Point. You will pass by MPH Bookstore, Uncle Samy's Claypot and Han's before you reach the building.

    The previous bus stop is at One Raffles Quay and you may need to cross a number of junctions before you reach Robinson Point. Your destination is equidistant from both bus stop. However, if you choose to alight at One Raffles Quay, you will walk past Rainbow Stationery Shop and Subway before you reach Robinson Point.
