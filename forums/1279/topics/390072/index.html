    A total of 100 bendies were roped in for the shuttle today. (Thanks XiaoTaro for the last rego) The shuttle starts at 8am from T2/T3 Arrival Hall Coach stand towards Airshow and ends at 3pm. For the reverse direction, the shuttle starts at 2pm and the last bus is at 7pm. However for the first bus shuttle timings it's actually much earlier because they had to cater to the large influx of people at about 12.30pm when the first shows ended. And for the last bus timings the 3pm last bus from CGA was extended because at 3.15pm I still saw people boarding the bus towards Airshow.
