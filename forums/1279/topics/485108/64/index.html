    Unlike Q2 and Q4, there is no four-weeks school holiday in Q3. So, the possible implementation dates of the GCM Loyang package is quite limited.

    My guess is that the package will be implemented on 7 August, 4 September and 11 September.

    This is because National Day is on 9 August (Tuesday), Hari Raya Haji is on 12 September (Monday), and the September School Holidays is from 3 September to 11 September.

    8 August (Monday) is half-day, 12 September (Monday) is public holiday and 5 September (Moday) is a school holiday, where the demand may be lower than usual. The Sundays before that might be the most appropriate days to implement the package.
