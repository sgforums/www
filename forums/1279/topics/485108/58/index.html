    I was at Boon Lay MRT Station just now. Got people distribute flyers, so I go take one.

    The flyer title is "Job Opportunities in the Bus Industry".

    There is a job fair coming up on 9 March 2016, from 1pm to 5pm, at Devan Nair Institute for Employment and Employability (at Jurong East).

    It is organised by NTUC e2i. It is supported by the LTA and the four bus operators (Go-Ahead, SBS Transit, SMRT and Tower Transit).

    For more information, you can visit their website, at

    http://etms.sg/e2i/publictransport

     

    Anyway, what I want to write is about the requirements for bus captains.

     

    According to the flyer, the hiring requirements for bus captains are as follows:

    - Valid Class 3 Driving License.

    - Min Secondary Education.

     

    In the flyer, they also have mention about bus technicians.

    The hiring requirements for bus technicians are as follow:

    - NITEC or Higher NITEC in Automotive Technology (Heavy Vehicles), Motor Vehicle Mechanics, Heavy Duty Diesel Mechanics or Mechanical Engineering.

    (I think Polytechnic diploma in MAE or EEE (power engineering) may also be accepted)
