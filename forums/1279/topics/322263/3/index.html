    Thanks so much for those who turned up just now at Central Mall! Any thoughts regarding this outing can just share here... Be it good or bad... =)

    For me, I find it very meaningful during the sharing session opposite Central Mall just now.. It's really very happy for me to see this whole group of bus enthusiasts, being able to gather together and have a kind of sharing session, whereby we will talk a lot of stuffs (mostly on the forum issues for this outing). Probably next outing we will have a heart-to-heart sharing session, whereby we will update with one another about our school life, etc, and also any thoughts which some of us here may have.

    In forums, I don't believe in just shooting and flamming poeple around. I believe that everyone here does make some mistakes at times, and no matter how minor or major the mistakes can be, I still think that we should deserve the equal chance to forgive one another and guide one another back to the right path, instead of trying to be very sarcastic and causing any unhappiness among the bus enthusiasts over here..

    Outing doesn't means that it has to do with bus spotting and photoshoot ALL the time.. If we can have normal outings (such as cycling, sharing session, etc) with our outside friends, I don't see the reason why the same thing can't apply to the fellow bus enthusiasts here..

    I believe that we will not be too obsessed with buses as our hobby, and we should also have hobbies/interests other than buses as well.. Let me ask you guys here a question which I hope you guys here will reply to it...

    "How will it affect your life when (for example) there will not be any buses from tomorrow onwards? Will you think that life is meaningless without buses?"
