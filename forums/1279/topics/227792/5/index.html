    Newly refurbished AMK Bus Interchange gets thumbs-up from commuters

    SINGAPORE : The newly refurbished bus interchange at Ang Mo Kio Hub officially opened on Saturday and it got the stamp of approval from commuters and businesses.

    The new interchange is fully air-conditioned, making commuting a breeze for users.

    It is also safer for passengers, especially with sensors that detect when a bus enters a bay.

    All 15 bus services were housed at a temporary interchange nearby when the Hub was being constructed.

    Retail and food outlets located at the nearby Hub have also benefited from the opening.

    A worker at one cosmetics shop says business has improved by at least 30 per cent and commuters are smiling too.

    "The new interchange is nice, it's clean, it's better than last time," said one commuter.

    "For the passenger, after shopping you can just hop on to the bus. It's more convenient," said another. - CNA/ch
