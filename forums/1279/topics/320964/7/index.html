    Heh - I can see that the thread I staerted so long ago still survives to this day, at least in concept...

    Anyway, just a few more dreams to share, other than that route 402 and the SBS Transit in SMRT area ones:

    1. I once dreamed of riding on a Nissan Diesel U21SXN bus (the 1982 SBS demonstrator) long before I actually knew anything about that bus. How I knew? In the dream, the bus I rode was very similar to the U31RCN we have right now, but instead of the up-down sliding window at the front (behind the front door), it was a sideways-sliding window instead. When I read about the U21SXN bus in that book about Singaporean buses at the library (can't remember it's title anymore), and saw photographs of it (along with that distincitive sideways-sliding window), I thought nothing of it - until I realised that I dreamt of it before. Creepy.

    2. This, I guess, is what they call a lucid dream. In this dream, I was at some unrecognisable bus terminal, and somehow I knew I was dreaming, and I could more or less have control over what bus models I wanted to see. From what I remember, there were Mercedes MB O405s (SMRT version), Hino RK176s, Volvo B9TLs, and a few other bus models I can't remember anymore in that dream.

    3. Before I begin, let me say that this dream happened before the SBS Transit bendy buses were sold, and before the Scania K230UB, Volvo B7RLE, and MAN 18.240 HOCL-NL came in. In that dream, I was at Woodlands bus interchange, and for some reason (SBS Transit takeover?!) at many berths were various SBS Transit demonstrator bus models, many of them departing or about to depart. I remember clearly seeing SBS 999U leaving one of the alighting berths, but most of the dream has been lost to the fuzzes of my memory.

    4. Random bus dream: a Hino HT238K (or one of it's variants) on a route 380 running down a wooden pier.
