    Part 2 thread since the old thread had expired and SBST have also begun mass replacement of the older L8Di AC unit with the LD8u AC unit again.

    How does one differentiate between the LD8i and the LD8u? The LD8i has 4 fans on the pod itself, while the LD8u has only 3 fans but of a larger size. A "bar" can be seen from the shape of the pod on the LD8u.
