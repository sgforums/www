Singapore Bus Model Show 2010

  • SBS997A
    8 Mar 10, 22:20

     

     

    Singapore Bus Model Show (SBMS) 2010 is an official event organized by BusInfoHub together with several bus enthusiasts who has interest in the collection of bus models.

     

    SBMS 2010 aims to bring enthusiasts together in the form of a model sharing and photography session. Enthusiasts who do not collect models are also welcomed to participate and take photographs of the bus models on show at the event.

     

    SBMS 2010 will be the largest gathering of bus models in Singapore till date.

     

    Interested enthusiasts can send an email to sbms2010@minibusgrp.com to indicate attendance. Enthusiasts with models can submit a list for review and unique models may be selected to be showcased on that day!

     

    Location: Costa Sands Resort Pasir Ris (Please contact Mr Lew at XXXXXXXX for chalet unit number. Note: on that day itself )

    Date: 21st March 2010

    Time: 430 to 1030 pm

     

    Contact:

    Will be given at a later date
