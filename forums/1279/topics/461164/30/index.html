    F1RST and legal Mandy picture by BIS~

    Let's welcome SMRT Buses' brand new MAN NG363F (A24) "MANDY", the first wheelchair accessible bendy bus in Singapore!

    It was photographed while on a test run with a "SMB0000W" test plate fitted, before registration in as SMB388S. The bus is said to be bodied by Lion's City G design (though the mold seems to be slightly different), uses a Gorba electronic signage and probably similar to the Lion's City G itself in terms of interior layout for the seats. It is also outfitted with what looks like Spheros aircon units at the roof to provide cool air and comfort for passengers.

    On a more sombre note, this is probably replacing TIB838H, the first ever bendy bus (Mercedes Benz O405G) to be in service in Singapore.

    The Gorba signage mounted on the left of the bus like the NL323F buses, and has the standard long vehicle X warning sticker at the bumper for a bendy bus. Otherwise, the rear is near identical to the Superlion NL323F A22 buses.
