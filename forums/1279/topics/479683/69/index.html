    Took a few trips of 979 today..but i could not count the exact number of pax as some pax even boarded from the rear door and buses were too packed!! (Timing is approx timing)

    930am: SMB3139A (979AP06) >> Twd CCK Cres

    Bus exit KJE with only 2 pax onboard, and only a couple of pax boarded along cck st 52. Many pax boarded at YewTee MRT though most boarded the 302 behind (SMB8019E). Loading was ok for this bus and was only fully seated when i alighted at Blk 681 along CCK Cres.

    10am: SMB3153H (979TQ02) >> Twd BP

    Long wait (20 mins) before this bus arrive Blk 681 bus stop, packed to the door. Good thing many alighted so all could board the bus. Before this bus were three 302s (2 bendies, 1 MAN A22). Large passenger flow over the next few stops and BC had to keep asking pax to move in. Many pax also tried to board from the rear door. Bus eventually skipped the last stop along CCK Cres and many couldnt board the bus at the 2nd last bus stop along CCK Cres. Most pax are going to BP so not much boarding and alighting activities at YewTee MRT either. Bus was packed to the door when it entered KJE.

    1030am: SMB3136H (979AP05) >>Twd CCK Cres

    Fully seated. Bus was 3/4 full upon leaving YewTee MRT.

    11am: SMB3153H (979TQ02) >> Twd BP

    Bus was again filled to the brim upon reaching Opp Blk 666 bus stop. Similar situation as the previous trip above. Left for BP with pax filled to the door.

     

    435pm: SMB1649H >> Twd BP

    Bus reached Blk 702 bus stop 3/4 full and left for BP around 90% full. A few pax boarded the bus along Woodlands Road and eventually, all but 3 pax alighted at Bukit Panjang MRT bus stop

    850pm: SMB3136H (979PM06) >> Twd CCK Cres

    Bus was already packed to the door at the 2nd stop (Opp BP plaza). Strangely, 3 pax boarded at BP LRT and alighted at Opp BP plaza stop. A few pax managed to board at Junction 10 bus stop and some boarded via the rear door and it entered KJE with full load. Bus went into CCK North 5 at 90% load...
