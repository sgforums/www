    Loading on svc 24 (SBS8268T) from Changi Airport PTB3 at around 5.40pm:

    This is not a proper loading level record because I didn't manage to count the number of pax onboard, nor did I count the number of pax boarded at the first few bus stops, because I was dealing with some trouble there. I only started counting at the second stop along Upper Changi Rd East and only counted number of pax boarding and alighting.

    6-7 pax were onboard when I boarded the bus at PTB3. At PTB1 & PTB2, at least 6 pax each boarded. By the time the bus left PTB2 it was fully seated but with no standees. Many pax boarded along Airport Blvd so by the time the bus entered ECP there were 8-10 standees.

    At the first stop along Upper Changi Rd East (@6pm), about 10 pax alighted while at least 10 boarded. After that:

    Upper Changi Rd East: -0/0, -0/0, -0/2, -0/4, -0/4, -1/3

    New Upper Changi Rd: -15/? (Tanah Merah Stn) (I alighted here)

    **I failed to count the number of pax boarding at Tanah Merah Stn. Before TNM, the bus was already packed to the door, while amazingly after TNM, it's almost the same. Seems that at least 12 pax boarded at Tanah Merah.

    **2 stops before Tanah Merah, I noticed a svc 38 bus following this bus. At these 2 stops many people (at least 5 each) went to take 38 instead.

    **Seems like svc 24 can be very crowded during certain times of the day. Sometimes I also take svc 24 from Bedok to Tanah Merah in the evening (lazy to take MRT) and it's always fully packed. Once I travelled in the evening from Bedok all the way to Changi Airport and noticed that many people alighted at the last stop along Upper Changi Road East. (seems that they went shopping at Bedok before taking a bus back home)

    **Saw a svc 20 DD along Upper Changi Rd East turning to Simei Rd @6.05pm. I could only see that upper decker was at least 1/2 full (most likely fully seated).
