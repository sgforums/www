    Fleet for CGA Services

    24 - Current fleet is doing okay. Many parallel services there to share loading, and most with high capacity buses. Huge parallelism in route with sv 22, sv 28, sv 2 along its route. Sometimes you will see bus crowded at Bedok MRT only to realize people alighted 2 stops later. So even if DDs were possible for this service, max needed few good to have DDs. So no bendy required.

    27 - Current fleet is doing okay. No problem with boarding bus due to high frequency. Issue is comfort level is poor as people have to stand long distance all the way through TPE. If proposed NEL/CBP service is launched, loading on sv 27 will get managed. Moreover new service will have majority high capacity fleet. So no bendy required.

    34 - sv 34 loading is more of concern than sv 27. It acts as intra town and quick connect to Punggol from Tampines. Comfort level is poor + sometimes pax need to wait for next bus. If proposed NEL/CBP service is launched, loading may get shifted and issue can be quickly resolved. So no bendy required.

    36 - Even after adding buses and 36B, sv 36 loading to Orchard in AM and from Suntec in PM is very high. Buses are over crowded. Problem for sv 36/48 is not Tanjong Katong road south, but on the return to MArine Parade, the sleep slope from Rochor to join ECP. Apparently, DDs cannot do the steep slope, not sure if bendies can manage. Even if bendies can, not sure LTA will be happy to introduce bendies in Orchard area. Only fix is if sv 36 is allowed to travel express along sv 518 via Marina Bay Sands to join ECP. Travel time will increase by 03-04 minutes, but maybe more comfort to people.

    53 - After 25 buses and no bus doing 53A/B anymore after 53M launch, I think sv 53 is doing quite okay. No need for additional bus or HC bus at the moment. It is just good to have. No bendy required.

    858 - This service required majority fleet or full fleet bendy. The long distance that it travels MANA22s cannot tank the load. Extremely important for LTA/SMRT to concentrate 858 with maximum bendies. 

    So in my evaluation, SBST does not need bendies (except sv 36 but may not be possible due to Orchard area). SMRT is the one that needs at least 20 A24s deployed on sv 858.
