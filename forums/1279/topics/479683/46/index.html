    Sbs 85 loading is mainly due to few factors.

    ---lower frequency than other services thus accumulated many people on 1 bus.

    ---peak hour especially high crowds.

    ---crowds from Punggol east/Sengkang east/sengkang west

    ---blooming number of HDB flats in sengkang west area

    ---only 1 bus service at sengkang west way (sbs 85) not counting other nearby roads

    ---fastest way from sengkang east/west area to khatib/yishun. 

    ---at present, LTA still not introducing new bus services to sengkang west way, thinking changing some single deck to double deck for SBS 85 will help.

    ---foresee new service will go to sengkang west very soon.
