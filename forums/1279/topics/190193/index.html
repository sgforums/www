    Route 856 also gets you there, but it only serves Canberra Road, and does not terminate or call at Sembawang Interchange. It can be quite a long walk from the nearest bus stop to Sembawang Interchange too.

    As for route 859, it does terminate at Sembawang Interchange, but upon arriving at Sembawang New Town, it first makes a turn into Sembawang Vista (the road to access Sembawang Interchange), but instead of going into the interchange, it calls at a bus stop at that road, and then proceeds to make a loop within Sembawang New Town. It then comes back to Sembawang Vista, and then finally terminates at the bus interchange.
