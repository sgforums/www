    Most (if not all) companies who provide passenger charter go by the following types

    1. One-Way Transfer
    2. Two-Way Transfer
    3. Disposal

    1) and 2) are flat rates and 2 locations are needed for this. Indication of pickup time is needed

    3) depends on the number of hours of charter. Private companies charge by the hour. the 2 PTOs have a minimum chartering period. Locations and pickup times are also needed.

    Chartering from private bus operators are cheaper than the 2 PTOs.
