    Recently about a few days back, I briefly heard from the news on TV that there is a decreasing number of people taking public buses these days....

    Okay let's put that aside for now... Let's say they really have a problem with their revenue and were forced to increase the bus fares... But with the increase in bus fares, were we given better services?

    1) Sometimes, being in an aircon bus can be a worse experience than being in a non-aircon bus... Simply because the aircon is not working and it's like an oven in the bus!

    2) Sometimes during rainy days, you find that you are getting wet despite being indoors on a bus... Do not fret because it's just another leak in the bus... Leakages are so common in buses that no one bothers to complain anymore...

    3) There was once when I was travelling in a bus, I heard a loud noise.. Sounds like something just dropped... When I turned around, I realised it was the cover of a lamp that had fallen down... and it actually hit the head of an old man... Why is this happening if there is regular maintenance?

    4) Irregular bus arrival timings - leading to extremely long waits and empty buses trailing one another...

    5)..........
