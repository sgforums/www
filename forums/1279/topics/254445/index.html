  • patdog112

    Event description:
    SMRT will be introducing a new premium service from Yio Chu Kang Terminal to Lentor Estate with effect from 26 May 2007 (Saturday). The service, which starts from Yio Chu Kang Terminal, will operate along Ang Mo Kio Avenue 6, Yio Chu Kang Road and loop through the Lentor Estate. Please refer to the accompanying map attched for more details. The service operates on Saturdays only from 3.00pm to 8.00pm with a frequency of 25 minutes.

    Fare: $1.70 flat fare (Transfer rebate and concessionary travel not applicable).

    Route Details: Yio Chu Kang Terminal/MRT Station, Ang Mo Kio Ave 6, Yio Chu Kang Road, Lentor Drive, Lentor Loop, Lentor Road, Lentor Cresent, Lentor Street, Lentor Loop, Lentor Drive, Yio Chu Kang Road, Ang Mo Kio Ave 6, Ang Mo Kio Ave 8, and Yio Chu Kang Terminal/MRT Station.
