    There must be reasons why SBST put high-capacity buses on certain routes. And definitely you just cannot judge that just by looking at certain segments or parts of the route itself.

    Take me as an example when sometime ago I mentioned about SBST's deployment of 2 Service 168's VSOs back to back. I saw them running empty in a matter of 5 minutes within each other at Bedok Reservoir Road. But when someone told me the deployment is crucial, as the bulk of passengers alighted at Tampines, leaving the bus empty after that, I immediately realised there was a strong reason for SBST to do that.

    A Service 22 Superbus might run empty at Serangoon, but it might be packed to the brim at Paya Lebar or Bedok Reservoir. So I advise you look at it as a whole, not just it's surface.
