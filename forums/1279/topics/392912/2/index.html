    Mr. Chen said that he saw the accident occurred and remained in the incident place. He heard the bus driver at the scene said the bus taking this road as usual, and turn right to enter the terminal, not from my expectation, suddenly a motorcycle rushing from the opposite direction.

    I do not know why the motorcycle lost control, and even went so far as it hits the rear door and went up the 3 level stairs located at the rear door. The stairs could not stop the impact of the motorcycle that it even turned left again and finally stopped in the middle of the aisle seat.

    Fortunately, at that time the bus journey had ended, apart from the bus driver, there are no other passenger. 

    Police spokesman confirmed that subject to investigation from the occurrence of the accident, the spokesman said a total of three people injured in the hospital from a car accident. One of the motorcycle, 27-year-old biker.

    The other two were on the bus was a pair of male and female, 49-year-old man and 43-year-old woman, both sustain minor injuries, these three people were taken to National Hospital for treatment.
