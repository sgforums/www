    Sv 966 would be using 12m O.405 with Hispano and Volgren bodywork as the start. Regarding to whether it would have bendies, that would really depends on how Sv 966 picks up during the monitoring by TIBS.

    As Sv 966 becomes part of the 'family' of Woodlands Depot, Sv 184 will not have Volgren Bendies anymore. The control of Sv 184's deployment would be moved over to Kranji depot with effect from 16 Nov, i.e. commencement of Sv 966. The empty slots of Sv 184's Volgren Bendies would be taken over by other Bendies in Kranji Depot, i.e. Habits, Hispano O.405G.
