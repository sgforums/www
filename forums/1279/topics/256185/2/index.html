    Talk about engine brake , anyone realised that when the B9TLs starts to brake , the engine emits a loud humming sound ? It seems like engine brake sound to me .
    Engine brake don't make a sound. You don't even have to step on the brakes to get the engine brake. Engine brake goes to work once you let go of the accelerator pedal. When you do that, the engine will slow down and this will in turn slow down the wheel. That is engine brake.

    A retarder creates a magnetic field around the engine which helps to slow it down at faster rate. This adds to the effectiveness of the braking system and reduces wear on the brake pads.

    What you hear on the B9TL is the exhaust brakes. Its purpose is the same as the retarder but it works differently. Basically, the exhaust gas is compressed inside the cylinder and the built in pressure helps to slow down the vehicle. But I'm not too sure about this, though.
