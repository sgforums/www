    Originally posted by 105090:
    and how come teban gardens got 331 removed? not recognised as j east town? 331 last time is 241 rite?
    Correct, service 331 was formerly service 241.

    When Jurong Depot was closed in May 2002, service 143 which terminated here (also known as "Teban Gardens terminal".) was extended to Jurong East interchange. Service 331 was withdrawn with service 143 extension. Similarly, amendments were made to service 51 to ply Pandan Gardens and service 78 to ply West Coast Rd (between Jurong Town Hall Rd and Teban Gardens Rd) as a replacment for the route of service 331.
