    Tough choices... But I'm voting for (A) because:

    - (A) has better coverage, and 45 is actually quite fast between Bedok and Lor Ah Soo / Serangoon. Only problem is the loss of link from Bedok North (mainly Bedok North Rd) to Bedok Rd / Eastwood, but should be able to manage by 14 and some transfers.

    - (B) may work for Bedok North / Chai Chee residents and improve bus network for Chai Chee, but demand remains as question (seeing 47's demand at Bedok South is much less than MP). And the route still heavily duplicates EWL, so pax may still prefer to transfer at TNM.

    - (C) is good mainly for ITE students in Tampines East and Pasir Ris, but not much for CBP. Tampines Ave 2 already has 20 (albeit too winding); Pasir Ris East can be done by 20-12 or 118-17 (although depends on which part or PSR East); Pasir Ris West is the problem but Dr 3 residents have 58-118, and even if 39 is extended there it is too winding. Would put this as the next best alternative after (A).

    - (D) is simply a merger of 43 and 47... And 43 is already too long.
