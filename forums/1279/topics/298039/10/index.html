    Originally posted by Scania N113CRB luver:
    This bus has a shorter rear overhang than the Scania K230UB ?
    Yes.

    Scania K230 has a rear overhang of 3310mm (from centreline of rear wheel to rear bumper)

    This Volvo B7 has a rear overhang of 3240mm (same method of measurement as the K230).

    Also from the B7 thread, someone mentions that the height clearance between the front bumper and ground got to be checked carefully.

    For general information, these are the height clearance:-

    K230 - Front bumper (till front wheel) - 250mm; Body clearance - 250mm
    B7 - Front Bumper (till front wheel) - 250mm; Body clearance - 250mm
    B9TL - Front Bumper - 305mm; After Bumper (before front wheel) - 250mm; Body - 250mm
