    New bus Stops at Kaki Bukit

     

     24 September 2017 Tap In Tap Out  0 Comment

     

    Several new bus stops have been spotted in the Kaki Bukit area, intended for an upcoming bus service likely plying between Sims Place and Upper East Coast. These bus stops and their future bus service(s) will bring about greater connectivity to the Kaki Bukit Industrial Estate which presently does not have bus connections north of Kaki Bukit Avenue 1.
