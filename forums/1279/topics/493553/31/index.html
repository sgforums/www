    Regarding 71, there used to be a bus service 71 running between Geylang Lorong 1 Terminal and Old Upper Thomson Terminal.

    The routing is as follows,

    Geylang Lorong 1 Terminal, (Geylang Road, Kallang Road), Sims Avenue, Paya Lebar Road, Upper Paya Lebar Road, (Upper Serangoon Road), Yio Chu Kang Road, (Old Upper Thomson Road) and Old Upper Thomson Terminal.

    And return via Old Upper Thomson Terminal, (Old Upper Thomson Road), Yio Chu Kang Road, (Upper Serangoon Road), Upper Paya Lebar Road, Paya Lebar Road, Geylang Road and Geylang Lorong 1 Terminal.

    *() denotes express sector.

    *Upper Serangoon Road was probably today's Boundary Road.

    This service was replaced with today's bus service 70, which runs about the same route as former bus service 71, between Paya Lebar Road and Yio Chu Kang Road, with minor difference in Serangoon area.
