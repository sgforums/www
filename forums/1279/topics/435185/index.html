    In the days when 4-5 74s could turn up at AMK (the good old days of 2 min frequency), pax had to tolerate the NAC DDs.

    74 was the last cross-country trunk to be converted to FAC along with 93.

    And then when the fleet became FAC, MKIIIs replaced the NAC DDs....and 74 also has a so-so frequency these days.

    74 is also one of those services where the BCs like to drive slowly. Increasing journey time.

    Yet few make noise about 74.
