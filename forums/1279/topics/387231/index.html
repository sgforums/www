Introduction of SMRT Resort World shuttle RWS8 on 9 Jan 2010

Next
  • SMB134H
    7 Jan 10, 03:24

    SMRT Buses will be introducing a new shuttle service RWS8 on 9 Jan 2010.
    It will ply from Resorts World @ Sentosa to loop at Telok Blangah Rd.

    Note: This service will NOT call at HarbourFront Interchange.

    The detailed route will be as follows:
    Via Sentosa Resorts World B1 carpark, Sentosa Gateway, Telok Blangah Rd, U-turn at junction of Henderson Rd/Telok Blangah Rd, back to Telok Blangah Rd, Sentosa Gateway and terminate at Sentosa Resorts World B1 carpark.

    This service will call at the following bus stops

    14519 Resorts World @ Sentosa
    14141 VivoCity
    14121 HarbourFront Stn
    14139 Bef Seah Im Rd

    Please note that for b/s code 14121 HarbourFront Stn, it is the bus stop outside HarbourFront Centre. 

    This bus will not call at the bus stop 14129 HarbourFront Stn, the one that is outside Seah Im Hawker Centre.  MRT passengers from HarbourFront Stn have to board RWS8 either at VivoCity or the bus stop outside HarbourFront Centre then the bus will U-turn at Henderson Rd junction before returning to Sentosa.

    Operation hours are from 0600-2330 daily (departing from RWS)

    A fare of $2 per person will be payable for the inbound trip into RWS, inclusive of the admission fee to Sentosa island.  Payment can be made by cash or CEPAS card.

    Passengers will not be charged for the outbound bus trip from RWS back to mainland.

    Concessionary travel (including concessionary cardholders with Bus concession pass) and travel rebates are not applicable.

    You can expect to see the publicity posters tomorrow and do check out the update on SMRT website.
