    Fare stage is by distance travelled. 1 fare stage is about 0.8km. The fare stages coincide with specific bus stops of each route.

    So, the longer the distance travelled, the more fare stages which means passengers pay a higher fare.

    For example, a bus ride from Woodlands Regional interchange to Admiralty Rd West Singapore Maritime Academy. Can take either services 169 or 856. The fare is $0.85 by ezlink for service 169 while the fare is $1.15 by ezlink for service 856 due to the longer route.
