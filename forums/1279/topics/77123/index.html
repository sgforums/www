    Thanks Bus_Guide and ZYX for the tips.

    I found this on PAP's website that shows an indication of the bus depot's location.

    http://www.pap.org.sg/Party_History/Timeline_of_Singapore_and_Party/1950s/1950.shtml

    Students were intimidating bus drivers by setting up a convoy of 20 lorries at Alexandra Road Circus
    By setting up a convoy at Alexandra Road Circus (Princess Circus), it indicates that the bus depot should be around that vicinity. The 1984 street directory (does not show the depot, though) indicates a large enough blank area opposite Princess House and next to the Circus.

    Therefore, we can conclude the depot was located opposite Princess House as what The_Bus_Guide has mentioned and ZYX has concurred.

    Another mystery closed.
