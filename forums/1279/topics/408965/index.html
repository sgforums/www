    Extracted from "Singapore Bus Enthusiasts on Facebook"

    TIB1105H is back on service after a two year repair following the fire in 2008. The bus features extensive rebuilding and refurbishments features with a mix of refurbished DAFs/Scanias & purple/red seats. It also has a LAWO-Sicma EDS installed.

    There is a possiblity that SMRT is also trialling the interior theme blueprint for future buses e.g. local spec-ed Citaro/Citaro G.

    The most notable difference of TIB1105H is the roof mounted Konvekta airconditioning, most likely procured as part of the OC500LE purchases.
