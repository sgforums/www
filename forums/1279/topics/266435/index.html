    From Woodlands to East Coast, you may consider taking route 168 to Bedok, and the transferring to route 401 if you are going on a weekend. Otherwise, catch route 15 from Tampines, which will get you to Marine Parade - you can walk or take a taxi from there. Alternatively, take route 858 to Changi Airport, and then transfer to route 36 at Changi Airport - alight at the first bus stop along East Coast Parkway after it completely leaves the Changi Airport area.
