    Originally posted by SBS6305H:

    Hey! Any services to reccomend for joyriding?Â 


    Depends on what you like while joyriding.

    1. Do you like to see the surrounding area?

    2. Do you want to observe the loading pattern of a route?

    3. Do you want to travel DD (level 2 view), bendy or SD?

    4. How much time do you have in hand for joyriding?

    5. Are there some interchanges that you want to observe?

    6. Is there a new route / extension you want to understand?

    These factors are important while deciding on joyriding.
