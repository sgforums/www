    Originally posted by carbikebus:
    I used to stay in boon lay in my primary school days just opposite the boon lay garden terminal.Anyone knows what service used to served there?Info that it is only jurong industrial services.
    Boon Lay Terminal is located at the junction of Boon Lay Dr and Boon Lay Place. It still exists but is used as a bus park. SBS services which used to end there are:

    247A to Jurong Pier Rd
    249A to Jln Samulun
    250A to Lokyang Way
    251A to Refinery Rd

    They operate in one direction on weekdays and Saturdays only. Buses head out of the terminal to their respective destinations during the AM peak and vice versa during the PM peak. They charge a flat fare of 40c. Info extracted from the 1985 Bus Guide.

    SBS pulled the plug on these services but Scheme B buses of the same numbers remained in service. I'm not sure whether the Scheme B services co-existed with the SBS services at one time or whether the services were handed over by SBS.

    Due to the LTA's policy for the duplication of bus service numbers, all 4 services were re-numbered a couple of years back. Unfortunately, I can't recall which number became which. I don't know if they are still around but the bus numbers are still indicated on bus stops around Boon Lay Garden.
