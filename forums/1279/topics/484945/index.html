Some bus services should be re-numbered to avoid confusion

  • Pervertedboy
    18 May 14, 14:51

    well, it is quite frustrating when you are at a bus stop waiting for a bus to arrive, then you thought you saw your bus arriving, get excited, then realised that actually, it is another bus service that has a bus number that looks identical to the bus service you want to take.

    It is even worse when you end up taking the wrong bus service because you saw the wrong bus number and mistakenly took the wrong bus, thinking that it is the right bus.

    I feel that LTA should re-number some bus services to avoid anymore confusion. To minimise short-term impact, the re-numbering should be done on bus services that are newer or less used. (I do not think there would be long-term impact)

    For instance, at Clementi area, bus numbers 105 and 185 look very identical. To avoid confusion, I think 185 (introduced in december 2006) should be re-numbered to another number that can distinct it from the other bus services in Clementi, Jurong East and Jurong West.
