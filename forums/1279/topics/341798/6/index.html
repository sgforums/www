    I hope to see a company operating all the bus services in Singapore and another operating all the MRT services. Buses can provide a platform as a complement to the MRT services but they can also provide a platform for competition too.

    One example for bus services acting as a complement to the MRT are feeder services linking HDB estates to the MRT.

    One example for bus services acting as a competitor to the MRT are trunk services which ply between towns.

    With one company manning all the bus services and another manning all the train services, I believe that there will be greater emphasis and need for them to improve their service standards and maintainance for buses/trains. Company Bus will think of new economically viable and faster routes to compete with the advantage of time saved by taking the train while Company MRT will think of ways to improve consumer's comfort while taking the train.

     

    Next, I believe this is more of a dream. I hope to see like the bus community can be like a sub group of the transport companies, providing more information related to buses, and MRT services to the rest of the community. At the same time, the bus community can provide "constructive" feedbacks to certain changes/policies being done or implemented by the companies.
