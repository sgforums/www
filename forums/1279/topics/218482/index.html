    ,I'm Norman here,Age 30,Married with two kids.I want to share something about my life as a BC.Some of the senior members here knew that i was an HGDEP BC previously,But due to some family bussiness,I decided to quit the job on March this year.But recently,I did make a comeback in this line but from a different district,That is East side.Possibility that i'm based at BRBP.Hope so,So this is a bit of my diary i want to share..

    Feb 2004,Went for Class 4A(I did not have Class 4 at that time)training(Both basic & practical)for at least a month plus before i graduate for service learning with of course three diff mentors for three diff learn services..

    1st Service:325(before amendment)for 1 week,Quite boring,Many trips,Old timers..
    2nd Service:83,Quite challenging,I was warned once for not turning on the lights @ Sengkang,Lucky my mentor who was once the perm BC of S116 explains to the Senior Timekeeper.My biggest challenges is from the Secondary School students at Punggol,Headache!! :wacko:Last for 2 weeks
    3rd Service:112,Boring sia,Almost every trip i was early for an average of 5-10 mins ,Boring point,Partial AC!!Last for 1 month
    4th Service:100.Aha,Real world,Real challenges,And my first time minor accident with a car at Aljunied Rd :angry:Attached for 6 months as a split-shift BC
    5th service:Standby BC,Quite fun to learn many services authorised by the despatcher there,But did not get the chances to drive all,Services that i've learn and drive,S11,S27,S62,S82,S83,S100,S101,S103,S105,S112,S113,S115,S151,S158,S315,S317 & S325,The ones that i've learned but got no chances to drive are S43,S51,S156,S161& S165.After that i was offered as a service 34 perm BC which was really bored for me.I've tried numerous of times pleading with the route manager for a transfer on other services or other Interchange but was rejected.I ride my Brother Motorcycle for the first month,After that i take employee bus on Night shift.I tried to apply at SMRT recently but as usual they always replied after i was selected by SBST <_< <_< :angry: But anyway,I hope for a better life at East district this time,One of my neighbour already learn Double decker after the 3 month confirmation and now are applying for a perm service at Ang Mo Kio :blink: It was almost impossible @ West district!I hope that i'll have a good luck this time around and hopes for you'll blessing
