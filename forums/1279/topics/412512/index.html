    For SMRT Buses:

    TIB488J - Only MB O405 (OAC) with Konvekta aircon.
    TIB572Z - Only public omnibus to be fitted with Alux rims.
    TIB700X - Only DAF to have a modified Hispano front.
    TIB792E - Only MB O405 (CAC) to have new non-sliding windows.
    TIB840A - The first and only ELBO (as of 6 October) to be refurbished.
    TIB890E - The only public omnibus in Singapore to have headrests for the seats.
    TIB905Y - The only bus in SMRT's fleet to have the original Volgren front.
    TIB1105H - The first bendy to be revived after a freak fire.
               The only Habit bendy to have new PVC seats and Konvekta aircon.
    SMB1H - The F1RST Euro V bus in S.E.A..
            The F1RST bus in SMRT's fleet to have the prefix "SMB".
    SMB135E - The F1RST China-made public omnibus in Singapore (Currently in non-user layout status, as of 6 October).
    SMB136C - The F1RST full low-floor bus in S.E.A..

    For SBS Transit:

    SBS8000Z/SBS8001X - The first hybrid public omnibus.
    SBS997A - The longest rigid in Singapore, has three axles, measuring 14.5m in length (Superlong bus).
    SBS998Y - SBS's 1st articulated bus. Asia's longest articulated bus, measuring 19m in length.
    SBS999U - SBS's 2nd artuculated bus.
    RD6066L (Research & Development) (Higer) - The first fuel cell public omnibus.
    SBS7500D - The first double decker to be bodied with the famous Wright Eclipse Gemini 2.

    Feel free to add on. :D

    Info from myself, users in buses.sgforums.com, SBS Transit (Wikipedia), SMRT Buses (Wikipedia), and (buses[IN]gapore!) blog.

    SBS8000Z/SBS8001X - The first hybrid public omnibus.

    SBS998Y - SBS's 1st articulated bus. Asia's longest articulated bus, measuring 19m in length.
    SBS999U - SBS's 2nd artuculated bus.
    RD6066L (Research & Development) (Higer) - The first fuel cell public omnibus.
    SBS7500D - The first double decker in Singapore to be bodied with the famous Wright Eclipse Gemini 2.
    SBS8030L - First Volvo B7RLE for SBST featuring the first bus in Singapore to use the new generation Vogelsitze seats and wrap around rear seats.
    SBS8033D - First Scania K230UB in Singapore and the only one with purple interior.

    SBS9168S - The last Leyand to be produced under Leyland name in the world.
