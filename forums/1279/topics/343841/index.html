    I was sitting in Svc 72, SBS 7423 T  Towards Yio Chu Kang at  11 40am on 11 Jan 09 at the intersection between Hougang Ave 3, Ave 2 & Upp Serangoon Road. The bus was waiting at the traffic light on the extreme right lane (Right turn only) along with lots of cars.

    Then, a Svc 51 WAB DD 'cutted' our bus to go to the 3rd lane from the left (straight only)

    The BC on that bus then 'turned hard right'. The car at the front horned at the BC when he turned.

    I was like WTF? & I looked on the ground and saw that the lane marking was straight only.

    When my 72 finally reached Outside Hougang Ctrl Int, I saw the 51 alighting passengers and saw the rego of the bus. SBS 7380 U.

     

    Does this kind of  BCs disobeying traffic lights/lane markings happen often?
