    For SBST,If some areas the air-con if malfunction,The BCs will ask the pax whether if they still want to continue their destination or not.Then the BC will proceed if the pax still want to continue but only with alighting activities.Thus the BCs first must remove all service plates to prevent any boarding activities.Once reach the terminating point the BCs will inform the Timekeepers to make a report and off to depot for replacement buses or other instruction by the Technical officers.I incline to think that it is the same procedure for SMRT Buses

    P/S:If the paxs want refund,Then they will have to alight and wait for another bus with a complimentary ticket which is valid for 1 month but for a single journey only.
