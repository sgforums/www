    frequency: 04-07 mins during peak hours.

    Rationale: The upgrades are mostly to meet the high demand between BPJ and Bukit Batok West town + helps connecting BBT-BBT West & Lakeside-Jurong East Ave 1. 

    188 - got 4 SD -> DD conversion. Now operating with only 04 SDs in its fleet.

    frequency: 07-08 mins during peak hours

    Rationale: The upgrade is mostly to meet the high demand between Trader Center/IBP to Bukit Batok town.

    856 - got reduction of 2 DDs... fleet size reduced from 31 to 29 buses. Frequency is maintained @ 05 mins/bus.

    857 - 1 DD -> SD conversion. This is on account of fleet upgrade on 854 whereby Yishun-YCK Road is managed by 854/857 well. Not really sure why 857 should have capacity downgrade when it serves long distance pax.

    960 - Reduced 4 buses. Now operating with 30 buses instead of 34 buses. Loading between Little India and BPJ taken by DTL2.
