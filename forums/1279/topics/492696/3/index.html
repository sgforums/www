    Originally posted by carbikebus:

    So technically its N series decker

    Nope. A K series bus or coach chassis means the engine is placed longidutinally with the gearbox in front of the engine. An N series bus means the engine is mounted transversely (on the right side at the rear of the bus) with the gearbox on the left side of the engine compartment. This is then connected to the axle via an angle drive.
