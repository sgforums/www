    the DDs on 222 and 291 seem to have disappeared. what we all know is that the DDs on 291 are nw on 19.

    SMRT, i would say, has a very smart way to planning out their feeder services. take a look at WDL. what they do is they've divided the whole area (Marsiling + WDL + Admiralty) into 6 areas. then, they have 3 Intra-town services gg to these 6 areas. by making the frequency long, they can maximise the use of Bendies and the profit would also increase as they use the same amount of fuel for more paying-passengers.
