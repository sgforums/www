    According to sunday times today, 20 heavily used bus routes will get more frequent and bigger buses during peak hours from tomorrow.

    SBST will add 24 bus trips a day from Mondays to Fridays and double decker buses will be deployed on services 9, 27, 45, 53, 131, 145, 163, 166, 183 and 198 instead of single decked ones.

    SMRT will add 47 bus trips daily and deploy more bendy buses for services 67, 75, 180, 184, 187, 188, 190, 858, 911 and 960. The company has bought 9 new buses to service those heavily used routes, SMRT vice-president said.
