    Originally posted by array88:

    If the loading is also poor, then this part of the route could have been improved.

    Actually, yes. The Anchorvale-Punggol Road sector has poor loading. A lot of people at Compassvale Walk are complaining that it is actually better to walk from Sengkang MRT Station than to take the bus from Sengkang Bus Interchange.

    Previously, 372 was running the other direction and connecting to Compassvale Walk straightaway.

    Then, in 2007, they changed the route to make it go one big round around Anchorvale Road and loop at Punggol Road instead, making it rather pointless to take 372 to go to Compassvale Walk.

    Now that the Compassvale Bus Interchange is opened, perhaps we can consider transplanting some trunk routes over to Compassvale, and make 372 run bi-directional instead of looping at Compassvale Road.

    This would benefit the people living in Compassvale Walk as they would once again have the direct bus connection to Sengkang Bus Interchange.

    Whereas for Compassvale Road, although it would be removed from the route, the proximity to Sengkang MRT Station and other 372 bus stops would compensate any inconvenience caused.

    Just my two cents worth, on behalf of the people living in Compassvale Walk.
