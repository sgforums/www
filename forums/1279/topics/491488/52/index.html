    Some general observations on 123's loading since its extension to Sentosa...

    IMO, 123 is not really a popular choice among Sentosa-goers as the loading during weekends between Tiong Bahru MRT and Sentosa is actually kind of poor as most pax alight at Bukit Purmei and only a handful (usually about 10 or even less) pax remain all the way till Sentosa. During weekdays peak, loading still remains strong but only between Tiong Bahru and Bukit Purmei and becomes kosong towards Sentosa while 123M has decent load to/from HBF. Loading during off peak is not really DD worthy as it is complemented well by 123M from Tiong Bahru to Lower Delta - don't really see much pax going up to the upper deck. Loading patterns remain relatively the same as before extension from Orchard/Havelock/Bukit Merah - remains moderate.

    I suggest building a pair of bus stops for 123 along Sentosa Gateway right outside VivoCity so that pax can still take 123 should they be heading towards HBF (albeit have to cross the overhead bridge and walk slightly more than 123M's stop at the entrance) and also pax taking from Sentosa can still choose to alight at HBF.
