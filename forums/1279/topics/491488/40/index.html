    I stopped recording here because: 1) Loading seems to be dying (maybe it will pick up again at 11pm or so, but I really don't know); 2) I was tired and wanted some rest.

    My conclusions:
    - Even with erratic frequency, 170/A still has SD loading only. Loading might be better from 6-8pm but I'm not sure.
    - 67 has been hit heavily by DTL2 - what I observed was actually SD loading. Guess DDs are there for the Tampines-Little India sector with better loading.
    - 960 has also been impacted. Previously bendies and DDs would be packed to the door. (Maybe it's due to the wrong timing)
    - 980 seems to be doing quite well. In fact it has the best loading among the SD services.

    Recommendations:
    - It does make sense for 170 to be cut to Bukit Panjang Int, provided if there's still space.
    - However, if that is done, there needs to be fleet addition for 960 on weekends. Based on my knowledge, most Indian workers take 170 to their dorms between BPJ and Kranji, so they will turn to 960. From above, 960 currently has 60-70 pax between 9-10 pm, and with pax from 170, it can easily hit 90-100 pax.
    - The remaining pax travelling to Bt Timah Rd / Upp Bt Timah Rd can take 67, which will improve its utility between CCK and Little India.

    Just my two cents worth.

    Your observations are similar to mine. I thought weekend loading would be better as some claimed, but doesn't seem so. Please note 6-8pm is definitely not the peak loading as workers will eat dinner and leave between 8-10 pm from Little India area.

    It is simple, most of them now take DTL2 to BPJ. Those who want to take bus opt for 960. Hence, you still see high loading on 960. 66/67 loading is poor. Your estimates are higher, because if you ever notice more people sit on the left of upper deck than on the right side. So you can still reduce some numbers. 170/A as I have always said will have some 20-30 pax throughout its route until it hits BPJ. + once 171, 961 kick in along the way, loading gets even further split.
