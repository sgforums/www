    *Pasir Ris Dr 3 alone has 21 pax boarded the bus, so we can see that during this hour Pasir Ris Dr 3 residents mostly use this svc to transfer to EWL and to transfer to other bus svc at Pasir Ris Dr 12.

    *Interestingly, loading after Pasir Ris Ctrl keeps dropping until the end of trip. We can see that high pax activity only occurs at key transfer b/s like Pasir Ris Dr 12, TPE and Punggol Rd.

    *In conclusion, demand is quite good in the Pasir Ris stretch, whereas on the other hand demand is very weak in SK/HG area at this off-peak hour.
