    Monday PM observation on sv 172

    Two DD timings 5.06pm, 5.38pm. I took the 5.38pm one to CCK.

    Boon Lay Interchange -- 39 (0/39)

    JW St 64 -- 39 (0/0)

    Jalan Boon Lay -- 39 (0/0), 41 (0/2)

    Jalan Bahar -- 44 (-3/6), 47 (-2/5), 47 (-3/3), 52 (0/5), 54 (0/2) aft Nanyang ave, 54 (0/0), 73 (0/19) opp bus stop also had many people, mostly in police uniform, 84 (0/11), 84 (0/0)

    I was amazed that bus already had +80 pax at Jalan Bahar before hitting Old CCK Road. Expected bus to get +100 pax.

    Old CCK Road -- 81 (-3/0), 81 (0/0), 81 (0/0), 81 (0/0), 81 (0/0), 82 (0/1) at least 10 pax waiting for 975, 86 (0/4) around 20 pax at bus stop who did not board, 88 (0/2), 88 (0/0), 88 (0/0)

    Surprisingly all along Old CCK Road only 7 pax boarded while around 40-50 pax waited for 975 and chose not to board. 975 frequency seems good because could spot at least 5 buses on 975/975A in opposite direction just along Old CCK Road. 

    Bus stops in opposite direction at Keat Hong Camp and Home Team Academy had around 30-40 pax (I assume all these are for 172 as 975A had just called the bus stop).   

    There is huge dormitary under construction b/w Home Team Academy and Brickland Road. It is HUGE. I think it could be getting built in anticipation of work for future Tengah town.

    Brickland Road -- 86 (-2/0), 84 (-4/2)

    CCK Ave 1 -- 80 (-6/2), 76 (-6/2), 72 (-6/2), 67 (-8/3), 62 (-7/2)

    CCK Dr -- 54 (-8/0)

    CCK Interchange -- 00 (-54/0)

    * Bus in front = 08 mins

    * Bus behind = 06, 04 mins. 

    * Average frequency noted is 06-08 mins on sv 172 which is excellent and hence it is doing okay with SDs also. Though DDs do well given that most commuters are long distance travelers.
