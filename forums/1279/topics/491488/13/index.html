    Yishun Interchange -- 26 (0/26)

    Yishun Ave 2 -- 45 (0/19), 45 (0/0), 45 (0/0), 58 (-1/14) Khatib MRT, 57 (-1/0), 57 (0/0)... non stop to Jalan Kayu.

    Jalan Kayu -- 60 (0/3), 62 (-2/4), 61 (-4/3)

    Sengkang West Way -- 58 (-3/0), 53 (-6/1), 45 (-9/1)

    Sengkang East Way -- 44 (-3/2), 36 (-10/2), 32 (-7/3)

    Sengkang East Dr -- 26 (-11/5)

    Compassalve -- 24 (-2/0), 21 (-6/3)

    Rivervale -- 17 (-3/0), 17 (0/0)

    Sengkang East Way -- 16 (-3/2)

    Sengkang East Dr -- 15 (-3/2)

    Punggol East -- 14 (-4/3)

    Punggol Central -- 15 (-5/6), 17 (-5/7), 17 (-3/3), 17 (0/0)

    * Loading on sv 85 has NOT improved. The increase in loading between Yishun and Sengkang West Town has been offset by introduction of new service 117 to Punggol.

    * Almost NO one went from Yishun to Punggol on this trip (100% sure). This leads me to believe, people have moved to 117 to Punggol MRT and transfer to Punggol East town. 

    * There are few who take sv 85 from Yishun to Rivervale, but considering the # pax taking from Sengkang West town to Yishun. Quite low. This leaves me to believe there is a sizeable number who would also be taking 965 and transfering at Punggol Road to sv 86 or at Sengkang MRT. 

    * Loading at Sengkang West/East Way on sv 85 has dropped significantly on account of sv 102. 85 came before 102 all along Jalan Kayu/Sengkang West Way, and there were good # pax waiting at bus stop for 102. This leaves me to believe 102 loading has improved (opp. direction buses seemed to have decent loading, not bursting)

    * 85 loading between Sengkang West and Yishun will continue to improve because the occupancy of the flats there still seems quite low

    * Punggol Central - Punggol MRT loading was also not great, even though there was NO 3/83 in front. It is also opposite direction. 

    * Is there still demand between Sengkang East town and Punggol East with sv 85 at other timings because buses spotted in opposite direction also had feeble loading (this was 2 sv 85 SD spotted at Compassalve and other at Punggol Central towards Yishun).

    * The DDs on sv 85 though NOT well utilized do offer comfortable journey from Yishun to Sengkang West town with upper deck having 36/57 pax.
