    Some background info, BYD is a chinese automotive company that builds mainly electric vehicles and Warren Buffet currently has a 10% stake in it.

    The K9, when in a full low floor configuration, stores its batteries ontop of the wheel arches and weighs only 6 tons (compared to 11ton which other full sized 12m buses typically weigh) and propulsion is fully achieved by 4 hub motors located on each of the wheels.
