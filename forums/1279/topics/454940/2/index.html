    Buses in Bangladesh...mostly dominated by Japanese, Korean and Tata brands. Those who do operate European makes are often quick to advertise this to middle-class Bangladeshis.....and the occasional tourist like myself. Green Line for example, are quick to advertise their MAN, Scania, Volvo fleet. Soudia operates a fleet of Merc coaches, and Hanif operates a fleet of B9Rs. 

    Unfortunately, it didn't quite matter where the bus is from if the driving sucks. I took the Scania coach thrice and the buses broke down twice. The first was because the driver kept half-clutching and the clutch eventually burnt. zzzzzz

    The second was equally stupid. The driver was weaving through the roads merrily, braking, honking, accelerating, and then he had to brake especially violently at one point while on the oncoming lane, and that's when all of a sudden the pneumatic system failed and the brakes on the right rear refused to let go....leaving the bus stranded awkwardly in the middle of the road. As the pictures you see here.

    A B10MD public DD taken from my office while waiting for the rain to stop. That bus is bodied by Alexander, hence the striking similarity with our Olympians. And the tri-axle bus is Hanif's proudest possession, the B9R.

    Below is a picture of a typical rest-stop for coaches. My bus can be seen here. Picture was taken in the wee-hours of the morning on the way to Sylhet. Such a bus is known as a chair-coach and is a much cheaper alternative to the aircon coaches that broke down on me twice in a row. Mostly Hino AKs. Uncomfortable, stuffy and violent driving styles, people are known to puke in the bus. And usually people are used to it and if you motion to the window, they'll open it to let you puke!

    My first experience of that was on a motorcycle. I barely missed the flying projectile.

    My second brush with that came on this trip to Sylhet, when I felt a couple of rain-drops as I slept. Only thing...it wasn't raining. And that hit home when the guy behind me stood up and abruptly closed MY window!
