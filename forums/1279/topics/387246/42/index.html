    Some differences between SBST KUB & Rapid Penang KUB when I went to Penang:

    SBS Transit KUB:

    K230UB

    Euro IV and V

    Two segment door in front

    Front (Where Scania logo is) has one long curve line across 

    12.0M

    Euro IV has vents on A/C on top, Euro V none

    Rapid Penang KUB:

    K270UB

    Euro IV

    One segment door in front

    Front (Where Scania logo is) has three curves across, bumper is not grey

    10.6M

    EDS much smaller

    Has SBST Euro V A/C (on top), but has Euro IV rear (window)
