    Originally posted by One Harmonius Blend:


    leapfrog as in downroute or what?


    leapfrog =/= downroute

    leapfrog is when the bus is heavily affected by delays, e BC will put down destos. it will alight wadever remaining passengers left on board and will not allow others to board.

    downroute is just starting a trip halfway along e svc route and making its way from there to its destination. this is common for SBST during peak periods, when demand is usually much higher than in off-peak periods.

  • SBS Transit and SMRT
    26 Jul 10, 18:38

    All PIS on SMRT Buses had adopted a new PIS format since today. Please refer to the following explanations below for more details.

    Old PIS [on most SMRT buses]
    The PIS will now only show the bus stop description without scrolling* and will not show "Please move to the rear" or "Have a pleasant ride".

    *If the bus stop description is too long, the PIS will still scroll but there will be no "Please move to the rear" message.

    Example 1

    opp Blk 628

    New PIS [on TIB1062Y, WLDEP/AMDEP Habits and OCs]
    The PIS will continue to show messages such as "Bus Stopping" and "Welcome Onboard". However, it will only show the bus stop description without scrolling*.

    *If the bus stop description is too long, the PIS will still scroll but there will be no "Please move to the rear" message.
