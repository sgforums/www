    SBS8934D (SLBP 240)  Service 240
    As the bus stopped at the junction of Boon Lay Way, Jurong West St 51 and Yuan Ching Rd, the Bus Captain contacted the SBS Transit Operations Control Centre and complained about a particular bus commuter who tried to board the bus at Boon Lay Dr (Blk 209B) but was being rejected as there were too much people on board. He insulted the Bus Captain and he was very angry and felt offended.

    SMB18K (KJDEP/BBBP 189)  Service 189
    1)     There is a white marking below the entrance that states "9/1/2010".
    2) There are no "Doors are closing" announcements at some bus stops along the way.
    3) A PIW and his mother boarded the bus from West Coast Rd (Blk 726 Clementi West Mkt) to Commonwealth Ave West (opp Blk 329). The Service Leader helped him to slot coins into the coin box and get him on and off the bus.

    21 May 2010 (Friday)

    TIB563A (WLDEP 187)  Service 187
    The Passenger Information System is not in working order during the journey.

    SBS473U (SLBP 78)  Service 78
    There are no interior registration plate numbers in the bus.

    SBS9131Y (SLBP 179*)  Service 174
    The rear service plate is inverted.

    Chartered Buses around Bukit Timah and Clementi area
