    9 May 12, 07:22

    There are NO height limit restrictions for DDs to enter Serangoon Bus Interchange, provided that the standardized clearence for every bridges, flyovers, etc constructed is 4.5m.

    Service 81 was made to loop at Serangoon Central instead, this is not some people claims that DDs cannot enter the interchange, but the link between Serangoon Central and Hougang/Pasir Ris/Tampines would cut-off. If Service 81 would amend to the new Serangoon Int, the route would have to be extended to Upp Serangoon Rd and followed by Serangoon Ave 2. If the amendment happens, then those regular Service 81 commuters who usually board from Serangoon Central (Serangoon Stn bus stop) will have to walk to the another side to the new interchange via NEX to board Service 81 or a distance walk to the nearest bus stop along Upp Serangoon Rd.
