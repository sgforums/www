    Originally posted by SMB128B:

    That's why... 43 dun even need DDs in the first place... SD fleet additions will do actually 


    Actually depends which part of the route you see 43. If you see 43 between NEX and Buangkok Green, there will be a very low probability that you will find the bus empty, even if there is a 70 right in front. The NEX >> BG direct travelers are around 40 pax at normal frequency (10min) without bus bunching. Then you consider the # of people who board the bus at YCK Road towards NEX. You can find a DD filled with around 90 pax.

    Between NEX and Paya Laber there are some 10 routes plying and hence, here I agree the loading can be less than 40 pax. Again at Paya Laber, you can see at least 50 pax loading in a 43. If a SD comes, many times people cannot completely board or have to be packed like sardines. The loading is good for two buses either 43, 76 or 43 and 135.

    There is one extra route from Paya Laber to Marine Parade. If 43, 76, 135 all come together, it is for sure, 1 bus will go empty - that's where 135 needs to be rationalized according to me.
