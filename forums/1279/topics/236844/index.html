    I am very unsad about the incident happened on 10th of Feb 2007. That day, I boarded Express 502 (SBS 369M) at about 10.30pm at the Esplanade bus This bus arrrived late. More importantly, the seats were all filled up by the passengers from Suntec City. This would affect the downstream passengers would be affected waiting along the Orchard bus stops. Of course, they got no choice but to stand as that was just a single decker bus. It gets worst when the bus approached the bus stop at DFS. Passengers were standing on the stairs at the entrance and most passengers had moved to the rear of the bus. You would say that they can wait for the next bus! What was the main problem was, this services which frequency was at about 20 mins for the next bus. Who would want to wait for the next bus?

    As the driver was behind time, he drove very fast. It was good that the driver knew that he was late and try to get back in time. It was be bad that the driver neglected the safety of the passengers. People standing on the stairs were be dangerous to them as the bus was moving very fast. In the journey, the driver stepped on the brake. Luckily, there were no more lost it balance and knock towards the windscreen. This is dangerous to us and not to say about the kids. Not only that, the bus was plying along the expressway on high speed with such a heavy loading!

    Finally, the bus got back to Jurong West Ave 5. The driver was still behind time. So there was a woman who was unable to scan her card. So she wasted some time by taking the Ezlink card out from her purse. I knew that was quite timing comsuning. The problem was, the driver need not complain by producing unhappiness speech and even scolded vuglar language!

    Is this the kind of service SBST should give? Why they do not schedule more bus during the weekends to solve the crowds? Why they do not want to operate 502A and 502B or even deploy more buses or Double Decker buses when they should knew long ago that there were high demand on this service during the weekends. This would greatly solve the crowd and not causing passengers to stand on the stairs. For the driver, even if you are late, you should not be giving this type of attitude to the passengers?
