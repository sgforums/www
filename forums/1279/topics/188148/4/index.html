    Old Jurong Depot / Bus Park.

    During the SBS days, CCK services 172, 175 and 188 were controlled by Jurong whereas services 185, 190, 300, 302 and 307 were controlled by Bt Batok. Service 67 was controlled by both Bt Batok and Bedok Nth.

    After Tibs took over these services, Kranji took control of all CCK services except partial control of 67 which is from AMK.
    Sv 172 is based on Boon Lay under SBS...it only passes by CCK on the way to loop @ Jln Anak Bt. Therefore Jurong buses were used instead.

    Sv 175 is based on LCK...only turn in to CCK but doesn't terminate there.

    Sv 67, 185, 190, 300, 302 and 307 are original CCK svcs, the whole batch under Bt Batok. Sv 67 was oso under Bedok Nth cos this svc goes all the way to Tampines. (Even under Tibs, 1/2 of the ctrl got to go to AMK due to the distance n the # of buses used 4 the long journey )

    Sv 188 in fact is the most extraordinary CCK service during its SBS days......it was based in CCK however Jurong was the one controlling this svc instead of Bt Batok
