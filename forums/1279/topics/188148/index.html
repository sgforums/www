    Service 190 in the 1980s used Albion Vikings and Volvo B57s.

    Somewhere in 1989-1990, the Volvo MK2s (WA Body) were also newly introduced to this service. Albion Vikings were then phased out.

    MB0405 OACs were then introduced to service 190 when it was converted to a PAC service in 1992.

    Volvo MK3s later replaced the MB0405s in 1993. Double Deckers LA2x were also introduced due to popular demand, followed by LO3x.

    In 1994-1995, VO2x and VO3x replaced the Leyland buses. Some Volvo MK4s were also introduced.

    In 1998, service 190 was converted to a FAC service, hence VO2x and MK2s were removed from this service.

    For those days, 190 was operated with a mixture of MK3s, Striders and VO3x. Not sure whether MK2 CAC buses were used too ??

    Service 190 was then handed over to Tibs in 1999. Tibs used MB0405 and MB0405Gs from then.
