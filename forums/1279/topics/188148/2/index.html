    Originally posted by ^tamago^:
    i thought 77 was introduced as a FAC service along with other new services introduced during their time? used Mk III and later Striders?
    Yes, 77 was introduced as a FAC service from Marina Ctr to Farrer Rd in 1995, all MK3s.

    It was later extended to Bt Batok somewhere in 1996-1997, Striders were added in.

    Service 77 was later converted to PAC in June 1998 when some services (eg.7, 14, 16, 61etc...) were made FAC. NAC MB0405s were used initially then the Volvo MK2s.

    In Jan 2000, service 77 was reverted back to a FAC service when some MK2s were converted to AC buses.

    In June 2000, service 77 was then handed over to Tibs, MB0405 OACs and CACs, Merz Volgrens were used.
