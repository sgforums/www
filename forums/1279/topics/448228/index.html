    Said the STOMPer:

    "This Caucasian man audaciously parked his bicycle in front of a SBS bus 197 when it came to a halt in front of a bus stop near Bukit Merah at 6.40pm, Feb 28.

    "He got up the bus and accused the PRC bus driver of attempting to force him off the road (while he was cycling) on two occasions.

    "Not being able to communicate in English, the PRC driver could only stare back at him in silence.

    "Passengers on the bus heard the Caucasian man threatened the driver, saying 'whack the hell out of you' and 'this is Singapore, not China'.

    "The Caucasian man used his mobile phone to call the police as well, claiming that he is not letting the bus go till police arrived.

    "I feel that he is simply ridiculous, overbearing and inconsiderate. Regardless of the unproven and groundless accusation, his actions delayed all the commuters who had to get off the bus and wait for the next bus.

    "Police arrived in about 10 minutes. No details on how the dispute concluded as I had to leave the scene by the time police arrived."
