    Originally posted by Tident:

     Oh, anything special when parking out? Juz curious.

    Well....early in the morning around 5:10am, when those 334 BCs drive out their buses from the depot, they will park their buses along JW St 51 and JW Ave 1, either sleep at their cosy seat, or lock their buses, go to Blk 502 hawker centre, have a plate of noodles or take-away a packet of coffee. I witnessed those before.

    Once time is up, around 5:25am, the first BC on duty will drive his MB O405 towards the bus-stop 28541 of JW St 42, have a final check of the lights, engine and controls. Once 5:35am is up.. he's off to pick up his few batch of passengers and his shift for the day has offically started.
