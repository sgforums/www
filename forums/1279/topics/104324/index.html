  • BluBlur
    26 Dec 04, 03:22
    On the issue of EDS vs plastic desto, the EDS is easier for BCs to manage. For example, the current practice for Woodlands feeders 911, 912 and 913 during a checkpoint jam will be avoiding the Woodlands Ctr Rd, and then switching between the routes to ensure continuity of service.

    Eg, If plastic is used, a Sv 913 BC will have to bring the plastic board for 911, change them on the spot, then continue his journey. With EDS he can just press a few buttons.

    As a result (seems so) SMRT buses are so highly mobile, not many buses are fixed onto a service number nowadays.
