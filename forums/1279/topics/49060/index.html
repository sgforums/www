    Sv. 170 passengers are allowed to use the ez-link card. I believe that when passengers alight for security check at Airfreight Center, they have to scan the card at exit reader, meanwhile driver programs the system to recognize where they boarded from and charge fares accordingly. After check they board, scan the card, the system remembers it and they pay the exact stipulated amount.
