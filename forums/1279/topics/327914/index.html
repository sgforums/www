    Logo, attire set express service apart
    5 Jul 08, the new paper

    WE refer to Mr Jason Heng Ching Sheng's letter, 'Be clearer about charges', (The New Paper, 30 Jun).

    Allow us this opportunity to inform that our Fast Forward services are clearly differentiated with a bold 'Fast Forward' logo which appears just beside the service number.

    This is to minimise confusion of Fast Forward services which run parallel to normal trunk services.

    In addition, bus captains driving Fast Forward services don a vest to differentiate themselves from the regular parent services.

    At the bus stops where Fast Forward services call, details including their routes, operating hours and fares charged can be found - once again to minimise confusion with normal trunk services.

    Since we introduced Fast Forward services in 2005, we have not encountered many instances of commuters boarding the wrong bus, but we will continue to monitor the situation.

    We thank Mr Heng for his feedback.

    Tammy Tan
    vice president
    corporate communications
    SBS Transit Ltd
