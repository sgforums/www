    just to explain

     

    Express services are only 502 518 and 506

     

    the rest are under different categories. becareful on how you relate such bus services. if u said express only means 502 506 and the 518s.


    Express - bus routes that simply go express and charge the express fares
    eg: 502  506  518
    Very orginal routes. Express buses were first rolled out in 1995

    Fast forward - Bus routes going along orginal bus routes but skip many several stops so as to achieve 20 percent faster running time .

    eg: 14e skips many stops n east and only oeprated the Orchard to bedok part of normal 14
    eg: SMRT 188e - runs entire 188 but skips many stops mainly on AYE

    Premium- these are flat fare routes. they bring you from township to XXX area super fast.
    eg: Shenton Way Premium like the 535 or the SMRt546 etc
    eg: CityShopper svc 576
    Premium services are good for students cuase.. 1 dolalr flat fare for concession card holers... power.
    eg: Tong tar Tpt svc 581 which runs Jurong East to NTU charging some high fares.
    eg: SMRT teachers estate shuttle svc to Yio Chu kang stn aka svc 590.

    Bus Plus
    orginal offered bby SMRt
    eg: svc547. charges diferent fares from pPremiums.
