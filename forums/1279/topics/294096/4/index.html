    Besides their King Long brand...Xiamen King Long Automative (ie, the main factory) also has another brand called Golden Dragon (direct translation), which is shown in those two photos, at least one full (should be 12m) coach example has been spotted in Singapore, the company is now trying to sell Toyota Hiace/Nissan Urvan like passenger vans in Singapore.

    Then we have another factory, called Suzhou King Long Automative (subidary of King Long), which has produced majority of King Long coaches found in Singapore and Hong Kong. The Suzhou King Long also has its own brand...called Higer. There is a Scania coach produced by Higer under licence from Scania (available only in China).
