    The transfer rebate is applicable for all transfers between basic bus services (feeder and trunk) except for transfer for the same number. Transfer rebates also applies if you transfer to/fro MRT.

    For MRT/LRT transfer, the transfer rebate has automatically factored in if you transfer seamlessly between the two modes. For example, from Queenstown to Petir LRT, if you exit at Choa Chu Kang MRT, then reentry at Choa Chu Kang LRT, you will not enjoy 25 cents rebate.

    Some non basic bus services offers transfer rebate, but that is at the discretion of the public transport operators.
