    i suppose tat's not a "bus emrgency stop button" but rather the emergency door cock button on the outside of the bus. pressing it opens the door, but at the same time enables the door-accelerator lock, preventing the bus from moving off until the door is properly closed again. thus, it appeared to most who do not know the real purpose of the button that this bus is used (solely) to stop the bus.
