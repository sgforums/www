    To add on for SMRT,

    Service 853 ropes in 3 rigids from service 854 to cater for Sundays and public holiday route to Upp East Coast (normally done by TIB736T and TIB943L nowdays).

    In the past service 854 had additional bendies from weekday s-shift to cover for the rigids that go over to service 853. Then there was another time whereby spare OC500LEs from AMDEP will replace bendies on service 854, but the plan had been abolished already. Now is back to normal sunday deployments for service 854.

    For TIB736T, it used to do service 882 as an additional full day bus on sundays and public holidays, that was until TIB820H went into service 882 perm due to route enhancement.
