    Weekend Fleet for..

    S27
    Sat: 22 out of 31 buses are used
    Sun: 19 out of 31 buses are used. Hence not surprising if you see many 27 K230s running around and yet not being able to spot a cameo on 27

    S55
    Sat: 13 SD blocks. If all perm SDs are used, you get 3 DDs running or 3 SD cameos if the DDs decide not to run.
    Sun: 10 SD blocks. Just nice 55 has 10 SDs in the perm fleet.

    S56
    Sat: 8 SD + 2 DD blocks. No problem here as there's 8 perm SDs on 56
    Sun: 9 SD blocks. Will get 1 SD cameo if all the DDs are not operating and all perm SDs running

    S58
    Sat/Sun: 13 SD blocks. Will DDs are not operating you will get 1 SD cameo and all perm SDs running

    S59
    Sat: 13 SD + 1 DD block. No operating issue here
    Sun: 14 SD blocks. No operating issue also. Just that weekends may see the T shifts having DDs.

    S100
    Sat: 17 out of 24 buses running
    Sun: 14 out of 24 buses running. Hence not surprising if you see S100 buses running around and yet not being able to spot a cameo on S100

    S154
    Sat: 21 out of 31 buses running
    Sun: 16 out of 31 buses running
