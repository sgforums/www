    The proposed peak-hour shortworking trips are justifiable for SBST or TIBS to consider. However I would like to express my disappointment and puzzled on Service 69A.

    As some of you might know, this service operates from Tampines/Bedok Interchange to Temasek Poly. Most of the other shortworking trip services will turn back to their originating point, ie Service 2A will turn back to Changi Village Terminal (from Tanah Merah MRT onwards) after serving its duty. But in the case of Svc 69A, and particularly trips starting from Tampines Int, will continue its journey WITHOUT any passengers towards Bedok Interchange after serving its duty up to Temasek Poly. Isn't it better if SBST increases the frequency of the service, rather than add a 'shortworking' trip. It's rather wasting to see a bus continuing its normal journey but without any passenger.

    Anyone has any other services to highlight?
