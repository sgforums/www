    Originally posted by The_Bus_Guide:
    There was also a bus terminal marked at Sembawang Rd, near Jln Ulu Seletar. Any re-collections?
    That is one bus terminal you missed out in your list of lost bus terminals/interchanges. It was called Upp. Thomson Terminal and was used solely by svc 171, which terminated there after coming from the city via the zoo. Do not confuse this terminal with the Old Upp. Thomson Terminal which held the very old, defunct svc 71.

    Later that terminal was scrapped (both were, actually) and 171 was brought forward to Yishun which later on, very much unfortunately was makan'ed by TIBS when SBS was forced to hand over certain treasures at gunpoint. Otherwise the north will have one more SBS service providing competition.

    The location of Upp. Thomson Terminal is currently within the expanded premises of Nee Soon Camp.
