    16 Oct 03, 16:28
    SBST service 60 (MacPherson terminal - Shenton Way)

    A loop service, it via MacPherson Estate, Aljunied Rd, Lor 22 Geylang, Guillemard Rd, then follow current service 70 route to Shenton Way.

    It was re-introduced last year, with the integration of services 226 and 385.

    SBST service 194 (South Canal terminal - Yong Siak St)

    A loop service to Tiong Bahru, it via South Canal Rd, North Canal Rd, New Bridge Rd, Outram Rd, Tiong Bahru Rd, Tiong Poh Rd, Guan Chuan St, Chay Chuan St, Yong Siak St, Moh Guan Terrace, Kim Pong Rd, Tiong Bahru Rd and return. Withdrawn in 1992.

    It was re-introduced in 1994, as the current service between Boon Lay and Jurong Bird Park.

    SBST 402 was introduced in 1994, as a loop service between Bedok interchange and SAFRA Resort (Changi Coast Walk), plying the same route of the current service 35 up to Changi Coast Rd.

    It was withdrawn in 1995, when loop service 35 was introduced to ply between Bedok interchange and Tanah Merah ferry terminal.

    Info on Changi Ferry Terminal

    Changi Ferry terminal is also known as Johor-Singapore ferry terminal, located at the end of Changi Ferry Rd. This ferry terminal is for ferry services plying to and fro Desaru in eastern Johor.

    No public bus services ever served this ferry terminal.

    If I'm not wrong, this ferry terminal belongs to Malaysia. Any drivers can drive their cars or motorbikes onto the ferry and then drive out into Desaru.

    Woodlands Feeder Services

    Former Tibs 900 (Woodlands interchange - Ave 3 WRI - WCT)
    Via Woodlands Centre Rd, Ave 3, St 13 and Centre Rd. Later amended to operate from WRI to WCT via St 13 in both directions. Finally integrated with service 901 to become intra-town 911.

    Current Tibs 900 (WRI - Dr 14)
    Current route.

    Former Tibs 901 (Woodlands interchange - St 81 Woodlands interchange - Ave 4 WRI - Ave 4)
    Via Woodlands Centre Rd, Marsiling Rd, Rise, Woodlands Ave 5, Ave 2, St 81, Ave 9 and return. Then it was extended to Ave 4 via Ave 7, St 83, Ave 4, St 82, St 81, Ave 9 and return.
    In Feb 96, with the opening of the Woodlands MRT line, all the feeder services except Tibs 901 are shifted to operate from WRI. This made residents along St 81 to 83 furious and complain to Tibs. Finally Tibs agreed to let service 901 operate from WRI instead of WCT and introduced service 904 to maintain the link between WRI and WCT via Marsiling Rise and Rd.
    Finally integrated with service 900 to become intra-town 911.

    Current Tibs 901 (WRI - Dr 16)
    Current route.

    Tibs 902 (Woodlands interchange - St 32 WRI - St 41)
    Via Woodlands Centre Rd, Ave 1, St 32, Ave 3 and Centre Rd. Later amended to operate from WRI to St 41 via Ave 1 in both directions and skipping St 32. Finally integrated with service 905 to become intra-town 912 and extended to WCT.

    Tibs 372 (Woodlans interchange - Marsiling Rd) Tibs 903 (WRI - Centre Rd)
    Service 372 via Woodlands Centre Rd, Admiralty Rd, Marsiling Dr, Lane, Rd, Dr and Admiralty Rd.
    In Feb 96, re-numbered service 903, via the current route.

    Tibs 904 (WRI - WCT)
    Originally part of service 901 route. It was introduced in Feb 96 when Tibs decided to let service 901 be relocated to WRI, after complaint from the residents.
    Via Woodlands Ave 5, Marsiling Rise, Rd and Woodlands Centre Rd. Finally integrated with service 906 to become intra-town 913.

    Tibs 905 (WRI - Ring Rd)
    Integrated with service 902 to become intra-town 912.

    Integrated with service 904 to become intra-town 913.
