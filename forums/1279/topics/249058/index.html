    I assume you will be at Bedok central, so here is my suggeston:

    1. Take MRT to Queenstown, alight, and at the concourse, turn left and walk to the bus stop there to take route 195. Can be quite crowded though (I sometimes take route 195 from this station to CMPB, so I know).
    2. Alternatively, if you want to avoid the crowd, turn right at the Queenstown station concourse, and take route 51 from the bus stop there. I only tried this once in the reverse direction, so I'm not so sure about the crowd. However, route 51 has tons of double decks, so the situation may not be very bad. It takes a rather indirect route, though.
