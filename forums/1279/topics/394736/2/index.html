    But there are certain bus makes no sense to chop off:

     

    They are:

    966, 985, 75, 36, 48, 518, 502 and 506.

     

    Reason:

    966 and 985 mainly is East - (North) West cross province bus with majority of the route in Expressway (PIE and BKE as well for 966)

    75 -- Mainly serves Kim Seng area to Orchard then Ulu Pandan / Bukit Timah (Which is quite Ulu and the usage not high) then end at BPJ

    36 and 48 -- Actually, 36 cannot be chopped becoz it is a "Airport Express Line" in Airport, Marine Parade then CBD (Suntec, City Hall and Orchard). It is because of its loop nature to have the fare stage so high (966 also btw). Unless Eastern Region Line is ready (By 2020), I see so reason to chop 36.

    48 is similar, in the sense that it is quite direct service to BNV, but not loop lah. I see the reason to chop 48 or maybe, shorten the route to Rochor Canal like 23 when the DTL Stage 2 is ready by 2015 because people go Bugis or even Rochor MRT station, take DTL to Botanic Garden then transfer CCL Stage 4 will do.

    502, 518 and 506: They are Xpress service with low utilization due to a higher fares although 502 seemed to have more passengers because of the Western area is big and far from CBD nature. So by shortening or axe the route, esp.502 will cause inconvience in Jurong West / Pioneer new town. 506 and 518 serve East Coast and Pasir Ris / Bedok Reservoir people respectively to Suntec. I see no reason to axe, despite it is long.
