    Under DBTF, (reference to Oct 08 Fare Structure)
    To Geylang Polyclinic, Joo Seng side pay $1.01 (+8c), Windsor Hotel, Aljunied Park & Merpati first stop pay 91c (+20c), Merpati Blk 14, Circuit Blk 77, 36, MPSS pay 81c (+10c), the rest of the bus stops unchanged.
