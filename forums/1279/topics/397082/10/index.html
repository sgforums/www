    LTA launches new Distance Fares Calculator

    SINGAPORE: Commuters can go online to check their fares before Distance Fares kicks in from July 3.

    The Land Transport Authority (LTA) will launch the new Distance Fares Calculator on the PublicTransport@SG portal Wednesday to help commuters calculate their exact fares.

    With fares shown against each journey option, commuters can then choose one that best suits their transport needs.

    LTA says distance-related fared will bring about a more integrated fare structure where fares are calculated on the total distance travelled in a single journey.

    It will also allow commuters greater flexibility in their travel choices.

    Commuters do not pay more if they choose to make transfers during that journey.

    Journeys with transfers cost more as commuters need to pay a transfer penalty.

    LTA will launch a series of road shows at selected bus interchanges and MRT stations with high commuter traffic to help commuters be more familiar with the new calculator as well as Distance Fares. - CNA/jy
