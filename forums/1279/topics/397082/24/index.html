    because the travel distance prior to transferring to Sv 179 is longer than 25.2km, thus decimating the cost of the fare on Sv 179 (3 fare bands for 2.4km at 1¢ per band).

    as for Sv 169, it's because your prior traveling distance before the transfer to Sv 169 is between 2.6km and 3.2km (which costs 71¢), causing your cumulative distance after the 1.7km ride on Sv 169 to fall into the 91¢ band.

    look at the fare as it is for the whole journey, not for every trip that makes up the journey.
