    Is it true that bus companies are now trying to close down the lower usage terminals to save costs?

    It recently first started with Svc 63 being amended to terminate at Eunos Int and Rumah Tinggi Terminal being closed down.

    Next, it was Svc 82 to be amended to terminate at Serangoon Int and Punggol Rd End Terminal to close down.

    Now, it is Svc 175 to be amended to terminate at Bt Panjang Int and Lim Chu Kang Rd End Terminal to close down.

    This shows that bus terminals serving 1 or 2 buses have a risk of being closed down and the following terminals I think are in the high risk group of being the next one to be closed:

    Admiralty Rd West Ter: Svc 980 may be rerouted to Woodlands Int or the new Sembawang Ter

    Ghim Moh Ter: Svc 100 and Svc 111 may be rerouted to serve the nearby Buona Vista Ter

    Queen Street Ter: Svc 170 may be rerouted to Marina Centre Ter or Geylang Lor 1 Ter

    Sembawang Rd End Ter: Svc 167 may be rerouted to the new Sembawang Ter

    Sims Place Ter: Svc 64 may be rerouted to Eunos Int

    What do you'll think about this?
