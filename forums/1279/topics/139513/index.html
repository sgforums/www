    RU = Restricted Use

    eg: Learner Driver cars at Singapore Safety Driving Centre (SSDC) have some RU plated cars. These cars are exempted from road tax, but can only be used within the centre's circuit; hence cutting down operating costs. These cars are not allowed to travel on public roads, as they are exempted from paying road tax.

    RU vehicles can also be found within Sentosa and Pulau Ubin.
