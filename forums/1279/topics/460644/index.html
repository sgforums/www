    33 Bedok Int --> Rumma Tunggi Terminal (loop)

    63 Eunos Int --> Kent Ridge Terminal

    Swapping the terminal points of the two routes, so that DDs can be deployed on 63. 63 has high loading from Bugis to Eunos, and 33 has high loading from Outram Park to Alexandra road.

    This way DDs can be used, and lesser buses will be needed on the two routes, yet providing people comfortable journey.

    54 - New Bridge Road Terminal / Bishan Int (via Chin Swee Road)

    174 - New Bridge Road Terminal / Boon Lay Int (via Chinatown)

    Swapping the Chin Swee/ Chinatown route for services 54 and 174. It is waste of resources for 174 DDs to ply to Chin Swee. The loading between Subordinate Court and Outram Park is never more than 30 pax. It will be better utilized if routed through Chinatown.

    Lost route of 174 can be covered by svc 54 still connecting Chin Swee to Clarke Quay and Outram Park MRT

    188 - Marina Center Terminal / CCK Int

    93 - Eunos Int / Clementi Int

    176 - Bukit Panjang Int / Harbourfront Int

    176 will terminate at Harbourfront instead of Bukit Merah. This is to make provision for DDs to use this service in future. 93 on the other hand has lost its loading from Harbourfront to Alexandra road.

    93 route will be modified.

    Alexandra Road (Opp Queensway Shopping Center) >> Depot Road >> Tehlok Blangah Way >> Tehlok Blangah Road >> Pasir Panjang road >> West Coast Highway >> Clementi Link >> West Coast Road >> Clementi Ave 2 >> Commonwealth Ave >> Clementi Int.

    This route of 93 will cover lost route of 176 and give an additional service on West Coast Highway. 93's lost route has alternatives such as 61, 100, 855.
