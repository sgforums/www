    Good news for residents of Tampines! A new trunk bus service - Service 4 - will provide better connectivity for commuters travelling between Tampines, Flora Road and Changi North Industrial Estate. The new service will begin operations from Sunday, 17 March 2013. Operating daily from 6.00am to 11.30pm, Service 4 will run at peak frequency of 10 minutes. This is the 7th bus service to be rolled out under the Bus Service Enhancement Programme. Check out the details at http://bit.ly/ZLEbCT.
