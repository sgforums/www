    SBS Transit Service 4 is actually a delicated bus service to serve residents in the Flora Road/Drive estate. There are about 9 large condominium projects around the area, with 3 more under construction. And also a single condominium at Tanah Merah Besar Road.

    The following condominiums are; Azalea Park, Ballota Park, Carissa Park, Dahlia Park, Edelweiss Park, Ferraria Park, The Gale, Hedges Park (U/C), Avila park, Estella Park, Palm Isles (U/C), Parc Olympia (U/C) and Compass Green.

    Concurrently, a ComfortDelgro Shuttle Bus service is available for Flora Road/Drive Residents, however, it cost $1.30 per trip. Its too expensive for a bus service. With the help of East Coast GRC MP, SBST 4 is inaugurated. 

    Changi North has also plenty of Industrial Companies, those mainly in the Aerospace Trade. Service 37 deals with the south end of Changi North. However, the northern end has no other bus services due to its restricted road space before 2010. Until recently then Changi North Way is extended to Tanah Merah Besar Road.

    For planespotters, Tanah Merah Besar Road (End) offers a clear view of the Runway (02L/20R), snap your pictures away!
