    Bus-Plus operates three scheduled services during peak hours. It ferry passengers from designated residential areas like Eng Kor Ave and Bayshore Park to the city like Shenton Way and Orchard and vice versa.

    The operating hours of our peak hour scheduled services are from 7 am to 9 am and 5 pm to 8 pm (Mondays to Fridays - excluding public holidays). Passengers can board the Bus-Plus buses at designated bus-stops along the routes. Waiting Time is about 20-40 mins

    Operating Details are as follows:-

    Service 541 - Bayshore Park to Shenton Way
    Service 547 - Meyer Road to Shenton Way
    Service 825 - Yio Chu Kang Interchange to Lentor Loop
