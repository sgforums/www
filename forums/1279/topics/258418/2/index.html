    i like buses coz its unique in its own way:

    - handstraps & bells (esp the strip bells)
    - the bus body is large enough to carry multi-colour livery and creative advertisements hence further enhancing its appearance
    - front & side destos, esp the acrylic/metal ones - got different designs
    - the bus route numbers displayed on buses making it look special & unique
    - various types of bus designs & engines, seating arrangements, configurations
    - bus got Metropolitan/City Buses, Interstate/Coach/Luxury catagories - with different engine types and designs
    - got mini, regular-sized, superlong,articulated,double-deck,bi-articulated and even articulated double-deck versions!
    - different types of bus tickets, bus service guides (eg from past to present, local to overseas)
    - the automatic doors on buses (i like seeing it open & close and also the sound!)
    - the interior lightings
