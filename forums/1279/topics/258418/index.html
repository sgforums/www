    The reason, for me at least, is that buses had a big influence on me during my childhood. I used to live near a bus terminus and my family went everywhere by bus because we didn't have a car.

    The thing I like about buses is that each type is different. Unlike cars, buses are not built as a whole. Only the chassis is produced by the factory. The bus body is assembled later, according to the requirements of the operator. So for one particular bus type, there can be many different types of bodywork styles.

    Another thig I like is the sound. Each bus is different. Some have loud howling noises, some make queer hissing sounds, some even whistling sounds. Sometimes, the sounds are unique for a particular type of bus. I enjoy listening to the sound and it sometimes puts me into a calm and relaxed state of mind.
