    I've been taking this bus for about a year and a half and I have some comments to make about it.

    Firstly, the bus is sometimes filled with rubbish left by inconsiderate passengers which even sometimes is not cleared by the bus cleaner. The seats are so dusty and it really affects those who have asthma like me and imagine you take the bus for 1 hour.

    Secondly, the bus engine is really noisy. I have to blast my music in order to listen to it. The seats are hurting my back and it makes my journey miserable.

    Thirdly, I feel that SMRT should remove the additional route to Bukit Panjang as it waste precious 12 mins of time of my bus journey. The current route is from Marine Parade to Woodlands Interchange. They included the extra route about 3/4 near to the destination. I also noticed that there is little or no passengers during the morning hours(7.30-8a.m) in Bukit Panjang.

    Lastly, I would like to say that I enjoyed taking SBS buses more than SMRT as the seats are more comfortable and it is less dusty.
