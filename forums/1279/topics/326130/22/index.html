    In Austria's capital, Vienna(Wien).
    Cross-country/Long distance buses for Eurolines and its affliates depart from Vienna(Wien) International Bus Terminal at Enkplatz, accessible by U-bahn Line 3, one stop before Wien Simmering.

     

    That Scania DD Ikarus operates from Rome(Italy) to Vilnius(Lithuania) via Vienna...
    It also takes stops at many other smaller but equally important destinations along the way.

     

     

     

    The high density SD bus brings pax from Austria's capital and capital's airport to Slovakia capital and capital's airport. Vienna International Busterminal(VIB) to Bratislava Airport via Vienna Schwechat Airport(1) and then Bratislava Nový Most(New Bridge) Centrum Bus Termini(2).
