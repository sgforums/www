    A Daewoo on Svc 303.. note the background shows Korea's famous dedicated bus lanes. only buses can go on it. My touring coach also used these lanes and zipped along at 70km/h without hinderance by traffic. Note the lane is 1.5 buses wide so overtakking if bus at bus stop possible.
