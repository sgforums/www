    KOREA set 1
    Ok so like i went on horiday in korea in may 2x 2005. the following are the pics i took.

    before anything muz explain their bus systems and stuff so u will understand what i about to describe.

    generally orea transport system underwent a compelte overhual in 2004. the state govt brough in more new buses and rationalsied the system. the bsues are mianly local products as they support the local bus building industry. Notably yndai and Daewoo are the main bus makers. howeer kia Motors make their own bus but coaches, the kia granbird coaches. later u will see.

    the bus system is as such:
    Red desto bsues are intercity or inter"state' express domestic bus... must use coach bus.
    Blue desto bsues are the Seoul metrooplis buses.
    Green desto buses are Seoul metro bus connector. liek feeder bus but go to specific MRT stations. these buses have 4 digit destos. i suspect the first two digits are for the train station number they go to. essentially like Tokyo ones.

    In Seoul buses cannot cameo as service stickers are plastered all over the buses.

    < U will notice that Rapid KL too have adopted the same color desto and same typre of bus sytem as in Seoul in 2006 (For Kl). >

    So Essentially say u live in suburb, u take a red bus svc, then find a Seoul emtro station and hop onto one of the 12 lines of Seoul MRT (one of them called SMRT!) . pop out again to either get on a town bus blue desto or the MT feeder of green desto. Hub n spoke system.
