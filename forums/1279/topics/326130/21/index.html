    RapidKL Part 1: Iveco Turbocities

     

    Back in March, There wasn't a lot of the Turbocities running around. This was a while after an article was posted regarding RapidKL's decision to dump hundreds of buses at a yard. Now, they're everywhere, in varying states and conditions.

     

    I 040 is a slightly refurbished unit, where the windows have been replaced. Other than that, the interior seemed untouched, and so did the exterior.

    This Turbocity (sorry for the block) received some mid-life refurbishments, including new interior, A/C unit (Sutrak) and front as well as new EDS units.

    This unit received a slightly different front, using a 2 piece windshield instead of a single piece. Also, this windshield doesn't extend to the EDS. This unit also received a Denso(?) A/C.
