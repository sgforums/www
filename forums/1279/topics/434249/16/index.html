    Just taken the MAN on a whim...

    This bus is absolutely unlike any type we have seen to date in terms of performance. Bus has got a great deal of power across a very broad spectrum of rpm. Very responsive under all kinds of traffic conditions. Very nice suspension and balance too, and the Gemilang body is pretty good. Aircon is good too!
