Services which bypass Security Checkpoints

  • Tomas_Rosicko

    Svc 9, 19 and 89 which go into Changi Airport Cargo Complex have to stop at the checkpoints along Airline Road, where police officers board the buses and conduct routine check, and only pass-holders can use these services to get to the CIAS cargo terminal.

    Withdrawn Jalan Kayu feeder services 214E and 214W have to bypass the Seletar Gate Guardroom and inspected by MPs/ RPs. Only pass-holders and camp personnels are allowed to use these services to enter Seletar Camp. But these MPs and RPs guard have been withdrawn a couple of years back and Seletar Gate is now free-access to all vehicles.

    SBS 160, 170 and SMRT 950 has to be inspected by police officers at both Singapore and Johor customs.

    Any such services u guys know which pass through security checkpoints ??

    You can't pass thru the checkpoint into Jurong Island if you do not have a pass. I used to enter Jurong Island last year for some working trips by a car. I had to exchange for a special pass with my I/C at the checkpoint & fill up a form before being allowed to enter. The form will ask for your particulars and your purpose of visit. Police will stop your car to inspect your car-boot. You are also NOT ALLOWED to bring any camera into the island with you.
