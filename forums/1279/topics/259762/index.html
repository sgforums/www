    =====Official Release=====
    Dated: 9th June 2007 12:03 AM
    by Singapore Buses sgforum

    A 14 year old teenager was happily joyriding on a bus at 7:30 pm when a chinese teenager, darely curses the happy passenger causing into a quarrel. The happy passenger, only to be know as Mr Azman was brutally cursed by the chinese teenager on a SBS Transit Bus heading to Changi Village. The driver took no notice of the incident. A chinese female scolded the chinese teen, which was later known to have diagnosed a severe mental problem kept on cursing Mr Azman.

    Mr Azman said in an interview at Changi Beach that he was saying 'blubber' to himself as the bus has encountered 3 red traffic lights. The unknown teenager then, points out the middle finger, causing Mr Azman to feel unpleasent. The chinese female sitting next to the weird teenager, which turns out to be the mother slapped the chinese teen for cursing Mr Azman, who was sitting right behind them.

    Although given warning, the chinese teen goes on cursing by pointing the middle finger. Mr Azman, although was angry at the time kept his cool. The teen, once again was scolded for his rudeness. After being pointed the middle finger for the 5th time, he went on to punch Mr Azman. Mr Azman was traumatized and angry and went to the back of the bus.

    The weird teen then went off the bus near Loyang Flyover along Tampines Ave 10 heading towards Loyang Avenue. He showed the middle finger and stick out his toungue while exiting.

    =====End of Official Release=====

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    This story is a true account.
    This is not made up.
    This happened on 8th June 2007, 7:30 pm, onboard SBS1712Z on Service 9 towards Changi Airport Cargo Complex
