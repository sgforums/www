    Service 170

    Service 170 (Red Plate) will use the coastal road and Jalan Lingkaran Dalam to Larkin Terminal.

    Service 170 (Blue Plate) will use the coastal road and Jalan Gertak Merah to Kota Raya Terminal.

    The services are nonstop enroute to Larkin & Kota Raya Terminals.

    How to get to City Square

    Alight at JB Customs stop and walk to the direction of the KTMB Railway Station. In other words, walk parallel or along the railway track.

    It's very easy. When you exit the immigations/customs checkpoint, you will see a shop selling magazines, newspapers etc. Beisde the shop is an underpass. Use the underpass to get to the other side. When you exit the underpass, walk straight pass the Mobil petrol station and Merlin Tower to get to City Square. You can't miss it.
