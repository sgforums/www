    Summary
    1. Service 159 is transferred from AMDEP to HGDEP. Ex-AMDEP buses were transferred to Service 57, while Service 159 uses a collection of buses from various HGDEP services.
    2. Service 57's Mk II buses were transferred from AMDEP to BNDEP for Services 32 and 66.
    2. Services 7, 32 and 66 are transferred from HGDEP to BNDEP. Some of their original fleet moved over to BNDEP as well, except Service 32 which had a complete fleet change on the BNDEP side.
    3. Other services affected to a minor extent include 64, 80, 147 & 165. Note that things may change on Monday where everything gets clearer.
    4. Speculated services NOT affected in this round are 64, 81, 86, 136 & 197.
