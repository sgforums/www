  • SBS9888Y
    11 Dec 08, 17:16

    Important

    1.    Observe safety as a priority, we do not want any casualties during the race.

    2.     Brief your team members to take down each other’s contact numbers in case.

    3.     Stick with your team members at all times.

    4.    Read all instructions clearly.

    Rules

    1.    Only travel by buses (any operator) or foot will be allowed unless otherwise stated.

    2.    If a team chooses to forfeit a task at a checkpoint, they may sit a 45minute penalty.

    3.    The checkpoint master will only give them their next clue when all team members are present.

    4.    A team will be given demerit points for disobedience to rules made by checkpoint masters for tasks at checkpoints.

    5.    NO bus guide and street directory related material  like service guides allowed.

    $2 will no longer be provided.

    Sorry for the late notice.

    Other than that, everything else is permissible but when running the race conduct yourself in a manner worthy of your own dignity.

    All other instructions would be found on your basic toolkit on the day of the race. Have fun.
