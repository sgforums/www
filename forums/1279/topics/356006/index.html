    some guidelines for foldable bicycles onboard buses & trains

    • Should not exceed 114 cm by 64 cm by 36 cm when folded
    • You're responsible for the safe carriage of the foldable bicycles
    • Should be folded at all times in the MRT/ LRT stations, bus interchanges/ terminals and on trains and buses
    • Wheels should be wrapped up if they are dirty or wet
    • Protruding parts likely to cause injury, dirty or damage property are to be covered
    • Should not block the aisles and doors
    • Only one foldable bicycle is allowed on each bus at any one time
    • Should use the first or last car, which is less crowded (for trains)
    • Not allowed on the upper deck of a bus or placed on the staircase leading to the upper deck
