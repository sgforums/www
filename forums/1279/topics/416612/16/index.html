    Originally posted by chefmac.org:

    what'sÂ a cameo?

    There's a deployment by the bus operators on which bus should serve which service daily.

    Cameo refers to a bus that is not deployed for that service, but goes to do that service instead for the day.

    Example.
    If TIB556X belongs to service 700, but it serves service 190 today, it will be a cameo.
    So it would become TIB556X on 190 (KJDEP 700)
