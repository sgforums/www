    Q1. Which 2 roads literally means 'small red soil' and 'big red soil'? What sole service plied near these 2 roads?

    Small red soil means Tanah Merah Kechil (Road), Big red soil means Tanah Merah Besar (Road).

    SBST 2, 9, 12, 14, 24, 31, 35 & 38 ply near Tanah Merah Kechil Rd.

    SBST 2 & 29 ply near Tanah Merah Besar Rd.

    Q2. Which is probably the first truly 'Townlink' service in Singapore? How many parts of the town the service plied, and name them.

    SBST 291 & 293. Svc 291 serve Tampines St 21, 32, 33, 81 & 83 while Svc 293 serve Tampines St 41, 45, 71 and Ave 3 & Ave 8. They each serve 2 parts of the same town.

    Q3. Services departing Bedok Interchange will ply these 2 previously un-named roads. Name them.

    Bedok North Drive and Bedok North Interchange.

    Q4. Name this popular food haunt in eastern Singapore.Which 3 services plied directly to this place?

    Bedok Market Place. SBST 2, 9 & 10.

    Q5. This is the only 'premium' service to terminate in front of a condo in eastern Singapore. Name the area and the service.

    Bus Plus 541 at Bayshore Park. Another possibility is Bus Plus 547 at Meyer Rd (Eastern Mansion).

    Q6. These 2 huge residential/industrial areas in Singapore has 4 MRT stations serving it. Name these 2 areas and the MRT Stations.

    Jurong East. MRT stations are Jurong East and Chinese Gardens.
    Jurong West. MRT stations are Lakeside and Boon Lay.

    Q7. Name the service which was made famous in a song. A bonus if you could include the singer's name as well.

    No idea. Is it a Chinese song? If it is, I think is Svc 88 and singer is Emil Chow...not sure...

    Q8. This 2 similarly named roads exist on 2 different locations in Singapore. Name them.

    Seraya Rd off Haig Rd in Katong area.
    Seraya Rd at Jurong Island.

    Q9. These 2 areas is served by 3 services each from Bedok. Name these areas and the services.

    Clementi, served by SBST 7, 14 & 196 from Bedok.
    Jurong East, served by SBST 66, 197 & 506 from Bedok.

    Q10. Where in Singapore is Jembatan Merah or 'Red Bridge'? What is the nearest landmark to this location? (Answers can be more than one)

    Do you mean Ang Mo Kio?

    Tanah Merah Kechil Road/Tanah Merah Besar Road , Service 2

    Q2. Which is probably the first truly 'Townlink' service in Singapore? How many parts of the town the service plied, and name them.

    Feeder 224, Bedok - Chai Chee Road via Bedok North Road and Bedok South Road

    Q3. Services departing Bedok Interchange will ply these 2 previously un-named roads. Name them.

    Bedok North Drive & Bedok North Interchange

    Q4. Name this popular food haunt in eastern Singapore.Which 3 services plied directly to this place?

    To be exact, the place is popularly known as Simpang Bedok. SBST Services 2, 9, 10

    Q5. This is the only 'premium' service to terminate in front of a condo in eastern Singapore. Name the area and the service.

    BusPlus Service 541, Bayshore Park

    Q6. These 2 huge residential/industrial areas in Singapore has 4 MRT stations serving it. Name these 2 areas and the MRT Stations.

    Jurong, served by Jurong East, Chinese Garden, Lakeside and Boon Lay MRT Stations
    Woodlands, served by Kranji, Marsiling, Woodlands and Admiralty MRT Stations

    Q7. Name the service which was made famous in a song. A bonus if you could include the singer's name as well.

    Service 13 (Bas Nombor 13), made popular by M. Nasir. The song is popular even in Malaysia, Indonesia and Brunei

    Q8. This 2 similarly named roads exist on 2 different locations in Singapore. Name them.

    Seraya Road, as mentioned by tranquilice. Others are Old Airport Road/Airport Road and Nicoll Drive/Nicoll Highway

    Q9. These 2 areas is served by 3 services each from Bedok. Name these areas and the services.

    Jurong East - 66, 197 & 506
    Clementi - 7, 14, 196

    Q10. Where in Singapore is Jembatan Merah or 'Red Bridge'? What is the nearest landmark to this location? (Answers can be more than one)

    Located near Kampong Java Road/Leng Kee Road. Nearest landmark is Tekka/Little India, Farrer Park & KKH
