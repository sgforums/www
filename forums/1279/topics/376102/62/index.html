    TIB427K is perm 963S159, the slot also formerly known as 169AM52. The old 169AM52 timesheet states that it will do 911E (WL St. 81 side) for a trip before doing 169A. Thereafter, the bus will do Svc. 169 from Yishun MRT to Woodlands and continue with regular 169 duties.

    The duty was changed due to introduction of 963E in the evening peak. The 169AM52 slot was renamed 963S159. The timesheet in the morning still remains, the 911E crossover and the 169A trip. It will continue to do 169 from then until S1 hours end and report back to depot. The bus for PM part of the duty would head from WLDEP to HBF and do 963E down to WRI and then report to WLDEP for the end of the shift. As to what I know, most of the time for SMRTB the perm buses do not really appear for the PM part of the S-Shift duties, hence its probably the reason why you hadn't seen it on 963
