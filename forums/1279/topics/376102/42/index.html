    Just visited SGWiki just now. Seems that some people have vandalised the pages and edited the deployments of certain buses. Well, here are some things listed below.

    1. 970 still has 10 AM/PM buses with 8 split shifts.
    2. TIB1054X is AM61, not AM 851 anymore.
    3, TIB569J, TIB674M, TIB731G, TIB733B and TIB734Z will be deployed on 856 to replace some of the missing Mercedes-Benz O405Gs on Sundays and Public Holidays. This is not true, it depends on the roster. And 856 has no other perm rigids than 742A and 751Z.
    4. 786Z is not perm 700 unless proven.
    5. An unknown bus will crossover to Service 911E (1 trip) before doing 963. Not true, this slot is TIB1242U.
    6. Any 1 rigid bus will crossover to service 966 during Weekdays AM Peak. Not true, this slot is done by TIB832Z.
    7. TIB661A operate during Weekdays AM peak only on 961. (What duty block does it belong to?
    8. What is this about buses from 178, 187, 852, 961, 966 are interexchangable? Actually, ANY bus in that depot can be exchanged, it all depends on the starter. This goes with exception for OCs and 950 buses.

    I know some of you people are trying to make the deployment pages as updated as possible, but don't vandalise it with information that is not true. Thanks. I hope this would not make SGWiki's info less reliable.
