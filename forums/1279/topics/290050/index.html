    I am using my own copy of my ticket so I shall type my example out:

    0465 25SE 911 1A 11 2653
    05:56 PA 90c

    0465 = TIB465A (in other words it is the rego)
    25SE = 25 September (also known as date)
    911 = Svc. 911 (also known as service no.)
    1A = Direction 1
    11 = Bus Stop no. (or is it Stage no.?)
    2653 = Serial No.
    05:56 = Time
    PA = Price Adult. For children is just a C, but last time they used PC
    90c = Price paid

    Used to have 2 more no. beside 90c. One is to show the amount deducted from the farecard (not sure though) and the amount left in the farecard. After the farecard was discontinued, the 2 side no. were gone because got no use at all
