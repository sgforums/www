  • service_238
    25 Aug 07, 01:55
    Long A shift hours are not as common as long P shift hours. long A shift hours means change shift after 4pm. long P shift is start before 1.30pm.

    S28 bndep and brbp the first few buses from the terminating point change shift very late due to the journey time and the P shift block. There's only 1 reason why the A shift has to change late - the P shift runs until after 1am. This means at least 5 trips for the P shift and a standard 6 trips for the A shift.

    Another example is S105, S30 or any other long haul trunk routes that has at least 1h 30min journey time during OFF PEAK hours.
