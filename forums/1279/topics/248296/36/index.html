    Originally posted by 566117:
    how do 'jump bus' work?
    I think i have said before. It occurs for single depot services whereby the driver drives different buses for different trips. Jump bus only occur at the terminating point where they change shift. This means for some shifts they take bus from interchange in the morning (so that jump bus can start), and end shift at the interchange (so that jump bus can end).

    Jump bus occurs from the AM Peak to the end of the morning meal break. Starts again during PM Peak for the evening meal break. Ends thereafter.

    Some examples are 12 15 22 24 25 86 88 100 130 133 135 231 235 291 293 358 359
    Exceptions are 92 and 36 whereby the driver jumps every single trip in their duty.
