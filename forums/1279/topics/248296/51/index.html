    Originally posted by BusSpeeder:
    can anyone tell me what are bus shifts and what do they mean?
    Shifts are like your normal work where you might assigned and need to either do morning shift or night shift.

    There are five types of shifts for SBST (I only know three for SMRT).

    SBST's shifts are:
    A: Stands for AM Shift. It's like your morning shift.
    P: Stands for PM Shift. It's like your afternoon and/or night shift.
    S: Stands for Split shift. BC drives from morning until the afternoon then go off duty, reports back in the early evening (1600 hrs something) and continues until the end of duty.
    E: Stands for Extended shift. BC drives from morning and goes off service at 8 to 9+. Returns at noon time and continues sometime till evening or night.
    T: Stands for Three Quarter (SMRT: xxx3QXX, where x is the Service Number and X is the order). It's more like your normal working hours from between 7 to 9 till evening.

    Correct me if I'm wrong.
