    It's just ironic that I have found missing seat screws on many of the VOs from all over, small parts of floorboard missing on one particular AM/BR MK IV DM3500, violent Scanias that rattle (and people DON'T seem to make a big fuss about it, compared to slow buses. Why?) Or even some B9TLs that seem near-dead. (Their door-closing chime sounds so much blander than the VSO's door closing chime.)

    1) Best buses from each depot
    2) Worst buses from each depot.

    --> Yes, it IS a ZF gearbox. Doesn't seem to showcase its performance. Weird.

    SBS 904 T (BN 36)
    --> aeroplane, no-no for the city, why is it still on 36??
    --> it looks like it's in a sorry state.

    SBS 1708 L (former AM 52 when I took it)
    --> I was told this was a decent MK II. The door nearly fully opened along PIE, without any BC interference.

    SBS 1784 R (current SL 79)
    --> I took it when it was full. Entrance sensor seems to be faulty, and there seems to be huge signs of an imminent breakdown. :S

    SBS 1984 E (BN 66)
    --> burnt from the front.

    SBS 1985 C (BN 2
    --> whining engine.
    --> suspension not expected of a good bus
    --> TOO FAST. Speeding along Paya Lebar is as good as risking standing passengers. (due to CCL)

    SBS 9681 B (former HG 51, when I took it)
    --> fast, so what?
    --> seems that the DTs have an unpleasantly dull colour scheme. Why is it so? No one talks about colour scheme when rating buses. Weird.
