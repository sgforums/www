  • ^tamago^
    15 Aug 07, 15:08
    Originally posted by dfs24:
    I was at pasir ris and saw some of the feeder buses there has got the sign "To interchange only". Anyone knows the purpose of putting up those sign?
    because they are townlink services (serves 2 neighbourhoods/parts of a town), but for that trip they will not travel to the other part of the town but will terminate at the interchange for a break.

    townlink services were originally created by combining two feeder services into one, allowing people to only pay the fare for one trip instead of two, and also saves the hassle of alighting and changing buses at the interchange.
