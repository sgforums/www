    The other day, someone asked for the timing of SBS 1711B. For this bus, you can normally catch it on Service 32 at around 1200hrs to 1400hrs and also in the late evenings (1655hrs to 1800hrs). These timings are those that depart from Bedok Interchange, and are weekday time slots only.

    Sometimes, this 'loner' in BNDEP can go and do other services. There was one occurence when SBS 1711B cameoed on Townlink 293. Four people, including me, witnessed the rare cameo appearance.

    In conclusion, it will normally do it's perm slot on weekdays, but may or may not appear on other services during weekends.
