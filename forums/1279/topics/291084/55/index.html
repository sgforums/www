    Post by Scania N113CRB luver: http://www.sgforums.com/forums/1279/topics/330680?page=9

    "Poor design of bodywork that hardly overcome structural resonance and excitation properties. The reason why Habits are one of those bodyworks that vibrates the least is because the Hispano engineers make sure that engine vibrations as well other stuffs does not excite the natural frequencies of the bodywork. If its excited, the entire bodywork can shake and vibrate.

    Take for example. Everyones' walking on a bridge right? You can hardly feel any vibration because everyone's walking at different frequency. However, if everyone is walking at the same pace, same movement etc like the soldier's march, the bridge itself will vibrate and shake, which can eventually lead to the collapse of the bridge,".

    ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    I believe this is the sole reason, which is the poor CDGE bodywork causing all these vibrations. Does the same go for the KUBs (Gemilang bodywork) that vibrate hard despite being new buses? Someone clarify please, I am not very sure.
