    Originally posted by carbikebus:

    SBST Cityshopper services are routes which link the towns to Orchard Rd/Suntec.

    KMB's Nathan Rd shopper (as it was called) was just a term for several routes travelling along Nathan Rd, they are primarily used for short distance travelling. Primarily used to attract people to take the bus rather than the MTR for that few stops.
