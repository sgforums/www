    hub reductions and Hypoids

    ever look and stop and stare at a scania k230ub rear axle? ever notcied  a cone shape object atatched to the axle n wheel?

    ever wonder why when the Scania k230Ub hit 5thgear, you hear this hub reduction like sound?

    hoho questions r answered here. i hope.

    As we al are famailir with now, ub reductions are atatched to mainly Mercs and MAns to ensure the bus have a good wheel turning efficiency such that fuel is not wasted. these hubs make a whirll whirll sounds. take a merc u will notice thse sounds.

    Hypoids are found in Scania k230Ub and our dear Volvo b7RLE Mark 3 bus. hypoids do he same function as hubs excpet that they are different by technical nature. i do not fully understand this, however they have just one aplanetary gear forming the hypoid. Hubs use two planetary gears.

    so u can 'cal" a hypoid a single hub..(that is of cuz nonsense). but juz for ourselves to understand hypoids n hubs do the same thing.

    So next time u hear the Scania go whirll whirll u know is the hypoids kicking in and they are of the  same type of bus mechanism as Hubs.

    gee i heard soemwhere the MAN 18.240HOCl-NL-R has a new gen of hubs that r so soft u can;t hear them...rats...i like hubs and hypoids sounds.
