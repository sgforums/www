    Originally posted by ^tamago^:

     

    if you read the whole thing about how rego works, you can find a lot more patterns. (: and maybe by then, you can recite the whole suffix series liao. =p


    Sxx series: for the other two letters, substitute A with 1, B with 2, etc until Z with 26. Do this equation: 1-9*(2nd letter)-4*(3rd letter)-5*(thousands place)-4*(hundreds place)-3*(tens place)-2*(ones place). Take mod 19 and you'll get a number from 0 to 18. From this, 1 will be A, 2 B, 3 C, 4 D, 5 E, 6 G, 7 H, 8 J, 9 K, 10 L, 11 M, 12 P, 13 R, 14 S, 15 T, 16 U, 17 X, 18 Y, 0 Z.

    For example, for SBS3816U, the 2nd letter corresponds to 2 and the third is 19. The equation 1-9*2-4*19-5*3-4*8-3*1-2*6 will equal -155. Taking mod 19 you will get 16 and thus, the letter is U.

    Hope this helps.
