    There are errors in the history section. It was mentioned that the service was re-routed to Teban Gdns terminal in 1995, but as far as I know, the service was already terminating at Teban Gdns terminal a.k.a. Jurong Depot in the 80s.

    It was on 12 May 2002, not 2001, that the service was extended to Jurong East interchange skipping Pandan Gdns, then on 19 May 2002, Toa Payoh aircon interchange opened. On 17 Nov 2002, the service was re-routed to ply Pandan Gdns again.
