    Currently, the route of service 143 in Pandan Gdns and Teban Gdns is very "stupid".

    I suggest that:-

    Service 143 to skip both Teban Gdns Rd and Pandan Gdns, it will via West Coast Highway, West Coast Rd, Jurong Town Hall Rd and continues its way to Jurong East Interchange. Alternate services are 51 in Pandan Gdns and 78, 79 in Teban Gdns.

    Service 285 to be extended to Pandan Gdns and Teban Gdns, in this case, the service may be converted to a trunk service. It will via Pandan Loop, branch of Pandan Loop, Pandan Loop, left into West Coast Rd, right into Pandan Gdns, left into Jurong Town Hall Rd, right into Teban Gdns Rd, left into West Coast Rd, left into Jurong Town Hall Rd and back. The branch of Pandan Loop will now be 2-directional, with service plate to indicate the direction.
