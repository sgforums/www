    Lorong Danau is a road which goes from Nanyang Avenue to Jalan Sungei Poyan. The speed limit is usually 35km/h (but now, it was increased to 50km/h by LTA). And, this is a public road. The forested area is on the right, but all the signs on the road junctions are brand-new. There are no buildings, apart there was no protected area sign, no sign showing '201 Squadron'. The eastern part has a protected area sign.

    Now many people use this road to go to National Shooting Centre, or Choa Chu Kang Columbarium. And on Old Choa Chu Kang Rd junction, they said 'Old Choa Chu Kang Rd (Jalan Sungei Poyan)'.

    And where's the 201 Squadron? I didn't see. The Old Choa Chu Kang Rd wad was using did not stop at National Shooting Centre.
