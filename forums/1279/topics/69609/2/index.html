    The first SBS air-con bus, a Leyland National 2, came in 1982. It was put on trial for a short period on sv. 143 (Toa Payoh - Jurong Rd 16.2 km).

    Svs 3 and 168 had existed since 1980, both were NAC in the beginning. When SBS bought over 8 Mercedes O302 buses from SABS, they were put on SBS's first air-con service in 1984. This new service was called AIRCON 168. Route details was the same as the normal sv. 168 except that AIRCON 168 operated from 0630 - 1645 with no operation on Sundays & PH. And it charged according to the air-con fare structure.

    Sv. 3 was also converted the same way as 168. In sv 3's case, the whole fleet was converted to AC and renamed as AIRCON 3. So, the first SBS service to be full AC right from its inception was actually AIRCON 118.

    Originally posted by tranquilice:
    Did service 3 originally plied Bedok - New Bridge Rd when it was introduced?
    Yes it did but it was a bit different. You see, in 1980 it was a semi-express loop service charging a flat fare of 70c. Route was as follows:

    Bedok Inter, Bedok North Ave 1, New Upp Changi Rd, Changi Rd, Geylang Rd, Guillemard Rd, Nicoll Highway, C'naught Dr, Stamford Rd, North Bridge Rd, South Bridge Rd, Neil Rd and New Bridge Rd.

    Return via Hill St, Vicoria St, Bras Basah Rd, Nicoll Highway, Guillemard Rd, Paya Lebar Rd, Sims Ave, Sims Ave East, New Upp Changi Rd and Bedok Inter.

    Note: Express between Changi Rd aft Still Rd and Stamford Rd bef Nth Bridge Rd.

    In 1985, it was a normal service charging stage fare. The express sector was deleted. The new route was as follows:

    Bedok Inter, Bedok Nth Ave 1, New Upp Changi Rd, Changi Rd, Geylang Rd, Kallang Rd, Crawford St, Nth Bridge Rd, Sth Bridge Rd, Neil Rd and New Bridge Rd.

    Return via Hill St, Victoria St, Kallang Rd, Sims Ave, Sims Ave East, New Upp Changi Rd and Bedok Inter.

    AIRCON 3 was extended to Jurong East Inter. in 1987 via Jln Bt Merah, C'wealth Ave, C'wealth Ave West, Boon Lay Way, Jurong East Central and Jurong East St 13.
