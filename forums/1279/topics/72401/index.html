    Another alternative is to alight at any stop along Jalan Eunos or Still Road, then transfer to Service 55*. From Service 55, you can alight at the terminating point, ie Mandarin Gardens. From there, it's only a short distance to walk to Bedok Jetty.

    The most easiest way to get to Bedok jetty:

    From service 966, alight at any bus stop along Still Rd or the bus stop along Still Rd South, transfer to service 13.

    From service 13, alight along Upper East Coast Rd at the bus stop outside "The Summit" (this bus stop is the 3rd stop after the junction with Bedok South Ave 1).

    After you alight, you will see the junction with Bayshore Rd, proceed to Bayshore Rd. Facing ECP, walk on the right side all the way to use the Bayshore underpass. After coming out of the Bayshore underpass, you will see the path which will lead you to the cycle and jogging track, Bedok jetty is on the left, not far away.
