    Basic Services

    Feeders - loop around town (one segment); fare capped at 3.2km

    Intra-town/Townlink - loop around town; the combination of two feeders; fare capped at  3.2km

    Parks - links to nearby parks, gardens; fare capped at 3.2km or flat fare

    Trunks - no more looping in towns; limited stops outside town and city; stops at schools

    Special services

    Stadium Direct - loops in town; links to National Stadium

    Other services

    Express - may loop around towns; non-stop outside town and city; use expressways; no transfer

    Fast Forward/Rapid - no more looping around towns; non-stop outside town and city; limited stops in towns; may use expressways

    Premium - connects mainly private housing to city area; non-stop outside city and private housing estates; use expressway

    NightRider/NiteOwl - bidirectional service between midnight and early morning; loops around towns
