    Originally posted by SGCar:


    Last time ads on SBS buses were full body instead of only Left, Right & Rear as of now. Last full body ad was Carrier on Volvo MKII IIRC.

    Cheers.

    At first, only SBS double-deckers have full body ads and like he said, even the front was painted in the advertiser's colours. Much later, SBS tried to have full body ads on its single decks. I think the Carrier ad was the only one to grace the whole bus. At least 3 buses had them. 1 NAC Scania N113, 1 Alexander B10M Mk-II and 1 Duple Metsec B10M Mk-II. It caused some confusion at first as commuters thought they were not SBS buses. So they put the SBS logo on the bottom nearside of the windscreen. Finally, the law decreed that the front must remain in the company's livery.
