  • ^tamago^
    9 Feb 04, 00:24
    1. MRT Ride
    You will be charged normal fares for your first MRT ride.

    2. Bus Ride
    Since this is a transfer from MRT to Bus (please board 30 mins from exiting the MRT fare gates) you will pay the normal fares minus a transfer rebate of 25 cents (Adult/Senior Citizen) or 10 cents (Child/Student).

    3. Second Bus Ride
    If you have boarded this bus within 2 hours from entering the MRT gate and within 45 minutes of tapping the reader to alight from the previous bus ride then this is also a transfer ride and you enjoy the transfer rebates.
