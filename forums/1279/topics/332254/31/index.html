    If I have posted this in the wrong thread, my apologies. :)


    Today afternoon some svc 81 and svc 53 buses had a short part of their routes diverted due to a traffic accident.

    At ard 4.30pm I happened to be on SBS9528L, svc 81 heading direction towards Serangoon Interchange.  When the bus was at Pasir Ris Drive 12, the bus would usually turn right, to enter TPE.  However, today the BC continued straight onto Tampines Ave 10 and turned right onto Tampines Rd.

    Original Route = Pasir Ris Drive 12 -> TPE -> KPE -> Tampines Rd (after KPE)

    Diverted Route = Pasir Ris Drive 12 -> Tampines Ave 10 -> Tampines Rd (entering at the junction near the temples / where svc 72 turns)

    Wen I asked the BC at the interchange, he said it was because a lorry had an accident at one of the exits along the expressway, hence some svc 81 and 53 buses heading towards Serangoon / Bishan had to be diverted.
