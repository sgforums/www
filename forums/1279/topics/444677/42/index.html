  • SBS8501U

    PA715B on Off-Service today. This Dennis Lance was spotted outside COURTS this afternoon, probably doing IKEA Shuttle Service.

    Pay attention to the doors closely. Notice that these are not the typical doors used for the Dennis Lances. Instead, you would have realised that these are actually doors from the SBST Mercedes-Benz O405! The red and white covering is just to cover up the minor details of the doors so they will appear more like ordinary Dennis Lance rectangular doors instead.  

    Some Dennis Lances do have different rear duct fittings as well. In this case, we have 12 rectangular ones, where there should be 6 circular ones typically.
