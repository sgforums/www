    A little off-topic but it's alright to post it here.

    Due to the disruption of train service from Yew Tee to Bukit Gombak, SMRT Buses were deployed to serve affected passengers from Yew Tee to Bukit Gombak heading towards Jurong East.

    They are:
    TIB857C (To Bukit Gombak)
    TIB1034D (To Bukit Gombak)
    TIB607H (To Bukit Gombak)

    In fact, TIB857C shows the OFF SERVICE on its EDS, while TIB1034D & TIB607H didn't display anything on its EDS at the time of spotting.

    Feel free to post the regos of other buses deployed if you happen to see it or post your comments here.

    Also take note that during the train disruption period, long queues for the shuttle buses inclusive of feeders 302 & 307 will be expected.
