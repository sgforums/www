    Let me give a constructive post

    B7RLE is quite good to me as it is special as it promotes social interaction through a U shaped back layout which allows passengers to see each other at a closer range, the overall look of the bus was also quite appealing and the bus look modern.

    However, B7RLE weakness is the seats problem, as old people need to stand if no kind soul wanna give up their seats, however it could be solved through public education talks and also be solved by putting into routes that are generally low in demand or short-haul routes.

    The B7RLE in Sentosa are generally well deployed as it show the transport in Singapore is good and it also allow foreigners to place their bags or some packages at the standing space and i felt that it was a good buy.

    Perharps SBST may replace 0405T with B7RLE instead of k230UB as it is generally more well planned than KUB

    MY DEPLOYMENT OF B7RLE

    SBS 8700 - 8750 BNDEP

    Will did feeders like 225 or 292 or short haul services such as 69

    SBS 8751 - 8830 AMDEP/BRBP

    Will do feeders or high load short haul services and airport services for streamlined look with airport and sentosa having same buses

    SBS 8831- 8870 ARBP/BBDEP

    SBS 8870 - 8887/ 8889-8899 SLBP

    Some feeders and JIS

    I feel SBS Transit should consider buying a few more Volvo B7RLEs (let's say 50 to 150 units) with the demonstrator's seating layout (but not the bodywork - there's a reason why I call it the Freaky New Bus  ) for their Premium and Express routes.
