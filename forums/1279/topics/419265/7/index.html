    10/4/2011

    • At 1605hrs, SBS900D (BNDEP 9) broke down just before the junction of Simei St 3 & 4.
    • Direction was twds Bedok Int.
    • Suspected due to engine malfunction as it suddenly died off when BC wanted to move off as the traffic lights turned green.
    • Depsite several attempts to restart, bus was still not able to start.
    • Bus was declared breakdown @ 1612hrs, BC cancelled ride & all pax alighted.
    • All destos were flipped over & breakdown triangle was placed at rear of bus.
    • SBST Recovery Truck arrived location @ 1636hrs & mechanics were able to get the engine restarted.
    • At 1644hrs, bus RTD (not towed) with the Recovery Truck travelling behind it.
