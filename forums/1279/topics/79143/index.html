    Dear JobStreet.com member,

    We wish to extend you a warm invitation to attend JobStreet.com's Industry Nites. Held at the hip and central library@orchard, many JobStreet.com members have benefited from these regular informative networking sessions.

    For May 2004, our focus is on The Transportation Industry. Our special guest is Mr Roland Yeo, Assistant Director of SBS Transit Ltd, who will share with us the various aspects surrounding this Industry.

    With 12 years of experience in public transportation, Roland's expertise covers the strategic planning of routes as well as executing operational policies at ground level. He was a key team member responsible for obtaining the ISO 9001 Certification for SBS Transit. As their Assistant Director, he is responsible for customer service delivery and bus service recovery arising from major disruptions. He holds a Master of Science in Transport from the Institute for Transport Studies, University of Leeds.

    Details of the event are as follows:

    Date: Tuesday, 18 May 2004
    Time: 7pm - 8.15pm
    Cost: Free
    library@orchard
    391 Orchard Rd
    #05-22/26
    Podium Block, Ngee Ann City
    Singapore 238872

    So, bring along your friends and colleagues to an enjoyable evening at the library@orchard. To register, send an email to sg@jobstreet.com with the subject "Public Transportation" and indicate the number of guests who will be attending together with you. Our email acknowledgments for all registrations will be sent to you by next Monday.

    We thank you and look forward to meeting you next Tuesday evening.
