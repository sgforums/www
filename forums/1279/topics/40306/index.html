    During that time, svc 16 operates from Changi Airport to Bukit Merah via ECP, Marine Parade, Joo Chiat, city and Tiong Bahru. Later in Sep 1998, amended to serve between Bukit Merah and Marine Parade (Siglap Rd).

    Svc 16E is a peak-hour service to supplement svc 16. It plys one-way from Bukit Merah to the airport in the morning peak and return one-way in the evening peak. The route difference is that svc 16E travel express on ECP all the way from the airport before exiting at Rochor Rd, Beach Rd, then resume its route with svc 16 at city hall MRT. Svc 16E was renumbered svc 36 in Dec 1997. In Sep 1998, SBST amended the route of svc 36 to become the present route.
