    Services 85, 860 & 865

    Svc 85 at first ply from Hougang South Interchange to loop at Lor 1 Geylang, it was later withdrawn.

    In June 1998, Tibs introduced Svc 860 to ply from Sengkang to loop at Hougang St 21, later in Dec 1998, it was extended to HarbourFront and renumbered Svc 865.

    In July 1999, SBS took over Svc 865 and renumbered Svc 85 in Dec 1999.
    _____________________________________________________

    Services 80, 84 & 864

    Svc 80 used to ply Tampines to Hougang Central from Sep 1996. It was integrated with Svc 72 in March 1998 to become new Svc 72.

    Svc 84 used to ply Hougang Central to HarbourFront. Svc 864 was introduced by Tibs in Nov 1998 to ply from Sengkang to Marina Centre. In July 1999, SBS took over Svc 864, later in Dec 1999, Svc 84 & 864 integrated to become new Svc 80 to ply from Sengkang to HarbourFront.
    _____________________________________________________

    Services 54, 104 & 134

    Svc 104 used to ply from Serangoon to loop at Upp Cross St via Serangoon Ave 3 & Lor Chuan. It was later amended to terminate at New Bridge Rd Terminal, and via Bishan instead of Lor Chuan. Svc 105 was amended to cover Lor Chuan instead of Bishan.

    Svc 134 used to ply from Ang Mo Kio to New Bridge Rd Terminal, and later shortened its route to Bishan.

    In 1993, Svc 104 & 134 integrate to become new Svc 54. Svc 54 maintain the link of former Svc 134 from Bishan to Thomson and former Svc 104 from New Bridge Rd to Thomson. New Svc 156 maintain the link of former Svc 104 from Serangoon to Bishan.
    _____________________________________________________

    Services 160, 161, 167 & 850

    Tibs Svc 161 used to ply from Sembawang to Shenton Way. It was later withdrawn and Svc 160 (now Svc 980) was amended to ply Middle Rd (to cover Svc 161) instead of Arab St and Weld Rd.

    Svc 850 used to ply from Yishun to Bt Merah. It was later withdrawn after Svc 167 was amended.

    Svc 167 used to ply from Admiralty Rd West to WTC. It later follow Svc 850 route from Thomson to Bt Merah and amended to ply to Sembawang Rd End Terminal to cover former Svc 161.
