    From Sunday, 27/02/2005, SMRT service 175 currently plying between Lim Chu Kang Terminal and Choa Chu Kang Int/Upp Bt Timah Rd, will be amended to terminate at Bt Panjang Int via Bt Panjang Rd and Jelebu Rd. This is due to the road widening works at Upp Bt Timah Rd. As such, service 975 will loop at Lim Chu Kang Rd End and skip CCK Int.
