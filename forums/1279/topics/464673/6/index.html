    From my berth, I can really see the difference in SBS Transit and SMRT in terms of their service standard. For 950, the wait can go up to 20 mins in between buses. There are 3 SBS Transit staff stationed at the berth. 1 will take down the plate number of the buses that pass by, 1 will stand by the entrance door and the last one by the exit door. When the bus is packed to the brim, the two staff at the doors will hit the body of the bus, signalling to the bus driver to close the door and to move off.
