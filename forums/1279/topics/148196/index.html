  • hseochin
    19 Aug 05, 05:19
    TIBS/SMRT my preferred operator over SBST !

    But my selection is largely based on the bodywork issue of their buses only as i personally don't find overall too much significant differences in their service quality which i consider as relevant to me.

    Therefore because i take the other factors largely as 'constant', the single most obvious difference is the superior class of (most) of the bus bodies used in TIBS/SMRT fleet.

    I consider the Hispano bodies overall definitely superior to the Alexander, Duple-Metsec & Volgren bodies is soildness of construction, finishing & trim quality even aesthetics. But most importantly to me the Hispano bodies does NOT tend to emit all those annoying squeaks, rattlling & cracking noises so notoriously famous in the British & Australian bodies which unfortunately consitute the SBST fleet while TIBS/SMRT only have some.

    On the majority of TIBS/SMRT fleet, any squeaking, rattling noises tend to originate from loose (non originally standard) fittings but not from the bodywork itself & the probability of getting a bus which does NOT squeak, crackle or rattle is very much HIGHER than on TIBS/SMRT & almost zero on SBST buses !
    For me, that alone is excellent reason to board a TIBS/SMRT bus over another from SBST @ a bus-stop if say both going to the same destination i want to go to & same route or time ! Even if that costs a higher fare, i'll still select the same !! I don't mind paying any premium fare for such superior bodywork !

    Resolution of any bodywork noise irritants on TIBS/SMRT buses are more obvious & straightforward as such, compared to the situation on SBST vehicles whereby the standard of those bodywork are 'like that' only so what to expect ?!

    So overall (for me) because i tend to get (otherwise similar) bus with superior class of body from TIBS/SMRT, i select them over SBST ! In cases when i meet up with a TIBS/SMRT bus using the other bodies, then my preference for TIBS/SMRT over SBST becomes almost nil, instantaneously. Both then roughly equal in appeal to me.
