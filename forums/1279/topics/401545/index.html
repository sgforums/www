3 bus services to RWS

  • QX179R
    5 Jun 10, 21:44

    VISITORS will now find it easier to get to Resorts World Sentosa (RWS).

    Local operator Transtar Travel has rolled out three bus services: from Dhoby Ghaut and Toa Payoh MRT stations, and from Hock Seng Building in Lavender.

    There are four to six stops for each route, with ticket prices at $4 for adults and $1.50 for children.

    Last Friday, RWS itself launched a free bus service from Tiong Bahru Plaza. The buses, from Transit Network Consortium's fleet, run at 30-minute intervals from 10am to 10pm daily.

    Transtar's red mini buses, which started operation on May 28, ply between 8am and 11.45pm.

    Mr Sebastian Yap, CEO of Transtar, said it is still early to say if its bus services, aimed at Singaporeans and tourists, will be a success. Transtar has no plans yet to operate similar services to Marina Bay Sands (MBS).

    -- ST
