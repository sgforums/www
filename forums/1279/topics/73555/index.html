DPM Tan says Singapore to have train marshals soon

  • wuming78
    21 Mar 04, 20:30
    SINGAPORE : Deputy Prime Minister and Co-ordinating Minister for Security and Defence Tony Tan has announced that Singapore will be having marshals on MRT trains soon.

    He says there is a need for MRT stations and trains to be safeguarded as they are facilities used by many Singaporeans every day.

    The recent bomb attacks on Madrid railway stations highlighted the need to adopt a multi-faceted approach to counter terrorism.

    Singapore already has air marshals on certain passenger flights.
    -CNA
