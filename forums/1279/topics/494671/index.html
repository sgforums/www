    Originally posted by array88:

    I don't think 61's main loading is between Eunos and city... Loading on the west side seems to be stronger.

    61's loading is high and low along its route.

    Bukit Batok - Ngee Ann (high - DD worthy)

    Clementi Rd - Harbourfront (loading is lost especially with removal of flats along Commonwealth Dr/Tanglin Halt, the bus passes the winding route taking no pax. Rest route depends on svc 93, 100 arrival times)

    Harboufront - Outram (loading very much depends on 143, 166 arrival times)

    Bugis - UBI (this is where loading can again be DD worthy during peak hours). 

    Hence, IMO makes absolute sense to split 61 into two to cater to the high loading sectors.
