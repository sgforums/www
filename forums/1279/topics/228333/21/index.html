    The Volgren Bendy you mentioned is TIB1193C. Quite common as it still uses Service 903 buses for morning crossover.

    As for TIB1129P, it's not considered as cameo as 1 of the Service 912 bendies (Duty 08 if i remembered correctly) does crossover with Service 902 (for 3 trips) before returning to Service 912.

    Hence, Service 902 perm buses fleets are as follow:
    - TIB644A (which will crossover to Service 964 afterwards in the noon/afternoon)
    - Another rigids from WLDEP (can be any rigid buses, not sure whether that bus also does crossover to Service 964 afterwards)
    - TIB1140E (903/902 crossover, but 903 volgren bendy on Service 902 is quite common)
    - Duty 08 of Service 912 bendy bus.
