    So I shall explain to him
    Cameo: One bus, e.g. SBS1709J (perm on Service 175), operates on another Service (16) for one (or sometimes more) day. Usually, It will not be perm unless 1) the service it is on has been withdrawn, or 2) It is operating on a new service especially on the first day (and sometimes a depot transfer has been made)

    Exchange of Timetable: Two or more buses exchange timetables with each other - e.g. SBS9814K (on 65) usually comes to this place at 11:00am. But today, SBS9806J comes at that time. Is this a cameo
