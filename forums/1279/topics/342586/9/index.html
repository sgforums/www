    Temporary Route Diversions This Weekend

     Several SBS Transit bus services will be affected temporarily as roads will be closed to vehicular traffic due to several events taking place later this week, from Friday to Sunday, 10 to 12 July 2009.

     

  • Night Festival 2009
    On Friday and Saturday, 10 and 11 July, a part of Stamford Road between Queen Street and Bencoolen Street will be closed to vehicular traffic from 9.00pm to 12.30am to facilitate the Night Festival. During this period, 13 SBS Transit bus services – Services 7, 14, 16, 36, 111, 124, 131, 147, 162, 166, 174, 175 and 502 – will be temporarily diverted to ply via Victoria Street and Middle Road.

    Please refer to the poster for details.


  • NDP 2009 National Education Show
    On Saturday, 11 July 2009, nine Services – 10, 56, 57, 97, 100, 130, 131, 195 and 196 – will be temporarily diverted due to the following road and lane closures for the NDP 2009 National Education Show:

    RoadClosure Time
    Connaught Drive
    - Stamford Road to Fullerton Road
    3.00pm to 10.30pm
    Republic Boulevard
    - Raffles Boulevard to Raffles Ave
    9.00am to 11.30pm
    Raffles Ave
    - 3 left lanes of Temasek Ave to Nicoll Highway
    Esplanade Drive
    - Nicoll Highway to Fullerton Road (South bound)    		 8.00pm to 10.30pm


    Please refer to the poster for details.


  • Cycling & Walk @ Hong Kah Town 2009
    On Sunday, 12 July 2009, between 8.10am and 8.20am, Service 160 passing along Bukit Batok Road (towards Choa Chu Kang) will have to stop temporarily as a stretch of this road between Brickland Road and Choa Chu Kang Way will be closed to vehicular traffic to facilitate the event.

  • POSB Run For Kids ‘09
    On Sunday, 12 July 2009, between 5.00am and 11.00am, five services – Services 3, 62, 83, 85 and 581 – will be temporarily diverted when these roads are closed to vehicular traffic to facilitate this event:

    Punggol East
    - Between Punggol Drive and Punggol Field (In the direction of the city)
    - Between Punggol Field and Sengkang East Drive (Both directions)

    Sengkang East Drive
    - Between Punggol East and Sengkang East Ave (Both directions)

    Please refer to the poster for details.


    Affected services will not be able to call at bus stops along the affected routes.
