    MTR apologises for wrong charges

    12-11-2009
     
    The MTR Corporation has apologized for incorrect charges made on about 2,000 Octopus cards. The mistakes were made at West Rail stations and involve wrong charges of between 20 cents and $2 per trip, totalling about $5,000. The MTR said the problem occurred between last Friday and Tuesday, and involved passengers transferring from buses or light rail to West Rail. Affected passengers will receive compensation.
