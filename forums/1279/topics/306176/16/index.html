    Hi! Welcome to Buses on SgForums!

    Regarding about split shift and 14S, those refer to the category of timesheets.

    Here's a summary of things you might want to know about the timesheet:

    1 Types of Duties (SBST and SMRT)
    1.1 A/AM means morning shift
    1.2 P/PM means afternoon to midnight shift
    1.3 S/S1 means split shift, driver does duties from morning till around 1130 hrs to 1200 hrs, then go for a 'swing' (a.k.a. break) before coming back for duty from 1500 hrs till 1900 hrs.
    1.4 E/S2 means extended shift, driver does duties from morning till 0800 hrs, then go for swing before coming back for duty from 1200 hrs till 1900 hrs.
    1.5 T/TQ means three-quarters, driver can start duty anytime from 0600 hrs till 1000 hrs until 1900 hrs
    2 Duty Numbers
    2.1 Duty numbers are represented by the format, xD
    2.1.1 x stands for the number (in sequence)
    2.1.2 D stands for type of duty
    2.2 Example: 17T (means the bus is 17th bus in sequence and driver is doing three-quarters)
