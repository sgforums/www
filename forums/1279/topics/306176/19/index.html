    The Airshow was a publicity disaster, besides the long queue at Pasir Ris in the morning (due to the small amounts of buses they have deployed, and the fact that the company they chartered their buses from is not infamous for its standard of service), there was the jam along Loyang Ave, another jam along the Aviation Park Rd (the road/taxiway leading to the exhibition center), the queue before the security checkpoint, the queue after the security checkpoint, the expensive but not impressive food. Although the organizer and SMRT finally realized in the afternoon that they were short of buses and added more buses into the shuttle. Things like 629U, 1182J, 1244P got roped into the shuttle from 67 and 969.

    The last time Reed Exhibitions did Asian Aerospace 2006, they did a better job, got a more decent bus company for charter, and got food and beverages stores over the place. There was a queue at Pasir Ris Int then, but it was way shorter than the current one.

    2006: Queue was from the Newspaper kiosk to the Tekong shuttle berth.
    2008: Queue was from the MRT to interchange, then u-turning back pass the MRT till Pasir Ris Central and then left turn to follow the whole length of White Sands along Central until the access road leading to white sands, then u-turn towards the MRT, then pass the MRT to the shuttle bus stop just right after the Sv 3 bus stop along Pasir Ris Central. Best thing was, there were DDs which can seat (and stand) more people than the current seating-only (except for some buses) bendies for the shuttle (they sort of restricted people to seating only).
