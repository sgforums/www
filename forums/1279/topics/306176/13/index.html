    812's fleet gone haywire!

    Since last sunday it's been observed that AMDEP is sending rigids to do 812, most probably for the fact that passenger volume is low during sundays and public holidays. Last week was 0527E (AM 854) on 812, duty 812PM03.

    Today, the following buses were on 812:

    TIB 0818 R (AM 811) Duty 812PM06
    TIB 0982 Z (AM 854) Duty 812PM03
    TIB 1058 K
    TIB 1218 R
    TIB 1231 B
    TIB 1232 Z

    0982Z was replaced by another rigid in the evening, 0811J. Which makes it 2 rigids on 812 today. Judging by the current trend there is a possibility of rigid perms on 812 during Sundays/PH. Buses not on 812 today were TIB 1053 Z and TIB 1207 Y.

    Note: Weekdays/Saturdays still full bendy fleet. Only last friday there was an unknown UK Lance doing 812 in the early morning (first bus for eastern loop).
