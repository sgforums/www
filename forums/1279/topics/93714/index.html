    "Dear forum users,

    I would like to emphasize the various variants of the various Volvo B10M:

    SBS 1696 L - SBS 1895 D
    Volvo B10M Mk 2

    SBS 0582 M - SBS 0881 A
    Volvo B10M Mk 3

    SBS 0882 Y - SBS 0996 C
    Volvo B10M Mk 4 W/ DM bodywork

    SBS 1896 B - SBS 1999 M, SBS 2593 T - SBS 2688 D
    Volvo B10M Mk 4 W/ Walter Alexander Strider Bodywork

    ***SBS 2689 B - SBS 2838 M
    Volvo B10M Mk 4 W/ DM 3500 bodywork

    *** Please be noted that for the model within the range of rego, they are called as Mk 4, and NOT Mk 5. This is because:
    1. Such naming convention for that particular model may result in misleading. Hence, it would be thought as a successor of a Mk 4, which is not the case.
    2. A B10M Mk 5 has NEVER existed in the Volvo B10M Variants. Hence, calling a Mk 4 as a Mk 5 would be inappropriate.
