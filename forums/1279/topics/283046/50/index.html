    Originally posted by nenepokey:
    167/980 are dual-controlled by AMDEP & WLDEP. therefore, WLDEP buses can be there to cameo on the services stated above.
    Normally even though they are so called dual controlled, but they have their individual time sheets of which is either stated from AMDEP or WLDEP. This means that each depot will have a individual fixed number of duties on services 167/980, meaning AMDEP and WLDEP buses have fixed timeslots and don't overlap each other's timings.

    Eg. 1st bus of service 167 that departs from Bt Merah interchange is a AMDEP timeslot so only AMDEP buses (regardless of perm or cameo) will arrive on that timing. Therefore is not possible to have a WLDEP bus arriving at theat timing (unless official depot transfer).
