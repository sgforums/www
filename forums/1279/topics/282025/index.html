    179 has the best frequency.
    96 probably second.

    74 is the bus service which has in sgwiki has the highest number of buses deployed on it. 74 freq in my opinion is probably third.

    Not forgetting 25 too. 25 is probably forth because even in sats and suns and off peak, the freq are not bad. The buses come at regular intervals due to the short route and high demand along the route.

    8 in my opinion has not a bad freq too.

    Other trunk services with high freq in my opinion.
    9, 24, 27, 60, 70 (only during AM peak), 72

    Feeder services
    242, 243G, 243W, 225, 222, 291, 293, 261, 238, 334

    That's all I can think of.

    For SMRT,
    trunk services
    190 (best obviously)
    854 (next best)
    67 (freq not bad)

    Feeder service
    804, 806 (best freq freeder services)
    300, 302 (not bad)
