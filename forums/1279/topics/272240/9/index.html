    Originally posted by dfs24:
    Then now you mention that there are many other factors and the air con does not necessarily need to be at the other end of the bus.

    Sorry then, let me clarify

    Basically there are several factors which bodybuilders have to consider before they place the aircon on the bus:

    1) Length of overhang (rear and front)
    2) engine position
    3) Chassis structure and weight (ie how much weight it can take)

    Basicallly for buses with rear engine and relatively short rear overhang (like Dart and Olympians), the aircon can be placed at the rear as the bus is less likely to lean to the rear than a bus with a longer overhang (Physics: moments), also for these buses, the front load may be acting as a counterweight.

    However, for buses with a longer overhang, chances are that the bus may lean to the rear if the air con is placed at the back. CNGs are exceptions as the tank is also quite heavy.
