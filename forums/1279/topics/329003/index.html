    SINGAPORE: Twenty-one people were injured when a car collided with a private bus early Saturday morning.

    The accident took place near Singapore Expo, at a junction of Changi South Avenue One and Xilin Avenue.

    According to the Civil Defence Force, a woman was trapped in the front seat of the car.

    She fractured her wrist and had cuts on her body and head.

    Another person in the car and bus passengers suffered minor injuries.

    The injured were sent to Changi General Hospital.
