    Hmm.. it's been quite a long time that I've not been posting any topics over here...

    Well, I wonder how many peeps know about this little town named Serangoon, a NE Town in Singapore.

    Now... How many buses services are available in Serangoon Area?
    In terms of Interchange, there are Sv 81, 82, 101, 103, 105, 158, 315, 317.

    How about passing by services? Sv 22, 24, 25, 53, 55, 70, 73, 74, 76, 107, 132, 133, 135, 136, 147, 153, 156, 159, 165, and 853.

    These number of passing by services is an accumulation of Serangoon Central and Serangoon North. However, according to my 'base', ie Serangoon Central, I don't think there might be lotsa services here. Connecting from here to town needs at least 2 svcs for me to reach there.

    Although there is NEL for me, it requires me to walk for at least 5-7 mins to reach there. I do really find it hassle to do so, especially the construction of the NEL requires me to en route Also, taking the NEL does make my ez-link 'pop' and a few bucks gone. That is why I would always make full use of my Monthly Concession Pass to take as many buses as possible.

    318 route as follows,
    Serangoon Int, Serangoon Central, Boundary Rd, Lor Chuan, Serangoon Garden Way, Lor chuan, Boundary Road, Serangoon Central, Serangoon Int

    317 route (ex 217) as follows,
    Serangoon Int, Serangoon Central, Yio Chu Kang Link, Yio Chu Kang Rd, Serangoon Garden Way, Chartwell Dr, Burghley Dr, Berwick Drive, Tavistock Ave, Hemsley Ave, Kensington Park Rd, Serangoon Garden Way, Yio Chu Kang Rd, Yio Chu Kang Link, Serangoon Central, Serangoon Int

    It was later amended to ply along Upper Serangoon Road, Boundary Rd, Yio Chu Kang Link and the rest remain the same.
