    My bus-spotting today (21 April)

    Board Tibs 963 (427E) along AYE at Clementi. Journey takes 45 mins to reach Woodlands. Along Woodlands Ave 3, I saw Tibs bus 1228L off service going to the interchange. Alighted and wait to see if there's any chance to take 168 VSO to Bedok, but couldn't see any at the bus park. Finally at 11.15 am, I saw 9843B on Svc 161 arriving at the interchange. Still didn't see any VSO on Svc 168, so I take bus 2724H to Bedok, this took me 55 minutes. Along Bedok North Ave 3, I saw a MB 0405 (DM) on Svc 168 on the opposite side. Reached Bedok interchange at 12.20 pm, I saw 9837U on Svc 25. Board CSS 608 (181A) and saw 9820S on Svc 25 arriving. Alighted at the Nicoll Highway area and walk to Beach Rd, saw an accident involving a Tibs 980 Scania bus, a taxi and a motorbike at the bus stop outside Keypoint. After that, take Tibs 961 (forgot the reg. no) to Chinatown and transfer to SBST 147 (9216L) to Clementi, this 147 bus has very few passengers and journey was ok until it reached Clementi when the bus was struck in a heavy jam due to an accident of a car and a motorbike near NTUC Fairprice.
