    Slightly OT, but still relates to the area...

     

    In conjunction with upcoming facilities in Changi Business Park area, svc.41 & 126 will be introduced to temporary loop at the Changi South Ave 1, outside the future Changi Business Park Bus Terminal. These 2 bus services will cater the needs to certain MRT stations and quick getaway near to city areas.

    On the other hand, new svc.44 will bring you to the growing town, Punggol. The inconvenience along Kaki Bukit has been a highlight for this introduction due to the stretch without any bus service plying.

     


    Route Infomation:

    Red line - Express Sector to Destination Trip.
    Maroon line - Express Sector of Return Trip.

    41 (Habourfront Int <> Changi South Ave 1) [LOOP]:

    Green/Blue

    44 (Punggol Temporary Int <> Changi Business Park Central 2) [LOOP]:

    Brown/Grey

    126 (St. Michael's Ter <> Changi South Ave 1) [LOOP]:

    Black/Orange
