    Pertaining to service from West to East...

     

    Rt. Suggestions 30X & 283...

    As some of you might have suggested on having a service from West to Changi South Ave 1, this svc.30X will mainly serve residents as a parallel service, nearest to the MRT stations and along the corridors of AYE. 
    Svc.30X is also a route that slight differ, not to complicate from its parent svc.30/30e to shorten the travelling time further, using at least 70% of expressway to get to the other end.

    As for svc.283, it'll serve Clementi & Pandan Gardens estates. The first phase of the route will head to Clementi Ave 1 and bring residents along the sector to Clementi MRT Station busstop.The second phase will start from this Clementi MRT Station busstop to Pandan Gardens via AYE, West Coast Way & West Coast Rd before heading back to Clementi Int.
