    Potong Pasir terminal - located between blks 135 and 141 based on the 1989 street directory.

    Upp Serangoon terminal - located at Upp Serangoon Road end (where Serangoon Sec sch is), all the services were made to terminate at the newly opened Hougang Central int in 1995. Upp Serangoon Rd is currently extended to Sengkang/Punggol east.

    NTI terminal - located right inside the NTU, not very sure of its exact location.

    Punggol/LCK terminal - it is now a looping point for services 82 and 975 at its respective road ends.

    Originally posted by SBS8533C:


    It was known as Hougang Ctrl Ter. The interchange was split into two on 24 May 1998, to be exact.

    These were the services called at the two split interchanges.

    Hougang Ctrl Int > 89, 97, 111, 153, 165, 325
    Hougang Ctrl Ter > 74, 147, 501, 502

    In reality, it didn't really made any difference.

    All alighting was still done at the 'Interchange' and boarding was done at the bus stop just outside (and virtually sharing the same roof) as the temporary interchange.

    Only difference was that the buses parked at the 'Terminal'.
