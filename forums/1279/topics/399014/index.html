    2) Tuas Terminal used to have Sv 175, at the extended part of Pioneer Rd.
    3) Clementi Terminal was located outside Blk 437.
    4) Upp Bt Timah II Terminal is located at Bt Panjang Circus (now Bt Panjang Rd/Woodlands Rd)

    NB: The Upper Bt Timah I Terminal is at Upper Bt Timah Rd (near Ewart Cirsus), around Jln Anak Bukit.

    You should check out...

    Admiralty Rd Ter (Marsiling Ter) - located at Admiralty Rd for sv 180, 182, 950, 951, 952.

    Crawford St Ter - located at Crawford St for sv 160, 181
    Elias Rd Ter - located at Elias Rd for sv 403
    Jln Eunos Ter - located at Jln Eunos for sv 50 & 51.
    MacPherson Ter - located at Circuit Rd
    Old Upp Thomson Ter - located at Old Upp Thomson Rd for sv 71, 73.
    Jurong Depot Ter - located at Penjuru Rd for sv 143.
    Potong Pasir Ter - located at Potong Pasir Ave 1
    Punggol Ter - located at Punggol Rd Track 26.
    Serangoon Garden Ter - located at Serangoon Gdn Way
    South Canal Ter - located at South Canal Rd for 55, 192, 194.
    Tampines Ave 5 Ter - located at Tampines Ave 5
    Toa Payoh Temp Int - located at Toa Payoh Town Park (converted)
    Upp Changi Ter - located at Upp Changi Rd for sv 80 & 81
    Upp Serangoon Ter - located at Upp Serangoon Rd End for sv 84, 136, 153.

    Yishun Ave 5 Ter - located at Yishun Ave 5 for Yishun feeders 800, 801, 802, 803
    Yishun Ctrl Ter - located at Yishun Ctrl for Yishun trunks 850, 851, 852, 853, 854

    Originally posted by LTnF1fan:
