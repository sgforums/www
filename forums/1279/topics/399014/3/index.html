    Anson Rd Terminal was never a roadside terminal. The terminal comprises only a bus bay lots (which means no alighting/boarding activities from that terminal) and a bus stop erected along Anson Rd. Passengers would have to board the services from Anson Rd Terminal from a bus stop at Anson Rd just near the terminal itself.

    Previously, the location of the terminal was at the entrance of Tanjong Pagar Container Terminal, Scotts Green Rd.

    Services from Anson Rd: 130, 131, 180, 186, 193, CBD1
    As for 167, it used to terminate at Lim Teck Kim Rd.

    Before all services were being consolidated to run from Bukit Merah Interchange when it began operations back in 1981, the bus terminal for Service 16 was at Telok Blangah Street 32 where it was an open air carpark.  See map link for exact location http://www.onemap.sg/?SearchVal=78A%20TELOK%20BLANGAH%20STREET%2032&LW:Y&wO5d:fJIh_Y,JYhJf_b,aYqqf_R,hbqIh_I,q .  Service 273 was then implemented to serve the residents after Svc 16 was cut back from the Telok Blangah estate.

     

    There was also a roadside bus terminal for the original Svc 92 (from old paya lebar international airport to depot road via bukit merah lane) near the bus stop outside of the current ISS international school.

    http://www.onemap.sg/?SearchVal=ISS%20INTERNATIONAL%20SCHOOL%20(PRESTON%20CAMPUS)&LW:Y&wO5d:JhfYY_a,JRYqR_c,JfIfb_J,JRhfJ_a,c . The svc was extended to end at Clementi Interchange but was subsequently integrated with the current Svc 14 and renamed as Svc 14.
