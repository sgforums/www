    Originally posted by carbikebus:

    Engine strained not because of the Voith gearbox but more to restriction..You put ZF but restrict to 59kmh also no use mah.

    Not true, for the A22s speed limited at 60kph, the engine runs at higher revs at 60kph with Voiths as compared to ZF. This is because the Voith only has 4 gears while the ZF has 6 gears, and typically on the ZF the 6th gear kicks in at around 55 to 60 kph, meaning that at 60kph, the engine will be running at lower speeds (ie at the lower end of the tachometer).

    Therefore, in the above case where the bus runs at the speed limit of 60kph, the engine works harder with Voith as compared to ZFs, I would expect the ZF to have a fuel economy advantage over the Voith in this scenairo.
