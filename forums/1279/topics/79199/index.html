    13 May 04, 20:56
    [Ok Let me explain to all of you about the timesheet in detail.

    In the morning, the driver gets the bus @0627 hrs. He can get it earlier if he can and reach JUR earlier than 0646 hrs. Most of the drivers usually reach the interchange before time.

    The driver goes for Service 105B in the morning. It terminates at Clementi MRT Stn bus stop - the bus stop after Blk 317 (Fare Stage 9). From there, the bus goes off service to JUR again for a trip of Service 105, starting @0734 hrs.

    Usually, split shift drivers like this one have TEA stated in their timesheets if the break is about 12-15 min. In this case, it has one @SGN between 0913 hrs and 0926 hrs.

    Another 105 trip back to JUR @0926 hrs, reaching there @1056. After disembarking the pax there, it goes for refuel @JI, reporting there at 1121 hrs. At the refuelling station, it spends 8 min to refuel and its back to SLBP @1149 hrs.

    During this period of time its known as SWING for split shift drivers. During this time they can go home for lunch and do whatever they want. They may also stay at the depot but apparantly there's nothing to do there.

    The driver reports back to SLBP by 1549 hrs to take his baby for 2 more trips in the evening. After that its back to SLBP directly from JUR in the evening @1948 hrs.

    FYI, tamago copied the timesheet WITHOUT the places as he stated. Instead, the bus stop codes are used beside the time. As for interchanges, T1 on that timesheet is SGN while T2 = JUR.

    As for the JI refuelling station, i'm not sure what was stated on the timesheet.

    Cheers!
