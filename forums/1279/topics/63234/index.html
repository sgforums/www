    Boon Lay was once served by 4 TIBS services.

    187 to Bt Panjang
    178 to Woodlands
    852 to Yishun
    951 to Marsiling

    W.E.F 10 Mar 96, the role of sv. 951 was split. Sv. 187 was extended to provide connection from Bt Panjang to Woodlands while the route between Bt Panjang and Boon Lay was renumbered as sv. 180.

    Sv. 852 was to be withdrawn initially but later it had its route shortened to Bt Batok instead.

    Sv. 178 had provided connection between Jurong and Woodlands since long ago.
