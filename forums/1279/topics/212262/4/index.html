    That road is Kampong Bugis. It was used as a temporary diversion road for vehicles going to North Bridge Road from Lavender Street / Kallang Road when Crawford Bridge was undergoing upgrading that time.

    To cross over Kalang river, a temporary steel bridge was constructed between Crawford Street and Kampong Bugis. The steel bridge was located just before the current Sam Tat Building.

    It was used for quite a few years, about 3 or 4 years, can't remember how long, as the re-construction of Crawford Bridge took a long time.
