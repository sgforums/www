    In Switzerland, most commuters carry a bus pass and they would board the bus through any doors. You don't even have to show your pass to the driver. But if you think you can enjoy free rides, then you're doing it at your own risk. Uniformed and plainclothes inspectors check the buses regularly and you will have to show your pass/ticket without demand. If you don't have one, you will be fined. There's no escape because up to 3 inspectors will board the bus at once.
