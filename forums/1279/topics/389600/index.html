    Feedback on Service 184

    Hi SMRT,

    I am a SIM Student and recently, there has been significant changes in our school timetabling system, that has resulted in a lot of students squeezing into some of your buses (especially 184) during certain times and wish to take you into attention on the issue.

    Our school timetable has been changed into 4 fixed sessions instead of the usual staggered timetabling system due to an increased intake of about an estimated of 2,000 students.

    The timings are:
    830 - 1130 hrs
    1200 - 1500 hrs
    1530 - 1830 hrs
    1900 - 2200 hrs

    I have observed in the past 3 weeks of the school semester that the buses were too crowded and there are many students who were stranded at the bus stop.

    This is especially so during the 1445 - 1530 hrs, 1815 hrs - 1845 hrs on both directions of the SIM bus stop (for 184) when students go home after school.
    There is an estimated of about 200 students on each side of the bus stop during these timings and about 40% of them (80 students) are boarding 184.
    About 10 students on average have to be left out at the bus stop on almost every 184 bus that stops at SIM bus stop.

    I understand that efforts had been made in the past to cater to students, but the current schedule seems to be postdated back to the olden days' demand.

    I wish SMRT can reschedule the bus timings and consider deploying more buses to reach the SIM bus stop during the 1445 - 1530 hrs, 1815 hrs - 1845 hrs so that it would not inconvenience students from SIM and Ngee Ann Polytechnic.

    I hope SMRT would look at my suggestions. Thank you for your attention.

    With Regards,
    junyang700


    --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


    RE: Feedback on Service 184

    Our Ref: BS-SG-1001-0041

     

    Dear junyang700,

    We refer to your email below and wish to inform you that we are looking into the matter.

    We understand that the timings that you have mentioned are school dismissal times, which will see an influx of passengers taking bus service 184. As buses have limited capacity, we might not always be able to pick up all students in one trip. We also wish to highlight that the scheduled frequency for service 184 during the time concerned is between 6 and 15 minutes.

    Nevertheless, we thank you for informing us of the change in your school timetable and will forward your feedback to the relevant department for their review and consideration.

    We thank you for writing to us.

     

    Yours sincerely,

    Vivian Cheong
    Customer Relations
    SMRT Corporation Ltd
