    Answering your first question, it's service 22 (there may be more than one answer)

    In the past, superbuses on service 22 had their front destos displayed as:

    ANG MO KIO                              TAMPINES
             via                      22                    via
    JALAN EUNOS                       JALAN EUNOS

     

    A stretch of Bedok Reservoir Rd used to be named Jalan Eunos. But after the renaming, SBST did not changed the desto. It was until SBST received public feedback that it reprinted the destos with just the number 22.
