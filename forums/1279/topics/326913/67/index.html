    b) When taking the higher fare stage of the 2 services

    3) Which 3 services duplicate each other the most in terms of fare stages?

    4) Which pair(s) of bus routes meets and separates the most number of times? Must have common bus stops when they meet.
    (Eg. 139 and 145 meets at Toa Payoh, separates and meets again at Balestier Rd, separates and meets again at Alexandra Road before separating again. Therefore they meets and separates a total of 3 times)

    5) A and B are bus stop/interchange/terminal. Which point A to point B has the most number of bus services plying, all using different routes. What are the services?
