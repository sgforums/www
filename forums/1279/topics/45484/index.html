    16 Jun 03, 19:01
    Good news for Punggol Residents:

    " RESIDENTS in Punggol will have more bus services in their estate by the end of the year, their MP, Ms Penny Low, promised yesterday.

    The number of services has not been confirmed, she said, but it will reduce, if not eliminate, the need for residents to take a feeder bus to the Sengkang Interchange for a transfer out of the north-eastern corner of Singapore to the city centre.

    This 'mini interchange', as Ms Low described it, is slated to be at the present Punggol Terminal, which has only one bus service now.

    The terminal is next to the Punggol MRT station on the North-East Line, which is scheduled to be opened on Friday.

    The current 12,000 homes in the two-year-old Punggol 21 estate will get a more comprehensive interchange when the population in the area increases, Ms Low told reporters after a walkathon at Punggol North to raise funds to help needy residents.

    Punggol 21, when fully developed is expected to house 80,000 families."

    Article extracted from Straits Time Interactive, 16 June 2003, article "Punggol to get more bus services"
