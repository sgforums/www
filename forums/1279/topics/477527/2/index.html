    To give the simplest and dirtiest answer to TS: NO. 

    The 12m DD has seating capacity in mind, and id believe should be deployed for trunk routes with few or multiple mass boarding/alighting points to better utilize the seating capacity of the DDs. As SBST has no other high capacity bus types to cater to route requirements, the 12m DD has degraded into a 'general purpose high capacity bus' for routes that simply have pax that a regular SD fleet cannot cope with. Add that to the kind of stops that trunks have to make in towns and people travelling the longer distances are going to end up delayed/angry/pissed off (whatever describes angry).

    although there is a deeper and chronic problem with how trunk routes, throwing 12m DDs to meet capacity is simply a convenient yet shortsighted way of catering to capacity.
