    CCK Rd is now broken into 8 stretches:
    1. CCK Rd end - Sungei Tengah Rd
    2. Now part of KJE
    3. Junction with Jln Lam Sam - Brickland Rd
    4. Brickland Rd - CCK Terrace (disappeared)
    5. CCK Terrace
    6. CCK Ave 1 (CCK Terrace to CCK Way)
    7. CCK Way (CCK Ave 1 to Bt Batok Rd)
    8. CCK Rd (Bt Batok Rd to Woodlands Rd/Upp Bt Timah Rd)

    CCK Rd once stretched from CCK Rd end to Woodlands Rd/Upp Bt Timah Rd junction.
