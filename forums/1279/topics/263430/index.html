    I think I know the various shift for both MRT and Bus.

    AM shift: BCs who operates the first 3-5 buses will work on this shift.
    PM Shift: BCs who operates the first buses entering depot at end of operation hours.
    Whole working day shift: BCs working from 7 am to 7 pm.
    Split shift: BCs who drive AM peak buses and heads back depot after AM peak hours and same BC will operate the same bus again for PM peak.
    PM peak start shift: BCs who starts work at PM peak and would end his day as the last few buses to enter depot.
