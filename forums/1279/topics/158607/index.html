  • Apex-LW'21
    21 Oct 05, 20:37
    All 350 retired Volvo B57 with NZMB and Metsec body were shipped to Ireland when they reached the end of the statutory age of 15 years.

    DAFs previously on CSS are now in New Zealand.

    SBS Leyland Atlanteans with Alexander L were shipped to HK in 1993 and withdrawn in 1998.

    SBS Leyland Atlanteans with Duple Metsec body are now in Shanghai while the 1st generation of SBS LAs with Baco and 1977 Metsec body are now in Guangzhou.

    SBS LO2x & MB O305 that were retired now didn't export to other countries, instead they were scrapped for metal and spare parts.
