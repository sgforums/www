    I got 2 copies of Volume 1, compliments from the author. I haven't buy Volume 2 yet. Speaking about custom orders, you might be able to do so from Times Bookshop. A friend suggested me the idea for he has obtained a special book using the same method. You may want to take down the book's ISBN number, which is usually found on the rear cover, and state this, along with the title, to the salesperson. Additional charges may apply.

    Its a tough choice between the two. Both contain priceless information on SBS buses. Vol. 1 covers from 1971-1984 and Vol. 2 is a continuation until the present day. DDs and AC buses are covered extensively in Vol 2. If you prefer the more familiar images, Vol 2 is the choice. If you're an old school junkie like me, then its Vol. 1.
