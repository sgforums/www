    TTS fleet stands at 367 buses for 26 services not including 974.

    Advantages:Save cost and not paying lease for more spare buses.

    Disadvantage:Engineering have to work tooth and nail to ensure buses in good condition, early PM BC that start from depot at times have to wait for buses to come back,Danger of wasting trips..

    GAS:Need to unlease back 10 DDs in place of 10 SDs especially from SMRT.
