    Here is the TIBS Fleetlist for everyone's perusal.

    TIB 362M - TIB 377X

    Nissan Diesel U31RCN. Bodywork by Fuji Heavy Industries. Bus was launched in 1993.

    Engine: Nissan PE6H
    Gearbox: ZF 4HP500

    TIB 425R - TIB 561E, TIB 572Z - TIB 634D, TIB 798P - TIB 832Z

    Mercedes-Benz O405 with Hispano body from Spain. Introduced in 1994 - 1995.

    Engine: Mercedes-Benz OM447h
    Gearbox : ZF 5HP500

    TIB 562C - TIB 571B, TIB 635B - TIB 674M

    Scania L113CRL with Alexander Strider bodywork from the UK. Launched 1995.

    Engine: Scania DSC1171
    Gearbox: ZF 5HP500

    TIB 675K - TIB 724C

    DAF SB220LT with Alexander Setanta bodywork. Introduced in 1996-1997. Commonly found in the west.

    Engine: DAF LT160L
    Gearbox: VOITH D381.4

    TIB 725A - TIB 797S

    Mercedes-Benz O405 converted air-con. It has Hispano body and introduced in 1996.

    Engine: Mercedes-Benz OM447h
    Gearbox : ZF 5HP500

    TIB 833X - TIB 837K, TIB 839E - TIB 848D

    Scania L113CRL converted aircon. TIBS only no-step entrance bus. Introduced in 1996. It has Elbo bodywork from Greece.

    Engine: Scania DSC11
    Gearbox: ZF 5HP500

    TIB 838H, TIB 849B - TIB 904A

    TIBS' first articulated bus was O405G TIB 838H. All have Hispano bodywork and were launched in 1996, 1998 respectively.

    Engine: Mercedes-Benz OM447hA
    Gearbox: ZF 5HP500

    TIB 905Y

    This was the first new Hino HS3KRKA bus for TIBS in about 10 years. It is a demonstrator and 11 more were ordered eventually. Bodywork by Volgren of Australia. Introduced in 1998.

    Engine: Hino K13U
    Gearbox: ZF 4HP500

    TIB 906U - TIB 946D

    Dennis Lance 245 with Duple Metsec bodywork bought from UMW-Dennis in Malaysia. Introduced 1998.

    Engine: Cummins B245
    Gearbox: ZF 5HP500

    TIB 947B - TIB 957Y

    Hino HS3KRKA. Found at Bukit Batok/ Choa Chu Kang. Bodywork by Volgren. Introduced 1999-2000.

    Engine: Hino K13U
    Gearbox: ZF 4HP500

    TIB 958U - TIB 972C

    New generation of Mercedes-Benz O405 with Volgren bodywork. Introduced 2000.

    Engine: Mercedes-Benz OM447h
    Gearbox: ZF 5HP500

    TIB 973A - TIB 1001Z

    Second generation of TIBS O405G with Hispano "Citaro-copycat" bodywork. Introduced 2000

    Engine: Mercedes-Benz OM447hLA
    Gearbox: ZF 5HP500

    TIB 1002X - TIB 1016G

    Dennis Lance 211 ordered new from manufacturer whereas the Lance 245s were actually left-over stock. Body by Duple Metsec. Introduced 2000.

    Engine: Cummins B211
    Gearbox: ZF 5HP500

    TIB 1017D, TIB 1018B

    Dennis Lance 245 with Volgren bodywork. Chassis was purchased without body. Introduced in 2000.

    Engine: Cummins B245
    Gearbox: ZF 5HP500

    TIB 1019Z - TIB 1023K, TIB 1025E - TIB 1178A

    Third-generation O405G. Bodywork is designed by Pininfarina and the bodywork is by Hispano. Introduced 2000-2002.

    Engine : Mercedes-Benz OM447hLA
    Gearbox: ZF 5HP500

    TIB 1024H, TIB 1178Y - TIB 1197S

    This is an O405G with Volgren body. TIB 1024H was the demonstrator and it led to further orders of the bodykit. Introduced 2001.

    Engine : Mercedes-Benz OM447hLA
    Gearbox: ZF 5HP500

    TIB 1198P - TIB 1247G

    Hispano Habit O405G, same as above, except it has a Voith gearbox instead of ZF. Introcuced 2001-2002.

    Engine : Mercedes-Benz OM447hLA
    Gearbox: Voith D863.3

    Engine : Mercedes-Benz OM447hLA
    Gearbox: Voith D863.3

    TIB 1198P - TIB 1207Y uses a ZF gearbox. From there onwards, Voith gearboxes are used.
