    and yours is already answered, so here's mine.

     

    M, N, O, P, Q, R, S, T, U, V, W are 11 bus services.

    M and Q terminates at a terminal in the central/northeast region of Singapore. When Q starts its journey at that temrinal, it will pass by a Junior College, MRT Station , MRT Depot and a bus interchange. Then, it will turn into a road and duplicate M in the opposite direction although they start from the same terminal.

    Before reaching that particular road, M duplicates N and P for a bus stop along a road. Then, they will be seperated and eventually, they will meet each other again at a road.

    The bus interchange that Q will pass by is the terminating point for N, P and U.

    M will pass by another MRT Station after duplicating N and P for a bus stop and Q in the opposite direciton. V will also pass by that MRT Station, which means it will also duplicate M.

    The MRT Station that M and V passes by is very near to a bus interchange. That bus interchange is the terminating point for O, R and S.

    When O ,R and S starts its journey at bus interchange, O and S will duplicate each other while R will go in another direction and duplicate M in the opposite direction. After duplicating for a few bus stops, S will turn left and R will turn right. S will duplicate M and V. R will duplicate M and V in the other direction and eventually meet with M(opposite direction) and Q(same direction) at road. Then, it will duplicate Q for another few bus stops and eventually duplicate with V.

    R and W has the same terminating point.  

    After passing by the MRT Station, M and V will meet S. M and S will then turn left and V will turn right and eventually duplicate with O.

    W starts its journey at Hougang Interchange.

    T duplicates S for a bus stop in the opposite direction. T will turn right and meet M. Then. it will turn off and will eventually reach its terminating point. Outside the terminating point, there is a bus stop. R and U stops by there. Previously, V terminated at this bus interchange before getting extended to another bus interchange. Outside the bus interchange V terminated at, there is a bus stop. W stops by that bus stop.

    U will not duplicate W but their routes are quite close to each other.

    R will meet S in the opposite direction after 10~20 fare stages from the bus interchange.

    Find M-W. If you can find the bus interchanges/terminal, MRT depot, MRT stations and JC, good for you.
