    Mind boggling Trivia

    A is a loop service that makes a U turn at Road M.

    A intercepts B at every bus stop along Road M that B plies on.

    C is a service that intercepts B at two different points for 2 stops.

    D is a service that starts out with C

    Both of them intercepts each other for many bus stops from the start till they hit O, a medical institution, where they branch out on their own.

    However, D will intercept with C for 2 another bus stops again before going on and terminating at the same interchange as B, interchange P

    99 E is a service that serves the west with the far west, adopting a jump bus system and only intercepting with 196 F (at C'wealth Ave West) and G.

    196 F is a WAB service. 196 F's majority control (bbdep) has a full ADD fleet on Weekdays. Road N (Queensway) is the only road where 196 F, 198 G, 105 H and 123 I will meet altogether.

    198 G is the only service that ends at interchange Q (Boon Lay) with buses from depot R (bbdep) and the other services A-L except G do not terminate At Q.

    105 H is a service that has renowned crossovers (105/334, famous for CNG xover), it also intercepts 123 I on three different roads, S (Holland Rd), T (Napier Rd) and U (Tanglin Rd). 105 H is known to intercept many of the services from A-L. Both ending points of 105 H is not shared with any other service A-L.

    123 I is a service that meets with all services A-L except E. (196 F/ 198 G - Jln Bt Merah/Queensway, 105 H/186 J - Queensway, 131 K - Lower Delta Rd, 195 L - Tiong Bahru Rd)

    186 J is a service in which the direction of its route (clockwise) is somewhat like 93 H. Ending point V (Shenton Way) of service 186 J is non-boarding and alighting.

    131 K has the same ending point as 186 J for one end (St Michael's) and with 198 G and 123 I for the other. It passes by service 186 J's ending point, V, (Shenton Way) too. 131 K will intercepts 196 F for 1 stop (Fullerton Sq) on 1 direction and 3 stops (Clifford Pier, One Raffles Quay, Opp DBS Bldg) for the other at this area (Raffles Place Financial District)

    195 L is a loop service that intercepts 131 K at Road W (Telok Blangah Way/Henderson Rd) that doubledecker cannot ply through. It intercepts 93 H, 123 I and 186 J for 1 stop at its looping point (Queensway at former New Town Sec).

    Find Services A-L, Roads M, N, S, T, U, W. Medical Insitution O, Interchange P, Q, Depot R, Ending Point V.
