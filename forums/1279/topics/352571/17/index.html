    163,B,136,D,E,153,62 are basic services in Singapore. All serve at least one NEL Station. 163,B and 62 have demonstrators, and 136 contains the same digits as 163 but in a different order. B and D both share the same int/ter, and E passes by D's looping point.     there are 18 bus services from A - R, 3 roads from S - U, 1 expressway V, 1 terminal W.

    A is a service that starts/terminates, not a loop service, at eunos.
    B is a 1 way service which operates only in the morning.
    C is a service which operates another alternate service after evening on weekdays, afternoon on saturdays and whole day on sundays and PHs.
    D is a service which also operates another alternate service similar to C.
    E is a 1 way service that operates only during peak hours on weekdays and unknown for other days
    F is a service that starts/terminates from jurong east
    G is a service that doesn't terminate at a proper terminal and which the terminal has no resting place for BCs to rest before continuing their journey.
    H is a service which terminates in a eastern interchange
    I is a service which terminates somewhere near a cross-border place
    J is a service which both interchanges it terminates at are air-conditioned
    K is a service which terminates at a western terminal
    L is a service which, like G, doesn't terminate at a proper terminal and which the terminal has no resting place for BCs to rest before continuing their journey.

    N is a service which loops at a road quite near to a MRT station.
    O is a service which starts/terminates at a CBD terminal
    P is a service which terminates at a western interchange
    Q is a service which terminates at a terminal that doesn't allow boarding and alighting.
    R is a service which terminates at a north-western terminal

    S is a road that L pass by, which this is the only main road where there is 1 bus stop for L to meet other services. after this bus stop, L will only meet 1 bus service at a smaller road. this service that L meets terminates at an eastern interchange.
    B and J pass by a road T, which B starts service from this particular road. however B and J will not meet each other at all.
    C & D pass by 2 roads U with their alternate services. however, C & D pass by in opposite directions (1 direction each for C & D for whole trip) and both services doesn't touch each other's direction, nor does it stop at each other's bus stops.
    A & R pass by a expressway V. however, A & R will not meet each other at all at V though before entering V, A & R meet up at this road at the west.

    A and H intersect each other for 5 bus stops in 2 roads together. in the other directions. they intersect for 4 bus stops in 3 roads together. 1 of the place where they intersect/separate is near an expressway.
    A and F intersect each other at a bus stop on a road along an expressway. after that, A and F does not intersect each other.
    A intersect Q for 2 bus stops at the same road. in other directions, A intersect Q for 3 bus stops, also at the same road.
    C and P intersect each other for 5 bus stops in 2 different roads. however, when C is operating its alternate service, both service intersect each other for 7 bus stops in 4 different roads. in other directions, C and P intersect each other for 6 bus stops in 4 different roads and 8 bus stops in 5 different roads when C operates its alternate service.
    D and K intersect each other at 2 roads for 4 bus stops each and 3 different roads for 3 bus stops together, which C, D, K and O intersects. for O, it also intersects K for 1 more bus stop at a road before both link up to the road which C, D, K and O intersects. in the other directions, C and K intersect for 4 bus stops at 3 roads together, D and K intersect for 9 bus stops in a few roads together before separating and intersecting at another road for 4 bus stops while O and K intersect for 5 bus stops at 3 roads together. it is unknown of whether C,D and O does still intersect each other after they separate with K.
    F, H and an unknown service terminates at the same interchange.
    I and N starts/terminates at the same interchange. after I & N departs from the interchange, I intersect N for 1 bus stop at a road. both of them intersect at the bus stop opposite this, except that they are going back to the same interchange.
    3 unknown services terminates at the same terminal W
    E does not intersect any of the 17 unknown bus service, not do they terminate at the same interchanges/terminals as any of the 17 unknown bus services.
    G intersect C, D, O for 2 bus stops on 2 different roads. however, when G intersect them, C, D & O would be travelling towards different directions for 1 of the bus stop; the other bus stop C, D & O would be travelling toward the same place..
    H & J intersect with each other for 3 bus stops on 3 different roads before separating.
    H & M terminates at the same interchange. M does not intersect any of the other 17 services.
    P & R intersect each other for 8 bus stops in 3 different roads.
