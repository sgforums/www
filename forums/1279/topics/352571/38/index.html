    Trivia

    A 16 is a service that plies a road with a height restriction.

    B 111 is a service that makes a loop in the city.

    B 111 adopts a jump bus system and all of A 16 and B's 111 buses originate from one similar depot/bus park.

    A, 16 B, 111 C 123, D 124 and E 175 along with some of the other alphabets will all ply this road, but C 123 and D 124 do not call at the same bus stop at all along this road.

    C 123 and D 124 will meet again at a cross junction but when C 123 and D 124 meets, they will turn right from opposite directions.

    D 124 is a loop service that does not share ending point with anyone (A-M) but M 139 will pass D's 124 ending point 

    E 175 will cross intercept C 123 at 2 road junctions before meeting at the road where A-E meet. E 175 is also known to meet C 123 at the opposite direction of E 175 after the 2 cross-interceptions.

    F 74 will meet with B 111, J 100 and K 200 altogether at this road for only 1 stop.

    F 74, G and H will enter this road at the same time and ply all the bus stop on this road before leaving this road at the same time.

    F 74 is known not to meet any other service (A-M) thereafter.

    G and H share one ending point that all the other services (A-M) do not.

    H meets many services (A-M).

    I 64 is a long loop service known to intercept J 100 for 4 stops at the first 10 minutes of its journey.

    I 64 and M 139 is known to intercept H on both directions but H will only call together with I 64 and M 139 on 1 stop on 1 direction and none for the other.

    J 100 will meet A 16 when J 100 turns right onto this road. A 16 and J 100 will then turn right together at the next junction and separates when J turns left out of the next road while A 16 continues going straight. A 16 and J 100 do not call at any bus stop together.

    J 100 and K 200's service numbers have 2 identical digits, one of which is the last one. example, sv143 and sv543.

    L 143 meets all services except F 74.

    L 143 is known to intercept D 124 for at least 6 stops before splitting and meeting again for at least 6 stops.

    M 139 shares one ending point with L and the other with A 16 and C 123.

    M 139 will intercept with I 64 for many stops at one point of time and 1 or 2 stops at the next time they meet.
