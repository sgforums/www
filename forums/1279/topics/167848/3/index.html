    Howdy all!

    I'm not so sure as to whether this becoming a public forum can be a good thing. Firstly, the problem arises in that when the general public sees the way bus enthusiasts behave here, it creates a bad impression (hence the forum was closed, then opened...then closed...then opened again). I'm not speaking for any of the other senior members of the Singapore Bus Enthusiast community, but as an administrator and co-owner of several other discussion forums (which have a closed membership), I feel that speculations, rumours, gossip, and complaints about services should be kept off the public domain, for the risk of repercussions, potential lawsuits, libel issues, etc. is too high. I am not trying to undermine Jason's authority here, in fact, I wish him luck, but any further association by the senior members of the enthusiast community may be more restrained, or diminished as a result of the re-opening of this forum. It had dimished greatly in the 4 years it has run, and I feel that what elements of this highly respected group will be almost totally gone. I beseech all contributors, both lurking and regular, to watch what you post, reduce flaming, and not engage in activites which bring contempt and misrepresentation of our hobby.
