    how you can do this (tracking the time taken for the journey) is, if you have a Sony Ericsson phone like mine (w595) install the iphone theme.. it would have an additional clock on the wallpaper..

     

    as soon as u board the bus, unlock and then lock the keys or go to your walkman app, but just remember not to go back to the home screen..

     

    the reason, once you go back to the home screen, you can see the time just before you locked the keys or went away from the home screen for a few sec., before returning to the real time. So you can conveniently see the time you boarded the bus.


    At all times, the phone's original clock would work as normal and if you need to check the time during the journey, just press the volume key to see the standby clock
