    {This topic is dedicated to suggestions on bus routes in the West: Tuas, Pioneer, Boon Lay, Jurong West, Jurong East, Jurong Island, Clementi, Bukit Batok, Bukit Panjang, Choa Chu Kang, Tengah and the Western Catchment area(NTU, NIE, Jalan Bahar, etc.).}

    Rather than mix up with the other parts of Singapore, it may be better if there are regional versions of it, so that there can be more focus and better resolutions can be made; “Economies of Scale”.

    Let’s try it out. If this is more effective, maybe other regional versions of it can be created next time.

    From the original (combined) version of it, it seems that there are a few areas which can be looked into in the bus network in the West region:

    • (Very) long feeder bus routes.
    • trunk routes are heavily packed or only serve the main roads.
    • lack of inter-subzone bus connection across neighborhoods and sub-zones.
    • possiblity of re-routing SMRT services to Joo Koon Bus Interchange.
    • some roads are heavily jammed during peak hours.
    • lack of bus connection to nearest MRT station.
    • bus connections to other parts of Singapore.

     

    However, as it is combined with the rest of Singapore, things seem to be quite messy and there are no proper discussions on them. Because it is quite messy, and there are many pages, I may have missed out certain areas from the list above. 

    Maybe it is better to have a separate topic where we only discuss about bus connections in the West regional area.
