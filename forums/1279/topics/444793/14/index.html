    F1 Singapore Grand Prix - Extention of services and temporary route diversion.

    (Updated) Bus and Train services for F1 Singapore Grand Prix

     

    SBS Transit will be extending its bus and train hours to cater to the F1 Singapore Grand Prix event, which will be starting from Friday, 21 September until Sunday, 23 September 2012.

    1. EXTENSION OF TRAIN SERVICES << Click for redirection to website.

    • NEL
    • LRT

    2. EXTENSION OF BUS SERVICES << Click here for pdf. file.

    3. BUS SERVICES AFFECTED << Click for redirection to website.

    • Service 130
    • Bus Stops Skipped
    • Premium Services
    • Night Owl Services
    • Change in Originating and Terminating Stops
      • Service 195
      • Service 97e
    • Iris NextBus

      Bus Stops Skipped

    From 19 to 24 September, 20 bus services - 10/10e, 14/14e, 16, 36, 56, 57, 70/70M, 97/97e, 100, 107/107M, 111, 128, 130, 131, 133, 162/162M, 195, 196/196e, 502 and 518 - will be temporarily diverted due to road closures in the Marina Centre area. As a result, they will skip several bus stops along their routes.

    However, Services 10/10e, 70 and 196/196e - will continue to operate on their regular routes in the direction of Shenton Way only at these timings:

    • 5.00am - 9.30am (Daily from 19 to 22 September 2012. Excludes Services 10e and 196e on 22 September.)
    • 6.00am - 9.30am (24 September 2012)

       

    Please refer to the poster for more information.



    Premium Services
    From 19 to 24 September, the 31 weekday Premium services to the central business district will continue to operate as usual. However, due to road closures, these services will skip the following bus stops:

    • Fullerton Square (03011)
    • Opposite AIA Tower (03031)
    • Prudential Tower (03021)

    They will all terminate at the bus stop opposite PWC Building (03041) along Cross Street.

     

    Please refer to the poster for more information.



    Nite Owl Services
    On 21 and 22 September, the six Nite Owl services will continue to operate as usual. However, their trips will start from the bus stop in front of St. Andrew's Cathedral (04167) along North Bridge Road instead of from the Marina Centre Terminal. As a result, these services will bypass three bus stops along Raffles Avenue:

    • Before Temasek Avenue (02101)
    • Seating Gallery (02051)
    • The Esplanade (02061)

     

    Please refer to the poster for more information.



    Change in Originating and Terminating Stops
    From 19 to 24 September, the originating and terminating points of Service 195 and Fast Forward 97e will also be changed temporarily.

    Service 195
    Service 195 will be amended to operate from the New Bridge Road Terminal instead of Marina Centre Terminal. As a result, it will temporarily call at 10 new stops along Eu Tong Sen Street and New Bridge Road. However, it will skip seven bus stops along Raffles Avenue, North Bridge Road, North Boat Quay, Parliament Place and Raffles Boulevard.

    Please refer to the poster for more information.


    Service 97e
    During the weekday morning peak hours, Service 97e will terminate at the Marina Bay Financial Centre bus stop (03391) along Marina Boulevard instead of at the Pan Pacific Hotel (02089). As a result, it will skip the five bus stops along Bayfront Avenue, Temasek Avenue, Temasek Boulevard and Raffles Boulevard.

    The service will start its weekday evening peak hour journey after Marina Station Road (03539) along Central Boulevard instead of at the bus stop before Temasek Avenue (02101) along Raffles Avenue. It will then have to skip the eight bus stops along Raffles Avenue, Temasek Boulevard, Temasek Avenue and Bayfront Avenue.

    Please refer to the poster for more information.


    Iris NextBus
    From 19 to 24 September, iris NextBus will not be available for the affected 57 bus services, including the Nite Owl and Premium services.

    Passengers who query iris via SMS for any of the affected services during this period will receive an SMS informing them that the bus arrival information is not available. This incoming SMS will not be charged.




    During this F1 period, commuters may experience some service disruptions/delays in their travel due to road closures. We seek their kind understanding.
