    SMRT adds more wheelchair accessible bus services

    SINGAPORE: Four more SMRT bus service routes will have wheelchair accessible bus (WAB) services from April 29.

    The four services are 187, 852, 857 and 970.

    These WAB services will connect wheelchair-bound passengers to MRT and LRT stations such as Boon Lay, Bras Basah, Bukit Panjang, City Hall, Esplanade, Phoenix, Promenade, Raffles Place, Tanjong Pagar, Teck Whye and Yishun.

    They will also call at medical facilities such as Econ Medicare Centre & Nursing Home in Choa Chu Kang, Gleneagles Hospital and Medical Centre, Jurong Medical Centre, Jurong Polyclinic, Kwong Wai Shiu Hospital & Nursing Home, Mount Alvernia Hospital, and Singapore Buddhist Free Clinic in Jurong and Redhill.

    With these new WAB services, SMRT Buses will operate a total of 17 WAB services.

    Each WAB can accommodate two wheelchairs.

    Currently, 245 buses, or about 23 per cent, of SMRT's bus fleet are wheelchair accessible.

    - CNA/wm
