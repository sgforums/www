    The number one sure way of differentiating both CAC Mk 2 and CAC Scania: Check the back windows on its left side.

    CAC Mk 2: Last 2 windows are of equal size and shape. No intercooler.
    CAC Scania: Last window is smaller than that of the 2nd last window. There's an intercooler below the 2nd last window.
