    EDS,

    Pros:
    No need to make desto during route amendment
    No need to maintain desto, e.g. broken desto
    No need to make desto at all/Environmentally Friendly. (PLASTIC desto)
    Reduces logistics headache, such as ensuring that buses have the necessary desto

    Cons:
    Need to maintain

    One could argue that EDS consume electricity and in the end may consume more resources than a plastic desto. This one I leave it up to you to decide. Keep in mind LED are highly energy efficient.
