    8867cc (8.9 litres) is the engine capacity, not the horsepower. The horsepower is 230.

     

    KUB4X2 would be the chassis code. The chassis model is determined by combining the engine's location and its power rating. Eg: K230, K270, K310 etc. AUTO should be for automatic transmission. ABS means the bus is equipped with an Anti-lock Braking System. TURBO means that the engine has a turbocharger.
