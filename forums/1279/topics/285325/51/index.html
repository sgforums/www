    I think 24 is more of a marketing thing.

    See....ever since SBS withdrew 34, which was a limited stops service between Bedok and Changi Airport, 24's load has increased, making travelling to and from Airport from bedok more difficult. The introduction of low floor buses can allow passengers to have an easier time to load their luggage, which would then entice some passengers to take 24 instead of the taxis or the ever unreliable train.

    Target Market: People who are sick and tired of the trains and complaining taxi drivers (like what many Tampines people do, take 27 instead of taxis, although with a DM Mk4, it is an obstruction)

    It may sound a bit too ridiculous for SBST to do so, but with more competition (rail and bus) coming up, how would SBST not grab any chance to attract more passengers to take the bus instead of other modes of transportation.
