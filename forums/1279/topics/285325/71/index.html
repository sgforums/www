    When SBS bought 500 units of Merc AC buses in 1991, the buses were initially scattered in all the depots. That was also the time when SBS progressively started introducing partial AC fleets to its existing trunk services.

    Movements started only when Volvo MK3s came into service in 1993, HGDEP and BBDEP/ARBP then gave up all the Merc buses to other depots. BNDEP gave up the Mercs at a later stage when the Volvo MK4 Striders/DMs came into service in 1995. After which only JRBP (now SLBP), AMDEP/BRBP have the Merc buses. Whereas the Volvos are mainly given to BNDEP, BBDEP, ARBP and HGDEP.

    Unlike Tibs, when they bought those secondary models like Scanias, DAFs, Lances and Hinos, there were automatically assigned to its respective depots upon registeration (except for TIB905Y and TIB950R)

    Still not sure about the Scania K230UB deployments though, there were scattered in all the SBST depots except for BNDEP so far. Just wait till the MAN buses also comes into revenue service.
