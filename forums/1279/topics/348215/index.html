    This evening I sms IRIS for next bus arrival for SBST200 at bus stop 16019 (outside Haw Par Villa).

    It was1901h, and the reply was NextBus 4min.

    Looking at the approximate bus arrival time bus-plate, it says the next bus shall arrive at 1911h instead.

    Anyway, IRIS is using GPS and I shall not doubt the the reply.

    At 1905h and 1911h, I still see no sight of SBST200.

     

    At 1913h, I request for another update, and this time it says NextBus 27min. Oops I have been waiting at the bus stop, and for sure I did not miss any SBST200 yet.

    A minute later at 1914h, SBST200 finally arrives.

    So I spent 10cents (5 cents per sms) and I did not get any useful reply.

    How the IRIS had replied 4min (for my first first request)? I still wondering.

    SMS query for NEXTBUS is a wonderful application, provided the reply is accurate. And for me, I shall probably not use that service again.
