    Originally posted by bus555:

    Wow that's rare!!!! So this is the 2nd feeder to have DD (the first is 242). Just hoping that 291 will have DD soon :D

    242 wasn't the first and 225 wasn't the second. I believe the first was 334. I remembered seeing DDs on this service as early as 1987/88, first using O305 followed by the LO2X until its retirement. The second would be 240, using LO2X. Then came 242. However, 242 may be the first and possibly the only feeder to have DDs right from the start in 1990. Other feeder buses using LO2X in Jurong include 243, 333 and 335 (I can't recall if 331 had any). For O305, in Bishan, there's 410, in Eunos, sv. 385 and, according to the postings above, they were also used for the Hougang feeders. Many LAs spent their twilight years on the feeder services in CCK, Bedok, Tampines and Pasir Ris. Services include 300?, 302, 222, 359 and others.
