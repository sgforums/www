  • TIB1171R
    29 May 10, 08:17

    Service 640 is a Scheme B bus service which used to run all the way to the far end of Admiralty Road West (Marsiling area there). However its route has been cut (illegally or not, I'm not too sure) such that buses now start running only from Canberra Road.

    Do not be surprised if you have to stand in the bus even on its return journeys from Tanjong Pagar MRT in the evening peak hours. With a max fare of only $1.10 and a shorter journey time compared to SMRT 167 (it goes past National Library, Little India and KK Hospital instead of Orchard Road), it's not surprising people are willing to pack into the coaches for a cheap cold ride home.

    And like 167, majority of the passengers will alight along Thomson/Upper Thomson Roads.
