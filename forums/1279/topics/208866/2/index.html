    All you need to know about the IMF/WB Meetings Shuttle Bus Services.

    All participating hotels which are providing lodging for the IMF/WB delegates are divided into 4 clusters, namely Red, Yellow, Green, and Blue.

    Each cluster is further divided into sub-clusters, which are each allocated a shuttle bus service between the hotels in the sub-cluster and Suntec City. These buses are numbered by their cluster letter followed by their sub-cluster number, e.g. R1, Y2, B3, G4, etc. The desto shows 'Suntec'.

    Also, each cluster has 2 shuttle bus services between certain hotels in the cluster and City Hall (Registration Centre). These buses are numbered as such, A & B (Red), C & D (Yellow), E & F (Green), and G & H (Blue). The desto shows 'Registration'.

    Shuttle Bus Guide

    Hotels - Suntec City (Meeting Venue) Shuttles
    - All services operate on a continuous one-directional loop
    - Shuttle buses operate between 11 to 20 Sept 2006 from 7.00am to 10.00pm
    - Frequency for 11 and 12 Sept is every 60 mins
    - Frequency for 13 - 20 Sept is as follows:
    ~ 7.00am - 8.00am: 20 mins (Normal)
    ~ 8.00am - 9.00am: 10 mins (Peak)
    ~ 9.00am - 1.00pm: 20 mins (Normal)
    ~ 1.00pm - 2.00pm: 10 mins (Peak)
    ~ 2.00pm - 4.30pm: 20 mins (Normal)
    ~ 4.30pm - 7.00pm: 10 mins (Peak)
    ~ 7.00pm - 10.00pm: 30 mins (Off-peak)
    - On 19 and 20 Sept, Red Cluster shuttles will operate at a frequency of 10 mins from 7.00am - 10.00am

    "All you need to know about Service 36 at Temasek Ave"
    By SBS9889U

    Basically, Sv36 does not stop at the bus stop that Sv107 serves BUT serves the original Carrefour bus stop(Opp Centennial Twr) in direction going towards Tomlinson Rd/Orchard and the direction towards Marine Parade/Changi Airport. There is no form of indication whether a bus is heading to Marine Parade or Orchard hence most BCs would tell you where the bus is going or ask you where you are going.

    In order to find out about which bus is going where yourself, from the bus stop Opp Centennial Twr(Or something spelt like that), look out for any Sv36 buses from opposite. If one Sv36 bus appears opposite you, the same bus would bring you to Marine Parade or Airport but if the bus just appear coming from Suntec, then it is going to Orchard.
