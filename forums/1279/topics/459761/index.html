    Ever-thinking of designing your own fictional bus fleet? Here's a chance to do. 

    Here you can think out of the box and let your imagination go wild.

    Take note of the standard rules here:

    1. You are only allowed to use SINGAPORE BUSES. Do not use foreign buses, cos' can be very confusing.

    2. You are allowed to combine SBST with SMRT and use any of the buses you want.

    3. Double-deckers with bendies? This is allowed.

    4. You are allowed to reuse existing bus services numbers, OR create a new bus service number on your own, which I recommend. You may also design the new bus fleet for BSEP new services, for example 860.

    5. You are allowed to use deregistered buses. However, you are NOT allowed to used unregistered buses.

    6. You are allowed to edit or change your bus fleet at any time you want.

    7. THE MAXIMUM BUS FLEET SIZE FOR ANY SERVICE IS 49!

    8. Do state your bus service type, whether it is Truck/Intratown/Feeder/Express/Premium.

    9. You can choose to make your own destinations, which is up to your choice.

    10. PLEASE FOLLOW THE FORMAT BELOW. IF YOU DO NOT WISH TO INCLUDE SPECIFIC BUS TYPES, AT LEAST INCLUDE THE BUS MODEL.

    11. Appreciate that this is something to be fun and not real so dun try to criticize the other people's bus fleet just because you dun think it's nice.

    Yep, that's about it. Do refer to the sample I have created.
