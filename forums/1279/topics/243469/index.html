    Starting 9th April 2007, SMRT Buses will introduce "Fast Forward" service 854E between Yishun Interchange and Bedok Interchange, which will only run during the morning peak hours on working weekdays.

    Express fares will be charged for this service.

    The 3 trips for this service will depart at 6.40am, 6.55am and 7.10am from Yishun Interchange.
