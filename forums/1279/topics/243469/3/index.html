    SMRT launches Express Service 854E

    SINGAPORE: The new Express Service 854E between Yishun and Bedok aims to cut travelling time by up to 20 percent.

    SMRT said it would call only at selected stops of its parent bus service 854 and take alternative routes via the Seletar and Pan Island expressways.

    Fares for this new service range from S$1.56 to S$2.31 for payment by ez-link card and from S$1.60 to S$2.35 for payment by cash.

    Transfer rebates will be given if applicable, but concessionary travel does not apply.
