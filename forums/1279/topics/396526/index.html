    Singapore Buses

    Advantages

    - Better Connectivity

    Commuters can board buses at their doorstep and transfer to other bus services to reach their destinations but commuters may need to walk or transfer more to reach their closest MRT or LRT station.

    - Seat Availability (applicable only during off-peak hours for most bus services)

    Commuters will be able to have a seat on the bus during off-peak hours but there will still be no seats in trains during off-peak periods.

    - Longer Idle Time

    Commuters will be able to stay in the bus for a longer period to finish reading books, newspapers or sleep more.

    Disadvantages

    - Frequencies, Travel Time, Traffic Jams, Bus Delays (fan pai), Bus Breakdowns
