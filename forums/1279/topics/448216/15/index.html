    Jurong Bus Park (located along Jln Ahamd Ibrahim next to Jurong Bus Interchange) was actually a satellite depot to Jurong Depot (located near Teban Gardens along Penjuru Rd), something similar to the amdep/bram and bbdep/arbp arrangement.
