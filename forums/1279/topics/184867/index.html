SIA Hop-on bus now goes to Sentosa

  • sbst275
    3 Apr 06, 19:10
    By Sharon See, Channel NewsAsia

    SINGAPORE : There is now one more way to get to Sentosa -- Singapore Airlines' Hop-on bus, which shuttles between popular attractions in Singapore, will take you straight there from Clarke Quay.

    SIA first started the bus in 1999 and now ferries some 150,000 visitors annually.

    Passengers of Singapore Airlines and SilkAir pay S$3 for an unlimited day pass on the Hop-on Bus, while others pay S$12 for adults and S$6 for children.

    The airline hopes the bus will become a connection between the city centre and Sentosa Island.

    Said Huang Cheng Eng, executive vice president (marketing and regions), Singapore Airlines, "We know that for Sentosa Island, besides a lot of Singaporeans visiting it, we also want many, many overseas visitors to visit Sentosa. There are a lot of new attractions coming up. I think it's a good way to make it very convenient for our overseas visitors to visit Sentosa. "

    The Clarke Quay-Sentosa bus service operates daily from 10am to 6pm at one-hour intervals. - CNA /ct
