    811E,812E,912E... all these are intra-town(2 towns) svc.. it means tat it terminates at the yishun instead of going to another town.. like for e.g 811.. 811 goes to khatib area and chong pang area.

    example
    Svc starts at Yishun Int as 811
    then to Chong Pang
    and when it reaches the bus stop outside northpoint,
    the driver will change the no. to 811E.
    It works for every alternate bus.
    so if u wait at that bus stop. u will see..
    811/811E/811/811E/811/811E.... etc
    then it continues into Yishun Int(811/811E)
    then to Khatib
    and back to Yishun Int..
    those that take 811E must alight while those on 811 can continue to Chong Pang..
