    SBST 4 (Bedok - St. Miachael's Pasir Ris - St. Michael's Tampines - St. Michael's)

    Service 4 was extended from Bedok to Pasir Ris via the former service 19 route, and then cut short to Tampines, finally withdrawn when it was integrated with then service 21 (Pasir Ris - Lor 1 Geylang)

    Route (Pasir Ris - St. Michael's): Pasir Ris Dr 3, Dr 6, Dr 1, Loyang Ave, Tampines Ave 7, Ave 4, Ave 5, Ave 1, Bedok Reservoir Rd, Bedok North Ave 3, New Upp Changi Rd, Changi Rd, Geylang Rd, Sims Ave, Lor 1 Geylang, Boon Keng Rd, Lavender St, Balestier Rd, Moulmein Rd, Thomson Rd, Balestier Rd, Whampoa Dr, Jln Bahagia.

    SBST 134 (Ang Mo Kio - New Bridge Rd Bishan - New Bridge Rd)

    Service 134 was cut short from Ang Mo Kio to Bishan via Bishan St 22.

    Route (Ang Mo Kio - New Bridge Rd): Ang Mo Kio Ave 3, Ave 6, Marymount Rd, Thomson Rd, Keng Lee Rd, Kampong Java Rd, Bt Timah Rd, Sungei Rd, Bencoolen St, Middle Rd, North Bridge Rd, South Bridge Rd, Upp Cross St, New Bridge Rd.

    SBST 149 (Toa Payoh - Changi Airport)

    Withdrawn in 1993 when it was integrated with then loop service 31 (Tampines - Stadium Boulevard). Now covered by service 646.

    Route: Current service 31 from Toa Payoh to Marine Parade Rd, then follow service 36 to Changi Airport.

    Tibs 161 (Sembawang Rd End - Shenton Way)

    Route: Sembawang Rd, Upp Thomson Rd, Thomson Rd, Keng Lee Rd, Kampong Java Rd, Bt Timah Rd, Sungei Rd, Bencoolen St, Middle Rd, North Bridge Rd, Bras Basah Rd, Connaught Dr, Collyer Quay, Raffles Quay, Shenton Way. In the return journey, it plys Cecil St and Coleman St.

    Tibs 182 (Marsiling - New Bridge Rd Marsiling - Shenton Way) Withdrawn in March 1996, one month after Woodlands Line opened

    Route (Marsiling - New Bridge Rd): Admiralty Rd, Woodlands Centre Rd, Woodlands Rd, Upp Bt Timah Rd, Jln Anak Bt, Dunearn Rd, Scotts Rd, Orchard Rd, Bras Basah Rd, North Bridge Rd, South Bridge Rd, Upp Cross St, New Bridge Rd.

    Route (Marsiling - Shenton Way): Same as above up to Bras Basah Rd, thereafter follow current service 167 to Shenton Way.

    Tibs 802 (Yishun - Yishun Ave 3) Now Intra-town 812

    Via Yishun Ave 2, Ring Rd, Ave 2, Ave 3, Ring Rd and Ave 2.

    Tibs 850 (Yishun - Bt Merah) Now majority of the route is covered by service 167

    Route: Yishun Ave 2, Lentor Ave, Ang Mo Kio Ave 6, Marymount Rd, Thomson Rd, Newton Rd, Scotts Rd, Orchard Rd, Bras Basah Rd, Connaught Dr, Collyer Quay, Raffles Quay, Shenton Way, Cantonment Link, Cantonment Rd, Neil Rd, Kampong Bahru Rd, Jln Bt Merah.

    Service 850 also plys Marina Centre via Raffles Boulevard and Raffles Ave on Sundays and public holidays.

    Tibs 950 (Marsiling - Shenton Way) Withdrawn in March 1996, one month after Woodlands Line opened. The link between Woodlands Ave 3 and Victoria St is now covered by service 960

    Route: Admiralty Rd, Woodlands Centre Rd, [Express sector from Woodlands Ave 3 to Bt Timah Rd outside Tekka Centre, via BKE, PIE, Whitley Rd, Dunearn Rd], Bt Timah Rd, Sungei Rd, Ophir Rd, Victoria St (amended from North Bridge Rd), Bras Basah Rd, Connaught Dr, Collyer Quay, Raffles Quay, Shenton Way.

    Originally posted by tranquilice:
    As far as I know, service 188 plied between former Jurong interchange and Jurong West St 91 (loop service).
    In 1980, there was a sv. 188 which terminated at Ulu Pandan Rd but I forgot where it went to (I think it was Prince Edward Rd). The Jurong Inter. - Jurong West St 91 route came in 1988. It was the first service in Jurong West Extension.

    Route details:

    Jurong Inter., Jurong Port Rd, Corporation Rd, Yung Ho Rd, Corporation Dr, Kang Ching Rd, Yuan Ching Rd, Boon Lay Way, Corporation Rd, Boon Lay Dr, Boon Lay Pl, Boon Lay Ave, Jln Boon Lay, Upp Jurong Rd, Pioneer Rd North, Jurong West St 91 & 92.

    Amendment 1:

    The junction of Boon Lay Ave & Jln Boon Lay, which had no traffic lights then, was sealed and prohibited motorists from making a right turn. Route was amended to ply via Boon Lay Dr & Boon Lay Way before turning into Jln Boon Lay. Original route restored when construction of traffic lights were completed. The return trip to Jurong Inter. was unchanged.

    Amendment 2:

    Amended to ply via Jurong West St 72 & Ave 5 in both directions when flats in the area were completed.
