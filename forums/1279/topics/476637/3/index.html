    Loyang Depot has a Capacity of 1000 buses when its fully completed.

    CGP (Changi Bus Park) will be utilised by PBOs (Private Bus Operators) under BSEP.

    Loyang Depot is Tendered for 10 years before making a move to the future site near Expo to intergrate fully with the completed Downtown Line. The future depot near Expo will be housed together with Rail & Bus Operations.

    Pasir Ris Services will be on trial for the 1st phase opening this June.

    Tampines, Pasir Ris, Airport & Changi Village services will fully utilise the Depot.

    Services will be shared among 2 depots to better facilitate timings, reliability & to meet the new guidelines set by LTA.

    Tampines Services that will be split into 2 Depots:

    8, 10, 22, 31, 65 & 72

    Pasir Ris Services that will be split into 2 Depots:

    5, 21, 58 & 88

    Airport Services that will be split into 2 Depots:

    24, 27, 34, 36 & 89

    Changi Village Services that will be split into 2 Depots:

    109
