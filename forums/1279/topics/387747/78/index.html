    Dear all,

    I just want to share with you all my experience with Newcastle Custom Tailor at Upper Cross Street. The shop at Holiday Inn Atrium is under the same boss. My experience with Newcastle Custom Tailor was extremely atrocious. Initially i was hesitant to share this but after some deep consideration, I hope you all will not have to go through the same experience as me.

    There were at least two things that went very, very wrong:

    First, initially, during the first visit, they charged me $370 but during the 3rd session, when I went to get my clothes, the boss demanded that I pay $440 in total. His rationale was that there was a mistake in the calculation during the first session and the actual amount should be $440 instead. When he told me about that, I immediately shared with him that I believe the mistake was genuine but it was definitely unreasonable for me to pay another $70. The reason was simple: the tailor promised to give me a discount if I get more pieces from them. But $440 was the full price for the 6 pieces of clothes. So 0% is the discount I get from buying more pieces? I am amused. Honestly, I could have reported this to the authority but I did not want to blow this out of proportion.


    Second, the tailors are extremely unprofessional and their workmanship is atrocious. During the fitting session, which is one week after my initial visit, the pants that I got was too short. It was at least short by two inches. Can you believe it? I do not think such a situation should even arise at all. Nonetheless, the tailor was at least nice enough to alter for me without any complain. One week later, I went to get the final version. Out of my horror, the pants - this time round, two pants, not one- were still short and one of them was so tight that I couldn't wear it properly. 


    My friends and colleague can testify that I am a reasonable person. But the situation was really atrocious.Take care and choose the right place.
