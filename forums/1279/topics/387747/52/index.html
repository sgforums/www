    TIB1096A - just a point you might want to consider while doing your own deployment.

    # buses is not an endless number as you are aware. Everytime you give buses to a service, it will impact other services.

    For instance, you move 10 additional buses to sv 190 or 8 additional buses to 812 and another 7 more to 811. Where do all these extra buses come from? Also, is there a need for so many buses?

    Do the travel time v/s number of buses. If you deploy 12-14  buses on sv 812, the frequency will be within 05 mins. Is it required for 812?

    52 buses on sv 190 will get the frequency under 03 mins. If there is any traffic and block for 10 minutes, you will have a train of 4-5 buses bunched up and the latter getting completely wasted. What SMRT needs is high capacity enhancement on buses with keeping the # of buses almost same or maybe 1 or 2 more in case where really needed.
