    My deployment for 979

    3 A22, 5 E500. 

    Fleet of 8 buses to start with. Estimated run time with break time: ~60 mins. Frequency: 06 mins for A22, 08 mins for E500.

    I am proposing frequency of 06-08 mins, since 979 does the job of a feeder more than a trunk, and hence quick arrival of buses is necessary.

    Majority high capacity fleet is required, because suppose 979 calls before 302 at CCK Crescent. Lot of people will board for YT MRT as well, and those heading further to BPJ will not get to board.

    Moreover I think 979 will be quite popular to get people to transfer to buses at BPJ road, Woodlands road. 979 -> 190 to city will be faster than 302 -> 190.
