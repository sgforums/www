    Originally posted by One Harmonius Blend:

    What does down route means? as in from the depot all the way to the stated place to start?

    Downroute basically means a particular bus service starting its trip halfway at a stated bus stop, instead of starting from terminal. They not only come from depot or interchange, but also after doing short-working trip. There are mainly a few types of downroutes. They include:

    Early morning (Scheduled)
    Purpose: Because of first bus timings, as explained by SBS7485P

    Peak hours or any other time (Scheduled)
    Purpose: Since not the entire route has the same passenger demand, downroute bus will come in at section where there is higher demand. So as to spread out loadings.

    Buses that are late
    Purpose: In order to catch up for lost time. Instructions will be given by OCC.
