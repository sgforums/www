    The two buses were used again for today.

    Free Shuttle/Bus Bridging Route: Refer to Google Maps

    Notice that there were two additional stops (labelled 2A and 2B) - the first at The Heeren and the next opposite 313@Somerset. I have no idea why these two stops were added to the route but it apparently was from the suggestion of the promoters on 8388E. Not sure if the promoters on 8211G also did the same.

    What you get for taking these buses?

    The promoters will distribute to the passengers the following items
    - 1x sample packet of Panaflex patch
    - Leaflet

    For correctly answering their question (generally only during the Bugis Junction - Far East Plaza sector), passengers also get a can of Dequadin Pastilles.

    What is being done on the bus?

    Promoters will promote the Panaflex patch to the passengers on board, generally talking about its features. They can also offer to let passengers try the patch.
