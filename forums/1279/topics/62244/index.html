    Originally posted by SBS9818A:
    I thought it operated from Holland Drive or Close, outside Bouna Vista Terminal, instead of going in?
    That's right. CSS sv. 602 did not terminate inside Buona Vista Terminal. Its terminal was along Holland Dr, opp the swimming complex. But sometime in the late 90s, the terminal was closed and sv. 602 started its route from Shenton Way Terminal and looped at Holland Dr. Route was as follows:-

    602 Shenton Way - Holland Dr (Loop)

    via Palmer Rd, Anson Rd, Cecil St, Fullerton Rd, Stamford Rd, Orchard Rd, Penang Rd, Somerset Rd, Orchard Link, Orchard Blvd, Grange Rd, Napier Rd, Holland Rd, Queensway, C'wealth Ave, Holland Dr (loop)

    and return via C'wealth Ave, Queensway, Holland Rd, Napier Rd, Tanglin Rd, Orchard Rd, Bras Basah Rd, Connaught Dr, Collyer Quay, Raffles Quay, Shenton Way

    CSS services

    602 Shenton Way - Holland Dr
    603 St Michaels - Upp Cross St
    605 Sin Ming Rd - Bt Merah
    607 St Michaels - New Bridge Rd
    608 Bedok - Bt Merah

    In the beginning, there were 9 services numbered 1-9. Sv. 1, 4, 6 & 9 were withdrawn in the 80s. The 5 remaining routes were re-numbered as above (eg. 2 -> 602) in 1997. Now, only 603, 605 and 608 remain. Sv. 603 will be withdrawn soon.
