    If you join SBST or SMRT, they will train you for a class 4A license. But after training, you can only cross over between these 2 companies. 4A license is only valid for driving SBST or SMRT buses.

    SBST and SMRT will also issue you a separate vocational license. Once you resign, you have to return the vocatinal license back to the respective operators. The classification VL for private and public bus operator is also different.
