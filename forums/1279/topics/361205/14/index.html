Because not everyone know everyone here, what if person A don't know B, B also don't know C, but D E F are good friends, I don't think it's fair.     Situation A = fairness, there won't be any group worrying about how to communicate.
    Situation B = unfair treatment, some groups worry, some groups enjoy.
