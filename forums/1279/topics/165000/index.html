    Amendment to Townlink Service 268A

    Effective from 01st December 2005, Service 268A will be amended to operate from Ang Mo Kio Street 63 (Ang Mo Kio Depot) to Ang Mo Kio Ave 5 (Techpark II). Operation time remains unchanged.

    This amendment is in conjunction with the amendment of Service 268 effective from 01st December 2005, with road changes within Ang Mo Kio Ind Park II.

    The current Service 268A route is from Ang Mo Kio ave 3 (Blk 700B) to Ang Mo Kio Industrial Park II (Caltex/ opp Blk 5023).
