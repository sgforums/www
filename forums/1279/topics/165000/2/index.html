    Special Bus Services For Upcoming Concerts

    In conjunction with the upcoming concerts of Stefani Sun, Backstreet Boys and another artiste (I can't remember who) at the indoor stadium, 4 special bus services will operate from the bus stop after the indoor stadium to the following interchanges, namely Bedok, Tampines, Boon Lay and Yio Chu Kang.

    Route details:

    To Bedok Int:
    Bus Stop after indoor stadium express Bedok Reservoir Rd Bedok North Ave 3 Bedok North St 1 Bedok Int

    To Tampines Int:
    Bus Stop after indoor stadium express Tampines Ave 2 Tampines St 32 Tampines St 34 Tampines St 45 Tampines Ave 9 Tampines St 41 Tampines Ave 7 Tampines Int

    To Boon Lay:
    Bus Stop after indoor stadium express Jurong Town Hall Rd Jurong East Central Jurong East Ave 1 Jurong West Ave 1 Boon Lay Ave Jln Boon Lay Boon Lay Int

    To Yio Chu Kang Ter:
    Bus Stop after indoor stadium express Ang Mo Kio Ave 10 Ang Mo Kio Ave 3 Ang Mo Kio Ave 4 Yio Chu Kang Rd Ang Mo Kio Ave 6 Yio Chu Kang Int

    This is as far as what I can remember. I read this notice at the bus stops where these 4 services serve. The fare for adults is $2 while it's $1 for students. I apologise for any mistakes in the above information and hope you'll will wait for the official press release next week.

    Temporary Closure Of Slip Road
    From Sims Way To Guillemard Road

    To facilitate works on the Kallang/Paya Lebar Expressway (KPE), the slip road from Sims Way to Guillemard Road, at the junction of Nicoll Highway/Mountbatten Road/ Guillemard Road/Sims Way, will be temporarily closed to traffic from Wednesday, 21 December 2005 at 10.00am. The slip road will be re-opened to traffic on Wednesday, 28 December 2005 at 12.01am.

    There will be changes in the traffic light phasing at the signalised junction during this period. Motorists turning left from Sims Way into Guillemard Road are advised to keep on the extreme left lane and make a left-turn at the junction.

    SBS Transit bus services 7 and 197 travelling along Sims Way to Guillemard Road will also be affected, but there will not be any changes to the service routes. Motorists are advised to drive carefully and follow the directional signs on display.

    A map is enclosed for your reference.
