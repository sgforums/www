    As you all know, after the sudden closure of Wikipedia @ SGBuses.com, the current SGWiki.com is currently un-updated since most of the pages are locked. As a solution, I have imported all its information to a newer wikipedia site called the 'Singapore Buses Wiki' website.

    Its website is:
    http://singaporebuses.wikia.com/wiki/Singapore_Buses_Wiki

    How do i use it?
    - First you would need to sign up in order to edit the pages. To edit a page, simply press the Edit button. You would need to press the ''Source'' button located at the right most corner that avoids confusion when editing a certain information. Click ''Save Changes'' and its done!

    Rules and Regulations
    - Please do not vandalise this website for the benefit of all bus enthusiasts community. 
    - Please do not press 'Save Changes' twice as it would result to an edit conflict; which then would not save your edited information.

    Have fun and enjoy! Mods, sticky this thread if you find this topic reliable. Thank you!
    - SMB92U
