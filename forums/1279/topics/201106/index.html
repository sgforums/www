SMRT Buses "Anyhow" Parked!

  • eX.A.K.R.
    20 Jul 06, 23:32
    I was out cycling in the Woodlands area just now, and I happened to pass by SMRT's Woodlands depot, when I saw something that just wasn't right.

    SMRT buses parked everywhere at the side of the road, outside the depot, illegally.

    To say I was shocked couldn't be more true. SMRT buses were parked at the side of the roads, around the depot area, mostly with the engine off. There were a few bendy buses among them too. Worse still, as if in a blantant disregard of security, one of the buses even had it's front door wide open!

    When I saw the inside the depot, I was even more shocked. There seemed to be not enough parking space inside the depot, and buses were parked everywhere! It was like, "D***it nowhere to park! Aiyah I will anyhow put it here." kind of a situation.

    I counted 11 or 12 buses parked outside the depot (one left when I was still there, and I suspect another bus that came in was parked like this too while I was there), and I estimate at least 15+ to 20+ buses parked in such an "anyhow" manner inside the depot!

    Overcrowding? This is definitely a sign of it. Short of buses? I really don't think so!
