    I wouldn't recommend taking video or sound recordings right at the rear of a bus, reason being, if you are at the rear, the engine sound would cover the transmission sound. At the same time, if you are too front, the engine sound would be too soft to be heard, such that you only get the transmission sound, therefore, the best place to take such recordings is actually between the rear door and the rear axle or above the rear axle, where you get a balance of engine, transmission and axle sounds.
