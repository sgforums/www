    985 goes from BB W Av 4 and right turn to BB W Av 7 then left turn into BB W Av 5, then right turn into Bukit Batok Rd. 985 passes by BBDC.

    985W goes from BB W Av 4, then left turn to BB W Av 7 then right turn into Bukit Batok Rd. This was the old route of 985 before it was rerouted to the current 985. They introduced 985W because people living at Blk 40X Bukit Batok West Av 7 complained about their link to CCK and elsewhere along 985's route.
