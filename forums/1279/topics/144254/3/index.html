    Hi, just came back from taking service 57. Here are my comments:

    Route - 10 / 10 : Very good and integrated route on this service. Serve Bishan, Toa Payoh, Whitely, Bukit Timah, Newton, North Bridge Road, Shenton Way, CBD, Telok Blangah, PSA, Keppel, Harbour Front, Bukit Merah. Good and Special Route

    Loading - 3 / 10
    Very few people take this service (as well as 5, Except me.

    Frequency -8 / 10
    Freq. range is not bad, just like other bishan service.

    Comfort - 5 / 10
    This bus mainly uses MKII and MKII has a lot of weakness - Noisy, air-con not strong, the bus shakes when at high speed.

    Bus Fleet - 6 / 10
    This service (as well as 5 uses old bus like MKII & CRB. Should use newer bus like MKIV DM 3500 or VSO.

    Overall - 60 / 100
    This service has room for improvement. But in the begining, its OK.

    I will evaulate service 58 next week (because it is holiday so I can take the service).
