    Christopher Tan. (2014, March 7). New bus service from Telok Blangah. The Straits Times. Retrieved from http://www.straitstimes.com/

    A new bus service will ply between Telok Blangah and the Tiong Bahru and Outram MRT stations from next Sunday.

    SBS Transit Bus Service 121 is a new trunk service that will connect commuters from Ganges Avenue, Kim Tian Road and Telok Blangah Crescent/Rise to Tiong Bahru and Outram Park MRT stations.

    Operating daily from 6am to 11.30pm from New Bridge Road Bus Terminal, Service 121 will ply Outram Road, Zion Road, Ganges Avenue, Lower Delta Road, Tiong Bahru Road, Kim Tian Road, Jalan Bukit Merah, Lower Delta Road and Telok Blangah Way, before looping back via Telok Blangah Crescent and Telok Blangah Rise. As Zion Road is one way, the service will ply Outram Road in the return direction.

    Besides enhancing connectivity to key transport nodes such as Tiong Bahru and Outram Park MRT stations, the new bus service will also provide residents with convenient access to amenities like Singapore General Hospital, Outram Polyclinic, Safra Mount Faber, Tiong Bahru Plaza, markets and food centres along Jalan Bukit Merah and Telok Blangah Crescent, as well as schools such as Radin Mas Primary School and CHIJ St Theresa's Convent.
