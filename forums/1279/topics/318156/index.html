    General method.

     

    1. go to http://www.sbstransit.com.sg and click on "Journey Planner".

     

    2. Key in your starting location and ending location which in your case will be "Ayer Rajah Crescent".

     

    3. Follow the instructions and out will pop out a suggested route.

     

    Specific answer.

     

    If Starhub is still at 2D Ayer Rajah Crescent, take 91 from Buona Vista MRT same side as MOE building, get off for Ayer Rajah Bus Park and ask for directions from there. You'll have to walk a bit.
