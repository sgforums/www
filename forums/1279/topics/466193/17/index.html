    Refurbished PA plated UMW Lances (PA639M, PA644Y PA669B) have 3 pairs of seats removed to create a standing space like on the revenue UMW/DSV Lances.

    Summary of changes (for refurbished PA plated UMW Lances, TIB plated UMW Lances capacity is correct as reflected on the wiki):
    Seating capacity: 41 (Design) -> 35 (Modified seating configuration)
    Standing capacity: 34 (Design) -> 46 (Modified seating configuration)
    Total: 75 (Design) -> 81 (Modified seating configuration)

    Some other corrections for the specifications:

    Correct Dennis Lance specs:
    Lance 211 engine: Cummins 6CT
    Maximum power output: 157kW (211bhp) @ 1,950rpm
    The 211hp variants are never known as the C211, however they are using the 6CT engine, same as those used on the KMB Lances (VIN plate starts with 11SDA). This is the correct engine specs and performance, however I could not find out how much torque the engine produces.

    Lance 245 engine: Cummins 6CT (C245)
    Like the 211hp ones, they are also using the 6CT engine (albeit a more powerful version), but unlike the 211hp variant, this engine is also known as the C245.

    Correct Volgren bodied Lance passenger capacity:
    Seating capacity: 39
    Standing capacity: 42
    Total: 81
