    Just took TIB828L earlier at about 8.25p.m. from Harbourfront Int. I found out that it was refurbished by Liannex, the company refurbishing the O405Gs (except the Volgren bendies). Here are the clues:

    1. The air-con vents (including those for the driver) were changed to those eyeball vents found on TIB827P and TIB831B. The aircon vents on the side of the air-con ducts were found with new screws, as well as some of the vents changed to vents with 4 outlets, instead of the usual 3.

    2. The bells (buttons) used to activate the 'BUS STOPPING' bell have all been swapped for new ones, including those on the poles and window 'dividers' (though the yellow bell has been retained)

    3. The bottom curve of the seat (below the seat covers) have been painted to a very bright (colgate) white, the same as those on the bendies (which have the whole plastic shape of the seat repainted)

    4. The roof of the interior (where the lights are located) seems to be gray, not the usual white.

    5. The poles that support the doors (and make the doors open and close) are repainted the same grey used on the poles found on the bendies. The doors (on the inside) seem to be repainted (or restickered) a matt black.

    Please tell me if the other 1997 O405s are refurbished by Liannex!
