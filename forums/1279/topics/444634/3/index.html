    I would say that HGDEP (or rather SBST) would rather deploy their new buses onto those long haul and popular high demand routes (eg. 27, 43, 105, 163 etc). Whereas those short haul and low demand routes (eg. 11, 103, 115 etc) gets only those MK4s in their fleet. Even services like 74, 89, 107, 153 and 159 till now had not even get any new buses at all. Sometimes it might be due to favoritism...

    To me 43 is considered a high demand route and it also serves as a subsidiary to 70/70M along Upp Paya Lebar and YCK road corridors. In fact 43 also deserves new buses too based on its long haul route, rather than sticking to its former MK3 fleet without any upgrades. Notice the crowds always come in around Serangoon MRT areas.

    Same goes to SMRT. Those popular routes like 190, 851, 961, 963 etc keeps getting the new buses. Whereas the unpopular ones like 178, 975, 980 etc gets only the secondary bus models.
