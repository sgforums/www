    Hi there.Regarding your post,this is the reply from LTA

    We refer to your email of 25 September 2012. 
     

    Currently, Clementi Town is served by 14 bus services viz. Services 7, 14, 96, 99, 147, 156, 165, 166, 173, 175, 196, 282, 284 and 285 from Clementi Bus Interchange to many other parts of the island such as Commonwealth, Bukit Merah, Chinatown, Orchard, Victoria Street, Ang Mo Kio, Hougang, East Coast, Bedok, etc.

    We have studied your request for a new service and our studies have shown that the proposed service will duplicate some existing services. In addition, long distance routes are vulnerable to en-route traffic conditions, which may affect its reliability and results in longer waiting and travel time.

    While we strive to provide as many direct services as possible, we seek your understanding that it is inefficient and expensive to provide a direct bus service between every origin and destination. A more efficient and transport model would be the hub-and-spoke model, of which transfer is a key feature. With the introduction of distance-based through fares, commuters will not be panalised for making transfers.

    As there are alternative connections available, we regret that we are unable to support the proposed new service.
     

    Nonetheless, every feedback is important to us, and we will take your feedback into consideration when we review the bus network in the area.
     

    We thank you for writing in.


     
    Yours sincerely

     

    HO TZE HUI SHARON
    EXECUTIVE, BUS SERVICE DEVELOPMENT
    BUS & VOCATIONAL LICENSING GROUP
