    There seems to be very few route adjustments these days.

    Hope SMRT can divert 1-2 trunk svcs to ply Yishun Ring Road (both the Blk 700s side and Blk 400s side). The Blk 400s is more critical coz it is only served by feeder 804, 806, etc. Many BTOs are coming up in this area. Trunk svc will benefit the residents.

    Example) In the direction towards Yishun Int, a Svc 85X can right-turn into Yishun Ring Road after Khatib MRT, then via Northbrooks Sec Sch, Blk 300s, then left turn into Ave 9 and return to Int. The same for the opposite direction.

    Another svc, in the direction towards Yishun Int, can right turn into Yishun Central, via Khoo Teck Puat hospital, Yishun North NPC, then return to Int. The same for the opp directon.
