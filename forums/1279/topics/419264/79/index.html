    I also strongly advocate sv 48 to go to Changi Airport via Siglap Road, while sv 36 goes to Bedok North Depot via Bedok South Ave 3.

    At the same time introduce 46E that will go from Changi Airport to Orchard road  via ECP (looping at Tomlinson like sv 36) at a 15 minute frequency.

    If you notice, people defer from taking 36 all the way to Orchard from Changi airport because of its looping around Marine Parade. Most of people who board sv 36 alight at Marine Parade Road. This missed section is also covered by sv 48 if routed to Changi Airport.

    At the same time with 46E you can have faster service from Changi airport to city.

    Also the big advantage is 36 can get DDs and can get rid of 36A/36B because the DDs will beautifully manage the Marine Parade - Orchard loading. It will also manage the Tanah Merah - Bedok south Ave 3 - UEC Road loading, where people struggle to enter 48 during PM peak at Tanah Merah. Food for thought for LTA/SBST.
