    In the past, there is a bus terminus near Woodlands Checkpoint, then serving as the bus interchange in Woodlands area, until Woodlands Regional Bus Interchange opened. Then it served as a terminus for 911, 912 and 913 although they still called at the regional bus interchange, until 2003 when they were amended to skip the terminus itself, calling the bus stop outside the present day Woodlands Train Checkpoint instead. In the previous years, several buses doing service 170 also parked there for breaks.
