    MAN Trucks
    MAN NL 232 CNG (Euro V and better)

    - lean-burn engine: MAN E2866 D; 6 in-line cylinders; process: 4-stroke aspirated Otto cycle; fuel receptacle: NGV-1
    - hp: 170 kW/ 231hp
    - max. torque: 860 Nm
    - displacement: 11.97 L
    - carries up to about 100 passengers
    - fuel storage: 120 L (water volume equivalent)

    MAN NUE 313 CNG (Euro V and better)
    - lean-burn engine: MAN E2866 LUH 01; 6 in-line cylinders; process: 4-stroke Otto cycle, intercooled, turbocharged; displacement: 11.97 L; fuel receptacle: NGV-1
    - hp: 228 kW/ 310 hp at 2100 rpm
    - torque: 1,250 Nm at 1,400 rpm
    - top speed: 97 km/h
    - cost not available
    - carries up to about 76 passengers
    - wide entries and wide gangways permit a rapid and smooth flow of passengers at stops
    - proven low-floor technology (entries without steps at doors 1 and 2) and the "kneeling" system provide for passenger friendly boarding and departure, lowering the entry height by a further 70 - 80 mm
    - disk brakes on the front and rear axle combined with an electronic brake system (EBS)
    - fuel storage system for gas compressed to 200 bar takes up more space than on conventional diesel city buses
    - MAN NUE 313 CNG needs eight aluminium composite pressure tanks while the MAN NUG 313 CNG needs ten
    - ZF 5HP592 model automatic gearbox with an integrated retarder
    - integrated rear view camera, which can also be switched over to monitor the passenger compartment

    MAN NUG 313 CNG (Articulated)

    - engine: CNG MAN E 2866 LUH 01 lean burn (l > 1)
    - displacement: 12 L
    - hp: 228 kW/ 310 hp at 2100 rpm
    - bus cost: about S$747,400 (CNG premium: about S$115,000)
    - carries up to about 151 passengers
