    Judging from the map, the housing areas at {Yung Sheng Road, Yung An Road, Yung Kuang Road}(basically, those along Corporation Road) , Jurong West Street 52, 41 and 42 have weak bus connection to Lakeside MRT station. I am quite certain that the new BSEP bus route will serve these areas. The pair of bus stops at Lakeside MRT station is definitely served by the new bus route, unless a new pair of bus stop is created at Yuan Ching Road, at where there was previously a taxi stand. 

    Personally, I foresee changes to a few bus routes. It is quite likely that 335 will be re-routed, possibly shorterned to Chinese Garden area - serving Jurong East Street 31 and 32, unless there is still demand for bus link to Chinese Garden/Jurong East from Jurong West Street 52/41/42 even after the new BSEP bus route is launched.

    Meanwhile, 334 is likely going to remain the same, otherwise being re-routed and lengthened to serve Jurong West Street 52/41/42, to compensate the link loss due to 335 being re-routed, if there is still demand for bus link to Chinese Garden/Jurong East from Jurong West Street 52/41/42 even after the new BSEP bus route is launched. 

    I am writing this on the assumption that the new BSEP bus route is a feeder service, providing bus connection to Lakeside MRT Station from areas that currently have weak/no bus connection to the nearest MRT Station.

    If this new BSEP route is a trunk road, it is quite likely that from Hong Kah, the route will go to the other parts of Jurong West, instead of Jurong East. If so, the route at Hong Kah will definitely go in this sequence:

    1. Jurong West Street 51
    2. Jurong West Street 41
    3. Jurong West Street 42
    4. Jurong West Avenue 1 (serving St 52)
    5. Corporation Road
    Thereafter, it is quite likely that it will go into Wenya, rather than Boon Lay. Hence, the sequence is likely going to be this:
    1. Wenya Avenue (if so, the route will loop here, since only a section of this road, of the road network in Wenya, is constructed.)
    2. Wenya Drive
    3. Jalan Bahar
    4. Jurong West Avenue 2
    This may be the reason why the route is not launched yet. Wenya Avenue is still not open to the (general) public. 

    With bus routes 99, 240 and 246, it may be redundant if the new BSEP route goes into Boon Lay from Hong Kah/Taman Jurong, since there is (are) already good bus connectivity between these areas.

    If it goes into the extension area (Yunnan and Central), it may be too similar to 185, which - on the other hand - can be re-routed to provide better bus connectivity in the extension area. But that is another topic already, so I shall not elaborate on it.
