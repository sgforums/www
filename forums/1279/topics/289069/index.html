    From Hougang Central, Hougang Ave 8, u can take 147 to Jln Besar first stop after Lavender St. Cross the road and walk through Petain Rd. U will reach the junction between Petain Rd and Beatty Rd. Proceed to Beatty Rd. U can take 21 from Jalan Besar to Serangoon Rd. Stop at the 2nd stop and cross the road. Walk along the traffic flow and u will see Sturdee Rd

    If not, at Hougang Central, or at Serangoon JC that area, u can take 80 to Geylang Rd and transfer to 21 and alight at Serangoon Rd B07 (2 stops after 21 turns right from Kitchener Rd).
