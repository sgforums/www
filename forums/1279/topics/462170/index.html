    With Effect from 16th December, Service 50 will be introduced from Bishan Bus Interchange to Punggol Interchange.

    It serves along the road of Ang Mo Kio Ave 8/5, Yio Chu Kang Road, Sengkang West Ave, Sengkang East Way, and Punggol Drive.

    It also by-pass ITE College Central, Sengkang Sports and Recreation Centre, and the town centre.
