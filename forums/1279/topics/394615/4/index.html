    Originally posted by AEW5001:

    i believe the root cause of this problem is the design of the brakes and suspensions.
    Kubs use disc braking, compared to the Volvo Mk4s, which uses air braking.

    Buses using air brakes usually brake more smoothly.

    Buses uses pressurized air braking or a combination of pressurized air and fluid as a medium to control the brakes, the disc and drum brakes are the point of friction.

    Imagine a bicycle, the brake cable is the medium, whilst the point of friction is the brake pads mounted on the front and back rims, same concept goes to the buses, but using air instead as the cable.
