    I was checking out the other forum when I saw that they have a thread on bus tenders (http://forums.sgtrains.com/showthread.php?tid=3094).

    I realised SGforums does not have a thread about bus tenders. So, I am creating this thread.

    Basically, this is like the press release thread, except this thread is about bus tenders.

    A bus tender is one called by LTA, SBS, SMRT, TT, GA, or any organisation related to bus services. It is to deal with matters related to bus, bus interchange/depot, etcetera.

    For example, there can be a tender about retirement of buses, a tender to change equipment in the bus interchange, etcetera. As long as it is about buses, whether bus or infrastructure, post it here.

    Tender information can be found at the respective websites of LTA, SBS, SMRT, TT, GA, etcetera.
