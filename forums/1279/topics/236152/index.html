    Redhill (English) = Bukit Merah (Malay) = Hong2 Shan1 (Mandarin)= Ang Sua (Hokkien)

    Older generations of Singaporeans know the area stretching from Bukit Merah Central to Lengkok Bahru to Delta Swimming Complex as Redhill. Informal of course.

    If you take 14, 196 or 197 from Bedok to Bukit Merah, it should take 1 hour 15 minutes or so (with no heavy traffic jams esp through Orchard area).

    14 and 197 travel the stretch from bus stop outside McDonalds Bukit Merah to Alexandra Village, before making a left turn to reach the AYE entrance to Clementi/Jurong East respectively.
