  • ^tamago^
    4 Mar 08, 23:52
    The intention to extend 852 to Sembawang stems back to Tibs' days in 1996, right after Woodlands MRT Extension and WRI opened. The original bus rationalisation plan calls for it to be withdrawn by March 1996, but it met with complains from parents as a lot of students residing in Yishun apparently need to use it to get to schoolin Bt Timah area, and 74 is too packed by the time they transfer to it at Ang Mo Kio. So before the 3 weeks' notice is up, the decision was reversed; instead, it was simply shortened from Boon Lay to Bukit Batok. If the decision had went through, 851 might have ended up with 5-minute intervals or a full bendy fleet. However, the decision to withdraw 852 was because it is not earning money for SMRTB even with its long frequencies as a lot of students take it, paying subsidised fare or using concession passes. So they thought of extending it to Sembawang to increase ridership. And it has been 12 years since....
