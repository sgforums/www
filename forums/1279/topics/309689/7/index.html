    Not too sure also. The service standards for 980 has been dropping since 2004. Now with AMDEP controlling high volume services like 106 and 190, I think the chances of weekend bendies have to be from WLDEP.. which normally only put one or two buses..


    Service 980 bendy deployments on Sundays nowdays always depend on situation one, sometimes have a lot of bendies, sometimes just 1 or 2, worse till at times don't even have a bendy at all. Since that week during the airshow, no bendies are being rotated to do service 980 on those asigned bendy slots, they deploy back the usual Lance permers instead.

    In fact year 2004 was the year when CSS began to transfer its old Nissan UD fleets into SMRT, deproving the standards of SMRT compared to Tibs. Service 980 was the least affected. In fact, service 178 was the worst hit, having Scanias downgraded to old Nissan fleets. Bt Panjang feeders also suffered Nissans before too, but original Merc/Scania fleet back in 2006. Service 975 also had some of its DAFs downgraded to Nissan buses in 2007 after CSS walk into History.

    For now is to look out for service 852, since WLDEP just took over AMDEP and it seems that WLDEP had assigned majority Nissans, based on the WLDEP's duty roster (AMDEP used only full Lances fleet).
