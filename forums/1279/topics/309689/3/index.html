    To make Sembawang bus services more logical, SMRT should have amended service 859 to loop around Sembawang new town and convert into a feeder service. Service 882 should also be converted to feeder service. Both these services will be charged feeder bus fares.

    Service 852 and 857 could be extended to Sembawang interchange via Yishun Ave 2, Canberra Link, Sembawang Way, Sembawang Drive and Sembawang Vista.

    Service 858 and/or 969 could also be diverted to ply along Sembawang via Woodlands Ave 7, Sembawang Way, Sembawang Drive, Sembawang Ave, Sembawang Road and then continue its current route.

    Eversince TIBS days, services 858 and 969 were often suggested to extend to Sembawang. However, TIBS shotÂ  down all requests (even SMRT) as that would breach the 1 hour travel time promise. However, in 2006, SMRT relented after MP Dr Lim Wee Kiak requested for service 858 to amend to ply Sembawang, but PTC shot it down citing reasons like increase in travel timing and fares.
