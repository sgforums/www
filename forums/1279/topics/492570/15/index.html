    Originally posted by @:)@:


    Not sure what u are driving at but JIS has mostly B9TL Batch 3s and 4s. And SLBP has the highest number of Batch 3s. Days of mass VO2x and LO3x are over. It's a new generation now.

    The days of mass LO2X, VO2X, LO3X and VO3X (Batch 1 & 2) are over. Volvo B9TLs progressively introduced after extending the remaining 25 buses permanently to 249, 251, 252, 254, 255, 257.

    2003: LO2X withdrawn, VO2X took over.

    2006: LO3X took from several services after being replaced by B9TL (CDGE).

    2007: VO3X also took the early batches for JIS 257

    2011: Mass withdrawal of VO2X from JIS and replaced by LO3X and VO3X.

    2012: Mass withdrawal of LO3X from JIS and replaced by VO3X.

    2013: Mass withdrawal of VO3X (Batch 1) from JIS and replaced by later batches.

    2014: Volvo B9TLs began to be progressively introduced.

    2016: JIS became WAB. VO3X (Batch 2) began to be withdrawn. Some Batch 3s were extended but they did not use so much.

    (Note: The VO3X Batch 3, SBS9670H will be the last non-WAB bus to be de-registered)
