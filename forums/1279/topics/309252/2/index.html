    Haha, those days when there were big changes in deployments of WL trunk services. From DAF fleets became Scania fleets. Whereas Yishun trunk services got those newly introduced Dennis Lance fleets.

    Due to the fact that AMDEP once housed all the Scania fleets. However AMDEP transferred all its Scania fleets over to the newly opened WLDEP progressively and got the newer batch of Dennis Lance fleets. Whereas KJDEP all the while had been housing the DAF and Hino fleets.

    Another fact was that Tibs exchanged CCK bus routes and Northeast corridor bus routes with SBS and a lot of bus services were affected by the depot deployments.
