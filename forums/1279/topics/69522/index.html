    This is not strictly about buses, but I have some queries on Changi and Upper Changi Roads.

    1) Since there is now a New Upper Changi Road, how did Upper Changi Road connect to Changi Road formerly? Upp Changi Road ends at Anglican High School now. I suppose it ran almost parallel to New Upper Changi Road and connected to Changi Road at Siglap Road junction.

    Before I touch on the roads in Changi, these are some of the road names in 1970s/early 1980s.

    Jln Bt Merah between Henderson Rd and Lower Delta Rd was known as Gagak Selari Timor.

    Jln Bt Merah between Lower Delta Rd and Kim Tian Rd was known as Gagak Selari Barat.

    Lower Delta Rd during that time stretch from Delta Circus (current Alexandra Rd/Ganges Ave junction) to current Jln Bt Merah junction. The stretch after Jln Bt Merah was known as Gagak Selari Selatan, and was a cresent road. Lower Delta Rd between Jln Bt Merah and Kampong Bahru Rd did not exist.

    Henderson Rd between Depot Rd and Telok Blangah Rd did not exist.

    Ophir Rd did not exist then, Rochor Rd stretched from Bencoolen St/Jln Besar junction to Beach Rd, after Beach Rd, was a small road called Jln Laut which is the present site of Rochor flyover.

    Clemenceau Ave stretch from Newton Circus to Havelock Rd junction.

    Orchard Boulevard between Tomlinson Rd and Grange Rd was known as Woodstock Drive.

    Shenton Way between Maxwell Rd and Keppel Rd did not exist. Traffic then used Anson Rd to get to Keppel Rd.

    Ganges Ave did not exist, however there was a minor un-named road that seems to be Ganges Ave.

    Upp Changi Rd was once a long stretch of road from New Upp Changi Rd to Changi Point (current Changi Village). The current Upp Changi Rd North/East and Changi Village Rd are called Upp Changi Rd back then.

    Upp Changi Rd started near the present junction of Bedok South Ave 1 and New Upp Changi Rd (near block 207 and 209), continued thru a disappeared stretch in Bedok town to Anglican High School, then continued along the current stretch to Simei Rd, then continued along Upp Changi Rd East, Upp Changi Rd North to the junction of Loyang Way (near Selarang camp), after that it aligned to the right and continued on the present "slip road" of Upp Changi Rd North, passed by Calshot Rd and then continue via the present disappeared stretch and meet up at the end of present Changi Village Rd, then again passed by Tangmere Rd and continued to end at present Changi Village.

    New Upp Changi Rd back then ended at the junction with Tanah Merah Kechil Rd. Chai Chee St and Chai Chee Drive was one road from Siglap Rd/New Upp Changi Rd junction to Upp Changi Rd.

    There was also a road connecting ECP after Bayshore exit (towards airport) to Upp East Coast Rd Bedok camp but is now disappeared.
