    I am not that old to remember routes before 1978.

    But I used to stay in Seletar Camp and we had the Jalan Kayu bus terminal. We had Services 70, 71,103, 163 and later 314E/W. Yio Chu Kang Road (old upp thomson) had 73 and 72.

    70 and 163 went to Prince Edward Interchange (opp International Plaza)

    103 went to Commonwealth (opp Buona Vista interchange)

    71 went to Geylang Lor 1 fringe car park.

    72 to Old Airport, while 73 to Toa payoh via Serangoon gardens.

    Prince Edward and Commonwealth were the main interchanges then with the most buses. ( I counted)

    Now only 103 comes to Jalan Kayu.

    Changi Point had services 1,2 and 3, now only 2 remains there.

    Kaki Bukit had 50 and 51.

    Kangkar (Upp Serangoon) had 136, 156, 111, 106, 107 and later 153.

    Somapah I remember 155 (did 158 end there?)

    Jalan Rumah Tinggi had 62 and 63 ( 1 remains there)
