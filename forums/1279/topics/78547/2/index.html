    I had the opportunity to compare the Volvo B10BLE with the OC500LE. It chanced upon me that 2989K came as I waited for 105 to get to Hyatt hotel. Although I like both of them the ride on the OC500LE is much smoother. I'm quite surprised that the engine is quiet for a Mercedes-Benz bus which are known to be very loud. I simply love the retarder sound of this bus. It steps down in quick succession and then lets out a monstrous moan. Cool! One thing in common is that sitting at the rearmost seat is a complete no-no. Very hot lah! Interior wise, I find the OC500LE to be a bit narrow and their doors were not as wide as the B10BLE. I really feel that SBS & TIBS should get a bus with wheelchair access (it doesn't have to be a CNG one). After watching the demo carried out by the Handicapped Welfare Association, I realised how easy and quickly they could roll on and off the bus. Impressive. I lost sleep today just to see the OC500LE and it was worth it. A once in a lifetime experience.
