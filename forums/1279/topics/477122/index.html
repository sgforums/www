    according to https://maps.google.com.sg/maps?hl=en&tab=wl , the fastest route to Techview from Eunos MRT Station is to 

    • go to the bus stop at the hawket side (Jalan Eunos) 
    • wait for bus service 15. 
    • board bus service 15.
    • alight at the first bus stop (Aft Jln Damai)(the 7th bus stop) after you see Bedok North Secondary School, at approximately 13 minutes later.
    • Techview is just behind the bus stop. (when you alight from the bus, you are actually facing Techview)
    Estimated travelling time = 17 minutes (~20 minutes)

     

    advantage of coming from Eunos MRT Station: less walking and you don't need to cross the bridge. :)
