    Service 284M will be introduced on Monday 29 June 2009. Notices are already put up at Clementi interchange and bus stops along the route. Online press release not up yet.

    The route of service 284M:

    Clementi interchange, Clementi Ave 4, U-turn, Clementi Ave 4, Clementi Ave 3 (NTUC Fairprice), Commonwealth Ave West (Clementi MRT station) and Clementi interchange.

    The parent service 284 will be changed to operate during the peak hours only, while service 284M will operates during the non peak hours on weekdays, weekends and operates whole day on Suns / PHs. I did not take down the operation timings.
