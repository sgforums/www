    Originally posted by Volvo Olympian:
    Turf City Shuttle got DDs?
    No.

    The database identifies each model thru the regos. The system is a bit 'cock-up' as you can see. Any regos starting with '95xx' and '960x to 966x' will be classified as Volvo Olympian without window, '967x' as Dennis Trident and '98xx' as VSO (Volgren). The digit '0' in front of the SH regos were ignored by the database.

    That explains why those SH-registered (Turf City) buses was classified as such.
