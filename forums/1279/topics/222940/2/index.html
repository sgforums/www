    I think our chicken friend here need to understand what is happening inside the O405. You see, the MB gearbox is a unique bloke. It allows the gear to 'slip' and then 'locks-up'. So that's the jerk while the bus moves. Then, when braking, it has a 3-step retarder. The retarder helps the bus to stop and ease the burden on the brakes. So that's the jerk while the bus stops. With all the ZF and Voiths around the SBST O405 stands unique by havings its own gearbox.

    I rode on 315T which Mr. Chickadee here claimed to have bad jerkings. Maybe I'm more tolerant as I have survived in much worse buses before; for I find 315T to be normal. A bit harsh while shifting, yes, but otherwise its OK; and quite enjoyable too. Perhaps they have fixed the bus?
