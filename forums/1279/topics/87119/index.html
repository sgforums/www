    Hi everyone,

    Can I assume the smaller the reg no, the earlier the bus was registered?

    yes, generally. but i know of one exception.

    for Habits, the registration nos. 1019Z to 1023M (1019Z, 1020X, 1021T, 1022R, 1023M) are registered somewhere together with 108x series. which means, the first Demo Volgren Habit was 1024H, then came 1025E and so on until 108x when they decided to use 1019Z to 1023M first. tis is because tis block was previously reserved for some new buses (most probably Volgren UMW Dennis Lance) before the Demo Volgren Habit came but Tibs decided they had enough of Lances and gave up the block eventually.

    the rest of SMRT Buses' registration nos. follow the chronological order.

    let me try to recall+research:

    MB Hispano O.405 Mk1: around Oct 1994. first served on svc 851 when it turned PAC from 1st Nov 1994.

    MB Hispano O.405G Box 838H: 16th March 1996. Served on Svc 171 for inauguration, then later went on to Svc 856.

    MB Hispano O.405 Carocera: late 1999 - early 2000.

    MB Hispano O.405G Habit: 2000. i have the advertisement about the launch of Habits but i need to find it and hence the date for batch 1025M to 1060C.

    Dennis Lance UMW: 1998. They were deployed on Jln Kayu services (59/866, 103, 163) for about a year before Tibs had to hand back Sengkang & Jln Kayu to SBS.
