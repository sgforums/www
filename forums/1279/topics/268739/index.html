    Originally posted by pierre^^:
    FRrom hougang

    Take 72 or 27 to tampines. Den?

    If you are taking Service 27,

    Take the bus to the bus stop outside block 401 (Opposite Tampines Regional Library), then cross the road and change to Townlink 293. You should take note of the 'T' (Termination) Sign on the bus. If a bus displays that, I recommend you do not take this bus unless you are rushing. If not, take the bus. Do not alight at Tampines Interchange unless you are on the bus which is terminating at Tampines Interchange. Continue your journey to the bus stop at Block 827, Tampines Avenue 3 and alight there.

    If you are taking Service 72,

    Take the bus to Tampines Bus Interchange. If you plan to do so, transfer to Townlink 291 or Townlink 293. Please check the berths ('Tampines Street 84' for Townlink 291, 'Tampines Street 71' for Townlink 293) Alight opposite Block 827, Tampines Avenue 3 if you are taking Townlink 291. If you are taking Townlink 293, alight directly at the bus stop outside Block 827, Tampines Avenue 4.

    Alternatively, a shortcut to this is to take to the bus stop opposite the Darul Ghufran Mosque and cross over to the bus stop near the Mosque. Take Townlink 291 and alight opposite Block 827, Tampines Avenue 4.

    I recommend taking Service 72 if you are taking from Hougang Bus Interchange. It will be much faster as compared to Service 27.

    Correct me if I'm wrong.
