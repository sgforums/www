    Bedok Area Route Planning

    67: Eunos Interchange <> CCK 

    - Follows current route, just truncated to Eunos

    17A: Removed and replaced with service 26 

    26: Toa Payoh Interchange <> Changi Business Park Terminal

    - Follows same route until Chai Chee, then turns left onto Chai Chee St/Dr, Bedok MRT (Upp Changi Road),  Bedok North Ave 3, Bedok North Ave 4, ITE, Xilin Ave, CBP

    - This will help provide alternate route to terminated 67 between Bedok MRT and Bedok North Ave 3 

    - This will provide direct MRT access to Chai Chee residents + trunk service to Geylang/Eunos/Bedok North/ITE/CBP

    - This will provide Bedok North Ave 4 residents connection to Bedok MRT along Upp Changi Road to transfer to other services (+ alternative to sv 17)

    - This will connect ITE, Expo, CBP directly to Bedok North and Chai Chee

    - Residents along Bedok North Ave 3 will have a direct connection to Geylang, albeit a bit more winding than current 67

    Proposed sv 26 should resolve issues sighted above with sv 67 and 17A.
