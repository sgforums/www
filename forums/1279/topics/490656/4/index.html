    Went to Bt Panjang area to take a quick look ... apparently there's posters for individual services (979, 983) - which I saw is up at BPJ Rd (44259). There's also the overall large poster for route realignments at BPJ, starting from 27 Dec, concerning 920, 922, 975, 979 and 983, and it is up at BPJ Rd (44259) and Upp Bt Timah Rd (44021), but not (yet?) at 44029.

    Overall there'll be 13 bus stops along the four roads surrounding BPJ MRT/LRT: 3 along UBT Rd (1 new temp stop: 44031, after 44029), 2 along Petir Rd, 4 along Jelebu Rd and 4 along BPJ Rd (1 new temp stop: 44909, after 44259).

    920 and 922 will be realigned to start from Petir Rd (44851) and Jelebu Rd (44631), and serves BPJ Rd (44259) and UBT Rd (44031) on return journey. Same for 979, but it doesn't serve the stop at BPJ Rd.

    983 loops around BPJ MRT, serving the stops at Petir Rd, Jelebu Rd and BPJ Rd, but not UBT Rd.

    975 starts at Petir Rd (44851), Jelebu Rd (44731), BPJ Rd (44259) before going towards CCK. On return journey, serves BPJ Rd (44251) and Jelebu Rd (44739).

    171 / 970 to serve 44909 instead of 44259.
