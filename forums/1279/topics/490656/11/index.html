    BSEP new bus service 258 has problem with schedule adherence. Just now, I waited for 258 at Boon Lay Bus Interchange. The wait was quite long. The queue line went from long to short to long, as bus service 243G arrives. Finally, the bus 258 arrived, at 8:15pm. I took a glance at the queue line, it seems to extend all the way until the end of the yellow painted line. That's not the point.

    The bus 258 arrived at 8:15pm at Boon Lay Bus Interchange. The point is that when that bus was at the first bus stop, I saw another 258 at the opposite side of JW street 64. When the bus was street 81, I saw another 258.

    Clearly, bus service 258 has problem with schedule adherence. I hope someone can help me inform the time keeper at Joo Koon/Boon Lay Bus Interchange about it.

    It has been fifty three days since bus service 258 was introduced on 21 November 2015. I hope by now the LTA knows the loading pattern of bus service 258. The loading is low (only a few cats in the lower deck) during regular hours and the loading is very high (up to half of upper deck occupied) during peak hours (up to around 8pm). I am sure the LTA knows exactly which are the hours the bus service has very high loading. I hope the pattern has been consistent enough over the past fifty three days, to deem it necessary for bus service enhancements (increase frequency) during the periods of very high loading.
