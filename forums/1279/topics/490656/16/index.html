    Originally posted by BusAnalayzer:

    Both 13 and 76 are doing okay. Really no need to introduce another service at Marine Parade, that too when 47 has just been introduced.

    Agree, splitting them at Eunos and introducing new services would be a waste to the area. I agree that Marine Parade/Bedok have a ton of services but the sheer amount of duplication in the area is a problem. It is a product of history in being one of the older population centers. There are too many east-west connections to the city which is a leftover of old pan-island routes that were necessary prior to the introduction of the MRT. Just look at all the services that duplicate each other and EWL along Changi Rd. While they may provide unique connections to the city and western areas, these destinations should be reached by MRT and not long-winded bus routes, plus their traveling distances can really affect reliability. The east coast is ultimately lacking in north-south intra-town connections that link school/shopping clusters with neighbouring housing estates, and has a suboptimal MRT feeder system where connections are too skewed to Paya Lebar and Bedok. I hope this will change with the TEL and more emphasis is placed on MRT access and intra-town connectivity rather than connectivity into the city. Many routes will be obsolete with the introduction of TEL and I hope we don't end up with a situation similar to Changi Rd. 

    13 is another classic example of the area's sheer amount of duplication. It duplicates 16, 43 and 48 in connecting UEC with Marine Parade. As such, buses often run very empty. So, I see no harm in eliminating this sector. It presents an opportunity to re-allocate resources to serve new connections. 

    I suggest amending 13 such that it plied

    Sims Av East > Chai Chee St > Bedok North St 1 > Bedok North Av 3 > New Upp Changi Rd > Bedok South Av 3 > Upp East Coast Rd. 

    This new sector is about 7.8km which is slightly shorter than the deleted sector from Eunos. It will benefit:

    1. Chai Chee residents traveling going to Geylang Serai and provides a new connection to Kembangan MRT

    2. New connection between Bedok South & Bedok North other than 506 which charges higher fares and is infrequent. 

    3. East Coast GRC =)

    68 (Eunos <> Jln Kembangan loop)

    Sims Av > Still Rd > Still Rd South > Marine Parade Rd > Marine Cres > Marine Parade Rd > Siglap Rd > East Coast Rd > Frankel Av > Jln Kembangan > (Same route) > Marine Terrace > Marine Cres > (same route back to Eunos Int)

    1. Two-way connection to Eunos from Marine Terrace/Marine Cres, plus new intra-town connection to Kembangan/Frankel/Changi Rd area. 

    2. Connection to Geylang Serai market is still provided, albeit on the way back, one must walk to Eunos Int (might be an added hassle for elderly). City Plaza connection already provided via 43, 76, 135 & 197. 

    3. Lost connection to Geylang Redlight district but can still be accessed via 197 from Guillemard Rd. 

    Alternatively, this service could ply Siglap Rd > Chai Chee St > Chai Chee Dr > New Upp Changi Rd > Siglap Rd. However, as my proposed 13 amendment already serves Chai Chee plus Kembangan having poorer connections, this above proposal may not be fair to introduce at the same time.
