    WAB buses bought after 2001 (before 16 years old) (Dennis Trident 3, B10TL, B9TL CDGE, K230UB Batch 2 Euro IV, Batch 1 Euro V, Batch 2 Euro V, OC500LE, B10BLE CNG) can be sold on Carousell (http://www.carousell.com)
