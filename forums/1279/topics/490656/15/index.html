    Again a new set of rumours, and I'm quite suprised they'd touch 13 because it remains the only unique connection between Marine Terrace and Geylang Serai/Geylang/Lavender St. There are several possibilities. 

    1. Truncating 13 at Sims Pl, and introducing a new Sims Pl <> Upp East Coast Rd service covering the old route. This would eliminate a connection between Lavender St/Marine Parade but it would open up more connections for the Sims Pl/Dr area. Though this would buck the rumours that have been circulating about a Eunos-MP service. 

    2. Amending 13 such that it plied Still Rd > East Coast Rd > Upp East Coast Rd. 13 is quite duplicative of 16, 43 and 48. Plying East Coast Rd improves MRT access which is very poor for this section of East Coast Rd (between Telok Kurau Rd & Siglap Rd). The only viable connection currently provided is to Eunos/Paya Lebar MRT through 155, which 13 would enhance. But this is far from ideal as Eunos can only be served in one-direction and is not the nearest MRT station, unlike Kembangan. It is sad that Kembangan can only be accessed by 32 indirectly and amending 42 is very difficult. Don't even mention Bedok MRT because it is really a huge back-track for most commuters who originate from the City. 

    Then M4 is reinstated as a Eunos> Sims Av > Still Rd > Still Rd South > Marine Parade Rd > Marine Cres/Terr loop service to make Eunos MRT access two-way and more reliable. 

    3. 13 is truncated at Eunos and a new service is introduced covering the deleted sector. However, I am skeptical as this would invariably make Geylang Serai less accessible. Even though Eunos Int is within walking distance, for many elderly who patronise Geylang Serai market, it can be an added hassle. 

    Anyways, a new route is introduced linking Eunos > Upp East Coast Rd though possibly this too could be amended to link Upp East Coast > Bedok South Av 1 > New Upp Changi Rd > Chai Chee Dr > Chai Chee St (loop).
