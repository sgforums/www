    117

    I like the route. It has low loading now, but high potential in future.

    Sembawang - future loading will increase to Canberra Estate (2017)

    Canberra - loading will increase as only service from Canberra Estate to Khatib, SAP and Seletar Circus Interchange. The new service that will be introduced is a short trunk from Canberra to Yishun/Sembawang. The only other service is 169 which has a very different route. 

    Yishun - expect loading to increase to Yishun Ave 1 in future (2017) even if 805 is there as both ply along Yishun Ave 2. In the direction of Yishun Ave 1-Yishun MRT, not so much as bus stops are in opp. direction

    Khatib - expect loading to increase to Yishun Ave 1 and SAP (2017) 

    Yishun Ave 1 - loading will increase (2017) towards Seletar Circus Interchange to transfer to 39, 86, 102, 168, 858

     Seletar Aerospace - loading will increase as still major development in on-going (2H 2016, 2017)

    Seletar Circus - major interchange point for SAP, Sengkang West, Punggol West and Yishun Ave 1 residents

    Punggol West - still Punggol Matilda under development (2017). Loading will increase further in 2017 towards Seletar Interchange & Yishun

    Punggol Interchange - loading here may not increase much expect for Punggol intra-town transfer.

    Overall, my estimate is in 2017 the 117 DDs will be housefull. The service was introduced way before and should have been introduced full fleet SDs and eventually upgraded to DDs as required in 2H 2016 and 2017.
