    Based on http://sgwiki.com/wiki/History_of_Services_361_-_410#Service_371, it seems that route 371 in the past before 2003 was:

    1. Sengkang Bus Interchange
    2. Sengkang Square
    3. Compassvale Road
    4. Sengkang East Way
    5. Rivervale Drive
    6. Rivervale Lane (route replaced with Punggol Road)
    7. Compassvale Drive (original route loops here via Sengkang East Road, Sengkang East Avenue and Sengkang Central)
    8. Anchorvale Drive (route replaced with Sengkang East Avenue)
    9. Anchorvale Lane (was eventually skipped)
    10. Sengkang East Avenue
    11. Anchorvale Road (was eventually skipped)
    12. Anchorvale Drive
    13. Compassvale Drive (route replaced with Sengkang East Avenue)
    14. Rivervale Lane
    15. Rivervale Drive (route replaced with Punggol Road)
    16. Sengkang East Way
    17. Compassvale Road
    18. Sengkang Square
    19. Sengkang Bus Interchange

    The original route 371 plied between Sengkang (SK) bus interchange and Compassvale Road. In 2000, it was extended to Anchorvale Lane. When the East loop opens in 2003, route 371 was changed to skip some routes and ply other roads. In 2005, it was replaced by 163A (163M).
