    Originally posted by TIB1096A:

    Connects Havelock,Kay Siang,Kim Tian and Bukit Merah

    By Havelock do you mean Havelock Rd/ Jln Bt Ho Swee? This is interesting as these areas are well-served by bus svcs along nearby main roads (Ganges Ave/Lower Delta Rd for Havelock, Tiong Bahru Rd/Jln Bt Merah for Kim Tian, Alexandra Rd for Kay Siang), but have weak or no direct bus connectivity (no bus svc along the one-way stretch of Havelock Rd/Jln Bt Ho Swee, Kim Tian Rd and only svc 32 along Margaret Dr with no bus svc along Kay Siang Rd/Dawson Rd).

    This should benefit the elderly who stay in Havelock and Kim Tian areas and have to walk some distance to the nearest bus stop along main roads, while providing greater bus capacity/connectivity to Kay siang/Dawson areas where the new 40+ storey flats are coming up. This bus svc could ply Dawson Rd/Kay Siang Rd then follow 111/132 along Tanglin Rd towards Orchard.

    On the other side closer to Lor 1 Geylang, this svc could provide one or more of the following connections:

    - 2 way connection between Mcnair Rd/Towner Rd and Boon Keng MRT, where svc 21 currently plys one way

    - bus svc into St George's Rd/St Wilfred Rd, where the old svc 20 looped (unlikely as this would require a red/white plate system)

    - bus svc to Kallang Way industrial area, currently served by 125 (can ply areas unserved by 125, such as Genting Lane)

    - 2 way connection between Sims Place/ Sims Drive and Aljunied and/or Kallang MRT (currently, 125 loops around Sims Drive/Ave and Aljunied Rd and only provides one way connection between Sims Drive and Aljunied MRT)

     

    *The above are purely my personal speculation, and do not represent any insider info from LTA or SBST.
