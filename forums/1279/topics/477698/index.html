    SBS Transit Ltd. (2014). Bus And Train Services During The Chinatown Chinese New Year Celebrations 2014 - Opening Ceremony & Official Light Up (11 January 2014) [Press Release]. Retrieved from http://www.sbstransit.com.sg/

    7 January 2014 - To facilitate the Opening Ceremony & Official Light Up of the Chinatown Chinese New Year Celebrations, on 11 January 2014, 15 SBS Transit Bus Services will be diverted due to road closures while the operating hours of train services and the three Chinatown Direct bus services will be extended.


    Bus Services
    On Saturday, 11 January 2014, from 2.00pm to 11.00pm, 15 SBS Transit bus services - 2, 12, 33, 54, 63, 80, 124, 143, 145, 147, 166, 197, CT8, CT18 and CT28 - will skip eight bus stops along Eu Tong Sen Street, New Bridge Road, Outram Road and Upper Cross Street due to road closures for the event.

    To better serve the travel needs of commuters, the service hours for Services CT8, CT18 and CT28 will be extended that evening. The last bus for these three services will depart from the bus stop at Eu Tong Sen Street - After Hospital Drive (bus stop number 10017) at 12.30am. CT8 serves Ang Mo Kio bus interchange, CT 18 serves Bedok bus interchange and CT28 serves Clementi bus interchange.

     

    Train Services
    Train service hours on the North East Line and Downtown Line will be extended on the night of 11 January 2014.

    The last NEL train in the direction of Harbourfront will depart Punggol Station at 12.02am, Serangoon Station at 12.13am, Dhoby Ghaut Station at 12.25am, Chinatown Station at 12.29am and Outram Park Station at 12.31am. In the direction of Punggol, the last train will depart Harbourfront Station at 12.29am, Outram Park Station at 12.32am, Chinatown Station at 12.34am, Dhoby Ghaut Station at 12.38am and Serangoon Station at 12.50am.

    The last LRT train will depart five minutes after the arrival of the NEL last train at Sengkang and Punggol Stations. The last Sengkang LRT train is scheduled to depart the Town Centre at 1.05am while the last train for Punggol LRT will depart the Town Centre at 1.07am.

    On the Downtown Line, the last train in the direction of Bugis will depart Chinatown Station at 12.19am, Bayfront Station at 12.24am and Promenade Station at 12.26am. The last train towards Chinatown will leave Bugis at 12.20am, Promenade at 12.22am and Bayfront Station at 12.24am.

    Please refer to the attached poster for more details.

    SMRT Corporation Ltd. (2014). SMRT Travel Advisory for Chinese New Year 2014 [Press Release]. Retrieved from http://www.mynewsdesk.com/sg/smrt/pressreleases/smrt-travel-advisory-for-chinese-new-year-2014-947309

    In light of increased demand during the festive season, train and selected bus service hours will be extended on 11, 18 and 25 January 2014.

    North-South and East-West Lines

    The last trains toward Joo Koon, Pasir Ris, Marina Bay and Jurong East MRT stations will depart City Hall MRT station at 12.30am on 11, 18 and 25 January.

    Circle Line

    The last train towards HarbourFront MRT station will depart Dhoby Ghaut MRT station at 11.55pm. The last train towards Dhoby Ghaut MRT station will depart HarbourFront MRT station at 11.30pm on 11, 18 and 25 January.

    Bukit Panjang LRT

    Passengers on the last northbound train can connect to the last Bukit Panjang LRT train at 1.30am on 11, 18 and 25 January.

    Extended feeder bus service hours

    Passengers on the last trains can connect to the following feeder bus services on 11 January, 18 January and 25 January at the timings below:

     

    • (Bukit Batok) 173A, 189A, 941, 945, 947 -  {Last bus timing} 1.40am
    • (Choa Chu Kang) 300, 302, 307 - {Last bus timing} 1.35am
    • (Sembawang) 859A - {Last bus timing} 1.25am
    • (Woodlands) 901, 911, 912, 913 - {Last bus timing} 1.20am
    • (Yishun) 804, 812 - {Last bus timing} 1.30am

     

    Bus diversion due to Chinese New Year Celebrations at Chinatown

    SMRT bus services 61, 190, 851, 961 and 970 will be temporarily diverted on 11 January from 2.00pm to 11.00pm, 30 January from 5.00pm to 3.00am and 9 February from 2.00pm to 11.00pm, due to road closures for the Chinese New Year Celebrations at Chinatown.

    Please refer to the attached poster for more details.

    Passengers may contact SMRT Customer Relations Centre at 1800-336-8900 from 7.00am to 11.00pm on weekdays and 9.00am to 1.00pm on Saturdays (excluding Sundays and public holidays) or visit www.smrt.com.sg for more information on the road diversion.

    SBS Transit Ltd. (2014). Bus Stops Skipped due to Thaipusam 2014 [Press Release]. Retrieved from http://www.sbstransit.com.sg/press/2014-01-13-01.aspx

    24 SBS Transit bus services - 7, 14, 16, 21, 23, 36, 64, 65, 66, 111, 123, 124, 125, 130, 131, 139, 141, 143, 147, 162, 162M, 166, 174 and 175 - will skip nine bus stops along Bencoolen Street, Bras Basah Road, Clemenceau Avenue, Middle Road, Orchard Road, Penang Road and Serangoon Road due to road closures for Thaipusam. The affected period will be from Thursday, 16 January 2014, 8.00pm to Saturday, 18 January 2014, 3.00am.

    Please refer to the attached poster for more details.

    SBS Transit Ltd. (2014). Extension of Train And Bus Services for Chinese New Year Preparations (18 & 25, 27 - 29 January 2014) [Press Release]. Retrieved from http://www.sbstransit.com.sg/press/2014-01-14-01.aspx

    SBS Transit will be extending the operating hours of its train services as well as its Chinatown Direct bus services in the weeks leading up to the Chinese New Year, so as to cater to the travel needs of commuters better.

    TRAIN SERVICES

    North East Line

    For the next two Saturdays, 18 and 25 January, the last train in the direction of HarbourFront will depart Punggol Station at 12.02am, Serangoon Station at 12.13am, Dhoby Ghaut Station at 12.25am, Chinatown Station at 12.29am and Outram Park Station at 12:31am. The last train towards Punggol will leave HarbourFront Station at 12:29am, Outram Park Station at 12.32am, Chinatown Station at 12.34am, Dhoby Ghaut Station at 12.38am and Serangoon Station at 12.50am.

    Sengkang and Punggol LRT

    Services on the Punggol and Sengkang LRT systems will also be extended to coincide with the last train arrivals on the North East Line for the two Saturdays, 18 and 25 January 2014. The last train on the Sengkang LRT will depart the Town Centre Station at 1.05am while the last train on the Punggol LRT will leave the Town Centre at 1.07am.

    Downtown Line

    Similarly for the two Saturdays, 18 and 25 January 2014, the last train in the direction of Bugis will depart Chinatown Station at 12.19am, Bayfront Station at 12.24am and Promenade Station at 12.26am. The last train towards Chinatown will leave Bugis at 12.20am, Promenade at 12.22am and Bayfront Station at 12.24am.

    BUS SERVICES

    The operating hours of the three Chinatown Direct Bus Services will also be extended in the weeks leading up to the Chinese New Year. CT8, which plies between Ang Mo Kio and Chinatown, and CT8, which travels from Bedok to Chinatown, currently operate on a daily basis. CT28 which plies between Clementi and Chinatown, on the other hand, operates only during the Chinese New Year festive period. Since 11 January 2014, CT28 has been in operation and will continue to do so until 30 January 2014.

    For two Saturdays, 18 and 25 January 2014, the last bus of CT8, CT18 and CT28 heading to Chinatown will depart from their respective heartlands at 9.00pm instead of their usual times at 8.00pm. The last bus back from Chinatown will depart at 12.30am from the bus stop after Hospital Drive (bus stop number 10017) along Eu Tong Sen Street.

    From 27 to 29 January 2014, three days preceding CNY eve, the last bus to Chinatown will similarly depart from the three heartlands at 9.00pm while the last bus will leave Chinatown at 12.00am.

    On all other weekdays, from 11 January to 26 January, the three services to Chinatown will operate from 10.00am to 4.30pm while the return journey will be from 11.00am to 5.00pm. On Sundays, they will operate from 10.00am to 8.00pm towards Chinatown while the return trip will be from 11am to 10pm.

    Please refer to the attached poster for more details.
