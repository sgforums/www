    Land Transport Authority of Singapore. (2014). Joint News Release by the Land Transport Authority & SBST - Route Extension of Bus Service 11 [News Releases]. Retrieved from http://app.lta.gov.sg/apps/news/page.aspx?c=2&id=87a5453e-8b3d-4391-8a03-d74e954b98e7

    30 May 2014 – From 8 June 2014, Service 11 will have its existing route extended to serve Tanjong Rhu. Currently, the service plies between Lorong 1 Geylang Bus Terminal and the Singapore Indoor Stadium along Stadium Crescent. With the extension, it will call at seven new bus stops along Tanjong Rhu Road and Rhu Cross. This will be the 27th bus service launched under the Bus Service Enhancement Programme (BSEP).

    The extended route will provide better connectivity for commuters in the Tanjong Rhu area to Stadium, Mountbatten and Kallang MRT stations, as well as Lorong 1 Geylang Bus Terminal. Besides enhancing connectivity to key transport nodes, the extended route will also provide convenient access to amenities such as the Singapore Indoor Stadium, Leisure Park Kallang and the upcoming Sports Hub and Kallang Wave.

    With the extension, Service 11 will operate an hour earlier from 6.00am, with the last bus departure at 11.30pm daily.
