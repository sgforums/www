  • TIB1234T
    22 Jan 09, 22:11

    I find advertisements from magazines, like "i weekly". If its nice, I will take the advertisements and do some measurements to it to fit my buses. If its ok, then I use printer to scan and print out the adverts on A4 paper.

    Step 1: Place the adverts properly the way u want on your bus.
    Step 2: Press the outline of the bus, including the windows (not so hard, so long as its visible to u).
    Step 3: Use pen-knife (for art use) to cut the outline properly w/ the help of long ruler.
    Step 4: Use normal glue (can be easily taken out) to glue the adverts onto bus body.
    Step 5: Cut out the outline of the picture or information u want to paste onto the windows.
    Step 6: Paste it onto the windows following Step 4.
