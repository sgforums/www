    Originally posted by yongjianrong:


    I think i'm a bit blur...What type of bus do I post here?

    use this to help you.

    http://www.sgwiki.com/wiki/buses

    example 1. SBS9547G does Service 143, post it here in this format

    SBS9547G on Service 143 (SLBP SP)

    the brackets indicate the status of the bus you are posting. in this example, SBS9547G is an SLBP spare bus.

    example 2. SBS8033D does Service 143, do the same. SBS8033D is perm on 183 under SLBP depot.

    SBS8033D on Service 143 (SLBP 183)

    if SBS8033D is doing Service 183 which is its perm service do not record down here. cameo spotting is for buses that do services that are outside of its status.

    hope this helps. do noteÂ SBS8033D and SBS9547G are not spottedÂ on Service 143 today, i'm just giving examples.
