    SMRT 980 to bus stop at Tekka Mall (this is the mall with Sheng Siong Supermarket and after the bus stop outside the Little India NEL Station).

    Cross the road, walk to bus stop outside Sim Lim Square, change to SBST 23.

    It's a 35-min ride to Temasek Poly from there.

    Or take MRT to Tampines, change to SBST 23. Stops right outside TP.
