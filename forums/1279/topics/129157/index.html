    My fellow Bus Enthusiasts,

    Many of you have come across the book on the History of Trams, Trolleybuses and Buses in Singapore from the 1880s to the 1960s at the National Reference Library. Mr Mike Davis, one of the authors for that book, is publishing a new book in the next few weeks as the first part of a vehicle history of SBS.

    Quoted from a message dated 19 Apr 2005.

    "Now I have almost completed SBS Part One - The early single-deck buses from
    1973 and the first double-deckers of 1977.

    The format follows the successful Hong Kong Buses series in giving a type by
    type account of the various makes and models operated by SBS from 1973 until
    approximately 1983, plus the single deck demonstration buser operated by SBS
    for various lengths of time.

    A fleet list is included showing as many buses as can be traced from those
    joining SBS from Associated, Amalgamated and United - the constituent
    companies (which themselves absorbed the old Singapore Traction Company).

    The price of this book is still not finalized but it won't be more than GBP
    Â£20 (plus shipping). It is approximately 100 pages, A4, with hundreds of
    pictures. About 15-pages will be in colour to show the liveries of the
    day."

    In a recent message, he mentioned that the book will be printed both in the UK and Singapore to reduce freight costs and make the book more affordable for us. Publication in Singapore will be in about 6 weeks time.

    I will keep an update on the developments of this new book, so watch this space!

    Regards,
    Suhirman "The Bus Guide" Mahmod
