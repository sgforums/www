  • The_Bus_Guide
    28 Mar 04, 10:20
    Originally posted by freakybuses:
    I thought 213 was serving from Changi Village to Old Sarum Rd via Telok Paku Drug Rehabiliation Ctr in 1982? Same for 210 serving Old Sarum Rd.
    That is correct. 210 and 213 have identical routes in 1980-82. The difference was that 213 will first proceed to the DRC and back. The buses went via Upp Changi Rd (this part is now known as Changi Village Rd), Netheravon Rd, Cranwell Rd, Tangmere Rd, Old Sarum Rd, Martlesham Rd, Cranwell Rd, Netheravon Rd, Cranwell Rd and Upp Changi Rd.

    210 was amended because Martlesham Rd became a restricted area. In Bus Guide 1985, instead of going into Old Sarum Rd, 210 proceeded to Upp Changi Rd and return to the terminus. Alternate buses proceed in different directions.

    Apparently, 213 was withdrawn. It re-appeared in the 1985 Bus Guide as the route mentioned (Marine Parade - Opera Estate)
