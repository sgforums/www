    19 Mar 04, 01:48
    Just for everyone's info. Feeder use Green/White Plates. Trunk uses Red/White Plates.

    St Michael's Estate

    Svc 21 124 125 131 186 use IN OUT cards and Red/White Plates.
    IN and White Plate means going INTO St Mikes Estate.
    OUT and Red Plate means going OUT OF St Mikes Estate.

    Svc 139
    Red towards BTM
    White towards TPY
    They have an additional Side Desto placed in front of the windscreen.

    Serangoon Gardens

    Svc 73
    Red towards AMK
    White towards TPY

    Svc 136
    Red towards PUN
    White towards AMK

    Serangoon Feeders uses placards like the St Mikes Services.
    Svc 315
    'S'goon North Ave 4' towards looping area.
    'Int' towards SGN

    Svc 317
    'Bwick Drive' towards looping area.
    'Int' towards SGN

    Macpherson Estate

    Svc 155
    Red towards MRP
    White towards TPY
    They have an additional Side Desto placed in front of the windscreen.

    Feeder Services
    All Feeder Services only call at one same stop: The Interchange. Other than that they do not call at any same bus stop.

    225G/410G goes clockwise
    225W/410W goes anti-clockwise.

    231G/243G goes anti-clockwise.
    231W/243W goes clockwise.
    --------------------------------------------------------

    Hope all these info are sufficent in answering all your queries and doubts.
