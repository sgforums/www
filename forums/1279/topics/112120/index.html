    Originally posted by boy20:
    I have some questions about SBST Buses, I hope you bus fans can help me.

    1. When will the 36XX, 37XX, 38XX type of buses be 'sent to sleep"?
    SBS 3573U to SBS 3722G - Scania N113CRBs, introduced in 1989. They should be retiring in 2006 when they reached statutory age of 17 years.

    2. The new 150 Double Decker buses that SBST are buying, are they air-con or non air-con types?
    They will be airconditioned. SBST and SMRTB will no longer purchase new non-aircon buses.

    3. When will the 72XX type of buses be "sent to sleep"?
    They should be retired beginning 2011.

    4. Will the Jurong Industial Services be "non air-con forever"
    That will depend on who operates the services in future.
