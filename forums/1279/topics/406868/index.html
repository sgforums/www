    In this session, I will bring you to the booth of YOUNGMAN NEOPLAN, YUTONG, GAC HINO and others exhibitors.

    YOUNGMAN NEOPLAN
    First of all, let us visit YOUNGMAN NEOPLAN. YOUNGMAN NEOPLAN was a joint venture between Chinese commercial vehicles manufacturer Youngman Automobile and German coach builder Neoplan. It is based in Jinhua City, Jiejiang Province. Youngman Neoplan mainly focuses on the production of high-class coaches, which are sold to the domestic market. Recent years, Youngman Neoplan also export their products to the neighboring countries, like South Korea.


    Youngman JNP6137WE-1 is a sleeper bus designed specially for China market. It is suitable for ultra-long travel. It is powered by a MAN D2866LOH29 diesel engine. It is fitted with Neoplan front and rear axles and ZF drive axle.



    Youngman JNP6127EA’s (right) design is based on Neoplan Starliner. This bus is featured with made-in-China diesel engines and axles.



    JNP6790T is a 7.9m-long bus for sight-seeing uses.

    YUTONG
    Yutong is the largest bus manufacturer in China. Its product ranges from low-class to high-class. Apart from its sales in the domestic market, Yutong also exports their bus to overseas market, including Russia, South America, Africa, etc.


    ZK6122H9:
    Bus fans in China usually call it “Birthday Bun”, as its appearance looks like a Chinese-style birthday bun.



    ZK6116HA9:
    It is a brand new inter-city model introduced by Yutong.


    Rear of ZK6116HA9 (left) and ZK6116HA9 (right)

    GAC HINO is a corporation established by Guangzhou Automobile and Japanese commercial vehicle producer HINO. Its headquarters is located in Shenyang.
