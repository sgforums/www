     Let's backtrack to the first time OC500LEs were deployed. Remember WLDEP did not take in any OC, and all the O405s (with the L113s from KJDEP) kept coming in at them? 

    And it was only when 128B - 134H entered WLDEP first on 961, that they transferred 119C - 122S from AMDEP. And over time, more OCs transferred to WLDEP from KJDEP and WLDEP in exchange for some O405s from WLDEP.

    Let's now go to the timeline of the Citaros. Excluding 136C, the PB Batch 1 buses were deployed to both WLDEP and AMDEP. 5 for each depot. Then, when the PB Batch 2 came out, KJDEP initially took in 149R - 151H, 169H, 177J, 181X - 184M, 186H - 188C. But then most of these buses got transferred to either WLDEP or AMDEP due to them taking in more MAN. 

    When MAN A22 PBs were first introduced, 192P 231K 236Y 238S 240J from AMDEP transferred to WLDEP, but in exchange for no buses. This left AMDEP with no MAN A22s. It was only much later on when some of the OCs, Citaros and O405s got transferred to KJDEP or AMDEP due to the intake of the 25x-26x series MAN. 

    And now, with the intake of 27x-29x series MAN to WLDEP, Citaros O405s have been transferred. 

    But don't you see the patterns? When one depot doesn't want/take in a certain model, eventually over time, the deployment will settle down for that depot? So I am hoping over time that the excess MANs be deployed to KJDEP and AMDEP hopefully, in return for the Citaros and O405s.
