    What is the model of the Scania bus?
    It is a L113CRL with Soon Chow body. There are 12 buses altogether. First deployed as an Airbus on 27 April 1995.

    Which other areas/Interchanges had similiar service?
    Clementi and Ang Mo Kio Interchanges. It is also used as a Ngee Ann Poly Shuttle Bus operating from Clementi Inter.

    Is it installed with EZ link card readers?Fares?Is it consider as an express service?
    No EZ link reader nor coinbox nor validator as these services are free. The buses run non-stop although the Clementi shuttle calls at a bus stop after Sixth Ave.
