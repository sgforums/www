    Why we bother about the registration numbers of buses? Simple enough, different buses may have different gadgets onboard, or even a whole new refurbished interior. Further more the buses might not stay on the same service always, hence this is another important factor why we bothered about that.

Buses go fixed routes and hence can be easily identified for our own cause.
