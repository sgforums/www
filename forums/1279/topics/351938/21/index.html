    27 Jul 09, 18:51

    Tampines - Pasir Ris is already well connected.

    Perhaps the only good use of this Svc is for Pasir Ris - Yishun

    I boarded this bus from Tamp for the purpose of going to Yishun. I must be the only one to have done so. No worries as I had a lot of time and already knew that it wud take a longer time than 969. But was surprised it took 30 mins even before entering the expressway torwards Yishun.

    I just modified it a little where in this case the connection between Pasir Ris - Tamp remains, but an additional link to Marine Parade is made. The total time taken would be of no difference I guess. Perhaps quite a  bit more time taken for Pasir Ris -Yishun. 

    Rationale of boarding this bus (Current):

    At Tamp - To go to Pasir Ris

    Pasir RIs - Go to Yishun

    and vice versa

    Rationale of boarding this bus (With proposed amendment):

    At Pasir Ris - To go to Tamp

    At Tamp - To go to Marine Parade or To go to Yishun with a slightly longer trip. Alternative to 969 if a 15 mins of your time  wouldn't matter or you think you just missed 969 and don't wish to wait for that. or To go to Pasir Ris in the opp direction.

    At Marine Parade - To go to Yishun/ Go to Tamp on the opp direction, Both ways would be faster than the current situation

    At Yishun - Marine Parade/Tamp alternative to 969 with same reasons above

    For Pasir Ris - Yishun travel, one can always get down at Tamp and make a transfer for a faster journey or if time is not an issue and you're joyriding this route would take approx 50 mins (20 min longer than usual)
