    30 Jan 10, 10:50

    Is it actually feasible to build a bus interchange at the future one-North MRT station at Portsdown Road? In this way, buses from Buona Vista terminal (and perhaps also from Ghim Moh terminal) can be diverted to a common bus interchange.

    • Services 32, 48 and 145 will be extended to North Buona Vista Road.
    • Service 74 will be cut back to Portsdown Road.
    • Service 91 will merge with 191 to form a service that plys from the Ayer Rajah Bus depot to Portsdown Road, but will serve the interchange / MRT station.
    • Service 200 will be re-routed to ply Ghim Moh Road.
    • Service 95 will be re-routed to serve the bus interchange.
    • Service 92 remains unchanged.

    The services that terminate at the new interchange will hence be 32, 48, 74, 91, 95, 145 and 200. More services can also be introduced from there to serve the one-north park and NUH / NUS.
