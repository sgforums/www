    133 Ang Mo Kio > Shenton Way will operate from Mondays to Fridays ONLY. Same operating times.
    133M Shenton Way > Suntec City will be renumbered as 133A and will operate on Saturdays and Sundays from 0630 > 2330 hrs.
    133M Ang Mo Kio > Suntec City(the old 133 route) will be introduced and will operate daily from 0530 > 2345 hrs.
    On Saturdays and Sundays,Passengers going to MBS(Marina Bay Sands) may alight from 133M at Suntec City and transfer to 133A to get to MBS(Marina Bay Sands)
