    Existing route  www.sbstransit.com.sg

    Route Amendments Summary
    - Commence operations from Boon Lay Bus Interchange
    (Even though there are space constraints in Boon Lay Interchange, this extension will benefit commuters.)
    - Call at 1 pair of bus stops along Jurong West St 64 and 3 pairs of bus stops along Boon Lay Way
    (The 3 pairs of bus stops along Boon Lay Way will be more conveniently served and complements the existing Service 254, while the pair of bus stops along Jurong West St 64 will serve Boon Lay MRT Station.)
    - Call at 1 new bus stop along Jurong West St 71 and another existing bus stop along the same road and 1 pair of bus stops along Jurong West Ave 4
    (Jurong West St 71 is only served by NightRider NR5 existingly. This route amendment will benefit commuters staying along Jurong West St 71.)
    - Skip 1 bus stop along Tanglin Rd and 2 bus stops along Orchard Rd
