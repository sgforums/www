    Changes For Service 700, 700A & 960.

    Service 700 & 700A will be amended to ply Bukit Timah Rd, Sungei Rd, Ophir Rd and Victoria St before continuing the route from Bras Basah Rd towards Shenton Way Terminal (Service 700) and looping at Teamsek Ave (Service 700A). In its returning trip, it will ply Victoria St, Rochor Rd, Rochor Canal Rd and 4 stops along Bukit Timah Rd before continuing its route towards Bukit Panjang Interchange. It no longer plies Scotts Rd, Orchard Rd and 2 stops along Bras Basah Rd & Stamford Rd, Orchard Rd, Penang Rd, Somerset Rd, Grange Rd, Orchard Link, Orchard Turn and Scotts Rd in its returning trip.

    Service 960 will ply Scotts Rd, Orchard Rd and 2 stops along Bras Basah Rd before the stop outside Raffles Hotel and continuing its trip towards Marina Centre Terminal. Towards Woodlands Regional Interchange, it will ply Stamford Rd, Orchard Rd, Penang Rd, Somerset Rd, Grange Rd, Orchard Link, Orchard Turn and Scotts Rd. It no longer plies Bukit Timah Rd, Sungei Rd, Ophir Rd and Victoria St towards Marina Centre Terminal & Victoria St, Rochor Rd, Rochor Canal Rd and 4 stops along Bukit Timah Rd towards Woodlands Regional Interchange.
