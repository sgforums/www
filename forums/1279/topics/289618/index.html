  • blur_guy
    4 Nov 07, 14:18
    To apply for SBSTransit as a bus captain, you can go to the Interchange's control room to request for the application form.

    If you are a Singaporean Chinese and meet CDAC training requirment, you may want to take your own class 4 /5 test 1st. Class 4/5 license is yours even if you leave the bus company.

    (Note: not neccessary for application as the bus company will send you for class 4A Vocational license training/test if you do not have).

    Details is as below:

    http://www.cdac.org.sg/cdac_corp/resource/skills_courses/eng/Class%204-5-web.htm

    CDAC SKILLS TRAINING AWARD SCHEME

    Course
    Class 4 or Class 5 Driving License

    Full Course Fee
    $550

    CDAC Sponsored Fee
    $412

    Upfront Payment by Award Holder
    $138

    *Achievement Incentive
    $110

    *Upon successful completion and attainment of Class 4 or Class 5 Driving License

    Award holders must achieve at least 75% attendance and sit for all assessments. Those who failed to do so would have to refund the sponsored amount of $412.
