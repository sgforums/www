    Disciplinary action will be taken against bochap driver who ignored suspicious bag: SBS

    After a post about an SBS bus driver ignoring an unattended piece of luggage, SBS Transit has since responded to say that disciplinary action will be taken against him.

    This was in response to STOMPer Siti's post about the driver being too bochap in handling the situation.

    The spokesperson from SBS Transit wrote:

    "The STOMPer, Siti, did the correct thing by informing the Bus Captain when she spotted the unattended bag.

    "We have investigated the incident and noted that our Bus Captain did come out of his seat immediately to check the unattended bag which is in line with our security guidelines.

    "However, he should have followed strict security protocols which all Bus Captains undergo as part of their training such as asking if the bag belonged to any passenger on board as part of his checks.

    "In this case, more should have been done and we are taking disciplinary action against the Bus Captain for his lapses."
