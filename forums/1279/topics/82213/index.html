    Just came back from KL today, after staying for 3 nights at a hotel at Petaling St which is so close to all the bussie action in KL. Took plenty of pix too which i will upload later.

    Basically, there are these main operators:

    Intrakota Consolidated ( the largest and most plentiful )Uses the same validator as singapore
    Metrobus ( 2 man operation )
    Park May ( city liner )
    Triton, famous for its lances and darts. Also run Putra and KTM Kommunter Shuttles
    Sri Indah, KL-Kajang Express but uses Hinos
    Transnasional wif its silver gleaming MANs to Klang
    SJ Bus ( only saw one OH )
    Selangor Bus

    All are full AC except the last with some very old Merz NACs

    Around the Chinatown area, the main bus terminus or bus stands are:
    Kota Raya
    Pasar Seni LRT station, which also houses the Klang bus station
    Mydin department stall
    Jalan Tun HS Lee
    Bangkok Bank

    Routes still compiling.....and oh one bus svc can be operated by a few bus operators, like svc 69 is operated by both Intrakota and Park may

    MOst of the areas os. KL do not have bus stop. Ask the locals for the location or flag it like a taxi.

    Fares typically range from 0.60to 1.60 RM for Intrakota, Park May
    Metrobus from 0.70 to 2.00RM. Do note that fare hijacking by the driver is a rather common occurance.
