    I departed from my house at 1330 hrs to Yishun to get my Bendy bus EZ Link card. Awaiting for Svc 853 to come at S'goon Central. Here it came, TIB 946D, the last Lance 245 in TIBS. At 1432 hrs, I got onto Svc 965 to Sengkang. However, it WASN'T A MB O405 VOLGREN!!! Haiz. Blame on my luck of not getting a MB O405 volgren. Instead, I got onto a MB O405 Hispano (TIB 713). I liked the driver speeding on SLE n TPE!!! FULL SPEED ALONG the Expressways! Wah hoo! 1452 hrs, I reached Sengkang Interchange to wait for 9888Y. As a result, I got off the bloody bus at Bendemeer Rd and transfer to Svc 23. That driver was so gr8! He did some speeding along Jln Besar. After getting what i supposed to get from Sim Lim Square, I went to Selegie Rd to take any bus home. Feeling thirsty, I got myself a can of coke.
