    Not really a bus observation, but rather an opinion.

    Recently, the government unveiled the master plan for Jurong Lake District.

    The master plan revealed the location of the HSR station, as well as the location of two new MRT stations which would connect the Jurong Region Line and Cross Island Line to the High Speed Rail Line.

    With regards to the provision of bus service at Jurong Lake District, I believe it would be just a mere amendment of bus routes that ply the Jurong Town Hall Road area, to make use of the new roads at Jurong Lake District to improve reliability and enhance connectivity.

    This is because there is already an over-supply of bus capacity at this area. Bus routes like 178 are running empty buses along Jurong Town Hall Road. An amendment of existing bus routes would be adequate. No need for new bus route.
