    I've noticed serveral times during evening peak where 258's queue have less than 15 people while the queue for 243G never stops coming

    My berth suggestion for BNL, based on my observations and from other forum members suggestions on swapping 258 and 256's berths

    Berth B1: 179A, 179, 193

    Berth B2: 192, 241, 242

    Berth B3: 243G, 258, 181

    Berth B4: 243W, 256, 199

    Berth B5: 240, 251, 172, 405

    Berth B6: 180, 178, 187

     

    Note: I wanted to put 180 and 172 together but as both buses operate on quite a number of bendies, I feel it is best to not put them together in the same berth

     

    Pros: The buses heading to Gek Poh from BNL are all grouped together, in the case where 258 arrives before 243G, people heading to St 81 can board 258 instead, same for 256 and 243W. 192 can help clear a bit of 241's loading for people alighting at Street 63.

    Cons: Putting 179 AND 179A together in the same berth, I'm not sure if this is a great idea though at least it is at the very end of the interchange where the human traffic isn't as high as the alighting and other berths
