    When (and why) was Princess Elizabeth Dr renamed Elizabeth Dr?

    There used to have an estate called Princess Elizabeth Park, between Elizabeth Dr and current Hillview estate. There were 24 blocks of lower storeys (Blk 1 to 24) in this estate while the neighbouring Hillview estate has 16 blocks (1 to 8 and 10 to 17, where is block 9?). Princess Elizabeth Park estate was demolished in the 90s.

    Roads in Princess Elizabeth Park estate were Philip Walk, Clarence Walk, Prince Charles Rise and Princess Ann Hill. All these roads disappeared with the demolition of the estate.
