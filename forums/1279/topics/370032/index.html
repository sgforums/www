    Here are some tips:

    For CB plate: you need to attach the " Form A" student namelist to register

    For PA/PC/PZ ( Excursion) Plate: Your IC , driving license & VL

    For PA/PC/PH ( Private Hire) Plate: You need to attach the workers' conveyance list

     

    Now for all buses to be registered , it must be a least Euro 4 , pls check the prices of buses from their various importers , COE [prices & insurance pakages.

    If you want to own a bus yourself , its best you get a china brand one , its cheap & if you take school children , get a CB plate one

    For a private bus (proper, ie not private hire), you need to have a registered entity.

    Anything can be a bus as long as it carries 8 or more people, a Toyota Hiace or Nissan Urvan can be a bus.

    Go down to LTA at Sin Ming or call 1800-2255582 for more information.
