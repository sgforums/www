    AMDEP had been very inconsistent with their fleets nowadays ever since they followed the BCM deployment, unlike KJDEP and WLDEP which still practices “perm fleets”.

    From what I notice, some weekends seems to have slightly more DDs then bendies running on 854. Sometimes very few DDs. Fleet is very inconsistent. On Sundays and PH, some of the bendies would run on 171/859 without fail.

    For the low capacity fleets on 854, it seems that there are more A22s running on 854 these days instead. The OC500LEs seems to be doing services like 980 lately.

    In fact 854 fleet had been doing quite fine with the WABs when AMDEP took over the B1 A95s from KJDEP back in Oct 2016. However things slowly went back to reality when the Woodlands services went under AMDEP due to BCM. It is all thanks to the Seletar package that Yishun feeders benefitted with the fleet upgrade and this landed 854 with all the spam old bendies affecting the WAB quota. Other services like 857 seems to be affeced too with more occurrences of old bendies these days. I am not sure about 169, 856 and 969. 963 seems unaffected at all.

    If I remembered correctly, 854 used to operate full WAB fleet at night. But ever since Yishun feeders got the upgrade, there are several old bendies running on 854 even at night.

    I hope AMDEP look into this matter. 854 WAB quota is really a no-go compared to every other SMRT trunk services.
