    SBS8689L

    Observations onboard this bus.

    1. Front doors seems to be mounted loosely.
    2. Rear doors open fine.
    3. Door panels are changed to matte surface, does not fit well with the rest of the interior.
    4. Door panels are made of cheap plastic and doesn't seem to be attached properly to the bus.
    5. As a result of that the panels shakes and rattles together with bodywork of the bus, very noisy when going over bumps.
    6. There is a small spot exposing the bodywork of the bus as a result of removing the old panel.
    7. Bus Stopping does not deactivate until either of the doors are closed.

    Overall impression: Cheaply made, just like the bodywork.
