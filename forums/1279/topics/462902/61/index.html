    Hi all!

    The link is updated pertaining to list of service guide cut off dates!

    Few interesting points in a nutshell:

    • Bedok racks are refilled with reprinted guides for 30,32,35/35M,40,197 after months of emptiness
    • SMRT has reprinted certain CCK guides for September this year, seems like their reprint dates has been confined to may and sep for each year based on trends since their re-implementation.
    • No guides released for 883 as of yet
    • A fellow collector dropped by Punggol GAS Office to ask for a copy of each individual service guide a few days back, office staff replied that they no longer have copies of it.
    Have fun collecting for those who do! Hoping for a new version of SBST guides to come soon, although chances are slim..
