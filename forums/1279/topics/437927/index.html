    SBS TRANSIT

    85X - 85M
    170X - 170M
    17A - 17M
    275 - 131M since no change to the bus fare
    123M additional bus stop along jln membina
    151/154 to serve Toa Payoh lor 6

    SMRT

    853(Sunday & Public Holiday route) - 853A

    925(Sunday & Public Holiday route) - 925A

    961(Sunday & Public Holiday route) - 961A

    Evening trip for express service 188E, 854E, 868

    Alternate service 950A to terminate at Larkin, if the Malaysian authority allow

    Alternate service 171A to serve Singapore Zoo in additional to its parent route between Yishun & Marina Centre.

    Conversion of 926 to trunk service.

    Intergration of bus services 825 & 927. In addition, 825 will extend to Ang Mo Kio to provide direct connection between Choa Chu Kang and Ang Mo Kio which will be faster that the current NSL. It will serve Ang Mo Kio Avenue 3/6, Yio Chu Kang Road, Lentor Estate, Yio Chu Kang Road, Upper Thomson Road, Mandai Road, Singapore Zoo and its original 927 route to Choa Chu Kang and vice versa. Alternate route 825A could cover the existing route of 825 to cater the passenger demand.

    (Below is my favorite since sometime i need a faster connection to the west)!
    New service from Yishun to Boon Lay. It will ply Yishun Ave 2/1, Mandai Avenue/Road, BKE, KJE, Choa Chu Kang Drive/Avenue 4/Way, KJE, PIE, Pioneer Road North, Boon Lay Way, Jurong West Street 64 and Vice Versa. It will enhance the connection between Yishun, Choa Chu Kang, Jurong West faster that the current NSL/EWL. Furthermore, it will provide a faster connection to NTU via Pioneer Road North for Yishun and Choa Chu Kang residents.

    Reintroduction of service 989. I suggest to reintroduce the direction connection between Choa Chu Kang and Changi Airport to shorten the journey at the exisiting NSL/EWL journey. It could enhance by starting the service from Bukit Batok and proceed to Choa Chu Kand and Bukit Panjang before continue its journey to Changi Airport

    Changes to allow service 966 and 985 to serve Toa Payoh Lorong 6 to provide a faster direction from Woodlands, Bukit Panjang, Bukit Batok, Choa Chu Kang beside the NSL.
