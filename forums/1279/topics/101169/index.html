    To the bus company and their workshops, a bus is generic, and not die die must be fixed to a particular service. Midi buses will not be a good substitute when they have to cover for other services. Besides, even low demand routes have their peak hours.

    Anyway.. since their fleet is majority of the same brand, operating multiple makes means more $$ tied up in spares inventory. Also.. workshop staff must then be trained to service diff makes.
