    Unhappy over pay revisions, 22 foreign bus captains from Bedok and Jurong East have resigned within a month. According to insider, local bus captains had a pay raise of $225 while foreigners recieved $70 to $150 pay rise. Foreign drivers are unfamiliar with local roads and therefore face more stress. These factors and the lower wage increment caused them to tender their resignations.
