    Originally posted by FireIce:
    this morning i saw the orange wordings one........tt one can see leh....no problem........
    Those are of new technologies, like LEDs. The older flip dot signs are harder to make out the letters, like those on old Tibs buses. They use flourescent tubes that's (I think..) the yellow dots will reflect light so harder to see. The LEDs uses individual small bulbs to light up a certain "dot" and these "dots" will make up the digits or letters and hence less reflection and more precise.

    I don't think the service 80 Superbus you saw is using the old flip dot technology but the colour is indeed hard to blend, unlike yellow or orange with black backgrounds.
