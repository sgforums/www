    I prefer bodywork which are able to meet my requirements, ranked in priority (1 means most important to me & so on) as below :

    1. Does NOT emit any squeaking, cracking & rattling noises !

    1. High (i.e. car-like) standard of finishing quality with attention to detail
    meaning external bodywork with visible riveting is OUT !! Use of hand brush
    to paint the bodywork also OUT !

    2. Built to same philiosphy & focus etc. as the maker of the bus-chassi, i.e. if in
    the case of not an 'original bus' but bus-chassi only. (Of course in the case of
    bus/coach with original chassi makers 'officially approved' bodywork this
    para. then not applicable as then the standards are deemed met !)

    2. Good functional & practical designs

    3. Timeless but modern & relevant styling

    Bodywork which best meet my requirements thus like M-B original Mannheim built bodies, Kassbohrer Setras etc. along such class.
