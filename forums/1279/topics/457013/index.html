    13 Aug 12, 22:22

    17 easily has the highest demand at Pasir Ris Interchange during the evening peak and at night, on par with 88 and 359. Even with a frequency that is better than services like 12 and 21, the queue for 17 can get really long. So yes, 17 definitely deserves the DDs. Demand from students on school days is strong as well, and the Bedok Interchange - Bedok North Ave 4 sector is obviously high in demand, or why else would there be 17A? Really, I'm surprised to hear you question whether 17 deserves DDs. It functions as an important and indispensable feeder service at both of its interchanges and as a frequent rider of 17, I daresay it deserves an even better frequency than what it has now - long queues are seen at Pasir Ris Interchange during off-peak hours, especially at night, when all the S-shift buses have gone off service.
