    SBS7207B - (SBS7198T to SBS7296T) - You can board them at Boon Lay Bus Interchange.

    SBS9889U - Typically serves 166, between Ang Mo Kio and Clementi Bus Interchange.

    SBS Sunlong - On Service 502, between Pioneer and Marina Bay Sands

    SBS Higer bus - cannot board, unable to take pic, locked up near NTU, able to see the bus while on double decker Service 179.

    SMRT Man - On Service 963, between Woodlands and Harbourfront Int

    SMRT Zhongtong - On Service 941/945 - 430pm - 630pm on weekdays should be able to board from Bukit Batok Interchange.

    SMRT Volgren Lance - On Service 961, between Woodlands Int and Geylang Lorong 1. Only one bus.

    SMRT Volgren Hino - On Service 927 - between Choa Chu Kang Int and Singapore Zoo.

    Scania L113CRL - Woodlands Bus Int (Service 178 , 187 (both woodlands int & boon lay int and many more...)

    Its safe to assume that the sun here will be in a straight line from east to west (and not tilted northwardly or southwardly) since we're near the equator.

    As for bukit batok, the exit faces northeast with the main road (bukit batok central) perpendicular running northwest and southeast. For woodlands, its an underground interchange and exits are woodlands ave 3 and 7 (opposite sides). If you look at maps by google or gothere.sg, its safe to assume that the sun will come from the left of the screen or right of the screen with slight tilt.

    Hope it helps, cheers!
