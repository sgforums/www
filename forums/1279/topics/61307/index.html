    The route details is as follow:

    And goes back to Marine Parade Rd,Still Rd South, Still Rd, Jln Eunos, Jln Kolam Ayer,Jln Toa Payoh, Whitley Rd, PIE, BKE, Woodlands Ave 3, Woodlands Reg Int.
