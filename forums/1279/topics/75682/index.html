    27xx buses are Volvo B10M Mk4 DM3500. They're found in some CBS routes (refer to Fantasy Bus Routes thread)

    27xx are found on services like 27 66 & 105. They're mostly based in SLBP and HGDEP if i'm not wrong.

    Just some info for u..
    SLBP - Soon Lee Bus Park controls Boon Lay (BNL as to what i call it) and Jurong East (JUR for this) services.

    HGDEP - Hougang Depot control Eunos (EUN) and all north east interchanges (PUN SEK HGN) except Serangoon (SGN).

    Volvo B10TL Super Olympian are SBST's newest buses. Rego from SBS 9801 Y - SBS 9849 K. They came into service in Feb/Mar 2003. They're currently deployed on 21 25 28 31 56 70 72 76 133 143 161 168.

    Newest single deckers are the 28xx Mk5s (DM3500) Rego from SBS 2800 X - SBS 2838 M
    They're deployed on 15 162 163 etc.. they're based in HGDEP and BNDEP

    BNDEP - Bedok North Depot controls all eastern interchanges.
