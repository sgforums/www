    Habits (and Volgrens)

    TIB 1025 E to 1062 Y - Konvekta, with a control panel beside EDS control. Can control 3 fan speeds with a tiny button beside the LCD (not sure what purpose)

    TIB 1063 U to 1092 K - Hispacold, controlled by only 2 switches rearmost the driver's side panel. Has total of 4 cooling units (split system) compared to 6 in all other 0405Gs

    TIB 1093 H to 1247 G - Hispacold, same as above. However cooling units are back to 6.

    Exceptions are the use of Hispacold control panel to offer better climate control that is on trial since last year, in addition to 1236 M, 1167 D and 1243 S (Sv 856) has them too.

    TIB 488 J also has the Konvekta control, where the internal cooling units were replaced by a single unit on top of the bus.
