    Rego = Plate Number, say SBS 9532 Z

    Cameo = A bus that is permanent on one service, but does another service, like SBS 9578 S is a permanent bus of Sv 81, but yesterday, it operated Sv 8

    Desto = Destination Plates, the number plate in front of the bus, the road plate on the side, and the number plate on the back.
