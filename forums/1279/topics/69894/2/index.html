    Governing fuel intake is NOT the same as governing engine revs. The former involves cutting off fuel flow to the engine when it reaches a particular speed, forcing the engine to gradually shut down. The latter involves limiting the engine to a particular rev, and when it hits that value, will remain there and not increase. They are VASTLY different things altogether.
