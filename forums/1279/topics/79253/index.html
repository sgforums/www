    14 May 04, 13:41
    Mk4 DM3500s - Excellent buses, good aircon, good speed, good interior pity SBS only bought about 130 of these buses.

    Mk4 Striders - Good buses, as the Mk4s all are, but interior worse than the DM3500s. Why do these look so much like the DM3500s when the bodies are made by different companies?

    Mk4 DMs - Nothing special, better than Mk3s though. Interior can do with some improvement though.

    Mk3s - Underpowered and sluggish, the unrefurbished ones really suck, but there are some exceptions.

    Volvo Olympians - Good buses, but I still prefer LOs.

    Leyland Olympians - I try to avoid the lower decks, very squashed compared to the spacious VOs, but the LOs are quite quiet on the upper deck and the engine sound is very good.

    Super Olympians - Excellent aircon, excellent interior but slow. Its only noisy on lower deck and outside, when on the upper deck its super quiet. Only 50 of them on Singapore roads, wish SBS bought more.

    Tridents - Good power, quiet, and good interior, pity only 20 of them on Singapore roads and they're only based at HGDEP.

    Scanias - The NAC and CAC buses are quite ok, but I try to avoid the OACs for as much as I can and the Scanias for long rides. Any bus is bearable for a short hop.

    SBS O405s - Very noisy and the interior is not good, try to avoid these during long rides.

    SMRTbus O405s - Ok bus, very noisy but comfort is ok, good power.

    Habits - Nice buses, good seats and interior, has a lot of power like all the SMRTbus buses.

    8th>Volvo B10M MK4 DM3500

    Less occurances of the fanbelt problem that made the Striders extremely noisy. But it remains to be seen when the batch gets older.

    23rd>MB 0405 Hispano & CAC

    Extremely worthwhile buses with longlasting reliability and power. However design wise the windows could have better sealed to prevent dirt and mould from entering the gaps.

    24th>Scania L113CRL Walter Alexander

    Offers great power. However it was plaqued initially because the aircon was too warm for good cooling. Nevertheless will be a valued asset for SMRTB.

    26th>Dennis Lance UMW DupleMetsec & UK DupleMetsec

    I'll take it as adding variety into SMRTB's stable of buses. Relatively powerful and excellent DM's design on the windows. However the aircon duct could be lowered to better cool the bus.

    30th>MB 0405G Hispano(box)

    The classic series, still running strong. The Konvekta aircon is excellent at cooling while being very quiet at top fan speed.

    32nd>MB 0405G Volgren
    33rd>MB 0405G Habit-Hispano

    Both aircons provided were too noisy. Otherwise both will be perfect buses. For the Volgrens the steering stability and braking are not as good as the Habits, probably due to the lower price.

    For Habits I like the multilayered glass windows that reduces fogging, and both plug in and out doors that leaves little gaps for warm air to leak in. However, ZF gearbox problems are affecting certain batches, which is unfortunate since many of them are very new.
