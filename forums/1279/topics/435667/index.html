    No one mentioned services 160 and 170?

    Service 170 had been using this desto since its NAC days. Even though 170 was officially converted to FAC, the same front desto and the side green/white desto remained in use thruout. When service 160 was reintroduced in 2004, the destos for 160 follow the same format with 170.

    Notice that services 91 and 191 are still using the old destos once in a while. Kind of nostalgic.

    Still remember those days when SBS used those front destos with destinations in the 90s for standard NAC buses, side desto green/white. Whereas for aircon buses, front desto with "AIR CON" was printed on the left side of the service no, side desto red/blue. It was only in 2000, SBS decided to standardalise all the destos to red/white for side, while front just show the service no (regardless of AC or NAC).
