    In fact, just Service 36 minus the Suntec and Marine Parade route. It used to use Rochor and Selegie Roads until Marina Centre was open.
    Service 390 was once a so-called express bus service from Changi Airport to Holland Road via Orchard in the 1980s, with full fleet Albion Vikings. Eventually became full AC fleet with OAC Scanias after the withdrawal of Albion Vikings. However this service was withdrawn in 1993, and service 16 was extended to Changi Airport from Marine Parade to cover for service 390.

    In 1995 a peak hour express service 16E was introduced between Changi Airport to Orchard. Somewhere in 1998 the service was renumbered to service 36. Further enhancements was made on service 36 to ply from Orchard to Changi Airport via ECP and Marine Parade areas, it became a full day service. Service 16 was then cutback to Marine Parade.

    Seems like passengers must have been complaining about the very long journey on service 16 from Orchard to Changi Airport after the withdrawal of service 390. SBS had no choice but to come out with this decision

    Difference between services 36 and service 390 is that service 36 goes by ECP from Marina Square / Suntec City, turns in to Marine Parade and then back to ECP again to Changi Airport. Whereas service 390 goes straight to Changi Airport from Marina Square in the past.

    Service 147 used to ply Victoria St, Kallang Rd and Lavender St (Lavender St, Crawford St and North Bridge Rd in the return direction). When service 147 was integrated with service 83 (Punggol - New Bridge Rd), service 147 was amended to ply Stamford Rd, Waterloo St, Middle Rd, Selegie Rd and Serangoon Rd (Jln Besar, Bencoolen St and Bras Basah Rd in the return direction).

    Perhaps to maintain the link between Victoria St / North Bridge Rd and the rest of service 147 route up to Clementi, and also to act as a supplementary service to service 147, FAC service 148 was introduced in June 1992 duplicating service 147 route between Clementi and Hill St / North Bridge Rd. Due to demand was not satisfactory, service 148 was withdrawn after 5 months, in Nov 1992. On the same day service 148 was withdrawn, service 196 was extended from Buona Vista to Clementi so that Clementi and Dover residents had another bus service to connect to Queensway and Jln Bukit Merah.
