    What destos do you like and loathe?

    Love:

    1. The TIBS electronic destos.....very informative, even if you have to stand there for a whole 2 mins to get thru all 5 desto scrolls (or "flashes" as they are known)

    2. The old school SBS destos which went as follows:

    (Destination A) (ROUTE NUMBER) (Destination B)

    similar to what appears on the Superbuses and the 2 axle Volvo Olympians.

    3. Oddball destos, such as:

    In Singapore:

    FERRY SERVICE (Ferry logo)
    PULAU TEKONG (seen upside down as well)

    In Sydney, Australia:

    L O S T ! ! !
    EMERGENCY! CALL 000
    SPECIAL 000 (Special bus, or what??)
    GETTING MARRIED
    SET DOWN ONLY
    B U S F U L L
    NOT IN SERVICE // WAIT FOR THE NEXT BUS

    // denotes desto scroll

    among other things.....

    Loathe:

    1. The fact that SBST still use plastic desto cards!!!!!! Even on the B10TL/Super Olympian and the B10BLE CNG!

    2. Anything that takes courtesy to the n-th degree:

    In Singapore:
    as per some of the TIBS buses on the side desto

    In Sydney, Australia:

    SORRY,
    NOT IN SERVICE why "SORRY"? It isn't like the operator will get sued for not apologising!
