  • TIB1018B
    17 Oct 06, 21:13
    At that time NAC Hinos were deployed mainly on Tibs services 5, 75, 176, 343 and 852.

    Service 5 was withdrawn and subsequently the NAC Hinos went to services 172 and 175 when Tibs took over from SBS in late 1999.

    Service 176 had its NAC Hino fleets withdrawn in 2000 when habits came in.

    Service 343 was renumbered to service 921 and the control then came under WLDEP, where 1 OAC Scania (TIB638T) and 1 NAC Nissan (TIB322D) were used.

    Services 75 and 852 operations were also subsequently transferred to WLDEP and AMDEP respectively, hence no more NAC Hinos was deployed.

    Ever since, the NAC Hinos continued to serve on services 172 and 175 till they were phased out in end 2001, where it was replaced with OAC DAFs.
