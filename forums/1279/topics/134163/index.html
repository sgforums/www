    Originally posted by muziq bus:
    Also, wat buses used to serve bb during 1985 to 1999?
    pls check out history of bus services numbers etc under Newcomers' Guide next time for ur research.

    used to:
    SERVICE 122 (St Michael's - Bt Batok):
    Bt Batok Central, Bt Batok East Ave 2, Bt Batok East Ave 6, Jln Jurong Kechil, Bt Timah Link, Jln Anak Bukit, Clementi Rd, C'wealth Ave West, C'wealth Ave, Queensway, Jln Bt Merah, Kg Bahru Rd, Eu Tong Sen St, Hill St, Victoria St, Kallang Rd, Lavender St, Balestier Rd, Whampoa Dr, Jln Bahagia & Whampoa Rd.
    And return via Whampoa Rd, Jln Bahagia, Kim Keat Rd, Balestier Rd, Jln Besar, Bencoolen St, Bras Basah Rd, North Bridge Rd, South Bridge Rd, Upp Cross St, New Bridge Rd, Kg Bahru Rd, Jln Bukit Merah, Queensway, C'wealth Ave, C'wealth Ave West, Clementi Rd, Upp Bt Timah Rd, Jln Jurong Kechil, Bt Batok East Ave 6, Bt Batok East Ave 2 & Bt Batok Central
    NOTE: Withdrawn in 1994.

    SERVICE 126 (St Michael's - New Bridge Rd)

    SERVICE 175 (Bt Batok - Jurong West)
    Bt Batok Interchange, Bt Batok Central, Bt Batok West Ave 3, Bt Batok Rd, Jurong Rd, PIE, Upp Jurong Rd, Jurong West St 72, Ave 5, Pioneer Rd Nth, Jurong West St 91 & 92 (loop).
    NOTE: Original route from Tuas (near Jln Ahmad Ibrahim) to Prince Edward. Tuas Terminal was later moved to Pioneer Rd and Prince Edward to Shenton Way. Shortened to becaome Tuas - Bt Batok in 1988. On 7th Jul 1990, made to start from Bt Batok and loop at Jurong West St. 91. Withdrawn in 1991. Re-introduced in 1993, loop service plied between Lim Chu Kang terminal and Upp Bt Timah Rd. Amended to ply Sungei Tengah Rd, Brickland Rd and Choa Chu Kang Ave 3, when parts of Choa Chu Kang Rd was closed in stages.

    SERVICE 179 (Bt Batok - New Bridge Rd)
    NOTE: At first, it was from Lim Chu Kang - Bt Timah. Withdrawn in 1983. Re-introduced in 1986 from Bt Batok - New Bridge Rd. Withdrawn again in 1990. Re-introduced in 1993, loop service which plied between Choa Chu Kang interchange and Fajar Rd. No route change. Handed to tibs in 1995. Withdrawn in Dec 1999. Re-introduced again in Jan 2001, loop service which plied between Boon Lay interchange and NTU. 179 is the most re-used number.

    SERVICE 189 (Bt Batok - Marina Centre)
    NOTE: Originally was from C'wealth Ave (Buona Vista) - Empress Pl. Extended to Bt Batok in 1985. Plied between Bt Batok Int and Marina Centre Ter. Merged with service 366 and shortened to loop at Clementi West St 2 in 1990. Extended to West Coast Ferry terminal in 1995. Amended to ply PIE and Clementi Ave 6 instead of Toh Tuck Ave and C'wealth Ave West in 1996. Handed to tibs in June 2000. Amended to Kent Ridge terminal in Aug 2001.

    SERVICE 366 (Bt Batok - Bt Batok Street 23):
    [1985] Bt Batok Terminal (near Blk 122?), (Bt Batok West Ave 3), Bt Batok Central, Bt Batok Ave 1, East Ave 6, St 23, Bt Batok Depot, Bt Batok St 23, East Ave 6, Ave 1, Bt Batok Central, (Bt Batok West Ave 3) and Bt Batok Terminal

    SERVICE SS3 (Clementi - Bt Batok West Ave 7 (Loop)):
    [1988] Clementi Interchange, (Clementi Ave 3, Commonwealth Ave West, Toh Tuck Ave, Bt Batok East Ave 3), Bt Batok East Ave 2, East Ave 5, West Ave 5, West Ave 7, West Ave 4, West Ave 2, West Ave 5, East Ave 5, East Ave 2, (Bt Batok East Ave 3, Toh Tuck Ave, Commonwealth Ave West, Clementi Ave 3) and Clementi Interchange.
    NOTE: Introduced in 1986-87. Withdrawn in 1990.
