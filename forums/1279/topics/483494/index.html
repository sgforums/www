    Anyway, my view on LTA's BSRF trial... I simply don't understand why they even need to fine SBS Transit and SMRT if a bus is just 1 minute late, when it's due to traffic conditons, which one cannot control... They are just getting money from fining the two PTOs, because they just want a perfect public transport system. I mean, I feel that both PTOs have dropped in maintenace standards recently, because of BSEP which = More buses and routes to manage. They simply don't have enough time to maintain their buses properly nowadays.

    Well, I know that SBS Transit's HGDEP buses are starting to lose out to its AMDEP buses, so I can agree on that point... As we all know, SBS Transit has timetables for their bus services, which their buses may not adhere very closely to. But, at least they still make the effort to do these timetables out on their website (most) and try to make sure their buses are on time at bus stops or when departing bus interchanges. Never mind about a few mins of delay or being earlier, but at least I can see they are trying to ensure they give good services. That's why SBS Transit is a much more reliable and efficient PTO than SMRT.
