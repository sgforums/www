    May not be 100% accurate, but from observations

    190 / 985 from AMDEP uses SLE / Mandai Road / BKE / KJE to get to Choa Chu Kang Int.

    75, 176, 177, 180, 184, 700, 970, 975 uses Jelebu Road then Bukit Panjang Road then Woodlands Road then turns into KJDEP on return leg after finishing their duties.

    BBDEP buses (77, 106, 189, 941, 945, 947) after refuelling at KJDEP will cut through Sungei Kadut to Choa Chu Kang Way, Bukit Batok Road, Bukit Batok West Ave 2, Bukit Batok Central then to Bukit Batok Interchange.

    CCK services (67, 172, 188, 190, 300, 302, 307) believed to use the same route but turns in at Choa Chu Kang Avenue 4 along Choa Chu Kang Way.

    Some Off service buses that end its trip on a Bt Panjang service may also use KJE back to KJDEP.

    Its off service route used by the buses are either (1) or (2):
    (1) Bt Panjang Int > Bt Panjang Rd (BKE direction) > BKE > KJE > Woodlands Rd > Kranji Rd > KJDEP.
    (2) Bt Panjang Int > Bt Panjang Rd (CCK Rd direction) > Woodlands Rd > Kranji Rd > KJDEP

    For CCK services - the off service route used by the buses are either (1) or (2):
    (1) CCK Int > CCK Ave 4 > CCK Way > Sg Kadut Dr > Kranji Way > Kranji Rd > KJDEP
    (2) CCK Int > CCK Ave 4 > CCK Dr > KJE > Woodlands Rd > Kranji Rd > KJDEP

    BOLD denots actual route taken

    Based on my observations which might not be accurate:-

    Sengkang services under HGDEP: Sengkang Sq, Compassvale Rd, Sengkang East Ave, Punggol Rd, Hougang Ave 10, 8, 7, Defu Ave 1 and straight to HGDEP. Some drivers take Upp Serangoon Rd and Sengkang East Dr to report from HGDEP to Sengkang interchange.

    Service 86 from Sengkang Interchange return to AMDEP via Sengkang East Rd, TPE, YCK Road and back to AMDEP.

    Service 136 from Punggol Interchange return to AMDEP via Punggol Way, TPE, YCK Road and back to AMDEP.

    Hougang services under HGDEP: Hougang Central, Ave 5, 7, Defu Ave 1 and back to HGDEP.
