    Hengtong Bus Debut in Singapore and be Well Positioned for Oversea Market
    Author:China Auto & Parts Source: China Auto & Parts Time:2012-4-5 13:52:13


    On March 17th 2012, the release conference of Hengtong Bus hosted by SCA was held in Singapore. About 300 guests attended the conference and cut the ribbon for the new models, including Chen Shaoquan, general manager of SCA, Shen Jinlong, manager of the machinery department of ITOCHU Singapore Pte. Ltd, Hu Yu, general manager of Huaguan International Industrial Equipment (CCIE), Tang Qiandong, vice president and party secretary of Hengtong Bus, general engineer Wan Chang, regional client representatives from Singapore and friends from media such as Singapore Lianhe Zaobao and Straits Daily. This conference demonstrates that Hengtong is officially entering the high-end market overseas, which makes Hengtong one of the few manufacturers that are selling domestically made buses to the market of developed countries.

    Entering the Singapore market with SCA

    SCA is one of the top 50 enterprises in Singapore, which is generally acknowledged as the most trustworthy coach and bust manufacturer by the local industry. SCA is dedicated in R&D and innovation, holding the palm in designing and manufacturing ordinary buses and sightseeing buses for many years.

    Hengtong was set up in 1939, which is one of the largest bus manufacturers in China. It is also one of the top ten bus makers with an annual production of 10,000 large buses. It has been constantly improving its professional abilities in manufacturing buses. Hengtong is in the leading position in the Chinese bus industry in terms of R&D, manufacturing and management. Its products are sold to 23 countries and regions such as Thailand, Peru, Syria and Malawi. It enjoys high brand recognition and reputation all over the world.

    The official release of Hengtong’s buses in Singapore makes the respective advantages of Hengtong and SCA complementary to each other, demonstrating the first achievement made by Hengtong and SCA cooperating with each other. It is also the beginning of the new journey Hengtong is making towards the Singapore bus market.

    Customization makes perfect

    Since the study group led by Chen Shaoquan, general manager of SCA paid a visit to Hengtong in August 2010, Hengtong has been making deep analysis and research on the situations such as road, passenger flow and tourism of the Singapore market with more than a year’s communication and exchange with SCA. After the targeted R&D of new models, Hengtong suggests SCA adopt CKZ6920CHB4 of its Xianglong family which is feasible and suitable for the actual conditions of Singapore.

    In September 2011, SCA signed a purchase agreement of 20 CKZ6920CHB4 with Hengtong. The released new model on the conference is the first high-quality bus in the agreement, which is customized according to the actual situation of Singapore by Hengtong. The body is elegantly and beautifully designed. The framework and skin of the body are made of two-sided galvanized materials, which greatly improve the corrosion resistant ability of the body to combat the humid oceanic climate in Singapore. The professional chassis is matched with the imported Cummins Euro IV diesel engine, which greatly reduces the operational cost due to its strong carrying capacity and features of energy conservation and environment protection. The seats are designed according to the body features of the Singaporeans, of which the comfortableness is improved. The luggage rack, LED TV and DVD equipped are simple and practical, forming a more comfortable environment for passengers.

    After the release conference, people attended the conference paid a visit to Hengtong Bus accompanied by Mr. Tang. They were giving high appreciation to the perfect shape, human-oriented design and delicate craft of the new model. Attendees of the conference were also very interested in the new model, and many of them even registered right at the conference to give a test try.

    At last, Mr. Tang said that Hengtong, as the globally recognized expert in public transportation solution, will set up after-sale service stations with SCA in Singapore under the principle of taking social responsibilities and providing perfect services for customers during and after purchase. He believes there will be more and more Hengtong buses running on the roads of Singapore, providing safe, environment friendly, comfortable and convenient services for the Singaporean people.
