    I always have this qn. Some bus routes are high in demand while some are low. However, it is during NDP cover that this qn dawns on me.

    My frenz went home on one day from Clarke Quay to his hse at Sengkang near Rivervale Drive.
    There are two best options that he could take to reach his destinations.
    1. Take MRT from Clarke Quay and transfer to LRT to his hse.
    2. Take 80 at the bus stop near Clarke Quay to his hse.

    In the end, he chose option 2 instead of option 1. I was rather surprised. The reason that he gave is that option 2 was though long, was a direct route to his hse. He could have a nice nap all the way.

    Hence the qn dawns on me.
    Suppose u guys are going somewhere using a public transport excluding taxi,
    will you
    1. Or take the fastest route to that destination.

    2. If possible, take the most direct route to that destination (meaning that u don't have to walk much throughout the journey) but it may not possibly be the shortest route nor fastest route.
