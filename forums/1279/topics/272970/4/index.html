    3. Toa Payoh East (Blk 25)
    4. Toa Payoh Lor 8 (Toa Payoh Polyclinic)
    5. Toa Payoh Lor 8 (Opp Blk 227)

    3. Toa Payoh Lor 8 (Blk 210)
