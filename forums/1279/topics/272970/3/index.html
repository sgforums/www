    Your service 507 sounds vague as you do not really specify which bus stops or roads that it will pass by.

    Regarding about your new service 41, I have calculated the fare stages with comparison with other services. It has roughly around 20.5 - 21 stages.
    Out of 20.5- 21 stages, there are 13 stages whereby this service duplicates with 851 from Yishun to Marymount Rd.

    The route along Lor 8 Toa Payoh is rather well planned as you have utilised that slip road onto Braddell Rd (where 238 loops at ) for one direction while using the junction between Lor 8 Toa Payoh and Lor 6 Toa Payoh at the another.

    I have some recommendations for this service in order for it not to be duplicated with 851 by a lot.
    1. This service can turn at AMK Ave 6 to AMK Ave 3 to terminate AMK interchange. First, it provides a direct link from Lor 8 Toa Payoh to AMK.
    2. Or if you don't like the first suggestion, here is another. If it goes to Yishun, I suggest this service to ply the east side of Yishun Ring Rd following 811 then 804 route. After that, turn back into Yishun Ave 2 and to Yishun Int after that.

    I guess probably Lor 4 Toa Payoh is a central road to divide Toa Payoh town and thus the reason why many trunk services ply there.
    I will also suggest services that goes in a westward direction after plying Lor 6 Toa Payoh to Braddell Rd to reroute so as to utilise Lor 8 Toa Payoh. Not sure whether DDs can be plied there. Services like 57, 88, 157, 163 can be rerouted.
