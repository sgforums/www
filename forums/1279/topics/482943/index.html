    Contractors Invited to Submit Bids to Build New Bus Terminal

    The Land Transport Authority (LTA) will build a new bus terminal near Changi Business Park, off Changi South Avenue 1 to support the Bus Service Enhancement Programme (BSEP).  

    Expected to be completed by second quarter 2015, the new terminal will incorporate parking bays for 12 buses, an office for the bus operator, a canteen, and a rest area for bus drivers. When completed, the bus terminal will be handed over to SBS Transit Ltd for outfitting and preparatory works before operation commences.  

    LTA is calling a tender to invite contractors to submit their cost proposals to build the new bus terminal.
