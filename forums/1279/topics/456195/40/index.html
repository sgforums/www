    • Which gearbox do you prefer for Wrights?
    • 1. Voith DIWA
    • 2. ZF Transmission
    Actually both have it's advantages and disadvantages.

    The Advantages and disadvantages for Voith DIWA are stated below:
    Advantages: Engine not so loud when on the upper deck, Moves faster abit sometimes and doesn't "tick" frequently for 70% of the buses
    Disadvantages: Sometimes is very jerky and when moving down from upper deck to lower deck will fall down(on SBS3081Y)

    The Advantages and disadvantages for ZF Transmission are stated below:
    Advantages: Moves faster, Not jerky at all
    Disadvantages: Loud engine when on upper deck, "ticks" more frequently
