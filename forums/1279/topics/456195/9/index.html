    22 May 1998

    HOUGANG CENTRAL AND TOA PAYOH INTERCHANGES WILL BE MOVING

     

    • Hougang Central Interchange - new interchange starts operations on Sunday, 24 May 1998

     

    To make way for the construction of the Hougang MRT Station, the Hougang Central Bus Interchange will be shifting to its new location along Upper Serangoon Road, next to Hougang Plaza. This temporary interchange will start operations on Sunday, 24 May 1998.

    Due to space constraints, only 6 services (Services 84, 97, 111, 153, 165 and 325) will use the new interchange. The remaining 4 services (Services 74, 147, 501 and 502) will not call at the new interchange but will have boarding and alighting activities done near to the new interchange. The existing interchange will become a terminal, with no boarding and alighting activities. 

    • Toa Payoh Interchange - new interchange starts operations on Sunday, 31 May 1998.

    HDB will be constucting their new headquarters at the existing location of Toa Payoh Bus Interchange. This new HDB Centre will also house a modern bus interchange.

    To facilitate the construction of the HDB Centre, the existing Toa Payoh Interchange will move to its new temporary location across Toa Payoh Lorong 6, beside the Toa Payoh Town Park. The temporary bus interchange wll start operations from Sunday, 31 May 1998.
