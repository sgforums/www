    29 July 2005 - Residents of Bishan, Toa Payoh, Bukit Merah, Serangoon, Bedok, Tampines and Pasir Ris New Towns have good reason to cheer. Commencing Sunday, 31 July 2005, they will enjoy greater convenience with new links to various destinations with the introduction of SBS Transit Services 57 and 58.

    SBS Transit Service 57

    SBS Transit Service 57 operates between Bishan and Bukit Merah. It will provide residents of Bishan, Bukit Merah and Toa Payoh with the connections as follows:

    • direct bus links between Bishan, Toa Payoh and Bukit Timah Road / Dunearn Road (between Whitley Road and Serangoon Road), Shenton Way and Bukit Merah,
    • direct link between Depot Road and Alexandra Road / HarbourFront Station.
    • new direct link between Bishan and Lorong 6 Toa Payoh
    • new direct link between Depot Road and Shenton Way / Cecil Street, Bukit Timah Road (between Whitley Road and Serangoon Road), Toa Payoh, Bishan

    The service operates daily with the first bus and last bus departing from Bishan Bus Interchange at 6.00am and 11.30pm respectively, and from Bukit Merah Bus Interchange at 5.45am and 12 midnight respectively.

    SBS Transit Service 58

    SBS Transit Service 58 operates between Bishan and Pasir Ris. It will provide residents of Bishan, Serangoon, Bedok, Tampines and Pasir Ris with the connections as follows:

    • direct bus links between Bishan, Serangoon, Bedok, Tampines and Pasir Ris
    • direct bus link between Serangoon Interchange / Station and Eunos Link for easy transfer between buses and NEL
    • In addition, it will also provide Ubi Avenue 3 with a bus service and serve the Tampines North Wafer Fab Park and factories along Tampines Avenue 10.

    The service operates daily with the first bus and last bus departing from Bishan and Pasir Ris Bus Interchanges at 5.30am and 12 midnight respectively.
