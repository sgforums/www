It also bears a ''Quitline'' ad, not the official one, with both sides skyliners, rear skyliner and full body ad except for the rear and front. I used it to tell my father to stop smoking.     Until I was 12, I began to rampage Google when I saw a KUB on 325.

    Then I saw ''SgWiki'' and ''SgForums'', providing informations on buses and chatting about buses respectively.

    But NONE, I mean NONE of my classmates are interested in Buses in anyways as I see.
