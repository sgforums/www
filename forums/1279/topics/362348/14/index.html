    171 has specific waves of commuter patterns from what I observe.

    Pax volume increases from Yishun  BPJ, city bound. When a number of the passengers alight at BPJ the bus gets relatively empty again, until about somewhere near Bt Timah when city-bound commuters utilise 171 again.

    Orchard  BPJ, during both off-peak and peak, the pax volume can get pretty high also. Lots of commuters alight at BPJ Plaza (and board, Yishun-bound).

    Seems like the passenger crowds direct mostly to BPJ. Not surprising to see bunchings during peak hours on both directions.
