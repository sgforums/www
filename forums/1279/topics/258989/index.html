    see a few posts above your post..

    downroute = certain bus will start journey half way or at certain bus stops to pick up passengers instead of starting from interchange/terminal..

    For example, full route of SV 268 = AMK Depot to AMK Ind Park II
    Down route of SV 268 during morning peak hours, some SV 268 will start their trips half way at AMK Ave 3 (Big Mac Centre bus stop, instead of AMK DEpot) to pick up passengers.

    So if u suddenly see one empty bus arriving at a bus stop during peak hours and started to pick up passengers, usually these are downroute.
