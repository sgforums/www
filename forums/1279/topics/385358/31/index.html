    Shall take this chance to remember those Mk3s that have recently been deregistered on 24 Feb 2012. 

    My beloved Service 39 Mk3, SBS752P, stayed on 39 since I came into this bus-spotting hobby in 2005. All these years, did not left 39 for another perm, serving the Tampines-Yishun dutifully so many years, seldom cameoing around.
    While other Mk3s such as 726R, 852J came to perm 39 shortly before leaving for elsewhere, 752P stayed all along.
    Even as the former-BN 39 DM3500s of 2828S, 2829P, 2830J, 2832D left for BBDEP, 752P still remained.
    Even as the newer KUBs came into 39, 752P stayed in 39's perm fleet and survived not being kicked out.

    Its mileage and wear & tear might be the higest since it practically did the expressway sector almost everyday, but at least it stayed till the very last day of its lifespan when I saw it still doing its loyal perm Service 39 yesterday.
    Its condition was pretty worn out when I took it last week, but at least it didn't break down or catch fire before it was due to go. 
    So thank you for your services 752P, hope the new permer that replaces you would be as long-lasting too. 

    Next to go is 771J which is still on 39 but came over from BN 6 and other perm services.
