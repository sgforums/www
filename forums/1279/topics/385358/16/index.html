    SINGAPORE: Minister for Transport and Second Minister for Foreign Affairs Mr Lui Tuck Yew, will be leading the Singapore delegation to two meetings on transport and energy by the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) in San Francisco.

    The Joint APEC Transportation and Energy Ministerial Conference and the 7th APEC Transportation Ministerial Meeting take place from September 12 to 14.

    The APEC Transportation and Energy Ministerial Conference will provide a forum for APEC transport and energy ministers to consider initiatives to steer the APEC region towards more energy-efficient, sustainable, and low-carbon transport systems.

    At the 7th APEC Transportation Ministerial Meeting, the APEC transport ministers will discuss ways to chart policies to foster closer co-operation and enhance development in the transport sector.

    The meetings will also explore measures to incorporate the concept of sustainable development in transport systems.

    Mr Lui will be accompanied by officials from the Ministry of Transport, the Civil Aviation Authority of Singapore, the Land Transport Authority, and the Energy Market Authority.
