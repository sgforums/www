    they need not be empty, if there are passengers wanting to board.

    Service 98 covers the full route from Jurong East Interchange via Jurong East Ave 1, to Lakeside Station, into Taman Jurong Estate down to Jurong Port Rd, to Jurong Port area and loop back via the same route in a vice-versa format.

    Service 98A covers 3quarter of the route from Jurong East Interchange via Jurong East Ave 1 to Lakeside Station and down to Taman Jurong Estate...it then loops at the end of Taman Jurong Estate via Yung Ho Rd, Corporation Rd, AYE and back to Yung Ho Rd and has 2 additional stops (AYE and Sheng Shiong) that Service 98 does not cover..

    If you are going to Jurong East Ave 1, Jurong West St 51, Lakeside Station and Taman Jurong Estate, you can take either Service 98 or Service 98A

    If you are going to Jurong Port, Jurong Pier and Fishery Port Rd area, you can only take Service 98.

    If you are going to the Sheng Shiong Hypermart, u can only take Service 98A.

    To differentiate Service 98 and Service 98A, look at the bus..buses that are plying the Service 98A route will have a "A" suffix on the desto.

    The buses alternate...one bus 98, then next one is 98A, then 98 then 98A etc. between 930 - 1630 and 2000 - 2400...

    Service 98 operates between 0530 - 2400.
    Service 98A operates between 0930 - 0030.

    Service 98 and Service 98A is a FULLY AIRCON service.
