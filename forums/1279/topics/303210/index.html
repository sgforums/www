    Now got new red with white plates (confusing for "new people/those not updated people" but regulars mostly know what they meant already (Refer to the meaning in my previous post).

    On the other hand, some 60A buses will put the 60A normal square desto at the front of the bus.

    But as to why 60 have to serve this way is due to historical reasons. 60 used to be feeders 226 and 385 until they merged to form service 60. Back then, the service 60 did not touch the bedok reservoir wet market + Eunos CC that part in the direction towards bedok. Therefore, SBST introduced SWT 60A from eunos int to that part of bedok reservoir road at about 1 hr per bus (Not restricted to peak hrs back then). 60A was = Feeder 385 in terms of its roue. However, people were unhappy about 60 not serving the sector that 385 used to serve and finally on 7 November 2004, sv60 was 'enhanced' to serve that stretch of road in the direction towards Bedok too. From then on, 60A gradually became a PM Peak SWT.
