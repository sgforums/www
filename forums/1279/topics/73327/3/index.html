    10 May 2004

    SMRT BRAND INTEGRATION LAUNCH

    Leveraging on SMRT's strong brand name, all companies under the SMRT Group will adopt the SMRT name, logo and corporate colours from 10 May 2004. With the alignment, the names of our business units will be changed as follows:

    Singapore MRT Ltd SMRT Trains Ltd
    Singapore LRT Pte Ltd SMRT Light Rail Pte Ltd
    TIBS Holdings Ltd SMRT Road Holdings Ltd
    Trans-Island Bus Services Ltd SMRT Buses Ltd
    TIBS Taxis Pte Ltd SMRT Taxis Pte Ltd
    TIBS Motors Pte Ltd SMRT Automotive Services Pte Ltd

    Now, our customers will be able to see us as one company that facilitates seamless travel. Prior to this, we introduced new staff uniforms as a visible manifestation of our 'oneness' in January this year. With our one look and identity, we also assure our customers of continued consistent service and quality anytime, all the time.

    There will be minimal changes to the appearance of the trains. For instance, the SMRT logo will be applied on the front detrainment doors to enhance visibility of the SMRT brand name. In addition, the existing turquoise green strip on the SMRT Light Rail trains will be replaced with a red strip to reflect the SMRT corporate colours.

    For buses, besides replacing existing logo with the SMRT logo, the yellow and orange strips on the buses will be replaced with a red strip. Taxis will retain the smart off-white colour with a change to adopt the SMRT logo.

    All the livery changes are kept simple to ensure cost effectiveness.
    In order not to cause inconvenience to our taxi hirers or disrupt bus operations, the complete livery change for taxis and buses will be phased over one and two years respectively.

    To see visuals, please click here.

    21 May 2004

    WITHDRAWAL OF NIGHTRIDER SERVICES 1, 5 AND 7
    FOR SUNDAYS - THURSDAYS

    With effect from Sunday 23 May 2004, SMRT Buses will be withdrawing NightRider services 1, 5 and 7 from Sundays to Thursdays, in view of weak demand. However, these services will continue to operate on Fridays, Saturdays, and the eve of public holidays.

    Schedules for all other NightRider services remain unchanged, that is, they will continue to operate on Fridays, Saturdays and eve of public holidays.

    NightRider Services (11.30pm to 4.30am the next morning)

    NR1 Marina Centre to Ang Mo Kio, Bishan and Yishun
    NR2 Marina Centre to Sembawang via Woodlands
    NR3 North Canal Road to Orchard and Choa Chu Kang
    NR5 Marina Centre to Jurong West
    NR6 Marina Centre to Sengkang
    NR7 Marina Centre to Tampines and Pasir Ris
    NR8 Marina Centre to Bukit Batok

    The public can call the SMRT Information Centre at 1800-3368900 for more information.
