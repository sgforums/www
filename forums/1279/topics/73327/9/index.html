    BUS SERVICE

    To Singapore River HongBao - Extension of operating hours and introduction of shortworking trips for CityBuzz Service C1

    To make it easier for the public to make their way to and from the River Hongbao, shortworking trips will be introduced on CityBuzz Service C1 to operate between Marina Centre and North Bridge Road (City Hall Station). The operating hours will also be extended from 10.10 pm to 12.00 midnight for the period from 7 to 23 February 2005.

    All the CityBuzz services, C1, C2 and C3, call at Marina Promenade, opposite Oriental Hotel and provide door-to-door services for those going to the Singapore River HongBao. These services also make travelling within the city area more convenient. For example, Service C 1 facilitates the visiting public to travel between Singapore River HongBao and Great World City / Orchard corridor; Service C2 between Singapore River HongBao and the Chinese New Year Bazaar in Chinatown; Service C3 between Singapore River HongBao and Bugis Village.

    RAIL SERVICE

    Extension of operating hours for North East Line as well as Sengkang and Punggol LRT

    North East Line train service will be extended between 4 to 7 February 2005. The last train will depart at Chinatown Station at 1.00 am for both directions to Punggol and Harbour Front.

    The operating hours for Sengkang and Punggol LRT will also be extended for the same period between 4 to 7 February 2005. The last train will leave the Town Centre stations at 1.31 am and 1.33 am at Sengkang and Punggol respectively.
