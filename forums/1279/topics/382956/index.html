    Hi all,

    I just developed an iPhone app, called "bus@sg", for finding next bus arrival time in Singapore. This is easier than using the SBS or SMRT websites on your cell phone.

    The app shows you nearby bus stops, the real-time bus arrival information for all routes that go by a certain bus stop, all bus stops along a route, etc. It includes all the routes for SMRT and SBS buses. You can also add your frequently used bus stops to your favorites list. 

    I hope this app will be useful to you all! If you want to try out this app, here are 10 promotional codes: (they can be redeemed only once, so whoever uses first will get it).
