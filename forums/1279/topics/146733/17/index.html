    YOUTH WALK 2005

    The week of 12-18 June was a time of celebration for the youth in our Achdiocese.

    The week started off with a 3-day 2-night Youth Walk. Sliding down a snow slope, walking together for long distances, sleeping in tents at night and a Eucharistic celebration outdoors were some of the unforgettable moments for the 100 youths from 20 parishes that took part.

    The highlight of the Youth Week was the Youth Fest. The festival providied a platform to affirm youth and their talents. The festival kicked off at noon with a carnival bustling with games and food, live performances by bands at the talent corner and an on-site music station controlled by DJs. There was also an hour-long live chat discussing youth issues. The grand finale of the day was the evening rally cum Eucharistic celebration under the stars.

    The Youth Week affirmed our youth that they matter to Jesus and our Church.

    See photos in the YMO photo gallery.
    See & download Youth Walk group photo (556kB).

    (Information taken from CAYC)

    About 100 youths from various parishes were at Orchard MRT station waiting for further instructions on 13 June 2005 @ 9am. Most of the youths were feeling excited about the Youth Walk until 15 June 2005.
