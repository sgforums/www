    The TL Electronic Guide haven't update the new Service 241 & Amended Service 242 (still show st91/92) after its actual introduction on 15 Mar.

    I know in the past, the Jurong West St 71 sector was used to serve by Service 243 before it gets extended to Jurong West St 61/62 via Pioneer Rd North and converts into Green & White Plate Service. Henceforth, the Jurong West St 71 (Os Blk 701) was skipped and the only service that ply that bus stop was NR5. Until when several residents live nearby along that paricular stretch of St 71 feedback to SBS about no linkage from that bus stop back to Boon Lay and have to walk a little bit to the nearest bus stop (Jurong West Ave 5) to take a bus back to Boon Lay. Hence, Service 242 was amended to ply there and Service 241 was introduced to cover the routes of Jurong West St 91/92 stretch that existing Service 242 used to ply.

    True to the fact, Service 243 that previously plys along Jurong West St 71 uses LO2x DDs in the past and that's why Service 241 gets the DDs.
