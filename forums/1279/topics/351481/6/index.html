    I really don't know what's your fascination with downroutes if the buses have to travel from Clementi Int anyway. Why not just improve the headway throughout the whole trip? Do you know SWT's and downroutes can only be slotted in-between full trips scheduled at regular intervals, and are only meant to supplement these regular trips, not replace them?

    SWT's are only scheduled at places with exceptionally high activity such that regular trips cannot cope with the demand. It's not like your chaffeur, with 2-minute frequencies from JW St 91 to Clementi at that time just because you want to go to work.

    And SWT's are most disruptive because of the tradeoff because some passengers will not be able to go where they want to (imagine people wanting to go to JW St 91 from Clementi yet you took away their bus instead of giving a 2-min bump in the headway for the entire route in that time period), and running empty buses from the ending point to the starting point is actually fuel-inefficient.

    As for the 99/556 crossover, there is no point talking about it.... especially when Service 99 does have less than 10-min headway and does not have any siginificant loading issues. For these 2 reasons, there is no need to waste buses just to spam your Service 99 when there are other services that might need the buses to run 10-min peak headway.
