    Originally posted by BusSpeeder:
    ah nobody seen my reply. where to find the bus sticker to say whether dat bus is perm on dat service or is sp only? any1 can tell me?
    bndep - black marking scribbled somewhere. either above driver's window or near the front rego plate. Do note some are old perm markings. Sometimes cut-out service guide also can

    hgdep - Black or white rectangular sticker that is type-written. very professional. black sticker is usually 2003/2004 perms. white ones are 2005 thereafter. sometimes they tear the sticker and write SP or something over it

    amdep - black rectangular sticker, also type written but in italics form and its much smaller than hgdep's. Either pasted above driver's window or ON the window itself.

    brbp - white sticker with red marker for 2007 perms. earlier perms have blue marker handwritten stickers at coinbox facing the driver

    arbp - red/pink marker/crayon written on the coinbox at the base where the driver sees the coins.

    bbdep - same as brbp but some of the stix is kinda outdated or they have no time to change it. Only diff from brbp is that the marker used is thicker.

    slbp - white sticker with blue or black marker written. Quite distinctive of all depot.

    All stickers otherwise stated can be found above the driver's window.
