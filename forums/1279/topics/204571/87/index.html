    Buses get redeployed for the following reasons:

    1) Advertising contract

    2) To avoid wastage of resources

    3) To make sure that buses of the same age get the same mileage, such that valuation would be easier in case they want to sell the buses, and also to prevent a particular bus from excessive wear and tear.
