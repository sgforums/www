Thanks to its innovative approach that focuses on safety, efficiency, and eco-friendliness, the new Mercedes-Benz Citaro (Euro VI engine variant) was awarded as the Bus of the Year 2013 by an international jury. The Citaro Euro VI beat out competitors that mostly fielded hybrid-equipped candidates.
