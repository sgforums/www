    Let me compile a list of what I consider special buses as a reference, so that people will also post their arrival/departure timings on which routes, if possible:

    SBS 9888Y (first low-floor SuperBus in singapore) - route 154, if I'm not wrong
    SBS 9889U (first bus to be designed by ComfortDelgro Engineering) - route 166
    SBS 1688K (only diesel Volvo B10BLE in Singapore) - route 163
    SBS 2888T (only Scania L94UB in SBS Transit's fleet) - Express 518
    SBS 997A (only non-bendy super-long bus in Singapore) - route 62
    SBS 2988M and SBS 2989K (first two CNG buses in Singapore) - former on route 105, latter on route 243
    SBS 310G (museum bus) - rarely sees revenue service, so will be almost impossible to catch...
    SBS 1881T (only single-deck bus within SBS Transit's fleet with an underfloor air-conditioner (if I'm not wrong), and a prototype of SBS Transit's 1998 refurbishment programme) - spare bus of BBDEP, so will be hard to catch...
    SBS 1888A (another prototype of SBS Transit's 1998 refurbishment programme) - route 151
    SBS 73R (OAC Scania with completely refurbished interior) - route 3
    SBS 79A (OAC Scania with modified air-conditioner) - route 45
    SBS 9810X (Melbourne-built Volvo Super Olympian and the only one now with EDS (except SBS 9889U)) - route 76
    SBS 2750G (only DM3500 B10M with Webasto air-conditioner) - route 81/ route 70 crossover
    SBS 2754X (only DM3500 B10M with plug-doors) - AMDEP spare bus, but look below for a hint on how to catch it...
    SBS 2779A (only DM3500 B10M with EDS) - route 153 (is it still a Nokia Music Mobile?)

    *TIB 838H (first bendy bus in Singapore) - route 172
    *TIB 1024H (demonstrator Volgren bendy) - route 913
    *TIB 1017D and TIB 1018B (only Volgren-bodied Dennis Lance buses) - both on route 851
    *TIB 1257C (former CSS bus and only Dennis Lance in CSS' fleet before it was transferred to $MRT) - route 980
    *TIB 905Y (demonstrator Hino Volgren) - route 941
    *TIB 700X (DAF with Hispano-like front) - route 172

    * - With the... faeces going on now about $MRT withdrawing route 608, I suggest that you put off catching special $MRT buses for the sake of catching it, or even boycott them as far as possible (except route 608, though), to show your displeasure .

    Hint 1: SBS 310G, SBS 1881T, SBS 1888A, SBS 73R, and SBS 79A are all old buses that may be scrapped soon or in a few years, so if you have not been on those buses, catch them now you must while you can!
    Hint 2: SBS 2754X often do route 162 on weekends!

    Feel free to add any other bus you think is special onto this list.

    Personally, I have been on all the SBS Transit special buses except SBS 310G, SBS 1888A, SBS 9810X, SBS 73R, and SBS 79A. As for $MRT, I have only been on TIB 1017D, TIB 1018B, and TIB 1257C. Not that it's a bad thing .
