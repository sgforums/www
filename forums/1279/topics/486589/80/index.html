    I concede that Marine Parade has many schools that would attract students living in Tanjong Rhu. However, this can already be made via both 11 and 158 by transfering to 16, 31 and 196 at Mountbatten. It's quite good that residents can rely on more than one service. Beyond this, shopping amenities are already available at SportsHub (which brings me to my next point), so there's no need to go to Parkway Parade. The majority of condominiums are within a short walk to SportsHub via Geylang River footbridge. This in turn also explains 11's poor loading. So increased frequency may to Stadium may not be very necessary. 

     Saving wasted resources as sv 11 loading continues to be poor, though provides unique connection bet Stadium MRT and Tanjong Rhu (20-25 pax) and Stadium MRT to Geylang (10-15 pax). With sv 15 at better frequency and longer route, loading can be expected to improve.

     I agree though that given the population of Tanjong Rhu, having 2 higher frequency services makes sense. (refer to my service 11 extension in route suggestions).

    Ultimately, your route would be extremely duplicative of 12, 14 and 196 along Mountbatten Road, and would merely enhance existing connections already provided by an assortment of services. The unique sectors, namely Tanjong Rhu to Marine Parade may be useful. However, I feel this sector is subordinate to other needs in the area such as Koon Seng Rd, Haig Rd, Kembangan etc, areas which have limited/no bus service. Thus, I feel the current services are adequate enough to serve this sector.

    Commuters staying at Tanjong Rhu ve to transfer using 11,158,158A to 10,14,16,196...

    Suggestions to ve special departures(2/3 trips at 15 mins interval beginning 0745) from Tanjong Rhu to Shenton Way AM Peak(158P) n vice versa (2/3 trips at 15mins interval fr 1815) PM peak(158S)  flatly rejected, reason commuters to tfr...
