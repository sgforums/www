    15 September 2015 - A new bus service - Service 102 - will be rolled out starting from Sunday, 20 September 2015, to provide a new direct link between Hougang and Jalan Kayu via Buangkok Drive and Sengkang neighbourhoods. It will enhance connectivity to key transport nodes such as Buangkok, Sengkang and Hougang MRT/bus interchanges and local amenities.

    2. It will ply along Hougang Central, Upper Serangoon Road, Buangkok Drive, Sengkang Central, Compassvale Drive, Compassvale Road, Sengkang East Way, Sengkang West Way and Jalan Kayu.

    3. Service 102 will operate daily from 5.45am to 11.30pm.

    Info extracted from LTA website.
