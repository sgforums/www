    All, the news is officially out. Posters are out at various bus stops at Punggol. Sbs 386 will be officially extended to serve edgedale plains and Punggol east with effect from 15 feb 2015. After coming out from edgefield plains loop deep inside, it will come out to Punggol drive (oasis lrt), then edgedale plains (horizon primary and greendale primary), then Punggol field (plaza stop) and turn to Punggol east (Riveria lrt) then Punggol central (Blk 162B), turn right towards edgedale plains (horizon primary school) and Punggol drive (oasis LRT), then back on current way to mrt.

    There is a bit of slight difference in that the two stops Blk 672A and Blk 670A inside edgefield plains will function as some sort of guiding principle. Blk 672A stop is for those going Punggol east and Blk 670A stop is for those going Punggol mrt.

    This is not too bad, linking oasis LRT and future mall and poly clinic to residents to shop at Punggol plaza and vice versa.

    It also brings edgefield plains residents to plaza and those restaurants opposite Riveria LRT.

    It also saves space at Punggol bus interchange since there is no new service to be introduced. Just an extension of sbs 386 which sees very low ridership.
