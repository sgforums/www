    Joint News Release by the Land Transport Authority & SBS Transit - Bus service 103 to be Extended to Yishun Bus Interchange from June 2015

    27 May 2015 - From 14 June 2015, SBS Transit bus service 103 will be extended to Yishun Bus Interchange as part of the Bus Service Enhancement Programme (BSEP). Service 103 currently operates from Serangoon Bus Interchange and loops at West Camp Road.

    2 The extension will provide Yishun residents with a new direct link to the Seletar Aerospace Park. It will also enhance connectivity to Yishun MRT and Bus Interchange, and to key amenities such as Khoo Teck Puat Hospital, SAFRA Yishun Country Club and Northpoint Shopping Centre.

    3 Service 103 will be extended from West Camp Road and ply Yishun Avenue 1, Yishun Avenue 11, Yishun Central and Yishun Avenue 2 before ending its journey at Yishun Bus Interchange. The service’s operating hours from Serangoon Bus Interchange will remain unchanged from 6am to 12.15am on weekdays, and from 6am to 12am on weekends and public holidays. From Yishun Bus Interchange, the service will operate from 6am to 12am daily.

    4 Please refer to the attached poster for more details.
