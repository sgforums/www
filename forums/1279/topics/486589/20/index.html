    Since LTA has been introducing many new M svcs/ full day SWTs lately, do you think we'll see more of them this quarter? Personally, I think it's a good way to improve freq. on sectors with heavier loading without having to add buses on the whole route.

    May have been suggested before, but here's my take on possible svcs with M variants:

    27M: Sengkang - Tampines (can deploy DDs)

    36M: Marine Parade - Tomlinson Rd (can deploy DDs)

    53M/113M: Kovan - Lor Ah Soo - Serangoon (combine 53A/B and 113A, can also be introduced as a new svc with a diff. number altogether)

    121M: Tiong Bahru MRT - Kim Tian Rd - Lower Delta Rd (return to Tiong Bahru via Lower Delta Rd express. have observed high full day loading at this sector, as Kim Tian residents prefer taking bus since now there's an option, while bus is much emptier towards Telok Blangah. may be introduced as peak hour SWT 121A first)

    172M: CCK - KJE/PIE (express) - Boon Lay (faster connection between CCK and Boon Lay by skipping Old CCK Rd/Jln Bahar, which has heavy boarding/alighting during peak hrs, slowing down the journey for pax between CCK and Boon Lay. I suggested a similar route before via KJE/PIE, but it was a new route between Bt Panjang and Joo Koon.)

    -----------------------------------------

    Since 189 was recently split into 189 (loop @ Clementi) and 201, I believe we could possibly see similar amendments in future to separate sectors with different loading patterns into different svcs, the question is when and how.

    One possible svc to be split is 858. Much inter/intra town travel in Yishun, Sembawang & Woodlands, but also provides fast, direct connection between CGA/ Northeast (via TPE & Jln kayu stops) and North areas. I believe the route can be split as such:

    858: Yishun - CGA

    968: Woodlands - Yishun MRT/Ave 2/Khatib MRT - Yishun Ave 1 (express) - Upp Thomson Rd - Yio Chu Kang Rd - Yio Chu Kang MRT/Int

    Although this will inconvenience pax travelling between CGA/Northeast and Sembawang/Woodlands, those from Sembawang/Woodlands can transfer from 968 to 858 along Yishun Ave 2. 968's sector from Khatib to YCK was suggested previously by other users in the route suggestions thread as part of another svc, which would provide better connectivity to Upp Thomson/Tagore/YCK Rd.

    The amended 858 would be less prone to bunching with a shorter route that mostly uses the expressway, and shorter sector in residential area with slower, more unpredictable traffic. The existing route's high loading sector between Woodlands and Yishun, as new svc 968, can get high capacity buses which cannot be deployed now due to CGA height restrictions.
