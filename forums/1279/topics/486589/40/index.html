    I just realised that, it seems that the way LTA is introducing new services now is kind of based on GCM.

    If you recall, in the Bulim Package there were originally 24 services, but in Oct 2014 LTA announced 26. In the next few months before closing of tender in Jan 2015, sv 41 and 990 were introduced and sv 189 was amended (sv 201 is also related to the amendment of sv 189). It seems that LTA was trying to finalise the routes in Jurong East and Bukit Batok and shape them into what LTA wanted them to be when TT take over the operations in 2016, so as to avoid changes to the services in the package which would be unfair to the tenderers. That's why LTA was rushing to introduce so many services in Q4 2014, even though some seems unnecessary at this moment.

    Similarly, Loyang Package has 3 services to be introduced. This probably means that we will see 3 new services in Pasir Ris and Punggol before the tender is closed in Aug 2015. After this, we may not see any changes until the second half of 2016.

    (I wonder, is this the reason why 301 and 983 are introduced which seem somehow redundant before the flats in Keat Hong are completed? Maybe they will be in some package soon so LTA is rushing to amend/introduce the services even when it's unnecessary at this point?)
