    You remember, last time sec three social studies, got teach about ERP, COE and Park-and-Ride? If I remember correctly, it is one of the first few chapters.

    In this Park-and-Ride scheme, car parks are provided for drivers to park their car at somewhere outside the city area, and bus services are provided for them to take bus into the city. These bus services were known as "City Shuttle Services" (CSS).

    However, there was poor response to the Park-and-Ride scheme and the demand for the CSS bus services was low. After a while, many CSS bus services were removed. CSS608 (City Shuttle Services bus service 608) was probably one of those that survived. Those that survived were subsequently absorbed by TIBS/SMRT, but eventually withdrawn by year 2007.

    but does that mean the Park-and-Ride scheme is not successful? no. although it was not successful in the past, it may be of good use now or in the future~
