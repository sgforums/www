    This route has many merits but I doubt this is a leak as there have been no official announcements on CBP. 

    But I would prefer the following:

    Service 47 (CBP - Eunos)

    CBP > Xilin Av > Tanah Merah MRT > Bedok Nth Rd > Bedok Nth Av 2 > Bedok Nth St 3 > Bedok Nth Av 1 > Chai Chee St > Chai Chee Dr > Changi Rd > Jalan Kembangan (U-turn at roundabout) > Frankel Av > East Coast Rd > Siglap Rd > Marine Parade Rd > Joo Chiat Rd > Koon Seng Rd > Still Rd > Chagi Rd > Jln Turi > Eunos Int. 

    1. Chai Chee Dr - This area is actually closest to Kembangan MRT and would make most sense connecting since it is Kembangan is nearer, instead of all the back-tracking from Bedok Int. This will also help to improve connectivity to areas such as Waringin Park which are quite far from Kembangan MRT. 

    2. Jalan Kembangan - Provides Kembangan Estate connections to amenities along Marine Parade Rd and CBP. While this would be an inconvenience to Chai Chee residents heading to Marine Parade, I believe it would be a minor one and in fact benefit Chai Chee residents as there will be a more direct connection the station compared with stopping at Masjid Kassim. CBP and Eunos bound buses will spot different coloured plates. 

    3. Frankel Av/East Coast Rd/Siglap Rd - Service 42 is such a waste of time given the trip inside Opera Estate, the 3-point turn at Aida St (what a graceful sight) and serves such a limited stretch of Frankel Av. Residents of Frankel Estate, Bowmont Gardens, Siglap Ctr, Keris Estate, East Coast Terrace, Ocean Park, Mandarin Gardens & Neptune Court will all save taxi money with bus access to Kembangan MRT. This will also better link Chai Chee, Kembangan Estate and Marine Parade with amenities in Siglap. 

    4. Marine Parade Rd - Schools are a big draw along Marine Parade Rd. This service will link Victoria Sch (to some extent), Victoria JC, Ngee Ann Pri, CHIJ KC (Pri & Sec), St Pat's and Tao Nan Sch with residents of Koon Seng, Siglap & Frankel, Kembangan & Chai Chee - links which do not exist today. 

    5. Koon Seng Rd to Eunos - This just makes more sense. Koon Seng to Kembangan is already served by Service 33 through Joo Chiat Place, and really it's not a fantastic MRT connection given the sheer amount of back-tracking. Eunos is much more logical. 

    Perhaps a 47M service could be introduced for higher demand between Bedok Nth Rd & CBP.
