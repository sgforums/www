    Hi bus fans...

    It's confirmed.

    The new aircon Boon Lay Interchange is scheduled to be opened on 20/12/09, before Christmas season.

    Media release from both PTOs are not out yet, but there is a circular in a SMRT bus depot informing SLs of the scheduled opening of the new BLI on the said date.

    SLs will be undergoing familiarization training of the new interchange layout, where the parking bay is, the alighting and boarding berth, etc, somewhere this week.
