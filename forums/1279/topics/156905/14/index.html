    TEMPORARY DIVERSION OF SMRT BUS SERVICES FOR
    STANDARD CHARTERED SINGAPORE MARATHON

    On Sunday, 4 December 2005, Shenton Way, Raffles Quay, Collyer Quay and the Padang areas will be closed between 3am and 1pm for the Standard Chartered Singapore Marathon.

    As such SMRT bus services 61, 75, 77,106, 171, 700A, 857, 960, 961, 970 and 980 will be temporarily diverted. In addition, SMRT's NightRider 1,2,5,6,7 and 8 will not operate on Saturday, 3 December 2005.

    Commuters can contact Transitlink Hotline at 1800-225 5663 or SMRT Customer Relations Centre at 1800-3368900 for more details.
