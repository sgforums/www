    SBL Press Release

    SBL acquires Services 61 and 68 from SMRTB and SBST respectively.

    SERVICE 61 (amended), Bedok Interchange - Bukit Batok Interchange

    Service amended to skip HarbourFront, Kallang Bahru, MacPherson Estate, Tanglin Halt and Eunos. Instead, it will be amended to serve Bedok North, Bedok Reservoir, Geylang East and Bukit Merah. Other operating details remain unchanged. DDs will be used on this service. SBST and SMRTB will deal with all the skipped parts

    SERVICE 68 (amended), Tampines Interchange - Bukit Batok Interchange

    Service will no longer call along Tampines Street 92. Instead, it will serve on a new route to Bukit Batok via Bedok Reservoir, MacPherson, Little India, Orchard Road, Bukit Merah and Bukit Timah. Operating hours revised from 0530 - 0000 hours on Mondays to Saturdays and 0600 - 0000 on Sundays and Public Holidays. Due to height restrainments at Bukit Timah, only articulated buses will be used.

    SBL Press Release

    1. From Sunday, March 13 2005, Service 150 will be amended to serve Tanglin Halt. From Holland Village, it'll turn towards Holland Avenue, Tanglin Halt Road, Commonwealth Drive, Queensway, and resume its usual route. This will re-establish the route lost when Service 61 was cut.

    2. New service 57 will commence operations from Bishan Interchange to Harbour Front Interchange. This service will operate until the Circle Line is complete in 2010. It'll give Bishan residents a link to Serangoon, Paya Lebar, Geylang, North/South Bridge Road, Chinatown and HarbourFront. This bus service will commence operations on Sunday, 27 Feburary 2005. Articulated buses will be used on this service. No DDs.

    3. SBL will begin operations on 3 feeder services, beginning with Service 4 on Sunday, 27 Feburary 2005. This service will operate from Pasir Ris Interchange to Changi Village Terminal as a loop service. 2 more unannounced bus services will operate in March.
