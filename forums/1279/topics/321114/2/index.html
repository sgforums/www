    26 Jun 08, 00:49

    Svc 587 & 588 do not have any crossovers....fyi

    They are "stand-alone duties: meaning these duties will pull out from WLDEP (587AM01) and AMDEP (588AM01), perform the service and then return to depots and that's it...end of day!

    The SLs who are driving these duties are normally worker transport SLs. They will drive worker transport on the night before and then after that, they will perform the PBS duties before they call it at day and go home.....

    Or sometimes, it will be driven by Lead or Chief SLs and then after the duty ends they will assist the full-time starters at the depot starter office...
