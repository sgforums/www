    Latest Update as on 30 July

    SBST has put up notices of the services at its bus interchanges. Below is the schedule from 06 to 14 August. Take note that some stops in Marina South are designated as boarding only or alighting only.

    Service 400 (New Bridge Rd terminal to loop at Marina South):
    Operates on 06, 07, 09, 13 and 14 August from 09.30am to 11.30pm and
    Operates on 08 August from 06.30pm to 01.30am

    Route via New Bridge Rd terminal ---> Church St ---> Maxwell Rd, continue with service 400 existing route into Marina South, Prince Edward Rd ---> South Canal Rd ---> New Bridge Rd terminal.

    Service 400 (Shenton Way terminal to loop at Marina South):
    Operates from 10 to 12 August from 09.00am to 11.30pm and
    Operates on 08 August from 09.00am to 6.30pm

    Service 400A (Marina Bay MRT station to loop at Marina South):
    Operates on 06, 07 and from 09 to 14 August from 09.00am to 11.30pm and
    Operates on 08 August from 09.00am to 01.30am
