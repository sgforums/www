    some clarifications. usually the shift change timings can be quite broad, ranging from 12+pm to about 3+pm. both E and S can start out very early as well, especially E. E will go back depot around after 2 trips, depending on the length of the route, while S stay out there longer to about late morning then go to depot. E will usually come out at noon to mid-afternoon, while S for afternoon, some can just come out at peak hours, do a few trips, then go back depot liao. TQ not necessarily be fixed 8am-7pm. plus 8-7 is 11 hours, TQ = three quarters. the BC will work about 8 hours. can come out at anytime from 7 to 11, go back to depot around 3 to 6.
