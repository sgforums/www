    LJS B9TL and Sony Cybershot B9TL that were featured in the pics and vids of the flooding are from Sv 7. Both were stuck side by side. Jam caused severe delay for all Orchard Svcs and Svcs plying thru PIE. Flooding was also witnessed at Newton Circus and at Whitley Road.     BKE (aft Dairy Farm towards PIE)

    Whitley Road (Heading into City)

    Rochor Road (Heading out of city)
