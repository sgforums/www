    Originally posted by All the Best!:


    What do u mean by perm stix

    Perm stix is usually a white sticker with the service number on it.

    Sticker formats (new):
    SV xxx - SV 168 for example
    SPARE - Spare bus
    Sticker formats (old):
    xxx - 168 for example
    SP - Spare bus

    Depending on bus type/depot, the sticker can be anywhere, they can either be spotted at the front near the door (especially on BNDEP SDs), dashboard, coinbox, driver window area or upper deck front (some BNDEP DDs).

    Note that sometimes the perm stix can be subsituted by cut out service guides (especially HGDEP buses), tape or sticker with the perm service number written on it, or just simply a number written on the front or dashboard (mostly on older buses).
