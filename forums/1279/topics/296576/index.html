    8 new City Shopper premium services from SBS Transit will commence from Saturday, 15th December 2007, bringing heartlanders to Orchard and Suntec City in comfort.

    551: Hougang Plaza/Ave 8/Ang Mo Kio Ave 3
    575*: Bedok South Rd/Ave 3/North Rd (via PIE/CTE)
    576*: Tampines Ave 7/4/St 81
    577: Ang Mo Kio Ave 9/4/3/10
    578: Compassvale St, Punggol Rd then 163 route till YCK Rd (via CTE)
    579: Tampines St 41/Ave 9/2/Simei St 3 (thanks to tip off from 28_29_293)
    580: Teban Gardens Rd/West Coast Rd till Varsity Park (via AYE/Outram/Paterson Rd)
    581: Punggol Central/East/Field (via TPE/CTE)

    Stops in town are:
    * Far East Plaza (except 580)
    * Heeren
    * Orchard Plaza (after OG Cuppage)
    * MacDonald House (between PS and Cathay)
    * NTUC Income Centre
    * Raffles Hotel
    * Suntec Convention Centre
    * Suntec Tower 2 (for 551, 577, 578 & 581)
    * Centennial Tower (for 575, 576, 579 & 580)

    Operation hours:
    10am to 4.30pm on weekdays, 10am to 10pm on weekends and public holidays
    Fare: $3.30 for adults ($3.60 for 578 and 581), $1 for child/students

    * Service 575 and 576 does an endless loop, hence on its return trip, a "Terminating" sign will be put and only alighting is possible. It also does not call at the first stop out of TPE i.e. Blk 370 along Tampines Ave 7, instead it calls at Opp Tampines JC.
