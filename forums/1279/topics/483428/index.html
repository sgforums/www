    Bukit Batok Bus Interchange has some capacity for additional bus services, but would not be able to accomodate anymore soon - as the Government develops the area west of Bukit Batok West Avenue 2.

    There has to be a bus interchange at Bukit Gomak MRT Station to accomodate bus services to the new area. 

    Based on the map, it seems that the only possible site for a bus interchange at Bukit Gomak MRT Station is at the triangle-shaped land between the MRT station and Bukit Gomak Stadium - which is only 0.43 Hectres - not big enough for a bus interchange.

    in my opinion, due to space constraints, there can only be a bus park at Bukit Gomak MRT Station. As a bus park requires less land and not a need to be next to MRT Station, there are more sites to locate it. Personally, I feel that the land along Bukit Batok West Avenue 5, opposite blk 359/369 and underneath the MRT tracks can be put to better use if the bus park is constructed there.

    To make up for the forgone bus interchange option, the Government can upgrade the pair of bus stops (#43571 and #43579) at Bukit Gomak MRT Station to accomodate the number of bus services that a typical small interchange would have.

    In short, 

    • -bus park opposite blk359/369 and along Bukit Batok West Avenue 5 to supply buses.
    • -upgraded bus stops (#43571 and #43579) next to Bukit Gombak MRT Station for the bus services to start/end.
    • -collectively, both function as a bus interchange, although there is no physical bus interchange facility.

    meanwhile, bus services that pass by the bus stops (#43571 and #43579) next to Bukit Gombak MRT Station can continue to serve those bus stops (#43571 and #43579). 

    Instead of being disadvantaged by not having a bus interchange, the residents covered can benefit as they can make transfers to by-passing bus services from the same location (bus stops #43571 and #43579), without having to walk a certain distance to a bus stop somewhere else.
