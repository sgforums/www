    History on Orchard rd Special Bus Services

    We have here a back in time trip to discover how special  Bus services to orchard have evolved

    1980s
    No MRT then. the first recorded express service to orchard area was
    Service 7   Chai Chee/bedok - Demspsey rd Ter(CMPB)
    The service had an express sector between Changi rd via Nicholl HW and Stamford Rd (Capitol). While not specifically publicised as an express service to orchard, the service is mentioned here. Its partner services 6 and 8 went to Chinaotwn and Shenton Way prespectively using the same express sectors along Changi rds and Nicholl HW.

    Service 390  Airport to orchard
    Widely publicised in Hotel guidebooks and spead among tourists alike, svc 390 was the serviice to take from the Airport to orchard Rd. Service introduced in 1981.  The service gained AIR-CON status in 1988. Service was withdrawn in 1994. (refer to 1990s section)

    Service 168 (AIR0CON 168)

    in 1985 the service went PARTIAL AIR-CON. Using the comfy Merc OF1417 air-coned buses with Merc O 303 designs of the day, people traveleling to orchard from Ang Mo Kio enjoyed comfy rides in air-con comfort. Withdrawn when MRT started in 1988 or so.

    1990s
    While 168 faded away apporx 3 years later, a  new generation Service 162 (Yio Chu kang - Marina Centre/Shenton Way)appeared. The service ha d a cool express sector along Thomson rd and Newton rd. However said service express sector was cut in early 1996 and the service now calls at all bus stops along Thomson Rd.

    1995 - The Express Services were introduced. 502 518 went to orchard.

    With the demise of 390, a service 16E was introduced to as a form of "fast forward" to then svc 16 which was extended to Airport. 16E was later changed svc no to 36. Service 16E skipped national Sstadium and went by ECP but served parkway to Airport.

    2000- a second set of Express services were introduced. in Set 2, Express Service 512 serving Hougang Int, Ave 8 , AMK Ave 3 and orchard was intrduced. however service operated during peak hours only and was directional based. For eg: evening peak operated from orchard to hougang. Svc was cut after NEL started.

    2005 - fast Forward 14e 174e were introdcued. also Peak direction based express typed services.

    2007 or 8 - SBS Cityshopper services were introduced.

    2009 Aug - They were totolally withdrawn.


    the article excluded CSS and Citybuzz and CBD 1 and 2 services.
