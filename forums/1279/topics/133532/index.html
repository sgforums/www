  • The_Bus_Guide
    25 May 05, 02:45
    Both types of Lances have the same Duple Metsec bodywork. The main difference is that the UMW Lance have a smaller front desto. This is because it was originally designed for holding plastic plates. The UMW Lances are older but they have a 245 bhp engine while UK Lances 211 bhp. There are also 2 Lances with Volgren bodywork. The story goes that UMW was looking to offload their Lances. TIBS secured a deal and took all the Lances, including 2 bare chassii. These 2 became the Volgren Lances.
