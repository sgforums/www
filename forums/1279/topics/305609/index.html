    (1) Take 800 (4 stops, on your right) or 804 (2 stops, on your left) from Yishun Interchange.

    (2) Take 856 from Yishun Interchange and alight two stops after Sembawang MRT (Sembawang Mart is on your right). Or alight at Sembawang MRT and walk along Canberra Rd towards Admiralty Rd West (northwards) for 600m.
