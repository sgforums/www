  • jurongresident
    10 Jun 15, 17:25

    1. when SBS6549X departed Boon Lay Bus Interchange on bus service 243G at around 4:30pm today, the bus was hot and stuffy. The air con was very weak. Could only feel hot air.
    1. went to look at Fairprice Hub level one layout. Saw the part of the Joo Koon Bus Interchange. Realised that B3 is two times the length of B2 and B1.
    1. went to check out Tuas Bus Terminal. NTWU calls it "Tuas Interchange", SBS Transit calls it "Tuas Terminal". but the infrastructure is clearly bus interchange, with three alighting berths and four sawtooth boarding berths.
    2. From the concourse, I looked at the bus bay and counted the number of bus parking lots. If I have counted correctly, there are thirteen bus parking lots at Tuas Bus Interchange. around thirteen. which means Tuas Bus Interchange actually has the capacity for (only) around six or seven bus services of feeder frequency.
