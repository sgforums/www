    Looking closely at the specifications of Panorama Melaka's BYD K9 eBus, it seems like it was the original demonstrator meant for SMRT buses.

    Observations:
    1. Mobitec EDS with ICU 402 controller
    2. Alcoa rims
    3. Volgelsitze System 750/3 seats
    4. Purple and red seat covers
    5. Foldable seat at wheelchair bay
    6. Driver's cabin roof has identical curved design (even with the 2 lines on it) to SMRT's MCV and Gemilang bodied MAN buses.
    7. Tongda TD-10 Bus Line Announcer System. Only SMRT MAN buses are fitted with it, seems to be a custom made variant for SMRT based on the original TD-09 which was first installed on SMB137A.
