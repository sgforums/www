    Buses were all late as a result. SMB1588A on 188E that was supposed to leave at 0723hrs ended up only reached the boarding bay at around 0728hrs. The 172 that i boarded (SMB59S -- 172AP02) was supposed to reach boarding berth at 0720hrs, ended up only picked up pax at 0729hrs. Good thing by the time SMB59S picked up pax the jam had subsided a little, and SMB59S managed to exit int smoothly.

    SMRT staff were seen running around to direct the buses...a super dangerous scene.

    CCK INT jam is not uncommon, but it was really the first time i see a jam to this extent...
