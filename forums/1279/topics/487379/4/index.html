    Today:

    SBS6159M entered HarbourFront Int and parked at the 408 berth at 1.05p.m. The bus was picking up about 10-20 BCs, though I'm not sure why (suspect ferrying BCs)... When SBS2830J (on 408) arrived back at HarbourFront Int at about 1.08p.m, it could not go into the 408 berth, because SBS6159M (and SBS2739R in the other berth) was parked there and there were no empty lots. After a KUB reversed out of 124's berth, SBS2830J's BC drove into the lot for 124's berth to let the passengers disembark before reversing out and waiting for a empty lot. Finally SBS2739R started its trip on 408 at 1.12p.m. (5 minutes late) and reversed out of the lot, allowing SBS2830J to park in the lot.

    I don't know if it's the fault for the BC of SBS6159M to park there, but I think it might also be due to the lack of parking lots there... I have seen 963 buses park at one corner during the PM peak to wait before getting an empty lot to park the bus.
