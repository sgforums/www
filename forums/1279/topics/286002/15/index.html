    Here are some problem areas which you need to take note of in the case of your SBS 9200G on 111

    a) Photo is too blur. Of course it's a night shot, so you need a stable structure or foundation (e.g. tripod, floor) to take a good night shot.
    b) There are obstructions in the photo, most notably the bus stop shelter and the passengers waiting to board the bus. Avoid such things in your bus photo. Go out to somewhere else to take a better shot.
    c) The bus in the photo is too frontal. Experiment with various angles, keeping in mind the front/rear and side, and see which is best.
