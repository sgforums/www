    It's just a cameo.

    Definition of 'cameo' = When a bus should have been on it's particular permanent service but went to do another service.

    Take SBS 1688K for example. It's perm (short form for permanent) service is 163. If it goes to do Service 153 then it's known as a cameo.

    Do look out for tamago's newcomer's guide for more info.
