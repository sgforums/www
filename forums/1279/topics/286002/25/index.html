    Hey there! I understand that this is your first attempt at taking bus photos.

    However, if you want to take better photos of buses, I don't recommend using a phone camera. If you have enough money to afford for at least a good digital camera, why not go for it instead?

    Here are some recent shots that I took.

    SBS 2708E on Express 502 (02/12)


    Location: Marina Centre. You can take Service 171 direct to the bus stop outside Esplanade.

    TIB 0661A on Service 178 (29/11)


    Location: Bukit Batok Central. You can take 852 direct to Bukit Batok and walk out to the bus stop (same location where I took this picture)

    TIB 1079A on Service 913 (29/11)


    Location: Outside WRI. You can take Service 169 direct to WRI (if the bus stop near your house serves this service), then walk out to where Services 161 and 168 will exit.

    PA 5418B on Hippo Tours


    Location: Suntec City. You can again take Service 171 direct to Marina Centre and find Hippo Tours and Duck Tours buses outside Suntec Convention Centre.

    Happy shooting!

    I saw 2 person standing on the traffic island / divider at Upper Paya Lebar Road this morning near to 11.30am, and was thinking to myself that it should be someone from this place.

    Let me give everybody a piece of advice.

    If you want to take photos at the centre median, go ahead, but always remember, SAFETY FIRST!

    Some traffic island consist of 2 pieces of D1 kerb placed together or just cast in-situ concrete divider. These are not a safe place to stand and take photos.

    Go ahead and stand along centre medians that are wide, like Kampong Bahru Road.

    So to TIB1049L, please take care of yourself. What you do has consequences for others, especially motorists who are afraid of what you are doing, taking photos.
