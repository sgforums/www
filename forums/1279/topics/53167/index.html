    Hi everyone I am new here but I am by no means new to the local bus scene. Here are my traits:

    1. I HATE DENNIS LANCES! 245 or 211, I hate them all. The most sucky buses in the whole bus fleet. I get motion sickness everytime I take them. And so pathetically little seats too. I avoid them whenever i encounter them. Even the UD's fare better.

    2. I only really care about service 851. I love to see the trio of 61, 67 and 106 get chopped or curtailed because these 3 services always steal bendies from 851. For example TIB 1120 M as someone mentioned in this forum. It was not originally in 106. It started life in 851!

    Okay enough about me. I could start by doing my bus spotting report for today.

    I stopped at Yishun Int this evening and snooped around for a little while. There are a quite lot of anormalies observed in a short period today:

    Whole of service 806 given unfamiliar faces
    ==================================
    TIB 0974 Y - from 67
    TIB 1072 T - from 61
    TIB 1074 M - from 61
    TIB 1080 U - from 61

    None of the usual 806 buses are around.

    TIB 518 G was spotted on service 811! Yes 811. Since when did it cross from WDLDEP to AMKDEP.

    On my way to yishun, TIB 582 U was spotted on service 980. TIB 984 T is becoming a regular at 980 too.

    TIB 584 P was seen on 852 for at least one trip.

    And as usual, lots of Lance 245's and 211's polluting high load services like 851 and 804.

    Also the evening peak "helper" cameo bendies for 851 for the day are as follows:

    TIB 1099 S (the one i boarded)
    TIB 1123 E

    Both vehicles retired at around 8:15 pm.

    Thats all for today. Have a pleasant ride folks.

Moreover, the probability of getting a sucky bus is also much less, and is inversely proportional to the number of choices and the mean frequency and overall fleet deployment.
