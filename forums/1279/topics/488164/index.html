    This thread discusses former bus services that should have been retained/current bus services that should have retained their original routes. While this seems like just digging back into the past of bus services, it allows the collation of the lost connections that can be brought back in the form of new/amendment of existing routes (although unlikely given LTA's concept of more short distance bus services today)

    Here's my top picks:

    Old sv85 (Sengkang - HarbourFront)

    If the former sv85 between Sengkang - HarbourFront was retained, sv119 wouldn't need to be introduced just to cater mainly to students. An alternative link to the city (Orchard Rd/Little India) would also have been retained for the northeastern residents other than the NEL (even though the route was withdrawn because of NEL in the first place). It would also aid in complementing part of sv147's high demand.

    sv989 (Choa Chu Kang - Changi Airport)

    If the former sv989 between CCK - Changi Airport was retained, Bt Gombak residents will have an additional full day bus sv by their doorstep and enhanced connectivity to the central region of SG. Bt Batok Ind Est would also have an additional helper to complement the high demand of sv189 between Bt Batok and Bt Batok Ind Est. The main reason would also be a fast alternative from the west to the airport apart from the rail network.
