    As the thread starter, here are my comments on my original intention of the creation of this thread:

    In the past few months, I had been looking through past bus guides and read up on the development of public bus services from the 1960s till today, through SGWiki, some threads in this forum years ago and several visits to the NLB for old bus guides.

    This was when I realized that there were several (not all) bus services that benefited the masses and provide good alternatives to the MRT, but were unfortunately being withdrawn or shortened. Thus, I was thinking that other members in the forum would probably feel the same way for certain services, which resulted in the creation of this thread, to discuss whether certain withdrawn services that benefited the masses can be brought back in a slightly modified fashion under the BSEP (which probably will never be the case).

    While I do feel that the shortening/withdrawal of several bus services during the NEL Rationalization were probably too harsh, I know that it is impossible to revert back the services to their original forms. Thus, this thread was created with the intent to discuss what can now be done (through the introduction of new routes) to provide comparably fast alternatives to the NEL.

    It is most unfortunate that this thread had been "misused" by Path Light in complaining about the reverting of all services cut/withdrawn during the NEL Rationalization. Moreover, not all of the shortened/withdrawn routes during the rationalization were bad; some were good in an effort to really reduce the duplication levels and would have been too slow to compete with the NEL even if retained by LTA.

    Therefore, I don't mind if this thread is locked by the mods, since its original intent is now "ruined" by Path Light's constant harping over the past rather than constructive discussion over the above points mentioned. These issues can be better discussed in other threads like "New bus services by LTA/PTOs". Cheers.
