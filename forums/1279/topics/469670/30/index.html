    Originally posted by carbikebus:

    Quite confused as theyre horizontal and vertical type...i didnt know there was even the 280hp/1250nm 10518cc EEV NL engine..

    The vertically mounted 6871 cc D 0836 LOH Euro 6 is offered with either the 250 hp or 290 hp output and is only available on the European spec Lion's City. Vertically mounted means that the engine is installed vertically, which allows the 3rd door to be installed.

    The 10518 cc D 2066 LUH engine however is horizontally mounted, and the engine is installed horizontally, which means that there is no space for the 3rd door to be installed.
