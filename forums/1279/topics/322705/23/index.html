    Originally posted by ahjack 77:


    wad is outside schedule buses?

    Its like slotting 1 bus into the current schedule without affecting the entire schedule at all in terms of timings and block numbers.

    Operates on a new duty code and a new block that is at the end of the current schedule. The only thing that gets boosted is the frequency that is abnormally good. Imagine slotting in 1 bus that currently has 10min frequency during this period.

    Without OS: 0639 0648 0657 0707 0717 0727 0737 0747
    With OS: 0639 0648 0657 0707 0712 0717 0727 0737 0742 0747
    Result: Add 2 outside schedule buses

    There's also another type of OS that does not have timesheet at all. Everythign is at the disposal of the Interchange timekeeper. That's the true outside schedule. lol..

    Originally posted by lifelikedrama:

    Am I right in saying Svc 119 schedule is changing to Holiday1 from 7th Sept onwards?
