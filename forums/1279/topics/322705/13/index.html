    Quite bored during the hols so i made this. (apologise if its alittle messy)

    made after looking at the current 154 timings. Sub a few S Shift here and there to make service cohesive. Here's a copy of my most elaborate block. I decided to sub sv90 during that time, the bus in front and behind of the crossover bus is about 11minutes apart while others are 9 mins apart. With this crossover, it splits the frequency to 6 mins and 5 mins respectively. of course after i adjusted 3 of sv90 behind the crossover to 9mins to become like that.

    Any comments? I know this block a lot of down routing which some drivers like but its placed at timings with the demand and it keeps early . Something drivers look forward to.

    Block goes like this:
    1) OFF SVC BRBP - 17151 Cwealth Ave West (Blk 410)
    2) S154  17151 Cweath Ave West (Blk 410) -  22009 T1 (Boon Lay Int)
    3) S154A 22009 T1 (Boon Lay Int) - 12101 Clementi Rd (Ngee Ann Poly)
    4) OFF SVC 12101 Clementi Rd (Ngee Ann Poly) - 21029 Jln Ahmad Ibrahim (aft Corporation Pl)
    5) S154 21029 Jln Ahmad Ibrahim (aft Corporation Pl) - 82009 T2 (Eunos Int)
    6) OFF SVC 82009 T2 (Eunos Int) - BRBP
    7) NO PAY SWING at BRBP from 0859-1300
    8) OFF SVC BRBP - 82009 T2 (Eunos Int)
    9) S154 82009 T2 (Eunos Int) - 22009 T1 (Boon Lay Int)
