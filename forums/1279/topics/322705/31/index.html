    Originally posted by One Harmonius Blend:


    i saw it on 487L on tuesday, thats why i asked how schedule like this operate

     

    How does 178 S2-01 operate? 


    178S201 operates differently because its a S2 shift which is basically a school peak hours shift that runs from 5:30am to about 9am in the AM part of the shift and then from around 12 noon to the evening for the PM part of the shift. As such, buses used in the AM part of the shift can be different from the PM part of the shift, especially in the case for SMRTB. There can be different scenarios for the buses on these shifts:

    1) AM part uses cameo, PM part also uses cameo (most of the time is a different bus from AM shift; though the same bus for both parts had happened before)

    2) AM uses perm, PM part uses cameo (which is mostly the case; the PM part may also use other S-Shift perms from other services)

    3) AM uses cameo, PM uses perm (quite unlikely for the case of SMRTB)

    4) AM uses perm, PM uses perm

    As the way the timesheet is named, this slot does not carry any crossovers to other services. There are many code names used on timesheets that shows what type of shift it runs and whether it has any crossovers to other services
