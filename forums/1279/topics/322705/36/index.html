    Originally posted by TIB657M:

    How does E Shift for SBS Transit buses work?


    E-shift for SBST = S2xx shift for SMRT

    Duty normally starts at 6-7am, end around 9am after which driver has swing break. Duty will resume again at noon and will end around 7pm in the evening together with the S-shifts (or S1xx for SMRT).
