    Originally posted by carbikebus:

    So for SBST there’s Clementi,Pasir Ris,Tampines,Bedok,Hougang.May i know which service park at Interchange?


    Its every interchange except Eunos, Serangoon & Harbourfront. Sengkang doesnt have but has some of its services parked overnight at Punggol.

    SBST does not have full service fleet unlike SMRT who park overnight at the interchange. They choose based on the earliest timing they can keep from revenue service, though it doesnt equate to first few buses to off service = overnight parking. Criteria would also include that in the morning it cannot start from int too late to affect daily operations, and no morning downroutes since they park at the int overnight.
