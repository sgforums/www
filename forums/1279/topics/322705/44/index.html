    If the bolded ones are wrong I need you to substantiate why its wrong. I dont have anyone to counter check with me and S36 is a tricky service where every trip the driver jumps bus.

    I'll quantify the bolded ones you highlighted for now:

    S36 Mon-Thu
    S: Tomlinson 0618-0939, 1750-1955 36B

    This S shift reports to Changi direct to take the bus from Duty 23P (previous trip 1534-1748) and it runs 36B till 1955 then keep at bndep. That's why you see the new bus stop poles having 36B running from Changi Airport instead of Lagoon View.

    S36 Friday
    S: PTB3 0702-1230, 1727-1954
    T: 0725-1455

    The S shift starts in between the previous S shift (starts 0609 in Tomlinson) and an A shift (starts 0622 Tomlinson). Upon arriving at Changi, he jumps 2 buses to allow the T shift to start at 0725. Break time just nice 19min. The S shift driver in front of him jumps to his bus (break 0708-0717). This S shift will have meal break 0946-1022 (jump 4 buses).

    The T shift as stated will start 0725 then meal break 0937-1016 (jump 4 buses) then break 1224-1241 (jump 2 buses) and ends at 1455 as a P shift starts at 1508 and if jump 2 buses again to 1521 would exceed 20min. Take note that S36 meal breaks can exceed 40min.

    S36 Sunday
    T: 0949-1709

    Starting at 0949 gives the S shift that starts later a meal break (0936-1011) but not the S shift on the earlier slot (break 0925-0938). The T shift will jump 3 buses when having meal break (1150-1229) then jump once for its last trip keeping at 1709. He will bring the bus back to bndep. There's a parallel timing for a downroute that starts PTB3 1658 that's operated by a P shift that takes bus from bndep.

    Sunday's schedule is a bit complicating but it will be a little easier if you slot the duties to the trips correctly bearing in mind the 2min layover at Changi and mealbreaks for  drivers has to be at least 25min and has the option of exceeding 40min.
