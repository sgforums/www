    Originally posted by Ngweitong:

    timings for any bus (if enquiry is a bus, not a shift) are based on 2 assumptions and both must be complied with
    1. Bus is fit for service
    2. Bus is on its own scheduled timing on its perm service.

    If 1 is fulfilled and 2 isnt, it would mean the bus is on cameo on another service, or its on another set of timings on its perm service.

    Since he has mentioned its in coma, the first point is invalid.

    in short no 8030 for you to take until its out of the workshop.
