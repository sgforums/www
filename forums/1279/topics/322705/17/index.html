    Changes to schedules from tomorrow for the following services due to QoS:

    S21
    S79
    S183

    The above 3 are the ones reflected in SBST's ETT section. But there are services like S63 & S73 which do not have ETT that are also changing schedules due to QoS.

    Peak hour headway lowered down to 10-11 mins. Using S79 as a comparison (because it's the easiest of the 3 to re-do):

    Before:

    From 6.28am - 9.50am, headway is 14-16 mins.

    After (similar time frame):

    From 6.32am - 9.57am, headway is 10-11 mins with the exception of a block of 15 mins in the middle.

    Comparing both as a whole the new schedule has introduced 10 more full trips during peak hour.
