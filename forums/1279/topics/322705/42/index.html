    Originally posted by Bus and Train LoVuR:

    Why sv 13 also don't show non-WAB timings?


    To date there are 2 batches of WAB-ed services that do not show non-WAB timings. All previous batches have both non-WAB and WAB timings.

    They are the 3 Dec 09 batch and the current 28 Feb 10 batch. There are differences though

    3 Dec 09  - Services with prior e-timetables before WAB-certified continue to have the full schedules thereafter (i.e. 15 52 54 57 333). Services without e-timetables will only have timings WAB slots (i.e. 13 36 291). Sv36 is as good as full schedule cos its full fleet WAB.

    28 Feb 10 - Services with prior e-timetables before WAB-certified are taken offline the day before and then reinstated with WAB timings ONLY from the day they are WAB-certified (i.e. 29 113). Services without e-timetables will only have timings WAB slots (i.e. 63 265 334 359).
