    Originally posted by 28_29_293:

    anyone complete Svc 293 n Svc 358 duty schedules (not bus) successfully? How u all did it?

    me la.. If the fleet size is even number, then they will be paired up when they ply the route as when one bus rests, the bus behind will jump in front and the cycle repeats. Cos each time the driver drives 2 full trips before taking a break.

    If the fleet size is an odd number, the odd one out will have the driver putting the T plate every trip. and his break per trip is about half of those driving 2 full trips then taking a rest. The odd bus rotates when there's a meal break/S shift intervention.

    It is better to sort the timings by the bus instead of duty type. Once the buses have been sorted out, then slotting in the duty codes would be much easier.
