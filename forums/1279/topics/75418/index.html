    MightyMinds Bus Guide

    - Higher price $3.90
    - Not available at MRT Stations
    - Bus frequencies not indicated
    - Get free large size map worth $8.00 (according to my local mag seller, this has been selling for $8.00 for more than 2 years)
    - More updated

    Transit Link 2004 Bus Guide

    - Price $1.50
    - Available at MRT stations
    - Text-size too small - difficult to read
    - No bus-stop numbers (as indicated by LTA's bus-stop numbering system)
    - Not all bus services covered (eg buses between Jurong Island and Jurong Interchange)
    - Free Shuttle services not covered
    - Bus stop descriptions outdated
    - Lots of errors
