     'I look forward to zero jerkiness.'

Passengers, especially the elderly, get a headache and feel nauseous. Will bus operators ensure proper training for their drivers and introduce a penalty system for those who cannot drive professionally? This will ensure that passengers get to enjoy smooth bus rides.
