    Drunk man paid insufficient fare and punched bus captain in the face

    Happened on a B9 on service 147 on Saturday at 9.30 pm, at New Bridge Centre bus stop. Bus was heading to Clementi.

    A 50 year old plus drunk man paid 50 cents, the bus captain told him it is not sufficient, the man then told the bus captain he had no more coins left and punched his face. He tried to run off but was caught by 2 men.

    The bus captain then locked the doors to prevent the drunk man inside the bus from escaping while waiting for police to arrive. He was brought back to police station to assist in investigation.

    The 40 years old plus bus captain was later send to SGH for outpatient treatment and given 2 days medical leave.
