    Students pay bus fare only to get scolding from bus captains

    http://singaporeseen.stomp.com.sg/stomp/sgseen/got_service_or_not/245412/students_pay_bus_fare.html

    STOMPer Eileen was scolded by a SBS bus captain for forgetting to bring her ez-link card, even though she had paid her bus fare using cash.

    This STOMPer recounts the incident:

    "On 3rd September, I had finished my tuition and was waiting at the bus stop near Hougang Plaza for bus 159.

    "I had forgotten to bring my ez-link card along with me so I had no choice but to pay the bus fare using coins.

    "I boarded the bus and paid the bus captain 60 cents as I was just a lower secondary student.

    "However, the bus captain stared rudely at me and asked for identification. I explained to him that I had actually forgotten to bring my ez-link card.

    "I understand sometimes some bus captains do ask students to pay adult fares as they have no identification. Instead, the bus driver started to scold me!

    "He scolded and said: "Forget to bring, forget to bring! Forget to bring don't board a bus lah!"

    "I know some bus captains may be afraid that they would get into trouble if passengers do not pay the correct fare.

    "However, he did not asked me to pay the adult fare and scolded me right in front of everybody.

    "This is ridiculous! My mum and I hope to get an explanation from SBS.

    "We boarded the bus around 7pm-7:10pm, bus plate number was SBS 2698, driven by an uncle who seemed to be in his 50s or 60s."
