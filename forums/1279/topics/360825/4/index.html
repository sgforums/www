    Bus guide accurate as at December 31 2008, so revised fare not reflected: TransitLink

     

    http://singaporeseen.stomp.com.sg/singaporeseen/viewContent.jsp?id=73122

     

    TransitLink says its 2009 Guide is accurate as at December 31, 2008, and as such, the fare revision for April 1 this year is not reflected in it.

    The reply from TransitLink came in response to a STOMPer's feedback, in which he said the bus guides published by TransitLink and Mighty Minds stated different fare prices for different bus journeys.

    In its reply, TransitLink said:

    "We refer to the forum post, “Which bus guide is correct” (Stomp, 29 May 2009).

    "We would like to take this opportunity to clarify that the information provided by the TransitLink Guide 2009 is accurate as at 31 December 2008.

    "As such, the 1 April 2009 fare revision is not reflected in the guide.

    "In order to facilitate easy reference, the TransitLink Guide separates the basic bus services from the non-basic bus services into different sections.

    "The non-basic services are further arranged in alphabetical order.

    "For updated bus and train information, commuters may log on to TransitLink website www.transitlink.com.sg ‘eGuide’ or call toll-free TransitLink Hotline 1800-CALL ONE (1800-2255 663).

    "We thank the writer for the feedback."
