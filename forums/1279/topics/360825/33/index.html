    Today hopped onto one bus, as usual at the last stop before getting off I would take one single overall shot of the interior. During the trip, I noticed the SL for some reason was sitting on the driver door when waiting at traffic light. Seeing the camera at the last stop and realizing that I might be yet another STOMPer, he closed the doors before I could get off and drove to the bus park. On the bus he kept interrogating me and refused to accept any explanation in Chinese, claims this is an act of transpass and I would be arrested by Police, and he will get his Senior to interrogate me. I just LOLed at his reaction.

    So at the park he closed the doors on me inside the bus, scared that I would run away. (Thinking that I have no idea how the door works) Then other passing SLs opened the door, very bewildered at what happened, some ask me to walk away and say that SL is using scare tatics. I just waited outside the control room to for someone who knows English fluent enough for my explanation (of which none appeared to be), the Senior he claimed never showed up after 20 mins. Then he brought me back to the bus and drove me to another section of the Interchange. Along this stretch he claims he has a job to keep and he would be in hot soup if I was let off and it goes to media, etc. etc. (Which I then realized what he is trying to do)

    Then I was handed to another stand-by Senior. The SL called up OCC before leaving and passed the phone to the stand-by Senior, and then I was asked  by the latter to explain the situation on the phone. After a minute of brief explanation and my understanding of the rules in English, the matter was considered settled.
