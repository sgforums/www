    Reliance Omnibus invests RM1 million to improve bus svcs

    Reliance Omnibus is a bus service company in Ipoh that specliases trips between Ipoh and Kuala Kangsar (æ±Ÿæ²™).

    As the CEO mentioned, the company will modify the bus route and upgrade the standards of its 17 buses. This includes having uniformed and polite drivers, and having their aircon systems improved.

    The time taken for the trip will be increased by 1/2hr to 3.5hrs, so as to allow more time for the driver to rest. Thus the driver does not need to speed.
