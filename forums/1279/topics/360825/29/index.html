    Considerate bus driver uses off-duty time to ensure passengers' safety
    http://singaporeseen.stomp.com.sg/singaporeseen/viewContent.jsp?id=83793
    This off-duty bus driver was seen seeking help from construction workers to ensure that a hanging tree branch at Boon Lay Drive would not be a danger to passing double-decker buses.

    STOMPer CHECKERJ7, who saw the considerate bus driver, said:

    "While passing by the the River Valley High School construction site at Boon Lay Drive at 10.44am (Aug 21), I noticed bus captain of SBS No: 257 was asking a few of the construction workers to help him to guide him in reversing the bus to the position of the tree branch that was protruding.

    "He wanted to check if the height between the top of the bus and the tree is safe for the passengers sitting in the upper deck area as the bus drive pass that area.

    "He must have used his break time to drive the empty double decker bus along Boon Lay Drive to check if the bus would hit the tree branch that was protruding out if the bus were to drive pass.

    "Thanks to this considerate bus captain which put the safety of the passengers first, so that accidents could be prevented earlier in future."
