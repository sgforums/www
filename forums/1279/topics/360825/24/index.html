    Install LED panels at bus interchanges so commuters get updates on bus schedules

    http://singaporeseen.stomp.com.sg/singaporeseen/viewContent.jsp?id=79403

    STOMPer OK says LED panels should be installed at all bus interchanges to keep commuters up to date with the latest bus schedules.

    In an email to STOMP today (Jul 22), the STOMPer says:

    “Every day and night, especially during peak hours, commuters are hanging around at the bus interchange. You will easily see some commuters run from one queue to another to catch the bus.

    “Why this is happening so often is because there is no information displayed to let commuter know which bus is going to depart soon.

    “Since most of the new buses are using LED panels to display the service number and routing, designation etc, I think it is the time to implement a new LED display panel at all bus interchanges.

    “The existing display panel does not show the schedule and timing for the next bus arriving/departing.

    “It is very useful and important to let the commuter know about the bus schedule, every service route with the real time departing from interchange information. Commuters will then decide where to queue for the earlier bus departing soon.

    “Although some of the bus interchanges are already air-conditioned and upgrading works is on the way to provide better service to all commuters and staff, some important and useful equipment and facilities are still not in place.

    “Every day, it is common to see people standing around the queue booth but not in the queue. Why? Because they do not know where to queue and which bus is coming soon due to lack of information given.

    “LTA and the operators should speed up and improve the information system at the bus interchanges island wide as soon as possible.”
