    Motorists to give way to buses at 15 more bays

    SINGAPORE: Motorists will have to give way to buses at 15 more bays from Friday. This is under the revision of the Mandatory Give Way to Buses Scheme by the Land Transport Authority.

    Motorists have to give way to buses when they see the triangular markings.

    And soon, 15 bus bays along Pasir Ris Drive 1, Canberra Road and Woodlands Avenue will go under the scheme, bringing the total number to 41 bus bays island wide.

    The LTA said the scheme has been effective in improving overall bus speeds. Buses are now able to exit the bays faster by up to 73 per cent. - CNA/vm
