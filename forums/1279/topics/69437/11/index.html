    CBSBus Updates

    CBSBus is proud to announce the official opening of a new route: CBS21. This route is actually SBST12. So there's nothing much to fuss about. I did fine tune the fare stage at the Chinatown area due to the staggering of the bus stops.

    As for bus deployment. CBSBus now has
    21 Mk4 Striders: CBS 1000 U - CBS 1020 L
    25 Mk4 DM3500: CBS 1300 D - CBS 1324 K
    30 Merz O405G Volgren: CBS 2001 K - CBS 2030 B
    31 Volvo Olympian 3 axle: CBS 9000 S - CBS 9030 E

    All these buses will be parked and maintained at BNDEP & ARBP while the other depots are being built. CBS's BNDEP is located just next to St Anthony's Canossian Sec, on the former Ind Park land. In short, its a stone's throw from SBST's BNDEP. ARBP for CBS Bus is located next to the MOE Examinations Branch @Ayer Rajah Cres.

    34 of these buses are now on CBS20. Another 20 of these will be operated on CBS21.

    CBSBus Press Release: 22nd March 2004.
