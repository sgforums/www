    AM Peak: High volumes of HCJC NJC NYGH TCH students from Tampines to their schools. High Volumes of MJC students from PSR to MJC. Fortunately, 5 min frequency is enough to sustain the loads of working people from Tampines and Pasir Ris to Buona Vista/NUS and vice versa.
