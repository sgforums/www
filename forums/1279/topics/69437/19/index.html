    heard that CBS bus wants to set up a GPS network.

    would take some time now to describe the ZYXbus GPS network systems

    control centre:
    located at all depots. each depot controls its buses and buses are kept to within to 3 mins of its timetable.In the evnt that a control centre computers fails...another depot takes over operations immediately.

    (GPS alert system)
    since the GPS alert system is fully computerised, the system will send alert messages (or update the indiactor line) via GPS to drivers alerting of how late or early they are. in normal mode drivers just peek at a indicator line at the bottem of the computer screen of the GPS headset in bus. a blue line indicates too early . a green line means just right. red line means u r late!.

    in computer programming terms blue: 2 mins too early
    green 3 mins within schedule
    red - 2 mins too late

    lolz

    every bus has a in-bus GPS head set. the headset allows drivers to download destos (EDS), chat to other drivers (lolz), GPS alert system , downlaod in bus LCD display scrolling detaisl.. the headset also doubles up as the register like in sbst buses.drivers touch screen to issue cash paying bus tix thru the thing.
    mobitec EDS systems on some ZYXbus are also integrated with this GPS head set thingy.
    the headset includes: a flat screen display that includes a touch screen function, CPU, keyboard.

    the GPS system will automatically run the "in bus LCD system" . that means the in bus LCD system is connected to GPS to update details for next bus stop. it is very accurate.(in bus LCD system is this 2 line LCD screen in all ZYXbus that shows next stop detials and bus stopping)
    of course the GPS system automatically updates fare stages on the ticketing equipment

    the GPS system also programmed to calculate fares thru the EZ link system. (GPS is linked to EZ link fare collection system)
    good for revenue checks lolz
