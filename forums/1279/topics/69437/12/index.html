    Earlier, someone mentioned (maybe in another thread) about combining 502 and 111. I've taken the initiative to create a combined route.

    In the case of this combined 111 + 502, it will skip 2 pairs of bus stops along Napier Road in both directions.

    In the direction to Suntec, it is Red Plate and stops at Ghim Moh Terminal and south bound Ghim Moh Road.

    It uses White Plate in the direction to Boon Lay and stops only on the north bound Ghim Moh Road and alighting only at Ghim Moh Terminal.

    Services 74, 99 and 105 are used as precedents for fare stage calculations. I've assumed 0.5 fare stage between Clementi Avenue 2 and Clementi Road in both directions as there is no precedent set.

    See here for the Excel file:

    http://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/~yusryhar/Yusry%20Bus%20Services.xls
