    CBSBus Updates

    Handover of 3 Eunos Services

    CBS is proud to announce the handover of 3 of its Eunos services to TransCorp. The handover will take effect from 1st June 2004. This is to give newcomer TransCorp a head-start into the Transport Business in Singapore.

    The 3 services that will be run under TransCorp are
    CBS57: Eunos - Opera Estate (Loop)
    CBS58: Eunos - PLAB (Loop)
    CBS59: Eunos - Ubi Ind Estate (Loop)

    CBS would like to take this opportunity to give CBS57 CBS58 CBS59 complimentary rides on its last day of operation: 31st May 2004. An evening gathering will be held on that day @Eunos Interchange to thank the commuters for their continuous support of CBS.

    CBS has exclusive rights to operate KMB Buses

    CBS had signed an agreement with Kowloon Motor Bus, a major bus company in Hong Kong on 24th May 2004 for CBS to have exclusive rights to buy and use KMB buses on Singapore's roads.

    Soon, Singaporeans will be able to see Wright Olympian 3 axle and Dennis Dart 11.8m in Singapore come late June 2004. The fate of where these buses will be based at still remains a mystery.

    At a press conference, CBS CEO serviec_238 says,"It's a good tie-up between both companies to exchange bus ideas and co-operate the same bus models in both Hong Kong and Singapore. I'm looking forward into this partnership"

    KMB President and CEO couldnt agree more.

    In return, KMB gets to operate 50 Volvo Super Olympian with Volgren bodywork come July 2004. These buses will be built and assembled in Singapore before being sent to HK for revenue usage. These 50 dudes will not be operating as citybuses in Singapore.

    CBSBus Press Release: 26th May 2004
