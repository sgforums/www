    Gearbox cant change the sound of the engine . Each engine produces its own unique sound note .

    Take the B9TL for example . There are Voith-ed ones as well ZF-ed ones . But we cant really tell what gearbox they using until we hear them change gears . The idling and move off sound is identical .

    Also , take the Scania L113CRB (Sentosa) and SMRT's Scania L113CRL . The Sentosa one have manual gearbox , whereas SMRT's have auto . But both of them have the same engine sound .
