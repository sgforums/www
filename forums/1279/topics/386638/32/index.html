    accident: is a specific, unidentifiable, unexpected, unusual and unintended external action which occurs in a particular time and place, with no apparent and delib at all, or impedes the vehicle's operation so much, that it is very difficult or nearly impossible, or dangerous to operate, or else at risk of causing further damage to the vehicle.

    Breakdown: is the mechanical failure of a motor vehicle in such a way that the underlying problem prevents the vehicle from being operated at all, or impedes the vehicle's operation so much, that it is very difficult or nearly impossible, or dangerous to operate, or else at risk of causing further damage to the vehicle.

    In my own definition, breakdown is caused by the prevention of engine of vehicle being worked whereas accident is something which can prevent to be happened.
