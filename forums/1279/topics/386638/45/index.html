    SMB97G on 851(AMDEP 851) was involved in a minor accident with an SMRT cab when turning out of Bukit Merah Interchange. Bus and cab were both slightly scratched, EDS remained on. Several SMRT and SBS Transit Drivers gathered near the junction to witness the commotion when the cab driver and bus driver took pictures and settled the problem.
