    Bus: SBS 1989S
    Service: 66
    Date: 11 Mar 2010, Thursday
    Time: 0850 hrs (at time of witness)
    Location: Bedok North Ave 3, before junction with PIE

    Details:

    1. SBS 1989S was operating Service 66 headed for Jurong East when it was involved in an accident with a car and a private bus.
    2. Destos have been taken down at time of witness. All passengers have also alighted from the bus.
    3. Damage to the bus was substantial. It had a cracked front windscreen to the right (looking from back to front). The right headlight have also dropped off.
    4. The Traffic Police closed three of the four lanes, leaving only the right most lane passable. A massive traffic jam was created and stretched as far back as to New Upper Changi Road. Buses from Services 14, 17, 18, 28, 45, 66, 67, 69, 168, 222, 228 and 506 were affected.
