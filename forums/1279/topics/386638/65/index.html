    Breakdown Report - 10 October 2010

    Bus: TIB 1130J (Mercedes MB O405G with Hispano Habit body)

    Route: 856

    Direction: towards Woodlands

    Location: Admiralty Road West, Woodlands Auto Hub (47049)

    Reason: punctured tire.

    Comments: Affected commuters were ushered onto another bus (unidentified Mercedes MB O405G with Habit body). Another bus, an unidentified Scania L113CRL, was seen earlier fully loaded, apparently from the impact of TIB 1130J's breakdown. Bus was later driven away slowly from the bus stop with an SMRT maintenance lorry following behind, presumably to go back to Woodlands depot. A replacement bendy bus, TIB 855H (Mercedes MB O405G with Hispano body MK1) was seen doing a downroute at the same bus stop a few minutes later, apparently as a replacement for TIB 1130J. Talked with driver who didn't seem to know of the broken-down bus though. Will be posting photos later when I get home - am posting this from my iPhone; I was cycling from Woodlands Waterfront Park when I saw the bus breakdown.
