    Jam due to 6-vehicle accident

    A DELIVERY van hit one car before swerving into a row of vehicles yesterday evening.

    The accident involving six vehicles happened at the junction of North Canal Road and Eu Tong Sen Street.

    The Opel van had been travelling along North Canal Road at about 6.15pm when the driver suddenly lost control of his vehicle and slammed into a Honda sedan travelling in front of him.

    The van then swerved into the adjacent Eu Tong Sen Street, where it was involved in another series of collisions involving four other vehicles.

    The van first collided with a taxi, smashing into it head-on and shattering its windscreen. The impact pushed the taxi into a Nissan sedan, bursting its right tyre and damaging the right side of its body.

    The van also hit a motorcycle before finally crashing into an SMRT bus, denting it and shattering its windows.
