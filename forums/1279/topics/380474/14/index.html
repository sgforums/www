    On one hand it's okay to take outside Boon Lay Interchange. Often, lots of people exploit this particular area and they all go home without incident.

    But on the other hand, it's easy for the Transit Security ("TS") to look at what's outside (especially Toa Payoh). While taking photos, you need to keep an eye on what's happening inside the interchange. When the TS comes out, it's time for you to run and look for another spot.

    Those photos that you saw are mostly taken in the mid-afternoon, sometime around 2 to 4 (some go till 5). For that spot, the best time to take is right now. You have till next year mid-Mar to do so.
