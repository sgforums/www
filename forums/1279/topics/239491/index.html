    Originally posted by Raraken:
    It looks as if the bus has one entrance step oni....
    Yes, it did have only one step, both on the entrance and exit, just like the Dennis Lance. However, unlike the Lance, the gangway was flat throughout. The seats at the rear half were placed on a raised platform, like the O405, and it had a horizontal engine, also like the O405. The Lynx ceased production in favour of Volvo's similar model, the B10B.
