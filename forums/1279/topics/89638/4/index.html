    Service 183 buses use the SBSL livery buses from ex-Express 507. In other words, the buses of Express 507 are now deployed on service 183.

    This is what happens to service 183 buses at Kent Ridge:

    Along Clementi Rd, the bus will turn into the bus bay at the stop just before Kent Ridge terminal, regardless of whether there are passengers boarding/alighting here or not. This is because the bus captain needs to flip over the side desto (side desto is both-sides printed) for the change of direction.

    The bus will then turn left into Kent Ridge terminal, but no boarding and alighting is allowed here, the bus will then exits the terminal by turning right into Clementi Rd and call at the bus stop opposite the terminal, and then continue to Jurong East interchange.
