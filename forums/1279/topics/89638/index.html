    With effect from Sunday 22th August 2004, New Trunk Service 183 will be introduced to cater to the rapid development and commuter demand of Toh Guan Ind Estate.

    It provides links between Toh Guan and Jurong East, Clementi and Kent Ridge.

    On the same day, Express Service 507 will be withdrawn.

    183: Jurong East - Kent Ridge (Loop)
    Via Jurong East St 13, Jurong East Central, Toh Guan Rd, Toh Guan Rd East, Old Toh Tuck Rd, Toh Tuck Ave, C'wealth Ave West, Clementi Rd (Loop)

    Operating hours: 0545 - 2330 hrs daily
    Peak: 12 min
    Off Peak: 15 min
