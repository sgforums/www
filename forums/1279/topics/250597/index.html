    Originally posted by Volvo Olympian:
    Take 965/969 to Sembawang Road (Chong Pang bus stop) change to 167/980 and alight outside ''The Springside'' Bus stop.
    Route 965 doesn't go to Chong Pang Camp, but you can still take it to Yishun Avenue 2 and transfer to route 169. My recommendation, though, is to take route 969 from there, alight at Chong Pang Camp (the Air Force camp), and then transfer to either routes 167 or 980. Ask the bus driver where to alight if you are not sure, but you should be able to catch sight of the camp's entrance just before it's stop (tip: look on your right for a Nee Soon Camp signboard).
