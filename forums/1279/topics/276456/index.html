    In the early 90s, SBS sold 100 Units of Leyland Atlantean AN68.2R / Alexander L type to HK CityBus. But due to CityBus practise, these 100 L type had their front rebuilt and thus, they had a R Type front while their stay in HK CityBus.

    After the LAs retired from their service in CityBus, most of them had been scrapped and a few lucky units managed to find their to South Africa, having to start a new life, serving the people there.

    Based on AMDEP, BBDEP and BNDEP. Mainly on Bt Batok, CCK, Bedok, Tampines services, also on selected AMK and Toa Payoh services too.

    Mercedes Benz 0305
    Based mainly on HGDEP and minority on AMDEP. Mainly on Hougang services. Also spotted on Eunos, Sims Ave and selected AMK and Bedok services.

    Leyland Oylampians
    Based mainly on old Jurong bus park (now SLDEP) and Toa Payoh depot (now BRBP). Mainly on Jurong services (regradless of trunk, feeder or JIS) and Toa Payoh services.
