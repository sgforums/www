    There were 2 versions of the NZMB bodywork on the B57. The 1st version had a raised canopy and flat roof. The 2nd version had a curved roof and its windsrceen is slightly shallower to fit the desto wihout raising the canopy. This was the type which was also fitted on the 1417. It was impossible to tell which was which if you saw one approaching with all its badges missing; which was very common as the buses near retirement. When new, the rear was used to display the service number. It was later disused and on some buses, they were even sealed up.
