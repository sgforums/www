    Ten passengers hurt in accident involving 2 SBS Transit buses

    Ten passengers were hurt when 2 SBS Transit buses collided along Bedok Reservoir Road on Monday morning.

    One of the bus drivers told the police that he was unable to stop in time and had bumped into the other bus.

    SBS Transit will also pay for the medical expenses of the 10 injured passengers. - CNA
