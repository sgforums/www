    Don't compare Merz with N113CRB for acceleration , as the comparison is unfair . Take it that both buses have same horsepower ratings and torque . The merz will still win. Why? The Merz have a Hub Reduction axle. What the Hub Reduction axle does is that it reduces rotational speed while increasing torque . A higher torque level = better acceleration . Hence the merz may win in the end.
