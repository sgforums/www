    On another note,

    Today is the first weekday (because 05 Oct was a Sun & PH at that) that Sv 39 & 109 are stopping at the new TPE stops. From what I saw, there were 3x LTA officers sitting at the bus stop a distance apart monitoring the situation (either they split the 5 new svcs stopping there among themselves, or they're monitoring all the svcs stopping there collectively).

    But from what I can foresee, the bus stop at TPE would be more beneficial for services like Sv 109 & Sv 168, because they tend to be like 80-85% during AM Peak before they enter TPE from PSR / Tampines. So at least there's space still for pax to board at the TPE stops.

    But for Sv 39 whereby typically the bus is fully packed even before it reaches PSR Dr 12 in direction of Yishun, I don't really see the benefit of having that stop for Sv 39 during AM Peak.
    Because high chance there wouldn't be anyone alighting there from Sv 39 anyway (& in turn no one can board that bus to Yishun from TPE bus stop too). Doesn't help too that the bus took additional 4mins because of that stop, but somehow overall runtime for Sv 39 on e-tt remains the same. 

    So Idk, maybe only for the TPE bus stop on the other direction, and during Off-Peak / Weekends, the TPE stop is more beneficial. it's good for the passengers living at the area for more connectivity. But not all the services will benefit from the stop in my opinion.
