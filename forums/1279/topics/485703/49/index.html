    BSEP changes from 15th February 2015:

    - Feeder 386 will be extended from Edgefield Plains to ply Punggol Drive, Punggol Field and looping at Punggol East.

    - Feeder 301 and Service 983 will be introduced to ply between Choa Chu Kang and Keat Hong Close (road extended from Choa Chu Kang Ave 5); Feeder 301 using Choa Chu Kang Ave 3 while Service 983 using Choa Chu Kang Ave 4. (Note: LTA Facebook page has mentioned that there are intentions for future extensions of Service 983 to Bukit Panjang and complementing the Downtown Line Stage 2.

    - Express 982E will be extended to start and end at bus stops along Keat Hong Close.

    - With the introduction of Feeder 301, Feeder 300 will be converted back into a G/W plate service (first in SMRT!), plying mainly Choa Chu Kang Ave 4, Choa Chu Kang Ave 1, Choa Chu Kang Ave 2 and Choa Chu Kang Way; it will skip one portion of Choa Chu Kang Ave 3 and Choa Chu Kang Ave 5.

    Review of services introduced under BSEP for SMRT: 

    803 - rating:good; doing okay, will gain more momentum once occupancy levels along ave 6 and st 31 increases

    860 - rating: excellent; loading very high to and from Khatib during peak hours; also provides relief to 804/806 along ring road towards Yishun. Need high capacity buses and loading will continue to increase given the high number of HDBs up for development and occupancy in the region.

    904 - rating:good; loading average, can expect to increase once HDBs along the route are complete and occupancy levels increase. 

    972 - rating: excellent; loading very high towards/from CBD during AM/PM respectively; provides as good alternative to 922 to BPJ. Once DTL2 is up, loading towards BPJ will increase; loading will continue to increase given new condos/HDBs will have occupancy increase in coming months around Fajar/Senja/Segar

    990 - rating: poor to average; route is quite unique but from what I read and see, route hasn't got much traction yet. Route could have been planned longer to start from CCK via Gombak or BPK via Hillview. This would have helped relieve load on other services. Also opposite direction to 189 around BBT Industrial does not help with relieving load on 189.
