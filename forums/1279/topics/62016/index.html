Services 97, 107 and Express 507 should merge

  • tranquilice
    16 Nov 03, 03:03
    Service 97 which plys between Jurong East and Marina Centre, service 107 which plys between Hougang Central and Shenton Way/Marina Centre, and Express 507 which plys between Boon Lay and Shenton Way, should be merged into one service Hougang Central - Boon Lay.

    The new service will bring back the link of former service 97 route between Hougang Central and HarbourFront, AYE and Jurong East.

    Route via service 107 route to Shenton Way, then follow by service 97 route to Jurong East Central, finally follow by service 507 route to Boon Lay. Marina Centre, Toh Guan and Jurong East interchange will be skipped.
