    Originally posted by SMRT BUSES LuvER:

    hey, just to ask, how do i see the registration numbers of a vehicle at ONEMotoring website? thx in advance.

    https://vrl.lta.gov.sg/lta/vrl/action/pubfunc?ID=EnquireTransferFee

    Enter unregistered bus rego + suffix (Eg. SBS8787L) and enter any 'transfer date'.

    If the bus is registered, it will show 'lifespan expirey date' if not, they will show 'No vehicle in database.'
