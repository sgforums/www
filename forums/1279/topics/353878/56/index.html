    12 Facts about SMRT's OC500LE (Update) :

    1. Batch 1 OCs are starting to 'lose' the rear door closing announcement.
    2. SMB5Y (KJDEP 172) has higher rear SMRT logo than the rest.
    3. SMB4A (AMDEP 851) & SMB61J (KJDEP 307) does not have rear SMRT logo.
    4. I've noticed that when the BC pulled the handbrake when it stops, the bus will go 'Tsssss... POOOOoooooo..(Imagine hearing a person fart.)' Noticed at SMB40U (KJDEP 307) & SMB61J (KJDEP 307)
    5. Batch 2 OCs have at least 4 CCTV cameras installed inside the interior while Batch 1 OCs does not (excluding SMB1H).
    6. SMB1H (KJDEP 172) is the only bus with 2 extra poles installed located at the 2 PIW slot.
    7. Service 307 is the first svc to have full OC (Batch 1) fleet, and then svc 945.
    8. SMB3C (AMDEP 67) & SMB53H (KJDEP 945) are the first buses to have full body advert while SMB24S (AMDEP 171) & SMB39A (KJDEP 307) are the first buses to have full rear advert.
    9. The first few Batch 2 OCs have a dual bus stopping chime.
    10. SMB42P (KJDEP 307)'s front A/C duct (near the 1st PIW slot) is starting to fall off.
    11. SMB34M (AMDEP 67) is the only Batch 1 OC to have new Batch 2 OC's PIW leather covers.
    12. Previously, all Batch 1 OCs have an odd design of the driver's window. Now all the buses have been replaced by the new, simple ones.

    for the bus stopping chime, it's actually fixed to both the exit and the entrance. however, the Batch 2's ones, for some of them, doesn't seem to be synchronised..

    fyi, for the Batch 2, there is a problem present for the KJ ones. which is about the doors closing chime. a few scenarios:

    • when both doors are open, press the door button each at a time. the chime will sound.
    • when both doors are open, press Master close, the chime shuts itself up.
    • for some buses only. when entrance is shut, but only open exit, when close, the chime shuts itself up. but for the rest of them, the chime will still be played.

    the newer batch performs weirdly nowadays..despite the age as compared to the newest Batch 1 bus, though its just a few months..
