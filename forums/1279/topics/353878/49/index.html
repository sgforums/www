    10 Facts about SMRT's OC500LE:

    1. Batch 1 OCs are starting to 'lose' the rear door closing announcement.
    2. SMB5Y (KJDEP 172) has higher rear SMRT logo than the rest.
    3. SMB4A (AMDEP 851) & SMB61J (KJDEP 307) does not have rear SMRT logo.
    4. I've noticed that when the BC pulled the handbrake when it stops, the bus will go 'Tsssss... POOOOoooooo..(Imagine hearing a person fart.)' Noticed at SMB40U (KJDEP 307) & SMB61J (KJDEP 307)
    5. Batch 2 OCs have at least 4 CCTV cameras installed inside the interior while Batch 1 OCs does not (excluding SMB1H).
    6. SMB1H (KJDEP 172) is the only bus with 2 extra poles installed located at the 2 PIW slot.
    7. Service 307 is the first svc to have full OC (Batch 1) fleet, and then svc 945.
    8. SMB3C (AMDEP 67) & SMB53H (KJDEP 945) are the first buses to have full body advert while SMB24S (AMDEP 171) & SMB39A (KJDEP 307) are the first buses to have full rear advert.
    9. The first few Batch 2 OCs have a dual bus stopping chime.
    10. Last but not least, the OC's front is similar to CapaCity front. ^^
