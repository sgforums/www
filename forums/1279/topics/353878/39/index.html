    You mean its rear axle? The rims for the rear is about the same for the OCs and O405s. Its only the axle thats different. The OC uses a single-drive hypoid axle. It doesnt have any more gears than a hub-reduction axle in O405s. The O405s hub-reduction axle have 3 or 4 additional gears at both axle ends, hence it looks different than most conventional axles. The gear reduction allows more torque, but at the cost of lower speed. The OC's axle also uses disc brakes while the O405's uses drum brakes.
