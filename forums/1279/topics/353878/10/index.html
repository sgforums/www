                                                    My Review For SMRT's OC500LE

    Good points:

    > Exterior of bus:

    •  Superbly design Citaro Capacity-like front
    •  A 'smile' shape at the back of bus
    • Nice looking Konvekta A/C
    • Cool third-braking light

    > Interior of bus:

    • Cold A/C (especially SMB59S)
    • Black coloured seats designed for priority seats

    Bad points:

    > Exterior of bus:

    • Looks better with silver rims than white rims...
    • Small rear SMRT logo
    • Rear of bus gets very dirty if not cleaned for months

    > Interior of bus:

    • A/C duct similar to Hispano Habit's (hard to configure)
    • Seats behind driver is hard & can only occupy one person (depending on the size of the person)
    • Lesser seats because of 2 PIW slots
    • Seats before the steps are low & have to raise your head up to see outside the bus

    Overall:
    Equipped with ZF6HP592C, the bus could go very fast on a straight road! Legroom is sufficient & comfortable in all seating places except the seats behind the driver's seat.
