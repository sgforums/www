    Woodlands Rd (Bet Woodlands Ave 3 & Woodlands Ctr Rd/ BKE/ Woodlands Crossings, WDL Ctr bound): Restricted to M'sian lorries (left lane) & public buses (right lane) on Weekday restricted hours.

    Woodlands Centre Rd (Towards Woodlands Rd): No right turn to Woodlands Crossing except public buses & M'sian school buses during restricted hours. Cars are to form up from BKE.
