    Since BNDEP keeps having fleet swaps here and there everytime, the years for the long perms might differ. For some of the east side buses that have remained despite new WABs coming in (that i can recall),

    752P & 886L (BN 39) - Deployed on 39 since launch, never left. Not sure if both were also from the AMDEP days of 39? 

    858T (BN 359) - Still on 359 now despite its siblings of 611M 663M 730C 841R 870G & countless others all deregistered and gone already. Even more so due to the influx of KUBs in recent years, this Mk3 is still actively serving its loyal perm till now. Not sure for how many years already, but when i came into this hobby in 2004, it was already on 359.

    1972P (BN 21) - The only bus that remained on 21 till now despite new KUB & CDGE B9TLs entering since 2006. All those from the same period all have gone elsewhere (think 986G, 987D, 2790R, 9811T, 9370Z, 9313P etc). Were there any previous perms for this bus?

    9354X & 9437R (BN 12)  - Same as 9450B, remained on 12 till now even when CDGE B9s entered and kicked out the rest of the VO3xs.

    9629D & 9631X (BN 17) - Despite Wrights & KUBs entering 17, these 2 have remained on 17 till now. I know they have a certain previous perm previously (106 or something), but IIRC when 17 first got DDs on its current route, these 2 were the initial ones allocated and remained till today.

    Thanks if anyone could clarify on these buses
