    Take 26 to Bedok Int.

    1. Transfer to 35, alight at Xilin Ave, walk in.
    2. Walk out to the bus stop outside the int (New Upp Changi Rd, Bedok MRT), transfer to 24, alight at Upp Changi Rd East opp Expo Hall 4/5/6, walk in.

    PPS: both services go past Tanah Merah MRT. U can alight there & take the shuttle too.
