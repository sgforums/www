  • hseochin
    12 Aug 05, 00:14
    Very briefly, the basic differences :

    O 306 - front engine OM 352 of in-line 6-cylinders 5675 cc
    130 BHP ~ 500 Nm, spring suspension. Produced in 1970's.

    O 305 - rear horizontally mounted boxer-style engine OM 407 h/hA of in-line 6
    11412 cc of 240 to 280 BHP ~ 900 Nm, air suspension with optional
    engine encapsulation. Produced till around 1985.

    O 405 - as per O 305 but using OM 447 h/hA/hLA of 11967 cc of 250 to 299 BHP
    ~ 1000 Nm with up to Euro-2 emission standards. Produced since
    around 1985 & still available in certain markets.
