    CNG Bus Explosion puts 22 CNG buses off the roads

     

    LTA has issued a ban of a model line of CNG Buses today pending investigations of an explosion which involves a CNG bus, killing the driver last month.

     

    The bus models manafcatured by King Long were pulled off the roads as a safety measure while investigation are ongoing. These buses operates under Sun Gee Travel. Sun Gee declined to comment on the incident.

     

    The buses has been on the roads for around 2 - 3 years and has served tourists and delegates for events such as the recent Youth Olympic Games.

     

    Concern is raised on the safety of CNG vehicles after another explosion involving a CNG bus occured the same week in Seoul, South Korea sending nearby pedestrians into panic. Only 18 people were injured.

     

    SBST's 12 units of Volvo B10BLE CNG models are not affected by this ban.
