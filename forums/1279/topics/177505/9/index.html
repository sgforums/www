    Things I do know about the B9TL that is unique.

    1. Its 0.05m fatter.
    2. Its taller by a bit.
    3. Unlike most buses that roll out at the beginning, the B9TL will have a low-profile front and central axle. (no wheel-rim covers)
    3. The gearboxes to be used, never used before in SBS, namely the ZF6HP500, and the 4-speed Voith.
    4. The engine also first-timer, its a D9A, 9400cc, which can deliver 300bhp (SBST turned down an offer for 340bhp), previous Volvo buses use the D10A.
    5. First bus to use full one-piece windscreen.
    6. First DD in almost a decade to use rear window.
    7. And of course the wheel-chair ramp.
