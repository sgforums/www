Hmmm.... actually the B9TLs has one of the most powerful engine in SBSTransits' DD fleet. Wiki says it has a maximum bhp of 340, more than the Trident.     It is possible to get some extra power from an engine; by installing an intercooler, for example. However, torque is a different story. How much torque an engine produce is dependent on the stroke of the piston. Larger stroke = larger torque. The B9 is offered with a 300 hp or a 340 hp engine. SBST chose the former. The Cummins M11 on the Trident is rated at 305 hp. When comparing power for size, the Volvo D9A300 is better.
