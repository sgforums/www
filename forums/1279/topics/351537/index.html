    Bus ride was too cold for comfort

    I HAVE always felt that the air in public buses is too cold and a ride last Monday evening on SBS Transit service 151 from the National University of Singapore towards Hougang confirmed my view.

    The air-conditioning inside added to the already wet and cold weather outside. It was cold from the moment I stepped on board.

    One passenger asked the bus driver if he could raise the temperature of the air-conditioner. He said he could not as the temperature setting was unfortunately not within his control - a fact that surprised me.

    I hope that public transport companies will look into this matter to make travelling by bus a more comfortable and enjoyable experience for commuters. It also saves energy.

    Nuurun Naqiah Muhammad Sadiq Toh (Miss)
