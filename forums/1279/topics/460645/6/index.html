    Game rules according to SBS7888K and SBS3174M:

    1. Starting point: Yio Chu Kang Bus Terminal.

    2. In total there are 2 teams. Each team will be given one clue and has to find the interchange accordingly to the clue given. 

    3. The clue may be in the form of

    • Rego and depot
    • Service and depot

     

    4. There will be a total of 5 clues and there is only one clue for each interchange. Meaning to say, there will be 5 interchange destinations which the teams have to find.

    5. Only buses are permitted. No MRTs, taxis or cars (if you are rich enough) is allowed at all times.

    6. The team which will win will be based on the shortest time taken to reach the destination. If the teams arrive at the same time, the winning team will be decided based on the total amount of money spent on transportation (lowest expenditure will win, of cos').
