  • ZYX2005

    Service was green Bus 1

    Then later became UBC svc 170

    Then SBS svc 170

     

    bus fleets / TMO to "OTS"

    late 1970s to early 80s - mercedes benz coach looking OF1413s
    middle 80s to early 90s - TMO o9peration with conductor handing out colourful SBS newer style bus tickets - fleet used B57s

    early 90s to middle 90s - Volvo B10M mark IIs. Conductor still ard till 1997. hence SBS conductor series tickets i have a lots.
    late 90s to new millennium - Mark 3 - no more conductor onbaord. Normal issuing ticket style.

    ZYX
