    Source: www.transitlink.com.sg

    I've just completed my studies. Can I still use the card?

    Congratulations on completing your studies! Not to worry, the School Smartcard can be used for a further 4 months upon completion of studies. The card can be further extended for students attending JC/CI/Pre-U.

    For Primary School Smartcard, concession fare will be deducted during the 4 months and Secondary concession pass can be purchased.

    For other School Smartcards, Adult fares will be deducted during the 4 months and concession passes cannot be purchased.

    We would like to kindly advise all Secondary 1 students to return their old Primary School Smartcard to TransitLink once they have received their new Secondary School Smartcard. At any point of time during the 4 months grace period, you may request for an immediate refund (if any) at any TransitLink Ticket Office or Concession Card Replacement Office. But once the 4 month period is up, your card will expire and cannot be used or refunded immediately. You will then be issued a receipt and the refund can be collected after 5 working days from any TransitLink Ticket Office or Concession Card Replacement Office.

    *Note: Students whose School Smartcards are linked to GIRO or Auto Credit Card will also need to present their cards at the Sales Machines or Counters (TransitLink Ticket Office/Concession Card Replacement Office, Add Value Machine Plus, General Ticketing Machine and Passenger Service Centre).

    Students and Parents can call the TransitLink Hotline at 1800–2255 663 for more information.
