    Just a little trivia of these buses...

    SBS 208 U: used to be a Toa Payoh Feeder Service bus when the Scanias are getting their makeover into being a AC bus. It was on Svc 56 for quite some time.

    SBS 441 L: It was a permenent Svc 56 bus in the mid-90s. I'm not sure when it was transferred to Svc 26. For now, its permenent on Svc 26.

    SBS 443 G - It used to be on Svc 130. I remembered that SBS 44x are on Svc 130.

    SBS 9144 K - It used to have a Carlsberg ad when it was serving Svc 56 in the late-90s.

    SBS 9164 C - It served Svc 31 until 2001 when the Volvo 2-axle migrated elsewhere and Mk3 replaced them. For your info, the Volvo 2-axles that served Svc 31 are from SBS 7281 R - SBS 7288 T.
