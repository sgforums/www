    Originally posted by service_238:
    I've been noticing since August/September this year about this NEW discovery.

    In the evening peak on weekdays, Service 238 has a crossover bus from Service 26. The plate is SBS 512 R, before that it was used to be SBS 323 U. The bus will arrive around 1810 hrs at the feeder services alighting area instead of the Svc 26 berth. The bus driver will then change the plate to 238 before running into service at around 1816 hrs. The bus will make 3 trips of Svc 238 before returning to the depot at around 2000 hrs.

    Interested parties, visit TP Int at 6pm tml to find out.
    This is the same case for some VO 3-axles of Service 30. The buses will serve Service 18 shortly after the buses arrive from Boon Lay at around 6pm. The buses will continue serving Service 18 until around 8, and straight to the depot after that. On certain occasions, the buses will serve Service 18 first before crossing over to Service 30 at around 8am.

    The arrangement for Service 222 Superbuses was the same as Service 18, before the service got its perm Superbuses ie SBS9211A, SBS9300B & SBS9587R recently.

    In summary, both Services 18 & 222 shared Superbuses with Service 30, with only Services 18 & 30 sharing Superbuses up to this day.
