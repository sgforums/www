    Automatic updating of bus fare stages, real—time bus arrival info hit by software glitch

    SINGAPORE: If you had problems scanning your EZ—Link card early Saturday morning, not to worry. The problem is not with the card.

    The Land Transport Authority said a software glitch had disabled the automatic updating of the bus fare stages.

    And drivers had to manually update the stages so that fares could be deducted accordingly.

    LTA said the software glitch had been resolved. Automatic updating of bus fare stages will be fully operational once again by day’s end.

    Real—time bus arrival information, which was also affected, will only be available when all the buses are switched back to the automatic update mode. — CNA/ir
