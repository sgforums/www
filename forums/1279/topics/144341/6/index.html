    Originally posted by muziq bus:

    the scheduled interval for 174 is about 13-17mins interval. So if you happen to encounter a breakdown, or even the one in front 3mins ahead of schedule & the one behind 10mins behind schedule, that'll total up to about 30mins. The same goes for the other direction, because of the Orchard Rd jam, the BCs can be sometimes up to 15mins late upon reaching NBR Ter, even if he leaves immediately for the return trip, he'll still be about 5 mins late (usual breaks for 174 at NBR Ter is about 10mins, so 15-10=5 ), encounter another jam of both human & vehicular traffic at around Somerset & Orchard area, and he'll be 15-20mins late by the time he reaches Farrer Rd.
    I've even encountered breakdowns of 2 consecutive buses at Boon Lay Int before. The total interval between the bus in front & the next bus at Boon Lay Int is 23mins even though the incoming BC only took a 1min break. Plus the extra load because of the huge gap caused even more delay.
