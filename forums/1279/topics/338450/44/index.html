    For X not equal 9,

    _ _ _ X -> _ _ _ X+1 is -2   eg   SBS3826R to SBS3827M is -2

    _ _ X 9 -> _ _ X+1 0 is -4   eg   SBS149G to SBS150B is -4

    _ X 9 9 -> _ X+1 0 0 is +3   eg   SBS599R to SBS600U is +3

    X 9 9 9 -> X+1 0 0 0  is no change  eg TIB999B to TIB1000B no change.

    start off this way first. i did this by trial and error
