    Today's experience in OC:

    This morning (SMB40U on 307) :

    • The ride was quiet. Not so many people.
    • When the bus was stopping at the traffic light before Northvale condo, there was another bus (private bus) stopping side by side. When the light turn green, the bus sped off so fast (like racing with the other bus).
    • The bad thing...I noticed the letter of some stickers (MIND THE STEPS, .etc) have been removed. Probably people step on it. The sticker at the front part (DO NOT SIT), the letter 'T' has been removed to 'DO NO   SIT'.

    Afternoon (SMB40U on 307...again)

    • Waited the bus for so long... The sun was too hot! When bus arrived, notice another bus (probably, SMB42P) stopped behind the bus at the same bus stop.
    • After the bus left the bus stop, the bus was so noisy and crowded!!! The wheelchair button was pressed 5-6 times!!! Always hear the 'ti ti ti ti ti ti ti ti ti' sound. That's not all...
    • After that no more 'ti ti ti' sound until a few minutes later, the wheelchair button was pressed again. This time the 'ti ti ti' sound couldn't stop until the driver tell the passengers to stop pressing the wheelchair button.
    • Almost every stop I hear 'Please move to the rear for the benefit of boarding passengers'.
    • Until Lot 1 bus stop, no more passengers, peace and quiet...
    • Probably the worst OC experience in my life.

    WHAT A DAY!
