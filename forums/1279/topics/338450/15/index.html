  • Scania N113CRB luver
    8 Dec 08, 12:52

    Those grey boxes formed behind the engine cover is probably dust. Dust is usually attracted there due to the heat on the engine cover. A surface with high heat tends to attract dust or sometimes, carbon molecules. Over time this forms a layer of "dust".

    The shapes of boxes you see is the shape of the frame of the rear cover. The lines werent covered in dust is because this areas have lower temperature (which was insulated by the frame), than its surroundings (those without the framework behind it).
