  • The_Bus_Guide

    Originally posted by freakybuses:
    Bukit Batok Terminal in Bukit Batok Ctrl... somewhere at the current West Mall site.
    Bt Batok Terminal was located along Bt Batok West Ave 3. It was near the present Bt Batok Polyclinic. There is an HDB Brickworks along BB West Ave 3. On a small road which led into the brickworks, you can see a small cul-de-sac. This was where the buses turn around. Sv. 122 was the first to terminate there in 1985.

    Adding on temporary terminals. Jurong East Terminal was located at Jurong East Central outside Blk 102. Tampines Terminal was at Tampines Ave 5 near Blk 147.
