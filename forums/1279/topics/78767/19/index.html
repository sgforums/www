    I can answer that for you...
    This duct is for the aircon system on test in this bus,it has appeared since last year. In the early stages of testing, there are 2 paper sheets saying " Air-Con on Test" in the interior of the bus. Now, the sheets have been removed for a few months.I have only seen SBS79A has this air-con test.
    Result of this air-con test ( based on my observation):
    - During night-time/rainy days, water droplets gathered on the air-con itself, dripping all the way to the ground. Surrounding seats cant be seated.
    - It is quite cooling , extremely freezing at cool weathers... You can see the window panes misty.
    - Havent catch this bus lately, dont know what is the latest performance.

    How to describe the air-con in the interior? It is just a white flat machine on top, with cold air coming out of the visible "holes".

    Ratings: Quite cold, but main problem is having dews on the flat machine at night or cold weathers, causing water to splash all over the rear of the bus.
