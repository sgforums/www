    Serving You Better, New Service 181

    Starting Sunday, 9th Jan 2005, 10.30am, New Service 181 will be introduced to ply between Jurong West Neighbourhood 2 and Boon Lay Interchange.

    With the new service, residents of Jurong West Neighbourhood 2 will enjoy (a) new link to Gek Poh Ville Neighbourhood Centre and an additional link to Boon Lay Interchange.

    Service 181 will on trial for a period of 6 months.

    Operational details:

    Boon Lay Int via
    Jurong West St 64
    Jurong West St 75
    Jurong West Ave 5
    Jurong West Ave 3

    From Boon Lay Interchange:
    6am - 12 midnight daily

    Frequency:
    12 - 15 mins
