New road paves new way for Service 806

  • mercedes_hispano
    10 Feb 05, 03:24
    A new road is going to be up, which will affect Service 806. The new road is going to be bulit somewhere just before the bus stop opposite Blk 367. Excavation works to clear the forested land are already under way. The road will link Yishun Ave 6 (right at the very back) to Yishun Ring Road.

    So, instead of turning into Yishun Ring Road from Yishun Ave 11, Service 806 will go straight until it meets Yishun Ave 6, making a left turn towards North View Primary.

    Yishun Int ---> Yishun Central ---> Yshun Ave 11 (A little portion of the old 804 route) ---> Yishun Ave 6 (Serving Blks 405, North View Pri and Blk 391) ---> ---> Yishun Ring Road (Blk 314) ---> Yishun Ave 9 ---> Yishun Central ---> Yishun Ave 2 ---> Yishun Int.
