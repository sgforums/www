    For those people who saw a thousand odd pupils from various school at Raffles Place MRT Platform, they are NE Show Spectators that schools asked SMRT to do a special train on each line to transport the P5 spectators to Marina Promenade. ACS (Junior), Jiemen, Baleister Hill, Fernvale are one of the schools that was granted their demand. 3 trains were used. 1 train from Yishun, 1 train from Pasir Ris and 1 from Boon Lay. The first batch of students at the start point will be ushered into the train by NDP Facilitators. This train, like I said it's special and no adults or normal passengers are allowed in the train. The kids will be seated, with the exception of the Northbound students, that needs to stand like a normal peak-hour train. Well, the kids will be seated on the seats and ON THE FLOOR!

    Well, luckily only Siemens and Kawasaki C151 Refurbished trains are used. T
