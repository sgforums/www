    I have some new bus models I've "created" as follows:
    B12TL- A 12 liter (11.6liter actually) bus with a stronger chassis than the B10TL, radiator at offside.engine at the back. Summary is that a 12 liter bus with stronger chassis. Can be fitted with a ZF 4/5/6 speed gearbox or A 3/4/5 speed Voith gearbox. Body can be anything like ALX 500, Volgern CR 221LD/222LD/223LD/224LD and etc to something like Berkhof.

    Citaro XS- Also known as the Citaro Access, XS means Super-small. This bus can Access anywhere cos its only 9.4 meters long. It is also Accessable to the disabled cos it has a full length low-floor. Fitted with a 8 liters engine, also known as the O.555LE (I don't like the C for Citaro series buses). Can be fitted with a ZF 2/3/4/5 speed gearbox or a Aliison 4/5 speed gearbox. Body can use a shortened Citaro body (Go to the Mercedes Bus website to see the body) or Plaxton or Volgern Cr228L (shortened) or Wright Explorer and all the bodies that can be fitted on the Darts SLF( found in the Dennis bus Website)

    If you have your own bus models and you want to share, please post here, Thank You
