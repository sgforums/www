  • Yusry
    13 Mar 04, 22:50
    I was on service 111 from Buona Vista to Suntec just now.

    Basically, the jam started from Scotts Road / Orchard Road junction. For this stretch, the cause was due to taxis turning into Lucky Plaza and cars jammed up by the jam further down Orchard Road.

    The jam further down Orchard Road is caused mainly by vehicles turning into CTE and incoming vehicles from service roads trying to cut filter to the left most lane to get to CTE.

    It's smooth flowing after Buyong Road / Orchard Road for the 200meters. The next jam was caused by cars filtering left to enter Oldham Lane (entrance Plaza Singapura carpark). Then it's slightly smooth flowing until we hit Bencoolen Street. Here, many vehicles filter left, right, centre, anywhere, to enter the roads at the next junction.

    The biggest jam was the Suntec City jam. This needs no further explanation.

    All in all, it took me 1 hr 40 mins to get to Suntec, with about 1hr 10 mins stuck in jams.
