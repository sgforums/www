    Not the OP here but I believe I have solved this quiz:

    Road A: Holland Ave (svc 48 does not traverse the entire length of this road)
    Services B, C, D, H, J, and the similarity between D and J,
    (in order) 61, 106, 970, 122, 186
    Similarity between D and J: From junction G, both services travel along Commonwealth Ave, Alexandra Rd, and also Church St/Raffles Quay to terminal F (Cecil St/Cross St in the other direction)
    Interchange E: Bukit Batok Int
    Terminals F, I:
    Shenton Way Ter, New Bridge Rd Ter
    Junction G: Queensway X Commonwealth Ave
    MRT Station K: Telok Ayer MRT
