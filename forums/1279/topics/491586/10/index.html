    SBS Transit bus services A, B, and C originate from Bus Interchange D located in Town D. Service A turns left from Interchange D, while B and C turn right from Interchange D. A, B and C are trunk loop services.

    Service A's first stop from Interchange D is on Road G. Service B also stops at that bus stop, but in the returning direction. Services A and C do not meet at all except at Interchange D.

    Service A will then go to a town north of Town D, then onto Expressway E to another town further east, then onto Expressway F to its looping point. At its last bus stop before leaving town D, only Service A stops there.

    Service B and C duplicate each other in opposite directions (i.e. both services duplicate each other on Road G, but C has already looped and is heading back to Interchange D, while B is heading to its looping point) on a section of Road G, to MRT station J, where both services turn into Street K.

    Service B will then turn right from Street K to Road H, then to Road L to its looping point. 

    Service C will turn left on Road H, then return to Interchange D.

    Identify:

    Services A, B and C;

    Interchange D, located in Town D;

    Expressways E and F;

    Roads G, H, L;

    MRT station J;

    Street K.

    Additional clue: Town D is not in the west of Singapore.

    Answers:

    A: 27

    B: 113

    C: 112

    D: Hougang

    E: TPE

    F: PIE

    G: Upper Serangoon Road

    H: Hougang Ave 1

    J: Kovan

    K: Hougang St 21

    L: Lorong Ah Soo

Service E, a service that does not call at Bus Stop C but passes by in close proximity, plies Expressway F and only calls at one pair of bus stops along this expressway.
