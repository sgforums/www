    There are these 6 Services A, B, C, D, E and F

    A, E and F are single depot controlled

    B, C and D are dual depot controlled

    B and C are controlled by the same depot

    C, D, E and F are controlled by the same depot

    A and B start at Interchange G. They turn to H (Name of Road) in different directions. A will meet C at Avenue I and travel until a junction. A will turn to Street J and go to its looping point while C remains on Avenue K and goes to Interchange L.

    Meanwhile, E will meet D and F at M (Name of Road). They will travel until they meet C at Lorong N. These 4 services will travel to Interchange O.

    B, on the other hand, meets E at P (Name of Road). They will then travel to Interchange Q.

    Find Service A, B, C, D, E and F,

    Interchanges G, L, O and Q

    H, M and P (Name of Road)

    Avenues I and K

    Street J

    Lorong N

    A: 334

    B: 197

    C: 157

    D: 8

    E: 26

    F: 142

    G: Jurong East Temporary Bus Interchange

    H: Jurong East Central

    I: Jurong East Avenue 1

    J: Jurong West Street 42

    L: Boon Lay Bus Interchange
