    Services A and B are both loop services. A's looping point is in Town C while B's looping point is in Town D. A serves MRT station E and B serves MRT station F.

    Interestingly, A and B have consecutive numbers (with B=A+1), C and D are adjacent towns, while E and F are adjacent MRT stations. 

    There are two bus interchanges within Town C. At A's bus stop outside MRT E's exit (not bus interchange), there is exactly one service each, Services G and H, that terminates at each of the two bus interchanges.

    Find services A, B, G, H,

    Towns C, D,

    and MRT stations E, F.

    B - 64

    C - Bukit Merah (with Bukit Merah Int and HarbourFront Int)

    D - Queenstown

    E - City Hall

    F - Dhoby Ghaut

    G - 80  (124 does not call at the same bus stop as 63)

    H - 851
