    Q: Luke walked to a bus stop and then took a bus to school. He spent 1/5 of his time walking to the bus stop, 1/5 of his time waiting for the bus to arrive and the rest of the time travelling by bus. The bus took 45 min to reach his school.     A: Time taken to travel by bus = 1 - 1/5 - 1/5 = 3/5

    3/5 = 45 min

    1/5 = 15 min

    Luke took 15 min to walk to the bus stop.
