    Service A originates at Interchange X and travels past army camps J, K, L, M, N, and O, before terminating at terminus Y.

    Army camps M and N are situated near to a loop point P, which is used by Service B, that terminates at Interchange W.

    Interchange W has a special service C, which duplicates a stretch of road Q with service A, and passes by army camps J, K, and L. Its service number is a square of a number T.

    Service C terminates at Interchange V, at which there is a service D that terminates at Interchange X, while there is another service E which passes in close proximity to Interchange X but does not terminate there.

    Service E shares a common terminus U with service F, which other terminus is interchange W. At this same terminus U, there is another service G that passes in close proximity to another Interchange S but terminates at Interchange R.

    From Interchange S, there is another bus service that passes by army camp I before terminating at Interchange X.

    From Interchange R, there is another bus service that passes by army camp Q before terminating at Interchange AA, which is in close proximity to important MRT stop Z and Interchange AA also has a service H which terminates at Interchange S.

    Service A: 171
    Service B: 138
    Service C: 169 (only SMRT service in AMK)
    Service D: 856
    Service E: 858
    Service F: 24
    Service G: 53
    Service H: 105
    Army Camp I: Seletar Camp
    Army Camp J, K, L: Nee Soon Camp, Khatib Camp, Dieppe Barracks
    Army Camp M, N: Mandai Hill Camp, Mandai Camp 2
    Army Camp O: Rifle Range Road Camp
    Loop Point P: Singapore Zoo
    Road Q: Sembawang Rd
    Interchange R: Bishan Int
    Interchange S: Serangoon Int
    Number T: 13
    Terminus U: Changi Airport PTB2
    Interchange V: Woodlands Int
    Interchange W: Ang Mo Kio Int
    Interchange X: Yishun Int
    Terminus Y: Marina Centre Ter
    MRT Stop Z: Jurong East (int btw NSL and EWL)
    Interchange AA: Jurong East Int
