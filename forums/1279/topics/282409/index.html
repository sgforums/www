    Do note that there is another one at Block 539A (Dry food and household items at 2nd floor and groceries and seafood at 1st floor).

    The Sheng Siong market at block 539A is only accessible by taking Service 225G. Look out for the red coloured plate which has the '225G' on it at Berth B4 (Do not confuse it with 225W) and board the bus to Block 534. Thereafter, you need to cross the road to the other side and you should be able to see the loading and unloading bay. Walk down a little bit further and you can see the market.
