    Looking at this post, i want to off-topic abit but it's still the problem with the BCs.It's my personal experience.

    Students holding tertiary cards for example Poly and Uni cards, do not scan your card on the ezlink reader on buses if the amount inside is negative. Luckily, I was able to get a refund from TransitLink.

    Please to not flame me, I'm just stating what happened to me, and I believe happened to some of you guys out there.
