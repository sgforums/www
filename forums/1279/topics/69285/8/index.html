    Forgot to post this yesterday

    SBS2730 on svc 36 was taken off service at Changi Airport PTB2 after it got foreign object damage along ECP. It was raining very heavily at that time when a few rocks dropped from a truck in front and the poor bus had the misfortune of speeding over it, generating lift that dented the intercooler and jammed the rear brakes. A vey loud thump was heard.

    Kudos to the driver for driving as he could control the bus and coaxed it into PTB2 so that the pax do not need to transfer in the heavy downpour. In fact, the grinding and screeching of the rear wheels was so audible that 2 guys in green was on hand to greet the bus when it pulled into its berth at PTB2. ( and i had a severe ear ache, but was interesting )
