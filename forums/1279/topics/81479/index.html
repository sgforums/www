    "Causeway Link will introduce bus services plying from Johor Bahru Larkin Terminal to Queen Street and Jurong in August this year.

    One service will ply from Johor Bahru to Queen Street via the causeway while the other will ply to Jurong via the Second Link. It was mentioned that the 2 new services had originally been planned for introduction late last year but met with some delays."
