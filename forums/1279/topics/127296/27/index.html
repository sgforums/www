    Originally posted by SBS 233 X:
    Is it they print by colours?
    if not by colours, then how they print it?

    back to basics, of course the guides are printed on paper, by machines, with different colour inks.

    Remember primary and secondary colours?
    Red + yellow = orange. etc.
    Cyan, Magenta, Black, yellow, blue etc etc.

    in the above example, lets say 4 colours was used. The printer can one shot print 4 colurs at once, meaning white paper in, completed guide comes out, time saving, quality compromising.

    In another scenario, the guides may be printed 4 times. Hence, u see the 93 guide with only orange ink behind etc.
