    u cant define those as services that pass by the town area. its true that most of the services with "To" on the guide do pass by the town area. but there's some exceptions

    3: Tampines to Pasir Ris / Punggol
    39: Tampines to Pasir Ris / Yishun
    151: Hougang Central to NUS / Kent Ridge
    160: Jurong East to Woodlands / Kota Roya (JB)
    183: Jurong East to NUS / Kent Ridge
    193: Boon Lay to Singapore Discovery Ctr / Tuas
    401: Bedok to East Coast Pk / Fort Rd

    so the best way to differentiate the "Between" and "To" services is that "Between" services only state their terminating/looping points. "To" services state the terminating/looping points and one important place along the route. The town services have Chinatown, Orchard Rd, Suntec City or Shenton Way as the important place, which may easily be misled that the "To" services all go to town.

    now for guides...
    84 Hougang - World Trade Centre had guides.
    84 Punggol - Punggol Field DO NOT have guides
