    Did anybody encounter with friendly BCs or SLs before? If so, lets share our experinces here!

    I joyrided service 36 (Rego:SBS8568C) on 26/november/2012 at 1020AM at PTB 2. When the BC entered the bus, the driver politely greeted the passengers in the bus with a warm "Good Morning!". The bus captain is a lady, who wears a blue tudong (?) [not sure if it is one, it just covers her hair and not her back], however, I did not manage to catch her name. Whenever she approaches a bus stop, she will smile and greet all the passengers without fail with a smile "hello!" or "good morning uncle!" etc. Since service 36 is a city bound service, she will also announce the places of shopping centres and MRT station to the passengers on board her bus eg: Lucky Plazza! Dhoby Ghaut Station! etc. She also drives smoothly with less jerks. As the bus approached Four Seasons Hotel, there was a caucasion women carrying alot of bags as she was going to the airport. She also asked if this service went to the airport and the BC answered her questions patiently. BC also offered to carry her bags but the women rejected. She also told her to take her time and would wait for her to put her bags down and to tap her card. I am impressed by the standard of customer service provided by her!

    If anybody had encountered this lady BC before, may i please know her name? And should I feedback my encounter to SBS transit?

    Thanks!
