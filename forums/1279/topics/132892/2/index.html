    Services at Marsiling Terminal were 180, 182, 950, 951 and 952. 169 also used to end here but it was extended to Woodlands Interchange when it opened in 1981. In the 1980 Bus Guide, this terminal is referred to as Woodlands. The name was changed when Woodlands Interchange was opened to avoid confusion.
