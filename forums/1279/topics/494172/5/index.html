    I was rather confused by the huge amount of posters they issued in respect of the changes (there are at least 2 to 3 posters for a service, in fact one poster for one change in calling bus stops). But I can roughly summarise the changes as such:

    - 176, 920, 922, 970, 972, 973, 975 and 979/979M will be calling at the ITH, while 75, 177, 180, 184, 700/700A and 971E will not.

    - 176, 920, 922, 970 and 975 will be reverting back to their original routes before the 2012 closure of Bukit Panjang Interchange, skipping those bus stops that they called after the closure and during the 2015 DTL rationalisation.

    - 972 and 973 will be skipping the bus stops which currently serves as their starting and ending points (Blk 183, Blk 604 and Opp Blk 183 along Bukit Panjang Road). 973 will also call at Bukit Panjang Plaza along Jelebu Road in the direction towards the ITH.

    - 75 and 184 will be calling at outside/opposite Ten Mile Junction Stn as their ending/starting points. 971E will start from/end at Block 602/Block 541A CP along Bukit Panjang Ring Road, skipping the bus stops outside/opposite Block 180.

    - The bus stops outside/opposite Bukit Panjang Plaza will be skipped by most existing plying services (except 920 and 922). As such, 75, 177, 960 and 963/E/R will call at bus stops outside/opposite Block 180 along the same road. 171 will not have new calling bus stops while 979/979M will only call at Blk 183 along Bukit Panjang Road in the direction towards Yew Tee. 

    - The bus stop Bukit Panjang Stn along Petir Road will be removed. 180 will call at Bukit Panjang Stn Exit A/LRT along Upp Bukit Timah Road, while 983 will call at Aft Bukit Panjang Stn along the same road as alternatives.
