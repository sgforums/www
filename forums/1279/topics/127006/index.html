    Heading to Johor Bahru for shopping or seafood? Ride on SBS TransitÂ’s Services 160 and 170, which offer direct links to Johor Bahru, and stand a chance to win up to $30,000 worth of prizes in a Lucky Draw promotion.

    From 18 April to 10 July 2005, commuters riding on Services 160 or 170 and their related SBS Transit transfer rides including the North East Line with their ez-link card will be given Transit points automatically. The Transit points will earn them chances to participate in six fortnightly sure-win draws and the Grand Draw. One Transit point is given for every 1-cent fare deduction from the ez-link card. Commuters who transfer between Service 170 and train at Little India Station will be awarded double Transit Points.

    The Grand prize is a product voucher worth $10,000 from AKIRA. For six fortnights, 30 $100 AKIRA product vouchers will also be given to commuters who have accumulated the highest Transit Points every fortnight. In addition, a bonus prize worth $2,000 from AKIRA will be given to the commuter who accumulates the highest Transit points over the entire promotion period.

    Mr Woon Chio Chong, Executive Vice President (Bus Operations) said, Â“We hope this initiative will encourage even more people to use our service 160 and 170 to Johor Bahru. We are happy also to be able to partner AKIRA for this promotion. Passengers on our Service 160 and 170 will not only enjoy what is possibly one of the most affordable ways to travel to Malaysia, but also stand to win handsome prizes.Â”
