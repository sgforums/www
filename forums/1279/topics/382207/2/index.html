    Likes:

    39 - Gets me to Pasir Ris within 35 mins during off peak. Relatively slow speed on the expressway though.

    151 - 45 mins flat on a weekend afternoon from HG to Clementi Rd!

    168 - Woodlands to Tampines direct. Good for going WRI from my school - takes only 15 mins from Jalan Kayu B/S to WRI.

    171 - Fast and direct to BPJ. Loves riding the OCs speeding down Mandai. (:

    180 - Together with 171, brings me to BNL from Yishun in a little over an hour.

    857 - Discovered that I can make it to GL1 in just 40 mins if I change to 985 at Jln TPY, compared to 853 that takes over an hour. 50 mins direct into Suntec City.

    927 - A fast alternative from CCK to Yishun if I don't want to take the MRT. Fastest time taken for 927-171 combo changing at Mandai was 30 mins, about almost the same time taken for MRT.

    965 - Direct from Yishun to Sengkang. Plus with their tight runtime, rides on the TPE are always a breeze. (:

    969 - Best service ever introduced. Direct from Yishun to Tampines.

    As you can see what I only like are fast and direct routes...

  • SBS 8030 L
    19 Nov 09, 18:48

    Favourite Services:

    174!!! - As all of you all know, this is one of my favourite services of all time. Why, got my favourite bus (SBS8030L) & some of the best leylands in SLBP. The route is nice and sometimes the route can cater to most of the place that I'm planning to go to be it a direct link or via transfer.

    174e - If you all wondered since if i like Sv 174 then what about FFW 174e. The answer is yes. Although seldom took this service, but when i took it most of the time it would be when I'm in a hurry & i tend to ride on SBS8210J or SBS8192C. It would be a breeze to take one while flying across Farrer Rd flyover towards Ulu Pandan esp on SBS8210J.

    Express 502 - If I'm in a hurry to get between west side & city area then this is the service for me. Help me to cut down travelling time by about 12-15 minutes instead of the usual Sv 174. I also have some modern memories with this service though.

    Sv 190 - If I'm lazy or if I'm bored to take Sv 174 then this is perhaps my favourite service in SMRT. Direct link between CCK - city area. I especially love taking this service back to CCK at night to go home if I'm alone. Especially the bendies which can chiong like nobody else along PIE & BKE given their night time schedule are quite tight.

    Sv 927 + 138 - Perfect combo for me to travel between CCK & AMK. Although it's a crazy idea to take this route but can be quite fun actually if you don't mind the frequency instead of the usual Sv 74 then Sv 67.

    Sv 168 + 858 - Perfect combo to get to Changi Airport from the west. Time estimated from CCK to Changi Airport is about 1 hour. Changi Airport is usually the place for me to Rest and Relax which i usually tend to go.

    Sv 107/107M: Fastest ride to town from HG and vice-versa (not including MRT Ride). Almost take this service everyday. Minus points will be long frequency waiting during off-peak and long run-time during certain parts of the day.

    Sv 985: Fastest ride from Jalan Toa Payoh to School (approx 10 -15 mins) if there is no jam along PIE. Like the Lances speeding along the expressway.

    Sv 854: Run-time is more tight than Sv 25, especially during late night. Like it to speed along Hougang Ave 3, Yio Chu Kang Rd and Lentor Ave. Only SMRT Bus Service to serve where I lived in.

    Sv 151/154: After approx 8pm, run-time would be tight and like it to speed along Bukit Timah Road and PIE

    Sv 119: Former secondary school bus.

    Sv 82: Former secondary school bus, tight run-time especially during night time and like it to speed along Punggol Road

    Sv 161: Direct link from Sengkang/North-east Area to Woodlands
