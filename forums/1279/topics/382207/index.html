    Like

    Service 5: Went for a spin on this service right on its Day 1 (11 Mar 07). Later found out that it's a service that connects me to some of the most frequented places I go to. Most importantly it gets me straight from Bedok Reservior to Jalan Toa Payoh (the Trellis Towers Bus Stop) and vice-versa.

    Service 168: Found out when I was in Sec 1 that it's a faster alternative as compared to Services 18 and/or 69 when going from Tampines to Bedok. Service also connects me to important places such as the Tampines Retail Park, Seletar as well as Woodlands. I had quite a number of favourite buses on this service, especially SBS 9199C.

    Service 225: The one and only service that gets me from home to the Bedok Interchange. On 18 Feb 08, became the 3rd (or is it 2nd) feeder service to receive DDs. Service is also believed to be the oldest surviving service with no admenments to route since introduction in 1972*.

    *Note: Usually I take the introduction of Service 225 to be in 1972, according to a source, though I may not be correct.

    Dislike (Somewhat)

    Service 45: Poor frequency; waiting for a bus on this service to come can be frustrating, especially when the bus stop does not have an next bus display. But I take this service so that I can connect between Tanah Merah MRT, my home and Eunos.
