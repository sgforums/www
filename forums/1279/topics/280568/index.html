    Originally posted by crimson soldier:
    then when the ezlink expires the concession also expires?

    Or what...?
    If you are using the Add Value Machine (AVM), you will not be able to encode BCP, TCP or HCP into your NSF ezlink card if you have less than one month prior to your ORD.

    However, you can go to the Transitlink Sales Office to encode bus stamps. The person at the counter can help you to encode the stamps for the remaining number of days till you ORDed and you will have to pay in proportion to the number of days it is encoded into the ezlink card with the total number of days in that month.

    Upon expiry of NSF concession, no concession stamps can be encoded.
