    For me i memorize all the perms as stated in sgwiki for SMRT side, I already memorize WLDEP,AMDEP,KJDEP perm buses but i do get memory lapses for KJDEP coz i hardly go to CCK,BBT area. AMDEP and WLDEP i memorize all the perm buses by sgwiki, personal observation and also i live in Woodlands and i commute to WRI everyday and i camped at WRI often to recognise the perms, but i go to Yishun n BPJ once in a while and also i constanty check on the redeployment page here always to get the updated redeployment of every buses. Some buses especially those WAB buses the regos are consecutively together example 178 perm buses SMB1311G to SMB1319K. 858 perm bus are SMB1301K to SMB1310J, 851 buses have SMB73A to SMB81B, 966 have SMB310R to SMB316A perm and so on. Thus, it is easy to remember. 965 ones also quite easy, 969 also easy, 961 also easy to remember. But this things take years, i dont memorize all these buses in 1 year, took me 3 years to memorize the regos of buses especially when they are redeployed or they transfer duties to another depot e.g 176,169,190,700 transfer duty to WLDEP as u need to recognise the buses wich are newly transferred. Just some tips to share ah, u need a long time to know perm buses of what service as changes are unpredictable and u need to adapt to changes there as deployments will change a lot esp SBST ones. The only SBST svc i memorize was 168 and 161 perms.
