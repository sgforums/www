    How to calculate for SMB plate?

    Download an app from AppStore called Singapore Number Plates.

    It allows you to calculate any kind of plate suffixes.

    Just use the Suffix calculator, 

    Key in "SMB" in the prefix box,

    And type any random 4, 3, 2 or 1 digit number at the number box

    And then press "Get Suffix".

    That is how I got SBS5756X.

    Addtional notes:

    In tens, for example SMB389 to SMB390, it is 'SMB390J' instead of 'SMB390L', skip 1 letter.

    In hundreds, for example SBS3199 to SBS3200, skip 8 letters and you get SBS3200X

    In thousands, for example TIB999 to TIB1000, it's the same and you get TIB999B and TIB1000B
