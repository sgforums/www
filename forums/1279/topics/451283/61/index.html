    10 Sep 14, 03:01
    Originally posted by ButIAmAToilet:

    I find Taros to be quite cold. VOs are managable. Some Mk IVs can be very cold.

    @DD77: guess I am not one of them. Oh well, I shall just endure.

    Anyway, I have another question about the aircon. Lets say that all available aircon ducts are blocked apexcept for one. Will that duct's aircon be very strong and very cold? Since there is only one exit instead of multiple exits on bus aircon system.

    Very Strong: Yes, all the aircon is concentrated into one outlet. Since the fan would still be moving the same amount of air, all the air has to exit at one place.

    Very Cold: Depends. On every bus there is a thermostat that measures the temperature within the bus. Normally this is near or inside the "aircon filter". This controls the power of the aircon by making it colder if it is not cold enough. So, if the outlet that you have left open is far away from the thermostat, the aircon will try to make itself colder and colder.
