    Originally posted by A Bus Observer:

    xover bus on AP shift, how can? am and pm running???

    Some A/P buses do perform crossover trips to another service, especially during AM peak... For example, 190AM50 performs a crossover trip to 307 during AM peak, but will not do the trip of 307 during the PM peak (it will still do 190 for PM shift). All crossover buses for NightRider/Nite Owl services are A/P buses (PM shift).
