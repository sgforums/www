    Actually it can be 335hp or 340hp. This is because the person who edited that wiki page simply converted 250kw to hp. But not many people know that there is 2 types of horsepower (Metric units and UK/Imperial units). 250kw = 339.90538122 hp/PS (Metric) = 335.25546466 hp (UK/Imperial).

    PS stands for horsepower in German.

    A website to help convert (don't convert using Google, it gives the imperial units)
