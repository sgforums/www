    Originally posted by All the Best!:

    Just a simple question here:

     

    How does you guys post a long list of cameos here? Do u memorises it, or take it down in a notebook?

     

    sometimes I hv a hard time memorizing them when I spotted abt 10 liddat 

    1) If you see any cameo, take down the bus registration plate number, perm service, and what service does it cameoing. [E.g SBS 7603R on 76 (AMDEP 168)]

    2) Write it in your notebook.

    3) When you reach home, copy whatever cameos you have, to the cameo spotting topic. So it's like copy from your notebook, to cameo spottings topic.

    Do take note that if a perm bus doing it's perm service (E.g SBS 7603R on 168), that is not counted as cameo. Because we already all know that SBS 7603R on 168.

    And also, some depots/bus parks buses are interchangeable. Like AMDEP and BRBP buses are interchangeable. This means that if a BRBP bus doing on a AMDEP service, that is counted as cameo. [E.g SBS 7503X on 72 (BRBP 60)]

    I hope you know how to do it. If you have any clarifications, please feel free to PM me. Cheers!
