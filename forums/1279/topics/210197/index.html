    This would be the air escaping the retarder system as air is built up to slow the bus down. The retarders are magnets which use a forcefield to slow the engine rpm down. It comes down to variances in gearbox build, some ZFs howl, some don't. I have programmed ZF gearboxes to lock up or not lock up, and alter shift points (ie, what engine rpm to kick up a gear).

And they definitely don't make such noises stated above.
