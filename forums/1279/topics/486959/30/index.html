    184 loading as an institutional service is pretty much similar to feeder services because vast numbers travel for that short distances. 

    Anyway for now, SMRT has limited DDs to spare around and the O405Gs are here to stay till 2017 atleast. There are still many long haul trunk services in need of DDs and some SD-only or minimal high capacity services like 187 that can afford to have more DDs. So I would rather SMRT use DDs in those areas first and there is no need for Bendy > DD conversion in areas where the bendys are doing a good job. Depending on what type of buses are purchased to replace the O405Gs in future, the bendys on these services can be replaced accordingly.

    SMRT also probably made a big mistake by purchasing MAN A22s at such high quantities. So even if want to do SD > DD conversion, the question that will need to be answered is, what can they do with those A22s then? So they probably have to use a larger fleet of SDs instead of replacing to utilise their buses.

    As for for BSEP service like 860, it would be interesting to see what will happen when LTA takes back the BSEP buses for Bulim Contract and SMRT will have to operate the service with their buses. Could possibly see deployment of Bendys there, that will ease the overcrowding and loading.
