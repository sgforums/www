    Hi people, would you like to share your experiences about taking long long long long rides on Singapore Bus Services?

    For me... yea... Service 51 (Hougang Central to Jurong East)

    A butt pain experience, and also somewhat sleepy. Worst thing of all, if the driver is driving 50km/h, you're gonna fall asleep. How long can it be? From Hougang Central to North Bridge Rd takes an hour, and from North Bridge Rd to Jurong East takes another hour to an hour 10 mins. Along the winding and long route, you might get to see something interesting, or otherwise. The Sv 51 bus I took was SBS 9503 H. A record time of 2 hours and 9 mins was made. If one would like to try this long long long long distances bus rides, please clear your "water tanks" before heading on. And also, do some stretching exercises to prevent yourelf from getting tense muscles.
