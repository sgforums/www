    Volvo B9TL EURO III - uniquely Singapore look. good looking bus

    MAN 18.240 HOCL-NL-R - i dig the MAN Lion's City original front and gasp! rear! nice nice nice nice. Nice rear. good copy.

    Scania K2304x2UB EURO IV - Ok lah a stripped down OmniCity front not bad lah. The rear could be more curvey and follow more of the Scnaia OmniCity curved window rear. But the air letting out strip works fine as a oblong straight line shape so rear is fine with bbox like windows.

    Wish the "radiator fins" of the Omnicity have stayed. And the use of middle placed fog lights for more Scania branding. But the accentuate smile front is better also.

    yeah digging it.

     

    Mercedes Benz OC500LE (Diesel)  SBS - arrrr not getting the front. stripped down Merc Capacity front is not da bomb. Not getting it.

     

    Mercedes benz OC500LE SMRT (Disel) - nice front. A direct copy of the Capacity V shape medal hanging front complete with Mercedes Plith to hold the logo proud. Sadly the V shape is not accentuated with glass, but despite not doing that bus kinda also has a Citaro like front without the more added glass area. nice nice nice.
