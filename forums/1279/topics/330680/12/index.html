    Actually you can't eliminate the problem of resonance because no matter how you build the body, as long as the engine vibration matches the natural frequency of the body, it will vibrate all the same, at various engine rpms.

    On the other hand, it is possible to damp these vibrations and make the rides less itchy to the ear. So my take is that those with less vibrations are able to achieve it by damping the transmission of energy to our buttocks, rather than taking into account whether the natural frequency will match whichever rpm. This should actually imply that chassis design matters too.
