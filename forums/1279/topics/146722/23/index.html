    SBST: A console for the Automatic Vehicle Monitoring System (AVMS). AVMS allows for dynamic, on-the-go and paperless bus scheduling, while ensuring that the HQ can contact the drivers in cases of severe disruption to service. A hidden, sensitive, omni-directional mic is also part of this setup.

    TIBS (SMRTB): A speaker. Part of the Integrated Bus Operation System (IBOS), which allows the HQ to contact the drivers when necessary.
