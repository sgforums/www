    1. the first stage of a bus service at the interchange/terminal is 1.0, not 0.0.

    2. it has always been that the fare for travelling first 4.0 stages is $0.64, and for 4.5 to 7.0 stages it costs you $0.85.

    3. please indicate the service you take and your alighting stop so we can confirm the correct fare you should be paying.

    4. if it an isolated incident and only happened once or twice, asking for a refund claim at the transitlink office might solve your problem.
