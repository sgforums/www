    SMRT Buses Introduce Express 868

    Starting from 28 September 2010, Express 868 will be operating 2 trips a day from Bukit Batok West Ave 5 to Temasek Ave.

    1st Trip is 7:30am
    2nd Trip is 7:45am

    Operate on Weekdays only (Excluding Saturday, Sunday and Public Holidays)

    2 Trips per day only.

    Originally posted by ^tamago^:

    Not really. No similarities whatsoever.

    The way I see it, Express 868 is actually a smart move by SMRT to take on the competition posed by Ric Tat's successful Premium 582 (Bukit Batok West to Shenton Way).

    P582 charges $4, E868 charges almost half. P582 winds around in Bukit Batok East and Jurong East and stops along AYE, E868 takes on BBT West Ave 6 not served by P582 and tries to take on a slightly faster route and goes non-stop along AYE.

    Chances are E868 might steal almost all of P582's pax in BBT West heading towards Shenton Way. Pay less for roughly the same amount of travel time, if not less.
