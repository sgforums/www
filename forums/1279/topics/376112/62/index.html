    The thing at the middle of the OC500LE's hub is a Central Tire Inflation System (CTIS). Quite a number of batch 2 OCs's CTIS was removed.

    According to a past post by XiaoTaro: the CTIS system is able to monitor and control the tire pressure of each tire (6 in this case) to provide optimal traction and fuel effiency for the vehicle. also the system is able to warn in case of tire puncture or leaking without the SL having to visually check it manually.
