    SBS Transit launches points system to reward loyal commuters

    SBS Transit is launching an island-wide "Ride & Redeem" programme to reward loyal commuters who travel on SBS buses, North East Line (NEL) trains and Sengkang or Punggol LRT.

    This is the first rewards programme of its kind for public transport services.

    From 1 April to 31 March 2008, commuters will be rewarded with Transit Dollars (TD) for every trip taken with SBS Transit using their ez-link cards.

    Commuters can then use the Transit Dollars accumulated to redeem various goodies like free brownies, manicure services or free dishes at selected dining outlets.

    Commuters can also earn Bonus Dollars (BDs) if there is a usage value increase of at least $1 as compared to the previous week.

    These Bonus Dollars can be used to redeem prizes such as resort stays.

    To participate in this programme, commuters will need to register their ez-link cards online at www.sbstransit.com.sg.

    Registration is free.

    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    What is Ride & Redeem?
    Ride & Redeem is a programme to reward SBS Transit commuters* for every ride they take with us. Commuters get to earn Transit Dollars for every ride they take, or Bonus Dollars when at least $1 more is spent week on week.
    * Transit and Bonus Dolllars will not be awarded to e-stamp concession stamp users .

    How do SBS Transit's commuters participate?
    Commuters who take SBS Transit's bus and rail services can simply register with us at here. Registration is free. First 2,000 registrants get to enjoy a free Mr. Bean Soya milk !
    Find out more about Ride & Redeem here

    What is Transit Dollar?
    Commuters who take SBS Transit bus and rail services with their ez-link card will earn Transit Dollars.
    If your deducted fare* is $1.90, you will earn 1.90 TD
    *Fare is net of transfer rebate given.
    To check your Transit Dollars, start riding with us and register here!
    Note: Transit Dollars will be shown approximately 5 days later from actual deduction.

    What is Bonus Dollar?
    Participants will earn Bonus Dollars when they ride $1 or more weekly. You can earn up to 3 Bonus Dollars per week!

    For Example:
    Upon registration, participant's weekly spending will be tabulated every Sunday. In Week 1, participant will earn 0 BD.

    In Week 2, participant rides $1.50 more than Week 1 and earns 1 BD.

    Week 3 sees an increase of $4.50 over Week 2 and participant will earn 2 BDs.

    In Week 4, participant rides $1.90 or more than Week 3, and 3 BDs is awarded. Participant will continue to earn 3 BDs if subsequent week's spending is $1 or more than the previous week.

    If participant does not ride $1 or more than the previous week, 0 BD will be awarded and subsequent Bonus Dollar awarded will start from 0. See illustration:

    Registration and Login

    How do I retrieve my password?
    Upon completion of your registration, a temporary password will be sent to your registered email address.
    You may retrieve your temporary password and follow the instructions in the email to customised your password or amend your personal particulars.
    Please refrain from sharing your password with others.

    What if I have forgotten password / user ID?
    If you have forgotten your password or user ID, enter your email address here. Your password and user ID will be emailed to your registered email address.
    If you have not already registered, sign-up now!
    Contact us if you experience other difficulties.

    How do I change my personal particulars?
    You may edit your personal particulars after you have login with your user ID and password here.
    Please ensure all changes are correct and saved before existing the webpage. No email notification will be sent for amendments made.

    What if I have more than one ez-link card?
    Please register the most frequently-use card to maximise your Transit Dollar earning!

    Can I combine Transit Dollars earned from different ez-link cards?
    No. Only one ez-link card number can be registered.
    Please refer to terms and conditions for more information.

    Can I register more than once?
    No. You can only register once. If you have forgotten your user ID or password, enter your registered email address here.

    Can SeasonPass users participate?
    Yes. However, SeasonPass users must have an ez-link card.

    Why after registration, I did not received any email notification?
    1. Please ensure that you have click on the 'Submit' button.
    2. Check your spam mail folder* for the email notification. You may add our email address to your 'safe email' list.
    *Note: Spam filter technology varies with different email clients. Please check with your email provider.

    Why after login, I did not see any Transit Dollars?
    1. Participants must ride with SBS Transit bus and rail services using their registered ez-link card.
    2. Transit Dollars will be shown approximately 5 days later from actual deduction. If you register on 1 April 2007, you can view your Transit Dollars earned on 5 April.
    3. If you experience difficulties, please contact us here.

    Why after login, I did not see any Bonus Dollars? 1. Participants must ride with SBS Transit bus and rail services using their registered ez-link card.
    2. Please see how you can earn Bonus Dollars here.
