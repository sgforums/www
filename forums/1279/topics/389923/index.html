    In case you're interested,

    You are cordially invited to attend Mad About Singapore Buses held by National Library.

     

    We all have our own bus experiences and stories to tell. Meet the man who is responsible for the making and assembling of most of the buses & coaches that most of us ride today. In case you didn’t know, buses such as the Hippo buses were produced from his very own workshop. Join Mr David Soh from the Soon Chow Corporation & other bus enthusiasts as they share their classic tales of Singapore buses.

     

    Mad About Singapore Buses

    Saturday, 6 February, 10.00 am – 12.00 pm, level 5, Possibility Room, National Library Building

    Meet our unbelievable bus fans who literally go mad, talking about buses! These bus enthusiasts collect old bus tickets, bus models, bus memorabilia, bus plate numbers, bus pictures & many more. You name it, they have it. Hear their stories and interesting experiences of how some of them ended up chasing after the buses just to capture a snapshot of a particular bus model.
