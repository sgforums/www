    there are some road/landmark names I wanted to point out..

    Gagak Selari Barat - around Lower Delta Rd
    Pesiraran Keliling - now known as MacPherson Estate
    Upp Changi Rd (original) - was broken down into segments. The newly aligned New Upp Changi Rd was constructed to replace the Upp Changi Rd due to HDB development.
    Ayer Rajah Rd/Upp Ayer Rajah Rd - well, if you see those bus stops along the AYE and you are still wondering, Ayer Rajah Rd/Upp Ayer Rajah Rd was converted into an expressway.
    Entire roads in undeveloped areas in the North East (Cheng Lim farwmay 1, Buangkok South Farmway 1, Lor Sengkang were demolished) - Sengkang New Town Was name after Lor Sengkang
    Joo Koon Rd - was name after a kampong primary school, Joo Koon Public Sch.
    Kg Sungei Tengah - not only the LCK area, but as seen on the map of an undeveloped area in Punggol, Kg Sungei Tengah was just off Punggol Rd and along Jln Kampong Tengah (now demolished)
