    Had been catching up with bendies on Service 851 recently. This time I board them 3 days in a row. All of them are permanent bendies of 851. Sometimes there is really a need to catch the arrival time of the bendies right.

    Last Thursday (12 July 2012), I boarded this bendy after letting 2 buses SMB78L and TIB523R run. It was 1800 then.

    This is the interior of the bendy I boarded, fitted with OC Passenger Information System, OC Seats of course. There aren't many people then so the trip is more comfortable. If you were to spot the ad of this bus on the window, the answer is pretty obvious which bus this is: TIB1096A!

    Finally, when I alighted at Little India MRT Station, I took the photo of this bendy's exterior.

    This is TIB1096A. Note that its EDS is changed to a Mobitec one. It also has a Hispano Logo at the front bumper. The rims are shiny and look different from typical ones as well (It is suspected that the rims are changed during the change of EDS). Its President's Challenge 2011 Ad is retained till today!

    Bendies of 851 alternates every week, you will expect a different bendy (But still a bendy) on the particular shift every week. Hope you guys like it!
