  • TIB1138M
    24 Nov 12, 10:39

    Apparently there's lots of Volgren bodywork buses in SMRT.

    The Dennis Lance Volgren bus. Only 2 models, TIB1017D and TIB1018B respectively. You don't get to see them often nowadays, especially when they have been converted to Training Buses. They use the green LAWO EDS. Both buses are however controlled by AMDEP.

    The Hino HS3KRKK Volgren bus. Apparently there's 11 models registered from TIB947B to TIB957Y. They do have the "Hino" logo at the front, but watch out, as TIB952K, TIB953H, TIB954E and TIB957Y do not have the "Hino" logo at the front. Like the Dennis Lance Volgren, they use the green LAWO EDS, with TIB952K being the only Hino bus fitted with the orange Mobitec MobiLED EDS. All 11 buses are controlled by KJDEP, with TIB947B and TIB948Z being Training Buses and the rest being SP buses.

    The Mercedes-Benz O405 Volgren bus. The rims are the same as the SBST and SMRT Hispano O405s and are different from those of the Dennis Lances and Hino HK3KRKK buses. You can actually identify the "Mercedes-Benz" logo at the front bumper, though again not all of them bear this logo as well. The O405 Volgrens also use the green LAWO EDS, but most of them are now using the orange Mobitec EDS, with TIB958U the exception using the orange LAWO-BENEFIT EDS. Most of the buses do have their rim guards retained with exception of TIB958U, TIB961J and TIB970H. Most of the O405 Volgrens are controlled by WLDEP, with TIB963D, TIB964B and TIB965Z under KJDEP and usually on perm 106, TIB969M on KJDEP Training Bus, and TIB958U the only bus under AMDEP, operating on perm 67.
