    To OMSI123: Bendy Bus Guide

    Mercedes Benz O405G(Hispano MK1 Box Bendy)- Similar to O405s, common on 180, 184 and 964.

    Mercedes Benz O405G(Hispano MK2 Smiley Bendy)- Some of them are not smiley though... A bit hard to catch them, can be found on service 176 etc.

    Mercedes Benz O405G(Hispano Habit Pininfarina)- The most common bendy in SG.

    • ZF 4HP 500 Gearbox: TIB1019Z - TIB1023K, TIB1061A, TIB1063U - TIB1177A, TIB1198P - TIB1207Y
    • Voith D863.3 Gearbox: TIB1025E - TIB1060C, TIB1062Y, TIB1208U - TIB1247G

    Mercedes Benz O405G(Volgren)- Very hard to catch unless you go to Woodlands Reg Int... Found on services 901(Okay but a little hard...), 903(Easiest), 912(Okay) and 913(Easy but prone to MK2s). 

    ZF 4HP 500 (TIB1178Y - TIB1187X) / Voith DIWA 863.3 (TIB1188T - TIB1197S)

    Credits to TIB1218R, Public Transport Hub and SGWiki. No copyright infringement intended.
