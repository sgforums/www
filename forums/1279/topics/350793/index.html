    I remember riding 371 when it was in service with PATH Transit.

    TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

    Engine: Mercedes-Benz OM 407 h (407.914) inline 6-cylinder 4-stroke naturally aspirated diesel engine displacing 11.413-litres (Bore/stroke: 125mm/155mm).  16.5:1 compression ratio.

    Max Power: 147kW (200hp) @ 2200rpm

    Max Torque: 715Nm (530 ft-lbf) @ 1400rpm

    Transmission: Mercedes-Benz W3D 080/2.2 R torque converter with integral hydraulic retarder.  3 forward ratios - 2.12:1, 1.35:1, 1.00:1; 1 reverse ratio - 2.23:1.  Hub reduction rear axle drive with 5.22:1 diff ratio, allowing a maximum speed of 87km/h @ 2200rpm in top gear.  "Piano key" gear selection.

    As for reliability, you can't kill one of these with an axe.  The main thing to look out for isthumping or slipping gearboxes.
