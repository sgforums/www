    EDS

    Electronic Destination Screen

    wrong use - electronic display sign,

    the EDS is so called because it uses elctornics, (LED and ciruittry and computers) and it shows the Destination for a bus and its a screen fitted onto the bus.

    EDsets

    EDsets are the road names, words, bbus no or whatever display set you wish to output to the EDS screen. EDsets are generally prepared on a normal computer and then imported into the EDS Hard Disk via a USB port.

    Today the modern EDset preparing programms are written in windows. legend has it that each and every pixel has to be clicked to "on" or "off" setting. So yoou cna imagine how long it takes to prepare a EDset.

    EDS Controllers
    These are like tiny calculator sized devices fitted near the dash. for driver to control EDS.

    most EDS come with basic functions

    > a scroll search for your EDS u want
    > functiion to change bbus destinatiion direction
    > functiion for special destos sets
    > 3 digit keying of svc no to get what u want fast

    Very few brands however allow you to program your own desto. This is due to the abbove fact that a EDset is hard to write even for the laymen driver. however there exists brand of EDS can allow you to output out service numbers as these have been pre keyed into the EDS hard disk for,
