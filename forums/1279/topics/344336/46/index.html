    Today, spotted quite a number of things, to post in this thread:

    1) TIB1242U back EDS spoilt again. Or should say sometimes working, sometimes not.

    2) Of all WL buses, only TIB435L gets interior GoGreenWithSMRT paper ads from WLDEP. Others like TIB670Z, they got it before depot-transfer. Can say almost all KJ & AM buses got it liao.

    3) A number of KJ buses would park at W'lands Rd bus stop, before oppo. Ten Mile Junction bus stop, waiting to do downroute 190 from Bt. Panjang to New Bridge Ter. Seen today, TIB1028Y and TIB547Y parking there with 190 EDS. Then come after are TIB475X, which off svc from BKE --> 920 route --> the bus stop --> 190(downroute). TIB791H was seen using the same route as TIB475X, so I assume its waiting to do 190 as well.

    4) Today, theres still NP Shuttle. Saw TIB440X with NP EDS. Normally, during holidays there won't be any shuttle. Maybe Its due to common test week. But I'm not sure about 902.

    5) SBS7467S seems to get repasted SBS Transit livery. Very bright colours.

    6) CSS204T on TRG at Bt. Batok.

    7) Took TIB670Z just now, rear Emergency Door came loose 2 times. There was alarm at the dashboard. SL had to close the door tightly, and ask a couple to seat at the last row for safety purpose. Not sure if theres alarm in every buses.

    8) TIB762S just came out from WLDEP, with refurbished interior, repasted SMRT livery(Can't see back Mercs badge liao), repainted black rims, but not sure about the seats.

    9) TIB661A front suspension spoilt, front part of bus very high, but still on svc 178.

    10) TIB1187X top compressor(on roof top), the bending part, broken. The middle section, articulator, is shifted forward and is worst than TIB1069D that time in an accident. Saw some springs tangling out. Not sure if it hits something at the top.
