    WRI is going to be like shopping centres' carparks and WLDEP liao.  

    The front entrance to WRI at W'lands Ave 3, had been having some renovation work to improve the prevention of having cars going into WRI. Red Pole dividers were built at the center line, including sensors, a swing-up-and-down gate(carpark and WLDEP ones) and 2 traffic lights.

    When buses like 963, 856, 960 are going into WRI, they have to queue up. 1 bus stop at the gate, let the sensors sense its presence, then green light and the gate swing up. After the first bus go, the gate will swing down. So, one bus goes at a time.
