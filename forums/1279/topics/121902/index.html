    Mmm...sticky gearbox. This is due to an electrical fault which fails to send an impulse to the gearbox and initiate a gear change. Its a technical problem and not related to the BC. Problem can be rectified by putting the bus to neutral and restarting the engine. Its not always the first gear though. I've rode on an O405 which was stuck in 2nd and was really slow off the mark. The bus can get to a decent speed (about 50 kmh) on 1st but the engine will be screaming.
