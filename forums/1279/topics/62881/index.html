    CSS is a joint-venture between Comfort and S'pore Shuttle Bus (SSB). Info found in Singapore Resource Centre, National Reference Library.

    FARES, as at 1981
    - Adult
    4 fare stages and below : 30 cents
    5 - 8 fare stages: 40 cents
    9 fare stages and above: 50 cents
    - Child/Student
    10 cents flat fare

    ROUTES (only those withdrawn long ago, due to lack of time), as at 1981

    CSS 1 (Marine Terrace - Queensway):
    Marine Terrace (Blk 53), Marine Parade Rd, Marine Crescent, Marine Parade Rd, Still Rd South, East Coast Rd, Mountbatten Rd, Tg Katong Rd, Dunman Rd, Old Airport Rd, Stadium Rd, Stadium Walk, Stadium Rd, National Stadium Car Park 'B' & 'C', Stadium Rd, Nicoll Highway, Stamford Rd, Orchard Rd, Penang Rd, Somerset Rd, Orchard Boulevard, Grange Rd, Napier Rd, Holland Rd and Queensway (terminates near Rumah Bomba Circus at Blk 53).
    And return via Queensway, Jln Bt Merah (U-turn at Jln Bt Merah near Brickworks Estate), Queensway, Holland Rd, Napier Rd, Tanglin Rd, Orchard Rd, Bras Basah Rd, Nicoll Highway, Stadium Rd, Stadium Walk, Stadium Rd, Old Airport Rd, Dunman Rd, Tg Katong Rd, Mountbatten Rd, East Coast Rd, Still Rd South, Marine Parade Rd, Marine Crescent and Marine Terrace.
    NOTE: Monday - Saturday, 0600 to 1930 hrs. No operation on Sundays and Public Holidays. 10 mins interval.

    CSS 4 (Marine Terrace - New Bridge Rd):
    Marine Terrace (Blk 53), Marine Parade Rd, Marine Crescent, Marine Parade Rd, Still Rd South, East Coast Rd, Mountbatten Rd, Tg Katong Rd, Dunman Rd, Old Airport Rd, Stadium Rd, Stadium Walk, Stadium Rd, National Stadium Car Park 'B' & 'C', Stadium Rd, Nicoll Highway, Stamford Rd, North Bridge Rd, South Bridge Rd, Neil Rd, New Bridge Rd and New Bridge Rd Terminal.
    And return via New Bridge Rd Terminal, New Bridge Rd, Eu Tong Sen St, Hill St, Victoria St, Bras Basah Rd, Nicoll Highway, Stadium Rd, Stadium Walk, Stadium Rd, Old Airport Rd, Dunman Rd, Tg Katong Rd, Mountbatten Rd, East Coast Rd, Still Rd South, Marine Parade Rd, Marine Crescent and Marine Terrace.
    NOTE: Monday - Saturday, 0600 to 1930 hrs. No operation on Sundays and Public Holidays. 10 mins interval.

    CSS 6 (Bt Merah - Circuit Rd):
    Bt Merah Interchange, (Bt Merah Central), Henderson Rd, Tiong Bahru Rd, Outram Rd, Eu Tong Sen St, Hill St, Stamford Rd, Selegie Rd, Serangoon Rd, MacPherson Rd, Aljunied Rd, Merpati Rd, Circuit Rd, Pipit Rd and Circuit Rd.
    And return via Circuit Rd, Pipit Rd and Circuit Rd, Merpati Rd, Mattar Rd, Aljunied Rd, MacPherson Rd, Bendemeer Rd, Jln Besar, Bencoolen St, Bras Basah Rd, North Bridge Rd, South Bridge Rd, Neil Rd, (Cantonment Rd), Outram Rd, Tiong Bahru Rd, Henderson Rd, (Bt Merah Central) and Bt Merah Interchange.

    CSS 9 (Geylang Lor 1 Fringe Car Park - Bt Merah):
    Geylang Lor 1 Fringe Car Park, Geylang Rd, Kallang Rd, Crawford St, North Bridge Rd, Bras Basah Rd, Nicoll Highway, Connaught Dr, (Fullerton Rd), Collyer Quay, Raffles Quay, Shenton Way, (Keppel Rd), Cantonment Link, Cantonment Rd, Outram Rd, Tiong Bahru Rd, Lower Delta Rd, Telok Blangah Way, Henderson Rd, (Bt Merah Central) and Bt Merah Interchange.
    And return via Bt Merah Interchange, (Bt Merah Central), Henderson Rd, Telok Blangah Rd, Lower Delta Rd, Tiong Bahru Rd, Outram Rd, Cantonment Rd, (Keppel Rd), Anson Rd, Robinson Rd, (Collyer Quay, Fullerton Rd), Empress Place Car Park, (St Andrew's Rd, Stamford Rd), Victoria St, Kallang Rd, Sims Ave and Geylang Lor 1 Fringe Car Park.
    NOTE: Probably withdrawn after EWL Kallang Station opened.
