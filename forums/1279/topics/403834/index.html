    Originally posted by Operation//Overkill:

    define floating gears?

    Its a way to shift gears without using the clutch by shifting gears based on the engine's rpm.

    Its not something easily explained, but basically the concept is that when yr vehicle is moving on the road, the transmission is turning at a certain rpm based on the vehicle speed. the engine and gears would then have to match to this rpm when changing gears.

    Heres a pretty good vid on it: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jLw0hAtOik4

    Google also have good info on it.
