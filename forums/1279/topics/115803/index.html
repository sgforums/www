    Originally posted by SBS3624G:
    Have a question: Are there 2 types of mkIIIs, I find that some mkIIIs sound like mkIIs minus the beginning whine, and others sound like the mkIV dm3500. The latter sound horrible and picks up damn slow, while the former has performance like the mkIV dm3500.
    There are no 2 types of MK3s, all Mk3s are the same. The 2 types you are talking about must be the fact that different gearboxes are installed in the Mk3s. Most Mk3s have Voith gearboxes and sound like the Mk4 DM3500 (very long, drawn out first gear, only 3 gears). Only 2 Mk3s have ZF gearboxes (short first gear, sound like Mk2s and Mk4 DMs and Striders, 4 gears), 876R and 877M. 876R is BN31 and 877M is AR195. Nice buses since they seem to have better performance than Voith Mk3s
