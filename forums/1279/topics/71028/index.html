    Commentary on Profit Before Tax (PBT)

    Full Year 2003 vs Full Year 2002

    Group PBT for the year ended 31 decembere 2003 declined by 40.9% or $17.7m to $25.7m compared to $43.4m for the corresponding period last year. PBT for the Transport Business decreased by 64.8% or $13.4m to $7.3m mainly due to the lower bus fare revenue as a result of SARS outbreak and rationalisation of bus routes, restructuring costs, write-down of rail development costs and rail revenue being insufficient to cover the operating expenses. These were partially offset by lower bis operating costs and a payment for rail commissioning costs from LTA. PBT for Advertisement Business of $13.2m was 31.55 or $6.1m lower than last year due to weak market conditions and keen competition. Other income of $5.1m was 54.9%or $1.8m higher than last year mainly due to rental incomefrom the lease of commercial space in North-East Line (NEL) MRT and Sengkang LRT stations, offset by lower investment income.
