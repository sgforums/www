    Why did bus driver take my ez-link card?

    I AM a Secondary 5 student waiting for my O-level examination results.

    On 20 Jan, I went to top up my student ez-link card and, to my dismay, I was told that I had to top up a minimum of $10 (adult rate) instead of my usual $5 (student rate).

    I didn't do so and paid for my bus fare with coins while showing my student ez-link card.

    When I boarded bus 168 that day at around 8pm at Tampines St 71, I did as I had previously - paid 55 cents while showing my ez-link card.

    However, the bus driver asked me to tap my card at the reader and I did so. Then he told me that my card was an adult card. Stunned, I told him I am a student.

    He took my card from me, printed a receipt and told me to call the number on the receipt after five working days. When I asked for my card back, he simply said: 'Cannot.' When I asked why, there was no answer and he started to drive off.

    I was dumbstruck. I didn't even know why my card was taken and I still don't know why.

    Now, I have to pay adult fare until I get my card back.

    Does this mean that because I am waiting for my exam results, I am not considered a student anymore?

    Why couldn't the bus driver just tell me why he took away my card?
