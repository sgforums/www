    WRONG! Its beside RI/RJC. Kong meng san is the Bright hill temple, his one is a another one called PEK SAN THENG(bi shan ting). There take SBST 93/156/157/163 to its doorstep from Macritchie. Saw quite a lot of light decorations today(morning) and dint know why. Forgot about Qing Ming Jie

    If you want to walk in from a more "bright" way, take Sv156 from Bishan MRT to Braddell Rd(Bradell-Westlake Sec) then cross the non-sheltered overhead bridge and then you walk a bit to the entrance.
