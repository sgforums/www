    From what I personally observed:

    TIB311K to TIB319P - Svc 167 (perm)
    TIB337L to TIB351U - Svc 854 (perm)
    TIB353P to TIB361R - Svc 852 (perm)
    Nissans TIB362M to TIB377X (except TIB369U, which started as SP) - Svc 854 (perm)
    TIB380K to TIB389L - Old svc 950 (perm)
    TIB562C - Svc 854 (non perm)
    TIB614L to TIB624H - Svc 171 (perm)
    TIB725A - Svc 805 (non perm)
    TIB754R to TIB756K - Svc 812 (perm)
    TIB784D - Svc 804 (perm)
    TIB833X to TIB848D (Except TIB838H) - Svc 851 (perm) (mentioned)
    TIB838H - Svc 171 (publicised but non perm) (mentioned)
    TIB849B - Either Svc 851 or Svc 812 (non perm)
    TIB850X, TIB851T, TIB852R, TIB854K - Svc 851 (perm)
    TIB973A, TIB974Y - Svc 67 (perm)
    TIB975U, TIB976S - Svc 67 (non perm) (disputed/debated)
    TIB977P, TIB978L - Svc 67 (perm)
    TIB995L, TIB996J - Svc 854 (perm)
    TIB1002X - Svc 854 (non perm)
    TIB1017D, TIB1018B - Svc 853 (perm for a while before moving to 851)
    TIB1025E - Svc 190 (perm for a while)
    TIB1257C - Svc 852?? (non perm)

    Some other firsts:

    Svc 167 was first TIBS service to have aircon buses, followed by svc 850 and then svc 852. However there had only 2 AC buses. First FAC TIBS service was the old 950, followed by 951 and 952. First PAC service (at least for Yishun) was Svc 854, followed by Svc 851. However Svc 851 was the first service in Yishun to receive perm bendies (171 only had TIB838H for a small while). Svc 854 received bendies only after Svc 851 did. First FAC service in Yishun was service 855. The rest of the non-FAC TIB services (by now all PAC) gradually and quietly transited to FAC status after the last of NAC TIBS buses were either scrapped or converted to CAC.

    Statements above open to debate and/or corrections.
