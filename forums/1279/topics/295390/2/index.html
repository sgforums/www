    Although not first deployment, but as I could remember, ex-KJDEP buses of service 989 were 432U, 436J and 479K which were transferred to other depots after the service was withdrawn, while the others stayed in KJDEP and 'helped' out other services.
    As memories held, 475X went to 189 after which to 67, 492X and 547Y went to 188, 493T went to 176, although info on sgwiki indicated 61 first, 520Z went immediately to 189 and stayed ever since, 521X, 537B, 539X, 611U and 800R went to 106 and stayed there as well. 543H went to 190, 591T went to 925 first, then spare and now serving multiple roles as shuttle and regular buses. 600B and 608E went over to 67, 605M served 189 for quite a while before going over to 176. 792E, which was seen many times on 989, cannot be confirmed as a perm and so is left out of this picture. Hino, DAF and Volgren Merc were some of those which also served on the route as cameos.
    And for those interested, there was actually a Habit 'serving' on 989 on the first day! Although the exact details whether it entered Changi International PTB or even ran the entire route is still sketchy, the rego can be certain and still can be found on the TIBS news magazine in the National Library.
    The bus is TIB1133B (KJ 106)
    Have fun!
