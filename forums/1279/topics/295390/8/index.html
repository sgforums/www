    Originally posted by AntiDennisLance:
    854 turned PAC late 1993 or early 1994. 854 got all the UD's. 171 would be late 1993 or early 1994 too.
    Service 854 was the first Tibs service to become PAC, somewhere in late 1993, when the OAC Nissan UDs came into service.

    For service 171, it was SBS that converted it into a PAC service. The service used SBS' NAC and OAC Merz buses from AMDEP. Tibs took over service 171 only in 1995, where NAC Nissan SCNs and OAC Hispano Merz were deployed. All of service 171's NAC Nissan fleets were redeployed to Scania ELBOs only in mid 1996.
