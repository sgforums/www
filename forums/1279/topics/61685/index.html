    What do you mean by "halfway", from stops or interchanges / terminals?

    If it's a short working trip, the bus could be schedule to pick up passengers at the other terminal (e.g. Service 2A went back to Changi Village to pick up passengers).

    If it's from interchange, like services 161 and 168, their buses go back to HGDEP and BNDEP respectively for re-fuel (most likely) during mid day.

    The most likely place is the depot. They can change shift, buses or re-fuel and do some simple maintainence.
