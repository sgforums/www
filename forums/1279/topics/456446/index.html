  • No_10_Tomas
    31 Jul 12, 15:25

    i don't think low tree branches are considered as official height restrictions

    low dangling or protruding tree branches are supposed to be trimmed periodically by the authorities!! otherwise it may pose a hazard to vehicles and even to pedestrians crossing the road...who knows when they will detach and drop off during heavy showers or windy days

    height restricted zones will be indicated by a signboard that legally prohibits a certain height...like those multi storey carparks or changi airport basement

    it's a myth to deduce that depot road restricts DDs due to tree branches, it's only hearsay and rumours, it's not official

    there are only two ways by which you can enter depot road, either via (southbound) Henderson Road or Alexandra Road

    i don't see any height restriction signs just before entry to depot road to prohibit any kind of vehicles

    in fact i've seen off-service DDs on depot road before..very rare..but it happened
