    The bus driver of bus number 132.

    It was raining. I boarded the bus at around 4.40pm and tapped my ezlink card and realized that both my ezlink cards have no value. I tried searching my bag for coins but could not find any. Then, I approached the driver and told him that I was sorry, I din bring coins with me. I took out a $2 note and wanted to give him.

    To my surprise, he said "never mind, you sit down." and he never ask me to pay..I sat all the way till I reached the bus interchange. I thanked him and he even said Bye to me.

    I dun wana post his vehicle number, afraid that will bring him trouble. But if he or his relative is reading this, just want to say thank you again. He made my day~!

    I ever saw bus driver delaying all the passengers just to get a student to pay the fares, bcos her ezlink card got no value. He even shouted at her. That's why I feel that this bus driver that I met is so kind..he din ask me to pay, he allowed me to till the last stop and was polite to me.

    I remember last time I dinoe my way, was trying to go to Ang Mo Kio, a particular bulding..that driver dinoe the building too. I wanted to alight and search for it myself, tapped the card and wanted to alight. He asked me to sit and alight at another stop, maybe the distance will be shorter. And he told me nvm that I already tapped my card. A very nice uncle also.
