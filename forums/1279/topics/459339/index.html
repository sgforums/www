    TIB676H, the first DAF SB220LT, will be de-registering on 5 October 2012, provided that the bus is not given a 2-year lifespan extension. Therefore, this thread is created to record the lifespan extensions (if any), lay-up, de-registration or redeployments of these buses.

    These are the de-registration dates of the DAF SB220LTs.

    5 Oct 2012: TIB676H
    27 Oct 2012: TIB677E - TIB679A
    9 Nov 2012: TIB681S, TIB682P
    30 Nov 2012: TIB683L, TIB684J, TIB686D - TIB688Z
    6 Dec 2012: TIB685G
    19 Dec 2012: TIB689X - TIB694E
    22 Dec 2013: TIB697Y, TIB698U, TIB700X, TIB720M
    31 Mar 2014: TIB695C, TIB699S, TIB701T - TIB707C
    25 Sep 2014: TIB708A, TIB709Y
    21 Nov 2014: TIB710S - TIB717Z
    27 Nov 2014: TIB719T
    1 Dec 2014: TIB721K - TIB723E
