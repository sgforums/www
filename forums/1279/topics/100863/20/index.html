    207 is both directional. In sgwiki, use "-" instead of "<->", all bus routes in JB are both directional. AFAIK, during the morning time, 207 will ply to town before heading to Larkin.

    Other updates

    16 (since introduction of 777) and 226 no longer available.
    Last trip for 15 from JBCS is 2345.
    77 and 188 only ply between Tiram and JB town, not Larkin.
    GML Line buses have service numbers, AFAIK, G3 (Larkin-Senai Airport) and G8 (Larkin-Masai/Pasir Gudang) [new service].
