    Johor Bahru bus routes

    10 (loop): Permas Jaya <-> Johor Bahru (thru Bakar Batu and Jalan Stulang Darat)
    10A (loop): Bakar Batu <-> Johor Bahru (thru Tama Pelangi, Taman Seri Tebrau and Taman Sentosa)

    11 (loop): Stulang Laut <-> Johor Bahru (thru Jalan Stulang Laut, Stulang Laut is where to Tax Free Complex is located)

    15 (loop): Taman Selesa Jaya <-> Johor Bahru (thru Taman Ungku Tun Aminah, Skudai, Tampoi, Danga Bay and Hospital)

    16 (loop): Kulai <-> Johor Bahru (thru Senai, Saleng, Skudai, Tampoi, Danga Bay and Hospital)

    39: Masai <-> Larkin (thru Plentong, Giant Hypermarket, Johor Jaya, Carrefour, Pandan and Johor Bahru town)

    41: Larkin <-> Kota Tinggi (thru Johor Bahru town, Pandan, Carrefour, Desa Cemerlang, Taman Desa Jaya and Tiram)

    42 (loop): Taman Daya <-> Johor Bahru (thru Kangkar Tebrau, Pandan, Holiday Plaza and make a loop in town)

    44 (loop): Taman Daya <-> Johor Bahru (thru Kangkar Tebrau, Pandan and make a loop in town, does not pass by Holiday Plaza)

    52 (loop): Taman Daya <-> Johor Bahru (same as 44)
    101 (loop): same as 10A
    123 (loop): Permas Jaya <-> Johor Bahru (same as 10)
    133: Masai <-> Larkin (same as 39)
    133A: Taman Kota Masai <-> Larkin (same as 39)
    138 (loop): Taman Megah Ria <-> Johor Bahru (thru Pasir Gudang Highway, Carrefour, Pandan and make a loop in Johor Bahru town)

    140: Larkin <-> Pasir Gudang (thru Johor Bahru town, Pandan, Pasir Gudang Highway and Taman Air Biru, Pasir Gudang)

    180 (loop): Desa Harmoni/Taman Molek <-> Johor Bahru ( thru Pandan, Holiday Plaza, Plaza Best World and make a loop in Johor Bahru town)

    188: Larkin <-> Tiram ( thru Johor Bahru town, Pandan, Carrefour, Desa Cemerlang and Taman Desa Jaya)

    205: Larkin <-> Tiram (same as 18
    207: Larkin <-> Senai ( thru Tampoi, Skudai, Saleng and Senai)
    208: Larkin <-> Masai (same as 39)
    210 (loop): Kempas <-> Larkin <-> Johor Bahru
    211 (loop): Kg. Melayu Majidee <-> Johor Bahru ( thru Holiday Plaza)
    212 (loop): Kg. Melayu Majidee <-> Johor Bahru ( same as 211)

    224: Larkin <-> Pasir Gudang (same as 140)
    225 (loop): Taman Johor Jaya <-> Johor Bahru ( thru Carrefour, Pandan and make a loop in Johor Bahru town)
    226 (loop): Taman Ungku Tun Aminah <-> Johor Bahru (same as 15)
    227: Larkin <-> Kota Tinggi ( same as 41)

    301 (loop): Taman Perling <-> Johor Bahru ( thru Tampoi, Danga Bay, Hospital and make a loop in Johor Bahru town)

    321 (loop): Taman Skudai Jaya <-> Johor Bahru ( thru Skudai Parade, Skudai, Tampoi, Danga Bay, hospital and make a loop in Johor Bahru town)

    322 (loop): Taman JB <-> Johor Bahru ( thru Skudai Parade, Skudai, Tampoi, Danga Bay, hospital and make a loop in Johor Bahru town)

    323 (loop): Taman Seri Skudai <-> Johor Bahru ( thru Skudai Parade, Skudai, Tampoi, Danga Bay, hospital and make a loop in Johor Bahru town)

    328 (loop): Taman Selesa Jaya <-> Johor Bahru ( same as 15)

    331 (loop): Taman University <-> Johor Bahru ( thru UTM, Skudai Parade, Skudai, Tampoi, Danga Bay, hospital and make a loop in town)

    347: Larkin <-> Layang Layang ( thru Tampoi, Skudai, Saleng, Senai and Kulai)

    348 (loop): Taman Seri Teratai <-> Johor Bahru ( thru Skudai Parade, Skudai, Tampoi, Danga Bay, hospital and make a loop in town)

    Transit Link is a conglomeration of several bus companies such as T. Hakkim, ALEC, South Johore, Zoomlink, Pantai Indah etc. Although they carry the name Transit Link in general, each bus/route is still owned by the respective companies. For eg: sv. 41 is run by ALEC and the O405 buses are from Zoomlink. Apart from Maju, Handal Indah and Triton, the rest of the companies are not based in JB. This includes S & S, PGBS etc.
