    Tour group is advisable, unless you have a friend that and he/she is willing to bring you around. Avoid driving into the downtown area, the streets are quite narrow such that congestion often occurs.

    Express bus from Larkin is RM14.60 per way per pax. Tickets are hotcakes in Melaka Sentral, buy earlier for the return ticket to JB or SG. I happened to fail to get the ticket, end up got to take another bus to Kluang (via Muar) and followed by another bus to JB. LOL!!

    Besides historical places, such as the Statutes (red houses) and some churches, there's also Stamp Museum, Independence Museum, Historical Museum, Bukit Senjuang (St John Hill), Jonker Street, Cheng Hoon Teng Temple. On the other hand, the Mosques there have squarish pagodas.

    Main shopping centres are Soon Seng Plaza (electronics/pirated vcds), Ocean, Mahkota Parade (Parkson/Giant/Cinema), Tesco (opposite Melaka Sentral), Jusco (Ayer Keroh).
