    Buses

    Other than 226, Huang Hou Hua Yuan (in Malay, Taman Ungku Tun Aminah a.k.a TUTA) is also served by....

    1. City Bus (orange/red) 15, 328
    2. Triton (navy blue/yellow/red) 502
    3. Bas Mini (peach/brown)

    Board the bus and tell the driver "Aminah", and you will be guaranteed to reach TUTA.

    Location

    TUTA is situated in Skudai (about 15km Northwest of JB, after Tampoi), along Skudai Highway after Bandar Sutera (Wu fu cheng shi) / Xtra Supercenter Skudai / Kolej Selatan (Nan fang xue yuan), and turn left at Skudai flyover / SRJK Kuo Kuang (2) overhead bridge, about 100m more, you will see the row of shophouses in the main street of TUTA.
