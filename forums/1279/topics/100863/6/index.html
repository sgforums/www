    tks
    Danga Bay ( about 5 km ) is a new seaside recreation project, along the seaside ( turn left from Checkpoint, continue straight all the way, 2~3km after hospital )

    Taman University ( about 20km away ) is situated near UTM ( University Teknologi Malaysia ), on the way to Pontian / Kukup.

    Skudai is the area, Northwest of JB, Taman University is one of the housing estates there.

    East of JB includes Pandan, Masai, Plentong, Pasir Gudang, Ulu Tiram, further up north you will reach Kota Tinggi using route 3.

    West of JB includes Tampoi, Kempas, Skudai, Kulai, Senai, further west you will reach Pontian using route 5.
