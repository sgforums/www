    From Larkin, take Maju Pontian Express 96. Alternatively, take South Johore express services. It is an express service, but sometimes, it will stop along Skudai to pick up some passengers, as for return trip, sometimes along Pekan Nenas.     Bus departs every 30-45 minutes, depends on the passenger load basis. Fare for express service is RM4.00 or RM3.80, differs from operator to operator.

    This service, I have took with my family before, in Dec 2004 Malaysia trip.

    It is ran by Transitlink and probably on one bus (hence its awfully long waiting time). It is a loop service given the arrangement of roads at Ayer Hitam, and its nature to reverse and turnaround at the foot of Bukit Bendera (in front of the Penang Hill Tramway Terminus).

    Also, at the Ayer Hitam stop, it is NOT a terminal. It is just a roadside stop, with no staff or shelter to shelter us from the scorching heat as we waited for the sv8.

    Finally, when it came, the bus looked dilapidated (with aircon though), with no proper placards. (Information only written on the carboards, not the plastic desto.)

    And when we boarded the bus, not many passengers were onboard. (Maybe only my family were the only passengers then.)

    Also when it was dark, there may not be a sv8 bus taking us down from the foot of Bukit Bendera (the Penang Hill Tramway) to the nearest connection for Georgetown at Ayer Hitam. (In this case, we took a minibus Bas Mini at nearby bus stop directly back to the KOMTAR.)
