    No SG-JB bus will stop @ the bus stop adjacent to City Square. 160 / CW1 / 950 are only permitted to allow passengers to alight @ Kota Raya Terminal, and 170 allowed @ both Kota Raya and Larkin.

    Only JB local buses are allowed to stop @ City Square. So, best way to go to City Square is either:

    1. Discontinue your journey @ JB Checkpoint and walk to City Square, just 300m away.

    2. Take 170 / 160 / 950 / CW1 up to Kota Raya Terminal, get down @ there, walk along Jalan Terus* ( follow the traffic flow, Jalan Terus is one way street ), turn left @ the staircase @ Public Bank, go down the staircase, and you will see an overhead bridge at the left side, cross the overhead bridge, you will be linked to City Square.

    * Jalan Terus is the one way street adjacent to the Kota Raya Terminal.
