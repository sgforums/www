    How can bus services to Orchard Road be improved?

    As one of Singapore's prime stretch of real estate, it suffers from constant traffic jams especially on weekends and public holidays. ERP has not lessened the number of cars, taxis that compete with public buses for road space.

    Are bus services to Orchard Road well-utilized? Are the bus routes well-planned? What areas of improvements can you think of?

    Some bus services which serve Orchard/Scotts Road:
    7 14 16 36 54 65 89 105 111 123 124 132 143 162 167 174 190 502 518 700 C1.

    Which bus routes can be further improved?

    Do you think any bus services should be withdrawn or increased?
    Are staggered bus stops a good idea?
    Are there too many or too few bus services serving Orchard Road?
    Should some bus services use only double deckers?
    Do other bus services need bendys?

    LTA did a major study sometime back to improve the congestion problem. But.. sigh...

    I think the biggest improvement that shd be done is to move all bus stops to the right of Orchard Road, similar to the bus stop arrangement outside Orchard MRT along Orchard Blvd.

    Most congestion is caused by cars turning left into the 2 CTE entrances, as well as into shopping centres like PS. Buses need to filter right to get pass, resulting in the centre lanes slowing down.

    Whether there is sufficient space on the right of Orchard Road is highly subjective. I think there is.

    For example, I am sure Ngee Ann city won't mind sacrificing some of the open space in front of their complex to make a bus bay. There are URA carparks on both sides of Specialist Shopping Centre to accomodate a bus bay, same for the green space opposite Meridien. Even the space above Dhoby Ghaut MRT is available.

    Also.. bus lanes should be applied 24 hours along Orchard Road, since the regular Bus Lane hours do not match the peak hours at Orchard Road.
