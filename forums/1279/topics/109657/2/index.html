    To summarise in short:

    1993: Service 16 extended from Marine Parade terminal to Changi Airport PTB, to cover part of ex service 149 route. Service 390 withdrawn with service 16 as the replacement service.

    Jan 1994: Service 16E introduced as peak-hours one-directional service (Bt Merah - Changi Airport), after receiving feedbacks from the public.

    Dec 1997: Service 16E renumbered service 36 under a renumbering exercise, with route and operation hours unchanged. A total of 3 services were renumbered in this exercise, with the others 2 services being M1 to 92 and M2 to 42.

    Sep 1998: Service 36 amended to become Changi Airport - Tomlinson Rd loop service operating on a whole-day schedule. Service 16 shortened from Changi Airport PTB to Marine Parade terminal.
