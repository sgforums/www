    A general guideline:

    Trunk Peak: T=2.52X+5.6 where T is time in minutes and X is no. of fare stages. Note that this formula gives an overestimate for services that are largely express like svc969. It should be accurate to 5mins, barring the fact of a major traffic congestion.

    Hope it helps
