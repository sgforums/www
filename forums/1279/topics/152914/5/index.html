    I always wonder why service 966 never ply Bukit Panjang New Town. This is because Bukit Panjang residents cannot get to Eunoes and Marine Parade area. Further more, 966 serve 3 very strategic bus stops

    1) Trellis Tower bus stop (at PIE) -- links to Toa Payoh/Balestier

    2) Jalan Toa Payoh bus stop just before the Macpherson Road- Serangoon Road junction-- Links to Potong Pasir (bus 142), Boon Keng (bus 31), etc.

    3) Jalan Eunoes bus stop (outside Eunoes MRT station)-- Links to Bedok Reservoir, Geylang Road (bus 51) etc. Too many possibilities.

    With these 3 bus stops, Woodlands and Bukit Panjang residents can get to transfer effectively. For example, last week, I need to go to MacPherson area. So I board 966 and alight at bus stop (2) as mentioned above. From there, I transfer to bus 90.

    However, there are no trunk services that can reach the bus-stop outside police academy (along PIE) that can allow the Bukit Panjang residents to transferif they board service 960,190 and 700. These services all exit at Whitley Road and cannot get to transfer to 966. It sounds silly for them to board bus 187,963 back to Woodlands and then transfer to 966 just to reach Marine Parade/Eunoes.

    Maybe 966 should ply along Woodlands Road and then turn into Bukit Panjang Road to serve the residents (similar to 960) before continuing towards Marine Parade via the expressways.
