    I have a map of the Singapore Rail Network in 2030, but unfortunately it has been displaced. I got that map from Berita Harian 10 years ago. On that map, I remember the NEL was extended from Punggol to Marina Centre via Pulau Ubin, Changi Airport, East Coast and Marine Parade. Others include:

    Buona Vista LRT - from Buona Vista MRT to Sentosa via Science Park and Pasir Panjang.

    Western Extension of EWL to Tuas via Upper Jurong

    The Circle Line via National Stadium, Paya Lebar, Serangoon, Bishan and Buona Vista

    Yishun to Pasir Ris via Punggol NEL Station

    Those are just someof the proposed MRT/LRT Lines that I can remember.

    Originally posted by oliver_yuen87:
    But in the newest proposed MRT map in Straits Times last year or two years ago, these tracks are not shown. Does that mean they have been shelved?
    Not sure if some of them have been shelved. But I'm certain that some of the mentioned ones have been re-planned. Examples are:

    Buona Vista LRT - now to be Stage 5 of Circle Line, new rail system to be built at Sentosa from HarbourFront replacing the current Monorail

    Extension of NEL to Marina Centre via Ubin/Airport/East Coast - this plan was shelved a few years ago, re-emerged as Eastern Regional Line covering Kallang, Kaki Bukit, Tampines and Marine Parade

    Extension of EWL to Tuas - government to consider this line very seriously as demand for this line has grown considerably.

    I've remembered another proposed LRT system from Tampines to Changi Airport as well as a new MRT Line from Hougang/Sengkang to Tampines, as future MRT/LRT Lines.
