    913 for Woodlands Circle to Woodlands Interchange
    168 for Woodlands Interchange to Tampines OR
    969 for Woodlands Interchange to Tampines Interchange

    I can't specifically tell which would be most convenient for you; neither did you specify the school you have to go to. But here are some info on the two services that you can take to Tampines:

    168: To Tampines, it should take about 35 - 45 mins to arrive. However, if you wish to go to Tampines Interchange, you will have to (a) alight at the bus stop opposite Tampines Regional Library. cross the road and transfer to another service to reach Tampines Interchange (1 stop), (b) alight at the Blk 242, Tampines Ave 4 bus stop and walk past Tampines MRT into Tampines Interchange, or (c) alight at the bus stop opposite Century Square and transfer to Service 3 (2 stops).

    969: No idea of how long it takes to Tampines, but the service will have to pass through Yishun before it hits the expressway; therefore, it may probably take longer to reach Tampines if you're directly using this service to reach Tampines. Furthermore, there will be no stops from the last stop in Yishun all the way to Tampines; the bus will just go straight to Tampines Interchange once it leaves the TPE.

    So it's your choice, you decide...
