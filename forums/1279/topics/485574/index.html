    SBS Transit Service 63M is a trunk route running from Eunos Interchange and looping at MacPherson Estate, passing through Ubi Avenue 1. Its parent route is Service 63, and was introduced to replace Shortworking Trip 63A.

    On 13th July 2014, Service 63M will be converted into a full-day service under the Bus Service Enhancement Programme (BSEP), with additional buses added but no change in route. This is the first time that an extension of service hours has been classified under the BSEP.
