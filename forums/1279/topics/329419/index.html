    SMRT to introduce six new premium bus services

    SINGAPORE: Commuters living in Toa Payoh, Sunset Way, Choa Chu Kang, Bukit Panjang and Hougang can look forward to more options when travelling to work in the morning from September 8.

    SMRT is introducing six new premium bus services, in response to higher demand.

    These services will run from the heartlands to Jurong East, Orchard Road, Shenton Way and Robinson Road during morning peak hours on weekdays. Fares range between S$3.50 and S$4.50.

    SMRT said ridership for its premium bus services has grown by more than 10 per cent since April this year.  - CNA/so
