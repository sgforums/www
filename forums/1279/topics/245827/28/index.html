    Those buses you mentioned above are not cameo appearances; in fact, they are x-overs.

    From today till the end of July, 179 is adopting the 'NTU Holiday timetable' (in my own words), and only 13 buses will be used throughout this period. The other 179 buses become temporary SP buses.

    For more info (by Stooper),

    http://buses.sgforums.com/?action=thread_display&thread_id=245827&page=21

    The 13 buses used for this year's Holiday timetable are:

    The 3 A/P Shift buses: 373A, 9031C, 9049C
    Another 2 buses that run full daytime shift only: I am trying to find out (don't seem to be 7236S and 7238L)
    8 split shift buses: 9111E, 9092B, 9128H, 9140X, 9064H, 9088P, I am trying to find out, I am trying to find out

    Interestingly, none of the 13 buses seems to consist of the 5 VO3x, so you may get to see the 5 179 VO3x on other SL services throughout May, June and July.

    The list in SG Wiki hasn't been updated yet.
