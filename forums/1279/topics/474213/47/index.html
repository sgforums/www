    There are these 4 services A - D.
    A and C are single depot controlled.
    B and D are dual depot controlled.
    B was initially single depot but was ammended to be dual depot.
    A B and C  used to come from the same depot until B and C moved to a nearby depot.
    B, C and A(note the order) are consecutive services

    C and D start their journey from interchange E, and exit from the same side. But the two split up immediately after exiting.

    A comes out from its interchange, and meets C at a bus stop near MRT station F which is near [A's] terminus.They come to a traffic junction, where A split and go one big round, pass by its interchange one more time, before rejoining C at Road G. Both services will travel together till they reach A's terminating point.

    B come from the same interchange as A but they exit at different sides and don't really meet at all along their route. The only time they meet is the time where A is turning into the interchange.

    Anyway carrying on, after the interchange, B and C travel together till they reach a junction near hospital H. where C will will make a detour before rejoining with B and both will continue on their journey till the reach expressway I. B will remain on Avenue J and proceed to its looping point, while C will mount up the expressway and rejoins with D.

    Both services will travel together until they reach Exit K, where D will exit expressway L and proceed to its terminating point, while C will remain on the expressway  and go to its looping point.

    What are services A - D, Interchange E, MRT Station F, Road G, Hospital H, Expressways I and L and Exit K and Avenue J?
