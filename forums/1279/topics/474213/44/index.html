    ANSWERS:

    There are these nine svcs, A - I. 

    Svc A (124) "replaced" a non-basic service (Parks 409) upon extension. It however turns towards the opposite direction when exiting the Int (HarbourFront Int) unlike the non-basic service. It meets with svc B - D somewhere along it route (Bras Basah).

    Svc B (857) shares an interchange (Yishun Bus Interchange) with another service here (852). It plies the same expressway (PIE, CTE) svc D would take, but does not meet on any of the expressway (confused with the map directions, so my very original statement was correct). It meets with four other svcs (124, 174/e, 518, 852) somewhere along its route (124, 174/e, 518: Bras Basah, 852: Yishun Int)

    Svc C (174/e)'s Fast Forward svc is the only of its kind that runs one of its express sectors not on the expressway, but directly next to the expressway itself (Jurong Rd, next to PIE). It shares a terminus (Boon Lay Bus Interchange) with svc E here. It meets with all other but two svcs (87, 89) here. 

    Svc D (Express 518) is known as the "sightseeing route" in the heartland it served as it plied the city and a full view of Marina Bay. It meets with five other services here (87 (just remembered sorry), 89, 124, 174/e, 857).

    Svc E (180) shares a terminus (Boon Lay Bus Interchange) with svc C. It is a loop svc. Its looping point (Jelebu Rd) is around the terminating point (Bukit Panjang Bus Interchange) of another svc here (184). It meets with two other svcs here (174/e, 184). 

    Svc F (87) runs together with svc H from the first stop after it came out from the interchange (Sengkang Bus Interchange) all the way until svc H turns into its terminus (Hougang Central Bus Interchange). This svc was involved in a fatal accident (July 2010, Bedok North Rd) which cost a bus (SBS9400X). It meets with only svc H throughout its route. 

    Svc G (852) runs from SMRT territory (Bukit Batok) into non-SMRT territory (Bukit Timah/Thomson), and back to SMRT territory (Yishun) again. However it is not a loop svc. In other words it goes from A to "foreign land" and terminate at B. It meets with three other svcs here (174/e, 184, 857). 

    Svc H (89) shares a similar trait with another svc here (174, both adopt the Fast Forward scheme). From the int (Hougang Central Bus Interchange) it runs together with svc F until F turns into its terminus (Sengkang Bus Interchange). Then it continues on and meet svc D, before separating with svc D and passing by svc D's terminus (Pasir Ris Bus Interchange, however not meeting svc D. It meets with these two svcs throughout its route. 

    Svc I (184) plies a polytechnic (Ngee Ann Ploytechnic). It loops at a place (Clementi Bus Interchange) where SMRT trains serves (EW23, Clementi MRT station), and is an SMRT svc. It meets with three other svcs here (174/e, 180, 852).
