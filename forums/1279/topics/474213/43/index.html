    Services B and D are consecutive services.

    Service C and D come from the same interchange. and exit at the same side, and both will travel together. At Avenue H, Service B joins in from Street I, and the 3 will travel for 1 stop. After the stop, they reach a traffic junction, where C will remain at Avenue H while B and D will proceed to Road J. Both service will travel together and bump into C at a traffic junction. this traffic junction is also where B will part from D. D will remain on Road K while B will turn and meet C at Road L near MRT station M and they will travel together until MRT Station N, where B will proceed to its nearby terminating point.

    Service A come from the same interchange as B, but they have nothing to do with each other. They travel together for a short distance then they will part and never meet. A will bump into C at its departure with B. But both services are going at different direction.

    Services C and D reunite at Road O, and both will travel and meet A at Road P. All 3 will travel together till they reach Q(name of road). Where D will part the 2 of them and proceed to its terminating point which is the same as A. However from Q onwards, D and A will not meet each other until their terminus.

    After passing by Q, A and C will travel together till they reach bus stop near Terminal R, both services will part and proceed to their respective terminating points.
