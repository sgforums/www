    There are these 4 services A - D.
    Only A is single depot controlled.
    A and B come from the same depot.
    B C and D used to come from the same depot until D moved.
    All 4 are SBST services
    3 of the services are WAB


    A starts its journey from Interchange E. It travels and meets C at Jalan F. it'll reach a junction where it splits and meets B. Both services will travel and  meet opposite direction C at Road G. All 3 services will travel till they reach a junction. At this junction B and C will split with A. After splitting, A will travel and meet D before they go to their terminating point. Both services terminate at the same interchange.

    After splitting with A, B and C will travel for 1 stop then they reach Avenue H junction. Both services will travel at Avenue H but at different directions. Both will meet D at Avenue H. For B, the D that it meets is going towards A's interchange. As for C, the D that it meets is going to its other terminating point.

    After splitting, B and C will not meet again until Drive I, and both of go to their terminating point. B and C terminate at the same interchange.

    What are Services A - D, Interchange E, Jalan F, Road G, Avenue H and Drive I?
