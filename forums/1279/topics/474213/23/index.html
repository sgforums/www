    Service 83 loops at a bus interchange. Service 119 used to terminate at this terminus, but was extended to the terminus of this service. It is one of two bus services that loops at this bus interchange. (the other being 965.)

    Service 119 uses a bus stop at the former Hougang South Bus Interchange. It has the same terminus (Punggol Bus Interchange) as Service 83.

    Service 156 is the only non-loop service here. It terminates at the looping point of service 83. It is also the only dual depot controlled bus service in this terminus. It shares a terminus (Clementi Bus Interchange) with the second WAB service (147).

    Service 27 meets all of these services near Hougang and Sengakang MRT stations. It is always fully packed during peak hours, but cannot use DDs due to height restrictions at its looping point (Changi Airport PTB bus terminal.)

    Service 112/113 terminates at the terminus of Service 27. It meets service 119 at the former Hougang South bus interchange.
