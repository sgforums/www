    Service 105

    Introduced in 1974 Bus Rationalisation, plying between Serangoon Gardens and Shenton Way, and was withdrawn on 20 December 1976. It was originally numbered 102A.

    Re-introduced on 13th March 1988, plying between Serangoon Interchange and Clementi Interchange.
