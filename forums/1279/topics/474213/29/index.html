    Service A and B are single depot controlled and are controlled by the same depot.

    Service A meets service C from its looping point at Road D. They will travel together all the way until Road E, where they will split, but will travel parallel with each other at different roads until Centre F. Then A will pass through a series of terrace houses, then A will continue to run parallel to C but this time at Road G. A will see, but not intersect, C again at a traffic junction before proceeding to its terminating point. Service B , though it starts at the same interchange as A, and is controlled by the same depot as A, has nothing to do with A. The only road that they meet is the road after the interchange. After that, they do not meet again. 

    C, after seeing A for the last time, meets B from MRT station H onwards. Then they will travel together till they reach bus stop near MRT station I. After that, they will part for a while, but they will meet again for 1 more stop at Avenue J. After that B will proceed to its looping point while A will proceed to its terminating point.

    What are services A, B and C, Roads D, E and G, Centre F, MRT Station H and I, and Avenue J?
