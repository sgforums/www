    There are these 4 services A - D
    A and B are single depot controlled and are controlled  by the same depot
    C is single depot its depot is very near A and B's depot.

    Anyway as mentioned above, A starts its journey at Terminal E. It travels solo(in this question's context) and past by C's terminus. It'll go one big round and meet C at Road F. Both services will travel till they reach a traffic junction. C will remain on Road G while A proceed to Road H.

    C will travel and will meet B at a junction near B's terminus. Both services will travel and meet A at Avenue I. All three will travel together until they reach A's terminating point. After ''sending A home'', B and C will continue on Avenue I.

    Both services will travel together till they reach a traffic junction where they will split. B will turn into Road J and proceed to its terminating point.

    C will go on to Avenue K and will travel solo till it meets D for 1 stop along Avenue L before going to its terminating point.

    D, ''after sending C home'', proceeds to its terminating point.

    What are service A - D, Terminal E, Roads F, G, H, and J Avenues I, K and L?
