    Service A is dual depot controlled.

    Services B and C are single depot controlled.

     

    One of the depot that controls Service A is the depot that controls B and C.

    Service B and C come from the same interchange D. From the interchange, they will travel together. Upon reaching Road E, A joins them and the 3 of them travel together till they reach a traffic junction where A will part them for a while.

    A will meet C again at Road F but this time at the opposite direction before proceeding to its terminating point.

    B and C, at the traffic junction where A parted, will travel together for 1 more stop, then they will part for a while. B will also meet C at Road F. And just like A, it's opposite directions for 2 stops before B proceed to its terminating point which is the same as A. C will proceed to its terminating point after meeting A and B at the opposite directions.

    After its first departure with C, B immediately meets A again. A will proceed to their terminating point. But B is not going in yet. It will go one big round, before finally going to the terminating point.

    What are Services A B and C, Interchange D, Roads E and F?

    All are SMRT services

    Service A come from starts its journey from interchange D and travel alone(in this question's context) until it reach MRT Station E, where C will join in from the interchange nearby. They will both travel together. Once the 2 of them reach Avenue F, B joins them in their journey. The 3 of them will travel together until they reach a traffic junction where A will remain on Road G while the other 2 will go to H(name of road) for 1 stop then the 2 will split

    On Weekdays & Saturdays, the junction mentioned above will be the last time that A will meet C, before A proceeds to its terminating point. But on Sundays/PH, A will meet C one more time for a short while at Avenue I  before A proceeds to its terminating point

    Service B, after splitting with A, meets C again for 1 stop at Road J then they will split again. They will go their seperate routes and will meet back again at expressway K and will travel together all the way till they reach their looping point.
