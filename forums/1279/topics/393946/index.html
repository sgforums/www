    They're usually given the authority by radio comms to return as soon as possible to the bus interchange due to them being exceptionally late, usually as a result of massive jams. Usually this is possible when the bus behind it is close to the bus ordered to return immediately.

    Think of it this way, if he were to continue picking up passengers, the behind bus will catch up and you'll have 2 buses running as late as the bus behind anyway. And there will be no buses at the interchange for those queueing to board it.
