    SBS was formed out of neccessity because the public transport system was a big mess. However, although the 3 bus companies merged on 1 July 1973, SBS remained inoperable. The companies that made up SBS showed no desire to merge their operations and continued to operate as seperate entities. The Government had to intervene to bring about true amalgamation which came about on 1 November 1973.
