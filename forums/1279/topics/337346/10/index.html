    Originally posted by TIB1234T:


    So. the 'M', 'R', 'X', etc. is like SBS svcs with 'M', 'A', 'B'?

    Not exactly, X is for partial express route... smethin like our Ffw but at normal prices... normali for routes to far out places like Yuen Loong... 3 digit buses beginning with 1XX is for the normal harbour crossing, 6XX for eastern harbour crossing, 9XX for western harbour crossing...
    P is for PEAK period operations, S is for special despatch[normali 1-5 buses]

    Check this website...
    http://www.nwstbus.com.hk/routes/routesearch.aspx?daytime=3&intLangID=1&section=routes&page=routeList
