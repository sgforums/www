    SBS has and did release scale models[mostly 1:76] to commemorate special events, eg 25th Anniversary, launch of SBS Transit...
    For the 25th Anniversary in 1998, they asked Corgi to produce the SBS "Superbus", a 3 axle double decker with route 25[SBS9426Y] and the 25th Anniversary decal pasted on buses windscreen in 1998.
    Corgi came out with the model but it is a pale and incorrect version of the actual Superbus in Singapore, there were major errors:
    1. Seating arrangement of the bus was that of KMB's version
    2. The top deck window is not "wrap around"
    3. No coinbox, farecard machine...
