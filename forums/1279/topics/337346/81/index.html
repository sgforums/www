    Book REVIEWS:

    Bus Review 2013 by Danny Chan (Northcord Publishing)



    A newly issued book by renowned Mr Danny Chan. For this annual issue, there is a focus on the N4BL, design and demonstrations.
    Point to note is also the nostalgic pictures, articles of old AEC Regent, DDs of Hong Kong buses in the 80s,90s.
    London had successfully completed the 2012 Olympics, the author looks at the buses(from all over UK)deployed for this significant event.
    There is also a section on the demise of the "Hotdog" buses in Hong Kong and the introduction of the Enviro 500 for both KMN, CTB. Detailed pictures and diagrams are available for that and also the Enviro400, BYD, Youngman
    Articles also write on how 268X, 373A were conceived by KMB
    Technology wise show the new bus stop info display for CTB and Cityflyer, the new aircon used for KMB aircon buses and much more

    Book Reviews:

    The SBST/SMRT bus fleet directory by Stanley Yung (BSI)


    Another new book launched in February with sole concentration on buses in SG. It has a comprehensive write out on buses used by SMRT, SBST, SBS from yesteryear Hawks, Leyland Atlanteans, O305, MK IIs to TIBS Hino(old school version), Nissan Diesel and more.
    Technical data on buses are also available on the excellent pages with pictures also showing SBS buses in use overseas.
    In the middle of the book are maps of different parts of SG with road and routes details! (same as the 1984 bus guide)

    A must get for local bus enthusiasts!
