    Creative Master Northcord Ltd ("CMNL") will be modelling First Glasgow's Volvo Olympian 30107, K174 EUX, which is SBS's ex-SBS 7204J. According to CMNL, the release of the model is hopefully expected to be launched by July 09.

M650 ELA (ex-SBS 7251Y) on the other hand has now come under Veolia Transport as 65010; was previously AV39 under Metroline. One of the best source I can find about the bus: http://groups.google.cz/group/Welsh-Bus-Photographs/browse_thread/thread/a7a4e916a8d14e3d.
