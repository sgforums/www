    Good comparison of choices... Here are mine:

    Models: I am a person who is super 'niao' (calculative) about technical (engine power and specs). This extends to models. If the real vehicle is underpowered or some cocky design plus no special livery/colors, then its a sure no-buy. Lousy or low specs but beautiful color/design (rarely the case) goes through consideration. Prices wise not more than SGD 100 or what its worth in my eyes.

    1/150 models: Definite for Japanese models, as I collect (powered) N-gauge model trains too, they are pretty useful for any diorama/comparisons.

    Books: Price justifies the book and what it contains. Translated versions I would be willing to pay only 10 - 50 percent less than original text. Most expensive hobby book that I have runs into the hundred (non-bus).
