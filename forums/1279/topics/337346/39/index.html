    Many thanks to those who came down to the Singapore Bus Model Show 2010 held last Saturday.

    This event also made several unofficial records in Singapore, namely the most 1:76 scale bus models gathered at a event, the most variety of bus models in one gathering (all continents except for the Americas, Africa and Antarctica) and of course the longest bus model show in Singapore (1630 to 2130 hrs, 5 hours).

    Participants: 17

    Models: 61 units 1:76, 1 unit 1:43. 59 units are unique (with exception of SBS/SBST VO and Citybus HK Scania K94UB).

    Asia: 30 units from Hong Kong (the most of a single location), 5 units from Singapore, 4 units from Japan.

    Europe: 20 units from United Kingdom.

    Australia: 2 units.

    Hampshire bus operator Solent Blue Line brought a splash of colour to the local transport scene when they reinvented their local network around Eastleigh as 'Red Rockets' in autumn 2006. Wanting to bring an element of fun and excitement to local bus travel, Solent Blue Line commissioned well known agency Best Impressions to design this striking red colour scheme.

    The new livery was applied to ten 'Mini Pointer Dart' single deckers, but the rebranding did not stop there! Each bus was decorated internally with vinyls promoting travel by Red Rocket and also recalling famous rockets in history. As a finishing touch, humorous slogans on a space theme were applied to both sides of each bus - all of them different - provoking a positive reaction from both passengers and passers by.
