    SBS Transit Press Release

    Temporary Diversion Of Bus Services Along Nicoll Highway

    Due to the collapse of Nicoll Highway, five SBS Transit Services, namely Services 10, 14, 16, 70 and 196 will be diverted to ply along Mountbatten Road, Geylang Road, Kallang Road, Jalan Sultan, Beach Road and Bras Basah Road before resuming the original route towards the city. In the return direction, these services would be diverted to ply Bras Basah Road / Stamford Road, Beach Road, Jalan Sultan, Sims Avenue, Geylang Lor 1 and Mountbatten Road until further notice.

    Meanwhile, we have deployed Traffic Inspectors and patrol cars on site to assist passengers at two pairs of bus stops along the affected stretch of road.
