    I think all of you need to understand one thing.

    The ezlink system is now about to check a student's ezlink card for its validity. The driver will set the reader to read your card. Messages like "Valid Pay Cash" etc will be shown. and of course the student pays the appropriate fare. If the card expires, you may need to surrender your ezlink card to the driver.

    Henceforth that message only appears if the driver adjusts the ezlink reader to check the card and not to record the entry of the card on the bus.
