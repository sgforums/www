    (After some consideration, I think I may want to come back posting here. I will only stick to posting inside information that I happen to come across, as well as the occasional bus photo and bus reviews. Threads discussing other topics (like the recent AMK bus depot fire), I will stay out of.)

    I'm glad someone posted a thread about the two new bus stops along Yishun Central 2. According to a notice posted at the Yishun Bus Interchange notice board (near the toilets), the new pair of bus stops are for an amended route 858, which will also be extended to cover Sembawang proper (restoring a once-dropped plan years ago to extend the route to cover Sembawang). The notice is to call for bus drivers to familiarise themselves with the new route, and was dated 8th of March, with the dates for the familiarisation set on the 14th and 15th if I remember correctly. According to a bus driver I spoke to, the route change will come into effect at the end of this month.

    As for the upgraded pair of bus stops along Yishun Central, I'm not sure as I have no information for any new or amended bus routes going there. Likely it might be for an extended route 812 (unlikely in my opinion though) or an amended trunk route, or may be even for a new bus route.
