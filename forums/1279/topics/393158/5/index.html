    Took SV858 on its second day of route amendment from Jln Kayu to Sembawang Stn @ 1631hrs @ Jln Kayu and second time from Woodlands Regional Int to Yishun Ave 2 @ 1840 @ WRI

    My overall observation is that Sembawang Residents is still not quite sure of the route amendment as spotted residents chatting over the SV858 route amendment. Demand for SV858 increases @ Yishun Stn towards SBW but still not many ppl alighted in SBW Area. Furthermore, for the trip towards CGA frm WRI, the demand usually come frm W'land area and not much frm SBW area. Boarding and alighting activity at SBW is almost zero. However, load at Yishun Ctrl 2 is average. But for a packed SV858 is almost difficut to carry the load at Yishun Ctrl 2.

    With regards to the headway, (Based on Duty 858PM09) The trip took 2 hours and 40 min for off peak timing,
    i.e: 1442 (WRI) > 1609 (CGA T2) > 1631 (Jln Kayu) > 1722 (WRI)

    And of cos the frequency departing from WRI is excellent. So overall SMRT did quite a good job in improving the frequency. But hopefully they would do something to PM Off Peak Freq which is arnd 11-19 min per bus.
