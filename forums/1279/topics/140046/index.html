    Erm.. please do not mix up with the bus interchange itself (which is the subject here) and the bus services which use the interchange.

    Woodlands Int has been just that and that ever since TIBS' times till now, with changes like control room for lift at MRT station above, closure of the last remaining provision shop next to 962's berth and new 966 berth.

    966/950 berth has been an alighting berth for feeders 900, 901 and 903. With the addition of this berth, buses still continue to use it as we cant possibly have buses creating jams alighting pax at the berths where 161/168 alights pax.

    Intra-town services 911, 912 and 913 now call at the berths towards checkpoint directly unless the drivers are ahead of time. This saves time as pax can board and alight at the same time. It also helps as there'll be space at the alightitng berths for other buses such as 161, 168, 962, 964, 965, 969 and 169 to alight pax.

    However, berths 1, 2 and 3 can be packed at times as 911, 912 and 913 buses park there for awhile before starting a new trip. Buses serving 856, 901, 900 and 903 also sometimes use these berths to park temporarily before starting a new journey as drivers just need to tap their card at the pax info counter before making another trip again. They will only park their buses at the proper bus park when their real break comes.
