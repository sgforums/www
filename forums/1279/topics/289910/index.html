    Next time, please kindly ask directions under the above link. Thanks.
    Anyway, to go from Bukit Batok to Paya Lebar (airport Rd), u can take 61 from Bukit Batok to MacPherson Rd and transfer to 90. It will take a long time.

    Or u can take 157 to Toa Payoh and transfer to 90.
    Or u can take 157 to Braddell Rd and transfer to 93.

    Or u can take 66, 157, 174 to Bukit Timah, transfer to 154 to Jalan Toa Payoh and transfer to 90
