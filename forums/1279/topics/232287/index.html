    When going to Boon Lay from Woodlands, I prefer to take 187 rathar than 178. Its because 187 goes by the BKE, which is nice. But I would always transfer to 180 at Bukit Panjang because 180's route is shorter. 187 would go round Bukit Batok and then through Jurong East and Jurong West.     I had to take 187 daily for 4 years and it was hopeless lah... especially in the peak hours. You can wait up to half an hour for a bus (at Jurong East area, heading to BNL). Then the bus is a full-to-the-door Nissan. Need to wait another 20min for another full-to-the-door 12m.
