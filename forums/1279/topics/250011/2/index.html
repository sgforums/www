  • TWS4793
    24 Apr 07, 21:59
    Favourites

    Service 5
    Goes to major places that I normally go such as Thomson Medical Centre, Great World City and even my tutor's home. Links to major towns such as Pasir Ris, Tampines, Bedok and Bukit Merah.

    Service 8 (2nd Position out of all Services)
    The fleet with the best selection of buses like SBS 1988U, SBS 2617J and SBS 0499X, Great for the trip to Toa Payoh Interchange.

    Service 21
    First service to operate B9TL's. I would use this service when I need to get onto B9TL's.

    Service 28
    Nice service to look at especially in the buses they used. A pity that the route is too long.

    Service 30
    Good route if I want to go and catch the 10.5m variants. Also got my favourite VSO SBS 9804P in this service.

    Service 51
    The longest service in Singapore. Uses a variety of buses and some of my favourites come from this service like SBS 7398H and the recently-become-spare-bus SBS 1865R.

    Service 145
    Nice selection of buses and goes to places I may go such as ICA Building, Vivocity (I haven't even gone there yet!) and my father's office (not going to mention where it is).

    Service 147
    Second longest service in Singapore. I felt it was a bit better than Service 51 in which part of this service is BBDEP based compared to Service 51's partly-SLBP based. Some nice buses such as SBS 0644T and SBS 7341X.

    Service 168 (1st Position out of all services)
    The service that I like the most. Another service with best selection of buses and good route. Compared to Service 18, 28 or 69, it shortcuts all three bus routes. Links to Bedok, Tampines and Woodlands.

    Townlink 293
    Nice selection of buses especially the Scania N113CRB's such as SBS 0076H, SBS 3663T and SBS 3664R.
