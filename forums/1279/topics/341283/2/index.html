    Does the sound of a bus matter? What we should compare is engine performance and not how much noise it makes. Is a quiet bus good? Is a noisy bus bad?

    Even so, a powerful bus is not totally good but a bus less powerful is not totally bad too. There are many other factors that come into place, above all which includes personal view and so on.

    Personally...

    1) The OC500LE has higher horsepower rating than the KUB
    2) The OC500LE consumes less fuel per KM travelled compared to the KUB
    3) The OC500LE is less jerky than the KUB (with some KUB sounding as if the underneath might just give way anytime - SBS 8327G of Sv36)

    Now, maybe you would say that you also want to factor in the sound of a bus. That I wouldn't stop you, but my point I want to make is that the amount of noise a bus makes isn't the main factor. You can have a loud roaring bus that drops you off at your destination much faster than a smooth quiet bus that may take a longer time to transport you to your destination. Given a choice, which would you choose?
