    Observation during heavy storm at bukit batok/jurong east ave 1

    1) six 185 buses stuck at junction of PIE & jurong town hall rd/bukit batok rd because they cannot turn right to e'way due to uprooted tree. return journey back to Soon Lee also affected because buses when leaving e'way, cant turn left to town hall due to tree branches. ALL vehicles were forced to turn right to bukit batok rd. because of this, IRIS for 185 are not available

    2)SBS7512U twrds BV also stop at slip road there before e'way, EDS off

    3) Massive Jam on both sides of Jurong East ave 1, starting from parc oasis condo till toh guan rd(toh guan direction) & starting from toh guan rd to after parc oasis condo (jurong west ave 1) as well as massive jam at junction of jurong east ave 1 & jurong town hall rd.

    4)Massive jam at jurong east ave 1 because tree branches fell at road, only 1 lane passable. even some road signs fly away from its original position & some uprooted too

    5) buses along jurong east ave 1 severely affected, especially 98, 187, 333, 502.others include 334, 198, 157, 99

    6)SBS3035G on 99 involved in minor accident. the exit door (left side) glass shattered, believed to be caused by fallen tree branch. EDS showed OFF SERVICE but saw that bus again, travelling along jurong east/west ave 1. 3035G accident happen at bus-stop outside parc oasis condo (twrds jurong)

    7)Unidentified Vo3x batch 1 (forgot rego) breakdown. saw red triangle with hazard light on. believe to be on 157. breakdown at bus-stop before PIE (twrds bukit batok)

    8)Jurong west ave 1 not affected at all

    9)Bukit Batok west ave 8 (both side) got majority of its trees uprooted. but 941 not affected as it can be passble in zig-zag manner (trees fell left & right lol)

    10)174 also affected (twrds boon lay). cant turn to jurong rd. instead, turn right to PIE.
    likewise twrds new bridge road, tree branch fell at stop line of jurong rd. i think 174 used PIE too to go to bukit batok.
