    Trains: SMRT trains:

    They are in this sequence: 3XXX,1XXX,2XXX,2XXX,1XXX,3XXX(the 3 rear cars are + or - 1 digit)

    First generation: Kawasaki Nippon Sharyo/Kinki Sharyo/Kawasaki heavy Industries/Tokyu Car Corpartion.Numbers mixed. Still having largest population of cars in SMRT.Bulit: 1986 to 1989
    Cars:10XX<20XX,30XX,11XX,21XX,31XX

    Second generation: Siemens. My favourite cars. Once used for testing for cars with no seat and have three grab poles. Bulit: 1994 to 1995 Cars: 12XX,22XX,32XX, sightly blue windows.

    Thrid generation: Kawasaki Nippon Sharyo(black cars), black in color with unique sound and with baggage racks. Cars: 13XX,23XX,33XX. Bulit 1999 to 2001.

    SMRT Trains do not have a certain depot so you will see some trian on the East West Line on the North South Line a day later.

    SBS Transh*t: Alstom(also the manufacturer of the TGV high speed trains in Frace, Frace company) black in color, ugly looking. IMPORTANT NOTICE: SCARPPPPPPPPP THEMMMMMM. they have 5 digit numbers: 7XXXX
