    CTE tunnels have a height limit of 4.5m which is the same as all arterial roads in Singapore, therefore there are no issues with ADDs using the tunnels. In fact, former services 501 & 502 ply the CTE tunnels using double deckers, and so does the current Premium Service 534 from Yio Chu Kang. Also, several buses use the CTE for their off-service routes, such as Service 147, and those are double-deckers as well.

    I shall list some services by their route sectors

    East Coast Parkway

    - Service 36 & 48: between Rochor exit & Tg Katong exit. Service 36 also plies the stretch between Bedok South Ave 1 & Changi Airport
    - Service 30: between Keppel exit & Fort Rd exit (also covers Benjamin Sheares Bridge, making this one of the most scenic bus service in Singapore)
    - Service 518 (towards Pasir Ris only): between Rochor entrance & Bedok South Ave 1; Service 518A proceeds on to PIE and TPE.

    Pan-Island Expressway - Eastern sector
    In order of introduction,
    - Service 23: The first service to utilise this sector, plies between Bedok North Rd & Bendemeer exit
    - Service 506: between Jln Eunos entrance & Kim Keat exit
    - Service 518: between Bedok North Rd & CTE
    - Service CT18: between Jln Eunos entrance & CTE
    - Service 966: between Jln Eunos entrance & Whitley exit
    - Service 59: between Jln Eunos entrance & Kim Keat exit
    - Service 5: between Jln Eunos entrance & Thomson exit
    - Service 45: between Jln Eunos & Bedok North Rd
