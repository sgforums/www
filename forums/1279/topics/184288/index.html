    30 Mar 06, 19:12
    FOR BUS ONLY:
    From Tampines Int/MRT Stn:
    Take 8 or 65, alight at MacPherson Rd, walk to bus stop outside POSBank, transfer Sv 125 to Genting Lane. (13.25km, 53mins)

    From Simei MRT Stn:
    Take 38, alight at Tampines Int, take 8 or 65, alight at MacPherson Rd, walk to bus stop outside POSBank, transfer Sv 125 to Genting Lane. (17.5km, 73mins)

    FOR MRT & BUS:

    Simei MRT / Tampines MRT> City Hall MRT (transfer to NSL) > Dhoby Ghaut (transfer to NEL) > Boon Keng > Sv 125. (From Simei MRT: 42 mins, From Tampines MRT: 45 mins)

    To Dead_Man_Inc:
    I suggest that you take the MRT & Sv 125. It is the fastest way.
