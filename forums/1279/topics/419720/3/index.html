    Bus services affected due to Chingay at Bishan - Toa Payoh

    Five bus stops in Toa Payoh and Bishan will be closed temporarily between 6.30 pm to 10 pm to facilitate Chingay floats this Sunday.

    SBS Transit says, the bus stops are along Bishan Street 14, Toa Payoh Central, Toa Payoh Lorong 4, 6 and 2.

    The affected bus services are - 53, 56, 105, 153, 232 and 410G. 

    Bus delays can also be expected along Geylang Road, Sims Ave, Sims Way, Ang Mo Kio Ave 1, Bishan, Sin Ming Ave, Upper Thomson Road, Braddell Road and Toa Payoh between 5.30pm and 11.00pm. 

    This is due to the convoy of Chingay floats travelling along these roads.

    -- 938Live
