    Demonstrator (demo) buses are one of a kind. They were either bought by SBS or sent by the manufacturer for evaluation purposes. Some have gone, some still survive while others were in SIngapore for a brief period. Here a some that I have recalled and the services which they used to run on.

    Volvo B10BLE (1688K) - 16, 36, 74, 85, 518

    Scania L94UB (2888T) - 7, 85, 518

    Volvo Super Olympian (9888Y) - 88, 502

    Volvo B10M-70 (997A) - 62, 107, 174

    Volvo B10MA (998Y) - 18, 290

    MB O405G (999U) - 18, 195, 290

    Leyland Lynx (3572Y) - 24, 106, 267, 269, now an Employee Bus

    MAN 16.240 (81S) - 76, now a training bus

    Volvo B10M w Van Hool body (9C) - 9, 111, 118, became a training bus before withdrawal

    MB OF1417 (1617R) - 78, 241, 333

    Leyland National 2 (6820L) - 143

    Nissan U21S (2452S) - 14, 328
