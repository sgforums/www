    On which service was SBS6790 used on? All I know was that it spent its last years as a training bus. Its bodywork was from Metal Sections (Metsec) not Soon Chow. This confusion is caused by the fluting on the body which was a characteristic of the early Soon Chow bodywork. The same Metsec body, minus fluting, was used on 150 Mercedes OF1413s introduced in 1978-79.
