    Hi Vera,

    Intermodal travel would necessitate transfer, so action should be taken to reduce the "transfer penalty".

    To name a few:

    Singapore has constructed transfer "hubs", where passengers can make train-bus transfers in air-condition comfort.

    In the area of fare integration, Singapore has moved to a "distance-based fare" system, where passengers pay by total distance travelled, regardless of the number of transfers made.

    See www.publictransport.sg

    Some major bus stops show bus arrival times on a display panel. Bus arrival times are also available on the phone/internet. This reduces uncertainty.

    Websites you might find useful:

    Regulator: www.ptc.gov.sg and www.lta.gov.sg

    Transport operators: www.sbstransit.com.sg and www.smrt.com.sg
