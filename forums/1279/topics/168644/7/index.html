    Thought the Enhanced Express 502 svc guide got say something like: "Different routing along non-stop sector may be used".
    Correct. It should have remained that way. Therefore BCs will decide the routings.

    Give 3 examples:
    1st example:
    BC finds out PIE is jammed at Toh Guan Road. What would he do? Can use Clementi Road/Holland Road@

    2nd example:
    Now BC thought using Clementi Road is safe, but a major accident occurred there. So he uses 106's or 105's route EXPRESS.

    3rd example:
    Let's say he still took Clementi Road, but found out Holland Road was jammed up due to a breakdown of an 18-wheeler. Therefore he can go on like 52 and take PIE again.

    Flexibility of routings for the fastest times.
