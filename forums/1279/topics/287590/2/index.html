    From Boon Lay to Jln Jelita: 157 or 174 to Jln Jurong Kechil, transfer to 61. 174 will be faster than 157 since it goes "express" at Jurong Rd.

    From Jln Jelita to King Albert Pk, u can try walking to Sixth Ave and take 77 (towards Bukit Batok) or 970 (towards Bukit Panjang).

    From Boon Lay to King Albert Pk, 154, 157, 174. For 154, u can stop at Clementi Rd opposite Ngee Ann Poly. For 157, 174, u can stop at the bus stop after passing by the KTM railway bridge.

    One can access China Square Central through a few methods:

    1. There're 61, 80, 145, 166, 197 along South Bridge Rd, which is at the bus stop opposite Hong Lim Complex.

    2. There're 186 and 970 along Church St, the road that leads Shenton Way.

    3. One can take NEL to Chinatown Station and walk about 7 mins by cutting across Hong Lim Complex.
