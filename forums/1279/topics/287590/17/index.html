    NS17 - Bishan.

    You need to take the Southbound service from Woodlands, meaning you take the direction that heads towards Marina Bay. Alight after 7 stations if you board from Woodlands, 6 if you board from Admiralty. Exit the MRT station at Bishan and walk through Junction 8 shopping centre - directly beside Bishan MRT.

    Once you cross J8 you'll reach Bishan INT. Take Service 410G - G denotes green plate which can be identified from the plates the driver uses when he drives 410 and take all the way to your destination.

    Total travelling time excluding waiting time would be 21 minutes (on the train) and another 13~17 minutes on 410G, depending on traffic situation.
