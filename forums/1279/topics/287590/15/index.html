  • service_238

    Originally posted by Master -_-:

    How to go from Marine Parade to Mediacorp?


    1st Solution

    Take 966 to Catholic JC at Whitley Rd
    Cross the bridge transfer to 132 or 156.
    Alight where the florists are. 5 stops from CJC

    2nd Solution

    Take 15/55/966 to Jln Eunos (Eunos MRT)
    Transfer to 93
    Alight at Lornie Rd 1st stop (after u see the Shell kiosk)

    3rd Solution

    Take 15/55 to Jln Eunos (Opp Eunos Mansion / After PIE)
    Cross the bridge transfer to 59
    Alight at Bishan Rd (Opp Guangyang Sec)
    Transfer to 157/93
    Alight at Lornie Rd 1st stop (after u see the Shell kiosk)

    All off peak journey times are around the same, depending on which bus comes first and whether you are able to get your connecting bus within 5min.
