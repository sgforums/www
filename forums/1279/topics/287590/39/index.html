    From Kaki Bukit Camp to SSDC:

    Walk out to Kaki Bukit Ave 1, cross the road (Kaki Bukit Ave 1) and take either service 58 or 87 to Eunos Link opposite Eunos Technolink Building, at the same bus stop, transfer to service 45 and alight at Ang Mo Kio Ave 9 outside the Police Division HQ, walk straight ahead and you will see a T-junction, turn left to Ang Mo Kio St 62, SSDC is located there.
