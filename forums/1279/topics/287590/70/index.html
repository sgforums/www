  • tranquilice
    28 Jun 09, 18:28
    Originally posted by linwei20:

    so after once bus31 turn left after the National Stadium, alight and cross the bridge? What about from ECP back to Toa Payoh? Same route?


    Take service 31 and alight at this bus stop:

    Old Airport Rd Blk 39 (fare stage 10), it's the 1st stop along Old Airport Rd after the bus turns left from Mountbatten Rd. It's the 14th bus stop from Toa Payoh interchange.

    Cross the road to get to the bus stop opposite. This bus stop is: Old Airport Rd Blk 22.

    Board service 401 at this stop, alight at the 8th bus stop: East Coast Park Service Rd opposite carpark C3 (fare stage 20), this is the bus stop nearest to McDonalds.

    When going back, take service 401 at the opposite side at this stop called 'Regent Bowl', alight at the next stop, this is the 1st stop after the bus crosses the flyover. Board service 31 here back to Toa Payoh.

    Alight along Marine Parade Rd outside Blk 83, this is the stop before the bus proceeds to the roundabout and loop.

    Walk thru Marine Parade Promenade which is the open space between the blocks of shops, walk to the end and cross the road Marine Parade Central, you will see Blk 79 infront. There is an underpass beside Blk 79 which leads to the park.
