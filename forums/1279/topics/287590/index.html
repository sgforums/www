    Originally posted by mhcampboy:
    How to get to Holland Village....

    Bugis??

    From woodlands, take 963 to Petir Rd, cross the road and take 75 to Holland Village.
    Or take 187 to Bukit Panjang after passing Bukit Panjang Plaza, and transfer to 970.
    Or take 960 to Petir Rd and take 75.

    From Bugis, take 7 or 61 at North Bridge Rd. For 7, u can alight at Holland Rd after passing by Holland Dr. For 61, u can either alight at the bus stop at Holland Ave or alight at the bus stop where 7 stops too. 7 will be faster than 61 since 61 is going to make one big round at Harbourfront.

    If u r at Victoria St, u can take 33 to Outram Pk and transfer to 970.
