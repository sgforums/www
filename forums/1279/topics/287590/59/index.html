    9 Apr 09, 22:37
    Originally posted by jayh272416:

    need help here

    57 New Upp Changi Rd to Neram Cres. Is the best way to take 14/9/35 to the int den switch to 854?

    31 to Tampines Interchange change to A or B.

     

    A is 72. Change to 103/103M at Yio Chu Kang Road. Take 103 till the last stop of Yio Chu Kang Road. Walk in to Neram Crescent.

     

    B is 27. Take till Compassvale Road. Walk into Sengkang Interchange and take 163. Alight at first stop on Yio Chu Kang Road. Walk in to Neram Crescent.

     

    Alternatively you can take 31 to Tampines Ave 5, and change to 168. Alight from 168 at Jln Kayu. Find the correct bus stop for 103/103M(going back to Serangoon Interchange). Alight at first stop along Yio Chu Kang Road and walk in to Neram Crescent.

     

    You can also take any bus from Bedok Int to the first bus stop of Changi Road and change to 854. Change to 103/103M at Yio Chu Kang Road too...
