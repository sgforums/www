    If you mean the one opp Xpo station then it goes like this.

     

    1. From Jln Toa Payoh(Trellis) can take 5 till Simei Ave(CGH) there and then change to 31... Alight at b/stop aft Xilin Road then walk in.

    2. From Jln Toa Payoh(Trellis) take 966 to Marine Parade Road change 31. Alight at b/stop opp Xilin Road then walk in.

    3. From Lor 6 Toa Payoh can take 31. Follow same as (2.) at opp Xilin Road.

    4. From Lor 6 Toa Payoh take 59 till Jln Eunos, cross road, take 45 till New Upp Changi Road. There, change to 31. Same procedure as (2.) at opp Xilin Road.

     

    Sv. 35 doesn't stop at the last stop(or was it last two?) at Upper Changi Road East before turning into Xilin Road. And if it is the Bedok Water Reclaimation Plant then there are shuttles to Bedok/Tanah Merah MRT Stn. Also there are tourist shuttles from Changi Airport T3 if you are lucky to have good timing...
