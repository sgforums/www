    Take 147 (towards Clementi) along Serangoon Central opposite the Interchange. Alight at Bt Merah Town Centre bus stop along Jalan Bt Merah. Journey time is about 50 mins.

    Or you can take bus 153 from any bus stops along Upp Serangoon Rd, which will bring you to Bt Merah Interchange direct. Journey time is about the same but likely to be less crowded.

    the stop after KFC. good u knew that KFC bus stop. i lived in the area.

    the bus stop opp st. theresa's home, is actually the one after bishan fire station/shell/marymount lane/shunfu estate. it is situated on the slip road before the on-ramp to the flyover above macritchie. it is important that u alight at that stop only as 163 turns into toa payoh thereafter. this is the only stop where both 52 and 74 stops together, allowing you take whatever comes along (74 comes fast, but journey is a bit longer while 52 takes a while to come but definitely preferred since it uses the PIE).

    iris: u need a 3G handset capable of surfing the web. head to www.sbstransit.com.sg/mobileiris. check ur telco for charges. or u can simply SMS if u're with singtel, 5 cents per sms. u need to know the bus stop code (5-digit number on the bus stop pole), and then enter the service and it'll tell u how long u've got to wait.
