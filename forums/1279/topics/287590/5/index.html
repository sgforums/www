  • ^tamago^
    18 Nov 07, 13:40
    Originally posted by ufocookie:
    Hello. I need help to decipher bus 70 routes. It seem as if there is 70,70# and 70A. Can anyone tell me the difference as i will be travelling on 70 to my job place. thanks.
    70 goes from Yio Chu Kang Terminal to Shenton Way Terminal.

    70# goes into Suntec/Marina and makes a loop, skipping the Shenton Way part. This is only during weekday and Saturdays (from 6pm onwards), and Sundays (whole day).

    To recognise between 70 and 70#, you have to check out its destination from the side since the front will only put 70.

    70A mainly operates during the night from Yio Chu Kang Terminal and terminates at Temasek Ave (after Suntec Tower 3), and is the one-directional variant of the 70# route. This is because late buses from Yio Chu Kang do not run back to Yio Chu Kang beyond midnight from Suntec but goes back to the depot straight.
