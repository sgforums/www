    Clementi Camp is located at Clementi Loop, off Clementi Ave 6.

    1. Take LRT/MRT to Bt Batok, then take 189 from the interchange. Alight at the first stop after the short express sector on the PIE. Cross the road and walk in.

    2. Take 184 to Clementi MRT, cross the overhead bridge and take 185/189 for 3 stops. Alight, walk further up to the road and walk in.
