    For your 1st option, you should take 42 from the stop OPP Kembangan station. Otherwise it would take one big round thru Lengkong Empat. But then again Senang Crescent is one road that stretches all the way from Sims Ave East to end near where the PIE is... So depending. If the place is near the end where the PIE is, then take 42 from Kembangan Stn.

     

    For your 2nd option, if you take 222, you should take it from the Central Stop which is near the Panasonic factory.

     

    To be honest, if your side of Senang Crescent is near the junction of Senang Crescent and Sims Ave East, you should take take any of the buses at Sims Ave East(Kembangan Stn) for one stop and walk in.

    Option 1.
