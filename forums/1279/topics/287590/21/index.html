    I would advise you to go to Vivo first. from Toa Payoh by my favourite bus service, 143. You can have a rather panoramic view of the City on a Double Decker 143. then head to Queensway Shopping Centre by 61, 93 or 100. After that board 33 from Queensway Shopping Centre to Redhill MRT and change MRT to Raffles Place to see the Merlion.

    Or if you are going from Toa Payoh to Queensway, you can take 153 directly to Queensway Shopping Centre. IKEA located a few minutes away from Queensway Shopping Centre is another recommended place to visit. It is a home furnishing chain with two outlets in Singapore (other at Tampines). do visit IKEA, the restaurant food there is really awesome. I miss meatballs haha. Also, vivo is located right beside HarbourFront MRT so all you need to do is walk. You can walk to the Merlion from Raffles Place MRT. Then you can walk to Funan via Fullerton Hotel and the few historical landmarks like the Old Supreme Court and Asian Civilisations Museum, quite a heritage route. cut thru via High St to Funan. From Funan, you can walk to the Singapore Flyer from Funan via Citylink Mall and Esplanade or you can take 195 from Supreme Court at High St and walk towards Marina Centre Terminal, there you will see The Singapore Flyer in the background.

    The best time to go for the Singapore Flyer would be in the evening.
