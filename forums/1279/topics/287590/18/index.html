    solution 1:

    take 151 or 153 from hougang int & alight at NUS & then tranfer to 67 or 170.

    or

    take train from hougang or sk to little india station & tranfer to 67 or 170.

    67 & 170 by past too many places where the morning crowds are from, e.g NUS, hwa chong jc, chinese high sch. this crowd of people usually take the 7am buses.

    so task:

    1) percentage of boarding the bus is lower

    2) alight & boarding time is slower

    3) bus is full & drove away is higher

    all this 3 point add together mean more time wasted.

     

    solution 2:

    Take bus 85 from sk to yishun int, then tranfer to 171 to mindef

    1) less crowd

    2) bus from int usually come on time

    3) expressway from sk to yishun in the morning usually no jam

    4) fewer bustop as it via the expressway

    5) there maybe a jam at lentor before reaching katib & the jam may sometimes up to 15mins or more depends task there is a risk

     

    solution 3:

    take 161 from sk or hougang int to wld int, then tranfer to 963 to mindef

    Reason:

    1) less crowd

    2) bus from int usually come on time

    3) expressway from sk to wld in the morning usually no jam

    4) fewer bustop as it via the expressway

    so the best way is solution 3 : take 161 from sk to wld int, then tranfer to 963 to mindef
