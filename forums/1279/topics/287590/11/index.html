    1. Take 197 from Kallang Rd opp ICA Building. Alight at the last stop along Marine Parade Rd (Hua Xin Ct). Walk.

    2. Take 12/32 from Kallang Rd opp ICA Building. Alight at East Coast Rd & transfer to 10/14. Alight at stop after Bayshore Rd (The Summit). Walk.

    If you're going on a weekend, you can take 32/33 from Kallang Rd opp ICA Building, alight at Old Airport Rd & transfer to 401. Alight at stop along East Coast Park Svc Rd opp lagoon. Take note of the operating hours of 401.
