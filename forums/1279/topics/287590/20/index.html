    Vivocity... take 160 to Jurong East then take 97. Dont consider 143 as it loops a lot around teban gardens. However if youre feeling damn bored then take 143 if u want. Also possible: 170 to Bt Timah walk a bit and take 93.

    Im assuming you mean Queensway Shopping Center... for that take 170 to Bukit Timah, change to 174 then 93. Alternatively if you have a low budget, you can take 170 then walk to change 93... saves you a few cents. If you have too much time on your hands, take 160 to J. East then take 197.

    Between Vivo City and Queensway Shopping Center there is a direct bus. 93.

    P.S. 93 has some non-air conditioned bus so be prepared. Fares cost less though =D
