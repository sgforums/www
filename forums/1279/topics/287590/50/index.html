    Originally posted by lolxzxz:

    13 will be the bus for you towards the direction of Upper East Coast (opposite Blk 103. )

    I am unsure which sailing clubs you are referring to.

    1. National Sailing Centre/Singapore Sailing Federation/MOE Sea Sports Ctr.

    2. Water Venture (East Coast)

    Both of them are situated around 600 metres apart.

    So, you can take 13 all the way to Upper East Coast Terminal near Guards Camp. Walk along Bedok Park Connector to reach ECP.

    Upon reaching ECP, to go to 1, turn left and walk about 200 metres. For 2, turn right and continue walking for about 400 metres.

     

    Can take 853 on Sundays and public holidays to Upper East Coast too.
