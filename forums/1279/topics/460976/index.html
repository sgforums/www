    I would like to share with you a experience i had this evening with a SMRT PRC driver. I really hope this message gets through to the necessary mediums as it really infuriated me.

After crossing the traffic, i would have to cross another short zebra crossing to get to the MRT station. Upon reaching the zebra crossing, remember its a ZEBRA CROSSING. So as the traffic was clear, some of the people in front began to cross and i follow suit, with a old ah pek in front of me. Suddenly there's a SMRT bus 969 coming towards us to left turn. Even though its a ZEBRA crossing, the bus did not stop at all. Instead he honked and drove as if he wanted to bang us down. I had to pull the ah pek in front of me out of the way and he nearly knock me down if i didn't siam in the nick of time. Then when i looked at the bus driver, it was a PRC driver with the stupid face. I was so angry at that point of time that i gave him the finger and slammed the bus as it passed by me but he did not give a hoot about me. I was like WTF, this stupid tiong or rather 'FT' nearly killed us and this is a zebra crossing mind you. This is not china !

I am not trying to find fault with anyone, but this really has to stop. I am sick and tired of putting our lives to the hands of these people, may it be the service sector or what not. Please give singaporeans a chance and not foreign trash.
