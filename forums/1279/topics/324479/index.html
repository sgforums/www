    The services serving Tuas, as you guys know, easily holds the highest demand in the early morning peak hour. These includes 251, 252, 254, 255 so on.

    For most services, the bus is usually filled to the brim but usually everyone gets on board at Boon Lay. Except one service. 251. This one loops at Benoi Rd.

    Apart from the fact that this is a complete NAC fleet with not even a fleeting sight of ADD cameos, which I don't mind, what pisses me off is that, even though 251 matches and actually surpasses the other Tuas services in terms of demand, this service also has the worst frequency, morning peak hour frequency being at 20 minutes.

    Thus 251 is the only Tuas service that consistently fails to be able to get everyone on board every morning. Seeing this, it really puts into perspective the silent sufferings of the many workers who rely on this bus to reach the ship yards, and the constant rambling of other commuters who tend to exaggerate their accounts.
