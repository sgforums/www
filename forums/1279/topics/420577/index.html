    OK finally to start w/ 3 SBST svs.

    30th Jan phase

    Sv 76: Amended to become Yio Chu Kang - Marine Terrace (Loop) service. To skip Marine Parade Rd (Marine Terrace - Victoria School) stretch.

    Deleted sector to be covered by Sv 135.

    Sv 315: Amended to cover Serangoon North Industrial Estate (S'goon Nth Ave 5). To skip 2 existing bus stop along Ang Mo Kio Ave 3 (Blk 516) & Yio Chu Kang Rd (Opp Gracehaven Salvation Army).

    It'll call the opposite direction bus stop along S'goon Nth Ave 4 as well (Blk 537 replacing Blk 530) & (Opp Blk 506A to replace Blk 504A MSCP).

    New service rotation: S'goon Nth Ave 1 > S'goon Nth Ave 4 > S'goon Nth Ave 5 > Ang Mo Kio Ave 3 (Blk 554) > S'goon Nth Ave 1

    Sv 410G/ W: Amended to serve Marymount Rd (Marymount Station), Shunfu Rd, Sin Ming Rd (Sin Ming Plaza for 410G).

    It'll skip the Upp Thomson Rd (Lakeview Estate) bus stop pair.
