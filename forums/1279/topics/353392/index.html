    Let me suggest...

    Take MRT from Sembawang

    Alight at Toa Payoh MRT, Take 31 from Toa Payoh Interchange.
    (This should be the fastest).
    For 31, alight at Opposite Parkway Parade Bus Stop and cross over the overhead bridge.

    OR

    Alight at Marsiling MRT, Take 966 from the opposite side of the MRT station.
    For 966, alight at the OCBC Bank Marine Parade, which is the Parkway Parade side.
