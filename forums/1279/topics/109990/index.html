    NSF ez-link card

    Refund of the NSF ez-link Card is not allowed while the concession eligibility is in effect.

    After your concession eligibility has expired (eligibility period is determined by the respective camps), the card can be used for a further 16 months (you can check expiry date at any AVM or GTM). You may request for an immediate refund at any Ticket Office during the 16 months grace period. Adult fares will be deducted during this grace period and concession passes cannot be purchased.

    After the 16 months period, the card will expire and cannot be used or refunded immediately. Instead, a receipt will be issued and the refund can be collected 5 working days later at any Ticket Office. All refunded cards will be retained by TransitLink.

    In addition,

    School ez-link Smartcard

    Refund of the School Smartcard / ITE Student ez-link Card is not allowed while the concession eligibility is in effect. After your concession eligibility has expired (Eligibility period is determined by the respective schools. You can check expiry date at any AVM or GTM), the card can be used for a further 6 months. You may request for an immediate refund at any Ticket Office during the 6 months grace period. Adult fares will be deducted during the 6 months and concession passes cannot be purchased.
