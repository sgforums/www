    In general, SBST timetables are quite standardized. Their mealbreaks range from 22 - 26 min long on a weekday and 28-30 min long on a sunday.

    Their meal break times is between 0900 - 1100 and between 1630 - 1900. Meal times differ from service to service. For example, 238 evening meal breaks are between 1640 - 1840, while one 26 meal break is at 1830.

    Drivers in swing shift do not have 2 meal breaks. They either have one meal break or even a tea break. A tea break is about 13-16 min long. It is usually just after the meal breaks at around 10+ or 4+. Timings differ for every timesheet.

    Correct me if i'm wrong because most of my views here is based on my 238 findings
