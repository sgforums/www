    Originally posted by Fryderyk HPH:

    I apologise. That was an honest mistake. I just turned on my camera to shoot it and pop went the flash. It was a new camera and I forgot to switch the defaults.

    Don't rely on auto. Try this, your next photos should improve a lot just by overriding the settings :

    - set ISO to Highest/Maximum
    - disable flash
    - enable multiple bursts
    - compensate for extra brightness or darkness using EV adjust. If your photos are consistently too bright or too dark, then you know you need to adjust. Don't forget to set it back to neutral when you move on from the location.

    These are band-aid fixes looking at how you are trying to shoot. They are useless against Coolair and SICMAs, but I presume you don't really want to cover them just yet.
