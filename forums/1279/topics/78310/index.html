    Good questions... Well it depends on the info you get and what are you going after? Let's say for today, I spotted SBS 0035 B on 142. This bus is considered rare as Scanias hardly operate on 142 except for SBS 3697 X earlier this year.

    I spotted it going to the 142 berth at around 1715 hrs. I quickly took out my digi cam so that i can take a shot of it before it leaves the interchange. However, i wasnt satisfied with the pics that i took. Hence I waited 25 min for it to return. When it returned at 1740 hrs, I took pics of it again.

    So here are e answers to your questions...

    1. Anywhere as long as you deem it to be suitable.
    2. Water and Camera. Camping for a bus can be vv taxing at times.
    3. Depends on your luck at getting the bus. My 33 Dart chasing took me 2 hours.
    4. Best place should be interchanges as you can see all kinds of regos and buses at your doorstep.
