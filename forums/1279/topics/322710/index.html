    Originally posted by 105090:

    can someone shed some light on thw optimum settings to shoot moving objects? like a high ISO or shutter speed or? many times the images appears grainy or noisy (like sv550).I'm using a canon btw.

     


    High Shutter Speed - optium setting to shoot moving objects like buses. But of course at the same time, there should be some amount of strong sunlight. Experiment, but try not to go over 1/1000 or 1/1600, depending on how strong the sunlight is.

    High ISO - more grain/noise in your photos. Highly not recommended during the day, but you can still try to set a small measure of the ISO (ISO 200 and below).

    Right, in the case of settings, I normally like to use Av set at f/5.6. Reason being the rear of the bus will not be blur in my photo.
