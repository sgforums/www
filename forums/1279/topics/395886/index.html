    For the first time in 7 years, DDs will be retiring from service. This batch of 200 are the last to leave the Leyland plant in 1993 with SBS 9168 S as the last chassis being built and assembled. It also boosts the one of a kind Alexander Royale bodywork, custom made for SBS.

    Maybe Stage may wanna stalk 9168S after 582M goes off. haha.

    Retirement Dates (17 years)
    26 Aug 2010: 9000-9009
    28 Sep 2010: 9010-9021
    19 Oct 2010: 9022-9031
    17 Nov 2010: 9032-9053
    21 Dec 2010: 9054-9090
    11 Jan 2011: 9091-9110
    25 Jan 2011: 9111-9130
    08 Feb 2011: 9131-9143
    22 Feb 2011: 9144-9157
    09 Mar 2011: 9158-9167
    22 Mar 2011: 9168-9187
    26 Apr 2011: 9188-9199

    There are 195 LOs left.

    As of today:
    48 in amdep
    31 in brbp
    116 in slbp
    22 spare buses amongst them for now. (Breakdown is 9/3/10)
