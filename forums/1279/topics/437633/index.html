    13 JANUARY 2011's reply : 13 Jan 2011 Dear Mr , Request for double decker bus to be deployed for service 113 We refer to your correspondence to us on the above, which we received via the Aljunied Town Council on 3 January, 2011. As conveyed by my colleague Lorelie in her reply to you, Service 113 is scheduled at an average headway of 12 minutes during the morning peak period, with 7 single decks in its fleet. Based on our checks conducted recently, the average capacity utilisation of this service is 31% between 0730hrs and 0800hrs, and 68% between 1745hrs and 1815 hrs. Hence, we regret we are unable to support your request. As a responsible public transport operator, we seek to keep our fares affordable, and hence, we seek your gracious understanding that we have to ensure that capacity is in tandem with demand. That said, we wish to assure you we are monitoring this service closely, and that we will seek the co-operation of our on-board passengers to move to the rear of he bus to make more space for our other customers like yourself to board. We thank you for writing in to us to highlight this issue. Yours sincerely
