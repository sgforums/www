    Sometimes a company will request that the bus be targeted at a certain area, especially if it makes reference to the location of the business, or whatever. Here in Sydney, some buses have been swapped, where a bus from one depot in the east of Sydney is placed in all over adverts, and is requested to be put on bus routes which are in the north of Sydney. So the bus is transferred to a northern depot, and a bus from the northern depot is sent to the eastern depot in replacement.
