    Yes, it may be possible. At WLDEP, we found that the driver had to key in 4 numbers, which are normally the same. Each service have a code, and hence, most trunk service have 2 codes, one for each direction.
    The first number display the route number
    The second would display the route details
    The third and the forth are for formatting the display, but are normally automatic, but for some unknown reasons, these 2 last numbers must be keyed in. The extra bold display on service 980 Lance 211s is due to these 2 numbers
