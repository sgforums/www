    KMB Introduces Route 88X

     

    As a part of Sha Tin Route Reorganisation, KMB will introduce a new route 88X [Sha Tin Station → Ping Tin (Circular)] on Monday 4 August 2014. The service hours will be between 7:00 a.m. and 10:00 p.m. daily with a frequency of 30 minutes. The departures between 7:00 a.m. and 12:30 p.m. will go via Tate’s Cairn Tunnel, Kowloon Bay, Kwun Tong Town Centre, Ping Tin Bus Terminus, Lam Tin (North) Bus Terminus and then return to Sha Tin via Kwun Tong Bypass and Tate’s Cairn Tunnel. The departures at 1:00 p.m. and thereafter will be switched to observe Kwun Tong Town Centre when going from Ping Tin to Sha Tin Station.
     
    The full fare of Route 88X will be $10.20.
     
    Passengers may call the KMB Customer Service Hotline on 2745-4466, or browse the KMB website at www.kmb.hk  for more information.
