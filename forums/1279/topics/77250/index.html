    Central-
    Ang Mo Kio: Merz Merz and more Merz.. all 3 kinds.. esp CAC ones. Among the Merz are VSO and LO as well..

    Bishan: Merz infested place.. with occasional VSO and Scanias.

    Toa Payoh: Place with most varied models under Central District... Merz & Scanias mostly... with some VSOs calling by once in a while and the LOs from 143 & 157. Mk3s from 28 31, Mk4 DM from 8 and VO 3x from 31 88.

    East-
    Bedok: Scania, VO3x and more VO3x... esp the 93xx and 95xx buses.. Not forgetting the invasion of Mk3s.. and also sights of LOs on 25 30, Striders on 33 66, Mk4 DM on 66 & Mk2s on 32 60.

    Tampines: Scania and Mk3 infested area! with VO3x up next of 31 81. Mk4 DM3500 on 10 72 81. With occasional apperances of LOs & Merz.

    Pasir Ris: Scania infested area. with Mk3s up next.. VO3x & Mk4 DM3500 as next common after these 2.

    North East-
    Punggol: Mixture of Mk4 DM, Scanias, Merz and VO3x.

    Sengkang: Mk4 infested area followed by Vo3x of 80 86 87 119. Occasional Merz on 159 and Mk3 on 163.

    Hougang: Tridents galore! With Striders and Mk4 DMs as quite common... However, this place is a Mk2 infested area!

    Eunos: Mk2 infested area with Vo3x of 154 and VO2x of 93. Occasional Mk4 DM from 63 and Mk5 Striders on 93.

    West-
    Bt Merah: Mk2 and Mk3 infested area... with VO3x as next common and occasional Merz on 131 139.

    Buona Vista area: Another Mk2 and Mk3 infested area.

    Clementi: Mixture of Mk3, Striders, Vo3x and a few Mk5s from 165.

    Jurong East: Merz LO infested area with occasional Striders on 506 and Mk4 DM3500 on 105.

    Boon Lay: Merz and LO infested area.. Vo2x infested also.. with occasional Mk4 Dm3500 seen on 154 and VO3x on 30 154 198.
