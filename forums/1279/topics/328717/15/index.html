    S858 does not need to have that extension. It's route is already long enough. People going into Tampines from Woodlands have the best alternative that they have - S168. 35 minutes from WRI to Tampines town.

    People going into Tampines from Yishun, just take S969. 40 minutes reach Tampines. Then change. Alternatively, take S39 or S858 to Jalan Kayu, then change S168. 40 minutes reach Tampines Town also. There's a reason why certain services ply the Jalan Kayu bus stop and some don't.

    S969 is a very high demand service. The loading at Tampines during evening peak hours is just madness. This is one of the best-planned routes ever SMRT has created, I say. Gives people working in the east a very direct link to the North. i.e. People living at Yishun and up get to benefit from journey times less than an hour. 1 trip up is 1 hour, 1 trip down is also 1 hour. And it skips all stops at Tampines because there are other services like S168 to cover it. People just need to change at Jalan Kayu to either S39 or S858 to Yishun.

    And even after relieving about 50% of its load at Yishun MRT, S969 continues to be packed. Alot of people take S969 from Yishun MRT because it's one of the shortest link currently into Woodlands Town.

    How can I not say this is a good route planned?
