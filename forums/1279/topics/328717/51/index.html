    After riding NR2 last night, I was inspired by it's routing to create a daytime route that could be similar to it's sector within Sembawang and Woodlands...

    Route 889 - Sembawang > Woodlands Checkpoint (loop)

    Option 1:

    Sembawang Bus Interchange > Sembawang Vista > Sembawang Drive > Admiralty Drive > Canberra Road > Sembawang Avenue > (follows NR2's route until just before Woodlands MRT Station/Bus Interchange) > Woodlands Bus Interchange > Woodlands Avenue 5 > Marsiling Rise, Road, and Drive > Admiralty Road, and Woodlands Centre Road (loop).

    Option 2:

    Sembawang Bus Interchange > Sembawang Vista, Drive, and Avenue > Canberra Road > Admiralty Drive > Sembawang Way > Woodlands Avenue 7 > (follows NR2's route until just before Woodlands MRT Station/Bus Interchange, then continues as per option 1)

    Benefits:

    • Creates new links between Marsiling, Woodlands Checkpoint, and Sembawang
    • Creates new direct links within Woodlands
    • Can potentially be extended beyond Woodlands to elsewhere via Bukit Timah Expressway or Woodlands Road in the future from the Woodlands Checkpoint side

    Drawbacks:

    • Both options are rather circuitious routes, especially within Sembawang
    • Duplicates different sectors of routes 962, 178, and 913 to a certain extent
    • Could excarberate crowding situation at Woodlands Interchange (one way to avoid this is to route it through Woodlands Avenue 5 completely and skip Avenues 4 and 7)
