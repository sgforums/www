    SMRT 948 (Peak Hour Service)

    *This service will duplicate North-South Line (Between Jurong East - Yew Tee) to ease the crowd at Jurong East Station during peak hour. So people from Jurong East who wanna go between Bukit Batok & Yew Tee can take this service.

    Ops on Weekdays Evening Peak Hour

    1730hours - 1945hours (6 bendy buses will be deploy)

    Frequency - 7~9 mins

    • Jurong East St 12 (Below Jurong East Interchange beside Popular)
    • Jurong East St 13
    • Jurong East Ctrl [Non-Stop]
    • Bukit Batok Ave 1 (turn Right)
    • Bukit Batok Central
    • Bukit Batok West Ave 2
    • Bukit Batok West Ave 5
    • Brickland Rd
    • Choa Chu Kang Ave 3/4
    • Choa Chu Kang Dr (Yew Tee Station - All Passengers Must Alight)

    After that, this service will go back to Jurong East St 12 by

    • Choa Chu Kang St 62
    • Choa Chu Kang North 6
    • Choa Chu Kang Way
    • KJE
    • PIE
    • Toh Guan Rd
    • Boon Lay Way
    • Jurong East St 12

    The last bus will x-over to Service 302 at Yew Tee Station for 1 Trip and off service to KJDP.
