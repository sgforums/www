    Some minor amendments of service 196 to ply NUS science fac rather than Dover.

    There are several reasons for choosing service 196.

    Reason 1: Service 95 is always jam packed during peak hrs probably due to its frequency. People have to take shuttle bus to Computer centre to take 95 or 96 there when going home. Service 196 can help to alleviate service 95 load by 1. providing a link from Buona Vista to NUH and NUS, 2. providing a link from Clementi to NUH, NUS.

    Reason 2: There are already 3 services going to Bedok from Dover. Service 14, 33, and finally 196. 2 services 14, 196 go to clementi. 14, 166 go to Dover MRT. 2 services 14 and 196 from there go to bukit merah central. Service 33 ply the Alexandra Rd area. In addition, the residential areas are situated near the main road North Buona Vista Rd. Residents who want to go to Shenton Way can walk out around 300 metres to the main road to take 196.

     

    In conclusion, service 196's route is the most suitable for amendments to NUS. Besides, dds can be deployed onto this new route since the bridge at the junction between Lower Kent Ridge Rd and Kent Ridge Cres is 4.5 metres which is as high as a normal overhead bridge.
