    Morning peak in opposite direction

    This service has almost the same purpose as the current SBST sv.128. To get to the NEL from Tanjong Pagar Stn, one will probably transfer at Outram Park Stn, which means a long walk from the EWL to NEL and vice versa. Another option would be to take EWL to Raffles Place, NSL to Dhoby Gauht and finally to NEL, two transfers on the train is also quite time consuming and troublesome. Therefore this service is targeted at Shenton Way workers living in NEL areas and I do foresee some form of demand for this service given a current report in zaobao that there are more people prefering buses over trains at certain circumstances most probably due to convinence of saving the trouble from walking up and down for transfers on trains. Upper Serangoon Rd is chosen as the express sector as that part is already served by sv.107.

     Fleet:

    SBS2694K (HGDEP 82 -> HGDEP 20/27)
    SBS2718B (HGDEP 156 -> HGDEP 20/27)
    SBS2763U (HGDEP 159 -> HGDEP 20/27)
    SBS2785G (HGDEP 159 -> HGDEP 20/27)

    These 4 buses will operate as Split shift buses which will operate on Service 27 during off peak and will crossover to Service 20 during peak hours. They will off service after completing the evening peak Service 20 runs.
