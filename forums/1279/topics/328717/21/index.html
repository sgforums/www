    Originally posted by tanjun:

     

    Proposed route suggestions of a new trunk service 126 from Ang Mo Kio to Boon Lay.

    The purpose of this service is to provide a trunk service to NTU campus.

    Based on the estimated demand of 179, the estimated demand of this service should be high too based on the factors listed below.

     

    1. This service links Ang Mo Kio to NTU. Students residing at Hougang Ave 8, Hougang Ave 4, Serangoon Nth Ave 1, AMK estate are most likely to utilise this service to travel to NTU.

    Take for example, a NTU student living at Hougang Ave 8 will take 74 and transfer to 126 at AMK Ave 3 instead of the conventional way (74 to AMK, AMK to Boon Lay then 179). From AMK to Boon Lay, it takes around 49 mins plus some additional time for transfer of 179.

    This service will take around 40 mins (off peak) to 80 mins (peak) to get to NTU from AMK. In addition, it saves the hassle of multiple transfers.

     

    2. This service also links Bukit Batok to NTU and provides an alternative from Boon Lay to NTU. NTU students residing along Bt Batok East Ave 3 can utilise this service to get to their school. In addition, students residing near Jurong West St 62, St 63 have an alternative to Boon Lay int (currently served by 243 and 257) and a direct link to NTU and AMK.

     

    3. It is the only service in NTU that provides links to beyond Jurong West estate and the distance of the route is relatively ok with 35 fare stages in total with 33% of it are considered "express sectors".

    Introducing the special School Express Services!

    (tanjun, your service is good, but you can add more express sectors and / or renumber it as 726.)
