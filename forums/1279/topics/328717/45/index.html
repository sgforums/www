    I thought this would be suitable to suggest.

    Current Service 168 route to Woodlands (Part)

    Bedok Reservior Road
    ...
    - Opp Bedok Reform Trg Ctr

    Tampines Ave 5
    - Blk 938
    Tampines Ave 4
    - UOB Tampines Ctr
    ...

    And vice-versa for the Bedok-bound route.

    But this route largely duplicates a number of services, most notable being Service 21, which also uses this same route. What I suggest is...

    Amended Service 168 route to Woodlands (Part)

    Bedok Reservior Road
    ...
    - Opp Bedok Reform Trg Ctr
    Tampines Ave 4
    - Opp East View Pri Sch
    - St. Hilda's Sec Sch
    - Opp Blk 945
    - UOB Tampines Ctr
    ...

    And vice-versa for the Bedok-bound route.

    Not that I'm being biased (should pay partly), but I have reasons why.

    1. Like I have earlier said, that part of the current Service 168 route largely duplicates Service 21, so maybe there should be an alternative in terms of the routing.
    2. Tampines Ave 4; Street 91 residents only have Service 22 (and 68 before it's withdrawal on 11 Aug 08) serving their house. They would have to go to Tampines or Bedok Reservior and transfer back to their intended route to head to a destination.
    (Say that the resident wants to go to Woodlands. He/She needs to go back out to Bedok Reservior, cross the road and transfer to Service 168. What a hassle!)
    3. We have students from all the three schools (served mainly by 22 and 293) who are mostly Bedok residents. There is no direct connection from that area to Bedok North Ave 3. The students there have to thus take 22 and then transfer to another Bedok-bound service. It's troublesome.

    What do you think?
