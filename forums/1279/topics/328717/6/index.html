    Feeder 241 - SLBP to Jurong West St 72 (Loop)
    SLBP, Soon Lee Rd (Non stop), Pioneer Rd North, JW St 63, St 65, St 62, St 61, St 71, Ave 5, St 75, St 73 (Non stop), St 72 (Loop), Ave 5, St 71, St 61, St 62, St 65, St 63, Pioneer Rd North, Soon Lee Rd (Non stop) and SLBP.
    * It will skip the pair of bus stops along Singapore Post Jurong Delivery Base in Pioneer Rd North.

    Purposes:
    - To provide a direct link for residents along JW St 63 to Pioneer Mall and Gek Poh Shopping Center.

    - To provide bus services on Jurong West St 65 which is currently unserved by any bus services.

    - To provide a link for residents near Gek Poh Shopping Ctr a direct link to the future Pioneer MRT station.

    Cons:
    - Does not serve Boon Lay Temp Int/Jurong Point thus demand should be smaller.
