    Enhanced route 882: Yishun > Sembawang Park (loop) via:

    Yishun Central > Yishun Avenue 3, Yishun Ring Road, Yishun Avenue 5, Sembawang Road > Sembawang Avenue > Sembawang Drive > Sembawang Way > Canberra Link > Sembawang Road, and u-turn at former Sembawang Road End bus terminal.
