    I don't think there is a need to reroute 55 and 76 to Bedok.

    1. 25 and 55 follows closely to each other from AMK Ave 3 all the way to Jalan Eunos. 55 I guess is probably a relieving service to 25 loads during peak hours. Service 25 has already provided the link from AMK, AMK Ave 3, Hougang Ave 3, Eunos MRT. Plus there is a 854 to Bedok too from Hougang Ave 3 onwards. In addition to all this, there are numerous services from Marine Parade to Bedok like 16, 31, 196, 197. Besides, the route that you are suggesting follows closely with service 16. Though it provides a new link from Siglap Rd to Jalan Eunos, Hougang Ave 3, it has also provided numerous fare stages duplication with the other bus services.

     

    2. For service 76, similarly, service 25 provides the link from AMK Ave 3 to Bedok. Next, service 24 provides the link from Upper Paya Lebar Rd, Paya Lebar Rd to  Bedok which 76 plies. When it proceeds to Tg Katong Rd, 31 does the job. When it reaches Marine Parade, 31, 196, 197 has done their job. The only new link that has created is from AMK Ave 4 to Bedok.
