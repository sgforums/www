    knowing this, i would propose the PM peak of 179A to be amended as follows.

    NTU non-stop Pioneer MRT

    the BC may do 2 or 3 179A trips during the PM peak before going to Boon Lay Int for break.

    NTU community saves travelling time
    free up alighting berth at BNL Int
