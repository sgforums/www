    (2014, January 14). New Sengkang bus service begins Sunday. MediaCorp Press Ltd. Retrieved from http://www.todayonline.com/

    SINGAPORE — A new loop bus service that will connect commuters travelling from Rivervale Crescent to Sengkang MRT Station and bus interchange will begin operation from this Sunday, said SBS Transit and the Land Transport Authority today (Jan 14).

    Service 371, which will operate daily from 5.45am till 12.35am, will ply along Compassvale Road, Compassvale Street, Rivervale Drive and Rivervale Crescent from Sengkang bus interchange.

    The new service will also provide convenient access to amenities including Rivervale Mall and schools, such as Compassvale Primary and Secondary, and Rivervale Primary.

    This is the 19th bus service launched under the Bus Service Enhancement Programme (BSEP).

    Royston Sim. (2014, January 14). New bus route 371 starts Sunday to serve Sengkang residents. Singapore Press Holdings Ltd. Retrieved from http://www.straitstimes.com/

    SBS Transit will launch a new feeder bus route - service 371- from Sunday to serve Sengkang residents traveling from Rivervale Crescent to Sengkang MRT station.

    The service will operate daily from 5.45am till 12.35am, and ply along Compassvale Road, Compassvale Street, Rivervale Drive and Rivervale Crescent.

    It will enhance connectivity to Sengkang MRT station as well as provide convenient access to Rivervale Mall and schools such as Compassvale Secondary and Rivervale Primary, said SBS Transit.

    This is the 19th bus route launched under the Bus Service Enhancement Programme, which will put a total of 800 new buses on the road to improve the bus network here.
