    Hi guys, let this thread be a short one.

    What i wanted to do is to compile the transport stuff that we will be expecting in 2017. Let us help each other by filling in this while we pass from 2016 to 2017, so that we will have stuff to look forward in next year. A wrap-up of 2016 is done in landtransportguru so just go there take a look. Here's what i anticipate nxt year (not according to chonological order, but rather what i can think of):

     

    1. Opening of Bukit Panjang ITH
    2. Opening of Sengkang Interchange Expansion (Compassvale Interchange)
    3. Opening/Relocation of Shenton Way Bus Ter and New Bridge Road Bus Ter
    4. Opening of TWE and together, the new Tuas Bus Ter
    5. Opening of DTL3
    6. Opening of Samudera LRT station
    7. Opening of Changi Airport Terminal 4 (and of course there should be amendments to bus services)
    8. Release of results for the 3rd GCM package
    9. New bus services (such as the 68 and 500 we have have discussing about :))
    10. Withdrawal of all premium services operated by public transport operators (but its just 555 and 598 left)
    11. Deregistration of some older buses such as O405G MKII (smiley)
    12. Awaiting press releases on developments and timeline of these projects (which may or may not come out nxt year):
    • JRL and CRL
    • SG-JB RTS and SG-KL HSR
    • Buangkok ITH


    These are just what i remembered. Do add comment if there are any left out. Thanks guys! :)

    And have a happy new year!
