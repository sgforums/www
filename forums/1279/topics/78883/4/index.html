    Forgot about services 2 to 20, here it goes. Most events mentioned happened from 1992.

    Service 2: Plied between Changi Village terminal and Buona Vista terminal. Shortened to New Bridge Rd terminal in 1993.

    Service 3: Plied between Bedok interchange and Jurong East interchange. Withdrawn in Dec 1990. Re-introduced in April 2002, with the merging of services 294 and 357. Extended to Punggol in Oct 2003.

    Service 4: Plied between Bedok interchange and St. Michael's terminal. Extended to Pasir Ris interchange when merged with former service 19. Later shortened to Tampines interchange. Merged with services 21 and 67 in 1994.

    Service 5: Plied between Bt Panjang terminal and Lor 1 Geylang terminal. Used to ply Queensway, C'wealth Ave, Alexandra Rd, Ganges Ave and Havelock Rd, amended in 1992 to ply Holland Ave, C'wealth Ave, Margaret Dr, Alexandra Rd, Lower Delta Rd, Jln Bt Merah, Kampong Bahru Rd and Eu Tong Sen St. Service 186 was introduced to cover the deleted sectors. Handed to tibs in 1995. Withdrawn in Dec 1999.

    Service 6: Re-introduced in 1993. No route change.

    Service 7: Plied between Bedok interchange and Jurong East interchange. Later shortened to Clementi interchange.

    Service 8: Re-introduced in 1990. Plied between Pasir Ris interchange and Toa Payoh interchange. Shortened to Tampines interchange in 1993. Amended to ply Tampines Ave 1 and Ave 10 in Sep 1995.

    Service 9: Plied between Lor 1 Geylang terminal and CACC. Shortened to Bedok interchange in 1993. In 1994, amended to ply Simei Ave and Simei St 3 instead of Upp Changi Rd East and Simei Rd. In 1995, amended to ply Bedok Rd and Upp Changi Rd instead of Upp Changi Rd East and Simei Ave.

    Service 10: Plied between Tampines interchange and Clementi interchange. Shortened to Kent Ridge terminal in March 1999, with the introduction of service 188.

    Service 11: Plied between Lor 1 Geylang terminal and Stadium Blvd. Amended in 1994 to ply Stadium Blvd in one direction only.

    Service 12: Plied between Pasir Ris interchange and New Bridge Rd terminal. No route change.

    Service 13: Plied between Yio Chu Kang terminal and Eunos interchange. Extended to Marine Parade terminal in 1998. Extended to Upp East Coast terminal in Jan 2002.

    Service 14: Plied between Bedok interchange and Bt Merah interchange. In 1993, merged with services 92 and 123, and extended to Clementi interchange.

    Service 15: Re-introduced in 1995, plied between Pasir Ris interchange and Eunos interchange. In 1997, merged with service M4 and extended to Marine Parade. Used to ply Marine Cres in both directions and Marine Terrace towards Pasir Ris, sectors deleted later.

    Service 16: Plied between Bt Merah interchange and Marine Parade terminal. Merged with service 149 and extended to Changi Airport PTB in 1993. Introduced service 16E in 1994, and re-numbered service 36 in Dec 1997. In Sep 1998, service 16 was shortened back to Marine Parade terminal. Became loop service in Sep 2001.

    Service 17: Re-introduced in 1993. No route change.

    Service 18: No route change.

    Service 19: Re-introduced in 1993, when service 29 was shortened from CACC to Changi Village. No route change.

    Service 20: Re-introduced in 1994, plied between Sims Place terminal and St. George estate. Merged with service 125 in 2000.

    139: Plied between Toa Payoh & Bukit Merah in the early 90s. Route was amended in 1993/1994 to ply River Valley Rd, Delta Rd, Alexandra Rd instead of Havelock Rd, Ganges Ave, Alexandra Rd, Lower Delta Rd, Tiong Bahru Rd. Deleted Sector now covered by SBST64. It was amended again in 1997 to ply St Michael's Estate instead of the entire stretch of Balestier Rd. It was amended again on 20th Jun 2003 to ply Serangoon Rd, Boon Keng Rd, Bendemeer Rd to serve Boon Keng MRT Stn instead of using Lavender St.

    140: Plied between Toa Payoh and Shenton Way in the 60s and 70s. Withdrawn during the NSL Rationalisation. Withdrawn sector is now mostly covered by SBST143.

    142: Plied between Toa Payoh & Potong Pasir Estate. No Route Change since 1993.

    143: Plied between Toa Payoh & Teban Gdns. Route was amended on 12th May 2002 to ply West Coast Rd, Teban Gdns Rd, Jurong Town Hall Rd instead of Pandan Gdns, Jurong Town Hall Rd, Teban Gdns Rd, West Coast Rd when SBST331 was withdrawn. That was also the same day that Penjuru Depot was closed and 143 was extended to Jurong East Int. It was amended again in early 2003 to serve both Pandan Gdns and Teban Gdns before going towards Jurong East Interchange.

    145: Plied between Toa Payoh & Buona Vista. No route change since 1993.

    147: Plied between Jurong East & Upp Serangoon Terminal in 1993 via Jurong East Central, Boon Lay Way & Commonwealth Ave West. It was amended in 1994 to terminate at Hougang Central when the latter opened. It was also cut short to Clementi in the same period. Route was amended again in 1999 to ply Prinsep St instead of Waterloo St and Middle Rd.

    149: Plied between Toa Payoh & Changi Airport via SBST31's route to Marine Parade, Siglap Link, ECP. Route was integrated with SBS31 to form the current SBST31. Deleted sector was covered by SBS16.

    151: Introduced in 1993/1994 to ply between Hougang South & Kent Ridge. It was amended on 15th Feb 2004 to ply via Hougang Ave 7/5 enroute to Hougang Central when Hougang South closed down.

    152: Plied between Toa Payoh & Bedok. It was integrated with SBS28 on 29th Oct 2000 to form SBST28. The integrated SBST28 was amended again on 29th March 2003 to ply Tampines St 32/34/45 instead of Tampines Ave 2/9.

    153: Plied between Upp Serangoon & Bt Merah. Route was amended to ply from Hougang Central when it opened in 1994. No major route change since.

    154: Plied between Boon Lay & Eunos. No route change since 1993.

    155: Plied between Marine Parade & Toa Payoh in 1993. It was amended to loop at Siglap Rd when Marine Parade Terminal closed down in Sep 2001. No route change since.

    156: Route was introduced in 1993/1994 between Serangoon & Clementi to cover the link between Serangoon & Bishan formerly covered by SBS104. Route was extended to Sengkang to form the current route on 25 Sep 2001.

    157: Plied between Toa Payoh & Boon Lay. Route was amended to ply Jurong West Ave 2 instead of Boon Lay Ave on 23th Dec 2001. No major route change since.

    158: Plied between Serangoon & Tg Rhu in 1993. No route change since except for the area at Geylang Lor 1 whereby a bus lane was opened in 1995.

    159: Plied between Toa Payoh and Ang Mo Kio via AMK Ave 8/3/6/1. Route was extended to Sengkang on 18th Feb 2001. No major route change since.

    160: Plied between Admiralty Rd West & Crawford St. It was amended on 10th Feb 1996 in the Beach Rd area to serve Bencoolen St, Middle Rd, Beach Rd towards Crawford St Terminal instead of Bencoolen St, Bras Basah Rd, Victoria St, Middle Rd, Beach Rd. In the direction towards Admiralty Rd West, it was amended to serve North Bridge Rd, Jln Sultan, Beach Rd, Middle Rd, Selegie Rd instead of North Bridge Rd, Arab St, Jln Besar, Bencoolen St, Middle Rd, Selegie Rd. Renumbered as TIBS980 in 1997/1998. Extended to Geylang Lor 1 via Kallang Rd when Crawford Terminal was closed at the same time it was renumbered,

    161: Plied between Hougang & Woodlands since 15th Oct 2002.

    162: Plied between Yio Chu Kang & Shenton Way/Marina Ctr since 25th Feb 2001. It was altered to call at all bus stops along its route in 2002. No route change since.

    163: Plied between Toa Payoh & Jln Kayu in 1993. Given to TIBS in 1995. No route change under TIBS. Given back to SBST in 1999. Route was extended to Sengkang via Sengkang West/East Ave, Punggol Rd & Sengkang East Way on 29th Dec 2002. No route change since.

    That's all for now
