    97 - Hougang to Jurong East -> Hougang Central to Jurong East -> Jurong East to Suntec City/Marina Centre

    97 is one of the first and the longest serving bus in Jurong East. Introduced in 1984, its original route was via Pasir Panjang Rd and Clementi Rd before entering the AYE (then known as Ayer Rajah Road). It was amended via Alexandra Rd when 79 was withdrawn circa. 1986/87. In 1994, it was extended to the new Hougang Central Interchange via Hougang Ave 2 and 10. In 2003, it was shortened to loop at Suntec City/Marina Centre as part of SBS's bus rationalisation with the commencement of the NEL.

    98 - Jurong East Interchange to Jurong Interchange -> Jln Ahmad Ibrahim -> Jurong Pier Rd

    98 is another old timer in Jurong East. Introduced in 1985, it was originally a loop service to the old Jurong Interchange. Its original route was via Corporation Rd from Jurong West Ave 1. In 1990, when Jurong Interchange was closed, it was made to loop at Jln Ahmad Ibrahim like the present 98A. In 1992, 247, which runs from Boon Lay to Jurong Pier Rd, was integrated with 98. At the same time, it was amended via Jurong West St 51 and Boon Lay Way to form the present route.

    93 - Paya Lebar Airport (Jln Ang Teng) to Alexandra Rd (Labrador) -> Labrador to Paya Lebar Airport -> World Trade Centre to Paya Lebar Airport -> World Trade Centre (Harbourfront) to Eunos

    93 is an old timer. Although its point to point destinations have changed but its route, for most part, has been intact since the 70s. One of the so called airport bus, it originally plied from Paya Lebar Airport to Labrador. When Paya Lebar Airport was turned into a military airport in 1981, it was made to loop there instead. In 1985, it was extended to the new World Trade Centre Terminal (now known as Harbourfront). In 1993, it stopped going to the airport and was extended to terminate at Eunos.

    96 - Clementi to Kent Ridge Cres

    96 was introduced in 1984 as a feeder service to NUS (OMO 40c). Route unchanged but operation hours changed for Sundays and Public Holidays. Initially, no operation on these days but later on there were but only until 2100 hrs.     Service 70: Plied between Jln Kayu terminal and Shenton Way terminal. In 1992, with the amendment of service 73 (then Toa Payoh interchange - Yio Chu Kang terminal), amended to ply between Yio Chu Kang terminal and Shenton Way terminal as a day service on weekdays and Saturdays, while on weekday and Saturday evenings, Sundays and Public Holidays, the service, known as 70X, terminated at Lor 1 Geylang terminal. In 2001, service 70X was extended to loop at Marina Centre. Also in 2001, amended to ply Yio Chu Kang Link and Boundary Rd. In June 2003, amended to ply Serangoon Central and Upp Serangoon Rd.

    Service 71: Plied between Lor 1 Geylang terminal and Old Upp Thomson terminal via Sims Ave, Paya Lebar Rd, Upp Paya Lebar Rd and Yio Chu Kang Rd. Withdrawn in 1992.

    Service 72: Plied between Serangoon interchange and Tanjong Rhu via Boundary Rd, Upp Paya Lebar Rd, Paya Lebar Rd, Tanjong Katong Rd, Mountbatten Rd and Fort Rd. Withdrawn in 1992. Re-introduced in 1996, as a weekday peak hours service between Yio Chu Kang terminal and Ang Mo Kio Ave 5 (Seagate). In March 1998, merged with service 80 (Tampines interchange - Hougang Central interchange). In June 2003, amended to ply Hougang Central.

    Service 73: Plied between Toa Payoh interchange and Yio Chu Kang terminal. Shortened to Ang Mo Kio interchange in 1992 via Ang Mo Kio Ave 3 and became green and white plates service. In 1993, amended to ply Serangoon Nth Ave 4. Later became loop service.

    Service 74: Plied between Ang Mo Kio interchange and Clementi interchange. Used to ply Clementi Ave 2 in one direction (towards Ang Mo Kio), in return journey, turned right into C'wealth Ave West from Clementi Rd. Extended to Upp Serangoon terminal in late 1980s. In 1993, merged with service 202 (Buona Vista terminal - Jln Anak Bt). In 1994, amended to Hougang Central interchange.

    Service 75: Plied between Choa Chu Kang interchange and Marina Centre terminal. Shortened to Bt Panjang terminal in 1993, with the introduction of service 179. (Choa Chu Kang interchange - Fajar Rd)

    Service 76: Plied between Yio Chu Kang terminal and Marine Parade terminal. Used to ply Ubi Ave 2, Eunos Link, Jln Eunos and Still Rd, amended to ply Tanjong Katong Rd and Amber Rd in 1993, with the introduction of service 55. (then Ang Mo Kio interchange - Marine Parade terminal) Became loop service in Sep 2001.

    Service 77: Re-introduced in 1995, plied between Marina Centre terminal and Farrer Rd. A year later, extended to Bt Batok interchange, skipping Farrer Rd.

    Service 78: Came about in 1990, covering parts of service 245 route. Amended to skip one pair of stops along Penjuru Rd and call at a new pair along West Coast Rd, when parts of Teban Gardens Rd was closed. In May 2002, amended to skip Teban Gardens Rd in one direction. (towards Jurong East)

    Service 79: Re-introduced in 1993, loop service plying between Jurong East interchange and Boon Lay interchange. A year later, amended into Chin Bee industrial area, with the amendment of service 246. Amended to skip one pair of stops along Penjuru Rd and call at a new pair along West Coast Rd, when parts of Teban Gardens Rd was closed.

    Service 86: Re-numbered from service 866 (service 866 is former service 59) in Dec 1999, plied between Ang Mo Kio interchange and Sengkang interchange. In Feb 2003, amended to ply Compassvale Dr instead of Punggol Rd and Sengkang East Ave.
