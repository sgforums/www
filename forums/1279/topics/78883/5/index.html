    Service 170: Plied between Queen St terminal and Johor Bahru terminal. Amended to Larkin terminal in 1995. Amended to use new Woodlands checkpoint in July 1999. Became FAC in Nov 2001. In 2003, split into blue and red plates service.

    Service 171: Plied between Yishun interchange and Queen St terminal. Shortened to Marina Centre terminal, and amended into Bt Panjang town in 1993. Amended to skip Mandai Lake Rd, and amended to ply Mandai Ave in 1994, with the re-introduction of service 138. Handed to tibs in 1995. Amended to ply BKE instead of Woodlands Rd and Mandai Rd in Dec 1999, with the amendment of service 927.

    Service 172: Plied between Lim Chu Kang terminal and Shenton Way terminal. Later shortened to loop at Jln Anak Bt. Amended to start from Boon Lay interchange in 1993, with the re-introduction of service 175. Amended to ply Sungei Tengah Rd, Brickland Rd and Choa Chu Kang Ave 3, when parts of Choa Chu Kang Rd was closed in stages. Handed to tibs in Dec 1999 and on the same day, shortened to Choa Chu Kang interchange.

    Service 173: Plied between Bt Batok interchange and Clementi interchange. Amended to ply Bt Batok Central, Ave 1 and St 21 instead of East Ave 3, and became loop service in 1993. Handed to tibs in Dec 2000.

    Service 174: Plied between NTI terminal and New Bridge Rd terminal. Amended to Boon Lay interchange in 1990. Amended to ply PIE (now Jurong West Ave 2) instead of Jurong Rd (between Corporation Rd and Hong Kah flyover) in 1993. Amended to ply Upp Cross St/Havelock Rd, Chin Swee Rd and Outram Rd in Nov 2003.

    Service 175: Re-introduced in 1993, loop service plied between Lim Chu Kang terminal and Upp Bt Timah Rd. Amended to ply Sungei Tengah Rd, Brickland Rd and Choa Chu Kang Ave 3, when parts of Choa Chu Kang Rd was closed in stages. Handed to tibs in Dec 1999.

    Service 176: Plied between Bt Merah interchange and Bt Panjang terminal. Amended to ply Bt Batok East Ave 2 instead of East Ave 4 in 1995. Handed to tibs in 1995 after the amendment. Amended to Bt Panjang interchange in Dec 1999.

    Service 177: Loop service which plied between Bt Batok interchange and Bt Panjang terminal. Handed to tibs in 1995. Amended to terminate at Bt Panjang interchange in Dec 1999.

    Service 178: Plied between Woodlands interchange and Jurong interchange. Extended to Boon Lay interchange in 1990. Extended to Woodlands Regional interchange in Feb 1996, and covered parts of route of service 372 (now service 903).

    Service 179: Re-introduced in 1993, loop service which plied between Choa Chu Kang interchange and Fajar Rd. No route change. Handed to tibs in 1995. Withdrawn in Dec 1999. Re-introduced again in Jan 2001, loop service which plied between Boon Lay interchange and NTU. No route change.

    Service 180: Re-numbered from service 951 (Marsiling terminal - Boon Lay interchange) in March 1996. Amended to Bt Panjang interchange in Dec 1999.

    Service 181: Plied between Woodlands interchange and Crawford St terminal. Amended to Woodlands Regional interchange in Feb 1996. Amended to Lor 1 Geylang terminal and re-numbered service 961 in Dec 1998.

    Service 182: Plied between Marsiling terminal and New Bridge Rd terminal. Amended to Shenton Way terminal in 1993. Withdrawn in March 1996. Re-introduced in July 1998, plied between Jurong East interchange and Tuas terminal. Shortened to Boon Lay interchange in Feb 2001. Amended to ply Jurong West St 64, Boon Lay Way, Upp Jurong Rd and PIE instead of Jln Boon Lay and Jln Ahmad Ibrahim in May 2002, when merged with service 183. Amended to call at all stops along Upp Jurong Rd in Nov 2003.

    Service 183: Introduced in July 1998, loop service which plied between Tuas terminal and Tuas South. No route change. Merged with service 182 in May 2002.

    Service 184: Re-introduced in 1994. Loop service which plied between Bt Panjang terminal and Clementi MRT station. Handed to tibs in 1995. Amended to Bt Panjang interchange in Dec 1999.

    Service 185: Plied between Choa Chu Kang interchange and Shenton Way terminal. Shortened to loop at Jln Anak Bt in 1993, with the re-introduction of service 186 and amendment of service 5. Handed to tibs in Dec 1999. Merged with service 989 in July 2003.

    Service 186: Re-introduced in 1992, loop service which plied between Shenton Way terminal and Farrer Rd. Extended to St. Michael's terminal in 1993.

    Service 187: Plied between Bt Panjang terminal and Boon Lay interchange. Handed to tibs in 1995. Extended to Woodlands Regional interchange in March 1996.

    Service 188: Re-introduced in March 1999, with the shortening of service 10. Handed to tibs in Dec 1999. No route change.

    Service 189: Plied between Bt Batok interchange and Marina Centre terminal. Merged with service 366 and shortened to loop at Clementi West St 2 in 1990. Extended to West Coast Ferry terminal in 1995. Amended to ply PIE and Clementi Ave 6 instead of Toh Tuck Ave and C'wealth Ave West in 1996. Handed to tibs in June 2000.
