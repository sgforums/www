    Changi Airport Bus Plan 1983

    The SBS Bus Plan
    The new Changi Airport Bus Plan consists of 5 services operated with 65 buses. 2 are existing services with slightly amended routes whilst the other 3 are new services.
    Under the Plan, 14 Scheme B buses now operating the SSB service will be integrated into the new Services 24, 57 and 149.

    The 5 services are:-

    Service 9 (Existing) (Stage Fare with TMO Buses)
    The route of this Service currently running from Geylang Fringe Car Park to Changi Point remains the same except that it will now terminate at the Cargo Complex instead of Changi Point. It replaces Services 391 and 395 to the Cargo Complex.

    Service 24 (New) (OMO Ticketing System)
    This Service replace existing service 392 from Somapah, Service 394 from Bedok and SSB Service 727 from Ang Mo Kio.

    Service 57 (New) (Flat Fare 80 cents)
    This Service operating the same route as SSB Service 757 which runs from Queenstown to PTB except that the route I slightly amended to avoid the Central Business District and join the East Coast Parkway after Keppel Rd.

    Because of the low passenger demand for the route as evident in surveys conducted earlier, this service will operate only specific trips during the peak hours in the peak direction.

    Service 149 (New) (OMO Ticketing System)
    This service, which runs from Toa Payoh to PTB, will replace SSB Service 737 from Roa Payoh and SBS Service 747 from Kallang Bahru.

    Service 390 (Existing) (Flat Fare 80 cents)
    This service currently running between Queen St and the PTB will be extended to Orchard Rd to serve a larger number of passengers.
