    All 24x and 25x services indicated below are from 1992 onwards.

    Service 240: Feeder service which plied between Boon Lay interchange and Jln Ahmad Ibrahim. No route change.

    Service 241: Feeder service which plied between Jurong East interchange and Teban Gdns Rd. No route change under this number. Re-numbered service 331 in 1998.

    Service 242: Feeder service which plied between Boon Lay interchange and Jurong West St 91. Amended in June 1992 to ply Jurong West St 72 and Ave 5 instead of Upp Jurong Rd and Pioneer Rd Nth.

    Service 243: Feeder service which plied between Boon Lay interchange and Jurong West Ave 5. Amended in June 1992 to ply Upp Jurong Rd in the return journey instead of Jurong West Ave 5 and St 72. Amended in 1994 to loop at St 71 via St 75 and St 81, skipping St 72. Amended in 1996 to ply express along St 64 instead of Jln Boon Lay and Upp Jurong Rd. Amended in July 1999 to 2-directional service via St 62, St 61 and Jurong West Ave 4. Also from this day, call at stops along St 64.

    Service 244: Feeder service which plied between Boon Lay interchange and Corporation Dr, via Boon Lay Dr, Ave, Place, Dr, Corporation Rd, Yung Ho Rd, Corporation Dr, Yung Kuang Rd (express) and return. Withdrawn in June 1992.

    Service 246: JIS which plied between Boon Lay interchange and 3rd Chin Bee Rd, via Boon Lay Dr, Ave, Place, Dr, Corporation Rd, Boon Lay Way, Yuan Ching Rd, Kang Ching Rd, Corporation Dr, Yung Ho Rd, Jln Tukang, Quality Rd, Chin Bee Ave, 3rd Chin Bee Rd, International Rd, Yung Kuang Rd (express) and return. Amended in 1994 to loop at Boon Lay Dr via current route. Parts of route covered by service 79.

    Service 248: JIS which plied between Boon Lay interchange and Neythal Rd, via Jln Boon Lay, International Rd, Fan Yoong Rd, Wan Lee Rd, Enterprise Rd, Kwong Min Rd, Soon Lee Rd, International Rd (express), Neythal Rd, International Rd (express), Tractor Rd, Jln Boon Lay (express between Tractor Rd and International Rd) and Boon Lay Way (express). Merged with service 250 in June 1992 and withdrawn. With the withdrawal of this service, Neythal Rd and Tractor Rd have no more service.

    Service 249: JIS which plied between Boon Lay interchange and Jln Samulun. From Feb 2001, call at all stops along Jln Boon Lay (used to express between Boon Lay Way and International Rd).

    Service 250: JIS which plied between Boon Lay interchange and 6th Lok Yang Rd, via Jln Boon Lay (express), International Rd, 6th Lok Yang Rd, Lok Yang Way, Pioneer Rd Nth, International Rd and Jln Boon Lay (express). Merged with service 248 in June 1992 to ply Soon Lee area. Merged again with services 192 and 252 in Feb 2001 and withdrawn.

    Service 251: JIS which plied between Boon Lay interchange and Refinery Rd. Merged with service 253 in Feb 2001 to loop at Benoi Rd, and skip Refinery Rd.

    Service 252: JIS which plied between Boon Lay interchange and Gul Circle, via Jln Boon Lay (express), International Rd, Joo Koon Circle, Joo Koon Way, Gul Way, Gul Circle, Gul Way, Joo Koon Way, Joo Koon Rd, Joo Koon Circle, International Rd and Jln Boon Lay (express). In 1994, with the modification of the junction of Jln Ahmad Ibrahim and Joo Koon Way/Gul Way, shortened to loop at Joo Koon Rd. In Feb 2001, merged with service 250 to ply Soon Lee area.

    Service 253: JIS which plied between Boon Lay interchange and Benoi Rd. In 1994, with the modification of the junction of Jln Ahmad Ibrahim and Benoi Sector, amended to enter and exit Benoi Sector via Pioneer Rd (express) and express along Jln Ahmad Ibrahim. Specific trips used to loop at Benoi Crescent during the peak hours. In Feb 2001, merged with services 251, 255 and 257, and withdrawn.

    Service 254: JIS which plied between Boon Lay interchange and Tuas Ave 1, via Jln Boon Lay (express), International Rd (express), Benoi Rd, Jln Ahmad Ibrahim, Tuas Rd (end), Pioneer Rd, Tuas Cres, Ave 20, Pioneer Rd, Tuas Ave 8, Ave 9, Ave 12, Ave 1, Ave 3, Tuas Rd and return.
