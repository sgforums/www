    All services are from 1992 onwards.

    Service 361: Feeder service which plied between Bukit Batok interchange and Bukit Batok West Ave 8. No route change. In June 2000, handed to Tibs. In May 2003, re-numbered service 941.

    Service 365: Feeder service which plied between Bukit Batok interchange and Bukit Batok St 34. No route change. In June 2000, handed to Tibs. In May 2003, re-numbered service 945.

    Service 367: Feeder service which plied between Bukit Batok interchange and Bukit Batok West Ave 4. No route change. In June 2000, handed to Tibs. In May 2003, re-numbered service 947.

    Service 371: Introduced in 1999. Feeder service which plied between Sengkang terminal and Compassvale Rd, via Compassvale Rd, St, Rivervale Dr, Punggol Rd, East Ave, Compassvale Rd, Dr (express), Sengkang Ctrl (express), East Ave and return. In 2000, extended to East Ave via Anchorvale Rd (express) and Anchorvale Lane (express). In 2000, amended to Sengkang temporary interchange. In 2002, amended to ply East Way, East Rd and Compassvale St instead of Compassvale Rd. In Jan 2003, amended to present Sengkang interchange. In March 2003, amended and extended to West Ave via current route.

    Service 372: Feeder service which plied between Woodlands interchange and Marsiling Lane, via Centre Rd, Admiralty Rd, Marsiling Dr, Lane, Rd, Dr and Admiralty Rd. In Feb 1996, amended to Woodlands Regional interchange and re-numbered service 903. Re-introduced in 2000, plied between Sengkang temporary interchange and Anchorvale Rd. In Jan 2003, amended to present Sengkang interchange.

    Service 378: Re-numbered from service 820 in 2001. Feeder service which plied between Jln Kayu terminal and Piccadilly. In Dec 2003, merged with service 103 and withdrawn.

    Service 379: Re-numbered from service 821 in 2001. Feeder service which plied between Jln Kayu terminal and West Camp Rd. In Dec 2003, merged with service 103 and withdrawn.

    Service 382: Feeder service which plied between Eunos interchange and Ubi Rd 3. No route change. In Oct 2002, merged with service 63 and withdrawn.

    Service 385: Feeder service which plied between Eunos interchange and Bedok Reservoir Rd. No route change. In Nov 2002, merged with service 226 to become service 60.

    Service 390: Loop service which plied between Changi Airport PTB Coach Park and Tomlinson Rd. In 1993, withdrawn with route covered by service 16.

    Service 400: Loop service which plied between Shenton Way terminal and Marina Mall. No route change.

    Service 401: Loop service which plied between Bedok interchange and East Coast Park Service Rd. In 1997, extended to Lor 1 Geylang terminal, amended to ply New Upp Changi Rd and Siglap Rd instead of Bedok South Rd and Bedok South Ave 1. In June 2003, skipped Lor 1 Geylang terminal and extended to Tomlinson Rd. In August 2003, skipped Siglap Link.

    Service 402: Introduced in 1994. Loop service which plied between Bedok interchange and Changi Coast Walk. No operation on weekdays. Withdrawn in late 1994.

    Service 403: Loop service which plied between Pasir Ris interchange and Pasir Ris Rd. No route change.

    Service 405: Introduced in 1993. Loop service which plied between Boon Lay interchange and Choa Chu Kang Rd End. Operates during festive days. In 1997, extended to Lim Chu Kang Rd.

    Service 410: Introduced in June 1992. "Townlink" service which plied between Bishan interchange and Sin Ming Ave. In 1993, change of plate colours for direction. (ie. direction 1 changed to White Plate, while direction 2 changed to Green Plate) No route change.

    CSS, JI and TC services are omitted.

    Express 501: Introduced in May 1997. Peak hours one-way service. Plied from Hougang Central interchange to Penang Rd in AM peak. Plied from Anson Rd to Hougang Central interchange in PM peak. In 1999, extended to ply Marina Centre and converted to loop service. A short route version (Express 501X) was introduced at the same time to loop at Marina Centre. In Sep 2000, extended to Sengkang temporary interchange. In Jan 2003, amended to present Sengkang interchange. Withdrawn in July 2003, under NEL rationalisation phase 2.

    Express 502: Introduced in May 1997. Plied between Hougang Central interchange and Boon Lay interchange. In Sep 2000, extended to Sengkang temporary interchange. In Jan 2003, amended to present Sengkang interchange. In July 2003, shortened to loop at Marina Centre from Boon Lay interchange, under NEL rationalisation phase 2.

    Express 503: Introduced in May 1997. Peak hours one-way service. Plied from Tampines interchange to Temasek Ave in AM peak. Plied from Anson Rd to Tampines interchange in PM peak. In Sep 1999, amended and re-numbered Express 505.

    Express 505: Re-numbered from Express 503 in Sep 1999. Loop service which plied between Pasir Ris interchange and Jln Bukit Merah/Telok Blangah Rd. In Sep 2000, amended and re-numbered Express 518.

    Express 506: Introduced in 1998. Plied between Jurong East interchange and Bedok interchange. In 2000, extended to Changi Airport PTB. In Sep 2002, shortened back to Bedok interchange.

    Express 507: Introduced in 1998. Peak hours one-way service. Plied from Boon Lay interchange to Shenton Way in AM peak. Plied from Anson Rd to Boon Lay interchange in PM peak. No route change.

    Express 508: Introduced in 1998. Peak hours one-way service. Plied from Sengkang terminal to Penang Rd in AM peak. Plied from Outram Rd to Sengkang terminal in PM peak. In Sep 2000, shortened to Hougang Central interchange with the extension of Express 501 and re-numbered Express 511.

    Express 511: Re-numbered from Express 508 in Sep 2000. Peak hours one-way service. Plied from Hougang Central interchange to Penang Rd in AM peak. Plied from Outram Rd to Hougang Central interchange in PM peak. No route change. Withdrawn in July 2003, under NEL rationalisation phase 2.

    Express 512: Introduced in Sep 2000 with the extension of Express 502. Peak hours one-way service. Plied from Hougang Central interchange to Orchard Rd in AM peak. Plied from Moulmein Rd to Hougang Central interchange in PM peak. No route change. Withdrawn in July 2003, under NEL rationalisation phase 2.

    Express 518: Re-numbered from Express 505 in Sep 2000. Loop service which plied between Pasir Ris interchange and Orchard Rd/Marina Centre. No route change.

    Express 520: Introduced in May 1997. Peak hours one-way service. Plied from Yishun interchange to Outram Rd in AM peak. Plied from New Bridge Rd terminal to Yishun interchange in PM peak. No route change. Withdrawn in Aug 2003.

    Express 521: Introduced in Sep 1999. Plied between Bukit Panjang terminal and WTC terminal. Charged normal trunk fares (for adults) during off-peak hours for a promotional period. In Sep 2002, amended and extended to Woodlands Regional interchange, converted to trunk service and re-numbered service 963.

    Service 700: Introduced in Aug 1998 as a RapidBus service. Plied between Woodlands Regional interchange and Shenton Way terminal. In Dec 1999, shortened to Bukit Panjang interchange. Service 700A was introduced at the same time to loop at Marina Centre during off-peak hours.
