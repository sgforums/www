    Well, the Estimated Next Bus Departure Time display was introduced in 1994 as a pilot trial. Two interchanges, namely Toa Payoh & Hougang (Central), were selected on the trial before other interchanges like Jurong East, Boon Lay, Hougang (South), Bedok, Tampines, etc. gets the departure display throughout the year 1995.

    With the introduction of IRIS, the main purpose in knowing about the departure time becomes outlined and more people uses IRIS instead in order to keep with the new technology. But then, its still a heavier disadvantage becuase the usage of IRIS are charged accordingly to your next mobile phone bill or you need to enquire the departure time at the interchange office if you don't wish to use the IRIS.
