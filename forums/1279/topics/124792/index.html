    The explosive force of CNG is much higher than petrol.

    Thus.. CNG dispensing locations.. cannot be near residential or high density areas. Given land scarcity in Sg.. is a big challenge to site such a location. The cost of compensation for the buffer zone will not be small.

    And using tankers.. CNG isn't like a liquid... enter tank by gravity. Requires a longer tank refill time. Not as efficient as existing options. Expect fare increase if this option is pursued.
