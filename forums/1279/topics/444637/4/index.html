    Sunday, 11/03/2012
    SBS815S on revenue service 325 had a minor crash with another motorbike at Hougang Avenue 9, which is apprently trying to cut the bus to drive into the HDB road. The right bumper of the bus is slightly dented, the right headlights having some cracks and the rubber protector at the right side of the bus being torn apart. I do not have any news of anyone being injured. As it happened at lunchtime break, many people are forced to take another 325 and it flooded the bus stop outside Hougang Point. As that road only had 2 lanes and with 1 lane blocked, the road had a heavy traffic jam, causing services 72, 109, 159 and 325 to be delayed from reaching Hougang Point bus stop. The bus is slowly towed back to HGDEP for inspection. The bus is currently laid-up till end of life-span as it only had 2 month's time left and having it's rear door having a minor hydraulic malfunctioning problem, the bus will be replaced with another Mk4 bus. But as for now, no permanent buses are filling in the missing slot.
