    Passengers forced to evacuate from bus with smoking wheel

    STOMPer Suresh says one of the wheels of the bus 169 he was riding in started emitting smoke, forcing passengers to be evacuated.

    The STOMPer wrote:

    "I was travelling on bus 169 today (Aug 28) at about 1.30pm when all of a sudden there was the smell of smoke.

    "A man approached the bus driver and told him there was smoke coming from the wheel of the bus.

    "We were somewhere along Sembawang Road at the time, near a construction site.

    "We all had to alight and board another bus.

    "It was just a few minutes later, another 169 came.

    "Most of us boarded it. I glad that we did not have to pay extra cash for the other bus.

    "I would like to thank the bus company for doing a good job in handling the situation."
