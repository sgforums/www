    Not all buses have emergency exit doors. The new generations of superbuses and all bendy buses (plus other model of buses) do not have any emergency exit doors, when in emergency, passengers are to break the glass windows with the hammers provided or use any hard objects.

    If the bus fell to the right, then exit via the front or rear windscreen by breaking the glass, it's quite difficult to get out from the left side in this situation, as the left side become the "roof" of the bus.
