    The only proposed route extension that I know of is the one done (at page 88 of http://ttsgroup.com.sg/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/Wenya-TIA-Report-May-2012.pdf) when the traffic was assessed on Wenya (Bulim) Industrial Park:

    1. Boon Lay Bus Interchange
    2. Jurong West Central 3
    3. Jurong West Street 64
    4. Jurong West Street 75
    5. Jurong West Avenue 5
    6. Jurong West Avenue 3
    7. Jurong West Street 22
    8. Jurong West Street 23
    9. Jurong West Avenue 2
    10. Bulim Avenue
    11. Bulim Drive
    12. Jalan Bahar
    13. Jurong West Avenue 5
    14. Jurong West Street 75
    15. Jurong West Street 64
    16. Jurong West Central 3
    17. Boon Lay Bus Interchange
    • It would make 181 loop at Jurong West Avenue 3/Street 22/Street 23/Avenue 2, Bulim Avenue/Drive and Jalan Bahar.
    • 181 would skip the two bus stops opposite blk 273 and blk 276 at Jurong West Avenue 3.

    I do not understand why they do not want Jurong West Avenue 3 to intersect with Bulim Avenue, and Jurong West Street 25 to intersect with Bulim Drive. Otherwise, 181 can just go straight from Jurong West Avenue 3 to Bulim Avenue and return from Bulim Drive, via Jurong West Street 25.

    Well, if 181 is extended to Bulim Depot next year, will it go into Bulim Depot, or will it just loop at the roundabout at Bulim Drive? Because I do not think Bulim Drive will be extended to Jalan Bahar soon.

    Anyway, the assessment done in year 2012 also indicated that the Wenya/Bulim bus depot could be a temporary facility. The site it is on now is actually meant for an integrated dormitory and for the Clean Tech industry.

    I guess Wenya/Bulim depot will be replaced with a train-cum-bus depot at the site between PIE Hong Kah Flyover and the junction of Jurong West Avenue 2 and Bulim Avenue, by year 2025 when Jurong Region Line opens.
