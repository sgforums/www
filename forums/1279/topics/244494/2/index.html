  • vivienlee
    1 Apr 07, 16:38
    163

    Early 1990s
    - Full fleet of NAC MB0405s

    Nov 1994
    - Sv 163 became a PAC service
    - Mixture of NAC and AC MB0405s (mainly MB0405 WA and 1 DM perm for AC buses)

    July 1995
    - Sv 163 became a FAC service
    - Full fleet of AC MB0405 buses (majority MB0405 WA and 1 DM perm)

    Dec 1995
    - Sv 163 was handed over to Tibs
    - Full fleet of OAC WA Scania striders used

    Nov 1998
    - Sv 163 became a full Dennis Lance 245 fleet when Tibs bought in the UK Dennis Lances in 1998

    Dec 1999
    - Sv 163 was handed back to SBS
    - Majority fleet of CAC MB0405 DM and 1 OAC MB0405 DM were used

    Dec 2002
    - Sv 163 was extended to Sengkang
    - Whole fleet of sv 163 became a Volvo fleet
    - Sv 163 initially had full Volvo MK3 fleet
    - After which different variants of MK4s (DM, Striders and DM3500) came in

    Mar 2005
    - Sv 163 had 1 Volvo B10 perm - SBS1688K
