    2) Jurong West St 63/ Pioneer Rd Nth junction. The left turning zebra crossing used by 255 & 257 to get to Pioneer Rd Nth from Jurong West St 63 that's the topic of talk. The crowd crossing the road is endless.

    3) Jurong West St 63/ Jurong West St 64 junction: The zebra crossing that 179, 179A, 241, 255 uses to get to BNL Int. Same issue as above.

    3) Dunearn Rd/ Kheam Hock Rd: Beating of red light and cars from Evans Rd would tend to drive into the yellow box to wait for pedestrains to cross (No traffic from Kheam Hock Rd).

    4) Bt Panjang Circus: Look out for vehicles making last minute clearance. The green light/ green man appears very fast.
