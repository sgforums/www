    let me help you with a bit of planning. Sometimes I find the sbs website gives weird solutions.

    If you do not mind waking up early in the morning you may take service 24 from the basement terminus to Ang Mo Kio Interchange, thereafter transferring 138 to the zoo. If you are rushing for time, take the train directly to Ang Mo Kio instead thereafter cross the underpass to the Interchange for 138.

    Here are the timings for 138 from Ang Mo Kio Interchange on
    Weekdays: 0739 0758 0813 0829 0844 0900 0920 0939 0959 1018 1038 1057
    Saturdays: 0739 0750 0801 0813 0824 0835 0847 0903 0919 0935 0951 1006 1021 1037 1052
    Sundays: 0732 0743 0755 0806 0818 0829 0841 0852 0904 0915 0926 0938 0953 1008 1023 1037 1050

    You may take 138 back to Ang Mo Kio Interchange and have lunch at the AMK Hub or any coffee shops nearby. From here i recommend 3 routes to the airport.
    1. Service 166 to Clementi, then train to Airport. Bus ride approx 1h45min, train about 55min cos of the waiting time at Tanah Merah. You may wish to change to the train at HarBourfront, City Hall or Little India train stations if time is of an important factor

    2. Service 25 to Bedok Interchange. Change to the train thereafter or 24 from Bedok station bus stop.

    3. 88 from the bus stop outside Ang Mo Kio MRT to Pasir Ris. From there I would advice you to take the train. Otherwise, transfer to 53 at the first bus stop after the express sector.

    you may consider other solutions but these are some ideas for you. :)

    Since this is the case, here's what i would propose for you

    I hope your nephew would recover from jet lag by the time he wakes up at 9am at the airport hotel. Thereafter, take a train to Tampines, or service 27 from the airport terminus to Tampines train station, transfer to 168 to Woodlands Interchange. 168 has the longest express sector of any basic bus service in Singapore. I believe your nephew would love it.

    At Woodlands Interchange, board 926 to the zoo. After your zoo trip, take 138 to Ang Mo Kio Interchange transfer the train to Clarke Quay via Dhoby Ghaut. From here, look for the MICA Building exit from the station and then walk up Fort Canning from River Valley Rd which is next to the building.

    With regards to lunch, you may eat at either of the 3 places
    1. Zoo
    2, AMK Hub which is located at Ang Mo Kio Interchange
    3. Central (shopping ctr) @Clarke Quay. Its above the train station of the same name.

    After the Battle Box, you may wish to hike your way down to Central @ Clarke Quay bus stop for Service 2 12 or 51. That is if you still have time for another double deck ride. Otherwise, take the train to the airport from City Hall which is within walking distance from Battle Box.

    For Service 2 or 12, you may alight at Tanah Merah train station bus stop for a transfer to Service 24 to the airport. For 51, the transfer is at Eunos train station bus stop.

    Help you plan ba.

    Take 53 to Pasir Ris Dr 1(Alight at the first bus stop aft turning into Loyang Ave)
    Walk along Pasir Ris Dr 2 to Pasir Ris Dr 3.
    Take 89 to Downtown East and have some fun with your nephew there. (If you took Tridents, good for you)
    Alight at Punggol Rd.
    Take 161 to Woodlands Int. (You might want to shop at Causeway Point.)
    Then, take 926 to the Zoo.
    Afterwards, take 927 to Choa Chu Kang. (You might want to shop at Lot 1.)
    Then, take 67 down to Bt Timah. (You might want to stop by Memories @ Old Ford or Bt Timah Hill.)
    Transfer to 77 to Orchard/Suntec City or 960 to Bugis. (Maybe you could show him around those shopping malls around the City.)

    Then, take 36 back to Changi Airport if you're in Orchard/Suntec City or 12 to Expo MRT and take the MRT to Changi Airport.
