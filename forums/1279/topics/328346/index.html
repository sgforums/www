    NightRider

    Flat fare: $3.50 (ez-link card/cash)

    To enjoy a free transfer to another NightRider service, commuters must tap their ez-link cards on the exit reader upon alighting from the first NightRider service. The time allowed for free transfer is 45 minutes.

    Concessionary travel (includes concession cardholders with Bus Concession Pass) is not applicable.

    -----

    NiteOwl

    Fares: $2.50 (ez-link), $3.00 (cash)

    Concessionary travel (includes concession cardholders with Bus Concession Pass, except Season Pass holders) and transfer rebates are not applicable.
