    Not really a photo of a bus, but stickers of both 5 and 175
    http://i120.photobucket.com/albums/o171/SBS9806J/DSC02799.jpg
    If you look carefully, you will notice that there's a slight difference between the "1" in the 175 sticker and the older "1"s; same case for "5" between the newer and older ones. The older font, I believe, is Helvetica, while the newer ones seem to be Arial.; you can read http://www.ms-studio.com/articlesarialsid.html to learn how to distinguish the two otherwise-identical fonts to the untrained eye.

    I think some typeface professional is going to go nuts with SBS Transit for replacing Helvetica for Arial (the latter being seen as a cheap, unprofessional replacement for the former - read this to learn about their history: http://www.ms-studio.com/articles.html), but if it helps SBS Transit saves money for them to invest on their more important business (running buses, the Sengkang and Punggol LRT, and the NEL), then it's actually not that bad a thing.
