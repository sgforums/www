    The complete collection of SBST Fast Forward (FFW) photographs

    Since the inception of FFWs on 3 January 2005 (starting with FFW 97e), FFW have since expanded to run on seven services currently.

    Note that they runs alongside their parent routes (but skipping some of the stops), charging adult fares.

    (corrections to some of FFW commencement dates; acknowledgements to brotherchen1982 for the correct information.)

    FFW 10e (commenced on 19 September 2005; between Bedok Road and Shenton Way)



    FFW 14e (commenced on 12 December 2005; between Bedok North and Orchard Road)



    FFW 30e (commenced on 19 September 2005; between Bedok and Pasir Panjang)



    FFW 89e (commenced on 19 September 2005; between Hougang Central and Changi Airport Cargo Complex, skipping Pasir Ris)



    FFW 97e (the first FFW in Singapore, commenced on 3 January 2005; between Jurong East and Fullerton Road)



    FFW 174e (commenced on 28 March 2005; between Boon Lay and North Bridge Road outside City Hall MRT)



    FFW 196e (commenced on 12 December 2005; between Marine Parade and Shenton Way)
