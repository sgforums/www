    Yes... in fact, SBS1688K started out as a BN bus upon its first appearance in Singapore. It was deployed on Service 36. If you guys could remember, there was a FCUK advertisement on the bus, which aroused many discussion because of the brand name.

    Also, SBS2888T was started out as a HG bus. It was deployed on Service 7.

    Due to the fact that bus company would rather concentrate on one specific model for ease of maintenance, SBST exchanged the houses for these 2 buses.

    History of the 2 buses are as follow:

    SBS1688K: 36 --> 518
    85 --> 82 --> 84 --> 163
    SBS2888T: 7 --> 85
