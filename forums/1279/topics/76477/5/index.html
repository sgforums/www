    Take the picture at highest resolution without digital zoom.

    Crop out the center of the bus (probably at least 1024x768 or larger)

    Resize it to something manageable like 800x600.

    Run unsharp mask.

    Should be sufficiently sharp for a low-end camera.

    Unsharp mask is not really very good for making the photo look better, IMHO. I avoid using any sharpness utility as far as possible as it may make the photo look rather artificial.

    You can use digital zoom, but i think that your camera does not have an in-built quality control that limits the extend of the zoom. For most cameras, a 3X digital zoom would only yield photos that are good at 680x480 resolution. Anything above the size would make the photo look yucky at first glance.
