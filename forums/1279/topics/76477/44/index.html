    Originally posted by iveco:
    Wasn't 160 full AMDEP until WLDEP became operational? Same goes for 167, 169 & 856.
    Service 160, 167, 169, 855 and 856 was partially controlled by AMDEP and KJDEP. When WLDEP became operational, services 160, 169 and 855 became AMDEP/WLDEP control. Service 167 was KJDEP/WLDEP control. Service 856 became full WLDEP control.

    When Sengkang was taken over by SBS in 1999, there were a lot of depot reshuffling (services 167 became full WLDEP together with 178, 187, 700, 960 and WL intratown services whereas services 855, 980 and YI intratown services became full AMK).

    Somewhere in 2001 the depot deployment was reshuffled between 167, 851, 852, 859 and 980 and AMDEP/WLDEP. From then service 980 became partial controlled by AMDEP and WLDEP again together with serice 167.

    In 2004, I also remembered that service 980 was fully under AMDEP for a short period of time when service 950 was just introduced. It then became partial control by AMDEP/WLDEP again (AMK control 9 whereas WL control 4).
