    For me, usually I felt that Singapore buses are slow but easy, and that they had a special quality of low ticket price, allowing every Singaporean to explore fully their homeland.

    Also, I felt that on board the bus, many different things can happen. One side, may seen people taking photos of en route sceneries. Another side, may seen people taking bunches and bunches of service guides. Even better, they might be chit-chatting loudly onboard.

    Finally, I believed that en route scenery in Singapore are far nicer than what it was in Hong Kong (my hometown) without much jams, worth taking photos.
