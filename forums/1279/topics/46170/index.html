    This is the full story:-

    In May 1997, SBST introduced Express 503, a peak-hour one-directional service which plys from Tampines Interchange to Marina Centre via Bedok, Marine Parade, ECP and Robinson Rd. Later in September 1999, Express 503 was renumbered Express 505 and amended to became a whole-day service, which plys from Pasir Ris Interchange and loops along Jln Bukit Merah (Bukit Merah Town Centre), Alexandra Rd (Queensway Shopping Centre, PSA Building) and Telok Blangah Rd (World Trade Centre) via Tampines, PIE and CTE. One year later (September 2000), Express 505 was renumbered Express 518 and plys from Pasir Ris Interchange to loop at Orchard Rd and Marina Centre.
