    Yes, it is a game. For the EDS, press 5 to display the selector, then press 4 to switch it on, then 1 or 2 to select what is to be displayed. 3 is to make it disappear.

    Basic route 1 will show like this:

    1 OSIEDLE PIASKI
    or
    1 OSIEDLE POGODNA

    on the EDS on the bus.

    For the Ikarus 260 (I presume you have no other bus), it uses plywood destos, not EDS. So it shall look different.
