    Yes alot of SBST services start from mid-way at least. Even though SBST153 departs BTM @0600 hrs, there's a Svc 153 bus at Braddell Rd towards HGN at 0610 hrs. I suppose this 153 start from Dunearn Rd, Whitley Rd or even Jln Toa Payoh.

    SBST56 starts from Toa Payoh MRT towards Marina Centre @0530 hrs on weekdays.

    In general, long haul routes will have their first bus starting from midway while some others start from the interchange.

    For more info, check out the SBST e-guide on a particular bus stop to check its first and last bus timings.
