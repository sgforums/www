    My suggestions to the affected services for closure of Lorong 1 Geylang and Sims Place terminals:

    Lor 1 Geylang terminal:

    Service 11 will be amended Marina Centre terminal - Boon Keng station (loop) via Raffles Ave, Nicoll Highway, thereafter follow present route to Lor 1 Geylang, continue via Boon Keng Rd, Serangoon Rd, Whampoa West, Bendemeer Rd and return. The amended route will provide a link between Stadium area and Marina Centre, and stadium area to EWL (Kallang) and NEL (Boon Keng) stations.

    Service 853 will terminates at Upper East Coast terminal daily.

    Service 961 will be amended Woodlands Regional interchange - Marina Centre terminal. The route will be the one on Sundays and Public Holidays from Woodlands to Marina Centre, and thereafter terminating at the terminal. In order to maintain the link between Beach Rd and Chinatown, TransitLink will amend service 82 to terminate at New Bridge Rd terminal, by following service 961 route from Beach Rd to New Bridge Rd.

    Service 980 will be amended Admiralty Rd West terminal - Marina Centre terminal. The route between Admiralty Rd West and Middle Rd will remains unchanged, thereafter via Beach Rd and Raffles Boulevard. Affected passengers between Beach Rd and Lor 1 Geylang terminal can transfer to service 100.

    Service 56 will be amended to loop in Marina Centre, via Temasek Boulevard, Temasek Ave and Raffles Ave, it will no longer terminates at Marina Centre terminal. This is to provide enough parking lots for the additional 3 services there.

    Sims Place terminal:

    Service 64 will be amended Eunos interchange - Bukit Merah interchange, via Changi Rd, Geylang Rd, Paya Lebar Rd, Geylang East Central, thereafter continue with present route to Alexandra Rd, then continue via Jln Bukit Merah and Bukit Merah Central, the service will no longer via Sims Place, Sims Dr, Stirling Rd, Mei Chin Rd and Mei Ling St. Service 125 will be converted to operate daily. Service 274 will be extended to serve Stirling Rd, Mei Chin Rd and Mei Ling St, via Alexandra Rd, Commonwealth Ave, Stirling Rd, Mei Chin Rd, Mei Ling St, Mei Chin Rd, Queensway and return.
