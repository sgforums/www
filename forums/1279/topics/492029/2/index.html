    To answer Cetra question about shift patterns

    Am-Pm(SMRT) and A/P(SBST) earliest start shift is around 0455-0625 the latest for Am and between 1300-1635 for Pm shift work.OT depend on shift pattern,Some start as early as 0455 and ends at 1530 and you calculate how many Ot hours while some start at 0600 but end at 1350 which is less than 8 hours.Pm start as early as 1300 or start late at 1635 ends at 2250 the earliest or up to 0200 the latest.

    Split Shift or S1 and S for SMRT and SBST respectively:Examples 0545-0945 break then start at 1645-2025 or start at 0645-1035 break then start at 1705-2055..Break in between is from 4-6 hours usually.

    Equilavent or S2 for SMRT and E for SBST,Same as above but with less break time in between.Examples 0550-0950 then start at 1400-1935 or 0645-1115 break then 1525-1925.

    Three Quarter:TQ and T for SMRT and SBST respectively..Most found during holidays and weekends though there is a few implemented during weekdays.Start from 0715/0945 and ends at 1750/2025..Usually 9-14 hours of work,Lots of OTs and the break times also quite good..Additional got tea break times also.

    Also got crossover schedules where you will drive service 67 from 0535-0735 then change to svc 969 from 0750-0945 before changing back to svc 67.For S shift after they crossover to other svc they will off service to depot.

    Also got off schedule shift OS where standby BCs which didnt have any job call,Starters will instruct them to drive bus to supplement certain service depending on demands.Usually will drive a few hours only before off service back to depot.
