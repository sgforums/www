    I think this topic is a little bit side-tracked from svc 920.

    However, answering to your query about my edited post on PBS 546 on sgwiki:

    Crossover from YIDEP 853
    Reduction from 2 trips to 1, withdraw supplementary from KJDEP 985

    Originally, when PBS 546 was first introduced, there were 2 trips departing Blk 73 Geylang Bahru @ 0825 (supplemented by KJ's 985S101) & 0835 (by YIDEP's 853S203).

    Due to "changing demands" as quoted by SMRT, the 0835 trip will be withdrawn effective from 20 Oct 08, leaving the one and only trip departing 0825 which is served by YIDEP's 853S203.

    Btw, the official terminology for "crossovers" is supplementary trips, that is why I wrote supplementary instead of "crossover" or "x-over".

    Hope this clarifies.
