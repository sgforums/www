    It is "Mighty Minds Singapore BUS GUIDE & BUS-STOP DIRECTORY NEW 2006 EDITION"

    I purchased my copy of this yesterday night at Books Kinokuniya (Ngee Ann City) at the cost of $3.90.

    Take note of the significant changes:

    1) Service 2 had its first bus timings from City changed (earlier by 1 mins)

    2) 97e, 174e are included.

    3) Svc 125A is removed.

    4) Mistakes found in the service 225 guide: all the first and last bus timings are printed wrongly.

    5) Service 170# (the blue plate svc from Kranji MRT<>JB Kota Raya 2) included but as (Queen Street<>JB Kota Raya 2)

    6) Extended version of 925C included

    7) BP 540 is removed. (Is it withdrawn and if yes then when it was withdrawn for what reasons?)

    TNC 714 disappeared from the 2006 version of the guide. I suspected that it was merged with 715 given the 715 had "added stops" including Jurong East interchange. (When and why this occur?)
