    Originally posted by kc_leung:
    Before that, bus information was printed only in street directory, am i correct?
    No. There were bus guides which dated back to as early as the 60s or 70s. These bus guides, as the name stated, only contained info on bus services.

    When Transitlink was set up, info on train services was added into bus guides and were re-named Transitlink guides from 1992.
