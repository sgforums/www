    Ok, I remembered.

    My first ride was on a bendy bus Service 960. My mother disliked to sit at the back part during that time as she would feel giddy. So, as i was a small kid, I must obey her and seat in front.

    My first ride on a SUPERBUS was on 190. Every weekend, I would go out with my 2 aunts and my mother during that time. My aunt wasn't married back then.I was the only kid in my maternal side of the family. They all like me soooooo much Now, I have 12 cousins. We would always seat at the front of 190 Superbus. As for my first ride on a DD, I could not remember, but I think it was on 190 before it was extended to terminate at CCK.
