    Well, since this topic have been discussed in facebook, I shall not be selfish, and share this with everyone here, for the benefit of the others who don't know... As I was taking SBS9841G on 133 today, I was very sian during the trip, I looked around, and realised that VSOs are actually similar to the Citaros, 100% Full Low Floor. Citaro standard: Low floored from entrance to exit, 1 step up to access seats at the side, but still having a stepless aisle, just that aissle is a little 'raised' some where. 1 step up to the last row of seats. VSO: shares the same features as Citaro, Low floored from entrance to exit, 1 step up to access seats at side, also still having stepless aisle and raised some where, 1 step up to the last row of seats. In this 2 comparisons, SBS9888Y would be S.E.A F1RST 100% Full Low Floored + Double Decker Bus. Well some said that VSO is incompatible to Citaro for not being Euro V and WAB. Thats not a problem, KMB sent its Volgren B9 back to Volvo, and brought it back with Euro IV engine. SBST could do the same, replace with Euro V, and then remove front granny seats for PIW area. Now, isnt it compatible with Citaro, or maybe I should say Citaro is not match for the new VSO? Its just my personal opinion, and my wonderful ideas, might be stupid, but if the above is possible to be done so, Im sure all bros would agree with me right?
