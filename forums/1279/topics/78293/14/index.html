    191 Bartley Rd is Millenia Institute (Outram Insititute Campus).

    Exit Braddell MRT Station by Toa Payoh Lor 1 Exit. Walk past Blk 109, Blk 110, 110A (MSCP) & 206. Cross Toa Payoh North. Use the bridge over the canal to get to Industrial Blk 1004 (near Blk 1008/Food Ctr). Find an exit out to Braddell Rd from the fence and get across Braddell Rd. by crossing the road at the junction with Bishan Rd under the flyover. You will reach a bus stop with Service 93. Make sure the bus is headed towards EUNOS, then take this bus for 12 stops and you will reach 191 Bartley Rd.
