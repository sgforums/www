    From Woodlands, take SMRT Buses service 966 to Jalan Toa Payoh and alight. Transfer to SBS Transit services 56, 105 (these 2 services require crossing road) or SBS Transit services 143 or 145 to Toa Payoh Interchange.

    The bus stop to alight is the stop after the Police Academy bus stop along PIE.
