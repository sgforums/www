    AVMS: Driver can communicate to OCC through the AVMS-DDU. AVMS DDU also shows how early or late is the bus. The information displayed on the screen does not take up much of the screen as the DDU is meant for something else, which may remain as an unknown until any press release or observations have been made.

    iBos: Driver can communicate to OCC just by pressing a button on the IDFC. The driver can check whether he or she is late through the IDFC (though I haven't seen this yet).

    Both systems are based on GPS and allow the OCC to know where the bus is.
