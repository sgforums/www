    You can get a list of SMRT buses here and SBST buses here.

    Just type in their full registiration number (i.e. SBS8033D, TIB425R) in this webpage (https://vrl.lta.gov.sg/lta/vrl/action/pubfunc?ID=EnquireTransferFee) together with a recent date.

    But do take note that you can't look up for deregistered buses.
