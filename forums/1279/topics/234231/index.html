    It depends on where is your exact starting point and ending point.

    During normal traffic, a bus ride from CCK Interchange to Orchard MRT is about 45 mins. Taking MRT from Choa Chu Kang to Orchard (via Jurong East / City Hall) takes 45 minutes as well.

    However, if you do not have direct bus 190 (i.e. need transfer to board 190), and are going to places nearer to Somerset MRT or Dhoby Ghaut MRT, then it would be faster to take a feeder to transfer to MRT (via Jurong East/City Hall).
