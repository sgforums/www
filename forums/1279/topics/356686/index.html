Panasonic celebrates Earth Day with free bus rides !!

Next
  • Hnk1

    This Earth Day (22 April 2009), go green, ditch your cars and hitch a ride on Panasonic’s ‘eco ideas’ decorated buses on routes 16, 70 and 100 for FREE, ALL DAY!!

     

    This will kick off the drive to collect ‘eco ideas’ on what you can do to commit to adopting a greener lifestyle. The best ‘eco ideas’ stand a chance to win two grandstand tickets to the Singapore F1 Night Race and other

     

    Panasonic is also offering participants with the best pledge a chance to attractive prizes such as Panasonic Lumix digital cameras.

     

    To submit ‘eco ideas’ or to find out about the arrival and departure times of the ‘eco ideas’ buses at the interchanges, please visit www.panasonic.com.sg/ecoideas
