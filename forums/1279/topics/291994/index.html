    Its along balestier rd. You have to walk in from there. If you are taking 130 from AMK, 139 145 from TPY, 21 131 from Novena MRT alight at the 2nd stop along Balestier Rd, cross the road, locate the Esso petrol kiosk and walk along the side road next to the kiosk till you reach MHA.

    If you are taking 130 145 from Bugis, alight after you see the SPC petrol kisok across the road, then locate the Esso petrol kiosk from there.

    Other services that pass by there are 21 131 186.
