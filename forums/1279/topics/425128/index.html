On 2004, they should have transferred the garage from HGDEP to BRBP since it is from Eunos route group; which have continuity of using Mercedes-Benz O405 and Leyland Olympian 3-Axles from BRBP, Volvo Olympian 3-Axles and JB-regged Volvo B10M Mark III from BBDEP. Then in 2008, it should have replaced by Scania K230UB and can have Volvo B9TL CDGE on fleet.

    67 has replaced Scania N113CRB with Volvo B10M Mark IV (DM) from BNDEP, while Volvo B10M Mark IV (Strider) from BBDEP replaces Volvo B10M Mark III. Mercedes-Benz O405 was replaced by Volvo B10M Mark III.

    If 67 is after 1999 within SBS Transit route group, then by 2008 it will be replaced fully by Scania K230UB as the Volvo B10M Mark IV (Strider) moves all the way to replace N113CRBs on BNDEP services.
