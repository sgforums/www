    Hey guys, 

    If you guys have ever tried the game Cities In Motion (a transport game), you've most likely encountered problems with bus brunching. 

    112/113's situation at KVN MRT is unique, because it's route is split into 2 segments. Take 265 or any townlink services as examples. The reason why 238/269 has a steady stream of buses picking up pax at the int because it's the first stop. 

    For 113, the situation is different because the stop you're taking buses from is located at the middle of the route. So during the first leg of the journey, the buses may brunch up because of traffic conditons/ or PIWs holding up travelling time. Also note that peak hours means more traffic, whether human or vehicles. 

    Take SBS's explaintion for an example:

    http://www.sbstransit.com.sg/doyouknow/facts_bus.aspx#1
