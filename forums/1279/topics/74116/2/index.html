    No.

    Service 148 was re-introduced in June 1992 (on the same day when service 196 was shortened to Buona Vista) to operate between Clementi and Lor 1 Geylang when service 147 (Jurong East - Potong Pasir) skipped Lavender St and Victoria St/North Bridge Rd to ply Serangoon Rd/Jln Besar. Service 148 was withdrawn in Nov 1992 due to low demand and it happened to be the same day service 196 was extended back to Clementi.
