    Originally posted by BusAnalayzer:

    178 has poor loading throughout from Boon Lay to Mandai Estate. Main loading of sv 178 comes between Mandai Estate and Woodlands Interchange.

    The issue is that the stretch of Corporation Rd (between Corporation Dr and International Rd) is currently only served by 178. Cutting it backwards from Boon Lay Int is undesirable at this point.

    A solution to support the cutback of 178 would be to amend an existing bus service from Corporation Dr (i.e. 30, 49, 98, 98M, 240 or 246) to Corporation Rd, but let's analyze their importance one by one:

    - 30: Currently the fastest route between Boon Lay Int and the residential blocks along Corporation Dr. With such high demand, diverting it to Corporation Rd is undesirable.
    - 49: A possible bus service to divert to Corporation Rd with the availability of 98, 98M, 240 and 246, but would that have defeated its purpose of having to complement the above services? Maybe.
    - 98/98M: Currently the most convenient and complete route between Lakeside MRT and the residential blocks along Corporation Dr (49 only covers part of Corporation Dr). With its importance in connection, diverting it to Corporation Rd is undesirable as well.
    - 240: Plays a supplementary role between Lakeside MRT and the residential blocks along Corporation Dr and is currently the only service plying Kang Ching Rd. It is undoubtedly an important route along Corporation Dr as well, given that a short working trip has to be operated every weekday morning to complement its demand.
    - 246: Plays a supplementary role between Lakeside MRT and the residential blocks along Corporation Dr and is the fastest route between Lakeside MRT and Tah Ching Rd. It may not be as important as the other services above in Corporation Dr, but by diverting it to Corporation Rd will mean skipping Lakeside MRT, which is a critical transfer point for the residents of Taman Jurong and Boon Lay Gardens.

    Another solution will be to introduce a new service to cover the secluded stretch of Corporation Rd and connect it to Boon Lay, Jurong East and Bukit Batok. However, given that demand along this stretch isn't really high for 178 already, will operating a new service along that stretch even be desirable?
