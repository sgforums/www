    Not sure if the Adam Rd - PIE junction would be modified to allow right turns (from eastbound PIE to southbound Adam Rd) in the future, but if it were, it would allow for some good rationalisation:

    • - 700: Bt Panjang -- Shenton Way   (via Adam Rd & Botanic Gardens, skipping Stevens Rd)
    • - 171: Yishun -- Bt Panjang   (Cut in entirety afterwards; replaced by 170 and 700)
    • - 67: CCK -- Lor 1 Geylang   (Using current 961 route after TKK MRT station)
    • - 1: Tampines -- either St. Michaels / Toa Payoh / loop near Little India   (via current 67 route)
    • - 961: Woodlands -- Bt Batok  (Via 178's route)
    • - 178: Bt Batok -- Boon Lay  (Other half of 178; subject to withdrawal / modification / merge with 258 depending on demand observed)

    Sectors saved:

    • - 67 between TKK MRT and Little India   (covered by 48, 66, 170, modified 700). Lost link between CCK and Little India though.
    • - 961 between Woodlands Int and Kranji MRT   (covered by 925, 960; mitigated by new route)
    • - 171 between Bt Panjang and Marina Ctr   (covered by 170, modified 700)
    • - 700 along Stevens Rd   (covered by 190, 960, 972)
