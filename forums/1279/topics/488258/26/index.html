    So it seems that the BPJ service amendments would not be announced by the end of the year ... but rather only until the opening of the ITH sometime around 2017 Q2.

    =====================================

    Expected route changes (in descending order of probability):

    - (1) Opening of BPJ ITH and rerouting of services 75, 176, 184, 920, 922, 970, 972, 975 and 979/M and some among 177, 180, 971E and 983 (unconfirmed) to terminate there. There is estimated space for twelve services altogether. Unknown if the bus park would still be operational.

    - (2) Introduction of new service (973?) from BPJ ITH to pass Petir Rd / Dairy Farm Rd & loop at Hume Ave (or even beyond)

    - (3) Truncation of service 171, at either BPJ (of which it may end up in the ITH), Jln Anak Bukit (of which there could be a likely chance of a future merge with 41), or reroute to some other location (such as via 176's route to BB, or even a merge with 308 to CCK, etc.)

    - (4) (Along with 5 & 6) Withdrawal of service 700

    - (5) (Along with 4 & 6) Extension of service 972 to Shenton Way terminal, and introduction of route variant 972A to loop at Suntec City. Also renumbering of 971E to 972E.

    - (6) (Along with 4 & 5) Modification of either services 190 or 972 (or one each) along two sectors: Petir Rd & Dairy Farm Rd (instead of BPJ Rd Blk 270), and Dunearn / Bt Timah Rd & Scotts Rd (instead of Stevens Rd) to cover the lost sectors of truncated 171 and withdrawn 700. (Mostly only RGS students would be affected, but RGS would be moving to Bishan in 2019.)

    - (7) Modification or truncation of any of the routes 178, 960 and 961 along Woodlands Rd.

    - (8) Introduction of new semi-express service from BPJ ITH (via BKE & either SLE / PIE) to central and/or northeast area towns.
