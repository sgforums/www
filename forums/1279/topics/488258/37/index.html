    Two new public bus services to complement DTL3 opening

         Commuters travelling along Downtown Line 3 (DTL3) can look forward to greater convenience and enhanced public transport connectivity with the introduction of two new bus services along the corridor. Services 137 and 298 are the 78th and 79th bus services introduced under the Bus Service Enhancement Programme (BSEP)[1], and will start operation from Saturday, 21 October 2017 in tandem with the opening of DTL3.

    2     At the same time, there will be a series of amendments to other bus routes, to further improve accessibility of the new DTL3 stations.

    DTL3 rationalisation, phase 1

    (From Saturday, 21 October 2017)

    ======

    New services:

    • - Service 137: Between Sims Place & Upper East Coast via Aljunied, Paya Lebar, Ubi Ave 1/3, Kaki Bukit Ave 2/3/6, Bedok North MRT, Bedok North St 3, Ave 2, Bedok MRT, Bedok South Rd, Ave 1, UEC Rd.
    • - Serving DT26/CC10 MacPherson, DT27 Ubi, DT29 Bedok North, EW9 Aljunied, EW8/CC9 Paya Lebar, EW5 Bedok.

    • - Service 298: Between Tampines Concourse & Tampines St 86 (Loop) via Ave 4, St 82, Ave 3/8/5/10. Serving DT32/EW2 Tampines.

    DT35 Expo:

    • - Service 20 amended to serve Somapah Rd & Expo MRT, skipping one pair of stops on Changi South Ave 1
    • - Service 118 amended to serve Expo MRT, skipping Changi South Ave 2

    DT33 Tampines East:

    • - Services 18 extended to serve Tampines St 34/33/32 for improved MRT connections
    • - Service 293 east extended to loop at Tampines East MRT, doing Tampines Ave 9 & St 45 in both directions, skipping Tampines St 43
    • - Services 3, 8, 9, 12, 17, 34, 38, 39, 59, 518 stopping at a new bus stop along Tampines Ave 2

    DT29 Bedok North:

    • - Services 23, 60, 87, 518 stopping at a new bus stop along Bedok North Rd
