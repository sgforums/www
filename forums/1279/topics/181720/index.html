    Thanks to an article in the Today newspaper a few days ago. The magical numbers are 8 and 1.5!

    Ok! Based on the above examples.
    For 26E, the actual overtime is 3h29 (11h29 - 8h) Multiply this by 1.5 and it's rounded off to 5h14. Add this back to the basic time, and you get 13h14!

    For 17S, the actual overtime is 1h48 (9h48 - 8h). Multiply by 1.5 and you get 2h42. Add this back to 8h and you get 10h42!
