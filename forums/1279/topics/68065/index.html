Hougang South Int: 322 & 327 = 112 and 321 & 326 = 113

  • ^tamago^
    29 Jan 04, 22:03
    these were spotted at the Hougang South Interchange's control office, pasted right on the window.. the confirmed facts are:

    Svcs 322 and 327 will merge into Svc 112
    Svcs 321 and 326 will merge into Svc 113
    All trunk svcs (51, 132 and 151) will terminate at Hougang Central Int

    Route familiarisation is Svc 112 is on 3rd Feb '04 and Svc 113 on 4th Feb '04.

    Route familiarisation for trunk svcs drivers will be on 3rd Feb '04 (Svc 51), 4th Feb '04 (Svc 132) and 5th Feb '04 (Svc 151).

    Route details of the new svcs and amended routes are not known at this point of time.

    The fate of Svc 328 is unknown, but it is most likely that it will be withdrawn since Svc 328 duplicates parts of Svcs 322 and 325.

    Looks like the amendments are not as far-fetched as we had thought.

    The notice issuance date stated on the notices is 28th Jan '04.
