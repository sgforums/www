    Heres a list of bus services from Singapore to JB :

    The routes are as follows :
    - Svc 170 (Queen St Terminal - Lakin / Kranji MRT - Kotaraya 2 Terminal)
    - Svc 160 (Jurong East Interchange - Kotaraya 2 Terminal)
    - Svc 950 (Woodlands Interchange - Kotaraya 2 Terminal)
    - Svc CW1 (Kranji MRT - Lakin)
    - Svc CW2 (Queen St Terminal - Lakin)
    - Svc CW3 (Jurong East Interchange - Bandaraya)
    - SJE (Queen St Terminal - Lakin)

    Theres counter selling tickets and u can pay by cash. If u decide to take CW2, u can pay by Ezlink. Remember to keep ur ticket throughout the journey as u need them to reboard at the customs.Tickets are 1 way.

    If u are coming back, im afraid its abit troublsome since u only intend to shop near the custom area. This is because SJE or CWs do not sell tickets at the customs. The only way u can come back is by 950 / 170 or 160 as u can simply tap ur card when boarding. If u want to take CW2 or SJE back to Queen St, u must take a cab to Lakin Terminal and back. U can try 170 back to Queen St. But make sure u board the Red Plate 170 from SIngapore customs and not the blue plate as they ends at Kranji MRT.

    If u decided to take SBST buses, u can board any of the 160s or 170s when u clear the malaysia customs. its the same.
