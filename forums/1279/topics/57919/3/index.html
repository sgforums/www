    In JB, fares are distance related. In KL, the area is divided into fare zones.

    Be prepared to wait a little longer for a bus. Buses have a habit of waiting at bus stops for passengers.

    Unlike in Singapore, farecards for buses cannot be used on trains. And different bus companies have different farecards.

    There is no proper bus/train guide. Train info can be obtained at stations but there's hardly anything on bus route info.

    Using a farecard for a ride on an Intrakota service (KL) is the same as in Singapore. Good news is that the farecard is transferrable, which means you can use the same card to pay for your friend.

    Only conductors provide change.

    Outside of town, you can flag a bus anywhere.
