    I'd like to find out what people missed about the good old days; be it interesting, pleasant or unpleasant memories...
    I missed the old buses. They have their own unique characteristics which are missing in the newer buses. Some of my favourites:

    Albion Viking EVK41L - Very loud engine. Crash gearbox which makes a loud crunch sound while changing gears. And when engaging 1st gear, there's a nice "DUNK!" sound.

    Leyland Victory - Very fast. Only bus with semi-auto gears. Love to see the driver flick through 'em. Most drivers 'flat-change' ie. keeping the gas pedal pressed while shifting gears, resulting in jerky movements and rapid engine / gearbox wear.

    Merc O306 - Doors open and shut with a shocking slam. Nice high seats over the wheel arches.

    Merc OF1413 - The DM bodied ones were re-fitted with auto gears. The torque converter makes a very loud howl.

    Merc OF1417 - Nice, loud exhaust brakes.

    Volvo B57 - ditto

    Leyland Atlantean - They look clumsy but I like them. At speed, there's a ghostly whistle.

    Merc O305 - Ah, the cream of the crop. Very powerful, very fast. Loud hub-reduction axle. Nice retarders too.

    Originally posted by AntiDennisLance:
    I think the merc OF1417 is also a semi-auto bus. The drivers have to change gears but there were no clutch.
    The OF1417 was fully automatic. It had a lever type gear selector with a round black knob; looks pretty much like a manual gearshaft. And the fact that it was located beside the engine cover made it look more like a manual shaft.

    In drive mode, there was a notch to engage a lower gear. Some drivers like to play with the gear selector as I recalled from my experience.

    I was going home in an NZMB OF1417 on sv. 99 from Clementi. I was sitting on the frontmost seat, observing the driver. After Clementi Ave 6, there were no traffic lights till Toh Guan Rd and the driver went nuts. As soon as the final gear was engaged he shifted to a lower gear. The engine screamed before easing back to the highest gear. Not satisfied, he did it again. And again. And again. The whole bus shook as it sped along the road. And the engine was making queer rattling noises too.

    He drove like that for practically the whole journey and I reached my destination in a record time of 30 mins!
