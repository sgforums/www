    Hi.. im new in SGforums... just wanna share with u guys this experience i had with Airebus (shuttle svc to KL, Mid Valley).

    Apparently i saw this coach at DFS Galleria one day and decided to check it out. So i called up the counter at Far East Plaza and the counter staff answered my call... she sounded rather impatient and quite rude. Havent even asked my first question she already sounded so impatient! Then when i went down to buy their coach tickets, the counter staffs there didnt even smile once. The one attendin' to me, looks quite old with acne on the face, showed me a black face throughout. Fine i wasnt quite bothered about it...

    Then on the day of departure, the coach actually didnt appear at all! Though i waited for like 1 hour, the coach still didnt arrive. I tried calling the counter a thousand times, no one picked up the phone. Getting more and more worried, i went up to the counter at Far East Plaza (dragging all my luggage). Its only when i reached the counter that moment THEN they informed me the coach broke down and must be sent for servicing, and that means i would have to take another coach up to KL at another timing. Unfortunately they didnt have any timing suitable and i ended up having to purchase tickets from another coach company. I even had to wait a month for my refund!!!!

    How irresponsible can this company be?? Im really disappointed in their services. Somemore this is only my first time with Airebus! I have also heard about many many bad experiences from other people. Wish to know more about your experience with Airebus (if u have any)..............
