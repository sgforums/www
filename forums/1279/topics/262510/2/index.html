    Originally posted by service_238:
    any services or specific points to recommend based on experience?
    I think for down trips, it's best to have flexible timings. For 179 and 242 especially. The usual timetabling is very tight for the first half of the journey, but rather slack in the second half.

    In "theory", the down trips are deployed in between big gaps. I.e. 179 buses leave BNL @ 4min frequency, but arrive back @ 2min.

    But actually, in reality, since the first half of the route is very tight, the buses departing from BNL would be late along the route.

    But the down trips would be departing on time, so almost always, the down trip buses will be ahead of their order and end up rather empty, while those which departed from BNL would be full to the door!

    So I think it's best to have flexible timings, so the order of the buses still remain the same. Maybe they can communicate with OCC to ask them about the exact position of the buses which are supposed to be in front or behind them.

    But I think this flexible timing thing will never happen though...
