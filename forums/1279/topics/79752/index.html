    Originally posted by deBRA:
    We still tap our EZ LINK card and pay the same amount, NOT A QUALIFYING TRANSFER ? I am suprised !!Do I have to skip 187and wait 190 to have a QUALIFYING TRANSFER ?? What's the logic behind ?
    Taking 187 after taking 187 is considered starting a new journey.

    EG. You take 187 to work and then 187 back home. This is considered two journeys. That's why 187 -> 187 is considered 2 journeys.

    Taking 190 and transfering to 187 is a single journey.

    And if you had not realised earlier, this system has been in placed since the 1980s when the farecard system was 1st implemented and no one made noise.
