    Yes...service 160 was partially operated by KJDEP even though the service ended at Admiralty Rd West Terminal. That explains why WA DAFs were deployed on service 160 in the past. Service 160 was eventually taken over by WLDEP in late 1998 when WLDEP came into control. AMDEP has operated on service 160/980 (regardless of partial or full control) throughout.

    Service 167 was also partially operated by KJDEP too even though the service ended at Sembawang Rd End, eventually came under full WLDEP and subsequently AMDEP/WLDEP controlled.

    Service 852 was once a full KJDEP controlled, after which it became full AMDEP control. That is the reason why WA DAF fleets on service 852 became Dennis Lance fleets.
