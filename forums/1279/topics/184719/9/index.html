    1 Aug 06, 08:33
    Ill timing for Service 106, TIB1158E
    I do not consider this as a complain, but just a sharing of some abnormal passenger flow on the morning of 1st August 2006.
    When I reached the bus stop in front of Yusoff Ishak Sec Sch, I missed a service 106 bus (TIB1177A). The time was 0723hrs.
    Considering the peak hours timing, the next bus to arrive at the stop should be at 0728hrs (and according to my records as well). However, the bus did not arrive until 0734hrs, by then the bus was packed 101 percent (the front stairwell was packed). I managed to board, so as the other commuters at that stop. The next stop at Burgundy Hill, the BC did not turn into the bus bay as the bus was already 102 percent full.
    More commuters alighted at the Ulu Pandan Refuse Incineration Plant, and the bus was then 98 percent full.
    The bus left Clementi MRT bus stop with 100 percent capacity, and at the stop in front of Block 365, more commuters tried to squeeze on board (this stop have more commuters than any other previous stops). Some alighted, and the bus left the stop at 102 percent capacity again.
    The next stop was opposite the Schol of Info-comm Technology, and a fellow schoolmate who wanted to alight was not able to do so as the doors closed too soon. I tried to press the bell again for the bus to stop, but the bell would not ring and the bus accelerated out of the stop. I then commented to fellow commuters that if the bus companies do not increase the frequency and improve the service standards, they do not stand a reason to increase the fares, and many of them agreed with me. Some even agreed to complain.
    This was the worse morning ride I have on Service 106 since April, and I do not hope to see this happening to any other people again. Bus companies (SBS Transit and SMRT alike) should listen to commuters' comments and do appropriate adjustments to their services.
