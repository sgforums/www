    Award Of Contract For Upgrading Of Junction
    At Commonwealth Avenue West/Commonwealth Avenue/North Buona Vista Road

    The Land Transport Authority (LTA) is pleased to announce the award of the contract for the upgrading of the junction at Commonwealth Avenue West/Commonwealth Avenue/North Buona Vista Road, to Feng Ming Construction Pte Ltd.

    The contract, worth S$1.2 million, involves the widening of Commonwealth Avenue West, between Ghim Moh Road and North Buona Vista Road, by an additional lane in each direction.

    The project serves to cater to the increased traffic volume along Commonwealth Avenue West and North Buona Vista Road, with the development of One-North nearby.

    The project, when completed in the second quarter of 2007, will help to alleviate traffic congestion along Commonwealth Avenue West and North Buona Vista Road.
    Expect Traffic jams during peak hrs.
