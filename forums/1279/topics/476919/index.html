    Actually can be called "Newcomers' guide to Singapore Buses" no need the year.

    Forms that's will normally be used in this forum:

    SBS Transit - SBST

    SMRT - SMRT

    TQ - Third Quarter 

    S - Split

    Eg. 13AM - 15PM (Note: Not Time!!!) Number - how many buses will be operating during AM peak and PM peak. so in this case, there will be 13 Buses operating at AM peak, and 15 buses operating at PM peak. Total - 15 buses not 28 buses. Readin: 2 buses are brought into the 13 buses to form 15 buses

    Cameo: Bus that is not on the service that it is deployed to. Eg. A service 66 bus on 30.

    Desto: Destination plate

    Rego: Bus registration number

    EDS: Electronic Destination Signage

    Perm: Permanent/Deployed service

    SP: Spare

    AMDEP: Ang Mo Kio Depot 

    ARBP: Ayer Rajah Bus Park

    BBDEP: Bukit Batok Depot

    BNDEP: Bedok North Depot

    BRBP: Braddell Bus Park

    CGBP: Changi Bus Park

    HGDEP: Hougang Depot

    SLBP: Soon Lee Bus Park (This is a major depot)
