    i have auite a number of paper buses. Take note that paper is quite soft.
    my advice to you is if you wanna make paper bus,
    you print it out and paste it on a very thin cardboard(the cardboard should be thiner that the back of a drawing block)
    Do use double sided tape instead of glue cos liquid glue will cause the paper to expand and glue stick does not provide good adhesive.

    My trick:
    paste the double sided tape along the edges that you wanna cut first and then cut out you paper bus and paste it on the cardboard.
