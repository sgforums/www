    CCK Feeders & Intra-towns

    300 --> 02-03 mins 

    Like 806, sv 300 has one of the best frequencies. Frequency was reduced to 04 mins after launch of 301/983, but suddenly 05 more buses were pumped into 300, to bring the frequency to 02-03 mins. In addition, has a decent number of bendies.

    301 --> 07-08 mins

    Still operating with 05 buses. Surprisingly, didn't get any fleet add in spite of Keat Hong Close BTOs opening up. Instead, sv 300 is the one that keeps getting more buses

    302 --> 02-03 mins

    Another service that operates at 02-03 mins frequency and still has a high number of bendies. Though at one time was a full fleet bendy. Bunching out of CCK Interchange itself of 2 bendies is a common sight.

    307 --> 03-04 mins

    IMO, this service has got one of the best frequency improvements. Operating at 08-10 mins at one point in time, not sv 307 operates at 03-04 mins though there has not been any new BTO development along its route. 

    308 --> 07-08 mins

    New service, may get fleet add in future.
