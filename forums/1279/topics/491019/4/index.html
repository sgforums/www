    BNL feeder frequency analysis for 241, 242 and 243G during weekday PM peak ard 5:30pm

     

    241 - average frequency 03-05mins, buses leave clearing the queue heading towards St 63 and 91 quite well, I've once taken a citaro 241 from BNL to Street 91 ard 5pm and the bus did not hit above 40, though the previous bus left 5mins ago, but when I spot it heading towards BNL, it has above 40 pax

    242 - average frequency 05 mins, single deckers can cope the load with the increase of 2 buses under BSEP, though I would prefer 1 DD split add instead of 2 buses, buses usually leave with more than 40 pax so it is quite good loading for an almost full SD fleet route

    243G - average frequency 03-06mins, though I feel that 243G seems to have some issues with SDs being plied on back to back slots as a crowd can be seen waiting for the next bus which can be a SD also, I would suggest cutting 2 SD for 1 DD

    In addition, I support the suggestion to swap 256 and 258 berths so that 256 and 258 can compliment 243G and 243W respectively

    Note: According to the mytransport app, there are some occasions (seem to happen more frequently now, usually on weekends) where 258 arrives at Boon Lay with "yellow" loading, indicating that the loading is above 80 (assuming no SD cameo 258 that day though i've only seen a 258 SD once)
