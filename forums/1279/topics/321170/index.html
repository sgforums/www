    Take MRT from Toa Payoh to Yishun then transfer to Service 169. "Journey should take about 15-20 mins (non peak) and look out on the right hand side. Along the way you will pass a few camp area. On the left you will see Kabit camp, then further down you will see Sembawang Air base on you right, about another 5 min you will see HQ 22SIB on the right again (although you are most likely to miss this as it is not that obvious) Then another 5-10mins, you will see a 6DIV Engineering on the right, that is where you press to alight and after the traffic junction, alight at the bus stop. Cross the road and follow a small road (at the junction) uphill... and you are at Nee Soon Camp.
