    May I ask, a cameo is for one whole day or one-trip only?

    Depends. It could be done on a full day or split.

    So eg for 169 split buses, AM usually would use perm PM would be cameo. eg morning I start with 1081S, afternoon I will start with a different bus (depending on the demand), SMB95L and end with SMB95L. However, buses that have perm Overnight parking might be different.

    cameo Full day means it will do that bus will do that service for one whole day or in short, Till or about 2300-2330.

    So eg, I take SMB97G on 106, I take that bus till the (PM) shift ends, which is about 2330 or close to that timing. Or AM shift means I will switch hands either at BB or SWT.
