    Anyway:
    Differences between VO3x (Batch 1, 2)and LO3x:
    VO3x Batch I: Emergency exit at back of upper deck, side of upper deck and at lower deck lol! TV Mobile on the left side of bus. Standee area on the right as well. Last window of the upper deck closer to the back of the bus than the LO3x.
    Grille at the back is 1 large rectangular row.

    VO3x Batch II: Only emergency exit on the upper deck is at the back. The rest are the same as Batch I

    LO3x: Lower Deck, there is something above the lower seats (Think it's part of the AC unit.) No standee area on the right, and TV Mobile on the right. Last window of upper deck of LO3x further away from the back of bus than VO3x (If you noticed properly.) Emergency exit of upper deck only at the back. Grille at the back seperated into 2 squarish-like ones.

    Mark III vs MK IV (DM)
    MK III: Square side desto, top part of the back portion is flat, purple A/C unit, driver's window is squarish, headlights are round. Most seats are pinkish. 6 pairs of seats on the left side of the bus after the back door.
