    Took service 75 on SMRT's Mercedes Benz Citaro rego "SMB172Y". I noticed that the halt brake is defective and the bus can move off even with the doors open. This Citaro has clocked 73,000km as seen on the odometer.

    First noticed this on another Svc 75 earlier this month, so this is the 2nd time. Cant remember if the particular bus is the same one.

    Whenever the doors are operated, the yellow indicator will light up and a diagnoistic message "Defect Halt Brake" will appear on the green multifunction screen. If the driver drive off with the doors open or halfway open(personally witnessed), the red indicator above will light up in addition and the error message will change to "Door System Failure".
