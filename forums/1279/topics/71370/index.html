    Sengkang means prosperous harbour.
    Hougang means back harbour.
    Bukit Panjang means long range of hills.
    Sungei Buloh means bamboo river.
    Pulau Mesemut Darat and Pulau Mesemut Laut (both are now part of Jurong island) mean land ant island and sea ant island respectively.

    Toa Payoh means big swamp land (same as Paya Lebar)
