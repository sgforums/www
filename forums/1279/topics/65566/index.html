    As we all come to the end of 2003, let's review the services that were admended.

    3: Extended to Punggol Int from October, converted to FAC status
    31: Converted to FAC status
    38: Same
    39: Introduced around Sept between Yishun, Pasir Ris and Tampines, a first SBS sv in Yishun aft 171.
    62: Admended to run between Punggol and Geylang with NEL
    68: Peak hour service between Tampines and Tampines Ind Est
    81: Shortened to Serangoon with NEL
    82: Serangoon to Punggol Road End (Loop) with NEL
    83: Starting from Punggol and looping at Sengkang
    84: Introduced as a peak hr service to complement 83
    85: Withdrawn with NEL, the former 85 was short lived, any problem with the no 85?
    97: Jurong East to Suntec City with NEL
    103: Shortened to Serangoon and Jalan Kayu (Loop) with NEL
    107: Extended to Hougang Central with the shortened of 82
    111: Ghim Moh to Suntec City with NEL
    119: Introduced to complement the loss of 85 to students
    136: Having ADDs
    161, 168: Having ADDs
    174: Having ADDs, Jurong Road trees cleared already?
    JIS: Started having VO 2x
    222, 242, 291: Having Superbuses even it is feeders
    378, 379: Withdrawn, covered by 103 as part of better coverage with NEL
    501, 511, 512: Withdrawn with NEL
    502: Boon Lay to Suntec City with NEL

    106: Bt Batok to Marina Centre by force with NEL
    963: Withdrawn of 521 and extended to WDL
    965: First Tibs service to Sengkang after 1999.
    966: First Tibs service to Marine Parade daily
    185, 985, 989: 185, 989 withdrawn, 985 shortened to Geylang instead of Changi Airport

    603, 607: Loss of CSS services and an end of CSS era as most drivers have been driving Tibs services as I see one driving 61 after 603.
