    Yishun - Sembawang Bus Enhancement

    860

    Objective: Reduce duplication with 807 and provide more intra-town connectivity within Yishun town. Highlighted in red are the amenities along the way to provide better connectivity. 

    Sembawang Interchange (Sun Plaza, MRT)>> Sembawang Dr >> Sembawang Ave >> Sembawang Road (Sembawang Shopping Center)>> Yishun Ave 7 (Ahmed Ibrahim School, Yishun Industrial estate)>> Yishun Ave 8 (new bus stop to cater to expanded industrial estate along ave 8)>> Yishun Ave 9 >> Yishun Ave 6 >> Yishun St 31 >> Yishun Ring Road (Hawker center, Northbrooks school)>> Yishun Ave 4 (Wisteria mall, Northland school)>> Yishun Central (KTPH, Yishun community hospital)>> Yishun Ave 3 (Yishun swimming complex, Junior college) & continues on current route to YCK. 

    Full SD fleet is okay.

    802

    Objective: 812 is removed and west loop is converted into feeder service 802, with no change to routing.

    812

    Eliminated and substituted by 859/883

    883

    Objective: Extended to loop at Yishun Ave 4 to substitute Yishun MRT connection for st 61 and provide alternative to 805 to Yishun, given that 805 will soon be packed to door from ave 1/st 41. DDs on 883 will get well utilized between ave 4 and Yishun MRT. 

    Sembawang Interchange >> ... >> Yishun Community Hospital >> Yishun St 61 >> Yishun Ring Road (new bus stop for transfer with 807) >> Yishun Ave 4 >> Yishun Central >> Opp Yishun Community Hospital >> .... back to current route

    859

    Objective: Extend to loop at Yishun Ave 4 to substitute missing link of 812 between Yishun Central, SAFRA and st 61/ave 4. Also provides direct connection to estate to Chong Pang. 

    Sembawang Interchange >> .... >> Yishun Ave 5 >> Yishun Central >> Yishun st 61 >> Yishun Ring Road (new bus stop for transfer with 807) >> Yishun Ave 4 >> Yishun Central (will stop outside Yishun interchange, same stop as 811 towards Khatib) >> Yishun Ave 5 .... >> Sembawang Interchange.

    806

    Objective: Connect new BTOs along st 42. Will skip bus stop along st 31 (alternative: 803 to Yishun, 860 to Ring road hawker center & beyond) and 3 stops along ring road (alternative: 804, 807, 860). This change will also provide faster access for Yishun Ave 11 to MRT in AM, while preserving 804 to ring road. With the change to st 42, the DDs on 806 will get well utilized again, as most people on ring road today prefer to take 807 to Khatib or 807/860 to Yishun MRT. Hence, AM loading on 806 has dropped significantly. 

    Yishun Interchange >> Yishun Central >> Yishun Ave 11 >> Yishun Ave 6 >> Yishun St 42 (2 new bus stops) >> Yishun Ave 6 >> Yishun Ave 11 >> Yishun Central >> Yishun Interchange

    117/169

    Will call at new pair of bus stops along Canberra Link outside Eastlace BTO and Brownstone condo. 

    965/969

    Will call at new pair of bus stop along Gambas Ave for direct Yishun/Admiralty MRT link for new commercial developments. A new route will be proposed as part of Woodlands-Sembawang enhancement in future to connect to Woodlands South and Sembawang MRT. 

    Your comments/suggestions are welcome on my above proposal for Yishun/Sembawang.
