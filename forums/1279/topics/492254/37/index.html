    Compassvale Int

    Compassvale St

    Sengkang East Rd

    Punggol Way

    Punggol Field

    Punggol Walk (New bus stop)

    Punggol Ctrl (Stops at Waterway Point bus stop like 381)

    And original route to Punggol Rd End and back.

    - Walk from Compassvale Int to Sengkang MRT may be a bit inconvenient for Compassvale Cres residents, but there are no better solutions (as LTA is reluctant to modify services at Sengkang Int)
