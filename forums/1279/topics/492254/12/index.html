    Amendment of Service 48

    Details (towards Buona Vista):

    (Existing route up till Bedok South Ave 1)

    ECP 

    Still Road > Marine Parade Road

    (Existing route till KK Hospital)

    Limited stops along Bukit Timah Road – calling at bus stops 40071 (Leng Kwang Baptism Church, opp Chinese Int'l School) and 41021 (Botanic Gardens Stn), plies Wayang Satu and Newton Flyovers instead.

    (Existing route till Buona Vista Ter)

    Route is symmetrical so the return journey should be roughly the same.

    Fleet:

    Service runtime decrease: 21 SDs  16 SDs

    2 buses allocated to services plying deleted sectors of Bukit Timah Road (66, 67), 3 buses allocated to services plying deleted sectors of Marine Parade Road (31, 36, 47 etc.). The eventual service that would receive the buses could be determined based on 1) the demand of the svc itself and 2) its reliability.

    Double deckers may be added where and when appropriate. 

    The main reason why I chose to amend 48 out of so many services and in such a way is because I saw great potential for this service to be a long-haul express link between the far East (beyong East Coast) and the west. For example, Holland Village is now made more accessible to easterners when a train ride could have taken an hour or above and the only other bus available is 61 (which is a far worse candidate for restructuring). 

    It also gives an express option for those living in Tanah Merah, as the area develops with the rise of several condominium projects. Train would definitely be insufficient to cover all the unique links that 48 provides, only to be hindered by its winding in Marine Parade and along Bukit Timah Road. 

    Passengers in the affected sectors are still well covered with alternatives and transfer locations. In fact, they could change at the bus stop itself with minimal walking. As mentioned above alternatives will have its frequency boosted which further minimises the impact on the short-haul travellers. 

    Any further views on this I will address based on Q&A style...
