    My suggestions:

    Svc 135 to merge with with Svc 47:

    New Svc 135: Ang Mo Kio - Changi Business Park
    Ang Mo Kio Interchange > (original route of 135) > Amber Rd > Marine Parade Rd > (original route of svc 47) > Changi Business Park Terminal

    --------------------------------

    Svc 47 to amend to ply the route of svc 46 from Tampines to Bedok North Ave 3, and then Bedok North Road, Bedok North Avenue 1, Bedok South Road, and finally the original route of svc 47

    New Svc 47: Tampines - Amber Road (loop)
    Tampines Int > Tampines Ave 5 > Tampines St 83 > Tampines Ave 3 > Tampines Ave 4 > Bedok Reservoir Rd > Bedok North Ave 3 > Bedok North Road > Bedok North Ave 1 > Bedok South Rd > Bedok South Ave 1 > Marine Parade Road > Mountbatten Road > East Coast Rd > Still Road South > (loop back to Tampines Int)

    Reason: 
    - Direct access from Bedok North to Marine Parade and Katong. (In my opinion, it's unnecessary for svc 47 to loop at East Coast Rd (Katong) because I can take svc 12 from Katong to Bedok South Rd, Bedok South Ave 3 and Tanah Merah. On the other hand, there's a lack of services from Bedok North Ave 1/2/3 to Marine Parade/Katong.)
    - Direct access from Bedok North Ave 1 and Bedok North Rd (between Ave 1 and 3) to Tampines. (There are 6 services from Bedok North Ave 3 to Tampines MRT, but none from B.N. Ave 1 and B.N. Rd between Ave 1 and 2.)

    --------------------------------

    Svc 46 to amend to ply the same route from Upp East Coast Ter to Tampines Ave 4, and then going straight along Tampines Ave 4, and then the route of svc 27 from Tampines Ave 4 to Hougang Central Int

    New Svc 46: Upp East Coast - Hougang Central

    Upp East Coast Ter > (original route of 46) > Tampines Ave 4 > (route of 27) > Hougang Central Int

    Reason:
    - To allow DD to ply the route of svc 27 between Tampines Ave 4 and Hougang
    - Faster access from Bedok South Ave 3 and Bedok North Rd to Tampines MRT
    - Direct route from Bedok South Ave 3 and Bedok North Rd to Tampines IKEA, Sengkang and Hougang

    --------------------------------

    Svc 27 to shorten to Sengkang/Compassvale Int

    New Svc 27: Sengkang/Compassvale - Changi Airport

    Reason:
    - Passengers travelling from Tampines to Buangkok and Hougang can take the amended svc 46.
    - Passengers travelling from Changi Airport to Buangkok and Hougang can take 27 and change to new svc 46 at Tampines, or (better still) take 858 and change to new svc 46 at TPE (before Punggol Rd).
