    Jurong West Enhancements

    52

    Boon Lay Interchange -- JW St 64 -- JW Ave 4 -- JW Ave 2 -- Corporation Road -- Boon Lay Ave -- Boon Lay Place -- Boon Lay Dr -- Corporation Road -- Lakeside MRT -- Boon Lay Way -- Jurong Townhall Road -- JE Ave 1 -- Toh Guan Road... continues on current route to Bishan.

    ** Connects SIM/Ngee Ann, Clementi, IMM, Eng Teng Fong, Traders Center, International Business Park, Toh Guan road to Jurong East Ave 1, condos/housing estate along Lakeside MRT, Chinese Garden, Boon Lay Place. Unique connection.

    ** Connects Boon Lay Place mall to newly developed HDB estate along JW Ave 2 and Boon Lay Ave (no connection today inspite of being so close)

    ** Alternative Boon Lay MRT connection for JW Ave 2, JW St 64 residents

    258

    Joo Koon Interchange -- Benoi Road (exp) -- AYE (256 route) -- Pioneer Road North -- Pioneer MRT -- current route -- JW Ave 5 -- Jalan Bahar -- JW Ave 2 -- Corporation Road -- JW Ave 1 -- JW St 51 -- Lakeside MRT -- Corporation Road (178 route) -- AYE -- Jalan Boon Lay -- International Road -- Corporation Road & back...

    ** Merges with 256 that failed to pick up. Gives direct connection to JW town residents to industrial estate along Pioneer Road and AYE as well apart from Joo Koon

    ** Reduces duplication with 243G/W by providing st 81/82 residents direct connection to JW Ave 5, Jalan Bahar, Lakeside (no service today). Jalan Bahar is particularly important to transfer to 172/199 and JW Ave 5 to transfer to 185. 

    ** Connects JW Ave 2/Ave 1 to Lakeside MRT (some duplication with 49 here)

    ** Connects Lakeside MRT to industrial estates along this stretch of AYE/International Road that does not have service today. It is considerable walk to 79/246 bus stop

    ** Covers missing link of abolished 178 as in my previous suggestion

    246

    Boon Lay Interchange -- current route -- Lakeside MRT -- Corporation Road (incl. sv 99 route) -- Boon Lay Ave -- Boon Lay Dr -- Corporation Road & back

    ** Minor tweaking to the route to provide direct Lakeside MRT connectivity to Corporation Road part where 99 plies

    ** 240/52 will continue to serve Lakeside - Boon Lay Place

    256

    Terminated and merged with 258. Number to be used in future for Tuas South Extension.

    192

    Diverted via JW St 61/62 to compensate loss of 256 between Pioneer-JW ST 61/62--Boon Lay.

    ** 241/179 continue to provide direct links between Boon Lay and Pioneer via JW St 63.

    ** Removal of 258 from Upper Jurong Road will also provide better loading to 99/192.

    76/134 inspired amendment for 40/152

    Service 40 (Bedok <> Mcpherson Estate)

    Current route to Tanjong Katong Rd > Geylang Rd > Paya Lebar Rd > Lor Bengkok > Circuit Rd > Pipit Rd > Circuit Rd > Lor Bengkok > Paya Lebar Rd > Sims Avenue > Tanjong Katong Rd > Current route to Bedok ITH. 

    Service 152 (Eunos <> Mcpherson Estate)

    Sims Avenue > Still Rd > Koon Seng Rd > Dunman Rd > Tanjong Katong Rd > Geylang Rd > Aljunied Rd > Merpati Rd > Circuit Rd > Pipit Rd > Circuit Rd > Merpati Rd > Reverse route to Eunos. 

    Benefits

    1. Service 40's new length will be 19km versus 23 km previously. This will enable it to be be operated at higher frequencies with its current fleet of just 7 buses. 

    2. New connection between East Coast Rd (I12 Katong area) and Paya Lebar MRT. Right now, the only MRT connection is at Dakota Stn via services 10 & 32. 

    3. New connection between Eunos & Koon Seng Road. Current EWL connection is at Kembangan MRT via service 33 at Joo Chiat Place which doesn't make sense. 

    4. New connection betwen Koon Seng Road/Dunman Road and amenities in Paya Lebar and Geylang Road. 

    Loss connection from East Coast Rd to Geylang Rd/Aljunied Rd/Merpati Road. 

    1. Merpati Rd/Aljunied Rd: Easily replicated by transfering to service 135 at Mountbatten Rd/Tanjong Katong Rd or by transfering to an assortment of services at Mcpherson Estate. 

    2. Geylang Rd: Transfer to service 152 at Tanjong Katong Rd or 197/134 and walk from Guilemard or Geylang East Central respectively.
