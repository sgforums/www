    Tampines East MRT rationalisation/amendments, draft 3.

    ============================

    A. Service 291 (East):

    • After Tampines St 21, reroute to St 24, Ave 7 (MRT), Ave 2. Skips St 23.
    • Alternatively, via St 24, Ave 7 (MRT), St 34, St 33, St 32, and back via St 34.
    • Provides MRT connection for St 21 and St 32/33 residents.
    • Note that St 24 / Ave 7 junction is right-turn friendly.

    B1. Service 39:

    • From Tampines: After Tampines Ave 2 (East CC), divert into St 23, St 24, Ave 7 (MRT).
    • Retains link between Tampines Int and St 23 (albeit longer), and provides MRT connection for St 23 residents.

    B2. Service 293 (East):

    • Re-loop via Tampines Ave 7, Ave 9, St 42/43, Ave 9, St 45, Ave 7.
    • Provides MRT connection for Tampines St 42-45 residents.
    • (BUT) Lost link to St 41, Tampines North CC and a few schools (East View Sec, Tamp Nth Pri).

    C. Service 18:

    • From Bedok: After Tampines Ave 2 (East CC), reroute into St 23, St 24, Ave 7 (MRT), St 45, Ave 9, Ave 12, Pasir Ris Dr 8, Dr 3, Pasir Ris Int.
    • Provides connections to both Tampines East and Pasir Ris MRT stations, plus Tampines East CC, for Tampines St 42-45 residents, as an alternative to changes B1 & B2.
    • Alternatively, may continue after Tampines Ave 9 towards Tampines North Int.
    • (BUT) Lost direct connection to Bedok from Tampines Ave 7, which can be mitigated with a quick transfer via 21 --> 168.

    ============================

    Here are my proposed changes to rationalise services for Tampines East MRT with (hopefully) the least number of major changes involved, with either three minor reroutes (A, B1, B2) or one minor and one major (A, C). NO NEW SERVICES are needed.

    This also does not take into account rationalisation along other stations involving cutting/rerouting of routes.

    The HDBs along St 42 are generally within walking distance to Tampines East MRT, so it's mainly those in the path of service 28 that needs coverage. 28 should not be unnecessarily modified. 291 has to be the bridging service to St 32/33, for introducing / diverting a service would be troublesome; this would call for a new service at St 23, which is provided by either 39 (B1) or 18 (C).

    A last possibility is to extend 46 to loop near Ave 7/9, but that's quite remote and there may be other things LTA intend to do with 46.

    ============================

    Comments?
