    I've been thinking about what services could possibly use bendies at Bidadari, and the only logical answer is: 53.

    So here comes my suggestions...

    Scenario 1

    Service 53: Bidadari - Changi Airport (Loop): https://routes.one/idea/860

    Service 53M: Bidadari - Hougang St 21 (Loop)

    Service 58 amendment to Bishan St 14: https://routes.one/idea/861

    Service 135 amendment to Serangoon Central: https://routes.one/idea/862

    - Allows bendies to be deployed on 53 for the heavily utilized PSR-CGA sector

    - Deleted sectors on 53: 

    • NEX - Boundary Rd: Covered by amended 135, also supplemented by 22 and 315
    • Boundary Rd - Bishan: Covered by 156
    • Bishan St 14 - AMK Ave 1 / Serangoon: Covered by amended 58
    • Lor Ah Soo - AMK Ave 1: Covered by 45
    • Lor Ah Soo - Bishan: Transfer to CCL or 58 at Upp Serangoon Rd

    - Puts 45, 53 and 53M at the same bus stops outside Serangoon MRT so that Lor Ah Soo commuters don't need to choose which one to go to

    - Improves reliability on 53 by making the route slightly shorter

    Scenario 2

    Service 53: Bidadari - Changi Airport (Loop): https://routes.one/idea/860

    Service 53M: Bidadari - Hougang St 21 (Loop)

    Service 81 extended to Bishan via 53's route: https://routes.one/idea/863

    - All deleted sectors on 53 covered by 81, except Lor Ah Soo - Bishan (transfer to 81 at Upp Serangoon Rd)

    - Commuters from Bishan / AMK Ave 1 to Tampines Rd and Pasir Ris can still take 81 which is actually faster

    Scenario 3

    Service 53: Bidadari - Changi Airport (Loop): https://routes.one/idea/860

    Service 53M renumbered to 108 and extended to Bishan via 53's route: https://routes.one/idea/864

    - All deleted sectors on 53 covered by 108; those travelling beyond Kovan can transfer to 53

    - 108 can get a few DDs as it helps both 53's and 113's heavy loading sectors at Lor Ah Soo

    - With 108, it's possible for 113 to be withdrawn or operate at reduced frequencies. 113A can definitely be withdrawn
