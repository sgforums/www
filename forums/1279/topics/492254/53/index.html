    Preliminary ideas for extension of 71

     

    IMO 71's usefulness will be very limited and definitely not DD-worthy, so an extension seems necessary to me.

     

    Plan A: Express to city from Bishan St 11 / Braddell Rd, via CTE

    - Preferably passing through areas in city not served by NSL/TEL, e.g. Little India, Bugis, Suntec, Chinatown

    - Offers direct link to city for AMK Ave 4/1 and Bishan residents

     

    Plan B: Extension to Compassvale or Punggol from YCK Rd

    - Preferably via Fernvale St / Anchorvale St

    - Supplements 50 and 163, providing additional connection between Sengkang and AMK

    - Offers direct link for YCK Rd workers living in inner parts of AMK town

    - Limitation: Too much duplication
