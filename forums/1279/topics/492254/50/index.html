    Please, there's no point in creating a long route that crosses two ends of the island. Because it will be too long and unfeasible for the bus driver / company (due to jams, etc)

    Existing cross-island routes such as 14, 51, 61 are already very problematic, prone to jams and bunching.

    Good routes serve towns direct to the point. Either Jurong East -- CBD, Punggol -- CBD, or Jurong East -- Punggol (without passing via CBD).

    In addition, please don't resurrect the 2003 (pre NEL-rationalisation) routes. Population and travelling patterns have changed after 14 years, people are already used to these services. Create new and better routes.

    Thanks.
