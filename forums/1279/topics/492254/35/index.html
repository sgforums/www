    I'm worried about loading imbalance for 18. The 225 part will surely have super high loading but the rest of this route does not deserve a majority DDS fleet nor a robust frequency.

    Can amend svc 18 at Bedok North and also at Tampines East, thus increasing loading more evenly throughout.

    Bedok Int - Bedok North Ave 1 - St 3 - Ave 2 - Bedok North Rd - Bedok North Ave 3 (Bedok Reservoir MRT) - Bedok Reservoir Rd - Tampines Ave 1 (Tampines West MRT) - Tampines Ave 2 - St 32 - St 33 - St 34 - Ave 7 (Tampines East MRT) - Tampines Ctrl 1 - Tampines Concourse

    This would connect Tampines St 33/34 to Tampines East MRT. For St 42/43/45, existing svc 293 can be amended to ply Tampines Ave 7/St 42 instead of Ave 9/St 42, and then split off as a separate bi-directional svc 294G/W, leaving svc 293 to serve the existing west loop only.

    Svc 294W:

    Tampines Int - Tampines Ave 7 - St 41 - Ave 9 - St 45 - Ave 7 (Tampines East MRT) - St 42 - St 43 - Ave 9 - St 41 - Ave 7 - Tampines Int

    Svc 294G:

    Tampines Int - Tampines Ave 7 - St 41 - Ave 9 - St 43 - St 42 - Ave 7 (Tampines East MRT) - St 45 - Ave 9 - St 41 - Ave 7 - Tampines Int

    Pax at skipped stop along St 42 (Blk 459) can either walk directly to Tampines East MRT or take svc 29 from Ave 9 instead. There may be excess capacity along the common sector at St 41/Ave 9 with the G/W routes, but this routing gives it a more direct connection to Tampines East MRT.
