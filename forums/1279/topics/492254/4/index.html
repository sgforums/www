    SWT 166A : Clementi Int - Alexandra Rd

    The section between Clementi Int and Alexandra Rd is where single-decker buses on service 166 usually have very high loading during peak hours.

    The loading is so high that it is almost not possible to board the single-decker buses on service 166 between Commonwealth Avenue West and Ayer Rajah Expressway, during morning peak hours.

    However, as 166 is a long route, it may not be wise to do single-to-double fleet upgrades just for that "short section" of the route (8km out of 29km).

    Therefore, I would like to recommend that we have a Short-Working Trip (SWT) of service 166 between Clementi Int and Alexandra Rd, during peak hours.
