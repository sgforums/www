    Service 108:

    Hougang -- Changi Business Park

    Via: Hougang, Sengkang West (Anchorvale / Cheng Lim), Tampines East, Upp Changi, CBP

    • - Route as a semi-companion of services 27 and 118.
    •  
    • - Brief stopover at Tampines with minimal detour, for ease of bus service transfers at Tampines Ave 7, and also providing inter-town links for neighbourhood 4.
    •  
    • - Links the upcoming Sengkang General Hospital with nearby residential areas not already served by 43/50/83/85/109/119/372, while maintaining route simplicity.
    •  
    • - Provides new/alternative direct link to the TPE stop, alongside 27/88.
    •  
    • - One-directional route length is about 22-23 km.
    • (Initial idea was to extend to UEC, but that resulted in route being too long. Also splits the CBP boarding bus stops on two sides for 108 and 118, which is not ideal.)
