    Hi, about 152, I hope you don't mind me sharing a bit of my rationale:

    Currently northeastern area is lacking a direct connection towards downtown and Marina Centre via the central corridor. 857 from Yishun also serves the Boon Keng-Little India corridor and is an unnecessary stretch especially during rush hour. This would help to bridge that gap (while making a detour to AMK to absorb rush hour loading) while allowing Sengkang Central / West residents to bridge their connection towards North-South Line at AMK without having to go from NEL-Serangoon-CCL-Bishan-NSL or at Dhoby Ghaut for direct NSL transfer. This is in fact targeted towards helping to relieve passenger traffic from Sengkang/Punggol to and from the AMK-TPY corridor on the NSL. 

    This will also help to relieve rush hour northeast / AMK loading towards downtown area and shopping belt, as well as providing connectivity to downtown and Suntec areas without troublesome transfers.
