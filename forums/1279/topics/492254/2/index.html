    5 Jul 16, 10:24

    Toh Guan Estate needs some bus restructuring. Almost all services are Jurong East bound. 

    41, 52, 105, 183, 333, 990 all go to Jurong East one way or the other. There is no direct service even to Lakeside and Boon Lay that are so nearby.

    My suggestions:

    41, 183, 333, 990 stay as it is and provide the necessary JE - Toh Guan MRT connection

    52 extend to Boon Lay via Jurong East Ave 1, Jurong Townhall Road, Lakeside MRT, Boon Lay Place, Boon Lay Interchange

    * This way you provide direct connection to IBP, Traders Center, IMM for people living along Chinese Garden, Lakeside, Boon Lay who have no option but to take EWL and then bus or walk

    105 extend to Bukit Batok interchange via Bukit Batok ave 1. 

    * In spite of Bukit Batok being very close to Toh Guan, there is no direct connection. 990 route is winding to Bukit Batok MRT. 

    These two changes can enhance connectivity between Toh Guan and neighbouring estates. There would still be 4 other services, hence it will not inconvenience current travelers.

    Feedback?
