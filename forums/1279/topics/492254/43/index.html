    During term time weekdays there is a shuttle bus from Sengkang MRT to NTU. It is free for NTU students. (You need to show your NTU student card to board.)

    http://www.ntu.edu.sg/has/Transportation/Pages/FAQ.aspx#sengkang_shuttle

    But the departure time is damn early, at 7:00am, 07:10am, 07:20am. There is no service on weekends, public holidays and university vacation. (This means that this service will only be available from mid-August.)

    There are also NTU morning shuttles from AMK, CCK, Marine Parade, Pasir Ris, Punggol and Tampines.

    In my opinion, there is a demand for express buses from the east and northeast to Jurong, thanks to the numerous industries, military camps and NTU. But LTA seems to be more interested in express/premium routes to the CBD, and make Jurong-bound passengers take the MRT.
