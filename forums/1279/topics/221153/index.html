    Originally posted by manjieass:
    wat is parallax error???
    Simply, it means not reading a reading on the same plane as the indicator. In the case of a speedometer, you read PERPENDICULAR to the measuring apparatus. In other words, immediately above the reading. (Not vertically above, speedometer is slanted). To correct parallax error, what some instruments have is a mirror. If the needle is seen in the mirror, it shows that there is a parallax error because of the laws of reflection, blah, that I won't explain. I would need a diagram.
