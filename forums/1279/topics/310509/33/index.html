    Originally posted by Oceane:


    Red colour signals are from the driver's DDU. The DDU tells if the driver is late or early. If the driver is ahead of schedule, the DDU will have a red light and there will be a higher pitch sound. If the driver is late, the DDU shows a yellow light and there will be a lower pitch sound. The extent of earliness or lateness (in minutes) can be seen at the DDU's screen. +(number) means early by (number) minutes, vice versa for -(number).

    if too early like +40 on 170 (a lot of the time), will oso hav a lower pitch sound and it is double like the sound of very late.

    so,

    early: single high pitch, red light
    very early: double low pitch, red light
    late: single low pitch, yellow light
    very late: double low pitch, yellow light
