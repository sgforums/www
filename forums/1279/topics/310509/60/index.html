    Originally posted by ftjj0298:

    What is jump bus?


    Its a term coined by SBST drivers (as only SBST drivers does this) to denote that they drive different buses throughout their course of duty in a day. It is usually done at the terminating point where they change shift at. For split shift drivers sometimes they may jump bus at the depot, which means before their swing break they drive Bus A, then after the swing break they drive Bus B.

    This applies to only single depot services. However, not all single depot services do jump bus.

    Official term is CLOB - Crew Layover On Bus.

    Some examples of jump bus and their locations are:
