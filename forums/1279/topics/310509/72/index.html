  • TIB1049L
    24 Nov 08, 17:28
    Originally posted by lianamaster:

    How do buses reach the status of "perm"? How do you guys know that that service must be a perm service?


    Well, this will be case by case basis

    1) The bus is confirmed perm when the duty roster in the interchange states so. Normally this will be confirmed immediately once posted

    2) A bus is cameoing for continuous days on a service non stop. Normally we would observe it for a period of time (which diff people have diff standards; mine is 1 month, but we double check until 3 months) and if it still stays on, it will be a perm.
