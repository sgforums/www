    Think most probably SBST wanted to deploy NAC DDs just to maintain its NAC/PAC status that's all. Same for service 90 which used LO2x in the past but no AC DDs till now. Service 359 used NAC Scania N113CRBs instead.

    Feeder service 335 used LO2x, together with some other feeders and short hauls in Jurong East / Boon Lay areas. After the LO2x were phased out, the MB0405s were mainly deployed. Only feeder 242 had LO3x, followed by feeder 243. JIS services only had VO2x as SBST wanted to maintain NAC status for those services.

    Don't remember feeder 228 use and DDs before. Anyway feeders 228 and 385 merged to become service 60, and this service currently have AC DDs and Scania K230UBs.

    Add on for feeder service 410 too, it used to have NAC DDs too, and once in 2005 a VSO cameoed before. However no more DDs on feeder 410 ever since then.
