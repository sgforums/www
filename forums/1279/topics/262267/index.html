    Frequencies have to be high (ie 1 to 5 mins) for ultra high demand services like 170, 190, 238, 960 and some of the townlinks/intratowns and all of Boon Lay's feeders, JIS, during peak hours.

    Frequency can be around 5 to 8 mins durng peak hours for high demand services like some of the trunks like 76.

    The thing about high demand is that it refers to a high number of passengers per unit time, so definitely x number of passengers have to be moved in a certain amount of time, thus it implies that frequencies have to be high too.
