    Boon Lay Temporary Bus Interchange

    After public bus services relocated out of the temporary facility since 8 May 2010, the bus interchange was vacated for a while and then fenced up with white colour hoarding.

    The facility was demolished in year 2012. Until recently, the hoarding was always there.

    Today, I saw that they are starting to take down the hoarding. Soon, some "state land" sign post would probably be placed there to tell people not to do certain activities on that land.

    Well, this means the Bangla, the PRCs and the Pinoys will soon be able to use that empty land outside Boon Lay MRT Station to gather and have picnic under the stars - no need to go to the empty land outside Jurong East MRT Station anymore! Yay! 

    This also means that pasar malams can now once again be held at that empty land, like how it was in the past before the temporary bus interchange was constructed. as the land is quite big, this means we can have pasar malams of larger scale, and even have carnival rides (eg. ferries wheel). Yay! 

    On a serious note, this means the land would probably be put up for sale soon, and construction works would soon begin on the upcoming Jurong Region MRT Line.
