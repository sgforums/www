    There are very few services with bendy buses that pass by scenic places.

    Below are the other services you can try:-

    a) 969: Between Woodlands and Tampines. Ply along SLE, can see nature area (bendy is mostly seen during peak hours)

    b) 176: Between Bukit Merah and Bukit Panjang. You can alight at Henderson Rd and get to either Telok Blangah Hill Park or Mount Faber, or alight at Pasir Panjang Rd to get to Labrador Park.

    c1) 190: Between Choa Chu Kang and New Bridge Road
    c2) 960: Between Woodlands and Marina Centre

    They ply along BKE, can see the scenic Central Catchment area and little view of the Upper Pierce Reservoir.
