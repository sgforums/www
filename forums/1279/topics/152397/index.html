    starting next monday, residents of hougang & sengkang will have a faster alternative to work at loyang ind est, changi village and changi airport cargo complex with the introduction of SBS Transit Fast Forward service 89e.

    89e Hougang Central - Changi Airport Cargo Complex

    Morning peak: 6.45am - 7.45am
    Evening peak: 5.45pm - 6.45pm

    >> Calls at the 3 stops along Hougang Ave 10
    >> Skips the stop opp The Rivervale
    >> Calls at all stops in Sengkang Town (Sengkang East Ave, Compassvale Rd/St and Punggol Rd)
    >> Express along TPE, Pasir Ris Dr 12 and Pasir Ris Dr 3
    >> Calls only at the 3 stops along Loyang Ave bounding Loyang Ind Estate
    >> Calls at the stop along Telok Paku Rd outside Changi Village
    >> Calls at all stops inside Airport Cargo Complex including Police Pass Office (In evening peak, calls at an additional stop along Nicoll Dr outside Changi International Exhibition Centre)

    note that fast forward services 10e and 30e will also be launched on the same day. (see other thread)
