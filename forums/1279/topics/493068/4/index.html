    Originally posted by Sbs6750E:

    Sooner or later feeders and intratowns have to use DDs instead.. then can leave the A24s for airport svcs only. Bo bian mah.

    They can do a mix. Some feeders can do v well with DDs where the estate/alighting point is further away from MRT.

    CCK

    300 -> B (most loading comes from 1st 3 stops along CCK Ave 4)

    301 -> D (most loading comes along Keat Hong Close/Ave 6)

    302 -> B (preferable due to two MRTs)

    307 -> S (no need for B/D)

    308 -> S (no need for B/D, if demand surges, DDs should be okay as Keat Hong estate is quite far from MRT)

    WDL

    900 -> D (alighting points along WDL Ave 4)

    901 -> D (alighting points along WDL Ave 1)

    903 -> D (most people alight along Marsiling estate)

    904 -> S (don't think this service will need HC buses)

    911 -> D (can do well given that serves only WDL MRT)

    912 -> B (prefer bendies as it serves 3 MRTs)

    913 -> B (prefer bendies as it serves 2 MRTs + stops along Woodlands Circle quite close to Admiralty)

    Yishun

    800 -> B (prefer bendy as most loading comes from 2 stops along ring road that are <5 min away from interchange. Otherwise full route 800 is strictly SD loading)

    803 -> S (service will not need HC buses)

    804 -> D (will do well with DDs as most load is towards Yishun st 41/ave 1 which is far from MRT)

    805 -> S (does not need HC buses, in future if demand surges can use DDs given that ave 1 is further away)

    806 -> D (demand is at ave 6/ring road, both quite far from MRT)

    811 -> B (preferable to use bendy as it loops Yishun MRT multiple times + Khatib with stops pretty quickly from interchange/MRT)

    812 -> B (prefer to use bendy but on this service DDs can be quite okay also)

    So overall following feeders/intra-towns can continue use bendies:

    300 (10), 302 (16), 800 (5), 811 (12), 812 (6), 912 (12), 913 (12). 

    * Just above 7 services will take 73 bendies more than the 40 WAB bendies available
