    1000th post.

    It's been 9 years since I first started posting. Much has changed since -- the public transport landscape, planning of new routes / new buses, and not to forget different forumers. 

    There had been rough times. Crowded buses, MRT breakdowns, BCM confusion during change of operators, fare hikes etc. There are also good occasions with better route introductions and improving MRT-bus connections. It is reported that an important phase of route rationalisation will take place next year, hopefully it will bring about reasonable and optimized changes to operations.

    As bus enthusiasts, we shall continue to play our part on the ground to observe deployment and commute patterns, connect with communities and grassroots and gather feedback to PTOs such that they will be aware of changes and adjust operations accordingly.

    Things will continue to change, as population rises, new towns are developed, new forms of transport technology emerge (both public and private), the economy restructures, SG public transport has to continuously evolve to meet the changing demands of the time, or become obsolete and subjected to public distrust.

    Hope the best for the future. ~
