    Therefore, I have come to a conclusion that Choa Chu Kang Interchange should be relocated to the open space beside Yew Tee MRT.

    Another idea is that NR3 should be converted into a normal Trunk Service.

    WHAT ARE YOUR VIEWS ON THIS?
    My view is.. if you were in charge of managing the land transport system, Singapore would be in disarray.

    It would totally be senseless and blatantly stupid to move Choa Chu Kang interchange to open space in Yew Tee MRT.

    Why?

    1. CCK vs Yew Tee.. CCK's central area has a larger population as compared to Yew Tee.

    2. Yew Tee is next to Kranji. Kranji, has no flats. Therefore, a linkage there would be useless.

    3. CCK is close to Bukit Batok, Gombak etc..therefore, it would make better sense to place an interchange in CCK. Better links to other town areas.

    4. CCK is closer to expressways too.

    5. More schools around CCK area. Some routes taken by 302 into the Yew Tee area does not bring in much revenue to the bus company.
