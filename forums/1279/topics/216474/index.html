    That model is a Volvo Olympian but it was a re-coloured SBS version of a HK bus. The model was released to commemorate SBS's 25th Anniversary in 1998 and is made by Corgi. The other model, released in 2001 to commemorate the 28th Anniversary as well as the re-branding to SBS Transit, was made by Creative Master Northcord and is based on an actual SBS Olympian.
