    Clue 2 (also a giveaway): Ending bus: 33

    Clue 5: Except for one instance, when I crossed the road to take a bus at the opposite bus stop, I always board a new bus at the same bus stop.

    139 Alexandra Rd - Bt Merah Int
    16 Bt Merah Int - Tiong Bahru Rd
    (cross the road)
    195 Tiong Bahru Rd - Commonwealth Ave
