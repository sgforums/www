    Bus Captains are bestowed the power to confiscate EZlink cards if they find that the Card Readers display and emit error warnings. Do not let them do it easily....of course you must be on the right side of the law in the first place.

    Example......if the reader displays RED and GREEN lights together, your card has insufficient value for further rides. If the driver says your card has errors and he need to confiscate it, do not surrender your card. Call SBStransit hotline (1800-2872727) on the spot and report him for lacking of knowledge and intimidation to passenger.
