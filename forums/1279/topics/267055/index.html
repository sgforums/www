    Bring you to the heart of the business district directly from homes with all passengers will be comfortably seated throughout the journey.

    Route
    A single trip from the designated neighbourhoods during the morning peak hours on weekdays (except Public Holidays).

    Premium 532 : Clementi Ave 4 to Fullerton Rd
    Premium 533 : Teban Gardens to Fullerton Rd
    Premium 534 : Yio Chu Kang Rd to Fullerton Rd
    Premium 535 : Toa Payoh Lor 8 to Fullerton Rd
    Premium 536 : Jurong West St 91 to Fullerton Rd
    Premium 537 : Jurong West Ave 5 to Fullerton Rd
    Premium 538 : Pasir Ris Dr 3 to Fullerton Rd
    Premium 539 : Pasir Ris Dr 4 to Fullerton Rd
    Premium 540 : Yuan Ching Rd to Fullerton Rd
    Premium 542 : Jurong West Ave 1 to Fullerton Rd
    Premium 543 : Jurong East Ave 1 to Fullerton Rd
    Premium 544 : Bedok North Rd to Fullerton Rd
    Premium 545 : Bedok North Ave 4 to Fullerton Rd

    Alighting stops
    Road Name | Bus stop name
    Anson Rd > Apex Tower*
    Robinson Rd > Opp SIA Building
    Robinson Rd > Opp TAS Building
    Robinson Rd > Opp AIA Tower
    Fullerton Rd > Fullerton Square

    * Premium Services 538, 539, 544 & 545 will not call at this bus stop.

    Fare
    Flat fares of $2.70 or $3.00 apply (regardless of boarding / alighting points).

    Transfer rebates are applicable on SBS Transit bus services (except Chinatown Direct), SeasonPass & concessionary travel are not applicable.

    * Taken from SBS Transit - Premium Services
