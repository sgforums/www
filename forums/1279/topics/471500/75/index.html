    Originally posted by Sbs6750E:

    Does batch 4 WEGs have different engine than the earlier ones? Why batch 4 sound quieter?

    New radiator fan, the old B9TLs have a single, large radiator fan that does not cover the whole radiator, the newer B9TLs have 6 smaller fans that cover most of the radiator, which helps dissipate heat more effectively. A lot of the noise you hear from buses is from the radiator fan.

    Maintenance of buses by individual compaies play a part too.
