    Was onboard SMB56A (KJ/CCBP 172) today towards Boon Lay and it declared breakdown at 0914hrs. It was on split shift but not sure which one.

    Bus had no air con, and engine died when stopping at bus stops 44099 (Former Keat Hong Camp) and 44079 (Opp blk 464). At bus stop 30089 (Aft Hai Inn See Tp), after alighting some pax, BC contacted OCC. This was the conversation:

    BC: Hello. My bus air con not working. And engine keep dying. How?

    OCC: What service and what number?

    BC: 56 on 172 towards Boon Lay

    OCC: Where are you?

    BC: Hai Inn See

    OCC: How many pax you have on board?

    BC: (after looking through the rear mirror) About 40. (Bus was fully seated with pax with no standee)

    OCC: You on split shift or AM shift?

    BC: Split.

    OCC: How many trips more?

    BC: One trip.

    OCC: OK. Alight all pax. Declare breakdown and let pax board next bus.

    So BC drove the bus out of the bus bay and asked us to all alight. BC cancelled ride, but never issue complimentary ticket.

    All pax boarded TIB716B (sv975) at 0920hrs and TIB714G (sv172 --172AP01) at 0922hrs.

     Took a picture of the scene but again i cant seem to upload the photo here....
