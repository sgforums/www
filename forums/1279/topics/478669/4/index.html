    SMB107L on service 307E (190AM50 slot)

    Time: 0730HRS

    Location: Opp CCK MRT (Lot One) stop

    Details:

    Bus rear door could not close after alighting its passengers. Apparently this bus has been facing problem along its way (info from another BC) and at least 2 buses (SMB213M, TIB738M) overtook it. When i board the bus, it was already 28 mins behind schedule, following closely behind TIB738M with less than 10 pax. At Opp CCK MRT bus stop, after alighting pax, BC tried to close the rear door many times, and even walked to the rear door to force it close, but to no avail. BC called OCC and OCC told him to declare breakdown. Good thing all the pax had alighted so no pax were affected. But it caused jam behind as at that timing, the bus stop is super busy with SMRT buses as well as private buses.
