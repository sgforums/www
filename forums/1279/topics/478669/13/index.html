    Not sure if to post here since it was a close accident...

    Date: 111114
    Time: 1706hrs (bus was behind schedule for 5 mins)
    Bus (bus service): SMB59S on 172 (172PM02)
    Direction: Towards CCK INT
    Location: Junction of CCK Ave 1 and CCK Way
    Loading: Fully seated with a few standees

    Bus was approaching the above-mentioned junction when the lights turn amber. BC decided to step on the accelerator and lights have turned red when the bus crosses the stop line. A lorry in the opposite direction was turning right from CCK Ave 1 to CCK Way at that time, so SMB59S BC quickly stepped on the brake and swerve the bus a bit to the right to prevent head-on collision with the lorry. Good thing that the BC swerved the bus a little to the right, if not it will be a sure-hit head on collision, and impact could be super great (with injuries) since the speed of the bus was pretty fast. Bus stopped at the middle of the junction for a few seconds before continuing along CCK Ave 1. BC kept apologising to the pax on board after that...wasnt sure if there were pax who actually fell down but those standing at the front portion of the bus lost balance and almost fell...
