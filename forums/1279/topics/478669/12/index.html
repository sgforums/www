  • SMB42P
    2 Oct 14, 22:58

    TIB715D (172S108)  met with a side swipe accident outside Boon Lay Int around 7.20pm just now (junction of JW Central 3 and Street 64) towards CCK. Bus was turning left when it side swiped the car that was on its left. The female car driver went shouting at the BC saying its his fault. The 172 BC was a new BC and the mentor went down to negotiate with the car driver. Soon the BC also went down and both parties started to take photographs and particulars.

    Bus finally resumed its service towards CCK at 7.35pm, but it has caused a huge jam behind as all buses and vehicles are unable to turn left. Some buses are SMB56A from 172, TIB809S from 178, 179, 180, 240, 241, 254.

    Think BC got demotivated and there were a few instances where he forgot to stop for ppl to alight when bell was pressed and mentor had to remind him.

    Couldnt see damage of the car as i was on the bus. Wasnt sure who is the actual one at fault also. Left side of the bus got scratches and one portion of the panel between the front and rear door got peeled off slightly, and there was a serious dent there too.. A pity that this bus just came back from repaint...and also pity the BC that it is only his first few days being a BC and such thing happened...
