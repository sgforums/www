  • TIB1224Y

    There are a few types of jerking on the KUB, one that jerks after the brakes (bus stop brake) have been released, as the bus prepares to move to acceleration stage.

    Another jerk that cannot be determined whether is the BC's doing or the mechanical side is the jerk after 1st gear lock up, on to the second gear, there would be a strong pushing/pulling force that constitutes as a jerk.

    First when s174 got these new buses (batch 1), the jerk often makes old folks (like the one in Stomp) to fall/lose balance. I lost balance in the jerk after lock up most of the time, before getting used to the pattern.
