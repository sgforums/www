    1. yes, it will go back to sengkang depot. note that this last train is automatically pre-programmed to go back by the Operations Control Centre (OCC). the 2 entrances to the depot are at southbound crossover after SKG and northbound crossover just before BKK.

    2. it is most probably the sensor detecting that someone is preventing the door from closing and thus it will open a bit to allow that someone some space to move clear of the door before attempting to close the door fully.
