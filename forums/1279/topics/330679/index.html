    The first part have expired, so lets continue here. This thread is good for new members who wish to catch the special buses. Honestly I have not completed all the special buses myself.

    SBS Transit

    SBS 9888Y (First low-floor SuperBus in singapore, only Volvo B10TL with a ZF 5HP590 gearbox) - AMDEP 25

    SBS 9889U (first bus to be designed by ComfortDelgro Engineering) - BBDEP 166

    SBS 1688K (only diesel Volvo B10BLE in Singapore) - HGDEP 163

    SBS 2888T (Only Scania L94UB in SBS Transit's fleet) - Express 518 - Update: On a Sunday I took it at around 1640 at Opp Tampines JC, Tampines Ave 7

    SBS 0997A (only non-bendy super-long bus in Singapore) - HGDEP 62

    SBS 2988M and SBS 2989K (first two CNG buses in Singapore) (SLBP 105) (SLBP 243G)

    SBS 0073R (OAC Scania with completely refurbished interior) - BNDEP SP

    SBS 9810X (Melbourne-built Volvo Super Olympian and the only one now with EDS (except SBS 9889U)) (BRBP 31)

    SBS 2750G (only DM3500 B10M with Webasto air-conditioner) - BNDEP 9

    SBS 2754X (only DM3500 B10M with plug-doors) - BNDEP 8

    SBS 2779A (only DM3500 B10M with EDS) - HGDEP 107/107M

    Newly added to the list

    SBS 8030L (Only Volvo B7RLE in SBS Transit's fleet) (SLBP 174)

    SBS 8033D (The demonstrator of Scania K230UB) (SLBP 183)

    SBS 8031J ( The only MAN 18.240 HOCL-NL in SBS Transit's fleet ) (BNDEP 17)

    SMRT Buses

    *TIB 0838H (first bendy bus in Singapore) - route 172

    *TIB 1024H (demonstrator Volgren bendy) - route 913

    *TIB 1017D and TIB 1018B (only Volgren-bodied Dennis Lance buses) - both on route 851

    *TIB 1257C (former CSS bus and only Dennis Lance in CSS' fleet before it was transferred to $MRT) - route 980

    *TIB 0905Y (demonstrator Hino Volgren) - route 941

    *TIB 0700X (DAF with Hispano-like front) - route 172

    Newly added to the list

    *TIB 0792E (The only CAC Merz fitted with OAC windows ) (KJDEP 307)

    *TIB 1034D [Only Hispano Habit Bendy fitted with LAWO Orange EDS (The type of EDS found on SMB0001H)] (KJDEP 190)

    *TIB 0484U (Hispano OAC Merz fitted with LAWO Orange EDS) (WLDEP 857)

    *TIB 0595H (Hispano OAC Merz fitted with LAWO Orange EDS) (WLDEP SP)

    *SMB 0001H (First OC500LE in SMRT's fleet) (KJDEP 190)
