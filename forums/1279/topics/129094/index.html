    Category : Transport

    Subject : Do we actually need aircon for feeder bus service?

    Suggestion : The feeder bus a run short trip around the estate to and fro bus interchange. Such trip will only last for about less <10mins for a typical commuter. The current fare is $0.83 for cash paying and about $0.6 for using EZ Link. 1) Do commuter actually need aircon for such a short trip? 2)In addition, it is fair for commuters who actually take the feeder bus to a market which is 3 bus stop away and need to pay $0.83 like the rest? 3)Suggest feeder service should be price according to the distance.

    Date of Reply : 07/02/2005

    Reply : Thank you for your feedback. This reply is provided in consultation with the bus operators.

    According to commuters? feedback, majority of the commuters prefer air-conditioned buses to non-air-conditioned ones given our hot and humid climate. It is also quieter and more comfortable to travel in air-conditioned buses.

    As for the suggestion to set feeder bus fares according to the distance travelled, we wish to clarify that the fares for feeder services are flat fares pegged at the minimum fare of trunk services, i.e., $0.80 for cash payment or $0.63 using ez-link card for adults. Many commuters using feeder bus services benefit from this flat fare as they would otherwise have to pay a higher fare if these services adopt the distance-related trunk service fare structure. As feeder services utilise the same level of resources as trunk services, it will not be viable for feeder services to set distance-related fares that are lower than the current flat fare.

    Commuters are encouraged to use ez-link cards to enjoy lower fares. In particular, ez-link card users can enjoy additional transfer rebates if they connect from feeder bus services to the MRT or other bus services.

    Should you require further clarification, please feel free to contact us at Tel: 6375 7085 or email at cut_waste_secretariat@lta.gov.sg. Thank you.
