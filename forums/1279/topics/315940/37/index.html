    Let's make a final speech on saying goodbye to all Scania N113CRBs in Singapore.

    "I would like to bid a Farewell to the Scania N113CRBs that had serve SBS well for the past 20 years. It's a very sad occasion to see good buses of all time leaving the SBS fleet gradually. The heydays of AMDEP, BRBP & BNDEP is totally gone and will always be remembered. How sad several of the NAC buses like SBS3590U were deployed to HGDEP TRG and several AMDEP/BRBP buses were ended up in BNDEP, which had abruptly ended its days on Services 70, 90, 94, 155 & 238 and apparently seen on BNDEP services. The saddest thing I would say is that many of it were lay up and de-register early. However, the unforgettable refurbished bus SBS73R and also the modified A/C bus SBS79A on which we will never forget. Anyway, I would say that SBS3719S & SBS3720L and those TRG buses like SBS80U & SBS3722G, that were long live survivors until the days had come to de-register. Nevertheless, I would say Goodbye to all Scanias and wish you Rest In Peace."

    GOODBYE, SCANIA N113CRB BUSES! =(
