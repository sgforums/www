  • SBS3624G
    19 Feb 05, 18:01
    Ways of being slow.

    1. Stop at bus-stop when nobody flagged it down. Opens door for 5s, then wait 1min for traffic to clear to move out. Common trick.
    2. Stop at pedestrian crossing to wait for people to cross, when there aint any. Also common.
    3. Slow down like hell at junctions. Very common. Less common, a few broke the record by stopping completely, let other cars cross.
    4. Behind no car, dun need to move at green light. 9620C did just that today. Except that it moved when the turn right sign was blinking, cos a taxi arrived, but it failed to cross. We know driver was aware cos he moved slowly to the intersection when he had the chance to move, then he braked, inexplicably. Deprived the other cars.
    5. Wait for ah pek to seat down then move off. Of course you can say they are considerate (I never pooh-poohed them), but it can also be called kill 2 birds with a stone. Or kill time if you like.
    6. Fiddle with the aircon. Most common.
    7. Wait for people running after bus. Falls under considerate and kill time category.

    Are there other ways, do say it out.
    I would like to thank svc 72 for all the methods stated above. I would not be here if not for it.
