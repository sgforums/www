    Audrey Tan. (2013, December 24). Bus services affected by the opening of the Marina Coastal Expressway. The Straits Times. Retrieved from http://www.straitstimes.com/

    The routes of four buses run by SBS Transit will be amended from Dec 29, when the Marina Coastal Expressway (MCE) opens.

    Bus services 133, 400, 402 and 502/502A will call at five new bus stops along Central Boulevard and Shenton Way. They will also no longer stop at four existing bus stops along Marina Station Road, Marina Street and Marina Place.

    Bus services 400 and 402 will also call at a relocated bus stop along Central Boulevard from its current stop at Marina Boulevard, SBS Transit said in a statement on Tuesday.

    The routes of another 11 bus services - 30/30e, express service 513 and nine premium services 538, 539, 544, 545, 552, 553, 554, 566 and 585 - will also be diverted to travel along the MCE instead of the East Coast Parkway. No bus stops will be skipped for these services.

    From what I see from the posters,

    Service 133 (towards Shenton Way) will be amended to ply Central Blvd and Shenton Way like Service 97/FFW 97e/Express 513. Return journey to Ang Mo Kio not affected.

    Services 400 and 402 to stop at Central Blvd bus stop (Bef Marina Way) where they previously ply non-stop, then turn left to Marina Way and utilise the complete Marina Boulevard instead of plying Marina Stn Rd and Marina St towards Marina Gdns Dr/Marina Coastal Dr. As Marina Blvd is a complete one-directional road, one of the bus stops will be relocated to the complete Central Blvd, thus both services will ply Central Blvd after Marina Gdns Dr/Marina Coastal Dr, then to Marina St and Marina Stn Rd (opposite direction), before plying Central Blvd and Shenton Way like Service 97/FFW 97e/Express 513.

    Express 502 will be amended to ply Central Blvd and Shenton Way as well in the same fashion as the services above. It will then ply Keppel Rd to join back its route along AYE. As such, Express 502A will terminate at Central Blvd (Downtown Stn).

    Express 513 is not going to ply MCE for now...rather it remains the same route, just a minor modification of the road name is shown on the poster - ECP and Sheares Ave.

No bus stops will be skipped for these services. Please refer to Annex B for the poster.

    Previously, route 513 (from Tampines) exited ECP at exit 17A (Marina Place). Now, route 513 (from Tampines) exits ECP at Sheares Avenue (which is similar to present-day Jurong West Avenue 2) but still plies the same route. Basically, I think the change to route 513 is only in the name of the road it travels on – it was previously ECP but now it is Sheares Avenue. The route stays the same.

    Currently, Central Boulevard is connected to Sheares Avenue in only one direction – towards East Coast. To get to Central Boulevard from Sheares Avenue, one has to go west to Marina Street before going east to Central Boulevard.

    In future, when the road network of Marina Bay area is completed, they will be connected in two directions. By then, route 513 (from Tampines) will be re-routed and would (most likely) go to Central Boulevard directly from Sheares Avenue – without going around Marina Bay MRT Station.

    For now, the route stays the same. 513 (from Tampines) will be re-routed in future when the finalized Marina Bay road network is completed.
