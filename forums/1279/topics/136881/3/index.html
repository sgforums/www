    Originally posted by SBS9889U:
    Malaysi registered buses uses regos just like their cars
    In Malaysia, vehicle regoes go by state. AFAIK, here are how vehicles in Peninsula Malaysia are registered:

      A*, AA* etc: Perak
      B*, BA* etc: Selangor
      C*, CA* etc: Pahang
      D*, DA* etc: Kelantan
      H (followed by state prefix): metred taxis, outstation cabs follow state-based rego.
      J*, JA* etc: Johor
      K*, KA* etc: Kedah
      M*, MA* etc: Melaka
      N*, NA* etc: Negeri Sembilan
      P*, PA* etc: Penang
      R*, RA* etc: Perlis
      T*, TA* etc: Trengganu
      W*, WA* etc: Federal Territory of Kuala Lumpur
      Z*, ZA* etc: Mindef vehicles
