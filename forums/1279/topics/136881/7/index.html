    PA16xx

    SP buses or cameos are driven by the original route BCs. They are normally used because their original buses are sent for repairs, regular maintenance checks, charters, cameos for other routes, etc.

    eg SBS1881T can be driven by anyone; SBS9026T cameo on sv9 will be driven by a sv9 BC
