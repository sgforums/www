    Originally posted by SBS1908B:
    What is the diff between the N113 chassis and the L113 chassis? Unless I am wrong, they are almost similar.
    The similarity is that both have an 11-litre engine. The engine on the N series chassis is mounted transversely (perpendicular to the chassis frame) in a vertical (upright) position. The engine of the L series chassis is mounted longitudinally (parrellel with the chassis frame) with the engine tilted at 30 degrees to the left. The K series chassis is similar to the L series except that the engine is in a vertical position.

    Here's a picture of an L113 engine compartment. The engine is in green.
