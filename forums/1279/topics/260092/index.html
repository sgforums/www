  • The_Bus_Guide
    11 Jun 07, 23:29
    To put it more specifically it is called a steered tag axle. Contrary to what I have believed previously, the wheels do not turn with the steering wheel. It merely 'follows' the direction of travel. This helps to reduce tyre wear as there is less force acting on the tyres while the bus turns. The noise and vibration from the B9/B10TL as compared to the LO/VO shows how difference it makes. However, I do not know to what extent the steered axle can turn.
