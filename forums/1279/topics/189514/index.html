    TIB353P-TIB361R (NAC Hinos), TIB380K-TIB389L (CAC Hinos) and TIB390G (CAC Nissan U31 SCN) were also transferred to CSS in 1996-1997. It was re-registered to TIB290T-TIB309A.

    When TIB405Z-TIB424T were re-registered to CSS270B-CSS289Z in 1996, most of its CSS routes were converted to PAC. Remembered tat service 605 got its DAF fleets in late 1996, followed by 602 and 607. 603 used mainly Hino and Nissan CACs, but had a DAF cameo before.

    CSS had progressively withdrew most of its services and at the same time phased out all the Hinos that had reached its lifespan of 15 years. After CSS 605 was withdrawn in 2004, it was the DAF fleets to be phased out by then. At the same time, 9 CAC Nissans were being transferred to SMRT and re-registered to TIB1248D-TIB1256E. The one and only DL245 on CSS fleet (CSS310U) was also transferred to SMRT and re-registered to TIB1257C.

    As of today, only Nissan U31 RCN (OAC and CAC) model is used to operate on CSS.
