    This post is meant to correct some errors that I found in sgwiki with regards to the duties for Intra-town 307 and feeder 302.

    1. Think I mentioned before that 307 only has 6 full AM/PM shifts, though it has 7 perm AM/PM buses. On any one day, one perm bus will not be on 307 coz the driver will be on rest. Pls kindly remove duty 307AP07, which does not exist. Similar to 302, only has 6 AP/PM shifts (inclusive BSEP) but 7 perm full AM/PM buses.

    2. SMB1329G is on duty 971ES104, not 307S102 as mentioned on sgwiki. Today SMB224G was on this slot and I managed to glance at the duty code.

    3. SMB3045L is on 302AP90, not 302S190 as mentioned in sgwiki. It will change shift at cck int at around 3pm everyday.
