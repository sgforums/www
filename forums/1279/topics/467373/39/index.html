    Hi mr jurongwestresident, 400 metres is generally used as a yardstick. The distance between 2 consecutive bus-stops length is 400-500 metres. So 400 metres or less is widely acceptable as within walking distance for a Normal working adult on a daily basis. Some people have bus stops at their doorstep. Some have it at less than 200 metres and some 400 metres. In general, most have a bus stop within 400 metres because distance between 2 stops is 400 metres generally. If you buy house next time and do not have a car, please keep a lookout for bus stops nearby as a guide.
