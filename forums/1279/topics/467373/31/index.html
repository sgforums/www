    SMRT had released another tender document with Tender No. QU14-008, dated 27 May 2014, for the sales of de-registered/scrapped buses.

    It mentioned about sales and de-registration of 130 units of scrapped buses, and an option to exercise the sales and de-registration of additional 126 units.

    The first batch of 130 buses mentioned contains up to 104 units of Model MBO 405 (12m) and 26 units of Model MBO 405G (Bendy) over the contract period from July 2014 to December 2014.

    The second batch of 126 buses mentioned contains up to 122 units of Model MBO 405 (12m) and 04 units of Model MBO 405G (Bendy) over the contract period from January 2015 to June 2015.

    Based on what it is mentioned above, it is confirmed that all of the remaining Hispano O405 (except 827P-832Z batches) and Hispano O405Gs regoed from 849B to 889K will all be de-registered during the time period above without lifespan extensions...do look out for more new buses to come in the following months.
