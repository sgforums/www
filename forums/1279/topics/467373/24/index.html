    I think there will be a very significant change to the public bus network in the Choa Chu Kang, Bukit Batok (areas along Bukit Batok Road), Jurong East/West (areas along Jurong West/East Avenue 1; I personally feel that there should be some rationalisation of bus resources, and some services should be re-routed to use Boon Lay Way or just bypass to Boon Lay via the expressway.), Wenya, Gek Poh (the addition of Joo Koon Bus Interchange will surely have some impact to the bus network here), Pioneer and Tuas areas, at around 2015.

    Why? Because

    • Joo Koon Bus Interchange opening.
    • Many new developments in Choa Chu Kang.
    • There has been publicly-known complaints about the bus services in Choa Chu Kang area.
    • Wenya is opening up.
    • SMRT will have a bus depot at Wenya - I guess LTA wants to try out the contracts model in public bus services, starting with the areas at and surrounding Wenya. Why? Because they already say the depot is developed by LTA and (only) leased to SMRT. So, there may be some "change in colours" due to change in bus operators.... 
    • Bukit Batok Road seriously needs review. the bus routes there may be affected.

    Until now, there is only one/two new BSEP bus route (excluding city direct) in the West area. I guess LTA is waiting for Joo Koon Bus Interchange and Wenya Bus Depot to open, and they are probably delaying the implementation of new BSEP bus routes in the west area.
