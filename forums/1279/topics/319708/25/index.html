    1. HGDEP had swopped a number of Volvo MK4s and batch 1 Scania K230UBs with ARBP/BBDEP in exchange for more batch 2 Scania K230UBs. After which some of the Scanias (especially those that were registered earlier) finally settled down on services 27, 156 and 163. The remaining ones were made up mainly of those newly registered 82xx series, Either some services will get Scania fleet addition or HGDEP might introduce WAB to another service soon (to add on that BCs of services 43, 85, 109, 158 and 325 went for WAB training). The ARBP/BBDEP Scania K230UBs are yet to be settled down, maybe to services 48, 151 or 156.
    2. SBS8265B SBS8269R SBS8270K SBS8273C should be perm service 39 already, but still unsure which buses are out.
    3. Service 36 is still a beginning stage to have Scania K230UB deployments, I believe that eventually service 36 will get more of them too. Some of the Volvo MK4s on service 36 might be redeployed to other BNDEP services which will be affected by the deregistering of the Scania N113CRBs.
    4. All the regos ending with "4" were not used for the registering of new buses ever since after SBS8074K met with an accident .
    5. Not sure about service 100, but I would think that SBS8242S is still a spare in HGDEP.
    6. Scania N113CRBs buses should either be laid up or being deregistered randomly. Don't forget that all Scania N113CRBs will be phased out very soon.
    7. I believe that those BNDEP's spare Volvo MK3s came from services 3, 39, 291 and 293 which already have new Scania K230UB deployments. Same for HGDEP which also have a lot of Volvo MK3/MK4 spares previously from services 27, 34, 156, 163, 165.
