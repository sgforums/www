Lousy Sv 26 frequency...

  • leeJZ
    28 Feb 04, 17:20
    Today I decided to go to Toa Payoh, and decided to use the Sv 23/26 combo instead of the direct Sv 8 service. Well we got out of Tampines Interchange at the same time as a Sv 8 bus, so I said great, lets see if I can beat that bus to Toa Payoh.

    Got off at Bendemeer Road (Blk 54) and waited. Two Sv 31 buses passed. So did two Sv 107 buses. 2 Sv 61 buses. 1 Sv 23 bus. About 2-3 other TIBS buses came. So did about 4-5 SBS buses of other numbers. Finally Sv 26 came....nearly 20 mins of waiting. Needless to say, the Sv 8 bus was already there when I got off at Toa Payoh.

    It was also like that last time. I was at Bendemeer Road waiting for a ride to Bedok. I waited for ages, dual or triple of other bus services had already came. I gave up and decided to hop on the next Sv 31 bus. Finally Sv 26 came, thankfully before the Sv 31 did or my ride home would be slower...

    I think the next time I go to TPY id take Sv 8 or do a Sv 23/Sv 8 combo...
