    Under SMRT Corp, there are many wholly-owned subsidiary companies managing their respective core competencies

    SMRT Buses Ltd takes care of bus operation

    SMRT Trains Ltd takes care of rail operation

    SMRT Light Rail takes care of Bt Panjang LRT

    SMRT Taxis takes care of taxis

    SMRT Automotives takes care of bus and taxi repair and maintenance

    SMRT Engineering takes care of rail repair and maintenance

    The same goes for ComfortDelgro

    So the underperformance in bus operation has got nothing to do with the train aspects.

    Be it train or bus, a duopoly is always healthier as it's in line with the objective of market liberalization. Government does not want any single player to monopolize the bus or train market.

    The concept of duopoly was introduced in 1983 when Singapore Bus Service was the sole bus operator. Primarily, the objective is to introduce another player to compete with SBS.

    The very same principle applies when government wanted Singapore Bus Service to rebrand itself as a multi-modal public operator SBS Transit to manage NEL in 2003

    The basis is to create a market consisting of two multi-modal operators
