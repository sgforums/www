    For me,
    SBST : Dennis Trident and Voith-ed DM 0405s especially. Others also include Mk4 DM3500, other 0405s and VO-3Ax ( esp the 96XX series )

    TIBS: Voith-ed 0405Gs, Scania ( both types )

    Fave seats:
    SBST
    VO3-Ax: Last seat lower deck left ( esp at night ) , first seat upper left, Middle to last 4 seats either
    VSO: Always the middle upper seat, side depends on sunlite and i will always choose the side with shade.....its the quietest at that position

    Volvo Single Decks: Middle, right
    For those mk4 striders with TV mobile, and if is under maintainance, then its the seat right after the rear exit. I avoid the last row at all costs.

    MB0405s: The seat just b4 the rear axle, right

    TIBS:
    Habits and "Citaros": Front: Any seat xcept the one after the driver, the face-face seats and the last seat b4 the articulator ( i would rather stand than to seat in that row!!! )
    Trailer: "Business Class" seats. ie the row after the articulator, left, and the one on the right with the "Emergency Exit" sticker

    Volgren 0405G : Front same as above, Trailer the seat after the 3rd door, left.....it has the widest pitch

    Box Bendy : same as the habits, xcept i like the 2nd seat b4 the 2nd door a lot

    0405: same as the SBST one, and the middle exit row

    WA Scania and DAFs: the second last row, or the exit row, either

    Lances: The 2 rows b4 the rear exit,.,...the pitch is just.......superb!!
