    Originally posted by hobochua:

    I've played the demo already now....so the results are...

    For those who loves to drive bus , like me, an absolute yes! Its very nice! U also get to open the doors by yourself. For those BC who loves to brakes hard, this game gonna deduct money from you if gonna do so, as braking hard causes discomfort to the passengers. Gearbox is automatic. Graphics is nice to me. Plus, theres also a timetable you got to follow. If you leave the bus stop too early, you will be given a penalty. If you are late, similarly, you are also given a penalty. Lights such as head lights, wipers during rain and all other things is all automatic. Only downside is, you cant see from the driver's view tho. Also, if you dont have a joy stick or steering wheel, it takes some skills to get some good turning and to avoid hitting objects as you drive, which also penalizes you if you hit any object. Basically, you just have to obey the traffic rules ( rules such as usage of turning lights is a must when changing lanes and you must stop at red lights.) and make sure you passengers have a smooth ride. Overall, nice game!
