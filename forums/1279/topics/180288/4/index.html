    1 Mar 06, 13:04
    Its not a fan. Its called a forced air ventilation system. What it does is take the air outside and force it through the vents through blowers. This is especially useful to circulate the air within the bus in times of rain when the windows are shut. If the windows are open, the effect is minimal but it still provides a certain degree of relief when the bus is still. Turning the blowers on/off is dependant on the driver.

    As for the fans on the Mk III, I believe these act like an exhaust fan, taking away the stale air inside the bus and improving the circulation. I've seen these fans working before. As with the blowers, these are turned on/off by the driver.
