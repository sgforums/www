    Originally posted by ^tamago^:


    low-profile tires for city omnibuses. volvo B10M's are coach chassis and uses higher-profile tyres.

    To answer exactly , high profile tires are there for higher speed and better cushion (more air inside bigger tires).

    Smaller tires are for lower speed, but better acceleration (lower tire rolling resistance).
