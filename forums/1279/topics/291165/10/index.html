    1. Its a K310UD, K = Vertically Mounted Engine, 310 = horsepower UD= Low Floor Double Decker. Similary, K230UB, is a low floor city bus having a 230hp vertically mounted engine. This is only applicable for the 5th series Scanias.

    2. Asking the obvious. All KMB "Golden buses" are wheelchair accessible, low floor buses. The first of these "Golden buses" was the Trident which was introduced 10 years ago.
