    a) When is the holiday schedule effective from and to when?

    c) Which services were not given the holiday schedule?
    d) A note on the letter indicated that Service 66 is an exception, given that [complete the sentence].
    There are 2 types of Holiday1 schedule - MOE school holidays and University school holidays. You as a secondary school student should know when are the MOE school holidays given you are in school for at least 8 years. University school holidays goes according to the academic calendar. It occurs from Mid-May to early-Aug and from early-Dec till mid-Jan.

    Services with Weekday schedule use Holiday1. Other services (like 174, 14) with Mon-Thu and Friday schedules use Holy1234 and Holiday5.

    There are still many services that do not use holiday schedule, primarily because it doesnt affect the journey time of the service. Some services include 2 6 15 19 26 36 45 48 57. Services that use Holiday1 schedule during the University school holidays are 95 96 151 179 199 242 243 255.

    Some services have no change in run-time during holiday1 schedule but still have holiday1 implemented because of other factors like xovers and such. One example is S238. S238's schedule is virtually the same but due to the absence of the S155 xovers during holiday1, they adjust some of the timings while leaving the rest unchanged.
