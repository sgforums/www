    As a frequent user of Sv156 for the past seven years, frequent meaning using it at least once per day, I would say that it is not really uncommon to experience the situation you mentioned.

    I have waited for 30mins plus minutes at the interchange for 156. Quite a few times.

    The timing for Sv156 can vary a lot in the morning. As long as it leaves Sixth Ave towards Sengkang later than 6.50am, then it will hit the massive jam at Bukit Timah road due to Nanyang Girls, Hwa Chong Institution, Raffles Girls Primary School National Junior College and Nanyang Primary. Near Nanyang Girls, a lot of students going to RGS will get on. Then after it slowly crawls past Adam road, then it would be SCGS that cause another massive jam. After reaching the bus stop pass Raffles town club, the RGS girls get off, and then a whole lot of people going to RJC gets on. Then at RJC everyone gets off, and then when you reach Bishan, there will be RI jam to deal with. So an hour could easily be added to the usual journey time. I guess for the return direction to Clementi, the main area which slows down the bus is at Whitely Road, and of course Bukit Timah Road.

    In my opinion, the frequency for Sv156 has been reduced, and is getting worse each year. E.g. I just came home, and 3 Sv151, 3 Sv153, 3 Sv105, and 3 Sv190, 2 Sv700 and 2 Sv960 passed me before Sv156 came.
