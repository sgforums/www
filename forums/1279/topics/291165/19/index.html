    SBS 9818 A (BR 56) --> (Am 56) --> (BN 30) --> (BN 30/C1) --> (BN 9/C2) --> (BN 31)
    SBS 9823 J (AM 25) --> (BN 196) --> (BN 196/C2) --> (BN 9/C2) --> (BN 9)

    SBS 9345 Y & SBS 9364 S swap with SBS 9378 C & SBS 9432 D when the 2 services S9 & S197 swap depot, meaning this 4 buses stayed respectively in hgdep and bndep when the services got transfered in Nov 2003.

    SBS 9378 C, SBS 9432 D & SBS 9587 R went to BR 28 in exchange for SBS 9803 S, SBS 9804 P & SBS 9805 L to BN 9. When CityBuzz started operations, SBS 9805 L exchanged with SBS 9813 M from BN 12, the other 2 VSOs did xover duty to C2 together with SBS 9823 J from BN 196. SBS 9803 S and SBS 9823 J then exchanged duties. SBS 9804 P then swap with SBS 9818 A.
