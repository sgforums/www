    Terms and Conditions for Transfer Rebates

    1. To enjoy a transfer rebate, a commuter must make a transfer within 45 minutes after tapping out of the bus or LRT/MRT. If the commuter failed to tap out when alighting from the bus, the 45 minutes is counted from the time of boarding of the last bus.
    2. Transfer Rebates apply to a maximum of three valid transfers on a journey which must be completed within two hours.
    3. For transfer between LRT and MRT, a transfer rebate is automatically given in one total entry and exit fare.
    4. No transfer rebate is given when a transfer is made between buses of the same service number.
    5. Transfer rebates do not apply to Chinatown Direct, Nite Owl and Stadium Direct services.
    6. The same ez-link card must be used throughout the journey to enjoy the transfer rebate.
    7. With the increase in transfer rebate, the maximum fare cap for a single bus journey will cease.
    8. Children/Students will enjoy:
      a) a 10 cents transfer rebate when transferring from a basic to
      another basic service of a different service number. Basic services refer to trunk and feeder services.
       
      b) a 10 cents transfer rebate when transferring from a basic bus service to an SBS Transit's Parks service.
       
      c) a 44 cents transfer rebate when transferring from a basic bus service to SBS Transit's Premium, CityShopper, Express or Fast Forward services.
       
      d) a transfer rebate of between 39 cents and 49 cents
      (depending on the fare band) when transferring from a train service to an SBS Transit's Fast Forward or Express busservice.

    * extracted from SBS Transit webby

    it means changing to any bus/MRT/LRT within 45 minutes from the last bus/MRT/LRT station you tapped out from.

    from 1st April all rebates are now 50 cents regardless of anything.

    Here is an example. All fares by ez-link card.

    Mr. A is travelling from Bedok North St 3 to Nanyang Drive at NTU.

    Mr. A boards a bus 225W at 8am at Bedok North St 3 and takes it to Bedok Int. He reaches Bedok Int and taps his card at 8.05am upon alighting and walks to the MRT.

    He transfers to the MRT at 8.10am where he will get a transfer rebate which means a deduction of 50 cents from his full fare as long as he transfers to the MRT within 45 minutes from the time he alighted from the earlier bus.

    He boards the MRT all the way to Boon Lay and reaches there at 9am. He then alights. He then decides to have breakfast at McDonalds in Jurong Point that stretched to 9.25am and walks to Boon Lay Interchange and boards a bus 179 at 9.35am. As it has been only 35 minutes, he will still be given a transfer rebate of 50 cents from the 179 fare also. He then reaches NTU Library 1 at 9.50am, and walks to his lecture venue right on time to attend a 10am lecture.

    Fare calculation

    Trip #1

    Service 225
    Bedok North St 3 - Bedok Int

    69 cents

    Boarded: 8am, alighted 8:05am

    Trip #2

    MRT Train
    Bedok MRT - Boon Lay MRT

    $1.77 (-50cents transfer rebate)
    $1.27

    Boarded 8.10am, alighted 9am

    Trip #3

    Service 179
    Boon Lay Int - NTU

    91 cents (-50 cents transfer rebate)
    41 cents

    Boarded 9.35am, alighted 9.50am

    Transfer rebates are not applicable for MRT to MRT transfers but MRT to LRT transfers are applicable. Alsom transfers from a bus to another bus of the same service number or subsidisary service, i.e sv 179 to 179A does not qualify for transfer rebate.

    The transfer rebate applies for up to 3 transfers made. Means you can take 4 buses in a row with 3 having transfer rebates but you must take the 4th bus/MRT within 2 hours from the start of your first trip.

    Hope this helps.
