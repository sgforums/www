    Old Voiths generally have a distinct scream/whistle or whatever you would call throughout the gears. Since all Voiths have only 4-gears, the engine has to work harder to get to the same speed as the 5-gear ZFs in the same time, needing the engine to get to a higher RPM thus making the engine sound louder. This video by glitchFan2428 will help you get a better gist of what I'm saying =)
