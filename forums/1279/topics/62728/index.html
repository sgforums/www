    AMENDMENT NOTICE ORIGINAL TEXT:
    Starting Sunday, 7 December 2003, Service 174 will be amended to ply along Chin Swee Rd/Outram Rd instead of Eu Tong Sen St/New Bridge Rd.

    With the amendment, Service 174 brings bus links closer to Outram Secondary School, flats along Chin Swee Rd, San Centre and Manhattan House.

    Route:
    towards Boon Lay: New Bridge Rd Terminal, New Bridge Rd, Outram Rd, Chin Swee Rd, Havelock Rd, Eu Tong Sen St and continue via its existing route.
    towards New Bridge Rd: from North Bridge Rd onwards, South Bridge Rd, Upp Cross St, Chin Swee Rd, Outram Rd, (New Bridge Rd) and New Bridge Rd Terminal.

    Stop along Chin Swee Rd located at:
    towards New Bridge Rd/ southbound: Opposite Block 13
    towards Boon Lay/ northbound: Between Blocks 8 and 13
