    SBS Transit will be trialling a King Long  diesel-eletric hybrid bus for a year, beginning mid 2011. The bus was brought in by King Long and ST Kinetics.     Diesel-eletric hybrid bus uses 23% less diesel than normal buses, and produces lesser exhaust smoke. There are many more advantages.
