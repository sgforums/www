    After repeatedly browsing through the 1985 Bus Map, I can make out the following for Service 8, Bedok to Marina Centre via Anson Road/Robinson Road, as stated in the Map itself. I'm not sure if the route was amended after 1985. Here goes:

    Bedok Interchange, Bedok North Ave 1, Bedok South Road, Bedok South Ave 1, East Coast Parkway, Keppel Road, Anson Road, Robinson Road, Collyer Quay, Fullerton Road, High Street*, St Andrews Road, Marina Centre**, East Coast Parkway, Bedok South Ave 1, Bedok South Road, Bedok Interchange.

    *Empress Place then was known as High Street
    **No indication in the map whatsoever how the route goes in Marina Centre, Service 8's route markings disappears at the junction of Nicoll Highway/Bras Basah Rd/St Andrews Road.

    Conclusion as of 1985: Service 8 did not pass Marina Centre twice. It exited from ECP at Keppel Road and enters back ECP from Republic Boulevard.

    Cheers and warm regards

    Originally posted by Yusry:
    Sounds plausible. I think Circuit Link did not exist then. Because even service 60 went by Aljunied Road to enter MacPherson estate before being amended to ply Geyland East Central.
    That is correct. Circuit Link didn't exist until 1986, methinks. There was a road named Lor Bengkok which connected Circuit Rd to Paya Lebar Rd (but not anymore). Buses passing through MacPherson Estate didn't use this road. Instead, they entered and exited at Aljunied Rd via Merpati Rd and Mattar Rd respectively. Hence, they used to ply the same set of roads in both directions and gave rise to the need of white and green destos.

    Firstly, Empress Pl is Empress Pl and High St is High St. Empress Pl is actually the area between Victora Theatre and the S'pore River while High St is a road behind Victoria Theatre.

    Service 8's route markings disappeared after Empress Pl because it ran express from there onwards. It went via Nicoll Highway, Mountbatten Rd, Fort Rd and ECP before exiting at Bedok South Ave 1. It didn't pass through Marina Centre in 1985 because there wasn't a Marina Centre in 1985. The route was amended to pass through Marina Centre in 1987.

    In 1985, it didn't exit at Keppel Rd but at Prince Edward Rd. Why? Because it called at a bus stop along Shenton Way in front of Marina Hse, looped at Keppel Rd and returned via Anson Rd.
