    History of Express Services

    Express 501

    Introduced in May 1997, it was a Peak hour one-way service. From Hougang Central interchange to Penang Rd in AM peak and from Anson Rd to Hougang Central interchange in PM peak. In 1999, extended to ply Marina Centre and converted to loop service. Short route (Express 501X) was introduced at the same time to loop at Marina Centre. In Sep 2000, extended to Sengkang interchange. Withdrawn on Sunday 27 Jul 2003, under NEL rationalisation phase 2.

    [edit]Express 502

    Introduced in May 1997. Between Hougang Central interchange and Boon Lay interchange. 
    In Sep 2000, extended to Sengkang interchange.
    On Sunday 27 Jul 2003, shortened to loop at Marina Centre starting from Boon Lay, under NEL rationalisation phase 2.
    On Saturday 02 July 2005, skipped the stop along Orchard Link and amended to call at the stop along Grange Rd.
    On 26 Dec 05, service was enhanced and extended to Pioneer Rd North and short trips were during peak hours, 502A and 502B with 502A skipping Jurong West/East Ave 1, Toh Guan Rd and Boon Lay way and 502B starting and ending at Jurong West Ave 1 skipping Pioneer Rd North, Jurong West Ave 5, St 75, St 64 and Boon Lay Ave.
    Double deckers were introduced back recently.
    On Sunday 15 Jun 2008, calls at an additional pair of bus stops along Jurong West St 64.
    On 23 Feb 2009, calls at additional bus stops along Soon Lee Road and Pioneer Road North. On 26 Apr 2010, calls at additional bus stops along Bayfront Ave and Marina St, skipping Raffles Ave, Stamford Rd and Orchard area. Service 502B is also withdrawn on the day itself.

    [edit]Express 503

    Introduced in May 1997. Peak hours one-way service. From Tampines interchange to Temasek Ave in AM peak. From Anson Rd to Tampines interchange in PM peak. In Sep 1999, amended and re-numbered Express 505.

    [edit]Express 505

    Re-numbered from Express 503 in Sep 1999. Loop service between Pasir Ris interchange and Jln Bukit Merah/Telok Blangah Rd. In Sep 2000, amended and re-numbered Express 518.

    [edit]Express 506

    Introduced in 1998. Between Jurong East interchange and Bedok interchange. In 2000, extended to Changi Airport PTB. On Sunday 15 Sep 2002, shortened back to Bedok interchange.
    On Sunday 12 Nov 2006, was extended to Upp East Coast Ter calling at additional bus stops along Bedok Nth Ave 3, New Upp Changi Rd & Bedok Sth Ave 3.

    [edit]Express 507

    Introduced in 1998. Peak hours one-way service. From Boon Lay interchange to Shenton Way in AM peak. From Anson Rd to Boon Lay interchange in PM peak. No route change. Withdrawn on Sunday 22 Aug 2004 due to poor demand.

    [edit]Express 508

    Peak hours one-way service. From Sengkang terminal to Penang Rd in AM peak. From Outram Rd to Sengkang terminal in PM peak. In Sep 2000, shortened to Hougang Central interchange with the extension of Express 501 and re-numbered Express 511.

    [edit]Express 511

    Re-numbered from Express 508 in Sep 2000. Peak hour one-way service. From Hougang Central interchange to Penang Rd in AM peak & from Outram Rd to Hougang Central interchange in PM peak. No route change. Withdrawn on Sunday 27 Jul 2003, under NEL rationalisation phase 2.

    [edit]Express 512

    Introduced in Sep 2000 with the extension of Express 502. Peak hour one-way service. From Hougang Central Interchange to Orchard Rd in AM peak & from Moulmein Rd to Hougang Central Interchange in PM peak. No route change. Withdrawn on Sunday 27 Jul 2003, under NEL rationalisation phase 2.

    [edit]Express 518

    Re-numbered from Express 505 in Sep 2000. Loop service between Pasir Ris interchange and Orchard Rd/Marina Centre. 
    On 26 Dec 05, service was enhanced to ply past Pasir Ris Dr 12 and covering more of Pasir Ris Dr 3. At the same time, short trips were introduced during peak hours, 518A and 518B with 518A skipping Tampines Ave 7, 2 and 1 while 518B starting and ending at Tampines Ave 7 skipping Pasir Dr 3, 12, 1 and Loyang Ave.
    On 26 Apr 2010, calls at additional bus stops along Temasek Ave and Bayfront Ave. Service 518B was also withdrawn on the day itself.

    [edit]Express 520

    Introduced in May 1997. Peak hours one-way service. From Yishun interchange to Outram Rd in AM peak. From New Bridge Rd terminal to Yishun interchange in PM peak. No route change. Withdrawn on Sunday 24 Aug 2003.

    [edit]Express 521

    Introduced in Sep 1999. Between Bukit Panjang terminal and World Trade Centre terminal (HarbourFront interchange). Charged normal trunk fares (for adults) during off-peak hours for a promotional period. On Sunday 29 Sep 2002, amended and extended to Woodlands Regional interchange, converted to trunk service and re-numbered service 963.
