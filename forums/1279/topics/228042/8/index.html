  • TIB1018B
    13 Mar 07, 23:22
    Originally posted by No_10_Tomas:
    the old svc 5 was never converted to partial aircon svc before it was withdrawn right?
    Former service 5 used to operate with full fleets of MB0305s and LAs. Volvo MK3s were adden in the fleet when SBS converted service 5 into a PAC service in December 1993.

    When TIBS took over service 5 in September 1995, Nissan U31 SCNs (NAC) and MB0405s (AC) were used by TIBS. DDs were withdrawn then. Deployment of TIBS buses on service 5 eventually became Hino HT328Ks (NAC) and WA DAFs (AC) over the years before it was withdrawn in December 1999. By the way service 5 was never converted to a FAC service at all.
