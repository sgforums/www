    On 21 March 2004, this coming Sunday:

    The Singapore fans of Taiwanese idols 5566 chartered a Bendy Bus to carry all 140 fans to chase after their idol.

    The bus had it's EDS programmed to "5566 Starland", which is the name of the fan club.

    The bus will transport the fans to 2 official events which include stamping of the limited edition 5566 ezlink cards. The events will be held beside Jurong East MRT station (2.30pm) and at West Mall (5pm).

    The above information is taken from Lian He Wan Bao.
