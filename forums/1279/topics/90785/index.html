  • tranquilice
    25 Aug 04, 09:53
    Originally posted by nemo77:
    seems like all these services linked to the north onli hor...
    The reason is simple. Looking at the MRT map, you will find that to get from the north to the east and vice-versa, you will have to go via City Hall station. This is a waste of time.

    LTA originally has proposed to build the North Coast MRT line, which will links the north and the east via the north-east. But now it is not feasible due to under-developed areas along the route and so the bus companies were allowed to introduce routes like services 39, 161, 168, 858, 969 between these areas via the expressways.
