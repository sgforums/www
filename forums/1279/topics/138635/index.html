  • ^tamago^
    24 Jun 05, 00:01
    2 new Scania L94UB's have arrived in Singapore for HippoTours. They are Pinkie (PA5420T) and Purpee (PA5418B).

    Pinkie will go on an exclusive route into Sentosa via Orchard.

    Purpee will go to Marine Parade, Katong and Geylang Serai.

    These rides will commence sometime in July.

    An attractive promotion will be launched when these 2 buses come into service, together with a small re-branding of the Hippo&Duck comnined tours (the name HippoTours and WackyDuck will remain). A good time to try the L94UB's again.

    Stay tuned for more information.

    There were only services A, B, C, E and M.

    A - WTC to Siloso Rd (Underwater World)
    B - Tiong Bahru to Allanbrooke Rd (former Central Beach)
    C - Tiong Bahru to Garden Ave (Ferry Terminal)
    E - Sentosa to Orchard and Marina Centre
    M - Tiong Bahru to Beach View (former Sunset Bay)

    When service M was withdrawn, service C was extended to cover its route along Beach View.
