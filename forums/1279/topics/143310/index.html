    Short-working Trip (SWT):
    Starts trip from interchange/terminal or somewhere along the route, ends trip at somewhere further down the route but not interchange/terminal unless otherwise stated. SWTs have bus stop plates along their routes to inform passengers of their existences.

    Down-route:
    Starts trip from somewhere along the route, ends trip at interchange/terminal. Down-routes do not have bus stop plates along their routes to inform passengers of their existences.

    Hope this gives a clearer picture.
