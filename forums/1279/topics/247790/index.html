    MCW was not bought over but sold off piece by piece. In 1989, the Laird group, former owners of MCW, decided to sell their bus and rail divisions. There was no buyer for the complete group so each product was sold seperately. MCW's bus design was sold to Optare and DAF while the rail division went to Alstom.
