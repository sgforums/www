  • PJ_Quek
    12 Aug 05, 14:08
    Originally posted by Altis84:
    well i need to get from bishan to boon lay interchange. Well currently mrt is the only way i know which takes ard 1 hr.. is there any way i can make use of any expressbuses or something to reduce my travelling time? Thanks. I dun mind taking mrt to certain station and change a bus too.
    Option 1. 156 CJC xfer 985 Upper Bukit Timah Rd/Jalan Jurong Kechil xfer 174 Boon Lay

    Waiting time could be quite long for 985. At Upper Bukit Timah Rd/Jalan Jurong Kechil, there's 157 to Boon Lay also, but it will be quite long winded with many bus stops and traffic junctions thru Jurong East/West Ave 1. 174 use Old Jurong Rd, which much less traffic, thus, faster.

    Option 2. 156 CJC xfer 506 Bukit Batok xfer MRT or 174 Boon Lay

    Option 3. 156 Bukit Timah Rd xfer 174 Boon Lay

    Option 4. 156 Bradell Rd xfer 157 or Lornie Rd xfer 157/74/852 (have to walk to another bus stop) Bukit Timah Rd xfer 174 (this option is rather similar to Option 3, only difference is that it skips Thomson Rd and PIE/Whitley, and shorten the journey between MacRitchie Rsvr. and Bukit Timah, waste $ though )

    Option 5: 156 Bradell Rd xfer 157 Boon Lay
