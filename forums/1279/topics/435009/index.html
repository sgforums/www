Suggestion to improve NightRider services

  • TIB1010Y

    Hi,

    I personally think that the following bus service can be enhance to better serve the commuters:

    Terminate all NR services at respective town areas on the journey back to city after 1am due to low demand during midnight. It means that all NR service will be 1-directional from city after 1am.

    Perhaps all NR service should start from Resorts World Sentosa.

    NR1

    Break up into 2 services, NR1 will serve Ang Mo Kio and Yishun via Moulmein Road, CTE - express after Newton Road. This will reduce travelling time for commuters. To better serve Ang Mo Kio Town, it wil serve Ang Mo Kio Avenues 5, 8, 1, 3, 4, 5, 6 and continue its original route to terminal at Yishun.

    New NR service will cover the City area and serve Toa Payoh and Bishan. It will ply the original NR1 route from Thomson Road onwards. It will serve Toa Payoh Lorong 2, 3, 4, East, Lorong 7, 6 and continue its original route to Bishan before terminating at opposite block 302 along Marymount Road.

    (Will serve Bishan Street 21 and back to original and new route on its journey back to City)

    NR2

    It will be enhance to better serve Woodlands and Sembawang. It will serve SLE – express, Woodlands Avenue 2, 1, Centre Road, Admiralty Road, Marsiling Drive, Road, Rise, Woodlands Avenue 9, 4, 5, 6, 9, Gambas Avenue, Sembawang Avenue, Road, Montreal Link, Drive, Wellington Circle, Canberra Road, Admiralty Link, Sembawang Drive.

    (Will serve Sembawang Avenue and back to original and new route on its journey back to City)

    NR3

    Service will start from city. It will better serve Bukit Panjang and Choa Chu Kang. It will serve Petir Road, Pending Road, Bukit Panjang Ring Road, Fajar Road, Bukit Panjang Ring Road, Jelapang Road, Bukit Panjang Ring Road, Bukit Panjang Road, Choa Chu Kang Road, Teck Whye Avenue, Choa Chu Kang Drive, North 7, Crescent, Way, Avenue 4, 3, 2, Way. It will terminate at block 113 along Choa Chu Kang Way.

    (Will serve Choa Chu Kang Road towards Bukit Panjang and back to new route on its journey back to City)

    NR5

    Break up into 2 services, NR5 will serve Jurong East/West via CTE/AYE/Jurong Town Hall Road/Street 11(Express) after Outram Road. This will reduce travelling time for commuters.

    New NR service will cover the City area and serve Bukit Merah, Queenstown and Clementi. It will ply the original NR5 route from Tiong Bahru Road onwards. To better serve Clementi Town, it will serve Clement Road, Avenue 5, Commonwealth Avenue West,  Clementi Avenue 2, West Coast Road, West Coast Highway, West Coast Link(Express) and terminate at block 726 along West coast Road.

    (Will turn back to Clementi Avenue 2 towards MRT and continue its new route on its journey back to City)

    NR6

    Break up into 2 services, NR6 will serve Serangoon and Hougang via CTE after Orchard Road. It will reduce travelling time for commuters. To better serve Serangoon and Hougang it will serve Ang Mo Kio Avenue 1, Lorong Chuan, Serangoon Avenue 2, Upper Serangoon Road, Serangoon Central, Yio Chu Kang Link, Road, Serangoon North Avenue 1, 4, Hougang Avenue 9, 8, Upper Serangoon Road, Hougang Avenue 5, 7, Tampines Road, Hougang Avenue 3, 1, Upper Serangoon Road, Hougang Avenue 2. It will terminate at opposite block 634 along Hougang Avenue 2.

    (Will serve Yio Chu Kang Road towards CTE and continue its new route on its journey back to City)

    New NR will cover the City area and serve Sengkang and Punggol. It will serve CTE/Ang Mo Kio Avenue 5 to reduce travelling time for commuters. It will serve Buangkok Green, Drive, Punggol Road, Compassvale Drive, Anchorvale Dr, Link, Sengkang East Way, Rivervale Drive, Punggol Road, Field, East, Central and terminate at opposite block 296 along Punggol Road.

    (Will serve Punggol Road towards Sengkang and continue its new route on its journey back to City)

    NR7

    Break up into 2 services, NR7 will serve Bedok and Simei via CTE/PIE after Orchard Road. This will reduce travelling time for commuters. It will serve Jalan Eunos, Bedok Reservoir Road, North Road, New Upper Changi Road, Bedok South Avenue 1, South Road, South Avenue 3, (New Upper Changi Road, Upper Changi Road East – express), Simei Road, Street 1, 3, 4. It will terminate at Simei Green along Simei Street 4.

    (Will turn back to Simei Road towards Bedok and continue its new route on its journey back to City)

    New NR service will cover the city area and serve Tampines and Pasir Ris  via CTE/PIE after Orchard Road. It will serve Tampines Avenue 1, 4, 3, Street 83, Avenue 5, 4, Street 21, 23, Avenue 7, Loyang Avenue, Pasir Ris Drive 3, 12, 1. It will terminate at block 210 along Pasir Ris Drive 1.

    (Will turn back to Loyang Avenue towards Tampines and continue its new route on its journey back to City)

    NR8

    It will better serve Holland Road, Ulu Pandan Road, Clementi Road, (PIE – express) and continue its route to Bukit Batok.
