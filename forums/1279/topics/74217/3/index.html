    Here are more numbering sequences.

    The 3x series were formally reserved for Bedok / Chai Chee services.

    30 - Jurong
    31 - Kallang Park
    32 - Upp Cross St
    33 - Kent Ridge

    The 6x series were once used for services at the now defunct MacPherson Estate Terminal

    60 - Shenton Way
    61 - Ulu Pandan
    62 - Rumah Tinggi
    63 - Labrador (later to Bt Merah)
    64 - Mei Chin Rd
    65 - Lower Delta

    The 9x series were formally allocated to Paya Lebar Airport services
    90 - C'wealth Ave
    91 - Prince Edward (later cut short to Geylang Lor 1)
    92 - Alexandra Rd (later extended to Clementi)
    93 - Labrador (later extended to WTC)
    94 - This was the odd one out as it originally plied from Sims Pl - Shenton Way, but not anymore.
    95 - Ulu Pandan (later extended to Clementi)

    30x were for Blue Arrow Services while the 4xx series were meant for special services.
