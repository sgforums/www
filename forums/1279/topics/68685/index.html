  • ^tamago^

    04/02/04

    To facilitate Thaipusam Religious Procession, the stretch of Clemenceau Avenue between River Valley Road and Penang Road (in the direction towards Penang Road) will be closed from 9pm on Wednesday, 4th February 2004 to 2am on Friday, 6 February 2004. Services 64, 123, 143 and 143 will be diverted and not call at two bus stops along Clemenceau Ave (04311 Sri Thendandayuthapani Temple and 08011) during the affected period.

    At the same time, a bus stop along Prinsep Street (08079 H0otel Rendezous) and a stop along Serangoon Road (07211 Sri Srinivasa Temple) will be skipped to facilitate this event. Services 21, 23, 64, 65, 66, 125, 130, 131, 139, 147 and 166 will not call at the affected bus stops. Passengers can board/alight at the nearest bus stop.
