    This bus interchange was shut down in early 2004 as part of the NEL rationalisation. The building is currently still there, now known as Kovan Hub.

    The sawtooth berth has turned into a bus stop for services 112, 113, 119, as well as a terminal point for service 115. There's also an express coach that goes to Malaysia from Kovan Hub too.

    The end-on berth side has now turned into an open space carpark.

    Hougang South interchange was also known as Hougang interchange in the 80s. There was another Upp Serangoon Terminal located at the road end. It was in Feb 1995 when another new Hougang Central bus interchange was built to replace this Upp Serangoon Terminal. In order to prevent confusion, Hougang interchange was renamed to Hougang South.

    Upp Serangoon Terminal was closed on 11th Dec 1994 with the opening of Hougang Central Bus Interchange. It was an expansion of its bus services in the Hougang areas, as the Hougang Bus Interchange does not have much parking space to accommodate more bus services.

    Initially, they want the interchange to be shut down in Nov 2003, but was given a deadline extension up till 15th Feb 2004 to vacate the premises for development purposes.
