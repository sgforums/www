    There's another advice I would like to give...

    Sometimes, SBS Transit would give last minute extensions; e.g. If a bus' lifespan expiry date is on 12 March 2012, either it will get its extension on 13 March 2012 (the next day), or reflected as de-registered for a while before being reflected as lifespan extended. This is a case of the Volvo Olympians in the past with real dates. Link is here.

    Although since then, there hasn't been any case of last minute lifespan extensions lately...so maybe the last few batches of Volvo Olympians Batch 2 would have been gone case.
