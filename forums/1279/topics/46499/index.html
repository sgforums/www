  • tranquilice

    New bus stops can be set up along some expressways which do not have any bus stops, purpose as a transfering point. Some examples:-

    1. A pair of new stops along the ECP between Fort Rd (Tanjong Rhu Flyover) and Benjamin Sheares Bridge, for passengers of services 30, 36 & 518 to transfer to one of the other services. (Eg. Board service 30 at HarbourFront, alight at this stop, transfer to service 36 to Changi Airport)

    2. A pair of new stops along the TPE between Pasir Ris Dr 12/Tampines Ave 10 (Api Api Flyover) and Tampines Rd (Halus Flyover), for passengers of 53, 81, 168, 858 & 969 to transfer to one of the other services. (Eg. Board service 858 or 969 at Yishun, alight at this stop, transfer to service 53 or 81 to Pasir Ris)

    What do you think?
