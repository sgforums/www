    Bodywork: Integral from Alexander Dennis
    Gearbox:  (Voith???)

In conforming with HK ruling EDS still shows yellow colour. Some meesages: " Lighter, cleaner and greener"  "innvoation with style"  "Alexander Dennis logo"  " Enviro 400"

    I am sure the Hong Kong fans had a nice time snapping away on road test day.

    As with the B7RLE the emer exit door is fully black.

     

    Also noticed is bull bar, a first for Hong Kong buses. Bull bar is sued to keep branches of low trees from hitting the bus too hard. bull bar will get the full impact and prevent damage to bus. The Bull bar is a common feature of Enviro buses in Uk and Canada and USA.

    The windows are modified to suit and conform to the Hong Kong bus fleet, no roudned windows.

    The front door egg shape styling is retained for this Enviro 400.

    Its called the Transport Department in hk, in short TD.

    And no, the emergency exit door is not a TD requirement, it is an initiative by KMB to provide a larger exit for emergency and so that kids dont play with the emergency exit. It is fully made of a piece of glass (use the hammer to break)

    They even came up with a press release just for this: http://www.kmb.hk/en/news/press/archives/news20090811465.html
