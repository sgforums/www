    topic; I love ZF cat calls

    yes sir. i love Zf ccatcalls.

    however in Singapore only one bus model produce this unique sound.
    the Scannia k230UB. this translates to me liking the k230Ubs.

    the cat call will only happen once the bus manages to shift all the way to 6.

    i'm not to sure how k230Ub shifts bitbut da it will do the following (in my opinion)

    engage D. move into first gear
    a slight jerk indicates torque converter then another slight jerk n change in engine tune to 2nd gear.IF only one jerk if torque converter sleep or slip gear occurs. at this point use Zf howl to decide if third gear...

    during the 1st and 2nd gear changes passengers will tend to feel the bus suddenly chu power n jerk around. is not driver fault. its all due to slip gears and torque converters

    a loud Zf howl to signify third gear now

    at third gear a sudden change in tone to 4th gear
    at 4th the tone will go up then a 'hub reduction' type of noise will happen. this noise is also noticeable in the b7RLE when it hits 5th gear..

    if the bus still shits, it means the hub reduction like sounds will disappear and the bus engages in optimal gear 6th. at this stage he engine will be in low pitch hum. Because the suspension of k230Ub abit hard, i dun get the floating sensation like SJE L113 sounding type scanias.

    the bus will eventually have to engage the reatarding sequences. before that some sort of locking sensation to the gearbox must happen.

    THIS is where the catcall happens.

    - the first time i heard it i thought got stray kitten on bus.
    note: some scanias do not display the above charcteristic because
    1) : Radiator too loud.
    2) the ZF whistle is obscuring the background engine noise. like 8080. i am so weak i cant detect any gear shifts at this point aas the whsitle pitches differently from engine pitch.
    3) the Zf whistle turns into a police car siren wailing and u get distracted by the police siren noise and ignore the rest of sounds. (SBS8067)

    hope u guys enjoy catcalls and Zf6speed gearboxes like i do

    ZYX
