    If you happen to miss 985, try this:

    Option 1
    Take 966 and transfer to
    190 at Bukit Panjang Road (Crossover overhead bridge @ Bukit Panjang Road)

    OR

    Option 2
    105, 151, 153, 154 and transfer to
    190 at Whitley Road (Crossover overhead bridge @ Whitley Road)

    985 loops too much, so it takes a while to reach PJC. You can try walking a bit. Takes lesser time, especially option 2 above.

    I assume you're taking bus from opposite Trellis Towers (BS 52079)

    Your options are :

    105/151/153/154 to Whitley Rd (Aft Tanglin CC - BS 40239).

    journey time from opp Trellis Twrs to Whitley Rd is roughly 5-7 mins at 6+ in the am.

    Cross the bridge to Raffles Town Club (BS 40231) and get on 190. 190 arrives at RTC at these timings: (approximate)

    0628 0638 0648 0658 0708

    From experience the 0638 bus gets you to Teck Whye Ave by 0705h. Factor in the 10 minute walk to school and you reach at 0715.
