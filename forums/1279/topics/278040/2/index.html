    ok i received your message , you can ask the driver to alert you when it reaches Blk45 Marine Crescent , it is in a small road , for you to know how to walk , you can go to http://streetdirectory.com and key in East Coast Park , you'll surely see Blk 45 , print it out & bring it along , you'll never go wrong with it. The second thing is alot of people take 13 to east coast park due to its short walking distance , perhaps you may follow them. I can give you a rough hint here but its preferred that you go to the street directory website above bold.

    After alighting at Marine Crescent , (a U-shape road) cross the road & under the flats , you'll definitely see the expressway , the underpass is just there or you may listen for the noise of an expressway. Anymore doubt you can go to the street directory website , it will help you the most. [/url]

    According to how to see roads , unless you're illiterate , you sit at the left of the bus & look for the stop " Marine Crescent" & alight or ask driver to inform you when it reaches the stop.
