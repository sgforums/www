    Originally posted by SBS5010P:

    Wab buses controlled by hgdep kena downgrade leh..

    Let me give you some hard facts for that statement:
    S27: Had full K fleet since WAB certification, no fleet change since
    S34: Had full K fleet since WAB certification. Had fleet add (2 Ks) when 34A was introduced
    S43: Had K + 4 B10M since WAB certification. Had fleet add (1 K) recently.
    S51: Full B9 CDGE as WAB fleet. Gradually increased to have Ks (replacing B10Ms) when WAB quota increased. Recently had additions of Wrights (replacing LOs).
    S64: Full K fleet (and non-WAB DDs). No fleet change since (not even to B9 CDGEs or Wrights)
    S80: Full B9 CDGE as WAB fleet. Gradually increased to have Ks (replacing B10Ms) when WAB quota increased. However, due to CCL there was fleet reduction of non-WAB buses (VOs) as well as converting VOs to B10Ms.
    S113: Full K + 2 B10M as WAB fleet. Had fleet reduction (B10M) & replaced a B10M from a K (that bus went amdep)
    S147: Full B9 CDGE as WAB fleet. Gradually increased to have Ks (replacing B10Ms) when WAB quota increased. Had fleet reduction (VOs)
    S151: Full K + 4 CDGE (rest B10Ms) as WAB fleet. No fleet change since

    So for that statement, only S113 makes the cut. So dont generalise hgdep downgrades WAB certified services.
