    This thread is to discuss Changi Airport bus infrastructure and services.

    Current routes serving Changi Airport are:

    24: Ang Mo Kio Interchange

    27: Hougang Central Interchange

    34: Punggol Interchange

    36: Tomlinson Road (loop)

    53: Bishan Interchange

    858: Woodlands Regional Interchange

    * All of the above routes have very high loading and are extremely winding and long, prone to delays

    With Changi Terminal 4 coming up, LTA really needs to figure out how it wants to manage the Changi Airport bus infrastructure and restructure some of the bus routes.

    Thoughts?
