    2 ways-

    1.
    506 to Bt Batok Rd. Cross the road.
    180 to Bt Panjang.

    Use this if you dont mind paying expensive express fares.
    Total time taken should be abt 1hr 15 min if you're lucky with their waiting times, esp 506.

    2.
    25/854 to Eunos MRT Stn bus stop. Transfer to 966.
    966 to CJC bus stop. Change to any other bus except 985.
    105/132/151/153/154/156 to Whitley Rd (Outside Whitley NPP). Cross the overhead bridge. Transfer to 190.
    190 to Bt Panjang Rd.

    Use this if you dont mind the multiple transfers.
    Total time taken should be abt 1hr 20-30 min if i nv calculate wrongly. Also, that is if you're lucky with the waiting time of 966.

    Enjoy the ride
