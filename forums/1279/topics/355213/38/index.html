    My review on Euro V: 

    Bus Taken: (EDIT)SBS8890X (Service 196: VS-Bedok Int)

    Seats: Undoubtably increased and taken the PIW slot, not much difference from previous batches, only comment is the new granny seat is excellent, can see BC driving plus get a full road view, only the handle is made up of metal a little odd.

    Suspension: Improved, bus does not shake so unnessary, however much work is needed to bring more comfort to the travel quality on KUB

    EDS: Clearer, and the mords are differinated from the others, and the new position of Side EDS let passengers see clearly, will not be block by people boarding the bus

    Gearbox. Although is a ZF 6 speed, it is still a little underpowered and cannot give the bus the push to go faster, and the gearbox sound like very roaring, should be slienced or at least like strider style, more quiet bus environment

    Overall: Not a great improvement, still okay ride.

    Improvements that should be made: Squeeze a additional row of seats at back potion like OC500LE, allow the side facing seats to be front facing, safer.......Improve suspension
