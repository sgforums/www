    Every 1000th rego with the last check alphabet, will end with the same letter as 999.

    Eg. SBS6999E then 7000 will be SBS7000E and TIB999B and 1000 will be TIB1000B.

    For the 100th rego, skip 8 letters.

    Eg. SBS1799Z then 1800 will be (XTRMKHEC - 8 letters) = SBS1800C.
