  • ak4711
    9 Apr 09, 12:15

    Got on to 8576D by chance on 3 today. First thing I saw was the huge Hanover EDS and Punggol Field. Inside the interior is somewhat more pleasing on the eye than the Euro IV and i dare say it's one of the best interiors in the SBS fleet. Also, the engine is also quiet at the front

    Took a seat at the back of the bus and when it went, I heard a faint turbo whistle and in my opinion the gear changes are faster, and so is the acceleration. BN may have tuned the engine because sometimes the engine hit high revs. However, unlike the Euro IV, when it revved, it didn't have the sudden vibrations. At lower revs the engine had some sort of a rattling sound but it went away when it hit higher revs. Also when it came to stop at a traffic light, it went to lower revs in neutral. Good for the environment and fuel economy.
