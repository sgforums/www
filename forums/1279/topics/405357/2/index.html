    Originally posted by NoTrainNearHome:

    Is there stilll a max fare e.g. previously if u stick to SBS, the fare is cap at about 1.8 or 1.9 ?

     

    Yes, if:

    1. your journey has a maximum of 6 trips
    2. you transfer within 45minutes between alighting and boarding
    3. first boarding to last boarding is within 2 hours
    4. your journey consists of a single rail entry and exit
    5. the fare for the rail portion of your journey consists of solely travel on NSL and EWL
    6. the other trips of your journey consist of only SBST and SMRTB basic bus services (trunk, feeder, industrial, townlink, intratown, but not express, FFw, parks, premium, chinatown, stadium, nite owl, nightrider)

    The fare cap is $1.94, and you pay that fare once your cumulative distance exceeds 40.2km.

    Originally posted by ignoramuses:

    May I ask how does the 3.2km fare cap for intratown services work?

    1. Transfer to intratown 811 from MRT

    2. Tranfser to MRT from intratown 811

     

    Thanks

    let say, you take MRT from Toa Payoh to Yishun (12.2km at S$1.33).. then you transfer to 811 at interchange... you can take 811 from start to end of the journey (i.e from interchange to interchange, which is 15.9km), you will be billed only 12.2km+3.2km(max distance charge for feeder/intratown) =  S$1.49 (instead of 12.2km+15.9km).

    let say, you take 811 from interchange and alight at Yishun Blk 289 (2.1km covered for 811), you will be billed for 12.2km+2.1km = 14.3km which is S$1.45...

    likewise, if you take 811 first... if the distance covered on 811 is more than 3.2km, only 3.2km will be added to the total journey travelled. i.e if you decide to take from interchange to interchange before taking MRT to Toa payoh, you will be billed 3.2km (not 15.9km) +12.2km = 15.4 km at S$1.49....
