    one of my fav method is my nodal spotting method. find out where most merc services go to what bus stops. then in ur planning draw up the bus stops together then decide how u cna travel from node to node easily and also clearing off one node off at a time.

    most nodes will share same buses.

    for exmple in m cse now the node for B9 currenly i go is HBF coz 143 n 80 new new B9s all there. then i also note down in my head which rego cd possibly come on 143 or 80. also if i hit a 80 bus i can go to another node call clarke quay MRT n hunt again o if another 80 comes along take it.

    or if i hit bus 143 i cna go to nodal point like orcahrd or the peoples park there for more B9 hutning. or stick to bus 143.

    also try not to limit urself to bus int as nodal points. its the out there kind of bus stop that gives more variety n surpises n kicks. i not saying int got no surpises. but possibly limited ur chances down.
