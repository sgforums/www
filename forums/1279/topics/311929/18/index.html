    Notes:
    1) Most of the MB O405 based under AMDEP were awaiting laid up and de-registered earlier than the lifespan expiry.
    2) SBS310G was used as a Museum Bus by NHB. The bus was repainted into SBST Livery when the bus no longer used for that purpose. It serves on its revenue service under AMDEP for a few months before going to lay up.
    3) SBS138M and SBS468K were converted for special purposes. SBS138M is now Drug Busters Bus & SBS468K is Molly Bus by NLB.
    4) SBS456U and SBS532H was burnt in the Great AMDEP fire in 1999.
    5) Other buses that have been de-registered can be found at the De-registration of Buses of the SGWIKI website (http://www.sgwiki.com/wiki/De-registration_of_Buses).
    6) Should there be any mistakes on my list, please point out the mistake and I will correct them.
