    I may not have the chance to get behind the wheel again, but I will love to take the rides round Singapore.

    The MB O405 is an excellent bus, good turning radius. Good brakes. Comfortable ride, except for the rocking horse effect after taking road humps (normal for all rear engine buses).

    When will SBS start adding some new exciting models??? I was in Germany recently, and I got myself a ride on the MB Citaro. The engine sounds like the SMRT OC500. Could be using the same engine. The acceleration is superb (tachometer show shifting at around 2400rpm). I counted 6 shifts.

    SMRT drivers are not maximising the OC500 accelerating power.

    The pedals are totally different from the current MB O405 (Webasto floor mounted brake pedal, and floor mounted gas pedal). The new Citaro has pendant pedals like cars, or just like KUB.
