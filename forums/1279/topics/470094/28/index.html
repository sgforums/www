    If you remember my post 2 months back, I had posted about sv 72. Last year, if you saw a sv 72 bus at YCK Interchange or at Tampines Interchange, each bus would leave the interchange with minimum 60-70 pax at first stop. This year when you observe, especially at Tampines, every bus leaves with only 40 pax and that is because of the huge capacity add from 21 buses to 27 buses and also capacity upgrade. There were 18 DDs, 3 SDs last year June, this year June you have 27 DDs doing sv 72. Of course, that has really reduced the passengers per bus.

    To add to it 116 coming before 72 of course further reduces the loading between HG Ave 9 and HG Int. If 72 goes before 116, you know that 116 will have single digit pax on board.

    At the end, I guess, we just need to be happy that the services have been really improved to an extent that you will not observe the same high loading on them like last year.
