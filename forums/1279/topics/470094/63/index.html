    7 Oct 13, 20:20
    Originally posted by BusAnalayzer:

    I don't get it... why is AMDEP / BRBP replacing its KUBs with Citaros. This seems to have become the new trend for AMDEP / BRBP management.

    It's down to the ease of deploying SP buses for the depot ops. Compared to Citaros, practically all the BCs in AM/BR are familiar with how to drive the KUBs given their much larger numbers than the Citaros. On the other hand, not many BCs other than those svcs with alr-perm Citaros (Sv 24, 70, 72, 53 etc) are familiar with driving Citaros. This is also the reason why you see those SP Citaros in AM/BR all along sticking to those svcs with alr-perm Citaros more often than other svcs~

    So if the starter wants to deploy a SP bus to replace another MIA bus, the KUB is easier to deploy out because the BCs are more familiar with it than the Citaros. 

    So they rather take out the KUBs from their respective perms to SP, than to face a restricted number of svcs to deploy their SP Citaros when the need arises.
