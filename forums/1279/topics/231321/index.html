    This was the problem when SBST withdrew service 85 during the NEL rationalisation. A lot of students in Sengkang and Hougang depended on 85 to go to school, but the usage level was not captured because students only tap in but never tap out. So SBST assumed nobody needed 85 and withdrew the service. In the end, students and parents complained, and SBST finally introduced 119 to cover part of the old 85 route......
