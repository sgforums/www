    grandeur, if things can be done so easily, it'd have been done long ago.

    the CTE/Lor Chuan height limit is due to the low flyover. there is no way you could get around that without massive disruption or implications to safety.

    there is no way we can assist u if u dun provide a service number but generalise. there are reasons why certain buses got double decks and others don't.

    the other reason why sixth avenue did not have DDs is due to its slopes. even SBST and SMRTB use only the best possible buses for these routes (10-litres for 156 and 11/12-litres for 77 and 970) to make sure they can climb the slopes. the reasoning is the same for kent ridge crescent in NUS.

    DD's below 4.3m is not feasible at all. and if we were to all use DD, frequencies will have to become longer to prevent brunching, and travel time will be lengthened unnecessary due to large passenger movement. a bendy will have very the same issues a DD faces even with an additional door.
