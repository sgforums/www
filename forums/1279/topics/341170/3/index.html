Buses are SBS 9231S for Green plate (3E) and SBS 9355T for White plate (53E). I was surprised when I learnt about it and sent out the info to almost everyone. Response was overwhelming - in one form, there were two of us who went out to take photos of it on their first day.

    Service 151 got theirs on 4 Aug, unusually used 4 units of B9s. Bus fans scrambled to Hougang Int as well as Kent Ridge to take photos of it on their first day. Later in same month, got VOs as cameos.

    Have you forgotten...

    ...that two Service 168 drivers lost their way earlier this year?

    The first came with SBS 9487X. The Chinese driver apparently did not turn out of SLE into Woodlands and ended up being lost along with his 20 odd passengers. He had actually insisted, according to various sources, that he knows the route and he could drive to the destination by himself but it did not materialise. The bus eventually found itself in Bukit Panjang at around 1300 hrs, a mere 2 hours after being lost. This was the subject of the headlines in the bus scene.

    The second one came on the same day. It was reported that SBS 3668E bound for Bedok also lost it's way - it turned into Tampines Interchange instead.

    ...that there were Free Rides on board 20 buses on 26 Aug 2008?
