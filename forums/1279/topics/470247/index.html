    The MAN NL323F A22 production batch passes by the demonstrator SMB138Y as it picked up passengers on service 963 towards HarbourFront.

    As of today, the MAN NL323F A22 chassis is very successful in Singapore with SMRT Buses, with estimated 400 units as of post date on service and likely with more delivering in the future.
