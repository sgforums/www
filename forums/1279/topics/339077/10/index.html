    Originally posted by Interception_7:

    would like to ask, sorry if OT, what shutter speed do you guys use to capture buses?


    Depends on the conditions and environment you are in. For night shot, you need a long shutter speed like 1 second and low ISO for non-grainy pictures. For day shot, you need a speedy shutter speed setting like 1/500 for bright sunlight or 1/125 for dim sun light and do remember to use low ISO.

     

    A tip from me is that do a test shot at the environment you're in so that you can know what to adjust
