Transmission problems on ZF gearboxes

  • BluBlur
    12 Apr 03, 23:01
    Hello everyone, have anyone noticed unfixed transmission problems for the above gearbox, especially for TIBS fleet? Symptoms include sluggish shifting and acceleration, or the gears don't really shift at all.

    Sent 2 emails to inform them of this problem, but their promises are always unfulfilled. As a gauge of timeframe, TIB825U is having this problem for more than 1 year. Surprised that the bus is still running without problems. Really bad maintenance.

    I've estimated the % affected is 3-4% of the total fleet. Mostly the affected ones fall into:

    TIB460-490, TIB730-750, TIB810-832,
    TIB1061-1091 (probably the worst batch affected), TIB1100-1110, TIB1130-1146

    It could be a problem of aging for the older buses, and a batch problem for the 10xx ones.

    The Voith gearbox don't exhibit this problem (for now).

    I think SBS is doing a good job on this right? Have only encountered such incident on SBS206A; the bus could only move at 20kph.
