    Where got 2 radiators lah??

    For Batch 2 & Batch 3 Volvos, it should be easy to differentiate since they have rectangular headlights. For Batch 1 VO3x & LO3x is a bit harder from far.

    EXTERNAL Differences

    For the first 100 of batch 1, they have emergency exits on the upper deck side windows whereas LO3x do not.

    VO3x has only 1 long A/C grille with horizontal louvers at the back of the bus, above the engine cover, whereas LO3x has 2 square grilles instead.

    VO3x Batch 1 only has 1 A/C grille on each side, behind the last rear window (or behind emergency exit). LO3x has 3 grilles (1 of them small) on the nearside and 2 grilles on the offside.

    INTERNAL differences

    VO3x has standing areas but not for LO3x.

    VO3x has the TVmobile placed on the nearside for lower deck but LO3x is on the offside.

    VO3x A/C filter is at the back of the bus, above the last row of seats so there's a big grille above the seats. LO3x has the grille facing downwards right after the exit door.

    VO3x has only 4 A/C vents for passengers on the last row compared to 6 for LO3x.
