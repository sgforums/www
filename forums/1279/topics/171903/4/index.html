    Realignment Of Jalan Buroh, Pioneer Road And Tanjong Kling Road

    To facilitate roadwork for the realignment of Jalan Buroh, Pioneer Road and Tanjong Kling Road , the Land Transport Authority (LTA) will be diverting the traffic between Jalan Buroh and Pioneer Road on Saturday, 4 March 2006 at 6.00am.

    Motorists travelling from Jalan Buroh, towards Pioneer Road and Ayer Rajah Expressway (AYE) and vice versa, will be diverted to the stretch of realigned Jalan Buroh and Pioneer Road . Motorists travelling between Jalan Buroh and Shipyard Road may use the existing Jalan Buroh or Tanjong Kling Road , and the new cross junction of Jalan Buroh/Pioneer Road/Tanjong Kling Road .

    This road upgrading work is part of the road development programme to upgrade the main arterial roads in the south-western sector of Singapore .

    Motorists are advised to drive carefully and follow the directional signs on display.

    Besides trunk services, SMRT Buses also operates 6 Intra-town services.
