    SMRT Launches Free Chinatown Shuttle BUS Service
    and Extends Train & Bus Services for LUNAR New Year

    Visitors to Chinatown this festive season can look forward to added convenience with the SMRT free shuttle bus service which operates from Friday, 20 January to Saturday, 28 January.

    The loop service, which runs at 20-minute intervals, will ply Maxwell Road (Tanjong Pagar MRT station), Robinson Road, Battery Road (Raffles Place MRT station), South Canal Road and South Bridge Road. Please see Annex for the route. Details on the schedule are:

    20 January to 26 January: 7.00 pm - 11.30 pm
    27 January: 7.00 pm - 12.30 am
    28 January: 7.00 pm - 1.30 am

    In addition, SMRT's seven NightRider services will be extended into Chinatown on 27 and 28 January 2006.

    SMRT's train and bus service extensions will commence two days before Lunar New Year, on 27 January. Train services will be extended beyond 1.00 am on 27 January, and beyond 2.00 am on 28 January.
    27 January

    Last train from Outram Park Station departs at 1.12 am (Eastbound and Westbound)
    Last train from Dhoby Ghaut Station departs at 1.12 am (Northbound) and 1.03 am (Southbound)

    28 January

    Last train from Outram Park Station departs at 2.12 am (Eastbound and Westbound)
    Last train from Dhoby Ghaut Station departs at 2.12 am (Northbound) and 2.03 am (Southbound)

    The operating hours of the Bukit Panjang LRT and selected feeder services at Bukit Batok, Choa Chu Kang, Woodlands and Yishun interchanges will be extended to match the last train timings. More details will be announced by Transit Link Pte Ltd shortly.

    Souce : SMRT Website
