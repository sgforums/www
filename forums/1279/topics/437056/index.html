    Still remember the SBS fleet those days:-

    Ang Mo Kio Depot

    Fleet: Merc OF1413, Merc OF1417 (Hawke), Merc 0305, Leyland Atlantean

    Areas of operation: Ang Mo Kio, Serangoon, Yishun (171 under SBS days)

    Bedok North Depot

    Fleet: Albion Viking, Volvo B57, Leyland Atlantean
    Areas of operation: Bedok, Tampines, Pasir Ris, Changi, Somapah

    Bukit Batok Depot

    Fleet: Albion Viking, Volvo B57, Leyland Atlantean

    Areas of operation: South Canal, Bukit Merah, Buona Vista, Ghim Moh, Bukit Timah, Bukit Batok, Choa Chu Kang

    Hougang Depot

    Fleet: Albion Viking, Volvo B57, Merc OF1413, Merc OF1417 (Hawke), Merc 0305, Nissan U31SCN (aircon 3 and aircon 118)

    Areas of operation: Lor 1 Geylang, Sims Ave, Macpherson, Eunos, Bedok, Hougang, Upp Serangoon, Punggol

    Jurong Bus Park

    Fleet: Merc OF1413, Merc OF1417 (NZMB), Leyland Olympian

    Areas of operation: Kent Ridge, Clementi, Teban Gardens, Jurong East, Jurong, Boon Lay, Tuas

    Toa Payoh Depot

    Fleet: Leyland Victory, Merc OF1413, Volvo B57, Leyland Olympian

    Areas of operation: Toa Payoh, Bishan, St Michaels’, Potong Pasir

     

    Feel free to add more.
