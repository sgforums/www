    When LTA & STK design the bus they didn't consider the Euro 6 emission rule as the bus was build before the Euro 6 emission rule was made official. Even if its a special project, LTA still have to follow protocol. If the rules says Euro 6 by 1st Jan 2018 onwards, they have to follow, meaning any vehicle registered after 1st Jan 2018 must comply to the Euro 6 emission rules that LTA set even if its LTA own projects. SG 5999 Z was registered to a operator at the end of last December which still falls under the old Euro 5 emission rules. However SG 4002 G didn't got registered to a operator and thus it can be on the roads anymore because LTA don't allow vehicles with Euro 5 & below to register anymore now its Euro 6 and Above. BTW Euro 6 emission rules are not made by LTA but by the Parliament where they discussed in Parliament House with PM Lee & other ministers.
