    I don't ever recall clear glass panels on the staircases of our double-deck buses. Up till the Tridents and the Volvo Super Olympians (sans SBS 9889U), all of our double-deck buses use plastic paneling for the staircases; SBS 9889U (the ComfortDelgro bodywork prototype) and the B9TLs all had frosted glass if I remember correctly. The fact that SBS 9889U predate the B9TLs, and the fact that I took it once and noticed the frosted glass before the B9TLs came in, suggest that the frosted glass had been there all along.

    Edit: found it on tintinspartan's YouTube page: a video of him riding SBS 9889U. This was in the middle of last year, and the frosted glass is already there (go to about 3:24 in the video; you see it best at about 4:30 when he pans over to the staircase).
