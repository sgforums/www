    Bus driver slashed in Johor

    A MAN wielding a parang has attached a Singapore bus driver in a robbery attempt in Johor Bahru on Tuesday evening.

    The SBS Transit 160 bus had just left the Kotaraya shopping centre when the incident occurred.

    According ot a MediaCorp report, the bus driver was slashed with the parang when he attempted to close the bus doors on his attackers who were trying to board the bus.

    The driver is in his early thirties and is believed to be a Singapore permanent resident.

    The injured driver, who is currently in the Johor General Hospital, will soon be moved to Singapore said an SBS Transit spokesman.

    The attacker left the scene after the attempted robbery. No other information is currently available.

    The 160 bus travels between Kotaraya and the Jurong East Interchange in Singapore.

    --ST
