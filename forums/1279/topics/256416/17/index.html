    How to take such beautiful night shot at night???

    Can share some tips???
    Some tips to share with you for night shots:

    1. Choose a spot whereby there's enough lightings to light up the front and side of the bus you going to snap.

    2. Play (or experiment) around with all sorts of your modes (for night shots, like Night Mode, manual Tungsten mode, etc) in your digital camera to see which mode suits the best.

    3. Try not to zoom (even for optical zoom, and not even to mention digital zoom) your camera when you are going to snap it.

    4. Remember, at all times, try not to use freehand shots unless your hand is really very stable (i.e don't move even a bit while snapping photos). Always try to find a base to support your camera so that it will be more stable when you snap night shots photo, be it railings, lamp post (at the small cover when there's a bit of pop up to at least stabilize your camera), or even floor shots (roads, concrete pavement) if necessary (usually i used it in times of interesting/rare cameos, i called it "emergency" method. Of course, tripod is still the best if you have it or borrowed it from other people.

    Have fun in your night photography shots!!
