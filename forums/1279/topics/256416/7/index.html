    Originally posted by SBS9806J:

    Regos have it in the sequence:
    A,Y,U,S,P,L,J,G,D,B,Z,X,T,R,M,K,H,E,C,A.
    Every XXX0, they will skip 1 letter from the sequence ieg 9809B, 9810X (Volvo B10TLs)
    Every XX00, they will skip 11 letters from the sequence, eg SBS7399E, SBS7400J (Volvo B9TLs)
    Every X000, the letter would be the same as the previous number. eg SBS999U (Mercedes Benz O.405G), SBS1000U (Mercedes Benz LP1113)
