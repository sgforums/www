  • Powered_By_CNG
    29 May 07, 21:16
    The Sony ECM-DS70P is a portable 'T' shaped electret condenser microphone that has a conventional microphone plug. If your MP3 player has a MIC IN plug, you can use this (or any other) microphone.

    The great thing about the ECM-DS70P is how sensitive it is - you can pick up sounds of people whispering at the front of the bus while you're sitting at the back of the bus recording. This, unfortunately, means that you'll need a quiet bus for a good recording (in terms of passenger numbers). This microphone is also very sensitive to wind, so you need to make sure that you're not in a drafty position when you're recording, otherwise you will get "flutter". The great thing about the sensitivity, though, is that you can pick up powertrain and drivetrain noises extremely well.

    Regards,
    Dave
