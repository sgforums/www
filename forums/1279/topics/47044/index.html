    ooh... That's kinda easy

    Service 80 uses Volvo Olympian 3 Axles, Volvo Mk 4 WA Strider, Volvo Mk 4 DM 3500, Volvo Mk 2 (Sometimes), Dennis Trident (Pretty rare, but might be usual in the future, i guess)

    Service 82 uses Volvo Olympian 3 Axle, Volvo Mk 4 WA Strider, Volvo Mk 4 DM 3500, Volvo Mk 3 (Sometimes), Volvo Mk 2 (Sometimes), Dennis Trident (sometimes)

    Service 83 uses Volvo Mk 4 DM, Volvo Mk 4 WA Strider, and sometimes Mk 2.

    Service 85 uses Volvo Olympian 3 Axles, Volvo Mk 4 WA Strider, Dennis Trident, Volvo Mk 3 (as cameos), Volvo Mk 2 (sometimes)

    Service 86 uses Volvo Olympian 3 Axle, Dennis Trident (as cameo), Volvo Mk 4 DM 3500, Volvo Mk 4 WA Strider. Sometimes Mk 3 and Mk 2 might slot in as cameo.

    Service 87 uses Volvo Olympian 3 Axle, Volvo Mk 3, Volvo Mk 4 DM, Scania N113CRB

    Service 88 uses Volvo Olympian 3 Axle, Leyland Olympian 3 Axle, Volvo Mk 4 DM 3500, MB O.405 WA, MB O.405 DM, MB O.405 DM CAC

    Service 89 uses Volvo Mk 3, Volvo Mk 4 DM 3500, Volvo Mk 2 (Sometimes)

    Service 27 uses Volvo Mk 4 WA Strider, Volvo Mk 4 DM, Volvo Mk 2, Volvo Mk 4 Dm 3500 (sometimes)

    Service 156 uses Volvo Mk 4 DM 3500, Volvo Mk 4 WA Strider (as majority), Volvo Mk 3 (as majority), Volvo Mk 2 DM, WA

    Service 159 uses MB O.405 WA, DM , DM CAC

    Service 501 uses Volvo Olympian 3 Axle, Volvo Mk 4 DM 3500, Volvo Mk 4 WA Strider, Volvo Mk 4 DM, Dennis Trident

    Service 502 uses Volvo Olympian 3 Axle, Volvo Mk 4 DM 3500, Volvo Mk 4 WA Strider, Volvo Mk 2 (Sometimes), MB O.405 WA, Dennis Trident

    Rachie, hope that this is a good guide for u to know what type of buses do these svcs use.
