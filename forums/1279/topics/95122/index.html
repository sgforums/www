    For Your info.. YES.. i do have a little bit of study on HongKong Bus.. the bus that you was talking about was a Dennis Dragon.. Well.. these buses was introduced in the 80s.. to cope with the growing population of HongKong.. there were 2 different types of these Buses available..

    - Dennis Dragon (Duple Metsec)
    - Leyland Olympian (Alexander 'R' type Body)

    These buses was special in a way that the seating plan for the upper deck was 3+2.. instead of the usual 2+2 that we have it now.. At that time, due to the needs of the different companies, there were different passenger capacities which vary between 149 and 164.. This is because that different companies have different seating plan on the lower deck as some company had more standing area than seating area..(upp deck is usual 3+2 seating plan)..
