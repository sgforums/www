    The long (maybe not) story:

    Since the slot was added last year, its perm could not be confirmed. It was originally thought to be SBS3329J, but it was confirmed later not to be true only in early January 2015. Later, it was permed by SBS3418K until it was deployed out suddenly in mid-2015. Then, it was permed from SBS3448Z, suspected to be a direct swap with SBS3418K to 89. Then, SBS3418K was transferred to SLBP, SBS3448Z went back to 89, and SBS9628G replaced it on 43.
