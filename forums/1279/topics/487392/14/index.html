    Bus invloved was SBS9612B. Accident happened at 10am and it uprooted a lamp post. The lamp post is trapped between the bus and the bus shelter roof, and could fall if disturbed; leaking diesel from the bus has also rendered the pavement slippery.

    2. Bus ends at Lee Wee Nam Library (BS 27211) in NTU, then goes straight into Nanyang Cres (opp direction of 199) and exit NTU via main exit to continue journey.

    3. (heard from BC) Single deck buses were deployed (epull) and some of them were made to go thru SBS lobby...but then the only single deck bus i saw was SBS8064P, epull from 243W, but BC of SBS8064P apparently dun know where to go as he was epulled to do 179...

    Road only cleared at 120pm when SBS9612B was finally towed out. The first buses to clear the road were SBS8064P and SBS3629T while the last bus to do re-route was SBS3642D that did the re-route number 1 (attempted to board this bus but BC say this bus dun go to NTU, later saw it turned into Street 91).

    At the moment SBS3629T passed by the accident area (i was on this bus), police, traffic police,NTU campus security and SBS Transit staff were still there investigating the incident...
