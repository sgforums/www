    Hi all, SBST has reclassfied it's interchanges into East and West districts.

    EAST DISTRICT

    Ang Mo Kio
    Bishan
    Serangoon
    Toa Payoh
    Bedok
    Tampines
    Pasir Ris

    WEST DISTRICT

    Boon Lay
    Bukit Merah
    Clementi
    Jurong East
    Eunos
    Hougang Central
    Sengkang
    Punggol Temp Interchange
    HarbourFront

    Those in red are new additions to the East district, orange to West district.

    In summary

    1. All Central interchanges (AMKDEP controlled) are handed to East district.
    2. North East and Eunos Interchanges (HGDEP controlled) are handed to West district.
