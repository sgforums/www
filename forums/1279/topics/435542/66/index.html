    New Service 85 From Punggol / Sengkang To Yishun

    The new year will usher in a new bus service for residents of Punggol and Sengkang.

    From Sunday, 1 January 2006, SBS Transit Service 85 will be introduced to operate from Punggol / Sengkang to Yishun via Tampines Expressway (TPE) and Seletar Expressway (SLE).

    The new service will provide residents of Punggol and Sengkang with links to Yishun as well as additional link between the Punggol and Sengkang neighbourhoods.

    "This new service will give Punggol and Sengkang residents a convenient and faster link to Yishun as it will ply the expressways. We believe this will be welcome news for residents in these three neighbouring towns," said Mr Ong Boon Leong, Executive Director, SBS Transit.

    Service 85, will operate from Punggol Bus Interchange, via Punggol Central, Punggol East, Sengkang East Drive, Sengkang East Way, Rivervale Drive, Compassvale Drive, Sengkang East Road, TPE, SLE, Lentor Avenue, Yishun Avenue 2 and Yishun Bus Interchange.

    The daily operating hours of the new service are from 6.00am to 11.30pm.Please see the attached poster for more details. SBS Transit service updates are also available on our websites at www.sbstransit.com.sg.
