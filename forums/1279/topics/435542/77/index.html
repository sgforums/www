    Back in the days when SBS was undergoing the fleet repainting to SBS Transit, the Leyland Olympian 3 axle (LO3X) fleet had their full red rims painted to sliver before the actual SBS Transit livery was applied - and all of a sudden it was hard to distinguish from far between its Volvo Olympian 3 axles (batch 1) (VO3X) cousins which had sliver rims (but with red hub) all along which was also being repainted to sliver rims & hubs.

    The LO3X and VO3X (Batch 1) fleet were still running around in SuperBus livery with sliver rims as each one of them awaits repainting - to the untrained eye, LO3X might be mistaken as VO3X batch 1. Eventually got used and learnt how to differentiate from the grilles, axle hub and upper deck window - roof space later on.

    Needless to say, the SBS Transit livery and sliver rims is bold and modern looking, it seemed like it was from a science fiction movie in 2003.

    Info from BIS number one page on Singapore Buses~
