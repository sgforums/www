    Service 138

    Started as a semi-express service between Ang Mo Kio Int and loop at Robinson Rd.
    Withdrawn on 24th Jan 1988, due to the service being affected by the MRT.
    Re-introduced on 1st May 1994 between Yio Chu Kang Ter and Singapore Zoological Gardens, with Service 171 skipping Mandai Lake Road.
    Extended to Ang Mo Kio Interchange on Saturday, 1st Oct 1994 to serve passengers better via Ang Mo Kio Avenue 8.
    Nearly became a Tibs Service but was saved.
    On Sunday 24 March 2002, with the opening of Ang Mo Kio Temporary Bus Interchange, it was enhanced to ply along Ang Mo Kio Avenue 3, 6 & 5, instead of along Ang Mo Kio Ave 8 & 5, to cover the deleted sectors of Service 269.
