    Press Release:

    TIBS LAUNCHES NEW SERVICE 864 ON SUNDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 1998- Free Rides On Day Of Launch

    Trans-Island Bus Services Ltd (Tibs) will be launching a new Service 864 in Sengkang on Sunday, 15 November 1998. To thank the Sengkang residents for their patience and understanding, Tibs will be offering free rides on Service 864 on the day of the launch.

    Service 864 is an air-con trunk service which plies between Sengkang New Town and Marina Centre. This service will provide Sengkang residents with connections to nearby Paya Lebar MRT station and the Central Business District. The service will operate from 5.45 am to 11.00 pm during Mondays to Saturdays in the direction of Sengkang to Marina Centre. In the direction of Marina Centre to Sengkang, the service will operate from 6.15 am to 11.30 pm during Mondays to Saturdays. For Sundays and Public Holidays, the service will operate from 6.00 am to 11.00 pm in the direction of Sengkang to Marina Centre. In the direction of Marina Centre to Sengkang, the service will operate from 6.30 am to 11.30 pm.

    For passengers’ convenience, posters announcing the introduction of the service have been displayed at the bus stops along its route. Leaflets are also being distributed to residents informing them of the new service.
