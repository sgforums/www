    A direct service from Woodlands to Sembawang from Sunday, 24 June 2001

    As part of our continuous effort to provide mobility and convenience to our customers, Trans-Island Bus Services Ltd (Tibs) will operate a new air-con trunk service 962 to provide a direct link from Woodlands to Sembawang from 24 June 2001. This new service will bring about added convenience especially to residents of Woodlands Neighbourhoods 5, 6 and 7 and Sembawang Neighbourhood 4. Students from Canberra Secondary School at Sembawang Drive can also take the service to reach Sembawang MRT station.

    The service also offers the following benefits:

    * Direct connection to popular shopping centres like Causeway Point and Sun Plaza 

    * Connections to MRT stations like Admiralty and Sembawang

    * Cool comfort (full air-con service) 

    Operating Details

    Service 962 plies from Woodlands Regional Interchange to Sembawang Drive via Woodlands Avenue 2, Avenue 5, Avenue 6, Avenue 7 and Gambas Avenue.

    Fares

    Commuters using farecards pay between $0.60 and $1.20 while cash-paying commuters pay $0.70 to $1.30. The usual concessionary fares and transfer rebates are applicable.

    Hours of Operation

    Service 962 operates from 5:30 am to 11:45 pm from Mondays to Saturdays and from 5:45 am to 11:45 am on Sundays and Public Holidays. The service operates at an interval of 10 to 12 minutes during peak hours and 12 to 15 minutes during off-peak hours.
