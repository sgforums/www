    Originally posted by th1988:
    36 duplicate with 16 as peak hr service between Bt Merah and Changi Airport. Why? Is it due to high demand?
    Service 16 "originally" operated between Bt Merah and Marine Parade. In 1993, services 31 (Tampines - Stadium Blvd) and 149 (Toa Payoh - Changi Airport) were merged. As a result, service 16 was extended to the airport to cover service 149 route from Marine Parade to the airport. Service 390 (Changi Airport - Tomlinson Rd) was withdrawn with service 16 as the alternative service. However, soon, service 390 passengers were complaining that with the amendment, they had to take service 16 to the airport which resulted in a longer travelling time. The press published the complaint and this "forced" SBST to introduce one-directional-peak-hours service 16E (Semi-express between Bt Merah - Changi Airport) not long after, which plied express along ECP, Rochor Rd/Ophir Rd, Beach Rd between the airport and City Hall MRT. In Dec 1997, service 16E was re-numbered service 36 with route and operating hours un-changed. Then in Sep 1998, service 36 route was amended to become the route today and changed its operation hours to a full day service, and service 16 route was shortened to Marine Parade on the same day.
