    Actually There's alot of other buses that are perm training.

    take it as a revenue/training xover. For those who are familiar with timesheets the block number is sw/se-871. It is usually found on services that do jump bus. They go back to depot before 0845/0900hrs and will only come out after 1600/1615hrs. This means the driver driving this bus will jump to another bus. In short, the morning and evening revenue drivers for this bus is totally different.

    Of course during the time in the depot they will act as training buses.

    S53, S72, S82, S86, S153 are some examples.
