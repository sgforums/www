    SBS bus and lorry collide along Airline Road

    SINGAPORE: A bus driver and a lorry driver were injured in an accident along Airline Road in the Changi area on Saturday afternoon. Police said they received a call about the collision between the lorry and the SBS bus at about 1.30pm. Both drivers had limb and head injuries, and were conscious when they were sent to the Changi General Hospital. Police said there were passengers on the bus, but none of them needed to be sent to the hospital. The bus driver is said to be in his early sixties and the lorry driver is in his mid-fifties. Eyewitnesses to the accident can call the police hotline at 1800-255-0000. - CNA/so
