    Serious bus accident at Clementi MRT bus stop

    STOMPer Val witnessed this dramatic bus accident at a bus stop at Clementi MRT this morning (Dec 03). A car and two buses were involved in the pile-up.
     
    Here's what he witnessed:
     
    "These two buses crashed into each other near a Clementi MRT bus stop.
     
    "The first bus, SMRT service number 189, hit a car in front of it. Then a Bendy Bus behind it, service number 106, was unable to brake in time and slammed into the rear of the first bus.
     
    "The back of bus 189 was badly smashed with its rear window shattered. Bus 106's windscreen was completely cracked and its bumper was severely damaged.
     
    "According to bus 189's Bus Captain, he had stopped before the bus stop as there were already two buses waiting in the bay.
     
    "He also said that seven passengers had sustained injuries. I saw some blood stains on the floor near the entrance of bus 106 as well.

    "When I spoke to him he broke down in tears and said that he had been driving SMRT buses for 20-30 years and this was his first accident. He told me he wished everyone involved a speedy recovery."
