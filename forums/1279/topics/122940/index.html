    For subway operations, a monopoly is better. Railways need high maintenance and it is better that they be maintained by one entity (preferably the authorities).

    Bus operations are a different story. In many cities, there is competition between bus services as differenc companies have their main bases in different sectors.

    It is better for SMRT to run ALL the subway lines, while bus services can be provided by a maximum of three operators based in different sectors.
