    That was a picture of a Volvo B57.

    The B57 came in 2 models; one depicted in the picture and ther other with a raised roof in front with a much flatter roof leading to the rear. Both models were designed by New Zealand Motor Bodies (NZMB).


    Source: ShowBus UK

    Another bus with the NZMB body is the Mercedes Benz OF1417. The main difference between the B57 and the OF1417 is the lack of the little window just after the entrance on the latter.

    9 Feb 05, 15:08
    Marsiling terminal (near block 19 hawker centre).

    Now gone only an empty field left. I love this bus terminal. After work or meeting frens, can order take aways from the hawker centre. Before Woodlands Central and Causeway point existed, this was the so called Marsiling Central.

    sv 182 - New Bridge Road (later diverted to Shenton way) via Bt Timah Rd, Orchard Rd.

    sv 180 - Anson Road (later to WTC) Via Bt Timah Rd, Clemenceau Ave, Havelock Road, Upp Cross st. Donno why it was withdrawn 1990. Miss this bus, sigh.

    sv 950 - Senton Way. First aircon bus in Woodlands. Via BKE, PIE Ophir Rd, Collyer quay, Raffles place. Well Planned!! Pride of Marsilians and Woodlanders!

    sv 951 - Boon Lay. Quick connection to Boon Lay via BP, Bt Batok Road. Sv178 ridership drops. Introducing DAF hispanos.

    sv 952 - Marina Centre. Via BKE, PIE, Scotts rd, Orchard Rd, Somerset and all. Another Pride of Marsilians and Woodlanders! Excellent route! Businessman, teenagers, kids, adults, ah peks, aunties and all board this service!! Featured MBO405 hispano.

    All of these services had been withdrawn. Now left the useless sv 960.. which is a waste of time passing thru Bt Panjang.

    Thank you
