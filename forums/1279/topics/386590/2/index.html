    Originally posted by SMB66X:

    Something like being spare bus or split shift.


    Anyway the term "spare bus" and "split-shift" cannot be used for one another. While spare bus means that when the bus comes out and do any service and is considered a cameo, Split-Shift buses do belong to a permanent service where they are assigned to do the split shift.

    And no one uses the term "Standby" for buses, although its used for BC/SL whereby standby BC/SL have no perm service and mostly cover for another BC/SL who is on leave/MC etc.
