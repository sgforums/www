    The following is the reply from Tibs about a suggested service between Woodlands and Bedok. (I have posted the suggestion here before). Judging from the reply, it seems impossible that the new service will become a reality.

    _____________________________________________________

    Corporate Communications Department
    Trans Island Bus Services

    NEW BUS SERVICE 968 (Woodlands to Bedok)

    Currently, Tibs bus connection between Tampines and Yishun/Woodlands is by service 969, which ply along the expressways.

    However, Tibs bus connection between Bedok and Yishun is by service 854, which does not ply along any expressway, and result in a longer time to reach the destination.

    When the bridge linking Woodlands Ave 7 and Sembawang Way is completed, a new Tibs service 968 can be introduced to ply between Woodlands Regional interchange and Bedok interchange via the expressways, for a faster connection between Yishun and Bedok, and to serve Sembawang passengers going to Bedok.

    Route: Woodlands Ave 7, Sembawang Way, Canberra Link, Yishun Ave 2, Lentor Ave, SLE (Express), TPE (Express), Tampines Ave 10 (Express), Bedok North Rd and Bedok North Ave 1.

    I hope you would consider and implement my suggestion which enhances connection between the North and East. Thank you and best regards.
    _____________________________________________________

    Thanks for your email. We wish to assure you that we have noted your suggestion to introduce a new service, linking Woodlands and Bedok via Woodlands Ave 7, Sembawang Way, Canberra Link, Yishun Ave 2, Lentor Ave, SLE (Express), TPE (Express), Tampines Ave 10 (Express), Bedok North Rd and Bedok North Ave 1. At this present moment, we do not have any immediate plans. However, we would give it due consideration in future. Meanwhile, you may wish to know that currently, beside the train, you can also take Service 168 from Woodlands to Bedok.

    Have a nice day. Wishing you Gong Xi Fa Cai.

    Warmest Regards

    DID : 6331 1152
    Fax : 6334 8051

    [Name and email excluded to protect privacy of sender]
