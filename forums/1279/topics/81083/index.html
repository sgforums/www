    Services 82 and 103 was amended on the phase 3 of the rationalisation.

    Service 82 was amended to operate from Serangoon Int instead of Shenton Way Terminal. At the same time, service 107 was extended to Hougang Central Int.

    Service 103 was merged with services 378 and 379 to become a Serangoon Int - Seletar East / West Camp service. Service 103W is denoted as the service to West Camp.

    Service 85 used to operate between Sengkang Interchange and HarbourFront via Kovan, Upper Serangoon, Bendemeer Rd, Little India, Orchard Road, Kim Seng Road, Tiong Bahru Rd, Telok Blangah Road.

    NEL Rationalisation: Phase 1

    Nine services were amended to serve NEL stations. The services include 21, 22, 53, 70, 130, 131 etc.

    NEL Rationalisation: Phase 2

    Services that duplicated the NEL line were either withdrawn or amended.

    Services withdrawn were:

    85 (Sengkang - HarbourFront)

    Express 501 (Hougang Central - Marina Centre / Shenton Way)

    Express 511 (Hougang Central - Moulmein Rd / Penang Road)

    Express 512 (Hougang Central - Orchard Rd)

    Services amended were:

    81 (Tampines - New Bridge Road to Tampines - Serangoon)

    97 (Hougang Central - Jurong East to Jurong East - Suntec City)

    106 (Serangoon - Bt Batok to Bt Batok - Marina Centre)

    111 (Ghim Moh - Hougang Central to Ghim Moh - Suntec City)

    Express 502 (Sengkang - Boon Lay to Boon Lay - Suntec City)

    NEL Rationalisation: Phase 3

    Services that duplicated the NEL line were amended.

    62 (Hougang South - New Bridge Rd to Punggol Int - Lor 1 Geylang)

    82 (Punggol Rd End - Shenton Way to Serangoon - Punggol Rd End)

    103 (Jalan Kayu - New Bridge Rd to Serangoon - Seletar East / West Camp)

    107 (Serangoon - Shenton Way to Hougang Central - Shenton Way)

    107X (Serangoon - Marina Centre to Hougang Central - Marina Centre)

    378 (Jalan Kayu - Seletar East Camp, merged with service 103)

    379 (Jalan Kayu - Seletar West Camp, merged with service 103, renumbered as service 103W)

    NEL Rationalization Phase 1

    9 SBST services were diverted to serve the NEL stations.

    svc 22, 53 and 70 were amended to serve NE12 Serangoon Station in both directions via Serangoon Central/Upp Serangoon Road

    svc 21 and 125 were amended to serve NE8 Farrer Park Station in the direction towards St Michael's Terminal via Jln Besar/Kitchener Rd/Serangoon Rd

    svc 130 and 139 were amended to serve NE9 Boon Keng Station towards Shenton Way and Bukit Merah respectively via Serangoon Rd(express) , Boon Keng Rd and Bendemeer Rd

    svc 72 was amended to serve NE14 Hougang Station via Hougang Central in both directions, skipping a portion of Hougang Ave 4

    lastly, service 83 was extended to loop at NE17 Punggol Station (via Punggol Central) from the previous Sengkang - Edgedale Plains route. [this was before Pg Temp Int was ready]

    For those who dont want to read all those crap... Here's a Summary

    Phrase 1
    9 SBST services amended to serve the nearest NEL station around.

    21 125 (Towards St Mikes) - Farrer Pk
    130 139 (Towards City) - Boon Keng
    22 53 70 - Serangoon
    72 - Hougang
    83 - Punggol

    Phrase 2
    9 SBST/TIBS services withdrawn/amended to avoid duplication along the North-East Corridor.

    81 - Cut short to Serangoon and amended to Hougang Ave 3
    97 106 111 502 - Cut short to Suntec City/Marina Centre
    85 501 511 512 - Withdrawn

    Phrase 3
    6 SBST services amended to avoid duplication in Chinatown area and to serve the North-East Corridor better

    62 - Cut short to loop at Kallang and extended to Punggol
    82 - Cut short to Serangoon and to serve Punggol Stn
    103 - Cut short to Serangoon and extended to ply Seletar Camps
    107/107X - Extended to Hougang Central to cover withdrawn sectors of 82 and 97.
    378 379 - Withdrawn due to closure of Jln Kayu Terminal (and Rationalisation of course)
