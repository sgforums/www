    Originally posted by SBS8138K:

    May i know how do i  resize a picture and upload to here? who cann give astep by step guide?


    If you have photoshop or some image editing software on your destop, open your image using any of these programs. For photoshop users, you can resize your image by clicking on:

    "Image" > "Image Size"

    For a regular photo size, usually we adopt sizes like:
    - 640x480
    - 800x600
    ....
    - 3264x2468

    Where the ratio of the length is 4:3 to the breadth.

    Just some simple guidelines. :)
