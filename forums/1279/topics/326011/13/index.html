    The cooling system uses a fan clutch. Unlike the other Hydraulically operated radiator fans. The fan clutch from SACHS is a brilliant technology. More reliable than the rest.

    These East Asian made buses are getting popularity in the region as they bear German components like ZF and Wabco but uses Chinese body and cabins which save costs. 1 MAN bus can pay for 3 Chinese buses.
