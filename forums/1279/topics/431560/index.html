  • dan474

    Route 1
    Take the MRT from Admiralty station to Woodlands station. Then, transfer to bus service 858 at Woodlands Bus Interchange right under Woodlands MRT.

    Route 2
    Take the MRT from Admiralty station to Bishan Interchange and transfer to the Circle Line heading towards Douby Ghaut. Then, transfer to the East-West Line at Paya Lebar Interchange heading towards Pasir Ris. After that, transfer to the Changi Branch Line at Tanah Merah Interchange. Board the Changi Airport Bound train at the middle platform.
