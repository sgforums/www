    The general trend for AM Peak is this:

    1st bus - quite packed, sometimes to door, especially if 2822H is 1st bus
    2nd bus - Least pack bus already, as BCs are always early, hence no standees at all
    3rd bus - fully seated, sometimes with a few standees
    4th bus - Standing capacity, sometimes to door
    4th bus onwards - stand to door most of the times, sometimes BC have to ask ppl to move in, sometimes skip the eunos mansion stop. If DD, then fully seated with some spaces in between.

    15th bus - 17th bus - No standees
    18th and 19th bus - Sometimes have standees
    20th bus onwards - No more standees (After 9.45am already)

    Observations based on the last bus stop before PIE
