    Date: 19 or 20 July 08

    Place: East Coast Park

    (Meet Up at Bedok first at 3:00 pm)

    Time: 4 pm - 6pm

    Cost:

    Compact Bike - $4 per hour

    Standard Bike - $5 per hour

    Mountain Bike - $6 per hour

    Dual - Seat Bike - $8 per hour

    Roller Skates - $10 for 2 hours  

     

    Anyone who wants to go, just join in. This is meant to make closer bonding amongst the younger bus fans and seniors if possible. We will take the 401 to East Coast Park and rent our bikes. After that, we will go for a trip to Bedok Jetty and enjoy the clear scenery it offers. Then, it's a bit of bonding. After the bonding session, it's to Marine Cove with a bit of 'chiong' race. But, make sure you know SAFETY COMES FIRST.
