    I shall attempt to do a simple guide on the basic functions of the Stop Reporter:

    First, power on the device by pressing the button on the top right (the circled button) or pressing "P" on the keyboard

    After that, you'll see something like this:

    At this step:
    Press "1" to switch to another route
    Press "6" to select that route

    After selecting that route, you'll see something like this:

    At this step:
    Press "1" to go back to route selection (previous screenshot)
    Press "2" to change direction (aka from T2 to T1) No effect on circular routes
    Press "3" to change announcement volume (from 1 (softest) to 8 (loudest))
    Press "4" to go to next stop (without announcement)
    Press "5" to repeat broadcast for current stop
    Press "6" to stop current broadcast
    Press "7" to go to next stop (with broadcast)

    Remember to load MM2 in safe mode and subsequently change your video setting in-game to hardware mode for it to work.
