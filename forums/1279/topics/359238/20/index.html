    Fyi For those Playing Service 3D, 14C,16M, 17, 89, 89B & 89X

    Bus Terminus Relocation of KMB Routes 17, 89, 89B and 89X

    To tie in with the redevelopment of Kwun Tong town centre, with effect from Sunday, 9 August 2009, the bus termini of Routes 17, 89, 89B and 89X will be relocated. The bus terminus of Route 17 will be relocated from Kwun Tong (Yuet Wah Street) Bus Terminus to Kwun Tong (Yue Man Square) Bus Terminus. The bus terminus of Routes 89 and 89X will be relocated from Kwun Tong (Yuet Wah Street) Bus Terminus to Kwun Tong Railway Station Bus Terminus. The bus terminus of Route 89B will be relocated from Kwun Tong (Yue Man Square) to Kwun Tong Railway Station Bus Terminus.

    A new bus stop of Routes 89 and 89X in the direction of Sha Tin will be added at Hip Wo Street outside Wo Lok Estate. New bus stops of Route 89B in the direction of Sha Tin will be added at Tsui Ping Road outside Tsui Pak House, at Hip Wo Street outside Wo Lok Estate and at Yue Man Square near Tung Yan Street. A new bus stop of Route 89B in the direction of Kwun Tong will be added at Mut Wah Street near Hip Wo Street.

    The fare of Route 17 is $5.00. The fare of Routes 89 and 89B is $5.80. The fare of Route 89X is $6.50.


    (7 Aug 2009)

     

    To tie in with the redevelopment of Kwun Tong town centre, with effect from Sunday, 19 July 2009, the bus termini of Routes 3D, 14C and 16M will be relocated. The bus terminus of Route 3D will be relocated from Kwun Tong (Yuet Wah Street) Bus Terminus to Kwun Tong (Yue Man Square) Bus Terminus. The bus terminus of Circular Route 14C will be relocated from Kwun Tong (Yue Man Square) Bus Terminus to Lei Yue Mun (Sam Ka Tsuen Ferry) Bus Terminus. The bus terminus of Circular Route 16M will be relocated from Kwun Tong (Yuet Wah Street) to Kwun Tong Railway Station Bus Terminus.

    A new bus stop of Route 14C in the direction of Lei Yue Mun will be added at Yue Man Square outside house number 16-18, while a new bus stop of Route 16M in the direction of Kwun Tong will be added at Hip Wo Street opposite Kwun Tong District Branch Office.

    The fares of Routes 3D are $3.20 for non air-conditioned buses and $4.40 for air-conditioned buses. The fare of Route 14C is $3.70. The fares of Route 16M are $2.80 for non air-conditioned buses and $3.90 for air-conditioned buses.

     


    (16 July 2009)

    Intro of New KMB Bus Services

    KMB Introduces Four Special Routes for “An Invitation of Love”

    For the convenience of the public travelling to Kwai Tsing District/ Tsuen Wan District, Tuen Mun District and Yuen Long District after attending the shows on Thursday 6 August 2009 and Friday 7 August 2009, KMB will introduce four special routes comprising 41R (Hong Kong Coliseum →Cheung Ching), 259R (Hong Kong Coliseum →Tuen Mun Pier Head), 260R (Hong Kong Coliseum →Siu Hong Court) and 269R (Hong Kong Coliseum →Tin Heng Estate). Buses will pick up passengers at the Coach Carpark facing the Green Section, and depart about 15 minutes after the event finishes.

    The fare of Route 41R is $16.20; the fare of Routes 259R and 260R is $20.80; and the fare of Route 269R is $23.20.


    (5 Aug 2009)
