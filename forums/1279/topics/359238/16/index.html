    Download MM2 from pg 8 (This forums). After finish downloading the thing, there is a folder at your Desktop(If you save to Desktop). Click it. Then on top of the folder, you will see a word "Extract Folders" or "Extract Files" . Click that then A thing will pop out and you click "Extract". Then you are done.

    For bus/map/cars mods, you just download it and extract it. You will have the files. Then you will see file(s) with the type : "AR Files". Then you drag that files to the MM2 Folder. Then you are done.
