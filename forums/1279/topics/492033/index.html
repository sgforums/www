Bus Services for New Developments in Future

  • BusAnalayzer
    2 Jun 16, 09:52

    A new development, whether it is residential (HDB/Condo) or commercial (industrial/business complex) affects the loading and operation of bus services.

    This has often been seen with some services not doing very well but once the developments are up, they are full to capacity (eg: sv 50)

    I want to discuss and document here all new development and related transport / bus infrastructure needed to suit the new development demands.

    Buangkok West

    I will start with the upcoming Buangkok West town, that is not as much discussed as Bidadari or Tampines North.

    Not sure if you know but there are around 5 major developments in Buangkok West. 

    The last one to open was Buangkok Vale in 2014 along Buangkok Green, catered by 43/43M/88/156

    Future developments are more around Buangkok Crescent and Link.

    1. Buangkok Parkvista (Q3, 2017)

    2. Buangkok Edgeview (Q4, 2017)

    3. Buangkok Tropica (Q1, 2018)

    4. Buangkok Square (Q2, 2018)

    5. New development next to Buangkok Square from Aug BTO exercise (2020)

    Existing service:

    Today there is only one service 101 catering to Buangkok Link. 

    Future Service:

    1. In future 101 will have to be extended to loop at Buangkok Crescent. This will connect people to Hougang MRT. 

    2. In addition there should be a new service such as 104 (removing 43M maybe) that does Bidadari Interchange -> Serangoon -> YCK Road -> Buangkok Green -> Buangkok Link/Crescent -> Buangkok Dr (u/c) -> Sengkang East Rd -> Sengkang East Ave -> Buangkok MRT (looping point) -> Buangkok DR & back.

    Tampines North

    Huge commercial/residential hub at Tampines North to be ready by 2019

    (1) JTC Space (TOP: 2016). This is along Tampines Ave 10. So current services 27, 34, 58, 72, 118, 168 can cater to the demand. 

    (2) Reebonz Building (2018). This is along Tampines Ave 11 & Tampines North Dr junction (both u/c)

    (3) Tampines Green ridges (Q1 2019). This is along Tampines Ave 9. Can still be catered with existing services 27, 72, 168

    (4) Tampines Greenwave (Q3 2019). This is right in center of Tampines North. Will need feeder/trunk service in future

    (5) / (6) New HDB flats announced in recent BTO excercise north of Tampines Ave 9 and east of Tampines Ave 6.

    The point to be noted is that each HDB development is huge with 1000+ flats at minimum. Moreover Tampines North will not have MRT/LRT. So it will require robust bus network. Few services from Tampines Interchange will have to be diverted here + expect new services.
