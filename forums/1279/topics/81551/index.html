    Me got stuck on 2096 (Kawasaki) tonight

    First I was at Raffles Place Station when the train took 15 mins to come, TV showed Jurong East everything seemed normal

    Then at the last 2 min, the TV changes to Newton (10:09pm)

    Then boarded the Train, along the way the motorman (Malay) kept saying Dear Passengers, For your Info, this train will be ending at Newton Station, please wait for station annocments at Newton Station, Thank you.

    Then between Orchard and Newton, the train stopped for about 20 mins (Stopped at 10:20pm), everyone started panicking and started calling/SMSing their friends/relatives. Some annocements were made to tell passengers what to do after alighting from the train at Newton.

    Then the train started moving slowly towards Newton, I alighted from the train but the annocments cannot be heared clearly as it was too crowded and too noisy.(10:40)

    Outside the station (10:45 when I was outside), passengers waited for their own bus back to their home not knowing that there was a free shuttle service to TPY by TIBS, saw a crowded 972C (The SMRT Volgren Merz) on 700A. All buses were crowded as some passengers changed to bus at Orchard. Finally the shuttle bus came(10:53) (TIB 504X of amdep 171) to TPY the driver did a full loop to Braddell MRT before going to TPY MRT.

    At TPY Station, passengers ask those SMRT staff for refund but they say they are busy. (b/w11:00 to 11:10pm)

    Finally home sweet home by taking 3668E of 238 (11:12 board)
