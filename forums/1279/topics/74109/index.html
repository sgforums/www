    A tidbit about service 63.

    The original 63 was a service from MacPherson to Alexandra Road (Labrador).

    The route of this 63 is exactly the same as the reintroduced 63 (Bt Merah - MacPherson and subsequently Rumah Tinggi - MacPherson) between MacPherson and Tiong Bahru Road/Lower Delta Road junction.

    From LDR, it went by Depot Road and Alexandra Road before terminating at Labrador.
