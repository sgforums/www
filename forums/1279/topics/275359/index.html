    Took SBS7233A today and this bus has a newly installed standing area.

    Same position as those on the other DDs. It has similar railings to those found on VO3x.

    But because the final forward facing seat (not counting the back row) is mounted on a step, there is a small wasted space. They have "cordoned" off that step with metal railings. Maybe can use for putting luggage...

    They have also crammed 5 seats in the back row and 4 seats in the sideway facing ones.

    They have also managed to squeeze 3 seats between the entrance and exit doors, with 1 seat before the handlebar and 2 seats after the handlebar, just like the LO3x, VO3x.

    Those seats are new flat and thin VO2x style of seats, not the thick LO3x/VO3x seen on some other VO2x which have crammed 5 seats in the back row too.

    The only bad point about the standing area is that the windows there are very low.
