    11 Jun 13, 11:59

    In addition to the Volvo B5RH Hybrid trial that is ongoing in the city, Transperth (Perth, Australia) has also received TP2323, a B7RLE with the new generation Volgren "Optimus" bodywork.

    It has been almost a decade since Volgren's CR228L bodywork was first introduced, of which many old enthuasists would recall TP1854's visit to Singapore.

    I haven't managed to take any photos yet, but here are some taken by other enthuasists in Perth: http://www.busaustralia.com/forum/viewtopic.php?f=46&t=73502

    Flip-dot destination signage is still installed on new Transperth buses. Mobitec is one of the last remaining major manfacturers of such technology. Mobitec has also recently come under the ownership of Luminator (parent company of LAWO), just as Transit Media was absorbed by Mobitec many years back.

    In other news, Volvo has released the specification sheet for the upcoming Euro 6 B8RLE.
