    Actually if you take 853, you can just walk from Yio Chu Kang MRT to the Singapore Safety Driving Centre. Most people, including me, do that and it could be faster than waiting for 70 at Yio Chu Kang. I think you could take 854 to Yio Chu Kang Rd and alight at the bus-stop just after Hougang Point. Take either 70 to SSDC or 72 to Yio Chu Kang, whichever arrives first. This is if you want to reach SSDC fastest. Of course, if you're too lazy to walk and just want to utilise your concession on SBST buses, then go ahead and wait for 70. If you want to reach SSDC at the lowest cost (assuming you have to pay for TIBS buses), then you should take 25 or 55 to Hougang Ave 3 and change to 72 to Yio Chu Kang where you can walk or take 70.
