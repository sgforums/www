    It started off with 50 rts with 800 buses

    They are runned by pte bus operator... They are required to run 210 days in 1v yr...

    Operational hrs are during the peak hrs...

    There are 2 types... Scheme I are those in red colour, those that goes to CBD area... Scheme II are those in Blue colour, those going to other places like Airport PTB, Ind Est..

    625 is special case, it uses mini buses (Single entrance/ exit).
