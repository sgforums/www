    Nice try, though I bet that your photos can be much better. See the following three examples as a guide on how to take better photos.

    SBS 0442J - Service 142 (One of 'my beloved' BRBP Merc )
    SBS 0723Z - Service 325
    SBS 9022D - Service 30

    I guess that by seeing the photos, you would have noticed something in common: angle + sunlight. Basically you need to find a suitable location to take your photos. As on the part on sunlight, it is not necessary (though you must still make sure that you and I can see the bus in the photo), but recommended. Also try to take at angles with strong sunlight.
