    26 Nov 07, 00:26
    1st Volvo MK2 WAs were introduced on service 174 in 1988.

    Volvo MK2 DMs were also introduced on services 82, 83 and 84.

    Subsequent Volvo MK2s were deployed on services 1 (withdrawn), 62, 75, 105, 189, 190, 390 etc. They were new buses which was bought in 1988-1989 to replace mainly the Albion Viking Soon Chow buses.
  • TIB1018B
    26 Nov 07, 00:30
    To add on...Clementi, Bt Merah, Bt Batok, CCK, Hougang and Eunos feeders start to have Volvo MK2s after the progressive withdrawal of Volvo B57 buses with the purchase of Volvo MK4s in 1995. Toa Payoh, Bedok, Tampines and Pasir Ris feeders had the Scania N113 CRB replacement instead.

    Service 170 started to have progressive Volvo B57 Volvo MK2 WA redeployments only in early 1993. In 2001, all switched to Volvo MK3 AC buses overnight

    Not sure about service 177 and Bt Panjang feeders though, they still had Volvo B57s until these services were taken over by Tibs in mid 1995. Not sure whether Volvo MK2 WA were used before.

    Services 11, 77, 91, 151, 156, 191 started to have Volvo MK2s too after these services were downgraded to PAC in 1998
