    Bus Stops Closed for NDP Rehearsals

    Two bus stops along Raffles Avenue will be closed on June 23, June 30 and Aug 4 from 8am to 11pm to facilitate National Day Parade Rehearsals.

    Due to the closure of the first left lane of Raffles Ave, nine SBS Transit buses will not call at the two bus stops before Temasek Ave and opposite The Oriental hotel.

    The nine services are 36, 56, 70, 97, 111, 133, 162, 195 & 502.

    Commuters can use the bus stops opposite the Ritz Carlton Millenia Hotel, in front of the Esplanade or in front of the Esplanade or in front of the Pan Pacific Hotel instead.
