    Monday 02 Feb

    Not bus accident, not bus breakdown, but a traffic incident caused at least 17 SBST buses to be delayed and other buses diverted.

    In summary:
    At around 6.55 pm, a truck pulling a trailer with container stalled at the junction of West Coast Rd, Clementi Ave 2 and Clementi West St 2. The truck (XB 9378 G) was turning right from Clementi Ave 2 middle lane to West Coast Rd, the truck stalled and together with the trailer, blocked the entire 2 lanes of West Coast Rd towards Jln Buroh, vehicles coming from West Coast Plaza area cannot passed thru, but vehicles from Clementi Ave 2 can still managed to turn right to West Coast Rd or travel straight to Clementi West St 2. At least 17 SBST buses cannot passed thru the junction and were delayed as a result. 2 police cars and 1 TP bike were at the scene. The trailer was later connected to another truck which came to the rescue. Traffic started to clear an hour later.

    Affected bus services were 30, 51 and 143 in the direction of Jurong East / Boon Lay. Services 78, 175, 189, 282 and 285 were not affected as they still can managed to pass thru.

    The 17 buses delayed in order (from front to back) during the one hour were:
    143: SBS 7460 K (SL 143)
