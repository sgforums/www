    Man injured in chain collision

    A CHAIN collision involving a car, a taxi and two buses occurred on Saturday night (031009) along Tampines Avenue 10 .

     The vehicles were moving towards Kaki Bukit Avenue 1 at about 9.50pm when they collided into each other before the junction of Tampines Road.

    The car driver, a man in his early 20s, suffered serious head injuries and was unconscious when taken to the hospital.

    Two Thai nationals, who were passengers in the taxi, suffered slight injuries to their bodies.

    All three casualties were taken to Changi General Hospital.

    Police investigations are still ongoing.

    Eye-witnesses can call the Traffic Police hotline at 1800-547-1818.
