    Breakdown of SBS2752B on S111 at one stop before Ghim Moh Terminal (BS 11351, Blk 13), travelling towards Ghim Moh Terminal:

    I was travelling on this bus intending to go to Ghim Moh Terminal. It travelled fine before the breakdown and started to move very slowly when approaching the Blk 13 bus stop. When it stopped there, the driver declared breakdown in chinese and said in chinese that he could not accelerate the bus anymore. Remaining passengers intending to alight at Ghim Moh Terminal continued their journey by foot as it was just a short distance ahead. Here are some pictures taken after I alighted.

    As my intention of going to Ghim Moh Terminal was to wait outside there and try getting a picture of SBS2743C (Moove Love Advert), I observed that SBS2752B was left stationary at that bus stop and approximately 36 minutes later, SBS2752B managed to start moving and then went off without destos by making a turn at Ghim Moh Terminal alighting area. I am not sure what was being done to SBS2752B for it to be able to start moving after breakdown as I already moved away from the bus stop where it broke down.
