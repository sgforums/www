    Oct 29, 2009

    NSman dies after collision

    By Muhammad Nurluqman

    -- ST PHOTO: SAMUEL HUA

    A FULL-TIME national serviceman died on the spot after after his motorcycle collided with a bus just before 11pm on Wednesday.

    Mr Liow Wei Hua, 21, was believed to be on his way back to his camp in Mandai when he collided with SBS bus service No. 157 at the junction of Jurong Town Hall Road and Jurong East Avenue 1.

    He was travelling along Jurong Town Hall Road towards Bukit Batok Road when his bike collided with the bus that was turning right from the opposite direction.

    His pillion rider, a NSF of the same age, had multiple injuries and is now warded in the intensive care unit of National University Hospital.

    Mr Liow was pronounced dead at the scene by paramedics at about 11.15pm.

    Three bus passengers, all women, were taken to NUH with minor injuries. They were given outpatient treatment and discharged on Thursday.
