    On new years eve, an accident involving a a van and a car caused a massive obstruction at the junction between Hougang Ave 9 and Hougang Ave 4....

    Buses along ave 4 (88, 161, 159, 156, 109) all bunched up as they couldn't pass. The van left the scene sometime later, allowing those going straight (88, 161) to pass leaving the rest stuck as the car could not be moved.

    Those turning into ave 9 remained stuck. One 159 tried to get around the situation by.....going straight down ave 4, turned to ave 8, and then entered st 61 (like NR6), and then.....found itself stuck there by the stricken car, adding to the chaos.

    As the other buses saw the 159 emerge from the street, they almost turned out. Fortunately, the 159 driver ran out across the street and told them to stay put.

    So the endstate, at least when I was there was the lane turning into ave 9 blocked by buses. And st 61 also blocked by bus! When actually cars could manage the turns.

    Imagine all the curses heaped at the stuck 159 at the one-way street, st 61.
