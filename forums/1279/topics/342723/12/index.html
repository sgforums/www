  • Superbus
    15 Mar 09, 22:47

    TIB1069D was working on Service 913 when it was involved in a collision with a car near the junction of Marsiling Rise and Marsiling Lane. It was heading towards WRI. The collision caused both lanes of Marsiling Rise to be obstructed and hence unpassable to any traffic. WRI-bound Services 178 and 913 were re-routed via Admiralty Road and Marsiling Lane, before joining the normal route via Marsiling Rise.

    No substantial damage on the bus except for a panel just behind the 2nd axle which fell off due to the impact.

    Both vehicles were cleared nearly an hour later, and the bus left the scene for the depot approximately 15 minutes after that.

    What happened?

    At around 10.45 am, a Hyundai Avante car along Marsiling Rise was turning left towards Marsiling Lane, when it hit a cyclist at the junction, swerved and went up the kerb at the opposite side and crashed onto TIB1069D which was waiting to turn to Marsiling Rise. Except for the cyclist who suffered abrasions, no one else was injured in the collision.

    Edited to add time of the accident.
