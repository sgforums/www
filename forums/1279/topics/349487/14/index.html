    rego formation

    from
    9 to 0 (except 99-00) +4 suffixes
    1 to 9 +2 suffixes per number.
    99 to 00 -3 suffixes.

    Example:
    SBS2800X (AMDEP 162)
    -3 (U, T, S) W and V is not suffixes.
    SBS2799S (AMDEP 166)
    +2 (T, U)
    SBS2798U (BBDEP 506)
    +2 (X, Y) V and W not suffixes.
    SBS2797Y (HGDEP 100)
    +2 (Z, A)
    SBS2796A (BBDEP SP)
    +2 (B, C)
    SBS2795C (BNDEP 15?) iirc
    +2 (D, E)
    SBS2794E (BNDEP 19)
    +2 (G, H) F not a suffix
    SBS2793H (BRBP 88)
    +2 (J, K) I not a suffix.
    SBS2792K (BNDEP 38/566*)
    +2 (L, M)
    SBS2791M (BNDEP 10)
    +2 (P, R) N and Q not suffixes.
    SBS2790R (AMDEP 162)
    +4 (S, T, U, X) V and W not suffixes.
    SBS2789X (BBDEP SP)
    +2 (Y, Z)
    SBS2788Z (SLBP SP)
    pattern goes on.
