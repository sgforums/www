    hi all

    Before taking the yu tong , i knew a few things

     

    1) i had taken Yu tong coaches in hong kong and Singapore. I expected a more neoMAn sound to the bus
    2) Suspension and ride comfort and the occassional air breaking sounds will enthuse me

    Both 1 and 2 were true for the citybus version

    I will compare the bus now to a regular China Yu tong and explain some detials

    Despite commenst on large windows, i feel the windows were short changed. Some Yu tong citybus in China have full length windows . these are full standee hopper window ala Chua heng bodywork styles. the glassed part above the windows reveals that the windows could had been extended to this height thus making the interior birghter. insetad we have glass peices there. drat.

    the Chiense buses have flat EDS and the numbers are display in calculator LED digits. As such they need not have a big EDS. Becuase of this restriction, our Yu tong too has a flatter and smaller EDS.

    Others comments + reviews :-
    the air-con ducting and air-con panelling design is of European origin. in particualr , Neoplan buses use this style. the Chiense bus makers Dong Feng and Yu Tong have adopted this. the Yu tong bus windows too are of neoplan design origin.

    the Yu tong bus front also has a neoplan style. but a disappointment here is that SMRT left the tapered front accentuation caved in insetad without  the breat glass peice. With a black glass peice the front the bujs will look more of a regular Yu tong bus. in fact the front breast glass piece looks to me very Neo plan as well.


    Suspension and parts:-
    it comes as no surpise to me the bus has air suspension. the Chinese do pride themselves in using full European parts. All Suspension, and sterring and gearbox parts come from Zf. essentially the bus underbelly parts are all European orgin. as such the ride comfort is to be expected.

    the bus uses the standard Cummins engine for Chinese buses. Some of u might thinkgthis sounds like Dennis. Dennis uses Cummins, thats why. However to me the bus sounds more of a Neoplan- Neo-MAN bus side. A pity was that the turbo sounds were not loud

    ZF gearbox:
    the bus works well with the ZF. catcalls are nice. When slowing down, the bus does not go into bursts of engine sounds unlike scania or merc with ZF6HP series gearbox. good diffrentilas were adjusted to the gearbox to give us a good fast accleration.

    bad points

    i need not elaborate on the EDS. the rear door is special. using a study constructiona nd method of using, the door plugs out using undereath opulleys, thus doors do not virbate. however the Yu tong bus comes porgram with door safety opening beeps. thats irritating. furthermore the bus rear door squeaks

     

    1) clip the wire to the beep (lol) (Short it, if u will)

    2) oil the door please.

    the bus comes with the standard 2 step to rear design. the tapering of the sharp edges do not help. instead less leg room on step.

    the bus interior is dull. except for the cool air-con panelling, the bus has no special astetic looks.

    Sorely missing is the Yu tong branding all over Yu tong Bus. seats? nothing no bdage. SL did not lower down sun filter, maybe have Yu tong ke Che. only Yu tong logo i found was on the window pane.

    Nor did i notice the Yu tong clamshell icon brand at front of bus

     

    ZYX

    i do not know if anyone has posted this info before

     

    but always good to remind

     

    the Chinese Chracters for Yu tong

    first chracter is Space - in particular a asteriod belt of sorts

     

    but with the "Tong" character, the two words combine to mean

    "Milky Way" the most common asteriod belt nearest to us.

    the word "tong" cna also mean trasnport and transit.

    The clamshell logo origin i dunno. but it represents clamshell.

    theoretically sea based bus logos may be based on  Irisbus (two dolphin)

    model no 61xx

    China bus models numbers have been a puzzle for me. All bus brands all seem to follow a common pattern. Eg: King Long KL6120UI, Yu tong ZK6126

     

    some cute guesses

    first digit 6 to signify is a bus products

    next two digit signify length of bus (eg 12m)

    last digit is model mark. eg we have Volvo Mark 2. etc
