    20 Aug 09, 21:37
    Originally posted by sbs&tibs:

    A short review.

    Took it with my classmate from Yishun on the 11am+ trip. Configuration of the bus is simlar to Batch 2 K230s, just that  it has 4 seats at the back compared to the usual 5. Seat belts installed for the middle 2 seats in th last row. It was quite cramp seating at the last row.

    Seat colour: Red for the needy, light brown for the standard seats. The light brown colour was very plain and dull. You can hear noise when people position themselves on the seats. Haha.

    No interior rego. Some squeaking sounds when rear door functions. Interior as usual, looks very home made from China, got those ventilation windows, etc.

    Destination and service number on rear EDS, and can even scroll. Format is the same as the front side EDS. Number on the right. Which meas you see the same thing for front, side and rear. Wierd.

    ZF gearbox for sure. Sounds like OC or some say 8030L.

    Luckily not too much announcements along te way. The bus was not very quiet imo. Bus jerks when stops. Loud handbrakes (reminds me of bbdep buses).

    Out of 10, I would probably give a 6. I didn't really enjoy the ride. Haha.

    OT: SBS8903T also got the squeaking sound.
