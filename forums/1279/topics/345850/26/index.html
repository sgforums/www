Just for e.g, hispano o405 rigids probably uses the least time for passengers to board and alight, as theres no bus stop lock fitted (the bus can move off while the driver closes the door at the same time), OCs cant do this due to the safety feature. If the two types are mixed together, it may also be a factor why buses bunch together.
