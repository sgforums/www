    The Transperth demonstrator (TP.1854) featured a model M 447 hLAG Euro 5/EEV natural gas engine.  The SMRT ones have a model OM 457 hLA Euro 5/EEV SCR diesel engine.  Apart from the bodywork and maybe some minor operator-related differences, they are both built on the same chassis.  TP.1854 is still a good performer and has covered quite a high number of kilometres, despite having been laid up for several months, since its return to Australia.
