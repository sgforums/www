    alright alright

     

    REVIEW  of the bus

     

    "

    I chance upon a ride in the all new SMb1H , a mercedes benz OC500LE of Singapore.

     

    I already had a preview of the bus on my Campus grounds on 28 apr 2008 (NUS). Already noted some extra features (no ride then)

     

    the wheellchair user will have seatbelts for xtra safety. this feature CAN ONLY be found in Hong Kong buses.

     

    the door closing chime might get irritating.

     

    the pls move to the rear announcement is cute.

     

    In terms of ride comfort, i chose a outer seat on my first ride. someone feedback to me that a inner seat ride will not be so good. I also tried a inner seat ride few days ago. i found the feedback to be true.

     

    also at the rear, there is no jutting out wheel curve, so ur leg dun feel chui after a long ride in an awkward postion.

     

    i will cocnentrate on the ride i had in a outer seat at rear middle.  the bus is soft. (dun sit at rearmost seats!), engine sounds like a Hino crossed with a Scania L113. however posesses much of a australian OC500LE sound as well. the retarding -final stage- ZF whine is very nice to my ears.

     

    I still find that the bus revving engine like mad when we slow down. it is bit rather irritating. much like how i found it irritating whilst on TP1854.

     

    when the bus managed to shift to 5th gear, we can hear a sort of a hub reduction sound. very in line with any Zf 6 speed bus. However what stunned me the most was when the bus hit the 6th gear.

     

    at 6th gear, IF you sit in a outer seat at the rear middle, u get this floating sensation. this is attributed to the engine coming down to a very low revolution rate, coupled with a superior suspension, u literally feel like you are floating on BKE. upon exiting form 6th gar, the bus does not have a pronnouned cat-call. this floating sensation CANNOT be found in any citybus in Singapore perhaps except TIb840 when u sit in front. floating sensation an be easily felt in good suspension scania coaches of the SJE Scania L113 (sit in front)

     

    However, the very loud whine ptiching before exiting form 6th gar rminds us that other units may have potential to have cat calls. i look forward to rides on other units when they come out. just for the floating sensations.

     

    ZYX
