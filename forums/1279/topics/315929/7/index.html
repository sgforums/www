    The OF, OC, and OH designations are used for chassis' produced by Mercedes-Benz do Brasil Ltda.  Coincidently, MBdB produces a chassis known as the OH 1830 LE, also known as the O 500 U.  The "F", "C", and "H" in this case stands for front-engined, centre-engined, and hind-engined respectively.
