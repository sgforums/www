    23 Nov 07, 20:15
    With effect from 26 Nov 2007 (Mon), the timing of the last 179A bus to leave NTU will be extended from the existing 1930 hours to 2000 hours. This will be applicable to the remaining examination days as well as for the normal academic school term. The service will not be available during Saturdays, Sundays, Public Holidays and during the NTU Vacation Period.

    This change by SBS Transit is based on feedback that many students are having difficulties in getting a bus out of NTU after the examination which end at 1900 hours or 1930 hours.
