    just rode on a bus from bus interchange to my house. Along the way, I observed the number of people alighting at each bus stop.

    wanted to write about it at "Bus Observations", but apparently that's for fleet-checking, not.... pattern?

    apparently, there is not yet a thread for people to write out their observations on individual alighting/boarding patterns at every bus stop. so, this is what this new thread is for.

    ----

    Was on bus service 243W at around 1735hrs. Bus was SBS8768S.

    When bus left the bus interchange, there were about 40 people, still have ample space for more people, but apparently, that's all the people wanting to take 243W.

    1. 1st bus stop: 3 people alight
    2. 2nd bus stop: 6 people alight
    3. 3rd bus stop: around 10 people alight
    4. 4th bus stop: around 5 people alight
    5. 5th bus stop: nobody alight
    6. 6th bus stop: 15 people alight
    7. 7th bus stop: 3 people alight

    by the 7th bus stop, the bus has (almost) "re-cycled" itself. 5th bus stop nobody alight very likely is because people there prefer to take 241/179 instead of 243W. the bulk of the initial loading alighted at St 61 (3rd) and Ave 5 (6th) bus stops.
