Consecutive Connections

  • tranquilice
    23 May 04, 19:32
    Ever tried consecutive connections before?

    For example, I'm at Tampines interchange and want to go to Boon Keng MRT station. This is the consecutive connections:

    Service 8 from Tampines interchange to Tampines Ave 2 (Blk 302)
    Service 9 to Bedok Rd (Bedok Shopping Complex)
    Service 10 to Mountbatten Rd (opp Handicapped Voc School)
    Service 11 to Mountbatten Rd (opp Gay World Park)
    Service 12 to Kallang Rd (after ERP gantry)
    Service 13 to Boon Keng MRT station

    So, I took services that go: 8 9 10 11 12 13.

    What's about your's?

    Rule: Can only board the next bus at the same bus stop/opposite bus stop/interchange/terminal where you last alighted from and no-where else.
