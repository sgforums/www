    Route amendment to Service 804 with effect from Sunday, 25th April 2004

    In conjunction with the opening of Yishun Street 41, Service 804 will be amended to ply along Yishun Central 1, Yishun Ave 2, Yishun Ring Rd, (Yishun Street 41), Yishun Ave 1 and Yishun Ave 11 with effect from Sunday, 25th April 2004.

    As such, Service 804 will call at the new bus stop outside Northbrooks Secondary School along Yishun Ring Rd.

    It will no longer call at the bus stops outside Blk 419 (Yishun Ave 11) and opposite Blk 433 (Yishun Ave 1). Affected passengers can board the service at the opposite side.

    On the same day, the shortworking variant, Service 804A will be withdrawn. Other operating details remain unchanged.
