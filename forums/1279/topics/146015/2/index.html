    Originally posted by CAIX:
    Oh yes, 1 question in my mind. Is there any MK 1 in the B10 M Family ? How come SBS 0009 C dosen't have any MK ?? only Vanhool Body ?
    SBS 9C was a Mk I B10M. It didn't have any Mk because when it was made in the 1980s, it didn't occur to Volvo that they would be making a Mk II and subsequently a Mk III and Mk IV. Bear in mind that there is no Mk V in the B10M family. The B10M is succeeded by the B12M.

    SBS 9C remained the one and only Van Hool bodied bus of SBS. It was sold to a coach operator in New Zealand and is still running today. But AFAIK, the original Van Hool body had been replaced.
