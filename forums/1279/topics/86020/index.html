    This list specifies first bus timings for services that have SWT (other than those stated like '105B') that departs from a bus stop halfway along a long route. This is provided as the TransitLink Guide & other bus guides only provides first & last bus timings from their origin interchange/terminal even though there are such morning SWTs to make sure those commuters towards the end of a long route need not wait for too long and be late for their work/school.

    For such purposes, looping point is provided as the 'direction' if it is a loop service. Brown colour is used to denote day(s) where SWTs are not applicable. Previous stops will not be applicable for bus stops after an express sector.

    More to be added. Additions welcome.
