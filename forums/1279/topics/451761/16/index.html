    Double deckers carry more pax than bendies

    Seating capacity: 82 to 85

    Standing capacity: 50 pax

    Max capacity: 130 pax but mostly 120 pax is what it is.

    For bendies

    Seating capacity: 57 to 60

    Standing capacity: 50 pax (including the joint area)

    Max capacity: 110 pax

    Bendies have smaller corridors, whereas DDs (Wrights have) 2 parking spaces for wheelchairs and a big standing area. This standing area is similar to that of the joint between bendies, where there is nothing to hold and very uncomfortable to stand.

    Most often bendies can carry only 100 pax.

    Both in terms of comfort (seating space) and number of pax - DDs are better.

    Yes bendy has one huge advantage: an extra door. But while boarding it is still one door!!!
