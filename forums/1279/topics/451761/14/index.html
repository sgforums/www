    For all those who have asked me to make a list of SMRT routes that should use DDs.

    61 - 2 DDs from each side. Total 4 max

    67 - bendies (2 bendies from each side once DTL opens)

    106 - 75% DD fleet

    169 - 75% DD fleet

    176- 50% DD fleet (move terminating point to Harbour front center or LTA to take action on trees on Henderson road) or 50% bendy fleet

    180 - 50% DD fleet

    184 - 100% DD fleet (branches / trees cannot be the reason why DDs cannot ply - decision is upto LTA)

    188 - 75% DD fleet

    189 - 75% DD fleet

    190 - 100% DD fleet

    800/804/806/811/812 - combination of DD / bendies can be used

    851 - 25% DD fleet

    852 - 2 DDs from each side. max 4 DD or bendy

    854 - 100% DD fleet

    855 - 2 DDs

    856 - DD or bendy

    857 - 2 DDs max

    858M - full day service 3 DDs

    900/901/903/911/912/913 - combination of DD / bendy / SD can be used

    960 - No bendy will be required once DTL opens. Till then bendy operations

    963 - 75% DD fleet

    962/964 - combination of DD / bendy / SD can be used

    969 - 100% DD or bendy

    980 / 985 - max 2 DDs 

    If you check the other routes, they do not require bendy or DD. So most of the worthy routes are covered by DDs :))
