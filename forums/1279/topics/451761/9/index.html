    i shall just repost the pros and cons of bendies

    Again, just my opinion, DDs have pros too.

    1.80km/h(efficient)

    2.Better acceleration from 1st to 4th gear

    3.Elderly friendly for seniors who dread stairs on DDs

    4.Higher capacity provided if people move 2 the rear

    5.Faster boarding and alighting(DDs have stairs and narrower doors)

    6.Feeder/Intratown svcs

    7.Clear the height clearances.

    Cons

    1.Take up road space

    2.Take up parking space

    3.Overheats easily

    4.Bad at doing 3 point turns, reverse turns and sharp turns(U-turns is ok)

    DD=Save space and to help us block the sunlight on a hot day.(to put it simply)
