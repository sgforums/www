    SBS139K cameoing on Sv 168 (22/1/06)

    Yesterday, 22/1/06, while I was in my parent's car, I witnessed that two of the cars that were turning the wrong way. Instead of turning left to Woodlands Ave 3, they used the road that leads to Woodlands Reg Int & the bus bay.

    The bus driver of SBS139K had asked the car driver to reverse and get back from the last road on where he had turned wrongly, which was at Woodlands Ave 5. Therefore, the other passengers were wasting at least 2 minutes of their journey time, and some were impatient.
