    I guess we cannot deny that buses can reach speeds that are faster than so-called permissible limits. But bearing in mind that speedometers in Singapore, fitted to every vehicle except for Police/Fire Department vehicles and except for those that are tempered with, shows only 95 percent of the true speed.
