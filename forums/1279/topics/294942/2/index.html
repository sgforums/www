    Are there any crossover between 60, 63 and 94?

    S60 used to have its 2 split shifts doing xover to 94 (PM Peak) and 63A (AM Peak) respectively.

    94's 2 buses xover to 63A during AM peak. They do 1 trip 63A at 6+, then 7+ do 94 (thanks to people booking in at 8am), then do 2 trips 63A before going back to depot.

    S139 has 2 extra buses on Sat (total 14), 5 more on Sun (total 17). Naturally they will use S94's buses because they dont operate on Sunday.

    Sgwiki got wrong info about svc 189. Presently there are 4 split shift bendies, not three as indicated.

    Currently School Holidays are over, the duties are S101, S102, S201 and S103. S103 still cross over to 77A for one trip.

    The duties in detail...
    S101, four full 189 trips in morning, two full 189 trips in the evening.
    S102, three full 189 trips in the morning, two full 189 trips in the evening.
    S201, two full 189 trips in the morning, four full 189 trips in the afternoon/evening, meal break in afternoon.
    S103, one 77A trip, two full 189 trips in the morning, two full 189 trips in the evening.
    S104 (School Holidays only), two full 189 trips in the morning, two full 189 morning trips in the evening.
    TQ01 (Sunday only), six full 189 trips as indicated 'three quarter', Meal break and Coffee.

    Presently all the duties are run by bendies. BC rotate among themselves.
