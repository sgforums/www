    Thank you for the meticulous recommendations.

    I would like to inform you that in any amendment made to any route, there will be people who would gain, while some skeptically affected. It is therefore necessary that the repercussion on patrons are gingerly appraised in advance of any amendment we may hereafter carry out.

    I have delved into your feedback to alter service 502 and introduce services 501 and 505.

    From my findings, it would appear that a consequential faction of existing patrons will be adversely affected if we change the route of service 502. They would lose their direct link between Jurong and the Central Business District, where the Orchard Road and the Marina Bay are located.

    I have seen the route of service 501 which you had proposed. While I acknowledge that this duplicates the route of existing service 502 between Jurong and the Central Business District, I am sorry to inform you that this is not a better alternative to how the existing route of service 502 is designed.

    The existing route of service 502 is economical, considering it takes the shortest possible alignment to navigate to and from the two areas it serve. Should we apply the route of service 501 you had advised, additional mileage would be incurred and fares may have to increase to correspond with the rise in costs of operations as a result.

    With regards to the idea of introducing service 505 between Jurong East and Seng Kang, I wish to inform you that it is not financially practical to run this service, as my observations have shown that very little people travel between these two places at any period of the day.

    Bearing in mind of the above, I am sorry to inform you that I am not able to acquiesce your requests. In spite of that, every suggestion is important. Do rest assured that all of us here have noted your suggestions and will allow for them when we want to suggest express bus routes.

    Thank you for writing in.
