    Let's consider some key points on the 171 route.

    • (A)  Yishun Int
    • (B)  Bt Panjang east
    • (C)  Bt Panjang west
    • (D)  Beauty World / Pei Hwa / KAP
    • (E)  Botanic Gardens
    • (F)  Stevens
    • (G)  Newton Circus
    • (H)  Orchard
    • (I)  Promenade / Marina Centre

    - C-G directly follows DTL2, and has the most duplications. C-F follows 67 & 170, while G-I follows 700. However, C-B-F-H is more effectively served by 190, 700 and 972. Also, A-E-D is back-uped by 852.

    - Most of the demand is between A-C/D. Some demand for D/E-H; depending on the Newton MRT transit area, this may or may not be transferred over to DTL.

    ===================================================

    Consider the transport links without 171 (A-C is only served by 171, and must remain):

    • From A: A-D is also served by 852, while A-G/H/I has alternative services 167, 851, 857 and 980, beside the MRT. A-E/F is unique, but demand is low and an MRT transfer from Newton is more efficient.
    • From B: B-D has 184. B-E is a little difficult, but 700 can be amended there if need be. 
    • From C: Aside for A-C, no problems: 67, 170, DTL up to Newton, and the rest covered by 190, 700, 972.
    • From D: No problems. E/F/G all covered by 66, 67, 170 and DTL. D-H also has 77 & 174, D-I also has 75 & 77.
    • From E: Unique link to B and H/I.
    • From F/G/H/I: Alternatives present to A/B/C/D. Only E-H/I is not directly served.

    Thus, if rationalisation is desireable, a suitable cutting point would be near D or E, with additional links for E-B and E-H/I.

    On 171:

    - Newton is NOT an interchange between NSL and DTL.

    (Bukit Panjang is also not an interchange beween DTL and BPLRT but I would not elaborate here)

        - The only transfer linkway at NSL Newton is near Exit A (at the right-angle turn after the escalator). As it's not feasible to relocate the fare gates (unlike NSL/CCL Marina Bay), this means that whoever wants to transfer from NSL and DTL will need to tap out at NSL, go to the transfer linkway, and tap in again at DTL.

        - If you go to DTL Newton Exit C at Bt Timah Rd, you will see the station code is only DT11, without NS21.

        - Bus stop at Bt Timah Rd has separate sighboards for DTL and NSL.

        - If I'm not wrong, LTA did mention that fare calculation methods will be adjusted when DTL opens. But still, be mentally prepared that a 'transfer' between DTL and NSL will not be counted.

     

    - Agree mostly with AJQZC, but:

        - 171 is the only service from Bukit Timah to Newton NSL (besides 700 that serves some bus stops). Which is important due to inconvenient transfers between DTL and NSL; and those going to City Hall / Raffles Place can also transfer to NSL as well. (similarly 700 is also an important service connectiong to Newton NSL - I will post in loading levels thread later.)

        - 171 is also the only service from Bukit Timah to Bukit Panjang interior. This is often used by students in schools along Bukit Timah, although its populatiry remains uncertain after DTL opens.
