    Still thinking of a way to tweak the BPJ - Mandai sector, I'm not the most familiar with that sector so I'm not too sure about what exactly to change yet. What I'm thinking is that 976 plies the full length during peak hours while 976 becomes Express along that stretch during non-peak. Of course, this might not be the most feasible.

    YCK stretch from Upp Thomson to Jln Kayu IMO shouldn't be made express along SLE, because the lack of coverage from any of the nearby services for the exact stretch between Nee Soon / Springleaf to Castle Green / YCK has no direct connection. Reasonable loading on 138 for that exact stretch but 138 funnels the loading back to AMK which is an indirect route to nearest station. Bus jumping occurs quite frequently because of 138 turning into AMK when pax want to continue down YCK road.

    976 can also serve AMK Industrial area - providing additional capacity for AMK Industrial - Jln Kayu stretch alongside 86 and 163. Generally fairly heavy loading around this sector during peak hours, with lunchtime traffic going in the other direction.
