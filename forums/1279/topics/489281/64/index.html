    MOE needed the land for the building of Victoria School in preparation for its integrated program. 196 extended to Bedok as it was too long to loop. The finishing touches were being applied to UEC. I know because my old army unit was located right next to it. The camp has since been demolished and its servicemen moved to another garrison.

    Later, 13 & 853 were extended to UEC. SBS then intro 48, 43 & 45 from scratch while the ex-MP routes continued to loop at Siglap. In the meantime, 608 was withdrawn, bringing the CSS story to a close. This paved the way for 16 to be extended to Bedok 2 years later. In 2010, 76 was amended to loop at Marine Tce. 506 was extended to UEC, providing a direct link between Bedok Reservoir and Bedok South. 48 was also extended to BNDEP via TNM MRT.

    This leaves 55. 135 & 155 still looping at Siglap. 155 could be extended to CBP, but it would duplicate 47. In the old days, 155 originated from Somapah, which made way for the current SUTD building.
