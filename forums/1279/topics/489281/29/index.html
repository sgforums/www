    If want to revert to original route, might as well let 242 enter Jalan Boon Lay directly from Jurong West Central 3, and enter Boon Lay Bus Interchange via Jalan Boon Lay, Boon Lay Way, Jurong West Central 2 and Jurong West Central 3.

    Then there will be complaints again, from the people staying at Jurong West Street 91. One is about long travel to Boon Lay MRT station. The other is about having to share bus with people staying at Neighborhood seven, that it is hard to take the bus.

    Alternatively, you could use my suggestion of having a new bus route from Boon Lay Bus Interchange that goes to Jurong West Street 91, via Jurong West Street 62, 61 and 71, Jurong West Avenue 5 and Pioneer Road North.

    There's Pioneer Mall and the future Jurong West Hawker Centre at Jurong West Street 61. At Jurong West Street 62, there's JW primary school, and BL secondary school is more accessible via this new route.

    This new route can ply Jurong West Street 91 in both directions, where after Jurong West Street 91, it can go to Joo Koon Bus Interchange, via Upper Jurong Road, Kian Teck Road, International Road and Joo Koon Circle. :)

    Besides, this route provides the shortest connection from Boon Lay Bus Interchange to Jurong West Street 91. If you stay at Jurong West Street 91, you may find this new route more attractive than 241. :)
