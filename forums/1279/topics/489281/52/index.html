    I was checking the Beeline routes and found out 4 new services (B46-B49) to Changi Naval Base. I wanted to suggest a peak-hour service, and after some modification, I came up with this instead.

    Extension of svc 17A: Service 44 & 44M

    44: Bedok - Changi Naval Base (Loop)

    (Operation during peak hours only)

    44M: Bedok - Tanah Merah MRT (Loop)

    (Operation during off-peak hours when 44 is not operating)

    https://www.google.com/maps/d/edit?mid=zvASY1AjT64M.kx34hn591RE4&usp=sharing

     

    - New public bus from Tanah Merah MRT to Changi Naval Base

    - Link from Bedok North Ave 4 to Tanah Merah MRT, for more direct MRT transfers and to connect to other services at Tanah Merah MRT (e.g. 12, 24, 38, 47)

    - New service for Upp Changi Rd and Tanah Merah Kechil Rd which don't have a service today (although there were old bus stops)

    - Improve utility and uniqueness of 17A

    - Can be amended to Changi Airport T5 once it opens

     

    Cons:

    - Changi Naval Base may not have sufficient demand (but Xilin Ave does)

    - Too windy around Tanah Merah

    Extension of Service 63 (Eunos - Bukit Merah)

    Direction 1
    Eunos Int, (Eunos Rd 2), Sims Ave, Jln Eunos, Eunos Link, Ubi Ave 2, Ubi Ave 1, Ubi Rd 1, Ubi Ave 2, Lor Bengkok, Circuit Rd, Pipit Rd, Circuit Rd, (Merpati Rd), Mattar Rd, Aljunied Rd, Geylang Rd, Kallang Rd, Crawford St, North Bridge Rd, South Bridge Rd, Upp Cross St, New Bridge Rd, Outram Rd, Tiong Bahru Rd, Redhill Cl, Redhill Rd, (Jln Tiong), Lengkok Bahru, Hoy Fatt Rd, Jln Rumah Tinggi, (Hoy Fatt Rd), Jln Bt Merah, Bt Merah Central and Bt Merah Int.

    Direction 2
    Bt Merah Int, Bt Merah Central, Jln Bt Merah, (Hoy Fatt Rd), Jln Rumah Tinggi, Hoy Fatt Rd, Lengkok Bahru, (Jln Tiong), Redhill Rd, Redhill Cl, Tiong Bahru Rd, Outram Rd, Eu Tong Sen St, Hill St, Victoria St, Kallang Rd, Sims Ave, Aljunied Rd, Merpati Rd, Circuit Rd, (Lor Bengkok), Ubi Ave 2, Ubi Rd 1, Ubi Ave 1, Ubi Ave 2, Eunos Link, Jln Eunos, Changi Rd, Jln Turi, (Sims Ave, Eunos Rd 2) and Eunos Int.

    - Uses red/white plates like many other existing services to differentiate between Eunos-bound and Bukit Merah-bound trips.
    - Provides connections between the residential blocks along Redhill Cl, Redhill Rd, Hoy Fatt Rd and Jln Rumah Tinggi and Bt Merah Int.
    - Provides an alternative service for the residential blocks along Lengkok Bahru to Bt Merah Int apart from existing Service 139.
    - Allocates a second originating/terminating point for the lengthy route of Service 63 with hopes to improve frequency levels and reduce bunching.
