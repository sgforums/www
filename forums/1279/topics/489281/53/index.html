    Rationale:

    • -- 852 has terrible frequency, even though parts of its route face high demand. This splits the route up and manage both halves with different frequencies to cater to the different needs.
    • -- Route sections of 852 between Bt Batok and Marymount are already well managed by 74 and 157, and there are many alternatives along Dunearn / Bt Timah Rd with the opening of the DTL. Remaining demand from 852 is mostly long-distance from AMK Ave 6 / Marymount to Upp Bt Timah / Bt Batok. This sector is now made express (848E).
    • -- Northern end extended to Sembawang to improve connections.

    Cons:

    • -- May increase strain on AMDEP.
    • -- Loading may not justify a new service, demand may still be low.
    • -- Possible waste of resources - other regions (Yishun East, Sengkang, Punggol) need buses more urgently.
