    Sv 117: Punggol Interchange <> Sembawang Interchange

    Punggol Interchange -- Punggol Central -- Punggol East -- Punggol Dr -- Punggol Rd -- Punggol Field -- Punggol Way -- TPE -- Seletar West Link (express) -- West Camp Road (3 stops) -- Yishun Ave 1 -- Yishun St 41 -- Yishun Ring Road -- Yishun Ave 9 -- Yishun Ave 6 -- Yishun Ave 7 -- Yishun Ave 2 -- Canberra Rd -- Sembawang Way -- Sembawang Cres -- Sembawang Dr -- Sembawang Interchange

    ** Offers all unique connections w/ very little duplication

    1. Intra-town within Punggol connecting Punggol central, Punggol dr, Punggol West (no service today ~ for part of it 386 does it very poorly!)

    2. Connects all of Punggol town (except Punggol field east) to Northern suburbs with quick link to Yishun West town

    3. Cuts through SAP quickly using Selatar West Link, but calls at few stops to facilitate future transfer to 102/103 as well as serving the SAP workers

    4. Connects different parts of Yishun than 103 providing trunk service to Ring road, ave 1, ave 9, ave 6, ave 7

    5. Connects Sembawang MRT quickly to Yishun West town (those going to Sembawang, need not take feeder to Yishun and then MRT)

    6. Connects Yishun Industrial Estate / Yishun Ave 2 / Canberra Rd to Sembawang MRT (no service today, especially important with upcoming Canberra estate)

    7. Connects Sembawang Crescent to Yishun town (today have 859 that is quite long winding + Sembawang Cres has quite a few developments coming up)

    I think this service will also do well with high capacity buses... but maybe first to start with all SD fleet, and then upgrade as done for sv 4/35.
