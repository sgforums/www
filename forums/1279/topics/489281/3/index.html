    Come on now, if you are not going to be detailed on your suggested route, taking considerations on existing parallel routes and physical feasibility (bus fleet, road, slope, use of DDs etc.), how do you expect us to know what you're talking about?

    It could well be a one-to-one direct service, with one stop at the start point and one at the end point. Anyone can suggest an infinitesimal number of such services, including one direct from their house to their school / workplace / whatever. Will that make any progress, or have any hope of possible implementation?

    Now say I want a service from Tuas Ave 11 to Canberra Rd. Or one from Redhill Close to Tengah Air Base. Or one from Bedok Int to Neo Tiew. Then other people would look at me strangely, and ask me to hail a taxi or drive my own car. Why bother introducing one-to-one services, let alone those that travel such bizarre and unpopular routes, for public transport when you need to cater to the public's demands.

    If you still don't get it, BE SPECIFIC in your suggestions, on routes, or justification for its demand as a public service. Your route doesn't have to be perfect, but it has to be useful in a known way and serve a purpose. If you can't be bothered to do that, then go to the Crapbox and speculate all you want, but don't irritate us all with empty hotheaded fantasies here.

    Just my two cents worth of advice, which I hopefully won't find a need to repeat.
