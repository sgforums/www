    Since sv 99 bunching is such a huge issue... here is my proposal for sv 99 modification to reduce bunching

    Clementi Interchange <> Boon Lay Interchange

    Clementi Interchange >> Jurong West st 91 >> Jurong West st 93 (skips st 92 bus stop) >> Upper Jurong Road (express) >> Pioneer Road North >> Jurong West st 63 (Pioneer MRT) >> Jurong West st 61 >> Jurong West st 62 >> Boon Lay Interchange.

    Opposite direction:

    will do st 91 bi-directional (with new stops), again skips st 92. 

    With this change, 99 will have two terminal points. Also covers missing link of 257, when 257 starts to operate from Joo Koon Interchange.

    This change to sv 99 will further enhance the intra-town connectivity of sv 99, while also relieving the load on 241 to some extent.
