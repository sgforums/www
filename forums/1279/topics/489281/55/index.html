    Sims Place area is really quite poorly served I feel. There are very few connections to nearby amenities.

    I would suggest extending 140 up to Sims Place and thus follow 20's old route. This would provide connectivity to Kallang Swimming Complex, Kolam Ayer CC,  Geylang Bahru Market, Upper Boon Keng Market etc. Only problem is that this will duplicate 64 to some extent as these destinations can be reached by walking from Bendemeer Rd. However, as Bendemeer is a one-way street, 64 really only provides a one-way connection. Walking out to Serangoon road is very far. 

    Of course, as has been discussed in LTA BSEP Part 4, truncating 76 at Sims Pl would also create a connection to Geylang Rd/Paya Lebar. 

    I reiterate my extension of 11 up to Sims Pl.

    Other than that, I would also propose this service. 

    Service 126 (Sims Pl <> Eunos)

    Sims Pl Ter > Sims Dr > Aljunied Rd > Lor 22 Geylang > Guillemard Rd > Cassia Link > Old Airport Rd > Haig Rd > Amber Rd > Marine Parade Rd > Siglap Rd > Frankel Av > Changi Rd > Jln Turi > Eunos Int

    1. Two-way service between Sims Pl estate and Aljunied MRT. Right now 125 only provides one-way service. 

    2. Connection between Sims Pl and amenities in Guillemard (i.e. Singapore Badminton Hall, Kong Hwa Sch, Mountbatten CC) , Old Airport Rd (Old Airport Rd Food Centre, Dakota MRT)

    3. Connection between Sims Pl and amenities at Marine Parade/Katong. Plus, enhances connection to Parkway Parade from Dakota MRT which is served by 31 only. 

    4. Serves the full stretch of Marine Parade Rd to connect all schools, and provides a new connection to Changi Rd & Frankel Av. 

    Total route length - 11.4 km

    The other alternative is Sims Pl Ter > Geylang East Central > Paya Lebar Rd > Tanjong Katong Rd > Marine Parade Rd (Continue with same route to Eunos)

    1. Connect Sims Pl to ammenities at Geylang East Central such as Geylang East Library and Geylang Swimming Complex

    2. Connection from Geylang East Central to Marine Parade

    However, this would remove the connection between Sims Pl and Aljunied MRT which I feel is much more pressing.

    was drawing on maps, when suddenly I realise I may have found the ultimate solution to improve the feeder bus routes in Jurong West Extension town.

    I know I ever said before that I had found the best, but I feel that this is even better.

    The whole idea is to re-route 242 to become like Sengkang's 372, while re-route 243G/W to become like Punggol's 382G/W.

     

    242: Central 3 > Street 64 > Avenue 4 > Street 72 > Avenue 5 > Pioneer Road North > Street 63 > Street 65 > Street 62 > Street 61 > Street 63 > Pioneer Road North > Avenue 5 > Street 72 > Avenue 4 > Street 64 > Central 3

    243G: Central 3 > Street 64 > Central 1 > Jalan Boon Lay > Avenue 4 > Street 75 > Street 82 > Street 81 > Street 71 > Street 61 > Street 62 > Street 64 > Central 3

    243W: reverse direction of 243G.

    The new length of 242 and 243G/W is only around 17.48 km to 17.98km, much shorter than the route suggestion I made previously. If implemented, this is only up to 2km longer than the current length of 242 and 243G/W.

    Yet, it would accept so many people's requests and enhance so many bus connections.

    The re-routed 242 would encourage people to use Pioneer MRT Station as an alternative to Boon Lay MRT Station, while enhance the bus connection between Jurong West Neighbourhood 7 and Boon Lay MRT Station.

    The re-routed 243G/W would enhance (or make no difference on) the bus connection between Boon Lay MRT Station and Jurong West Neighbourhood 8, as it skips Pioneer Road North and uses Jurong West Street 71 to go directly to Jurong West Street 81. Although it would be a longer ride to/from Jurong West Street 75, most of the people who take 243G/W and 181 at Jurong West Street 75 actually stay along Jurong West Street 72... so, it's okay. in fact, it would make things right, cuz right now a lot of people at Street 72 walk long distance to Street 75 to take 243 and 181, just because 242 takes a longer route to Boon Lay MRT Station... by right, it's majority wins.... so, why not let 242 use the shorter route instead? :)

     

    Meanwhile, 258 can be re-routed like this:

    258 (to Joo Koon): Central 3 > Street 64 > Street 75 > Street 82 > Street 81 > Avenue 5 > Pioneer Road North > Avenue 4 > Street 61 > Street 63 > Pioneer Road North > Upper Jurong Road > Benoi Road > Joo Koon Circle

    258 (to Boon Lay): reverse direction of 258 (to Joo Koon)
