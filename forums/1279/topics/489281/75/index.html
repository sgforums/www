    Currently, there is no bus connection between Kaki Bukit Avenue 1 and Eunos Bus Interchange. Meanwhile, the Downtown Line Kaki Bukit station opens next year.

    For the above two reasons, I would like to suggest a new bus route.

    Bus route 152

    1. Eunos Bus Interchange
    2. Eunos Road 2
    3. Sims Avenue
    4. Jalan Eunos
    5. Eunos Link
    6. Kaki Bukit Avenue 1
    7. Bedok Reservoir Road
    8. Bedok Reservoir Road (Christ Church and Blk 608)
    9. Bedok Reservoir Road
    10. Jalan Damai
    11. Kaki Bukit Avenue 1
    12. Eunos Link
    13. Jalan Eunos
    14. Changi Road
    15. Jalan Turi
    16. Sims Avenue
    17. Eunos Road 2
    18. Eunos Bus Interchange

    The length of this route is 9.62 kilometres and the estimated travel time of this route is 49 minutes.

    For this bus service, I recommend using five single decker buses on around thirteen minutes frequency.

    51% of this route duplicates route 15, and 60% of it duplicates route 60 - similar to new route 308 where 67% of that new route duplicates route 172.

    Nonetheless, 40% of this route is new.

    Thank you for taking the time to read this post.
