    COMPARE DARE: NWFB20040 / KMB80884


    *CH pix

    *JT pix

    Folks, if you have thought the NWFB is just another retired Dennis "Dragon". Well you are not that correct. NWFB, CTB acquired the Duple Met Sec Dennis and rename it Condor so it does not have any semblance or sound like their competitors version in Kowloon. There are at least 13 differences listed below:

    FRONT FACE:
    1. NWFB condor upper deck front windows are sliding while KMB dragon has a push out window
    2. NWFB condor desto has has a smaller font than KMB dragon
    3. NWFB condor wind screen wipers are at the tip of the upper window whilst KMB dragon has one on the upper tip, the other at the lower side.
    4. NWFB condor has an extra small grille in front of the BC while KMB dragon doesn't
    5. NWFB condor front gille extends to the bumper while KMB dragon does not and leaves a buffer

    DOOR SIDE:
    1. NWFB condor glass panels on the door does not extend full length while KMB dragon does
    2. NWFB condor desto is above the side window while KMB dragon is in the side window.
    3. NWFB condor has full sliding side window while KMB dragon is half sliding window
    4. NWFB side indicator light is next to the entrance door while KMB dragon is above the side window
    5. NWFB condor emergency engine cut is at the rear(door side) above engine panel while KMB is at the back of the bus

    BACK SIDE:
    1. NWFB condor rego(rectangle) is below the route destination while KMB dragon is on the right side below the emergency stop button

    INTERIOR:
    1. NWFB condor seats have a plastic grip cover while KMB dragon are metal
    2. NWFB condor lower deck seats are row bench (similar to SBST VO2X) while KMB dragon are face to face
