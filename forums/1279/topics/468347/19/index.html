    INFO BITS:

    The DA93 from NWFB is officially retired from service in 2013, June 1 after faithfully serving Hong Kong public for more than 17 years.

    Previously owned by HACTL(Hong Kong Air Cargo Terminal Limited) as Citybus operated the service, it was transferred to NWFB in 2000 as CTB/NWFB are both operated by Chow Tai Fook Holdings.

    Fitted with a Cummins engine and Voith gearbox, this workhorse is assigned to Southern Horizons rts. 590/590A and is a favourite among bus fans for its work rate.

    Do note as the bus was originally a Citybus, its face is fixed with the Alexander R type(Economies of Scale) rather than the standard Dragon face used by KMB.

    A great commemorative keepsake for all bus fans to have the DA93 in St. John's livery
