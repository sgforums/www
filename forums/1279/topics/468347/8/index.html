    Writeout!

    ukbus 6039 & ukbus 6043 Alexander-Dennis Enviro400
    Stagecoach London and First London
    Northcord Model Company is proud to release its first two London bus models on the former CMNL Enviro400 tooling.
    This is launched on ukbus 6039, Stagecoach London, and ukbus 6043, First London. Both models feature the latest specifications requested by Transport for London and include the current, modern version of London “Buses” Roundel.
    ukbus 6039 depicts Stagecoach London ADL Enviro400 19729, LX11 AZR. This vehicle, which was new in March 2011, is one of 31 identical Enviro400s based at Stagecoach London’s Rainham Garage for use on Transport for London contracted route 174 (Dagenham–Harold Hill) and 287 (Barking–Rainham). The ADL Enviro400 has become the Stagecoach London standard bus for new double deck deliveries and a total of 235 are now operated by the company including 26 E400H hybrid versions.
    ukbus 6043 features First London DN33638 (SN11 BPO) carrying the standard Transport for London all-red livery, working on route 26 bound for Waterloo in Central London.
    The replicas feature excellent detailing, inside and out, such as realistic-looking handpoles and handrails. The Enviro400 model is a one-piece casting whose clean lines accentuate the windows and livery application on which all logos and lettering are clearly legible.
    The brand new ukbus 6039 and ukbus 6043 models are each based on a Limited Edition of only 504 pieces produced.
