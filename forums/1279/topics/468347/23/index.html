    REVIEWS!

    NWFB Dennis Trident ALX500 10.6M Aircon bus

    NWFB introduced the 10.6m ALX500 in 1999 to replace the short NAC buses that were due to retire. A total of 62 buses were purchased brand new to improve the service that was previously catered by NAC short buses. As the ridership for NWFB bus services increased, most of the buses were sold to KCRC as part of KCRC's bus routes expansion. So spotting an NWFB 10.6m ALX500 is quite a rarity nowadays on HK island...

    A good model to have if you do not have one 10.6m ALX500 aircon bus!
