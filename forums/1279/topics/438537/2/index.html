    I still prefer Citaros over the KUBs.

    Citaros:

    Strenghts:

    - Looks better, more rigid and better quality build

    - Smoother and faster engine

    - Stepless aisle

    - Lower in height and thus don't take up higher spaces.

    Weakness:

    - Weak aircon.

    - Narrow aisle at the rear

    K230UBs

    Strenghts:

    - Better air con

    - Rear aisle wider but need to climb steps

    - Exterior design looks good, although not as good as the Citaros, but better than OCs.

    Weakness:

    - Lesser seats

    - Inferior body work that vibrates and some internal parts still vibrating

    - Noisy radiator noise

    - Need to climb up 2 high steps to the rear part of the best, commuter unfriendly 

    - 1100 of them. Too much of them.

    - Engine seems to be powerful but seems less torque than the Citaros.
