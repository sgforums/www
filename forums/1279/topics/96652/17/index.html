    Foxtrot, what does HG... mean?
    If a bus is indicated as HG132, the HG means that the bus is from Hougang Depot and it is a permanent service on 132 (although it may be deployed on other services occasionally as a cameo). I can't remember which Great Eastern adverts have the pregnant women but I'll supply you with all the double-deckered buses which bear the Great Eastern advert (as far as I know) so you can check them out. I suggest that you camp out at Hougang Central Interchange since 3 of the below-mentioned bus services terminate there. Another thing to note is that some advertisements may bear different images on the left and right sides of the bus, i.e. the pregnant woman image may only be found on the right side of the bus and not on the left side of the bus.
