    1. Service 45 - Very good links towards AMK/YCK, O405 fleets from AMDEP, Mark IVs from BNDEP not bad too.

    2. SMRT Premium 531 - Very high demand with evening trips. The best ever Premium Service planned by SMRT Buses. Will joyride on this service on day.

    3. SBS Transit Premium 558&559 - A rather special SBST Premium service serving an SMRT area, provides commuters in this SMRT area another choice, rather than the usual crowded feeders and crowded MRT stations in SMRT areas.

    4. Service 8 - A fast link from the East to TPY, maybe also nice for joyriding.

    5. Service 10 - Links the Upper East Coast area to many places, a rather fun route for joyriding, with high chance of getting DM3500 at certain times of weekdays.

    6. Express 502 - Allows me to get in and out of the West fast, also nice for joyriding with the O405 fleet.

    7. Express 518 - Allows me to get in and out of the East fast, also nice for joyriding.

    8. Service 174 - Competition with SMRT svcs 77&106 in linking up Bukit Batok & the city, a rather well-planned route. Not to forget, a nice route to joyride an LO3x.

    9. Service 77 - Have not really joyrided on this service yet, but the demand seems rather low most of the time. Maybe a good service to joyride a Volgren Merz?

    Sorry for listing more than 5 services. Of course there are other service that I like but I won't possibly list them all down.
