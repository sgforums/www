    Sgforums post pic instructions

    Take photo with smartphone

    Upload to Imgur using imgur uploader app

    Copy Link (make sure link ends with .jpg)

    Login to SGForums from your smartphone and chosen thread

    Deselect "Visual"

    Add this extension <img src="your link here" alt="">

    Tap "Save reply"

    Done! Alot faster and does not need a PC
