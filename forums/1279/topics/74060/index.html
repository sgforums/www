    More from me
    Upper Serangoon Road => Punggol Nasi Lemak, Chong Pang Nasi Lemak, Midnight Curry Rice, Teochew Porridge

    East Coast Road => Marine Parade Laksa (note: not Katong Laksa), Katong Tau Kwa Pau, Ampang Yong Tau Hoo, Chin Mei Chin Confectionary

    Jalan Kayu => Thasevi Roti Prata (cheese prata and ice cream prata)

    Seah Im Market (Harbourfront) => Cheap FOOD! Especially the duck noodles, tom yam noodles, fried carrot cake, etc etc.

    Corporation Drive Food Centre => Super cheap and good food.

    Changi Village Food Centre => Nasi Lemak (try the stall opposite International Nasi Lemak, $2 only)

    Hougang South Food Centre => Geylang Fried carrot cake

    Old Airport Road Emporium and Food Centre => Kway Chap, Toa Payoh Rojak, Nam Heng Hokkien Prawn Mee, Lao Fu Zi Char Kway Teow, Hua Kee Hougang Famous Wanton Mee, To-Rico Kway Chap

    Holland Drive => Blanco Court Kway Chap, XO Fish Head Bee Hoon

    Bedok North Rd Blk 85 => Bak Chor Mee, Chai Chee Pork Porridge

    Lavender Food Square => Kok Kee Wanton Noodles

    Golden Mile Food Centre => 75 Ah Balling (dont try anywhere else, this is the original stall), Granny Zhao An Chin Chow
