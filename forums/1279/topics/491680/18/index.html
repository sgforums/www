    Here u go, some history about these bus svcs... dug up from landtransportguru and sgforums archives

    825

    1990s: Introduced between Springleaf Garden and Yio Chu Kang (loop)
    1995: Amended to start from Yio Chu Kang Ter
    1996/7: Shortened to Lentor Estate
    2007 (12 Mar): Extended to ply Lentor Way and Lentor Vale

    529 / Lentor Estate Premium service

    2007 (26 May): Introduced between Yio Chu Kang and Lentor, same route as 825. Operated on Sat 3pm - 8pm, flat fare $1.70

    2009 (01 Jun): Lentor Estate Premium service renumbered to Premium 529
    ??: Fare revision for Premium 529 from $1.70 to $2.20, then to $2.70
    2016 (31 Dec): Last day of operations for Premium 529; service withdrawn after 31 Dec 2016.

     

    590 / Teacher's Estate service (route)

    2006 (26 Jun): Introduced between Yio Chu Kang and Teacher's Estate. Operated full day till 8pm on weekdays, half day on Sat, flat fare $1.15. Used Bus-plus buses

    2007 (Aug): Service withdrawn

     

    593 / Springleaf, Springside Estate service

    2007 (25 Dec): Introduced between Yio Chu Kang and Springside/Springleaf estates. Flat fare $1.30. Used Bus-plus buses

    2008 (Aug): Extended to Ang Mo Kio Int.

    2009: Service withdrawn.
