    • 11: low demand
    • 15, 33, 150, 155: Joo Chiat Road
    • 16: Joo Chiat Road only
    • 24, 27, 34, 36, 53, 858: Changi Airport
    • 57, 175, 195: Depot Road. But since 195 used to have a bendy bus, perhaps can trial MAN A24 when SBS takes over Yishun? (because got no other choice).
    • 77, 156, 970: low demand and Sixth Avenue, although it is a rich man estate
    • 120, 124, 273: Telok Blangah Heights/Dr, very hard to maneuvere
    • 121, 272: Telok Blangah Crescent/Rise
    • 131, 141: Pek Kio
    • 173: Toh Tuck roads, although it is also rich man estate
    • 177: Gombak Drive, although it is in the army camp that has large admin and ministerial stuff, the army guys will ask for ID verification.
    • 200: South Buona Vista Road, low demand and multiple turns make it not suitable for any high-capacity fleet.
    • 317: Serangoon Gardens (but you can try taking a DD on it)
    • 400: Marina Gardens Drive (although DD can do experiment like 228, wrong deployment!)
    • 401: East Coast Park Service Road
    • 402: low demand due to Marina South Pier MRT Station and new CBTC
    • 403: low demand still
    • 405: low demand too, but how to turn at Old Choa Chu Kang Road end? Soon, it will be extended to SAFTI City building.
    • 882: low demand
    • 883: low demand
    • 975: got to go along the Old Lim Chu Kang Rd, how to get DD?
