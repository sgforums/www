  • carbikebus
    5 Jun 04, 06:59
    My learning cum touring trips with others 18 trainee captains

    2200-2210,Assemble @HGDEP,Sign attendance form and off to Tampines Int.
    2223-2310,Reach Tampines Int,Went to control room and the Instructor showed us how to scan cards.Seperated into 3 groups,6 pax board 291,Another 6 292 and My group of 6 board 293 and observe the bus captain duty on Feeders/Townlink services.
    2325-2355 Reach HGDEP,Take short break and all board into the bus and went to the Auto wash booth,Instructor explain the procedures.
    2355-0040 Observe bus captain on end of duty procedure,How to park and hand over the Number plates for spare buses.Also introduce the usage of waybills,How to sign and submit it into box.
    0040-0120 On the way to Clementi Int for rest time.
    0145-0220 To SLBP,Learn how bus captain enter and park at Multi-Storey buspark,Was surprised to see a few Turf City B10Ms just respray to SBSL Livery.
    0255-0400 Reach Woodlands,Observe Bus captain from JB transport buses change to SBST employee buses,Total 7 employees buses.
    0435-0445 Short visit to BRAM and off to HGDEP to observe the procedure of Bus captain on how they report for the early morning duty.Short break.
    0500-0510 Reach Hougang Int and observe bus captain duty at the Interchange in the early morning.
    0520-0535 Feedback & Q&A sessions,Sign off attendance and back to HGDEP
    0550-0600 Reach HGDEP and all dismissed

    Too bad forgot to bring camera
