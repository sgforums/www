    Originally posted by AJQZC:

    DT1 Bt Panjang: Exits on both sides of Upp Bt Timah Rd. Pair of bus stops fully upgraded and operational, though road works are still going on.

    DT2 Cashew: Exit on road's east side only. Southbound bus stop still upgrading. No bus stop directly opposite, and considerable distance to the nearest stop (43091), without sheltered walkway.

    DT3 Hillview: Exits on both sides of UBT Rd. Southbound bus stop upgraded, and 176/963/970 do not stop there. The exit on the west side does not seem to connect to a bus stop (to be confirmed).

    DT4 Hume: Road works. Not sure if they are actually building the station, preparing for its construction, or otherwise. Bus stops not affected.

    Some minor observances from DTL2 open house today:

    DT1 Bt Panjang: List of bus services to other MRT stations (hereby known as "Buslist") somewhat incomplete, as 176/178 to BB, 176 to JE not listed. Buslist also doesn't include nearby stops along Bt Panjang Rd & Petir Rd.

    DT3 Hillview: Northbound bus stop on west side of Upp Bt Timah Rd is still not ready. Based on Buslist, 176/963/970 may not be stopping at this stop. Buslist also doesn't include nearby stops along Hillview Rd. 

    DT5 Beauty World: Numbered bus stops are (1) (42091, Upp Bt Timah Rd), (3) (42151, Jln Jurong Kechil) and (4) (42109, Jln Anak Bukit). (1) & (4) are designated stops for train disruptions. Supposed new bus stop (2) directly opposite (1) is built but not ready yet.

    Bus stops 42159 and 42259 along Jln Jurong Kechil are not included in Buslist, even though both are considerably nearer to the station exits than (4).

    ** Note that a right turn from Upp Bt Timah Rd (42119) to Beauty World Plaza is under construction.

    DT6 King Albert Park: New bus stop along Dunearn Rd (42069) is not ready yet.

    DT10 Stevens: Only exit along Bt Timah Rd. Passage to Dunearn Rd bus stop relies solely on unsheltered pedestrian crossing (traffic lights) at the junction, as there are no overhead bridges or underpasses built.
