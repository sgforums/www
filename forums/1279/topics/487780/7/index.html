    The Land Transport Authority (LTA) will be commissioning the traffic lights at the following locations on Thursday, 10 December 2015 at 10:30 a.m:

    (a) Junction of Choa Chu Kang Ave 7/Keat Hong Link
    (b) Junction of Choa Chu Kang Ave 7/Choa Chu Kang Grove
    (c) Junction of Choa Chu Kang Grove/Keat Hong Link
    (d) Junction of Brickland Road/Choa Chu Kang Grove

    Please refer to the attached map for more details.

    Motorists are advised to drive carefully when approaching the new traffic signals and to observe the traffic signal indications.

    http://goo.gl/lbOC5w

    • 1 new pair of bus stops along Choa Chu Kang Ave 7
    • 1 new pair of bus stops along Choa Chu Kang Grove
    • 1 new bus stop along Keat Hong Link

    All these new bus stops might be related to Service 983.
