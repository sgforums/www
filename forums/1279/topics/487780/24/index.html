    Updates on bus stop names:

    - 57131 (Sembawang Road) had been renamed from 'Selataris Condo' to 'Opp Jln Kemuning'.

    - 57019 and 57011 (Upp Thomson Road) had been renamed from 'Nessea Club' and 'Opp Nessea Club' to 'Springleaf Nature Pk' and 'Opp Springleaf Nature Pk' respectively.

    - 56099 and 56091 (Upp Thomson Road) had been renamed from 'Opp Former Seletar Inst' and 'Aft SLE' to 'Aft Springleaf Rd' and 'Bef Springleaf Rd' respectively.

    - 56069 and 56061 (Upp Thomson Road) had been renamed from 'Bef NTUC FairPrice Ctr' and 'Aft NTUC FairPrice Ctr' to 'Bef Tagore Dr' and 'Aft Tagore Dr' respectively.

    - 56059 and 56051 (Upp Thomson Road) had been renamed from 'Aft 3M S'pore' and 'Opp 3M S'pore' to 'Bef Tagore Rd' and 'Opp Tagore Rd' respectively.

    - 56029 (Upp Thomson Road) had been renamed from 'JCU S'pore' to 'Bef Sembawang Hill FC'.

    - 46721 and 46729 (Woodlands Ave 8) had been renamed from 'Opp Pacific Vinitex' and ‘Pacific Vinitex’ to 'Bef Woodlands Ind Pk E7' and 'Aft Woodlands Ind Pk E7' respectively.

    - 58069 and 58061 (Sembawang Road) had been renamed from 'Opp The Ashram' and 'Aft The Ashram' to 'Opp Durban Rd' and 'Aft Durban Rd' respectively.

    - 59073 and 59072 (Yishun Ave 2) had been renamed from ‘Aft Northpoint Shop Ctr’ and ‘Northpoint Shop Ctr’ to ‘Opp Yishun Stn’ and ‘Yishun Stn Exit E’ respectively.

    - 46059 and 46051 (Woodlands Road) had been renamed from 'Chartered Semi-C Bldg' and 'Opp Chartered Semi-C Bldg' to 'Global Foundaries/Aft BKE' and 'Bef BKE' respectively.

    - 46049 and 46041 (Woodlands Road) had been renamed from 'Bef Sri Arasakesari Tp' and 'Opp Sri Arasakesari Tp' to 'Global Foundaries' and 'Opp Global Foundaries' respectively.

    - 46039 and 46031 (Woodlands Road) had been renamed from ‘Bef Woodlands Ind Pk D’ and ‘Aft Woodlands Ind Pk D’ to ‘Opp Kranji Lodge 1’ and ‘Kranji Lodge 1’ respectively.

    - 46029 and 46021 (Woodlands Road) had been renamed from ‘Aft Woodlands Fire Stn’ and ‘Opp Woodlands Fire Stn’ to ‘Aft W'lands Ind Pk D St 2’ and ‘Bef W'lands Ind Pk D St 2’ respectively.

    - 44059 and 44051 (Woodlands Road) had been renamed from ‘Opp Heavy Veh Pk’ and ‘Aft Heavy Veh Pk’ to ‘Blk 632 CP’ and ‘Opp Blk 632 CP’ respectively.

    - 43771 (Bukit Batok Road) had been renamed from ‘Aft LP 100’ to ‘Bef Bt Batok West Ave 2’.

    - 43789 and 43781 (Bukit Batok Road) had been renamed from ‘Aft LP 83’ and ‘Aft LP 74’ to ‘Blk 436A’ and ‘Opp Blk 437A’ respectively.

    - 43759 and 43751 (Bukit Batok Road) had been renamed from ‘Aft LP 59’ and ‘Aft LP 56’ to ‘Blk 443D’ and ‘Opp Blk 443D’ respectively.

    - 43769 and 43761 (Bukit Batok Road) had been renamed from ‘Aft LP 37’ and ‘Aft LP 32’ to ‘Dulwich Coll/Blk 445’ and ‘Opp Dulwich Coll’ respectively.

    - 52219 and 52211 (Toa Payoh Rise) had been renamed from 'Aft Lor 1 Toa Payoh' and 'Bef Lor 1 Toa Payoh' to 'Bef Caldecott Stn' and 'Lighthouse Sch' respectively.

    - 02119 (Nicoll Highway) had been renamed from ‘One Raffles Link’ to ‘Opp War Memorial Pk’.

    - 66039 and 66031 (Lor Chuan) had been renamed from ‘Opp Cardiff Ct’ and ‘Cardiff Ct’ to ‘Blk 307’ and ‘Cardiff Residence’ respectively.

    - 60209 and 60201 (Upp Serangoon Road) had been renamed from ‘Opp Leong Bee Ct’ and ‘Leong Bee Ct’ to ‘Sant Ritz’ and ’18 Woodsville’ respectively.

    - 46389 and 46381 (Woodlands Avenue 3) had been renamed from 'Tech' and 'Opp Tech' to 'Aft Woodlands Ind Pk D St 2' and 'Opp Woodlands Ind Pk D St 1' respectively.

    - 47621 (Woodlands Avenue 7) had been renamed from 'Flextronics' to 'Aft Gambas Ave'.

    - 58369 (Admiralty Street) had been renamed from 'Bef LP 8' to 'Bef Acacia Home'.

    - 46931 (Woodlands Avenue 9) had been renamed from 'Anixter Bldg' to 'Opp Woodlands Spectrum 1'.

    - 60089 and 60081 (Jalan Toa Payoh) had been renamed from 'Opp St. Andrew's Jnr Sch' and 'St. Andrew's Jnr Sch' to 'Opp St. Andrew's Village' and 'St. Andrew's Village' respectively. 

    - 92279 and 92271 (Still Road South) had been renamed from 'Aft Grand Hotel' and 'Bef Grand Hotel' to 'D'Ecosia' and 'Opp D'Ecosia' respectively.

    The list above is non-exhaustive as there are quite a number of changes to bus stop names all over Singapore, notably, bus stops near MRT stations will now have its exits indicated and those that did not have the MRT stations as their names will now be changed to reflect as such.
