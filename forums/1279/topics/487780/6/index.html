    Originally posted by BusAnalayzer:

    How many bus stops have been added along Jurong west st 91/93 for the extension of sv 99? 

    There are ten new bus stops at Jurong West Street 91 and 93 which Trunk bus 99 will call from 21 November 2015.

    Two are opposite existing bus stops, Two are completely new bus stops at Jurong West Street 91. Six are completely new bus stops at Jurong West Street 93.

    It's not about how many, but where the new pairs of bus stop are located. That one pair of new bus stop at Jurong West Street 91 is meant to improve accessibility to bus services for people staying at blks 901-911. Feeder bus 241 may call at one of the pair at a later date.

    Whereas for the new pairs of bus stop at Jurong West Street 93, the first pair is at the market area, the second pair is at the stadium. The third pair is at the open field.
