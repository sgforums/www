    Take a taxi for RM15 cheap cheap..

    if not, go to the bus stop opp City Square and take the following buses:

    9B, 18, 20, 41, 66, 77, 99, 188, 205, 227, 410, 506, G8

    for 9B, this bus will turn left from the Tebrau highway and will stop along the doorstep of Jusco

    for 20, it takes a turn inside one of JB towns but will end up opposite Jusco. (stops opp the stop which 9B will bring you)

    for the other buses, thats 18, 20, 41, 66, 77, 99, 188, 205, 227, 410, 506, G8, stop along the Tebrau Highway. You can alight after you see Jusco to your left. its a 10-15min walk. dont be lazy. take the first bus that comes, else you may wait up to 1 hour

    fare will be about RM1.80 - RM2.00

    theres also a free shuttle bus on Fridays, Saturdays, Sundays
