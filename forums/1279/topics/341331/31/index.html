  • SBS2601D
    19 Jun 09, 19:21
    Originally posted by sgbuses:

    What's the secret behind the O405s that none of them ever seem to vibrate? (which is what that makes O405s great)


    Good damping effect by the engine mountings. That's definitely one of the major factors.

    But the W3E's lock-up clutch has the tendency to connect too quickly when connecting driving shaft and axle, resulting in jerks when the lock-up engages, and when the bus changes gears. But that's also what helps in the pick-up speed, since a fully connected system obviously allows more power to be generated to the axle. So the less time taken to engage means, less time wasted in-between gear changes but jerky rides.

    Interestingly, the lock-up can disconnect while on the move for prolonged periods if the BC knows the right moment to lift his foot up (either by rough handling on the gas pedal resulting in a confused W3E, or when the lock-up disengages to change gears).

    All these obviously contributes to rough rides on the SBST W3E equipped 0405, even if the bus has little vibrations.
