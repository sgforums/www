  • TIB537B
    14 Apr 09, 23:12
    • Originally posted by Lameomaster:

    What are all the differences between EURO 4 Scania K230 and EURO 5 K230

    • Better EDS (Coolair VS Hanover)
    • Better performance/less Jerky/less vibrations
    • More seats (extra 3 seats in 1 PIW slot and a granny seat on the left of the bus
    • Different aircon system
    • Extra pole above the 4 piority seats on the left that supposedly stops the vibrations when the bus is moving (Euro V)
    • The door closing is a red strip of light
    • Euro (EE)V engine lol

    External appearance

    • The front grey portion is rounded instead of streamlined with the front
    • The EDS on the left is now at the top to allow passengers seating on the left front seat to see out to the left
    • The aircon pod design is different (looks more conventional now)

    Likely i got miss out some other vital points. So far i only saw the Euro (EE)Vs, haven't got to board one, so these are gathered facts
