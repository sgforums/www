If you want to see an example of Svc 903's loads using rigids there is the 903/961 crossover slot where the bus would be filled to the door with people squashed to the glass panels (Cram Jam style) when it reports back to WRI regardless of the bus used is a Scania, Box Merz or OC. RP students also sometimes use Svc 903 to avoid the massive queues for Svc 902 and Svc 169 somehow. In the afternoon Svc 903 becomes a school bus for the secondary schools in the area that it plys and half-filled buses during off peak hours are also rather common ... ...
