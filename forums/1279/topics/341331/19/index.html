    Originally posted by TIB537B:

    When the bus is about to be scrapped, why is it in a non-user lay up first before being de-registered and scrapped?


    mainly for some reasons:
    Some non-refurbished mercs in AMDEP are lay up early because the depot itself has to get the new intake of Scania K230UB buses and replaced the older mercs fleet and to avoid overflowing of its strength of the fleet in AMDEP.

    But for Scania N113CRB buses in BNDEP, I'm not too sure about this but possibly Mk3s/Mk4s are taking over the scanias. But I believe that there are not much spare parts for those Scanias and are unable to replace it cos those Scanias are reaching the end of its lifespan of 19 years.

    As for SMRT areas, the Nissans & some ELBOs were gone for lay up due to the delivery of the new OCs which were advancing to both depots, KJDEP & AMDEP and the massive depot transfer of several buses, which involved in 3 SMRT depots. eg. a merc O405 from AMDEP got transferred to WLDEP, etc.
