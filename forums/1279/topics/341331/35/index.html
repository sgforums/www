  • Oceane
    5 Jul 09, 07:09
    Originally posted by Djmeowmeow:

    Where do bus drivers have their mealbreaks. Lets say Service 14. Do they have it at Bedok or Clementi. And for Service 195, where do the bus drivers have their mealbreakes? I dun see anywhere near enough for them to have their meals


    For services terminating at two ends with big fleets it is quite hard to arrange mealbreaks at only one location because of the time range it takes. So most of the time especially during their dinner they have it at dual interchange locations. For services terminating only at one end (i.e. loop) their mealbreak is where they terminate and rest.

    Small fleets are generally easier to slot mealbreaks. Say Sv52, everyone eats at JE. Bigger fleets like Sv143 for example, 30-40% of them have mealbreaks at TPY even though there is parking constraints. This is so that the drivers can have their meals at appropriate times and not some unearthly hour (imagine eating dinner at 3.30pm or 9.30pm).
