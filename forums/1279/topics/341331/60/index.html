  • sgbuses
    8 Nov 09, 13:50

    I noticed on sgwiki that O405G Habits are divided into Batch I Mk I, Batch I Mk II and Batch II. What are the differences between them?

    I also noticed that the original Lawo EDS size does not correspond to whether it is a Mk I or Mk II. Can someone confirm?

  • TIB1049L
    8 Nov 09, 17:48

    Batch I Mk I are a mixture of Voiths and ZFs without the intercooler (as said by someone else earlier in the forums). The Habits that had been burnt before are all ZFs from this range of regos. ZFs from this range are known to sound like box bendies/Hispano MkII bendies while Voiths (in my opinion) from this range isn't as powerful as those from Batch II. TIB1063U to TIB1147L has the smaller EDS

    Batch I Mk II are all ZFs with intercooler. This batch of buses sound like Volgren Bendies.

    Batch II Habits are mixture of Voiths and ZFs with intercooler. It is known that certain buses from this range got a pair of "horns" protruding from the bumper and some of these buses in this range have different blinkers from the original versions

     

    Correct me if I am wrong though; not really good with mechanical stuff
