    Basically for alot of SBST services we have the e-timetable that displays the departure timings of bus stops. Hence it's easy to see a downroute. I believe for other non-ETT services it was done by pure spotting a.k.a. the old school method.

    Last time SMRT website does not have the above function, and still doesnt have. However, you are able to obtain their early morning downroute times at publictransport.sg as the board of every single bus stop in singapore can be found there.
