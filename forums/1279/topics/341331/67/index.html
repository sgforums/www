    Originally posted by SBS9818A:

    AMDEP used to control Hougang South while HGDEP controlled St. Michael's. AMDEP took over St. Michael's in the late 1990s while HGDEP took over Hougang South probably in the mid 1990s. Not too sure about the dates.


    Hougang areas was all the while under HGDEP controlled. If I remembered correctly service 74 and 165 were among a few Hougang services under AMDEP. Only in Mar 1998 SBS standarlised 74 and 165 to be under HGDEP. St Michael's routes went under AMDEP in return.

    Service 132 was also under AMDEP in the past with LO3x and Merc 0405s. It came under HGDEP only in Apr 1997, that was when VO2x and VO3x were deployed on service 132, together with occasional Volvo MK3s and MK4s.
