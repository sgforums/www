    Originally posted by SBS BUSES LOVER:

    what special refurbishment did sbs436C undergo ?

    Trial refurb, together with 293Y, before the Scanias came in, these two trial refurb buses were perm 268. If 293Y's or 436C's refurb was successful, they would have been appiied to all SBS Mercs during their refurb program around 8 to 10 years ago. Which also means that there may had been one other bus which was part of the trial that had the current configuration and the trial interior of this bus was chosen for the current one you see on all Mercs, except for 293Y, 310G (blue, earlier refurb, de regged) and 436C.

    The refurb is such that the aircon ducts are silver/metallic in color, with larger aircon outlets, and additional metallic window frames, thats why when you see the bus from the exterior, there are black borders on the windows which wouldn't be there on normal Mercs.
