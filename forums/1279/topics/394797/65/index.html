    I went down to Changi Airport to check today on the EDS details of the Airport services again. A few observations:

    1) Service 36 front EDS route details no change, looks the same for both Euro IV & Vs.

    2) So far only the perm Airport services buses have the newly reprogrammed and much simplified front EDS details. Cameos for these services (such as 6137B on 27, 8846A on 53) are still using the old format with 2 rows of words for the front EDS details for 27 & 53. But 8689L on 53 today is showing the newly programmed details for 53 even though it is a cameo, which probably makes it an exception so far even though it used to be ex-perm 24.

    3) Confirmed that there is no longer a need for BCs to tap in at the SBST office at Changi Airport PTB2. However, some BCs who tend to chiong too fast, or purposely want to be late so that they can chiong on the highway back to Pasir Ris/Bedok/Tampines often make use of the stop at PTB2 to park their buses for a few mins & use the toilets instead. But didn't see any of the BCs tapping in today. Toilet break & go.
