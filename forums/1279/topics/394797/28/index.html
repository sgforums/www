  • sbst275
    24 Nov 10, 23:39
    Originally posted by C751A 70049/70050:

    I got a question:

     

    Whats is the difference of a BRT overseas and a SG Bus?

     

    Also, is the Express / Fast Forward / Premium or even Scheme B buses in SG a sort of BRT in overseas?


    BRT is dedicated lanes (barriacaded up) for these buses to ply. Usually they're 2 lane in case of breakdown and ability to implement express routes (leapfrog and only serving major focal points).

    Express & FFW are dedicated svs serving focal points on shared roads. The term semi express is not really applicable for FFW.
