  • SMB116K
    16 May 11, 14:35
    Originally posted by Amir95express:

    how to know which bus comes from which depot?

    and does it always be parked there at the end of service?

    depends of svc. Some svc will do overnight parking at some interchange. Eg. 307 will do overnight parking at cck int, svc 965 buses can do overnight parking at woodlands int and 853 can do overnight parking at yishun int. Majority of the buses are parking at the respective depots allocated to each svc eg 190. Svc 190 SL will park their buses at KJDEP. Svc 858 SL will park their buses at WLDEP and Svc 851 SL will park their buses at AMDEP.

    Some svcs are fully controlled by a certain depot like 190,188,851,965,858 et cetra. You can refer to 

    sgbuses.com/wiki 

    for the deployments of buses, whether is it SMRT or SBST Buses.
