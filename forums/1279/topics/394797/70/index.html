    3 Mar 12, 18:18

    Camoeing : Is a permanent bus to a service but may crossover to ANY other service at any time as long as it is not doing shifts.
    X-Over :SBS2666S [ HGDEP 112/325* ], which crosses from 112 [ IT's PERMED bus svc ] to 325 [ It's X-OVERING BUS SVC ]. 
    Camoeing : SBS920X [ HGDEP 325 ] is PERM 325. But it is saw doing service 132. It is called camoeing.
    JUMPBUS : It is like an X-over BUT to ANY other service, X-over have FIXED about of times it does on the X-OVERING BUS SVC and have a FIXED bus service to X-OVER.
    Camoeing is like PERMANENT bus service but is made to do 1 - 4 times on another bus service due to problems like filling up the missing bus slot.
    JUMPBUS have X-Over frequencies [ VERY COMMON ] but to RANDOM bus services.
    Eg. SBS2656X PERMs 325. But it is seen doing other services for a long time. But it STILL perms 325 after all those. THAT IS JUMPBUS.

  • SBS2656X
    3 Mar 12, 18:23

    Basically, 
    Camoeing : A bus service's bus without a bus to crossover operates on RANDOM bus services.
    Crossover : A bus service's bus REGULARLY seen to operate between it's PERMed svc and it;s crossover service. [ Operating on it's PERMED service is seen MORE often. ]
    JUMPBUS : A bus service's bus is seen REGULARLY to crossover to RANDOM bus services.
