    Originally posted by MoltenFalcon:

    Hey all.

    Just want to clarify something here.

    How do you find the difference between a LO3x and a VO3x?

    Exterior: VO3X rear has one long vent while LO3X rear has 2 square vents. LO3X has very slightly larger upper deck front windscreen than VO3X. Some VOs and LOs still sport their respective branding on the front (VO has a slash while LO has Leyland written)

    Interior: Most notable is at lower deck, VO3X has large standee area while in the LO3X its replaced with forward facing seats. BC dashboard also diff. If u see sth like in a B10M or VSO then its a VO. Anything else then its a LO3X.
