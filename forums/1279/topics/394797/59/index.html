    Originally posted by Hoteenwa:

    Hi all, is it possible to own a bus and convert the interior into an alternative setting and use it for commerical purposes? E.g. the interior is converted into a library setting with book shelves and seats or into a billard room with billard table and bar counter etc. If so, where can I get such a bus? Contact manufacturer or contact SBS/TIBS to see if they sell away their old buses? Also, what license or which department will I have to apply to? Many thanks!! :)

    Yes, its possible and there are past examples of this (search for epBus & molly bus).

    However, do take note of the statutory maximum lifespan of commercial vehicles here is 20 years while the public buses has a max of 19years on the road so you'll only have a few years to do things with it. Of course if u own some land somewhere to permanently park it, this rule wont apply.

    If you are intending to drive it on the roads, then it will be considered a class 5 vehicle. You can check out the requirements of such a vehicle on lta's website or one motoring.

    Otherwise if its just to be permanently parked, a vehicular license is not required as it is just a structure on your own property.

    You may wish to contact sbst/smrt to see if they have any older buses to let go.
