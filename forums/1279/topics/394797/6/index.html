    Originally posted by sbs&tibs:


    Why not I say, Jurong East is nearer to bbdep, why not Jurong East go under bbdep control but slbp?

    Very tiring to explain to people like you, seriously.


    SBST bus operation is under two districts concept : East and West.

    Districts manage the Route Groups (RGs) and bus parks/depots.

    Route Groups take charge of the respective interchanges and terminals with the assigned service routes and BCs (bus captains).

    Bus depots/bus parks are used for despatching, garaging, refuelling and bus-washing/cleaning.

    For example, WD has the following bus depots/bus parks : Soon Lee, Bukit Batok, Ayer Rajah and Hougang.

    WD also managed the following RGs : Boon Lay, Jurong East, Clementi, Bouna Vista, Bukit Merah, Hougang Central, Serangoon and Sengkang.

    New Bridge Road terminal is managed by BL RG as Sv 174 terminate at this location.

    Harbour Front is managed by BM RG with buses depatched from ARBP.
