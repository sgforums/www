    Originally posted by TIB 585L:

    I want to ask abt duties in public buses. I realize some duties got AM50/PM50, why there is 50,i understand AM01 is duty 01

    167AM53/PM53 means there is a x-over, So 167AM53/PM53 will crossover to one svc before going back to AMDEP 167 - Normally AM5X/PM5X duties hold x-over duties before proceeding back to their own service.

    S1 duties, Depending on service, it ends about 1100-1215(AM portion) 1500-2030(PM portion), Some goes back to depot, whereas some park at interchange for swing break, like 167,856 and 859 - which practices swing at SE int. S2 duties, Like 178S201 - Starts at about 1210 and ends at about 1900, But each duties differs - So I can't guarantee my post is accurate - You have to see the timesheet to know more about that particular service.
