    Service 185 K230UBs cameo on Service 199 for Preventive Maintenance. because 185 S Shifters are Leylands and that many of the 185 Full Day drivers have no C1 (doubledecker license) tag so they cannot swap over with the 185 Leylands for Preventive Maintenance. also because i think they dun want 185 Leylands to full day too. so they have to swap with the K230UBs under 199 S Shift (8442 - 8444). thats why you often see 199 K230UBs on 185. same example with 66 and 183. 183 S Shift is also a Preventive Maintenance ground.
