    You have seemed not to even read up before you post. 'AVBW' is the type of bus; the letter designation is assigned by Kowloon Motor Bus (KMB). In this case, a Volvo B9TL Wright Body. '15' is the fleet number. Since HK registrations are randomly assigned, they will each have fleet numbers. Like the recent KMB KUD NE 6817 is assigned 'ASU2'. Read more here: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kowloon_Motor_Bus_Fleet.
