    SBS 0152X on 232 (AMDEP 58)
    SBS 0443G on 238 (BRBP SP)

    TIB 0982Z on 812
    TIB 1022M on 812 - Tomato Head drive, 1231B replacement.

    Sungei Kadut had a serious accident today whereby a trailer laden with logs overturned near Kranji Dam. Service 925 was diverted while they cleared the logs, having to U-turn just after KJDEP, out into Woodlands Road and enter again via another road. Frequency was jumbled up as many CCK-headed bound buses had to take the WRI-bound route, causing much confusion (That was the only shortest route available to get to the other side of Sungei Kadut). I took the bus out from WRI @ 4.20pm ++, and at 5.21pm the bus was still inside Sungei Kadut towards CCK, with the timesheet stating that 5.01pm arrival at CCK for mealbreak, and depart for WRI @ 5.23pm. Most buses did downroute upon reaching the other terminal.

    Most buses were at least late by 30 ~ 45 minutes. Many S-Shift buses bound for CCK were forced to U-turn after turning out from KJDEP, and coupled with the jam, many buses were late. TIB 1102R was forced to do a 3-point turn, Lol.

    Roads have been re-opened and traffic stablilzed as of 6.00pm.
