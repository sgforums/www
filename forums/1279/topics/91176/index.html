    Ok, the system is quite simple

    1) You can make transfer between 160 and 170 bet the 2 checkpoints free
    *Meaning, if I take 170 earlier, I can take 160 to JB Checkpoint from Woodlands w/o having to pay. Later, I take back 170 again. It can also be the other way round

    2) Special rebates
    $0.63 for adults $0.45 for students if swaping bet svs bet the 2 checkpoints

    3) Faster clearance
    Based on this, frequency will be better as it will be 1-4 mins at the checkpoint as there are abt 27 made in 1 hr

    However, this means it is harder for Causeway Link to complete as it may be harder because of its frequency. It has been unpopular already to most as it does not accept ez-link card, making it expensive ($1.30) w/o rebates from the MRT ride while 170 cost $0.88 with rebate
