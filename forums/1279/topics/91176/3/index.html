    CTS is a relatively new scheme.
    Under SMRTB, only s969, s965 and s966 are counted as CTS.
    The rest of the services, even though they run into SBST's territories, are not considered so as they were due to historical reasons. A number used to be under SBST and were exchanged or handed over.

    The CTS scheme is quite complicated. Reciprocity is very much in the picture...so it's quite tricky. It's not as simple as I run into whatever town I want and in exchange, u can do likewise. Again, there are rules governing this. Also, both companies are very cautious and protective of their areas...so alot of restrictions abound.

    Anyhow...for now, I dun think there's gonna be any new CTS in the pipeline.
    With the expansion of the rail network and all, quite difficult to come up with a route that the train doesn't cater for and has earning potential.
