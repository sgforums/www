  • The_Bus_Guide
    19 Sep 09, 11:14

    The first service to the infant Jurong East New Town was 76.  It was introduced in Nov 1981 as a loop service between Clementi Interchange and Jurong East Ave 1 (loop at Jurong Town Hall Rd and return via Boon Lay Way), charging a flat fare of 40c.

     

    The first trunk route to end at the Jurong East Terminal was 79 (introduced in 1982, I presume), which looped at Bras Basah Rd. The Jurong East feeders 333, 335 and 334 were gradually introduced as more flats were completed.

     

    Since 79 duplicated the route of 147 between C'wealth Ave and Hill St, in 1984, 79 was withdrawn and 147 extended to cover its route between C'wealth Ave West and Jurong East Central. During this period also, 7 was extended to Jurong East; services 97 and 98 were introduced. The original 97 was via Clementi Rd and Pasir Panjang Rd.

     

    Jurong East Bus Interchange opened in 1 June 1985 and with it come a few changes. Most notably, of course, was the relocation of the present services  to the new site. 99 was introduced (Jurong East - Boon Lay Garden), 79 was resurrected as a loop service from Jurong East - Empress Place (later it would terminate at Marina Centre) and 76 was amended and became a loop service from Jurong East to Clementi. 335 was amended and 336 was introduced.

     

    76 was withdrawn in 1987 when 99 was modified to run between Clementi - Jurong West St 91. In 1988, 79 was withdrawn and 97 was amended to cover Alexandra Rd and AYE while 51 was extended to Jurong East from Labrador via Pasir Panjang Rd. The present 79 was the 3rd coming, introduced in 1993.

    About Jurong East services.

    Since The_Bus_Guide has mentioned certain parts of the Jurong East Services, so there's no need for me to explain about it

    Continuing from 1986 onwards

    In 1986 we have the following services in JUrong East Int.

    7 - To Bedok
    76 - To Clementi (Loop)
    79 - To Empress Place (Loop)
    97 - To Hougang
    98 - To Jurong Int (Loop)
    99 - To Boon Lay Ave (Loop)
    147 - To Potong Pasir (Loop)
    333 - To Jurong East St 32 (Loop)
    334 - To Jurong West St 42 (Loop)
    335 - To Jurong West St 52 (Loop)
    336 - To Jurong West St 41 (Loop)

    The changes of Jurong East Services from 1987/88 onwards till the present time.

    3 - Full Air-Con Service to Jurong East. Extended to Jurong East Int in 1987via Jln Bt Merah and c'wealth ave, with a slight amenmdent to Upp Cross St and New Bridge Rd. This service was withdrawn during the 1992 MRT Rationalisaiton
    7 - Service shortened to CLementi intercnahge as part of the MRT Rationalisation.
    51 - Service extended to Jurong East Int with tthe closure of Labrador Terminal. In 2002, amended to Pandan Gdns and on 15 Feb 2004, with the closure of Hougang South Int, this service amended to Hougang Ctrl Int.
    52 - Service introduced on 23 Oct 2005 as Bishan to Jurong East int.
    66 - Second air-con service in Jurong east. Service introduced from Tampines to Jurong East, but it was shortened to Bedok Int on 26 May 1991.
    76 - (as mentioned already)
    78 - Service introduced in 1988 from Jurong East to Clementi Ave 3 (loopsvc). this service was amended to pandan rd to cover old 245 route. The amendments were made  around Teban Gdns subsequently in 2001 and in 2005, amended to ply West Coast Rd and bypasses the Teban Garden Est
    79 - (The Jurong East - Marine Centre part mentioned already). Service re-introduced from Jurong East to Boon Lay Int (Loop) on 24 May 1992. When 246 amended, sevice 79 plys along the deleted sectors of 246 around CHin Bee Rd. Amended in 2001 to serve West Coast Rd.
    97 - Service was extended to Hougang Ctrl in 1994 and was shortened to loop at Marina Centre due to the NEL Rationalisation in 2003.
    97e - The first FFW service. Introduced in Jan 2005 to provide faster travel. It calls on certain bus stops with high demand. Express variant of 97.
    98 - Service integrates with 247 and loops at Jurong Pier Rd.
    99- (as mentioned already)
    105 - Service extended to Jurong East when service 104 was amended in 1992.
    143 - Service extended to Jurong East with the merger of service 331 and it was amended to ply back Pandan Garedns. The Jurong Depot Ter was closed on the same year.
    147 - Megred with Service 83 and extended to Upp Serangoon Ter.With the extension of service 105, service 147 cutbacks to Clementi Int.
    160 - Second cross border service from SBST. Service introduced on 5 sep 2004 from Jurong East to Kotaraya II Ter.
    176 - Service extended to Bukit Merrah Int which replaces the old 184 route along Jurong Town Hall Rd to Bt Merah stretch.
    182 - Service introduced as Jurong East to Tuas Terminal in 1998 but it was shortened to Boon LAy Int in 2001.
    183 - Service introduecd as Jurong East to Kent Ridge Ter with the withdrawal of 507, in 2004. In Oct 2005, this service loops at Science Park Dr.
    197 - Service cutbacks to Jurong East Int and amended to serve Bedok Int instead of Geylang Lor 1.
    241 - Service amended to Jurong East Int when Jurong Bus Int closed in 1990. Service renumbers to 331 in 1999.
    333 - service amended to serve Toh Guan Rd and Jurong Town Hall Rd.
    335 - Service integrated with 336 to serve Jurong West St 41.
    336 - Service integrated with 335 and withdrawn. Part of Jurong West St 41 is part of 335 route.
    506 - Express Service introduced as Jurong East to Bedok. In 2000, extended to Changi Airport and in 2002 back to Bedok Int with the opening of Changi Airport MRT.
    711 - JIS Service introduced in 2000, plying from Jurong East - Seraya Ave (loop)
    712 - JIS Service introduced in 2000, plying from Jurong East - Sakra Rd (loop)
    713 - JIS Service introduced in 2000, plying from Jurong East - Sakra Rd (loop), via Pulau Ayer Merbau.
    CW3 (Handal Indah) - Service introduced on Sep 2005 from Jurong East to Larkin Ter via the Tuas 2nd Link.
    715 (TNC) - Service introduced in 2003 by TNC, plying from Jurong East to Jurong Island Checkpoint. In 2007, the service was enhanced to ply Seraya Ave and Pulau Ayer Merbau instead of stopping at Jurong Island Checkpoint (for visitor pass exchange) and transfer to 714 and 716 to Seraya Ave and Sakra Ave respectively.
    717 (TNC) - Service re-introduced in 2007 by TNC, plying from Jurong East to Sakra Ave and Pulau Ayer Merbau.

    NOTE: All 3 JIS services were withdrawn on 30 June 2003 and TNC tooks over the operation of Jurong Island Services.
