    Yup, I remembered service 390 with Albion Viking fleets in the 80s.  In 1989 service 390 got upgraded to Volvo MK2 fleets, followed by Scania N113CRB fleets and later it was converted PAC then FAC with OAC Scania N113CRBS and Merc 0405s. 

    Service 1 (Changi Village - Delta) also had MK2s too before it was withdrawn.

    Service 9 also had Volvo MK2s too in 1989. That time it was still terminating at Geylang Lor 1.

    The BBDEP took in most of the WAs. It was first deployed on service 174, followed by services 75, 105, 176, 189, 190 etc. Service 170 started to get MK2 WAs in 1993 replacing Volvo B57s.
