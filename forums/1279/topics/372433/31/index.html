    When 302 was introduced in early 1994, this service started off as Choa Chu Kang to Choa Chu Kang St 52 Loop Service.     Feeder replacements after Volvo B57s and Merc OF1417s were gone:-

    • Bt Merah, Buona Vista, Clementi, Bt Batok, CCK feeders under ARBP/BBDEP: Volvo MK2 (mostly WAs)
    • Hougang, Eunos feeders under HGDEP: Volvo MK2 (mostly DMs)
    • Jurong East, Boon Lay feeders under JRBP (SLBP): Merc 0405s
    • Ang Mo Kio, Serangoon feeders under AMDEP: Merc 0405s
    • Toa Payoh, Bishan feeders under TPDEP (BRBP): Scania N113CRBs
    • Bedok, Tampines, Pasir Ris feeders under BNDEP: Scania N113CRBs

    To add on that Kent Ridge route group was under JRBP in the past, services 151 (partial JRBP) and 200 operated with Merc 0405 fleets. It was after the major SBS/Tibs swop in 1999 when Kent Ridge bus routes were given to BBDEP.

    In Sept 2004 Serangoon route group went under HGDEP, whereas Eunos route group went under BRBP.
