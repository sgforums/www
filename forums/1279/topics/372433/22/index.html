    The SBS Krnji Turf Club services were introduced in mar 1999, when the new Kranji Turf Club is opened to the public. There were 5 services altogether but due to underutilisation of its service, all of its turf club service were withdrawn in sep 1999.

    781 - From Tampines Int
    782 - From Bedok Int
    786 - From Hougang South Int (hmm, did it started off from Hougang South before shortening to Serangoon?)
    787 - From Yishun Int
    788 - From Clementi Int

    The defunct SSB company, which operates the CSS services, also operates the Turf Club services (Bukit Timah Turf Club). It only operates on major race days and charged a flat fare of $3 for AC and $2 for NAC.

    TC 1 - From Lor 1 Geylang Fringe CP
    TC 2 - From Eu Tong Sen St
    TC 3 - From Bedok

    These 3 services were withdrawn in 1999, due to the shifting of its Turf club operations to Kranji
