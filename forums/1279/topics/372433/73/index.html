    Originally posted by SBS 1000U:

    May I know when was M1, M2, M3 and M4 was introduced and what are so special about these services, since they are specially numbered that time?


    These were shuttle services between housing estate and the nearest MRT station.

    M1 - Buona Vista MRT to Mount Sinai / Science Park Dr

    M2 - Kembangan MRT to Opera Estate

    M3 - Tanah Merah MRT to Lucky Heights

    M4 - Eunos MRT / bus interchange to Marine Crescent / Terrace

    Both M1 and M2 were renumbered 92 and 42 in 1997. M3 was withdrawn. M4 was integrated with service 15.

    Service 859's fleet had several fleet changes and depot transfers ever since its introduction in Apr 1999.
