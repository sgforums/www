    SBS2500L to SBS2592X were all O305 DDs. They were deregistered between 2000 and 2001, and majority of them were based in HGDEP. Its known deployments were the following:
    5, 7, 14, 51, 61, 64, 74, 97, 111, 132, 147, 153, 154, 165, 197, 321, 322, 326, 327, 328, 385

    A few of the O305 DDs in this range together with SBS2478T, and partial buses ranging from SBS2481H - SBS2499H were under AMDEP back then. SBS2477X & SBS2488P was under HGDEP.

    For the case of SBS2569P that became the Magic Ark bus, when SBS1793P (ex-YK5257P) became the Magic Ark SD bus, SBS2569P was deregistered straight after.

    TIB551J to TIB559M were new to KJDEP 187.

    Since 187 was still PAC back then, it uses the following:
    1. NAC Nissan UDs. (for the case of TIB311K, TIB312H, TIB323B and some other Nissans in WLDEP, these buses can still be appeared on 187, even in year 2000)
    2. OAC Nissan UDs (some ex-CSS buses also did 187, like TIB1250X was one of the first ex-CSS Nissan to appear on 187 after final touch up on its SMRT livery)
    3. NAC Hino HT238
    4. NAC Mercs O405 (TIB772M was one)
    5. OAC Mercs O405 (largely common back then)
    6. DAF Hispanos (TIB41X and TIB42X series) - they appeared in a very short while.
    7. DAF WA Sentanta (TIB676H, TIB690R, TIB711P and many others did 187 too, just that it is very common on 178 as well)
    8. Scania ELBOs (when 187 became WLDEP, it began to make use of ELBOs to 187, still NAC back then)
    9. MB O405G bendies (the 1st & 2nd gen ones, TIB855H also did 187 when it was WLDEP 900 and WLDEP 184)
    10. MB O405G Smiley buses / Habits/Volgrens (okay, its very common even today)

    THE HISTORY OF CITYSHOPPER SERVICES

    Cityshopper Services were only introduced by SBS Transit once - on 15 December 2007. They were introduced to cater commuters to the city in shopping comfort. Unlike normal bus services, these services have been introduced to cater specifically to a group of commuters who are willing to pay a bit more for a more comfortable and convenient ride. They are also designed to make maximum use of expressways to offer commuters a more direct and faster journey.

    The routes of the 8 services is here as shown below:

    Cityshopper 551 - Upp Serangoon Road - Orchard Road/Suntec City

    Upp Serangoon Road, Hougang Avenue 8, Hougang Avenue 2, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 3, CTE, Moulmein Road, Newton Road, Scotts Road, Orchard Road, Bras Basah Road, Temasek Boulevard, Rochor Road, Rochor Canal Road, Bukit Timah Road, CTE, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 3, Hougang Avenue 2, Hougang Avenue 8, Upp Serangoon Road. (Resting point at Hougang Central Interchange.)

    Cityshopper 575 - Bedok South Road - Orchard Road/Suntec City

    Bedok South Road, Bedok South Avenue 3, New Upp Changi Road, Bedok North Road, PIE, Moulmein Road, Newton Road, Scotts Road, Orchard Road, Bras Basah Road, Temasek Boulevard,  Temasek Avenue, Raffles Boulevard, Republic Boulevard, ECP, Bedok South Avenue 1, Bedok South Rd, Bedok South Avenue 3, New Upp Changi Road, Bedok North Road. (Resting point at Bedok Interchange.)

    Cityshopper 576 - Tampines Avenue 7 - Orchard Road/Suntec City

    Tampines Avenue 7, Tampines Avenue 4, Tampines Avenue 3, Tampines Avenue 8, Tampines Avenue 1, Bedok North Road Extension, Bedok North Road, PIE, CTE, Moulmein Road, Newton Road, Scotts Road, Orchard Road, Bras Basah Road, Temasek Boulevard,  Temasek Avenue, Raffles Boulevard, Republic Boulevard, ECP, PIE, TPE, Tampines Avenue 7, Tampines Avenue 4, Tampines Avenue 3, Tampines Avenue 8. (Resting point at Tampines Interchange.)

    Cityshopper 577 - Ang Mo Kio Avenue 9 - Orchard Road/Suntec City

    Ang Mo Kio Avenue 9, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 4, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 3, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 10, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 1, CTE, Moulmein Road, Newton Road, Scotts Road, Orchard Road, Bras Basah Road, Temasek Boulevard, Rochor Road, Rochor Canal Road, Bukit Timah Road, CTE, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 1, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 10, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 3, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 4, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 9. (Resting point at Yio Chu Kang Terminal.)

    Cityshopper 578 - Compassvale Street - Orchard Road/Suntec City

    Compassvale Street, Punggol Road, Sengkang East Avenue, Sengkang West Avenue, Fernvale Lane, Jalan Kayu, Yio Chu Kang Road, CTE, Moulmein Road, Newton Road, Scotts Road, Orchard Road, Bras Basah Road, Temasek Boulevard, Rochor Road, Rochor Canal Road, Bukit Timah Road, CTE, Yio Chu Kang Road, Jalan Kayu, Fernvale Lane, Sengkang West Avenue, Sengkang East Avenue, Punggol Road, Compassvale Street. (Resting point at Sengkang Interchange.)

    Cityshopper 579 - Tampines Street 41 - Orchard Road/Suntec City

    Tampines Street 41, Tampines Avenue 9, Tampines Street 45, Tampines Street 34, Tampines Street 32, Tampines Avenue 2, Simei Road, Simei Street 1, Simei Street 3, PIE, CTE, Moulmein Road, Newton Road, Scotts Road, Orchard Road, Bras Basah Road, Temasek Boulevard, Temasek Avenue, Raffles Boulevard, Republic Boulevard, ECP, Xilin Avenue, Simei Avenue, Simei Street 3, Simei Road, Tampines Avenue 2, Tampines Street 32, Tampines Street 34, Tampines Street 45, Tampines Avenue 9, Tampines Street 41. (Resting point at Tampines Interchange.)

    Cityshopper 580 - Teban Gardens Road - Orchard Road/Suntec City

    Teban Gardens Road, West Coast Road, West Coast Highway, West Coast Road, Clementi Road, AYE, CTE, Tiong Bahru Road, Zion Road, Hoot Kiam Road, Paterson Hill, Paterson Road, Orchard Road, Bras Basah Road, Temasek Boulevard, Temasek Avenue, Raffles Boulevard, Republic Boulevard, ECP, AYE, Clementi Road, West Coast Road, West Coast Highway, West Coast Road, Teban Gardens Road. (Resting point at Jurong East Interchange.)

    Cityshopper 581 - Punggol Central - Orchard Road/Suntec City

    Punggol Central, Punggol East, Punggol Field, Punggol Way, TPE, CTE, Moulmein Road, Newton Road, Scotts Road, Orchard Road, Bras Basah Road, Temasek Boulevard, Rochor Road, Rochor Canal Road, Bukit Timah Road, CTE, TPE, Punggol Way, Punggol Field, Punggol East, Punggol Central. (Resting point at Punggol Interchange.)

    The fare for adults is $3.30 for commuters on 551, 575, 576, 577, 579 & 580 and $3.60 for commuters on 578 & 581. Child's fare is $1 on all service. (However, in Mighty Minds Bus Guide 2008 & 2009, there is an error stating that the services' fare are paid by fare stages.)

    Due to Cityshopper 575 & 576 plying the same stops before & after plying the city, there will be a terminating sign to show that it is terminating.

    Initially at first, the demand was quite good, but due to the expensive fare, the demand decreased and on 21 July 2008, Cityshopper 551, 575, 577 & 580 were withdrawn.

    Onn 28 October 2008, Cityshopper 576 was suspended, but was never re-introduced and was subsequently counted as withdrawn. The other 3 services' timing was also adjusted. Finally, on 27 July 2009, the 3 services were withdrawn and this removed the Cityshopper Services concept.

    SEMBAWANG BUS SERVICES

    Previously, at that time Sembawang was in a prelimary stage in development. The only TIBS service that serve that area was 160, that plies along Canberra Rd. Some ex-RAF private roads, such as Old Nelson Rd, Kowloon Rd, were still in existence.

    When Sembawang New Town began to sprung up at the middle stage of development in 1998/99, a new bus service 859, connects to the new Sembawang New Town, but however, there were no plans for a new terminal to be constructed at that time, and was made to loop st Sembawang from Yishun.

    Later on, a new peak hour one-directional service, 981, was introduced from Sembawang Way to Senoko Loop (for the AM Peak); whereas for the PM Peak, it served vice-versa.

    It was planned that a new bus interchange should be erected near their doorstep. The Sembawang Bus Interchange began constructed in early 2004 and began its opeartions on 20th November 2005. Refer to the post on "Sembawang Bus Interchange" below.

    SEMBAWANG BUS INTERCHANGE

    The Sembawang Bus Interchange, commenced its operations on 20th November 2005. It's main purpose is to house some existing services plying along the Sembawang area into a one-stop public transport hub in Sembawang, except for 169, 856, 962, on which its route remains unchanged and will still serve the Sembawang area.

    Services 167, 859, 980 & 981 were amended to the new Sembawang Interchange and a new service 882, was introduced to link from Sembawang to Sembawang Rd End Terminal (Loop)

    Its route changes:

    1) Service 167, previously from Sembawang Rd End Ter to Bt Merah, was amended to Sembawang Vista, Canberra Rd, Canberra Link, Sembawang Rd and continue its route to Bt Merah

    2) Service 859, previously from Yishun to Sembawang Cres (loop), was amended to Sembwang Vista, Canberra Rd, Admiralty Lane, Admiralty Link, Sembawang Dr, Sembawang Cres and continue its route to Yishun Int

    3) Service 980, previously from Admiralty Rd West to Lor 1 Geylang, was amended to Sembawang Vista, Sembawang Dr, Sembawang Ave,and continue its route to Lor 1 Geylang

    4) Service 981, previously from Sembawang Way to Senoko Loop (One-directional), was amended to Sembawang Vista and Sembawang Dr and continue its route to Senoko Loop.

    New Service

    Service 882    , was introduced on 20th November 2005, the same day Sembawang Interhcange commenced its operations. It plies from Sembawang Int to Sembawang Rd End Ter (Loop)

    (Service 882 was also being amended to serve Montreal Dr, Montral Link, Wellington Circle a few years later)
