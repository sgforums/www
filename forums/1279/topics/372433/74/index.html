    Originally posted by SBS7440T:

    860: Sengkang - Hougang St 21 (loop), similar to 119.

    864: Sengkang - Marina Centre, follow 80 to Hougang Ave 10, Hougang Ave 2, Upp Serangoon Rd, follow 80 again to Upp Paya Lebar Rd, Geylang Rd, follow 2 to Victoria St, Bras Basah Rd, Temasek Blvd, Temasek Ave, Marina Centre ter. Ply Temasek Ave & Temasek Blvd bi-directional. 

    865: Sengkang - HarbourFront, extended and renumbered from 860. Follow 119 to Upp Serangoon Rd, Bendemeer Rd, follow 65 to Orchard Blvd, then follow 16 to Tiong Bahru Rd, Lower Delta Rd, follow 65 again to HarbourFront.

    866: Sengkang - Ang Mo Kio, extended and renumbered from 59, similar to 86 with route difference in Sengkang (86 was amended before).
