    24 Aug 09, 11:47

    For Tibs/SMRT services which had a number of major redeployments due to  WLDEP opening followed by mass CCK/BBT and SKG/PUN SBS/Tibs swop in 1999...

    In the past before WLDEP opened,

    AMDEP/KJDEP dual control
    160, 167, 169, 855, 856

    Full AMDEP
    All Yishun (except 852 which is full KJ), Jln Kayu and northeast corridor Tibs services

    Full KJDEP
    852, 927 and all Woodlands and Bt Panjang services

    When WLDEP opened in 1998,

    Full KJDEP
    700, 911, 912, 913, 925, 926, 927, 960, 964 and all Bt Panjang services

    KJDEP/WLDEP dual control
    167, 178, 181, 187, 852

    AMDEP/WLDEP dual control
    160, 169, 171, 851, 853, 854, 855, 856
    >> Eventually 851 and 856 became full WLDEP

    Full WLDEP
    811, 812, 858, 900, 903

    All the Tibs services in Northeast corridors remain as full AMDEP, together with feeders 800 and 804.

    And in 1999-2000 when Tibs swop with SBS,

    Full WLDEP
    167, 178, 187, 700, 911, 912, 913, 960, 961 (former 181), 964
    >> services 925 and 926 remained as KJDEP thruout

    Full AMDEP
    171, 811, 812, 853, 854, 855, 859*, 980 (former 160)

    * Service 859 was introduced in Apr 1999 with full OAC Scania fleet from WLDEP.

    AMDEP/KJDEP dual control
    67, 852

    Whereas KJDEP operated all the CCK services when Tibs take over from SBS.

    When BBT came under Tibs in 2000,

    Full WLDEP
    75, 177, 184
    >> services 176 and 180 remained full KJDEP thruout

    Full AMDEP
    852

    AMDEP/KJDEP dual control
    61, 106

    All other BBT services came under full KJDEP.

    Feeder 343 was renumbered to 921 in Apr 2000 and converted to PAC, duties were handed over to WLDEP with TIB322D and TIB638T perm. On the same day feeder 920 was introduced and was under WLDEP too.

    Another round of depot transfers took place in early 2001,

    Full AMDEP
    851

    Full WLDEP
    859

    AMDEP/WLDEP dual control
    167, 980

    Service 852 went back to dual AMDEP/KJDEP.

    However with the introduction of services 962, 965 and 969 in 2001-2002, services 177 and 184 returned to full KJDEP.

    Somewhere in 2004, services 852 and 859 went back under full AMDEP. Service 980's WLDEP duties were reduced and given to AMDEP when service 950 was introduced in Sept 2004.
