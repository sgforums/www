    Originally posted by SBS7440T:

    What are the routes of 169, 178, 181 & 182 in 1995 before amended to Woodlands Reg Int?


    Service 182 (Tibs) plied along the whole of Bt Timah / Upp Bt Timah / Woodlands Rd stretch from Woodlands to Scotts Road, then along Orchard Rd, Bras Basah Rd, Connaught Dr, Fullerton Rd and terminate at Shenton Way terminal. On its return trip it plied along Coleman St and Hill St.

    Withdrawn in March 1996 due to Woodlands MRT rationalisation. It also duplicated service 171's route between Woodlands Rd to Orchard Rd.
