    SEMBAWANG BUS INTERCHANGE

    The Sembawang Bus Interchange, commenced its operations on 20th November 2005. It's main purpose is to house some existing services plying along the Sembawang area into a one-stop public transport hub in Sembawang, except for 169, 856, 962, on which its route remains unchanged and will still serve the Sembawang area.

    Services 167, 859, 980 & 981 were amended to the new Sembawang Interchange and a new service 882, was introduced to link from Sembawang to Sembawang Rd End Terminal (Loop)

    Its route changes:

    1) Service 167, previously from Sembawang Rd End Ter to Bt Merah, was amended to Sembawang Vista, Canberra Rd, Canberra Link, Sembawang Rd and continue its route to Bt Merah

    2) Service 859, previously from Yishun to Sembawang Cres (loop), was amended to Sembwang Vista, Canberra Rd, Admiralty Lane, Admiralty Link, Sembawang Dr, Sembawang Cres and continue its route to Yishun Int

    3) Service 980, previously from Admiralty Rd West to Lor 1 Geylang, was amended to Sembawang Vista, Sembawang Dr, Sembawang Ave,and continue its route to Lor 1 Geylang

    4) Service 981, previously from Sembawang Way to Senoko Loop (One-directional), was amended to Sembawang Vista and Sembawang Dr and continue its route to Senoko Loop.

    New Service

    Service 882    , was introduced on 20th November 2005, the same day Sembawang Interhcange commenced its operations. It plies from Sembawang Int to Sembawang Rd End Ter (Loop)

    (Service 882 was also being amended to serve Montreal Dr, Montral Link, Wellington Circle a few years later)

    May I share the history of Service 182?

    In 1972, it's between Benoi Rd & Upp Bt Timah Rd, under the ownership of UBC. In 1974, it was changed to ply New Bridge Rd from Upp Bt Timah Rd. When the Woodlands Int opened, it was amended to there, and subsequently to Marsiling Ter when it opened. In 1987, it was amended to Shenton Way Ter. In 1996, it was amended to Woodlands Reg Int and soon withdrawn.

    In 1998, it was re-introduced to ply Tuas from Jurong East via Jurong East Ctrl, Jurong Town Hall Rd, Boon Lay Way, Corporation Rd, Jln Ahmad Ibrahim. In 2001, shortened to Boon Lay Int, skipping Jurong East Ctrl, Jurong Town Hall Rd, Boon Lay Way, Corporation Rd, serving Jln Boon Lay

    In 2002, merged with 183 to ply between Boon Lay & Tuas South and to ply Jurong West St 64, Boon Lay Way, Upp Jurong Rd and PIE instead of Jln Boon Lay and Jln Ahmad Ibrahim. Amended to call at all stops along Upp Jurong Rd in 2004. An alternative service, 182#, will run between about 0900 to 2300 hours but not calling at Tuas Checkpt.

    In 2008, service 182# was renumbered 182M.
