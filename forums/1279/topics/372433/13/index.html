    Originally posted by Rk 2010:

    How did the actual routes of 74, 154, 7 and 30 came about?


    Service 154 was between Jurong interchange and MacPherson terminal. Extended on both ends to become present route.

    Service 7 was between Bedok interchange and Dempsey Rd. In the 80s, extended to Clementi then to Jurong East then back to Clementi and amended to ply Victoria St / Kallang Rd and Crawford St / North Bridge Rd instead of Nicoll Highway.

    Service 30 was between Bedok interchange and Jurong interchange. Extended to Boon Lay interchange in July 1990. Amended to ply ECP and Fort Rd instead of Robinson Rd / Shenton Way and Nicoll Highway in June 1992. With the amendment, service 10 was amended to ply Tanjong Katong Rd and Old Airport Rd to connect Old Airport Rd to the Shenton Way area, the Mountbatten Rd sector between Tanjong Katong Rd and Old Airport Rd was deleted. There was also an amendment in the 80s to ply Teban Gardens Rd towards Jurong interchange instead of proceeding straight along Jurong Town Hall Rd and turn left to Jln Ahmad Ibrahim towards Jurong interchange.

    Originally posted by Rk 2010:

    What about the route of 186, 147, 63 and 166? How did they came about?


    186: Shenton Way to Farrer Rd (loop) > Shenton Way to St. Michael's. Was re-introduced to cover the route of service 185 (Choa Chu Kang to Shenton Way) when it was shortened to Jln Anak Bukit. Extended to St. Michael's.

    147: Jurong East to Potong Pasir > Jurong East to Upp Serangoon > Clementi to Upp Serangoon > Clementi to Hougang Central. Was extended from Potong Pasir to Upp Serangoon when service 83 (Punggol to New Bridge Rd) was withdrawn. Also amended to ply Selegie Rd and Jln Besar / Bencoolen St instead of Lavender St, Victoria St / North Bridge Rd, with this amendment, service 148 (Clementi to Lor 1 Geylang) was introduced in June 1992 but withdrawn 5 months later. Another amendment was to skip Waterloo St and Middle Rd and instead ply Prinsep St.

    63: Re-introduced to ply between Bukit Merah and MacPherson (loop). Amended to Rumah Tinggi when service 62 (Hougang South to Rumah Tinggi) was shortened to New Bridge Rd. Extended to Eunos following the integration of service 382.

    166: Ang Mo Kio to Labrador, then extended to Clementi.

    When Tibs took over from SBS bus services,
    2 remaining SBS services remained in Tibs Territory.

    The notorious 170 and 171.

    171 was amended to serve Bukit Panjang in the mid 1990s, plying from Woodlands Road, to Bukit Panjang Road, then Pending Road, then Cashew Road, before plying to its original route.

    171 then came under Tibs later when Tibs took over Bukit Panjang.

    171 was then amended in 1999 with the opening of the LRT. Instead of using Mandai Road and Woodlands Road to reach Bukit Panjang, it was amended to serve BKE and then Bukit Panjang Road, Pending Road, Petir Road, Jelebu Road, Bukit Panjang Road, back to Upper Bukit Timah Road before continuing its former route.

    927 was extended from Mandai Road To Choa Chu Kang in 1995, serving BKE/Bukit Panjang Road/Teck Whye Avenue/Choa Chu Kang Avenue 4.

    Service 927 was amended to cover 171's deleted sector of its amended route in Mandai Road and Woodlands Road and oso started plying KJE in 1999 with the LRT rationalisation.

    Service 190 covered the deleted sector from Choa Chu Kang Int to Bukit Panjang Service 927 formely covered.

    Service 951 serving Marsiling To Boon Lay was deleted with the opening of Woodlands Int in 1996. Service 187 was extended from Bukit Panjang Int to Woodlands Int to cover most of the missing routes 951 formerly did. 180 was introduced to cover the existing route of 951 from Boon Lay to Bukit Panjang Road in 1996.
