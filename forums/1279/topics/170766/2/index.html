    If it is a vertical engine, then it cannot be horizontally opposed. The piston in a vertical engine moves up-down while that of a horizontal engine moves left-right. A horizontally opposed engine have 2 sets of pistons placed directly opposite to one another.

    The correct term for the 'N' in 'N113' is transversely mounted, vertical engine. The 'C' in 'CRB' stands for single deck rigid bus.
