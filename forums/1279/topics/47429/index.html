    Hi,

    On top of the list of DD you listed above, which were bought in bulk, SBS also did purchase a few other models as demonstrators.

    They are:
    1. Scania BR112DH (SBS7000E) with East Lancs body, introduced in 1982 and was taken off service around the time Service 31 was extended to Toa Payoh.

    2. Volvo B55-Ailsa (SBS4741U) with Alexander body, introduced in 1985 and was deregistered around the mid-1990s.

    3. Volvo B10MD (SBS4961B) with East Lancs body, introduced in 1984 and was deregistered in the mid-1990s.

    Cheers and warm regards
