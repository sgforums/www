    The tender this time round is NOT by Trips.

    SAF gives out the tender stating the number of expected crowd that they would be expecting everyday during the Open House.

    Bus companies would then tender for it by stating how many buses they would be deploying on the different days and how much.

    The staffs at the different departure points are counting the number of ppl departing is actually for admin purposes. So that they can actually decide if they shld grab any additional buses or they shld take out extra buses. Its actually up to them. At the same time, they record down the rego so as to have better control over the buses. As this time round, the tender went out to TNC and the buses belongs to different co. by recording down the departure timings and rego would ensure that they bus drivers do not wander off somewhere else.
