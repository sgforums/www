    Anyway, from Pasir Ris Int,
    1) Take Sv 15, then change to 16 at Marine Parade Rd.
    2) Take Sv 12, alight at UEC Rd [The Holy Family Church], than walk to Joo Chiat Rd [Katong Mall] and change to 16.

    From Tampines Int,
    1) Take 31 to Marine Parade Rd, then 16.
    2) Take 10, alight at UEC Rd [The Holy Family Church], than walk to Joo Chiat Rd [Katong Mall] and change to 16.

    Both of the first options are faster and you won't have to walk so much
