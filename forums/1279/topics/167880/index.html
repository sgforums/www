    Originally posted by ah-soh:
    is marina centre bus terminal controlled by smrtb? is that's so then is it the reasons that many SBST services like 97, 111, 133, 502 etc looped at Marina Centre while SMRTB services like 171, 960, 75, 77, 106 etc terminated at there?
    There are 2 containers at Marina Centre Terminal, 1 belongs to SBST, the other belongs the SMRT.

    SBST services that terminates there are 56, 195 & all Citybuzz services. Note that although 174M starts from there, the BCs are not required to report to the timekeeper.

    SMRT services that terminates there are 75, 77, 106, 171, 960, NR2, NR5, NR6, NR7 & NR8.

    Marina Centre Terminal cannot support too many services due to the limited parking space for buses. If you go & see the terminal during this coming Christmas Eve when 162M & 181M are in operation as well, you'll see the buses parked along the roadside all the way to & including the first bus-stop.
