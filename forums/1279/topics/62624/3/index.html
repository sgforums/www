    Headway in someway is similar to frequency. But note:

    Shorter headway = higher (or faster) frequency.
    Longer headway = lower (or slower) frequency.

    The headway of all services depends on the number of buses deployed, weather / traffic conditions and the speed drivers drive the bus.

    The headway of 9 to 14 minutes is just a rough estimate. Sometimes we have to give some incentive to the waiting time.
