    Withdrawal of Service 116

    14 August 2006 Â– Service 116, which was introduced on a trial basis in April 2006, will be withdrawn from today (15 Aug 2006) due to a lack of demand.

    The service had been on a three-month trial, plying a new route covering Hougang Central Bus Interchange, Hougang Avenue 4 and Buangkok Green Medical Park.

    With the withdrawal, commuters can take alternative Services 101, 161 and 325 to get to Institute of Mental Health from Buangkok Green.
