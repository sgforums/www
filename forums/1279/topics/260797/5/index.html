    PAC = Partial Air Con
    FAC = Fully Air Con
    OAC RCN's , the Original Air Con UD's , those TIB3xx , CSS178-187
    SCN CAC's , the Converted Air Con UD's (not the current ones , which is actually RCN's) , no longer around now
    WA DAF , the Alexander Setanta DAF , the one u see on Services like 172 , 985 , 975 etc
    Hispano DAF no longer around but is now in NZ

    Nissan U31 SCN CAC buses were TIB279Z-TIB280R, TIB311K-TIB317U, TIB390G. TIB390G was transferred to CSS and became CSS294H. All these buses no longer around, however similar to Nissan U31 RCN CAC buses (those buses than can open window upwards), mainly found on SMRT services 178, 187 and 975.

    Hispano DAFs were buses like MB0405 but their EDS were different, rego was TIB391Z-TIB424T. TIB391D-TIB404B were sold off in 1997, TIB405Z-TIB424T were transferred to CSS and became CSS270B-CSS289X. All these buses sold off in 2004.
