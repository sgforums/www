    Well, Nissans was deployed on 925 many years ago till the DAFs came and replaced them. Now, we are going to see a revival.
    History of bus deployments on service 925 from 1995-1998 (as far as I know )

    Jan 1996
    - service 925 (feeder service then) was converted PAC
    - Nissan Diesel SCN CACs (TIB317U) was deployed

    Mar 1996
    - service 925 was extended to Choa Chu Kang and converted to trunk service
    - Nissan CAC SCNs were eventually replaced by Hispano DAFs and WA DAFs

    Dec 1996
    - when Hispano DAFs were progressively transfered to CSS, Nissan Diesel OAC RCNs were deployed on service 925, together with the WA DAFs
    - those Nissan OAC RCNs (eg. TIB371L, TIB374D, TIB375B etc) were transferred over from AMDEP at that time

    Feb 1997
    - service 925 was converted to a FAC service
    - all NAC buses (Nissan SCNs and Hinos) were withdrawn
    - service was mainly made up mainly of Nissan OAC RCNs and WA DAFs with occasion MB0405 cameos

    1998
    - service 925 progressively became a full DAF fleet when all the Nissan OAC RCNs were transferred to WLDEP
    - ever since no Nissans were spotted on service 925
