  • SBS 9631X
    12 Feb 13, 18:07

    Sv 53: Downroute from PIE, start 1st trip at Loyang Ave 0655+hrs towards Bishan. 

    Sv 53's downroute is abit interesting, because unlike Sv 9, 31 etc, the BC driving this bus has absolutely no place to stop after he comes down from PIE before starting his downroute trip. So if he arrives at Loyang Ave 5mins earlier than his downroute, that's it, his entire trip is early by 5mins or more. If he's late, then the reverse happens. That's why I say his trip is at 0655hrs+, because the timing for that downroute can fluctuate +/- 10mins depending on how fast he exits from PIE. HAHA. This downroute is actually damn difficult to catch, but easy to spot, cause it's the bus towards Bishan with only students & no Airport workers.
