Renovation Works At Sengkang Interchange

  • TWS4793
    17 May 07, 23:08
    I went to Sengkang Interchange yesterday, despite having to go through the 'dark pit'.

    Here are some of the things I've noticed in Sengkang Interchange:

    1. New glass panels are being set up for the alighting berths, suggesting that the alighting berths are becoming air-conditioned too.
    2. Selected glass panels are being replaced progressively.
    3. A new automatic door is being installed in the boarding berths area.

    With the improvements made to Sengkang Interchange, users can now enjoy the convience of cool air-conditioning when they first step into Sengkang Interchange.

    To facillitate the renovation, Service 83 and 86 berths have been temporarily shifted to Berth B4 (Don't know whether it's the correct berth number).

    However, what I hope that the contractors could do is:

    1. Remove the idea of a 'dark pit'. It is almost impossible to see anything inside the bus park and also it is scary to some especially when the bus arrive into the bus park.
    2. Remove the extra door which separates the boarding berth and the alighting berth. It would be more convienent for passengers in this way.

    Any comments?
