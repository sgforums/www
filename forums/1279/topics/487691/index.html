    First opened on 26 December 1999. It was closed since 16 December 2012. It will be re-constructed and re-opened soon - either this year or next year.

    If this year, it's likely to be around December.

    If next year, it's likely to be together with DTL2 opening.

    "The new 7,350 square metres bus interchange will be seamlessly linked with LRT/ MRT stations and adjoining developments ... Eleven bus services will operate out of the new interchange. Presently the existing bus interchange serves about 7,500 to 8,000 commuters daily. Together with potential new users, these commuters will directly benefit from the enhanced integration of the new bus interchange with the existing Bukit Panjang LRT station and upcoming Bukit Panjang MRT Station." - Land Transport Authority of Singapore. 2012. New Integrated Transport Hub in 2015; Greater Convenience, Seamless Transfers for Bukit Panjang Commuters. [Press Release]. [Accessed 14 February 2015]. Available from: http://app.lta.gov.sg/apps/news/page.aspx?c=2&id=84f77f97-2e8a-43e6-b9de-83e456a0fb1a

    Bukit Panjang Bus Interchange is 7,350 sq metres. Bedok Bus Interchange is 16,000 sq metres and has 58 parking lots. A simple calculation of fraction and multiplication would suggest that Bukit Panjang Bus Interchange has around 26 parking lots. 

    However, considering there will be parking lots for articulated buses, let's halve the number to 13.

    If SMRT decides to operate 100% DD/SD at Bukit Panjang, then it's 26 parking lots. Otherwise, it's around 13 parking lots.
