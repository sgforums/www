    Route History

     

    Service 71 serves as a residential trunk route connecting the estates of Yio Chu Kang, Ang Mo Kio and Bishan. The route serves residential estates along Yio Chu Kang Rd, Ang Mo Kio Ave 4, Ang Mo Kio Ave 1 and Bishan Rd with enhanced connectivity to transport nodes at Yio Chu Kang and Bishan in addition to existing bus services to Ang Mo Kio.

     

    On 28 August 2017, SBS Transit’s FirstMAN A95 (SG5835M) was spotted displaying Service 71’s route details while on route training.
