    State-Assisted Funeral for the late Mr Othman Bin Work

    We are deeply saddened by the passing of Mr Othman Bin Wok yesterday and will like to express our condolences to the family.

    The State-Assisted Funeral Procession will leave Sultan Mosque for Choa Chu Kang (CCK) Muslim Cemetery at 2pm. 

    We seek bus commuters' understanding to expect delays in their journeys along the affected routes, where special traffic arrangements have been made to facilitate the Procession from Sultan Mosque to CCK Muslim Cemetery. 

     

    Stamford Road - Service 36

    Bras Basah Road - Services 36 and 518

    Eu Tong Sen Street, New Bridge Road - Services 2 and 12

    We apologise for any inconvenience caused. 

     

    -- Go-Ahead Singapore
