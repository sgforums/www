    Cessation of Peak Period Short Services.

    Hey folks! From 1 September 2016, there would be changes to some of our Peak Period Short Services (#PPSS).

    Services 222P, 240P, 268P, 307P and 912P will be renamed 222B, 240M, 268B/268C, 307A and 912M respectively, and operated by either SBS Transit or SMRT. The bus routes will remain the same, and continue to offer you an alternative to get to a nearby bus interchange and/or MRT station during peak periods!

    On the other hand, services 291P and 293P will be replaced by SBS Transit Bus Services 291 and 293. Additional trips will also be added for these services.

    So, if you have been taking these PPSS frequently, do check the posters for any changes to the operating hours and help spread the word too :)
