    Go-Ahead Singapore Resumes Staff Bus Service

    Go-Ahead Singapore today confirmed that it has resumed operation of its staff bus service with immediate effect.

    This follows a four-week period during which the company subcontracted it to Tower Transit due to a shortage of Bus Captains (BCs).

    David Cutts, Deputy Chairman, said: “We continue to make good progress recruiting BCs and are grateful to Tower Transit for their help”.
