    ========================================

    Common types of press releases:

    • - New or alternation of bus services (in particular, relating to BSEP).
    • - New procurements or features introduced by operators
    • - Policy changes (by LTA, e.g. GCM)
    • - Temporary diversions
    • - Fare adjustments
    • - Opening or closure of bus-related facilities (e.g. Interchanges, bus stops)
    • - Extension of bus operations on eve of particular events
    Major related discussion threads:
    • LTA's Bus Service Enhancement Programme (BSEP) (Part 4)
    • New Bus Services Announced by LTA/PTOs (Part 3)
    • Bus Contracting Model (Part 2)
    • GCM New/Transferred Buses
    • Capacity changes on bus services (Part 2)
    Please contribute to this thread:
    • - Uniform Resource Locators (URLs) - along with at least the title - of any (Singaporean) bus services' updates and announcements.
    • - Comments on (Singaporean) bus services' updates and announcements. (Also refer to the major related discussion threads listed above).
    • - any other events related to the Press Releases on bus services in Singapore.
    Thank you!

    The first generation was individual threads for only one event. The second generation was threads for press releases from bus companies. The third generation was threads for press releases on a quarterly basis. The fourth generation was threads for press releases on a yearly basis.

    We are now in the fourth generation.

    The first idea of the fifth generation of press releases threads was to have them in parts, where the first thread accumulates two thousand replies, before the second thread is created. However, this was rejected by the moderators as they feel that it is not wise to combine many years of press releases into one thread.

     

    I have a new idea on the fifth generation of press releases thread. This new idea moves away from the tradition of posting only press releases. Besides press releases, we could post news articles, blog articles, etcetera. Besides buses, we could extend our coverage to trains, since bus and train complement each other. Meanwhile, the threads remain on a yearly basis.

    This new idea may encourage a more active discussion, as discussions are not limited to only the press releases. Whenever there is an online article, any article, about buses and trains, we can discuss about it.

    This new idea is not intended to replace the other discussion threads. It is only meant for recording (a greater variety of) online articles and having casual discussions about the articles. For content-specific discussions, we can have them at the other discussions threads.

    What do you think of this idea?

    If you have an idea on how to improve on this idea, or another idea on the fifth generation of press releases thread, do write it here.

     

    Thank you for taking time to read this reply.
