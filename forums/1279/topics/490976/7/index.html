    Parks Service 408 will cease operations from 1 August 2016

     

    Route 408 will officially be withdrawn possibly due to low demand. Info: Parks Service 408 will cease operations from 1 August 2016. To get to Mount Faber Park, commuters can board Service 131 or 145 from the bus stop (14121) in front of the HarbourFront Station and alight at Telok Blangah Heights (14041) along Henderson Road. For those heading to the Labrador Nature Reserve, they can get there by the MRT and alight at the CC27 Labrador Park Station. They may also board Service 10 or 30 (including Service 143 or 188 from HarbourFront Interchange) and alight at the bus stop opposite PSA Building (15141).
