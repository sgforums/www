    Before we consider whether the other services would be diverted, there was one key preliminary issue to be considered: how would the services be affected?

    Marine Parade Station was just opposite Parkway Parade as well as the Marine Parade neighbourhood. The diversion (as per what you referred to), regardless of where it goes, would effectively mean that the crucial bus stops opposite/before Parkway Parade would be skipped. In my view, it would not be a practical option to divert them away from Parkway Parade simply because it would cause inconvenience to passengers.

    The diversion would also cause the loops of services 15, 47 and 966 to become redundant (in fact, it is impossible to amend 47 for the purpose of diversion since it cannot ply the temporarily closed Joo Chiat Road). Put simply, there is no other better routings for these three services other than to remain in their current state. Therefore, I personally do not think that the services would be amended to skip Parkway Parade area.
