    LTA-SMRT Joint Release: New Bus Service 944 to Serve Bukit Batok

    New Bus Service 944 to Serve Bukit Batok

    As part of the Bus Service Enhancement Programme, a new bus service, Service 944, will begin on Sunday, 27 August 2017, to serve new housing developments in Bukit Batok West linking commuters and residents between Bukit Batok Bus Interchange / MRT Station, Bukit Batok West Avenue 6 and Bukit Batok Road. The service will also provide more convenient access to key amenities such as Bukit Batok Polyclinic and West Mall. 

    2.    Service 944 is the 76th new bus service introduced under the Bus Service Enhancement Programme, which targets to launch 80 new bus services between 2012 and 2017. It will operate daily (including public holidays) from 5.30am to 1.00am. The Annex provides more details of the service route and bus stops which Service 944 will be calling at.

    3.    Passengers may contact the SMRT Customer Hotline at 1800-336-8900 from 7.30am to 8.00pm on weekdays (excluding public holidays) or visit www.smrt.com.sg for more information.
