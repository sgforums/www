    x-over means same BC drives on the secondary service. For eg. 243/198. It's driven by pemr 243 BC when it does 198 x-over.

    But x-over is different from AM-PM shift.

    There are many times that a perm bus is replaced by a cameo bus during PM shift, especially on the SMRT services

    For example,

    TIB672T on 965 (AM shift) which is driven by one of perm 965 BC

    For afternoon, it does 178 (driven by another BC- one of perm 178 BCs)

    So, to conclude, another bus would replace TIB672T on 965 for PM shift.

    TIB672T on 178 probably replaces one of perm/ cameo buses on PM split after finishing its AM Shift on 965.

     

    Note: AM-PM shift =/= AM/PM split

    Hence, AM-PM split (changing of buses after AM shift has ended) is not a x-over bus.
