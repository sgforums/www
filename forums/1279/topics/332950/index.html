    Series U/UA31 of Nissan Spacerunner.

    First produced in 1980 Japan Domestic Market.

    N denotes 5.5 meters wheelbase.

    Length 12 meters

    Width 2.5 meters

    Nissan Diesel PE6H engine, longitudinal mount, 230PS (226 bhp), Japan calendar Showa 54 (1980) emission regulations.

    Fuji Heavy Industries R15/5E bodywork. Do note that FHI no longer produces bus bodyworks as of 2003.

    ZF4HP500 gearbox.
