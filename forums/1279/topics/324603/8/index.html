    Express 502

    Introduced in May 1997. Between Hougang Central interchange and Boon Lay interchange.
    In Sep 2000, extended to Sengkang interchange.
    On Sunday 27 Jul 2003, shortened to loop at Marina Centre, under NEL rationalisation phase 2.
    On Saturday 02 July 2005, skipped the stop along Orchard Link and amended to call at the stop along Grange Rd.
    On 26 Dec 05, service was enhanced and extended to Pioneer Rd North and short trips were during peak hours, 502A and 502B with 502A skipping Jurong West/East Ave 1, Toh Guan Rd and Boon Lay way and 502B starting and ending at Jurong West Ave 1 skipping Pioneer Rd North, Jurong West Ave 5, St 75, St 64 and Boon Lay Ave.
    Double deckers were introduced back recently.
    On Sunday 15 Jun 2008, calls at an additional pair of bus stops along Jurong West St 64.
