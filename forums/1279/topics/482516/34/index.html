    sv 168 bunching is very common. Earlier 2 DD bunching was common, now even 3 DDs coming together is a usual sight. 

    168 is operating at 05-07 mins frequency during peak hours. It is so easy to bunch given there is crash loading at SAFRA, Century Square, Tampines MRT, Regional Library, bef TPE stop. During peak hours, you need 2 DDs to clear the crowd bef bus reaches TPE. 2 DDs coming together helps distribute loading to 70-80 pax per bus. 

    In spite of the awesome frequency, buses departing WRI also leave with 60-70 pax per bus. If a bus is delayed, can expect 100 pax on first bus, 50 pax on the next.
