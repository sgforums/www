    It has been a while ever since the B10M Mark III DMs have been deregistered.

    Now, the time for the B10M Mark IV DMs have comes.Â 

    Many of these buses, however, were given a 2-year extension and a repainted livery, and refurbishment, which include the silvery rims, most likely for the last time.

    But apparently, not every bus got to enjoy such luxury. Some were, unfortunately, deregistered earlier than the others, and never back to the roads of Singapore again.

    It has come to our attention that together with these "going to be deregistered" DMs, the well-known Superbus SBS997A will be walking into history as well, with its last permanent service on 83. Much as we hope that SBS997A will also receive refurbishment and extended lifespan, it does not.

    So, let us say our farewell words to these buses, to those deregistered DMs, and also the only 15m Superlong bus in Singapore.

    As for me, I have never taken SBS997A before. However, I have boarded the Mark IV DMs countless times when I'm young. Although the times I have boarded or seen you guys is much lesser today, the memories of riding on you, young or old, especially the times when I dun even know what bus-spotting is, always remain in our memories.

    Farewell is not just words. For those lucky DMs which receive 2-year extension, hope you guys have a good time for the last few years with us. As for those DMs and SBS997A which are going to be deregistered, I shall say: Thanks for serving us for the past 17 years, and we shall not forget you!

    How about you? Do you have any farewell words to these DMs and SBS997A? Do share here!
