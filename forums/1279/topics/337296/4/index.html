    Time to get used to seeing buses off the roads.

    Back then, bus models were plenty. We have Berliet, Ford, Austins, Seddons, Leylands Atlantean (Soon Chow, WA, DM), Leyland National, Renault, Scania BR112, Dennis Dominator, Volvo B57, Volvo B55 DD (East Lanc), Nissan Diesel (Fuji Body), Leyland Victory (or Guy Victory), MB 1417 (Soon Chow), MB 1413, MB LP300, Albion Viking etc.

    I have taken almost all the models, except the Bedfords.

    In my school years, I had taken MB 1417, MB O305 (Willowbrook), Volvo B57, Volvo B10M (Van Hool), Renault, Volvo B55 double decker, Leyland Lynx, Leyland Olympian, Hino RK176, Hino HT228, Hino HT238, DAF 220LT (with ZF gearbox), Leyland Victory.

    Back then, bus seats were all red. I remember having taken a Volvo B10M in a very unique body. But can't remember what was the body.

    Then came TIBs with Hino RK176, and Nissan Diesel U31. The U31 is the first bus model that arrive with ZF gearbox.

    So dear bus fans and enthusiasts, the only way to continue our little hobby is to get SBS to expand the variety of the buses.

    AND STOP BUYING THE KUBs and START BUYING OTHER MODELS!
