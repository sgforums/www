    With effect from Sunday, 7 Dec 2003, Service 82 will be amended to operate between Serangoon interchange and Punggol Rd End, and will be diverted to serve Punggol MRT station enroute.

    http://www.sbstransit.com.sg/whatsnew_7dec2003_82.asp

    With effect from Sunday, 7 Dec 2003, Service 62 will be amended to operate between Punggol interchange and Lor 1 Geylang (loop).

    http://www.sbstransit.com.sg/whatsnew_7dec2003_62.asp

    With effect from Sunday, 7 Dec 2003, Service 103 will be amended to operate between Serangoon interchange and Seletar Camp (loop). Services 378 and 379 will be withdrawn on the same day.

    http://www.sbstransit.com.sg/whatsnew_7dec2003_103378379.asp

    With effect from Sunday, 30 Nov 2003, Service 107 will be extended to Hougang Central interchange.

    http://www.sbstransit.com.sg/whatsnew_30nov2003_107.asp
