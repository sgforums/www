    AIRCON 168 may be the first aircon service but there was also a normal sv. 168. AIRCON 168 only had 8 buses and its operating time was limited. Although AIRCON 168 was withdrawn in 1987, the normal 168 continued to run until 1989. The first FAC sv. was AIRCON 3 while the first aircon sv. from the start was AIRCON 118.

    The desto @ the door side would be the re-bodied Leyland Victory with aircon.
