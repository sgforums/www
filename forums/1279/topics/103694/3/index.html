    Originally posted by Volvo Olympian:
    Last time CSS is under comfort... That bus is blue in colour...
    No, no, no. Last time, City Shuttle Service (CSS) was jointly operated by Singapore Shuttle Bus (SSB) and NTUC Comfort. CSS was launched in 1975 for connections between fringe carparks and the CBD. This was later extended to serve outlying housing estates. There were 9 CSS services. 1, 4 and 6 were operated by Comfort while 2, 3, 5, 7, 8 and 9 by SSB.

    In the picture, only the first bus is light blue. That is the Comfort bus. The other 2 MB 309 buses behind it are yellow. They belong to SSB. Note that the stripe is thicker and not angled like the first one. All the buses had CSS regos.
