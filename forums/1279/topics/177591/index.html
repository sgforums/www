    A shift = AM shift (9 hours)
    P shift = PM shift (9 hours)

    S shift = Split shift
    N shift = Night shift

    SBST:

    A shift = start of operation to ard 1500hrs
    P shift = ard 1500hrs to end of operation
    S shift = Split shift(sumtimes SWING from 0900-1200, sumtimes 1200-1500, it all depends)Normally found on weekday
    T shift = Day Shift(ard 0700-1900)Normally found on weekday, so for e.g., 997 does S Shift on Weekdays and does T shift on Saturdays
    E Shift = Split shift(Extended, like Day shift)Acts like student bus
    N Shift = Night shift(Found on Nite Owl/NR/409)

    SMRT:

    AM Shift = same as SBST A shift
    PM Shift = same as SBST P shift
    S Shift = same
    TQ = same as SBST T shift

    18 Mar 06, 09:43
    A/P Shift comes with Meal and normal Breaks (rare cases has Tea Breaks)
    S Shift comes with Tea and and normal Breaks (some cases have Meal)
    E Shift comes with Meal and normal Breaks (rare cases has Tea Breaks)
    T shift comes with Tea, Meal and normal Breaks.

    Normal Breaks lasts up to 19min for a A/P Shift
    Normal Breaks lasts up to 10min for S/T shift
    Meal breaks begins from 20/21min for any shift
    Tea breaks begins from 10-19min for S/E/T Shift

    A/P Shift with Tea breaks last 20min exactly (or 20min or more but shorter than the Meal break)

    No break will be indicated if the break time is 4min or less

    Change shift break lasts from 2min onwards.
