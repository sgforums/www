    SBS Transit Introduces Stadium Direct Bus Services

    Concert goers, football enthusiasts and food-lovers will soon have reason to rejoice. From 23 December 2005, SBS Transit will be introducing a new set of bus services named Stadium Direct which provide direct links to popular housing estates during special events at the Kallang area when demand for public transport is high. The Stadium Direct services will serve commuters in Ang Mo Kio, Bedok, Boon Lay and Tampines.

    The buses will be at the bus stop after Kallang Theatre along Stadium Walk to pick up passengers after the end of each selected events. For a start, Stadium Direct services will be deployed for the following concerts held at the Indoor Stadium:

    Â• 23 December 2005: Very A-Do Live in Singapore
    Â• 14 January 2006: Stefanie Sun Live in Singapore
    Â• 24 January 2006: Backstreet Boys Â“Never Gone TourÂ”

    The four different routes of Stadium Direct services will ply along these roads:

    Ang Mo Kio Route

    The Ang Mo Kio Route will ply from Stadium Walk to Ang Mo Kio Avenue 10, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 3, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 4, Yio Chu Kang Road, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 6 and terminates at Yio Chu Kang terminal.

    Bedok Route

    The Bedok Route will ply from Stadium Walk to Bedok Reservoir Road, Bedok North Avenue 3, Bedok North Street 1 and terminates at Bedok bus interchange.

    Boon Lay Route

    The Boon Lay Route will ply from Stadium Walk to Jurong Town Hall Road, Jurong East Central, Jurong East Avenue 1, Boon Lay Avenue, Jalan Boon Lay and terminates at Boon Lay bus interchange.

    Tampines Route

    The Tampines Route will ply from Stadium Walk to Tampines Avenue 2, Tampines Street 32, Tampines Street 34, Tampines Street 45, Tampines Avenue 9, Tampines Street 41, Tampines Avenue 7 and terminates at Tampines bus interchange.

    Stadium Direct services will charge a flat fare of $2 for adult and a flat fare of $1 for student, child or senior citizen paying by ez-link card or cash. Concession rate and e-stamp will not be applicable for these services.

    As Stadium Direct services are deployed for selected events, posters will be put up at the four terminating bus interchanges and terminal as well as at bus-stops that these services call at to inform commuters whenever these services are available. Commuters can also log on to the SBS Transit website, www.sbstransit.com.sg to find out more about Stadium Direct services.
