    50th of the production batch.

    9888Y and 9810X are not part of the production batch (according to VIN), these two buses had their chassis built in Irvine, UK while the others are built in Poland. These two buses are also assembled in Volgren's plant in Dandenong, Victoria, Australia.

    The mystery about 9810X is whether SBS Transit ordered 51 B10TLs, or this was given as a Voith demo, but did its tests before it got registered and eventually ended up in the main fleet.

    VIN:
    YV3S1E117XC050141 B10TL-7300 JG5446 Ar H59/33D 11/1999 Kowloon Motor Bus 3ASV50

    YV3S1E110XC050143 B10TL-7300 JG2912 Ar H59/33D 10/1999 Kowloon Motor Bus 3ASV43

    Taken from: www.buslistsontheweb.co.uk

    I believe the chassis was done in around 1999. The date there is the new date, ie the first registration date, it is basically built together with the 3ASVs of KMB. Also note that 3ASV50 and 3ASV43's registration date was around the same time as 9888Y, October 1999.
