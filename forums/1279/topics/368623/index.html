    managed to stumble upon a little of how it looked.

    the layout is very similar to the previous Boon Lay Bus Interchange but with lesser sawtooths.

    colour scheme is dark blue, gray and white. not as plain as the previous aircon interchanges.

    there are 5 alighting berths, which is different from the 3 in the layout.

    then there are 6 sawtooth boarding berths, each for 3 services only.

    22 end-on berths serves 11 services. I am very sure about this. a unique point is that 4 of the end-ons in the middle are designed with twice the length of railings for queueing.

    I think 30, 154, 174 and 198 will get them since they consistently have longer queues.
