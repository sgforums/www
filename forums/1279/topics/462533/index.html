    For Tampines residents, there will be a new Express Service 513 plying between Tampines West and the CBD from 17 Dec! 

    With limited stops and the use of ECP, the journey time is expected to be comparable to taking a feeder service and transferring to a train into the city. This service will operate two trips on weekday mornings at 7.30am and 7.45am from Tampines Ave 5 towards Shenton Way. In the evening peak hours, the service will operate two return trips from Anson Road towards Tampines Ave 5 at 6.15pm and 6.30pm.
