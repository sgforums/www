    Well, I have been alone on a bus many times... 85, 95, 200, 34, 82, 43, 48, 58 (when it first started), 30 (the last bus), etc etc... But my funniest experience was on CSS603...

    A few years back, during the SARS scare, I went to HK to attend my cousin's wedding. When I came back, the people in my army camp scared I got SARS. So I went to Tan Tock Seng Hospital to check whether I had SARS. In the end, I was verified to have no SARS.

    After the check-up, I walked to Balestier Rd and took CSS603 to Orchard. On the way, the sergeant major of my army camp's medical centre called me and asked me how's the check-up. I told him I went to TTSH and everything was fine. Then, dunno why, all the passengers in the bus suddenly alighted along Stevens Rd, and I was all alone in the bus from Stevens Rd to Orchard Rd.

    Maybe the passengers heard me talking about TTSH and SARS... Hence everyone kiasee and alighted, before I actually told the sergeant major that "I'm fine and I've no SARS"...

    Although I had no SARS, my company CSM told me to "rest" at home for 2 weeks (at if he's scared my clothes and my skin and my hair got SARS virus after the TTSH visit...) So I had 2 weeks of "unofficial leave" and I avoided a major exercise + my army camp's spring cleaning... Haha!
