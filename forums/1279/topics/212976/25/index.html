    Remembered along Sembawang/Upp Thomson Rd, service 171 was the first to have bendy buses (TIB838H) in Mar 1996. TIB838H then went to serve service 960 when it was introduced in July 1996.

    Service 160 was then the next to have bendy buses when Tibs bought in more Bendies in 1997. Bendy buses was deployed on service 160 only on Sundays/Public Holidays PM Peak.

    Bendies were withdrawn from the service when service 160 was renumbered to 980 in 1999. Checked with the Tibs and they claimed that there was a parking constraints at Lor 1 Geylang terminal However bendies were redeployed on service 980 once again in year 2000 much to my delight Deployment also on Sundays/Public Holidays with cameos on weekdays once in a while.

    Service 169 also started to have bendies too in year 2001, with many of them during the weekday peak hours.
