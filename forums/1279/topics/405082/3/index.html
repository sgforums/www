    FEEDBACK NUMBER:20100811-0031

    We refer to your email dated 11 August 2010.

    You may wish to know that service 170X and 170 are considered the same service. Hence, transfer between these two services is not valid according to the transfer rules and the Fare Calculator is unable to allow calculation of fares for transfer between these 2 services.
