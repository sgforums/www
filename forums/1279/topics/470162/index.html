    Service 141

    Service 141 will provide better connectivity for commuters travelling between Lorong 1 Geylang and Toa Payoh.

    From Lorong 1 Geylang Bus Terminal, the new trunk service will ply along Lavender Street, Jalan Besar, Rangoon Road, Thomson Road, Toa Payoh Rise and Lorong 1 Toa Payoh before terminating at Toa Payoh Bus Interchange. As Jalan Besar has a one-way road network, the service will travel in the opposite direction along Gloucester Road and Serangoon Road on the return trip. This service will also call at four new bus stops along Toa Payoh Rise, Gloucester Road and Rangoon Road.

    Service 141 will enhance the connectivity to key transport nodes such as Kallang, Farrer Park, Novena, Caldecott and Toa Payoh MRT stations. Additionally, it will provide commuters with convenient access to key amenities including the Jalan Besar Stadium, medical institutions such as Thomson Medical Centre, Tan Tock Seng Hospital and Mount Elizabeth Novena Medical Centre and shopping malls such as City Square and Velocity@Novena Square.
