New service 119: Sengkang - Hougang Street 21 (Loop)

  • off_service
    5 Sep 03, 17:01
    SBS Transit will introduce service 119 to cover part of the former 85's route, from Sengkang to Hougang Street 21 (Hougang South Interchange). It will serve the 12 schools along the route.

    The service will start operation from 14 September 2003.

    For more details, please refer to SBS Transit for more details.
