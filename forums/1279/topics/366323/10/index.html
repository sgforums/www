    Originally posted by SBS9364S:

    Anyway, wad r the basic skills 2 take photo which will let BC unnotice when u r taking picture of the buses?

    Hmm.

    • Take from afar, but your camera must have some awesome zooming capabilities.
    • You can try to turn invisible
    • Pretend you are fiddling/testing with your camera while having your camera pointed at the bus ready for shots. If possible, pretend to shoot the scenery
    • Prented to be a tourist!. Have a mindset of a tourist, and shooting at the city area can be more easier on your part.
    • leave your camera on a stationary object after adjusting your angles and stuffs, using a camera remote/timer then look away then press.
    • Try not to wear bright colour shirts so that BCs won't notice you so easily.
    • If you want, use a small, stealth camera (pen camera or something). That, the BC confirm can't even suspect.

    i think it's still ok to take openly ba, it's like any other photography. Just point, shoot, thank the driver by waving. At least let the driver know that hey, he's in a photograph that may appear somewhere.
