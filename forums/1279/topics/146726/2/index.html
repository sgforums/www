    TEMPORARY DIVERSION OF SMRT BUS SERVICES
    FOR MID-AUTUMN FESTIVAL CELEBRATIONS AT CHINATOWN

    In conjunction with the Mid-Autumn Festival celebrations at Chinatown on 4 and 18 September 2005, a stretch of Eu Tong Sen Street and New Bridge Road between Upper Cross Street and Kreta Ayer Street will be closed to all vehicles from 3pm to 11pm. As such, the routes of SMRT Services 61, 190, 851, 961 and 970 will be diverted during these hours. Please refer to the attached map for details.

    Commuters can contact Transitlink Hotline at 1800-2255663 or SMRT Customer Relations Centre at 1800-3368900 for more information.
