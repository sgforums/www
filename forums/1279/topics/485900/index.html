Route Amendment of Service 853 from 10 Aug 2014

  • SBS7557R
    29 Jul 14, 22:52

    Starting 10 August 2014, parts of the route for Bus Service 853 will be amended to bring greater convenience and accessibility to passengers visiting the temporary Yishun Polyclinic located at 30A Yishun Central 1, S(768796).

    Please refer to attached poster for more details.

    Passengers may contact SMRT Customer Relations Centre at 1800-336-8900 from 7.30am to 6.30pm on weekdays or visit www.smrt.com.sg for more information on Service 853.
