  • off_service

    Emission standards for diesel driven vehicles will be raised to Euro 4 from the current Euro 2 standards.

    The government initially wanted to raise the standard to Euro 3 in the past 2 years but scrapped the idea due to the economy downturn.

    Taxi operators are also encouraged to use CNG taxis.
