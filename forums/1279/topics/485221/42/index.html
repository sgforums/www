    851 did have a period without bendies.

    On the day of CCK handover from SBST to TIBS:

    850X 851T 852R 875A 879P - WLDEP 851 > WLDEP 960

    On that same day 960 was transferred from KJDEP to WLDEP. All 960 KJDEP bendies (869T. 872H etc) went to 190.

    In this period the ex 851 bendies were replaced by random rigids (of all types - O405, Scania 113, UD etc)

    Only during the weekends that 856 bendies (856E, 859Y, 860S, 861P, 862L etc) did 851 in that period.

    It was after the last of Mark II bendies were registered that 851 got 3 of them perm:

    997G 999B 1000B > WLDEP 851

    These 3 were quickly replaced by 3 Habits (of course after 190 and 960 got all the new Habits first):

    1042E 1045Y 1046U > WLDEP 851

    1045Y were then replaced by 1001Z.

    In this period WLDEP was spamming 851 with Scanias. There were not enough O405's to go around and the CAC's were still NAC's.

    849B (perm WLDEP 960) did one trip during late morning post-peak on 851 (9am'ish) after doing 856.

    851 was then transferred back to AMDEP, with 859 going the other way to WLDEP. As a result 851 got the 2 859 bendies in exchange for the 3 in WLDEP:

    1054X 1057M > AMDEP 851

    As the Habit registrations picked up speed that 851 got the 8 or so batch preceding the current one:

    1023K 1057M 1081S 1112L 1113J 1114G 1120M 1201M 1137R 1138M

    1120M and 1137R then left for AMDEP 106 with no replacement. 1201M also left, replaced by 1147L.

    1114G then went to 800, replaced by 976S from 812.

    In this period 1096A, 1097Y, 1098U, 1099S 1198P and 1199L cameo'ed on 851 often. 1097Y then went to KJDEP 106. These guys were not perm 851 as they did 61/67/854 more than 851.

    In this period AMDEP was spamming 851 with very frequent and regular Lance cameo's. 1002X, 1017D and 1018B were perm, with the Volgren ones coming from 853. Whenever a bendy is sucked away to 61/67, it is usually replaced by a Lance or CAC cameo.

    By now all the AMDEP Scania's (including ELBO CAC's) had gone to WLDEP (secondary bus types: KJDEP - DAF, WLDEP - Scania, AMDEP - Lance). UD's were running between all 3 depots.

    Quite recently when 1023K, 1057M and 1081S left then 1096A and 1099S replaced them on a perm basis.

    976S then left 851 for good half a year ago or so. Only a few weeks ago did 8009J come in.
