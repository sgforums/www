    AMDEP feeders

    I don't know if it is only me but I find the way AMDEP has managed AMK feeders is quite lousy. Compared to them BNDEP, CGBP, SLBP does quite a good job with its feeders. 

    Look at 334 - now has 9 DDs no BSEP

    240 has 7 DDs no BSEP

    241 has 9 DDs only 1 BSEP

    243 has so many DDs + BSEP 

    291 has 11 DDs with 2 BSEP

    293 has graduated to have 5-6 DDs

    225 has around 4 DDs in its fleet

    Now look at AMK

    261 no DD, 3 BSEP add for such a short route - 8 buses

    269 no DD, 4 BSEP add - 10 buses

    265/268 just add BSEP as well as PPSS

    * None of the above have got DDs, though they deserve it to clear the crowds. Its been very lousy deployment on AMDEP side.

    Cons of doing this:

    * Way too many buses clogging interchange during peak hours

    * Return journey of most of these buses is empty, wasting resource

    * The job can be done by DD on S-shift, but adding so many SDs is increase in number of BCs, fuel and maintenance costs

    * Heavy bunching on all these services and delays as first bus super crowded slowing down first bus, next buses come and bunch

    I think AMDEP seriously needs to consider deploying DDs on its feeders and reduce 2 buses for 261 and 269 each.
