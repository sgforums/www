    Originally posted by BusAnalayzer:

    Sengkang East --> Sengkang East Rd (many take as intra town and alight at Sengkang east road, Kopitiam, transfer to other services)

    There are few who head to Yishun... 

    Sorry, but I have to disagree with your "distinct loading patterns" with my own experience of loading of svc 85. This is loading on sv 85 on 4th June, at 6.15pm: (I posted this in "Loading levels on buses" thread as well)

    Punggol Temp Int - 18 (Svc 3 just left 1 min earlier)
    Punggol Ctrl - [-1] 17, [+5] 22, [-5+3] 20, [-1+6] 25
    (Note that at least 11 pax who board from interchange have not alighted yet)
    Punggol East - [+6] 31
    Sengkang East Dr - [-2] 29
    Sengkang East Way - [-1+3] 31
    Rivervale Dr - 31, [-1+7] 37
    Compassvale Dr - [-3+3] 37, [-1+6] 42
    Sengkang East Rd - [-2+17] 57 (BC had to tell people to move in)
    Sengkang East Way - [+2] 59, [-5?+3] 57, [+2] 59
    Sengkang West Way - [-1+5] 63 (KUB in opposite direction had 10 standees), [-6?+6?] 63 (totally lost count, actual pax onboard may be slightly smaller but not much difference), [-4?+5?] 64
    Jln Kayu - [-2+3] 65, [-1+3] 67 (Citaro in opposite direction had around 15 standees)
    Lentor Ave - 67 all the way
    Yishun Ave 2 - 67, [-26?+2] 43 (Khatib MRT), [-1+1] 43, [-12] 31, [-3] 28 (Yishun MRT)
    Yishun Temp Int - [-28] 0

    So, if you do some simple calculations:

    1. At Sengkang East Rd, there are still many ppl on the bus who boarded from Punggol. At least 7 pax from Punggol MRT were on the bus before Compassvale Dr, of whom at least 3 pax did not alight until Sengkang East Rd (this is based on the assumption that those who alighted before Sengkang East Rd all boarded at Punggol MRT, which is obviously not true). At least 23 pax from Punggol (including Punggol Ctrl/East) were on the bus before Sengkang East Rd, and not all alighted at Sengkang West or Jln Kayu -- I believe there must be some of them who alighted at Yishun/Khatib. Take note these are the minimum numbers.

    2. 17 pax boarded at Sengkang East but only 2 alighted at Sengkang East Rd. I even doubt that all of them alighted before hitting TPE.

    3. I agree that most ppl taking sv 85 at Sengkang East Rd use it as a feeder to Sengkang West. However, take note that many boarded at Sengkang West towards Yishun also.

    4. There's a good 67 pax on board when reaching Khatib MRT, so I'm afraid the statement "There are few who head to Yishun" is untrue.

    I also thought 85 is an almost useless service before I personally went down to do loading analysis. I was also shocked when some ppl were almost unable to board.
