    All, hooray. On SBS 3504U double decker sbs 117 from punggol to sembawang now. 

    Riveria LRT bus stop: 5.59am wait sbs 3/83/85

    6.03am take sbs 83

    6.11am reach Punggol int

    6.17am sbs 117 arrives at berth B4

    6.47am reach khatib Mrt

    6.59am reach sembawang int. 

    Just to get an estimated feeling of the time to reach destination. 

    Of course not going to be super accurate because today is Sunday and it is the 3rd trip for a new launched service.

    Initial findings:

    48 min from Punggol east (Riveria LRT) to reach khatib mrt. This is slower than sbs 85 which takes 33 min plus 10 min waiting time.

    The longer time for sbs 117 is attributed to the winding around seletar aerospace and the super long yishun ave 1. This is as good as sbs 85 going 1 round in Sengkang east to west. So to all detractors who say sbs 117 is much faster, please think again. Take note there are only 2 passengers on sbs 117 today and the timing is already as such. 

    Exactly 1 hr from Punggol east (Riveria LRT) to reach sembawang int. Sbs 117 takes 42 min from Punggol int to sembawang int. In future, with increase in load, may need add 10 to 15 min.

    Cheers. Thanks.
