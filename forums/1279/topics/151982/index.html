    from today, commuters whose fares are deducted wrongly due to system errors will get a full refund of the fares paid instead of the difference..
    Example: charged $1 for an 80c ride, you will be refunded $1 (the old system is you will get 20c back).

    OR a Complimentary ticket can also be given instead of a refund.

    the scheme does not applies to human error such as forgetting to tap when alighting (passenger gets refunded for difference in fares only)

    claims to be made within 3 days at transitlink offices or via online or call
    1800-CALL-ONE
