    Hey there everyone!

    Hmmm... Asking me what had inspired me to really like buses, it could be traced way back 3 to 4 years ago, when Service 5 was withdrawn and Service 970 introduced.

    When Service 5 was still around, I started observing the yellow-orange striped Tibs buses turning from Alexandra Rd down to Lower Delta Rd, but nothing beyond that. It was when Service 970 started that I really began observing buses, because from that first trip on the service onwards, I fell in love with MBO.405s

    Proceeding from there, I started observing the permanent buses deployment on the service and the cameos used. And my interest in buses really widened when I took the bus down to Bukit Panjang and saw all the other yellow-orange striped Tibs buses Because I was still using BCP then, I used to come down to Bukit Panjang after me dinner and homework and revisions and stroll over there, looking at the Tibs buses roaming to and fro...

    Erm... I think if I were to continue telling me story, you'll all gonna fall asleep, so that's how Service 970 inspired me to like buses
