    My passion for buses started when i was in kindergarten. I believe even just before i entered kindergarten, the passion for buses was just at its infancy stage. Over the years, a lot of changes, both big and small have taken place in the world of transportation. I remember when the govt announced the transfer of 15 of SBS services to Tibs in 1994. I was like: Huh?! What the hell is an SBS bus doing in a Tibs infested area? I thought they a had rule or something against that... Anyway, i always pray and hope for the best in buses future although i am aware the govt is embarking on making rail the backbone of the transport industry in Singapore....
