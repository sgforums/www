    Besides targeting at school kids, safety campaign also target at bus operations

    this post will cover bus driver

    the bus driver must always make sure his passengers r safe. if accident occur due to bad driving then it is he to blame n not the bus or anyone else for causing harm to passenger. a bus is an object that move. anything that move is liable to mishap.

    as such our bus driver r called bus captain s he is the sole controller of the bus.

    the bus driver is expected to master smooth driving of a bus. much training goes into his driving skills to see that he does not jerk n jerk the bus. there is a famous test carried out on bus called the glass of water test. if the water spill out of the glass, u fail in ur drive test.

    still today i experience hard baking . this is probably no fault to the driver on those new generation disc brake buses like B9 or K230Ub where brakes are so afe, they literally make the bus stop real fast. as times progress brake technology progress so brakes get real effective. however it i no excuse for drivers who rive old gen bues to break excessively when stopping. when stopping or slowing down always have a good distance of road to slow down before coming to complete stop and apply brake softly.

    in todays world, the bus driver has only to take care of driving, issue bus ticket, update the IDFC if got a card type flashing pax and then ely on the auto gearbox to move the bus. certainly much easier job to do. but he still has to be mindful of alightting passengers

    there is a bus driver on 196 that drives b9 that closes the door on passengrs who are still alighting.

    drivers master the skill to peer into mirrors to check passenger if he has alighted from the bus or not. the same for outside the bus when driving off from a bus stop he bus driver can also check using a wide angle mirror to see the left nearside of the bus for people . if they r near the bus he has to take care when driving off. this same mirror also can catch people running for the bus. n if so the driver can choose to open door n wait for runner to run to bus.

    whilst driving, driver has to be mindful of the situation on the bus. if a molst case occurs act accordingly n proceed for police station. if soemone suddenly ill or have heart problem n fall on bus. driver must handle the situation.

    one recent case is the ex SBS driver who fell off his seat after suffering a heart problem on bus 156. the driver reacted right but sadly the ex bus driver died on way to hospital.

    in all a bus driver has to mind the passenger's safety . a huge responsibility if u think about it when he drives a bus load of 130 over people if it is superbus.
