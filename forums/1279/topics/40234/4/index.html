    a) Tibs new service 979 to ply from Choa Chu Kang to Serangoon.

    route via CCK Ave 4, CCK Dr, Teck Whye Ave, CCK Rd, Bt Panjang Rd, Jelebu Rd, Petir Rd, Pending Rd, Bt Panjang Rd, BKE, Mandai Rd, Upp Thomson Rd, AMK Ave 1, AMK Ave 3, Yio Chu Kang Rd, Yio Chu Kang Link & Serangoon Ctrl.

    b) Tibs 989, presently plying CCK to Changi Airport, will be amended to become CCK to AMK.

    the route from CCK to Jln Toa Payoh will be unchanged, after that via Lor 6 Toa Payoh (TP), Lor 4 TP, Lor 6 TP, Braddell Rd, Bishan Rd, Bishan St 22, AMK Ave 6, AMK Ave 3 & AMK Ave 8.

    c) Tibs 171, presently plying Yishun to Marina Ctre, will be shortened to become Bt Panjang to Marina Ctre.

    d) Tibs 172, presently plying Boon Lay to CCK, will be extended to become Boon Lay to Yishun.

    the route from Boon Lay to Sungei Tengah Rd will be unchanged, after that via KJE, CCK Way, CCK Ave 4 (MRT), CCK Dr, Teck Whye Ave, CCK Rd, Bt Panjang Rd, then continue with present 171 route to Yishun.

    e) SBST 36, presently plying Changi Airport to Orchard, will be extended to become Changi Airport to Boon Lay.

    the route from Changi Airport to Orchard will be unchanged, after that it will continue with present Exp 502 route to Boon Lay. The extended service, although merge with Exp 502, will charge normal trunk service fare.

    f) SBST 198, currently plying Boon Lay to Bt Merah, will be extended to become Boon Lay to Marina Ctre.

    route from Boon Lay to AYE (Clementi Rd) will be unchanged, after that via Dover Rd, then continue with present 198 route to Jln Bt Merah, after that via Kampong Bahru Rd, Cantonment Rd, Anson Rd, Robinson Rd, Collyer Quay, Esplanade Dr & Raffles Blvd.

    g) SBST 31, currently plying Toa payoh to Tampines, will be shortened to become Toa Payoh to Siglap Rd (loop).

    h) SBST 196, currently plying Bedok to Clementi, will be amended to become Tampines to Bt Merah.

    route from Tampines to Bedok South Rd/Marine Parade will be the same as present 31, after that, the route will be the same as present 196 to Shenton Way, then via Keppel Rd, Telok Blangah Rd & Henderson Rd.

    i) SBST 51, will be split into new Svc 71 & 183.

    Svc 71 will ply from Hougang Ctrl to New Bridge Rd via Upp Serangoon Rd, Hougang Ave 3, then continue with present 51 route to Upp Cross St, then turn into New Bridge Rd.

    Svc 183 will ply from Jurong East to Shenton Way, route from Jurong East to Queenstown MRT is the same as present 51, then continue with present 186 route to Shenton Way.

    j) SBST 186, presently plying St. Michael's to Shenton Way, will be amended to become St. Michael's to Buona Vista via Commonwealth Ave.

    k) SBST 64, currently plying Sims Place to Mei Chin Rd (loop), will be amended to become Sims Place to Bt Merah.

    route via present 64 route to Alexandra Rd, after that via Jln Bt Merah & Bt Merah Ctrl.

    l) SBST 274, presently plying Bt Merah to Depot Rd (loop), will be extended to become Bt Merah to Mei Chin Rd (loop), to cover present 64 route along Stirling Rd, Mei Chin Rd & Mei Ling St.

    m) SBST 63, to be extended from Rumah Tinggi to Bt Merah Int.

    n) SBST 131 to integrate with SBST 275, the new 131 route will now cover Bt Purmei Ave.
