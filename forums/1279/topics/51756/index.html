    I have not taken Express 507 before, but I estimated the journey takes about 30 to 40 minutes, depending on the traffic condition along AYE.

    Express 507 is a peak hours only service. It's one way from Boon Lay to Shenton Way between 6am to 9am and one way from Shenton Way to Boon Lay between 5pm to 7.30pm on weekdays, 12.30pm to 2.30pm on Saturdays. The service does not operate on Sundays and Public Holidays.

    MRT ride from Lakeside station to HarbourFront station (transfer at Outram Park) takes about 27 minutes.
