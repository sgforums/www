    Service 84

    This service existed three times.

    Between Upp Serangoon terminal and World Trade Centre terminal. Amended to Hougang Central interchange in 1994. Merged with service 864 on Sunday 12 Dec 1999.

    On Monday 15 Sep 2003, re-introduced as a peak hours loop service between Punggol station and Punggol East. On Sunday 30 Nov 2003, amended to Punggol temporary interchange.
    Withdrawn on Saturday 12 Mar 2005, under Punggol LRT (East Loop) rationalisation.

    Re-introduced again on Saturday 3 September 2011 plying between Punggol Interchange and Punggol Road End (Loop) replacing part of Service 82 which is amended to ply between Punggol Interchange and Serangoon Central/Boundary Road (loop).
