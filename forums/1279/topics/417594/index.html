    Maybe you should try the walk in interview first before concluding anything. Anyway a face to face talk with a recruiter will be able to help you more as you can directly convince them with your years of skills and experience and your interest to work with them as a BC in SBST.

    An email will be doubtful to their HR since in Singapore, hardly anyone will really believe that an European will come down and work in menial jobs like a bus captain. To any Singapore HR point of view, such email could be unconvincing. 

    I think it is the best you go down and meet them and convince them your interest in applying the job.
    If not, ask them what is the reason behind it, etc, employment laws, wages doesn't meet expectation or whatever reason. In this case, you can roughly know whether you should work in Singapore as a BC, in any company.

  • kengkia
    15 Dec 10, 22:06

    Update:

    Just as i am planning to go down today for walk-in interview @ AMDEP @ 0800, sbs side called me yesterday and ask me to go down today for interview @AMDEP @0800 .

    I had headed down to AMDEP for interview, took my eyesight test, and asked me to take medical test and bring along the medical result slip, bank book,and 6 passport sized photo.

    According to the HR officer who interview me :

    • Working days 6 days per week with one day off, which day be of chosen by company.
    • Working Hours : AM shift early morning while sky still dark(exact time forgotten by time of posting) to 1400/1500. PM shift 1430 to 0130. Split shift work 4 hrs on AM shift and 4hrs on PM shift. Point to take note new BCs cannot choose to do split shift.
    • Basic pay S$1375, got 13th month bonus, got daily attendance allowance of S$2.50, got DD allowance. Basic will be increased year depending on individual performance.
    • There will be 3 months of training(same as army BMT) , after which you will be posted to a bus interchange(i thought it's depot?). No choice of which interchange u wanna work at, you take whatever interchange they give you.
    • At first i thought only lao chiao BC can drive DD until today, DD can be driven by new BCs as well as long there is in need of BCs to do DD svc.
