    Services 185, 300, 302 and 307 were handed over to Tibs in exchange for service 82 on 29 Aug 1999
    Services 67, 172, 175 and 188 were handed over to Tibs in exchange for services 103, 163, 820 and 821 on 26 Dec 1999
