    TIB541M (WLDEP 176) - aircon spoilt... One of the faster O405s with awesome hub reduction!

    TIB598A (KJDEP 61) - 'DOOR CLOSING' chime sounds like a music piece, a bit like TIB511A... 

    TIB788T (KJDEP 106) - all interior ads have been taken off, except the one at the front left (close to the front door)... 

    TIB803H (AMDEP SP) - very fast doors! DOOR CLOSING chime working, but sign is spoilt... 

    TIB810L (KJDEP 188/188R) - super cold aircon! Engine now back to normal, with nice ZF! 

    TIB815Z (WLDEP 911E/169A/169) - middle 'BUS STOPPING' sign spoilt again...

    TIB890E (WLDEP 971E) - rear left aircon filter gave whirring sounds... All doors are like those on the MK 2s and Habits, with a constant pattern of opening and closing...
