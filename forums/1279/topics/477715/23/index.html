  • SMB42P
    24 May 14, 00:31

    Weird spotting for 172AM06 (57Y slot) today

    SMB159L came back to CCK INT at 1150am. BC got off after parking the bus, with EDS showing OFF SERVICE. At 12pm, BC went onto another bus (TIB546A) and was preparing the bus for service. 172AM06 was scheduled to leave int at 1154am but BC only drove out TIB546A to 172 berth at 1207pm. Worse thing is behind bus (SMB58U) got e-pulled to 180 at that time so no bus came back to int after SMB159L to start trip, so all had to wait for 172AM06 to finish changing bus. Next bus, TIB714G on 172AM01 haven even reach int when TIB546A left int. Good thing not much people waiting. However, thats not all...

    The ez link machine was running on 302 route details, duty as 30206. PIS also showing 302 route details. BC didnt realise at first but guessed he finally realised after the bus left Lot One stop. BC just let the meter run as though it's on 302 until it reaches 7.5km and prompts BC to end trip. BC had to "reduce the km" so that the meter will not hit 7.5km. As a result, some pax could not tap out as some had board the bus at, say 6.2km, but when tap out, it was 4.4km. But this also means the remaining pax enjoyed feeder fares rather than trunk fares...

    TIB546A was later spotted in the evening doing 172PM06...
