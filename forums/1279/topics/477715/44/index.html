    SMB1415R (307PM04) almost rear-ended SMB1413X (927). Location was at NorthVale stop (BS 44541). SMB1413X was on the outer lane while SMB1415R was in the inner lane. Think pax at bus stop suddenly wave for 927 so SMB1413X slowed down and swerved from outer lane straight into the bus bay. BC of SMB1415R, which was just behind in the inner lane, had to jam brake to prevent rear-ending SMB1413X. SMB1415R eventually also went into the bus bay.

    At the traffic light, BC of SMB1415R drove next to SMB1413X, stopped, and communicated with BC of SMB1413X, telling him that if it was him, he will not pick of the pax as it was too sudden and may cause danger to the traffic behind.

    After the traffic light turned green, both buses continued on their journey to YewTee and Zoo respectively...
