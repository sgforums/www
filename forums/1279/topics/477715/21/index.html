    SBS3013U (SLBP 97) – Voith gearbox quite obvious when decelerating…

    SBS9512G (CGBP 293) – nice loud 7-Eleven bell! 

    SBS9649X (CGBP 31/31A) – rear axle right wheel has new rim… Looks like a stock aluminum silver rim with black bolts…

    SMB328R (WLBP 75) – ‘BUS STOPPING’ chime and both ‘DOOR CLOSING’ light and chime spoilt… Only the ‘BUS STOPPING’ light is working… 

    SMB1338E (WLBP 852) – whole rear door area shakes very badly over bumps… Front doors 'quiver' when opening... 

    TIB832Z (WLDEP 963) – PIS has white tape blocking the display, but PIS is still working…  Aircon not powerful, only blowing little cooling air… 

    TIB1075K (AMDEP 812) – left rear aircon filter shakes when the bus is idling, creating a jackhammer noise…  

    TIB1235R (KJDEP 176) – one of the nicer Voith MKII Habits!  Cooling aircon, though the exit doors seem to be missing a pole in the middle for both exits…
