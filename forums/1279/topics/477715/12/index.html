    An SMRT bus, service number 190, broke down along the Bukit Timah Expressway (BKE) at the entrance of Bukit Panjang Road Thursday, Feb 6, 2014, morning.

    The breakdown caused a long line of buses behind the broken down vehicle and brought morning rush hour traffic to a standstill.

    According to a report on The Straits Times, commuters could be seen alighting from stationary buses and walking away.

    Stomp contributors Simon, Dion, Vincent and Jeslin sent in pictures of the incident.

    Simon commented on the scene:

    "Traffic has been at a standstill in Bt Panjang going towards BKE .

    "The line of buses is longer than the MRT train."
