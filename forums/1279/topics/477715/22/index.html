    Last weeks edition of 'Coach and Bus Week' included an article about Dave Roger's latest acquisition, the last ever Leyland Olympian to be built, SBS Transit's SBS 9168S.

    Dave is now faced with a bill of almost £3,000 in import duty as according to HM Revenue and Customs the bus does not meet its criteria for being a collectors piece of historic interest. An urgent appeal has been made on the Leyland Society website, just follow the link for further details.

    SBS8169X (BNDEP 36) - one of the KUBs with sturdy doors that don't shake over small bumps... In fact, very little shakes unless there is a violent bump in the road. One of the few (if not only) KUBs to have different screws for the panels at the sides of the rear window inside... Suspect assembled by Soon Chow, very nice 36 perm Euro IV KUB! 

    SBS8262J (BNDEP 36) - nice engine! Had Service 5 quides last Saturday, although it was on 36... Very nice engine sound when the BC does kickdowns! Apart from being my favourite KUB, it is also a very nice 36 perm Euro IV KUB! 

    SBS8309J (HGDEP 156) - one of the KUBs with sturdy doors that don't shake over small bumps... In fact, very little shakes unless there is a violent bump in the road. Very nice engine sound when the BC does kickdowns! One of my favourite KUBs! 

    SMB16R (KJDEP 189) - tape placed over 'DOOR CLOSING' chime, not sure why...

    SMB26L (AMDEP 171) - 'DOOR CLOSING' chime spoilt...

    TIB482A (WLDEP 856) - middle 'BUS STOPPING' sign in red, using regular bulbs rather than LEDs on TIB475X...

    TIB518G (AMDEP 67) - has no hub reduction, like TIB520Z... Front 'BUS STOPPING' sign is the same as TIB589B... Nice aircon!

    TIB609C (KJDEP 970) - nice aircon! One of the few O405s to have speakers onboard, with one removed at the rear door, which has been covered a piece of metal... Nice ex-176 perm O405! 

    TIB825U (KJDEP 970) - nice aircon! Engine sounds like TIB542K, with nice ZF... Very good Batch 3 O405, feels like a Batch 2 O405! 

    TIB854K (KJDEP 188) - one of the few O405Gs with single seats at the front trailer...

    TIB1089X (WLDEP 187) - rear trailer 'BUS STOPPING' sign with tube connected to it... One of the few Habits to have a different deceleration sound, like TIB899G...

    TIB1148J (AMDEP 854) - rear trailer exit door only opens a few seconds after the middle door, causing many passengers to press the bell again many times... This also caused the 'BUS STOPPING' sign to only turn off after the rear door opens... Nice aircon like TIB1235R! 

    TIB1211J (KJDEP 190) - aircon filter panels cleaned, the other panels very grey and dirty...  Another case of poor maintenance of the KJDEP 190 perm Habits...
