    Honestly I don't understand a heck why everyone is making a big fuss over SMRT.

    Over the years I've OBSERVED SMRT is trying to improve alr eg. Fix its freq and service and I'm starting to like SMRT over SBST.

    I honestly think that SMRT have the sincerity to improve and serve the commuters with a smile. Its significantly cleaner buses, better-maintained buses as a whole has convinced me that SMRT do give a damn about how commuters feel during the ride.

    SBST? Dirty, ill-maintained buses with no heart to improve on its innovations. You may argue that they are focused on good svc. You are wrong. SBST svcs arent much better off from SMRT's.

    This year have been a breakthrough for SMRT. I was both elated and disgusted by how SMRT had already embarked on new innovations eg. DDs and new bus makes while SBST hasnt had any significant breakthrough of its own since 2010 and simply refuses to accept the changing nature of bus operation (and ofc the changing nature of its competitor)

    I know this would spark off lots of flamings and debates. Truth is that doesnt bother me. I just wanna share my two cents and that SMRT is outweighing its competitior slowly but steadily. I wish SMRT all the best in the tendering of routes.
