    Bus observation for SV 172 weekdays thus far ever since go BLBP (updated as of 080914):

    1. AP buses the timings more or less the same. Few timings i took still took back the same AP bus as when during KJDEP times...maybe at most 2-3 mins difference due to adjusted runtime. Heard runtime was reduced even further during off peak (saw 39 mins from boon lay to cck during late morning). Peak hour think extended runtime by about 1-2mins...

    2. Split shift buses was chaos. Was unable to observe much since majority are cameos (see my long list of 172 cameos these few days). Only managed to confirm some slots when i board them, or even recognise BCs for bendy slots...
