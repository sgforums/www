    Dear all,

    Just a very gentle reminder to those who is taking the Singapore's public transport to work, my experience with them wasn't all that pleasant and I would like to share it around.

And so I waited, the door was about to open and the ez-link machine is displaying ENTRY/EXIT, the man in front of me was impatient and tapped before the door opened and was fine. The door already opened so ,I went on ahead to tap after him but again I was shocked to see this NEW RIDE coming out. So I talked to the driver about it and he was rude, claimed that it was my fault that I did not waited till the bus door opened. But I did, he kept on trying to held me responsible for the problem and due to a lack of time I just went rushing off to work, going to complain him later. Paid $4.24 coming to work when my normal fare is only $1.80, overcharged by 235% of my normal fare.

But the cold Singapore elitist system will rip me off heartlessly, one of which is this. Ok, a little poor now but still managing, thanks SBS for contributing a little bit to that problem. Because the catch is, you have to wait 1 month and a few more days before you can get your refund.

    Another important point is, out of these 2 times I got double charged, one is on the latest ez-link machine and one is on the old ez-link machine.....upgraded but still continue to charge people like that?


    It is highly illogical by the way, to wait until the front door is fully opened and possibly even longer if the operator forgot to switch the mode, if the BUS is already THAT crowded and people flooding to board the BUS. They are talking about bringing our population to 6.9m and the last thing I want to see is many people paying twice or more of their transport fare travelling to work.

    Kenna this the second time already....
