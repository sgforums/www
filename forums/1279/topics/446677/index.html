    Originally posted by leewan:

    Thanks of the info. Thought it was SBST for a moment. What's the diff between K230 and K270 ? And what's the interior like ? The driver's compartment different for a start.

    As Scania's entire product range is modular to begin with, the only major difference of the chassis between sbst's kub and wt's kub would be that a different engine is installed (if it is even different in the first place), and some minor upgrades to electronics and dashboard as the two vehicles are manufactured 1 year apart.

    The rest of the differences you see here are bodywork differences between sbst's configuration and wt's configuration of the Gemilang bodywork.

    Being modular allows the manufacturer to use the exact same barebone chassis, and include components of choice specified by the bus operator, without a redesign of the product.
