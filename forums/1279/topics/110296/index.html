    SERVICE 224 (Bedok - Chai Chee Rd)

    Bedok Int, Bedok North Ave 1, Bedok North Rd, New Upp Changi Rd, Bedok South Ave 3, Bedok South Rd, (Bedok North Ave 1), Chai Chee St, Rd, New Upp Changi Rd, Bedok South Rd, Bedok South Ave 3, New Upp Changi Rd, Bedok North Rd, Bedok North Ave 1 and Bedok Int.
