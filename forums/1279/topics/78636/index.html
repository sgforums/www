Withdrawal of NR1 NR5 NR7 weekday operations

  • service_238

    With effect from Sunday 23th May 2004, NR1 NR5 & NR7 will no longer operate from Sundays to Thursdays. However, they will still continue to operate on Fridays Saturdays & Eve of Public Holidays. Other operating details of these services remain unchanged.

    Other NR Services (NR2 NR3 NR6 NR will remain unchanged and will still continue to provide their services on Fridays Saturdays & Eve of Public Holidays.

    Any inconvenience caused is regretted.
