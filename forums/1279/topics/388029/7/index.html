    21 injured when SBS bus rams into bus stop at Eunos Link

    Channel NewsAsia - 2 hours 23 minutes ago

    SINGAPORE: An SBS Transit bus service 51 rammed into a bus stop at Eunos Link on Saturday, injuring 21 people.

     

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force said six ambulances were used to sent the victims to hospitals.

    15 were sent to Changi Hospital and the remaining six, who are children, were sent to KK Women’s and Children’s Hospital.

    One of the victims suffered head injuries and was semi—conscious, while the rest were reported to have light injuries.

    In a statement, SBS Transit apologised for the incident and said it was sending teams to the hospitals to ensure the injured are well taken care of.

    SBS Transit is helping the police in their investigations.
