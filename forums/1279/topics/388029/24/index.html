    Third Quarter 2010 Financial Statements

    Group revenue of $184.9m for 3Q10 increased by 6.0% (or $10.6m) from $174.3m in 3Q09 due mainly to higher bus and rail fare revenue and higher advertisement revenue.

    Revenue from Bus Operations for 3Q10 at $140.2m was higher by 2.9% or $3.9m due mainly to the increase in average daily ridership of 5.7% as compared to 3Q09. Operating profit for 3Q10 of $3.0m increased by 91.4% or $1.4m from $1.6m in 3Q09 due mainly to higher bus fare revenue and lower repairs and maintenance costs, partially offset by higher premises costs, higher depreciation expense and higher other operating expenses.

    REVIEW OF GROUP PERFORMANCE

    Operating expenses of $169.1m for 3Q10 increased by 4.1% or $6.7m from $162.4m in 3Q09 due mainly to higher premises costs, higher fuel and electricity costs, higher depreciation expense and higher other operating expenses, partially offset by lower repairs and maintenance costs. The significant increase in premises costs was due mainly to the higher maintenance including security fencing costs and the absence of the rebates from Singapore Government
