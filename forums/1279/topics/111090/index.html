    SBS Transit introduces new Chinatown Direct services

    SBS Transit will introduce 3 new Chinatown Direct services from Friday, 21st January 2005, providing commuters with an express link from Ang Mo Kio, Bedok and Clementi to Chinatown (actually New Bridge Rd Terminal).

    CT8 will ply between Ang Mo Kio Int and New Bridge Rd Terminal via Ang Mo Kio Ave 8, (Ave 1, CTE, Havelock Rd) and Chinatown.

    CT18 will between Bedok Int and New Bridge Rd Terminal via Bedok North Ave 1, North Rd, (PIE, CTE, Havelock Rd) and Chinatown.

    CT28 will between Clementi Int and New Bridge Rd Terminal via Clementi New Town (map not clear), (Clementi Rd, AYE, CTE, Merchant Rd) and Chinatown.

    () denotes non-stop sector

    These Chinatown Direct services can be identified with the dragon logo on the bus stop plates as well as on the desto plates.

    Note that there is no mention that these services will run on a trial basis, so expect to see them after the Chinese New Year festive period.

    Operating Hours:
    Weekdays (Mon to Fri)
    From "ABC": 10am - 5pm
    From New Bridge Rd: 11am - 5pm
    Weekends (Sat, Sun, eve of P.H.s and P.H.s)
    From "ABC": 10am - 10pm
    From New Bridge Rd: 11am - 10pm

    Frequency
    Not stated.

    Fares
    Adult: $1.50 (cash/ez-link card)
    Senior Citizen: $1.00 (cash/ez-link card)
    Child/Student: $1.00 (cash/ez-link card)

    No transfer rebates will be given, nor will concessionary passes be accepted.

    A promotional fare of 88 cents (flat fare) will be charged for the months of January & February 2005.

    LetÂ’s say the depot has 5 buses, A,B,C,D,E. and the depot has 4 duties to fill, 1,2,3,4.

    By default, the perm buses would be A=1, B=2, C=3, D=4 and E is kept as spare bus.
