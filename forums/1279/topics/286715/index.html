    For Little India (I only know this place) > Bedok MRT (> ECP)

    Take either Service 66, 67 or 67W (bi-directional Sunday and PH short trip from NE7 - Little India MRT Station to Tampines Interchange during afternoon peak hours) from Little India to Bedok.

    Alight at:

    - Bedok Interchange if you take Service 66
    - At the bus stop directly outside Bedok MRT Station if you take Service 67/67W

    -

    If you plan to go to ECP, take Service 48 from Little India and alight at the bus stop opposite Laguna Park. Then walk to the underpass and cross the underpass to ECP. You will sure see the expressway (or the beach).

    However, if you are coming in from Bedok,

    a) After you alight from 67/67W, you will notice that there's a sign which says, "Central Stop B". Cross the underpass to the opposite side of the bus stop and take Service 31 to the bus stop outside Laguna Park. Walk to the underpass and cross the underpass to the opposite side of ECP.
    b) After you alight from 66, transfer to Service 197, which is at Berth A5 to A7. Similiar to Service 31, take to Laguna Park and then walk and cross the underpass to the opposite side of ECP.

    Correct me if I'm wrong.

    Let me clarify one thing.

    67 doesn't go Boon Keng MRT station. Upon reaching the 4th Stop at Serangoon Rd in the direction from Little India to Boon Keng, it turns right to Lavender St to proceed to Kallang.

    Let me state the ones with direct routes first.
    Farrer Park/Little India to Bedok MRT -> 66 or 67. 67 is faster to go to Bedok than 66.

    Boon Keng to Bedok MRT -> 66

    Little India to ECP -> 48

    Boon Keng to ECP -> 31. Take 31 at Bendemeer Rd and not at Serangoon Rd

    Farrer Pk to ECP -> Take 67 to a stop before Kallang MRT station, which is at the part where 67 turns left to Kallang Rd from Lavender St. Take 197 to ECP.
