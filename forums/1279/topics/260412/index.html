    Originally posted by the Bear:

    There is a difference between financial year and calendar year. Most companies have their financial year mirroring calendar year (i.e. FY2006 is for period Jan 2006 to Dec 2006). However, SMRT financial year starts from April. So it's FY2007 is actually from April 2006 to March 2007.

    As of now, they are in their FY2008 (April 2007 to March 2008 ).
