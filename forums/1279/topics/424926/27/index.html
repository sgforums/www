    Originally posted by TIB783G:

    What models were CSS220T-CSS309A before they were de-registered?

    CSS230S-CSS266P were Hino HT238, registered in 1983 and pharsed out by 1996.

    CSS267L-CSS268J were NAC Nissan Diesel U31SCNs, registered in 1985 and de-registered in 1997.

    CSS269G was a NAC Hino HT238K, registered in 1986 and de-registered in 2001.

    CSS270B-CSS289Z were Hispano DAF SB220LTs formerly registered as TIB405Z-TIB424T, re-registered in 1996/1997 and pharsed out by 2005.

    CSS290T-CSS300Z (except CSS294H) were AC Hino HT238Ks formerly registered as TIB380K-TIB389L, re-registered in 1996/1997 and de-registered in 2003.

    CSS294H was an AC Nissan Diesel U31SCN formerly registered as TIB390G, re-registered in 1997 and de-registered in 2004.

    CSS301X-CSS309A were NAC Hino HT238Ks formerly registered as TIB353P-TIB361R, re-registered in 1997 and pharsed out by 2003.
