    Originally posted by SBS7440T:

    Then what is old 207's route?

    UBC 207's route details (after re-organisation on 11 April 1971):

    Bukit Timah Rd 7m (UBC Depot) - Toh Tuck Rd End (loop)

    Bukit Timah Rd, Upp Bukit Timah Rd, Jln Jurong Kechil, Toh Tuck Rd (End), Jln Jurong Kechil, Upp Bukit Timah Rd, Bukit Timah Rd.

    This service was merged with service 201 during the 1974 bus rationalisation.
