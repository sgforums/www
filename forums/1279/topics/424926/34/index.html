    Route [BG86] - Bedok - Bedok Sth Ave 1 > Bdk Int, Bdk Nth St 1, Chai Chee St, Chai Chee Rd, New Upp Changi Rd, Bdk Sth Rd, Bdk South Ave 1, New Upp Changi Rd, Chai Chee Rd, Chai Chee St, Bdk Nth St 1 & Bdk Int.

    No route change since but was renumbered as svc 229 in 1998.

     

    Service 220                                                                                                                      First feeder bus of Chai Chee/Bedok, introduced in ard 1977.

    Route [BG77] - Chai Chee - Bedok New Town > Chai Chee Ter, New Upper Changi Rd, Bdk Plain (Sth Rd), Bedok View (Sth Ave 3), New Upp Changi Rd, Bedok Walk (Sth Ave 2), Bedok Plain (Sth Rd), New Upp Changi Rd & Chai Chee Ter.

    Route [BG79-80, 80-81] Kg Chai Chee - Bedok South Ave 3

    Route [BG82, 84, 85] Chai Chee - Bedok South Ave 3

    Route [BG86] - Bedok - Bedok South Ave 3 > Bdk Int, Bdk Sth Rd, Bdk Sth Ave 3, New Upp Changi Rd, Bedok Sth Ave 2, Bdk Sth Rd & Bdk Int.

    No route change till its withdrawal in ?? (replaced by svc 38).
