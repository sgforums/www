    At Jurong Bus Interchange, during its 12 years of operation, its concourse was upgraded in 1987, with a canteen located upstairs and opened to the general public. A self-refilling point was also installed at Jurong Bus Interchange.

    Service 192 was introduced from Jurong Interchange to South Canal terminal but was subsequently withdrawn.

    In Jan 1988, there were feedbacks about the Jurong Bus Interchange that is located out of the way, when Boon Lay / Jurong West housing estates were partially developed and furthermore, there would be an MRT station constructed nearby (which is the present Boon Lay MRT Station). The feedbacks draws out positively and proves to be true and Jurong Bus Interchange would move out on 30th June 1990.

    With the Jurong West New Town development was ongoing, those relying on feeders will have to travel all the way down to Jurong Bus Interchange to transfer to other bus service or utilise the nearest bus stops for a faster destination. A feasible & convenient bus interchange is called on, during its development stage in the late 1980s, when the MRT was also under construction back then.

    192 started off as Tuas Ter (Pioneer Rd Extension) to Boon Lay Way (Lakeside MRT Stn), when Service 175 was shortened as Bt Batok to Tuas Rd.

    The changes reflect between 1st & 7th Jul 1990 respectively.

    On 1st Jul 1990 (opening of Boon Lay Int), the service was amended to Boon Lay Int to Boon Lay Way

    On 7th Jul 1990 (opening of Boon Lay MRT), the service was extended to cover Tuas Industrial Estate, to cover the former Service 175 route. Service 175 route was shortened to loop at Jurong West St 91.

    When there were new bus stops erected at Tuas Industrial Estate to provide bus service coverage, Service 192 was amended to loop at Tuas Ave 1. It was amended to Jurong West St 64 & Jurong West Ave 4 and was swapped route with Service 193 when Tuas Terminal opens in Jan 1998.

    Service 192 was also amended to cover the former Service 250 route on 11th Feb 2001 (JIS Rationalisation).
