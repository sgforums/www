    13 Dec 11, 21:32

    To answer this question over Sv merger and cancellation

    There was 2 main reason for rt merger and cancellation, in fact 3 even..

    1) CBD Rts: They wanted to reduce the no of buses inside the CBD and even the roadside terminals aft the MRT commenced.

    This clause saw Svs like 20 removed, M2 was introduced w/ the MRT > feeder concept.

    2) Buses became bigger: Bigger buses meant more capacity, I guess some Svs were removed. Some examples was CSS 6 that duplicated with 65.

    3) MRT: Some long distance routes were simply shortened as there's no point in creating long dist rts... Sv 2 was shortened to just ply CBD - Changi Village instead of plying all the way to Buona Vista.
