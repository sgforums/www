    Originally posted by Gus.chong:

    When was the year where SBS5300C - SBS5399T were manufactured and de-registered and what models are they?


    SBS5300C to SBS5324J were Mercedes-Benz OF1413, bodied by Soon Chow, registered between 1971 to 1975 and all pharsed out by 1987.

    SBS5325G to SBS5344B were Leyland Atlanteans with mix of BAco and Duple Metsec bodies, registered in 1977 and pharsed out by 1989.

    SBS5345Z to SBS5360D were Mercedes-Benz OF1413, bodied by Soon Chow, registered between 1971 to 1975 and all pharsed out by 1987.

    SBS5361B to SBS5390S were Leyland Atlanteans with mix of Alexander L and Duple Metsec bodies, registered in 1980 and all pharsed out by 1994.

    SBS5391P to SBS5394G were Mercedes-Benz OF1417 with Hawke bodies, registered in 1980 and all pharsed out by 1994.

    SBS5395D was a Mercedes-Benz OF1417 with NZMB body, registered in 1980 and pharsed out in 1994.

    SBS5396B was a Leyland Olympian ONTL11/2R, registered in 1981, returned to Leyland in 1982/1983.

    SBS5397Z to SBS5399T were Mercedes Benz OF1417 with Hawke bodies, registered in 1980 and all phrased out by 1994.
