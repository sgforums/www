    19 Jun 11, 17:11
    Originally posted by SBS7440T:

    When 980 was under WLDEP control in 2001, how many U31RCNs and L113CRL Striders did it receive?


    Service 980 had many movements between AMDEP and WLDEP for the past 12 years:-

    Late 1998: Partial WLDEP controlled with majority Nissan UDs in its fleet. 368Y 370P 372J were among the perms. That time the perm Scanias were still under AMDEP.

    July 1999: 980 became full AMDEP controlled and perm Lances 907S 908P 909L 926L were brought in.

    Jan 2001: Partial WLDEP again (5 full + 1 split to WLDEP), this time round Scanias were deployed. 565U 658K 665P 666L 672T 835R were deployed. 673R eventually replaced 835R.

    Sept 2004: Back to full AMDEP again, after which became minority WLDEP controlled. 916R 917M 1257C perm under AMDEP side whereas 658K 665P 673R remained under WLDEP side (WLDEP controlled only 3 full days).

    Mar 2005: 3 duties (2 full + 1 split) from AMDEP to WLDEP. 1254K 1255H 1256E from AMDEP SP moved to WLDEP 980, 916R 917M 1257C out. Return of the Nissan UDs again...

    June 2005: 2 full duties from WLDEP back to AMDEP. 1255H 1256E from WLDEP 980 moved to AMDEP SP. 929D and 1257C perm from AMDEP side. 1254K remained as perm split under WLDEP.

    June 2006: (CSS) 191X 192T regular cameo on 980 under WLDEP side replacing the 3 Scanias.

    Oct 2006: 658K 665P 673R back to 980. 1254K replaced with (CSS) 183U for split.

    May 2009: 2 more full duties to AMDEP, 934M and 939A perm under AMDEP side. WLDEP left with 1 full and 1 split. 658K became perm split, 183U and 673R out.

    Nov 2010: 4 full duties from AMDEP back to WLDEP. 661A 664S 666L 667J perm WLDEP side. 929D 934M 939A 1257C out.

    2011: 907S became perm RWS8, replaced by 1257C. 970H to WLDEP, replaced by 934M.
