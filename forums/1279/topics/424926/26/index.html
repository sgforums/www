    63 was never under HGDEP controlled until the merger with 382 took place in 2002. I believe MK3 and MK4 from ARBP were deployed those days.

    Anyway the history of service 63's depot controlled since its introduction in 1992:-

    • 1992 to 2002 - Full ARBP
    • 2002 to 2004 - Majority HGDEP, minority ARBP (merged with 382 and extended to Eunos)
    • 2004 to 2009 - Majority BRBP, minority ARBP (Eunos routes came under BRBP)
    • 2009 onwards - Full BRBP
