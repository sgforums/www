    Originally posted by Gus.chong:

    When is the year SBS4225T - SBS4424K were manufactured & de-registered and what models are they?

    SBS4287P to SBS4293X were Nissan RX102 with Fuji Heavy Industries Bodies, registered in 1967/1969 and pharsed out in the 1980s.


    SBS4294T, SBS4296M to SBS4298H were Nissan 4R94 with Fuji Heavy Industries Bodies, registered in 1969 and pharsed out in the 1980s.

    SBS4299E was a Nissan 4R94 too but with Singapore Nissan Bodies, registered in 1970 and pharsed out in the 1980s.

    All were ex-STC buses.

    SBS4225T, SBS4254J to SBS4257B were Mercedes Benz OF1113 with Soon Chow bodies, registered in 1971 abd pharsed out in the 1980s.

    SBS4258Z, SBS4259X, SBS4269S to SBS4279M, SBS4334L, SBS4423M to SBS4424K were all Mercedes Benz LP1113 with Soon Chow bodies, registered between 1969 to 1971, and all pharsed out in the 1980s.

    SBS4246H to SBS4253L were Bedford SB, believed to be bodied by Soon Chow, registered in the late 1960s to early 1970s and all pharsed out in the 1980s.

    SBS4240Z to SBS4245K were Albion Viking EVK55AL, bodied by Soon Chow or Strachan, registered in 1971/1972 and all pharsed out by the 1980s.

    SBS4226R to SBS4239D, SBS4300J to SBS4422R were all Albion Viking EVK41UL, bodied by Soon Chow and Strachan, registered in 1973/1974 and pharsed out by the 1980s.

    SBS4261M to SBS4268U, SBS4280H to SBS4383U were Albion Viking EVK41L, bodied by Soon Chow, registered in 1971 and pharsed out by the 1980s.

    SBS4320B to SBS4328E, SBS4335J to SBS4357X, SBS4361H to SBS4369L were Mercedes Benz OF1413, bodied by Supreme Star and Soon Chow, registered between 1971 to 1975 and pharsed out by the 1980s.

    The rest have no records.

    SWT 97A was withdrawn on 26 Apr 2010 after 97 was extended to Marina Centre.

    Note that some short trip service will be withdrawn without prior notice when its parent service had been amended as a route that's a bit different from the short trip service as the short trip service is redundant.
