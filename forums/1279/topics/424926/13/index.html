    350: Loop at Pasir Ris Dr 4 / Dr 3 via Dr 3, Close, Dr 6, Dr 1, Dr 4, Dr 3, Close and Dr 3. Amended later to ply St 41 instead of Dr 3.

    352: Loop at Pasir Ris Dr 2 / St 21 / Loyang Ave via Dr 3, Dr 4, St 21, Dr 2, Dr 1, Loyang Ave, Dr 3, Dr 2, St 21, Dr 4 and Dr 3.

    353: Loop at Pasir Ris Dr 10 via Dr 3, Central, Dr 1, Elias Rd, Dr 3, Dr 10, Dr 1, Central and Dr 3. Amended later to ply Dr 3 instead of Dr 1.

    355: Loop at Pasir Ris Dr 10 / St 71 via Dr 3, Dr 12, Dr 1, Dr 10, St 71, Dr 1, Dr 12 and Dr 3.

    356: Loop at Pasir Ris Dr 10 / St 71 via Dr 3, Central, Dr 1, Elias Rd, St 71, Dr 10, Dr 1, St 71, Elias Rd, Dr 1, Central and Dr 3.

    From 1997, Townlink Pasir Ris services were introduced:

    352 merged with 356 to form Townlink 357, lated merged with service 294 to form service 3.

    350 merged with 353 to form Townlink 358. 354 was introduced.

    351 merged with 355 to form Townlink 359, later amended to ply St 51.

    When is the year where SBS1530J-SBS1636K were manufactured and de-registered and what models are they?


    SBS1530J-SBS1616 were Mercedes Benz OF1417s with Hawke bodies, registered in 1980 to 1982 and deregistered in 1993/94.

    SBS1617R was Mercedes Benz OF1417 demo with Supreme Star body, registered in 1982 and believed to be deregistered in the early 90s.

    SBS1623Y-SBS1636K were Mercedes Benz OF1417s with NZMB bodies, registered in 1983 and deregistered in 1994/95.

    SBS1618M-SBS1622C were Leyland Atlanteans with Walter Alexander R type bodies, registered in 1983 and all deregistered by 2001.
