    I want to point out something about the history of Dec 1990 bus rationalisation.

    Someone might be confused about 13's history, when it become a Eunos - Yio Chu Kang service from a Bedok - Ghim Moh service. However, I shall make it clear.

    13 was first shortened to Eunos (not sure when is it), making it Eunos - Ghim Moh service. In Dec 1990, it was then shortened to Yio Chu Kang from Ghim Moh. On the same day it was made FAC.

    On the same day, 20, which also plied between Bedok and Ghim Moh, was withdrawn. With the withdrawal of service 20, service 100 and 111 were amended to Ghim Moh from WTC and Buona Vista respectively.

    With the amendment of service 111, service 191, which plied between Ghim Moh and Portsdown Rd, was amended to Buona Vista Terminal.
