    Originally posted by Gus.chong:

    Ah...the SS services. I still remember that I take SS7 before to Choa Chu Kang when I was young.

    SS services were introduced as temporary services between new satellite towns when the MRT was under construction, otherwise when the MRT route haven't been extended yet. 

    Let's look at the list of the SS services below.

    SS1 - Yio Chu Kang to Yishun Ave 9/Ring Rd (loop)
    (Introduced on 13th Dec 1987, withdrawn on 20th Dec 1988)

    SS2 - Clementi to Jurong East St 32 (loop)
    (Introduced on 13th Mar 1988, withdrawn on 6th Nov 1988)

    SS3 - Clementi to Bukit Batok West Ave 7 (loop)
    (Introduced on 13th Mar 1988, shortened to Jurong East on 6th Nov 1988 and withdrawn on 10th Mar 1990) 

    SS4 - Tampines to New Upp Changi Rd (loop)
    SS5 - Tampines to New Upp Changi Rd (loop)
    SS6 - Simei Rd to New Upp Changi Rd (loop)
    (Introduced on 5th Nov 1989, withdrawn on 16th Dec 1989)

    SS7 - Woodlands to Choa Chu Kang
    (Introduced on 11th Mar 1990, amended to the new Woodlands Regional Bus Interchange on 4th Feb 1996 and withdrawn on 10th Mar 1996)
