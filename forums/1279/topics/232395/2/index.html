    Honestly speaking, everything depends on human relations. We see SMRT and SBST staff greeting and chatting with each other because some of them may have already known each other long time ago. (For your information, there are former BCs from SBST in SMRTB and former SLs from SMRTB inside SBST)

    Why most bus drivers from both sides dont greet each other is because well.. When you meet with strangers, wont you approach with caution even though you may want to be friendly with them? The same applies. And dont assume that they are from the same bus companies, they will be friendly with each other. Some bus drivers even though they are from the same companies dont greet each other because they may not feel pleasant being with another colleague, sometimes because of off-the-job reasons.

    It all depends on human relations. All and all.

    And observe. There are different gestures that bus drivers use to communicate with each other. Not just saying hi
