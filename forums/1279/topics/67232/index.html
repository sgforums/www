    Originally posted by Ah Ma:
    from parkway parade , what bus can go to aljunied MRt????

    Thanks !
    You have 2 options.

    Walk + Bus
    Walk to East Coast Road (have to cross Marine Parade Road), busstop outside Roxy Square. Take bus 40 direct to Aljunied MRT.

    Bus + Bus
    Take bus 31, 76, 135, 197 and alight anywhere along Tanjong Katong Road. Take bus 40 from the bus stop which you alighted.

    Cheers and warm regards
