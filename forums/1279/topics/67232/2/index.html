    Originally posted by Ah Ma:
    the bus 40 frm roxy sq is near to roxy sq or mus cross the road one?
    Outside Roxy Square 1, no need to cross the road.

    You have to use the overhead bridge near block 87 Marine Parade Central (near the bus stop), enter into Level 3 of Roxy square 2, walk to the back, open the exit door and enter into Roxy Square 1 Level 2, walk to the end, out of the door and you can see escalators and stairs, get to Level 1 and you will see the bus stop to your left when you face the road.

    Hope it won't confuse you, or you can use your way.

    I personally do not recommend 40 as the frequency is a killer.

    From parkway parade

    Option 1
    76 135 to Paya Lebar MRT
    155 to Geylang East Central - Alternatively u can walk to Geylang East Central to take 80 instead of waiting for 155
    80 to Aljunied MRT.
    Alighting Place: Aljunied Rd - underneath the MRT viaduct.

    Option 2
    31 to Kallang MRT
    Cross the road and take 26
    Alighting Place: Sims Ave - outside the HK Tim Sim coffee shop.

    Option 3
    135 to Macpherson Estate (Macpherson ITE)
    40 63 158 to Aljunied MRT
    Alighting Place: Aljunied Rd - underneath the MRT viaduct.

    Although none of them is the shortest route, i hope one of them can provide you with the fastest route there.
