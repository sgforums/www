    The format for this time round is very interesting. Putting it exam terms, it is no longer "Answer all questions in linear" format, but more like "Choose any questions. The more you attempt, the higher your score". This means that teams can go for whatever they are more confident with.

    Some of the more higher-point questions :

    * Take a photograph of your team at Kupang LRT Station

    * Take a photograph of Old School (Clue : CBD Area) (I heard nobody got this one right. I attempted this one, no it isn't SAM)

    * Take a photograph of Coolair Trident at Geylang Lor 1 (i.e. SBS9680D)

    * Take a photograph of your team at Mt Faber.

    For 25 points..

    • Take a group shot at Kupang LRT Station

    For 20 points..

    • Take photographs of 30 cameos

    • Take a photo of a bus at Labrador Park
    • Take a photo of a Coolair Trident at Geylang Lor 1
    • Take a group photo shot at Mount Faber
    • Take a photo of Old School

    For 15 points

    • Take a photo of a shuttle bus at Turf City
    • Take photos of 5 buses featuring different female models on advertisements
    • Take a group photo at Holland Drive Terminal where only CSS602 used to terminate
    • Take a group photo shot at Marina South Pier
    • Collect a ticket of service 35 at Fare Stage 12.0
    • Take photograph with a landmark named "Bedok Reservoir - Punggol"

    For 10 points

    • Take a group photo at Tg Rhu Bridge
    • Take a photo of yourself with your lunch
    • Record your rides from start to end
    • Take photographs of 20 cameos
    • Take 5 or 10 photographs of buses with advertisements that are related to tourism/beverages/handphones/Panasonic/Cameras/Food/Government Campaign Messages.

    The rest are 2, 4 or 6 points.
