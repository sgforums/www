    Svc 10 (Weekday):

    BBDEP:

    1A/7P = SBS6050S

    2A/8P = SBS6351Z

    3A/9P = SBS3041M - ends PM at Tampines

    4A/10P = SBS5006C - starts AM at Tampines, ends AM/PM at Kent Ridge ( along with SBS7658H)

    5A/11P = SBS7658H 

    6A/12P = SBS3087G

    15S = Random DD

    CGBP:

    18A/29P = SBS7680S - downroutes from Mountbatten Road to Tampines Int during AM peak. Ends AM/PM at Tampines Int

    19A/30P = SBS8580R

    20A/31P = SBS8577B or SBS8682E

    21A/32P = SBS8577B or SBS8682E

    22A/33P = SBS9667T - starts AM and end PM at Kent Ridge

    23A/34P = SBS8586A

    24A/35P = SBS7671T = Last bus to Tampines Int

    25A/36P = SBS3607G

    26A/37P = SBS8621G - may have changed slot since 13 Oct...

    27A/38P = SBS7661Y

    28A/39P = SBS7660A

    Notes:

    - 1 overnight A/P bus on CGBP side.

    - SBS3088D is between SBS7661Y and SBS7660A during PM peak.

    Splits:

    BBDEP: SBS3040R, SBS3088D, SBS3816U and two unknown buses

    CGBP (all 10e except BSEP): SBS6429J (BSEP), SBS9661J, SBS9666X, SBS9809B
