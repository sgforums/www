    Dear SgForums

     

    Did you guys know that there is a free shuttle bus provide by Jurong Point Shopping Mall to Choa Chu Kang MRT Station & Jurong East MRT Station.

     

    The bus is in white in colour. Please takes note of the timing.

     

    Departs Choa Chu Kang MRT Station(bus stop outside Lot 1)

     

    10:15 AM, 11:15 AM, 12:15 PM, 1:15 PM, 2:15 PM, 3:15 PM, 4:15 PM, 5:15 PM, 7:15 PM, 8:15 PM.

     

    Departs Jurong Point to Choa Chu Kang(Bus Stop beside the bus int)

     

    11:00 AM, 12:00 PM. 1:00 PM, 2:00 PM, 3:00 PM, 4:00 PM, 5:00 PM, 6:45 PM, 8:00 PM, 9:00 PM.

     

    Departs Jurong East MRT Station(behind Popular Bookstore)

     

    10:45 AM, 11:45 AM, 12:45 PM, 1:45 PM, 2:45 PM, 3:45 PM, 4:45 PM, 6:15 PM, 7:45 PM, 8:45 PM

     

    Departs Jurong Point to Jurong East MRT Station(behind Popular Bookstore)

     

    10:30 AM, 11:30 AM, 12:30 PM, 1:30 PM, 2:30 PM, 3:30 PM, 4:30 PM, 5:45 PM, 7:30 PM, 8:30 PM

     

     

    DO NOT GET CONFUSE WITH THE IMM FREE SHUTTLE BUS. IMM FREE SHUTTLE BUS NO LONGER GOES TO BOON LAY AFTER 2 JANUARY 2009.
