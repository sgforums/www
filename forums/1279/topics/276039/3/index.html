    There was actually some changes.

    The original 133 was AMK area > CTE > Braddell Rd >> Upp Serangoon Rd

    When Serangoon was built..
    There was this 133A that ran alongside with 133
    AMK Area > Lor Chuan > Serangoon Ave 2 > Upp Serangoon Rd

    After sometime, they found out that 133A got more demand and thus, they admended 133 and they use 133A route instead.

    In the past, 133 follows 61 route to enter North Bridge Rd. Victoria St was only a 1 directional road back then. Change over to Victoria St at around 1994/5.

    Enter Suntec in the year 1996 and became a loop service ever since.
