    Okay, the answers.

    Many could get questions 1, 3 and 5 correct.

    5. SBS 8059E

    Okay, now I shall tell you all why.

    2. Many people thought that it was the recent SBS 8074K being the first KUB accident. But no, they have apparently left out another accident. It's actually SBS 8049J.

    4. It can be pretty confusing memorising cameos even after the previous day. However, there are some significant ones. The first one I remember is a KUB "loan" from Service 64 - SBS 8066J on Service 100.
