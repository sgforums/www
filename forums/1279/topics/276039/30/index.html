    Originally posted by sBs_boy:


    Jurong Depot is aka Teban Gdn Depot.

    The one located at Penjuru is Jurong Bus Park.

    Jurong Depot was aka Teban Gardens Terminal and it was located at Penjuru Rd. This was where 143 used to end. Jurong Bus Park was located at Jurong Port Rd, right next to Jurong Interchange. Both the interchange and the bus park actually occupied the area between Jln Ahmad Ibrahim and the railway tracks. Jurong Port Rd was the entrance.
