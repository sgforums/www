    29 May 08, 22:35

    A B7RLE Mk I has fuel tank below the rear portion of the bus.
    Regos: RU3718U to RU3722G, PA4136X, PA4139M, PA4144Y

    A Mk II has fuel tank above the front wheel arch of the bus
    Regos: PA5278M, PA5279K, PA5286P, RU4127T (with new dash)

    A Mk III has a similar configuration as the Mk II for fuel tank, with some minor adjustments to the chassis, and comes with Euro 4 and 5 (with Adblue) engines, and a new dash (as with newer Mk IIs).
    Regos: RU4290D, SBS8030L.

    A B7RLE indicates that it is a Bus with an engine of approximately 7 liters which is placed at the Rear/Back of the bus and is a Low Entry bus.

    And in any case, try and avoid the Mk Is (until someone does something to them), they have the strongest vibrations of any bus (don't think a Scania or DAF can rival it)
