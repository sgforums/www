    Question: For each set of three, give one similarity and one difference in the following, then explain why you think it is so. [3 points per part question]

    a) SBS 0594D SBS 0635U SBS 2745Y
    b) SBS 7457X SBS 9052S SBS 9840J
    c) SBS 7302J SBS 9166Y SBS 9412L
    d) SBS 0346D SBS 0928A SBS 2713P
    e) TIB 0502B TIB 0676B TIB 1255H
    f) SBS 3659G SBS 3661Z SBS 3664R
    g) SBS 0461D SBS 0499X SBS 3816U
    h) SBS 0374Y SBS 3734Y SBS 7234Y
    i) SBS 2820M SBS 2835X SBS 3734Y
    j) SBS 9120D SBS 9149Y SBS 9165A
    Grading:
    The explanation part is in your answer, so if you get BOTH similarity and difference correct with some elaboration, 3 points are yours to take.

    Answers (More than one answer is accepted):
    a)
    Similarity: All have Pizza Hut ads.
    Difference: SBS 2745Y is a MKIV, SBS 0594D and SBS 0635U are MKIIIs.
    b)
    Similarity: All have been on Premium Services before.
    Difference: SBS 7457X does not always do Premium Services, the other two are perm on Premium Services.
    c)
    Similarity: All buses use to have a "Hua Yu Cool!" (Hua Yu means Chinese/Mandarin) ad.
    Difference: SBS 9166Y have the IRIS NextBus ad, the other two have the "Read Singapore" ad.
    d)
    Similarity: All buses have had a Mustafa ad.
    Difference: SBS 0346D have the red colour version of the ad, the other two had green.
    e)
    Similarity: All buses have the Marigold HL ad (Pink).
    Difference: TIB 0676H is based in KJDEP, the others are WLDEP buses.
    f)
    Similarity: All are Townlink 293 buses and/or had the Sleekangel ads before.
    Difference: SBS 3659G's rego plate is new, the others haven't gotten the new rego plate.
    g)
    Similarity: All buses are currently spares in BRBP.
    Difference: SBS 3816U is former 88 BRBP, the other two are former 28 BRBP.
    h)
    Similarity: The numbers, 3, 7 and 4 are used in the regos.
    Difference: SBS 0374Y is a BRBP bus, the other two are SLBP buses.
    i)
    Similarity: All buses have the 'L'occitane Beauty Wellness' ad.
    Difference: SBS 3734Y is a Merc, the other two are MKIVs.
    j)
    Similarity: All buses used to be ARBP buses.
    Difference: SBS 9120D is in BRBP now, the other two went to SLBP.
