    Travel Trivia

    This would be useful for those who travel often as well as those who dont.

    1. From Boon Lay Interchange to King Albert Park, is 154 157 or 174 faster? If you miss 174, which service would you take and why? Time range is just before PM Peak.

    2. How much time will you save if you take the following route(s) from Bedok Int/MRT to Toa Payoh Swim Pool during PM Peak?
    i) 26 direct
    ii) 28 direct
    iii) 60/87 to Kaki Bukit Prison Ctr change 59 
    iv) 25/854 to Jln Eunos change 59
    Determine the order of which route is the fastest to the slowest, and the time difference between each option. How much time will you incur if you chose S5 (change bus at Trellis Towers) over S59 for (iii) and (iv) ?

    3. At 0650h, if you miss 985 at Trellis Towers, would taking 151 or 154 to the King Albert Park area be faster?  How about at 0830hrs? How many minutes faster for each case?

    4. You are at Old Airport Rd FC bus stop and you are taking a bus to HarbourFront. If you miss S30 during AM Peak, is it faster to take S10? How about during off peak hours?

    5. How much time will you save if you choose 36 over 196 at the Parkway Parade bus stop and alighting at One Raffles Link/Esplanade during the weekend? How about during weekday off peak?

    6. At 1700hrs a S105 and a S106 (in this order) approach the SP bus stop. In what circumstances would the S105 be the front bus when it arrives at Gleneagles Hospital?

    All schedules are based on Weekday Peak unless otherwise stated.

    Have fun
