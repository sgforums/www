    Originally posted by chickenlittle2:

    Does the mechanic from another depot help to replace minor problem of buses from different depots?

    Example:

    1) This bus had some problem when it is in Boon Lay interchange

    SBS 214B (BR 157) <-- Door problem

    2) This bus had some problem when it is in Toa Payoh Interchange

    SBS 339A (SL 157) <-- Wiper not working

     

    When I was still around, I told the RGT to help out the faults that they can rectify regardless which depot are they from. Because ultimately if my guys don't help out, the route group is going to suffer with a lack of bus. And then if the Route Group suffers, the commuters suffer because lesser bus to take.

     

    I also advocated the release of expensive items to be kept by RGT to be used to repair buses which are in need. Eg. Signal indicators. And if in the case when these expensive items are given to the other depot buses, we will record down and the manager decide whether to claim from the depot during the productivity meeting.

     

    Some door problems can be rectified on the spot. Like the door cannot close. Many times it's due to the door button at the driver side. Or the door ECU. The Door system of SBS Transit is under DEANS. Or sometimes it's due to door pump leaking.

     

    Wiper not working could highly be due to corroded wiper motor splines. I gave spring washers to the RGT to temporarily tighten the wiper blade to the splines. But if the wiper motor is not working and it's raining plus the driver is not confident to drive in the rain the bus will have to return.

     

    However I know that some RGT from other depots do not practice what I did. They simply just send the bus back to SL because it's not their bus. But it's an integrity issue. I'd rather help out when my guys can help out since everyone is under SBS Transit. And the commuters' interests should be highest priority.

     

    Now that I have left, I do not know how my successor has planned things out. But likely things should remain the same.
