    In my opinion, I think LTA should create the following kinds of bus route:

    1. bus routes that provide alternative routes (eg. via connecting to another MRT station) to heavily utilised sections of Public Transport.

    3. bus routes that substitute the MRT by providing a connection that is faster than what MRT is providing (eg. Pioneer to Bukit Panjang, via expressway).

    5. bus routes that complement the MRT and feeder bus routes to divert traffic (eg. combine feeder routes of different towns together, connect via expressway, and make it a new trunk route) from the MRT and feeder bus routes.

    7. express bus routes that behave like MRT and only stop once every two/three kilometres (best is to only stop one or two times in a town that it serves), charging fares equivalent to MRT.
