    After you are done with the routing, these are the steps to enable us to view the plan...

     

    From Google Map:

    1. On top right of the map, click on "Share" button and the "Sharing Settings" will appear.

    2. Under "Who has access", click on "change" to change the setting to: 
    "Anyone with the link"... Then save.

    3. Click on "Done" when you have returned to "Sharing Settings".

     

    On this page:

    1. Copy & paste the planned map address here.

    2. You'll see "Insert/Edit link" and "Unlink" icons lighted up beside the Image, B, I, U icons above... Click on "Insert/Edit link" icon...

    3. A screen will appear, and just paste the same address into the "Link URL" box, click "Insert" and you are done...
