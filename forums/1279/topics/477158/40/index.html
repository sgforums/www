    Toa Payoh Int [Toa Payoh MRT]

    Bartley Rd (non-stop)

    Bartley Rd East (non-stop, uses Bartley viaduct)

    Tampines Ave 10

    TPE (non-stop)

    Pasir Ris Dr 8 (new bus stops)

    Pasir Ris Dr 1

    Pasir Ris Ctrl [Pasir Ris MRT]

    Pasir Ris Dr 3

    Loyang Ave

    Cranwell Rd

    Netheravon Rd

    Changi Village Ter

    *map: http://goo.gl/maps/h1ZAc

     

    -Offers a fast and direct link between Toa Payoh and Tampines/Pasir Ris/Loyang, via Braddell Rd and Bartley Rd (East). I suggested a similar route previously but it was longer, from Changi Village to Toa Payoh to Jurong West/Tuas.

    -Provides an alternative bus svc to Lor 2 Toa Payoh, connecting it to Lor 6 and Braddell Rd for more bus transfer options

    -Provides a faster connection between Tampines Ave 10 (industrial areas and Tampines retail park) and Pasir Ris MRT/ town centre, via TPE

    -Connects upcoming flats/condos along Pasir Ris Dr 8 to Pasir Ris MRT/ White Sands shopping mall

    -Provides an alternative bus svc to Netheravon Rd, connecting it to Pasir Ris MRT (nearer than Tampines MRT via svc 29)
