    The current public bus network at Jurong West Extension (Gek Poh & Pioneer) is good. However, there is clearly room for improvement. Existing routes are experiencing constant increase in demand, even during non-peak hours. Some bus connections can be shortened.

    I would like to propose a re-route to existing routes 192, 242, 243G and 243W. The strategy used here is to reduce the coverage of 242 and 243. A new route will cover the deleted sectors while enhance bus connection. This new route may bring relief to routes 243, 242 and 241. For easy reference, I shall number it 244.

    1. 192
    2. re-routed to connect to Pioneer MRT Station, and
    3. re-routed to ply Jalan Boon Lay.
    4. affected St 64 residents take 192 from Ave 4, Jalan Boon Lay or JW central.
    5. affected residents may take 242 or new 244.

    7. 242
    8. re-routed to ply Jurong West Street 64, and
    9. re-routed to loop via Jurong West Street 61.
    10. affected N7 residents take 242 from Ave 5 or St 71.
    11. affected N6 residents take 242 from St 61.
    12. affected Ave 4 residents may take 192 or 99.

    14. 243
    15. re-routed to skip Jurong West Street 61 and St 62.
    16. re-routed to loop via Pioneer Road North.
    17. affected St 61 residents may take 185 or 243 (at Ave 4) to Gek Poh.
    18. affected St 62 residents may take 192 or 242 to Gek Poh.
    19. affected N6 residents may take 242 to Ave 5.

    21. 244
    22. takes over Jurong West Street 61 and St 62 from existing route 243.
    23. loops at Jurong West Street 91, via Street 71.
    length:
    re-routed 243 - 6.5 km - 1.4 km extension - (including deleted) 3.7 km shortening.
    re-routed 242 - 6.2 km - 0.5 km extension.
    new 244 - 5.6 km
    re-routed 192 - 2 km extension
    total = 4.4 km new route.

    Here is the address to the Google Map showing the proposed network amendment: https://www.google.com/maps/d/edit?mid=z0EIBCs5wxHI.k7FxJyf-imU0
