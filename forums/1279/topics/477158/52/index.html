    Service 955 [Woodlands Int <> Joo Koon].


    https://www.google.com/maps/d/edit?mid=zs70d3gkW184.k8C2BrRDonMY

    First of all, Woodlands Int is currently full to bursting point, and can hardly accomodate new svcs. Next, Woodlands hardly needs so many new svcs to the city area. 951 (trunk variant of 951E) is ok, but can exit at Moulmein or Kampong Java and serve Newton/Scotts as well. 952 and 954 would take too much time at Ang Mo Kio and Sembawang Rd, too slow for pax travelling from Woodlands to city.

    953 too long and winding, can start from Sembawang instead of Woodlands. Not many ppl would go to Sixth Avenue, while Holland Village and Buona Vista got MRT. May be better to go via Clementi Rd, to serve NP, SIM and maybe SP.

    955 is a good route, fast connections between Woodlands, Yew Tee and Jurong West/ Joo Koon, viable alternative to MRT, although prone to jams at Woodlands checkpoint. May be better to terminate at Boon Lay instead (which should have space after some JIS move to Joo Koon), as off peak demand to NTU and Joo Koon may be quite low. Boon Lay also a more convenient transfer point for Jurong West residents.
