    The Jurong section is already covered by several bus routes - 157 (to Bukit Batok), 98 (between Jurong East and Jurong West), 187 (between Bukit Batok and Jurong West Street 51), 99 (from Jurong West/East to Commonwealth Avenue West), etcetera .

    I heard from somewhere that Commonwealth Avenue West has serious traffic congestion problem. Until measures are made to solve the traffic congestion problem, no new bus route should go along that road. Besides, there is already 99. It may be better if bus route 99 is re-routed instead, to use Jurong East Central to go to Boon Lay Way instead of Jurong Town Hall Road.

    The Vivo City section seems to resemble bus route 30. The section to Changi Airport may not be feasible to operate.

    You know, there is a reason why MRT can co-exist with bus. Considering the traffic jams during peak hours along AYE and a few other roads, using the MRT along the way may be a faster option to go to the Vivo City area from Jurong and Bukit Batok. Likewise to the city and Changi Airport.

    Nonetheless, the bus connectivity between Toh Guan area of Jurong East and Bukit Batok should indeed be reviewed.
