    Well done. 

    Finally a service express from Sembawang Int. This is a very good route that I foresee will significantly boost connectivity between Sembawang and North/Central/Northeast (163) Singapore. One example is how Fernvale residents and YCK Rd commuters can now choose to travel 163 <--> 164 as a direct and fast mean to connect between Sembawang Town/Admiralty and their respective locations. 

    Another thing I really like about the route is how WDL Ave 12 is being fully utilised here. With the new flats and the new hospital coming up that place will surely thrive with heavy traffic, both vehicle and human, and it would be a enormous contrast to Ave 12 today, where only a few cars pass through plain fields to get onto SLE, and hardly a trace of human movement. As such a new service would definitely be required to cater to such potential location. As I have said before, this service is high in potential as it plies express to YCK Rd, and plus altogether how it brings ppl from Ave 12 (especially those from hospital) to nearest town centre (Sembawang) and MRT and it allows residents and patients and visitors to seamlessly transfer to the many services along YCK Rd and Toa Payoh within just a few stops after the expressway, I can foresee that this service will always be packed to the door due to high demands. Thus I recommend 60% DDs on this route, and frequency to be around 10 mins or less. 

    Excellent service you have proposed here, keep it up!

    Edit: Oh but on a side note I discourage this service to be under SBST as it does not blend in well with the Woodlands side, and dual-depot will also not be possible if SBST takes the service. Anyways great service there!
