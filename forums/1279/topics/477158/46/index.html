    Pasir Ris - Hougang St 21 via Changi North, Bedok North, Lor Ah Soo OR Upp Paya Lebar

     

    Pasir Ris Int (Pasir Ris MRT)

    Dr 3, Ctrl, Dr 1

    Loyang Ave, New Loyang Link, Old Tamp Rd

    Upp Changi Rd North, PIE

    Bedok North Ave 3, Rd (Bedok North MRT), Reservoir Rd

    Kaki Bukit Ave 1 (Kaki Bukit MRT), Eunos Link

    From here, there are two options

    Option 1

    Hougang Ave 3, Lor Ah Soo

    Option 2

    Airport Rd, Upp Paya Lebar Rd (Tai Seng MRT)

    Upp Paya Lebar Rd, Upp Serangoon Rd

    Hougang St 21, Ave 1, Tamp Rd, Upp Serangoon Rd (Kovan MRT)

     

    Pros

    Link Blk 442 (market place area in Pasir Ris East) to Loyang Ave (area old 357 loop at, no direct link to Pasir Ris Int without walking), and Loyang Estate (no link to Dr 1 from there)

    Link Dr 1 to Changi North (multiple services to transfer from Dr 1 compared to Dr 3)

    Direct link from Bedok North to Changi North (fastest currently is transfer to svc 2 at Bedok MRT, or 5 at Bedok Reservoir)

    Link Bedok North MRT to areas around Fengshan Pri (none of the 4 services ply at area of North Rd

    Option 2 - Link Kaki Bukit MRT to Airport Rd

    Option 1 - Alternate for 113 users to Kovan

     

    Cons

    From Pasir Ris Int to Changi North, 5 is faster

    Somewhat duplicate 5 from Pasir Ris to Kaki Bukit

    When Downtown Line is completed, if 18 retains its current route, it will connect Fengshan Pri pax to the MRT line better compared to this service (1-2 stops compared to 3-4 stops for this service)

    Option 1 - Somewhat duplicates 45 from Bedok - Lor Ah Soo, and 87 from Bedok - Hougang Ave 3

    Option 2 - Somewhat duplicate 58 from Kaki Bukit to Upp Paya Lebar

    Upp Serangoon Rd has heavy traffic, may not be a reliable alternative to 113 pax at Lor Ah Soo
