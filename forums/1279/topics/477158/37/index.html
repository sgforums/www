    Going with the M trend

    2M: Changi Village Terminal loop @ Bedok Interchange 

    * caters to the heavy loading in both directions between Bedok & Upper Changi

    17M: Bedok Interchange loop @ Bedok North Depot

    * replaces 17A and runs full day

    27M: Sengkang Interchange loop @ Tampines Ave 4

    * replaces 27A and runs full day incl. weekends

    28M: Tampines Interchange loop @ Bedok Interchange

    * operates on weekends as well

    32M: Buona Vista Terminal loop @ Redhill close

    * additional stop along Redhill close, meant for connecting new residential complex along Margaret Drive to Redhill/Commonwealth MRT

    33M: Bedok Interchange loop @ Dunearn Rd

    *replaces 33B

    34M: Punggol Interchange loop @ Tampines Ave 7

    * caters to the high loading area within Tampines town & to/from Punggol, given major CGA bound load will take 858 from TPE stop

    36M: Siglap Terminal loop @ Tomlinson road

    * replaces 36B and operates on all days incl. weekends

    43M: Punggol Interchange loop @ Serangoon Central

    * replaces 43A and operates full day to cater to bi-directional high loading between Serangoon and Buangkok. Also urging more people from BGK Green to choose BGK MRT (which is majorly under-utilized)

    67M1: CCK Interchange loop @ Lor 1 Geylang || 67M2: Tampines Interchange loop @ Rochor Canal

    * caters to the high loading between two clear segments of the route

    70M2: Ang Mo Kio Depot loop @ Serangoon Central (70M1 will loop @ Suntec)

    * caters to the high bi-directional loading between YCK Road and Serangoon MRT, replaces 70B
