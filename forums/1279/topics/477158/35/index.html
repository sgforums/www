As a result, there are people who are actually under 242's coverage but they go and take 243 or 181. This may be the reason why 243 and 181 seems to be under-supplied. 

    If we let 242 provide a direct bus connection to Street 72 from Boon Lay, those under 242's coverage will really go and take 242. Meanwhile, the re-routed 242 can provide N7 and N8 people a bus connection to Pioneer MRT Station. The connection to Pioneer MRT may help to relieve some load over at Street 64, which may bring about another benefit, which is that the loading on buses at Street 64 and 75 may be reduced. 

    Meanwhile, at Gek Poh and Street 82, even with the re-route of 243, there's still 181 providing direct bus connection to/fro Boon Lay. Whereas at Street 81, the route to Boon Lay (via Pioneer Mall) will become shorter, which means a shorter ride for commuters going there.

    Nonetheless, this proposal would provide three new bus connections and help to reduce loading. For instance,

    1. * Some commuters can take 242 from Pioneer MRT Station instead of Boon Lay Bus Interchange. This should reduce loading off buses (eg. new 242 and 181) coming from Boon Lay Bus Interchange and heading towards Jurong West Street 75.
    2. * With 242 providing direct bus connection to Jurong West Street 72, this should encourage some commuters going to Jurong West Street 72 to take 242 instead of other buses (that goes to Jurong West Street 75), thus reduce loading of those buses (eg. 181).
    3. * By then, there may not be a need to have two bus services at Jurong West Street 75 providing direct bus connections to Boon Lay Bus Interchange, so we can re-route either 181 or 243 to ply Jalan Boon Lay and compensate the loss due to 242 amendment.     5. * With the new bus connections, people can have direct bus connections to some destinations (at Pioneer Road North, Jurong West Street 61 and Jalan Boon Lay), that they need not change buses at Boon Lay Bus Interchange or wait for buses (eg. 99) at some major bus stops (eg. those along Jurong West Avenue 5).
