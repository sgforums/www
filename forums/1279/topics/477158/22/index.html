    Not sure if this has already been suggested.

    So far, most of the buses linking HDB towns are concentrated in the Central - NE - Eastern corridor, and with the Nature Reserve taking a large portion of space between Central and West HDB towns, buses connecting the West side (BB, CCK) have to make a large loop down to the south (PIE, Bt Timah) to connect with Central or CBD. There are virtually no buses that make the loop over from the north (SLE, Mandai) to make these connections, so commuters have to travel the long loop north to Woodlands or south to Buona Vista / Bt Timah.
