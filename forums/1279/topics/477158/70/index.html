    Route revision to Service 66, at JE and BBT:

    • Jurong Gateway Rd
    • Boon Lay Way (via JEM, Westgate)
    • Jurong Town Hall Rd
    • Bt Batok Rd
    • Bt Batok West Ave 3
    • Bt Batok St 11
    • Bt Batok West Ave 6
    • Bt Batok West Ave 3
    • Bt Batok Ctrl
    • Bt Batok East Ave 2
    • Bt Batok East Ave 5
    • Bt Batok East Ave 4
    • Bt Batok East Ave 2
    • Bt Batok East Ave 6
    • (original route towards Bedok Int)

    Rationale:

    • - Both towns (and this route) will be under Tower Transit in the near future, so no conflict of territory between PTOs. SMRT will be guest PTO.

    • - Provide direct connection for Bt Batok St 11 / East Ave 4 residents to both BBT MRT as well as the malls and developments at Jurong East, and back. (There's already 176, but this amendment serves non-176 areas as well, as mentioned. Science Ctr link still provided by 178.)

    • - Relatively high likelihood of 66 being amended / cut short in its central segments due to opening of DTL2 and DTL3, so this change focuses it more towards short-distance connections, at least for the west side.

    • - Parallel route along BBT East Ave 3 already doubled by 61, 157, 174, 178 and 852, so it's not particularly needed there. (And there is 990 direct to JE already)

    • - After which, 506 can be re-routed to serve JTH Rd along the Science Ctr stretch, and 941 can be re-routed to serve West Valley (West Ave 8/9) when it is ready in 2018.
