    Before there's any argument made, can I just say that at the southern section of Taman Jurong along Corporation Road, actually that area is within close proximity to 240? As such, priority should be given to Tukang Industrial area.

    This new route has tons of benefits. Present it to the local RC people and they will surely be able to relate to it and tell you all the benefits. basically,

    • shorter public transport travel for people at Boon Lay Avenue, Jalan Boon Lay, Jurong West Avenue 3 and Tukang Industrial area.
    • shorter bus connection to/fro Yung Sheng-Yung Kuang area.
    1. If we route it to ply along Corporation Drive and use Tah Ching Road to ply Yuan Ching Road, this new route can effectively replace 246.
    2. Likewise, we can route this new route to ply Boon Lay Drive and Boon Lay Place, while 240 is re-routed to ply Corporation Road directly to Boon Lay Avenue.
    3. Affected commuters at the skipped sector of Boon Lay Drive are within 250m from the next nearest bus stop in the vicinity. So, there's really not much of an impact.
