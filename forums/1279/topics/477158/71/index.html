    When the North East Line (NEL) first opened on 20 June 2003, a mass rationalization exercise was conduced in three phases on 21 June, 27 July and 7 December for Services 21, 22, 53, 62, 70/70M, 81, 82, 97, 103, 106, 111, 130, 139 and 502, while Services 85, 501, 511 and 512 were axed.

    While the mass rationalization exercise was deemed necessary in order to reduce the duplication of public transport services and encourage commuters to switch to the NEL, it has resulted in the loss of certain direct connections which could have well served as alternatives for commuters in the event of train service disruptions or divert the high passenger demand to.

    It is agreeable that the NEL does provide certain connections from the residential towns of Serangoon, Hougang, Sengkang and Punggol more quickly in comparison to the bus services shortened or withdrawn during the rationalization. However, they could have been better tweaked to reduce duplication and serve as an alternative to the NEL.

    Nevertheless, it is impossible to bring the routes back to how they were. Moreover, with an additional rail line (i.e. CCL serving Serangoon New Town), the "pre-NEL" routes would be even more redundant today. However, having some alternatives that provide travel time that equates to travelling on the NEL/CCL may be a good move.

    Currently, Punggol New Town has no full day bus service that connects to the city areas, namely Orchard, Chinatown, Shenton Way, Bugis and Marina Centre, while Sengkang New Town only has Service 80, which has to go through the eastern parts of Singapore before serving the city regions. Thus, most commuters can only rely on the NEL to get to town (excluding peak hours w/ City Direct 654/Premiums 550, 569) As such, greater focus will be placed on the connections for Sengkang and Punggol New Towns in the route suggestions below.

    It is understandable that the Sengkang & Punggol Ints are running low on space to accommodate more bus services. Nevertheless, the route suggestions below will take a hypothesis that the bus interchanges still have some space for new bus services.

    Route 146: Punggol Temp Int - HarbourFront Int

    Slightly mirroring the old sv85 between Orchard & HarbourFront but with a faster route from Punggol/Sengkang/Buangkok via the CTE.

    Direction 1

    Punggol Temp Int, (Punggol Pl), Punggol Central, Punggol Rd, Sengkang East Way, Sengkang East Rd, Buangkok Green, Yio Chu Kang Rd, (CTE), Bt Timah Rd, Sungei Rd, Bencoolen St, Middle Rd, (Prinsep St, Bras Basah Rd), Bencoolen St, Orchard Rd, Penang Rd, Somerset Rd, Grange Rd, Orchard Blvd, Paterson Rd, (Paterson Hill), Irwell Bank Rd, Kim Seng Rd, Outram Rd, Tiong Bahru Rd, Lower Delta Rd, Kg Bahru Rd, Telok Blangah Rd, (Seah Im Rd), HarbourFront Int.

    Direction 2

    HarbourFront Int, (Seah Im Rd), Telok Blangah Rd, Kg Bahru Rd, Lower Delta Rd, Tiong Bahru Rd, Zion Rd, Hoot Kiam Rd, (Paterson Hill), Paterson Rd, Orchard Rd, Prinsep St, Selegie Rd, Bt Timah Rd, (CTE), Yio Chu Kang Rd, Buangkok Green, Sengkang East Rd, Sengkang East Way, Punggol Rd, Punggol Central, (Punggol Pl), Punggol Temp Int.

    Route 148: Sengkang Temp Int - Shenton Way Ter

    Slightly mirroring the old sv82 between Serangoon/Bendemeer Rd & Shenton Way Ter with an extended loop via Marina Centre & Bayfront but a faster route from Sengkang/Hougang via the CTE.

    Direction 1
