    With the Bukit Panjang MRT station coming up, I foresee some route changes in CCK to better connect certain areas to Bukit Panjang MRT. Teck Whye Ave has 975 and 190 to Bukit Panjang, while Choa Chu Kang Way has 67 as well as the LRT. However, the western side of CCK unserved by the LRT as well as Yew Tee up north have no direct connection to Bukit Panjang. Thus, I propose the following new routes:

     

    Feeder 303

    Choa Chu Kang Int [Choa Chu Kang MRT/LRT]

    Choa Chu Kang Dr [non-stop]

    Choa Chu Kang Ave 3 [new bus stops near the junction with CCK Central]

    Brickland Rd [new bus stops]

    Bukit Batok Rd

    Choa Chu Kang Rd

    Upp Bukit Timah Rd [Bukit Panjang MRT]

    Petir Rd [Bukit Panjang LRT]

    Bt Panjang Rd

    Choa Chu Kang Rd

    Bukit Batok Rd

    Brickland Rd [new bus stops]

    Choa Chu Kang Ave 3

    Choa Chu Kang Dr [non-stop]

    Choa Chu Kang Int [Choa Chu Kang MRT/LRT]

     

    -connects existing and upcoming flats at western side of Choa Chu Kang (Ave 3 and Brickland Rd) to Choa Chu Kang and Bukit Panjang MRT/LRT, as well as amenities such as ITE College West

     

    Feeder 304

    Choa Chu Kang Int [Choa Chu Kang MRT/LRT]

    Choa Chu Kang Ave 4 [non-stop]

    Choa Chu Kang Way [new bus stops]

    Choa Chu Kang North 7

    Choa Chu Kang Dr [Yew Tee MRT] [serves the NR3 bus stop pair]

    Choa Chu Kang North 5

    Stagmont Ring [non-stop]

    Woodlands Rd

    Senja Way [new bus stops]

    Senja Rd

    Bukit Panjang Ring Rd

    Bukit Panjang Rd

    Upp Bukit Timah Rd [Bukit Panjang MRT]

    Petir Rd [Bukit Panjang LRT]

    Jelebu Rd

    Bukit Panjang Ring Rd

    Senja Rd

    Senja Way [new bus stops]

    Woodlands Rd

    Stagmont Ring [non-stop]

    Choa Chu Kang North 5

    Choa Chu Kang Dr [Yew Tee MRT] 

    Choa Chu Kang North 7

    Choa Chu Kang Way [new bus stops]

    Choa Chu Kang Ave 4 [non stop]

    Choa Chu Kang Int [Choa Chu Kang MRT/LRT]

     

    -connects Yew Tee and Bukit Panjang/Senja via Woodlands Rd, providing convenient access to Bukit Panjang MRT/LRT and amenities such as Choa Chu Kang stadium

    -connects Mowbray Camp and Kranji Camp II to Choa Chu Kang, Yew Tee and Bukit Panjang MRT stations

    *I have previously suggested a route between CCK/Yew Tee/BPJ and Punggol/Sengkang that provides a similar connection between Yew Tee and BPJ, but a short feeder or trunk route like this one is more likely under LTA's so-called hub and spoke transport model.

     

    Amended service 171

    (existing route from Yishun to Bukit Panjang)

    Jelebu Rd [Bukit Panjang MRT/LRT within walking distance]

    Bukit Panjang Rd

    Choa Chu Kang Rd

    Teck Whye Ave

    Teck Whye Cres [new bus stop]

    Choa Chu Kang Dr

    Choa Chu Kang Int [Choa Chu Kang MRT/LRT]

     

    -removes the Bukit Panjang - Marina Centre portion, which duplicates other bus services and the upcoming Downtown Line stage 2

    -provides direct bus connection between Yishun and Choa Chu Kang

    -serves schools and flats along Teck Whye Cres
