    24X & 33X are meant for feeder bus routes.

    Speaking of which, I have a suggestion for a new feeder bus route/service in Jurong West (Pioneer). This new route plies Jurong West Street 65 and completes quite a number of "puzzles" (providing new bus connections). Distance is around 6.1 to 6.2km, similar to route 241, hence I categorise it as 'feeder'.

    New Jurong West feeder (loops at Pioneer Road North)

    1. Boon Lay Bus Interchange
    2. Jurong West Central 3
    3. Jurong West Street 64
    4. Jurong West Street 65
    5. Jurong West Street 63
    6. Pioneer MRT Station
    7. Pioneer Road North
    8. Jurong West Avenue 5
    9. Jurong West Street 71
    10. Jurong West Avenue 4
    11. Pioneer Road North
    12. Pioneer MRT Station
    13. Jurong West Street 63
    14. Jurong West Street 65
    15. Jurong West Street 64
    16. Jurong West Central 3
    17. Boon Lay Bus Interchange
    • Benefits:
    • 1. Shorter bus route from blks 97X, blks 83X and blks 71X to MRT. 
    • 2. Complements heavily utilised bus routes, such as 243, 242, 241 and 181.
    • 3. Enhanced bus connection for blks 67X, blks 63X and blks 61X.

    New Trunk route/service which would create new bus connections while improve bus connectivity, in Buona Vista, Clementi, Jurong Gateway, and most importantly, to/fro Ngee Ann Polytechnic.

    Hopefully, if this route/service is a reality, the congestion problem at Clementi MRT Station bus stop can be relieved.

    New Buona Vista-Jurong East route

    1. Buona Vista Bus Terminal
    2. North Buona Vista Road (Buona Vista MRT)
    3. Vista Exchange Green
    4. Commonwealth Avenue West (serves new HDB flats at Ghim Moh Link)
    5. Commonwealth Avenue West (Dover MRT)
    6. Clementi Road
    7. Clementi Road (Ngee Ann Polytechnic)
    8. Pan Island Expressway
    9. Toh Guan Road
    10. Jurong Gateway Road
    11. Jurong East Bus Interchange (Jurong East MRT)
    Length to Jurong East: 11.5km
    Length to Buona Vista: 13.2km
    Total length: 24.7km

    Length on PIE (towards Jurong East): 3.9km (33.9% of route)
    Length on PIE (towards Buona Vista): 3.9km (29.5% of route)
    Total length on PIE: 7.8km (31.6% of route)

    Note: to Buona Vista:
    • - exit PIE via Upper Bukit Timah Road, Jalan Jurong Kechil, Jalan Anak Bukit and Clementi Road. 
    • - enter Buona Vista Bus Terminal via Commonwealth Avenue, Holland Avenue and Holland Drive.
