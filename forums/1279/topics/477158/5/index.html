    This is a new trunk route which loops at Bukit Gombak.

    First, it complements route 945 and provides bus connection to Bukit Gombak MRT Station for residents staying near Dazhong Primary School - there is currently no bus connection to the MRT station.

    Second, it provides inter-town bus connection between Jurong West and Bukit Batok - as passengers of Jurong West can commute on their individual feeder bus and  transfer to this new trunk service at bus stops along Jurong West Avenue 2/4, while those staying around Jurong West Avenue 2/4 can simply take this new trunk service from their doorstep.

    • With this new trunk service, Jurong West passengers going to Bukit Batok would no longer have to stopover at Boon Lay Bus Interchange - may help to relieve load off bus routes 174, 157 and 187, and benefit passengers commuting on those bus services, while reduce the crowding level at Boon Lay Bus Interchange.
    • Same applies to Bukit Batok passengers going to Jurong West.

    SBS Transit should operate service on this route, with buses originating from Soon Lee Bus Depot and Bukit Batok Bus Depot (injecting into service from Bukit Batok West Avenue 2, via Bukit Batok Central, BB Avenue 1. BB East Avenue 6 and BB Street 23).

    Length = 6.95km (750m longer than 241)

    Benefits:

    1. 1a. This route improves Boon Lay Bus Interchange's feeder coverage at st 93. currently, st 93 residents are not really well-served by public buses because all the buses are at Pioneer Road North. If they want to take buses 241 or 179 to Pioneer MRT Station, they have to walk at least 200m to the nearest bus stop at blk 643, which they might as well just walk to Pioneer MRT Station. As there is a road barrier in the way, they cannot directly cross the road. So, although the nearest bus stop is just opposite them, it is actually not the nearest.
    2. 1b. In addition, this new feeder would enable residents at blk986-990 to have feeder coverage to Boon Lay Bus Interchange. 
    3. 2. shorter travelling time than route 241. The traffic at PRN is much heavier than than the traffic at JW st 93. This alternative route to/fro JW st 91 and Pioneer MRT station is faster. (vague statement)
    4. 3. To some extent, this new feeder route complements feeder 241 and relieves the load off feeder 241.
