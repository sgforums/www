    There's only a fraction of 225 passengers actually willing to go up onto the upper deck of the bus. Not the elderly though, there's actually more school children and the young on the upper deck of the bus. When SBS 9578S cameoed on 225G on Thursday and I took it at about 5+, there were higher ratios of people on the lower deck compared to the upper deck. It took me some time to squeeze in to get to the steps to the upper deck, also considering that I have to carry a heavy bag + three files, hand carried.

    Whether the mindset of the people will change, it will have to depend. But even with such introduction of DDs on a feeder service and popularity of people going up to the upper deck is low, perhaps some day there may be a blessing in disguise - more people may be willing to move to the upper deck instead of crowding the lower deck in the future.
