    Actually, what makes you think there will be a re-shuffle of bus services at the existing depots? I feel that everything will remain the same, as we never know the GCM may fail and we have to fall back on the existing depots.

    If Bulim GCM or Loyang GCM or whatever GCM fail, who is going to take over? Yes, according to a bill recently passed in parliament, there will be an interim operator taking over, but in the initial stage, it's quite likely that the interim operator is the incumbent operators SBS and SMRT, and for operations to go on efficiently, they may want to use the depots they have been using all along, instead of go over and temporarily manage a new depot.

    So, yeah, I do not think there will be a re-shuffle, at least in the initial stage. In the later part, as more and more bus services are transferred out of the existing depots, the incumbents SBS and SMRT may merge and shrink operations at their existing depots to just one or two depots each, and then eventually phase out everything.
