    Sengkang Bus Interchange

    There are sixteen parking bays, three alighting bays, four sawtooth boarding berths.

    Currently, the four sawtooth boarding berths can accomodate at least twelve bus services, yet the sixteen parking bays can only accomodate eight bus services.

    The air-con replacement will be completed progressively by third quarter this year. I hope they can also take this opporunity to make the place brighter, by installing lamps with higher lux, painting the walls with white or some bright colours, etcetera.

     

    Sengkang Bus Interchange expansion

    Construction started around October last year. It will be constructed by the third quater of this year.

    Looking at the location, I feel that it is quite a walk from Sengkang MRT Station. Have to cross a bridge then can reach.

    As we all know, there will be twelve parking bays at the bus interchange expansion. With the expansion, there will be a total of twenty eight parking bays; there should be space for around fourteen bus services.

    I feel that we can actually fit around fourteen bus services at the four existing boarding berths. Two of the berths can have four bus services, while two berth can have three bus services. If like that, then the people do not have to walk long distances and climb the bridge to go over to the bus interchange expansion.

    The parking bays of the current interchange and expansion should be connected, with a common junction, so that buses can run between the two parking bays... but then, apparently, that is not the case. :(

    Having said that, it does not really matter. Not because I do not stay in Sengkang, but currently there are already bus services in Sengkang that only pass through Sengkang along Compassvale Road. For example, bus service 27. There are people who after alighting from MRT at Sengkang station have to walk long distances climb bridge and walk somemore to the bus stop at Compassvale Road just to take those bus services. So, it should not be a problem for more people to take the trouble to walk to the bus interchange extension.

    Nonetheless, it would be good if bus routes originating from the bus interchange expansion can call at the bus stops outside Sengkang MRT Station, along Sengkang East Way.

     

    Currently, there are eleven bus services. The current bus interchange parking bays can only accomodate eight bus services, so it's obviously undersized. With the expansion, hopefully there's enough parking bays for all the bus services.

    This also means Sengkang Bus Interchange can only have three more bus services after the expansion. With only three vacancies available, we should use them wisely.
