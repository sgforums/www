    How can bus services to Chinatown be improved?

    As one of Singapore's tourist destinations, it suffers from constant traffic jams especially on weekends and public holidays. ERP has not lessened the number of cars, taxis that compete with public buses for road space.

    Are bus services to Chinatown well-utilized? Are the bus routes well-planned? What areas of improvements can you think of?

    Some bus services which serve Eu Tong Sen St/Sth Bridge Rd:
    2 12 33 51 54 61 63 80 124 143 145 147 166 174 190 197 851 961 970 C2.

    Suggestions for improvements/opinions:

    Which bus routes can be further improved?
    Which bus routes should be amended? Are they too long?
    Are there duplication of bus services?
    Do you think any bus services should be withdrawn or increased?
    Are staggered bus stops a good idea?
    Are there too many or too few bus services serving Chinatown?
    Should some bus services use only double deckers?
    Do other bus services need bendys?
    Are major residential areas well-connected to Chinatown?
    What are your experiences taking the bus to/from Chinatown?

    Grievances, complaints, positive feedback, praise - let's hear it all!
