    Originally posted by Gus.chong:

    Oh...I saw your post in the past deployment thread, but I wonder it is true, cos Sgwiki never show signs of WLDEP 980 appearing on the TIB658K section in Former Deployments section (twice).

    Somehow the deployment for TIB658K went like this:-

    Initially was perm 167 in 1999-2000, and at that period 167 was under full WLDEP whereas 980 was full AMDEP. It was the depot redeployments in Jan 2001 that made both 167 and 980 under dual AM/WLDEP controlled, hence TIB658K went over 980.

    980 went back under full AMDEP controlled for a short period of time in Sept 2004 (when 950 was introduced) and TIB658K was out together with the other Scanias. It landed on 167 again to replace some 0405s that got redeployed to 950 or something. And in Oct that year 980 went back under partial WLDEP again, eventually TIB658K and 2 other Scanias went back to 980.

    In May 2008 there was another depot transfers for 980 and WLDEP was left with 2 duties - 1 split and 1 full (TIB665P full while CSS183U split), TIB658K and TIB673R left. A few weeks later TIB658K made a comeback to 980, CSS183U went out.
