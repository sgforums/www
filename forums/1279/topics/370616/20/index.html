  • sbs&tibs

    Originally posted by Fryderyk HPH:

     

    Can someone give me advice on night shots?


    Are you currently using digital camera?

    Adjust shutter speed. I prefer to put a least one second. Change the white balance to Tungsten mode, your bus in the photo will not look so orangey. Don't set your ISO too high. Use a tripod and set timer for your camera.

    Like sunlight shots, I like to take night shots under correct lightings (lamp post), so that your bus pics do not look so dark.

    Enjoy experimenting.
