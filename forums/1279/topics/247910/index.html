    Originally posted by Volvo Olympian:
    Service 10

    Use to be from Tampines to Jurong? Then shorten to Clementi and finally, Kent Ridge.

    quote from sgwiki webby (http://www.sgwiki.com/wiki/Svcs_2_to_20)

    This svc was originally between Upp Changi Rd 13.3 km and Jurong Interchange. The former was subsequently moved to new a premise, at 15.5 km, called Somapah Interchange.
    Was extended from Somapah to Tampines Ave 5 Terminal in 1984.
    Was extended to ply from Tampines Interchange when it opened in 1988.
    Was shortened to ply from Tampines to Clementi when Jurong Interchange closed.
    Route was amended to ply:
    - (Direction 1) Mountbatten Rd, Tg Katong Rd, Dunman Rd, Old Airport Rd, Mountbatten Rd instead of serving the full stretch of Mountbatten Rd.
    - (Direction 2) Mountbatten Rd, Old Airport Rd, Dunman Rd, Tg Katong Rd, Mountbatten Rd instead of serving the full stretch of Mountbatten Rd.
    Was further shortened to ply from Tampines to Kent Ridge.
