    ComfortDelGro has since started the Mobileye Advanced Driver Assistance System trial for 30 cabs and an unknown number of buses based on an article published on the Straits Times.

    The Mobileye 560 Advanced Driver Assistance System includes a windshield-mounted vision sensor unit, a round display/control unit, called the "EyeWatch", and an audio alert buzzer.

    Example of the system installed on SBS3256K.

    SBS Transit Buses with Mobileye 560 installed (5 buses):
    > SBS3066S (AMDEP 58)
    > SBS3200X (SLBP 243)
    > SBS3256K (AMDEP 58)
    > SBS3271R (AMDEP 58)
    > SBS8118T (BBDEP 175)
