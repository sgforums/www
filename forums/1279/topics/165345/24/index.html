    Originally posted by iveco:
    172 & 206 used to serve LCK Rd (end).

    In fact, the only part of 172 route that remains today is the (old) CCK Rd stretch. The construction of CCK town and KJE caused permanent changes to the road system in the area. Not to mention the amendment to start from BNL Int and shortening from Shenton Wy to Upp Bt Timah and later CCK int.
    Service 172 used to serve Lim Chu Kang to Shenton Way in the 1980s till it was shortened to Jln Anak Bukit in early 1990s. Service 206 was between Boon Lay (Jurong) to Lim Chu Kang.

    In 1993, when aircon service 175 was introduced between Lim Chu Kang to Upp Bukit Timah, service 206 was withdrawn whereas service 172 was amended to serve from Boon Lay via Jln Bahar to cover the route of service 206.
