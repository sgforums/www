  • The_Bus_Guide
    3 Apr 07, 21:28
    Originally posted by SBS BUSES LOVER:
    i heard there was a hillview terminal last time ( AKA princess elizabeth terminal ), anyone got info on it ?
    It was known as Princess Elizabeth (Estate) Terminal, named after the housing estate of the same name which used to occupy the area. Now, the area where 24 blocks of mostly 1-room flats used to stand is a vacant grassy plot of land, at least the last time I went there, anyway. The terminal was located along Elizabeth Dr, of which the part of the road where the terminal stood is now covered. But it is roughly in the proximity of the Red Cross Home of the Disabled. The last known services at the terminal was 173 which went to New Bridge Rd and 193 which went to Shenton Way. 193 was withdrawn first.
