    Regarding the questions about low values on EZ-Link cards and still being able to ride, it is possible to make a transfer from bus to bus or MRT to bus with zero or negative value on your EZ-Link card, as ong as it is a sucessful transfer. I tried it before, and I also once asked some SMRT staff who said that this is possible.

    Here's a somewhat related question:

    Under what circumstances will your ride cost you $0.00 (no, this is not a mistype)? I actualy have seen this happen before with my adult EZ-Link card before, the most recent being when I was taking either route 2 or 24 (can't remember).

    Here are the details of that trip as far as I can remember:

    Route 859 - Sembawang, between blocks 357 and 359 -> Yishun, block 923
    Route 39 - Yishun Interchange -> Pasir Ris, block 738
    Route 89 - Pasir Ris, Block 738 -> before Singapore Aviation A.C. (near Changi Vilage)
    Route 2 - Changi Village -> somewhere along Upper Changi Road East (can't remember exactly where) (I took this bus some time after my trip on route 89 as I had lunch at Changi Village; the reader still showed that I made a transfer if I remember correctly)
    Route 24 - Upper Changi Road East -> Changi Airport Terminal 2

    The portion hilighted in red was the portion I recall seeing a fare deduction of $0.00 (not sure exactly which route).
