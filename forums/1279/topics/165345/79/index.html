    Originally posted by Y_Shun:
    btw, the ezlink system is called DDU
    It is not the ez-link system, its another system which is connected to the ez-link system. Its proper name is called AVMS DDU or many call it AVMS (think last time when they have the newspaper article, they did mention what does AVMS mean). SMRT one is IBOS.

    In an SBS Transit bus, you would have the folowing equipments near the driver's cab:
    AVMS DDU (and the mircophone)
    IDFC
    BEXP (BEP and BXP)
    VLS (hidden)
    On board computer (hidden behind driver)
    Sanyo S-light fare vaildator.
