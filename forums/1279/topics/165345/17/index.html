    So, what are the exact routes, and where did they ply?

    I do know about the following:

    700 - formerly RapidBus 700 that linked Woodlands, Bukit Panjang, Orchard, and Shenton Way using B.K.E. and the Upper Bukit Timah Road/Durean Road corridor. Eventually shortened and later turned into a normal trunk route with Woodlands sector withdrawn, becoming a dedicated Bukit Panjang service. Route 700A introduced later (in it's RapidBus days) to operate in the evenings and weekends, skipping Shenton Way and looping at Suntec City.

    711, 712, and 713 - former SBS Transit Jurong Island routes, all withdrawn when SBS Transit lost control of the Jurong Island routes. They plied to different areas within Jurong Island - can't remember exactly where.

    714(?), 715, and probably others - current Jurong Island bus routes, provided by a private bus company called Transport Network Consotrium. Not sure of it's routing...
