    Originally posted by stooper:
    Does anyone know why, for some services, 2 identical timesheets per duty are printed, and each timesheet is used every other day (as in rotated daily)?
    Does anyone know the answer to the above question?
    I know at least 179, 242 & 255 have 2 printed identically weekday timesheets. There's one proper one and the other is a "cheapskate" version.

    The proper one has all the handwritten parts (i.e. the arrow poiting to the next page, the leaving depot order, highlights) and also has the Effective date chop.

    Whereas, the cheapskate version is only highlighted (sometimes with the leaving depot order too).

    They are used on alternate days. 242 and 255 are in sync, whereas 179 is not in sync with them, i.e. 242 & 255 will use the proper timetable and 179 uses the cheapskate version on one day. Then vice-versa on the next day.
