    Originally posted by busmanic:
    2) How are those B-signals located at certain junctions being activated? Are there sensors located at the kerbs?
    The B-signal sensors at Raffles Ave, Anson Rd & St Andrew's Rd are located in the bus bay. The rest are located on the bus lanes just before the stop line. The sensors can be identified by black rectangular outlines, 2 for the front wheels, 2 for the rear wheels. Cars are not able to activate the signals because they are not long enough to reach the rear sensors.
