    Usage of ez-link card on Buses

    TRANSFER TIME
    To qualify for transfer rebates, please ensure that your transfer is made within the allowed time :

    From MRT/LRT to bus, bus to bus, or bus to MRT/LRT, you must transfer to the next service within 45 minutes of exit from MRT/LRT gate or from tapping the card on the bus exit card-reader.

    For all concessionary travel on buses or trains, the time allowed for the next transfer will also be 45 minutes of exit from MRT/LRT gate or from tapping the card on the bus exit card-reader.

    The first, second and third valid transfers on a journey must be made within 2 hours of the first boarding on the same journey

    TRANSFER REBATE
    With the ez-link card, you enjoy transfer rebates for the first, second and third transfers on a journey when all conditions for transfer are met.

    The rebate for trunk bus, industrial services, feeder bus services and intra-town/Town-link service, the MRT or LRT transfers are :

    25Â¢ for Adult, Senior Citizen, NSF and Tertiary Student ez-link card users; and

    10Â¢ for Child ez-link card and School Smartcard users.

    The same ez-link card must be used on two successive portions of your journey to enjoy transfer rebate.

    To enjoy transfer rebate between the LRT and MRT, or vice versa, passengers must not exit from the paid areas of both system.

    The following do not qualify for transfer rebate:
    1. MRT to MRT transfer does not qualify for transfer rebate but does not affect eligibility for subsequent transfer to bus

    2. Transfer between MRT and LRT

    3. Transfer from a bus to another of the same service number

    4. Transfer to/from Sentosa Bus, City Shuttle Services, Night Services, C & CT services

    5. If Bus Concession Pass (BCP), Train Concession Pass (TCP) or Hybrid Concession Pass (HCP) is used

    6. Fourth transfer onwards
