    SERVICE 185 (SOON LEE DEPOT - BUONA VISTA TERMINAL)

    Soon Lee Depot, Soon Lee Rd, Pioneer Rd North, Jurong West Ave 5, Jurong West Ave 3, Jurong West St 24, Jurong West St 23, Jurong West Ave 2, Corporation Rd, Jurong West Ave 1, Jurong East Ave 1, Jurong Town Hall Rd, PIE, Clementi Ave 6, C'wealth Ave West, C'wealth Ave, Holland Ave, Holland Dr, Buona Vista Terminal.
