    maybe not new bus stops, but some just needs to be relocated to make it more convenient for people:

    Admiralty Rd West (Opp Sunshine Bakery): should move to opposite Sembawang Camp, which is right in the middle of the 2 bus stops. only the opposite side has a stop outside the camp. the pair are not close together at all.

    Dunearn Rd (Raffles Girls' Primary School): there is a corresponding pair at Bt Timah Rd (The Chinese High) so why not at Hillcrest Rd? it's sad to see primary school kids 600m to the nearest bus stop at Dunearn Rd (Bougainvillea Park).

    Namly Ave (SIM Management House): students studying there currently have to walk 600 metres from Bt Timah Rd and about 20m uphill to their campus. at night, it could be even more dangerous. i wonder if at least one svc can turn in & exit at Sixth Ave.

    Binjai Park/Bt Tinggi Rd/Swiss Club Rd: visitors to Canadian Int'l High Sch, Embassy of Switzerland, German Sch, Hollandse Sch & estates along the route have to take a taxi in (more than 1km off Dunearn Rd).

    PIE (Adam Rd Flyover): a transit stop for services plying PIE but not convenient to change at Whitley Rd (506, 985 <-> 190, 700, 960).

    Tai Seng Ind Est./Kaki Bt Ind Est.: some parts are really far from the nearest bus stop.

    Changi North Ind Est.: some parts are really far from the nearest bus stop/ Expo station.

    Changi Coast Rd (Tanah Merah Country Club, Garden Course Entrance): the nearest bus stop is at Laguna Nat'l Golf & Country Club)

    Woodlands Dr 17 (Singapore Sports School): nearest bus stop is at least 500m at Ave 1 (Opp Blk 540) with only Svc 901.

    Woodlands Ave 2 (Woodlands JC (U/C)): operations expected to commence early 2006.

    Yishun Ave 9 (North View Primary School): too bad there's no solution at this very moment.

    Killney Rd/River Valley Close/Martin Rd/Mohd Sultan Rd/Saiboo St

    Labrador Villa Rd/Port Rd (Labrador Park)
