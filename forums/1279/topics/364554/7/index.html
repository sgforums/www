  • Ex-busdriver
    30 Sep 09, 17:26
    Originally posted by linwei20:

    You have been a Bus Captain for how long? Why did you quit? Is it stress? The pay is very little too? Can share more about your experience?

    Not very long. Only 4 months because my Uni holidays had ended.

    The pay was ok, about $1250.00 not including OT.

    I was a standby crew for 3 services; 174, 190 and 66. I had driven Volvo B10M MK2, MK3, MK4 Euro 1, MB O405, Leyland Olympian DD, Volvo Olympian DD,

    I had a few boo boos. Once I drove the bus service 174 all the way from Boon Lay Interchange to Bukit Batok without updating the stages. Several passengers got fined by ticket inspector. But they all got their refunds back when the ticket inspector found it was my fault.

    There was an occasion when I was instructed to drive the bus (Leyland Olympian DD) to jrbp after finishing the last trip for 190. But I drove it to bbdep instead. The crew at jrbp couldn't find the bus the next day. By right, the crew at bbdep should have known that the bus was supposed to be in jrbp when they took out the ticketing console. But I was not the only one who made such mistakes, even experienced drivers too.

    There were several such incidents. Coincidentally, Leyland and MB were all sent to jrbp, while Volvos came to bbdep and arbp.

    I see that Volvo Olympian is also assigned to the jurong bus routes too.
