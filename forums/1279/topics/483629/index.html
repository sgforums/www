    Still remember taking SBS6888S regularly on 198 in the mid 1990s' when I was still a primary school student then.

    Still remembered that SBS9250L to SBS9259M were the first batch of ADDs to ply my neighbourhood in 1994 doing 99.

    Then a year later in 1995 198 started using VO3X also from SBS9359H to SBS9368G and SBS9372T.

    Then in 1996 157 also started using LO3X. Still remember the 'pioneer batch' of ADDs SBS9002L, SBS9096R, SBS9187L, SBS9155D, SBS9032A, SBS9082E, SBS9104B, SBS9125R, SBS9078T, SBS9115U etc. SBS9173B, SBS9174D, SBS9128H, SBS9055J and SBS9036P 'joined' 157 later while somewhere in 1998 when some services became FAC some of them were redeployed to 98 like SBS9082E, SBS9115U, SBS9125R, SBS9104B etc while remaining JRBP LO3X SBS9128H and SBS9155D were redeployed to 179 in 2001 leaving JRBP side no LO3X for 157 after a year or two when buses like SBS9039G were redeployed to 157.
