    Okay sorry, let me put it in another way. 

    Crowding on MRT. Crowding on bus. Which is worse?

    On a train, you get speeds not much faster than a bus, but you have to stop mandatorily at stops and wait for pax to get on and off. You get a much noisier interior, and worst of all you will almost certainly have no seats and even if you have one it's those hard shell ones. 

    And what if MRT requires transfer while bus doesn't? (happens most of the time btw)

    Bus is not much better either. Cramped interior, potential traffic conditions, and also a chance of not having a seat. 

    However the seats are comfortable, the interior is more calm, can fly stop if there is no pax Getting on or off, and of course (scenic) view!

    This is why I liked express buses way more than trains in most times unless there is direct train connection. IMO express buses encounter the very least jerks and sudden brakes as it travels on expressway and flyovers without traffic lights. Even the trains jerk and fling my body more often than express buses. Just my two cents from experiences in HK, but it should be the same in SG too if more express buses are introduced, where I would prefer express bus to train that require transfer.
