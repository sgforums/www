    They have airbag suspension, like all modern citybuses from about the 90s. It is a rubber oval shaped, tubular object which sita between two metal plates, and the shock absorbers. What the hissing sound would be is just air escaping the air-bag, especially caused by the weight of people stepping on and off the bus. You will find on buses there are 3 air reservoirs. front and rear for the brakes, and an auxiliary reservoir for the doors, suspension, maxi-brake (hand brake), and other uses.
