    Bus Driver is a vehicle game released by SCS Software (18 Wheels of Steels series) and released in 2007.

    The game have relatively simple and perfect traffic rules. However, the game's physics and simulation is really bad.

    If you want a good bus simulation game, i rather you go for Midtown Madness 2 (Mods) or VBus.

     

    Since Bus Driver/Simulator 2008 was released, the bus simulation games industry seemed to "pick up". Upcoming bus games include:
