    G'day folks,

    Many people here don't realise that it's not just initial cost that bus operators look for when purchasing new vehicles.

    There are definitely more factors involved, things such as:
      Availability and pricing of spare parts

      Fuel consumption and/or cost of fuel and fuelling infrastructure (if alternative fuels are involved)

      Adequate power and torque figures from engine

      Compatibility with existing fleet (i.e. parts interchangeability)

      Warranty and product support and it's availability


    Based on all this, I would think that new Mercedes-Benz low-entry rigid vehicles would seriously be considered by SMRT.
