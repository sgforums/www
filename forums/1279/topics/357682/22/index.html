    One of the possible reasons is because even after many years of normal use, the flip dot EDS may cause "wear and tear", i.e. several of the small flip dot on its matrix will not be flipped well, just like the Transit Media ones on O405g habit bendy. Take a good look at TIB1018B or TIB1240A, several of its small flip dots are already not flipped well (some yellow dots can be seen when the EDS is programmed to blank)
