    The N113 from 1989 overall of these 2 models the more appealing.

    Largely due to the full engine encapsulation & charged-cooled engine during an era where no other diesel full-size bus or coach in Singapore is as quiet externally ! Even today, the external noise level ranks among if not still the lowest for 16t GVW bus/coach here.

    Internal noise levels however not so different from much of the rest & it's not the quietest internally. [That i'll associate with the M-B OH 1627 L here with its potent & silent big capacity V-8]

    The N113's engine is still quite advanced today with above-average performance to match. My strongest dislike of the N113 (as with almost all Scanias i've sampled) is the relatively noisy & inferior suspension ride comfort although some consolation here is that the Volvo B10M instead is the worse in this area for an air-suspended vehicle here.
