    Hi Apple,

    a) As mentioned, service 25 from Bedok to Ang Mo Kio Interchange and transfer to service 138.

    b) Service 22 from Tampines to Ang Mo Kio Interchange and transfer service 138.

    c) Take service 67 to Choa Chu Kang and transfer svc 927.

    d) To save time from "c", you can choose to take MRT to Choa Chu Kang.

    e) On Sundays and PH, you can take service 168 from Bedok to Woodlands and transfer to service 926, a Sunday/Public holiday service.

    Above are some ways you can get to the zoo from East.

    I would still recommend "a" or "d".

    Enjoy your trip to the zoo.

    The faster way to get to the zoo is to board a train to Ang Mo Kio, and transfer to service 138 at the interchange.

    Alternatively, you can board a train to Choa Chu Kang via Jurong East, and transfer to service 927 at the interchange.

    If you prefer to take buses all the way to the zoo, and if you are going there from Bedok, then you can board service 24 (to Ang Mo Kio) or service 67 (to Choa Chu Kang) outside Bedok MRT station, or service 25 (to Ang Mo Kio) at Bedok interchange. If you are going there from Simei, you can board services 9, 12 or 38 and transfer to service 24 (to Ang Mo Kio) outside Tanah Merah MRT station.
