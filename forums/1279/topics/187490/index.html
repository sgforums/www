    To add - while Fast Forward routes do not necessary have to ply expressways (remember, their express sectors change according to road conditions), a few of them must travel on an expressway along an express sector due to the way their route is designed. Route 30e is an example - no matter how good or bad the road conditions on and below the Keppel viaduct is, it must climb onto it at it's E.C.P. end and travel via the Benjamin Sheares Bridge (where the A.Y.E. and E.C.P. meet) to reach Fort Road, it's next stop - no other possible or logical routes exist for this sector.
