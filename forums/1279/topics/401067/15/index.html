    Originally posted by TIB770T:

    RP holiday schedule do not have the X-over slot,yes I know that,thats only for normal days.


    During RP holiday the xovers still exist, and the timings did not differ too much. This is to prevent S107 from having a totally different schedule during RP holidays though they also run under the schedule type Special1.

    In addition, when xovers dont exist during holiday periods (whether MOE schools or others), the parent service normally gets fleet reduction (e.g. 157/154 xovers). For this case, S107 isnt possible at the current point in time.
