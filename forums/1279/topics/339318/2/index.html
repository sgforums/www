    While we regulate the frequency from the interchange, factors such as adverse traffic conditions and heavy passenger loading may lead to prolonged waiting time at bus stops.  At SMRT, we continuously monitor our bus services and conduct site surveys on a regular basis to ensure that our services are able to meet the demand on the road. Please be assured that we will continue to monitor our bus services and make adjustments when necessary.
