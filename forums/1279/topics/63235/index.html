    I personally find Bishan does not need new services, unless the services are introduced by TIBS. Services 54 56 & 162 provides town coverage while Service 13 & 55 provides eastern coverage. For the west, there is Service 156 and for the north and north-east, there is Service 88.

    The pair of bus stops outside Bishan MRT acts like a mini central stop. Moreover most people in Bishan are young adults to middle-aged people who often rely on the train to get to town where most of them are working.

    The fewer number of bus services might be due to a few factors
    1. Bishan town is not as big as AMK or TP.
    2. Bishan town is a newer estate than AMK & TP
    3. Many places within Bishan North & Bishan East are within walking distances.

    Besides if they want to get to places that are served by buses only, they can take 56 or 88 to AMK or TP before transferring to another bus.
