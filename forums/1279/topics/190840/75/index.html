    smrt route renumbering exercise II [just suggest]:

    • 700A > 700C
    • 853 > 853C [UEC route]
    • 925 > 925C [sungei buloh route]
    • 961 > 961C [marina ctr route]
    • 900A > 900C [sports school route]
    • 307E > 307T [terminating route]
    • 811E,812E > 811T, 812T
    • 911E,912E,913E > 911T,912T,913T

     

    reason: to avoid confusion between two diff routes. 'T' means terminating rather than an 'E' that may mistaken as express [not all SG people know for smrt routes] 'C' means alternative route.
