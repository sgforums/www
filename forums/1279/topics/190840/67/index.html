    Slight amendment to service 18.

     

    1. It aims to provide a link from Tampines Ave 9 to Pasir Ris.

    2. It assists to further enhance the links at Bedok Nth St 3 by linking it to Tampines Ave 9 and Pasir Ris.

     

    The reason why 18 is chosen to be amended is due to several other prevailing bus services that meet up with 18 often.

     

    Some examples:

    1. Bedok Reservoir to Tampines Ave 7: 21

    2. Tampines Ave 2 to Tampines Ave 7: 8

    3. Bedok interchange to Tampines Ave 7: 38

    4. Bedok Nth Ave 3 to Tampines Ave 7 (B05 and B06 bus stops): Take 168 and alight at Tampines Ave 4.

     

    Fare stages updated as of 24/7/2008.
