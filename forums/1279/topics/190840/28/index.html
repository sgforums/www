    For your first suggestion about the broken links that affect Bedok Intercange and Kaki Bukit, maybe why not I extend the route to Bedok Interchange while still leaving the full route unchanged?

    For your second suggestion, there is a reason why I suggested of the service to make a big loop at Kaki Bukit/Chai Chee.
    a) At Bedok North Road, there is a small road (Chai Chee Street) that links to the main Chai Chee Street if you're coming in from the East. However, if it's coming in from North (Tampines Avenue 10), there's no way that the drivers could turn into the small road that I mentioned earlier. Drivers had to either do a three point turn at Bedok North Road (Bedok North Avenue 1) or turn at Bedok North Avenue 1 and then turn back in to Chai Chee Street to continue the journey.
    b) Notice that route duplication would be heavy if I just make this service continue on Bedok North Avenue 1 to New Upper Changi Road? That's why I suggested that the service should turn into Chai Chee Road. Also, if you want a short and direct way of getting from Bedok Interchange to say, the bus stop at Chai Chee Drive, it's better to have a direct way.

    However, the route duplication will be heavy once the service turns into Changi Road. There's no way the service could go to Siglap Road if it wants to fully utilise the DDs.

    As for your last suggestion, good point there. However, again there's route duplication since Service 16 and 197 ply Marine Parade Road (not sure whether Service 16 does that). But I will think about your suggestion and make necessary changes if there are any or are required.

    So, in summary, the things that I would do are:
    a) Either realter the service to make it ply the suggested route that you have suggested, or
    b) Introduce a new mid-haul service that will ply from Bedok to Sims Place through Marine Parade (especially calling at the bus stops where the former CSS 608 will call at).
