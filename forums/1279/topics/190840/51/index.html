    Between Tampines & Tuas.

    Tampines Ctrl 1

    Pioneer Rd North
    Pioneer Rd
    Tuas West Dr

    * Service may be amended to the new Joo Koon Terminal once it opens via Upp Jurong Rd, Benoi Rd, Joo Koon Rd.

    Service links Tampines, Toa Payoh and Jurong at a faster travel time for those who prefer to get a seat on a bus than to squeeze on a train.

    Service provides quick links for students from Tampines, Toa Payoh and Jurong to and from Temasek Poly, Catholic JC and Jurong JC.

    Services links IMM to Jurong West which is currently untapped by revenue bus routes besides 502.

    End to end: 1 hour 40mins. Break 12-20mins. Meal 25mins.
    Tampines - Lakeside 50mins.
    Lakeside - Tuas 50mins.
