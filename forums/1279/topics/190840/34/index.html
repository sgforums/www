    Bukit Batok Interchange to Punggol Interchange

    Direction 1

    via BB Interchange, BB East Ave 3, Toh Tuck Avenue, Commonwealth Ave West, Clementi Road, AYE (semi-express), Alexandra Road, Telok Blangah Road, Kampong Bahru Road, Eu Tong Sen Street, Hill Street, Bras Basah Road, Temasek Boulevard, East Coast Parkway, Kallang Paya Lebar Expressway, Tampines Expressway, Punggol Road, Punggol Field, Punggol Place, Punggol Interchange

    Direction 2

    via Punggol Interchange, Punggol Place, Punggol Field, Punggol Road, Tampines Expressway, Kallang Paya Lebar Expressway, East Coast Parkway, Raffles Avenue, South Bridge Road, Neil Road, Kampong Bahru Road, Telok Blangah Road, Alexandra Road, AYE (semi express), Clementi Road, Commonwealth Ave West, Toh Tuck Avenue, BB East Ave 3, BB Interchange.

    Frequency: 6-10 minutes.

    Added: Operation time: First bus BBT at 0530hrs, first bus at PGL at 0600hrs, last bus BBT at 2330hrs, last bus at PGL at 2330hrs. During festive seasons, buses will run 24-hour, in which buses departing after 2330hrs will be renumbered 982N and charge 125 percent of normal fares.

    Travel time (one direction): 70 minutes in off-peak, 90 minutes in peak.

    Buses: 23 buses A/P, 5 buses split shifts.

    Main commuters: Punggol to City, Punggol to Chinatown area (lesser), BB to Habourfront area, BB to Chinatown area. Buses will not be able to slash North East Line (25-28 minutes without waiting) Punggol to HBF/Chinatown timing (projected time 40 minutes) but will be able to do for Punggol to City (projected time 25-35 minutes). North East Line cannot reach City Hall area directly, need to switch at Dolbhy Ghaut so bus service wins over this.

    Depot: Bukit Batok area. Not a SBST nor SMRT service.
