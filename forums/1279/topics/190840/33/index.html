    Something for all the people here to ponder about.

    Service 35M
    Plys the same route as Service 35 but will ply via Changi Coast Walk to go to NSRCC Sea Sports Centre and SAFRA NSRCC. This service will be a convenient link for people who would like a direct access to these two places without having to walk down Changi Coast Walk.
