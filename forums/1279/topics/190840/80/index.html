  • tanjun
    22 Aug 08, 09:22

    Fantasy route change to service 23.

    Service 23 is chosen to be extended for the benefit of Tampines North residents and Pasir Ris East residents in the following ways.

    1. Students living in this area will have a direct link now to Temasek Poly instead of relying on 15 which ply only at Pasir Ris St 52 and Tampines Ave 4.

    2. Residents staying at the eastern ends of Singapore (Tampines and Pasir Ris) have a quick link to Little India and to the town areas.

    So, for example, to get to Orchard from Pasir Ris, simply take this 23 to Bendemeer Rd and transfer to 65, or 147 to Chinatown or 133 to Bugis or Marina Centre.

    In addition, I have perceived 23 to be a semi-express service for Tampines currently. The current route has the demand  and I hope that the benefit can be extended to the neighbouring towns.
