    I think that rerouting 160 to pass by Bukit Panjang followed by BKE then Turf Club Ave and Woodlands Rd is a good idea due to several reasons.

     

    1. Its route will be more unique instead of duplicating 170, 960, 961 from Ten Mile Junction all the way to Kranji MRT station.

     

    2. Residents residing near Bangkit Rd do not have direct bus to Jurong East interchange. The best direct bus is by 187 to Jurong East Central followed by a 2 bus stop distance walk from there to Jurong East.

     

    3. As a result of this rerouting, residents residing along Bukit Panjang can utilise 160 to Johor instead of taking 960 to transfer to 160 or 170 along Woodlands Rd.

     

    But sadly, Bukit Panjang is a SMRT dominated territory.
