    Some route suggestions:

    Svc 321 - Hougang Central to Defu Ave 1

    (Two-directional Weekday peak hour services with a frequrncy of 25-30 minutes)

    AM Peak: Hougang Ctrl Int, Upp Serangoon Rd, Hougang Ave 3, Tampines Rd, Defu Ave 1

    PM Peak: Defu Ave 1, Tampines Rd, Hougang Ave 3, Upp Serangoon Rd, Hougang Ctrl Int.

    Svc 141 - Geylang Lor 1 to Potong Pasir (Loop)

    Geylang Lor 1 Ter, Sims Ave, Sims Way, Jln Kolam Ayer, Upper Serangoon Rd, Potong Pasir Ave 1, Ave 3 and Ave 2 (Loop)

    And return via Upp Serangoon Rd, Jln Kolam Ayer, Sims Way, Geylang rd and Geylang Lor 1 Ter.

    The purpose of this svc is to link Potong Pasir with East-West line MRT (142 links the town to North South line) and provide a service in Sims Way which is currently not plied by any services.

    Svc 71 - Yio Chu Kang to Tagore Lane

    Weekdays two-directional peak hour service with 20-25 minutes frequency.

    Morning peak - Yio Chu Kang Ter, AMK Ave 6, YCK Rd, Upp Thomson Rd, Tagore Dr, Tagore industrial Ave, Tagore Lane

    Evening Peak - Tagore Dr, Tagore Industrial Ave, Tagore Lane, Tagore Rd, Upp Thomson Rd, YCK Rd, AMK Ave 6 and Yio Chu Kang Ter.
