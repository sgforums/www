    Some suggestions for easing the heavy load of MRT during AM peak hour from Boon Lay to CBD.
    Jurong West St 91 - Fullerton Rd
    Jurong West St 91, St 92, St 91, Ave 5, St 72, Ave 4, Jln Boon Lay (Express), AYE (Express), Anson Rd, Robinson Rd & Fullerton Rd

    Jurong West St 62 - Fullerton Rd
    Jurong West St 62, St 61, Ave 4, Pioneer Rd North, Ave 5, St 81, St 82, St 75, St 64, Ctrl 1 (Express), Jln Boon Lay (Express), AYE (Express), Anson Rd, Robinson Rd & Fullerton Rd.

    Operating hours:

    Monday to Fridays 0700 - 0900 hours
    Frequency: 15 minutes
    Fare: $1.50 flat fare (NOT a premium service so standing is allowed)

    Reason that I came out with this suggestion is due to every morning peak hours Boon Lay MRT is loading with people. Most of the time the MRT is full when departing from Boon Lay and people from Jurong East, Clementi etc are unable to onboard the train. For most of them are getting alighting in CBD hence this idea for easing the MRT load.
