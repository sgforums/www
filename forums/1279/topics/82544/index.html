    Yishun RIng Rd
    Yishun Ave 9
    Yishun Ctrl
    Yishun Ave 2
    Yishun Int

    The link had not been named yet. It will link ave 6 to ring rd, and follows the path of the canal and the khatib bongsu park connector situated besides it.
