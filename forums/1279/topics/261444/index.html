    SMRT Buses . (rigids)

    Best Worst IMO .

    Mercedes Benz O405 Volgren . One of the few O405s that have a very modern looking interior .

    Mercedes Benz O405 Hispano Box OAC . A/c very good , shiok .

    Mercedes Benz O405 Hispano Box CAC . Altho a/c lousy , i enjoy their quick acceleration and good suspension .

    Dennis Lance UK/Volgren . A powerful bus with a powerful acceleration , but lousy brakes .

    Dennis Lance UMW . Its powerful acceleration compensates its ugly interior and lousy suspension .

    Hino Volgren . A rather nice bus to take .

    Scania L113CRL ELBO . I find this bus a nicer bus to take than its Strider brother . Only downside is its horrendous rattling and poor aircon .

    DAFs . This bus has a rather good acceleration and top speed . But i dont really like its bodywork design and leaky a/c . Suspension quite good IMO .

    Scania L113CRL Strider . This bus has a rather good aircon , but as above , i dont like its bodywork . Also , sitting at the back of the bus for long distance can be very demanding .

    UDs. Needless to say .
