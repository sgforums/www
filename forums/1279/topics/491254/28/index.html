    Originally posted by SMB3163D:

    979 is under BSEP hence the BSEP logo on 979M.

    SMRT does not use M suffix for such modification, instead uses C for Sunday & PH routes (853/C, 925/C, 961/C) or as per "short working trips" or alternative routes (859/A/B, 700/A). These suffix do not appear on EDS at all for the former.
