    * This service provides MRT connection at Paya Lebar for EWL, Macpherson for CCL/DTL3 and Kaki Bukit for DTL3. 

    * The service is made to ply Bartley road east given the high number of lodges/dorms in the area + many new industrial buildings TOP in last 2 years + coming in 2017

    * This is primarily an industrial service, hence suggest it is full fleet DD with high frequency of 08-10 mins to begin with and off-peak/weekends to have frequency of 17-20 mins.
