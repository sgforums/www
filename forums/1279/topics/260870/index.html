    Yes, it states the expiry date... but only if the intended transfer date is later than the expiry date

    http://vrl.lta.gov.sg/lta/vrl/action/pubfunc?ID=EnquireTransferFee
    [You have to retype the url before it works]

    Step 1
    Enter these:
    Vehicle No.(e.g.): SBS881A
    Intended Transfer Date (e.g.): 08122010 (DDMMYYYY)

    [For the year, just put something like 2222. It will be accepted]

    Step 2
    Below the Terms & Conditions box, click Agree

    This message will then show:
    WR03822: Intended transfer date must be on or before lifespan expiry date 07 Jun 2010.

    Underlined portion is the lifespan expiry date
