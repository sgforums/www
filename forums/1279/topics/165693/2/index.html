    The actual fact is this:-

    LTA will call a contract for the upgrading of existing MRT stations to be disabled friendly. The Contract Sum is fully paid for out of the Government's Coffers. This Contract Sum is paid to the sucessful Contractor who tender for the job, and carries out the full scope of the work as per the Contract Specifications.

    When the whole Contract is completed, there is a 1 year defects liablity period (or called DLP Period for short). What this means is that at the end of the Contract, the works will be handed over to the operator (Which is SMRT). SMRT will operates equipment and within this 1 year period, if any problems occurs with the equipment, the Contractor is supposed to rectify it at no cost to either SMRT or LTA (if deemed to be a construction or manufacturer's defect).

    After this 1 year, SMRT will have to handle the maintenance part, whereby they will have to pay for the maintenance of the euipment and servicing. LTA does not pay for it.

    If like what you have siad, tha the Contractors pay for it, then that means we don't need the current Government ......

    Oh yeah, btw, LTA manages the Contract. They will have supervisors who will have to ensure that the works are carried out as per the Contract Specifications and they will also certify that the work is done so that the Contractor will get paied for his labour.
