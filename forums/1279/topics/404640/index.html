    There should be only one card for SBS Transit, known as the Waybill. Everything including safety message and first/last parade, etc is integrated into it. The chinese text is simply the same meaning as English, just translated.

    For the A-shift BC, he will pass on the waybill to his buddy BC who is doing the P-shift till the end of duty.

    The first and last parade is simply just this : The BC is responsible for checking that the bus is in good condition for revenue service (first parade), and to report any faults when the bus returns to depot at the end of its run (last parade).
