    I don't have a clue as to how SBST deployment works, But will there be a chance from other buses coming down from other districts, Like SBS 9417Z from BNDEP, SBS9463P(BN) SBS9520H(BN) or (HG)SBS9478Y etc coming down to SL, Other than HG,BB/AR buses?

    9417Z used to be BN 65, but has been stuck in AMDEP for many years already..now AM 25 btw. 

    The thing is, West district depots (HGDEP / BBDEP / ARBP / SLBP) they usually swap buses among themselves, ditto for the East district depots (AMDEP/ BRBP / BNDEP). Very seldom in recent years have they did cross-district swaps..the last i remembered was 8242S which went over to AM 130 from HGDEP, that was a cross-district swap from West (HG) to East district (AMDEP).
    The influx of Mk4 DM3500s & Striders from BNDEP to BBDEP for the JB-cross-border fleet renewal years ago was another case, since they had the need to do so to replace the outgoing JB Mk3s. But other than those 2 cases, I don't remember any cross-district swaps lately..

    It's not that the cross-district swaps won't happen, they do happen but in recent years it's very rare. Unless there is an urgent need for buses from other depots for a particular purpose (like Mk4s from all the diff depots to BBDEP for Sv 160 & 170 fleet renewal), the East-district buses are more likely to stay in the East district depots no matter how they swap. 

    And looking at the still-significant amount of SP VO3xs BBDEP & HGDEP still have now, and their amount of SP Wrights, I won't bet against more VO3xs from these 2 depots going over to SLBP. SLBP already laid-up quite a significant number of LO3xs & VO2xs..so I guess they won't have any severe shortage of buses anytime soon given the number of SPs SLBP now have to go around. So I think the cross-district swap for LO3xs replacement might not happen..but within the West district itself, yes very likely unless they decide to let all the JIS go WAB suddenly with new Wrights.
