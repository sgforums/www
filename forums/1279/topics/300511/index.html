    Route of service 242 poorly planned

    I COMPLETELY agree with Ms Jennie Lee Hin Ming on her feedback on the unreliable feeder service 242 from Jurong West Extension to Boon Lay Interchange (my paper, Jan 9).

    In addition to the problems she has raised, the bus frequently gets caught in traffic jams along Jalan Boon Lay in the morning.

    This turns a 15-minute journey into a 45-minute one.

    This is highly frustrating, especially since there is little need for service 242 to go down Jalan Boon Lay at all.

    There are only two bus stops along Boon Lay Way, and few passengers get on or off there.

    This is an example of poor route planning.

    Feeder services must be improved before we can claim to have a world-class transport system.

    - Ms Rachel Eileen Poon Hiu Mun
