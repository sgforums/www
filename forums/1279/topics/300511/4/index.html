    ROAD CLOSURE ON SUNDAY

     

    EU TONG SEN ST and NEW BRIDGE ROAD will be closed from 3pm on Sunday to midnight because of a celebration to mark the 15th day of Chinese New Year.

     

    Fifteen SBS Transit services will be diverted because of the Chinatown Yuan Xiao Celebrations.

     

    They are: 2, 12, 33, 54, 63, 80, 124, 143, 145, 147, 166, 197, CT8, CT18 and CT28.

     

    West bound services will use Upper Pickering Street, South Bridge Road, Neil Rd and Kreta Ayer Rd. Those headed east will use Outram Rd, Chin Swee Rd & Havelock Rd.

     

    Passengers can board the affected buses at bus stops along the diverted routes. SBST will also deploy 20 traffic inspectors to help passengers along diverted routes.
