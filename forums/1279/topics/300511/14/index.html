    FORMULA 1 Grand Prix
    A series of road closures affecting the Marina Centre area will take place from 20 September to 2 October 2008 to facilitate the setting up and dismantling of the race infrastructure.

    A total of 27 public bus services travelling to the Padang and Marina Bay areas will be diverted to the nearest adjacent route. Commuters affected by the bus diversions and road closures also have the option of using a free bridging bus service which connects the City Hall and Bugis MRT stations to the Suntec-Marina Bay and Millenia areas. This bridging bus service will operate from 23 September to 29 September.

    LTA Press Release: http://app.lta.gov.sg/corp_press_content.asp?start=2001

    Restricted Acess into Woodlands CIQ via Woodlands Centre Road (Slip road).

     

    Note that cars could still acess the slip road if they are travelling to Woodlands Road.

     

    From 15th Sept, no acess is granted into the checkpoint from the sliproad at Woodlands Centre Road which connects with the junction at Woodlands Checkpoint Viaduct. Only Sv 160, 170 and 950 are allowed to acess via it.

     

    Cars and bikes could only acess via BKE. Lorries would access from old Woodlands Road.

     

    This rule is created due to many complaints by passengers, buisnesses and institutions in the affected CIQ area of noise, inconvinience, etc. SMRT recieved complaints by passengers of long waiting time and inconviniencee caused by the diversions.

     

    Buisnesses like Sheng Siong, Andalus, Elias Kindergarten and a few others complained due to drop of customers and inconvinience for customers.

     

    Rule operating hours:

    Mon - Fri: 4 - 10 pm

    Sat and Sun: 10 am - 10 pm
