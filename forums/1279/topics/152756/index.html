    MOUNTBATTEN RD - 'GAY WORLD PK' (from TL Guide 2004)
    Gay World Park has been sold in 2000 and demolished. It was an amusement park in the 1950s till the 1970s. When Gay World Pk closed as an amusement park, the place was operated as a car repair and motor workshops in the 1980s till the 1990s.

    MARGARET DR - 'GOLDEN CITY THEATRE' (from TL Guide 1995)
    Golden City Theatre was since converted to a church after it closed somewhere in the 1980s.

    OLD AIRPORT RD - 'BLK 29'
    Blk 29 has been demolished in 2000 for future developments from HDB. (Blk 29 is Kallang Airport Est - 2nd Blk.)

    BENDEMEER RD - 'BLK 10'
    Blk 10 Kempas Rd has also been demolished. Commentary as above. No longer mentioned in TL guide 2004.
