Bus Diversion due to Marina Coastal Expressway

Next
  • TPS Timothy Mok
    28 Nov 13, 18:06

    The Marina Coastal Expressway will be opening in 29 December 2013. In view of these, there will be changes to bus routes.

    30: Will be rerouted via ECP, MCE and AYE.
    502: Will be diverted as Bayfront Ave > Central Blvd > Marina Stn Rd > Marina Street > Marina Place > Marina Blvd > MCE > AYE >
    513 (to Shenton Way): Will be diverted as ECP > Sheares Ave > Central Blvd >
    513 (to Tampines): Will be diverted as Marina Blvd > Sheares Ave > ECP >
    518: Will be diverted as Bayfront Ave > Marina Boulevard > Sheares Ave > ECP >
    538, 539, 544, 545, 552, 553, 554, 566, 585: Will be diverted as ECP > MCE > Maxwell Rd.
    NR1, NR6: ... > Keppel Rd > Anson Rd > Maxwell Rd > Marina Stn Rd > Central Blvd >
