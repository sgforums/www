    I only have copies of the TransitLink Guide up to 1993. beyond that i can't be of service to you ppl.

    ( ) denotes non-stop sector.

    FEEDER SERVICES
    ------------------------

    SERVICE 221:
    221G (Bedok - Bedok North Ave 3): Bedok Interchange, (Bedok North Ave 1), New Upper Changi Rd, Bedok North Rd, Bedok North Ave 3, Bedok North St 1 and Bedok Interchange.
    221W (Bedok - Bedok North Ave 3): Bedok Interchange, Bedok North St 1, Bedok North Ave 3, Bedok North Rd, New Upper Changi Rd, (Bedok North Ave 1) and Bedok Interchange.
    NOTE: Now part of Townlink 222 since 1997.

    SERVICE 266 (Ang Mo Kio - Ang Mo Kio Ave 5):
    Ang Mo Kio Interchange, Ang Mo Kio Ave 3, Ave 4, Yio Chu Kang Rd, Ave 5, Ave 4, Ave 3, (Ave 8 ) and Ang Mo Kio Interchange.
    NOTE: Withdrawn in 2002 due to the shifting of Ang Mo Kio Interchange to Ang Mo Kio Temporary Interchange 400 metres away along Ang Mo Kio Ave 8. Route now mainly covered by SBST 138, 265 and 269.

    SERVICE 267 (Ang Mo Kio - Ang Mo Kio Ind Park 2):
    Ang Mo Kio Interchange, Ang Mo Kio Ave 8, Ave 5, Ind Park 2, Ave 5, Ave 8 and Ang Mo Kio Interchange.
    NOTE: Amended in 2002 due to the shifting of Ang Mo Kio Interchange to Ang Mo Kio Temporary Interchange 400 metres away along Ang Mo Kio Ave 8. Now part of Townlink 268.

    SERVICE 290 (Tampines - Simei Estate):
    Tampines Interchange, (Tampines Central 1 and 3), Ave 7, Ave 2, (St 31), Simei Rd, Simei St 1, Simei St 3, St 4, Simei Rd, (Tampines St 31), Ave 2, Ave 7, (Central 1 and 3) and Tampines Interchange.
    NOTE: Now part of SBST 38 since 2002.

    SERVICE 294 (Tampines - Tampines St 21):
    Tampines Interchange, (Tampines Central 1 and 2), Ave 5, Ave 4, St 21, Ave 2, St 23, St 21, Ave 4, Ave 5, (Central 1 and 2) and Tampines Interchange.
    NOTE: Now part of SBST 3 since 2001.

    SERVICE 295 (Tampines - Tampines Ave 8 ):
    Tampines Interchange, (Tampines Central 1 and 2), Ave 5, St 83, Ave 3, Ave 8, (Central 1 and 2) and Tampines Interchange.
    NOTE: Now part of Townlink 291 since 1996.

    SERVICE 297 (Tampines - Tampines St 45):
    Tampines Interchange, (Tampines Central 1 and 3), Ave 7, St 41, Ave 9, St 45, Ave 7, Ave 9, St 42, St 43, Ave 9, St 41, Ave 7, (Central 1 and 3) and Tampines Interchange.
    NOTE: Now part of Townlink 293 since 1996.

    SERVICE 301 (Choa Chu Kang - Teck Whye Lane):
    Choa Chu Kang Interchange, (Choa Chu Kang Loop, Ave 4), Choa Chu Kang Way, (Ave 3), Central, (Ave 1), Tech Whye Lane, (Choa Chu Kang Ave 1), Central, (Ave 3), Choa Chu Kang Way, (Ave 4, Loop) and Choa Chu Kang Interchange.
    NOTE: Now part of Townlink 307 since 1997.

    SERVICE 303 (Choa Chu Kang - Choa Chu Kang North 5):
    Choa Chu Kang Interchange, Choa Chu Kang Dr, North 5, Dr, Ave 4 and Choa Chu Kang Interchange.
    NOTE: Introduced in 1994; Now part of Townlink 307 since 1997.

    SERVICE 323 (Hougang South - Defu Ave 1):
    Hougang South Interchange, (Hougang St 21), Tampines Rd, Defu Ave 1, SBST Hougang Depot (HGDEP), Defu Ave 1, Tampines Rd, (Hougang St 21) and Hougang South Interchange.
    NOTE: Withdrawn in Feb 2002; formerly known as SBS 329 in 1980s.

    SERVICE 331/ 241 (Jurong East - Teban Gardens):
    Jurong East Interchange, (Jurong East St 13), Jurong East Central, Town Hall Rd, Teban Gardens Rd, Penjuru Rd, West Coast Rd, Pandan Gardens, Jurong Town Hall Rd, East Central, (East St 13) and Jurong East Interchange.
    NOTE: Service number change from 241 to 331 in 1999; Withdrawn in 2002, Route now mainly covered SBST 51 and 143 since.

    SERVICE 343 (Bt Panjang - Petir Rd):
    Bt Panjang Terminal at Petir Rd, Petir Rd, Gangsa Rd, Bt Panjang Rd, Pending Rd, Petir Rd (U-turn at Petir Rd End) and Bt Panjang Terminal.
    NOTE: Route adjusted and renumbered as 921 since 2000 after the opening of Bukit Panjang Bus Interchange.

    SERVICE 344 (Bt Panjang - Bt Panjang Ring Rd):
    Bt Panjang Terminal at Petir Rd, Petir Rd, Gangsa Rd, Bt Panjang Rd, Bangkit Rd, Bt Panjang Ring Rd, Bangkit Rd, (Bt Panjang Ring Rd), Pending Rd, Petir Rd and Bt Panjang Terminal.
    NOTE: Introduced in 1994; Route withdrawn in 1999 due to opening of Bukit Panjang LRT system. Route now mainly covered by 920, which was introduced in 2000 together with the opening of Bukit Panjang Bus Interchange.

    SERVICE 345 (Bt Panjang - Saujana Rd):
    Bt Panjang Terminal at Petir Rd, Petir Rd, Gangsa Rd, Bt Panjang Rd, Bt Panjang Ring Rd, Fajar Rd, Bt Panjang Ring Rd, Jelapang Rd, Saujana Rd, Bt Panjang Ring Rd, Pending Rd and Bt Panjang Terminal.
    NOTE: Introduced in 1997; Route withdrawn in 1999 due to opening of Bukit Panjang LRT system.

    SERVICE 350 (Pasir Ris - Pasir Ris Dr 4):
    Pasir Ris Interchange, Pasir Ris Dr 3, Pasir Ris Close, Pasir Ris Dr 6, Dr 1, Dr 4, Dr 3, Pasir Ris Close, Pasir Ris Dr 3 and Pasir Ris Interchange.
    NOTE: Service will only ply to Pasir Ris Close from 0900 to 2100 hrs on weekdays and 0900 to 0030 hrs on Saturdays, Sundays and eve of Public Holidays.
    NOTE: Now split into 2 services since 1997 - SBST 354 serves Pasir Ris Close while SBST Townlink 358 covers the other areas previously covered by Service 350.

    SERVICE 351 (Pasir Ris - Pasir Ris St 11):
    Pasir Ris Interchange, (Pasir Ris Dr 3), Pasir Ris Central, Dr 1, St 11, Dr 1, Pasir Ris Central, (Pasir Ris Dr 3) and Pasir Ris Interchange.
    NOTE: Now part of Townlink 359 since 1997.

    SERVICE 352 (Pasir Ris - Pasir Ris St 21):
    Pasir Ris Interchange, Pasir Ris Dr 3, St 21, (Dr 2), Dr 1, Loyang Ave, Pasir Ris Dr 3, (Dr 2), St 21, Dr 3 and Pasir Ris Interchange.
    NOTE: Introduced in 1994; Became part of Townlink 357 since 1997; Now part of SBST 3 since 2002.

    SERVICE 353 (Pasir Ris - Pasir Ris Dr 10):
    Pasir Ris Interchange, Pasir Ris Dr 3, Dr 10, Dr 1, Elias Rd, Dr 3 and Pasir Ris Interchange.
    NOTE: Introduced in 1994 due to new developments in Pasir Ris West; Now part of Townlink 358 since 1997.

    SERVICE 372 (Woodlands - Marsiling Rd):
    Woodlands Interchange (then, at Woodlands Checkpoint), (Woodlands Centre Rd, Woodlands Rd), Woodlands Centre Rd, Admiralty Rd, Marsiling Dr, Marsiling Lane, Marsiling Rd, Marsiling Dr, Admiralty Rd, (Woodlands Centre Rd, Woodlands Rd) and Woodlands Interchange.
    NOTE: It looked like the first feeder service introduced in Woodlands Estate by SBS before TIBS took over. It continued to use this number until the Woodlands MRT Extension and Regional Interchange began operations on 10 Mar 1996, in which it was adjusted to operate from the new interchange as TIBS 903.

    SERVICE 378 (Jalan Kayu - Picadilly Rd):
    Jalan Kayu Terminal, Jalan Kayu, Picadilly Rd (U-turn at Medical & Dental Centre), Jalan Kayu and Jalan Kayu Terminal.
    NOTE: Was numbered 214E. TIBS took over it on 17th Dec 1995 together with all the Jalan Kayu services and renumbered it as 820 in 1999. SBS Transit took it over again in 2000 when they were awarded the NEL contract in exchange for Bt Batok and CCK bus services and renumbered it as 378, which is under the Sengkang Route Group. To be integrated with SBST 103 and 379 on 7th Dec 2003.

    SERVICE 379 (Jalan Kayu - Seletar Wast Camp):
    Jalan Kayu Terminal, Jalan Kayu, New West Camp Rd, West Camp Rd, New West Camp Rd, Jalan Kayu and Jalan Kayu Terminal.
    NOTE: Was numbered 214W. TIBS took over it on 17th Dec 1995 together with all the Jalan Kayu services and renumbered it as 821 in 1999. SBS Transit took it over again in 2000 when they were awarded the NEL contract in exchange for Bt Batok and CCK bus services and renumbered it as 379, which is under the Sengkang Route Group. To be integrated with SBST 103 and 378 on 7th Dec 2003.

    SERVICE 382 (Eunos - Ubi Ave 1):
    Eunos Interchange, (Eunos Rd 2), Sims Ave, Jalan Eunos, Eunos Link, Ubi Ave 2, Ave 1, Rd 3, Ave 2, Eunos Link, Jalan Eunos, Changi Rd, Jalan Turi, (Sims Ave, Eunos Rd 2) and Eunos Interchange.
    NOTE: Now part of SBST 63 since 2002.

    SERVICE 385 (Eunos - Bedok Reservoir Rd):
    Eunos Interchange, (Eunos Rd 2), Sims Ave, Jalan Eunos, Bedok Reservoir Rd (Loop at N6), Jalan Eunos, Changi Rd, Jalan Turi, (Sims Ave, Eunos Rd 2) and Eunos Interchange.
    NOTE: Now part of SBST 60 since 2002.
