    SERVICE TIBS SS1 (Yio Chu Kang - Yishun (Loop)):
    [1988] Yio Chu Kang Terminal, Ang Mo Kio Ave 6, Lentor Ave, Yishun Ave 2, Central, Ave 9, Ring Rd, Ave 3, Ave 2, Lentor Ave, Ang Mo Kio Ave 6, (Ave 8 ) and Yio Chu Kang Terminal.
    NOTE: Introduced in 1986-87. Withdrawn in 1989 after inauguration of Yishun extension on 20th Dec 1988.

    SERVICE SS2 (Clementi - Jurong East St 32 (Loop)):
    [1988] Clementi Interchange, (Clementi Ave 3, Commonwealth Ave West, Boon Lay Way), Jurong East Central, Jurong East Ave 1, Jurong East St 32, Jurong East Ave 1, Jurong East Central, (Boon Lay Way, Commonwealth Ave West, Clementi Ave 3) and Clementi Interchange.
    NOTE: Introduced in 1986-87. Withdrawn in 1989.

    SERVICE SS3 (Clementi - Bt Batok West Ave 7 (Loop)):
    [1988] Clementi Interchange, (Clementi Ave 3, Commonwealth Ave West, Toh Tuck Ave, Bt Batok East Ave 3), Bt Batok East Ave 2, East Ave 5, West Ave 5, West Ave 7, West Ave 4, West Ave 2, West Ave 5, East Ave 5, East Ave 2, (Bt Batok East Ave 3, Toh Tuck Ave, Commonwealth Ave West, Clementi Ave 3) and Clementi Interchange.
    NOTE: Introduced in 1986-87. Withdrawn in 1990.

    SERVICE 318 (Serangoon - Serangoon Gardens Way (Loop)):
    [1988] Serangoon Interchange, (Serangoon Central), Boundary Rd, Lor Chuan, Serangoon Gardens Way, Lor Chuan, Boundary Rd, (Serangoon Central) and Serangoon Interchange.
    NOTE: Introduced in 1986-87 when Serangoon Interchange shifted. Then, SBS 315 plied straight to Serangoon North Ave 1 via Yio Chu Kang Link/Rd. Integrated with SBS 315 in 1988.
