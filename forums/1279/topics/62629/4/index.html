    From Jln Bahru Selatan, there are two ways of reaching Jurong Rd. Jln Bahru Utara branches towards the west and Jln Lama branches towards the east. Jurong 10-mile market is located near the junction of Jurong Rd and Jln Lama.

    Jln Bahru Selatan and Jln Bahru Utara (from West Coast Rd to Jurong Rd) were renamed Jurong Town Hall Rd between 1974-1976 but was probably cut short later when Jurong East Central area & Bt Batok New Town were developed.

    To understand it more clearly or if u still have doubts (1971 guide has a map which i think is very well-done), please visit National Reference Library and go to Current Affairs counter to make a request for them to retrieve the closed-stack titles for you. Bus Guides for 195?, 1969, 1971, 1974 and 1976 are available.
