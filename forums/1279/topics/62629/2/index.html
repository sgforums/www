    NOTE: Service was renumbered to service 36 in 1998

    SERVICE 123 (St Michael's - New Bridge Rd):
    St Michael's Terminal, Whampoa Rd, Jalan Bahagia, Kim Keat Rd, Balestier Rd, Moulmein Rd, Newton Rd, Scotts Rd, Orchard Rd, Clemenceau Ave, Havelock Rd, Outram Rd and New Bridge Rd Terminal.
    And return via Outram Rd, Havelock Rd, Clemenceau Ave, Orchard Rd, Scotts Rd, Newton Rd, Moulmein Rd, Balaestier Rd, Whampoa Dr, Jalan Bahagia, Whampoa Rd and St Michael's Terminal.
    NOTE: Withdrawn on 4 April 1993 between St Michael & Scotts Rd; withdrawn route covered by SBST124. The New ammended service operate between New Bridge Rd & Tomlinson Rd. However was later converted into Loop services between Bukit Merah to Orchard.

    SERVICE 124 (St Michael's - New Bridge Rd):
    St Michael's Terminal, Whampoa Rd, Jalan Bahagia, Kim Keat Rd, Balestier Rd, Thomson Rd, Stevens Rd, Scotts Rd, Orchard Rd, North Bridge Rd and New Bridge Rd Terminal.
    And return via North Bridge Rd, Stamford Rd, Orchard Rd, Penang Rd, Sommerset Rd, Orchard Link, Scotts Rd, Stevens Rd, Thomson Rd, Balaestier Rd, Whampoa Dr, Jalan Bahagia, Whampoa Rd and St Michael's Terminal.
    NOTE: Was diverted to Moulmein Rd, Newton Rd & Scotts Rd instead of Whitley rd & Stevens Rd. Also extendsion of service to Bt Merah Interchange via Lower Delta Rd, Telok Blangah Way & Henderson Rd with EFFECT from 4 April 1993.

    SERVICE 125 (St Michael's - Bt Merah Interchange):
    St Michael's Terminal, Whampoa Rd, Jalan Bahagia, Kim Keat Rd, Balestier Rd, Moulmein Rd, Thomson Rd, Kg Java Rd, Bt Timah Rd, Bencoolen St, Bras Basah Rd, North Bridge Rd, Raffles Place, Shenton Way, Keppel Rd, Telok Blangah Rd, Henderson Rd and Bt Merah Terminal.
    And return via Henderson Rd, Telok Blangah Rd, Keppel Rd, Anson Rd, Cecil St, Fullerton Rd, Stamford Rd, Prinsep St, Selegie Rd, Bt Timah Rd, Thomson Rd, Moulmein Rd, Balaestier Rd, Whampoa Dr, Jalan Bahagia, Whampoa Rd and St Michael's Terminal.
    NOTE: Was diverted to Lavender St, Jalan Besar, Ophir Rd (Along Rochor Rd in reverse direction), Beach Rd and Terminate at Shenton Way Terminal. This service was also converted to a day service on 4 April 1993.
    With the implementation Of NEL in 2003, sevice 125 route was replanned to serve between St Michael's Terminal to Sims Dr as a Loop Service.


    SERVICE 131 (St Michael's - Shenton Way):
    St Michael's Terminal, Whampoa Rd, Jalan Bahagia, Kim Keat Rd, Balestier Rd, Thomson Rd, Owen Rd, Kg Java Rd, Bt Timah Rd, Sungei Rd, Bencoolen St, Brash Basah Rd, Collyer Quay, Raffles Quay and Shenton Way Terminal.
    And return via Anson Rd, Cecil St, Fullerton Rd, Stamford Rd, Prinsep St, Selegie Rd, Serangoon Rd, Rangoon Rd, Kent Rd, Owen Rd, Thomson Rd, Balaestier Rd, Whampoa Dr, Jalan Bahagia, Whampoa Rd and St Michael's Terminal.
    NOTE: Extended to Bt Merah Interchange on 4 April 1993 via Keppel Rd, Telok Blangah Rd, Henderson Rd, Telok Blangah Way, Lower Delta Rd, Jalan Bt Merah. The service was extended to cover the withdrawn section of service 125 between Shenton Way & Bt Merah.

These are my findings (it's late in the night and i'm tired, so i won't really elaborate, jus listing):

    * Chin Swee Rd had no bus services listed on the map.

    * It appears that service 2 used to ply beyond New Bridge Rd from the eastern area towards the west beyond Commonwealth via Jalan Bt Merah and Queensway.

    * It appears that there was a fully air-conditioned service 3 that used to ply from the eastern area towards the west beyond Commonwealth via Geylang, Kallang Rd, North Bridge Rd/Victoria St, Chinatown, Kampong Bahru Rd, Jalan Bt Merah and Queensway.

    * It appears that there was a service 28 that operates from New Bridge Rd Ter to some area in the east, via Eu Tong Sen St/South Bridge Rd, Hill St, Victoria St/North Bridge Rd, Kallang Rd, Sims Ave/Geylang Rd, and continue towards the east.

    * It appears that there was a service 41 that operates from Bukit Merah Int to some area in the east, probably Bedok, via Jalan Bt Merah, Kampong Bahru Rd, Tiong Bahru Rd, Outram Rd, Eu Tong Sen St/South Bridge Rd, Hill St, Victoria St/North Bridge Rd, Kallang Rd, Sims Ave/Geylang Rd, Mountbatten Rd, and continue towards the east.

    * It appears that there was a service 50 that operates from Bukit Merah Int to some area in the east, via Jalan Bt Merah, Kampong Bahru Rd, Tiong Bahru Rd, Outram Rd, Cantonment Rd, Anson Rd/Shenton Way, Robinson Rd/Raffles Quay, Collyer Quay, St. Andrew's Rd/Connaught Dr, Beach Rd, Crawford St, Kallang Rd, Sims Ave/Geylang Rd, and continue towards the east.

    * It appears that services 61, 64 and 154 used to terminate at MacPherson Estate Terminal at Circuit Rd.

    * It appears that service 71 from Old Yio Chu Kang Rd Terminal terminates at Geylang Lor 1 Terminal.

    * It appears that service 72 used to ply Tanjong Rhu before SBST 158 took over.

    * It appears that service 91 terminates at Geylang Lor 1 Terminal.

    * It appears that service 103 used to ply beyond New Bridge Rd from the north-eastern area towards the west beyond Commonwealth via Outram Rd, Tiong Bahru Rd, Alexandra Rd and Commonwealth Ave.

    * It appears that there was a service 118 that operates from the north-eastern area to Tomlinson Rd, and from what i know, is the first air-con bus service in Singapore (SBST always have the ad on their LCD TVs in the NEL trains, "warm response to a cool ride", when they show the milestones they've been through)

    * It appears that there was a service 124 used to ply Stevens Rd.

    * It appears that service 167 used to terminate at World Trade Centre Terminal via Cantonment Rd, Kampong Bahru Rd and Telok Blangah Rd. Now it terminates at Bt Merah Int.

    * Service 171 used to terminate at Queen St Terminal from Yishun during the SBS days (i remember clearly that now).

    * It appears that there was a service 173 that operates from New Bridge Rd Ter to somewhere?

    * It appears that there was a service 179 that operates from New Bridge Rd Ter to some area in the west, probably Jalan Anak Bt or Bt Panjang, via Chinatown, Stamford Rd/Bras Basah Rd, Orchard Rd/Orchard Boulevard, Scotts Rd, Bt Timah Rd/Dunearn Rd, and continue towards Jalan Anak Bt direction.

    * It appears that there was a service 180 that operates from World Trade Centre Ter to some area in the west, probably Jalan Anak Bt or Bt Panjang, via Keppel Rd, Shenton Way, Upp Cross St, Clemenceau Ave, Selegie Rd/Bencoolen St, Bt Timah Rd/Dunearn Rd and continue towards Jalan Anak Bt direction.

    * It appears that there was a service 184 that operates from Bt Merah Int Ter to somewhere via Kampong Bahru Rd?

    * It appears that there was a service 189 that used to ply from Marina Centre Terminal towards the west beyond Commonwealth via Shenton Way, Cantonment Rd, Neil Rd, Kampong Bahru Rd, Jalan Bt Merah and Queensway.

    * It appears that service 198 used to terminate at Sims Drive Terminal, travelling past Aljuned Rd and Jalan Besar, operating to probably Boon Lay.
