    Sorry dude, but BusPlus as the third public bus operator? Nah, absolutely impossible.

    First, BusPlus is an exclusive express service designed and catered for the middle-to-upper class commuters. Hence the slogan "Business Class On The Roads". Bear in mind also that BusPlus is a wholly-owned subsidiary of TIBS. As you can see right now, TIBS is closing down CSS, which can be considered as a "low-fare" bus company. For BusPlus, which is serving a different market than CSS, to take over the CSS bus operations is illogical.

    Secondly, it is technically impossible for the minibuses to be fitted with 2 doors, let alone an outswing plug door! The buses already has the EDS installed. And again, BusPlus serves a different niche than the trunk services. BusPlus does operates a couple of so-called "recreation services", though it is more of a shuttle service. Among them are:- Bugis MRT-Big Splash & Tampines MRT-Loyang Fish Farm.

    Lastly, when you say remake, what do you actually mean? It is rather sound to say SBST should buy NEW articulated buses, like the Scania L94UA or the Volvo B12 artic. And the authorities have called upon SBST and TIBS to purchase ONLY low floor buses for their fleet. For their diecast models, SBST- can be bought during their coffee evenings as well as from their Corporate Communications Department. As for TIBS, I don't think it is available even as we speak. I'm still eagerly waiting for their release of the Habit Diecast Model.

    Cheers and warm regards
