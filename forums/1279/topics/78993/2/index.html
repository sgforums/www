    What more school bus service than Service 158 between Kallang and Dunman High School. With TWO shortworking trips (158A and 158B) specially customised for us.

    The morning trip even calls at stops in the "opposite" direction-- It calls at Kallang MRT and Outside DHS, both stops towards SGN, even though the bus is in the Tg Rhu direction. It also skips the stop at Fort Rd.

    During the "peak periods", the bus can be 90-100% Dunmanians for both full route and short trip between the school and Kallang Stn. Very packed at times, very roudy also.

    Other than my good ol 158, here are others:

    Service 60: Eunos Primary (my alma mater) is dependent on it since it moved to the Chai Chee temporary site.

    Service 151: Has the youngest population from the most varied institutions. CJC, SJI, SCGS, RGS (transfer), SMU, NYPS, RGPS, HCJC, TCHS, NYGH, NJC, MGS, Ngee Ann Poly, Maju Camp, and ending at NUS.

    Service 966: CJC in one direction and VJC in the other. Also has ppl from bukit timah schools mentioned above, including me (HCJC).
