    I started taking buses when i was still baby. My mother told me before that she everytime carry me take CSS 8 to Upp Cross St. I can still remember i started playing toy bus at about 2 - 3 years old(Only had 1 bus back then and it is in a terrible condition now)(Terrible condition = wheel drop off, DD became SD but it can still be fixed for the wheel part). When i was 4 years old, i remember taking the Volvo B57(Or was it something else?) on then 385 and that was when i started collecting tix. Later, i no longer took CSS 8 and took 2 and 51 to Chinatown rather frequently and the journey is about 1 hour or more hence sometimes my family decides to take taxi. When 168 was introduced, it was the 1st time i went to Woodlands. Finally, on 21 January 2005, Chinatown Direct came into the picture. Hence i have 2 direct choices to Chinatown (51 and CT1 and on seeing that CT18 duplicates exactly with the route that taxi takes, i decided to try CT18 on the 2nd day of service and it took only 17 mins on my 1st ride (just a few minutes slower than taxi YET much faster than 51 or 2). At that time as there was a "free ride home" promo, hence the original $1 a trip became $1 for 2 trips, only 10cent more for me as compared to 51 but for my parents, $1.80 for 2 trips is CHEAPER than 51 as 2 trips on 51 would cost $2.20 or around that. However, that promo ended soon. Right after CNY, another promo (Lucky Hongbao) came and it was cutting some coupon from Sin Min or Lianhe Wanbao and give the driver(paid trip) in order to receive a Red Packet that has a coupon or something like that. The next promotion after the CNY promotion was the Mid Autumn Festival Promo (1 cent tix). That promo i missed out almost totally except i got 1 X 1 cent tix. At the same time, there was another promotion with Chinese Garden (Service 154). I took many many trips on 154 but don't have the coupon until i encountered one Mercs and got 4 of them. This year, Lucky Hongbao Promo was back again and this time round only need to fill in a form and give the driver(paid trip). This time round i got 10 blue dogs(Exchanged away), Organics Shampoo, some polished diva thing (gave other ppl) but sadly dint get red dog . The next promotion was Chinatown Dumpling Promotion. This time round i also took a lot of CT18 but then i dint see any promos on any CT18 perms but luckily spotted some at Kreta Ayer Square (After 3PM near a box)(Before 3PM dun haf) but this time round i only wanted to collect tix
    Now Mid autumn is nearing but no promotion yet
