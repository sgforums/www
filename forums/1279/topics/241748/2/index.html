    The reason that buses are doing that it's because the OCC tells them to do so. Once a bus is full and the fact that the driver is running very late, OCC will ask the Driver to take out destos and continue the journey without picking up the passengers. By doing so, it will then try to help the BC gain back soem time. This is why during the IT Fair recently, many services like 518 133 36 70, who were running late, were asked to remove destos.
