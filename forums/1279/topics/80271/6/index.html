    The idea of this game is fairly simple. I will specify a boarding stop and an alighting stop. You must try to think of a combianation that can bring you from boarding to alighting stop. However, you are required to report at a specified stop once anywhere in the journey.

    Restrictions:
    1) You must take at least 10 buses during the whole journey. No repeat taking of the same bus service is allowed.

    2) You are allowed to take Express buses only once.
