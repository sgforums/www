    Yeah this is Singapore, we cant do much! So many of our bus routes (excluding those with express sectors) are more suited for bendys than DDs simply because of the number of busstops along the route. General public chooses to take bus to MRT station and transfer and DDs really lay over for quite long. But this is Singapore, we cant afford to use so many bendys with the land constraints that we have.

    So according to PTOs, when people complain cant board, they just throw in DD and the ball is back to the public's court as to whether they want to go up or not. But either way, they cant complain the PTO is not doing its job properly. So the solution? Its obvious, we cant move to having more bendys on the roads, so the choice will be to design bus trunk routes that suit DDs like those in HK so that the DDs are used properly and not just there for show that the PTOs have done their part. Feeders to provide linkage to MRT for people who prefer MRT comuting and trunk routes as alternatives to MRT for people who prefer bus. This will probably take ages or may never ever happen unless someone takes a bold step forward. Its the sad reality.

    Till then, just live with it. Even though the DD is quite empty on top, it still carries more people than a SD and so its still better because you cant use so many bendys on our roads. Its because 169 has bendys as well that people can do this comparison and say bendy is better etc. So many SBST services also go this issue but we never had a direct comparison so people just live with it. Atleast I still do respect SMRT for seeing the use in bendys for their feeders and still purchasing new units for their feeders as opposed to SBST which is just throwing in DDs to show they have done their job. 

    Woodlands, I doubt will leave the hands of SMRT even in GCM. So many bendys over there...
