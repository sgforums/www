    19 Dec 09, 17:16
    Originally posted by SBS9067A:

    See if there's anything wrong with this SBS9810X. There is about..6 differences.

    The edits that were spotted were:

    1. The picture of Laa-Laa (from the children TV series Teletubbies)
    2. The sun with the baby looking face (also from the children TV series Teletubbies)
    3. Three rear wheel axles instead of two.
    4. The EDS, which was actually cropped from SBS 9888Y and pasted over.
    5. Two side indicator lights (usually only one)
    6. On the EDS itself, the destination was changed to "Boon Boon" (original was "Boon Lay").

    Note that the black bumper was a feature of this bus at the time the photo was taken (6 Jan 2007). This bumper has since been repainted to the corporate purple colour.
