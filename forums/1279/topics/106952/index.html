    Hmm, i will never forget this bus ride.

    2 years + Back, when i was taking 98 DD from JE. Me and my friend were on the upper deck, and the entire DD was empty except for us.
    Then comes a malay couple, with the girl leading the guy by the hand, practically running towards for the back of the bus. I was puzzled, most people would want to run OUT, and not INto the bus right?

    So i looked at them. And it was there i got quite a shock. The girl was adjusting her long dress, (didn't think too much about this), and then i looked away for a while to chat with my friend, (he was facing the front, i was at the seat infront of him, turning back), and when i looked back, the girl was sitting on the guy, and then standing up the next moment (repeat x10).

    I was like wtf? Looked for a while more, and than told my friend about it. The malay guy had a bochup look on his face too. (maybe trying to act cool or something.) The girl just look at us, and looked away. They were about 16-18 i guess.

    T'was a pity i had to alight just about now, i alighted, and looked up. They were still going at it, but as it was a cold night, the fog was blocking the windows.

    What an experience i'll never forget~
