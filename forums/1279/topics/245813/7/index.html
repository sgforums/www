    1)Woodlands MRT: To meet Teacher
    2)Redhill MRT: To meet seller of a collector item
    3)Blk 4A Boon Tiong Road: To meet another seller
    4)Victoria Street: Tea-Break
    5)Esplanade Library: Red some music song books by Roy Orbison and Charles Gounod's Faust
    6)Victoria Street Library: To do some research
    7)Shenton Way: To transfer bus
    Kent Ridge Terminal: To joy-ride service 200
    9)Mount Sinai Rise: To joy-ride the Darts
    10)Jurong Town Hall Road: To transfer bus home
    11) Home

    Things I wore: Beige Plusfours, blue sweater with white collar shirt underneath, Black Loafers.

    1st Stop: Woodlands MRT

    178: Nissan U31SCN OAC TIB368Y to Interchange

    I met my Teacher to collect some project papers and the moment she saw my plusfours, she said, 'Why you dressed so cute?' I sighed

    2nd Stop: Redhill MRT

    MRT Service: WDL JRE RHL

    I met a seller of a Tintin water bottle and he never comments about me. I proceeded to my next stop

    3rd Stop: Blk 4A Boon Tiong Road

    SBST Service 33: Volvo B10M Mark IV DM3500 with Ad

    I rode the bus and no one comments. I went on to meet my second buyer and he never comments. Went on

    4th Stop: Victoria Street

    SBST Service 63: Merc 0405 DM OAC

    Went to 7-11 for a drink and caught service 133 to Esplanade

    5th Stop: Esplanade

    SBST Service 133: Merc 0405 DM CAC

    Went to Esplanade Libarary. Red through some lyrics by Roy Orbison and Charles Gounod. Had Chocolate Milkshake at the cafe. Headed back to Victoria Street.

    6th Stop: Victoria Street

    SBST Service 133: Leyland Olympian 3-axle

    Researched on Tintin and ligne-claire art. Went to the cafe for dinner of a sandwich and hot Milo. Headed down and took service 130 to Shenton Way

    7th Stop: Shenthon Way

    SBST Service 130: Another Merc 0405 DM CAC

    Waited for Service 10 at a bus stop filled with office workers. None took attention of my cos-play Tintin costume. Service 10 arrived and rode till Kent Ridge.

    8th Stop: Kent Ridge

    SBST Service 10: Volvo Super Olympian (B10TL) SBS9840

    Waited for Service 200 and took some pics of the Volvo B10M Mark IIs there. Service 200 arrived and set off.

    9th Stop: Science PArk

    SBST Service 200: SBS1895L Volvo B10M Mark II NAC SBST Service 92 SBS8010 Dennis Dart SBS7373 Volvo B9TL on 198

    Rode through the Kent Ridge Backroads. Stopped at Science PArk for Service 92. Took SBS8010 till Buona Vista and rode 198 till Jurong Town Hall. From Jurong town hall took 178 to home, where my parents called my cos-play a clown costume.
