    The AWC and PT logos have a meaning. AWC and PT logos still exist.

    AWC-Adult Workers Contract, meaning, buses that have been allowed to operate as factory buses.
    PT-Public Transport, meaning private bus that can be used as a public bus, ie scheme 'B'.

    As for the owner/company details, ie:
    SINGAPORE BUS SERVICES (197 LTD
    205 BRADDELL RD, SINGAPORE 2057
    and then the tyre size, MLW, ULW and capcity

    and other examples, I suppose they now allow buses to just put EXCURSION BUS, PTE HIRE BUS, PRIVATE BUS, SCHOOL BUS on them, without owner's or company details.

    However, for busesgoing into Malaysia, the owner's or company's detail must be there, ie:
    SBS TRANSIT LTD
    205, BRADDELL RD, SINGAPORE 579701
    then the laden weight and capacity

    For excursion buses, worker's transport, they are required to put BAS PERSIARAN, BAS PERKEJA
