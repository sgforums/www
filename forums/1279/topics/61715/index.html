    In responce to Victoria St section, generally New bridge Rd Terminal-bound & Outram Rd-bound services use this road. As for 133, it used to ply North Bridge Rd before it was amended to loop at Marina Centre instead of terminating there. I'm not sure of 130.

    As for 124 174 851 961 turning from Upp Cross St is because they served City Hall MRT Station directly for commuters to make easy transfer to these and other services that ply that segment.

    Services 143 147 ply new bridge rd as to have a so-called 'backbone' for Kg bahru rd/Bt merah-bound buses. Some commuters need not walk all the way to south bridge rd just to take a bus to those places.

    One main reason of the staggering buses along the Victoria St/Chinatown areas is to group buses with either a simliar origin/destination/passenger load. (i.e Orchard Rd & Eu Tong Sen St Bus Stops)

    Service 130 131 are 'weird' services. Svc 130 ply kitchener rd area is because of the residential area there. The Shenton Way-bound 130 could not serve there due to road constraints on Syed Alwi Rd.
    Service 131 ply Cambridge Estate area to serve the residents there. The St. Michael-bound 131 need to turn at Rangoon Rd instead of Bt Timah is because there's no 'return road' for Dorset Rd.

    As for 961 and 980 in the Jln Sultan area, their route was like this before Crawford St Terminal was closed down and these services were extended to Gey Lor 1 Terminal. I think that TIBS has no intention of amending these routes after it was closed.
