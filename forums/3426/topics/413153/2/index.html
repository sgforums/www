    KTCK , with the flap down or up , the thing will still record . Be sure to check if the recording  timer moves.

    Basically from what i feel , this camera is at best just for minimal protection as it is not wide angled , the view that is recorded is very limited. It can prove certain cases like people whacking onto your side when you are in lane , People beating red lights and causing a junction collision , cyclist coming into your path , pedestrians dashing out suddenly. Anything more , the camera is a bit lacking.

    I placed it in the centre just below the rear view mirror . be sure not to stick the suction cup on your solar film visor at the top.

    And also do not buy a micro sd card with adapter coz of contact point issues , do get the 4 GB or 8 Gb sandisk SDHC card. Mustafa sells the 4 gb for 12.90 and the 8 gb for 22.90

    The camera allows you to set recording in clips of 2 mins , 5 mins and 15 mins. I set at 5 mins .

    A 2 min file is abt 83 MB whereas a 5 min one is abt 206 mb.
