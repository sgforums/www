    Visit  : http://www.taxi.org.sg/tdvl_course.html

    for course details and schedules

    Course Durations:
    Choose from Full-time or Part-time courses:

    Part-time Course: 
    20 days (5-6 days per week) 
    1 lesson per day (7.00pm - 10.00pm)

    Full-time     Course: 
    10 days (5-6 days per week) 
    2 lessons per day (10.00am - 1.00pm & 2.00pm - 5.00pm)

    There will be tests at the end of the course on all modules using touch screen tablets. Candidates have to pass all modules (1 to 5) to attain the TDVL Licence. Licence is issued within one month by the LTA.
