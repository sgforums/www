    The 16th round of the Trans-Pacific Partnership (TPP) negotiations will take place at the Grand Copthorne Waterfront Hotel in Singapore from March 4-13, 2013.  

    Stakeholder Event

    The Stakeholder Engagement Event is scheduled from 9:30 AM to 1:30 PM on Wednesday, March 6, 2013, at the Grand Copthorne Waterfront Hotel.

    Tables to display and distribute TPP-relevant materials will be set up for stakeholders who request one, on a first-come-first-served basis.

    At the event, stakeholders will have the opportunity to raise questions and share views directly with negotiators and other stakeholders.

    This will be followed by a Stakeholder Briefing with the Chief Negotiators of each TPP delegation from 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM.

    UPDATE: Singapore has just announced that they will accommodate stakeholders, on a first come-first serve basis, who wish to make presentations, from 9:30 AM to 3:30 PM on Wednesday, March 6, 2013, at the Grand Copthorne Waterfront Hotel.
