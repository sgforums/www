    Question 1

    Self-study, computer type, this has been dis-continued. They now have the Express 3 weeks course and the Normal 6 weeks course. But remember to factor in the 'wait time'. Even though it's 3 weeks, if the course starts in June, also LL.

    CDG and SMRT have 'Train & Place' Package. meaning they will give you special incentives if you register for the course thru' them. The last time I read through their Contract Terms (in January 2012), CDG requires you to sign with them upon completion of course, whereas SMRT, you will only loose your incentives, no need to sign with them.

    If you read through this forum, the regulars here will advise you to go Relief first, which means the incentives is no use since it is only for the hirer.

    I have seen a lot of my classmates sign for the New Epica, because the feeling is great to drive a New Epica. But a few weeks later, they are crying, desperatly looking for somebody to relief with them. At $100+ per day, they cannot afford not to drive everyday, and they must drive at least 6 hours minimum to break even.

    Sharing my experience here...

    During the first 2 weeks of driving, cannot make head or tail of what is happening, just drive.

    (This is like BMT in the army. You charge up and down the hill, and you dont even know why)

    After 2 weeks, start to 'see' some patterns, able to improve on driving strategy.

    (This is like Vocation training in the army where you understand why certain things happen)

    After 1 to 2 months, start to fine tune driving strategy.

    (This is like posted to unit, where you can start to 'keng')
