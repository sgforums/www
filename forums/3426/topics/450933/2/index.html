    Dun think too much now. Put other things aside, get your TDVL first.

    No more online TDVL course.

    Express/Full day course is 6 hours a day for 3 weeks

    Normal/Part time course is 3 hours a day for 6 weeks.

    TVDL courses are conducted by STA (6472 7351), Comfort Transportation (6550 8681), SMRT Taxis (63690111).

    Call the 3 companies above and check with them their earliest date available for your intended course (full or part time).

    When you received LTA's approval letter, dun go to SATA, as it will take 2 to 3 working days to get the medical fitness certification paper. Check with StriveOn how he get it on the same day.
