    Bro, If you are driving with CDG a.k.a comfort. First thing to do is relax. Make sure your printer is working. Tell pax, "sure, no problem. Give me a second. 1) Press K1 on your meter until the receipt starts printing from your printer. 2) Upon completion of printing, Press OP on your meter (start your meter), wait about 3secs and stop your meter. 3) With the printed receipt in hand, proceed to edit the amount accordingly. 4) The printed receipt will have all the details of the trip like time start, km run and booking fee etc. 5) Show the receipt to the pax and tell the pax the amount due.
