    Hyundai keys cannot be duplicated outside. My method...

    Duplicate at outside locksmith a few keys which can only open the car door. Keep an original key which can start the engine inside the car in a safe place. Your relief uses his spare key to open the door and then takes the original key to drive. Replace when done, locks the car with his spare key. Outside 1 key $2 or $3 kao-tim-sai, don't need $50. How many reliefs also no issue, just give them a spare key to open the car door.
