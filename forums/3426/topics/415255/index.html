    In a deal estimated to be worth $75 million, the group will supply Trans-Cab - the second-biggest cab operator here - with 1,000 Renault Latitude sedans from next year, with an option for several thousand more.

    The Korean-assembled Renault Latitudes, fitted with a 2-litre engine and a six-speed automatic gearbox, will replace the operator's ageing Toyota Crowns. They will be the first Renault cabs here.

    Trans-Cab managing director Teo Kiang Ang said: 'This European car is very comfortable for the driver and passengers. I tried it, and it is better than all the other cab models here.'
