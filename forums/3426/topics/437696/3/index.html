    As for TS issue about wife on board, I can make it into 2 points,

    1st, officially speaking, paxs are customer, and as  direct customer service provider, we have to as much as reasonable, satisfy the customer, if you wish to go further and delight customer, by all means. If you go down to the protocol, we need to ensure our vehicle is in ok condition, clean and comfortable, on the other hand, we also need to ensure that we are in good shape, alert,  and at all time, have a safety mindset on the road. Knowing that Taxi is one the most Taxing transport on land here, we definitely expect customers demand privacy, faster and a comfort journey to their destination. So, if you are with family, you can switch to busy to ward off unnecessary flag down issues, and not matter whoever complained, you are protected and have your right. With a family member and a green for HIRE sign on top is certainly not encouraging.

    Different countries have their own way of driving taxis, each own culture, and for Singapore Taxi, the culture of having family member on board with pax is not a norm. If so, all of us will be doing the same and that can  end up with lots of problem, like pple said about China or india taxis, before going there, we already know such culture exist, so when we met one, no problem, cos it is a norm, but if a tourist is to come here and see such action, what will he or she thinks? Oh, singapore taxi same like india and china style, they drive with their family member on board.

    2ndly, unofficially speaking, it is a give and take issue, pax can choose not to take, if paxs start to take taxi with family member on board, then it will inculcate the trend that having member on board is ok, if so, 4 paxs together will be unable to take your taxis, and with a FOR HIRE sign on top to ply the road, you are basically not ready for hire. Of course, if mutual agreement can be reach, no problem, just drive on, but not all paxs are the same, they are the customers, and by the laws of business, customers is alway right. Turn left, you turn left lor, even you know it wrong, you just do as they said, nothing will go wrong.
