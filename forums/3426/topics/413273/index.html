    Enraged

    STOMPer Mike Tyson was at Clarke Quay last night (Oct 16) when he saw a man with blood all over his shirt surrounded by paramedics and police officers. He had apparently been punched by a taxi driver in a fight.

    Said the STOMPer:

    "A man had apparently been punched in the face, leaving him all bloody, after a fight with a cabby at about 12am at Clarke Quay yesterday (Oct 16).

    "I'm not sure what happened, but according to a passerby, the man and his friends were crossing the road when the cabby honked at him, which led to the fight.

    "From the video, the man who was punched seemed to be upset with the police as well, for some reason.

    "He and a male and female friend were heard confronting the police officers.

    "At one point, the man who was punched was seen walking off in the middle of the road all of a sudden, before a police officer stopped him.

    "His female friend could also be heard shouting at passers-by. telling them off for watching the drama.

    "The, dressed in black pants and a white long-sleeved shirt, was seen with another group of men whose car was still at the scene, being questioned by police officers."
