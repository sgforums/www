    But $20 for 11-7pm and only from Mon to Thurs, after careful consideration, I've decided to forgo this offer.

    My reasons:

     

    1) Why this hour? From the thread Quickpick showed, it's clear the hirer is trying to save on season parking of $90 a month.

    2) Why only Mon to Thurs and not offer Friday? If Friday was included, I would probably take it up. Yeah sure, he has rental to pay and maybe needs to earn a bit more for himself and family etc etc, but he's carefully chosen timings that in any case, he can't or won't be driving as an OMO. And he wants to maximise this to the fullest, and where possible offer only crumbs.

    3) As a night driver, in any case I almost never ever drive until the wee hours on weekdays. Usually close shop around 1-2 am, even earlier if my first few midnight end up near my home. (Once you come within a whiff of home, become lembik already and will find all sorts of excuse to go home, watch TV, drink coffee or surf the net) But with this scenario, it means that goes out of the window and I must drive until at least 3am and then if I want to rest, stay awake until 5am for any advance job (5-6am) and die die must find 1 between 6-7am.

    4) In other words, I will still be working 7-8 hours like I do on weekday nights now, but will surely earn less or be pressured to hunt for pax. Is that worth it, compared to working from say 6-7pm to 1 or 2 am and pay $30-40 more in rent and diesel?

    For these reasons, I think I must reject the offer, unless the timing comes down to 10pm. In any case the hirer by offering this clearly shows he's very calculative and I don't like people who calculate every damn thing.
