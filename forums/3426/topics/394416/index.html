    Let me share my personal opinion on smrt.

    Basically the limitation on type of vehicle is an issue. Manual pretty standard, crown and cedric. Auto you guys know, the "Only SMRT Have" type of vehicles, ssangyong, chrysler, azera (CNG) with 180km tank, market standard mercs and Hyundai Starex. So definite no no auto for any newbie starting out cos expenses high and hard to get relief at all.

    Rental wise, too many scheme too choose and with many hidden benefits. Same model of taxi can have 2-3 different schemes and benefits. Guess that the reason why nobody can list out the price and benefits.

    Customer service wise, so far comparable to comfort.

    Amenities wise, as in driver lounge, so far the best among taxi companies. Free cappucino, green tea, sleeping lounge (so you can forgot waiting time), TV and DVD shows while waiting for vehicle. Previously they still got massage chair somemore.

    Call booking, they upgraded most to the lastest MDT, quiet the same as comfort. But of cos the numbers of call cannot be compared. But I see an increase in the numbers of call coming in everyday. Call booking no "must take" policy like comfort. you want you book, you can also set your mdt to busy so u dun take booking

    Diesel, SMRT only have 3 refilling station, woodland, AMK industrial park, geylang (former bus terminal). Only Geylang is 24 hours, the rest is from 7am-7pm. So night shift driver usually have to go SPC (current 18% discount) for diesel.
