    From my point of view;

    1) SMRT - "Good" scheme for beginners to start a taxi career but be aware of the points listed earlier on. Diesel is $0.93. Accident access is low, better booking system now. But choice of auto taxi sucks.

    2) Premier (Kia) - Not a good choice for beginner. Cos overhead expenses is high and the benefit is not attractive. Accident access is very high. No satelite Booking system. Kia is on diesel.

    3) Comfort - Good for beginner to start as a relief, not as an OMO cos overhead high. Used to have good booking, but now bookings alot of taiko one. But for a beginner it a good enough way to start off. Sonata all are diesel and comfort offer cheapest diesel, if i remember should be around $0.88

    4) Prime - Not for beginner cos got to commit $5k deposit for 5 years contract. All prime taxi are CNG. Can only relief 2 days (correct me if i m wrong)

    5) Transcab - Good for OMO cos benefits attractive, cons is toyota wish is on CNG, making it difficult to find relief.

    So to say comfort offer a good way to start as a relief. But if you wanted to start off as OMO, transcab is better cos attractive benefits.
