  • Guess?
    13 Aug 12, 08:22

    The maximum number of passengers for a taxi in Singapore is follows:

    a. 4 adults, or

    b. 3 adults + 2 children, or

    c. 2 adults + 3 children, or

    d. 1 adult + 4 children, or

    e. 6 children

    (Note: children = under 12 years old)

    So it's not possible to pack 3 adults and 3 children into a Singapore taxi.

     

    For diagram, visit 

    http://www.cdgtaxi.com.sg/commuters_seating_capacity.mvn;jsessionid=C5383AB2E6F83E2A87438CF7DE9F1643?cid=1320114
