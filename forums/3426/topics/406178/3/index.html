    For newbies,depending on what kind of learning stage and targeted earning you want. If you are looking at 150 nett per day and not familiar with route, OMO is the choice. Unless you are so experience like people here, then you start your relief career.without experience and do relief, you might have miserable income,at least for the beginning.Experience people need minimal time on the road to meet target so talking is very easy to them.Newbies have no choice but to slog for the beginning.And if you start as OMO and switch to relief after you gain enough experience, it will be heaven for you.And to be half day driver, strongly suggest you switch to Transcab. Because when you are a hirer of transcab, you get much incentive compare to other company. So after you find a relief, your rental is approximately 60 per day. if you use the incentive to offset for your rental, use a calculator and you shall find out how many rental free day you have per month.so your balance rental per month will be approximately 1200 which you will be able to clear within a week,again with experience. Balance 3 weeks income is yours nett after petrol and cng.
