    Day or night shift depends on your registered hirer.  If he wants to drive day.. then you have to drive night...

    If you wants to drive day shift - then you have to state your shift specifying to the potential partner. You can do it Online (some TD's website) or thru taxi company.

    If you intend to drive OMO, then you can request for a cab from the company, after that, you can search for a night relief if you felt tired driving alone.

    Day shift has it advantages (got time for family in the evening). Few of the disadvantages is the HEAT from the Sun, Heavy traffic Jams during peak hour, also Many White Monkey hiding at Over-head bridge with a speed cam.

    Night shift needs alot of sacrifices (no social life). the advantages are the opposite of the disadvantage mentioned above... except for traffic jams.

    As TD income is not fixed = it depends on the trips/luck of that particular day..

    Furthermore, we encourage fellow TDs not to reveal their daily income online...  the reason....? you guess... ( this is a national forum page - every one can access into this page - including the taxman ).  lol.

    If you have a Full-time Job at the moment... stay with it.... Unless you are unemployed and having a Driving Licence, then go for the Vocational Licence (that is TDVL).

    As long as you are honesty to yourself, don't  overcharge, always ask the pax for his/her preferred routes, then you should be fine...

    cheers.

    OMO = One Man Operation ( one man = 2 shifts ).. organise your time, maximize your income.

    Hirer = Registered to be responsible for the cab well being.  Company will deduct daily rental thru Giro from the Hirer's a/c only.  If he has a relief, relief TD will just pay direct to him.

    Hirer can be a OMO if he chooses to... but by economical means, he should have a relief partner to cover the alternate shift.

    let get this straight (One more time).

    Hirer can be a sole driver (hence in taxi terms, we called him OMO)... he has 24hrs to himself (but normally working hours is ard 16-18hrs).  Driving cab need sufficient rest.
