    Many bros here says SMRT call booking system is no good. I agree as there are only about 1400+ call bookings per day.

    I am also with their Central Limo group 6, and this 1 really benefits me. SMRT have counters at T2 & T3 for SQ cabin crews. So whenever I am near airport, I can try the arrival hall at T2 or T3 for PNG.

     

    Fyi, the pick up fare is the same as the airport Q. Of course, there will be many short trips but on tues got 1 Jap SQ chio bu to Bukit Merah View & thurs during peak hr, I got 1 fare from T3 to Queen St. This cabin crew reside in JB.

     

    Of course like what Teolk posted, the SMRT lounge for drivers is 1st class. They also have people who are visually handicap to provide shoulder or foot massage at $20 for 20 mins, i think.
