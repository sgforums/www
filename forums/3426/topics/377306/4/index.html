    If you can fix 2 evenings, say Fri & Sat OR Wed & Fri, then you chances are that you may find a hirer willing to take you. If you are on rotating shift and no fixed rest day then that might be a bit difficult.

    Relief only need to deposit $1000 with the taxi company you are driving with. Other fees like registration/admin fee, personal insurance cover for relief driver etc. depends on the individual taxi company. Different company have different policy. The company will pay for the vehicle's insurance to cover 3rd party claim in case of accident. You still need to pay an excess of $1000~2000 (depends on coy) if involve in you-bang-other accident.

    Most company pay benefits under different name.

    1. Performance incentives - no customer/company/LTA complaint

    2. Safety (no accident) incentives - driver not involve/wrong in accident

    3. Diesel subsidy - company sell diesel to driver cheaper than outside petrol kiosk

    4. Road tax rebate (Govt) - Company returning this rebate to hirer

    Actually these benefits are based on quarterly, half-yearly or yearly. Best is call some of these taxi companies and enquire about the schemes they offered.
