    Congratulation for joining red engine.

    To survive in this industry you have to go through a school call " School of Hard Knock". Getting left right up down from pax and you will learn faster this way. The first 6 months is the most crucial period, you will go through lots of up and down but push on. Perserverance, diligent and hardwork is the keys to succeed in this industry. No shortcuts. Ask any lao gui in this forum I belive they will tell you the same thing.

    1) Perserverance - You must be willing to put up with all kind of nosense pax will gave you. Bear with it, take it light hearted. DO NOT take it personal or you will feel a drag going to work everyday.

    2) Diligent - Drive with diligent. Know the in and outs of the industry. Go read thru the past post / thread and know when, where, who, how to find pax. Army now is already 3G, taxi driver cannot stay in 1G or 2G. Must think differently.

    3) Hardwork - No shortcuts. To make $$$ you must work hard, and also work smart. Don't put your target too high as a beginner. Set like 50-60% what some lao gui make in this forum. Slowly increasing it when you get the hang of it.

    As for GPS, i do have one inside my car, but i use it mainly to serach for addresses, not so much for navi. GPS can be rather stupid. It doesn't factor in traffic conditions and traffics light.

    When I first started to drive, I trace out copies of HDB area map and put in a small B6 hardcase cover. It easier to visualize the whole of HDB area at one glance than to look through street directory, which is often break up in many pages. For town, very hard to draw , too big. You can break down the town into region, like CBD, Orchard, Orchard Outskirt etc and learn it bit by bit.

    CNG station, the toh tuck and mandai are 24 hrs. Serangoon north close when they dry out of gas. I don't have the current price, maybe pool can update.

    Petty cash wise depends on individual, i like to have 15 - $10 notes, 10-$5, 25-$2, coins i have 15-$1 coin, 15-$0.50, 25-$0.20, 25-$0.10

    You can join lakeview. They have a special price for new member. You can check with pool.

    Sorry lah, very long winded. Let me know if you need any help
