    STOMPer Lovell Tan has never seen such an unusual taxi before -- the interior of the taxi is decorated with obituary photos, as well as toys and figurines of the dead.

    Said the STOMPer:

    "I was walking with my friend and happened to stumble upon this taxi at Choa Chu Kang.

    "It was over-flowing with decorations -- the front and back windows was filled with figurines, there was an amazing amount of written notes and stickers all over the taxi, and obituary photos were hanging from end to end.

    "Even the car boot was filled with toys and figurines and photos of the dead.

    "As I was taking photos of the taxi, the driver came up to us and I asked him if we could take a photo of him with the taxi. He obliged.

    "I wonder if anyone would dare to ride in his taxi."
