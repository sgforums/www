    If you are driving as a relief, all company policy is almost the same. Half shift = half rent. Full shift = Full rental.

    Just that certain hirer are more generous and they like to round down (eg Full shift $102 they run down to $100). The reason behind why u should go for Comfort is that u can depend on calls when you experienced difficulties in sweeping the floor.

    With calls, you can really learn the trick with reasonable good starting income initially. When you have become more experienced after a while, then start thinking about what's next.

    Dun be hasty, just wait a while longer to find the perfect hirer u need.
