    I learnt thru the bus routes plying in the estate.


    Tampines
    Blk 100+ is St 11/12, nearest to PIE. Cluster is MRT Line, PIE, Ave 5/4
    Blk 200+ is St 21/22/23, north of Ave 2. The whole cluster bordering MRT Line, Ave 2/7/4
    Blk 300+ is St 31/32/33 behind Esso/Ngee Ann Sec, nearest to PIE/TPE. The whole cluster bordering Ave 7/2/St 31/PIE/TPE
    Blk 400+ is St 41/42/43/44/45, the whole Tampines North. Ave 7 below, Ave 9 on top.
    Blk 500+ is at Tampines Central
    Blk 600+... eh dont have!
    Blk 700+ is at the north west side of Tampines, side nearest to IKEA. Ave 9 on top, Ave 5 below.
    Blk 800+ is almost the whole Tampines West. Ave 5/8/1/4 borders these blocks
    Blk 900+ is the small cluster of blocks opposite SAFRA, HDB blocks only at St 91. St 92/93 is ITE and industrial area. bounded by Ave 4/5/1
    Once u zone these 8 places out, then how each St is aligned as well as the order of the numbering in each area.

    Sengkang
    Rivervale = 100+. Right side Sengkang East Drive, left side Punggol Rd
    Compassvale = 200+. Right side Punggol Rd, left side Sengkang East Rd
    Anchorvale = 300+. Right side Sengkagn East Rd, left side Sungei Punggol
    Fernvale = 400+. Right side Sungei Punggol, left side Jln Kayu.
    Once u zone these 4 places out, then learn the small roads as well as the order of the numbering in each area.

    Simei
    Simei ah.. small place la.. St 1 on top, St 3 at the bottom. but got Blk 100+ and 200+.. 200+ only 2 clusters - 1 nearest the MRT, 2 is at St 4.

    Hougang
    Hougang... big place leh! 100 200+ at Kovan area - Chan Shao Mao area
    2 digit, 300+ & 700+ is at Yaw Shin Leong area.
    400+ is near Hougang MRT/Mall & Punggol Park west side is Ave 6, anything west side is 500+ until Ave 4/St 51
    600+ big area.. main Rd Ave 4 & Ave 8
    800+ is the Hougang MRT area
    900+ is where Ave 9/Buangkok Green/Cres
    Big estate and tricky. But mostly main road is the Avenues so wont go wrong..

    Bedok
    Bedok South & North 2 different places. Got double digits, and 100+ duplicates in these 2 zones. Get them right.
    Bedok South double digits occupy most of the estate, the 100+ is nearer to Upp east coast rd & south ave 3
    Bedok North double digits and 100+ is on the east side of Bedok North where North St 2, North Ave 4 and North Rd are. West most rd is North Ave 3.
    200+ is Bedok town centre. Wont go wrong.
    300+... dont have!
    400+ is the area enclosed by North Rd, North Ave 3, North St 1 and North Ave 1. Damn small area. wont go wrong also.
    500+ is bascially where bus 225 goes. the whole St 3 area is there. Enter via North Ave 1 or North Ave 2.
    600+ is at Bedok Reservoir west side. borders Kaki Bt Ave 1, bedok North Rd, PIE, Eunos Link.
    But hor got cluster of 100+ at Bedok Reservoir also. wah headache ah. got Blks 101-151 at Bedok Reservoir, Blks 100-140 at Bedok North, Blks 156-172 at Bedok South.
    700+ is at Bedok Reservoir above Bedok Town Park. wont go wrong here. Remember Sheng Shiong? there.
    800+ 900+ dont have

    Woodlands
    Big is big ah but ah wood can be done.
    double digits are at Marsiling estate. Or if u feel u are in malaysia, you are in marsiling. Marsiling has 200+ blocks from 201-218. not all are double digits
    100+ are nearer to Marsiling MRT, but north of Marsiling MRT. St 11/12/13
    200+... have.. only 18 blocks in Marsiling estate.
    300+ is south of Marsiling MRT. got St 31/32
    400+ is one small cluster, south of Marsiling MRT, nearest to BKE and the American School. Just find St 41 and all the blocks are there.
    500+ is ALL blocks south of Ave 5, south east of Woodlands MRT/Causeway Pt. got Dr 14/43/44/16 here.
    600+ is ALL blocks south of Admiralty MRT, east of Ave 4, and north of Ave 5. Got Dr 42/62/63/64/71/75/Ring Rd. Tricky cluster here so always ask if its near 3M, Greenwood Pri, Woodlands NPC, or Woodlands Ring Pri.
    700+ is ALL blosk north of Admiarlty MRT, east of Ave 4, south of Ave 9. Got Dr 40/60/61/70/72/Circle/Cres. Tricky cluster if go by the Drive number. Just ask if its near Admiralty Sec, Admiralty Pri, Evergreen Pri or Woodlands Mart.
    800+ is all blocks bordered by Ave 9, Ave 4, Ave 5, Ave 2. 890+ is south of the MRT line, the rest on the north side. South side only Dr 50. North side St 81/82/83.
    900+ dont have/

    Yishun
    Another master town to be mastered. Ring Rd makes u blur only.
    The oldest part of Yishun is at Chong Pang. So naturally 100+ starts there. The whole north west Yishun bordered by Ave 7/2/5/Sembawang Rd is 100+. Got St 11 only
    200+ is North East Yishun, bordered by Ave 2/7/6/9/Ctrl. Got St 20/21/22
    300+ is Central East Yishun, must use Yishun Ctrl or Ave 1 to get there (other than Ring Rd la duh!) lower 300+ is nearer to Yishun Town Centre, further ones are along Ring Rd/Ave 6
    400+ is at Far East Yishun. Access from Ave 1/11
    500+... dont have
    600+ is Central South East Yishun. Access from Yishun Ctrl/Ave 4/Ring Rd. Got St 61 there
    700+ is West Yishun. North is Ave 5, East is Ave 2, south is Ave 3/Ring Rd, west is Sembawang Rd. Got St 71/72 which mainly serves the lower 700+. higher 700+ is via Ave 3/Ring Rd
    800+ is South East Yishun. lower 800+ is next to Khatib MRT, the rest opposite khatib MRT. Access from St 81/Ave 1/4/Ring Rd.
    900+ is town centre


    Detailed enough? or still hard to picture and understand?

    Just drive and learn, everyday is a learning journey, learn abt location, abt communicating with different peoples, abt driving skills, and so on.

    For location, if I do not know, I will ask pax, if pax also not sure, then use my smarthphone to locate, then on meter and move off, on reaching, mark and note down the location, later go home, study it, it will go direct into your memory because you had been to this place. Then study the road surrounding this location, ask yourself what other option I can take to this destination.

    By reading and memorising location can be quiet taxing, furthermore, you only lean it by theory, you could had memorised woodland drive 40, but once you turn into Ave 12, you go blank, then you make a wrong turn and pax start to flare up, that time you start to say, i remember this and that, pax would had scolded you deep deep.

    Do not get excited and be scare of location, no doubt singapore is small, the the road mass per sq km is one of the world most congested, if you go malaysia or other places, they just got a few straight roads and one main road, singapore is full of it, three main road crisscrossing each others and within, many small lanes and roads inside a km sq. So, i understand that it is not easy, and we are usually get block by HDB or other tall buildings unlike other countries with a low flat landscape which you can pin point and see the location.

    By order of learning, the location that i went wrongly and scolded by pax is usually the one I will never forget. Knn, this shows we need to learn the hard way
