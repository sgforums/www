    Just relax and drive, most local will know the road, what TD show know are the main road, and as time goes on, the smaller ones, remembering of prominent building and places like mosque, temples, CC or school is also good, so that you know turning at the mosque is Eunos and so on.

    TD is a practical job, not a tie and shirt theoretical sales job that buy your soul and kill your pocket, so practically speaking, it is better to learn on the road, go back, go thru the events and places, its help alot. Study the road so hard but when you really go there, you also blur, infact, can be more blur as you try to figure out your theory, and with the pax stare boring down on your back, you get more nervous and worrying. Then you tell pax that by right, I studied the road should be this and that, pax will F you and ask you if you know the road or not. Relax lah, dunno, ask them lor.

    Singapore roads actually provide many sign and board, unlike other like malaysia, you dunno where you are, no sign, no road name too., all the jalans here and jalans there.

    Place that you go 3 times and still dunno the place, frankly, you can quit driving taxis, there is alway a first time to everything, you fell when learn how to ride bicycle to become good, get a few scolding here and there also good, cos scolding wake you up to remember the place forever until your deathbed.
