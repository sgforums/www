    Scheduled Offences Under VLPS

    3 Demerit Points
    Fine : $100

    1. Failing to surrender taxi vocational licence upon suspension or cancellation of licence
    2. Hindering or obstructing other taxi drivers from picking up passengers
    3. Failing, without reasonable cause, to proceed to destination named by a
      passenger by shortest and most direct route
    4. Setting taximeter in motion before hiring
    5. Failing to stop taximeter at end of hiring
    6. Resetting taximeter during hiring
    7. Attempting to overcharge
    8. Verbally insulting, intimidating or harassing a passenger (other than in a sexual manner)
    9. Taking up or setting down passenger at unauthorised place
    10. Failing to drive into public stand from rear and into foremost adjacent vacant space thereat until reaching front of public stand
    11. Wilfully obstructing, impeding or preventing other taxi drivers from stopping,
    12. parking or stationing their taxis at the public stand
    13. Stopping, parking or stationing taxi at public stand when unable to let out taxi for hire
    14. Obstructing Registrar or authorised officer in the exercise of his power and duty to examine taxi

    5 Demerit Points
    Fine : $200

    1. Driving taxi with expired taxi vocational licence
    2. Using, wearing or having in his possession the vocational licence of another person
    3. Causing hurt to passenger in taxi through carelessness or wilful misbehaviour
    4. Covering or obscuring face of taximeter
    5. Terminating hiring of taxi or requiring passenger to leave taxi, without reasonable excuse, before passenger is conveyed to destination
    6. Verbally insulting, intimidating or harassing a passenger in a sexual manner
    7. Retaining possession of passenger’s property left behind in taxi for more than 24 hours but less than 7 days
