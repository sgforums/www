    There is this stigma that once you have converted yourself into being a cabby from a job, you tend to want to earn the same amount as your last draw paid from your last job, or some even wanted to earn more.

    So if you last drawn paid is 4k, you will be very yao to make it, and that frankly, can be near impossible in long run.  But if your last drawn paid is 2k and below, you will be a happy cabby because taxis can provide you better income than what you did last time.

    So, I would said it all depend on which direction of life you came from before becoming a TD. The only differences is that as TD, your income in a way become stagnated, in working jobs, it varies from year to year with increment and promotion.
