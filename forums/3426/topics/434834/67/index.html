    For nite driving, you certainly must make more while working less, this is graveshift, out of norm, even workers or staffs working at nite also get paid more for less job done, like those 24hrs shop, at nite, the staff get higher nite shift allowance, yet less customer to deal with.

    But against the backdrop of a normal life, it is out of the scope of day time work, nite time sleep, and that working less with high return is the only consolidation for nite drivers, of course there will be less traffic, no sun, more cooling, but those with family will miss their kids, especially those school going kids.

    Those with young family drivers tends to seek nite shift relief so to have more time with family and be on a more normal life
