    imho (am no LJ, only drove for 10 yrs), auto is quicker (so u can compete better) and easier to drive. Thus u can spare attention for more pressing matters such as looking for pax, answering pax questions, looking for landmarks etc esp for newbies. You'll hv better endurance too!

    To me productivity and biz opportunity is the most important. That why i hv chosen yellow.

    Pros

    1. hassle free and quite good cab maintenance, at least from my exp.

    2. significantly cheaper diesel and easily available island wide.

    3. best cashless: all sorts of credit cards, nets, epins, ezlink, CDG corporate cards, the works!! so no need to waste precious time n entertain "uncle, pls look for atm, hv no cash" or "do u accept credit cards?" n see big fish slip off yr hook! can"jump queue" too esp when pax aproaches u with CDG corporate card.

    4. tons of calls esp in ulu areas. juz imagine trying to look for pax in hugh Tuas!

    5. good IT support. website for cabbies to check account, look for reliefs, checkout and bid for adv bookings. Phone apps for adv booking bidding available too!

    Cons

    1. quite strict on discipline esp with public image and pax complaint so u need to be accountable for yr actions

    2. big corporation (public listed company) so everything iaw (in accordance with) SOP, say rigid if u r the type who prefer some flexibility

    3. red offers more discounts esp for cng wish but range and topping up of cng can be quite a challenge

    4. can't thk of anything more at the moment..

    haha, no, am not doing PR for yellow/blue. juz that hv been driving yellow all this while and there are no justification for me to jump ship
