    -excellent steering feel and turing circle, but bad turning radius

    -excellent comfort level with exceptional smooth acceleration attributed to the V6. Good insulation and good suspension set up for street.

    -Poor lateral balance. Anything front drive with V6 is wrong. Plus Malaysian 205 profile tyres not doing any favour to hold the weight. Geometry is correct thus handling still of decent standards.

    -V6 turbo enough to pull away from most other Cabs. Perhaps on par with Chrysler.

    -huge brakes matching huge weight. Brake pumps known to fault.

    -Over invested, under-rated

    -and how can anyone not know, it's an Audi A4 in a different badge.

    -I'd pay $130 if they are replacing with new models.

    -if kept clean, enough to scare away cheapo pax.
