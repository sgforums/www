Height barrier collapses onto taxi

  • QX179R
    17 Feb 12, 17:26

    A height barrier near a carpark in Toa Payoh collasped onto a taxi parked near it after a lorry had brought it down earlier today (Feb 17). According to STOMPer Jasper, no one was hurt in the incident.

    The STOMPer wrote:

    "I was seated at a coffeeshop near the multi-storey carpark beside Block 233 Toa Payoh Lorong 8 when I saw a white lorry collapse the height barrier after going past it.

    "A taxi, which was parked nearby, was caught under the fallen barrier.

    "No one was hurt in the incident."
