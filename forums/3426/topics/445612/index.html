    Been during for 3 weeks liao and life is a breeze so far. Enjoyed every moment of it so far. Most pax has been very kind and understand and directed me to their destination if I am not to familar with it, even Ang Moh, whom I find real impressive with their knowledge of the road - until today, I finally met my devil.

    Had 3 advanced bookings all lined up

    5.30 Rasa Sentosa to Airport

    6.50 Maplewoods to SIA Training Centre

    8.00 Toa Payoh to Airport.

    The second one comes with special instructions to be punctual.

    I finished the 1st one at 6.00 and as I am early, too a slow drive there and reach there at 6.20. As I am required to wait at the basement and worried that reception for my MDT would be bad, I called this Ang Moh lady pax's mobile and told here that I had arrived. But she said that she could only be down at 6.50. (sounds fair right?)

    At about 6.48, was already feeling "high tide" so I ask someone there if there is a toilet around and was told that there is one. As I noticed I got only 2 mins more and if I go to toilet and she came down and dun see me, all my effort to be early would be gone down to waste, so I "loooooon".

    I expect her to be on time as she might be rushing for meeting, that's why need me to be punctual, so I give her my respect and stay put. Which the clock struck 6.50am, no sign of her. Waited till 6.55, still no sign and the MDT's "NO SHOW" appeared.

    I pressed the "NO SHOW" sign but still stay and wait and 3 mins later she came down, no apology.

    Moving off, I ask her if I take the PIE and she reply YES. Then I ask her if she like me to take the Anak entrance or U-turn to Sixth Avenue, which from my map, seems shorter. She told be not to U-turn and I told her that route seems longer but she replied that she have been using it for two years.

    Then I ask her which exit should I take on reaching there and if it's ok for me to use the Simei exit. She said "My God, u mean u dunno the way? It's not fair. Why dun u call your company and ask them for direction"

    I mention that using my way, I can send her to her destination but if she got other prefer route, she can direct me accordingly and she remarked that she is not going to the Simei route but say she doesn't know which other exit. She said I can go all other ways except the Simei exit. I told her that I only know that exit but if she prefer others, I need her to direct me. She say u don't turn to Simei, yo u go straight.

    When we reach the Bedok exit, she said "Mr Driver, where u leading me? do u know the way? I reply that I am waiting for her instruction and we still haven't even reach Simei yet.

    Then she said she need to rush to work and want to call my company NOW.

    That really pissed me off and for the first time, I tell her off.

    I told her that the route I know will lead me there but if she got other alternative, I am prepared to follow. I told her that I am only trying to help send here there on time and she is not prepared to help.

    I told her that I am only trying to earn a living and she dun have to make things so nasty. I respect your time and you ask ppl to be punctual and yet you are late. You are driving for 2 years and you should know that every minute, the PIE's traffice condition changes.

    I told her that because she is late, I got to cancel my 8.00 appointment and because I respect her, I cannot use my phone to call and cancel while driviing. I ask her to think of that poor lady at Toa Payoh and all these because of her.

    I also tell her that because I respect her time, I rather not go to toilet and be there on the dot cos she need me to be there.

    I ask her how would she react if I am the one who is late for just 1 min.

    Finally when we reach the Simei exit, she ask me to go straight and give some instruction to trun right, turn left and in less than a minute, we are there.

    I told her if she can be like other ppl who just give such simple instruction and not scold and threaten to call office, we could have solved such a simple problem. She knows and had her preferred route but yet she want to make things difficult for us.

    The worst thing is, all this while, she said that she had recorded everything I said and ask if I would to add on.

    Upon reaching, she ask if I know how to handle the Nets machine (sarcastic right?) as she wants to pay by  NETS.

    But I still settle everything nicely for her and help her carry her precious luggage down for her (must be important stuff as she said she dun want to put in the booth but want it with her) So I ask if it's fragile and put it lightly on the floor.

    Then she offer her hand for me to shake and said let's forget the whole thing.

    Haha, sigh!

    For me in this case:

    1)  I will never call/sms the pax from my hp, unless I really want this fare. Once you call, you are obliged to pick them up (to a certain extent). Moreover many pax will delay because they have your number as opposed to the auto reply which they cant call back.

    2) If I am early I will only activate the call button around the 10 min mark, not earlier. No show 5 mins is 5 mins. 4 mins late I may already have left, unless I want this fare (midnight or ulu place with no good prospects). I only wait longer in special circumstances.

    3) I never take 2 or 3 advance jobs in consecutive hours unless midnight, and never in peak period hours, because effectively ties my time up. Unless the 2 jobs not far from each other, eg; Job 1, Jurong to Airport at 6 am, Job 2 Pasir Ris to Shenton Way at 7am. After dropping pax 1, I can go for breakfast before Job 2, but definitely I won't do it if I ahd 30 min of peak period to kill after dropping pax 1. But this is up to individual.

    4) We all were new once and like wise we wanted to please customers and offer suggestions of routes we think is better or maybe we could be nervous because we new and don't want to offend or inconvenience pax, so we ask where to turn etc. But morning office advance jobs is standard, they take it everyday, so I will just ask how you normally go. Then the pax must advise accordingly, which route and exits to take.

    If the pax, very saart or yaya, then no need to talk so much, I just take the shortest route unless they advise otherwise. If kena jam, too bad, as long as pax paying for the jam, I don't care. If their route also jam one, then I just follow, no need to advise.
