    Yesterday brought whole family to Tan Qwee Lan street to makan at the chinese restaurant directly opposite the taxi stand. Empty slot in taxi stand, so I parked there lor. haha. Somemore can jaga and make sure nothing happened. Anyway, the food was sibei solid. Black pepper chilli crab, mother's day tofu, fish slice bee hoon, dry hor fun, fried pork ribs, fruit juice. Everything was finger lickin good. After that, we went MBS. Parked inside carpark. Believe it or not, I saw so many taxis inside - citycab crown, mercedes. Wa lan eh... Come here gamble or spend time with family one?

    Went to see the occulus. Its a huge glass fountain. the glass is 20cm thick!!! The bay walk was simply beautiful. Can see Fullerton hotel. I imagine got many LV anchors hanging around ************ while I relax myself. Went to the MBS hotel, nice live jazz music from the FUSE. Saw the huge potted trees too. FINALLY, went to see the taxi queue. Laughed at them inside my heart. Got money to come here, no money to call? But got 30 mins to wait in a 20 meter queue?

    But one thing for sure, MBS got 4 storeys of casino on the right side of the convention center. It looks much bigger than the Sentosa 2 storeys. More grand better design. Can see people gambling inside from the entrance. Of course we never went in.
