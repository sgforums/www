    Firstly, maid as domestic helper, they are not cover by the employment act, employment act cover max 44 hrs per week working, anything more is consider OT, but because of the human rights arugement, maids on a normal day, can work until midnite or wake up morning 5am to start work, it is a different contractual of work, therefore, in goodwill, and also in respect to their countries of origin, they are given a day off.

    We are not maids, we are taxis driver, slave of the road, not domestic helper. Frankly, nobody ask you or if you dun mind, force you to be a TD, you go in with your eyes open and signed, trained and drive on, once you signed, the laws recognise that you understand and agreed to the contractual agreement and obligation required. Taxis company do not force you to drive, you can rest for 2 days for all they care, they care only about you paid rental or not. Seen you are given that freedom, it is up to you to take your own off day. If you do not like it, you can throw key after the contract or even during the contract with some compensation, same goes to the taxi companies, if they do not want you, they can remove you after the contracted period or even before the contract end by compensating you.
