    You can also apply for the Wharf Operation Supervisor (WOS) in PSA Terminals too. Your job is to supervise the movement of Quay Crane Operator, the Prime Movers, the loading and discharging containers, the Ship Traffic Assistant and lashers onboard the vessel and within your wharf operational section. Gross salary 2k plus with containers rate incentive, Overtime pay, Attendance allowance, Safety allowance, CPF and Medical benefits. You will be working beneath the quay crane, in a small air-condition cabin and monitor the movements with a computer. You have to attend 2 weeks WOS course in class, Attachments and OJT for 3 months at PSA Terminals and pass the test before you can be considered a qualified WOS. No experience required, no age limit regardless of genre. You can get in touch with PSA or the existing contractors that PSA has outsource this work to them. Its a 12 hours job shift if you work with PSA contractors and 8 hours job if you work with PSA. When you work with PSA outsource contractors, its 20 days work and 10 days off per month. For PSA, 8 hours shift (3 shifts) will be working for 6 days and 1 day off.
