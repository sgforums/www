    How many power sockets in your cab/car doesn't matter. Use the correct one. For the above mentioned camera, you need to use the socket which is engine "ON" power "ON" , engine OFF power "OFF". You can do a simple test to find out by using your car hand phone charger. Just ON/OFF engine and check the indicator light. i.e when engine is OFF the indicator light is still ON. Is a always ON power socket ! If you use the always "ON" power socket your camera will drained the car battery. When the engine is OFF. Camera with surveillance function need a always "ON" power socket. You might need to increase the car battery capacity plus some circuit modification.
