    Procedures in place to protect cabbies

    THE National Taxi Association and taxi companies are aware of the issues raised by Miss Janelle Seah ('Protect cabbies like my dad better'; March 22) and are equally concerned about enhancing the welfare of taxi drivers.

    Issues such as the safety of drivers and fare cheats had been discussed and deliberated

    previously at an industry level. We have also been working closely with the Land Transport Authority and the police on efforts to enhance taxi drivers' safety. Taxi companies have

    mechanisms in place to help drivers deal with emergencies such as robberies and fare cheating.

    As for the representation of self-employed taxi drivers, the association, which is affiliated with the National Trades Union Congress, fulfils such a role.

    A key aim of ours is to make representations on our members' behalf on matters affecting them to the relevant authorities, and promote cooperation and better understanding between members and companies.

    Wee Boon Kim
    President, National Taxi Association

    ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

     

    Dear Mr Kim,


    While i can agreed the National Taxi Association (NTA) being affiliated with NTUC has a role to fulfill, discussion and deliberation seems to be the most common answer given.

    But after all these many years, yet no action have been taken to protect the risk and lives of all taxi drivers.

    Taxi drivers are still risking their lives in the course of their work as a public transport provider.

    Every sons and daughters, brothers and sisters of a cabby are still praying for the safety for their loved ones whenever they leave home to earn three decent meals for the family.

    We don't need to see several more cabbies lose their arms and limbs, or even their lives before concrete action then be taken.

    We hoped we can see some precautionary measures taken as soon as possible rather than NTA doing lip services on behalf of all taxi drivers member. Thank you.
