    I hv seen and taken cabs with women Tds at night, but most only do night after doing day  for sometime and then confident about the switch.

    But I think your case as a mother, it's better for you to be home when your daughter finishes school, not just fetching and cooking which will make you very tired.

    Drive morning, come 4 or 5pm and then prepare dinner, check on your kid etc and then go to bed is best for family persons, not just female, most TDs will tell you that.

    As for fetching, honestly it doesn't work well for TDs to be doing this unless the first job is to send your spouse or kid to school,work, or the last job is fetching for home and then close shop.

    Let your kid take a school bus if very young or public if older. As a TD, you wanna be free to take any jobs at any time, if that timing your momentum is good why break it, in order to fetch? You can carry on, earn a bit more then next day, you can relax a bit.
