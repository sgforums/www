    First Day OTR

    0500

    - took the first feeder bus to hirer's place 2 bus stops away (actually can walk but lazy.. lol)

    - start car, adjust seat, figure out where everything is... err... where is the handbrake?

    - spend 5 mins laughing at myself (really laughed out damn loud) when i figured out the e-brake is foot brake

    - come out from mscp got better reception... logon mdt, cashless, on gps... accidentally pressed the OP on the taxi meter (means I have to pay myslef $4.50)!

    - turn out main road see a pax wave hand... before i can even on the hazard signal, one blue cab zapped in from dun know where and took the pax... relax!

    - first pax on board 0545!

    0600 - 1100

    - tried out different ways of finding pax: sweep, png, hotel lobby, airport q, mbs taxi stand, ikea taxi stand...

    - few times forget to start meter... two times pressed meter when no pax!

    1100 - 1230

    - breaktime @ geylang!

    - dun think crooked hor... went to temple at geylang east to pray for smooth smooth and eat vegetarian lunch...

    1230 - 1500

    - street jobs all the way... tried to take calls but always too slow...

    1500 - 1600

    - agar agar know where the cng station is... juz drive relax relax along aye - come out to commonwealth ave west... woah... many red red heading same direction... follow sure wont wrong de..

    - top up quite fast... while waiting... sum up collection - woah.. slightly more den expected...

    - put busy and straight back to home base...

    1600

    - when handover still fell like haven done enough... but after dinner and a nice shower - den realised actually quite tired out..
