    About TDVL Refresher Course

    In order for Taxi Drivers to stay relevant in this industry, they are required to attend the Taxi Refresher Course once every six years. This will equip them with the necessary skills to improve their services.

    It is an 10 hours theoretical lessons to be completed in one or two days (Subjected to the training centres schedule). In order to renew your Taxi Driver Vocational Licence attendance is complusory.

    The registration fee for the course is $50/$53.50 (Inclusive of GST) fees varies with different training centres. For course subsidy with UTAP please check here. Course fee include training materials and a copy of the latest street directory.

    As of 1 June 2012, TDVL holders will be allowed to extend their vocational licences up to 75 years old, subject to passing a special assessment annually.

    Applicants upon receiving the notice from LTA can register at any of the approved training centres:
