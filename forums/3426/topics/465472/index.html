    When ever involved in a accident. Before determine who is right or wrong First. Take photo of the accident of your vehicle and the other party vehicle of the position Take from few angle and also take close view of both vehicle damage. Then you determine the position and see if the case is more favourable to you or to other Party. Also note the number of pax of the other party.
