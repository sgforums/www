    A taxi driver pinned down a man who tried to flee without paying his fare of $18. 

    The passenger tried to run away when the taxi stopped at a traffic light junction of Woodlands Avenue 4 and 7 at about 12.40pm on Mar 18. 


    The cabby, Mr Chan, gave chase and caught the man, who was later arrested by the police. 



    The man had boarded the taxi in Jurong. His identity is still unknown.
