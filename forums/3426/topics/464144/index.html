    The Urban Redevelopment Authority (URA) is looking at turning Club Street and Ann Siang Road into pedestrian-only streets on two evenings a week.

    To gather feedback on the change, the URA sent letters to tenants in the area inviting them to dialogue sessions to discuss the possibility of closing the streets to traffic on Friday and Saturday evenings.

    The URA has confirmed that they are looking at this, but said it is still too early to talk about their plans at this stage.
