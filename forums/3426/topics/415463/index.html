    Lionel,

    I used to be LV member, but you must understand that to be a successful radio taxi call taker, you must be able to ambush the hot calls by staying around the right place at the right time. It takes considerable skill and experience make your LV membership worthwhile. Personally I do not find it worthwhile to pay >$65 every month for this service. In spite of whatever people say about comfort, they do have many good calls. I was able to grab calls even after midnight, and this is very rare in LV.

    Like you I also got a day job. I work 5 weekdays and drive every night. Everynight sure net $XXX between 4.30pm - 12.30am. Public holiday also same, just that I need to make about 18 trips. Weekdays after about 13 trips I can close shop with $XXX net. Enough time to sleep, enough energy to work next day.

    Dont waste your time worrying too much about SATS. You can try, but I made more money playing the CBD area than with SATS.

    For those $5 plus meter cases from LV, it is usually from those CBD border areas like Far East Plaza, Shaw Center types. The $5 is to entice taxi uncles to come in. If they already in CBD, no need to offer the extra $5 carrot right?
