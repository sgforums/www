    I think basically, the relationship between the taxis driver and the pax must be a mutual one, as said by some here, first, ask if Taxi uncle do not mind a $50, and as a Taxi driver, even if he do not have changes for a $50, he should inform the pax politely and the solution can be done by deciding on making a change at a petrol kiosk or 7 eleven, or coffe shops.

    Unlike food centres or shops, a $50 can be changed easily as many stalls are around, taxi do not have that luxury. If both the pax and the TD have that mutual understanding, I believe that even a $100 bill can be settle, and we can end up having a happy cabby and a happy pax.

    However pax should not take advantage in cases whereby the fare is $12 and the pax said to have only a $100 note and a $10 notes resulting in the TD taking the $10 and forgo the $2. I had met one of these kind before, Knn..give $1000 note, i also change for you.
