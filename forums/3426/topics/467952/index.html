    1. Throw out of the Taxi

    2. leave it at the back seat and let other passenger take it

    3. Drop it infront of the police station and drive away

    4. Throw it into rubbish bin or any public places

    5. Mail it back to the owner

    6. Pass it to MRT Control station counter

    7. Return it to the taxi company
