    Originally posted by ALN:

    teo, if want to keep blue tang healthy for long, what is the min tank specs har? 4ft with sump and solid skimmer?

    actually 4f is good enough though the bigger the better. better get those custom made type 4 by 2 by 2 is ideal. custom make the sump tank,cabinet & hood together.

    Of course if your budget allow,get the best skimmer u can.skimmer is very useful esp if u r keeping delicate fish that require excellent water qty.it will really remove huge amt of org waste from the water. Tangs require lots of green food like veg & algae.I feed my tangs with fresh "xiao bai chai" also feed them with fresh lettuce chop into very fine pieces.

    Tangs also require strong movement of the water so u can consider invest in a wavemaker. If these demands are not met,the tang will become stress & get "white spot" easily

    Yellow tang,purple tang & blue tang are moderate easy to keep,except they tends to be agressive towards its own kind.

    Tangs to avoid are Archilles Tang,Power blue tang,sohal tang etc...require excellent water condition & get "white spots" easily
