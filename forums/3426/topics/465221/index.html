    Transcab

    Toyota Wish 1.8

    Daily Rental with GST: $94.16 ~ $99.51 (Depending on age of vehicle)

    Rebate: $665 (OMO) $605 (hirer with relief)

    Rebate Breakdown:

    If driver don;t owe any rental and no service offences:

    Performance incentive :

    $250 ( If never owe )

    $160 ( if owe 1 day and less than 4 days)

    $100 ( If owe 5 days and less than 8 days)

    Road Tax rebate : $15

    Good Performance Incentive :

    $400 (If never owe rental more than 5 days)

    $200 (If owe more then 5days but less then 7 days)

    So total $665 if never owe rent and never give problem.

    Credited into your bank account on the 10th of every month, unless 10th falls on Sunday or Public Holiday then will be credited next working day. Cannot miss rental for one calendar month. Meaning if you start on 15th June, you will not get incentive on 10th July, only after whole July you don't miss rental payment.

    Depending on which day 10th falls on, usually they minus of that day rental and credit balance into your bank. As example, rental $94.16 and10th December was on Monday, usually Monday night will deduct Sunday + Monday Rental, but since incentive day they offset from rental. So Sunday+Monday rental won't deduct but they credit $665 - (94.16x2) = $476.68 into your bank.

    If pay rental by GIRO will deduct at night, some say 8pm but i notice mine 11pm then deduct. On the folder the give they state please bank in rental before 9pm daily and before 8pm end of the month.

    CNG Price at Toh Guan: $1.68/kg TransCab driver enjoy 7% discount before GST, add up GST 7% become same.

    Mileage for 1.8 Wish depends on condition on car. Generally 220km-250km must top up liao. But yesterday i manage to clock 310km still got one light left on CNG indicator. For the 310km i paid $30.02 CNG. But i don't think other Wish 1.8 can get that mileage, my wish full overhaul before, gearbox overhaul, ECU remapped, all 5 tyre including spare new, and just recently change drive shaft and full servicing. Normally before all that i can only manage 230km.

    Security Deposit : $1500, you can deposit $1000 first if you don't have enough and deposit another $500 more anytime, but if you don't pay the $500 before your first incentive rebate, they deduct $500 from your incentive so $665-$500=$165.

    On paper it state can deposit $300/$500/$1000/$1500 but the girl only say $1500.

    If we follow paper if u deposit

    $300 can owe up to 3 days rental.

    $500 can owe up to 5 days rental

    $1000 can owe up to 7 days rental

    $1500 can owe up to 9 days rental.

    If you choose to pay rental by GIRO Sunday there is no rental deduction. On Monday it will deduct 2 days rental (Sunday + Moday). If Monday holiday, then on Tuesday will deduct 3 days rental (Sunday + Monday + Tuesday)

    Other stuff:

    Admin Fee (One time): $32.10

    Windscreen Insurance (Yearly): $32.10

    CNG card deposit: $10 (pay to CNG station)

    4 day leave per year, March, June, Sept, December (Applicable to no outstanding Rental of more than 8 days)

    Loyalty bonus $500 (Yearly)

    Accident Free incentive $100 (Yearly)

    After 8 yrs of service bonus: $2080

    Additional Info on TransCab Wish 1.8 not covered on my earlier post

    FAQ

    Q: What if i owe one day rental, how much incentive i get?

    A:

    Miss one day rental up to 4 days you will only get

    $160 (Performance Incentive)

    + $400 (Good Performance Incentive)

    + $15 (Road tax rebate)

    So total $575

    If miss 5 - 6 days

     

    $100 (Performance Incentive)

    + $200 (Good Performance Incentive)

    + $15 (Road tax rebate)

    So total $315

    If miss 7days

     

    $100 (Performance Incentive)

    + $15 (Road tax rebate)

    So total $115

    Q: If month end i deposit rental after 8pm, for GIRO deduction will they consider it as miss 1 day rental?

    A: YES! If for example month end 31st Jan u deposit after 8pm and GIRO deduction fail, your incentive (10th Feb) will only get $575 instead of $665

    Q: What is the 4 day leave thing?

    A: Basically it's 4 rent free days per year.

    Other stuff:

    There is $1 before GST off rental price yearly, base on the date your vehicle is registered.

    For example, 

    My WISH 1.8 vehicle registration date is 22-Jan-2009

    My rental is $94.16 (inclusive GST)

    On 22 Jan 2013, they minus off $1.07

    So from 22 Jan 2013 onwards my rental is $94.16 - $1.07 = $93.09

    So 22 Jan 2014 they will deduct $1.07 again.

    This one not official, but i just got one time $8 recently stated as yearly rental rebate, nothing in writing to explain it.

    Accident Claims and Repair

    Accident liabilities:

    - Not at fault

    - 50 / 50 (Excess applicable) meaning investigation shows both party at fault usually the case for accident at junction, etc. TD must pay Excess.

    - At Fault (Excess applicable)

    Accident Excess:

    $2140 (Inclusive 7% GST) per accident claim.

    Driver can either pay by installments or offset the incentive (If any).

    Coverage:

    - Towing Fee

    - Downtime Refund

    - Repair Cost

    - Third party's claim

    Windscreen / Door Glass Breakage:

    (Unlimited replacement of windscreen within a policy year)

    Insurance premium 

    Toyota Crown - $21.40

    Toyota Wish - $32.10

    Mercedes Benz - $42.80

    Operating Hours for Workshop

    Monday to Saturday:

    0800 - 1700 hrs

    Sunday & Public Holiday

    0800 - 1200 hrs

    If vehicle got problems after workshop hours, but still driveable, example when my radiator leaking every hour must top up water at 10pm, just drive to workshop, park in front of workshop gate. 

    Remove any valuable, lock the vehicle, pass the key to the diesel pump attendant in front of office. Write the problem, time and date you "check in" your vehicle on the book, if don't know where the book ask the pump attendant.

    Walk home, take bus or MRT. Best solution take a  TC cab, no need wait so long cos surely got cab pump diesel. If really nobody pump diesel, walk outside sure got CDG cab, cos they have diesel pump somewhere there. Supoort your local cabbies!

    If vehicle not driveable, example engine can't start or gearbox problem, happen to me twice. Call the tow team!

    Downtime for both situation will be given, but do take note, if call tow, they minus 1 hour.

    Meaning if u call tow at 715am, your downtime deduction only start at 815am
