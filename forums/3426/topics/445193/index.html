    Originally posted by kengkia:

    how does the radio call system works in general?


    Huh ? Hmm .... all we need is a P-T-T walkie talkie . Every one of us , aka the members , including the Call Centre .

    PTT walkie talkie is activated by Sim card too , like mobile phone . In fact PTT walkie talkie is a mobile phone , which come with radio transmissions .

    In Singapore , only Grid Communication provide such services .

    Not Singtel , not M1 or Starhubs .

    Commuters need a cab , eg , they have our contact 6254-1117 .

    Jenny our Call Centre In charge , will answer if u call in for a cab .

    She will listen to your pick up point , and destination u are going , and she will use that walkie talkie to page out the jobs .

    At the meantime , u hold on for less than 1 min , in 1 min u will know if any cab available for you . In fact , all those paging and response , u can hear all !

    It's 100% Human Technology .

    It's an old method , but effective method to get a cab .
