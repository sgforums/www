    13 May 12, 06:07

    Bros decent02, Moring if kenna Benoi, Gul, Pioneer or Tuas

    From 0700am to 0910am immediate goto Joo Koon Circle MRT, Join the Q provide
    JKC - Benoi or JKC - Pioneer or JKC - Gul or JKC - Tuas shuttle service.
    (You may see 8~10 Cabs Q Up, But it take you ONLY 5~10 Minutes)

    Every short trip take you 10~20 Minutes include Q time and Pay you $ 8 - $12.
    If you fast enough, you can get $30 Per Hour.

    If kena Tuas South etc, 2 Hours you also can do 3 Trip of Tuas South.
    Each trip $15~$18, 2 Hours you still can get about $50.

    Every moring, You can see 20~30 Blue, Red, Gold regular shuttle service there.

    After 0910am, don't waste time to Q there, Becuase the Q Up time getting longer.
    Goto JW St 6x, JW St 7x, JW St 8x there are a lot of Pax going to CCK, Bk PJ etc
    (But don't goto JW St 91, 92 or 93, because too many Blue there)

    After 0910am, Or You can take AYE goto Taman Jurong,

    After 0910am, Or go even further to Bk Bt or Hillview, 0900am to 0930am Hv many Pax go downtown or CBP.

    After 0910am, Or you can take KJE to CCK, Bk PJ, There are also a lot of Down town Pax.

    Try to don't go Boon Lay MRT or Jurong MRT, Becuase you sure Kena Penjuru or Jurong Island.

    1030am onward just go around the Chu Ka, You will pick Up Ah Soo from CCK to Bk PJ @ $5~$6,  Pick up Ah Gong from Boon Lay to JE........

    Some time it can been non stop till Noon time.
