    STOMPer Dat Mago was incensed at the actions of a taxi driver, who swerved into his lane recklessly to pick up a passenger. When confronted, the cabby challenged the STOMPer to 'give me a fine lah'.

    The STOMPer wrote:

    "I was sending my wife to work, with my 2 children, when this taxi driver dangerously cut into my lane to pick up a passenger who flagged him down.

    "Luckily, I managed to jam the brakes although it caused me to skid a little bit. The car behind nearly knocked into me as well thanks to this driver.

    "To make matters worst, he even said to me, 'fine me lah. What the hell, man'.

    "Luckily, I have a dashboard installed to record this incident.

    "So, taxi drivers, please don't jeopardize the lives of other people!"
