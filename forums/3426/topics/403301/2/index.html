    Originally posted by Ronghua6636:

    hi guy, i still waiting for the LTA letter after 2weeks of waiting.So question this online-course is faster that full time class course izzit?

    Full time class is 3 weeks (I think...).  Whereas for online course, it's own time own target.  How fast depend on your own progress.

    The thing is that, with online course, you need to "pass" an online quiz for the different modules before being allowed to book the excursion.  This excursion is compulsory and your test date will be allocated on the excursion day as well.  The thing is.. between passing the online quiz and the excursion, there could be a gap of 2-3 weeks. And, between the excursion and the actual test is another 1 week at least.

    So, if you take your time to study and pass the online quiz, you will still be stuck with 2-3 weeks of wasted time before attending the excursion.

    So, what some of us do is that we purchase the course manual and try to "pass" the online quiz fast by doing an open book test.  I'm not that smart and I took 2 hours to pass the online quiz.  You can see from some replies above that some did it within 1.5 hours. Once you passed the online quiz, you can then book the excursion.  Then, you can take your own sweet time to go through the course in detail...

    Won't actually say it's really faster lah... If you look at the time frame from signing up for the online course to taking the actual test, it's also about 3-4 weeks.  So, it's like LPPL. :)  Only scenario where it may be faster is if the classroom course is available at a much later date.  Like in my case, online course start on 8/7/10 whereas for classroom course, it's only available in mid July (if I recall correctly).

    HTH.
