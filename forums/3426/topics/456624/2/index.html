    i think i am one of the newest drivers here and the least qualified to offer any insights onto the TS's point.

    Its true that TS point does act as a "not so desirable" incentive to go in to the CBD for booking calls.  I have been analysing the points:

    You have to go through many gantries that may amount to more than $3 - I thought for the booking to come in, you should be in the vicinity and likely that you have already passed some if not all the gantries (either via touring around or via previous passenger drop offs?).

    Besides, assuming you paid $3 to go in, in the end you get $3 CBD surcharge + $3.30 booking + Meter Fare, isnt that a good deal? even if you breakeven and dont earn any CBD surcharge and get $2.30 as booking fee (loss of $0.70) for a ready passenger just on the next few streets, is not so bad at all also right? or would you rather drive around with uncertainty and loss diesel and time?

    seriously, if it was me, i wont mind just driving nearby to pick up and loss/gain abit of $ rather than tour around and waste time and diesel and maybe end up even no passenger for the next 20 min.  Of course ideally is our previous passenger brings us in the ERP and bears all the charges and we soon soon take a booking nearby and hit jackpot but it doesnt happen this way all the time.
