Interesting Finale

  • Bingjames
    30 Jan 12, 07:48

    Have quite a good run on Sat night ending Sunday morning.

    was thinking of rounding it up by getting a paid trip home.

    Heard Clarke Quay's street all full of passenger waiting for taxi, so put my MDT on changing shift mode - CCK (pump diesel)

    Came from Sentosa, so took the CTE tunnel and exit from Merchant Rd, wow, pax were literally waiting for me at the mouth of the CTE Merchant exit. whole road full of pax.

    Now, apply the new pattern that I have learn, be more selective.

    First few are groups of 4, all skip.

    Finally, stop for a couple, later I realised, Indian guy and Pinoy gal, looked at my Changing Shift location and ask "Uncle, Chinatown can?"

    Then I remark " Chinatown? here is Chinatown already!"

    Then he said "Chinatown Hotel81". Huh? Just one turn, I tell myself.

    Then I told them ok, come onboard.

    By right, just turn right to New Bridge Road, just one bus stop, reached liao.

    But to make it more "value for money" for him, I take Pickering, South Bridge Rd, Upper Cross Street and stop at door of Hotel81, meter still the same, $3.20.

    They happy, I happy, collect money and tell the guy "u enjoy".

    Turn right and have another go at Clark Quay again.

    Again, do my screening and pick up one young couple. When i stopped for them, this guy raised his hand in jubilation as if he just scored a goal. Must have flag many taxis and one finally stop for them.

    I pointed to the Change Shift location and the girl said "Uncle, Bt Panjang?"

    wah lao, like going home, I said "Hop in"

    When I drive further down, the girl was winding down the window and calling to her friend and the guy comment "do u really have to show off that you finally got yr cab?"

    Reached destination and stop meter - $13.20, gosh, Clark Quay to Bt Panjang only $13.20, so dam cheap. Whos' talking about taxi fare expensive?

    Anyway, trip home is paid for, so nothing to complain about.
