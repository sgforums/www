    In this industry a 'fair deal' is an agreement that both parties are happy

    Some examples :

    Day shift usually don't pump diesel, reason being handover is very rush, the Q at the diesel kiosk is very long, many uncles at the washing bay, so count by mileage

    Night shift is the one that pumps diesel and wash car

    Base on my experience, the fifth generation sonata abt 2 ~3 years old, see pic below, can go 9 ~ 10 km per litre of diesel


     

    Those face lift versions which are less than 1 year old, see pic below, can go 10 ~ 11km per litre of diesel

      

    But the above is only a guide, it all depends on how heavy you right foot is and also are you cruiser or taxi stander, city or expressway traveller
