    2 common reasons why some disagree on taxi fare hike.

    1. Passengers stay away -- This is only temporary. When they need a cab they will be back at the taxi stand. Even without increase, they will not board your cab if they don't need one.     2. Taxi fare increase, then surely rental also increase -- Taxi rental isn't going to go down or remain the same for long. Unless there is a serious economic crisis here in this region. Taxi drivers here will be downgraded to cheap labourers.
