    1 thing I always do if I take call and I very special one, only take call if I like the sound of passenger name, the timing after midnight, the distance is within range (no traffic jam, roundabout way to get there) and not in an area where many street hire cabs are available depending on time (if say AMK Ave 3 at 815pm, and you are at AMK ave 10, by the time you come there in 3-5 mins, passenger already find another cab).

    The 1 thing i do is always press 3 min, even if i am far away, because if you press 5, 7 0r 10 min, you go there, pax already ciao or never come, you gonna have to wait another 5 min before system will let you cancel.

    Also I never call pax on my hp, unless really absolutely necesarry, you call on your hp and he answer, you committed to wait for him. Just go and press 3 min, wait max 5 min and then leave, no extra extra unless after midnight in some ulu place where you know pax cant run away and you cant find another fare for next 20 mins.

    If you reach and press arrive and call, and suddenly pax cancel, no need to waste time, put busy and dont take any other pax from same area. Some very cunning think very smart and cancel so can hop on taxi without booking charge. Never mind let him win by cancel, but you dont lose by taking for nothing in same area.

    Thats my advice.
