When otr, Khoon always teach me where to find pax and guide me through alot things which really help me alot when driving. For me, it really make my driving easier n i learn many things from him, slowly i adjusted n found a driving pattern for myself which i feel very comfortable now. As a OMO really not easy, need to find a suitable driving style n timing so that not to burn out myself and can go far. Anyway, i find my 1st month in TD very interesting. Like everyday otr is a new experience and meet many different ppl n get to drive to different places in sg which as a local oso dint realise that there are such places. The only bad point is i got into 3 accidents which all are "so called" bang by ppl. Imagine turning left into a street and the car next to me can oso come into my lane n bang into me. And recently involve in a chain accident which I am the 2nd car. Who more unlucky than me? Ofc I feel very sad about all these but I take it as a experience and I am now enjoying my holiday at home. Heeheehee..... Lastly, keep driving happily n stay healthy everyday to all my TD brothers.... Hope to see and learn more from all brothers in here and share more of the experience....
