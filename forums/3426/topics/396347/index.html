    I have set a standard for myself which I named it as Dollar Per Kilometer(play too much MMORPG).

    A TD fully maximise his fuel usage when the gap between the 2 is small. As such, my take is that short distant trip is better. TD earn more with shorter distant travelled unless the TD like to idle his engine for long period of time.

    However, in reality, time and combination of both long and short trip will go a long way. As such, I see no point is arguing whether long or short trip is better. We need both. Very rare I see every single trip we pick up during the shift is less than 5km and roaming/waiting time is near to 0.

    A wise TD, in my own opinion, will know roughly when he need to catch up on his dollars vs mileage. Therefore what is really important is the mindset and experiences of the TD to fully maximise his earnings.
