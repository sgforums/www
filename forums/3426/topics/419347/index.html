    And worst case senerio:-

    Will I be able to stop visit there again if I suffered big losses? Probably not as most ppl will want to take revenge and the repurcusion can be unthinkable...

    _________________________________________________________________

    So the reason for posting this thread is to serve as a wake up call to myself as well as a reminder to all friends in the same trade. I noticed quite a handful of taxis parked in the parking lots and even bumped into one of my course mate in there...

    The temptation will always be there especially when we are so exposed to the casinos, so please do not get into any situation where it's too late to turn back...
