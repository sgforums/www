    Congrats bro LDM!

    Your situation is exactly the same as  mine two years ago. Waiting for TVDL before CNy and later LTA give us the license inside a red packet :)

    When unker Poolman say, you should pay attention, even though he had said it for one thousand two hundred and thirty forth time. First one to learn is the CBD and expressway exits.

    I think for new cabbies, it will take around two weeks to learn all the expressway exits. Then take another two to learn the major roads in the housing estate (eg, AMK Ave1/3/5/6/8/10, etc). After that maybe a few more weeks before learn the smaller streets (eg, AMK St 11/21/31/41/51/61, etc).
