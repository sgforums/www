    Here's some tips from Poolman on CNY night shift drivng:-

    The common strategy for CNY EVE is to "attack" places with massive flow of tourists .

    Pls do not waste time go places like industrial park , office towers , all half day .

    ERP also off half day .

    Airport will be one place with not much taxis , esp 4pm onwards to 6pm , cos majority chinese cabbies makan at that time .

    Zoo all the way . Zoo is one good place u can connect pax to town ( hotels ) .

    MBS and RWS will be one place where Singaporean will go .

    Bird Park also one place .

    Shopping arcade like Orchard stretch , got timing one , 4pm onwards , to 7pm .

    Pls do not waste time cruising from 5pm to 8pm at HDB area .

    All still eating , hdb area's street will be one hell of quiet .

    Harbourfront will be the worst place to hail for a cab

    Place like HDB shopping mall .... u will expect good human Q .

    But f*ck la , all is like Lot-1 to CCK St 51 , waste time type .

    Then from 7pm , loose crowds at city area .

    Cos most shopping arcade closed at 6pm .

    From 7pm onwards , time to get back to basic , HDB .

    Chinese ?

    Mix .

    Boyfriend go to girlfriend house .

    Movie goers see movies .

    3 words : Non stop actions .

    9pm .... still hdb .

    Be prepare to sent families down to Chinatown or Marina area .

    Chinatown , close for sure .

    Pls alight paxes at Outram or Chinatown Point , tradtional la knn .

    Pls inform paxes in advance : " Jam hor ! "

    Question : 9pm onwards , town how ?

    Bad !!!

    Cos all taxis head to Chinatown . Orchard shops all closed !

    Then how ?

    Shoot back to HDB la .

    Or try RWS or MBS , will be slow , not fast .

    Not forgetting many taxis also at MBS and RWS .

    Another question .

    Many ang mor as usual ?

    Answer : Nope !

    Most ang mors will stay home , or go short trips to Batam , Bintan , KL , Thailand , Phuket , Langkawi , Bali etc .....

    9pm to 12MN , still HDB to HDB , the best !

    Be prepare to see coffee shops most closed .

    Places that will need taxis badly ?

    Woodlands , Jurong , Sengkang , Punggol , Bt Batok , Yew Yee , Yishun , Sembawang .

    Cos most taxis at all over Singapore , not Saturated .
