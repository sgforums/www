    Bukit merah central hawker centre (old HDB):

    Char kway teow (no lard, small hums) corner near CK store, used to sell for only $2 till cannot tahan several months back then up to $2.50. To me, really nice!

    Opp cut fruit stall 40 cents per piece. opp stall kopi-O 60 cts.

    Many other nice food there too.

    There is everything you want there for taxi admin. OUB bank/cash dep m/c (main rd jln bt merah), POSB atm, 4D, 7eleven, NTUC and many more...

    Only thing most times hv to wait a bit (usually not more than 5 mins, 10 am plus or 11 am plus) to enter the car park (erp) underneath hawker centre. Sometimes if I dun want to wait, will circle to bus interchange mscp behind. Walk a bit, good exercise before/after food.
