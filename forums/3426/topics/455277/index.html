    When to OMO?

    - You need a car to do other stuff that have a fixed schedule (ferry loved ones for medical, run errands)

    - You drive Taxi just to earn 'pocket money'

    - You are so dedicated to driving that you dont need to have 'off days'

     

    When NOT to OMO ?

    -  You have a tendency to 'park' the taxi

    -  You cannot do split shift (unable to rest in between)

    -  You cannot drive long hours (old or medical problem)

    -  You cannot accept the fact that Reliefs takes 2 hrs to break even, whereas you take 5 hrs.

    -  You have a lot of 'other things' to do

    -  You want to spend more time with your loved ones (your pet dog?)
