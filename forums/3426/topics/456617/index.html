So those just outside the line of CBD, don't blame us, LTA do not have any encouragement for us to serve you yet.

    Pax, Paxes...... where are you

     

    We talk about the unavailability of Taxi for decade...... with a s.....decades. And i believe we will be still talking about it for decade.... with a s....decades.

    This lure me into the trade thinking it's good money making here.

     

    I do my streching, rubbing my fist and get ready for showtime. Who's knows........... it's the reverse...... where are the paxes?

    I use 80/20 rule, 80% of our time are in searching (sweeping), queueing for paxes and 20% of our time are then into business. Well, this is what business is about.

     

    During low period, my record is sweeping for 1 hour 23 mins, and got a pax for a $4.80 trip. (i follow regulation for not joing into queueing when there is more then designated waiting lots available in queue) 

    Almost every traffic light change, there will be more then 1 (that's me) empty, hungry Taxi in search for pax. During waiting at traffic light change, there will be more then 1 empty, hungry Taxi passes by my intention routes.

    It require high level of accuracy, the pace of pax walking toward the road side and the pace my my vehicle moving, it take less then a second to hit nicely.

     

    I have to break the rule, queue at the Taxi Stand. It is very risky, might require to use more then my daily income to pay for a photography services.(worst is, i don't even get to see how posh i look in the vehicle)

    Queueing at Taxi Stand is slightly better, it is almost a guarantee pax within 15 mins. But is usually a feeder service  (80/20 rule apply). 15 mins of waiting, a 5 mins trip, 5 mins to return back and another 15 mins of waiting.........40 mins for $4.80. If god is blessing, still manage to do 3 trips........ i used up my blessing for $15 in an hour.

    That is reality during low period. It's ok, i still have my peak period, my 20%.

     

    During peak, we enjoy additional 25% above meter. Filled my stomach, do my streching, rub my fist and ready, it's showtime.

    Taxi Driving, Career ,Transit or Loitering

     

    Career, is a job one can built, bring oneself to another level for a better future. 

    What can we build as a Taxi Driver?

    From a normal cabby to a limo cabby, from a limo cabby,built up clientee base to a Private Limo driver, from driver to boss........

    It is still a path one can consider. It's not the best option, but just another option.

     

    Transit, is a cross over period in our career life.

    Taxi Driving is a good transit platform, it provide food to keep us going and freedom of time to search as well.

    Do not fall into the trap if one is taking this as a transit, chasing for money. It will exhaust your burning desire to success. That extra few hours is enough to put mind and body into a procrastination stage or even pull you away, further to your intented destination.

    By the time you realise, you are actually Loitering.

     

    Loitering, is a stage when one is living aimlessly, from days... to years...... to even decades.

    Your life is fix, Your status is fix, Your history is fix

    Do not need a crystal ball, you can almost tell your future.

     

    There is no right or wrong here, there is only what we want.

    And are we moving to that direction?
