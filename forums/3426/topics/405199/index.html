  • La2534
    14 Jul 10, 00:58

    Initially is like that one. Drive long hours to learn. But it will get easier. This was wad I do,

    1) As you drive, observe where all the official taxi stands are found. Also keep a look out on places where u find long taxi queue on certain places with no taxi stand.
    2) Do not be afraid in unfamiliar ground. When in taxi queue or having a break, just flip through the street directory just to get a rough idea on where the main streets are.
    3) Always ask yourself some simple questions like :

    - During lunch time, where u think the crowd will be in the area. For eg, if u at Jurong East. International Business Park is a good place to hunt.
    - Or peak hour, Ask yourself, where are the offices found. If not go for the nearest hospital (eg If in Hougang or Sengkang - u can try IMH or Serangoon Nt Ave 5 area)

    4) Remember all the surcharges well.

    For a start, just do this will do. Just keep on driving. After 1-3 months u will notice you only need to drive shorter hours to earn more money.

    When you are more experienced, then you can start to plan a rough schedule. For eg, by 5pm you want to go near city, then go to the places where you know the chances to fetch pple to city is greater. It may not be always accurate, but you will notice you can steer urself to a particular area some of the time.
