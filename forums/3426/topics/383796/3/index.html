    Originally posted by Navyseal sg:

    Hi Mr Poolman,

        Wats the meaning of 35% charged during driving and hw it works at all....

     

    Is the any way to know the CBD areas and the surchage....Thanks

    35% charged means additional 35% of fare would be charged on top of ur original trip fare. E.g. ur trip fare is $14. additional $4.90 would be charged to ur trip fare. So instead of paying $14 , u pay $18.90 during peak hours [ morning 7~10, evening 5~8](not so sure about this). Let's say midnight. It's no longer 35%. It's 50% additional. Same rules , $14 original trip fare , u will end up paying $7 extra. So u will pay $21 when u travel by taxi after midnight. Feel free to ask questions again in the future.
