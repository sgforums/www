    Taxi meter for Premier and Prime

    Pax on board,

    1. Press the HIRED button to start meter.

    2a. To add surcharge like CBD or Airport surcharge, press the HIRED button once to call up the display under the "Extras" display section.

    2b. Press the EXTRAS button to cycle through the appropriate surcharge then press the PRINT button to confirm it.

    3. End of trip, press STOP.

    4. To show total fare, press the EXTRAS button once.

    5. Press the PRINT button to print receipt.

    6. Press the VACANT button to clear meter. NOTE: Once cleared, cannot print receipt anymore.
