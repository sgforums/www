    When ON CALL , how to get the taxi number on your roof top ????

    Press K3 , until you find VEH NO , confirm by pressing K4 .

    U'll have your taxi number plus ON CALL on your taxi LED indicator .

    Note : U must be ON CALL then can do it , normal For Hire cannot !

    For Comfort fleets , button is K2 . Reverse of TransCab .
