    hi Ah Cheng

    I m not  TD yet but i just finished TVL test, so can kp advise abit

    is all MCQ, all 5 sections divided into 2 sessions.

    my test started on 2pm, 1st sessions include section1 (rules & regulations) and section2 (Routes & landmarks), total 80 questions.

    after 1st session, they ask u go for  a short break for kopi and come back at 4pm to continue 2nd session, (section 3, section4,section5) also 80 questions

    my experience is that section 2 (routes & landmarks) is most tough. i almost failed by 2 questions coz most routes are similar, my friend failed becoz of section 2 also. so put more effort on "routes and landmarks"

    As for section 5, u can get tips (think already posted  in this forum). thanks to that as well coz i aso benefit from the tips.

    The rest of the sections are quite common sense type, but there are few i think i got it wrong like. some question i can rem :

    tyre rotations, how many km need to rotate, bus lane timing , starting fare, midnite surcharge calculation (calculator provided), public holidays surcharge (only 5 holidays)

    how long to renew TVL ? ...

     

    paya laber ways (2 landmarks), bedok to paragon route, bukit batok to alexandra hosp, chaichee to indo embassy....anyway try extreme hard on this section. coz retest need to take 3 weeks.

    I always feel that the faster route is the better route, but I did not become a millionaire, so don't take my word for it. I took the online course with SMRT thinking it would be faster but I think it did not.

    There was a complusory excursion that we had to take, which I attended with 30-40 people (I did not physically count them). An instructor from LTA took us on a bus ride on the most important routes that is inline with the test. Apart from the online assignments, this was really beneficially, he bascially brought us to see things that I never knew existed in Singapore and I guess would really help in our work later as taxi drivers.

    And all of this was before the test in which I took last friday and passed and is waiting for the VTL.

    It is expected that I would be recieving the VTL next Tuesday. In a reality time check, online course still took a month if you have done everything right.
