    When you stop the meter (maybe at 0605 hrs), mdt reflect the total fare as at 0605 hrs.

    But if you observe next time, the meter fare will continue to increase (even though meter is stopped) whereas the mdt amount will not change. That's why when you print using K1, info (from the meter) on the receipt is different from that on mdt.
