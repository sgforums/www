  • bowah
    27 Dec 10, 16:21

    My Hirer only 33yo, taxi drivers is not an advancing job, after 15 years, peoples working in corporation may go up to managers, supervisors or some senior techs, even food seller can expand business and can drive merc, taxi driver would still be a taxi driver, you can said you are very experience and skill in driving and directions but there are no grade, no designation, and certainly no promotion, just a couple of not so grand awards maybe. But then, once you are in, after a few years of driving, it is very hard for you to go back to corporate world or take up some career courses.

    You can said you earn 4k hard cash every month, the same people who earn 3k still get employer CPF contribution to top it up, medical n dental benefits, leaves, paternity and maternity leaves, bonuses, allowances, overtime...and so on. And bear in mind your 4k (if you can earn that  much), not only depend on economy, you also have to work very hard, stressed, get old easily with an occupational hazard of digestive system and urinal congestion, 30s look like 40s already. Then there is accident to care for, once hit, you may have to lost alot, unlike working, go 8 to 5pm, any extra will be o/t, can take leaves, do not need to be so alert, eat snakes abit, and never worry about paying companies but then, have to look at peoples face to work, whereas taxi face direct customers.

    But then, TD has it fun side too, own time, own target. It is good to see young peoples taking taxi jobs, as for now, times are good, most of these young peoples may not experience the bad times which hopefully will not happen. I hope they stay on and deprive the foreigners, especially the PR prowling to get into this trade.
