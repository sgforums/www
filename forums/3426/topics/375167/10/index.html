    Midnight is very challenging and different from day time driving. You have to find out which Q area is better for you one by one and slowly increase your options. It is different sometime and hard to tell. So increase your own database will help you in the long run.  Some Q maybe super long but it is moving fast...

    One beauty of Midnight is --- any pax - any where in singapore is the same "power fare".

    Day time -- there are many Q at MRT, hospitals, shopping mall type....unfortunately i don't Q at those areas and i hate it....I most of the time RTS.

    Day time - don't underestimate 4.50 to 6.00 trip... i love those if you always get connections one after next....it is a powerful type in half an hour almost 18 to 20 provided you know the are quite well.  No driving stress somemore...Just turnaround 4.50 and another turnaround 5.00....
