  • StriveOn
    5 Dec 12, 08:52

    The idea is not to remember by heart

    But to have a strategy to find a location fast.

    Follow what is taught, dont short cut the process.

    For example, to find a POI, some go straight to the page and find. WRONG.

    You have to go to page 87 to find the map grid,

    THEN go to the Map page to scan that grid.

    It seems like more work but actually it is faster.

    Module 4 is actually very easy.

    Follow the route given in the MCQ. When you notice there is an illogical jump, (that means they missed one road), then eliminate that choice. Very fast can get the answer.

    Hope it helps.
