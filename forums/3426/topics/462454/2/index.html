    Have just passed mine today, so thought can share with you the experience.

    Basically for the ENTIRE test, it is not based on luck. It is based on SPEED.

    Simply put, for module 3, you only have 1 min 12 sec for EVERY single question. For module 4, 2 mins each question. So you have to challenge yourself to answer the qeustion within this time frame. How? By simply racing yourself aginst the clock, you have to think and make decision fast, if you can't answer it, mark it for review and go on to the next qn. Answer as many of the 50 qns you can, as my instructor said, the very difficult one, just let it go... This would help you increase your chance of passing greatly. My fellow classmates today couldn't finish the all questions, some left 5 unanswered.

    Another way to find what you want fast is to use coloured rulers in the index pages, how? Treat me kopi, I'll show you how. Remember that on the first page is the contents page for the index, forget which page Private Housing is on, flip to the first page and find! It will save you precious seconds for every question!

    I have some worksheets you can practise on for module 3 and 4, but best not to put up public here, however I can meet you and pass to you.

    For module 4, did you watch the DVDs given? Watching the DVD and plot it against the 40 routes papers given, you will see a lot of landmarks. Some people will say the DVDs are useless, but watch it and plot against the paper route given, you will find it easier to remember the routes.

    Familiarize your Orchard area, City area maps, places like Shenton Way area, Suntec area, Chinatown area. This are areas with many roads and they like to kill you there, cos you will spend a lot of time trying to look for a bloody building or taxi stand.

    For the 30 routes, which route to go if you are given 3 different routes? Take the route with the most ERP gantries. Dun believe me, try the route from airport to four seasons hotel. You will see what I mean, there are other alternative that DO NOT NEED to pass through ERP.

     Hope this helps....
