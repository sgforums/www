    If U respond to current booking (under 30 mins), a booking fee of $2.30 applies (uniform to all taxi companies), or a booking fee $8.00 to advance booking (31 minutes or more).

    $3 surcharge is meant for passengers taking cab from CBD (5pm to 12am daily).

    25% surcharge of basic fares is meant for peak hours' demand (6pm to 12am daily).

    Starting Fare: $8 + $3 + 25% fare + flagdown fare.

    Hope this helps U.
