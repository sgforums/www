    From Tampines to Orchard, look around Orchard see got what major road/expressway. CTE comes to mind.

    Then see any expressway/major road link to CTE that leads to Tampines. PIE comes to mind. So using this reverse "engineering", it's easier to get a picture of the route.

    You can also take the new Bartley Road East->KPE->Nicoll H'way

    Or Bartley Road East->Bradell->CTE

     

    Sometimes it depends on which part of Tampines you're talking about and also the pax's preference.

    The most important thing is know all the expressway well and you will solve 70~80% of the equation.

    Building like Hyatt & Hilton (one on Scott Rd, the other Orchard Rd), Spore Cricket Club & Spore Recreation Club (both on Padang), you need to think of a way to remember them.

    Hyatt is on Scott Rd b'cos of the "tt" so Hilton cannot be on Scott Rd so it must be Orchard Rd.

    Spore Cricket Club is near to the clock tower b'cos Cricket starts with C. Then Spore Recreation Club is on the other end.

    Similarly for Woodlands Circle & Woodlands Ring Rd. See the Admiralty MRT? The R in MRT tells you that Ring Rd is near the MRT. Circle is on the other side.

    You need to devise your own way to remember places with similar and confusing names.
