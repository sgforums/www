  • TaxiNoob
    16 Sep 10, 12:35

    Though I just started driving for less than a month... (Wait!! Tomorrow marks the 1 month anniversary of my TVL collection...), I already forgot my first pax liao. LOL...

    However, until today, I still kenna destination that I only know the general direction to the area but not the specific place that pax wanna go to.  Hence, most importantly is to be frank to them and explain that you are new lah, still trying to familiarize with routes lah... Most paxes are understanding and some will even encourage you verbally.  The most important thing to avoid is not to tell pax that you don't know the way and then in the end bing them on a merry-go-round.  If pax can't give direction, then probably it's best to tell them to get another taxi. (Though I have never had to resort to that since I had a GPS and on the rare occassions that pax don't know the way, they are ok with me referring to the GPS.)

    Hope this helps...
