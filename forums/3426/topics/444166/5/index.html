    ration party starts around 5am, prepare all the containers & cutleries. go cook hse & get the food. sometimes have to "ta pau" into styrofoam box instead of putting in containers. travel to exercise ground & serve. must wait till all have eaten then pack up, go back camp to wash up the mess then prepare for lunch.

    after lunch will be dinner & night snack. after serving night snack, back to camp & finish washing up it's about 2am+. sleep till 4.45am & prepare for the next day. more "shiong" than dig trench. only plus point is can go back camp & bath.
