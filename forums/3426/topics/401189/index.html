    Just made a decision to book a Toyota Prius with Prime after considering between the Honda FIT and Toyota Prius.

    Reasons to choose the Prius even when it's rent is $20 more than the FIT,

    1. Fare starts $0.20 more than FIT. For a 20~25 trips working day, it's $4~5 extra.

    2. Conservative fuel consumption estimate for Prius is 20km/litre (FIT is 15~17km/l). Another $5 savings in fuel after about 16% discount at ESSO station for a 400km work day.

    3. $5000 bonus after 5 years contract - more attractive should I look for someone to take over my remaining contract. ie. easier to "sell" my remaining contract. FIT DOES NOT comes with the $5000 bonus.

    4. Bigger luggage compartment.

    Test drive the FIT (actually my hirer test drive it and I was at the back seat) and I felt that,

    1. it is quite narrow at the back seat, I don't feel comfortable in the back seat, especially the head rest.

    2. not very stable at speed of 90+Km/h, the tyre size is 175 only. Could be due to the test drive was done on Tuas/AYE, many big vehicles like cement & container trucks caused the FIT to sway slightly as these big trucks passes.

    3. a bit noisy when compared to the Prius.

    4. need to pay $500 cash for the wrapping of the floor and the seats with leather-like materials for easy cleaning.

    After considering the pros & cons, I decided to take the plunge and book a Prius.

    The Deal:

    Vehicle: Toyota Prius

    Rent: $89.80 (inclusive GST)

    Contract: 5 years

    Deposit: $5000 (Booking fees: $1000, not refundable if cancel order. On delivery, pay another $4000. Total $5000 used as deposit.)

    Free Credit Card device use

    Free Reverse Sensor & $250 cash (Conditions apply)
