    I am not only a new driver but also a new citizen , I got no Friends driving taxi or kopi sessions or even a backup to call if i dont know the way( which is most of the time) , I have been driving both OMO and as shift Hirer since Feb 23 2012 so far I havent " die cock stand". so Here is my two cents worth.
    1. Invest in a GPS unit , i know everyone will jump saying brain best gps etc , hear me out type in destination and see the route computed it takes about 25 sec you already know roughly where and how to go confirm with passenger and drive second advantage near the destination it guides you to rough area (within 200 meters) of the alighting point of coustomer. this will help you with those coustomers who also dont know where their destination is
    2. If you got those type of passengers who die die save 10 cents they will guide you not only the entire route but even which  lane to keep along the way that sorts your problem out
    3. third type of Passengers are daily users / very aware of route to their destination just be honest and tell them before you press meter that you are not sure of the way can they guide you how to go
    4. get a smart phone Android or Iphone dont matter install "sgmaps" an app from street directroy this also help you as you can find out route from "point A to point B". also very handy if you forgot to turn the meter on and on destination have no idea what to charge , through this app you can get approx taxi fare and charge fairly
    5. be preapired to offer $1 or $2 discount if pessanger grumbles a bit on taking longer route after all at the end of the day you always make the money as per meter as some pessagers do tend to leave tips as well ( i get them daily without fail)
    hope this helps you to start off in this industry
