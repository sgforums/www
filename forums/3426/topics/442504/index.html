    There are 2 types of diabetes :

    type 1 : need daily insulin injections

    type 2 : usually occurs in adulthood because of obesity and usually on oral medications

     

    A diabetes has a higher risk of getting hypertension (high blood pressure) as well.

    If a person is both diabetic and hypertension, the risk of getting kidney disease is also higher.

    Then there is also a higher risk of getting heart disease and stroke.

    Symptoms of a diabetic person :

    Blur vision

    Excessive thirst for sweet or gassy drinks

    Fatigue or get tired easily

    Frequent urination

    Hunger and weigh loss
