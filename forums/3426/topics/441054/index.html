    want to increase earning out from the book?

    1) Speed - Drive as fast as your car can go, express way min 150km/hr, normal road 110-130km/hr. When your are 2x faster, your income also will come in 2x faster.

    4) Route - Do not take a longer route, that only increase your income alittle, go for a longer detour route, the distance can be double.

    5) Tips - Ask for it. You never know what you could get if you never ask.

    Practice these 5 point, your home will be a landed property for sure.
