    They did this test for courier services on mythbusters in USA. Where a well known package courier service has planned all its routes for its delivery vehicles to take only right turns along all its delivery routes. Right turns only because USA is left hand drive, and right turns are not usually subjected to traffic light conditions. So the same applies in Singapore, but since singapore is left hand drive, we should be going all left hand turns.

    They found out that despite routes being much longer when the all right turn routes are used. It takes a shorter time to complete, and less fuel consumed. But it applies mainly to delivery trucks. Cars well didn't show an outstanding difference in fuel consumption only.
