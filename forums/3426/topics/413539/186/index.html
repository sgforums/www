    Day 1 Singapore  Narita

      Assembly time for your flight check-in at Singapore Changi International Airport Terminal 2 will be at 10:00PM.

    Day 2 Singapore  Fukuoka - Nagasaki (Via Narita) 
    (Breakfast on Board / Lunch / Dinner)

      You are departing for Fukuoka via Narita. Upon arrival, lunch will be served first before you head off to the Dazaifu Tenmangu Shrine. It is a place where people from different countries flock in to worship the god of education.

     

      Afterwards, you are to proceed to Tenjin for a shopping spree. At night, we will head to Nagasaki Chinatown.

     

      Accommodation: Nagasaki New Tanda Hotel or similar.

    Day 3 Nagasaki - Beppu 
    (Breakfast / Lunch / Dinner)

      In the morning, you will visit the Glover Garden wherein the foreign settlement and various western buildings are located. It's also noted for its scenery which reminds people for Puccini's opera, Madam Butterfly. 

     

      Then we will visit the Oura Cathedral before heading off to the Peace Park. It commemorates the atomic explosion during World War II. In this park stands the Statue of Peace and the Atomic Bomb Museum, where preserved evidences of the atomic blast are kept. You can take pictures while at it.

     

      The tour will continue to Sun Plaza where you will be given time to do some shopping. We will head to Beppu, afterwards, to visit the famous Jigoku (Hell Valley) to see the blood pond and sea pond.

     

      Accommodation: Beppu Kamenoi Hotel or similar.

    Day 4 Beppu - Mt Aso - Kumamoto City Tour 
    (Breakfast / Lunch / Dinner)

      We are bound for Mt. Aso after breakfast. A bird's eye view of this panoramic mountain may be seen by ropeway, so long as the weather permits. Its charm is ultimately brought by its huge crater basin and the unique Komezuka "Hill of Rice". 

     

      Then, we will visit the Flower Park where you are to see beds of beautiful flowers that are in season. En-route to Kumamoto, we will stop for a quick photo-op at the majestic Kumamoto Castle (admission fee not included in the tour fee). It is one of the three most beautiful castles in Japan. 

     

      We will also visit the famous Suizenji Park, a symbol of Kumamoto.

     

      Accommodation: Kumamoto New Sky Hotel or similar.

    Day 5 Kumamoto - Kagoshima - Ibusuki 
    (Breakfast / Lunch / Dinner)

      After breakfast, we will drive to Kagoshima. It is a city that is abundant with scenic attractions and hot springs. It is basically dominaed by Sakurajima, one of the world's most active volcanoes.

     

      After lunch, we will proceed to Ibusuki, one of the most celebrated hot spring resorts in Japan. Its especially unique form of hot sand-bathing is a great way to relax.

     

      Accommodation: Kagoshima Tokyu Hotel or similar.

    Day 6 Kagoshima - Tokyo 
    (Breakfast / Dinner)

      You will be transferred to Kagoshima Airport for your flight to Tokyo. Upon arrival on the city, you will visit the Asakusa Kannon Temple. Then at the Nakamise Shopping Street, you will be given time to shop around the colorful area for souvenirs and traditional Japanese snacks.

     

      Afterwards, we will proceed to Ueno, where you can purchase almost anything - from food products to fashion items - for reasonable prices. Continuing the shopping experience, we will also go to Narita AEON Shopping Mall.

     

      Accommodation: Narita Port Hotel or similar. The hotel is not located in Tokyo City. It is near Narita International Airport.

    Day 7 Tokyo  Singapore 
    (Breakfast)

      In the morning, you are free at leisure until it is time for you to transfer to Narital International Airport for your departure flight to Singapore.
