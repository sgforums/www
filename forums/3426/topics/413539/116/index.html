    Hiroshima Prefecture, rich in nature, has many delicious foods such as okonomiyaki, meaty oysters and sea eels.
    The seasonal flavors and local specialties of Hiroshima will be an unforgettable part of your visit to Japan.

    Our Recommendations

    Okonomiyaki

    When you are in Hiroshima, you should not miss out on okonomiyaki. Hiroshima-style okonomiyaki comes with either Soba noodles or Udon noodles. It is an enjoyable experience to eat it from the hot grill using a spatula. The standard okonomiyaki is made with pork egg, and noodles. There are various other unique toppings.

    Oyster dishes

    Situated on the Seto Inland Sea, Hiroshima is famous for its oysters. The vista of Hiroshima Bay is unique in that rafts used in oyster cultivation bespeckle the sea. They are delicious whether you eat them raw, baked in the shell, stewed, or deep-fried. There are numerous oyster restaurants in the city.

    Anago-meshi (rice topped with conger eel)

    Anago meshi is made with fresh conger eel from the Seto Inland Sea. The eel is broiled using a special sauce whose ingredients are kept secret by each restaurant.
