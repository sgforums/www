    The Kanoya Rose Garden

    Since opening in 1994, the Kanoya Rose Garden has become popular place for Kanoya locals as well as many tourists. The garden currently covers 8 hectares and holds over4,000 kinds of roses. The roses are at their best from the middle of April through July and from the middle of October through December.

    As well as the garden itself, there are many other facilities including; the ‘Rose House’ a multi-purpose hall and store.  There is also a restaurant that overlooks the garden. Be sure to try the rose curry and the very popular rose ice cream!


    The rose garden and the surrounding  ‘Kirishimagaoka’ park is a very popular weekend destination, especially in the summer months.
