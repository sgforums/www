    Girl rudely: we dun wanna to go buangkok. we wanna go punggol, punggol! (wah, getting offended liao. small girl dun know anything, no big no small somemore!)

    Auntie: the most $8+

    Me: huh? you must be kidding. anyway you can pay me $8

    Auntie: nevermind you go yr way

    Inside tunnel me: after tunnel go buangkok or tpe?

    Auntie: not this tunnel leh. we never take this tunnel. (xx@@??!!)

    Me: there's only one tunnel to punggol. which block?

    Auntie: 199

    Me: you better say which way, tpe or..?

    Auntie: nevermind you go yr way but I never come this way before! (????!!!, gave up liao)

    So whacked tpe and just before turning right into punggol,

    Auntie pointing to the left: there last time we came by sengkang...but nevermind. (me really wordless liao)

    Girl to mother: aiyah... not here lah, punggol plaza that side lah!

    Reached, fare $12.22

    Me: pay me $8 lah, I also won't get rich with a few dollars more.

    They quickly threw down $12.50 on arm rest and alighted.
