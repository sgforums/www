    I am a birder in Singapore for over twenty over years, the Bird onecabby posted is called Common or Asian Koel (Eudynamys scolopacea), it will start it morning call at 5.45am, it look like Crow but can be easily identified from it rubby coloured eye, Koel in Singapore help to fight off crows, Koel were observed chasing crows from their nesting area and destroying crows' nest thus help to reduce crow population in Singapore. It was also observed that Koel is very territorial, it will guard the tree it perch and will chase away Myna, Crow and other male Koel that come near. One would also notice that the area with Koel holding would see less crow around.
