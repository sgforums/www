    Nite time in town also can try Samsung Hub and 1 Raffles Quay. Mid-long trip from 2230hrs to about 0200hrs.

    All these are bank workers which work Western countries timing. So they go back at these timing.

    If u at Woodlands and at Check point. After 3am there are 2 queues. One short and one long. If u wan long trip (but may wait longer) then join the normal queue to the taxi stand. If u wan short trip (like Marsiling/Woodlands/Kranji/Yishun area) there is another queue opp the taxi stand. (they park in reverse).

    If u driving normal cab, then u can try Orchard tower and Emerald Hill pubs area (just before Centerpoint). All usually short and easy trip.

    At Middle Road/Park Lane area, go to the small road (Mount Sophia I think ) there is a 24 hrs food court and big nite clubs, u can try. But chances of drunkard very high.
