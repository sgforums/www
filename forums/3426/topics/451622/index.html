    Got something worrying me currently.

    On the 8 April, my picture was taken by LTA Officers in front of Clifford pier, because I was inside a full day bus lane.

    Pax wanted to go Marina Bay Financial Center. Somewhere near Clifford Pier, she says turn left, so I turn la.

    Then she says "no, no, no", further up.

    I already in Bus Lane, then I saw this guy taking my picture.

    I was in the dotted line Full Day Bus Lane. The slot where I left the main flow of vehicles has already closed up.

    I make a sharp right turn and wait for a slot so that I can join the main flow of traffic.

    If I move forward 2 meters, I will enter the unbroken line full day bus lane.

    So I die, die dont move forward. Until a slot appears in the main flow of traffic, and I move in.

    Until today, 1st May, I have not received any letter from LTA.

    Praying and hoping that I WILL NOT receive the letter.

    Suddenly you put new meaning in an old lifestyle.

    In a way its true. When I was working in the factory, employment contract says 8 hrs. But just to make sure everything OK so that Boss will not screw me, can easily spend 12 to 14 hours in the factory.

    Used to joke, never see the sun. Morning go to work at 6.30am. Factory and canteen inside the building. Leave workplace between 7.30pm to 9pm. How to see the Sun?

    But then we can snake here and there.

    Taxi Driving cannot snake.

    If Got passenger, brain have to think what route to use and how to get there safe and fast. Fast so that can do next job.

    If No passenger, got to scan the road side for pax, very tiring to the eyes. And also must keep an eye on the vehicles in front.

    TIPS

    Yesterday, I ferried a couple and a kid from Ikea to Henderson.

    He have some stuff plus a long thingy that strectches all the way to the headrest of the front seat.

    He said"sorry,sorry". I said no problem, as long as it does not affect my driving.

    Fare was $5. Passed me $10 and gave him back $5.

    Then I got down to help him unload. Everything cleared, Wished him a good day, and got back into my Driver seat.

    Before I moved off, he opened the rear passenger door to check the seats. I turned around and says, "nothing there".

    Then he look at me and pass me the $5 that I gave him earlier.

    Shiok !!
