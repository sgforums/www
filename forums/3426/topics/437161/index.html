    Welcome

    Actually not much training requires once you have the TDVL. You can start driving immediately as long you find a suitable hirer. Both hirer and you need to register with the company.

    For comfort, there will be an orientation and MDT course. Actually I attended it after I start driving for two weeks. Get the hirer to teach you how to operate the MDT and some basic rules of picking (i.e. airport and CBD)

    For initial start up cost, Comfort will require $1k as deposit. It is payable in 4 installment of $250. You just need about $280 initially and you can start work.
