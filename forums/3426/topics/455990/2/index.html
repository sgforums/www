    A good partnership needs time to nurture, one can be good friend, but when come to work together, can turn up to be enemy.

    Most of us do relief for cabbies near our places, same goes to hirers, they also prefer taking relief near their place. Even when you part with each other, at least try to leave some gentlemenship within, so that when you meet up on the street or anywhere near your place, still can talk and greet each other as buddy.

    The rule of engagement for partnership is as said before, start with a friendly mood, then the problem come in, then the stormy mood from both side, and once the storm is over, the rainbow arrive, and thing may get better to betteress.

    But usually at the mid of the storm, the relationship tends to cease, because of different interest. In getting partnership, alway go for a win win situation, and to do that, both need to find a common interest and work on it, and as for Taxi driving, one common interest is to make money for both so as to give our family or ourselves a better tomorrow.
