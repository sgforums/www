    LTA rolls out Choa Chu Kang-CBD bus service

    It will start operating on 18 March.

    A new SMRT parallel service – Service 982E – that connects the Choa Chu Kang neighbourhoods to the Central Business District (CBD) will begin operations from Monday, 18 March 2013. 

    This is the eighth bus service to be rolled out under the Land Transport Authority’s Bus Service Enhancement Programme (BSEP).

    The new service will provide greater connectivity for Choa Chu Kang residents travelling to and from areas in the CBD during weekday morning and evening peak-hours (excluding public holidays).

    Parallel Service 982E will operate two one-way trips on weekday mornings at 7.40 am and 7.50 am from Choa Chu Kang Avenue 5, Choa Chu Kang Avenue 4, Choa Chu Kang Way, Choa Chu Kang Avenue 1 and Teck Whye Avenue to areas within the CBD such as Anson Road, Robinson Road and Marina Boulevard.

    On weekday evenings, two one-way trips at 6.15 pm and 6.30 pm will connect commuters from areas such as Central Boulevard and Shenton Way to the Choa Chu Kang neighbourhoods.
