  • Poolman
    26 Jul 10, 03:31

    1. Any contract need to sign or deposit with hirer ? Answer : NO , refundable security deposit $1k must be given to taxi company .

    2. Rental pay daily/weekly/monthly ? Answer : Daily after shift ended . Cash

    3. Have to register with taxi company first then can be a relief to that company ? Answer : When u found a partner to drive for .

    4. Taxi company provide training for their relief ? Answer : TransCab nothing , CDG has to go MDT training . How to take call ? how to use Visa Card ? Corporate Card ? Never heard before other taxi companies got training provided by them , SMRT i think have .
