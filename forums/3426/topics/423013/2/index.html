    Depends.

    Firstly the driver should be careful. Pedestrian make mistake or act rashly does not mean you can ram him down. You are operating a vehicle, you have a duty to look and use the brake. Driver at fault.

    Secondly, the accident cannot be avoided. Like a jaywalker just dash across the road, he clears the first vehicle but kena ram by the second vehicle following behind on the right. Sometimes the pedestrian take the chance thinking the vehicle will brake and try to dash across. But suay suay the driver at that time look at the rear mirror or side mirror and accelerate hard. Then hear brake sound and boom. Driver get caution.

    Thirdly, driver try to beat yellow light, and speed, and straight across pedestrian see yellow light and start to cross. Both at fault.

    Eye witness very important, or else the driver take most of the blame.
