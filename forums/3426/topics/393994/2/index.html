    OMO

    1 When you start your engine in the morning, you will have to think how fast can you cover your rental. If you start at 6am, on a goodday by noon your cost shd be covered

    2 Total stress out after 0930am where the streets is empty and you only have hotel,hospital,zoo,birdpark etc to cover

    The best option for beginers, fast way to cover cost and start whacking to make more income. The plus side is, you will have enough rest and free time after your driving shift.
