    I know Poolman has elaborated on CDG's call booking, whereby they might not activate your cab even if you're the nearest because they wanna boost their advance job.

    And also the loophole which allows a caller to play tricks by booking numerous cabs.

    I also found out another thing, when a pax calls for a cab via his app, he has to press send (if say he's just wanting 1 cab).

    But because there's some lag issues with wifi or connection, when he presses send, the page doesn't change and he thinks he hasn't booked one yet. Then he presses again and again until it changes, but because of lag, every press equals 1 booking, so if he pressed 3x, 3 taxis will be desptached.

    Unless he cancels the other 2, you will there blur blur and wait LL. Later curse the pax. Of course we can say the pax is wrong for not cancelling, but some may genuinely not know they have called for more than 1 cab or others might just be too lazy to cancel, even tikam tikam, first come first served.

    So CDG drivers be aware of this bug, when you decide to accept calls, don't steam one like how some drivers do, see MDT ting, steam already, blindly go to blind alley
