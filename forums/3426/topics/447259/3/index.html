    As far as allowing you dump them, that's a figment of imagination on your part I'm afraid.

    CDG now operates fully as a cash cow. As long as your earning money for them, it's all well and good. The moment you become a liability, they will have no hesitation in terminating you.

    Eg: You hirer for 3-5 years. You pay $3k or more a month for the cab. For that amount you can easily pay for a brand new Mercs private car installment and have lots of change. You pay 36k a year in rental, after 3 years, they have already recouped that amount and even profits from it.

    But if you owe 3-4 days rental, they'll chase you worse than Ah Long. Everyday got call to pay. You cannot suka suka tell them you clear slowly, they give you a time frame, usually ask to pay in 2 halves. You cannot make it, they repo the cab. And after you clear the arrears, they will also tell you, sorry hor, now you only can become relief, no more hirer - policy.

    They are always at the upper hand, even the 'benefits' they offer when you 1st sign up, is not a problem. You drive 5-6 months only also no problem, whatever they have given you, is already made back.

    You cannot comply or struggle to pay, never mind off you go, there's already a new batch just out of the academy, more than willing to take your place.

    They never owe you anything as far as they are concerned, just look at their handbook for hirers and relief. Everything they already cover themselves. You take it or leave it.
