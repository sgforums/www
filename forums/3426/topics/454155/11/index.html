  • Sotongking73
    6 Jul 12, 14:54

    Some old birds here might disagree with me. I believe most bro here would suggest newbie to drive day shift. However, I strongly recommend night shift for newbie. 

    The peak hour/City surcharge and midnight charge, greatly helps to compensate the incompetency of a newbie to generate a more acceptable income.

    The road after 12am are less conjested, so, newbie can auto roam and get familiar with the CBD area more leisurely, and changing of lane suddenly are much easier.

    If lucky enough, just need to pick up 2 different pax within CBD, and the 3rd going to long distance, could fetch about $40 - $50 in an hour. The down side of long distance is that have to travell 20km back to town after the long journey. Sometimes when luck is on my side, I only need to travell 200km in a shift, to make the same amount of profit as 300km.
