    Monday night started at 5.30pm, thinking that just cover cost and make little bit then keep by 12am. However up to 11pm only made $20 when reached CCK. Thought just queque at Yew Tee MRT taxi stand to pick up short trip before close, since the profit was so bad. Pax boarded, want to go Yishun, aiyah anything also take lah, as the night was dam down.

    Reached Yishun, wanted to head back home, kenah flag by 1 guy go Lavender St. Since already there and midnight charge started, drove towards Mustafa to pick ah neh. Before I could turn right at Desker Rd, 1 uncle flag and go Sengkang. At Sengkang wanted to go home via AMK ave 5, along the way a lady flag go Woodlands. When reached Woodlands, told myself must go back liao, very hungry and tired backs and legs.

    While heading back, pax flag go Jurong, knn, tired also have to go. Reached Jurong got another connection go Depot Rd. After alighted pax, I can really feel the pain on my back. Park in a carpark nearby and have smoke break. After that head back to CCK to pump diesel, when done, along CCK Way, a lady flag, thought it is some short trip, so I stop. She wanted to go Tampinese, but told her to take another cab. This trip midnight I think can fetch another $35 -$40. Dam heart pain, wanted to carry on, but my body already started to complain a few hrs ago. Went for makan at Yew Tee around 3am, then went home.

    At least my hard work is worth something tonight. Made $1xx after cost. Midnight driving is really full of suprises sometimes.
