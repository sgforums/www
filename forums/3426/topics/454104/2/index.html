    I may be new, but I think the way to progress is

    3 to 6 months = Just sweep. Learn where to sweep. Sweep,sweep,sweep.

    2nd month onwards = Start to take Current Bookings, especially during Peak Hours.

    6th month onwards = learn how to interpret Airport arrivals. Dont be a kayu, queue 45 mins to get a $16 fare from a 15 mins ride. It could be worse.

    There are 2 things which I find quite silly (my opinion only)

    1.  Taking advance booking. This system really screw up your time/location management. Unless it is the first fare of the day. 

    2.  Queuing at the airport for 45 mins, up to almost 2 hours, due to "try my luck".
