    14. NATIONAL Solidarity Party candidate Nicole Seah cried during a rally on Thursday night, while recalling a meeting she had with a poor family in MacPherson.
    15. The 24-year-old, who is contesting in Marine Parade GRC, said she was saddened when the mother said she could not get help to fund her son's $80 tuition fees. Worse, when the mother sought help, she was chided for the small amount.
