    Potholes form when water seeps through tiny pores on the road surface into the asphalt pavement.

    During rainy seasons when the top layer of the pavement is soaked in water as a result of prolonged rainfall, the composition of the top layer is weakened and clusters of aggregates may be loosened and subsequently removed by traffic running over it.

    Potholes are therefore more evident during rainy seasons.

    You can call LTA Customer Service Line at 1800-CALL LTA (1800-2255582) to report the locations of potholes.
