    Dec 11, 2010

    Chaotic scene at airport

    ON THURSDAY I landed at Changi Airport Terminal 1 on an early evening flight and was greeted by a chaotic scene near the taxi stand.

    The area was undergoing renovation and a huge section had been cordoned off. The crowd of passengers who had just arrived, together with families and well-wishers, spilled onto the road where taxis were driving in to pick up fares. Add to that the snaking taxi queue and it was hard to believe that this was the same Changi Airport which had won several best airport awards.

    There were some airport personnel directing the traffic, but the most commendable were the few trolley service staff, who carried on working amid the sea of people and whizzing taxis. They efficiently and quickly cleared the trolleys from the taxi pick-up area.

    i hope the management of Changi Airport will look into improving this situation.

    Ethel Chong (Ms)
