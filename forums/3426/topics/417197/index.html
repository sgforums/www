    Waiting in the rain as cabs whizz past

    ON NOV 25, between 9pm and 10.15pm, I and two family members were standing in the rain trying to hail a cab along Jalan Besar near the junction of Kelantan Lane.

    No fewer than 10 taxis drove past us but did not stop even though they were empty and the 'for hire' light was switched on. There were other people waiting along the same stretch of road and they too had the same experience.

    This is not the first time this has happened to me.

    Also, taxi drivers parked at taxi stands, especially in the Race Course Road area, often refuse to take passengers. The authorities should impose tougher fines on such cabbies.

    Muhamad Noh Sapari
