    How? Let me give you a piece of what the govt is going to do to the wages of Singaporeans working class before I answer it.

    After the election, the govt recognised that wages of Singaporeans' worker need to increase by 30% in a 10 period in order for average singaporeans to be able to cope with the inflation, that stand at a all time high of 5.5% now. Spread across a 10 years period, 3% wage increase is on the card, for those lower income, more need to be done, thru skill training and productivity.

    Coming back to TD, how? Can taxi fare increase by 30% in 10 years time??

    Now in earning, there are 2 types of earning, one is call variable component, the other is non variable component.

    In a working condition, variable component comprises of bonuses, profit sharing, productivity sharing, lump sum, exordinary bonuses for good performing. It is more of a one time off payment and it can move and change with times. For TD earning, Peak hrs and surcharges are variable component, it can change here and there, depending on condition, both internal and external environment, the situation and the regulations. Surcharges can be remove and also can be implement here and there, so are peak period, these are much beyong our control, because it is variable, someone on the top decide.

    The non variable factor is the wages, the fix wages, and to TD, is the fares, increase flag down fares, per km fares, or 1st km fares will ultimately change TD earning tremendously, more importantly, it is fixed, and I believe that everyone, including workers and TD will like these type of increment. However, for TD, the govt choose to slowly increase such fares, as TD, you should know how many donkey years they increase one time. And because they refuse to increase the fare, with some putting it on a temporary basis. like premier 50 cents removed, govt/LTA can therefore manipulate you here and there by giving non variable gain of peak hours and surcharges. For once, I prefer they increase taxi fares, 1st km fares and per km fares, then going into extending peak hours for their gain.

    Like what BCL said, stand on one rate and just increase on that rate, which is the fix fares, and fix fares stay with us forever. Just a worker, he prefer a fix salary increment than some lump sum, because that increase go with him forever.
