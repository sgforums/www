    katosan,

    weekdays afternoon shenton,suntec (1 Raffles Quay usually good), battery rd. no cruising in town, must join taxi queues. i like int'l plaza after 3pm. bugis junction oso ok but pax most likely send u out of cbd. u have to recce abit to find out what time queue where. generally, long queue = good queue cos the lao gui drivers know where is good. just follow them. take a little time to observe the queue movements.

    afternoon orchard very hard to join taxi queues, mostly already taxis long queue. (lucky, centrepoint, paragon). plaza sing, far east plaza not so bad.

    punggol, sengkang, simei, tampines morning before 8am you go = die. cck, woods also. jurong e, bkt. batok better.

    airport, not my cup of tea. i only do airport if pick and go. never queue. minimum waste 1 hour. shift drivers can't afford to waste 1 hour.

    daily $150 is bad wor. normally my collection...

    6.30-10.30 ~$100-$120 (must hit target, afternoon can relax abit)

    10.30-11.00 ~ break

    11.00-11.30 top up diesel (auto stop + $3) + wait for call. (must know which kiosk can get calls, lunch time pple mah). top up at this time best cos no pax otr, and no taxi queue at kiosk

    11.30-13.00 ~$140-$150

    16.00 keep ~$180-$230

    bad days wif 2 hours tea break usually at least $160+, good days ~$220+,

    very good days $250+

    weekends don't need to say lah. more info ref my previous posts on sat/sun.

    hope this helps u. good luck!
