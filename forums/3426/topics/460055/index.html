    Most people think this one air horn:

    it's actually not and it's legal. Yes its louder, but since the tone is lower it has shorter reach.. On highway at speeds you horn vehicle, the vehicle in front sometimes don't realise which direction the horn coming from.

    I prefer snail type horn for use in city area, where speed is low and vehicle close together.

    Loudest electric automotive horn so far IMO is this:

    Hella SuperTone 139db, it's about 50% bigger then normal stock horn. Has further reach then snail horn type. Can view stock horn and Supertone sound comparison video: http://youtu.be/3XLULsqTIpA

    Stock vs Supertone size comparison:

    Most air horn or snail type horn rated at 110db - 118db, so supertone easily louder

    But since it uses a compressor to generate the sound it is clasified under air horn so illegal in singapore.
