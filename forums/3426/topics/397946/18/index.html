    Originally posted by gung:

    Yo Bro, good news from me, manage to get a good hirer willing to let me try out every week thurs, friday and sat nite, as I am very new, btw any one know knows how much I need to pay the security deposit when I goes down with my hirer this few days time, hopefully have instalment if not die cock stand...:)

    Security Deposit for Comfort is $1,000 - payable in 4 equal instalment.  There is also another Admin Fee of around $15 (GST payable).

    Have you gotten the GIRO form from Comfort?  You need to get your bank to endorse the GIRO form so that Comfort can:

    1. Deduct the Security Deposit (if you choose to pay be instalment)

    2. Deduct the daily Taxi Rental (only applicable to Hirers)

    3. Deduct money from your account for Call Levy (think is $0.50 per call that you take)

    4. Put money into your account for those customers who paid by cards.

    By the way, Comfort (& CityCab) only uses UOB Savings Account for the above.  So, you need to open a Savings Account with UOB before going down with your hirer to Comfort.  This will require another $500 for opening new account.

    HTH.
