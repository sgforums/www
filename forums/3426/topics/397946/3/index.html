  • La2534
    8 May 10, 02:00

    Wad tips u wan?

    Seriously speaking, not much tips except the advice of intense driving for the first 1-3months or so. After that u become old bird and easier.

    Buy GPS or get the latest Street Directory and always refer to it when in doubt.

    When new, dun be afraid to tell ur customer that you are unfamiliar with routes. Most of the pple are nice and understanding. Worst come to worst, just tell them nicely to move to the cab behind if they not happy with a newbie.

    As you drive, remember all the position of Taxi Stands, especially those with long queue of taxi. Remember, if got no pax taxi wun go there.

    Finally, be patient and dun give up. There are tons of money to earn, it is how you gonna earn it. Bear in mind that there are good days and bad days. If bad day comes, dun take it too hard, try next day.

    When you are queing in a taxi queue. Quickly take out ur street directory and study the surrounding area.

    Remember all famous hotels in town (and Grand Mecure Roxy in Marine Parade). You will come across them very frequently.
