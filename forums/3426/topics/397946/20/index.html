    I would said that it is true that as a TD you must have a good self discipline, and there are pros and cons between TD and working in a company, TD are actually working for a taxi company, but on a self mode condition, the key word here is "in" and  'for".

    The corporate world also have stress, pressure, politics, colleague pressure,  boss and subordinate disatisfaction  and many  more, and you have to look at people face to work, more so is the 8 or 9 to 5 routine job, more routine than a TD. 

    TD, is own discipline, own time and own target,  they may not enjoy MC, leaves, and CPF, but they get cash, can plan their time and leaves, and more so, they are the frontline contact with customers which help singapore as an important transport mode.  Other than having to pay the hiring fees to the compay that provide them the taxis, they do not have to support a high overhead cost like in company, where there are MD, GM, managers, Admin and office peoples, exectutives are overhead that suppressed the real productivie workers/staffs' wages.

    So, i do not really agree that taxis career is not better than some others, but the most important is that, as long as you are happy in what you are doing,  go ahead, take pride in it, stay healthy  and dsicipline, takes thing easily, you can be a very happy cabby.
