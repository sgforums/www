    These are the places i frequent ( during my shift ) whenever i am nearby their area.

    Nasi lemak ( the one with the long long Q ), one of the best imo - Changi Village

    Lor Mee - North Bridge Road

    Tom Yam Soup with rice - Coffeeshop along bencoolen Street ( opp hotel 81 ), stall selling thai food . They are moving on the 27th July to Sunshine plaza .

    Beef Noodles - Lavender Eminent plaza , good for chop chop makan and fark off.

    Teh tarik , prata , briyani - Lim Teck Kim Road., many TDs go to this place. cant miss.

    Bak Kut Teh - Balestier , shop just beside Boon Tong Kee

    Pig Parts Soup - Foch Road , very famous also, no need introduction.

    Western Food - Lorong 28 Geylang coffeeshop , so far the best western stall available at nite.

    Scissor Rice - Kitchener Road , occasionally got makan there but find parking a hassle.

    Hokkien Mee - Clementi Ave 2 Hawker Centre , will cheong there if im around at 11 plus , uncle nowadays huat liao , close shop very early

    Mutton Soup - Adam Road , solid stuff .

    i have een eating at these few outlets for years already , never get sick of it.
