    i was lucky to be earning well in the early years of my mum illness to be able to cover most of the expenses and also able to save quite a substantial amount of $$$ as business was booming for me at that time.

    Started investing (actually i have always been investing in stock yrs ago but took a bigger gamble this time.) in 2008 when market was turning bear can still remember to actual date 22nd jan 2008. Im not a stock gambler. i always invest with a mid to long term view and settle all my stocks contracts.

    Late 2008 barely 11 months after putting my investments i started slowly pulling out most at a lost of 50% and above. Reason? My mum medishield has reach a claim cap of $200000. Medical expenses started to double from what i was previously paying and i was also not earning as much as i was previously. All my saving from than was bleed almost dried till im back to square one again

    we r all self employed here and have to contribute compulsary medisave, i have have since learn to insure myself more intelligently so as not to burden my kids thru what i have gone thru.
