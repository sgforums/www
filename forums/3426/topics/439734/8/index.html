    In this world, there are 3 kind of job, one is a job that you can see a bright future, eg you can become a MD, a manager, a GM or a senior enginneer or chief engineer, another one is you can also see a future, but that future is the more or less the same as now, eg are Taxi drivers, guards, cleaners, sweeper, operators, etc, all these give you very limited opportunity to go up, and mostly likely in 20 years time, you will be still driving a taxis if you are a taxi driver, maybe just better taxis.
