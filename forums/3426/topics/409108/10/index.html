    An advice to those new cng drivers .

    There is a few timing usually good to top up cng ....

    An hour before rush hour for taxis .

    I call it low period .

    I dunno about day shift's style .

    My style , 7.30pm onwards , i'll head to Toh Tuck from town if my mileage is above 100km used , cos i rarely are out of town during this time .

    So 7.30pm onwards , i will move out of town , using Bt Timah Road as a routine , if my direction is towards west , most likely i'll get one to west , very likely

    Cos 7.30pm is what i called low period . .

    Then i move to Toh Tuck .

    After topping up full , i'll create one connection at Tuas most likely . 8pm Tuas power-pack one .

    If i am near Jurong , instead of toh tuck , i'll use Jalan Buroh , then Tuas again my target .
