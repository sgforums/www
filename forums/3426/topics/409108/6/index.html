WTF!!!  At Geylang, kenna rejected by Ah Neh (those who frequent Lor 24 will know what I'm referring to...).  Kenna reject by Ah Tiong... Never mind, go queue at Lavender 81.  1st in the queue, thought ho say liao... See one couple came out after their session, look around, then walk around 20 meters away and started flagging other cabs.  WTF!!!? Subsequently also go a few saw my cab and don't flag.  Once I pass them, they start flagging...(LOL... Maybe they think my cab black colour / or big size, hence there is difference with the fare.  I did have a white collar at Battery Road TS asked me about any fare difference or not...)

    Lucky that night I already hit my target and just cruising around for kopi money.  I think that night is the 15th of 7th month. 

    That was the last straw for me man... The rejection, plus the hassle of having to top up CNG, plus my hirer chut pattern 2 times (handover car with CNG at last light bulb only, can only manage at most 40km nia) made me reconsider carrying on with driving that Wish. I ever go from Bedok all the way to Jurong to top up gas (even on a night with heavy rain where I can only proceed with 60km/h on PIE) and he can tell me 5pm Serangoon already no gas???  I may not be smart, but I'm definitely not stupid lah... Somemore during Hari Raya he wanted me to OMO 5 days for full rental.

    Now, I'm driving with Comfort.  Just started for a week only.  Hirer is only 3 blocks away from me, so it's just a 5 minutes walk from home to take over car.  My earnings did increase from driving with Yellow Top, but not much.  Last night was a stinker.  During to meter problem (meter hanged, cannot start clocking fare, down for almost 2 hours, lucky pax is kind hearted and pay me what she normally pay....), only managed to clock $40 plus by the time peak hour is over.  Thought siao liao... But, heng heng after midnight continuously kenna 4 trips from east to west to east to west to east again... Not my best collection per night, but exceed my target.  Go home a happy man... :)

    Currently, driving 5 weekday nights + full Sunday. Hirer asked me Saturday also want or not, told him will try 1 week first.  So, this week will be 6 nights + full Sunday.  I know... You guys will say siao ah... Boh bian lah, need money lor... LOL...

    Since you are not in a tight financial situation, please choose a more humane driving schedule.  Now, I do feel a bit sian when time to take over car comes.  But, I just remind myself lor... End of the day, cash in my own pocket + able to bring my passengers home safely and comfortably after a day at work and the sian feeling goes away... A bit "Ah Q", I know... :)
