    19 Mar 12, 21:05

    A few learning points.

    There are a lot of short trips around an area, and then one pax will shoot to another estate. Then again, there will be a lot of short trips around that area before another pax shoots to yet another estate.

    Connection is good during the weekend.

    $10 runs out very fast because all you need is 3 people paying with $50, you could give away $120 worth of $10. No, I wont get angry with the pax for paying with $50. I must stock up on $10.

    Well dressed people are not as generous as those approaching the Ah-mah category. 2 days driving, and already got 2 incidents where the Ah-mah asked me to keep the change. Not much, but their heart is generous.

    If you are generous, and you round your fares to the nearest 20 cents, you only need 3 types of coins, $1, 50 cents and 20 cents. So the coin box no need to be so cluttered. If not so generous, add in the 10 cents and 5 cents. (and 1 cents if you kiasu, but dunno whether LTA allows?)

    My Challenges

    I am still having problem with routes. This is the number one reason that makes me nervous. Until now Pax have not whack me yet. But there are some who says will guide me, but I afraid they give half past six instructions. Normally, I have to say “Sorry, I am new, let me check the map” then I set the GPS. But sometimes the GPS play me out with “Not Found”. I don’t mind driving less during this initial period, and keep on re-thinking the routes I have taken so that I no need to rely on the GPS. But, a person can only learn if he keeps on doing the things he wants to learn, that is to Drive.

    City. Most TD loves it. When I was there, everywhere long Q (of Taxis). Not my priority to learn how to tackle the City, as I enjoy Road Sweeping. But still, have to learn how to unlock the wealth in the city.
