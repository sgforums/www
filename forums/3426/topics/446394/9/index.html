    17 Feb 2012. Yesterday completed Names & Locations. I think most people find this section difficult is because they flip left, flip right (the Street Directory). Like what Ywth says, English educated should be able to handle it better.

    I believe that if you have a systematic way of doing it, then it should not even be a killer section. When given a name of building, give your best guess under what category (Is it a Condo, Commercial or Government, etc.). Go to Pg 1, Index. Then to the category (eg PCI). If wrong, what is the second best guess of what building is it, and do the same again. If still cannot get, put under review. When looking for a building on the Map Grid, glance through first, if cannot find, then must imagine tiny little 1 cm square to look for it and run finger across the Map Grid.

    Never try to be smart by flipping through the pages without going to Index. Once or twice lucky, but over 50 questions, you will loose a lot of time.

    Today started Routes and Landmarks. Watch movie, on the route taken. At first inetersting, but after a while, boring, because first 10 routes all start from AirPort.

    Completed 8 days out of 16 days. Half way thru' course.
