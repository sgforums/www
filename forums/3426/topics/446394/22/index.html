    26 Mar 12, 22:10

    My first trip of the day, I ferried a family of 4, Parents and 2 teenage kids at 6.30 am. From Serangoon to Beach Road.

     

    I did not use GPS on 8 trips out of the 20 trips. (T1, T4, T11, T13 T14, T15, T16, T20) Some of the routes may be easy, but when you brain jam, easy also become hard.

     

    The unproductive time is waiting at the Airport, 35 mins and 40 mins. By the time pax alight, just manage to hit my hourly target (Newbie target).

     

    Another bad timing is the 11.00 am to 12 noon bracket. Wasting fuel, today I got less than $6 for this timing ($5.82 to be exact, T12). No wonder why somebody advised me to go early lunch if I drive morning. This is the timing where everybody already reach their office, and waiting for lunch time.

     

    These are my experiences, other TD with other strategies may have different results
