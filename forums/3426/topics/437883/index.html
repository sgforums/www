    STOMPer skystar witnessed this horrific accident at Bukit Batok this morning (Sep 13) where a cab mounted the pavement and crashed into a bus stop. One man was seen bleeding profusely from his head. 

    The STOMPer described:

    "An accident happened at Bukit Batok Ave 2 this morning at about 7.30am. 

    "A red TransCab was travelling very fast and crashed into a bus stop. 

    "An Indian man in his early 30's was injured on the left leg, and bleeding profusely from the head. 

    "The driver was unharmed without any injuries, but stared blankly. 

    "He was also sent to the hospital for a routine check-up. 

    "The ambulance arrived in 5mins but the police car never arrived."

    The SCDF reports:

    "We received a call this morning at 7.26am.

    "The accident took place near Block 315 at Bukit Batok.

    "We sent an ambulance, which conveyed the casualties to National University Hospital (NUH).

    "There were three casualties who suffered minor injuries, and they were conscious and in a stable condition. 

    "The Indian man had suffered a cut to his forehead, but is in a stable condition."
