    So just pass my TDVL on 22nd June 2012. Last Fri 6th July go Sin Ming collect TDVL at 230pm, take bus go Defu go sign for Wish 1.8, take cab after everything around 630pm.

    Very blur don't know how to operate meter and MDT. Just know login and BUSY sign, Toh say everything inside the file... Go pump petrol at Yio Chu Kang Esso, buy parking coupon go home, reach 7pm+

    Open file find how to use meter and MDT, wah more confused!!

    Near my house saw one Comfort driver having smoke break, ask him how to use meter. Then understand bit more liao.

    Drive my cab around 11pm+, first day no rental so i thought drive drive a bit. Go Jurong Point see see crowd, havent reach Taxi stand one guy pop out of bush stop my Taxi, drunk indian guy, then suddenly one more friend come in taxi, say go Kian Teck, lucky i know the place, nearby so ok lah .

    Half way driving then think wah jia lat what if kena rob at Kian Teck there. Reach there lucky nothing happen they pay and go.

    Dunno where to go next so just drive Yew Tee, pinoy couple stop me ask go AMK ave 5, lucky know. 

    Total $30+, go home liao cos need to wake up early go buy season parking.

    Saturday start drive 9pm. Go Toh Tuck get CNG card and pump gas.

    Lucky got one guy there also just start 3days, ask him how to use CNG card and ask him more about meter and MDT function. Then go IMM four time, send all nearby, warm up...

    Then try go town, slowly drive from Boon Lay Way minor road all the way, missed 2 passenger cos cannot see! Then realise i never wear my contact lens! At night a bit blur!!!!

    Ok nvm drive slowly, must remember buy contact lens next day. After mifdnight, Bay View Hotel pick up couple go Sembawang. Then drive slowly down Upp Thomson Road go back town, Thomson Rd pick up one go Bukit Batok. Drive back town check out where all cabby go queue. Near Somerset pick up couple go Bukit Batok then Pioneer Mall. Then go home, think better get contact lens then drive next day.

    Sunday contact lens wear liao, but start 930pm. Thought go Tuas just drive only cos never go there many years liao. Near Tuas West Road, one guy stop me all sweaty! China Man don't know english so much. He say Tampines. I say, WHAT?? he say, Tampines St 41, scared wrong info, i on my phone GPS show him the road name. He confirm.

    He say very hard to get Taxi, i say Tuas u must call, then he say no money.. I say, Then no money how to pay me later!! Then he say no money call cab... Ohhh.. Ok lor Tuas to Tampines, i drive he sleep. Reach, $35+ he pay me all $5 and $2, good i got small change liao.

    Drop him pick up one go Hougang, then i go Pasir Ris, pick up couple go Tampines. Then want go Shell Upp Changi to pee but close down liao! U turn pick up two indian guy go Chai Chee and Ubi. 

    Then go around town, wah Sunday night very quite, try go East Coast Park pick up Indian Couple go Lorong Lew Lian, forgot where it is. Ask them, they say go straight reach liao. I know go straight reach Fort Road.. LoL just drive lor, then they sleep. Damnit! Ok traffic light i on my phone GPS find, then remember its Serangoon Central there.

    Go Harbor Front, very quite thought call it a night, near Alexandra pick up 2 Myanmmar guy go Kian Teck, just nice after that can go home. Drop them, end up pick up 2 gerls one go Bangkit then the other go Woodlands CheckPoint, stay johor. First time go checkpoint, taxi stand got many queue up also ah!

    Decide must go liao, put busy go Toh Tuck pump gas wash car, go back..

    OMO not easy to earn for newbie, must be discipline and start early. I bit busy in day doing other business, so 9pm-4am only enough cover rental and fuel, wash and maybe $20-30 profit
