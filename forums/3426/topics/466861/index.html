    To all TD wannabes,

    Dun come in and ask from all the lao gui. They can only advise based on their experience and personal preference. Some might even want to "force" their opinion on you. Just read these posts with a pinch of salt and decide for yourself.

    If you have decided to be hirer or OMO for whatever reasons you may have, just press on. By the time your contract ends, you will be in a better position to decide whether your choice is right.

    In td, BEST is rent low, fuel low, income high and not forgetting short hours. But I do agree to start as a relief and learn on the job is still the best choice.

    Good luck to all newbies.

    From my experience since oct 2012 till now.

    Im a super relief driving on friday/saturday night.

    Why go relief/super relief when you just start?

    because you only pay 1/2 of the rental, shorter hours on the road (12hours).

    Meantime you can learn the routes and the traffic.

    I tried being a OMO for 2days.i gave up.my 1/2 shift earning is more than a OMO calculate in hours.

    Let me give you an example of ali & baba

    Ali is a OMO.

    He pay $120 daily for rental, diesel 450km ($50) total operation cost $170

    Hourly income for a TD is $20/h with the crazy traffic.(could be worst)

    1st 7-8hours with short break to cover rental, another 2-3hours to cover diesel, another 4-5hours for salary of $60-80. Total hours put in 15hours.

     baba is a relief driver.

    He pay $60 daily for rental, diesel 350km ($40) total operation cost $100

    Same hourly income of $20 /h with the crazy traffic. 1st 3-4hours cover rental, 2-3hours cover diesel, 4-5hour for salary. Total hours put in 12hours.
