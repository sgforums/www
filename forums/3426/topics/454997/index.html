    I think Poolman mentioned this once, you're new best Co to join is CDG, either Citycab or Comfort.

    Not because they are the best ( Icertainly don't think so) but because they are the biggest. They have the most pumps, their repairs/sewrvicing are generally done faster and if you suay suay kena accident theirs is still the lowest excess (not sure, others may clarify).

    No need to enjoy their perks by becoming hirer, it may add up to $2000 a year, but this amount you can easily recoup if you take 1 extra pax a day.

    Be relief best. learn the ropes, and CDG got many hirer for you to choose, dont like 1, throw, find another.

    If really want to be hirer, join them but do not take their perks. Just a normal hirer, I think they have Crown which offers $800 after 6 months as hirer, this you can take, plus if you got friend recommend, the friend gets $500 if you stay for 6 months.

    Sonata now got 3 month waiting period. But you got time what to learn the ropes as a relief first.

    Or as I suggest sometimes, check with your course mates, maybe 2 of you can take 1 cab and 1 of course is the hirer (he takes the perks on paper, but you 2 split it between yourselves). Both are new, both can learn and support each other.

    Transcab (must be very careful here) better ask regulars about them. They are super if everything goes smoothly, better than CDG, but if there's slip on your part, it's money talks, first. Plus about their methods also find out more.

    SMRT, I also urge caution, heard a number of horror stories but my info is old, so they could be much better now.

    I think Silver is alright as a company but repairs/servicing cannot fight. 

    So join the 'Mustafa' first - CDG, everything have but not the best, can find better elsewhere. Gain experience and then you can switch to another company. Remember you got 1 year to enjoy the perks on offer or is it 6 months. Drive for 6 months then make a better judgement - your own.
