    @Superbeffect,

    The waiting time for CDG to assign you a vehicle don't neccesarily have to be that long,  I know of some factors that can speed up or slow down the waiting time drastically.

    If u don't mind, 

    - manual Crown - almost immediate assignment.

    - whether the assigned vehicle blue or yellow - time shortened a bit.

    - whether the vehicle got advertisements - shorthened further

    - the age of the vehicle - shortened somemore

    How I know? Very recently, 4~5 of my classmates and I from the same TDVL course all got our vehicles in less than 3 weeks upon getting license.

    3 weeks is really not a long time considering before we can even become their hirer we still have to go to CDG attend don't know what MDT course lah, Basic service training for new hirer lah and another don't remember call what...it is for 1st time hirer of auto taxi one.
