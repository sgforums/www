    Yes, scenario painted by ReliefDriver is probably what happened.  ON CALL is triggered by the system, no way we drivers can manipulate it.  Only thing we can control is the BUSY and TAXI and CHANGE SHIFT.

    Hence, after spending time circling around looking for his pax and waiting, of course he's going to tell you to wait for another taxi since the "call booking" is still active on his terminal.  Since he is making his round for about 20 minutes, more like than not he is played by his passenger since the max we can press on the terminal is 10 minutes.  When you see the ON CALL changed back to TAXI is most likely he pressed the NO SHOW and it's approved. This is also changed by the system, outside of our control.
