    TOTO MURDER

    In another case, a dishevelled looking man was charged in the Subordinate Court yesterday for Monday's murder of a part-time Toto lottery draw results announcer.

    Fang Gao San, 53, is said to have killed Mr Sito Woon Chee at around 6.41pm in front of PoMo Mall along Selegie Road. Mr Sito, 59, was stabbed minutes after he announced Monday's draw results.

    Fang, who allegedly fled the scene on foot, was arrested by police officers with the help of Mr Sito's two colleagues. Fang and Mr Sito reportedly did not know each other.

    During the short hearing yesterday, Fang - who was spotted with a bruised right eye and shoulder-length hair - repeatedly asked the court interpreter to speak loudly. He has been further remanded for psychiatric evaluation and will next appear in court on June 23. If convicted, Fang faces the death penalty.
