Taxi crashes into lorry on poorly lit road in Clementi

  • QX179R

    STOMPer yanntheangel noticed an accident at Clementi last night (Feb 28) involving a taxi who unknowingly drove into a lorry's wheel in the dim lighting.

    The STOMPer wrote:

    "This accident took place around 11.15pm along Clementi Avenue 4.

    "A lorry was parked on left side of the road under a canopy.

    "As there was no street lamp in the area, a taxi driver did not notice the stationary lorry in the poor lighting and drove into the outer wheel of the lorry.

    "The accident blocked the road and caused the entire street to jam."
