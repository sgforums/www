    Heard from my buddy... (well this is my opinion only)

    Must agree to arrangement between 2 parties before starting the partnership on the said cab

    Pointers need to be clarify:-

    Shift timing - Normally (not all), 2shifts drivers starting from 5am to 5pm amd 5pm to 5am respectively.

    One hour/hours late compensation (sometimes of personal committment or jobs)... As for market practise, after 4hrs of your shift started, partner returns late, free rental for the night.

    Incentives like free rental only provide for partners driving the fair even days than the registered hirer of the cab.

    Bonus given to the registered hirer depends on certain requirements set by the taxi companies - Hirer must not be penalised for 1) Late Payment of rental.   2) Accidents claims within that period of time.    3) Complaints made by passengers, road users.

    As bonus is an entity given to the hirer for his duty for maintaining the cab to useable condition (higher responsibility than relief driver), it is really optional and subjective to give half of it to the relief driver.   Well, depends on the hirer lor.  ( the relief has to be with the hirer for continously 365 days before arriving to this sharing of bonus ).  Sharing this benefit is on the discretion of the Hirer..

    Washing cab !! Night shift driver is responsible to wash the cab !! also need to clean inside and dust the mats....

    Hirer (normally drives the day shift), will have to maintain the cab's engine to tip-top conditions + air-con blowing cool air...

    Other issues like falling sick (with medical leaves), going on holiday (compensation per day), other expenditures (like sharing the useage of walky talky), accidents caused and lost of potential income, ... need to be iron out before agreeing to be partners.

    If you wants to be a ONE MAN OPERATION (OMO), all these issues are waived.
