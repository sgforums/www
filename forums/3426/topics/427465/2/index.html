    Not sure about now. Last time average  for FASTEST time to get yout TDVL is about 12 weeks, but it could be done in 8 wks (trust me I did it)

    First it takes 4 weeks after the first time you apply to LTA before you get the prov letter. LTA did mean it when they say it takes 3~4 weeks processing, they will take their own sweet time. :(

    With the letter then you can register with SMRT or CDG for their courses. It may take a couple of weeks before they call for initial briefing. Just tell your grandmother story and try to get to that week briefing. At this briefing you will be given your pasword to the online course.

    Finish the online course within one day and register for excursion. Again the next available excursion may be 2 weeks away, call them up and tell another grandfather story to join the same week excursion.

    After excursion you can book the exam time. Then you will get your TDVL in about 2-3weeks after passing the exam.

    Good luck!!

    Technically speaking...super express course 3 weeks. If you pass, 2 days to inform you the results, 7 days to process the TVL. Therefore, slightly more than a month to get the Taxi Licience. For me, I signed up with SMRT on 11 March My first session starts on 21 March Course completes on 6 April Exam on 8 April On 11 April, I got a call to say I pass with test 21 April - Can get my TVL. So overall, 5 weeks plus I got my licience. Remember you need LTA approval prior to the start of the course. LTA takes approximate one month to approve. SMRT is kind enough to let me start the express course without approval from LTA. My approval date from LTA is on 4 April. Comfort on the other hand need you to have an approval from LTA before they sign you up with the express course. There fore, if you go thru normal procedure. i think it will take 3 months to get the TVL provided you pass the first time.

    Another impt point... if you have criminal records or kenna charged with CBT, LTA may not approve you to be a taxi driver.
