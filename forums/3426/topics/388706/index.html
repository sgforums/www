    Road tax ? In diesel taxis , we called it diesel taxes , u pay rental , all included inside . Insurance also .

    Radio license ? By right , when renew road tax or diesel tax , u must pay , but knn when i hirer , by left i act blur one . Act blur and save $27 ? Then why not act more blur ?

    Tyre ? This one Taxi companies will act blur , u must ask to change if u think botak liao , they sometime act blur one . They must change for you . If they dun change , u said : " If i skid , u responsible for the accident ? what's your name ? I feedback . "

    If tyre punctured or burst , hirers must pay for the damage if it's still new . If need to mend , $5 only . If burst , new but cannot be use anymore , u will compensate it .  

    Season parking ? If u need season parking , u must buy la , if Multi Storey $90 fee , try to save by putting Night Coupons . If Open car park $65 , just buy .

    Season parking if got relief , can save money use night coupon , if OMO , better to buy .

    Vehicle Servicing , those brakes , those wipers , those engine oil change , all done by taxi coy la .

    Radio must be instal by hirer la . No need permission .

    That's all .

    If u drive past ERP gantry with pax and the IU beeps, that pax pay the ERP charge. if no pax, u pay.

    When pax take ur taxi within CBD between 5-11.59pm (Mon-Sat), u can press $3 city surcharge.
