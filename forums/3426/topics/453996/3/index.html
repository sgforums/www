    Not to promote CTOA, how it work while pumping diesel (CDG kiosk)?

    Once you are a member, after pumping the diesel, scan with the CDG card, you will prompt to pay an extra of 10% of the total diesel you had pump.

    For eg, 30L=$3 extra, so this $3, CTOA will contribute on behalf for you at year end, some sort of force saving.

    So if $3 each day, x 365days will come to $1095...Definitely more than enough to cover your medisave contribution..

    For us we pump outside, pay roughly $4 more for nothing but CTOA members pay a bit more for their own benefit.

    That explain why CDG kiosk always full house..
