    Bingjames, at this early point in taxi driving, best is to go and learn how to q everywhere. Q at real taxi stands, taxi stops and imaginary taxi stands. Imaginary or ghost taxi stands are those mutually known to regular passengers and taxi drivers. Some TD is bound to stop and wait there at certain times of the day.

    Airport q system also must master. U must fully understand how to q there and all the shortcuts from t1-t3, t2-t1, and all the various lanes inside. Trust me, after 1 yr u still may not know all the airport "secret paths" and free waiting areas and the routes some taiko drivers even taiko chrysler cabbies use to shortcut the q at certain timings. Lots of mickey mouse going on all the time at airport. Waste time or not, at this stage just follow n learn.
