  • bowah
    16 Dec 11, 14:52

    First of all, from your shift, you are a relief driver, not a super relief driver. My advice will remember the prominent object or tall building and the road surrounding it, for eg, if you see pinacle at cantoment road, you know where you are, from there you can go into tanjong pagar, outram, neil, chinatown, south bridge road, maxwell road, anson road and so on. Also remember famous food centre like maxwell foodcentre, or temple like sri maniam and so on. Sometime pax may tell us a small road which we might forget, so ask for any prominent building or object around it, if he said near pinacle, you will then be in a better picture to know it, or you can tell pax that you know where is pinacle, when reach there, kindly guide you along, Most will be willing to do it, afterall the best stop is what the pax wanted to stop at. But if you tell pax I dunno or never said anything, then pax may not be happy. Relationship between pax and TD is like all other relationship, it start at a point of interest, once that contact is make, it can become a friendly and happy ride for both pax and TD, unless you get those fark up one, then too bad.

    Tell the pax you're new and not sure of the way, no need to be shy. Ask them if thats ok or if they can guide you. Most will agree. Dont go and pressure yourself, relax you're not driving Barack Obama, dont need to be nervous.

    Most places now got 2 or 3 way to go, ask pax which way he like. Eg Tampines now can go by 3-4 way depends on exact location, dont always think must go by PIE or TPE, can use Tampines Road or Bartley also, ask them.

    Use your MDT map or if you have GPS use them, sometimes you free can cruise around estates and see the many ways to come and go.

    On top of what Gagger mentioned about asking pax to guide u, here my 2 cts worth:

    When i started, I had this huge map from NTUC income showing the expressways and main arterial roads. I studied it to hv an overview of the singapore topography. For eg, there are several huge obstacles that requires drivers to do big detours such as Bt Timah Nature Reserves, Blank Spaces which are Military Installations like Paya Lebar n Sembawang Airbase, etc.

    From north to south, what are the expressways (CTE, BKE n KPE) n main arterial roads (eg Upp Bt Timah rd/Clementi rd,  Jln Bahar/Jln Boon Lay, Upp Thomson/Thomson rd, Upp Serangoon/Serangoon rd, etc). Likewise for east to west.

    Next I tried to memorise all the HDB estates around our central Bt Timah Nature Reserves, east (eg , Bishan, AMK etc) west (eg, Bt Panjang, Jurong East/West,etc) north (Woodlands) n likewise for the south.

    Then I tried to visualise what HDB estates lie above/below PIE, what lies between PIE/AYE, ECP/TPE, SLE.

    After a while, there was this grid that formed in my mind which helped me to learn my routes faster n also understand why certain destinations require certain routes.
