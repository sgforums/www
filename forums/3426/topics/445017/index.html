  • JoeRaj
    7 Jan 12, 14:25

    From my previous experience:

    Serious moving offences are:

    a) Careless driving

    b) Inconsiderate driving

    c) Dangerous driving

    d) Reckless driving

    Normally for dangerous or reckless, there needs to be an observation of vehicle for about 400m. In your video, this is at most inconsiderate driving at the highest or the lowest changing lanes without signalling. (depends if he appeals an the IO grants a request to reduce the charge). If you were a police officer, even without video the charge could and would stick. But as a civilian, it will depend on your statement, willingness to testify, the video and of course his version. It will involve a lot of time on your part, so that's what you need to consider. Most of ex-colleagues have retired from TP, but technically I believe the driver could be charged if there's a will on the part of the IO.

    For BCL's part, even a video or speed camera in your vehicle cannot be used in court as it not tested and certified. Previously only TP motor-cycles and highway patrol cars had them, the speed gun and now also the camera. Normal patrol cars could not book a driver for speeding but a chase or observation of more than 400m, could easily result in a charge of dangerous or reckless driving.  Those days when I pvte limo driver sometimes, when CIsco of Aetos police riders or cars, I wouldn't even care and continue to drive fast (but of course not until over 120 kmh) If they go at 100, I would go at 110. But now I'm not sure because TP nowadays like to taiko all their duties, so maybe Cisco or Aetos motor-cycles may have been tested and certified.

    The thing to notice is the 2 red lights on their bikes. All these bikes have 2 speedometers, 1 for their own speed and the other to record an offenders, when they click the button, the red lights come on, so take note when you see 2 red lights on a bike, could be a TP bike recording someone's speed. For overhead  bridge hand held cameras, those days can only record 1 particular lane, but now I heard they got a better one that can cover 3 lanes, very clear and of course with your speed.

    And TP riders also have the hand held cameras, but I think this one is for 1 vehicle at a time.

    1 tip though: If you want to go fast, always better to have a leader. Eg: you driving fast but there's a car going even faster than, that car will become the target car not yours normally, of course you can't be 2-300 meters behind him, like that TP will catch you la, easier what.  But for speed cameras, all can be captured, this is only for mobile squad chasing. Normally if you go over 15kmh, they might leave you alone, but speed camera must be careful, expressway I think over by 10-12kmh, you are quite safe, normal road speed camera don't play, under 10kmh at most.
