    Wed, 14 Apr – Why $4.05 ??

     

    A 9% pax jumped into my front pax seat while I was waiting to cut in to New Bridge Rd from Teo Hong Rd (near Bt Pasoh). This guy must have the slickest Ninja skill, I didn’t even see him coming.

    Pax: “Uncle, go to Selva’s restaurant”

    Me: “Ah?? What??”

    Pax: “I want to go near Selva’s restaurant at River Valley Road, Delta House. You know?”

    What? River Valley is River Valley, Delta House is on Delta Rd what? OK lah, roughly I know where he wants to go, but what the F with Selva’s restaurant? Geez.

    Pax:” You just go, I will tell you where”

     

    I pressed the meter and started moving the cab.

    Pax: “I have to pay $4.05 ah?”

    Is this a trick question or what? NO lah, you moron. You have to pay at least a minimum of $3 + $4.05, or maybe around $6+$5+ when we reach your place.

     

    Turning into Outram smart aleck me told him : ”Next time you walk out and take taxi from here, then you can save $3”

    Pax: “Last time the taxi driver never charge me CBD charge”. Yeah right, and he also knows where Selva’s restaurant is, right?  Nabei.

    After turned from Zion to River Valley he pointed to the roti prata shop and said “This one is Selva’s”. Oh, thank you Sir for informing me such an important information, otherwise my life may get much shorter without knowing this info.

     

    I slowed down, trying to filter left to stop, then he said “No, no. You go, you go”

    Finally he got off before the traffic light near Valley Park.

     

    Note: Mr Walking-talking-eating GPS, please tell me that Teo Hong Rd is inside CBD, else I need to go to Selva’s restaurant to look for him to refund the $3. J

    Sat, 29 May - Car cannot reverse - WTF?!?

    After the dissappointing Friday which follows the holiday eve Thursday, Saturday was promising to be another big day. Town was packed with people, orchard rd jammed, thanks to the GSS. It was easy to pick pax inside other part of CBD and also the "ang moh area" was busy with many many booking call.

    At 23.55 again my MDT "ping". I quickly accept the job, hoping that the ang moh will be asking to go to airport. Alas, just when about to reach the  pick up location, the car refused to go stun. What The F&^# !??!     So I tried again and again but still it wouldn't reverse. After a minute I gave up and call the call centre to dispatch another taxi. Actually I was hesitating whether I should still go and pick up the pax (since the car can still move forward) but then I thought what happened if the gear box gave way halfway to the airport? It wouldn't be fair to the pax rushing to the airport right?

    Anyway, I am more angry with the fact that I couldn't continue to chiong the "after midnight" 50% surcharge period. But I guess it was my fate not to be able to do so that night, so with a very heavy heart I dialed for a tow truck to collect the cab away.

    Haiz...!!

    Tue, 1 Jun - Shortest trip from AP

    Finally I tasted the infamous AP inter-terminals short trip. Damage : $4.60+ $2.30 midnight surcharge. Hopefully this is the last time.

    It was also the first time I "ilegally" pick up pax from along the Departure hall stretch.

    I arrived to ICC1 shortly just after 3am last night (or technically, this morning), jus to find the place had been deserted. Not only there was no more worker, even Pool's latest favourite XYZ was no longer there. HAIZ.....  (later on I found out why they leave so early, coz everyone of you took 2 vouchers home, only me getting one voucher to tai kor place at 2am. Phui! )

    While driving past through the BT departure terminal, I saw the Cisco AP officer beckon my taxi to stop. Stop near him and wound down the front pax window. "Uncle, could you please take this lady to T2? She is late for her flight already". I glanced at the damsel in distress. Not bad, a nice looking foreign girl in mid 20s.  "Walk a bit to the main Q at ther arrival hall lah", I answered. "But she is late already" he said.  "OK OK, hop in"

    Reached there , the damage was $6.90. OK lah, I took it as consolation for not getting the last SATS job home. I was quite lucky as: a) got midnight charge.  b) did not have to wait 30 minutes in the queue.
