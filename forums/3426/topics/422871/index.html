    Dirty toilets and a lack of decent waiting areas make servicing at Transcab's Service Centre a daily ordeal for cabbies, says a STOMPer. By the time cabs are roadworthy, they've missed morning rush commuters.

    The STOMPer commented on the poor conditions at the centre:

    "There is a need for Transcab to really improve on their Service Centre's environment and the coordination for taxi servicing.

    "The company demands that drivers reach the service centre by 6am, but at 6am there is already a long line of taxis waiting to be serviced at the Transcab Service Centre due to poor coordination.

    "Not only are we required to wait in our cabs for hours, we lose precious income daily due to the fact that we miss the morning rush by the time servicing has ended, at around 9am.

    "In addition to that, the conditions at the service centre are horrible! The facilities are in such poor condition! Does anyone even clean the place? The floor is basically black from all the years it hasn't been cleaned.

    "The smell in the toilet is unbearable, and no one even cleans it. There is no toilet paper or anything.

    "For refreshments there is just a vending machine only, and the price is insane. There are only a few chairs for sitting.

    "Therefore don't push the blame to the taxi drivers if you can't catch a taxi in the morning, or you face a traffic jam while trying to navigate past these streets."
