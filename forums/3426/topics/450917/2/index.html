    2. With car prices sky rocketing, newbies may want to have a car to drive around at their own leisure time and a feel owning of it

    3. The illusion that being young, together with better education and good time management ,discipline, he or she can make it as an OMO, and for some who worked as superior or skill work before, may think Driving taxis is sup sup water.

    4. After passed the TDVL, the usual kanchiong mentality that allow other no option but straight to OMO.
