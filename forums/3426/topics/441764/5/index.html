    Originally posted by Bingjames:

    just curious, what's Pte Limo?

    a privately registered car used for taxi? can we do that?

     

    Private Hired Limousine (with a registered company).

    Well, the vehicle is privately owned & service the company's passengers exclusively.  Pte Limo can also do events job, arrival, departure, disposal etc. (No street pick up... because not a taxi). No meter.

    At the end of the day, the operation cost is much lower and after paying off your mortgage loans, the vehicle belongs to the company you registered.

    Some owners of Pte Limo have more than 1 vehicle.  i  got some contacts having Alphards (Vellfire), E-Class & S-Class, Toyota Hiace (highroof) 13 seaters and even Toyota Estima.
