    Today, out of 10 Cabby, at least 3 carry iPhone.

    During our TVL training, STA train us use the Street Directory to search for Name & Location of Building. 

    LTA regulation, every Taxi MUST have a copy of Updated Street Directory or else they can fine us.

    And it's true that the Street Directory is very useful to locate any Building or Apartment etc.

    But if you have an iPhone with, why don’t try to make use of the latest technology.

    Try to installed some GPS Application, the best is FOC.

    Out of so many iPhone GPS Apps, most of them cost you a Bomb. But there are still some excellence FOC iPhone GPS Apps.

    1) OneMap Singapore By NIIT Technology Pte Ltd. a PCMMi level 5 Certified & leading IT consulting Singapore company.

    http://itunes.apple.com/app/onemap-singapore/id429657299?mt=8

    They also have their Web versin copy of Singapore Street Directory:

    http://www.onemap.sg/index.html

     

    2) Singapore Maps By Streetdirectory Pte Ltd 

    http://itunes.apple.com/app/singapore-maps/id388128906?mt=8

    They also have their Web copy of Singapore Street Directory:

    http://www.streetdirectory.com/

     

    If you are carry an iPhone, try to download and installed these applications then try to compare with searching with your hard copy of Street Directory by Might Minds.

    Example : A Pax board your cab and said Uncle I want to go to Pinehurst Condo or a Ang Mo board your cab and said Uweei Building Please.

    How long you take to locate these address with hard copy of Street Directory and How long your iPhone Apps (OneMap or Singapore Maps) take to locate your Pax designation.

     

    Beside act as a Street Directory, these applications still can direct you to your searched designation.

    Use your iPhone OneMap Singapore to locate Uweei Building follow by press the “Get Direction” then “Go” Key, within seconds it show the routes from your current location to Uweei Building.

    Off course it may not the Best routes but at least it give you a guide to your Pax designation.

    Since you have invested so much to get an iPhone then make full use of it.
