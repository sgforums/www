    For day shift

    One must be on the ball for peak hour .

    Must endure the heat , the morning hours jam , the ERP cost , the nastiness of paxes , your road cruising stamina must be good . Like me , if i cruise more than 30 minutes without paxes , my next destination is coffee shop .

    Don't expect to work lesser hours for day shift , unlike night shift .
