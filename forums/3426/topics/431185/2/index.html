    It is good to take some experience driver advices here, however, take it as a guidelines, I too take some good advices here and enjoyed the jokes very much after a hard day work especially from ALN and Komade and many others, their posts are very much relevant in our trade which in one way or the other, you will find similar situation while driving.

    Then from these guidelines, do your own planning and the way you wanted to drive as you drive on.     And then, more importantly, you need to find time to pamper yourself once awhile for your hardwork, be it enjoying outing with family, exercises or massage etc etc. Give yourself a pat.

    As for income, as you drive on, you eventually can estimate yourself and set target with a given tolerances, do not set a target that ask for heaven and hell, you will find you struggling with others and may, touch wood, get into an accident, or booked by LTA or TP, then that is too bad.
