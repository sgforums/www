    But recently I begin to notice a trend of having 4 seaters purposely park half a car length behind my cab. Already it is quite bad that some pple avoided 7 seaters but then when they do that it makes the whole matter worst. Pple will think something wrong with this taxi and all go behind.

    And the culprit is usually a comfort cab or older taxi drivers.

    Yesterday at Tanglin Mall this happen to me. Never had I experience a problem at Tanglin Mall. Even there are pple who avoided, it is usually only 1 or 2 pax skip and I would go off in no time. Bloody comfort cab purposely park a distance when I go forward into the lot like everyone else. kanna skip until my blood boil. I so piss off that I simply reverse back and block the entire lobby. In the end, less than one min I got a pax and go.

    Crap man, where all the ethics and moral goes to. When I am in a queue, even pple come to me i would refer them to the first taxi in queue unless I know normal cab cannot take the pax for some reasons(eg bulky items or 5 pax and above).

    Now I have to play dirty also, if the 4 seater horn I let them be. Damn piss off. usually it wun happen, but now it is getting more and more common.

    I wonder has Estima Driver come across this ?

    And today at Woodlands MRT Taxi Stand. Long queue of pax no taxi. I go in first lot and no one wan to board. Ok nevermind, I take out my story book and I read and ignore those stingy faggots. 5 min or so pass and still no other cab come.

    Finally one guy from behind the queue approach me and ask me why i am infront of the taxi. I told him this fat golden cow (of course I never use this term on him just in case some of u think I am so daring) is actually a taxi and if he wan to board. In the end he did, the one bloody pax 2nd in the queue can say Why the fuck I block the taxi stand.

    So damn piss. I am begin to experience penting up of anger inside me these few days. tulan man.
