    This bring me to a term call Maslow motivation laws of human hierrachy,

    Maslow is a professor in managment who said that peoples have their needs and ambitious, it takes a good leader or manager to identify it and bring the best out of them, on the hand, individual can also put himself up to a state of achievement. However, all these motivation can diminish when the needs are not met.

    Example, if you are a crown driver, you think of driving a sonatas or wish, given the good opportunities, you move up, then you want to drive limo, maxi, pte limo and so on, each platform must provide the motivation for one to move up, one must see the good and the needs to move up, taxi management must provide the conducive enviroment to upgrade it driver and provide care, once this is met, people will excel and climb up the hierrachy ladder to be the best cabby. This is call Maslow laws of motivation, because he understand that human wants to excel, but you need to met their need before they can excel to the next platform and so on.

    But sad to say, taxi companies and govt LTA take more care of themselves, there is not enuf support and motivation to bring out the best of a cabby, if you like my hirer driving a crown for years, sure get bore and low in morale, it takes alot of self motivation to bring one up the ladder of achievement.
