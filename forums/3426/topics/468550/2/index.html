    species # 1: Bok Kwa Cheow,  specis # 2: mata puteh, species #3: tng beh (long tail), specis # 4: Huay Bee.

    My first Bok Kwa Cheow is a species from Borneo Island and was given to my by my brother in law when it is 1 year old. Big size and different from regional breeds. Good stamina in singing. Good fighting spirit. I even trained this bird to be free out of the cage. The cage was specially handmade by one of the cage maker in Strathmore and i pay him quite a sum. This bird can come out of the cage and go back in again. 

    My mom cannot tahan me but slowly also become my assitance.  My granny always say : Chee Cheow Kay Hay Liao !  My nevertheless she still help me shower the bird and learn how to make my special "ingredient" food stuff for my bird.  My father also help me. My first old bok kwa cheow is my alarm clock early in the morning.  This is the bird that stay with me for 10 years and die of old age.  Incredible feat for a Bok Kwa Cheow to live 11 years. When my bok kwa cheow on prime time, people offer to buy at very high price from me due to special breed and performance. But not for sales.

    I give up birds becos of my previous job that i travelled a lot and always not in SG.

    Next time when my children grown up i will start again.   Back to Bok Kwa Cheow, and will try my technic on a good breed "Kah Leng" from Borneo.  May be add on a parrot and together with the Kah Leng - will do "cross-talk" scolding people.
