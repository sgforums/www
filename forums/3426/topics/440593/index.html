    As related by a blue sonata cabby friend (Richard) of mine:

     Richard was looking for a lot in an open-air car park of a hawker centre during his lunch break and he noticed a silver Kia on his left preparing to leave. All lots on his right are season lots. Judging that the Kia is not ready to leave soon, he circled to the other side looking for other lots.

    Unsuccessful, he returned to the Kia’s lot and as he was approaching the Kia (about 12 lots away), a car left its lot about 4 lots away from Richard’s left. Richard stopped to allow the car to pass and was pleased that he got a lot as the car park was crowded at the time. 

    Suddenly, from nowhere, a driver in a black BMW (ASS) dashed from the opposite direction and reversed into the lot. ASS, in his haste, did not manage to park properly in his first attempt. As the Kia just left his lot, Richard inched forward to indicate that he wanted to pass. ASS immediately dashed forward again blocking the path of both Richard and the Kia. He looked at Richard angrily and refused to reverse back. After several seconds, ASS finally reversed into his lot. 

    Richard shook his head in disgust but continued towards the lot vacated by the Kia. However, a van was already preparing to reverse into the lot. Cursing his bad luck, Richard waited for the van to park. Whilst waiting, Richard noticed from his rear view mirror that ASS has left his car and was walking towards his cab gesturing to Richard with his index finger insultingly, summoning him. “wtf, u took my lot, make me lose another and I must report to u for lecture? Who u think u are!” Richard thought. Richard ignored him and continued his search but noticed that ASS had angrily took out his mobile and started snapping shots of his cab. 

    As Richard circled around to the other side of the car park, ASS continued to pursue Richard obviously wanting a confrontation. Richard was really irritated and wound down his window and showed ASS the big BIRD so that he can BACK OFF. 

    Richard finally parked at roughly the opposite side of the car park where ASS parked. ASS had followed Richard for 60-70 metres round the car park on foot just for a confrontation. As Richard got out and closed the door of the cab, ASS approached menacingly and with his face barely 2-3 cm away from Richard’s, yelled: “why you show middle finger at me?” Richard thought he was about to get assaulted but stood his ground and retorted: “so, you going to assault me for a small matter like this? Fxxx you!” 

    Realising that Richard wasn’t that easy to bully, ASS changed tactics and started taunting Richard to repeat what was just said so he could tape down the conversation. “I’m going to complain to your company. You all taxi drivers….” Richard walked off towards the hawker centre not wanting to hear any further insults. On looking back, Richard found ASS still hanging around his cab. Richard returned to his cab and fearing for the cab and his safety, decided to call the police and report of harassment and attempted assault. ASS continued to taunt him and challenged Richard to stay and “settle” the matter and wait for the police. Richard ignored him and went to the hawker centre to order food. 

    Two constables arrived in a patrol car about 2 minutes later and listened to Richard’s complaint. ASS was nowhere to be found. When asked to identify the car, Richard could not as in the lot where Richard remembered ASS parked was a black Volvo! “Is this the car, I thought it was a BMW! I’m seldom wrong about such things. Maybe he had left” Richard thought. 

    I feel that Richard’s dignity and rights had been violated. ASS obviously thought he had the upper hand and could bully someone who he thought could not retaliate.

    BMW Torque Specs

     

      • Torque is the force that turns the car wheels.

        For the American market, as of October 2010, the BMW company made the 128i, the 135i, the 328i, the 335i, the 528i, the 535i, the 550i, the 650i, the 740i, the 750i, the 760i, Alpina B7, the X3, X5, X6, Z4, M3 and M6. For these models, there are many different trim levels, but all boast respectable, high-end torque specs.

      1-Series

      • The 128i trims have 3.0-liter six-cylinder engines producing 230 horsepower (hp) and 200 foot-pounds of torque.

        The 135i has the same general engine specs as the 128i, but the 135i trims produce 300 hp and 300 foot-pounds of torque.

      3-Series

      • The 328i trims have a 3.0-liter, six-cylinder inline engine producing 230 hp. These cars produce 200 foot-pounds of torque.

        The 335i and the 335i XDrive, with the same general engine specs as the 328i trims, produce 300 hp and 300 foot-pounds of torque.

        The diesel 3-Series sedan, the 335d, has a 3.0-liter displacement with six cylinders but is turbocharged, and produces 265 hp and 425 foot-pounds of torque.

      5-Series

      • The 528i sedan has a 3.0-liter, six-cylinder engine producing 240 hp and 230 foot-pounds of torque.

        The 535i trims have the same engine displacement as the 528i, but produce 300 hp and torque of 300 foot-pounds.

        The 550i trims have a 4.4-liter V8 engine and produce 400 hp and 450 foot-pounds of torque.

      6-Series

      • The 650i coupe and convertible have a 4.8-liter V8 engine producing 360 hp and 350 foot-pounds of torque.

      7-Series

      • The 740i and 740Li sedans have a 3.0-liter six-cylinder inline engine producing 315 hp and 330 foot-pounds of torque.

        The 750i and 750Li sedans have a 4.4-liter V8 engine producing 400 hp and 450 foot-pounds of torque.

        The 750i and 750Li ActiveHybrid sedans have a 4.8-liter V8 engine producing 455 hp and 480 foot-pounds of torque.

        The 760Li has a 6.0-liter V12 engine producing 535 hp and 550 foot-pounds of torque.

        The Alpina B7 has a 4.4-liter V8 engine with 500 hp and 526 foot-pounds of torque.

      X5 and X6 Series

      • The X3 trims have a 3.0 six-cylinder engine, with the 28i trim producing 240 hp and 221 foot-pounds of torque and the turbocharged 35i producing 300 hp with 300 foot-pounds of torque.

        The X5 trims have turbocharged 3.0-liter, six-cylinder engines apart from the X5 XDrive 50i, which has a 4.4-liter V8 engine with 400 hp and 450 foot-pounds of torque. The gasoline X5s produce 300 hp and 300 foot-pounds of torque against the diesel 35d with 265 hp and 425 foot-pounds of torque.

        The X6 ActiveHybrid has a V8 with 480 hp and 450 foot-pounds of torque. The X6 XDrive35i has a turbo 3.0-liter six-cylinder engine with 300 hp and 300 foot-pounds of torque. The X6 XDrive 50i has a 4.4-liter V8 with 400 hp and 450 foot-pounds of torque.

      Z-Series

      • The Z4 has a 3.0-liter six-cylinder engine with 255 hp and 220 foot-pounds of torque for the sDrive 30i, 300 hp and 300 foot-pounds of torque for the sDrive35i, and 335 hp and 369 foot-pounds of torque for the sDrive35is.

      M-Series

      • The M3 trims have a 4.0-liter V8 engine producing 414 hp and 295 foot-pounds of torque.

        The M6 trims have a 5.0-liter V10 producing 500 hp and 383 foot-pounds of torque.

        The X5M and X6M have a 4.4-liter V8 engine producing 555 hp and 500 foot-pounds of torque.
