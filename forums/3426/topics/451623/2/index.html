    Originally posted by InSPirE78:

    There will be a senior workshop personnel (Mr.Toh) who will be with you when you collect your vehicle. He will go through all scratches and dents with you and mark it on a form, so when you return all those that are recorded will not be charged to you as new damage. Check and ask him about the tyres.

    I never knew about the tyres thingy until the day when I return my taxi. Insisted to Toh that this particular tyre came issued when I collected from him. In the end he relented and did not charge me for it.He just took another from an accident wish and replace with mine.

    But I did pay for another tyre which I had bought resale outside because of sidewall puncture. Lesson; if you have sidewall puncture, it is still cheaper to buy from Transcab because when that tyre runs bald they will charged you for not using their original tyre.
