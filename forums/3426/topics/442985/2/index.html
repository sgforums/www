    Yo Bro Mercabs,

    Not all Pte-Limo (Vito & Highroof) have contracts to work on.  If you basically depends on AP transfers, you won't make much.  Taxis can Q at AP, Pte Limo can't.  Unless you get one arrival job paged out in the Pte-Limo Group.

    So, most of the time, you may have to come down empty to town (Vito standard orders @$45 point 7.  Which means you only made $38 for 2 trips (up & down), excluding diesel & possible ERP tolls.  

    Vito & Highroof are in the same category of buses, hence, 50% more than TDs for ERP tolls.

    Hence, if you can get some regular pax (from your current Mercedes cab trade), who can gives you hourly booking, then you can consider coming out as a Pte Limousine.

    W/o hourly booking, it is a risk that you have to bear.  Pte Limo or Limo cab groups, Hourly bookings normally go under-tunnel... .  Like it or not..... caller will arrange for his own kakis 1st. ( Correct ? ).
