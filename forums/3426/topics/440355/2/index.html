  • Just_do_it_lah
    2 Nov 11, 20:31
    Originally posted by Steaks:

    I would like to become a part time cabby next year when I reach 30.What are the steps to get a taxi license?

    1stly, make sure that your medisave are fully top-up...

    2ndly, need to go LTA Sin Ming Rd to pay S$40 for registration (non-refundable) & wait for 2 weeks (a letter)....

    3rdly, they will send you a letter (if approved), to go for Medical checkout (polyclinic)

    4thly, you need to apply for VL (course)... not too sure on the procedures now... done it almost 9 yrs ago... .  Maybe you can do more research on the googles search or taxis' webpages.

    5thly, once you passed the MCQ tests at Taxi Academy at Red Hill, you need to look for a available vehicle (best within your estate) to be a relief....

    If you want to drive on OMO (One Man Operation), you need to go down to any (of your choice) taxi company to apply....

    Good Luck....
