    Somehow, this, I have to agree with chief. For taxis driver, GPS is a training tool, over a period of time, it should become more of a locator which smartphone can also do it, thereafter, it is not much of a use.

    Adults learn by practical and reality, they do not learn thru classroom and guide, that is why adults education involve lots of practical real stuff, Newbies should take every trip as a learning journey, and in learning, we must be ready to face the music and learn from it.

    Most of us learned the hard way without GPS, not the mention smartphone that do not exist during our times, maybe only a streetdirectory. Therefore, we become very flexible with routes and demand from pax. Take for example, with GPS, you already plotted out a road , then all of the sudden pax said, go by another road, and you got a brain stuck there, but for most of us who do not depend on GPS will not be a problem, we can even think of the road the pax is thinking. And once you reach that stage, you can drive very relaxing and when you are relaxing, you get less stress, less fatigue and more happy.
