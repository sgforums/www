    Hi. Been a silent follower of the forum for so long, cant resist my fingers to post a reply to this thread. FYI I am a OMO For e start, I think it is perfectly fine to charge relief $50 for a fri or sat 8pm to 8am shift. Relief will have no problem to fetch a decent return as town is full with paxs even during midnite. If lucky, can get connections at hdb estate. But to charge relief $50 for a weekday 8pm to 8am shift is subjective. For me is a no no. Granted that there are 6 hrs of peak hrs and 6 hrs of midnite surcharge. Relief will face a challenge to make it works. For example. Relief starts driving at 8pm at CCK, if lucky he can get a fare straight away at CCK. Cross his fingers that the trip will bring him to town or near it. If not shuttles between hdb towns, or drives to nearby business/ industrial district and waits for call. Likely he will need to drive empty to town starting fr 11pm. By then town is almost dead. After 2am, waiting time will be longer. Midnite drive during mon to thurs is never easy. No doubt relief can still earn a minimum of $100 for this 6 hrs of midnite driving. Considering his higher usage of diesel, his hourly average will be lower than those driving during non peak hrs daytime. Come to morning peak, relief will have to drive very restrictedly as he will have to return cab by 8am. Can he take call or fetch street pax to airport and rush back to CCK in time? Imagine getting himself stuck in jam on expressway during his way back. Lastly should he need to wash car or top up diesel, more downtime needed. Finally it still comes down to the supply vs demand thingy. If relief willing to bite e bullet and take e 8pm to 8am slot for weekdays, it will be his choice. He has only himself to answer for his decision. As a responsible hirer, one has to set terms to achieve a win win situation for both hirer and relief. Then relationship will then be a long lasting one
