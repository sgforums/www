    Huh? Wad 200 wad 120? I lost u there? 4 hrs for $120?? If u are not driving a limo, $30 per hour although too low but still ok. Take it as a lesson.

    I suggest u dress well on that day. Cause dressing well will give u good impression. Pple pay for looks.

    If got pple ask u so exp if u charge $40 the next time. Tell them it is a personalise service + u will absorb all ERP etc.

    I forgot to tell u, when u charge flat rate, ERP/Peak Hour rate/Booking are all inclusive. To play safe, I always tell customer I absorb up to $5 per hour of booking.

    Ask them give u the locations they wan to go. For value added service, u can give suggestion on what time to go where and how to go to minimise ERP and time.

    U should tell them to give u a SMS for pick up as u will not be parking at paid carpark and will be waiting near by. Scarly they wan to leave early then u die.

    VERY VERY IMPORTANT,

    Negotiate such rates only when you are in the taxi with customer or over the phone. Do not taut on the road. U cannot charge flat rate for when customer flag u down.

    After a while, as you are ur own boss, you fix a rate u feel it is worth for u.
