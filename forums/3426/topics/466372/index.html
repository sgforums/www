He was pronounced dead at the scene by the Singapore Civil Defence Officers. He was hit near the Farrer Road Market bus stop. The driver of the Volvo, a woman who looked to be in her 40s, declined to comment. She looked visibly shaken and was dabbing her tears with a tissue. She was overheard telling SCDF officers: “It happened too fast and I couldn’t stop.”
