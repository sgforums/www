    Bro, just for you...so how's driving with your new Chevolet?

    Normal taxi picking at AP had to be tactful. CAAS very strict on that. If not careful, summon will fly in and will have hard time appealing.

    Anywhere in AP, you are not allowed to pick up pax at Departure Hall, the fine is $200....

    T1, no problem...make sure your pax know where's pick up point, beside the big open carpark...Pillar 9 the safest, mata won't disturb you.

    Never pick up pax at T2 arrival hall driveway, no matter what unless you are limousine taxi. The CCTV won't give chance, do not risk regardless whoever tell you can. Park at proper carpark and greet your pax at belt is the easiest.

    Same go to T3...unless you process the SAT sticker or else don't risk also.

    Budget Terminal is small, park at open carpark and greet your pax, should be fine...do not pick up at glass door near arrival hall..easy target for CAAS.

    Operating of meter, you have to figure out yourself..$8 plus meter for departure is attractive enough but not for arrival...waiting time from flight landing to picking up will kill off all your time........but if is your good friend, no harm done.

    Good luck, just remember, many TD had been issue summon by CAAS, the successful appealing rate is not very high. Do what you deem right.
