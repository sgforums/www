    Open house at Bayfront, Marina Bay stations on 13 Jan

    The Land Transport Authority (LTA) is inviting the public to visit the two Circle Line extension stations, Bayfront and Marina Bay on Friday 13 January.

    The Circle Line 2.4km extension with two stations will open on Jan 14.

    From 6pm to 9.30pm, the public can look forward to an entertaining evening with street buskers and stage performances of music genres ranging from jazz to pop.

    Public can take unlimited free rides throughout the duration of the Open House to explore and view the different architectural details such as the Art-in-Transit artworks in the two stations.

    The first 3,000 visitors to the Open House will also receive an exclusive Circle Line extension door gift.

    To get to the Open house, public is encouraged to take a train to Marina Bay station on the North South Line.
