    Mon - Sun & Public Holidays: 6.00pm to midnight

    Midnight Surcharge - 50% of metered fare

    Midnight to 5.59am

    The reason why I also look into split shift is also because of the peak hours surcharge. If one were to work from 9:30am to 6:00pm (8.5 hours), theoretically he will miss out the 25% extra collection compared to someone who work from 6:00am to 9:30am (3.5 hours) and from 6pm to 11pm (5 hours). The off-peak hours TD will have to drive another 2.5 hours (8.5*1.25) to earn the same as the peak hours TD.

    The peak hours TD can do a lot of things during the off-peak hours (8.5 hours). He can have both his lunch and early dinner with his family, run errands, rest or even sleep especially during friday where it is possibly one of the best day to drive.
