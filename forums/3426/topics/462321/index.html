  • ericgo
    3 Dec 12, 04:01

    This is probably old news to some. Anyway for those parents(Self Employed) who are not sure you are actually entitled to this claims.

    From what i know many self employed doesnt know and have never claim before.

    So all Tds here pls note as you are considered as self employed.

    You are allowed to claim up to 3 working days per work year as long as your child is below 7 years old on or after 31 October 2008.

    http://www.mom.gov.sg/employment-practices/employment-rights-conditions/leave-and-holiday/Pages/childcare-leave.aspx#claims

    As to how to claim?

    Just log in to your CPF account using your singpass.

    Go to "My Request" that is located at the left side of the screen.

    Click on "Self Employed Matters" and than Govt-paid Childcare Leave(Self Employed)

    And you can proceed to submit your claims.

    As to how much govt is paying will be based on your iras income declaration the previous year.

    Takes abt 2 working weeks for the money to be credited to your bank account.
