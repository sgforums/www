    After driving taxis for so long, let me ask you just one question, have you see new taxis rental fall before?

    In business, there are 2 kind of costs, one know as the intangible, the other know as the tangible cost.

    Intangible meaning fix cost, this one usually will only move up, seldom go down

    Tangible meaning flexible cost, this one of being flexible of cost can move up and down lah

    Taxi rental price is intangible, it will remain and go up when necessary

    It is the perks and benefits that come along to offset the rental price or make it looks attractive, we said the intangible price.

    If cost in operation is down, you tends to see taxis companies start to provide this perk that 660 per month, this fuel rebates, loyalty bonuses and all these keep changing to lure people in and at the same time instill discipline like must not have 3 x complaint, must not have accident, must not delay rental.

    So if you will to ask me if rental price will go down, I will said NO, more benefit and perks, I will said yes but at the expense of more and better discipline expected from TD.

    Remember almost all our Taxi companies are govt link, they work in a way that even the money is yours, they expect something from you, from performances to no claim and so on.

    In every BSheet account statement of a company, you can alway see this tangible and intangible asset/cost
