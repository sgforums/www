    IMHO if you want to be a relief driver with CDG.Registered yourself with CDG,place your security deposit with them.Ask them for driver portal CDG website login & password.Go matching tab to check for suitable hirer,off course must call and discuss with them the price,timing,term & conditions....so and so...For my side Jurong West & Boon Lay,no problem finding a hirer."It is a relief choose hirer market" especially for Sat & Sun relief job.I don"t really know why the turnover rate for vacancy of night relief at my side is always very high so not really a problem if i choose to drive @ night.But sometimes having too many option to choose can be a bad thing too,its like you dun know which one to choose.Just like you go to a car park with abundance of parking lots,you dun know which one to parkHope this help!
