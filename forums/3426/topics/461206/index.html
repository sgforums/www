    GPS is good to have but cannot 100% depend on GPS.

    I use it also, but mostly when me and pax don't know how to get there..

    The trick as a newbie like me is ask, ask, ask ..

    Like Pool said before in other thread, as a newbie, know the route also ask, don't know must ask.

    For example:

    Pax: Go Jln Kechubong

    You: Jln Kechubong, hmm.. Where is it near ah? (1. If you don't know)

    You: Jln Kechubong, near Yio Chu Kang Rd ah? (2. Roughly know)

    Normally pax will tell u near some major road, if still don't know, ask for a landmark.

    For this case pax might say near Yio Chu Kang.

    Yio Chu Kang very long, so ask pax again near Upp Thomson side or Serangoon.

    Pax will normally say near Jln Kayu.

    Still can ask its near Jln Kayu towards Hougang or towards AMK

    From there you should know roughly which route to take. Key in to your GPS also just in case.

    When u roughly know how to go, ask pax.. Wanna go by which way, if pax say anyway. Then say what u have in mind, let's say CTE- exit AMK ave 5.

    Then pax will say go CTE - exit YCK.

    Lets say exit CTE, GPS say turn right, just confirm again with pax, "I go right ah?"

    Normally if u ask a few times pax will know u new/not sure they guide u all the way.

    Near destination, GPS sure ask u turn left into Gerald Dr.

    Confirm with pax again, "i turn left at traffic light there ah?"

    From there must remember how to go already.. After pax alight, try to visualise the route you take. Then try see in GPS or street directory if got alternative route or if u come from other place how to go that same place.

    Important thing to note, be humble bro, especially with pax. See some of your post sometimes you the type easily get angry.

    If pax angry angry you go wrong way, do what TDVL instructor taught. Either give them discount or if see pax sure going to complain type. Just give free ride, free ride no receipt so unlikely pax complain.

    Got once i go wrong long way, pax also know..

    End trip fare was something like $22.60, i say $20 will do since i went wrong way earlier.

    Pax gave me $25 ask me keep the change and ask me have a nice day!
