    On 24 Sept 11, I picked up a taxi booking call from MBS hotel Tower. Along the way at Marina Boulevard, I saw a lot of zombies walking and flagging but no taxis in sight. At 512am, a male in his twenties with his female companion boarded my cab at MBS. I dropped the lady first at Elizabeth towers condo near Mt Elizabeth hospital. The guy going to lavender, I started to go towards Orchard then via Serrangoon to Lavender, when the guy reminded me to go by CTE and exit via Moulmein, I said not very sure leh but make a U-turn to CTE anyway.

    On the way, he started to question me why I do not know the way and did my company send me to go for any course before started to drive a cab. I said I have recommended a route to Lavender via Serangoon road and just that not all taxi drivers know the best routes to take at a given location. He went on to give sarcastic remarks like if you do not know the way, how are you going to fetch customers to their destination. I said I've already recommended a
    different route.

    He also said something like: What happens if you have customers like me who don't bother to tell you which way to go to their destination, I said: Oh, I've never met such a person like you before". From here, he started to give problems. He kept giving sarcastic remarks but I kept quiet until he said:" I pay you money and expected you to know all the routes well and bring me to my destination". Then I blurted out: Your money is big fucxx is it? After that he accused me on using vulgar
    language on him and then started to call my taxi company a few times, accusing me of threatening him, abusing and using vulgar language on him, and also he feel threatened, and demand a explanation from them latest by the next day. He also called the polce and repeated the same blown up issues to the police and call the police to come when I stopped at the exit at Moulenmein.

    I also said that "the police will not entertain you". Who will have thought that this
    idiot blown the issues out of proportion by exaggerating that his life is threatened, and he felt threatened, when I have not even touch him or use any threatening words. A small argument can become a life threatening issues blown up by that idiot so that the police will come to help him to save face. What a moron can think of to waste police resources. He even mentioned that he is a professional and "I know the Law, ok".


    I then asked him : "Did I ever point a knife at you".  When he says he feel threatened. I aslo asked him to F.O. from my cab, after which later he asked me what is F.O. and I said Fucxx off. He repeatley said that he wanted to ask his lawyer to sue me or file a civil suit. While waiting, I have asked him that I just wanted to apologise and move on, thinking that its not because I was wrong but to let him win, and close the issues fast, but he insisted a written apology and attached it to be sent to comfort also. I was thinking this guy is really asking too much.While waiting for the police to arrive, I kept saying that "you are really wasting my time."

    I also said that I will countersue him from my lawyers.I refused to accept his fare I said that is was unfair to do so as the meter was running while stationary and waiting for a very long time, and also I did not bring him to his destination. He insisted to pay me as he said money is not the issue here and even boasted that he will gladly pay up even if the fare is a few hundred dollars. (telling me you are rich?). I still refused his fare and also refused to print a receipt for him ($36+), as I did not collect any fare from him.
