    Hi Bros, Just a two-cent worth opinion from an old man but half-new TD (only 6 mths old OMO).

    In every trade, there are bound to be black sheep, but at the same time, we must not forget the nice and friendly ppl too.  In my 6 mths' stint as a OMO TD, I have my fair share of the so-called "ugly" paxs who would show no mercy at thrashing at the TD at the slightest thing.  However, I have also met wonderful paxs, who thanked for the trip driven, asked before eating in the taxi (mostly those on way to work, eat breakfast in hurry), those who thanked for helping them into cab, putting luggage into boot, and of course those who tipped generously (these are not confined to only angmohs or tourists).

    This is a hectic line, and if we should allow ourselves to be affected by some out of the blue kind of unhappy incident, our mood will be lousy and rest of driving time will not be too smooth either.  I always try to forget/delete any 'ugly' paxs once they have alighted, look forward to another pleasant trip with another pax.  End of the day is the fare I can collect for the day. 

    Start the day with a whistle and end it with a song - my philosophy!  Hope u all don't find me lor soh, my ah pek style of staying happy with the job.
