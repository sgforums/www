    The Singapore Exchange Ltd will be organising their annual running event, SGX The Bull Charge 2012 on 16 Nov 2012. If you are heading down to the CBD area via Esplanade Drive, please take note of the following road closures which will take effect from 5.30pm to 6.30pm on 16 Nov 2012:

    - 2 left lanes of Esplanade Drive in the direction of Fullerton Road.
    - 2 left lanes of Fullerton Road in the direction of Collyer Quay.
    - 2 left lanes of Collyer Quay in the direction of Raffles Quay.
    - 2 left lanes of Marina Boulevard in the direction of East Coast Parkway (between Collyer Quay and Bayfront Avenue).

    SINGAPORE: The annual Utsav Deepavali Street Parade will be held on Saturday. 

    The following roads will be closed to vehicular traffic from 1pm-11pm:

    Race Course Road (between Hampshire Road and Kinta Road), Rotan Lane, Race Course Lane (between the entrance of the open public carpark and Race Course Road), and the carriageway of Kinta Road in the direction of Race Course Road (between the entrance of the open public carpark and Race Course Road).

    Police say parking restrictions along the peripheral roads will be strictly enforced. Vehicles found parking and causing obstruction will be towed away.
