    For me when driving, i'm very particular about one thing. Clear windscreen. When i got my Wish 1.8 from TC can see that the car been there quite some time, windscreen very dirty. So go car wash, then realise got water marks on front and side windscreen.

    Everyday i clean the glass at least twice, car not shiny nevermind, can see clearly more important. Then one day it rained, the water marks and wiper marks really driving me crazy.

    So i google for a solution, some recommend using glass cooking stove liquid cleaner. So i purchased one, comes with a scrubbing pad scrub scrub scrub, looks ok at first. But then rain again. Still have water marks, which really hinder my sight.

    Then i go Autobacs, look see, bought Autoglym Glass polish, stated can remove stain. Autoglym, german brand must be good. Then use, wah good very clear. I use on inside also.

    Following their video http://shop.autoglym.com/glass-and-mirrors/car-glass-polish.html i clean my wiper with it too.. Really good at cleaning wipers.

    Then rain again, still got watermarks!!!!

    Then go Autobacs again, see Soft99 Glass refresh, written in japanese don't understand what it was for. I also read some glass  restorer might even make the glass more blur it never use properly, so i went their website check instructions before buying.

    http://www.soft99.co.jp/english/products/carcare/glass_cleaner/glass_refresh.html?pid=05066

    Hmmm, so simple. Aiyah just buy lah $16+ only.

    Then go to nearby carpark, use the included pad  buff buff buff. I got no pail to wash it off so use a bottle of water wash off a bit so can see ok liao, later can go nearby carwash to fully wash off. Then i went carwash, When they spray water, WTF! All the water marks now gone.

    The windscreen wet still super clear like not wet.

    Those wanna buy maybe could share with a friend, cos i did whole car windscreen still got a lot left. Wish got front, left and front right, 2nd row left and right, 3rd row let and right and rear, still got about half bottle left.

    Still i would also use Autoglym Glass Polish for inside and cleaning wipers make it super clear like no windscreen.. LoL
