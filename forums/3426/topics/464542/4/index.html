    Hi everyone, I am back. To start this journal for what new TD to be, this is a run down of module you will go through and my 2 cents experience about the test.

     

    There is a total of 5 module:

    Module 1: Taxi Service Overview and Road Safety

    Module 2: Taxi Rules and Regulation

    Module 3: Locate Destination

    Module 4: Plan Routes

    Module 5: Deliver Quality Taxi Service

     

    Module 5 is a class room test individually. There are a total of 3 practical tests in 3 days.

    1. The test is about how you check vehicle, vehicle condition, on duty checklist

    2. Wheel chair handling and,

    3. Role play of pax on board and alighting.

  • 602
    8 Mar 13, 21:39

    Module 1 (Score 40 of 50 question to PASS), Module 2 (Score 43 of 50 to PASS)

    Module 1 and 2 being the most easier one unless one who are not in English. Module 2 is mostly common sense question and straight forward.

     

    Module 3: Locate Destination

    Module 3 has a total of 50 question and you need to score 41 to pass. A street directory is provided to locate a location, building, Taxi stand, Place of interest, POI etc.

     

    This is the paper I assume is the 2nd most hardest one as it consume a lot of time flipping directory to locate a place. It requires memorize of the street directory legend, index and contents.

     

    Module 4: Plan Routes

    this is the hardest module to pass. It has 30 question but require 24 to pass. It is not tricky nor difficult. The challenge is becoz there is no way we can remember all the singapore road name (perhaps not for me). Despite of the 40 Route given in the notes, we needed to memorize them even though we know the short cut. 

     

    Usually, module 3 and 4 is tedious due to the time constraint. And during the test, we need to keep focusing on the street directory and flip from pages to pages. Concentrating for 1 hour on the map can be painful and hot boiling blood.

     

    All test are carried out in multiple choice and is constraint to be completed within 1 hour. It is tested using iPad in the class room.
