  • bowah
    23 Feb 12, 11:49

    I think it is good to chat when the topic is good, no harm done, as taxi Driver, we also cannot put ourselve inside a cocoon, need to ask and talk to people of different trade, exchange view and keep ourselve abreast of what is happening.

    But of course, you must know where you stand, not to overly indulge into debate that determine who win or lose kind of silly stuff, just be moderate, humerous and humble. Just ah ah, ok ok,,...can also give people an impression of proud TD, but that is also fine, people will just go along.

    For me, when the topic frequency is right with the paxs, I can also make them very happy, in return, I feel less stress and tire, as good conversation alway helps to keep me awake and somehow, long journey become short when we are in conversation.

    A good humerous conversation will alway leave a long trail of smile our face, I think all of you would had experienced it, you get a good guy talking happily with you, even after both had parted, you just smile along driving, and accordingly to mind happiness, the longer that smile hang on in your face, the more jovious and happy is the encounter.
