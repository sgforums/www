    Sincerely, do your research carefully before committing on a long contract.  As you mentioned, you're in debt now.  Do you have the spare money ($3,000 at least) to hand over to Prime?  Secondly, although low rental, Fit runs on petrol.  So, higher cost where fuel is concerned.  Thirdly, I seem to read in the forum that Prime do not allow relief for their vehicles.  (Maybe I read wrongly, but that's the impression I get.)  So, if you are only driving at night, then what happens to the other hours?  Park vehicle in carpark? Then, wouldn't it seems like you paying rental for vehicle to park in carpark most of the time?

    Anyway, you know why you can't get only Friday and Saturday nights ony?  These 2 nights are supposedly easier to earn money than the rest of the week.  So, what you think?

    My suggestion is since you're in debt (me too), avoid huge outgoing cash, avoid commitment to long contracts, maximize your income.  One such example is I read in the forum... Lot's of taxi drivers take off on Sundays or at least drive on Sundays only to cover rental.  Hence, if you offer to drive full day for Sunday, market rate is about $20 lower than regular rental.  Lower rental + cheaper diesel.
