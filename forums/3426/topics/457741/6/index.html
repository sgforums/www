    Did u loose ur Passport or IC? For lost or new passport application u don't need to surrender ur IC. If u lost ur IC, ur new IC can be ready in 3 working days. Only holders of Singapore Citizens are allowed to hold a TDVL. That is why whiever company signs u up for the TDVL course needs to verify if u are Singapore Citizen. So if u lost ur IC it should be ready by next week.

    Here is what experienced when I applied for my TDVL. I went to CDG(Comfort) in Sin Ming in 2nd July, I was given a brochure for their TDVL course. Was told by CDG that their course will start in 27 August for full time course and sometime in September for the part time course. Details of what is in CDG brochure u should go and find out yourself cause it may not be the same anymore. I felt that start date for course was too far away so I went to SMRT, SMRT has 3 location where u can sign up which are Kallang MRT, Novena MRT and Woodsland SMRT Depot. I think the SMRT sign up booth is opened till 9pm. I went to the Novena MRT SMRT booth on the 3rd July and my full time course starts in 5th July. These are the only 2 companies u can apply for the TDVL course, the other place is STA(Singapore Taxi Academy) but u may not be able to recover ur TDVL course fees if u apply wt STA. u can call SMRT sign up centers and ask if they will accept ur temporary IC. U can find them in the Internet, this is where I found most of mine. You should make an effort find out information before u ask or else u malu ur degree. Cheers and best of luck to u and ur colleague.
