    Martingale will work for short term only. Small wins but if you lose 6-7 hands in a row, you are dead. Look at Baccarat, ask yourself how many times you have seen Yi Tiao Long of 5-6 times?? The longest i have seen is 17 times open Banker. So losing 5-8 times in a row is very very possible. Low return High risk, that is the Martingale system. If you want to know more about betting systems and why they don't work go to http://wizardofodds.com/gambling/betting-systems.html

    I also have spreadsheets of RWS actual baccarat games for 2 days and applying various betting strategies, 90% of the time is you start winning but in the end you loose. Dare place a bet of $51,200 to win a $50 bet, that is the next bet after you loose 10 bets in a row starting from $50 only. If you win the next hand of $51,200, you really only won $50 cause $51,150 was to recover all the losing bets in a row.
