    Maybe as a beginner, I think got beginner luck. Got a few good connection, Pick-up pax at CBD then to Jurong west then to Bukit Batok. Then thinking how to go back to town. (Dunno got chance anot). Since at CCK central, then turn to MRT to try my luck. Just before turning into MRT, saw 1 pax flag n I pick-up. (Yes, my 1st midnight pax). Zroom my way to Sengkang. My biggest collection for the night.

    Overall, I am satisfied with my 1st night. Hit my target. (my target very low one). Driving from 6pm to 2am, covered 259km. Now I understand, what is body cannot take it. 1st night only body ache liao.
