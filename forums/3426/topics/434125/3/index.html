    For discussion sake, (not to offend any),

    can the authorities do this ->>

    1) for personal matters such as meals/toilets, TDs choose an option that put the fare meter in "suspend" mood, meaning the display on top of taxi is off/blacked out. And this "suspend" mood can only be done for 2hrs max. And this can't be activate 2hr before midnight, unless #condition4.

    2) when TDs get confirmed orders, the display on top will automatically go to "On Call". Once the fare meter is off, the display will revert. TDs can't change this option.

    3) Once fare meter is running, the display on top is changed to "busy". TDs can't change this option.

    4) For leaves/breakdowns, TDs have to input time/date into monitor to relay to HQ to put the fare meter into "sleeping" mode. And display on top will be "off".

    will this helps?
