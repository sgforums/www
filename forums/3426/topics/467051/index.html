    As Poolman had said, Traffic cameras r focused on the 1st-2nd or 3rd left lanes heading straight. I was once "caught on camera" back in 2006 @ Tampines Ave 7 towards Pasir Ris and Loyang Ave. As the traffic turned red, i filtered from 2nd lane into 3rd lane to make a turn (right) into TPE (Towards PIE-ECP). Due to the sudden change of lanes, my tyres touches the wires at 2nd left lane (going straight only) and it activated the camera to flash. A TP letter came within 14 days mentioned that i have "beat" the red light. (but actually, i only turned right, which have a green arrow). I appealed sucessfully and was given a warning for changing lanes at the last minute. Haha, better than 12 demerit points and $200 fine right? Hence, traffic cameras r only for cars beating the red lights.... However now, with so many kinds of traffic lights cameras at junctions, they may have changed their stand.
