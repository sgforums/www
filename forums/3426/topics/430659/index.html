    The test paper is the same for  STA, Comfort and SMRT.Currently there is 3 type of courses available as below->

    3.Online cum Classroom

    Course Duration:3 hrs orientation for online with password issue,38 hrs classroom session for 3 subjects.

    Name & Location of Building--18 hrs

    Routes & Landmarks--15 hrs

    Integrated Training--5 hrs

    Above is the type of course at my time at April'2011

    Hi..

    Just went down to Comfort to register for the course last friday, so just to share on what I've got :)

    Basically as what Firebee had mentioned. (I've only know for comfort, as I only check on them).

    1.Full Time Course(Express)

    Course Duration: 16 days; Mon ~ Sat; 3pm ~ 10pm

    2.Part Time Course

    Course Duration: 30 sessions; 6 Weeks; Mon ~ Fri; 7pm ~ 10pm

    3. Classroom & E-Learn

    Course Duration:

    i) Classroom: 10 sessions; 2 weeks; Mon ~ Fri; 7pm ~ 10pm

    Plus one Sun session10am ~ 1pm, 2pm ~ 7pm (intensive session?!)

    ii) On-line Training: Password valid for 2 weeks with unlimited hours of usage at own pace.

    I choose option 3 and the start date for the course is now in Sept.

    Hope this is useful :)
