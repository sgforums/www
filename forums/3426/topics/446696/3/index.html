    i think basically you sit now and have a coffee with your new relief and talk about common understanding and both party expectation for certain things about the partnership on the driving

    1. handling over of taxi - time

    2. washing vehicle - don't say such thing like not dirty no need to wash, just put as who role to do it daily

    3. sick/holiday - whether to cover half day rental or at a certain rate for both party

    ---- sick half day but try not last min

    ---- holiday up to few days

    note.. relief can claim and pay his rental to you (no need another rate)

    4. toping up fuel, what level is ok

    5. dented or damage to the taxi (own damage)  relief to pay full rental ?

    6. accident -  what to do

    7. any unforseen matter?

    lots of term are market practise, but i doubt anyone put black and white

    there is few post on this matter and many lau jiao has also given their advise

    take this as a guide while chit chatting with new relief.
