    He asked me CNG issues .

    I reply here .

    CNG u must produce a TransCab letter to purchase a card at Toh Tuck .

    Then u can enjoy the 7% discount and the tax free from TransCab .

    Hirers cannot otang more than consecutive amount of $470 per giro payment , or ur $400 of the $665 will be bye bye .

    Left about $225 they will pay u .

    1.8L Wish u max can get 240km per full tank . If lucky , u get a overhaul one , which can reach 260km for some .

    Every CNG taxi must have some petrol inside your petrol tank , for charging of CNG and for starting of the car .

    Every week i use about maybe 5L of petrol for my 2.0L Wish

    Now new strategies , CNG once finish , petrol all the way , no need run all the way to Toh Tuck . Petrol if got Shell card , cheap cheap .

    For myself , Pump No 15 and Pump No 9 is the 2 best pumps in Toh Tuck .

    This two pumps can pump my Wish up to 23kg .

    Which mean i can run up to 300km for most times using No 15 and No 9 .

    Normal default pressure for most pumps are at 20.75kg for most pumps . Some even less 19- 20kg stopped pumping .

    Which means u can go up to max 240 - 260km .

    As for TransCab , u will get more calls if u contribute more credit from payless .

    The more card payment jobs u do , the more they will give you call .
