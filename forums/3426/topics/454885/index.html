    SINGAPORE: Prime Taxi, one of seven cab operators in Singapore, has launched the Prime Royal Limousine Vellfire which runs on environmentally friendly hybrid technology.

    Currently, there are two such taxis and their numbers will increase to 30 by the end of the year.

    Prime Taxi said it would only roll out hybrid models as it wants to support Singapore's green efforts and gradually phase out its CNG taxis.

    There are under 100 CNG taxis in its fleet of about 700 taxis.
