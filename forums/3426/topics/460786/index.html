    reported in CNA : Revision of ERP rates for six gantries during morning peak hours
    Posted: 29 October 2012 1552 hrs 

    SINGAPORE: The Electronic Road Pricing (ERP) rates for six gantries leading to the CTE and ECP will be revised from 5 November.

    Four of the affected gantries are located on the southbound CTE after Braddell Road. From 7.30am to 800am, the ERP charge will go up by $1 to $4.

    Two other affected gantries are located at Fort Road leading to the ECP and the KPE slip road onto the ECP. From 8.30am to 9am, the ERP charge will go up by $1 to $5.

    The rates for the other gantries remain unchanged.

    The Land Transport Authority said the increase comes as it has completed its quarterly review of traffic conditions on ERP-priced roads and expressways    .
