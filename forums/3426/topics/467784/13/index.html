    For iphone Users

    Iphone users should know by now that when u go on air and someone else does at the same time , you will get cut off.

    So to solve this problem , you need to toggle between the Voice Play/Silent function.

    When u wanna tcss or go on air , go to settings/group notifications/ SILENT , this setting will allow u to say your piece without getting cut off. Drawback here is auto play function will be disabled.To enable auto play again , set it back to PLAY.

    What i do is if i wanna go on air , i set to SILENT , if i dont wish to say anything and just wanna listen , i set to PLAY.

    To efficienly make use of an iphone to palingo , set your key lock to NEVER , hang it on a phone holder , hook it to a bluetooth headset. However  This will drain your battery in double quick time so be sure to have a car charger with you.

    Hope this helps.. cheers
