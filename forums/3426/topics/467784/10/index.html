    Sunday night went to toa payoh Blazing handphone shop to get a a2dp headset for my iphone and walkie. Decided on the jabra Clipper II , nabey went home cannot pair the 2 devices.

    Went back to toa payoh on monday , the guy say will replace a new one for me , ask me come back on tuesday , so tuesday went back , nabey even new set also cannot pair, best part is my iphone and the shop staff's iphone both also cannot pair.

    Look at the box clearly state ' multi point' knn, so the boss say change another one , now change to sony ericcsson MW-600 , same problem!! It was past the shop's closing time and the boss decided to refund me my $. This shop service sibeh good and sibeh steady , highly recommended la.

    Then this evening i decided to go i-tec showroom at machpherson to check out the voiceclip 609S, i told the staff there i will only buy if my iphone and walkie can be paired.

    Amazingly , the 609S can pair . so i bought it @ $71.

    Morale of the story - When u shop for a2dp headsets , dont assume that the box says multipoint means confirm can pair, i assumed it would and i was made to run up and down over a pair of headset.

    So if u are considering to buy , make sure on the spot ask the staff to pair 2 devices for you.
