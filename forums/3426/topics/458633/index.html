    If u deposit $1000 can only owe 5 days, $1500 can owe 8 days. Must complete 1 calendar month then can get incentive. Example if start on 7th July, for month of July never get cos not 1 calendar month. So 1st August till 31st August then get.

    1st month those never get like me is because i deposit $1000, they deduct $500 from the $665 rebate so left $115, that $115 they offset from rental. That also the girl got tell me, only i never understand at first.

    For me my daily rental paid thru giro so won't miss one, cos my bank always got cash.

    Those pay monthly they get $60 extra, meaning $665+$60 = $725, so end month when they pay 1 month rental they offset $725.

    Example:

    Daily rental $94.16

    Whole month: $94.16 x 31 = $2918.96

    Total Rebate : $665 + $60 = $725

    Total amt to be paid end month: $2918.96 - $725 = $2193.96

    The incentive breakdown for Wish 1.8 As stated in the yellow file when u sign for car:

    Performance incentive: $250 - Applicable to rental owing More than 5 days but less than 7 Days.

    Good Performace incentive: $400 - Applicable to rental not owing more than 5 days

    Road tax rebate: $15

    So if u made $1500 deposit and miss 1-5 days rental:

    $250 + $400 + $15 = $665

    If u miss rental 6 - 7 days u get:

    $250 + $15 = $265

    If u miss rental above 7 days ie, 8 days u get

    $0

    Above 9 days rental missed TC can take legal actions against you.

    Maximum number of days can owe rental based on the amount of deposit paid, taken from the yellow file:

    $300 deposit - Owe max 3 days rental

    $500 deposit - Owe max 5 days rental

    $1000 deposit - Owe max 7 days rental

    $1500 deposit - Owe max 9 days rental

    If u made $1000 deposit like me then first full calendar month rebate they deduct $500 and credit into the security deposit, and balance offset from rental.

        • then and in any such case, the Hirer shall pay the full cost or restoring or replacing the Taxi and/or the full amount of the third party claims, as the case may be, and shall indemnify the Owner, in full for all other losses, costs and expenses which may be incurred by the Owner.
