    The policeman then called you and asked you to turn up at the police station to collect the fare.

    You have managed to collect the fare but what about the time spent to make the report and to return to collect the fare especially the fare is a few dollars.

    Of course, as a matter of principle and to shame the passenger, you can choose to do so.

    It is to be noted that taxi driver has no legal right to demand and retain the IC or credit card even if the passenger does not have the money to pay the fare. The passenger is not legally bound to hand over the IC or credit card or handphone to the taxi driver even if the passenger does not have the money to pay for the fare.

    If the taxi driver resorts to threat or physically snatch these items from the passenger, the taxi driver can be charged in court.

    So, currently it seems that the taxi driver is always at the losing end if the passenger does not have the money to pay for the fare.
