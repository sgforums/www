    really out of topic, but cannot resist sharing this with you guys.

    I stay in Bt Panjang and my MP is Dr Teo Ho Pin.

    My wife had been pestering me to write to Dr Teo asking him to give us a taxi stop. We stay at Blk 211, the block just next to the BKE where vehicle just enter Bt Panjang from the BKE.

    Most of the time, taxi dun stop there as it's quite dangerous due to fast moving traffic and we need to walk almost to the bus stop to get a cab.

    Just to save on booking charges, she will have to walk almost 200 m with her senile mother,  a very challenging task.

    As such, she wanted to request Dr Teo to carve out a taxi stop just after the BKE turn in.

    I wrote the email at 12:00 and guess what? Dr Teo replied me at 15:00, just 3 hours on a Sunday and cc a copy to his assistant who replied that he had "propose a location for LTA's approval" just an hour later. Al these happens on a Sunday afternoon. Very different from what we know must submit in black and white and wait for 7 working days type, haha and of course approval from higher authority.

    This not the first time Dr Teo help us. Just last month, I wrote to him about at least 15 overdue summon in HDB car park fine which had grow from $50 each to average $300 each and some of them pending court case. He had managed to help me waived off some and reduce the rest to just the original amount and even allow me to go on installment.

    another instant was some 3 years ago when I just bought a new bicycle but we dun have a bicycle park in my block and just 3 days after the email, the parking lot was built.

    This type of MP, how to complain right?
