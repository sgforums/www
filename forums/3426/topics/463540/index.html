    Auxiliary Police officers will be stationed at all affected road junctions to regulate traffic and assist motorists.  Traffic advisory signs and electronic Variable Message Signs will also be put up near the affected roads. Motorists are encouraged to tune in to the radio for the latest traffic updates. 

    Parking at peripheral roads and stopping of vehicles along the ECP (Benjamin Sheares Bridge) to watch the fireworks is strictly prohibited.  Enforcement action will be taken against errant motorists who do so. Vehicles which are parked causing obstruction will be towed away.

    Those proceeding to Marina Barrage are advised to use public transport or the shuttle services provided by the event organiser.

    Bus inspectors will be stationed at selected locations to advise commuters on the public bus services affected by road closures.  Information signs will also be placed at the affected bus stops.

    As heavy traffic and delays are expected, the understanding and cooperation from the public is much appreciated.

    For enquires, the public may contact the event hotline at 6828 8377 or visit the Marina Bay Singapore Countdown 2013 website at www.marinabaycountdown.sg.
