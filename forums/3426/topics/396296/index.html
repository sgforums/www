    Picked up an Ang Mor family of four near Orchard. Headed to Sentosa.

    I kept to myself mostly along the way because I thought they look frustrated by the weather. I just turned the air con up. One of the little daughters commented this cab is really tidy and smelled nice, the mom and dad chuckled.

    When we got nearer, I prompted the Father about the possible charges into Sentosa since they didnt have any other pass, he was very glad I did so. He said he usually expected drivers to keep dead silent til they dropped bombshells at the end of the journey. I told him I couldnt stand surprises personally so I thought maybe my passengers wouldnt enjoy it either. Further that they were just here.

    When they got off (another 2 new passengers already self boarded at the back of the seat), the Father who was sitted in front paid me the meter, AND then he kept ruffling for other notes. I said its fine, but he said he must tip me..the 2 passengers behind, also Ang Mors and I was like !?..then that Father dug up a Red and pass it to when the meter (alrdy paid) was just slightly over 10 total.

    Thats not the end of the story. The 2 Ang Mor ladies who climbed in by themselves at the back also tell me to keep the change when I reached their place. Meter was under 12 i think, but they gave me 2 Reds.

    hmm.
