  • CharlesTD

    I am driving a SMRT Epica and yesterday my Meter hang and could not be taken out of Hired mode. I went to SMRT service this morning and overheard another SMRT TD having same problem. The SMRT technician taught him over the phone how to resolve the problem. The SMRT meter has the ability to be put into "Fix Fare" mode so when u ferry ur family or friend and do not want to charge them u can put $0.01. It seems that sometimes the meter hangs when in that mode, this is my 1st time after 3.5 mths so it is not too often. So the following steps is how to resolve it:

     

    There are 2 Red wires with fuse holders connected to the battery. Open the fuse cover and there should be a 5amp and 7.5amp fuse. Pull out 7.5amp fuse for 10 secs and put back fuse. The meter should have reset in "For Hired". The other 5amp fuse is for the ERP device, the tech told me if ERP device no power then check the fuse. Cause the fuse sometimes gets loose. He say this are 2 common problems that can fix by urself.
