    If for CDG , just go to their Portal and find one relief .

    Just click whatever they need you to click , and just wait for phone calls .

    Upon confirmation , bring the new partner to Sin Ming Drive , and sign whatever that is needed to sign .

    Along the way , talk about those conditions that good for everyone .

    1. Pay rental promptly .

    2.Responsibility of day driver of sending vehicle for servicing

    3.Responsibilty of night driver to wash cab every one or 2 days .

    4.Pump CNG or diesel to a standard practise . How to get full tank .

    And some u just wanna be sure .

  • Poolman
    13 Apr 11, 04:16

    For most company , like Comfort , all hirers need to bring their relief to go down to HQ and sign up .

    Inform relief Security Deposit is needed , be it by cash or by instalment .

    Prepare a photo for TransCab , for CDG , relief just need to take a Photo from Sin Ming .

    Pls remember to bring along a UOB endorsement letter for giro payment for Comfort .

    TransCab no need , TransCab just need a ATM card .
