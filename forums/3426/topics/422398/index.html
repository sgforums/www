    You would need a walkie talkie for this, and u subscribe Grid to use the talkie, grid is just like a company that provide the usage of the talkie....just similiar as u pay ur handphone bills.

    Usually an advice to newbie, who are not well verse with roads would not want to take bookings. It is a very competitive world rushing against time.

    I think the talkie booking, when the job is offered, u have to press the talkie fast enough to get the booking and give ur eta.

    If another member said on the spot(eta=0min), then he will get the job even u pressed earlier.
