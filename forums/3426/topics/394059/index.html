    I actually cannot bear to see new drivers straight jump into OMO bandwagons .

    I still believe , every step must be gradual .....

    From crawlings , standing , walking , running etc .... In order to built up that self belief in yourself .

    I learnt from sweeping floors in town ... then learnt to sweep trying to find some at HDB , along the way , think of when and why they take taxis , get a bit of understanding can be useful in taxi driving .

    Then up a level to use bookings , and always find more about other MDT . Power likewise etc ....

    Then up another level into Jurong Industrial areas like Tuas .

    Then slowly to Loyangs , Changi , Pandan , Penjuru etc .... my network of call taking . So when and where need calls , it's in my finger tips .

    Keep thinking of ways to up another level .

    Slowly move up more levels up into Shenton Ways etc .....

    Then everyday improving my income .

    Always wanting to reach aother summit .

    Then slowly up another level , to master Airport .

    Then walkie talkie etc .....

     

    This process took me 9 years .
