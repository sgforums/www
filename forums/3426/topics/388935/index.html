    Hi Rover, charges for taxi into sentosa as below:

    Wkdays 0700 - 1159hrs: $5                              

                   1200 - 1400hrs: $2 

                    1401 - 1900hrs: $5                                                                   

                    1901 - 0659hrs: $2

     

    Wkends & PH 0700 - 1900hrs: $6

                              1901 - 0659hrs: $3

    Charges for per taxi only,regardless of no. of pax

  • Teolk
    23 Jan 10, 20:10

    If your pax has a pass,letter or any other documents to allow free entry,just show it to the counter staff. A beep will still be heard at the iu but don't worry the cashcard will not be deducted.

    if pax has to pay for admission charge, the $2-$6 will be auto deducted from your cashcard. then when you press OP to stop the meter,the amount will be shown in the "extra" screen (exactly like how we pass thru erp gantry & collect $$$ from pax)

    Remember it is charged according to per taxi. we don't have to tell them how many pax we have on-board.

    If u are going in empty for on-call,to queue for pax or for my case go in underwater world hahaha, it's FREE! again you will also get a beep in the iu
