  • f1taxidriver
    17 Apr 10, 10:37

    The most important mantra as a TD is this:

    Your job is to find paxs who are going to the same place as u. You must interview the pax. And for taking calls, the destination must be listed.

    The only time when it is safe to pick and go is when the road traffic is going to be light, and we all know it only happens after midnight, when street hires are almost non-existent.

    When the TD does not bother to ask for destinations, it is a sign of very bad business. The walkie is silent, the MDT is silent, all taxi stands are packed with a long q of taxis, and almost no street paxes. When this happens, any taiko destination also grab lor.
