    As an aviation enthusiast, just to help you all...

     

    Did some analysis... Over the last few weeks between T2 and T3 for SQ and Silkair flights if you wanna hunt up some good incoming ones...

     

     

     

    AM Arrival Peak
    (5a.m to 8a.m)
    ››› T3 mostly, T2 very little


    PM Arrival
    (12p.m to 6p.m)
    ››› Evenly spread out between T2 and T3


    Evening Arrival Peak
    (8p.m to 10p.m)
    ››› Evenly spread out between T2 and T3. T2 mainly Silkair, T3 mainly SQ regional.


    Night Arrival
    (10p.m to 11.59p.m)
    ››› T2 mainly Silkair and SQ regional for the end of the working day.
    ››› T2 is a ghost town after midnight. DON'T FETCH FROM THERE.

     

     

    Just my two cents worth.

    Of course, the best time arrivals for T1 is when the Qantas, British Airways, Finnair, Cathay Pacific, Air France, and Japan Airlines drop their huge sai at the 5p.m to 7p.m time frame, but I'm sure you all know that by now...
