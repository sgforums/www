    Go to any industrial building with printing companies and get cheaper factory rates. The higher the quantity you print ie. 500 or 1000, the lower is the cost/card.

So if you order more the price won't goes up too much as compared to low quantities. The printing press runs '000 of cards in an hour and the time to setup the press is longer than the actual printing of the card.
