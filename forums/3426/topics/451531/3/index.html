    GPS

    Actually, Singapore is very small and doesn't really need GPS if one is always on the road. However, I have not drive for nearly 10 years, so I might need some time to get used to it. 

    I don't want to lose a passenger because I do not know how to get there. I don't want to be get scolded or complained.

    I just bought Garmin 2565 at Funan for S$279 (member price). Come with 5" carrying case and 1 free LED touch worth S$12. 

    Car Camera

    I don't want to wait for taxi company to install for me. God knows when they will install. I want it there the first day I drive, just in case I need it as prove.

    There are many available online. gmarket, ebay, aliexpress, blah blah blah. Can easily get 1 for only S$35.

    Name Card

    I know some TD drivers do not carry name card. But, when passenger like my service and want to become my regulars, I make sure I have my name card ready for them. Beside driving, I also have other business which they might be interested.

    1 name card is usually less than S$0.10, but can also be free.
