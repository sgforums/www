    Too fast, I'm furious: Cabby races from Marina Bay Sands to Pasir Ris in only 13min

    STOMPer Lean feared for his wife and himself as the cab driver of his cab sped at 150km/h on the KPE, making the 19km route from Marina Bay Sands to Pasir Ris in only 13 minutes.

    Google Maps states the distance from Marina Bay Sands to Pasir Ris via the Kallang Paya Lebar Expressway as 21km, which should take an average of 21 minutes.

    According to the receipt provided by STOMPer Lean, he was picked up by the cab at 12.06am, and reached his destination at 12.19am, after travelling 18.5km.

    The STOMPer recounts:

    "My wife and I took a cab from MBS and arrived at Pasir Ris in a whooping 13 minutes!

    "You can check my receipt for verification.

    "Well, we had a ride of our lifetime.

    "The cabby drove at almost 150kph inside the KPE tunnel, only slowing down twice to 80kph as we approached the speed camera.

    "Why is this kind of dangerous driving allowed?"
