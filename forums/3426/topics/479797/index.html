    Google Maps as mentioned here is a cheap alternative, but if you're looking for something better, Singapore Map by street directory on Google Play is better since the publishers are mainly in Singapore.

    But if you're looking for GPS systems, some taxis come with them. If you're driving with Comfort or Citycab(now both known as ComfortDelgro Taxis), knowing some parts of the road would be enough. But their GPS system inside is shitty, they provide you with a freaking dot and a straight line to your destination.

    You could check Garmin's website, they're quite renowned for their Satellite navigation systems. Here. There are no pricings on their site as you have to go to authorised retailers to buy them.

    But ultimately, Sim Lim Square is the best option.

    Couple of solutions to choose from.
