    Whether to stop or drive on if a motorist met with an accident with a dog depends on the situtaion. I think its safer to first stop, access the situation, then decide whether to drive on. Pay attention to things like the location, onlookers, type of dog(pet/stray), etc. because the motorist can get into trouble if somebody were to pursue the matter.

    According to Road Traffic Act, when a motorist had an accident with a dog(both pet and stray) its actually the driver's duty to stop and to render assistance.

    Failure to comply if convicted faces a fine not exceeding $3,000 or to imprisonment for a term not exceeding 12 months.
