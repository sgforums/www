    Maybe you are new to the world of driving. Read the insurance manual on what to do when there is an accident.

    It does not matter whether the other party says OK or not. Get particulars, take pictures. If no give particulars, ask one more time to confirm he does not want to give particulars (so that you can tell the court you ask him one more time). Make report to Insurance company to cover your backside.

    If want to settle out of court, you need to get a written, sign note to say there will be no action taken. Both parties sign. Sometimes the amount of compensation is also stated in this note.

    It is obvious you did not do all this, and if the Insurance company wash hands, you may have to bear the full compensation to the other party.

    The only thing you can do is quickly go and find out from the Insurance company, what are your options. They may, or may not be able to help you in some ways.
