    A new fleet of SMRT taxis will hit the road soon

    to offer better comfort to passengers

    1. The public will witness a new sight on the streets on Tuesday (March 15), as SMRT

    Taxis rolls out its latest taxi – the Chevrolet Epica.

    2. Passengers riding in the new maroon taxis will be seated in luxury and comfort, in a

    quieter interior with on-board magazines to accompany their trip. As an introductory

    promotion, 35,000 tissue packs will be given out to passengers, while stock lasts.

    3. The taxi windows are all lined with a solar film to keep the sun and heat out, while the rear side windows and windscreen have been tinted to lessen the glare.

    4. Other features of the vehicle include automatic headlights, rain-sensing wipers, an

    anti-lock braking system (ABS) and a water-resistant floorboard.

    SMRT Taxis’ fleet to be increased and improved with the Chevrolet Epica

    5. The Chevrolet Epica taxi is in line with the company’s plans to increase its fleet to

    meet rising demand. More than that, however, SMRT Taxis aims to do this by

    enhancing the passengers’ travel experience.

    6. Flag-down rate for the new Chevrolet Epica taxi will be at $3.20. Booking fees will be as per current booking fees for standard taxis – $3.50 per trip from 7 to 9.30am and 5 to 11pm on Mondays to Fridays; and $2.50 per trip at all other times, including

    Saturdays, Sundays and public holidays. Advance booking fee remains at $5.20 per

    trip.

    7. “This new fleet of taxis will give passengers a smoother and more comfortable ride

    with its better comfort, performance and reliability,” said Ms Teo Chew Hoon,

    Executive Vice-President, Commercial and Roads. “The move to improve our

    vehicles is just one of the ways SMRT Taxis is realising its commitment to serve its

    customers better”
