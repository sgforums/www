    Who will get arrested first and why?

    Ah Beng - bo bey chow will get arrested anywhere from 2 weeks to 1 month after starting operations, Ah Seng might be be able to last months and even years (yes, I know some football bookies who still are operating from cofee-shops).

    Football betting is so widespread and Singapore Pools doesn't cover all games, and their odds are not industry standard, they don't have a monopoly, therefore no immediate pressure.

    Same for taxi - LV services do not compete with CDG and another companies, they are offering an extra or a premium, it does not eat into the  regular client pool/pax that other taxi companies are offering, therefore a) it's not illegal and b) it's a service that only certain types of pax prefer or want - hence they have to be allowed to operate.
