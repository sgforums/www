    CNY no need to pressure yourself so much, everywhere got passenger except during certain times like 2nd day morning. 1st day also got, many people go to temple.

    This is the best time of the year for drivers. Roads are empty, no jam, so don't go rushing and pressure yourself. HDB estates, even private ones, also can find pax. No need to rush back to town, just go at an easy pace and you'll get a fare OTR or via call booking.

    Be helpful and tolerant, no need to get angry, New Year time must be calm and set good omen for the year. Even though I not Chinese, I still treat it like a New Year for me - it's still the Lunar NY. 

    Of course, if very drunk pax, then as Malay say - pakai akal. Other than that, just enjoy 3-4 days of easy relaxful driving, you won't be getting such days for long time again, make sure you make the most of it (not necessarily financial) and when the normal routine returns, you can say, you enjoyed your driving during this period.
