    Concrete debris damages vehicles near mall
    by Wong Wei Han
    Updated 10:50 PM Nov 18, 2012

    SINGAPORE - Concrete debris falling down from a construction site along Somerset road damaged as many as 6 taxis that were queuing to pick up passengers at the 313@somerset shopping mall.

    TODAY understands that no one was injured.

    According to Mr Chanchan, a site supervisor, the accident happened at about 8.20pm when a concrete formwork at the 15th floor came apart, allowing the concrete it held to come raining down on the vehicles below.

    Drivers of the damaged taxis told TODAY that the debris fell for at least one minute, and some of the bigger debris were fist size. Windshields and windows of the taxis were shattered.

    Affected taxi drivers are in negotiations regarding compensation.
