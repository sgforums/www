    You are right about the mercedes fuel economy. It is definitely better to drive a mercedes than a sonata. Personally, I would go further and say that it is better to drive a smrt / transcab mercedes compared to the comfort mercedes. The F3 plus taximeter used by smrt and transcab gives the TD more 'control' over the fare. Eg private limo jobs where the fare has been pre-agreed. Furthermore u got the $3 MBS surcharge on sat and sun which comfort dun have. Lastly, it is very strange but I find that the F3 taxi meter jumps faster compared to the F2 taximeter used by comfort. Maybe the correct way to say is that the F3 meter measures distances with greater precision, so perhaps the comfort F2 taxi meter is underclocking the real distance covered and as a result the cabby is collecting less than what they ought to. This was my personal experience. I made more driving a smrt nissan cedric compared to a citycab sonata.

    The comfort limo and normal MDT bookings are really of little use in terms of improving your daily takings. If the location and timing works out, great. But most of the time, the timing, location and fare is not worth the trouble at all. I swept 99% of the time with a comfort mercedes. The only bookings that I bothered taking at all was to try my best to secure an early morning departure for my first trip the next morning, during the evening time. But this works out only if u OMO.
