    Hi everyone, Had been awhile since I logged in. Thanks bro poolman for introducing his friends for me to take over this wish for 10 days. Today the wish went dead on terminal 1 and therefore now writing here on my iPhone while waiting for the cab to be repair. I would like to share my thoughts with everyone and especially the newbie wannabe. There are sure money to be made driving taxi but it will never be any professional first choice of job. Super relief is very great in demand and you pay lesser rental and most important is no commitment. And here why driving should not be the first choice of people with certs; 1. You will be starting your day at 6am to start picking people up. Failure to do so will result missing out on the golden period. 2. You will end your day after 10pm. That mean that you will not be able to eat dinner and spend quality time with your family. 3. You will feel very demoralize as you can see other professional boarding your cab and realize that you miss the feeling being able to knock off after 5 or 6. 4. You can earn the same amount with lesser time. And there is a limit on how much. 5. You will start your meal at a very different time. Resulting in some health problem if this were to continue. 6. Your brain will start to deteriorate as driving a cab is all about experience and discipline. Of the above, I think the main reason why you should consider cab driving when you are jobsless is that; Your children But I think most of the fellow drivers are very friendly and helpful. Did make a lot of friends here though...
