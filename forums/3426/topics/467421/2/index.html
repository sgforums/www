    I;ve had eczema since 13 years old and now I'm 50. Seen so many skin specialists and only one managed to help me control my flare ups. Like most of you have mentioned here, there is no cure for eczema. Gradually, as you get older some of us may get better. It's hereditary and you'll either develop sinus, asthma or eczema. Stress, heat, sweating, etc will aggravate the eczema. If serious, then ingesting steroids in pills form will help to ease the itch, but not recommended for long term usage. Cortiseroid creams are usually prescribed for application on affected area but use sparingly as it will thin the skin. The only good thing about my eczema is that it got me downgraded during NS.
