    SMRT TAXIS TO REVISE LOCATION SURCHARGE FOR MARINA BAY SANDS FROM 21 FEBRUARY 2011

    1. From 21 February 2011, SMRT Taxis will remove the $3 per trip location surcharge
    for trips originating from Marina Bay Sands between 1700 hours and midnight on
    Monday to Saturday.

    2. The removal of the location surcharge is done in response to LTA’s adjustment of the
    ERP cordon to include the Marina Bay area as part of the Central Business District.

    3. The $3 per trip location surcharge from 0600 hours to midnight on Sundays and
    Public Holidays is still applicable.
