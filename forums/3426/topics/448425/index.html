    Yes, definitely can.

    But must consider what Mr Khoon has said. Also will be difficult for you to get out of the queue and fetch the pax, say road obstruction etc? And what abt the possibility of the pax hopping into another cab? If queue for pax isn't long, that's a high possibility. If demand exceeds supply, then should be alright.

    Also must consider when these type of calls, could be a lot of barang barang. This is like a lottery, sometimes staying in the queue is better sometimes taking these calls are worth it. Last Friday I was No 3 at American school slow moving queue, suddenly got call. Really think hard, but decided to take the risk, pax did come out and take my cab, some more go Orchard Hotel, peak period, trip to CBD.

    Some call that a durian, but I rate my trips after horses, so that's an IG 1 (International Group 1) type of fare.
