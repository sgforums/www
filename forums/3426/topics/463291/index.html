    Answer to Question 1:

    No Fixed Rule... Muz pre arrange with Hirer...

    Some Practices as Follows:

    1. RELIEF Pay Full Rental Every Day - including Off Days... Regradless of driving or not driving... the cab will still be at your disposal during your shift hours since u are paying rental...

    2. RELIEF NO Pay Rental on Off Days - you will not be able to use the cab in tis case... as you never pay rental...

    3. RELIEF and HIRER Cover each other on Off Days - U OMO on the day your hirer is off... He OMO on your off day... the one OMO on tat day take care of the Entire Day's rental..
