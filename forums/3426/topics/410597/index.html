    I read so many of TD's encounters with bad passengers who accuse TD for driving longer routes. But personally, I met a TD who really go the longer route even after I told him I'll guide him to take my route.

    I've been going from my home in Hougang to my company in Tampines central for years via Old Tampines rd (bus 72's route) and it's faster than whatever expressway route (I forgot the name of the expressway, think it's CTE. This expressway route also taken before with a few TD, but always got traffic jam so I prefer Old Tampines rd).

    So whenever I take cab (due to me waking up late sometimes) to company, I'll always ask the TD to go by old tampines rd.

    But once, there's this tmd TD uncle who asked me what route to take, I asked him to go by Old Tampines Rd, he said that road & expressway always jam. I replied I always take bus 72 which also go via Old Tampines Rd but I've never experienced jam. Then he insisted on going by Jalan Kayu Rd then to some expressway. I quickly pointed out that would be a longer route, he said no choice because of jam and just started driving.

    I was shocked but too late to get off already. Never mind, so I let him drive. Even for those TD who took some expressway route, they never have to drive via Jalan Kayu road. This is clearly a rip off! Thoughout the journey, he keep on saying Old Tampines Rd jam.

    Personally, during those times when I woke up on time, and take bus 72 which go to Tampines Central via Old Tampines Rd, the traffic is always normal one. Then I SMSed a colleague who took bus 72 to office, she also said that morning Old Tampines Rd no jam, normal road conditions.

    When nearing Tampines Central (near the temple & dormitory area), that tmd TD went to the HDB flats area in the national library side to took another longer route. I pointed to him again, that he can take the route next to Tampines dormitory (after the temples) to access central (bus 72's route), he said that road cannot take. I was like "What?!"

    Then he go all the way to the road beside Courts/GIANT to the HDB flats near the national library area.

    When reached Tampines Central finally, I paid $27 (my usual is $10.** only!) and I'm still late despite taking a cab. Normally, when I woke up late, I take cab which go by Old Tampines Road, I'll still be on time and paying only $10.**.

    This is clearly a rip off! So, don't blame us pax for being suspicious or agitated when asking about route!
