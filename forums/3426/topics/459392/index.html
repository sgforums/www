    Drove the Crown for the last time yesterday.

    Wow I remember back in those days when it first came out, not bad the model has been around for nearly 20 years.

    Good experience to drive manual after so many years again. But for me no problem, switching from manual to auto, the trick is to always let your left foot rest on the raised platform on the extreme left (where the clutch is supposed to be).

    That platform not for fun, it actually helps in sudden braking as your transfer the pressure to your feet to the left foot and thus your right foot on the brake has lesser pressure - something I learnt from Police driving = less chances of skidding (if your vehicle has no ABS).

    From next week all the way auto - so I guess the era of driving manual has ended for me.
