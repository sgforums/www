    Just a piece of advice....

    1) 4 years cab = abt 9 cents per 1km on CDG pumps.

    2) conditions on cab depends on the previous users lah.... who can tell how problematic the cab before ?

    3) should be allocated 62xx to 63xx plates' cabs (euro4 engines).. 630x is from June-July 2006.  If you get 2xxx or 3xxx (euro3 engines).. better don't take.. high fuel consumption for sure. lots of mechanic problems too.  (the mentioned plates numbers are for Mercedes cabs under Sovereign (ex-Yellow Top Dept, direct Comfort Mercedes).

    4) ex-City Cab Limo (Presido) 4 years cabs = 8xx series. (also euro4 engines).

    5) Flag down at $3.20.

    6) Limo Jobs ? have lah... but see if you lucky or not.... now that there are about 600(approx) Mercedes cabs in Comfort.  Better Go join a Limo Group = Limo2000, Royal or K-Limos...

    7)Limo Counter at Airports (needs lot of patience). Not only CDG mercedes, there are also SMRT, Silver Mercedes, SMART, Dynasty Unions.  Per Trip is $45 (less $7 for airport counter).  Midnite +$10. ( DON'T Waste time there, unless you f**king free and tired ).  Some time waiting time can be easily 2hrs ( for $38 nett, don't worth it ).

    8) Ocasionally, CDG Premier services do have events = like Airshows, Expo events, Swiss Club (Meter+$10) etc.

    9) wear long sleeves white, black pants, leather black shoes.  (if you get Limo jobs - have to be smartly dress).  if you sweep floor... wear watever you like.. nobody bother.  (if you sweep floor.. go drive a sonata).

    10). Must drive with a right attitude.. be it Limo or normal... Limo drivers no big F.

    just my 2 cents of advice.

    good luck.
