    For CDG taxis, there's a small red button on the side floor panel near yr accelerator pedal. if you place yr right foot on the pedal, its just located slightly on the right side, just fit for you to 'press' using yr right foot. 

    step on the button and hold for more than 1 sec then release, step again for more than 1 sec then release, these actions must be done within 10 secs.

    Then yr MDT will pop up a message: "pick up at changi airport".

    press OK if you huv accidentally activated the emergency button.

    DO NOT PRESS OK if you are in danger!
