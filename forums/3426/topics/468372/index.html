    Why high consumption? simple

    Because of aging, piston rings worn, liner bore worn result in fuel slip thru, on the other hand carbon built up at the piston head cover and liner can also result in reduction of space for combustion, resulted lack of power and therefore stepped more on the fuel pedal.

    Of course, the best is to overhaul the engine, but that take time and expensive, overhauling the most is once in a life time for car, overhaul twice, something is very wrong, pte car half overhauling already cost nearly 5k, full about 10 to 12k, parts change not yet included, and if need to change piston rings, pistons and casket, it could amount to more than 15k, that is why, most car at 8 or 9 years, changed, some 5 years changed liao. Engine overhauling is like human going for heart operation, you dun go heart operation often.

    So, those who want to buy 2nd hand car, please look the engine,

    One method is use mineral oil and run the engine on the road for 500 to 1000km, then change it to normal oil, do not do engine flushing which is alway recommend, it will end up more fuel usage.

    The engine is like a human heart, it take time to warm up, you cannot keep jogging it, like some at ap, stop off engine, then on again, it work more hard and in long run, worn out faster, if you flush it, it is like cleaning the drain with chemical, the drain cement also wash away, yes the drain become very smooth for water to flow, and imagine your fuel will flow like water...

    Like a human heart, time to rest, it must rest, best is constant timming, 6 hrs rest per day in between shift.

    And for reverse gear, give time for it to change before reverse, imagine you suddenly turn around, sure to fall, in long run, crankshaft worn faster or even crack, then god bless us all.

    To check engine, on engine for a minute, keep looking at the cooling container, if bubble come out, engine leaking air liao...no good. This is call bubble test, if no overhaul, engine get heated up more easily. High rpm will be needed to run a hot engine, and that translate to more fuel use.
