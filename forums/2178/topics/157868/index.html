    Question: How should I place my faith on any religion? How could I lead a spiritual life?

    Answer: Don't have faith in Buddhism; don't have faith in Christianity, don't have faith in Religion. Have faith in Goodness, in Virtue, in Compassion. That's more important. And by showing goodness, virtue, compassion, you are leading a spiritual life.

    This was from the Q&A session during the talk given by Ven. Ajahn Brahm at Bright Hill Monastery yesterday.
