    Destination Tintin, Tintin Themed Vacations Start with Brussels Week-End in August

    The estate of Hergé, Moulinsart, has partnered with tour operator On the Go to offer under the banner Destination Tintin a number of Tintin themed vacations.

    The adventure starts in August with Brussels 3 Day Week-Ends for a taste of the home of Hergé.

    "This semi-independent three day package allows the flexibility for you to choose the standard of accommodation you desire and the times you wish to arrive and depart and to visit many of the sites at a time that suits you, whilst providing entrance tickets for the Hergé Museum, the Magritte Museum and the services of a knowledgeable guide for an enlightening Tintin & Hergé Walking Tour in Brussels!"

    Which means that except for the guided tour you set your own schedule. Does not include transportation to and from Brussels as for meals only 2 breakfasts (no lunch or dinner). No price set yet, contact On the Go for details...

       

    Other Destination Tintin vacation offerings include Jordan and the Rose City (8 days), Land of the Pharaohs (10 days) and A very Indian Adventure (15 days)...

    Mille Milliards de Mille Sabords, ship me to Brussels...
