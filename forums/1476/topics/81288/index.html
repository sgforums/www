    Last time I took a couch there. think its about 9-10 hrs ride. Recommend you take the VIP bus (i.e. 3 seats in a row) cause it is more spacious. Think it cost around SG$40 single trip. They usually starts at night around 10PM and reach there early in the morning around 6AM. There are frequent stop over to have short breaks and relief your bladder. Suggest you book your return trip from MY cause it is cheaper (they charged around the same dollars but in RM )

    Drove there with my friends once. Also take about the same time cause got to navigate using the map. Recommend to have at least 2 drivers so that you can take turn to drive. One thing about driving there, you have to park the car at a parking lot. CanÂ’t remember the charge but the risk is whether your car will still be there if stolen.
