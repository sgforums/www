    TO the Forum Friends.......

    My wish for you is a man/women who's love is honest, strong, mature,
    never-changing, uplifting, protective, encouraging, rewarding and unselfish.

    So, which ever phase of life u r in, make the most of it.

    To those Who Are........... SINGLE
    Love is like a butterfly. The more you chase it, the more it eludes you.But if you just let it fly, it will come to you when you least expect it.

    Love can make you happy but often it hurts, but love's only special when
    you give it to someone who is really worth it.
    So take your time and choose the best.

    Those Who Are............ PLAYERS TYPE

    Never say "I love you" if you don't care.
    Never talk about feelings if they aren't there.
    Never touch a life if you mean to break a heart.
    Never look in the eyes when all you do is lie.
    The cruellest thing a guy can do to a girl is to let her fall in love
    when he doesn't intend to catch her fall and it works both ways...

    Those Who Are............ MARRIED

    Love is not about "it's your fault", but "I'm sorry"; not "where are you',
    but "I'm right here"; not "how could you", but "I understand"; not "I
    wish you were", but "I'm thankful you are."

    The true measure of compatibility is not the years spent together but how
    good you are for each other.

    Those Who Are............ HEARTBROKEN

    Heartbreaks last as long as you want and cut as deep as you allow them to
    go. The challenge is not how to survive heartbreaks but to learn from
    them.

    A sad thing about life is when you meet someone and fall in love only to
    find out in the end that it was never meant to be and that you have
    wasted years on someone who wasn't worth it.

    If he isn't worth it now he's not going to be worth it a year or 10 years from now.

    Time to ....let go.....

    How to be in love: Fall but don't stumble, be consistent but not too
    persistent, share and never be unfair, understand and try not to demand,
    and get hurt but never keep the pain.
