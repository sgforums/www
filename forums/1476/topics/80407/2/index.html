    I lost about 9 kg in 3 months.

    what I did:

    1) Go exercise every 2 days where possible. Half hour jog at own pace, then another half hour with sit ups, crunches, dumbell training etc.

    2) Nice to do it in a gym, but sometimes run around the neighbourhood reduces the boredom of doing it in a gym.

    3) Determination - this ingredient is crucial.

    4) Watch diet - but must occasionally treat yourself with chocolate or deep fried stuff, say once a month?? Otherwise it will seem routine and unmotivating to exercise. This is also crucial.

    5) Climb stairs up to your place, wah this one very effective.

    6) Keep a positive mind!

    Hope this helps!
