    My toys are all deboxed and place inside display cabinets. Some of them are in boxes so that I can recycle the display when I feel like it.

    My wife's dolls are inside a 8 ft x 7 ft cabinet with the inside back mirrored. Her dolls are also inside two other narrower cupboards in another part of the house. Some (perhaps 20 of them) are not on display cos she has no space.
