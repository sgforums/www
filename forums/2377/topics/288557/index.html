About Singapore Christian Forum: Aims/Rules/Regulations

  • laoda99
    29 Oct 07, 19:45
    This forum, formerly known as toys forum, was set up on 29 Oct 2007 as a temporary measure becoz the owners of Eternal Hope (EH) has decided to announce its closure.

    1) Why I setup this forum:
    There is a need for a temporary Christian forum for Christians to share and fellowship until owners of EH are being persuaded to restore the forum.

    2) Rules of this forum:
    a) Play nice: no insults. Christians do not claim to know everything. If you are really seeking answers/fellowship, you are welcome here. Otherwise, please refrain from posting insults or go somewhere else. I believe forummers are matured enuff to post sensible stuff. I also believe in self-moderation, but bear in mind that should the authorities be alerted by any forummer in the event of any law-breaking, then it is beyond the moderator's control not to take action against the offender. Should there be any request by the authorities, the moderator would cooperate with the authorities to take action against the said offender. In the eye of the law, there is no ignorance. So do take extra care before u post sensitive stuff.

    b) Adhere to any other measures setup by moderators from time to time. Moderators reserved the right to edit/close any offending thread and there is no "ifs" or "buts" about this. The moderators' decision is final. When u visit a person's house, u play nice. If not, please leave. We may not be able to do anything to u, but remember God is watching. If you do not believe in God, believe in your own conscience.

    I hope Christians and seekers would be able to have matured discussions and grow spiritually together here. EH has always been a good place to meet Christians/non-Christians from all walks of life, so keep it this way.

    Thank you.
