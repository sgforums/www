    greeting all toy collectors, just to share some of the locations of toy stores i frequently visit.

    Simply Toys @ Suntec City (Opposite the cinema) & Raffles Xchange
    Sells mostly movie replica, statues and lego.

    Sunshine Plaza
    2 shops selling anime figurines, and 2 shops selling gundams.

    China Square Central
    Truly a toy collectors' heaven. sells anime figurines, transformers and many more. every sunday afternoon there's flea market selling lots of toys.

    Far East Plaza
    2 shops selling anime figurines.
