    Rules and Regulations

    Note: Please read and acknowledge all terms and conditions before continuing your stay here in "Jukebox". Any violation of the forum threads will result in the culprit removed from activity.

    Threads

    1. Please!!! READ THE RULES!!! If you have the time to request for songs, meaning you'll have the time to read the rules. I don't care if you're a newbie and "didn't have a chance" to read the rules. No, I'm not giving out any more warnings. I'm sick and tired of people who doesn't bother to read the rules.

    2. This Forum is strictly for discussions of music and entertainment. Anything irrelevant or off-topic will be closed or locked by the moderator(s).

    3. Please post accordingly to the topics. For instance, please do not request Chinese songs in Korean Song Request threads. Those who do not post properly, their requests will not be offered, as a result of wrong posting. Failure to abide this rule will be issued a warning by the moderator.

    4. Everyone is allowed to make song requests in this forum. Everyone is also allowed to upload their files and share them here.

    5. Any song requests made by anyone MUST have the tag [req] beside the song title. This is for clearer identification of requests and faster responds from fellow forumites.

    6. No CROSS-POSTING is allowed. Failure to abide this rule will be issued a warning by the moderator(s) and his/her topic closed or deleted immediately after the first warning.

    Behavior in this Forum

    Behave yourselves. As you know, we all want to be comfortable in this forum where we can interact with other forumites in many greater ways. Additionally, we'd want to enjoy surfing this forum and for some, it's a place to chill. So, there MUST be cooperation.

    1. No one is allowed to start a thread that is subject to flames and other sensitive or crude remarks. The thread will be closed or nuked if someone does this.

    2. In time, when I get complaints from anyone here being uncomfortable with certain forumite(s), I will need your VALID reasons. Without strong reasons or existing evidences, I will not go to the extend of banning anyone.

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Alright guyz, here are a few added rules by icyprincess and a few by me. Please abide them too.

    Chinese/English Song Requests
    1. Each person may request from icyprincess up to 4 songs/videos, given the amount of space constraints in individual hosts and also other life commitments. Hope you all can understand. If there're any more files that you may want to get, you may come back in a few hour's time or perhaps the next day.

    2. If you are requesting songs especially from icyprincess, I'd recommend you to get a gmail or streamload account so as to make the transfer easier.

    Korean Song Requests
    1. Each person may request up to 5 songs/videos from the list I've shared. If there're any more files that you may want to get, you may come back in a few hour's time or perhaps the next day.

    2. I would recommend each forumites here to get Streamload or You Send It to make the transfer easier.

    PM-ing the Mods
    Only email addresses are allowed to be PMed to the Mods. NO REQUESTS ARE TO BE DONE VIA PMs! They'll be ignored.

    File Transfer
    Music files are huge when one requests them at one time. They come in Megabytes (MB)! So if you have gmail accounts or any other forms of accounts, PLEASE clear enough space for the song files you've requested to be sent to you. I'll only say this once! So if you don't heed this advice, don't come to me threatening to bite my head off. ALL PMs will be ignored.

    File Sharing
    1. Please try and share a little as well as requesting. This forum depends largely on the forumites' willingness to share their files, not just simply requesting.

    2. As for album requests, or requests for more than five songs, I would try to send it. As you must understand, compiling an album or several songs takes time.

    3. Do not report your post to me to have it fulfilled.
    - Honestly, what do you expect me to do? :X

    4. Don't try a, "where the f**k is my song? give it to me now!" - try being polite.

    5. If you haven't receive your requests in more than two weeks, you can PM me or icyprincess to bump ur requests. Please try to understand, that we mods have our own lifes to lead and we aren't that free to send out every songs in every second. So, if you guys would be nice, we will appreciate it.

    Additional Rule by icyprincess

    Sorry but this is to make things easier on us as well as the other forumites.

    Please dun request a song that you have already requested like, a day before. Keep tabs on what you have requested and what you have not. I will be sending out the songs with the titles as the topics, so it is pretty easy to keep tabs I think. Thanks.

    More rules will be added depending on the situations to come.

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    This forum needs your support and cooperation. If you follow the rules and regulations above, you'll be fine.

    Enjoy your stay here!

    your moderator,
