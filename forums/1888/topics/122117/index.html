    Okay, I know of a few places in S'Pore where one can go rent some VCDs.

    There's one in Hougang Street 21 opposite blk 200+, just next door from a coffee shop. Reasonable price, and also giving away discount coupons for returning VCDs earlier. VCDs range from English, Chinese, Korean, Japanese, Cantonese.. to other few.. which I've not known yet. Good place.

    There's another one in amk central. As I don't frequent that place, I don't know the price (guess should be quite cheap bua). It's next to the larger S11 coffee shop.
