    Song: Radical Dreamers (6mb)

    This song came from the Chrono Cross OST, which happened to be one of the most highly rated game, and OST.

    Amazon Chrono Cross OST review

    Amazon Chrono Cross Game Review

    I like the way the guitar is played throughout the song. Coupled by the vocals of the singer (whom I don't know who she is), the song is extremely smoothly and attempts to bring you away into another world. In fact, all the songs in this OST try to do just that.

    For those who want to find this OST in Singapore, I can say that you can't. I've tried. You have better luck ordering from Amazon. It is worth every dollar.
