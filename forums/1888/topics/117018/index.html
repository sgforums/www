    Well, here are the suitable hosts and some of my comments. Anyone else are free to add any other sites, which I may not know or hear about. If you do, I'll add them up here and I'll credit you.

    Remember! I'm just trying to help, so DON'T ever come to me whining or complaining about anything. You will be ignored. All PMs will also be ignored. It took me a long time to look up all the info on everything... especially on sites that I've not personally tried yet. So don't bite me if you don't like one of the sites I suggest.

    WARNING: ~ If you ever come across any site that does not have any kind of terms of service/policy, FAQ, or any of the other important information a site is supposed to have, consider it fishy and stay away!

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    You Send It - For temporary uploads. There is a 7 day / 25 download limit (whichever comes first) until the file expires.
    http://www.yousendit.com/

    Angelfire - Remember to change your file extention AFTER you upload, so they do not recognize it as a media file (preferably mp3 format). Also, you have to create a website to make it look like you're really using it the account. Speaking from experience.
    http://www.angelfire.com

    Ripway - 5 MB of daily transfer usage.
    http://www.ripway.com

    Geocities - Very limited bandwidth (3MB/month).
    http://geocities.yahoo.com/ps/learn2/WhatYouGet4_Free.html

    Free Homepages - Can still be used until you violated their terms of not d/loading music or hot-linking.
    http://www.freehomepages.com/

    Cyber Turf
    http://www.cyberturf.com/

    Hay Wired
    http://www.haywired.com/

    Home Pagez
    http://www.homepagez.com/

    Kool Pagez
    http://www.koolpagez.com/

    Mega One
    http://www.megaone.com/

    Odd Worldz
    http://www.oddworldz.com/

    Page Realm
    http://www.pagerealm.com/

    PC Pages
    http://www.pcpages.com/

    Powow
    http://powow.com/

    Bravenet - Restricted to only {.htm, .html, .gif, .jpg, .png, .txt, .swf, .mid, .rmi, .au, .wav, .js, .css, .ico, .bmp, .class} file types.
    http://www.bravenet.com/

    Dreamwater - 10MB free web space; unlimited bandwidth; you actually have to make a site + link back to every file to a html file on your webspace.
    http://www.dreamwater.com/

    Eccentrix - 50 MB web space, 900 MB bandwidth/month. Didn't know much about the terms. But it's worth a try though...
    http://eccentrix.com/

    Free Web Page - 150 MB of space. The maximum per-file size is 900KB. Exceed that limit and your account will be deleted.
    http://www.freewebpage.org/

    ICQ - Get ID. Kids under 13 are not allowed. ...funny & wierd policy
    http://www.icq.com/hp/start.html

    S4U - 100 MB of space; 1200 MB bandwidth per month; 40 MB bandwidth daily. Free service is limited to what file extentions you can upload.
    http://www.s4u.org/

    SP Hosting - 35 MB of space; 3 MB bandwidth per month; 2.5 MB limit on file size. Free services do not allow hotlinking to mp3s.
    http://www.sphosting.com/

    Spiral Pages - 150 to 500 MB of space; 2000 to 15,000 MB of bandwidth per month. Illegal mp3s not allowed.
    http://www.spiralpages.com/

    Top Cities - 150 MB of space; 600 MB bandwidth per month; 900 KB file limit. Pirated software (including mp3s) not allowed.
    http://www.topcities.com/

    Streamload (A site recommended by icyprincess)
    http://www.streamload.com
