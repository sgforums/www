    Truly Strange

    A lawyer named Strange died, and his friend asked the tombstone maker to inscribe on his tombstone, "Here lies Strange, an honest man, and a lawyer."

    The inscriber insisted that such an inscription would be confusing,
    for passersby would tend to think that three men were buried under the stone.

    Howecer, he suggested an alternative: Here lies a man who was
    both honest and a lawyer. That way, whenever anyone walked by the tombstone and read it, they would be certain to remark:
    "That's Strange".

    Talking To God

    A man was taking it easy, laying on the grass and looking up the clouds. He was identifying shapes when he decided to talk to God.

    "God," he said, "how long is a million years?"

    God answered, "In my frame of reference, it's about a minute."

    The man asked, "God, how much is a million dollars?"

    God answered, "To me, it's a penny."

    The man then asked, "God, can I have a penny?"

    God answered, "In a minute."

    A drunk guy staggers into a church. He sits down in a confession

    A caterpillar was looking to hitch a ride, when it finally found a family of bees. It hopped onto the bees' car and began the journey.

    Throughout the journey, the car stopped at intervals and Papa Bee kept on getting off the car to pee into the fuel tank. The caterpillar saw this and got curious.
