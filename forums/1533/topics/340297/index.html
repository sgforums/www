    Show Time & Date: Premieres 15 December 2008, Every Mon to Fri, 10pm

    Set during the Joseon Dynasty, Ijimae is of noble birth who lost his father, an official, in a political execution.  Having to encounter a series of traumatic events on top of witnessing the brutal death of his father at a tender age, he loses his memory and is later adopted by a village commoner. 

    With his father’s enemies trailing hot on his tracks years later, bits of Ijimae’s sorrowful memories return. He begins searching for the truth as well as his long-lost mother and sister. 

    He seeks revenge hence robs the rich, corrupted officials to aid the poor and to find that one thing to prove his father’s wrongful execution.
