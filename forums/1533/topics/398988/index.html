Show Luo and Rainie Yang get intimate at concert

  • TTFU
    11 May 10, 10:00

    Change is the only constant, and it can't be more fitting for Show Luo and Rainie Yang.

    Once at loggerheads with each other, they behaved so intimately onstage last Saturday it was easy to mistake them for lovers.

    Taiwanese singer, host and actor Show invited his former nemesis to be guest performer at his Hong Kong concert after featuring Singaporean songbird Stefanie Sun in the first show. Known for her cutie-pie image, Rainie was unusually decked out in a sexy, revealing outfit, though temperatures shot up further during the duet.

    At one point during their song 'In Your Eyes', Show pointed at Rainie with his finger and, unexpectedly, the latter bit and held on to it. Show then moved forward, held both of her hands and kissed her on the cheek, sending fans wild.

    The two, both famous for their strong personalities, were not on good terms due to a misunderstanding years ago. According to them, Show finally approached Rainie one day seeking to clear the air. Following their lead roles in smash idol drama Hi My Sweetheart, they became good friends.

    When introducing his newfound friend, Show said, "This is a singer I like a lot. We were once on bad terms, but we got to know each other again."

    Rainie later said, "I wasn't afraid, since we've kissed before on [Hi My Sweetheart]," but admitted his action took her by surprise.

    "He just wanted to make his fans happy."

    The singer-actress also gave the thumbs-up to her outfit, although she was visibly uncomfortable onstage.

    "I asked them what I should wear inside, and they said just a bra would do. The audience won't be able to see much. Although Show is taller than me, we're so close already, it's okay."
