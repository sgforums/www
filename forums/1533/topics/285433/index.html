    SINGAPORE : The Star Awards is back and the list of nominations looks as stellar as ever.

    Forty artistes have been nominated this year.

    They include Qi Yu Wu, the leading actor of the critically-acclaimed local movie 881.

    Also nominated is actor Christopher Lee, who revealed during the news conference that wedding bells will not be ringing this year for him and actress Fann Wong.

    From next Thursday, viewers can start calling in to nominate their favourite artistes. - CNA/ms
