    Edison Chen vows to have no more photo scandals
    Posted: 16 December 2011 1637 hrs
         

    TAIPEI: Hong Kong singer apologised once more for his involvement in a photo scandal with 16-year-old model Cammi Tse and vowed that such things won't happen again, when he attended a promotional event for his fashion label in Taiwan on Thursday, reported Hong Kong media.

    "I am bad, I was wrong. That is it. The whole thing was because I am wrong. I was stupid, I need to be smarter," said Chen.

    When asked if he would stop taking intimate photos of himself and his girlfriends, Chen refused to answer.

    "I don't want to answer you all because that is my private space.

    "This sort of thing won't happen in the future, if it does, you won't see Edison Chen again," said Chen.

    The 31-year-old actor also expressed disappointment that the media has consistently focused only on his mistakes.

    "When I do something good, nobody says anything," Chen lamented.

    "But when I do something a little, little bit bad, it becomes a very big story. I can't be that bad right?"

    Prior to his photo scandal with Tse, Chen had been involved in a massive sex photo scandal which involved top stars like Cecilia Cheung and Gillian Chung in 2008.

    Despite all that negative publicity, Chen's showbiz career seemed none the worse for wear.

    The singer-actor revealed that he is preparing to release his new music album and will hold a solo concert next year.

    Chen said he is also gearing up to run a marathon for charity in February.

    -CNA/ha
