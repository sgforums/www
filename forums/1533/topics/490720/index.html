Aaron Kwok admits to dating 27-year-old model

  • FireIce
    2 Dec 15, 18:02

    He has been talking about being ready for marriage and fatherhood of late, and now Hong Kong Heavenly King Aaron Kwok admitted that he is currently dating.

     

    As reported on Apple Daily, the 50-year-old star recently posted online a photo of two hands, a man’s and a woman’s, holding each other inside a car, and wrote, “You drive a little slower if you drive like this”, indirectly admitting to a new relationship.

    Although Aaron didn’t mention any names, he instead tagged a woman on Weibo named Moka Fang, a 27-year-old model from Shanghai.

    When asked to confirm the news, his manager Sui Mei stated that it’s a happy matter that Aaron himself should announce.

    In a phone interview later on, Aaron admitted his May-December romance with Moka and asked everybody to wish him happiness.

    This is the first time that the Hong Kong star openly acknowledged his relationship. His previous relationship with model-actress Lynn Hung was kept hush-hush for seven years.
