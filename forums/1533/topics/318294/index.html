Ellen DeGeneres & Portia de Rossi are getting married

  • Honeybunz

    Ellen DeGeneres and longtime girlfriend Portia de Rossi are jumping at the chance to get married. DeGeneres announced their engagement during a taping of "The Ellen DeGeneres Show," telling the studio audience the news that the California Supreme Court had struck down state laws against gay marriage. "So I would like to say now, for the first time, I am announcing: I am getting married," she said during the show, which aired Friday. The crowd in the studio audience gave her a long standing ovation, and de Rossi was in the audience, beaming and clapping. DeGeneres, 50, and de Rossi, 35, have been a familiar couple at Hollywood events. Previously, DeGeneres had a high-profile relationship with actress Anne Heche.
