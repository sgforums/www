    According to a Hong Kong magazine, Andy Hui has broken up with girlfriend Michelle and is actively trying to win his ex-lover's heart back.

    "I don't know anything about him or his girlfriend. You can go ask him instead."

    As for the possibility of them getting back together, Sammi admitted that it gets awkward when she sees him around.

    "Given my age, I would think I would prefer on-to-one relationships. It's better for me to avoid the complicated ones."
