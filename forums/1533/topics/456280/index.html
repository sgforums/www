    Wonder Girls to stage S’pore concert on 8 September

    By Jeffrey Oon | Singapore Showbiz – 22 hours ago

    The Wonder Girls are back performing in Asia after cracking the US market. (Photo courtesy of PMSA Alpha)

    Leading all-girl K-pop group Wonder Girls will be in Singapore for a one-night-only performance on 8th Sep at the Resorts World Convention Centre.

    The Wonder World Tour 2012, which kicked off in Seoul earlier this month, will be the group's first full solo gig in Singapore as they make their way back to Asia after venturing into the US market.

    The five -- consisting of leader Sun, rapper Yubin, vocalist Yenny, Lim, and Sohee -- catapulted to stardom after their 2008 smash hit and karaoke sing-along favourite, "Nobody".

    Best known for their infectious catchy tunes and synchronized sleek dance moves, the group will also be treating their fans to a old hits such as 'Tell Me', 'So Hot' and 'Be My Baby', as well as songs from their latest album Wonder Party including the chart-topping 'Like this' and 'Girlfriend'.

    Tickets to the one-night only performance range from S$188 to S$228.

    Tickets will be available via all SISTIC outlets from next Monday, 30 July 2012 at 10am.

    Coincidentally, Hong Kong superstar Aaron Kwok will also be staging his concert on the same night at the Indoor Stadium, setting the stage for a Cantopop vs K-pop fanfest.
