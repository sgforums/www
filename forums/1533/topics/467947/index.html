    It was a priority booking scheme that was supposed to reward the die-hard fans. But instead of feeling rewarded, some fans are upset and crying foul.

    One Production, concert promoter for K-pop superstar G-Dragon, announced on its Facebook page on Tuesday (Apr 9) that a one-day priority booking for his two concerts in June will start at 10am on Apr 11 through Sistic.

    But in order to be eligible to buy tickets before anyone else, each fan has to buy at least two tickets for the show – one for each night – regardless of whether they plan to catch both shows.

    Upset fans immediately took to forums to slam the unusual promotion, claiming the promoter was mercenary and penalising those who are less well-off.
