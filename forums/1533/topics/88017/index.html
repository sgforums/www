    SEPT 16, 2004
    Vicki Zhao linked to attack again
    HONG KONG - First, Vicki Zhao was accused of instigating an attack on her estranged business partner, Zou Xue.

    Now, the 28-year-old Chinese actress has been linked to an attack on her one-time alleged boyfriend, hairstylist Yi Ming, say Hong Kong reports.

    Zhao worked with Yi last year on the Ann Hui film, Goddess Of Mercy, and there was talk last month that they had been dating.

    Recently, Yi received two threatening SMSes which warned him to shut up.

    On Sunday night, he and a female assistant were about to enter a commercial building in Beijing when two men, armed with bricks, beat him up.

    The men hit him in the mouth for about three minutes and shouted at him to 'shut his mouth'. They did not specify about what.

    He suffered such severe injuries to his lips that he could not speak for some time, reports said.

    His colleague, a Madam Qu, was quoted in Hong Kong's Wen Wei Po as saying that the incident was linked to the rumours of his romance with Zhao.

    But Zhao's agent and sister-in-law Chen Rong denied that the actress had dated the hairstylist and said Zhao did not know that Yi had been assaulted.

    Yesterday, Yi himself was quoted in a report on China's Sina news portal as saying: 'I'm positive the people who attacked me have nothing to do with Vicki Zhao.'
