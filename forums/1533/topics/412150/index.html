The 10 Worst Celebrity Beards

    10. Zac Efron
    Beard-story:Zac is trying to make us all forget about that Charlie St. Cloud bomb he dropped on movie theaters this year with a lil' bit o' chin pubes. We don't totally hate it, but we will if it reaches Joaquin Phoenix lengths.

     

     

    9. Robert Pattinson
    Beard-story: Rob finished shooting Water For Elephants and immediately revolted against the clean-cut, clean-shaven look he sported for the film. Sure, he bearded up so he could follow Kristen Stewart around North America incognito style. But it didn't work (yes Lubbock TX, we're looking at you), and we want that pasty, perfect skin back!

     

     

    8.David Beckham
    Beard-story: Becks has been rockin' the summer casual facial hair look for too long, and we were pleased to see he's now buzzed back to his clean shaven look. When in doubt about facial hair, remember: WWVD - What Would Victoria Do?

     

     

     

    7. Ryan Gosling
    Beard-story: The Gos sported this man-mess while promoting Lars and the Real Girl in 2008. The look was only, uh, accentuated by a wicked case of bloat face. Thankfully he has since lost the muff and the puff.

     

     

    6. Conan O'Brien
    Beard-story: Conan was forced into early retirement (for a few months) and immediately tossed the razor aside. His ginger growth is still going strong - he's been rocking the beard in promos for his new show.

     

     

    5. John Travolta
    Beard-story: Travolta's been fighting off rumors of fake hair (on his head) for years, so consider this his weird way of retaliating against the gossip. He's currently freed his face; no comment on what's going on up top.

     

     

     

    4.Brad Pitt
    Beard-story: Brad is, quite possibly, the most gorgeous clean-shaven man in the world. But earlier this year he insulted millions when he sported a scraggly beard complete with tiny braids. Thankfully he stopped letting his kids style him and he's gone back to the blade.

     

     

     

    3. Mel Gibson
    Beard-story: Mel sported this beard in 2008; he was apparently just trying to break us all in for the crazy that was yet to come.

     

     

     

    2. Spencer Pratt
    Beard-story: There's douche, and then there's douche with a bit o' beard on top. Spencer's facial hair has made him look - and act - that much more insane.

     

     

     

     

    1. Joaquin Phoenix
    Beard-story: Joaquin sported this beard as he turned his career into the trainwreck that can now be enjoyed on the big screen, the fake-umentary I'm Still Here. Joaquin is, indeed, still around. His facial disaster is thankfully long gone.
