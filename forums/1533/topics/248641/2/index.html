    Britain's Prince William seeing his ex again: report

    Britain's Prince William is seeing his ex girlfriend Kate Middleton again, despite their split in April, the Mail on Sunday reported.

    The couple met up again during a party at the barracks where the second in line to the throne is posted as an army officer and were spotted kissing on the dance floor before going back to his room, the paper said.

    The pair have met several times in recent weeks and Middleton will attend a rock concert in London organised by William and his brother Prince Harry in memory of their mother, the late princess Diana, on July 1, it added.

    "Kate and William have been in touch and secretly seeing each other for about a month now," an unnamed confidante of the 25-year-old prince told the paper.

    "It's been kept very quiet and -- until the party two weeks ago -- confined to only their tightest social circle."

    Many royal watchers had tipped the couple to get married before their shock split.

    One British retailer was so certain that an announcement was imminent, it was ready to sell 20 different souvenir items, from cups to computer mouse pads, that bear the smiling faces of the prince and Middleton.

    Indeed, bookmakers William Hill slashed the odds on the couple getting engaged Sunday, following the paper's report, from 33/1 to 3/1 that the prince will propose to Middleton.

    Both have been pictured hitting the town since the break-up -- Middleton with a series of young men -- but no new serious love interest has been confirmed on either side.

    (source: Yahoo News)
