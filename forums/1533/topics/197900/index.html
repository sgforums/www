    Mystery girl demands goodnight peck from Prince Harry -- and gets it



    Mystery surrounds a girl in tight jeans and top who partied all night with Britain's Prince Harry, then refused to leave without a goodbye kiss from him.

    Paparazzi snapshots showed the brunette exiting a basement flat in South Kensington, west London -- and she was clearly not Harry's regular companion, blonde Zimbabwe-born South African heiress Chelsy Davy.

    Wednesday's London Evening Standard said Harry -- awaiting deployment as a newly-minted lieutenant in the British army -- went to the flat with a friend to escape a melee at a nearby nightclub in which he was not involved.

    Four others in a taxi soon joined them, playing loud music until dawn, before the mystery girl, on her way out of the flat, called down to the prince inside: "Oh Harry, give us a kiss!"

    "He replied, 'Oh, not here,' and she went down the steps again to get a goodnight kiss from him," the Evening Standard said, adding that she admitted to being "quite drunk" when she re-emerged and asked a royal bodyguard for help to get home.

    Harry, 21, the son of Prince Charles and the late Princess Diana, is a notorious party animal, though he has sought to project a more serious side through charity missions in Africa and his decision to join the military.
