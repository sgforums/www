    The long-awaited fan meeting for the fans of Running Man – yes, the cast of the top variety Korean programme Running Man will be heading to Singapore in September!

    Via omy, the Fan Meeting will be held at Marina Bay Sands on September 28th. The duration of the event will stretch over one and a half hours, and song-dance performances as well as fan interaction games will be prepared. It is also said that the cast who will be attending the Singapore Fan Meeting includes Kim Jong Kook, HaHa, Gary, Song Jihyo and there may be one more member to join them as well. Seems like a fan meet not to be missed!

    Ticket prices to Running Man fan meeting in Singapore announced

     Ticket prices to Running Man fan meeting in Singapore has been announced.

    ONE TV is set to organised the Singapore leg of the Running Man Fan Meeting Asia Tour 2013 at the Singapore Expo Hall 2B on Saturday, 19 October at 7:30pm.

    ONE will welcome five of the seven popular hosts including HaHa (Ha Dong Hoon), Kim Jong Kook, Jee Seok Jin, Gary (Kang Hee Gun) and the sole female cast member, Song Ji Hyo, for ‘Watsons Race Start! in Singapore’.

    The Singapore event is the first leg of the Running Man Fan Meeting Asia Tour 2013 that Song features in.

    Fans can expect an engaging and intimate two-hour program at the fan meeting event, to get to know the Running Man stars like never before.

    Fun and exciting games will see lucky audiences share the stage with the five personalities, and there will be live performances by the singers among them.

    Tickets will go on sale at SISTIC from 21 September 2013, with tickets priced at S$248, S$218, S$188 and $158.

    The first 300 to purchase Category 1 tickets will get passes to receive posters autographed by one randomly picked Running Man cast member at an upcoming meet-and-greet session. Details of the meet-and-greet session will be released at a later date.

    In addition, priority ticket sales are being lined up for presenting sponsor Watsons Singapore.

    Exclusive to Watsons Card Members, spend S$100 nett in a single receipt at any Watsons store from 5 – 19 September to be eligible to purchase "Watsons Race Start! In Singapore" tickets at SISTIC outlets on 20 September, limited to 1 pair of tickets per Watsons receipt.

    Watsons Race Start! In Singapore

    Date: 19 October, Saturday
    Time: 7:30pm
    Venue: Singapore Expo Hall 2B
    Ticketing: S$248 – Category 1 / S$218 – Category 2 / S$188 – Category 3 / $158 – Category 4 through SISTIC (www.sistic.com.sg) from 21 September.
