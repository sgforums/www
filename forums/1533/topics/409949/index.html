    Patrick Tse mulls following 26-year-old girlfriend to UK
    Posted: 02 September 2010 1312 hrs

     
     
    Photos 1 of 1

    Patrick Tse in a promotional photo for the ATV drama "Central Affairs 2". -photo from hkatv.com.
       
     

     

     

    HONG KONG: Veteran Hong Kong actor Patrick Tse Patrick Tse, 73, has denied paying for his 26-year-old girlfriend CoCo's education in the United Kingdom and said on Tuesday that he is thinking of following her there, reported Hong Kong media.

    "She already has lots of money, I will consider becoming a tag-along boyfriend and follow her to the UK," Tse told reporters.

    Tse, who is best known for his role as "Fourth Brother" in the drama "The Shell Game", also clarified that he and CoCo had never broken up as the tabloids have claimed.

    "We never broke up. I don't even know how these rumours came about," said the actor, revealing that CoCo had just finished a year of study in the UK and returned to Hong Kong for the holidays.

    Tse's relationship with his Shanghainese girlfriend CoCo came to light in 2008 when he was spotted taking a flight with her to Mexico. At the time, Tse claimed CoCo was his assistant.

    CoCo is about the same age as Tse's daughter, Jennifer Tse.

    Tse, the father of movie star Nicholas Tse, had previously expressed that he had no qualms about dating a woman 47 years his junior.

    "It's okay as long as the both of us do not mind being together," said Tse in an earlier television interview, pointing out that he was "much more energetic than most of the young adults these days".
