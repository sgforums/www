    Yueniang & Chen Xi to get extra time together!


    Following the widespread public upset over the two-hour ending of The Little Nyonya, a short special has been filmed to wrap up Yueniang and Chen Xi’s relationship!
     

    1 / 4
    January 9, 2009
     
    Kwok Kar Peng
     
     
    In response to the public outcry after the last episode of The Little Nyonya aired on Monday Jan 5, the production crew has just filmed a three-minute capsule titled The Little Nyonya: The Final Chapter to be aired Sunday Jan 11 at 9.55pm.
     
    The special will feature only Jeanette Aw and Qi Yuwu and is not an alternative ending to the drama. Rather it’s an add-on to explain clearer the relationship between Yueniang and Chen Xi and will continue from their separation at the railway track.
     
    Jeanette and Yuwu filmed the scene this morning along the railway track to Malaysia and news of it was deliberately withheld to prevent the media from springing a surprise visit to the crew. The scene also had to be completed in two hours because of Jeanette’s schedule.
     
     
    Why not an alternative ending?
     
    No doubt many of you are wondering that too; we spoke to Ms Kok Len Shoong, the Senior Vice President of Chinese Drama and understand that there are several reasons for filming an add-on rather than an alternative ending.
     
    For one, the production crew would have to film an estimate of half an episode (equaling many days of filming) to put together an alternative ending and in Ms Kok’s words, “It won’t be nice to watch if we create an ending just to force them to be together.”
     
    Secondly and more importantly, the relationship between Yueniang and Chen Xi has gone beyond a love between a man and a woman. Yueniang, though bold and resilient, lives in a time when divorce is an extremely serious matter. She is also a woman who always considers the different aspects of a situation. Whatever she does, she does it for others first.
     
    And thus, Yueniang has to consider the reputation of Chen Xi’s family before she can be with Chen Xi and bear him children, especially later when she decides to help Yuzhu raise her baby.
     
    “My hair stylist told me that her 80-year-old mother said divorce is very severe matter back then and that it would be impossible for Yueniang to be with Chen Xi. On the other hand, I also had a discussion with someone else who told me that drama is for the audience and so we should satisfy them.
     
    “We are very thankful to the public for their support towards the drama and we understand their opinions. We are torn too and we hope our viewers can understand our motives and see different sides of the story and the multiple layers of the ending,” Ms Kok said
     
    Ms Kok also added, there are bigger themes in the drama, of remembering one’s ancestors and as Chen Sheng asked of Yueniang, to promote the Peranakan culture.
     
    “Yueniang also helped Chen Xi get together with his second wife. In the end, both Yueniang and Chen Xi are very happy without each other too.”
     
     

    The Little Nyonya: The Final Chapter will be aired this Sunday Jan 11 at 9.55pm.
