    HK actor Moses Chan goes commando after losing underwear
    Posted: 21 May 2009 1346 hrs
         
         

    HONG KONG : Strange things have been happening to Hong Kong actor Moses Chan, popularly known as Chen Hao, at the gym lately.

    The 38-year-old actor said first his underwear went missing, then he received a surprise in his locker - a rose.

    Chan said his underwear was first stolen when he was showering after a gym session, "I left my towel and underwear outside the cubicle and when I returned, the underwear was missing. Thankfully the towel did not get stolen. And I had to walk around without it (underwear)!"

    And on a separate occasion, he found a stalk of rose in his gym locker.

    Chan saw the funny side of the incident and said he was grateful for it but declined to accept the rose and left it in the locker.

    The actor who recently had to put on a bit of weight for an upcoming television series, has been hitting the gym regularly to shed off a few extra pounds. And that was when he started attracting unnecessary attention.

    The actor who currently weighs 200 pounds, revealed that a slimming company has expressed interest in signing him on as a celebrity ambassador. Chan said he is interested in the offer and wouldn't mind showing some skin for the advertisement.

    "I'm fine with the idea, as long as my body is fit and muscular. I do not mind showing it off as long as it is done well and artistically."
