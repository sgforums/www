Bon Jovi guitarist breaks his arm

  • the Bear
    30 Mar 06, 21:49
    LOS ANGELES, March 29 (UPI) -- Bon Jovi guitarist Richie Sambora fell in his Los Angeles home and broke his arm, it was reported Wednesday.

    The guitarist is expected to perform with his arm in a sling when the New Jersey band begins its tour of Japan April 8, People magazine reported.

    He is expected to be recovered fully by the time the band's "Have a Nice Day" tour rolls into Europe in May.

    (source: UPI News)
