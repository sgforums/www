    Fann-Lee wedding on hold 8:53 AM

     

    MALACCA - THE most anticipated wedding of the year here between MediaCorp's A-list stars Fann Wong and Christopher Lee has been put on hold, The Star reported on Sunday.

    The couple, both 38, were said to be making plans to tie the knot in Malacca this September but decided against it following the death of Lee's father Shu Qin, 68, after a heart attack on Tuesday.

    Some 100 family members and friends gathered at Lee's family home in Ujong Pasir on Saturday to pay their last respects before the cremation at the Melaka Memorial Park in Sungai Putat.

    It was reported the lovebirds had announced the planned wedding with receptions in Malacca and in Singapore.

    Their nuptials had been widely speculated to take place on 09/09/09 - a date that comes only once every thousand years.

    But according to Chinese custom, a couple will either have to hold the wedding within 100 days of the close kin's death or wait for three years.

    The pair have been courting for almost eight years and recently starred in their latest RM5.3 million love-comedy movie The Wedding Game - depicting them as a celebrity couple getting hitched.
