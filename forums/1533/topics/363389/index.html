    Lead actress of Taiwanese film Spider Lilies and a supporting actress in the film, The Mummy: Tomb of the Dragon, Hong Kong artiste Isabella Leong gave birth to a baby boy in April. The news was revealed last Thursday.

    The 21-year-old-to-be actress has been dating 42-year-old businessman Richard Li for over a year. During this time, she was involved in a contractual dispute with Emperor Entertainment Group for half a year before they agreed to terminate the contract. Since then, Leong has kept a low profile but was rumoured to have been pregnant and awaiting delivery overseas. Last Thursday, the Hong Kong media received news of her baby's birth.

    Leong gave birth naturally to a seven pound baby boy in Toronto in end April. Grandfather of the baby, Hong Kong entrepreneur Li Ka-Shing, named him 'Ethan'.
    A spokesperson of Leong's revealed that the child has been planned for, and that she is recovering very well from the delivery under her mother's care.

    She said, "Isabella feels blessed to be bestowed with the baby boy and is glad that Ethan is a happy baby. She is currently busy taking care of her newborn and has no intention of resuming work anytime soon."

    Though they have a child now, Li and Leong have no intention of tying the knot just yet.
