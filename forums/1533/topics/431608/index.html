    Felicia Chin returns to headline new Ch8 drama

    Taking time off from showbiz to hit the books has proved to be a good break for Felicia Chin who returns to local TV looking more cheery and upbeat than ever

     

     

     

    She promised to be back when she bade goodbye in July last year. True to word, Felicia Chin is jumping at almost every opportunity presented to her to meet with local TV viewers -- that is if her schedule permits.

    Looking fresh and young with her stylish short crop at the imaging session for Love Thy Neighbour, the Felicia standing before us today reminded us of a 20-year-old budding showbiz newbie rather than a 27-year-old actress with seven years of experience in the industry.

    Even the director and drama's executive producer Winnie Hong echoed our sentiments and remarked on Felicia's "different look in her eyes" and the "innocent vibes" emitted by the latter.

    Perhaps her return to school has simplified the life of the former full-time actress and allowed her to catch up on a chapter of schooling life she never got to fully savour.

    She thoughtfully commented, "Being away from the industry for one year, I think my thoughts have maybe changed a little, like what the director and the rest are saying 'I see something different in your eyes'."

    In Love Thy Neighbour, Felicia will be starring as the spunky Wang Tian Hu alongside veteran actor, Ha Yu, as they play a pair of father and daughter who goes round conning others. The drama also stars Shaun Chen, Aileen Tan, Huang Wenyong and Vivian Lai.
