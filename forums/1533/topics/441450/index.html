    Top celebrities head to Singapore for 2011 Mnet Music Awards

    After reporting that 2011 ‘MAMA’ will use twenty celebrity presenters to host the show, the celebrities themselves are heading towards Singapore on November 28th from Incheon International Airport.

    With the celebrities ambushed by fans and paparazzi, each celebrity also showed off their unique fashion, ranging from the sleek black boots and jackets to the colorful green sweater. Despite the early morning departure, many of the celebrities greeted their fans with warm smiles and handshakes. Some of the featured celebrities include Lee Byung Hun, Song Seung Hoon, Yoon Eun Hye, Han Hyo Joo, Park Si Yeon, and Park Si Hoon.

    The 2011 ‘Mnet Asian Music Awards’ will take place in the Singapore Indoor Stadium on November 29. The grand opening of the red carpet will last from 5 to 7 PM, while the presentation of the awards and performances will be from 7 to 11 PM, the whole broadcast lasting for around 6 hours with a live broadcast in Singapore.

    Song Joong Ki, Go Soo, Bae Soo Bin, Kim Hee Sun, Han Chae Young will arrive later on.
