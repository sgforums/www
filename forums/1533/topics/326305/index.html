    Biren has left MediaCorp!

     
         

    August 4, 2008

     
    Text by Kwok Kar Peng
    Editorial team: Dion Tang
     
     
    This news has come as a shock to veteran actress Hong Huifang who returned from Beijing only today to hear of the departure from us. The two were co-stars in the 2003 drama Vive La Famille II. To Huifang, Biren’s decision to leave her first job of 19 years to become a home-maker isn’t a surprise as the latter gave birth to her second child only four months ago.
     
    “Biren places a lot of emphasis on her kids’ childhood and education, and so given her character, becoming a full-time mother is something that she would do,” Huifang said.
     
    Another reason for her departure is the terms offered in the new contract, where Biren would not receive medical benefits, annual leave and CPF contributions. With her leaving before the TV’s annual glitz event Star Awards, Biren may not be able to receive the All-Time Favourite award, given out to artistes who have received the Most Popular Artiste award 10 times.
     
    When asked, the Vice-President of MediaCorp TV’s Branding & Promotions Mr Paul Chan is unable to confirm whether the company will invite Biren back to receive the All-Time Favourite award as the committee for March’s (2009) Star Awards has not yet been set up. He said, “Her contributions are witnessed by all and we will miss her, but this is the path that she has chosen and for a mother of two to choose between career and family, I think family is more important.”
