    SBS’ Running Man is taking its race and missions overseas once more.

    SBS’ variety department revealed on January 3 the show is currently working on a special episode, set to take place abroad, titled, ‘Running Man in Asia’. 

    Described as a tour race, the show’s producers are said to be finalizing approximately two countries to hold the special race in, as well the other details, before filming sometime in early 2013

    The department added the show’s popularity abroad in other Asian countries continued to take the show by surprise as it continued to receive countless invitations and offers to film in various places abroad. 

    With the show and its cast enjoying high popularity abroad, the show’s producers added it expected the special would receive favorable response from fans.

    The popular variety show has, in the past, filmed episodes in places like China and Thailand.
