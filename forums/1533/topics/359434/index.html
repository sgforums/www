    A grand live stage version inspired by the hit TV serial, The Little Nyonya, this Peranakan fiesta stars leads Jeanette Aw and Dai Yang Tian from the original cast amongst a host of other MediaCorp artistes! The Musical Play will also feature a stellar cast of guest artistes such as Olivia Ong as well as theatre professionals.

    An evening recreating the emotional highlights of the drama serial with a plot set in a Peranakan Ball (Tok Panjang!), with the much-loved Yue Niang, helping a downtrodden Nyonya servant girl much like herself (in the past) in a wealthy Peranakan household to find the Baba of her dreams.

    Traditional Peranakan song and dance woven seamlessly into the live stage show promises a colourful and spectacular extravaganza as more of Peranakan culture comes to you live this May!
