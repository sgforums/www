    The latest TV adaptation of "Three Kingdoms" is on Jiangsu and Chongqing StarTV, as well as on NHK in Japan. The show has had high rankings ever since its first broadcast. Interestingly, the series has also been the target of many ironic comments. Let’s examine some of the latest hot topics about "Three Kingdoms":

     

    Only 10 Secs for "Taking Oath in the Peach Garden”

    The story of Liu Bei, Guan Yu and Zhang Fei taking Oath in the Peach Garden takes only ten seconds. This is satirized by the audiences: “Even quicker than Cao Cao’s toilet break, you must be kidding me! Now it's the tail wagging the dog!”

     

    The Villain Cao Cao Becomes the Leading Role

    The first three episodes are like Cao Cao’s monodrama. This is taken to be misleading to otherwise uninformed youth.

     

    Diao Chan is not That Pretty

    Diao Chan is known as one of the four beauties in Ancient China. However, the actress who plays Diao Chan was rebuked for her homely appearance.

     

    Despite all these criticisms, the new "Three Kingdoms" is still worth watching. Do not worry if you miss the first round live show, it will be available online later on. If you can’t wait for the televisions series to be released you might try Three Kingdoms Online, a game that’s taking over the SLG scene these days. It is run by Koramgame, based on the warring era of Three Kingdoms, you can raise troops, develop cities, explore the epic cities and recruit epic heroes. King of the Hill is my favorite event: you can get great rewards and collect taxes on the other cities. It is also nice to have a league in which brotherhood is treasured. I strongly recommend it to you! I think Three Kingdoms Online is now available in English, for all those non-chinese speakers out there.
