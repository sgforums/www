    30 Sep – – After photos of the TVB actor and co-star Aimee Chan living together was revealed, Moses Chan finally admits that Aimee is officially his girlfriend but dismissed the allegations of them co-habiting as false, according to a report by the Hong Kong media.

    "I do visit her, but we haven't reached that stage (co-habitation) yet, we can't be so impatient," said Moses Chan, at a publicity event on Thursday which he and Aimee Chan attended together.

    Recently published photos showed the pair feeding each other and getting comfy at home, however the 40-year-old actor insisted that he only visited Aimee occasionally "to cook" and expressed that he had never spent the night there.

    Even after being secretly stalked by the paparazzi for the intimate photos, Moses appeared calm and said he planned to buy curtains for his girlfriend's apartment to avoid peeping toms.

    Moses Chan had previously been romantically linked to Hong Kong actress Bernice Liu but were said to have ended their six-year romance before he met Aimee Chan, a former Miss Hong Kong, on the set of the TVB drama "War of the Heart".
