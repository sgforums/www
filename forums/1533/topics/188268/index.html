    Jolie Tops Beautiful People List
    Friday April 28 12:16 PM ET

    Her humanitarian efforts, combined with her stunning good looks, have earned Angelina Jolie the title of People magazine's Most Beautiful Person.

    By FilmStew Staff Report, FilmStew.com

    You'd be hard-pressed today to find a man, or person for that matter, in America who doesn't think that Angelina Jolie is one of the most beautiful people in the world. Sure, she's become a tabloid headline ever since taking up with Brad Pitt, and she does have that beautiful pregnant woman glow now that she's carrying Pitt's child, but those aren't the reasons she's been chosen as the cover girl for People's "100 Most Beautiful People" issue.

    The magazine cited Jolie's recent humanitarian efforts as the biggest influence as to why she was chosen as this year's Most Beautiful Person. "She looks the most beautiful when she's in the field natural, no makeup, nothing," Wyclef Jean, who has worked with Jolie on a relief efforts in Haiti, told People, according to the Associated Press. He continued, "Because you see Angelina, the angel. It doesn't get any better than that."

    This was the fourth year that Jolie has made the issue, but it was the first time she has graced the cover. The rest of her family, including Pitt and her two children, were also recognized by the periodical as the "World's Most Beautiful Family."

    The list, which was expanded from 50 to 100 people this year, also included: George Clooney, Halle Berry, Scarlett Johansson, Julia Roberts, Ryan Seacrest, Matt Leinart and Kirstie Alley, as well as all 26 spokesmodels from the hit TV show Deal or No Deal. The issue hits newsstands Friday.
