    AUG 17, 2004

    Keanu proposes


    The wedding ceremony is expected to be a simple one as Keanu hates fuss and attention.

    LOS ANGELES - Keanu Reeves has proposed to his ex-girlfriend, actress Autumn Macintosh. Britain's Daily Star said he reunited with her recently, after a brief relationship in the early 1990s, and popped the question at a restaurant in Los Angeles.

    He reportedly told her that he is ready for some stability in his life after the past 12 years of tragedy and loss.

    Before getting back with Macintosh, the 39-year-old The Matrix star stayed single for years. His long-time love, Jennifer Syme, gave birth to their stillborn baby in 1999 and died in a car crash in 2001.

    He has also been busy caring for his sister, Kim, who has leukaemia.

    A source said: 'Keanu and Autumn have known each other for years. In May they got together again and over the past few months, Keanu started thinking about settling down.

    'They've both told pals to expect the announcement later this week. It will just be a simple ceremony. Keanu hates fuss and attention,' added the source.
