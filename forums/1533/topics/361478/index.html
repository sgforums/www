    Ever thought of catching all your favorite stars at one spot, come on down to Toa Payoh HDB Hub Mall to watch the launch of the

    True Hearts Show 2009.

    Be enthralled by a stellar cast of mediacorp celebs on that day.

     

    Quan Yi Feng together with Cavin Soh will be doing the hosting duties, Fiona Xie will be seeking some rhythm on the drums while our loveable Nonya Jeanette Aw will be performing a ballet performance.

     

    Not forgetting the guys, Dai Yang Tian from the table tennis show will be doing a wushu demonstration as well.

    Catch them all and many more celebs at Toa Payoh HDB Hub Mall

    7 June 7 pm.

     

    See ya there!
