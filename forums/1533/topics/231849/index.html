    Very little is being done to alter the winning composition of American Idol. Not that we're complaining. Bad singers, we're sure, will invade our horrified (yet delighted) sights and eardrums when no less than SIX audition episodes arrive fresh for the toasting in our living rooms. And honest, nasty judge Simon Cowell will raise controversy and step on everyone's nerves with his chilly and irate insults - again to everyone's glee.

Guest judges for the auditions include Jewel and Olivia Newton-John.
