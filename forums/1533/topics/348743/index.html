    Mediacorp TV gets exclusive rights to co-produce and distribute Just for Laughs to Asia

    Fans of the award-winning comedy show, Just For Laughs can look forward to a new series with an Asian twist come January next year.

    Mediacorp TV has signed an exclusive agreement with Just for Laughs to co-produce and distribute the popular comedy series for the Asian market.

    With support and input from JFL, Mediacorp TV will produce the first season.

    Mediacorp will then produce the show in Singapore and other Asian countries for subsequent seasons.

    The content produced in Asia will be integrated with material produced in Montreal, United States, the United Kingdom and sold worldwide.

    Director General of the Just for Laughs production company, Mr Pierre Girard elaborates on the significance of the joint venture.

    "You know we did the gags for 10 years, we're quite good at that. I think with the joint venture with Mediacorp, it would be fantastic and they help us alot to understand how the people are, how they will react,how we have to adapt our script to make it happen perfectly."

    Mr Kenneth Liang, Executive Vice President of Mediacorp Channel Five's TV programming and production, sees the collaboration as a win-win situation for both parties.

    "We are really very excited and this is the first of our international collaboration, this is the first baby that we are giving with collaboration from our friends in Montreal and Singapore and we hope that we can add alot of value to Just of Laughs with our Asian productions."

    The first season will be filmed partly in Singapore and Montreal.

    It will comprise 33 half hour episodes and distributed to other Asian countries like Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia, Phillipines.
