Choi Jin Hyuk, Song Ji Hyo to hold fan meeting in Singapore

  • no display name
    10 Apr 14, 20:00

    Korean drama "Emergency Couple" stars, Choi Jin Hyuk and Song Ji Hyo, will be making their way to Singapore for a fan meeting on 16 May 2014, at The Kallang Theatre.

    "Emergency Couple" is a romantic comedy drama about the love and hatred of a divorced couple, Chang Min (Choi Jin Hyuk) and Jin Hee (Song Ji Hyo), that reunited as they met again in the same hospital as interns.

    Unlike typical Korean romantic comedies that portrays love as cute and fluttery, this drama demonstrated a more realistic side of romantic relationships, the misunderstandings and arguments, something which viewers can better relate to.

    While Song Ji Hyo made her first official visit to Singapore with the Running Man team last October, this is Choi Jin Hyuk's maiden trip to meet the fans here.

    Priority ticket sales to the fan meet will start on 12 April at SISTIC outlets from 12pm (exclusive to SingTel Mio TV subscribers). All SingTel Mio TV subscribers can enjoy additional 5% discount for all ticket categories from 12 April till 16 May (for purchase at SISTIC agent only). Public ticket sales will start on 13 April 2014 on SISTIC website and outlets at 12pm.
