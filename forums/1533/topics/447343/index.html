    Posted: 08 February 2012 1508 hrs
    HONG KONG: Hong Kong actor Bosco Wong was photographed partying at a club and getting dead drunk over the weekend, while girlfriend Myolie Wu was away in China for work, reported Hong Kong media.

    The series of photographs, which were published in a Hong Kong tabloid recently, showed Wong slipping away from a number of sexily-dressed women who had approached him for photographs.

    The photos also showed his friends helping Wong, who appeared dead drunk, out of the club at 4am on Sunday.

    When asked about his weekend escapade, Wong said he was simply being a good host to his friends from Malaysia and had brought them out for a night on the town.

    "Because I have some friends from Malaysia over, so I went out with them.

    "We haven't gone out to party for a long time," said Wong, before going on to explain that he only became drunk because his friends forced him to drink.

    "I was indeed made drunk, but I have so many friends around at the time that I wasn't worried," said Wong.

    He added that he was not worried Wu, whom he had been dating for seven years, would become upset as she too knew the friends he went out with that night.
