A-Sa seriously ill?

  • Rooney9
    23 Jul 10, 01:04

    Hong Kong celebrity Charlene Choi (popularly known as A-Sa) was hospitalized yesterday.

    A-Sa was slated to appear in Shanghai for an event yesterday as a spokesperson for a Shanghai radio station. However, the event was cancelled when A-Sa could not make it.

    According to reports, A-sa was supposed to get on a flight to Shanghai but around an hour before the flight timing, the radio station received a call from A-Sa's company that the star had fainted at home and was being rushed to the hospital.

    According to A-Sa's weibo (Chinese equivalent of Twitter), she has been on medication for her flu since last week. The medication has been causing her discomfort and before she fainted, she has been vomiting.

    A-Sa's manager Mani told reporters that while A-Sa is now back at home recuperating, her condition does not seem to be getting better.

    "Her condition is quite serious, for a better understanding you can read her blog. We do not wish to divulge too much here."

    After the news broke, netizens were doubtful, saying that perhaps A-Sa was never invited by the radio station in the first place.

    To that, the radio station spokesperson said that A-Sa was attending the event as a form of public service so there are no monetary losses on their part.

    "However, we still hope that A-Sa can recover soon and come back to Shanghai for the event."
