Netizens spread malicious rumours that HyunA is pregnant

  • FireIce
    11 Jan 14, 21:21

    Cube Entertainment will not be going easy on the people who started and spread rumors about 4Minute‘s HyunA being pregnant with BEAST‘s Jang Hyun Seung’s baby.

    According to an article in Soompi, on January 10, a deceiving article that looked like an official statement by Cube was shared through a smart phone application.

    The article said that Trouble Maker members HyunA and Jang Hyun Seung had an inappropriate relationship and that HyunA was pregnant because of it.

    These rumors began to spread through other social networking platforms.

    Cube Entertainment released an official statement about these malicious rumors. “We will take tough legal actions against the netizens who started and spread these malicious rumors. The legal team is currently collecting data in preparation to take legal action as quickly as possible. Because these rumors are so hurtful, we had to do something about it.”
