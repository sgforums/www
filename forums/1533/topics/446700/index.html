    Fann Wong up against hubby Chris Lee for Favourite On-screen Couple award

    The nominees for Star Awards 2012 online awards were announced yesterday

     

    Fans, are you ready to vote?

    Nominees for the online awards were announced yesterday.

    Newcomers Ian Fang and Edwin Goh of On the Fringe fame will go up against idol Elvin Ng -- nominated for his role as Sergeant Teo Kwee Xiang in C.L.I.F. -- for the Favourite Male Character award.

    Other nominees include Tay Ping Hui, for his role as a ruffian chef in Bountiful Blessings, and Christopher Lee as a Chinese physician in The Oath.

    For the Favourite Female Character category, Taiwanese pretty babe Cynthia Wang's role in drama Devotion will go against last year's winner Rui En, who is nominated for her portrayal of Zhang Ya Le in A Tale of 2 Cities.

    Also worth a mention is up-and-coming young actress Kimberly Chia, who shot to fame with her fellow young co-stars from On the Fringe. Other nominees include Ann Kok (Kampong Ties) and Jesseca Liu (The Oath).

    In the Favourite On-screen Couple (Drama) category, Fann Wong and Li Nan Xing (On the Fringe) will be vying for the award with Fann's hubby Christopher Lee and Jesseca Liu (The Oath).

    However, it might be a tough match for these two pairs, as they go up against idol couples Rui En and Elvin (Code of Honour), as well as Joanne Peh and Qi Yu Wu (C.L.I.F.). Rounding up the list is Pierre Png and Rui En, for their match in The In-Laws.

    Additionally, making the list of Favourite Variety Host are Bryan Wong, Fann Wong, Mark Lee, Pornsak and Vivian Lai.
