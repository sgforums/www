    Jacelyn Tay to marry her boyfriend of two years, Brian Wong, this Sunday, 10th October 2010

    According to local publication 8 DAYS, former Star Search winner and MediaCorp actress Jacelyn Tay is finally tying the knot with her 42-year-old boyfriend, Brian Wong, after two years of courtship on Oct 10.

    35-year-old Jacelyn, who wants a hot air balloon wedding and once said she can never be tied down, has finally found her Mr. Right.

    However, the actress revealed that it was definitely not love at first sight for the couple. They used to be business acquaintances when Brian was still the VP of Sales for MediaCorp, and Jacelyn, the owner of The Health Club (now known as Body Inc.).

    Love blossomed between the pair one year later when they met again through mutual friends in 2007. She took him to church and things took a slow but steady turn then.

    Celebrity hair-stylist David Gan and make-up guru Andy Lee will be taking care of the bride-to-be, who will be decked in Vera Wang on her big day.

    The couple will hold their private wedding banquet at the Pan Pacific Hotel with approximately 560 guests, including celebs like Thomas Ong, Chen Hanwei, Yvonne Lim, Pat Mok, Christopher Lee and many more.
