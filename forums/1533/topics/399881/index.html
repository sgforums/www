    Popular Taiwanese host Dee Hsu has dismissed the latest saga involving her marriage.

    After recent reports of a shaky relationship and domestic violence, her husband Mike Hsu was spotted last Saturday clubbing with a "hot young chick", the second such incident in the last months.

    But Dee commented at Monday's press conference for the Golden Melody Awards that she and Mike had agreed before their nuptials to not restrict each other's social life.

    She said, "To be honest, going to a nightspot isn't something serious. I knew he liked it. I did too, but now that I'm older I'm worried alcohol would affect my skin. Nightspots are noisy and when you talk to people, it's inevitable you have to go near. It's nothing."

    Mike and the unknown woman were seen conversing intimately.

    This is not the first time Dee, a mother of two girls, has stood up unquestionably for her husband. Their marriage has been the subject of much controversy, with the good-looking Mike often tracked by paparazzi. When this latest incident broke, she immediately stated through her manager he has her full support.
