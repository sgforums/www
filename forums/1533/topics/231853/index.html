    It's the clash of the first-timers versus the seasoned nominees for the The 64th Annual Golden Globe Awards.


    NOMINATION LIST

    Just hazard a look at the Best Director of a Motion Picture nominees. There's the old blood: Three-time Golden Globes Best Director (Million Dollar Baby, Unforgiven and Bird) & 1988 Cecil B. DeMille Award recipient Clint Eastwood for Flags of Our Fathers and Letters from Iwo Jima and Golden Globes Best Director (Gangs Of New York) Martin Scorsese for The Departed. Then there's the new blood: first-time nominees Stephen Frears for The Queen and Alejandro Gonzalez Inarritu for Babel.

    Clinching a double coup, like director Clint Eastwood, is Leonardo DiCaprio, who is nominated twice for Best Actor (Drama) with The Departed and Blood Diamond.

But Penelope Cruz is up against stiff competition from the likes of Judi Dench, Helen Mirren and Kate Winslet who have all been crowned Golden Globes Best Actress at least once in their careers, while Maggie Gyllenhael has been nominated once.
