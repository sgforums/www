    LONDON - BRANGELINA might soon be just Brad and Angelina.

    A British report says actors Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie have signed a deal to end their five-year relationship and split their US$205 million ($287.5 million) wealth. 

    Hollywood's A-list couple, who are not married, filed documents with a Los Angeles lawyer early this month to pave the way for a smooth separation, said the News Of The World yesterday, quoting unnamed sources.

    The couple is also determined to have joint custody of their six children. The couple have three biological children, Shiloh, Knox and Vivienne, and three adopted children, Maddox, Zahara and Pax. According to the same report, the children would live with Jolie, 34.

    Despite rumors of a split, Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie have remained mum on the state of their relationship, said a New York Times report.

    However, a source close to Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie issued a statement refuting claims that the couple is going separate ways, reported msnbc.com.
