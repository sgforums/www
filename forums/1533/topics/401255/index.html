    Minor-league model Honey Chen has continued to ride on superstar Jay Chou's popularity and revealed more details about their "secret" relationship.

    For her newfound fame, Honey was invited onto Taiwanese variety show Liang Chen Ji Shi's first episode, which aired on Monday. But despite claiming she did not want to discuss the mumbling singer-songwriter, the model opened up about the 'romance' further.

    Looking uncomfortable, Honey stuttered, "I feel bad to have hurt other people I don't want to think back on those 'unpleasant memories'."

    But she did exactly that, revisiting her story of the "secret room", alleging Jay had tried to kiss her against her wishes.

    Hosts Blackie Chen and Vincent Liang even phoned a "close friend" of hers on set to verify the secret affair.

    When asked if she has anything to say to Jay, Honey responded, "Sorry, I really didn't want to hurt anyone. I'm sorry that I exposed and couldn't protect our relationship."

    Her comments riled many of the guests on the show, who criticized her openly, leading to her manager and mother, who were on set, disrupting the studio recording several times.

    The model even boldly declared, when asked if she's afraid Jay might sue, "If he sues it'd be good. Then we can do lie-detector test."

    In the aftermath, Blackie, who is Jay's good friend and defended him during the interview, quit the series after appearing in just one episode.
