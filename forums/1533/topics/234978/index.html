    Start: Feb 3, '07 10:00am
    End: Feb 3, '07 4:00pm
    Location: Bloodbank@HSA
    On Saturday 3rd February 2007, our local Star Wars and Star Trek communities will come together for an inaugural blood donation event, which will then continue as a year long challenge to determine which group will be the winner of the coveted Blood Wars Challenge Trophy.

    Friendly competition aside, the objective of the event is to encourage more
    people to become blood donors and help the BloodBank@HSA meet the blood
    transfusion needs in Singapore. Every year, the BloodBank faces blood supply
    shortages, especially during the holiday season.

    In appreciation of blood donations received during the event, each blood
    donor will receive a FREE photo printout (Limited to 1st 100 donors) taken
    together with Star Wars and Star Trek characters who will be present
    throughout the event.

    Date --- Saturday 3 February 2007
    Time --- 10am to 4pm
    Venue --- Bloodbank@HSA
    http://www.hsa.gov.sg/html/consumer/ctm_donation_locations.html

    To find out if you meet the requirements to donate blood, please refer to
    the HSA website at
    -- http://www.hsa.gov.sg/html/consumer/ctm_how_to_be_a_donor.html

    If you are not able to donate blood, you can still be a part of the event by
    encouraging and accompanying your friends and family to come and donate
    blood during the event. Let us all do our part to help those in need of
    blood transfusion.

    More details and updates will be published on our website at
    www.bloodwars.sg, www.startrek.sg, and www.starwars.sg
