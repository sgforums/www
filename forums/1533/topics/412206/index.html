    We hate to shatter fan girls' hearts, but a romantic relationship with fans is not possible - at least "not for now", so said Jay Park.

    The former leader and member of Korean boyband 2PM is so busy jumpstarting his career that romance has to take a back seat.

    "Right now, I'm just kinda trying to do my thing with crew Art Of Movement (Seattle-based b-boy dance group), and to find my sound and place in this business and as a person. So... (a relationship is) not (possible) right now."

    Making his return as a solo artiste, Jay's career is taking flight - his debut single Nothin On You reached more than 70,000 copies in sales within the first week of its release.

    Jay was in town for his one-night only music showcase at Wave House Sentosa last Saturday. The singer-break-dancer, together with AOM, also participated in a dance exhibition battle with local dance group.
