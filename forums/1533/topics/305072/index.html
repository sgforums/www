    All eyes were on Hollywood star Eva Longoria last night as she arrived at London's Claridge's Hotel.

    But despite her thigh-skimming black frock and cheeky poses, it was her unsightly knobbly knees that really caught the attention of onlookers.


    Knees up: All eyes were on Eva Longoria as she arrived at Claridge's Hotel in London last night

    The petite star, who flew in for the UK premiere of her new film Over Her Dead Body, signed autographs before later heading out to Shoreditch House.

    Earlier this week, the star made a detour to Manchester's MEN Arena for the Spice Girls return to the north of England.

    She enjoyed a backstage reunion with her close friend Victoria Beckham.


    On show: The petite star's short black frock revealed her knobbly knees

    Despite her multi-million pound income and access to designer labels, Eva also made time to stop in at high street store Topshop during her stay.

    She was accompanied by her hairdresser to the stars Ken Paves and group of friends, who helped the star pick out a number of garments.

    The actress's new basketball star husband Tony Parker sat this trip out, but she now has a permanent reminder of him in the form of a tattoo.

    She recently had the word "nine" inked on the base of her neck, which is her husband's jersey number.
