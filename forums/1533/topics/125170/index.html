Presenter Lisa Ang and husband name daughter Ariel Faith

  • sbst275
    4 Apr 05, 08:34
    SINGAPORE : Channel NewsAsia presenter Lisa Ang and husband Ivan Rantung are on a natural high as they welcome an addition to their family.

    The little girl, Ariel Faith Rantung, was born on March 30 after four-and-half hours of labour.

    They named the baby Ariel after Ms Ang's all-time favourite character from The Little Mermaid, and faith, which has taken the couple pretty far in life.

    "A lot of people said that Ariel takes after Ivan but we've seen her smile once or twice and we see a hint of a dimple. So I guess she's a nice combination of him and me and it's too early to tell," said Ms Ang.

    "Well, this is the high life for me," added Mr Rantung. - CNA
