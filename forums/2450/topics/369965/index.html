Chocolate Wonton Kisses

  • Blackswan
    27 Jul 09, 17:35
    As promised in my An Evening With Celebrity Chef Bob Blumer, here's my 1st demo on Bob's creation - Chocolate Kiss Wontons but I prefer the name Chocolate Wonton Kisses.

    I've made some changes to the recipe. The original recipe calls for Hershey's caramel chocolate but I found them too sweet so I've substituted with Meiji's Melty Kiss instead. Also, I've used chunky peanut butter for extra crunch instead of the normal ones. I omitted using powdered sugar for dusting too but you can add these if you've a sweet tooth.

    8 wonton wrappers
    8 pcs of chocolates
    Half a banana
    Peanut butter (smooth or chunky)
    Powdered sugar for dusting (optional)
    Bowl of water
    Cooking oil
    1. Peel banana & cut into slices.
    2. Spread peanut butter on the center of the wonton skin & put a slice of banana on top.
    3. Place chocolate over it.
    4. Wet the outline of the wonton skin with water (acts as glue) & seal the edges well. (If you're not going to fry the wontons immediately, cover with a damp cloth & refrigerate).
    5. When oil is hot, deep-fry wontons till golden brown. Remove & place on paper towel to absorb excess oil. Dust powered sugar (optional) & you've got your Chocolate Wonton Kisses!

    These special wontons were wrapped by my son, with extra ingredients & toppings (ice-cream sprinkles & cocoa powder). If you've kids, try it with them. They'll love it!
