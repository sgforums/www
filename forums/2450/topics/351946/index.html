    EGG AND KETOLA SOUP

    Ingredients

    • 1 dessertspoon oil
    • 500ml water
    • 1 clove garlic, chopped
    • 3 shallots, sliced
    • 50g small prawns, shelled
    • pepper
    • 1/4 tsp salt
    • 150g ketola, peeled and cut into slices
    • 1 egg, beaten

    Method

    • Heat oil in a saucepan. Fry garlic and shallots until light brown
    • Add prawns and fry until cooked
    • Add water and salt. Bring to the boil
    • Add the ketola. Simmer until tender
    • Stir in the egg
    • Add pepper just before serving
    • Serve hot

    OMELETTE CURRY

    Serves 2

    Ingredients

    • 2 eggs, beaten with ¼ tsp salt
    • 2 tbsp oil
    • ¼ large onion, sliced
    • 1 red chilli, sliced, with seeds removed
    • 2 shallots, sliced
    • 1 dessertspoon curry paste
    • 200g grated coconut for 100ml thick milk, 250ml thin milk
    • ½ tsp salt

    Method

    • Heat 1 tbsp oil. Fry onion until light brown
    • Add beaten eggs and chilli. Cook until one side is light brown
    • Roll omelette and cut into think slices (2cm)
    • Heat remaining oil. Fry shallots until light brown
    • Add curry paste. Fry until fragrant
    • Add thin coconut milk and salt. Mix well and simmer for 10 min
    • Add thick coconut milk and bring to the boil. Simmer for 5 min
    • When gravy is thick, add the omelette slices. Cook for another minute
