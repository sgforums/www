Singapore Chicken Curry

  • Bangulzai
    13 Mar 09, 02:37

    Serves 2

    Ingredients

    • 2 chicken thighs, each cut into two
    • ½ large onion, sliced thinly
    • Pounded --> (4 shallots, 1 green chilli, 2 cloves garlic, ½cm ginger)
    • 1 tbsp curry powder
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • 1½ tbsp oil
    • 1 large ripe tomato, chopped
    • 200ml water
    • ½ tsp salt

    Garnish

    • Chopped coriander leaves

    Method

    • Mix pounded ingredients with curry powder and ground coriander
    • Heat oil in saucepan. Fry sliced onion until golden brown. Drain the fried onion and dish onto a plate
    • Add the pounded mixture and fry until fragrant
    • Add tomatoes and chicken. Stir for a while
    • Add water and salt. Bring to the boil. Cover and simmer until chicken is tender
    • Add the fried onion. Mix well
    • Garnish and serve with hot rice or bread
