    Grilled Tenderloin of Pork with Papaya Salsa

    Pork:
    2 1/2 tsps. chipotle powder
    1 tbsp. paprika
    1 1/2 cups fresh cilantro leaves
    1/2 tsp. fresh thyme
    3/4 cup lime juice
    1 1/2 tsps. grated orange rind
    1 1/2 tsps. olive oil
    1 3 lb. pork tenderloin
    2 oven-roasted garlic cloves, sliced
    Papaya salsa, recipe follows

    Mix chipotle powder, paprika, cilantro, thyme, lime juice, orange rind and oil in blender or food processor.
    Stud pork with garlic; coat with rub. Grill till internal temperature of 160 degrees. Let stand 5 minutes.
    Slice on diagonal and serve with salsa.

    Papaya salsa:
    1 1/2 cups jalapeno pepper jelly
    3 ripe papaya, diced
    6 tbsps. cilantro, mint, chopped
    6 tbsps. mint, chopped
    3/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
    3 tbsps. yellow pepper, finely diced
    3 tbsps. red pepper, finely diced
    3 tbsps. lime juice
    3 tbsps. lime zest

    Heat jelly until melted. Off heat, mix in remaining ingredients. Let stand 1 hour.
    Makes 12 servings pork, and 3 cups salsa.
