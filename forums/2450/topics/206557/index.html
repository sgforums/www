    Abobong Manok (Chicken Adobo)

    Yield: 6 Servings

    Ingredients

    3 lb chicken pieces
    1/2 c soy sauce
    3/4 c vinegar, white
    1 ea garlic head
    2 ea bay leaves
    1/2 tb peppercorns
    1 salt; to taste

    Instructions

    Boil Chicken with soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves and peppercorns and simmer for half an hour. Remove the chicken pieces from the pot and broil them in a pan for 10 minutes. Let the sauce in the pot boil until it is reduced by half. Add salt to taste. Cover the broiled chicken pieces with the sauce. Serve hot. Serve with rice. Note: I find this dish doesn't need any salt as the soy sauce is salty enough.
