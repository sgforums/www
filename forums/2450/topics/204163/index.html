    kidney bean, pumpkin and risoni soup

    serves 6

    1 tablespoon olive oil
    1 medium onion, finely sliced
    2 cloves garlic, crushed
    2 bacon rashers, finely sliced .
    1/2 small butternut pumpkin, finely diced
    2 x 410g cans chicken consomme
    1 litre (4 cups) water
    2 x 300g cans kidney beans, rinsed, drained
    3/4 cup risoni pasta
    salt and pepper to taste
    1/4 cup finely shredded fresh basil
    crusty bread to serve
    extra fresh basil leaves to serve

    1. Heat oil in a large pan. Add onion, garlic, bacon and pumpkin; cook, stirring, until onion is soft.
    2. Add consomme, water, beans and pasta; bring to the boil. Simmer, uncovered, for 20 minutes or until pasta is tender. Season with salt and pepper; stir in basil.
    3. Serve soup with crusty bread; garnish with extra fresh basil leaves.
