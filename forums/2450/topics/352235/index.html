Chicken in Tomato Curry

  • Bangulzai
    18 Mar 09, 15:50

    INGREDIENTS

    • Chicken - 1.4kg (3lb), cut into pieces
    • Cooking oil
    • Curry powder - 2 tbsp, mixed to a paste with 3 tbsp water
    • Tomatoes - 4, quartered
    • Red chillies - 4, slit lengthwise
    • Green chillies - 4, slit lengthwise
    • Coconut - 115g (4oz), grated and mixed with 225ml (1 cup) water, squeezed and strained for coconut milk

    Rempah

    • Shallots - 115g (4oz), peeled
    • Candelnuts - 2
    • Lemon grass - 1 stalk, sliced
    • Galangal - 4 slices, peeled
    • Ginger - 4 slices ,peeled
    • Red chillies - 4
    • Dried chillies - 10
    • Bird's eye chillies (cili padi) - 10, optional

    Marinade

    • Salt - 1 tsp
    • MSG - 1 tsp, optional
    • Pepper - 1 tsp

    Seasoning

    • Tomato ketchup - 2 tbsp
    • Salt - 2 tsp
    • MSG - 1/2 tsp, optional
    • Sugar - 2 tsp
    • Lime juice - 2 tbsp

    METHOD

    • Pound rempah ingredients to a fine paste. Set aside
    • Mix the chicken with the marinade ingredients and leave aside for 1/2 hour
    • Add cooking oil to a heated pan and stir-fry the chicken until brown. Remove to a plate
    • In a clean pan, heat another 4 tbsp oil. Add rempah paste and curry paste and fry over a moderate heat until oil separates
    • Add the cooked chicken, tomatoes, red and green chillies, seasoning ingredients and half of the coconut milk. Stir for 5 minutes. Pour in the rest of the coconut milk and allow curry to boil
    • Reduce the heat. Simmer covered until chicken is tender, about 1/2 hour. Serve.
