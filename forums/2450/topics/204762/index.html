    Fish and potato curry

    Preparation time: 15 minutes
    Cooking time: 15 minutes
    Serves 4

    1 tbs vegetable oil
    2 tbs green curry paste 2 brown onions, diced
    2 potatoes, peeled, cubed, part-cooked
    1 green chilli, deseeded, sliced
    2 cloves garlic, chopped 1 red capsicum, sliced
    1 zucchini, sliced diagonally 400g can coconut milk 500g diced
    boneless fish fillets
    Cooked jasmine rice
    spring onion to serve

    Method
    Heat oil in large saucepan. Add curry paste, onion, potatoes, chilli and garlic. Cook 3 mins. Add capsicum, zucchini and coconut milk. Cover. Cook 5 mins or until vegetables are just tender. Add diced fish. Cover. Cook 5 mins. Serve with rice, garnished with sliced onions.
