    US style fried rice

    1/3 cup brown rice
    1 egg, lightly beaten
    1/2 teaspoon ginger, grated
    1 clove garlic, crushed
    60g lean leg ham, chopped
    1/2 cup baby corn kernels
    80g green beans, chopped
    1/4 red capsicum, chopped
    1 spring onion, chopped
    2 teaspoons soy sauce
    2 teaspoons hoisin sauce
    3 teaspoons rice wine vinegar

    1. Cook rice according to instructions on the packet.

    2. Heat a small non-stick frypan coated with cooking spray to medium. Add egg; cook for 15 seconds or until set. Remove from pan; chop roughly.

    3. Re-spray pan. Add ginger, garlic, ham, corn, beans and capsicum; stir-fry for 3 minutes or until vegetables are tender.

    4. Add rice, egg, spring onion, sauces and vinegar; stir-fry until hot and serve. hint For even cooking, chop beans and capsicum to the same size.
