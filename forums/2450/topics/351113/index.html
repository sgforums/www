Fish Curry I

  • Bangulzai
    10 Mar 09, 13:54

    Ingredients

    • 2 small ikan selar, cleaned
    • 2 tbsp oil
    • 4 shallots, 5 dried chillies. 1 clove garlic, 1 slice ginger, ¼ stalk serai (pound altogether)
    • 1 tbsp curry powder
    • 1 brinjal, cut into 3 cm pieces
    • 2 ladies' fingers (okra), cut into 3 cm pieces
    • 150g grated coconut for 200ml coconut milk
    • 1 tbsp tamarind, 60ml water (mix together)

    • ½

      tsp salt

    Necessary Equipments

    Frying Pan, Muslin Bag, Paring Knife, Pounder

    Instructions

    1. Heat oil. Fry pounded ingredients until fragrant
    2. Add curry powder and fry for 1 minute
    3. Add brinjal, ladies' fingers, fish, coconut milk and salt. Bring to the boil
    4. Simmer until vegetables are cooked
    5. Add tamarind juice. Bring to the boil again
