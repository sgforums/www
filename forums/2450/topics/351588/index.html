Potato Meat Curry

  • Bangulzai
    13 Mar 09, 19:31

    Serves 2

    Ingredients

    • 175g meat, diced
    • Pounded --> (½ large onion, 2 cloves garlic, 1 red chilli, ½cm sliced ginger)
    • 2 tbsp white vinegar
    • 1 dessertspoon curry powder
    • ½ tsp tumeric powder
    • ¼ tsp salt
    • 2 tbsp oil
    • ½ large onion, sliced
    • 1 small potato, diced
    • 175 ml water

    Garnish

    • Chopped coriander leaves
    • Fried onion

    Method

    • Mix the pounded ingredients, vinegar, curry powder, tumeric powder and salt
    • Heat oil in a saucepan. Fry the sliced onion until light brown
    • Add pounded mixture. Fry until fragrant
    • Add meat, potato and water. Bring to the boil. Cover the saucepan and simmer until meat is tender
    • Garnish and serve hot with rice
