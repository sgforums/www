    *APPLE PIE*

    2 Galloms Apple Cider
    1 Bottle Everclear(r) (I've been told you can sub any vodka here, it just
    won't pack the same punch"
    1 cup Honey
    1 cup Sugar
    7 Stick Cinnamon

    Mix all above ingreadients in large container, pour into covered glass
    containers and let set for two months ocassionally shaking. After the two
    month time period, and when ready to enjoy, do not filter drink. Shake
    container and pour. This is an especially good drink for those cold winter
    nights, and will warm you to the core.
