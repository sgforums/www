    CRAZY SMOOTHIE

    Ingredients needed:

    - 1 cup seeded, quartered mangoes
    - 1 cup stemmed, halved strawberries
    - 1 banana, quartered
    - 1 cup plain yogurt
    - 1/2 cup orange or pineapple juice
    - 1/2 cup dark rum
    - 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
    - 1/4 cup coconut flakes

    Place the mangoes, strawberries and banana on a small baking sheet in the freezer to partially freeze, about 15 minutes.

    Place 4 ice cubes in a blender and crush. Add the cold fruit, yogurt, juice, rum, and lime juice and process on high speed until frothy and thick. Pour into 2 tall glasses and top each with 2 tablespoons of coconut.

    Serve immediately.

    Yield: 2 servings
