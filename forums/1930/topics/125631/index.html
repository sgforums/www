    RULES AND REGULATIONS for the Trios League @ Hougang Superbowl

    ok,. there is about 4 pages worth of rules n regulations, BUT, i'm not gona type everything out! hahaha.. i'll juz list out the main important ones that may be different from other leagues.

    The league comprises of 12 teams and shall run for 22 weeks on a 2 round robin system. The league runs on a 16 point system.

    1 point for each game won by bowlers with hdcaps 9 games (9 points)
    1 point for the bowlers series with hdcaps 3 games (3 points)
    1 point for teams games with hdcaps 3 games (3points)
    1 point for teams total series with hdcaps (1point)

    total 16 points

    [Round Robin]
    The team with highest pinfalls every week (with hdcap) will get 12 points. 2nd highest 11 points and so on. if a tie occurs the points will be divided simultaneously. The last team will get 1 point.
    Total overall points for standing position will be 28 points.

    *Important for latecomers*
    No player can join in after the 4th frame. And the 1st few frames will have zero score, until the point that he starts bowling. so, if he joins in on the 3rd frame, "frame one 0-, frame two 0-, frame three *watever he bowled*" got it? so, DON'T BE LATE!!!!

    The blind score is 120 pins scratch. And $15 must be paid even if there is a blind.

    [Roll Offs]
    Each team is allowed 3 roll off per round and must be bowled 6pm before the scheduled night, after which will be considered a walkover. There shall be no roll off during the last 2 weeks of the league season. League matches shall be postponed during all holiday eve and public holidays.

    3 walkovers considered withdrawal from the league!

    [Qualifiers for prize awards]    In order to qualify for individual and team prizes, a bowler must have bowled:-
    Individual High Game/Series = 15 games
    High Average = 21 games
    *Master event = 12 games

    * the rules for the masters will be posted up next time. anyway, there will only be ONE master event at the end of the league. (10 games series)

    okay, PERFECT GAME AWARD, will only be claimed ONCE a year. Roll off matches are not eligible for house awards, but during masters, it will be recognised. During first 4 weeks of the league, anyone who hits 300 will not be recognised.

    alright,.. thats all for now. if you guys have any more enquires, juz post it up here.. okie? Thanks!
