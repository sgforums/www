    25.0 Masters Event
    25.1 There will be an Individual Masters Event (2 divisions) at the end of the League. The top 50% of the bowlers in the League (based on week #24) will be categorized under the "Open" Division and the remaining 50% will be categorized under the "Graded" Division.
    25.2 All bowlers must bowl a minimum of 24 games throughout the 24 weeks to be eligible for the Masters Event. The average to be used for the "Open" and "Graded" Division will be decided after the 24th week of the League.
    25.3 League Handicap will be used for MQ and the Masters Finals. 50% for male bowlers and 67% for female bowlers.
    25.3 In order to qualify for the Masters Event (both divisions), bowlers will have to play an MQ. MQ tries will begin from the start of the 25th week. Only the top 27 bowlers from the Open division MQ and the top 27 bowlers from the Graded division MQ will proceed to play in the Masters Final.
    25.4 There will be no restriction to the number of MQ tries for the Masters Event.
