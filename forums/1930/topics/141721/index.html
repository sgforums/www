    Hello folks,

    Welcome to the 3rd edition of the F.O.S. Challenge, hosted by F.O.S's very own James Koh (JK), to be held in Tampines SAFRA this time around. Special thanks to Daryl for laying most of the groundwork!

    Venue: Tampines SAFRA
    Date: 17 July 2005 (Sunday)
    Time: Check in 0930 hrs, Roll Off 1000 hrs
    Cost per pax: $42
    Number of games: 6 (not including playoffs)

    Estimated Pax: 35 pax

    Prize structure: (To be confirmed, but roughly in this region)

    Champion: $120
    Runner Up: $80
    Semi Finalists: $60 (x2)
    Quarter Finalists: $40 (x4)
    Round of 12: $25 (x4)
    13th place onwards: TBC.. depending on remaining funds

    High Game Men: $20
    High Game Women: $20

    Trophies will be provided for Champ, RU and SF.

    ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Format:

    From the 6 game qualifying total, top 12 will advance to the playoff rounds, with top 4 having a first round bye.

    First Round

    8th qualifier vs 9th qualifier (Grp 1)
    5th qualifier vs 12th qualifier (Grp 2)
    6th qualifier vs 11th qualifier (Grp 3)
    7th qualifier vs 10th qualifier (Grp 4)

    Quarter Finals

    Winner Grp 1 vs 1st qualifier (QF 1)
    Winner Grp 2 vs 4th qualifier (QF 2)
    Winner Grp 3 vs 3rd qualifier (QF 3)
    Winner Grp 4 vs 2nd qualifier (QF 4)

    Semi Finalists

    Winner QF1 vs Winner QF 2 (SF1)
    Winner QF3 vs Winner QF 4 (SF2)

    Finals

    Winner SF1 vs Winner SF2

    -----------------------------------------------------------------------
