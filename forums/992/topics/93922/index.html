    I think most couples will usually separate the expenses between themselves.
    eg the husband pays for everything concerning the car, the wife pays for food / utilities / household stuff etc.
    Otherwise, they might open a joint account that each will put in a certain sum every month for all general expenses.

    In such cases, then I don't think the husband needs to "give" the wife any money.

    On a side note, money is sometimes the cause of marital problems. Better to resolve and state clearly who pays for what and how it is paid etc before a couple gets married.
