Simple Ways to be Romantic

  • InnoHippo

    Make a decision to be romantic.

    Call your spouse during the day.

    Leave a love note for your mate to find.

    Suggest going for a walk together and hold hands.

    Remember to give a lingering kiss for no reason at all.

    Say "I love you."

    Watch a classic romantic movie together.

    Send a romantic card.

    Plan a candlelight dinner.

    Pick a flower to put in a bud vase.

    Write a loveletter.

    Nibble on your spouse's ear and whisper something sweet.

    Schedule an evening to have a date with one another.
