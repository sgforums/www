    M1 And Research In Motion Launch UMTS-enabled 8707v BlackBerry Handset In Singapore - Quick Facts

    Thursday, July 20, 2006; Posted: 01:09 AM

    (RTTNews) - Singapore's mobile and IDD service provider M1 and Research In Motion (RIMM | charts | news | PowerRating)(RIM.TO) announced the launch of UMTS-enabled 8707v BlackBerry handset in Singapore.

    RIM said that its BlackBerry 8707v enables users to speak on the phone, simultaneously sending and receiving email, browsing Internet or using other data applications, even while the handset is being used as a modem.

    RIM noted that BlackBerry 8707v functions on 2100 MHz UMTS networks as well as supports 850/900/1800/1900 MHz GSM/GPRS networks to provide roaming coverage.
