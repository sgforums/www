NCS takes delivery of new cyber security operations center

    Cybertrust Delivers State-of-the-Art Security Operations Centre to NCS; Brings New Security Competencies to Singapore

    SINGAPORE, (BSW) - Cybertrust(R), the global information security specialist, today announced the provisioning of a new Security Operations Centre (SOC) for the NCS Group, a leading regional information technology and communications engineering service provider in the Asia Pacific and Middle East regions. With best practices and methodology supplied by Cybertrust, the SOC will enable the delivery of an integrated suite of information security services to NCS customers. Cybertrust will also partner NCS for the provisioning of Managed Security Services (MSS) to NCS customers, which will be delivered from the new NCS SOC.

    The NCS SOC is based on Cybertrust's security operations best practices and experiences. Built in compliance with strict local regulatory guidelines and industry-wide benchmarks, the NCS SOC is a state-of-the-art facility that will allow NCS to deliver a full-spectrum service portfolio to organizations requiring real-time monitoring and management of their security devices and applications. Cybertrust worked closely with NCS to develop the SOC procedures, processes and policies, including training of NCS staff on operating and maintaining the SOC. The NCS SOC will be fully run by NCS with services provided by NCS.

    Cybertrust will partner with NCS in delivering MSS to its customers, tapping into the expertise and reach of one of Asia's leading vendor-independent IT and communications engineering solutions providers. Established in the early 1980s as the principal IT solutions provider to the Singapore government in the computerisation of the Singapore public administration, NCS has helped implement over 1,500 large-scale, mission-critical, and multi-platform projects. With some 3,000 staff in Asia Pacific, NCS boasts the region's largest pool of Certified IT Project Managers (CITPM).

    "Managed Security Services are designed to allow organizations to cost-effectively outsource their IT security management, monitoring and response needs by leveraging the knowledge of Internet security experts to protect the value of networked assets and infrastructure," said Jeremy Pizzala, Cybertrust's vice president and general manager, Asia. "Through NCS, Cybertrust will help customers identify their most critical assets, delivering an ongoing strategy that ensures greater visibility and control of information security risk and demonstrating ongoing regulatory compliance."

    Foo Nian Chou, Chief, NCS Infrastructure Management and Solutions Division said: "We are glad to partner with Cybertrust to enhance managed security services to the market. The NCS SOC is a state-of-the-art facility that conforms to strict regulatory guidelines, and will be an important component in the overall delivery of managed services. Customers can look forward to an efficient and cost-effective solution to ensure that their information systems are properly managed and secured, freeing up resources to focus on core business initiatives."

    In collaboration with Cybertrust, NCS MSS will provide the following benefits to help our customers improve their overall security posture:

    -- An efficient and cost-effective solution for securing business data and identities, managing compliance issues and avoiding breaches

    -- Ability to efficiently prove and effectively maintain compliance with industry standards

    -- Consistent measurement and analysis of information security controls

    -- Proactive policies and processes to help effectively evaluate and respond to threats

    -- Identification and remediation of vulnerabilities within business systems

    -- Better information security for organizations than could be achieved alone

    -- Assurance that information systems are being properly managed and secured, freeing up staff to focus on core business initiatives
