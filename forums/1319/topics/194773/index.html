    With the ez-link card, you enjoy transfer rebates for the first, second and third transfers on a journey when all conditions for transfer are met.

    The rebate for trunk bus, industrial services, feeder bus services and intra-town/Town-link service, the MRT or LRT transfers are :

    25Â¢ for Adult, Senior Citizen, NSF and Tertiary Student ez-link card users; and

    10Â¢ for Child ez-link card and School Smartcard users.

    The same ez-link card must be used on two successive portions of your journey to enjoy transfer rebate.

    To enjoy transfer rebate between the LRT and MRT, or vice versa, passengers must not exit from the paid areas of both system.

    The following do not qualify for transfer rebate:

    MRT to MRT transfer does not qualify for transfer rebate but does not affect eligibility for subsequent transfer to bus

    Transfer between MRT and LRT

    Transfer from a bus to another of the same service number

    Transfer to/from Sentosa Bus, City Shuttle Services, Night Services, C & CT services

    If Bus Concession Pass (BCP), Train Concession Pass (TCP) or Hybrid Concession Pass (HCP) is used

    Fourth transfer onwards
