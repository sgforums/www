    Children Should'nt Play With Dead Things.

    This movie was introduced to me by a fellow horror fiend who happens to have really good taste in horror flicks.
    In case you did'nt get the point from the title alone...Children really should'nt play with dead things.
    Alan Ormsby, playing the character of "Alan" the dick-head, egotistic- maniacle director, will surely make you want to rip your eyes and ears out as well as his. The sweet release of watching this character die off might be the strongest hope throughout this bizzar flick.
    There is a strong sence of the Evil Dead in this movie. More like The Evil Dead meets Animal House. Oddly enough, the director of this haunt, Bob Clark, also directed Porky's, Porkey's 2 , Black Christmas, and A Christmas Story.
    So there is a bit of dark humor that comes along with the killings and what not.
    Anya Ormsby, playing the role of Anya, is excellent at what she does. This woman can make you feel whatever it is she's feeling...Terror...Insanity....Euphoria...you name it, she'll send it.
    From the get go the movie will grab ya, no pun intended, hahaha....some people might think it to be a bit slow at first, but again the sheer hatred that will develope for Alan just might sustain your blood lust until the killing starts.
