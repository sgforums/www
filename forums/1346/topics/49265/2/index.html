    Release Date : 3 July 2003

    In the rundown Bangkok railway station, ace vampires slayers Reeve (Ekin Cheng) and Lila (Josie Ho) prepare to take on a pack of blood sucking killers, led by The Duke (Mickey Hardy). In order to become invincible, the duke need to drink the blood of the fifth prince Kazaf (Edison Chen). Though born a vampire, Kazaf never had to hunt for food, he and his manservant, Prada (Anthony Wong), receive blood and cash shipment from Kazaf's father, the vampire king. By chance, Kazaf meet Reeve's younger sister, Helen (Charlene Choi) and soon make a deep impression on him. On arrival at Helen's apartment, Reeve found that he has got a new partner Gypsy (Gillian Chung), a girl of Helen's age. Reeve and Gypsy track the vampire pack to Hong Kong's bar area where a fierce fight ensures and both sustain injuries. Helen takes the weakened Kazaf to steal blood from a hospital and were discovered by The Duke. They are saved by a kung-fu fighting ambulance driver, Jackie (Jackie Chan). He subsequently becomes a mentor to Helen. The Duke arrives in Hong Kong with the rest of his legion, and the final battles begins...
