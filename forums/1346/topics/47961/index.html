    So, the idea is to convince these guys you're not worth it.

    (C) HOW

    Several defense mechanisms he taught us are:

    (1) If someone is following behind you on a street or in a garage or with you in an elevator or staircase, look them in the face and ask them questions like " what time is it? My friends are supposed to be here 10 minutes ago." etc.

    This is to show that your friends are on the way, so it's not worthwhile for him to take the risk.

    Secondly, it shows that you're a CONFIDENT person and you're not the type that look timid and can be easily taken over.

    Thirdly, now you've seen their face and could identify them in a line-up if they are caught. So, with this you lose appeal as a target to these rapists.

    Again, they are looking for an EASY target.

    This instructor was told by a woman in his class of defence, that she used the underarm pinch on a guy who was trying to date-rape her and he was so upset as she broke through the skin and tore out muscle strands!! - the guy needed stitches.

    (6) When the guy puts his hands up to you, grab his first two fingers and bend them back
    with as much pressure as possible.

    The instructor did this to me without using much pressure, and I ended up on my knees and both knuckles cracked audibly.

    IN ALL CASES, DO NOT PANIC!!

    By the way, it is also worth it to leave scratch marks on the rapist on any part of his body for easy identifcation if he is caught.
