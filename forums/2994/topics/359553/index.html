    Events - 23rd to 31st May 2009

    International Museum Day (IMD)

    Since 2006, the National Heritage Board (NHB) has celebrated the IMD in Singapore through events and activities at the NHB’s museums during the last two weeks of May.

    This year's theme is 'Holiday Fun at Home', focusing on family and children to encourage a regular museum-going culture among families.

       IMD 09 will feature a Family Carnival at National Museum.

    A slew of activities for children include puppet and storytelling performances, craft workshops and an interactive playground for young children.

    There will be a Chinese culture medley at the Asian Civilisations Museum, healthy ice-cream making at Healthzone, adventure trails at Baba House and the National Archives of Singapore, and the 'I Can Guide'programme at the Peranakan Museum, which trains children to be museum guides for their own peers.

       Besides all NHB museums, the Marina Barrage,the NEWater Visitors Centre, the Malay Heritage Centre and the Red Dot Museum will be open to the public - free of charge - on 31 May.

    Senior citizens have free entry to all NHB's museums and the Malay Heritage Centre on the 25th May.

       Open House Days :

    24 May - Raffles Museum of Biodiversity Research

    30-31 May - the Asian Civilisations Museum

    31 May - The National Museum of Singapore, the Mint Museum of Toys, the Malay Heritage Centre, the Singapore Philatelic Museum, the Singapore Art Museum, 8Q Sam, the contemporary art wing of SAM, the Marina Barrage,the Malay Heritage Centre, the NEWater Visitors Centre and the Red Dot Museum
