    Line-up for SPH Gift of Music 2009

    Thu, Mar 19, 2009
    AsiaOne

    Singapore's very own musicians will be taking turns to perform on the stages of Gift of Music (GOM), an annual music fiesta organised by Singapore Press Holdings.

    Music lovers can enjoy free performances held at the heartlands, parks and campuses (denoted in green), as well as the Esplanade Outdoor Theatre (pink) and the Paragon Shopping Centre (purple). The line-up at Paragon is packed every weekend from April to May.

    There's something for everyone, even on festive periods like National Day and Christmas.

    Date: 20 March 2009, 6pm
    Venue: Nanyang Auditorium
