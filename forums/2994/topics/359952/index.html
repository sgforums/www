Contest To Express Yourself Thru Art

  • Sgalistan
    14 May 09, 15:12

    Julia Gabriel Centre for Learning is running a contest now for people to express themselves through art. There are three categories:

    1) Performance Art - could be anything from dance, song, drama, etc

    2) Visual Arts - fine art, graphics, photography or even design and multimedia

    3) Literary Arts - creative writing or factual writing

    The contest is open to anyone of any age and you can submit your entries as an individual or as a group. Deadline is 30 June 09.

    The prize is $500 cash! Check out www.juliagabriel.com for more details.
