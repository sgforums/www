    Inter To Offer Mancini For Real Madrid's Wesley Sneijder – Report

    The Nerazzurri could be set to offload their Brazilian winger in return for the Dutch international.

     

    Inter’s Mancini has failed to live up to the expectations that surrounded his move from Roma at the commencement of the season.

    He has struggled to perform well on a consistent basis and thus has rarely featured for the Nerazzurri this term under the club’s new boss, Jose Mourinho.

    With this in mind, the reigning Italian champions are keen to offload their Brazilian flop, and according to the latest reports from Mundo Deportivo, the technical director at Inter, Marco Branca, has spotted a potential replacement at     Real Madrid.

    Wesley Sneijder is the man that is currently being tracked by the San Siro giants, and he is seen as an ideal addition to Mourinho’s squad.

    Inter are confident of striking a deal with the Spanish side, as they have been long-term admirers of Mancini, even back in his Roma days.

    Neither club have confirmed these details, but it looks set to be an extremely interesting development.

    Inter have also been linked with Rafael van der Vaart, Sneijder's compatriot and club-mate.
