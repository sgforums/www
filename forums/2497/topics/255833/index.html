    Arsenal join in the pursuit of Bale

    Arsenal have joined Tottenham and Manchester United in the race to sign Gareth Bale. Southampton last night began talks with the Wales left-back's representatives and although they are prepared to offer the 17-year-old a new contract, initial indications suggest he will decide to make the step up to the Premiership immediately.

    Manchester United and Tottenham made offers for Bale in January but his Saints deal expires at the end of next season and after nurturing him since the age of nine, they will not want to risk losing him for a significantly reduced fee.
