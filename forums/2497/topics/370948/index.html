    Manchester United star Michael Owen splashes the cash - spending £170,000 on Ferrari's stunning new supercar

     

    Michael Owen will soon be driving around town in a Ferrari California

     

    Striker Michael Owen has celebrated his move to Manchester United by splashing out £170,000 on the latest Ferrari supercar.

    Owen, 29, will pick up the 193mph California model later this month, and will sell his old Ferrari with only 9,900 miles on the clock.

    The star's £222,000 599 GTB Fiorano F1 - up for grabs for £149,950 - is the same model Cristiano Ronaldo wrote off earlier this year in a tunnel near Manchester airport.

    A United insider said: "Michael ordered the new California, which is being fitted to his specification.

    'It is a different shape to his old Ferrari and although it is not as expensive it is equally impressive.

    'He wanted a car to celebrate his move. I think he wanted to treat himself.'

    The California, which has Ferrari's first retractable hard-top roof, was launched at last year's Paris Motor Show and is sold out till 2012.

     

    Owen has been in impressive form for Manchester United in their pre-season friendlies
