    Liverpool sign 18-year-old French defender Chris Mavinga

    As they did last summer with the shock signing of David Ngog from Paris Saint Germain, Liverpool have returned to the Parisian giants for another French starlet, defender Chris Mavinga.

    The 18-year-old, who has played for France at Under-18 level, has signed a three-year deal at the Merseyside giants, according to France Football.

    Mavinga turned 18 on May 26; he has not made a first-team appearance for PSG but is highly-rated and can play at full back or as a central defender.

    In April Arsenal were linked with a move for Mavinga with the Gunners France-based scout Gilles Grimandi having watched him play in a PSG reserve game.
