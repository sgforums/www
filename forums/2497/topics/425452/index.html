    Mancini blasts ‘stupid’ Balo

    PUT BOOT IN ... Balotelli saw red for this awful challenge on Popov
    .
    .
    .
    ROBERTO MANCINI last night insisted 'stupid' Mario Balotelli had cost Manchester City their Europa League dream.
    .

    The Italian bad boy saw red in the 36th minute for an outrageous chest-high challenge on Dinamo Kiev's Goran Popov.

    It gave City a mountain to climb after losing 2-0 in the first leg in Ukraine.

    And although they pulled one back through Aleksandar Kolarov, the Eastlands men could not turn the last-16 tie around.

    City chief Mancini said: "It's a problem because Mario can be a fantastic player.

    "But when he does stupid things like tonight it's difficult for me, for him and the team. Am I angry? What do you think?

    "I didn't see the foul but I'm disappointed because this was a difficult game and you can't have a stupid red card like this.

    "It was tough but if it had been 11 v 11 we would have scored two or three goals.

    "With one player less and two goals down it became very difficult. But the other 10 were fantastic for 60 minutes.

    "Is he a frustrated man? I hope so because with 11 we would have gone into the quarter-finals."

    Mancini hinted he may axe Balotelli from his squad for Sunday's showdown at Chelsea.

    The pair are likely to have talks at Eastlands today but Balotelli's red-mist moment left supporters fuming.

    And there were claims he clashed with a group of Kiev fans outside the ground after the game.

    City defender Vincent Kompany added: "It's difficult to comment on what happened but with 10 men it is difficult."
