    PHIL SCOLARI has told Chelsea to sell Didier Drogba. The new Blues boss wants funds to rebuild the squad and SunSport can reveal he has told owner Roman Abramovich the Ivory Coast hitman can go.

     

    Chelsea want £22million for Drog, with AC Milan desperate to get him after missing out on Emmanuel Adebayor. And Big Phil has also offered Florent Malouda to Barcelona as part of an £18m bid for striker Samuel Eto’o.

     

    Scolari officially started work yesterday and, having landed Deco on Monday, aims to shake up the squad. Chelsea are still keeping tabs on Ronaldinho, who Milan also want. But Barca team-mate Eto’o, 27, has become top target.

     

    He wants £160,000- a-week but Blues are confident signing-on and loyalty bonuses will reduce his wages.
