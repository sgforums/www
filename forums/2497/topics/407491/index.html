Report: City's Onuoha Heads List Of Departures

    Report: Manchester City's Onuoha Heads List Of Departures

    Manchester City defender Nedum Onuoha is expected to leave Eastlands this week, according to the Daily Mail.

    Premier League,Nedum Onuoha,Manchester City(Getty Images)

    Roberto Mancini is widely expected to sell a host of players, including Onuoha and Stephen Ireland, as he trims his current squad to 25 players, while also tying up deals to bring Aston Villa midfielder James Milner and Inter Milan’s Mario Ballotelli to the club.

    Sunderland are favourites to sign Onouha, while Ireland will be used as a makeweight in a £26 million deal involving Milner that is expected to be completed in the next 48 hours.

    Restrictions placed upon Premier League clubs this season could also mean strikers Roque Santa Cruz and Craig Bellamy leave the club, especially if Mancini completes the signing of Balotelli or includes Slovakia’s 20-year-old winger Vladimir Weiss in his squad.
