Walcott: We Will Now Be Feared

  • omgukilledkenny
    23 Oct 08, 01:56

    Theo Walcott believes that Arsenal's attacking prowess will now leave European teams quaking in their boots.

    On a night of festival football Arsenal and Fenerbahce produced one of the matches of the night with and end to end display of attacking fluency.

    Emmanuel Adebayor opened Arsenal's account within ten minutes and moments later Theo Walcott doubled the Gunners' advantage.

    Speaking after the game, Walcott told the Telegraph: "All the teams will look in Europe and see the 5-2 win, Arsenal away, with a young team and I think the other teams will be scared to play us.

    "That's the impression we want to give out to all the other teams. We do that and we have always got them on the back foot and hopefully we can just keep performing like that," he added.

    "It was absolutely brilliant. The main thing was getting the three points and everyone got their jobs right and we scored five goals and it was nice to see us taking our chances... This young team has had a lot of experience at Champions League level now.

    "Everyone got their jobs right - we studied Fenerbahce and how they play," Walcott continued.

    "We knew their fans would be right behind them but we quietened them down straight away by scoring two very early goals and it was a brilliant performance."
