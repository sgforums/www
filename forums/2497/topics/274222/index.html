    Valencia have made an offer for Hamburg midfielder Rafael van der Vaart.

    The Dutchman, who was linked with a move to Real Madrid earlier this summer, is contracted to Hamburg until 2010.

    However, Valencia are keen to bring van der Vaart to the Mestalla and have bid 14 million euros (Â£9.5million) for the player.

    Van der Vaart is eager for the move to go ahead and admits it would be disappointing if he had to stay in Germany.

    "If I had to stay in Hamburg it would be painful."

    Hamburg have confirmed there was an offer but insist van der Vaart will not be allowed to leave.

    "In this particular case money is of no importance," said sporting director Dietmar Beiersdorfer.
