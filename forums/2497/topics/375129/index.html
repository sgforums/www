    Barcelona have raided Olympique Lyon for SEVEN-YEAR-OLD Kais.

    The youngster was signed by Lyon after being spotted playing for FC Gerland, however, after two days of trials in May he was offered a place at Barca's academy.

    Kais will move to Barcelona once he finishes his school year.
