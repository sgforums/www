    Arsenal ace Cesc: I will return to Barcelona ... when I retire

    Arsenal captain Cesc Fabregas says he's thinking of returning to Barcelona - when he retires.

    And although the Spain midfielder admitted he was fed up with the English climate, he said London would remain his home for now.

    The Gunners captain told Catalunya Radio: "I've never hidden that one day I would return to Barcelona.

    "I have been a lifelong Barca fan. For one reason or another had to leave, but you never know what can happen.

    "I do not need anybody to tell me that Barcelona is the best city in the world to live or about the quality of life there. I know that perfectly. I don't like the London weather but as a city it is spectacular. But yes, when I retire I will live in Barcelona."
