Carling Cup Quarter Final Draw

  • omgukilledkenny
    30 Oct 10, 23:09

    Man United drawn against West Ham in Carling Cup. Gunners host Wigan

    Manchester United will travel to West Ham in the quarter-finals of the Carling Cup as it chases a third successive title.

    Saturday's draw also saw Arsenal handed a home match against Wigan. Unlike in previous seasons, the Gunners are using many of their top players in the competition as they try to win their first trophy since 2005.

    A Birmingham club is guaranteed a spot in the semifinals after local rivals Birmingham and Aston Villa were paired in the draw.

    Second-tier club Ipswich, the only side from outside the top flight remaining in the competition, will host West Bromwich Albion.

    Games will be played in the week beginning Nov. 29.
