    Former Inter boss Roberto Mancini would love to be Bernd Schuster’s successor at Real Madrid.

    Coaches used to respect their colleagues in the past, and refused to speculate about their future, but nowadays, stating you are interested in a position even if there is still a manager in charge has become a habit.

    And Mancini is the latest tactician that has expressed his attraction to a coaching role, at Real Madrid, while the under-fire Schuster is still there.

    When asked about Real’s possible interest in his services, the Italian said: “I wish that was true. But I know nothing. I can hope that these rumours turn out to be true.”
