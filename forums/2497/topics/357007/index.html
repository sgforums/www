    Florent Malouda has revealed he wants to remain at Chelsea long-term and says he is desperate to win the Champions League this season.

    The France winger, 28, has struggled to live up to expectations since his big-money move from Lyon in 2007, but he has shown signs of recovering his form under Guus Hiddink.

    And, while he had already signalled his intention to see out his three-year deal at Stamford Bridge, he has now suggested that he is ready to sign a contract extension with The Blues.

    "I still see my future at Chelsea,” he said.

    “Everyone is talking about my future but I will stay here for a long, long time.”
