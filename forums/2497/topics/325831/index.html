    Arsenal have made a dip in the transfer market by wrapping up a deal for midfielder Amaury Bischoff.

    Gunners chief Arsene Wenger has been relatively circumspect in the transfer market, with Bischoff becoming his fourth signing of the summer.

    The Portugal Under-21 international has been brought to England from Germany, where he had been playing for Werder Bremen.

    The Bundesliga side attempted to tie the 21-year-old to a new deal, but he had set his heart on forging a career in England with Arsenal.

    Bischoff has made the trip to Austria to link up with the club on their pre-season tour and it appears the midfielder will be given the chance to stake a claim for a first-team place.
