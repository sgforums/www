    Ancelotti: Chelsea Have Done Better Than Manchester United This Season

    'Carletto' coy about what the future holds.

    Premier League : Carlo Ancelotti (Chelsea)

    Chelsea manager Carlo Ancelotti insists that his side have so far fared better than fellow Premier League contenders Manchester United.

    Despite the recent losses to Blackburn Rovers and Manchester City, the Italian feels that the Londoners' situation is superior to that of their northern counterparts.

    "Until now, we have done better than Manchester United," Ancelotti is quoted in The Sun as saying.

    "We have two more points at the top and Chelsea won the game against them."

    However, the former Milan manager kept his feet rooted to the ground, as he claimed that the future remains uncertain.

    He added: "United are a fantastic team.

    "We have to race with them until the end of the season but I don't know what will happen in the future. Until now, we've done better than them."
