    Everton, Fulham draw tough Europa League groups

    Everton and Fulham have been handed difficult draws in their respective Europa League groups.

    Everton will face Portuguese giants Benfica in the Europa League group stage. The Toffees, who beat Czech side Sigma Olomouc 5-1 to reach the group phase, will also face Greek side AEK Athens and BATE Borisov from Belarus in Group I.

    Fulham face a trip to Italy to take on Roma and must also tackle Swiss side Basle and CSKA Sofia of Bulgaria in Group E.
