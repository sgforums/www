    Jan Sebek’s agent expects the young goalkeeper to seal a dream move to Chelsea sooner rather than later.

    The 18-year-old experienced two trials with The Blues and impressed during a reserve game with Arsenal, during which he kept a clean sheet.

    Viktoria Plzen ace Sebek has been offered a permanent deal by the Stamford Bridge outfit according to his agent, who has announced that the Czech starlet will commit his future to the London side at the start of July.

    "I can confirm that Sebek is going to sign a professional contract with Chelsea on 2nd July," Pavel Zika, told deniksport.cz.

    For his part, the young shot-stopper has called it a ‘dream move’.

    "It is a dream come true for me,” he said.

    “I had been playing football at Tachov until I was 15 and I could not imagine that I would be going elsewhere then.

    "After moving to Viktoria Plzen and a first half-year I earned my first international call-up and then the Chelsea offer came."
