Keane & Defoe answer Redknapp's desperate plea for strikers

  • limpper
    18 Jul 09, 14:50

    BOURN AGAIN ... Robbie Keane

    Bournemouth 0 Tottenham 3

    ROBBIE KEANE and Jermain Defoe answered Harry Redknapp's desperate plea for strikers.

    Redknapp has been linked with a move for Klaas-Jan Huntelaar as he scours Europe for a man to lead the line.

    But Spurs' deadly duo scored either side of the break to demand Redknapp look closer to home.

    Wilson Palacios tapped in a third after Bournemouth keeper Shwan Jalal palmed away a Defoe thunderbolt.

    Earlier, Redknapp's hopes of offloading flop David Bentley took a hammer blow when the midfielder limped off after just 15 minutes.

    He crumpled under a challenge from Cherries' Warren Cummings.
