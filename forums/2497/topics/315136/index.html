Inzaghi: We Need Young Players

  • omgukilledkenny
    21 Apr 08, 18:39

    Milan veteran Pippo Inzaghi says that the club needs an injection of young players for next season.

    The Rossoneri have had a desperately disappointing campaign, and are looking likely to miss out on qualification for the Champions League.

    Many people believe that the squad is simply too old, and that fresh, young blood is needed if a new winning cycle is to begin.

    "Now we must focus on what remains because there is still a possibility of winning fourth place,” said Inzaghi following yesterday’s 5-1 thrashing of Reggina at San Siro.

    "We have leaders who in recent years have worked in the right way. Is something missing? The club and the coach will decide. Now we are the Milan everybody knows.

    "We have done well today, now we are all happy.

    "It is clear that the club knows what must be done. The backbone of Milan is still young. I am an old man, I am convinced, we need to give space to young players, I know that I can always play, but now that we have a game once a week I can play with less effort."

    The 34-year-old Inzaghi has been in stunning form in recent games, and his goal yesterday took his recent tally to five from just three games.
