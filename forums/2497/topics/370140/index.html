    Inter Milan transfer plans do not end with Eto'o

    Inter Milan's transfer business isn't over with the arrival of Samuel Eto'o.

    As they welcome the former Barcelona striker, Inter will also finalise the loan deal for Alex Hleb today, says Gazzetta dello Sport. FC Twente centre-forward Marko Arnautovic could also ink an Inter contract today as he continues his rehab from a foot injury at the club's facilities.

    Meanwhile, Inter coach Jose Mourinho is keen to reduce the size of his existing squad and has given the green light for Victor Obinna, Nicolas Burdisso, Patrick Vieira, Amantino Mancini, Nelson Rivas and Kerlon to find new clubs.
