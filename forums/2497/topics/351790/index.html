Barcelona coach Guardiola to cash in on Krkic

  • limpper
    15 Mar 09, 18:48

     

    Barcelona coach Pep Guardiola is already drawing up transfer plans for next season.

    AS says Guardiola is unlikely to offer goalkeeper Albert Jorquera a new contract, while he is also prepared to sell Alex Hleb, who is a target for Bayern Munich.

    Also to be shown the door is young striker Bojan Krkic, whose availability is sure to spark the interest of Europe's biggest clubs.
