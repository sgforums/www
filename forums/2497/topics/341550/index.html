N'Zogbia Confirms Newcastle Quit Plans

  • omgukilledkenny

    The Frenchman wants to move his career to the next level, and wants out of St James' Park.

    Newcastle United left-sider Charles N'Zogbia has comfirmed that he wants out of St James' Park, as clubs queue up to secure his signature.

    The Frenchman has been on Tyneside since 2004, but has struggled to make himself a certain starter under a clutch of different managers.

    The 22-year-old admits that he has grown frustrated with life in the north east, and has declared himself ready for the off.

    Arsenal, Aston Villa and Paris St Germain are all credited with interest in the former Le Havre prospect, who could depart for pastures new as soon  as January.

    N'Zogbia told L'Equipe: "After four years at Newcastle, I want to reach a higher level of ambition. I don't think that is possible here.

    "I have given my all to the club, and I will go on to do so as long as I play. But I would like to reach a new level by finding a new challenge at a new side."
