    Ferdinand seals Black Cats move

    Sunderland have completed the signing of West Ham defender Anton Ferdinand for an undisclosed fee, believed to be in the region of £8million.

    The England Under-21 centre-back has signed a four-year deal at the Wearside club.

    Keane told his club's official website: "We needed to strengthen our defence and Anton is a great signing."

    The 23-year-old Peckham-born player, brother of Manchester United and England defender Rio, becomes Keane's ninth signing of the summer.

    Keane said: "He'll bring quality and competition for places which is what we want, hopefully we can take him to another level here at Sunderland.

    "He has come through the ranks at West ham and maybe just needs a change."
