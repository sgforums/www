    Manchester United forward Carlos Tevez appears set to sign for the Red Devils' city rivals City, after reports claimed the Argentine has enrolled his four-year-old daughter at a private school in Cheshire.

    According to The Sun, the 25-year-old's daughter Florencia is due to start at the Cheshire school in September, hinting that the diminutive front-man's future almost certainly lies in the North West.

    The news will come as another blow to Chelsea, who have been thwarted at every turn this summer in their search for new signings.

    Tevez, whose two-year loan at United ends next Tuesday, is set to sign a £160,000 a week deal with the Blues, signalling yet another message of intent from Mark Hughes's men ahead of the new season following the captures of Gareth Barry and Roque Santa Cruz.
