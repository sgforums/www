    Sheff Utd v Chelsea
    Ch 23 - 7:45pm (Live)

    SHEFFIELD UNITED against Chelsea kicks off the weekend's Premiership programme. This clash pits the hosts, with just one top flight win to their name in this campaign, against the visitors, with only one defeat.

    Sheffield United defender Claude Davis returns from a one-match ban to face Chelsea. The Blades are set to make wholesale changes from their Carling Cup defeat by Birmingham but Colin Kazim-Richards (knee) is their only injury doubt.

    The Blues have not won at Bramall Lane in 15 years, since a 0-1 top flight triumph on 3 September 1991 with a winner from Dennis Wise. Having said that, there have only been two Premiership fixtures subsequent to that victory.

    SHEFFIELD UNITED

    Rob HULSE is Sheffield United's top scorer with three goals.

    HULSE and Paul JAGIELKA are the only players to have scored in the Premiership for the Blades.

    Only JAGIELKA and Chris ARMSTRONG have played every minute of every one of Sheffield United's Premiership matches this season.

    CHELSEA

    Didier DROGBA is the highest scoring Premiership player with nine goals - five of which were in the League itself.

    Only Michael ESSIEN has figured in every minute of every one of Chelsea's Premiership matches this season.

    If involved, Joe COLE will be making his 100th Premier League appearance in a Chelsea shirt.

    REFEREE
    Martin Atkinson

    Defender Claude Davis returns from a one-match ban whilst wholesale changes are likely for the visit of Jose Mourinho's team.
