Scolari Hails Anelka

  • omgukilledkenny
    16 Nov 08, 21:25

    Chelsea manager Luiz Felipe Scolari has praised Nicolas Anelka following the France international’s return to peak form in the Premier League.

    Despite an indifferent start to his career at Stamford Bridge, Anelka has now scored 12 goals so far this season – eight of those in his last four games – and he looks to have found the form that once made him one of Europe’s most deadly strikers.  

    The performances have not gone unnoticed by Scolari, and the Brazilian tactician believes Anelka has regained his confidence and is capable of continuing his goal-scoring exploits as The Blues go in search of Premier League and Champions League glory.

    "Anelka is happy because he has more confidence. The players, the fans believe more in Anelka than before. They give him more chance to score goals,” said Scolari.

    "Anelka is a very good player, has good skill, good delivery, good shots. In all the games now he is scoring one or two goals. It is fantastic for us.”
