    Given offered new contract by Man City

    Manchester City have offered Shay Given a new contract.

    The Mirror says Fulham are keen on Given as a replacement for want-away keeper Mark Schwarzer who has his heart set on joining Arsenal.

    But Manchester City do not want to let Given go and have offered him a new five-year deal to keep him at the club until he is 39 even though he will be understudy to England number one Joe Hart.
