    Obertan on brink of Man Utd breakthrough

    Gabriel Obertan is set to make his Manchester United debut later this month.

    The News of the World says the France Under-21 international has been in full training this week after finally recovering from a spinal injury.

    Obertan signed for United in the summer, but has yet to make an appearance because of problems with his injury.

    The former Bordeaux winger stepped up his training last week and Sir Alex Ferguson is paving the way for his inclusion in the squad in the next few weeks.
