    CHELSEA are ready to reignite their interest in Micah Richards.

    Jose Mourinho almost signed Manchester City’s teenage England defender 16 months ago in an £18million deal before it was vetoed by owner Roman Abramovich. But the Blues have watched the unstable situation at Eastlands and believe now could be the time to move.

    Richards has signed a new £50,000-a-week five-year deal but he has been unsettled by the impending departure of manager and mentor Sven Goran Eriksson.

    Richards, 19, was linked recently with Manchester United and would cost at least £20m.
