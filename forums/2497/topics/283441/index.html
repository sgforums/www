Cannavaro: Madrid Can Win The Champions League

  • omgukilledkenny
    2 Oct 07, 19:59


    Fabio Cannavaro believes that Real Madrid have the squad to make an impact on this season's Champions League, but says that his side have to finish top of their group to do so.

    Having never won the Champions League, Cannavaro has his sights set to securing the trophy with Madrid and has faith that the current squad can go all the way.

    "I want to win it with Real Madrid," he stated. "I would love to celebrate winning the title in Moscow with the fans next year.

    "I think we have a good enough team to win or reach the final stages of the competition. The key will be to qualify for the next round as group leaders in order to have the home advantage."
