    Arsenal face Celtic in European play-off


    Arsenal will face Celtic over two legs to determine who progresses to the group phase of this season's Champions League.

    The Bhoys sealed their first away win in Europe in six years in midweek, triumphing 2-0 at Dinamo Moscow to cancel out the Russians' 1-0 victory at Parkhead in the first leg.

    However, the Glasgow outfit's hopes of reaching the lucrative group stage of the competition have now suffered a major blow, after being handed the tough task of facing Arsene Wenger's Gunners.

    In the other play-off ties, Lyon will meet Anderlecht, Romanian outfit FC Timisoara face Stuttgart, Sporting Lisbon play Fiorentina and Panathinaikos meet Atletico Madrid.

    The draw for the Europa League play-off takes place later, with Fulham, Aston Villa, Everton and Hearts all awaiting their fate.

    Champions League play-off draw:

    FC Sheriff vs Olympiakos
    SV Red Bull Salzburg vs Maccabi Haifa
    FK Ventspils vs FC Zurich
    FC Copenhagen vs Apoel Nicosia
    Levski Sofia vs Debrecen
    Lyon vs Anderlecht
    Celtic vs Arsenal
    FC Timisoara vs VfB Stuttgart
    Sporting vs Fiorentina
    Panathinaikos vs Atletico Madrid

    First legs are to played August 18/19. Second legs August 25/26.
