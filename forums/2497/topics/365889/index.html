    Inter Milan frustated in Chelsea transfer talks due to Kenyon doubts

    Inter Milan are struggling to find an agreement with Chelsea over Portuguese pair Deco and Ricardo Carvalho.

    The Sunday Times says Inter have been frustrated in their attempts to conclude the joint purchase of Carvalho and Deco, complaining that conversations with Peter Kenyon have been handicapped by doubts over the chief executive’s future.

    “We are talking with Peter,” said a source, “but nobody knows if he stays or leaves. It depends on [Roman] Abramovich.”
