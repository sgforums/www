    The Frenchman spent last season on loan in Spain with Levante and Real Mallorca and Real Betis have both enquired about taking him back to Spain.

    Kapo has hinted he would prefer a switch to England, although he admits his future is in the hands of new Juve boss Claudio Ranieri.

    "At the moment I am only thinking of my holidays and it will be Juventus that decides my future," Kapo told the Spanish press.

    "The Premier League is enormously attractive for any player. For me it would be excellent but I repeat that it does not just depend on me.

    "My experience in Spain was positive but Levante were only fighting to avoid relegation and I desire a club with more aspirations."
