    FLAMINI MIGHT HAVE STAYED

     

    If only Arsenal had won the title...

     

    MATHIEU FLAMINI admitted last night he might have stayed with Arsenal if they had won the Premier League.

    The French midfielder, who will go to AC Milan in July, said: "That would have weighed in the balance. It would certainly have counted. "I actually wanted to sign a new contract at Arsenal last July but it didn't happen. AC Milan did so much to sign me I couldn't refuse."

    Flamini spoke exclusively for the first time of the dilemma that faced him and the frustration that now threatens the future of Gunners stars like Alexander Hleb and Emmanuel Adebayor.

    Painful

    Flamini said: "What happened to us after leading the League for so long was the most painful experience I had during four years at Arsenal. "It has hurt terribly to have invested so much of yourself, to have given everything, to have gone beyond pain and to fall short in the end.

    "I hope with all my heart that Arsenal win the title next year. And I would love to play against them in the Champions League Final next year."
