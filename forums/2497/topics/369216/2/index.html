    I have been a Liverpool Fan ever since my uncle gave me a Liverpool jersey when i was 7. Since then i have become a die hard Liverpool supporter, and after i read the articles on The Newpaper today, I decided that i would like to share my views regarding the online petition.

    Firstly, it has been 8 years since Liverpool has landed on our shores. Thanks to Profitable Group, Singapore fans now have a chance to see their idols. I've read some comments that some fans have spoken that since PG should have known that Stevie G could not make it, they should not have used Gerrard's photos on any form of advertisements or marketing for this trip. While i am vastly disappointed that Stevie G is not coming, we should also understand that Liverpool FC is not about one person, its the whole team that matters. If you really support Liverpool, i would believe that it is as good to be able to watch the rest of the players here as well.

    Secondly, some have put forward the fact that $88 or $188 is so much expensive compared to Manchester United's match in Malaysia, and that if Manchester United can play their stars, so can Liverpool. However, i hope that fans can understand that we're alreadying comparing in different circumstances. Do anyone know what price Man U will charge if they are playing in Singapore instead? Comparing to prices across the border is totally different, considering the differences in our exchange rates as such.  And also, when fans bought the tickets, i believe at least half of you knew that Rafa would definitely play his youngsters, but you fans decided that love for Liverpool is what matters and decided to go ahead with it. In addition, he did not play a Full team of youngsters, he gave players like Babel, Kuyt, Carragher a run out too. How can you complain when you guys knew that this was going to happen? One fan complained in the paper that he could have watched a match in England for $70, does the trip there not cost anything? Why not let Rafa choose who he wants to play instead? He's the manager isn't he? Do you want to see Torres getting injured by Precious or Noh Rahman, and end up missing for Liverpool the whole season?

    In conclusion, to Liverpool fans who loves Liverpool and have not complained, continue to show Liverpool your unwavering support and loyalty. To those who have complained, think rationale and decide if you still think that Liverpool should play their 1st XI. Think again, and stop complaining.

    Die Hard Pool Fan
