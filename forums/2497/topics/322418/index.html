Malaysian Club Boss Concerned At Circling Loan Sharks

  • omgukilledkenny
    28 Jun 08, 10:39

    According to Steve Darby, some Malaysian players are being approached by loan sharks offering to lend them large sums of money.

    The coach of Perak FA believes that the failure of State FAs to pay players on time is only making the situation worse.    

    “This is an unhealthy situation,” Darby told The Star.

    “Desperate players are vulnerable and it could lead to other undesirable things like illegal bookmaking and match-fixing. But my players have been magnificent. They have turned down the offers and approached me for help.”

    Perak FA are also suffering financially and haven’t paid players or staff since March 30. The team may not be able to afford to travel to a June 30 away match against Johor PG.

    ''It is unfortunate but it is true. With our bank account already empty, we do not have funds to make payments for the bus and the hotel,'' said Darby.
