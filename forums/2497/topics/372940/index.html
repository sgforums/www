    TEAM NEWS

    Burnley

    Skipper Steven Caldwell is still out for the Clarets, whilst Michael Duff is a doubt with an ankle injury.

    Possible Starting XI    : Jensen; Mears, Bikey, Carlisle, Jordan; Blake, McCann, Alexander, Elliott; S. Fletcher, Paterson.

    Everton

    Jack Rodwell and Steven Pienaar are injury doubts, whilst Lescott could remain surplus to requirements.

    Possible Starting XI: Howard; Hibbert, Yobo, Neville, Baines; Osman, Gosling, Cahill, Pienaar; Fellaini; Saha.

    PLAYERS TO WATCH

    Burnley

    All eyes will be on Robbie Blake at Turf Moor to see if the striker can repeat his goalscoring heroics after hitting a spectacular winner against the champions in midweek.

    The 33-year-old bagged nine goals for the Clarets last season, and with one already in the bag, will be hoping to beat that tally in the top flight.

    Everton

    Louis Saha was the sole bright spot of Everton’s defeat to Arsenal, scoring their only goal after arriving as a substitute in the second half.

    The Frenchman then went on to grab a brace in midweek in Europa League action, and with three goals in two games looks to be in top form heading to Turf Moor.

    PREDICTION

    The Clarets’ sensational win over the champions in midweek will have given the newly-promoted side a huge confidence boost ahead of the visit of Everton.

    Despite last weekend’s humiliation to Arsenal and the ongoing Lescott saga, the Toffees remain difficult opposition and may just bring the home side back down to earth somewhat by bagging a point.
