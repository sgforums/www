    Confident Arsene Wenger says Arsenal will end trophy drought this season

    Arsenal manager Arsene Wenger celebrated the call-up of a trio of his youngsters to the England squad  on Saturday and claimed they could finally win the title again.

    Wenger saw England manager Fabio Capello include Jack Wilshere, Kieran Gibbs and Theo Walcott in the squad to play Hungary on Wednesday after Arsenal's 6-5 win over Legia Warsaw in Poland.

    Back in the fold: Walcott has been called up to the England squad after his World Cup omission

    Wenger said: 'Gibbs is coming back to his best and that is good news for us and for England. Walcott and Wilshere were a bit jaded today but in general they have all had sharp pre-seasons and they will do a good job for England.'

    The manager added: 'I'm confident we can win trophies. We were third last season, we only have two more steps to climb to win the title, and I'm confident we can do it.'
