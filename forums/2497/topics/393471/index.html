    VILLARREAL MOVE FOR REDS WINGER RIERA

    Liverpool ace can replace Pires

    RIERA: Villarreal want to bring him back to Spain

     

    ALBERT RIERA is a £6million target for Villarreal as they seek to replace Robert Pires.

    The Spaniard has made just 33 league starts for Liverpool since he joined from Espanyol for £8m in September 2008.

    Riera, 27, has featured less this season than last term and has been pushed even further down boss Rafa Benitez's pecking order by the arrival of Argentine star Maxi Rodriguezback in January.

    A source for Villarreal said: "If a deal for Riera is feasible, we will have to explore our options."

    Former Gunner Pires, 36, is set to leave El Madrigal at the end of the season after four years at the club as his current one-year deal comes to an end - and Riera is seen as the ideal man to replace him on the left flank.

    Sevilla are also interested in bringing Riera, who had a loan spell at Manchester City in 2006, back to his native Spain.
