Messi: I could stay forever

  • turbo_drift
    26 Jun 07, 17:59
    Lionel Messi insists he has no intention of quitting Barcelona following Thierry Henry's arrival.

    Over the weekend, Messi revealed he would be welcoming the France international's signing, despite the increased competition for a place in the Barca attack.

    And, after Henry was paraded by the club on Monday, Messi reiterated that he will not be looking to leave Camp Nou over the summer.

    "I have no intention of quitting Barca and I could stay here forever," Messi told Argentinian TV station.

    "People treat me very well and I have no problems with the board - I have everything.

    "However, maybe some day, it would be nice to play in Argentina as I dreamt when I was a child."

    There have been suggestions that Henry's capture from Arsenal could lead to some disgruntled figures in the Barca dressing room.

    But the mercurial Messi does not believe there will be a single player at the club who is against the signing of the striker.

    He added: "Is there anyone who does not want Henry in his team?

    "I am very pleased to have him as a team-mate at Barcelona because he is a fantastic player."
