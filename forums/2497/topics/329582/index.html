    I am a footie nut and I love to digest books on footie.Anyone shares the same passion?

    For me,the following are great reads

    Brillant Orange: The Neurotic Genius of Dutch Football by David Winner

    Those Feet: A Sensual History of English Football by David Winner

    Morbo: The Story of Spanish Football by Phil Ball

    Tor! The Story of German Football by Ulrich Hesse-Lichtenberger

    Soccer Nemesis by Brian Glanville

    The Story of the World Cup by Brian Glanville

    Flat Back Four: The Tactical Game by Andy Gray

    Inverting the Pyramid: The History of Football Tactics by Jonathan Wilson

    Calcio: A History of Italian Football by John Foot

    Forza Italia: The Fall and Rise of Italian Football by Paddy Agnew

    The Italian Job: A Journey to the Heart of Two Great Footballing Cultures by Gianluca Vialli and Gabriele Marcotti
