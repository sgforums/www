    Bolton are being linked with a swoop for Italian star Antonio Cassano, who looks to be heading for the exit door at Real Madrid.

    Cassano has struggled since leaving AS Roma for the Bernabeu and the Spanish giants are keen to offload the Italian forward before the transfer window closes on August 31.

    Reports now claim that Madrid have offered the player to Bolton and West Ham, but if a deal is to happen it would have to be a loan swoop as the total transfer package involved in a permanent move is likely to be too big.

    A return to Italy would be the player's first choice but he may also fancy a crack at the Premiership if a deal can be sorted out in time.
