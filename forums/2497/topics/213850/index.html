    Welcome to the prediction manager. Its a simple game which requires you to predict the winner of EPL games and Euro 2008 Qualifying matches. One point will be given to each correct prediction. Feel free to join in!!!

    Points gain weekly will be added to your account and at the end of each month the winner will have the honour of being the manager of the month.

    For those who are interested to be a part of this game, pls take note:

    The team at the front will be the "Home team" the second team will be the "Away team"

    Just state a Home win, Away win or a Draw along each line, for example:

    England v Scotland - Home win ( England win )
    England v Scotland - Away win ( Scotland win )
    England v Scotland - Draw

    After the games have been played and all the results are out, we would appreciate if you could highlight your correct predictions in Blue (optional) and state how many points you have gain from all your predictions for that weekend so as to make it easier for the mods to update your total points.

    All the matches for that week will be posted on the weekly created "EPL Game (number) Fixtures" thread or "European C'ship Qualifying Fixtures" thread. Final predictions should be place at the Prediction Manager Thread.

    No editing of your prediction post 1 hours before the first match kicks off or all your predictions for that week will be consider void

    For those who wish to join in after we have started, feel free to do so as we will be counting on weekly results, therefore you will still able to see how well you have fair for that week and will only accumulate them at the end of each month.
