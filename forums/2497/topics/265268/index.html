List of Premiership players available for nothing

  • zocoss
    2 Jul 07, 22:47
    As the elite clubs scrambled to complete their line-ups with new signings, 65 players who were with Premiership clubs last season were left with feelings of uncertainty as July 1 marked the start of their free agency. Nine of those players, including Manchester City's Michael Ball and Sheffield United's Keith Gillespie, are mulling over offers of new contracts from their clubs. The other 56 have less than six weeks to find another team before the new season kicks off.

    The experience of those released ranges from 19-year-old Daniel Rose, who was unable to break into the Manchester United first team, to the 41-year-old Teddy Sheringham, who has been released by West Ham after last season becoming the oldest outfield player ever to kick a ball in the Premiership.

    Boudewijn Zenden can be picked up on a free only 40 days after starting for Liverpool in the Champions League final. Jerzy Dudek and Robbie Fowler have also been shown the door at Anfield and are targets for Real Madrid and Rangers respectively. Relegated Charlton have shed the most - eight - from their wage bill.
