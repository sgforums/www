    Tottenham offered Flamengo star Adriano for nothing

    Tottenham have been offered rejuvenated Flamengo star Adriano - for nothing!

    The Mirror says Adriano has been offered to Tottenham on a free transfer for the January transfer market.

    Former Inter Milan striker Adriano, 27, is in the middle of a one-year contract with Brazilian club Flamengo but his representatives are hawking him around Europe.

    They claim that Adriano has a clause in his contract allowing him to leave on a free transfer in January if a European club makes a move to sign him.

    Adriano is keen to return to Europe to try and secure his place in the Brazil World Cup squad after injuries, form and fitness has left his place in danger.
