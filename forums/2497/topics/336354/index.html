    TOP HEADLINES : Barca to renew Fabregas bid

    Prodigal son: Barcelona say all is forgiven and want Fabregas to leave Arsenal

     

    As if Arsenal manager Arsene Wenger didn't have enough on his plate right now, it has been reported that his star player, Cesc Fabregas, will become the subject of a renewed £30 million bid from Barcelona.

    According to the Star, Barca will do 'whatever it takes' to get their man, and have made the 21-year-old the club's No 1 target next summer.

    And, it seems that, following a board meeting earlier this week, the Catalan giants are prepared to start the process of luring him to Spain immediately.

    Fabregas has previously said he would like to return to the Nou Camp, and a source is quoted in the Star as saying: "His departure for England has been completely forgotten. (Gerard) Pique returned to Barcelona and now we want Fabregas back. It will take a lot of money, but he will be the No 1 priority."
