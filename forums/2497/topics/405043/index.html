Blues in for Coentrao

  • a-Lost-9uY
    12 Jul 10, 14:36

    TARGET ... Fabio Coentrao

    CHELSEA will move for Portugal left-back Fabio Coentrao if they lose England star Ashley Cole.

    Real Madrid boss Jose Mourinho is said to be keen to capture Cole and the Blues are bracing themselves for a bid.

    They do not want him to leave but fear he may demand a move to escape his private-life turmoil.

    He suffered flak over his split from Cheryl Cole and England's flop at the World Cup will not have helped matters.

    Chelsea have looked for a replacement and are keen on Benfica ace Coentrao, 22, who won rave reviews in South Africa for his attacking bursts down the left flank.
