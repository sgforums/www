    Carlos Tevez has once against indicated his desire to play for Los Merengues.

    Inter Milan might have pulled out of the race to sign Tevez but the likes of Real Madrid and Arsenal, and even Milan, are all still in the fray.

    Any move will only take place after the Copa America, but 'El Carlito' has once again stressed on his desire to move to the Bernabeu.

    "I would like to play at Real Madrid, but there is no offer from Real Madrid," he told Radio Punta.

    "I have yet to hear anything from Real. I am awaiting offers and I will see which is the best for me.

    "I don`t know what will happen next season."

    He also reiterated that he had never claimed he wanted to move to Inter, something that was already denied two days ago.

    "I do not prefer to go to Inter Milan. That is a lie. It has not come out of my mouth.

    "It is not true, because I have not signed anything with Inter or with anybody."
