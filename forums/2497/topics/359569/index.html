    The former Italian international’s fashion label Sweet Years will today unveil a new brand of condoms.

    Christian Vieri left Serie A club Atalanta through mutual consent in April and has since tried to extend his footballing career by landing a contract in Brazil.

    He was allegedly rejected by Flamengo and Santos, though, and the globe-trotting striker may now consider hanging up his boots and furthering his other passion…

    The 35-year-old carries the reputation of being the ultimate ‘latin lover’ in the peninsula, after having much-publicised relationships with several showgirls.

    Vieri has also become a great fan of Texas Hold’em Poker of late and has attended tournaments in Los Angeles and in Monte Carlo.
