    *Villa warn Blues off Barry*

    Aston Villa boss Martin O'Neill has warned Chelsea off making a move for Gareth Barry.

    Reports over the weekend claimed Chelsea were lining up a bid for the England international in the January transfer window.

    O'Neill has rubbished the rumours and is determined to keep his captain at Villa Park.

    Paper talk
    "I don't think we would be welcoming it," O'Neill told The Times. "I don't know whether it's just paper talk and no one has contacted us about this at all.

    "He's playing for England now and he's playing for England because of how he's played for Aston Villa. That's something we're quite pleased with.

    "I assume he's making enough progress for clubs such as Chelsea to be alerted to him. But we're hoping to stave off those kind of things and the best way to do that is to make progress [as a club] ourselves.

    "I would hope we're building a team not, not dismantling one."
