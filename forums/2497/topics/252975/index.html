    GUARD DUTY JOSE

    CHELSEA boss Jose Mourinho, who offered congratulations to Sir Alex Ferguson, promised to return the compliment of Manchester United's 2005 Old Trafford guard of honour before Wednesday's game at Stamford Bridge.

    Mourinho said: "They did it for us and saw us celebrating the title and it's obvious that we will do it for them.

    "We have to do the same for them."

    Asked if he conceded the better team had triumphed, Mourinho added: "United are the team with more points. That's enough."
