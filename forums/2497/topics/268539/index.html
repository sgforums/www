    Cristian Chivu has hit out at his agent after moves to Barcelona and Real Madrid collapsed and Inter announced that they will do no more to sign him from Roma.

    The Romanian central defender is furious with his representative, Ion Becali, that negotiations broke down with the two Spanish giants.

    Inter are also keeping their distance for fear of upsetting Roma and Chivu is reported to be ready to axe his agent as a result of the failure to find him a new club.

    Now he has demanded that Becali remedy the situation and begin talks again.

    "You {Becali] have put me and Inter in a difficult situation and now you have to fix it," Chivu told the Italian media.

    "We gave you our confidence, the same as I did with Inter and the result is that now everyone has problems with me."

    Chivu had the choice of joining Barca or Madrid last month, but negotiations between the clubs and his agent faltered over Becali's cut of the deal.

    After hearing the demands both clubs began talks with other players as the Blaugrana signed Gabriel Milito from Real Zaragoza and los Merengues paid Â€30million for Pepe from Porto.

    "You left me alone and all this after two trains called Barcelona and Real Madrid passed by," the former Ajax star continued.

    "When trains like this pass you are obliged to jump on board because, if not, then they do not stop and will carry on to the next station.

    "Things can not continue like this."

    Despite reports that Roma would take him back, Chivu is said to believe that the relationship with the club is now untenable and he wants to move before the start of the new season.
