    In the past, kopitiams (coffee shops) in the neighbourhood used to show EPL matches live. Nowadays, kopitiams do not show matches anymore, they only show mediacorp channels only. Gone were the times where people gather at kopitiams to watch the matches and make a lot of noise whenever a goal is scored. They drink beer and make friends with other people. Due to complaints from residents, kopitiams are prohibited from showing matches during late hours, they must turn off their tv sets by 11pm so that residents can sleep. Only places like clarke quay and chinatown show matches nowadays. Without kopitiam live screening, people now have to subscribe to their own cable tv service in order to catch them and have to pay monthly subscription fee while in the past, they can go to kopitiam to catch the matches for free (since the kopitiam boss subscribe and pay for the cable tv). Watching football alone at home is lonely compared to watching them with many people at the kopitiam.
