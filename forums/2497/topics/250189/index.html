Gattuso proud of United interest

  • turbo_drift
    23 Apr 07, 19:19
    Gennaro Gattuso sees his future at Milan, but is proud to have been wanted by Manchester United manager Sir Alex Ferguson.

    Ahead of the two clubs' UEFA Champions League semi-final meeting on Tuesday, Gattuso revealed his admiration for English football.

    But the uncompromising Italian, who is under contract at Milan until 2011, admits he does not expect any further opportunities to move to Old Trafford.

    "Some contacts were made, but I see my future at Milan now," Gattuso told The Sun.

    "But you cannot be anything other than proud when the United manager wants to sign you.

    "I once said my dream was to play in England and that the team I liked best was United.

    "I really like the atmosphere in England and I think my way is probably more suited to English football than Italy."

    The midfielder is desperate to see off United in the semi-finals as he wants the chance to exact revenge on Liverpool for Milan's penalty shoot-out defeat in the 2005 Champions League final.

    He added: "After the final against Liverpool I wanted to quit the club, not because I didn't like Milan any more, but because I was so sad about what happened.

    "Now I would really like to play Liverpool in the final again."
