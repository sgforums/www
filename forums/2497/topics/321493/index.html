Barca Mock Madrid's Ronaldo Chase

  • omgukilledkenny
    19 Jun 08, 21:02

    Barcelona's Ferran Soriano took a swipe at Real Madrid by claiming that they were not using common sense if they were prepared to pay €90m to land Cristiano Ronaldo.

    The Blaugrana economic vice-president stated that the Catalan club would never pay that much for one player because it would it would go beyond what was acceptable.

    While Madrid continue to clamour for the Portuguese star to join them, Soriano said that while Barça could afford to join the bidding, it would no be sensible.

    "There is no limit for what we can spend. But the marker has to be common sense," he explained. "That is an example of no common sense and Barça would not do that."

    Soriano then revealed that talks with clubs about off-loading Ronaldinho, Deco and Samuel Eto'o were all taking place and indicated that deals could be reached soon.

    "They are going well and are advanced," he said.

    Finally, the Barça money man revealed that the club is being run on sporting values and not on commerical lines and in another sly dig at Madrid, stated that selling merchandise was not a priority.

    "It does not worry us much if we sell a certain number of replica shirts or not," he concluded.
