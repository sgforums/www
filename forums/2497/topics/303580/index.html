    Warriors show Tigers no mercy

    In their first friendly game for the new season S.League champions and RHB Singapore Cup winners, SAFFC, had little trouble accounting for an experimental Balestier Khalsa outfit at Choa Chu Kang Stadium on Wednesday night.

    The Warriors, missing their internationals Aleksandar Duric, John Wilkinson, Daniel Bennett and the recently returned Therdsak Chaiman, won 3-0 with new recruit Masahiro Fukasawa showing up very well.

    While the home side have completed their signings and have a settled squad the Tigers used the game to look at a number of prospective signings.

    A nice move in the 37th minute begun by Fukusawa on the right led to Hafiz Osman slipping the ball in the path of Norikazu Murakami who had a simple tap-in.

    In the 77th after Balestier had introduced seven new players Fukusawa scored himself with a wonderful grounder from 25 yards that thundered into the back of the net.

    In the 85th Young Warrior Kamsani Buang finished off a nice move to round off the scoring.
