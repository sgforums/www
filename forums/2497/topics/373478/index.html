    Barcelona's Ibrahimovic slams Real Madrid: What have you done?

    Barcelona ace Zlatan Ibrahimovic has taken a swipe at Real Madrid ahead of the big Spanish Liga kickoff.

    "Madrid needs to win because it's been going two years without any title," said the Swede. "And it goes without saying that the pressure will be on them to do so.

    "What have they done? They've bought players to win, to make a winning team . I have come to a club that already is a winner."

    He added: "I think that Barca has an advantage because Real is forced to create a team from scratch. And it is clear that this cannot be achieved within 24 hours, or in a week or a month. It requires time."
