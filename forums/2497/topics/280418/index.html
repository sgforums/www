    The Thai trio that spent a month on trial at Manchester City returned hone yesterday and admitted that their time in England was a tough one.

    Right-back Suree Sukha, striker Teerasil Dangda and centre-back Kiatprawut Saiwaew joined up with the national team squad for next monthÂ’s 2010 World Cup qualifier against Macau.
