    Barcelona ace Henry: Arsenal wanted Ibrahimovic

    Thierry Henry has welcomed the imminent arrival of Zlatan Ibrahimovic to Barcelona.

    Asked about the Inter Milan striker, Henry told Sport: "Ibra is very strong. I noticed him when I was at Arsenal because the club wanted to sign him when he was young.

    "He's done incredible things in Europe. Ibrahimovic has all the qualities to play in Spain."
