Real Madrid Offer Ezequiel Garay To Manchester United

  • Poignant

    Report: Real Madrid Offer Ezequiel Garay To Manchester United

    The Old Trafford side could be ready to discuss a possible loan move.

    Ezequiel Garay, Real Madrid (Getty Images)

    Real Madrid have offered defender Ezequiel Garay to Manchester United, according to reports in the English media.

    The Argentine stopper has allegedly fallen out of favour with coach Manuel Pellegrini, with the Chilean looking to offload the former Racing Santander man in the summer.

    The English champions are currently suffering an injury crisis at the back, and publication The Daily Mirror comments that they could be ready to negotiate with the Spanish side regarding a possible loan move until the end of the season.

    Garay, who netted the winning goal in the 3-2 victory over Valencia at the Mestalla on Saturday evening, was regarded as the future at Los Blancos but has failed to impress his new coach and is ready to seek a move away from the Bernabeu.

    The 23-year old is available in January and with Rio Ferdinand struggling for fitness Sir Alex Ferguson could see Garay as a stop-gap solution to allow him to strengthen his side in the summer.

    Garay signed with Real Madrid in 2008 but moved on loan to Racing for a further season, returning to the capital club in the summer.
