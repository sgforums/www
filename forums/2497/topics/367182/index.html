    Don't ask me about the Special One

    Carlo Ancelotti insists he can handle the long shadow of Jose Mourinho at Chelsea.

    Asked if he might be the manager to consign Mourinho to the history books, Ancelotti laughed: "I don't know any 'Special One' and I don't want to make comparisons with other coaches.

    "Of course I know that nobody has lasted a season since he left and we have to change this. I sense a good feeling and think I can do great work here.

    "I think that I, Roman and the club have the same objectives - to do very well this season and to win, to have a team that plays good football, creative football and naturally winning football."
