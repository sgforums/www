    Suarez wants Premier League move

    Uruguay striker Luis Suarez has revealed that he would love a chance to move to the Premier League and has named Tottenham Hotspur and Manchester United as possible options.

    Suarez has had an eventful World Cup this summer, scoring three goals for the South American side and helping the team beat Ghana in the quarter-finals thanks to a handball on the goal line in the last minute of extra time.

    The 23-year-old is currently playing for Dutch side Ajax and scored over 50 goals for the club in all competitions last season.

    Suarez is now eying a move to the Premier League and has suggested there are a couple of clubs he would like to play for.

    He said: "I am not going to make any secret of my admiration for the Premier League.

    "Manchester United are a great side and, along with Real Madrid are the most prestigious club in the world."

    "Tottenham hold appeal as well though, they are a team with great ambition and have done well to reach the Champions League," he added.
