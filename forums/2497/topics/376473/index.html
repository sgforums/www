    Chelsea skipper Terry: 2010 my biggest year

    Chelsea captain John Terry says next year will be the biggest in his career.

    He said: “I think it’s probably the biggest since I’ve been playing actually with the World Cup at the end of it. It’s important that myself and the other lads stay fit. I’ve been asked the question, as the games come thick and fast towards the end of the season, are you going to be worried about injuries but I think that’s something that myself or the other lads can’t be too worried about.

    "We have got to go into the games for our clubs, who pay our wages, and we love to fight and win games for our clubs so we have got to make sure we are in top form, we stay fit for our clubs and if that’s the case then we will get picked by the England manager to go to the World Cup at the end of the season.”
