    PICTURE SPECIAL: Drinking with the enemy - Look which Gunners joined Rooney and Ronaldo at the Manchester United Christmas party

     

     

     

    Wayne Rooney (left) and Cristiano Ronaldo show off their hairy chests outside Movida and get a helping hand on their way.

     

    Wayne Rooney, Cristiano Ronaldo and Co were joined by two unlikely revellers during the Manchester United's Christmas celebrations.

    United's stars, some of whom appeared to have enjoyed themselves rather too much, were spotted partying with Barclays Premier League rivals William Gallas and Emmanuel Adebayor at London's trendy Movida nightclub.

    As several suited and booted Old Trafford stars were assisted from the club, the Arsenal pair appeared in casual dress, relaxed and rather more fresh-faced. Perhaps they were happy and contented after a successful evening talking tactics with their great rivals from the north?

     

     

    I spy: Arsenal players Emmanuel Adebayor (left) and William Gallas keep an eye on their rivals

    Extra time: Rio Ferdinand (left) and Anderson show their endurance by being two of the last United players to leave the party.

    Meanwhile, it's good morning Japan...

    United players stepped out at Narita Airport, near Tokyo, surprisingly fresh-faced for their assault on the club World Cup this week.

    The squad flew out for the tournament on Sunday, shortly after their revelling in Soho. United face Gamba Osaka in the semi-final on Thursday, with the final set for Sunday.
