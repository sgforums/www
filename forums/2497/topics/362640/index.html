    Man Utd's Hargreaves determined to bounce back

    Manchester United midfielder Owen Hargreaves has vowed to get over his recent knee problems.

    The England international is recovering from tendonitis surgery on both knees at the Colorado clinic of surgeon Richard Steadman and still cannot set a date for his comeback.

    A source close to Hargreaves told the Mirror: "Owen feels good about his knees, but it's difficult to say if everything is OK or not until he starts training again.

    "It will take time, but he is hopeful he will play again some time next season."
