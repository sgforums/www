    NEW INTER BOSS ... Leonardo

    LEONARDO has been named as new Inter Milan boss after signing a one-and-a-half year deal.

    The Brazilian succeeds Rafa Benitez in the San Siro hotseat and should know his way around having played for and managed city rivals AC Milan.

    A statement confirmed that the 41-year-old will take up his role officially on Wednesday.

    It read: "Welcome to Leonardo. The Brazilian will be our new coach to lead the team from December 29.

    "Leonardo has agreed a contract that will keep him at Inter until 30 June 2012.

    "We sincerely wish Leonardo the best of luck and believe that he has the class and experience of a champion and that his mentality will bring the team together to get the results we need."

    Inter are sat in seventh place in Serie A, 13 points behind his old club who lead the table.

    When the Champions League kicks off again in February they face Bayern Munich in the knockout stages.
