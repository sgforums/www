    Real Madrid sporting director Predrag Mijatovic says that on-loan Arsenal winger Jose Antonio Reyes might yet have a future at the Bernabeu.

    There had been doubts that Reyes, whose two goals on the final day of the Primera Liga season helped Real beat Real Mallorca 3-1 and become Spanish champions for the first time since 2003, would remain at the Bernabeu when his season-long loan from the Gunners ran out.

    However, Mijatovic has hinted that a possible deal may be in the pipeline for the Spanish international.

    "(Reyes) helped us win the league. He's an excellent player and there's a chance he'll stay with us," he said.
