    Arsenal’s new away shirt for 2009-10

     

    THE stars will be out in stripes for Arsenal this season.

    Today the club reveals its third kit for 2009-10 and it's a return to the white away shirt.

    But a major difference to last term is a redcurrant pinstripe and white polo collar and cuffs.

    On the back of the shirt is a panel of horizontal stripes, while engineered into the side seam is a small red label featuring two Arsenal cannons either side of the club motto.

    The shorts for this third choice strip are anthracite grey with a redcurrant stripe along the side.

    A graphic showing an Arsenal cannon is on the back of the waistband, while the draw cords feature Arsenal's name on the tips.

    The look is completed with white socks, again featuring a vertical redcurrant pinstripe pattern with an anthracite stripe on the top band and Arsenal printed on the calf.
