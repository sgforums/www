Barca enter Drenthe chase

  • omgukilledkenny
    24 Jul 07, 03:07
    Feyenoord star Royston Drenthe has replaced his agent as speculation grows over his future.

    Drenthe, who was the Player of the Tournament at the recent Uefa Under 21 Championship, has replaced Andre Wasiman with Sigi Lens.

    The 20-year-old has been linked with some of Europe's big hitters including Chelsea, Barcelona and Real Madrid and the Camp Nou titans are believed to be leading the chase for the player.

    Drenthe's former agent, Wasiman, has spoken about the 20-year-old's options revealing that the youngster is leaning towards a move to Barcelona.

    "Frank Arnesen (Chelsea's sporting director) was keen for things to go far," Wasiman told De Telegraaf.

    "The Catalans said they first had to sell and Frank Rijkaard told Drenthe he had to wait before everything would become ok. He is looking to play in a team with Ronaldinho, (Samuel) Eto'o and (Lionel) Messi," Wasiman added.

    Wasiman explained that Real were keen on bringing Drenthe in but had plans to play him at left-back rather than his preferred position of left-wing.

    Wasiman said: "At Real Madrid he could look for the left back position, Drenthe however is not interested in that."
