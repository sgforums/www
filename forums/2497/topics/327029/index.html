    DAVID BENTLEY and Darren Bent scored two apiece as Spurs hammered Roma at White Hart Lane.

    The Tottenham faithful were treated to an impressive display with Bentley starring in his first home appearance.

    Bentley opened the scoring with a free-kick and Bent continued his fine pre-season form by slotting home the second.

    Dimitar Berbatov was handed a start by Juande Ramos but it was the Spaniard's £17million summer signing who stole the show.

    Bentley drove home his second of the game on the stroke of half-time and Aaron Lennon struck five minutes after the restart.

    Bent rounded off the scoring when he bundled home his second as Spurs dominated the second half.
