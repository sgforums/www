Huntelaar Rejects Inter Move

  • omgukilledkenny

    Klass Jan Huntelaar's agent Arnold Oosterveer has revealed that his client will not be joining Inter after talking to Roma's transfer consultant Paolo Alberto Faccini.

    The arrival of Jose Mourinho has prompted a major shake up at the San Siro and Inter are currently chasing a number of top class players including the Ajax forward.

    Huntelaar has hit the net 70 times in 82 games for the Amsterdam club, using the Eredivisie as a shop window for his goalscoring prowess.

    Reports in Italy have linked the man to both Juventus and Inter but with Amauri's arrival in Turin, Inter seems the only viable option.

    However, his agent has revealed that he has spoken to the player and Huntelaar has snubbed the Italian Champions.

    Former Roma player and transfer consultant Paolo Alberto Faccini spoke to Oosterveer and he has revealed their conversation.

    “I spoke with Huntelaar's agent and I told him the names of the Italian teams interested in his player,” Faccini told Radio Centro Suono.

    “When I told the agent about Inter, he told me that he spoke to Huntelaar and the player said that he was not really interested in moving to the Nerazzurri.”
