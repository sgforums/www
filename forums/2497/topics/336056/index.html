Ribery committed to Bayern

    Bayern Munich star Franck Ribery, voted Bundesliga player of the season last term, has committed himself to the German champions despite reported interest from Chelsea.

    The France international made a huge impression in the Bundesliga last season, and continues to shine for Jurgen Klinsmann’s outfit despite having suffered knee damage during the summer.

    Ribery, who has scored three times in the league so far this term, has been linked with Chelsea, but appears to be very happy in Bavaria.

    “I felt good here last season, but the fact I am starting to speak a better German is a huge help for daily life, even if it is a tough language,” Ribery told L’Equipe.

    “People love me here, so do I, because they are lovely and respectful, they never cross the line.

    “They are always polite when they ask you for an autograph, which pleases me a lot.”
