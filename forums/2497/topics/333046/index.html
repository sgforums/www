    Monaco starlet Fredric Nimani claims he has been watched by Arsenal scouts this season.

    The youngster admits he is in the dark over his future, as he will be free agent at the end of the season, but understands ASM could offer him a new deal.

    Promising striker Nimani, who turned 20 on Wednesday, has bagged three goals so far this season and understands Arsenal, as well as Inter and Juventus, sent scouts to Stade Louis II in order to watch him in action.

    When asked if those three clubs actually came to watch him, he said: “I am aware of it, I have been told about it.

    “That’s immense pleasure but I’m not the kind to lose my head. Let’s say it’s a good sign, it proves I’m on the right track. But nothing’s done yet,” he added to football365.fr.

    “Today I want to focus myself on playing as I still have a lot of work to do. Time will tell.

    “My agent met with the chairman (Jerome de Bontin) and for the moment there have been few concrete discussions.

    “We should know more about this in the next few weeks, but the club seem to want to keep me. We will see how it ends up."
