    UNVEILED ... new Liverpool away kit

     

     

    CALLING all Liverpool fans — here's the new kit Steven Gerrard and the rest of Rafa's men will be wearing next season.

    The Reds have unveiled their latest away shirt for the 2009-2010 campaign, which will be black with a gold adidas trim and red piping and emblazoned with the Carlsberg logo.

    Liverpool had been discussing a white shirt as their change strip but decided on this option due to sales of a previous black shirt.

    The club's current European kit has not been a hit with fans and a second design of a white top could be launched next term in a bid to revive flagging sales.

    Liverpool are also looking at releasing a second red shirt for European games, as the current home kit will not be replaced for another 18 months.
