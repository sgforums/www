    Liverpool lose Fernando Torres, Steven Gerrard and Yossi Benayoun to injury

    • Fernando Torres faces six-week lay-off with torn cartilage
    • Yossi Benayoun sidelined for up to four weeks with broken rib

    Fernando Torres limps out of Liverpool's FA Cup defeat to Reading. A scan has revealed a torn cartilage which will sideline him for six weeks.

     

     

    Saturday, 16 January 2010     

    Channel 27:  "Live" 
     
    Stoke City - Liverpool   - 8:45 pm   
     
     
    Torres - Torn cartilage
    Benayoun - Broken rib
    Gerrard - Hamstring strain
     

    Liverpool today revealed the extent of the injuries sustained by Fernando Torres, Steven Gerrard and Yossi Benayoun in the FA Cup defeat by against Reading last night.

    A club spokesman said: "Steven Gerrard and Fernando Torres both underwent scans earlier today after coming off in the FA Cup tie against Reading.

    "Steven has a hamstring strain and will be out for a fortnight, while Fernando has torn a cartilage in his right knee. The injury will require surgery and he is expected to be sidelined for six weeks.

    "Yossi Benayoun also suffered a fractured rib in the game and will be unavailable for between three and four weeks."
