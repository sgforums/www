    Cannavaro Signs For Juventus

    A statement on the club's official website reads:

    "Fabio Cannavaro again with Juventus from July.

    "Juventus football club has deposited the contract of Fabio Cannavaro which will run from July 1, 2009 until June 30, 2010. As noted, the player's contract with Real Madrid expires on June 30 2009."

    The player, who was in Turin to sign his contract, admits his joy at returning to the club he left in 2006.

    "I am proud to wear the Bianconeri jersey once again," Cannavaro said.

     

     

     

    Juventus Fans Jeer Fabio Cannavaro On His Return To Turin

    Real Madrid's Italy captain Fabio Cannavaro will return to Juventus this summer, despite protests from fans of the Serie A club.

    Juventus fans are still unhappy with the defender after he signed for the La Liga side in 2006 after Juve were demoted for match-fixing.

    While Cannavaro quit the club, the likes of Alessandro Del Piero and Gianluigi Buffon remained with Juventus as they played in Italian football's second tier.

    A small group of fans have protested at recent matches against Cannavaro's return, but he said: "I am sure I will be able to convince even the most sceptical fans thanks to my hard work, professionalism and passion with which I will approach this new adventure."
