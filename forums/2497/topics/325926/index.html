    Jose Mourinho has 'no chance' in Fantasy Football, says Alan Hansen

    Inter Milan coach Jose Mourinho won't be so special as a manager in this season's Telegraph Fantasy Football.

     

    http://link.brightcove.com/services/link/bcpid1529569285/bctid1703371991

     

    That's the informed opinion of Telegraph columnist Alan Hansen, who has poured scorn on the former Chelsea manager's chances of making an impression as Fantasy Football's latest big-name signing.

    "He's got no chance," Hansen said as he looked ahead to the new season. "If he finishes in the top 300,000 he'll be doing well."

    Former Liverpool and Scotland defender Hansen predicted that Mourinho's instinct as a manager would make him "too defensive".

    And Mourinho's team, which included Michael Essien at the expense of Frank Lampard, proved the Special one was making the "cardinal sin of going for balance".
