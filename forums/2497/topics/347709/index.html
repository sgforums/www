    Gremio starlet Douglas Costa has set his sights on securing a dream move to Europe, and possibly to Arsenal or Manchester United.

    The 18-year-old is one of the most promising players of the Brazilian league and he has revealed his attraction to The Premier League.

    The Brazil Under-20 international has welcomed the interest of great European clubs but at the same time committed himself to Gremio.

    When asked if he was aware of Barcelona’s, Real Madrid’s, Juventus’ and Manchester United’s interest in his services, Costa told Calcio Mercato: “Yes, these great clubs are interested and I spoke about it with my lawyer, but for the moment I’m staying at Gremio.

    “We will see how it develops, but for the moment I’m a Gremio player.

    “Playing in Europe is my dream. I watch as many games as I can, the German league, the Italian, the English and the Spanish league.

    “I think that the most beautiful league is the English one and I love Italian football.

    “What I want to do is play for a great club like Manchester or Arsenal. There are a lot of teams that I love.

    “Even in Italy there are great teams like Inter, Juventus, Milan and Roma.”
