    JOHNSON ... Rafa reckons full-back is money well spent

     

     

     

    GLEN JOHNSON will make his Liverpool debut tonight with boss Rafa Benitez insisting he is worth his £17million transfer fee.

    The former Portsmouth and Chelsea defender, 24, is set to face St Gallen in Switzerland.

    And, while Benitez admits he agrees with Manchester United chief Alex Ferguson that the transfer market has gone mad this summer, he does not think Johnson's price was over the top.

    He said: "Yes, the money people are asking for is crazy. But the money for Johnson is OK.

    "He is an English international with quality and he is worth the money.

    "He is a very good offensive full-back who can give us more options - especially when we are at home and teams are defending against us.

    "Going forward he will give us something special."
