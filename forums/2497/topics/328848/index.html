    STALLED ... Liverpool's new stadium will not look like this for some time

     

    LIVERPOOL have been forced to delay the plans for their new stadium in Stanley Park yet again.

    The news will come as a massive blow to the Kop faithful with many fans fearing that American owners Tom Hicks and George Gillett cannot fund the £400million project. But the club insist the latest development is purely down to the credit crunch.

    An official statement said: "Our commitment to building a new world-class stadium is undiminished. "Like many other major development projects in the UK and overseas we are affected by global market conditions and as such work on the project will be delayed in the short term.

    "We will use this period productively and revisit the plans for the stadium to increase its capacity to 73,000 seats."
