    Nicklas Bendtner's wrecked car reveals lucky escape for Arsenal star

    Smash: Nicklas Bendtner's wrecked Aston Martin rests next to the tree

     

     

    This is the picture that shows just how lucky Nicklas Bendtner was to emerge with just bumps and bruises after his horror car crash.

    The Arsenal striker wrecked his £120,000 Aston Martin on the way to training on Sunday when he collided with a tree on the A1.

    Bendtner escaped with minor injuries to his knees and shoulder, and is expected to return after sitting out of Tuesday's Champions League clash with Olympiakos.

    The Denmark star is now training alone as he attempts to bounce back from the shock incident.

    'Nicklas has been training by himself on a bike in order to be careful and watch out,' said Thomas Bendtner, his father and agent.

    'He is badly shaken and has some bruises, but knows he was very lucky.'
