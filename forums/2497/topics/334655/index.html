    ARSENE WENGER has revealed Croatian striker Eduardo could be back in action next month.

    The former Dinamo Zagreb ace suffered his horrific break following a tackle from Birmingham’s Martin Taylor in February.

    Arsenal boss Wenger expects the forward to have fully recovered from the inury by mid-November, in time for the hectic Christmas schedule.

    And Eduardo could be back in first-team action in as little as three weeks' time when the Gunners face Wigan in the Carling Cup on November 11.
