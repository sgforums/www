    Right, before we get into the serious business, let's start with a funny.

    The Aston Villa boss has charmed his way around the room, as usual, before one intrepid reporter raises his hand.

    'Mr O'Neill, I think you look like an old Harry Potter, have you got some magic up your sleeve for the game?'

    Cue the sound of jaws dropping among the English contingent who are secretly  thinking: 'This is going to be good.'

    'Er, I think he said you looked like Harry Potter, boss...'

    How Young kept a straight face, I'll never know. He probably wanted to stay in a job, that would be it. Fantastic moment.
