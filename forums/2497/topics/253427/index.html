Jose makes up with Ronaldo

  • turbo_drift
    9 May 07, 17:34
    Cristiano Ronaldo has revealed Chelsea boss Jose Mourinho has apologised to him for criticising his upbringing.

    Mourinho was quoted as describing Manchester United star Ronaldo as 'ill-educated, disrespectful and immature' last month after a war of words between the two.

    Mourinho's attack earned an angry rebuke from United manager Sir Alex Ferguson who defended Ronaldo.

    Ahead of Wednesday's clash between the two sides Ronaldo has revealed Mourinho has apologised for his comments and that he has no problems with his fellow Portuguese.

    "Mourinho has apologised to me and now I have no problem with him," Ronaldo told the Daily Mirror.

    "I am pleased that this has happened. As far as I am concerned, the whole thing is now in the past."
