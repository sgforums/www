    Former Chelsea boss Jose Mourinho can spot a decent pair of strikers from the touchline - and, just like Fabio Capello, his close-range vision seems up to scratch, too.

    Inter Milan manager Mourinho was subjected to the same scantily clad dance display on Italian TV as England's boss was in January.

    And neither football chief was able to avert his gaze from the athletic assets presented before them.

    Mourinho, who left Chelsea in 2007, struggled to hide his embarrassment - or was it pleasure - during the filming of television show Chiambretti Night.

    Capello appeared on the same show at the start of the year. Unfortunately, both managers failed to keep their eyes on the ball.

    Mourinho, 46, who is happily married to wife Matilde with whom he has two children, appeared not to be the only Special One in the television studio. Sportsmail counted at least two...
