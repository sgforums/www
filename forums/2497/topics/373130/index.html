    Chelsea's Terry has dig at Man Utd, Liverpool over sales

    Chelsea captain John Terry had a pop at Manchester United and Liverpool after victory over Fulham yesterday.

    Terry says keeping the club's best players, like Didier Drogba and Ashley Cole, will be critical in this season's title race.

    “I stayed because I loved this club. I have had 14 years here and it has never been an issue for me. What was important to me was that the likes of Ash and Didier stayed. I’m delighted that we’ve kept our squad together, unlike the other two,” said the defender.
