Referee abandons Euro qualifier

  • turbo_drift

    Sweden's Euro 2008 qualifier away to Denmark was abandoned after 89 minutes when a fan tried to attack the referee.

    Denmark had fought back from 3-0 down to make it 3-3 when Herbert Fandel sent off Denmark defender Christian Poulsen.

    After a spectator ran towards the referee and was intercepted by Danish players the official walked off.

    Uefa say the result will not be confirmed until their disciplinary committee has read the referee's and delegate's reports from Copenhagen.

    They could decide to award a 3-0 victory to the Swedes.

    The match was in Group F, which is currently led by Northern Ireland.

    Fandel was in charge of the Champions League final last month, which saw AC Milan beat Liverpool 2-1.
