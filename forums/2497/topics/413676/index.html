    You're next! Hat-trick hero Emmanuel Adebayor wants to punish Arsenal now, says Manchester City team-mate Micah Richards

     

     

    Antagonising: Emmanuel Adebayor ran the length of the pitch to celebrate in front of Arsenal fans


     

    Sunday, October 24 2010

     

    Manchester City v Arsenal      11:00   Mio Channel  102 / 110

     

     

     

     

    Micah Richards says Manchester City team-mate Emmanuel Adebayor took out his 'frustration' at not playing regularly for the club on Lech Poznan and will do the same to Arsenal in Sunday's showdown at Eastlands.

    Adebayor had been left out of City boss Roberto Mancini's first choice line-up for the last few weeks and was down in the dumps.

    He was recalled against Blackpool last weekend but failed to score before responding with a stunning hat-trick against Lech Poznan in the Europa League on Thursday night.

    Now Richards believes the Togo frontman is in the perfect frame of mind to punish the Gunners in the same way.

    He said: 'Emmanuel has been quite down for the last couple of weeks but he is buzzing now. He has been hoping for a goal. With a hat-trick he should soon be flying again.

    'I can understand his frustration. He is a quality player and when you don't get in the first XI it is frustrating. But we have a dressing room full of international stars, so when a chance comes along, you need to take it.

    'I am sure he will play some part. Even if he doesn't start, I am sure he will come on. When he played last time it was his best game of the season. Hopefully he can do well for us again.'
