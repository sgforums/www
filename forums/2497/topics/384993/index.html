    Report: Fiorentina To Fine Adrian Mutu Over Late Night Partying

    The Romanian is set to be disciplined after getting into a fight in the wee hours of last Friday.

    Adrian Mutu - Debrecen-Fiorentina - Champions League (AFP/Getty Images)

    According to La Repubblica newspaper, Serie A side Fiorentina have decided to slap star striker Adrian Mutu with a hefty fine in the wake of a much-publicized brawl at a Florence hotel.

    Mutu has admitted to having punched a man of Libyan origin at the Four Seasons hotel after being insulted and Fiorentina have indeed accepted that the former Chelsea hitman was provoked.

    But Mutu will not get away scot free as the Gigliati’s code of conduct outlines that players must adhere to a midnight curfew.

    “I have to reproach Adrian because a professional shouldn’t be out at 3:00 a.m.,” Fiorentina chief executive Sandro Mencucci insisted yesterday.

    Therefore a fine looks to be on the cards for Mutu, who is unlikely to be pleased by the decision as he was still sidelined at the time.

    “But it is a behavioral issue,” concluded Mencucci.
