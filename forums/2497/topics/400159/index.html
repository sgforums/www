    Barcelona Technical Secretary Txiki Begiristain: We Have To Show Arsenal Respect Over Cesc Fabregas Transfer

    Txiki wants to show Gunners respect…

    EPL: Cesc Fabregas

    Barcelona technical secretary Txiki Begiristain has revealed the Blaugrana don’t intend to approach Arsenal midfielder Cesc Fabregas until the Gunners are happy to allow the club to speak to him.

    "The player [Fabregas] has shown a desire to play for Barca,” Begiristain told the club's official website. “But we will not be entering into any talks until Arsenal are happy.

    “Cesc has a contract with his club and we have to be respectful," he added.

    Fabregas is reported to have already expressed his desire to join Barcelona in a meeting with Arsenal manager Arsene Wenger and looks set to become the Catalan club’s second major transfer of the summer following the arrival of Valencia striker David Villa.

    Reports both in Spain and in England suggest Cesc is eager to wrap up a deal before he joins up with la Furia Roja to prepare for the World Cup next week.
