    Real Madrid To Offer Jose Mourinho Lucrative Bonuses To Lure Him Away From Inter - Report

    The Merengues are willing to splash the cash for the services of 'The Special One'...

    Inter Milan Jose Mourinho

    Inter boss Jose Mourinho is already the highest paid coach in the world, but Real Madrid are willing to offer him an even better contract to join them this summer, according to Sport.

    The report claims the Portuguese tactician earns a net amount of €10.5 million per season in Italy after everything is said and done, and that president Florentino Perez will bring that amount up to €11 million plus substantial bonuses. For every title 'Mou' procures for the Spanish giants, he would receive an extra €1.5 million.

    After failing to advance past the round of 16 in the Champions League yet again this season, despite spending in excess of     €245 million on players, current Madrid coach Manuel Pellegrini's job is by no means safe this summer.

    Meanwhile, Mourinho has helped Inter to the Champions League quarter-finals by defeating Chelsea and his side are currently topping the Serie A table.

    However, the Nerazzurri tactician's constant clashes with the Italian press continue to result in speculation he may seek a move elsewhere during the summer, with Liverpool also a reported destination.
