    Manchester United defender John O'Shea linked with surprise Barcelona switch

    Barcelona are considering a shock bid for Manchester United utility man John O'Shea.

    The European champions have been looking for cover on the right side of defence since last summer and see the Republic of Ireland international as someone who could be useful in a number of positions.

    Barca failed to land Emmanuel Eboue or Bacary Sagna from Arsenal before the start of the season and have lined up either O'Shea, who is currently sidelined until March, or highly rated Spain Under 21 defender Cesar Azpilicueta as alternatives.

    Shock switch: John O'Shea has been linked with a summer move to Barcelona

    Osasuna's attacking full-back Azpilicueta is likely to be Pep Guardiola's first choice, but Barca would face competition from Arsenal, Manchester City and Real Madrid for the 20-year-old.

    Arsenal's Cesc Fabregas and Bayern Munich's Franck Ribery are top targets for the European champions this summer.

    Top target: Barcelona are eager to land the services of Arsenal skipper Fabregas

    But dealmaker Txiki Begiristain, the club's technical secretary, has drawn up a list of other options that would put Barca in competition with a number of Premier League sides this summer.

    Benfica star Angel di Maria is an alternative to Ribery, but the 22-year-old Argentina international has also attracted interest from Manchester City and Chelsea.

    Versatile Valencia midfielder David Silva is also on their hit list, but Real Madrid, Manchester United and Liverpool are also keen on the 24-year-old Spain international.
