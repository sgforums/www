Cruz is facing a Roque ride

  • limpper
    21 Jun 09, 13:47

    MEDICAL ... Roque Santa Cruz

     

    ROQUE SANTA CRUZ will be put through a tough medical before he completes his £15million move to Manchester City in the next few days.

    Blackburn and City were last night close to a deal which would see the Paraguayan reunited with his old boss Mark Hughes.

    But the striker's future club want to put him through his paces first when he returns from holiday next week.

    City will pay close attention to the knee injury which kept the former Bayern Munich star out of the last 10 weeks of the season for Rovers.
