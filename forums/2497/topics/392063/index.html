    LIVERPOOL have confirmed Martin Skrtel has broken a metatarsal in his right foot.

    The centre-back was injured during the the 3-1 Europa League win over Unirea Urziceni last night.

    And X-rays have confirmed the injury is worse than first feared.

    Reds boss Rafa Benitez said: "We were really disappointed because it was a good result but then we had the bad news about Martin.

    "He's had x-rays today and they confirmed he has a broken metatarsal.

    "We have to check over the next couple of days how long it will take to recover."
