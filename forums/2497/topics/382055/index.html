    MANCINI DREAMS OF ENGLAND

     

     

    Roberto Mancini admits England is his next port of call as he looks for a return to management. 

    Mancini has been out of the game since leaving Italian champions Inter Milan in May 2008, following a falling out with Nerazzurri supremo Massimo Moratti. 

    The Italian brought considerable success to Inter during his time there as he guided the Serie A giants to three successive titles and, while he has no shortage of admirers in his native Italy, it is the English Premier League he has in his sights. 

    "I've annulled my deal with Inter because I want to come back coaching," he told Italian TV show Le Lene. 

    "Where? I'd prefer the (English) Premiership. I've not coached in more than a year and in this period I've had about three or four proposals. 

    "But it's not true that I'm not coaching because I want too much money. 

    "Money for me has never been the most important thing, either as a player or as a coach."
