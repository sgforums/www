    UEFACL 07/08 Match Day 6 (Group D only)
    All matches kickoff 3.45am (Singapore time), unless otherwise stated
    Results in brackets show last meeting

    Tuesday 4 December 2007
    Due to Milan's Club World Cup commitments, the UEFA Champions League Group Stages for clubs in Group D will come to an end 1 week earlier than the other clubs.

    FC Shakhtar Donetsk v SL Benfica
    Live on Ch 24, 3.30AM
    Benfica is already out, but they will need to win this one to stay in Europe by entering the final 32 of the UEFA Cup.
    Shakhtar will need to win this one and depend on Milan to win to still be able to qualify for the last 16 on head-to-head results. A loss would mean Shakhtar is completely out of European competitions.

    My prediction: Shakhtar 2 Benfica 0

    A.C. Milan v Celtic FC
    Live on Ch 23, 3.30AM
    Milan, though they have lost the previous meeting in Glasgow, have already qualified for the last 16. The Rossoneri will be looking to round off their final home game with a win before travelling to Japan for the Club World Cup.
    Celtic need a single point to ensure qualification for Round 2, but they have a very poor away record, losing 14 and drawing 1 game. A loss would mean the Bhoys would need Benfica to beat Shakhtar.

    My prediction: Milan 3 Celtic 1
