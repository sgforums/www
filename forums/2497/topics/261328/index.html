    West Ham have missed out on signing France goalkeeper Gregory Coupet, according to The Sun.

    Coupet, 34, who has just signed a new three-year contract with Lyon, had reportedly considered a move to Upton Park, but instead chose to commit his future to the French champions.

    Coupet said: "Last weekend I was leaving. I believe my officials understood it.

    "I had a proposition from a foreign club and I was tempted by that experience, even if it would have been less prestigious than Lyon.

    "It was an opportunity to discover a new league, a new world.

    "Sometimes, it is almost easier to leave than to stay."
