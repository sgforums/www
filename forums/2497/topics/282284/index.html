Angry Sanchez hits out at referee

  • omgukilledkenny
    27 Sep 07, 19:45


    Fulham boss Lawrie Sanchez has launched a scathing attack on referees after his side had a goal disallowed during their 2-1 Carling Cup defeat to Bolton.

    "I'm getting fed up with goals getting disallowed for no apparent reason. It's ridiculous. Referees and linesman are costing us week in week out," he fumed.

    "They aren't up to the job and something needs to be done."

    Sanchez was furious after referee Steve Bennett ruled Diomansy Kamara's effort offside with the score at 1-1.

    He continued: "Whether they think they are coming for a nice trip or something, I don't know. But we have seen the goal, and it is definitely onside.

    "He's blatantly onside by a yard at least, if not two yards, when the ball's been shot by David Healy."

    Stelios Giannakopoulos won the third-round match for Bolton with an extra-time strike, after coming on as substitute.
