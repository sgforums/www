  • omgukilledkenny

    Ronaldinho has confirmed that he will not join Manchester City this summer.

    The Brazil maestro has been linked to the Eastlands outfit since falling out of favour at Barcelona, but he opted against moving to City last summer and finally put pen-to-paper with Milan.

    The attacker’s first season at the San Siro has been far from rosy, and Roberto Assis, his agent and brother, has recently hinted that Ronaldinho could change clubs in Europe at the end of the season.

    The talented player aims very high with the Rossoneri and he is apparently not after a transfer.

    “Moving to Manchester City is not part of my intentions,” Ronaldinho told La Reppublica. “I am good at Milan even if the truth is that I wish I had played more.

    “A great club must have high aspirations and next year we will win the Champions League and the Italian league. This is our thinking.”
