Eto'o retains Henry hope

  • turbo_drift
    24 May 07, 18:47
    Samuel Eto'o remains hopeful that Thierry Henry could still sign for Barcelona.

    The Arsenal striker was strongly linked with a move to Barca last summer, before he committed to The Gunners by putting pen-to-paper on a new long-term contract.

    However, speculation about Henry's future has resurfaced following Arsenal's poor season, and Eto'o believes there is a chance he could yet move to Camp Nou.

    "I hope so, I really do," said Eto'o at an event run by the feelfootball.com debate website.

    "Obviously it is nothing to do with me, but I would be delighted if he came to play here."

    Though he accepts signing Henry would have created huge competition for places in a forward line already including Ronaldinho, Lionel Messi and himself, Eto'o regrets that Barcelona missed out on the Frenchman last year.

    He explained: "We were ready to welcome him with open arms. We would have been really happy had he come in and we would have had a great team.

    "We still have a great team, but it would have been special.

    "I don't know if we could all play together. That's the coach's job.

    "All I know is that I want the best players in my team and Henry is one of the best.

    "He's a great player. He's number one of the great players."
