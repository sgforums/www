    Juventus Keeping Tabs On Liverpool Midfielder Alberto Aquilani - Report

    The Bianconeri are watching the Reds midfielder...

    Alberto Aquilani, Liverpool

    Juventus are reportedly interested in Liverpool midfielder Alberto Aquilani, hoping to bring the Reds player to Turin during the upcoming summer transfer window.

    The former Roma midfielder has not settled in as expected at the club, and could be departing for the Bianconeri, according to Novantesimo.it.

    Aquilani could also go to the Turin giants because of the side's strong link with     Rafael Benitez, who may take up a coaching position with the Italian club.

    It was the Spaniard who pursued the Italian international, and brought him to the English club over the past summer, but injuries have restricted the amount of playing time the 25-year-old received.
