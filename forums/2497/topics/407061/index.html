    Rooney, 24, could be fined with the issue set to hit board level as the club tries to attract sponsors from around the world to fight the club's debts.

    Ferguson will also demand to see the fitness reports on Rooney, who reported back to training last week, to see if he has kept his weight down during a month off.

    Rooney is due to play tomorrow in Dublin in a friendly but was going to be rested on Sunday ahead of the big kick-off.
