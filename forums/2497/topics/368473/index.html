    Liverpool launching £15M bid for Roma's Aquilani

    Liverpool are making a £15 million offer for Roma midfielder Alberto Aquilani.

    Gazzetta dello Sport says two Liverpool officials are in Rome today to deliver their bid and also discuss personal terms with Aquilani.

    Liverpool manager Rafa Benitez sees Aquilani as a replacement for Real Madrid target Xabi Alonso and is willing to offer the midfielder a £2.5 million-a-year contract to bring him to England.
