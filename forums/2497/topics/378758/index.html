    Chelsea boss Carlo Ancelotti appeared on top Italian TV show Chiambretti Night — and, like Fabio Capello and Jose Mourinho before him, seemed to have no problem with this particular form check.

    At the beginning of the year, in January, 61-year-old England boss Fabio Capello raised eyebrows when was treated to a erotic dance by one of the show's dancers.

    Indeed, it added an added degree of spice to his return to Italy where he had initially checked in to personally witness David Beckham's progress at AC Milan.

    And the former Stamford Bridge boss Jose Mourinho was made to blush when he made an appearance on the same show three months later in April.

    In a bid to endear himself to the Italian public, 'The Special One' also enjoyed a private performance.

    But while Jose's former sparring partner in Milan, Ancelotti maybe enjoying life in London, on this evidence it won't be too long before he opts to return to his homeland.

    True to form, the Inter Milan coach did not leave without adding controversy, stunning the audience with an unprovoked attack against his San Siro predecessor Roberto Mancini.
