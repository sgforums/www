    JOSE MOURINHO reckons Arsene Wenger’s Gunners are too young to win the title this year. And the former Chelsea boss is tipping Liverpool to claim the Premier League.

    “Man United and Chelsea will be fighting but one day Liverpool are going to get that title. I think it might be this year.”
