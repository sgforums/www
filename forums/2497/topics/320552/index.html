VELA: GIOVANI TOO GOOD FOR SPURS

  • zocoss
    10 Jun 08, 21:29

    Mexican Carlos Vela taunt Tottenham

     

    ARSENAL ace Carlos Vela has taunted Tottenham by claiming Giovani Dos Santos will move to a bigger club.

     

    Ex-Barcelona ace Dos Santos, 19, agreed to join Spurs last week in a cut-price £4.7million move. But his Mexico team-mate Vela believes the switch is just a stepping stone in the wonderkid's career.

     

    Vela said: "I am happy for him. "I hope things go well for him at Tottenham, that he gets the minutes he wasn't given at Barcelona and that he shows what he can do. "He is a very good player and let's hope he can then move on to a better team."
