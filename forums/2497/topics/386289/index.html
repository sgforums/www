    Manchester City Boss Mancini: Shay Given Among Top Five Goalkeepers In The World

    Given that he's one of the best in the business.

    Shay Given - Manchester City (PA)

    Manchester City manager Roberto Mancini believes that goalkeeper Shay Given is  one of the five best keepers in the world.

    Martin Petrov and Carlos Tevez produced the firepower up front to hand 'Mancio' his first victory on his managerial debut in England against Stoke City, but not before Given was forced to make a couple of great saves and consequently keep a clean sheet.

    "Shay is among the top five keepers in the world," Mancini is quoted by The Press Association as saying.

    "You have Buffon, [Petr] Cech and a couple of others but Shay is right up there. Against Stoke he made saves in one or two situations that were absolutely crucial."

    Following the victory over the Potters, Manchester City take on Wolverhampton Wanderers at Molineux on Monday.
