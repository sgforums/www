Rossi reveals England options

  • omgukilledkenny
    9 Jun 09, 17:25

    Villarreal striker Giuseppe Rossi claims he has options in England should he ever wish to make a Premier League return.

    The USA-born Italy international striker had spells with Manchester United and Newcastle before joining Villarreal in search of first-team football.

    The 22-year-old has impressed greatly during his two seasons in Primera Division, prompting speculation that a return to England could be on the cards.

    While Rossi has stated he remains contracted to the Yellow Submarines, he is not ruling out a Premier League comeback should the right opportunity arise.

    "I have a contract here, but I know anything is possible and there are opportunities in England," Rossi told the Daily Mirror.
