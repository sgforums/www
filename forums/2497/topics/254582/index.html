Gerrard promises title push

  • turbo_drift
    15 May 07, 18:44
    Steven Gerrard has promised Liverpool will mount a serious challenge for the Premiership title next season.

    The Reds have failed to close the gap on the leading duo this time around, finishing 21 points adrift of champions Manchester United.

    However, Gerrard feels with investment in the squad this summer, Rafa Benitez will have a squad capable of going all the way in the league next season.

    Although Gerrard is thrilled to have reached the final of this season's UEFA Champions League he knows it is time for them to start performing consistently in the league.

    "The league is always your bread and butter as a Liverpool player - and we're not far away," he is quoted in The Sun.

    "With investment in the summer, and the squad already staying together, we can promise to push Chelsea and United all the way next year.

    "You ask the manager or any of the players and they would say exactly the same thing - we had bigger ambitions for this season than just the Champions League.

    "We always had one eye on Europe but we also wanted the league flag flying back at Anfield."
