United make move for Benzema - agent

  • omgukilledkenny
    22 Nov 08, 22:29

    Manchester United are holding talks with Lyon over the possible transfer of Karim Benzema to Old Trafford, according to the striker’s agent.

    Sir Alex Ferguson has made no secret of his admiration for the French goal-machine and it would appear the Scot is ready to steal a march on the rivals for Benzema’s signature.

    The biggest clubs in Europe are on the trail of the 21-year-old, but Benzema’s agent Karim Djaziri claims United have made contact with Lyon.

    Djaziri told El Mundo: “I know that Lyon’s directors have met with Barca, Real Madrid and Manchester United’s representatives and that all those three great clubs are very interested in signing him regarding next season.”

    Lyon have resisted offers for Benzema in the past, as they have made it clear he is integral to their hopes of winning the Champions League, but it remains to be seen how long they can retain his services.
