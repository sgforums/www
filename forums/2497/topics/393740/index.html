    WANTED MAN ... David Beckham

    DAVID BECKHAM has been swamped by offers to be a World Cup pundit in South Africa.

    BBC, ITV and ESPN all want Becks on their sofas for the finals and are prepared to pay whatever it takes.

    But Beckham is not certain to accept any of the offers as he recuperates from the torn Achilles tendon which has wrecked his dream of appearing at a record fourth World Cup.

    We revealed in today's SunSport how Fabio Capello had invited Beckham to join up with the squad in the summer — even though he would not be able to play.

    Beckham, 34, also has the chance to step up his duties as the country's 2018 World Cup ambassador during the finals with the vote on where the tournament will be staged taking place at the end of the year.

    A source close to Becks said: "David has a lot of different offers coming in but he cannot get his head round any of them at the moment.

    "The most important thing he is thinking about right now is that he recovers from this injury and gets back playing again."
