    Rafa Benitez is being lined up by Paris St Germain

     

     

    WANTED ... Rafa Benitez

     

     

     

     

    FORMER Liverpool and Inter Milan boss Rafa Benitez is being lined up as a replacement for Paris St Germain coach Antoine Kombouare.

    It is claimed Ligue 1 side PSG have sacked Kombouare and have approached Benitez, who is available after being dismissed from Serie A club Inter last December.

    Benitez enjoyed success at Liverpool between 2004 and 2010, winning a Champions League in 2005 and an FA Cup in 2006.

    PSG are second in the French top flight after they ended their three-game winless run with a 3-2 victory over Auxerre on Sunday.

    Qatar Investment Authority took control of the Parisian club in May and have invested heavily, bringing in big names such as Javier Pastore and Jeremy Menez.
