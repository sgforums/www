    Report: Milan Ace Ronaldinho Goes Clubbing In Brazil

    Ronaldinho back on the clubbing scene.

    Ronaldinho - Milan (Getty Images)

    Milan ace Ronaldinho is using up his free time away from the Brazil squad by clubbing.

    Globoesporte reports the playmaker, who was excluded from the Selecao 23 for the World Cup, has been spending time in Brazilian resort Florianopolis.

    Ronaldinho has checked into the Majestic Palace Hotel with a number of friends including Alexandre Pato and former Manchester City man Jo.

    And, rather than watch the World Cup on TV, Ronaldinho has been using his time to go clubbing, partying late into the night in many of the night spots around the resort. On Thursday night he stopped to greet fans to sign autographs.

    The party doesn't end there though. 'Dinho will reportedly attend a concert by Brazilian rap star Marcelo D2.
