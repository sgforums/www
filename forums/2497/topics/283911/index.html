Kaka Shocks Milan: I Would Like To Play In Spain

  • omgukilledkenny
    5 Oct 07, 15:03


    The Milan forward declared that a move to Real Madrid or Barcelona attracts him and that in the future it can become a possibility.

    This was revealed by the same player to the website of the Spanish daily newspaper 'El Pais', where the Brazilian player re-opened the doors for a move to Real Madrid or Barcelona after closing them in the past summer.

    When asked which Spanish club he would prefer to play in, the Brazilian replied without any problem.

    "I already play for a great club like Milan, but one day I would like to play in a great team in Spain, like Real Madrid or Barcelona," he replied.

    "The transfer market rumours are things which the press reports, it has always been like this. However, for a player, knowing that a club, or two or even three, are interested in him is a satisfaction."

    These words are surely arrived in the worse possible moment for Milan and stir new speculation on an eventual and possible transfer of Kaka to Spain at the end of this season.
