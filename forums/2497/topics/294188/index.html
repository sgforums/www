Tottenham and Arsenal after Italian marksman Toni

  • zocoss
    29 Nov 07, 02:33


    Tottenham have been linked with a move for Bayern Munich's unsettled striker Luca Toni but they will have to battle neighbours Arsenal if they want to sign the Italian marksman. Toni is reported to be finding it hard to adapt to life in the Bundesliga and there are claims he longs for a return to Serie A, where he was a legend at Fiorentina.

    His aggressive style and ability to convert chances would make him a natural for the Premier League, however, and Spurs and Arsenal have been impressed with his success in germany where he is the joint-top scorer.

    Spurs boss Juande Ramos tried to sign the 30-year-old in the summer when he was in charge at Sevilla while Gunners manager Wenger believes Toni would provide the firepower needed to end the wait for silverware after a couple of barren seasons.

    Toni recently helped end Scotland's bid to reach the Euro 2008 finals when he scored against the Tartan Army at rainlashed Hampden Park as the Azzuri stormed to a 2-1 victory.
