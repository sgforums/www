    Inter switch from Mascherano to Melo after Liverpool demand too much money

    Inter Milan claim they have ended their interest in Javier Mascherano because of Liverpool's £25million asking price.

    Rafa Benitez was keen to bring Argentina's World Cup skipper with him to the San Siro and Inter president Massimo Moratti says they now want Juventus' Brazilian midfielder Melo.

    "Mascherano is a strong player, but it is unlikely that he will arrive," said Moratti. "He simply costs too much and it is not certain that Liverpool will sell.

    "Melo is an intriguing idea and one that we are working on."
