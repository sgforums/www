    Robben rejects exit talk

    Winger happy to stay at Real

    Real Madrid winger Arjen Robben has played down talk linking him with a move back to the Premier League.

    Robben's future at El Santiago Bernabeu has been the subject of intense speculation following the big-money arrivals of Cristiano Ronaldo, Kaka and Karim Benzema.

    Reports have suggested Real are prepared to offload Robben and a host of other players as they look to recoup some money after splashing out over £180million already this summer.

    Tottenham have been credited with an interest in Robben, but the former Chelsea star insists he is eager to stay at Real.

    "I am very happy at the club, full of confidence and I am really looking forward to next season," Robben told Sky Sports News.

    Asked if he would be seen back in the Premier League for the new season, Robben added: "No, I don't think so."
