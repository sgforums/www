    Saturday

    Wigan v Manchester City     - (7.45pm)
    Manchester City have won just one of their last 16 Premiership away games. Only Sheffield United have a worse conversion rate than City who have scored with just five percent of their shots. Wigan have only kept one clean sheet in their last 15 Premiership matches.

    Charlton v Watford - (10pm)
    There have been 11 goals in the last three meetings between Charlton and Watford with The Addicks winning their last four home encounters with The Hornets. Watford have the worst passing accuracy in the top flight, connecting with just 55 percent of their distribution.

    Chelsea v Portsmouth - (10pm)
    Portsmouth have failed to score against Chelsea in The Premiership in six attempts. The champions have never failed to score at least two goals in Premiership matches against Pompey and are unbeaten in their last 48 Premiership home games - a league record.

    Everton v Sheffield United - (10pm)
    These two sides have not played each other since United were relegated from the Premiership in 1994. The Blades have won five of their last seven meetings with the Toffeemen but have failed to score in their four Premiership away games.

    Aston Villa v Fulham - (0.15)
    Villa have taken 13 points from 15 in home games against Fulham, who have managed just one goal in their five Premiership trips to Villa Park.
    The Cottagers need just two points to equal their away points tally from the 2005-06 season and have scored 99 goals on their travels in Premiership history.

    Sunday

    Manchester United v Liverpool - (8.pm)
    Liverpool have not managed to score more than once at Old Trafford in The Premiership since 1995. The scorer of those two goals, however, was Robbie Fowler - now back at the club. But United have failed to score in three of their last six home league games against Liverpool, the Merseysiders winning all three 1-0.

    Blackburn v Bolton - (10pm)
    Bolton haven't conceded against Blackburn in almost six hours of Premiership football and Rovers have failed to score in three of their last four Premiership home games against Bolton. Rovers have not kept a clean sheet at home in The Premiership this season.

    Middlesbrough v Newcastle - (10pm)
    Newcastle have only ever lost one of their 10 Premiership trips to Middlesbrough but three of the last four meetings between the two clubs have ended as draws. Shola Ameobi has netted three times against Middlesbrough - more than he has bagged against any other team in The Premiership.

    Tottenham v West Ham - (10pm)
    Spurs are unbeaten in their last five Premiership home games against West Ham but have won just 18 of 68 Premiership London derbies at White Hart Lane. Former favourite Teddy Sheringham has only ever scored one goal in 11 Premiership games against his former club Spurs.

    Reading v Arsenal - (11pm)
    Arsenal have played Reading five times in their history and won every game.
    But only Everton and Bolton have a better conversion rate this season than Reading who have found the net with 15 percent of their attempts.
