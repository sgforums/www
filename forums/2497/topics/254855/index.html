    Jose takes aim at press

    Chelsea boss Jose Mourinho has urged the media to end their hounding of his children following his caution by the police on Tuesday evening.

    The Portuguese was arrested and later released without charge following a misunderstanding concerning the documents required for the registration of his dog.

    Mourinho attended his press conference on Wednesday and took aim at sections of the press who chose to follow his children to school.

    "If I have to say something more about it, it is please contact your colleagues and leave my kids alone," he said on Sky Sports News. "If you want to help me, please do that.

    "Because to go to school followed by ten cameras, 30-40 photographers and 20 more people behind is not a nice situation for the kids.

    "You have me all the time, you have me when you want more or less, so enjoy me and leave my kids to get on with their life."
