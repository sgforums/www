    Tottenham have won the race to sign Belgian starlet Paul-Jose Mpoku.

    The 16-year-old striker spent time on trial at White Hart Lane last season, but was also being tracked by a host of clubs including Manchester United.

    However, Spurs have now sealed a deal with Mpoku's current club Standard Liege.

    Mpoku is reported to have agreed a four-year deal and will link up with the north London club for pre-season training.

    "Paul-Jose will play for Spurs' Under-17 team and live with a host family," a Liege spokesman told the Sunday Mirror.
