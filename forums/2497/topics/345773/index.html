    League Fee

     

    Weekend League

    For a Saturday or Sunday League, each league match costs SGD 150 per team (and this includes fixture, field, referee) to be paid in advance of 4 League Games Installments

    I.e. SGD 600 before every four games

     

    Midweek League

    For a Midweek League, each league match costs SGD 220 per team (and this includes fixture, field, spotlights, referee) to be paid in advance of 4 League Games Installments.

    I.e. SGD 880 before every four games

     

    Futsal League

    For a Futsal League, each league match costs SGD 82.50 per team (and this includes fixture, field, spotlights, referee) to be paid in full in advanced

    I.e. SGD 1120 for 14 games in a season
