Reds duo aim to copy United pair

  • turbo_drift
    7 Jun 07, 10:19
    Liverpool new boys Krisztian Nemeth and Andras Simon are hoping to emulate the successful strike partnership of Andy Cole and Dwight Yorke at Anfield.

    The Reds beat a host of clubs to the signatures of the highly-rated duo from MTK Hungaria.

    18-year-old Nemeth and Simon, 17, have signed five year deals at Anfield and Nemeth admits once Liverpool came in for him his mind was made up to go to the Merseysiders.

    "Many clubs showed interest but now it doesn't matter which ones because I've chosen the best one," Nemeth told Vasarnap Reggel.

    "I had only one wish when I signed my contract: I want to play as much as possible."

    Nemeth has vowed to do all he can to win a place in Rafa Benitez's plans and he revealed the two Hungarians hope to be as good a strike partnership as Cole and Yorke were during their time together at Old Trafford.

    "I know that I have to develop physically and I'm sure we'll have tough training in Liverpool.

    "But I'm very motivated, I'll work till I die!"

    "'We [himself and Simon] would like to be the white Yorke and Cole in Liverpool."
