    Frank Arnesen to Step Down as Chelsea Sporting Director

    Chelsea sporting director Frank Arnesen will leave his post at the end of the season, the Premier League club said on its website.

    Arnesen has overseen Chelsea’s youth development and academy since joining the club from Tottenham in 2005. He also coordinates first-team activities and works with manager Carlo Ancelotti.

    “We have a great crop of young players now coming through, and I feel it’s the right time for me to start looking towards a new challenge,” Arnesen said on the Chelsea website.
