    Inter coach Jose Mourinho has raised the bar for his side by revealing that he wants to win everything and he sniggered at Milan’s ambitions for the UEFA Cup. His latest rant and rave is likely to cause another stir in Serie A.

    “We want to win everything, but not just this year, we want to create a group who can also win over time,” Mourinho told LA 7 TV.

    “Moratti has the same ideas as me and we are working in that direction.

    “We, like Juve, Roma and Fiorentina have to try and do what the English clubs have been doing in the Champions League and that is to get as many teams in the semi-finals.

    “We can only become enemies once we get through the group stages.

    “Milan? They have no choice but to win the UEFA Cup. They are a team who has high objectives so if they play in it, they have to win it.”

    The Nerazzurri take on Sevilla in the Amsterdam tournament later this evening.

    Mourinho’s latest rant against Milan is likely to cause the Rossoneri to react as the lyrical assaults continue to fly about.
