    STEWART DOWNING WARNING TO UNBEATEN MANCHESTER CITY

     

    Stewart Downing's warning

     

     

     

     

     

     

     

     

     

     

     

     

     

     

     

    Sunday, 27 November 2011

     

    Liverpool v Man City     12:00     mio : 102 / 110 

     

     

     

    LIVERPOOL winger Stewart Downing has warned unbeaten league leaders Manchester City: ‘We’re coming to get you!’

    The Anfield winger firmly believes the Reds are capable of inflicting a first Premier League defeat of the season on Roberto Mancini’s free-scoring side.

    Downing said: “I think we should look forward to the game and say ‘Why can’t we be the first team to beat them?’

    “We are at home at Anfield so I’m confidentwe can get a result. We have belief in our own ability and confidence is flying, especially the way we won at Chelsea.

    “Inside the club we have the belief that we have a really good team but we have quietly gone about our business and got some good results.”

    The England star added: “We made a big statement on Sunday by beating Chelsea.”
