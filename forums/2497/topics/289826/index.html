    Vedran Corluka reckons Sven Goran Eriksson has turned Manchester City into one of the Premier League's entertainers.

    The former England boss has performed a remarkable revolution in only a short time at Eastlands - and will lead them back up to third place if they beat Sunderland on Monday.

    But Corluka believes one of his most impressive achievements has been to turn the Blues into one of the league's most stylish outfits.

    "We like to play the ball on the floor and people enjoy that.," he said.

    "We have a lot of skilful players and our results overall have been good.

    "No-one really expected us to be high in the table at this stage but I feel we can still improve."
