    Liverpool boss Rafa Benitez admits they'll be cheering on Manchester City in today's derby against Premiership leaders Manchester United.

    Benitez's men are above Manchester United on goal difference following the 3-0 win at Liverpool, courtesy of two goals from Steven Gerrard and a late Ryan Babel strike.

    "It's a derby and if they make a mistake they will be feeling the pressure. Manchester City are a difficult team to play against."
