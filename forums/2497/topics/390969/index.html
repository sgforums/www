  • Poignant

    Klaas-Jan Huntelaar out to impress Manchester United as Sir Alex Ferguson plots summer swoop for AC Milan

    Glowing Dutch: Milan's Huntelaar

    Klaas-Jan Huntelaar is set to line up against Manchester United in what could be an audition for a summer move to Old Trafford.

    The Holland striker, long admired by Sir Alex Ferguson, is ready to quit AC Milan in the summer unless he is handed a regular starting place by boss Leonardo.

    Huntelaar, who scored twice in his side's 3-2 defeat of Udinese on Friday, is expected to play up front in the Champions League first leg against United because of injury to Mario Borriello.

    Reports in Italy claim United have renewed interest in the 26-year-old, who has struggled since a summer move from Real Madrid.

    Milan could be ready to cash in as they prepare a summer move for Wolfsburg striker Edin Dzeko

    Huntelaar agitated for a move in January because of his fears of missing out on the World Cup and is only prepared to see out his three-year contract if he sees more football.
