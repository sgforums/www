    Blackburn have signed experienced Switzerland international midfielder Johann Vogel until 2010 after the 31-year-old impressed during a trial.

    Vogel has played at the highest level in Europe with Real Betis, AC Milan, and PSV Eindhoven among his previous clubs.

    The defensive midfielder, who captained the Swiss at the 2006 World Cup, was a free agent after being released from Betis following a legal dispute with the club.

    Subject to international clearance, Vogel has signed a two-and-a-half-year deal with the option of a further year at Ewood Park.
