    Undies for Losers

     

    This time they've designed an underwear to help garner support for their cause!

    Austrian graphic-designer Stefanie Schoeffmann presents a mens underpants with a slogan reading ‘hosted by losers’ in Vienna this November 22, 2007 file photo. Austrians underwhelmed by their soccer teams’ sporting prowess can make light of the matter with a range of clothing and underwear in the national colours bearing the slogan “hosted by losers”. Produced by Schoeffmann, 27, the slogan mimics the official slogan used by the German organisers of the 2006 World Cup of “hosted by friends”. “This is just another way of celebrating the fact that we are staging Euro 2008.” Schoeffmann said.
