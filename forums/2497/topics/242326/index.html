Saviola resigned to Barca exit

  • turbo_drift

    Javier Saviola has conceded he does not expect to stay at Barcelona next season.

    The Argentine striker is a free agent at the end of the season when his contract with the Spanish giants expires and as yet he has not received any offer of a new deal.

    Saviola is resigned to leaving Camp Nou with a number of clubs thought to be waiting in the wings to sign the diminutive forward.

    "I think it will be difficult for me to continue with Barcelona," said Saviola.

    "My first option is to stay, but the club still hasn't made an offer to me.

    "If the situation does not change it is normal that I will leave the club."

    Barcelona's sporting director Txiki Beguiristain also cast doubt over Saviola's future by revealing his contract situation was not a priority for the club.

    "It is still too soon to speak about the subject of Saviola," said Beguiristain.

    "It is still not a priority for us."
