Real Madrid sign Hamit Altintop on a four-year contract

  • Bus&Soccer l0v3r (VO3x 1)
    20 May 11, 22:32

    Real Madrid have announced the signing of Hamit Altintop on a four-year contract.

    The Turkish midfielder has left Bayern Munich after four campaigns with the Bundesliga side, decided to leave the Allianz Arena on a Bosman after struggling to make an impact on the first team in 2010-11.

    Altintop is the second piece of transfer business conducted by the Spanish giants for next season, after the capture of Nuri Sahin from Borussia Dortmund last week.

    A statement on the club's official website reads: "Real Madrid C.F and former Bayern Munich player Hamit Altintop have reached an agreement in which he will join the club for the next four seasons."
