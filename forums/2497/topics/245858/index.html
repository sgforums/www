Robben staying at Chelsea

  • turbo_drift
    1 Apr 07, 13:29
    Arjen Robben will not be leaving Chelsea in the summer, according to the player's agent.

    Robben's agent Ton Smit has held talks with The Blues' management regarding his client's future at Stamford Bridge.

    The Dutch ace has endured a below-par season in West London with injury and loss of form restricting him to just 16 Premiership starts.

    Robben has been linked with a move away from Chelsea with Barcelona reportedly interested, however, Smit has dispelled the rumours.

    "The Chelsea management have been quite clear that Arjen is not leaving," he is quoted in a newspaper.

    Robben has been a big hit with Chelsea fans following his big-money move from PSV Eindhoven back in 2004.
