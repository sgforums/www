Capello has MLS offer

  • omgukilledkenny

    Former Real Madrid coach Fabio Capello has confirmed he has been offered the chance to manage in MLS.

    The vastly experienced Italian tactician is currently on the look-out for a new job after being given his marching orders by Real despite guiding the Spanish giants to the Liga title.

    The 61-year-old former Roma, Juventus and Milan tactician is mulling over the chance to work in America, but has not yet divulged which club have targeted his services.

    "They have contacted me from the United States, however I must think about it," he told Gazzetta dello Sport.

    "It would be a new experience that would intrigue me not for the money but to see if anyone could make American soccer take off."

    Capello also had options to work in Ukraine and Russia, however, he has already decided to shun Dynamo Kiev and Lokomotiv Moscow's advances.

    "Dynamo Kiev and Lokomotiv (Moscow) have come to me with interesting work propositions but I have left it," he concluded.
