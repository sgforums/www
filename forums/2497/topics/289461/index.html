Berbatov To Hold Talks Over Future

  • omgukilledkenny

    Dimitar Berbatov is set to hold talks with Tottenham Hotspur over his future at the club and will make a decision whether to stay or go on Sunday.

    The Bulgarian striker is reported to be disillusioned after Martin Jol's exit from the Premiership outfit and wants talks to about the situation.

    New Tottenham coach Juande Ramos and sporting director Daniel Comolli will meet with Berbatov and his agent, Emil Dantchev, in the next few days.

    After having his contract improved over the summer, Berbatov appeared to be happy at White Hart Lane, but the recent chain of events has unnerved him.

    Despite that, Dantchev was giving awy no clues as to whether the highly-rated striker would commit his future to the club or state a desire to move on.

    "In five days the situation will be more clear but until then it is not my job to sya whether Dimi is happy or not," the agent said.

    After Jol was sacked last week, one rumour suggested that Berbatov told the Dutchman that if he was sacked then he wold follow him out of the club.

    Whether that is true is unknown, but Berbatov appears to be becoming more and more unsettled as Spurs season has not gone as expected.
