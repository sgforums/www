    Blues go back to black! Chelsea unveil new away kit ahead of Premier League title defence

    Back to black: Chelsea's new away jersey

    Being an Italian, Carlo Ancelotti demands his side do everything with a sense of style.

    Chelsea irresistibly strutted their way to a historic Premier League and FA Cup double last season - smashing home 120 goals in the process.

    Ancelotti will again hope his side are the talk among fashionistas on the Kings Road next season after the Stamford Bridge club unveiled their brand new away kit.

    The new black second strip  - which will be worn for the first time when the Blues face Hamburg in a pre-season friendly on August 4 - benefits from adidas' cutting edge Climacool and TechFit technology.

    John Terry and co are the bookies favourties to win the title again next season.

    But even if they fail in their quest to retain their Premier League crown - at least they'll look good.

    The new kit will be available to buy from August 5.
