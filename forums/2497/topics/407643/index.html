    Ireland wants to be highest paid at Aston Villa to leave Man City

    Aston Villa must make Stephen Ireland the club's biggest earner to seal James Milner's sale to Manchester City.

    The People says Villa have finally accepted City's offer of £17 million cash and 23-year-old Ireland valued at £10 million as part of the deal.

    However, Ireland is still considering the switch to the Midlands while Villa fear the player's £72,000-a-week wages could be a major stumbling block.

    Ashley Young is Villa's highest earner on £65,000-a-week and many of O'Neill's established stars could seek better deals if Ireland joins.
