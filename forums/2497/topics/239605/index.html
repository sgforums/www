    Gary Neville was reportedly forced to travel down to London just to be told he was dropped by Sir Alex Ferguson.

    According to The Sun, Neville travelled to London expecting to face Fulham on Saturday lunchtime, only to be told he was being dropped as punishment for his rant at Ferguson during the Champions League clash with Lille.

    Neville was incensed with his manager for telling him not to get involved when Lille players were threatening to walk off the pitch and his tirade was captured on TV.
