Torres: Ronaldo carries United

  • zocoss
    18 Apr 08, 10:56

    FERNANDO TORRES has told Manchester United: You’re nothing without Ronaldo. The Liverpool striker reckons Barcelona can dump United out of Europe by stopping their superstar winger Cristiano Ronaldo.

    Torres, 24, said: “I watch Ronaldo every weekend and he is massively impressive. He carries United on his back in some matches. “Barca have no idea how spectacular Ronaldo is or how far ahead of every other player in the world he is right now. "But if they keep the ball away from him Barca will get to the Champions League final. “United suffer against teams who keep possession of the ball.”

    As for Liverpool’s semi with Chelsea, Torres warned: “It won’t be as colourful as the amazing game against Arsenal. “It will be a really tactical and strategic game. “Just one tiny detail, one mistake or one set-play will decide our semi-final.”
