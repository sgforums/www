    Liverpool approach Stoke for Beattie

    Liverpool have sounded out Stoke City about a price for James Beattie.

    The People says Reds boss Rafa Benitez is desperate to add more firepower and provide some much-needed cover for Fernando Torres. But his targets are significantly limited by the club's financial plight.

    Stoke striker Beattie would, however, be well within his price range and, following his bust-up with manager Tony Pulis last weekend, is available in January.

    Beattie's wages - believed to be around £20,000 a week - would not be a problem for cash-strapped Liverpool, as the player would cost only around £500,000.
