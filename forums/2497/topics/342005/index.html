Mourinho: This Inter Is Like My Old Chelsea Side

    The 'Special One' has drawn comparisons between his former Blues side and the Nerazzurri.

    Inter coach Jose Mourinho believes his current Inter side is the same as the Chelsea team he coached during the 2004/05 season.

    The 'Special One' joined Inter after he was sacked by the Blues. Mourinho won many trophies in West London and he is on course to do the same in Milan.

    Inter are speeding away in the Scudetto race as the others look on and the Nerazzurri coach is using the Christmas break to boast about his squad.

    "My Inter are almost resembling the Chelsea of 2004/05; a winning machine," Mourinho told Inter Channel.

    "I want to make this club one of the best and I am happy about the work which has been done so far.

    "I have such strong players that I have the feeling I can go on and win anywhere and anything with this team.

    "Inter are playing some spectacular football and it seems like this year's campaign is better than last term's."

    The Beneamata have a six point advantage over Juventus in the Serie A race and they will be back in action on January 10th against Cagliari.
