Everton Sign Chinese "Football Idol"

  • omgukilledkenny
    17 May 08, 20:40

    Everton have completed a deal that will bring the winner of a Chinese reality show called “Soccer Prince” to Goodison Park.

    19 year old, Jin Hui beat off competition from 18,000 other young hopefuls to clinch the top prize of a place in Everton’s academy.

    Chinese officials claim that up to 130 million people watched as Jin Hui make it through the selection process to win the once-in-a-lifetime opportunity.

    The programme and indeed, the final selection decision was overseen by Everton Academy director Ray Hall. He praises the honesty of the programme makers and explained that although Jin Hui will spend time at Everton, he will not be guaranteed a spot in David Moyes’ first team squad.

    Hall said: “The prize is an experience at a Premier League club, obviously our own. It wasn't about the winner 'will play for Everton'.

    "I think if Jin Hui achieves a professional contract anywhere around the world then it will be a real achievement for him."

    The winner himself told BBC Radio Merseyside: "There are many people who like the English Premier League in China. I can improve a lot through training and playing in the games."
