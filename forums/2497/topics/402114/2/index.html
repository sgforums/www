    There's a bet type called "1/2 goal" in Sg Pools.

    Usually the favorite team out of the 2 teams involved in a match will be assigned to "give ball" while the underdog team will "eat ball".

    E.g. SA vs MEX, sgpool think that SA is the favorite, so SA will give 1 1/2 goals (-1.5) and likewise MEX will eat 1 1/2 goals (+1.5). This means that at the start of the game (0-0), the betting score is actually SA 0 MEX 1.5. SA need to win by 2 goals or above to clear this handicap, and likewise for people who bet on MEX +1.5, they just need MEX to NOT LOSE by 2 goals or above to win the bet.
