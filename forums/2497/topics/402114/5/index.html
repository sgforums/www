    what's above and under?

    Usually Above and Under means the bookie / Singapore pools will give a predetermine amount with a 0.5 (Example on Denmark VS Japan is Above / Under 2.5).

    So you can bet either the match Above 2.5 (Meaning Denmark & Japan together must score more than or equal to 3 goals) or Under 2.5 (Meaning Denmark & Japan together must score either 0, 1 or 2 goals)

    To simplify: If you choose Under 2.5 for this match (Which I did) means you must pray for the result to be either of the following and you will win:

    Denmark VS Japan

    0 V 0

    0 V 1

    0 V 2

    1 V 0

    1 V 1

    2 V 0

    And any other score means Above 2.5 wins (Which happened to be so, since Denmark VS Japan was 1 V 3)
