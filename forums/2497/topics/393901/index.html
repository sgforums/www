    TOP TARGET ... Fernando Torres

    THE second revolution at Chelsea will begin this summer with a £50million bid for Fernando Torres.

    Roman Abramovich has told Carlo Ancelotti he will have up to TWICE that amount to overhaul the squad.

    Red Rom has put his faith in the Italian and will back that up with a monster transfer kitty. Several players will also leave.

    Ancelotti is hopeful of signing at least two big names, with Torres top of his hit-list closely followed by Atletico Madrid striker Sergio Aguero.

    Bayern Munich midfielder Bastian Schweinsteiger, Inter Milan right-back Maicon and a new keeper are targets too.

    Abramovich told Liverpool to name their price for the Spain striker last summer, as he attempted to start the reign of Ancelotti with a bang.

    The Anfield club refused to sell but financial pressures could change their position this summer.

    Last week, Torres even admitted he may have to leave Liverpool but that he wanted to stay in England.

    Manchester City are also preparing a bid. Yet if they fail to qualify for the Champions League and Liverpool do likewise, the Londoners will be favourites to sign Torres.

    Ancelotti has met Abramovich twice since the defeat by Inter on Tuesday night and was assured his job was safe.

    A further get-together has been planned for next week, when details of the summer targets will be discussed as well as a review of the entire football operation.

    A senior source told SunSport: "Roman has recognised things need to change at the club to make it successful in the way he wants it to be.

    "He's willing to spend big on star players but there will also be an effort to recruit young talent, which can be nurtured into the first team.

    "Along with all of that, Ancelotti will be asked to consult on a sweeping review of the whole football department - including the coaches and the academy.

    "The plans are beginning to take shape and, as well as new faces, there will be a few departures too."

    Salomon Kalou Set To Lead Summer Exodus As Chelsea Plan Massive Clearout - Report

    Chelsea are ready to launch a summer clear-out of Carlo Ancelotti's squad, with striker Salomon Kalou heading up a list of star names who could be set to depart, according to a report from The Guardian.

    Deco, Ricardo Carvalho, Paulo Ferreira, Juliano Belletti are also believed to be set to depart from Stamford Bridge, with Deco having recently told Brazilian radio that a move back to South America in the summer would suit him for family reasons.

    Contract negotiations with Nicolas Anelka, Joe Cole and Michael Ballack could all be affected by a reported desire amongst the Chelsea hierarchy to revitalise an ageing and seemingly somewhat jaded squad.

    Arsenal boss Arsene Wenger has been rumoured to be interested in making a move for Kalou in the past, with the Gunners chief believed to have made an enquiry when he sold Emmanuel Adebayor to Manchester City.
