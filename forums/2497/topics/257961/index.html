Gunners heading to Amsterdam

  • mcfadden
    31 May 07, 13:07
    Arsenal have been confirmed as one of the sides who will play in the prestigious Amsterdam Tournament this summer.

    The four-team tournament played at the home of Dutch titans Ajax is one of the most high-profile of the pre-season competitions.

    Manchester United won the eighth edition of the tournament last summer, but it is Arsene Wenger's Arsenal who will represent the Premiership this year.

    Italian outfit Lazio and Spanish side Atletico Madrid make up the competition which will take place at the start of August.

    "Ajax first plays Atletico Madrid (on 2nd August) and on Saturday (4th August) the Dutch Cup winners will encounter Arsenal in the Amsterdam ArenA," read a statement on Ajax's official website.
