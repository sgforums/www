    Wenger: Told to stop whining...

     

     

    Saturday, 10 January 2009

    Channel 27:  "Live"
     
    Arsenal v Bolton  - 11:00 pm 
     
     
     

    GARY MEGSON has told Arsenal boss Arsene Wenger to stop making a fuss ahead of Bolton’s trip to the Emirates tomorrow. 

    Wenger makes no secret of his dislike for Wanderers, who have been one of the Gunners’ bogey teams over the years. 

    Yet Trotters gaffer Megson reckons Arsenal have given as good as they have got in the two games since he took charge. 

     

    FLASHPOINT ... Diaby's foul

     

    Arsenal were fuming at Kevin Davies’ tackle on Gael Clichy in September but Megson insists Wenger’s men are no saints. 

    He said: “We didn’t make anywhere near the fuss that would have been made by others when Abou Diaby was sent off for the tackle on Gretar Steinsson. It was shocking but we just accepted it.” 

    Yet the Gunners called Davies’ tackle on Clichy ’disgraceful’. 

    Megson said: “There was a complete furore over it. Yes, it was a firm challenge but he has momentum and has caught Clichy. But he trained on the Tuesday! 

    “I can’t speak about before I was here but our last two games with Arsenal have been really good.It’s not bad being viewed as having problems when you are fifth top and in the Champions League. I wish we had their problems!”
