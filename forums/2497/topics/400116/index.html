    Filippo Inzaghi Signs One Year Extension With Milan

    Filippo Inzaghi has reached an agreement with Milan and signed a new one year contract, the club said in a statement on Friday.

    Uncertainty surrounded Inzaghi's future as his old deal expired in June, and he was linked with a move to Parma where he would have teamed up with life-long friend Tommaso Ghirardi.

    However, the player can look forward to another season in Milan after CEO Adriano Galliani offered him a new deal.

    A statement on the club's official website reads:

    "A.C. Milan communicates that the football Filippo Inzaghi has renewed his contract until June 30, 2011."

    Inzaghi made his debut for Milan in September 2001 against Bate Borisov after joining from Juventus.
