    Malaysia vs Singapore; It's about silencing noisy neighbours

     

     

    Malaysia vs Singapore

     

    28 July 2011 

    Channel 5 : 8.45 pm (Live)

     

     

     

     

    THE MIND games continue ahead of the return leg of the Causeway Derby.

     

    Dismissing Singapore as "not much of a team" without their five naturalised players, Malaysia captain Safiq Rahim also suggested that the Lions couldn't deal with the 85,000 Tigers fans that will be baying for their blood at the Bukit Jalil Stadium tomorrow.

    However, Singapore have a good record away from home in front of crowds much larger than the 6,000-capacity Jalan Besar Stadium.

    In fact, the Lions themselves are relishing the thought of silencing their noisy neighbours.
