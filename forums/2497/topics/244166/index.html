  • zocoss
    22 Mar 07, 23:39


    The question is... Is Real Madrid Losing its attraction?

    Real Madrid has not won the Spanish title in the last 3 yrs, and during this time they didn't even came close to Barce in the league...

    As we saw from last season... Players like Fabregas and Kaka have turn them down or are not interested to join them when they came calling...

    Now we are hearing that their no. 1 target for next season Ronaldo is also not interested in joining them... And if you thought that it couldn't get any worse... Rafael Benitez, a Spaniard himself who they had put right at the top of their wanted list as manager today stated that he is 'completely committed' to the Anfield club and wasn't interested on taking up the managerial job there.

    Seems like lately, players are refusing to join them that they ended up buying players that their clubs wanted to get rid off... Players like 33 yrs old Fabio Cannavaro, highly paid but was getting on in years... Juventus got rid of him for roughly 20 million euros at the same time reducing a huge chunk in their salary budget after being relegated to the lower league... Next, Ruud van Nistelrooy... another 30 yrs old player also on a very high salary with his previous club. His previous club wanted to get rid off him too after arguments with its manager... Then there is David Beckham... Real had a new 2-4 yrs contract on the table for him but the former England captain decided to go to the USA instead... He choose LA Galaxy over them... what more can you say...

    Real is probably going through what Barce went through during the late 90s and early 2000 when they were winning almost everythings while Barce stand and watch. They seem to be in decline over the last few years... But now there appear to be a silver lining... Barce are beginning to look not as great this season as they were over the last 3 years and should they lose Frank Rijkaard for next season... Who knows, could be Real's best chance for a comeback.
