Fulham looks to Boumsong to shore up defence

  • omgukilledkenny
    8 Jan 08, 23:45


    Skysports.com understands Fulham are considering a move to bring Jean-Alain Boumsong back to England.

    Boumsong is weighing up his future after dropping down the pecking order at Juventus and could be interested in a move during this month's transfer window.

    The French international was brought to Juventus by Didier Deschamps, but he has not figured regularly under Claudio Ranieri.

    New Fulham boss Roy Hogdson is drawing up his list of transfer targets as he looks to bolster his squad and he has made defensive reinforcements one of his top priorities.

    Hodgson is now mulling over a move for Boumsong as he aims to shore up his leaky defence.

    Boumsong endured a difficult spell in England with Newcastle before his move to Juventus and he could be keen on a return to the Premier league to prove his doubters wrong.
