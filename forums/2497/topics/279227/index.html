    MATCH UPS: Man marking.

    MIDFIELD STRIPE: The halfway line.

    ON FRAME: A shot on target.

    ON THE DOORSTEP: In front of the goal.

    REAL ESTATE: Used to describe the space or lack of space in the penalty area.
