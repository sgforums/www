    Manchester City Fan Tattoos 'Champions League Winners 2011' On His Shoulder

    One of the Citizens has taken to the needle and ink following the club's mega-spending during the summer transfer window...


    Manchester City's latest signing Emmanuel Adebayor shows a preview of his trademark goal celebration after being revealed as a Citizen (PA)

    A Manchester City fan has gone a step ahead and proclaimed through body art that the club will win the Champions League next season.

    Kirk Bradley, a 25-year-old Citizen has inked 'Champions League winners 2011' on his right shoulder, along with a picture of the trophy.

    "I really believe we have the quality and the ambition to do it," Bradley is quoted by The Irish Independent as saying.

    "All my mates think I'm crazy, but I don't care.

    "At least no one can doubt that I am a true fan."

    The Sky Blues have started their Premier League campaign with minimum fuss, registering a 2-0 victory against Blackburn Rovers. Their first real test of their strength will be on September 12 against Arsenal.
