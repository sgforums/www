Scolari joins Uzbek champions

  • omgukilledkenny

    Former Chelsea manager Luiz Felipe Scolari has been appointed as the new coach of FC Bunyodkor, according to the Uzbek club.

    Bunyodkor may be in a relative football backwater but they boast both ambition and extensive funds and currently employ World Cup winner Rivaldo as their star player.

    The club also count Zico amongst their former managers and now another Brazilian, Scolari, has joined the club.

    ‘Big Phil’ parted company with Chelsea at the start of February following some unconvincing form and has made an unexpected return to the game.

    Any public declaration from Bunyodkor is bound to be viewed with suspicion after they incorrectly claimed they had signed Samuel Eto’o last season, but Scolari has signed an 18-month deal, according to their official website.

    “Uzbek football now creates its own success story, especially Bunyodkor with its remarkable dynamism and prospects for the past four years,” said Scolari.

    “I know that I am in the right place at the right time and in the right team.”
