    AC Milan star Ronaldinho cannot rule out a summer move to Manchester City.

    The former World Player of the Year has been linked with City after failing to breaking into the starting XI at Milan since the turn of the year.

    "When I'm playing well I am happy," said Ronaldinho. "I have to respect the professional judgments of the coaches, but I cannot say that I'm happy if I am on the bench."

    Asked directly about City, Ronaldinho replied: "At Milan I'm happy, but in life never say never."
