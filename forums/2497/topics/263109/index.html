    Nedved Set To Retire - Report

    The Czech Republic midfielder is destined to hang up his boots this summer, even though there still exists a slight chance that the former Golden Ball winner changes his mind.

    Apparently the decision will be taken next week, unless the club decides to call him up for a meeting to convince him to remain for another year as the Bianconeri prepare to face a difficult season back in the topflight after returning from a season of Serie B football.

    This would undoubtedly be a huge loss for the Old Lady, even though with the acquisition of the new players, Tiago and Almiron, it is likely that the Czech player would have found less space in the Bianconeri's first eleven next season.

    Nedved has been with Juve for the past 6 seasons after joining the Bianconeri from Lazio, following 5 years in the Capital city side.
