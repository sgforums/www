    Gabriel Milito's move to Juventus has been thrown into doubt following a fresh bid from Liverpool.



    Juventus were said to have practically completed the signing of Gabriel Milito for a fee in the region of 20 million euros.

    The move was set to go through following this evening's La Liga action, with Zaragoza keen to concentrat on securing a Uefa Cup berth first.

    However, The Sunday Star claim that the Reds, who have also been following the player, have made a 20 million euro bid of their own.

    The player had previously declared his desire to move to England, though his latest comments put him closer to Italy.

    "I just want everything to be solved in the best possible way, because I want to play for Juventus," he said earlier this week.

    "Playing in Italy - the land of my forefathers - has always been a dream for me."

    However, it is understood that Liverpool are willing to offer him a more lucrative contract. The coming week will tell!
