Hleb happy at Barca

  • omgukilledkenny
    2 Jul 09, 18:47

    Barcelona's Alexander Hleb has received transfer offers from England and Germany but is happy at Camp Nou, according to the midfielder's agent.

    The former Arsenal player has struggled for first-team football in the Catalan capital since quitting the Premier League last summer.

    Stuttgart, where Hleb played for five seasons before joining the Gunners in 2005, are keen on a reunion, while Aston Villa have been mentioned as admirers.

    The 28-year-old's representative claims he has been approached by clubs around Europe, but insists his client is content with European champions Barcelona after being given assurances.

    Uli Ferber told Sport: "We have four or five offers from England and Germany.

    "The club knows this but sporting director (Txiki Beguiristain) has told me that Alexander has a guaranteed place in the squad and that is decided by the coach (Pep Guardiola).

    "Hleb is happy at Barcelona."
