    Asean Football Championship 2007: Group B

    vs

    Singapore vs Indonesia

    Wednesday 17 Jan - Ch 24 - 8:00pm - (Live)

    Venue : Kallang National Stadium

    Defending champions Singapore play their last group match against the Indonesian in Group B of the Asean Football Championship.

    Following the draw against Vietnam on Sat and the thrashing of Laos on Monday, Singapore will next play Indonesia on Wednesday, 17 January 2007.

    Tickets are available at the FAS Office, Jalan Besar Stadium from Mondays to Wednesday, during office hours (9am to 5pm).

    Tickets are also available on Match Day, tickets can be purchased at the National Stadium (on 17 Jan), East & West Entrances, and Jalan Besar Stadium (on 17 January), North & South Entrances, two hours before kickoff time.
