    David Villa could move to Spurs in £20m deal

     

     

     

    Tottenham have reportedly agreed a £20 million deal to sign Valencia striker David Villa. Villa has attracted plenty of headlines with his goal-scoring form for Spain at Euro 2008, and has been linked with a host of top clubs since it became clear Valencia were willing to sell.

    Chelsea, Arsenal, Real Madrid and Barcelona have all been touted as possible buyers. However, the Sunday Mirror claims Villa has been Spurs' No 1 target for some time and that manager Juande Ramos has said he will pay the £20m asking price.

    It had been reported that Villa did not want to leave Valencia, but the Spanish international has since come out and said: "In the event that Valencia told me they were going to sell me then I would address my options.

    "It's an issue between the clubs. It's not something I'm thinking about. "My focus is on this tournament and making sure Spain do well. This is not a time to be thinking about my personal future."
