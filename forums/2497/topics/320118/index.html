    AOS means either team scores 6 goals or more i.e. 6-0, 6-4 etc, and also including the highly impossible 5-5 result (4-4 is not included).

    9+ goals is simply an outright total goal tally of 9 goals or more, which can be 5-4 (which will not win you anything if you bought AOS).

    AOS means "Any Other Score" that is not offered in the list. There are only 2 conditions:
    1. Any one team scoring 6 goals or more, regardless of how the other team has performed.
    2. A score of 5-5.
