    FA to interview Howard over Emre racial abuse allegations

    The Football Association is seeking to interview Tim Howard as it investigates allegations that the Newcastle midfielder Emre Belozoglu racially abused the Everton goalkeeper and his black team-mates during last Saturday's Premiership game at Goodison Park.

    The referee, Dermot Gallagher, who oversaw the hosts' 3-0 win, did not hear the comments alleged to have been made by the Turkey midfielder following the award of a penalty to the visitors immediately before half-time. However he has included mention of the claims, believed to have come from Howard, that racist abuse was made in his match report submitted to Soho Square.
