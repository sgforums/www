Friedel: 'Don't Believe The Hype!'

  • omgukilledkenny
    20 Jan 09, 22:55

    American international Brad Friedel believes that Aston Villa should ignore the hype around their league ranking, and concentrate on each game as it comes.

    No club has made the first etching on the Premier League crown yet and the fact that Liverpool are continuously dropping points means that the gap between first and fifth is now just six points.

    All of the traditional big four clubs are now regarded as in with a shout of winning the Premier League, as are fourth-placed Aston Villa.

    Martin O'Neill was recently asked in a post-match interview whether the Villans harbour title ambitions of their own, to which the Ulsterman retorted with the immortal line: "Have you been drinking?"

    Goalkeeper Brad Friedel is also quick to distance such talk, and believes it is far more important to remain focussed on each matchday.

    "I don't think about where we are in the Barclays Premier League. I think of one game at a time," he told AVTV.

    "That's all you can think about. I don't get carried away with the table. I'm not going to get carried away with any outside talk either.

    "We don't have the luxury of going into games thinking we are better than we are," he said. "We have a lot of hard work ahead of ourselves.

    "It's a tight league - and it's the toughest one in the world in my opinion - and we have to stay focused.

    "We cannot think ahead, other than the next game on the fixture list."

    Aston Villa will contest the fourth round of the FA Cup this weekend, and will travel to either Cheltenham or Doncaster - who are yet to conclude their third round replay.
