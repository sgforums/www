Schmeichel seals permanent Manchester City exit

  • omgukilledkenny

    Kasper Schmeichel has joined Notts County Football Club.

    The 22-year-old Danish goalkeeper was down the pecking order at Manchester City, and he has now joined his former Eastlands boss Sven Goran Eriksson, who is the club’s director of football.

    "It is a great signing for the club, obviously. He is one for now and one for the future," County manager Ian McParland said in the Nottingham Evening Post. "Hopefully he will be here for a long time.”

    It is believed the League Two club have smashed their transfer record to land Schmeichel, who is the son of former Manchester United great Peter.
