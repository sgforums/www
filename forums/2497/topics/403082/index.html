World Cup: Prediction of final group games

    All 32 teams now have one group game left. Let's predict the final outcome!

     

    Group A

    Prediction: Both games (Uruguay v Mexico and France v South Afria) to end in draws, Uruguay progress as surprise group winners, Mexico runners up

     

    Group B

    Prediction: Argentina to beat greece, s.korea force nigeria to a draw. ARG group winner, SKorea runners up

     

    Group C

    England to defeat Slovenia, USA to beat Algeria (hell, anyone can beat Algeria right?). Both teams overtake poor Slovenia to go through.  US top with superior goal difference.

     

    Group D

    Group D teams have the luxury to 'pick' their round of 16 group C opponents having kick off 4 1/2 hrs later. Germany, threading on thin ice having lost their 2nd group game, will do their best to defeat Ghana. Serbia and Aussies fight out an entertaining draw. Serbia and Ghana to finish level on pts. Depending on whether head-to-head result or goal difference comes first, one of them will progress as runners up behind the germans. Germany v England round of 16

     

    Group F

    Strangely, group E-F is the only one that does not go with ascending alphabetical order in terms of timing of kick offs. Group F kicks off their final grp games ahead of grp E. The italians scrap to a 1-goal victory over slovakia while Paraguay crash new zealand to finish top. ITA runners up.

     

    Group E

    Denmark defeats japan, holland fields a 2nd team and defeats cameroon. Holland 1st, danes 2nd, japanese heartbreak. Italy v Holland rd of 16

     

    Group G

    At the time of writing, Portugal is leading the north koreans by 3-0, with more than 30minutes remaining. I predict that the portugese will score at least 1 more to overtake the brazilians at the top of the grp ahead of the final game. Dunga's samba boys, feeling the pressure of missing out on top spot, will beat the portugese by one goal to finish top. POR 2nd irregardless of the outcome of the other game. POR-NKOR 4-0 already by the time i finished typing this , LOL. Nice goal Tiago.

     

    Group H

    At time of writing, 2nd round of grp games have not kicked off. I dare not make predictions after seeing european champions spain crash to minnows Switzerland. I hope Spain do a portugal tonite and crush the hondurans. Chile might edge out the swiss, going with the trend of superb results from south american teams in this year's competition.
