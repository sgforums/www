    Arsenal boss Wenger to bid for AZ star Dembele.

     

     

    Arsenal boss Arsene Wenger is ready to move for AZ Alkmaar's £5m-rated Belgian striker Moussa Dembele.

    Dembele, 21, has broken into the Belgium team after playing in last summer's Olympics.

    Wenger's scouts have checked on Dembele who plays through the middle or out wide and has four goals in 16 internationals, says the Mirror.

    AZ boss Louis van Gaal said:" He needs to improve but has the attributes to play in England or Spain."
