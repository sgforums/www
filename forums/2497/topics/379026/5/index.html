    LIFE'S A BEACH ... Darren Bent fires in Sunderland's winner off a beachball

    MIKE JONES is set to be bounced off this weekend's match list for his beachball clanger at Sunderland.

    The Cheshire ref allowed Darren Bent's winner against Liverpool to stand after it cannoned in off a red beachball thrown on to the pitch by a young Kop fan.

    But the effort should have been chalked off and the match stopped.

     

    JONES ... goal blunder

     

    Jones, 41, in his second League season, will be asked to explain his actions - as will his three assistants - after all appeared unsure about the rules.

    According to FIFA's laws, the goal should have been disallowed because of 'outside interference'.

    Jones will be hammered when the Professional Game Match Officials board meets today.

    Former top ref Dermot Gallacher said: "It was such a glaring error. Everybody knows that when something encroaches on to the pitch the game stops."
