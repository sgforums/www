    Asean Football Championship 2007: Group B

    vs

    Singapore vs Laos

    Monday 15 Jan - Ch 24 - 8:00pm - (Live)

    Venue : Kallang National Stadium

    Defending champions Singapore, are grouped in Group B of the Asean Football Championship.

    Teams in Group B are 2004 runners-up Indonesia, Laos and 1998 runners-up Vietnam. Singapore are the tournament's co-hosts, along with Thailand. All group matches would be played at Singapore's National Stadium and Jalan Besar Stadium, and Thailand's Suphachalasai Stadium and Army Stadium.

    Following the draw against Vietnam on Sat, Singapore will next play Laos on Monday, 15 January 2007 follow by Indonesia on Wednesday, 17 January 2007.

    Tickets are available at the FAS Office, Jalan Besar Stadium from Mondays to Friday, during office hours (9am to 5pm).

    Tickets are also available on Friday, 12 January 2007, from 10am to 7pm, at the East Entrance of the National Stadium.

    On Match Days, tickets can be purchased at the National Stadium (on 13, 15 and 17 Jan), East & West Entrances, and Jalan Besar Stadium (on 17 January), North & South Entrances, two hours before kickoff time.
