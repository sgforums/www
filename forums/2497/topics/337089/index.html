    BUST-UP ... Jose Mourinho on Italian TV

    JOSE MOURINHO has fuelled speculation over his future as Inter boss after he got involved in a heated bust-up live on Italian TV.

    SunSport revealed on Tuesday that the Inter Milan boss could be on his way back to the Premier League as Manchester City chief, after getting increasingly fed up with life in Italy.

    In a live interview on Sky Italia Mourinho got het up after journalist Mario Sconcerti spoke about previous Inter boss Roberto Mancini and compared him to the former Chelsea supremo.

    A fuming Mourinho said: “I know why you’re saying this. I’ve been told you’re a friend of Mancini.”

    Sconcerti hit back: “I am a friend of everyone even you.”

    But Mourinho snapped: “You’re no friend of mine.”

    He then proceeded to say: “I’m a coach, I don’t have to sit here and listen to you talk for two hours.”

    The ex-Porto manager is increasingly frustrated at the criticism levelled at him by the Italian media.

    He has already got involved in numerous spats with rival managers and club directors - most famously with Juventus boss Claudio Ranieri.

    And City are waiting in the wings to offer a jaw-dropping £15million a year deal to the Portuguese boss.
