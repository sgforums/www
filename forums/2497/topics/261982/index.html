Wolves eye Hinkel swoop

  • turbo_drift
    19 Jun 07, 09:29
    Wolfsburg are reportedly looking to bring Germany international defender Andreas Hinkel back to the Bundesliga.

    Reports in several Wolfsburg newspapers claim that new coach Felix Magath is looking to bring the 25-year-old Sevilla player to the VW Arena this summer.

    Hinkel was part of Magath's successful team during his spell in charge of VfB Stuttgart.

    "I can only say the very best things about Andreas," Magath told Wolfsburger Allgemeinen Zeitung.

    "I worked very well with him during my three-and-a-half years in Stuttgart.

    "Time will tell how great my interest is."

    Hinkel, who has won 17 caps for his country, admitted that he would be interested in a return to Germany although he has yet to be officially contacted by Wolfsburg.

    "I am ready to talk about my future. I don't want to close the door on anyone," he told Wolfsburger Nachrichten.
