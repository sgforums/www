    Real Madrid could make a sensational move for AC Milan star Kaka THIS MONTH.

    After seeing the Brazilian reject an offer from Manchester City yesterday, there are big influences inside Real that believe the time is right to go for the midfielder.

    A source told AS: "Now is the time to sign Kaka. He is the best player out there who could join this team.

    "The problem is convincing (new president) Vicente Boluda, who says there will be no new signings."

    Real sports director Miguel Angel Portugal hinted at the shock thinking within the club when admitting last night: "Real need reference players.

    "We have Ruud van Nistelrooy and Raul. But any inclusion of a big name is good for Real."
