    Valencia defender Raul Albiol has admitted he would relish the chance to play in England amid reported interest from Liverpool.

    Los Che have substantial financial problems and are expected to sell off the likes of David Villa, David Silva and Joaquin in the summer to help ease the debt.

    The club’s sporting director, Fernando Gomez, has confirmed that he is aware of interest from England and the 23-year-old Spain international, who can also play as a defensive midfielder, has now revealed he would be keen on the move.

    “If I do leave Valencia, it would only be for a big club,” he said in the Daily Mirror.

    “The Premier League would be perfect because I have always been a fan of the football they play.”

    Reports suggest Albiol would cost around £10 million.
