    I think all of you thoroughly deserve each other and I hope you continue to 'entertain' each other to no end. End of the day, I believe offline all of us still have great times watching football with our real friends no matter what football club it is they support or whether they even have a favourite club at all.

    You see a table of friends sharing a jug of beer with one wearing A Manchester jersey, another wearing Arsenal, another wearing Liverpool, another wearing Chelsea and maybe even one wearing Newcastle. Very often you rib each other about the other person's favourite club or favourite players, and even though the words are the same as those of you throwing insults at one another here, it all has very different meaning and intention.
