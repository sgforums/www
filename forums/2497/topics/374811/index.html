Managers wages' list release

  • limpper
    8 Sep 09, 22:35

    Capello on bigger wages than Man Utd boss Ferguson, Chelsea's Ancelotti

    England boss Fabio Capello is better paid than the Premiership's highest earning managers, it has been revealed.

    A list of the top ten best paid coaches in the game has been published by Sport, with former Chelsea manager Luiz Felipe Scolari rated No1 at Uzbekistan's Bunydkor on an amazing €16.6 million salary.

    The full list:

    1) Luiz Felipe Scolari (Bunydkor): €16.6m
    2) Jose Mourinho (Inter Milan): €11m
    3) Fabio Capello (England): €8.8m
    4) Sir Alex Ferguson (Manchester United): €7m
    5) Roberto Mancini (still paid by Inter Milan): €6m
    6) Carlo Ancelotti (Chelsea): €6m
    7) Manuel Pellegrini (Real Madrid): €5.5m
    8) Louis van Gaal (Bayern Munich): €5.2m
    9) Guus Hiddink (Russia): €5m
    10) Arsene Wenger (Arsenal): €4.8m
