    Man Utd see Chiellini as Vidic replacement

    Manchester United boss Sir Alex Ferguson is chasing Juventus defender Giorgio Chiellini.

    Manchester United To Replace Nemanja Vidic With Giorgio Chiellini - Report

    Manchester United are reportedly ready to allow Nemanja Vidic to leave Old Trafford for Barcelona next summer and will replace the Serbian international with Juventus’ Giorgio Chiellini.

    Giorgio Chiellini - Italy (Getty Images)

    Vidic’s future in Manchester has been the subject of much speculation this season and, according to the News of the World, Sir Alex Ferguson is now willing to allow the 28-year-old to quit the Red Devils after four years service.

    The report also claims that United are hoping to raise £25 million from the sale of Vidic to Barcelona and will spend half of that cash on Chiellini, who is viewed as an ideal replacement.

    The tabloid believes that Chiellini is open to a move to the Premier League and with Juventus currently struggling in Serie A, could easily be tempted to head for England.

    The player’s agent Davide Lippi was also believed to be present at Eastlands to watch United take on City in the first leg of the League Cup semi-final on Tuesday, increasing speculation that Roberto Mancini could also be interested in the defender.
