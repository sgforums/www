    Koller dismisses Reading rumours

    Czech Republic striker Jan Koller insists he is happy at Monaco and has heard nothing about a possible move to the Premier League with Reading.The 34-year-old was linked with a transfer to the Royals over the weekend but is adamant he will start next season in Monte Carlo.

    "I have not heard anything. There is no point in talking about it, I have a contract with Monaco and I am happy there," Koller told the Czech newspaper Sport.

    Monaco finished the recently-completed Ligue 1 season in ninth place and will go into the new campaign under the leadership of former Bordeaux boss Ricardo Gomes.

    "We have a new coach, I do not know what he will be like, but I will start summer training with maximum effort and I want to see out my contract (which expires at the end of next season)," Koller added.

    Koller once admitted it was his dream to play in England but that is no longer the case.

    He said: "It is not true anymore."
