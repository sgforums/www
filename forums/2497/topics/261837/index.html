Lyon To Cancel Wiltord Contract

  • omgukilledkenny
    18 Jun 07, 18:24
    French champions Lyon have confirmed that Sylvain Wiltord will be leaving the club this summer, and may even have his contract cancelled.



    The former Arsenal star has one year left on his deal but will be shown the door after breaking the club code.

    The player has been suspended by the club for inviting girls to the players' hotel before a game against Rennes last season.

    "Sylvain will leave. He crossed the yellow line," said Lyon chairman Jean-Michel Aulas.

    "We are not discussing a transfer with him but a departure. We could even cancel his contract."

    PSG are being linked with a move for Wiltord.
