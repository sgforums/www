    I've joined Barcelona to play

    Maxwell is delighted with his move to Barcelona.

    The former Inter Milan wing-back said: "I know that I've come to replace Sylvinho, but that does not mean to be a substitute. I think the competition with Eric Abidal will be positive for the team and of course I come with the intention of playing."

    Asked about the prospect of Inter striker Zlatan Ibrahimovic also moving to the Nou Camp, Maxwell added: "Zlatan is a player with fantastic ability and a great person. He is my friend, in fact, we've shared a room, but it should be him and other people who decide his future and I have nothing to say about it."

    Asked about Ibra's rebellious streak, Maxwell added: "Personally, I've never witnessed an act of indiscipline from him."
