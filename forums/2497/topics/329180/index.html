    Nash Signs On At Everton

    After finally completing a deal for Louis Saha, Everton have secured their second deadline day signing in the shape of veteran goalkeeper Carlo Nash.

    David Moyes had been frustrated in his attempts to add to his Toffees' squad for a long period this summer. In the past week however, he has seen the signings roll in, with Louis Saha, the latest acquisition, finalising his move from Manchester United earlier today.

    Now, Moyes has added a more austere player in the shape of Carlo Nash, journeyman goalkeeper of nine clubs, who will provide competition for Tim Howard.

    The 34-year-old Nash has most recently been plying his trade at the JJB Stadium with Wigan Athletic, where he was third choice behind Chris Kirkland and Mike Pollitt.

    It is believed that Nash has been added as a free-transfer by the Goodison Park side.
