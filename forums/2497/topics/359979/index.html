Ovrebo: There's Norway I'm going to quit

  • omgukilledkenny
    14 May 09, 17:26

    The match official that has been held responsible for Chelsea’s Champions League exit, Tom Henning Ovrebo, says that ‘everything is fine’ as he prepares to return to the refereeing fold.

    Ovrebo has been widely criticised for his performance at Stamford Bridge after turning down a succession of penalty appeals before Andres Iniesta’s late goal saw Barcelona sneak into the final.

    Both Michael Ballack and Didier Drogba reacted furiously and could well face Uefa punishments, while Ovrebo was the subject of death threats and was quickly smuggled out of England following the semi-final, second leg.

    Ovrebo will referee in the Norwegian top flight this weekend though and claims he has not been affected by the recent furore.

    "It's been normal," he told Verdens Gang.

    "I have been visiting my family and I have been to work, my other job as a psychologist.

    "I have it good, my kids are fine, everything is fine."
