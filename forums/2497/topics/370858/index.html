    LAST GAME? ... Xabi Alonso on the subs bench for Liverpool against Espanyol last night

     

    RAFA BENITEZ last night insisted he is still hopeful Xabi Alonso will stay at Liverpool.

     

    The Kop midfielder, a £28million target for Real Madrid, made what looked like a cameo farewell appearance in their friendly defeat in Spain.

    But boss Benitez said: "He is our player and we want him to stay." 

    Luis Garcia - a namesake of the former Anfield player - put Espanyol ahead before ex-Chelsea reserve Ben Sahar scored a second-half double to give the hosts an impressive win. 

    Liverpool failed to threaten despite having Steven Gerrard, Fernando Torres, Jamie Carragher and Javier Mascherano in their starting line-up.
