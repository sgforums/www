    Leonardo fed-up with AC Milan market failures

    Leonardo has expressed frustration with AC Milan's failure to sign a new striker.

    The new Milan coach is fed-up with the situation and told Corriere dello Sport: "Yes, now I can say that Luis Fabiano (Sevilla) will not be coming and also the same for (David) Trezeguet (Juventus), we cannot do anything.

    "Certainly, so far we have not strengthened. We have sold Kakà but then nothing has happened.

    "My first approach with Milan was to begin with the opportunity to have a certain type of player like Edin Dzeko (Wolfsburg), for example, and now the system needs to be done differently."
