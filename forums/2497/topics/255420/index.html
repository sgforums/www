    Lamps assures Blues fans

    Frank Lampard has assured Chelsea fans he wants to stay with the FA Cup winners for the remainder of his career.

    Although Lampard has two years left on his current deal, a new Fifa rule means the remainder of his contract can be bought out this summer.

    However, the former West Ham ace loves The Blues and has stated he hopes contract negotiations will be sorted out soon.

    "This is my club. I love this club, I want to stay here forever," he told Sky Sports 1.

    "(The fans) are my people and I hope it'll be sorted out very soon."

    Chelsea won the Cup for a fourth time thanks to Didier Drogba's winner and Lampard is thrilled they have lifted the trophy for the first time at the newly rebuilt Wembley.

    "That means everything," he continued on winning the Cup at Wembley. "It's fantastic for our fans.

    "Chelsea won the old game at Wembley and the new game at Wembley. The fans have deserved that."
