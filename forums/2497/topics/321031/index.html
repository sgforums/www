    Gunners want Plymouth starlet

    Media reports suggest that Arsenal are one of three clubs to have tabled an offer for Plymouth Argyle teen sensation Aaron Spear.

    The Gunners, who have already signed one of the hottest properties in British football in Cardiff's Aaron Ramsey this summer are now believed to be hot pursuit of Spear.

    The young whiz-kid was thought to be heading to Newcastle following meetings with their director of football Dennis Wise, but Arsenal and Portsmouth are firmly thought to be in the running for the young striker.
