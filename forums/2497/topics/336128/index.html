    Steve Gerrard is a diver. And a bloody good one too. He's even written books on the subject.

    The Cenotes of the Riviera Maya is the book Gerrard penned about some of the mysterious caves off the coast of Mexico. Check it out here...

    Here's some of the testimonials.

    "Steve Gerrard's new book, The Cenotes of the Riviera Maya is a must buy for any diver. I keep a copy in the van I use for diving just to show my diving buddies and other divers that I meet, and I have yet to meet a diver who is not instantly taken by it." – Donn Ellerbrock

    "Steve Gerrard shows off his consummate skills as a cave diver in a useful and entertaining format. This book is necessary for cave divers and will be treasured by anyone interested in discovering the most beautiful and mysterious place on earth." – Bradley A. Caudle
