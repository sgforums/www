    Update: Saturday Sales for Suzuki Cup Semi Tickets

     

    Singapore vs Vietnam

     

    Tickets to this Sunday's AFF Suzuki Cup Semi-Final 2nd Leg match between Singapore and Vietnam will be on sale on Saturday 20 December from 10am to 5pm at the North and South Gates of Jalan Besar Stadium.

     

    The Football Association of Singapore encourages Singapore fans to purchase their tickets early ahead of Match Day in view of the expected big turnout.

    Grandstand tickets have sold out while Gallery tickets (Adults and Students) are still available. They are currently on sale today (Friday) from 1pm to 8pm at both the North and South Gates at Jalan Besar Stadium.

    All available tickets at the close of sales on Saturday will go on sale on Match Day from 5pm at the East Entrance of the National Stadium.

     

    Match Day Shuttle Bus Service

    The FAS is pleased to announce that there will be a shuttle bus service for fans on Match Day from Kallang MRT Station to the National Stadium from 5pm to 8pm. The buses will run at intervals of approximately 10 minutes.

    The pick-up point will be at the Car Park opposite Kallang MRT Station next to the Bus Interchange.
