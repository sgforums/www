    Cristiano Ronaldo heads a list of six nominees for this year's Professional Footballers Association's (PFA) Player of the Year award.

    The Manchester United forward, who has scored 37 goals this season, is the favourite to retain the award after another outstanding season at Old Trafford. Ronaldo has also been young player of the year award.

    The Portugual international is joined by Liverpool pair Steven Gerrard and Fernando Torres as well as Cesc Fabregas and Emmanuel Adebayor of Arsenal. David James, the Portsmouth goalkeeper, completes the list.

    Torres and Fabregas are also in contention for the young player of the year award alongside Gabriel Agbonlahor and Ashley Young, the Aston Villa forwards, and Manchester City's Micah Richards.
