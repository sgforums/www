Roma Fans Go Berzerk At Trigoria And Throw Eggs At Players

  • omgukilledkenny
    27 Oct 08, 20:00

    Around fifty Roma fans have gathered outside the club's Trigoria training ground to cause trouble and fight with the players, according to Sky Sport Italia.

    The players have been locked in their training head quarters since last night for crisis talks and they are yet to emerge onto the pitch.

    The group of fans who have rumbled their way through and climbed on the wall have armed themselves with eggs and a number of objects as they attempt to cause havoc.

    Sky Sport 24 claim the Tifosi are hellbent on causing trouble and they have already thrown some eggs over the fence and towards the team bus as well as a number of players cars. The Ultras have also written graffiti on the walls.

    "Go to work, shame on you, embarrassing, stupid buffons and a disgrace," are just some of the words being roared from the upset fans.

    The Giallorossi have been woeful in Serie A and they are just two points off the relegation zone. It seems as if this abject form has caused the die-hard fans to spring into action as they demand answers.

    It's not the first time the fans have taken the law into their own hands as only last month the same thing happened when some of them blocked Luciano Spalletti from entering the training ground, forcing the coach to jump over the fence.

    The latest reports from Italy suggest the police have been called to calm the situation. Roma players are no where to be seen and it is believed they are staying inside the building for their own safety.

    The fans have asked for Francesco Totti to come out and explain the shambles but the club has refused.
