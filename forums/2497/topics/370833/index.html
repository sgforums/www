    Tevez has £3.7m home trial

     

     

    THIS is the stunning £3.7million mansion that new Manchester City star Carlos Tevez now calls home - but he's still only giving it a trial run. 

    The 25-year-old striker is paying more than £12,000 a month to RENT the five-bedroom house close to the posh Premiership playground Alderley Edge in Cheshire before deciding whether to take up an option to buy.

    He moved in with wife Vanesa and daughter Florencia, four, after his controversial transfer from City's bitter rivals Man United. 

    Set in half an acre, with its own pool, gym and media room, the palatial property is a far cry from the Argentine slums where Tevez was brought up. 

    "This house is a dream come true for Carlos," said a pal. "It has all he needs to keep in shape and have fun."
