    FAS hosts trials for 2008 COE programme

    The Football Association of Singapore will be hosting trials for young players for their 2008 Centres of Excellence programme this weekend.

     

    On Saturday 09 February, the trials will be for players born in 1992 and 1993.

     

    On Sunday 10 February, the trials will be for players born in 1990 and 1991.

     

    The trials for both days will run from 8.00am till 11.00am.

     

    Players must report to Mr. Nor Azli at the Jalan Besar Stadium for registration by 7.30am sharp. Players must be appropriately attired and be wearing football boots and shinpads.

     

    What: FAS Centres of Excellence Open Trials

     

    When: Saturday 09 February (for players born in 1992 and 1993

               Sunday 10 February (for players born in 1990 and 1991)

     

               Trials will run from 8.00am till 11.00am on both days

     

    Where: Jalan Besar Stadium
