    CHELSEA LEAD RACE FOR 'NEW DROGBA'

    LUKAKU (right): Potentially a world-beater

    Chelsea are also leading the chase for wonderkid Romelu Lukaku.

    The 6ft 3in Anderlecht striker has banged in six goals in 14 games for the Belgian club.

    And he has already attracted interest from AC Milan, Real Madrid and Manchester United.

    Lukaku was linked with a move to Stamford Bridge last summer but decided to remain with Anderlecht in a bid to further his development.

    And the 16-year-old striker, who has already been dubbed the 'New Didier Drogba' could make the switch to west London in the New Year should the Blues come calling.

    The young powerhouse is under contract in Brussels until 2012.
