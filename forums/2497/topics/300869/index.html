    Sanfrecce Hiroshima star Yosuke Kashiwaga is said to be eyeing a move to Europe and was reportedly scouted by Real Madrid.

    The Japanese midfielder has apparently attracted interest from European clubs, and though there are no suggestions Madrid will necessarily sign the player, a scout being sent to observe him suggests he could well be one of the better prospects to come from Asia.

    Just 20 years of age, the upcoming Beijing Olympics will be the ultimate opportunity for him to showcase his talents to a wide audience, and his dream move to Europe could well be on the cards.

    "In Beijing, I really hope we can show the world what Japanese soccer is all about," he declared.
