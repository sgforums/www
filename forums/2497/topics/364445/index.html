    CHELSEA have been alerted after Brazil star Maicon revealed he could leave Inter Milan. 

    The right-back is unsettled at the San Siro and the Blues have been linked with a swoop. He admits he would consider a switch if Inter do not prove they can win the Champions League. 

    Chelsea have reached two semi-finals and the final in the past three seasons. And Maicon would be the ideal replacement for Jose Bosingwa, who is wanted by Bayern Munich. 

    New Blues boss Carlo Ancelotti, who is also tracking Portsmouth's Glen Johnson, is understood to be a big fan of the defender. 

    Maicon said: "Inter have to think big. "I want to win the Champions League because the Champions League is an important trophy for a player's career. 

    "If I can win it with Inter, fine. Otherwise it'll be necessary to consider going elsewhere to try to win it."
