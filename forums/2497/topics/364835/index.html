    Ex-Liverpool striker Garcia eyes Premiership return

    Luis Garcia's agent claims the Atletico Madrid hit-man could return to England this summer.

    Garcia's representative insists the 30-year-old is in no hurry to find a new club, but admits he will be weighing up his options over the coming weeks.

    "The kid is calm at the moment because he still has another season to run on his contract at Atletico Madrid," Manuel Garcia Quilon told skysports.com.

    "If they decide that they no longer require his services, then some clubs have already asked about his situation - clubs in Spain and across Europe."

    On a potential return to the Premier League, where Blackburn and Fulham are said to be interested, Quilon added: "Luis really enjoyed his time at Liverpool and it is impossible to rule out the possibility he will move to England."
