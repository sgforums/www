    Man City boss Mancini has cash to land Sampdoria ace Cassano


    Roberto Mancini could seek to replace unsettled Craig Bellamy at Manchester City with Sampdoria ace Antonio Cassano.

    The Daily Mail says Cassano has undoubted ability and Mancini is a confirmed fan, having tried to sign him at Inter.

    The £22m-valued forward turned down City after leaving Real Madrid two years ago but he has fallen out with Sampdoria.

    Cassano is a secondary striker and Mancini may opt to fill other positions first - unless Bellamy decides to demand a move away.
