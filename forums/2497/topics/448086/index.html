    A Cheetahs Fan Blog


    This blog is about passion and love for our S-league side - Hougang United Football Club; and it is run by the Hougang United Supporters Club a.k.a     Hougang HOOLs.


    We are a community of working individuals who not only enjoy watching and supporting our BPL clubs but we also enjoy going to matches and cheer on the Cheetahs of HGFC. 

    Rather then depend on the "offical football body" to facilitate the success of the S-league, we feel the onus is on football fans like us to generate local football interest amongst our coutrymen. It will be a long road but we will get there.

    And we understand this nationalistic view, that a base of loyal & vocal supporters is important to Hougang United's growth as well as the S-league.

    In this blog, we bring you News, Match Reports, Match Previews, Videos and other topics surrounding Hougang United FC. 

    You can also find us on facebook @ https://www.facebook.com/HougangHools 

    CHEERS!

    By Hougang Hools

    Match Day: Saturday, 3rd March, 2012
    Venue: Hougang Stadium, 7.45pm kick-off
    Tickets: $5.00 (Adult), $ 1.00 (Student)

    Two weeks without any football action from Hougang United since the 3-0 thumping of Courts Young Lions has had the Hougang Hools in a state of withdrawal; but nonetheless Saturday is approaching and we can't wait to get ourselves onto the terraces again and into the thick of action.

    Geylang United's arrival at the Hougang Stadium or what fans affectionately call it as the "Cheetahs Den" is nothing short of a derby especially when both teams are equally pitted and have beaten each other in stunning fashion last season.

    Last March, Geylang put 2 goals past Hougang in a space of 15 minuntes after the Eagles trailed by a goal down and the game ended 2-1 in favour of the Eagles; and then in June delivered a punishing 2-0 win against the Cheetahs.

    But then Hougang United have had their moments of glory when in September the Cheetahs returned Geylang United the favour by scoring 2 goals after a goal behind. Hougang whilst playing with only 10 men and a penalty saved clawed a comeback in the dying 10 minutes to lay claim a famous 2-1 victory.

    Last week's calamity witnessed S-League's side Geylang United destroyed by a 7-1 Home United rout in front of the Geylang crowd.

    Home United got into the thick of action early in the 13th minute with former Albirex Niigata striker Shotaro Ihata opening the floodgates slotting in four past the Eagle's custodian. His colleague Frederic Mendy further compounded Geylang's misery with notching in a couple more goals.

    That first win performance puts Home United's title contentions back in track and the S-league powerhouse will now pay Hougang United a visit at the Jalan Besar Stadium. A win here would place the Protectors top of the league.

    Previous fixtures between Hougang and Home have shown that the Protectors still have an upper hand when it comes to churning out results. They thumped Hougang 5-0 last March and fashioned a 4-2 win against the Cheetahs in May.
