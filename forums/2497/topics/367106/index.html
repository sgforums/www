    LES IS MORE,.. City want Lescott

    MANCHESTER CITY target Joleon Lescott stayed at the same Dubai hotel complex as Mark Hughes and John Terry last month.

    SunSport told you yesterday how City boss Sparky and Chelsea's JT holidayed separately at the Jumeirah Beach resort just days before City's £30million bid.

    We can reveal Lescott also chose to relax at that spot.

    The Everton defender was already there when Hughes and his family arrived and they were surprised to see him lounging by the pool.

    City have been pursuing Lescott, 26, for most of the summer and are willing to pay around £15m.

    But the Toffees - who will have to pay at least 10 per cent to his old club Wolves - are holding out for £20m.

    City's chase has gone cold as they have turned their attention to England skipper Terry.

    A deal for Lescott could yet be revived if the player indicates he wants to leave but, at the moment, the two clubs are at stalemate.

    Arsenal's Kolo Toure also remains a target.
