    Mourinho To Kick Adriano Out In January - Reports

    Lightening may strike twice for Inter hit-man Adriano as he has been linked with a January exit, according to Il Corriere Dello Sport...

     

     

    Mourinho is still unhappy with the striker after his night clubbing antics on Sunday. The Brazilian was punished by his coach as he sat out the game against Fiorentina.

    Il Corriere Dello Sport claims the 'Special One' has not seen Adriano since Tuesday but they are set to have a meeting today in order to patch up their relationship.

    Mourinho warned that Adriano has plenty of work to do in order to win back his faith and, if he doesn't apologise, he could face an imminent exit.

    The former Chelsea man feels he has been betrayed by the striker as he did tell his player not to get back to his old habits of clubbing and slackness in training when he took him back from Sao Paulo in the summer.

    Mourinho thinks the striker could have a negative influence in the dressing room unless he changes attitude. The same thing happened last year when former coach Roberto Mancini shipped the Brazilian out following his lack of discipline.

    It's Adriano's last chance saloon but drinks are certainly off the menu. An apology to his coach as well as hard work on the training pitch is the only way forward if he is to avoid being on the wrong end of Mourinho's boot.
