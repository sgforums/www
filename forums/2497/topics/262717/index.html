    The Professional Footballers' Association is 100 years old this year and as part of their centenary celebrations are giving supporters the chance to vote for and select a Team of the Century.



    The PFA is running an on-line poll to find the Team of the Century, and, ultimately, the greatest player to have graced our Football Leagues over the last 100 years.

    Fans from all clubs, leagues and generations are invited to take part and have their say on who are the greatest players of all time.

    Have your say by going to the Premier League website and voting for your favourite player.

    Each day they feature a genuine legend of the game but it's up to you whether he would pick him?

    Everyone who votes will be entered into an exclusive draw to win VIP tickets to the England XI v World XI match and PFA gala dinner.
