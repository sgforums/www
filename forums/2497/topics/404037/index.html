    Barcelona claim greatest annual income for any sporting club anywhere in world - €445M

    Barcelona financial chief Xavier Sala i Martin claims the club's results from the last financial year are a record for any sporting club anywhere in the world.

    Barca's recorded annual income was set at €445 million, with the club making a profit of €11 million.

    "This €445.5 million is a record in the history of Barcelona, the highest annual income presented for any sports entity in any country, including the U.S. It is a 16 percent higher than last year, 10 percent more than we budgeted and achieved all in a context of a general crisis, " Sala i Martin beamed.

    Sala-Martin also revealed a sponsor had come forward with €22 million so Barca could continue with the UNICEF symbol on the front of their shirts for the new season.
