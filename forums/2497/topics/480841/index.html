    Barclays Premier League 2013/14

    Chelsea v Tottenham

    Date: Sunday 9 March 2014

    Time: 1:30am

    Venue: Stamford Bridge

    Live on SingTel mio TV Ch 102 and StarHub TV Ch 227

    The big clash of the weekend. Chelsea and Tottenham come into this one on the back of wins against Fulham and Cardiff last weekend respectively. Chelsea manager Jose Mourinho is still unbeaten at home in the Premier League (74 games unbeaten), so Tottenham would find this fixture tough to win. This match is Tottenham head coach Tim Sherwood's first visit to Stamford Bridge. In the reverse fixture at White Hart Lane, Tottenham led 1-0 at half time through a Gylfi Sigurdsson goal but John Terry's header in the second half gave Chelsea a point.
