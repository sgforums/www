    JOSE MOURINHO will take UEFA to the Court of Arbitration for Sport to get his five-match ban lifted.
    .

    UEFA banned the Real Madrid chief for his rant after his side lost the first leg of their Champions League semi with Barcelona in April.

    Mourinho has accused UEFA of fixing refs to favour Barcelona.

    Mourinho has also insisted that both Karim Benzema and Gonzalo Higuain will remain at the Spanish club despite transfer speculation.

    The Portuguese coach said he expects the strikers to help the team build 'a new era in European football'.
