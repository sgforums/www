    Wenger: described Eduardo as the unluckiest player ever.

     

     

    ARSENAL have insisted serial-crock Eduardo will be fit for next season. The Croatian, who spent a year out after a horror tackle by Birmingham's Martin Taylor, has made just two starts for the Gunners this season. 

    Earlier this month, boss Arsene Wenger described him as the unluckiest player ever. After his return from the broken leg in February, Eduardo picked up a groin injury. And he has now had an ankle operation which will condemn him to the sidelines again. 

    An Arsenal statement said: "Eduardo underwent simple revision surgery earlier today. "The operation went well and Eduardo will follow a dedicated rehabilitation programme throughout June. "He is expected to return to full training in pre-season with the rest of the squad."
