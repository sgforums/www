    Atletico Madrid striker Sergio Aguero insists he's not angling for a move away.

    Reports on the weekend suggested Aguero had instructed his minders to start sending out feelers for interested clubs.

    But Aguero said yesterday: "Now I am happy here and if the club still want me then I will remain.

    "Every time I play a good game there are rumours, but as I said, if one is content and happy at a club then it is impossible to leave. I have no reason to do so."
