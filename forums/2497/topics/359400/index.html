Another trashing to take for spanish giant

  • limpper
    10 May 09, 16:26

     

    Valencia 3 - 0 Real Madrid

     

    After their thrashing by Valencia, Juande Ramos insists he wants to stay as coach of Real Madrid next season.

    Ramos could offer no explanation for Real having conceded NINE goals in the last two games and said: "Winning the Liga is now impossible.

    "We played well for the first 20 minutes, at 0-0, but then Valencia were able to out-play us and deserved their victory.

    "We lacked balance and so conceded several goals. We were also missing some players."

    Asked about his future, Ramos replied: "Of course I want to stay as coach of Real Madrid next season."
