    New Real Madrid coach Jose Mourinho has urged club president Florentino Perez to forget signing Manchester United's £94 million-rated striker Wayne Rooney.

    According to Sport, 'The Special One' has declared that Madrid should concentrate on landing Inter pair Maicon and Diego Milito as the England man is unattainable.

    Previously, Mourinho had stated his desire to coach Rooney but he is not expected to act on this wish.

    The Spanish daily also stated no move will be made as Mourinho does not want to offend Sir Alex Ferguson. The fiery Scot exploded at Madrid's repeated attempts to land Cristiano Ronaldo before finally sanctioning a world record £80m move last summer.
