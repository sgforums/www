Lazio eye Silvestre

  • turbo_drift
    26 Jun 07, 18:00
    Lazio are chasing the services of Boca Juniors defender Matias Silvestre.

    Both teams are close to reaching an agreement for the transfer of the player for around $2.5million.

    "I have been told today about the negotiations that Boca are having with Lazio," Silvestre told Radio del Plata.

    "The officials asked me if I was interested in playing in Italy and I said that if the club had interest in transferring me because it's good for the club then I agree."

    In the first six months of 2007 Silvestre has not featured too much in coach Miguel Angel Russo's plans.
