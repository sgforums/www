    Drogba: I sacrifice myself for the good of Chelsea

    Chelsea striker Didier Drogba has called himself one of the most unselfish players in football.

    Drogba said: "I often sacrifice myself for the good of the team. I don't look at my scoring statistics. Have you ever seen any other great strikers do as much defending as I do?

    "We are very confident and relaxed as well. Our team is very solid and physically very strong.

    "Carlos Ancelotti is highly respected here, and the players regard him as one of us. That's why I consider the spirit within the camp to be really good."
