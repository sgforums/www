    Robbie Keane has described Tottenham's season as "remarkable".

    He said: "It has been a remarkable season. I've never known one when, at the end of March, you have people talking about relegation and Europe.

    "The priority has to be safety but once we've achieved that we'll look to get as high up the table as possible.

    "I am the same as all the players here, we just want to win. But as we found out in the Carling Cup final the finest margin can make the difference between winning and losing."
