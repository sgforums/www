    JUANDE RAMOS has dropped a huge hint that he will raid the Premier League for a winger in January. The new Real Madrid boss has been linked with Aston Villa’s Ashley Young and Tottenham’s Aaron Lennon since taking over at the Bernabeu. And he has earmarked a right-sided midfielder as his next signing following the capture of Lassana Diarra from Portsmouth earlier this week.

    Ramos said: "There are some interesting players in the English market and I know clearly who they are. "I have told the club what I think and now it is a question of money. "It is true that the market for wingers is limited and the clubs that you call raise the price because they know that we need the player.

    "It is important that we bring in a right winger to help us in the Champions League and in the league. "It seems to me that with Arjen Robben on the left and another player on the right we would be complete.

    “Although Robben can play on the right, I prefer wingers to play on their strongest side and not switch over."
