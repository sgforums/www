    FA CUP DRAW 2011

     

       

     

    Crawley and Leyton Orient will join the likes of Manchester United and Chelsea in the draw for the fifth round of the FA Cup on Sunday.

    The draw will be held at Wembley Stadium at 12.10, with comedian Mark Watson and ex-Liverpool and England defender Neil Ruddock.

    Crawley beat Torquay to become the first non-league side in the fifth round for 17 years.

    Leyton Orient of League One beat Championship side Swansea to progress.

    The fifth round will be played on the weekend of 19/20 February.
