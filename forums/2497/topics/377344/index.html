    Liverpool lost because of several reasons. Firstly the manager. Benitez thinks he very smart coming out to say we can beat anyone, and the match after he said this, he was beaten. Benitez though he won the CL, is not a great manager. Too much rotation and reliance on a handful of players.

    Secondly, over reliance on Gerrard, Torres and Kuyt. Benitez only know how to rely on the 3 of them. If any 2 of them injured then i think liverpool is gone. Gerrard is getting old as well. A football team plays football as a team, not just 3 superstars unless you are lionel messi, cristiano ronaldo or kaka, but torres, gerrard and kuyt are just not them.

    Thirdly, carragher is getting old. His defense is not as good as what he is used to be. Skrtel is just an average player. They have a gem in daniel agger, but benitez does not play him.

    Fourthly, failure to replace alonso. Alonso is the brightest player, the best player last season for pool. Way ahead of torres and gerrard. Yet, benitez sold him and betrayed him (by wanting to buy barry). Benitez then went on to buy that roma guy and until now is still injured. Poor replacement in my opinion. Alonso's loss is massive.

    Fifthly, Mascherano's head has been turned by the attention of barcelona. He wanted to leave pool for barca, but benitez would hear none of it. Mascherano's tigerish approach of his game is gone and is now frequently warming the bench.

    Sixthly, benitez insistence on playing the Lucas guy. That guy is simply 1st division material. He is the weakest link in the liverpool lineup. If benitez insist on playing him, then its really bad.

    Lastly, the fans. Sometimes liverpool fans are too big headed thinking that they are the best team in the world and thus giving the team too much pressure. Pressure on the team is good, but too much may in fact be bad for the team.
