Show me the money, pleads Moyes

  • omgukilledkenny
    9 May 09, 18:29

    Everton boss David Moyes admits he would love to have a "busy summer in the transfer market" - but fears he will not get to splash the cash.

    Moyes missed out on a number of targets last pre-season after being frustrated in having funds released at Goodison Park.

    Despite guiding the club to the FA Cup final and into Europe for the fourth time in five seasons, Moyes is wary that substantial funds may not be available to him to strengthen his squad.

    "I would like to think it will be a busy summer in the transfer market but I am worried it will not be," he said .

    "I've had a look and have got an idea of what I would like to do, but I need to wait and see what finances are made available."

    Moyes believes the failure to strengthen his squad early last summer hindered his side's progress at the start of the season.

    "Someone told me the other day if the season had started in October we'd be in the Champions League places," added Moyes.

    "Buy you don't. You start in August and you have to be ready."
