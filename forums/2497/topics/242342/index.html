    Spanish giants Barcelona and Real Madrid have both expressed their admiration for the midfielder in the past, while Serie A leaders Inter Milan would also be interested in a move.

    The Times claims Chelsea chief executive Peter Kenyon has not contacted Lampard's agent for more than three weeks, prompting the midfielder to make the threat.
