    Ashley Cole is hopeful Chelsea can add to their Carling Cup triumph.

    The Blues are still in the hunt to secure a clean sweep, having moved into the final of the FA Cup with victory over Blackburn on Sunday.

    England defender Cole believes some people are of the opinion that Chelsea have had a bad season, but he is hopeful of ending that talk by adding to the Carling Cup trophy they claimed in February.

    "Everyone says it is a bad season, but we are in the semi-final of the Champions League, the final of the FA Cup, won the Carling Cup and are not too far behind Man United in the league," Cole told the website.

    "So it has been pretty good so far and hopefully we can keep going and winning games and have something to show at the end."

    Cole's team-mate and namesake Joe believes Chelsea showed their mettle to see off Rovers and book a trip to Wembley.

    He said: "They really came at us and they are a threat from set-pieces.

    "It was a good test for us and is nice to get the win and we are going to Wembley."
