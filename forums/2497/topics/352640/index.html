    GLORY ... Petr Cech reckons Chelsea can be Euro kings

     

     

    PETR CECH insists Chelsea can go all the way to the Champions League final again. 

    The Czech keeper suffered Moscow heartache as the Blues were beaten by Manchester United in a penalty shootout last May. But Cech believes Guus Hiddink can lead them all the way to Rome. 

    He said: “After two semi-finals and one final defeat, you yearn for another go and for the chance to win the Champions League. “I believe we’ll have a chance to get to the final again this year. “We can go as far as we did last year. We’re a slightly different team but in terms of quality, this team is at least as good as last year’s.” 

    Cech, 26, says Chelsea’s notable triumph over their old boss Claudio Ranieri and Juventus in the last round demonstrated their credentials. He added: “The two matches with Juventus proved that — they are a very strong team. 

    “We can’t forget they beat Real Madrid twice in the group stages. That places even greater value on our victory. “The manager has got to know the team in a short time and therefore he has acted in a relaxed manner.”
