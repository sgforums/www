    We love Spurs... but City will win the title

    PEER REVIEW ... Prem bosses reckon Roberto Mancini will lift the title
    By GEOFF SWEET

      

     

      

     

    MANCHESTER CITY will win the title but Tottenham are the best team to watch in the Premier League.

    That is the verdict of the men with the inside knowledge — the top flight's 20 managers.

    A survey shows that three quarters of them rate Harry Redknapp's Spurs as the most entertaining team, even though they have scored less goals than United, City and Arsenal.

    However, the majority agreed that City (67 per cent) will pip Manchester United (33 per cent) to win their first championship since 1968.

    The research — carried out by Barclays, title sponsor of the League Managers' Association
