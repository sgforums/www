    Kewell Agent Rubbishes Galatasaray "Transfer"

    Bernie Mandic, agent to Harry Kewell, has vehemently denied his client has sealed a move to Turkish heavyweights, Galatasaray, despite claims on the club’s website stating the contrary.

    The Australian dribbler’s agent said that no decision regarding the national captain’s future would be made until Saturday the 5th of July. Mandic told Liverpool’s team website: “We have not finalised a deal for Harry and it’s down to two possibilities. We will not make any further comments until Harry decides on the options around noon on Saturday European time.”
