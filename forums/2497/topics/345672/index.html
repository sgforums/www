Man City ready to splash out on Liverpool striker

  • limpper
    27 Jan 09, 16:36

    The unsettled Liverpool's striker, Robbie Keane.

     

    Manchester City are ready to make an offer for unsettled Liverpool striker Robbie Keane.

    The Sun says the striker did not even make the bench for Sunday’s 1-1 FA Cup draw with Everton.

    He also failed to turn up to watch the match, throwing into doubt his Anfield future.

    City manager Mark Hughes signed £14million striker Craig Bellamy from West Ham this month but is a huge admirer of Keane.

    Kop boss Rafa Benitez insists the Irishman still has a future at Liverpool.

    But Keane, 28 — a £20million summer buy from Spurs — has been substituted in all but one of the 16 Premier League games he has started for the Reds.
