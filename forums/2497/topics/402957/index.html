    Barcelona star Messi rates Man Utd ace Rooney ahead of Ronaldo

    Barcelona star Lionel Messi rates Manchester United ace Wayne Rooney as a better player than Real Madrid's Cristiano Ronaldo.

    Rooney had to play second fiddle to Ronaldo while they were together at United.

    Messi told the Daily Star Sunday: “At the moment, there are ­maybe ­Ronaldo, Rooney, Xavi and one or two more – but I can’t think of one that plays with the desire of Rooney.

    “We all know football ­players at the top level are blessed with high wages – it’s no secret.

    “But Rooney would play for 100 euros-a-week. You can see the fire in his eyes. It’s that fire that makes him the best of the best.

    “If Rooney is ever sold, I think he will become the first 100million euro player. With all r­espect to Ronaldo, I think Rooney is a more important ­player for United than he was.

    “Ronaldo’s ability is undoubted but Rooney is not only the ­talent but also the heart of the ­Manchester United team.”
