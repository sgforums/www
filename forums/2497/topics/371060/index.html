    Van der Vaart blasts Real Madrid coach Pellegrini

    Rafael van der Vaart has launched an attack on Real Madrid coach Manuel Pellegrini.

    The Dutchman is fed-up with the way he's been treated in preseason and told Algemeen Dagblad: "Now I have nothing more to say to Pellegrini. His attitude is clear. What can I expect from him when all preseason and in full preparation I am glued to the bench?

    "When a coach has that attitude, it is clear that you are obliged to take note. My chances at Real Madrid are close to zero, if not nil. We must be realistic.

    "The worst thing is that Real Madrid have not been clear if they want me.

    "It's incredible."
