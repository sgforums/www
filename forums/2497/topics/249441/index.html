Lamps: Barca is a Gud life

  • turbo_drift
    19 Apr 07, 19:19
    Frank Lampard admits that former Chelsea team-mate Eidur Gudjohnsen cannot stop telling him about the virtues of playing for Barcelona.

    The England star has long been linked with a move to Catalunya and he has admitted in the past that a move to Camp Nou does appeal.

    Lampard has now revealed that Gudjohnsen has been waxing lyrical to him about the Azulgrana.

    "He speaks wonders of Barcelona," Lampard is quoted by Spanish daily newspaper.

    "I have spoken with him and he is enchanted with the life and lifestyle in Barcelona.

    "He has explained to me that every movement is analysed by the fans and press - I believe it would be a great experience for a footballer to play in a club like that."

    Lampard also admitted that the training approach at Chelsea and Barcelona appeared to be different.

    "There is also a lot of difference between the training," he said.

    "At Chelsea we look at the physical aspect a lot, but at Barcelona it is more technique based and he [Gudjohnsen] enjoys that because he is a quality player."
