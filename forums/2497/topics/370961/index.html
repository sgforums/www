    Rensing is Bayern's number one

    Louis van Gaal has decided Michael Rensing will go into the new German Bundesliga season as Bayern Munich's first-choice goalkeeper.

    The 25-year-old struggled in his first campaign as Bayern's number one last term and lost his place to Hans-Jorg Butt towards the end of the season.

    Bayern were linked with a move for Schalke's Manuel Neuer earlier this summer, but coach van Gaal is prepared to put his trust in Rensing.

    He played in Bayern's 3-1 DFB-Pokal win over sixth-division side SpVgg Neckarelz on Sunday.

    "I have watched for four-and-a-half weeks and now have made my decision," Van Gaal said on Bayern's official website.

    "I am putting my faith in Michael Rensing."

    Rensing added: "We have both had a good pre-season, so I am luckier that I have been chosen. I have believed in myself and my abilities."
