    Spurs boss Redknapp mocks England selection for Arsenal kid Wilshere

    Tottenham boss Harry Redknapp has mocked the England selection of Arsenal teen Jack Wilshere.

    England boss Fabio Capello included Wilshere, yet to make a Barclays Premier League start for the Gunners, and Kieran Gibbs, understudy to Gael Clichy at left-back.

    "It's a strange old squad," said Tottenham manager Redknapp. "Suddenly players not good enough for the World Cup are in the England squad.

    "It's unusual that players not in their club's team are in the England squad.

    "Wilshere is a good prospect but I thought England were supposed to be better than than any club side. Maybe Arsenal are better than England."
