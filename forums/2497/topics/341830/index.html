    ARSENE WENGER insists it will not be the end of the world if Arsenal fail to qualify for the Champions League.

    After 11 consecutive years in Europe’s top competition, the struggling Gunners are in real danger of missing out on a top-four finish.

    And Wenger is already preparing worried Arsenal fans for the worst as they travel to third-placed Aston Villa this evening.

    He said: “In sport you have to accept that nothing is guaranteed.

    "It is only because we are in the Champions League so often that people expect it to happen every year.

    “AC Milan did not qualify this year but did not die!”

    Failure to qualify for the Champions League would cost Arsenal at least £20million.
