    NUTS AWARDS: Which player has the dodgiest haircut in the Premier League and whose fans have the best chant?

    Cristiano Ronaldo and Robbie Keane continued their outstanding seasons after scoring a hat-trick in the nominations for this year's WKD Nuts Football Awards.

     

    The star players, topping the shortlist with three nominations each, go head-to-head for the coveted title of Hero of the Season and the Special Award for Man Love, which honours players over enthusiastic goalscoring public displays of affection.

    Ronaldo also picks up a nomination for Goal of the Season, while it's Keane's goal celebration that earns him a third nomination in the Most WKD Goal celebration category.

    Nuts sports editor Gershon Portnoi said: "Manchester United and Arsenal may have picked up the most player nominations but it's good to see that the top four clubs will not dominate everything this season."

    Voting opens at 8am on Tuesday, April 15 at www.nuts.co.uk/football.
