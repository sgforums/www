English giants tracking Sissoko

    Toulouse starlet Moussa Sissoko says he is not ready to make the switch to the Premier League just yet.

    Arsenal, Manchester United and Liverpool are reportedly tracking the 18-year-old's progress at the Stadium Municipal.

    But Sissoko, who came on as a second-half substitute in last week's UEFA Champions League qualifying first-leg defeat against Liverpool, says he is happy at Toulouse and wants to continue his apprenticeship with the French outfit.

    "I am still young," Sissoko told the club's official website.

    "I have been followed by English sides, but I prefer to stay at Toulouse. It's better for me to stay and play regularly.

    "Of course the English league attracts me a lot, but I have to play games before I consider any move."
