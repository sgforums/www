    New Chelsea starlet ready to shine

    New Chelsea signing Philipp Prosenik says he is relishing the chance to prove himself at the highest level.

    The 16-year-old, who currently plays for Rapid Vienna, has agreed to move to Stamford Bridge in the summer despite interest from Bayern Munich, Milan and Juventus.

    And the Austria Under-17 international is desperate to make an impact with the first-team sooner rather than later.

    “Chelsea is just perfect,” he told APA.

    “England is quite simply the best league. The best players are there and they play a really fast style.

    “I want to join up with the pros as soon as possible.

    “I have thought about going abroad for a long time, and it can only benefit me, not only as a player but also as a person.

    “I can – and will – be much better.”
