  • omgukilledkenny

    Filippo Inzaghi has stated he will end his career with AC Milan after turning down an offer from MLS outfit New York Red Bulls.

    The Rossoneri started their pre-season training on Monday and Inzaghi revealed he turned down an off from the Red Bulls.

    "I received a very interesting offer from New York. It's an amazing city where there are many Italians.

    "Furthermore, I would have had the possibility of challenging David Beckham and his LA Galaxy team, and they would have covered me in gold.

    "However, I had no doubt whatsoever. I will end my career in the Milan shirt, which has become a second skin for me," said the 33-year-old striker.
