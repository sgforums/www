    NON-LEAGUE Barrow have been handed a trip to Premier League Middlesbrough in the FA Cup third round.

    After knocking out League Two side Brentford on Friday, Barrow have been paired with Gareth Southgate's side.

    Kidderminster have been handed a trip to Coventry, Histon host Swansea, Torquay are at home to Blackpool and Forest Green will entertain Derby.

    Manchester United and Liverpool have been handed tricky trips to Southampton and Preston respectively.

    Chelsea will host Southend, Arsenal have a home tie against Plymouth and holders Portsmouth face Bristol City at Fratton Park.

    There are three all Premier League ties - Hull v Newcastle, Tottenham v Wigan and Sunderland v Bolton.
