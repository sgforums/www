    Manchester United were forced to scale down comments made by Wayne Rooney about 'hating' Liverpool ahead of tomorrow's big match.

    The Times says the comments were posted on United’s official website yesterday lunchtime as part of an article entitled “Rooney out to finish off Liverpool”, but they were deleted within two hours of it being pointed out to club staff. The England forward’s original quote was replaced with: “I’m also very excited about the game because I grew up as an Everton fan.”

    Rooney made the remark to MUTV, United’s in-house television station, after the 2-0 victory over Inter Milan in the second leg of their Champions League first knockout round tie at Old Trafford on Wednesday evening.
