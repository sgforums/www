    Bayern Munich Winger Arjen Robben: The Situation At Real Madrid Was Ridiculous

    Dutchman was told to leave because the club needed the cash...

    Arjen Robben

     

    This weekend, Dutch international Arjen Robben will return to one of his old stomping grounds with Bayern Munich to take on Inter Milan in the UEFA Champions League final.

    As the night looms ever closer, the former Real Madrid man took time to reminisce on his short time in the Spanish capital, describing his last days at Santiago Bernabeu as ‘ridiculous’.

    "It was a ridiculous situation at that time,” Robben told Goal.com of his final days as a Real player.

    “They said only: ‘We want to sell you because we need money.’ The football aspect was not talked about there, only the financial aspect was relevant to those responsible.

    On the one hand, they saw that only Wesley Sneijder and I could bring money into the club, on the other they have spent huge amounts.”

    Finally, the former Chelsea man spoke of his great friendship with former team-mate Sneijder, who will face off against eacho ther this weekend as both sides gun for a historic treble.

    "Wesley and I are very good friends,” he said. “We are in regular contact, even this week. I'm happy for him that with him in Milan but hope he will not win the treble that we want."
