No Chance Of Berger Loan - O'Neill

  • omgukilledkenny

    Aston Villa boss Martin O'Neill has ruled out the possibility of veteran Patrick Berger leaving the club on loan.

    The former Czech Republic international signed a new one-year deal with Villa last month yet was linked with a loan move to League One side Oldham - much to the surprise of his manager who insists the ex-Liverpool and Portsmouth star is staying.

    "It's the first I've heard of it. I just don't know where that story came from," said the Aston Villa manager.

    He added: "Patrik Berger has agreed new one-year contract and I'm delighted about that.

    "There's no chance of him being sent out on loan - absolutely no chance of that.

    "He did enough over the last few months of the season to earn another deal and he's very much in our plans for next season."
