    Sir Alex Ferguson insists Liverpool are in a false position ahead of the Barclays Premiership showdown with Manchester United at Old Trafford.

    Most pundits believe United will end Liverpool's lingering title hopes if they collect a win which would open up a massive 11-point gap on their fierce local rivals.
