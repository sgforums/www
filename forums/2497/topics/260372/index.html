    Iaquinta close to Juventus switch

    Iaquinta is on the verge of a move to newly-promoted Serie A rivals Juventus, Udinese owner Giampaolo Pozzo has said.

    Udinese and Italy striker Vincenzo Iaquinta is on the verge of a move to newly-promoted Serie A rivals Juventus, Udinese owner Giampaolo Pozzo said on Monday.

    "Iaquinta should move to Juventus. There is an agreement between the two clubs and I think he's already signed a contract," Pozzo said.

    "There's nothing official yet, but all there is to do is sort out personal terms between the club and the player."

    Iaquinta, 27, has been at Udinese since 2000 and was a member of Italy's World Cup-winning squad last year.
