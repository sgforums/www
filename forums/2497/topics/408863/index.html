    Man City offering Lescott around Premier League

    Manchester City are offering Joleon Lescott around the Premier League on-loan.

    The Daily Star Sunday says the England defender is fighting to save his City career following the departure of the manager who fought so hard to sign him from Everton – Mark Hughes.

    Sparky’s successor, Roberto Mancini, clearly sees Lescott, 28, as a squad player at best and the central defender, who can also operate at left back, is not guaranteed to be in City’s 25-man Premier League squad.

    That has been underlined by the fact that clubs have been offered Lescott on loan by agents.

    Fulham, now managed by former City boss Hughes, are likely to be interested in the proposition.
