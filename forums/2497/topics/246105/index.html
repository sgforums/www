Villa aims to down Chelsea

  • turbo_drift
    2 Apr 07, 20:28
    Valencia striker David Villa is determined to fire his side past Chelsea and into the last four of the UEFA Champions League.

    The Spanish international, who has been linked with a move to Stamford Bridge, is again one of the leading scorers in La Liga this season and he found the back of the net in this weekend's win over Espanyol.

    Villa now has his sights firmly set on Chelsea.

    "I'm scoring goals and the team is winning. Now that has to be repeated in London," he told the newspaper.

    "This is a vital moment for us and we have to be ready and at full strength to face Chelsea.

    "They have many quality players and an experienced coach who is determined to win every game.

    "If a chance falls to me, I have to be fully focused to make sure I put it away - that is what is expected of me.

    "We know it won't be easy and one of the strongest teams in the competition stands in our way. But we believe we can triumph and make it to the semi-finals.

    "Chelsea are a strong side and solid in defence but I will do my best to lead the attack so that they don't have a moment's rest. It just makes me more determined."

    "I'm full of confidence now I'm back scoring goals. We've got a bit of time to rest and prepare for Chelsea to make sure we go out there at full strength.

    "They have a lot of internationals and it's been a busy period for their important players. But physically, we'll be at the same level as them now that we have players back from injuries.

    "This is a huge month for us. We'll play two vital games against Chelsea and then face our rivals for the league title by playing Sevilla and Real Madrid.

    "What happens in the Champions League will affect everything and we have to be strong against them.

    "By coming out on top in the Champions League we will give our morale a big boost to finish the season."
