    Eto'o ready to leave Nou Camp

     

    Samuel Eto'o said he is ready to leave Barcelona 'to whoever pays most' if the Catalan giants failed to win any piece silverware next year.

    Barca have won nothing since the 2006 UEFA Champions League, and this season was nothing but mediocre to their standards.

    "If we continue not to win any trophies next year, then I will go somewhere else," Eto'o told RAC1 radio station.

    "I want to win titles and if I can't do it here then it will be in another club.

    "I've done it all in Spain, my cycle has finished. I have touched the ceiling in Barcelona, so if I leave I will go abroad. I will go to whoever pays the most."
