    Cesc Fabregas will quit Arsenal to join Barcelona next summer, according to the Spanish press.

    Cesc Fabregas of Arsenal (Getty Images)

     
     
     
     

    The countdown to the arrival of Arsenal ace Cesc Fabregas has started in Barcelona, with Sport.es convinced he will be at Camp Nou in ten months.

    Despite Javier Mascherano's arrival from Liverpool, coach Pep Guardiola has left a space in his team for Fabregas, with the Blaugrana chiefs under order to sign the Gunners ace next summer.

    Barcelona were hopeful of landing the playmaker this summer, but Arsenal and boss Arsene Wenger were able to resist offers and turn Fabregas' head back towards the Emirates.

    However, speculative voices in Spain suggest the relationship between manager and player has become frosty, and Fabregas will be convinced to pack his bags if Wenger's promises of trophies fail.
