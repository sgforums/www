    Arsenal Analysis: How Arsene Wenger Rebuilt His Team - And Made £25m For The Club Too

    Bargain hunter has made a £4 million-a-season profit since last title

    Since the 'Invincibles' swept all before them in 2003-04, Arsene Wenger has generally wheeled and dealed in the cheaper end of the transfer market.

    His consistency in getting Arsenal value for money has been astonishing.

    From the 2004-05 season to date, the club have a remarkable net spend of minus £24.7 million, providing a profit of just over £4m a year.

    This is the lowest spend of players purchased minus players sold of all 20 clubs in this season's Premier League.
