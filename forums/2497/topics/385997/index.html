    Arsenal Target Loan Move For Milan Striker Klaas-Jan Huntelaar

    The competition is hotting up to sign the out-of-form Dutchman.


    Arsenal are believed to be considering a loan move for Milan striker Klaas-Jan Huntelaar, according toÂ the Daily Mail.

    The Gunners have been stifled with several forwards spending time in the treatment room. And with the likes of Robin van Persie unavailable for an extended period of time, manager Arsene Wenger is thought to be considering a short-term loan stint for the former Ajax star.

    The Dutchman has had a largely ineffective stint at the San Siro, and has in the past indicated that he may be willing to ply his trade at greener pastures to boost his World Cup chances.

    Netherlands boss Bert van Marwijk has also explained in the past that the 26-year-old forward needs to play regularly if he is to earn a place in the squad that will travel to South Africa next year.

    Huntelaar has also been linked with a move to Manchester United.
