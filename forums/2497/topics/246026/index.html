    Fulham's loan star Vincenzo Montella has admitted he is missing Italy.

    Montella is on loan at Fulham from Roma until the end of the season after arriving during the January transfer window.

    The Italian international says he is enjoying his English adventure, but concedes he misses Italy.

    "I feel really good in England, but I'm missing Italy," Montella told one of it's TV.

    "I live in the centre of London, which is a beautiful city, and there are many Italians who help me to feel a little more at home.

    "I can say this is a good experience.

    "The thing I miss the most about Italy is the great passion for the sport that the fans have,"

    "In England it's like they're going to the theatre.

    "Obviously I mean the positive and legal support that transforms into sheer love for the squad. In England people go to the stadium as if they were going to the theatre."
