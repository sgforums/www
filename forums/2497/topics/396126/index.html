    Makelele agrees one-year deal

    French veteran to give career one more shot

    FORMER Chelsea star Claude Makelele has signed a new one-year contract with Paris St Germain.

    It was rumoured the midfielder was ready to hang up his boots after his deal expired at the end of last season.

    But Makelele, 37, was persuaded to prolong his career by PSG president Robin Leproux.

    The ex-France international said: "I'm relieved because I believe in this club.

    "There will be some important recruitment this summer and the target is interesting — to make this an important club again in France.

    "I share the ambitions of the coach and the club."

    Makelele joined PSG in 2008 after a successful five-year stint at Stamford Bridge. He played 31 games for the French outfit last term.
