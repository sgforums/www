    Cristiano Ronaldo may be about to earn a salary with Real Madrid that most people could not comprehend but even he needs to sell off clutter before moving to a new life abroad. However, rather than, say, finding a new home for old (tight-fitting) clothes and bottles of tanning oil, for him that means putting his fleet of supercars up for sale, beginning with his Porsche 911 convertible.

    Available on the Auto Trader website, the player's black Carrera 2 Tiptronic S has a 3.8-litre engine, 16,000 miles on the clock and zips from 0-62mph in 5.4 seconds (presumably without inexplicably toppling over). At £69,995, it's yours for about half-a-week's worth of his wages at the Bernabeu, and you get his autographed old log book for free.
