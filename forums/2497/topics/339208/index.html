    CHELSEA PLAYERS WANT MALOUDA OUT - REPORT


    Senior Chelsea players are reported to want Florent Malouda out of the team and Wayne Bridge played on the left side instead.

     

    According to The Independent, 'the consensus is that Malouda, who joined from Lyons for £16.5m last year, and Didier Drogba, injured then suspended, have failed to pull their weight of late.'

     

    Bridge was again left on the bench on Sunday against Arsenal as first Malouda and then teenager Miroslav Stoch came on and made little impact on the game.

     

    Malouda has been a massive flop since joining for his inflated fee and the players are said to be in favour of playing Bridge in the midfield instead.

     

    But the England left-back could well be sold in January to raise funds for a new striker, with Manchester City said to be interested.
