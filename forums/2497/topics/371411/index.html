    Chelsea boss Ancelotti not scared of Hiddink ghost

    Chelsea boss Carlo Ancelotti insists he isn't worried about living up to Guus Hiddink's impact last season.

    He insisted: "I don't think about the ghost of Hiddink. Guus is a fantastic coach and he has worked very well wherever he has been.

    "I want to work my maximum for Chelsea and I certainly don't have any ghosts.

    "The players knew I was going to be the new trainer of Chelsea and I'm confident I am not a disappointment for them.

    "I haven't had the opportunity to speak to Guus but I would very much like to.

    "I'd like to meet him very much but it has not been possible. We have not found the right moment. He is a good person and I hope one day soon he can find time to come to London."
