Rafael Benitez Coy On Liverpool's Summer Transfer Plans

  • ASKL!
    30 Mar 10, 01:13

    Liverpool manager Rafael Benitez has insisted that there are plans in place at Anfield for the summer's dealings in the transfer market.

    However, the Spaniard is playing his cards typically close to his chest when it comes to the question of specific targets.

    "We are working behind the scenes, trying to improve things for the future," the Spaniard told reporters at a press conference following Sunday's victory over Sunderland.

    "But now we just need to concentrate on football if we can."

    Sunday's win gave the Reds a crucial boost in their quest to seal a fourth-placed finish in this season's Premier League.

    Benitez feels confident that his side can help him meet his guarantee to the fans of Champions League football next season - which means finishing in the top four this term.

    "We know we have to keep going. It will be difficult but we have to try," he added.

    "The players have confidence and if we play at this level we can win more games, that's for sure."
