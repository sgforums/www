    Down the toilet: BBC1 were showing highlights of the League Two clash between Torquay and Chesterfield when it cut to pictures of Kate Bosworth using the toilet in surf movie Blue Crush


    A new BBC show got off to a shaky start on Sunday morning after highlights of a League Two match suddenly switched to footage of a woman undressing for the toilet.

    BBC1's new late-night Football League Show was showing highlights of Saturday's Torquay v Chesterfield game before suddenly cutting to show images of a young blond woman pulling down her underwear.

    The film, teen surf movie Blue Crush, showed actress Kate Bosworth in the cubicle and a group of other girls coming in to use the bathroom.

    Luckily for fans who had tuned in to see highlights of the opening day encounter from Plainmoor stadium, the glitch did not mean any goals were missed and programmers quickly cut back to highlights of the following game featuring Port Vale and Rochdale.

    However the interruption, which happened at 12.50am on Sunday morning, did cut out the post match interviews and a series of half chances.

    A spokesman for the BBC apologised to viewers and said 'This was a technical hitch.'
