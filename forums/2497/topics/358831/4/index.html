    Silvestre hits back at 'disrespectful' Evra

    Mikael Silvestre believes Patrice Evra lacked respect towards some Arsenal stars when he called The Gunners ‘children’.

    Manchester United left back Evra labelled the Champions League semi-final, second leg with Arsenal a game between ‘adults and children’ as United progressed to the final.

    But former United man Silvestre is not happy with his compatriot's declarations and has backed his team-mates to bounce back and prove themselves next term.

    “I think it is a lack of respect to players like Kolo Toure, Emmanuel Adebayor or Robin van Persie. They are not babies, they are over 25,” Silvestre told Le Parisien.

    “For sure, conceding a goal early in the game was a blow, but we have to remember that we were without William Gallas, Gael Clichy and Andrei Arshavin.

    “It would have been a different story if they had been there. It is up to us to show next season that he [Evra] is wrong and that Arsenal can win silverware playing our football.”
