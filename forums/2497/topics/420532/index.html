    Liverpool Weigh Up £10 Million Move For Aston Villa Winger Stewart Downing - Report

     
    EPL : Stewart Downing

     

     

     

    Liverpool are attempting to persuade Aston Villa manager Gerard Houllier to part ways with England winger Stewart Downing, according to The Express.

    New manager and Liverpool legend Kenny Dalglish is looking to expand his squad’s depth and apparently believes that Downing would give the Reds' midfield some of the much-needed pace and creativity it desperately lacks when skipper Steven Gerrard is unavailable.

    Goal.com UK previously revealed Liverpool’s interest in signing Downing’s team-mate Ashley Young, but it now looks like the Reds may have to settle for the ex-Middlesbrough winger as Houllier has claimed it would take "crazy money" to lure Young away from Villa Park.

    It is reported it would cost Liverpool around £10 million to secure Downing's services.

    Liverpool Approach Aston Villa Duo Stewart Downing And Ashley Young - Report

     
    EPL,Stewart Downing, Ashley Young
    Liverpool are preparing a double swoop for Aston Villa wingers Stewart Downing and Ashley Young, according to the Metro.

    The pair have both been targeted by Reds boss Kenny Dalglish as signings that could reinvigorate the side's ailing midfield, with a lack of creativity being noted as one of the major downfalls of the current crop at Anfield.

    It is thought that ex-Liverpool chief Roy Hodgson made an enquiry in the summer regarding the England internationals but his efforts were rebuked, with then Villa manager Martin O'Neill unwilling to part ways with his star wide men.

    However, with both Hodgson and O'Neill no longer at the clubs, it is rumoured that a double deal could be put in place, with current Villa boss Gerard Houllier looking to completely re-invent his squad, something that he would only be able to pursue if funds were made available by selling some of the club's most lucrative assets.

    The move would signal a much-called for change of position for Reds star Joe Cole, who after being largely utilised on the wings under Roy Hodgson, would be enabled to move into a preferred central role, providing support to Spanish superstar Fernando Torres.

    A stumbling block may come in the form of Aston Villa's high valuation of Ashley Young, with a fee of over £14 million being touted, which may be above and beyond Liverpool's price bracket.

    Liverpool Look To Solve Left-Back Problem With Aston Villa’s Stephen Warnock - Report

     
    Stephen Warnock,Liverpool

     

     

     

    Liverpool manager Kenny Dalglish is set to offer Stephen Warnock a return to Anfield to help plug the club's leaky defence, according to The Mirror.

    Dalglish is keen to bring in the former Red on loan with a view to a permanent move as he looks to solve the left-back problem that has plagued Liverpool all season.

    England right-back Glen Johnson has been played out of position to accommodate the emergence of Martin Kelly.

    However, the move hinges on Villa boss Gerard Houllier agreeing to do business with his former employers.

    Liverpool hope to bring in the former Melwood trainee who has not been a first team regular under Houllier.

    The Reds have also been linked with Warnock’s Villa and England team-mates Stewart Downing and Ashley Young.

    Any of the deals could be done as Houllier looks to do some business of his own with speculation surrounding an £18 million move for Sunderland striker Darren Bent.
