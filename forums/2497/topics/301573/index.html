Wenger swoops for teenage striker

  • omgukilledkenny
    6 Jan 08, 15:33


    Arsenal will prepare for a new generation of young talent by completing the signature of 15-year-old Luke Freeman from Gillingham.

    The left-sided striker became the youngest player to appear in the FA Cup proper when, at 15 years and 233 days, he came on for the last 20 minutes of Gillingham's 2-1 first-round defeat to Barnet.

    Dartford-born Freeman, released by Charlton at 11, also interested Newcastle and West Ham but will join Arsene Wenger's team in March when he turns 16.
