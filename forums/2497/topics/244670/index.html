Laporta: Ronnie not for sale

  • turbo_drift
    25 Mar 07, 22:49
    Joan Laporta has reiterated that Barcelona have no intention of selling Ronaldinho.

    The Brazilian has endured a difficult season at Camp Nou and has been linked with a move to Milan in the summer.

    Barcelona vice-president Ferran Soriano has tried to quell speculation about Ronaldinho's future over the last couple of weeks and president Laporta has now played down talk of a move as well.

    "The club has no interest in selling Ronaldinho," Laporta told the Spanish press.

    "We want the player to stay at Barcelona."

    Ronaldinho is contracted to Barca until 2010 but talks have not yet started over a possible extension.
