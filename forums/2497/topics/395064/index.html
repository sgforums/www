    Naughty boy! Liverpool's £20m flop Alberto Aquilani gets ticking off for not learning English

    Expensive: Alberto Aquilani cost Rafa Benitez £20m from Roma last year

    Alberto Aquilani has revealed he is still struggling to learn English and admits he must improve his language skills in order to make the most of his Liverpool career.

    The 25-year-old Italy midfielder arrived from Roma for £20million last summer, but has since struggled to break into the Anfield first team through injuries and poor form.

    And Aquilani has now admitted he is struggling both with learning English and the weather. He does insist, however, that he is happy at the Reds and wants to succeed for the club's supporters.

    'I don't speak English very well,' he admitted. 'Now I understand more than I did before but I have to improve.

    'I am not having regular lessons at the moment. At first I did but it has been difficult more recently and my English teacher is angry about this.

    'It has been difficult because at one time my family were here so I couldn't do the lesson, another time we had too many games and sometimes it just feels too much.

    'But I like everything in England, I just don't like the weather. I like the people, the supporters, the city and playing here. And I want to play well here.'
