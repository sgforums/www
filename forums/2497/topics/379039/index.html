Mourinho mocks Arsene 'n Rafa

    JIBE ... Jose Mourinho has mocked Arsene Wenger and Rafa Benitez's lack of silverware


     

    JOSE MOURINHO has reopened his war of words with Arsene Wenger and Rafa Benitez by mocking the duo's lack of silverware.

    The ex-Chelsea boss was famed for the mind games with the Arsenal and Liverpool supremos during his time in England.

    Mourinho's three-year stay in west London saw him lead the Blues to two Premier League titles, the FA Cup and two League Cups before he left the club in 2007.

    Since joining Inter Milan last year, The Special One has already won Serie A and the Italian Super Cup.

    In contrast, Wenger's Arsenal have not won a trophy since their 2005 FA Cup triumph.

    Although the Reds have won the FA Cup and Champions League, Benitez has failed to guide them to the Premier League crown.

    And Mourinho has had a sly dig at his old adversaries by claiming the pair would not have survived in Italy.

    He said: "Wenger has been Arsenal's manager for 15 years but he hasn't even won a Carling Cup for six years.

    "Benitez hasn't won a League title in six years but he continues to be Liverpool's manager.

    "This is not the Italian mentality. To stay here I must keep winning and doing well."
