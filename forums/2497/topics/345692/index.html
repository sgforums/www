Young sets sights on title

    Luke Young sees no reason why Aston Villa cannot spring an upset and take the Premier League title.

     

    Martin O'Neill's side have upset the form book this season and currently sit just three points adrift of leaders Manchester United.

    Having maintained a consistent level of performance thus far expectations around Villa Park have sky-rocketed.

    A top four finish no longer represents the pinnacle of their aspirations, with Young adamant that the top-flight crown is there for the taking.

    "Nobody is talking about that but why not?" said the England international.

    "We're currently three points off the leaders. If we're still there in March, then why not?

    "I think a lot of people have been thinking we will crack and slip up, but at the moment we haven't done that.
