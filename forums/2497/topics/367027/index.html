    Hangeland rejects Fulham contract offer

    Brede Hangeland's future at Fulham is in doubt.

    The Times says Hangeland has turned down Fulham’s offer of a new contract worth twice his present salary of £15,000 a week.

    The Norway defender, 27, who has two years left on his contract, has been the subject of interest from several clubs this summer, although none has yet made an offer.
