    Arsenal's Bendtner offers to pay for fans' shirts after number change

    Arsenal striker Nicklas Bendtner has offered to fund his fans' change of number for their jerseys after he switched from No26 to No52.

    He told Arsenal's website: "Before it starts I wanted to change my squad number from 26 which I’ve obviously had for a number of seasons now. I chose to move to 52 because it’s a special number to me personally, and I hope that it brings me good luck for the new season.

    "I appreciate that a good number of fans have bought their kits for 2009/10 already with names and numbers printed up so I’d like to personally cover the cost of replacing anyone’s shirt that has my previous number. It means a lot to see supporters wearing your name and number, and I want to ensure people aren’t inconvenienced by the change."
