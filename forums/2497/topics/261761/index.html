Prem pair tracking Kanoute

  • turbo_drift

    Fredi Kanoute's agent claims Newcastle United and Portsmouth are tracking the Sevilla striker.

    Kanoute, who has previously played for Spurs and West Ham, has enjoyed great success in La Liga.

    The France-born Mali striker has 21 goals to his name this term and has been pivotal to the club's success.

    However, a move could well be on the cards with his agent Christophe Mongai confirming interest from two Premiership clubs.

    "It's true Newcastle are interested, but so are Portsmouth," Mongai said in the News of the World.

    "There's no concrete offer yet, but Fredi is interested."
