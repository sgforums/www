Ronaldinho rules out Bridge move

  • omgukilledkenny

    Barcelona star Ronaldinho has ruled out a move to Chelsea, saying he cannot imagine leaving the Nou Camp at the moment.

    Barca fans will be glad to hear that their hero is keen to stay with the Catalans, as his presence in the side will boost the club's chances of regaining their La Liga title.

    The Blues are one of only a few clubs who could afford a deal for Ronaldinho but the player says he has no intention of swapping Barcelona for Stamford Bridge or any other venue, despite the interest from overseas.

    "I am very happy playing in Spain and I can't imagine being elsewhere," Ronaldinho revealed. "I know there are other good leagues, but at the present time I do not imagine life outside Barca."
