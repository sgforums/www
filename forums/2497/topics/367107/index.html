    ALEX FERGUSON has bagged Bordeaux midfielder Gabriel Obertan. 

    The 20-year-old, regarded as one of France's hottest prospects, passed a medical at Manchester United yesterday. And he will tie up an £8million transfer when he pens a four-year contract today. 

    Obertan, who can play in a central midfield role or up front, had hoped to return home to Bordeaux after his physical check-up. But United chiefs told him he must start training with his new team-mates this morning. 

    Chelsea, Arsenal and Inter Milan had been watching the youngster.
