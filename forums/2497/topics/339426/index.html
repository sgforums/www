    AFF Suzuki Cup

    Raddy announces finall squad of 22 for defence of AFF Suzuki Cup

     

    National Coach Radojko Avramovic has shortlisted the final squad of 22 for Singapore’s defence of the AFF Suzuki Cup in December.

     

    Coach Radojko has picked an experienced squad with over half the team seeing action in earlier editions of the Suzuki Cup.

    2008 NTUC Income Player of the Year Aleksandar Duric will be making his debut together with his Singapore Armed Forces FC team mate John Wilkinson. 

    Notable absentees include Fazrul Nawaz and Khairul Amri who both got injured in the course of the 2008 NTUC Income – YEO’S S.League season.

    Shaiful Esah, Rosman Sulaiman and Hyrulnizam Juma'at are the only three uncapped players brought into the squad by Coach Radojko.

    Singapore are in Group A of the biennial tournament alongside Myanmar, co-hosts Indonesia and a yet to be determined qualifier with our first match on 5 December. Group B in Bangkok features another qualifier, Malaysia, Vietnam and co-hosts Thailand.
