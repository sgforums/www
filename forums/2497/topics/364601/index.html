    DREAM RETURN ... Alvaro Arbeloa

    ALVARO ARBELOA is all go for joining the Real Madrid revolution - as SunSport exclusively revealed on Saturday. 

    We told you first that Real supremo Florentino Perez wanted the Liverpool full-back to return to the Bernabeu - where he made two appearances at the start of his career. 

    Real have made an £8million offer for the Spanish international, and while Reds want £10m, boss Rafa Benitez is under pressure to sell. 

    And last night Arbeloa, 26, said: "Going back to Spain is always an idea that's in my mind. 

    "I'm happy in Liverpool but I'd like to go back. "I have a year left at Liverpool but anything could happen." 

    Reds could sell as they are set to land England right-back Glen Johnson.
