Crespo hoping for Inter stay

  • turbo_drift
    4 May 07, 20:59
    Hernan Crespo has said he does not want to return to Chelsea at the end of the season.

    The Argentine striker agreed a two-year loan deal at Internazionale last summer, but Chelsea have the option to recall him after one year.

    Jose Mourinho recently admitted that he was keen to bring Crespo back to Stamford Bridge, but the player is confident the Premiership club will respect his wish to stay in Italy.

    Chelsea have until the end of the month to decide but Crespo believes they are too absorbed in their Premiership campaign to think about him, and hopes that remains the case.

    "Chelsea have until 31st May for asking me to return. Just with sending a fax," he told the South American media.

    "If they don't do that my loan at Inter gets renewed automatically.

    "But if they want me to return we will have to have a conversation about that. That's why the less it is spoken about the better for me.

    "At this moment Chelsea are totally concentrating on the next games of the Premiership so I don't think that they will be thinking of me but if they are they know what my opinion is."

    Crespo is grateful to Mourinho and Roman Abramovich for letting him go to Inter and is desperate to stay where he is happiest.

    He explained: "If I came to Inter it was because Jose Mourinho and the president did a personal favour for me. I asked for this the day that Abramovich desired to renew my contract.

    "He didn't want to let me go but in the end he understood what I was asking for.

    "I know that they want me to return but I want to stay at Inter because of personal reasons.

    "It's not true that I don't like England or Chelsea, it's because I have a personal decision of living in Italy because all my family is here."
