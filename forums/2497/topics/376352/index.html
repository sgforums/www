    Jack Wilshere lives it up at (milk) bar as Arsenal fans mob teen star

     

    Hippy hippy shake: Wilshere (left) joins Clichy and his girlfriend (right) at the opening of a new milkshake bar in Finchley, north London

     

     

    Arsenal's 17-year-old prodigy Jack Wilshere has been photographed sinking a cold drink in a bar just two days before he is likely to be called into Carling Cup action.

    Fear not, however, the under-age midfielder will not fall foul of the licensing laws as he attended the opening of Milk Shake City in Finchley, north London.

    Wilshere joined Gunners team-mate Gael Clichy and the France full-back's girlfriend as hordes of fans clamoured for and autograph and picture of the stars.

    Arsenal thumped Wigan 4-0 at the weekend, although Wilshere was not involved. The England Under 21 player is likely to feature in the Gunners' Carling Cup tie against West Brom at the Emirates Stadium on Tuesday.

    Manager Arsene Wenger is expected to again give his Arsenal youngsters and fringe players a chance to impress against the Baggies.

     

     

    Pop stars: Arsenal players Jack Wilshere and Gael Clichy are besieged by teenage fans in Finchley on Sunday

     

    Star guests: Arsenal stars Jack Wilshere and Gael Clichy attend the opening of Milk Shake City

     

    Centre-half Philippe Senderos should provide some experience at the back after remaining at Emirates Stadium despite the chance to leave during the transfer window, along with veteran Mikael Silvestre, who has made just one appearance so
    far this season.

    Sanchez Watt, 18, was on the bench for the Champions League game at Standard Liege and could start, while Kieran Gibbs, Fran Merida, Aaron Ramsey and Wilshere may also feature as they stake a claim for a regular first-team place.
