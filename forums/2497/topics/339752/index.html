    We'll shut Big Phil's big mouth

     

    KEEP QUIET ... Scolari's remarks angered Cluj

     

     

    CLUJ star Juan Culio has warned Phil Scolari: You will pay for your lack of respect. 

    Scolari said he would return to Brazil if Chelsea failed to beat the Romanians tomorrow and qualify for the Champions League knockout phase.

     

    CLUJ STAR ... Juan Culio

     

    But Argentinian winger Culio fumed: “That was a lack of respect for Cluj and Romanian football. We’ve proved we’re more than good enough to beat great sides and we can certainly beat Chelsea.

    "He can’t just say that if Cluj win or draw he will leave and go to Brazil.”

    Chelsea need to beat Cluj or Bordeaux could pip them to second spot in Group A if they beat Roma.

    PHIL SCOLARI is plotting a Champions League gamble by unleashing Drogula on Dracula.

    The Chelsea boss wants to pair Didier Drogba AND Nicolas Anelka for the first time against Transylvanian side Cluj tonight.

    Drogba is still short of fitness but Scolari declared: “It’s my way to have two strikers in my team and in my system.

    Chelsea must win at Stamford Bridge tonight to definitely make the group stages of the Champions League.

    Amazingly, Scolari claims he will not be desolate if Chelsea go out of the Champions League — a competition that is worth around £70million to the winners.

    Scolari said: “If we lose we lose. Maybe it’s possible. This is life. This is football.”
