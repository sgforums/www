Ranieri defiant: I'll be in charge of Juventus next season!

  • limpper
    10 May 09, 16:08

     

    Juventus coach Claudio Ranieri has defiantly declared he will be in charge next season.

    “I am in charge and will be the Juventus Coach next season too,” he insisted.

    “I try to do my best and I speak to the directors every day, so I certainly don't feel a lack of faith in my abilities.”

    Ranieri continued: “Milan-Juventus is a very interesting game and we are aware that our opponents are going through an excellent period of form, while we are not doing as well as we'd like.

    “Nonetheless, we are going to go for it the same way we always have done. We are coming off a relaxed week and everyone here is calm.”
