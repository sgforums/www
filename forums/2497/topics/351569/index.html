Quintuple will deliver MASSIVE bonus to Man Utd players

  • limpper
    13 Mar 09, 17:11

     

    Manchester United's stars are in line to earn a massive £9 MILLION in bonuses should they win the Quintuple.

    The Sun says some stars could pocket up to £400,000 each. The bonus schedule was agreed before this season started and includes separate payments for each trophy won.

    But there is also an extra wedge of between 10 and 15 per cent of the total earned based on the number of trophies they bring home.

    United players have already earned around £85,000 extra for winning the Carling Cup and Club World Cup.

    Victory in the Premier League and Champions League will see that figure rise by up to £200,000.

    And if they complete a remarkable grand slam by winning the FA Cup on May 30, another £65,000 will be paid out.

    On top of the basic incentives, United are also in line to pay out a Quintuple bonus based on each player’s appearance tally in each competition.
