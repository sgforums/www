Pato yet to decide on future

    Brazilian Alexandre Pato is yet to decide where his future lies.

    The Internacional forward is wanted by Chelsea, Milan, Internazionale and Real Madrid.

    The English giants were considered favourites, but his agent Gilmar Veloz insists that the 17-year-old is yet to make up his mind.

    The Brazilian is currently playing in the World Under-20 Championship in Canada and nothing will be decided until after he has returned.

    "I have received many phone calls regarding Pato," Veloz told Lance Press.

    "He is a young player that has a great future. Milan is interested in him, and Chelsea, Inter and Real Madrid also want him and he is who is going to decide what to do.

    "I am sure that he will be very soon playing in Europe, but first of all we will have to wait until he comes back from Canada were he has been playing the Under-20 World Cup."
