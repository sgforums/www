    Adebayor : to get his new improve deal of £80,000-a-week.

    Arsenal are confident AC Milan will shelve their interest in Emmanuel Adebayor when player and club sit down to discuss his future. Adebayor will open contract talks this week where he is expected to be offered a new-improved £80,000-a-week deal to stay in London. That would eclipse the £35,000-a-week deal the Togo international signed last year.

    Milan director Adriano Galliani was planning to fly to London this week to talk to Arsenal officials to strike a deal with the club. That plan could be put on hold following the 24-year-old Adebayor's claim that he is happy to stay in England.

    "My future with Arsenal has never been in doubt and I want to assure fans I will be here for many years," Adebayor said in The Sun. "My agent has met with coach Arsene Wenger about a new deal and I am happy about how the talks are going so the fans can rest assured."

    Adebayor's agent Stephane Courbis, meanwhile, has indicated that the player would still be interested in a move to Italy but that it is down to the clubs to reach an agreement which suits both parties.

    Courbis said on Italian radio: "Adebayor is happy at Arsenal, but when a footballer receives an offer from a team such as Milan it is difficult to say no.
