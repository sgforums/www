    Kaka Says Yes To Florentino Perez - Report

    Kaka could be on his way to Real Madrid this summer as Marca continue with claims of major scale spending in Spain...

     

    Real Madrid, Milan and Kaka have reached a pre-agreement which will see the Brazilian move to Spain this summer, according to reports.

    Madrid-based paper Marca's front page splash on Monday morning led with the headline "Kaka Says Yes To Florentino". The report goes on to tell of a secret meeting which took place last month between Rossoneri vice president Adriano Galliani and Los Merengues presidential candidate Florentino Perez.

    Real's grandiose scheme to thwart stiff competition for the player will see them pay Milan €60million over a four-year period in order to lure the Brazilian ace to the Bernabeu. The report also suggests Perez is trying to muscle his way in in an attempt to underline his plans to take the club back to the Galactico era, and to the pinnacle of European football.

    In addition, the millionaire is trying to make early progress to fight off competition from Chelsea and Manchester City. The Citizens tried and failed to land Kaka back in January. The latest voices from Spain come just days after the same publication reported that Perez was showing his hand for Manchester United ace Cristiano Ronaldo.

    So far there has been no reaction from the Milan camp.
