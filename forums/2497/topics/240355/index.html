Fabregas wants Barca switch

  • zocoss

    CESC FABREGAS wants to return to Barcelona. Fabregas, 19, is contracted at Arsenal until 2014. But the ex-Barca midfielder has hinted he would like to move back to his homeland.

    Fabregas said: "It is obvious that I am very happy at Arsenal, but I cannot predict what will happen in the future. "My girlfriend is Catalan and I expect that I will finish my career playing back in Catalunya."

    Fabregas claims he does not know if he will fulfil the seven-year contract he signed with the Gunners last October. He added: "I do not know where I am going to be in two, three or four years. "I am 19. I am only young and I have much more to learn."

    Fabregas is also backing Barcelona to overturn the 2-1 deficit when they travel to Liverpool in Tuesday's Champions League clash. He said: "It will be difficult. "But I am convinced that Barca can win the tie because they have have great players who are capable of coming back from 2-1 down."
