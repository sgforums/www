Zigic Reiterates Premier League Desire

  • omgukilledkenny
    14 May 09, 17:07

    The 6ft 8 inch forward wants to hit the heights in England's top division.

    Zigic, 28, said “My agent made me aware that several English clubs are following me regarding next season, but there is no offer on the table.

    “The Premier League is one of the best competitions in the world and it would be a good option if the sporting project was good and if I was valued on the market," he told setanta.com.

    “I have heard about English interest since last season but Valencia set a price for me that cannot be dropped. I have no idea of what is going to happen this summer.

    At the present time, my objective is to play well for Racing Santander and prove myself. To my mind, nobody can question my contribution since I joined the team in January," added the forward, who has an impressive tally of 11 goals in 17 games for his temporary club.

    “I am a professional football and will not rule anything out. I have to return to Valencia and a decision will be taken in the summer.

    “The papers are saying that they want to sell me, but I have never been told anything officially. A good offer could make me depart from Valencia.”
