    Chelsea FC have appealed against the decisions taken last week by the UEFA Control and Disciplinary Body in relation to the UEFA Champions League semi-final second leg against FC Barcelona in London on 6 May. (Click here for details.)

    Suspensions
    Chelsea striker Didier Drogba was suspended for six UEFA club competition matches – the last two of which are deferred for a probationary period of two years – while defender José Bosingwa was suspended for four UEFA club competition matches, the last of which is deferred for a probationary period of two years, for offences at the same game. Chelsea were fined €100,000. The date of the appeals hearing will be communicated in due course.
