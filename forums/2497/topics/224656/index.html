    AC Milan 0 - 0 Torino

    79' Alberto Gilardino (pen miss 79)

    Alberto Gilardino missed a late penalty as AC Milan's inconsistent form in Serie A continued on Sunday when they were held to a goalless draw by Torino at the San Siro.

    Gilardino's 76th-minute spot-kick beat Torino goalkeeper Christian Abbiati but rebounded off the post, much to the displeasure of the Rossoneri supporters.

    The Italian international had earned the penalty after being brought down by defender Gabriele Cioffi.

    The Rossoneri were fortunate to escape defeat after Torino substitute Roberto Muzzi was twice denied by the post in the closing stages of the game.

    Coach Carlo Ancelotti is sure to face more pressure from the media as Milan have now drawn three of their last four league games and are 10 points behind fourth-placed Catania.
