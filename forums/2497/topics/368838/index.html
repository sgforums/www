    Real Madrid's Torres: Ronaldo first to arrive at training, last to leave

    Real Madrid fullback Miguel Torres admits he's been amazed by the power of new teammate Cristiano Ronaldo in their first week of preseason training.

    Torres said: "Cristiano Ronaldo strikes me as not the same on TV as in person. He is a hard worker, the first to arrive and the last to leave.

    "He has shooting power I've not seen since Roberto Carlos left, and running with the ball, along with Messi he is the best in the world.

    "Let the games begin so people talk about how good he is and not what he cost."
