Cech: No more dropped points

  • turbo_drift
    4 Mar 07, 09:33
    Petr Cech feels Chelsea can not afford to drop any more points in the race for the Premiership title.

    The reigning champions trail current leaders Manchester United by nine points, but are hoping their rivals slip up at Liverpool on Saturday lunchtime.

    If United befall the same fate as Chelsea at Anfield, The Blues could then close the gap to six points with victory at Portsmouth come Saturday teatime.

    Mindful of the advantage The Red Devils currently hold, Cech believes Chelsea must boast a perfect record over their final 11 games.

    "The weekend we lost at Liverpool, Manchester United lost at Arsenal," said Cech.

    "It shows they are not unbeatable.

    "We have to play against them at home and they still have some difficult games, so they can drop points again.

    "What we have to do now is make sure that our loss of points against Liverpool is our last of the season."
