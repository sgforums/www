    Women's tennis world number one Caroline Wozniacki has made the dreams of several thousand Liverpool fans on Twitter come true after proudly displaying her team colours on court for the first time

    After promising her followers she would don the famous red shirt earlier on Thursday, the Danish pin-up took to the arena with a signed Steven Gerrard No.8 top on for her match with Italy's Flavia Pennetta.

    Earlier, the playful 20-year-old posted a picture of herself wearing the shirt after announcing to her followers she had received 'a GREAT gift' from a friend.

    'I open the gift and what's inside? A Liverpool shirt with Gerrard and number 8 on the back,' she wrote.

    'Personally signed for me :) Now I will be looking great next time I go to Anfield for a game :) '
