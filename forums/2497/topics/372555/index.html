    Toure: I joined Man City to win Champions League

    Kolo Toure says he's joined Manchester City to win the Champions League.

    The Ivory Coast star left Arsenal this summer to reinforce City's defences as they look to make a big imprint on the game.

    And one of the targets he has set for City is to face the Catalans in Champions League combat themselves.

    "That is my dream and something I want to be part of," he said.

    "People here are working very hard to get there. We saw it happen with Chelsea, why not Manchester City?"

    Man City defender Toure keen to face Barcelona's Yaya

    Manchester City defender Kolo Toure is ready to face brother Yaya tonight in Barcelona.

    "It will be the first time I have played against my brother for Barcelona, and will be a great game," said Toure ahead of the Joan Gamper trophy match.

    "We talk a lot. We didn't talk about me coming to City at first but when I was signing and told him I was coming here he was quite happy."
