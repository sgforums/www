Ibrahimovic drops move hint

  • omgukilledkenny
    25 Apr 09, 02:31

    Inter star Zlatan Ibrahimovic has hinted he may leave the San Siro for a fresh challenge.

    The admission comes on a day when fellow striker Adriano has severed all ties with the club and it leaves boss Jose Mourinho with a lot to ponder.

    Mourinho has publicly backed Ibrahimovic, labelling the striker the best player in the world, but the Sweden international has hinted on numerous occasions recently that he may consider a summer move.

    "I have a contract with Inter and I am happy here, but at the same time I would like to try something new because I have been in Italy for five years,” he told Eurosport.

    “I have won everything in Italy and I have learned a lot about the country, but there comes a time in your life, as I found when I was at Ajax, when you have to tell yourself to make a change.”
