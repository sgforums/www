    Ballack: Liverpool aren't good enough to end their title drought

     

    Michael Ballack thinks Manchester United will be Chelsea's main challengers for the title

     

    Michael Ballack has dismissed Liverpool’s chances of winning the title, claiming Rafa Benitez’s team are better suited to the Champions League.

    The Chelsea midfielder’s words were particularly provocative given that Benitez’s side are level on points with Chelsea.

    Ballack said: ‘It’s always the big four everyone speaks about, but I still think it’s about Chelsea and Manchester United.

    ‘Liverpool are very good but for me they are a club for the Champions League, partly because they can put their focus on one or two matches.

    'Arsenal are already behind, which makes it difficult for them.’
