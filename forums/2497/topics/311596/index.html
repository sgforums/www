    Singapore coach Radojko Avramovic has finalised his squad of 22 players for this Saturday’s international friendly against Australia and next Wednesday’s crucial World Cup Qualifier against Lebanon.

    The players to make way from the provisional squad are defenders Faizal Hamid and Sevki Sha’ban, midfielder Itimi Dickson as well as forward Masrezwan Masturi.

    Singapore lost their opening their opening qualifier 2-0 in Saudi Arabia last month and are looking to bounce back against Lebanon on March 26.

    Australia are preparing for a game in China.

    Singapore training begins on Wednesday 19 March with the team going into camp the day after.

    Singapore squad: Lewis (Home United), Sunny (Tampines Rovers), Chan (Young Lions); Khaizan (Geylang United), Emuejeraye (Gombak United), Jantan (Home), Bennett (SAF), Abdul Samat (Tampines), Yunos (Young Lions), Noh Rahman (Geylang); Ishak (Home), Jiayi (Home), Wilkinson (SAF), Muhamad (Tampines), Mustafic (Tampines), Harun (Young Lions), Halim (Young Lions); Nawaz (Geylang), Casmir (unattached), Sahdan Daud (Home), Duric (SAF), Amri (Young Lions).

    This match is televised on SuperSports, Channel 22, 6.55pm today ( 22 March ).
