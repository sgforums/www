Anelka will be a Chelsea player for another year

  • Poignant
    25 Jun 10, 05:13

    Chelsea boosted as Anelka signs one year extension


    Chelsea star Nicolas Anelka has agreed a one-year contract extension with the club until 2012.

    The 31-year old striker has scored 42 goals in 138 appearances since he joined from Bolton Wanderers in 2008.

    Speaking to the Blues' official website, he said: "When I first came to Chelsea I wanted to stay much longer than what I did in the past with other clubs.

    "It is a big club, you play with big players and I like this club, I like London. There is no need to move anymore and so I am very glad to sign a new contract and I know we will play in big games and win titles. I am very glad."

    Boss Carlo Ancelotti added: "Nicolas is a fantastic player who has good technical qualities and movement.

    "When I was coach at Juventus I wanted to buy him, so it is good now he has signed for me and I think Chelsea is his home. I look forward to working with him for one year more."
