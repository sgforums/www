    EPL: Ashley Young, Aston Villa v Bolton Wanderers (Getty Images)


    Tottenham Hotspur are believed to be in line to make a £15 million bid to sign Aston Villa winger Ashley Young.

    The two clubs began talks a few weeks ago with regard to the player's transfer to White Hart Lane, as exclusively revealed by Goal.com UK.

    It has been widely believed that Spurs boss Harry Redknapp could add more weight to his squad before the transfer window closes, in a move to bring in more quality players as the north London club step into the Champions League for the first time in almost half a century.

    Now the Daily Express claims that the former Portsmouth boss is also ready to sugar coat the potential offer by turning it into a player plus cash deal, as Robbie Keane, Jermaine Jenas and Alan Hutton are all said to be available if Villa should want any of them.

    For his part, Young is thought to be interested in a move to Tottenham.
