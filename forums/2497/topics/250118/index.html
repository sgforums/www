Crespo intends to stay at Inter

  • zocoss
    23 Apr 07, 10:49


    Hernan Crespo has revealed he has no wish to return to Chelsea and is anxious to stay at Internazionale. The Argentine striker joined Inter last summer, but Chelsea have a clause enabling the club to recall Crespo at the end of the season.

    Jose Mourinho has suggested he could exercise that option, although his comments have perplexed Crespo. The former Milan forward, who has scored 20 goals in 33 Premiership starts, has confirmed he has no desire to go back to Stamford Bridge. "I know there is a loan agreement between Chelsea and Inter, but sooner or later there will be a solution," Crespo told The People.

    "Then I will be able to completely cut the ties with Chelsea and continue my career in Italy. "Negotiations are open and Chelsea know my intention is to stay at Inter. "My agent is working hard on the subject in London, but I have not spoken with Mourinho since summer.

    "I have been told about Mourinho's words about my possible return and I don't understand them. "Last year he did not use me as much as he needed to, despite my giving everything in training and in games and scoring goals.

    "Now why would I return to be on the bench?"
