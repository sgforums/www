    The vital match between Termeno and Dro in the Italian seventh tier went to penalties.

    With the scores level at 4-4 and in sudden death, Termeno's Michael Palma stepped up to take the spot-kick which would keep his side in the contest — only to hit the bar.

    Dro keeper Loris Angeli ran off to be congratulated by his team-mates but what happened next was a total freak.

    The ball landed on the pitch after flying in the air, span back towards the goal and trickled over the line.

    Angeli was left red faced as he walked back towards the goal with Palma overjoyed, just seconds after his despair from missing.
