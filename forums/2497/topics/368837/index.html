    Michael Ballack has warned Chelsea boss Carlo Ancelotti he faces the axe if they do not win silverware this season.

    Ballack: Chelsea should focus on Premiership ahead of Champions League

    Michael Ballack says Chelsea should focus on winning the Premiership ahead of the Champions League this season.

    He said: “We have to focus on this, that’s for sure. We are really hungry to win the title back.

    “We dropped too many points against lower teams last season. But sometimes if you want to be on top you need a little luck.

    “Apart from this, Europe is a big target again because we are a big team with a lot of big international players who want to win the Champions League.

    “Even if we weren’t in the final, we’re still confident we’re one of the best teams in Europe. To know this is good for the future.”
