Blues enter race for Quaresma

    Chelsea have reportedly entered the race to sign Porto winger Ricardo Quaresma - but have been warned it will cost £24million to sign him.

    Reports emanating from Portugal claim Chelsea are ready to table a bid for the skilful wideman.

    The 24-year-old is thought to be considering his future at Porto and could be interested in a move away to a top club.

    Porto are facing a fight to keep hold of their star players following their expulsion from next season's Champions League.

    The news has alerted the likes of Chelsea, Internazionale and Real Madrid to Quaresma's possible availability this summer.

    Porto president Jorge Pinto da Costa has warned any interested club that it will take a bid of 30m euros (£24million) to buy the Portuguese international.

    "Those who are interested must pay his 30 million euros buy-out clause," said da Costa.
