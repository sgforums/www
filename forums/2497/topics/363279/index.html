Hiddink Questions Fergie Tactics

  • omgukilledkenny
    8 Jun 09, 18:26

    Guus Hiddink has had a pop at Sir Alex Ferguson for his lack of tactical preparation for the Champions League final.

     

    The Dutchman came within seconds of beating Barcelona over two legs in the semi-final but Ferguson's United never got close to the Spanish champions.

     

    Hiddink has laid the blame at Ferguson's door, saying: "I had no joy watching the Champions League final. It was boring. There was only one team on the pitch and it was Barca.

     

    "There was no real fight and it was proof that it's very important to analyse the opponent. Barcelona played their own football, but they also knew in advance all the weaknesses of Manchester United."
