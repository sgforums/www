    Florentino to kickoff Real Madrid reign tonight with Kaka ceremony

    Florentino Perez's new era at Real Madrid officially begins tonight with the unveiling of new signing Kaka.

    The ceremony will begin at 8:30pm at the Santiago Bernabeu in front of over 350 accredited journalists and an expected crowd of 20,000 fans.

    Real's honorary president Alfredo di Stefano will present Kaka with his first Real shirt and end speculation over which number the Brazilian will wear.

    Over 2,000 Kaka shirts have been sold by Real's official club shop in the past week - without a number listed.
