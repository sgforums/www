    Huntelaar denies United offer

    Real Madrid striker Klass-Jan Huntelaar insists he has received no offers to quit the Bernabeu, dampening speculation linking him with a summer switch to Manchester United.

    The goal-poacher moved to the Spanish giants from Ajax in January, but has failed to win over the Madrid fans and has been linked with an early departure from the club.

    Sir Alex Ferguson was reportedly keen to offer the Dutchman an escape route to Old Trafford, but Huntelaar says there has been no contact with other clubs.

    "I have had no offers," he said.  “I now have two games for Holland and then I can enjoy my holidays.”

    Huntelaar was signed originally as a replacement for the injured Ruud van Nistelrooy, but will have to fight it out with his compatriot for a starting berth next season.
