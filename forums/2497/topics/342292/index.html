Dirk Kuyt: This Is Just The Start

  • omgukilledkenny
    30 Dec 08, 02:28

    Liverpool’s Dutch forward Dirk Kuyt has admitted that the hard work starts now for the Reds as they enter the New Year as Premier League leaders.

    A 5-1 demolition of Newcastle yesterday confirmed Liverpool’s position at the top of the table with Chelsea being held to a 2-2 draw by Fulham. 

    Rafa Benitez now sees his side three points clear of Phil Scolari’s Blues, and a first title for the Merseyside club since 1990 is a real possibility.  

    Kuyt however is refusing to get too carried away, telling the club’s website that the hardest part is still to come.  

    “It's great to be top at the end of the year, but it's just the beginning for us” he said.

    “We need to step up every week and in every game and try to perform as well as that in the next match.

    “I enjoyed playing up front again. Unfortunately I couldn't get my goal but I'd prefer to play in a team like this and win 5-1, than score every week somewhere else.  

    “I really enjoyed the game and it caps a good Christmas period in which we have picked up six very important points and scored eight goals along the way.”
