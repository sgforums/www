FA clear Tottenham

    Tottenham Hotspur have been cleared by the Football Association of any blame after one of their fans aimed a punch at Frank Lampard.

    The incident occurred after the FA Cup quarter-final between the two sides at White Hart Lane.

    The FA were forced to look at Spurs' security provisions and analysed their safety report.

    After doing so, the FA have decided that Tottenham are clear of any guilt.

    "After reviewing a video of the incident and receiving a full report from Spurs, there will not be any disciplinary action against the club," said an FA spokesman.
