    Manchester United boss Sir Alex Ferguson banishes Anderson to the reserves

    Out in the cold? United misfit Anderson

    Anderson's career at Manchester United hit a new low when the £18million Brazil midfielder was dumped in the reserves.

    A frozen pitch caused the game against Manchester City to be called off, but the move is a sign of Sir Alex Ferguson’s frustration with the player.

    Sportsmail revealed last week that Ferguson had fined Anderson for going AWOL and the

    21-year-old was then left out of the squad for a third game running as United beat Arsenal.

    Paris Saint-Germain and Lyon expressed interest in taking Anderson on loan last month, but United were reluctant to let him go amid concerns over Owen Hargreaves.
