Carragher arrested and cautioned by police

  • omgukilledkenny
    28 Feb 08, 21:33

    Liverpool defender Jamie Carragher has been arrested and cautioned by police for common assault after a 'fracas' on the street.

    The Reds vice-captain was involved in an altercation on Tuesday morning as he was on his way to training at Melwood.

    A witness called police to report that two men were arguing by the side of the road and identified Carragher, who was later informed that the other man had lodged a complaint against him.

    Carragher later attended the police station with his solicitor and accepted a caution for common assault.

    A Merseyside Police spokesman said: "We can confirm that a 30-year-old man from the Blundellsands area received a police caution for common assault in relation to an incident on Liverpool Road, Crosby, on Tuesday February 26."
