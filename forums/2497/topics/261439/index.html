    Slade sad to see Terry leave

    Yeovil manager Russell Slade has admitted his disappointment at seeing midfielder Paul Terry join League One rivals Leyton Orient.Terry, the older brother of Chelsea and England captain John Terry, snubbed a new contract at Huish Park and opted for a move back to London.

    "I think the lure of returning to London was too much for Paul to turn down," said Slade.

    "I've enjoyed working with Paul and wish him all the best for this new stage of his career."

    Terry made 154 appearances for the Glovers, scoring 12 goals.
