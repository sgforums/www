    The Football Association of Singapore has today named a 30-man-squad to the Singapore Senior and Under-23 Teams who have been scheduled to play a total of six international friendlies later this month.

    The squad features 17 members of the team that successfully defended the Asean Football Federation Championship with a 3-2 aggregate win over Thailand in February 2007.

    SAFFC midfielder John Wilkinson has been called up to the Singapore National Team for the first time.
