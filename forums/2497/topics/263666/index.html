    Wigan are hoping to ressurrect a move for Everton midfielder Lee Carsley if he becomes available later in the summer.

    But the midfielder, who has just one year left on his Goodison park contract, is yet to receive confirmation that David Moyes will offer him an extension and may be happy to accept a two-year offer at the JJB Stadium.

    The 33-year-old has been a regular in the Toffees midfield for the past three seasons but could see his place under pressure from new signings in the coming weeks.
