Gunners dream for Moscow star

  • omgukilledkenny
    16 Dec 08, 00:46

    Lokomotiv Moscow striker Peter Odemwingie has admitted being an Arsenal fan and dreaming of The Premier League.

    Nigeria international Odemwingie made a name for himself with French side Lille in the Champions League before moving to the less media-exposed Russian league.

    The 27-year-old has revealed for the first time being an English football admirer and an Arsenal supporter.

    And Uzbekistan-born Odemwingie would jump at the chance of playing in his favourite league in the future.

    Odemwingie said: “I am dying to play Premier League football, as this league is viewed by many of my compatriots.

    “I am interested in all the top of the table teams, but as far as I’m concerned, I love Arsenal.

    “They play a football that I fancy and I watch them every time I can.”
