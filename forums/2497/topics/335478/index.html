    LAMPARD IS BEST PLAYER IN WORLD CLAIMS SCOLARI

     

    LUIZ Felipe Scolari hailed Frank Lampard’s opening goal last night as one of the best he has ever seen and called for the Chelsea midfielder to be recognised as the world’s greatest player.

    Ricardo Carvalho limped off at the end with what looked like a hamstring problem and Scolari fears a scan today could rule him out for a minimum of three weeks. But even that could not wipe the smile off his face after Lampard’s matchwinning performance.

    England international Lampard has been named on the list of candidates for the annual FIFA World Player of the Year award, but Chelsea boss Scolari believes that with his world-class strike last night he showed he is one of a kind.

    He said: “Maybe only Lamps could have scored a goal like that – he is such an intelligent player. I consider him the best goalscoring midfielder at the moment. It was one of the best goals in the world. One of the best goals I have seen in my life.”
