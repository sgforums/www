    Robinho in new charity controversy at Man City

    Robinho's future at Manchester City has again come into question.

    The News of the World says Robinho's City future looks doomed after snubbing the club's Christmas party for foster kids.

    "They were a credit to their club and their profession.

    "But the feeling was that Robinho had shown some very unfortunate children a complete lack of respect.

    "It just shows how one person can let the whole side down."
