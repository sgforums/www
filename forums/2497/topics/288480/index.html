    Milan 0 - 1 Roma



    Mirko Vucinic's late goal was enough to punish ten man Milan at the San Siro. It was yet another disappointing home defeat for a side that just cannot find Serie A form...

    Time could be running out for AC Milan coach Carlo Ancelotti as the rossoneri once again fell to a home defeat, this time at the hands of a resurgent Roma.

    Milan couldn't be faulted for effort or desire early on but, as has been so often the case this season, the Milanese looked good on the ball but were bereft of end product.

    What's more, Roma came with ambition - not too surprising, when you look at the other clubs that have taken poinst at the San Siro this season - and this ultimately punished the home side.
