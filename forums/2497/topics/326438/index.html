Eduardo: I feel good

  • omgukilledkenny

    Arsenal striker Eduardo will be back at training in the near future and is looking to put his horror injury behind him.

    The Croatia international was saluted by the Emirates Stadium faithful on Sunday, when he made a brief appearance before the friendly with Real Madrid.

    Eduardo looked great despite suffering a horror leg fracture last February, and the Brazil-born forward is back in London after following a rehabilitation programme in Sao Paulo.

    The 25-year-old is hopeful his injury nightmare is behind him and wishes to come back to action before the year ends.

    “I don’t feel pain anymore,” Eduardo told Globo.

    “It’s like I never broke my leg.

    “I never watched the pictures of my injury and don’t want to see it.

    “I’m hopeful I will forget it and continue my career as soon as possible.”
