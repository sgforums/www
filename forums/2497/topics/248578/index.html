Rafa wants focus on Premiership

  • turbo_drift
    15 Apr 07, 10:59
    Rafa Benitez is keen for Liverpool not to be distracted by thoughts of a UEFA Champions League semi-final.

    Fresh from seeing off PSV Eindhoven in midweek, The Reds returned to Premiership matters with a 0-0 draw at Manchester City.

    Though the game was a forgettable affair, Bolton Wanderers' defeat at Arsenal ensures Liverpool remain firmly in the top four.

    Benitez is eager for his side to secure a place in next season's Champions League and has upcoming home matches with Middlesbrough and Wigan Athletic to prepare for.

    Following that double-header is the first leg of the semi-final with Chelsea, but Benitez wants the focus to be on the Premiership.

    "For us at this stage of the season playing in the Champions League semi-final in a few days, it was important to win because maybe we could secure a top four position," Benitez told the popular website.

    "But we don't lose, so we have one more point and Bolton have lost.

    "We need to keep going and keep winning, and focus on every game.

    "After that we have time to think about the Champions League."
