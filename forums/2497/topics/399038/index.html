    Man Utd boss Ferguson confident Fulham will win Europa League

    Manchester United boss Sir Alex Ferguson is convinced Fulham will beat Atletico Madrid in tomorrow's Europa League final.

    Ferguson cannot see Atletico beating the west Londoners, who have benefited from having a settled side over the season.

    "I think there's only one choice to be honest with you," Ferguson told Sky Sports News. "Roy Hodgson's performance and feat in taking Fulham to a final after 63 matches is quite amazing really.

    "I think that the great thing about Fulham at the moment is you know what you're getting every week. There's a consistency and there's a good team selection all the time.
