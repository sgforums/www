    Chelsea Plan Move For West Ham United Goalkeeper Robert Green - Report

    The England international could be set for a move across London...

     

    Chelsea are set to launch a January move for West Ham United goalkeeper Robert Green, according to a report from The Daily Express.

    Concern is apparently growing at Stamford Bridge over the current form of Petr Cech, and the Stamford Bridge hierarchy believe that England international Green represents a ready-made replacement.

    Cech has often looked indecisive at set-pieces this season, with 11 of the 13 goals conceded by the Blues coming from dead-ball situations.

    With Aston Villa and Sunderland also reportedly keen on Green, Chelsea are believed to want to move swiftly to seal a deal for the England international.

    Should Villa sign Green Brad Friedel could well move in the opposite direction.
