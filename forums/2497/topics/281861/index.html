Gabby has eyes on England prize

  • omgukilledkenny
    25 Sep 07, 02:04


    Aston Villa's Gabriel Agbonlahor has set his sights on breaking into the senior England squad after his goal in the 2-0 win over Everton.

    Match Of The Day pundit Alan Hansen is predicting that Agbonlahor can become a star of the future if he maintains his current rate of progress.

    Agbonlahor produced a superb piece of control and a razor-sharp finish to score the decisive second goal - his third in four matches against the Toffees - as Martin O'Neill's side climbed to eighth spot in the Premier League.

    He said: "It helps you to improve your game when you are playing for the Under-21s against the best young players from other countries.

    "As an Under-21 player you have to be aiming to break into the full England squad and that's what I am doing. To play for your country is everyone's dream.

    "Gareth, Ashley and Scott have all been rewarded for performing consistently for Villa by getting an England call and that's what I have to do."
