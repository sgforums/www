    Uncertainty surrounding Liverpool’s Lucas

    Liverpool midfielder Lucas Leiva has hinted at a move away from Anfield if he is not used by Rafael Benitez this season.

    The Brazilian has started in just 25 league matches in two years after joining from Gremio and now another Brazilian club are interested in bringing him home.

    Corinthians are believed to be keen on acquiring the 22-year old and he will consider the option of he is not a Reds regular in 2009/10.

    "You have to see what Benitez plans but the president of Corinthians already knows I am interested and what I want,” Lucas said.
