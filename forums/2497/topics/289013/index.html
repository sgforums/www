    Ronald Koeman Appointed Valencia Coach

    Valencia CF have confirmed the appointment of Ronald Koeman as their new first team coach.

    Said the club in a statement:

    "Ronald Koeman will arrive at the club on Friday, the 2nd of November, to take over as head coach.

    "He will sign a deal for two seasons in addition to the current one. He will be presented to the media at 1300 (CET) on Friday."
