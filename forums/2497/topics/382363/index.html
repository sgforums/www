    Ricardo Quaresma, Felipe Melo & Klaas-Jan Huntelaar Make ‘Golden Bin’ Shortlist

    The candidates for the not-so-coveted ‘Bidone d’Oro’ award have been revealed…

     


    Italian radio show Catersport has announced the shortlist for the annual ‘Bidone d’Oro’ - 'Golden Bin' - award, which ‘honours’ the worst player of the calendar year in Italy.

    Last year’s winner, Ricardo Quaresma, has once again been named on the shortlist of ten players for the 2009 'accolade', but the Inter winger will come up against the likes of Milan striker Klaas-Jan Huntelaar, Juventus midfielder Felipe Melo and Roma hitman Julio Baptista.

    Previous winners of the ‘Golden Bin’ include Rivaldo, Nicola Legrottaglie, Christian Vieri and Adriano.

    ‘Bidone d’Oro’ shortlist for 2009

    1. Ricardo Quaresma (Inter)
    2. Christian Poulsen (Juventus)
    3. Amantino Mancini (Inter)
    4. Nelson Dida (Milan)
    5. Klaas-Jan Huntelaar (Milan)
    6. Julio Baptista (Roma)
    7. Juan Pablo Carrizo (Lazio)
    8. Felipe Melo (Fiorentina/Juventus)
    9. Tiago (Juventus).
    10. Ronaldinho (Milan)
