    Fed-up Pavlyuchenko wants out of Tottenham

    Roman Pavlyuchenko says he's ready to quit Tottenham.

    "No footballer likes being on the bench game after game," the 27-year-old told the Daily Mail. "I could lose my place in the Russian squad for the 2010 World Cup.

    "I'm ready now to listen to offers from other clubs. But I can't say anything concrete right now."

    Lokomotiv Moscow have expressed interest in the Russia striker.

    Lokomotiv president Nikolay Naumov said: "Pavlyuchenko is a strong player, who, of course, we are interested in, but his transfer is unlikely at the moment as he costs too much money."
