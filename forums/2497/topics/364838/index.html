    Barcelona No2 Tito: Messi, Iniesta better than Ronaldo, Kaka

    Barcelona assistant coach Tito Vilanova has dismissed Real Madrid's capture of stars Kaka and Cristiano Ronaldo.

    "We are not afraid of Real Madrid ... we're not frightened of anyone in football," said Vilanova.

    "They are being strengthened and Cristiano Ronaldo and Kaka are very good, but they had to do this because of what happened last season. Also, for me, Andres Iniesta is the best midfield player in the world and Lionel Messi the best in the forward area.

    "Iniesta and Messi are better than Cristiano Ronaldo and Kaka, in my opinion."
