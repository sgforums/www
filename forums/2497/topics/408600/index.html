Spurs keen to ship Jenas & Keane out to fund for major bid

  • Poignant
    19 Aug 10, 22:49

    Tottenham boss Redknapp ready to sell Jenas, Keane to fund £18M Fabiano bid


    Tottenham boss Harry Redknapp is keen to raise funds to bid for Sevilla striker Luis Fabiano.

    The Mirror says Redknapp wants to ship out midfielder Jermaine Jenas and will listen to offers for Robbie Keane to clear the way for Sevilla’s Fabiano.

    Redknapp has not disguised his admiration of Brazilian striker Fabiano, but that deal looked dead until this week when Spurs were sounded out again to see if they were still keen.

    Spurs are not rushing to meet Sevilla’s £18million asking price and it will also take some sales to make the deal happen. But Redknapp, quiet so far in the transfer window, is convinced Fabiano is worth the effort.
