Man Utd transfer target deny interest

  • Poignant
    6 Jul 10, 17:29

    Manuel Neuer has dismissed reports linking him with a move to Manchester United this summer by stating his intention to remain in Germany.

    The German number one has impressed, as he has helped his country to the World Cup semi-finals.

    The 24-year-old has now been linked with a number of clubs from across Europe, including United.

    But Neuer has insisted that he has no plans on departing Schalke this summer.

    "I will definitely stay in the Bundesliga," he said on Sky Sports News. "I have a contract until 2012 and I am not going to comment on any transfer rumours at this stage."
