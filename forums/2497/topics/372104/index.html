Mourinho found new excuse to explain on poor pre-season form

  • limpper
    14 Aug 09, 17:51

    Inter Milan boss Mourinho: I don't read newspapers

    Inter Milan coach Jose Mourinho has dismissed criticism of their preseason form.

    "I have not read either the comments or criticisms. I do not really need to. It was more important what I had seen and what I thought, what I 'feel' within us," said Mourinho.

    "Of course, after the defeat against Lazio, there was the frustration for the negative result, but we remained strong, have great awareness of our resources and what we can do in this new season."
