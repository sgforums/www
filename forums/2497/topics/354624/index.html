    Joao Moutinho has given his clearest indication yet that he could soon be playing in The Premier League.

    The Sporting midfielder confesses that he would find the opportunity to move to England difficult to turn down, alerting Everton to a potential summer bid.

    The Toffees have long admired the 22-year-old Portugal international, with Sporting claiming last July that an £11.8million offer was rebuffed.

    However, Moutinho told Portuguese media: “If I receive a good offer from England it will be difficult to reject.”

    Moutinho is under contract at Estadio Jose Alvalade until 2013, which means any potential clubs interested in the youngster would be forced to pay top price if they are to tempt Sporting into selling one of their brightest talents.
