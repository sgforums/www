    Final Day

    Portsmouth were very active, signing Fulham midfielder Papa Bouba Diop and Chelsea right-back Glen Johnson.

    They also sold defender Dejan Stefanovic to Fulham, while the sale of midfielder Gary O'Neil to Middlesbrough went through after a hitch over the player's medical.

    Lassana Diarra made a late move across London from Chelsea to Arsenal for an undisclosed fee. "He's a multi-functional player, making him a great addition to our squad," said Arsene Wenger.

    West Ham left it to the last minute to tie up the signings of Nolberto Solano from Newcastle (free) and Henri Camara from Wigan (one-season loan).

    Newcastle completed the signings of Senegal defenders Abdoulaye Faye and Habib Beye for undisclosed fees from Bolton Wanderers and Marseille, respectively.

    Derby County signed Celtic striker Kenny Miller on a three-year deal, while West Brom defender Curtis Davies moved to nearby Aston Villa on a season-long loan.

    Aston Villa also signed Togo midfielder Moustapha Salifou for an undisclosed fee from Swiss side FC Wil on a one-year contract,

    Reading signed Liam Rosenior from Fulham, with Korean Seol Ki-Hyeon moving in the opposite direction.

    Fulham were also active on the last day - manager Lawrie Sanchez bringing in Spurs midfielder Danny Murphy and Crystal Palace striker Shefki Kuqi, both on loan deals.

    Birmingham signed Honduran midfielder Wilson Palacios, 23, on loan from Olimpia until December, when they will have the option to make the deal permanent until 2010.
