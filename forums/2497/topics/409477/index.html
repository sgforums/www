    Trezeguet joins Hercules

    French striker David Trezeguet has ended his 10-year stay with Juventus after reaching a mutual agreement with the club to terminate his contract. Trezeguet has signed with Spanish club Hercules on a free transfer.

    The 32-year-old striker joined Juventus in 2000 from AS Monaco, scoring 138 goals in 245 appearances. The former Serie A Football of The Year was deemed surplus to requirements heading into the 2010-2011 Serie A season and currently sits fourth among Juventus’ all-time goal scorers.

    Trezeguet was part of the French squad that won the 1998 World Cup, earning 71 caps for France before retiring from the international stage in 2008
