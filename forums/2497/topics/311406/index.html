    THIERRY HENRY insists he has no regrets over leaving Arsenal — and jokes that playing for Barcelona is like being in The Beatles.

    French ace Henry, 30, is playing on the left side of midfield this season but has still scored 13 times.

    He said: “It has been a fantastic experience. I am enjoying it.

    "When you play with Barca away from home, it is chaos. When you travel with Barca, it’s like The Beatles are in town.

     

    Henry: I love Barcelona

    “I don’t have any regrets. Not at all. I’ve only been on the bench three times and one of those was for a rest.

    "I have seven goals in the league, three in the Champions League and three in the cup. And I have eight assists. For a winger, that is not bad!

    “I love London, don’t get me wrong. But I also love Barcelona. The weather here is amazing and the food.”

    Barca are seven points behind leaders Real Madrid but Henry said: “We can still catch them.”

    Henry Rules Out A Return

    Barcelona striker Thierry Henry says he has no intention of returning to England despite admitting that his form has been affected due to being apart from his daughter.

    Much has been made of Henry's struggles since his transfer to the Nou Camp from Arsenal.

    The 30-year-old admitted last week that any dip in performance was because he was "not happy" at not seeing his two-year-old daughter as often as he would like following his divorce last year.

    This news led to reports in the Spanish and British media that Manchester City were preparing a bid to bring the France star back to the Barclays Premier League.

    But speaking in Barcelona daily Sport on Tuesday Henry simply said a return to England "is not a possibility".

    Instead Henry preferred to concentrate on his future in La Liga.
