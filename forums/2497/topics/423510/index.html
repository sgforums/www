    Liverpool manager Kenny Dalglish and Andy Carroll share Boyzone bonding session

    When Kenny Dalglish became Liverpool manager the club's fans hoped for a return to the glory years. One period Anfield doesn't remember too fondly is the mid-1990s.

    So it seems odd that Dalglish should choose to celebrate that period so publicly by taking his new £35m striker to see reunited Irish pop group Boyzone at Liverpool's Echo Arena.

    The pair were spotted by Twitterer Sheena Williams who posted this picture of the happy couple enjoying all the hits of Ronan, Shane, Keith and the other one.

    The erroneously-named boyband were in Liverpool on their current tour, but it's anyone's guess why Dalglish and Carroll felt compelled to attend.

    Suggestions have included Carroll picking up hair maintenance tips, Dalglish watching four men move in unison in a way his back four can only dream of, or a simple love of insipid ballads and screaming middle-aged women.
