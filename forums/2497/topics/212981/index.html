    Manchester United and Newcastle will both be looking to get back on track following a disappointing couple of weeks in The Premiership.

    Manchester United
    Gabriel Heinze is set to continue at left back following his recent return from injury, while Nemanja Vidic could be given the nod once again at centre back.

    Both Ryan Giggs (hamstring) and Park Ji-sung (ankle) are still out, while Alan Smith still has some way to go before he can be considered for a starting spot.

    Newcastle
    Newcastle travel to Manchester in the knowledge that they have not won a league game at Old Trafford since 1972.

    Titus Bramble and Celestine Babayaro are both suspended for the clash, but Steven Taylor has recovered from a foot injury and will play. Craig Moore is likely to be recalled as well. Damien Duff and Scott Parker are likely to start, especially with Nolberto Solano struggling with a hamstring injury. Kieron Dyer is still out.
