    Olympic Qualifiers Preliminaries: Singapore vs Pakistan - Ticketing Details

    The Singapore Olympic football team will face Pakistan in their first match of the Preliminary Competition, Games of the XXIX Olympiad, Beijing 2008, on Wednesday, 7 February at Jalan Besar Stadium. Match will kick off at 7.30pm.

    Should Singapore defeat Pakistan in this two-legged preliminary tie, they will advance into the qualification round, where our boys will be in Group A, alongside Bahrain, Kuwait and Qatar.

    Singapore Squad List as below:

    Games of the XXIX Olynpiad, Beijing 2008 - Preliminary Competition
    Singapore vs Pakistan
    Wednesday, 7 February 2007

    Adult: $6
    Children: $2

    Ticket sales start at 6pm on matchday at the North and South entrances of Jalan Besar Stadium.

    The second leg of the tie will be held at Punjab Football Stadium in Lahore, Pakistan on Wednesday, 14 February 2007.
