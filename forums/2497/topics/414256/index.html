    Ronaldinho recalled by Brazil for Argentina clash

    Brazil coach Mano Menezes on Friday named Ronaldinho in his squad for the prestigious friendly game against arch-rivals Argentina in Doha, Qatar on November 17.

    Ronaldinho, who was not selected for the World Cup by Menezes's predecessor Dunga, is joined in the squad by AC Milan team-mates Thiago Silva, Robinho and Alex andre Pato.

    The 30-year-old Ronaldinho, a two-time FIFA World Player of the Year, has not represented his country since he played in a World Cup qualifier against Peru in April 2009.

    Brazil squad

    Goalkeepers: Victor (Gremio), Jefferson (Botafogo), Neto (Atletico Paranaense)

    Defenders: Daniel Alves (Barcelona/ESP), Rafael ( Manchester United/ENG), Adriano (Barcelona/ESP), Andre Santos (Fenerbahce/TUR), Thiago Silva (AC Milan/ITA), David Luiz (Benfica/POR), Alex (Chelsea/ENG), Rever (Atletico Mineiro)

    Midfielders: Lucas (Liverpool/ENG), Ramires (Chelsea/ENG), Sandro (Tottenham Hotspur/ENG), Jucilei (Corinthians), Douglas (Gremio), Philippe Coutinho (Inter Milan/ITA), Ronaldinho (AC Milan/ITA), Elias (Corinthians)

    Forwards: Robinho, Alexandre Pato (both AC Milan), Andre (Dynamo Kiev/UKR), Neymar (Santos).
