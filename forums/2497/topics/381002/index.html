    Kuszczak hits out at Man Utd No1 Van der Sar: Maybe he doesn't like me

    Tomasz Kuszczak says he has little to do with Manchester United teammate Edwin van der Sar.

    Polish international Kuszczak has been at United for more than three seasons but has failed to dislodge Dutch veteran Van der Sar and has made only 35 first-team starts.

    Kuszczak told MUTV: "I must say this, and Edwin won't like this too much for sure, but he doesn't help me too much. I have told him a few times already to give me some more advice because he's got more experience.

    "I say: 'You have to give me something more because I like to know what I do wrong and like to watch people because I can improve as a result of the advice.'

    "But I don't know, maybe Edwin doesn't like me. You'll have to ask him."

    Kuszczak unsettled at Man Utd

    Tomasz Kuszczak admits he's unsettled at Manchester United.

    Kuszczak - who could not even get in the team when Edwin van der Sar missed the start of the season - also hinted that he is growing frustrated with his lot.

    He told MUTV: "As the manager says, you need the whole squad to win trophies. Some play more, some play less. But sooner or later everyone must go and play important games.

    "The question is how long do you want to wait? Because I try every day. It's a slow process. It's not so easy. I've already waited three and a half years. I will wait longer but my ambition is so big and I want to have a nice feeling after games, be tired and go home and think, 'Oh, you did a good job'."
