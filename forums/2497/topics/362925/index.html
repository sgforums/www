    Ferguson knows a £75m fee is good value and it would give him the funds to sign two or three players. United have not raised the subject with Ronaldo, who has been on holiday before joining the Portugal squad for their World Cup qualifier away to Albania.

    However, Ferguson has told friends in the game that £75m is the level that would make United take Real seriously and see the Reds star head for Spain.
