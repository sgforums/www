    Real Madrid coach Pellegrini still wants three signings

    Real Madrid coach Manuel Pellegrini admits his squad is still THREE players short.

    Despite their mega spending on Kaka, Cristiano Ronaldo, Raul Albiol and Karim Benzema, Pellegrini says his midfield options are still short.

    "The attacking department is complete," said the Chilean, "but we miss a couple of midfielders and a wide player."

    Pellegrini added: "I would like to have the squad structured as soon as possible.

    "By July 27 would be good when the international players are due back."
