    Benfica Reject Chelsea's €19.7 Million Bid For Defender David Luiz - Report

    Chelsea have had a £17 million bid for Benfica’s highly-rated Brazilian defender David Luiz turned down, according to the Guardian.

     
    David Luiz

     

     

     

    Manager Carlo Ancelotti is known to be monitoring the injury situations of key defenders Alex, who has had knee surgery, and John Terry, who has a sciatic complaint, and he is said to be an admirer of the Benfica man.

    The Italian has, however, hinted in recent weeks that a British-based player could be a more suitable option, and Portuguese-speaking Luiz would have to break the language barrier before he could settle into life in England.

    Chelsea’s reported £17 million offer falls way short of the £43 million needed to activate the release clause in Luiz’s contract and it is said that Benfica would rather sell players such as Fabio Coentrao and Carlos Martins as they look to raise the funds to be able to keep Luiz in their ranks.
