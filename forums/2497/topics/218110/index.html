    Welcome all. The game is a simple one. Predict either a Home Win, an Away Win or a Draw in any of the respective weekly stickied threads which includes the EPL, Uefa CL and selected English Cup matches and you could easily earn a point.

    Underline the team you choose to win. Underline both teams if your prediction is a Draw and fill in the HT & FT scores only for the Match of the week games (Will be stated by the side of our weekly fixture schedule).

    Example:

    England vs Scotland
    This will be consider as a England win.

    England vs Scotland
    This will be consider as a Scotland win.

    England vs Scotland
    This will be consider as a Draw.

    Also for the "Match of the week" games, the team at the front will always be taken as the Home team, in this case : England to lead 1-0 at HT and to win 2-0 at FT. That goes for the rest of the fixtures too... Teams at the front should always be taken as the home side. Should any of you are not clear just leave your question and the mods here would get back to you.

    England vs Scotland
    HT: 1 - 0
    FT: 2 - 0 .

    Correct prediction per match will earn you 1 point on the score board below. Exact scoreline for our Match of the week will earn you 3 points and also an additional point for a correct Half-time score.

    Note: Your prediction postings should be posted before the match " kick-off " and no editing of your prediction post once the match starts or the matches before your editing time will be consider invaild.

    Manager of the Month will be announced after the last game of each month. The more you play, the more you win!

    So what are you waiting for, do join in the fun and test yourself against the guys here and see if you have what it takes to be the New Prediction Manager.



    NOTE: No posting and predictions will be done here
