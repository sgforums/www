    I'm Glad Jose Mourinho Is Not Barcelona Coach - Johan Cruyff

    The former Dutch master has taken a swipe at 'Mou'

    Johan Cruyff

    Honorary Barcelona president Johan Cruyff has lashed out at Inter coach Jose Mourinho, saying he is glad he doesn't coach the Catalans.

    Perhaps Cruyff is still annoyed after Inter reached the Champions League final at the Blaugrana's expense. And he has taken a swipe at the reportedly Real Madrid-bound Mourinho.

    "Some years ago he [Mourinho] could have been the coach of Barcelona. And, now, after some time, I can say that I am happy that he doesn't coach our team," Cruyff told La Gazzetta dello Sport as reported by Tuttomercatoweb.com

    Mourinho spent time at Barcelona as one of the late Sir Bobby Robson's assistants whilst he learned the trade.

    And recent reports in both Spain and Italy have claimed he could leave Inter for Real Madrid if he wins the Champions League later this month.
