    Ronaldo tribute to Man Utd boss Ferguson

    Real Madrid signing Cristiano Ronaldo has paid tribute to his former Manchester United manager Sir Alex Ferguson.

    "He's the master. He was my father in football. He has been instrumental in my career, and outside of football," said Ronaldo.

    "Talent is not everything. You can have it, but you need to learn this craft to be the best. When I came to England I was a lad of 18 years. Mr Ferguson has taught me to refine my faults, namely to give a pass at the last moment, to make the right decision in the area."
