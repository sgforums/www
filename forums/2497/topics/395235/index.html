    Benfica vs Liverpool

    Thursday, 1 April 2010

    Channel 61 Football Frenzy :  3.00am  -  (Fri Morning) (Live)

    Europa League Quarter Final Game (1st Leg)

     

     

     

    Benfica winger Angel Di Maria has played down a possible move to England before targeting victory over Liverpool in the Europa League tonight.

    The Argentina international, who put Everton to the sword in the Portuguese giants' 5-0 and 2-0 wins over the Toffees earlier this season, is one of Europe's hottest properties and is wanted by Premier League giants Manchester United, Manchester City and Chelsea.

    The winger will line up at the Estadio Da Luz tonight in his latest English audition but the 22-year-old refused to comment on speculation surrounding his future.

    'I am not interested in the rumours - in England they look through everything with a magnifying glass but I don't have to worry about it,' said Di Maria.

    'At the moment I am only interested in Benfica and I think we can win anywhere in Europe.'

    Looking ahead to tonight's quarter-final first leg, Di Maria was in no doubt over who would pose the biggest threat to his side.

    'Fernando Torres is one of the best forwards in the world,' said Di Maria. 'He is the big danger - but our defence has enormous quality and can stop him.'
