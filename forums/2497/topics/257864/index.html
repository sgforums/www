    HERNAN CRESPO'S Chelsea career is over after he agreed a deal to stay in Italy with Inter Milan. The Argentine striker, 31, joined the Blues in 2003 and scored 20 goals in 49 games. But after failing to cement his spot in the side he was shipped out on loan to AC Milan before moving across town to rivals Inter last season.

    Crespo's agent Fernando Hidalgo has revealed that his client will stay with the Italian champions on loan for another season. He will then sign a two-year deal for Inter when his Chelsea deal ends in 2008.
