    Aston Villa's Stephen Warnock: We Can Test Arsenal

    It's no shock that we beat big teams, says defender.

    Stephen Warnock, Aston Villa (Getty Images)

    Stephen Warnock believes he and his Aston Villa team mates have the confidence to beat Arsenal, when the two sides face each other on Sunday.

    The Villans are level on points with the Gunners, and the left-back reckons his side have what it takes to beat Arsene Wenger's men.

    “It’s a game of third versus fourth so there’s going to be a lot of hype,” said Warnock in The Birmingham Mail.

    “They’ll be talking about where it will leave us towards the end of the season and whether we will finish in the top four.

    “I think the way we’re playing, we’re confident going into the game and confident of beating anyone who’s put in front of us."

    But the former Liverpool defender warned his team mates against complacency and over-confidence.

    “But we’ve got to understand that Arsenal are a very strong team at home and we know it’s not going to be an easy game," he said.

    “When we beat these teams no-one says that’s an upset.

    “With the money that has been spent over the summer the manager has strengthened the team and the squad, and people expect more of us.

    “I think ourselves, Tottenham and Manchester City have all been placed in the same bracket as potentially the teams that are going to break into the top four.

    “So it doesn’t come as a surprise when we do beat teams.”
