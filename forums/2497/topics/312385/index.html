    CROCKED Arsenal star Eduardo could still be part of Croatia's Euro 2008 campaign despite his horror injury.

    The Gunners ace was ruled out until Christmas after breaking his leg against Birmingham last month.

    But the striker will be invited to join Croatia's squad at this summer's finals to boost team morale.

    Boss Slaven Bilic said: "I want Eduardo there and so do the players, it would also mean a lot to him.

    "He has been a driving force on our road to the finals and has a big desire to be a morale-boosting factor during the event but it's up to his club Arsenal.

    "He is making very good progress and we expect he will be able to jog lightly by the time the European Championship starts but he will need plenty of time to make a full recovery."
