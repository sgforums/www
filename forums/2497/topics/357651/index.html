Xavi: Barca more beautiful than Blues

  • omgukilledkenny
    27 Apr 09, 00:26

    Xavi favours Barcelona’s brand of the beautiful game than the version practised by Champions League opponents Chelsea.

    The midfield schemer claims he knows what to expect from the Stamford Bridge side when they visit Camp Nou for the first leg of a Champions League semi-final on Tuesday.

    Barca’s intricate style of play will be pitted against a typically physical approach from English opposition.

    “They play a very effective game, but perhaps you could it is not the most beautiful to watch,” said Xavi.

    “We know what Chelsea are about. They are about playing physical and tough football – we like to play football a little bit more beautiful than that.

    “If we play football our way and at our pace, we know we will be very difficult to beat.

    “It is not the Barcelona way to change our game just because some teams play tough football.

    “We can out-pass you and we can out-think you – that is our brand of football.”
