    Tottenham target £15m Chelsea striker Anelka

    Tottenham have made an enquiry about signing Chelsea striker Nicolas Anelka in case they fail to sign Samuel Eto'o from Barcelona.

     

    With Dimitar Berbatov almost certain to leave Spurs are looking for replacements.

    They have targeted Valencia's David Villa - as revealed by Sportsmail - as well as Eto'o, with chairman Daniel Levy reported to be ready to fly to Spain to bring the player back.

    But reports in the Express suggest Tottenham have shortlisted Anelka and are ready to offer £15m for the France star who joined Chelsea in January.

    However, with the Blues set to lose Didier Drogba they may decide to keep him - or at least prevent London Premier League rivals buying him.
