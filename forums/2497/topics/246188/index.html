  • anhydrouscoppersulphate

    United confirm Vidic break

    Manchester United have confirmed that Nemanja Vidic suffered a broken collarbone in Premiership action on Saturday.

    Vidic fell awkwardly midway through the first half of United's 4-1 win over Blackburn and was immediately stretchered from the field.

    Sir Alex Ferguson admitted after the match that he expected the Serbian to be sidelined for four to five weeks.

    Vidic will certainly miss both legs of United's UEFA Champions League tie against Roma and also his club's FA Cup semi-final date with Watford.

    The player will be sidelined for Premiership matches against Portsmouth, Sheffield United and Middlesbrough, while a recovery in time to face Everton on 28th April also appears unlikely.
