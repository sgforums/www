Bayern confirm Robben liking

  • zocoss
    12 Jan 07, 22:05


    Bayern Munich have confirmed their liking for Chelsea star Arjen Robben.

    Reports in Germany claim that The Blues have made secret enquiries for Manchester United target Owen Hargreaves, and from this Bayern expressed their interest in Robben.

    The German giants are looking for a spark out wide which has seen them make enquiries for Franck Ribery of Marseille - but Robben could be a real alternative for Felix Magath.

    "He is a good player, very good dribbler and quick," he told Bild.

    Munich chairman Karl-Heinz Rummenigge also admitted that he was a player that was an attractive option.

    "He is an interesting player, that every team would fear to face," he added.

    Robben is currently anything but a regular starter for Chelsea having become the victim of Jose Mourinho's new tendency to play two up-front.

    The Dutchman has two years left on his current deal and has previously attracted the interest of Real Madrid, and should Chelsea decide to listen to offers - then there will be no shortage of suitors.
