    Chelsea plan cash-plus-player bid for Atletico Madrid ace Aguero

    Chelsea are launching a cash-plus-player bid for Atletico Madrid ace Sergio Aguero.

    The Daily Mail says Chelsea are believed to be ready to offer £38million plus Deco for Atletico Madrid striker Aguero.

    The Blues will argue that the bid meets the Argentina star's buyout clause, which stands at about £51m plus taxes.

    Manchester United are also keen on the 21-year-old, but both clubs would insist on driving down his £200,000 a week pay demands.

    Former Barcelona playmaker Deco is likely to welcome a move back to Spain after struggling in his debut season and criticising the club.
