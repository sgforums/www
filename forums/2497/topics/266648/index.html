    Rangers have signed Northern Ireland international Roy Carroll.

    The Ulsterman has penned a one-year deal following his release by West Ham.

    Gers boss Walter Smith knows Carroll from their time together at Manchester United and he wasted little time in sealing a deal for the 29-year-old.

    Carroll will compete with Allan McGregor and Graeme Smith for the number one jersey at Ibrox.
