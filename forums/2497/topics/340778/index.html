North London rivals eye Gervinho

  • omgukilledkenny
    16 Dec 08, 21:38

    Le Mans forward Gervinho has revealed that scouts from Arsenal and Tottenham Hotspur recently watched him in Ligue 1 action.

    The Gunners have been linked with the highly-rated Ivory Coast international in the past, although the Premier League side have yet to comment on the speculation.

    Gervinho has now intimated that Spurs have demonstrated an interest in his services after he featured in the recent 3-1 loss at Paris St Germain.

    The 21-year-old told Sky Sports: "I was not aware of Tottenham and Arsenal officials in the stands.

    "I was told after, however it does not concern me as I need to do my job for Le Mans at the moment.

    "They are both good teams. I have friends in both sides. I like Arsenal, but Spurs are good as well.

    "I do not favour either team. I do like the Premier League."
