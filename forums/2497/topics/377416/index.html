    The Football Association of Brunei Darussalam has been suspended indefinitely by FIFA due to "government interference''.

    FIFA's ruling came about following the Brunei government's decision to create a new national governing body.

    "The Football Association of Brunei Darussalam (BAFA) was suspended due to government interference in its affairs which started with a decision by the Brunei authorities to dissolve the BAFA and to replace it with a new association,'' read a FIFA statement.

    "The suspension applies with immediate effect and means that Brunei club DPMM will no longer be permitted to play in the Singapore S-League.

    "The suspension will run until the BAFA is reinstated by the government.''

    DPMM are fourth in the S-League, with five matches left to play.
