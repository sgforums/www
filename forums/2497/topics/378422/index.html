    SINGAPORE: SingTel unveiled its price plans for the Barclays Premier League on Saturday, which it won three—year exclusive broadcast rights to, commencing August 2010.

    The Barclays Premier League will cost S$23 (excluding GST) per month and will include all 380 Premier League matches, as well as a high—definition set—top box.

    As an additional bonus, customers who sign a one—year plan to watch these matches will also enjoy the UEFA Champions League and UEFA Europa League at no additional cost till May 2011.

    Customers who enjoy other sports will also have the option to subscribe to a wider range of sports programmes at S$25 (excluding GST) per month on mio TV.

    Besides the Barclays Premier League, this will include other content which will be revealed once the composition of the additional channels is confirmed.

    SingTel said more pricing and promotional information, as well as sign—up details will be announced shortly.
