    Xavi insists Barcelona deserve to win the Champions League this week.

    Discussing their final clash with Manchester United, Xavi declared: "I think it would be a reward for the football we have played this year. The team deserves it, we play the best football in Europe and I think we deserve it, but it is a final and in football anything can happen."

    Xavi is also backing Lionel Messi to silence doubters questioning his ability to handle Premiership opposition: "This is the best footballer in the world and I am sure he will show this because he has grown and he knows he is the best.

    "We are very confident and for us it is a privilege to play this type of game because it will prove who is number one."
