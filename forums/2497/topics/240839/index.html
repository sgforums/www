    Duo in running for Racing pair

    Arsenal and Bolton have reportedly joined Manchester United in the pursuit of Racing Santander's highly-rated defensive duo Ruben Gonzalez and Ezequiel Garay.

    Representatives from the Premiership clubs were in attendance for Racing's 1-0 win over Osasuna on Sunday.

    Although Racing confirmed the presence of the two clubs at the match, they claimed they had held no talks over specific targets.

    "All we know is that passes were issued for the match but there has been no contact regarding individuals," said a Racing spokesman.

    Racing's surprise success in La Liga has seen a number of players perform impressively and giant striker Nikola Zigic has also been linked with a move away.

    The Serbian is reportedly being trailed by Tottenham and Juventus.
