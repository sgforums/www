Jose warns United

  • turbo_drift
    9 Apr 07, 20:51
    Chelsea boss Jose Mourinho has warned Manchester United boss Sir Alex Ferguson the pressure is on them now.

    The Blues cut United's lead at the top of the table to three points at the weekend following their win over Tottenham and United's defeat at Portsmouth.

    Mourinho has now stepped up the mind games with Ferguson by claiming the pressure is on United and that Chelsea can still land an unprecedented quadruple.

    "Yes, United are under pressure now. It's been a good weekend for us, but we don't control the fixtures any more," said Mourinho.

    "We never again play before they do for the rest of the season.

    "Yes, we can win all four competitions but we know also that we can lose all three of them. It's been a good weekend for us but now it is about the Champions League and FA Cup.

    "I don't really want to speak about the Premiership at this stage. I only want to think about the quarter-finals of the Champions League against Valencia and the semi-final of the FA Cup with Blackburn.

    "They are big games to play.

    "We will think about the Premiership next week. They play Roma and Watford. I wish them good luck against Roma. I hope they win."
