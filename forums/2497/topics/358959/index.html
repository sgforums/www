    United boss Sir Alex snubs Becks after Arsenal thumping, preferring to cuddle up to Capello.

     

    Well done, Fergie: Fabio Capello (left) and Sir Alex Ferguson embrace after Manchester United's win over Arsenal as David Beckham (right) waits patiently.

     

    'It was just one of those things,' claimed David Beckham back in 2003 after his then boss Sir Alex Ferguson has kicked a boot in his face - but perhaps the Manchester United manager is still holding a grudge.

    Beckham and England head coach Fabio Capello waited in the Emirates tunnel last night to congratulate Ferguson following his side's resounding Champions League victory over Arsenal, but Sir Alex appeared to snub his former midfielder and only had eyes for Capello.

    The Italian and Scot joked and hugged each other as the former England captain waited patiently for his turn - but Becks never got one.

    After Ferguson finishes chatting to Capello, he walks past Beckham giving him an extremely faint, awkward pat on the shoulder, but doesn't appear to say anything.

    Beckham is left looking bemused by his ex-manager's lack of warmth. Could that old boot in the face have been intentional after all?
