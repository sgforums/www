    Mellberg plays down Juve reports

    Aston Villa defender Olof Mellberg has poured cold water on reports suggesting he has agreed a switch to Serie A giants Juventus.

    He said: "I was linked with Juventus but I am focusing on doing well for Villa."

    Villa boss Martin O'Neill revealed last week the club were in talks with Mellberg over a new deal.

    "We are in the throes of things, but there's nothing definite just yet. We're in discussions," he told the Birmingham Mail.
