    WAYNE ROONEY has hailed Paul Scholes as the best passer in the world — even at 34!

     

    Scholes shone in Manchester United’s 3-0 win over Fulham on Wednesday, hitting a volleyed opener and dictating the game with his range of passing.

    Team-mate Rooney said: “Paul is amazing. Some of his football I don’t think any other footballer in the world could produce. His passing was a joy to watch.”

    Rooney, who scored with his first touch as a sub, said: “We played some brilliant football and fully deserved the three points.”
