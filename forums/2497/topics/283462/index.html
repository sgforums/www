    Tottenham supporters have been offered free heart healthchecks after it was revealed they are most stressful top-flight team to watch.

    Season ticket holders have been urged Spurs fans to turn up without an appointment at any Lloydspharmacy in London for a free Heart Healthcheck - on production of a season ticket with you.

    It is all part of a drive to improve heart awareness as the trials and tribulations of following a football team have been known to cause health problems.

    When England played against Argentina in the 1998 World Cup Finals the number of hospital admissions for heart attacks increased by 25 per cent over the course of the following 48 hours.
