Van Marwijk and Advocaat : Wenger 'always moans'

  • zocoss

    Arsene Wenger 'always moans': Bert van Marwijk and Dick Advocaat attack Arsenal boss

     

     

    Under attack: Van Marwijk (left) and Advocaat have slammed Wenger

     

    'Always moaning': Wenger 'Moaner'

     

     

     

    Arsene Wenger came under heavy fire last night after Holland coach Bert van Marwijk told the Arsenal manager to stop trying to influence his selection policy and Russia boss Dick Advocaat accused him of ‘always moaning’.

    The club versus country row involving Wenger and two of his international counterparts escalated after the Frenchman had insisted it would be ‘a scandal’ if striker Robin Van Persie was picked for Holland’s friendly against Turkey on November 17. 

    The 27-year-old has not played since injuring his ankle in August, but Van Marwijk included the striker in his 24-man preliminary squad for the Amsterdam game.

    Responding to the Arsenal manager’s comments, he said: ‘They don’t mean anything.
    I’ve spoken to Robin personally and he is doing well. He will rejoin training there so I had no hesitation picking him.’ 

     

     

    Out: Van Persie suffered an ankle injury in the 2-1 win against Blackburn at Ewood Park

     

     

    Asked whether he intended to speak to Wenger, Van Marwijk added: ‘Has there been any contact with the medical staff of Holland and Arsenal? I don’t know. I don’t care, because I spoke to Robin personally and I just made the selection and will wait for any comments to come.’

    Russia coach Advocaat also hit out at Wenger after naming Andrey Arshavin in a provisional squad to face Belgium, even though he missed the defeat at Shakhtar
    Donetsk due to illness. 

    Asked if he was worried about Wenger’s reaction, Advocaat said: ‘He is always moaning about something. Mr Wenger wants to become the champion of England; I
    want my team to qualify from the Euro 2012 group. We need to respect the interests of each other.’
