    Real Madrid eyeing Russian transfer market

    Real Madrid are eyeing the Russian transfer market.

    New Real president Florentino Perez is keen to establish a presence in Russia and has representatives actively exploring potential partnerships.

    Sport-Express says along with mining the most talented youth, Real are also eyeing CSKA Moscow goalkeeper Igor Akinfeev, teammate Alan Dzagoev, the attacking midfielder, and Dinamo Moscow attacker Feder Smolov.
