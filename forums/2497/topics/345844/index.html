    Arsenal striker Eduardo da Silva has, surprisingly, been named in the Croatia squad for a friendly against Romania in Bucharest on February 11.

     

    Eduardo has now recovered from a broken leg and dislocated ankle suffered last February, but has yet to make a first-team appearance for the Gunners since suffering the horrific injury against Birmingham City.

    Trabzonspor's Hrvoje Cale and Ivan Juric of Genoa have been called up to the national squad for the first time, while Tottenham's Vedran Corluka and Luka Modric and Portsmouth's Niko Kranjcar are also included.
