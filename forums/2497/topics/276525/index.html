    Marca claims that the Anfield club is prepared to pay 20 million euros for the attacking midfielder, 4.5 million of which would go to Baptista's former club Sevilla, due to a contractual clause.

    Baptista is thought to be happy at Real Madrid - as long as he is not formally told that there is no room for him in coach Schuster's plans. During the summer he received a recall to the squad, putting paid to that idea for now - but he's barely featured since then.
