    Fulham claim hat-trick of awards at Premier League AGM

    Fulham Football Club collected a hat-trick of awards at the recent Premier League AGM at Stapleford Park.

    The Cottagers collected the Barclays Premier League Fair Play Award for the Team’s behaviour on the pitch, whilst Fulham’s fans topped the Behaviour of the Public League.

    In addition, Roy Hodgson picked up the Barclays Spirit Award – which was devised by the Premier League in conjunction with their 'Get on with the Game' project and the Football Association's 'Respect' campaign.

    Fulham Chairman, Mohamed Al Fayed told the club's official web site:
    "I am delighted to see that Fulham’s long-standing traditions of fair play and sportsmanship have been upheld by Roy Hodgson, the Team and our wonderful supporters. It has been a magnificent season and these awards have proven that there is a place for respect and integrity in the modern game. We will be proud to represent England in Europe next season."
