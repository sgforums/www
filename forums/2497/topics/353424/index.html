Milan to face Inter in Massachusetts

  • limpper
    25 Mar 09, 22:35

     

    AC Milan will play Inter Milan in a Massachusetts friendly on July 26 in what the clubs said would be their first derby in the U.S. since 1969.

     

    "After 40 years there will be another derby in the United States," AC Milan director Umberto Gandini said at a news conference giving details of a six-city round robin tournament also featuring Chelsea and Club America of Mexico.

    Chelsea will also play a match against the Seattle Sounders, the newest addition to Major League Soccer, while AC Milan have a match against Los Angeles Galaxy as part of an agreement to extend David Beckham's loan to the end of the Serie A season.
