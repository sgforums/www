    Isreal 2 - 1 Russia



    A goal in the second minute of stoppage time by Omer Golan gave Israel a 2-1 victory over Russia and threw England an amazing lifeline. This result means that Steve McClaren's side need only to draw against Croatia in their final qualifier on Wednesday to secure a Euro 2008 place at Russia's expense.

    England's reprieve was all down to the Israelis. They took the lead through Elyaniv Barda when Yitzhaki played a deft ball into the box and Barda produced a cool finish with the outside of his right foot to beat the Russian keeper.

    But Diniyar Bilyaletdinov equalised on the hour mark when Andrei Arshavin rolled the ball to him and he fied home.

    Then Russia hit the post late on, with England's hopes looking distinctly tenuous.

    But Golan rescued them as the game moved into stoppage time. Russia were caught napping in defence and he nipped in to beat first the offside trap and then goalkeeper Gabulov, to keep England very much alive.
