    John Terry turns karaoke king at wild Chelsea Christmas party

      

    Soul mates: Chelsea skipper John Terry (right) at the Funky Buddha nightclub, where he sang Luther Vandross number Never Too Much

    England captain John Terry committed Blue murder at his club’s Christmas party – claiming Luther Vandross, Biggie Smalls and Soulja Boy as his victims. We listened in disbelief as the karaoke killer bellowed out a hat-trick of tunes, then watched in horror as he joined boozing teammates throwing shapes for the girls at Chelsea’s riotous Christmas shindig.

    Natural born leader Terry showed his troops the way, grabbing the microphone for Luther’s classic Never Too Much and serenading the babes round the dancefloor.

    The footballers had banned their Wags but there was plenty of female attention. Scantily-clad girls queued for hours at Funky Buddha in London’s Mayfair to get up close with the Premiership stars — only to be left watching from the touchlines.

    But we infiltrated the tight security of bodyguards and Chelsea staff and our reporter Sarah Tetteh partied alongside Terry, Peter Cech, Wayne Bridge, Salomon Kalou and John Obi Mikel. Seemingly endless bottles of champagne and vodka mixers made their way to the VIP section.

    At the opposing end of the club stood rivals Aston Villa, who partied with Beckham — Joanne that is, who was dividing her time between Villa’s Zat Knight and Blue striker Nicholas Anelka.

    Nigel Reo-Coker was the life and soul of the party, the Villa captain’s dance moves being anything but sensible.

    As the party hotted up, the Chelsea stars took it in turns to trap girls who were walking past.

     

    Terry joked to one: “You want some of this?” before serenading the lucky lady with “You are my shinin’ star, my guiding light, my love fantasy”.

    Then, on his way to the loo, he gyrated his hips behind a stunning model while winking at her friend.
