    Manchester City forward Robinho has been questioned by police investigating an allegation of sexual assault.

    The Brazil international, who strenuously denies 'any allegation of wrongdoing', was held by police in relation to an alleged incident at a night club in Leeds.

    West Yorkshire Police said a man had been arrested on Tuesday in connection with the incident on 14 January.

    Robinho's spokesman Chris Nathaniel insisted: "He strenuously denies any allegation of wrongdoing or criminality and is happy to cooperate with the police if required further."

    Robinho, 25, is the most expensive signing in the history of English football, with City having shelled out £32.5 million for the former Real Madrid forward last August.
