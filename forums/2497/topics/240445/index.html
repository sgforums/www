Diarra content at Chelsea

  • turbo_drift
    3 Mar 07, 17:15
    Chelsea midfielder Lassana Diarra has expressed his content at having finally broken into Jose Mourinho's first team thoughts.

    Diarra has very much been on the periphery of Chelsea's first team since arriving in West London from Le Havre and has in the past spoken of his frustration with regards a lack of playing time.

    It had been suggested Paris Saint Germain were keen to take Diarra back to France, and the city of his berth.

    Diarra, though, rejects claims that he is hankering after a move to the French capital, instead reiterating his desire to secure a regular starting spot at Stamford Bridge.

    "There aren't any problems between me and the coach," Diarra told AFP.

    "I just wanted to repeat that I am happy here, that I want to play for Chelsea, that my future is here and that I am not looking to move elsewhere.

    "Even if Paris is my birth city and that I have always been interested in the results of the club, I have never expressed to anyone a desire to sign for PSG.

    "Both clubs might have discussed a loan move or something like that, I don't know.

    "I just want to play."
