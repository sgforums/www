    Mourinho rules out Lennon move

    Chelsea coach Jose Mourinho ruled out a move for winger Aaron Lennon after the youngster condemned his side to a 2-1 defeat at Tottenham.

    England international Lennon was linked with a move to Stamford Bridge in the days leading up to Sundays fixture, but Mourinho firmly quashed any further speculation over Chelseas interest in signing the 19-year-old.

    Lennon scored a superb second-half winner at White Hart Lane to earn Spurs their first league win over the Blues for 16 years.

    Mourinho declared: "No, completely not interested. The boy is good, but we have no interest in buying or changing anything. Our interest is to keep Shaun Wright-Phillips - who was supposedly involved in any deal. No way. Its not true."
