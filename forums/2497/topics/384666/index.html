    Bayern Munich set to land Arsenal defender Senderos

    Bayern Munich are set to land Arsenal defender Philippe Senderos in a £1 million deal.

    The Sunday Mirror says the German club were keen to sign the 24-year-old Swiss international six years ago but Senderos moved to Arsenal from Servette in a £500,000 move.

    Senderos left Arsenal for a year’s loan to AC Milan but came back to his parent club this summer.

    But he has again failed to establish himself in the first team and is likely to leave in the transfer window.
