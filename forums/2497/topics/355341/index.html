    LIVERPOOL midfielder Lucas claims his team-mates still have the belief to beat Chelsea over two legs.

    The Brazilian was part of the side that crumbled to a shock 3-1 defeat at Anfield last night. But the 22-year-old is adamant Rafa Benitez's men have already shown they can score away goals in key games this season.

    He said: "We know it will be really difficult but it's not impossible. "Chelsea came here and scored three and we have the quality to do the same there.

    "We will have to try until the very last minute at Stamford Bridge. I think we need the perfect performance. "It's a different game but we have shown already this season we can win 4-1 at places like Old Trafford."
