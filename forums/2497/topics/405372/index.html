    Liverpool Agree On £5m Fee For The Sale Of Emiliano Insua To Fiorentina - Report

    Emiliano Insua - Liverpool (PA)

    Liverpool are believed to have agreed on a £5 million fee for the sale of full-back Emiliano Insua to Fiorentina.

    The young Argentine was understood to have been a key target for the Italian club, with speculation suggesting that several offers were made before La Viola got close enough to Liverpool's valuation for a deal to be considered.

    Now The Daily Mail reports that the clubs have reached an agreement over the fee and that the 21-year-old is set to join the Serie A outfit in the next 48 hours.

    The potential transfer of the former Boca Juniors youth product would leave a question-mark over the left-back spot at Anfield, as Fabio Aurelio and Andrea Dossena have already made exits from Anfield.

    However, it has been reported in the past that manager Roy Hodgson has lined up Wigan Athletic's Maynor Figueroa as a replacement.
