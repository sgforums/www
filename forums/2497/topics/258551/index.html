Tevez open to Hammers stay

  • turbo_drift

    Carlos Tevez claims he is still in the dark over his future, but says he could well stay at West Ham.

    The Argentine almost single-handedly kept West Ham in the Premiership with his performances towards the end of last season.

    His future is a hot topic of debate, as a clutch of clubs are believed to be keen on securing his services.

    Hammers chairman Eggert Magnusson has made it his priority to keep Tevez at Upton Park and he is sure to be buoyed by comments from the Argentine.

    "Regarding what I am asked every day, I still don't know what I am going to do in the future," Tevez told in an interview. "West Ham has the priority for keeping me in the team.

    "It has been said that there are several offers for me but West ham has the first choice on me.

    "I speak all the time with my agent about what I will do in my career and for the moment I am still a player of West Ham."
