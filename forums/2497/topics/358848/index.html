    Manchester United winger Cristiano Ronaldo is planning to buy a red double-decker bus and plant it in his back garden for his nieces and nephews to play in.

    The 24-year-old is a fan of the iconic London transportation, and presumably gazes at them fondly when the Red Devils travel south to play matches in the capital.

    And the Portuguese speedster - who in January write off a £200,000 Ferrari on his way to training - is now set to purchase a bus and convert it into a playhouse in the garden of his £4 million home in Alderley Edge, Cheshire.

    “For me the sight of the red London buses is what my first thoughts of England were," he told the Portuguese press.

    “My nieces and nephews would love to play on one, so I’m going to buy one for them.”
