    Tottenham have signed veteran goalkeeper Carlo Cudicini on a free transfer from Chelsea as Harry Redknapp looks to strengthen his squad.

    Cudicini arrived at Spurs' training ground on Monday and he has quickly agreed a move to White Hart Lane.

    Redknapp admitted earlier this month he was keen on bringing in a new goalkeeper after allowing Cesar Sanchez to join Valencia and he has turned to Cudicini.

    Cudicini has seen his first-team opportunities at Stamford Bridge limited since the arrival of Petr Cech in 2004 and he has now decided to move on.

    Chelsea have granted the long-serving goalkeeper a free transfer as his existing contract was due to expire in the summer.
