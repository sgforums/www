    Barcelona agree massive fee for Shakhtar Donetsk defender Chygrynskiy

    Barcelona are set to land Shakhtar Donetsk defender Dmytro Chygrynskiy.

    Sport says Chygrynskiy will join Barca on Saturday after agreeing a five-year contract.

    The two clubs thrashed out terms yesterday over a fee worth €25 million. Aware of the effort made by the Treble champs to win his signature, Chygrynskiy has agreed to significantly reduce his personal demands.

    He will move to the Nou Camp on an initial deal worth €1.2 million-a-year.
