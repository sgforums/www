    ArsÃ¨ne Wenger last night stoked the simmering relationship between Arsenal and Chelsea by reminding Jose Mourinho that winning the Champions League with Porto in 2004 does not to make him a "great" manager. The London rivals meet in Sunday's Carling Cup final at the Millennium Stadium and Wenger has responded to Mourinho's jibe about Arsenal's record in Europe's elite club competition by launching a thinly veiled attack on the Portuguese.

What kind of quality have you brought through in your work and on how consistent a level have you done it?
