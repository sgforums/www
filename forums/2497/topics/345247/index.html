    MANCHESTER CITY last night launched a £19million raid for Sporting Lisbon star Miguel Veloso. 

    The Portugal midfielder, 22, has been a long-term target for Manchester United boss Alex Ferguson. 

    City are bidding to snatch the player from under their bitter rivals’ noses but the Eastlands club may have to match Veloso’s get-out clause of £28m. 

    A source close to Veloso said: “We know City have made an offer and the club are considering it. “It all depends on whether Sporting will demand City pay the get-out clause of the player.”
