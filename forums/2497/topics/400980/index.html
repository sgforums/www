    Arsenal have made an inquiry about the possibility of signing Chelsea midfielder Joe Cole but he has yet to make a decision on the move.

    Cole is a free agent but his wage demands of £120,000 a week have proved too much for Chelsea, Tottenham and Liverpool.

    Manchester United remain interested and Cole is expected to hold further talks this week.

    Meanwhile Chelsea are continuing to monitor £15m-rated Shakhtar Donetsk right-back Darijo Srna but are weighing up other priority positions first.
