    Here's one that I didn't find the video but just text;

    Kevin Doyle For Reading

    BEST TRAINER...
    James Harper, he never stops running. He's in early and always the last to leave.

     

    WORST TRAINER...
    Probably (Ibrahim) Sonko, he's been injured for a while to be fair so it's harsh, but in the circle of things he just doesn't like it, he just boots the ball away.

     

    QUICKEST PLAYER...
    There's a few quick one, I think Shane Long. In matches if there is a ball over the top you fancy him to beat any defender in the league.

     

    SLOWEST PLAYER...
    A few lads look like they've got parachutes on their back to be fair! (Dave Kitson) Kits i suppose, he's so tall it's tougher for him and a few of the central midfielders but yeah Kits.

     

    THE JOKER...
    James Harper, he takes the mickey out of everyone including the manager. Whoever is standing infront of him it doesn't matter and he's pretty funny to be fair but the new lads aren't sure what way to take him.

     

    MOST SKILFUL...
    Tugay, the Turkish maestro is unbeleievable and in training does things that amazes people so i'd say Tugay.

     

    BIGGEST HARDMAN...
    We haven't got anyone who goes around knocking people out, but hardest in the tackle would have to be Steve Gerrad again. He's our captain and i think everyone knows how competetive he is.

     

    MOST INTELLIGENT...
    The two Icelandics Ivar (Ingimarsson) and Brynjar (Gunnarsson) i think it's because they like to seem intelligent.

     

    WORST TASTE IN MUSIC...
    Hunty (Stepehn Hunt) he had Mariah Carey on his ipod a few weeks ago before a game, Heartbreaker or something like that...shocking.

     

    WORST DRESS SENSE...
    Leroy (Lita) has got a bright orange tracksuit which one of tha lads wore at training one day, so i'll give it to Leroy for that.

     

    BEST MOANER...
    Bobby Convey i think, the two American Marcus Hahnemann as well, the two of them like a moan but i think Bobby just pips Marcus.

     

    AND...WHO'S THE LONGEST IN THE SHOWER...
    There's a number of contenders for that, i think Leroy...he's a tripod!

    Here's one for the long-time Liverpool fans

    Bruce Grobbelaa for Liverpool

    BEST TRAINER
    Craig Johnstone. We just called him Skippy because he ran and ran. Of course ex-pats went over to Australia and shot kangaroos, which is probably why he ran so much!

     

    WORST TRAINER
    Sorry, Jan (Molby)...

     

    THE JOKER
    In the Liverpool pack there were probably about five or six of us. The biggest one was probably Alan Hansen, but there was Ian Rush and Ronnie Whelan... you know who you are!

     

    MOST INTELLIGENT
    There was a lot of people who thought they were intelligent... Mr Alan Hansen!

     

    LEAST INTELLIGENT
    There were a few of those, but I think you're looking at Steve Nicol, my old room-mate. He was one sandwich short of a picnic.

     

    WORST TASTE IN MUSIC
    The Liverpool team! The only good part about that Cup Final song was John Barnes - he could rap. I think the only people that know the words to that song by heart are my daughters!

     

    WORST DRESS SENSE
    It was me!

     

    LONGEST IN THE SHOWER
    You mean literally? Who was out of the dressing room last, the one that wanted to get himself looking all nice? John Barnes!
