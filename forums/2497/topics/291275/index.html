    Lions coach Radojko Avramovic has named a 20-man-squad for the second leg of the 2010 FIFA World Cup Asian Zone Qualifying Tournament Second Round tie against Tajikistan, scheduled for Sunday 18 November at the Central Stadium in Dushanbe.

    Singapore hold a 2-0 advantage from the first leg played at the National Stadium on 9 November, thanks to a brace from Aleksandar Duric on his international debut

    Young Lions midfielder Hariss Harun, who will turn 17 the day after the scheduled match, has been called up into the squad. He is in line to earn his first cap outside of Singapore if he features on Sunday. His three previous caps were all earned at home against DPR Korea, Saudi Arabia and Australia respectively.

    The squad will depart for Dushanbe via Istanbul on Tuesday 13 November and will return to Singapore early next Monday.
