Jay-Jay Okocha To Retire In June

  • omgukilledkenny
    5 Mar 08, 22:20

    Hull City Midfielder Jay-Jay Okocha has expressed his desire to retire at the end of the current season.

    The Nigerian player arrived at the Football League Championship side in September 2007 after playing in the Middle East for Qatar Sports Club for a year.

    The former Bolton player, two-times winner of the BBC African footballer of the year, has revealed that he will leave the Tigers in the summer to focus on a non-football career.

    "I don't think I will go into coaching for now, I would like to try some other things, there are a lot of things to do and I will try to start a new life, a new challenge and I'm looking forward to it," Okocha told BBC.

    Okocha has made eighteen official appearances for Hull City, eleven of which in the starting line-up, having yet to score for the Kingston upon Hull side.
