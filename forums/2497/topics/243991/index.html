    FURIOUS Jose Mourinho last night claimed everyone is "against Chelsea" after they were left with a nightmare fixture pile-up - but insisted they can still pull off a remarkable quadruple. Chelsea boss Mourinho faces a run of three games in eight days at the end of the season which includes their Premiership title showdown with Manchester United on Wednesday May 9. And they will also have to travel to Arsenal on May 6 and play Everton on Sunday May 13 as Premiership bosses will not extend the season.

    But paranoid Mourinho said: "Everything in this league went against us from the beginning, injuries, decisions, everything. We started the season without Petr Cech, after that we lost Joe Cole, then John Terry and then Michael Essien. But when we do play with a team close to our best, we show what we can do.

    "The quadruple is still possible. We have a 25 per cent chance to win the FA Cup, we have a 12.5 per cent chance to win the Champions League. "To win the Premiership, we need to win every game and Man United to lose four more points - if we could play them three times it would help."
