    The Blaugrana are ready to enter the Italian transfer market with two Romanian stars as their main targets.



    Barcelona have decided to get involved in the Italian transfer market. Objectives are Romanian stars Christian Chivu and Adrian Mutu, particularly the latter, and the double Spanish champions have plenty of money to spend.

    To Fiorentina the offer for Mutu, a player Rijkaard appreciates very much, will be of 15 million euros. It was the Dutch coach himself who indicated that the ex-Bianconeri striker could be the first reinforcement for the Barcelona of the future.

    For the Roma defender they are also ready to join the race, although they will have to beat stiff competition from Inter. Fiorentina will evaluate the offer carefully, but they will be aware that after losing Luca Toni, letting go of another star player might be a big loss for coach Cesare Prandelli.

    The Viola have also offered a three-year contract to Ludovic Giuly, also of Barcelona. In the next few days there will be the answer from the player.
