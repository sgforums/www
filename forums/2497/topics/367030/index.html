    Ronaldo ceremony as big as Real Madrid v Barcelona

    Real Madrid are expecting a full house tonight for Cristiano Ronaldo's signing ceremony.

    Taken aback by the 50,000-plus that attended Kaka's presentation a week ago, Real have doubled their staff at the Santiago Bernabeu for tonight's event.

    It's not just Madrid locals who will attend in massive numbers, there are also fans from Lisbon and the Algarve due at the Santiago to see Alfredo di Stefano present Ronaldo with his old No9 shirt. Full flights from Ronaldo's Madeira are also booked to arrive during the day, leaving Real officials convinced of a crowd to match any game against Barcelona.
