    Real Madrid tell Arsenal: €35M for Higuain


    Arsenal have registered their interest with Real Madrid for striker Gonzalo Higuain.

    Marca says Gunners boss Arsene Wenger sees Higuain as an ideal strike-partner for Robin van Persie and intends to move for the Argentina international in the summer - if not sooner this month.

    Real have informed Arsenal they want to keep Higuain, though would be prepared to sell him for a price in the region of €35 million.

    Gunners captain Cesc Fabregas could come into play, with Real and Barcelona both chasing the midfielder for next season.
