    JAMIE CARRAGHER reckons the Germans have an advantage because they have used the World Cup ball for five months.

    The Jabulani was made by German firm adidas and used in the Bundesliga since February. 

    After watching Joachim Low's side beat Australia 4-0, Carra claimed the experience gives them an edge.

    The Premier League has a contract with Nike and Carra said: "It gives them an advantage, that's obvious. The ball is so inconsistent you don't know what it's going to do.

    "It makes it difficult which is the same for every team except Germany."

    But he thought Low's side were superb, especially hitman Ozil Mesut.

    He said: "It was impressive. I thought Ozil was outstanding."
