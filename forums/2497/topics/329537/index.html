    Peter Kenyon is on the defensive after failing to sign Robinho.

    Robinho has admitted Real Madrid were never going to do business with Chelsea after their staggeringly arrogant shirt screw-up, but Kenyon has now claimed the player is "not worth 40 million euros."

    Remember what happened the last time Kenyon failed to sign a Brazilian superstar?

    That's right, straight after screwing up Ronaldinho's move to Manchester United, Kenyon took up a massive offer to join Chelsea, then flush with new investment and promising to transform the English game.

    After this latest failure though, you can't see Abu Dhabi squillionaires being quite as keen on offering Kenyon a job...
