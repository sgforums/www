    Harry wants Taylor stay

    Harry Redknapp has rejected reports linking Matt Taylor with a move away from Portsmouth.

    Reports over the weekend suggested a number of clubs were eyeing the versatile performer with Liverpool and Aston Villa credited with an interest.

    Taylor has established himself as a key player at Portsmouth and finished as their second-highest scorer with eight goals last term.

    Redknapp is determined to keep the 26-year-old as he looks to build a strong squad at Fratton Park.

    "I want to keep Matt, and as far as I am concerned, he is going nowhere," Redknapp told the Daily Star.

    "I am trying to strengthen my squad not weaken it."
