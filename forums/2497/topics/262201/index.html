Robinho wants Kaka at Real

  • turbo_drift
    20 Jun 07, 08:54
    Robinho has called on international team-mate Kaka to join the Brazilian contingent at Real Madrid.

    Kaka continues to be linked with a move away from Milan over the summer, despite the European champions' insistence that the playmaker will not be sold.

    Real are known to be keen on Kaka's signing ahead of next season and the Brazilian schemer's father recently met with officials from the Spanish club.

    Robinho would love Kaka to head to the Bernabeu and join the likes of Cicinho, Marcelo and Emerson at the Liga champions.

    "To have the chance to play with a star like Kaka would be something wonderful," Robinho said after arriving ahead of the Copa America.

    "We have a very good understanding on and off the pitch so it would be great to have another Brazilian with us at Real Madrid."

    Kaka is widely regarded as currently the best player in the world after helping Milan to UEFA Champions League glory in the past season.

    Robinho one day hopes to follow in Kaka's footsteps, but for now would settle for playing alongside the world's best player.

    He added: "It's a dream to be the best player in the world like Kaka is and it would be something great for me to have the chance to play next to the best player in the world.

    "I hope that someday I can be in his position. I am going to work a lot at Real Madrid and with the national team. I hope that in the future I can also be considered one of the best players in the world."
