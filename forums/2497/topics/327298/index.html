Injury-hit Gunners set for new face

  • omgukilledkenny
    13 Aug 08, 22:01

    Arsene Wenger has revealed Arsenal could make a new signing this week.

    The Gunners are experiencing an early-season injury crisis and ahead of Wednesday’s Champions League qualifier against Twente, Wenger has been hit hardest in midfield.

    “It’s the most problematic start to a season numbers wise,” said Wenger.

    “It’s a lot of responsibility for the young player because money is an issue. It is the biggest game of the season because if you lose a Premier League game you can still get back. Lose a Champions League qualifier and it’s a big deal for us."

    Wenger maintains that he needs to sign at least one more new player, with reports claiming Udinese midfielder Gokhan Inler is a target.

    “If you consider all that, you can see our squad is so short. I have said many times we are short of one player and I hope I will be capable of doing something this week or before the transfer deadline.”
