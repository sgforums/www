    O'Hara signs for Spurs - and Pompey

    Jamie O'Hara signed a new contract at Tottenham Hotspur - then promptly joined Portsmouth on loan.

     

    O'Hara, who has commited himself to Spurs until 2013, has been at White Hart Lane since 2005 and has spent short loan spells at Chesterfield and Millwall.

    He joins Pompey on loan until January, with an option to extend it until the end of the season.

    "I'm always going to give 100 per cent and hope I'll fit in straightaway and get to know the lads quite quickly."
