    Italian jobs! Chelsea face Napoli with Arsenal up against Milan in Champions League

     

     

     

      

    Crunch time: Arsene Wenger and Andre Villas-Boas will looking to steer their respective Arsenal and Chelsea sides through to the quarter-final stage

     

     

     

    Arsenal and Chelsea face tough Italian jobs to stay in the Champions League after being drawn against Serie A giants AC Milan and Napoli.

    Andre Villas-Boas' Chelsea side were paired with Manchester City's group stage conquerors Napoli in the last 16 of the Champions League.

    Arsene Wenger's Gunners were handed the draw all the top seeds wanted to avoid, facing a difficult visit to the San Siro next February.

    French outfit Lyon were the first team out of the draw, landing a plum tie against Cypriot side APOEL.

    The opening round of first leg ties will be played on February 14/15 with the remainder following a week later on February 21/22.

    The second leg clashes will be played on March 6/7 with a host of other fixtures concluding on March 13/14.
