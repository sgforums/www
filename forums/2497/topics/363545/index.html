    Â 

    Italian football seems to be on the decline recently.Kaka has left.Now there is talk of even Pirlo and Pato leaving.The top players nowadays seem to be attracted only to the English and Spanish leagues.

    This state of affairs seems to weaken Italian football in the short run but it may be a blessing in disguise in the long run. With less foreign players coming,Italian clubs have no choice but to cultivate their own indigenous players.

    The fundamentals of Italian football are still sound-Italians are still excellent at what are the basics of The Game-playing football.

    As long as the fundamentals are sound-Italian football will experience a glorious renaissance in the near future.

    This sounds like a unorthdox viewpoint at this moment in time,but the history of football parallels the history of humanity-it is also cyclical.
