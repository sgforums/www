Drogba: Injuries cost us

  • turbo_drift
    10 Jun 07, 11:52
    Didier Drogba believes a spate of injuries severely cost Chelsea the chance to retain their title last season.

    The Blues had won back-to-back Premiership trophies prior to finishing second this time around.

    Jose Mourinho was robbed of the likes of Joe and Ashley Cole, Petr Cech, Arjen Robben and John Terry for periods of the season due to injury.

    With the likes of Cech and Joe Cole unavailable over Christmas, Chelsea drew at home to Reading and Fulham, dropped points Drogba believes were crucial.

    "Chelsea lost the title when we drew with Fulham and Reading," he told the club's season review DVD.

    "That was the period when we had all those injuries.

    "Our fantastic players had shown every quality but, unfortunately, there were so many injuries to cope with."
