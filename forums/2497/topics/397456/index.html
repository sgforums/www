    Report: Portsmouth Send Fax To Premier League Clubs Stating Entire Squad Is For Sale For £30m

    FA Cup: Aruna Dindane - Frederic Piquionne - Portsmouth - (Getty Images)

    Portsmouth has sent a fax to Premier League clubs, declaring that the entirety of their squad is for sale, reports The Mirror.

    Football agency Icon Sports Management have been brought in to sell the entirety of the Pompey squad, and the fax was accompanied by the players asking price.

    The club is reportedly demanding a combined total of just under £30 million, with midfielder Kevin-Prince Boateng the highest valued player at £5 million.

    Other notable names include England goalkeeper David James, who is listed as a free transfer, although the South Coast club are believed to want as much as £3 million for defender Tal Ben Haim – desire having signed the Israeli for nothing, in a free transfer deal with Manchester City.
