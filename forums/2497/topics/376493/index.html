    Man Utd coach confirms market search for Van der Sar replacement

    Manchester United goalkeeper coach Eric Steele admits they're scouting for new goalkeeper talent.

    He told the Manchester Evening News: "At this football club what would you expect other than that we will always be looking.

    "The manager wants the best available squad and it is my job in the goalkeeping department to make sure we have got potential targets that I have checked out.

    "The manager will also have checked them out. That is what this club is all about. You have to be ready for every eventuality.

    "We have had the Russian goalkeeper (Igor Akinfeev) linked and we've had all sorts linked with us. You will never fail to have that. This football club will always be `in' for such and such a player according to the press and speculation.

    "It is always going to happen and this season it has happened because they will have seen Edwin out after his operation and if Ben had had an injury we would be thin on the ground.

    "We would be very foolish people if we were not prepared. That is the same in every position.

    "It is no different now from 12 months ago. You work with the keepers you have, but you also have to know what else is around. You have to have that in the back of your mind. It is the only sensible way to do it."

    Man Utd keeper Van der Sar: Retirement decision made in December

    Manchester United goalkeeper Edwin van der Sar says he'll decide in December if this will be his final season.

    "I've said for a few years that it sometimes may be my last season," Van der Sar told Sportsweek. "Six months before my contract with Manchester United ended in December 2006, we extended for one year and so far it has been the same every time.

    "I wait each season to see how where I stand, if everyone is happy and if I can still get motivated. But I know, because I always get older, it is increasingly likely that this will be my last season."

    He added: ""In December, it will be clear how it goes. By then, I'll have clarity. It is a horrible dilemma. On the one hand I do not oversleep, I feel as good as a few years ago."
