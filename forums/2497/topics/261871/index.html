Giuly Blasts Henry Report

  • omgukilledkenny
    18 Jun 07, 20:21
    Barcelona star Ludovic Giuly has blasted a report that claimed Thierry Henry has contacted him to discuss a move to the Catalan club.



    Giuly was reported to have been contacted by his fellow Frenchman about moving to the Catalan club and asked to find him a house, but he was adamant that the report had no truth.

    Giuly said: "Thierry didn't call me to talk about his transfer to Barcelona.

    "France Football talk bullsh!t.

    "He hasn't asked me to find him a house. I am not an estate agent and I think he is old enough to phone an agent on his own."

    Giuly has himself been linked with a move away from the Nou Camp this summer.
