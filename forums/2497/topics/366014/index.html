    Manchester City Eye Arjen Robben

    The City revolution seems set to continue with the capture of the Dutch winger...

     

     

    Real Madrid winger Arjen Robben could be on his way back to the Premier League with Manchester City keen to snare the flying Dutchman, according to The People.

    City are currently in talks to sign strikers Samuel Eto'o and Carlos Tevez, both of whom will surely greatly enhance the quality of the team if they are purchased, and now Robben is also thought to be on their radar.

    City appear to be in the middle of a revolution at the moment, with the Abu Dhabi United Group providing millions in transfer funds, and manager Mark Hughes is set to spend big this summer.

    They have already bought Gareth Barry from Aston Villa and are now looking to sure up their defensive and attacking options in time for the new season.

    Robben has been told by Madrid that he can leave the club, with the new era of Galacticos currently under way at Santiago Bernabeu.

    The 25-year-old is thought to be valued at around £21 million, with Madrid needing to make money on the players they sell, after they spent enourmous sums on the likes of Kaka and Cristiano Ronaldo, and they have stated that a lot of their squad players are available to buy.

    The former Chelsea winger is, apparently, willing to leave the club, fearing first team opportunities will be severely limited next season.
