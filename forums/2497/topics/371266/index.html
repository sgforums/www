    CHEL HELL ... for their former boss Mourinho

     

     

    JOSE MOURINHO claims Chelsea are ramping up the prices of his targets Deco and Ricardo Carvalho to spite him. 

    The Inter Milan boss says Deco had been available for next to nothing earlier this year to anyone else - until he started making enquiries. 

    Former Blues chief Mourinho said: "I feel it's very difficult for a Chelsea player to come to me. 

    "Every time I'm interested in one of their players, it looks different. 

    "In the summer we were told he was almost free to come. Two weeks later, when they know Inter are interested, it goes from zero to £5million. 

    "And for Carvalho it's gone from £5m to £15m - something like that." 

    Both men played under Mourinho at Porto, while centre-half Carvalho was one of his compatriot's first signings at Chelsea. 

    Carvalho, 31, said he wants to rejoin Mourinho but Carlo Ancelotti wants him to stay at the Bridge.
