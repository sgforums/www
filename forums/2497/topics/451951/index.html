    Revenge is a dish best served cold, or in Didier Zokora's case, a wild kick to your opponent's crown jewels.

    The former Tottenham midfielder incredibly escaped with only a caution after booting Emre up into the air – a revenge tackle a certain Roy Keane would have been pleased with.   

    Emre, a former Newcastle star, was slapped with a two-game ban earlier this season for using offensive language towards Zokora, with the former Spurs man claiming it had been racist.

    Although those allegations were unproven, Zokora took justice into his own hands on Sunday night by refusing to shake Emre's hand before Trabzonspor's home clash with Fenerbahce and then promptly giving him a right good kicking right where it hurts.

    Emre took to the turf in agony, but the Turkey international came out on top in the end with Fenerbahce winning 3-1.
