    Chelsea star Juliano Belletti set for 'surprise' exit as Brazilian champions Flamengo make move

    Flamengo hope to clinch a deal to sign Juliano Belletti from Chelsea before the end of the week.

    Heading off: Juliano Belletti is set to join Flamengo

    The 33-year-old is out of contract at the end of the season and the Brazilian champions want him on a free transfer for the start of the Copa Libertadores - South America's equivalent of the Champions League - which they kick off next month.

    Flamengo will offer Belletti a two-year deal.

    The versatile Brazil international has played a bit-part this season but has proved useful while the likes of John Mikel Obi and Michael Essien have been on Africa Cup of Nations duty.

    Flamengo are confident of a deal, but may have to wait till the end of the month, and Belletti last night warned: 'The offer is surprising to me because Chelsea have not said they will release me in January.'
