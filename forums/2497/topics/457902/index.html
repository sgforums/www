    Reds face Hiddink’s big spenders

    Liverpool to play mega-rich Anzhi in Europa League

    INTO THE UNKNOWN ... Brendan Rodgers' Liverpool face European debutants Anzhi

     

     

    LIVERPOOL have been drawn against Guus Hiddink’s mega-rich Anzhi Makhachkala in the Europa League group stage.

    The Russians, who boast former Barcelona star Samuel Eto'o in their ranks, are the fourth seeds in a tough Group A which also includes Serie A side Udinese and Young Boys from Switzerland.

    Newcastle’s return to European football sees them lock horns with French side Bordeaux, Club Brugge of Belgium and Portugal’s Maritimo in Group D.

    While Tottenham will face Greek side Panthinaikos as well as Italian giants Lazio and Maribor from Slovenia in Group J.
