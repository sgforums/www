    Tottenham boss Redknapp fires top four warning at Arsenal

    Tottenham boss Harry Redknapp is convinced they can shock Arsenal and dump them out of the top four this season.

    “I think we have a good strong and balanced squad and I will be extremely disappointed if we don’t achieve a top six finish,” Redknapp told the Sunday Mirror.

    “Ultimately our goal in to break into the top four and while I accept that is a big ask it won’t be for the lack of trying.

    “I expect Manchester United, Chelsea and Liverpool to battle it out for the title but if there is a weakness among the four teams you would have to say Arsenal are probably the most vulnerable.

    “Manchester City will definitely surprise a few people this season. I realise it’s difficult to gel so many players together in a short space of time but it’s achievable.

    “Mark Hughes has spent well in the transfer market and they are going to cause a few teams problems.

    “You would also expect Everton and Aston Villa to push on so teams at the top are not going to get everything their own way.”
