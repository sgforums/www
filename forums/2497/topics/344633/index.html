    Portuguese footballer Cristiano Ronaldo's car problems do not seem to end, with him getting a ticket recently for parking at a bus stop for two hours.

    Just last week, the Manchester United ace survived a crash in his Ferrari, and this time he left his Bentley GT Speed at a bus stop while he went for lunch with his family.

    Ronaldo, 23, was at the Gusto restaurant in Alderley Edge, Cheshire, when he was served with the ticket.

    "I saw at least one bus struggling to let people on and off," the Daily Star quoted onlooker Colin Ferguson, 29, as saying.

    "It's a disgrace leaving it for two hours. How is a parking fine going to affect someone on his pay? If they wanted to teach him a lesson they should have towed his car away," he added.
