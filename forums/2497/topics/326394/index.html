Terry warning for United

  • zocoss
    5 Aug 08, 18:39

     

    JOHN TERRY has warned that Chelsea are ready to snatch the title back from Manchester United. The Blues lost out to their arch rivals in the race for the Premier League crown last season. But new boss Phil Scolari has reinforced his squad this summer as he prepares to go head-to-head with United chief Alex Ferguson.

    Terry has now revealed he has spoken with team-mate Frank Lampard over their desperation to bring the title back to Stamford Bridge. And the Chelsea skipper reckons that while United have already hit their best form, his side still have room for improvements.

    The defender said: "We've had talks and the Premier League is the one we're after — we want to get that back. "And over the course of the season, to show that consistency and get that back off Man Utd would be fantastic. "They have had two good years and you have to stand back and take your hat off to them but we know we were good last year and we have another level to go to.

    "Last year I think we saw the very best of Man Utd but we have got an awful lot to come from last year. "We have got improvements to make but I think Man Utd are at their peak. "The Premier League is the one we want and when the Champions League starts again, it's going to be exciting."
