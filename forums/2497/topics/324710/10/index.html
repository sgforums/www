    Ronaldinho Shines In Brazil Win

    Ronaldinho scored one and also provided a beautiful back-heeled assist as Brazil began its preparations for the Beijing Olympics with a 3-0 win over Singapore.

    The Selecao travel to Beijing as one of the favourites for the tournament, along with Argentina, Italy and the Netherlands. As part of their preparations Dunga’s men took on Singapore at the National Stadium, and ran out comfortable 3-0 winners.

    New Milan signing Ronaldinho played the entire 90 minutes, and showed that his talent has not left him by setting up the first goal on 20 minutes for Diego with a delightful flick, the Werder Bremen star rifling home from the edge of the box.

    Ronaldinho then doubled Brazil’s advantage seven minutes later, as Diego's chipped ball caused problems in the Singapore defence, allowing the former Barcelona man to score from close range.

    Singapore improved after the break, and went close on occasions, however Brazil substitute Jo added gloss to the scoreline by heading home a third on 84 minutes.

    Brazil were reduced to ten men in injury time ad defender Alex Silva saw red for violent conduct. The Selecao will play another friendly on August 1 in Hanoi against Vietnam.
