    Carlo Ancelotti's departure from AC Milan in the summer looks even more certain after his latest retort to journalists in Italy.

    Ancelotti has repeatedly declared his happiness at the San Siro but after recent rumblings from the Rossoneri hierarchy over the team's performances in the league the 49-year-old's future has been cast into doubt.

    "My future will be decided over the next few days. We have no meetings on the agenda. Now we will focus on this game, the rest is just chatter," he said.

    "What are my plans for next week? That's nobody's business but my own. All I can say is that on Tuesday I will be in Bari."
