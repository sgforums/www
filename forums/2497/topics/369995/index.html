    Vidic told to forget leaving Man Utd

    Manchester United boss Sir Alex Ferguson has ruled out selling defender Nemanja Vidic.

    The People says Vidic will fly out to join the Manchester United squad in Germany today and will be told by Ferguson that he will not be allowed to leave the club.

    Ferguson will tell Vidic there is no chance he will be sold despite continued interest from AC and Inter Milan, who believe Vidic could seek a move because his wife is having trouble adjusting to life in England.

    Vidic was left out of United's Asia tour, with injury reasons cited, but he was also being given extra time to spend with his family.

    United have looked around for a potential replacement in case the Vidic affair became a crisis, but no-one came close to filling the big Serb's boots.
