    'VILLA ONLY WANTS LIVERPOOL'

     

    Brief line in The Sun this morning, who claim that David Villa will only come to England if it is to sign for Liverpool.

    The paper claims Villa has told 'close friends' that he would 'only leave Spain to link up with fellow countryment Rafa Benitez and Fernando Torres'.

    However, that probably isn't going to happen, given that Liverpool barely have the money for new shoes, let alone someone like Villa, who looks far more likely to join either Barcelona or Real Madrid.

    Villa will only move to England for Benitez's Liverpool

    Valencia ace David Villa would only move to England to play for Rafa Benitez's Liverpool.

    The £40million-rated Valencia hitman is being chased by Chelsea, Manchester United, Barcelona and Real Madrid.

    Yet Villa has told close friends he would only leave Spain to link up with fellow countrymen Rafa Benitez and Fernando Torres at Anfield, says The Sun.

    Amazingly, Liverpool boss Benitez is not even in the running for Villa.
