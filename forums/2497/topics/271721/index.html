    Bojinov revealed he had spoken to fellow countryman Martin Petrov about the proposed move a number of times. Petrov snubbed Tottenham to join Eriksson's City revolution and is due to make his debut in a friendly against Shrewsbury tonight.

    "I am very pleased I will have the chance to play alongside Martin as we have done in the national team," said Bojinov. "I have already talked to him several times about the situation."
