    Man Utd offered chance to buy back Villarreal star Rossi


    Manchester United could buy back Giuseppe Rossi from Villarreal.

    The Daily Star Sunday says United sold Rossi to the Spanish club for £6.7m three years ago – but manager Sir Alex Ferguson insisted on a clause being inserted in the deal which gave him first option on buying back the American-born Italy international.

    And a number of clubs are now eyeing Rossi, 23, with German club Hoffenheim leading the chase.

    There is also expected to be interest in Turkey and from the Premier League and, if Villarreal receive an acceptable offer, they would then have to offer him back to United.
