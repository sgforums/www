    Yaya Toure agent tells Arsenal, Man City, Chelsea to make them offer

    The agent of Barcelona midfielder Yaya Toure has told Chelsea, Manchester City and Arsenal to make them an offer.

    The midfielder, 26, younger brother of City's Kolo, has failed to nail down a regular spot in Barca's first-team this season and could be on his way for £10million.

    His agent Dimitri Seluk told The Sun: "The situation must change soon or the kid leaves Barcelona in January.

    "Yaya is not for the bench and this situation isn't helpful for them or us.

    "We do not understand Barca's motives and if a club like Arsenal, Chelsea or Manchester City are interested in the transfer window, then we are open to negotiation."
