    JOSE REYES insists he has no intention of returning to Arsenal at the end of the season. Reyes joined Real Madrid on loan in August in a straight swap for Julio Baptista. The 23-year-old is currently recovering from a knee injury but is keen to ensure he stays in Spain with Baptista remaining in London.

    Reyes said: "I've had a lot at stake since I first joined the team. "The end of the season is just around the corner, so I have to prove my worth. "But I am not worried. I know the club have faith in me and vice-versa. "I always wanted to play here and I want to stay."
