Fulham let Radzinski go

  • turbo_drift
    20 May 07, 09:01
    Fulham have released Tomasz Radzinski, while on-loan striker Vincenzo Montella has gone back to Roma.

    Following his permanent appointment as Fulham manager, Lawrie Sanchez has made decisions on the future of his out-of-contract players.

    Sanchez has allowed Radzinski to leave Craven Cottage, after the Canadian attacker rejected an appearance-based contract midway through the season.

    No further offer was made to Radzinski, who scored twice in 35 Premiership appearances during Fulham's battle to avoid relegation.

    Claus Jensen was offered a similar deal to Radzinski and has also left the club after criticising Sanchez's tactics earlier in the week.

    Montella has headed back to Roma, while fellow loan signings Wayne Routledge and Jan Lastuvka have returned to Tottenham and Shakhtar Donetsk respectively.

    Veteran duo Mark Crossley and Mark Pembridge have also not been offered new contracts by Sanchez.
