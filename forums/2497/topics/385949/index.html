    Ancelotti: England Have Six World Class Players

    'Carletto' believes Three Lions have a stellar six.

    Premier League : Carlo Ancelotti (Chelsea)

    Chelsea manager Carlo Ancelotti believes that six of England's best could make it into the line-up of any country in the world.

    The Italian insists that his own trio of John Terry, Frank Lampard and Ashley Cole, along with Liverpool skipper Steven Gerrard, and Manchester United's Wayne Rooney and Rio Ferdinand would be welcomed into any other side.

    "I think England have six top players," Ancelotti is quoted by The Times as saying.

    "There's my three - Lampard, Terry and Ashley Cole.

    "Then there's Rio Ferdinand, who's not having a good time of it now, but who is a great defender.

    "Then Gerrard, and I’d put Rooney in too.

    "Maybe Spain [have as many] but Brazil definitely don't have six of them and neither do Italy."
