Get Real, Stevie won't leave Kop

  • limpper
    16 Mar 09, 00:37

     

     

    FERNANDO TORRES insists Steven Gerrard would never leave Liverpool for Real Madrid.

     

    The England midfielder has been linked with a summer switch to the Bernabeu after his sensational performance against Real in midweek.

    But Torres, who played for Real's rivals Atletico Madrid, reckons Gerrard is going nowhere.

    He said: “There isn't a team in the world that Steven wouldn't get into - so yes, I am sure he has his admirers.

    “But there are some players who love their club so much that they won't leave no matter what money is thrown around.

    “We saw Kaka stay at AC Milan and that was nice to see in these times.

    “Like Stevie, Kaka is one of the best players and in the same way he loves Milan, Stevie loves Liverpool.

    “It's not about how we would get him to stay, he would never leave.

    “The fans love him, the manager loves him, and he is our captain. Trust me when I say he is going nowhere.”
