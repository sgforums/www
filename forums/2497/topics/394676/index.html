    Rafa Benitez fears Liverpool could lose Steven Gerrard and co if Reds miss out on top four

     

    Exit fears: Benitez is worried Liverpool could lose their best players this summer

     

     

    Rafa Benitez fears he will lose his best players this summer if Liverpool fail to finish in the top four and qualify for next season's Champions League.

    The under pressure Spaniard believes his side's final league place this term will have a significant bearing on his transfer options in the summer but could also see the likes of Fernando Torres, Javier Mascherano and captain Steven Gerrard up for grabs.

    'Players want to win trophies and want to finish in the top four,' said Benitez. 'This club is a massive club so if we can progress in the Europa League and finish in the top four a lot of [other] players will be thinking whether to come here or stay where they are.'

    Liverpool are sixth in the Barclays Premier League, four points behind fourth-placed Tottenham having played a match more, having slipped to ten defeats this term.

    But Benitez, who has already agreed a pre-contract deal with Standard Liege forward
    Milan Jovanovic, believes the incentive to finish fourth and win the Europa League
    applies not only to his players but any prospective new signings.

     

     

    May the fourth be with you: Benitez wants to keep hold of Gerrard and Torres but admits Liverpool must finish fourth and qualify for next term's Champions League

     

     

    'The players know it is important to perform now for the future and they want to stay here so I think it will be okay. 'We are now in a situation where we have to move forward and try to bring the best from each player. 

    'It is not easy to stay at a very good level in a season. The main thing is we have some players coming back from injuries and if they are together I'm sure we'll do really well.'

    Liverpool host Sunderland on Sunday and go into a Europa League quarter-final against Benfica next week hoping there is still a chance of winning some silverware.
