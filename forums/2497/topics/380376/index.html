    CRISTIANO RONALDO is on course to retain his crown as FIFA's World Player of the Year.

    The Real Madrid ace, who won the award last season for his stunning form with Manchester United, has been named on a 23-man shortlist for this term's accolade.

    And Ronaldo, 24, heads a familiar cast of the beautiful game's leading talent.

    Spain provide six nominees — goalkeeper Iker Casillas, defender Carles Puyol, midfielders Andres Iniesta and Xavi and strikers Fernando Torres and David Villa.

    England's Frank Lampard, Steven Gerrard, Wayne Rooney and John Terry also make the list.
