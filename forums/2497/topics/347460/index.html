'Frustrated' Fabianski won't want away

  • omgukilledkenny
    11 Feb 09, 01:40

    Lukasz Fabianski insists he does not regret his decision to join Arsenal, despite admitting that it is frustrating playing second fiddle to Manuel Almunia.

    The 23-year-old Poland international joined The Gunners from Legia Warsaw in 2007 but he has made only a handful of first-team appearances for Arsene Wenger.

    Despite the lack of opportunities, though, Fabianski insists he has been given great encouragement by the Arsenal coaching staff.

    "I'm not thinking too much about my future," Fabianski told onet.pl. "In football, things can change quickly.

    "I hear all the time that the coaches are pleased with me so I will be patient.

    "Of course the situation is frustrating, but I have been offered advice from good people and I think this will allow me to cope with the situation.

    "It seems that the decision to join [Arsena] was the correct one."
