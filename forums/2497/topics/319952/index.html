Real Madrid Track Lichtsteiner

  • omgukilledkenny
    5 Jun 08, 21:16

    Real Madrid are apparently following the progress of Lille OSC right-back Stephan Lichtsteiner.

    With just one year left on his contract, the Swiss ace could leave Lille for a comparatively low fee in summer.

    Real Madrid's technical staff are thought to be eager to seek cover for Sergio Ramos at right-back, and the 24-year-old fits the bill in that regard.

    For now, though, he has other things on his mind.

    He told L'Equipe, "I am very proud and honoured that a club like Real is interested in me.

    "But my priority is Euro 2008. I'll let my brother [Marco, his agent] deal with things like that."

    The former Grasshopper-Club stopper is said to be unwilling to extend his deal any further with les Dogues.
