    Fixtures for 2010 FIFA World Cup Asian Zone qualifying tournament third round announced

    Singapore will kick off their 2010 FIFA World Cup Asian Zone qualifying tournament third round campaign against 2007 AFC Asian Cup runners-up Saudi Arabia on 6 February 2008.

    The Lions will host Lebanon in their second Group 4 match on 26 March before welcoming a busy month of June that sees them playing four matches in the space of three weeks.

    On 2 June, Singapore will host Uzbekistan before traveling to Tashkent for the away fixture five days later.

    The Lions welcome Saudi Arabia on 14 June before completing their Group 4 commitments with a road game against Lebanon on 22 June.
