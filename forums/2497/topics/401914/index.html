    Guardiola green light for Barcelona to sell Ibrahimovic


    Barcelona coach Pep Guardiola has given the green light to the sale of Zlatan Ibrahimovic.

    Marca says Guardiola has decided the Swede does not fit into his playing system and it would be best for Barca to cut their losses this summer.

    With Manchester City already in talks over a deal for Ibrahimovic, it's understood Barca will seek €40-45 million for his transfer.

    Ibra has also attracted enquiries from Chelsea and Arsenal.
