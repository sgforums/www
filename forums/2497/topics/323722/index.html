    ARSENE WENGER is leading the chase for Colombian scoring sensation Falcao.

     

    The Arsenal boss is desperate to sign a striker with Emmanuel Adebayor’s uncertain future at the Emirates and Eduardo’s broken leg.

     

    Falcao, 22, scored 38 goals in 58 games for River Plate. He has terrific pace and is able to finish with either foot.

     

    Wenger is lining up an £8million bid but the Argentines value him closer to £12m. Arsenal’s interest is sure to annoy Manchester United boss Alex Ferguson.
