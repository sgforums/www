    World Cup 2010: Provisional Italy Squad - Giuseppe Rossi In, Nicola Legrottaglie And Luca Toni Out

    Juventus defender Nicola Legrottaglie will not be going to the World Cup...

    Marcello Lippi - Italy (Getty Images)

     

     

    Marcello Lippi's provisional 30-man Italy squad has been announced and confirmed, the FIGC said in a statement on Tuesday afternoon.

    Juventus' Nicola Legrottaglie has been dropped, as has Roma forward Luca Toni. Giuseppe Rossi of Villarreal is newly included. As expected, Roma's Daniele De Rossi is in the squad after missing out on last week's training programme due to the Coppa Italia final.

    Here is the full squad list as confirmed by the FIGC on their official website.
