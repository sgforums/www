    Chelsea defender Paulo Ferreira insists he has held no talks about a possible move away from Stamford Bridge.

    The Portuguese right back, who has figured infrequently this term because of injury and the form of Jose Bosingwa, has been linked with a move to Premier League rivals West Ham and Aston Villa.

    Ferreira has started just once in the league all term, and that at Aston Villa in the unfamiliar position of left back.

    “I have a contract with Chelsea. I have seen some rumours in the newspapers but I don’t know anything nor has anyone spoken to me," said the 30-year-old, who is contracted to the Stamford Bridge side until June 2013.

    A cruciate ligament injury sustained during training in March curtailed Ferreira’s season, but the former Porto defender is confident he will be fit for the start of the new season in August.

    “The season was strange because of injuries, but after the operation I will be perfect for the new campaign,” added Ferreira.
