    Imminent Man Utd signing Owen puts Ferrari up for sale

    Imminent Manchester United signing Michael Owen has put his Ferrari up for sale.

    The Guardian says the current Newcastle striker has put the motor up for sale for £150,000.

    With one careful owner and only 9,900 miles on the clock, the two-door 599 GTB Fiorano F1 in metallic Tour de France blue is being sold at a dealership in Harrogate, North Yorkshire.

    The former Newcastle United striker's contract has run out and he is looking for a new club but the car sale is unlikely to be due to the multimillionaire being strapped for cash. The 2007 612bhp model, with two years' warranty left for the cautious buyer, would have cost £198,000 new but Owen had £24,000 worth of extras fitted.
