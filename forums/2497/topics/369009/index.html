    Chelsea expect Terry to break silence over Man City this week

    John Terry is set to break his silence over his future at Chelsea.

    The London Evening Standard says Chelsea expect England captain Terry to release a statement pledging his future to the club in the next few days.

    So far, the player has chosen to say nothing about where he will be playing next season but his silence over Manchester City's two bids to sign him has led to further speculation that he might be tempted by a move to Eastlands.

    Sources inside Stamford Bridge insist relations between club and player have not deteriorated during the City saga and say he is taking an active part in their pre-season tour to the USA as usual.
