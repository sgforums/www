Inter Milan chief announces Maicon, Balotelli will leave

  • Poignant

    Inter Milan chief Paolillo announces Maicon, Balotelli will leave

    Inter Milan chief Ernesto Paolillo admits Douglas Maicon and Mario Balotelli are set to be sold.

    Brazil fullback Maicon is wanted by Real Madrid, while Manchester City are finalising terms for striker Balotelli.

    Asked about the pair, Paolillo declared: "We are in the Stock Exchange building where the basic rule is to announce business when it is final. But I think the idea is extremely reliable.

    "Nothing has yet been completed, but that seems the way. For both, it is a yes."
