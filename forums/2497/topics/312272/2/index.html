    Bolton 2 Sunderland 0

     

    EL-HADJI DIOUF was the man to fire Bolton to a vital win over Sunderland — despite the fact he won't be playing at the Reebok next season.

    The Senegal striker recently declared he will quit the club in the summer regardless of whether they survive or not. But Diouf was on hand to grab a vital first half goal for Gary Megson's side in their battle to beat the drop.

    And with a Daryl Murphy own goal adding the crucial second, Bolton now go into their last game against Chelsea knowing only a deluge of goals will prevent them from playing top flight football next season.

    With Roy Keane's side having secured their Premier League future last week, Bolton were the more imposing of the two sides, and dominated possession from the outset.
