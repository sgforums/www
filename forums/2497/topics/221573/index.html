Neville knew Saha would miss the penalty

  • anhydrouscoppersulphate
    22 Nov 06, 23:02
    Celtic captain Neil Lennon claims that Gary Neville told him that Louis Saha would miss the late penalty in their Champions League clash.

    The Frenchman had his spot-kick saved when Celtic were leading 1-0 and an equaliser would have sent United through to the knock-out stages.

    Neville apparently predicted the miss but rather than - as captain - asking someone else to take the penalty, Neville watched Saha take a penalty that was at the perfect height for Artur Boruc to save.

    "Gary Neville actually came across to me and said about Saha, his head has gone, hes going to miss this," said Lennon.

    Sahas miss meant Celtic went through to the knock-out stages, leaving United needing a result against benfica to claim a place in the last 16.
