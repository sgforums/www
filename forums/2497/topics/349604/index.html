Man Utd ace Tevez: Pressure was greater at West Ham

  • limpper
    27 Feb 09, 23:23

     

    Carlos Tevez says there was greater pressure playing for relegation-threatened West Ham than he's experienced since moving to Manchester United.

    “The pressure I felt at West Ham when we had to keep them in the Premier League was intense,” he told the Daily Express. “When you are at the centre of a team that is fighting to stay up that is much harder pressure to deal with. The pressure builds up match by match, especially when so many people love the club and want to see it survive. It is not pleasant.

    “It is much easier when you are fighting for trophies. It is a nice kind of pressure and the sort of thing I have been dealing with since my time at Boca Juniors. Win, win, win – that’s all you have to do!”
