    Report: Inter's Materazzi Launches iTunes Store Application

    The Matrix has expanded, and can now be found on the Apple iTunes Store.

    Marco Materazzi - Inter (Inter.it)


    Inter defender Marco Materazzi has launched an application, whereby a fan can find the latest news and photos about the World Cup winner.

    The 'Matrix' can now be found on the Apple iTunes Store, providing a last-minute present "in time for Christmas".

    "I had promised my fans that I would do this," Materazzi was quoted as saying to the club's official website, Inter.it. "And I try to maintain what I promise.

    "Thanks to Reset Communication, in time for Christmas, you can download my application from the iTunes store, only for iPod Touch and iPhone.

    "Touch the display icon and you can have access to a world of knowledge about me.

    "I am the first sporting person to have the pleasure of providing such a program with my friends and fans," Materazzi concluded.

    Materazzi is not the first Italian player to venture into the technological world, as Roma captain Francesco Totti launched his own website only a few weeks ago, whereby fans can get in touch with Il Capitano.
