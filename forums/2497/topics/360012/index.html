Rooney keen to stick it to Arsenal fans

  • limpper
    14 May 09, 20:51

     

    Manchester United striker Wayne Rooney admits he wants to stick it to Arsenal fans this weekend by winning the title at Old Trafford.

    "One point will win the title for us. Hopefully that can come on Saturday," said Rooney.

    "It would be brilliant to do it in front of our own fans and brilliant to win it against Arsenal, who are a great footballing team.

    "When they come to Old Trafford you always hear them singing about winning the title at Old Trafford, so it would be nice to win against them. That would be special."

    A draw or better against Arsenal would mean United have won the title three years in a row for only the second time in their history and put them level with Liverpool on 18 League crowns. Even if Arsene Wenger's side win, United have an away trip against Hull eight days later in which to clinch top spot.

    "We want to do it in front of our own fans, I think the manager's said we've only done that once in 17 years," added Rooney. "And to win it three years on the run would be amazing."
