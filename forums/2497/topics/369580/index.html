It could be a two to one swap deal for Liverpool

  • limpper
    24 Jul 09, 22:13

    Liverpool boss Benitez insists on Real Madrid's Negredo in Xabi deal

    Liverpool boss Rafa Benitez wants Real Madrid to include a quality player in their bid for Xabi Alonso.

    The Independent says Benitez is holding out for a Madrid player to be included in the deal, with striker Alvaro Negredo still a possibility.

    If Real agree to include Negredo then the defender Alvaro Arbeloa would be likely to join Alonso in moving to the Bernabeu.
