    January transfer window

    All the January transfer moves from every Barclays Premier League club.

     

    Several Barclays Premier League clubs brought players in on loans and free transfers in the January transfer window but there have been few big-money deals.

    Patrick Vieira returned to England for the first time since leaving Arsenal in 2005 as he joined Manchester City on an initial six-month deal from Inter Milan.

    Another ex-Gunner, Sol Campbell, is back in the Barclays Premier League - with Arsenal. Campbell's second stint at the club he left in 2006 will see him stay at the Emirates Stadium until the summer at least.

    Liverpool signed Argentina international Maxi Rodriguez on a free transfer from Atletico Madrid, while the Reds offloaded Andrea Dossena and Andrey Voronin to Napoli and Dinamo Moscow respectively.

    Elsewhere, Birmingham City splashed out on Spanish midfielder Michel, and Sunderland brought in Matthew Kilgallon from Sheffield United.
