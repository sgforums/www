    ALEX FERGUSON believes David Moyes is the man to succeed him at Old Trafford.

    SunSport revealed yesterday the Manchester United boss is ready to step down after one more season.

    Ferguson, 68, is convinced that Everton boss Moyes can provide the continuity United crave to keep them challenging for the top honours.

    Jose Mourinho is the bookies' favourite and enjoys a close relationship with Ferguson from his time as a rival boss with Chelsea.

    But the United hierarchy, including the influential Sir Bobby Charlton, are not convinced.

    Ferguson knows fellow Glaswegian Moyes, 46, well and has constantly championed his achievements at Preston and now Everton.

    He also believes it is right to give a good British manager the chance to take one of the big jobs in football.
