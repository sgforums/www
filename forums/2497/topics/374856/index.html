    Chelsea's Terry: Nobody gave a s*** about England

    Chelsea and England captain John Terry couldn't care less if the big nations miss next year's World Cup.

    Argentina, Portugal and France are all facing the prospect of qualification failure.

    A World Cup without Messi, Ronaldo and Ribery? "Bring it on," said Terry yesterday, if only because it would enhance England's chances of success.

    "Nobody gave a s*** about us when we missed out on the Euros last summer," he added.
