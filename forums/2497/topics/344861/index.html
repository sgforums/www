    City terminate Kaka discussions

    Manchester City have brought an end to their interest in AC Milan's Brazilian midfielder Kaka.

     

    Following a meeting in Milan on Monday, City have terminated discussions with AC Milan over the proposed transfer of the former World Footballer of the Year.

    City confirmed in a statement that they had pulled out of negotiations as there was little chance of a deal being done.
