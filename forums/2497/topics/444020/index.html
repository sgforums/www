    The last-16 Dutch Cup tie between Ajax and AZ Alkmaar was abandoned on Wednesday after Alkmaar's goalkeeper, Esteban Alvarado, was sent off for kicking a fan who had run on to the pitch.

    The Costa Rica international Esteban appeared to retaliate against the fan after being kicked himself.

    After he was dismissed the Alkmaar coach, Gertjan Verbeek, ordered his team to leave the pitch and the match was called off with Ajax leading 1-0.
