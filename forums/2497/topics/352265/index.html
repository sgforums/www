    Arsenal midfielder Amaury Bischoff has revealed that he is tempted to move to Sporting.

    The Portugal Under-21 international has revealed that Sporting have enquired about him, as well as another ‘great Portuguese club’.

    The 21-year-old was a shock signing for The Gunners last summer, as he had never played for Werder Bremen in the German top flight before securing a move to Emirates Stadium.

    “Arsenal remain my priority,” he told Aujourd’hui Sport.

    “I am aware that two great Portuguese clubs are following me.

    “But I do prefer Sporting – they are a prestigious club that play beautiful football.”

    Bischoff has come on as a substitute in the cup games with Wigan, Burnley and Cardiff City this season.
