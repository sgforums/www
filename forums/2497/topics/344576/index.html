    Sir Alex Ferguson has been voted the World's Best Club Coach of 2008, by the International Federation of Football History and Statistics (IFFHS).

    The Scot last won this honour after Manchester United's Champions League victory in 1999.

    Not a single English manager made the cut as Ferguson was the only British representative.

    The Top 10 Club Coaches for 2008

    1. Sir Alex Ferguson (Manchester United)

    2. Dirk Advocaat (Zenit St Petersburg)

    3. Edgardo Bauza (LDU de Quito)

    4. Arsene Wenger (Arsenal)

    5. Rafa Benitez (Liverpool)

    6. Carlos Luis Ischia (Boca Juniors)

    7. Roberto Mancini (Inter Milan)

    8. Avram Grant (Chelsea)

    9. Manuel Jose Jesus Silva (Al-Ahly Cairo)

    10. Frank Rijkaard (Barcelona)
