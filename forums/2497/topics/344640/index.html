    Xavi warned Barcelona fans against already celebrating the title after thrashing Deportivo La Coruna.

    Barca won 5-0 thanks to goals from Lionel Messi and doubles for Samuel Eto'o and Thierry Henry, but Xavi cautioned: "Once again we won at home and again with great feeling. The team has a great feeling.

    "We do not want to end this streak, because the fans and we are enjoying it as well. But we must be more humble than anyone else. The target will be the titles.

    "This now is useless without championships. We are in mid-season, there is a long way to go."
