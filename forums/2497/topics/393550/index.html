    Furious Hertha Berlin fans went on the rampage on Saturday following their home defeat to Nuernberg.

    Hertha's 2-1 loss was their 12th home game without a win and left them rooted to the bottom of the Bundesliga.

    And around 100 angry supporters decided enough was enough as they stormed the pitch at the end of the game, trashing the dug-outs as they attempted to attack the club's players and coaching staff, before police intervened to restore order.
