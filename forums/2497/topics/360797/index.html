    Arsenal. 30 players at a cost of 80 million (approx)
    Chelsea. 28 players at a cost of 226 million.
    Liverpool. 24 players at a cost of 146 million.
    Man Utd. 30 players at a cost of 200 million.

So whilst United have a bigger squad to choose from, the average cost of a player is less than 600,000 each, hardly a fortune. And United's squad is bigger as it's packed with far more academy players than Liverpool's (Nev, Evans, Brown, Gibson, Giggs, Fletcher, Scholes, Wellbeck, O'Shea).
