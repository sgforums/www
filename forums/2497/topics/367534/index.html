    Real Madrid Accept Stuttgart Offer For Klaas-Jan Huntelaar - Report

    The only obstacle remaining appears to be the Dutchman agreeing personal terms with the German club...

     

    According to Marca, Real Madrid have accepted VfB Stuttgart's €20 million bid for Klaas-Jan Huntelaar, who is out of favour at the Bernabeu.

    The German side are believed to be the only club to have matched Real's valuation of the player, who joined 'Los Blancos' from Ajax in the winter transfer window.

    Despite Huntelaar's €3.8m-a-year current contract, Stuttgart are ready to match the striker's wage demands following the lucrative sale of Mario Gomez to Bayern Munich.

    The Spanish paper claims that the only thing left for Huntelaar to complete a deal to Stuttgart is for him to agree on personal terms with the German outfit.
