Cicinho lined up to replace Alves

  • omgukilledkenny
    17 Aug 07, 19:04


    Sevilla are considering signing Real Madrid right-back Cicinho as a replacement for Chelsea-bound defender Daniel Alves.

    Despite Sevilla turning down an offer from Chelsea on Thursday for the transfer of Brazilian right-back Alves, the player's future remains up in the air.

    The Andalusian club have already expressed an interest in 27-year-old Brazilian Cicinho, who is seen as the ideal candidate to replace Alves.

    Cicinho, who has been placed on Madrid's transfer list, has revealed he would welcome a move to Sevilla although he is also considering an offer from Roma.

    "If Real Madrid and Sevilla reach an agreement that satisfies both sides it would be perfect," said Cicinho.

    "Right now I am still a Real Madrid player and I awaiting what my agent and Real Madrid sporting director Pedrag Mijatovic decide.

    "It's true that I have several offers, Roma have called me and it is a club that I like, just as Sevilla is."
