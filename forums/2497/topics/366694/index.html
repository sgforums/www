    Perez targeting 'two more'

    Real president linked with move for Alonso

    Real Madrid president Florentino Perez feels that 'two more signings' will complete his overhaul at the Santiago Bernabeu.

    The Real supremo has already landed Kaka, Cristiano Ronaldo and Raul Albiol as part of his bid to rebuild the 'Galacticos' era.

    Lyon striker Karim Benzema's move to the Spanish giants is on the brink of being completed, with the club already including the Frenchman on their website's squad list.

    Liverpool midfielder Xabi Alonso continues to be linked with a switch to Real, and Perez has stated that he is "until August" to acquire his services.

    Perez told Cuatro: "I think that with two more signings we will have renewed the squad."

    However, Perez appears defeated in his bid to land Bayern Munich star Franck Ribery, with the Bundesilga club adamant the France international will not be sold.

    He added: "That is the reality right now."
