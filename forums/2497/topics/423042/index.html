    CAMPED OUT ... Blues owner Abramovich

     

     

     

     

    BARCELONA are expanding their famed academy by moving in next door to Chelsea.

    The Spanish giants are setting up camp at the school attended by the kids of several Blues stars.

    And former Barca defender Julio Alberto, who is heading the expansion into England, claims he doesn't care if Chelsea owner Roman Abramovich has problems with the plan.

    Barca are holding coaching classes for 300 children aged six to 16 beginning in April at the ACS school in Cobham, which is less than two miles from Chelsea's training ground.

    And Alberto said: "The fact we are near Chelsea is not an issue. If there is any surprise I will explain it to Abramovich myself."

    Barcelona academy coach Xevi Marce added: "We are going to start slowly but surely to build a team here, train all the kids and get that passion."
