    Ballon D'Or winner revealed...but is it Cristiano Ronaldo?

     

    The trophy is so shiny that the winner's face can be seen reflected in the top of it

     

    The European footballer of the year is set to be announced by French magazine France Football - but they may have given the game away with their sneak preview...

    Pictures of the winning footballer with the trophy have already been taken - with a teaser picture put up on the France Football website.

    Except it maybe gives a little too much away. The trophy is so shiny that the winner's face can be seen reflected in the top of it, and if you turn your head sideways it looks distinctly like Cristiano Ronaldo.

    The Portuguese winger is widely expected to scoop the award after his phenomenal form powered Manchester United to a League and Champions League double.
