    Portsmouth sign shirt deal with Jobsite


    Portsmouth have signed a three-year shirt sponsorship deal with online recruitment website Jobsite, the Premier League club said on Friday.

    The agreement, announced on the club's website, comes days after Portsmouth confirmed a takeover led by Abu Dhabi billionaire Sulaiman al-Fahim.

    The club did not disclose financial details of the sponsorship deal.
