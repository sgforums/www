    Chelsea include Pizarro in new offer for AC Milan's Pirlo

    Chelsea have made a new offer to AC Milan for Andrea Pirlo.

    Corriere dell Sera says Chelsea, aware of Milan's problems in finding a new centre-forward, have offered to include Claudio Pizarro in their bid for Pirlo.

    Milan coach Leonardo is keen, knowing that Pizarro enjoyed an outstanding season last term on-loan at Werder Bremen.

    Chelsea are willing to also put around £8 million on the table to close the deal.
