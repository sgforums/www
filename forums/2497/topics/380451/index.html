    LAST YEAR`S FINALISTS CLASH AGAIN

    .

    .

    Holders Manchester United will face Tottenham in the quarter-finals of the Carling Cup next month.

    The match is a repeat of this year's final, which United won 4-1 on penalties after a goalless 120 minutes at Wembley in March.

    Chelsea - who saw off Bolton to reach the last eight - face another team from the north west as they bid to reach the semi-finals. Carlo Ancelotti's men take on Blackburn at Ewood Park.

    Arsenal, conquerors of Liverpool in midweek, will face Manchester City at Eastlands.

    And the draw, featuring eight teams from the Barclays Premier League, is completed by Portsmouth's clash with Aston Villa at Fratton Park.

     

     

     

    Full Draw

     

    Blackburn v Chelsea

    Man Utd v Tottenham

    Portsmouth v Aston Villa

    Man City v Arsenal

     

    Matches to be played week beginning November 30
