    Torres eyes more goals



    Fernando Torres has expressed his delight at scoring his first goal for Liverpool and hopes it is the first of many for his new club.

    The club's record signing got off the mark for Liverpool with the opening goal in Friday's 2-0 win over Shanghai Shenhua.

    The Spaniard hopes to build on the strike when Liverpool get their Premier League campaign underway at Aston Villa next week.

    Delighted

    "I'm very pleased to have scored my first goal," Torres told the club's official website. "I'm happy with that and with the victory for the team. I hope that it will be the first of many.

    "Right now I'm going to enjoy this moment. I intend to score lots of goals and when the league starts that's where they will be the most important."

    Torres says he is enjoying his early experiences at Liverpool and has thanked his new team-mates for helping him settle in.

    Praise

    "It's good with my team-mates," added Torres. "I'm enjoying everything about this great club and with the great players it has I can learn a lot and have been since my very first day.

    "It's all new for me but I'm learning more and adapting to playing in the team.

    "Some things are made easier in one way because everyone is doing everything they can and everything possible in order for me to adapt and settle in quickly.

    "It's the same both on and off the pitch, they are all supporting me and giving me a hand and I'm so lucky because with people like that it's much easier."
