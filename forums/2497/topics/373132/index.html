    Real Madrid's Ronaldo: Kaka and I want same things

    Cristiano Ronaldo is confident of playing successfully with Kaka at Real Madrid.

    The winger said: "He likes to win and me too. We want to experience new challenges and I am prepared for them. Here I want to win everything possible, I am very positive on this point."

    Ronaldo added: "The Galacticos are already gone. It was a time to admire and meant a lot for the players they had. They made history in this club. We are a new team with new thinking and all we want to do is win."

    Kaka: I joined Real Madrid to win Champions League

    Kaka says he's joined Real Madrid to win the Champions League.

    On the first time he pulled on the Real shirt, the former AC Milan midfielder said: "It was very emotional, I feel very proud to belong to a club recognized throughout the world, its history, its triumphs and the players who have left their mark.

    "The big motivation is to win the Champions League. It would be great to play the final at the Bernabeu."
