    Arsenal striker Nicklas Bendtner is the shock target for Inter Milan in next month's transfer window.

    Inter confirmed Chelsea's Didier Drogba is not on their wanted list but Denmark international Bendtner has been identified as a possible new recruit.  

    If Eduardo proves his fitness in the coming weeks after his broken leg, Arsene Wenger may consider selling Bendtner to help finance the purchase of a central defender and midfielder.
