    Chelsea boss Ancelotti ready for "mind games" of Man Utd's Ferguson

    Chelsea boss Carlo Ancelotti is looking forward to his battle with Manchester United counterpart Sir Alex Ferguson.

    He said: “The last time we met we drank red wine in Manchester. It will be exciting matches with Manchester United.

    “When I think about the Community Shield at Wembley in August I have a lot of positive sensations. We’ve had a good relationship. We’ve played against each other and for me it went well. Every match has a different story.

    “I have heard of his mind games. I am not sure if he will play them with me. I think irony is important thing in-English. If you use irony but maintain respect for me that’s good. I accept irony. We will see each other on August 9.”
