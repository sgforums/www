    Arsenal manager Arsene Wenger has revealed that he is in two minds over whether or not to reinforce when the transfer window reopens in January.

    Arsenal manager Arsene Wenger has said that he is yet to decide what his transfer plans are in the January transfer window.

    According to press reports, Wenger attempted to seal last minute deals on September 1 (deadline day) in an attempt to bring a suitable partner for playmaking midfielder Cesc Fabregas, including Liverpool's Xabi Alonso.

    "It's far from decided," Wenger told the club's official website when asked about his future transfer plans.

    "We will make a check at the end of December and we will see. We have had many players out and showed we have a great squad that can win the big games."

    Arsenal have recently been linked with AS Roma's combative midfielder Daniele De Rossi.
