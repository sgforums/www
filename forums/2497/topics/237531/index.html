    Italian midfielder Gennaro Gattuso is hopeful that AC Milan will seal a deal for Barcelona superstar Ronaldinho.

    The brilliant Brazilian has been linked with a move away from the Nou Camp this summer and Gattuso believes he is just the player the Rossoneri are looking for.

    "I hope that Ronaldinho comes to Milan," declared the combative midfielder.

    "He is one of those players that plays in a position where he can really make a difference."

    Ronaldinho's strike partner for Brazil, Ronaldo, made his debut for the club on the weekend against Livorno, and Gattuso was happy with the impact his new teammate made.

    "Ronaldo came on and showed great enthusiasm, as soon as he entered the field he showed his quality," he said.
