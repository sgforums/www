    The Wembley Cup is an invitational football tournament due to take place on 24 July 2009 and 26 July 2009 at Wembley Stadium, England. Similar to the Amsterdam Tournament and the Emirates Cup, four-teams will compete across two matchdays with three points awarded for a win, with a bonus point for every goal scored.

    Barcelona, Tottenham Hotspur, Celtic and Al-Ahly will be the competing teams in a format consisting of four games.
