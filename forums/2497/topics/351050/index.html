    ADEBAYOR ... future hint.

     

     

    EMMANUEL ADEBAYOR has rocked Arsenal by admitting he could still quit the club. The Togo striker signed a new £80,000-a-week deal with the Gunners last summer, after AC Milan and Barcelona were both linked with £32million bids.

    Former Monaco forward Adebayor smashed 30 goals in 48 games last season but has hit just 12 so far this campaign. And although the 25-year-old insists he is happy with his decision to stay in north London, he has refused to rule out a switch away from the Emirates Stadium in the future.

    Adebayor said: "I knew that Milan and Barcelona were interested in me but in the end I chose Arsenal. "Arsenal are a club that I want to repay with important victories for having allowed me to become one of the great players in world football.

    "I find myself well here, even if, as I say in life, you can never know what will happen in the future."
