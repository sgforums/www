    HARRY REDKNAPP will collect a £4million bonus if he keeps Tottenham in the Premier League.

    The 61 year-old manager has made an immediate impact on his arrival at White Hart Lane.

    Saturday's incredible 2-1 victory over Liverpool crowning an incredible first week in charge at White Hart Lane.

    Spurs have now played three games since Redknapp's appointment, winning two and sharing the spoils in the 4-4 thriller at Arsenal.

    Chairman Daniel Levy has torn up his continental system of appointing a director of football and handed control over player recruitment to his new manager.

    Redknapp is being paid £4m-a-year in his three and a half year contract.
