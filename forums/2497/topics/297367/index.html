    Hamburg star Rafael Van der Vaart claims Juventus are interested in signing him and that he would be interested in a move to Italy.

    Van der Vaart is thought to be keen on leaving Hamburg with a number of clubs expressing an interest in his services.

    The Dutch international admits he would consider any approach from Juventus as he weighs up his options.

    "I will only leave for a big club, like Juventus for example," Van der Vaart told Voetbal International.

    "I know that they are interested and if the Bianconeri were to make me a concrete offer, I would consider it very carefully. I think that Hamburg would be ready to discuss terms with them."
