    Nani admits he's been frustrated with the lack of games he's had for Manchester United this season.

    "Last season was good. I was pleased with my games and my contribution to the team," he told the Manchester Evening News. "This season I hoped it would be better. When I get my chances my aim is to do better than I did at the start of the season.

    "I want to play more. I would like to play more times but it hasn't been the same as last season.

    "I feel good when I try to do my best to help the team. In some games I have done well but others I haven't done so well, but this is football. Your fitness when you don't play every week suffers. But I am training good and keep preparing for the next match and hope to play.

    "My focus is always doing well for the team. If I do that then everything will be going well for me. I am still confident. It is difficult when you play one game and then are out for two matches. You want to stay in for a run of matches.
