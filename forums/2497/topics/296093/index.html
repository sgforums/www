    Good news for Serie A fans

    Kaka, Ronaldo and Totti will be coming to your TV screens soon. Channel 5 is bringing you Serie A from 16 Dec. Channel 5 will also bring you a magazine show Total Italian Football.

    Telecast Details:
    16 Dec: Lazio vs Juventus - 12.30am (delayed)
    16 Dec: Cagliari vs Inter Milan - 3.30am (LIVE)

    23 Dec: Inter Milan vs AC Milan - 12.30am (delayed)
    23 Dec: Juventus vs Siena - 2.30am (delayed)

    Total Italian Football - Every Saturday, 12.30pm.
