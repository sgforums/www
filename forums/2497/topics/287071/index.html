    Full-back Alvaro Arbeloa was also ruled out with a thigh injury, while Daniel Agger has not fully recovered from a broken metatarsal.     DUO TO MISS OUT FOR CHELSEA

    Chelsea are still without captain John Terry (knee) and left-back Ashley Cole (ankle) for their Champions League Group B game against Schalke at Stamford Bridge.

    Terry underwent surgery on his left knee last Friday and is out for up to three weeks, while Cole is sidelined with ankle ligament damage. Michael Ballack is the only other long-term absentee as he continues his recovery from two ankle operations, while Wayne Bridge has returned to the squad.

    Chelsea : Cech, Cudicini, Hilario, Ferreira, Belletti, Alex, Ben-Haim, Lampard, Obi, Essien, Bridge, J Cole, Carvalho, Makelele, Sidwell, Malouda, Drogba, Shevchenko, Pizarro, Kalou, Wright-Phillips.
