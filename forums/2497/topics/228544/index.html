    Internazionale supremo Massimo Moratti has reportedly launched a bid to persuade Luis Figo to remain in Serie A.

    Figo's contract with Inter is set to expire in June, but Moratti could now be set to offer him a season's extension to stay in Italy.

    The 34-year-old is now thought to be reconsidering his options before making a final decision on his future at the beginning of the New Year.
