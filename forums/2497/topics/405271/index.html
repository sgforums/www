    Porto prepare to sell Bruno Alves to Chelsea

    Porto are ready to sell defender Bruno Alves.

    A Bola says Porto management are already seeking a replacement for the experienced centre-half as they prepare to cash in on Portugal international Alves.

    Chelsea are favourites for the centre-half, who is keen on pursuing a career in England.

    With David Luiz ready to pen a new deal with Benfica, Chelsea boss Carlo Ancelotti is now focused on bringing Alves to Stamford Bridge as his prime centre-half signing this summer.
