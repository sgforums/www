    JUVENTUS PLAN £8M BID FOR SKRTEL

    Liverpool defender interests Serie A giants

    SKRTEL: Slovakian interests Juventus

     

    JUVENTUS will make an £8million summer bid for Liverpool's Martin Skrtel.

    The Italians - who had also targeted Reds boss Rafa Benitez - are looking for a stopper to replace the ageing Fabio Cannavaro at the heart of their defence.

    They believe the Slovakia international fits the bill as a no-nonsense defender with the ability to organise the backline.

    Skrtel joined Liverpool in 2008 for a £6.5m fee from Zenit St Petersburg and Juventus are willing to bid close to £8m to land the defender.

    The hardman defender may welcome a summer move if Benitez leaves Liverpool.

    Juve are enduring a poor campaign in Serie A and will look to rebuild during the close season.
