    Blues still tracking Carlo

    The Blues were believed to be looking elsewhere after Ancelotti insisted last week that he would be staying in Italy.

    However, it now appears Chelsea have resurrected their interest in Ancelotti with a high-ranking official from the Premier League club visiting Milan this weekend.

    Reports have suggested that Chelsea owner Roman Abramovich is preparing a record compensation package worth £20million for Milan to part with Ancelotti.

    Roma coach Luciano Spalletti, Fiorentina boss Cesare Prandelli and former Internazionale chief Roberto Mancini have also been linked with the Chelsea job, while Portugal's Luiz Felipe Scolari has stated that he will not discuss his future until after 2008.
