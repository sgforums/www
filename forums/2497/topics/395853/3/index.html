    Real Madrid's Manuel Pellegrini: I Feel Frustrated And Impotent

    The Chilean coach feels his players could have done more.

    Real Madrid coach Manuel Pellegrini was predictably disappointed after his side's 2-0 home loss to Barcelona.

    "I feel frustration and impotence," he told As.com.

    "We could have done more. Barcelona weren't like in other matches, but they scored first and that made them stronger. We are capable of more than we showed.

    "We're the same team as has being playing recently but against this team we rushed things.

    "In the end we lost to a superior team."

    Now the challenge for the Liga resumes.

    "There are 21 points left to fight for and we're not going to give up," Pellegrini said.

    "We will force Barcelona to win their matches. They also have the Champions League. If we keep the pressure up we have a chance.

    "It's a major loss, but there's a long way left to go. I don't think a champion was crowned tonight."
