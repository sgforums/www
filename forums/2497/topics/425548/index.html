    Carlo: This has to end, friend

    GIVE OVER, PAL ... Carlo's plea

      

      

    Sunday, 20 March 2011 

      

    Chelsea v Man City     12:00am (Mon Morning)   Mio  102 / 110

      

      

      

    CARLO ANCELOTTI will remind Roberto Mancini they are supposed to be pals ahead of Chelsea's clash with Manchester City tomorrow.
    .

    The pair were Italy team-mates and have remained in close contact throughout their managerial careers.

    But Ancelotti has lost all three games with Mancini as Chelsea boss and cannot afford another defeat.

    He said: "Roberto was my friend in the Italian team but now he is not such a friend because he has won too many times against me.

    "He has to understand you sometimes have to give something to your friends! All three times they have beaten us on the counter-attack."
