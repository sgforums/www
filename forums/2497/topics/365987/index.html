    Torres urges Liverpool to go for Valencia star trio

    Fernando Torres is demanding Liverpool raid Valencia for three of it's top names.

    "We lost a lot of points against teams in the middle of the table," admitted Torres. "And we have to improve on that because if we can get 10 more points at home I think we can win the Premier League.

    "English football is very difficult because it's very physical and played at a high tempo. We have a fantastic team to play against the big teams and we are competitive.

    "But maybe against those teams lower down we are not good enough. We have to improve that and I think we can. I think we can definitely be stronger at home. We have some very good players but maybe we need other players. I hope Liverpool will bring people like Silva, Mata or Villa. These are small players with quality like United had with Carlos Tevez and also with Paul Scholes.

    "They are clever players and maybe they can make the difference. Yossi Benayoun plays like these kind of players and I hope we can bring more like that so then we have different options to play against all the sides."
