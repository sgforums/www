    Jose Mourinho Prepares €30m For Atletico Madrid Star Sergio Agüero - Report

    Agüero has turned Mourinho's head in his direction and the money needed to land him is available, according to reports in Italy...

     

    Massimo Moratti has given Jose Mourinho a blank cheque book for the summer transfer window, which the coach will use to spend €30 million on Atletico Madrid hitman Sergio Agüero.

    The former Chelsea coach had his eyes set on Manchester United's Carlos Tevez before Sir Alex Ferguson offered the former West Ham man a new deal. Mourinho has now turned his head towards Agüero and Sportmediaset.it claims that he is ready to offer the Spanish giants €30m plus two players in return for the striker.

    Didier Drogba has been closely linked with the Nerazzurri, but Agüero's age and prowess compared to the Ivorian's will represent more value for money, given that 'Drogs' is 31 and Agüero is stil only 20.

    In addition, the Nerazzurri need a world-class striker to partner Zlatan Ibrahimovic up front and the Argentinean is, perhaps, the best match in terms of tactics. Mario Balotelli and Adriano have been unreliable and the 'Special One' doesn't want any further problems next season.

    Convincing the Colchoneros to sell will be tough, of course. However, Mourinho is undeterred and has a cunning plan up his sleeve. The coach will insert Nicolas Burdisso and Luis Jimenez as part of the deal, as both players are wanted by the Rojiblancos. This will work for 'Mou', as he will also be trimming down his squad.
