    i think there are plans where they will grant you a loan if you sign up with a line with a phone company or courts.

    it works like this : you go to the company, tell them how much u want (got limit one but im not sure how much) and then you go courts and buy stuff on credit and you give them in return for the loan. Interest rate is extremely high, 22% to 24% if im not mistaken. But in my experience, unsecured loans are usually this high (16% to 24% range).

    some of these companies can be found in the advertisements section of TNP daily. However, whether or not they are legal is a grey area.

    i hate to be the one highlighting this option to you but i just feel you should know that these options exist.

    Alternatively if you have a property under your name you could apply for an overdraft facility with a bank, interest rate is lower at about 8% thereabouts. It's like taking a loan and you pledge your property.

    Good luck with finding money.
