    If you're a private individual, your best option would be to trade precious metal-linked ETF's (ie. SPDR which tracks gold). Essentially, these ETF's trade like stocks with the underlying driver being the precious metals.

    As with all securities, there's no certain "good way to make money" if you do not know what you're doing...
