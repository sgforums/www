    Union Steel is involved in the collection and processing of scrap metals. The recycled metals are sold to steel mills, foundries, metals fabricators and metals brokers. It sells ferrous metals (such as steel and heavy melting scrap steel) and non-ferrous metals (such as copper, aluminum and stainless steel), as steel plates, reinforcement steel bars, H-beams, I-beams, pipes and sheet piles to our customers which include trading houses, construction companies and engineering companies. It operates its own island-wide scrap metal collection to obtain scrap steel at low cost.

    The company generated sales of S$112m during FY2004 (86% from recycling, 12% from trading, 2% from other activities), and profit from operations of S$12.7m. Most business is derived from China, Indonesia and Singapore. Union Steel states an initial intention to pay out 30% of net earnings as cash dividend, which should be nearly 1 cent per share for FY2005 based on first-half results (almost 5% yield at the 20c offer price).
