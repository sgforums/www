    Hello....

    What Im going to say will be hard for you to accept, but please do spend time to think about it.

    Stock research or stock analysis is useless.

    The only utility you can derive from them is that they will miss their forecasts for sure. Therefore don't take any positions as they told you or recommend you.

    Why are they useless?

    Because any analysis or research in this field will involve quantification and forecasting. It is not possible to quantify all variables in stock market dynamics and use a model to numerically compute and then forecast the future. It is not only misleading but dangerously so. It will lead you to disaster, as you will be like a pilot reading a malfunctiong altimeter.

    For further conviction, you can do some reading on your own on the subject matter, or try it out yourself.
