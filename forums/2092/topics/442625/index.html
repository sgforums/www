    Somebody was telling me unless you are very desperate for a place to live, better to wait and see how prices go in the next year or so.

     

    Her reasoning is this:

     

    If prices still persist in going up in the next 12 months and you are waiting to buy, then assuming a gradual appreciation as shown in past quarters, the increase in price should be roughly equivalent to what you would have saved in the next 12 months say eg. $10000 a year. (cash + cpf) 

     

    That amount saved would have been roughly equivalent to your using the money to pay off the monthly mortgage had you bought the flat now at present time anyway. 

     

    But if prices drop in the next 12 months and you purchase 12 months later, you would have gotten a better deal by paying less.

     

    She is of course holding other factors constant and assuming interest rates are stable.

     

    You think she makes sense?
