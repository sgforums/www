    Private dental healthcare group, Q & M Dental Group (Singapore) Ltd, has signed a memorandum of understanding with the National Taxi Association (NTA) to provide affordable dental care to its members and their families on Friday.

    The pact will benefit 11,000 taxi drivers and their immediate family members, by offering them affordable dental care for two years, with savings on basic dental treatments.

    Q&M said in a statement that this MOU will take place with immediate effect.

    Dr Ng Chin Siau, Q & M founder and group chief executive officer, noted that taxi drivers are the first faces that many tourists encounter. He said that the group aims to help drivers show happy smiles to their customers.
