    Welcome, basically there are a few groups

    1) CityMaxi (about 100+ Maxicabs in this group)

    2) Star Vito (about 100 Pte Vito/Viano , Highroof and also handful of Alphards)

    3) Eagle (about 30-40 plus Pte Vito and A few Maxicab)

    My advice is to join a group that has many callers, ie drivers who have too many jobs that he can't handle and has to page out thru the walkie network. But, page out jobs, if the group is too big, there will be a lot of competition for that job, a lot of callers usually take care their kaki first, ie underwater, if kaki cannot do, then the page out.

    Rates for Maxicab is $50 but there is always a GST to the caller or to whoever, have to be prepared. MDT jobs also got GST!

    I heard the queue to get he Maxi is very long and the daly rental for the new Viano is $155, so if you just sweep the floor alone, not easy to make a good profit.

    But anyway, welcome to Maxi driving... You have to drive to feel how t is, I can't advice you too much, but at least better than taxi as taxi is only metered fare. Maxi can sweep, can do limo jobs and can d disposal. Wish you all the best!

    Fyi, Mercedes Vito, Viano weighs only (approximately) 2,300kg even for the longest version (long wheel base, like Sam & I is driving now).

    Vito has 3 versions.  Normal, Long, Extra Long.

    MaxiCabs belongs to Long version (which is about 24cm shorter than the Extra Long) @ around 5m.

    But due to the weight of the vehicle, it is still NOT classified as heavy vehicle. 

    Hence, you can still drive with Class 3 driving licence.
