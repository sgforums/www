    TOYOTA HIACE HIGH ROOF

    13 seaters without luggage, 9 seater with luggage

    Lenght is 5.3M, height is about 2.35M

    Cost of the car now is about $105-$110K

    Basically, this vehicle Huat during October to January and also June and July. This vehicle is favored by big families and large business groups of more than 7 and business groups who engage this vehicles are more of middle management.

    Very few chances for hourly disposals, more on running trips, eg VIPs who arrive will take Viano/ Alphard, etc, this vehicle can be the luggage van. Disposals all will be makan by the Durian Head first. Bones give to those junior drivers like me! :)
