    I have spend a big part of my TDs days driving as relief....  I have tried the last year of TD on OMO.

    Frankly speaking, driving OMO is no easy task.  You must have the discipline day in day out... even if you don't feel like driving, you still need to breakeven on the rental.

    There are a few options, if you got a corporate a/c or a/cs to service (on weekdays), get a weekend(s) relief @ discounted rent.  Drivers need sufficient rest.

    Secondly, get a full time relief.  Share your rental liabilities.  Share your corporate a/cs with him/her.  Your revenue may not be lesser, it could even be better on low seasons.

    IMHO,

    OMO 

    Pros> OTOT, ur vehicle ur responsibility, everyday just start car and good to go, got jobs can just take since all 24hrs ur veh is on standby with u. Problem ? confirm your own problem no need to recall as much lol

    Cons> Full rental liability, dun drive or cannot drive also pay 100% rental, if lack discipline end up going "over drive" and suffer fatigue

    Split shift with relief 

    Pros> Less rental to headache which also translate more income from it, "forced" to work within a fix time frame so will have more time for personal leisure.

    Cons>Can be a hassle to find a suitable relief and got to ensure a relief who can perform jobs well for u esp if u have regulars who may require service beyond ur shift hrs and of course vice versa, u may have to help him as well...(esp for limo TDs) GST for each other? can be another headache at times lol
