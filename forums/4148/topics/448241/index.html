    1 Mar 12, 23:06

    My previous thread....http://sgforums.com/forums/4148/topics/448190

    If you are asking for the best taxi company with good infrastructure and networking(charge cards and corporate cashless payment, MDT calls), then... w/o 2nd thought... it is definitely CDG.

    Other companies tried to compromise with the above issues by given more benefits to the drivers.  For eg. Transcabs Dynasty Mercedes are giving $245 back to hirers every month.  And if the performance (of the hirer) is good, he will gets another $50 as a further incentive.  which means, he can get at $3,540 bonus ($295 x 12mths).   And at the end the calender year of his initial contract signed, he will get another incentive.  **The above conditions are last updated on September 2011, any changes will need to be vertify with Jasmine, the Operation Manager of Transcab.

    Other then the 2 above companies, the other more aggressive taxi company will beSMRT taxis.  As their core business is not taxis, hence, they have fallen to 3rd biggest fleet of taxis after Transcab.

    Their (SMRT) mercedes are the older models.  There are no indications that they are planning to venture into the new EClass CDI.  They might even convert all their white limousine cabs(when the lifespan of the vehicles' expires) to black Chryslers limousine cabs instead.

    Hence, at the end of the day, Taxi companies are like rental companies,  they are definitely looking after their Profits generated from taxis' rentals more than the benefits of the drivers.

    There is never going to satisfy everyone when comes to comparsion.  If you are in CDG, it will do you good more than bad.

    This is only my personal point of view.  Pls take it only as an advice.  Keep your opinions opens.

    Cheers

    JDIL.
