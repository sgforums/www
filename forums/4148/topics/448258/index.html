    2 Mar 12, 10:12

    The taxis companies allow any persons currently driving with their taxis - regardless whether you are a normal TD, Limousine TD and /or Maxi TD as members to their respective associations.

    For example, if you are driving for CDG, it is CCOA,  if you are driving for Transcab, it is CTOA.

    The benefits of joining such associations spell clearly in their websites (into details).  In the event of any problems you face when you are on the road, you may approach them for advices.  You may even get traffic summons over turned. 

    If you are carrying GRID walky talky, you also get a discount on the monthly subscription and some events' vouchers..

    In my opinion, I will probably join one if I'm a current TD.  It is just for a minimal fee.  You never know when the unfortunate event will happens.  The association may just be the right persons you turned to.

    Cheers.
