    Normally when this particular job show no interest to bidder, Comfort will raise the stake to be more attractive. Is nonsense to many limo drivers, but what to do, they are the biggest DH in Singapore. From 3 pts to 12 pts..of course you can slowly wait for the increase, but the system in such a way that, from 3 pts-5-7-9-12 than will go back to 3 pts again and etc.. So it depend on which point of time you see this job. As it was a reimbursement from Comfort, the extra points won't be credited to your account on the same day. Usually will take 3 working days or more. And constantly had to call up to check, very troublesome. You can call 6550 8622 if still don't received this extra points. Nevertheless, any premier jobs come with extra points, you know la, what kind of situation it will be. However, I myself don't encourage pick and choose jobs, just bid to your comfortable zone, and everyday will be a happy day, cheers!
