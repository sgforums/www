  • Just_do_it_lah
    9 Nov 12, 02:42

    Normally,

    before u start driving for your "Potential Hirer", u need to talk "Terms & Conditons" verbally with him....

    1) Starting shift hours, if you are driving night, try to go for 4pm to 4am. (reason - currently, by 11pm, the streets in CDB is already desserted, hence, earlier shift changing will help you to generate more income before midnight).

    2) Pump your own diesel, do not "agree" to pump diesel based on mileage (reason - if the engine is running, the mileage is constant, hence, if your partner sleeps in the cab, the diesel run goes into your expenditure).  Get your own diesel card from Esso or Shell.

    3) Late on shift change.  Ocassionally, your partner may be held up in traffic or dued to personal engagement. Normally, there is a charge of S$15-$25 per hour.  If it is only on ocassion, then give and take (no charge), but if you do mention this "Condition", then chances the HIRER will not take you for granted.

    4) Drivers' monthly / quarterly driving bonus.  This is an entitlement to the Registered Hirer.  Some hirer (steady) shares with relief drivers provided the relief drives EVERYDAY (7 days a week).  If the Hirer is NOT WILLING TO SHARE, then, you can opt drive 5-6 days a week.

    The above mentioned is some of the essential points you MUST TAKE NOTE.  Not to be too calculative on small issues (it will spoilt a good partnership) but the important ones must be stated and clarify clearly.

    Cheers.
