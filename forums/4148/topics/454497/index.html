  • Just_do_it_lah
    23 Jun 12, 13:20

    By LTA regulations.... you have to switch on the meter everytime a passenger come aboard your cab (regardless whether it is a hourly booking job or a street pickup).  But you do adjustment on your meter by pressing F1+F4 .... right ? 

    As the passengers you have are considered as private arrangement, which means, your guests and you must agreed to a specified hourly charge.

    You can refer to the limousine industry practise by limousine cabs, currently at S$50 per hour, minimum 2 hours.  A 10 mins grace will be given to the new hour (for eg, 2hrs, 15mins = 3hours of booking).  If it is a 2hrs 5mins, then consider 2hrs lah.

    Explain to them in a nice way. The hourly booking includes ERP Tolls, diesel run and other expenses like parking the cab (parking fee) as the guys might want to go on foot at certain locations.

    Also plan your locations...

    where to start, & where to stop.  Do it smoothing and everything should be fine.  Best is to ask for a deposit if they gets out of cab every now and then.... in case they run away... or cannot be located, you won't be burnt badly.

    Cheers & Good Luck.
