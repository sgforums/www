    Limousine.... must be more special than the normalimousine.... 

    Of course, the necessities are essential.

    1) Camera - in the event of a possible accident (actually, your mobile with camera can also).

    2) Walky talkie - for newbies - listen to traffic information, pick & go locations besides taking group jobs

    3) Street Directory (in case your GPRS screw loose).. Manual ones are still number one.

    4) You ever leave your mobile at home w/o going out ?

    5) Car chargers for your walky and mobile... (this is very important) becos, you may have disposal jobs until don't know when - you passenger has to be in touch with you.

    6) Mineral water (ice mountain or aqua) bottles for passengers consumption.  There is a wholesaler at Tiong Bahru selling 24bottles @ $6-7 per carton.

    7) Panadols. For you or your passengers. 

    8) Spare Cash cards.  You are adviced to keep another spare cash-card in your wallet or pocket in case the other one is running low or misplaced.

    This is about all I can think of... May add up the list. 

    Cheers.
