    Some events call for more than a taxi ride. Maybe it’s your senior prom, wedding day, or special birthday or anniversary. Whatever the event may be, a ride in a limousine could be the perfect way to celebrate. However, when the limousine service doesn’t go as planned, your evening of luxury can turn into an evening of drudgery.

    Here are just a few difficulties you may come up against with a so-called “professional” limousine service…

    Late Driver

    Good transportation businesses thrive on punctuality – and as for the constantly late transportation businesses? They are only able to stay in business because customers don’t complain enough! If you’ve experienced having to rely on a late limousine service, then you should file a complaint with Complaints List immediately. This kind of behavior is simply unacceptable in any kind of transportation business. However, it’s especially egregious coming from a supposed “luxury” limousine service!

    Uninformed Driver

    Perhaps you didn’t have to suffer a late driver. It could be that your limousine service sent you an on-time but uninformed driver. An uninformed driver is one who can barely tell north from south. Surprisingly, there are a lot of limousine drivers who don’t know the city as well as you do. Being a backseat driver is never fun, but it’s especially annoying when you have to assume the role from the backseat of a limousine. Your limousine service should always provide the driver with specific directions and an itinerary. If the limousine service you hired failed to do so, you should file a complaint on ComplaintsList.com immediately!

    Limousine in Poor Condition

    Then again, your driver may not be the problem. Did you agree to rent one product according to a website picture or over-the-phone description, but receive something entirely different on your big day? The frustration of receiving a limousine that’s below your expectations is definitely complaint worthy. After all, if you paid for that LCD TV and mini-bar, but received a beat up limousine with worn burnt orange shag carpet, then you’ve got a bona fide complaint on your hands. Don’t let a limousine rental service get the best of you – file on ComplaintsList.com.

    Over Charging/Credit Card Abuse

    Not all limousine service issues happen inside the car. You could have been taken advantage of by the limousine service through the means of your credit card. Did the limousine service over-charge your credit card? Maybe your information was leaked or identity stolen shortly after doing business with this less-than-reputable company? If so, the time to file with Complaints List is now!

    Don’t let any of your issues drag out any longer. You deserve justice, and Complaints List is here to help you get it. When you file a complaint with us, your voice will be heard by the limousine service that wronged you.
