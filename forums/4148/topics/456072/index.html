    Actually, day driving is better for limousine driver as most of the times, my hourly disposal comes in the morning to evening. As I did mentioned before (abt 8mths ago in Taxi Forum), Limo drivers are adviced to do limousine transfers, like Hotels' Standard Orders (page out from Limousine groups) or, Q at AP limousine Q to farm & harvest your own "towkays". Those willing to pay limousine rates are those quality passengers you are looking for. If a limousine driver only sweep the floor and take MDT current bookings, how r u supposed to find these quality passengers? Everyone starts from zero including me. So, you really have to plan, organise your timing to the AP Limousine Q so as to achieve the lesser possible waiting time. Good luck.
