    There are some Pte Limo drivers whom didn't went thru the Taxi driving industry and yet be sucessful in the industry, but those minority are slaved to the jobs co-ordinators giving them a hard time as they have zero clientele base.

    Rome is not build within a day --- quoted by TS Bro MM069 (he, also, like you, haven't even sit for the TDVL yet , but he has the determination to be successful which is, a good start in his self-motivation. 

    Many of the Private Limousine guys I knew in the industry had TD experiences. Road exposures are very important as a limousine driver, whom supposed to know most of the landmarks in the city at your back of your mind.

    Hence, my advice to you and MM069 is to get some experiences from normal cabs, then upgrade to a limousine cab or Maxi cab and do your own marketing.

    Once, you have sufficient clientele bases, then you may consider to upgrade your service into Private limousine at your own leisure.  Even if you failed to make it in Pte Limo sector, you still got the TDVL to fall back on.  

    If next door forum (TD) don't give you a satisfactory advice, you may open a thread here (limo forum) and we can give you the best advice we would.   Good Luck.
