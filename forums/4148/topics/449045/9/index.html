    Hi Bro 5M

    Well, the scope of business are wider for the Mercedes Limousine cabs as compared to normal cabs.

    1) Mercedes cabs - can Q at Limousine Qs at the AP. (arrival at $55 per trip before levy).

    2) Limousine MDT jobs (you can still get current normal jobs thru the same system).

    3) Hourly booking thru MDTs and Walky Grouping.

    Every taxis in Singapore can do street pickup, but with a limousine grouping (walky talkie talk group), the scope of business have widen to passengers willing to pay a premium fee for a more prestige ride.

    The daily rental is only slightly higher than normal cabs... but the fuel (diesel) consumptions for Mercedes Benz vehicles are better (lesser) than the Korean made taxis.

    Many TDs have seen a increased in their revenue and it is really up to individual, who want to convert into a limousine career.   If you basically sweep the floor with a limousine cab, then my advice is that you stay put with your normal cab driving.

    Good Luck.
