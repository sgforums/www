    Before you drive for him....

    Negotiate terms with potential hirer.  Day day or night shift.  Changing shift hours (4pm to 4am) or (5pm to 5am), or (6pm to 6am)....

    If you are driving night, go for 4pm to 4am... 

    Also, ask, if you are driving 7 days week, do you get any share of the bonus given by the company.  If no... then, drive 5-6 days week enough.... .  Take one day off.

    Wat is the point, sharing half his rental's liabilities and you don't get a share from the drivers' bonus ?

    Car washing is normally the duty for the night shift driver.  Which meant that you need to wash the cab, inside & outside (by yourself or by @ the kisok).  Day shift driver (normally the hirer) will be responsible for the cab's servicing @ office.

    Topping up diesel.  Normally, done individually.  Hirer topped up before his shift ends and you do vise visa.

    If you want to ends the driving agreement - do it on a amicable way... give a one week notice. 

    *** Also check for any dented parts already on the cab with the hirer before you take the keys and become his relief.
