    For GDI, (without allies, single player) employ a rotating defence of 3 squads of titans and wolverines so u can repair one while two units patrolthe base. One squad must contain a mix bag of at least 7 vehicles to be effective. This is useful when playing network games too.

    Fixed defences aren't effective as they can be easily overun but concentrating several in strategic locations against air assaults are important... walls are cheap and useful for slowing down approaching enemy units so build them whenever u can.

    In attack, try using combined arms to attack. Scouting out the openings are crucial so sacrifice fast units for this purpose. (u can use air units too)Once a general layout of the base has been established, use deception to distract them. ( if u have enuff orca bombers u can try taking out a vital structure in preparation for ur attack, not nesessarily a key installation)

    Amass one large force outside the chosen entrance (but this is not ur trump card) This is to serve as bait. Then draw out their mobile defences and attack them from the same entrance with a larger force once their defences hve been weakened. The trick is to go quickly for key structures; ignore all their vehicles (con yard and weapons factory) then fan out against their fixed and mobile defences. Titans are very useful here as they are hardy and reasonably inexpensive.

    As they are likely to hit out at ur attacking units with orca bombers, have some MLRS on standby to bring them down.

    Caution: If u're playing of pre-prepared land by the game, u're likely to only have enuff tiberium to try this out once. So make sure ur enemy does not get the drop on u.
