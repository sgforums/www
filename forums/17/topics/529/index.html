    Just for the records, there is no such thing as Singapore version of PS2. There are only 3 versions of PS2 in the market:
    1) NTSC - J (JAP)
    2) NTSC - U/C (US)
    3) PAL (UK)

    Those you see in SONY shops in Singapore are the JAP versions ($399).

    The US ones are above $400.

    PAL versions are seldom seen here and the cost is between JAP and US.
