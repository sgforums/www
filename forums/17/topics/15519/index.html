Singapore Unreal Tournament League (SUTL)

  • StoneCold
    26 May 01, 03:25
    This June, paGn will be doing the Singapore Unreal Tournament League (SUTL) at E-Games over 4 days. Only 10 Teams are allowed for this professional full scale league and they will play each and every other team in a round robin system.

    That means every team gets to play at least 9 games even if they lose any games.

    The registration fees are $100 for a Team of 4 players and up to 3 reserves (Reserves are optional and swappable between games). This cost barely covers the gameplay time (9 hours of play x $3 per hour = $27 per person) So your
    slightly getting a discount. To register, go to the league's official site at http://www.pagn.net/unreal/ctf_league/ and fill up the registration form and submit it to unreal@pagn.net and we'll make an arrangement with you to collect the fees.

    4 players from every team also get to play free in the MechWarrior 4 : Trial by Fire tournament as well

    Details at a glance:

    Prizes:

    1st $400 plus trophy
    2nd $200
    3rd $100

    Gametype: 4 vs 4 CTF

    Maps Used:
    1) Coret-CE
    2) Dreary-LE
    3) Face-LE
    4) November-CE
    5) Command-CE

    No. of players: 40 Players (10 Teams of 4)

    Location: E-Games is at 8 Grange Road #03-08 Cathay Cineleisure Orchard Singapore 239695

    Date: 4 Sundays in June, Starting from 3rd June

    1)Day1 3rd June
    2)Day1 10th June
    3)Day1 17th June
    4)Day1 24th June

    Time: 10AM to 3 PM on all days

    Registration fees: $100 for a Team of 4 players and up to 3 reserves (Reserves are optional)

    For more Information, check out : http://pagn.net/unreal
