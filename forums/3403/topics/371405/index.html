    Scams?

    Scams are when 1 man gets rich by making a fool of others -- as what happens daily in stock markets.

    Scams are seen in pyramid selling where there's no real product, but a case of money moving around in a first come, first to get paid basis, and where if you came in latest, good luck in finding more people to join under you.

    Scams are when there's no real business opportunity involved and the operations are non-transparent and can get a little bit shady reading about them.

    As a rough gauge, anything that promises huge gains for little investment in little time, it reeks of a scam.
