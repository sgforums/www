    Although the rebellion ended, the chaos brought by the rebellion, together with the natural disasters that strike China during that time, had already weaken the dynasty.

    Moreover, because of the rebellion, the local government have gained more military power as the local government need the troops to end the rebellion too.

    Soon, the emperor (Ling) passed away (leaving his 2 young sons, aged 14 and 9, and his wife) and the central government broke into 2 major factions. One consist of eunuchs and the other consist of court officials.

    During that time, chief general was He Jin and he sided with the court officials. He ordered Dong Zhuo to enter Luoyang(Capital) but before Dong Zhuo arrive, he was assassinated by eunuchs.

    His ally, Yuan Shao, led troops into Luoyang and try to kill all the eunuchs. In the chaos, the remaining eunuchs fled the capital together with the 2 sons but along the way they met Dong Zhuo and Dong Zhuo bring the 2 sons back to the palace.

    But when Dong Zhuo enter the capital, he turn it into a living hell as he massacre court officials and peasants. He also replace the current emperor with the younger one which make Yuan Shao fled as he diagree with Dong Zhuo's decision.

    The doings that Dong Zhuo did led to a coaltion force formed by the different lords with Yuan Shao as the leader. Dong Zhuo, who know of the coalition, move everyone in the capital to Chang An and razed the capital. His troops and him remain in Luoyang to stop the coalition forces

    Although alot of people are in the coalition force, none of them are willing to attack. Only Cao Cao attacked but he lost. In the end it was Sun Jian who defeated Dong Zhuo and Dong Zhuo fled. Eventually Dong Zhuo died under his own fellow subordinates, namely Li Su and Lu Bu.
