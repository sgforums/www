    Commercial vehicles to get 30% road tax rebate for one year starting July

    Owners of buses, goods vehicles, goods-cum-passenger vehicles and taxis will get a 30 per cent road tax rebate when they renew their road tax for the period July 2009 to June 2010.

    The rebate was first announced in the Budget Statement last January as part of the Governmentâ€™s relief measures for businesses during this economic downturn.

    Owners of commercial vehicles, buses and taxis will be able to renew their road tax based on the revised rates from Sunday .

    Those who have already paid based on the old rates, would have the excess amount automatically offset the tax payable at the next renewal.

    The public can visit www.onemotoring.com.sg for the revised road tax rates for buses, commercial vehicles and taxis.
