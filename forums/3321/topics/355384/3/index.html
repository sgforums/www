    trucks in this photo are the Nissan Cabstar (GP742A) & the Daihatsu (YK4646G)..

    here's a brief decription of a minor incident that happened to the white Daihatsu lorry on the 27th July 2009 at about 1830hrs..

    the field was super wet after a heavy downpour earlier & after the crew of the white Daihatsu lorry had finished their work of erecting the blue signboard, the driver wanted to drove off when its wheels got stuck (refer to the marks on the field)..no matter how hard the driver tried, the wheels sank deeper into the soggy field..

    few mins later, the Nissan Cabstar lorry arrived, one of the crew members tied a tow rope on the rear of the Cabstar which was attached to the front of the Daihatsu lorry in which to try to pull the Daihatsu lorry out but no luck..

    in the end, the crew assisted the help of some workers whom were doing roadworks nearby & "borrowed" their excavator in which the Daihatsu lorry was finally "rescued"..

    Driver injured after truck overturns at construction worksite

    SINGAPORE: A driver was injured after his sand dumper truck overturned on Friday evening.

    The incident took place at the Pan United Construction worksite, near the Jalan Bahar Flyover.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) said that the driver's legs were pinned under the collapsed dashboard of the cabin.

    SCDF rescuers had to use a hydraulic ramp and a spreader cutter to release the trapped driver.

    The driver was conscious when he was sent to the National University Hospital. He suffered a fracture in his lower left limb and injuries to his abdomen.
