Lorry rams into Lorong Ah Soo road divider

  • QX179R
    1 May 12, 17:37

    A lorry was reported to have knocked into a road divider along Lorong Ah Soo earlier this afternoon according to STOMPers tan j y, Robert Devasahayam and Rachel Durano Wan.

    STOMPer tan j y wrote:

    "I was at home in Lorong Ah Soo around 3.50pm today (May 01) when I heard a loud braking sound -- seemingly from a heavy vehicle.

    "When I went outside to have a look, I realized that the lorry had hit the divider.

    "No one was hurt and no other vehicles were damaged in the accident, though the driver was last seen speaking with personnel from the Traffic Poilice.

    "I feel the lorry may have been speeding due to the loud braking sound I heard earlier."

    STOMPer Robert Devasahayam wrote:

    "The lorry had done a U-turn, probably to avoid something, and crossed the divider.

    "This happened in Lorong Ah Soon -- opposite Block 125 -- at about 4.00pm today (May 01)."

    STOMPer Rachel Durano Wan wrote:

    "I was walking home along Lorong Ah Soo at about 5.10pm when I saw a large truck that was 'broken'.

    "It seemed to have crashed into the fence that seperates the two roads."
