Volvo FL wins Fleet Vehicle of the Year award

  • NoobBOY
    5 May 09, 21:13

    The Volvo FL has won the 2009 Fleet Vehicle Award in the Fleet Awards Polska Trucks category. The award was jointly organised by the Fleet Management Institute in Poland and the trade magazine Flota.

     

    Fleet Awards Polska aims at recognising the best fleet vehicles on the market, focusing on the opinions of fleet managers responsible for vehicle purchases in their companies. All the nominated vehicles were available for testing and votes were submitted on-line over a three-month period. The nominated trucks were the Iveco Eurocargo, Mercedes Benz Atego and Volvo FL, which took the first prize.

    Volvo Trucks has won the Fleet Vehicle of the Year award for the third time and the Volvo FL is the winner for the second consecutive year. In 2007 the trophy was awarded to the Volvo FH.

    "We are very pleased that our Volvo FL series received the Fleet Vehicle of the Year award. It proves that the Volvo Trucks offer is very strong in the segment of trucks for lighter applications as well, such as local and regional distribution," said Pontus Stenberg, General Manager of Volvo Truck Center Poland.
