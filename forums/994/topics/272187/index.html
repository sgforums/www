    Below are some helpful sites to help entrepreneurs or aspiring entrepreneurs to expand or start up their businesses respectively.

    SINGOV - Singapore News Publications
    http://www.gov.sg/

    eCitizen - Online Govt Services
    http://www.ecitizen.gov.sg/

    EnterpriseOne Information to Starting your business
    http://www.business.gov.sg/

    None-Residents - For people who wants to vist, Re-Locate, Work or Do Business in Singapore
    http://www.ecitizen.gov.sg/nonresidents/

    Spring Singapore - Web-site with rich contents on how to go about doing business
    http://www.spring.gov.sg/Content/HomePage.aspx

    Association of Small And Medium Enterprises - For Entrepreneurs
    http://www.asme.org.sg/

    International Enterprise Singapore - Helps you to either improve your local biz, and or to help in expansion of biz overseas
    http://www.iesingapore.gov.sg/wps/portal

    Singapore Land Authority - Enquire on Rental of Office Premises etc..
    http://www.sla.gov.sg/htm/hom/index.htm

    Economic Development Board - Stay updated with news of Businesses
    http://www.edb.gov.sg/edb/sg/en_uk/index.html

    Intellectual Property Office of Singapore - Resgistry of Trademarks, Patents and Designs
    http://www.ipos.gov.sg/main/index.html

    CCS (Competition Commission Singapore) - Site with information on business law mergers etc
    http://www.ccs.gov.sg/

    Energy Market Authority - Get your Energy, Gas Supplies
    http://www.ema.gov.sg/

    Public Utilities Board - Water Supply and Water Related Links
    http://www.pub.gov.sg/home/index.aspx

    National Environment Agency
