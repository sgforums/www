    Hi Jason: Since this is a new Forum and you have yet to post any rules...

    Guess I might help a bit.

    Posting Rules:

    No Advertisements Allowed.

    Sharing of experiences and ideas will be good to help cultivate and promote entrepreneurship in Singapore. (Going in line with what Govt encourage)

    -No Repeated/Double Posting on the same Topic.

    -No Lame Replies/ Pictures.

    -No Offensive/ Harsh Comments... (Everyone is learning)

    -No +1 or NPNT or similar thread replies.

    -No Singlish, No *$#&!! Or *** to represent vulgarities.

    -No Spammings

    -No Scammings

    -No Selling of products/services of your own business

    -No Recruitment Thread for MLM Purpose

    No Flaming, nor Saying black name an individual/company without sufficient evidence and reasons nor advertising. (Example: A is a company whereby they lie cheat and pressure customers to buy the products or they can't leave the company; B is a company that you must go to this company etc.. there's Chio Bu as the staff there...)

    This forum is a place where we should help one-another to learn and grow in terms of ideas to cultivate entrepreneurship in each and everyone of us.

    This is a learning ground for every individual who's keen to make a positive change in their lives...

    Ideas should be categorized into different sections.

    Example:

    Subject:

    1) Fashion
    2) Home-Based Business
    3) Beauty
    4) Wellness
    5) Designing
    6) Import; Export; Trading
    7) Motor-Vehicles

    Than must state the area which you want to chat/discuss about.

    Example:

    Subject:

    1) Fashion - Shirt
    2) Fashion - Shoes
    3) Home-Based Business - Printing
    4) Designing - Wedding Cards
    5) Design - Clothes
    6) Entrepreneurship - Creating your own Label (For aspiring entrepreneurs-to-be)

    Note: Pictures that are allowed are those pictures who can help each of us to put on our thinking cap or something similar... Example like those inspirational cards/ posters sold in popular etc...
