    Autopsy Photo – Insides of the Skull with Brain Removed

     

    Ever wondered what the insides of the skull looks like when the brain is removed? Well, the ages old question is answered in the autopsy photo above. I know you would naturally expect there to be a hole for the spine, but it’s not until you see a photo taken from this viewpoint when you realize that the hole looks like washroom drain. This is the type of view all of us who don’t work as morticians are deprived of.

    From the condition of her skin, I’m judging the victim is a woman in her 30′s. I don’t know any more details regarding her death – why she’s dead, what killed her, where it took place, etc. If anyone knows please share. So far the best autopsy photo I’ve seen. Mot other photos are taken from other angles, never really exposing the insides of the skull in such clear way. They sure cut them up well during autopsy, eh?
