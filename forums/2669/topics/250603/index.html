18 girls made to strip as school punishment

  • [K]iyoshi
    25 Apr 07, 19:24
    April 25, 2007

    HUMILIATION of the highest degree.

    That's what 18 female students at the Institute Kemahiran Belia Negaraa in Kuala Terengganu had to undergo last Thursday.

    They were ordered to remove their bras and panties by two female instructors, reported The New Straits Times (NST).

    The forced stripping was said to be punishment for a fellow trainee's disobedience.

    The girls, who were enrolled for a course in beauty treatment, alleged that they were also made to stand for four hours and listen to abuses hurled at them.

    According to a trainee who wanted to remain anonymous, they were called into class at 8am to answer charges that another student had left the institute without permission a day earlier.

    She said: 'The instructors told us that they had received orders from their superiors to instil discipline in us. They confiscated our handphones before instructing us to remove our clothing. They become furious when we didn't follow their orders.'

    Another student, who did not want to be named told Harian Metro: 'They called us pigs and dogs over a problem we were not even involved in.

    'Of course we were ashamed. When we protested, the instructors threatened they would do something worse.'

    She added that they had no choice but to comply. They got more humiliated when male trainers passed the class they were in.

    NST added that some parents of the affected students lodged police reports after hearing of the incident.

    District police chief superintendent Hud Abdul Rahman confirmed a number of police reports had been lodged.

    Meanwhile, the institute's service faculty head Ms Rubiah Kassim said the incident was a result of a 'communication breakdown between me and my staff'.

    She said: 'It was not our intention to humiliate the trainees and I admit the trainers had gone a little overboard.'

    She added that the institute had been facing disciplinary problems from the students, from drinking liquor to lewd behaviour. 'We had to do something about it.'
