Baby girl with heart problem needs RM30,000

    Che Basah Watih with her daughter Siti Zalina Mat Isa

    GEORGE TOWN: She has a cute nose and big brown eyes, but 6-month-old Siti Zalina Mat Isa is small for her age.

    And because she has a serious heart condition, she suffers frequent chest infections.

    She needs surgery to correct a hole in the heart and a leaking valve, which will cost RM30,000.

    Her parents, from Kuala Ketil, Kedah, are unable to pay for the surgery.

    Her father Mat Isa Hamid, 58, is a rubber tapper who earns RM500 a month.
    Her mother Che Basah Watih, 40, had to quit her job at a rubber factory to look after her.

    The couple has five other children, aged from 10 to 21.
