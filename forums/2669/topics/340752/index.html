    Paranormal Encounters @ Work

    I used to work in this bldg somewhere near Jurong East area..Within the International Park area..It is normal for some for us to work into late late @ night & we are kinda used to it..

    But the problem is..NO ONE is near that photocopier @ all..How come the zapping won't stop? We stared @ one another, grab our bags & run off immediately..(w/o even switching off the lights or whatever)

     

    By the time we reach downstairs (while waiting for a cab)..we noticed that the lights on our floor was off! =.='''

     

    The next morning when we went back to office..another colleague was already there..so we asked if she did saw alot of papers in the copier (Since there was so much zapping noise generated from the copier yesterday. Hence, we assume that there will be alot of papers being generated).

     

    BUT, our colleague said no.

     

    In actual fact, the copier was spoilt for the whole of yesterday!!
