    This is my ghost story....

     

    I was nine and sleeping sideways on my bed this night. I also had a big blanket on, so big till it touched the floor. There seemed to be a finger lifting it from below. Wah lau eh, it was enough to make all hair on my body stand. Then I lifted the blanket, peeked below and shouted, who's there?? No one was there....I just ran to my parents room and slept there the next few nights.
