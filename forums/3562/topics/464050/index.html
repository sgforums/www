    Experience the evolutionary sounds of Chinese classical music with Hong Kong Chinese Orchestra (HKCO) Huqin Principals, Hsin Hsiao-ling (gaohu), Hsin Hsiao-hung (erhu), Liu Yang (zhonghu) and Tung Hiu-lo (gehu).

     

    Venture into a world of musical possibility as the four virtuosi perform works such as The Land of My Home by acclaimed Chinese composer Zhou Chenglong, Ran Shao Xi Wang by Robert Zollitsch, and more. Featured in this recital are pieces specially composed for the eco-huqin—an innovation in traditional bowed instruments that replaces python skin with synthetic sound membrane—showcasing their harmonious tonal colours.
