    Hahaha..no guys..ok..here goes..

    AVI is the acronym for Animals & LiveStock set by IATA. Dogs, cats, horses, Fishes( AVI.F),birds are all labelled AVI n has to conform to a AVA clearance. Also AVI need some TLC from handlers n crew and are usually loaded in the bulkhold of aircraft where there is ventilation & thermostat control to keep them alive.
