    SINGAPORE : Four airlines have agreed to the Civil Aviation Authority of Singapore's (CAAS) offer to move their operations to Changi Airport's new Terminal 3.

    They are China Eastern Airlines, Jet Airways, Qatar Airways and United Airlines.

    They will join Singapore Airlines at the new terminal, which will open on January 9.

    CAAS said the move will optimise the usage of airport capacity and facilities. - CNA /ls
