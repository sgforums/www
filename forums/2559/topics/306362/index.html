    A380 to fly Singapore-London route from March 18

    SINGAPORE: Singapore Airlines will be flying its third Airbus A380 to London from 18 March.

    This is the first such commercial service to Europe for an A380.

    The first two aircraft have been flying the Singapore-Sydney route since October 2007.

    SIA has also confirmed that there will be an additional 17 A380s to join the existing three.

    Seats on A380 services are open for sale and those interested can purchase tickets online at     www.singaporeair.com - CNA/vm
