    Naga is my favourite hero man! LoL. He can like.. counter alot of spells if you know how to time them. For example. anyone throws a stun spell on you, like storm bolt, you can just mirror. And if cm casting freezing field, just cast sleep. the net also got a small stun, so it can also counter tp scrolls and other chanelling spells.

    For me. She is a highly hard to use properly hero but extremely fun to play with.

    Usually i buy ror in the begining and a flask, then get 2 bracers. My reasoning is that in the early game, Naga cannot really do much damage, all she can do is survive and farm. I will proceed to buy diffusal as the feed back adds alot of damage and gives you another spell to chase. Next but a health orb to further increase your health, preparing for manta for even more damage.

    After that its just looking at the situation. If there are many stunners, casters or whatever, just buy bkb. If not, go for bashers! Haha.. Though the images can't bash, you can. One bash from you = 8 more hits, including images of course.

    For killing heros that have windwalk or similar annoying spells, go near them and not let them see you yet. Walk out and immediately sleep them. Then you image and wait. The sleep makes him immune to all damages and spells for the duration of the sleep(Nerfed from previously versions, previously, the starting few seconds of the sleep have immunity, which leaves you a clean safe space to net him without him having a chance to ww before you net). So after he wakes up, instantly net him. And whack. usually bone would just die after a few crits.

    To sum this all up! NAGA OWNS!
