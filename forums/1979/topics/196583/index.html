    -ap = All Pick (You can pick your hero of your choice)

    -ar = All Random (Everyone's hero are chosen at random)

    -tr = Team Random (Everyone's hero are chosen at random, but only from the taverns of their sides)

    -id = Item Drop mode (When every a hero dies, he or she has a chance of droping one of his or her items from invetory)

    -dm = Death Match (Everytime you die, you pick a new hero and if a team dies 36 times they lose)

    -random Random (You get YOUR[Only You] Hero At Random)

    -sh = Same hero mode (The host picks one hero at random or his choice and everyone in both team gets the same fella.)

    -sm = changed to -em (Same as -em) (NOT IN USED ANYMORE)
