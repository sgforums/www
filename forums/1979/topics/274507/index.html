    DOTA COUNTRY RANKING - by your votes

    copied from dotaguide.blogspot.com

    The ranking results as of 17 Aug 07 stands in this pecking order:

    1st Philippines 25%
    2nd Singapore 24%
    3rd Malaysia 22%
    4th Indonesia 11%
    5th Romania 3%

    It is heartening to know that there are at least one votes per country that has been listed by www.DOTATIP.com. For any readers that feel that your country should be listed, please vote under "others" and drop a comment or email me to the details of your country.

    As this is not a vote in terms of proficiency (which, I feel, is very hard to gauge, even if we analyse results from the cybergames as they only represent a very small percentile of the population that is playing DOTA All-Stars, and so what if a few gamers from a country is good? Why would we want to know that?) The more important point of this survey is to gather everyone in the DOTA community all over the world to give a show of support for their country in terms of DOTA popularity. Isn't it to nice to know that you have many other gamers from your home country that enjoys the game and shows it as well?

    Results to be updated again at end August 07.
