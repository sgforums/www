    My item build for Enchantress would be:

    Bracer
    Bracer
    Threads
    MoM
    Scythe
    Linken Sphere

    The 2 bracers add 288 HP and 6 damage. Insignificant during late game, but very helpful in early game. 288 HP makes a difference when you're facing a level 6 Witch whereby an Impale and Finger of Death can take you out almost immediately.

    If there are no nukers, skip the bracer and go for MoM straight. I use MoM 'cos I pump the first two skills. Auto-cast Impetus, cast 2nd skill (slow) on target, activate MoM and he's dead, unless he stuns or hexes you. Scythe is very useful in mid-game to late game. Adds INT and quite a lot of damage as Enchantress is an INT hero.

    Linken Sphere nullifies the first spell cast on you, which can determine life or death during a gangbang on you since:

    They're slowed by 90% when they attack you
    You can hex one of them with the Scythe
    You can cast slow on another

    If the game is not over by then, get a MKB or Burize for more damage =)
