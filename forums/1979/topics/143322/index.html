This is not in black in white as to the radius of how far you need to be to not get banned for TPing into their base, as usual, mods will have full discretion when deciding, don't be stupid about it.     I've been thinking and reading the various tactics to counter backdoorers.. and i'll try out a couple of them when i get an opportunity once again.. but in case any of you want some ideas.. the key points of defending a backdoor are:

    1) True Sight. (for obvious reasons)
    2) Disabler. (to make sure the backdoorer pays dearly for his silly act)
    3) Stealth (this is an optional skill which has plenty of advantages... allows you to backdoor enemy as well)
    4) Teleportation ability (If your hero cannot naturally blink/tp.. BoT, Scroll of TP and even Dagger helps)
    5) BKB (optional but somewhat essential especially since the backdoorer would have to be super pumped to have the confidence to backdoor)
    6) Teamwork (the most important of all.. make sure someone's defending when the team attacks)
    7) Radiance (if you can afford.. it helps to farm while defending as well)

    I should think there's more to it but that's what i've come up so far.. For heroes that would make perfect anti-backdooring..

    1) Furion (Sprout and Treants are pretty damn effective)
    2) Crazy Nuker (Luna, Rylai, Lion, Lina etc. Lich would NOT be an effective choice since his Ult neeeds to bounce around)

    I left techies out for a reason.. he's an excellent defender and farmer.. but at the late stage.. putting him in charge of defending the base isn't a very great idea.. I would rather use him to suicide push because it's extremely difficult for such a slow character with low damage to actually defend.. The best way he can do so.. is stun ward the entire base.. it's much more effective than mining the place especially if you're up against heroes with crazy regen rates..
