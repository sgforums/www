    =.=

    Tournament warlock users usually solo at mid lane.

    Startin it would have tangos and iron wood branches. its a good solo hero. should be able to contain any hero even if its 2v1 against u. Do not run lanes for kills. concentrate on farming till u get preserverance and a boots. U should be ard level 11 by now with lvl 2 of infernal.

    Now its time for your team mates TO RUN YOUR LANE. NOT u run their lane. They will come and help u gang mid. U drop your infernal and its 100% chance for your team to get kills even if there is 2 heroes at mid, u should still get a kill. After killing, they go back to their lanes, u should stay and farm till u get refresher by lv 14.

    By the time u reach lvl 14, get rdy to push with your team. Warlock is a good pusher at lv 14 because it has refresher already. U should be able to push till their base. Wait for your ulti and refresher cd, den push their base tower again. 100% win game if your teammates and you have good team work. By lv 16, u should have Boots, Refresher, Guinsoo. Get a boots of travel too if u could farm. When clash, shadow word is prefered to heal your fighters. etc, SF, Viper, Moprhling.

    Warlock work well in this combo.
    Warlock, Zeus, Veno, SF, Rk.
    Never choose warlock in tournaments first.
    Always draft QoP, if its taken, den draft either a viper or warlock.
    Always choose zeus in your 2nd turn. Else its taken. Base on my tournaments
    games and clan matches. 3rd try to get veno. NoVa + warth works extremely good. Veno also works well if almost any combo. Last 2 choices r choosing a stunner and a finisher depending who u wanna choose first.
