    Slayer

    early game get a ring of regen, 1 flask and 1 clarity.
    learn the 2 nuke skills, with the dragon slave as the priority. Why? Because its hard to aim with the stun as it takes some time before you can see the effect, its useful for long distance spam too. microing takes some time to get used to, since she attacks slowly but her damage is quite high. get blink dagger and boots of speed afterwards.
