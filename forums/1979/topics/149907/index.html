    3 Feb 06, 05:32
    TS definately needs Soul Booster and Preserverance.

    He needs lots of hp (to survive nukes) and lots of mana (to spam diabolic & ult).

    As TS, your main goal (early game) is to max out Diabolic Edict & lightning asap. Then wonder around the rivers and try to kill those low hp enemies 1 v 1. If the enemies wise up and travel in groups, then too bad.

    Diabolic Edict can also be used on towers.

    If nothing cocks up (provided the enemies not too pro), then use the gold to upgrade to BoT and Guinsoo.

    Basically, TS is an awesome farmer, if used correctly.
