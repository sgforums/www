    seen the previous topic about him, had tried him out several times, all i can say that he's the best ks-er, gd farmer, pusher and one of the best late game hero. here's my mini guide.

    items to get:
    PT
    Y&S
    Radiance
    Hyper
    *Butterfly
    *Divine

    *usually, game alr ended be4 u got these items.

    skills:
    ww lvl1
    max lance
    max jux
    max ww
    max stats
    PE when available

    early game, try to get RoH asap. dun buy those 'petty' items. go for RoH immediately. den slowly get ur PT and Y&S. note to take, be very very very caution in early game. nv go solo lane unless the lane only got 1 enemy and he's not very gd. always try to get the last hits. ks if possible

    by mid game, u may still building for ur Y&S. DO NOT take part in any heads-on (ganging ppls is fine), unless the opp have pushed into your base. go and find a lane which is suitable for farming. take the creeps if no choice. by now, ur illusions shld be popping our freely.

    late game, just do what u're supposed to do.
