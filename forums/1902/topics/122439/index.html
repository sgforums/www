    Sudden but the truth, one of my ninja hamsters has just passed away with no fore sign that it'll happen....

    She's the most active one, like to travel and escape and even dig a big hole to go underneath of the box she is in. We have to place 2 to prevent her from escaping.

    There was one time when I feed her... her food left untouched....
    later we found out that she has jumped off the very tall tank.... started her journey exploring...

    I dunno how she escape. Later then I found out she jump from the roof top of her hut to the top of the water bottle, then climb upwards. If she failed, she will try again and again and again till she was tired. After that, she repeated again n again.

    There was once... i suddenly saw her on her hind legs, curiously looking around ...

    do you know where she was then? make a guess...

    She was standing on my dirty clothes in my laundry basket, which was right under the table that held her cage. That's about 1 meter height away

    Just yesterday, she was in her usual underground hideout... when I stood there with a banana, cld see her underneath the top box, slightly compressed and moving toward the big hole she has created to resurface ... Then I was laughin that she looked n moved like a crocodile.....

    There are so much memories she left behind....
