    Photos from this dive site - Ko Bon West Ridge. Ko Bon is famous for its Manta Ray sighting...... big beautiful stingless Ray that glide through the water.... but as my 'ASS' luck have it..... no Manta Ray sighting when I was there..... sucks


    The great sunrise from outside my cabin.... we were traveling to Ko Bon from Ko Tachai, where we spent the night in the bay.



    The price of being too fat to wear a 'full' wetsuit. Cuts on my legs.... coral, sand and some sea anenome sting...... painful lah


    Big Star Puffer under a rock. No joke..... damn big.


    Another Spotfin Lionfish..... my fave type of lionfish. Smaller then the common lionfish, but with more beautiful fins......


    Big schools of Glass Fish aka Golden Sweeper. Only about 3-4cm long. But the whole school is damn large. This shot is a close-up. When I was still at dive site - Richelieu Rock - , the school of Glass Fish is so 'thick', my DiveMaster have to use his hands to 'sweep' the fish aside inorder for us to photo things behind the fishes......


    Banded Sea Snake...... common in Asia. Have seen it in but Tioman & Aur in Malaysia..... also Manado in Indonesia. And yes, it is venomous...


    A small fish called Leopard Blenny hiding inside some hard coral.


    2 Peaco-ck Grouper


    2 White Collar Butterflyfish..... sorry, 'ASS' shot again.


    Can you see the Scorpionfish
