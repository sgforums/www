    Hi.... just came back from my Perhentain dive trip. Perhentain island is really 2 island close together...... Know as Perhentain Big & small. Located on the East coast of Malaysia, in the state of Terengganu. We were staying at the 'BIG' island.....






    As you can see.... nice white sandy beaches + clear waters......

    Sadly, we were there too ealy into the diving season..... the visibility under water is not good..... on the avg only 5-7 metres. Compair to Similan (thailand) or Manado (indoneasia) visibility of 25-40 metres.... it suxs. Also not much good stuff to see. Hope you guys/gals enjoy the photos.
