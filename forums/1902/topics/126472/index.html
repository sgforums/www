    Dear stormax,

    You will actually be surprised to learn how easy is it to maintain a healthy relationship between you and your partner.

For example, you will never suspect whether you significant other is seeing other people behind your back. Why? This is because you trust her. Of course, trust is easier said than done. And where better to get recognition and respect? From your love ones of course. In addition, the feeling of being stifled will certainly not result in a healthy relationship.

    With trust, you can minus the suspicions, jealousy, and hatred in any relationships. With the negative feelings removed from any relationships, more time can be spent showering your partner with more positive feelings like care and concern. With care and concern, the both of you will communicate more. Communication is the key to a healthy relationship.

    Healthy relationship is not limited to the above only. If your partner choose to spend his/her life with you, then it is your responsibilty to take care of her, to provide for her, to make sure that she loves everyday in happiness. You shall never make her sad.

    Learn well, and you will never see the needs to ask whether are you in a healthy relationship again.
