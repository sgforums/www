    Yoz so happy to see so many pple interested in planting . Erm well as an advice for pple trying to grow plants , please don't squeeze too many into one pot bcos they need nutrients . And by combining so many plants together in one pot , they have to fight for it which is very unhealthy as some of them are bound to be undernourish . Actually there are ways to boost their growing speed that is by using commercial chemicals to make the soil release nutrients faster , but that will also cause the soil to lose their nutirents faster so you need to fertilize them more often. As for pple trying to grow cherry tomatoes , thsi plant need alot of space to grow and to grow roots so as to ensure enough nutrients and most impt in all plants , sunlight so that they will produce the cherry tomatoes . You need very strong sunlight for veggy type of plant otherwise it may not grow fruit and even if they grow , they will be smaller then normal .
    i myself have tried growing many types of veggy plants for example ; hulu ( bottle gourd ) , japanese cucumber , corn , big and cherry tomatoes chilli padi , cauliflower , kang kong , tua cang , spearmint and currently cotton plant with much success all thanks from my old vege farmer advice . He taught me to use chicken manure as fertilizer and commercial chemicals , and most impt of all is the type of soil used . To grow veggies , black most soil is the best , don't use clay type those are meant for cactus .
    Would appreciate it if someone could teach me how to put pictures into sg forums then i will show you all some of my plants . And by the way if anyone wants seeds for white carrot , red carrot , cotton , chilli padi very small type can contact me . i bought alot of seeds but n chance to use them cause not enuff sunlight to grow some of them . Veggies need alot of sunlight to grow , the best is direct sunlight from the sun esp for cauliflower , need more then 10hrs of direct sunlight to grow to market size and not to forget fertilizer . Chicken manure is the best if you find iut too smelly then if you drink tea or kopi , just throw the remaining leaves or residue into your flowerpot they are slow release organic fertilizer and when you wash rice , the white milky water is also good for your plants .
