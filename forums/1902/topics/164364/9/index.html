    Firstly i want to ask how to use paintbrush to resize pictures . Then to the question you all have to the best of my knowledge , actually stem cutting is also called narcotting . Its is where you use a knife to scrape off the bark of the plant then you wrap some damp soil ard the cutting using either plastic bag or coconut husk . If you use coconut husk to wrap then u need to water it more frequently because water will flow out of it . And i will advise you to water the narcotting part untill you can see roots ard the soil if you use clear plastic bag to wrap it . Or maybe about 1 month then you cut the stem beneath the wrapping then open up the wrapping very carefully so as to ensure not to break the roots inside . Then you plant the whole thing into another pot with the roots intact of course so that it is able to suck water from the roots to maintain itself . The plant may wilt off but it doesn't mean it has diead , let it stay in the pot and continue to water it for a month . If you are lucky , it may grow a new shoot from its stem and behold you have a very healthy new plant . At least that happen to my cotton . It was actually broken by my sister kids while they were playing ard it . I cut it off , trim it by cutting it 45 degrees then put it into a cut bottle to let it suck water the same way as you would put flower into a vase . For my cotton plant i soak it for about 4 months before the roots grow out . When i plant it into my pot , it struggle to survive pathetically before growing a new stem grow out of the old crooked n bent stem , then now is a very beautifull bloming cotton plant better then its first sprout from the seed. The fertilizer that you say got many different colour in pellet form is actually quick release agent for your soil to release its nutrient faster . And of course you must replenish the actual fertilizers into the soil more often . And the type of " shit " best for soil is actually 1st chicken , 2nd goat then i thnk is cow . Chicken " shit " is the best . It is not advisable to use carnivore shit . because it may still have chunks of meat inside which take a long time to decompose and will attract alot of pest .
