    This is aphids



    This is mealy bugs:


    Photo by a_splash

    Is your chilli plant placed in a not-so-sunny spot?

    If it's mealy bugs, you can try to remove them with a damp tissue or soft brush, and place the plant in a more sunny spot. However, if the plant is overly-infected, then it may not work.

    The other method is to use a little dishwashing liquid (diluted with water), to wipe them off - my neighbour tried this on her pandan leaves and it seemed to work.

    Good luck.
