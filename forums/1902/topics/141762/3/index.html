    Marine Park

    Marine Park is suitable for snorkeling and scuba diving. It is an area of the sea zoned as a sanctuary for the coral reef community. The coral reef community is considered as possibly the most productive eco-system in the world, with its diversity of aquatic flora and fauna. Coral reefs are also important breeding and nursery grounds for many commercially important species of marine organisms and fish. Marine Park Tioman is located at Tanjung Mesoh (about 15 min from Tekek jetty). There are about 6 shipwrecks at Marine Park. Max depth is about 23m. The visibility is often poor due to muddy bottom but it is really worthwhile as you have a chance to feed the fish and you will be surrounded by varieties of fish.
