    Bahara Rocks (Tokong Bahara)

    Located at the southwest of Tioman. Average depth is 15m and the maximum depth is about 18m. About 20-30 min from Genting village. You will be able to find various pelagic species as well as local reef fish. Numerous species of prolific marine life abound in the richness of this coral reef and mini drop-off. Bahara rock is one of the best dive sites.
