    Outlook for 2006:

    Career
    For the Dragon this 2006, there may not be much opportunity for windfalls or huge financial gains. Plus, there may be more than one rainy day in store for the Dragon, so do spend your money very wisely and keep a chunk of it in the bank. Other than this, your success can only come if you work on everything from scratch by yourself. You may not be able to receive assistance from others to help you out.

    Though this is the case, Dragons who are in creative industries such as writers, designers, copywriters and artists should have no problem in the year of the Fire Dog! As a general rule, though the Dragon's wealth luck is weak, his career luck is on a high..

    Relationship
    The Dragon in a committed relationship may find some strain due to the clash between the Dragon and the Dog. But, this is more likely for couples in the initial stages of getting to know each other, rather than those who have been together for a long time. The Dragon also needs to practice plenty of patience and restraint - there is a possibility of the Dragon succumbing to his volatile ways and wreaking all kinds of havoc! One way to solve this dilemma is for the couple to spend a little time apart. Sometimes, absence makes the heart grow fonder.

    For the solo Dragon, it may be very difficult for you to meet up with someone that clicks perfectly with you, due to the negative arrangement of certain stars.

    Health
    Because of the clash between the Dragon and the Dog in year 2006, the Dog person may be susceptible to sensitive or weak stomachs, chests and rib-cage areas. Do be careful of what you eat, and stay away from unhygienic eating outlets. You may also risk accidents from high-octane activities such as rock climbing and sky diving.
