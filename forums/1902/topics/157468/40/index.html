    Healing the spirit

    IS IT proper for a doctor or a nurse to proselytise at work?

    The discussion cropped up following newspaper reports.

    vodka7up sums up the sentiments of many when he says that doctors should 'just cure patients of their bodily pains or illnesses'.

    Food rule feud

    MOM Teresa thinks the ban of durians on public transport should be extended to cooked food as well.

    HappyH agrees, and says that sometimes commuters do not just take food onto trains and buses but eat it, too.

    Condemner however pooh-poohs Mom Teresa's suggestion. 'I'm sure one day you will be thankful that there is not such a stupid rule. Otherwise, how are you going to tar-pau good food home? Take the taxi every time? If you are super rich, then OK lah.'

    Humans, he says, can sometimes stink more than durians and food combined. What would happen if people were to board a bus in sweaty, smelly clothes?
