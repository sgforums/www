    Records say that the first people arrived on easter Island around AD400. legend has it that there were 2 groups of people on the island: the Long Ears and the Short Ears. The Long Ears were the rich who made their ears grow long by hanging heavy weights in them. They ruled over most of the population, including the Short Ears.

    They forced the Short Ears to build the statues. Archaelogists believe that the statues were intended to be monuments for the dead. Other thinks that they were used to remind the Short Ears of the Long Ears' power and amke them fear their rulers.

    According to morden research, the exsisting Short Ears are Polyneians, like the inhabitants of other Pacific islands. One theory suggests that the Long Ears came from South America. Another theory suggest that the Long Ears were also Polynesians.

    Saw this documentary on Easter island.

    People believed in ancestor worship, hence the huge face of the dead leaders. But to move these stones from the quarry need a lot of trees... something easter island lacked.

    Easter island used to be covered with trees and a haven for migratory birds who will rest in the canopies of these trees. Easter island inhabitants diet consist of birds as food.

    however to build these huge statues, the cut down all these trees... erosion resulting in bad harvest, no birds hence no meat, and no tree means they are stuck on the island. Mass famine, and war. The more they make these statues, they do not work. (sound like earth now...)

    When the westerners arrived, they brought disease unknown to these people resulting them to die off.

    Btw, these statues have white eyes on them. Scientist found out.
