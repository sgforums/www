    Giant Euro-wasp invasion warning



    People are being warned to watch out for huge wasps from Europe that have started to inhabit parts of Essex, Norfolk and Suffolk.

    Their football-sized nests are being found in garden shrubs and bushes and can contain up to 20,000 insects.

    The "super wasps" belong to a strain which grows to more than twice the size of the native British species.

    Wasps spread across the country when a female leaves the nest and finds a safe place to hibernate through the winter.
