    Try very diluted copper sulphate solution (5 flakes for 2 gallons pail). Spray on the plants or pour into soil sparingly. Keep the insects from eating the roots or shoots.

    Hope this help.

    PS Chili padi seeds need to be treated in vinegar or mild HCL or H2SO4 before planting to be successful.
