    Enhancement of SMRT bus service 925 from 17 July 2005

    Starting Sunday, 17 July 2005, the route of SMRT Service 925 will be amended to facilitate travel to and from the farms in Kranji on Sundays and Public Holidays.

    The service, which will be extended from Sungei Buloh Wetland Reserve to loop at Lim Chu Kang Lane 3, will operate at an average frequency of 45 minutes from Woodlands Regional Interchange (please refer to the attached poster for details). Posters with the approximate arrival times of the service are displayed at bus stops along the route.

    This service extension is introduced in support of Kranji Countryside Association's efforts to promote Kranji as a recreational and educational venue for commuters.

    Commuters may contact Transitlink Hotline at 1800- 2255663 or SMRT Customer Relations Centre at 1800- 3368900 for further information about the service.
