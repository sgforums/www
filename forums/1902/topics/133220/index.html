    Yup, take care not to stock up your tank too quickly... for sensitive fishes try running the filter on an empty tank of water for a month before adding the first fish.. to allow the nitrifying bacteria to build up in the tank first.

    If possible, set up a separate tank as a quarantine tank.. any new arrival goes into the quarantine tank for ~ 1 week, as any latent disease the fish may be carrying will manifest itself in this one week. Transfer the new arrival to your main tank only if it has proved to be healthy and feeding by the end of the week.
