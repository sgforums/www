Australian beach shore waves were all turned into bubble

  • Phoebie
    25 Oct 07, 16:13


    Australia New South Wales state Namuyamba coastline has become a "
    Berry coffee" in the oceans, well up from the sea shore the waves were all turned into a bubble and fell under the shore vacationers and buildings. This strange phenomenon has not brought about any danger, and have actually added to the visitors behind the fun, especially children. It seems like everybody is in great enjoyment in the bathtub bubble-bath. People holding hands will bubble up and then watch them wind plume.

    It was understood that similar scene 30 years ago has happened. Scientists explained that the bubble is from the river because of the substantial outflow of fresh water into the sea, and sea-borne substances, such as chemicals, the death of plants, fish and rotting seaweed excreta mixed form.
    In addition, the region is in the strong airflow under the influence, from the New South Wales and Queensland coast north of the airstream formed on the surface of the giant waves that promote bubble rushed shore. When the waves toward the shore, the water quickly so that bubble upward rotation, and gathered on the surface, then created a "bubble waves." The waves, the greater the greater the number of bubbles.

    Scientists said that since the larger waves, the bubble will not be immediately dissipated. Only when the waves decreases, the bubble will gradually dissipated.
