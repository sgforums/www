    Travel by bus details

    Board from City Square (Can take SBS bus 160/ 170 from Causeway, ez-link fare $0.65 alight at CIQ there and walk to City Square), take those buses that have plates indicating "Kota Tinggi", "Johor Jaya", "Ulu Tiram"..     JUSCO is aft Carrefour along Fed Rt 3... After the now under construction Pasir Gudang Highway/ Fed Rt 3 flyover interchange... Beside JUSCO itself is a Pro - Jet Petrol Station

    When going back, board those buses with plates "Larkin", take till Larkin and transfer to SBS 170... Remember, pay by CASH! RM1.30 (Cos Card is S$1.15)
