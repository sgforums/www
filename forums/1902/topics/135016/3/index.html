    Different Symptoms of Sleeping Problems

    Falling Alseep Disorders: You have difficulty falling aslp if it takes you 30mins to an hr or even longer.

    Waking in Middle of Night: If you wake up more than twice and find it hard to get back to sleep, you have a problem. Usually afflicts older age group.

    Waking up Very Early: If you wake up more than 2hrs before you planned to and cant get back to slp, you fall under tis category. Usually happens to older pple and id often alleviated if you go to bed earlier.

    Duration of Sleeping Problems

    Temporary Sleeping Problem: Lasts afew days. Usually you are a good, healthy sleeper but problems tend to occur when your circumstances change, such as travelling or when working hours become irregular.

    Short Term Sleeping Problem: Lasts 1 to 3 weeks. Usually occurs when you are ill or stressed.

    Long Term Sleeping Problem: Lasts a month or more. It's then time to consult your doctor.
