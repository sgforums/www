    *Cyber Hi* to Kenashi...

     

    I believe for budget airlines, the earlier you get/book your tickets, the cheaper it will be so if you are planning to go in Aug, you should be already booking your air tickets now la... (*~*)

     

    For reference, i booked mine in late Feb and together with Hotel Accommodation [the boutique hotel is quite new too, in business only Jan 2007], i am paying a total of SGD479/=.  Of cos this will not include spending money like food, shopping, as what the others had shared on this thread...

     

    There are actually lots to do in Bangkok other than shopping [of cos main attraction is shopping la].  Food, massages and tailoring is good too.

     

    Regardless, if anyone keen on shopping/food/tailor tips, i can recommend, not alot though, but definitely a few lah.

     

    Tailoring on replicas is especially good eg. if you have a favorite pair of pants, shirt or dress, you just need to bring it to the tailor and you'll see, apart from different material, print and color, exactly a same piece staring at your face at the end of the day!  The last i went, in May last year, i tailored a male's pants for less than SGD50 and a man's shirt (2-thread fold) for less than SGD30 and mind you, for this price you pay, you are not getting inferior quality that you can find at "pasar malams"...

     

    A foot massage, with occasional shoulder/neck massage thrown in, costs only THB199...

     

    For ladies, a complete set of manicure/pedicure nail art [meaning all 10 fingers and toe nails] costs only THB399 when back here, you'll probably need to pay SGD140 for same...

     

    Hope this information will help in your decision, :-0

    DAT
