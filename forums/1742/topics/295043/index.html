    to book CC badminton courts online, go to

    http://one.pa.gov.sg/CRMSPortal/CRMSPortal.portal

    Select Book Facility at lefthand column

    Rates are:

    Membership type | Normal Hours | Peak Hours
    Public Customer | $ 10.00 | $ 12.00
    Other Member | $ 7.00 | $ 8.00
    Passion Card Member $ 7.00 | $ 8.00

    IIRC, booking window is 15 days in advance.

    Good luck!
