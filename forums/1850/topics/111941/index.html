    Hi Bladez87,

    Your bicep weights are too light!! Do heavier weights - a rough guide is so that you have difficulties at the last two reps of every set. And you also have to gradually increase your weights (ie. pyramid system).

    For example:

    Set 1: 6 kg x 12 reps
    Set 2: 8 kg x 10 reps
    Set 3: 10 kg x 6-8 reps (if you can).

    This is just a rough guide...you can do 6 kg, 7kg (if there is such a thing), 8 kg....or even 6 kg, 8 kg, 8kg, etc. Up to you. For me, it depends on how lethargic I feel that day .

    If you really want to build, its heavy weights with low reps (not more than 8 reps). And max. 3 times per week. If you do it everyday, you're just over-straining the muscle.

    Oh, don't forget to warm-up and stretch first....and also stretch after. And you need to eat lah. Just makan and makan proteins - white meats, egg whites, tofu, etc.

    Lastly, don't work only on biceps. Do an overall body workout (believe me, I made the mistake when young and now some parts are permanently bigger than others).

    Good luck!
