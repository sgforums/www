He does not like to follow conformity, but always want to search for new ventures, new mystery. He cannot easily understand things, so he will gradually learn about u till he fills up all his questions. He knows so many people, but he has few friends. He may wonder how many people think like he does, but he does not want to be like the others. A man with a conflicting personality. He is cool, understanding, able to work well, & very artistic. Taurus man could be an artist. He could shock u as much as he is able to calm u down when u'r upset. He is a free spirit who likes to venture, but when he wants to be alone, do not touch him but to let him be. He won't disappear from the crowds too long, he will be back. He will give u straightforward opinion or comments, but will never advice what he thinks u should do. He doesn't like people to tell him what he should do too. He thinks each individual dreams & thoughts should be very private. He will use his brain not his body strength, so he will let other guys compete. He hates lies, so he will not tell u lies. If he finds it is necessary to lie, he will find other ways not to tell u or avoid telling u anything. If he really has to lie, u will never be able to tell that he is lying. He can really keep secrets, so u will hardly know that he is a lonely soul. If u want this kind of guy, u have to be an interesting person. He has to be curious about u. Hell for him is "No Freedom", so if he marries u then u should know it is the biggest decision in his life. Always be interesting, then u could have him beside u.
