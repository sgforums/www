    Train delays on NEL

    There were delays in the train service on the North-East Line, which is operated by SBS Transit, from 7.10 to 7.31 this morning.

    A caller to the MediaCorp News Hotline, Mr Faizi, said the train he took from Seng Kang stalled at Serangoon Station for about 10 minutes. The train was also delayed at every station till Dhoby Ghaut Station, where he alighted.

    Another caller, Ms Liao, who took the train from Seng Kang to Harbour Front, also complained about delays.

    An SBS Transit spokesman said "technical difficulties along the line resulted in irregular headways".

     

    (Source: Afternoon edition of the TODAY newspaper - 1/4/2008)
