    Spotted 1 set (1 pair of machines) of the new model of SMRT fare gate in use at Somerset Station. When I used it, some non-uniformed people had their laptop and devices connected to the fare gate and were doing some test monitoring. Features:

    1. No more green arrow or red cross sign on the front of the machine if I remembered correctly. Just a red or green illuminated bar of light below the card reader at around waist height. Solves the frequent problem of many passengers who try scanning their card on a no-entry fare gate.
    2. No more pinholes on the machine for the sensors as well. Black plastic are used to cover the sensors.
    3. Makes low buzzing sounds for card-scan success or error.
