    Two paragraphs on Titan Fare Gates quoted from SMRT's 04 April 2010 press release Pages 10 and 13 respectively:

    For added passenger safety and security, SMRT developed the Titan Gate, a state-of-theart rail product that features neural-logic intelligence, which enable the built-in sensors to differentiate between human and inanimate objects. This results in highly accurate detection, increasing throughput of passengers and passenger safety by minimising cases of passengers being hit by gate barriers while maintaining high security such as the detection of tailgating. The well-thought through design also takes into consideration maintenance and environmental factors that are aligned with SMRT’s commitment to protecting the environment and ensuring high system availability. Titan faregate is now on trial at Somerset MRT Station.

     

    Developed by SMRT, the Titan fare gate system is expected to save up to 10% in energy consumption due to in-built eco-friendly features such as low energy LEDs, energy-saving LCD displays and low energy servo-motors.
