    MRT Old Announcements (By Junita Melson)

    In train Announcement (Both SMRT and SBS Transit)

    NOTE: * Refers to station name varies.

    Next stop for - Dhoby Ghaut / Outram Park Interchange :

    [BUZZER] Next Stop, *Dhoby Ghaut Interchange*. Passangers going towards HarbourFront or Punggol, please alight at the next stop.



    Arriving at Dhoby Ghaut / Outram Park Interchange :

    [BUZZER] *Dhoby Ghaut Interchange*. Passangers going towards HarbourFront or Punggol, please alight and transfer to the North East Line.

    Next Stop for - Boon Lay, Pasir Ris, Changi Airport and Marina Bay (End Stations)

    [BUZZER] Next stop, *Boon Lay*. This train is terminating at *Boon Lay*.

    Arriving at Lay, Pasir Ris, Changi Airport and Marina Bay

    [BUZZER] *Boon Lay*. This train is terminating at *Boon Lay*. All passanger please alight. Thank you for travelling with SMRT. *Boon Lay*.

    Next Stop for - City Hall Interchange (NSL) (Towards Marina Bay)

    [BUZZER] Next stop, City Hall Interchange.

    Arriving at City Hall Interchange (NSL) (towards Marina Bay)

    City Hall Interchange. Passangers going towards Bugis, Pasir Ris Or Changi Airport, please cross the platform and transfer to another train. City Hall Interchange.

    Next Stop for - Raffles Place Interchange (NSL) (Towards Marina Bay)

    Next Stop, Raffles Place Interchange.

    Arriving at Raffles Place Interchange (NSL) (Towards Marina Bay)

    Raffles Place Interchange. Passangers going towards Tanjong Pagar, Jurong East or Boon Lay, please cross the platform and transfer to another train. Raffles Place Interchange.

    Next Stop for - Raffles Place Interchange (NSL) (Towards Jurong East via Woodlands)

    Next Stop, Raffles Place Interchange.

    Arriving at Raffles Place Interchange (NSL) (Towards Jurong East via Woodlands)

    Raffles Place Interchange. Raffles Place Interchange.


    Next Stop for - City Hall Interchange (NSL) (Towards Jurong East via Woodlands)

    Next Stop, City Hall Interchange.

    Arriving at City Hall Interchange (NSL) (Towards Jurong East via Woodlands)

    City Hall Interchange. City Hall Interchange

    Next Stop for - City Hall Interchange (EWL) (Towards Boon Lay)

    [BUZZER] Next stop, City Hall Interchange.

    Arriving at City Hall Interchange (EWL) (Towards Boon Lay)

    City Hall Interchange. Passangers going towards Tanjong Pagar, Jurong East or Boon Lay, please cross the platform and transfer to another train. City Hall Interchange.

    Next Stop for - Jurong East Interchange (EWL) (Towards Boon Lay or Pasir Ris)

    [BUZZER] Next Stop, Jurong East Interchange.

    Arriving at Jurong East Interchange (EWL) (Towards Boon Lay or Pasir Ris)

    [BUZZER] Jurong East Interchange. Passengers going towards Woodlands and Ang Mo Kio, please transfer to another train at the middle platform. Jurong East Interchange.

    Next Stop for - City Hall Interchange (EWL) (Towards Boon Lay)

    [BUZZER] Next stop, City Hall Interchange.

    Arriving at City Hall Interchange (EWL) (Towards Boon Lay)

    City Hall Interchange. Passangers going towards Orchard, Yishun and Woodlands, please cross the platform and transfer to another train. City Hall Interchange.

    Next Stop for - Raffles Place Interchange (EWL) (Towards Boon Lay)

    Next Stop, Raffles Place Interchange.

    Arriving at Raffles Place Interchange (EWL) (Towards Boon Lay)

    Raffles Place Interchange. Passangers going towards Marina Bay, please cross the platform and transfer to another train. Raffles Place Interchange.

    Next Stop for - Jurong East Interchange (EWL) (Towards Boon Lay or Pasir Ris)

    [BUZZER] Next Stop, Jurong East Interchange.

    Arriving at Jurong East Interchange (EWL) (Towards Boon Lay or Pasir Ris)

    [BUZZER] Jurong East Interchange. Passengers going towards Woodlands and Ang Mo Kio, please transfer to another train at the middle platform. Jurong East Interchange.

    Arriving at Jurong East Interchange (NSL) First / Last Station

    Jurong East Interchange. This Train is terminating at Jurong East Interchange. Passengers going towards City Hall, and Pasir Ris, please alight on the Left, and transfer to another train. Passengers going towards Chinese Garden, Lakeside, or Boon Lay, Please alight on the Right, and transfer to another train. Thank You for travelling with SMRT. Jurong East Interchange

    Next Stop For- Dhoby Ghaut Interchange / Outram Park Interchange (NEL)

    [BUZZER] Next Station, Dhoby Ghaut Interchange / Outram Park Interchange. Passengers may alight and change to the North South / East west line.

    Arriving Dhoby Ghaut Interchange / Outram Park Interchange

    [BUZZER] Dhoby Ghaut / Outram Park Station. (Repeat)

    Standard Ticket

    [BUZZER] Dear passengers, if you are using a standard ticket for a single trip, you can obtain a refund of your one dollar deposit, at the ticketing machine, at your destination station. Thank You.

    No Eating & Drinking

    Your attention please, eating or drinking is not allowed in the stations and trains. Thank you for helping us to keep the stations and trains clean, for the comfort of all passengers.

    Suspicious Article

    [BUZZER] Attention all passengers. Please do not leave your belongings unattended. If you see any suspicious article, please inform our staff or call 999.

    Cram Train

    Your attention please, for the convenience of all passengers, please move towards the centre of the car. Thank You.

    Train terminates at a particular station.

    YOUR ATTENTION PLEASE ! For your own safety, please stand behind the yellow line.

    No Eating And Drinking In Trains And Stations

    YOUR ATTENTION PLEASE ! Eating or drinking is not allowed in the stations and trains. Thank you for helping us to keep the stations and trains clean for the confort of all passangers.

    Last Train Departing (Taken From muziq bus)

    [BUZZER] Your attention please, the last train to *Pasir Ris* is at platform A. Please board immediately. Good Night. (Repeated twice)

    No More Train At A Particular Platform

    [BUZZER] Your attention please, there are no more trains in service at platform A. There are no more trains in service at platform A. Good Night.(repeated five times a set)

    DNB Trains (Do Not Board)

    [BUZZER] Your attention please, the train that is approaching Platform A is not for passenger service. Please wait for the next passenger service train. Thank You.
