    Lines of the Singapore MRT
    North South Line
    [NS1] Jurong East <-> [NS27] Marina Bay

    Special Services:
    Lunchtime Express: [NS16] Ang Mo Kio <-> [NS27] Marina Bay
    The 1st train arrives at Ang Mo Kio at about 12 PM and the last train leaves Marina Bay at about 1.45 PM.

    Peak Hours: [NS13] Yishun <-> [NS27] Marina Bay
    Morning Peak: 7.30 AM to 9 AM
    Evening Peak: 4.45 PM to 6.45 PM

    Morning Crossover Special Service: [NS16] Ang Mo Kio -> [EW1] Pasir Ris
    Services: 6.50 AM to 8 AM

    Evening Yew Tee Special Service: [NS1] Jurong East <-> [NS5] Yew Tee
    Services:5.45 PM -> 7 PM

    East West Line
    [EW29] Joo Koon <-> [EW1] Pasir Ris

    Special Services:
    Lunchtime Express: [EW16] Outram Park <-> [EW9] Aljunied
    The 1st train arrives at Aljunied at 12 PM and the last train leaves Outram Park at about 1.50 PM.

    Peak Hours: Whole line
    Morning Peak: 7.30 AM to 9 AM
    Evening Peak: 4.45 PM to 6.45 PM

    List of Short Forms

    • STARIS: SMRT Active Route Map Information System
    • -> Route map: Electronic Active route maps above doors
    • -> VFD: Vacuum Fluorescent Display
    • AST: Another STARIS Train
    • ART: Another Refurbished Train
    • EWL: East West Line
    • NSL: North South Line
    • CGL: Changi Airport Branch Line
    • NEL: North East Line
    • CCL: Circle Line
    • DTL: DownTown Line
    • LTE: Lunchtime Express
    • EWL Perm: Trains that frequent on the East West Line
    • NSL Perm: Trains that frequent on the North South Line
    • Bar Train: Trains with 84 seats removed and replaced with bars
    • DNB: Do not board
    • PSD: Platform Screen Doors (In General)
    • HHPSD: Half-Height PSD (1.5m for above-ground stations)
    • MIA: Missing in Action
    • TRN: Train run number
    • BVE: Most popular train simulator amongst SGForumers
    • CHD: Changi Depot
    • BSD: Bishan Depot
    • UPD: Ulu Pandan Depot
    • KCD: Kim Chuan Depot
    • SKD: Sengkang Depot
    • GBD: Gali Batu Depot
    • Refurb: Trains that had undergone refurbishment
    • Unrefurb: Trains that had not undergone refurbishment
    • TO: Train Officer/Operator
    • CSO: Customer Service Officer
    • Camp: Stay in a few stations on a particular line to see trains
    • VPIS: Visual Passenger Information System
    • RATIS: Rail And Travel Information System
    • ATS: Automatic Train Stop
    • ATC: Automatic Train Control
    • ATP: Automatic Train Protection
    • ATO: Automatic Train Operation
    • AM: Automated Mode - Trains drive automatically
    • CM: Coded Manual - TOs drive trains manually
    • RM: Restricted Manual - Same as CM, but motors cut off at 15 km/h
    • NB: Northbound
    • SB: Southbound
    • EB: Eastbound
    • WB: Westbound
    • TW: Tailwall
    • HW: Headwall

    • OT: Off topic
    • OTW: On the way
    • BTW: By the way/Between
    • IMO: In my opinion
    • KTM: Keretapi Tanah Melayu

    Your attention please, for the convenience of all passengers, please move towards the centre of the car. Thank You.

    [BUZZER] Your attention please, the last train to *Pasir Ris* is at platform A. Please board immediately. Good Night. (Repeated twice)

    No More Train At A Particular Platform

    [BUZZER] Your attention please, there are no more trains in service at platform A. There are no more trains in service at platform A. Good Night.(repeated five times a set)

    DNB Trains (Do Not Board)

    [BUZZER] Your attention please, the train that is approaching Platform A is not for passenger service. Please wait for the next passenger service train. Thank You.
