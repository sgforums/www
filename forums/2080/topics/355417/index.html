    At around 2.05pm today (10 Apr), trainset 201/202 overshot its tracks at EW23 Clementi MRT towards Pasir Ris.

    Just before it stopped, a few loud hisses were heard as the air were released from the brakes in a few attempts to stop but the train still overshot. The doors did not open , preventing passengers from departing or alighting at the station, and the train remained for a while before proceeding to EW22 Dover.

    Passengers who were heading to Clementi enjoyed an extra workout on Good Friday as they had to walk down and up the staircase/elevator to the other platform, given that Dover station has side platforms.

    It was not ascertained as to whether the Siemens train had been set to CM mode prior to the incident

     

    - Channel News Kantang
