    Channel NewsAsia - Thursday, February 14

    Power trips delay trains on 2 sections of North East Line

    SINGAPORE : Train journeys on the North East Line were disrupted on Wednesday evening.

    SBS Transit said services affected were those between HarbourFront and Outram Park MRT stations and between Dhoby Ghaut and Little India MRT stations.

    A MediaCorp Hotline caller said a staff member at the Outram Park station announced that there would be a 25—minute delay. The caller said many commuters were waiting at the station.

    SBS Transit said the disruptions were caused by two power trips.

    The first started at 6.34pm and this affected the line between HarbourFront and Outram Park stations.

    The second began at 6.53pm and this time the line between Dhoby Ghaut and Little India stations was affected.

    In both cases, trains still ran but they were delayed because precautionary measures were taken to check the rail line.

    SBS Transit said the longest delay passengers experienced was 17 minutes. — CNA/ms
