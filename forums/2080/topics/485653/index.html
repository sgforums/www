    in the MRT game, players choose a MRT station.

    for instance, player A choose 'Pasir Ris', player B choose 'Boon Lay', player C choose 'Jurong East', player D choose 'Marina Bay', player E choose 'Punggol', player F choose 'Harbourfront', player G choose 'Dhoby Ghaut', .... 

    say you choose 'Commonwealth' and someone says 'commonwealth', the players beside you will beat your lap repeatedly, until you say another choosen station (eg. Marina Bay).

    the purpose of beating your lap is to make it hard for you to think.

    when you say another choosen station, the beating will stop and the other player who chose that station will have his lap beaten, until he says another choosen station. 

    and the game goes on.....
