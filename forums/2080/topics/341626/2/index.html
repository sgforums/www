    25 Dec 08, 02:21

    For LRT,

    Bukit Panjang, 19 cars, 101 to 119.
    Double cars can be chosen randomly, although there is a tendency for SMRT to deploy cars which car numbers are close to each other, like 102-103 or 109-111.

    SPLRT (Sengkang Punggol),  41 cars, 01 to 41.
    SPLRT do not operate double cars.

    Changi Airport, 10 cars for North side, 01 to 10, 6 or 7 cars for South side.
    Double cars are similar to BPLRT, can be chosen randomly, but there is tendency to deploy cars which car numbers are close to each other.
