    17th April 2010

    The opening of CCL Stage 1 & 2 was also a joke. Wanting to experience something that I have never done before, I was challenged by somebody. Took a cab from my home to Paya Lebar. Recalled what I told the taxi uncle "uncle, i want go paya lebar ccl entrance". Spend S$5.00 and board the first train...

    18th May 2010

    Till this moment, i always recall what happened on 18 May 2010... It totally change my life completely. Somehow I really wonder what am i doing at the point of time...

    Before 03.34pm - Camped at the EB platform at TNM for that train with my friend...
    03.35pm - Saw the train pulling into the WB platform, run down from the EB platform to the WB platform. Jumped into it, the train stopped for around 1 minute as TO was changing shift.
    03.40pm - Jump off Kembangan station and film the train departing...
    03.43pm - Boarded 089/090(EB) and went back TNM.

    27th July 2010

    I was still having student attachment in Jurong. After work, was tired and decided to go for some joyride. Took the SB train and when it reaches Yishun, I was deciding...should i take the next train behind since it is empty and there are seats. Alighted at Khatib station and wait for the next train to arrive which was 321/322. Went into the train and to my surprise, hey how come c751b got STARIS...it was like OMG OMG...
