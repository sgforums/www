    tintinspartan,

    Nice to see you are working on such a project. I read the KTM forums and heard you were working on KTM stock from 1986 to present day. I happened to have done this last year albeit incomplete. I upgraded my Trainz version from UTC to TRS 2004 a few months back and have stopped work on the coaches permanently, however i have files of the old skins (some a bit messy lah). Please notify me if you would like a copy of these skins.

    Skins include

    ABC, AFC, AEC, E-PLUS, ASC, TC and SK2 (Selun Khas 2) in their different liveries.

    Now for some advice. I saw the youtube videos and would like to commend you on your work. However, Trainz Paintshed (the medium you are currently using) is not the only method of reskinning or skinning trains and coaches. Notice that in the folder for your custom train in (C:/Program Files/Auran/Trainz/World), the skin you created in Paintshed is in a bmp file. This basically means that you can edit the skin in MS Paint. But mind you, it is a long and tedious process which involves pixel-by-pixel work.(presuming you want to produce accurate models)

    I used this medium to create all the coaches i listed above. you can search the KTM forum for pics of these coaches.

    As I have said, you can use MS Paint to create advanced skins. Which means you can create an SMRT train as well in its original livery. It gets even better if you have real photos of the train itself because you can just cut and paste onto the bmp file.

    If you want to, you can download content from Auran's DLS and create the SMRT route if you'd like to. It's just a suggestion but i think it would be really good if you could.

    Good Luck,
