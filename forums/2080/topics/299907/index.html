    This is an urgent request. As we all know, the C151s are undergoing the MLR as of now. You also know that before MLR, each of the C151 driving trailers had a builder's plate in them right next to the emergency exit door. However, during MLR, these plates are removed. This basically means it is our last chance to record down the builder of each and every trainset. (The whole C151 fleet was built by several builders).

    I am keeping a list of the builders of each EMU set, however it is incomplete. If you wish to help me record down this piece of history, please post the set number of the unit, along with its builder and build year.

    So it will look something like "Set 009-010 1987 Kawasaki Heavy Industries"
    or "Set 127-128 1989 Tokyu Car Corp"
