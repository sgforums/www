    I was on the affected train, it happened around 11.30am. Train involved is 097/098, with Car 3097 hitting that moron. Train entered station normally, just started to engage in B2, when TO suddenly pushed to EMG brake. Train stopped exactly halfway into the station. SCDF personnels came within 10mins, who are despatched from Yishun Fire Station. Bus bridging service between Woodlands and Yishun is activated within 1/2 hour from the happening of the incident. not sure for trains @ Woodlands side, but train service @ Yishun can be said to be split into 2, resulting in approximately 12min waiting time for each direction before train service resumed. Trains which are turned around @ Yishun to head back to Marina Bay used the tandem crossover before the station to head into Platform B. Passengers are all forced to alight and those continuing their journey to Jurong East have to wait for the next train @ Platform A.
