    Today took the NSL around 11am to Raffles Place Interchange from Admiralty on 077/078. For some reason the STARIS was the most worst I have seen. I board at Admiralty, the STARIS & VFD were both showing that the train will terminate at Ang Mo Kio (the train terminate at Marina Bay actually) and it was showing NS9 Woodlands instead of NS10 Admiralty, so the train continue under Sembawang when the STARIS realise that it is wrong so it jumps to Sembawang but immediately after the train starts moving off, STARIS and VFD immediately jump to NS13 Yishun thinking that we have reach Yishun station when we just moved off Sembawang. This problem later happens at Yishun, later at Khatib, it finally realises that the train is terminating at Marina Bay so it started to light up the stations to Marina Bay but the jumping station problem still exist. Later at Yio Chu Kang, STARIS decide that the train will now terminate Ang Mo Kio again instead of Marina Bay, so when the train Ang Mo Kio, I expect it to display the Marina Bay route again, guess what instead it went to display the Jurong East route with no next station so all green light till Jurong East (starting from Ang Mo Kio) when the train is going towards Marina Bay. Well I guess the STARIS seems to have a mind that decide where the train terminate, the direction it go.
