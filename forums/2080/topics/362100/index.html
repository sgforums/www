    Why 3 cars? Because initially when LTA planned the line, it was planned as 5.4 km with 6 stations only. Due to this, it is to be a medium capacity line known as the Marina Line (MRL).

    Quote: The 5.4-km long medium capacity Marina Line, with six stations, will run from Dhoby Ghaut MRT Station, below Bras Basah Road to Marina Centre and then continue below Nicoll Highway to the Stadium Boulevard. Construction work on the Marina Line is scheduled to begin at the end of 2001 and is target to complete in 2005/2006.

    http://app.lta.gov.sg/corp_press_content.asp?start=172

     

    In 2001, they decided to extend the Marina Line to become a circle, which gives Circle Line its name. Also they decided that although the line is extended with another 23 stations, it will remains as a medium capacity line.

    http://app.lta.gov.sg/corp_press_content.asp?start=237
