    Closing date: 16/05/2011

    Closing time: 12:00 PM

    Procurement Type: Goods

    Procurement Nature: Non Period Contract

    Other evaluation criteria:
    Tenders for Contract 751C and Contract 830C will be considered from Tenderers with: a) proven expertise and experience for the design, supply, installation, testing, commissioning and putting into service of trains for Mass Rail Transit System in a timely and safe manner; and b) Financial capacity to undertake and sustain a multi-million dollar project in Singapore.
