    Originally posted by mounsterazmee:

    hello. im new here. wanna ask, how do LRT get their power supply? specifically, the BPLRT. not used to the singapore trains here, except for the SMRT and malaysian train

    Bombardier: Simply from the electric rails located above the guide rails. This does not apply on switches as there are no electric rails on the switches.

    Mitsubishi Crystal Mover: From the electric rails on one side of the side of the train, there are two rails, one provides the current to the train, and the other is for the return current.
