    Evening TRNs are rather unreliable.

    Only morning TRNs + special svcs' TRN can be trusted. Of course weekends too. After morning peak some TRNs are changed as well, eg some non-crossover trains will get crossover TRNs, and vice versa. So we can't really tell if a train did crossover in the morning if we see it with a crossover TRN in the afternoon or in the evening.

    Fridays have a different schedule as compared to Mon to Thurs. For evening peak, the 1st train leaves CHD at 4pm WB, and the next one would arrive TNM middle platform to go EB at aboiut 4.45pm.
