    Just a question I'd like to ask. Which train do you guys like/prefer?

    Trains involved: C151, C651, C751B, C151 Refurbished

    Consider terms like:

    Motor sound when accelerating & stopping. (Quietness, sounds nice/soothing)

    Interior design (LED displays/colour/chairs/doors)

    Exterior design (Eg, run number, doors etc.)

     

    For me, I like all 3 in terms of motor sound so it's hard to decide. C151 has a soothing sound, C651 is music to my ears, C751B sounds cool.

    For the interior I like the C651. It has nice doors, and the non-wooden like floors look good.

    Exterior I'd prefer the refurbished C151. It has nice blue tinted windows, nice head, shiny walls.

     

    But I would probably like the refurbished C651 when it comes out :D
