    Miscellaneous announcement.
    1.
    Your Attention Please, For your safety, please use the lift in the station if u are carrying bulky items or feeling unwell. Thank You.
    大家请注意，为了你的安全，如有携带大件物品或感到不适，请考虑使用站内的电梯。谢谢。
    Sila Ambil Perhatian, demi keselamatan anda, sila gunakan lif-lif di stesen jika anda membawa barang-barang yang besar atau berasa kurang sihat. Terima Kasih.
    2.
    Your Attention Please, if you are unable to board the train at Platform A, please wait for the next passenger service train. Thank you for your cooperation.
    3.
    Your Attention Please, the train that is at the Platform A will travel towards Pasir Ris via Jurong East interchange and Raffles Place. Passengers going towards Boon Lay, please alight at Jurong East interchange to transfer. Thank you.
    4.
    Your Attention Please, Please give way to alighting passengers before boarding. Thank you.
    5.
    Tanah Merah Interchange, This train is terminating at Tanah Merah Interchange. Passengers going towards City Hall or Boon Lay, please alight on the left and transfer to another train. Passengers going to Simei, Tampines or Pasir Ris, please alight on the right and transfer to another train. Thank you for travelling with SMRT. Tanah Merah Interchange.
    6.
    Going to Johor Bahru, take the SMRT cross border service 950 from Woodlands regional interchange to Kota Raya II Terminal.
    7.
    Dear Passengers, if you are going to Johor Bahru, you may take the SMRT cross border service 950 from Marsiling MRT Station to Kota Raya II Terminal.
    8.
    Your Attention Please, please do not leave your belongings unattended. If you see any suspicious articles, please report to our staff or call 999.
    大家请注意，请看好您的随身物件。若发现任何可疑物件,请通知我们的执勤人员或拨电九九九。
