  • SBS7322B
    14 Sep 09, 18:58

    I have started working on some minor things even before I officially start work.

    I have finished working on the tunings. Maximum acceleration of 4.5 km/h/s and top speed of 90 km/h. Don't worry. It will take a long time to accelerate from 80km/h to 90km/h.

    I will only officially start work around mid-October and expected completion is end-November.

    As taken from my previous post, this is what you will expect:

  • Photo-realistic exterior as well as interior
  • Animated doors 
  • Animated door closing warning lights (The red lights blinking at the sides of the doors)
  • ATC-equipped (ATC functions are made from the route file, Ctrl+S to activate security system and Page Down/Up to select ATS/ATC).

    • I'm also looking for contributors for photos of the C751B cab. PM me if you are interested in contributing your photos of the C751B cab.
