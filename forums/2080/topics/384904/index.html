  • E5
    15 Dec 09, 14:29

    Hmm let's see...

    - Remove the "Normal Mode" operation when train is operating in Auto mode
    Pros: Improve/shorten commuting time from A to B
    Cons: Nil
    Constraint: Nil

    - Removal of Yishun & Yew Tee shutter service during peak hour
    Pros: Improve frequency of train between Woodlands and Jurong East on NSL
    Cons: Passengers from Yishun onwards may not be able to secure a seat during their journey
    Constraint: Nil

    - Legthen the train from 6-car to 8-car
    Pros: Each train will be able to transport more passengers
    Cons: Increased Operation Cost
    Constraint: Design of existing stations does not allow future expansion with low cost. Though it can be done with low cost, it requires track to curve within station. But this idea will definitely be mooted.

    - Implement Rapid Service
    Pros: Enable train to skip certain station due to low demand and thus, benefit passengers who are travelling for a long distance on train
    Cons: Need to upgrade existing station used for overtaking
    Constraint: Infrastructure limitation due to island platform design

    -Upgrade signalling system, remove automatic operation, converting to full-manual
    Pros: Full-manual system can "crush train" during peak hour better when compared to auto system.
    Cons: Increased operation cost due to increased manpower
    Constraint: Nil

    This are just few of the many which I can think of. =)
