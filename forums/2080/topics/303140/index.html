15/01/08: KTMB Ekspres Rakyat extended to Butterworth

  • tintinspartan
    15 Jan 08, 15:37
    Today is a historic event.

    KTMB's Ekspres Rakyat, which formerly ran from Singapore to KL Sentral have been extended to run it's original route between Singapore - Butterworth. A ceremony was held at Butterworth to send off the down train from Butterworth to Singapore. The down train will arrive at Woodlands CIQ at around 8:45 pm if there are no delays and will arrive at Tanjung PAgar KTM Station at 9:45 pm.

    Express Rakyat was introduced to run from Butterworth - Singapore. It was shortened to Singapore - KL in 2001 due to Rawang - Ipoh electrification and double tracking projects.

    Now the video of the first up train departing Singapore is available.
