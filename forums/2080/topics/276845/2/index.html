    If you want to take pic in MRT, let me just say you have to have quick hands, and be skilled at photography. It helps if your camera is quick too.

    Take one photo and done. I have taken more than one, but that is within 2 sec.

    For digital SLR people, jsut treat it as thought it's film. 1 shot, finish. for film people, you konw what I mean.

    Second (for digital only) carry an extra SD/comapact flash with you. switch quickly if you see em coming. if they ask you to delete, you can deny you ever took the photo. I have done this with police before.

    Third, NOBODY has the right to ask for your IC. The only people who have the right, are the police. You can sit in the MRT and refuse to give info to anyone except the police. And also, you can refuse to allow them to view the pics and delete on your SLR/DSLR. Wait for the police and make life difficult if you want.

    Finally, you are allowed to call 999 yourself, if you feel the MRT staff are getting too aggressive.
