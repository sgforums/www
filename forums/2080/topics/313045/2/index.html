    Answers:

    1) Bukit Panjang and Fajar. Bukit Panjang has 2 lifts per platform instead of 1, which the other stations have. Fajar's outer loop platform has the lift in the middle, unlike the others, which are at the end of the platform.

    2) Clarke Quay. Artwork on the floor which is a map of Singapore River.

    3) Braddell. Blk 203 Toa Payoh North.

    4) Fernvale. It has side platforms instead of an island platform. Also, the outer loop platform has a closed exit to a future development.
    Kangkar and Farmway. They are stations without a large emergency platform at the ends. Instead they have the transformer rooms there.

    5) Choa Chu Kang LRT station. Passengers will have to tap their card on a special machine, which will print out the coupon on a piece of thermal paper which you can present it at the Lot 1 Shaw Cineplex.

    6) Punggol. The middle track is covered over. If you look at the ends at the middle of the platform, you can see a trackway. The trackway is for the Punggol North LRT line and is currently unused and not powered.

    7) Ten Mile Junction. Passengers will have to walk to Phoenix or Bukit Panjang station, though it is unknown why they dint direct passengers to Senja.

    8) Its for people who have underpayed or lost their ticket to get their exit ticket to exit the station. It is situated at stations without a customer service office or an exit of a station which have the customer service centre located at another side of the station.

    9) Expo. There are 8 GTMs in the concourse, and 2 at the platform level. GTM 10 is used for the purpose mentioned in 8)

    10) Ten Mile Junction. This station has the smallest entrance, one lift door and one staircase (which cannot be opened from the station itself) are the only entrances to the station. It also has low ridership (as compared to Route A and B), even with the already long 20 minutes frequency. There is no emergency bus service planned for the station and the aircon is controlled by a normal aircon controller which you can find in offices. Luckily Sheng Shiong moved in...if not it would be worse. Simply Pathetic.
