    Originally posted by TownLink 291:

    So.. got shuttle train frm Boon Lay lah?

    most likely between Pioneer and Joo Koon only.

    main reason is because if you tap in at the station you board (e.g. Clementi) and when you reach Pioneer/Joo Koon without tapping out, the system would have no idea where you got off.

    the same applies on the way back. without tapping in at Joo Koon/Pioneer and you tap out at Clementi, how would the system know where you got on?

    the best that can be done is to program the faregates at Joo Koon/Pioneer to assume passenger got off at Boon Lay, but this disadvantages those getting on from Boon Lay itself..
