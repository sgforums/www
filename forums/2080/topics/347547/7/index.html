    And even though its a new station just because its an SMRT station they still followed SMRT’s practice of having only 2 plasma displays at 1 location per platform. And we all know SMRT only flashes train arrival timings every 10 seconds or more. Its irritating enough not to be able to know what time the next train arrives and have to stare at the screen for 10 seconds, worse still, there are only 2 screens so its even harder to see the small font.

     

    SBS Transit has the “always on” train arrival timings and not only that they even have 4 “without burn marks and crystal clear” LCD screens on all platforms.

     

    And overall though the interior of the roofing is not worth a "wow!", its still decent and am overall appreciative that the station has wide platform space and looks capable of aging gracefully with time.

     

    Just my two cents.
