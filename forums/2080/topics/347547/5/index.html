    btw there is no such thing as a shuttle train. let me try and break it down...

    (1) Trains run as per normal on East West Line.
    (2) At Boon Lay, all passengers get off and it goes off revenue service.
    (3) Train goes to pioneer and pick up passengers to Joo Koon...
    (4) ...Then from Joo Koon bring passengers back to pioneer.
    (5) At Pioneer, all passengers must get off (again).
    (6) Train heads back to Boon Lay, re-entering revenue service.

    so that is why trains are running through from Boon Lay since 15th Feb other than to smoothen the tracks I guess? practice for today.
