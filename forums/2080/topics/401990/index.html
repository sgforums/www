    Updates on stage 5 stations except HarbourFront:

    Haw Par Villa station: Reinstatement of West Coast Highway (City-bound) to its original alignment was completed last week. As for Jurong-bound will be taking place soon. Station entrance not constructed yet.

    Pasir Panjang station: Station entrance partially completed, can see the escalators and stairs, but haven't build the roof yet. Lift shaft under construction. The new pedestrian overhead bridge leading to the station is under construction.

    Cripple Siding between Pasir Panjang station and Labrador Park station: This section of Pasir Panjang Rd (towards Jurong) near Mapletree Business City / Bayanihan Centre was diverted due to deep excavation to construct the criple siding underground. Reinstatement of road to its original alignment will be taking place soon.

    Labrador Park station: Station entrance not constructed yet. Lift shaft can be seen.

    Telok Blangah station: Station entrance completed with roof. Lift shaft can be seen. New pedestrian overhead bridge leading to the station is under construction.
