    When did you first see it? I first saw one in late January 2007, at Yishun.

    When it arrived I thought it was a C751B, but it looked weird in some ways, but when the motor sound kicked in, it sounded C151-like! I was so shocked! I did remember the news saying about trains, and I thought that that refurb C151 was a new train.

    But after some research, I found out that it was a refurbished train instead.

    Subsequent meets:
    - One at Dhoby Ghaut Southbound (I was going northbound)
    - One at Tampines Eastbound (083/084, I camped for it to return to westbound just to ride on it!) 
    - One at City Hall Northbound (019/020, second ride)
