    You are right that LTA is keeping mum on the opening schedule. The original schedule is to open the stages in sequence, starting from Stage 1 in 2007 to Stage 5 in 2010. However due to the Nicoll Highway incident, LTA has not make any concrete announcement on the revised schedule.

    However, we may infer how the it will open based on proxy source. A close proxy is Circle Line Art Competition, where LTA invites artwork/design to be incorporated into the new Circle Line system. The competition has two phases: first phase for stations in Stage 1-3, and second phase for Stage 4-5. Hence, we can infer that it is most likely that Circle Line will be opened to public in two phase: Stage 1-3 then Stage 4-5.
