    Originally posted by SmRtFrEaks:

    the stations are already long enough for at least two trains....but i agree that why are they putting single trains rather than double...

    It all depends on the demand. The Sengkang and Punggol LRT Lines have equal demand for all routes served, so it can run single trains on a higher frequency. The Bukit Panjang LRT however has more demand (roughly twice more) on Service B than a during the morning peak hours, hence, running 2-car trains on Service B and 1-car trains on Service A will justify the amount of trains with the actual passenger demands.
