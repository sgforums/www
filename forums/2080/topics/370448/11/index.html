    Went back to Switzerland just a few days ago for a 2 day trip to see my father's friend living in the German part of Switzerland.

     

    Was travelling alone again, but I used the 2 days as a holiday and it is winter now, so I practically made my way throughout Switzerland with a rail pass and joyrided on their trains throughout...

     

    I know some of the pictures are blur, sorry about that but it is hard to take photos especially when trains speed past at 150kmph(standard) to 250kmph!

     

    I only toured the French and German speaking regions of Switzerland, no time to go to the Italian Quarter.

     


    Lausanne, in the French speaking quarter of Switzerland. Announcements are in French. Double Decker InterCity train at Platform 1.

     


    Speeding out of the Swiss Alps. This SBB CFF FFS train is headed for Bern.

     

    Really cold that day... This InterCity is headed for Lausanne.

     


    Suspect train is Regio-Express, headed for either Interlaken Ost or St Gallen.

     


    SBB CFF FFS loco pulling a Austrian OBB train to the Swiss-Austrian(Luxembourg) border where loco will be substituted for an OBB one. Train is pulling out of the last of Zurich Hauptbahnhof's many sub-stations.

     


    Picture taken while on another train, the narrow gauge Die Zentralbahn... Old rolling stock.
