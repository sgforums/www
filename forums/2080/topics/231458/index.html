Works at tunnel between BBT/BGB stations

  • jetabt767400
    14 Jan 07, 02:44
    Due to heavy rain late December, the soil and roadbed at the tunnel entrance/exit have became loose, and some SMRT engineers were seen doing works at the tunnel portal on Bukit Batok side.
    I started seeing them last week, and yesterday (13th) many engineers came down and trains have to be slowed down at the area. Notice as follow was placed in the rack at the platform for TOA reference.

    (marker written)Inform TSC when
    soil movement detected
    SPEED
    RESTRICTION
    AHEAD
    (marker written)40/39
    at NW498

    Such was the notice.
    And so was the soil movement so great that you can feel on the train? Well, I tested out and took 053 train 222 to BGB, and other than slow entry, nothing else was out of ordinary, neither did anything happen when I took 074 train 205 back, other than stopping to 0 speed in the tunnel twice and seeing the engineers waving the train past. However, the signal 40/0, signaled by a loud beep sound (I not sure, but I can guess its absolute zero speed command) was issued after 205 passed the portal and the TOA braked hard. After which it was reset with communications to 'TSC' and we continued on the journey.
    You can too try out if you are free!
