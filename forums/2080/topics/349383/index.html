    Hi preservedorange,

     

    For scenario 1, when you travel on the MRT/LRT, you must complete your trip within the allowed time. The time limit is as follows:

    Exit 1-2 stations away = 20 mins

    Exit 5-9 stations away = 40 mins

    Exit 10-15 stations away = 75 mins

    Exit 16-24 stations away = 100 mins

    Exit over 24 stations away = 120 mins

     

    If you exceeded the time limit, your ticket will be rejected. You have to go to the Passenger Service Centre and pay an administrative charge of $2 for your ticket to be upgraded.

     

    For scenario 2, the minimum fare will be deducted when you tap out. If you change your mind after you have tapped in, go to the Passenger Service Centre. Your journey will be cancelled and the staff will let you out.
