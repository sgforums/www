    Originally posted by willis_chong:

    2 trains - 12 min
    3 trains - 6 min
    4 trains - 3 min

    Your timings do not tally.

    2 x 12 = 24 (reasonable, as pause time is slightly longer)
    3 x 6 = 18
    4 x 3 = 12

    What it means is that when there are 2 trains, the 1st train would come back to the original stn in 24mins when there are 2 trains, 18mins when there are 3, and 12mins when there are 4. TNM to CGA alr takes 8-9mins, plus the pause time at terminus stns (1min x 2) when 4 trains are running, the minimal time needed for the 1st train to return to the original stn when 4 trains are running is 8+8+2 = 18mins.

    Thus, I feel that, when there are 2 trains, freq is about 12mins
    3 trains -> 7-8mins
    4 trains -> 4-5mins
