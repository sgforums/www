    Restricted Manual Mode: When the mode selector is placed in FORward, REVerse position. In Restricted Manual Mode, the mode selector may be placed in FORward, STAND-BY or REVerse according to the demand of operation. This mode is used for the reversing of train or when the ATC system fails. The Restricted Manual Mode is also used when train is moving inside the depot.
