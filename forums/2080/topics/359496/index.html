    11 May 2009

    Was a really extraordinary journey. :D Just now I boarded 021/022 from Yishun southbound to Marina Bay, another STARIS train.  Then I heard some new announcements between Ang Mo Kio to Bishan (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SbYAkFvMFjE)! After that I transferred over to the NEL to do some little things.

    After that, I saw some new stickers for Priority Seats.

    From Dhoby Ghaut, I transferred to the NSL. After Raffles Place, the train stopped to wait for the train in front. The TO forgot to apply brakes, and the train slid back for quite a distance! Luckily the train in front didn't take long to leave.

    I went back to Marina Bay then go back to Yishun. I recording the train numbers on my file, then the TO walk past me and said, "you train enthusiast ar?". I replied a yes, then he nodded his head then go to the other end. LOL.
