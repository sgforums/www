    Actually just to share some of my bits here.

    You can't really tack a controller system just through how it make sounds. Specifications are very important. I say this as an engineering student grad myself. I can't differentiate a drill from another just by sounds. Perhaps able to if they have differences of 500 RPM.

    Personally I am not into European system and is not totally aware of the version difference of the Onix system, but from the traction controlling there is indeed some difference from the C751A and C830.

    VVVF Inverter is a traction control system by itself, whether its a GTO or a IGBT semicon regulator actual differences are minimal. Switching speed is the main one, current draw is another. Shall not get too deep here if not I will be writing an essay. PM if interested to know more.
