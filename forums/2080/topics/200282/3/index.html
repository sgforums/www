  • brotherchen1982
    28 May 07, 11:07
    Originally posted by tintinspartan:
    Well, nice one but what happened to the back road of the last pic with the NTU Arch. I remembered back in P1 that there used to be a busy road passing through but why did they close it and where the road currently leads?
    It's Upper Jurong Rd, with Service 192/193 used plying along this road.

    The road U see in the picture is JW St 93 & it leads to the new stretch of Upp Jurong Rd.

    Phase 1
    Half stretch of the road(bef JW St 93 was extended) was closed for construction of underground pipes while the other stretch provide linkage to St 93 & Service 192/193 was amended to ply Jurong West Ave 4>Pionner Rd Nth to the new stretch of Upp Jurong Rd connected by the extended Boon Lay Way.

    Phase 2
    The other half of the road was demolished aft JW St 93 was extended to the new stretch of Upp Jurong Rd & it was used as part of construction site for the Jurong West Sport Cplx.
