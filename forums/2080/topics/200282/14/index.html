    Sorry for false surprise.

    Standard waiting time for trains for stations from Khatib to Marina Bay is 2 to 3 minutes (no change) but Sembawang to Jurong East is 3 to 5 minutes, occasionally 6 minutes (also no change). Northbound Trains at Yishun arrive between 2 to 3 minutes but trains terminating there arrive between 2 to 3 minutes also. So waiting time to head to Jurong East is 3 to 6 minutes. Southbound trains vary from 2 minutes to 6 minutes (some trains which do the turnaround head towards Bishan Depot directly as a Do Not Board train).
