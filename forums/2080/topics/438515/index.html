8 1/2-min delay on EWL at peak hour

  • QX179R
    24 Sep 11, 15:49

    About 2,800 commuters were affected by a delay on the East-West Line towards Pasir Ris during peak hour on Friday evening.

    The delay, which started at 7.25pm and lasted 8 1/2 minutes, was caused by a train fault.

    An SMRT spokesman said passengers on an east-bound train were asked to alight at City Hall MRT station due to a train fault.

    To minimise the impact to other passengers and to maintain east-bound train service, passengers on a west-bound train were asked to alight at Bugis MRT station, so the train could turn around and serve east-bound passengers, the spokesman said.

    --ST
