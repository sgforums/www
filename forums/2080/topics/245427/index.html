SMRT Community Emergency Preparedness(SCEP) Programme

  • brotherchen1982
    30 Mar 07, 11:31
    Organized by SMRT in partnership with MHA, SCDF, SPF & LTA.

    To train & equip the community to play a more effective role in public transport safety & security.

    All participants will undergo 3 hrs of theory & 4 hrs of practical lessons.

    theory lesson include :
    1. Introduction to security threats & challenges such as chemical, biological, radiological & explosive attacks
    2. Training on how to be vigilant on SMRT's transport networks
    3. Training on reporting procedures & emergency response

    practical training lesson comprises on-site role-playing, identification & reporting of threats, & a train evacuation using derainment ramp.

    training lesson are conducted fortnightly, subject to min. class size of 50 persons.

    graduates may take part in any upcoming Emergency Preparedness Exercise held at MRT Stn, Train Depots or Bus Int(optional).

    Registration is FREE & application are available at ALL SMRT Stn Control, SMRT Bus Ints.

    For information, email to scep@smrt.com.sg

    Note : The SCEP Programme is open to all Singaporeans/PRs who are at least 13 yrs olds.
