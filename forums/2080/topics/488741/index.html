Sengkang LRT Kupang station opens 27 Jun

  • FireIce
    15 Jun 15, 14:01

    Sengkang LRT fully operational by June 27 when Kupang station opens

    SINGAPORE - The last mothballed station on the Sengkang LRT line will open on June 27.

    Operator SBS Transit said on Monday that Kupang station, located in Fernvale on the western loop, will open to cater to the travel needs of commuters moving into the new residential developments in the area.

    With this, the Sengkang LRT - completed in 2004 - will be fully operational.

    To cope with increased demand, an additional 194 weekly train trips will be added to the Sengkang West Loop. Forty will be added to weekday mornings from 6am to 9.30am, while another 40 will be introduced during the weekday evening hours from 6.15pm to 9.30pm.
