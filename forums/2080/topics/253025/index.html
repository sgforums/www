Man's feet caught in platform gap

    SINGAPORE: A man got his feet caught in the platform gap at Tampines MRT Station but was pulled to safety by a member of the public.

    This incident happened around 11am on Monday.

    According to SMRT, the passenger hit his head against the train body, which caused him to fall. As a result, his feet were caught in the platform gap.

    Civil defence officials said the man is believed to be in his 50s.

    He suffered injuries to his left ankle and head, and was rushed to Changi General Hospital in a semi-conscious state.

    Viewers who called our hotline said they saw blood and bandages on the platform floor.

    Westbound train services were disrupted for about eight minutes.

    Police investigations are underway.
