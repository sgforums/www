  • jcqh
    29 Jul 10, 21:46

    Quite an eventful day for me today, and first to spot quite a number of stuff

    7.24am: Onboard 207/208 betw JUR and CLE, peered into UPD and saw a half train parked at an open lot - not the end of the train facing the buffer but the exposed half, which was pretty weird, plus the fact it was in UPD.

    8.04am: EMTC at CTH, spotted 211 back with 212.

    4.31pm: Feeling sian, onboard sv105. Looked on my left and see a C151 heading SB. 3057 1057 2057 2112!!!! Called Willis immediately and sent SMS to some train fans. Choing-ed towards the city.

    5.20pm: Reached Newton, where PSDs are clearest acc to 8730A. Boarded 057/112 from there to MRB and then alighted at RFP when NB.

    6pm: Tried my luck by train camping at JUR. Hoping for 058/111 to be on YWTS. A huge C751B spam disappointed me once again.

    6.24pm: I was about to leave until.... 3111 enters JUR EB!!! I knew it was 058/111 and squeezed across to the other platform via the C751B at the middle platform.

    6.30pm: Alighted at DVR, took a pic and departing vid. Headed home.
