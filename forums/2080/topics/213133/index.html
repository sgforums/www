    station-to-station timing is fixed in multiple of minutes (60 secs). the usual timing is 2 minutes though.

    during peak hours, the trains go faster but the dwell at stations longer.

    hence, the only way to shorten it is to have less stations. (in fact, opening of dover station in 2001 added one minute travel time to the tally). but u cant really do tat on our current track since there are no tracks for overtaking. u're limited by the train in front.

    so this is not possible at all.
