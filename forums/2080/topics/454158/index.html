    STOMPer otaku came across this video of a commuter who stuck his camera on the exterior of the MRT train so as to film the scenery from outside. The STOMPer feels that this act will endanger the lives of passer-bys below the train tracks should the camera fall.

    Said the STOMPer:

    "Recently a commuter stuck his camera on the exterior of the MRT using a suction cup to get a 'exclusive exterior point-of-view video'.

    "This is dangerous as his camera might fall off the window and hit people below.

    "This is also a breach of security.

    "What if someone had stuck something even more dangerous on the exterior of the train?

    "It will surely pose a threat to us commuters and passer-bys as well."
