    currently, NE2 is a provisional alphanumeric code for a future station which has not been built yet. The station, if built, would probably be around the Bukit Purmei/ Tanjong Pagar area. Reasons cited for not building the station yet include low passenger demand, but another reason is that there may be disputes if the station is built around the Tanjong Pagar KTM Station area, as Malaysia claims the area to be its land. There are speculations that the station will be called Kampong Bahru.

    NS6 was supposed to be part of the Woodlands MRT extension in the initial plan. The station was due to be located in the Sungei Kadut Industrial Estate. However, it just did not make sense to have an MRT station in the middle of an industrial estate, and the plan for the station was scrapped. However, the alphanumeric code has been left empty as there may be 'future developments' to 'warrant cause for building and operating the station'.
    NS12 was due to be built in between Yishun & Sembawang stations, where a new housing estate Simpang was proposed. However, due to a change in plan, the Simpang HDB estate project was indefinitely shelved. NS12 is a provisional station which will be built if URA sees the need to redevelop a completely new housing estate there, though there are currently no plans to do so, at least not in the near future.
