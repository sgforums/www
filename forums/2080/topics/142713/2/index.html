    Its blurry but a fair shot, nonetheless. That loco is a 19 Class shunter. It is most likely to be No. 19103 as this is the only known shunter assigned to Tanjong Pagar at present. Shunters do not work the main line. They work in the yard such as arranging consists into various sidings or fetching the coaches from the shed to the platform and vice versa. That is why they are painted in bright orange. Normally, it is paired with a brake van where another crewman directs the driver as well as help to couple/uncouple the cars.

    The 19 Class is the last shunter model of KTMB. They were manufactured in Japan by Hitachi and were new in 1983. This diesel-electric locomotive has 650 HP and a Bo-Bo wheel configuration.
