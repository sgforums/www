    KTM Train derailment accident pictures

    On the afternoon of 14 August 2006, a KTM sugar liner (full container consist with sacks of sugar inside) from Prai, Penang, Malaysia to Singapore yard (Kampong Bahru Road area) derailed near Lower Delta Road, Singapore. This had caused disruptions to KTM services in Singapore for approximately one day.

    By 8pm of 15 August 2006, wreckage of sugar liner are removed from the tracks, while the tracks are relaid manually. Services resumed back to normal at sometime near 8pm that day (refer to KTMB press release).

    However, as of now, the wreckage have yet to be cleared from the scene---they are only cleared to one side of the railway line. A tent was set up near to the scene to oversee the situation.
