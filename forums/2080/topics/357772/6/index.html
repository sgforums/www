    Enjoyed myself today, just a detailed story of my trip.

    Coz I was at Eunos at around 1.30pm, so I took 93 to harbourfront and changed to MRT, to Serangoon. Then I was thinking if I can take the free train to save money on the 158, as I booked Bartley, then they say 4:30 then can go in, CRAP right?

    So I walked to Serangoon Int to take 158, and caught 2637, and alight at the wrong stop, was the exit of the station. It was like.....LOL when i asked the staff. So I walked up and crossed the overhead bridge and gave my confirmation form to the staff. I wored my wristband and walked into the station.

    When I walked into the station, saw the thingy blocked the entrance. So the staff opened, and I walked down to the platform. Seeing ppl solving the MLRT Syatem map, I asked the staff then I went up and play. After playing, I recieved a badge written "A HAPPY JOURNEY STARTS LIKE THAT"  The staff say " Wah sei, break record seh...."

    I went to the platform and boarded the trainset through the last door, should be trainset 25 or 52 if I am not wrong. I took the whole ride(with looping) and recorded them down. If you see me holding a Olympus black cam, that's me, also saying out the announcements at the same time.

    At Marymount from Bartley, I played some games, should be the matching game, I focused on the puzzle, and finally my turn to go up, I so "kia-su" that I opened two at a time -_-" Then I recieved a big bunch of sovenirs.

    Then I went to Serangoon and din do much, forgotten what I did

    I went to Lor Chuan and played the so called "Tic Tac Toe", must answer question then can do a move one, I won a cute little kid LOL

    Then I went to bishan, din wanna say much, then went home after eating McD by 59, and 28,then I end my day already.

    A lot of train fans seen there at all stations
