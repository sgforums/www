LTA awards contract for Beauty World station

  • QX179R
    25 Mar 09, 20:08

    The Land Transport Authority has awarded a contract to design and build an MRT station at Beauty World to an Australian firm.

    The $340 million deal will go to McConnell Dowell SEA, the second civil contract awarded for Downtown Line stage 2.

    The construction firm will also be responsible for the tunnels associated with the station.

    Located along Upper Bukit Timah Road, the station at Beauty World will double up as a Civil Defence shelter.

    Construction is scheduled to start in the second quarter of 2009 and is expected to complete in 2015.
