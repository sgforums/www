    Question 2.
    Which trainset was involved in the making of the Anti-Terrorism video shown in SMRT train platform Plasma Screen TVs?

    [A] 049/050
    [B] 045/046
    [C] 075/076
    [D] 079/080
    [E] Nah, I don't like the Plasma Screen TVs in the platforms.

    B. The video was produced on the car 3046.

    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    Question 3.
    Which trainset was involved during the SMRT Open House: SMRT Cram Jam 2007 in February?

    [A] 027/028
    [B] 127/128
    [C] 061/062
    [D] 057/058
    [E] I didn't go for the Open House.

    A. 027/028 was advertised with "SMRT Cram Jam" Advertisement solely for the Open House before undergoing refurbishment.

    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    Question 4.
    How many minutes does it take for a train to travel from Yio Chu Kang to Khatib?

    [A] 3
    [B] 4
    [C] 5
    [D] 6
    [E] I don't take the NSL.

    C. It takes 5 minutes to travel from YCK to KTB.

    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    Question 5.
    Which of the following was the first Alstom Metropolis trainset that arrived in Singapore from France for the Circle Line (CCL)?

    [A] 001/002/003
    [B] 118/119/120
    [C] 043/044/045
    [D] 031/032/033
    [E] Whats CCL?

    D. 031/032/033 was the first trainset to arrive in Singapore, bearing the numbers 8031, 8032 and 8033.

    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    Question 6.
    When did the first ever Siemens C651 trainset, 201/202, arrive in Singapore?

    [A] January 1994
    [B] February 1994
    [C] March 1994
    [D] April 1994
    [E] I wasn't born at that time.

    B. The whole train was assembled by January 1994 and shipped to Singapore in February 1994.

    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    Question 7.
    Which trainset used to bear a Guardian Advertisement?

    [A] 007/008
    [B] 085/086
    [C] 039/040
    [D] 105/106
    [E] What's Guardian?

    A. 007/008 used to have Guardian Multi-Colour Advertisement before undergoing refurbishment.

    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    Question 8.
    What is the Train Run Number (TRN) of the first train running from Jurong East, Southbound towards Marina Bay?

    [A] 051
    [B] 050
    [C] 078
    [D] 079
    [E] I'm too lazy to wake up at 5am.

    C. 051 starts from Bishan and then turns around at Yishun. 050 runs to Kranji (Off-service) and starts service from there. 078 is the first passenger service train that starts off from JE.

    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    Question 9.
    Which refurbished Kawasaki C151 train did not have a specialized logo for it's glass panels?

    [A] 001/002
    [B] 003/004
    [C] 005/006
    [D] 007/008
    [E] What's a glass panel?

    C. 005/006 did not have any logo for it's glass panels; the rest had.

    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    Question 10.
    How many crossover tracks (Both sides) are there between Ang Mo Kio and Yio Chu Kang?

    [A] 3
    [B] 4
    [C] 5
    [D] 6
    [E] Whats a crossover track?

    C. 2 on each side for the middle tracks to transfer over, and 1 more for NB Crossover SB just off YCK.

    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    Question 11.
    What was the original purpose for the introduction of Kawasaki/Nippon Sharyo C751Bs?

    [A] Woodlands Extension
    [B] Circle Line
    [C] Changi Airport Extension
    [D] Pioneer/Joo Koon Extension
    [E] What's a C751B?

    C. 21 trains were purchased; some were for the Changi Airport Extension whilst the rest were to ply on both NSL and EWL.

    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    Question 12.
    Which train was involved in the major power cut in the North East Line (NEL) from Dhoby Ghaut to Harbourfront?

    [A] 017/018
    [B] 043/044
    [C] 025/026
    [D] 039/040
    [E] I never heard of the NEL.

    C. 71026 was the leading cab that caused the major power cut.

    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    Question 13.
    How many wheels does a normal SMRT train possess?

    [A] 72
    [B] 60
    [C] 30
    [D] 48
    [E] My maths fails.

    D. 2 boogies each of 4 wheels per car = 8. 6 carriages = 8 x 6 = 48.

    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    Question 14.
    Which NEL station is below road levels?

    [A] NE15
    [B] NE17
    [C] NE03
    [D] NE16
    [E] I never see leh.

    B. NE17 (Punggol) is the only station in the NEL that runs below roads.

    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    Question 15.

    [A] 021/022
    [B] 039/040
    [C] 085/086
    [D] 107/108
    [E] I don't care about Singapore's Birthday anyway.

    C. 085/086 used to possess "Happy Birthday Singapore" Advertisements before undergoing many many changes.

    Question 16.
    Which trainset used to bear a Guardian Advertisement?

    [A] 021/022
    [B] 107/108
    [C] 105/106
    [D] 075/076
    [E] Why got 2 same questions one?

    A. 021/022 was the 2nd train apart from 007/008 that had Guardian Multi-Colour Advertisement before undergoing refurbishment.
