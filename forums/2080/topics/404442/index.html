    Post any train records in this dedicated thread here! The records can be from the refurbishment of trains, first train station to open and so on.

    Here are some examples of train records:

    MRT/LRT stations

    Toa Payoh - Singapore's 1st MRT station to commence revenue service

    Braddell - Singapore's 2nd MRT station to commence revenue service

    Bishan - Singapore's 3rd MRT station to commence revenue service

    Ang Mo Kio - Singapore's 4th MRT station to commence revenue service

    Yio Chu Kang - Singapore's 5th MRT station to commence revenue service

    Bras Basah - Singapore's deepest MRT station

    Dhoby Ghaut - Singapore's 2nd deepest MRT station

    Raffles Place - Singapore's 3rd deepest MRT station

    Redhill - Singapore's smallest MRT station

    Woodleigh - The MRT station that is closed for the longest period (2003 - current)

    Changi Airport - Singapore's 1st MRT station to have fare gates at the platform level

    Boon Lay - The longest terminal station (from Wikipedia)

    Pasir Ris - Singapore's 1st elevated MRT station to start operations of PSDs

    Yishun - Singapore's 2nd elevated MRT station to start operations of PSDs

    Jurong East - Singapore's 3rd elevated MRT station to start operations of PSDs

    MRT/LRT Trains

    005/006 - 1st C151 to be refurbished

    053/054 - 1st C151 to have STARIS installed and operated

    089/082 and 101/102 - 1st 2 C151s to be modified

    047/048 - 1st train to be sprayed with graffiti

    MRT/LRT Depots

    CHD - 1st depot to be trespassed by unauthorised personnel
