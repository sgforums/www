    4 of them start from AMK. One starts from Marina Bay all the way to Jurong East and continue onto the EWL to Pasir Ris and continue serving EWL til the end of the AM Peak usually 2 park at Changi and 3 park a Ulu Pandan. In the evening peak, 3 trains from Ulu Pandan will crossover to NSL to do Yew Tee evening shuttle service for 2-3 trips and one trip down to AMK where it terminaes and heads back to Bishan

    Changi Depot trains need to park at Ulu Pandan on the next day before being able to be deployed onto the NSL.
