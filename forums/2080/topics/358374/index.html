    Originally posted by PWNED32:

    wait wait wait guys!!!. I'm new to this whole train spotting thing. so what am i suppose to see in here?

    See the train numbers.

    Normally, the train numbers are made up of odd and even numbers. The odd number is always the smaller one.

    Let n be the odd number. The train will be:

    XXn/XXn + 1

    E.g. 123 is the odd number. The train number will be 123+1=124. Therefore the train will be 123/124. However this train was coupled wrongly and became 123/126 and 124/125.
