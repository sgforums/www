      Originally posted by ditzy:  

    Trains don't stop at traffic lights.

    Signals... 

    Trains...
    Advantages
    - Faster
    - Lesser breakdowns
    - More user-friendly (announcements)
    - More capacity
    - Better frequency than buses

    Disadvantages
    - If theres a disruption, the whole line would be affected
    - Limited routes
    - Nosier at times
    - Accidents cause serious injuries or death of many
    - Breakdowns cause disruptions

    Buses...
    Advantages
    - Can go wherever the roads take them
    - If there's a disruption, the service of the route can carry on as usual
    - Accidents may not cause as much injuries and deaths
    - Breakdown buses can be replaced very fast

    Disadvantages
    - Not very fast
    - Not very user friendly unless there's PIS, even though there's PIS also not very user friendly because there's no audio (Fare stages may not be understood by passengers)
    - Capacity not very high
    - Frequency not very good for most services
