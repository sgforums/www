    Required Files:

    OpenAL32.dll (C:\Windows\System32)

    wrap_oal.dll (C:\Windows\System32)

    Tao.OpenAl.dll (C:\Program Files\TaoFramework\bin)

    Tao.OpenGl.dll (C:\Program Files\TaoFramework\bin)

    Tao.SDL.dll (C:\Program Files\TaoFramework\bin)

    alut.dll (C:\Program Files\TaoFramework\lib)

    SDL.dll (C:\Program Files\TaoFramework\lib)

    SDL_mixer.dll (C:\Program Files\TaoFramework\lib)
