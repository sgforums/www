    Nope, the train doors will not suddenly open while the train is moving.

    This is because all of our MRT trains have a protection system whereby when the train doors are open,the train will not move even when the driver hits the accelerating button accidently.(Including driverless trains)

    Like wised when the train is moving and the driver hits the door opening button accidently ,the doors will not open until the train stops.
