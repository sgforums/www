    Ok, as I today had went to One-North / Kent Ridge, I can assure you that between them got a place called: Temporarily Staging Area (TSA) and one aspect of C830 is to do with with C830 TAS-ONR (ONR = One-North)

    From what I have deduced, it would probably mean a Stadium - Mountbatten style and not a Paya Lebar station style with the following reasons:

    1: Where else can trains in Buona Vista / One North extend if there is a middle platform? The Paya Lebar is possible is because it was meant to connect the "interchange-less" Eastern Region Line.

    2: One North has only 2 exits, with a rectangle shape (Instead of a "T" shaped)

    3: If you recap, if the Circle Line opens in order (Stage 1 to be the first opened), then the trains in terms of HBF bound will first terminate at Stadium then proceed to middle track and return. It is the same for Stage 2, except that it is not a middle track but a split track in Tai Seng (For Stage 1 case can refer to Outram Park LTE and Stage 2 case can refer to Bartley bound trains before CCL 1 and 2 opens, in case you failed to get a DNB on CCLD which many does as the trains' doors was closing too fast.). Stage 3 case, is present day Marymount terminus, and hence, in terms of logical order, Stage 4 terminus which is Kent Ridge, should be similar to Stage 3 (Crossover BEFORE station instead of after).
