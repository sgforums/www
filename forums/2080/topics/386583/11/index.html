    1. C751B 351-352 WDL to JUR

    At JUR, I felt like passing motion, so I went to the toilet. Also, the EB train was arriving in 6 mins.

    After I came out of the toilet, I saw an EB train arriving at the platform. Thinking that it was a C151, I did not rush to go up to the platform. However, after I heard a soft whining on deceleration, I knew that it was a C751B. 

    When I got to the platform, the DCWL was already blinking. I just boarded the C751B, not caring about the manufacturer. (I personally prefer the ones made by Kawasaki)

    Then, SMRT gave me something in return for easing myself:
