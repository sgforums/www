    Originally posted by c751bLoVuR:

    Hmm. Anyone know why there's a breaktime on LTE almost 1pm?

    NSL
    NB train - LTE train - NB train - NB train - LTE train

    This is a special case that occurs between TRNs 217 and 211. As you already know, trains alternate between LTE ones and full route ones. At the start of LTE, TRN 217 would be deployed from BSD, and as it departs AMK, TRN 211 would still be on its way to BSH. TRN 217 will be at BSH SB, while TRN 211 will be at BSH NB. Thus, when TRN 211 arrives at AMK, a SB train would already have left. Since a stop time is necessary, another SB train will pass before TRN 211 joins in to SB. Therefore the 2 train gap.
