    How do you differentiate a C151 and a C751B

     

    Share your interesting ways =P

    For me

     

    1) Colour of motor gearbox, Rusty - C151 | Shiny - C751B

    2) Train's head, slightly tilted - C151 | Tilted at a steeper angle - C751B

    3) Motor sound 

    4) Arrival sound, Normal - C151 | Station and platform vibrate like siao - C751B

    5) LED light position on train head, Sticking out - C151 | Covered by train head's design - C751B

    6) Trainset number (Everyone should know this)

    7) Train length, Longer - C151 | Slightly shorter - C751B

    8) Train flooring, Plain - C151 | With some spots or some design on it - C751B
