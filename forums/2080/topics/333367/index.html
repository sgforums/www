    The Hwa chong guys are doing it again, that time they suggest Hwa Chong as the name of the station outside their school (which was tentatively Duchess), now they want that station named after their founder, Mr Tan Kah Kee. There are three choices for Duchess: Duchess, Watten and Kah Kee.

    Yes Mr Tan Kah Kee has been a great contributor to Singapore and the Chinese community, but naming the station outside Hwa Chong after their founder is too much. Already Zaobao is reporting that the students would vote for Kah Kee, so chances of them winning is quite high if there is only a few people participating in the naming exercise. But is it necessary for a single non-university institution to have a station with a name related to their school outside their school, if like this, every other school with a MRT station should participate in naming competitions naming it after their school.
