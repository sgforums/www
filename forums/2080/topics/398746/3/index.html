    Temporary Road Diversion along Marina Coastal Drive
     

    To facilitate the construction of the North South Line Extension, the Land Transport Authority will be temporarily diverting the stretch of Marina Coastal Drive between lamp post no. 9 (adjacent to the Marina Coastal Expressway Project) and Marina South Pier building from 7 am on Thursday, 13 May 2010.

    The existing bus stop in front of Marina South Pier will not be affected by the temporary road diversion. The temporary road diversion is expected to remain in place till December 2010.

    Directional and information signs will be placed along Marina Coastal Drive and at the bus stop to inform and guide motorists and commuters.

    Please refer to the attached map for more information.

    Should motorists have any queries, they can contact the LTA hotline at 1800-CALL LTA.
