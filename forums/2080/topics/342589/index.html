    The five stations are:

    CC12 Bartley

    CC13/NE12 Serangoon

    CC14 Lorong Chuan

    CC15/NS 17 Bishan

    CC 16 Marymount

     

    Stage 1 of the Circle Line will not open until Stage 2 is complete due the Kim Chuan depot situated between Bartley & Tai Seng Station.
