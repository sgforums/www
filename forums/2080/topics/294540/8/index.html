    Just to confirm these terms:

    Ext. ad - Outer body ad only, but interior is plain white
    e.g. Courtesy ad on 039/040

    Int. ad - Interior ad only, exterior is original livery
    e.g. Champion's Showdown ad on 021/022, 087/088, 101/102

    Full body ad - Both ext. and int. with the ad
    e.g. Sony Ericsson ad on 093/094

    Window ad - Only window has ad, int. and ext. is plain
    e.g. Escape to Egypt with SMRT ad on 103/104
