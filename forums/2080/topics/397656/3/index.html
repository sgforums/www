    Flat wheels is quite hard to fix. First of course there needs to be a newly forged set of wheel component, then they have to take the wheelset out of the bogie, take out the wheels from the axle and fit new wheels in.

    Apparently because our bogies have motors mounted in the WN Drive configuration so its easier somehow to remove.

    Wheels can be reconditioned (made completely circular again) but that will be some cost.

    The series 0 (C151) motors are already one of the best for its class. Chopper control 135 kW and 3.6 km/h/s acceleration, very hard to find. Plus 210 tons of deadweight.
