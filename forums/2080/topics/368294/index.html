    This is for openBVE:

    The "-" key on number pad: Play the "For the convinence of all passengers, please move towards the center of the car, thank you" announcement. Press again to stop.

    The "+" key on number pad: Play the doors closing announcement.

    The Enter key: Sound the train horn.
