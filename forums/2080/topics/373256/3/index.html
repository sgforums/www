    Something that i have not commented for a long time - I don't feel that when you don't meet a train it is "unlucky". Likewise, your "lucky" trains are those that bring you luck, and not that you see it often. Though the definitions of both "lucky" and "unlucky" are wrong, saying "lucky train" sounds more appropriate than saying "unlucky train" in terms of the number of times seeing it. I feel that saying a train is "unlucky" would mean you have met with a mishap or some unhappy incidents because of that train, which is 031/032 for me.
