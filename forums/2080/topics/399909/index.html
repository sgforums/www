    LRT doors suddenly open in the middle of journey

    STOMPer Esther was travelling on the Bukit Panjang LRT towards Keat Hong Station when suddenly, the doors opened. If the LRT was fully packed with people, those standing close to the doors will surely have fallen onto the tracks, she says.

    She said in an email (May 18):

    "It was during peak hour, at about 6.40pm.

    "The LRT was travelling toward Keat Hong Station when the doors suddenly opened.

    "Luckily, it was not fully packed till the doors with people, like when it left Choa Chu Kang Station.

    "If it was, people will surely be falling onto the tracks when the doors opened.

    "Can the authorities please kindly check the technical aspects of your trains carefully everyday?

    "Imagine the consequences if people were to really fall onto the tracks in a similar incident."
