    What is going to happen is that this perimeter line is a mixture of different lines:

    Stage 1:

    Changi Airport → Pasir Ris → Punggol → (Seletar - Yishun East) → Sembawang --- No comments, it should be the planned North Shore Line in concept plan 2001.

    Stage 2:

    Sembawang → Admiralty → Woodlands --- WTH? A future line is duplicating with NSL? My take is either this is a NSL Woodlands Extension OR North Shore Line extends to:

    Sembawang → Senoko (TSL) → Marsiling (Near Woodlands Checkpoint)

    Stage 3:

    Woodlands → Lim Chu Kang → Nanyang --- LCK? Too near to live firing area, unless there is a HDB estate gonna to be rebuilt in the next 30 years, I dont forsee such a line. If there is any, it would likely to be:

    Marsiling (Near Woodlands Checkpoint) → Neo Tiew / Kranji / Sungei Kadut → Lim Chu Kang → Nanyang

    But since this sounds too crazy, then it would likely to be:

    DTL Stage 2 extension (Who say it must end at Bukit Panjang or Kranji / Sungei Kadut. The concept plan 2001 shows this part should have extended all the way to Marsiling Checkpoint and turn a curve to meet the present TSL, maybe 1 station here can compenstate 1 station in my version of Bedok spur line posted earlier.) + Holland Line (Possibly together with Jurong Region LRT as well).

    Detailed description:

    Marsiling (Near Woodlands Checkpoint) → Gali Batu / Bukit Panjang → Hillview / Beauty World → Bukit Batok East (Toh Yi / Toh Tuck) → Bukit Batok Interchange (Yes, the concept plan did draw a line interchange with BBT and CCK --- excluding the LRT) → Bukit Batok West (Near MI) → Hongkah / Jurong Canal? → Nanyang

    Stage 4:

    Nanyang → Pioneer → Tuas South → West Coast --- Jurong Region LRT for Nanyang - Pioneer while Pioneer - Tuas South would be BLE + Tuas West & South extension since it would likely to be no demand to build Jurong Region LRT towards there or build another MRT spur line for that sector.

    Tuas South - West Coast case will be like my illustration of Tuas Express Line / Western Region Line, even though they might consider to connect Kent Ridge instead as the following reason:

    1: Kent Ridge has a slidings like Outram Park. All stations with special crossovers will make them a bright station. (With bright future) Source: It has a Temporarily Holding Area or sort of and it is known from people who went to KCD tour that when CCL 4 / 5 opens, the SWT will terminate in One North (And use the ONR - KNT slidings, similar to Mountbatten - Stadium case.) and Paya Lebar middle track.

    2: Read on to Stage 5 of this passage.

    Stage 5:

    West Coast → Pasir Panjang → Telok Blangah → Harbourfront → Marina South -- Very obvious, this is Circle Line Stage 5, with the Harbourfront - Marina South sector is more likely to be "Stage 6", and instead of South, it should be closing the loop / or top up with Marina South extension in NSL will do.

    What I forsee is that instead of a new line duplicating this sector, they should do this (If they do the Stage 4 part of this passage, they should top up with this to make it better)

    Kent Ridge → Alexandra → Bukit Merah → Radin Mas → Bukit Prumei → Keppel (And interchange with "NE2", and optionally) → Marina South / Barrage

    Stage 6:

    Marina South → East Coast → Marine Parade → Upper East Coast → Changi Airport --- No comments. This should be the route for Eastern Region Line, with Changi Airport extended upwards to Changi Point.
