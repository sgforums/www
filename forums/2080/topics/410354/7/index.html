    My take is, if there a line along Bukit Merah estate in between of EWL Redhill - Buona Vista sector and CCL Stage 5, then it will have to interchange with Kent Ridge instead of Haw Par Villa. These are my reasons:

    1: To facilitate people from the West / South-West to get to NUH / NUS quickly. I think more people will go to NUH / NUS rather than Haw Par Villa (Except on festive seasons.)

    2: Last time I saw Haw Par Villa was like.... I think there is a lack of space for any possible interchange, not even a CCL version of Newton. (Newton will be interchange for DTL, but that was not planned when they build the NSL in the late 80s.)

    3: From Kent Ridge, the line can go straight towards Alexandra / Depot Road and heads to Bukit Merah Estate, but for Haw Par Villa, it would requires a sharp left turn.

     

    However, even if the possible benefits for Kent Ridge to become Interchange, these are some of the difficulties involved when constructing it:

    1: Kent Ridge is a "big station", but its surroundings is expressway and NUH.

    2: Both Kent Ridge and Haw Par Villa, to proceed towards Bukit Merah, it is likely to involve a hilly terrain.

    3: From West Coast / Clementi West St X estates area and head towards Kent Ridge, it will form a "-\-" shape, somehow.
