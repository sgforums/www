    I have to share my opinion.

    I'm not trying to offend fans of the old announcement system's voice but I have to say that the new one is better.

    Why?

    2) The old announcement system is to an extent broken. What I mean is that over the past few years, so many new announcements have been recorded that it does get to you hearing so many different voices.

    As such, I think that SMRT actually is doing something useful for the commuters.
