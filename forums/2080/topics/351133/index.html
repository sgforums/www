    ME: excuse me. i cant go out. can u check my card for me pls.

    MRT Staff: u owe 0.16 cents. thats y cannot go out

    ME: but i just bought concession. i even have the receipt.

    ME: still cannot

    ME: ok i top up.

    after she topped my card i proceed to go out but the light still flashed red so i went back to her again.

    ME: erm i still cant go out

    MRT Staff: wait i check for u. u nv scan properly. wat station did u come from?

    ME: so its not about the top up at all la. is i nv scan properly.

    MRT Staff: it is both. u need minimum 10 cents or 5 cents to go out. gere i help u already.

    If its really bout the 16 cents that i owe, how come i can go in and out without any trouble for the past few months. thats not all. when i tapped out my card i realised they charged me for my trip. even though i had concession! so i went back to her again

    ME: they charged me for the trip but i have concession

    MRT Staff: OK I DO FOR U NICELY! u go ticket office and collect ur refund ok!

    and so i finally went out  of the station after paying 10 dollars and getting scolded even though its not entirely my fault. yea. sure i owe 16 cents. bu i have been able to go in and out so u gotta admit that the lady is at fault too. she just kept insisting its because i owed money and didnt want to check what was the problem properly. if its really bout the money i would have been not able to go in and out months ago and i would be able to go out after i topped out. Nice customer service huh. i was angry but i kept clam and speaked to her nicely but got shouted and blamed at for nothing.

    infact, i went to the ticket office as instructed and after 10 minutes of clicking on the com, the guy gave me a receipt and ask me to collect one week later. the best part about all of this is that the value claimed on the receipt states $0.00 . nothing. zero. lol. when i asked wat amount i was owed, the guy said he doesn;t know and ask me to check 1 week later. so do i check 1 week later and see myself embarased again because i tried to claim nth? wat a "nice"  train trip and served by such "good"  service huh. lol.
