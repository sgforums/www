    You need any directions inside IMH can ask me, i very familiar with that place(go there very often).
    From Hougang MRT, take bus 101 or 161 to IMH. 161 stops right outside IMH while 101 stops opposite. 325 goes there too but takes a longer time.

    If you taking 43, alight opposite IMH there(1 stop before HG Avenue 4)and cross the overhead bridge, turn right after u come down from overhead bridge and right again. Walk along the pedestrian walkway and walk in from the small gate along Buangkok Green quite near the bus stop and walk all the way straight along the footpath and you will reach a road, walk straight and you will reach BLk 1 aka main office of IMH, walk straight and turn right, u will reach Ren Ci Blk8/9. Turn left you will reach SILRA.

    Even if u r inside IMH, you wont get to see mentally ill patients UNLESS you enter the wards, so its quite safe (Soccer field exception)
