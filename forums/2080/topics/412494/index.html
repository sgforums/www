    According to The New Paper today, SCDF is selling its MRT train carriage which is used for training, as well as 34 used vehicles, including ambulances and fire engines.

    The MRT train carriage looks and feels like the real thing inside and even comes with its own set of tracks.

    The items were open for public viewing on monday and tuesday at SCDF Mandai Training Village, The public auction is held today at 10am.
