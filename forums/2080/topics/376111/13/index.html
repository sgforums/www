    Ok...Answers to be revealed for the trivia:

    1) 123/126, 124/125, 207/231, 215/226, 129/010, 211/227, 057/112, 058/111
    2) C.
    3) B/C (Refer to Latest Adverts on Trains Parts 1 and 2).
     041/042 and 085/086: DBS
     039/040 and 115/116: Burger King

    4) 089/090
    5) 1 min or 1 min 40s (1 min 40s is the average)
    6) 2 (when Singapore Expo doesn't have a large scale event) and 3 (when there are large scale events.) C651s are the more commonly used trains on CGL.
    7) Function 1: Depot
        Function 2: Short trips (as in Yishun, LTE and Yew Tee Shuttle, CCL Peak Hour  Trips and lastly CGL shuttle)
        Function 3: In case of emergency, the middle track will function (as in the case of the incident at Tanah Merah.)     Note: if you list Function 2 or 3, you are right.
