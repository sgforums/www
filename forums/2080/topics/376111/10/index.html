    Answers:

    1. Door 17. (1 mark) 3 is the centre point where both doors meet when closed, platform side. (1 mark) (If you guys wanna know, 1 is the left-most side of the left door, 2 is the right-most side of the right door and 4 is the centre point where both doors meet when closed, track side.)

    3. 4 power notches (0.5 mark), 1 neutral notch (0.5 mark), 3 brake notches (0.5 mark). 1 emergency brake notch (bonus 0.5 mark). There is no Dead Man's Switch or Pedal (0.5 mark).

    5. Wye junction. (2 marks)
