    4: Which 2 MRT stations has skipped exit (I.e Having a largest alphabet used on exit as H but there is no Exit C)

    List 2 exits that was skipped (One exit per station respectively, so assuming that there is an option E - Changi Airport and Punggol and you choose it, you should state which exit alphabet was skipped in both Changi Airport and Punggol respectively.)

    Sample format for Qn 11 (If Person B chose option E in Qn 4, Changi Airport and Punggol and wrote the following for Qn 11.)

    11: D,B. -> This means that Changi Airport has skipped Exit D and Punggol has skipped Exit B.

    Hint: In the case that you feel that in question 4 with the assumption of Option E exists, "Changi Airport" and "Ang Mo Kio" has skipped exit, then you are definetely wrong. Please choose either "option E" (Changi Airport and Punggol) or "option C" (Outram Park and Ang Mo Kio). Also if person A, selects "C" in qn 4, and answer D,B in Qn 11 while person B as above, selects "E" in qn 4 and  answer D,B in Qn 11, they meant different exits since person A refers to Outram Park has skipped exit D and Ang Mo Kio has skipped Exit B while person B feels that Changi Airport has skipped exit D and Punggol has skipped exit B.

     

    For this bonus question, one exit worth 10 points, so a total of 20 points is awarded in this bonus question. (All other MCQ worth 1 point each)

     

    I hope it is not that confusing. In summary, in order to get Qn 11 to be correct, your question 4 must be correct as a pre-requiste. I cannot entertain in this example case where people who chose option E and assuming it is correct, and write D,B when they mean Punggol has skipped Exit D and Changi Airport has skipped Exit B (Answer in backwards) for clarity and fair reasons.
