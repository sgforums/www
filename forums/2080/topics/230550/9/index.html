    no. all are still on old ATO mode but 021/022 ran on new ATO mode for that particular trip.

    SB had its ATO codes changed late last month. now it halts briefly on SB at crossover after YCK before accelerating to limit. it also halts earlier moving out of BIS resulting in slower cruise inside tunnel to BDL. on fast mode, out of BIS it halts at same position just before entering tunnel, brakes briefly and accelerates to 70 as per last time. these two changes have increased travelling time on that sectors and trains now tend to run on fast more at AMK-NOV to make up for it. no changes observed on NB.
