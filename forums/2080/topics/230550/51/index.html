    How many times have I said over and over again....

    Trains DO NOT have a fixed depot they can be parked in!!!

    Every few days they can be switched; one day it might be at Bishan, the next day it is at Changi. Why so?

    Simply, there are certain train timetables that indicate different parking facilities. For example if the Operator takes a train from Changi depot, he might be instructed to park it at Ulu Pandan when his duty ends, VICE VERSA.

    Alternatively, trains that depart Bishan depot for Pasir Ris bound services; 3 of them end at Changi depot whilst the other 3 do until PM Peak and park at Ulu Pandan depot.
