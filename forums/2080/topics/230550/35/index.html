    The main refurbishment works are often carried out inside the main workshop. Those who went to SMRT Open House last year may know about it.

     

    If you could see a car or 2 from your MRT train under a small shelter near to the Train Washing Machine, that's where part of the works are done. You could see a refurb train at the inspection bay everytime you pass the depot by MRT.

     

    For those who spotted half of an MRT at the stabling yard nearest to the maintanance crew yard, that's where they store MRTs which are undergoing repairs or refurbishing on Weekdays (Morning till late afternoon) when there's simply no space for it.
