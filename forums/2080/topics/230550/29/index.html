    I got include 057 leh.

    During Sundays and PH, trains are rolled out from the first TRN 051 to 068. That makes 18 trains running full day on the NSL. Same thing for EWL.

    For weekdays, trains operate on off-peak before the morning rush hour, and during rush hour trains are again introduced, however some early morning off-peak trains return to the depot whilst the rest (18 ~ 19 depending on the line) run for the rest of the morning till late afternoon.

    When the evening peak reaches, trains are again introduced, and those trains that have operated for the rest of the morning and afternoon return to the depot, while the rest run till the day closes.

    That's why you get a jumbled up TRN pattern during weekdays, but a straightforward and consecutive pattern during Sundays/PH, because the trains running on Sunday/PH run for the entire day.
