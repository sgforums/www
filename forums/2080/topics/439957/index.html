    STOMPer SN spotted Transport Minister Lui Tuck Yew at Bishan Circle Line station. He appeared to be observing the situation at the station, where there have been commuter jams recently.

    Just a few days after the new section of the Circle Line opened on Oct 8, massive crowds filled the Bishan Circle Line station. Thankfully the situation has eased, said STOMPer SN.

    The Transport Minister has been previously spotted on public transport by STOMPers on a number of occasions.

    Wrote the STOMPer (Oct 19):

    "I saw the Transport Minister today on the CCL at Bishan at 8.10am.

    "It looked like he was here to check out the situation at Bishan.

    "It's certainly much improved from the first day the CCL opened!"
