    The first three new lines will open between 2012 and 2014, followed by two more by 2020.

    Marina Bay got the known MRT lines:

    North South Line (Marina Bay / Marina Pier as terminus. Marina Barrage extension is possible as well) ---- Opening in 2014 for Pier

    Circle Line (Marina Bay Extension, Bayfront, Marina Bay terminus) --- 2012

    Thomson Line (Possibly Marina Pier or Bay and Marina Barrage) --- 2018

    Eastern Region Line (Probably Marina Barrage)  --- 2020
