    from smrt website: http://www.solution1.com/smrt2008/trains/JEMP_FAQ_01.asp

    How will train service at these stations be affected?
    A shuttle train service will operate between Jurong East and Lakeside Stations while a looped train service will operate between Lakeside and Joo Koon Stations. Commuters travelling from Joo Koon Station to Clementi Station will need to alight at Lakeside Station and board the train bound for Jurong East Station. Commuters continuing their journey to Clementi Station may wish to take the free shuttle bus service to Clementi Station or seek alternative modes of transport. 

    Please be informed that trains plying between Jurong East and Joo Koon Stations will be travelling at a slower speed. Do pay attention to service announcements and look out for directional signs when travelling in the MRT network during the above mentioned weekend.

    While we have made considerable effort to ensure a smooth journey, inconveniences and longer travelling time are unavoidable. We seek all commuters’ understanding.
