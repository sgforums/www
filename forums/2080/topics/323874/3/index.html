  • E5
    17 Sep 09, 20:24

    Because of technical constraint. The upgrading work must not affect the normal operation of the train during the construction period. If they were to built the station like City Hall or Raffles Place, then there would be two problem;

    -One of the track will have to demolished and it's track realigned

    -Jurong East station is already quite tall. Adding an additional storey pair of tracks will be a engineering challenge itself.

    Beside, the above two points would mean that train operations will be compromised during the construction period, which goes against what I've said earlier.
