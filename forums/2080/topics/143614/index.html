    the following is a feedback reply from LTA regarding the A & A works at NS17. was surprised that they will be building another platform at NSL.


    PLATFORM SCREEN DOORS FOR NS17 BISHAN

    FEEDBACK NUMBER:

    Thank you for your email of 03-JUL-2005.

    You may like to know that the upgrading and Addition and Alteration (A&A) works to the existing station is in progress together with the construction of a new station to interchange with Circle Line (CCL).

    The A&A works include air-conditioning and installing platform screen doors at the existing station platform. In addition, a new platform would be constructed to serve the southbound train service, leaving the existing platform for serving northbound trains only. This would ease congestion at the current platform. The A&A works are expected to be completed by end 2008.

    Once again, thank you for your feedback.

    Yours sincerely,
