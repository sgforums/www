    The possibility of the MRT line going into JB is next to nothing. From what I can recall, the SG govt plan is for all KTM trains to stop at Woodlands and henceforth, the passengers would transfer to the MRT. But that would mean the loss of the KTM land in Singapore, something which the MY govt is not prepared to do and with good reason. The Singapore route has the largest proportion in terms of passenger traffic and revenue due to the exchange rate.

    This issue is purely between the 2 governments so let's leave it at that. Anyway, it's good for railway fans that it didn't materialize. We can still enjoy the sight and sound of a locomotive chugging down the tracks with passenger or freight cars.
