    Commuters asked to leave MRT train at ToaPayoh after air-con unit emitted smoke

    Commuters travelling on a southbound train at Toa Payoh this afternoon had to be off-loaded
    after smoke was detected from one of the air-conditioning units in one of the train carriages.

    938LIVE received several phone calls when the incident happened at around 1.25pm.

    A spokesperson from the Singapore Mass Rapid Transit Corporation says the train was removed from service after passengers disembarked.

    This resulted in a train service delay of about seven minutes.

    SMRT apologised for the inconvenience caused and is investigating the cause of the incident.
