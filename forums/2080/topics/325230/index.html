    New Bishan MRT southbound platform to open on Sunday

    The new south-bound platform of the Bishan MRT Station will start operating from Sunday.

    The Land Transport Authority says the new platform is fully air-conditioned as it is below ground level and does not receive any natural ventilation.

    At present, the trains heading south towards the city as well as north-bound trains share the same platform.

    LTA anticipates that the existing platform will no longer have sufficient capacity to cope with increased ridership, particularly when Stage 3 of the Circle Line opens in the middle of next year. --938Live
