    Think bus fans are bad? Take a look at these overzealous rail enthusiasts

    STOMPer Angela shared a video of rail enthusiasts running amok in their attempt of snapping photographs when there was a disruption in the Downtown Line train service yesterday (Dec 22).

    The STOMPer said they were shouting remarks and train numbers like they own the train line.

    The train service was delayed for 15 minutes, reports The Straits Times Online.

    In an email to STOMP, the STOMPer wrote:

    "Look at these rail enthusiasts making a scene when the there was a disruption in the Downtown Line service yesterday (Dec 22).

    "They are seen running around, shouting remarks and train numbers like they own the train line!

    "This is all over a faulty train being pulled out of service.

    "Can they be more disciplined and not be nuisance to others?"

    Recently, there has also been controversy surrounding bus fans,  viewed by the general public as "reckless teenagers" who endanger their lives to capture snapshots of buses.
