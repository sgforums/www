    Welcome to Train forums.

    Well, ever since the public complained on the closure of Buangkok (Especially as there is some flats at around half km away and originally not intended to closed and leave it as an white elephant) and Woodleigh [Not so, as the station is located in cemetry that had since closed and the only benefitters were a small condo near there and people living in Joo Seng Road / Mount Veron police camp mainly the Nepalese ones.] (The "first station" to be closed / not operating along with the line was Tanah Merah, but since the residents there appealed to their MP and hence no choice but to open Tanah Merah and hence the first station not operating along with the line were Buangkok and Woodleigh), the government has announced of no more mothballing of stations, meaning one will not see Buangkok incident again, but instead, what happened will be like Bukit Brown (CC18) / Kampong Bharu (NE2) where a structure / provision was made respectively.

     

    What happened was on DT4, there will be a station as the distance between Beauty World to Hillview is about 2.6km, undoubtly one of the longer stretch between any stations (Comparable ones are Khatib - YCK, Expo - CGA, Outram Park - HBF etc), but since they cant build station and leave it there like Woodleigh as promised, hence they just build a station structure as provision, and to fit the interior once it is ready.

     

    FYI, DT4 is located near Old Fort Factory, the war memorial part as well. So I see no reason of no MRT station in long term plans, but I am afraid if they build a station now, the only benefitters is a few tourists visit that memorial factory and the residents of the condo nearby.

     

    To add on, the LTA had recently posted tenders for TSL, out of it, T4, T4a and T10 will be like this DT4 / Bukit Brown station where they are slated as RTS (Rapid Transit System) structure, meaning to be a future station. (T4 / T4a should be around Mandai / Upper Thomson - Lentor area while T10 is likely to be at Whitely Road)
