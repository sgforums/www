    Efficient SMRT staff flushed out flasher

    Today
    January 14, 2006

    Letter from Wong Yu Yen

    On Wednesday evening, on my way home, an elderly man flashed his private parts to me on the train.

    I was shocked. I thought he might have forgotten to zip up, so I observed him before raising the alarm. I saw him cover himself with two plastic bags when another man sat down two seats away from me.

    After the train had passed Ang Mo Kio Station, the elderly man moved to sit between two women.

    Not long after, the woman on his right suddenly stood up and gave him a disgusted look before alighting from the train. This time, I was certain he was up to no good.

    I alerted the SMRT staff at Yishun Station. Within 15 minutes, I was told they had caught a suspect at Woodlands matching the description I had given. They also reported this incident to the police.

    I was very impressed with the SMRT staff who were efficient and handled the situation so professionally.

    I thank them for keeping me company while I was waiting for the police to arrive so I could give my statement.
