How to make your own XO Sauce

  • Mospeada
    27 Mar 06, 00:02
    Ingredients:
    150 g fresh red chillies
    250 g dried scallops (conpoy)
    150 g garlic, minced
    150 g shallots, fresh, chopped
    100 g tiny dried shrimp - "hae bi"
    50 g Chinese ham or Yunnan Ham or Jinhua Ham, steamed and diced
    50 g salted fish, diced
    25 g large dried shrimp
    1/2tbsp coarsely ground pepper
    25 ml brandy

    Instructions

    1. Seed and wash chillies. Briefly stir fry over low heat until heated through. Remove from heat and drain. Dry in the sun for 2-3 days, until completely dried. Alternatively, u can buy dried chillies to replace

    2. Wash and clean conpoy. Soak in water for 2-3 hours, then steam for 3 hours until soft. Cool. Finely shred the conpoy by hand. Reserve the "conpoy stock" from the steaming, to be used later.

    3. Soak the tiny shrimp in water for 3 hours. Drain and mince finely.

    4. Heat 4 bowls of oil over high heat. Add garlic, shallots and tiny shrimp and fry until the mixture stops Â“steamingÂ”.

    5. When the mixture stops steaming, add chillies, ham and fish and continue to cook until chillies become translucent.

    6. Add conpoy and shelled dried shrimp and Â“pepperÂ” and reduce heat to low. usually, i will slowly fried until the conpoy until they ar kinda "crispy"

    7. Add brandy and adjust seasoning. Continue to cook until only a little steam rises from the mixture. Remove from the heat and allow to cool.

    8. Place in a container and seal. Store in refrigerator.

    Note: This is my own version " a mix and match" from 2-3 recipes and with a few refinement of my own.
