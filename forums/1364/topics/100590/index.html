    The committee members have met last Friday evening and have arrived at some meaningful decision.

    VENUE
    In view of our young fellow forumnites whose spending power is limited, we have decided on the following potential venues:


      1. THE CHEVRONS (Jurong East)
      2. Fullmoon Beach Bar & Grill @ East Coast Costa Sand Resort
      3. Downtown East
      4. Raffles Town Club
      5. Orchid Country Club
      6. Sunset Bay Sentosa
      7. Fishermen Village (Small Bar at the end)


    We will be talking to each of these venues to get the best quote as possible for all our fellow forumnites.

    We welcome all suggestions of other possible venues that we may have overlooked.

    DATE
    We have taken into serious consideration when deciding on the date for the Dinner & Dance, and have arrived at the following dates:


      1. Saturday, End-February 2005, after Chinese New Year
      2. The 1st Saturday when school holidays start in Mid-March 2005
      3. The last Saturday during the school holidays in Mid-March 2005.


    Once again, we will do our best to pinpoint a date that is most convinient for all fellow forumnites to attend this Dinner and Dance. Tis your night after all....we will work hard to ensure you all an enjoyable evening.

    We will be bringing all of you the latest updates on this Dinner & Dance as and when the information is available.

    All comments/remarks are most welcomed.
