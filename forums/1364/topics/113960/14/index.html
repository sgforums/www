    Thank you for the comments. It is very encouraging to see experienced organisers showing the rope to greenhorn like me.....

    I guess the starting point for all outings, in the mind of all organisers, is to provide a social gathering for any and every interested party, be it from the forum or not from the forum.

    When the organising committee 1st had their kick off meeting, we all had visions of our fellow forumnites supporting this event and enjoying themselves during such a event like that of the BBQ.

    We have, no doubt, lack the experience of organising event on such a large scale basis, but we are not afraid to try. Every small step we made, we hope it will evoled into something great for all our fellow forumnites.

    Thus, we set out on a quest to run the D&D for the forum. We made numerous calls, soliciting proposals, hoping to get the best deal for all participants. Sometimes, there were some disagreements among the memebers, but we were able to resolve it ( I hope so ) at the end of the day, cos' we are all working towards the same objective.

    We do not expect everyone from the forums to attend (if can, the best lah... ), but we were hoping to see better response...but things did not turn out the way we foresee...nonetheless, we persisted....we even forked out money from our own pocket to pay for the deposit, hoping that miracle would appear towards that evening....

    I hope this Twilight Summer Dream is not just a Dream of the organising committee...but a Dream to remember for those who attended for as long as they stay with sgForums.....
