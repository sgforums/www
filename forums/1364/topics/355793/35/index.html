    After a long time, I went back to pet society and realised they added front and back gardens to your rooms in the house. You can go and buy flower/trees/vegetable seeds to grow.

    The flowers are not really useful. After its grown, you can pluck and put into pots and either decorate ur own house/sell/give away.

    Trees grow into fruit trees. Pets attain high satisfaction from home grown fruits compared to those bought in stores. At the moment, I have a few orange trees, apple, banana and a cupcake tree. My friend has a coconut tree. Haven't seen other fruits yet ...

    Vegetables.. leave them to grow to large size then uproot them to your chest. Sell the vegetables to earn coins. There's a profit there.. can't remember how much though..

    Rather fun to see if your trees bear fruits or not, so i have been going back to pet society once every couple of days..
