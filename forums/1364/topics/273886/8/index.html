    Anyway, I wanted to zhi xiao my friends, so I made my way slowly in the dark to the adjoining room which is at the other side, through the door connecting both rooms. The other room was also pitch-black, but there was no one inside.

    I went inside and then I sat inside to wait for my friends to find me missing. (Okay, I'm bloody childish, I know.) 5 min later, I swore I felt something creeping up onto me. I dismissed it as my imagination going mad because of the dark.

AND THEN I started hearing footsteps coming in my direction. Call me disoriented and confused, but I'm darned sure the noise was coming in the opposite direction I had come from.

    I freaked out and started walking back, using hands to feel my way and then I yelled for the lights to be switched on. It came on and I got a nice screwing from my friends.
