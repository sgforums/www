    Well, well... I shall speak...

    This really isn't any ghost story, no girl-in-red or vampires. But it was something that I bear witness to. Let me give some background information, I wouldn't want to be mistook as a tomb robber.

    I've developed the odd habit of visiting cemetary at the strangest hour for almost 2 years now. I would go all the way to LCK cemetary to visit my late girlfriend, even at odd hours like 4am in the morning.

    Over the past 2 years, I've visited her uncountable times. I used to be a little fearful of the atmosphere of that place, but it hasn't done me any harm and gradually I got used to it. Now I can walk in total darkness, just feeling at peace with myself. And no, I haven't seen any ghosts or anything of that sort.

    It happened many months ago, I arrived at the Christian cemetary around 2am. I parked my car by the main road as usual, and I would walk some distance and all. The Christian cemetary is rather dark, there're hardly any street lights near compared to Chinese one. But I guess I kinda developed some sense and know the way well after so long.

    I was sitting by my late gf's tomb and just spending some time and all. And I saw some lights from a distance; took me awhile to realize they were actually vehicles' lights. I could tell that there were 2 vans traveling down the small path and going further into the cemetary - actually they were quite near where I was; maybe 100 metres or 120 metres away.

    At first, I thought nothing of it. Although I haven't seen or know of anyone else that would actually visit their loved ones at this hour - it was already close to 2.30am in the morning.

    Then I saw the strangest scene, a few men lifted the coffin out of the van and many people followed behind. I stayed to observe the whole thing and it was no doubt a funeral procession - with burial and all.

    What puzzles me is that it's not usual for Christians to like do this at this hour?
    If anyone has got knowledge in this area, do enlighten me.
