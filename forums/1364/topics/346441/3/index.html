    CLASS A
    Ming Yuan Chun Lor Mee (amoy street food centre, level 2)
    Talking point: best egg gravy, great fried prawn crackers and fish crackers, best vinegar sauce, fish meat peeled from whole steam fish; the low point is the lor bak (not succulent enough)

    CLASS B1 (worth the trip there)
    Soon Heng Food Delights (block 6 tangjong pagar road, #02-20)
    Talking point: 5 different types of fried stuffs, ingredients more than the mee!! fried shark meat definitely better than the tiong bahru's

    Fu Jian Xing Fa (block 91 whampoo drive stall 19)
    Talking point: best lor bak, some old timers prefer to just order the mee with the lor bak and non-fried fish meat; some say this is the second best in singapore!!

    Xin Mei Xiang Lor Mee (block 51 old airport road food centre stall 116)
    Talking point: plenty of fried battered fish, great gravy except poor garlic sauce and chilli padi instead of usual big chilli

    CLASS B2 (worthwhile to go there only if you are a lormee fanatic)
    Bukit Purmei Lor Mee (bk 109 Bukit Purmei Rd, coffee shop near roundabout, stall 1)
    Talking point: good gravy, choice of two types of mee but low point is that they use cheap char siew and fish cake which adulterate the dish

    Original Tiong Bahru Lor Mee (blk 155 bukit batok west av 8 st11 stall 324)
    Talking point: plenty of ingredients, if you like fried stuffs, you like this but the fried stuffs seem abit overfried

    Chen Mei Shi Lor Mee (stall #14 tanglin halt food centre bk 48 commonwealth drive)
    Talking point: very generous ingredients fried wanton (the best wanton), fish nuggets; the dark sauce chicken wing compliment the lor mee taste unlike the shredded duck meat used at the Lorong Ah Soo Lor Mee

    Tong Zhee Lor Mee (stall #01-100 Golden Mile Food Centre)
    Talking point: started with prawn mee but now more famous for its lor mee, great egg gravy, any time better than the tiong bahru, include an interesting fried lotus root, but the wanton is too thick and overfried making it hard

    Ang Mo Kio Loh Mee (#99 Chong Boon Food Centre Blk 153 Ave 10)
    Talking point: great vege fried dumpling, egg, fish cake, meat cake, egg gravy. Some said its the best lor mee in AMK, but reason for the long queue is the fried vege dumplings which is made and fried on the spot, the meat cake is good also, but theres only one, so you got to add yourself

    CLASS C ( eat there only when you happen to be around there)
    Chuan Bee Lor Mee (hong lim food centre #02-55)
    Zhong Xing Fu Zhao Lor Mian (maxwell food centre stall 62)
    Soon Li Lor Mee (chinatown complex food centre stall #02-75)
    Tiong Bahru Lor Mee (tiong bahru food centre, seng poh road stall#02-80)
    Lorong Ah Soo Lor Mee (Hougang 105 Hainanese Village Centre #02-12)
    Liang heng Cooked Food (#02-21 Blk 127 Toa Payoh Lorong 1 Food Centre)
    Chen Hao Lor Mee (#97 861 North Bridge Road food Centre)
    Chye Huat Lor Mee (Blk 31 Kelantan Road Coffeee shop Stall 2)
    178 Lor Mee (tiong bahru food centre, seng poh road stall #02-58 )

    OTHERS (when you really got no choice)
    Toa Payoh Lor Mee (Blk 85C Toa Payoh Lorong 2 #01-368CK Food court)
    Shi Jia Lee Lor Mee (#243 Bedok South Food Centre)
    235 Lor Mee (#02-04 tiong bahru food centre, seng poh road)
    Geylang Bahru Lor Mee Blk 68 Geylang Bahru Food Centre #2737
    Fu Fatt Lor Mee (#36 Hougang 105 Hainanese Village Centre)
    Heng Kee Lor Mee (#87 Bendemeer Food Centre)
    Ming Fa Lor mee (Blk 724 amk centre market stall#30)
    Sha Yuan Vegetarian Lor mee (#75 Bendemeer Food Centre)
    Penang Delights Lor Mee (Blk 711 AMK ave 8 Stall 8A)
