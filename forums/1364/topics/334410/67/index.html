    Oooooh interesting...

    New Loot! Ooze and Spider mice are sometimes dropping pieces of radioactive blue cheese. Keeper mice are dropping Corrupted Radioactive Blue Potion. While all rank levels of Legendary and up have the option of spending 309 gold per piece to use SB+ with this potion to convert it (cost of corruption to fix it), a hunter can also choose to spend 613 gold per piece to convert brie to RB. Legendaries can convert 30/5, Hero 33/6, Knight 36/7 and Lord/Lady 39/8 respectively.

    Scrap Metal as a new crafting item being dropped by Scavenger mice was first reported by Charles Hunt. A description of the item reads: "These jagged shards of scrap metal resemble the result of a steel mouse versus a drillbot." Interesting... the description of the Bottomless Grave trap reads: "Although Digby drilling technology has often been regarded as the quickest to dig a grave, ambitious scientists were still tempted to experiment with rocket-propelled drills. The focus of these experiments has now turned to researching where the high speed drills have gone." Perhaps these scraps of metal are going to lead to new traps? We sure do use drills a lot here in Gnawnia in more than one trap style.

     

    You may have also noticed the change to your King's Rewards where one now has to use logic to answer. An example here: http://s439.photobucket.com/albums/qq112/NibblerNews/NewKR.jpg Please, use your head and actually solve the puzzle and don't enter the entire string of numbers. It's actually easier once you get the hang of it.

    Think we're done yet? Heck no, there is also a new tool for hunter's knapsacks called the "Hunter's Hammer" which is soon to be standard issue to all hunters on the crafting page. When one clicks on the arrow next to the hammer picture, right now you'll be told, "You have not yet received your hammer". This is a SMASHING new development indeed! While it remains to be seen if items can be uncrafted, or what will happen to items once whacked with a hammer... well, we just hope people don't go running around the boards singing "If I Had A Hammer..." or smiting other hunters with it.
