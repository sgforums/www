    these items are needed together with other crafting items for you to craft special cheese like susheese, combat, and glutter.. then u bring these cheeses and go down to meditation room and try to catch the master of cheese claw, master of cheese fang, master of cheese belt.. these masters will individually drop the master shards of their respective study..

    the 3 shards can be crafted together to get the master seal which is an item needed to craft the ambush trap, the master seal are also needed in the recipe to make rumble cheese which is used in the pinnacle room to catch the amster of dojo. the 3 shards can be used separately (i.e no need to be crafted into master seal), to make rumble cheese also..

    recipes on how to make the cheeses and ambush trap can be found on the facebook group The MH Kitchen or http://mousehunt.hitgrab.com/wiki/index.php?title=Main_Page
