    Details: 4 Munchie exchange for one Joan of Farovia Attractant.

    Details: 4 Hair Gels exchange for one Ninja Attractant.

    Details: 3 Grim Attractants exchange for one Joan of Arc Attractant.

    Ninja Attractant (You do not have any Ninja Attractant)

    The ninja star shaped bottle contains an ancient purple juice that no Ninja Fish can resist.
    Details: One Ninja Attractant exchanges for 4 Hair Gels.

    Schitzo Attractant (You do not have any Schitzo Attractant)

    Farovian Scientists are still unsure of what exactly these pills contain.
    Details: One Schitzo Attractant exchanges for 3 Hate Attractants.

    Joan of Farovia Attractant (You do not have any Joan of Farovia Attractant)

    A lovely perfume, for a lovely lady.
    Details: One Joan of Farovia Attractant exchanges for 4 Munchie.

    Joan of Arc Attractant (You do not have any Joan of Arc Attractant)

    This cross shaped bottle helps attract a different type of lady that few men in Farovia have encountered.
    Details: One Joan of Arc Attractant exchanges for 3 Grim Attractants.

    Liquid Gold (You do not have any Liquid Gold)

    The rare Liquid Gold Attractant highly increases your chances of catching one of the 4 rarest fish, which includes Elite Fish. Rare fish are dependent on your current pole and skill.
    Cannot be exchanged. The Ancient Mariner occasionally gives the Liquid Gold Attractant as a gift to wranglers who wield a level 50 pole.

    Liquid Gold has taken the place of 'Elite Attractant' for various reasons, particularly that it's too great of an advantage and would be far too rare for the average player to find.
