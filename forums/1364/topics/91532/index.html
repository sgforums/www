    Apocalypse

    ALIAS: En-Sabah Nur

    DAY JOB: Philospher on Darwin's Theory of Evolution.

    DESCRIPTION: He's big and purple and blue, with some freaked-out stuff going on with his lips. He can control every molecule in his body, allowing him to transform.

    ACCOMPLICES: The Dark Riders/Horsemen of the Apocolypse. Archangel.

    ARCH-NEMESIS: Cable, and possible Angel/Archangel.

    KNOWN HIDEOUTS: If I remember correctly, he liked to hang out in the Pyramids on the X-Men cartoon series.

    PREVIOUS OFFENCES: Numerous murder and public havoc charges. Changing Angel into Archangel, which just wasn't very nice.

    BIO: Apocalypse believes in "survival of the fittest", and seeks to prove that that means him, and nobody else. Apparently, he got his powers by wandering into an alien spaceship. (If I had a dime for every time that happened...)

    QUOTES: "Threats. Consider me duly intimidated."
