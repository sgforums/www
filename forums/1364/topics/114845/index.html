    FAQ for D&D

    How to register?

    Fund Transfer to D&D designated DBS Account (Recommend method)

    Stage 1

    Fund Transfer using POSB/DBS to DBS Saving Account: 015-7-040593
    Keep the payment receipt (This will be your entrance ticket)

    Stage 2

    Send an email to sgforumdd2005@yahoo.com.sg with the following details:

    Nick: As per what you used in sgForums
    Full Name: As in NRIC
    Date: Date of transaction
    Time: Time of transaction
    Your Account number: The POSB/DBS account you used for fund transfer
    Amount: what is the amount you have transferred
    Contact number: Preferably Mobile Number

    Passing the $ to a committee member

    There are some outings which you are likely to meet up with one of our committee members(Mospeada,Kops21,Meowsusan,Kenashi). Do pass the $ and information to him/her.

    A receipt will be given on the spot. Keep the receipt as it will be your entrance ticket.

    What do I need to bring?

    Just be yourself.

    Can I bring friend not from the forums?

    Feel free to do so.

    Can i bring along camera?

    NOPE. No photography is allowed, except authorised official photographer.
