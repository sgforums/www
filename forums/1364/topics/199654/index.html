    Hongkong cafe seems to be the "In" thing in Singapore nowadays...

    Adding to the growing numbers of HK cafe in Singapore is the one at Orchard Cineplex, which I have just tried.

    Located at the second level of Orchard Cineplex, it is not difficult to find, with the striking deco...

    The service was good..perhaps it is newly opened..lets hope it can sustain the service level, but i would appreciate if the staff can recommend me which are the "zhao pei" dishes...

    Nonetheless, I ordered the authentic HK dish - Wanton Mee..and the authentic drink - Ying Yong

    Waiting time...15 mins...

    The drink came before the food. If you have been drinking the ying yong from yat kun or other local stores...u are going to foresake those for this...it is good...but...if it can be a bit warmer, it would be nice...cos towards the end of my drink, i realised the sugar did not melt totally.

    The moment that I have been waiting for...HK wanton mee....

    i was abit disappointed when i 1st saw the wanton mee...cos the size of the wanton is not as big as those in HK...

    the taste...the soup tasted nice....the char siew...the pieces of char siew literally melted in my mouth into little meat floss...yummy...

    despite the size of the wanton...the taste was good...the shrimp taste fresh..and yes...u can actually tell that there is shrimp in each wanton....not just shrimp paste or simply pork in local wanton...

    as for the noodles...it was a bit lumpy...

    overall experience...i would give it a 3.5 out 5....cos there is more i have yet to try....
