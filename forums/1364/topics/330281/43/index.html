    I posted this on Club 30 "Share your recipes" thread a while ago.... you can try it out...

    Beckham Pear and Blue Cheese Salad

    Ingredients:
    2 x big Beckham Pear - preferably very ripe ones (more juicy)
    Mayonaise
    Blue cheese/Cream Blue cheese

    Method:
    Peel off the skin on the beckham pears. Cut them into small cubes and put them in a bowl. Add in some mayonaise (just enough to mix well with the pear) and a small bit (to your liking ) of blue cheese. Mash the blue cheese in the bowl and mix it well with the mayonaise and pear.

    It makes a very good side dish with BBQ/grilled food.
