    My band, The Auditory Effect will be playing on Channel 5's Live And Loaded program which features local bands and music talents.     1. Get tickets and come and watch us. The tickets are free but it is limited as of now. You can let me know if you can interested to come down and I will try to see any more tickets left. You would need to be there around 730PM. (Most tickets given out liao)

    3. Catch us on TV on Channel 5 on 2 April 2009, 11PM onwards.

    We hope you will show your support for everything local, not just us but for the other acts as well!
