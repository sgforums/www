    Other dish is sauteed mushrooms. My receipe is very similar to the Bear's

    Just buy any packet of fresh mixed mushrooms from the supermarket or buy your own mix

    Cut them to size you like
    Cut one whole yellow/green onion into slices

    Heat up butter
    Add onions and cook till semi-soft
    Add mushrooms
    Add some oyster sauce to taste if you like
    Add white wine or beer and simmer till liquid thickens

    Very savoury dish with rice
