    Some places where you can find the courts,

    1. Safra ( Yes they still do have. I know for sure, Toa Payoh and Tampines)

    2. Schools (NUS, NTU, most of the polytechnics and JCs)

    3. Singapore Sports Council facilities
    -Burghley Squash Centre
    -Kallang Squash Centre
    -St Wilfrid Squash Centre
    -Yio Chu Kang Squash Centre

    4. Private apartments. ( Ask around, see if anyone lives in a condominium equipped with squash courts)

    It's a dying sport but it's NOT dead. There are still people actively involved, both playing ( leisurely and compeitively) and managing.

    SSC is working towards reviving Squash by targetting the younger generation. All is not lost yet.
