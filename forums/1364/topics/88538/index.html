    About Out of the Pan (Lifted from RafflesCity Website)

    Located in Basement One of Raffles City, Out of the Pan is the converging point for shoppers and gourmets in search of an innovative dining experience. Patrons are given an extensive menu featuring over twenty types of savoury fillings such as Tiffin Vegetable Curry, B.L.T, Tandoori Prawn Nisha or Makkni Chicken. Fillings are prepared using authentic Asian or Western culinary techniques, resulting in dishes that are true to their original flavours.

    Out of the Pan's signature savoury crepes come in a choice of three batters - corn, whole wheat or sun-dried tomatoes. Patrons have the freedom to design their meal by matching a favourite filling with their choice of batter, complemented by a selection of salads, soups and side orders. Salads are a specialty here at Out of the Pan - one can choose to enjoy salads served in a bowl or as a crepe filling! For desserts, Out of the Pan offers a tempting array of waffles, sweet crepes, ice creams and cakes prepared by an award-winning pastry team.
