    There was this other time when I was driving my car and coming out from a T-junction.

    It was a one-way road, so I looked towards the right for any cars passing by. Aside from a construction truck parked and occupied nearly half the road, there were not much cars passing by.

    I decided to wait for a few more seconds, still no car, and no sound of car coming by. So I made my turn. Just as I was about to move towards one-half of the lane, a car travelling at a velocity of about 70-80 km/h sped through right in front of my face without giving a sh.it of who is turning out.

    He had about 20m to see me after passing through the construction truck, but I guess since it was 11.45 pm at night, speeders usually came out.

    It didn't scrape my car, but it was a near-distant, about a few centimeters off. The driver even dared to flare his middle finger out from his window after he sped by.

    It sure send palpitations and profuse sweating down my panicked body...

    What a night!
