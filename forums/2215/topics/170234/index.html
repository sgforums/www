If you take blood and allow it to stand for a while before making the smear or if the technique is poor, you can can create rouleaux. True rouleaux formation may indicate increased plasma proteins such as in myeloma but it is hardly likely in such a young age. If your ESR is normal and they do not find a paraprotein, threre is nothing to worry about.
