  • MythosLogos

    Perio problems can't be solve by the regular scaling and polishing, you have to do what we call Root Planning under local anaesthesia and this usually require a few visits which the dentist will also advise you to use chlorhexidine mouthwash as well. If perio problems are left untreated over a period of time it will lead to advanced periodontitis which can lead to loosening of the tooth and tooth lost eventually. Do seek treatment early. Root Planning is more costly as Dentist normally charge per quadrant.

    However if you have any extractions done before the teeth can shift as well over a period of time which will result in an unsightly gap.

  • MythosLogos
    12 Nov 09, 22:16

    Root Canal Treatment means removing the pulp/nerve in the tooth (under local anaesthesia). After RCT is done the tooth is considered "dead" and if it's a molar it's better to get it crown to prevent fracture due to heavy biting. RCT is done when the tooth is deem not fit for the regular filling anymore, usually the decay has reached the nerve in the tooth and most patients will experience moderate to severe pain before they decide to visit the Dentist.

    I believe the price includes RCT+Crown. Is it a molar(back tooth)? RCT takes 1-3 visits and around 2 more for crowning.
