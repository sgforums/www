    Rett's Disorder - Rett 1966

    Slow growth rate of brain after 5 months, decline social interest and lost of hand movements. Found only in girls

    William's Syndrome - Willams, Barret-Boyes & Lowe 1961

    Caused by microdeletion on long arm of Chromosome 7
    Distinct facial features, unusually sensitivity to to environmental sounds etc

    Turner Syndrome - Turner 1938

    Absence or structural abnormality of one X chromosone, disrupting normal X pairing in the foetal ovary, leading to premature germ cell loss
