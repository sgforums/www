    NEW MYSTERY

    Humans have two eyes. But, the world appears the same even if you have one eye, and no problem walking around with one eye....

    Qn: What are the uses of having two eyes?
    There are two main reasons for this.

    MYSTERY SLOVED

    Main two reasons:
    1. Have a wider visual field. however this is nothing compared to animals who have eyes on their sides, eg, rabbits, birds, esp. eagles, for prey and predator (survival uses)

    2. For effective motion detection and processing in the brain. The brain process the differences in moving images between the two eyes to judge the speed and direction of the moving object.

    As mentioned, depth (2D or 3D) cues can be detected even with one eye, therefore, they are usually considered monocular cues.
