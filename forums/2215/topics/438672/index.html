    The service standard at Government hospitals and Polyclinics have improved significantly over the past twenty years. I have some pleasant experience and bad experience at Government Hospitals and Polyclinics. Pleasant Experience Most of the staff at the service counter are helpful and friendly. They are courteous and they provide prompt service. The doctors are caring and experienced. They are sincere and they showed respect to their patients. The nurses are also helpful, friendly and experienced. Bad Experience I had a bad experience when I went to T.T.S.H in 2006. I knocked on the door before I entered the consultation room. I greeted the senior consultant and sat at the chair next to him. He gave me a very cold look and asked: "What is your problem?" Recently, I went to a polyclinic. I had a bad experience with a nursing staff. I was directed by a doctor to do a blood test at a clinical room. I knocked on the door and entered the room. I saw two staff chit chatting. I told them I was there for a blood test test. One of the staff replied: "Wait Outside!" These are some of my experience at government Hospitals and Polyclinics. Please share your experience.
