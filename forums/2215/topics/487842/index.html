Applying to Study Medical School in Singapore

    There are 3 medical schools in Singapore. These are:

    1) NUS Yong Loo Ling or

    2) NTU Lee Kong Chian or

    3) Duke-NUS Graduate Medical School.

     

    The first two accepts student after completion of their A Levels. And both also requires a mix of entrance exams and interviews. Admission period is usually around March, after the release of the A Level results.

    For those who wish to study medicine at Duke-NUS, the application period opens from May onwards and closes by December. Duke-NUS requires applicants to submit the MCAT results as part of the admissions process.
