  • renorenal
    11 Dec 05, 15:32
    Originally posted by DeathEdge:
    Hey Doc, this morning i woke up and turned my head.. suddenly i hear a loud crack sound.. den the area behind my neck becomes damn painful.. till now i cannot anyhow turn or else a sharp pain will come back again.. anyway to cure it?
    A loud crack sound suggests the possibility of involvement of bones and joints, and probably a nerve as well. It's best to get an X ray done to check if the pain does not resolve instead becomes more painful.

    Restricted neck movement can also mean that it is muscular in origin, but I doubt the pain is that sharp and involves any cracking sound. If muscles are involved, probably due to poor sleeping positioning, the pain would resolve faster compared to the ones above and would respond to simple painkillers, such as analgesic creams applied on the site of pain.

    However the thing is, the muscular involvement type of pain usually appears on the sides of your neck, where it goes into spasm from probably too long a period in maintaining a single posture or straining it. Your pain that occurs at the back of your neck makes bony involvement more likely. Hence, I would advise you to get it checked up.
