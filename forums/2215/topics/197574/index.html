For families in a dilemma - discharge bedridden from hosp

  • icsm
    27 Jun 06, 17:55
    Hi bros,

    Just found out from this forum that many people do not know of other option to nursing home and maid, for their bedridden elderlies. I came across the below message:

    ************************
    My grandmother was bedridden for 2 years due to massive stroke. We put her up in a nursing home. Her monthly cost for the nursing home was $2,000 to $3000. She passed away since. But during the 2 years, the family spent around $100,000, including hospital visits.

    :happy4:
    ************************

    There is a better and cheaper option. My father had a stroke and we were also in this predicament. AMK Community Hospital was chasing us to transfer my dad out of the hospital and we were hard-pressed to find him a caregiver. We called many nursing homes and yes, they were simply out of our budget ($1800 to $3000).

    Fortunately one of our friends introduce us to an agency to get private nursing (only $900 salary a month). After using the private nurse, I found that this option is probably much better than the nursing home in many ways: (1) Cheaper (2) Need not travel daily to visit my dad (3) 1-on-1 nursing care (4) Now my house has a nurse (in case anything happens to my kids) (5) My dad is very happy as he need not go to a nursing home and he could be with his grand-kids.

    I am very thankful to my friend for his recommendation. Hence, for all with bedridden folks in nursing homes (except those with no place for them in their house), this could be a better option ;-)

    I know there are many who will be in the same predicament as I am. So, I hope to this option could help .... like it did to me
