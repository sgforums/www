    What do you want to know about ASCP accreditation? The ASCP (American Society of Clinical Pathologists) does run accreditation programmes for laboratory technicians. I had some dealings with them way back when I considered sending my technicians for some sort of professional qualifications.

    There are two major issues. First, you must have a recognized University degree in a relevant discipline. Second, you need to work and train in a CAP (College of American Pathologists)-accreditated laboratory, unless you intend to go over to the US for your training. Then you need to sit the various examinations...the syllabus and past years papers are available for purchase.
