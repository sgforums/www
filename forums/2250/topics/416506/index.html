    If you are a die hard internet explorer fan, download the latest IE9 (Beta).It's a significant improvement over it's predecessor, comparable to chrome. It's better than chrome, if you consider hardware acceleration(use of graphics card to help boost performance) offered by IE9

    http://ie.microsoft.com/testdrive/info/downloads/Default.html

    If you have issues after installing this IE9, eg: It keeps crushing after you install it, do let me know. Hardware acceleration is enabled by default, and that's probably most of the time, the reason for the crash. (Drivers have to be updated/not compatible)

    You can disable hardware acceleration by going to your control panel, then Network and internet, followed by internet options, clicking on the advanced tab, and then mark " use software accelerating instead of GPU rendering"
