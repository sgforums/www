    1. Right click on your desktop and select Properties.

    2. Select the Desktop tab and click on Customize Desktop button.

    3. Untick Internet Explorer box in the General tab.

    4. Click OK to apply the settings.

    5. Right click on your desktop and select New > Shortcut.

    6. Copy and paste this in: "C:\Program Files\Internet Explorer\IEXPLORE.EXE" -k

    7. Name this shortcut Internet Explorer.

    This will remove all his toolbars, the close button and other kinds of buttons.

    Steps 1 to 3 are optional. Only do this if the Internet Explorer icon is on the desktop.

    To show him that you are a clever guy, refer to this guide.

    Enjoy!
