    Unless you plan to transfer 48 GB of textual documents (eg. Word), don't bother with the compression utility. The disk space saved from compressing media files such as JPEGs, PNGs, MP3s and AVIs is close to zilch because they are already in a compressed format.

    If you need to transfer lots of stuff, do consider getting a portable hard disk instead.
