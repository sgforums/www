    step by step troubleshoot
    1)open your cpu
    2)disconnect your psu from all devices
    3)Smell the cpu(not funny, this is to check got burn or not, if got surely got burned smell)Don't have? move to the next step
    4)Power up your psu; just plug the cable into direct power source, should be able to on. Cannot on? put aside.
    5)take other psu and connect all the wires on to your motherboard and try to power it up. Cannot on?, your psu is safebut your motherboard isn't, if can on your psu dead liao;need to change
    6)If you have any other extra mobo, try to turn it on using your(working psu). If cannot, mobo dead, need to buy new mobo.

    Hard disk or other problems
    1)Turn on your pc, if it hangs or crash, most likely a virus or some conflict.
    2)If got several beeps means one of your hardware is dead
    3)disconnect all hardware(do not disconnect your psu from your mobo) and plug it in one at a time(plug in one device and see if can turn of without beeps), Don't have beeps, proceed to other devices. If after a particular device you plug in got beeps, take it out, and continue with other devices. The ones which you plug in got beeps means that its faulty.
