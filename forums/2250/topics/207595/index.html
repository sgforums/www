    For Linksys routers, it's possible to do so. There's an option to set to either wireless B or wireless G. Wireless B is the slower counterpart of wireless G. The maximum speed is 11Mbps.

    Theoretically, one would be connecting at 54Mbps. In practice, though, it's not possible, due to other factors like signals interference. His speed drop could be due to that there are more people around his area using wireless and could be using the same channel. He can try using another channel.
