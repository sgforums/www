    A few other things to take note.

    1 - What is the model of the external HDD?

    2 - Indicator light is on, are you using a USB external hdd drive with no external power?

    3 - Depending on the type of HDD, the interface may be different

    4 - Are there any sounds, e.g tapping sound (thud thud) or spinning sound (wheee)?

    These will help to identify to possible problems.
