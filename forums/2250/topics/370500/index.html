    In terms of specs, Ultra Touch is a better one.

    The most important thing though, is how comfortable you feel with the phone and if all, or at the least, majority of the features that you are going to utilize are present in the phone.

    A lack of ease in using the phone will make you frustrated about it, and the lack of features that you use most of the time will make you regret your decision.

    If you find yourself liking Preston more, due to its ease of use and no lack of features that you currently use, I would suggest that you along with it. But if the specs are a main concern, Ultra Touch would be a better choice.
