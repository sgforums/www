    Originally posted by RockeY:
    Erm how do i go about doing that?
    The router comes with a cable. Use that cable to connect your PC to the router.

    Now, open your browser. Type in this address: 192.168.1.1

    This is the default address for MOST routers. If this is not your router's default address, refer to the manual given. From there, log in to troubleshoot.

    If you are not able to solve the problem, press the reset button and redo everything right from the beginning.
