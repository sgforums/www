    Maybe some firmware updates for both of your optical drives may help. Also befre you purchase an optical drive (especially DVD-writers) please read the technical specification and the listed, tested and approved media brands it can read.     http://www.hardwarezone.com.sg/articles/view.php?id=1874&cid=29&pg=8

    Sony DVD-writers are a little picky on the media it reads, while TDk and Verbatim media may go easy with most DVD writers.
