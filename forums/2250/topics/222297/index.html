    iPod will allow MPEG4 Part 10 (aka AVC or H264) and MPEG4-ASP to be played back

    Creative ones will generally allow MPEG4-ASP, DivX and Xvid variants to be played back as well.

    Of course, there are Audio format, Video bitrate, quality and resolution specifications that you will need to conform to for the various players.

    btw downloaded movies are generally in .avi container with Xvid or DivX as the codec.
