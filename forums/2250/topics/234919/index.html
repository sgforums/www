    Meat Pao.
    ISP use firewall, but not for YOU. The firewall purpose is to block websites, that's why certain websites can't be accessed from here.

    For those who use router, yes, it's a hardware firewall. It's protects your home network. However, it doesn't notify you of any funny things that are going on, specifically outgoing connections.

    Spywares and viruses love to make outgoing connections, which can be and is blocked by firewalls if they use known ports. If known ports are not used and you set the firewall to notify you for every small little thing, you can see where the connections are going and block them. That's why it's still necessary to use a software firewall.
