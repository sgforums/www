    The Wi-Fi access key can be discovered easily. It is to keep the "normal" people out only.

    You may edit your router's Wireless > MAC Filter, insert a list of your devices mac addresses and select "Permit computers listed below to access the wireless network". This will block unauthorised access to your router. But when you have visitor(s) wanted to use your internet, your need to add them to the MAC Filter list. The most secure way is always the most troublesome.

    Or there is aslo another option called "Block computers listed below from accessing the wireless network", which your can block intruder(s). Usually, thereis a "Veiw Client List" button, to view all devices' mac addresses connected to your router. Those mac add that are not belong to you, add them to the MAC Filter List. This can keep them out.

    You can choose the black list or white list.
