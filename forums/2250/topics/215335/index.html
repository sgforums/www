    FYI there are 2 basic diferent types of hdd.
    1) external (so called with 'case' but prefered to be called as mod case)
    2) internal (ATA or IDE/ SATA case)

    for external hdd there are 2 basic speed transfer (USB/2.0 or firewire a or b). to add to the various capacity available in GB or gig, there are also sizes of 1.8" or 2.5" for you to choose from. that do not mean that the overall size of the unit is 1.8" or 2.5" it simply refers to the hdd used inside the overall modded case. Inside the external hdd is still an internal/ IDE/ sata disk.

    for internal/ IDE/SATA hdd it is usually used in portable devices like notebook computers and some even in larger mp3 players. interface type can be SATA/ IDE and boosts speed of up to 5400 rpm.
