    The receiver is just a small wireless transmitter/receiver that is connected to your usb port. As long as your don't remove it from that particular port, I don't see how it would be damaged. 

    There are various factors to consider 

    1) the D+ and D- pins (data) in the cable is shorted by default renders it only for charging 

    2) if the keyboard supports using usb - drivers will have to depend on Logitec - a look at logitec's website proves no documentation or refences about using it connected to the USB

    Taking into account these factors and your concerns, why don't you just get a USB keyboard instead?
