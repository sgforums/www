    eDonkey's ass is shut

    RIAA hee haws

    By Nick Farrell: Wednesday 13 September 2006, 06:40

    ONLINE FILESHARING outfit eDonkey has been forced to close and pay $30 million to the recording industry for ever existing.MetaMachine, which owned eDonkey, was one of seven outfits to receive shut down letters by the Recording Industry Association of America. BearShare, i2Hub, WinMX, and Grokster have reached similar agreements. Warez P2P, Limewire and Soulseek have not made any agreement yet.

    A spokesRIAA described the eDonkey closure as another domino falling, although that is usually seen as a bad thing, so we think he might have got a bit mixed up.

    Under the agreement MetaMachine agreed to immediately cease distributing eDonkey, eDonkey 2000, and Overnet. It has also said that it will take steps to stop punters using previously downloaded versions of the eDonkey software.
