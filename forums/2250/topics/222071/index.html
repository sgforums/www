    Different brands, different pricing.

    Different standards, different pricing.

    As simple as this.

    An example to illustrate (cost is made up):

    Linksys router, wireless G: $80
    Linksys router, wireless B: $60

    Wireless G can support speeds up to 54Mbps, while Wireless B can only support up to 11Mbps. That resulted in a price difference.

    Take another brand with the same speed will cost differently, because each company has their own pricing and marketing strategy.
