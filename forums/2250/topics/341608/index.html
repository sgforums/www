    SMS = Short Message Service

    To send SMS, you will need to sign up with a SMS gateway provider, e.g. http://www.clickatell.com/.

    They have an email to sms feature, http://www.clickatell.com/solutions/business/email.php but you will have to figure out how to make your email client or email server auto send out the email to the sms service.

    If Foxbox doesn't turn out to be the right fit, you might want to look at Digium's PBX cards. A Linux mail server equipped with their telephony card + Asterisk Open source can do voip/landline/SMS/email/database etc - fully programmable/customisable/integrated.
