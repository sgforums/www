    Originally posted by lpx88:
    Oh ya..can we mix and match RAMs with different brands and latency?
    Different brands - yes.

    Different latency - possible, depending on mobo.

    Let's say your mobo support 400 and 533MHz of RAM.

    These 2 RAMs, often have different latency.

    When you put them together, it will run at the slowest possible speed (@ 400MHz and the highest latency).
