    P.S maybe wrong section to post lol. 

    1. Specs difference. Each and every laptop model got different specs. Also price difference. Same model in school tends to cost less than when you buy outside.

    Also, everything is pre-configured for school lappies. Outside lappies need to bring to school to configure. While you think you save time queuing in school, you end up waiting for more days to configure a laptop.

    2. Yes, if the school needs certain customized software. For RP, students and teachers have a customized software called UT Client, which is for taking tests. Teachers don't take tests, they use this client to generate a unique key so that students can log in to a server. Also, this client enables them to mark the tests.

    Your school will let you know if such a customized software is needed.

    3. Bring to school to configure, especially if they need to connect to a domain. That one has no choice. Only the domain administrator can add you in to the domain.
