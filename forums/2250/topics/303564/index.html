    Originally posted by Y_Shun:
    Just realised how cool and configurable Opera ish!!!!

    Now I want to remove the Menu Bar...but it won't disappear
    Tools > Preference > Advanced > Shortcuts > Keyboard setup > Choose the one you are using > Edit > Search for "F11"

    You should see two entries, "F11 ctrl" and "Platform Unix, F11 alt". Remove the "Platform Unix, " text and save your settings. You should be able to hide the menu bar by pressing Alt+F11 now. Let me know if you still can't figure it out; I have some screenshots here which I'm kinda lazy to upload now.
