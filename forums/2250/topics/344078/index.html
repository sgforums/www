    Originally posted by Samsudash:

    Nope! I also tried using a new PSU. Nothing could be switched on. Wad could be the problem?

    Well, that would be complicated, because if your second system can't work with the PSU from the first system, obviously that means your first PSU is spoilt. But if your first system can't work with a brand new PSU, then it means that there is another component on your first system that is also spoilt. You'll need swap each component of both systems part by part until you can determine the second spoilt component.
