    There are more than a dozen of beeping sounds to be interpreted.

    1. If you still have your motherboard manual, refer to it and tell us what BIOS it is using. Otherwise, tell us your motherboard brand + model number.

    If you have lost it and can't figure it out, please at least tell us the model number.

    2. Turn on the computer. Listen carefully to the beep sounds. Any specific patterns? Like long, short long long. Also the pitch. Are all high pitch? All low pitch? Or some high pitch, some low pitch?

    3. On the monitor, is there any error messages shown?
