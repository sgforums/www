    Then open up the headlight photo inside of photoshop and make sure you use the color sampler. Take some samples of the different colors that are being used in the photo-light. Make a little list of them.

    Then on your testing circle, play with those colors, making a higher brightness, or more/less saturation, etc.

    That way at least your colors will be in the same family.
