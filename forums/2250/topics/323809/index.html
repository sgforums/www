    This is normal for Starhub Maxonline,due to the technology they use(Hybrid Fibre Co-axial) you do not get a dedicated line as it(a very large one) get shared by one block or area.

    Â 

    For Singnet, they use the older Twisted Pair which goes through the phone line.The advantage is that you enjoy a dedicated line so your bandwidth is unaffected by traffic(not as much as Starhub at least) and hence faster speed.

    The disadvantage is, the stability of twisted pair is not as good as HFC.There is reports of certain Singnet areas where disconnection/disruption issues is encountered commonly or rarely.In contrast,Starhub Maxonline is stable with very very rare disconnection/disruption isses.

    So its up to you if you want stability or speed.My starhub has never encounter a disconnection nor disruption for years until it happened a few days ago,and even so in the middle of the night.
