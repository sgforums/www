    http://www.bravehost.com/

    This is free, bandwith is limited. You can upload your finished HTML pages by following these pages: manage hosting > file manager. If you wish to upload multiple files, just click on upload multiple files.

    Don't use their FTP manager, it's laggy and some files can be lost in the process.

    Note: only files with these extensions can be uploaded.

    .htm, .html, .gif, .jpg, .png, .txt, .swf, .mid, .rmi, .au, .wav, .js, .css, .ico, .bmp, .dcr

    If you create via their templates, your website would be named anyname.bravehost.com

    If you upload your HTML files, your website would be named anyname.bravehost.com/homepage.html or anyname.bravehost.com/homepage.htm

    Substitute homepage.html to the first page you wish your visitors to see.
