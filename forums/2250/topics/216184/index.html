    most PC case weighs so your request is hardly possible. to mostpc builders who are in to games, their concern is never the weight. god knows shit happens but for them they are concerned about compatibility issues and most of all - the performance.

    if u want a lighter PC gaming rig get an alluminium case as compared to steel cases they are lighter.

    my suggestion is if you need to bring your whole PC down to a tech guy to look inot, just dismantle and remove all the cards and hard disks and power-on card ( with push button) in the case and bring it over to the tech. set up there (withoout the case is possible) and let the guy work on the system for you.

    ok serious, some gaming cases come with a carrying handle at the top so its more convenient to be moved around. Some Antec LanBoy cases have strap-ons that allows the case to be slung over like a haversack or bag, or hand-carried like a suitcase. you should check out such cases.

    back to the wieght there is nothing much we can say 'cept that the total weight of the hdds, boards and cards and Psu weighs just so and the better part of the difference lies on the case itself.
