    I don't know if this works, but most likely it will, since XP is one of the most insecure OS around.

    Start > Control Panel > Administrative Tools > Computer Management

    On the left hand side, click on the + sign to expand Local Users and Groups.

    Click on Groups.

    Double click on Administrators on the right hand side.

    Click on Add when a new window pops up.

    Add in your username. Then click on Check Names.

    Click OK all the way, restart PC.

    Go to Start > Control Panel > User Accounts and see if your username is under Administrator group.
