    All softwares used in polys here are licensed, so you need to verify yourself to be part of the school. One way is to join domain. Then you will be able to download and install the softwares (maybe no installation, he has no administrative rights).

    Another reason for using a domain is because of security reasons. Most people, no matter how tech-savvy they are, will always do something that will compromise a company or school's network. By being in a domain, the administrator will know what is going on throughout the whole network and quickly fix them if something happens.
