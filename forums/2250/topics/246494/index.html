Wasn't the console suppose to be packed with an adaptor. i mean its a singapore region 3 machine. And he wanted to charge me $79 for the adaptor.he failed to mention that it did not include an adaptor. I was just furious, i didnt want to talk to him anymore. I packed my ps2 and he was like continually insulting me with his bad manners, he wasnt even interested in serving me anymore. Folks, the ah kow tax and overcharging aside, if it was you being insulted and continually being hurled abuses at, would you take it lying down? Is that the way to be serving your customers? sure i was furious but i kept my cool and tried reasoning with the salesman but he gave me a bad attitude. If Singapore wants to provide quality service then is that the way to be treating its customers? Oh and for your info, I bought the adaptor at another shop for $15. Can you say 'overpriced'?
