    Bloodhound virus is real.

    The SysProtect website is malicious, it is already scanning in background without your permission, resulting in all sorts of rubbish popups, like it haven't finished scanning.

    WinAntiVirus Pro is a fake antivirus. A search on Google says that it is good, in fact, it is not. It is causing lots of problems for users. It is very hard to remove WinAntiVirus Pro as other antivirus also trust it.

    If you suspect that you have gotten the bloodhound virus, read Symantec's writeup on bloodhound.

    The reason why it ran again when your PC starts up is because it has written a value in your registry, telling it to run every time despite being quarantined or deleted by the antivirus. You have to delete that registry value and probably configure some of the startup programs that will run once your PC starts up.
