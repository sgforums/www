    How do I forward my Yahoo! Mail Plus mail to another account?

     

    Forwarding is as easy as it sounds.

    First, click Options in the upper-right corner of your Mail page and select Mail Options.

    Under "Management", click POP & Forwarding.

    In the bottom half of this page, click inside the circle next to "Forwarding". Then enter the email address in the blank and click "Save."

    Zing! – your mail now gets forwarded to that email address.

    Note: You can’t simultaneously Forward and POP your mail.
