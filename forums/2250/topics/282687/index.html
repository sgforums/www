    Of course a GeForce Mx 440 would be better than the Intel integrated graphics card.

    A GeForce Mx440, although having only 64MB or RAM, is dedicated to video rendering. It is built solely for video processing.

    Intel integrated graphics on the other hand, is just a chip in the motherboard. It shares your onboard RAM to render graphics and is generally inferior to a dedicated graphics card in all aspects.
