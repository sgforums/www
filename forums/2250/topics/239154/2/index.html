    First question, what brand of router is she using? Don't be like my cousin, use 3Com and kena screwed up by Singnet.

    If she's using Singnet and 3Com, ask her to login to her router and try these settings:

    VPI/VCI - 0/100
    How the ISP connects - PPPoA
    Username - whatever@singnet (replace whatever with her username)
    Encapsulation - VC MUX
    IP address - assigned by ISP

    Using other routers, ask her reset everything. Either there is a reset button, or if none is present, most likely is hold the power button for a few seconds. Or if possible, reset the router via a browser, meaning logging in to the router.
