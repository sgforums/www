    Can you try putting back the RAM?

    Then note down the BSOD error. Usually, when a BSOD occurs, Windows will restart in a few seconds, barely enough time to note down the errors. To stop the restarting, do this:

    Right click My Computer and select Properties.

    Select the Advanced tab. Under Startup and Recovery, click on the Settings button.

    Under System Failure, untick Automatically restart box.

    Click OK.
