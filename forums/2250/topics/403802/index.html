    Go into the BIOS. I don't know the layout of the Fujitsu BIOS, so I can't quite say which menu to go to. Go to something like "Advanced", or "Advanced chipset features" or something around that line. Look for a field like SATA Mode, or HDD mode or something like that. Ensure it is set to IDE, and not AHCI. AHCI mode requires drivers to be installed during installation.
