    The reference at "http://www.aumha.org/win5/a/xpvm.php" is useful for further information.

    But, I have extracted the main gist.

    Click on "Start Menu"
    Click on "Control Panel"
    Click on "System" (switch to Classic View if it does not appear)
    Click on "Advance" tab
    Under "Performance", click on "Settings" button
    Click on "Advance" tab
    Click on "Change" button

    You will come to a window titled "Virtual Memory"

    Under the "Total Paging File Size for All Drives", check the "Recommended" value. Mine is 334 MB.

    Click on "Custom Size" radio button, and specify the rounded maximum and minimum hundredth value. For me, it is 400 MB and 300 MB respectively. (300 MB > 334 MB > 400 MB)

    Click on Â“SetÂ” button
    Click on "OK" button
    Exit all the windows
    Reboot your computer

    Try using your computer for a while.
    If symptoms perist, increase the maximum and minimum values of the paging file size.

    Cheers.
