    Originally posted by toyota-corolla:
    how you make your clock to 24hours one ar?
    Start, Control Panel, Regional and Language Settings.

    Select the Regional Options tab. Under Standards and Formats, click Customize.

    Another window will open. Select the Time tab. In the Time format, change it to HH:mm:ss.

    Capital letters means the time will be in 24-hour format, small letter means it will be in 12-hour format.
