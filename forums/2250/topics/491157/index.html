ViewSonic introduced 4000 lumen projectors

  • NCgong
    27 Jan 16, 17:38

    ViewSonic just announced new additions to its LightStream™ 7 projector series. The LightStream PJD7325 and PJD7525W 4000 lumen projectors are now shipping and feature enhanced color and sound technologies making them the new standard for corporate or educational environments. 

    PJD7325
    Ideal for settings where a combination of brightness, smart features and functionality are imperative, the ViewSonic LightStream PJD7325 and PJD7525W projectors deliver a perfect balance of color, light and front-of-screen performance. These two projectors are engineered with the company’s SuperColor™ technology which features a newly designed color wheel. Both projectors provide best-in-class color accuracy in an intuitive, sleek, and user-friendly form factor. The 4000 lumens of brightness, in combination with SuperColor technology, ensure projected images are vivid and vibrant. 

    PJD7525W
    The PJD7325 and PJD7525W are built with ViewSonic’s proprietary SonicExpert™ enhanced sound technology. The integrated audio system consists of a built-in 16W speaker that delivers an immersive audio experience. SonicExpert delivers superior audio performance with greater frequency range and minimized distortion — even at the highest volume levels. 

    The PJD7325 features native XGA (1024x768) resolution, and the PJD7525W features native WXGA (1280x800) resolution. These projectors carry on the newly designed form factor common throughout the ViewSonic LightStream series, including smart features like ViewSonic’s PortAll™ – an integrated compartment supporting a MHL/HDMI port. This allows users to wirelessly stream multimedia content via an optional wireless HDMI dongle such as ViewSonic’s WPG-300 dongle. 

    “These two additions to the LightStream 7 series of projectors enhance the family with their brightness level and exceptional audio/video technologies,” said Vincent Liou, PM Director for ViewSonic Asia Pacific.. “With 4000 lumens combined with wireless streaming capabilities and smart functionality, the PJD7325 and PJD7525W deliver notable out-of-the-box color and sound performance for businesses, classrooms and other environments where video content needs to capture the audience’s attention.” 

    The PJD7325 and PJD7525W include a remote network management function that can control over 250 LightStream projectors, and with a single remote, users can operate up to eight projectors in the same room. Advanced setup selections include vertical/horizontal keystone, corner adjustment, and an innovative cable management system that eliminates unsightly cable clutter. The PJD7325 and PJD7525W come with HDMI v1.4 and HDMI/MHL ports, as well as RGB, Composite, Audio In/Out, VGA, RJ45 and RS232 connectors.
