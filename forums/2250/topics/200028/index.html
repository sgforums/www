    13 Jul 06, 22:46
    For Internet Explorer:

    Tools, Internet Options. Click Delete cookies. Click Delete files. Tick the box that says Delete all offline content. Apply settings and restart Internet Explorer. Try accessing Friendster after this. If you still can't, try this below:

    Tools, Internet Options. Select the Privacy tab. Choose anything from accepting all cookies to Medium High. Don't go higher than this, you might have problems accessing it.

    For Firefox:

    Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete to clear everything. Restart Firefox. Try accessing Friendster again. If you can't, try the method below:

    Tools, Options. Select Privacy. Under Cookies tab, tick all the boxes.

    For ZoneAlarm firewall:

    Go to Program Control. In the Access column, left click and change to Allow for your internet browsers. Don't change anything under Server column.
