    first question, yes. Its also recommended that you remove your battery from the laptop after charging finish and continue your work on AC power. It is said to reduce the lifespan of the battery if its fully charged and you left it in the laptop while using it on AC power.

    Second question yes, but I'll recommend that you charge finish your battery at one go instead of leaving it half-charged. It is alright to let it charge overnight or for several hours if your computer is shut down.
