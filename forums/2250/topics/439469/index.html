    Some are bundled with your isp home interenet connection.

    They are often of higher speed.. such as 3.6mpbs - 21mpbs

    You only need to bring your laptop out once it in a blue moon or if you have a tablet but don't use it's 3G option that often.. then this would be the one for you.
