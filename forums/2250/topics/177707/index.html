    i remember i used to have this toolbar called "whenusearch".. this "whenusearch" toolbar didnt even have the remove toolbar option, i painstakingly searched through the task manager

    1. press ctrl alt del, go to processes and then look under the processes tab

    2. try to close the processes that are related to the internet, which would most probably include the toolbar if it belongs to spyware.

    3. search for the filename of the toolbar where u closed in the processes, and then delete the spyware files.

    that was the only way i managed to destroy that irritating toolbar i had.
