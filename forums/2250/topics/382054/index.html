    The price you mentioned sounds okay. Wii Sports is supposed to be freely bundled with the Wii. So it shouldn't be a factor in the cost.

    The default bundle comes with one controller, a additional one might cost around $50. You can get the game Wii Play which comes bundled with a controller (ard $60) if the shop doesn't bundle in one more controller.

    There are typical 3 version of the Wii:

    PAL, NTSC-J and NTSC-U... Singapore official wiis should be NTSC-U -- same as US wiis, except the power adapter is for our 220V. You shouldn't need another adapter if you get the singapore one. There might be an issue with support in Singapore if u get another version.

    You will also need to get games that are compatible with your wii (PAL, NTSC-J or NTSC-U).

    All wii models can be modified, but nowadays heard Nintendo tries to make it harder. But new mod chips are being designed every now and then to counter. haha.

    I am not sure which shop does wii mod, you can ask the shop you patronise. I got mine modded from a wii-modder free-lancer. Cost about $100++,  hehe
