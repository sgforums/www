  • ndmmxiaomayi
    15 Sep 06, 18:10
    If you reply to any thread, you will need to back a few times because of re-directions.

    Press Back one time, you will receive this message about redirecting you back to the thread.

    Press the second time, will go back to the reply.

    Press the third time, and back to thread.

    Also, this doesn't happen when you reply to any thread. Your browser keeps track of your surfing history. If you use the Back button extensively instead of clicking on links, you have to press Back a lot of times.
