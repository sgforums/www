    Invitation to a Focus Group Discussion on The Design for Singapore Government Website

    The Government is embarking on an effort to revamp the Singapore Government Website (www.gov.sg) and will be conducting Focus Group Discussions (FGDs) with members of the public for the purpose of seeking feedback on the new design. Feedback gathered at the discussion will be used to help shape the new "gov.sg".

    If you are living in Singapore and keen to have a say in the future design of "gov.sg", we would like to invite you to attend the FGD, which will be conducted between 1 Nov 2008 and 5 Nov 2008 (details can be found in the Reply Slip). Please complete the Reply Slip and return it by 20 Oct 2008 either by fax :6354 8128 or email reach@reach.gov.sg. 

    For further queries, please email to IDA_PS-Online@ida.gov.sg.
