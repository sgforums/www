    Singtel. However, be aware that most mobile broadband are using 3G for data transfer, and if the 3G antenna ain't present, your speed will just drop to accomodate GSM. One of the biggest blindspots have to be Outram station to Tiong Bahru.

    Alot of HDB flats also do not have it, so you'll be getting ranged signals from the nearest antenna, or not get it at all. The higher you live up, high chances you'll get weak to no 3G signal.

    I live on the 17th floor, and I got no 3G, until I step into the lift. Can be a pain sometimes when your home wifi doesn't reach far enough to the corner of your home.
