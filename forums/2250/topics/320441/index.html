    It would support if it has a PCI-express 2.0 x16 slot, newer boards like urs should have.

    One other thing is you must have a pci express 6pin or 8pin(or both)connector that can be readily connect the power supply to ur graphics card, if you do not have it, make sure that the graphics card comes with a 6pin or 8pin(or both) to molex connector.

    Some graphic card use two 6pin or one 6pin and one 8pin connector, im nt sure whether ur 8800gts uses which, you also need to make sure that ur power supply can draw enough power( not peak power) to supply the graphics card
