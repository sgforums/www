    There's 2 Centrino, namely the single core and duo core.

    The single core Centrino is also known as Pentium M. On 1 to 2 years old laptops, you will still see this kind of specifications:

    Intel Centrino Pentium M 1.X to 2.X GHz OR Intel Pentium M 7XX Processor

    The newer types of Centrino, are all duo core.

    Specifications look like this: Intel Centrino Duo Core 1.XX to 2.XX GHz or Intel Core 2 Duo 1.XX to 2.XX GHz

    Most companies now offer Duo Core laptops, so it no longer says Pentium M. If you see a Pentium M, it is most likely a single core chip.
