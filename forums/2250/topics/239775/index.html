    Internet Explorer takes a while to clear everything. It's worse if your computer is built something more than 3 years ago and has never upgraded.

    One way is to use 3rd party applications.

    CCleaner (http://www.ccleaner.com/) is one of the suggested ones.

    Another one is ATF Cleaner (http://www.atribune.org/content/view/19/2/). ATF Cleaner requires no installation, which makes it easy to use.
