    NEC assemble test notebook - okay shipped to dealers (e.g abc shopr or 'best') with warranty card. on the warranty card will have the NEC computer model name, sku, serial number and date of manufacture. there will be a blank column on the warranty card that is for the dealers' stamp and endorsement that the dealer is selling that unit of computer. When you buy that computer from the dealer, include and keep the receipt as the proof and date for purchase. Include that in the warranty card and send it back to NEC. NEC receive the card from you and file it or update in their customer list. When you produce the warranty to NEC for repairs they check the serial number on card and over your computer against date of purchase, they authenticate before proceeding with the diagnolsis and repairs.
