    I suggest, u bring along ur motherboard or the measurements during ur casing hunting. Because SFF till now doesn't really have a fixed form factor, some use Micro-ATX, some use ITX, some use their own form factor.

    The problem with this is, ur motherboard may seems to be able to fix in but u might find later on that the mounting holes within the casing might not match, that is why I suggest u bring along ur motherboard.

    Please also note, some equipment might not able to fix in, such as ur CPU heatsink and PSU. U may need to purchase one that is able to fix in. (sometimes even ur video card, those with big heatsinks).
