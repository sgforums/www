K-Ninja, another browser for Windows

  • ndmmxiaomayi
    6 Aug 07, 00:18
    After K-Meleon, there's K-Ninja. It works very much like K-Meleon, except that everything now is iconized. No more menus... argh... a bit lost.

    The good thing is, you can right click on those cute icons and get a menu.

    Some minor changes which you may not understand.

    New Layer - essentially, it's just a new tab. Shortcut is still the same, Ctrl + T (if you have been using other browsers other than and don't know this shortcut, please take a cold hard look at the help files )

    Close Layer - close a tab. For those who love to Ctrl + Z, please don't do it in K-Ninja. It will close your current tab.

    Save Layers as Group - well, it's just another way to describe bookmarks. However, it saves all the tabs opened to a group of bookmarks.

    Nothing as fancy as Firefox, those who love simplicity will love it.

    Download link: Join the Google group to get it. http://groups.google.com/group/kninja

    Lazy people can get it here. Get it here

    Just extract it to a folder of its own and run it. No installation required.
