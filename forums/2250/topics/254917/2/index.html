    You better read carefully, or your thread is going to be closed.

    1. Plug in the printer, and ignore whether or not the wizard appears.

    2. Turn on the printer.

    3. Double click and run the file you've downloaded earlier.

    1. Turn off the PC, unplug the printer.

    2. Wait for 5 minutes, then re-plugged it into another USB port.

    3. Turn on the PC.

    4. Wait for the hardware wizard to appear.

    5. Follow the instructions on the hardware wizard.

    Method 3

    1. Start > Control Panel > Add/Remove Hardware

    2. Click Next to search for newly added hardware.

    3. Let it search. Once done, look through the devices and locate your printer.

    5. Click OK to install it. Reboot when prompted.
