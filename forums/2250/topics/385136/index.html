    Well, when you uninstall a program, sometimes the entries are still kept in your registry

    However just one or 2 strings of binary codes of the registry are not enough to bring the program back to work

    What you can do is to go to
    START>RUN>type in "regedit" without the " ",

    and

    expand(by clicking on the small arrow at the side of the word)

    1. HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE ,

    then expand

    2. SOFTWARE

    In that section you can see many names of programs which are installed on your computer, maybe the program you just uninstalled is still left in the registry, and the name of the program will be reflected there, thus u can re download the program and unlock your files

     

    Remember to 'unexpand' after viewing

    NOTE: WRONG EDITING OF THE REGISTRY WIILL CAUSE PROGRAMS TO CRASH
