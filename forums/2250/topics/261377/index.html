    I am using a DDR3 7900GS. Don't get 8600GT although it supports DX10.

    My memory bandwith for 7900 is 42.2 while 8600GT is 22.4, meaning my memory data transfer of video memory to CPU is greater than the 8600GT.

    My texture fill rate is 9.billion pixels per sec while 8600 is 8.6. Even though the difference is small, the bits and pieces add up to form a huge difference.

    If you really want to get a DX10 card, get the 8800GTS or wait till end of the year, not much games to support the technology now.
