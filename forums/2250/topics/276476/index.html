    Wireless have a speed range of up to 11Mbps for 802.11B products, and 54Mbps for 802.11A/G products.

    This is the speed within your own network, when you are transferring files. On the internet, the max is 1Mbps.

    Frequent disconnections can be caused by interferences, connecting to the wrong wireless network, or something is blocking it.
