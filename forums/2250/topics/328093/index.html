  • unfilterd
    22 Aug 08, 09:45

    I had a feeling you might be using singtel the moment i read the first post. As LatecomerX asked, is the problem coming from the wireless router or the actual Singtel connection itself?

    If other computers on your wireless network get disconnected frequently as well, there's 2 things you need to check.

    Are all the phones sharing the same line connected to microfilters (the little phone line sockets that came along with your modem package)? If I'm not wrong, if someone else in the house uses the phone while you're on the internet, you could get problems with your connection. If need be, you might wanna go down to a computer shop to get yourself a few of them.

    When you use the phone normally, is the line noisy? If this is true, good luck! It's something I used to hear alot from my friends who use Singtel. They'd be happy to be able to remain connected for even 5 minutes at a stretch. If this is the case, you can try calling Singtel to inform them of the situation. And they will TRY to solve the line problem for you. 'Try' being the keyword here, cos it's not an easily resolved situation. The problem could lie anywhere from one of Singtel's base stations to even the phone wiring in your house. But I believe one of my friends managed to resolve the situation by connecting the modem to a phone jack that was located nearer to the main door (because the wiring to his room was slightly faulty).

    Hope this helps you!
