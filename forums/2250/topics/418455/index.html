    20 Dec 10, 23:42

    Starhub modems require it to be connected to a Starhub (SCV) television port - those white boxes (for older hdb flats) or the tv port (enclosed in the wall)

    If you want to broadcast your network over to your whole house you have to buy a wireless router (requires your laptop/desktop to have a wireless card)

    or a powerline eternet adapter (basically, you connect this to your power socket, connect the internet cable from the modem to it and then at the other end of your house let's say socket B, have another power line eternet adapter connecting to your desktop
