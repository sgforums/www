    U must see what type of HDD it is, nowadays people are using SATA2. U need to see what is connected to the back of the HDD

    1. There would be a cable, 4 pins for the power, which is called the molex

    2. There would be another cable, if it is wide in width and flat, probably ur HDD is a IDE

    3. If it is just a normal thin cable, ur should be a SATA HDD, it is written on the Hardisk itself

    4. if u plug out the hdd, make sure when u plug into the other comp, u must plug the wire into the hdd and the motherboard(very hard to find the plug), not recommanede if u are not sure where it is located

    The harddisk is located at:

    1. slide open ur case, and look inside.

    2. On the top right hand corner, u should see ur CD/DVD drive, now, look down to the bottom left hand corner of ur case, u should see a rectangular shape object that is shiny and covered in metal, look for a pic on the net for HDD. U cannot miss it

    The RAM is located on the motherboard. The motherboard is the big big circuit board which takes up almost the other half of the casing.
