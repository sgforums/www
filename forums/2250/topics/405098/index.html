    Ahh,  since you do not have to bring the drive in and out of your house, get the 3.5" Hard disks instead.

    They are cheaper for the capacity. A 320gb 2.5" can cost the same as a 500TB 3.5" . 
    Now, what's the differece between a 2.5 and 3.5 inch. 

     

    One difference is that the 2.5 inch hard disk requires no external power source (just power from the USB slots) , while the 3.5 inch hard disk requires a power socket. 

    Because of the portability factor, the 2.5 inch hard disks and the point that i have stated above, it's generally more expensive / gb. 

    So if you are out shopping. Get a 3.5 Inch hard disk instead.
