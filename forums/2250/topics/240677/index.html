    Originally posted by alwaysdisturbed:
    i didn't know mouse lifespan is short. what the estimate span and reasons?
    If you are a heavy user (using it for more than 10 hours a day), then the estimate lifespan is 2 years or perhaps shorter.

    If you spend less than 2 hours with your computer, the estimated lifespan is high. I've got a mouse which lasted me 5 years when I was too busy with studies to use the computer.

    In most of these cases, a USB mouse spoils faster than PS/2 mouse, I have no idea why. It could be the speed, as a USB technology is faster than PS/2 technology.

    Wear and tear is one reason why a mouse can't last long. Those who mistreats their mouse (like banging it when it's not working), will not get it to last long either.
