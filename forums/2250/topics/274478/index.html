    1. That company name is ODEX.

    2. ODEX is in the process of getting court orders to get PacNet to submit all relevant data. No ISPs will be spared.

    3. You can ask your friend; however, if you are caught, that's none of our business and his business.

    4. As long as those short clips are not legal, you can be sure ODEX will be right after you.

    5. Don't ask us, ask ODEX.

    6. Anime is animated cartoons from Japan.

    7. How it involves ODEX, because they are selling anime.

    8. If you never download, you are safe. However, if you use P2P to download other stuffs such as copyrighted music, don't ever think you will be spared. Data gleaned by ODEX can be used by RIAS to sue you as well.

    And the last thing, please check Club Otaku sub forum for more details. There are 23 pages worth of data to read, all are very relevant.
