    -----------------
    1. intel pentium e4300(low end core 2 duo) + MSI G965M-F (this one meet just nice your specification built)

    1. the size of LCD screen.
    2. the casing....

    intel Core 2 Duo E4300 (1.86GHz/1066/2M/Box).. ~$275
    intel Core 2 Duo E6300 (1.86GHz/1066/4M/Box).. ~$303

    intel Core 2 Duo E6600 (2.4GHz/1066/4M/Box).... ~$525
    intel Core 2 Duo E6700 (2.66GHz/1066/4M/Box).... ~$888

    since u are doing graphical work, i suggest u take the lower end processor... at least that when it depreciate its value, the price wont be so much a difference. and also, the performace between the higher end and the lower end processor has no much difference since u are doing graphical work.
