    Actually it can be a yes, your monitor might affect performance indirectly. The bigger your monitor, the bigger your resolution have to be to get the optimum clarity and the bigger your resolution, the more stress on the GPU.

    Ussually a 17" LCD needs a minimum of 1024x768 resolution for optimum clarity.

    If you have 22" LCD and run at 1024x768, the clarity will be poor.

    All this are subjective though. If you don't mind clarity, you can play at lower resolution to trade of clarity with speed.
