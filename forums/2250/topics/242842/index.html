    16 Mar 07, 22:00
    Take mine as example:

    [boot loader]
    timeout=3
    default=multi(0)disk(0)rdisk(0)partition(1)\Windows
    [operating systems]
    multi(0)disk(0)rdisk(0)partition(1)\Windows="Microsoft Windows XP Professional" /nonexecute=optin /fastdetect
    C:\CMDCONS\BOOTSECT.DAT="Microsoft Windows Recovery Console" /cmdcons

    The bolded parts - show that Recovery Console is installed. To remove, just delete this line.

    In your case, it should be replaced with Tuneup Utilities.

    Also delete the Tuneup folder.
