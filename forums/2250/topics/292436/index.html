    Get an antivirus, firewall and HOSTS file.

    HOSTS file re-directs all the bad sites to your computer. Before saying I harm you, it's actually not. Your computer is not a server, thus by re-directing all bad websites to your computer, it's equal to accessing your computer. The bad websites files don't exist on your computer, neither is your computer IP address usable on the Internet, so it's pretty safe.

    Antivirus and firewall... basic protection. AV for the viruses out there, firewall to block malicious incoming and outgoing threats.

    And common sense. None of the protection programs will help if you are going to do dangerous stuffs.
