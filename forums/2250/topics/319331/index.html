    1. press ctrl- a ( cmd-a for mac) <--- at this step, its the same as select>all


    2. press alt-ctrl-d( apple-cmd-d for mac)<-----at this step, its the same as select>modify>feather.

    3. press ctrl-shift-i ( cmd-shift-i for mac)<--- at this step is same as select>inverse


    4. input the feather radius u want. eg 10.


    5. press delete and u are done
