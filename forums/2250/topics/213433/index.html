    What is DrSmartLoad?

    DrSmartLoad is a malicious executable program that is usually installed without user consent or knowledge. DrSmartLoad may have the ability to secretly monitor, record, and transmit computer activity. Possible symptoms of DrSmartLoad include, but are not limited to, sluggish system performance, frequent system crashes, advertisements (Pop-up Ads) and/or high CPU usage among other characteristics. DrSmartLoad may cause a slow internet connection as well due to the increased usage of bandwidth. Replication is not common with DrSmartLoad. It is however highly recommended that you remove DrSmartLoad.
