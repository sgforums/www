    Funan DigitaLife Mall, the upmarket competitor to Sim Lim Square, is set to close later half of this year to make way for a new creative hub redeveloped by the same owner CapitaLand Mall.

    Challenger is one of the major tenants in the mall, taking up the entire space on level 6. In line with the redevelopment plans, Challenger will be closing down their store in Funan and is currently holding an All Must Go clearance sale from now till end June 2016.

    Expect lots of discounts as much as 80% on selected products valid only at Challenger Funan. While stocks last and no exchanges/returns allow on clearance items.

    Apart from the sale, Challenger is making bigger plans online, closing down their online shopping function on challenger.sg and teasing soon to be launched tech marketplace Hachi.tech. The new website is expected to launch on 8th April at 10am.
