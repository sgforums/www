  • MyPillowTalks
    16 Mar 10, 21:01

    1. You can connect the fan to the molex of your powersupply

    2. You can take a fan which has three pin, and find out which pin is +ve, which pin is -ve
         After which, you can connect the positive pin to the red wire on your molex, and the -ve
         pin to the black colour wire of your molex. You can find a converter or manually connect
         yourself

    It is the best way i can think of.

    Becareful NEVER to let the +ve and -ve terminal touch each other when not connecting

  • MyPillowTalks
    16 Mar 10, 22:38

    The fan connector on the motherboard is 3 pin, wheras your fan is 4pin molex

    What im talking about is making this converter

    One side goes to your fan, one side goes to the motherboard

    Go to simlim and ask for a 3pin fan to molex converter

     

    The one that they sell at simlim will look like this:

    http://www.aria.co.uk/Products/Peripherals/Cabling/Power/3+PIN+FAN+TO+4+PIN+MOLEX+CONVERTER+?productId=6600

    Notice the head may be different, so make sure u get the right connection type
