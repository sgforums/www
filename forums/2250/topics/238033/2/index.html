    There's only one administrator group for every computer.

    However, there is another group called power users, whereby they could act like administrators, but with limited functionality. For example, they can install and uninstall programs which normal users couldn't. While administrators can run registry hacks, power users can't.

    To gain administrator access to school computers, figure a way to add your account to the administrator group.
