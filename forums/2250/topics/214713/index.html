    Either it has not been sent, or it's being hidden somewhere on the computer. Show hidden files and folders on the PC.

    Go to My Computer > Tools > Folder Options.

    Select the View tab.

    Under Hidden files and folders, select this option: Show hidden files and folders.

    Apply the settings and close My Computer. Open My Documents and see what hidden files have been modified today.

    All folders inside My Documents must check as well.
