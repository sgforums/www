    OK.

    A quick overview of the new stuffs.

    1. You are able to sign in to MSN on multiple computers.

    Notice the 2 places at the top? That's the new multiple sign-ins. Present since private beta.

    2. See the Favourites? Again, new. Favourites, like its name, is all favourite stuffs at one place.

    3. See the top. Whereas previously the most one could change is the colour, you could now use background. Microsoft calls it Scene. Mouse over to the top right hand corner of the background, you will see a brush. Click on that to change the background.

    4. Animated avatar. There are a couple of animated avatars in the options which you can select. I'm using the shooting stars. Screenshot can't show it. Hehe

    5. What's New at the bottom. Basically tells you what your contacts have been doing. Post a note seems quite useless for now. Can't get it working.

    6. PhotoIM. No idea what is that. MSN help is useless, but I suspect it's related to sharing photos.

    7. Signature Sound. As it is named. Something that will identify you.

    8. Groups and categories. It's like folders and files.

    Groups = folders in Windows

    Categories = sub folders

    Contacts = files

    For neat freaks, me thinks.

    Example:

    Group - Friend

    Category 1 - Primary

    Category 2 - Secondary

    Category 3 - Poly

    Category 4 - Uni

    Things that have changed.

    1. Viewing of contacts. What's wrong with viewing contacts by their email address?

    2. Number of status. Reduced by quite a bit. Following Yahoo Messenger, lesser is better. But unlike Yahoo Messenger, it lacks the ability to add more statuses.

    3. Messenger icon near the clock. One word - UGLY.

    Screenshot from Vista. See the difference between XP and Vista. Vista picture is clearer, font is clearer.
