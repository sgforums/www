    Can you connect to your own router's AP first?

    Connect to your AP.

    After that, log in to your router to configure everything.

    Usually the log in address is http://192.168.1.1

    If you can't get in to that address, open Command Prompt (Start > Run and type in cmd, then press Enter)

    In Command Prompt, type in ipconfig /all and press Enter.

    You will see a Gateway Address. That's the webbie of your router.

    When prompted, enter the username and password to log in.

    If you still have no idea, please post the router brand + model number up.
