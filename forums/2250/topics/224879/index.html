    If it's in your registry, it will infect your thumbdrive and pass on to other users.

    Delete the key in your registry, and remove the ravmone.exe file.

    And please format the thumbdrive.

    Before formatting, off auto play in Windows (without your thumbdrive plugged in). This will prevent the virus from running the moment your thumbdrive is plugged in.

    http://tinybox.bravehost.com/OffAutoplay.html

    After this, format your hard drive.
