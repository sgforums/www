    You are not connected to the Internet. Log in to the router, navigate around the settings. See if there's any button for you to click to connect to the Internet.

    Also, change the settings to keep your internet connections alive even if you are not using it or when it's turned off. Most routers set this to time out after 10 minutes of no usage or upon turning off the router.

    Also, routers take some time to start up and connect to your ISP. Typically, this takes about 5 minutes. If your computer finished starting up in less than 5 minutes, wait longer before trying again.
