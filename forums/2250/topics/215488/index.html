    Starting from software.

    It could be you have viruses or spywares. Please update your antivirus and anti-spywares programs and scan. Remove anything found.

    Update your Windows. MS releases patch regularly, which fixes bugs here and there. This might fix your hanging problems.

    Next, did you install or uninstall any programs lately? If yes, they could be the cause of hanging. If you installed something, try uninstalling it. If you have uninstalled something, try System Restore if you are using XP.

    How much free space is left in your hard disk? If it is less than 5%, please remove any unnecessary stuffs, like pictures, videos, songs, etc. If you want them, burn them to a DVD or CD or keep them in an external hard disk.

    Now, digging deeper into your hardware.

    Does your system clock shows the correct time each time the computer starts up?

    How old is your hard disk?
