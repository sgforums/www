    1. Start > Control Panel and double click on System (or right click on My Computer and select Properties)

    2. Select the Hardware tab.

    3. Click on Device Manager button.

    4. Find your Bluetooth device. Right click on it and select Disable.

    Next...

    Click on Start > Run and enter in services.msc

    Find any services related to Bluetooth and disable them.
