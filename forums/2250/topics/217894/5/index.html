    basically it is asking your broadband modem/router - the firewall inside to ignore the listening port that was used by the bit comet.  That mean you have to copy the listen port number from bit comet and go to your modem/router setup to ask the firewall to ignore that port.

    Do the below.

    -Do not be selfish - open up your download and upload .... do not put restriction ....
    - next is to go to bitcomet refer to 2) - look for your own listening port number . Copy down that port number . Then follow 3) instruction to go to your broadband modem/ router - there you are going to tell the firewall session to ignore that listening port that is used by bit comet  by asking  it to ignore range xxxx to xxxxx  (xxxxxx is the listening port you copy from bitcomet just now ).

     If you done it correctly ... your should instantly notice a significant to huge change in your kb/s rate.
