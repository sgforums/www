  • ndmmxiaomayi
    4 Jun 07, 13:15
    Originally posted by coldzero:
    First time bittorrent user here. :/

    I've noticed that the download speed of torrent is not as fast as my usual download speed from direct downloads. Also, the download speed fluatates, sometimes quite high then drops to quite low. Any reasons as to why for my two questions?

    Thanks
    Torrent download speeds depends on two factors

    1. The number of seeds

    2. The number of leeches

    The higher the number of seeds, the faster your download. The reverse is true if the number of leeches is high.

    At different times of the day, there are different number of people seeding or leeching, which resulted in a fluctuate.

    As for why it's slow... it's caused by the seeding and leeching as well. Because it's a peer-to-peer technology, everybody is a server as well as a user. You as a server is limited by your ISP.

    For direct downloads, it's faster because more bandwidth are generally allocated to them, plus it's their purpose to serve, not do other things, which speeds up things a lot.
