    How do I access Gmail from a mobile phone?

    You can log in to your Gmail account using most mobile phones. To do so, visit http://m.gmail.com in your phone's web browser. Most phones let you open a web browser from the main menu.

    Each phone and mobile provider has a different way of letting you view websites like Gmail Mobile. Here are some general instructions that will help you access your Gmail account from your mobile phone:

    1. Connect to the web. If you aren't sure how, scroll through your phone's menu, and look for the option to access the web and launch your phone's web browser.
    2. Select the option to enter or access a URL.
    3. Enter http://m.gmail.com , and select OK (sometimes called Go To or Send).

    If you aren't sure that Gmail Mobile will work on your phone, or you'd like to learn about the minimum phone requirements needed to use Gmail Mobile, please read Which phones work with Gmail for mobile?
