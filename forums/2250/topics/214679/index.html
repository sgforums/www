    Can I suggest that you just do it the simpler way?

    Go to Start > Control Panel > System or right click My Computer and select Properties.

    Select the Hardware tab.

    Click on Device Manager.

    Locate Display adapters.

    Click on the "+" sign.

    Your graphics card will show.

    Right click the graphics card and select Update driver.

    Follow the instructions on screen.
