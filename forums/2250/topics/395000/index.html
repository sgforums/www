    Symantec Endpoint is usually meant for enterprise. Ask her administrator for help.

    Even if you somehow manage to find the "disable" button, it will likely not work if the administrator configures it correctly.

    The other way is to uninstall it... but if it belongs to an enterprise, it may be password protected to prevent it from being uninstalled.
