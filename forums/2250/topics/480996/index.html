Android Tips - Display SG holidays on Samsung calendar

  • kira.sg
    10 Mar 14, 20:53

    You need to have a Google account.

    Step 1:
    On your PC, login to your Google Calendar, at "Other Calendar", select "Holidays in Singapore"

    Step 2:
    On your phone, click on Samsung "S Planner", tap on the menu button (bottom left), click on "Calendars", under your email, Tick "Holidays in Singapore".

    Step 3:
    Switch on the phone's wifi/data connection.
    Sync with your Google Calendar.
    Go to Phone's settings -> General -> Accounts -> Google -> your email -> Sync Calendar

     

    After successful sync, your Samsung's calendar will show the holidays.
