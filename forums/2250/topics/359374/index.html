    but one thing i had experienced, is that one of my compatible ink is also susceptible to deterioration due to time, as I print very little things at home, and thus i had been continuing to use my compatible ink cartridge from 2006 until recently, and the cartridge leaked ink.  That means, after almost 2.5 yrs, cartridge will become lousy, so therefore, if you use compatible ink cartridge, i suggest use finish all the ink within the expiry date.
