    Linksys and Skype to Launch Dual Mode Internet Telephony Kit

    SINGAPORE, September 1, 2006 -- Linksys announced the launch of a new dual-mode wireless handset that takes free Skype Internet phone calling off the computer and puts it into the hands of callers.

    Based on the CIT200 cordless phone which was released in October last year, the new Internet Telephony Kit (CIT300) features a dual mode capability that allows users to switch between making calls over the Internet or over their existing traditional telephone line. Users now have the convenient alternative of a cordless handset and the freedom to make free Skype calls whether they are at home or in the office.
