    new to this but i know it uses php and needs a mySQL database. a local hosting company says needs LINUX platform only but my current US host is using Windows platform

    Actually, PHP and mySQL doesn't need Linux specifically, but a Linux platform is preferable. The reason why Linux is preferred is because a lot of guides out there assumes you use Linux.

    yes. for credit card payments i need HTTPS?

    Yes.

    no specific needs, just something reliable. and need to send attachments of maybe 10mb

    That's rather huge. I haven't heard of many email hosts that allows that. You can try Yahoo or Hotmail (paid version). They allow you to send attachments greater than 10MB.

    could not stay logged on the Horde email for more than 15 seconds.

    It's a problem with Singapore's internet. Nothing wrong with the host. I'm not too sure what causes that, but I suspect it could be the compulsory proxy that Singapore uses and the dynamic IP addresses that caused the problem.

    Horde email always give problems to Singapore users unless you have a static IP address. That you have to request from your ISP.

    probably global customers..is local webhost better in this case?

    Does your webhost allow you to see the server's logs? If so, you can tell where your customers are from, based on their IP addresses. If most are local, you can consider a local webhost.

    For a non-local webhost, you can consider Bluehost - http://www.bluehost.com/
