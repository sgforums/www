    Did you change out your power supply?

    When you re-installed the OS, did you also clean out the hardware?

    E.g. removing the CPU heatsink to clean out dirt.

     

    The issues are likely to be hardware-related, but here are a couple of possibilities that point to a hardware issue.

     

    First and foremost, the power supply could be a piece of utter crap that can't withhold the demands of the CPU.

     

    Second, the CPU may be overheating. Playback of 720p video generates reasonable heat in a system where the CPU is doing all the decoding.

    When you removed the heatsink (if you did), did you ensure to clean off the residue of the old thermal paste, and re-apply a new layer?

    If you did not, then that may be the issue here.
