    Originally posted by laurence82:
    why are they called security and not IT dept or something?

    Collectively, it's known as the IT department. But within them, there are even more sections, dealing with the different areas of IT.

    Some are with the security, ensuring that nobody except the staff can read and update or change records.

    There's also the database guys, to make sure that they can recover everything in time if their database server crashes.

    The programming guys, to help develop a program for the staff to use.

    The technicians, helping out with general tech stuffs, be it customer service or their own department.

    Some big companies are known to split them up (like MS), while others just call it the IT department.
