    Can you make sure that all applications are closed during the transferring and deleting of files. Windows doesn't like any related applications to remain open while transferring or deleting files.

    Say you are transferring music files, make sure that all music players are closed. Sidetracking a bit, Internet Explorer or any browser you are using will need to be closed as well. These browsers usually have some kind of plug-ins related to music and Windows will refuse to transfer or delete when they are open.

    Another question. Do you have administrator rights on your PC?

    Go to Start > Control Panel > User Accounts

    You should the word Computer Administrator under your account. If you can't see, means you can't delete files, nothing to do with write protection. Files transfer shouldn't be limited, unless your computer has certain restrictions set.
