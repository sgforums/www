    Look at the LED lights. See if anything of the LED lights are lighted up. Make sure you see carefully. Some LED lights are for the hard disk and will be blinking.

    Most likely it's Num Lock or Pad Lock that is lighted up. Just press the Fn key + the Num Lock or Pad Lock to turn it off.

    The Num Lock or Pad Lock key is printed on the keyboard itself, lettering colour is same as the Fn Key.
