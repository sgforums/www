    Originally posted by ChairmanJay:
    how to upgrade driver?
    Start > Windows Update or http://udpate.microsoft.com/

    Select the Custom button.

    If there are drivers available, download and install.

    If not, right click My Computer, select Properties.

    Go to the Hardware tab. Click Device Manager.

    Go to your DVD/CD-ROM drive. Click on the + sign.

    Right click on your drive. Select Update Driver.
