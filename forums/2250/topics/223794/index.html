    Sorry, ASUS M/B i'm not too sure.... using Gigabyte now.... If your ASUS M/B is the Intel 775 type, there is only 1 IDE port. Yor CD/DvD rom will have no place to put. I use SATA 2 HDD while my DvD writer + DvD rom use the IDE.

    Yes, if your M/B support SLI, then you can use 2 graphic card..... but expensive lah...... the cards you listed can't support DirectX 10, therefore even if your SLI is good, can't really use for Window Vista games. Suggest like me, just get a mid range or slightly better card to use for next 9-12 mths, then upgrade to a DirectX 10 card and use window Vista..... 9-12 mths should be enough for Microsoft to solve most of the bugs on Vista.

    7600GS & 7600GT

    GS stands for the lowest range of the 7600 serise cards
    GT stands for higher end ones....

    Example - lowest to highest

    7600GS - 7600 - 7600GT - 7600GTX

    As far as i know, GTX is the best.......
