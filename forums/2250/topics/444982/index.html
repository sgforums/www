    There are many factors which contribute to the success or failure of an ISP. Among them; Quality of Service, Download & Upload Speeds, Quota, Customer Service, Availability, Cost, Technology, Customer Satisfaction, Quality of Hardware and many more. What you said it absolutely right. If the service is good, the customer will be satisfied, or at least, not complain. If the ratio of complaints to non- complaints is low, the ISP is good. In your figures, the ratio in M1 is 0.05. SingTel's is 0.07. So I guess this makes M1 the superior ISP. Of course, these are not official figures lah... God knows what the real numbers are. For the most part, a good ISP can be judged using a scientific and mathematical approach. If too many things fail, then the product ultimately fails as well. A product can only fail or succeed if the overall produce exceeds or falls short of expactation. No one factor can justify the success/ failure of the product. At the end of the day, most people just judge based on personal experience. Conclusion; we will never get solid answers. That's why we need as much feedback as possible.
