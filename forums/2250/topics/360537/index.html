    Actually, the  RAMPAGE II GENE mobo from, EVGA or ASUS? Its actually the first X58 Micro-ATX mobo out.

    CPU-wise, get the 920. The 965 and the future 975 are a waste of money. Like, $300+ to upgrade from a 920 to the 940 or 950.

    GPU-wise, don't get 4870 or 4850. Get 2 4770s (if you can find them, anyway). Their CrossFireX performance is much higher than a single 4890, and they cost less, to boot.

    But if you looking for Single-slot, then almost all 4850s will fit.

    RAM: Kingston DDR3-1333 should do the trick, or if Cash isn't a problem, Go with Corsair DOMINATOR series. 

    HDD, CD/DVD/BD/HD DVD all can find very easily.

    PSU: Again, if cash is not an issue, go with a Cooler Master or Corsair PSU, at least 700W to leave some leeway for any future upgrades.

     

    CPU cooling, look up for MPTs recommendations.
