They made it a very smooth and easy to arrive and begin training.  The instructor I had for both my courses was very knowledgable in what he was teaching; it was very indepth and detailed information.  I was very pleased with the experience I had here in GOA and will make sure to come back soon.   The pass rate is very high as its very detailed information and the fact the teacher to student ratio is very low..aka 2 or 3 to 1 teacher.  My instructor was also very easy to understand and was able to describe things very well.  As to the Accom..Its up to you on where you want to stay from apartments to 5 star hotel.  I chose the 5 star and wasnt let down by any means.  Hotel was also another great experience.  Hope I have helped you some!
