    According to independent reviews by two respectable video-enthusiast communities, the best is H.264 codec, as of now.

    H.264 is implemented best by x264.

    X264 can be ported and used within VirtualDubMod environment, through the VFW version, since VirtualDubMod uses VFW.

    deaththesheep.uni.cc

    Please also read the manual on that website, it will teach you how to optimize the settings.

    For further reference you can check www.doom9.org on the codec comparisons, or also www.compression.ru which has useful additional filters as well as comparisons.

    Personally I use x264 myself.
