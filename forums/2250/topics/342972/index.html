    Lcd light flashing meaning the monitor is not in synch with PC... Do u see anything on the screen, like ram test...etc when power up?

    I think u can follow the following steps to isolate the problem:

    1) Power off the PC. Remove all the ram except 1, if there is only 1 ram, swap to other slot. Power up and try, if does not work try step 2.

    2) Power off the PC. Replace a working graphic card and try again, as long as u can see something display on the monitor, it means ur graphics card is working. U might want to try swap to another available slot if u do not have extra graphic card to test if the slot is the issue. 

    3) Check the CPU fan is turning or not when u power up the PC. Chances of this is low cos the PC is power down for some time, so overheat issue is unlikely. But not harm checking on this

    4) If neither of the steps work, i believe might be the motherboard related problem. It would be wise u let someone familiar with pc hardware to handle ur issues or u might risk getting more things damage.

     

    Conclusion: Most likely is hardware issues, i will narrow it down to mb and graphics card that would be the likely causes. Hope this helps!
