Google Nexus 7 Available in Singapore from 29 Sept for S$399

  • Ellakuok87
    28 Sep 12, 12:25

    The Google Nexus 7 will be available in Singapore starting 29th September, with the 16GB (Wi-Fi) version priced at S$399. You can purchase the Nexus 7 from stores islandwide such as Harvey Norman, Newstead and Challenger.

    First unveiled at Google I/O 2012 in June, the Nexus 7 is the world's first 7-inch quad-core tablet. It is also the first device to run on Android 4.1 Jelly Bean, the latest iteration of the Android platform. Here's a quick recap of its specs:

        * Android 4.1 Jelly Bean
        * NVIDIA Tegra 3 quad-core 1.2GHz processor
        * 1GB RAM
        * 16GB internal storage capacity (no memory card slot)
        * 1.2-megapixel front facing camera
        * 4,325mAh battery
        * 198.5 x 120 x 10.45mm
        * 340g
