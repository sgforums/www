    Have you tried adding the old HDD into your computer and try accessing the old HDD through the new HDD?

    Set your new HDD as Master and the old HDD as slave. After running the new operating system in the new HDD, the new operating system should be able to detect the old HDD as secondary drive. Just double click on that old HDD and copy the files you want over to your new HDD.

    After that, do whatever you want with it. Either you remove the old HDD from your computer or you format it and use it.
