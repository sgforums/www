    Eh if you want a numbered list to increment its list counter, the elements must only be a newline character away. Eg.

    ----
    #dumb

    #mayi
    ----

    gives you:
    ----
    1. dumb

    1. mayi
    ----

    and

    ----
    #clever
    #mayi
    ----

    gives you:
    ----
    1. clever
    2. mayi
    ----
