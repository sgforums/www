ViewSonic presents stylish multimedia monitors

  • NCgong
    13 Apr 16, 17:32

    High style and high tech come together in the sleek, sculpted design of ViewSonic stylish borderless VX2573 and VX2771 entertainment and multimedia monitors. ViewSonic Corp., a leading global provider of visual solution products, brings in stylish design elements to the 25-inch VX2573 and 27-inch VX2771 series. The ViewSonic VX Series of monitors is designed to satisfy users with discerning eyes to image quality and entertainment applications. Besides delivering outstanding multimedia performance, the new stylish monitors also make the statement for a perfect blend of style and performance.


    ViewSonic’s premium 25-inch VX2573, which includes VX2573-sg and VX2573-shw, comes in a sleek, borderless, glossy-finish, and gold/silver color design. In addition to 1920 x 1080 resolution and 80M:1 dynamic contrast ratio, it is equipped with SuperClear® AH-IPS technology, delivering a 178 /178 degree wide viewing angle and stunning image quality with brilliant visual performance. It also comes with Flicker-free and Blue Light Filter eye-care technologies, ViewMode technology for optimal color rendering for different scenarios. With an amazingly convenient My Button hotkey design, it allows users to easily access these functions for further adjustment with one press of the finger. This monitor also features low EMI emissions for a reliable and sincere commitment to our end users’ health. The VX2573 is ideal for homes and commercial environments with a contemporary décor.

    The new 27-inch VX2771 series, which includes the VX2771-smhg and VX2771-smhv-2, comes with a premium design of gorgeous gold or dazzling silver colored hairline metallic finish stand. Learning from sport car-type design, ViewSonic takes a leap into the future and proves the greatness from within. VX2771 blends an edge-to-edge display with borderless. The visible bezel is only 0.38cm thin and delivers an increased screen surface area that lets you see more without taking up valuable desktop space. In addition, the slim bezel enables easy configuration of multi-screen setups with a sleek, fashionable look that stands out in any setting.

    The ViewSonic VX2771 series carries Full HD 1920 x 1080 resolution which enables users to discover more delicate details and more comprehensive content. Equipped with SuperClear® IPS technology, it delivers a 178/178 degree wide viewing angle with stunning image quality and powerful visual performance. It also includes HDMI and VGA inputs, enable users to connect to game consoles, Blu-ray players, digital cameras, laptops, satellite boxes, and other high-definition devices. The VX2771 series has two integrated 2W stereo speakers for enhanced multimedia performance with crystal-clear stereo sound. The VX2771 features including Flicker-free and Blue Light Filter eye-care technologies aim to establish a reliable and sincere commitment for the users’ health.
