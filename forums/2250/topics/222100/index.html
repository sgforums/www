    Question 1: If you have upgraded your PC quite a number of times, and your XP CD is a legal one, you have to call up Microsoft. They will give you a new CD key to activate Windows. This "illegal" Windows hit a few legal PCs because they do upgrading and Microsoft thought they are fake copies of Windows.

    Question 2: Once your antivirus expires, there's no way you can update it without paying for it again. Either you get a free one (AVG or avast!) or you pay for it again. Symantec's site would show you how.

    Question 3: As per answered by abao. You can always change back to the ball mouse or get an optical mouse pad or use an exercise book if don't want to buy. Exercise book works very well and it's free of charge if you have lots of them at home.

    Note: I assumed that all the copies of software you have are legal before this "illegal" prompt by Microsoft and Symantec. If they aren't, it won't work.
