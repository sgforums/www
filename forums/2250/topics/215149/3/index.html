    Originally posted by edwin3060:
    sorry for being a noob, but is wireless@sg 802.11 a, b or g?
    They are just different wireless standards.

    802.11a operates on the 5GHz frequency, has a theoretical speed of 54Mbps.

    802.11b and 802.11g operates on the 2.4GHz frequency, has a theoretical speed of 11Mbps and 54Mbps respectively.

    The new upcoming standard, 802.11n also operates on the 2.4GHz frequency, but has a theoretical speed of 108Mbps.
