    Excellent parts you got there! Very good cooler, great motherboard, but the RAM may pose an issue, since the Ripjaws kits are meant for Intel, not AMD. If you can find the GSkill Flare kits that would be great, but you probably won't get the most out of the Ripjaws. The graphics card is an excellent choice, but the PSU might be a bit overkill for the system, unless you plan on doing CrossFire a year or two down the road. Other than that, it's a pretty good system for gaming, the Thuban CPU won't let you down.
