    Speeds of 11Mbps, 54Mbps, 100Mbps, 300Mbps (I think?) and 1000Mbps are all your link speed to your router/Wireless Modem, not your internet plan. They are 802.11 b,g, Ethernet, N-Draft (Or final already?) and Gigabit LAN respectively. It is how fast the info goes from your Local network (or LAN) to your com and vice versa, not to your ISP. Link speed and Internet Plan Speed are 2 different things.
