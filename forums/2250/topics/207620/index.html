    You have to understand that SFX in video editing applications are limited. There are different types of effects too. Simple and common ones like ghost trails or motion blurs can be found in video editing softwares but advanced effects like flaming text or compositing (think Lord of the Rings and Fantastic 4) requires seperate applications. For Windows, its Adobe After Effects and for Mac theres Motion (2D) and Shake (3D Compositing)
