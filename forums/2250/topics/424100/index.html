    In iTunes:

    Go to iTunes Store

    At Right hand corner, click on your email address

    A box will pop up asking for your password

    Type it in and press View Account

    You will go to Account Information

    Click on Purchase History

    That's where you can see what kind of stuff you have made purchases on.

    If there are any errors, you can click Report a Problem.

     

    2 likely scenarios though.

    1. You bought something without realizing it.

    2. You made an in-game purchase (like buying the big eagle in Angry Birds). The app might be free, but the item will still be charged.
