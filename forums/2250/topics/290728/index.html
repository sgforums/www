    How about try giving your cache bigger space?

    Firefox:

    1. Tools > Options

    2. Select Advanced at the top

    3. Under the Cache section, put in a number greater than 50MB, say about 100MB

    4. Click OK to apply the settings. Close and open Firefox again

    Internet Explorer 7:

    1. Tools > Internet Options

    2. Select the General tab

    3. Under Browsing History, click on Settings

    4. In the Disk Space to Use: Up the number to about 100MB or something higher than your current configuration

    5. Click OK to apply the settings

    6. Close and open Internet Explorer again

    See if it's able to load fully when it disconnects... if it doesn't... I think it's a cookie issue...
