    Most likely you are using single click or your mouse is hyper sensitive.

    1. Open My Computer.

    2. Click on Tools > Folder Options

    3. Select the General tab

    4. Check that this button is not selected: Single click to open an item. If it is, change to Double click to open an item.

    5. Click OK to apply the settings.
