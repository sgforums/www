    Vista or xp?

     

    Prevent it from happening again.

    Vista:

    1. Start.
    2. type in windows update
    3. At the left side, click on Change Settings
    4. Tick: "check for updates but let me choose whether to install them"
    5. click ok (uac required)

     

    Untick update.

    1. Follow steps 1 to2 above.
    2. At the main screen click on "view available updates."
    3. Untick them
