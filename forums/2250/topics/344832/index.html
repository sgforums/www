    The camera is alright only in good sunlight. It's not even as good as a 3.2 mega pix cybershot years ago.

    The browser is awesome on the huge screen but it doesn't offer built in flash 10 support. Rumoured Skyfire browser will offer that soon. Internet speed on wifi and 3G is really quick.

    It's quite easy to navigate with the touch screeen and type stuff but its definately not as responsive as the iphone. But I can live with that.

    Video playback, esp if you get the latest copy of Core media player is exceptional. Nothing comes close. It thrashes the iphone in this category. The HD's resolution is 2.5 TIMES the iphone.

    As for apps, plenty of apps for Windows Mobile 6 around. Many for Windows Mobile 5 are also compatitble.

    After a few charges, the phone can last 3 days of moderate use. I.e some phone calls, surfing and watching videos.

    There's built in threaded sms. And download the free Live messenger to make it the complete messaging device.

    TouchFlo is stunning.

    Some people have even started modding the Android to work on the HD.

    It's a great phone if you're a geek, or into watching movies on the go.

    Only complaint maybe that for a beginer its not exactly user-friendly at the start. You have to get used to it. Depends on what you're looking for. If you're looking for powerful hardware its the HD. If not the iphone is a great alternative.
