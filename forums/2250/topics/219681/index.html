    Dun worry too much.

    Many laptops faces this problem, especially those compact versions, or a AMD-chip system.

    Many models also lack an effective cooling systems. 30min is still under acceptable range.

    What you can do is:

    1) Cooler base - recommended
    2) Operate in air-conditional rooms
    3) Level the base of the notebook up slightly

    Although hot environments shortens lifespan of notebooks, effects are not so distinguish. Also, once reached a dangerous lvl, most notebooks shut themselves down.
