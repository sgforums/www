    Hi nfshp253!

    To be honest GTX680 in SLI configuration is not optimal in terms of price to performance ratio. I would suggest you go with either GTX670 in SLI, or even GTX660 TI in 3-way SLI.

    SSD for RAID is a bit too expensive for your budget. But if you must, use something like OCZ Vector, or another model that supports TRIM in RAID 0 configuration.

    PSU, go for something like Seasonic, or another OEM manufacturer. Corsair, Cooler Master buy their PSUs from Seasonic and the likes, and relabel them and sell them at a slightly higher price.
