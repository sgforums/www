    Actually it's easier to buy a casing and put that old HDD inside (and it becomes a usb external harddisk) and then copy and paste the documents onto the new laptop HDD. Reason being because for the new laptop, there's new drivers involved for the hardware, so just by cloning the entire HDD, your new notebook may not function at its optimum level.
