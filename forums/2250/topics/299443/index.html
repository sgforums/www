    When you push the restart button, was the hard disk operation light on? If it was writing to your hard disk and you did a reset, you could have damaged the hard disk.

    That's why your Windows couldn't boot up. Your system files could have been damaged.

    Check that your monitor cables are properly plugged in, with the power off ensure that the graphics card is properly slot in too and is the power supply to the graphics card plugged in.

    How old is your graphics card?

    If it's under 1 year, it might still be under warranty.

    Can try clearing out your BIOS too. There are 2 jumpers you have to short in order to reset the BIOS (you have to look at your motherboard manual to know which are the 2 jumpers).
