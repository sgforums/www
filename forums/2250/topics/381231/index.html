    Generally there are no major price differences in PC shows. At the most, you will probably see about $300 difference. You are likely to get more goodies though when you buy from a PC show than when you buy from a retail shop.

    However, with Windows 7 launch, I'm not sure if there are such goodies... usually a new Windows launch means that prices will get higher for a while before it starts coming down.

    Lappy prices don't change much, unlike PCs. If you want to play games, suggest you go for a PC instead of lappy, unless you are playing casual games such as those on Facebook, Miniclip, etc. PCs are also simpler and easier to upgrade, thus saving money.
