    Buy what your manual says.

    Supports DDr2-400/533
    Either DDR2 400 or DDR2 533

    Slotting in is easy. Look for the "teeth"



    Notice in the above picture that there's a hole. Before slotting in, hold it slightly above the RAM slots, make sure that this hole matches with the slot. If it doesn't, just flip over (not turn it upside down, but turn from left to right or vice versa).
