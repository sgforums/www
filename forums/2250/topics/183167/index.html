    Originally posted by skeujin:
    recently my contacts who r tokg to me will kena d/c and cnt even msg me unless they close the existing chatbox n open a new one.

    anyone can advise me on wats wrng?
    Ask them check their connections. Those using wireless especially. Wireless signals can be strong or weak, and sometimes they are so weak that they will be disconnected. Also check for viruses and spywares.

    There's no need to close and open a new one, unless you are having mass chat. For one to one chat, just repair the wireless, then sign in again.

    For those using wired connections, check for spywares and viruses. They may be clogging up the connections to the extent that it may disconnect.

    For Singnet users, there's been a lot of intrusion attacks lately. They are recommended to paste their firewall logs and send to Singnet. For those using ZoneAlarm firewall, go to C:\Windows\Internet Logs\ZALog.txt

    Open up this log and copy the whole thing. For users of other firewall, they have to find them out themselves.

    The website: http://www.singnet.com.sg/tou/misc/ia_incidentreport.asp
