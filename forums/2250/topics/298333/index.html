    An update.

    noopi, are you using Norton or McAfee?

    http://www.infoworld.com/article/07/12/19/Microsoft-offers-workaround-for-IE-security-patch-problems_1.html

    Some problems have been reported with users who have Norton or McAfee and a particular Windows Update.

    1. Start > Control Panel and double click on Add/Remove Programs.

    2. Tick the Show Updates box at the top right hand corner.

    3. Find this Update - Security Update for Windows XP (KB942615)



    4. Click once to select it. Click on Remove to uninstall it. Restart your computer.

    5. Uninstall your Norton or McAfee. Restart your computer.

    6. Go to Windows Update again and update your computer. After that, restart your computer.

    7. Reinstall your Norton or McAfee and restart your computer.
