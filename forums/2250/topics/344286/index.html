    I have read your thread, and I shall give a few points based on my view here.

    1. The IT industry is saturated indeed - with people who know little shit about IT.
    2. If you wish to compete in terms of salary (like what SG government has been doing to our workforce, and the opposite to themselves), you'll probably lose out to those in China and India in the long run.
    3. Smart employers (unless you want to work with dumb employers who would shout at you every single working day) don't look at the cost of an employee alone - they do factor in the quality and speed of their employees. For example, if there's two salesmen applying for a job, one asks for a salary of $5,000 and promises a turnover of $100,000 within a month for your company, and the other demands $50,000 but can ensure a turnover of $1,000,000 within two weeks - who would you hire?
