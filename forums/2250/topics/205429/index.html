    maybe u can try x264 x264.nl to compress the video

    or XviD http://www.koepi.org/xvid.shtml

    or DivX http://www.divx.com/

    x264 give a better compression while maintaining watchable quality, but its very CPU intensive and will take much longer than the other 2 codecs.


    You might also want to use VirtualDub as your GUI for encoding video. http://virtualdub.sourceforge.net/


    What audio quality are u recording in? maybe u can use iTunes and recompress the audio to a lower bitrate in AAC.
