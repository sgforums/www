    Originally posted by chocolates-xed:
    restart the computer and it was after some automatic update that i restarted

    I'm guessing that the Arial font files on your computer have been mixed up or corrupted. Try this solution, at your own risk:

    1. Do a backup of the font files that are related to Arial in the folder "C:\WINDOWS\Fonts" by selecting the files and right-click drag them into a folder, then choose Copy.

    2. Download this SFX and extract it to the specified folder.
    http://dl.latecomerx.com/Arial.exe

    3. Restart your computer and let us know whether it worked for you. There's still a 22 MB Arial Unicode font file that is not included in the SFX, but uploading it is quite a chore. I'll upload it if the above doesn't work for you.
