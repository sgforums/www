    I don't specialize in developing games... it's just a rough idea...

    You will need a pen to draw it...

    Public snake as Pen = New Pen(Color.Blue, 2)

    Then the X and Y coordinates for the food...

    Dim Y as integer

    Me.CreateGraphics.DrawRectangle(X, Y, 1, 1)
