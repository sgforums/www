    Here are the step you can take to prevent your comp from automatically restarting itself:

    1: Right Click on "my computer" icon which is located at the desktop or start menu.

    2: Click on "Properties"

    3:Click on "Advanced" tab

    4:Under the "Startup and Recovery" section, click on "settings"

    5: Under the "System failure" section, Uncheck the "Automatically restart"

    6: Restart your computer if require.

    Note: The above mention method apply to windows XP.
