    Which is which, no sound from laptop or no sound from external speakers?

    Try isolating the problem whether is it laptop or external speakers problem.

    Unconnect external speakers from laptop, set sound volume to mid-range or max range, then try play back mp3 using a media player of your choice. 

    If there is no sound from your laptop internal speakers, then chances are there is a resource conflict in your windows settings, corrupt sound driver or your laptop's audio processor chip is spoilt.

    If there is sound from your laptop internal speakers, then problem maybe is at the external speakers.  Just connect the external speakers to another PC / laptop and you will know.
