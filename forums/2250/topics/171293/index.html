    Is your wireless network secured? Like chanff8 have said, it could be due to unstability.

    Another reason for slowing down could be because your network is not secured. Someone could just tapped it and used it to download things which can clog up the bandwith. And there could be more than one person.

    The last reason will be handphones and microwave ovens. These two devices work at the same frequency as the wireless devices. Putting these two devices near your computer will cause unstability as well as a drop in signal strength. Move away from these two devices and you will notice a mark improvement in your speed.
