    you mean most of your icons became like that?

    • To fix your problem, download and install TweakUI for Windows XP  

    http://www.microsoft.com/windowsxp/downloads/powertoys/xppowertoys.mspx

    • After installing, go to Start > Programs > PowerToys for Windows XP >
      TweakUI.   
    • Click the "Repair" link, in the right pane of TweakUI, click on the pull-down and choose "Rebuild Icons".
    • Click "Repair Now", then "OK" to close the dialog box.  

    If it doesnt work go to:

    C:\Documents and Settings\user name\Local Settings\Application Data\iconcache.db

     to see if the icons are there


    If a prompt is given when you click on C drive to "show hidden files", click to show it.
