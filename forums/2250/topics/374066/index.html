  • ndmmxiaomayi
    3 Sep 09, 21:12

    Hmm... Blogspot probably no need to submit, but I would still suggest to submit anyway.

    To Google - http://www.google.com/addurl/

    To Yahoo - http://siteexplorer.search.yahoo.com/submit

    If you have RSS feeds, you can use the "Submit Site Feed" from Yahoo.

    Once done, will need to check some settings in Blogger.

    1. Log in to Blogger.

    2. Select Settings > Basic. Ensure that "Let search engines find your blog?" is set to Yes

    3. Select Settings > Comments. Scroll down to Backlinks. Set it to Show.

    4. Include the following to your blog, right under the <head> section:

    <meta name="keywords" content="blog, Blogger, blogspot, test, testing" /> change the words "blog, Blogger, blogspot, test, testing" to something relevant to your blog
    <meta name="description" content="Just testing" /> change the words "Just testing" to something relevant to your blog
    <meta name="author" content="mayi" /> change the words "mayi" to your name

    5. Title of your blog - think through it carefully. If it's too common, you are going to fight with a million and one blogs which has similar title. But don't be too cheeky either, users probably won't use such words to search.

    6. Try to use labelling in your posts if possible. Though Blogspot labelling sucks for SEO.

    7. Have a good URL structure. This requires quite a good bit of technical knowledge in structuring Blogspot's URL.

    8. Update your blog regularly. Search engines don't like to crawl pages that are not updated regularly. If it's updated regularly, it will prompt the robots to crawl more regularly, so there's a chance your blog will appear in search engine results.

    Other tips - http://www.google.com/webmasters/docs/search-engine-optimization-starter-guide.pdf
