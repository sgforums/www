    Originally posted by blade22224:

    i hoped that the laptop would last throughout my poly life...anyways that i can maintain the acer's lifespan?


    That's a tricky question.

    well it's kinda fated.

    There are ways to extend and mantain it.

    1. Try to keep the laptop and laptop adaptor as cool as possible -- to prevent the burn out of parts. you can do this by having a fan to blow over it/ get a laptop cooling pad or both.
    2. Try not to over work it -- overworking of processors/graphics/fans can cause the life span to shorten
    3. Try not to bump your laptop here and there.
    4. Keep the laptop clean (maintain cleanliness)
    5. Defrag regularly (faster laptop)
    6. Scan for viruses/spyware regularly
