    1. Plug in power cables into router and modem

    2. LAN Cable connect one end to modem, the other end to the router. Plug into the connection that says "Connection to Modem" or something along that line. The connection is usually seperated from the other LAN inputs.

    3. Make sure your internet modem is connected to the TV output from the wall. The wire ends should be circular with a pin in the middle.

    4. Once everything is done, you should be able to surf wirelessly straight away. To access the control panel of the router, check the router's manual, which should tell you the default address of the control panel.
