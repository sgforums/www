    A. To check if your hard disk is failing. 
    1. When your PC boots up, access the bios. 
    2. Do a hard disk diagnostics. 
    3. If It takes too long and gets stuck, ultimately giving you a reply that it's failing, it is. 

    B. If your PC is from a PC manufacturer such as HP/SONY/ACER. 
    1. Check for your COA Sticker - it is most probably at the side of the desktop. It will look like this. 
    2. Check the sticker for the operating system. If it states Windows Vista or Windows XP. The operating system is not the OEM operating system.
    3. Check with the person who gives you, if the upgrade disc to Windows 7 is given by the original equipment manufacturer else go to Scenario C.
    C. If your PC is a custom built one. 
    1. Download the ISO from here. (Which ever you once used)
    -Win 7, Professional 32 Bit
    -Win 7, Home premium 32 Bit
    -Win 7, Ultimate 32 Bit 
      2. Burn ISO into a disc if you are unsure, look here.  http://www.zdnet.com/blog/hardware/how-to-burn-your-windows-7-iso-to-dvd-disc/3317

    3. Refer to here on how to reinstall your operating system detailed guide 
    Mod's note: Reinstall will preserve all your files and user accounts (leaving everything intact) Clean Install will remove everything. 

    Mod's 2nd note: Windows 7 Cd key is needed. You have to get it yourself. This forum will not give out any CD keys, it's a legit forum for help.
