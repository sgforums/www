    PC (1) ---- Router (2)  ---- Modem (3) ---- Internet (4) ---- Modem  ---- Router ---- Server

    Possible causes:

    1. Your PC is infected with malware and is busy sending viagra-related spam to your friends, girls included. How to verify: Do a anti-virus scan.

    2. You're using a wireless router and some stranger is leeching your bandwidth. How to verify: Plug the modem directly to your PC and see if the connection improves.

    3. The cables between your PC, your modem and router, or even your router and modem themselves are damaged. (Rare) How to verify: Change them and see if the situation improves. You may want to get SingTel to install a new phone line as well, but they would charge $50 for it fyi.

    4. You're downloading from an overseas server which happened to have a slow uplink. How to verify: Test your speed by downloading a file from a locally-based server (go somewhere like www.maplesea.com and download their game client).

    Not sure what are the exact differences between SingTel and StarHub, but the issue about lagging during peak hours with StarHub seems to be common.
