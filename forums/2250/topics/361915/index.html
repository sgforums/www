    Control Panel >> Regional and Language Options >> "Keyboard and languages" tab

    >> Click on "Change Keyboads" >>  A new window (Text Services and Input langauge) will pop out.

    Under General tab, Installed services ... click on the "Add" button. Select on "Chinese (Simplified) - Microsoft Pinyin IME" and click 'Ok'

    Go on to 'Langauge Bar' Tab, select "docked in the taskbar"


    Now it is done, u can switch between chinese and english text typing ...
