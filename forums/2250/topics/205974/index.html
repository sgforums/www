    Intel has dropped the 'Pentium' naming sometime back on their processors. One of the newer processor is named 'Core Duo'. There should be no such a thing as a 'P5'. PGA775 (not 'Boeing 775') is just the pin out and a lookout for processor versus mobo fitting. If the number fits a board and a core, it is usually compatible between the two.
