    u can only tweak ur PC to work at the max d/l speed possible for ur connection. Some other factors cannot be changed.

    in China, last time i use one plan that the max can go 1000k/s(limited hours per month,extra hours need to pay extra) , but later i changed to a plan that max can go 250k/s but unlimited hours.

    the reason is that even i can d/l at 1000k/s, the source i d/l from may not be able to give me that kind of speed. The situation i need high speed is d/l things from BT, but only when the file is a popular files with many seeds/users, or i am downloading a lot at the same time, then i can get to see the speed go up to 1000k/s, most of the time, i dun get 1000k for download one or two movies. Unless i d/l ten movies at a time then i may get 1000k/s in my BT.

    So i changed to a max 250k/s unlimited hours plan. then when i download in BT, it easily go to the max. And i make it a habit that i visit BT sites before i sleep, let then run for the night while i am sleeping, the next morning i wake up it should be all finished.

    Of cos it's faster the better, but depending on how u use it, u may not really need that fast.

    Using download softwares wont really help to increase ur max download speed(cos it's limited by what type of connection u use), but it helps by downloading thru mutli connections(if server allows, some ftp servers dont allow one single IP to have more than one connections).
