    Originally posted by 8008s:

    will my data be gone during this process?

    Depends on what you mean by your data.

    If your data means:

    1. Customized Windows settings

    2. Your files

    3. Your installed programs

    Number 2 & 3 won't be lost, unless something goes wrong with the repair process. I can't recall for Number 1.

    If you have a live CD such as Linux or UBCD4Win, try booting up to it, copy all your personal files to a thumbdrive or external hard disk before performing a repair.

    Otherwise, if the repair process goes wrong, you will eventually lose everything.
