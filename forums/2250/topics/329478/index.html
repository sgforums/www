    I'm not sure how I could advice you, but for me, I do not like being tied down to a contract, thus did not renew the contract after mine ended a few months ago, though I'm still with Singnet right now.

    Regarding the 2Wire Wireless Gateway from Singnet, it's pretty good in my opinion (my sis is using it), but the additional $4 x 24 you pay could get you one from Netgear or D-Link out there as well. It's pretty much a matter of preference here, since to me, 2Wire's products are on par with those of popular brands such as Netgear and D-Link in terms of performance and stability.
