    For gaming, an Athlon II X4 640 will do.

    It's not like you're doing intensive stuff, so the L3 cache of the X4 945 is completely pointless.

    And you probably won't be overclocking, looking at the kind of hardware you originally picked, so why don't you get this instead.

    CoolerMaster Elite 341 Chassis
