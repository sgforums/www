    Honour Your Website with Singapore Website Awards (SWA) 2017

    No one recognized your web design talent and hard work?

    Good news for you - Singapore Website Award (SWA 2017) is here! Get your website recognized and become a Website Superstar now!

     

    SWA 2017 seeks to recognize and reward the outstanding performance, talent and effort of the best web developers, web designers and web agencies and at the same time, encourage creativity, sharing and improvement of overall web design quality in the country. SWA 2017 will be awarded based on 3 categories:

    1. Best Personal Category

    2. Best Commercial Category

    3.    Best E-Commerce Category

     

    Why do I need to join the SWA 2017?

    1. Great opportunity for your website to get more exposure.

    2. Get your talent recognized by experts

    3. Exceptional way to build your portfolio 

    4.    Compete in a friendly way with fellow friends

     

    Your website will be judged based on 5 criteria:

    1. Design

    2. SEO

    3. Accessibility

    4. Performance

    5.    Content

     

    If your website meets these criteria and you believe that it will be the best, submit your website now and get awarded!

     

     

    How does the awards work? There are four simple steps to follow:

     

    1. Check out and determine the categories of your website

    2.  Submit Your Website to SWA 2017 website. Please confirm that your website is complying with our Terms and Conditions before applying.

    3. The submission will be verified by SWA’s independent judges. A notification on the approval of the website will be sent through another email.

    4.    At this point, you just need to Share your website to your Friends and get as many votes as possible, and leave the rest to the Judges to evaluate your website.

     

    3 awards will be given out for SWA 2017:                                    

    1. Site of the Month - Rated by SWA Independence Judges

    2. Site of the Year - Rated by SWA Expert Judges

    3.    The Public's Favorite Website - Voted by the Public

     

    It is so simple to get your website awarded. What are you waiting for? Submit your website to get the award now!
