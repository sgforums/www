    When the thumb drive is infected by the virus, a virus scan will not show any virus as the virus has overtaken the anti-virus program. The virus will  be detected on other non-infected computers whereby the anti-virus program will detect the virus and flash the message that a virus is detected.

    It is likely that not only the thumbdrive is infected but the PC or laptop is also infected too.
