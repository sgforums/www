    PC Magazine, PC World, CPU (Computer Power User), Official Windows XP, Macworld, Linux, Maximum PC and HWM.

    PC Magazine and PC World has a Singapore version and can be bought from newsstands or bookshops.

    Macworld are in major bookstores, particularly Popular.

    CPU, Official Windows XP, Linux and Maximum PC might have to spend some time hunting. They are not always in Singapore.

    Gaming magazines I can't really recall the names, but the names are pretty obvious. Gamers or something similar. Popular bookshops have them.

    Gadgets wise, it's mostly related to photography and some entertainment stuffs like speakers. Major IT magazines do write up on them, but not as detailed. Again, at major bookstores.

    Other rarer magazines will have to hunt some smaller bookshops around neighbourhoods or those that rent out books. I saw a Hackin9 magazine around my area. Hackin9 is about security and networking.
