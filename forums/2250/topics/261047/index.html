  • ndmmxiaomayi

    No. He can't possibly transfer any money out without your permission, unless you explicitly grant it (for example, giving him your password to your online bank account).

    You will call or email the person (in this case is call).

    You will give him your account number and some other details. He promises to contact you again (which he does).

    He asks you to transfer some money to his money and gives you his bank account number. The amount varies, anything from thousands to tens and even hundreds of thousands.

    Once transferred, he promised you that you will receive the money in a few days and ask you to check.

    But you will never receive the money.

    For lucky ones, at the most you lose a few thousands. Those unlucky ones may have their bank account wipe out, especially if you really give out the password to the online bank account.

    Those involve in calling (like in this case), may find yourself paying not just the transfer money, but also international call fees, which is sometimes a lot.
