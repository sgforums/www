    you might also want to consider this, since you mentioned you are 45 M (150feet)  away from your router

    It's called an ethernet powerline adapter , the essence of how it works is basically this:

    1. Connect an Ethernet cable output from the back of your wireless router to this device as shown above. Then plug it into the powersocket. 
    2. Connect the companion (the receiver end) into a power socket at your desk/workspace. Then connect another Ethernet cable from there to your PC
    The genius of this would be that internet signals are transmitted via the powerline socket itself, it's plug and play essentially. 
    Depending on the brands, the range might vary.
