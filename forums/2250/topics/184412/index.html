    hmm it depends on what kind of print you want

    basically the 2 most common Tshirt prints are iron-on and silk screening

    Iron-on you just take your design to the shop owner and he will help you transfer it to an iron paper then use press method to imprint on the T-shirt

    As for silk screening, it's more complex, you will need to separate out the colours of your design into different silkscreen boards then screen those colours 1by 1 onto the Tshirt
