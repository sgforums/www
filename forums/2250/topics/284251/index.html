    Stop it from restarting and type out the full error code.

    To stop it, do this:

    1. Right click on My Computer and select Properties.

    2. Select the Advanced tab.

    3. Under Startup and Recovery, click on the Settings button.

    4. Under System Failure, untick this box: Automatically restart.

    5. Click OK all the way and apply the settings.
