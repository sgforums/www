    starhub-cable
    which means it's shared. For example, you've applied for a 10mbps connection. If your area has lots of ppl using starhub, you'll never get the full 10mbps. That explains the slow down during the holidays and after school hours.Weather might affect speed. The good thing is that it's very easy to install. Just plug and play.
    singnet-ADSL
    which means it's not shared. You apply for a 10mbps connection, you can get the full 10mbps connection(that is if nobody taps into your connection). It's not shared so the whole connection is yours. No slowing down and sometimes not affected by weather. Got 'ping power' which lowers your pings in some online games.
