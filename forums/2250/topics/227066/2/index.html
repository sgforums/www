    Actually it depends on the user.

    I've been abusing both for quite some time to say this...

    - Singnet takes fewer hops to reach destination (aka good latency)
    - Singnet has an overall high upload speed which is not likely to affect its download speed. Starhub will be bogged down once you maxed your upload, you will be snailing with your download.
    - Cable modem is more likely to be damaged by lightning than adsl modem.
    - Higher population density in your neighbourhood = lower bandwidth for starhub

    - Starhub has a general better download speed if you know how to tweak.
    - Starhub is generally more reliable as a host. (No once a month server reset)
    - Starhub plans are unlikely to become more expensive once contracts end
