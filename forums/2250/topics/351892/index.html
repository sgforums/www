    Lithium Polymer battery (which are used on laptop, handphone and other movile devices) have no memory effect which means that even if you do not take it out when your laptop is on AC will not reduce your battery life.

    Heat generated by the laptop is the main reason why your laptop battery life are reduced. It's all depends on how well your laptop disperse the heat.

    Give you a real life example - My friend and I were using the same laptop model. After a few months, my battery couldn't even last 10 mins whereas my friend's battery was able to last about 2 hrs.

    The difference? I play games on my laptop and I use my laptop frequently since I do not have a desktop, thus lots of heat were generated by the laptop and the ventilation design on my laptop sucks which affected my battery. I didn't know heat reduced the battery life. By the time I know that, it was too late.

    Guide on how to prolong your lithium battery - http://spicygadget.com/2006/12/24/guide-getting-the-most-out-of-your-lithium-battery/
