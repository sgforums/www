    Webmail is a class of web applications that allow users to read and write e-mail using a web browser, or in a more general sense, an email account accessed through such an application.

    Webmail is commonly offered as a service by Internet companies, sometimes in exchange for providing personal information for marketing purposes. It may also be offered by one's Internet service provider for remote access to a regular email account. Many companies also provide web-based access to their internal email systems, for use by employees when at home or travelling.

    Some mail servers, such as Microsoft Exchange , Kerio MailServer or Atmail, contain built-in webmail interfaces.
