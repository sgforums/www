    Well you got quite a decent computer, probably you need to update the graphics card & if you want the processor. I have no idea why you gta iv runs that slow, probably the settings are wrong, my specs is almost like you except that I have 2gb of RAM & a 8600 GT 256MB, but I able to get decent framerate of around 30fps. You can try to adjust the graphics settings like adjusting your aspect ratio and resolution correctly & set all the details to medium while the texture quality at high (adjust it according to your computer peformance) and for the slider, I drag all the 4 slider to the lowest except the detail distance (highest) and view distance to an appropriate value. Lastly apply UGT, it should save you a few framerates.

    Macbook /(Pro) can be good if you are not into serious gaming but if you are into serious gaming than it is the wrong choice, they are more useful for documents, casual gaming or photos/video editing.
