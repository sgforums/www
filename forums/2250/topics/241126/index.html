    looking at your budget,you should be able to get most of what you want.

    You stated online games,maybe you could state more specifically which cos there are online games that goes easy on 3d graphics and those that are very 3d graphic intensive.Also you may like to take note that graphic card for laptop has very little chance for upgrade once you get it so you may like to plan it out for future games you want to play too.

    Battery life,laptops come with a default battery (6 cell) that provide about 2-3 hours of batt life but there are also separate higher cell (12 cell) batt that can provide about 5-6 hours of batt life so if you need this,you may like to ask if the brand you are buying sell this separate batt

    Warranty is also important.Most come with 1 year warranty and there are others that provide up to 3 years (some compaq/hp models),Asus laptop come with 2 years warranty.
