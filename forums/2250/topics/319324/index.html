    A google search on 'Zen White Screen of Death' (http://www.google.com.sg/search?hl=en&q=Zen+white+screen+of+death+&meta=) would reveal that this problem is a common one and that it is a bug with the software/hardware. I kena this problem only after using my Zen for 1 month and decided to call the Creative customer service hotline to request for a solution to the problem (though it has been proven online that there's actually no solution to the problem - it's just a disastrous manufacturing defect on the part of Creative). The customer service staff recommended me to reformat the Zen or update its firmware. Now, how am I supposed to reformat the player when I can't even enter the boot-up screen (there is only a empty white screen!)? Ok, so I attempted to update the firmware via the Creative MP3 Player Recovery Tool (though I have already updated it prior to the problem), but the problem remains. So, both solutions don't work. I searched for alternative solutions on the webby and tried those out too. For example, one required me to lock the Zen, and then press and hold the play button while unlocking the Zen again. It worked once, but that's about it.

    So far, Creative has refused to address the problem. Several people have reportedly sent in their defective units to Creative and got a replacement, but the replacements throw up the same problems. Based on the conversation with the customer service staff, it seems that Creative still refuses to address the problem and worse still, she gave me the impression that it's not their fault.

    Before getting the white screen of death problem, I have also encountered frequent 'freezing' of the Zen (that's what the reset button is for, man!). Also, the software kept on messing up with the formats of my music files (so instead of .mp3 and .wma files, I have files that are of unknown formats). Not only that, I have discovered that the Zen mysteriously and automatically deletes away some of my files. The white screen of death is my final straw and I am truly fed up with Creative's poor quality control of the Zen.

    For those of you have the Zen, I believe you should be familiar with the notorious white screen of death problem. YES, it's a disastrous manufacturing defect, and as a customer, I feel VERY cheated because Creative refuses to address this problem, so what I have with me now is essentially a piece of garbage - useless and crappy piece of gadget. I can't believe it's not doing anything and leaving buyers stranded.

    This incident has left me very dissatisfied with Creative and I intend to call Creative on the next working day to ask for a full refund (not a replacement because the replacement will give me the same problems again).

    All Creative Zen units that you see on the shelves now are not guaranteed to be in working condition. Most, if not all, have this problem, and if you buy the Creative Zen, you risk being a victim of this unsolvable white screen of death problem. Therefore, I would advise all who are planning to buy the Creative Zen to steer clear immediately of the product lest you want to be cheated and frustrated.

    Do not be tricked by Creative's 'Credit Card-sized wonder' ads for the Zen! If Creative refuses to grant me a full refund or grant me a reasonable explanation, I may consider bringing this matter to CASE.

    I will never buy another Creative product ever again.
