    Out for test!

    Looks quite good. Very similar to HWZ board. Here are some of the major improvements:

    1. More colours are available. No longer have to refer to HTML Colour Code guide.

    2. A Flash tag (works much like image tag)

    3. A [ * ] tag for adding on to the list. Instead of typing manually. Lazy people, lazy ways.

    4. Allows you to choose if you want to parse URL links automatically.

    5. Can upload files, like some Invision Boards. An example of Invision Board is Geeks to Go.

    6. You can even save the message, though I would find it redundant anyway.

    7. Subscribing to a topic is now easier. Click on the topic, look at the top and click on Subscribe topic.

    8. Can view a topic in Print view, so you no longer need the Print Preview function.

    9. Bookmark a topic, if you don't want emails flooding you.

    10. Email a friend. Very obvious to what it does.

    11. Report a post. It's shown by an exclamation mark (!) on a post. Very big and ugly post.

    12. Tags are now automatic. You no longer have to click once to open, another time to close. Click once to open and close a tag.
