    The warranty covers part by part. That means, if something spoils, you go to different companies to replace it.

    For example, your hard disk and monitor spoils. Say it's a Seagate hard disk and Phillips monitor. You bring the Seagate hard disk to one place for replacement, and the monitor to Phillips designated place for repairs.

    Whereas for branded PCs, they repair/replace everything at their designated repair centres.
