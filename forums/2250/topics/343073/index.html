    Good and cheap don't usually go together leh. My nokia e90 plays them mp4 container, in xvid format. I use mediacoder to convert all types, mkv, rmvb, and such. I encode at 1056kbps, and 30fps, coz the processor, and ram can support it. Of coz the files will end up big, but no problem for an 8gb card. Using a quadcore 2.4g, it shouldn't take you over 20 mins to convert a 2hr+ movie from any format. Make sure you have the latest k lite codec pack installed, makes mkv conversion a breeze.
