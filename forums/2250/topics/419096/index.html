Help! Screen blackout in normal mode

  • Weychin
    29 Dec 10, 19:19

    Hi ! I'm currently compac presario laptop, with vista operating system.

    Recently, my screen have blackout during the normal mode. However, when rebooted in the safe mode, the screen is back. Based on this, the assumption is that it is not a hardware problem. I've managed to get the screen working in normal mode somehow, however, it has blanked out again. Previously the computer have some issues in the sleep mode, problem could be related to this. 

    Care anybody advise me on how to reboot in normal mode with a working screen.

     

  • kenn3th
    29 Dec 10, 20:31

    Do this

    • Power off, Pull out the battery, power adapter connected to the laptop.
    • Press the power button for 10 seconds.
    • Put back the power adapter and switch on the power
    • Press F8 repeatedly 
    • You should come to an advanced boot up menu
    • Enter this. 
    Try this first. 
    Else, check the hinges. The older Compaq presarios do have issues with their hinges.

    You should defrag your laptop- something that is normally not done - the windows default defrag software is not really the best - too slow.
