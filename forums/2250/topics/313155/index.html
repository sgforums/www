    the best thing is to go to a large electronics store or headset specialist shops and try out the headsets one by one; best if you use your own favourite music track, encoded at a reasonably good bitrate (mp3 - 192kbs 44100Khz =<, lc-aac - 160kbs, 44100Khz, wav 1411kbs, 44100Khz, 16bit).

    also focus on the dB per Ohm to find out its energy efficiency and make sure the headset can reproduce the human auditory range of 44100Khz or higher.
