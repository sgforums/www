    The blue coloured file names is because of encrypted files, not compressed files. It only occurs in NTFS because NTFS allows encryption.

    To demonstrate this effect, open My Computer.

    Go to Tools > Folder Options.

    Select the View tab.

    Untick this box: Show encrypted or compressed NTFS files in colour.

    Click OK. Close My Computer and open it again. Go to C:\Windows and look at your files, it won't be coloured in blue.

    Reverse it, the files will be in blue again.
