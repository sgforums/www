    For most wireless routers, unless you run through a set up in My Networks Place in every computer, computers sharing 1 internet source will not see each other. This is normal.

    For the wireless adaptor computer, check whats the speed. Maybe check the connectivity as well, in case you really have a damn big house, or placed the router in a bad place.

    You will only experience a big lag in speed when your neighbours use your network and downloads lots of data. With broadband and a decent wireless router, you can easily support 3 to 5 computers.
