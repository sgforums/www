    Originally posted by Gasfene:
    okay i just want to use chinese words on other forums, ms words and read chinese words from the internet.

    What programs do i use?
    Go to Start, Control Panel, Regional and Language Options.

    Make sure that Install files for East Asian Languages is checked. Then click on Details. Click on Add. Under Input Language, find Chinese (PRC). Click OK. Then click Language Bar. Check the Show the Language Bar on the Desktop. Click OK and you can use Chinese words.













    To read Chinese words on browsers:

    For Internet Explorer:

    Right click, Encoding, More, Chinese Simplified (GB2312)



    For Mozilla Firefox:

    View, Character Encoding, More Encodings, East Asian, Chinese Simplified (GB2312)
