  • MyPillowTalks
    23 Nov 09, 21:15

    I am using Mio. Only internet and mobile and home

    Problems i encounter before:

    - Microfilter by 2wire is sometimes faulty, which will either cause modem to disconnect, which lead to no phone, even in emergency. Reason is because mio voice is using Voice over internet protocol(voip), and the phone is connected into the modem. Thus, no modem, no phone.

    - 2wire modem does not save your login username and password, it seems to be the router's problem, singnet changed the router for free

    - People calling my home, but phone does not ring(i duno why either)

    Before i change to mio 4 years back, im on singnet broadband, 3mbps, no problems at all. But when i started to use mio 2 years ago, and maybe at that time the adsl2+ or voip technology is not that stable on the modem, it caused me to have a lot of problem with my house phone when my uncles and aunties call my house phone to look for my mum(you know they prefer house phone over handphone). If you use ur handphone more then it is fine

    I used mio tv 2-3years ago, not very satisfied. It seems that your TV will hang when u are using internet at the same time. Not sure how good it is now.

    Now im on mio 8mbps(internet and mobile+house phone only, no tv all those), things seems to have stablise a lot from the past recent years, maybe some rare hiccups here and there. Singnet has been very nice to me for tech support all these years. It is worth getting singnet in my opinion.

    The only thing i think is 'special' is that if you get the mio plan with handphone, is that if someone calls your house phone and u didnt pick up(either becos u were sleeping, or because  the modem is giving problems, screw ups etc...) it will give u a sms alert to your mio plan mobile phone
