    PowerColor releases Golden Pig card

    X1650 gets a custom golden cooler

    By Theo Valich: Thursday 15 February 2007, 12:53

    THE CHINESE year of the pig begins this Sunday, and the pig is an animal considered to bring good fortune.
    And in order to celebrate the Chinese New Year, PowerColor launched a special piggy product.

    The board selected to bear a special custom built golden cooler in the shape of a pig is built around ATI Radeon X1650Pro, featuring a GPU clocked at 600MHz and 256MB of GDDR-3 memory at 1.4GHz.




    You may want a window in your PC to show off the pig

    As you can see on the pic, bigger part of the board is covered with a golden plated cooler, and you can read more about the product in Powercolor's press release.

    Who says animals and computers don't mix?
