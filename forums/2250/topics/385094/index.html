    Lines appearing(They call/pronouce it as artifacts) may be caused by software issue, did you overclock your card using the ATI overdrive in the catalyst driver software? IF yes, that might be the case.

    If not, it could meant that you need to update your graphics driver(like what Playmemory stated)

    Hardware side, an overheating Graphics processing unit/Graphics card(GPU)or a GPU with defective video ram will also cause artifacts, try to give your graphics card some ventilation while gaming and see if there are any improvements

    If not, you can bring your graphics card down to the distributor for a check if it is under warranty

    Graphics card malfunctioning. I had the same problem  because I had an unstable overclock on the GPU.
