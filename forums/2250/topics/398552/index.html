    Are there many users in Singapore facing the same problem recently?
    Yes -> Singnet's problem. Call them up.
    No -> Your problem, continue below:

    Do other computers on your home network have the same problem?
    Yes -> Your router, modem or both is screwed. Connect directly to the modem to figure out which is screwed.
    No / I don't use a router -> Your PC, connection to router/modem or both is screwed, continue below:

    Does another computer using the same LAN cable to your router/modem have the same issue?
    Yes -> Change your cable.
    No / Wireless -> Your PC is screwed, continue below:

    Does switching to another web browser help?
    Yes -> Your IE is screwed. Use Chrome/Firefox.
    No -> Your PC is really screwed, continue below:

    Does running an anti-virus scan help?
    Yes -> Your PC is fixed.
    No -> Your PC is really, really screwed. Wait for more replies on this thread.
