    Originally posted by elinda:


    But I saw some normal labtop costing about $900 is use "Intel Pentium". Is this better or lousier than dual core?

    Make sure it says Pentium Dual Core, not just Intel Pentium. The Intel Pentium is a single-core CPU, while the Pentium Dual Core, is, well, Dual core. The laptop you are talking about probably has the Pentium Dual Core, since the only laptop I can think of that uses the Pentium is one of the Acer Timelines.

    If you look for the laptop, the label should say Pentium Dual Core T4200 (The current generation PDC CPUs) and not anything else.
