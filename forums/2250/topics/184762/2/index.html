    ps/2 keyboard : pluck out every key tab, unscrew the entire key case, soak all the keys in conc detergent (soak n wash), remove circuit board, wipe down all, wipe down PS/2 wire, put back everything.

    laptop : unscrew laptop casing, remove topshell, horse hair brush out dust, reverse vacuum blow + long hair brush dusting into corners, remove all keytabs (soak n wash), possibly remove HDD to get to CPU fan n sinks, put back everything
