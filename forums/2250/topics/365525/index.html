  • MyPillowTalks
    25 Jun 09, 15:10

    Actually 7 series graphics card are hard to get now

    Q6600 cannot be bought now, it is now replaced by the 45nm Q8200

    1k can get u a decent gaming system, but it will not be the fastest.

    You can learn how to config your system by looking at the pricelist

    Things that you should buy:

    Processor + Motherboard
    Rams
    Graphics Card
    Harddisk Drive
    Dvd drive
    Powersupply
    Casing

    You can get a E7400 with a P43 motherboard
    Ram just get value ram if you are not going for extreme performance
    GPU is the headache part, different gpu perform differently, and some requires you to buy a big powersupply

    HDD just get the normal 7200rpm ones if you are not those going for high performance

    dvd a normal one to suit ur reading/burning

    Powersupply must get a true power powersupplyfor gaming systems. One tiip is just to feel the weight of the powersupply. A true power 450w power supply will weigh must heavier than a normal standard rated poower 450w powesupply

    Casing is important if you are getting high end stuff, u need to make sure u have good cooling and ventilation
