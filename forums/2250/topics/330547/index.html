    1. To answer your question, no. Charger has no effect on a laptop's performance, unless your charger isn't charging your laptop. This will force the laptop to go into power saving mode and affect the performance. This behaviour is default in laptops unless you've explicitly tell it not to.

    Do note that even if you tell it not to, when battery levels go low, it will work in power saving mode as well. Reason is to protect itself. Battery that dies too many times will affect the hardware.

    2. What version of Firefox are you using? You don't have to re-install Firefox to keep your bookmarks. There's a simpler way.
