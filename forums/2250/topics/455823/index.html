    No you cant upgrade the GPU unfortunately, that's one of the cons for using a laptop for gaming. Few laptops have interchangable GPUs. Try dropping all the settings to the lowest. Better off with a desktop for gaming if you want decent graphics at a reasonable price and upgradabilty.
