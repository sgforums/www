  • kenn3th
    19 Jun 08, 18:58

    This is how your hdd looks like inside the casing.

    It is made up of DISCS. from the name hard disc ..

    there is a reader/writer needle or head which writes data and reads data.

    The screech like you said, is infact cds scratching each other, most prob with the writer head.

    These are signs of a failing hdd.

    I cannot tell you when it will fail, however, from what you have said : louder and louder.

    It means the end is closer and closer.

    My suggestion:  back up your data first. Save it maybe in an ext hdd/ back up dics (dvd). Do constant backups. You will not know when it would fail. (it could be as long as 2 years?)

    Or..

    It might be the fans. ( check properly).
