    Ah...

    Well, to a certain extent, the author is right.

    Depending on how your system handles folders and how mess up your registry is, you may or may not have a slow machine.

    The best will be not create too many folders inside My Documents. In fact, leave it alone will be best.

    Create a new folder outside My Documents, for example in C or D drive, then put all your files there.

    Remember to defragment your hard disk after moving those files, because moving a few GB worth of data will fragment your hard disk pretty badly.
