    YAY! AMD FTW :D

     

    GA-MA785GMT + Athlon II X4 620 $311 (630 costs $7 more BTW)

    Team Elite 1333MHz CL9 RAM 4GB kit - $162 ( I choose this cause Corbell > Any other distro)

    1TB Western Digital Caviar Green Power 64MB Cache - $135

    CM Elite 310 $69

    Vantec ION2 520W PSU - $79 (Personal recommendation)

    Total cost: $756

     

    Will it run GTA IV? No duh. It will run anything you can throw at it and the kitchen sink. Up to 1920x1080, anyway. 4850 or 5750? If you have the 4850, don't bother with any 5700 series cards. How do I know? I have a similar set-up (Similar, but not quite :P)

     

    Phenom II 955 + MSI 785G-E53

    4GB Team Xtreem 1600MHz CL7

    Powercolor 4850 PCS

    2 x 160GB Samsung AID 0, 1 x 160GB Seagate

    CM Elite 334

    Vantec ION2+ 500W

     

    I've thrown the kitchen sink, the refrigerator and a car at the computer. Will last you quite some time.
