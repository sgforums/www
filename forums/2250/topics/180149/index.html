    The following are short forms used in the digital world. If I leave anything out please help to add on.

    Lol - Laughing out loud
    Lmao - Laughing my ass out
    Rofl - Rolling on floor laughing
    Combined: Roflmfao - Rolling on floor laughing my farking ass out
    Noob - Mostly used in games, meaning you are a stupid and lousy player
    Newb (theres a difference between noob & newb) - New player
    IMO - In my opinion
    IMHO - In my humble opinion
    Gtg - Got to go
    Brb - Be right back
    Wth - What the hell
    Wtf - What the fark
    Tmd - Tamade
    Tmrw - Tomorrow
    Npnt - No picture no talk
    Flame - Scold or verbal abuse
    Omg - Oh my god
