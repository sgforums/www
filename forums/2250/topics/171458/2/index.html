    If you are using IE, you can clear those temporary files

    Go to Tools, Internet Options. Under the General tab, click Delete Cookies and Delete Files.

    If you are using Firefox, you can clear the cache.

    Go to Tools, Options. Under Privacy tab, select Clear Cache now. Alternatively, just press Ctrll+Shift+Del to clear private data. Everything will be cleared.
