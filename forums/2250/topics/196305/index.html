    In my opinion, your combined usage of Trend Micro and ZoneAlarm is a sure way of inviting BSODs. And usually, BSODs are pointers to memory violation or conflict errors, brought on by applications, or failing RAM modules or a combination of software and hardware faults.

    Note, Firewalls and antivirus apps suck up memory. Your use of both Trend Micro and ZoneAlarm just increase memory usage, and ZoneAlarm has got security issues. You should do a Google search on Zone Alarm security flaws - you will find juicy articles about it.
