    Here's a few I know of:

    1. Download Express

    2. Free Download Manager

    3. Star Download Manager

    4. Orbit Downloader

    5. WinWget - http://www.cybershade.us/winwget/index.html

    6. True Downloader - http://francis.dupont.free.fr/truedownloader/

    7. VisualWget - http://khomsan.ph.googlepages.com/visualwget.home

    8. wxDownload Fast - http://dfast.sourceforge.net/

    9. Fresh Download

    10. GetRight

    11. LeechGet

    Those with links, means that they are hard to get. Those without links, just Google. First few results are your answer.

    I like Download Express, but if you download too many files at one go, you slow the PC down to a crawl.

    Free Download Manager doesn't have this issue. Orbit Downloader only has a 10 thread limit. wxDownload Fast is my all time favourite, although it doesn't seem to be in development much.
