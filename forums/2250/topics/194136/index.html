    Originally posted by Marco_Simone:
    The seeder list is 2 while the leecher list reads 4,averege progress=47%.are there any implications of this?
    Seed means a person who has the full file(s) and sharing it online. Leech or peer means someone who has the incomplete file(s), downloading and sharing at the same time.

    Brief explanation. The more seeds the better. The more leechers/peers, the higher the number the seeds have to distribute to and this may lead to slower download, unless the leechers/peers are generous enough to have high upload (sharing) rate at the same time. If only leechers/peers are present, you can never acquire 100% of the file(s).

    I usually leave my computer on overnight to download. If your BT software can set listening port, set it to above 50000, any number. My upload rate is 8kbps max
