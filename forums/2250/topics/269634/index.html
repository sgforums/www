    Uninstall the driver. Reboot and let Windows reinstall it for you.

    1. Right click My Computer, select Properties
    2. Select the Hardware tab
    3. Click on Device Manager
    4. Under DVD/CD-ROM Drives, click on the + sign next to it to see a list of devices
    5. Right click on your DVD drive and select Uninstall

    Restart computer.
