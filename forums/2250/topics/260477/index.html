    Most computers have sound card installed for alot of users.
    Some of the DIY computers need to add/install the sound ourself.
    So here is a guide to those who doesn't have a sound card like me.
    For Window Xp

    1 - Click Start and go to Control Panel.

    2 - Click Add Hardware and Click Next. (It will search for your hardware)

    3 - Click Yes. (Have You Already Connected This Hardware To Your Computer?)

    4 - Click Audio Codecs. (Alt sound Click Vinyl AC'97 if you have one)



    5 - Click Next and Click Finish.

    Hope It Helps!
