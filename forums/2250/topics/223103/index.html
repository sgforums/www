    Ho seh... though I don't really know much about Acer PC, those sliders are for adjusting voltages. By adjusting with the power turn on, you are asking for trouble. Thank goodness only smoke came out, or you could see yourself in hospital.

    Reading your description, I can guarantee that at least your PSU is gone case, since the slider is on the PSU. There's also a chance that your whole CPU might be gone, so you need to purchase a brand new one. Luckily SITEX is until Sunday... Thanks for boosting Singapore's economy.

    If your warranty is not over, you have to bring over to Acer Service Centre at Jurong. Just lie to them that there is a power outrage and it becomes like that. And slide back the red slider to its original position if not you have to pay a great deal. If warranty is over, just bring it to your nearest PC shop. Depending on how severe the damage is, you have to pay anything from $30 to $300.
