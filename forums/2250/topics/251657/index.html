    I am not sure why you want to get a new motherboard with all the new features and yet do not want to make use of the very basic SATA connectivity. Mayit is because you still have a working ATA hard disk?

    Not to worry. If you just have an ooptical drive you can use an ATA hard disk together with it. The ribbon cable that fans out has 2 connection as generic to all IDE interfaces. One primary one secondary so you can connect the optical drive to be secondary as it reads from the primary ATA drive.
