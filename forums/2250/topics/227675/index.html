    Firefox:

    Tools > Options

    Click on Privacy at the top.

    Under History, tick the boxes that you want.

    1st box: Will remember all the URLs for a certain number of days
    2nd box: Works like IE auto complete.
    3rd box: Self-explanatory

    As for the cookies part, it's up to you.

    Under Private data, untick the Always clear my private data when I close Firefox box.

    Click OK.

    IE:

    Tools > Internet Options

    Click on the General tab.

    Under History, increase the number of days...

    Then click on the Advanced tab.

    Tick this box: Use inline auto complete

    Untick this box: Empty temporary internet files folder when browser is closed.

    Click OK.
