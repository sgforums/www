    Even if you don't use Nero most other programs may still jumble up the order. You have to understand how a basic computer reads and aranges data by default. With your naming convention of course you will get confused!

    Try putting zeroes in front of your tracks. The number of zeroes equals to the total number of tracks you going to burn. In this way the track queue will never go wrong.

    Like this:-

    01
    02
    03
    .
    .
    .
    09
    10
    11
    12
    .
    .
    19
    20
    ....and so on.

    If you have 100 tracks to burn, use three digits and put 2 zeroes in front. e.g:-

    001
    002
    .
    .
    100
    101
    ...and so on
