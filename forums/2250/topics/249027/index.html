    Start > All Programs > Accessories > Entertainment > Volume Control

    Optional step if you don't have the MIDI column

    Click on Options > Properties

    Under Show the following controls, tick this box: MIDI

    Click OK

    Now look at your MIDI column. Drag the slider bar all the way up.

    Make sure Wave and Play Control columns sliders are not right at the bottom.
