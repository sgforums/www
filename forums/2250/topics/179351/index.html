    Have you done any spyware/adware scan lately? High chance that you remove these spywares/adwares after the scan. The application probably didn't remove the remains of the spyware/adware, resulting in this error. As the spyware/adware is removed, it will not pose any serious problems to your computer, other than this irritating popup at startup.

    To remove the irritating popup at startup, go to Start, Run. Type in msconfig in the dialouge box. Under Startup tab, uncheck msnucti. Apply and restart computer.
