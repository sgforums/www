    STOMPer LeCorbeau came across these disturbing works of art made from bread on the internet. "They apparently still taste like normal bread," said the STOMPer.
     
    These gruesome bread sculptures are created by Thai artist Kittiwat Unarrom (whose family also runs a bakery). The "body parts" are sold at his family's bakery in Ratchaburi, Thailand.

    According to the STOMPer:

    "The bread sculptures are so unbelievably realistic! They really look like human heads.

    "Apart from heads, Unarrom also makes feet, hands and internal organs which come displayed impaled on hooks.

    "I can't help but have trouble believing that they are entirely made from dough, raisins, cashew nuts and chocolate.

    "It's all edible, but it isn't something that I'll call appetizing. The whole concept is kind of twisted and cannibal-ish.

    "I'll probably have trouble cutting up the bread, let alone keep it down.

    "But if I should ever visit Thailand, I'll definitely bring a couple of heads back to Singapore."
