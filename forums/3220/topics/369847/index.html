    “Can’t he afford to pay for parking in the car park? He didn’t give a damn about obstructing cars entering the carpark.

    “Look at the long queue of cars having to keep to one side just to get into the car park.

    “When he finally got back into the car about five minutes later, he revved his engine just to show off again before driving off.
