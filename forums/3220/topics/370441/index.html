    STOMPer LionCity was annoyed by a Singaporean family of six who were making so much noise and behaving like kids while on board a plane.

    "On a midnight flight from Hong Kong to Singapore on July 14 2009, they were restless from take off in Hongkong to landing in Singapore Changi Airport.

    "They were yakking and jumping non-stop, disregarding other tired travellers who were sleeping and resting on this midnight flight.
