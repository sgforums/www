    What's wrong with our kids Part 1: Breaks into school to steal 'stop' sign

    After breaking into their school, these secondary school students stole a ‘stop’ sign from the carpark and even had the cheek to proudly pose with it and post the pictures onto their blog.

    In the blog, the boys said they had nothing better to do and so decided to climb into their school at night and go for an adventure. They boasted about entering their school illegally and being smart enough to evade all the cameras. When they were about to leave , they thought that it would be fun to bring back a souvenir.

    In an email to STOMP today (Nov 17), the STOMPer who came across their blog says:

    “A few secondary school students, apparently from Dunearn Secondary School, entered their school illegally, and stole the ‘stop’ sign from the school’s carpark.

    “They even took it to a bus stop a few blocks away from the school.

    “Proud of their actions, they took photos of their act and posted them onto their blog.

    “Does the school know anything about this at all? Hopefully the school will do something about this behaviour and uphold the dignity of the school.”
