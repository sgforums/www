    Ghosts of British troops killed in Alexandra Hospital appear during 7th Month?

    A father waiting for his son's dental appointment at the carpark of Alexandra Hospital captured this photo of a window. It appears to contain ghostly images, seemingly of British troopers killed there during WW2.

    He took the photo after observing a lamppost near the window blinking continuously with a greenish light.

    The father, David, reported the incident to the Singapore Paranormal Investigators (SPI), who, through close analysis, discovered that the photo might contain images of two British soldiers who were apparently killed by Japanese troops there.

    The bloody incident occurred on Feb 14 1942, and was later called the Alexandra Hospital Massacre.

    On that day, Japanese soldiers had approached the hospital and killed a lieutenant bearing a white flag with a bayonet thrust.

    A number of patients and staff were also killed. The next day, about 200 male staff members and patients bound the previous day were ordered to walk 400 metres to an industrial area. Anyone who fell along the way was bayoneted by the soldiers.

    They were forced into very small rooms without water and some died as a result. Many were bayoneted the next day, and in all only five survived the ordeal.

    SPI also discovered that their informant, David, had a special 'gift'.

    David told them:

    "To be honest, my sixth-sense is very strong.

    "Even before I saw the lamp post blink, I'd already sensed that someone was watching me at that particular window, and that urged me to take the photo.

    "I've practiced 'mao shan shu' since I was young and my son also has the 'third-eye'.

    "That was about 25 years ago, when I joined a Taoist Mao Shan sect.

    "My master was very reluctant initially but gave in at last as he couldn't go against the heaven's will. He passed away peacefully exactly 108 days after accepting me as his last disciple as he had predicted himself earlier.

    "From that point on, I practised my special power and was actively involved in temple activities. Exorcism and spiritual healing are what I offered in charity to the needy."
