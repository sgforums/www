    Bus stop seat for kindergarten kids, or very short people

    A STOMPer came across this bus stop in Marsiling with impractical and low bus stop seats that are perhaps only meant for kindergarten kids or short people to rest on.

    Long Legs told STOMP via email today (Sep 14):

    "This picture was taken at a bus stop at Marsiling Lane.

    "The seats are very low and they are suitable only for kindergarten kids.

    "If adults are to sit down here, then they will have a hard time getting up if the bus is arriving.

    "Perhaps this is the only bus stop in Singapore with such low seats and small wonder many adults avoid sitting on them.

    "I hope SMRT and SBS Transit will phase out such seats or alternatively have a high bench and a low bench."
