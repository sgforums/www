    Rubbish truck hits tree, lamp post, then crashes into Pasir Ris block

    http://singaporeseen.stomp.com.sg/singaporeseen/viewContent.jsp?id=64554


    STOMPer Jim saw this rubbish truck crashing into Block 146 Pasir Ris Street 11, after hitting a tree and a lamp post at about 8am today (Mar 31).

    He said:

    "As usual, this rubbish truck came about 8am to go on it rounds to collect rubbish from Block 146 Pasir Ris Street 11.

    "Suddenly it over ran and hit a tree, lamp post and a pillar of Block 146."

    According to the STOMPer, no one was injured and the driver of the truck managed to escape unhurt.

    He said a traffic police officer and two petrol cars were arrived at the scene later.

    STOMP is contacting the Police for more information.
