    Act of disgrace: Errant driver parks at sheltered walkway for pedestrians
    http://singaporeseen.stomp.com.sg/singaporeseen/viewContent.jsp?id=80686
     
    STOMPer Joyce was in disbelief when she spotted this car parked at the pedestrian's sheltered walkway at block 32, Marine Crescent.

    Says this STOMPer: 

    "Just want to share how disagraceful people can get - look at this car owner who had the audacity to park her car at sheltered walkway of block 32, Marine Crescent! 

    "I am in disbelief and to think that this car owner could be my neighbour!! 

    "Last Friday, I came back at about 4.50pm in a cab with my niece, Michelle, only to find a car parked at the end of the walkway at Block 32 Marine Crescent where I live. 

    "I just cannot imagine the audacity and the extent of irresponsibility of a driver. 

    "I then took the first 2 pictures and waited to see what happened (Sigh, my camera didn’t do quite a good job though).

    "As I had to take my little niece home, I didn’t stay to watch, but whilst walking away from the block, I saw a man walking to the car with a trolley followed by a women in the driver's seat. The man then helped to guide the lady out of the walkway and left. 

    "I intend to send these pictures to LTA and to my Town Council and I sincerely hope they will take serious action against this particular car owner. Such acts should be dealt with seriously."
