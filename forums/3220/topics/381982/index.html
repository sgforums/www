    Selfish old man 'chopes' parking lot by standing in front of it

    This old man selfishly tried to reserve this parking lot in Boon Keng by standing in front of it, even though there was a long queue of motorists waiting for parking lots.

    STOMPer Kachin75, who witnessed the incident, said:

    "I was looking for a parking lot near the Boon Tong Kee chicken rice stall in Boon Keng and saw this old man selfishly blocking the vacant parking lot by standing right in front of it.

    "He wanted to reserve the lot for someone else even when there was a long queue of vehicles behind me all searching for parking lots.

    "In the end, he walked away pretending nothing has happened when he decided he no longer needed the lot for his friends or family. What a selfish man!"
