    Unremorseful China national causes 25-min jam by parking lorry in middle of road

    http://singaporeseen.stomp.com.sg/singaporeseen/viewContent.jsp?id=65854


    A Chinese national parked this lorry right in the middle of the road beside Tampines 1, halting traffic all the way to the road junction. When he finally returned from making his deliveries, he did not seem the least bit repentant for his actions.
     
    Here's what an upset STOMPer had to say of the incident:
     
    "This incident happened yesterday (Apr 9).
     
    "This delivery lorry was parked for 25 minutes in the middle of the road beside Tampines 1, the new mall.
     
    "The driver (the tall man in blue in the pictures) had left his vehicle there with the engine shut off, oblivious to the jam created behind him.
     
    "He had gone to make his deliveries, and was away from 3:45 to 4:10 pm.
     
    "His lorry blocked the entire road and all other vehicles were stuck behind him.
     
    "Everyone was busy looking around for the driver the entire time.
     
    "After 25 minutes, the driver, a Chinese national, appeared and he wasn't the least remorseful for causing the jam behind his vehicle.
     
    "I could tell from the way he spoke he was from China.
     
    "By that time, a huge crowd had gathered and the security guard has been alerted and told him to pull to the side to take down his particulars.
     
    "To make matters worse, the number plate at the front of the truck is broken with some letters missing.
     
    "I wonder if the police should give this driver a hefty fine for blatantly obstructing the traffic.
