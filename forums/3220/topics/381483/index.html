    Strange uncle sings and waves hands around on train

    At first, STOMPer Don thought this man was talking to his friends, but upon a closer look, he realised that the man was singing and dancing.

    In an email to STOMP today (Nov 8), the STOMPer says:

    “This incident took place on Nov 6 when I was on board a train heading towards Marina Bay.

    “At first I thought he was talking to his friends, but his actions became more and more weird and I became curious and started walking towards him.

    “To my surprise, he was dancing and singing.

    “People were staring at him and I don’t know why he was doing that. It was so embarrassing for him to do so.

    “I was laughing all the while because it was really funny.”
