    It must have been a bad day for the trash collector, says the STOMPer who spotted this heap of rubbish, which had fallen out of the trash compactor and onto the road.

    STOMPer Thomas came across the pile of fallen garbage while travelling along Ang Mo Kio Avenue 1 today (February 3).

    He says:

    “Here's what a start of a bad day looks like. Something must have gone wrong when that entire dump from inside the trash compactor was expelled out onto the pavement.

    “Pictures were captured at around 9.30am on February 3 2009 along Ang Mo Kio Avenue 1 towards the direction of Boundary Road.

    “Unfortunately, my camera lens made the pictures look smudgy but fortunately there was no casualty so I guess it was a good laugh.”
