    Danger of deadly stampede due to this big human jam @ Tanjong Pagar MRT station
    http://singaporeseen.stomp.com.sg/singaporeseen/viewContent.jsp?id=76785
    STOMPer Growiser found himself stuck in a massive human jam at Tanjong Pagar MRT station this morning due to the heavy rain. He wonders why no announcements were made to inform commuters so they could have used other exits.

    He reports:

    "I took these photos in Tanjong Pagar MRT station this morning at 8.44am.

    "Because of the heavy rain, commuters refused to get out of the station, resulting in a human jam.

    "SMRT could have announced over the PA system that there is massive congestion at Peck Seah Street exit and at least commuters can consider using other exits at the station.

    "While I was struck there for more than five minutes, there was no announcement at all.

    "Things could turn very ugly if for example, Mas Selamat's friends had struck with a grenade. 

    "Maybe a smoke grenade or thunderflash is sufficient to create fear, confusion and stampede."
