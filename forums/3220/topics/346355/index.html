    Car and police car crash in Yishun with such force one ends up on a grass patch

    A car and a police car collided at Yishun Ring Road. Such was the force of the impact that the private car's momentum carried it another 10 metres onto a grass patch.
     
    STOMPer Singelringen describes what he saw:
     
    "This accident occurred at the junction of Yishun Ring Road and Yishun Street 72 today (Feb 2) at about 3:00 pm.
     
    "The private car did not stop after the crash and continued to travel another 10 metres until it reached the neighbourhood park grass turf."
     
    The Police have confirmed the accident and are currently investigating the matter. No one was injured in the incident.
