    Foul-mouthed S'pore blogger slams decision not to extend school hols due to H1N1

     http://singaporeseen.stomp.com.sg/singaporeseen/viewContent.jsp?id=75973

    A Singaporean blogger slams Singapore's decision in not extending the school holidays due to the H1N1 virus, even congratulating the country for having three locally-infected clusters of the virus.

    A STOMPer, who came across his blog, said:

    "I came across a Singaporean blogger who wrote in his blog about how Singapore made the wrong decision in not extending the school holidays further.

    "He even had the audacity to congratulate us with three locally-infected clusters of H1N1.

    "He even quoted he was happy about it."

    The blogger wrote in his blog:

    "Congratulations to Singapore, with 3 locally-infected clusters of H1N1. Mad happy? Yes.

    "Soon, we'll all be captivated in our own homes and that will be thanks to Singapore's ingenious plan...Like seriously lor, why can't they make a right decision? Dammit, dammit, dammit. With already 142 cases, I think that we're going to reach 300 in no time. Bat an eyelid and we'll already be there. It's just a matter of time."

    The STOMPer added:

    "With the H1N1 flu affecting our daily lives, why isn't this blogger trying to help play a part in this situation instead of cursing us behind his computer screen?

    "Is it right to say that Singapore made the wrong decision?

    "How would people from other countries look at us?

    "At this crucial moment, instead of yakking away behind the computer, shouldn't we play our part to help improve the situation and work together in the society?"
