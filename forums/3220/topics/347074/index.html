    STOMPer Awyong86 was awakened from his sleep at about 4.50am today (Feb 8) by an accident in Queensway involving a Jaguar and a Nissan Latio.

    He said:

    "When I was sleeping, I heard the sound of an emergency brake and crashing!

    "So I woke up to see what happened!

    "When I got to the road, I could see two cars -- a Nissan Latio and a Jaguar in the middle of the street."

    According to him, the driver of the Jaguar was a woman in her 20s.

    No one was injured in th accident, said the STOMPer.

    STOMP is contacting the Police for more information.

    "One of the passengers in the car, a Chinese woman in in her 20s, complained of dizziness and body pain.

    "She was conveyed conscious to Alexandra Hospital

    "The driver of the car which went over to the other side, a Chinese woman in her late teens, was arrested for drink driving.
