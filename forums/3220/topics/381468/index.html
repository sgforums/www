    Teacher blogs about students' compositions and laughs at them

    This blog entry containing summaries of students' compositions belongs to a secondary school teacher, who is extremely insensitive, says STOMPer Hipper. 

    In the entry, the blogger talks about some compositions she came across in class. Not only does she point out their weaknesses, she also jokes about them. 

    In an email, an upset STOMPer Hipper says: 

    "I would like to complaint about a teacher who, I felt, has breached the law of MOE." 

    "I was blog-hopping and came across this blog entry dated July 16 2009, which had pointed out works of secondary three students. 

    "I felt that should the students happen to come across the entry, they would feel extremely demoralised being publicly humiliated by the actions of the teacher. 

    "Her tagboard too had rude comments about the government. It's sad to know she is paid by the government but only to think negatively about them. 

    "I hope action can be taken against teachers like her. In their position, they should be professional about it and correct their students instead of making the students weaknesses a joke for her blog readers."
