    Imagine being muffled, left in a small, cramped space in the hot, hot sun. That was the plight faced by at least four dogs being transported aboard this SAF vehicle.
     
    The STOMPer who came across this sight was appalled by this.

    In an email to STOMP, he said:

    "I was driving behind this SAF vehicle heading to Exxon Mobil along Jalan Buroh.

    "I spotted at least four dogs muffled and which were standing in the cramped, stacked cages while being transported in the hot afternoon.

    "As an ex-SAF I understand it can be very stuffy and shaky sitting behind in these SAF trucks.

    "I don't understand why the dogs need to be muffled and cramped in tiny cages as this does not allow them to stick their tongues to cool themselves.

    "I hope the welfare of this guard dogs is properly handled and they are transported safely."
     
    STOMP is contacting the SPCA for a comment.
