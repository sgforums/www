    Foreign workers help push overturned car over in carpark

    In a display of collective effort, foreign workers and kind motorists at this shopping centre carpark helped push a car which had been overturned in an unfortunate accident.

    A STOMPer, who came across this video online, was amazed by the efforts of the helpful passers-by who managed to push the car upright.

    According to the person who uploaded the video, this happened at a local carpark and many foreign workers came over to help.

    In an email dated November 15, the amused STOMPer says:

    "I saw this video and it seemed very interesting.

    "More than 10 people helped turn the overturned car at a carpark."
