    STOMPer RL and her group of friends were at the coffeshop at Ang Mo Kio Ave 6 Block 730 for supper and were told off by the owner of the ‘Zhi Cha’ stall that they were not allowed to join tables and sit together.

    The STOMPer and her friends were vexed and tried to reason with him, but failed. They were shocked as they had not heard of this ‘new regulation’ before.

    “On Friday (May 15), at about 11.00pm, a group of friends and myself decided to have a gathering and supper at a nearby coffeeshop (Ang Mo Kio Ave 6 Block 730- just next to Courts) .

    “As there were quite a number of us, we decided to join our tables.

    “In the midst, we were stopped by a man (whom we believe to be the owner of the ‘Zhi Cha’ stall-Da Ji Bao).

    “We kindly questioned him on the reason but he returned rudely to us that we were not allowed to connect tables in a coffeeshop!

    “We were shocked to learn about such a ‘new regulation’! We reasoned with him politely that we heard of no such ridiculous rule before and realistically, we were there to bring him business.

    “However, he was still stubborn and insisted that we sit separately. 

    “To our horror, he took out a camera and tried to take shots of us! We warned him if he proceeded with such actions, we will do likewise and have it posted online. He went ahead anyway and so did we! 

    “We suggest that the stall owner should have all the tables screwed down to the ground (like some other coffeeshops) if he is so unhappy about customers shifting the tables.

    “Honestly, we have had gatherings in multiple coffeeshops before.
     
    “In addition, some of us have been living in the AMK neighbourhood all our lives but this is the first time we have ever heard of such an atrocious, outrageous, ridiculous and most unacceptable "regulation" and behaviour from the owner!”
