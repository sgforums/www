    Most important of all when you are looking to join a network marketing company, you got to take a look at the 4Ps: -

    16 Nov 04, 10:23
    hi the den,

    there are too many black sheeps in the MLM industry and so be very careful...

    The things to look out for are :

    1) What is the cost to join? It MUST NOT be more than the cost of a starter kit to start your business AND do not need to buy any products in order to join.
    Some perfume MLM costs like $1000 (they claim to be register fee + some products for you to try) to join, so you should avoid that...

    2) what is the cost to maintain? NuSkin, Bel air i heard need at leat 1000 points to maintain (which is about = to $1000 or so)

    3) the MLM companies should have written in BLACK and WHITE for refund of unused products. Many MLM companies claim to to able to return if you not happy but when u want to return, they find all kind of excuses. So if it is written in BLACK and WHITE, then at least you have a case if they do not honour their claim.

    4) Look at their rewards plan. It is good?
    a) If it is too good and fast, then something may be wrong...
    b) can you overtake your upline if you do more sales than him/her? There are some plan that will not allow you to overtake NO MATTER how much sales you do. This is unfair.

    5) Their products.... It is really that good for that price?
    Also, the product must be bought on a regualary basis such as monthly thing. Why? so that you can keep earning points... for perfume MLM, it cost easily $200 or so, then how often do you need to buy the oil or lamp? It is afforable for most people? If people cant afford, you can earn points!

    6) lastly, talk to as many MLM people as possible b4 you join. Compare their rewards plans and decide for yourself.

    Hope all this infor will help you and do take your time ok?
