Are these MLM Techniques Familiar?

  • bahama
    29 Dec 06, 21:09
    If you join a network marketing business today, are these some of the techniques you are taught to do?

    -Hear 10 no's before you Get a Yes.
    -Consolidate the contact details of 100 friends and call them 1 by 1 and bring them to your office.
    -Buy Leads and Cold Call.
    -Go to Conventions where there are alot of people and sell your business.

    I personally find such techniques difficult and I say no way will I do them. Here's why.

    I don't do much explaining. I offer something free to get people to give me their name and email address. Something like a free report or a free eBook that explains how to make money online. Then I follow up with an autoresponder that is already pre-programmed with a series of message that offer good reasons to get into network marketing, and show them how easy it to get prospects to sign up using a marketing system.

    That's all, no selling, no cold calling, no asking for referrals, or calling people for dinners and coffees and paying the bill. Unfortunately, from the seminars I have attended, MLM companies still preach the typical "ask your contacts to go down to the office and show the plan" strategy.

    That's dinosaur marketing, unfortunately its still not extinct. So it is for that reason that I say the typical approach is a dead way to do network marketing recruiting. People go online to do research. You may as well let them use a system thats easily duplicated and let them get signups using the internet and an auto responder.
