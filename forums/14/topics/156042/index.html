  • OH-FF
    6 Oct 05, 17:21
    The products and its sales should be the main core business.

    The distributors are also customers themselves because they'd signed up to purchase the goods at distributor prices.

    There is a difference between active distributor who does active sales(Salesman) and a passive distributor who waits to be served. (Customers)

    Passive distributors are just members who are only customers, and they only introduced a couple of friends to be members to use the products at distributor prices. The one who serves the products to them are the active distributor , usually their Upline.

    So the $$$ is made by the salesman providing active service , this way, he can get the accumulated points/commission of sales by customers who choosed to be served. Thus experience in Sales and Service and keeping your customers plays a vital role in MLM.

    Do not expect your downlines to stock up so that you can accumulate points, get them to learn the sales and service. Only people who had put in Effort gets the points .

    Sitters who wait to be served are customers.
