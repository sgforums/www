    The following pointers are just some basic briefing of which everybody should kown so as to not be tricked into similar schemes, if there's any questions from anyone, just PM me.

    Point No 2 -Pyramid

    Often their usual practise will be to encourage people to sign up for the Promotion Package on the sport Rather Than letting the clients/prospect take the Information of the Offer to go back and discuss + decide at their own pace.

    Offers are always being given to only those who sign on the spot, and a lot of emphasis on what will happen and what will they Lose out if they didn't purchase the Promotion on the spot.

    Often those who function in Pyramid structure will not attempt to contact the person who's not interested after 1st time of contact and knowing the person aren't interested, the agent will than divert their attention to get more Victims/Clients (if the agents they themselves don't know their in a pyramid scheme)

    MLM - Often the agents of various MLM companies will be patient and sincere to share the promotion with their friends and clients, if they don't want, they will add on to say - 'i'll update you if there's any new promotion'

    Of course, the pyramid scheme agents will also update the clients, if they want to cheat their money, but at such circumstances, you have to observed how they carry it (the idea & message) across to you, if they allow you to take time to analysis it on yourself [for detailed version, those who are interested,we can meet face to face to discuss more]

    Point No 3 - Pyramid

    Often when the Pyramid/Money Game's Agents bring the idea across to the prospect/victim, they will emphasis on I) MONEY $_$ & II)Opportunity to Earn FAST BIG BUCKS WITHOUT DOING ANYTHING

    Often products are not being introduced to the prospect, if there is, very often products that comes with the compensation scheme will be of Little or NO VALUE such as Paper (contract) and maybe some cheap items like glass bottles or small gifts that can be found easily in the market.

    Products are used in the event of those experienced Pyramid Scheme Leaders, and if they don't offer any products, but THEY MAKE IT A POINT TO DISPLAY PRODUCTS IN THEIR SHOWROOM to give clients a false impression that they have those products.

    But very often during the whole discussion and sharing, they the agents of the pyramid scheme will not touch on any product that they have in the show room.

    They will just emphasis on how safe the investments are with them, and focus on a PERSON (Superstar) who has earned that amount of money per month.
    NOTE: They will not place the attention on other agents/clients who earned money, as they are not earning as much as the SUPER-STAR who is the pioneer in the Money Game.

    MLM - Often only those RESPONSIBLE AGENTS will make it an attempt to share with the clients how the products can help the prospects and how they can share the products with their friends and family memebers to maybe just earn an SMALL Amount of Refferal Commission. [this is to let clients have residual icome just be introducing products to their friends] and the agent will help them plan and place the products in a way to enable the clients higher refferal pay-out.

    Point No 4 - Pyramid

    Every Single Pyramid scheme will enforce the clients/prospects to JOIN THE SCHEME INORDER TO TAKE PART!!

    For those people who knows that it's a Pyramid Scheme, but still join just to make quick bucks (often will give their friends a reply once the scheme is declared by NEWS it's a Pyramid/ILLEGAL SCHEME by saying - "Hey, I'm also a VICTIM HERE" [ but deep down, they already pocketd the commission that they earn, and disappear elsewhere already.

    MLM - Customers usually can buy the products from the agents, and the agents are more than happy to help their friends (clients) to buy and deliever to them without them signing up with the company, all they do is to buy the product that their friends want and have the Cash On Delivery system.

    Point No 5 - Pyramid

    Usually while in the sharing of the Pyramid/Money Game Scheme, the focus of the whole ideas and information shared with the VICTIM/Prospect will focus on TWO FACTORS; 1-FAST EASY NO RISK $_$; 2-YOU DON'T NEED TO DO ANYTHING, JUST 'INVEST $$ SIT AND WAIT TO COLLECT R.O.I/DIVIDENDS'

    Sounds FIRMILAR perhaps?

    MLM - The Agents will be able to promise them a certain percentage of commission pay-out and the pay-out is not very high, as it's the rebate that they get from purchasing the products. (Like NTUC/POPULAR Membership Card - You buy an item, show your Membership Card to the cashier, you get 10% discount [for popular ; products with Net Price are not included]

    But for PYRAMID , they can tell you that you BUY this Item/Promotional Package, you buy 1, we give you 10 if not more when you made a purchase. IS it logical? - You buy 1 item and get 10, such a way to run Business = Sure Collaspe in No time. Take a Look at the Banks, if they can't offer High Interest Rates, than it's logical, as they are not playing our money with HIGH RISK STOCKS which can have HIGH RETURNS BUT ONLY WHEN CLIENTS CAN AFFORD TO LOSE, WHICH IS WHY banks CAN'T GIVE HIGH RETURNS. (I have a article on this playing of stocks, next time than show it to you all )

    Point No 6- Pyramid

    They usually last only for a short shelf life of 2-3yrs only.
    Only those people who join at the very PIONEER will be able to earn money (not just money, but higher returns than anybody else who joins later - Early Bird Programme - To MAKE USE OF THE GREED FACTOR what everyone of US have inside) [Details on why I say so, we'll Discuss FACE TO FACE as it's a bit advance thus I don't want to complicate those newbies who don't know about identifying of Pyramid Schemes.]

    MLM - Those who join later, can overtake those who join earlier if they work harder. Simple as that.
