Lets be realistic, i believe any mlm company can do one... is just whether the system suits u or not, most important is to ask yourself 3 questions:

    1. Do you really need to join an mlm company? Can it benefit you somehow?
    2. Can the mlm company create passive or residual income for you?
    3. Can the mlm company brings u what you really one? Can it bring u there?

    Some advice on joining an mlm company.
    1. Remember that repeat sales is the key to success, not recruitment. Prts must be able to repeat sales.

    2. Is the compensation plan fair to everyone, that means the lowest guy can earn and promote higher than other pple on top of him.

    3. The product must be good and the system must be workable.

    4. Training and education is a must to make sure u succeed in the company.

    5. Once joined, don't give up, keep moving whether fast or slow, as long as u go through the training, education and work on it, you will progress ....

    Some things to ask yourself before joining.

    1. Can u afford the time?
    2. Do u believe in the mlm company?
    3. Your attitude counts a lot to succeed.
    4. Are u willing to learn and progress with this mlm company?
    5. Life just live once. to be or not to be.

    So lets be realistic, if you cannot do those things to join an mlm company, then don't join, back to your old self. For me, one income is certainly not enough for cashflow, try to get more income not just mlm!
