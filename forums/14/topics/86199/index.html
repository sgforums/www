    Are you familiar with the Amazon and McDonald's business model? Where they grow using a force of 100% commissioned entrepreneurs? Instead of using sales employees that must be paid salaries each month?

    Network marketing offers the same business model- growth through the use of commissioned entrepreneurs who help expand the enterprise

    If you are someone who can distinguish between 1) working 70 hours a week, and getting a pay check in return, even if it is big, and 2) working the same amount of time, but spending that time to set up a regular customer base that can throw off the same kind of income without the 70 hours a week, then you will understand that MLM or network marketing is a real business.
