So when ppl come and tell you to join, telling that you can earn money fast and easy, you just stop and think, why? why he ask you to join if it's easy money that he can earn it? Why does he need you?? Why dont he earn the money himself.

    The ugly human nature is, we make use of each other. Is someone making use of you?? When i look for you and ask you to join, there must be some reason behind, why i need to ask you to join? Because i need to make use of you, i want you to join, so that i earn more. If i really have something that can sure earn fast and easy money, i would earn it myself, i would not look for you at all.
