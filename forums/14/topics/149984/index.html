    Hi, this is my first ever attempt in an online discussion. I'm a full time networker & I found this forum by accident because I was searching the Internet to know how our society is perceiving MLM after it had been legalised for 5 years.

    From the discussion in the forum, I found some meaningful feedback but there are also too much bickering. However, I would still like to share my experience to all who are interested in this business model.

    Before we decide on what company, product or COM plan to market, I think we've to understand the MLM concept more. MLM is not about selling because I've met a top sales person who join the company for 1 day, close a total of 8 sales with 8 new distributors in that first day but disappeared in a month. (This person is only 1 of top sales people I met in the industry). MLM is not about recruiting 10, 100, 1000 .... downlines into your organisation in order to be successful.

    MLM is about sharing products that the distributor have used and at the sametime showing the business plan to the prospect. To win in this business, the distributor must be able to duplicate all he had learnt from his team to his downlines. Selling (sharing & making the sales) & recruiting is relatively easy if the products are of value to the people. However, the most time consuming and most important task is to Duplicate. From my uplines and senior experience, it will normally take a person 3 to 5 years to duplicate. In short, it takes as much effort for a person to be successful in MLM as in other businesses. This is the reason why there is high dropout in any MLM company, a true blue MLM company can never have overnight success story.

    Back to selecting a MLM company, my suggestion is to first ask yourself why do you want to do the business at all? The reason being that if you think it is easy (because of low investment) then you will disappoint yourself for the time and money invested. Most of all your reputation will tarnish among those friends that you have sponsored and spoken to.

    If you think you have a good and compelling reason for you to succeed in this industry, I have a few suggestion in the selection of a company:

    1) Company: at least 7 years of MLM experience, no. of countries that it had penetrated, track record in handling disputes in these countries, preferred public listed to verify their earning and payout, and their vision.

    2) Product: There are 1/3 of the successful MLMers whom started the business because they love their product so much that they can share it with great passion. Preferred product that are highly consumables for repeat sales, affortable (very subjective, a product can be expensive but valuable to certain group of consumer), user friendly to people of all ages, result from usage can be quantify so comsumer can easily refer others to you, R&D must be strong with top scientist and not some university fresh grads, multiple Patents and multiple product types and range to cater to all daily needs.

    3) COM Plan: There are front heavy plan such as binary or back heavy plan such as stair-step breakaway. 2 other plans are unilevel and matrix, many companies may say that they have a revolutionalise plan but mostly they are just a variant or hybrid of the 4 basic plans. If you are looking for suplementary income, binary plan is suitable because you can earn little profit right from the start. However, if you dream of becoming millionaires in MLM, go for stair-step breakaway. 76% of the world top MLM company uses stair-step breakaway plan.

    4) Team: Do the team you join has the right training system to guide you? how many successful leaders are there in the team as compared to other teams? what are the training material available and are they reasonably price? What is the vision of the team?

    5) Leader: who did you join? can he help you or when he is not around who can support you? is he only interested to train you to be his sales rep or he want to invest time on you to make you a leader?

    Sorry for the long message, hope it helps. Thanks
