  • Eikei
    14 Jun 06, 21:27
    Nobody other than the police in consultation with the Attorney General can decide whether a scheme is indeed a fraud. It is unfair to MLMs to label them as frauds in general but certainly, recruiting MLMs which have little or no product movement is definitely fraud. A MLM that does not show the minimum, maximum, mean, median and average income of its distributors violate MLM law. It all boils down to a case by case basis and that website is a good indicator to help you decide if the particular MLM you are interested in is potentially a fraud. Another website is http://www.mlm-thetruth.com/.
