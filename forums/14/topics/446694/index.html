    Networking is very important. The more people you know, it's easier for you to get recommendation and help when the time comes.  Of course you must be very good at maintaining relationship with the people. I would say people skills is very important. No matter what jobs we're holding now (e.g. me tutor), most of my jobs came from people's recommendation. I would still keep contacts and try to link up with them as and when.

    People skills is like how you treat other people your colleagues /work partners. Treat them well and they will return the favour. Remember their birthday, send them greeting cards etc. It'll all help to keep that contact. Treating customers/clients like your friends, it's one of the very good way to start networking.
