    After seeing many posts and topics, i realise one very common point...

    many people seem to join a particular company by comparing the marketing plan and the ease of how easy it is to make money from it at many times is the geez of the topic.

    Personally, i feel that the bottom line should be that everyone should ask himself this question: If you are not doing the business, will YOU yourself still keep on using the product?

    If yes, i feel that the sincerity to share the opportunity is more credible. This is due to the fact that he/she is sharing the chance for another person to use a product that will benefit him/her first and as a result, gain monetarily as the reward. (Like what Brian Tracy call this business, WORD OF MOUTH business)

    If No, then i believe the stability of the organization is at risk. As the whole group is built on riding on the marketing plan, anyone will simply lose his whole group when another plan that pays better comes onto the market.

    Why not just choose a company with a product or services you will always use, and the company pays reasonably and punctually. There will be no pressure on anyone then.. right? The problem is that many networkers work too hard to convince people, and not to simply expose/educate people about the opportunity .
