    One of the common misunderstandings revolving around Binary Plan is that it's a money game, get rich quick scheme that should be avoided at all costs, by any networkers. It propels the upline to focus on helping the weaker side.

I've heard of many 'reputable' companies asking new distributors to pump in thousands of dollars for the inital investment... *faint*
