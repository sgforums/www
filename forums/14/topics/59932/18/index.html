    Hi, I really think you have mistaken.

    All consumption products DO NOT need to be approved or even issue certification to be on sales. For electrical appliances, it is a must to be sent for safety testing. As for drug, cosmectis, food, dried food,etc,etc, there are no law that it is a must to be sent for testing. There are millions of products in local market and new products up to thousands are in the market monthly.
    The only law that stated for consumption is to state the ingredients. Just like Slim 10, is it alright to sell, however it's the responsible for the dealers if anything goes wrong. As for consumers, it is your duty that you are aware of what you are consuming/ using.

    What HSA does is to do QC for products without the dealers/ agents knowing. They will do testing when many complains on a particular product are received or when they suspect that there are banned substances in the products.

    HSA will base on the current list of banned substances. Yet if the products were tested before and then the contents changes or HSA has newly banned one of ingredients in the products, it is not requested to submit for testing again unless the products got complained or suspected of banned substances.

    SPRING Singapore do not only test electrical appliances. They represent Singapore government to do testing on anything to check the effectiveness, safety, percentage, environmental friendly of contents. This is to ensure that all products tested are under international standard. Spring test is one of the most recognised in the whole world for its extremely strict, reliable and high standard testing, no undertable problems.

    As long as a company has Spring lab test reports, it's widely recognised. However many and really many products do not dare to be sent for testing. In addition,many companies will state that due to the small scale of the company, they do not have the money to do testing. If a good company no matter how big or small scale, they will send for SPRING testings no matter how much they have to pay as they do not exchange money and conscious to consumer's health, safety and life.
