    Hi everyone! I heard so much about sgForum from my friends so I decided to check it out yesterday. Upon scrolling down all the top forums I came across 'Multi Level Marketing' so here I am.

    Firstly, I want to make it clear that I have never been a distributor of any MLM companies BUT I have worked with them. I used to work as a Product Trainer for a MLM company for many years.

    The word 'MLM' has always been associated with 'Pyramid Selling' and 'Ponzi Schemes' ever since the days of Gemini Chit Fund. If I am not wrong, the general public's opinion is that ANY kind of money-making venture that requires one to put down some capital outlay a.k.a. investments with promises of high returns, is a scam. The GCF drew a lot of flake from the public who demanded that the government bodies DO something to prevent innocent people from being cheated of their live savings. The government did. The Pyramid Selling & MLM Act was enforced.

    http://statutes.agc.gov.sg/non_version/cgi-bin/cgi_retrieve.pl?actno=REVED-190&doctitle=MULTI%2dLEVEL%20MARKETING%20AND%20PYRAMID%20SELLING%20%28PROHIBITION%29%20ACT 0a&date=latest&method=part

    However, in view of the marked differences in MLM and Pyramid Selling, the Singapore government lifted the ban on MLM in Year 2002.

    http://app.mti.gov.sg/default.asp?id=81

    MLM companies from all over the world flood the market.

    However, to operate a MLM company in Singapore, there is an organization that is working very closely with CASE (Consumer Association of Singapore) and MTI (Ministry of Trade & Industry) in weeding out undesirable 'MLM' companies.

    Basically, for anyone who is interested in joining a MLM company as a distributor, he/she should know his/her rights.

    As the common phobia is of 'being taken for a ride' by MLM companies, this link would be good for you. Please note Clause No. 5.

    http://www.dsas.org.sg/dsas/Html/afterlogin.htmI have a tendency to write very long articles so please forgive me.
