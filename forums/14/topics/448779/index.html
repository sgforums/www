    1st - Look at the history of the company. Do you want to enter into a newly company or a company with many years of success and still going strong? No one can argue over success.

    2nd - The compensation plan. Which is also the most important in all MLM Companies. Do you want to work hard for a company with a payout of only a little % or do you want to take advantage of a long establishment company that have the highest payout?

    3rd - Look at where the company is heading. Are they only heading locally only or globally? Do you want to have a market share of only in Singapore or globally around the world where you be earning USD?

    4rd - Look at the people who are endorsing the company products. Artists, models, famous big players?

    5rd - The company system. What are the activities that the company is allowed to do. Roadshows, trade events etc.

    6rd - Is the company under any assurance? Direct sales Association? Case trust with money back guarantee?
