    I have long since wondered and never come to a conclusion. Where do we come from? Adam and Eve? Particles from the cosmos? Is there even meaning to our lives? What if it's all a lie? A conspiracy? A joke?

    Actually, I think it's really scary to think about such stuff. It makes you question life, the meaning of it, religion etc. Although it might sound dead depressing, if you ever sat down and thought about it, you'd realise that there is some kind of fraud in all this.

    Maybe it's constructed reality. We create the meaning in our lives, we go around explaining the inexplicable, making ourselves great, mighty and important. If we take an atheist stance to it, there'd be no God, no faith to provide the fuel to live on. Are we then living life aimlessly, without promise for a better tomorrow?

    For all we know, we're in a little glass tank and a superior being is looking down on us, laughing at us as we proudly imagine we're the most intelligent beings.

    There are so many stands to take on this. Sure, the fundamentalist Christians will all slam me, as this IS a very controversial topic. But if you are sit back and think about it, use intellectual rigour to test your faith, the result could be intriguing.

    This is of no offence to anyone. I just want to hear what others have to say.
