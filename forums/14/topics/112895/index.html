    Actually.... jei sao fei is how MLM started. The first group of consumers use the products... say about 100 consumers. 90 of them got good results. 10 of them give up on the products due to various reasons : not within their budget, not suitable or dun need the product anymore. The 90 consumers will be encourage to spread the word of mouth on the product results to their contacts. The company is willing to pay them for that. This is a lot cheaper than paying the TV commercials and selling the products thru distribution channels.

    The 90 consumers will be divided into the various categories:
    - Want to make a living out of sharing the product results. Thus, in a way, they are doing business now. Consumer Business people. This is the group of distributors that are highly active in selling and recruiting.
    - Want to make small income. These people do not want to make a living out of this. But they don't mind sharing the products with their friends and still get paid.
    - Only want to use the product. That's it.
