    My awareness about Diamond Water is that it is a MLM company. I do not know much about this company, but feedbacks about Diamond Water product were superb.

    Yes, it used to be that MLM do not do advertisement in the inception phase of this industry in SG. Strictly by word of mouth only. Then some smarter companies realised that Singapore is too small a market and there was a need to re-inject and reinnovate their marketing strategies and hence we now see advertisements. For a handful few or maybe just one, went further on TV and was a sponsor for NKF show and National Day. Part of a branding process.
