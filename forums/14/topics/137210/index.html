    MLM is a marketing tools for selling products which need one to one explaination.

    You may think that all products are the same.

    For e.g. if you go to a retail store to buy health supplements and the retail asst do not have in depth knowledge or worse no time to explain to you what's the benefit and who should be taking the supplement.

    In order to understand a product, the best way is by face to face, one to one explaination, by doing so, the customer will be more comfortable and have confident in the product. Any doubts from the customer can be answered immediately.
