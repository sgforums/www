    Alright, after reading all the threads on MLM in this forum, I keep asking myself why people want to join MLM?

    I've personally joined a couple of them for a short period of time and understand how the "Leaders" psycho the newbies to work for passive incomes, which in turn make these leaders more money lah.

    Ok brief reason why i joined MLM companies:
    I used to work as a sales person in an exhibition, my then boss was a real good one, he was willing to teach what he knows to us staffs. At the end of the exhibiton, he advised me to join a good MLM company to learn how to communicate with people because all successful businessmen relied on "partners" to make them rich. So i listened to him and got myself into my first MLM company, which was a direct selling one. What i learned was how to sell a product. And how to treat your customers as your friend and not your enemy, no use pressurizing the sh-it out of them to buy from you, talk to them the way you would like to be talked to, I'm sure no one likes the feeling of being pressurized to buy something (Looks at AMH ).

    Now my personal opinion on all these MLM thing is that don't join a MLM company for life, instead if you really have to be involved in the MLM industry, start one your own. Because no matter how much money you are earning as a member of the company, it's always the "LONG TOU LAO DA" or the owner of the company that earns the most. No matter how hard you work, your BOSS is always going to earn the most.

    Just my humble opinion.
