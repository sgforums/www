    After reading this blog, I guess I am not the only one that was trick by their special tailored documents. I was so surprise when I received an invoice stating that I have advertise with ' Singapore Business Pages' because I thought that document send to us was purely a verification document. I call them and scolded them about the way they write their document. Firstly, there was no title to tell us that it is a contract. Secondly, it starts with a verification statement and they purposely make the services wrong so that we will change it without awareness of the top portion that states the contract because it looks like yellow pages verify document. We assume that the services were wrong and change and sign and than fax to them. Totally disarmed our awareness that this is a contract. Than 19 days later they invoice me stating that I have advertise for the directories. I complain but they say you have 7 days to cancel. I complain that it is a pure misinterpretation but they suggested that I can cancel the contract but have to pay cancellation charges.

    After a while I notice something strange. Which is? When I sign the documents I did not get their acknowledgment (like a courtesy call or a thank you call or a invoice) within the 7 days, than how on earth I can know that I have made a mistake and cancel it within 7days???? They only send me the acknowledge invoice after 19days.

    Something even more strange is that when I was told to write in to cancel the contract by the lady. I wrote in and ask them to void the contract due to misinterpretation. You know what? They can come up with a long list (About 2 pages A4size paper) on terms and conditions for cancellation just within 2hrs and 36mins!!!!! Amazing isn't it!

    Acknowledgment for document takes 19days but cancellation takes only 2hrs 36mins. Wow! That's strange?

    Than I know this company actually focusing on making money based on other people's mistakes through their specially tailored document rather than through proper business practice. But I am still no sure until something prompt me to search on the internet to see whether there are similar cases. And not to my surprise, I found that I am not the only one.

    They are definitely not practicing a fair business deal.

    I am very annoy with them and I would appreciate some advice or suggestion as to how to effective counter such unfair practice business company.
