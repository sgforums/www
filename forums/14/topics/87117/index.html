    Hi i have been researching into MLM for quite sometime now and like to point out a few important factors on how to choose a good MLM.

    "makelifebetter" has pointed out a few good points in choosing a good company in another thread i just copy what i have written over here hope it's useful.

    1. Company backgroud
    2. Products
    3. System
    4. Timing
    5. Team

    1. Company backgroud - A good company should be debt free and does not involve in any form of investment and listing. It is a risk for the company if investment is involved and listing only tells u that the company do now have sufficient capital to further expands its business therefore it is looking for shareholders. As MLM meant bringing the products from the manufacturer straight to the the user to reduce product cost. Therefore its is important that the company has the ability to manufacture and distrubute the products itself. Its manufacture and distrubution should not be of different company as it will mean extra cost and possible change of manufacturer.

    2. Products - Not all products should be sold in MLM structure. In MLM repeated sale is important as it will ensure that its distrubutor will have a stable income instead of a one time sale. The product must be expandable, meaning can be used up and need to restock. The product itself must have the ablility to prove its own usefullness. If today u were to setup a company what would u sell? of course u will look for something that is really good and incurr good comment from the consumer and encourage propagation from words of mouth. When a product is good, distrubutor need not push hard sale as the product speaks for itself.

    3. System - After comparing products only a few company is left. Then the system comes in. I have seem quite a few system from various companies but most of them are hard to accomplish. Most company focus on finding extremely strong downline in order to rise up in high level. But that means not everyone can do it, for example some system said that u must have 3 downline which are doing a certain amount of sale inorder to attain a certain level, that also mean that the person need to find very good distrubutor to rise up in level. Some company runs binary plan which mean balance both side to earn. But what happen if i find an extremely strong distrubutor as a downline does it mean i must restrict him from expanding or i will not be able to earn? Therefore a system must allows everyone have an opportunity to success no matter how weak or strong he.

    4. Timing - MLM can be catagorise in to 3 stages young, medium and mature. This catagorises meant the numbers of years the company have survived. Nowadays with comparision to the oldest company in the market. Young suggest company less that 10years, medium range from 11- 30 years and mature mean more that 31 years. Its is good to join a company in the medium range as it is more stable and has less market exposure.

    5. Team - This factor decide whether the distrubutor of the company is pratising MLM correctly. Honestly, Love, Ethic, team work and a lot of other factors contrubutes to the success. The culture induced into the team will reflect alot on the whether the team will put through in the end. Many distrubutor have the thought of if i can sign more people under me i will be success, however this is not true. Success depends on whether the team have the correct attitude to attract other people into the business. Ethic also plays an inportant role. I have seen distrubute not willing to sell a product because it doesn't suit the consumer's needs. Sometime selling a product is not everything in doing business, its the trust and professionalisium that is important.
