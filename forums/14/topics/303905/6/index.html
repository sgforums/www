If he had been a Scam Artist, why is he calling this new project the same name as the one that had failed? This time round, he has enough money to do any kind of project he desired. Once this new project is completed, he will not sell, but instead, he will give it away for free. To whom? To the victim of the previously fail project. I do not know how is he going to do that? I love to see how it's gonna be done. So my question is whether it is fair for those who had turned their back on James Phang to receive a free unit on this new Empire Magic Kingdom? Will they be embarrassed for the rumors that they have told about him?

    In the course of his presentation, James Phang gave his word that - "He will deliver whatever he had said." So far, I have not heard any corporate leader would say such a thing in front of 3000 participants. He certainly has a lot of good values that we can learn from.

    Personally, I would like to thank James Phang for his vision in creating the Empire Group Alliance that has such great resemblance to GNN of OKA. At least there is a chance for me to fulfill my dreams now.
