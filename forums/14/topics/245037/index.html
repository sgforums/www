    Hi only joined today

    Mlm would have a structure like most businesses, the thing about most businesses today is that most of them pay stars big money to advertise their company or product where as in network marketing the money is put into a network to pay the the distributors bonuses and residual income, all advertising is word of mouth.

    When you bring in a new person you make residual income on their business, I like to call mini franchsies.

    There are many good companies out there and then there are some bad.

    My advice is not to jump right into anything, try the products or service first, ask around forums for postive feedback.

    One company to be careful of is worldprofit, they have a website deal which souns very good, if you opt to buy at $99 per month and then opt out they will send a legal letter.

    Fortunatlly theres not many companies that bad, but ther are many good well establidhed network marketing companiesout there, some of the big ones are members of the dsa.
