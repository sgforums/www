    Good question.

    Our co is a MLM using the direct buying concept.

    Let me explain the differences :-

    Conventional business

    Manufacturer --> Agency --> Wholesaler --> Retailer --> User
    (M) (A) (W) (R) (U)

    From Manufacturer to Retailer, everyone makes money. For instance a can of coke from manufacturer cost a few cents but we are paying so much.

    Later Direct Selling(DS) came in,

    Direct Selling Co --> Stockist --> Member --> User
    Manufacturer
    (A) (W) (R) (U)

    A Direct Selling Co normally manufactures their own star product and sources from other manufacturers. They appoints stockist which distributes to the members where they can buy for own use or sell it to make profit. In DS, a large portion of the income comes from selling.

    Is there a lot difference Conventional business compared with DS?
    Not much.

    DS Concept : You buy from us, we give special price. You buy some more, you sell you make money.The more you sell, the more you make.

    Direct selling is good and many have suceeded provided they overcome 4 obstacles.

    1) 97% Of the people don't like selling. How many people like to knock at people's door to sell soap powder? Definately not me.

    2) Cost/Free. Your friend ask for cost price, so how to make money? Your uncle/auntie say try first, good then buy. Give first, hope they buy.

    3) Delivery/Capital. Do you have time to be a delivery boy for all your members? If you member calls you up to get some product for them, are you going to collect money first or buy the product first? Hopefully they have money to pay you when you deliver.

    4) Undercutting. An older member may have higher commission/discount rate compared with a new member. If you buy from the older member, you can get more discount lah.

    Now back to Direct Buying,
    ................................ Pay $$
    ...................... [color=red]<---------------------[/color]
    Direct Buying Co ...... 1 price system ....... User
    Research & Development
    ........................ [color=red]---------------------->[/color]
    .......................Deliver Direct(Courier)

    (A) (U)

    DB does not manufacture, only Research and Development. Of course sourcing from other manufacturers. as a member, you pay direct to DB and the products delivery directly to your door steps.
    With the one price system, everyone buys the same price, how to sell to make money? Buy yourself to use lah.

    DB Concept: You buy from me, I give you discount. You tell your friend to buy from me. I give your friend discount, you also get discount and on top of that, I pay you money.Help your friend to make money, you get more money

    From the different type of Co, you should be able to see where the $$$ comes.

    Compare both DS and DB concept, which is better? You be the judge. Need to know more...email me at benwong99@hotmail.com.
