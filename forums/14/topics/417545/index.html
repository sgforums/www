    To answer on broader note, Duplication is always one of the biggest challenges in network marketing and goes hand-in-hand with working a "proven" system to enhance successful performance. In working any MLM business, it is important to implement a training regimen of downline for duplication of success. It is imperative that team members learn how to "work" their businesses in a conformed manner - you want to make it as easy as possible. For this reason, it has always been important to have someone "show or teach" you how it is done. You want to learn from those who are ethically and honestly gaining success in your business. Attend trainings and learn to become proficient at gaining sales and recruiting business partners.

    The problem with most people is they do not wish to take the time to learn and therefore effectively fail (give up) at MLM, thereby giving the industry a bad name when they say MLM does not work. The reality is that most people will fail at almost everything because they do not or will not give enough time or effort to learn how to become successful. Anyone can do it  . . . it just takes learning the right principles, practice, and perseverance (over a period of time) to succeed.

    I am growing my organization (over time) and continue learning from my mistakes. I also have mentors to help guide me. In the past, I could not be everywhere for everyone and I cannot not possibly expect everyone to be at or go to trainings. It was a frustrating challenge to grow my organization initially and have everyone on the "same page". My organization grew, but it did so slowly until I began to seek out and use other resources available to me. I now have a huge team subscribing in my organization (PM me if you are interested to know which one) in a number of countries - but it was not always that way.
