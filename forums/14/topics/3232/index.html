    Love. A precious emotion shared between a male and a female, sometimes everlasting, other times, ephemeral. But how do you know when to hold on and when to let go?

    I don't believe there are any *sure* signs that indicate whether you have found your soulmate. Some people say it's chemistry, others say it's the meeting of minds, but most common of all, it's a gut instinct.

    Perhaps love is a gamble. You have to trust intuition to tell you whether you should stay with the person or not, and never regret the opportunities you pass up. Yet, if you decide not to hold on and check out all the opportunities you pass up, you could have missed "the one". Or is there even, a *one* for you?
