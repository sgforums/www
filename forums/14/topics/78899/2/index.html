    Hi guys, I believe all of you know the concept of Network Marketing aka MLM.

    I have been to too many sales talk about each and individual company. I came to a few conclusion on how to choose a good company. (my PERSONAL OPINION)

    1) How long has the company exist? How is its history and culture? Any Debts? How much is the company owning? Worldwide recognised?

    2) Products - variety more or less? Whether the company owns the processing and developing of the products? Any patern process? Has the products been able to speak out? Is it useful and handy that people can use easily? Is it expensive or reasonable? Is it copyright? What kind of certifications?

    3) Sales volume? Business Plan - Fair or Not?

    To myself, MLM is not a easy job yet its one of the job that can offer you free time, free finance. If anybody can see that you will earn big money after a few months of starting, just be wary.
