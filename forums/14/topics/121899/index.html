    1st method
    You can do stacking, ie, recruitment a couple or three others who want to do the business. That way your points can be combined and less points will be needed for you to be SV. The total points required will be more. Eg. if you get a person to join you, both of you need a combined VP of 5000 instead of 4000. This means, however, that each of you only need 2,500 VPs. If you get two other persons to join you, the total required will be 6000, which means that each person needs only 2000 points.

    2nd method
    Another way is to get three Success Builders, ie each purchases 1000 VP of products. You only need to have 1000 VP.

    The first method is better because it allows the others who join and combine VP with you to be SV as well. The second method only allows you to be SV.
