    Hello kyoshirou77,

    I'll try my best with my understanding of the SE business model; do check with your introducer (if any) or a good BRM for details.

    I won't know exactly how much u are paid in CRP, as it depends on the monthly global turnover.

    There is no interest.

    CRP is not guaranteed; it is a privilege for Prime status merchants only (BP, SP, GP). For example, a Bronze Prime is only entitled to a maximum of 300 e-points (S$480.) which is paid out over time, on the 10th of each month.

    e.g. You decide to upgrade your merchant status to prime status so you purchase a BP for 375 e-points (S$600), thus you are entitled to CRP. Every month on the 10th, you will receive rebates, and this will stop once the total of 300 e-points (S$480) is paid out to you.

    There is no ROI (rate of interest).

    My expectation of profits? I am not the auditor. >_<
