    dear wolala

    well, there are many products out there that are overpriced too (grossly or otherwise) & whether overpriced or not really depends on your financial status, so this makes it a very subjective statement. if you are rich, you do not feel the pinch when you spend over 200sgd for SK2 masks (when 1 of my female friends told me, i nearly chocked on my coke, hahaha) but for the average s'porean, OUCH!!!

    i assume that the reason you ask this question is because you feel that the products are overpriced to let their distributors earn from the sales of the product to you. this is one of the reasons why MLM has a bad name in S'pore. many people have the mentality that friends should not take advantage of friends & not earn their money. I find nothing wrong with this view.

    wherether overpriced or not, it is as I suggested, subjective, but it is true that the bulk of the sales price goes to the distributor / agent / MLMer / etc. this is one of the fundamentals of the MLM structure & this is the reason why the successful MLMers can achieve financial freedom. of course it is coupled with many other factors which I will not discuss here today.

    i guess you feel that your friends are earning from you when you buy from them, therefore you ask this question. this is usually the case when their product is more expensive than what is available outside. it might not be the exact same product, but might be compared to something similar.

    not that I want to advertise but there are MLM companies whose products are sold more expensive in retail stores than when bought from a distributor from that company. this is the way MLM should be conducted in my opinion. this gives the consumer more incentive to get the product from the distributor rather than make their own way down to the shop, & let the retail shop earn from the sales too.

    I hope this answers your question.

    cheers.
