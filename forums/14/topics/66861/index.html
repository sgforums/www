    I think that there are many disgruntled ppl here in this forum due to mlm. The fact that they are may be due to being cheated either monetary or trust. From my opinion, MLM is a good business system. Problem lies with the ppl which is what the whole business is based upon.

    I've been in several MLM companies, some whch were set up by ppl who were previously from other MLM cos. I've many friends who join MLM companies yet the only ones I see making money is the boss. Many times we fail to see the thin line separating truth and deceit. We choose to believe in something which we can see but refuse to believe what we actually feel. We see achievers, successful ppl, but fail to see the failures cos it's negative to see them. Where then is the truth?

    Many times these achievers buy their own sales. Money from where we can only assume. I know of a few so called achievers who borrowed money through different means so that they can achieve where they are. But think of the stress they have when they have to pay. So they influence their downlines in whatever way possible to work and do whatever neccesary to get the sales to pay back. In the end only the boss earns. What kind of money is this?

    Pls open your eyes and see the truth.MLM is a good business but our society and population does not allow us to grow MLM. It is not a lifestyle, unlike other countries which are bigger. They have the population to sustain. We Singaporeans have a kind of metality that if you have a good lobang I also want. KS syndrome. So how to do MLM? If you can make money, I also want. But why not I make more? So anyhow whack. In the end downline suffer. Lots of hit n run cases. So many ppl fed up with MLM.

    Everything is a lie here. The management does not have a correct attitude or action. All they want is money. Makes sense for business but look at the social damage it has caused. I hope that we Singaporeans can really look clearly at MLM. There are many ways to make money. Do you want to make money with a good conscience? There are lots of us who knows the truths of some MLM companies but unfortunately not many have opened up. I have made money thru MLM but I have also experienced the stress. You need a proper heart and mind to make it in this business with no major stress. But it'll need lots of work. So think about it. I bought my first car with my 1st job after quitting MLM. I got my 1st gold card in my 2nd yr in the same job too. I also dun have high qualifications but I was willing to see the truth and work hard.

    My wish is that there will be less ppl having problems or feeling cheated becos of MLM. That there will be less MLM companies coming out just to cheat ppl. May there be happiness for all.
