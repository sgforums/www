    If you are lucky enough to hit upon a unique idea and your business grows
    quickly then you are in the minority (but congratulations!). If you aren’t that
    lucky, don’t get discouraged.

    Understand that success is available to any person that patiently works
    towards a goal. Some get there faster than others so if you expect a long
    ride you may just be pleasantly surprised when you wake up having met
    your goals six months earlier than expected rather than disappointed when
    you are only half as far as you thought you would be.

    Network marketing business takes time to perfect. I remembered, ability to
    setting GOALS was the first learning in this industry and I always practised to
    do it. Setting GOALS is important for success, If you want to get anything faster
    than you ever thought, you have to learn how to setting goals and plan to
    achieve them.

    "People who are unable to motivate themselves must be content with
     mediocrity, no matter how impressive their other talents"

    Why write down your GOALS?

    I heard 'Jim Rohn' said it best when he said...
    "If you go to work on your goals, your goals will go to work on you. If you
    go to work on your plan, your plan will go to work on you. Whatever good
    things we build enp up building us."

    Goals Can UNLEASH Your Inner Power!

    If you're strong enough with you goals and honestly passionate about what
    you want in life...these "passion" become the strongest motivation you will
    ever have in your lifetime!

    There are several different excellent software programs and seminars that
    deal with this important subject. But I'm not writing this here to sell something.
    Instead, I've learned one simple method which work extremely well for many
    highly successful people and would like to share.

    YOUR Personal "REASON WHY"

    Step 1. This will require a couple sheets blank paper(or a notebook). Start
    writing down 100 goals as you have that and would like to accomplish over
    the next 5 to 10 years (you don't have to stop at 100...keep going if you like).
    Something like:

    • -Pay off XYZ loan by___________date.
    • -Buy a new big screen TV before__________date.
    • -Take a classon [fill in the blank].
    • -Travel to [fill in the blank].
    • -Build up my investment portfolio to $1,500,000 Net Worth on or before _______month/year.
    • -Get to the next level position in network marketing business within _________days/month.
    • -You get the picture?

    It's important to put in some small attainable goals mixed in very BIG DREAM!
    It feel so very good when you can scratch goals of your list as your business
    helps you achieve them.

    TIP: The more specific and measurable your goals are, the more likely you
    will be able to achieve them.

    Step 2. Take a highlighter marker and highlight YOUR TOP 10 GOALS -those
    you want to achieve the absolute positively most in your life.

    Step 3. Of YOUR TOP 10 GOALS, single out 5 that you feel right now would be
    the easiest for you to achieve.

    Step 4. Get three 3 x 5 index cards and write your MOST IMPORTANT 5 GOALS
    down on each of them all five goals go on each cards.

    Step 5. Put one card where you are sure to see it several times during your days
    (for example, maybe the dashboard of your car if you drive back and forth to
    work each day).

    Step 6. Tape one card to your bathroom mirror where you will see it every
    morning when you wake up (and maybe you will see it at the end od each day
    before you sleep at night).

    Step 7. Put one cards in your wallet or your purse and carried with you. Pull it out
    and read it when you need the inspiration to focus on where your future is now
    headed thanks to your home business.

    Special Tip: Many succesful people will complete at least on goal on their TOP 5,
    then repeat the exercise doing a new TOP 5 or keeping the 4 not complelte and
    adding a new goal to create a new TOP 5 card set.

    "Excellence is not a singular act, but a habit. You are what you repeatedly do." -Aristotle

    You know, a lot of people deride at the idea of writing down their goals - reading them
    everyday - seeing them everyday....but there is a very powerful reason for doing this.
    Two things will begin to happen.

    1. You will constantly be reminded of YOUR REASON WHY, something you
        will need during the ups and downs of running your own business. This will
        be a powerful tool to help you carry your dream through.

    2. You're not going to go into the whole psychology thing of it here, but I
        will say this - when your subconscious mind picks up on the message over
        and over and over, you may find that you begin attracting the means to
        reach your goals a lot faster than you ever imagined.

    Don't believe it? Well, That's OK...Just trust in process--call it an experiment in time
    and see exactly what happens if you follow through. Make an appointment with yourself
    to revisit this exercise 5 years into the future(print this exercise) and tell you sponsor
    what happened!

    HOT TIP: Some people have accelerated their goals achievement by cutting out pictures that relate to their goals and posting them in a special area (their work cubicle, their home office bulletin board, around their computer monitor) as a constant reminder of the DREAMS they are building thanks to their own business.
    Try it! You maybe pleasantly rewarded!
