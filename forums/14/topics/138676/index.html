    It has come to my attention that these 3 companies are majoring in magnetic bed as their main sales product in order to gain the most PW for promotion. I have notice their product differences in terms of quality and functions.

    Enyouth=Product proclaimed to be from USA,due to some info i heard it was from taiwan. Selling at price of 1500+ for a single. However a research in website shows that the bed is worth less than $1000 in the market.

    Asayo=Product from Japan,almost similar to the bed of enyouth. Here comes the tricky part. Magnets inside the bed are claimed to be fourth generation,whereas enyouth are still using 1st generation.(Feel the bed from enyouth for round magnets whereas for Asayo it is rod shaped magnets arranged in a square manner). Addition of colour therapy for better effects and has 7 layers which make up the bed instead of 5 from enyouth. Cost of single bed varies of mere $100+.

    DW=Products very similiar to Asayo,including colour therapy,but i heard they modified the colour structure and arrangements.(thus rendering it useless) Cost of single is similar to enyouth.

    Asayo proclaims that DW and enyouth "stole" materials from their sales kit and incorporate some materials as part of it. DW's product is a copy of Asayo and enyouth's is of older generation magnetic bed.

    I like to hear any view's arguements about these facts? So who is the black sheep among these 3 companies selling magnetic bed products? Which company will u choose if u are to buy a magnetic bed?
