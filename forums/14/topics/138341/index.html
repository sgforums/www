    If you are a Marquis, but there wasn't any new counts into your network. You will NOT get five digits income. You may be able to enjoy some overridding if someone within your network purchase BA products. If no activities at all, the painful truth is ZERO. But if you can at least recruit about two new counts into your network every month at all levels, your rewards will be huge. Not many in Singapore, especially today, are able to do this now.

    Btw, there is now one active group who is propagating the investment of a CM (Challenge-Marquis) which is a sum of approximating 50K-70K. If you have already been approached, please seriously consider all factors before making the decision. Do not act based on initial impulse and greed, they have good tactics into influencing your decision.

    Hope this help.

    A Concerned Singaporean.
