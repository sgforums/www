If you look at the amount of products they are selling, then you can consider them as poor based on average sales per product, as compared to years ago when they have less products. Think of this, if Amway is a good business, it should be easy to do, why should there be so much trainings and meetings ?

    d) For your info, Amway only ranked no. 3 in Malaysia today. No. 1 and 2 are Elken and Cosway, both Malaysian owned Companies which has a much much shorter history. Why do you think Amway could be taken over ? Majority of their members left them after experiencing the truth.

    e) I understand Jedi's position, he was trained to understand in this way. Nearly all the MLM Companies I knew which have weaknesses will train their members to emphasize on their strong points and when their weak points are being brought out, they will turn to other directions and say, "...we are taught not to bring down other Companies...... blah, blah, blah....." I feel that they is really nothing wrong to bring down anyone or Company if what is mentioned is the truth. Isn't this better than allowing more people being victimised ?

    f) I have personally seen many people, some my friends who worked very hard in Amway and spoke just like Jedi. Some even declare that they will never leave Amway or join other Company. Few years later, they are forced to live up to their words, not making money from Amway but too ashamed to join others.
