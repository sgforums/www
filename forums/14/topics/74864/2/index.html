    fine, I will calculate one bonus for you, the InDept Bonus of 1%.

    in Amway System, you have no limitation of generations. Means your network can grow until 100 generations and all business are yours.

    so, what is InDepth Bonus of 1%? this is a bonus paid to you when your 2nd generation and others (not 1st generation front line) manage to achieve 21%, you will get 1% of InDepth Bonus.
    So example,

    you > A > B > C > D

    when A achieved 21%, u get 4% leadership bonus. when B achieve 21%, you get 1% InDepth Bonus. But what about C & D?
    in Amway, if A, B, C & D all achieved 21%, so you can get 4% from A, and 1% from B, C & D (total 3%)!
    Imagine this, what if your network growth until 100 generations and ALL achieved 21%? What is the total percentage you will get if 100 of your downline in one row achieved 21% at the same time?

    I give you one example how outside play trick with this so called payout percentage.

    trick 1#
    BV (Business Volumn) = 250BV
    Actual Sales = $500
    Percentage achieved : 6%
    bonus : 250BV X 6% = $15

    trick 2#
    PV (Point Value) = 250PV
    Business Volume = 500BV
    Actual Sales = $1000
    Percentage achieved : 4%
    bonus : 500BV X 4% = $20

    this 2 company both claim that their payout percentage higher than Amway, Company 1 pay 6% and company 2 pay 4%.

    Now, let me calculate Amway bonus.
    PV = 250PV
    BV = 500BV
    Actual Sales = $500
    Percentage achieved : 3%
    bonus = 500BV X 3% = $15

    now compare with company 1#, company 1# gives 6% but multiple by 250BV (half of the actual sales), so comes to the same although the percentage given is higher.
    Company 2# gives 4% with the bonus of $20, but actual sales is $1000. If I use Amway calculation, $1000 x 3% you should get $30 and NOT $20.

    Can you see the differences?
