    From my past experiance by ebay & other on-line:

    1. Local Banks can accept "Other" currency's cheque with a transaction fee. To what I remember POSBank cannot accept foreign cheque.

    2. Be aware of "Money Order". I have received Canada "Local Money Order". Very clearly, it for local (Canada) use only.

    3. The charges for clearing into Singapore is pretty high. Prepare to pay SG$20 to SG$50 per cheque. Plus low exchange rates & 2 to 4 weeks to clear. So if you need your cash flow, be prepared.

    My recommendations is use Paypal & accept their low exchange rate, but can download $ directly into DBS account. Takes about 1 week to clear & charges is SG$1 per download.

    Use Western Union Money Transfer. It's immediate you can take your $ using codes only. Exchange Rate is reasonable. There is no charges for receiving $. All charges paid by senders. Available in all Post office.

    If you need to use cheque, ask if you can receive in Singapore Dollar Banker's Cheque. As if in Singapore Dollar, either there is no service charge or very low service charge, no worrys of exchange rate & take shorter time to clear.

    See you don't have to just use their on-line service. You have the power to choose. Check it out carefully. There are many loop holes by creating a account.
