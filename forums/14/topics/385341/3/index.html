    Topic revolves around the word "BEST". This can be subjective or objective, depending on the author's original mindset.

    Best at what? Sales, recruiting, product, for making profit, appeal to consumers? The thread is a perfect example of how one person starts something and it get's morphed or distorted as each person "gets involved" in the process or providing a reply to the thread.

    For statistical issues, visit the DSAS. For opinionated issues, this thread provides insight as to the myriad of thoughts, approaches and perceptions of the industry and its players.

    As for who will be around and who won't, who the players are and who are not . . . MLM is just like any other business arena . . .there are businesses that have been around for a very long time and there are NEW busines that submit for license and permit everday. Those who succeed are going to have a mutli-faceted program and approach that appeals to one's personal criteria - either as a company to work with and be involved in, or as a company that can provide them with products/services they desire or need. Remember, for any business to surive it must provide a product or service that is marketable and in demand (or demand that can be created) - and any business must be conducted above board (with integrity). 

    In the end, we are all providers or consumers of "something". Does not matter if you buy from retailer CK Tang or your next door MLM neighbor. What is the "best" is ultimately determined by YOU, though you may be influenced by other factors!
