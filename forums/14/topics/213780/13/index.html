    I was called by a stranger abt more than a mth ago, telling me abt a job which seems lucrative. Can help me write resume and all. Seemed too gd to be true. Someone unknown calling me out of the blue, saying abt all those gd stuffs. Who wouldn't be fascinated? Being naive and having zero knowledge abt mlm, I decided to check the company out out of curiosity. I was merely looking for a decent job and working exp but I got more than that.

    i just felt dat more and more students are being targetted by MLM companies. when more students join, students will poach more students.

    Anyway, my upline told me to come back the next day and also telling me NOT to say anything abt the company, which I blindly followed.

    2nd day: training started. They got me and some new recruits to watch some video, which might hv been used as a means of brain wash. They even taught phone techniques, which is often used to call up clients. I learnt that they used "cold contacts" to get at strangers to make appointment to come to the company. That muz hv been how they get the numbers to "cold contact" ppl I hope they hv shredded my contact list and the rest of the people who hv left. It's really intruding the person's privacy.

But the SLOW and NAIVE me didn't reslise that at that point of time. Which decent company will want to dig out EVERY SINGLE DETAIL of their employees? It's madness!

It was supposed to be to "get my parents' consent" for working at VE, since they still don't know I'm working for them. The Marketing Manager (MM) who went with me kept using me as a reason for them to support me. He says I shd be benefitting my family and relatives with the health pdts from VE, but it was just an EXCUSE for targetting ur close ones. VE just wanna sap all the money from us. Any company's aim is to make as much $$$ as possible. So yea.

    I was quite in a dilemma coz my parents wanted me to stop working for them, since someone else from VE hv approached them earlier and they had rejected their pdt. On the other hand, I was quite controlled by my MM who wanted me to do things as he wanted. I was like following his every single command. I don't know what was up with me

    Anyway I decided to stop working for VE on the 3rd day. Told the MM my decision and he told me to come over to the office to talk things out. I agreed, but little did I knew I wld be dragged inside again. I decided to explain my reasons for leaving because I felt it was a decent way to resign from a job. Sheesh, I didn't see that coming. Somehow they convinced me to stayed on and yup, went for even more SA which didn't went well.

    During the SA, I felt like a robot. I didn't know what to do. I felt weird in front of my relatives. it was just not me. I was totally controlled by them VE ppl. haiz

    After going for 3 SA (one with my parents, 2 with relatives) I asked my MM "in what other ways can I close the sale?" (means being successful in selling the pdt to the customer). He said he withdrew his savings from his bank acct (which I presumed he had done b4) and I HAD to agree to that!

    So on the next day, w/out my parents knowing, I went with my that MM and another lady (prolly a MM too) to withdraw cash (I wont say it here but it's really a lot). This was to buy up the pdts so I could get promoted to a Marketing Executive in 3 days from the day I started out as a Marketing Agent.

    Let me explain this bit here: For a new recruit who have closed their 1st sale with someone (usually parents or guardians), they will be promoted from a Marketing Agent (Probation) to Marketing Agent [MA(P) to MA]. From there, they are encouraged to close a few more sales to hit Marketing Executive rank in THREE days. Usually ppl wld sell off their pdts to their relatives successfully to complete the sales but for others who wanted the award, they might resort to using their own money to buy up the pdts and get promoted to ME.

    For my case, it was because I was pressured by my MM in a way, because it was the end of the month then and I was in a VERY GOOD POSITION to boost up their team's sales to compete for the Best Team Award (meaning the team which can make the most sales in a month). And I had to freaking helped them to bosst up the sales with my own $$$. Ouh crap! *bang head against wall*

Coz I realised being ME isn't a big deal. It means anyone who hv enuf $$$ in their acct can simply rise up to ME like that. And my MM can try to cheer me up by saying "why aren't you happy? u can earn more now"
