The reason i quit was because their products were overpriced and they couldn't give a good explanation for why this was so.

    The first product, the water purification system is about $2000 by a brand known as cal ion water. They say that alot of research has gone into the product and that it is used in korea but you can't find anything about it on the internet and the ppl at VE told me that all the websites are in korean

    The second product, the magnetic matress is sold for abt $2000 for queen and $3000 king. The matress is manufactured by VE in the US. If u searched for magnetic matresses on the internet you will find that all of them are priced between 300 to 500 US dollars. The excuse they give for this is that the mattresses made by other companies are lousy quality even though these companies sell their products to a global market and their products are reviewed by websites like amazon.com

According to them, they spend money converting the voltage of the product, which u can bypass by buying a $30 step down adapter. They also told me that shipping costs are included and since only ppl at VE are trainned on repairing the product ppl have to pay extra for service.
