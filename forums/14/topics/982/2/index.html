    Cantofire: There r a few criteria that will help u 2 get into OCS:
    1. U must have at least an A-level or Diploma Cert. If u r a scholar(special paper), your chances will increase by 50%.
    2. Talk 2 your PC abt. it n b more "on" in front of him.
    3. I do believe your ECA record helps.
    4. Peer Appraisal does have a certain weightage, so treat your peers well.
    5. U need not b super-fit, as I had seen pple who juz got a pass in their IPPT into OCS.

    Btw, I'm from the 19/95 OCC n my vocation is an OCS Infantry Instructor. OCS is not as xiong as what many pple think, but it stresses alot on the 7 core values. i.e. Loyalty 2 country, leadership, Discipline, Professionalism, Fighting Spirit, Ethics n Care 4 soldiers. One of the incentive is the $500++ allowance.
