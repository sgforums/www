    To me, products come and go. Today, this product the best. The next thing you know, someone will claim to have a better product.
    What happen now? Will the business last? Decisions to be made.

    There are a few questions to ask :-

    1) Are you in for short term or long term?
    2) You want to make quick bucks and get out fast?
    3) Are you willing to start all over again?
    4) Which is important to you? People, Product, Profit.
    5) What type of income do you want? Active(JOB - Just Obey Boss) or passive (Residual Income).
    6) To be truely confident, you must be convinced a 100% you are with the right company. Are you?

    Good luck.
