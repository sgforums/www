    Three. Income, who did you hear from? Okay nevermind that. Ask them where do they get their income from, how much. What are the chances of success. Do they preach having 5 digit income? Ask them for Balance Sheet, Financial Report, Profit & Loss statements. LOL. It's like saying... I see some artists sell their paintings for 50M USD, so I buy easels, canvasses and paints, and hope to have their kind of sales. Success? High monetary income? It's extreme materialism to link high earnings with success.
