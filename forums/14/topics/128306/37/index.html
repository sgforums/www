    Hi everyone,

    I am personally not involved in Enyouth and neither am I interested in this company. But I grew curious abt this company as my friends and I were being 'targeted' by one of the distributors there.

    Honestly speaking, I have no comments about how the company distributed their products. It is true that network marketing is one of the 'growing' distribution channel in the new century besides franchising. And no doubt of that, many did lose their time and money by committing themselves in any network company. It really depends on how the company is going to ensure their distributors are all going to benefit from the marketing plan and where the company is going to bring them to in the near future.
    So, The COMPANY and ITS LEADERS play a very important role in attributing to the success of their associates.

    From what I know, ENYOUTH previously was once known as ASTRALMAG (Do correct me if I am wrong). Many may think that the change of branding is for the better and its rather unnecessary to have worries abt it but I myself do feel otherwise.

    Take for instance, how abt if one day 7-11 decided to change its branding to '7-24' (since it is operating 24hrs daily) and Giordano to 'Go-Blue' ?
    Or how abt Nuskin to 'New skin' and AMWAY to 'TOPWAY'? Will anyone be able to accept the change? The branding of a company is very important as it helps the consumers to recognise their products and services immediately. Needless to say, if the company does not want to, they will have to keep changing their branding. (get what I mean?)

    And also, I was slightly turn off by the attitude of the distributors there. In fact, I was so surprised to see one of their distributors scolding vulgar language to show his disagreement against someone else's post. I thought its a cool idea to work together in such a vibrant environment but I guess its so so wrong.

    Although this is not a school but I guess the environment does play a very important role for the people to grow and learn to be mature in terms of their thoughts and verbal language. If I am one of the prospect of this company and their distributors are behaving in this manner, I guess its going to be tough for me to accept that the company is going to be a highly potential established company. As I believe that in any network marketing comapny, you are not only selling the products or company alone, you are ALSO selling YOURSELF. Leaving a good impression on the prospects is very important for every successful networker. Leaders do play a very important role as this is essential to upkeep the reputation of their company. But through how the younger distributors have expressed themselves, I am starting to have 2nd views on their leaders.

    'Who you follow is what you will become.'

    Anyway, I must say that their marketing plan is really attractive but like what I have mentioned earlier, only The Company and its leaders can determine the future.
