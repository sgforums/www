    BARRICHELLO TO START FROM THE BACK
    Last Updated: Sunday, 03, April, 2005, 11:21

    Rubens Barrichello will start from the back of the grid in Bahrain after his final qualifying session was blighted by more technical gremlins with his Ferrari F2005.

    The Brazilian was on course to move up from 15th on the overnight grid before a slow final sector signalled that something was amiss.

    Upon crossing the finish line Barrichello pulled abruptly to a halt and walked back to the pits on foot.

    When the F2005 was returned to the Ferrari garage the mechanics hurriedly investigated the rear end of the car and technical director Ross Brawn later confirmed to reporters that the gearbox was the culprit.

    Barrichello was hit by a similar problem on Friday afternoon, causing him to miss the entirety of yesterday's free practice to preserve the rebuilt 'box.

    Ferrari arranged for a new unit to be delivered from Maranello on a private jet overnight, but the replacement proved equally troublesome.

    The team has had to put the old gearbox back in for the race, as well as a new engine because of concerns over the high temperatures it was subjected to.

    Barrichello will go into this afternoon's race on a wing and a prayer, having completed hardly any mileage all weekend in a car he has only driven for a single day in testing at Mugello the week before last.
