Now, the job of the Frenchman is to teach him all the specificities of the rally. "At the beginning, the most important thing is to make him understand he has to drive keeping the car, the terrain and his brain in harmony. He has to take the time to understand the world around him on a rally. Then we will take care of the real technic, how to drive safely but quickly in the dunes. This experience is a real pleasure for me, because it's a marvellous feeling to share your own experience. It's like looking at your own child growing up. That's fantastic, really!
