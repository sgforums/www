    Right click your IE icon --> go to Connections tab --> highlight on the name of the dialer you are using then click on Settings --> under the heading 'Proxy server' --> put a tick on 'Use a proxy server for this connection' then enter your ISP proxy address & port no.

    If you are a Singnet dialup subscriber, your proxy address is: proxy.singnet.com.sg & Port: 8080
