    Just about 3 weeks ago, my soon to be 67 years old dad was hospitalised and was diagnosed with a minor stroke. That made him a diabetic with stroke now. He had been hospitalized years ago due to his diabetes were almost out of control.

    However, this time around, me and family had encounter several strange happenings to us since my dad was away in hospital. Its the first time we had such encounters.
    First, my sister and mum had smell the perfume of my dad at home when in fact he wasnt there. Few days later, while at home, i heard someone sneezing outside my house which sounds like my dad. Next was, the rice in the cooker, which never has turn bad despite being kept for more than a day, really turn bad that evening when my mom had it cooked in the morning. And my mum cooked the rice everyday, same time, but never before this happens. In malay culture, they believed that when a rice turn bad suddenly during a time when a family member is badly sick, it means that that person's death is imminent. Im not too sure if that is true or not. And just in the wee hours of this morning, my sister saw a red grasshopper in the kitchen sink. We had never seen grasshoppers in our home and I know grasshoppers in the house would mean something. My mum claimed that she seen some changes in my dad's face like deepening of cheek bones and his nose has crooked. However, i dont see that when i visited my dad.

    The doc said his stroke is stabilized now except that his blood pressure drops when he stands, thus making him too dizzy to walk on his own.

    All Im wondering if these strange weird things has something to do with my dad being sick. I sometimes asked myself and my family if we are just over reacting.
