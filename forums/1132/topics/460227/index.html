They will naturally attract a certain number of people with compatible frequencies, but definitely not all people's.

    At the same time, people are naturally attracting people of compatible frequencies, so as long as people continue to improve their state of mind and emotional state, their vibrational frequencies will continue to rise and attract equally positive people into their lives.
