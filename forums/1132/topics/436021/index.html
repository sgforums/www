    Question : Where do you think you are lightly to see a ghost? In a cemetry or in a temple?

    There was once we had a overnight topography exercise. We had to go to various check point in order to complete our exercise. We had a long day and ended up at the last checkpoint in Mandai forest. Our rest area was near the Mandai cemetry. We had to sleep there for the night.

    My buddy Ah Boon insisted that we should sleep in the temple beside the cemetry than among the tombstones in the cemetry. He says that it is "cleaner" there. I had no objection so we slept in the temple instead.

    In the middle of the night, Ah Boon woke me up. He looked frightened and asked me if I hear anyone crying outside the temple. I looked at him in suprised and told him I heard none. He then asked me to go back to sleep. As I turned my back against him, I could hear mumbling from him. I was too tired to stay awake and soon fall back to sleep.

    The next morning, Ah Boon told me that he had heard crying from some wandering spirit pleading outside the temple asking for help to find them a "home" so that they will no longer be wandering spirit. As there were 2 guardian god painted on the door, the wandering spirits were not able to enter. The mumbling I heard from Ah Boon were his chanting to keep the spirit away.

    The end....

    The moral of the story?.....

    Stay away from the temple near the cemetry.
