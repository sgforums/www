    @SoulDivine

    I agree that UFO in general is of course exist. However, they may only be space debris or military secret weapon or some natural phenomenon. 

    However, I do not see how the mere fact that we possess a certain level technology justifies the conclusion that there are other creatures outside of our galaxy to possess a more advanced level technology. Their existence is also questionable since we have practically have no evidence of their existence (well, you mention that there are lots of evidence, however they are highly contentious). 

    Now, what about the idea that since we have spaceships that is able to travel to other planets mean there is good possibility that there are other advanced civilization out there is capable to do so.

    First, I think it is pretty well established that there is no advanced form of life form within our solar system.

    Second, so far there have been many claims of habitable planets out there in other galaxies, however they are hardly habitable in the Earth sense (some have water, but extremely cold), (some have right temperature, but wrong pressure and all sort of things). However, they are so far away that it may take much longer time for them to travel here.

    Third, there is a physical limit on how fast we can travel (which is speed of light).

    Fourth such speed is impossible to reach by famous equation E=MC^2/(1-v^2/c^2), since energy required will reach infinity. Even if we reach the 99.9% of speed of light, the energy required is ridiculously high. To move 60kg being requires 5.38 x 10^21 J of energy. (We spent 4.74 x10^20 a year in 2008).

    Fifth, momentum is mxv, travelling at the speed of 99.9% of speed of light will ensure collision with even a pebble the size of pinky will ensure damage equivalent to several megatons of TNT exploded.  

    Thus, I think it is impossible for UFO to be alien spaceship or the like.
