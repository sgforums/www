    Pattaya police officers were notified after the electrocuted woman’s spirit possessed her mother while monks were chanting at the funeral. The mother cried and hugged her husband and told everybody that her child and grand chid were were murdered.

    The police found some clues and searched the neighbor’s room and found a wire with no plug. The case concerns a Sattahip mother and her 5 years old child who were recently electrocuted by a clothes line.

    The Sattahip electricity authority went to check and did not found any electric leaks. Pol.Lt.Col. Rattapak Sopap investigation officer in charge sent the bodies for autopsy.The next day at 15:00 Mr.Saitong Sanusan 29-years-old, the husband showed a video clip at the funeral. Bangsare Nork Bangsare, Sattahip.

    There after he said he wanted to disclose important information to be used in the investigation concerning the death of his wife and child. In the tape while 4 monks were chanting, Mrs.Somrak Kimsom, the mother of the death is possessed.

    And she hugs him and tells him they were murdered at noon, the day of the electrocution. She said she knew her daughter had quarreled with a neighbor then she touched the clothes line, suddenly she was dead and her daughter, too.

    Then she said with anger “they can’t escape, I won’t let them. My child and my grand child is haunting them because they have done this evil to them.”

    And her relatives said that she should not worry, she could rest in peace and when the monks stopped chanting she said “ I love you, mom” then she went out from her mom’s body”. From this incident Mr.Saitong believed that his wife tried to tell something, so he told her in front of her casket that please tell him who had killed her.

    Mr.Saitong Suksant said that he and all her relatives believed that the death of his wife and child were murdered because from the electricity authority said there was no electric leak.

    So the they suspected that the man in the next room who had quarreled with his wife had connected the wire in order to electrocute her.

    Pol.Lt.Col. Rattapak Sopap-investigation officer said that there are many clues in this case, regarding the leak and the statement from the Sattahip electrical authority that there was no electric leak and the medical autopsy said that Miss Shanita was killed by electocution and Yada Sanusan her daughter’s had a burn wound in her hand. The police is still waiting for further lab tests.

    The police also checked room # 3, the room next to the victim occupied by Mr. Wuttichai Kenkoke 35-year-old commuter driver of Thai Taffeta company, and Miss iptarntong Suebsuan 32- year-old, a couple from Khon Kaen, the tenants.

    In the room the police officers found a new 3 metres long wire, the head end and tail end were cut off, and when the police officers tried to connect it from the plug to the cloth line in the victims’s room, it was exactly the length. So the police collected the evidence before they let the tenants go.

    The chief of Sattahip police station has sent the investigation team to re-check the crime scene and will further interrogate all the neighbors.
