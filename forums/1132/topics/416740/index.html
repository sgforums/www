    Ghosts, who have learnt to be less attached to their previous "alive" experiences, are seeking out people to ask for help in leaving this Earth realm properly. If the ghosts happen to be in the exact location of your home/office, those people they are trying to contact would be yourself, your family member or colleagues.

    Ghosts can do many things to get the attention of the living. Below are the more common ways:

    1) enable themselves to be seen: you may refer to my previous posting on how to sense  them (http://www.sgforums.com/forums/1132/topics/406094)

    2) manipulate electrical equipment, eg. TV, radio, handphones, lights, etc. Reason is it's much easier for them to disrupt electrical energy, than physical objects.

    3) move physical objects: This is not that common, as it requires far more effort from them, as compared to electrical stuff.

    4) touch people, to let them know their presence: this is why some people feel as if someone or something is grabbing or touching their hands/legs/bodies, though no one is around. Sometimes, they may even sit on your sofa, chair or bed.

    5) attach themselves to a living person: this is often more subtle and most people are not even aware that they have "energetic attachments". All beings are basically energy. The effects of "energetic attachments" can range from increased emotions of irritation, uneasiness, anxiety, anger, depression, short-term memory loss. In more severe cases, people with such "energetic attachments" can become too depressed to the state of being suicidal.

    People need to understand that ghosts' consciousness is very different from when they were alive. Though most of them don't mean living people any harm, but they really are in a very confused state, and are somehow stuck in a time warp of the time when they died. If they died unpeacefully, they remain in that state as ghosts, where they still feel anger, fear, confused, threatened, abused, betrayed, etc.

    Ghosts are not able to rationalise properly like living people, as they only have memories of their final state. Communications with them can only be about topics relating to their final memories, eg. if they died because of being murdered, then we can talk to them about who murdered them, and advise them to forgive their murderer. But the ghost will not be able to communicate on other topics like the time when he/she last had outings with their families, etc, unless the ghost remained earthbound as a result of their over-strong attachment to their families.

    Spirits on the other hand are different, as they had already moved on to the Light successfully upon death, and merely returned to Earth realm to visit. They are considered safe and positive beings, and will have full memories of their previous life, unlike ghosts.

    There is no need to be afraid of ghosts or spirits, as long as people understand the reasons for their state of existence. Helping ghosts leave this Earth realm, and move on to the Light is of utmost compassion, and brings good karma to the people who choose to help them, one way or another.
