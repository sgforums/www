    About "Singapore My Home" Photography Competition

    The “Singapore My Home” Photography Competition is a nationwide competition, which provides an opportunity for Singaporeans and residents to share through their lenses what Singapore means to them. Nothing tugs at the heartstrings more than emotion-evoking images that reflect our common identity, and our shared memories as Singaporeans.

    The theme for Singapore My Home 2009 is "Come Together".

    All budding and seasoned shutterbugs are invited to capture the essence of what brings us together as Singaporeans. It may be friends getting together or special family moments, a game of soccer or a feast of local food, a celebration of joy or overcoming adversity together. Indeed, it is this spirit of togetherness that bonds us as Singaporeans, and reminds us that Singapore is truly Home.
