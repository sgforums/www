Temasek the only party in restructuring talks with CAO

  • sbst275
    4 Jan 05, 13:50
    SINGAPORE : Troubled China Aviation Oil says it has not received any offers from other mainland China firms, and that Temasek is the only party currently in restructuring talks with CAO.

    The company also says that Chinese creditor banks have not offered to write off its debt and recapitalise the company.

    CAO has to come up with a rescue plan by January 21.

    Monday's comments follows recent reports that a consortium of six China banks were planning to write off US$120 million of its debt.

    There was also talk that Air China could take a stake in CAO's parent company. - CNA
