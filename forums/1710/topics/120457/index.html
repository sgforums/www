    SINGAPORE : Air quality in Singapore improved further as rain continues to fall over the island.

    Overall, as of 4pm on Wednesday, the PSI level was 31 - within the "good" range.

    This is an improvement from the PSI level of 44 on Monday when the rain returned.

    The east of Singapore, which used to be badly affected by bush fires during the hot spell, is now enjoying the best air quality, with a PSI level of 19.

    The Meteorological Service has predicted that widespread showers will likely continue, in the mornings of coming days.

    However, slightly hazy conditions can also be expected. - CNA
