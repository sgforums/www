    Africa - Asia Svs

    EAF5 (New) - Calls PTP

    Sarfari 1 (New) - Calls PTP (East and West) and Singapore (East)

    Far East - Middle East Svs

    FM3 (New) - Calls PTP (West)

    Transpacific Svs

    TP2 - Laem Chabang (South East Asia - USA Sv)

    Summary of changes to Maersk/ MCC Tpt route rationalisation

    1) Straits - Jakarta

    1 weekly direct sailings to Jakarta from Tanjung Pelepas and vice versa
    4 weekly direct sailings to Jakarta from Singapore and vice versa

    2) Straits - Songkhla (Thailand) & Sihanvoukville (Cambodia)

    1 weekly direct sailing to Songkhla & Sihanoukville from Singapore and vice versa
    No more connection from Tanjung Pelepas to these 2 ports

    3) Straits - Thailand

    3 weekly direct sailings from Laem Chabang to Singapore and vice versa
    2 weekly direct sailings from Laem Chabang to Tanjung Pelepas and vice versa

    4) Straits - Kuantan

    1 weekly direct sailing from Kuantan to Singapore and vice versa replacing the Vietnam - Straits via Kuantan service
    No more connection from Tanjung Pelepas to Kuantan
    Dedicated point to point feeder service

    5)
    Dedicated sailings from Singapore and Tanjung Pelepas to Haiphong and Ho Chi Minh, no more double calling of the same ships

    6) Straits - India service
    Currently served by slot charter with Bengal Tiger lines. Weekly Calls to Singapore
