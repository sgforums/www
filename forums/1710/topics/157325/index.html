Meritus Negara Hotel offers mineral water swimming pool

  • sbst275
    13 Oct 05, 21:05
    SINGAPORE : Guests at the Meritus Negara Hotel are soaking in the luxury of a swimming pool filled with mineral water.

    The hotel, at Claymore Road, uses an ionisation technology to convert the original chlorine water into mineral water.

    Swimmers may not suffer from dry skin or reek of chlorine when they get out of the water.

    The hotel spent about $15,000 to install the ionisation system for the pool.

    It is believed to be the first hotel in Singapore to use mineral water for their swimming pool. - CNA/de
