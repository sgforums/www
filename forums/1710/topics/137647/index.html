Discounts being reduced at some petrol stations

  • sbst275
    17 Jun 05, 22:37
    SINGAPORE : A day after motorists had a field day at the petrol stations with discounts as high as 26 percent, petrol stations are now starting to reduce the discounts offered.

    Meanwhile Singapore Petroleum Company's discount remains at 23 percent.

    Caltex confirmed with Channel NewsAsia that its pump stations would now offer a discount of 10 percent from 11pm on Friday.

    Some Shell petrol stations have also started to lower their discount to 10 percent. - CNA/ms
