SingTel to launch 3G trial services from Friday

  • sbst275
    22 Sep 04, 13:50
    SINGAPORE : Singapore Telecommunications Ltd announced it would begin a month-long trial of high-speed third-generation mobile services from Friday.

    It has selected 150 consumers and corporate customers to take part in the trial, which will last from September 24 to Ocotber 24.

    Advertisement

    Those taking part in the trial will be provided with a 3G handset and have their existing mobile phone numbers ported and tied to a 3G SIM card.

    They will also be given free usage of 3G services such as local video calls and video streaming for the duration of the trial.

    During the trial, customers will experience seamless 2G/3G handover when they move to places without 3G coverage. The handover experience forms an important part of the trial.

    Third-generation mobile technology allows voice, video and data transmission many times faster than current technology allows.

    Singapore is set to be second Asian country to commercially launch GSM-based 3G services after Hutchison Whampoa Ltd began selling the service in Hong Kong.

    Japan and South Korea already sell high-speed services using a different technology. - CNA
