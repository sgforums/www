    MediaCorp artistes hit the green to drive charity fund raiser

    SINGAPORE: Fifteen MediaCorp artistes hit the green on September 11 to raise funds for the Association for Persons with Special Needs.

    The artistes are part of the Celebrities Sports Club, which organised its first Charity Golf Tournament on Thursday, and among them were Zheng Ge Ping and Constance Song.

    Participants of the tournament will not only get to play golf with the local stars, but also stand a chance to win a Porsche Cayman S and Corum watches.

    The club hoped to raise up to S$100,000 for the cause from a series of activities, including a charity auction late Thursday, which will feature items like vintage wine, fine watches and art works by students from the beneficiary.

    Corporations chipped in for the cause and MediaCorp was among the sponsors by giving a 4,000-dollar sponsorship.
