Teen student murdered

  • QX179R
    19 Mar 09, 11:36

    A 14-YEAR-OLD student was found dead with a stab wound in her torso in a Woodlands flat early on Thursday.

    Police were alerted to the death at Block 852 Woodlands Sreet 83 at around 2.50am.

    The victim, a Chinese girl, was pronounced dead by paramedics at the scene at around 3.06am.

    A 51 year-old woman, believed to be the girl's mother, has been arrested.

    Police have classified the case as murder, and will charge the suspect in court on Friday.
