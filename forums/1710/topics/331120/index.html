New locations for Yishun and Choa Chu Kang libraries

  • QX179R
    20 Sep 08, 00:10

    New locations for Yishun and Choa Chu Kang libraries from Nov

    SINGAPORE: The Yishun and Choa Chu Kang public libraries will be relocated to larger premises in November.

    To facilitate the move, Yishun Library will be closed from October 13, while the Choa Chu Kang branch will close on November 1.

    The National Library Board said during the temporary closure, library users will not be able to access certain services. These include reservations and book drop services.

    Users can return their library items at other branches.

    The new Yishun Library will be located at Northpoint Shopping Centre and Choa Chu Kang Library will re-open at Lot 1 Shoppers' Mall. - CNA/vm
