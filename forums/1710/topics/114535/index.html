Singapore to unveil 2005 budget on Feb 18

  • sbst275
    27 Jan 05, 15:21
    SINGAPORE: Singapore will deliver its 2005 annual budget on February 18, says the Clerk of Parliament.

    The budget statement will be delivered by Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong, who is also the Finance Minister.

    He will deliver the statement during the parliament sitting that day. - CNA
