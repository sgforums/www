    SINGAPORE : Two foreigners were caught distributing election-related pamphlets at Bedok North Avenue 2 on Saturday.

    Police say they received a complaint about it at 1pm.

    Officers despatched there found that the two Chinese nationals, both in their 30s, were employed to distribute the pamphlets.

    They were not arrested but the case is being investigated by police.

    Under the Parliamentary Elections Act, a person who is not a Singapore citizen is prohibited from conducting election activity.

    Due to the ongoing investigations, police say they are not able to disclose further details. - CNA/ms
