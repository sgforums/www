270,000 applications received for NDP tickets, funpacks

  • sbst275
    6 Jun 05, 21:21
    SINGAPORE : About 270,000 applicants had asked for NDP 2005 tickets or funpacks when applications closed on Sunday.

    More than half applied through the National Day Parade website.

    Organisers say most were eyeing the coveted tickets to the parade at the Padang.

    Balloting will be completed by Wednesday, June 15 and successful applicants will be notified by telephone between June 16 and 22.

    They can collect their NDP tickets or funpack coupons from the Ticket Collection Centre at "The Pavilion on the Square" in Far East Square from June 24 to July 3, between 10am and 8pm daily.

    They should bring their identification cards when collecting their tickets.

    Those collecting on behalf of others must also bring their identification records.
