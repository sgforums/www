Man Shu Sum leaving MediaCorp Studios to join MDA

  • sbst275
    28 Apr 05, 20:57
    SINGAPORE : The Executive Advisor of MediaCorp Studios, Mr Man Shu Sum, will be leaving the company to join the Media Development Authority of Singapore (MDA) at the end of May.

    He would have spent five months with the company, joining after the media merger.

    His role was to advise the MediaCorp Studios on the integration of production staff and operations.

    Mr Man said he was heartened to note that former staff of SPH MediaWorks had settled in well at MediaCorp.

    And he looks forward to the challenges of his appointment at MDA as it helps Singapore's media industry expand overseas.

    MediaCorp Group CEO Mr Ernest Wong said Mr Man had been an asset to the company and was critical in ensuring the smooth transition during the merger. - CNA/de
