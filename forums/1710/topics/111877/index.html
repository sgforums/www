Ren Ci Charity Show raises $6.6 million

  • sbst275
    10 Jan 05, 14:53
    SINGAPORE : Venerable Shi Ming Yi did it again, with yet another amazing feat for the Ren Ci Charity Show on Sunday night.

    After abseiling down Suntec Tower 2 in 2003 and immersing himself in a ton of ice last year, he performed a stunt where he balanced atop a 12-metre tower on a plate measuring 20cm by 20cm for 1 hour and 45 minutes.

    The number of calls from donors jumped dramatically whenever he appeared on screen during the show, which was broadcast live on MediaCorp TV Channel U.

    His stunt was one of many performed during the third annual charity spectacular. In all, $6.6 million was raised for Ren Ci Hospital and Medical Centre.

    The hospital, founded by Venerable Shi, has been caring for chronically-ill patients since 1994. The hospital also intends to contribute $500,000 to the the tsunami disaster relief effort.

    The charity show was also a milestone for MediaCorp TV Holdings - the result of the MediaCorp TV and SPH MediaWorks merger. It was the first major event featuring almost 40 artistes drawn from both stables.

    A number of international celebrities also took part, including Hong Kong actress Nancy Sit, Taiwanese singer Richie Ren, Malaysian singers Z Chen and Fish Leong, and local singers J J Lin and A Do. - CNA
