DPM Tan to retire from Cabinet on 1 Sep: PM Lee

  • sbst275
    27 Aug 05, 18:16
    Deputy Prime Minister Tony Tan, who is also the Coordinating Minister for Security and Defence, will retire from the Cabinet on 1 September.

    DPM and Law Minister Professor S Jayakumar will take on the additional responsibility of Coordinating Minister for National Security.

    Wong Kan Seng will become DPM while continuing to be the Home Affairs Minister.

    A new face - MP Gan Kim Yong - will be appointed Minister of State for Education and Manpower, from 1 October.

    These changes were announced by Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong in a statement on Saturday.

    PM Lee thanked Dr Tan for his many years of service to the Government and the nation. - CNA/ir
