Comfort taxi drivers to get bonus to cope with higher oil pr

  • sbst275
    23 Nov 04, 19:50
    SINGAPORE : All Comfort taxi drivers will be given a special one-time bonus to help them cope with higher operating costs resulting from high oil prices.

    The handout will involve 16,098 taxi hirers.

    ComfortDelgro Corporation said on Monday the special one-time bonus will range from $240 to $360 per driver depending on the number of years of service.

    It will be given out in four equal instalments in January and February 2005.

    All hirers under the ComfortDelGro Group - comprising Comfort Transportation, CityCab and Yellow-Top Cab - will be eligible for the bonus as long as they are still in service during the payment dates.

    Taxi hirers are encouraged to share the benefits with their relief drivers.

    The bonus scheme is the latest in a series of measures by ComfortDelGro aimed at rendering assistance to its hirers. - CNA
