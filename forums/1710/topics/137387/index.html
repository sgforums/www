Body parts found in two boxes in Kallang River

  • sbst275

    SINGAPORE: Two boxes containing body parts, believed to be of a woman, were found in the Kallang River on Thursday.

    A cleaner found the first cardboard box at about 9am during a low tide, police said.

    It contained the lower abdomen of a woman, wrapped in a translucent plastic bag.

    A few metres from the first box, another box was found containing the upper torso, without the head and legs.

    A search is on to locate the remaining body parts, and the police coast guard has been called in to comb the Kallang River waters. - CNA/ir
