Body of 20-year-old man found floating in Marina Bay

  • sbst275

    SINGAPORE : An old man's fishing trip at Marina Bay was cut short suddenly when he found a body floating in the water.

    He first saw the body of the 20-year-old man in the shallow end of the bay.

    Police were notified of the incident just before 2pm on Saturday.

    Officers at the scene found no visible injuries on the swollen body when they brought it ashore.

    But they did find a work permit, which stated the man was a foreign worker from Myanmar.

    He was wearing a grey shirt, black pants and socks.

    The case has been unclassified as unnatural death and investigations are underway. - CNA /ct
