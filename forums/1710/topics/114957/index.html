MediaCorp wins 3 awards at New York Festivals 2005

  • sbst275
    29 Jan 05, 21:29
    SINGAPORE : MediaCorp has won three Bronze World Medals at the prestigious New York Festivals 2005 International Television Programming and Promotion Awards.

    The winning entries were MediaCorp Channel 5's situation comedy "Achar" on inter-racial marriage.

    Its Home of Blockbusters on-air campaign also received an award, as did Channel 8's promotional trailer for the movie "A Man Called Hero".

    14 other entries from MediaCorp received Finalist certificates.

    The New York Festivals is the world's largest awards competition for broadcast and non-broadcast media.

    Into its 48th year, it received entries from 35 countries. - CNA
