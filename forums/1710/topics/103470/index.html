    SINGAPORE: The results are out: the first Singapore Idol is going to be either Taufik Batisah or Sylvester Sim.

    Olinda Cho was voted out after Thursday's Movie and Musical episode.

    The result was announced on Friday.

    Olinda had the lowest number of votes, but her elimination disappointed hordes of fans who have been supporting her for more than nine weeks.

    NSman Taufik Batisah and 21-year-old Sylvester Sim will compete at the grand final to be held at the Singapore Indoor Stadium on Wednesday, 1 December.

    Tickets will be given away through contests held on MediaCorp's various TV channels, Perfect 10 radio station, 8 Days and the TODAY newspaper. - CNA
