    SINGAPORE : NSmen will soon be able to do their Individual Physical Proficiency Tests at SAFRA's swanky new S$30 million clubhouse.

    Early next year, NSmen doing their IPPT will be running on treadmills at SAFRA Bukit Merah's new clubhouse in Mount Faber.

    Currently only two SAFRA clubs, Toa Payoh and Tampines, conduct the indoor IPPT.

    But besides working out, NSmen will also have a whole range of interesting activities to choose from.

    The 1.4 hectare club, which brands itself as an entertainment centre, boasts facilities like a 34-lane bowling alley, a sunken pool bar, and even a disco-cum-KTV lounge.

    The 22-year-old SAFRA Bukit Merah club moved out of its old premises late last month. - CNA
