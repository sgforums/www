Parts of Chinatown closed to traffic for Chinese New Year ev

  • sbst275
    12 Jan 05, 17:10
    SINGAPORE : Parts of New Bridge Road and Eu Tong Sen Street will be closed to traffic between 3 pm and 11pm for the Chinese New Year Light-up Ceremony in Chinatown this Saturday.

    After the light-up, several roads in the area will continue to be closed to vehicular traffic during the the Chinese New Year Bazaar from January 16 to February 8.

    The roads affected include Pagoda Street, Temple Street, Trengannu Road, Smith Street, Sago Street, Dickenson Hill Road and Banda Street.

    On weekdays, the roads will be closed from 6 pm to midnight; on weekends, till 1 am. - CNA
