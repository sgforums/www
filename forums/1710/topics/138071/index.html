Singapore deploys RSAF tanker aircraft to help in Iraq's rec

  • sbst275
    20 Jun 05, 17:06
    Singapore is deploying a Republic of Singapore Air Force KC-135 tanker aircraft to support the multinational effort for the reconstruction of Iraq.

    The Defence Ministry said the Chief of Defence Force, Lieutenant-General Ng Yat Chung, officiated at the send-off ceremony at Changi Air Base on Monday morning.

    The KC-135 detachment comprises 35 personnel.

    During its three-month deployment, it will conduct air-to-air refuelling missions in support of the multinational forces.

    This is the second time that Singapore has deployed a KC-135 tanker aircraft to help with the reconstruction of Iraq.

    Since 2003, Singapore has also deployed two Landing Ship Tanks and a C-130 transport aircraft for this effort. - CNA /ch
