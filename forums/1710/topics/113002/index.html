    SINGAPORE : Fans of local productions can look forward to an eye feast beginning January 2005.

    Arts Central will launch a total of 11 new productions showcasing fresh and original programmes all made in Singapore.

    Viewers can expect a walk on the wild side, a look at plush urban hangouts and also witness some high energy dance moves.

    Arts Central says these local productions demonstrate the artform of television craft and hopes to capture both the local and expatriate audience.

    Most of these programmes will be screened weeknights at 9pm or 10:30pm. - CNA
