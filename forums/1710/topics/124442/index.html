Temporary glitch in StarHub Cable services affect subscriber

  • sbst275
    31 Mar 05, 08:58
    SINGAPORE : Many areas in Singapore experienced problems accessing their StarHub cable services on Wednesday afternoon.

    StarHub confirmed that the problem was due to a technical glitch in the eastern parts of Singapore.

    The fault was caused by a cable cut by a Land Transport Authority contractor working on the Kallang Paya Lebar Expressway at Kolam Ayer.

    The company said the cable TV services were restored by 5pm and the modem services about an hour later.

    StarHub said it was unable to ascertain the number of customers who were affected.

    Their investigations showed that cable TV services were affected in Paya Lebar, Hougang, Changi, Pasir Ris,Yio Chu Kang and Tampines, while cable modem services were down in Paya Lebar and Hougang.

    Channel NewsAsia had received numerous hotline calls from those affected in Hougang, Serangoon, Tampines, Changi, Lorong Ah Soo, Sengkang, Pasir Ris and Simei. - CNA
