GCE O level results to be released on Monday Feb 28

  • sbst275
    24 Feb 05, 14:55
    SINGAPORE : Results of the 2004 GCE O (General Certificate of Education Ordinary) Level Examination will be released next Monday, February 28th.

    The Education Ministry says school candidates may collect their results from their schools at 2pm on that day.

    Private candidates will be informed of their results by post.

    They can also check their results online from 2.00pm at the e-Services website.

    Students hoping to go on to the polytechnics, junior colleagues or to the Institute of Technical Education can start applying from 3pm that day.

    The Minstry will be conducting the Joint Admissions Exercise over 5 working days till 4pm on March 4.

    Applications should be submitted online through the JAE website.

    For more information on the JAE, applicants can also call the MOE's Customer Service Centre at 6872-2220 during office hours

    The results of the application are tentatively scheduled for release on Mar 23 through the same website.

    Applicants will require a valid SingPass in order to access the website.

    Applicants can also call the Education Ministry hotline at 6691-2131 for more information. - CNA
