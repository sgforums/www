Foreign workers to get free IDD calls to tsunami-hit areas

  • sbst275
    31 Dec 04, 09:41
    SINGAPORE : Foreign workers in Singapore who have families in tsunami-hit areas can call home for free, courtesy of a local company Telecom United.

    The workers can make the free IDD calls from Telecom United's telephone lounges in three foreign worker dormitories.

    They are the Five Star, SCAL and Westlite Dormitories.

    Alternatively they can also get the service at authorised Telecom United Shops along Serangoon Road.

    These are located at 115, 178 or 322 Serangoon Road,133 Dunlop Street and 12 Dalhousie Lane.

    The company says S$50,000 worth of IDD talk time will be given away on a first come first serve basis. - CNA
