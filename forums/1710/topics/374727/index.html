    A website allegedly owned by a Singaporean escort agency offers 22 women for hire in its online 'catalogue', complete with statistics of each escort and the fees she charges.

    Featuring a simple, blog-like layout, the website offers potential customers a choice of Singaporean and Korean models, as well as 'girls-next-door' of both Singaporean and foreign nationality.

    The website describes the 'girls-next-door' as women who do not qualify as models, but are nevertheless pretty and able to offer good service. Escorts in this category include 'office ladies' and students.

    Customers wanting to engage the services of these escorts must be prepared to part with a significant amount of money, as rates start from $250 an hour for a 'girl-next-door' and can go up to $1,500 an hour for Singaporean models.

    The website also listed among its escorts a 'commercial model' who works for well-known magazines like CLEO and Her World. Her fee was the highest, commanding a $1,500 hourly fee.

    The website also stated that many escorts are willing to offer 'overnight' services. The 10-hour stays range from $1,000 for a Mongolian or Chinese escort, to $4,500 for the CLEO model. Two of the escorts listed on the site even offered to perform certain sex acts should they be hired together.

    Each escort's online profile comes with at least three photos, a short description of her character, her stats such as age, bust size and weight, as well as her fees.

    However, all the pictures are cropped such that they do not reveal the models' face. The website states that it does this to protect the identity of the women.

    Those interested to engage the services of any escort on the site are required to call or send a text message to a man named 'Charlie', whose Singaporean mobile phone number is listed throughout the site.

    Detailed information on the booking of its escorts and the different payment and pickup modes are also clearly stated.

    In the 'FAQ' or Frequently Asked Questions page, the site states that customers who spend more than $5,000 within a three-month time span or those who leave a $3,000 credit with the agency will be given 'Gold memberships' and are entitled to certain benefits and discounts.

    Repeat and frequent customers may even be promoted to become 'Gentlemen Club' members, where they will have access to special escorts such as FHM models.

    Among the models and escorts available to the general public are a Eurasian model, a model who is taking a part-time business degree and an Indonesian Chinese secretary.
