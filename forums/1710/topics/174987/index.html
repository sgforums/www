Singapore's Budget Day on Feb 17

  • sbst275
    20 Jan 06, 20:45
    The Prime Minister and Minister for Finance, Mr Lee Hsien Loong, will deliver the FY2006 Budget Statement in Parliament on 17 February.

    Singaporeans can now visit the Budget 2006 website to give their suggestions for the Budget.

    Budget is about making choices, each with its own tradeoffs.

    What are our national priorities? How do we balance competing, and often equally compelling, needs?

    Singaporeans can learn more about the difficult trade-offs that have to be made through "Voice Your Choice", a Web-based tool which allows them to set spending priorities and find out if they are running a budget surplus, balance or deficit. - CNA/ir
