Malaysian King to make state visit to Singapore

  • sbst275
    20 Jan 06, 20:47
    SINGAPORE : Malaysia's King, Tuanku Syed Sirajuddin, will visit Singapore from January 23 to 25.

    He will be here at the invitation of President SR Nathan.

    During his three-day state visit, the King will meet Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong.

    President Nathan will also host a state banquet in his honour.

    In Singapore, the King will visit a number of facilities and attraction sites.

    These include the SAF's Armour Headquarters, the Biopolis complex and the HDB Hub at Toa Payoh.

    Some 60 officials, including his wife, are expected to accompany the King on this visit.

    The King will be visiting Indonesia first before arriving in Singapore.

    It will be his first visit to Singapore. - CNA /ct
