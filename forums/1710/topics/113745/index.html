Star-studded launch party held for MediaCorp Radio's Lush 99

  • sbst275
    22 Jan 05, 17:16
    SINGAPORE : MediaCorp's newest radio station, Lush 99.5 FM, held a star-studded launch party at the Victoria Theatre on Thursday night.

    It was a night of glitz and glamour as radio personalities and TV celebrities congregated on the red carpet for the launch of Lush 99.5 FM.

    Lush DJ Chris Ho said, "Lush is about sexy central smooth music, hip and cool music, and I don't think we have ever had a station like this in Singapore."

    The station will pump out a mix of new age, down-tempo chill, bossa nova, and nu-jazz 24 hours a day.

    Listeners can also expect lifestyle tips from former ShowBuzz host Vivien Tan.

    "Where to go, what to do, where to eat, where to shop -- that's my favourite part," she said.

    Lush 99.5 will embody the urban chic and targets listeners between 25 and 40 years old who are keen on the arts, wellness and travel. - CNA
