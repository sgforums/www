10 NSmen hurt after 5-tonne truck lands on its side

  • sbst275
    13 Oct 04, 15:40
    SINGAPORE : Ten National Servicemen were injured on Wednesday morning when a 5-tonne truck they were in landed on its side.

    Mindef says the accident happened on Pulau Tekong at about 7.20am, when the truck was transporting stores.

    It is believed the truck hit a tree.

    There were 13 servicemen on board the truck.

    Two servicemen sustained fractures and facial injuries and were evacuated by helicopter to Singapore General Hospital.

    Mindef says one of the men is a regular servicemen and the other an NSman.

    It says both men are in a stable condition.

    Eight others, who suffered minor abrasions, were treated as outpatients at the Pulau Tekong Medical Centre.

    Mindef is conducting an investigation into the incident. - CNA
