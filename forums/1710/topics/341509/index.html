New market and food centre for Sengkang planned

  • QX179R
    23 Dec 08, 14:32

    Sengkang residents can look forward to a new market and food centre come 2010.

    The Housing & Development Board has launched a tender for a Market and Food Centre Development at Sengkang Square/Sengkang East Road.

    The land parcel is located opposite the Sengkang MRT/LRT station and bus interchange.

    The project will offer residents an estimated 40 market stalls selling a variety of fresh market produce and 50 cooked food stalls.

    This development arose from the Forum on HDB Heartware, led by Senior Minister of State for National Development, Ms Grace Fu last year.

    Amongst others, the Forum recommended to resume building of wet markets and hawker centres, promote community bonding and strengthen local identity. --938Live
