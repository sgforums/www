    Reasons for hiring Poly Grad
    Well, for a job that a poly grad can handle, there isn't much reason for an employer to pay more to hire a uni grad. Even if a uni grad is willing to take lesser pay now, the employer will forsee the uni grad switching job when the economy gets better.

    Reasons for hiring FTs
    For a job that requires a uni grad to handle, the employers are tempted to hire the FTs who ask for lesser pay (e.g 2k for master degree holder).

    Reasons for experience
    1) Employer also wanted to hire those with experience is because they are proven.
    2) And also the employer worries that the person leaves after the employer spends so much time training the person.

    BTW, if statistics show that a poly grad get job easier than a uni grad, i think it is probably due to a poly grad lowering his/her expectations (e.g Engrng poly grads doing selling etc).
