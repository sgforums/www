Joint Admissions Exercise posting results out on March 22

  • sbst275
    18 Mar 05, 23:16
    SINGAPORE : The Ministry of Education will be releasing the posting results for the 2005 Joint Admissions Exercise on March 22.

    Students can obtain their results from 8am through various ways, either via the Internet at http://www.moe.gov.sg/esp/schadm/jae or by calling the MOE hotline at 6691-2131.

    They can also see the posting lists displayed at Junior Colleges and Millennia Institute.

    All applicants will receive a copy of the results by mail.

    Students posted to JCs and Millennia Institute should report to the respective institution on March 23 at 7.30am.

    Those posted to a polytechnic or Institute of Technical Education will receive further instructions and an enrolment package by mail. - CNA
