Russia's Transaero Airlines starts flights from Changi

  • sbst275

    SINGAPORE: Russia's Transaero Airlines has become the seventh new airline this year to start flights from Changi Airport.

    It will fly once a week non-stop between Moscow and Singapore, re-establishing a link between Changi Airport and the Russian capital.

    The new service comes as both sides signalled a readiness to step up bilateral business links.

    In fact, on 15 October, Russia's biggest association of small and medium-sized enterprises signed a memorandum of understanding with the Singapore Business Federation to pave the way for greater co-operation beween their business communities.

    Apart from Transaero Airlines, the other airlines which started operations at Changi Airport this year are First Cambodia Airlines, Shandong Airlines, Austrian Airlines, Thai AirAsia, ValuAir and Tiger Airways. - CNA
