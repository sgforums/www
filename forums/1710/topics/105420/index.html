    Singapore : The region's top broadcasting talents and programmes were honoured at the Asian Television Awards ceremony at the University Cultural Centre on Thursday night.

    And MediaCorp actress, Selena Tan won the award for Best Comedy Performance by an Actress.

    Selena won for her role in an episode of Daddy's Girls' entitled "Violet's Old Flame".

    But she was not in town to receive the award.

    Korea's Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation's Channel 11 won three awards, including Channel of the Year.

    Japan also won big, with six broadcasting stations collecting at least seven awards in all.

    This year the ATV awards committee received more than 1,500 entries from 15 countries.

    But only 155 entries were shortlisted, with MediaCorp receiving 20 nominations. - CNA
