1 dead, 1 injured at Jurong West

  • QX179R
    3 Mar 09, 15:24

    A dead woman and an injured man were found at an HDB block in Jurong West this morning.

    Police received a call at 5.50 in the morning, reporting of a body lying motionless on the parapet between the 2nd and 3rd floor of Block 658A Jurong West Street 65.

    The victim, believed to be a Thai national in her late 20s, was pronounced dead at the scene by the paramedics at about 6.10 AM.

    A Singaporean Chinese man in his early 20s was also picked up at the scene.

    The man had sustained an arm injury and was conveyed conscious to the National University Hospital.

    Police investigations are still ongoing.
