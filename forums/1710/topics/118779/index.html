    SINGAPORE : The Public Utilities Board has a new tagline -- "Water for All: Conserve, Value, Enjoy".

    Unveiling the tagline on Monday, Minister for the Environment and Water Resources, Dr Yaacob Ibrahim, says it embodies the PUB's new approach to involve the people as well as the private and public sectors in the management of Singapore's water resources.

    "Water For All" encapsulates PUB's role in making certain that Singapore's four sources of water -- water from local catchments, imported water, NEWater and desalinated water -- never run dry.

    "Conserve, Value, Enjoy" conveys the critical dimensions of Singapore's water management strategy.

    Dr Yaacob says to provide water for all, every Singaporean should play his or her part to conserve water, keep Singapore's water catchments clean, and build a closer relationship with water through enjoying its water resources.

    If everyone does this, Singaporeans can and will have sufficient water for all uses -- for living, for life, for industry.

    Dr Yaacob was speaking at a ceremony to celebrate the completion of tunnelling works for the Deep Tunnel Sewerage System. - CNA
