SLA to start exhumation of graves

    SLA to start exhumation of graves at Kwong Hou Sua Cemetery

    The Singapore Land Authority will soon start the final phase of the exhumation of graves at the Kwong Hou Sua Teochew Cemetery off Woodlands Road.

    The land, which is about 70,000 square metres, is planned for industrial developments.

    Last month, the Land Transport Authority started the earlier phase of the exhumation for the portion of the land required for the Downtown Line depot development.

    About 1,000 graves will be affected.
