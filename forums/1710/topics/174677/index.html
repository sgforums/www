Naked body found floating in Singapore River

  • sbst275

    SINGAPORE : The naked body of an Indian man was found in the Singapore River on Wednesday morning.

    He was found floating face down near the Asian Civilisations Museum, 10 metres from shore.

    The man, in his mid 20s, was with two other friends at that time.

    He apparently decided to go swimming and jumped in alone.

    After his friends realised he was missing, they called the police.

    The Police Coast Guard retrieved the body.

    The two friends are still in shock and have yet to assist police with investigations.

    Police have classified this as unnatural death. - CNA /ct
