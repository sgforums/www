    SINGAPORE : Red packets and new year goodies may have ushered in the new Year of the Rooster.

    But not quite for some Toa Payoh residents who called Channel NewsAsia News' hotline about a lost monkey on Sunday.

    The monkey was spotted at Block 222 Toa Payoh Lorong 8.

    It charmed residents by climbing coconut trees.

    The monkey even caught the attention of the Police who arrived at the scene, but scampered off before it could be caught.

    The Police are contacting the Agri-Food and Veterinary Authority and the Singapore Zoo to look into the matter. - CNA
