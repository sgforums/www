Malaysian minister in S'pore for inaugural MediaCorp-RTM pro

  • sbst275
    23 Apr 05, 08:12
    SINGAPORE : Malaysian Information Minister Abdul Kadir Sheikh Fadzir will be in Singapore for the inaugural Muzika Ekstravaganza - a joint production by MediaCorp and Radio Televisyen Malaysia (RTM).

    The Malaysian Minister is visiting Singapore over the weekend (April 23-24) at the invitation of Minister for Information, Communications and the Arts, Lee Boon Yang.

    Both ministers will officiate the launch of the musical gala on Saturday.

    The show will be telecast 'live' simultaneously to both countries.

    The idea of a joint production goes back to the 1980s when both national broadcasters produced the popular Rampai Sari.

    In December last year, the two ministers met in Kuala Lumpur and agreed to resume the annual variety show. - CNA/ms
