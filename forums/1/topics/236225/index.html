    Set your browser not to.

    Those using IE, sorry, no help on that. IE click on clear, means clear everything (except cache)

    Those on Firefox, go to Tools > Options

    Click on Privacy at the top

    Under Cookies, tick Accept Cookies from sites

    From the Keep Until drop-down box, select They Expire

    For those who set Firefox to clear your private data each time you close it, click on Settings

    Untick Cookies box

    Other browsers, go and find the similar settings...

    Those using ATF Cleaner

    Untick Cookies box for Main, Firefox and Opera

    Those using CCleaner

    Click on Options on the left

    Select Cookies box

    Choose the cookies you want to keep and press -> to move it to the keep column

    Edit: On phpBB forums, to keep yourself logged in, tick this box: Log me on automatically each visit

    On other forums, there should be a similar box. Tick that to stay logged on

    For sgF, this function doesn't exist...
