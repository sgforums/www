    To have your own avatar, you need to be a premier member.

    To have a smiley from another URL, you need to get the URL of the smiley, right click properties and copy the whole line including the .gif or .jpg.

    eg.
    the url of this smiley is
    http://members.lycos.co.uk/kohanson/smiley/stunt.gif

    you will need to do this:
    [i[/i]mg]http://members.lycos.co.uk/kohanson/smiley/stunt.gif[/img]
