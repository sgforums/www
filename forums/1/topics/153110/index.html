    Before voting for a Featured Topic, please ensure that the first post meets minimum standards.

    These minimum standards are:

    It should be well written.
    It should not be a 'chat' topic.

    Characteristics that should lead to a 'Yes' vote are:

    A topic that sgForums is PROUD to show the world!
    Interesting
    Relevant
    Intelligent

    Characteristics that should lead to a 'No' vote are:

    Stupid
    About someone's personal life no one else would want to know about
    "Everybody let's vote for this topic to become featured!"

    FAQs

    What are Featured Topics?
    Featured Topics will help visitors locate the most relevant and interesting topics by putting them on the front page, and at the top of the forum pages.

    How come no matter how many people I get to vote for my topic it still isn't featured?
    Your topic probably fits all the characteristics of a 'No' vote and so all your 'sarporters' have their votes ignored forever.
