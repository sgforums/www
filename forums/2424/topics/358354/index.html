    PARK JI-SUNG last night verbally agreed a new £50,000-a-week deal that will keep him at Manchester United for another four years. 

    The winger, 28, has 12 months left on his current contract — but will put pen to paper on improved terms next month. 

    Park, a £4million buy from PSV Eindhoven four years ago, said: “I have no reason to move. I play at the best club in the world.” 

    United hope to agree a new deal with keeper Ben Foster, who is a target for Tottenham.
