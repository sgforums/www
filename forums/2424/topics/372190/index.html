    Terriers take Drinkwater

     

    Red Devils youngster to take in spell in League One

    Manchester United youngster Danny Drinkwater has joined League One outfit Huddersfield Town on loan.

    The 19-year-old, who is held in high regard at Old Trafford, joins the Terriers on a season-long deal.

    Drinkwater will be hoping to pick up invaluable first-team experience during his time at the Galpharm Stadium, and judging by Lee Clark's comments he should be in luck.

    "Danny is a player we have tracked for a long time," the Terriers boss told the club's official website.

    "He can play in all four positions across midfield and I'm very happy that we have struck a deal."
