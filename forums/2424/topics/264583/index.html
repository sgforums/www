    Young defender Gerard Pique is looking forward to a chance to prove himself at Manchester United.

    Pique's loan spell at Real Zaragoza ends on the 30th of June, and though the Uefa Cup bound club side are keen to hang on him, the player himself sees himself at Old Trafford.

    "I have one foot, almost two, inside Manchester United," said the former Barcelona youth team player.

    "In football anything can happen. However, this doesn't mean that the last word will be left to Zaragoza.

    "It's logical that I will continue my career in England."
