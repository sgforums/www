    Veron reveals brawl with Reds captain

    GARY NEVILLE appears to have previous when it comes to spats with Argentinians - as former Manchester United star Juan Sebastian Veron explains.

     

    "One Christmas, me and my team-mates at United went out to celebrate," he recalls.

    "We went to a lot of bars in a bus. I invited Argentine rugby players Agustin Pichot and Felipe Contepomi, who were playing in England.

    "I took a microphone and started to sing 'Let's go Argentina' and then Neville started to sing for England.

     

    "So I hit Neville on the head with the microphone and there was a big fight between me, Pichot, Contepomi and the English. There was blood everywhere."

    "When I came home, I had loads of scratches and my shirt was ripped."
