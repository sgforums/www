Giggs primed for Liverpool test

  • omgukilledkenny

    Evergreen winger Ryan Giggs feels it is important for Manchester United to get a positive result away to Liverpool on Sunday.

    Giggs does not expect Sunday's Premier League matches to have a decisive impact on the title race, but is hoping to emerge from Merseyside with a positive outcome.

    "I'll start preparing now for the Liverpool game if I'm not involved in midweek," said Giggs, who is likely to be rested for the Champions League trip to Roma, in the Daily Mirror.

    "It's such a big game, especially at Anfield, and we know we're going to have to play well to come away with a result.

    "The top four are all playing each other next Sunday, so no one's going to lose the title on the day, but it's important for everyone to try to get a decent result."
