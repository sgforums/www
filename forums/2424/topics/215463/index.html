    Foster tipped to be the best

    Adrian Boothroyd has backed Ben Foster to become the best goalkeeper in the world.

    Foster is spending his second season on loan at Watford to build up his first-team experience.

    After impressing in The Championship last season, he has also made an impact with a series of strong displays at Premiership level this term.

    He has also been included in Steve McClaren's England squads. Hornets boss Boothroyd is a huge fan of the keeper and believes Foster has all the attributes to be the game's best.

    "He can be the best in the world, he's got everything, "He has terrific distribution from either foot - he used to be a left winger up to 15.

    "And he's got a safe pair of hands, a big personality and his frame is fantastic. All he needs is the time. He's maturing like good wine and will be a top, top player.

    "This is the best place for him to be at this particular time, it's a learning environment where he can make mistakes, recover and get better.

    "And he's training with the likes of Chris Kirkland and Paul Robinson on England duty. Both those guys are terrific goalkeepers, no matter what other people might say."
