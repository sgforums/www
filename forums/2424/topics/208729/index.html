Robinho unsettled at Capello's Real Madrid

  • Dead_Man_Inc

    tribalfooball.com - September 05, 2006

    Wing wizard Robinho's future at Real Madrid is in serious doubt.

    The Brazilian, who dazzled in his country's 3-0 whitewash of Argentina in London on Sunday, feels he doesn't have the confidence of Real coach Fabio Capello and is concerned over the arrival of Jose Reyes from Arsenal.

    Robinho doesn't fit into Capello's strict team structure and a close friend told AS: "If Robinho is unhappy and doesn't believe he has the confidence of the coach, then he is only 50 per cent the player he should be."

    The Brazilian is also said to have told another close friend: "I am going to struggle under the orders of Capello."

    Robinho was the subject of interest from AC Milan over the summer and also can count on Manchester United boss Sir Alex Ferguson and Arsenal's Arsene Wenger as long-term admirers.
