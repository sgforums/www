West Ham Want United Defender

    Silvestre has been at United since signing from Inter Milan in 1999 and has won four league titles and two FA Cups with the club.

    However, he suffered a serious knee injury last season which limited him to just three Premier League starts and ensured he spent most of the season watching from the sidelines.

    Whilst Silvestre is a versatile defender able to play as a left back or centre back, United are well stocked in both areas and possessed the best defensive record in the league last season.

    Indeed with Nemanja Vidic and Rio Ferdinand forming an impressive pairing at centre back and with Patrice Evra in superb form at left back, Silvestre’s days at Old Trafford appear to be numbered.

    The People reports that West Ham have taken notice of this fact and are willing to meet United’s valuation of £2million for the player who will be out of contract at the end of next season.

    West Ham may be in the hunt for a centre back as reports linking Anton Ferdinand with a move away from Upton Park continue to circulate, with Tottenham Hotspur believed to be favourites to sign the defender.

    Silvestre, meanwhile, had been linked with a move back to France with Bordeaux although it is believed that the French side were unable to afford Silvestre’s wage demands.
