Park focused on Premiership title

  • Dead_Man_Inc

    United midfielder Ji-sung Park has set his sights firmly on the Premiership title this season, despite talk of an unprecedented Double Treble.

    The Reds are in the Champions League quarter finals, FA Cup semi finals and six points clear of Chelsea at the top of the Premiership.

    The 26-year-old South Korean midfielder scored twice for the Reds in the 4-1 win over Bolton a fortnight ago. On Saturday Blackburn travel to Old Trafford for the first of eight remaining games in the title run-in.

    "It is possible for us to get the results to succeed in all competitions, " he told South Korean reporters. "But, personally, my number one target is the Premier League."

    The Reds follow up the clash against Rovers with a busy month in April with a two-legged Champions League quarter final against Roma and an FA Cup semi final with Watford on 14 April.

    Park has experienced chasing multiple trophies before having done the Double with his former club PSV Eindhoven in Holland.

    "I have had a similar experience in the Netherlands," he added. "Even if we have bad results, we have to bounce back quickly and concentrate on the next game."
