Evra Happy On The Wing

  • omgukilledkenny
    7 Aug 07, 20:50


    Patrice Evra has told Manchester United he is happy to play in midfield if required to do so.

    The French ace was given a wide midfield berth in Sunday's Community Shield with Mikael Silvestre starting at left full-back.

    Evra sometimes played further up the field for Monaco and is happy giving United an extra option as it increases his chances of starting for Sir Alex Ferguson.

    "Playing as a midfielder gives the team an additional option," Evra told L'Equipe.

    "Plus it increases my chances of starting. I did well because I've already played there for Monaco."

    Evra is delighted to kick off the season with a trophy, but acknowledges it will be tough to reclaim their Premier League crown.

    "The Community Shield is a trophy and to start the season with a trophy is a good thing," he continued.

    "We have to retain our title, we have no choice. Everyone has strengthened so winning the title will be harder than ever."
