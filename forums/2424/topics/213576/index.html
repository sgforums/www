    To kick off, we're asking you to vote for the best team the manager has assembled at Old Trafford since his arrival in November 1986. Sir Alex has built several great sides over the 20 years. Here's our shortlist of five:

    1991 European Cup Winners' Cup team
    Sealey; Irwin, Bruce, Pallister, Blackmore; Phelan, Robson, Ince, Sharpe; McClair, Hughes.

    Schmeichel; Parker, Bruce, Pallister, Irwin; Kanchelskis, Keane, Ince, Giggs; Cantona, Hughes.

    1996 League and Cup Double winners
    Schmeichel; G Neville, Bruce (or May), Pallister, Irwin (or P Neville); Beckham, Scholes, Keane (or Butt), Giggs; Cantona, Cole.

    1999 Treble winners
    Schmeichel; G Neville, Johnsen (or Berg), Stam, Irwin (or P Neville); Beckham, Scholes, Keane (or Butt), Giggs; Cole, Yorke (or Sheringham, Solskjaer).

    2003 Premiership champions
    Barthez; G Neville, Ferdinand, Brown (or Blanc), Silvestre (or O'Shea); Beckham, Keane, Scholes, Giggs, Solskjaer (or Veron), Van Nistelrooy.
