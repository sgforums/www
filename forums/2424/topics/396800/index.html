    Tosic double downs Bochum

    On-loan winger scores in each half to leave opponents struggling

    Zoran Tosic scored both goals on Friday as Cologne beat Bochum 2-0 to all but make sure of avoiding relegation in the Bundesliga.

    Winless in nine games, Bochum remains one point above the relegation zone. It could drop into it when the 31st round is completed over the weekend.

    Tosic picked up a long pass, broke free and rounded Bochum's goalkeeper Philipp Heerwagen to score from a sharp angle in the 15th minute.

    The Serbia international, who is on loan from Manchester United, sealed Cologne's third home victory of the season - and the first since December - with a superb finish after swapping passes with Lukas Podolski in the 77th.

     

    The Canadian Press
