Tosic ready for United action

  • omgukilledkenny
    6 Jan 09, 20:09

    Zoran Tosic claims he will soon be ready for Manchester United action after sealing his move from Partizan Belgrade.

    Teenage team-mate Adem Ljajic will not arrive until he turns 18 but winger Tosic is poised to play a part in United's assault on four fronts this season.

    Sir Alex Ferguson wants the wide man to essentially replace Old Trafford legend Ryan Giggs as a left-sided player because he now sees the Welshman as a central midfielder.

    It is a tall order for Tosic, who initially turned down personal terms with The Red Devils, but he is buoyed by the presence of fellow Serb Nemanja Vidic in the squad.

    "I dreamed of playing for a club like Manchester United," he told uefa.com. 

    "Now the dream has come true and I’m very happy for it. I wish to become a world-class footballer wearing the United shirt and not miss my chance to be remembered for my contribution to the biggest football family.

    "It's a great feeling to be here, but now I’m only thinking how to adjust to The Premier League as soon as possible.

    "Nemanja Vidic’s presence will be of immense help as we now each other well from the national team.

    "I am absolutely confident and ready to learn fast and I hope the manager will be able to count on me soon."

    Meanwhile, former youth team skipper Sam Hewson has been allowed to join Hereford United.

    The loan deal will run until early April for the combative young midfielder.
