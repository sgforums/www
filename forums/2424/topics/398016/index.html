    Points to be taken from the match:

    1. Berbatov needs to be sold, ASAP, to anyone willing to take him. I'm sure Fergie's patience with him has run out judging from his priceless expression when Berbatov could have had a hat-trick. His positioning is poor, he cant shoot, he cant head.

    2. Overconfidence Back 4. How many times United defence was pressured and gave way for Sunderland to counter? I remember Evans, O'Shea and even Vidic grew too much confidence, holding the ball for too long, inviting Bent and Campbell to pressure them into giving the ball away.

    3. John O'Shea. This guy is reserve at best. Please dont play him in the starting line-up Fergie. He has no pace and posseses no threat whatsoever going forward. I've mentioned last season that the only plus points he has is his physical presence. Thats all he has. A good utility man for the team and his place will always be on the bench.

    Sorry Sand King, i know O'Shea is your fav, but he really isn't a 1st team material.

    4. Johnny Evans. Now we see why Fergie buys Smalling and tried for Rodwell? Simple, Evans is proned to errors, as seen in the Sunderland match.

    5. Back Pass. Not sure if anyone notice but United always seem to pass all the way back to their goalkeeper quite often, the usual culprit is Evans. Why wait till opposition pressurized you before making a pass when you have plenty of time to do so?

    6. Creative Midfielder. Seriously lacking here. Giggs and Scholes are getting old. Look at how much Fergie depended on these 2? They lasted the whole 90 minutes! Nani was the only younger creative midfielder playing last night. What's up with buying Obertan and Tosic only to put on substitute' bench and loan out?
