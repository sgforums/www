    Manchester United manager Sir Alex Ferguson has not finished with his Serbian invasion at Old Trafford.

    With Zoran Tosic joining Nemanja Vidic at the world champions and Adem Ljajic arriving soon, Ferguson is certainly getting full value out of scout Budimir Vujacic in the region.

    Vujacic reckons Filip Djuricic is as good as Ljajic and the Under-17 international is having a week's trial in Manchester.

    The teenager is available because of a complicated background after he joined Olympiacos from Crvena Zvezda, only for the move to collapse because he was too young to be allowed to finalise the transfer.

    Djuricic returned to Serbia to link up with hometown club Radnicki Obrenovac, who are only in the Third Division in their country.

    Ajax have also been trailing the young playmaker but he will get the chance to impress Ferguson and his coaching team at United and earn a dream transfer.
