    Squad sheets: Manchester United v Everton

     

    A home match against a mid-table side is attractive to any team in sight of the title, but this fixture might not be as simple an exercise as it appears for Manchester United. Everton have not lost a Premier League match since mid-February and that form has taken them to seventh place. With matches against Arsenal and Chelsea to come, this is a critical phase for Sir Alex Ferguson's side, who cannot afford any more of the drabness seen in the goalless draw at Newcastle United if they are to secure a 19th league title.
