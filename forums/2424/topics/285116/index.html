Neville out of reserve outing

  • omgukilledkenny

    Gary Neville will not feature for Manchester United's reserve team when they take on Liverpool on Thursday night.

    United boss Sir Alex Ferguson revealed earlier this week that, following a fortnight's full training, Neville was looking at a return in Thursday night's reserve-team clash against Liverpool's second string.

    However, the England international full-back is deemed to be not quite ready and, rather rush him back and run the risk of suffering new problems, Neville will wait until the Wigan reserve-team encounter in just under two weeks' time before allowing him to play.
