Manchester United and Tottenham want Milik

  • ifan
    7 Dec 12, 07:52

    Manchester United and Tottenham Hotspur have both registered Interest in Polish Scoring Sensation, Arkaduisz Milik.

    18-year-old Milik was watched by scouts from all over Europe, when his Polish club Gornik Zabrze played Ruch Chorzow in the league. according to The Daily Mirror.

    Milik have scored 9 goals in all competitions this season, whilst making 5 assists.

    The youngster has also made 3 National Team appearances for Poland.

    United scouts watched Milik a fortnight ago and are believe to rate the striker highly, However Tottenham have joined the race to sign Milik, after they failed with an attempt to bring the player to London 2 seasons ago.
