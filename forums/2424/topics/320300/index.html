    Manchester United winger Cristiano Ronaldo says he has made no decision over his future, despite reports claiming he wants to join Real Madrid.

    The Portuguese ace has been at the centre of the transfer story of the summer as Real Madrid look to land him for a reported record-breaking fee.

    The 23-year-old appeared to be leaning towards a move to the Spanish capital after comments suggested he has already set his sights on a move.

    But he has now admitted he is unsure of where his future lies.

    "Still I do not know where I will play," Ronaldo was quoted as saying in Spanish daily Marca.

    "I have not yet decided. It will be decided after the European Championship."
