    Manchester United Defender Nemanja Vidic: I've Never Said I Want To Join Real Madrid

    Serb stopper is staying at Old Trafford until 2012 at least...



    Nemanja Vidic



    Manchester United defender Nemanja Vidic has again moved to quash rumours he is planning to quit Old Trafford in the summer and join Spanish giants Real Madrid.

    The 28-year-old centre-back has been the subject of intense transfer speculation for some time, with reputed interest from Real, Barcelona and AC Milan.

    However, the Serbian has moved to allay fears of his departure, claiming he wishes to honour his contract at United that runs for another two years.

    “I have never said I'd like to move to Madrid,” Vidic told the Daily Star.

    “I will definitely stay here until 2012 at least - and then we have time to speak about whatever may happen in the future.

    “I am enjoying it here and I think I've showed that on the pitch. For now, all I'm focused on is playing the game and doing my best.”
