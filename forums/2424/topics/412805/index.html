    Alexis Sanchez Wants to Join Manchester United

    Udinese winger Alexis Sanchez has offered a view on his future as a number of clubs line up with offers.

    Malaga, Manchester United and Hamburg have all been linked with the 21-year-old Chilean ace who is tied to the club until 2014.

    Udinese value him at around €20 million, and he has hinted he could leave in future.

    "Of course I would like to play for Manchester," he told AS.

    "Malaga? Why not. As things stand though all questions about my future are in the hands of my agent."

    Despite his enthusiasm to embrace either the Premier League or the Spanish Primera Division, Udinese sporting director Fabrizio Larini recently told Italian media that "there was no chance for Sanchez of leaving in January".
