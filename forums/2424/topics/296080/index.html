    It's usually Premier League defenders who are left in a spin when Cristiano Ronaldo shows off his devastating acceleration and sharp turns.

    But hours after scoring both goals in the 2-0 victory against Fulham on Monday, it was the Manchester United winger who was left red-faced on Monday.

    For the 22-year-old lost control of his Audi R8, spun the car 180 degrees and found himself facing the wrong way at a roundabout.

    A fan told the Sun: The road was a bit slippery and he ended up losing control, power-sliding around the roundabout.

    "He did manage to turn to car safely and avoid any damage ... but he's had a real scare."
