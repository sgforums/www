O'Neill Angling For Silvestre Loan

  • omgukilledkenny
    1 Aug 08, 18:24

    Aston Villa manager Martin O’Neill, anxious to bring in cover at left-back following the injury to Wilfred Bouma, has reportedly made a bid to sign Manchester United defender Mikael Silvestre on loan.

    Manchester United‘s France international defender Mikael Silvestre is understood to be wanted by Aston Villa manager Martin O'Neill to solve a temporary full-back problem at Villa Park.

    O’Neill is now seriously short of cover at left-back following the dislocated ankle suffered by Dutch star Wilfred Bouma during Villa’s InterToto Cup clash against Odense at the weekend. Bouma could be out of action for up to six months, leaving Villa exposed in that position, with Silvestre seen as a possible solution to bridge the gap.

    O'Neill has reportedly approached United counterpart Sir Alex Ferguson about the possibility of taking Silvestre on loan, and with John O'Shea able to cover for Patrice Evra, Fergie may be amenable.

    However, Silvestrre, 30, is also a target for French capital club Paris St Germain, who are busily putting together an experienced squad after last season’s flirtation with relegation. That could pose Villa another problem, though PSG are thought to be seeking a permanent deal for Silvestre.
