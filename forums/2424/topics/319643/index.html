Park Pondering Olympics Possibility

  • omgukilledkenny
    3 Jun 08, 00:05

    Manchester United winger Park Ji-sung has opened the door to the possibility of representing South Korea in the Olympics this summer, but admits that it would not be a straightforward choice even if it were completely up to him.

    Sir Alex Ferguson has not yet revealed whether he will release the ex PSV star for the Beijing Olympics in August, as he would miss almost the entire pre-season preparation.

    "That's not something I can decide myself," Park told a Korean reporter.

    "If I play in the Olympics, I'll be able to help the juniors exempted from military service by winning a medal.

    "Or, if I stay with Manchester United, I'll be able to devote myself to preparing for the next season.

    "Whatever happens, there will be something I have to give up."

    Park will be eager to solidify his standing at United, after being a surprise ommission from the squad that conquered the Champions League in Moscow last month.
