    Sir Alex Ferguson says he would love to be playing in his current team - and reckons he would bag a few goals too.

    Despite his glittering success at Manchester United, which has seen him amass an incredible 20 major trophies in his 21-year Old Trafford reign, Fergie was less distinguished as a player and never managed to win a medal during his 14 years as a player in Scotland.

    And although he says he has more than made up for his failings as a player through his success at United, a part of him wants to be on the pitch playing with the likes of Cristiano Ronaldo and Wayne Rooney.

    "I look at some of my players now, the wonderful football we play and I'd love to be out there playing with them," said Fergie. "I'd love to have been able to play with the likes of Ryan Giggs, Ronaldo and Rooney. I'm sure I'd score a few goals playing with them."
