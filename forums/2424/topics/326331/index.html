    Manchester United star Michael Carrick has claimed to be a roadie during his spare time.

    Taking time out from his celebrity lifestyle to help out his brother-in-law’s band, The Sound, Carrick claims to see a lot of fun in setting up for the band prior to a low-key show.

    "I love going to their shows and you can get stuck in and lend a hand setting up – their music is great so that always helps," Carrick, who’ll soon have to take time out from the set-up job in order to concentrate on his own  football commitments, told the Sun.
