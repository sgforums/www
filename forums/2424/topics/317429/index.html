    CRISTIANO RONALDO has rocked clubs hoping to sign him by declaring: "Why would I want to leave Manchester United?" The Red Devils ace has been in electric form this season and has fired the champions to the brink of the Double.

    Real Madrid head a list of the world's top clubs who would love to tempt Ronaldo away from Old Trafford. But the Portuguese ace, who has scored an incredible 41 goals this season, said: "I'm at the right club, playing with the right players and I learn things all the time. "That's what great about being at United. I improve all the time and I want to improve every season. I think I'm a better player now than five years ago.

    "This season has been a great one for me. I've scored a lot of goals, the team have played very well and we're champions. It's amazing."
