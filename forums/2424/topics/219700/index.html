    Lets predict the score of every United matches as well as the scorers and the starting lineup. Points are awarded. At the end of the season we'll see who is the best pundit.

    For the exact scoreline - 10 points
    For correct match outcome- 5 points
    (i.e Win, Lose or Draw)
    For Incorrect match outcome - minus 5 points
    (i.e Win, Lose or Draw)
    For each correct scorers predicted - 2 points
    (can only choose the same number of players as the score predicted.
    e.g if you predict United to win 2-1, U can only choose 2 scorers(or 3 if u wanna bet an own-goal. ) If the number of players chosen exceeds your prediction then the extra players would not be considered)
    For each player correctly identified in the starting lineup - 1 point

    And Dead_Man_Inc will keep track of the score.

    Cheers.
    C.K
