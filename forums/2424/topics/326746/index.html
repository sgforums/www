    High Roller Cristiano Ronaldo has celebrated his decision to stay at Manchester United by splashing out on a £340,000 Rolls Royce.

    The motor-mad soccer star, who has infuriated United fans by courting a move to Real Madrid, has ordered a flash Phantom convertible.

    The Roller will join the goal poacher’s fabulous fleet. He also owns an Aston Martin, a BMW 6 Series and a Ferrari which he has recently been whizzing around his native Portugal.

    The Phantom will replace his £140,000 Bentley Continental.

    Ronaldo ordered it through an elite car firm called Liquid 45.

    He gave instructions for it to be “fully loaded,” a trade term for all the added extras.
