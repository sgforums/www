Vidic: I want more goals

  • Dead_Man_Inc
    5 Nov 06, 17:04
    Nemanja Vidic was delighted to score his first goal at Old Trafford and hopes to add to his tally in the near future.



    The Serbian defender, who opened his Reds' account at Wigan last month, rose to head home Gary Neville's cross on 66 minutes, before celebrating in front of the Stretford End.

    "I'm really pleased to get another goal," Vidic told MUTV. "I hope I can continue to score more in the future including some important ones.

    "This goal was our third in the game and we were quite comfortable by then. So I'd like to score a really important goal for the team one day."

    The Reds romped to a 3-0 victory over Harry Redknapp's side after going ahead inside of three minutes through a Louis Saha penalty.

    "We started the game very well just like we did against Bolton," acknowledged Vidic. "We know that if you don't start well then things can be difficult.

    "But we always aim to start every match well and be fully focused on getting an early goal. We did that today which was important especially after losing to Copenhagen.

    "It's great for us to be top of the Premiership," he added. "We will now wait and see what Chelsea and Arsenal do on Sunday."
