Van der Sar set to call it quits

  • Dead_Man_Inc
    9 Oct 10, 10:59

    EDWIN VAN DER SAR will call time on his illustrious career at the end of the season, according to Manchester United goalkeeping coach Eric Steele.

    The Dutch star, 39, has enjoyed a hugely successful spell at Old Trafford since joining from Fulham five years ago.

    In that time, Van der Sar has won three Premier League titles, the Champions League, Carling Cup and FIFA Club World Cup.

    But he has yet to get his hands on the FA Cup and Steele revealed the Red Devils' No1 intends to put that right in his swansong campaign.

    The United coach said: "I think Ed has made his mind up and said this is his last year.

    "He hasn't won the FA Cup and that is a big target for him.
