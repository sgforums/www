    Trophy Tour: Singapore itinerary

     

     

     

     

    Manchester United’s Asia Trophy Tour arrives at its sixth and final destination on Wednesday – Singapore.

    Fans can meet Edwin van der Sar and see the Barclays Premier League trophy at the restaurant at Brewerkz Bar & Grill in Clarke Quay from 19:00, but get there early to avoid disappointment.

    Edwin is also taking part in a Nike event at Singapore’s Jalan Besar Stadium from 13:30 to 15:30, when he’ll meet around 50 of Singapore’s national youth team players.

    The recently retired goalkeeper will then appear at a Hublot boutique event from 16:30 to 18:00, where he’ll answer questions from the media and sign autographs, before moving onto Clarke Quay for the fan event.

    And the Trophy Tour continues on Thursday with several photo opportunities for fans with the silverware at iconic locations around the city. ManUtd.com will have more details soon.

    The champions have been taking the trophy around Asia and meeting fans for the last two weeks. Read more, view galleries and watch video in our dedicated Trophy Tour section.
