    A Bilbao v Man United preview

    Team news for Thursday night's Europa League clash between Athletic Bilbao and Manchester United at Estadio San Mames (kick-off 6pm).

    Valencia: Returns to face Bilbao

     

     

    Antonio Valencia is set to return from a four-week absence with a hamstring injury as United look to overturn a 3-2 first-leg deficit in Spain.

    The winger returned to full training on Monday and offers Sir Alex Ferguson a useful attacking option given Nani is among those who did not travel to northern Spain.

    Phil Jones is also missing from the trip, in addition to Anderson, who is thought to have a hamstring injury, but Chris Smalling is available again after a head wound re-opened during last week's loss to the Spaniards at Old Trafford.

    United (from): Amos, De Gea; Evans, Evra, Fabio, Ferdinand, Rafael, Smalling; Carrick, Cleverley, Giggs, Park, Pogba, Scholes, Young, Valencia; Berbatov, Hernandez, Rooney, Welbeck.

    Here we go: Rooney (R) and Rio Ferdinand pass the Guggenhein Museum just hours before their crunch Europa League clash

     

    Smile, Wayne: Athletic Bilbao fans mob Manchester United striker Rooney during his pre-match stroll

     

     

     

    Wayne Rooney was mobbed by Athletic Bilbao fans as Manchester United took a pre-match stroll around the Spanish city.

    The England striker is expected to lead the line for United this evening as manager Sir Alex Ferguson fields a strong line-up against the La Liga side.
