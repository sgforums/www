    CARLOS TEVEZ is eyeing a move to Real Madrid after struggling to pin down a place in the Manchester United first team.

    That is the verdict of fellow Argentine Ossie Ardiles, a close friend of the 25-year-old striker.

    The legendary midfielder claims Tevez has been unhappy ever since the the arrival of Dimitar Berbatov, and a switch to the Spanish giants would appeal.

    Ardiles said: “Until that day he had been happy in Manchester. But for sure if he doesn’t play, moving to Madrid would delight him.

    “Tevez has been playing less since Berbatov arrived. If he’s not played in the starting line-up, then he will have to think twice about his future.

    “Because every time he was given the chance to ply his trade, Carlos has played well and scored goals.”
