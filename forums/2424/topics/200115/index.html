    Peter Schmeichel Without a doubt, the greatest Man Utd Goalkeeper ever to don in Man Utd colours.I just loved him. I loved the goal he scored against Dynamo Kiev (i think)

    Defenders

    Paul Parker The best right back for Man Utd, imo. Hard tackling and no nonsense defender which i think Gary Neville can never emulate.

    Denis Irwin Cherubic Left Back that plays with grace who can delve in the attacking zone as well as defending the posts.

    Paul McGrath God damn legendary defender. Really really tough tackler and damn impossible to get through him in the 80s

    Jaap Stam Though brief, his stay is fruitful bearing the Treble of medals.

    Midfielders

    Norman Whiteside Under Ron Atkinson, he is lethal in the air and could shoot with both power and accuracy. He was also never afraid to try something different or unexpected, making him a constant threat to opposing defences.

    Paul Ince The Guv'nor! Before Cantona, it was Ince who captained Man Utd with excellent leadership and wisdom

    Ryan Giggs The icon of Man Utd. His first and only club. Prolific during his heydays though now can see slump ah

    Eric Cantona Without a doubt, Eric the King plays more of an attacking midfielder to assist the forwards.At Leeds, Cantona was an idol, at Manchester United he was a God. Fans worshipped him the like of which Old Trafford had not seen or may ever see.Even to this day 3 years after his departure fans still sing his name. No United player has ever had such a relationship with his fans.

    Forwards

    Sir George Best RIP Georgie. Arguably, the best winger/forward to ever don Man Utd colours. He was the reason why Ryan Giggs start kicking the ball and obviously, many more Man Utd stars..

    Ruud Van Nistelrooy Even though there may be some games he may not be performing, you cant stop smiling when he breaks that streak. An opportunist and prolific finisher and one of the top scoring Man Utd players, i hope he doesnt leave
