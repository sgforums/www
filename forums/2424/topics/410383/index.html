    His Manchester United team-mate might be facing a personal hell but Rio Ferdinand appears not to have a care in the world.

    The centre-back looked very relaxed as he enjoyed a break in the south of France with his wife Rebecca Ellison.

    And he happily scoffed on an ice cream cone before showing off his diving skills to a couple of admiring youngsters.

    He and Rebecca snuggled up on their sun loungers on the beach as they played with their phones, no doubt catching up on the news back home.

    And although it was only a short break for the couple, who jetted back to the UK from Nice yesterday, they both looked refreshed, tanned and in love.

    Ferdinand, 31, and Rebecca had been in France when news of his England and Manchester United team-mate Wayne Rooney apparently having cheated on wife Coleen with prostitute Jennifer Thompson first emerged.

    And while Ferdinand may have been concerned about his team-mate, he appeared most set on spending time with Rebecca and returning home to their two sons, Lorenz and Tate.
