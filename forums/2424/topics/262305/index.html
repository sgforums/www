Ferdinand sets sights on quadruple

  • omgukilledkenny

    Manchester United defender Rio Ferdinand is eyeing a clean sweep of trophies next season.

    The champions have already added Nani, Anderson and Owen Hargreaves to their squad and look set to conclude their dealings with a swoop for a top striker.

    United may have won the title last season, but they fell at the semi-final stage of the Champions League, lost the FA Cup final to Chelsea and crashed out of the Carling Cup in the fourth round to minnows Southend.

    Ferdinand believes with the new signings on board, the club should be aiming to win every available trophy next season.

    He told Sky Sports News: "Next season we won't be talking about what happened last season, it's about what we want to win in the future,

    "We want to win the league, the FA Cup, the Carling Cup and the Champions League.

    "We know it's going to be difficult, but you've got to set your standards."
