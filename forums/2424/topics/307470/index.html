    Cristiano Ronaldo has reportedly been fined £8,000 for texting during a training session.

    The United star is said to have been caught by manager Sir Alex Ferguson sending four texts during breaks in the middle of a session at the Carrington training complex.

    And the 23-year-old was landed with on-the-spot £2,000 fines for each offence as part of a new crackdown on the use of mobile phones at the club, it has been reported.

    Disciplinarian Sir Alex has earned a reputation during more than 21 years at Old Trafford of ensuring his players focus on their game and are not distracted from winning trophies.

    A spokesman at United declined to comment.
