    Smith tipped for England recall

    Manchester United striker Alan Smith looks set for a recall to the England squad for their Euro 2008 qualifier in June.The Red Devils forward was watched against Roma and Watford by England boss Steve McClaren, who saw two impressive performances from the former Leeds man.

    Smith now looks likely to get a call-up for the clash against Estonia in Tallinn in the absence of the suspended Wayne Rooney.

    He could partner Peter Crouch for that game as Michael Owen is unlikely to be fit to play, especially after he was withdrawn from the reserve squad to face Middlesbrough on Monday night.

    Andrew Johnson, Darren Bent and David Nugent are also in contention to partner Crouch in Tallinn.
