Kuszczak looks for loan deal

  • omgukilledkenny
    31 May 09, 19:59

    Keeper Tomasz Kuszczak is unhappy at Manchester United and wants to go on loan.

    The Poland star, left, who had only four Premier League outings last season, is United's third choice behind Edwin van der Sar and Ben Foster. Kuszczak, 27, said: "This has got out of hand. It is high time for me to look into the option of being loaned out.

    "I am a player at the best club in the whole world, and that alone makes me very happy.

    ''However, that is not the case when I look to next season and the prospect of playing only a few matches."

    Kuszczak, rated at £4million, is wanted by several Premier League clubs. Russians Zenit St Petersburg and Turkish side Besiktas are also interested.
