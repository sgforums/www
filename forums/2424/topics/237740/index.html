Deco: Barca's ideal for Ronaldo

  • mcfadden
    16 Feb 07, 13:14
    Deco believes Barcelona is the ideal place for international team-mate Cristiano Ronaldo to ply his trade.

    The Manchester United winger has been the subject of speculation over his future recently, with Real Madrid and Barcelona both expressing an interest.

    Ronaldo himself has been coy as to where his future lies, although The Red Devils are determined to tie their superstar to a long-term contract.

    Deco has called on the 22-year-old to join him at Camp Nou if he decides to quit the Premiership anytime soon.

    "I would love it if he came to play at Barca," Deco told Radio Marca. "I believe it is the ideal club for him.

    "There are big clubs interested in Cristiano Ronaldo, but I personally believe that Barca would be better than Madrid, because of the team's form, the city is an ideal place to live, etc."

    Meanwhile, speaking about the row involving Samuel Eto'o and Ronaldinho earlier this week, Deco insists there are no lingering problems between the pair.

    The Brazilian playmaker was critical of Eto'o after the striker refused to come on as a substitute against Racing Santander on Sunday.

    "It is difficult to get back to your normal rhythm and that creates anxiety, because people pressure you," Deco added.

    "The press were anxious to see something and they (Eto'o and Ronaldinho) talked about giving each other a hug to see their reaction.

    "There weren't any problems, everything had been sorted out before and they just wanted to see how people reacted.

    "It was a bit of a joke really."
