Giggs wins Actim accolade

  • Dead_Man_Inc
    16 Sep 06, 20:22
    Ryan Giggs has received a boost as he prepares to sit out several matches.

    Actim Index statistics show he's the top performing midfielder in the Barclays Premiership and the third best player overall. His stats for the season so far show two goals, eight shots, eight tackles, eight dribbles, 11 crosses and 52 passes.

    Louis Saha is also ranked highly, in fifth position overall. Actim stats show Saha has contributed two goals, 15 shots, 15 tackles, 42 passes and five dribbles in 350 minutes.

    The full list of United players in the Actim Index Top 50 is as follows:

    3. Ryan Giggs - top midfielder - 102 points
    5. Louis Saha - 4th best striker - 97 points
    8. Wes Brown - 2nd best defender - 83 points
    19. John O'Shea - 6th best defender - 72 points
    25. Edwin van der Sar - 2nd best goalkeeper - 68 points
    27. Rio Ferdinand - 11th best defender - 68 points
    30. Mikael Silvestre - 12th best defender - 66 points
    40. Cristiano Ronaldo - 10th best midfielder - 61 points
    48. Wayne Rooney - 14th best striker - 58 points.
