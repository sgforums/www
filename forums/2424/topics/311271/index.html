    Fabio Capello thinks Roma can defeat Man Utd in Champions League

     

    Fabio Capello warned last night that Manchester United are in grave danger of being dumped out of the Champions League by Roma.

    United are heavy favourites to beat the Italians in the quarterfinals after thrashing them 7-1 at Old Trafford at the same stage last season.

    But England boss Capello claims playing Sir Alex Ferguson's team will be ideal for Roma.

    Capello said: "Roma have drawn the team best suited to them from the four English clubs, and one they know very well.

    "I am certain that Roma will be a very different proposition from last season. If they play in an ordered way, they can do very well."
