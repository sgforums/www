    According to reports in various British tabloids, Manchester United's Cristiano Ronaldo and Newcastle United's Steven Taylor went head-to-head in the tunnel during the half-time break on Wednesday night.



    Towards the end of the first half at St. James' Park, the Magpies defender appeared to have struck the Portuguese winger in the face when challenging him for the ball.

    However, referee Steve Bennett seemed to have either missed the incident or not deemed it too serious as Taylor was only shown a yellow card.

    The Sun claims Ronaldo responded by telling the England Under-21 defender, "Your style of football is s***," to which Taylor replied, "Well, you are ugly!"

    However, the Daily Express reports that it was Wayne Rooney who told the 23-year-old centerback, "You’ve always been a s*** footballer."

    The 6ft 2in defender's response to 'Wazza' is said to have been, "I might be a s*** player but at least I’m not ugly like you. You’ll always be ugly."

    Either way, it is thought that there was a clash between the players that could be investigated by the FA if the referee included it in his match report.
