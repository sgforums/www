    EXCLUSIVE: Macheda rewarded for his heroics with new £10,000 a week deal at United

     

    Federico Macheda is congratulated by Wayne Rooney after his crucial winner for Manchester United at Sunderland

     

    Federico Macheda's stunning start to his Manchester United career is to be rewarded with a new £10,000-a-week contract this summer.

    The 17-year-old, known as ‘Kiko’, currently earns £400-a-week but is on the first-team appearance bonus of £2,000 a game in the Premier League.

    He scored United’s winner at Sunderland on Saturday just 46 seconds after coming on as a substitute.

    The Italian, who hit a stunning injury-time winner against Aston Villa on his debut, signed a three-year deal when he joined from Lazio in September 2007, but his elevation to the first team squad has set the wheels in motion for an improved deal.

    United boss Sir Alex Ferguson has recognised his remarkable contribution to their title challenge.

    He said: ‘The lad has something special. I’ve had a chat with his family and I’m sure they will keep his feet on the ground.

    ‘He has that quick thinking, that instinct, which good goalscorers have. He wasn’t fazed by anything when he came on.’
