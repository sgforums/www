    CROCK SHOCK ... Van der Sar is sidelined

     

     

    EDWIN VAN DER SAR will be out for eight weeks after breaking two bones in his left hand.

    The Manchester United keeper underwent surgery yesterday.

    Van der Sar, 38, picked up the injury saving a penalty in the Audi Cup game with Bayern Munich last Thursday.

    It is a blow for the Dutchman, who is expected to announce his retirement at the end of this season.

    Ben Foster now has a chance to stake his claim between the sticks, starting with the Community Shield clash against Chelsea on Sunday.
