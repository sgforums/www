    Tosic deal in doubt after Serbia winger rejects United contract.

     

    Zoran Tosic

     

    Serbia winger Zoran Tosic has failed to agree on a contract with Manchester United, but said he remains optimistic he will join the club in January.

    The 21-year-old Partizan Belgrade player visited Old Trafford last week with his manager, but revealed United's offer to sign him 'does not coincide with' his demands. 'We agreed to stay in touch and I believe that everything will be fine in the end,' he said.

    Tosic's manager Zoran Pavlovic confirmed the player was unhappy with the salary on offer, announcing the winger may even consider finding another club.

    Tosic was granted a British work permit last month, after United manager Sir Alex Ferguson made a personal plea at a hearing in Sheffield in order to get the green light to complete the £3.5 million deal.

    The versatile Tosic has made 12 international appearances, including at the Beijing Olympics, and can play on either flank.
