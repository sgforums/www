    Shock as Fergie makes a mistake over Manucho

    Sir Alex Ferguson has made a mistake, Sportsmail online can exclusively reveal.

     

    No sooner had the Manchester United manager secured the services of striker Manucho last month, than he has loaned the Angola Africa Cup of Nations star out to Panathinaikos.

     

    And Sportsmail online has uncovered video evidence which suggests that Sir Alex is missing a trick by allowing the 24-year-old centre forward to head to Greece.

    Before you all shout out that he's not needed at Old Trafford, take a look at the belter Manucho scored against Egypt and imagine him in the same side as Ronaldo and Rooney.

    Manucho signed a three-year contract with United during last month's transfer window after moving from Angolan side Petro Athletico.

    "After the Africa Cup of Nations I will return to Luanda and then head to Athens," Manucho said. "I am looking forward to playing in Greece before hopefully being a success at Old Trafford."

    Ferguson, who has steered United to nine League titles, explained: "We have farmed him out to Panathinaikos for the rest of the season as he will get games there. "They have a Portuguese coach Jose Peseiro and as Manucho speaks Portuguese, it is comfortable for him."
