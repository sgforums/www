Quinton Fortune signs one-year deal with Brescia

    Former Manchester United star Quinton Fortune has signed a one-year deal with Italian Serie B side Brescia.

    The 31-year-old South African has been without a club since leaving Bolton two seasons ago and was on trial at Sheffield United.

    Fortune has been training with Brescia for the past month to prove his fitness after a succession of knee problems.

    Fortune said: "I am now physically ready and willing to take to the field and I just want to make a contribution after being out for so long."

    The midfielder is in the squad for today's clash with Modena where a win for Brescia would lift them into the top four.

    Fortune was part of the United squad that won three Premier League titles during his seven years at Old Trafford.
