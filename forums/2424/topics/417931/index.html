    Manchester United Set To Launch €6.5 Million Bid For Valencia Winger Joaquin - Report

    Joaquin is on Man United's radar...

     

     
    Joaquin, Valencia

     

     

     

    Manchester United are eyeing a January move for Spanish winger Joaquin from Valencia, according to reports in England.

    TalkSport claims that United manager Sir Alex Ferguson was hoping to land the 29-year-old last year when Antonio Valencia got injured, and given his good form for los Che so far this campaign the Scot is ready to make another approach.

    It's been reported that United will make a bid of €6.5 million to land the former Real Betis winger in January.
