Ruben flattered by United talk

  • turbo_drift
    1 Mar 07, 18:42
    Racing Santander defender Ruben Gonzalez has admitted he is flattered by reports linking him with a move to Manchester United.

    The 25-year-old has blossomed this year in Racing colours, having made a summer move to Santander from Real Madrid, and his efforts have caught the eye of a number of clubs.

    United's chief scout Martin Ferguson is believed to have taken in a number of Racing games this term and has been impressed with the efforts of Ruben.

    It is believed that Ezequiel Garay was the player United initially went to watch, but the efforts of Ruben are rumoured to have hogged the spotlight.

    Ruben is contracted to Racing until the summer of 2009 and the club have insisted there has been no contact from Old Trafford, but Ruben is aware of the speculation linking him with a move to England.

    "During last season I received contact from clubs in England, but Real Madrid rejected them as they had injury problems with Woodgate and Helguera," said Ruben.

    "This season I am playing and I am happy, the results are good and that is good for the players.

    "For me the chance to move to Manchester United would be spectacular, but I don't want to think about theory - only reality.

    "It is logical that this is good for me as it demonstrates that I am playing well.

    "If an offer arrives then I would want a meeting between all parties to decide my future."
