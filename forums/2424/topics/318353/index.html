Huntelaar plays down transfer talk

  • omgukilledkenny
    22 May 08, 21:19

    Holland forward Klaas-Jan Huntelaar insists he has no intention of quitting Ajax in the summer and claims his only focus is on Euro 2008.

    Huntelaar is attracting interest from a host of top European clubs - Real Madrid reportedly among them - after he finished as the Eredivisie's top scorer last season with 34 goals.

    But the 24-year-old has downplayed the speculation over his future.

    "When you achieve good things you know that clubs are going to be interested," Huntelaar told www.ajaxf-side.nl.

    "I am not thinking about a transfer. At the moment I play for Holland and I want to achieve things with them in the coming weeks."

    He added: "I still have a contract for two years with Ajax.

    "If Ajax doesn't want to let me go, then I will stay in Amsterdam, it's that simple."

    Ajax will play in the UEFA Cup next season after they lost in the Eredivisie Champions League play-off final to FC Twente.
