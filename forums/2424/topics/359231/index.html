Patient Tosic waiting for next season

    Zoran Tosic is happy to bide his time before making his mark at Manchester United.

    The January arrival from Partizan Belgrade made a brief appearance against Tottenham in the FA Cup but has been overlooked since, despite impressing in the reserves.

    Lee Martin, who has been farmed out on loan on a number of occasions, was preferred to the Serbian on the bench on one occasion but Tosic is relaxed as he appreciates he will probably not be ready to shine until next season.

    "It was the best moment of my life when I came on," he told uefa.com when asked about his debut.

    "75,000 people. I think I did well in that game.

    "Now I hope we will win the championship and try to win the Champions League then, after that, when next season starts, I'm going to get the chance to play.

    "I'm happy here in Manchester. I haven't played a lot of games for the first team, but I have come to the best club in the world.

    "I know I have time. I have to practise, get stronger, get faster, be better in all aspects. I feel the [upcoming European] Under-21 championship will help me and I can prove I'm a United player."

    Nemanja Vidic is helping Tosic to settle as the fellow Serbian also needed some time to find his feet, even though he did enjoy more first-team action.

    "He has helped me a lot, not only on the pitch but off it too," Tosic continued.

    "English football is a bit different to the other leagues so he told me some things about how I should play and different things that I would have to get used to.

    "A player that learns to play in The Premier League can play wherever he wants."
