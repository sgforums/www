    Liverpool target David Silva sets his sights on joining Manchester United

    Chase me: Silva (left) has opened the door for Manchester United to make a move

     

     

    Spain star David Silva would jump at the chance of a move to Manchester United.

    United manager Sir Alex Ferguson has been keeping tabs on Valencia's attacking midfielder and is reported to have sent scouts to watch the Euro 2008 winner this season.

    Silva, who was strongly linked with a move to Liverpool until cash-strapped Valencia put a £30million price tag on his head, told the Daily Mirror: 'It's an honour for any player that a club of the prestige of Manchester United should follow them and that in itself is enormous praise.

    'I am committed to Valencia and my dream is to win the title but in the future anything is possible. 'Valencia have rejected offers for me in the past few months and I am happy here. But I also know that the Premier League is a big competition and hard to resist.'
