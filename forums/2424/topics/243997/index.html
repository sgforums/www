    Grant Tway Martin, I notice that Cristiano Ronaldo has got 16 Premiership goals already this season. I was wondering if this is a record for a midfielder already - or how many he has to get to be the highest midfielder scorer in one season of the Premiership?

    MARTIN SAYS: It is not an outright record yet Grant, but with eight games to go and Cristiano Ronaldo in the form he is, you wouldn't bet against him surpassing the season's best for a midfielder which does indeed stand at 16. Of course, these questions drag up the old debate of whether we class wingers as midfielders, but neither Andrei Kanchelskis in 1995-96 and Frank Lampard in 2005-06 played up front as such, and are the joint record holders for a Premiership season. Other more 'bona fide' midfielders, such as Gus Poyet, Freddie Ljungberg and Robert Pires have also led the scoring charts for non-strikers, and here is a complete rundown of the top midfield scorers for every Premiership season.

    SEASON MIDFIELDER
    1992-93 Mark Walters Liverpool 11
    1993-94 Ryan Giggs Manchester Utd 13
    1994-95 Ruel Fox Norwich City 10
    1995-96 Andrei Kanchelskis Manchester Utd 16
    1996-97 Juninho Paulista Middlesbrough 12
    1997-98 Marc Overmars Arsenal 12
    1998-99 Gus Poyet Chelsea 11
    1999-00 Gus Poyet Chelsea 10
    2000-01 Gus Poyet Chelsea 11
    2001-02 Freddie Ljungberg Arsenal 12
    2002-03 Robert Pires Arsenal 14
    Harry Kewell Leeds United 14
    2003-04 Robert Pires Arsenal 14
    2004-05 Robert Pires Arsenal 14
    2005-06 Frank Lampard Chelsea 16
