    There were a few moments that exposed United... Everyone was so attacking minded that they left the defence open at times... Only the poor finishing of Arsenal's strikers didn't see the scoreline for them higher... Evans is still a little to easy to get panic kind... Evra left his position open too often... Jones made one mistake which is like a cardinal sin for defenders... Never head the ball back across your own goal mouth! Fergie must drill this into his head as it could be a costly mistake in future...

    Overall, United didn't play with a defensive midfielder so it's understandable as everyone wanted goals... Nani lost the ball on a few occasions where had he chose to pass would have been a better option... Rooney look very upset with him on those occasions... There seem to be something going on there between them...

    Also, Hernandez doesn't look like the player he was last season when he came on... I never thought I would say this, Now we are going to miss Welbeck...

    On the positive, Rooney is in red hot form... Ashley Young has become United's 2nd most dangerous player after Roon... Jones was very lucky he didn't got a serious injury as Arshavin's tackled could have damage his leg badly, A good thing he is young and able to take that blow, an older player might have been injured from the looks of that challenged by Arshavin. Also, Anderson is looking like the creative midfielder we need...

    Finally, back to De Gea again... Now someone here calls it "The De Gea moment" which is very true... Looks like he can't finish one match without making a mistakes kind... That Arsenal first goal was so soft I don't understand why he didn't used his hands to try and save it cos from the looks of it, that was savable... Once again, the goals scored bail him out... Let's hope they can keep bailing him out until he cuts out all these silly mistakes from his game...
