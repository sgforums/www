  • zocoss

    WAYNE ROONEY is having extra training sessions in a bid to end his dramatic slump in form.

    The Manchester United and England striker has looked a shadow of his former self since returning from a three-match suspension and has cut a peripheral figure in his last four games.

    And with United taking on Newcastle tomorrow and Rooney back in the Englad squad for the upcoming Euro 2008 qualifiers against Macedonia and Croatia, the 20-year-old is desperate to end his worrying loss of form.

    United assistant boss Carlos Queiroz said: "Wayne finished training then stayed a while working alone because he knows he needs to do something.

    "It's good he is conscious of making mistakes. It just means he comes in every day with a greater desire to work. But game by game it will start to come right."
