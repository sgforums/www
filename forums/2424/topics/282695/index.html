Giggs: I don't know when I'll quit

  • omgukilledkenny
    29 Sep 07, 21:36


    Manchester United winger Ryan Giggs is refusing to put a date on when he might call an end to his playing days.

    United head to Birmingham on Saturday evening looking to record a fifth straight league win and Giggs cannot be found wanting in his desire.

    "I never give a thought to how long I can go on," he said. "I don't think you can afford to.

    "I don't look beyond a season. If I am being honest, I don't even look beyond a week.

    "I am not as quick as I used to be, that is a fact. But I have adapted my game and hopefully I can keep contributing.

    "There are obviously so many forward options for the manager now, so it might be a little harder to get back into the team if you are not playing well but I have confidence in my own ability.

    "But I thought last year I might have to accept less games last season and then I ended up playing more than I have done for the previous 10 years.

    "The main thing is I still feel fit. It is a little bit harder to recover now but the fact I am not playing for Wales now is helping and hopefully that carries on."
