    Keane eyes Evans deal after failing to land Tottenham's Kaboul

     

    Manchester United defender Jonny Evans is back in Sunderland's sights after boss Roy Keane failed to sign Tottenham's Younes Kaboul.

    Evans has had two loan spells at the Stadium of Light and Keane hopes an offer of £6million plus add-ons may persuade Sir Alex Ferguson to sell.
