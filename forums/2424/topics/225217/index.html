    Giggs wants Hargreaves at Man Utd



    Ryan Giggs is hopeful Manchester United can agree a deal with Bayern Munich to sign midfielder Owen Hargreaves. England international Hargreaves has repeatedly expressed his desire for a move to Old Trafford, but Bayern have so far rejected his pleas.
