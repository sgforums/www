Jones joins Derby

  • Dead_Man_Inc
    18 Nov 06, 03:12
    Reds' midfielder David Jones has joined Derby County on loan with a view to a permanent move, possibly in the January transfer window.

    The 22-year-old has agreed personal terms with the Rams and underwent a medical on Thursday at Pride Park.

    Derby boss Billy Davies, who worked with Jones during a loan spell at Preston, is delighted to be acquiring the midfielder's services.

    "He has got a wonderful left foot, with a great ability to create and score goals and he is very comfortable in possession and dangerous from set pieces," he said.

    "If he does what he does well, I am sure Derby County fans will enjoy watching him play."

    Jones has made just three appearances during his Reds' career since joining as a trainee on 1 July 2003.
