    Manchester United have confirmed the signings of Serbian duo Zoran Tosic and Adem Ljajic from Partizan Belgrade.

    17-year-old Ljajic, who is already a Serbia Under-21 international, will join Tosic at Old Trafford at the start of 2010.

    Tosic initially rejected personal terms with the European champions but will wear the number 14 shirt and challenge the likes of Park Ji-sung, Ryan Giggs and Nani for a place in the side.

    United have agreed the deal with all parties and obtained a work permit for Tosic but there is still some paperwork to be finalised over the double transfer.

    A statement declared a press conference will officially announce their arrival next week, once the FA Cup tie at Southampton.
