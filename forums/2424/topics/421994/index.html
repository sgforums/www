  • Rooney9

    Manchester United have been put on red alert after Udinese star Alexis Sanchez revealed he will leave the Italian club at the end of the season.

    Sir Alex Ferguson has been tracking the progress of the Chilean winger who has been outstanding in Serie A this season.

    Sanchez, 22, has also been attracting the interest of Inter Milan, Manchester City and Barcelona.

    But reports in Italy suggest that Manchester United are the strong favourites to clinch his signature and his agent Fernando Felicevich admits a move could be finalised at the end of the season.

    Felicevich said: "Various clubs have shown an interest and he will leave Udinese at the end of the season.

    "Despite everything that has been said and written, neither I nor the club have received offers for Sanchez [in January]. But I can confirm he will change clubs in June."
