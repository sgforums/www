    There's no domestic action this week, but United's stars are in Euro 2008 qualifying action across the continent.

    Fifteen Reds are in action over the next week in vital games for their respective nations. In one of the weekend's most intriguing games, Darren Fletcher could face Louis Saha, if the French striker shakes off a hamstring injury to face Scotland.

    Also noteworthy for Reds fans is the fact that United have an impressive seven members in England's squad, including Wayne Rooney, who returns to the England fold.
