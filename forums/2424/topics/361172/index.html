Park: Tevez must accept role

  • Dead_Man_Inc
    23 May 09, 18:10

    Park Ji-sung claims forward Carlos Tevez is the best player at Manchester United but rotation is the Achilles’ heel of the Argentine.

    Tevez does not expect to be a Manchester United player next season due to the protracted negotiations over a possible permanent deal and his limited starts.

    But Park sees Tevez as the perfect impact player with the Argentine having stormed off the bench to crucially reinvigorate Manchester United performances on a regular basis this season.

    The South Korean believes manager Sir Alex Ferguson has the balance just right in order to keep his players fresh and not overstretched which Tevez must come to grips with.

    “Every player has a different mind,” Tevez said in The Sun.

    “Some want to play every game, some want to be at a big club and just help the team but they accept how big the squad is and their role.

    “If he doesn’t like his role then he can move but if he can stand that, he can show his ability on the pitch and get more chances.

    “It is down to a player’s mind. Which one is the right way? There are no answers.

    “Man United has a lot of players. Someone can’t play every game in one season because it is too hard. You need rotation, otherwise you will be tired.”

    There is strong possibility that Park will start the Champions League final in Rome on Wednesday and Tevez will be on the bench once again.

    This situation is of little consequence to Park who was left out of the squad which dramatically beat Chelsea in a rain-soaked Moscow a year ago.

    “It was the biggest disappointment in my career so far. I’ve had crucial injuries but this was the hardest thing,” added Park.

    “All players want to play in big games like the final. So if you have the chance and then cannot play, it is heartbreaking.

    “You just have to get over it and believe that the next time or in the future you can do something.

    “I used it as a motivation. I believed United could get to the final again and I wanted to be there this time.”
