    INJURY WOE ... Wes Brown

     

     

    ALEX FERGUSON admits Wes Brown will not play again for Manchester United this season. Brown, 29, has managed just two substitute appearances since last October after picking up a foot injury.

    He appeared to be on course for a first-team return against Middlesbrough last week but was ruled out after suffering a minor setback. And Ferguson has now effectively written the defender out of his plans until next term.

    The United boss said: "Brown had a little setback during the week. "It is not major but the fact the season is coming to an end makes it difficult for him. "It is disappointing for him."
