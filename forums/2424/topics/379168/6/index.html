Rooney was covered well by Carragher, and while Berba did try to do his magic, it seems he has not established a good r/s and had had a few mis-passes to no-one where normally, a Valencia/Oshea will be there to support.     My thoughts on this match are, Basically, United did ok and rather well too cos least we forget, this was Anfield and Pool were really pump up for it... And the 2 owners were there with their families as well. But they are not going to be there every week...

    On the defence, I think we should not blame it totally as they did very well in my opinion... They are still the only defence not to concede in set plays. The problem in this match was mainly the weak midfield... It didn't do enough thats why the defence came under more pressure then usual... Rio and Vidic did very well in cutting out most of Pool's crosses into the box areas.
