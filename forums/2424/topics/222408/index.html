    SIR ALEX FERGUSON has told Louis Saha and Cristiano Ronaldo to decide who takes Manchester United's next penalty.

    Saha has netted two from the spot this season but was devastated after missing in the 1-0 defeat at Celtic last week.

    And Ronaldo, who scored in Portugal's World Cup shootout victory over England, is ready to step into the Frenchman's shoes.

    Sir Alex said: "I have left it between Louis and Cristiano to decide.

    "Sometimes if one is playing better than the other it is sensible for the one who is playing really well to take it.

    "But I have told Louis if he needs one for his hat-trick he should take it."

    Saha bounced back from his Champions League nightmare with an outstanding goal against Chelsea yesterday.
