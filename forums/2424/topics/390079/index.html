    AC MILAN are ready to join the queue for Manchester United's unsettled defender Nemanja Vidic.

    And the Italian giants will offer Dutch striker Klaas Jan Huntelaar as a makeweight in the deal.

    Vidic, 28, is already a £20million target for Real Madrid at the end of the season, although United would like another £5m.

    Alex Ferguson has paved the way for Vidic's exit by signing £10m Fulham star Chris Smalling.

    The offer of Huntelaar, 26, in part exchange could appeal as United have been short of senior forwards all season.

    Dimitar Berbatov has been playing through the pain of a knee injury while Michael Owen's long-term future is still in doubt.
