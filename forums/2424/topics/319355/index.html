Real to play Ronaldo chase by the book

  • omgukilledkenny
    31 May 08, 20:34

    Predrag Mijatovic insists Real Madrid have no agreement in place with Manchester United's Cristiano Ronaldo about a possible move to Spain.

    Madrid's pursuit of Ronaldo has been making headlines for the last few weeks, with barely a day going by without some media speculation regarding the Primera Liga side's interest in the Manchester United winger.

    Real's sporting director Mijatovic admits Madrid are keen on the 23-year-old, but has dismissed any suggestion that they have agreed anything with the player.

    "He is an objective, but we cannot do anything if his club do not want to part with him. Until this time we do not have anything with the player," he said in Portuguese newspaper Diario de Noticias.
