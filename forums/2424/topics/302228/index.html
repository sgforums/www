Neville to face Merseysiders

  • Dead_Man_Inc

    Gary Neville will make his first competitive appearance since October when he lines up for United's Reserves on Wednesday evening.

    The Reds' club captain is part of the squad which will travel to Widnes to face Everton, fuelling hopes that he will be ready for a senior return in the near future.

    Neville, who has struggled with an ankle injury since March last year, is by far the most senior member of a 16-man squad selected by Brian McClair and Jim Ryan, although first-team squad member Danny Simpson also travels.

    Ritchie Jones, Craig Cathcart and Michael Lea are also included in the party, having returned from their respective loans at Yeovil and Royal Antwerp.

    United squad: Zieler, Heaton; Neville, Simpson, Eckersley, Chester, Cathcart, Lea; Hewson, Gray, Jones, S Evans, Cleverley; Dong, Brandy, Fagan.
