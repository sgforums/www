    HOT PROSPECT At just 18... Rafael Da Silva

    NEMANJA VIDIC has hailed Rafael Da Silva as a future Manchester United star.

     

    Serbian centre-back Vidic played alongside Brazilian youngster Da Silva in United's 3-0 win over Aalborg on Tuesday and he was impressed with what he saw.

     

    Vidic said: "Rafael will be a great player.

     

    "He has a very big personality. He played his own game, had the courage to go forward but got back as well.

     

    "At just 18, he deserves a lot of credit to play at this level, in the Champions League, the way he did.

     

    "He is not afraid to get forward and did everything else as well. He has a great future at this club."
