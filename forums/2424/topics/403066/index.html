    Ronaldo – I am not going back to Manchester United

    Real Madrid star Cristiano Ronaldo has made it clear that he has no intentions of going back to Manchester United.

    Ronaldo enjoyed six great seasons at Old Trafford before making a move to Real Madrid last summer. Ronaldo was the first Ballon d’Or winner for Manchester United in over 40 years.

    Real Madrid bought Ronaldo for a world record transfer fee of £80 million. The former Sporting player enjoyed a good first season with Madrid but the club failed to win any silverware.

    Ronaldo said “Yes, Of course I miss Manchester United. If it wasn’t for Manchester, then I won’t have become the player that I am now. United is a fantastic club and everyone dreams of playing there.”

    “However I am extremely happy with my current club and I have no intentions of leaving Madrid any time soon. Even if I do leave in the future, I won’t be going back to Manchester United.”
