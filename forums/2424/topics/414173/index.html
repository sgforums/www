    Ji-Sung Park will only consider rumours of a Manchester United exit at the end of the season.

    Park, 29, has been linked with being part of a clear out by manager Sir Alex Ferguson after he endured a disappointing start to the season. The South Korea international, however, enjoyed a goalscoring display in Tuesday night's League Cup victory against Wolverhampton Wanderers and hopes to continue in that vein in the coming weeks.

    "I'm not thinking about leaving at the moment. I'll think about my future at the end of the season," Park was quoted as saying by skysports.com.

    "The win [over Wolves] was a satisfactory result and I am happy with my performance.

    "My recent form hasn't been that good so it's good to see progress."
