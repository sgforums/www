    Alan Smith has just returned from long-term injury, so I don't expect him to be as sharp as before.

    The FA Cup match against Middlesborough on Saturday again showed the Red Devils's weakness in defence, and Premiership teams will surely fancy their chances to grab at least a point United, just like they did when Chelsea's John Terry was injuried.
