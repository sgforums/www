United youngster going nowhere

    Chris Eagles will refuse to join Wigan, Sunderland and Derby when the transfer window opens again in January.

    All three clubs are hoping to lure the youngster away from Old Trafford with the promise of more regular first team football but Eagles is more than happy to stay at United under any circumstances.

    The 21 year old told the News of the World:

    "I'd happily stay here all my life. United are the only club I want to play for."
