Man Utd 'target' is for sale

  • omgukilledkenny
    5 May 09, 20:53

    Reported Manchester United target Sofiane Feghouli can leave Grenoble for the right price according to the club’s general director.

    The 19-year-old has been one of the sensations of the French league this year, with the attacking midfielder impressing the observers throughout the season.

    Sir Alex Ferguson is rumoured to be an admirer of the audacious Frenchman, and the player himself recently welcomed United’s reported interest with a massive smile on his face.

    Grenoble general director Pierre Wantiez has added fuel to fire by declaring that the teenager is the only player of his squad that could be allowed to move clubs this summer.

    “Our transfers will also depend on the sale of players,” Wantiez told the Dauphine Libere.

    “Today Feghouli is the only one who could move on. He has expressed the desire to embark on a new challenge and that is the moment for him.

    “If a reasonable offer is made, then we will see. But movements on the market will start later than usually because of the crisis.”
