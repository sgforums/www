    Manchester United want to re-sign Stoke City defensive wardrobe Ryan Shawcross, who left Old Trafford three years ago. A clause in his contract says United can have first dibs on him if they match whatever anyone else is willing to pay, particularly if it's a knock-your-baseball-cap-off £45m-plus-Kolo-Toure-and-35-other-fringe-South-Americans offer from Manchester City. Liverpool are also interested.

    Oddly peripheral never-quite-was Wes Brown says he might be on his way out of Manchester United, but he's not getting depressed about it. "I'm not getting any younger now and every game I do play, I just try to enjoy and do my best for the team," he said in a droney Leonard Cohen voice, picking listlessly at a detuned acoustic guitar.
