Owen Hargreaves Vows To Put Injury Woes Behind Him

  • Dead_Man_Inc
    29 Mar 09, 09:51

    Manchester United midfielder Owen Hargreaves is aiming to play better football than he ever has when he finally returns to action next season.

    The England international has been limited to just 37 appearances for the Red Devils since signing from Bayern Munich for around €25 million (then £17m) in 2007.

    The 28-year-old admits he has been devastated at being restricted to the sidelines with a knee problem and says he is looking to make up for lost time.

    "I'm going to come back stronger and hopefully add a couple of years on to my career," Hargreaves is quoted as saying in The Manchester Evening News.

    "It's been frustrating at times. I don't really watch our games because, as an athlete and being so competitive, it's hard to watch when you normally would be out there."
