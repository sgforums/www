    Bruce Resigned To Auctioning Stars Off

    Wigan Athletic manager Steve Bruce has admitted he'll have little choice but to sell to the highest bidder if top clubs come in for South American stars Wilson Palacios and Luis Valencia.

    “I don’t want to lose players of their calibre but I’m a realist," admitted Bruce. “If the big clubs wave their cheque books it’ll be hard to send them away. And it will have to be the big clubs because of the money we’d want.”

    The Latics want around £10m for 22-year-old Valencia, who joined the club for just half that from Villareal. 23-year-old Honduran Palacios cost the club a bargain £1m from Olimpico and Wigan are hoping to seal a massive profit by selling him less than a year later for £5m.
