    Five Reds named in England squad

    Five United players have been named in Steve McClaren's first England squad for the friendly with Greece next Wednesday at Old Trafford.

    Defenders Gary Neville, Rio Ferdinand and Wes Brown have all been selected in the 25-man squad, along with midfielder Kieran Richardson and goalkeeper Ben Foster, who will spend the season on loan at Watford.

    Wayne Rooney is suspended for England's first two European Championship qualifying matches against Andorra and Macedonia and is not included in the squad to face Greece.

    Tickets are still available for the match. More info is available on the ticket news page.

    In other international news, Giuseppe Rossi has been called up to the Italian U21 squad for the first time for the friendly with Croatia on Tuesday.
