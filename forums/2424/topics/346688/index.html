EPL Return? Only To Man United

    The on-loan Milan star has revealed a number of clubs were after him before he joined the Rossoneri, and that he could never play for any other English side apart from the Red Devils...

    David Beckham is loving his adventure with Milan and has revealed he snubbed major offers from other clubs for his services in order to join the Rossoneri.

    The Englishman has been outstanding this season and the club are doing everything to make him stay. Becks is also keen on staying in Milan and he is delighted with how everything has fallen into place so far.

    "I received offers from English teams and two other Italian sides, but I wanted Milan from the first moment I knew they were interested in me," Beckham told Sportweek.

    "I am trying to learn Italian and this will take time. I think it's important to learn the language and integrate well."

    A number of reports claimed Beckham would be heading back to the Premier League at the end of his career. But the former Manchester United man has moved to distance himself from the paper talk.

    "I don't think I will return to the Premier League because the only return I would have liked was Manchester United," added the player.

    "I enjoyed 10 great years there and it would be the only team I would play for if I returned home.

    "Manchester United are not one of the teams who contacted me though."

    Becks believes he is privileged to be playing in such a great team and he has hailed his team-mates.

    "Playing with Kaka, Ronaldinho and Pato is just too good to refuse. I think Kaka is the best in the world," concluded Beckham.
