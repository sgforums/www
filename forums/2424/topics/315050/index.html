    Despite AC Milan boss Carlo Ancelotti's declaration that Gennaro Gattuso was 100% certain to stay at the San Siro, rumours in Italy indicate that he could be en route to Manchester United...

    United coach Alex Ferguson is said to be a huge admirer of the former Rangers man, and would love to bring him to Old Trafford.

    To that end the Red Devils are alleged to be preparing a bumper four-year contract worth €3.8m (£3m) per annum for the veteran.

    However, the rossoneri are not willing to let the effervescent 30-year-old go without a fight. Any potential suitor would need to part with a figure well in excess of the €12.6m (£10m) that has been mooted by the press so far.

    Other interested clubs include Rangers, Real Madrid and Bayern München.

    Meanwhile Clarence Seedorf is said to be considering moves to Bayern or Valencia, while misfit forward Alberto Gilardino could switch to Sampdoria, Fiorentina or Roma.
