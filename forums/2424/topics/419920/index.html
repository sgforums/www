    Manchester United Targeting Move For Borussia Dortmund Starlet Shinji Kagawa - Report

     
    Shinji Kagawa, Borrusia Dortmund
    Manchester United are believed to be plotting a £15 million move for Borussia Dortmund midfielder Shinji Kagawa.

    The Red Devils are thought to have been chasing the young midfielder since his move from Cerezo Osaka to Dortmund six months ago, as a potential long-term replacement for the ageing Paul Scholes.

    Now The News of the World claims that United have made the 21-year-old attacking midfielder a £15m target, after the Japan international scored eight goals in his 17 appearances since his switch to the Bundesliga club.

    It is thought that Manchester United will also watch Kagawa during the Asian Cup.
