    EDWIN VAN DER SAR has ruled out a return to Ajax after he retires.

    The veteran keeper plans to stay at Manchester United in a non-playing role.

    Van der Sar, 40, will quit at the end of the season but will not be making good on a 12-year-old pledge to return to Amsterdam just yet.

    He said: "I'm not going back to Amsterdam, though it would be very comfortable there with Frank de Boer, Danny Blind and Dennis Bergkamp.

    "When I left 12 years ago, I said I would return but I did not know then what great years I would have with Manchester United.

    "I might occasionally visit training with them but I will not be going regularly."

    Instead, Van der Sar is mapping out a role with United.

    He added: "It may be that I'm going to give Manchester United clinics in Asia, where the club is very popular, or something in commerce.

    "I think this is interesting. I have already held some talks."
