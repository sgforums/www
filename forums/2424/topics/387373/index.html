    ROONEY is poised to become Manchester United's new captain

     

     

     

    WAYNE ROONEY is poised to become Manchester United's new captain next season.

    SunSport revealed yesterday how Gary Neville, 34, will retire this summer. And boss Alex Ferguson believes Rooney, 24, is ready to take over the armband.

    Fergie sees the striker as one of United's most vocal players and somebody the others look to for inspiration.

    He has said of Rooney in the past: "Wayne is determined, hungry and has a desire to do well. He always tries no matter how the team is playing.

    "These are wonderful qualities for a captain."
