    REAL MADRID are set to scupper Manchester United's bid for Karim Benzema.

    The Lyon striker is on Alex Ferguson's radar after he agreed to let Carlos Tevez leave Old Trafford.

    United boss Ferguson is a long-term admirer of 21-year-old France star Benzema.

    He scored 27 goals in 50 matches last season and has attracted interest from Europe's biggest clubs.

    Lyon director of communications Olivier Blanc said: "Benzema and Lyon have both confirmed their intentions to continue working together.

    "The only new issue is that Real Madrid contacted us a few days ago to discover the club's position regarding Benzema.

    "But we know the player wants to stay one more year at Lyon and we want to keep him."
