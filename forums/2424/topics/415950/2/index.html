    BERBA's So high AFTER Five

    HEAVEN ON TURF ... Berba can't believe it

     

     

     

    DIMITAR BERBATOV was in dreamland after making Premier League history.

    The Manchester United hitman bagged an astonishing FIVE goals in his side's 7-1 demolition of Blackburn.

    Berbatov joins Andy Cole, Alan Shearer and Jermain Defoe as the only players to reach the landmark.

    And the Bulgarian said: "I can't believe it.

    "I have scored that many before but it was a long time ago back home in Bulgaria.

    "Today I did it in the Premier League. To stand next to people like Cole and Shearer is a great honour."

    Berba hit the ground running and laid down a marker with a goal after just 71 seconds.

    And the former Spurs man added: "The first goal was the most important.

    "It gives you the confidence to play.

    "As a striker, people do tend only to look at the goals you score.

    "I am more concerned with how I play but today was all about goals for me."
