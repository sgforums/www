  • omgukilledkenny

    According to reports on Saturday morning, the Reds are believed to be pondering a surprise £5million move for West Ham United’s Anton Ferdinand, younger brother of current number five Rio.

    The 23-year-old has often been credited with having as much talent as his sibling, but he has so far been unable to really stamp his authority on the Premier League and earn a place in the senior England set-up.

    However, with Sir Alex Ferguson looking for a replacement for Gerard Pique who is off to Barcelona, the younger Ferdinand may be given the dream chance to link up with Rio at Old Trafford.
