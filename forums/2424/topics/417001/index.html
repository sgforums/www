    Danny Welbeck in plans of Manchester United boss Sir Alex Ferguson

     

    No joke: Danny Welbeck is wanted back at United

     

     

    Sir Alex Ferguson was tempted to send a joke text to Steve Bruce this week insisting that Danny Welbeck had to return to Old Trafford immediately from his loan at Sunderland.

    It's not true, Welbeck is locked into staying for the whole season, but the fact that the player is so clearly at the forefront of Ferguson's mind shows he is one of the United starlets Ferguson plans to build his next team around.

    Despite Welbeck having a slow start in the north-east, four goals in his last three games justifies the decision to loan him out for experience.

    'Danny is doing brilliantly,' said Ferguson. 'He had problems by growing too fast. It meant he wasn't strong enough and missed a lot of football.

    'He's started to put on muscle now and looks a big lad. He played well against Chelsea and Everton and has shown what he can do in big games.'
