    Squad sheets: Manchester United v Everton

     

    Beyond this encounter is the 162nd Manchester derby on 30 April, and Sir Alex Ferguson will tell his men that if they beat Everton then a 20th championship could be clinched at the Etihad Stadium whatever Manchester City do later on Sunday afternoon at Wolverhampton Wanderers. This is the dream scenario – one David Moyes hopes he can prevent, though being able to pick his men up after the despair of being knocked out of the FA Cup semi-final at Wembley by Liverpool may prove too tough a task even for his man-management skills.
