    Manchester United’s Sir Alex Ferguson and Cristiano Ronaldo have been voted Manager of the Month and Player of the Month, respectively.

    The Red Devils continue their course to The Premier League title and their tactician Ferguson has once again shown his ability to rotate his squad at the right moments.

    It is not a big surprise that the Scot has been voted Barclays Manager of the Month for March, during which the reigning champions continued to rule their domestic league.

    Ronaldo received the award of Barclays Player of the Month, following a string of exceptional displays, including a brace at Old Trafford against Aston Villa (4-0).

    "I am sure I am a better player now than I was one year ago,” he told The Premier League’s official website.

    “I hope that trend continues. I definitely have more chances now and that is because my team-mates help me.

    "This squad is the best I have been involved in since joining United. It is more consistent and more mature than any other side."
