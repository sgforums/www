    Federico Macheda's agent has played down talk of the Manchester United starlet joining Sampdoria on a season-long loan.

    The young Italian made an instant impression in The Premier League with strikes against Aston Villa and Sunderland. He also recently started the games with Everton in the FA Cup semi-final at Wembley and at Middlesbrough.

    There is little doubt Sir Alex Ferguson views the teenager as a first-team player but some reports have indicated that Sampdoria will try and lure him back to his homeland to gain more experience.

    "I do not know anything," agent Giovanni Bia told TMW.

    "I do not know if the two clubs have talked but I do not think so."

    With Carlos Tevez's position at Old Trafford still unclear, there could be some changes to United's attacking options next term but Macheda is clearly somebody with a bright future.
