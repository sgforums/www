    BIG guns Manchester United and Tottenham have avoided each other in the Carling Cup semi-finals.

    The two remaining Premier League teams will meet in the final at Wembley if they can overcome their lower-league opposition over two legs next month.

    Last season's Double winners United face Derby, who upset Stoke this week.

    And holders Tottenham are up against shock kings Burnley, who have already knocked out London giants Arsenal and Chelsea.

     

    Carling Cup semi-final draw

    Tottenham v Burnley

    Derby v Manchester United

    Ties to be played over two legs during weeks commencing January 5 and 19
