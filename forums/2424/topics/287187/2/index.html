    1 Manchester United are returning to their best attacking football. The kind of football that won them the Premier League last year.

    2 But they still have a big John O'Shea-shaped weakness in their side. Fergie has fairly ruthlessly moved on United youngsters who didn't quite make the grade to lesser clubs. How did O'Shea survive?

    3 Whoever said Manchester United lacked a natural goalscorer this season had obviously forgotten about Rio Ferdinand!

    4 Wayne Rooney is also rediscovering a taste for finding the net.

    5 Man Yoo could have scored any number of goals if their finishing had been more clinical.

    6 Dynamo Kiev hate defending almost as much as United love to attack.

    7 In another life Manchester United's attack would be a sprint relay squad (according to Clive Tyldesley).

    8 The number of headed goals scored by Cristiano Ronaldo defies both the amount of gel on his hair and his reluctance to get any other part of his body dirty.

    9 Darren Fletcher is Mr Invisible. I honestly forgot he was on the park at times last night.

    10 Anderson is starting to fulfil his potential a little. He put in one of his best United performances so far (not that there had been too many vintage ones beforehand!).
