Ronaldo - Nov Player of the Month

  • zocoss

    Portugal wing wizard Cristiano Ronaldo has won November's Player of the Month award after inspiring Manchester United to an unbeaten month.

    As well as creating numerous goalscoring opportunities for his team-mates, Ronaldo was in lethal form himself in front of goal, netting a free-kick in the 3-0 win over Portsmouth and another long-range effort in the 3-0 success over Everton.

    The winger's two goals in November took his season tally in the Premiership up to five, while he has also notched up three assists. This is the first time Ronaldo has won the Player of the Month award.

    Jim Hytner, from the awards panel, said: "Ronaldo has been in excellent form, putting in a string of outstanding performances on the wing.

    "His strike against Portsmouth has got to be a contender for goal of the month."
