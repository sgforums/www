    MANCHESTER United may have the best football stadium in Europe - but they've been given a red card for their match-day food.
    02 October 2007

    MANCHESTER United may have the best football stadium in Europe - but they've been given a red card for their match-day food.

    A survey of fans in Britain voted Arsenal top when it comes to nosh.

    And the Gunners' new Emirates Stadium came second in the best grounds league.

    Liverpool 'kopped' the award for best atmosphere, followed by Celtic and Portsmouth.

    Barcelona came second in the culinary stakes, with Norwich City third.

    After Man United, fans reckoned the foulest grub is dished out in Scotland by Rangers and Celtic.

    One Manchester United fan said: 'The grub on matchdays is terrible. It's always half-cold and is overpriced as well.'

    But an Old Trafford spokesman insisted: 'We serve up the right ingredients on and off the pitch.'

    Tottenham's White Hart Lane stadium has the worst public transport access, according to the survey of 1,100 season ticket holders.
