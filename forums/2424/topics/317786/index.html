    Wigan star Antonio Valencia claims to know nothing about Manchester United's reported interest in him and suggested that he is likely to stay at the JJB Stadium for at least another year in any case.

    The Ecuador winger has blossomed into one of the brightest young talents in the Premiership, which has prompted rumours of firm interest from the likes of Barcelona and United.

    “I have not been told anything about it,” he insisted. "I never spoke with Manchester United."

    When pressed on whether he would be at Wigan next year, the 22-year-old replied: “Yes, I think so. It seems that they want me to stay one more year.

    “I feel good to be able to play in The Premier League," he added, before admitting that he doesn't plan to stay at Wigan forever.

    “I hope this will help me play for a bigger team. If it does not happen this year, maybe next year," he concluded.
