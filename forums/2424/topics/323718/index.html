    Brazilian twins complete their move to United

     

     

    Brazilian twins Fábio and Rafael da Silva have completed their move to Manchester United.

    Fábio, a left-back, and right-back Rafael agreed to join in United February 2007, but under FIFA rules they had to wait until they turned 18-years-old on Wednesday to sign professional contracts.

    The Brazil Under-17 internationals have spent the last year with Fluminense and will now move to Manchester with their family.
