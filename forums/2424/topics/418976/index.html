    Manchester United Linked With Move For Real Madrid Midfielder Lassana Diarra - Report

    Real Madrid enforcer Lassana Diarra being monitored by Manchester United.

     

     
    Lassana Diarra

     

     

    Manchester United are set to launch a January bid for Real Madrid midfielder Lassana Diarra, reports from Spain suggest.

    The former Arsenal and Chelsea midfielder has had his appearances for Los Blancos limited by the form of Xabi Alonso and Sami Khedira, and is thought to be looking for first team football away from the Santiago Bernabeu.

    The Red Devils meanwhile are looking to add depth to their central area, with Owen Hargreaves perennially injured and Paul Scholes edging closer to retirement, and Diarra's power and physical approach have impressed Sir Alex Ferguson.

    Publication AS comments that the English Premier League side have tabled a €12 million bid for the French international, and the Spanish giants may consider cashing in on Diarra in order to fund swoops for other targets.

    Diarra moved to Madrid in January 2009 from Portsmouth for a fee of €19m.
