    6. Quagliarella seems the most likely of all listed here. From what I've seen, he does seem to be more of an instinctive striker. Haven't seen his shots to goal ratio, but his style doesn't quite seem to United now. Likes to hog the ball.

    7.Huntelaar seems a bit lightweight at times. Instinctive goal poacher who's sort of a mix of Pippo Inzaghi and RVN. Would likely be expensive and might resist a move away, especially since Ajax were just pipped to the title by PSV on the last day by goal difference.

    I'd like Afonso Alves as always, tall, strong, with good movement and he's a dead-ball specialist in the mould of Juninho Pernambucano. I believe he'd suit United as of the times I've seen him, he's always passing and moving,the United way. The lure of European football from United might be too tempting to resist.
