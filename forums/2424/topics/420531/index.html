    Manchester City manager Roberto Mancini is reportedly making a last attempt to sign Sunderland midfielder Jordan Henderson in front of bitter rivals Manchester United, according to The Mirror.

    The newspaper revealed last week that the 20-year-old would prefer a move to United because of a higher chance of receiving regular first-team football.

    However, reports suggest that Mancini remains a massive fan of the youngster – who was hailed as “the best young British footballer around” by manager Steve Bruce.

    It is estimated that it will cost City £20 million to lure Henderson to Eastlands.
