    The Wembley WAGS

     

     

     

     

    A little-known fact about footballers is that they occasionally meet girls and are seen out and about with them.

    But ever since the England WAGS made such a splash in the forest retreat of Baden Baden during the 2006 World Cup, the spotlight at major events has been not just on the men in competition, but on the women supporting them.

    With some star names bound to walk Wembley way and support their husbands and boyfriends (HABS? It will never catch on), allow us to brief you on who's with who - from pop stars to models to childhood sweethearts...

    GERARD PIQUE AND SHAKIRA

    The defender is currently winning just about every trophy going with Barcelona, and now has the 34-year-old pop star Shakira on his arm. Life's tough.

     

     

    WAYNE ROONEY AND COLEEN ROONEY

    All's bliss in the Rooney household - these days, anyway. Here they are on a lovely day out seeing what daddy does at work.

     

     

     

     

    LIONEL MESSI AND ANTONELLA ROCCUZZO (OR LUCIANA SALAZAR?)

     Puyol has been with Spanish model Malena Costa since September 2010. Earlier in the season he dedicated a goal to her. Rumours of a wedding proposal-based goal celebration if he scores in the final are unconfirmed.

    Make no mistake, this is Ryan Giggs's wife - the pair married in 2007 and have two children together. Stacey made sure to go out on the field and join in with the team's celebrations at the weekend as they won their 19th league title.

    BIG CALL ... Javier Hernandez

     

     

    JAVIER HERNANDEZ was tonight handed a starting spot for Manchester United in the Champions League final.

    The Mexico striker, 22, has impressed by netting 20 goals in his first season in English football.

    And United boss Alex Ferguson has put his faith in the young star by giving him a place against Barcelona at Wembley.

    Darren Fletcher was only able to make the bench as Fergie picked the same line-up as SunSport revealed on Thursday.

    Top scorer Dimitar Berbatov was not even named as a sub.

    Barcelona's starting XI was widely predicted and boss Pep Guardiola has selected his tried and tested players.

    Former Liverpool midfielder Javier Mascherano will start at centre-back with Barca skipper Carles Puyol only on the bench.

     

     

    Manchester United: Van der Sar; Fabio, Vidic, Ferdinand, Evra; Valencia, Carrick, Giggs, Park; Rooney, Hernandez. Subs: Kuszczak, Smalling, Fletcher, Scholes, Anderson, Nani, Owen.
