    Manchester United are seeking their 100th victory in the Champions Cup/Champions League.

    Sporting Lisbon and Man United have met each other only twice in European Cup football.

    In the 1963-64 Cup Winners' Cup, Sporting eliminated United in the quarter-finals.

    After United won their home leg 4-1, Sporting bounced back big by them United 5-0 in the return, and went on to win the Cup, their only European trophy ever.

    Sporting Lisbon beat MTK Budapest in the final of the 1963-64 Cup Winners' Cup. They are participating in the Champions League for the fourth time, and have never survived the group phase.

    Manchester United have won the Champions Cup/Champions League twice (1968, 1999), the Cup Winners' Cup once (1991), the European Super Cup (1991) and the Intercontinental Club Cup (1999).

    They're about to embark on their 13th Champions League campaign, which makes them joint record holders with FC Porto. It is also their 12th successive Champions League campaign, which is the best streak ever.

    Sporting have not won any of their last five Champions League matches, losing the last two.

    Manchester United have lost their last two Champions League away matches.

    Carlos Paredes and Marat Izmailov are the most experienced Champions League players in the Sporting squad with 16 Champions League matches each. Derlei is the Champions League top scorer at Sporting with three career goals.

    Gary Neville (98 ), Ryan Giggs (95) and Paul Scholes (95) are three Manchester United players that are ranked amongst the 10 players with most Champions League appearances.

    Giggs is the Champions League top scorer in Manchester United's squad with 23 Champions League goals.
