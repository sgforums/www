Man Utd pre-season tour to South Africa

  • Dead_Man_Inc
    13 Jul 06, 21:03
    A United squad blending youth and experience will fly to Durban to begin their 10-day tour of South Africa this afternoon.

    The old heads of Ryan Giggs, Paul Scholes and Ole Gunnar Solskjaer are joined by teenage Academy graduates Danny Simpson and Jonny Evans, while Ben Foster is set to make his first United appearance almost a year after joining from Stoke City.

    Foster spent last season at Watford, and is joined by five other returning loanees - Simpson, Liam Miller, Chris Eagles, Phil Bardsley and David Jones - all looking to make their mark in the absence of first-team regulars.

    Chinese striker Dong Fangzhuo - guesting from Royal Antwerp - also makes his second appearance on a pre-season programme, having played and scored in last summer's Asia tour.

    Sir Alex Ferguson's side have one full day to prepare for their Saturday clash with Orlando Pirates before departing Durban for Cape Town, where they will face Kaizer Chiefs next Tuesday.

    Both South African sides will playoff on Thursday July 20 for the right to face United again, in Johannesburg on July 22, before the Reds fly home.
