    Nani has dismissed suggestions that he is unhappy at Manchester United.

    The Portugal international has been tipped for a summer exit after failing to stamp his influence at Old Trafford, despite occasional flashes of brilliance.

    After a bit-part role in the latest championship triumph, his future is unclear but the winger claims he is settled in Manchester.

    "I won one more title and am very happy to win it for these two consecutive years," he explained to Maisfutebol.

    "Being at such a club is a privilege."

    Portugal are struggling to qualify for the World Cup finals under Carlos Queiroz but Nani remains focused on the job in hand.

    "Everyone likes to be in big competitions and it would be great for me because I have never been at a World Cup," he added.

    "We are playing well but not scoring goals. We've just been out of luck."
