Ronaldo and Rooney fall foul of clothes thief

  • omgukilledkenny

    Not too many will come away from Old Trafford with anything this season, but that isn't the case at Manchester United's training ground.

    Despite tight security at Carrington, first teamers are reporting a rash of mysterious disappearances from their personal belongings. Ronaldo has lost his shirt while Wayne Rooney has seen a pair of Gucci shoes go walkies.

    Staff are looking for someone with the same size feet as Rooney and assistant manager Mick Phelan, who is currently missing a pair of trainers.
