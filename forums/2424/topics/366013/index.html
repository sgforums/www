    Wigan's Antonio Valencia Poised For Manchester United Medical - Report

    Sir Alex Ferguson is close to sealing his first signing of the summer, as the Ecuadorian jets in for a medical...

     

    Antonio Valencia is set  to undergo a medical at Manchester United today, as his transfer from Wigan Athletic looks ever closer to completion.

    According to The Daily Mirror, Valencia flew in to Manchester last night, with both sides having agreed a £16 million deal. The Ecuadorian winger will go some way towards filling the gap left by Cristiano Ronaldo's departure for Real Madrid.

    Los Merengues and Bayern Munich were also said to be interested in Valencia, but, having settled in the Premier League, his first choice was always United. His wages will be approximately £75,000 per week, around four times what he was earning at Wigan.

    Valencia made 47 appearances for the Latics in all competitions last season, scoring six goals.
