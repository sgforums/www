Rio Keen To See New Boys

  • Dead_Man_Inc
    14 Jul 07, 00:01
    Reds defender Rio Ferdinand is looking forward to casting an eye over United new-boys Nani and Anderson.

    He'll have to wait to see Brazilian midfielder Anderson, who is likely to join up with his new team-mates after the forthcoming Asia Tour, but Rio is almost certain to see first-hand what Portuguese winger Nani can do in the next two weeks.

    The 20-year-old looks set to be included in the squad that departs for Tokyo this weekend as part of a four-game tour of the Far East, and should be joined by fellow close-season arrival, Owen Hargreaves.

    And Rio says that it's not just the fans who can't wait to see them in action.

    "We're looking forward to seeing them play as well," he told MUTV. "All the players here are the same, we want to see them play.

    "Obviously we know more about Owen than Nani and Anderson," he added. "I've not really seen either of them play. Anderson hasn't joined up with us yet, but when he does, as with Nani, we'll be keen to see what he can do."
