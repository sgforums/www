    But nothing stays perfect in football very long and amid many reservations about the strength of his midfield, Ferguson has added quickly to give himself a much stronger pool.

    However, he now needs to make sure that the areas in front of and behind that midfield have as much strength in depth, while the exit door at Old Trafford also beckons for several players.

    Kieran Richardson, for one, surely needs to move on. The arrivals of Nani and Anderson do not bode well for his future at a club where his game has failed to evolve since he returned from his successful loan spell at West Brom two years ago.

    And while he has far too much promise to be offloaded to Championship side Sheffield United, as has been speculated, a move down the Premiership beckons for a player who is not going to learn anything if he continues to win medals by virtue of playing (or at least appearing in) enough games.
