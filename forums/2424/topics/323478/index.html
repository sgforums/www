    Swansea boss Martinez in Man Utd assistant link

    Swansea chairman Huw Jenkins has strenuously denied media speculation Roberto Martinez is in the running to become Sir Alex Ferguson's assistant manager at Manchester United.

    With Carlos Queiroz linked heavily with the vacant managerial job with the Portugal national side, there were reports Swans boss Martinez was in the frame for a move to Old Trafford.

    But Jenkins said: "It's nonsense."

    He added to the Swansea Evening Post: "We haven't heard anything and I know for a fact there's nothing in these stories."
