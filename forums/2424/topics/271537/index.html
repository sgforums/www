No Italy return for Rossi

    Giuseppe Rossi is unlikely to return to Italy next season despite his recent successful loan spell with Parma.



    "There are a lot of foreign clubs interested in him and I think his future will be abroad [outside Italy]," Pastorello said.

    Parma had reportedly bid eight million euros for the Italy Under-21 star, whose nine goals in 19 games for them last season effectively saved the Ducale from relegation to the second division.

    This offer was not accepted, however, and speculation that the hitman might move to Napoli or Fiorentina appears to have little foundation.

    Rossi now looks set to either stay in the Premier League or seal a move to a Spanish Primera Liga outfit.

    "Lots of English and Spanish clubs are on his trail and I am confident of closing a deal as soon as possible - maybe before Wednesday," added Pastorello in an interview broadcast on Radioblu on Tuesday.
