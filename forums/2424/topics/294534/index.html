    Sir Alex Ferguson has expressed his belief that Portuguese wing wizard Cristiano Ronaldo is developing into a better player than Eric Cantona.

    The Manchester United manager heaped praise on Ronaldo after his match-winning free-kick gave the Old Trafford side a 2-1 Champions League victory over Sporting Lisbon on Tuesday.

    Ferguson said: "I have had some great players here over the years - Bryan Robson and Roy Keane. I love Eric Cantona. He was a marvellous player.

    "But, in terms of sheer ability and imagination, Cristiano is without doubt looking the best now."
