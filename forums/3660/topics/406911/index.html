    Hello,

    are you specifically interested in only CJC and MJC or perhaps the Arts stream in general.

    If you are interested in the former then I would suggest CJC because I'm an old boy! Haha, jokes aside I personally feel that standards wise both CJC and MJC are on par if you are talking about the Arts stream. Rankings can back me up on this. When I was still in college the cut-off point was 14 for Arts, I believe it is now 11 or 12? Furthermore CJ has a very strong Arts culture and experienced and knowledgeable teachers who know what they are doing. For my batch of History and Lit students, we had 100% passes and 75% distinctions! With the introduction of the new ELP or smtg like that, CJ arts faculty is actually more enhanced towards provided you with a better arts education. Another plus point is that the school is located right next to town!

    I don't think I'm the right person to comment about MJ since I'm not an alumni.

    So here's my 2 cents worth. I encourage you to go to CJ or maybe just check out the culture and student life there during the open houses. I never regretted going there.
