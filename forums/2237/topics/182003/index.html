    BANGKOK : Thailand warned Saturday of a series of unusual underwater earthquakes in the Andaman Sea, urging caution along its southern coast where the tsunami hit in 2004.

    The National Disaster Warning Center said that between March 9-11 it had detected 31 quakes ranging in magnitude from 4.0 to 5.3 near the Andaman Islands, some 500 kilometres (300 miles) east of the southern Thai province of Ranong.
