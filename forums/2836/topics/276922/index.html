    Why did you take up archery? What makes it attractive?

    I guess I have to start the ball rolling in this forum with something simple.

    I'm an avid fan of shooting sports. I love to shoot. It doesn't matter whether its air rifle or air pistol, M-16s or pistols. There is always this magical feeling whenever I launch a projectile off at the target. I wish I could describe how that feels with words, but I really can't.

    I took up archery by accident when I enrolled in a local institution that did not have an active air rifle club. I was rather disappointed at first, until I saw the archery club. The shooter in me figured that, the next best thing to air rifle was archery. Before I knew it, I was hooked to the sport.

    I guess one main attraction to archery is that the bow can be owned, instead of being locked away in some club armory. Various modifications from different manufacturers also makes the bow a more... personal piece of equipment.

    And thats how I started archery, and why I still am shooting.

    For right handers, the bow is held in the left hand, the string in the right hand.
