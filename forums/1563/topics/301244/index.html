    The very first local series set in an old folks' home will have you grabbing your sides in chuckles! Stars Bryan Wong, Kym Ng, Marcus Chin, Henry Thia, Li Wenhai, Tang Hu, Henry Heng

    From Jan 9, 2008, every Wed @ 8.30pm
    Total no. of episodes: 13

    Cologne (Bryan Wong), a washed-up and out-of-love scriptwriter ends up at a home for the elderly as a caretaker/janitor. He unwittingly turns the place inside out, and transforms everyoneÂ’s life in the process. He encourages the old folks to relive and pursue their dreams, turning the austere and oppressive home into a vibrant and colourful place. In return, the old folks work together to help Cologne realize his dream.

    Follow the misadventures of Cologne and five colourful denizens of the home - Xiao Bai Long (Marcus Chin), Han Lao Shi (Li Wenhai), Xiao Ming (Henry Thia), Tian Cai (Tang Hu) and Ge Ge (Henry Heng). Along the way, he antagonizes, but is also attracted to the young and beautiful Miss Lim (Kym Ng), the director of the home who administers a strict set of regulations.
