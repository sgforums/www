    This survey is conducted by The Straits Times YouthInk for the column "My Poll", which seeks to highlight trends and perceptions among youths in Singapore. The results will be collated and may be published in The Straits Times YouthInk section.

    Please answer this survey only if you are aged between 17 and 27, and are currently resident in Singapore or Singaporean.

    This week, we are examining a political event that we as Singaporeans have no control over, but whose outcome will undoubtedly have significant global repurcussions.

    It has been brewing since 2006 and will finally reach its thrilling conclusion when voters head to the polls on November 4th, 2008 -- The US Presidential Elections. Given the chance, who would you vote for? Would you even vote at all? What are the factors influencing your decision? Do you see Singapore ever having such a direct and conrontational style of political sparring? Tell YouthInk what you think!

    The survey will take 5 minutes to complete. All questions marked with a red asterisk are compulsory. All survey responses, apart from comments, will be treated with the utmost of anonymity.

    Thank you for your time!
