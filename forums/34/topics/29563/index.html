    If you are ever there at the Esplanade, you may want to check out these restaurants:-

    Level 1
    California Bistro (#01-11)
    The best of American food await you at California Bistro. Pasta, pizza, ribs, freshly baked breads, cakes and pastries will tantalise your taste buds!

    Colours by the Bay #01-13/15)
    A new concept in dining with seven restaurants under one roof -
    ThaiExpress, Shima Aji (Japanese), Al Dente (Italian), Local Bite (local cuisine), Tien Yuen Kitchen, The Embassy (wine bar/disco) and Garlic Restaurant (Asian-Western fusion). Sit in one eatery and order from all seven!

    Fabulous Fizz(#01-09)
    Housing an exquisite c! ollection of the best champagnes and sparkling wines, this restaurant cum bar serves modern French cuisine.

    Ristorante Teatro (#01-14/16)
    Ristorante Teatro (Italian for Theatre Restaurant) combines continental fine-dining with live stage performance experience.

    Via Mar (#01-10/12)
    Spanish specialty restaurant and wine bar serving an array of tapas, paella and exotic Cochinillo Asado (roast suckling pig).

    Well-Bean Bistro (#01-02/04)
    Healthy lifestyle bistro offering healthy & tasty cuisines, juices & snacks and the Original Well-Bean Coffee with soy benefits.

    Level 2
    Chinese Tea House (#02-02)
    Enjoy a truly aromatic experience with the widest selection of tea
    leaves from China and Taiwan. Tea wares and accessories available as well as tea sampling and tea-brewing demonstrations.

    My Humble House (#02-27/09)
    Serving an enticing combination of traditional and progressive Chinese cuisine, My Humble House showcases the private collections of three Top Tung Lok masterchefs combined with Beijing's renowned designer-classical musician cum chef, Zhang Jin Jie's exceptional cooking and artistic presentation.

    Ichiban Boshi(#02-14)
    A generous selection of fresh sushi served on conveyer-belt at
    this Japanese family-oriented restaurant.

    Rice Paper (#02-25)
    An Australian-influenced Vietnamese restaurant featuring all-time
    favourites such as Pho Bo (beef noodle) and Goi Cuon (Prawn Roll). Expect also a wide variety of salads and ricepaper wraps, which will be making their first appearance in Singapore.

    Senses (#02-12)
    Savour a fusion of sumptuous Eastern and Western cuisine offering nachos, steaks, stews, pasta, Asian favourites and local fare. Featuring also a cocktail and sandwich bar as well as a VIP room for private events.
