    RESULTS

    A-Type
    You are mentally about 65 years old. A few more years
    and your peers will consider you to belong at a
    nursing home. Regretfully, you are really living a
    life of a 65 year-old senior citizen. You are lacking
    a great amount of ambitious spirit and cringe at the
    thought of accomplishing, what is otherwise a fun
    task. But your human warmth is as deep as the ocean
    and your resilience doesn't fluctuate whatever you are
    working. Keep these strong points in mind and think
    about mental attitude.

    B-Type
    You are mentally about 35 years old! Your behavior is
    in the 20's but you don't experience the many
    embarrassing moments anymore, which make you mentally
    an older person. You think you no longer have time to
    waste anymore and smiling doesn't get you far anymore.
    It is understandable to ignore strangers in that way
    but you have to be aware of others around you with a
    certain politeness or they will perceive you to be of
    bad character. Supposedly, it is very common for
    people in their late 30's to experience sudden
    emotional explosion. Bring your mental age down by
    becoming aware of your innocence. Try wearing white
    clothes, which will present a pure, youthful
    appearance. You should also temporarily get rid of
    your long lasting pen and start using a pencil (symbol
    of clarity/purity) to further enhance a younger state
    of mind.

    C-Type
    You are mentally in the Late 20's. If you are teen and
    got this result, you are associated to an old baby
    (oxymoron). You are a motherly character to your
    friends, looking after them and wanting to protect
    them. Your mental age is about 26 at this point.
    People feel very comfortable around you because you
    fuss around.But you should remember not to become idle
    about your appearance. The clothes you wear also match
    the mature age you are at. Bright clothes, such as
    yellow, could make you feel younger if you ever think
    about returning to your early 20's. How about chatting
    with your friends over greasy hamburgers at a fast
    food place? Remember that it is up to you to create a
    vibrant atmosphere.

    D-Type
    You are mentally about 20 years old. Your actual age
    and mental age are at the same level, which is the
    ideal. This means that you are a well-balanced person.
    At this point there is nothing to worry about when you
    show immature signs at times because that is normal
    for your age. Remember to live happily day-by-day and
    not to lose your confidence or self-esteem. The color
    of confidence is brown, so grab a pair of shoes.

    E-Type
    You are mentally in the teens. It is your naive that
    leads you to act selfishly and without concerning
    about others. The world revolves around you and you
    have the tendency to steer towards the other direction
    if you don't agree with others. It is important to
    listen to other people's opinion or advice but they
    just don't sit well in your stomach. If you would like
    to hear compliments, you should try to curb your
    temper and to be less selfish. Try wearing blue clothes and
    musky perfume, which may help pacify your impatient
    mood.

    F-Type
    You are mentally a baby. You have to realize that
    you are the only person who is responsible for your
    life's decisions. It is inexcusable to call out your
    mother's name for every little problem and this is the
    obvious reason why you are a joke to others. Question
    yourself how much you should depend on others and when
    you are your own person. Green is a sign of maturity.
    Carrying around a snakeskin or crocodile skin wallet
    may give off an aura of elegance. Choosing a religious
    faith or becoming more serious with your current faith
    may help you achieve a more mature you.

    If you can't change your fate, change your attitude..
