    "Style Wedding 2004"

    STAR STYLE Text By: Steve Thio
    CELEBRITY JEANETTE AW IS KNOWN NOT ONLY FOR HER MEGAWATT SMILE, BUT ALSO FOR HER FUN, CASUAL SENSE OF STYLE.
    As the spokesperson for SK Jewellery, she epitomises all that is young, vibrant and stylish in today's modern woman.
    More than just a collection of sleek pieces for stylish gals, SK also has simple designs to match any modern bridal ensemble, Jeanette took time off her busy schedule to share some of her wedding wishes with us, and selects her favourite SK pieces to complete her dream bridal look.

    WHAT WOULD YOUR DREAM BRIDAL GOWN LOOK LIKE?
    Something simple and elegant, like a white gown with a champagne sheen. Elegance would be the key for the design. After all, which girl doesn't want to look like a princess on her big day?

    DESCRIBE YOUR DREAM WEDDNG.
    Definitely a beach wedding! I love the sea and it's the ideal location for a romantic celebration. Being in a relaxed, natural surroundings with family and close friends would make it a dream come true for me.

    TO MATCH YOUR BRIDAL THEME, WHAT JEWELLERY WOULD YOU SELECT TO MATCH YOUR GOWN?
    Something simple yet sparkling and elegant enough to make me look like a princess!

    ARE THERE ANY PIECES FROM SK's COLLECTION YOU LIKE?
    The EternityTM range, which has diamonds from Belgium and comes in classic styles of the solitaire and trilogy designs. And I love the Starry StarryTM piece, which I received on my birthday!

    WHAT IS IT ABOUT SK JEWELLERY THAT APPEALS TO YOU?
    SK's pieces are really simple yet elegant, nothing too extravagant or fleshy, which is what I find most appealing. And they match my casual and formal clothes easily. Ultimately, jewellery is meant to complement your own individual style and SK Jewellery does that for me. Their designs reflect my taste in fashion and lifestyle perfectly!
