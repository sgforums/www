    Deep down in everyone's heart,
    this Jigsaw with the illustration of LOVE
    is waiting to be completed...........

    Not all shall complete this Jigsaw using a lifetime
    some never....some manage.....
    some r happy......some r not.....
    some regretted.....while others live happily ever after....
    so wat's urs?

    Putting together the small pieces
    the Jigsaw is slowly completing
    except for the missing pieces....
    WHERE R THEY?...juz cant seem to be found???

    The Search begins & during the journey
    some give up while the rest move on...
    explaining why some never....some manage.....

    Moving on does not promise a smooth journey as well
    Some take a Short Time.
    They rush into fitting all the 'missing pieces' together
    without much thinking & certainly with much recklessness
    either blindly or by force......

    Observing carefully, one can notice the distortion in the Jigsaw
    Lifting up the fitted 'missing pieces'
    reveal how badly spoilt the shape of the pieces has gone thru......
    Nonetheless, this Jigsaw 'seems' completed
    or rather, even if the RITE 'missing pieces' come along
    they can never fit in properly.....

    Moving on does not promise a smooth journey as well
    Some take a Longer Time.
    They meet many potential 'missing pieces'
    but completing the Jigsaw is not possible
    as some 'missing pieces' r still not found
    However, Life still goes on......
    with some grumbles & hopes tt the Jigsaw may be completed

    Days & Yrs passed.....
    the remaining 'missing pieces' r found....
    and they juz seem to fit in so nicely
    onto the uncompleted Jigsaw
    Potential 'missing pieces' found earlier
    r removed fr the Jigsaw.....
    Now they look pretty much older
    with scatches on them....
    Discarded aside to find some fitting uncompleted Jigsaw
    explaining why some r happy......some r not.....
    some regretted.....while others live happily ever after....

    There r still many other possibilities to complete the Jigsaw....
    Some begin the Search with some
    [i]'Novice' ideas of Illustrating Love[/i]
    but only to realize tt the whole Illustration for
    [Love Changes with time...[/i]
    another Jigsaw will be Illustrated with Love again.....
    Again & again........................

    [b]Maybe Deep down in everyone's heart,
    this Jigsaw with the illustration of LOVE
    tt is waiting to be completed.........
    has long been Tainted..................................
    the 'Normal Cycle' tt Love shall follow
    is no longer juz Normal....
    it's now following a 'Norm Cycle'.....................................    [b]
