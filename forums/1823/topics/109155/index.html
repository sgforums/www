    The Nokia Video Call Stand PT-8 enables face-to-face communication in the 3G network with your compatible Nokia phone. Intuitive and easy to use, it is an essential tool for effective communication.

    Product Compatibility:
    Nokia Video Call Stand is compatible with the Nokia 6630 phone.


    Unique Video Call Solution
    Enables face-to-face video telephony call in 3G network when connected to the compatible Nokia phone
    Adjust volume, mute received audio
    Mute the video

    Intuitive and Easy to Use
    Comfortable indoor use (no hands needed to hold the phone)
    Integrated Hands Free from compatible phone and Bluetooth headset can be used

    Quality Video Picture
    CIF MOS sensor with 352x288 resolution and 16 bit color (65k)
    Video picture capturing at QCIF (176x144) resolution at 15 frames per second
    Fixed focus lens from 0.4m to 1.0m
    In-built 2x digital zoom functionality


    Classic and Distinctive Design
    Adjustable and foldable desk stand design
    Functions as a desktop charger


    Setting up video telephony
    User sets up a video telephony by attaching compatible phone to the video call stand
    When recipient answers, both faces can be seen on the screen of the compatible Nokia phones and they can hear each other through the phone loudspeaker or wireless headset
    Video telephony can also be set up even when the compatible phone is not attached to the Nokia Video Call Stand PT-8. Images can only be transmitted when the phone is attached to the video stand
