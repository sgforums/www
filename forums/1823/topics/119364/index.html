The chic, matte anodized aluminum case resists stains and scratches, all the while protecting your iPod mini.     The Firewire cable can be used to transfer songs (provided you got a Firewire card in your PC) or to connect your iPod to the AC Adapter for charging.

    Since the 2nd generation iPod mini doesn't come with the Firewire cable and the AC Adapter, you have to charge it by connecting your iPod mini to your PC using the USB cable. The optional AC Adapter costs $54.

    The 4GB model costs $348 while the 6GB model costs $438 in most stores. If you're a student or educator, you can buy the 4GB model at $320 and the 6GB model at $403. Apple will deliver it to your house without additional charges.
