    Heres a rundown of the terms and codes every Honda lover should know.

    Terms/Abbreviations
    --------------------------
    JDM - Japanese Domestic Market
    PDM - Philippine Domestic Market
    USDM - United States Domestic Market
    EUDM - European Domestic Market
    OEM - Original Equipment Manufacturer
    CTR - Civic Type R
    ITR - Integra Type R

    ENGINE CODES
    ---------------------------
    ZC - 1600 cc carburated DOHC
    D12- 1200 cc stock of EG Hatch
    D15- 1500 cc stock of Lx
    D15B- 1500 cc Vtec 20 lobes
    D16Z- 1600 cc Vtec stock of vti
    B16A- 1600 cc Vtec DOHC stock of JDM EF SirII/EG6/EG9
    B16B- 1600 cc Vtec DOHC stock of JDM EK9 (Civic Type R)
    B18B- 1800 cc DOHC stock of USDM Integra GSR
    B18C5 - 1800 cc Vtec DOHC stock of Integra Type R

    B18B- 1800cc DOHC non-VTEC(USDM Integra LS)
    B18C- 1800cc DOHC VTEC(USDM/JDM Integra GSR)

    F20 - SOHC 2.0

    F22B -DOHC 2.2 non-VTEC

    H22 - DOHC 2.2 VTEC

    H23 - DOHC 2.3 non-VTEC

    Just some more info on the K20 engine

    JDM Integra DC5R - K20A
    USDM RSX Type S - K20A2
    EUDM/JDM Civic Type R - K20A2
    CR-V/USDM RSX Base - K20A3

    K20A3 - 02/03 civic SI, base 02/03 RSX
    K20A2 - 02/03 RSX-s
    K20A - 02 CTR
    K24 - element, TSX, accord

    CHASSIS CODES
    -----------------------------

    JHMxxxxxxxxxxxx - unit manufactured in japan (Sayama plant)
    1HGxxxxxxxxxxxx - manufactured in US (Ohio plant)
    PHxxxxxxxxxxxxx - locally stamped/manufactured

    there are some locally distributed units that start with JHM, these are the 1200cc EF sold in 1991. all 1000+ units were imported from japan (SKD)

    Civics / Integras

    EF - 88-91
    EG -92-95 (Esi)
    EK -96-00(Vti, Sir)
    ES -01-present(Vti-s)

    EP3 - 2002 Civic Type R
    EK9 - 1998 Civic Type R

    DA1= USDM 1986-1989 (1st Gen Integra) Coupe
    DA3= USDM 1986-1989 (1st Gen Integra) Sedan
    DA6= USDM 1990-1993 (2nd Gen Integra) Coupe
    DA9= USDM 1990-1993 (2nd Gen Integra) Sedan
    DC2=USDM 1994-2001 (3rd Gen Integra) Coupe
    DB7=USDM 1994-2001 (3rd Gen Integra) Sedan non-VTEC
    DB8=USDM 1994-2001 (3rd Gen Integra) Sedan VTEC

    DC5 was introduced in late 2001 as a 2002 Model lineup sa US.

    DC2 - original Integra Type R (1993-2000)
    DC5 - new Integra Type R (2001-present)

    additional:
    * there are two versions of the EK, one from 96-98 and 99-00
    the 99-00 or SiR look is also called the Global look.
    * EG Coupe chasis code is EJ
    * ES Coupe or the new Si or Type R chasis code is EP

    CR-V

    B20 - 2000 cc DOHC stock of 96-00 CRV
    Gen1 97-98 engine = B20B 126 Hp
    Gen1 99-01 engine = B20Z 146 Hp

    RD1 - B20B-powered, 4WD, gen1 (128 HP)
    RD3 - B20Z-powered, 4WD, gen1 (148 HP)
    RD4 - K20A-powered, FF, gen2 (Philippine "AUV" version)
    RD5 - K20A-powered, 4WD, gen2 (JDM)
    RD7 - K24x-powered, 4WD, gen2 (USDM)
    RD8 - [?] no description available

    Torque for both engines is the same 133 lbs ft @4,300 RPM higher compression ratio for the B20Z (9.6) compared to the B20B (8.
