    synopsis

    Jinsha - A love story across space and time, a magical journey to a land of grandeur

    Archaeologist Sha found one half of a Sunbird relic by chance. To uncover the mystery, he travelled 3,000 years back in time.

    There, at a sacrificial ritual, Sha met and fell for Jin, who was wearing the other half of the Sunbird relic. Jin decided to follow Sha into a thousand years later.

    Unfortunately, the lovebirds were caught in the war between Shu and Han, and were separated amid the chaos.

    After a millennium of separation, they met again at a royal banquet. It was there that they received the blessings of Lady Flower for their enduring love.

    Will their love finally withstand the passage of three thousand years? What would Sha eventually discover?

    This popular musical production set a new Chinese Musical Record, having been staged over 300 times in Chengdu, China.
