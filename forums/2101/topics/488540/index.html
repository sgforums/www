    Overview of Encore 2015

    Encore 2015 is a collaboration between nEbO and Timbre Music Academy (TMA) with the aim to provide a platform for young budding talents between 12-25 years old to showcase their vocals and stage presence through a singing competition. Through this platform, contestants are given an opportunity to go through music lessons fully sponsored by nEbO to learn from instructors from TMA. In addition, they are able to perform at Switch by Timbre or Timbre @ The Substation through live showcases to further mould their confidence and vocal powers.

    Event Details:

    Title : Encore Auditions 2015

    Date : 11 Jul 2015

    Time : 11am to 7pm

    Registration : Not charged

    What can you expect as a 1 of the 12 Finalists of Encore 2015?

    1. A chance to undergo vocal training at Timbre Music Academy

    2. A chance to perform your very own music showcase from Aug 2015 – Dec 2015

    3. A chance to walk away with a 3 months paid residency stint at Timbre and other prizes awaiting
