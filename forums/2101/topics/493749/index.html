    SOTA Open House 2017
    8 April 2017, Saturday
    9am - 4pm

    Join us for a day of fun and discover what SOTA has to offer! Find out more about SOTA's 6-year integrated arts and academic curriculum leading to the International Baccalaureate Diploma at our Open House on Saturday, 8 April 2017. Some highlights of the SOTA Open House 2017 include:

    School Leader's Talks - Our school leader will share about SOTA's 6-year integrated arts and academic curriculum. Parents will also get to learn about the admission requirements and application procedures for the Talent Academy.

    Campus Tours - Wish to tour our award-winning campus? Let our friendly student ambassadors share their experiences and guide you on this rare opportunity to explore our vibrant campus and learning spaces!

    Curriculum Exhibition - Chat with our amiable teachers and students at the Curriculum Exhibition at the Multi-Purpose Hall on Level 6 to understand what you child may experience in his/her unique journey in SOTA.

    Arts showcases, Workshops, Curriculum sharing sessions - Experience and find out about the arts curriculum and training your child will be exposed to in SOTA! (Please note that the showcases are ticketed. Admission tickets to showcases will only be released on the actual day at the ticket collection booth, on a first-come, first-served basis.)

    More information about the programme will be updated in April 2017.

    See you at SOTA on 8 April 2017, Saturday!
