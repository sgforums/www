    Do spread the word around or email this out to friends whom you
    think may be keen to try out. The auditions are for our travelling
    roadshows. Various roles and characters for different shows in 2006.
    Suitable for full-time freelancers or actors who need a constant day
    job.

    ACTORS NEEDED:

    Actor (Male)
    18 - 28 years old
    Any race
    Fair, tall and slim
    Speaks fluent English
    Must have completed NS

    Actor (Male)
    20 - 35 years old
    Any race
    Big size and burly but cute
    Pleasant personality and can relate to children audience between the
    age of 7-12
    Speaks good English
    Must have completed NS

    Actor (Female)
    18 - 30 years old
    Malay
    Comfortable in playing dual roles
    Speaks good English

    Actor (Female)
    18 - 25 years old
    Any race
    Bubbly and fun personality
    Able to act and host and keep a crowd on their toes
    Cheerful and pleasant

    Dew at da_kinksta666@hotmail.com (with the subject: Stagehandz
    Audition).

    DATE: Friday 23rd Dec 2005
    TIME: 2.00pm - 4.30pm
    VENUE: ACTphabets, #02-19, Esplanade Mall (The Esplanade)
