    Art & Performing Arts

    Istana Art Event 2011

    Aug 07, 2011
    Istana Ground

    8am - 5pm

    (Arts and Heritage Village activities start from 10am)

    Price: Free admission

     

    ($1 for non-Singaporeans and PRs)

    Description
    Come on down to the Istana Open House and celebrate National Day with fun-filled art activities that will colour your imagination. Draw your wishes for Singapore in the On-The-Spot Art Competition and stand to win fantastic prizes. What’s more, the first 1000 submitted entries will receive an Istana Art Event goodie bag!
    Take part in a series of art activites at the Arts & Heritage Village, featuring a total of 12 museums, the People’s Association as well as the National Library Board. Be Inspired by the Installation A Birthday Card to Singapore, made up of selected entries from our nationwide Birthday Card competition. View how these wishes represent this year’s National Day theme ‘Majulah! The Singapore Spirit’.

    Official Website: http://www.singaporeartmuseum.sg/
