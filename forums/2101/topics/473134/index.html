    EVOCATION 2013: THE ART OF GIVING
    Date: 27 October 2013
    Time: 7.00 pm
    Venue: School of the Arts Concert Hall
    Price: $20 (Free Seating)

    Hi there!

    EVOKX is back this year for Evocation 2013! EVOKX, founded in 2010 by our choral director Terrence Toh, comprises of like-minded choral singers who sing for charity and engage in community and social services.

    Since the premiere of the Evocation series in 2010, our subsequent annual concerts have seen proceeds going to the Rainbow Centre Singapore, Chee Hoon Kog Moral Promotion,
    and Very Special Arts Singapore.

    This year for Evocation 2013, we are pledging our proceeds to The Leukemia and Lymphoma Foundation. We hope to enchant you with our joy of choral singing, in the name of giving back to society!

    So come join us for a night of contemporary choral music by featuring pieces by Sydney Guillaume, Petr Eben, Hugo Alfven, Eric Whitacre, Ko Matsushita and more. The choir will also
    perform new works by local composers Albert Tay and Low Xu Hao.

    Make a date with us by ordering tickets from our website www.evokx.org. Alternatively, you may contact Jacob Seet at 97312480 or email ticketing@evokx.org.
