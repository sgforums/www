    We are a group of students embarking on a project to reviving the culture of Chinese Opera among youths as part of our Project Work. In August, we carried out a trial of the concept of new age marketing technique to raise awareness of the culture that is relevant to the youths.

The videos can be viewed via our websitewww.yueju.wordpress.com. I apologise that footages of the flash mob were not captured properly but basically its just a small group of youths doing Chinese Opera stage movements in the middle of a crowded central plaza.
