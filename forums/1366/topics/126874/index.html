    Centre officials are unsure when and if Mount Talang will erupt again, but they believe there is little imminent danger at Lake Toba.

    Dr Fauzi, Head of Earthquake Engineering and Tusnami Division at the Indonesian National Seismological Centre, said: "The activities of the volcanic eruptions is triggered by large earthquake. That can happened. Again, this depends on the status of the volcano. If the status is very dangerous, then one trigger from the large earthquake will explode the volcano.
