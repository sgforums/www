    Enthusiastic, ardent, determined, you can know exuberant, exalting but tormented love affairs. Under the spell of a passion, you can let yourself go completely, for then nothing counts more than the actuality of your desire. In order to conquer the object of your passion, you are capable of any audacious act and the greatest sacrifices. But one could blame you for somewhat lacking tenderness and romanticism.

    You are too passionate and too impulsive to ignore ardent sentiments. Therefore, loves at first sight are frequent under your first name. This can make you live a more or less difficult adolescence because of your too precocious affairs. Also, on the spur of the moment, you can endanger your couple life when the noontide of your life comes, for you live too much in the present to care for past ties.

    Since conquests excite you more than anything else, single life does not annoy you overly. Indeed, it allows you to change mates as often as you wish! There will be the need of an exceptional event to incline you to look for stability in love and to make a home.

    As a general rule, you are on the lookout for a mate who admires you passionately and who follows you blindly. Any resistance exacerbates your desire, any refusal offends you, and any wait seems more or less unbearable to you. However, you don't bear too accommodating a mate either. The difficulty with you is to strike a right equilibrium between resistance and submission, which allows your love to remain alive.

    Thus, you're by no means a partner easy to live with. However, despite the conflicts, disputes, and the feverish climate in which you delight, your charm is not lacking.

    Living in couple, you need that the other one be totally confident in you, for you bear neither too heavy protection nor the troubles of hurting suspicion.

    Love & My Name...

    In love, you feel very strong attractions, without always being able to distinguish what concerns physical love from what is connected with sentimental passion which inclines you to try to conquer the beloved one. In any case, if she wants to charm you, the other one must pose as a prey to be conquered. Indeed, you are inclined to consider love as a competitive sport which necessarily implies winners and losers.

    Endowed with a passionate and expansive nature, you are more sensual than sentimental. But, even though you're subject to passions, these generally only last a short time.

    Your curiosity and your need for freedom often lead you to short-term relations. If you're married, it can also be the same; but your sense of duty prevents you from showing off with your occasional mates and instructs you to act discreetly in all circumstances. The woman who loves you should not dramatize the pranks which you may indulge in: those are only short-lived love affairs without real significance if one did not give them too much importance.

    You need to feel in accordance with your loved one and share ideas, projects, yearning for adventures and travels with her. You hate make-believes, lies and hypocrisy, and are incapable of pretending to feel what you don't. If you love no more, you simply go away, but your delicacy and kindness force you to do everything possible to avoid that the other one suffers too much.

    Your merry nature and your unfailing optimism allow you to bear single status without frustration, even though it's not a deliberate choice. Furthermore, your exacerbated taste for independence does not make common life easy. Sometimes, you categorically refuse all tie which is too restraining. You then want to live day after day and to savor intensely the numerous pleasures that will present themselves to you.

    26 Jan 05, 13:40


    For you, "love is all eyes and doesn't have a single good one" (Chinese proverb). Thus, you are careful about the dangers of passion. You don't want to let yourself be taken to any mirage, any dream. It's why you often find yourself deprived of beneficial fantasy and rather disarmed when you deal with the sentimental sphere.

    You run the risk of having serious difficulties before securing a successful love life. On top of such difficulties are your recoiling reaction, and your will to take time to analyze a project, to weigh the pros and the cons of a proposal. Sometimes this recoil is so obvious that your partner gets discouraged and looks for someone else.

    When feelings overwhelm you, there's often a sort of panic that invades you. Emotional outbursts appear somewhat suspect to you for you are excessively reserved. You're also greatly in awe of ridicule, and as a general rule it's only through a delicate, sincere approach that one can tame you.

    Paradoxically, you can let yourself be charmed by more instinctive, more expansive beings. But then there can be established certain misunderstanding between the two partners, one being too serious and the other too unstable.
    In order to have a successful sentimental life, you must learn not to let your career life bear exaggeratedly over your couple life. You must also accept a small part of fantasy and unforeseen events, which will help avoid monotony. For the great majority of bearers of your first name, an amorous union must be concluded on the base of mutual esteem and, consequently, can bloom in a discreet but profound and durable love.

    As you are extremely cautious, you still prefer loneliness to an ill-assorted union. This first name supplies a large group of single people. One cannot see a matter of hazard here. On one hand, there's mistrust ; on the other hand, exactingness. You protect yourself against all intrusion, you distrust the excesses of passion. You're afraid to be violated, invaded, or to be asked outer demonstrations which you abhor. Your reservedness, your reticence certainly do not help communication, which you refuse with every fiber of your being.
    Of course, you are not allergic to marriage as a matter of principle, but you wait to find an ideal partner before engaging yourself definitely. Nevertheless, at times, contrary to your ingrained habit, you may want to have a few short-lived adventures, deciding that your great love can wait.

    Living in couple with you is not very entertaining, unless your partner possesses treasures of patience and understanding.
