    When we actually witness real magick first hand and know it is real it is something we never forget. Getting back to candle flames

    Candle magick can be fun and it's easy to do. Here is a list of how to interpret the candle flame. Try it during your next ritual.

    A Strong Flame: this indicates that power and energy are going into your
    desire for manifestation.

    When a figure candle is used, a strong flame generally means that the person represented is winning or angry or using authority over another.

    When two figure candles are present, the higher and stronger flame represents authority over the other.

    A Weak Flame: this can show that you are facing heavy opposition and must redo the magick several times in order to overcome the power you are facing.

    The weak flame can also indicate, on a figure candle, that the subject is
    losing the battle.

    The weak flame that goes out means that you've missed
    your target altogether, start over.

    A Jumping Flame: This is an attention getter. It can indicate raw emotions along with explosions of energy.

    Direction of flame movement:

    NORTH: On a waxing moon, something physical is manifesting.

    EAST: something mental is manifesting.

    SOUTH: in love matters, you can expect a steamy love affair that doesn't
    last too long.

    WEST: emotional aspects. A good sign of success and love. This helps you divine things by candle flame and see which way a spell is going!

    WAX SYMBOLS

    After a candle has finished burning, check the melted wax for any signs of symbols that may have formed. You may, for example, find some affirmation that your spell was succesful.

    DANCING/FLICKERING FLAME

    A dancing or flickering flame may indicate that the spellcaster is not quite clear on his/her objectives. It may also mean that bad energy is present.

    VIOLENT ERUPTIONS

    For a candle to hiss, spit or throw off sparks is generally a bad sign. These violent acts may mean that there is bad energy around. (Personally I think the Spirits just wanted attention -Maiya!) Also, though, it may be the opposite. You may see these eruptions as bursts of strong energy being sent out instead.

    BAD LUCK

    A drip of wax down the side of a candle at a seance presages bad luck or death to the person nearest to that side. Take with a pinch of salt as i do not believe this at all!

    The Witches' Triangle

    The Witches Triangle is an ethical statement. These ethics are what keep us grounded. Some witches get too high on their broomsticks for their own good. These ethics are to help prevent an over-inflated ego, and to aid in making your magickal workings more powerful.

    Here they are:

    * To Know

    * To Will

    * To Dare

    & most important

    * To Keep Silent

    Okay, okay, I can hear you now!!! "A triangle only has three sides, why does the Witches' Triangle have four? Well, let's look at a triangle. Start at the top point. The top point is "To Know". Follow it down to the bottom left point, "To Will". Now, over to "To Dare", and then back up to the top point, "To Keep Silent". If you only had three, it would make the shape of an L. Now, let's look at this further. The two ethics on the top can be seen as the most important. If you don't "Know" what you're doing, your magick won't work. The same goes for "To Keep Silent" ... to talk about your magickal workings is to diminish their energy.

    To Know:

    When you are going to do any magickal working, learn all you can. Learn about the herbs you use, not only what they are used for in magick, but also what they have been used for medicinally. Whatever God or Goddess you call upon, research them! Find out all you can about them, their origins, their children, parents, their correspondences. Never use anything that you aren't familiar with. Strange symbols and languages are mysterious and beautiful, but they won't help you. In fact, using things that you aren't sure what they are can actually make your spells backfire. Also, just because someone says this "Intergalatical Qabbalistic Symbol of Babalyon" is used for money spells, don't just take their word for it. Research it.

    To Will:

    Take the time to re-evaluate the situation. Do you really want to do a magickal working for this situation? Make sure that what you are doing is in accordance with your personal ethics. There should be no uneasy feeling about what you're going to perform. If you feel like this might not be the best way, pack up your athame, and call in sick. This is your will. Don't let anyone pressure you into any magickal working, because it will be you who will face the consequences of your actions. Which brings us to number 3.

    To Dare:

    This can be the hardest. Before doing any magickal working, sort out all your thoughts. Are you willing to accept any and all karmic responsibility for your actions? If your spell shatters, are you willing to clean up the mess?
    If you are afraid someone is going to find out about this magickal working, then don't do it, because it means you have something to hide. Magick isn't done behind closed doors where no one can see. The God and Goddess see everything, and you will be judged accordingly.

    To Keep Silent:

    Now, be honest with yourself, would you do this spell if there was no one to give you credit? Are you using magick to impress or frighten people? This is a BIG karmic No-No!! The Goddess hates this.

    Remember that magick is used to aid in improving your life. It doesn't involve anyone else. But what about spells you do for others? Do you have their permission? Do not force or press your help on them. If they do not want a spell done, don't do it.

    Remember that a spell you do and never speak of, will be 10 times stronger than a spell you tell your friends, coven members, and that really cool aunt who's into crystals and their pet's past-lives. It takes away from the energy you have put into making it.

    Making Your Own Herbal Paper

    Materials
    You will need several sheets of typing paper, a bowl, hot water, a
    fork, two kitchen twoels, a metal screen, a wooden frame, a stapler, a
    rolling pin, and two tablespoons of ground herbs of your choice.

    Procedure
    1. Tear several sheets of paper into pieces the size of a penny or
    smaller.

    2. Place the pieces in a bowl and cover with hot water.

    3. Stir to make sure all of the paper is wet.

    4. Let stand overnight.

    5. The next day, tear the paper into pieces as small as possible and
    then stir with a fork for several minutes. (You now have made pulp.) This
    can also be done in a food processor if you don't mind adding the
    mechanical energies, but like any magical tool, you are better off doing it
    by hand. As you stir, add in the ground herbs. They need to be ground
    or finely chopped so that they don't create flaws in the paper.
    Experiment with various herbs, keeping in mind thier magical qualities. The
    pulp should remain a thick mush in texture. If this is part of a spell
    working, chant or focus on your purpose as you do this.

    6. Prepare a metal screen (like those used in windows or screen doors)
    by stapling screen to a wood picture frame. You can use a small window
    screen if you like. The dimensions can vary, but it should be larger
    than you want the finished paper.

    7. Smooth out an even layer of pulp (approx. 1/8 inch thick) onto the
    screen by hand.

    8. Let the water drain for a couple of minutes.

    9. Flip your screen over so that your paper pops out onto a clean
    kitchen towel.

    10. Cover the paper with another kitchen towel so that you have a
    "sandwich" of paper between the two towels.

    11. Use the rolling pin to press out more water and flatten the paper.

    12. Carefully peel off the top towel. Turn the wet paper over onto
    either a smooth counter top or on a piece of glass (you can use a window
    for this) paper side down, and then carefully peel off the remaining
    towel.

    13. Let the paper dry overnight or longer.

    14. If you want to smooth the paper for writing on, spray the dried
    paper with a fine mist of water. Place a clean smooth rag over the damp
    paper and iron it with a slightly warm iron until the paper is dry.

    You can use your paper for gifts, for spell work, for stationery or
    just for plain fun! Enjoy!
