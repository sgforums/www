    Hey~

    I think that exploring tarot is a good thing. Do know that reading a book about cards and actually having experiences with them are quite different. Depending on the books that you have about the cards- it could make a whole world of difference as well.

    You are just starting out, lower your expectations.  Do not strive for 100% accuracy.
    I feel that in order to be accurate, you will need to find your own voice. Your way of relating to your cards.

    When you say that your reading was totally off the point, was it addressing an issue that your father may not be too forthcoming about?
    Or was it talking about an altogether different issue from what you were asking?
    Did it make sense, or was it totally nonsensical?
    In retrospect now, did that "off-the-point" reading now make sense in regards to your situation back then...?
    All of these are different situations that you may encounter, and will result in a different explanation and advice to proceed.

    If you do encounter any more issues, feel free to enquire around. People here are generally helpful (if you can find them online, in time).
    Do remember-- practise safely, and do not dabble with anything beyond your scope.  (Beginner's luck can only cover you so far- beyond which you had better know what you are getting yourself into...)

    Until then, lucks with your exploration, and be safe~
    Take care!
