    Moonflower

    My Beloved Beauty

    Dear
    When I see you
    I feel so blissful
    Reveling in your beauty

    So calm and wondrous you are
    You never cease to amaze me
    Your beauty
    One can never not look at again

    You feel me
    When I am sad you soothe me
    When I am happy you bloom for me
    All my feeling you are there

    Yet here I am
    Feeling sorrowful
    For you are not there
    Beneath the tree you lie

    But fear not
    Your life is always there
    In the air
    In your children

    Here I am
    Feeling for you
    With your presence surrounding me
    Content and loving I am

    MI
