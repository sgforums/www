    When we actually witness real magick first hand and know it is real it is something we never forget. Getting back to candle flames ...look at this! BB Maiya
    Candle magick can be fun and it's easy to do. Here is a list of how to interpret the candle flame. Try it during your next ritual.

    A Strong Flame: this indicates that power and energy are going into your
    desire for manifestation. When a figure candle is used, a strong flame
    generally means that the person represented is winning or angry or using
    authority over another. When two figure candles are present, the higher and
    stronger flame represents authority over the other.

    A Weak Flame: this can show that you are facing heavy opposition and must
    redo the magick several times in order to overcome the power you are facing.
    The weak flame can also indicate, on a figure candle, that the subject is
    losing the battle. The weak flame that goes out means that you've missed
    your target altogether, start over.

    A Jumping Flame: This is an attention getter. It can indicate raw emotions
    along with explosions of energy.

    Direction of flame movement:
    NORTH: On a waxing moon, something physical is manifesting.
    EAST: something mental is manifesting.
    SOUTH: in love matters, you can expect a steamy love affair that doesn't
    last too long.
    WEST: emotional aspects. A good sign of success and love. This helps you divine things by candle flame and see which way a spell is going!

    WAX SYMBOLS
    After a candle has finished burning, check the melted wax for any signs of symbols that may have formed. You may, for example, find some affirmation that your spell was succesful.

    DANCING/FLICKERING FLAME
    A dancing or flickering flame may indicate that the spellcaster is not quite clear on his/her objectives. It may also mean that bad energy is present.

    VIOLENT ERUPTIONS
    For a candle to hiss, spit or throw off sparks is generally a bad sign. These violent acts may mean that there is bad energy around. (Personally I think the Spirits just wanted attention -Maiya!) Also, though, it may be the opposite. You may see these eruptions as bursts of strong energy being sent out instead.

    BAD LUCK
    A drip of wax down the side of a candle at a seance presages bad luck or death to the person nearest to that side. Take with a pinch of salt as i do not believe this at all!
    It is considered bad luck to fall asleep with a candle burning. OOOOOOOPPS
    "If a candle falls and breaks in two, double trouble will come to you!"

    DREAMS
    To dream of a black candle is an omen of death or illness.
    To dream of a white candle is an omen of true love.
    If a young woman sees two white candles in a dream it is a sign that she will soon receive a proposal of marriage
    A red candle that appears in a dream symbolizes passion and sexual desire.
    To dream of five candles is an omen of love and marriage.
    To dream of a candle in a holder is an omen of a happy and prosperous future.
    To dream of a candleholder without a candle in it foretells sorrow and misfortune.

    GHOSTS AND EVIL SPIRITS
    A blue light from a candle is a sign that good spirits are nearby.
    A tall straight flame on a candle during a seance is a sign that a spirit is present.
    In Ireland, it is an old funeral custom to light 12 candles around a corpse to protect the soul of the deceased from evil forces, for it is believed that ghosts and demons cannot cross into a circle of lighted Candles.
    Always light candles at moments of birth, marriage and death to ensure evil spirits are kept at bay during these crucial moments.
    Light a brown candle on the eve of Candlemas for protection against evil spirits and ghosts.
    Place a lighted candle inside a carved-out pumpkin on All Hallows Eve to keep evil spirits and demons away.

    GOOD LUCK
    In Sicily, a fisherman burns ornate candles to their patron saint to obtain blessings and protection.
    A bay-berry scented candle burned all the way down will bring "good luck to the home and gold to the pocket".
    Light a brown candle in each room of your house on Candlemas Eve to attract good luck and keep away bad spirits.
