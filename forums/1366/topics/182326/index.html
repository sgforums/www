    ABOUT TONI



    Born in Melbourne, Australia on the 20th October 1954 of Italian parents. An Artist, Writer and Holistic Healing practitioner, dedicated in his pursuit of exploring the principles of creativity and healing.

    Toni believes that love is the essence of creation and creativity, and that love is the greatest healer.

    All of Toni's work whether it be Painting, Writing or Healing is done intuitively, that is, he listens to and is guided by, the light of the soul rather the rational logic. His painting method is the opposite of what is generally taught in art schools. He has no preconceived idea or image of what he paints, nor does he give it any conscious thought.

    In his paintings and writings, the spirit of creation which flows through every living thing is both seen and felt. The invisible becomes visible as spirit and matter merge. One of Toni's greatest passions is to share his knowledge with others, which he does through his Inspired Creativity Workshops (c).
    Toni believes that creativity is not exclusive to some but that we all have unlimited creative and healing potential and energy within us, it is simply a matter of being open to that energy and allowing it through. In his workshops on "Inspired Creativity" students transcend the limitations of the mind and merge with the endless source of creative and healing energy that stems from the soul.

    Through this process one's true purpose in life may reveal itself in the form of heartfelt inspiration which then motivates us into action.

    He is the Artist and Author of the best selling "Universal Love - Healing Oracle," "Universal Wisdom Oracle, and "Spirit Oracle" and the author of several books including "Jewels Within A Teardrop."

    Toni's inspiring images and words are slowly becoming known to more and more people around the globe. His Universal Love - Healing Oracle has been translated into different languages including French and German and is now available, published and distributed by AG Muller of Switzerland.

    Reflections on Mary Magdalene
    (Taken from the introduction of the Magdalene Oracle Guidebook)

    To me, Magdalene is not solely a biblical figure; rather, she is a modern day Goddess who holds a most sacred and ancient wisdom; organic and tactile, and deeply connected to our beautiful planet. Magdalene lives eternally through present day women and she will no doubt continue to be a source of inspiration for women in generations to come.

    Her wisdom is beyond words, images or concepts. She is an archetypal spirit, soft and gentle, yet strong and unswayable. Magdalene stands firmly in her truth, representing strength and dignity.

    She assists in elevating the status of women around the globe and helps free both men and women from the closed mind sets that have kept the true beauty, wisdom and creative essence of the feminine spirit from fully emerging.

    I had never given Magdalene much thought until recently. While giving my wife, Martine, a Reiki some months ago I became aware of the spiritual presence of Jesus and Mother Mary, as I often do. However, during the session I felt the presence of a third, somewhat shadowy figure, which I intuitively recognized as Mary Magdalene.

    Magdalene's energy feels distinctly different from Mother Mary's. I would not describe Magdalene's energy as maternal. She appears dark and mysterious, partly hidden from view; like silver moonlight shining through the clouds on cool dark night. She stands proud regardless of what others think of her or how they choose to label her. For centuries there have been many who have sought to diminish her worth through lies, intimidation and even physical and emotional abuse. Yet in spite of this, Magdalene's presence and influence remains.

    Magdalene represents different things to different people. The following passage written by my friend, Moon Yin, I think beautifully captures the essence of what Magdalene represents to many of us.

    "The Biblical figure of Mary Magdalene represents the archetypal neglected Feminine principle, fluttering at the edge of our dreams. She hovers at the doors of the moon-lit collective unconscious, awaiting our invitation to enter.

    Magdalene is the dancing Dark Goddess of gentle strength and authority, bearer of infinite compassion. She is the healer of the wounded female, healing the desolate places of the heart, where lie the issues of abandonment and betrayal which all women carry. She holds up to them the mirror of their true nature, in which they will see themselves reflected in all their beauty and power, and thereby step into their wholeness."
