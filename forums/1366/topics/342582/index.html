    That's a really pretty deck!

    It's not a necessity to verbalise your question. Although if you vocalise it, you get additional focus. So if you cannot really hold your intentions or you keep getting distracted or something, saying your questions out loud can help.

    No, not really.
    Those accessories can help set the ambience/mood for a reading. Although, the wood and granite surface bit can be useful as those are natural materials- helps you in connecting. One thing though, if you start to get attached to your cards, sometimes you'll start to want to provide them with those accessories. Like a fancy box so you'll know where the card is, and it protects them from getting bent or missing or blown all over by the wind, etc. A cloth helps in picking up less environment vibrations and also creating a surface to work with.
    Think about this... Did you really spent $50+ on a deck of cards just to get them stained with food or drink or condiments...?
    It's something you want and choose to do, but again, it all depends on individual preference.
