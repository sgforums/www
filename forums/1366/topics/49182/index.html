    Just a short intro of tarot here as well as advertising my sevices



    Tarot has existed since around the late 14th century, although some believed it to be even earlier. The history of tarot is a mystery of it's own and that may be why it is so popular with people, even until today... The general believe was that when it first started out, tarot was just another card game for the public, but then was used as a divinatory tool, as was all card games generally Only difference between tarot cards and normal cards is that the tarot consists of 78 cards, while the normal deck have only 52... Oh, and nicer graphics

    With tarot, there are also four suits, but with extra 22 trump cards and an additional card for each suit as compared to the playing cards. The trump cards or the major arcana is used mostly as a highlight or outline of events, with the minor arcanas, or the suits to provide more details. Generally tarot comprises of a lot of things, it can be linked from the esoteric studies like astrology and khabbalah to more familiar topics as psychology. Surely tarot is an art waiting to be mastered...

    Being very interested in tarot, I took to learning this art a while ago. So just a beginner looking for anyone interested for a read, giving me a chance to polish my skills
