    I will recount an incident in the begining of this year. There was a well-known channeler who came to town (Singapore). She held a free reading session.

    As a sucker for free experiences... I went. It was not long before she invoked the angels and goddess, she works with.

    After a while, I notice that there is an layer of coloured luminousity around her. The luminousity spanned evenly about 3-4 inches from her body. Around the body was a very beautiful and brilliant blue layer. Her head region was a beautiful green.

    As, I am not one who see coloured aura... I was pleasantly surprised.

    After her talk, I went up to her to tell her that I saw her aura. She asked me what colour did I see, which I replied. She says that what I saw was accurate. According to her, those are the energies of the non-physicals beings that she works with, with consists of angels and a goddess. The blue that I saw is the energy of the goddess.
