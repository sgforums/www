    ~ Sagittarius ~



    The Archer (Nov 23 - Dec 21 approx)

    Planet - Jupiter

    Element - Fire

    Best time of the day - 10am to 4pm

    Best Day - Thursday (also Saturday or Sunday)

    Number - 3

    Birthstones - Turquoise (also Topaz, Moonstone)

    Metal - Tin

    Colors - Purple (also Blue, Yellow, Scarlet)

    Flowers - Narcissus or Holly

    Your Relationship with other Signs -

    Aries - Will add spice to your life.

    Taurus - Maybe, but Taurus will have to try very
    hard.

    Gemini - Will compete, not good.

    Cancer - Different Values, Forget it.

    Leo - Good, He'll help you along.

    Virgo - Won't go along with your interests.

    Libra - Very Good. You understand each other.

    Scorpio - Will restrict you.

    Sagittarius - Touch and go, Maybe.

    Capricorn - Stay away, can be deceiving.

    Aquarius - Good. You can lean on each other.

    Pisces - It's a risk. Can't keep up with you.

    What you're really like -

    You're the straight-shooter. The honest one, the
    wise one.

    Especially helpful is your ruling planet Jupiter
    (It means, money, money, money) Your fire helps
    to keep you barreling along.

    You're the smart one in your crowd, and you use
    your brains to get what you want (but you've
    always got that bow and arrow ready).

    You're out to have the best time of your life
    and people who bog you down, you shoot down.
    You're always ready when adventure calls, and
    there's probably nothing. You'd like nothing
    better to do than to go exploring (for anything
    from the newest, grooviest night spot to the
    wilest, most remote hunting lands). You go where
    the action is thickest and run into the thick
    of that.

    And you're lucky; everything turns you on. You
    love parties because your energy lights the
    crowd. Exotic foods? Great! Discovering a new
    dish is like discovering a new continet.
    Both Sagittarius males and females love way-out
    clothes. You're the fashion plate. You can't
    feel completely yourself unless you're dressed
    to kill.

    Most of all, you must have a philosophy to live
    by. Your outlook is generally optimistic and you
    detest hypocrisy. You dislike extreme formalities
    as well as excessive political and social pomp
    and ceremony.

    Your Love Life -

    You love loving, and all those dates you've got
    prove it.

    The only time you spend Saturday night home is
    if you've got a swinging date to keep you
    company. And with all your swinging dates
    you just don't have time for a steady.
    If you're a Sagittarian female, you're fiercely
    independent. The guys have to come to you; you'll
    never have to chase them. And that's the
    independence that causes the problems; you feel
    no man can measure up to your standards. No matter
    what the guy is like (even if he looks like Clark
    Gable and has the strength of Hercules) you'll
    find something the matter will him. By the time
    you finally decide on a mate there might not be
    any mates around.

    Males aren't that hug up with choosing, but with
    all that energy in them, they keep looking for
    girls just waiting to get warmed up.
    You'll probably marry later than most because your
    fire will burn longer. But when you finally decide,
    it'll be for a quick courtship and immediate
    marriage. You'll be happiest with a sign who spirit
    is as free as yours.

    Your Future -

    Your bow is aimed for the top of the heap.
    Your love of people makes you a natural for fields
    involving public contacts; like entertainment or
    advertising or public relations. You could also
    burn up a courtroom with flaming oratory or set
    the salesworld on fire with your dynamic energy.
    Money will come to you because Jupiter is your
    boss (and Jupiter is the MR Moneybags of the
    planets). You females won't be the homemaker
    type. Nobody could keep you in the kitchen; it
    would be cruel. You'll probably be a career woman
    and have a maid do what you'd never be caught
    doing.

    You'll probably live in the city (That's wqhere
    the action is), but you'll spend a lot of time
    in the country where your adventurous spirit can
    be satisfied. Travel is your meat and you'll be
    feasting every chance you get. You've got to set
    the world on fire while you're doing it.

    Aim high Sagittarius, you just can't miss.

    ~ Capricorn ~



    The Goat (Dec 22 - Jan 19 approx)

    Planet - Saturn

    Element - Earth

    Best Time of Day - 8 AM = 2 PM

    Best Day - Saturday (somtimes Thursday)

    Number - 4 (representing Saturn)

    Birthstone - Garnet,Lapis Lazuli, Onyx, or Ruby

    Metal - Lead

    Colors - Gray, Black, Deep Green, Violet, or Deep Blue

    Flower - White Carnation

    Your Relationship with other Signs

    Aries - Too many differences. A doomed relationship.

    Taurus - Will give you much understanding, one of
    the three best signs for you.

    Gemini - You'll like Gemini, but Gemini may roam.

    Cancer - Wants to stay at home with you

    Leo - 50-50 chance.

    Virgo - Very well matched. One of the three signs
    best for you.

    Libra - Good for your socail status.

    Scorpio - Nope, Tempers will flare.

    Sagittarius - Can help you. But hard to hold.

    Capricorn - Aims are the same, but could get into
    a rut.

    Aquarius - Give it a go.

    Pisces - Good. A great helper.

    What You're Really Like -

    With Saturn ruling your life (Saturn reflects
    responsibility), and Capricorn being a cardinal
    earthy sign, you know off the bat that you're the
    one in the crowd with both feet on the ground.
    Though you're not the out-going type, (no one can
    ever accuse you of coming on too strong) only
    Cancer and Scorpio out of the entire Zodiac can
    dominate you (that's your inner power, Capricorn).

    You're not the one to run off in a hundred
    different directions at once. No, you sit and
    plan out all your moves down to the minute detail
    (this usually gets to be a bit of a drag to your
    friends and close ties). And while your associates
    dream of tomorrow and talk of what it may bring,
    you're busy pocketing today so you can make of
    tomorrow anything you'd like (if one word had to
    describe you it would be ambitious).

    Let the others knock the establishment and drop
    from reality, you're here to take the world and
    make reality do what you want it to.
    And besides, the establishment offers all the
    better things in life that you want (you're
    amazingly materialistic). You don't go in for
    fads (anything that's unstable is not your style);
    you go in for the better clothes, the biggest cars,
    anythng beautiful and anything expensive.
    And while you may look the essence of upper crust,
    nothing could give you more pleasure than monkeying
    with an engine, or pulling apart a clock to see
    what makes it tick. You've got to know "the how",
    not just "the is".

    You're a complicated person Capricorn; and though
    your friends may not be able to figure you out,
    you're never at a loss for people around you.
    Because Capricorns are always there when they are
    supposed to be. You are the Rock of Gibralter, the
    most dependable sign of the Zodiac (but you can't
    knock others for not being as perfect as you; not
    everyone is a Capricorn).

    Your Love Life -

    Capricorns are even dependable when it comes to
    love. No hearts and flowers for you, you're see
    someone for a time rather than run off for some
    madcap impetuous love affair. You call the shots
    in romance, but you always remember the birthdays,
    the anniversaries, the little things that mean
    more than a lot. You'll have a longer than usual
    courtship, that's the Capricorn's way; but once
    you're married your home will be a well ordered
    showplace. But if you're through with someone,
    you never go back - you're finished for good!

    Your Future -

    It'll take a bit longer for you to hit the mark,
    but it's only because you'll take your time doing
    it; that way you can be safe and secure. Success
    will never be a passing thing. Once you've got it,
    you'll never lose it. Let the others run around,
    you've got work to do (you'll always stay late at
    the office and even give up your week-ends for
    your job). While others can't take a 9-5 schedule
    because it inhibits them, you keep adding hours
    onto your schedule. You'll head a corporation, or
    run a country. Your planning abilities are unequalled.

    You've got people President Nixon as Capricorn
    keeping you company. You're also the sign that will
    know how to use your success. You'll have the best
    of everything; first class all the way. And it
    won't matter where you settle down, just as long
    as it's near your work.

    You can be happy anywhere. So lower your head
    and dig in, Capricorn' the world is yours.

    Venus in Sagittarius
    October 7 until November 5



    Venus, the planet of love and beauty, is off to adventure in experience-seeking Sagittarius. Lust for life and expansion of the boundaries of pleasure are supported now. Love is about expanding limits here. We may give up some of the comfortable patterns of the past and risk relationship safety for new levels of experience. Love needs to grow now; it will not stand still.

    Travel, learning and a taste for all that's foreign may be heightened. It's hard to stick to the old routine when the planet of pleasure hits the road. This desire for the new and exotic may reveal some weakness of judgment. Purchases are more likely made with joyful abandon rather than with an eye on long-term value. People, too, can be overrated, particularly if they offer the promise of adventure or enlightenment.

    Among those born with Venus in Sagittarius are spiritual teachers Sai Baba, Paramhansa Yogananda, Alan Watts, Albert Schweitzer and Deepak Chopra. Rockers David Bowie, Jimi Hendrix, Tina Turner and Pete Townsend have Venus in Sag, as do both Paul Simon and Art Garfunkel.

    Quite a number of writers were born with the planet of the arts in this literary sign. Art Buchwald, Lewis Carroll, Kurt Vonnegut and Mark Twain were some who wrote from a humorous perspective. Political figures with Venus in Sagittarius include Winston Churchill and Margaret Thatcher from England's Conservative Party, and Russians Boris Yeltsin, Joseph Stalin and Alexander Solzhenitsyn.

    Finally, we must thank Venus in Sagittarius for her great sense of humor. Two contemporary examples of Venus in Sag's "almost anything goes" comedy are the free-spirited Whoopi Goldberg and Roseanne. Laughter lifts our spirits, opens the heart and shows us the limitless boundaries of joy symbolized by Venus in Sagittarius.

    Venus
    Venus is the planet of love, beauty, pleasure, relationships and attraction. It is associated with the signs of Taurus and Libra.

    Venus describes what we like and what we need to be liked for. It is, therefore, a key to partnerships, particularly personal ones. A venusian person is pleasant, gracious and socially skilled. She has good taste and a strong desire for harmony.

    Self-worth is also connected with Venus. To a certain extent, our value to other people will be reflected through Venus' sign and house placement in the natal chart.

    Sagittarius
    Sagittarius, ruled by Jupiter, hungers for experience, quests for knowledge and wrestles with idealism.

    Exuberant, optimistic and fun loving, Sagittarians are gregarious and social by nature. They love good conversation. Unfortunately, when this gift of gab collides with their need to tell their whole truth, Sagittarians often say more than they have to. Yet, these communication blunders spark laughter more than argument. At the core, Sagittarius is the sign where we strive to integrate and synthesize our seemingly dual nature. Symbolized by the centaur, Sagittarius embodies the struggle to integrate the human condition-and all of its appetites-with the longing to experience our divine origins.
