    MOON IN VIRGO



    THREE WORDS: SELF-CONTAINED, TENACIOUS and PREPARATORY

    The Virgo moon is a transitional moon from the active Leo moon. It is an
    earth sign, which is a grounded sign...which is wonderful for almost all
    about. Earth signs are the most productive of all signs, when people
    become very fixated on many work and project related facets of their
    lives but it does not have an edge of workaholic to it, which is a
    perfect balance.

    This sign does well for all of us, no matter WHERE in the moon cycle it
    falls. In the waxing moon, it helps us to get active and focused,
    harnessing the energy of the moon as well as the sign for a double
    whammy. During the waning moon, it helps us to stay steadfast in
    cleaning up projects and such - rather than becoming listless as many of us do, during the waning moon.

    MAGIC DURING THIS MOON: Grounding spells are wonderful during this moon. Use orange candles, especially geared towards career and success and pair it with patchouli. This is a good moon time to also do various earthy things...like look at seed books, do garden planning, or dig out weeds. Another good grounding ritual to consider is to smudge your home, garden and/or workshops - even your workspace (if you can).

Blackberry picking is now the last ritualistic seasonal collecting of wild plant food still practiced in industrialized Europe. The root produces an orange dye.

    ~ Virgo ~



    The Virgin (Aug 23 - Sept 22 approx)

    Planet - Mercury

    Element - Earth

    Best Time of Day - 4pm=10pm

    Best Day - Wednesday (also Friday and Saturday)

    Number - 5

    Birthstone - Sapphire, pink jaspers, jade, marble,
    diamonds

    Metal - Quicksilver

    Colors - Blue (also violet, brown, grey yellow)

    Flowers - Cornflower, Aster

    Your Relationship with other Signs

    Aries - Too quick for you.

    Taurus - Excellent, one of your best bets.

    Gemini - Poor, will distract you no end

    Cancer - Good. Home-loving and cooks well.

    Leo - Too different in aims.

    Virgo - Compromise, but both practical.

    Libra - You'll help each other.

    Scorpio - Not the best. Different Values.

    Sagittarius - Poor, both will go different ways.

    Capricorn - One of your best Signs.

    Aquarius - Could be a kooky relationship.

    Pisces - 50-50 chance

    What You're Really Like -

    Flying high with Mercury, you're above it all.
    You're the epitome of aloofness. And though you
    seem like you're a cold marble statue, there are
    plenty of human feelings swirling around that
    placid exterior. Your trouble is, your head rules
    and not your heart. Torrid expressions of love
    are not your style. You feel you're above it
    all. And you can turn off many a propective mate
    just because you come on this way. Yet once
    someone gets to know you, they find that you're
    fond of knocking yourself. You love to rip
    yourself apart. Then you start analyzing whoever
    else is around you. But it all comes to naught,
    because you can't supply solutions to your own
    problems. You've got an amazing retentive mind,
    but can never make it up. Only when it comes to
    perfection do you drive yourself without end.
    And though you may wind up with 100%, down deep
    you punch yourself because you didn't make it
    101%. It's this rigid code that you also apply
    to others and consequently very few people will
    measure up.

    Order is your word. And you can just reel off
    fact after fact after fact on anything from who
    went out with whom four months ago, to how much
    nutmeg to use in some obscure recipe for cake.
    The trouble is, much of your information is
    amusingly trivial.

    If you're the gal, you're the type with the
    quiet elegant fashions; and the finest quality.
    You also love cooking, but none of that instant
    mix for you. And if you're the male, you'd rather
    spend your time pulling apart a car than getting
    pulled apart by phonies at a party.
    Remember, from your vantage point way up there,
    you can tell who's real and who's not.

    Your Love Life -

    No hearts and flowers scene for you, you're above
    it. So the guy or gal you finally settle on will
    have more of an intellectual appeal than emotional.
    You'd rather sit around discussing a good book
    than swoon over some romantic movie.
    Though marriage has an even chance of coming to
    you either early or late, it makes no real
    difference to you. Your love of travel is also
    a great factor in your love life. It could keep
    you out of a permanent arrangement for many many years.
    But when you finally do settle down, you'll throw
    yourself wholeheartedly into your household. And
    a well ordered household it'll be. Whoever gets
    you will be a lucky individual; after all, a
    home perfectly kept is the best of blessings.

    Your Future -

    On the wings of Mercury you can go anywhere. And
    though success will come, very often it won't be
    because you had anything to do with it.
    Though jobs will be plentiful, they'll never be
    your life; there's not enough money in the world
    for you to be saddled with a 9 to 5 job.
    Intellectualism is the key. Research or science
    or education would be your better fields. These
    offer you the chance to travel and the chance to
    study.

    Your critical nature makes you a natural for the
    theater or the art circles, while your love of the
    detail makes you a cinch for jobs dealings with
    math (like engineering). Design will also be
    quite stimulating and fashion is more than
    appealing to you. You're probably wind up living
    in the city because of the lure of the arts; but
    weekends in the country are what you need to
    refresh yourself and put you back in the spirit
    of things.

    You've got the wings Virgo and you can fly to any
    height you want.

    ~ Libra ~



    The Balance (Sept 23 - Oct 22 approx)

    Planet - Venus

    Element - Air

    Best Time of Day - 2 PM to 8 PM

    Best Day - Friday

    Number - 6

    Birthstone -
    Tourmaline, or sapphire (also opal, beryl, jade)

    Metal - Copper

    Colors - Any Pastel Color

    Flower - Marigold

    Your Relationship with other Signs

    Aries - Doubtful, Sparks really fly.

    Taurus - 50-50 chance of making a go.

    Gemini - Good. You think alike.

    Cancer - Fair, lots of cooperation needed.

    Leo - Good, you like the same things.

    Virgo - Makes you stable if you cooperate.

    Scorpio - Never. You are too opposite.

    Sagittarius - Fine. You get along well in situations
    requiring cooperation.

    Capricorn - Good Provider for your needs.

    Aquarius - You're very alike. An excellent match.

    Pisces - So-so. Can pamper you, but can be too moody.

    What You're Really Like -

    You're the incurable romantic, the aspiring idealist.
    The classic free spirit. You're anything but practical.
    You may give the impression of being a softie, but
    your sign holds sway over half of the signs in the
    zodiac. Yet even though your sign is so strong,
    you're constantly in a state of worry. The smallest
    thing can set you off into deep moods of depression.
    And though you might stay depressed for awhile,
    you'll do everything to tip the scales back to your
    favor. In fact, things usually turn out rather well
    because you need to be with people. So you quickly
    pull out of it.

    But, when things don't go well; you're out for justice.
    Revenge. Days, weeks, months, even years may go by,
    but eventually you'll get even with whomever or
    whatever upset your balance in the first place.
    It's the same passion fir justice that leads you
    to a penchant for neatness. Everything's got to be
    ship-shape or you ship out (or ship for someone
    else out).

    And while you're busily seeing that everything is
    clean, you're apt to forget many of the other
    important tasks you've got. But it will never
    interfere with your basic likability. You're
    always the first to go along with the crowd, and
    also one of its big morale boosters.
    Yet when it comes to important decisions you
    weigh all the evidence very carefully. After all,
    justice isn't blind when you've got anything to
    do with it. While not neccessarily a devotee of
    the great outdoors, you enjoy nature. A long
    walk in the park or woods, working in the garden,
    a family picnic are the type of activity apt to
    give you a great deal of pleasure.

    Your Love Life -

    You can't take the fights and quarrels that
    usually accompany the average love affair. So
    you more than often try to stay away from
    getting tangled up in emotional relationships.
    You'd love to be in love, but it's got to be
    beautiful or else forget it. That's why you marry
    later than most of your friends.

    You're the type pf person always ready to give
    your mate the benefit of the doubt. You're not
    likely to quarrel over little things. This sense
    of fair play gives you the potential to be a
    better than average spouse. Marriage is likely
    to be a very happy affair if you find a partner
    who meets you halfway.

    Your Future -

    You're a procrastinator. You'll hang around school,
    and when you finally get out, you'll either travel
    or take an excessively long time making up your
    mind what to do.

    The arts seem to be your meat. With a great emphasis
    on music. But you can also shine brilliantly in
    the theatre or the motion picture industry.
    Movies would be better, because they won't tie
    you down like a long-run Broadway play.
    Grade school teaching is also for you, you love
    to help children. And anything in medicine is a
    natural.

    Your home will be wherever you hang your hat. It
    doesn't matter. And wherever it is you'll make it
    a true palace of Venus.

    Don't worry about anything Libra; the scales are
    tipping in your favor. No matter what you do.

    The Scorpion (Oct 23 - Nov 22 approx)

    Planet - Mars and Pluto

    Element - Water

    Best Time of Days - 12 noon=6pm

    Best Day - Tuesday

    Number - 8

    Birthstone - topaz (or diamond)

    Metal - Fine Surgeon's Steel

    Colors - Scarlet (also deep blue or green)

    Flower - Red Carnation

    Your Relationship with other Signs -

    Aries - Too much of a drain on you.

    Taurus - Strong hold on each other.

    Gemini - Quite disconcerting and elusive.

    Cancer - Good. You're much alike.

    Leo - Too Different. Much too stubborn and proud.

    Virgo - Good. Very Soothing.

    Libra - Too different.

    Sagittarius - Fair. Too easy-going and restless.

    Capricorn - No Good. Too demanding.

    Aquarius - Poor. Too much independence.

    Pisces - Very good. Especially for your home life.

    What You're Really Like -

    Being a child of Mars, you've inherited his traits.
    You're throughly energetic and very high strung.
    Anything can set you off at anytime.
    This energy ignites all around you; and anything
    can be yours. Action is your word and you've got
    all the fire to set it in motion. But you're also
    a water sign, and the water comes in handy in
    putting out some of that fire when it gets too out
    of hand. So you're in control all the time.
    Without the water. you could burn yourself out.
    For instance, chances are that you'll just keep
    at a project until you finish it (even though the
    world could be collasping around you, you won't
    notice).

    That's concentration for you!
    It's the same determination that puts you on all
    those committees and social activities. Your
    trouble is that you come on like a Wellsian
    Martian trying to rule the world.
    Being so outgoing can be a tremendous asset, but
    it can also scare people off. If they knew how
    lonely you are, maybe they'd understand.
    But you'll never let them know. Mars is a soldier,
    and soldiers bear wounds bravely.
    This also explains the fact that for all the
    aquaintances you have, you don't have close
    friends. Your compensation for this self-imposed
    isolation is achieving your goals. A pat on the
    back from your boss or a mark or commendation
    from your teacher is all the consolation you
    need.

    You Martian boys will be highly athletic. Soldiers
    have to be in tip-top condition; so when summer
    comes your physique really shines.
    Martian girls are in the whirl. You're the spark
    in your crowd. You have quite a build and look
    fantantastic in your bikinis. But dieting is the
    necessary thing if you want to stay that way.
    You're like the Irving Berlin song "After You
    Get What You Want You Don't Want It, If They
    Gave You The Moon, You'd Grow Tired Of it Soon".

    Your Love Life -

    Meeting Scorpio can mean trouble to some signs
    because Scorpio rules half of the Zodiac. In fact,
    if you're a Scorpio girl, you'll be the one to
    make the first move and arrange all these romantic
    little get togethers. But you can easily frighten
    away a shy male. Scorpio males are the same way.
    Girls melt before your flame. And many times you
    even burn them up (that's a sure way of getting
    your flame put out when you least expect it).
    Yet when you finally meet the person for you, no
    one else will shine in your eyes. You'll be
    intensely loyal and never for a moment will
    your mate have to doubt you.

    Your Future -

    You can go higher than the heavens if you just
    don't get trapped by your own self-doubts. Riches,
    fame - they're all yours if you can keep your
    energy from burning you up before you get them.
    You can make it big in the arts and sciences, but
    because half of you is water and the other half
    is fire it will take you a while before you hit
    your peak of success. Let others fall by the
    wayside, you'll be shooting straight for the top.
    Forget the rut of a 9 to 5, you've got your sights
    set on more than that.

    Then, once you've got success, you're flying off
    to the next challenge. You'll never sit still,
    after all, fires always spread and consume all
    around them. When you settle down you'll make
    your home in the city because that's where the
    bright lights are. Yet you'll try to have a place
    by a lake or the ocean so you can satify your
    latter half.

    Remember Scorpio, let your energy burn, don't let
    it rage, and keep it well under control, you want
    to conquer the world, not make it a cinder.
