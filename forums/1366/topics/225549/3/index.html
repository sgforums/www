    Merry Meet To All,

    What are Binding Spells? A binding spell is one intended to prevent harm, avoid danger, or to stop someone from performing particular acts.

    Is it a Black, Grey or White magick? Well Binding Spells are manipulative in their nature, Spell of darker means, more of Grey or Black Magick.

    Do they ever work? Yes they do work but dependable on the Power of Caster on their effects. All Spells have their limitation in their working, spell can't go on forever unless the Caster has very strong Powers. Binding Spells should be done with care, not just anyone can do it, they should be done only when its necessary if nothing else can work, this should be the last resort. Remember as with all binding spells, you are also binding yourself to the other person, think before you do it! Binding spells have a tendency to becoming real complicated! Personally, i do not encourage the casting of such spells unless there is no choice or necessary. Binding Spells are not easy to cast or accomplished. Love Binding Spell should not be done as its not correct as its manipulative, Real Love must be out of free-will to bring about Joy & Bliss. Love Binding Spells can lead to unexpected, adverse complicated results & effects, at times i feel it does more harm than help depending on situation.

    Thanks again.



    Blessed Be,
    Oceanos
