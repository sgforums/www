    There are many ways to guess the reaction of the person you loved and how they will respond to what you say. One of this ways is to picture their response in your mind.

    How to do it

    First close your eyes and breathe in deeply, then start focusing on the thing you're going to say. Then clear your mind. After you cleared your mind, try to picture them.

    If you can't picture them, the affinity is not strong enough or you're not doing it right.

    If you picture another person's face, then never bring up the subject.

    If you picture her face...

    Her mood will basically tell you her most likely response.
