    Let us assume that this birthdate belongs to someone... Guy named XYZ

    The sun sign is the main sign that we all know. The symbol is that of a circle with a dot in the middle.

    Sun in Leo

    Sun in 10th house.
    This house placement gives XYZ's Sun some of the qualities of Capricorn, no matter what sign his Sun is actually in. (Natural ruler of 10th house is capricorn- remember? ) When the sun is in the 10th house, there is Great ambitions to succeed. A natural leader and has strong desire to achieve positions of power and authority. Diginified and willing to put in hard work to succeed, managerial ability goes with those who have their sun in this position.

    Moon in Scorpio
    XYZ is likely to be a guy troubled by his childhood. Most probably had a love-hate relationship with his mother. Prolly had a mother who was too possessive, demanding or controlling, eitherway as an adult, the desire for emotional intensity is present as the moon is where one would seek comfort from. Exposed to the type of nuturing from his mother, XYZ would, consciously or unconsciously, nuture himself the same way, thus emotional drama may be present in many stages of his life.
    XYZ would also be a character which is suspicious of others intentions and wary of other's hidden workings or motivations. Highly likely to keep a very close watch over his environment and surroundings. Can be vulnerable and moody at times, but is sympathetic, highly sensitive and somewhat charitable. XYZ is likely to feel emotions at an extremely deep level and might react blindly to the emotion stimuli. His challenge is to be aware of his emotions, feel it and yet not be overwhelmed by it.
    XYZ would need time for his own inner reflections and ample opportunities to process their feelings, he is likely to be highly empathic (as with all those with moon in water signs) and would need to distinguish between their own feelings and that of others.

    Moon in 12th house. (Examine the chart, you'll find the cresent above the ASC or 1st house cusp line, yet still within range of scorpio sign)
    XYZ is greatly influenced by his past, it is his weakness, an insecurity. Many with the moon in this placement have good psychic abilities. As a person is somewhat shy and easily hurt. Would likely to be confronting issues on his childhood even until now and may find it re-playing itself in some ways in the family he will start.

    Rising Sign (Ascendant) in Scorpio
    Prefering to choose own path in life, XYZ is self-reliant and have a sense of self-determination and therefore not very easily swayed by the arguments of others. XYZ is someone who wants to be his own boss and not have others question his motives or meddle unnecessarily in his affairs. He does enjoy helping and teaching others and teaching others to help themselves however, once crossed his biting sarcasm and lust for vengence would surface, and may well be provoked to physical violence. He have a strong sense of self-respect that causes others to admire him and turn to him for advices. An intensely loyal friend and lover, he'll never forget a kindness or a slight and will not rest until he have repaid either, in kind.
    Generally hardy, the weakest part of his body would be the reproductive organs, nose and throat. Varicose veins and nosebleeds are common problem for those with this ascendant. In order to preserve health, XYZ must not work too hard or develop a negative attitude towards life, or that would manifest itself as a physical ailment. Prone to obesity and might have eyes that seem to penetrate thru the innermost thoughts of people. XYZ's body would tend to be more hairy than usual and might have lips that are full and sensous.
