    Basically what SCV81 is selling is called a "black box".

    A decoder (set top box) is modded to be able to descramble channel signals so that you are able to watch all channels.

    Sadly, this is ILLEGAL to use as it is considered Signal THEFT.

    Black boxes was a prevalent problem in North America that cable operators gave up and switched to digital signals. So far, there are no cases of signal theft yet as encrpytion is far more sophisticated.

    Black boxes have a few types, and you need to get one that is "bullet proof". What it means is resistant to "shut down signals" sent by Starhub.

    As a cable operator, they are aware that black boxes exist and the most practical solution is to send signals (or "bullets") to shut down your signals so that you can't watch unauthorised channels.

    A cable operator IS ABLE to detect usage of black boxes. Since the problem is not prevalent in Singapore yet, I guess this issue has been low profile.

    But in North America, Cable operators who cracked down on black box users often sue the users and they may be fined up to US$18,000 for lost revenue. The fine varies between US States and Canada provinces.
