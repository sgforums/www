    Here are the steps to posting up an image:

    1) Right click on the image and select " Copy Image Location ".

    2) Press the tree-like icon all the way to the left and on the same row as the 'Bold', 'Italic' and 'Underline', which is just above the space you are posting in.

    3) Paste the link which you have copied into Image Url, and finally press "Insert".

    On a side note, you can use the rest of the functions to change the Image's size and positioning.

     

    Here are the steps to linking an URL :

    1) Type out or Copy and Paste the URL.

    2) Highlight the entire URL

    3) Click the 2nd icon from the left of the toolbar next to the tree-like icon.

    4) Type out or Copy and Paste the URL into "Link URL" and press "Insert".
