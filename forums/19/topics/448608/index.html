    For that special, maternal figure in your life: Vita Cura® 5 Phase Firming Facial

    Specially formulated for mature skin, the Vita Cura® 5 Phase Firming Facial invigorates, lifts and firms while providing advanced skin repair. Created with natural marine and herbal ingredients, the treatment leaves mature skin looking and feeling younger instantly.

    The 5 essential phases in the treatment:

    Phase 1

    Vita Cura® Enzymatic Micropeel

    This papaya-based peel, a gentle resurfacing procedure, reveals a brighter, younger looking skin and a vibrant complexion.

    Phase 2

    Vita Cura® Cell Renewal Serum

    This serum is massaged into the skin to promote cellular repair, improve circulation, decrease dryness, and provide protection from oxidative damage.

    Phase 3

    Vita Cura® Triple Firming Treatment Cream

    This phase firms the skin in three dramatic ways. Messenger Pentapeptides act faster than Retinol, without the redness and irritation, Marine Biopeptides stimulate collagen production and cell renewal, and Red Clover Extract provides a hormonal trigger to younger looking skin. The Triple Firming Treatment cream is applied using the Lydia Sarfati Classic Facial Massage Technique, a vital phase in this treatment.

    Phase 4

    Vita Cura® Firming Mask

    Spirulina and Laminaria Digitata Seaweeds firm and smooth the skin while enhancing the action of previous layers.

    Phase 5

    Vita Cura® Opti-Lift Serum

    A Biopolymer of Almond protein provides immediate and visible lifting properties that you can see and feel long after this treatment is completed.

     

    And for younger skin: Hydra Refine® Facial*

    Work and daily environmental stressors can often leave younger skins oily, blemished and dehydrated. Hydra Refine® Facial for Oily Skin is the best natural solution. It aids in eliminating impurities in the skin, controlling oil production and reducing breakouts. Natural ingredients such as Seaweed, Lemongrass and Cucumber are incorporated into the facial, leaving the skin feeling thoroughly clean and revitalised.

    *Suitable for Normal to Oily skin.

    Toning

    This step is important to minimise pore size and purify the skin while acting as a soothing, cooling and most importantly, a healing agent.

    About Repêchage®

    Repêchage® is French for "to rescue" or "a second chance."
