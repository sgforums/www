    500-650 can get you a decent entry-level mtb. Few months back I got a bike at around this range so I roughly have an idea of the possible options.

    My eventual purchase was a Diamondback Response Sport 07 (pluto silver). The new 08 is out on the website but I don't think it's as nice. There's also the lower-end Response (w/o Sport).

    Other options:
    GT (Avalanche series)
    Orbea (the mtbs have quite a wide price range)
    Jamis
    Wheeler (Pro 29 / Pro 39)
    Merida
    Scott (Reflex / Sub / Sportster series)

    Commonly recommended bike shops are Hup Leong, Chapter 2 Cycle. If you stay in the west I can recommend you more shops which suit your budget.
