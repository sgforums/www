    orchid $1 Hon Sui Sen (without red seal) = $10

    to add on to my previous message:

    For orchid notes to be really worth alot of money, they have to be: (basic criteria = minimal EF-UNC condition)
    - Z series (replacement notes)
    - error notes
    - golden nos. like 888888, 999999, 777777, 111111, 222222, 333333....etc
    - Goh Keng Swee signature for $5 and $100 notes
