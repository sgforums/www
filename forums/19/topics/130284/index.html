    if you want to keep your airsoft gun working, 6mm paintballs are a distinct no-no.

    why? because more of them break within the mechanism than they do getting out of the barrel, because theyre inside under stress, and the mechanism is only set up for plastic bb's.

    so, youre running a huge risk of them actually breaking your airsoft gun.

    you can check on any airsoft forum, everyone recommends that you keep 6mm paintballs well away from airsoft. not worth it unless you have loads of money to spend on either repairing or buying new guns to replace what you break..
