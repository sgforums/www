Recommend Golf Shop @ Sunset Way

  • JoyGolf
    24 Jun 10, 11:02

    My friend recommend me to a  better pre-owned golf shop at Sunset Way,  so, I'm gonna to recommend to the golfers out there in Singapore.
    The clubs prices range from $1 to $100+, If you are lucky enough, you can find affordable and really good clubs!
    The shop owner is a very friendly old guy , he gives valuable advice related to golf (awww...just too bad he doesn't coach!!!)

    The shop also does customization, alteration the length of shaft, re-grip, i have my whole sets altered nicely according to my height. Overall, the service is really good there!

    nterested golfers can go,
    Blk 106, #02-38, Clementi St 12 (Sunset Way)
    (S) 120106, Tel: 67779120

    *Look for Golfers Friend signboard if you exit from the food court to the outdoor carpark, is kinda tricky to find the place.
