    The Holga Camera 135TIM is the first HOLGA half frame camera. This is a 35mm film camera basically a half-frame camera with the unique feature of two independent lens/shutter mechanisms to enable the use of only half a regular frame of film for each picture taken. As a result, the multiple options are available for selection to enhance the fun in your imaging activities:

    (a) Use two lens separately to take completely independent pictures

    (b) Use the two lens together to take two basically identical pictures at the same time

    (c) Use the two lens together to take 3D pictures of objects at a close distance and view by Holga 3DCV.

     As each image taken, be it identical or separate, occupies only half of the regular 135 film frame, it becomes much more economical when it comes to purchasing films and film development in addition to the advantage of multiple usage options mentioned above.

    There is a hot shoe on 135TIM, when taking pictures at dark environments, it is necessary to install a flash unit.
