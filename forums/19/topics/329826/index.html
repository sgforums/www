    Come visit Charity Flea Markets this coming weekend!

    For the first time, Weekends.sg and Singapore Art Museum will be collaborating with Project First Chance, participants of this year’s Citibank-YMCA Youth for Causes (www.youthforcauses.com), on two separate charity flea markets this coming weekend. Here are 4 reasons why you have to come visit us:

    1. It’s the weekend, and it’s a time for giving, sharing and having fun!

    2. You will be helping the mildly intellectually disabled youths of Happy Arts Enterprise under Metta Welfare Association. This social enterprise is involved with imparting vocational skills to such youths and placing them with jobs in the mainstream society. For more information or donations, please visit http://www.metta.org.sg/social.htm

    3. There will be plenty of stalls hawking exciting wares like handmade accessories and imported apparel at affordable prices, so you can be sure to shop till you drop!

    4. The best part? Rental charges and a portion of sales proceeds will be donated by our charity stall vendors to Happy Arts Enterprise for charity.

    What are you waiting for? Mark these dates on your calendar now!

    Dates: 11th (with food vendor Happy Arts deli, a part of our beneficiary), 12th and 13th September, Thur to Sat
    Venue: SMU School of Economics
    Organizers: Weekends.sg and Project First Chance

    Date: 14th September, Sun
    Venue: Inside Singapore Art Museum (SAM), Glass Hall + Courtyards
    Organizers: SAM and Project First Chance

    For more information on stall rental etc, please email project.first.chance.2@gmail.com or call 81002819.

    Thank you!
