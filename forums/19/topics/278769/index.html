    Pheidologeton is distributed throughout S. E. Asia and has been recorded in Hawaii. It is most common in Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand and Indonesia. Colonies are very large and well protected furthermore, there is only one queen, making it difficult to obtain a colony. However, during nuptial flights, the queens are attracted to shiny and bright surfaces. They usually fly into houses and mate on the ground. This will give you a good chance to catch them.

    The ants carry out nuptial flight once every two or three months unless you want to brave digging for a queen
