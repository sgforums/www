    Pit Grandstand partial description on official website:

    No other grandstand allows you to get closer to the nerve centre of the race as spectators here can observe the teams position their cars on the grid ready to start the race. The majority of seats have views into the team garages where fans can marvel at the teamwork and precision on display whilst selected seats offer views of the podium ceremony. Be the first to witness the crowning of the champion after the chequered flag is waved on the finishing line.

    The Pit Grandstand is close to the F1 Village, where spectators will enjoy staged and roving entertainment as well as a multitude of food, beverage and merchandise outlets.
