    Singapore Entertainment Awards 2011 (SEA)
    After raising the decibels in 2010, Singapore Entertainment Awards makes a comeback for another awesome blast!
    Following its astounding success last year, the Awards will be moving into its 3rd year in 2011. Organised by Singapore Press Holdings' Chinese media Lianhe Wanbao, Radio 100.3, omy.sg and UW (U-Weekly), the objective of the SEA is to support the Chinese entertainment industry that popularises the use of the Chinese language. The awards recognise a spectrum of entertainment ranging from music, movies, TV, radio to concerts. Apart from honouring the best talents in Singapore and Asia, it also helps to promote the local and regional Chinese entertainment scene.
    The qualifying period for award nomination is from 1 December 2009 to 31 December 2010. Fans get set to scream for your favourite stars who will battle for top awards in the "Most Popular" and "Best" categories! Awards in the "Best" categories will be judged by a panel of renowned professionals in the entertainment & media industry across the regions. From 7 Feb to 21 March 2011, public voting for the "Most Popular" categories can be done through SMS or online. SEA 2011 promises to be more exciting with the inaugural OCBC Titanium Star of the Year Award. See details in sgeawards.omy.sg.
    Winners of the SEA 2011 will be announced in March 2011. This year's SEA 2011 celebration concert will be held at the Singapore Indoor Stadium for the first time on 2 April 2011. Join us and the award winners as well as other performing artistes at the celebration concert!
