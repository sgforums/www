    Etymotic mc3 Headset + Earphones

     

    Etymotic makes the world's most accurate noise-isolating headsets and earphones in all categories. Most competing low-cost earphones are made with dynamic (moving coil) drivers that have undesirable frequency response peaks and weak highs. Etymotic created a design that makes dynamic drivers sound like high-end drivers by inserting a proprietary acoustic side-branch to redistribute energy, smooth the peaks and compensate for weak highs. The result is the Etymotic mc series, with best-in-class performance in its price range.

    The mc3 delivers tight, accurate bass with crystal clear high-frequency detail at all listening levels. You'll experience 35 dB - 42 dB of noise isolation, which is the documented highest noise isolation of any earphones or headphones on the market today. The mc3 features a close-proximity microphone with Apple 3-button control.

    Etymotic's mc3 looks and feels great, too. With a high-gloss polished aluminum finish available in three colors, the mc3 ships to you with an assortment of noise-isolating ACCU-Fit eartips to ensure proper seal and comfort. You also have the option of ordering custom molds through Etymotic's CUSTOM-FIT program.
