    View and print photos using your TV

    Fun for all the family
    When is a TV not a TV? When it's an imaging station, too! Discover the easiest and most enjoyable way to get stunning 4x6" photo-lab quality prints at home. Simply connect the stylish SELPHY DS700 to your TV and let the entire family share in the fun of viewing and printing their favourite photos.

    Easy memory card printing via your TV
    Sit back and relax as you use the remote control handset (included) and simple on-screen menus to select and print your digital camera images, with no PC required. The SELPHY DS700 supports all popular memory cards and is beautifully styled to complement your existing entertainment equipment.

    Fast, professional photo quality
    Print a beautiful 4x6" borderless photo in only 85 seconds. The SELPHY DS700 produces vibrant, durable prints using unique inkjet printing technology and a resolution of up to 4800 x 1200 dpi. Canon's exclusive FINE print head features Micro-Nozzles that eject microscopic 2pl ink droplets with high precision for exceptionally detailed prints.

    Print directly from your camera or mobile phone
    If you prefer, you can print photos direct from your digital camera or mobile phone. The SELPHY DS700 supports PictBridge for direct photo printing from all compatible digital cameras and camcorders. Or you can even print photos direct from an IrDA compatible camera phone.

    Beautifully finished prints
    The SELPHY DS700 supports a wide range of Canon photo papers so you can print photos with a satin or glossy finish - just like you would get from the photo lab, but in the comfort of your own home.

    Connect to your computer
    For the flexibility of being able to connect to a PC, a USB port and drivers for the Windows and Macintosh operating systems are also included.
