     * * * * * * At sending you this reviews pertaining to the above at your request. The following are my subjective observation of the MOGAMI RCA IC which I would classify under the following: 

     

    [b][color=red]Highs:[/b][/color] Detailed, Good Insight to lower-high frequency in particular instruments like cymbals, high-hats, zijians shone through loud and clear. Micro details are good but not excellent if compared with my reference $200 pure silver interconnects. 

     

    [b][color=red]Mids:[/b][/color] This is the MOGAMI cable forte’s positive aspects like [b]Warmness, Openness, Realism, etc[/b] comes to mind; very Studio-like 

     

    sounding. [b][color=red]In my book, this MOGAMI cable is virtually faultless in Mids! [/b][/color]

     

    [b][color=red]Lows:[/b][/color] Prominent, weighty and punchy are the adjectives to this MOGAMI cable. I would use to describe the [b]bass region[/b] a bit a little one-note at times. Very un-Japanese style I should say. 

     

    [b][color=red]Pros:[/b][/color] [b][color=red]MOGAMI is Natural somewhat Open, Warm and Wide sounding. Organic and Real are the other positive attributes of these chords. Never shut-in or puny, MOGAMI is bold and upfront but not too much like the in-your-face that kind. [/b][/color]

     

    [b][color=red]Cons:[/b][/color] The above mentioned lower treble is a double edged sword its bane is that it’s a little prominent that if it’s not carefully matched, might be a little tiresome on prolong listening. Compared with my reference cables, the MOGAMI bass sounded ever so slightly one-note and especially compared MOGAMI with my Home-grown pure silver cables, the latter exposes the former's weaknesses in areas of speed, detail, transient and control in low freq. ; [b][color=red]of course, we are talking about below $50 VS $200 ! [/b][/color]

     

    [b][color=red]Conclusion:[/b][/color]: I auditioned and gave my subjective views in the absolute sense; meaning I am comparing the MOGAMI cables with a much more expensive competitors like Van den hul. [b][color=red]Honestly I would rate the MOGAMI I/Cs highly at $150! But at selling price below $50, I think buying a pair or even a few pairs is a no-brainier![/b][/color] Only caveat, try not pairing them with bright sounding or bass prominent equipment. *
