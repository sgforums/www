    <<Guide for Sellers on Product Condition>>

    New - The entire set is still in its sealed protective wrapping, untampered. No dents or scratches on the box itself whatsoever.


    Like New - Product or products in the set have been unwrapped, played once or twice, but DLC has not been used. Product(s) are still in a flawless condition.


    Very Good - Product or products in the set may have one or two surface scratches, box may have a dent or have standard surface wear and tear, DLC has been used or is expired BUT no items in the set are missing or impaired in functionality.


    Good - Product or products in the set may have more than two surface scratches OR at least one deep scratch, such that a hole may have appeared in the product. Box may have wear and tear, and deep dents such as holes or minute tears in the corners. Products are still in good working condition but no longer as aesthetically appealing. Up to 1 item in the set may be missing or impaired in functionality.


    Fair - Product or products in the set may have at least three surface scratches OR at least two deep scratches, such that a hole or serious tears may have appeared in the product. Box has sustained serious damage due to improper storage conditions or box is already disposed of. Products may no longer be functioning properly, but still in working condition but no longer as aesthetically appealing. Up to 2 items in the set may be missing or impaired in functionality.


    Acceptable -  Product or products in the set may have more than three surface scratches OR at least three deep scratches, such that a holes or serious tears may have appeared in the product. Box has sustained serious damage due to improper storage conditions or box is already disposed of. Products may no longer be functioning properly, but still in working condition but no longer as aesthetically appealing. Up to 3 items in the set may be missing or impaired in functionality.


    Junk - Many items in the set are missing, damaged or have impaired functionality, or may only include the main highlight of the product itself (e.g. the console). Product aesthetics have seen better days. Items in this category may not sell above $10, unless photos of the products are sent to be scrutinized.
