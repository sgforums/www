    Although the models you mentioned are digital SLRs, they are classified under the entry level and professional level. If you are comparing entry level models (D70) to more professional models (MKII and D2X) then it is not the camera body concerned.     Pixel rate as high as 10 or over mega pixel is very crucial for professional photgraphers. They need the pixels for large print outs of their works. You can never take a 1 mega pixel photo and try to photoshop it and hope to print a good one out in A4. The most it can do is a ppostcard sized photo. The basic building block of a good photo print to retain the original colors is in the number of pixels a digital camera can capture and produce.

    The similar range of D70s/80s though called SLRs are just entry level that even a rookie in photography can buy and familiarise and perfect over time.
