    There are common rooms for rent at Sembawang B422 which are comparatively very small 200 Sq Ft and would cost you somewhere around S$400. A good option is to look for HDB for rent, the rental for a 100 Sq Ft HDB would be around S$600. There are many properties available for rent; a good way to search for rental property would be through property portals like Property Guru.
