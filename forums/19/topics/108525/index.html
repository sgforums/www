  • nightzip
    29 Dec 04, 20:50
    think to see cells, you need at least 700-1000 times magnification?

    optical microscopes can only go up to 1000 times max...but have to trade between depth of view and field of view...not so good.

    for >1000 times up to 500,000 times max...you need an electron microscope, but samples may need to be conductive, else cannot see anything...higher mag ones you can use field-emission or high-mag electron microscopes..

    for up to 1 million times and over, use TEM. (can see atoms some more)!
  • nightzip
    29 Dec 04, 20:54
    ohh..to approximate,

    700-1000 times can see up to 10-20 microns level,
    500,000 times can see up to 0.02microns or 20 nanos level, or nano-structured materials
    1million times above can see up to 5-10 nanos level down to atomic level, but need super resolution microscope.
