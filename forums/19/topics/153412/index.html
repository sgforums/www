    go along to AirsoftAsia and see what they use.

    Tokyo Marui is a good reliable bet, even though theyve had their crown tipped over the last few years by many china[hk] and taiwanese competitors.

    you say you dont want airsoft yet your requirements are way off both [decent] airsoft and air rifles and air pistols.

    anyway, for accurate shooting, within the airsoft range, you could go for the tokyo marui VSR-10 series of spring powered sniper rifles [< 1joule ] - low powered but very accurate and would meet your other requirements if not price [they are the cheapest out of box best performers - most other sniper rifles would require some sort of tweaking or ugrading to get the best out of them, VSR's often perform superbly out of the box].

    [for example i had the L96, and spent a few hundred on upgraded [but swappable] bolts and cylinders to get the performance i wanted - not exactly cheap for a 'sunday sniper']

    compared to air rifles and pistols these are pretty quiet too.
    [and some can come withsuppressors or silencers - certain VSR types do come in this configuration as well]

    do google search and you'll come up with reviews and details.

    anyway, the only way to do it legally would be through a club.

    AirsoftAsia would be a start though, even if you just want to go along and have a look at what they do [competitive IPSC shooting].

    personally i cant stand air pirstols and air rifles - airsoft has always been a good bridge bewteen the real that you can never have [in terms of looks and weight, functionality, operation] and safety [they dont really hurt, have never killed and are far safer than air rifles and air pistols - and hurt alot less than painball!]
