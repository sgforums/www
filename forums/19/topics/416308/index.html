    Come Saturday, 4 December 2010 between 5pm to 8pm, SAFRA Mount Faber would be organising its Christmas Light-up Ceremony!

     

    This year, we're getting the whole family involved.

     

    We are looking for adults and children between the ages of 4 to 70 years old. Parade down and be part of the festivities together as a family.

     

    The fees will cover :

    * Santa / Santarina Outfits(It's yours after the parade!)

    * Workshop on how to perform and be part of the parade

    * Light refreshments

     

    There will also be stage shows & fun games throughout the day as part of the Light-Up Celebrations at SAFRA Mount Faber. Be a part of this jolly feat!

     

    Fees  :

    $16.05 (SAFRA Member); $19.25 (Guest)

    $10.70 (SAFRA Junior Member); $12.85 (Junior Guest)

               

    We hope to have you on board for this grand ceremony.

     

    To register, please let me know. Thank you.
