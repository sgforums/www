    If you intend to get a bed frame from ikea, take note that ikea's bed sizes are proprietary. This means, standard bedlinen won't fit ikea's products. So you can happily go robinsons and pick out that 600 egyptian combed cotton bed set and it'll never fit any of your ikea's bed products. Only ikea bedlinen can fit ikea's bed. That's just a general reminder. Everywhere else, bed sizes are standard, not ikea.
