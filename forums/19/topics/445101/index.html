    General Epistemology (Theory of Knowledge):
    Knowledge and Inquiry - An Introduction to Epistemology 
    by Charlene Tan, Lachlan Crawford · hard to find + written with the a'level curriculum in mind. published in singapore by pearson. 

    Thinking through Philosophy - An Introduction
    by Chris Horner, Emrys Westacott · covers both general epistemology + major fields of knowledge & significant thinkers in great detail. published by cambridge.

    Problems of Knowledge - A Critical Introduction to Epistemology
    by Michael Williams · one of the most exhaustive introductions to general epistemology, highly rewarding. published by oxford. 

    Philosophy - The Basics
    by Nigel Warburton · acclaimed for its clarity and precision. published by routledge.

    Understanding Philosophy (Teach Yourself)
    by Mel Thompson · an ideal introduction to philosophy - covers both general epistemology and specific fields of knowledge, great for independent learning. published by McGraw-Hill.

    Specific Fields of Knowledge:
    Philosophy of Science - A contemporary introduction

    Introducing Science 
    by Ziauddin Sardin and Borin Van Loon · offers a good summary and handy examples for essay writing. published by icon books.

    The Philosophy of Social Science - An Introduction
    by Martin Hollis · covers social scientific knowledge in great detail, ideal if you'd like to specialise in social scientific knowledge for Paper 1 or the IS. published by cambridge.
