    [color="#800080"][b]Fashion Labels of the Night[/b][/color]
    [b]Chaos[/b] designs, develops and produces a distinctive line of women’s apparel. With it’s exclusive bold prints and a signature look of sensuality, our chaos line appeals to the confident, body-conscious woman who’s not afraid to express her individuality. Look good and feel good in Chaos.

     

    [b]Déjà Vu Vintage[/b] is stylish, one-of-a-kind and heart-achingly precious. Most likely eco-friendly too.  From casual, quirky day dresses to stunning cocktail numbers, sassy shirts, jackets, skirts, shirtdresses, accessories, dots, prints, psychedelics and intricate handmade lace...what’s in store is completely unpredictable from week to week.  There exists only one of each precious piece at Déjà Vu Vintage – each item is personally selected, and imported from the U.S, Europe, Australia or Japan. We also carry an exclusive range of handmade accessories from local and overseas designers. Everything in Déjà Vu Vintage promises to be one-of-a-kind. And to make sure shopping truly is retail therapy, we've banished the messy displays and rummaging associated with vintage shopping. Shopping at Déjà Vu Vintage is true pleasure in an elegant, spacious environment.

     

    [b]Grey Vibes[/b] is a boutique with a delectable choice of clothes with reasonable price ranges.Expect to choose from a coterie of styles ranging from casual to sexy which could take you from day to night. We use plenty of chiffon and satin which are comfortable yet fashionable, and once in a while, we decide to be experimental in other types of fabric. Grey Vibes is truly for the modern woman who is independent and bold.

     

    [b]Eyeplay[/b] is all about having fun dressing up.  With an acute sense of fashion and trends, the brand offers a wide selection of affordable eye apparel in a myriad of styles and colours every season - bringing eyewear beyond the functional to add panache to your style.  Eyeplay are bound to give you limitless fun in mixing and matching eye wear with different outfits to create the desired look for you.

     

    [b]Ling[/b] boutique, a contemporary lifestyle product label, fuses form and function into working pieces of artistry.  At The Ling Boutique, we believe in paying attention to especcially the little things in life, the everyday objects that we see and use.  This is because we understand that it is the little things in life that matter most.  Good taste pervades into the smallest tings we see and use.  We know tht even the smallest things in life illlustrate ones taste and ability to appreciate the finer things in life.  The Ling Boutique carries a comprehensive range of contempory lifestyle products fusing storng functionality and practicality with taste in its design philosophy.  Lifestyle objects found and used everyday by everyone across the world, bringing the job of good taste to the little details of life.
