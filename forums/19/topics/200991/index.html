    For RC or Fuel engined cars, best to buy new kits... Brands like Kyosho, OS, Tamiya, JR are good and famous for their kits... Can get them from $120 to $900 for electric or fuel-engined RC car... Of course it depends whether you are getting an entry level RC kit to advanced kit... Chassis material also plays a part like carbon-graphite, aluminium alloy, anodised alloy... Another important will be the motor or engine to be mounted... For electric motor, there are different types for different needs like sports-tuned (15 turns), raced-tuned (8 turns), and for engine like 2-speed or 4-speed flywheel or clutchwheel type...
