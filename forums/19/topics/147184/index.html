    Hi guys,
    Im not sure if you all have received such messages from any of your auctions before, but well beware of them.

    When someone email you they are in a hurry to buy an item at high price and can't wait for the bid to end, especially when they request you to send item to overseas, this is some tell-tale sign of scam email, be very careful. This happens especially to electronic items and expensive items.

    Either you ignore and delete such email, or wait till you receive and SEE the real money.

    Symtoms of a scam:

    1. Offered much higher prices to buy your item

    2. Insist that they pay by BidPay Money Order

    3. Items to be sent to Africa/Nigeria

    >>Please be very VERY CAREFUL! Do not be blinded by GREED!!

    >>Please do remember that www.bidpay.com DO NOT ask for tracking number. Once anyone indicate that you must sent a tracking number to bidpay.com (the email address is a fake) this transaction is a scam.

    COMMON SCENARIOS:

    -- Received email from western union about the confirmation of the amount received from buyer.

    -- Buyer request to shipped out the goods.

    -- After you have already shipped out the goods, you nv receive the $$ -- Checked with western union bidpay on the status of your money order, realised that there was NONE
