    Custom homes are called to be the homes of the olden days generally. But earlier the term was used for the big houses built by the government to house many of its offices and the single building will then act as the administrative office for the same. The houses constituted of gigantic entrances, excessive use of wood in the construction, rough and beautiful floors, wide and lengthy walkways and so on. The houses thus built were also suited best for the respective climate as well. This is evident from the difference of the custom houses been built in the northern and the southern parts of India. In the northern part the custom houses use to have a dome at the top whereas at the southern part the custom houses have the slanting mud brick roofs. Those custom houses use to possess some qualities of the newly built green homes as well.
