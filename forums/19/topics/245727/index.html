    digital cameras are pretty cheap these days and anything around 5MP will get you damned good pictures for print up to about A4 size or more...

    read up in dpreview about the types..

    a few practical things for normal pic taking.. look at how much batteries and what type they use... AA or AAA batteries will put a hole in your pocket as most digicams are pretty high drain

    but the batteries which are made for the camera is expensive so there is a balance which you must find if you want to take a lot of pictures...

    media is pretty cheap these days so you can forget about these things.. most use sd or xd and CF.. Sony ones ALL use their memory sticks which are more expensive...

    just go walk around and have a hands-on with them..

    the main brands all have pretty much the same performance these days with 3x to 5x zoom and macro..

    for really small compacts, go with the new sony or casio ones... they're very small..
