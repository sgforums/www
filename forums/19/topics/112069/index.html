    Seems to me that it might not be your monitor problem.

    Possible problems
    1. Hard Disk problem (crash/boot sector corrupted)
    2. VGA Card (loose/damaged)
    3. Motherboard problem (unlikely but possible)
    4. Monitor faulty.

    Soultions
    1. If you have another spare monitor try connecting to the PC, if same problem occurs the above point 4 is out.
    2. Try disconnecting everything from your CPU (mouse, keyboard, power etc), wait for 5 minutes, then connect everything back (power last to connect) then try switching on again.

    If then above two solutions doesn't work might need to further troubleshoot further to pinpoint on the faulty part.

    Hope that helps.
