    Originally posted by eviljustice:
    wat does channels mean
    channels mean an individual control function...most rc helicopters have either 4 or 5 servos to control...aileron, elevator, tail rotor, throttle, and collective pitch for non fixed pitch models...more channels than that are usually used as on/off switching for things like retractable landing gear(on-gear up, off-gear down) flying lights, etc.
