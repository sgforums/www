    Great thanks for the overwhelming response!
    Here's a general FAQs for the fishes

    Basic Care Instructions
    Must do
    Apply anti-chlorine in tank before putting fishes in
    Aeration to the tank using air stones, etc
    Filters for the tank

    Feed the fishes daily (Yes! you still need to feed the fishes) with normal fish food for chin chins and sinking fish food pellets for Garra species
    Use a UV clarifier (Pre Filter) to sterilize the water and algae control (Optional)

    Do a 1/2 tank water change every 2-4weeks for chin chins and as and when needed for Garra Species. If you have Water clarifier like TetraAqua CrystalWater, TetraAqua Easy Balance, you can reduce the amount of water changes up to 6months!

    Please note Garra species have a tendency to jump (HIGH!) while its adapting to the new tank, so covering the tank up with a net would be a good idea. They tend to jump towards water flow if you have a water inlet flowing over the surface of the water.
    DO NOT put any exposed pipings in the water for Garra species as they have a bad habit of checking out small pipes/tubings and you might end up with like LOTS of Garra species dead in a pipe/tubing.
