    CRAZY IS THE MONKEY

    Crazy is the Monkey,

    Give him a nut he goes nutty

    Give him a banana he goes crazy

    Give him a chance to Go West

    To redeem thousand years of sins

    Forever playful is the monkey,

    Talent put to the wrong use,

    Unrepentent stole he the heavenly peach,

    Once again Banished and full of pity.
