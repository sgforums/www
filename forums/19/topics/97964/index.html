    Got a Fortis Day/Date Cosmonauts Chronograph for sale, for S$430.

    As shown in the pics below (sorry for the poor quality), it is one of the blue face/leather strap ones, and comes with the original box and leather pouch that contains the insteuction booklet and warranty card.

    It is a little over a year old, and has been used, but most certainly not abused (read: the strap has formed into 'buckled' shape).

    For those of you not familiar with this particular timepiece, the movement is automatic (2836-2); with hour, minute and second hand (in orange); a unidirectional bezel; and the date and time display (at the 3 o'clock position).

    It was designed in 1994 for the Yuri Gagarin Russian State Scientific-Research Test Centre of Cosmonauts Training in Star City (in other words NASA's Russian counterpart).
