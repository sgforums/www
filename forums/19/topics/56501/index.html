    How It Works?
    * The Dual SIM Adapter fits under the battery or between phone and rear cover without the need to change the battery or the rear cover allowing you to use the face and rear cover of your choice. Simply switch between networks by Off-On phone or #3370#.

    Uses:
    * Easiest way of enabling the use of 2 SIM cards in one cell phone.
    * Keep business and personal calls separate.
    * Use another country's SIM card when traveling abroad.
    * Using a dual SIM phone the user can mix and match networks for cheaper calls.

    Pros:
    * No need for a new bulky back cover.
    * Compatible with the phones original battery & rear cover.
    * No modifications to the phone required.
    * No cutting of SIM card.
    * The SIM card and cellphone's warranty is unaffected.

    Cons:
    * You cannot make / recieve calls on both sims concurrently, only 1 will work at a time.
    * Depending on phone model, your last dialed, recent received & dialed numbers will be erased when you switch to the other SIM.
