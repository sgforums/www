    I want to downgrade my mobile phone, a Nokia express music smartphone which I bought last year in March from an Ah Beng shop. I still have a M1 contract running, which means I am only eligible for a 12-month upgrade. The reason? Because I feel that a mobile phone should be.. as the name suggests, just a mobile phone. No twitter, no facebook, no blogging, no forum surfing, and since I do not drive, I hope I will not miss the GPS features that a smartphone has to offer.

    Or to put it simply, I need a M16, not a SAR21 with all the bells and dongles. And it’s because I miss my old M16’s recoil. I want to feel, and I want especially the big buttons, which like my M16 trigger, when she is pressed it jerks and reacts violently and I feel she is alive.

    My touch screen phone, with all its Wifi and what naught, feels dead. I play with it every day, and it is worse than my pen is. At least my dick reacts violently at my age.. although it is already showing signs of losing energy due to wear and tear. My handphone, the only physical reaction it offers me is the vibration which I can easily pay $10 to get a vibrator, and a bunch of electronic sounds which mean absolutely  nothing to me. Whimsical to say, not only do I need a yesteryear, non-state-of-the-art handphone, I need it to be a used 2nd hand gizmo, as much as possible. And I need to love it. The idea now is that I want back the Human Technology that says, I FEEL.

    And I will only accept a Nokia. Because I am human. I came from the era where the Nokia 3210, my first handphone, was the most advanced piece of Human Technology I have ever seen as we headed into the new millennium.

    The options I have at M1 shop are already too advanced for me.

    -          Nokia C2-01

    -          Nokia X3

    -          Nokia E5

    Or I will consider a HTC Snap Non Camera as a last resort.
