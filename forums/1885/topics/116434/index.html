Egg-flower waterchestnut

  • icebaby0
    7 Feb 05, 15:17

    Ingredients :
    5 cups of water
    1 bundle of pandan leaves
    80gm-100gm rock sugar
    200gm waterchestnut, cut into pea-sized pieces
    1/2 cup fresh or frozen corn
    3 level soupspoon(porcelain type) waterchestnut powder
    1/3 cup water (to dissolve waterchestnut powder)
    pinch of salt to taste(optional)
    1/2 egg, beaten

    Method:
    1. Boil water, pandan leaves with rock sugar until sugar melted.
    2. Add waterchestnut, boiled for 3 minutes.
    3. Add corn.
    4. Add waterchestnut thickener. Cook until boiling and thick.
    5. Add salt (optional).
    6. Turn off fire. Add egg slowly and swirl to make egg flower pattern.
