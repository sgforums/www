Apple Strudal Recipe

  • icebaby0
    7 Feb 05, 14:52
    400g flour
    180g iced water
    10g salt
    10g caster sugar
    80g unsalted butter
    1/2 tsp vanilla flavour - optional
    1/2 tsp butter oil - optional
    150g pastry margarine

    Put all ingredients EXCEPT pastry margarine in a mixing bowl.

    Mix the dough preferably with hook attachment in low speed for 2-3 mins, turn to medium 1 min & finally high for about 3 mins.

    Rest the dough for 15 mins. Keep it covered with a cloth.

    Roll out to oblong shape. Roll pastry margarine to likewise & put on top of the dough.

    Fold the dough into 3 equal lengths, roll & fold again for 3 more times.

    Rest it in the fridge for 30 mins.

    Take it out & roll out to 1/2 cm thick.

    Cut 3 pieces 5" by 11" long.

    Brush with egg wash & sprinkle with sugar.

    Rest the dough for 20 mins.

    Bake in preheated oven 200 deg. C for 20mins.

    Let it cool & cut lengthwise into halves.

    Put in filling.

    Filling

    2kg red/green apple skinned, sliced or cubed
    1litre water
    250g sugar
    2 sticks cinnamon
    Juice of 1 lemon
    80g cornflour mixed with a bit of water to make paste

    Boil water, sugar, cinnamon & lemon juice.
    Add cornflour paste & stir smooth.
    Turn off fire & add apples,s tir & cover.
    Let it cool.

    The chef gave shortcuts - buy prepared puff pastry - follow the instructions to bake.
