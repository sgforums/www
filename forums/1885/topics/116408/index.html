Japanese Cheesecake Recipe (1)

  • icebaby0
    7 Feb 05, 14:48
    Cake A
    500g cream cheese (Philadelphia brand)
    100g castor sugar
    150g double cream
    4 egg yolk
    90g plain flour add in 30g sifted corn flour

    Cake B
    4 egg white
    80g castor sugar

    Method:
    Preheat oven at 150 deg C.

    Cake A
    Beat cream cheese till smooth, add in sugar n beat till fluffy. Add double cream n beat till smooth. Add in egg yolk n mix well. Fold in sifted flour. Put aside.

    Cake B
    Whisk egg white till frothy, add sugar n whisk till stiff.

    Mix A n B together n pour into pan. Put pan in a larger pan half filled w hot water. Bake at 150C for 1 hour or till golden brown n cooked.

    Let cake cooled by turning off oven n opening oven door. Do not remove cake from oven before cooled as cake will cracked.
