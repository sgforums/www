    Ingredients :

    1. 14 Tiger prawns (medium)
    2. 1 stalk lemon grass
    3. 2 sliced fresh red chili
    4. 2 sliced dried red chili
    5. 2 tbsp Thai chilis jam
    6. 1 tbsp sliced dried green veggie
    7. 1 tbsp sliced black olives
    8. 1 tbsp sliced green olives 
    9. 1 handful sliced onions
    10. 1 tsp chopped garlic

    Steps:

    1.Peel prawns and set aside.
    2.Slice lemon grass to 1-inch length (or less).
    3.Heat pan to high heat, add oil and toss all ingredients except Thai chili jam. Saute till almost cooked.
    4.Add Thai chili sauce and toss till all ingredients are coated.

    Tips :

    • Top with sesame seed or spring onions.
    • This is a spicy dish. Can avoid spiciness by adding little thai chilli jam.
    • The colouring of the dish makes it even more appetizing.
