Chocolate Chip Muffins

  • icebaby0
    8 Feb 05, 10:37

    1 1/2 cups plain flour
    1 1/2 tsp baking powder
    1/2 cup caster sugar
    1/2 tsp salt
    1 cup milk
    1 egg
    1/3 cup butter, melted (can melt in microwave)
    1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

    Preheat oven to 175 C. Lightly grease or line 12 muffin cups

    In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar and salt. Stir in the milk, egg, butter and chocolate chips. Pour batter into muffin cups.

    Bake in preheated oven for about 15-20mins, until toothpick inserted into the center of muffin comes out clean.
