Banana Loaf

  • icebaby0
    13 Feb 05, 16:18

    Oven - 190C

    Ingredients

    2 ripe bananas (I use Del Monte)
    2oz margarine/butter
    5oz caster sugar
    8oz self raising flour
    2 eggs
    pinch of salt

    Method

    1. Mash bananas
    2. Cream margarine/butter & sugar
    3. Beat in eggs
    4. Add flour, salt & bananas - mix well
    5. Put in greased loaf tin - 1 x 2lb tin or 2 x 1lb tins
    6. Bake for about 1 hr until golden brown, springly to touch & shrinking slightly from sides of tins.
    7. Turn out & cool on wire racks.
