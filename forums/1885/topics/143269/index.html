    Walnut Mooncake

    Ingredients

    360 gm of plain flour
    25 gm Custard Powder
    1/2 tsp Sodium Bicarbonate
    50 gm icing sugar
    50 gm Crisco shortening
    220 gm butter (salted)
    1/2 tsp vanilla flavour
    1/2 tsp Ammonium Bicarbonate
    1 egg
    Walnuts for decoration

    Filling

    1 kg Lotus Paste
    200-250 gm Walnut (toasted and chop)

    Methods

    1. Cream sugar, shortening, butter, vanilla flavour and ammonium together until slightly white. (slow speed first then change to high speed)
    2. Gradually add in egg.
    3. Lastly add in sifted flour, custard powder and sodium bicarbonate to knead into a dough. (don’t mix too long) Leave dough in fridge overnight or for a few days.
    4. Take out dough from fridge and divide into portions of 30 gm each.
    5. Divide fillings into about 40 gm each.
    6. Wrap fillings into dough and decorate with walnut. Apply egg wash and bake at 160ÂºC for 10 mins. Take it out and leave it for about 10 mins and apply egg wash again. Bake for another 15 mins.

    Yam Moon Cake

    Ingredients
    600g steam yam
    400g cooked lotus seed
    300g sugar
    200g corn oil

    Method ? Grind all ingredients together, cook over fire till a dough is formed, leave it to cool

    Pastry

    - Oil Pastry
    500g flour
    260g lard
    Mix evenly, divide into small portions.

    - Water Pastry
    500g flour
    60g sugar
    200g corn oil
    220g water

    Method
    1. Mix all the ingredients together, divide into small portions, enclose each portion with 1 portion of oily pastry
    2. Roll up and knead the combine pastries, repeat procedure and leave aside for 15 min
    3. Divide the dough piece into halves, roll out into circular shapes, enclose each piece with filing. Deep fry in preheat oil for abt 3 min, remove and bake for 8 min @ 180C

    Mooncake crust

    Ingredients:
    300g Plain Flour (or Hong Kong Flour)
    6 tbsp peanut oil
    1/2 tbsp Alkaline water
    3/4 bowl Sugar Syrup
    Method:
    Mix well and let the dough rest for 1 to 2 hours...
    Press into the mould and knock the cookie out and place on the tray.
    Bake for 5 mins, brush it with egg. Then bake for another 10 mins or until it's done.
