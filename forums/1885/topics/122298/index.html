Chilli Crabs Recipe 2

  • icebaby0
    16 Mar 05, 09:32
    My method is "agaration one"

    Ingredients
    1. Del monte tomato sauce
    2. Sin Sin chilli sauce
    3. 3 tablespoon of oyster sauce
    4. 2-3 eggs (beat the eggs)
    5. Lots of mince garlic ( around 6 pieces)
    6. 2 big purple onions, cut into quarters or eights

    1. Put oil into wok, fry until crab is half cook or turn red lah
    2. Take out the crab

    3. If you use alot of oil, then throw out some oil. 8P.
    4. Add garlic into wok, fry until fragrance come out
    5. Add the onions in
    6. Add half a bottle of the tomato sauce, half bottle of chilli sauce then add the oyster sauce. (You may need to adjust to taste)
    7. Add the crabs in and fry.
    8. Make sure that woks is very hot, add the eggs in and mix. This will make the eggs come out as shreds.
