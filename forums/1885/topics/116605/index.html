    FRIED BEEF WITH KAI LAN (Kai Lan is said to benefit the spinal cord & kidneys. Beef is "blood-building".)

    300g Lean Beef
    300g Kai Lan
    2 slices old ginger, shredded
    1 clove garlic, shredded/minced
    1 tsp Chinese Rice Wine
    ? Tbsp Sesame Seed Oil 1Tbsp Oil
    Seasoning: ? tsp salt, 1 tsp sugar, ? tsp light soya sauce. Marinate : 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp light soya sauce, 1 Tbsp oyster sauce, 1Tbsp cornflour, 1 Tbsp Oil

    Rinse & pat dry beef. Slice thinly across the grain. Marinate for 15mins.
    Slice off the fibrous parts of Kai Lan Stems. Cut into thick slices. Wash & drain.
    Heat oil , then fry ginger & garlic until fragrant.
    Put in Kai Lan & seasoning & stiry-fry for 2mins. Remove & set aside.
    Reheat wok with sesame seed oil until hot. Then add marinated beef & stir-fry for 2mins.
    Return vegetables to wok & stir-fry with beef for a while. Serve.
