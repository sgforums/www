Sweet sauce chicken - microwave oven method

  • icebaby0
    8 Feb 05, 10:27
    Ingredients:
    1/2 chicken (cut to pieces)
    some mince garlic

    To marinate chicken:
    little salt to taste (not too much as there's sauce to add later)
    little pepper
    corn flour

    Mix the following for Sauce:
    3tbsp tomatoes ketchup
    1tbsp each black vinegar, sugar, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce
    enough water

    Method:
    1. Heat oil (agak-agak or estimate amount) on high power for 1 1/2 mins.
    2. Fry garlic on high power for 2 1/2 mins. Add in Chicken pieces, continue to fry for 2 mins.
    3. Pour the pre-mixed sauce and mix well with chicken. Cover and Cook on High power for another 10 mins, continue with medium power for 10 mins. Check in between to ensure that there's still water/sauce there (or else u will get 'burnt' chicken.

    Done! If you wish, u could add potatoes wedges in, big onion, vege. Cooking time may vary a bit.
