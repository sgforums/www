Tiramisu Recipe (2)

  • icebaby0
    7 Feb 05, 14:29
    250g Marscarpone cheese (fr Cold Storage)
    250g Thick cream (Can substitue wif thicken cream)
    2 packs of Sponge fingers (sponge fingers work better but can substitue wif Lady's fingers)
    2 cups of cooled Black coffee (no sugar nor milk added)
    1/2 cup baileys or kahlua or fruit liquer
    unsweetened cocoa powder

    1) Whip Marscarpone cheese & thick cream together till no more lumps.
    2) Mix cooled coffee wif liquer of yr choice
    3) Line rectangular cake tin wif cheese & cream mixture,
    4) Dip sponge fingers into coffee mixture & line ontop of cheese mixture

    Repeat steps 3 & 4 till ingredients are all used up.

    5) Put the cake into the fridge overnite (at least 18hours).
    6) Overturn cake onto a plate & dust wif cocoa powder. Serve.
