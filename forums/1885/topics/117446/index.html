Steamed Egg with fresh milk

  • icebaby0
    13 Feb 05, 16:07

    1 big egg
    1/2 cup fresh milk
    2 tbsp granulated sugar

    1. Mix sugar with 1 tbsp of boiling water. Blend well until sugar is dissolved.

    2. Beat egg, add in fresh milk, beat well. Add in sugar solution & beat well. Pass thru' a sieve to get a smooth custard.

    3. Pour egg custard in a bowl, cover with aluminium foil & steam for about 18-20 mins.
