Chicken in coke

  • icebaby0
    16 Feb 05, 13:30
    Ingredients
    - 1/2 a btl of Coke (the big btl one hor.)
    - 1 medium size chicken (cut into large pcs or 6-8 chicken wings)
    - Thumb size Ginger (sliced)
    - 4-6 cloves of garlic (minced 2 gloves & give a slight bash wif the back of chopped for the rest)
    - 1-2 star anise
    - 5-8pcs of cloves
    - 1 cinamon stick
    - Chinese wine
    - Soy sauce
    - pepper

    Method
    - Marinate chicken wif chinese wine, abit of ginger, pepper & soy sauce for at least 1/2 hr.
    - Heat oil in pot. Stir fry remaining ginger & mince garlic till lightly brown.
    - Add in star anise, cloves & cinnamon stick & stir fry for abt 1-2min.
    - Add in chicken & stir fry for 5mins.
    - Pour in Coke & add in remaining ingredients & bring to a boil.
    - Then lower fire & simmer for at least 1 hour. Add soy sauce & pepper to taste.
