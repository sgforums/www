    Coconut & Chicken Soup

    1/2 coconut(brown skin scraped off)
    1/2 chicken
    3bowls of water

    Boil the chicken in a small pot for 2-3mins.
    Put boiled chicken & coconut pieces into slow cooker cook for 3-4hrs.

    *no need condiments. chicken will be tender & very nice!
