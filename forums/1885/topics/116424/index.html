Fruitcake Recipe

  • icebaby0
    7 Feb 05, 15:09
    Ingredients:
    250g Unsalted butter
    190g Dark Brown Sugar
    5 Eggs
    30g Ground Almond
    190g Plain Flour
    1/2 tsp Baking Powder
    1/2 tsp Cinnamon Powder
    300-400g Mixed Fruit (depending on taste)
    2 tbsp Rum Flavour
    50g Pitted Prune (cubed)
    50g Dried Apricot (cubed)
    1 tbsp Treacle
    1/2 tsp Nutmeg Powder

    Method:
    1. Mix butter, sugar and treacle until creamy.
    2. Slowly add in the egg (one at a time) and rum flavour
    3. Mix the flour, baking powder, cinnamon powder and
    nutmeg powder and mix well. Then add in the mixed
    fruit, pitted prune and dried apricot.
    4. Pour in the mould and bake ata 170degrees for 1 hour.

    If you wish, you could soak the mixed fruit in rum for a
    few days to add flavour to your cake. I also plan to add
    some walnuts the next round.
