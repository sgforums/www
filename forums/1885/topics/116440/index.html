    Give Me S'More Brownies

    Ingredients:
    4 whole (5 x 2 1/2 inch) graham crackers
    2 milk chocolate candy bars (1.55 ounces each)
    1 package brownie mix (19.8 - 21 ounces) plus ingredients to make brownies
    1 cup miniature marshmellows

    1. Preheat oven to 350F (190C). Spray bottom of 9x9 pan with vegetable oil. Prepare brownie mix according to package instructions. Pour brownie mixture into 9x9 pan. Spread to corners and edges. Bake 25 mins.
    2. Coarsely chop graham crackers and candy bars. Remove brownie to cooling rack; immediately top brownie with marshmellows, chocolate pieces and graham crackers, pressing lightly. Return to oven; bake 5-7 mins or until chocolate begins to melt.
    VOILA!
