Stir Fried Prawn With XO Sauce

  • Bangulzai
    28 Mar 09, 00:47

    Ingredients: -

    • 6 medium prawns (shelled)
    • 1 celery (sectioned)
    • 50gm snow peas
    • 1/2 big onion (cut wedges)
    • 1/2 red capsicum (cut wedges)

    Seasoning: -

    • 2 tsp XO sauce
    • 1 tsp oyster sauce
    • 1/2 tsp chicken stock granules
    • 1/2 tsp sugar
    • 1/2 tbsp water
    • 1/2 tsp cornflour (mix with water)

    Method: -

    1. Heat up wok with oil until warm, add in prawns and leave until cooked, dish up. Turn to high heat, heat up oil, add in celery and snow peas, blanch in for a while, dish up. Heat up another wok with water until boiled, blanch prawn, celery and snow pea forÂ a while too, dish up.

    2. Heat up wok again with little oil, saute onion wedges and capsicum until fragrant. Then add in remaining ingredients, stir well and pour in the seasoning, keep stirring when pouring cornflour solution, stir until gravy is thickened.
