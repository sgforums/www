Kai Lan with Beef Strips

  • icebaby0
    8 Feb 05, 10:30
    Ingredients:
    2 tsp of ginger strips(sliced first, then cut into long thin strips)
    1 tbsp of cooking oil
    5 stalks of Kai Lan vegetables, separate stalks from leaves
    200 g of beef(striplion or chuck tender) cut into strips
    1 tbsp sesame oil
    1 tbsp cooking wine(or ang moh brandy--also can)
    1 tsp corn starch
    1 tsp Worcestire sauce or HP Sauce
    1 tsp sugar

    To marinate:
    Add beef, 1 tsp ginger strips, sesame oil, cooking wine, corn starch, Worcestire sauce and mix in a bowl. Leave inside the fridge for half an hour.

    cooking method:
    1. Boil a pot of hot water to cook the kai lan stalks for 5 minutes.
    2. Sieve and discard water.
    3. in a frying pan or wok, heat cooking oil.
    4. add the rest of the 1 tsp ginger strips, stir fry for 1 min.
    5. add marinated beef.
    6. stir fry for 2 mins, add 2 tbsp of water to it.
    7. then add kai lan leaves and cooked/boiled stalks. Stir fry for 2 mins.
    8. add the sugar, stir fry just to mix it.
    9. turn off heat. serve on a plate with toasted onions sprinkled on top.
