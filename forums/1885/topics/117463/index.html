White chocolate cookies

  • icebaby0
    13 Feb 05, 16:42
    130gm softened butter
    1/2 cup sugar
    3tbsp sweetened condensed milk
    1 1/2 cups plain flour
    1tsp baking powder
    1 cup mini white chocolate melts

    Preheat oven to 180C
    Cream butter, sugar and condensed milk till white and fluffy.
    Add the sifted flour and baking powder, and mix in chocolate melts.
    Roll tsp of mixture into balls and placed on a greased paper-lined baking sheet.
    Press down with a fork and bake in an oven for 18-20 minutes or until the cookies are cooked through.
    Makes 30.
