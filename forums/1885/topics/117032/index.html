Grill Pork Chop (Can use for BBQ too)

  • icebaby0
    11 Feb 05, 09:17
    Ingredients:
    2 pork chops with bone

    Marinate pork with (for few hours/overnight):
    1 tbsp mince ginger (squeeze the juice to the pork first n put in all the ginger)
    1 tbsp mince garlic
    1 tsp worcestershere (sp??) sauce
    1 tsp soy sauce
    1 tsp rice wine
    1/2 tsp sugar
    salt to taste
    black pepper

    Grill the above on a preheated oven for 8 minutes each side.

    Food is served!! Yum yum..... DH and I like it!!

    P/s if u like the colour to be a little darker, perhaps u could add in dark soy sauce
