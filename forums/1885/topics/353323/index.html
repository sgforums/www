Pan Fried Prawn In Gravy

  • Bangulzai
    25 Mar 09, 02:26

    Ingredients: -

    • 6 fresh big prawns
    • 1/2 big onion (chopped)
    • 1 pip garlic (chopped)
    • Some young ginger (chopped)
    • 1 red chilli (chopped)

    Seasoning: -

    • 2 tsp oyster sauce
    • 1 tbsp light soya sauce
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • Pinch of chicken stock granules
    • Pinch of pepper
    • Adequate dark soya sauce
    • 150ml water

    Method: -

    1. Clean prawn, dust with cornflour. Heat up wok with oil until boils, deep fry prawns until golden. Dish and drain, for later use.

    2. Heat up wok, add in little oil, saute rest of the ingredients until fragrant. Return deep fried prawns to wok and pour in all seasoning, stir frisky until gravy is thicken. Serve.
