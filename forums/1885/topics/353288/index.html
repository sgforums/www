Stir Fried Celery With Squids

  • Bangulzai
    24 Mar 09, 23:08

    Ingredients: -

    • 200gm celery (sectioned)
    • 1 squid
    • Adequate carrot (sliced)
    • 2 pips garlice (sliced)

    Seasoning: -

    • 1 tsp oyster sauce
    • 1/2 tsp chicken stock granules
    • Pinch of pepper
    • Pinch of sugar
    • 100ml water

    Â 

    • 1/2 tsp cornflour (mix with water)

    Method: -

    1. Clean squid and cut into slices. Heat up oil and soak celery and squid slices in oil, blanch for a while, for later use

    2. Heat up wok, add in little oil, saute garlic slices, carrot slices with oyster sauce until fragrant. Add in celery and squid, stir frisky for a while, pour in water and other seasoning, keep stirring while pouring cornflour solution. Stir fry until gravy is thicken
