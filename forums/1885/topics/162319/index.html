    FYI guys and gals!

    Dear All,

    SBST is celebrating Christmas this year with the "Christmas Double Promotion" and would like you to help spread the cheer! Please tell your friends and family that from 12 Nov 05 to 2 Jan 06, passengers taking all SBST bus and rail services will earn Transit Dollars or Transit Tokens to stand a chance to win $10,000 cash and other attractive prizes in our "Christmas Double Promotion".

    As the name implies, there are not 1 but 2 exciting events:

    "12 Days Before Christmas Online Auction"
    Use the Transit Dollars you earned from riding with SBST to bid for exciting in our 12 Days Before Christmas Online Promotion! Every day from 13 December 2005 to 24 December 2005, you can take part in our Online Auction and use your Transit Dollars to bid for attractive prizes!

    Prizes
    Total of 37 items for auction including 1 20-inch LCD TV, MP3 players, DVD and CD players.

    "Christmas Lucky Draw"
    Get your Transit Dollars or Transit Tokens by riding on SBS Transit buses and trains from 12 November 2005 to 2 January 2005 and stand to win attractive prizes in our lucky draw at a grand prize of $10,000 cash.

    Prizes
    Total of 18 prizes with top prize of $10,000, 20-inch LCD TV and Digital Cameras.

    How to Earn Transit Dollars and Transit Tokens
    Transit Dollars are automatically earned when fares are deducted from your ez-link card for rides on SBS Transit buses, NEL or the LRT (eg $0.39 Transit Dollars is earned for a deduction of $0.39). Users of concession e-stamps will not be issued Transit Dollars but are issued 1 Transit Token for each complete trip instead. You can even better your chances and earn double Transit Dollars or Transit Tokens when you ride on one of 83 selected services.

    Please refer to the website for more information:

    http://www.sbstransit.com.sg/whatsnew_promo-1.asp

    While ComfortDelGro and subsidiary company staff cannot participate in this promotion, please encourage all your friends and family members to ride with us this festive season. Or better still, why not forward this email together with your Christmas greetings to spread the cheer!!!
