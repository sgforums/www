Crispy prawn with wanton noodles

  • icebaby0
    16 Feb 05, 13:31
    Ingredients:

    Wantan wrappers, thinly shredded
    medium size prawns, leave tails intact
    egg, lightly beaten
    cornflour
    salt and pepper to taste

    Method:
    1. Marinate cleaned prawns with salt and pepper for awhile.
    2. Just b4 frying, coat each prawn with cornflour, lightly dip in egg, then loosely sprinkle with wanton shreds.
    3. Fry prawns in heated oil in batches and cook until golden brown.
    4. Serve when it's still hot, YUMMY & CRISPY!

    Note: The fried prawns turn out to be much bigger! Good to serve visitors...
