    Chicken Curry

    Grind into a paste.
    1 tblsp ginger
    1 tblsp garlic
    1 red onion
    Baba's meat curry powder
    1 tblsp chili paste (if u want it hot :fire )
    Marinade the above paste with the chicken (cut into 10-12 pcs) & set aside.

    Fry 1 big onion slice thinly, 1 inch stick of cinnamon,3 cardamoms,2 cloves & 1 anistar.
    Add in the chicken and fry for 2-3 mins, add in 2 halved chilies, 2 tomatoes (quarted),2 potatoes (quarted), fresh/dry coriander & mint leaves.
    Add in water, 2 tblsp of plain yogurt, 1 tblsp of tomato paste & salt to taste.
    Cook till the chicken & potatoes are done.

    You can serve it with plain rice/paratha/french loaves.
