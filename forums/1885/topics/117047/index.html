Yogurt Curry Chicken

  • icebaby0
    11 Feb 05, 09:56
    Ingredients to be blended
    an inch of ginger
    4 cloves of garlic
    1 large onion
    1 1/2 tomatoes (medium)
    1/2 teaspoon of black pepper seeds
    1 teaspoon of cumin seeds
    1 teaspoon of coriander powder
    1 tablespoon of chilli powder
    2 tablespoon of curry powder
    3 tablespoon of yoghurt
    salt
    water

    Ingredients to be fried
    1 whole chicken ( cut to about 8 - 10 pieces depending on size)
    3 cardamons (pelaga)
    cloves
    cinnamon sticks
    star anise

    Method:
    Fry the chicken, cardamons,cloves,cinnamon sticks and star anise together till the chicken is half cooked.
    Add all the blended ingredients.add water and salt.
    Mix and cook well till the oil rises to the surface.
    Finally , just add corriander leaves.

    Walla your yoghurt curry chicken is ready.
    Happy trying.
