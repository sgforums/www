    SESAME SEED OIL CHICKEN (This dish is said to promote blood circulation, provide nourishment & stimulate energy)

    150g Chicken Fillet
    4 slices Old Ginger
    3 Tbsp Sesame Seed Oil (If you find this too heaty, you can use less sesame seed oil & substitute the remaining portion with normal oil)
    1 tsp light soya sauce
    1 tsp sugar
    1 Tbsp Chinese Rice Wine

    Wash Chicken & cut into serving pieces.
    Heat Oil. Fry ginger slices until fragrant.
    Put in Chicken pieces & stir-fry for approx 5mins.
    Add soy sauce & sugar. Cover wok & cook for 5mins approx.
    Add Chinese Wine & stir-fry for another 1 min. Then serve.
