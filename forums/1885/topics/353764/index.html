    Ingredients :
    200g chicken (cut in shreds)
    100g crabstick (cut in shreds)
    2 stalks of spring onions (diced)
    4 to 5 eggs
    3 tablespoons of oil

    Seasoning A (Chicken Marinade)
    1 tablespoon of light soya sauce
    1/2 teaspoon of sesame oil
    Dash of pepper
    Dash of corn flour

    Seasoning B
    1 teaspoon of chicken stock powder
    1/2 teaspoon of salt
    1/2 teaspoon of sugar
    Dash of pepper
    80ml of stock

     

    Steps :
    1.Marinate shredded chicken into Seasoning A for 20 minutes.
    2.Lightly fry chicken with 1 tablespoon oil and set aside.
    3.Beat eggs, add shredded chicken, crabstick, spring onions and Seasoning B, then mix evenly.
    4.Pre-heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a wok, place egg mixture in a wok and fry under low-medium heat.
    5.Remove from heat when cooked, sprinkle with shallots and serve.
    Tips :
    •Do not overheat the wok. For optimal flavour and texture, fry eggs under a slow fire.
    •Substitute crabstick with crab meat for a more sumptuous dish.
