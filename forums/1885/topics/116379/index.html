    Red Bean-paste Ricecakes -
    Ang Gu Kueh

    Ingredients
    (A) 50g Rice Flour, 180ml water

    (B) 300 g Glutinous Rice Flour
    230g ml Water
    4 tablespoons oil
    60g Castor sugar
    Â½ teaspoon red colouring

    Ingredients for the filling :
    300g Split Beans
    300g Castor Sugar

    Method
    Wash beans and soak for 2 hours.
    Drain and cook with about 3-4 bowls of water until soft.
    Stir over pot until mixture is quite dry and add in sugar, continue to stir until mixture is thick.

    Method for paste
    Mix (A) and (B) together and knead well.
    Wrap with filling and steam for about 7 minutes.
    Remove and oil 'Ang Ku Kueh'.
