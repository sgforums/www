    Fried Carrot cake

    Ingredients:

    (A)- Steam Carrot cake
    Rice flour 200 gm
    Shredded radish 400 gm
    Dried shrimp 20 gm (optional)
    Oil 2 tblspoon
    ShaoXin Jiu 1 capful
    Sugar 15 gm
    Water 350 gm
    Pork lard 20 gm (I omit this)
    Salt a pinch
    Pepper a pinch

    (B) - Fried Carror Cake
    (A)- steam Carrot cake
    Oil - 2 tablespoon
    Minced garlic - 2 tablespoon
    Prawn 200 gm
    Cai po 20 gm
    Some garnish (I used parsley)
    Large eggs 2-4
    Soya sauce/sweet sauce/fish sauce
    Pepper
    ---------------------------------------------------

    For (A)

    1. Soak rice flour with water(not the water in the ingredient) for 10-20 mins. Use a cloth bag to contain the flour, squeeze the wet rice flour. It should be like a paste. (200 gm flour becomes 300 paste)

    2. Soak the dried shrimp. Fry shredded radish and shrimp with oil and Shaoxin jiu.

    3. Add 2000 gm of water to the mixture and boil it until the radish is cooked.

    4. Add the rice paste to the balance (150 gm) of water. Mixed well. Add the diluted paste to the above mixture. Continue stirring. Mix well. Add all other ingredients in (A). Mix well.

    5. Pour into a 9" plate/bowl(must be cleaned and greased). Use strong heat and steam for 1 hour until its fully cooked.

    Things to note when preparing (A):

    1. When boiling radish, the radish must turn alil translucent. Before steaming, the mixture must be half cooked like a paste. The paste must be place evenly in the steamer. Water amt may be adjusted for different quality of rice.

    2. Heat/fire must be high when steaming. Steam around 1 hour to fully cook it.

    Once you cooked (A), proceed to (B)

    For (B)

    1. Add oil to frying pan, add the steam carrot cake,fry and scoop one side of the pan. Add cai po, and prawn. Fry till the prawn turn red.

    2. Add 2 scrambled raw eggs into the pan, scoop the carrot cake and cut it into smaller pieces. Sauteed it further until the egg and carrot cake become crispy. Add fish sauce, pepper.

    3. Scoop up and add garnish.
