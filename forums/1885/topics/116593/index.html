Baked salmon fillet

  • icebaby0
    8 Feb 05, 10:42
    250g salmon fillet

    Marinate with the following for 1/2 hr:
    1/2 tbsp soy sauce
    1 tbsp oyster sauce
    1 tbsp sesame oil
    1/2 tbsp Lea Perin sauce (spelling??)
    little bit of saffron powder (kunyit powder)
    little bit of mince garlic, fresh ginger
    little bit of sugar
    little bit of ground black pepper

    Wrap marinated salmon fillet, grill in a preheated oven at 180 degrees for 10 mins, open the aluminium foil (just the top) and continue to grill for another 3 mins until the salmon is cooked.
