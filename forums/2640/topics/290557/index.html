Andy Warhol Exhibition: 17, 18 November 2007

  • Kuali Baba

    A Is For Andy will be held at 72-13 Mohamed Sultan Road from 13 - 18 November 2007, but it's open to the public only on the last 2 days from 10am - 10pm.

    It's featured in Thursday's edition of ST Life! (page 4). 216 of Andrew Warhola's works will be exhibited. It's part of the festivities commemorating the 20th anniversary of his death this year.

    Is anyone making plans?
  • Kuali Baba
    17 Nov 07, 23:08
    I went there this evening, and yup, it's pretty crowded for an art exhibition. Designers, students and socialite characters were present.

    They have a demonstration of silkscreen printing, and the resulting prints (of Warhol's protrait) are sold at $10 each.

    The works on show include Venus, Mao, Marilyne Monroe, Beethoven, Grapes, Money, Mickey Mouse, and several more. It's quite interesting to note the explorations with colour and composition.

    The guided tours tomorrow will be at 12 noon, 2, 4, and 6 in the afternoon/evening.

    Another thing: no photography of the exhibits is allowed.
