    For those who are interested and/or live near the venue:

    "The Way of Asian Design" is the first ever show at ADM's gallery. This exhibition showcases the work of four stars of Asian Design:

    Kohei Siugura (Japan)
    Ahn Sang-Soo (Korea)
    Lu Jing-ren (China)
    Kirti Trivedi (India)

    The exhibition will open daily from 29 Nov to 8 Dec, 9:30am to 7 pm, at Nanyang Technological University's School of Art, Design & Media (ADM)

    You are also invited to attend the 1-day Panel and Seminar on Friday 30 November, from 9:30 to 5:30, at the ADM Auditorium. (Free Admission, Lunchbox provided)

    Morning Session 9:30 - 13:00
    Thematic Presentations by the 4 exhibitors

    Afternoon Session 14:30 - 17:30
    Design Education for the Future
    Panel Discussion Moderated by Prof. Nanci Takeyama
    Participants: Prof. Kohei Siugura, Prof. Ahn Sang-Soo, Prof. Lu Jing-ren, Prof. Kirti Trivedi
