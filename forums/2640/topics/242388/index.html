    Layers are all gone whether you save in JPEG, GIF or PNG.

    Layers are inner clockworks of Photoshop, and is only saved in PSDs. Though, PNG supports alpha transparency, thus it has -one- editable layer when you open it in Photoshop.

    Also, yes, colours are compressed in JPEG. But as compared to GIF, JPEG wins it in richness of colours.
