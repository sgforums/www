    P1

    I don't think that amount is enough for the shoes + ball. SST6s cost about $240. Oh, you may want to have a look at the new ABS shoes from Japan. There are VERY good things said about them in the states. You may read about them at forums in PBA.com or ballreviews.com - just search for the keywords.

    About your thumb... For your thumb to expand so much in 5 games, the issue may lie in your release. Of course this could simply be down to many other factors, such as insufficient warm-up resulting in a non-representative thumb size before play, et cetera. But if you want to resize your plug, do it judiciously. You could always D.I.Y. when you need to anytime on your own with sandpaper if it's not wide enough.
