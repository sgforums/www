    22 Nov 02, 00:35
    More details:

    Cost per team entry is $300, so each person will have to fork out $27 plus. I will settle the deposit of $120. We have up to week 5 to pay up, so.... I will hound you by then, don't worry.

    Each league session will cost each bowler $15, or $60 per team.

    The league is made up of 3 rounds of 11 sessions each. So you can tell there are 12 teams taking part.

    Interesting to note: Points won/lost are reset back to 0 after the first round. But team total scratch pinfalls, handicaps etc will be carried forward.

    League runs on a 20 point system, exactly the same as Classic. The only difference is the score is upgraded to 150 instead of 160 to calculate handicap. Otherwise everything else is similar.

    Clothing wise, standard but with 1 difference. Must wear long pants, jeans, etc...(No shorts, bermudas, etc as per normal) but we are allowed to wear round neck shirts/t-shirts. Collars are not necessary. But a minimum of short sleeves! No sleeveless wear, please. (Yes Brendo I'm talking to you)

    Prize money is quite attractive, please take this league seriously and give nothing short of your best. Esp spare making.

    Overall 1st position to 12th position gets, 2000, 1500, 1000, 700, 600, 500, 400, 300, 200, 150, 100, 100 respectively.

    Leaders after Rd 1 get $500, Runner up gets $400.
    Leaders after Rd 2 get $300, Runner up gets $200,
    Leaders after Rd 3 get $150, Runner up gets $100.

    The reason for this prize distribution is that there are no repeat winners. If you won 1st place in Rd 1, you don't get anything even if you win Rd 2 and 3... etc.

    Any other details you fellas need?
