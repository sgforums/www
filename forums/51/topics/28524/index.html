  • Mage^_^
    4 Nov 02, 00:42
    Well, the Tomahawk is a excellent ball for spinners...
    Other balls include, Crush/r, Ultra C, Ninja Fury, Ninja Master.

    Of cos, if u are talking about a good ball to use as a 1st ball for training, the white dot is the way to go.

    Packages are good, then again it depends on where u buy it. Dun go AMK Pro-shop cos they know nuts and keep trying to push their old stock to u, drilling sucks too.

    Do get someone experienced with the style and has a good average to teach u cos this style is very prone to injuries, therefore, u need to have someone to guide u.

    When u begin, my advise is not to try and achieve lots of spin but to train ur footsteps timing and accuracy, the basis of all gd bowlers is to have a stable and comfortable timing then building the rest of ur game will be easier.

    Take care and have fun!!!
