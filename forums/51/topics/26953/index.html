    Up till about 5 months ago, i was using a mongoose. Couldn't use it after the finger got injured. In fact, i suspect it was a contributing factor to the injury. But i was trying some weird releases during that time, so i had it coming.

    Anyway, i liked the mongoose because it wasn't as complicated as a mech, yet it provided enough assistance to the wrist. Simple gear. It gave me some form of consistency coz i had a weak wrist.

    But at the back of my mind, i always wondered what it would be like to be able to bowl regularly without a guard, to not be a slave to it.

    So 5 months later, i'm bowling without a guard, with the lightest ball i have. I can only hope that one day, i'll be strong enough to play with 15lb stuff again. Not that it's a bad thing really...... the injury forced me to work on other areas, like footwork and timing, which needed a massive overhaul. Blessing? Maybe.
