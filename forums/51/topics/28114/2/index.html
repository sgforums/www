  • prataman
    7 Nov 02, 12:30
    Point to note.

    Just like to mention that the league is actually an upgraded handicap league.

    So for any of you guys who score below 160 for a game, your handicap is calculated as if your game score was 160, not whatever you bowled.

    So for example, if you hit a 140 140 230 (170 avg), normally you will have a 15 handicap. But for this league, your 2 140s will be upgraded to 160 for the purposes of calculating handicap only, therefore (200 - ((160+160+230)/3))/2 = 9. Rather than the 15 you would normally be getting.

    Morale of the story. Try our best each game, don't get below 160. This league doesn't allow you to pull handicap. Even if your opponent is scoring 250, try to score as much as you can.[/b]
