    Details, details.

    Date: 19th January 2003 (Sunday)

    Venue: Starbowl, Kim Seng Plaza (opp. Great World City)

    Time: Report 0830, Singles Rolloff 0900, Trios Rolloff 1400

    Price: $25 per bowler for 8 games     (4 singles, 4 trios) Each institution will charge its bowlers with its own rates.

    Singles Event Format
    Ladies and gents, you'll be playing against your own kind. I mean, you'll be competing with members of your own sex. So no handicap for ladies in this case. Please refer to equipment to determine which category you fall under.

    Trios Event Format
    Any and all permutations with regards to gender are allowed. Ladies get a handicap of 10 pinfalls, which adds up to 40 extra pinfalls over 4 games.

    On to the incentive scheme.

    Shiny plastic trophies will be purchased on behalf of the following bowlers:

    Singles Event

    Top 5 Ladies
    Top 5 Men

    Trios Event

    Top 5 Teams

    Overall

    Ladies High Game
    Men High Game

    And a rather nice non-plastic object as a token of appreciation to NUS.

    Oh, there's some cash to help tide over the economic slump, courtesy of SIM.

    Singles

    Ladies and Men

    1st: $30
    2nd: $25
    3rd: $20
    4th: $15
    5th: $10

    Trios

    1st: $60
    2nd: $50
    3rd: $40

    Recipients are encouraged to use the money in a sensible fashion, such as buying stationery or donating a part of it to the SIM Bowling Club Treasury. Thank you.
