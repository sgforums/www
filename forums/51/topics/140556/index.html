    Hm.. I personally feel that spinners these days are very different from the legendary spinners of the past. When I see spinners at our local lanes.. the most common sight I notice are balls at high speed, hitting the head pin, and wham! everything flies.. very much like a hook bowler.
    Whereas for the older spinners, esp from taiwan.. their ball will hit the head pin, and carry down by the side.. mixing up all the pins in the process.
    Looking at the balls hook bowlers carry nowadays, they have a huge advantage over spinners in competitions if the spinners play like those that I see.. As much as we say spinners are not affected by lane condititions, we're actually still affected by the oiling, and the weight of pins.
    So my view is that spinners are starting to lose out becos of their pin reaction.. anyway, thats just my opinion
