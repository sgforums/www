    cos i figured i might use it on the early parts of my game.
    The PVC tape, which has a glossy look, i use it in the early stages when my thumb shrink a lot. I paste in the front og the thumbhole towards the fingers. As it has a waterproof surface, hence it gives a lot of grip. After my thumb has swell, then change to my 3M tape.

    The 3M tape has a matte surface, so it is better to put behind the thumbhole, to fill up the excess space bet yr thumb and the thumbhole. Never put in the front as it will cause u to slip out too much. In fact, the texture is similiar to the plastic black tape which u see in pro-shop

    The disadv of those black tapes, as well as the white rough tapes seen in pro shop is that it left a residue behind. So, what i normally do is i put the pvc tape in first and followed by the white tape, and this help me to peel off the tape faster. The white rough tape is used when i want a grip that is between the PVC and the smooth one of 3M.

    These days, i hardly put on tapes liao. All thnks to mr James Shi who drill my thumbhole such that it fits snugly

    Guide to Proper insertion of tape.

    Firstly, dry the area in the hole.
    second, clean it the best you can.
    third, insert the tape using scissors or the proper insertion tool.

    not recommended for other forms of insertion. may cause pain and distress.
