    I agree about the importance of team spirit and bonding in the bowling squad.

    Throughout the few leagues that I have been a part of over the last few years, having a strong team spirit is great when the team is doing well, but it is even more important when the team is NOT doing well!

    Commitment and Karma are important ingredients for a team. Nothing is more frustrating for a team captain than having bowlers that is always unavailable, or doesn't show up at all whenever he/she is not playing. As for Karma, well, it's a simple thing... you have to bowl with people you enjoy being around... not much point in having a team mate that you hate the guts of.

    I sincerely hope that Team FOS would stay together as a unit for many leagues to come!
