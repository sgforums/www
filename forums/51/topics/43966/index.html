    Lane #1 balls don't all outhook the rest, but, it does hit harder than other balls because of its cores. The Super Carbide and the Super Carbide Bomb(being particle balls) are the ones that hook alot. But the other balls are all designed to go longer and snap harder, making it controllable and stronger at the backend, especially their new Cherry Bomb which goes really long, then snaps really hard.

    By the way,since you mentioned it, Lane #1 designs their balls (designs their coverstocks, cores,etc., then decides which to use with which) but their balls are made by Brunswick. Thus meaning to say that the Carbide Particle and Resin Formulas,the Powerkoil18 Reactive Urethane formula and their Xtra-Strength Rhino Pro Reactive Coverstocks belong to Lane #1.
