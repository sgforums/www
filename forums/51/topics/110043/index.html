    Alrighty folks, some details on the Thursday Katong league.

    There are 10 teams participating in this competition, and this coming Thursday on the 6 January will be week 3 of the league. Team FOS was originally not participating in this league, but we jumped in to plug a gap left by a team who pulled out after 1 week.

    Ok, the nitty gritty:

    It's a 3-leg league of 9 sessions per leg (27 weeks in total)
    Average is based on a ((180 - average)/2). Max handicap for males is 25.

    ---------
    Edit:

    Each team can win up to 30 points each week. That's right. 30.

    12 pts -> individual bowler individual game score (w/hdcp) vs direct opponent
    3 pts -> individual bowler overall score (w/hdcp) vs direct opponent
    4 pts -> team individual game score (w/hdcp)
    1 pt -> team overall score (w/hdcp)

    Interesting twist is the round robin points awarded. Each week, the team with the highest overall pinfalls (w/hdcp) gets 10 extra pts, the 2nd highest get 9 extra pts ..... until the lowest pinfalls get 1 miserly extra pt.

    At least this way ... even if any team gets walloped 0-20 .... the minimum they can still obtain is 1 pt! Hee hee hee...

    However, this means that NO HANDICAP TARIK-ING is allowed please. Every pinfall would count as it can mean another round robin point. Then again, I don't think we actually tarik handicap in the first place......

    End edit
