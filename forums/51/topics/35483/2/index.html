    Had a not too bad day today. As compared to the last mth classic tourny, well, my position improve by 2 places and my avg also improve by another 2 pins. So basically was satisfied despite the small improvemments.

    Today amost everything new for me. "new" 2nd hand bolwing balls(had them drilled on fri only, so only today get to try out); tried a new concept bwling shoes which my frd got for me in the States, and of course using my favourite cleaner which now cannot find in singapore (neo tac renew it). So was able to get a 177avg, i more than happy alrdy.

    Frankly, to get into 5th place came more as a suprise,esp when the counter announced after the end of 6 games, i was hovering at 6th. 2 games of 180+ and 190+ and 4 games of 160+ and i still have a chance to get into top. Really a susprise. I think among the top few, i may be the only bowler who played all 8 games without any games hitting beyond 200

    I always like to reflect the goods and bads after a tourny. Perhaps the good thing is i managed to improve on my avg. 2nd good will be that i have kept the difference bet my high and low games to less than 30pinfalls. 3rd good will be still able to bowl some decent games after all.

    The bad? much more. my sparing really bad. 4 games of 160++ shows how much spares i have missed and the other 4 games of 180++ also shows missed spares, which would have otherwise lead to some 200 games. Lesson leant.

    2nd bad...unable to adjust as quickly as i could. Till only the end then i decide not to roll the ball and loft the ball and playing "area" bowling. Should have done that by the 3rd game when my orange ball starts to over-hook at the 11th board. Experience learnt

    3rd bad, unable to read the breaking down of the oil as fast as i can. only after 5th game then i realise i can't release the ball on the dots cos there wasn't any oil to allow my ball to skid.

    having learn all these, hopefully next time got chance to do better.
