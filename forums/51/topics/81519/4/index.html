    regarding this issue....its been around for years..... there are a few methods to remove oil....even oil u dun see

    2. wrap ur ball with newspaper put inside a box....and leave it under the hot sun for a few hrs.... this method's result varies.... because u don't know how strong is the sunlight and how long u can put under the heat to force the oil out.

    3. worst of all 3 methods... put it in ur car boot....leave ur car under hot sun... i think 1-2hr later u should see the oil on the surface....

    but all 3 methods are suggested for balls that are 3yrs ago....i heard lately that new balls cannot go thru this kind of treatment....will die even faster....
