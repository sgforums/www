  • harmzuay
    11 Sep 02, 01:10
    awhitea

    The majority of bowlers and coaches who have written about this say that revolutions are generated by the acceleration of the wrist through the ball during release. The more acceleration one has, the more revolutions one imparts to the ball.

    This means that one has to have enough strength to do this, and note that this acceleration of the wrist is only applicable to the time from when the thumb exits and the fingers exit. Period.

    That's the formula for generating revolutions.
