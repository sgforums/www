    In this league there is no point pulling your score below 160 because you simply don't get extra handicap by doing so.

    You can only "pull" when your score will not have any effect on
    1) your team winning the particular game
    2) your team winning the overall series
    3) yourself winning your matchup!

    So basically the only possible time you can purposely "relax" your score is in the last few frames of your 3rd game.
