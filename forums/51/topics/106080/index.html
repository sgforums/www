    RULES AND REGULATIONS

    Date: Saturday, 11th December 2004
    Venue: Victor's Superbowl (7 Marina Grove Singapore 019597)
    Check-in: 0930 hrs
    Roll-off: 1000 hrs

    Entry Information:

    Closing Date for entries 9th December 2004
    Entry is on a first come first serve basis, and institutions are advised to register early as vacancies are limited. The organizers reserve the right to limit the number of teams, or to reject applications if the quota is exceeded.
    Confirmation will be issued to successfully registered teams by 9th December 2004.

    1.0 Objectives:

    1.1 To foster and promote goodwill and sportsmanship amongst the bowlers in the bowling fraternity.
    1.2 To promote and improve the standard of bowling among Tertiary Institutions in Singapore.

    2.0 Rules:

    2.1 STBC and FIQ playing rules and regulations shall apply throughout this tournament.

    3.0 Eligibility:

    3.1 This tournament is open to all students of SIM (UOL/RMIT)
    3.2 This tournament is by invitation only; all students of the invited institutions may participate.
    3.3 Female bowlers will receive a handicap of 10 pins per game throughout the tournament
    3.4 A maximum of 5 teams from each institution may apply.

    4.0 Entry Fees:

    4.1 S$75.00 per team of three (S$25 per bowler).
    4.2 Fees may be paid in cash on the day of competition.

    5.0 Attire:

    5.1 Bowlers must be appropriately and modestly attired.
    5.2 Men Â– Polo T-shirt and slacks
    5.3 Ladies Â– Polo T-shirt and skirt or Bermudas.

    6.0 Format:

    6.1 This is a team event and is open to all SIM bowlers and invited teams. Bowlers will form teams of 3 (trios) and bowl 5 games each. Female bowlers will be given a handicap of 10 pins per game while all male bowlers will receive no handicap. The total pinfall after 5 games for every bowler in the team will count towards the total team pinfall. There will be no changing of lanes throughout the whole event.

    7.0 Tie-breaker:

    7.1 Should there be a tie in total scratch scores, the bowler with the least difference between his/her highest and lowest games shall be declared higher ranking.

    8.0 Bowling Balls:

    8.1 If at any time a player is found to have used an illegal ball, he/she will be disqualified from the tournament and stripped of all his/her winnings.
    8.2 It is the bowlerÂ’s responsibility to verify that all his/her equipment conforms to FIQ regulations.

    9.0 Bowling on the Wrong Lane:

    9.1 When a bowler has bowled on the wrong lane and the error is discovered before another bowler has bowled, a dead ball shall be declared and the bowler will be required to re-bowl that frame on the correct lane.
    9.2 If the error is not discovered until after the opposing bowler has bowled, the scores shall count and the bowlers will be required to bowl their subsequent frames on the wrong lane.

    10.0 Protests:

    10.1 Any protests should be made immediately in writing, accompanied by a cash deposit of S$150, which will be returned only if the protest is deemed valid and successful by the Appeals Committee.
    10.2 Protests should be made as soon as possible; any issues raised after presentation of the awards will not be entertained.
    10.3 In all matters, the decision of the Appeals Committee and the Tournament Manager is final.

    11.0 Absent / Tardy Bowlers:

    11.1 Bowlers arriving late will still be permitted to join in the game; however, he/she will commence bowling from the frame which the other bowlers on the lane are at, forfeiting the previous frames.
    11.2 Teams with absent bowlers will be given a blind score of 100 pinfalls per game per bowler; pacers may or may not be assigned.

    12.0 Matters Not Provided For:

    12.1 Decisions on matters not provided for in these rules and regulations shall as far as possible be governed by STBC playing rules and regulations.
    12.2 All judgments will be made solely by the Tournament Manager.

    13.0 Prizes:

    13.1 Trophies will be awarded to the top 5 teams.
    13.2 High Game Series for top 3 bowlers (male and female).
    13.3 High game Award for male and female
    13.4 Cash Prize for top 3 bowlers
