    Dear SIM/UOL Bowling Club members,

    Greetings from the Management Development Institute of Singapore (MDIS)!

    MDIS 7th Bowling Championship is here again and this year, we are proud to extend the invitation to SIM/UOL bowlers to be part of our annual event. This championship aims to provide individuals the opportunity to network and foster team spirit.

    Attractive cash prizes of up to S$1200 await the winning top team! Among the prizes are the top 3 ranking of (1)Teams, (2)Ladies / Men and (3)Individual categories. Simply form a team comprising of 3 representatives and be treated to a day of fun and excitement! As a gesture of our appreciation for your support, SIM/UOL bowlers will enjoy a 10% discount when you submit more than 3 teams for this coming championship!

    If any of your SIM/UOL bowling club members are interested in participating, please do not hesitate to contact us at 64193 121 / 122 / 123 / 120 / 118 or email us at members@mdis.edu.sg for the application forms and the publicity posters.

    Preliminaries (Choose any date and venue)
    6th, 13th & 20th September 2003 (Saturdays)
    Time: 2.30pm to 5.30pm

    7th, 14th & 21st September 2003 (Sundays)
    Time: 10.30am to 1.30pm

    Venue: Choose either Cathay Bowl Orchard Cineleisure or Super Bowl Marina Square (First come first served basis)

    Finals
    5th October 2003 (Sunday)
    Time: 10.30am to 2.30pm
    Venue: Super Bowl Marina South

    Fees: S$18 per participant
    Clsoing Date: 23rd August 2003

    We look forward to having a team from SIM/UOL Bowling Club!

    Many thanks!
