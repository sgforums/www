    well, i didn't think the ball was pearlised inspite of its phasing because the Track Voodoo isn't classified as a pearl despite it's cover.
    The number of boards a ball covers after making the turn is not a function of drilling. It's more an equation which includes elements of the bowler's characteristics and ball cover vs. lane condition match-up which eventually determine how much energy is left after the turn. i.e. how many more boards the ball can still travel after changing directions. the drilling only changes this by dictating what shape (arc/snap/straight) the hook takes on - the number of boards covered is always down to the energy retained in the ball.
