    One doesn't have to be chunky or built like Arnold Schwarzzenegger to have the potential for power. Long limbs can also be a potent source of power.

    Long legs mean more leverage in the slide and long arms mean you get more velocity in the shot (remember Force x Distance in physics?). Broad shoulders and narrow hips are helpful too.

    Most of the taller people I know don't even require backswings that end up parallel to the ground at their highest point, but still generate immense torque on their releases.

    If you've got it, use it.
