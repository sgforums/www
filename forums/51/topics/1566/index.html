    Hi,I'm currently an undergraduate student of SIM-RMIT BBACC. I can say this course is worth the money that you invest, provided you study for the exam. I like the modular subjects as they are very useful in working life.

    The experience I have is there's hardwork since you have to do a lot of assignments and then a month break for exam preparations.

    The advice that every lecturer give in class is "Study now, not on the month break". This is quite true, no laughing matter

    For new students, make more friends as every semester, there's a group assignment.

    By the way, this is the only SIM forum that I can find. If anyone know of another SIM forum, please inform. Thanks!
