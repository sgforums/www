  • jae
    2 Oct 04, 00:22
    hm, actually different alley got different leagues, with differing scoring systems.
    got 10-pt system, 20-pt system, round robin system, with trios, or foursome format.

    using the planet bowl tue trios league as an example, (20-pt system, 4 games, trios)

    player vs player (1st bowler vs 1st bowler, 2nd vs 2nd and so on..)
    each bowler can get maximum 5 pts, (4 from games, 1 from overall)
    therefore 3 bowlers maximum 15pts

    Team game (team overall score for 1st game, 2nd game, and so on...)
    maximum 5 pts (4 from games, 1 from overall)

    therefore this is a 20 pt system league...something like that...

    then got handicap for bowlers, generally, the more handicap a bowler have, the 'lousier' he is... though some bowlers will 'cheat' on their handicap by lowering their scores when they play against weaker opponents so that they can 'ta-rek' their handicap...this is a common yet unprofessional practice, not allowed in the 'Code of Conduct' in league...
