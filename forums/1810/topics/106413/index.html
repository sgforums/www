    Loving someone is a lot about loving yourself. What you see in the other person is what you do not have. But with time, you will realize, he/she is so much like you and it becomes so difficult to breathe. you want you space. He wants his. You start thinking about the relationship, he wants his space still. When you finally realized, a little breather will do both good, it is good. But i guess loving someone is also about loving him for wanting to do certain things without you around.

    Currently in a relationship now, when things are smooth going, no one complains. When things get rough, misunderstandings occur, one blames the other for it. It is bad enough when 2 have a bad temper and are born in a family where everyone gives in to them. And come in a relationship, neither know what is compromisation.

    Oh well, its all about striking a balance isn't it?
