SMU Animal Day: Free carnival on 13 Mar

  • yangus16

    SMU's People for Animal Welfare is holding our annual Animal Day on Saturday, 13 March 2010! We want to celebrate the unconditionally loving pets who provide us with so much joy, and to spread the message of responsible pet ownership. For one day only, anyone can bring his/her pet(s) to SMU's Campus Green, and participate in a series of games and contests with them. But of course, this event is open to everyone, with or without a pet, who loves animals.

    Venue: SMU Campus Green (opposite the Singapore Arts Museum, and between the Li Ka Shing Library and School of Information Systems. See [url]http://www.smu.edu.sg/campus/map/[/url])
    Date: Saturday, 13 March 2010
    Time: 11am to 4.30pm

    Apart from the fun and games, there'll also be free goodie bags, and booths selling pet stuff:

    - Pets MoMo selling pets clothes
    - Pets Magazine selling subscription & Runway Pets 2010 tickets
    - Pawsitive Sensations doing pets massage
    - PawGlam! selling hand-made collars
    - Oven-baked Tradition selling pet food supplies
    - Free photo sponsored by Canon
    - Lopp's Online Pet Plaza selling pet supplies
    - among others!



    You can also take part in a photo contest to win prizes. Simply send a photo of you and your pet(s) (max. 800KB) to [email]paw@smu.edu.sg[/email] by Wed, 10 Mar, 2359hrs. Remember to include your name and contact number.

    Hope to see you there! I promise it'll be fun :D
