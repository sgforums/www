    Notice To All Dog Owners!

     

     

    Below is some important information for all dog owners attending the event:

     

    • All dogs must be leashed in a public place.

     

    • If your dog is of a large breed like a Rottweiler or Mastiff, it must also be muzzled in a public place. Owners are also advised to muzzle their dogs in a public place if their dogs are known to be ferocious.

     

    • If a dog bites a person, it can also become Police case.

     

    • Breeds of dogs required to be muzzled in a public place include:

     


    i) Pit Bull, which includes the American Pit Bull Terrier (which is also known as the American Pit Bull and Pit Bull Terrier), American Staffordshire Terrier, Staffordshire Bull Terrier, American Bulldog, and crosses between them and other breeds, Akita, Neapolitan Mastiff, Tosa, Dogo Argentino, Fila Brasileiro, Boerboel, and their crosses; and

    ii) Bull Terrier, Doberman Pinscher, Rottweiller, German Shepherd Dog with its related breeds such as the Belgian Shepherd Dog and the East European Shepherd Dog, Mastiffs including the Bull Mastiff, Perro De Presa Canario, Cane Corso and Dogue De Bordeaux, and their crosses.

     

    • The maximum fine for letting a dog stray is $5,000 for an offence. In the case of dogs not muzzled in a public place, the offender may be fined up to $10,000.
