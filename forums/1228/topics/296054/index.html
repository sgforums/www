Rude shopowners of a pet store

  • xidigou
    8 Dec 07, 22:47
    Would like to share an experience at PetDepot at Upper Serangoon Road near Kovan mrt station. It is right beside the Selegie soyabean stall. Don't mistake it for the store opposite the road which is beside the durian store, that one is completely nice.

    PetDepot has the worst service anyone can ever find, specifically from the lady owner, Daisy.

    The incident happened today when I was standing outside the store with my kid brother looking at the rabbit and hamsters on display. My brother, eager to take a closer look, stepped up and placed both his palms flat on the glass wall. A quarter minute later, the lady owner stormed out, opened the door and snapped "OEI hello this is not spiderman ok. Can you dun lidat?", rolled her eyes at my brother and went back in. I saw the male owner smiling to this lady named Daisy and said something to her.

    Before I went away, I glanced at her standing far back in the cashier of her store and she put her hand up and tilted her head upwards in such a gesture as if picking a fight.

    I admit my brother's actions were not polite. But hers were much worse, considering the fact that neither my brother nor I spoke a word to her or even glanced at her impolitely.

    Please do not patronize the store if you would not like an unpleasant shopping experience.

    P.S. I know her name is Daisy because it is stated on a piece of paper at her store.
