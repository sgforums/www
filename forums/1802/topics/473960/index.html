Please Don't Stop On Expressways To Change Tyre

  • JRII
    9 Sep 13, 13:12

    I have been busy this past 2 weeks but there is a story I want to share. I think 2 Saturdays ago, I was out with friends, and was driven along AYE-ECP on the Keppel bridge. This bridge you all know is 3 lanes straight road with a very small shoulder. Cars like to speed here because it's striaght. I saw 1 TC Wish driver parked on the left, changing his tyre. Be cause the shoulder is very narrow, the 3rd lane is very close to it.

    I was so worried seeing the driver doing that, I wanted to ask my friend to park behind him, but then we would also be in danger, so I called the police to assist him in at least providing some hazard warning.

    So please try not to change tyres on expressways, try somehow to go to a side lane or exit and do it. Expressways got so many blind drivers. This very bridge (at the end) was where the lambo fatal accident happened. It's a very dangerous place to stop.
