    If ur cab TAXI .... free ! No need to pay any $$$ .

    ON CALL also no need to pay .

    If u taking customer in HIRED.... $2 admission charge per head . Customers head , yours dun include .

    U can pay first , take from them later , but usually i asked customer pay .

    If u taking hotel's customers , they will have a hotel authorised pass .... to show to the entrance staffs . To get in no charge .

    Asked if they are tourists visiting sentosa , or hotel's guest .

    Once a customers visiting Sentosa Spa , just told the entrance staff : " Spa ."

    This caucasian actually got a free entrance .

    After 9am , the working crowd will come in ....... pace gets rapid at 10am , many calls , most go Orchard ,  town area.

    It can really go mad up to 12pm , then pace slow down , but quite consistent the taxi-takers . U alight one pax , another 100m , there'll be one new pax . Most short distance , seldom exceeding $12 trip after 12pm .

    Saturday beware , after 3pm , except in town , the rest of S'pore basically totally slow down .

    Then the whole market can be really dead by 6pm ..... to 8pm . Dinner i think .

    Then after 8pm , goes lively again . Cos peak hour over , they came out to catch a cab . All the way it's madness up to 6am . Non -stop .
