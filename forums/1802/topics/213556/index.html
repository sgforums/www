    Ssang Yong Rodius joins SMRT Taxis fleet

    1. SMRT Taxis has added 50 units of the Ssang Yong Rodius SV270 to its fleet of taxis. Branded SMRT SPACE, the Rodius is the largest MPV taxi in the market with its luxurious passenger and luggage capacity.
    2. The 2.7 litre automatic eight-seater can accommodate seven adult passengers, and is designed to appeal to families and tourists with its space, comfort and power. SMRT SPACE comes with special leather seats, LCD TV and DVD players, seat trays and cup holders as well as an in-built audio system for better driver passenger communication. For families with infants, child seats are available upon request.
