    For those who had , taken a JB taxi before , should have no complaints over Singapore taxis .

    I for one , took several JB cabs to go around the JB City , not a single Proton Saga Taxis really impressed me .

    First their aircon , anytime any cab , can never be cooling enough for our torpical standard .

    2nd , their vehicle , for whatever reason , a bit filthy to my standard . Internal or extrenal .

    3rd , their drivers speaks little English .

    4th , their cabs really smell awful .

    5th , their seats really stiff and cushion really old .

    6th and lastly , their vehicle suspension really bad for their already bad road condition .

    Last and foremost , for those people in S'pore who are so demanding over service standard in S'pore , try taking a JB taxi ..... then u come back with a conclusion .

    And whatever reason , they don't seemed to use their taxi-meter when i am on-board !!!

    All flat fare , anytime , any taxi !!
