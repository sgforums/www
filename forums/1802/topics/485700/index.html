    AsiaOne | Friday, Jul 11, 2014

     

    SINGAPORE - A passenger has filed a complaint against a Premier taxi driver for watching a pornographic film while driving, reported Lianhe Wanbao.

    Chen Zhi Yong, 30, told the Chinese evening daily that the film played for nearly 10 minutes during the journey yesterday from Amoy Street Food Centre to Empress Place Market and Food Centre, which took about 25 minutes.

    Mr Chen told Lianhe Wanbao that he was shocked to see a naked woman on the screen of an iPad that was mounted next to the steering wheel.

    He added that the sounds coming from the film made him feel very uncomfortable.

    He felt that such behaviour was dangerous not only to himself, but also to the driver and other road users, Lianhe Wanbao reported.

    The paper quoted a spokesperson from Premier as saying that the alleged incident is being investigated, and that action will be taken if it is found to be true.
