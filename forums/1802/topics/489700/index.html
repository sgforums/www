    For me, full-day bus lane and explicit no U-turn areas I would not violate the regulations.

    The only rule I violate is the no stopping at within 9 metres of bus-stop rule.  The way I see it is customer wait at bus-stop but decided already if bus come first, I take bus but if taxi come first I take taxi.  Also it is actually safer and less-traffic obstructing to stop at bus-stop than at 9-metres away due to the concave kerbs.  However I always ensure I would not be obstructing any bus if I do stop at the bus-stop always choosing to stop at the very end or behind an existing bus if there is no other bus coming from behind.

    The rules were erected to make our roads more safe and minimize traffic congressions.
