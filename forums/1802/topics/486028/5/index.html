    Actually it is not got pax or not, but what kind of returns or yield at a particular time and place. CDB, you get the surcharge and usually longer distance trip to home. Heartlands, no surcharge and more likely shorter trips.

    To each his own, but a lot also depends on luck. I try not to go AP empty, not worth queueing. Even if you use the mobile apps, sometimes also cock up, got to wait long long and waste time. Time is all we have.

    My strategy, don't mind sharing because it is not new anyway, is to head towards town during the "Golden Period" from 6-12pm - lots of pax, calls and surcharges. After midnight, too many cabs in town, too much competition, very hard to get pax quickly unless you are lucky. See the long long Q of cabs also sian liao. On possible way out to keep cruising and not join the long Q. Sometimes can get pax from unexpected places in town area.

    Mon to Thurs, quite tough to get pax after midnight. So have to cheong before that and take minimal breaks. Singapore got lots of ghost towns after midnight.
