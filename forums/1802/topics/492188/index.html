    SINGAPORE: Parts of the Kallang-Paya Lebar Expressway (KPE) and Marina Coastal Expressway (MCE) tunnels between the Tampines Road and Central Boulevard exits will be closed to vehicular traffic on Sunday (Jun 26).

    It will be closed as part of a joint exercise from midnight to 5am, the police announced on Thursday.

    In the release, police said that motorists driving on the MCE must exit to Central Boulevard at Exit 2, while motorists driving on the KPE must exit to Tampines Rd at Exit 9A.
