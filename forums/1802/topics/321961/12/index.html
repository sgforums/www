    hi max, can do easily but you have to start earlier. Your takings low caused by your late start. If you start at 6.30am like me, by 7.30am already around $30+ collection. Before 7am demand is high. 7am-8am demand is not so good. From 8.30am onwards best. On a good day 6.30am to 4.00pm can nett $150+ though typical is $100-$120. If you start at 5am like kin, even better cos got 1 hour 50% surcharge. Nowadays strategy alone is not enough, you need to be lucky as well. Just knowing where the demand is at what time, where & when to queue or not to queue is insufficient cos cabby and his brother also knows this. Generally..before 8am demand is in the west, after 8 demand is in the east. After about 2 months on the road, your earnings will stabilise as you would have gained sufficient knowledge to "go until where, fetch until where". No need to go back to familiar areas which both wastes time & diesel. Time is our enemy really. You really need to rethink your choice of hirer location. Probably you will want to become a hirer after 2-3 months. Next week is the last burst for day-shift cabbies. Make full use of it cos CNY week after Mon./Tue. guaranteed quiet. Good luck.
