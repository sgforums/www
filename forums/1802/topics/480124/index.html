    As a night driver my points are

    depends on which days u plan to drive longer

    mon-tues = quiet after midnight,can keep early or u can wait for staffs to finish wrk (afternoon shift mostly use cab charge)

    wed = ladies night can drive till morning,mostly drunkards or ppl already high (standby plastic bag)

    thurs-sat = can drive till morning 

    sun = quiet after midnight,can keep early 

    midnight trips always one way only,hv to return bk to town empty

    unless kena some hotspots like geylang or those famous 24hr makan places 

     i don't go ap after midnight to queue 

    me perfer to queue at pubs after midnight

    my fav spot st james 

    try to clock as much as u can during peak hrs if possible (6pm-0000)

    take shorter breaks during peak hr bcoz after midnight u got plenty of time to kill while queuing 

    weekdays after midnight around 3-4am the rds are like cemetery,damm quiet n empty 

    best time to enjoy the cold breeze n the quietness
