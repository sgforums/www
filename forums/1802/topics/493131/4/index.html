    What has our NTA done for us taxi drivers besides collecting money for nothing.

     

    In the last few days I have been to 4 of the taxi companies. I think in order to pressure the taxi companies to lower rental we have to either sacrifice the weakest link. Here are the so call promotions from each of the companies. I will start wt the worst offer.

     

    Premier. Their Optima K5 is offered at $120 per day. Incentive is $300 discount per month. Isn't it the same as before when they offer $119 per day and $10 fuel discount per day. LPPL. Deposit is $1.5K. Relief allowed. Offer rent is for 6 months only.

     

    Transcab. They have offer for Wish, Epica and Renault. According to newspapers it claims that the Renault is $88 per day but on Transcab sign up document it is $101. Deposit is $1.5k.  Incentive is $90 per month. Rental price has no expiry.

     

    SMRT. The Prius is 2 weeks free rental and 5.5 months at $59 per day. No relief driver allowed. Deposit is $1.5k. 

     

    Comfortdelgro.  They offer 50% off standard rental rates for 1st 3 months and 40% off for next 3 months. Deposit is $488. Early termination is only $300. 

     

    So let's do a calculation for 6 months rental.

     

    Premier  K5 $120 x 30 day x 6 mths -$1800 incentives = $19,800 

    Transcab Renault $101 x 30 day x 6 mths - $540 incentives = $17,640

    SMRT Prius $59 × 30 days x 5.5 mths = $9,375

    CDG i40 $65×30 days x 3 mths + $75 × 30 days x 3 mths = $12,600

    So just by jumping to another company u potentially save $10,000 for the duration of 6 months. At the end of 6 months u can always jump to another company and continue saving. There are 5 taxi companies so u can keep jumping for 2.5 yrs. Best is to select the smallest company with lousy rental price to jump to other company, this will surely pressure them to drop price. This is time to unite and fight back.
