    Engine oil leaking can be due to aging and poor casting of engine block, on the other hands, new engine can also get oil leaking due to misalignment and one more common causes is water inside engine.

    So, brothers, be it your own pte car or taxi, never never go into a flood or pool of water above 1/4 of your wheel base, the air intake will take in the water instead of air, this will dampen your air filter and result in water going into your combustion, and due to water unlike oil, it is not subject to pressure and reject any force, this can result in engine block crack.

    On the other hand, it can also due to lower casket burnt or worn, casket burnt you can smell it, this can be all due to cooling problem whereby, cooling chamber are choked or that cooling rubber pipes get hardened and leaking
