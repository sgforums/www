    One easiest reason is ofcourse, the FASTEST way to quickly get a job and start getting income.

    All would know that interviews for professional jobs takes 3-4 rounds of interviews, processing takes another 2-3 weeks. So, let's say you send out 50 CVs, which already take you 1 full month's work, inclusive of searching and crafting coverletter, refining CV to suit job etc, then attending interviews etc.

     

    So, let's compute, finding work = 2- 3 months (unsure, but taxi is sure)

                                   Interviews = 1 - 2 months (some wont get back to you, so you gong gong wait, or some will reply only after 2 weeks or 1 month, Yo! check out the SPF thread and you see them wait for 3-6 months!, plus rounds of interviews)

                                  Confirmed, but admin process = 1 month

    Total = 6-8 months

    But taxi, once you get the license, you go to the taxi company and it will allocate a cab for you to start work!

    The is smart choice. But as people in the blog says, do not get too used to cab driving life, else, it may result in you loosing your aspirations for other professional work, or it will make you too tired to search for other professional work in your spare times (e.g. those time you not driving cab)
