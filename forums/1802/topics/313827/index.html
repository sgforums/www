    Mentor Poolman, ALN.

     

     

    This what happen with my second outing today, I took five on-call jobs. Just keying in my password this morning at 306 carpark at Senkang 7am.  Job offer from 308 came in. Took this  job order heading to airport. Look...  every minute the network is watching at you at any time.

     

     

     

    My Hirer  told me cause you are new last night,  try not to pick paxs with big or small plastic bag heading to Chua Chu Kang  cemetary very heavy jam this Sunday, you may encounter lost time. Maybe empty one way journey back.

     

    Pickup  pax at Yishun Ring road, a man with his two kids with few plastic bags. I asked him Sir where you are heading, Chua Chu Kang. A bit worry by now, asked him again, Famous fish farm there Chua Chu Kang, Safe....at the destination he never alighted  please continue further, you know where he is heading Chua Chu Kang cemetery. Cannot tahan, asked him Sir you told me Chua Chu Kang fish farm, than now why it is cemetary. Sir said you taxi drivers if i never said fish farm you will never send me here.

     

    For me maybe god know that I am new, after that family alighted  at the cemetary another Mother and son pop in immediately, destination to Jalan Bukit Merah, press the button immediately started $2.80 no lost time.

    This two trips everthing is cover today my rental of the day $45.80.

     

    Total 266.30 km i cover from 7am to 4.30pm as a beginner like me without knowledge, my Sincere Thanks to my two gurus for  coaching me and knowledges  willing to pass-on to fellows new recruited as taxi driver and to their success, Three Cheers Guru.
