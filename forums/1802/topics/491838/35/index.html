    Originally posted by Jasonlkl:

    Are cabcharge used only for bookings? need to swipe/ insert/ tap the card? Or no need do anything? 

    Not necessarily.  For Pandanchen, cabcharge is a cashless payment system for comfort/citycab taxis only.

    Got many types of cabcharge cards.

    1. Cabcharge e-voucher - white colour, blue/black words.  Got company name and serial no. Always used for bookings by companies e.g. certis cisco. Bookings cos company pay mah.  But street hail oso can. Got magnetic strip, so swipe.  Valid for one trip only. Throw away after use.  Usually cabbies keep and throw for pax.

    2. Cabcharge Corporate - blue colour.  Can be for bookings or street hail.  Tap and go.  Must return to pax one hor. And usually got cab rewards one.

    3. Cabcharge Personal - Black colour.  Same as blue.

    4. Cabcharge Priority - White colour yellow/black words.  Same as 2 and 3.

    5. Cabcharge 3Gen - pinkish colour.  Same as 2.

    So far, mostly encountered 1 and 2 only.  The rest seldom.
