    As you can see, there are currently 5 taxi operators, namely ComfortDelgro, SMRT Taxis, SMART Automobiles, Union Energy and Premier Taxis.

    SMART Automobiles operates SMART cabs.

    Union Energy operates Transcab.

    Premier Taxis operates Silvercab.
