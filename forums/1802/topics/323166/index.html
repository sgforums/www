    a few..

    1. Going to 1 Raffles Link : go into the carpark at Marina Mandarin, follow sign board to Raffles Link, turn right to 1 Raffles Link.

    2. Thomson road to Tanglin/Orchard, avoid traffic lights : whitley rd, dyson rd, chancery hill rd, chancery lane, balmoral rd, anderson rd, orange grove rd. detour at chancery lane to mt. rosie, malcom rd to access stevens rd. more useful for avoiding traffic jams rather than shortcut actually.

    3.stevens rd (small lane) next to The Draycott condo at Draycott Park..joins to main stevens road directly at the sloping u-turn to scotts rd.

    4. carpark jurong east st31<=> jurong east ave 1

    5. getting to upper circular rd from eu tong sen rd, no need to u turn along hill st. from eu tong sen turn right to carpenter st, turn left to carpark, exit straight ahead to upper circular rd.

    6. grange rd <=> tanglin rd : one tree hill, jalan tupai, tomlinson rd (also to cuscaden rd)

    7. river valley <=> grange rd : nathan rd, chatsworth rd (chatsworth rd/ave, jervois rd all quite useful for moving to & from grange/tanglin)

    8. chartwell, li hwan : useful for escaping serangoon gdn/lor chuan

    9. toa payoh lor.1 to thomson rd : use toa payoh rise (1 way only)

    10. clementi ave 3 from PIE/clementi ave 6 : u turn at clementi ave 4 instead of turning right on commonwealth ave west at the ntuc (takes too long)

    11. loyang ave to pasir ris dr 3 (from changi village) : avoid the right turn. on loyang ave, turn left into the shell station and right into new loyang link, go straight for dr 3

    12. jalan haji alias along sixth ave to get back to the coronation/duchess area

    13. bukit timah rd after sixth ave (condo area) to holland rd : old holland rd/wilby rd/blackmore rd => holland link/plain/road

    acutally many more...but we should always learn new ones to help ourselves respond to bookings quickly and best of all don't need to fly. 5 min got time to go toilet somemore. rotfl
