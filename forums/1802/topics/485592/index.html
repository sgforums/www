    Stomper Sebastian spotted this cab with its left wheel stuck in the hole at a construction site at Geylang Lorong 26 this afternoon, Jun 7.

    According to him, it was about 11.30am and the taxi was travelling from Geylang Lorong 26 into Geylang Road when the accident happened.

    He said:

    "He was making a left turn into Geylang Road when his left wheel got stuck.

    "Only one taxi was involved in the accident. I'm not sure if anyone was injured."

    It is unclear whether the taxi had rammed into the cones placed around the construction works, or if the cones were not placed properly to warn the drivers.
