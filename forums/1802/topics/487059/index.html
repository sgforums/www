    From experiences, CBD will only start to get hot after 2pm those auditors & executives go running errands. Places like Suntec, SGX, MBFC.  HDB & private residences good market whole day long. The challenges is job with no destination also take because you will never know where the pax can bring you too. Sometimes the more choosy the more jialat. I also like CBD $8 short trip in one hour intervals. $8+$3 = $11+meter one short trip UOB Plaza to Raffles Town Club can clock $25.

    Airport unless drop pax at airport. Will use Apps like SG Flight Info to check flight landing time. Pax normally check out from customs half hr or more after landing can use that as a benchmark. One flight average can carry 200 pax. SO if that hour 6 flights or more landing vs taxi queue of 80 taxis still can queue. Also can gauge by queue length. T1 queue at control tower considered short. T3 normally less flight but less taxis also. Sometimes lunch time & change shift time can get pick & go. My teacher told me lunch time Indonesian flights mostly go Orchard / Newton.

    Some of my colleagues like MBCC but to me the $5 not worth it vs the morning off peak demand.
