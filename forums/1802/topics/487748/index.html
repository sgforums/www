    Dear Cabbies 

    We have launched the Call Levy Points (CLP) system. 

    The main purpose of this new scheme is to encourage drivers to take more booking jobs. 

    WHAT IS CALL LEVY POINTS (CLP)? 

    CLP is a CREDIT ACCOUNT for all drivers to offset their Booking Call Levy. 

    1 CLP is equivalent to $1. 

    For example, your call levy for the week is $8 and you have 5 CLP. System will use this 5 CLP ($5)  to offset your call levy of $8 first and the remaining $3 will then be deducted from your bank account. This offsetting process will be done together with the call levy collection on every Tuesday. 

    HOW DO I EARN CLP? 

    You can accumulate these points via incentives or goodwill payment. We would like to share the following scenarios where CLP will be offered to you. 

    ·        Call Centre asks you to pickup an urgent booking request and will offer you 5  CLP.   

    ·        You encountered No-show for your advance booking and you have a good booking record . As a form of goodwill, company refund 5 CLP. 

    * 5 CLP can be used to offset approximately 15 current jobs’ call levy 

    We thank you in advance and looking forward to your continuous support.
