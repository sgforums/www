    "My father stopped his car and this is when the epic driver banged into the back of the car in front.

    "It was purely her mistake and negligence.

    "Even when the driver of the car she hit came down to take a look, she was STILL busy pointing her fingers at my innocent father.

    "From the video, you can tell that the driver did not even bother about her accusations and was trying to highlight HER mistake to her, because she simply thinks she is not at fault.

    "The episode does not end here... The culprit still continued to point fingers and started snapping photos of my father's car. What the heck is she trying to do?!

    "I also think she did not have her seatbelt on, did she?

    "After my Father drove off, this epic driver chased up and was on the lane to the right of my father's and she braked quite a few times just to allow her passenger to take MORE photos.

    "Such a hazard on the road!

    "This epic driver should learn how to signal before changing lanes or overtaking and please, learn to better control your brake pedals!

    "Do not accuse anyone just as you like, it's not like we did you any wrong,  hello!
