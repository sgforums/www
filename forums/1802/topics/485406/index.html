    What's New:

    1) New outlook for Job Bidding Screen

    2) There is a Estimated Taxi Fare for the trip that you are bidding.

    3) In the setting mode, you have a choice of turn the screen OFF to save battery,
    Screen sleep while no Job bidding is on.
    where the job bidding is ON, the screen will light up.

    4) You can view few difference bids at the same time by scrolling the screen up and down.

    The biggest change in V4 : 

    Before that every GRAB TD ONLY allowed to have one registered IMEI.
    i.e. you can only use the registered IMEI Anroid Phone to take call.

    Version 4 is wokrs based on your GRAB Taxi registered Email address,
    i.e. if your registered Email address is BruceLee@gmail.com

    Then you can use any Anroid Phone that using BruceLee@gmail.com account
    to installed the App and take call.

    This great improvement will help TD that having a few Phone etc.

    (Before that when you switch from your Samsung S5 to SONY T3, you need to contact GRAB Taxi help line to change your registered IMEI to your SONY T3 IMEI or else, you can't use your SONY T3 with the GT TD er App.)
