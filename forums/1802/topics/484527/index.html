    I'm on a break, says cabby who parked at Rivervale Plaza taxi stand

    Printer-friendly version
    Stomp | Monday, May 12, 2014

    Stomp contributor onetousandandone saw a taxi parked at Rivervale Plaza taxi stand while its driver was out on a break on Saturday night (May 10).

    The Stomp contributor waited for about 15 minutes but the driver still did not return.

    The Stomp contributor said:

    "I spotted a taxi parked at Rivervale Plaza taxi stand at around 9pm on May 10 (Sat).

    "I took a look inside the taxi and realised that the cabby driver was out on a break, as seen on his display monitor.

    "He shouldn't misuse his rights by parking at the taxi stand and going for his break.

    "I waited for about 15 minutes and he still did not return.

    "A taxi stand is for taxis to wait for their passengers to board.

    "He shouldn't use the space as his parking lot."
