    Cab driver made famous by MP!

    Cabby Chan got his face in the papers, thanks to a kind act.

    MP for Bishan-Toa Payoh GRC Mr Zainudin Nordin posted on Facebook yesterday, praising cabby Chan Teck Koon for finding and returning his wallet, which he had left in the cab on Monday, Nov 4.

    He wrote:

    "Yesterday I lost my wallet. Panicked and felt silly for being careless. Made all the necessary calls and even reported the loss to the NPC. Was resigned to not finding it again.

    This morning, I received a call from Mr Chan Teck Koon who drives a "TransCab" taxi. He had found my wallet in his cab. What a relief. I am so greatful and thankful for his honesty and care. Met the humble family man at the Marine Parade HDB carpark to collect my wallet.

    Mr Chan Teck Koon, thank you."

    According to Wanbao, cabby Chan refused to any token of appreciation. Mr Chan also said it's the first time he has ferried an MP in his 13 years as a taxi driver, but did not recognise Mr Zainudin.
