    Well, yesterday afternoon, I went to the Lee Kong Chian Reference Library of National Library, located on the 11th storey.

    At the same rack as the reference books for buses (such as TL guides), I also found some books/booklets about taxis, below the TL guides.

    They were namely:

    1) Singapore Taxi Service (a 1980s book published by EPB)

    2) Taxi Guide (also in 1980s by NTUC Comfort?)

    3) Cabby newsletter for Citycab/Yellow-Top cab.

    To find them, first take a lift to 11th storey, put down any bags with you into the locker(a must!), go into the reference library until reaching the corridor that is between two book racks, and walk until you see the rack of audio-visual materials to your right.

    Then turn right and walk until you find the TL guides to your right.

    And look into the two racks that is below the TL guides.

    The books about taxis are found in these two racks.

    Enjoy reading and researching more about taxis!
