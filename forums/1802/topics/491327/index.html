    SINGAPORE: Two roads will be closed to vehicles for the Chingay Parade on Feb 19 and Feb 20, as well as for the National Education Show that will take place on Jan 30 in the lead up to the parade, the Singapore Police Force said in a press release on Tuesday (Jan 26). 


    The parade, organised by the People's Association, will be held at the F1 Pit Building near the two closed roads. 

    During the road closures, access will only be granted to police and emergency vehicles. Parking restrictions along the peripheral roads will also be strictly enforced, the police added.
