    I once had a conversation with a taxi driver... He told me something like how he work. Firstly your dad needs to know where is the best place to pick up customers. When you know this.. Keep it to himself and do not share with others.

    Secondly, during night time, especially those early and late mornings.. Ask ya father no point joining the taxi queue in order to pick up the people that just finish chiong... Especially at the liang court that side where you can see a whole stretch of taxi queuing up for customers. This is because sometimes you queue for maybe up to 1 hour... and then ya customers say tiong bahru.. From liang court to tiong bahru how much can ya earn?? Nothing much.

    Dont spent too much time to chit chat with other drivers is also a good way to earn more money. just driving during peak hours is not enough, your dad needs to know where to pick up the most customers. If he goes town, he can pick up customers from town easily, but if he goes to neighbourhood during the day then he might have difficulty picking up customers then..
