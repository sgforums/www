    recently know of a new friend going to OTR and asked for my opinion to start with day or night shift.

    seems there's a never ending pros and cons for each. however my stand was to advised him to build his confidence with day shift, as everything is more visible than driving at night, but of course with less extras and surcharges..

    in summary this is what i feel...

    DAY SHIFT

    Pros

    - more consistent demand for taxis on normal work weekdays, less the low key period between 10-11am.

    Cons

    - more traffic jams, road works and hot weather

    - weekend crowds start later, u make less on weekends

    - if its a weekday public holiday or long weekend, there can be less demand

     

    NIGHT SHIFT

    Pros - more surcharges and extras to make

     - cooler weather, lesser traffic as it gets later

    Cons - need to make more empty trips to town to get pax, more fuel used (justified by the extras u received)

    - usually means more queueing at taxi stands for pax, unless u know where to find pax

    - night drivers are more agressive, more reckless and pax snatching situation
