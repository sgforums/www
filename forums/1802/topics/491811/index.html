    Yesterday I was dropping passenger at Woodlands check point at night and I noticed after dropping the passenger that an LTA officer was standing in the vicinity and jotting down something.

    As the lane closest to the building was occupied and queued by at least 20 taxis (all green lanterns), I could only drop the passenger at the second lane which actually was painted with double zig-zag lines (which means cannot stop there).

    After seeing the LTA officer there, I felt that I could have gotten a ticket and therefore made one circle back to see the situation.   I saw most of the taxis that were dropping passengers there stopping at exactly there I stopped (which actually is illegal) except one who turned out to drop the passenger at the stop line area (the one just before turning out to main road).

    So just wondering where you guys drop your passengers there ?   There seems to be no correct way to drop passenger as the double zig-zag area really should have been single zig-zag (so that one lane is for waiting and another lane for dropping like at Lucky Plaza).
