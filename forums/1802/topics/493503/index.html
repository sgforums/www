    Currently, diesel cars and taxis are levied a lump sum Special Tax, regardless of the amount of diesel used. "At the same time, I will permanently reduce the annual Special Tax on diesel cars and taxis by $100 and $850 respectively. In this way, we shift from an annual amount of tax to one which is related to usage." For most drivers, the Special Tax reduction will offset the impact of the new diesel duty. "To help businesses adjust, I will provide 100 per cent road tax rebate for one year, and partial road tax rebate for another two years, for commercial diesel vehicles," said Mr Heng.
