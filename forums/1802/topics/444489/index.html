    She stopped for a cuppa and lost her cab

    December 27, 2011 - 11:45pm

     

    SIXTY seconds. That was all the time it took for someone to jump into her cab and make away with it.

    The cabby, who wanted to be known only as Miss Teo, 56, told The New Paper that the brazen theft took place in front of a packed coffee shop at Geylang Lorong 29 on Dec 16 at around 3am.

    Miss Teo had stopped her taxi in front of the coffee shop for a quick coffee to keep her going, leaving the engine on as she often had trouble starting the car and was just a short distance away.

    The silver Kia Magentis Premier taxi is still missing.
