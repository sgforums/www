    18 Aug 15, 07:44
    Originally posted by stilnox76:

    Hi to all the senior, apologie if these post is in the wrong thread.. Just want to know if it is possible/easy to earn nett $2.5k a month being a NEW TDVL doing OMO ($135 RENTAL) any thing more is a bonus.. Thank you for your feedback

    It is possible to earn $2.5k a month doing OMO but unlikely for a new TDVL unless he drives 15 hours a day.  This is due to the fact that he is likely to be still unfamiliar with the routes and places and would take longer time to find customers.  

    It is easier to start off as a relief driver to learn the trade before switching to OMO (if one desires so).

    I started off as a relief working 11-12 hours per day 6 days a week and hit that amount or exceeded during my first few months (but during that time fuel prices were higher).  My rental was $80+ cheaper for my 12-hour shift.   $80+ is 3-5 hours of extra driving for 6 days a week so it is a lot of extra driving if you do OMO.  You also have to drive on the 7th day.

    Take my advise, start off as a relief first and then make the decision whether to switch to OMO after at least 6 months into the trade whether to switch over to OMO, remain as a relief or become a hirer (recruiting one or more reliefs).  Life is a lot easier as a relief.
