    STOMPer Ivan was annoyed to see this taxi that was parked at a taxi stand outside Geylang East Polyclinic, while the driver was nowhere in sight.

    Said the STOMPer:

    "After visiting Geylang East Polyclinic, I saw a cab at the taxi stand right in front of the Polyclinic, without any taxi driver.

    "Because of this, other taxis didn't want to stop.

    "We patients had to go to the roadside to get a taxi.

    "So why make a taxi stand for the polyclinic?"
