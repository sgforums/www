    5.Short distance cabby - Never go beyond $5 trip of cabby , anything beyond that , they have to go coffee shop sit one hour to rest .

    6.Long distance cabby - Simply adore Tampines to Jurong type of cabby , the longer the better . This make them steam all day long .

    7.Midnight cabby - No 50% surcharge they no motivation .

    8.Industrial estate cabbies - They only head to Tuas , Jurong area to get paxes , mainly walkie talkie taxi kiosk cabbies .

    9.LV cabbies ( Las Vegas )- Always head to clubs to get their next pax , man or woman , woman mainly , saliva drips Geylang trip their favourite .

    10.Hotel cabby - Always support hotel Q .

    11.Taxi stand cabby - Always go to nearest taxi stand , long or short Q , prefer to wait than to cruise .

    12.Tom Cruise cabbies - Will kill them if u get them to Q at taxi stand . They only cruise , cruise and cruise until next pax . Never stop . Vehicle always on the move .

    Many many more ......
