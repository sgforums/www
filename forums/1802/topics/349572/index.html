    For newbies .... I hope to share my knowledge about Tuas with you .

    Briefly , Tuas Industrial is separated by four square plot of land .

    The 4 squares is mainly Joo Koon area , Gul area , Tuas area , and Tuas south area .

    All separated by 3 main roads : AYE , Pioneer Road and Tuas Road .

    Joo Koon square , the whole area is about a Circle , we name it " Joo Koon Circle " , within Joo Koon Circle , there will be some " fishbone" type of subsidiaries , namely Joo Koon Way , Joo Koon cresent , Joo Koon Road .

    Gul Area also got a circle , " Gul Circle " , the whole circle is about 15 minutes 60km/h drive . Then within the circle , like a fishbone , all his subsidairies , Gul Road , Gul Way , Gul Drive , Gul Street , Gul Ave etc ...

    Gul Circle is complex , especially the offices and complexes and got no number sequence , one moment u see No.10 , next could be No.56 , without any neighbours .

    Gul Circle can be access through Tuas road ( PIE ) , or Gul Way ( AYE ) .
