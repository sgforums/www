    In working, employers contribute 16% of your monthly paid into CPF, you contribute 20%, altogether 36% into yr CPF account, meaning if you are earning 1000, $360 will go into yr CPF which is then distributed into SA and MS. So, simply speaking, working, boss paid for yr CPF + you yourself In Taxi driving, you paid CPF for others
