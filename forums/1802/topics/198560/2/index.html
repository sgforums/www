    As a ex-cabby, i strongly agree communication between drivers & customer is a important factor.

    1) Always greet them, some customer will return a smile some will just ignore

    2) Always ask for destination & propose few ways for the customer to chose*Do not press meter till you are clear where & how to go

    3) Go extra mile by asking , air-con ok for them or radio station they prefer

    4) Upon arrival, wish them a good day ahead.

    Normally it works for me
