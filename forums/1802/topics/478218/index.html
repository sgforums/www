    For SG residents, depending on where they stay, some like to use this method to Airport -Yio Chu Kang ( or Boundary) Upp Paya Lebar to PIE. So they may also refer the same route back. It's logical to use same route to and fro most of the times.

    Unless they stay Li Hwan area or Burghley Drive side, then they may prefer use either AMK Ave 1 or 3 exit, then take the CTE to SLE (or PIE, if Ave 1 normall using the PIE is shorter).

    The return journey would be the same, SLE then exit Ave 1 into Li Hwan ( Cardiff) then to their road. Can use the PIE and CTe but depends on the timing as the CTE can get clogged up at certain times before 11 pm.

    The simple solution is to ask how they went to airport and then ask whether to use the same route back.
