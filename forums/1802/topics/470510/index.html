    This morning when i walk near to my cab, a strong diesel smell came to my nose. I noticed that the smell came from engine compartment but i choose to ignore. Thinking to complete  my peak hr then send for repair.

    According to workshop, they explained that the leak oil travelled to fan belt n causes it to torn. without fan belt, brake n power steering will not function. 

    Just a reminder to fellow Td, do get a tow truck immediately when similar situation happens.
