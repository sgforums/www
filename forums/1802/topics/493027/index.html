  • Taxilim88
    30 Nov 16, 10:53

    Taxi Company will not act for you.  You have to help yourself. Your case was in 2015.  A bit too late to fight now.  Let me give you my own case.

    I was at a cross junction turning right.  The right turn green arrow was in my favour.  As I was turning, the other car going straight in the opposite direction beat the red light and bang into me. I did not have any video footage as the bloody Sonata camera did not work.  The old cameras will fail when there is an impact.  I tested it myself when I took the camera out.  Shake the camera a bit and the video recording stopped.

    Without video evidence, I was 100% liable as I was the turning vehicle. I had to pay $2,000 to Comfort.  What I did immediately was to get the video footage from LTA and make a police report.  They have the j-eyes or junction cameras.  I had to write to LTA and called them as well.  Luckily, they had the footage of the accident and said they will pass it to TP.

    Then I had to call TP to make sure they get the footage from LTA.  Chased TP everyday to make sure they handle my case promptly.  I also went to GIA and paid to get the other party's accident report.  True enough, the other party denied everything in his report.

    It took TP almost six months to settle the case.  In the end, they charged the other driver in court under Section 65(b) for inconsiderate driving.  The culprit pleaded guilty and TP sent me a letter to inform me.  Only then with that letter can I go back to Comfort to reclaim back my $2,000.

    With that TP letter, Comfort won the case and claimed loss of income for me.  Only recently after more than a year is my case fully settled.

    In your case, you should have immediately requested for SMRT or SBS Transit for video footage as all MRT exits and drop offs has CCTV to deter illegal parking. Still you can ask for it, but a bit too late now.
