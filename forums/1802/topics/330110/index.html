    Cabbies who want to master Changi Airport , must do some homework one , blind blind chiong into airport arrival taxi stands no use .... do not alot , just a piece of paper and a pen will do .

     

    From Monday to Friday , the arriving flights quite standard for major airlines , major as in Qantas , British Airways , Emirates , Cathay Pacific etc Big Airlines . Saturday airport has the least arrivals . Sunday is the best . Write those timings and total flights , and slot into your wallet , and when u need it , just open up the paper and see , will do the business for you .

    For example , what i did .... 5pm-6pm (T1=6 T2=5 T3=5) 6pm-7pm (T1=12 T2=11 T3=9) and so on .... if got time , do a daily glance into their websites before start work . Hope it works for you guys .
