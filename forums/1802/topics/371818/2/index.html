    Talking about day..Day shift.

    As a beginner, you will find hard to move around.

    Why?...on the road...all are less than ten years experience, more than ten years experience. All got (ang kong) meaning they got their own turf...cause most of them are hirers and very tough and robust intercept paxs with you with their machine the taxi. we will just a bystander.

    Like me..first new like sotong...where to find my turf...taxi stands, hospitals, mrt stations, shopping centers. where there is available chance to catch your pax there are many taxis... there those season drivers there.

    So...as time go by day by day...just wasted more diesel and look see look see. And today not a probelm. You name it i will fullfill you, you just pay the fare, i will bring you there.

    Dun worry..skin must thick...must be brave can or cannot, just go.

    Now pax on board...destination not sure, asked again.

    like example...belly street.

    you know negro...knn their english the most difficult to catch...talking like singing RAP

    Something loud sometime soft...there was this negro...belly street. repeated 4 to 5 times than i catch no ball from jurong shipyard.

    Where? belly street Where? belly street.

    Where? service apartment street where? belly?

    Near where... I don't know. Than how to go, already on board.

    One more time belly street...near belly point.

    Knn catch ball liao...Valley Point...Ok ... River Valley. yayaya. Valley suite.

    So..something sotong also got their day...another point  and destination gained stored into your database.

    Never mind today no collection tomorrow try again, as time go by...

    some knn new driver everyday only know how to calculate. Only panic just need income.

    You must know set a target...one month or two month get yourself to lose some time and some money...later when you are ready on the market...income, your strategy how to run your route than that is your sucess.

    Timing.

    Morning 5am to 6am...early birds..this are all the sotong pax either wakeup late to work or rushing to the nearest connection like company transport provided pickup point, all maybe hawkers and various other.

    Morning 6am to 8.30 am...this slot like kaleidoscope... many pattern and many kind of colours pax...students, office workers, blue collar workers, teachers, factory workers and many many...but one thing...those knn  (ang kong with tatoo) hirers are there. You must know how to hander them, and must know how to dogfight and intersept them.

    Morning after 9am to 12pm...mostly hospital, banking, offfice matter, company appointment meeting, customer call, going to work shopping  center personnel, marketing housemakers.  Aiya..most of the hirers will be resting having their coffee break or early lunch...this is a good slot...i will be rounding when they are not there, plenty of catches. So like this kind my strategy from my own experience.

    Afternoon 12pm to 5pm.

    Basically, the sun is on your top, meaning very hot...so where will you go...housing estates not much...so Q at taxi stands or waiting for calls or sometime, tikam tikam on some routes you think it protential...that is all about day.
