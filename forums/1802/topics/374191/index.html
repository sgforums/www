    Singapore is the host of the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC)
    meetings in 2009. The highlight of the meetings takes place during Leaders’
    Week which will be held primarily at the Suntec Singapore International
    Convention and Exhibition Centre from Sunday, 8 Nov to Saturday, 14 Nov
    2009. Leaders’ Week will culminate with the Leaders’ Retreat at the Istana
    from Saturday, 14 Nov to Sunday, 15 Nov 2009.

    Appropriate security and traffic measures will be taken to minimise the
    impact of the event on affected stakeholders and members of the public.
    This includes the implementation of an APEC Lane to facilitate movement
    between meeting venues.

    The APEC Lane, which is the extreme right lane of affected roads, will be
    demarcated by cones and water barriers. This lane will only be closed to
    the public prior to and during the movement of APEC vehicles. Auxiliary
    Police officers and Traffic Police officers will be deployed at the
    affected roads to regulate traffic and direct motorists during the closure,
    including those entering or leaving buildings along the APEC Lane. Aside
    from the APEC lane, all the other lanes will continue to remain open to the
    public.
