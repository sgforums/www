    Eat Healthy

    Singapore where to eat?
    Healthy and tasty food to share with all cabbies.



    What? - CityZoom Minced Fish Noodle
    Where? - Blk 57, Lengkok Bahru, #01-489, Singapore (151057)
    Who? - Contact Tom @ 97420865
    When? - Operating hours: 7am to 3pm (Mon - Sun)
    Off on holidays & alt. Sun (Please call/sms to confirm)
    How? - Please refer to the map below on how to get there.
    (Nearest mrt = redhill, building is next to e2i employment & STA academy)


    (Map obtain from courtesy of http://mylifestylemap.com)
    Please click on image to see larger display of map clearly.

    Brief description:
    - This stall is basically selling minced fish noodles by Tom, a very young boss. All ingredients are fresh and tasty. Food stock is ordered daily and sold out on daily basis. All ingredients are fish and no other meat is included in the noodles. Even the wanton is fish wanton. It's that healthy and tasty. Some customers like the tasty soup and will ask for a 2nd bowl.

    Speciality:
    - Fish noodle (made using fish meat). First glance, it has the bee-tai-mak look but it is different. The quality is VERY GOOD and the texture of the noodle is damn Q! Most customers like this noodle and is sold out everyday, mostly before lunch.

    Price:
    - Normal:- $2.50/bowl
    - Fish Noodle:- $3.50/bowl
