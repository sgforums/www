    There is no such thing as owning a business without initial capital, it is either you pay now or later. Taxi is a small proprietor trade for small businessmen, people who joined taxis or phv driving from all walks of working life will then understand basic accounting of business, like profit and lost, cash flows and managing cost. Rental is the main cost, an invest capital that pay later, other are cost of goods like fuel prices, carpark fees, summons, knocks and puncture etc etc. Just like hawkers, unlike stable business like Uber, grab or courts etc, boss just sit and think of strategy to make their sale revenue up, taxi and phv have to plough the field for long hours, They are the boss, the workers, the staff, the financial accounting, the business planner, sales, marketing...all lump into one. So, in a nutshell, after joining taxi or phv, you see the drivers tends to become more calculatives and mindful of even the smallest expenditure or lost, this is the evolve of business mind, Unfortunately, the return for such business is pathetically little, given the life time you hv to spend on the road, and the liability of knocks and summons, not to mention the health deterioration due to stress and agony on the road.
