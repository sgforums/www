    There are 5 modules where module 2 and 5 are hardest to pass on routes and landmarks.  There is also a 1 day (8.30am to 1pm sunday) bus orientation with guidance by a LTA staff.  Mine was good as he gave us lots of important info to watch out in the test.  Pay attention and write down the hints and tips.  There are quite a number of questions not found in the online lessons and model test questions.  Example the online teach you how to calculate fare from 0001hrs to 0559hrs for midnite charge.  Read the test question carefully as I kenna one that says pax board taxi say at 5.40am and alight at 6.10am.  How to charge? hehehehe..

    Also if possible take the morning session test as result is known immediately and you can consider to re-take in the afternoon session for those failed papers ($10 + $5 per paper). If you take the afternoon session test, by the time you are done, the office close and you need to come another day to re-take.

    Hope this helps and good luck!
