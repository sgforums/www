    Police car badly damaged after collision with taxi at Killiney Road


    Printer-friendly versio
    Stomp | Wednesday, Apr 30, 2014

    SINGAPORE - An accident between a taxi and a police car took place on Apr 27, 2014 at around 8pm near the SingTel ComCentre in Killiney Road.

    Stomp contributor Ah Dien had been passing by the area and contributed photos of the incident.

    He told Stomp in a phone conversation: "I was passing by and saw a police car and badly damaged at a junction in Killiney Road, near the ComCentre.

    "I think the taxi driver was injured and sent to hospital."

    Accident in Toa Payoh leaves taxi badly smashed in middle of road


    Printer-friendly versio
    Stomp | Wednesday, Apr 30, 2014

    SINGAPORE - Stomp contributor F spotted a crashed taxi in the middle of the road near Blk 60 Lorong 4 Toa Payoh on Apr 28, 2014 at around 12.30am.

    The front of the taxi was smashed with the hood of the vehicle bent up at an angle.

    The contributor thinks it must have been a hit-and-run as there were no other cars on the road at that time.

    According to the contributor, the driver of the taxi was safe and talking to the police.

    An ambulance arrived shortly after.

    F said: "I think the other car ran away because there was only this cab in the middle."
