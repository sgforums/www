    STOMPer Jason says a cabby whose taxi he had boarded at Yishun kept jamming on the brakes whenever they approached a traffic light. He then realised the latter was watching a movie on his phone.

    "At first I thought this was his habit, but later I heard some talking sound coming from the dashboard. So I followed the sound to find out more.

    "To my surprise, I realised that he had placed his smart phone at the speedometer in front of him.

    "He had been watching movies with his smart phone while driving."
