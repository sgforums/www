    SINGAPORE AIRSHOW 2014

    Singapore Airshow 2014 will be held from 11 – 16 February 2014, please see the attached key information about the event:
     
    11–14 Feb (Trade Days): 45,000 visitors over 4 days
    15-16 Feb (Public Days): Over 100,000 visitors over 2 days  
    Official Opening Hours: 9.30am – 5.00pm
    Last Admission Time at 4.30pm
    Access to Static Aircraft Display Area from 8.30am – 5.00pm   
      
    On 6 Feb 2014 (Thurs) from 4.30pm to 6.00pm, a Taxi Orientation Exercise will be conducted to familiarise our cabbies with the traffic circuit at the event venue – Changi Exhibition Centre (CEC) at 9 Aviation Park Road, and the designated pick-up/drop off taxi bay just outside the exhibition hall.

    Please click here to see the map of Changi Exhibition Centre.

    The first 200 taxi drivers who turn up at the Taxi Pick-up Point will each receive a goodie bag, which includes 2 public day tickets (limited to one goodie bag per driver while stocks last).  

    This year, no airshow public day tickets will besold on 15 & 16 Feb 2014 at both Changi Exhibition Centre as well as Singapore Expo (where the airshow shuttle pickup/dropoff is located). Those that turn up at the show site without a public day ticket will be turned away.
     
    Please be advised to inform the passengers bound for Singapore Airshow 2014 that there will not be any public day tickets sold at Changi Exhibition Centre and Singapore Expo.
