    Dear all brothers,

    I have officially return my transcab merc 368 and tore up my taxi licence on the last day.

    This is my sentiments on driving a taxi for half a year.

    When i first started , i was full of zest and enthusiam for my new found "career". However to be blunt , in my 38 years of life i have never found a job that i can meet so many UGLY singaporeans and those foreign talent included. They order u like DOGS , LEFT RIGHT . Come on passengers do i fxxx own u a living ? In my life i have never been rude to even the aunties that clear my plates at hawker centre , was asking myself what level of hell am i in to meet all these idiots.

    There are rude taxi drivers but most of us are just trying to make a living by giving you passengers a convenient ride home , when someone help you home with that peanuts money dont you think you should be appreciative? In case those frog in the well didnt know , taxi charges are the lowest amongst developed country and be thankful for that. It was not in my character to be revengful but i did in last sunday the last day of my driving , i drive pass passenger and purposely when close by switch to BUSY sign ; the notion never take things for granted. We are provider of service and not server and lord relationship.

    Another issue , do you passengers think taxi drivers are a bunch of uneducated fools. I have been insulted about wearing a fake rolex that cost me 30K to put on my wrist. To all those that have sat in my taxi namely SMRT azera 4773, Kia 6928 and transcab merc 368 ; i have finally found a job as a NYMEX oil trader and can well afford another watch with my one month pay so Fxxx yourself.

    Pardon me for all these have been pen up inside me for long.

    Also in appreciation got to thank poolman , patrick992 ( this idiot never help me when i got into an accident..lol ) , zeemax ,ken5048 and all these bros i forgot to mention in earning my dough. We share the same shit and that make me eternally grateful for your help. And to all those idiots trying to cut q etc.....uncles , we are all in the same trade please help each other ; the cake is big enough to share.

    The good thing that came out of me ; I have learned to value money a lot more. I have learned to put aside my ego( leo fxxxer) for the sake of my family to make a living and lastly i have found back GOD and return to church.

    Thank You lastly to all from the heart.
