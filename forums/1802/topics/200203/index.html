Different type of Taxi drivers

  • Xcabby
    15 Jul 06, 02:01
    I've seen, spoke to many different type of taxi drivers in all walks of life. Lets see what r those you've met before

    1 60-60 plus senior driver

    Habits-

    1 Open driver door or lower their window to spit

    2 Can spot 1 copy of chinese newspaper

    3 Both hands on steering, swing left & right

    4 Likes to drag gear

    5 slow drivers

    6 Hardly talk

    7 Hang 1 small horse racing calender near the air-con vent

    2 50s drivers

    1 Talk about their prime time history

    2 Listen to 933 & 958

    3 Wear pants with track shoes

    4 Lots of pail, cloth, & tieing rubber behine the booth

    5 Lay newspaper in the car booth

    6 On call & changing shift sign already standby at the dash board

    40s drivers

    1 Try hard to act cool & speak well

    2 Play english song that they don't even know what song they r playing

    3 Good stereo system

    4 Lots of nice gadget in the car, neon lightings etc

    5 Always round down the taxi fare if he enjoy talking to you

    6 Talk mostly about daily life , their family, child etc

    7 Fast drivers & love to zig zag

    30s drivers

    1 Very rare

    2 Customer always tell them they don't look like a taxi driver

    3 Good customer service & well spoken

    4 Mostly kena retrench & can't find a job

    5 Very down to earth & drive with pride

    6 Their taxi mostly very empty, nothing much cos they r not driving for long

    7 Well dress & smell good

    8 Most of them owns a car , a nice flat & married

    9 Carry the latest range of handphone

    10 Listen to class 95, lush 99.5, 98.7

    11 Always get tips due to their talking skill
