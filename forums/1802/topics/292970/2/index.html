    28 Nov 07, 23:25
    Actually without booking, I still can made decent income from driving SMRT taxi. The problem with booking is that sometime lots of time wasted waiting for passenger to come down, time travelling to the pick up point (you will curse and swear especially during traffic jam), the worst is after waiting 10 mins the passenger never turn up or the booking was cancelled.

    Driving taxi as a career can be a relaxing job too. After switching (from comfort to SMRT), I feel more relaxed in my driving, and more I got 24 free rental day in a year, unlike comfort only 3 in a year

    Let do the math :

    Comfort rental : $93.16(per day) x 365 day = $ 34,003.40
    Less 3 rental day = $34,003.40 - $279.48
    Total Rental Paid = $ 33,723.92

    SMRT Rental : $81.32 (per day) x 365 day = $29,681.80
    Less 24 Rental day = $29,681.80 - $1,951.68
    Total rental Paid = $27,730.12

    Difference = $5,993.80

    The key is whether you know where to pick up passenger. I made almost the same amount as I was driving comfort, without the stress, frustration. And most importantly, I can rest 2 sunday every month, while in comfort, I got to drive like a bull.
