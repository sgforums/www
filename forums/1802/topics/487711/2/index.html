    Gentlemen, As a cabbies, as i said many times, there are lot of hazards in your job, from no show to safety to summons to accidents to lousy pax..you name it, you got it. When we had decided to be a cabbie, these are things you will met in and out of your trade, touch wood on accident. However, it happened only once a while, if too often, no one would drive a taxis in Singapore. Next, in any job or business, nothing is gain or done until the job is done, so as a cabbie, bearing in mind that a call does not represent a good income until the journey is completed or that a customer who board you cab by the street is not a business until the journey end, transaction take place and then you can rightly said, business is done, profit gained. That is how the system works, for a pax can just end up quarrelling with you and walk off without paying. Such are the hazards that we must get enlightened, and when you understand this farking system, you might get angry over a call cancelled, but then realized, that is how it works, at least, it cancel the call, worst are some dun even bother to cancel and off their phone. But no matter what happened, we, as matured men and women, must remain calm and steady in any situation, this can help to enhance our thinking and making decision, it will also help to maintain our health and happily carry on with our work. Lastly, there are alway claim of wrongly orderd foods by customer in any restaurant kitchen, but that does not prevent the cooks and the waiters from getting angry and frustration and thus serve customer badly, life just hv to move on.
