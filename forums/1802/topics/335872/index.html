    ...sat in a couple of these iCabTV cabs myself since I don't have a car here in Singapore and have to take them to commute everyday.

    was quite amused when I sat into one initially a couple month ago... but, I got totally bored after 5 minutes. as I only see some news clips and latest movie trailers with some stupid TVCs.

    screen is a little dim by default under sun, good that it allow you to adjust the brightness and volume.

    I have to say, overall Singapore taxi is more advanced then Shanghai or other Asian cities I've been to where if there's a TV, it is a tiny screen blasting boring advertisements at you which I can't even turn off. :(
