    Just to further clarify on the Foreigners (PR n WP) can drive Uber n Grab Car issue

    There are 3 types of Private Hire Car market:

    1. Grab n Uber X

    2. Traditional Pte Limo (individually owned like many ex taxi drivers who buy their own Viano n operate limo services on their own)

    3. Traditional Limo Companies like San Tours, RMG Travel, etc... - these companies limo drivers are "Employed" on salary.

    With this NEW  regulations, ONLY SINGAPOREAN can set up.Sole Proprietor company PR and WP CANNOT.

    So technically,  they can't be uber or Grab driver.

     

    Now when MOT said foreigner can also drive Pte Hire Vehicle - it is meant to be if you are "Employed - paid a salary by a transport Limo company".

    So like San Tours or RMG, they may employed a PR to drive because their business already existed for more than 20 years.
