    Drivers may like to know that not all Meters are set/adjusted to same % slow

    Read more details here:

    https://docs.google.com/document/d/1QaOmMd-3vgqoohLBzXPYJuY2W3WhE77EHT5knlHgrTI/pub

    Your veh is 1st test run over a certain distance to get the setting/pulse to be set on your meter.

    The higher the number the slower the Meter.

    Press K2 & K3 (at the same time) of Digitax Meter Models will display Meter pulse.

    eg  2580  2590 2670

    Keen to know how slow is your Meter. run a distance test with your smart handphone GPS apps and compare with your printer receipt distance run.

    Veh technometer is not accurate
