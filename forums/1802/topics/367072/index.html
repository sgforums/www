    morning peak hours the flyover only 1 direction - going in. Got to be careful not to use the lanes on the right hand side of the flyover cos later you'll have  problems getting to the taxi lane. If no need to go to pass office, best is to stick to left side flyover, extreme right lane, this will lead directly to the taxi lane. This case when coming out, you must park your cab in the left hand side lots of the kiosk for returning the pass. It's on the opposite side of the toilet. I think got about 10 lots. Off peak hours, can park taxi beside toilet or just drive through to exit lane and return the pass at the table where u handed over your TVL or DL. Watch out for the speed camera just after seraya ave. 70kmh.
