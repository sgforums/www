    I'm sure you guys have read about the case of the unfortunate murder of taxi driver Yuen Swee Hong on Aprill 11th 2009. His murderer, Chinese national Wang Wenfeng was convicted and sentenced to death.

    However with the review of the death penalty, all capital cases had sentencing reviews and Wangs case came before trial judge Lee Sieu Kin in November last year and he accepted the defense argument and reduced the sentenced to life imprisonment plus 24 strokes.

    The prosecution is now appealing the new sentence. I am usually not keen on the death penalty but in this case I believe it should be imposed. It's not because he's a foreigner or because the victim is a TD, or even the cruel mental torture he put Mdm Chan (Mr Yuen's wife) through. That's extortion and a separate issue from murder.
