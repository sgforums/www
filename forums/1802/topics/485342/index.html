    Cabby, bus driver stop vehicles in middle of road to argue


    Printer-friendly versio
    Stomp | Monday, Jun 9, 2014

    SINGAPORE - A ComfortDelGro taxi driver and an SMRT bus driver were seen stopping their respective vehicles to argue in the middle of the road near Kranji MRT station.

    The incident occurred at 7.05am last Saturday when the cabby tried to change lanes, a witness told citizen journalism website Stomp.

    The Stomp contributor said that the bus driver did not give way, and both got out of their vehicles to argue.

    He said: "This happened near the taxi stand, and a traffic jam formed because these two vehicles were stopped there."

    He said that the drama ended at 7.12am when the cabby got back into his taxi and drove off.
