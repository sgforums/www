    Peter Yu, the former actor who was also Quan Yifeng's ex husband, is now apparently a taxi driver.

    Shin Min Daily reported that he is currently earning roughly $150 a day in his new profession.

    The Chinese newspaper spoke to Yu over the phone and found out that he is now a Christian, and that he has since remarried someone who volunteers at the church he goes to.

    Yu is slated to shed light on his life, including details on the period after his highly-publicised divorce with Quan, in an interview due to air on Oct 1.

    The couple's divorce was finalised in 2009, and they were both granted shared custody of their daughter.
