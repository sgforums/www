    For those newbies .... this road , many would never imagine how long is it !

    It's from Jurong Bird Park up to Tuas checkpoint !

    And opposite .

    Jalan Ahmad Ibrahim , is the 2 sides road besides AYE .

    This road was created to gain access to all the Guls , and Tuas and Pioneer without disrupting the fluency of the AYE . Mainly cater for long big containers and trucks .

    Companies with even numbers , is located direction towards Tuas checkpoint , companies with odd number , is located down town AYE .

    Nevermind la , take it as a knowledge , seldom pax tell you : " Jalan Ahmad Ibrahim ! "
