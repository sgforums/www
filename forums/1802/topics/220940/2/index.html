    helo, peak period 5pm to 8pm and 7am to 930am... 35% of fare during these duration. e.g. if you board before 5pm, the 35% surcharge is only imposed on the fare incurred from 5pm till end of your journey, not whole fare... booking fee or CBD surcharge are not subject to 35% surcharge...hope this clears
