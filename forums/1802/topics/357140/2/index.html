    Really needed an income back then. No time to dilly dally with hirer/relief problems. Signed with Comfort 6mths and bring taxi home. (Anyway they sponsored the course and paid for my time at the academy).

    6mths of doing both day and night shifts makes me learn faster.  The day shift can bring you to places you don't during the night shift and vice versa.

    Morning customers can be more critical as most will be rushing to work, you have to know traffic conditions and be in the correct lanes in traffic jams. Some lanes move and some don't. Many customers can guide you because they travel through the same jams regularly.

    'Going-home' passengers are more relaxed. They most probably won't be time constraint.  That's why evening jams are slower and takes longer to discisipate. Damn those drivers taking their own sweet time where we cabbies need to be fast to make the most of time left.
