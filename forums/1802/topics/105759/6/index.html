    Sharing: strategies of taking taxi photos/videos

    1) Pick a good location (such as taxi stands) to take photos of taxis.

    2) Respect passengers' privacy. DO NOT TAKE PHOTOS OF TAXIS WITH PASSENGERS INSIDE

    3) Safety first. DO NOT RUSH OUT ONTO THE ROAD JUST TO TAKE PHOTOS OF TAXIS

    4) Use a camera that has fast response (i.e. Canon Powershot A540) if you need to take photos of fast moving taxis.

    5) Do take note of special features (i.e. on-call signs, change shift signs, special emblems)

    6) If possible, do put your signature tag on your photographs as a mark of copyright. Try to put signature tags at corners of the photograph.

    7) Turn off any click sounds if possible.

    Anyway, with immediate effect, you may post URL links to taxi videos/audio clips.
