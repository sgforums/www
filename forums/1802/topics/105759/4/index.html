    The Singapore - Johore Taxi Service

    These cabs mainly provides a fast link between Singapore (Queen Street) and Johor Bahru (Larkin), with a flat pricing of $40 per passengers.

    The taxis would go by way of Queen Street - Bukit Timah Road - Stevens Road - PIE and BKE - Singapore-Johore Causeway - Johor Bahru

    During the immigration checks, the procedures would be the same as the private cars, just that the cabby was to hand over his/her passport together with his/her passengers' passports for inspection. Meanwhile, the passengers would stay on the cab throughout the entire procedure.
