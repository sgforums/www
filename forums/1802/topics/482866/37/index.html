    • Hands-free & Cash-free: Enjoy the freedom of cashless and forget about fumbling for change when you step out of your ride to head into office first thing in the morning, and to your business appointments throughout the day.

    Steps to enjoying cash-free, worry-free rides in the CBD:

    1. Install the Grab app on your smartphone – if you haven’t already.
    2. Select “Menu” and “GrabPay”.
    3. Add your credit card. If you have already added a card but never used it on GrabCar rides, you still qualify for this promotion.
    4. Select your pick-up and drop-off locations within the CBD, tap “GrabCar (Economy)” and remember to set credit/debit card as your payment method.

    Grab is Southeast Asia’s leading ride-hailing platform. We solve critical transportation challenges and make transport freedom a reality for 620 million people in Southeast Asia.
