    A Pasir Ris resident came across this accident involving a car which had crashed into a forested area along Elias Road.

    The STOMPer said this was not the first time an accident happened in the area, which has a sharp bend. 

    Said the STOMPer:

    "The photos were taken on May 20. Not sure what time the accident happened but the police arrived in the morning after receiving reports. 

    "I suspect that the accident happened the night before.

    "From my understanding, the driver was not present or could have fled or gone to the hospital. 

    "If you look carefully at the photos, this is a 'sports' version of the Audi. 

    "Looks like the S or RS series. Powerful car. 

    "Many cars speed at this Pasir Ris Park area from Elias Road and this is not the first time an accident has happened due to the sharp 90-degree bend. 

    "This is the second 90-degree bend before you reach carpark D. 

    "Luckily there was some water meter chamber that the car hit. 

    "Or else it would have gone down Sungei Api Api."
