    If u pass the test .... when u are on the road ....

    A few circumstances  ....

    1. Customer totally dunno location circumstances ... park vehicle one side , looked for location via Street Directory . Safest and most reliable . Accident free .

    2.Customer reached there then know circumstances, then follow his route , reach that zone , then park vehicle one side , look at Street Directory again , if drive comfort or citycab , just key in building or road name , the system will locate for you , technically , looking at the GPS sytem while driving accident prone .... cos u cannot do 2 things at one time if u're no good at doing it .

    3.Customer know the location , but act dunno circumstances , so when u hit the error button , they will whack you upside down ,  this one u have to lan lan . But learn this way is the best , the hard way .
