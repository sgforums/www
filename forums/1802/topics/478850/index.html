    I support this idea.

    Taxi should have only two fares : Normal taxi and Limousine taxi fares.

    Obvious benefit is off coures to the commuters fir having flat fare sructures, but there are also other benefits to other stakeholders.

    It is time that the power should be taken away from taxi companies. We all know that any fare adjusments (read : fare hikes) is only to the benefit of the companies and not to TDs, or worse to pax.    

    By setting same standard fare accross the taxi companies, they are forced to compete (for renntal business from TDs), either by giving better services (DROs, workshop, rebates, etc) and also being more efficient (ie, pushing down their costs, capital expenditures for new cheaper fleet, etc).

    Taxi business should first be a service to commuters and not means for taxi companies to maximise profits.
