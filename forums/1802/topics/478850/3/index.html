    Company employees are protected by unions and the government i.e.NWC. They set and negotiate for wage increases that take into account price inflation.

    Taxi drivers on the other hand are self employed. We are not protected.  We do not have the power to set prices (i.e. Taxi fares) for our labour and are therefore more vulnerable to inflation.

    Hawkers and restaurant owners are self employed too.  But, they have the power to set the price of their goods.  If their food is good, they can set a higher price and people will still buy from them. That is the difference.

    Inflation benefits the rich and punishes the poor.  The rich can hedge their wealth against inflation. The poor have nothing. Inflation creates greater income disparity between the rich and poor and in the process, greater social tension that we are now experiencing..
