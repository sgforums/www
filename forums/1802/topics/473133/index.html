    TransCab Cabby takes woman to wrong place -- then demands $5 to fix mistake

    STOMPer Grace says this taxi driver not only failed to take her to the correct destination, he also tried to charge her an extra $5 to take her to the right place.

    According to Grace, the cabby was also rude and confrontational during the ride.

    The STOMPer wrote:

    "This is regarding the very bad behaviour of a Trans Cab driver.

    "On Aug 5, I made a taxi booking with Trans Cab, and SHD5745K, a red-coloured vehicle, responded. The trip started at 2.26pm.

    "I told the cab driver my destination was Great World City. However, after traveling halfway, I realised the route taken was slightly different and that it was taking longer than the usual route that I usually take, so I asked the driver if he was taking a longer route.

    "The first time I asked him, he did not give me a reply. The second time, he said in a harsh tone,' when you directed me out of the place, there was only one way out'. So I said okay then.

    "Five minutes later, he stopped outside Vivo City.

    "I was furious. I told him that I clearly said that my destination was Great World City and he took me to Vivo City.

    "At this point I told him that I would get off the cab. He then told me that he would lodge a police complaint against me saying that I boarded the cab and refused to make payment, threatening me by saying that he had all my information since I had made a booking.

    "At this point, the cab fare was around $17-18.

    "So I made him an offer saying, okay then, let the meter stop and take me to Great World City and I would drop the matter.

    "He said 'No. You have to top up an additional $5. If not I wont drive you to the location and you get off here'.

    "I asked for his name and he said very rudely, 'There is no need for me to give you my name, you just take down my taxi number'.

    "He continued by saying that 'When you lodge the complaint, I can argue with the company or police saying that you spoke inaudibly and said Vivo City and Not Great World city, and I can further say that why would I be so foolish to drive you to the wrong location? "

    "All of these was said in Mandarin.

    "I was already very late for an important job interview, and this particular driver still refused to budge or apologise when he is in the wrong.

    "So I had no choice but to agree and let him take me to Great World City with the additional $5 charge making it $22.

    "It is very frustrating that Trans Cab is allowing this kind of driver on the road. Taking a cab is meant to be a pleasant and enjoyable experience and yet this driver is making it troublesome for me.

    "The entire journey the cab driver was rude and unreasonable.

    "It is wrong that he took me to the wrong destination, and he even refused to apologise, insisting that he is right and that I was the one speaking inaudibly.

    "I would like that Trans Cab do something about this serious matter."
