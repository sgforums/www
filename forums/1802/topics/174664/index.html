    SINGAPORE : The recent heavy afternoon downpours may be creating woes for commuters but one cab company is certainly singing in the rain.

    ComfortDelGro's group of taxi companies, which include Comfort, CitiCab and Yellow-Top, saw a record number of 75,000 call bookings made in a single day.

    This was on January 12, one of the wettest days so far this year.

    The weatherman says Singapore will continue to have heavy rain in the afternoons over the next few days. - CNA /ct
