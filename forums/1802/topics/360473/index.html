    It's a every 5 years a must go for taxi driver , current or previous dvr ( as long as u need to renew TDVL , u must go ) .

    I got it this year ... TDVL expire on March , i must go and booked a course . Or my TDVL cannot be renew !!!

    My option : To attend 12 hours in-class training @ any approve training centre .

    Or : On-Line training for 4 hours .

    I chose the latter ... cos 4 hours nia .

    How am i right .... it take me only 1 hour 5 minutes to complete the whole course !

    The syllabus is all online .... 4 topics in all .

    1.Rules & Regulation

    2.Routes and Building + Landmark

    3.Defensive driver

    4.Miscellenous as in attitute , attire , MDT , booking , courtesy , taxi stands etc small issue taxi drivers face each day .

    U must click OK for each syllabus inorder to proceed and take test .

    Each topics about 30 question and answers .

    I just glance thru ..... it took me 15 minutes for finish a topic .

    1 hr to cpmplete everything

    The instructor will know online whether u studies all topics and take test .

    All topic must be 70% passed and then they pass u .

    Summary : A waste of my time actually , it can be done online at home .

    As easy as ABC ...

    So infuture , if need to go for refersher course , pls opt for online ... time saving process .

    Good luck .

    At the end of the course , a certificate of attendance was given to me .
