    This one you play within Traffic Police's rules.   

    True, it is double zig-zag lines there but after that, it becomes double yellow lines which means can stop (also there is not X within circle sign there).   The only problem is there are green railings throughout the stretch so you must stop closest to the double zig-zag lines.
