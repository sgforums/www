    Stomp | Saturday, Jul 27, 2013

     

    Stomp contributor Suresh came across an accident site at Simei, where a lorry had mounted the kerb after colliding with a taxi Thursday afternoon (Jul 25).

    He added that the lorry driver seemed to be injured as paramedics were seen attending to him.

    He wrote in an email to Stomp:

    "Saw this at Simei, just before going in Tampines, this afternoon at around 2.30pm.

    "High speed driving resulted in less time to respond and stop.

    "It caused the lorry to mount the kerb.

    "There were no fatal injuries but I saw paramedics attending to the lorry driver."
