    Parking your taxi and leaving it at the stand unattended.  As I see it, you could be charged for the following:

    1. Hindering or obstructing other taxi drivers from picking up passengers.

    2. Stopping, parking or stationing taxi at public stand when unable to let out taxi
    for hire.

    3. Wilfully obstructing, impeding or preventing other taxi drivers from stopping, parking or stationing their taxis at the public stand.

    Total : 9 Demerit Points, $300 fine.

    On top of that, you can be charged under the Road Traffic Act  for leaving your taxi unattended without reasonable cause or excuse.
