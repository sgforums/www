    Honestly I dont like the term obk, that is only true when there is a major crisis like SARS or the crash of 1997. (even the 2009 one not so killer).

    So its all mental, work hard, drive smart, get rest, learn the ropes (yourself thru your own experiences daily) and have a proper attitude. At the most you'll get a 10-30% drop and only for short periods of say 1 week to 10 days (usually after Christmas)

    Tell me if you work like any normal worker 8 hours solid (9 hours if include a break in between), you cannot make it?

    8 hours means no fooling around, no unnecesarry lining up (like say if Changi airport got queue to PIE or very long), no stopping to sms/whatsapp/palringo etc, no relax one corner except during a break (you either take 1 hour straight or break it up into 4 x 15mins, 3 x 20 mins or 2x 30 mins). If you do this I fail to see how you can fail.

    Of course you dont be dumb and drive aimlessly at Tuas after 10 or 11pm and similar areas, where got nobody). You always head to the source of pax, you dont be choosy, sit down one corner hoping for call or durian job. If wait for call also never more than 10 mins, if CBD cannot get, you go out and pick, even if its Tekka etc. Morning shift you dont skive between 6-9.30am. Evening 5 or 6 to 10 pm no nonsense. Why can obk?

    And this I'm just referring to a system, never take into account luck. Sure luck can be bad sometimes, if 1 day screwed up doesn't mean everyday will. And if everyday lucky sure got unlucky day too. But these things even itself up.

    Cannot be OBK one except just after Xmas (before NY's eve) and the first 10 or so days after Chap Goh Mei. Some ppl say World Cup business bad. But WC before I drive never a problem, and I go back to watch football also. Must know what time to come out and where to find.

    To cut it short - just operate like CT2220, sure can succeed one, if you can match his attitude and work ethic.
