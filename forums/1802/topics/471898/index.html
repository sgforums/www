    THE WESTIN SINGAPORE - OPENING 1ST NOV 2013

    Another giant will enter Singapore luxury hotel market in Singapore come 2013:

    Starwood Hotels & Resorts has announced the opening of the Westin Singapore Marina Bay on 1st Nov 2013, to be located at Asia Square Tower 2.

    The Westin Singapore will hold claim to the highest hotel lobby in Singapore, so visitors will get to quite literally make an entrance. Occupying the 32th to 46th floor of Asia Square Tower 2, the 305-room Westin Singapore will overlook the glittering skyline of Marina Bay, and feature the signature Heavenly Spa by Westin and an expansive 480 square meter banquet hall. 

    The hotel will also hold the Gastro Bar Specialty Restuarant and Season Taste restuarants, both of which use soothing, nature-inspired materials such as bamboo and timber paneling for their decor. Other luxe amenities include a gym and business center.
