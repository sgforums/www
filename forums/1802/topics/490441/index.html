    SINGAPORE: From next month, motorists who have accumulated half or more of the maximum allowable demerit points can remove three demerit points from their driving records, after attending a Safe Driving Course.

    However, motorists can have the three demerit points cancelled only twice during their lifetime. The Traffic Police said the course is designed to correct dangerous driving behaviour and encourage good road habits.

    Eligible motorists will receive notification letters from Nov 4. They can then register for the course at the three licensed driving schools. These are the Bukit Batok Driving Centre, ComfortDelGro Driving Centre and the Singapore Safety Driving Centre.

    The course consists of a 3.5-hour theory session and a half-hour practical lesson under the supervision of a driving instructor. However, course fees vary across the different driving schools.

    Those who choose not to attend the course will continue to have their demerit points on their record for the stipulated period.

    Safe Driving / Riding Course (SDC)

    INTRODUCTION

    • This course is for motorist who has accumulated between 12 to 23 demerit points and are close to being liable for their first suspension under DIPS. This course is aim to overcome and correct dangerous road behaviour through early corrective education and encourage positive road behavioural change. Three demerit points will be deducted for motorist who has completed the course.

    ENROLMENT DOCUMENTS

    • NRIC / Passport
    • Employment Pass, Work Permit, Student Pass, Dependent Pass (For foreigners)
    • Photocard Driving Licence
    • Letter of notification from Traffic Police
    • Enrol personally

    COURSE CURRICULUM

    • 3.5 hours of theory lesson
    • 0.5 hour of practical lesson

    COURSE FEE

    • $103.26 (inclusive of GST)

    WITHDRAWAL/TRANSFER/REFUND

    • Upon enrolment, course fee paid will be non-transferable and non-refundable.

    DISCLOSURE OF PERSONAL DATA

    • Upon enrolment, I consent to the disclosure of My personal particulars, Name, Nric, Passport or other Identification Number, Telephone Numbers, Email Address and any other information relating to Myself, to the Centre and to Government Authorities, Land Transport Authority, Traffic Police, Insurance Company and its agent issuing insurance coverage for Myself and to any other interested or relevant parties.

    • Enrolment form to include theory date and practical lesson date and time. Please be punctual for your lesson and report to the classroom at level 4 for your theory lesson. Report to level 3 information counter for your practical lesson. Bring along your NRIC or Identification Card and Photocard driving licence when attending your course.
