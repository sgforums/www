    Use EZ link to pay for your SMRT cab fares

    Come next month, commuters will be able to use their EZ Link cards to pay for their cab fares on board SMRT taxis.

    About 600 of its fleet of 3,000 taxis will be installed with the card reader first, with the rest progressively installed with the readers by March next year.

    SMRT is the first taxi operator to allow the use of EZ link cards to be used in cabs.

    Other forms of payment include credit cards and cash.

    The company says the new reader will allow the passenger to check for the balance in the ez link card before he pays for the fare.

    However, GIRO-linked cards will not be accepted.

    An administrative charge of 10 cents for each transaction will be waived until 2010.
