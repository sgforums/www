    Usually they will give you the well known roads e.g Linden Dr or Kheam Hock rd or Dunearn rd.  Then they guide you once you reach there.  Cos they know from experience if they tell the driver Oriole Cres, most of them will stare at them blankly.

    For those kwai lan ones who really give the street that they live, I use GPS to find where the road is.  Once I know where, going there is easy.

    Finally, open your mouth to ask if you really don't know where the road or condo is. There are more than 5000 road names in Singapore.  You are not expected to know all of them. But once you are told where, you are expected to know how to get there using the shortest route.

    Simple lah. Don't know, ask lah.

    If pax don't know where they want to go, then anywhere is the right place.

    If they can tell you the name of the place but don't know how to go, use GPS.

    If GPS cock up, use Singapore Map app on your smart phone to check.

    Pek kio - Cambridge rd/ owen rd

    Tanglin halt chap lao - tanglin halt area

    Tangline halt chap lark lao - commonweath crescent, close area

    Cheet koh - Bukit timah plaza area

    Chap koh - junction of CCK & woodlands rd

    If really don't know, ask them take another cab lah.....don't be so stressed up, there are plenty of fish in the ocean :)
