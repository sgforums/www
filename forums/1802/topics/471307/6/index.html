    Today mark my last day as a CityCab driver. These are the steps to return your vehicle as hirer.

    1. Park you car at 383 Sin Ming Dr.
    2. Go to General Office and Take Q Number.
    3. When you number is called, go into Rm 10 / 11
    4. The personal there will advise you on your outstanding balance. She will give you a vehicle return form
    5. Bring the form and your vehicle to the back where you first collected your vehicle.
    6. Go into the office there and give them the form.
    7. They will inspect the vehicle, including the undercarriage and will advise you on the repair cost you have incur.
    8. Sign the form.
    9. Go holiday / find another coy or scream ORD loh!

    Additional

    1. Total damages will be send to you by Mail 2 weeks later.
    2. All installment will crease. You will have to talk to Credit Control if you have problem paying your outstanding.
    3. If you are terminated by them and have an existing contract. You are still required to compensate them according to the term of the contract...

    IE, 1 yr contract, 6mths into it and they initiated crease of contract. You will be liable to pay whatever is stated in your contract with them.

    The keyword in the contract is "If you failed to complete term of XX mths of the contract, you will have to pay $XXX" or something like that.
