    SINGAPORE: A French man in his late 40s was killed in an accident along Tuas Ave 2 on Wednesday morning.

    Police say he was riding his motorbike when he collided with a taxi.

    He suffered serious injuries to his head and chest, and was pronounced dead at the scene.

    The taxi driver, a man in this mid-40s, was arrested for a rash act causing death.

    He is now released on bail and Police are conducting further investigations into the accident. - CNA/yy
