    SMRT taxi drivers get bonus in firm's 20th anniversary celebration

    As part of SMRT's 20th anniversary celebrations, SMRT taxi drivers will be given S$2,800 cash payouts over a period of 18 months.

    This is to reward existing drivers for their loyalty and support.

    In addition, the company will be offering a cash bonus of S$20 every week for a total of 20 weeks.

    For active relief drivers, they will also each receive a one-time S$30 cash bonus.

    Lo Chee Wen, Vice-President of SMRT Taxis, said: "SMRT Taxis is, today, one of the key taxi operators in Singapore and this has come about through the strong support of our hirers for the past 20 years.

    "The cash bonus is our way of thanking them for placing their trust and loyalty in us, and to spread the joy of our 20th anniversary as a company."
