    [Date]

     

    Your reference: TP/IP/54531/2015

     

     

    Traffic Police

    [address]

     

     

    LETTER OF APPEAL - BY POST

    Vehicle No.

    ACCIDENT ALONG PIE ON (DATE/TIME)

     

                Please refer to the Notice of Traffic Offence served on me dated 15 November 2015.

     

    Brief Facts of the Accident

     

    2          On (date) there was a chain collision involving FOUR vehicles, namely, (          ). On the material day of the accident there was a heavy downpour along the Pan Island Expressway and visibility was affected by the heavy rain. 

     

    3.         I wish to appeal against the traffic summon against me for the following reasons:

     

    (a) Brake was not effective.

     

    I wish to state that upon seeing the front vehicle (registration no.) came to halt along the PIE, i applied brake but my vehicle (registration no.) continued to move forward until it collided with the front vehicle (registration no.). Prior to that, i had maintained a safe distance between the front vehicle and my vehicle. I was at all time traveling within the road speed limit of 90 km/hr.

     

    (b) Lack of thorough police investigation

     

    To the best of my knowledge, the vehicle i was driving was not tow back to Traffic Police for investigation. As such the Police cannot rule out mechanical fault instead of human's error or omission as the cause of the accident. I also observed that the Investigation Officer did not record a long statement from me. It is possible that no statements were recorded from all parties involved.

     

    (c) Relevant and Admissible Evidence

     

    I am instructed that the damages to the front of my vehicle and the other taxi show that while i had exercised proper lookout and applied brake before the collision, the other taxi driver failed to do so. As a result the damages to the front of the other taxi were far more intensive and extensive (see photos attached).

     

    (d) Compassionate grounds

     

    As a result of this accident i had to pay cash of $2040 towards the insurance claim by the driver of the first car. Additionally i suffered loss of income from not able to drive my taxi for (number of days). I humbly submit that this is sufficient punishment for me. 

     

    (e) Clean driving record

     

    Prior to this accident I have maintained a clean driving record since I obtained my driving license on 7 May 1996.

     

     

                For the reasons highlighted above, I sincerely implore you to consider withdrawing the summon against me. For any clarification i could be contacted via mobile number 9695 8777.

     

     

    Sincerely Yours,

     

     

    [insert name and address]
