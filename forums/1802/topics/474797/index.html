    Man allegedly tries to flee without paying taxi fare -- but is stopped by this hero

    This article is contributed by the STOMP Team

    A bystander confronted this man dressed in a black tank-top who had allegedly tried to flee from a petrol kiosk in Yishun on Saturday morning (Oct 5) without paying for his cab fare.

    According to a description accompanying the video on facebook, the man attempted to use aggression to scare off the cabby and was claimed to be on the verge of beating him up until the bystander came into the fray and stopped him.

    In the video, the man can be seen explaining that he had lost everything on him, and that he just wanted to go home and get money to pay the cabby but was told by the bystander there's 'no need to talk' and to 'just wait for the police' to arrive.

    The bystander, who was the one who posted up the video, wrote in replies to comments left on the video that he felt he had to step in because he could not stand to see the cabby getting such treatment.
