    Passengers can now look forward to "cashing"in on their free SMSes and save on "talk-time" when booking a ComfortDelgro taxi with the new SMS taxi booking mode .

    With the latest technology , simply key-in "BOOK", along with the postal code of their pick-up address to 71222 to book a taxi .

    Currently on trial , SMS taxi booking is expected to be available to all commuters by the first half year of this year .
