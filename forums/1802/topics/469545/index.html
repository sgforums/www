    A motorcyclist was left lying on the road after his bike collided with a taxi along Paris Ris Drive 1 yesterday afternoon at around 5pm.

    A Stomp contributor had called the Civil Defence upon hearing a loud bang and seeing the accident site.

    Here is an eye-witness' account from Stomp:

    "I heard a loud bang outside my house along Pasir Ris Drive 1 at about 5pm.

    "I saw this motorcyclist lying on the road.

    "SCDF responded to my call within three minutes, I presumed there were other calls ahead of mine.

    "The accident vehicles took up both lanes so the traffic along Drive 1 was a virtual standstill.

    "Cars were making illegal U-turns at the pedestrians traffic crossing.

    "A SCDF personnel directed and moved traffic so that the ambulance could reach the accident site.

    "Police came at about 5:30pm.

    "The biker had apparently hit the taxi's rear windscreen. Fragments of glass were on the road.

    "I saw that the taxi was slightly slanted to the left lane."
