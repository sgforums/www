    no prob bro. tips..no lah. jus the usual things...brakes (test it) , brake lights, lights, meter, mdt, tyres (bald?). other than that u dun have much choice, do you? jus check the things related to safety and rice bowl. maybe almost 99% are lemons and the rest salted prunes. jus hope that u get "the best among the worst" and pray that it doesn't break down too often.
