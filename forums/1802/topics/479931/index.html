    Some people add additive is to prevent leakage, such are so called synethic additive with a heavy adhesive and denser properites, one famous brand is STOP. Sometime when we go to carpark to take our cab, we might notice some black oil (not water) batch on the floor below the engine, this show a leakage, either from engine body crack or in between fitting gasket leak. This additive act as a glue, it will slip into the cracks or gap to form a blockage, as such leaking may stop. Engine now more leak proof and can therefore function better
