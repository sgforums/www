    Health Promotion Board Taxi Stand

    Here are the pictures of taxi stand outside the Health Promotion Board.

    Usually there would be some passengers taking cabs from here, since this building is has many other organisations/clinics operating from this building (i.e SingHealth polyclinics, School Health Service, Child Guidance Clinic, KKH Child Development Unit, Healthzone, Health Information Centre....etc) and not just the HPB itself.

    Woodlands Train Checkpoint

    This taxi stand serves the following types of passengers mainly:

    1) Walk - ins from Johor Bahru, Malaysia.

    2) KTM train passengers who chose to disembark the train at Woodlands (NOTE: this option is only available to passengers of Singapore-bound trains)

    Sometimes, as shown in the first picture, there may have only one cab waiting (some others may have gone down to Tanjung Pagar from Woodlands after seeing the train at Woodlands)

    Other times, especially when a Singapore-bound train arrived at Woodlands, there may have lots of taxis waiting for train passengers.

    Note that the train shown to the left of the first picture is bound for Singapore.
