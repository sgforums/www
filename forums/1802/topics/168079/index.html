    And so in correspondence to the photos of taxi stands which I had posted before, I would like to add on the facilities and statistics (according to the respective taxi stand signs) of each taxi stand featured in this topic.

    They are as follows:

      Parco Bugis Junction: 2 taxis at a time
      At times could see more than 2 taxis waiting for fares
      Some passengers uses it (it is just next to the MRT exit)
      Had queueing ranks

      Potong Pasir MRT (Upper Serangoon Rd) : unspecified (photo taken from bus)
      Had queueing ranks

      Queen Street SG---JB taxi stand :Has parking lots for taxis plying SG---JB
      Had a control room, a waiting lounge and some informations on the board

      Allson Hotel : unspecified, only a small place for passengers to wait

      Swissotel the Stamford: unspecified, but had spaces for passengers to wait, and had the privilege for "Airport bound passengers" as seen from the sign.

      Raffles City: only some space for passengers to wait. Lots of cabs waits here for passengers.

      Changi Airport PTB1: can have more than 5 taxis waiting
      Has a queueing rank
      Can see luxury cabs parked at one end of the stand

      Outram Park MRT: 5 taxis at a time, but can see more than 5 waiting.......
      Has sheltered queueing ranks, fare table and a phone-booking info board

      Pearl's centre : 2 taxis at a time, has fare table and queueing ranks
      Always misused by eateries nearby, or even by other cars
      Hence did not see any passengers using this stand.

      Kembangan MRT: 2 taxis at a time
      Also serves as passengers' pick up point
      Not many passengers uses this taxi stand
      Had queueing ranks and fare table
      Specialty: prone to bus running into this stand accidentially in mistakes of BCs/SCs assuming this as "Kembangan MRT bus stop", until after the taxi stand sign and the height limit sign is shifted to easily-visible place (refer to SBS/SBST newsletters of past 10 years)
