    Finally got me a radio at the place at Upp Paya Lebar Road yesterday. Name of place Sin Hoe Whatt if I remember correctly. Lotsa taxis over there.

    Their brand new CD player/radio is an old model of Pioneer, the 6750. Selling for $160.

    I asked for a second hand CD player but too bad they didnt have stock for those.

    So it's either $70 for a radio with a spoiled CD slot or $50 for a radio with a spoiled cassette slot..... can imagine how ugly one of those look. So I got a 2nd hand Blaupunkt radio with a CD slot that doesn't work. Managed to get $60 for it.

    Installation not easy cos I think the last hirer uninstalled his stereo himself, so all the connection wires were cut. The tech had to join up all the wires to the adapter and replace a blown fuse, something I wouldn't have been able to do simply. The uncle there told me his labor charges are $20 for installation and $10 to uninstall.

    So basically I got the radio for $40 plus $20 labor. Not a bad deal I think. Nice bunch of people at the shop too. Have had my fair share of lousy service at car accessories shops so this place was a nice surprise. They don't have many good, high end stuff tho. Not that I'm looking for any at the moment.

    Btw, the 2nd hand set I got is a 2004 model of Blaupunkt.
