Different manual car have different biting points, usually you can hear the sound of it or ...usually when you see the speedometer, each gear change is roughly around 5-10kmph difference....

    The trick is simple. Start off, usually 1st gear in the HDB carpark....or 2nd gear but for new learners, know the basics 1st.

Once you turn and drive out to main road is 40 to 50 kmph. Usually this speed hits 3rd gear already. Control your accelerator lo.... and always look infront of the cars, the distance of the cars between u and the car infront of you...and also observe the switching of the traffic lights. From far, you can also see the 3 white arrows when approaching the traffic light zone. If still scared, no worries. Always view your speedometer, at times...the road and the speedometer..This i dont need to teach u right? 2nd level is hear the engine sound, if you drive long enough you can hear the car engine on when to switch gear or not to switch....and also the road condition also. 3rd level is dont need hear, your eyes already adjusted to the speed of your car movement.
