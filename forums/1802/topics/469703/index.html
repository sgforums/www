    Originally posted by Ct2220:

    Oh Ya, for those member that know Insurance well, I had a very  ignorant questionï¼š Can I take up any other Insurance policy to cover this $5000 Excess ???? 

     

    Not that I know of.  Some insurance companies do waive off the excess under certain conditions, e.g. if the driver has 50% NCD. But, you cannot take up insurance against this excess as it is a form of co-insurance to ensure that drivers will drive carefully if they know that they have to pay something in the event of an accident.

    For taxis, do note that it is 3rd party insurance (some include fire & theft). That means the excess of $5K applies only to 3rd party claims, not your own damage. So, in 100% at fault cases, if 3rd party damage costs $6K to repair, you pay $5K and Insurance co. pays $1K. But, your own damage, you pay in full even if it costs $10K.

    I am not an insurance expert, so I stand corrected. Anyone care to share?
