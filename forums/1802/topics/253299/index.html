Taxi rams into pub, injures two pedestrians

  • SMRT
    8 May 07, 23:03
    Tuesday May 8, 10:17 PM

    SINGAPORE : Two pedestrians were injured when they were hit by a taxi which rammed into the entrance of a pub at Kampong Bahru Road.

    Police said the accident happened at about 11pm on Monday.

    The taxi had apparently collided with a car, went up a curb and hit another stationery van before stopping at the front part of the pub.

    The two pedestrians suffered leg injuries and were sent to the Singapore General Hospital which is near the accident site.

    The taxi driver, his passenger as well as the passengers of the other vehicles were not hurt.

    The front part of the pub was also damaged.

    Police are investigating.
