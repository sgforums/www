    Just receive this email

     

    Dear Cabbies 

    We regret to inform you that with effect from15 Jul 2013, VISA cards will no longer be accepted in our taxis.   

     


    We have been accepting credit cards for the past 20 years with an administrative fee.  VISA has now informed us that they do not allow us to charge any administrative fee.  However, the administrative fee is necessary for the following reasons: 

     


    1) to invest and maintain our payment terminals and systems 

    2) to pay the banks’ fees and telcos’ charges 
    3) to refund drivers by the next working day before receiving payment from the banks 
    4) to absorb the fares in the event of customers’ disputes, if necessary 

     


    We will be replacing the cashless window decal in your taxis in the coming weeks.  You will also be issued a placard to be placed at the headrest to inform your passengers of the changes.   

     


    If your passengers have further query on the VISA non-acceptance, you can advise them to call their card issuing banks for clarifications 

     


    Rest assured that we will continue to promote cashless payments in our taxis with the other card issuers.
