    Last Friday, I was out with my wife to collect our free 'sharp - Oven' which we got through some redemption point. As usual, i have postponed the collection until it reaches the dead end of the voucher expiry date. Suddenly,we sprung and decided to collect it on the last day and were out there. But the time we choose was really bad, around 5.00 pm, again, last minute of the last eligible day!! So, you guys should know about how planned i am.

    After collecting our 'free one', Saravana bhavan restaurant was at sight just opposite side our the road. We thought, ignoring some good evening tiffin is a sin and decided to enter there. After successfully, having our mini - dinner,We are out again towards our home. But it is already 5.30pm and we very well know that singapore efficient MRT trains will be jam packed like our pallanvan buses(Chennai - India). So there is no question about taking my big box in MRT train. Fellow, communters would have killed me if i have done so. During the peak hours, people will stand on side by side, with just few inches shoulder freedom!!

    So, marched towards the taxi stand and got puzzled to see the queue there like for a public bus. Deciding not to waste money!!, we decided to join the queue, luckily the queue moved fast and with in few mins, we are in the turn to take the cab. Everyone in front of us called the taxis and went off. I was amazed why these people are so restless and can't even stand for few mins to get a cab? Anyway, there impatience is good for me. Not long before, i realized that how wrong i am, we were in the queue even after 30mins but no sign of empty taxi. Though we spot some, it will be 'Busy' and within few mins will come back again with 'On call'.

    Now, we know, how the taxis cheat in CBD. It might be harsh to say cheat, but, that is the truth. After 40 mins of standing, one taxi came and he asked me where we are going, when we said our address, he decided to let us in. We i get in to the taxi, the additional charges are already more than $4 dollars without even moving a feet.

    Not sure, where is the fault. Is that LTA is really doesn't know what's happening on the roads or they are turning a blind eye towards it.
