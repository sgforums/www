    Finally i tried Wish ....

    A good one day trial .

    Verdict of Wish : 8/10 satisfaction . Sonata 7/10 .

    Leg space 9/10 : Wish hands down , for both driver and passenger seat .

    Power 7/10 : Extremely smooth for long & straight distance , 110km/h easily & extremely quiet engine . 

    Aircondition : The temperature can be control ( digital ), unlike Sonata , but rear customer might not enjoy cooling effect , due to boot exntension . 8/10

    Absolutely no blind spots , unlike Sonata . Better and wider side mirror .10/10

    Foot brake instead of hand brake . 9/10 .

    Handling is 7/10 , never enjoy it's centrifugal force when manouvre cornerings .

    Minus point 1: The pick-ups , Sonata beat them all the way !!!

    Minus point 2 : Boot space is extremely limited , due to the space occupied by CNG tank .

    Minus point 3 : Took bigger U-turn space .

    CNG price , converted to litres : $1.02/litres

    Diesel price , $0.88/litre .
