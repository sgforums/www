    Hyundai in, Mercedes out as Spore limo

    Singapore (dpa) - Hyundai has overtaken the Mercedes as the largest limousine taxi in Singapore, operators said on Wednesday.

    The Hyundai Azera is the choice of Smart Automobile, which has secured a fleet of the cars which are longer, wider and taller than the Mercedes-Benz E-class cabs plying the roads.

    The Azera's 3.3-litre V6 engine is also the largest among cabs in the city-state.

    Smart Automobile managing director Johnny Harjantho told The Straits Times that each Azera costs around 70,000 Singapore dollars (46,000 US dollars) with the necessary certificate, a third less than the Mercedes E-class taxi.

    The savings comes from a "green rebate," given to environmentally friendlier vehicles.

    The Azera is the first six-cylinder car in the city-state that will use natural gas. Smart Automobile is converting the petrol-driven autos.

    Taxi drivers operators have been opting for Korean and European vehicles since the stringent Euro IV emission standard came into effect last October.

    Copyright Bangkok Post

    The company that has been contracted to do the CNG conversion is Scantruck Engineering.
