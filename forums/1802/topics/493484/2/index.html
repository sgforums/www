    Depends, as taxi driving is a sole proprietor business, like all other business, many factors can affect the profit, major factors are the economy status, the individual personalities and characters, the impact of external and internal competition and customers behavior changes and finally the govt policies and regulation. Therefore, from a holistic approach, taxi driver income varies from month to month, and if you compare it on year to year, it is diminishing at a rate of 5 to 8% per year since the introduction of UG, however, as taxi companies step up their marketing, this high percentage might reduce, which had yet to see till next year to compare. Given that taxi driver earning is in a downward trends, do you think it is wise to be a taxi driver in an on going concern of high cost of living getting higher and higher. Base on all these points, we are unable to provide you a definite answer of how much a taxi driver can earn, but on the modest point of view, not to mention IRA agent around, I can say base on my earning alone, it is only $900 per month/ $30 net per day.
