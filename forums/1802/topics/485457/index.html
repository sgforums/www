    Stomper Chew's relative, a cabby, was involved in a collision with this woman, who had apparently dashed across a road into the path of his taxi.

    According to the Stomper, both the cabby and the woman were charged by the traffic police for careless driving and failing to keep a lookout for traffic respectively in the wake of the incident.

    The Stomper feels this is unfair because the cabby was not to blame for the collision.

    Immediately after the accident occurred, both the cabby and his passenger had alighted to render assistance to the woman, who appeared to have suffered a sprained ankle and was left limping. 

    The Stomper wrote:

    "This incident happened in January this year.

    "As seen in the video, the woman jumped over the road divider before bumping onto cab.

    "The woman was definitely not watching out for moving vehicles before crossing.

    "The driver is currently being charged for careless driving, while the woman is being charged for failing to look out for moving traffic.

    "We've been trying to appeal with the different departments but to no avail. 

    "Although we feel that this is not fair, there are few options left.

    "We hope that by sharing the video, drivers out there will be more alert.
