    U must be happy at what u want to do , dun worry about what others say .....

    I being a taxi driver for the last 8 years , would never tell my fellow juniors that luck is the most important thing u need to drive a cab .

    Whichever angle u see , in whichever job , job is not a gamble , u need brain storming , need changes , need no luck .

    Eg , like a coffee shop , the business no good , cos it's old and requires rennovations .

    Do u think with a massive facelift , or rennovation , the business will gets better ? It will , cos people like changes , like new environment , and it's pretty clear , needs changes , not luck to pursue a better business opportunity .

    Eg 2 , a handphone shop , business and display same for the last 5m years , customer are so stale about the shop , business drop .

    But , by changing the merchandised display , make some changes in the sale personel , improve sales promotion , do u think the business will go up a bit , then current status ? yes it will .

    In business , i always motivate my salesman , remember the 3 letters , A-S-K , to succceed , for better comm and pay .

    A = Aggressiveness , in sales approach , sales talks and sales technics .

    S = Skills . Customer service skills , convincing skills , closing skills .

    K = Knowlegde , Knowledge of products , knowledge of market demand and supply , knowledge of price differences .

    In sales , we never emphasis luck to our salesman .

    No Management teams and directors will want their subordinates to wait for the rabbit to hit the tree all the time .

    There'll will be one careless rabbit to did that , not all rabbits .
