    1) Stop meter

    2) Finger press on "TAP"

    3) Scan EZ card on cashless machine (machine will show green APPROVED and receipt will auto print) PLEASE bear in mind transaction not completed yet even through  the receipt printed with full fare but without $0.30 EZ transaction surcharge.

    4) Finger press on EZ-Link bar on the cashless machine

    5) Scan the card on the cashless machine AGAIN

    6) Wait for Approved for transaction to be completed.

    #  4 - 6 are the previous way before the implement of the CabReward system.

    Check the 2nd time printed receipt Must have $0.30 charge for using ez-link.
