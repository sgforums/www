    "Saddened to hear the news that the taxi driver involved in the accident had succumbed to his injuries and passed away," wrote the MP for Pasir Ris-Punggol GRC in a Facebook post.     The driver, who was in his 70s, suffered head injuries and breathlessness in the accident. The incident near Greenview Secondary School involved his taxi and a car.

    Mr Zainal, who witnessed the accident on Saturday, and shared footage of the accident from his dashboard camera on Facebook. The taxi was seen crashing into the car in front of it before hitting a tree.
