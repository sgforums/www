    Most taxi drivers i been on have been good to excellent in their service attitudes but i meet a few wankers. What are our options in regards to these situation that most of us encountered before or might encounter in the future.

    I;m an easy going person, to me service as long as u do your job properly and speak in a netural tone, don't even have to be cheery, and understand that you are providing a service and i am paying for it, that is fine with me. But sometimes u meet those who act like u are the one working and they are the boss.

    Yesterday i had a driver who i told him to bring me to point A first to pick up a friend which is also going to point B as well. As we were on the way to point A, i explained to him that we were in a rush to for time and if possible, to drive faster and reach the place before a specific time. Note, all i did was communicate that i was in a rush and i mentioned clearly if possible, drive faster and make it there before the time.

    When reach point A, i called my friend as point A is big place and of course my friend had to find our cab as well. Note that my friend took only around a minute to come on the cab after we reach Point A. During this minute or so, the driver was very rude and commented in a hostile and sarcastic manner " why your friend? why is he not here yet" all this while i was on the phone talking to my friend to help locate as well, and " LIke that how to reach before 10pm Huh?" like as if it's matter to him. I could give a couple more example but i think u can get the point of the story already.

    I was in a pretty good mood then so it didn't affect me like angry or upset even but it got me thinking, should we be paying rude taxi drivers like that? My friend was around so i didn't want to affect his mood as well as he was unaware of what was going on before he come on. Beside he is a visitor from another country so i didn't want him to see the ugly scene.

    Next time if i encounter this situation, i might just do the following... for any rude taxi driver

    Sorry, you can stop right now, we will both get out of the car, and i want to speak to your cab company and ask them for a representative to come down right cause your service is unacceptable and i refuse to pay. And if still cannot settle, we can call police in.

    Think about it, do u think the guy will dare to continue his sh*tty attitudes when another customer comes on his cab again? For all of us who take public transport, i hope not.

    I'm not sure if we have this right as consumers. so hopefully somebody reading this can share the info. Thanks for reading.
