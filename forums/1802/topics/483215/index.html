    Taxi driver flees after reversing into 2 bikes at Tampines Carpark

    Stomp | Monday, Apr 21, 2014

     

    SINGAPORE - A cabby was spotted reversing into two motorcycles and fled the scene.

    Stomp contributor zmac saw a taxi driver who ended up hitting two motorcycles at Tampines Avenue 4 after reversing his vehicle too quickly on Sunday, Apr 20, 2014, evening.

    He said the cabby simply left without a word.

    Wrote zmac: "A taxi reversed very fast and hit two bikes in the carpark at Tampines Avenue 4 today at around 7pm.

    "He then fled without any note or contact."
