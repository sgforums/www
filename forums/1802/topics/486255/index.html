    A taxi driver was caught on camera being a road bully to a motorcyclist by swerving dangerously onto another lane without signaling.

    In the video posted by Facebook user Alan Kong, the taxi driver was apparently swerving in and out of a lane for around five minutes.

    According to Alan, he said the driver was trying to make the biker hit him.

    In the comments section, netizens have said that the behaviour was dangerous and urged him to report this incident to the police.

    He said:
    "As a ex-biker, I feel that we cannot tolerate this kind of behaviour on the road.

    "As for the fellow bikers on the road, please remember we are vulnerable on the road.

    "It might not be the fault of the biker but the biker always gets the injury.
