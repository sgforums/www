    Only 4 adults allowed in taxi?
    Watch how this 5th passenger blatantly bends the rules

    Even though the maximum capacity of passengers in Singapore's taxis is four, a group of five men decided to flout the rules by boarding all five -- with one man in the boot. 

    Stomper Jinko alerted us to this video posted on Youtube by MSadamgasm.

    This incident seems to have occurred at Orchard Road, captured on a driver's dashboard camera.

    In the video, the five men were seen running to board a taxi while it stopped at the traffic junction. As three boarded, one was seen knocking on the boot for the cab driver to open it.

    A friend then helps him climb inside and boards himself.

    The Stomper said:

    "Due to the 4-adult rule (in a normal taxi), one decided that entering the boot was the best idea.

    "They seem to have done it before, since they did this pretty fast with no hesitation."
