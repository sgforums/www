How to survive Woodlands (wu ren or woo lang?)

  • luckycabby
    18 Feb 15, 11:29

    Many of us kena Woodlands because it is a huge estate. While it is no issue if you are there on weekends, it can be a nightmare if you kena sent there on weekday nights ( I don't know about weekday day time).

    So how to survive wu ren (but woo lang on weekends)?

    I think:

    1) try the regional centre MRT stand

    2) try Causeway Pt

    3) try check pt

    4) try industrial estates at ave 9, 4

    5) try Marsiling lane, rise,riverside road

    6) try Woodlands ave 5, 6 area(seagate, woodlands 11, etc)

    7) try gambas ave, woodlands loop, ave 10 (seagate, 3m, etc)

    8) drive back to town

    Anymore ideas?
