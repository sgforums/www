  • SGEMP
    10 Nov 06, 22:55
    To me, asking cabbies for discounts is a shame.

    If a passenger ask for discounts, that means he/she may be a person who cannot afford for transport fares.

    And asking for discounts is unfair to cabbies anyway.

    Think about their hard work. Some of them have to drive around everywhere for passengers --- and empty for a very long time still!

    Don't forget that they may need more money from passengers to cover the fuel wasted on their hunt for passengers as well.

    When they have managed to pick up a passenger, it is indeed a good, happy thing for cabbies. But if the passenger ask for discount, then the cabby may feel that his/her efforts of going around in empty cab for passengers may have wasted.

    Furthermore, did the passengers ever think twice before asking for discounts?! I am not sure if there are any passengers who "spontaneously" and habitually ask for discounts.

    So, try not to ask for discount from cabbies.
