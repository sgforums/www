SMRT guarantee new drivers income of at least $800

  • Poolman
    10 Sep 05, 00:54
    SMRT taxi drivers can now enjoy a guaranteed income under a new scheme from the transport company.

    New drivers can earn at least S$800 monthly, but they can earn up to S$1,500 and more.

    They also do not have to deal with the hassle of a security deposit.

    Drivers also get free diesel, season parking, vehicle service and maintenance support.

    The Workforce Development Agency is supporting the scheme by giving S$100 to each driver in its Re-Employment Support Scheme.

    NTUC's Skills Redevelopment Programme is also providing a funding of S$400 to help SMRT for the first three months.

    Drivers may end up ferrying more than just ordinary passengers.

    Said Seng Han Thong, NTUC Assistant Secretary-General and advisor to the Taxi Associations, "We are exploring the possibility of providing a taxi medical transfer service, parties involved are LTA, hospitals, and the taxi companies. So we have to look into every aspect of the issue. What are the required medical knowledge and the required medical facility in the vehicle." - CNA
