    Originally posted by foolsh_2000:

    delgro foreign coy?


    No lah.  Delgro was formerly Singapore Bus Service (1978).  In 1997, they renamed SBS(1978) to Delgro Corporation and formed a subsidiary, SBS Limited.  In 2001, SBS Limited was renamed SBS Transit and the name remains till today.

    Delgro is formed by 2 words, Delta and Growth.  The mouth of a river is known as a delta.  Deltas are very fertile grounds with many opportunities for plants and vegetation to grow.  Hence, Delta Growth or Delgro in short.

    The old Delgro Corporation logo reflects this ideal.
