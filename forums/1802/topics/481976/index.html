    Assuming a normal work week.

    Weekdays

    early am as early as from 0500hrs till 1030hrs, busy timing almost everywhere.

    u can see many advance booking for weekdays mornings 

    1100-1200, lull period. 1300-1400 also lull period generally.

    1400 - 195900hours, especially late pm from 1600hrs, busy time.

    early week nights generally slower after 2300hrs, that's why got surcharges to make up.

    Only Friday night, everywhere is good all the way. Advance jobs also pick up from late pm onwards.

     

     

    weekends

    no surcharge for day, demand starts from 0900hrs, thereafter usually non stop crowd.

    less advance jobs as well.

    2000 - 2130hrs usually lull period, thereafter pick up till early am..

     

    The cycle repeats again..

    Personally depends on where and when u are to, but generally it's like that... AM - people leave from home to work, reverse in the evening. Weekend, people move around everywhere, but as there's no rush like weekday, most more relax, less advance booking.

    airport slow period are early am and around noon till early pm, can see many TD go ther sun tan in the pm.. Which I believe things are really slowing down outside
