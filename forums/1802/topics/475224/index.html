    AsiaOne | Monday, Oct 21, 2013

     

    SINGAPORE - SMRT Corporation Ltd (SMRT) will be bringing in 600 Prius Hybrid taxis over the course of next year, making it the largest hybrid fleet owner in Singapore.

    With this fleet, environmentally friendly taxis will now account for approximately one-fifth of SMRT's total fleet of over 3,500 taxis.

    In a statement to the press, SMRT revealed that the Prius taxis will progressively be made available from Oct 25 onwards.

    Passengers can look forward to a smoother and quieter taxi ride.

    Senior Vice President of SMRT Roads Mr Benny Lim said: "Opting for Hybrid vehicles is a conscious move on SMRT's part in generating more positive environmental footprint on roads.

    "We are also pleased to share that beyond enhanced features like better stability, driving mode switch, our taxi partners will also benefit from lower fuel costs from the vehicle's greater fuel efficiency features."

    "Commuters who wish to travel green can specifically request for a green taxi when they book with us through our hotline."
