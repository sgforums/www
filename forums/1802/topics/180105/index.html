    Hi.. Welcome to Singapore!

    Taxi Drivers usually dont have such a practice. But you can try..

    The meter fare from Changi Airport to Woodlands is about $16. Add that to surcharges of $5, + flagdown $2.40, thats already $24.

    On the other hand, i heard of a scheme, which travellers from Johor to any location in singapore, pay S$30 per taxi ride. So from Changi, this may be $40 instead due to surcharges

    However Singapore Taxis will NOT bring you to Senai Airport. Instead, they only bring you to a place in Johor Bahru Town Centre, aka "Kota Raya". At Kota Raya, Malaysian Taxis will bring you to Senai Airport (30-50mins ride) for RM30 (about S$14.00).

    So add these 2 up, total fare may be S$40 + S$14 = S$54.

    So, in other words, theres really no direct method to Senai from Changi on Public Trasnport. Minimum is to change taxis at JB Kota Raya.

    hope this helps
