    This thread is specially for us to share bits and pieces of Singapore taxis in the past.

    Some facts I have here:

    1) In the 1990s, there are Opel stationwagons for Comfort Taxis

    2) Again, in the 1990s, Toyota Corona are mainstay of Comfort Taxis. Now, only a few of them are still plying on Singapore's roads.

    3) I think the "oldest" taxi stand in Singapore was in Tanjung Pagar KTM Station (built in 1932).
