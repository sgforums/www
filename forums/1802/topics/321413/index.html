    BREAKDOWN OF TAXI SURCHARGES

    Midnight Charge:

    ComfortDelGro Taxis

    Midnight to 5.59am: 50% of basic fare

    SMRT Taxis

    11.30pm to 11.44pm: 10% of basic fare
    11.45pm to 12.59pm: 20% of basic fare
    12am - 12.59am: 35% of basic fare
    1am to 6am: 50% of basic fare

    Premier Taxis

    12MN - 5.59am: 50% of basic fare

    Peak Period Surcharges:

    ComfortDelGro Taxis

    Monday - Friday (7am to 9.30am): 35% of metered fare
    Monday - Saturday (5pm to 8pm): 35% of metered fare

    SMRT Taxis

    Monday - Saturday (7.30am to 9.30am): 35% of metered fare
    Monday - Saturday (5pm to 8pm): 35% of metered fare

    Premier Taxis

    Monday - Friday (7am to 9.30am): 35% of metered fare
    Monday - Saturday (5pm to 8pm): 35% of metered fare

    City Area Surcharge:*

    ComfortDelGro Taxis

    Monday - Saturday (5pm to 12mn): $3

    SMRT Taxis

    Monday - Thursday (5pm to 8pm): $3
    Fridays & Saturdays (5pm to 11.30pm): $3

     

    *City area surcharge payable on top of peak hour surcharge & not applicable on public holidays

    i started taking cabs this week after i ditched my car a few days ago.

    gotta say the cab fare is quite `siong' nowadays. chatted with a couple of drivers and they themselves admit that fares are high nowadays and more people are shunning cab rides. on the other hand, they too are faced with increased costs, their take home pay are not any better. they are between a rock and a hard place.

    as for me, i am considering the following options other than cabs:

    1. join ntuc co-op car sharing scheme. i signed up but have not paid yet. the $200 joining fee is prohibitive given that i only want to use this for 6 months or so. i would gladly sign up if they have a 6 monthly membership. so, i will decide when they call me up to finalize my application. 

    2. plan my trip more efficiently. combine mrt and cab to lower down the fees. there are stretches of road where the train is actually a better bet in terms of time saving.

    for the honest cabbie drivers, i emphatize with them. if the reported fuel surcharge is applied to cab, then i will surely avoid them as much as i can.
