    i'm surprised to know some of my friends joining this trade.

    took class with smrt and passed and now await meter training.

    but undecided to be hirer or relief...

     

    but i can only conclude

    Hirer /  Relief

    Advantages

    1. OTOT, u decide and when u want to drive /  Drive within your time range, anytime thereafter, rest time

    2. Car at your disposal, no need handover /  Must make sure vehicle returned on time, but after that none of your business, don't like hirer, can just return key and move on..

     

    Disadvantages

    1. Higher cost = means need more time on the road to see profit / lower cost, easier to manage, but drive within your time shift

    2. If cannot drive, or go holiday and can't find suitable people to cover, need to throw rental / hirer can terminate partnership with u anytime
