ComfortDelgro cabbies spread joy to less fortunate

  • QX179R

    Starting today, 35 ComfortDelGro cabbies from the CabbyCare Charity Group will be foregoing two hours of their time daily to deliver 2,000 food hampers to 1,000 beneficiaries of The Boys’ Brigade Sharity Gift Box .

    They will do so till the 30th of December, making 200 free taxi trips, which translate to about $6,00 in foregone fares.

    On average, about 20 ComfortDelGro taxis will be out every day making deliveries to some 100 needy families over the next two and a half weeks.

    CabbyCare is not new to such voluntary efforts.

    The 100-strong volunteer group has been delivering unsold bread and meals to the less fortunate on a daily basis for five years. --938Live
