    Dialogue with Christ: Blessed are you, Lord, because you gave me a lamp, and you commanded I set it lighted upon a lampstand so that whoever enters may see the light, which is you. It is not about my own vanity. Often you call me to lift up your light to others so they can see beyond the tinsel of foolish appearances to the misery of the world. We can then remain in the true light, which is you. Let us remain with you Lord, every moment of every day, be it in our prayer or in doing your will.
