    Topic : Compassion

    October 25, 2004
    Monday of the Thirtieth Week in Ordinary Time

    Luke 13:10-17

    Petition: Christ, please give me the strength not only to contemplate your example but also to come out of this meditation with a firm resolve to imitate it.

    1. Following the Example of Jesus.     Resolution: I will visit or pray for a sick relative or friend in the next few days.
