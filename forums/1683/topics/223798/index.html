    Hello there, welcome to Roman Catholics @ SGforums!

    Well for starters, why do you attend Mass?

    Secondly, not eating meat on Friday is not merely a rule. The point of not eating meat on Friday is to make a sacrifice in remembrance of Jesus. So actually, this rule can be a very personal thing.
