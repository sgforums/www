    If you have loved ones affected by the storm, please submit your intention at http://www.regnumchristi.org/english/intenciones/index.phtml

    To make a financial donation to relief efforts, please visit Catholic Relief Services at www.catholiccharitiesusa.org/news/katrina.cfm

    The United States Conference of Catholic Bishops has announced 195 dioceses in the U.S. will take up special collections at masses this Sunday to aid hurricane victims.
