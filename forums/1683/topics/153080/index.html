    1. A Sign of Contradiction    . Jesus was a sign of contradiction, and he calls his followers throughout the centuries to be signs of contradiction too. How true this is for us today!     2. A Sword Will Pierce Your Heart    . If we are true followers of Christ, we too will experience the sword that pierces our hearts. What was that sword for Mary? It revealed itself in many ways: the insults she received for being a virgin mother, the sorrow she felt at the rejection of her son, the agony she shared with him on Calvary.
