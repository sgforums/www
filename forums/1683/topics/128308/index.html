    St Francis Kids Club was set up in 1991 by parents of preschool kids with the encouragement of Fr John-Paul Tan. The current Priest Adviser is Fr Martin Low. The Club is named after St Francis, the Little Poor man of Assisi, a giant among saints, gentle and joyful, the happiest of saints!

    Aim & Membership

    The Club aims to assist parents in their challenging role of helping their children to be faithful followers of Jesus and active members of His Church.

    Parental Involvement in a childÂ’s faith education has been recognised by the Church as essential. The Church sees parents as the first and best educators. Although it is not compulsory, parents are encouraged to be involved, with at least one parent attending the sessions with the child on a regular basis.

    Membership is open to families with preschool kids. We hope that in the process of caring, sharing and supporting one another in the formation of your childrenÂ’s faith, you will experience the loving community spirit of GodÂ’s big family. Sessions are held either on Saturdays from 4pm to 5pm or Sundays from 10.10am to 11.10am

    Club Set-Up

    The club directly assists parents through

    The Family Assisting Team, comprising Saturday and Sunday Group Coordinators with help from small group Anchors. Their main role is to assist families on a regular basis during session time, and
    The Family Resource Centre, comprising a committee with help from ad hoc members. Their main role is to provide resources e.g. library materials; to organise talks, workshops, big group events
    Registration & Re-registration
    Registration is usually done in November & January. Membership is valid for one year and the fee is $20.00 per child for the whole year. Re-registration is required if families wish to continue for the following year.
