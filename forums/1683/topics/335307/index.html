    Originally posted by Fantagf:

    Hey, I am a non Catholic trying to find out more about Novena Devotion.  I was told by people about the 9-week Novena, can any of the Catholics tell me more about it?  

    Can we request for more than one thing?   How effective is this?   If busy to attend, can we miss just one week and make up for it the next week?

    If you want to find out more, read http://www.catholic.org.sg/cn/wordpress/?p=2614 which will give you some ideas and also visit the Church website at http://www.cssr.org.sg/.

    The best way is to go and attend the Novena service yourself and buy a book which cost $1.00-$2.00 I think. Then you know how the service goes.

    Important to note is that you are not worshipping Mother Mary at Novena but asking her to pray with you and for you - not unlike how you and your friends gather to pray together. Big difference is that Mother Mary is a Saint and mother of God while we are all sinners.

    All petitions are granted by God and never by Mother Mary. Just to make sure you are not confused by some who may tell you that Novena is about praying to Mary.

    You can write your petitions to our Blessed Mother and you can request for more than 1 thing. You can marked it as private and confidential if you do not want them to be read out by the priests during the service.

    All petitions received are placed at the alter of our lady and prayed daily for 9 weeks. I think you must do 9 consecutive weeks and no break in between but I am not entirely sure....you can go to a service and approach the priest to ask them.

    If you are serious, why would you want yo miss a week? I am there every Saturday..... and they have service every hour from 7.00am till 7.00 pm. Only 12.00pm is lunch break and 3.00pm for Mandrain service. No reason to miss really and all it takes is only half an hour.

    Many who goes to Novena are non-Catholics, non-Christians and even Protestants Christians. Go and experience yourself.....

    Originally posted by Fantagf:

    Thanks, appreciate it.   Must we write on the paper for our petition and bring it there or we will be provided paper to fill in petition?

    You can write or type in advance, put in an evelope and put them into the box at the Church. Some of the letters received in the week will be read out but all letters will be place at the alter and prayed. You can visit the website to check as I think you can also send in by e-mail..... You can go anyday to put in your petitions and every Saturday is Novena service.

    If you go to the church, there is an admin block beside the church hall. This is where you can see the priest after the service and approach the admin office counter if you need any help. Get a acopy of Dear Padre from the counter available every Saturday..... and visit the bookshop (where you can buy the Novena service booklet).

    Start going this Saturday as last week was the first special Novena leading up to Christmas (yes only 8 more weeks to Christmas) and hope you find peace and joy attending the service.

    May God bless you and grant you all your petitions.
