    HAIL, HOLY QUEEN


    The Gospel Truth About     MARY


    Mother of God, the New Eve, the     Ark of the New Covenant and
    the Queen Mother

     

    The dogmas proclaiming Mary as Mother of God, as the new Eve, as the Ark of the New Covenant and as the Queen Mother may make sense to many Catholics, but can they be explained to questioning Protestants through scripture? We find Mary in the New Testament but do you know we can also find scriptural references to Mary in the Old Testament? How should a Catholic respond to questions like:

    • Where do we find Mary in the Old Testament scriptures?
    • What do we mean when we refer to Mary the Mother of God?
    • How was the 'knot of Eve's disobedience loosened by Mary's obedience?
    • Where do the scriptures say that Mary is the new Eve?
    • Why is Mary the Ark of the New Covenant?
    • What are some significant references to Mary, the Queen Mother found in the OT & NT, and why do we say that Mary has royal authority?
    • And more…

    About Robert Oh

    Bob 'Scott' Oh is fondly nicknamed by family members as the Scott Hahn of Asia Pacific.  A "Bible-believing" Protestant, he is interested in facilitating church unity by helping both Catholic and non-Catholic Christians to understand the scriptural support for Catholic doctrines. For most Protestants – the teachings about Mary are superstitious nonsense, idolatry, and blasphemy.  However, Bob begs to differ and he tells a compelling account with a fresh perspective about the biblical support to many of the Catholic teachings regarding Mary. Bob and his wife Kim (coincidentally Kimberly Hahn is Scott Hahn's wife) have 3 teenage children.   

     

    Never before in Singapore has anyone found a better, more comprehensive talk on the mountain of evidence – both historical and biblical – behind the belief in Mary.
