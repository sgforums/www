    We all face challenges in our lives. To ask God for help is to humble ourselves and recognise that we need God in our lives and that everything we have comes from him. It is a proud man who says that he does not need God and that all his successes stem from his own efforts and abilities. And praying need not always be for 'selfish' reasons. Besides asking God for help to deal with challenges, folks may also ask that God help a friend in need, that God comfort those afflicted by natural disasters or that God help oneself avoid sinful living like lust and prnography.
