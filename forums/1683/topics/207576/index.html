    Hello, as requested we have decided to set up a prayer box. As such, all Masses, rosaries and prayers will include these intentions in mind.

    Orate Fratres means "Pray, brethren".

    All are welcome to post their intentions here!

    We invite you to say one Hail Mary after posting your intention.

    Hail Mary,
    Full of Grace,
    The Lord is with thee.
    Blessed art thou among women,
    and blessed is the fruit
    of thy womb, Jesus.
    Holy Mary,
    Mother of God,
    pray for us sinners now,
    and at the hour of death.

    Amen.
