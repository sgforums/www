    Besides hearing confessions and celebrating Masses, priests' work include:

    - Visiting parishioners who invite them to help with various matters, including house blessing
    - Conducting weddings
    - Counselling for people in trouble
    - Managing the parish's finances
    - Ensuring that the physical infrastructure of the parish is properly maintained, with the help of other lay people
    - Planning of activities
    - Providing spiritual guidance to parish-level ministries (including the choirs) as well as be the final authority for making certain decisions and resolving conflicts
    - Teaching RCIA
    - Being on standby 24x7 to respond to calls to minister to those who are dying
    - Being spiritual director for diocese-level ministries, and taking part in their activities

    In addition, for their own spiritual well-being, priests are required to pray the Divine Office each day.

    On the other separate points, choirs are present during Sunday Masses. Weekday Masses do not usually have choirs present due to the fact that all choir members are volunteers. As for ushers, each parish would have a hospitality ministry where ushers volunteer to serve during the weekend Masses.
