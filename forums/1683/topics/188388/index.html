    The Cathedral of the Good Shepherd,will be celebrating its 160th Anniversary on Corpus Christi this year. A Triduum will be conducted by Rev Fr. Augustine Mundackatt. A Solemn High Mass will be celebrated on Corpus Christi followed by an outdoor Candle-Light Procession of the Blessed Sacrament. A multi-cultural Food Carnival and Performance will also be held on the same day. Further details in Catholic News.
