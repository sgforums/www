    Personally, Ever since I became Catholic in 2002, i've been deprived of a good christian youth fellowship group.

    What I look for in such a group is slightly similar in form to what I have been accustomed to while I was a Protestant.

    1. Regular Bible Study/Sharing

    2. Regular Prayer Meets (may be combined with bible study/sharing)

    3. Fellowshipping (just hanging out together - makan, chit chat)

    4. Accountability (Being accountable to one another - Sharing times)

    While I was in Campus Crusade for Christ, I think I built up quite a bit as a Christian.. and with the constant (we met everyday for prayer meets!) and strong ties with a group, we could spur one another on to run the race and not fall back. It was easier. Interestingly enough, I became a Catholic despite (more like as a result of) all that :p

    The limitation today, of course is also that I'm working and I stay in the East. I miss those days and I wish there was some way to start a similar youth fellowship of sorts.
