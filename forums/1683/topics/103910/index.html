    1. Used to be from Holy Spirit. Now at OLSS due to where I live. Moving back to Holy Spirit soon. Still helping in RCIA there.

    2. Novena Church is not a parish because it is not part of the diocesan structure. It belongs to the Redemptorist missionaries. Basically Catholic churches can either be governed by the diocese or a missionary. They are distinct governance structures as specified in Canon law.

    3. The Cathedral is the 'church of the bishop'. It's not a parish.
