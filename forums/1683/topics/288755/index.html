    Originally posted by AndrewPKYap:
    What does this phrase mean?

    "days of obligation Mass"
    Holy days of obligation are special feasts on which Catholics who have reached the age of reason are seriously obliged, as on Sundays, to assist at Mass and to avoid unnecessary work.

    I think in Singapore, we have 4 days right?
    1. Ascension of Our Lord, 40th day after Easter
    2. Assumption of Mary, August 15
    3. All Saints' Day, November 1
    4. Nativity of Our Lord (Christmas), December 25

    In other countries, there are a few more extra days, such as
    1. Mary, the Mother of God, January 1
    2. Immaculate Conception, December 8
