    SAF Day 2007 Thanksgiving Mass

    Organised by: Catholic Military Apostolate
    Location: Church of the Sacred Heart (111 Tank Road)
    Email: fralen@pacific.net.sg
    Date: Fri Jun 29 2007
    7:30pm - 8:30pm

    The adhoc organising committee of the Catholic community in the SAF invites all regulars, NSFs, Nsmen and SAF retirees and their families to join in a Thanksgiving Mass on the occasion of SAF Day. This year marks the 40th year of National Service in Singapore and also the 40th anniversary of the Republic of Singapore Navy. You are encouraged to come in either No. 3, No. 4 or 5T uniform. There will be a reception at the multi-purpose hall after Mass.

    For more details, please contact Francis (9685 2537), Felix (9769 8141) or Abel (9068 5302).
