    Today, we've made a number of changes to the Grand Exchange feature on the website.

    First, the lists on the main Grand Exchange page are now computed by their average daily price change over the last week instead of a single day. This should mean that very low-priced items that rise by 1gp should now no longer appear, which wasn't particularly useful information.

    Next, we've amended the time periods that graphs can show. There are now options for 30, 90 and 180 days (the 7-day graph option has been removed). We've also removed the 5- and 20-day average lines from graphs.

    Finally, the ‘Most Expensive’ and ‘Most Traded’ lists have been removed for two reasons. One, they very rarely changed, so were not particularly interesting, and two, to make room for us to extend the ‘Biggest Price Rises’ and ‘Biggest Price Drops’ lists.
