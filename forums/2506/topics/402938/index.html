    [Runescape > PayByCash > MOLPoints] - Why this payment method?

    - PayBySMS is very expensive. ($18.00 / month). At the same time, PayBySMS does not work on prepaid SIM, thus not many youngsters can pay through this method.

    - By using MOLPoints method, it saves about $5.00 to $6.00 a month as compared to PayBySMS.

    - Most youngsters do not have an ATM card. They only have cash (probably using their pocket money). Thus, players who only uses cash can use this payment method to purchase for Runescape membership easily.

    How to pay for Runescape membership (1 Month):

    Note: This payment method is only for Singapore players ONLY. It is recommended to turn off Pop-up blocker when using this method.

    This subscription method is a "One-Off" payment method. Thus, there is no need to cancel your membership if all your membership credit is used up. This means, your account will automatically become free-to-play status if your membership credit becomes zero. To re-subscribe simply do the following steps below to renew your membership.

    Before we get this started, you must have a MOL account. If you do not have one, you can create one for free at http://sg.mol.com/.
    You will need this account to top up money (via Prepaid Card) and also to make payment for Runescape membership.


    First Step (MOL - Money Online):

    1 - Create MOL account at http://sg.mol.com/. IMPORTANT! Make sure you are in "MOL Singapore" Portal!
    (Skip this step if you already have MOL account.)


    2 - Purchase MOLPoints prepaid card from the retail stores and top up money into your MOL account. (You can use AXS Stations to top up if you have an ATM card)

    List of available prepaid cards:

    Prepaid Cards ---------- Price (With GST Included)

    $20 Prepaid Card ------ $21.40
    $50 Prepaid Card ------ $53.50
    $100 Prepaid Card ---- $107.00

    Refer to this link for the list of retailers selling this prepaid card.
    http://global.mol.com/sg/portal/en/wtg_MOLepoints_retailers.aspx


    3 - If this is your first time paying for Runescape membership (1 month) using this method, It is recommended to buy and top up $20.00 to your account.

    The 1 month membership costs you about $11.00 to $13.00 depending on the currency rates. This is why you need to top up $20.00 to your account in order to cover the cost of the 1 month membership.

    (After you paid for your 1 month membership, you will have about $7.00 ++ remaining in your account. The next time you need to re-subscribe again for 1 month, you just need to buy $10.00 card as the previous balance of $7.00 ++ in your account will cover the cost for your next subscription.)


    4 - After topping up the required amount into your MOL account (Using MOL Prepaid cards or AXS Stations), you are done for the first step.

    How to Top Up money using MOL Prepaid Card:


    1 - Visit any MOLPoints Retailers.
    Refer to this link below for the list of retailers selling this prepaid card.

    http://global.mol.com/sg/portal/en/wtg_MOLepoints_retailers.aspx

    2 - Request for MOLPoints in the denomination of your choice from the cashier. You will receive a physical card which will contain your MOLPoints Reload serial number and PIN.

    3 - Visit http://sg.mol.com and log in to your MOL Member account by keying in your username and password. If you do not have an existing MOL Member account, create one for free at http://sg.mol.com/.

    4 - From “My Member Page”, click "Physical Card" under the MOLPoints Reloads section.

    5 - To reload your own account, select “Own Account” and enter the serial number and PIN located on the back of the physical card. Click the reload button to complete the transaction.

    Next Step: (Runescape > PayByCash > MOLPoints)

    Note: It is recommended to turn off Pop-up blocker when doing the following steps below.

    1 - Go to Runescape homepage (http://www.runescape.com) and click "Upgrade Your Account".

    2 - When you are at a stage where you need to choose the payment methods, select "PayByCash".

    3 - Read the "Terms and Conditions" and click the "Accept" button. You will be forwarded to PayByCash payment page.

    4 - At PayByCash payment page, select "MOLePoints" as the payment method. (Display Logo may be outdated so it doesn't matter)

    5 - Fill in your particulars and click "Continue" button.

    6 - Click "Make MOLePoints Payment" button. You will be forwarded to MOLPoints payment page. (Pop-up window page)

    7 - At MOLPoints payment page, log in to your MOL account.

    8 - Verify the amount you need to pay for the Runescape Membership and press "Confirm" button.

    9 - The amount for the Runescape 1 month membership will be deducted from the MOL account.
    (At this stage, a receipt will be emailed to you with payment ID, order ID etc. in case if there are any problems in your payment)

    10 - Press "Go Back to Merchant" button. (Unable to confirm if this step is needed)

    11 - Success! you just gained yourself 31 days of Runescape membership credit!


    If you encountered any problems, Always contact PayByCash or MOL, and NOT Jagex. This is because Jagex is not the one operating this payment method. However, please wait for about 3 days before contacting them if your Runescape account has not been activated after you made your payment to PayByCash.

    _________________________________________________________________________________________________________


    Summary: How this payment system works

    Many players asked why Runescape is not listed inside MOL games list. This is because, MOLPoints payment method is NOT a direct payment to Runescape. The MOLPoints payment has to go through PayByCash for processing. Once PayByCash receives the payment, PayByCash will process and communicate the payment to Jagex (Runescape) for membership activation.

    Basically after all the above explanation, you do not see "Runescape" inside MOL games list. However, you will see "PayByCash" inside MOL games list instead.

    Here is the summary of how the payment method works:

    - You make your MOLPoints payment to PayByCash
    - PayByCash receives the payment from MOLPoints and thus, communicated the payment to Jagex (Runescape)
    - Jagex (Runescape) acknowledges it and activate your subscription.

    In Summary, this method is an indirect way of paying for Runescape membership.
