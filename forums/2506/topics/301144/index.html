    Once your inventory is full of shrimps , you can start a fire

    # Left-click on the tinderbox in your inventory so that it is outlined in white. Then left-click on the logs to use them with the tinderbox. You will then place the logs on the ground and attempt to light a fire.
    # Alternatively, you can drop the logs on the ground and left-click on the tinderbox so that it is outlined in white. Then left-click on the logs to use the tinderbox with the logs.
    (From www.runescape.com)

    To cook using a fire, left-click on the item you would like to cook in your inventory so that it becomes outlined in white, then left-click on a fire.



    The 'Cook X' option allows a player to cook multiple foods (of the same type) without having to select the item repeatedly. When you use a food item with a Cooking range or an open fire, a screen similar to the one below will appear.



    After this screen appears, right-click on the object shown and the option to cook one, five, X or All items will be shown. To cook one item, select the 'Cook 1' option. To cook five items, select the 'Cook 5' option. To cook X amount of food, select the 'Cook X' option and type in the amount of food you would like to cook. To cook all of the same type of food in your inventory, simply select the 'Cook All' option.
