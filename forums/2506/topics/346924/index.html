    Erm, for Runescape, there's no right or wrong skills to train. not like other mmorpg games where if you train the wrong skill your character is screwed up.

    so, now what would you like to train first? 

     

    If it's combat, i suggest you to fight the dummy @ the lumbridge tutor (near the castle that you were spawned at) and train your attack (twin sword icon) first option of it.

    The second option will give higher damage. it's called strength.

    Third will give you more armours to wear and prevent hits.
