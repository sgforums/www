    1 in 3 retailers sells items past expiry date

    Survey of 42 outlets, both big and small, by Case spots 14 culprits. Most of the food items past use-by date are snacks

    By Glenys Sim

    ONE in three retailers here is selling food products that are past their expiry dates, according to a survey.

    Spotted: A Straits Times check on 12 shops yesterday turned out one errant retailer. It had two products- a packet of chips and a box of biscuits- past their expiry dates.

    Most of the items are snacks, such as biscuits and crisps, but they include powdered milk for infants as well. The items were between one and 203 days past the expiry dates stamped on the packaging.
