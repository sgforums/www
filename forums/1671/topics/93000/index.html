    post a picture... only if linked from the internet...

    and it's not on a site where things are blocked from hotlinking...

    if your own picture, sign up with a site like www.photobucket.com and put your picture inside...

    okay.. now that you've found the pic you wanna post.. if you use MSIE, right-click the picture and choose "properties"

    you will see the address or URL... copy the whole thing (usually, http://www.pic.com/argh.jpg)

    paste it in the box you wanna post...

    highlight the stuff you pasted and click on the Img clickybox thing...

    click on preview to check...

    if okay, click on submit...

    pic posted!
