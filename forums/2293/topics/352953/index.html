    The snow has melted, the skies are blue and body-hugging spring fashions are finally coming out of hibernation. This season, make the most of curve-conscious clothes with the help of a few key pieces from Secret Fusion. The winter months might have left women with a few dangerous curves, but these body-shaping intimates will turn them into long, lean elegant lines for spring.

    WIN THE BATTLE OF THE BULGE

    A few hours at the gym will go a long way health-wise, but the quick-fix solutions from Secret Fusion ensure that women always have the look of a trim, toned body. Each piece of the collection is made from a lightweight nylon and elastane microfiber that comfortably turns lumps and bumps into smooth, sleek lines. No matter what they slip into, women can count on a smooth, bulge-free look thanks to seam-free edges, while the ultra-soft material glides over the skin for luxurious, comfortable support. The Secret Fusion Brief ($13.97) is a staple for every spring wardrobe. Worn under business suits or lightweight skirts, the brief flattens tummies faster than a 20-minute workout.

    TIES THAT NO LONGER BIND

    Wearing shapewear to create an elegant, feminine appearance used to feel as constricting as an 18th century corset. But Secret Fusions innovative microfiber ensures that its garments are comfortable as well as long-lasting. The Secret Fusion Mid Thigh ($19.97) shaper invisibly trims tummies with its front-fused panel while giving the appearance of a long, lean leg. Creating an hour-glass silhouette couldnt be easier thanks to the Secret Fusion High Waist Mid Thigh ($23.97) shaper. Fitting snugly beneath the bust and extending to mid-thigh, this shaper naturally enhances and flatters the figure, ensuring maximum attention when walking into a room. Thanks to their supple, light-weight material; these garments allow women to breathe easy while comfortably enjoying a shapely silhouette from morning to night.

    ITS GETTING HOT IN HERE

    Flirty capri pants might be ideal for the warmer weather, but when worn with underwear that creates visible panty lines the look wilts faster than a bouquet of fresh-cut daisies. Women can keep their cool and look sleek while wearing the Secret Fusion Capri ($23.97) shaper thanks to its light-weight, cool touch breathable microfiber that wont constrict or bind. Gliding on like a second-skin, this shaper wont add extra bulk as it slims the tummy and tones the legs.

    DRESSED FOR SUCCESS

    Body-skimming dresses and feminine shirts that nip in at the waist are must-have items for this spring. But pair them with inappropriate undergarments and they quickly become fashion donts. With its adjustable straps, low-cut neckline and laser cut finish that prevents visible lines, the Secret Fusion Slip ($13.97) is as essential as a pair of strappy heels when it comes to outfitting a dress. The Secret Fusion Camisole ($19.97) features a deep V cut on one side and a regular cut V on the other. Wear it one way for casual daywear and reverse it for plunging necklines that come out at night.
