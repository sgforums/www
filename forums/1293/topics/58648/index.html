    FFX-2 International and Singer Update.
    Hi all,

    I'm sure all of you by now have heard that Square Enix is indeed releasing an International version of Final Fantasy X-2. What's different about it is the following:

    - Includes a Last Mission (with a new environment to explore).
    - New Job Class.
    - Bonus Disc (including all FMV's of the game with three unreleased scenes.

    Up to now, it is only planned for a Japanese release this winter.

    As well, the FFX-2 International + Last Mission official site has been opened.

    - Download the FFX-2 International Theme. (Credit goes to T!dus Beta for ripping this song.)

    In other news, rumors of a November 18th North American release date are currently flying around, seen at both EBGames and Gamestop for pre-orders.

    On another note, new rumors are stating a new and more talented English singer to replace Koda Kumi in the North American, PAL, and International versions of Final Fantasy X-2. It is *confirmed* that Jade from Sweetbox *WILL* be singing real Emotion and 1000 Words English versions for the International version, however, it is still not clear on the North American and PAL versions.

    - Picture of Jade from the FFX-2 International Official Website.

    For those of you curious, here's a song of hers called "Boyfriend", a big hit in Japan. It is English and has absolutely NO relevance to Final Fantasy X-2 what-so-ever. Me posting this is so you can hear her voice. We will post her versions of real Emotion and 1000 Words when they are released.

    - Sweetbox - Boyfriend

    One one more final note, I might add that the November issue of the U.S. Official Playstation Magazine (this months's) contains a trailer and an E3 video of Final Fantasy X-2. December's issue of the magazine will contain a playable demo of Final Fantasy X-2. Be sure to pick it up when it hits the shelves.
