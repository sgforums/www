    6 Jan 04, 19:29
    hey i got all the ultimate weapon liao except for wakka's

    as for the chocobo racing mini game, in the first part, keep at ur current position and juz head straight (or juz turn abit). dun reach out for the first balloon if its too far away. After u make the turn, this part i cant really help u..but juz try to take as many balloons and dodge all the birds. When u reach around 3/4 of the way b4 the camera angle switch, go all the way to the right, the birds cant attack u there. After the camera angle switch, u should be way ahead of the trainer, so there will be 4 birds, and they come in pairs. 1 pair is coming towards u, while the other pair is going towards the trainer. So wat u can do is dodge the pair that come for u, and when the pair that is going for the trainer appears, juz ignore them and grab as many balloons as u can. After that juz race to the finish line and u are done.

    Note: After the first turn, since the distance between u and the trainer is very near, it is hard to distinguish which pair is heading towards u and which pair is heading towards the trainer, so i advice that u try to dodge all of them. Only dodge the pair that is heading towards u, and ignore the others, AFTER THE CAMERA ANGLE SWITCH.
