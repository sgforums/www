    Personally I feel that being Single is more happier than married. The reason is because I love freedom, less expensive/expenses and no commitment needed. Freedom will be the most important factor which I'll consider. Being a single, I can go anywhere I like and no need to report to my significant others.

    Monetary-wise, I feel that being a single means less expenses. I am spend less money, and no need to pamper my partner. Being in a low-middle income family, money is really an important factor when considering entering a relationship or getting married.

    No commitment please, it's quite tough for a person who likes freedom to commit into a relationship without sacrificing some personal time. And I really enjoy being in a solitude.

    Let's face it, I feel that when I'm in a relationship/marriage with a person, freedom is definitely less than what I have when I'm single. I do enjoy carefree life.

    Getting married means you need a sum of money for the wedding preparations, and money is what I don't have. Without money, it's very difficult to maintain a marriage or any relationship.

    Yea. So I still feel that being Single is more happier.
