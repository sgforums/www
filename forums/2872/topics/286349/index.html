    For the blue part, the phenomenon is called "Global Warming". Smoking does not contributes to Global Warming significantly. It's negligible.

    It's the use of fossil fuel, which produced Carbon Dioxide as a by product, that is causing global warming. CO2 is one of the most significant contributing factor.

    Back to the Smoking topic.

    First I must say that smoking does cause harm to the smoker itself and people around. 2nd hand smoker are more likely to develop cancers as compared to smoker itself.

    That is the reason why smoking is banned in public places like coffeeshops, busstoops etc. I recognize this as a good start to make public awareness of the harm caused to the 2nd hand smokers.

    But what if the smoker is smoking at a place where there is no one? Does it caused harm to people around? Probably not.

    The tax earned by the government each years from cigarettes is substantial. It is almost impossible that the government will ban cigarettes completely.

    I believe the best approach to solving this smoking issue is to educate our younger generation about the harm of smoking. Let them know the awareness of the harm caused to themselves and people around.

    We should also open up more channels for people who wants to quits smoking. Encouragement from the general publics and their family members does plays a major role.
