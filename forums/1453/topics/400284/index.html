Steven Gerrard To Resolve Liverpool Future After World Cup

  • ASKL!
    23 May 10, 21:17

    Reds skipper could leave after a rough season at Anfield.

    Steven Gerrard revealed that he will make a decision on his future with Liverpool after the World Cup.

    Real Madrid has expressed interest in making a move for the Reds captain this summer and after a disappointing season at Liverpool the midfielder could be keen to leave the club.

    The 29-year-old is frustrated with Liverpool’s seventh place finish this season, and the Englishman has cast further doubt on his future at Anfield.

     “I won’t consider my future or think about what is going to happen to me until after the World Cup, Gerrard told the Daily Star.

    “After the World Cup you have three or four weeks holiday to consider what the next best move will be for you.”

    Gerrard will also consider his future with England after returning from South Africa.

    Since his debut with the club in 1998 Gerrard has won a Champions League, two FA Cups and two League Cups but with the Reds poor season the midfielder could leave this summer.
