    Flashback to 1981, Liverpool beat Real Madrid in the final. In that same year, Prince Charles got married. Characters of the popular television hit series Coronation Street, Ken and Deirdre tied the knot.

    Today, Liverpool are in the semis, drawn against Chelsea. We all know that Prince Charles got married to Camilla Parker Bowles and amazingly, Ken and Deirdre remarried on Coronation street.

    Furthermore, Liverpool finished 5th in the league that year. Today, the reds are lying in that exact same position.Gawks.

    This year also has parallels with 1978, when Liverpool also lifted the European Cup. That was the same year Pope John Paul I died and Wales won the rugby grand slam. Today, we all know that Pope John Paul II's funeral was held last friday and Wales are winners of the grand slam tournament.

    Adding more bizarreness to this coincidence, in 1978, Liverpool also lost the League Cup final to the then eventual League winners. We all know Chelsea and running away with the league this season and Liverpool lost in the Carling Cup final to Chelsea.

    Its bizarre how coincidental such things are. Who can say for sure that the Reds won't be victorious come May in Istanbul? Oh and the Singapore pools odds for Liverpool lifting the cup stands at 5.8.

    I'll say why not and good luck.
