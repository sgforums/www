    Juventus offer Poulsen for Liverpool defender Agger

    Juventus are seeking swap talks with Liverpool for defender Dan Agger.

    Juve management are determined to make a new centre-half signing and are offering Christian Poulsen to Liverpool in part exchange for Agger, says Tuttosport.

    If Liverpool refuse to do business, Juve will push through a deal for Inter Milan defender Nicolas Burdisso, whose agent has already warned AS Roma of the Bianconeri's interest.

    Juve are also in talks with Lyon for left-back Aly Cissokho.
