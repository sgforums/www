    Liverpool in Juventus talks over Poulsen fee

    Liverpool are closing in on Juventus midfielder Christian Poulsen.

    The Liverpool Echo says Reds boss Roy Hodgson has Poulsen top of his shortlist of midfield transfer targets.

    Juve are willing to sell for around £5 million.

    Liverpool Given Permission To Talk To Juventus Midfielder Christian Poulsen - Report

    Liverpool have been given permission by Juventus to talk to midfielder Christian Poulsen, according to the latest reports emanating from the peninsula.

    Juve have formally given the green light to the Merseyside club to talk to the 30-year-old, according to Sky Sport Italia. The midfield hardman is reportedly deemed surplus to requirements by new coach Luigi Del Neri in Turin for the upcoming 2010-11 season.

    It's also been reported that Juventus will enter into negotiations with Liverpool over a transfer fee after the player agrees personal terms and conditions with the former European champions.

    Juventus signed the Denmark international from Sevilla in 2008 but he has been unable to impress much in his two seasons with the Bianconeri.
