    There are suggestions former assistant coach Pako Ayesteran could return to Liverpool after he was spotted at Anfield this week.

    The Spaniard left the club earlier in the season after a reported fall-out with Rafa Benitez, who he also worked with during the manager's time in charge at Valencia.

    Ayesteran joined Spanish side Real Sociedad as director of football, but announced he was leaving that role earlier this week following the recent resignation of coach Chris Coleman.

    Many fans have pointed to Ayesteran's departure as a key reason for the Reds' slump in form this season. Benitez did not officially name a replacement, with first-team coach Alex Miller asked to have more of an input.

    But there is now speculation suggesting the manager and assistant partnership could be reformed, after Ayesteran was spotted at Liverpool's youth game against Arsenal this week.
