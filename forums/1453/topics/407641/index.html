    Kuyt commits to Liverpool after Hodgson summit

    Dirk Kuyt is happy to stay with Liverpool.

    The Mirror says Kuyt is set to remain at Liverpool after positive talks with Roy Hodgson, ending any speculation over a move to Inter.

    The word coming out of Italy is that Rafa Benitez has ended his pursuit of his former Dutch workhorse after being informed that Kuyt was no longer contemplating a switch.

    The news is yet another major scalp for new Liverpool boss Hodgson, who has also convinced Steven Gerrard and Fernando Torres to remain with the Reds this summer.
