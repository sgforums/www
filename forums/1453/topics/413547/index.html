Blackburn to turn screw on Hodgson

  • ASKL!
    21 Oct 10, 10:36

    Blackburn Rovers defender Martin Olsson expects to heap more pressure on struggling Liverpool boss Roy Hodgson when the two sides clash at Anfield on Sunday.

     

     

    Hodgson's Liverpool have won just once in eight Premier League matches and dropped to second from bottom after a 2-0 defeat to Everton in the Merseyside derby last weekend.

    League Two Northampton Town and newly-promoted Blackpool have both recorded unexpected wins at Liverpool this season and Olsson thinks all the pressure will be on the Reds.

    "It's always hard to go to Anfield and pick up points," Olsson said. "But we should relax about it. The pressure is on Liverpool and there is no pressure on us. We can go there and enjoy it. If we get one point, that will be fine and three points would be a bonus."
