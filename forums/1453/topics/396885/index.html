    Liverpool target summer bid for Feyenoord wonderkid Georginio Wijnaldum - with or without boss Rafa Benitez at the Anfield helm

    Liverpool may be up for sale and Rafa Benitez’s future in the balance but they are still making transfer plans.

    However, their sights will not be on established stars.

    A measure of the kind of player they will be chasing is their interest in Feyenoord’s
    19-year-old winger Georginio Wijnaldum.

    Kop that: Liverpool are interested in Feyenoord teenager Georginio Wijnaldum

    And new Liverpool chairman Martin Broughton faces a tough proposition selling the club if they fail to reach the Champions League.

    New club sponsor Standard Chartered have paid £80million for a four-year deal but qualifying for the Champion League is one the prerequisites for the full amount.
