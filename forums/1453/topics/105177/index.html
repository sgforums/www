    Milan Baros is hoping to be back in the Liverpool attack for their Champions League clash with Olympiacos next week.

    Baros has been battling to overcome a hamstring injury sustained while on international duty and is now doing some light training with the rest of his team-mates at Melwood.

    Despite the wonderful victory over Arsenal on Sunday manager Rafael Benitez is desperate for his striking options to increase, and he today revealed the Czech ace could be just a week away from playing again.

    He said: "Milan is back doing some jogging. I think he will be out for another week.

    "He wants to play against Olympiacos next Wednesday. We'll see. It will be good for us if he can make that, but we will have to wait and see. I don't want to use him too early and risk losing him for another two months."
