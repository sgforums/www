    Liverpool have emerged as front-runners in the race for Tunisian Hatem Trabelsi.

    Skysports.com understand talks are at an advanced stage between the two parties, but Liverpool are yet to decide whether to finalise a move.

    Trabelsi is keen on the switch and is thought to have made Anfield his preferred destination.

    Coach Rafa Benitez has been actively pursuing Sevilla's Daniel Alves - but that move looks to have faltered, which may well have hastened his move for out-of-contract Trabelsi.

    Trabelsi seems set to leave Ajax and following an impressive display at the World Cup finals - a move to England appears the most likely option at this point.

    Arsenal are also known to have contacted Trabelsi's advisors - but the Liverpool interest is the furthest forward.
