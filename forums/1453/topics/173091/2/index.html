    Finally woke up. Match not good for the heart. Must rest today.

    Comments:

    Central defence really getting tired. Instable offside trap. Riise put the Luton player on for the first goal. Carra too tired to track the player for the second goal. Normally, he could have prevented that. Pitch is also a problem. Third Luton goal, oh thank you, Hyypia, I was saying to myself. Daniel Agger better come quick and fit in quickly. All other Premier League clubs are ready to attack Liverpool after this.

    Disappointing players: Sissoko and Crouch. Sissoko looked, again, totally lost at centre midfield. Cannot get a tackle right, couldn't get hold of the loose balls and just let Luton players run around him in circles. Taking him was a good move and a masterstroke.

    Crouch, for all his height, couldn't even get to head the ball at all. I wonder, can he jump? Or is he too lazy to jump? Dun be surprised if he goes on another 18-game barren streak.

    My MotM: Xabi Alonso. Okay, he didn't do well in the first half but I think he, ironically, stabilised the midfield after Sissoko went off. I was thinking he don't know how to work with Sissoko and hence all that confusion. Liverpool only had that 1 shot on target on the first half. Clearly, that centre midfield pairing doesn't work.

    His passes in the second half were brillant, full of awareness and intelligent play. His goals were awesome, opportunistic and impressive.

    Good job Liverpool for never admitting defeat. Stamina, grit, determination and quality won in the end.

    Onwards to Round 4!
