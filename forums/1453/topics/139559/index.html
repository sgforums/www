    Liverpool makes offer to Middlesbrough's Zenden
    tribalfootball.com - June 29, 2005

    Liverpool will make a contract offer to Middlesbrough winger Bolo Zenden this week.

    The midfielder, 28, becomes a free agent on Friday after failing to agree a new contract at Middlesbrough. Benfica have been chasing him while Valencia, Werder Bremen and Galatasaray are also tracking Zenden.

    But Kop boss Rafa Benitez plans to pip the rest by holding talks with the Dutchman in the next few days.

    Boro want him to stay but Zenden's father, who is also his agent, believes he will agree a move within a week.

    His dad Pierre insisted: "Money is not an issue for Boudewijn but where the club stands in terms of Champions League or UEFA Cup is really important.

    "Boudewijn has had a great time at Middlesbrough.

    "He would like to continue there but we don't know what will happen yet.

    "We expect to announce something next week."
