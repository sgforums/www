    Citizens set to make mega offer...

    With Steven Gerrard set to quit Liverpool this summer Manchester City are prepared to make a £40million move for the star midfielder.

    City’s owner have permitted the club to go for the Reds skipper and anticipate discussing what it would take to get the England international to come to the City of Manchester Stadium, according to The Mirror.

    With Liverpool’s financial difficulties a big offer from City could prove too tempting for the Reds to pass up on.

    Rafael Benitez could permit Gerrard’s exit if he gets a guarantee that the funds that Liverpool gain in the deal could be used to strengthen the team in the summer.

    After a scoreless draw against Hull City finished a disappointing season for Liverpool, Benitez said that he would not sell his captain or Fernando Torres if he remains with the club.

    Spanish giants Real Madrid are also interested in Gerrard, but City will be looking to announce their bid in a few days time and would likely out-bid Real for the 29-year-old star.
