    Macia in Italy as Liverpool go for AC Milan midfielder Flamini

    AC Milan midfielder Mathieu Flamini is in the sights of Liverpool.

    Milan sources say Liverpool have approached the Rossoneri to open talks over the former Arsenal midfielder.

    Liverpool chief scout Eduardo Macia was spotted in Milan yesterday, sparking speculation of transfer talks with both AC Milan and Inter. Flamini would arrive as a direct replacement for Inter target Javier Mascherano.

    The France international has refused to close the door on a return to England after admitting frustration over a lack of regular match time with the Rossoneri.
