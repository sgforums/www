Fabio Aurelio set to sign with France club

  • Poignant
    21 Jun 10, 14:24

    Marseille set to land Liverpool fullback Aurelio

    Olympique Marseille are set to snap up Liverpool fullback Fabio Aurelio.

    The People says Aurelio is set to join French club Marseille after being released by Liverpool.

    The 30-year-old Brazilian defender left Anfield after rejecting a pay-as-you-play deal and Marseille are now in talks with the free agent.
