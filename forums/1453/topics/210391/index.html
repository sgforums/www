    Liverpool boss Rafa Benitez is believed to have introduced afternoon kips for his players!
    The Liverpool Echo says the team have been having snoozy Spanish-style siestas at the club's Melwood training ground.

    Benitez is believed to have ordered a respite area to be built at the West Derby ground after introducing double workout sessions at the start of the season.

    The area has a number of comfortable beds which allow players to "chill out" between the morning and afternoon sessions. It is aimed primarily at players who live in outlying areas.

    Liverpool FC declined to comment.
