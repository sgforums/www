    Cut-price Voronin gives Liverpool priceless lead



    Toulouse 0 Liverpool 1

    Andriy Voronin struck his first goal for Liverpool on his full debut to give his new club a hard-fought victory in their Champions League third qualifying round first leg. The stocky Ukrainian lashed a stunning 30-yard effort into the top corner of Nicolas Douchez's net just two minutes before the break.

    That was the incentive Liverpool needed to play out the second-half against an increasingly frustrated Toulouse, who will now not relish the return at Anfield in a fortnight.

    Toulouse: Douchez, Ebondo, Mathieu, Cetto, Fofana, Paulo Cesar, Sirieix, Emana, Dieuze, Elmander, Bergougnoux. Subs: Riou, Jonsson, Mansare, Batlles, Fabinho, Gignac, Sissoko.

    Liverpool: Reina, Finnan, Carragher, Hyypia, Arbeloa, Benayoun, Gerrard, Mascherano, Babel, Voronin, Crouch. Subs: Itandje, Agger, Riise, Torres, Alonso, Kuyt, Sissoko.

    Referee: Kyros Vassaras (Greece)
