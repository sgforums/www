    Inter Milan boss Benitez already eyeing Man City job

    New Inter Milan boss Rafa Benitez is already eyeing a return to the Premiership.

    The Daily Star Sunday says Benitez is already plotting a shock return to the Premier League – and would love to boss Man City.

    Benitez’s controversial Liverpool reign ended with his switch to Inter Milan after six years on Merseyside.

    But the Spaniard and his family loved their stay in England with his two daughters Claudia, 11, and Agatha, 8, flourishing in English schools. So much so that Benitez and his wife Montse have decided not to sell of their luxurious Liverpool home.

    They want to keep the 5million Wirral mansion for the Benitezes plan to return in two years time when his Inter contract runs out.
