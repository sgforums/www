Juventus and Liverpool Reached An Agreement For Poulsen

  • ASKL!
    11 Aug 10, 09:14

    The Bianconeri and Reds have arrived at an agreement...

    Juventus and Liverpool have reportedly come to an agreement over midfielder Christian Polusen.

    According to Tuttomercatoweb, the Bianconeri are willing to offload the Danish international for €5.7 million, with €1 million in bonuses should certain objectives be met by season's end.

    There are also little details which need to be finalised in order for the move to be made official, which are expected to be completed in the next 48 hours.

    The midfielder has already undergone a medical examination by the Reds, and passed.

    Poulsen will skip Denmark's friendly encounter against World Cup semi-finalists Germany in order to sort out his club future.

    He could also make a shock appearance for the Reds in their season-opener against Arsenal this weekend.
