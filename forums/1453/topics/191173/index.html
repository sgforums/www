    Liverpool striker Fernando Morientes says he's not prepared to hang around at Anfield if it means sitting on the bench.

    The former Real Madrid hitman has never found his form since Rafa brought him in and he looks nailed on to leave over the summer.

    Spain overlooked him when they named their World Cup squad earlier in the week and he's now officially sulking as a result.

    Morientes said: "I've been at Liverpool for a year and a half and with injuries and other things I've never felt right.

    "I'll have to speak to the manager to see if he wants me but I can't stay if I don't play.

    "I left Madrid because I didn't feel useful.

    "If I'd played more, if I'd scored 15 or 20 more goals, I'd have been able to expect something better but I haven't played well.

    "My playing style was my guarantee but the terrible year I've had has put paid to that."

    Rafa is already making moves in the transfer market for replacements as he looks to offload Morientes along with Djibril Cisse.
