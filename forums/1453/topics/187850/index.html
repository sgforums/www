    Strikers
    Cisse out (I know he scored 2 last night, but he's still too inconsistent and I think he doesn't fit into Benitez's game plan most of the time)
    Pongolle back from loan
    Fowler contract extended
    So Strikers would be from the quartet of Crouch, Fowler, Morientes and Pongolle
    Doesn't seem like Liverpool is gonna be able to sign the right strikers for the right price and knowing Benitez he'll say "Don't sign any then"

    Right midfield
    Gonzales in
    Simao in
    Simao would be first choice for right midfield. Gonzales would be back up to both Simao and Kewell in injuries or rotation
    I still think area is still a bit short so I won't be surprised if another right midfielder (maybe Giuly on a free?) would be signed as well.

    Left Midfield
    Kewell is first choice of course.
    Zenden when fit again would be the other option for injuries and rotation.
    Riise would also be another option if we have the luxury of fit left backs and the game needs Riise in left midfield

    Central Midfield
    Sissoko first pick for holding role
    Hamman as back up for holding role with perhaps Jack Hobbs being groomed as replacement in that position
    Gerrard-Alonso first pick central partnership
    Zenden may also be another option as an attacking central midfielder should anyone be injured

    Now for the defence. One thing to note is that any good manager does not rotate the defence unless he has the luxury of great players to rotate amongst which also means he has spent money on them which is a DAMN LUXURY to have that probably only Chelsea can afford! Also defenders don't get injured as often and as likely as attacking players in midfield or strikers by nature that the other players don't tackle defenders! All this means that you don't need to have that many defenders as there is little variation and less injuries.

    Right back
    Finnan first choice
    Kromkamp second choice
    Antonio Barragan to make his debut at some stage next season

    Left back
    Riise first choice
    Fabio Aurelio in from Valencia on a free transfer 2nd choice
    Stephen Warnock 3rd choice back up. If he doesn't like it, he'll be sold
    Traore to be sold in the summer

    Centre backs
    Carragher-Hyppia partnership to start next season and see if Hyppia still has it in him
    Agger to be back up for Hyppia and for any injuries.
    Paletta signing in from Argentina will be another prospect. To be groomed as replacement for Carragher.

    Goalkeeper
    Who else? Reina DA MAN!
    Carson to be reserve keeper.
    Dudek to be sold after World Cup

    If this really happens, and there are no major injuries to key players I think Liverpool would be challenging for EPL championship and CL finals at the end of next season.
