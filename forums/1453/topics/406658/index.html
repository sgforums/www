    Tottenham target Parker unsettled at West Ham as Liverpool circle

    Scott Parker is considering his future at West Ham United.

    The Daily Mail says West Ham are locked on a collision course with Parker after flatly rejecting a £7million offer from Tottenham.

    Hammers co-chairman David Sullivan has vowed once again not to sell Parker 'at any price' and has offered him a new five-year contract worth £20m.

    But Sullivan's stance is frustrating the 29-year-old, who is far from comfortable in his role as the only West Ham player not for sale, and for whom the idea of one more fling in the Champions League has a certain appeal.

    Aston Villa are also interested in Parker and Liverpool will be too if Javier Mascherano gets his wish of a transfer to Inter Milan.
