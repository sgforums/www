Fernando Torres Wants David Villa At Liverpool

  • Poolman
    16 Jun 09, 19:06

    David Villa would be playing his football at Anfield next season if his Spain team-mate had anything to do with it...

    Torres has stated that if it were down to him then Liverpool's one major signing of the summer would be to take David Villa to Merseyside from Valencia.

    The Reds' striker would enjoy teaming up with his international strike-partner on a weekly basis in England and has told Rafa Benitez that he should see what could happen.

    With clubs such as Barcelona, Manchester United and Chelsea believed to be mooting €50 million bids though, Torres recognises that Liverpool joining the chase may not be feasible.

    "Rafa Benitez makes the signings and I'm sure those who come will improve us," he is quoted as saying by the Daily Star.

    "If it was me I would bring David Villa. But that may not be possible.

    "We play well together and link up so well. It's a pleasure to play with him.

    "I don't know if we are the best partnership in the world but we are European champions and that is almost the same."

    Lucas Brown, Goal.com
