    Here's a list of the finalists and winners in each World Cup

    1930 Uruguay Hosts : Uruguay 4 - Argentina 2
    1934 Italy Hosts : Italy 2 - Czechoslovakia 1
    1938 France Hosts : Italy 4 - Hungary 2
    1950 Brazil Hosts : Uruguay winner (this world cup was not a knockout format)
    1954 Switzerland Hosts : West Germany 3 - Hungary 2
    1958 Sweden Hosts : Brazil 5 - Sweden 2
    1962 Chile Hosts : Brazil 3 - Czechoslovakia 1
    1966 England Hosts : England 4 - West Germany 2 (after extra time)
    1970 Mexico Hosts : Brazil 4 - Italy 1
    1974 West Germany Hosts : West Germany 2 - Holland 1
    1978 Argentina Hosts : Argentina 3 - Holland 1
    1982 Spain Hosts : Italy 3 - West Germany 1
    1986 Mexico Hosts : Argentina 3 - West Germany 2
    1990 Italy Hosts : West Germany 1 - Argentina 0
    1994 USA Hosts : Brazil 3 - Italy 2 (on penalties after 0-0 extra time)
    1998 France Hosts : France 3 - Brazil 0
    2002 South Korea and Japan Hosts : Brazil 2 - Germany 0
