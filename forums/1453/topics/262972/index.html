    Carson Offered Liverpool Chance

    England Under-21 goalkeeper Scott Carson will be part of Liverpool's first-team squad next season.

    Carson, who had a spell on loan at relegated Charlton, is expected to push Jose Reina for a place in the starting line-up.

    Liverpool manager Rafael Benitez has been pleased with the former Leeds goalkeeper's progress.

    "I was speaking to Scott recently and I told him he will be part of our first-team squad next season," Benitez told Liverpool's website.

    "He must fight with Pepe Reina now for a starting place. We knew when we fought off Chelsea to sign him he was a talented goalkeeper.

    "He got some good experience last season and now he is coming back to us."
