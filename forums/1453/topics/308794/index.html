    Schalke full-back Rafinha talks up Liverpool move

    Brazilian full-back Rafinha has revealed he would be interested in a move to Liverpool.

    The Schalke defender is under contract with the German club until 2011 but would relish a summer switch to Anfield.

    'Liverpool is a top destination,' the 22-year-old said in the Sunday Mirror.

    'If a move would suit Schalke as well as myself, we can discuss it.'

    Schalke director of football Andreas Muller said: 'It is good that our players are being linked with such prestigious clubs, but we don't want to let anyone go.'
