    Real Madrid Diarra pair offered to Man Utd, Liverpool

    Real Madrid midfielders Lassana Diarra and Mahamadou Diarra are being offered to English clubs.

    Marca says Real Madrid are ready to hawk their cast-offs to English suitors after building a squad Jose Mourinho believes can compete for La Liga and the Champions League this summer.

    Former Chelsea, Arsenal and Portsmouth midfielder Lassana Diarra has been linked to Liverpool, but Roy Hodgson has already signed Christian Poulsen in anticipation of Javier Mascherano's departure.

    Manchester United were linked with Mahamadou Diarra in the winter and may still be in the market for a tough-tackling midfielder if the price is right.
