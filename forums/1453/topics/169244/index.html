  • aTasteOfVintage

    And just to clarify in case some of the readers may be confused.

    This Toyota Club World Championships is only the 2nd ever. This new format of club champions for their respective continents contesting the cup was introduced in 2000 and held in Brazil where Corrinthians eventually won. Real Madrid and Manchester United represented Europe back then. It was scrapped for 4 years and brought back only this year in Japan.

    The Toyota Cup( European vs South American Cup ) is the equivalent and have been played since 1981. It's only involves Europe and South America.

    Hope this bit of info helps.
