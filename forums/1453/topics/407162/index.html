    Benayoun coy over Liverpool ace Torres following him to Chelsea

    Chelsea midfielder Yossi Benayoun has played questions about good friend Fernando Torres' future with a straight bat.

    Torres this week has pledged his commitment to Liverpool after a summer linking him with Chelsea.

    Former Reds teammate Benayoun said yesterday: “I am a very good friend of Fernando. We speak every day but not about his future.

    “Do I want him at Chelsea? He is a great player and any team in the world would want a player like Fernando.”
