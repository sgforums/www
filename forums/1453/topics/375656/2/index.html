    Liverpool complete signing of Maxi Rodriguez for £1.5million


    Maxi Rodriguez

     

    Liverpool have clinched a deal to bring Atletico Madrid 's Argentine midfielder Maxi Rodriguez to Anfield, signing him on an 18 month contract.

    The 29 year-old has been at Atletico Madrid since 2005 and was also rumoured to be wanted by Boca Juniors.  Rodriguez will link up at Liverpool with compatriot Javier Mascherano and former team mate Fernando Torres .

    Liverpool boss Rafa Benitez will be delighted to have captured Rodriguez for a knock-down price, as he had been told that he must sell before bringing in new faces at Anfield.  However, Atletico were eager to sell or else face the prospect of losing the player for free when his contract expired in the summer.

    Rodriguez has 32 international caps and 9 goals to his name.  He is set to make his Liverpool debut in Sunday's crunch clash against fellow Champions League qualification contenders Tottenham at Anfield
