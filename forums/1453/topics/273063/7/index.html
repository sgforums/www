    New feature!

    Think your season is over? Think again! The FPL Fantasy Cup starts in Gameweek 18 (December 22nd) and all existing teams are automatically entered.
    Fantasy Cup - Qualifying

    Gameweek 18 (December 22nd) is a qualifying round. The top 1,048,576 scorers in Gameweek 18 will enter the first round in Gameweek 19 (December 26th).

    The Gameweek 18 score used will not include any transfer points deductions. If there are more than 1,048,576 qualifiers then there will be a random draw between the lowest scoring teams to see who qualifies.
    Fantasy Cup - The knock-out rounds

    Each qualifying Fantasy Premier League team will be randomly drawn against another in the first round. The winner (the team with the highest gameweek score minus transfer points), will progress to the second round and another random draw, the losers are out! This process continues until the final Gameweek when the two remaining teams contest the FPL Fantasy Cup final.

    Remember. This season, transfer points are deducted from your cup score!

    If a cup match is drawn, then the following tie-breaks will be applied until a winner is found:

    1. Most goals scored in the Gameweek
    2. Fewest goals conceded in the Gameweek
    3. Virtual coin toss
