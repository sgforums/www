    DUDEK: RAFA WILL WIN PREMIERSHIP

    Jerzy Dudek believes Rafa Benitez will soon end Liverpool's tortuous wait to win the Premiership.

    Dudek claims the current Reds squad is the best he's been a part of during his five years on Merseyside. And he reckons it's only one or two players shy of being a championship-winning outfit.

    "We are all happy with the way we have played in the Premiership in the second half of the season and I feel we are not too far away from winning it," the Pole told LFC Magazine.

    "Rafa knows what he has to do to take the club across the finishing line. If he is able to bring in quality players in the summer in one or two key positions, then we will be very close next season.

    "If it doesn't happen next year, then the Premiership will be at Liverpool soon."

    Despite his confidence that the league title will soon be coming home, Dudek is one man who won't be around to enjoy it.

    The 34-year-old will move on in the summer in a bid to get regular first-team football Â– a decision he has made with a heavy heart.

    "It makes me a little sad that I won't be here on the pitch to enjoy a title success but I will be watching from wherever I am. I will be as happy as any supporter from the Kop," he said.

    Meanwhile, Dudek also spoke of his admiration for the man who has taken his place as Liverpool number one.

    He said: "Everything Pepe Reina does he does well. He does things in a way that gives confidence to the rest of the team.

    "He will come for crosses all the time in certain areas, which takes the pressure off the defence, and his distribution of the ball is better than some midfielders. He can launch the quickest counter-attacks in the Premiership.

    "Since he has come here we have always done well Â– and the two things are not a coincidence."
