    The injury is likely to see Kewell sidelined for six weeks and means he will be missing for the kick-off of The Premiership on August 13.

    Kewell has plagued by a series of ailments over the last year and this new injury is a major blow to his hopes of holding down a regular place in Rafa Benitez's plans.
