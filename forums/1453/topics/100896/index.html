    Indeed Man U got very arrogant especially during its so called hey days in the 1990s and early part of this century. That era was really the golden period for the team and they reigned unchallenged and supreme in the EPL and also did well in Champs League.

    During this time, not only the club and Fergie but also the fans and supporters became very cocky and arrogant, teasing not only the Liverpool but also other supporters and at times going over the top.

    From a personal perspective and point of view those were bad days and I really prayed and wished that if not Liverpool, another team could rise to the scene and be a worthy opponent to Man U and wasnt it just sickening to see them get trophy after trophy and victory after victory?

    Looking at this scenario, it reminds me of something like the cold war last imt between the old Soviet Union and America. Last time cos of the presence of the USSR, the power in this world was balanced and in fact I felt it was better that way. Now with USA as the sole superpower, America could become very arrogant and cocky, if they are not already like that.

    Like the EPL, there must be also a balance of power. Man U became so arrogant cos they were previously the sole power, in the 1990s and early 21st Century. Now that Arsenal has arrived on the scene, I really hope that Arsenal and even better other clubs can come onto the scene and challenge the dominace of Man U.

    In a way it really seems to me that Arsenal are the answer to my prayers and that they have come to knock Man U off the summit.

    This is rally a huge relief for the Anybody But Utd group as Man U were so powerful at 1 stage that it seemed that nobody would ever be able to touch them and they were going stronger and stronger.

    If other clubs especially Liverpool can rise up and challenge that will be so much better as it will make for a much more competitive EPL.

    I do notice nowadays that the Man U fans are not so cocky anymore and in fact they do not seem to want to acknowledge that Arsenal are rising. This really gives me the impression that they are very clever and smart to boo and jeer others when they are on top but do not want to take the flak when the tide turns and the boot is on the other foot.

    I presonally do not really mind Arsenal rising up cos for one thing, they help to balance the power in the EPL and wipe the smile off Man U faces especially from that on Fergies face. Moreover from my personal experience and observation, Arsenal fans are not so cocky and arrogant.

    At the same time I do wish to urge caution in writing Man U off prematurely, Arsenal have not won the title yet and Man U are not completely out of the running. Arsenal must still win more EPL titles to confirm their status as the superpower of the EPL.

    Just my $5 worth.
