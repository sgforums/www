Aimar eyes reunion with Benitez

  • EricDraven
    28 Dec 04, 20:35
    Valencia midfielder Pablo Aimar wants former boss Rafael Benitez to sign him for Liverpool.

    The 25-year-old Argentine is not a regular in the Valencia side under Claudio Ranieri, the former Chelsea manager.

    He is now ready to quit Spain, with a move to the Barclays Premiership top of his wish list.

    'This season I am not happy at Valencia,' the midfielder told the Daily Star.

    'I do not understand why I have not been playing. Ranieri has not told me the reasons but I am obviously not part of his plans.

    'Rafa Benitez is an excellent coach who knows me very well and Liverpool would be an ideal place to go if I cannot sort out my future here.

    'From what I can see, English football is the best in Europe next to Spain and I do not think I would have a problem adapting to the Premiership.'

    Source: soccernet.com
