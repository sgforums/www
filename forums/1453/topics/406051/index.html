    AC Milan preparing bid for Liverpool winger Babel

    Liverpool winger Ryan Babel is emerging as a target for AC Milan.

    Corriere dello Sport says Milan will move for the Dutchman should they find a buyer for Klaas-Jan Huntelaar.

    Milan chiefs are convinced they can help Babel revive his career after a stuttering time on Merseyside.

    A swap involving Huntelaar has also been mooted.
