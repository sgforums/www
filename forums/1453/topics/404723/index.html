Arsenal Interested In A Move For Manchester City Winger

  • ASKL!
    8 Jul 10, 20:02

    Spurs and Liverpool are also keen to buy the Englishman

    Arsenal are set to fight it out with Premier League rivals Liverpool and Tottenham Hotspur for Manchester City winger Shaun Wright-Phillips.

    The Gunners want to acquire the England international for a reduced £6 million price and are considered the favourites to land him this summer, according to The Sun.

    However, the 28-year-old’s wages are a main issue as City would sell Wright-Phillips at a lower price just to get his salary off of their books.

    Wright-Phillips, who has two years remaining on his current £60k-a-week deal, and is hoping to move from the Eastlands club.

    The speedy winger is not happy that City did not offer him a better deal last season and fell out of Roberto Mancini’s line-up, losing his place to England starlet Adam Johnson.

    Liverpool are interested in a move for Wright-Phillips as well and are attempting to set up a deal to bring him to Anfield.

    Spurs manager Harry Redknapp is also keen to nab the winger but Wright-Phillips’ hefty wages could be a deterrent for Tottenham.
