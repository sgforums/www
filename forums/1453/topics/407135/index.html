    Chelsea midfielder Benayoun eager to avoid Cole comparison

    Chelsea signing Yossi Benayoun is eager to avoid comparisons with Joe Cole.

    Benayoun has arrived just as Cole left Chelsea for Liverpool.

    "I don't like to compare myself to another player, I come here to do my best and hopefully it will be good enough. Joe is in Liverpool, I wish him the best, I think he is a great player," Benayoun.

    "I feel very good. From the first moment they gave me a good feeling like I am already a long time here.

    "I am enjoying every day and getting closer to everyone. I am sure I will have a great time here and hopefully we will be successful."
