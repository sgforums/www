    i've been a liverpool supporter all my life. the sad state of the team is disappointing. its been so disappointing that i no longer get disappointed when they lose - and that's really disappointing.

    in the past when the team lost, i couldn't eat or sleep. but now i've gotten so used to losing that i can say 'c'est la vive'! i thank houllier for making me such a gracious loser. another loss? another poor draw? hey, what's new?

    i heard one radio-caller on saturday just before the middlesborough game. he was a middlesborough supporter. in his thick northern accent he said "i look forward to the liverpool game. its not a problem. we'll be unlucky to draw". liverpool is no longer a club others fear. unlike man u, arsenal and now chelsea, other teams actually look forward to playing liverpool. when they see liverpool they see cheap points! there is a sign in anfield that says 'this is anfield'. they should add in fine print just below 'please help yourself to 3 points'.

    i don't see liverpool gaining even a champions league place this season. the aim to win the league title is now a joke. houllier has been given more than enough time and has run out of excuses. its time he was shown the door. its time to make liverpool great again.
