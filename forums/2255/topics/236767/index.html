    I post this tip in the graveyard b4, so I post here again 4 u guys

    Minotuar, bandits, manticore too difficuit 2 beat, u can beat them with ease with ramius blessed weapon buff

    - store ur favors to above 600 til level 9

    - When u start a new day at level 9, do not go to level 10 immediately, do as per normal, just dun go to level 10, suicide at level 9, go to graveyard, use 500 favors to bless ur weapon and resurrect

    - Once u resurrected, do ur usual routine stuff (ignore the forest and quarry, u can go there after finishing the 3 quest), start doing minotuar quest, then bandits quest, then manticore quest after With ur blessed weapon, doing the bandits quest and killing manticore is a walk in the park.

    If ur experience pts is too high, suicide @ level 8, blessed ur weapon, resurrect. After resurrection, ur master will challenge u, defeat ur master and u can start doing the minotuar, bandits and manticore quests.

    Side effect: with Ramius blessed weapon buff, u can't suicide in the forest until the buff effect wears off after sometime (mayb 6-7 game days)
