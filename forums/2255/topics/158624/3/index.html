    well some tips for new players

    try to stay as low level as possible until the master come catches you to fight you for skipping the fight (when you have enough exp to level 2 levels)

    Reason to do so,cos it is much easier to get a flawless fight at low levels.Each flawless fight give you a free turn so when you keep getting them,you will save up your gold very fast.(you will have to choose option for slumming in the forest fights)

    Also try to skip the stages in weapon/armor upgrade since each time you buy something and sell it back to the store,the item has a lost value of 25% which can be a lot for mid-high level items

    Also when you die in temasek town,you can actually revive for a new day immediately but with fewer turns than a actually normal 6 hour interval day.(6 less than normal)(also you will need to torment souls there until you get 100 favors at least to get the revival)

    hmm also put your gold into the bank each turn as you will earn interest for them,also much more important after you reach temasek as you are now open to pvp attacks,if someone kill you,you will lose all your gold.You will also lose all your gold if you die to a forest fight.

    Another question i see being frequently asked from new players is what are gems for.Well they are used to buy special items like pets and stuff.

    k good luck,have fun.
