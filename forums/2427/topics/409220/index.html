    X-RAY of the Week features the Polish man with a bullet in his head – it’s been there for five-ish years.

    The man, who lives in the western German city of Bochum has had the a .22 calibre bullet in the back of his head ever since a New Year’s Eve Party in 2004 or 2005, he can’t recall which.

    He had been “very drunk”.

    Anyhow, the 35-year-old went to the doctor to have what he thought was a cyst removed.
