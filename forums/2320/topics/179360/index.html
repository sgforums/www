    DMD1A Forum Rules - Revised 20/2/2006

    1. No racism or sexual harrassment.

    2. Personal flaming depending on serverity may be edited out of a post. Excessive flaming will be dealt with on a case by case basis.

    3. Repeated spam will be deleted. Excessive spam will be dealt with on a case by case basis.

    4. If you make an accusation against someone, you must provide evidence for it to be considered legitimate. Otherwise it will be treated as flaming and be removed.

    5. If we feel a posted topic is in the incorrect forum, we may move it. If a reply to a topic is unrelated to the topic, it will be treated like spam.

    6. No foul or inappropriate material. If you feel you have to post these, post a link to the picture so a user can make their own choice about whether or not to view the item.

    7. In order for this class forum to thrive and grow, members must attempt to be an overall positive influence to it. If you are a repeatedly negative influence, you may be informed of your actions and dealt with accordingly.

    8. NO POSTING OF CLASS PHOTOS HERE. THIS IS NOT A SECURE FORUM.

    9. No requesting or sharing of files of any kind in this forum.
