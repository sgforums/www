    we made our first challenge of the day on a group of guys whom we encountered last week, and we lost. nevertheless, we shook their hands as a gesture of sportmanship.

    surprised to see stefan, someone whom doesnt swim very much, could kayak pretty well.

    and the guys went second round of kayaking because cheryl (another dry duck) needed some stand-in lifeguards, which explains my fatigue afterwards.

    it was one of the latest nights we spent at sentosa as we proceeded to tiong bahru foodcourt to satisfy all our starved stomachs.

    Qoo joined in during dinner.

    the entire group was split into two for aftermath. my side went over to chinatown to catch liverpool live on tv, ended up watching them lose. anyway, they deserved it, having to take chances for granted after they took the lead.

    as soon as the match was over, kopi and silver came to join us for like half an hour. silver looked extremely tired, though she didnt join us for sentosa. i believe kopi can take good care of that.

    the night ended right after we had frog legs for supper.

    feeling kinda warm on my way back home and i sure hope i aint in for fever. i dont wanna miss soccer later, and surely, i dont wanna miss work on monday.
