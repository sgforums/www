    What is Paintball?

    Paintball is one of the world's most exciting participative sport. It is a sport in which men and women, young and old compete equally. Size and strength are not as important as intelligence and determination.

    Paintball is also a character-building sport. Players learn the importance of teamwork and gain self-confidence while developing leadership abilities. But, above all, Paintball is fun! Once the adrenaline starts pumping you can't help but love the thrill of the game!

    Paintball is a team sport and is similar to many other "ball" oriented sports, in that 2 or more teams striving to achieve a goal or objective. Paintball has rules, a scoring system, referees, leagues and international tournaments. It is a combination of tag, hide and seek, and human chess. The sport of Paintball is a non-contact, action-packed, physical and mental activity and is the most fun you will ever experience.
