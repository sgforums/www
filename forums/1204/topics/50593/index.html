    Getting There

    Click Here For Island Map

    Getting to Sentosa
    By Bus
    Take bus service number: 10, 30, 61, 65, 80, 85, 93, 97, 100, 131, 143, 145, 166, 188, 505, 507, 521 & 855 to HarbourFront Centre & Cable Car Towers.

    By MRT
    Change route at Outram, alite at the new NEL Harbour Front MRT Station. Transfer to the Sentosa Bus to the island.

    Click Here For NEL Stations Map

    By Car
    For more details on driving into Sentosa, park on mainland & transfer to the Sentosa Bus or ferry to the island.

    (a) HarbourFront Centre Multi-Storey Carpark
    - S$1/hr (7am - 5pm)
    - S$2/entry (5pm - midnight)
    - S$2/hr (midnight - 7am)

    (b) Seah Im Carpark next to hawker centre
    (URA coupon parking)
    - S$0.50/half hr OR S$4.00/day (7am -10pm)
