    Sians Tabloid Weekly Special Feature Issue 1

    The Sians Tabloid Weekly was recently invited to a special news conference held by Mr ATK, CEO of Sians Press Holdings at Siansllerton Hotel. Below are some excerpts from his news conference.

    " Being the CEO of SPH as well as the Raja of Siansgapore. It is will great pleasure that I introduce to the Siansitizens a new phrase dedicated to Mr & Mrs Kopiosatu - "Niam Tee Tee"! This phrase will now replace our old phrase of "Tian Mi Mi" to describe the act of lovingness and trying to siam the public eye. It is with great pleasure that I wish Mr & Mrs Kopiosatu the most blissful of their fake-here-fake-here-den-later-stick-here-stick-there relationship."

    And therefore, from today onwards, we shall use "Niam Tee Tee" to describe any act of lovingness by any Siansitizens.

    The End.
