    How to get there siloso beach all by yourself!
    - alight at habourfront mrt
    - follow the signs that shows "sentosa shuttle service" when u are outta the control station. to be exact, its turn right, turn right and turn left.
    - walk towards the bus interchange. facing the fleet of buses, sentosa shuttle buses are the ones on the extreme right. board it and get into sentosa first.
    - alight at the entrance to buy ur entrance ticket and board the blue line bus.
    - the third stop is ur destination when the driver annouce underwater world, siloso beach.
    - facing the sea, walk towards the left along the beach.
    - once u come across a few volleyball courts and a hut, u can find us right outside the hut on one of the tables.

    Will the event still goes on if it rains?
    if it is only drizzling, of course we proceed.
    working backwards, most of us will be leaving home around 10am. and if there's a downpour at that time, the event will be cancelled by default.

    Who should I contact?
    drop me a note and i will pass u my contact.

    I'm going to be late. How am I gotta meet u guys?
    follow the above guide and you should get there without much of a fuss. i mean you can still contact me if you lost your way, but i just hope i'll be near my bag to hear the ring.

    i would advise u to either be there on the arranged meeting time and venue, or tag along with someone. after all, its better to get lost in pair than be get lost all by yourself...
