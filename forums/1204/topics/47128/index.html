    kopi o - black coffee

    kopi o kosong - black coffee no sugar

    kopi o siew dai - black coffee not so sweet

    kopi o kar dai - black coffee sweeter

    (the above 3 applies for kopi(white coffee), teh o(black tea), teh(white tea))

    kopi si - white coffee with evaporated milk instead of condensed milk. same for tea si
