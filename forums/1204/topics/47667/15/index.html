    yum

    long long ago, used to get this at the Tekka Market...

    buy the stuff, throw in some garlic, vinegar and chilli.. eat with porridge.. yum!

    another home-made thing to eat with porridge is preserved cockles.. just cook the cockles in boiling water for a few seconds.. before that, prepare a glass jar (a jam jar's best), put a lot of dark soy sauce, chilli and smashed garlic... shell the cockles and toss them into the jar... after filling the jar, add more soy sauce and chilli and garlic until all cockles are soaked.. put in fridge..

    after a day or 2, it is excellent with porridge!
