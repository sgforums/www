    gals with good skin
    - can go get some real good powder
    - Chanel's Powder quite good, very fine
    - Virgin's powder(currently using when i makeup) not bad too
    - for a flushed look, the right tone of blusher have to be used too
    gals with not so good skin
    - since you dun have a good base
    - best to get into some real good cleaning regime
    - good base is what u need
    - but still can makeup lah

    For mascara
    - try MAC's Prolash, good
    - to prevent clumping of the mascara
    - use those kind of clean brushes similar to the mascara brush to separate the lashes after every coat of mascara.........
    - remember, dun wait for the mascara to dry to add another coat, it will become clumpy.......

    For Lips
    - remember, if u do not want ur lips to get flaky after applying any lipstick
    - prep ur lips with lip balm(nivea strawberry BEST) 5mins prior to applying any lipstick or gloss or whatever~~

    removing makeup tips
    - remember to use cleansing milk for the face
    - get eye and lips makeup remover too
    - if you dun remove the makeup properly
    - very soon you will have clogged pores leading to pimple breakout and that i'll have to say, GOOD LUCK TO YOU~~
