    I would like to thank all participants and spectators for attending the paintball party held today at Orchid Country Club. It is the second outing in our paintball series and I must say it was quite a success due some reasons compared to our first trip.

    Firstly due to the professionalism of the TAG Paintball team, the briefing wasn't short and sweet yet it was entertaining and informative. They wiped our face masks after every break and the paintball guns were very well maintained. Compared to the one at Singapore Discovery Centre, these guns could should straight and when there was no ammo, you would know it. The 3 metre minimum distance was helped by the divider at the centre, so there were no bloodied casualties this time round.

    Secondly due to the weather, it wasn't too warm or too wet considering that it is the monsoon season now. Except for some spots where there was mud, overall the ground was soft and nice to roll around in.

    Thirdly it would be due to the people who attended the outing and gave their all to win every round.
