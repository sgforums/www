    Sians Tabloid Weekly

    News Flash

    Today at Sentosa No. 60 Siloso Beach, Sunset Bay, the Sians Canal was officially opened by Mr ATK, President of Siansgapore. Also present at the opening ceremony were Mr Wee, Mr joHo, Ms Qoo`~, Mr dotsg, Mr Devil1976, Mr dark_slayer, Miss MeowSusan and Mr Callan among other VIPs of Siansgapore.

    The Sians Canal is used to carry rain water from the beach of Siloso to the sea, so as to prevent clogging on the beach, thus providing a drier, less swampy court for beach volleyball.

    The Sians Canal is now fully complete and will be utilised whenever there is heavy rain on every Sians Sentosa Outing. So if you are free do take some time to visit Sians' very own Sians Canal at Sunset Bay at every Sians' Sentosa Outing.

    The End
