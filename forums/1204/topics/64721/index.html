    Ingredients

    1 2/3 cups water
    1 pandan leaf
    3/4 cup black glutinous rice
    1/2 cup brown sugar
    1/4 cup white sugar
    2 cups coconut milk

    MethodIn a saucepan bring water and pandan leaf to a boil. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes. Remove pandan leaf and stir in brown sugar and white sugar. Continue to cook for 5 minutes. Stir in the coconut milk and remove from heat.
