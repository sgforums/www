    Dear Lovely Forum Mates

    This will again be my Last posting for the moment and I would like to take this; my last post to thank you lovely people.
    I am truly honoured to be inducted into the [color=red]SgForums     especially to The Sians and SNTC Forums. Thank you; to the wonderful and lovely people for allowing me to contribute my post and to all of those who supported me in my thread. I would not be here today if it was not for the incredible support I have received throughout my stay here. I would like to thank the lovely forum mates, especially to all the SgForums Moderators, for their unwavering support. I would also like to thank the most significant person in my stay with SgForums, www aka Da ge. Many of the accomplishments I am being recognized for today are the direct result of the efforts of the dedicated team of people who work and organise outing for the forum and most importantly, the outing Organisers and participants we work with. There are also many others forum mates or webby supporters of this forums, whom I have met or yet to meet them and I would like to thank them all for their vital long-term support of this Forum.

    I'm are here today celebrating an accomplishments - we as a community of SgForums, and those who surf with us should be very proud of what we have achieved. When I look back over the time since I join the SgFamily few months ago, I see that we have made great progress: advancements in assistive participantion; socialable outing is much more accessible; the general level of awareness is substantially better; and opportunities for those new forum mates to join in the SgFamily are beginning to improve. Drawing from my own recent experience at the few organise outing, I look with great promise on our forum outing, one which I hope will soon be a national initiative, where our participants are getting the opportunity to get to widen our circle of friends and share valuable experience, and in some cases, enjoy each other's company. These kinds of initiatives give me hope - I say hope because we have only just begun. Its easy to be fooled sometimes into thinking that we have progressed farther than we have - The reality is that a great majority of them are still oppressed in our forums.

    Many of us here are committed to a vision of full inclusion -contributing members of our communities. Meeting this vision will take a major commitment and a major outing on the part of our SgFamily as a whole. It will also take the same kind of determination modeled by www. We know what needs to be done. Now we need to go out and do it.

    Thank you for this great honour - thank you all for your own dedication and to all our SgForums Creators and Moderators to making this forum a better place for us. I look forward for future posting with you lovely people and everywhere to achieve our vision of full inclusion.

    Thank you!!

    Cheers
