    Contrary to what most people are led to believe, Fong Seng's is NOT the only restaurant available at this end of Clementi Road. The truth is that "Fong Seng's" is what everyone calls the row of delightful 24-hour eateries located behind Eusoff Hall. Here, there is the option of Fong Seng with its Nasi Lemak, The Cheese Prata Shop with its Cheese Prata (what else?) or Fu Xiang with its cooked-on-the-spot meat and vegetable dishes with rice. Today, the place sees them all -- locals and foreigners alike -- hungry supper-seekers everyday , clubbers on Wednesdays and weekends, and midnight oil burners at exam time. Or ppl like us....supper everyday, 7days a week, 4 weeks one month...hehehehe!!!
