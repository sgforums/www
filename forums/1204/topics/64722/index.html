    Entertaiment Special

    Launch of PartyWorld's New Branch

    Introducing Partyworld's newest branch at Clementi Central. Situated at the old arcade and lan gaming center above Macdonalds, it is set to bring Karaoke to the surburbs.

    STW discovered the new Partyworld recently as its reporter was reporting for work at a certain Monday morning. At first look, it looked like a pirated version of the popular Partyworld karaoke. Sensing something, the reporter decided to interview the branch manager. During the interview, the manager revealed that opening Partyworld in a surburbs is to grab a even larger portion of the karaoke market. Challenging KBox and K-One to a piece of the pie.

    Will we see competition heat up? Will we see more karaokes opening up in surburban areas like Bkt Batok, Ang Moh Kio, Dairy Farm, Jurong and Telok Blangah? Lets wait and see. - stw
