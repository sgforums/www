    field report from the outing..

    was kinda late. needed to regenerate myself before surviving another late night..

    the tempo of the music was slower as compared to the last few sessions which was held at mdm wong.

    half of the time, the bday was nowhere to be found. the only time i get to see her was that she was sleeping or chanting "wo yao tu.."

    fish was injury-proned (assualted by three unidentified personnel). fisherman was pretty dormant that night, spending most of the time performing hugging exercise.

    dot was by far the most active dancer for the night. he was virtually dancing till the lights was turned on. btw, nice fishing rod u had there...

    qoo. unfriendly. left without notifying. didnt update the names of people who contributed the ang pow. spent most of the time sitting on the couch with estee.

    estee. did i say qoo spent most of the time sitting on the couch with estee?

    chronos apparently wasnt enjoying himself, for the start. as soon as he arrived, he sat there for at least an hour or so, before considering moving his butt to get some drink and hit the dance floor.

    the casualty count and rating are never drops with tony's presence. but this is also the main factor why clubbing can never be as fun with his absence..

    yes, lynn, i know. the music was a little weird. i too wanted to get down to mdm wong. but at least we had that much space around us. not to forget the couch.

    wee was good company to both chronos and the bday girl until the dying end of the night. i still recall having five rabbits on the platform...

    mistook sillyme as rb's boyfriend. spent much effort getting them to the floor. rb acted paiseh for a while and the last i saw, she was doing coyote ugly on the platform railing.

    dont know which is tare. i suppose she's the one dancing with thhong and susan.

    hong hong and susan was just as energetic. if dot was the most active male of the night, they were the nominees for the female category.

    i gotta head for the platform when xian arrived, coz he ended on my right and susan was on my left. nevertheless, he was one of the few survivors left on the dance floor.

    oldman went mia half of the time. but he never failed to spot a vancacy at the platform.

    mille didnt dance at all. saw him sitting on the couch for a while. the next time i got to see him, it was outside of the newsroom.
