    With Draggy feet....I've visited ChinaBlack once again and found a whole new CB. I heard it was renovated...not only that, they've have a new DeeJay, though not perfect yet...but as least he mixes the music well.(Say bye bye to staggers and intervals!!!)
    The mixes of music ain't ones which make you get higher and higher...but i believe it'll get better and better.
    They play Disco Pop (think Kylie, Madison Avenue, Cher, Tom Jones(???)) before 12mn
    After 12mn will be all RnB dances.
    For the first time in ChinaBlack...i've enjoyed myself. That was a huge (pleasant)surprise for me.

    So i'd encourage you guys to go have a look even if you're not interested(I know some won't even want to go back)
    This time round...Cover Free on every Wed, Fri, Sat from 8-10pm.
    Have a look at the babes and guys there.
    Crowd - Ranging from NS guys, JC Girls to Young Working executives.

    Additional Knowledge:
    Note that the 8-12mn glass and juggies promo only offers soft drinks and gin mixes.
    Rest mixes will range $34 - 46.
    So if you're there for cheap drinks...take only soft drinks mix.

    Chillout and dance.

    ADMISSION CHARGE:
    Cover Charge before 8-10pm waived
    After 10pm:
    Mondays to Thursdays, $15 (2 drinks for ladies and 1 for men)
    Fridays, Saturdays, and eve of Public Holidays, $18 for ladies, $22 for men
    Sunday, $18 for ladies, $25 for men

    Drinks Charge
    $3 housepours & draft beer by the glass and $10 juggies between 8pm to 12mn at Chinablack every Wednesday, Friday, Saturday and Eve of Public Holidays. Cover charge starts at 10pm. Age requirements Female and Male : 18 and above.

    8pm to 11pm: Absolut - $260 for 2 bottles, Jim Beam Black - $300 for 2 bottles, Martell VSOP - $380 for 2 bottles, Chivas Regal - $310 for 2 bottles Remy Silver - $300 for 2 bottles. Members get an additional $60 off.
