    GETTING THERE

    NEW VENUE @ MARINA BAY WATERFRONT
    The never before used venue for such an event is in itself a treasure, that lauds the beauty of the Singapore skyscape and presents a magnificent backdrop for a dance party in an urban setting.

    Sited in the heart of mainland Singapore, the venue is ideal for a two-night party; with its vast open-space, sea breeze and manicured greens perfected with excellent drainage. Fronted on two sides by the bay, it presents a brilliant setting for dancing in trainers & chilling out on mats.

    BY FREE SPECIAL ZOUKOUT SHUTTLE BUS:
    A special free bus service has been provided by Zouk. We strongly advise you to take these dedicated feeder buses to ease transportation worries.
    The free ZOUKOUT SBS Shuttle Bus service will operate from 7.30pm to 4am on Day 1, the 25th July and from 7pm to 7.30am on Day 2, the 26th July.

    Pickup Points are: Temasek Boulevard, beside Conrad Hotel (Bus Stop), Esplanade (Bus Stop), Clifford Pier (Bus Stop), UIC Building (Bus Stop), Marina Bay (Bus Stop) and the ZoukOut site.

    BY CAR:
    There is ample parking space at the party venue and getting there is easy. Head toward Singapore Conference Hall (SCH) and turn left into Marina South. Look out for the ZoukOut signages that will lead you to the venue.

    BY TAXI:
    We advise all traveling by taxi to drop off at any of the shuttle bus pick-up points mentioned and hop onto our free shuttles that go directly to venue.
