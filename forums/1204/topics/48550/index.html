    Basic
    Hello
    (konichiwa - used when meeting ppl who u do not really noe very well..japanese use "yoz" between frens just like some of us..)
    Thank you
    (arigato..more formal way will bi arigato gozaimasu..super formal - domo arigato gozaimasu)
    Sorry
    (gomenasai.."gomen ne" or "suman" - usually used between frens..for someone u dunno well - sumimasen)
    Good bye
    (sayonara..between frens - "ja ne" or "mata ne")

    Intermediate
    What is your name?
    (namae wa nan desu ka?)
    How old are you?
    (nan sai desu ka?)
    What is your telephone number?
    (denwa bango wa nan ban desu ka?)

    ---> these 3 r actually the standard way of asking..very good if the person is someone u dun really noe well...for frens..for example, when asking for fone number..can actually say..chotto denwa bango oshiete..

    Where do you live? (i donno)
    ---> formal way: doko ni sunde imasu ka? casual way: doko ni sunderu?
    Are you free tonight? (i donnO)
    ---> formal way: konya ojikan arimasu ka? garang way: nanpa sh|te mo ii?
