    Organised by the Asian Wakeboard Association (AWBA) - which consists of the Japan Wakeboard Association (JWBA), WBA (Thailand) and WBA (Singapore), THE ASIA WAKEBOARD PRO TOUR 04 is the inauguration competition tour for the new AWBA, which was set up to enhance relationships between all wakeboarders in Asia.

    The Japan leg of THE ASIA WAKEBOARD PRO TOUR 04 was held in Osaka, in August 2004, and the next stop is right here in sunny Singapore!

    Besides being able to catch superb wakeboarding action from some of the top Asian riders in Japan, HK, Thailand and Singapore, there'll be lots of fringe activities which you can take part in! So bring your family and friends, wakeboarders and non-wakeboarders alike, and spend a fabulous weekend in the sun with our Asian riders on Coastes beach in Sentosa!

    The final leg of THE ASIA WAKEBOARD PRO TOUR 04 will be held in Phuket, Thailand, on the 12-13 November 2004. There'll be a huge team going up from Singapore and Japan, so if you want to join in the party, let us know!
