    The new 612 Scaglietti is the perfect marriage of sporty thoroughbred Ferrari Berlinetta and excellent onboard comfort for four occupants. This is the replacement for the highly successful 456M.

    Designed by Pininfarina to epitomise dynamism and elegance, the 612 Scaglietti boasts characteristic scalloped sides that recall the famous 375 MM bodied by the Turin coachbuilder for Rossellini and used by Ingrid Bergman.

    The new model has a roomy enhanced by significantly larger dimensions, a 60 kg weight reduction and a range of uncompromising design solutions.

    An all-aluminim space frame chassis and bodywork construction is used for the first time on a 12 cylinder Ferrari and is built entirely by Ferrari at the Scaglietti light-alloy technologies facility.
