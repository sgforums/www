    i'll just talk about the guns - theyre RAM/RAP's - Real Action Markers [Real Action Paintball].
    calibre is either .43 or .40
    paintballs are optionally enclosed within brass or plastic shells.

    'shell catchers' are provided if brass shells are used as these are reusable.
    [box fitted on right, over ejection port]

    adjustable fps via regulator - 400fps is a general standard
    CO2, tank hidden as stock buffer tube.

    Magazines are prone to breaking [weak internal parts] but magazine capacity is about 20+ rounds.

    Gas lasts for a couple of magazines.

    Receiver is metal - origination: Airsoft/Paintball
    RAM's receivers, parts, etc are made by a Chinese company, known in the west as Classic Army, and are known for producing metal upgrade kits and full metal guns for the Airsoft/Skirmish/Wargame/Survival League sports. [Currently banned in Singapore, except for http://www.airsoftasia.com who do bb IPSC only]

    Models available are: M4 variants, MP5 and Glock's.
    [The Glock RAM got a bad name in the west when they blew up on testing, design flaw or something]

    RAM's also used for LE/Mil-Sim training, as they can be refitted for LE/Mil-Sim training rounds like 11mm rubber balls.
    They have also been refitted for 6mm Airsoft ball rounds as well.
