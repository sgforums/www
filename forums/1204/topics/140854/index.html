    DJs BEHIND BARS - WKRZ 91.3FM 2005 FUNDRAISER FOR ACTION FOR AIDS

    July 15th 2005, 8pm - 11pm, DXO

    Dedicated to philanthropic and humanitarian endeavors within our community, this WKRZ91.3FM-styled event held at the swanky new DXO club at Esplanade will be a fantastic casual but purposed opportunity for individuals and organizations to contribute and be engaged in the cause against HIV/AIDS.

    For a minimum donation of $10 per drink, WKRZ 91.3FM DJs and special guest celebrity, Maia Lee will go behind bars to mix and serve drinks.

    With part of the proceeds going to the Action for Aids, WKRZ 91.3FM hopes this fun and engaging charity event will raise funds for the Action for Aids (AFA) Medical Subsidy Fund for HIV+ patients.

    With HIV/AIDS cases on the rise, we urge you to join WKRZ 91.3FM as we ally ourselves against the spread of HIV and AIDS.

    See you at DXO on July 15th, 2005!!

    Transactions by cash terms only. Club rules apply.

    DXO is located at Colours By the Bay, 8 Raffles Avenue, #01-13F, Esplanade Mall.

    * Catch Rico (host of Liberation) and Jensen (host of Evening Rush) spin during the charity event, followed by Resident DJ Jason T and guest DJ David Duriez.
