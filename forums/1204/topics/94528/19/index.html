    Will I be bored?

    When will the programme end?
    We are not a rigid bunch of people, so the concensus will usual determine when where why and how we move on from sentosa. We all gotta eat, so usually that's what we do when night falls.

    Why does the poster say FREE when i have to pay $3 to get into Sentosa!??
    The organisers do not charge for co-ordinating and the effort put in. They also do not have control and have nothing to do with Sentosa's management, which applies the entry fee. I encourage you to buy an "Islander Card" which costs $15 and entitles free entry to Sentosa for a validity period a year.
