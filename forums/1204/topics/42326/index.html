    EXCLUSIVE @ D's-O Chevrons

    The editor (yours truly) was specially invited to the first ever special event organised by D's-O (short for Dot's-Outing)at Chevrons. The crowd started off with a heated competition at Chevrons Cathay Bowling, where Mr Wee and Miss C lost by 21 points to Mr Dotsg (the event organiser). Mr Wee and Miss C started off well with some good bowling, but Mr Dotsg soon caught up with them, with some strikes and spares of his own. For the competition, Mr Dotsg won 3 jugs of Tiger Beer as reward from Mr Wee.

    Next, we went on to the Karaoke, where the King of All Singers, Mr Dotsg and Mr www, together with Queens of Singers, Ms C, Ms Qoo and Ms Esteee belted out songs after songs which were wonderfully soothing to the ears. Tunes from Zhang Hui Mei, Fish Leong, MayDay, Jacky Cheung and Jay Chou were beautifully sang by the Kings and Queens of Singers.

    Lastly, to end the wonderful night, the crowd moved onto Zes Disco Pub. The crowd went really wild at the disco, dancing to groovy tunes by the DJ. Drinks of course were not to be missed. Mr Callan and Mr jOhO went wild by drinking Samborca and setting flames in their mouths for the smokers to light their cigarettes! The day then ended with a peaceful meal at Toh Guan kopitiam and everybody went home happy and satisfied. Go Go D's-O!!!
    The Gorgeous Crowd at Chevrons: Mr Dotsg, Ms Esteee, Mr www, Mr Callan, Mr jOhO, Ms Qoo, Ms Cheryl, Mr Wee, Mr Kopiosatu(and his 11b), Ms Mapp|ezz, and Mr Tony Tan

    The End
