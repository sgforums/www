    Dear Lovely Friends,

    Needless to say, I have vivid memories of my first post to this SgForums on the occasion when I was given the Freedom Of Speech. I hope you will understand when I say that it was quite a daunting occasion for a relatively junior Crapper, but I still remember it with pleasure. So much has happened since then that it is difficult to appreciate quite what life was like just few months back after the comeback of SgGirls. My generation had barely had time to experience life in peace time before the Forum broke out. My formative years were spent in more or less violent conflict, both in this Forum and in various other parts of the SgForum. I only got back to this forum recently after much disappearence in January 2003.

    I imagine there are a few fortunate souls who have managed to get through life without any anxieties, but my experience is that life has its ups and downs. I just want to assure you that coming to this wonderful Forum, or to the SgGirls or to any of the SgForums has always been a pronounced `up' in my life. The Forum has a well-deserved reputation for its combination of active business initiative with the highest standards of probity. I know that this reputation is jealously guarded by the Forum Moderators and I am sure that it is the reason why SgForums is held in such high regard around the SG webby. [color=red]Cheers SgForums...Keep Up The Good Work People

    I am also delighted and honoured to be associated with so many Forums mate. Their quiet dedication to humanitarian issues is impressive and I have never failed to be encouraged by the sympathetic response to worthy causes with which I have been associated

    Thank you again for your hospitality, your very welcome "INVISIBLE" present and for your good wishes for my birthday. I can assure you they are all deeply appreciated.

    Thank You All.....

    Cheers Cwill

    PS: Hope You All Like My Speech...Heehee!!![/color]
