    [b]Walking on the road
    I do not know where I am heading
    I know I want to go somewhere
    But my mind is closed to me
    As I sit at the bus stop
    I wonder which bus I should get on
    When one came
    I just got on without much thought
    It was a short ride
    To a place where I was familiar with
    I went to my favourite fast food restaurant
    And I got my favourite drink
    But I still felt lost
    'Cos I did not know why I was there
    I sat and I stared
    I still couldn't figure out
    Why I was there
    People walked past
    I hoped that someone would come to me
    And give me an answer
    Let me know what I was doing
    Why I was doing it
    And how I could do it
    I left the place
    With a million questions in mind
    Questions with answers
    Answers not clear to me
    Why?
