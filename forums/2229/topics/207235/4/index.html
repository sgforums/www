If I could find a legal way to test my skills out in the streets without getting hurt bad or hurting people bad, and without malice and anger, I would do it.

    Competition also helps to weed out what works from nonsense. Without UFC, we will still be in the dark about what works and what doesn't. Without cross-training comptitions, akido people think that their art can help them defend themselves in a street confrontation. Some even think taiji will help you in self-defense. A friendly competition with a kyukushin karate, muay thai or BJJ fighter will change that misconception very quickly, and help us learn.
