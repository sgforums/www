    You're looking for a fighting sport that is based on speed, uses punches and kicks, and is safe to practise? Sounds like modern Mixed Martial Arts would be right up your alley. Depending on the intensity of the class you choose, you might suffer no more than the occasional bruise.

    Taekwondo's generally very safe too, though accidents can of course happen. It's also very cheap if you practise at a CC.

    If you want some nice unarmed and weapon work, you could check out Filipino Kali/Escrima/Arnis (all three names refer to basically the same thing).
