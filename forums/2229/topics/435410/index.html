    I give you some tips and you can be considered a semi-pro in the field of martial arts if you practice them.

    1) Aim the vitals (Between the legs).

    2) Pretend to hit elsewhere but main focus is inflicting as much impact as possible on the vitals.

    3) Distract your target. Strike when the the legs are the most wide open or hip exposed for instance when target does a high kick.

    4) Chain Combos. Once the vitals are damaged you will have at least 3 extra seconds to work more kung-fu on his vitals till he give up/cant move.

    5) Smile and give eye contact to your target. Eyes look at his eyes, but always take note of the position of your target's vitals using your lateral/peripheral vision.
