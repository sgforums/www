    I second kumuraguy. Darren is a great coach but more importantly, a great friend.

    As for "fighting" vs "traditional", I think what he means is that Darren has modified traditional ring muay thai from Thailand by including boxing footwork and hands into it, etc.

    Conversely, there's also "Muay Boran" which is the REALLY traditional muay thai which has forms and stuff - resembles chinese kung fu/karate.
