    :) It's the MK guys who compete, not the wrestlers. In Melbourne almost every club has open mat though, so definitely good wrestlers do the rounds of other submission disciplines, but they don't go into BJJ competitions. The match would never end as wrestling is all about pinning, not finishing. MK have defeated the very best wrestlers Australia has to offer in free style grappling. Top level wrestlers are very very tough though, and very athletic, hard to beat without a high level of grappling skill. Google youtube and Martial Kinetics for some of their BJJ competitions. A lot of detractors will say that MK is just BJJ done well, but actually MK does differ a lot in technique and emphasis.
