    Pocketing can be thought of as the opposite of traditional body piercing. In pocketing, the ends of the jewelry are placed under the skin in small "pockets", while the body of the jewelry remains outside the skin. As this piercing heals, the jewelry can be removed and replaced, simply by pushing it to one side, releasing one end, then sliding it out.
