    Misae Nohara



    She's Shinchan's mother. She rules the Nohara household with an iron fist that is regularly whacked against Shinchan and her husband's head. Lurking behind the good little housewife exterior is a very short temper that Shinchan can snap with almost no effort. Shinchan can always send her over the edge in an endless multitude of ways.
