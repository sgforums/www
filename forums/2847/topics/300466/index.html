    All MediaCorp Radio stations to run 24/7 from 1 Jan

    SINGAPORE: From 1 January 2008, all 13 MediaCorp Radio stations will be transmitting 24/7.

    The company said it recognises the growing demand to provide engaging radio entertainment all day long.

    So Capital 95.8, Symphony 92.4, 938LIVE, Ria 89.7 and International Channel 96.3F are the final five MediaCorp Radio stations to join the others which are already running 24 hours.

    9 out of 10 top stations in Singapore in 2007 belong to MediaCorp Radio, according to the latest (AC Nielsen Radio Diary) survey results.

    And ratings have been steadily increasing to the current figure of 3,178,000 listeners.

    The top 3 stations are Y.E.S 933FM, Love 97.2FM, and Class 95FM.
