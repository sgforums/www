    Oh yes, all of what you stated is being stated in buddhist doctrines, and there are such people.

    Even today there are no lack of practitioners who develope psychic abilities. It is not uncommon. I have known of many buddhist teachers, masters, who have developed these supernatural powers. I shall not say Who. I have also known some Hindu Yogis who developed these supernatural powers. Psychic abilities are a side-product of Concentration, Jhanas developed from meditation.

    However I have to warn everyone here not to simply trust a boastful person about his own powers. True practitioners do not usually boast about such powers even though they may possess themselves. However this is not to say that whoever you heard that possess "Supernatural powers" is false, just that the practitioner himself don't go around boasting it. (edit) [NOTE: I do not consider Blue-mind as boasting, just sharing of experiences.]

    There's a danger. Just yesterday in my dharma centre, I have heard from a person who sought help from my dharma teacher.. its about a group of ppl... they have sought a person who claims that he has "surpassed the triple worlds". Some conflicts happened and bad things happened to them, they developed rashes, fall ill, and it was quite clear to them that this person who claims that he has "surpassed the triple worlds" is responsible for doing some sort of "magic" on them...

    My dharma teacher replied that there are only two possibilities:

    1) Such a person, his practise and cultivation has deviated from the Buddha Path and becomes "demonic".
    2) Such a person, he was being possessed/given powers and aid by Gui-Shen. [Gui-Shen are spirits of better karma. But these types of beings u cannot invoke or anger them, because they are attached beings who can easily be angered. For example if u promised them something u must surely return them. Spirits have their own powers.]

    If kena such things... theres nothing you can do, You create the Karmic cause, you have to take it. The best solution now for them is to keep chanting and repenting... so this karma will pass soon.

    Don't let curiosity kill you.

    Be careful of whom you trust..!
