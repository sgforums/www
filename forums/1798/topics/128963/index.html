    Buddhism wants you to doubt. Big doubt = big realisation. Small doubt = small realisation. No doubt = no realisation.

    Kinds of dreams:
    1) se yin chan meng (form-based dreams) - what we see in color can become dream. Example you see a base-ball tournament, then when you come back you dream about it.
    2) shou yin chan meng (feeling-based dreams) - you go to a place and there's feelings, example when people insult you or praise you. They can cause one to dream about it.
    3) xiang yin chan meng (perception-based dreams) - think of it now and daily, like thinking of fishing, then one day, you really will go fishing.
    4) xing yin chan meng (mental fabrication-based dreams) - example when you come back from fishing, and you feel happy about it, then when you dream, you'll still dream of fishing.
    5) si yin chan meng (consciousness-based dreams) - 'si' or consciousness is dualism. Thinking of how in the past people used to fish and how people use to fish now, then when you see documentary on fishing and you knew the know how. Our dualistic views can cause dreams too.
    6) sickness/4 elements imbalance based dreams - Another form of dreaming is condition by the weather or in other word the 4 elements. Due to sickness, our 4 elements are in balance.
    7) karma seed based dreams - Dreams that are due to the accumulation of karma seeds be it good or bad, in our Alaya (Stored Consciouness) through out our cycle of rebirths.
    8 ) karma fruition based dreams - Dreams that are due to the coming fruition of karma (future karmic events about to unfold)
    9) emotional based dreams - Dreams that are due to family love, friendship love, spouse love, etcs. The emotion of 2 party coming together
    10) divine intervention based dreams - Dreams that are cause by intervention of Buddha, bodhisattvas or saints, for the sake of delivering sentient beings.

    note:
    All the 5 aggregates can cause dreams. Another form of dreaming is condition by the weather or in other word the 4 elements. Due to sickness, our 4 elements are in balance. Example when dream of flying, the wind element is in balanced. For people who always get angry easily, they are bound to dream a lot. If one imagine of flying, one can also dream of flying. Like for children who watch Superman, at night they too can dream of flying. All are projection from the 8th consciousness.
