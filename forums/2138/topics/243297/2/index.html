    I all,

    I have seen The Phantom of the Opera in London. Pretty good!! Not to be missed if you are die hard opera person. I have the book, the music CDs and everything, since 10 years ago.

    The show in Singapore will be different because this team is from US, not UK. And, another consideration is, in UK, the have the same show, run in same opera house, more than 20 years. That theather only run this show, so you can imagine every setting is for this show.

    Things to note.

    1. try to get a seat in the middle (not left or right)
    2. 2nd floor is good. Coz the phantom sometimes appear in the air.
    3. Live orchestra, so sure you will enjoy.

    - No, it is by different team of people.
