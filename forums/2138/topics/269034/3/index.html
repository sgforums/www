    Socks always match the pants, not the shoes. You only wear white socks with black pants and shows if you're in New Delhi.

    Cuffs of pants should just about reach the bottom of the shoe's heels.

    Shirt cuffs should reach the base of the palm, and jacket sleeves should always be shorter than shirt sleeves. Show cuff and plenty of it.

    Jacket lapel should be of the same maximum width as the tie.

    The tie should reach the belt line - too short and you look like Dilbert.

    If you're building up a suit/office wardrobe, start with navy, then black - they're the easiest to match.

    Belt and shoes should be the same colour.

    Blue and brown do not go together.

    And for crying out loud, no plastic pens or watches.
