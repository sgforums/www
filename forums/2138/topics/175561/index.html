    MAGICAL at BARRACUDA DIVE RESORT

    This paradise city called Manado is situated on the northern tip of Sulawesi in Indonesia. Off its coast lies the world famous Bunaken National Marine Park, which consists of five islands, namely Bunaken, Manado Tua, Siladen, Mantahage and Nain. These islands offer spectacular wall dives with a wide range of marine life and beautiful hard and soft corals. The waters in this region allow year round diving, with visibility of 30 metres on average. Do bring your cameras and reef guides along, as this trip promises to show you the creatures that you have only been able to see in pictures.

    The resort boasts excellent service and is located at Molas beach that is about 40 minutes from the airport and half an hour from Manado City. The accommodation of 20 standard / Deluxe two to a room cottages is nestled among lush landscaped grounds that faces the sea. All rooms are twin sharing with air-conditioning and hot and cold showers as well as a mini-bar. The restaurant serves both Indonesian and Western cuisine and the lounge is the perfect place to enjoy your favourite drink at the end of a perfect day of diving. Two fast dive boats bring divers to the Bunaken National Park dive sites in about 40 minutes. A full service dive centre with rinse tanks, shower, lockers, workshop, rental equipment, compressor room and classroom complete the facilities available.

    This package includes airport transfer, accommodation (twin sharing), all meals at the resort (breakfast, lunch, dinner), except for lunch on the first day and last day. 3 boat dives a day, tanks, weights and dive guide. Snorkelling and non-diver packages are also available. Come with us and experience for yourself one of the top dive destinations in the world bearing world class sites at a premier resort for that perfect dive vacation you have always dreamed about.
