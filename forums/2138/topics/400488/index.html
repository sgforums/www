    For those interested in learning how to compose songs, Lee Wei Song School of Music is organising just such a course for you!

    No previous music background is required. Details of the course as below:

    Course Details

    Time period: First 2 weeks of June

    Fees: $250

    Number of lessons: 3 lessons per week, 6 lessons in total

    Duration of lessons: 2 hours per lesson

    Aim of course: Upon completion of the course, students should be
    able to sing and play on the keyboard and be able to write a
    simple song.

     

    You can find out more at http://leeweisong.wordpress.com/holidays/

     

    How to sign up

    You can either come down to Lee Wei Song School of Music at

    110, Killiney Road. (Click here to find out how to get to

    the school) or you can do a bank transfer. If  you wish to
    do bank transfer, please contact Jun Yue at 9060 5282
    before doing so.


    Questions, questions

    If you are interested or if you have any questions, you can
    contact Jun Yue at jytan@leeweisong.com or call/SMS at
    9060 5282.
