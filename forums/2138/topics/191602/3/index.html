    In the Tunnel, look out for exhibition Surface Structure by Jaz Loh & DJ Tommy. While Jaz deconstructs elements from popular culture and remixes them out of their original context. DJ Tommy, Hong Kong's premier scratchmaster and producer (who has played alongside great international spinners and acts like Prodigy, DJ Shadow, DJ Krush and DJ Food), sets the mood with his music.

    At the Observation Deck (Singapore Art Cafe, Library@Esplanade), American artist Derek Hess, whose work has transformed rock concert posters, album covers and more into works of art coveted by rock bands, the Louvre and art lovers alike, lets you into the labyrinth of his mind! Get to know what inspires his dark, apocalyptic imagery and dig deep into his past.

    At the Waterfront, don't miss Derek Hess Trail, an exhibition of Derek Hess' art, evocative works in which beauty and horror, control and chaos come together.
