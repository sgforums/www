    hi guys. fri the 13th.

    This is a poem.. or rather a little passage that i wrote myself years ago when i was a young young man. while trying to go after one innocent young girl who has evolved into a very mature feminine lady now.

    lets hope she sees this someday.

    Its for you..

    Life is a journey for us all.. There are ups and downs... Victories and Failures..
    If all of us were to slow down and take a look at our surroundings, there are people who care for you.. Be it your friends ur family members or your admirer.. They are there, silently supporting you in the backstage of your life. They do little things, to make you smile.. A smile to you but a glitter of gold to them.. They don't ask for much.. Just for you to be happy..

    Have you ever, ever take a look at your surroundings? Did you think of how ur friends felt when this happens?
    There are many challenges in life, overcoming it is not a problem no matter how impossible the challenge may seem to be. Impossible = I M Possible. If theres a will, theres a way..
    Nothing is too late.. If you have the determination.. You can definately bring things back to before..

    Life is travelling at the speed of light...

    Have you slowed down to take a look at your surroundings?

    Have you really tell yourself that you can do it?

    Ups and Downs are here to stay..

    Defeating it... Its your say..

    Take a look at your surroundings, notice who is there for you most when you are down..

    Notice who likes to be with you when you are up.

    But most importantly, notice who is silently there with you when you are down and when

    you are up.. silently in the background..supporting you and cheering you on...


    You say time will tell everything..

    But a first step is needed to kick start the clock..

    Who is gonna start the clock? Is it you? Or is it me?

    When will time tell...

    When will it really tell..
