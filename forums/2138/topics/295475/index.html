    Discipline Chief denies having toyboy army
    by Wan Sui Ye

    The Chief of Regimentation and Discipline, Noiseology, laurence82 yesterday came out to deny any udnerground effort to build up an army of toyboys to rebel against the United sgForums Alliance. This comment came in the wake of accusations by Chief Dishwasher and Laundrywoman, Ferret. Ferret alleges that laurence82 has been quietly recruiting forumers, boys as young as 17 and 18, to join a group called the Toyboy Army. She pointed out that the main of this group is to turn sgForums topsy turvy at an opportune time, and in doing so, laurence82 can topple the other leaders of sgForums and establish a democracy in sgForums.

    This caused shock and apprehension among the denizens of sgForums, so long used to dic_ktatorship under the leadership of CTX and stooky. As crowd grows larger everyday at the door of Noiseology seeking confirmation or denial to these rumours, laurence82 feels that its time to come out and explain everything.

    "I regret that such lies and blashphemies have been committed against me. Ferret cannot be trusted. We all know she is a ferret, and she cant be trusted, period! Perhaps its my generosity and kindness to the cute young boys of sgForums that create such jealousy. Nonetheless, I urge all not to fall for these lies that can destabilise our peaceful online society!"

    A list of members of the Toyboy Army was secretly emailed to the Wan Bao yesterday, and the names of several members of sgforums were in it. These include dead man inc, agenda, bluewinged and even oldbies like babyboy, weidev and extremex. Meanwhile, none of them can be contacted for comments.
