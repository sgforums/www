    Maia Lee pregnant

    SINGER Maia Lee, known for her wild girl image, is pregnant.

    The Stomp Star Blogger is expecting a child with Zidov Dominik, a contestant from The Contender Asia, a reality fight contest that is being shown on television here.

    When Life! contacted Dominik in Koh Samui, Thailand, over the phone yesterday, he admitted that the baby is his and that they will be keeping the child.

    'The baby is mine and I will be there to be his father. I will take care of the baby,' the 26-year-old Croat said.

    The full-time fighter, who lives in Thailand, said that they would not be getting married.

    He said he and Lee are 'not together'', and that they are just 'very good and close friends'.

    Lee, 24, a single mum, has a five-year-old son, Tyrese, from a previous relationship.

    She shot to fame in 2004 when she took part in the first Singapore Idol contest and dated the runner-up, Sylvester Sim.

    She declined to be interviewed and would only say that the child was conceived on Christmas last year.

    'I'm very happy to be pregnant,'' she said.
