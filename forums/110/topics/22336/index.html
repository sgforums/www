    Q: Sally likes 225 but not 224; she likes 900 but not 800; she likes 144 but not 145. Which does she like:
    A: 1600. Sally likes perfect squares.

    Q: If two typists can type two pages in two minutes, how many typists will it take to type 18 pages in six minutes?
    A: 6. One typist types one page in two minutes.

    Q. If you count from 1 to 100, how many 7's will you pass on the way?
    A: 20.
    If you found 10, you forgot about 70, 71, etc.
    If you got 11, you forgot about the 70 series, but remembered 77 had 2;
    if you got 19, you forgot about the extra 7 in 77;
    if you got 21 you created a 7 out of thin air.

    Q: There are 1200 elephants in a herd. Some have pink and green stripes, some are all pink and some are all blue. One third are pure pink. Is it true that 400 elephants are definitely blue?
    A: No. The other 800 elephants can be any mix of all blue and pink and green stripes.

    Q: If a circle is one, how many is an octagon?
    A: 8. Circle has one line, octagon has eight.
