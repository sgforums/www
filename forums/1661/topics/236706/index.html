    GERRARD FIGURES FOR REDS

    Liverpool will have captain Steven Gerrard in the squad for the trip to Newcastle United after he played just 45 minutes for England in midweek due to a 'niggling' injury picked up last weekend in the Merseyside derby against Everton.

    Spanish midfielder Xabi Alonso, who scored from his own half when the teams met at Anfield earlier in the season, misses out this time due to a one-match suspension.

    Boss Rafael Benitez could call in Mohamed Sissoko, who has been back in full training following a dislocated shoulder sustained at Birmingham in November.

    Newcastle full-back Celestine Babayaro could return to the starting line-up for the first time since mid-December.

    The 28-year-old Nigeria international has missed the last 11 games with a hamstring injury, but is back in the squad after returning to training.

    Kieron Dyer is fit despite suffering from the after-effects of his exertions for England in midweek, but full-back Stephen Carr could be sidelined for a month with the thigh injury which forced him to withdraw from the Republic of Ireland squad.
