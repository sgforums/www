    EXCLUSIVE: HELEN IS NEW FOE FOR HARRY
    By Fiona Cummins

    ENDURING Love star Helen McCrory has been lined up to play evil Bellatrix Lestrange in the next Harry Potter film.

    McCrory, currently dating Damian Lewis, wowed film bosses with her West End performance as Rosalind in As You Like It.

    It will be 35-year-old McCrory's first major Hollywood role and should lead to lucrative exposure in the US.

    In Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix, twisted Bellatrix fights on the side of the teenage wizard's arch enemy Lord Voldemort.

    A source revealed: "The producers are trying to keep the cast a secret until it's all sealed. But they are really keen on Helen.

    "They think she has the gravitas and looks to pull off the part of Bellatrix."

    Daniel Radcliffe, Rupert Grint and Emma Watson are already pencilled again in to play Harry, Ron and Hermione.

    Shooting is expected to start at the beginning of next year and the film will be released in 2007. - mirror.co.uk
