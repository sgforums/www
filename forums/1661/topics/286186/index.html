Wardrobe malfunction gets JKR

    United Kingdom News.Net
    Tuesday 16th October, 2007 (ANI)

    London, Oct 16 : Harry Potter author JK Rowling was left embarrassed at the launch of her first U.S. book tour after accidentally revealing her bra to the audience.

    The best-seller author bared her undergarment on Oct 15, while talking to children at the Kodak Theatre in Hollywood, reports the Daily Mail.

    The 42-year-old writer didn't notice her dipping neckline, and carried on talking while her image flashed around the world.

    However, Rowling was soon made aware of the slip down by a fellow presenter, who ran forward to cover her up.

    The writer quickly rectified the wardrobe malfunction and continued her literary engagement.
