    LATE TEST FOR BENT

    Charlton Athletic striker Darren Bent requires a late check on a knee problem before it is decided whether he will be risked in the Barclays Premiership clash with Newcastle United.

    Andy Reid and Ben Thatcher will miss the game at The Valley on Sunday due to hamstring injuries.

    Defenders Madjid Bougherra and Hermann Hreidarsson and forward Marcus Bent, though, are all in line for a return.

    Newcastle United will be without midfielder Nicky Butt against Charlton Athletic on Sunday as they attempt to bounce back from their demoralising UEFA Cup exit.

    Butt is suspended after reaching five bookings for the season, while full-back Stephen Carr is likely to miss out again with the thigh problem which kept him out against AZ Alkmaar.

    Oguchi Onyewu is available following his ineligibility in Europe, while Craig Moore returned to the bench in Holland for the first time since picking up a hamstring injury in November.

    However, Celestine Babayaro and Peter Ramage (both hamstring) and long-term absentees Shola Ameobi (hip) and Michael Owen (knee) are still out.
