    OWEN MUST WAIT FOR MAGPIES RETURN

    Newcastle striker Michael Owen will not make his comeback against Chelsea on Sunday.

    The 27-year has suffered a slight groin strain during his recovery from his knee problem and will not be risked, while Shola Ameobi (hip) also needs further time.

    Shay Given is still missing with his groin injury but Kieron Dyer (hamstring), Scott Parker (ankle) and Antoine Sibierski (knee) could all return.

    However, winger Damien Duff will miss the rest of the season with the ankle injury he sustained at Portsmouth last Saturday.

    Chelsea have no fresh injury worries ahead of the trip.

    Ukraine striker Andriy Shevchenko is expected to have shaken off the minor leg injury that kept him out of the 4-1 win over West Ham United in midweek.

    Arjen Robben is recovering from knee ligament damage but is not ready to return to first team action.
