    In the past , RSAF used three types of Bell UH-1s

    UH-1B, UH-1H, and UH-1N (Bell 212)

    UH-1B is smaller size , and used mainly for training purpose.

    UH-1H is used for assault transport , light gunships ( with GPMGs mounted at side of the doors ) or heavy gunships ( miniguns pod and rockets ) ,

    UH-1N ( Bell 212) (longest in length ) , painted with civilian colours ( red and white) is for Search and Rescue missions.
