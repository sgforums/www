    Different weapons on armour provide different abilities.

    Larger calibre weapons (75mm or more) are actually very good direct fire support weapons.

    Mortar is good for indirect firepower - very crucial in providing closer range,quicker kabooms to clear areas and break lines.

    40mm grenades have their special uses.

    0.5cal is more for general fire suppression/assault and engagement of small targets up to 1+km?

    25-30mm CGs are for reinforced/armoured targets.
