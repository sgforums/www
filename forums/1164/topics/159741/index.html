    I read a review somewhere that said the film is not completely realistic. After watching the film, I concur.

    For instance, they showed RAF Harriers in one scene and RN Sea Harriers in another, when they both didn't go to the same exercise.

    Some of the fight scenes showed them flying over green fields with meandering rivers. Nevada isn't that green.

    The close-up bomb explosion scenes also seemed like staged pyrotechnics, rather than actual ordnance exploding. The bombs are so accurate? Hit the tank but just missed the camera just beside the tank?

    The internal E3 Awacs scenes also doesn't seem real, looks like a studio.

    But overall an eye opening film, albeit too short.
