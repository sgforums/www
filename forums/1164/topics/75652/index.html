    Signing on in the Army is a major career move and should not be rushed into.

    Ask yourself, do you really like the Army in the first place? If u don't want to be stuck with a shit job for at least 4 years. After that if u want to enter the private sector for another job, the skills u learned before may have become irrelevant in the ever-changing economy.

    Don't know if u like it? Then I suggest u use the NS time to get a taste of Army life first before u come to a decision. After all, it's 2 and a half years and u can sign on any time.
