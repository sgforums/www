    The Joint Light Tactical Vehicle (JLTV) Family of Vehicles (FoV) is a joint pre-MDAP program currently for the(US) Army and Marine Corps. The JLTV goal is a family of vehicles with companion trailers capable of performing multiple mission roles that will be designed to provide protected, sustained, networked mobility for personnel and payloads across the full Range of Military Operations (ROMO). JLTV objectives include increased protection and performance over the current fleet; minimizing ownership costs by maximizing commonality, fuel efficiency and other means; maintaining effective competition throughout the lifecycle.

    The JLTV FoV includes ten (10) sub-configurations (and companion trailers) in three payload categories. However, a single vehicle and/or trailer may be able to fulfill the requirements for multiple sub-configurations
