    Is that what they call LOAL? Fire and fix?

    In LOAL modes, for those missile which are capable of that, eg Hellfire etc, the missile is launched at a trajectory without a guaranteed lock. After launching, then the target is locked on. For aircraft like the apache/longbow, it allows them to fire from behind terrain masking, set the missile to clear a certain altitude, before another aircraft locks on to the target.

    This is generally bad news for those tanks with Active defense systems, where they are able to detect radar , or laser designators illuminating them. Reason is cos it really reduces their reaction time.
