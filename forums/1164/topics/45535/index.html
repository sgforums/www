    Some highlights are:
    Lots of lecture and test: Military Law, Map Reading etc
    Weapon Training: GMPG, SIG Sauer P226 pistol(used by US Navy
    SEALS)
    Most Siong : Unarm Combat
    Others: POW handling, Section training
    + lot of others of interesting stuff for you to find out

    MP training is not that siong when compared to the BMT 10+ years ago where lot of tekan occurs and very little free time. Probably the BMT has less tekan session now more free time, so MP course seems siong.

    After your course, posting depends on your luck and background
    to places like Istana,SAFDB, Motor Cycle platoon, external unit etc

    Most people whor reamain in SAFPU will attend a basic 1 month precision drill course.

    Is basically a rather enjoyable and interesting course. After course, experience between MPs may varied due to different job assignment.
