    Concept to Capability
    Synopsis:
    Concept to Capability is a 24-minute documentary that provides viewers with an insight into the Singapore Armed Forces' journey to a third-generation force, through the transformation of concepts, technology and people. It focuses on new fighting concepts using Integrated Knowledge Command and Control, Unmanned Platforms, Network Connectivity, the Digital Soldier and Precision Fire.
