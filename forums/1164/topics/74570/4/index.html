    I think you should really invest in a compressor and airbrush if you are really into this hobby.An airbrush can do wonders from painting,shading and dust staining etc.All this techniques are very crucial if you want a realistic model.
    Single action airbrush are for general spraying work.Press the trigger and the paint sprays whereas for double action airbrush you can control the amount of paint flow.This is very important if your model require camouflaging.
    I don't know about other modellers but i love my Tamiya compressor and airbrush.Served me well for many years and still going strong.

    Originally posted by KiloClass:
    is there a special kind of adhesive that you ppl use to build these models ? or is it just plain contact glue ?
    Most modelers plastic cement for gluing parts together. I mostly use normal Tamiya cement as I find the bond of the super-thin stuff not strong enough.

    However, I personally also use superglue for some applications, as well as White Glue to attach weapons on my planes as I can remove them later when I want to switch weapons.
