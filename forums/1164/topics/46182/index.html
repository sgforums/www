    For mono intake, you do your BMT in the unit, pass out as a private, continue on for your vocation training/commander training and eventually return to the same unit as a men/commander. You will be together with the same peers you had during your BMT.

    For some units, if you enter mono intake, you can't be a commander, only men.

    For BMTC, depending on which month of the year you enter, only certain vocations are available. You can go to the commander's school (SISPEC or OCS) from BMTC if you are selected.
