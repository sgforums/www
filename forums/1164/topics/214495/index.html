    Hellfire AGM-114K is a laser guided ATGM, as well as the Mokopa etc , quite a lot of them.

    One way to counter them is by way of softkill APS with RWR + smoke grenade, or decoys, and electro-optical signals, infrared or laser jamming etc

    To counter this countermeasure is by not lasing directly at the target initially, but say a few metres in front of target, only "paint' it , say the ATGM is abt to close in , so let as little time as possible for target to activate it's countermeasure.

    Spike is optically guided by fibre optics as well as by TI, to counter perhaps softkill APS with RWR + smoke grenade, but there is very little reaction time , making it worse by the loft trajectory of a top attack ATGM.

    Therefore softkill APS will be quite difficult counter modern ATGM, Hardkill APS with MW radar plus some kind projectile to intercept incoming ATGM.
