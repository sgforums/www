    Ex Western Arc is part of the original agreement between Singapore and France which also saw the basing of 20 A-4SU/TA-4SUs in France. It was designed to increase interaction between the French and Singaporean Air Forces and sees bilateral exercises between the 2 Air Forces.

    FYI theres also an Exercise Eastern Arc, held once every 2 years between the 2 air forces over Singapore. French AF Mirages would come with the French contingent for Asian Aerospace and conduct exercises after the show. 2 have been held so far, in 1998 and 2000. There was one scheduled for 2002 but was cancelled due to post-Sept 11 uncertainties. Not too sure if previous Western Arcs have been held, but I do know 142 Sqn took part in ODAX in S France using 150 Sqn's aircraft before.
