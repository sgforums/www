    Forgot one very good one, called Dark Blue World. Czech production, about the plight of Czech pilots who escaped Nazi occupation and fought against the Nazis with the RAF in WWII but villified and jailed by the communists on their return home.     Well, one that i have to mention isn't a movie, but a TV series. Anyone remember MASH? It was a comedy about a field hospital in the Korean War...

    It was interesting to watch a bunch of anti-authority, anti-establishment people stuck in a war none of them wanted, but had to fulfil their duties as medics and doctors to save lives. On top of that, the characters and their antics were hilarious.... Like Clinger, the medic who is desperate to get out of the war and always wears dresses to convince everyone he's insane, and the poor CO who's more of a doctor than a soldier and hasn't the faintest idea how to run a military unit, and whose authority nobody seems to recognise.

    On a more serious note, i think that Tora Tora Tora was one of the best war movies of all time. It was realistic, gripping, and informative at the same time.

    My two cent's worth.
