    S'pore, India air forces exercise together
    http://www.mindef.gov.sg/imindef/publications/cyberpioneer/news/2007/November/27nov07_news.html

    The Republic of Singapore Air Force (RSAF) and Indian Air Force (IAF) are conducting a bilateral air exercise, codenamed SINDEX, involving F-5 fighter aircraft and MiG-27 ground attack fighters.

    Held from 26 Nov to 13 Dec at the Kalaikunda Air Force Station in the eatern part of India, the exercise will see personnel from both air forces training together in realistic and challenging conditions.

    SINDEX 2007 is the first exercise to be conducted after the recent signing of a bilateral agreement on joint military training between the RSAF and the IAF.

    It marks another milestone in the warm defence relations between Singapore and India.
