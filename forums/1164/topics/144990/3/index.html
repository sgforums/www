    As you can see in the pics I posted, not ALL the units look hi-tech and professional - by western standards.

    But of all the PLA and Armed Police units I saw in China from HK all the way to Tibet, one thing is very significant is the level of discipline they project. They are well-behaved in public and take their duties very seriously.

    Maybe some would find this hard to believe especially after Tiananmen but nearly all Chinese people I know think very highly of the PLA. The fact that the poor troopers often had to risk their necks in disaster rescue and relieve efforts probably contribute to this. PLA also provide free medical care to poor people especially in the countryside.

    One of my travelling companion from HK was on the receving end of such kindness when she fell ill in Tibet.

    Was it propaganda? - you bet, but very highly-appreciated at that time.

    I would say the PLA is disciplined and professional as a whole - but a lot of old communist-style equipment and weapons around. They also have a lot of practices western-minded people do not understand.
