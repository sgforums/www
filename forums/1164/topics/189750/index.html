    One of the core strengths of the German Army was its leadership to men ratio. It was significantly higher compared to the allies. I can't remember it off hand, but i'm sure something will turn up quickly through a web crawl.

    I'm not saying that we are "Fortress Singapore." Just that an invasion force will have too much to contend with. The first front being the Intelligence Battle. Its very HARD to hide a major force build up.

    Another of Hitler's mistake was overconfidence in the "blitzkrieg."
    1. The blitzkrieg is over rated. It was an excellent tactic when numerically superior, technologically superior, as it prevented the enemy from massing enough firepower to slow the advancing armor. Airpower is a MUST. Against a technologically equivalent allied army, with lesser tanks than the allies, the Blitzkrieg failed him.

    2. Hitler's personal meddling in Tank design, that resulted in the monstrosity called the KingTiger and its predecessor, the Tiger, added the final nail into the blitzkrieg coffin. The hideously slow and ultra heavy tank could not keep up with blitzkrieg style tempo of ops. Firstly, its a gas sucker, and too heavy for the terrain they were supposed to operate in.

    3. Hitler's lack of confidence in his commanders, and the German Army's capability to fight defensive battles. The German's were experts at defensive operations. They were extremely organised and more than capable of slowing and inflicting casualties on a defensive fight. He failed to allow his commanders to conduct defensive battles in depth, instead ordering them to counter-attack (blitzkrieg style, yes) whenever the opportunity arose. Fear of the secret police saw to it that the commanders obeyed that order.

    Finally, I believed if Hitler allowed Rommel to position armor behind normandy, the allies could not have gotten a beach head at all. They would have been counter attacked immediately on the beach. Instead, armor was positioned more centrally, in a more conservative bet that they would be able to counter-attack against any location the Allies chose to land. He should have gambled with the DesertFox.
