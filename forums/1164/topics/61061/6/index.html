    A Platoon mate told me his recent experience.....

    He was on scout course at that time, and was navigating around lim chu kang. ( Guys who trained there should know this place, where the poyan reservoir is. The track that heads north, the ending point where it acts as a dyke. ) His group came upon this old hut with only it's partitions around, no roof. It was getting dark and they decided to harbour around there. At midnight, a tune with a female voice starts singing.... they were hearing it as if from a distance. My friend:" Everybody shut up! ". He wanted to listen more clearly and just before he want to say anything, his friend:" Now you shut up, everybody go sleep ". At that time, my friend was still maintaining comms. After the navex, the team that comms with my friend said:" Wah! you all balls beri big, dare to bring radio outfield still on so loud over the comms? ". What the other team heard was the voice was very loud, however, what my friend heard, was from a distance.

    Came ex.pegasus, he was deployed as blockforce at the same area. Again, the tune starts singing. It lasts around 15mins to 30mins. The same as his previous experience.

    Hi,

    Share my reservist ghost story with you guys.

    Back in Dec 2002, we had an 4 days 3 night exercise at Pulau Tekong. On the first day, we digged trenches facing Malaysia. On 2nd day, most trenches were ready. On that 2nd night, as I was inside my trench alone, the time was about 2am, I saw a few white translucent objects flying in front of such trees. There was a bright moon that night. At first I thought I was dreaming. I later confirmed with anothe platoon mates that he also seen white objects flying in front of the trees. One of my platoon mates even seen a soldier like white object sitting nearby.

    Maybe we were all tired... Maybe we were all dreaming ...

    The white objects btw were flying in the air. I couldn't see their faces. They were about 20m away from me. I wasn't scared then as I was really tired as I didn't sleep for 2 days.

    Regards

    That time, since got quite a few days "off" days...so applied for off on my 21st bday...stupid me...should have applied for leave. Cause one of my team mates "keng"...go report sick for some minor illness.

    Since TSR states that must have signaller, my WO called my home, cancelled my off, and ask me go report to excerise.

    Anyway....was deployed alone in the middle of Poyang cemetery, tomb stones all over. Day time was okay...as there was some human traffic at the temple downhill, and got my FM radio.

    Was supposed to pick me up at 6pm, but nobody show up. Around 8+ pm....the dogs started howling...and making eerie sounds. The dogs (4 of them) seemed to form a semi circle, and barking at a certain spot. The dogs started "reversing", but still keeping the semi circle...barking and howling away.

    Suddenly my FM radio died, and my skin hair all stand....what frightens me is the spot the dogs barking is shifting nearer and nearer. And my eyes tell me, seems to have something white..floating

    Balls shrink....manpack my signal set, and ran downhill to the temple. Think I must have beaten my best SOC timing

    The temple door was closed, so sat there.....taking out all my "amulets" and chanting whatever prayers I know.....various religions

    Radioed my officer, he said reaching in 5 min. Learnt that they went nearby kopitiam makan, forgot abt me The spot the dogs barking, however keep shifting nearer. Saw the rover headlights some distance away....ran with the radio set to the rover, jumped on...and told my driver friend to step on it.....Only when reached camp, did I tell them the story. Damn sway !! Thinking back after so many years...still feel eerie eerie.
