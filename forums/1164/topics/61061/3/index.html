    my encounter in Tekong was a strange one. If I remember correctly there was a bunk in camp 1 foxtrot company was used as a storeroom but had an extra door on the long side of the bunk. I was told that someone commited suicide in it and haunted that bunk. So some priest came in and suggested to bulit an extra door on the long side of the bunk. It was build to free his spirit into the spiritual realm. The bunk was used as a storeroom from then on.

    One evening, we had a night march and I was the platoon IC. We had to march along side of the bunks and the road took me next to the that particular bunk. I happened to look at my platoon when I passed by that door and I saw my whole platoon's future rank. It shone on their arms and I could see where they were posted to. After that night's march, I told 5 them what I saw and where they were heading after passing out of BMT. True enough, they were posted as I had said.

    Strange as it seem that I saw no ghost throughout my whole bmt. It was only in my unit that I had constant encounters. I had to often battle with them. My unit was a former hospital where the mortuary was there in world war 2. Anyone from Seletar camp?

    This is something I experienced myself.
    'A' coy 99' BMTC 1

    It was the night before the 24km POP route march. We were to wake up around 5am+ to prepare. So feeling excited and anxious, I had a light sleep. Midway though my sleep, I was awoken with sounds of banging on a metal cupboard in the bunk, I took a look at my alarm clock and it's about 4am++.

    As I slept at the end of the bunk (@ the window), I can hear that the noise was coming from the other end of the bunk. I decided to ignore it at first thinking might be my bunkmate opening his cupboard, but the noise is persistant for a few minutes!! So decided to take a peek towards that direction. And guess what, I saw nothing and the sound is still there. Slowly I crept back into my blanket hoping it'll end soon. And finally about 5 mins of terror, the noise's gone.

    So after a fellow bunkmate turned on the lights when he woke up, I walked up to the guy sleeping beside that cupboard where the sound came from and I ask him, "did you heard sounds from your buddy's cupboard?" And indeed he had......... Eerie....

    I truly believe SAF knows something about these weird encounters in tekong. When Sispec first moved from Pasir Laba to Rocky Hills in Tekong, all our bunks and beds are blessed. How do I know? Coz' all our bunks and beds had some rice all over it.

    During my unit time, my friend had an encounter in Lim Chu Kang too. But I only know parts of the story so not very clear. Maybe in the future when I know the whole story, I'll post it again

    heres a personal experience back in march or feb.

    We have a multi purpose hall above the cookhouse. Its pretty old, and within the hall there are some storerooms which u can only enter from the hall itself. I knew the storeman in charge of the sports store. Once i was supposed to go collect some stuff from him, but he wasn't around. I had a rec with me, both of us wanted to slack a bit, so instead of going back to e office, we decided to wait in his store, since he never locks the doors. The doors were the normal double doors with a door latch, which opened outwards. The hall itself is enclosed by steel shutters when its not used. That day when we were there, it was all closed up. We entered the hall by a small access door at the back of the hall.

    We went in, and i stood beside some stores facing the center of the room, my guy was sittin on e table in the store facing the door. While we were talking, he suddenly shifted his eyes to look at something beside me. I turned and saw the door opening slowly and steadily outwards. Then as we were looking, it stopped, then slowly and steadily closed back. Just b4 it closed completely, it suddenly slammed shut with a loud bang. That spooked me and i got him out of the store immediately. There was no wind, since the whole hall is enclosed, and the door to the hall is within the hall itself. And i am doubly sure its not the wind because the door opened OUTWARDs 1st, since it can only open outwards, and the store itself is also enclosed.
