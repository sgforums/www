    Oh about Pontianak, I was about 10-12 yrs old. Cant remember that well. Used to take the BUS No. 61 or 63?? from Holland Road to Lucky Towers on Grange Road to visit my friend. Those were the days when 61/ 63 is a smaller bus that dont have doors and is empty most of the time. On a couple of occasions, I noticed a Malay lady who always wore traditional clothes and tie a bund with her hair taking the same bus. She wasnt pretty, I remembered thinking that she had a face of a horse. She always got on the bus before and after my stops. And being young and "silly" during that time, I always had a strong feeling that she wasnt human.

    Anyway, since I had a bus card then, I use to take any bus that goes to the bus stop along Botanic Gardens to change any other buses that came along to go to Grange Road - to save time. One day, I was doing the same routine when I noticed that she was also on a non-61/63 bus. I had this strange feeling that she was following me and I know it because when I deliberately alighted at one the stop that is "less popular" most of the time along the stretch of the road. She followed me down the bus! I ran as fast as I could to Marco Polo hotel. From then onwards, I never took the bus again. To go to my friend's house, I detour by taking 191 to Lido Cinema and change a bus or cab to Grange Road.

    But you know what, I am now 34 years old and I still can visual her face and clothes.
