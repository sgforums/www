    Found another one... (in original Singapore Air Defense Command colors, last aircraft).

    There's a bit of a tragic story behind this particular aircraft. Originally in service with the RSAF, it was sold to Jet Fighter Flights, based in Bankstown, Sydney where it was used to give short adventure flights to paying passengers. Registered as VH-AKY, it was involved in a fatal crash in October 2006 when its right wing separated during a simulated dive bombing run, causing the aircraft to lose control and crash with 2 fatalities, one of which was a paying passenger whose family had given him the flight as a gift for his 50th birthday, the other was the owner of the flying facility.

    Here are some other shots of it in happier days. Beautifully restored and with original military registration of 315.
