    S'pore sends 4th Chinook helicopter to aid US hurricane relief efforts
    By Julia Ng, Channel NewsAsia | Posted: 05 September 2005 1952 hrs

    SINGAPORE : The Republic of Singapore Air Force is despatching another Chinook helicopter to help in the Hurricane Katrina relief operations.

    This will be the fourth military helicopter that Singapore is sending from its Grand Prairie base in Texas to assist in re-supply and airlifting missions in Louisiana.

    Three RSAF Chinook helicopters joined in the rescue operations last Friday.

    Forty-five RSAF personnel are now deployed in the mission.

    The Defence Ministry says the RSAF Chinooks have flown 39 sorties, transporting several hundred evacuees and security personnel, and more than 10 tonnes of equipment and humanitarian supplies. - CNA /ct



    American Ambassador to Singapore Frank Lavin said the American people were deeply grateful for Singapore's help. He added that RSAF personnel were saving lives in Louisiana and the other Gulf Coast States and Americans would long remember this.
