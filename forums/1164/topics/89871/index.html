    We seem to suffer from abuse of proper terms in the SAF

    SBO = Skeleton Battle Order - No.4, Webbing, Identity Disc (not dog-tags), FAD (First Aid Dressing not 'Kortex'), weapon (either SAR, M-16 etc), combat load (I think we have moved from 2.25x contact rate to 1.5x contact rate), SOP items (varies from unit to unit), other misc. (like comms cord, torchlight etc.).

    FBO = Full battle order - SBO plus fullpack, go figure what's in there.

    Rounds are unfired ammo, the empties are called cartridges when expended.

    Hope we have not confused anyone.
