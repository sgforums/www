    Just a few things, Gary. I don't think you have to be a regular when you sign up for selection - the guys from CDO I knew who went for selection were NSF when they did it. Also, once you're posted to SOF, you stay there except when you're rotated through your tours of duty, typically in 1 CDO (e.g. a CSM tour if you're a specialist, or an OC tour if you're an officer).

    As for the training, I was from 1 CDO, but some of my company mates went over to force line, so I know a fair number of the guys there. They don't talk much about the training, and I don't ask. What I do know is that it's not so much a question of what you do, but what the selection panel sees of how you do it.

    yeah, they'll send you an invitation when you are about to ORD if you are eligible to go for selection coz' my friends and I got 1. I still have that letter with me but I don't think i can reveal too much details about it. BUt i'll list out the pre-requisites as indicated.

    1) All male serviceman (regulars/nsf) who completed at least 9months of service.
    2) Below 30 yrs old
    3) PES B or better
    4) at least 1.57m
    5) at least 54kgs
    6) 6/12 eyesight or better and no color blind
    7) N level or better

    if you fit the above description, you'll be eligible to go for selection
