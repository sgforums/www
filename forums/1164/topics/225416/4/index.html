    (weight with 30rnd magazine, LAD or equivalent, scope)
    M-16A2/A4 (20" barrel) ... 4.49 kg
    HK 416 (14.5" barrel) ... 4.45 kg

    SAR-21 (20" barrel) ... 4.44 kg
