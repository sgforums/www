Soldiers ordered to take up ballet

  • kiri
    2 Nov 04, 11:09
    Soldiers in Belarus have been ordered to take up ballet in a bid to improve "devotion and discipline".

    Military top brass have said that officers and soldiers are to learn to ballet and also be step perfect in traditional Belarussian folk dances.

    They say the discipline involved in learning dance moves and the passion music can produce are fundamental to military success, according to the MIGnews agency.

    The move by officials in Belarus, seen by the west as one of the last Stalinist-style dictatorships, has been backed by organisations including the Belarus Republican Youth Association.
