    Made my enquiry this morning. Technically, the instructors can, and are, being brought up on criminal charges. In terms of a civil lawsuit, because the incident happened during training, the instructors will legally come under the aegis of the army, regardless of their breach of TSR. So the short answer is, no, the instructors, CCO or COA cannot be individually sued.
