    Back to page 1. To reiterate the concept of RSN light carriers are based on: (i) for SLOC defence SG's sea routes are far beyond land-based air cover. (ii) for flight training in international waters instead of relying on overseas bases and limited SG air space. (iii) alternative basing ie swap a land base for a sea one. SG land very ex and an air base takes up a lot of space. It may actually be cheaper to operate a few CVLs than a land base. (iv) security Land based air bases are vulnerable to ground suppression eg rockets etc. Sea bases increase operational vectors and are less vulnerable due to mobility.
