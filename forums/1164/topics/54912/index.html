    To help you get started, here are some tips

    You can start to play by going to the website http://www.conquestgamesite.com. There are 3 ways to play:
    1) IRC log on to irc.undernet.org and then join #conquest. From there send a message "/msg conquest newplayer" to create a player. Your nick will be your character's name. Then follow the instruction to change your password.
    2) Telnet from the website, and follow the instructions given
    3) Directly from the web pages, webpage playing, and follow the instructions.

    More commonly used commands are :
    1. Person
    2. Review
    3. Info
    4. Kia
    5. Rank
    6. Population
    7. Quest
    8. Attack
    9. buy item/sell item
    10. buy (from the cities market)
    11. travel

    The manuals on the website is very helpful but may not be very updated, so I suggest just fiddle around with the cmds and use the Help menu (cmd "help").

    It gets very exciting when you reach lvl 3 because you can change class to mage, cleric, barbarian, vampire or ranger.

    Note: From the website, there is the Log section, very useful to see what is going on in the game.

    It is always good to build up before attacking others.

    One very good way to build up from lvl 1 (A Knight) is to quest for items "quest item" as it only takes two move pts. Try to get the "Ring of limited wishes" item, and then wish for land (need land to build more castles and then can shelter more troops). This game is run on move points, no mv pts means cannot do anything. From the "info", you can check when the mv pts will be distributed again (every 15 mins).

    There are many items in the realm, some are mentioned on the website under magical items. The most powerful is the "Orb of Safety", as this item makes you become protected by the Emperor, means no one can attack you. (After you initiate a battle, you will no longer be protected). You can also get protection if you level.

    Also, when you are going to attack, use the "set surrender" cmd and change your surrender to 100 (100%), so that your troops won't surrender and flee when your troops gets 25% death (default is set at 25%).

    Hope this helps.

    Hi want to know,

    I played the game for quite sometimes aready. I got the advance cmds not in the manuals from the news (cmd "news") in conquest. To validate, "/msg conquest validate ". Remember that your irc nick must be the character's name.

    There is also the house-keeping to take note of. The reason why your items disappear is because of the New Year House-Keeping or NYHK for short. There are 4 HKs, and the NYHK is singapore time 6am daily. The others are at 11am, 6pm and 12mn daily. Items do not disappear during the normal HK. What the HK does is renew all the moves pts for you (so you have moves), renew your number of attacks, your peasants produce food, your troops consume food, and various price changes in all the cities. There will be a lot of activities going on during HK, such as pple attacking, etc...Because everyone is fresh with move pts. Moves pts are allocated every 15 mins also (5 pt every 15 mins). To check this, use cmd "info".
