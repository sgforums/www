    I don't know of which specific report you are referring to.

    There are budget constraints in the US military. Also they are re-structuring their deployement and structure to better suit the new security enviornment and to better deploy newer technology.

    The M1 is predominantly designed to combat the Soviet Armoured threat in Central Europe. That threat is gone. and suitable terrain/threat to deploye the M1 is no longer present.
