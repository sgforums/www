Germany also sent a military adviser in the form of General Alexander von Faulkenhausen and General Hans von Seeckt as military adviser to CKS. Von Faulkenhausen later become the military commander of occupied Belgium during World War Two. New Arsenal in China that produced rifles, gas masks and others was also modeled on German production. Initially, a grand plan of 60 German trained divisions were planned in the Nationalist Army, this plan was however reduced and only 8 full divisions were German trained.In fact, CKS son Chiang Ching Kuo trained with one of the German Mountain unit and took part in the Auschluss of 1938. He subsequently earned the rank of lieutanant in the German Army.

    However, by the end of 1938, as war was looming in Europe, German cooperation in the Far East shifted from China to Japan. Firstly, tis was as Hitler viewed the Japanese as a better pawn and ally in a war against the Soviet Union. Secondly, Von Ribbentrop was also not interested in fostering relation with China but rather with the Japanese. Thus, as mundane Ribbentrop could get, he set about ruining relationship with the Nationalist as best he could. After the outbreak of the Second Sino-Japanese in 1937, cooperation between China and Germany largely ceased.
