    Indonesia's military is mostly for territorial defense. The majority of its army for example, is used for territoral defense, prevent uprisings, internal security and keeping the peace within its assigned military regions and districts.

    Any National Service plans for them will be likely for the same purpose. These units will be used for local security. However, this is not confirmed - there is debate, as funds for National Service can be used instead to improve the current all professional force.
