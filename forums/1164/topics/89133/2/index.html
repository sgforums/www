    No. It is not possible to fire a Nato 7.62x51mm round in the AK47 as the ammo for it is 7.62x39mm. The 51mm and 39mm refers to the case length of the round. Even if it were possible to chamber the round, the bolt will not close. The case of the Russian ammo is tapered while that of the Nato is parallel. Furthermore, the chamber pressure for the Nato round is so much higher than that of the Russian one.
