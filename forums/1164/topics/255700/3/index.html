    Yup, most definitely!

    This image shows an unfired 45 ACP 230 gr. FMJ projectile next to one that has been fired and recovered mostly intact:



    On the fired projectile on the right, you can clearly see the striations on the widest part of the projectile body made by the rifling in the bore. You can even make out the spiralling (i.e., the twist) of the striations indicating that the rifling in the bore ran in a counter-clockwise direction.
