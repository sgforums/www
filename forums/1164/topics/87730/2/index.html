Even U.S DELTA Force which was moulded from the Brits SAS is not primarily used for hostage rescue, as evident in the 1st Gulf war where they were used to hunt scuds & carried out recon.

    Same goes for SAF's SpecOps thats why STAR team has every trainning( Sea, Air & Land ) that their military counter-part has, the differences is that SPF does not have all the supporting resources that SAF has. Eg, a STAR Team member comes face to face with a terrorist with hostage in hand, the STAR officer can't "actually" immediately put a head shot to the terrorist, especially if the terrorist decide to surrender. For the military SpecOps, the rules would be different, the terrorist are considered as "enemies". That is why its tougher to be a STAR Team operator then a military operator.
