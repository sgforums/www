    Ever wondered why the sensor packages are grouped accordingly,
    meaning...

    - radar for air and surface threats
    - sonar for underwater threats

    The term 'surface' means anything just in the positive vertical direction above sea level, for radar to detect something like a ship or even a sea-skimming missile if possible (not withstanding problem of radar clutter at sea)

    This could be a very critical area to exploit in terms of attack methods against surface vessels - e.g. wave-riding weapons ?
