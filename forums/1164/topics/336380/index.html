F/A-18C Flight Sim: Seven-G

  • Shotgun
    8 Nov 08, 00:07

    http://www.seven-g.com/

    By far the most promising Hornet Flight Sim so far.  In terms of realism, its planning to match Falcon 4's level of detailed operations.  Graphics seem much better, even though its just in its beta stages now and still a bit unpolished.

    I love the dynamic lighting effects, and being shot off the deck of a carrier is just thrilling!

    Flight dynamics seem pretty solid. Love the HUD cum Head movement effect, feels like the real thing.  You can't always see the HUD when your head's out of position.

    I messed with a few landings, touch n gos mainly.  The AoA Bracket works differently than on the F-16.  

    Snagging the wire on a carrier landing is fun too!  The feeling of not knowing if you've caught the wire and hitting full power the moment you touch the deck must be as close as it gets to real carrier landings. 

     

    The 1.02 beta downloads are now available.  Any MilNut simmers who are interested in flying a hornet with good graphics ought to give it a shot.
