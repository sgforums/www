SAF's 9 Terrex vehicles arrive in Singapore

  • FireIce
    30 Jan 17, 15:43

    The nine Singapore Armed Forces (SAF) armoured vehicles that were seized in Hong Kong last November have arrived in Singapore.

    In a statement, the Ministry of Defence said the nine Terrex Infantry Carrier Vehicles and other equipment arrived at 2.40pm on Monday (Jan 30), and will be transported to an SAF Camp for post-training administration.

    It said this process includes thorough "serviceability checks" and routine maintenance of all vehicles and equipment.
