    Yeah sort of. Until one day, our company storeman found one of our kittens run over by a landrover. It was dying slowly and miserably. One of the PCs and myself ( a PS) ... we like the animals... but we did not know what to do with the dying kitten. It was brought into our company games room. He and I sat there wondering if we should kill it (put it out of its misery) by breaking its neck???? The difficult thing was to motivate each other to do it. Funny and heartbreaking... 2 guys in camouflage and pretty senior in the company, considered by our troops to be one of the more macho guys around..... at a loss about the dying kitten.

    In the end. we suffocated it to put it out of its pain. Its back was broken and the kitten was shitting uncontrollably and also every now and then it was in spasms which caused a lot of pain.

    From that time on, we gave up on having too many animals.
