    Pretty long but it's worth reading.

     

    Let's say you are a leader of a faction in country x , if there is a civil war in country x and an invasion from country y at the same time, would you settle an invasion or civil war first?

     

    If you say, to counter the invasion first because of nation pride, would the two factions in country x unite together to fight effectively?What if the other faction slacks and gather strength while you bear the brunt of the fighting and becomes weaker?

     

    If you say to finish the civil war first, is it ok to let other country  invade your country easily while you are fighting the other faction? Furthermore, there might be danger that the civil war might weaken both factions and the foreign power may conquer the whole nation more easily.

     

    For example,say the chinese civil war,the nationalists ,the communists and the japanese.The nationalist wanted to destroy communism first but under pressure, it are forced to unite with  the communists to defeat the japanese.However,the communists, who were supposed to be wiped out by then,took the japanese invasion as a period to grow in strength and ensure its survival.Furthermore,both sides didnt put in much effort to counter the japanese because they want to keep their best troops to fight each other when war with japan is over.

     

    Wouldnt that may as well be considered as negligible resistance?

     

    In the end, the nationalists got trashed by the communists.

     

    Put this in another way, your skin got some horrible disease but you got a heart attack too but you can only treat either the skin or the heart.Treating the heart may save your life,but is it ok while your skin is rotting away. But,if you treat your skin,your skin wont rot,but your untreated heart may kill you.

     

    So, i ask, would you choose to eliminate internal or external threat first? (assuming no foreign aid)
