    Heh heh. In my batch (5 years ago), there were still occasional turnouts in the night, and I remember getting a maximum of 4.5 hours of sleep every night (and it was very often less than that).

    The tradition of BCT turnouts at Changi Beach is alive and well , and while we didn't get GPMGs as alarm clocks, they gave us thunder flashes on 'special occasions'

    I think intense harassment is just part and parcel of Commando training, and is necessary for conditioning people to work in unexpected, high stress situations. I don't really see any way out of it, unless you want a less effective soldier, but maybe that's just me.
