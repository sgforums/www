    How do I put it?

    Wordy Portion
    There are lamps within the Tank/APC, Yellow or Red (Tactical) light. Normally not used, as to keep light discipline. Some Command Tracks, experimented with other form of lightings.

    Under the Armour, light only come in from openings, such as hatches, vision block, periscopes.

    Quick Answer
    Not Dark for Crew, as they are usually looking through the various vision blocks/sight to notice. Dark for Passengers AI(Armoured Infantry)
