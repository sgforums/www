    Hi there,

    Just wondering if anyone has seen and encountered rare animals in their training experience in Singapore (particularly). I guess I am asking an unusual question which is different from the usual "Has anyone seen or heard a ghost in NS?" question.

    To start off with, I will relate my personal experience during NS....

    This happened around 1984 (yes, long time ago). Was taking my BMT recruit platoon to the 100 m range near Mandai reservoir. It was late afternoon (5 p.m. or so), range was almost over and a bunch of us were standing next to the safety Land Rover parked at one side of the range next to a woodcutter's trail leading into the forest. We were "talking cock" and clowning around as usual.

    As the sun was low in the horizon, it cast a nice warm glow and sunshine along the trail. I was enjoying the moment when I saw what I thot was a dog -like animal on the trail about 80 m away. But it did not move like a dog and in fact seemed to be grazing along the trail ... picking at the grass and plants. I moved about 10- 15m closer, and this animal looked up ... sensed that I was drawing near. At that moment, I saw its body shape more clearly and realised that in fact it was a Mousedeer ! A rare thing, previously thot extinct in S'pore, as our ancester hunted it for its meat.

    After a few seconds, it decided that I was too close and trotted off into the belukar.

    Cool stories guys... I am a bit of an animal buff, hence my interest.

    2 of the stories standout.

    The one about the "raptor" creature. I had an encounter with something quite large (medium size dog-size) in an area called "Bah Soon Pah". This area is now an HDB estate just north of Orchid Country Club.
    Happened to lean against a tree at night. Disturbed something in the tree. It fell down a few feet behind me and gave a loud "HISS". Could sense that it adopted a kind of aggressive stance as its feet shuffled in the grass and was still hissing behind me, and probably figuring out what animal I was also.

    I turned around with bayonet in hand (!!!), and wanted to face down whatever animal it was. It then scuttled away. My sense that it is some kind of large lizard.

    The one about crocs (in Tekong ?!). This is new to me. I am impressed. I did read articles in the Straits Times about crocodiles in the sea off Woodlands, around the mangrove swamp areas. But these articles were in the late 1970s when I was a primary school kid.
