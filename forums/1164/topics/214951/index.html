    2.Indo-Singapore joint air exercise in November
    Wednesday, 11 October , 2006, 14:07

    Shillong: Indian Air Force would undertake a joint exercise with its Singapore counterpart at its Kalaikunda airbase in November 2006.

    During the nearly three-week long exercise in India, Singapore Air Force would bring eight F-16s and other aircrafts, Air Officer Commanding-in-Chief (AOC-in-C) of Eastern Air Command (EAC) Air Marshal F H Major said here.

    IAF would put in place a number of its aircrafts depending on the parameters and other criteria, the AOC-in-C said on Tuesday.

    The Singapore Air Force was scheduled to arrive at West Bengal's Kalaikunda airbase governed by the EAC on November 2, he said.
