    I think the most distinctive difference between Infantry, Guards and Commandos are:

    - The training they go through (Guardsman have to pass their Guards Conversion course and Commandos have to pass BCT and BAC....) this made them more specialised compared to conventional Infantryman.

    - Scope of operation (Infantry are the main fighting backbone of the army, they engage the enemies directly in battle, Like Infantry, only that Guardsman are trained at a higher level to enable them to be more vesatille, they could be inserted at any point of time during battle using helis, on the other hand, Commandos are rather differnet, they are usually inserted using airborne to conduct sabotage/ Recce missions on the enemies instead of engaging large scale enemies directly.)

    - Degree of self-sufficiency (Every commando have to be trained to be proficient with at least one skill, medic, demolitions, sniper, boatman etc... this allow them to be self sufficent and survive on their on during missions, this makes them different from Infantryman and Guardsman)

    In the past, Guards do not have a distinctive beret but wear the Infantry green beret. They were also under HQ Infantry. To differentiate them from normal infantry (SIR) and to distinct their status as elite infantry, they are given a "Guards" tab to be worn on their shoulder. Also in the past, Guards have a slightly higher "entry" requirement like PES A only, min education level and eye-sight.... In the past, hardly see any specs in a Guards unit. But now with more and more enlistees being myopic and more people with A levels and diploma, all these have changed.

    When Guards are seperated into a seperate formation under HQ Guards. They have a new and distinct khaki brown beret with backing behind the crest. However they retained the "Guards" Tab.

    The role of guards have evolved over the years, they used to be infantry which are supposedly fitter and more able compared to SIR units. Also have a rappelling role with more flexible heli-insert options.

    But now more like RDF and "Marines" type role with specialist in heli and seabourne insertions. Also they have more mobile and powerful weapons like the LSV.

    The Commando all along distincguished by their red beret. When in No. 1, No. 3 or smart 4 can see red beret. In SBO/FBO you should be able to distinguish them by their airborne wings and dagger.
