    RSAF OPEN HOUSE '11

    The Republic of Singapore Air Force (RSAF) Open House is back! From aerial displays and the latest military aircraft to popular local TV celebrities, we have it all. The 3rd Generation RSAF spreads its wings and promises to deliver a fun-filled experience for everyone. This is an event not to be missed!

    The RSAF Open House will be held at Paya Lebar Air Base from 28 to 29 May 2011. The theme of this year's Open House, “Our Air Force - Defending Our Skies”, emphasizes the role of the RSAF in defending our sovereignty and aims to share with the general public on the RSAF's capabilities and operations.

    The RSAF Open House is open to members of the public, with the following details:
    9:30am - 7pm 28 MAY 2011 (SAT)
    8:30am - 7pm 29 MAY 2011 (SUN)
    Look out for the following attractions at the RSAF Open House:

    Defending Our Skies
    Watch our pilots thrill the crowd with exhilarating aerial displays and breath-taking manoeuvres!

    Take To The Skies
    Take part in our Aircraft Rides Lucky Draw Contest and stand a chance to win a ride aboard the RSAF's CH-47 Chinook helicopter, Fokker-50 maritime patrol aircraft, C-130 Hercules transport aircraft or the SYFC's DA40 trainer aircraft.

    Up Close And Personal
    This year, the static display features a wide spectrum of aircraft and weapon systems, including our new F-15SG fighter aircraft and S-70B Naval Helicopter.
    Know Our Air Force
    Learn about how the RSAF Commands work hand in hand to defend our skies and the full-spectrum of operations that the RSAF undertakes with the other Services.

    Armed And Ready
    Aircraft enthusiasts can enjoy viewing action-packed displays as our airmen demonstrate how our aircraft are loaded and armed.

    It's All Fun And Games
    A fun-filled experience awaits you! We have many games lined up for your enjoyment. SAFRA Radio and the SAF Music and Drama Company will also be lighting up the stage with live performances.
