    What do support unit do in a infantry regiment ?
    Hang on - do you mean "support company" or "support unit"?

    A Support COY within a SIR unit consists of a mortar platoon, signal platoon, antitank platoon & pioneer platoon. Together with HQ COY & 3 line COY's, they form one infantry battalion.

    A Support unit itself could be combat-based like 3rd SIG BN, or service-based like 6th DS MED BN.
