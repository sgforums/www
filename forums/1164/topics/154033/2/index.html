    Combination is not practical.
    Submarines main advantage is stealth. It moves silent.

    The bigger your submarine, the more moving parts it has, the more drag it makes in the water... all this leads to more noise and less stealth.

    Also submarines are most vulnerable on the surface.
    To submerge you have to get your pumps working and dive. You can't just turn the ship 90 degrees and shoot down, you have to do it at an angle.
    Water resistance will also slow it's entry so bigger will be slower.
    Can you imagine how long your carrier sub will take to dive?

    Even if it's half the length of a normal carrier, it'll still take much longer to dive than a normal sub and probably make twice as much noise in the process.
