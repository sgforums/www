    Some of the books I have read on Israel -

    General -

    1) Arab-Israeli Wars - Chaim Herzog.
    Contents - the war of indepence (194, the sinai campaign 1956, the six day war (1967), the war of attrition, yom kippur war (1973), entebbe, operation peace for galilee (1982).

    2) The Sword and the Olive - Critical History of the IDF - Martin Van Creveld.
    Contents - the surge (1907-1949), the years of greatness (1949-1973), running out of steam (1974-1997).
    (I like this book the best - good and bad stuff about the IDF)

    Others -

    3) Yom Kippur war - Walter J Boyne.

    4) Six Days of War - Michael B. Oren.

    On Mossad -

    5) Gideon's spies - Gordon Thomas

    6) By way of deception - Victor Ostrovsky

    7) Vengeance - George Jonas

    (Note all the internal politics within Mossad)

    On IDF Special Operations -

    Secret Soldier - Col. Muki Betser.
    Very good book. About his own experiences in IDF's special ops. Everything from conventional wars, hostage rescue, entebbe, etc
