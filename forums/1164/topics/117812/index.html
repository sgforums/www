    You'll quickly learn about an unwritten rule in the SAF (also known as the SAF 8th Core Value): Do whatever you want, but don't get caught. I expect that your BMT instructors will be telling you this over and over again in the course of your BMT.

    All forms of removable storage media are not allowed in camp. Since MP3 players can double up as thumb drives or portable hard disks, they are not allowed as well. You can try to smuggle them in and out of camp, but if you do get caught.... what can I say.... tough luck.
