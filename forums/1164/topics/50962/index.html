    Not that advertising another forum here is particularly good form, but I would recommend TankNet to anyone who is interested:

    http://63.99.108.76/cgi-bin/ubbcgi/Ultimate.cgi

    The forum was started by a group of American armour enthusiasts, but it has since expanded to cover other military subjects. It is still quite American in outlook, but there is a fair contingent of Europeans, Australians and Israelis to keep the balance (in fact, at any given moment, there is usually at least one heated debate about the Middle East carrying on). About half of them have had military experience, and some of them have worked on interesting projects. If I recall correctly, there was one poster who was an engineer on the B2 project.
