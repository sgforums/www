    I believed you are referring to the Flag-bearing flypast? If yes, that is correct of your observation. The entry direction of the National Flag flypast is from the right to the left (from the position if you are sitting from the VIP podium).

    Therefore from the Padang/N-Stadium viewpoint, this would mean the direction of this group is, as you said, from 'east to westernly direction' (to put in this perspective, the Flag team would fly roughly along the length of the Benjamin Sheares bridge). However if you apply this direction to this year's Parade location (where the parade location is situated roughly in parallel to the Bridge), this would mean the Flag team would be flying straight towards the VIP Podium! and the view (of the Flag) could only be viwed in its full glory only when this team is about 90 degrees to the left of the podium and the VIPS & spectators MUST turn their heads to their left to see this!

    Therefore, the Flag-team this year would be from the North-Southern direction (along the waterfront of the Esplanade).
