    Yoz everyone, check this out..

    There'll be a big paintball event (the Malaysian International ECO Paintball Challenge) in KL on Jun 6th and 7th, with player configurations as big as 150v150. Each box of 2000 paintballs is going for a cool price of $180. I'm sure this will be an exciting event for all who love to shoot!

    Now, scenario Paintball is totally different from the usual paintball games you play in Singapore. To begin with, there is no middle line dividing each half of the battlefield thus allowing for flanking maneuvers and ambushes along the trails. Some 'small' gamesites can be as large as a football field.

    As the name suggests, the game is played around an overall theme (D-Day, Hamburger Hill) with many different objectives and missions for each team to complete. With rounds flying past your head and the adrenaline rush, this is easily the closest 'live' battlefield that you will want to get to.

    There is also a special offer for hardcore paintball fans! There are 2 upcoming paintball games in JB:
    ---
    Op Blue Skies, 24th May, $99.90 (Introductory Paintball Game - for newbies!)
    Op Shattered Glory, 26th July, $99.90 (Large paintball event)
    ---
    Players who sign up for either event are entitled to free transport and lodging for the KL Paintball Challenge! All you have to pay for are the paintballs you shoot. Bring your friends along, as it is hoped to have at least 40 Singaporean players! How will Singaporeans fare against their Malaysian or even International counterparts? So how, interested yet? Come join and support the Singapore team!
