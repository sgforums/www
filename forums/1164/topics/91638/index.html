    Well for Pes C you can expect to be enlisted for the modified 7 weeks BMT course, which means you'll get a so called outfield training of 5days and since you're L3 which means fit for "Operational Duties At Bases Only" , you do not have to stay outfield, in this I meant that although you would be going for outfield training, you would not have stay there overnight during the course of training. For the modified BMT, you would not have to go through SOC (Standard Obstacle Course) and Basic Close Combat Training too. You would not also be put into combat "chiong sua" vocations and most probably you would end up as a Storeman, Clerk, Driver or Technical Support etc. Since your PES (Physical Employment Status) is one of a temporarily one, you too might have a chance of being upgraded should the MO (Medical Officer) thinks you're no longer having any mental conditions, alternatively you could also request for an upgrade in your PES status through the MO (Medical Officer). Hope this helps
