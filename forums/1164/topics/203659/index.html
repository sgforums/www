    The RPG-FAE thingy would either be the TBG-7V (introduced in 198 or TBG-29V grenade. The Russians seem to have this thing with FAE, quite a few of their ATGMs are also available with FAE warheads (eg. At-6/9/13/14). Other recent adoption by armies that have "seen the light" would be the SMAW-NE in use by the USMC.

    However, point to note would be that the FAE warheads have less penetrative capabilities than HE/HESH/HEAT rounds. For maximum effect, they'd need to be fired through an opening into the room/building/bunker.

    Marine Corps Gazette:
    "Due to the lack of penetrating power of the NE round, we found that our assaultmen had to first fire a dual-purpose rocket in order to create a hole in the wall or building. This blast was immediately followed by an NE round that would incinerate the target or literally level the structure."
