    There are now many more options for APS  which have been developed in the past few years. They have now reached a stage where they are reliable, light weight  and cost effective.

    We can guess that the SAF/DSTA  is exploring these options for installation on at least our Leo2s.

    The Western systems include : -

    ASPRO-A / Trophy

    Iron Fist

    AMAP  APS

    AWISS

    LEDS150

    Hope to see any of these systems to be installed in our Leo2s as there is a limit to adding more armor.
