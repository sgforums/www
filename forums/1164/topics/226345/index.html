    Originally posted by IronBandit:
    As for the Fire-Trench... do you think the roof we create or pack can withstand the type of pounding of artillary shell ?
    Artillery fire is used to spread destruction over an area. This means that arty shells aren't designed to make direct hits on targets as small as trenches. Thus, the trench & FOPK are designed more as protection from flying shrapnel and to minimise injury from nearby explosions. You cannot compare the protection offered here to a direct pounding by shells. A direct hit would be fatal in anything less than a reinforced concrete bunker.
