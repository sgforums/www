  • Moxie
    4 Sep 04, 11:03
    Technically, it's still under-40 years old for non-officers & under-50 for officers.

    The training cycle applies only if you're with a reservist unit; that's more or less why I faithfully attended my ICTs. As a result, I was never posted out, or had to do make-up, or recycled midterm to a newly-ORD outfit.

    The call-ups were untimely at times, but their completions also meant I got my medals & then basically "retired" at age 35, as part of a unit roster that's transferred to PDF status. Better reward: No need to do IPPT anymore too.
