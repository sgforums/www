    Technical Specifications

    Maximum Range: 19 km (conventional); 30 km (extended range).

    Rate of fire: 3 rounds in 24 secs (burst rate); 4 rounds per min for 3 mins (maximum rate); 2 rounds per min for 30 minutes (sustain rate).

    No of crew: 6 - 8.

    Self propelled speed: 12 km/hr (max).
    Towed (cross-country): 50 km/hr (max).
    Towed (paved): 70 km/hr (max).
    Towing vehicle type: 5 ton or 7 ton truck.
    Engine: 21kW Lombardini 9LD625-2
