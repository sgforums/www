    How does one get selected to receive the Sword of Honour in OCS? Sword of Honour being the highest award in OCS right,what is after that then? And what is the normal sword that all commissioned officers get?Thanks in advance
  • Obersturmfuhrer
    18 Oct 05, 04:08
    Sword of Honour
    Sword of Merit
    Normal sword

    All commissioned Officers get the same types of swords. The only difference is the engravings on their awarded swords (as well as their names of course). SOH candidates have the SAF crest, OCS symbol and Singapore's Coat-of-Arms engraved on it. Other officers only have the Coat-of-arms on their swords. Not sure which one SOMs dun have out of the 3.

    SOH is just an oversized ceremonial sword which you get to keep (if you get it) for about a month or so before its returned.

    Criteria is to be the best performer in your whole cohort (depending on your arms) and there can be only one for each arm ie infantry, armour, navy, etc. There might sometimes be "politics" involved when choosing the SOH, as was experienced during my cohort.
