    This is not strictly a military subject but nuclear technology is usually assiociated with WMD.

    Firstly, as we all know fuel prices is at its all time high. It is just US$70 per barrel not too long ago and it is predicted that it may even reach US$100 a barrel in the near future. We as a tiny nation with no natural resources have to depend on foreign imports for 100% of our energy needs. Should the prices increase to US$100 per barrel which is very possible, our economy will be in deep trouble.

    One may argue that we can make use of other forms of energy such as solar power,wind etc. This will be a way to minimise use of fossil fuels but due to our land constraints it will be hard to see large wind farms anytime soon.

    Therefore i propose nuclear energy as a long term source of cheap and clean energy. We don't really need a massive reactor, we can start with a small one like what Israel have. At least by then we don't need to be totally dependant on foreign imported fossil fuels for our energy needs. Regional countries such as Indonesia and Malaysia already have research reactors

    We can have the reactor in one of the offshore reclaimed islands. It can be deep underground and surrounded by thick concrete for better protection against terrorists and minimise fallout during accidents. This island is to be heavily guarded by the KINS, CISCO,PCG maybe even the Gurkhas during peacetime.

    Accidents do happen from time to time around the world but nuclear technology is generally considered safe. Statistics have prove that nuclear accidents are relatively few compared to fossil fuel power stations. The only big problem with nuclear power reactors is waste management. But i am sure we can find a way to work things out in the future. Comments?
