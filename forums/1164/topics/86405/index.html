    A: All armoured vehicles. Not just tanks. Can include Bionix, M113 Ultra, maybe even the V200. Now that SAF has the Primus, I'm guessing Primus can also be considered "A" vehicle.

    B: All unarmoured wheeled vehicles like 5 tonner, 7 tonner, Unimog, recce jeep, command jeep, landrover, GP car, recovery truck, etc. Emphasis on unarmoured 'cos the V200 is wheeled but is armoured so it wouldn't be considered "B".

    C: I'm not too sure about this category but I believe it includes stuff like excavators, bulldozer, chain saw, trailers, tugboats, etc. Am quite sure about the last 4 I listed 'cos I was a "B" mech & I know a few "C" mech.

    I believe bridging engineer vehicles e.g. Candace, almond, comet, etc MAY also fall under "C".
