    Just to clear up some things...

    Helmets are not entirely illegal in Singapore. It is however a Controlled Item officially.

    It is under "ANECON004" in the official list which is classified as a "Steel Helmet".

    The Police Arms & Explosive Branch's stand is that it can be approved but in the event that it is not, the buyer may request for it to be sent back to the sender via a courier at his own cost.

    If it's an old helmet it's usually a high chance of it being approved since it's mainly for collectible or curio purposes.
