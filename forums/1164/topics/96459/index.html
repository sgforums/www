    Platoon Commander - Brigade Recon Coy

    Exercise duties quite clear cut:

    Lead a team of XX, including yourself and conduct insertion behind line of conflict to gather information on general enemy desposition, location of key obstacles along Brigade route of advance. Keep your eyes and ears open. (It may be your platoon but you only control XX-1 men in actual fact)

    Administrative duties include:

    1) regular stints as Brigade Duty officer (very few Lieutenants at Bde HQ so its a short turnaround time b4 your next duty)

    2) Write lesson plans for Coy training sessions

    3) Kena all sorts of arrows like investigating officer or something from HQ to organise any minor event. Worse if going overseas, the coy will kena most of the duties such as emplaning and duty-free. (Coy only has 6 junior Officers including the OC so you'll have a 20% chance of getting the arrow)
