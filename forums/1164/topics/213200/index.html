    Strv 103 is unique in that 105 mm gun is fixed in glacis plate n is aimed in traverse by pivoting vehicle on its tracks n elevation by adjusting hydropneumatic suspension from -10Â° to +12Â°, with driver acting as gunner. Armed with auto loaded 105 mm gun firing anti-tank rds (APFS/-DS), HE n smoke rds, empty cartridges cases being auto ejected from rear. It has three 7.62 mm MGs (two parallel with main gun n one on com's cupola). Normal range for 105mm gun is up to 2000 m n rate of fire is 10 rds/min.




    Strv 103 weighs 39.7 tonnes n has crew of three: commander, driver(also operating as gunner) n radio operator(also function as driver when tank is going in reverse). Max road speed is 50 kph. On-road n off road ranges are 390 n 150 km respectively.

    Strv 103 is equipped with a float-skirt n can be made ready for fording within 30 min. The driver stands on a special platform n steers with reins. After fording, vehicle can be made ready for action again within 10 min.

    Strv 103s are fitted with a dozer blade n can be fitted with mineclearing rollers at front of the hull.
