    Singapore's defence force chief visits US
    By S Ramesh, Channel NewsAsia

    SINGAPORE : Singapore's Chief of Defence Force, Lieutenant-General Ng Yat Chung, is on an eight-day visit to the United States starting Sunday.

    MINDEF said this was General Ng's first visit to the US as chief of defence force.

    He will call on the chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff, General Richard Myers, Secretary of the Navy Gordon England, and other senior US Armed Forces officials.

    General Ng will also visit the US Joint Forces Command in Norfolk, Virginia, and the Republic of Singapore Air Force's training detachments in the US.

    MINDEF said that General Ng's visit underscored the strong defence relationship between the US and Singapore.

    The two armed forces interact regularly and this has served to enhance their professionalism and inter-operability. - CNA /ct
