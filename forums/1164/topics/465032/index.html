    I did dumbbell training for my BMT, unfortunately AFTER I went in to the NAFTA fail + few months. Found it much more effective than pure pullup training, provided you work out ALL the muscle groups (forearm, biceps and deltoids) 2 months of "standard" exercises from NS and no improvement, but once I started on the weights, it shot up from 3 to 12, so I'd say it works. Procedure was 5kg weight, Right arm: Wrist Curl 10x (forearm training) Forearm lift (your standard Bicep flexing) 10x Side lift 10x (stand up, lift the weight from your waist to fully extend your arm 90 degree to the side) Left arm: Repeat above. Don't "pia" and do all in one shot on one arm, alternate L/R arms, give your muscles a few min to recover before the next set. Draining an already empty tank don't result in much improvement. 3 cycles a day week 1 4 cycles week 2 5 cycles week 3 2 days on, one day off to give your muscles time to recover. So 4x a week.
