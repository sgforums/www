    just relax la...

    the harder tests i find are the the breathing test and the hearing test. the rest are just normal stuff.

    eyesight test - look at the letter from a distance and color blind test.

    hearing test - u sit in a room with headset on and u press a button everytime u think u hear a sound.

    blood test and urine test - as per normal. dun eat anything from 10 pm onwards the previous day.

    heartbeat test - u lie on a be where they will attach a number of wires to u. u just breath normally.

    dental test - check for cavities. they will inform u if u ned to have ur teeth filled or what so no worries.

    breathing test - u suck n blow into a tube. there is a block in the tube and u have to push the block pass a certain mark to pass so blow with all ur lung muscles

    ear check - check ur ears for any irregularities

    thats about all ba... good luck
