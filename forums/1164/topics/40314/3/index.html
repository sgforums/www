    My pratical concept for the new light tank that should replace the AMX-13 SM1, introducing the SM2 tank:



    Weight maybe 15-20 tons, autoloading 120mm low recoil gun plus 7.62 coax. Commander's weapon could prehaps be CIS .50 cal or Ultra Light 40mm AGL. Armour, like the AGS, is completely modular with the maximum protection should provide resistance to RPGs and AT-4s and stuff like that.

    Crew of 3, powered by the same powerpack that runs our 5-tonner trucks, I suppose. Should give it decent mobility. Thermal sighting systems, maybe a CITV could be added also? The clean and angled lines of the hull should be calculated to manage it's radar signature and some measure taken to affect it's infrared as well. Active defences include turret mounted grenade launchers that can lay down heated smoke (to foil thermal sensors) or WP rounds to deter or flush out infantry.

    This system is the most pratical, high end type of light tank singapore, if they ever get down to it, would develop.
