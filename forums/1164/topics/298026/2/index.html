    20 Dec 07, 22:06
    Hmm. I just noticed that your model of Luminox has a push-in crown instead of a screw-down design. That makes it even more important that the gaskets are in good condition because they don't have the screw-down section helping to keep water out. Since it's a quartz model, I'd recommend having the seals checked and replaced when you change the battery. I expect there would be four gaskets on that watch - caseback, crystal and two in the crown, and gasket kits often come as a complete set.
