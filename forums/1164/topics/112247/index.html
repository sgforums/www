    Answer is "yes" we have the resources in public and private sector to design and build. Some of the electronics may have to be purchased off the shelf. But the problem is economics....

    As a rule of thumb, you need to sell about 400 aircraft of the same variant in order to just re-coup your R&D and production costs for these 400. And each aircraft is going to cost you at least USD 70m , excluding munitions. This would buy you an aircraft of about F16 D or F18 E standard.

    The RSAF is unlikely to put in an order for 400 a/c at one shot. And we are pretty sensitive about exporting our own technology to other countries. Unlikely to sell more than 150.

    So at the end of the day, Mindef might as well do a deal with the US government and manufacturers , and negotiate to buy more F16 C or Ds at USD 50+m each.
