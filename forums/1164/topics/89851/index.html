    20 Aug 04, 20:51
    some likely 8-to-5 vocations:

    Clerk (most units. some units may require stay-in, like PLAB. certain 'garang' combat units like 1CDO requires top people like CO, CI etc to leave first before stay-out personnel can leave.)
    Storeman (probably about 50% of units, and it is usually a service unit like GSMB, HQ INF. most combat units require stay in.)
    Vehicle Mechanic 'B' or 'C' (depending on your unit.)
    RP (again, depending on your unit.)
    On-course personnel usually get to stay out for the period of their course. However, the pool has just got smaller as HQ S & T has now required trainees to stay in. Note also that most combat schools are stay-in.

    stay out units:
    CMPB
    HQ MINDEF
    HQ INF
    HQ MES
    OETI

    staying out is possible if you have a medical/family/personal problem that warrants it. it is likely that you may want to consult your PC or MO about this. if your medical condition is of such severity that you have to stay out or can only do clerical work, do ask your specialist to write a memorandum regarding your condition so that the MO can make arrangements for you.

    clerical work is usually taken by PES CxL9 or E personnel. note that tis is not a definitive rule: PES E personnel can undertake vehicle maintenance, store management or combat supervising duties, while there are clerks around with other PES status, including PES A and B. this is because the posting centre will post your vocation based not solely on your PES grading but on other specific physical/medical/psychological conditions, which are determined by the MO, on the advice of specialists if necessary.
