    Depends on when both of you are enlisted. The PTP enlisted in dec can still beat you if you are enlisted in April.

    Calculate this way. I assume you not PTP. PTP will serve 2 months more than you (they serve either 2 year or 2 year 6 months). However, PTP BMT is only 16 weeks, which is 6 weeks more than your BMT. Assuming he enlisted earlier, but both of you POP on same day (subsequently enter your new unit on same day). He will ORD 2 weeks later than you 'cos 8 weeks (2 months) - 6 weeks = 2 weeks.

    I ORD on Aug 4 2002 (2 year 4 months). John ORD on Aug 22, 2002 (2 years 6 months).
