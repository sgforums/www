    The Ultimax was in service in the former Yugoslavia even before Croatia and Slovenia declared independence in 1991. I was thumbing through a issue of TIME as a 14-year-old when I came across an article about tensions in the Yugoslav state of Croatia, and they had a pic of 2 Croatian national guardsmen with and AK and an Ultimax. It was used throughout the war in the former Yugoslavia by the Croatian army and their militias, and I also saw one carried by another Croatian soldier sitting on a M-84 (Yugo built T-72) during the 1995 offensive that overran the last of Serb-held Croatia

    Sidetracking a little, the Croatians also used SAR-80s and Armbrusts. And I also saw Somalis with SAR-80s.
