    Israel to acquire Trophy APS

    By Alon Ben-David JDW Correspondent
    Tel Aviv

    The Israel Defence Force (IDF) is set to begin procurement of the Rafael Armament Development Authority's Trophy Active Protection System (APS) for its Merkava Mk 4 main battle tanks (MBTs).

    At the same time, the IDF has requested that Israel Military Industries (IMI) accelerate development of its Iron Fist APS. IMI claims its system is capable of defeating kinetic energy projectiles, as well as ATGMs, with reduced collateral damage compared to the Trophy.

    IMI has appealed to the Ministry of Defence on the Trophy decision, claiming that its Iron Fist is only three months behind Rafael in completing development. "Such a short time difference between the systems could not be the basis for a decision, especially when Iron Fist has advantages over the Trophy, both in dealing with a wider variety of threats and in reduced collateral damage," an IMI source told Jane's.
