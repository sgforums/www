    10 Feb 07, 07:24
    The Russian Kornet is still based on older technology having SACLOS laser beam guidance with direct fire profile. i.e. not fire and forget and non top attack ATGM.

    Whereas our Spike ATGM has 2 type of guidance using thermal imaging is able to lock on target and fire in fire & forget mode or in observe and update mode using the fibre-optic data link.

    It could also be used in a Non Line-of-Sight (NLOS) mode where it will be fired in a Lock On After Lanuched mode, for example over a smal knoll etc due to it loft trajectory and it's datalink.

    So Spike is more advanced technology wise as compare to the Kornet.

    Kornet has longer range of 5km whereas our Spike LR is 4 km , Kornet has greater penetration of 1200mm RHA whereas Spike has rumoured to be abt 700mm RHA but this does not matter becos Spike is a top attack ATGM where it will be going for the turret top where it has thinest armour.

    Spike also enjoy good sales with 6 nations using including 4 European nations and more is expected, most nations who is going for the latest ATGM is looking at either the Javelin or Spike.
