SENIOR FPDA OFFICIALS OBSERVE INTERDICTION OPERATION AT SEA

  • arburthen?
    15 Sep 04, 10:15
    (source: Mindef News Communique)



    Members of the Five Power Defence Arrangements (FPDA) Consultative Council, including Permanent Secretary (Defence) Mr Chiang Chie Foo, New Zealand and United Kingdom, and the Deputy High Commissioner of Malaysia, observed a maritime interdiction operation (MIOPS) serial conducted by FPDA armed forces in the South China Sea today. In the MIOPS scenario, FPDA forces exercised procedures to localise and interdict a vessel. The MIOPS serial was part of EX BERSAMA LIMA 04, a major joint FPDA exercise conducted in the South China Sea and the airspace of Peninsular Malaysia and Singapore.

    Non-conventional threat scenarios such as maritime security will feature increasingly in FPDA exercises. In June, the FPDA Defence Ministers recognised the need for the FPDA to adapt to new challenges in the regional security environment and endorsed a set of proposals for increased cooperation to tackle non-conventional security threats such as maritime terrorism.
