    - The PLA was an expeditionary force with little or no armour, arty or air aupport. So forget about combine arms etc. Infantry was all the PLA had.

    - The terrain were often barren hills with no cover and the UN were dug in on hills and mountain ridges. Some places where armour cannot climb even if they existed for the PLA. If not full frontal infantry assaults, then how else can anyone capture an objective?

    The PLA conducted some skillful ambushes that nearly destroyed a US division or corps - all done with just infantry and some arty mostly mortars.
