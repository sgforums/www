    The F-19 was the name given speculatively to the "rumoured" (then) Stealth fighter by those not in the know. Reason is as Gedanken said, there was a gap between the F-18 and F-20. Hence it seemed logical at that time that a new Stealth fighter would be called F-19.

    One of the rumoured reasons for the F-117s unique designation is due to the fact that when the USAF tested captured Soviet MiGs/Sukhois out of the infamous 'Area 51', they were given the type designations F-112 to F-116. F-117 was to disguise the true nature of the aircraft and pass it off as a new Soviet type the Americans got their hands on

    As for the other gaps in the US fighter designation series
    F-17 - Loser of the LWF competition won by the F-16. By Northrop. Only the YF-17 existed. Later formed the basis of the F/A-18
    F-19 - See above
    F-21 - Designation given to the IAI Kfirs leased by the US Navy for Adversary training
