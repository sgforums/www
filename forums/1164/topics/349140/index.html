    ''Highlights of ST Kinetics' presence at IDEX 2009 include:

    1. Bronco ATTC - the Bronco is an all-terrain articulated tracked vehicle with an unsurpassed payload of 5 tons. Its versatility is proven in the numerous configurations built - from armoured troop carrier to resupply vehicle with material handling system to 120mm mortar vehicle. On display, for the first time, is a variant that features the Platt MR550 Shielded Ring Mount and external fuel tanks - features that would be found in the 'Warthog' configuration ordered by the UK MoD.

    2. Total 40mm Solutions - ST Kinetics' 40mm payload systems provide the soldier with an unrivalled range of terminal effects from a full array of grenade launchers. ST Kinetics prides itself as the world's leading provider of 40mm solutions and on show at IDEX 2009 are:

    (i) 40mm Automatic Grenade Launcher

    (ii) 40mm LV ABMS - Airburst Munitions System for 40mm Grenade Launcher

    (PL READ BELOW for link)

    (iii) 40mm High Velocity Ammunition family, including:
    a. Combat - HE S412, HEDP S411, TPT S415A & TPM S422
    b. Self Destruct - HE-SD S427 & HEDP-SD S413
    c. Enhanced Blast/Insensitive - HE-SD EBIX S428 & HEDP-SD EBIX S425
    d. Airburst/Programmable - ABM HE S418 & ABM Flash & Bang S420

    (iv) 40mm Low Velocity Ammunition family, including:
    a. Combat - HE S405A, HEDP S401B, TP S406C, Illumination & Smoke
    b. Self-Destruct - HE-SD S433 & HEDP-SD S408
    c. Enhanced Blast/Insensitive - HE-SD EBIX S437 & HEDP-SD EBIX S435
    d. Airburst/Programmable - ABM HE S403
    e. Surveillance - SPARCS "Camera" round

    (v) REDBACK Remote Weapon System with 4 barrels of 40mm stacked electronically ignited munitions (EIMs), shown in collaboration with Metal Storm, a defence company that specialises in electronically initiated stacked projectile weapons technology
    (vi) 3GL - 40mm Grenade Launcher for Stacked EIMs

    3. Compact Personal Weapon (CPW) - The 9mm CPW is a cost effective, lightweight and compact weapon with very low recoil and designed for greater lethality and accuracy with high capacity magazines. Combining the hand held designs of compact pistols and sophisticated sighting systems with the retractable butt-stock feature of a full sized submachine gun, the CPW is the perfect replacement for handguns, submachine guns and even rifles in all aspects of military and law enforcement operations that require compact firepower.

    4. SAR 21 - Built on the "bull-pup" design, the 5.56 calibre gas-operated assault rifle is lighter and more compact than most similar class rifles. The SAR 21 boasts of many outstanding features, including low recoil, integrated 1.5X Optical Scope, built-in Laser Aiming Device (LAD) and patented safety features. The excellent system is available in various configurations to suit varying operational requirements.

    5. Ultimax 100 - The Ultimax 100 is the world's lightest and lowest recoil 5.56mm calibre light machine gun that meets all modern combat requirements. It is a magazine-fed, gas piston-operated, rotating bolt and air-cooled weapon that can be fired accurately from the shoulder, hip or bipod in full-automatic mode. Designed from the onset for ease of operation, the Ultimax 100 is an excellent weapon for various kinds of terrain and environment.

    6. HMX1100 Transmission - The HMX1100 Infinitely Variable Transmission combines hydraulic and mechanical drive technologies that deliver improved performance and efficiency for powerpack configurations from 275 to 400hp. Improvements include better acceleration, speed and cross country manoeuvrability. The HMX1100 upgrade kit offers an outstanding application for M113s and a wide range of defence or commercial tracked and wheeled vehicles.

    "At IDEX 2009, ST Kinetics is showcasing exciting solutions for the warfighters that not only enhance operational effectiveness but are also cost effective. These include our class leading Bronco All Terrain Tracked Carrier and widely recognised Total 40mm Solutions," said SEW Chee Jhuen, President, ST Kinetics''EOQ
