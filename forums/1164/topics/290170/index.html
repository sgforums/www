    Managed to catch this latest 3 series on Speed (9-10pm) this evening.

    It showcase how multinational AF came togather to train in US Red flag and profiles on Operation Maple Flag training.

    The interesting part is it featured RSAF F16D contingent which was involved in the Red Flag segment. One RSAF jockey is "Capt Kenneth" who narrator says is a 1st timer in Red Flag and hence a USAF pilot accompanies him in the F16D backseat in his 1st time in huge scale exercise.

    Don't know I heard that right, but the narrator mentioned after the trainig results of the RSAF was impressive, which includes going up against F16 Aggressors and opfor simulated SA11 SAMs. The RSAF F16s playing escorts were able to down "6 opfor jets" during that single the exercise.

    Not bad for these new groups of pilots.

    Other flights in the package group inc Gemany F4(SEAD) and CF-18 (strike)
