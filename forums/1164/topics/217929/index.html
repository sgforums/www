    Lightweight Small Arms Technologies

    Key Objectives

    * Reduce weight of weapons carried by infantrymen by at least 35 percent.
    * Lessen weight of ammunition by more than 40 percent.
    * Maintain or improve lethality and reliability over current systems.
    * Improve ergonomics.
    * Reduce training and maintenance time.
    * Ensure compatibility with the Future Force Warrior (FFW) Soldier System.

    Caseless ammo is totally cylindrical with the bullet completely inside the case.

    No extractor device needed to extract & eject spent brass case - weapons is simplifed & no jamming can occur as extraction & ejection sequence is a major cause of weapon stoppages.

    Also, no case means less weight - can carry more ammo.

    No case to eject means no problem for left handed shooters who are hit by flying cases in right handed weapons.
