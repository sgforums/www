    The term "Mechanised Infantry" is used to describe the main composition of a unit. In this case, it just indicates that that division is more heavily composed of IFVs and IFV-mounted troops, the way "Armoured" refers to a division being more tank based, or "Motorised" being more on troops being truck-mounted.

    Tank units have lower manning requirements compared with Infantry.

    For eg a tank platoon of four tanks will have 4 x 4 = 16 men

    For Mech Infantry platon four Bradleys 4 x 8 = 32 men

    Therefore, tank heavy units (armoured divisions) will typically have less men then mech infantry units.

    US TOE has the following types of divisional units
    - Heavy Div (Armoured)
    - Armoured Div
    - Heavy Div (Mech)
    - Mech Infantry Div
    - Light Infantry Div
    - Airborne Div
    - Air Assault Div
