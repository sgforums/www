    Speedbrakes are not necessarily used in landing approaches. It all depend on the lift/drag ratio in the approach speed regiime and it varies with aircraft types.

    The arresting hooks are only for emergency uses. However, when the hookwire is engaged, the runway will be closed till the aircraft involved is removed from the hookwire, i.e. nobody lands or take off right after the hookwire is engaged.
