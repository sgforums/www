    1 Jul 04, 10:42
    SAF workyear starts from Apr of everyyear.

    So if you kena something before Apr, it belongs to last workyear and something else in Sept of the same calendar year belongs to the next work year.

    I have been back for IPPT after ROD in 93 till 97 when I finished school.

    Then
    98 - kena overseas exercise 19 days
    99 - Local exercise 6 days
    00 - mob
    01 - Local exercise 9 days
    02 - mob
    Feb 03 - low key
    Nov 03 - low key

    so it is like every year since 97.
