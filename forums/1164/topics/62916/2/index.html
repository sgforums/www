    The Soviet navy did not think of carriers in the american sense, in fact the term they use is "Tyazholiy avionosyy kreyser" translated as Heavy aircraft carrying cruiser.

    That was done both to circumvent Turkey, and also because the aircraft was regarded as secondary armament.

    Just at that particular moment in time, did the Typhoon, Delta really start coming in, and Gorshkov was given the go-ahead to build escorts for them.
