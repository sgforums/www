    As for the SAR21 and M16 comparisons....
    My personal opinion only ah.. so dun get offended... =P

    Live fired SAR21 twice, dry fired with blanks alot of times and i find the M16 more stable.. the M16 is more slim and provides more comfort support because i have a long arm. the other thing is that the m16 is easier to carry around.     Conclusion is.... i give the trusty M16 a thumbsup... For the SAR21, it still needs time for us to accept it...
