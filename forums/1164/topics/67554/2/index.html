    The Armbrust I believe have amour penetration of up to 300mm which is quite limited against most MBT even by the 80s when it was first introduced into SAF.

    Most modern MBT(designed since late 70s and 80s) have armour protection of more than 300mm in the turret and glacis.
    But with introduction bolted ERA and other applique armour, it even less likely to penetrate even a older tank. It will be lucky to hit a mobility kill targeting the side and the rear of the tank.

    Therefore, the Armbrust was not designed to handle MBT but rather against APC, bunker and other light vehicles which they are more effective. It is also cheap (USD1.5k) and simple to use, but the probability of hits is quite low too especially against a moving target.
