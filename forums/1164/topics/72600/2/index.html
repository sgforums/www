    The V200 is a very unstable vehicle, even when out of the water, as it has a very high center of gravity, with its bottom being nearly 600mm above the road surface.

    Its engine is also mounted in a lop-sided corner of the vehicle, which made matters worse.

    In the water, it has no water jet or external propulsion, and depend entirely on the thread of the four tires to 'stroke' itself across the water.

    During the early days of trials in the V200 versatility in swimming across water obstacles was quite a joke.
