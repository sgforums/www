    Trembles of hand when firing revolver can be resolve in a few steps.
    1)Ask your instructor to check you shooting techniques to see if you lock your hands properly, if locked properly that will help a little.
    2) another is breathing technique. First you have to learn that you are a person that is excited by the shoot, so try to calm yourself down, trying to control your breathing, slow down your heartbeat thus excitment. Next when shooting that time, the moment you up your revolver, take a deep breath but force back 3/4 ( or 1/2 ) of the whole breath, count as you aim 1,2,3,4 at 5 fire then do the same with breath and aim 1,2,3,4 count and 5 fire. This way you will control the trembling.
