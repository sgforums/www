    Originally posted by 16/f/lonely:


    "Not under command" indicates catastrophic failure.

    Ball diamond ball, Vessel restricted in her ability to manoeuvre means a vessel which from the nature of her work is restricted in her ability to manoeuvre as required by the rule ...include but not limited to

    1) Vessel engaged in laying servicing, or picking up a navigational mark, submarine cable or pipeline.

    2) Vessel engaged in dredging, surveying or underwater operation

    3) Vessel engaged in replenishment or tranferring persons, provision, or cargo while underway.

    4) Vessel engafed in lanching or recovery of aircraft

    5) Vessel engaged in mine clearance operation

    6)Vessel engaged in a towing operation such as serverely restricts the towing vessel and her tow in their ability to deviate from their course.

     

    In the case of failure of steering or propulsion, vessels will require the signal of Not Under Command

    Vessel not under command which means a vessel which through some exceptional circumstances is unable to manoeurve as required by the rules and is therefore unable to keep out of the way of another vessel. 2 black ball and 2 all round red light.

    More specifically you should  also put up the signal of

    Foxtrot flag for 'I am disabled. Communicate with me'.

    or

    Delta flag for 'Keep clear of me, i am manoeuvring with difficulty'.

    or for engine failure.

    Romeo November flags for 'My engine is out of order'.
