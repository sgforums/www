    There are different standards in every unit. Some units have seperate WO messes while some units have WOs share the Officers mess. For decorum purpose, salutes are only accorded to commissioned officers although not many are so tight-arsed for it. WOs are given the courtesy of being called a "Sir" by specialists and enlisted personnel, and in some cases by junior officers.
