    Here's your 40mm grenade launcher.

    Used by SAF SOF. I assume for the "hush puppy" role - meaning sentry killer. I also assume that this was picked for a number of reasons.

    SAF has never believed much in sub-calibre SMG like 9mm or .45 as they are too low velocity, has round nose which lack penetration of body armour etc.

    But a 5.56 is probably not quiet enough for the silenced role.

    Hence, a PDW calibre of 5.7mm calibre was picked. (Another competing PDW calibre is H&K 4.6mm. Don't know if SAF considered this.) The 5.7mm has lower power than the 5.56 and thus more controllable full-auto. But its bullet is a good armour penetrator.

    Singapore has also picked 5.7mm as the kinetic part of the SSW - Section Support Weapon.
