    The guys over at ar15.com seem to think pretty highly of them. They are similar to the steel magazines manufactured by Imperial Defence Industries of England, and I believe they are intended for the SA-80. I have two of the IDI magazines--they're much more durable than the aluminium USGI ones, but I had to file away about 1/32" of metal from the top of the latch engagement hole in order to make them lock into the magwell.

    As a bonus, the IDI mags already come with the upgraded "anti-tilt" followers.
