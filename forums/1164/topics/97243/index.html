    10th mono here, all those stories about the statue disappeared with the renovation of the camp.

    I've heard stories about the bravo coy building (sightings, unique and unexplained power failures) and the old 622 blocks though (occasional sightings, general creepy feeling) but nothing major that shocked the entire unit.

    The only major "incident" that did shock the unit ironically happened in P.tekong where we went for exercise.

    I was told when I went in that 4SIR was by "tradition" the infantry unit to score crap for ATEC. But 10th mono changed that by getting the best ATEC score in WY 2002/03; beating CDOs and both guards units by not-a-small margin. We got a high recon 2A for ATEC, almost kena Charlie Mike for recon 1 in the process. Some c0ckups during the mission ensured that we did not make history getting the rare recon 1 score.

    However as the BCU competition considered a lot of other administrative factors that we weren't good enough to coverup/cheat at, 4SIR only got Silver (or whatever it's called) for BCU.
