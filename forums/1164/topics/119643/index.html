    The 12.7mm gun was actually meant for gunnary training , something like sub-calibre live firing, but when the Israeli were fighting in the urbanised Lebanon, it found the additional HMG quite useful on the upgraded Centurion Sh"ot , thus it is still use in combat.

    The 60mm mortar on most Israeli tank were due to bad experience when the tanks encountered ATGM manned by infantry team especially those under cover during the Yom Kippur.

    Thus 60mm mortar will save cost on avoiding using the more expensive & limited tank round against infantry , also more effective against infantry especially with it's indirect fire against undercover infantry.
