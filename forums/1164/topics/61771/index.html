    hmm ... i can remember a few 'life' lessons :

    - assume = 'ass' 'u' 'me' ... so must never assume.

    - know what you are suffering for. e.g. during safincos, trainees go in, get tekanned, go into jungle blurr blurr, get tekanned, come out blur blur. sometimes we have to take a step back and ask ourselves what the crap is going on and what exactly we are suppose to do.

    - make sure the 'small' things are taken care of. more often than not, these little small things left unattended will come back and screw up your whole mission and exercise.

    - when in a leadership appointment, behave, act and talk like a leader (even when you feel like crying). But humility and respect will make it work.

    - love your parents

    - a helping hand goes a long, long way.
