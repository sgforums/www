    My Korean friend burst out in laughter when i said that Koreans are tougher and more patriotic than Singaporeans.

    There are few reasons my friend explained to me.
    Firstly, NS is extremely unpopular in Korean due to lots of abuses be superiors, high suicides, high accident rates, poor conditions and some even defect to the north or are kidnapped.

    Secondly, ROK was more or less a repressive police state even till the 1980s. My friend even told me about how her account was taken away by the Military police overnight because he had talked about protesting in front of the blue house.
    Thus, people don't hold the military with high esteem and think that it is very dirty and full of social outcasts.

    Thirdly, a lot of the people are always trying to escape NS in Korea. There are a few ways to do so. The most popular is to tattoo yourself as tattooed people are not allowed in the military. Next, if you are rich, take an overseas degree and never come back. In korea, NS can be served when you are 20+.

    Anyway, its no big deal that most korean males are black belts. This is because it is compulsory to take taekwondo in school for PE anyway.

    In any case, i have met Koreans who think that the SAF is tougher than their ROK soldiers. The reason they give me is always the same. They say that Singapore is so warm and humid, Jungle training is just about the toughest kind of training in the world. IN korea, they train in cool temperatures and do not need to sweat so much.
