    Pretty much what baer said: odd numbers were for fighters and attack aircraft and even numbers were for other types. However, this rule was a very loose one. As observed, some Sukhois have even numbers and transport aircraft like the AN225 are odd-numbered.

    Note also that the nomenclature differs from the American system. Since the 60's the US system follows a sequential order with no mention of the manufacturer in the designation. The Warsaw Pact system, however, indicates the manufacturer and allows for number repeats, e.g. MiG-15 and Su-15.
