    a division is a higher organizational structure within an armed forces compared to a brigade..

    in some countries.. 2 brigade makes up 1 division.
    in other countries... up to 7 brigade makes up 1 division.
    in some cases... 1 brigade is designated as a division in some nation.

    the number of man per division also differ from country to country in each nation.

    a rapid deployment force RDF... is dependent on airlift to be "rapid" but in order to "fly" to a destination safely... they must first estab air superiority.

    a RDF that cannot fly.. is no different from an infantry in trucks or on foot.

    a RDF once off the air... is also no different from an infantry...
