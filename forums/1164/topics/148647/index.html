    Originally posted by lionnoisy:
    huaichien:how to search AFB of US?i think many want to know.thanks.
    Keying in the latitude-longitude is the best way to 'fly' there. You can find coordinates at http://www.globalsecurity.org/military/facility/afb.htm. Alternatively, visit http://www.wikipedia.org, their entries on military facilities often have coordinates. You have to know the name of the facility beforehand of course.

    Keying in the name of the base, followed by the state may get you there, but not very reliable.
