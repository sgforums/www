    Driving is too troublesome. Forget about it.
    If you intend to drive, the nearest available parking spaces will probably be in Simei and Tampines areas. Places like Expo will probably have their spaces full quite early.

    MRT can take to Simei MRT Stn and take Bus 9 from the bus stop directly outside Simei Eastpoint (beside Simei MRT station).

    Alternatively, can take MRT to Bedok and take Bus 9 from the interchange there.

    Last option can take Bus 19 from Tampines Interchange.
