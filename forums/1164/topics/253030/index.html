    By Wong Mun Wai, Channel NewsAsia | Posted: 17 July 2007 1635 hrs

    RSS Formidable plays central role in annual Singapore-US exercise

    SINGAPORE : Singapore's newest high-tech warship is taking part in its first military exercise.

    The RSS Formidable was commissioned two months ago and plays a key part in the annual Singapore-US CARAT exercise.

    The vessel is the first stealth frigate in the Singapore Navy.

    It is designed to be the centre of military sea operations and has been described as the most advanced warship in the region.

    Colonel Wellman Wan, Commander, 1st Flotilla, Singapore Navy, said: "What it brings to the whole CARAT exercise is (that) it levels the whole exercise one notch up, purely because of the capability the ship brings to the exercise.

    "She is a ship with good capabilities in air defence, underwater warfare and surface warfare and again, with the introduction of aviation, we should see quite interesting new areas of engagement with the US Navy."

    The ship is the first of six frigates coming into service over the next two years.

    RSS Formidable is the command and control centre for both navies at sea during the exercises.

    And for the first time, the frigate will be using a navy helicopter for anti-surface and anti-submarine exercises.

    The helicopter being used is similar to the Sikorsky naval helicopters ordered by the Singapore Navy.

    Using it in the exercise will allow the Navy to assess the helicopter and the frigate before the Sikorsky helicopters arrive in the next three years.

    The annual Cooperation Afloat and Readiness and Training, or CARAT, exercise will involve land, sea and air elements from both countries for two weeks. - CNA/ch
