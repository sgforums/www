But even while swearing, they understand that what they do is important and meaningful, and take the difficulties in their stride.

    As long as you continue to understand that NS is meaningful, that Singapore must be for Singaporeans and defended by Singaporeans, everything will be alright. Once you understand that your choices shape your life, rather than the other way around, you will truly grow up. And NS will help you understand.
