    Depending on your group size etc... You can try contacting this company:
    Golden Season
    Website contains details:
    http://www.army-technology.com/contractors/transport/golden_season/index.html

    It's a local company that markets military equipment to the overseas market...

    From previous enquiries for my own trips, they'll willingly cater so long as your order is decently big (100 plus packs). Since your trip is long, and I assume you're not going alone, maybe you can combine with a few people to order.

    Another thing to note is that since they mainly cater to the military market, they might reject your order if they aren't currently in the middle of producing a batch. They shut down their manufacturing lines if there isn't a current military or governmental order.

    Try your luck.

    Try something like this... I see a lot of this happening in the UK, when people go on their own expedition....

    Go and approach SFI (S'pore Food Industries) Corporate Comms dept. . Tell them you are going for an expedition. Ask for sponsorship.... offer to give detailed feedback on the food that they produce, in return for free MREs. Be prepared to even carry their branding on your rucksack for instance.

    Usually if you do a bit of "wayang" most large companies would give them free to you, in exchange for either carrying their logo somewhere or to give feedback on their products.

    But do look professional and show that you have organised your trip well.
