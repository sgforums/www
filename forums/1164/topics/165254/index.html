    What's all this about skipping RT or getting med cert?

    If you have legitimate medical grounds, go get a medical declaration from a govt clinic = go for a blood test/full med checkup and get a certificate. Then you need an endorsement from a resident doc at the clinic (GPs are not allowed recognized), certifying you medically unfit. There is some SAF procedure on this so you have to ask or check www.MIW.gov.sg

    Else, just go for the bloody RT and maybe you'll find actually there's nothing wrong with you. RT isn't that bad...
