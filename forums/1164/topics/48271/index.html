Malaysia interested in developing missiles

  • cavsg
    11 Jun 03, 18:12
    Malaysia is studying the development of missile technology in various countries with a view to producing its own guided missiles, Deputy Defence Minister Shafie Apdal said.

    He said among the countries considered were Bosnia and Brazil, as they have the technology to produce high quality missiles.

    Mr Shafie was speaking to reporters during a visit to the bomb and ammunition manufacturing company, SME Ordnance Sdn Bhd, in Batu Arang village near Rawang.

    SME Ordnance is a Malaysian company producing bullets and bombs.

    He said the government would make arrangements for improving cooperation and sharing of technology.

    Mr Shafie said the missile development was based on the country's needs in the current global situation, including the wars in Afghanistan and Iraq.

    He urged local companies involved in the defence industry to market their goods internationally.
