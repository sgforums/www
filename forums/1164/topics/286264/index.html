Misfired Patriot missile hits farm in Qatar

  • Manager433
    17 Oct 07, 22:16
    A PATRIOT missile hit a farm in Qatar after being accidentally fired from a base used by US forces in the Gulf Arab state, Al Jazeera television said today.

    The Qatar-based television said the missile launched from Assayliyah base did not cause any casualties.
    The Patriot system is an anti-missile system.

    Officials at Assayliyah were not available for comment.
    US-allied Qatar was a launch pad for the US-led invasion of Iraq in 2003.
    It hosts the US Army's Central Command (CENTCOM).
