Cheating Death : Rescue Choppers

    Take a look at the dramatic world of helicopter rescue, as seen through the eyes of three top pilots and a female paramedic in Israel's elite search and rescue team - the Black Hawks. From the shores of the Dead Sea to the trance clubs of down-town Tel Aviv, we follow Yoni - trance addict by night, helicopter hero by day; Yedidya - living dangerously among his goats in the disputed territories of the West Bank; Ran - dividing his time between degree studies and daring rescue; and Ravit, a woman struggling to make her mark in a man's world. We follow them into the rescue zone, where, quite literally, they hover on the thin line that separates life and death. Three very different men and one daring woman, all united by their love of flying and their willingness to put the lives of others before their own - be they soldier or civilian, friend or foe.
