FPDA member countries start 15-day Exercise Flying Fish

  • IAF
    21 Jun 03, 21:16
    By Ca-Mie De Souza

    Armed Forces from Singapore, Malaysia, Australia, New Zealand and the United Kingdom have started a 15-day joint exercise.

    Code-named Exercise Flying Fish, it is held every three years among members of the Five Power Defence Arrangements to enhance mutual cooperation.

    This year, it is hosted by Malaysia.

    Mindef said the integrated maritime, air and land exercise will be conducted in the South China Sea and in the airspace of Peninsular Malaysia and Singapore
