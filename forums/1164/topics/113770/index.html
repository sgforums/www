    Yes, agree. We should have got all our AIM-120s back.
    The agreement was NOT that we would receive the weapons when "things got hot". Rather, it was that the missiles would remain stored in the US until another country introduced similar ranged missiles first. This point was to prevent an arms race.

    Since, the AA-12 (also known as R-77) Adder has been introduced by China & Vietnam, the agreement now allows us to claim our missiles.

    For those unfamiliar with missile terminology:
    - the AIM-120 missile is called the AMRAAM (Advanced Medium Range Air to Air Missile)
    - the AA-12 Adder is also commonly nicknamed the "AMRAAMski" as many suspect it's a Russian copy and is similar in many respects.
