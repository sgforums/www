    Gday all!

     

    I'm sure some of you must still be serving at the moment.

    Has there been any changes made to the rifle cleaning kit?

     

    Not sure if this has been introduced to the SAF, it's called the BORE SNAKE.

    IMO, our steel cleaning rod should be replaced for good. The bore snake weighs a fraction of the cleaning rod. It's a pullthrough with a combination of a bore brush and rag, and you can also fit a piece of flanalette patch at the end if desired.

    While this may not be the best for a 100% clean barrel, it's definitely perfect for the field. Also, it does not damage the barrel as the metal parts are all made of brass.

     

    Hopefully a regular in the army picks up this post and introduce it to the SAF! It's not something new at all.
