    If you want to order anything in the eMart online, just go to the NS portal : http://www.ns.sg and log in with your Singpass (If you didn't have Singpass account, go to the Singpass website to register or check their counter location to register physically).

       Once you are inside NS Portal, you will see the eMart column where you can check your balance and items available online, it also have an link on the outlet locator where there are an list of stores' contact and address where you can physical get the item.

       Do notice that if you want to access to eMart on camp, you will need to get SAF 100 (or Esaf 100) by appointment before you can get in.
