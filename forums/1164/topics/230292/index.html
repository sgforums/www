    SPEAR handles prison riots, hostage situations, escape attempts & escorts high risk prisoners.

    A civilian can apply to join since training is provided. But you go thru their basic training first. Just like how someone who signs on to be a CDO goes thru BMT first. And SPEAR does NOT do regular warden duties. When there's no incident that needs their response, they just train & train. Just like how STAR keeps training daily to prepare for incidents.

    Once you see SPEAR around, it either means something big has occurred or is about to occur. Most of the time they are pretty low profile & are seldom seen, pretty much like STAR.
