    Bus services to Nee Soon camp are 167, 169 and 980.

    Method 1:
    Take service 169 opposite Yishun station (along Yishun Ave 2 outside Blk 923), alight at the 9th stop from the boarding stop.

    Method 2:
    Take services 167 or 980 opposite Sembawang station (along Canberra Rd), alight at the 12th stop from the boarding stop.
