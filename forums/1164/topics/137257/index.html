    Before we get army involved in IS work, we have the special operations command (the "ang chia" ppl remembered by the older folks). In any case, internal security operations by army is mostly POI, checkpoint manning.

    The SOP reads like the Guardroom SOP, fire rounds into the air after verbal warnings. If push comes to shove, we learnt (so many eons ago) to fire low (not necessarily at the legs, more like into the ground to bounce rounds into crowds' lower limbs (DON't QUOTE ME as this is obviously SUPERCEEDED).

    Those were dangerus times as had a case study when a guard room was 'taken over' by some rioters causing trouble etc. The Guard command and DO panicked and lost control over situation, only the G2IC had prescence of mind to shut camp gates and summon off-duty personnel. Shots were fired (apparently in struggle). Eventually BDO and SnDO were charged for losing control over the situation.
