    If the AWACs E2C or E3s can pick out this low surface skimming missile, out of the low level surface clutter, it will be doubtful if the smaller radar on the Air-Superiority Fighter Aircraft can perform this challenging task - (and much less chance for the tiny homing radar built into an "air-to-air" missile to separate ground clutter to the low level surface skimming target).

    The AMRAAM is an air-to-air anti-aircraft missile, heat seeking or infra-red guided, with possibly a homing radar of its own but requiring some initial direction guidance.

    The most suitable "Sunburn" Killer, will be either a CIWS weapon of either the multi-barrel Phalanx "Gatling Gun" system, or the Rolling Air Frame Guided Missile System - both from Raytheon.
