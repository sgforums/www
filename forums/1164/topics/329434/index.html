  • Shotgun
    4 Sep 08, 01:27

    Do well in your mathematics.

    Start playing a few flight sims to develop hand-eye-coordination skills.  Also, it helps you gets used to the different orientations an aircraft like a fighter might find itself in. 

    Stay physically fit, because pulling-9Gs require you to be fit.

    Sharpen your situation awareness.  Try to keep a mental picture of ur surroundings in your bus rides.  Whenever the bus turns, do an orientation check.   In an aircrat, it gets trickier, cos movement is now in 3 dimensions.

     

    Have fun, and all the best.
