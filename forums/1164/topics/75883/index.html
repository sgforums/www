    first 3 wks of PTP - confinement wks. but if there is a public holiday during that period, he will get to book out. the parent visit is usually scheduled on the 2nd weekend if there are no public holidays in that period.

    subsequently he will get to book out every weekend.

    he will transit into bmt after ptp. however if he passes any of the disruption IPPT tests during ptp, he will get to leave ptp, and will not need to go back until bmt starts.

    yes he can go overseas. but since he has registered for ns, i think it is better to find out if he needs an exit permit to do so.
