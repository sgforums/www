    The formal unveiling of the new 67km-range 155mm self-propelled (SP) howitzer system, developed in South Africa from the Losvoor technology base program (whose existence was first revealed more than five years ago - see IDR 1/1998, p16), was staged at the IDEX exhibition at Abu Dhabi in March.
