    The Japanese version of the F-16 called F-2 is a special variant. It is diffrent to the F-16 in quite a few ways.

    Some differences in the F-2 from the F-16:

    -a 25% larger wing area

    -composite materials used to reduce overall weight and to reduce radar signature

    -longer nose to accommodate a phased-array radar

    -larger tailplane

    -two-piece cockpit canopy

    -capabilities for four ASM-1 or ASM-2 anti-ship missiles, four AAMs, and additional fuel tanks
