    Where is Moyock?
    Moyock is in the Northeast corner of the state of North Carolina. Blackwater's maingate is located in Moyock. Blackwater ranges are in Camden North Carolina, our driveway is nearly 4 miles long.

    Are civilians allowed at Blackwater?
    All people of goodwill are welcome at the Blackwater Training Center. We are strong proponents of the Second Amendment.

    Can I stay at a local motel/hotel/bed and breakfast?
    Most people who train at Blackwater enjoy staying at our facility for its comfort and convenience but you are more than welcome to stay at one of our local bed and breakfasts or a hotel/motel in Elizabeth City or Chesapeake.

    Do you sell ammunition?
    Yes, we have ammunition and a fully stocked Pro-Shop.

    Do you sell the targets that you shoot at Blackwater?
    Absolutely, checkout http://www.blackwatertargets.com

    Can I lock my gun up in your vault?
    We have a large walk-in vault for you to lock up your guns if you desire.

    What should I wear at Blackwater for my course?
    Blackwater is located just miles from the coast and usually is very temperate...BUT always bring rain gear and if it is considered winter in the rest of the country then it may be winter in North Carolina, of course it may not but better to be safe than sorry.

    If I don't stay at Blackwater where can I stay?
    We have some excellent local B and B's as well as chain hotels. Click here for more info.

    What are the hours of the Pro-Shop?
    Monday, Wednesday and Friday - 7:30 AM - 5:00 PM

    Can I buy ammo at Blackwater and what kind do you sell?
    Yes, we keep all small arms ammo in stock. We use appx. 1 million rounds per year.

    Do you sell guns?
    Yes, all guns sold at Blackwater are transfered to an FFL in your local area.

    Do you provide cleaning equipment?
    No, we provide a modern cleaning area with compressed air, cleaning tables and sinks. Cleaning gear is available in the Pro-Shop.

    Will I get a certificate of completion?
    Yes.

    Do you have hook-ups for Recreational Vehicles?
    No, all RV's must be fully self contained.

    Do you shoot when it is raining?
    We shoot all the time...bring rain gear.

    If I fly in, do you have a service to pick me up?
    No, it is highly recommended that if you fly into Norfolk International that you rent a car for your stay.

    Can I purchase a Gift Certificate for training or targets?
    Yes, call us at (252) 436-0020

    When can I check in on Sunday?
    You can check in between 4:00 PM and 9:00 PM on Sunday. Please let us know if you will have a late check-in so we can accomodate.

    What is your normal instructor/student ration?
    1:7

    How many employees do you have at Blackwater?
    175 -- and growing

    What if I have to cancel my course?
    If cancellation is made 30 days or more prior to a scheduled course, a full refund of moneys paid will be made.
