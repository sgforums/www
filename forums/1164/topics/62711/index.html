    okay think i'll help out here abit. been in the police academy for the past 5months and i've had my share of experiences and stories to tell =P

    for starters, theres this really old tree between the echo and delta company. funny thing bout this tree is that theres a well right in the middle of it. kinda looks like the one right out from 'the ring' and it gives you a really eerie feeling everytime you look at it, even during the day. apparently, one of my friends living at the delta block witnessed an individual climb onto a chair and throw herself into the well, this was around 12midnight.

    another thing about PA is that its quite common to hear things moving in the trees when your walking alone at night.. thats why, word of advice to those getting posted to police, try not to walk alone =) there was this once i was on my way to the guardroom to report for sentry duty, this was around 8pm and it was pretty deserted already. while toying around with my baton (i was pretending to hit something), i heard somebody behind me running towards me.. then a loud female scream. but there was no one there, till now i don't know where that came from.

    of all the spooky places i've been to, none beats the PA clinic. this building hasn't been in use for sometime, how long i don't know. it looks really old, like those old buildings you see in chinatown. in the day the clinic looks okay, but at night the place totally radiates an evil atmosphere.. in fact if you look from afar, the whole place around it seems to be greenish, kinda like an aura.. step inside and theres this room padlocked with the lights on..whats strange is that it only turns on at night. unfortunately, i haven't heard of any sightings yet. perhaps some brave soul will pay a visit there and maybe spend the night?

    theres also other common stuff like seeing figures walking around in the ajoining room, waking up ouside the barracks, having people knock your windows at night, seeing figures fly past your window, female voices whispering in your ear, babies crying downstairs, oh yeah and most commonly.. beds shaking at night when you don't sleep. experienced it myself a couple of times.

    p.s ill try and take pictures next week of all all the sites i've mentioned when im back in camp. =)
