    Originally posted by fudgester:
    The platoon's radio set is carried by the platoon runner.
    Yup, I was the pl runner.

    Generally, the platoon runner will carry the longer range radioset. This will be tuned into the company net. I will only be talking to the other platoons' PC/runner, platoon support section Cmd and the Company CO/runner.

    The Company HQ will have 1 runner with a radioset the company net and a signals-equipped landrover to communicate on the Battalion net and beyond.

    Less common depending on the nature of the mission, the PC, Platoon sergeant and other Section commanders can have small handheld sets to talk to each other within a "platoon net".
