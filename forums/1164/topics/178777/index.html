    Welcome to P company. Welcome to depot para. Outside of the various selection courses for the various elements of special forces, this is arguably the hardest course in the british army
    This is a one hour documentary about a group of trainees trying out for P company. P coy is the 3 week selection course that all who want to enter the Parachute Regiment ('The Paras') must pass. This documentary of course cannot begin to depict accurately the entire course since it has condensed the course to one hour.

    The docu was screened at around 1990, but I have been told that the format of the course is still largely the same.
