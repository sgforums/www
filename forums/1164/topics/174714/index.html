    Was wondering, how would the police/SOF react in the event of a Hostage or Terrorist situation involving a bus. As we know, a bus is basically a rectangular shaped container with seats and wheels. Personally feel that the level of technical difficulty is much higher then that of say...an aircraft,building or ship.As the space constraint is more so on a bus and the number of shooters able to be in the bus when the shooting begins is way lesser.So if lets say someone hijacks one of them and threatens to blow it up or start killing people if their demands are not met and STAR/SOF & CBRE responds.

    Have read in foreign magazines/books and saw videos that the British SAS basically will swarm the bus from one side or from both the front and back. Primary weapon being that of the handgun for fear of over penetration. While a containment is setup around the site, snipers may be called in to deploy around the bus while negotations are carried out. But of course, what we are interested in is when the word "Go" is transmitted over the radios.

    The magazines say that from "Go" assault teams already in position will move tactically towards the bus, while the Snipers may be tasked to take out visible suspects/tangos. Once in position, the assault teams will put ladders in place, break the windows with hooligan tools and throw in flashbangs.Once those go off, in the window they go and the shooting begins.

    However, as I take a double-deck bus to school almost every morning, I wonder how is it possible to do a double decker take down? Have yet to seen any feasible way of raiding a double-deck bus full of hostages and tangos.
    Please share if you have any info, if OPSEC is of concern, pls.pm.Thanks!
