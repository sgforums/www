    The competition involves 8 segments. China won 2 gold medals in the individual events, 30 rds pistol.     Ok, this are some of my thoughts on this event. Considering this competition is an international one involving so many nations, all nations most probably send their best shooting teams to the competition.     AFAIK, the SAR-21 uses the normal M-16 barrel length, so their ballastic features must be more or less the same. The bullpup cuts the overall length by changing the configuration, not shortening the barrel. There are SAR micro versons I heard that use the M-4 carbine barrel length, and these are even smaller.

    I think bullpups and conventional layouts have their own pros and cons, it really boils down to how well you want to adapt your tatics or make new ones to make up for them.

    But to be objective, the SAR-21 offers some very important pros over the M-16. Targets are a lot easier to engage with the SAR as you do not have to worry about a proper sight picture, if you sight down the scope properly you have it there liao. Also the compact frame of the SAR makes aiming easier and you can snap off a shot a lot faster. Under the stress of battle this is extremely important, and equates to an increase in combat effectiveness, not to mention your chance of getting out alive.

    In the cons section reloading takes slightly longer, but I think I prefer having each of my 30 rounds easier to aim and shoot then to trade it for faster reloading.

    Aye, not to mention the weapon jams far less and is easier to maintain as well. More importantly, it can eat more sand and mud and keep going after the M-16's bolt is stuck in the buffer. That said, I like that little plastic rifle despite its quirks that has made some people detest it. And the LAD is extremely useful in FIBUA and night fighting if used properly as well.

    But they really should give the SAR a proper set of iron sights, you know, for the old fashioned grunt in all of us. Sometimes when I shoot with the SAR, I realized using the scope backup pistol sight is good enough, and allows you to adopt a more comfortable firing position without worrying about putting your head to the scope. And yeah, it works up to 200 meters on fig 11!
