    Sukhoi 30-MKM Fighter Planes To Be Stationed At Gong Kedak

    PASIR PUTEH, Kelantan, Sept 21 (Bernama) -- The Defence Ministry will station its fleet of Sukhoi 30-MKM fighter planes at the military airport at Gong Kedak near here.

    Defence Minister Datuk Seri Najib Razak said the ministry would take delivery of the 18 Russian-built planes from 2005 onwards.

    He said various facilities would be built at the airport base to accommodate the planes.

    Najib said this to reporters after attending a subscription dinner in aid of education here lSaturday night.

    -- BERNAMA

    Footnote: Gong Kedak is in Terengganu, Peninsula Malaysia.
