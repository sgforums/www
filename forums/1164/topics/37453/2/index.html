    When a multiple explosion form a salvo of MRLS rockets is to fall into a neighborhood of several blocks of HDB flats, the enclosed areas will be ideal in multiplying the pressures of the multiple explosive effects of the salvo.

    The block that has all the salvos falling around it, will suffer the greatest damage.
