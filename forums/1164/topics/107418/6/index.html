    I saw the GLADIUS at the SHOT Show. Pricey, dude. Surefire is already getting expensive and now Blackhawk introduces GLADIUS at US$200+.....nope, no thanks. I understand a light is not a light anymore but most folks stateside are always looking for performance without the hefty price. I remember paying US$50 for my Z2 when it first came out (Gen I). Still have it but it's pretty battered especially around the bezel.

    Anyway, we were looking at a bunch of other lights at SHOT when our rep brought someone to our booth to show us some new lights. The first light I saw was one to compete directly with the 6P, but at 75 lumens with 90 to 120 minutes of continuous use......US$40. That was just for starters; he started pulling out some other weapon mounted lights and I just kept on drooling. I called some friends (former SOF guys) over to check them out and suffice to say everyone agreed that this guy's lights are a great buy.

    Last weekend, we tested these on M4s and the small ones using Rogers or Harries during a tactical 3-gun match and although I realized that these weren't Surefire, they got the job done very well and inexpensively. I ordered 200 of these and plan to showcase them at this month's Global Security Expo in Singapore.

    On a separate note, High Speed Low Drag is a term we usually use for Operators.
