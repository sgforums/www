    TRS-3D

    Naval Surveillance Radar
    General Information

    Multimode acquistion radar for surveillance and weapon assignment. Automatic detection, track initiation, and tracking of air and sea targets, in particular of small fast low-flying threats.
    Key Features

    *
    Automatic and simultaneous detection and tracking
    *
    Fast target alarm
    *
    Calculation of the target data and determination of target type
    *
    Effective suppression of sea, rain and ground clutter.
