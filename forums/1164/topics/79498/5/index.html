    1) there is this thing called snatching where u 'compensate' the recoil by either jerking down (most of the time) or jerking up just before the pin engages, dats how ur shot misses.

    2) u might not place ur eye really behind the rear sight...so ur shot 'leans' left or right accordingly

    3) rare : when u squeeze the trigger, the gun snatches left/right just b4 the pin engages

    4) shaky hands

    5) the 3 'dots' not horizontally aligned...for those guns with the tritium dots to aid in dim conditions.

    6) the sights are 'screwed up, the frontsight gets dented but u still think its perfect

    7) for moving target, where u point is not where the shot will land as the target itself is moving and u must 'compensate' by aiming 'ahead' or foolishly 'track' the spot.
