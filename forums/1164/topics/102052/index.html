    Electric demolitions are initiated by an electrical denotator which is set off by electrical source (i.e. a wind up, like the old plunger you see is movies).

    Non-electric are initiated by non-electric means i.e. burning fuze or chemical reaction (timer fuze). Both have advantages and disadvantages.
