    The Army Open House 2007, or AOH 07, is a signature event for the Army. It serves as a showcase of the Army to the Singapore public and our defence partners. The AOH 07 would be held at Pasir Laba Camp from 31 Aug 07 to 3 Sep 07.

    Ferry services to and from AOH 2007 at Pasir Laba Camp is available at Jurong Camp One (See *1 on map) and Boon Lay Bus Interchange (*2 on map).

    From Jurong Camp One/Boon Lay Bus Interchange to Pasir Laba Camp:

    First shuttle service - 9.30am
    Last shuttle service - 6.30pm

    From Pasir Laba Camp to Jurong Camp One/Boon Lay Bus Interchange:

    First shuttle service - 9.30am
    Last shuttle service - 9.00pm

    Limited carparking lots are available at Jurong Camp One. Visitors are encouraged to take public transport.
