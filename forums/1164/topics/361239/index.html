    In 1983, the RSN ordered six Victory class corvettes from Fredrich Lürssen Werft of Germany.[15] The first corvette was built in Germany while the remaining five were built locally by ST Marine. The corvettes were also the first class of ships in the RSN to have an anti-submarine capability.[16] The corvettes are noted for their tall mast, making them top-heavy compared to ships of similar class. However, this is suitable within the fairly calm Singapore waters.[citation needed] The six corvettes form the 188 Squadron of the RSN.
