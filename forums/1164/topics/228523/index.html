    The SCAR is much more like An FNC over the M16/M4 type!

    Bascially the M16 and M4 as we know looks kool. very accurate and ergonomics is the best! but it has an inherit problem whereby the gas system having to blow back into the bolt carrier that create 1) access HEAT in Full auto firing 2) carbon built up! thus reliability suffers!

SO to answer most questions, the FNC type action will be much more reliable than M4! hands down!! erognomics may not be there..

    Not saying M4 is not good just there are better system out there.     basically instead of having the direct gas impregnation which the gas is blown from the hole in the barrel to a gas tube and blowing directly into the bolt carrier to move the bolt carrier back. We have the gas blowing into a piston which in turns move the bolt carrier back.

    Bascially without the gas blow back into the bolt carrier, you don;t get all the hi pressure crystalized carbon, hi temperature in sustain firing. thus a much more efficient system of operation with less heat!

    IN that! you get a much more reliable weapon in all ways ! ALso reason the SCAR is being use ! it uses a piston just like the FAL, AUG, AK, SIG, HK G36 etc etc! the direct gas system of the M16 is flawed in the begining.
