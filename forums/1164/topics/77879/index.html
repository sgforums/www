    As have been mentioned by the other members here, they will mail the SAF100 to you, then if you don't respond by x number of days, they will send it via registered mail, and if you still don't respond by x number of days, they will personally deliver one. So unless you did not notify MINDEF of an address change, or become untraceable, its quite unlikely that an SAF100 will not reach you.

    At my last ICT, my S1 was not too pleased that a large number of us did not respond to the first SAF 100, and most only reposnded after the registered mail and some only after the unit had to send a personal courier!
