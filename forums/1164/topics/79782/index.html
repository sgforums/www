    What is the selection criteria in the choice of balaclavas being used. There are three known common types, differing mainly in the areas of the face that are exposed, and some cases differing in materials, e.g. either Nomex or other polyester material

    The types of balaclava known:

    a. Single slot for both eyes exposed
    b. Double slotted for both eyes exposed
    c. Triple slotted for both eyes and area between lower nose and upper mouth exposed. Eye slots are slanted towards the nose to show a 'frowning' look

    Personally feel c. is the most fearsome balaclava design, favoured by SAS/IRA
