    ok, if u sign on right after u POP/POC from tracom during your police NS time, u sign on 5 yrs which includes ur 2.5 yrs NS. during the 1st 2.5 yrs, u will receive regular pay (without NS bonus) after 2.5 yrs u receive the NS bonus included in ur pay.

    Typically, u will normally be posted to land divisions as NPCO for a year or so. posting varies, if u would like to be posted to a certain branch, pls let your NPC's CO/OO know so he can recommend u if that certain branch needs people.

    For STAR, u just go for the 1 day 'screening', if u clear it, you go for a 12 day in-camp selection, if u clear it again u go for 4 mths of basic training and viola, u are a STAR officer. then u will go for other more specialised courses.

    For PCG, obviously u need to go for a course to learn how to drive the boats etc...

    If u sign on after your army NS, its 5 yrs with the NS bonus included in all your pay packets.

    As an NPCO, u will need to patrol, attend to cases and lodge report for complainants, there is also CSSP activities and etc....its running a 12 hr shift system....after a few weeks u will get the hang of it.
