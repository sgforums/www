    Spec. of MG3:

    http://www.world.guns.ru/machine/mg08-e.htm

    BTW, I just found out the Swedish desgination of MG3 is Ksp-94.

    The latest variant of Ksp-58 is Ksp-58DF:



    The new modifications include:

      A new and shorter flash suppressor, in order to reduce the length of the weapon.

      10cm shorter barrel, in order to shorten the weapon and reduce the weight.

      Cutouts along the barrel(fluting) in order to reduce the weight and increase its heat emitting capabilities.

      A new gas regulator - reduced to four positions is made at half of the trail weapons. The last position is painted red and is intended for "emergencies".

      The carrying handle is shortened to half its original length, in order to increase the space to the benefit of added sightsystems.

      A MilStd 1913 "Picatinny Rail" is added. Half of the weapons has a adjustable rail - the others a fixed.

      During the trails the weapons are equiped with a Aimpoint red dot sight (the same as used by the AK-4B).

      The trigger group is marked with the weapons serial number.

      The safety levers positios S and E have been colored.

      A retractable buttstock has been added and half of the trail weapon also have gotten a butt rest.

      Half of the trail weapons are painted black, the other half is painted green.
