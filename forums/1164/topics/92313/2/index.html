    The 84mm Gun is a company level support weapon. Key word "support". And it remains in many armies around the world as good support weapon.

    It is a 84mm "section" within the company, and not platoon size (only 9 men). All 84mm rounds come in 2-round canisters.

    All 84mm team members should know all this. I've been in the section since 1990. Me and my buddy have lots of 84mm gun stories to tell. Try carrying the gun in Brunei for one.

    There is a lighter, updated model of this gun. Not too sure if the SAF will consider it or replace it with something else. (lightweight AGL??)

    But the most important thing about the 84mm gun is the round it fires (eg. airburst HE rounds).

    Just want to add my view here:

    Matador LAW :
    1) Against thin skinned & light armoured veh(IFV , light tank).

    2) No tandem warhead, thus ineffective against ERA armoured veh.

    3) Penetration is at least 450mm RHA as reported, Pzfaust 3 has a cone penetration ratio of 6.36, so Matador could have penetration up to 572mm RHA, since both rocket technology are from the same company.( my guessitimate)

    4) Secondary anti bunker role and able to fire from confined space.

    5) Effective range 400m

    Additional info is where I have posted in the new forum.

    84mmRR:
    84 has many kind of ammunitions , thus it is quite muliti purpose, below are the interesting one.

    1)FFV-751 Tandem-HEAT could penetrates 500mm behind ERA, thus effective against ERA armoured AFV.

    2) FFV441 is a HE Sharpnel round, useful in a "lobbed" trajectory to 1,000m, which can be fused to fire on impact or airburst.

    3) FFV502 is a dual-use HEDP round for anti bunker role.

    In conclusion , the 84mm is more multi purpose and have much longer range than the Matador, especially the Tandem warhead which is effective against ERA thus much more effective AT weapon as compare to Matador. New warhead are still being developed for this weapon.

    Currently, the Spike/Milan(depending on unit) are our dedicated ATGM system against MBT, the missile are much more costly,therefore for multi purpose role, our 84 will be much more cost-effective.

    ST has developed the 14 kg SLWAGL is light enough for infantry personnel to operate. It could be deployed as another coy level support weapon as a two men portable weapon system.
    With AMBS it could air bursting capability.
