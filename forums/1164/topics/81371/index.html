    Oh yeah, once you strip it down to the bolt cam pin, that's enough. You should have these parts with you at the end:
    1. Upper & lower receiver group (still together)
    2. Corking lever
    3. Carrier group
    4. Retaining pin
    5. Firing pin
    6. Bolt
    7. Bolt cam pin

    No need to furthur strip the bolt and the lower receiver group, unless you intend to clean them.

    There's also a certain arrangement for the items stripped.

    if i still can remember,

    STRIPPING:

    step 1:
    remove magazine
    step 2:
    pull back charging handle and check chamber clear of rounds(VERY IMPT)
    step 3:
    turn fire selector to safe
    step 4:
    push out catch pin
    step 5:
    remove bolt carrier group and charging handle.
    place handle on the ground.
    step 6:
    remove holding pin from bolt carrier. if need be, use your fingernails and dig.
    once out, place on the ground.
    step 7:
    remove firing pin.
    place on ground.
    step 8:
    push the bolt in, twist the bolt cam pin and pull it out.
    place on ground
    step 9:
    remove bolt. place bolt and carrier on ground.

    ASSEMBLING:
    just do e reverse in order.
