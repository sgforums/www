    F-16's advantage is that it can climb faster due to its high thrust to weight ratio. Being able to climb fast is a dogfighter's best ability.

    Having more thrust to weight ratio also means u can quickly get into ur maximum "corner" airspeed, faster than the opponent can.

    2ndly, the F-16 has a slightly lower aspect ratio when compared to the F/A-18C. 3.2 vs 3.5, and for the super bug, aspect ratio of 4. The lower the aspect ratio, the faster it can roll. Rolling is a crucial to close air combat since, u need to roll the aircraft into the correct lift vector to bring the enemy on ur nose.
