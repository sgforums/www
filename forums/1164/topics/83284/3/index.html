    More tekong stories ... afterall i was there for 8 whole months training, and got to know lots of people who later stayed on as instructors.

    All accounts are 2nd to 3rd hand, and in all cases i know the person involved personally. Seems tekong still isn't "clean" despite all the "yang" energy on the island.

    ***

    Just a weird one to start things off ...

    My sispec section had finished a night section exercise. We checked ourselves clear, had all our expended cartridges collected while still outfield. Then we had our arms cleared by our instructor using his torchlight, then was declared and searched (blah blah usual).

    As we walked back to our coy line, we passed our canteen. My trigger-happy section mate was playing with his trigger all the while we were walking. It was going "click click click ..." as there was nothing in the M16 chamber. Suddenly, his rifle just went *bang* and I saw a flash come out of his M16 as if he had just fired a blank round. Yet his ejector port was still closed.

    I was rather near beside him on his right. If he had loaded his rifle while on the way back i would have known. If he had been on safe mode, then suddenly put it to fire and squeezed off I would have known too. If he closed the ejector port after firing off, I would have seen it too. Furthermore he had no reason to firing off so near our instructor and risk burning 3 weekends for mis-firing.

    The strange thing was that we could not find the expended cartridge anywhere at all. It was a big main road, and was rather well lighted by the streetlamps. But still all our 9 plus pairs of eyes couldn't find that one cartridge.

    In the end, we all pretended nothing happened that night and went back to our coy line.

    ***

    A case similar to Joshua1975's "gui zher yan" experience in Tekong. I was in sispec then, same thing my coy was doing uncontrolled torpor. But this case happened in bright daylight. A particular group was doing torpor (area i forget liao), and they just could NOT get of this certain area. Mind you it wasn't in the vegetation, it was a open place with roads. But no matter how they walked, they seemed to be circling the area. All 4 members of the group were torpor-trained and had maps/compasses ...

    In the end they just gave up and sat down on the road. After some time a safety rover stopped in front of them, and the group kena f*** for non-tact behavoir, and for actually sitting down in the middle of a road. From that point, whatever spell over the area seem to be broken, and the group could finally walk out of that area.

    ***

    Some of my friends got posted to BMTC as instructors and I heard some of their personal stories. Apprently the bridge along the School 1 SOC ground is "dirty". My friend (or one of his prowlers i can't remember) actually saw white figures of what looked like children playing underneath it at night.

    And they're not the only ones to see all white figures that same bridge. There are a few stories about that place. Some claimed that they had seen a female white figure, some say japs and so on ...

    ***

    A freaky one ...

    My friend (let's call him B) was doing guardcom in his aircon room when he saw an old man on 1 of his circuit TVs. It was rather late at night then, so he radio-ed the prowlers to go there to check it out. Upon arriving there, the prowlers radio-ed back and claim that there was nobody there at all. But B could still see the old man on the CCTV.

    Thinking that the prowlers were playing a trick on him, B went down personally to the spot, but it was as his prowlers said, there was nobody there. B, puzzled, radio-ed back to his guard-2IC and asked him if he could see the old man.

    His guard-2IC radio-ed back "He's right above you all ..."

    And they all fled the spot.

    One night I found everyone in my bunk has decided to stay out. Others went to sleep in other bunks with their friends.

    I decided it would be OK to sleep alone in the long shaped bunk. I left some lights near the door on.

    I dreamt I saw a black shadow running back and forth across the breath of the room near the door. In my dream I got and and ran towards the shadow and grabbed him. That's when I woke up. I was sleeping facing the door exactly the same as my dream. I went back to sleep until morning - nothing happened.

    Some hokkien peng commented on my experience and said it was good that I got up in my dream and try to grab the shadow. It meant I was stronger and that's why I slept peacefully the rest of the night.

    Part 2 of Ulu Pandan Camp.

    A few months later, one Saturday afternoon everybody has booked out. I was very tired so I cleared the desk of the S2 branch office and I climbed onto it to sleep. I was facing the window and it was a bright sunny afternoon and i could see the swimming pool and the coconut trees swaying in the breeze.

    For some reason I remembered the shadow incident and all of a sudden, in my tiny little office a man's low voice laughed. He laughed in the most "b-grade" horror movie fashion. Damn tacky.

    But I was suddenly frozen. I couldn't move. I could still look around the office and still see the the coconut trees swaying in the breeze. But I couldn't move a muscle.

    Slowly, what seems like eternity, I managed to move my fingers. I tapped them on the table. Slowly then faster.
