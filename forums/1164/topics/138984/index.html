    I was along esplanade drive and caught glimpse of the helio flypast carrying state flag and 21 gun salute... Some tourists seemed bewildered and walked in towards Padang as the music came from there.

    Then finally came the long-awaited RSAF flypast and mobile column. I must say it's the first time I saw RSAF F16 with afterburner!! KC-135 was flanked by 4 F5s, very majestic indeed! I was proud of it. Sadly, the skyhawks were out this year. I was hoping to see Apaches this year but it's still stuck in USA.

    The mobile column was a great catch. But the AMXs looks like some antique tank used in WW2 when blended in with the bionix and primus. There were more than 200 over vechicles. Also saw the new police Subaru highway patrol car.

    Anyone with more information from the HDB estates and anyone who was there watching the flypast and mobile column please share too. Anyone with nice pics please share also.
