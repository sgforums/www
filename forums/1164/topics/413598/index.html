    Originally posted by dlima:

    they are calling after i graduate, but from then onwards, any ways to prolong enlisment date?

    In that case, no.

    Your excuse for deferment will last only as long as you are still studying at JC/poly level or equivalent.

    Once you've graduated, the excuse disappears.

    Even if you are planning to go to uni, you will still be expected to serve out your NS liability first.
