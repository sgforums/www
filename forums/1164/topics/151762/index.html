    for our inf 84 section during typical field ex. The following are common SOP items carried by second gunner in his canister or by the section lobos.

    1 Packet "ang hoon" or "drum" (last resort then open)
    A few packs of ciggie per person (personal/shared)
    A few packet maggie mee
    a few small cans of cocktail sausages or spam
    1-2 bottles of coke
    3in1 nestle coffeemix
    3in1 'supercoffeemix' mai pian
    butane fuel and messtins
    Dog biscuit(stored in personal sbo pouch last resort then open)

    Our section has been know to bring "bak kua" and sometimes stash a small bottle of cheap whiskey but we never let other section in the company know. twisted:
