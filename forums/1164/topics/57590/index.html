    The AR15 assault rifle family (carbines not included)

    M16 - No Forward Assist, 3 firing modes: Safe; Semi and Full Auto
    M16A1 - Forward Assist, 3 firing modes: Safe; Semi and Full Auto
    M16A2 - Forward Assist, 3 firing modes: Safe; Semi and 3-round burst.

    The M16S1 built in Singapore is an M16A1 copied lock stock and barrel. Literally.

    The A1/S1 has no 3 round burst facility, I know because my unit used to go full auto on our rifles to finish up rounds after range.
