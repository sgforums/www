    40mm Air Bursting Munition System (ABMS)

    ST Kinetics' 40mm Air Bursting Munition System (ABMS) is the next generation of 40mm munition systems providing greater firepower in terms of higher lethality and improved accuracy. This system can be used on current automatic grenade launchers such as CIS 40AGL, MK 19 MOD 3, ST Kinetics' Super Light Weight Automatic Grenade Launcher (SLWAGL) and other AGLs.

    ST Kinetics' 40mm ABMS comprises:

    * 40mm Air Bursting Munition (ABM)
    * a 40mm Air Bursting Weapon Upgrade Kit
    - Fire Control System (FCS)
    - Ammunition Programmer (AP)
