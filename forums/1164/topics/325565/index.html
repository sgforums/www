    er.. does it hurt getting hit by a airsoft round? how you guys figure who got hit or who "dies" during games.. care to explain airsofters?

  • 4Justice
    1 Aug 08, 19:00

    Airsoft is largely based on the honor system. Depending on rules used for each round, most of the time if you're hit anywhere you're dead. Once you're hit, you shout "HIT!" loudly, put yr hands up in the air and stand up. As you go back to respawn shout "dead man walking" so people know u're dead. Generally hits to any part of your gear or you counts as hits. Some sites consider weapon hits to be hits, others don't. There are also bang rules, tag rules as well as surrender rules, which govern close quarter engagements. Also most sites have marshals to make sure people take their hits and ensure fair play.

    As for whether it hurts, it depends on how close you are to the opponent when he fired at you. Generally, yes, it hurts a bit, but not that much really. Being a girl myself, I say that since I can stand the pain of getting my eyebrows plucked, airsoft hits are nothing.
