    Not sure what is the standard load-out for a "Grenadier". But in reality very few people actually have involved in "real" ops before. right?

    I don't think the "FlashBang" is a standard issue. The use of Flashbang is predominantly to protect hostages from being harmed. in a SAF FIBUA scenario, everyone in the building is considered hostile, so no need for FlashBang. In a Anti-Terror or HRT or SOF scenario, the above will not apply.

    To answer your question, LIVE firing Fibua in Singapore is conducted near Sungei Gedong camp (gotta report to this camp to commence firing here).

    The ball projectile will not bounce around the wall as the fibua room has been designed to prevent this. The side walls are fortified with rubber matting just like those on running tracks. The wall you will be shooting at has dense rubber curtain reinforced with steel mesh. There is state of art ventilation system in place so that we will not be breathing in lead dust.

    Only Ball Projectile up to 7.62mm is permitted to be fired here. No tracers, wadcutters, hollow points etc.
