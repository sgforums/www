    RSN recently did quite badly (IMO) when it comes to naming ships. It cannot decide whether it wants to use an adjustive or verb or noun.

    Just the the MCV Victory class for example:

    1. Victory (n.)
    2. Valiant (adj)
    3. Valour (n.)
    4. Vigilance (n.)
    5. vengeance (n.)
    6. Vigor (n.)

    Here we see a ship name in adjective form in the mist of names in noun form.

    Take another example, the new Endurance LPD class, it consist of 4 ships with the names are (not necessary in order):
    1. Endurance (n.)
    2. Resolution (n.)
    3. Persistence (n.)
    4. Endeavour (n.)/(v)

    Here the names are consistently in the form of noun, which makes sense.

    I hope some RSN people will take note of this. BTW,
