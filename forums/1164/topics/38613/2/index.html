Final outfitting as well as the sea trials will be carried out by Penang Shipbuilding, the yard of the customer.

The newbuilding l aid down in Kiel on November 13, 2001, which has been built according to the Blohm + Voss developed and patented MEKOÂ® concept, has a displacement of 1650 t and is especially suited as coast guard ship.

    The patrol vessels have a length of 91.1 m, a breadth of 12.85 m and a draught of 3.4 m and are equipped with two 3616 Caterpillar diesel engines each producing a power of 5450 kW. With two CP propellers a speed of 22 knots can be reached. The crew comprises 78 persons with a reserve of 15.
