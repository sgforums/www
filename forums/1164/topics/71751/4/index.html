    then maybe radar A-G ranging is not right and you have to re-do the transformation for the HUD etc

    Of course when you put in the black boxes you have to consider cooling and routing new air ducts ...... then run tunnel tests on the airflow around it .....

    After you lay the cables (for the A-4 it was really tough because the conduit to the cockpit is only 4 to 5 cm dia! So how? Cut another hole in the frame and risk structural integrety .... ) and after all the boxes connected you need to do EMI/EMC testing......

    Then you need to put in (more like stick on) all the sensors and data logger to monitor Gs, heat etc for the prototypes ..... where to find the space?

    Hey it's not easy! I know! But it's easier the second time around and yes it takes a long long time.
