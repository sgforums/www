    I like, "Be the master of your weapon."

    The weapon should come up to a position that is to ur comfort and stability. Don't tilt the head to look thru the sights, cos tilting is very inconsistent. ALways bring the weapon up to u, not ur head down to the sight. MASTER the weapon.

    In prone positions. Bring ur body closer to the weapon to aim lower, and push ur body further away to aim higher.

    As for the 1/3 lung capacity, When in doubt, just hold ur breath. ATP or BTP doesnt give u the luxury of time to practice breathing controls... thats more for snipers or sharp shooters.

    Don't stare at ur foresight tip. FIX ur gaze on the target and bring the aim onto the target instead.

    Don't pull the trigger. GENTLY Squeeze it.

    FOLLOW through! Keep ur gaze ON the target and if possible minimal movement in ur body other than inevitable recoil action.
