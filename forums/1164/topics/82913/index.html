    The SPIKE article is a joke. The author for that article mixed up the Rafael's SPIKE missile and the SPIKE missile which is being developed by the Naval Air Warfare Centre. Yes. There are TWO missiles which are named SPIKE.

    I'm sure we are damn familiar with the Rafael SPIKE which we are using, but the other SPIKE is a very small and VERY cheap missile with an IIR guidance, which means it is essentially fire-and-forget. However, its warhead is correspondingly small, and its target set consists of mainly thin-skinned vehicles or lightly armoured vehicles at most. In fact, this missile was designed with the Korean theatre in mind, for use against saturation attacks by N.Korean attack boats.
