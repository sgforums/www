    First of all, they cannot be classifed as LMG, as they are merely upgraded assault rifles given a heavy barrel, bipod and (sometimes) open-bolt firing. The British call their upgunned SA-80 Light Support Weapon.

    LSW weapons like the SA-80 LSW or the SAR-21 LSW have a built-in IA/stoppage problem: every 30-round you have to stop and change magazine.

    It's bad enough that people try to turn 30-round mag assault rifles into support weapons but it's worse when these weapons are bullpup. Changing mags must be hell especially when prone with bipod. Not much of a "support weapon" if you ask me.
