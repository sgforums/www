    certainly robust. we have carried it across swamps, rivers, etc. and it will still fire.

    Using live rounds, very few malfunctions...the integral scope which is part of the body/handle is factory-zeroed and won't shift/change in any condition.

    Using plastic blanks, due to insufficient pressure/gas from the lower amount of powder, there can be quite some malfunctions....

    It has also been tested to be rolled over by 5-ton trucks (it actually weighs 8 tons and can carry 5 tons)...with no problems. The specially engineered polymer material is nearly as strong as metal....
