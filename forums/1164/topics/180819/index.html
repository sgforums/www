    A competition for long-range recce and rescue.

    What is ERNA ? One of the world's longest and most difficult military competition, Erna Raid, is organized by the Erna Society of Estonia. This is to commemorate the actions of Erna long-range recce group in the summer of 1941. In addition, the Erna Society organises a seminar about national defence, the competitions Utria Assault and the Avo Sults memorial competition with the Off-road Club.
