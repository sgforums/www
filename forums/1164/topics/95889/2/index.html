    As requested the various classes of body armour explained.

    The National Institute of Justice (NIJ) standards usually used are:-

    Level I (22 LR; 380 ACP; 38 Special)
    Protects against .22 LR Lead Round Nose of 2.6 gm (40 gr) at 320 m/s (1050 fps); 380 ACP Full Metal Jacketed Round Nose (FMJ RN) of 6.2 gm (95 gr) at 312 m/s (1025 fps) and 38 Special round nose lead bullets of 10.2 gm (158 gr) at 259 m/s (850 fps). Also provides protection against most handgun rounds in calibers 25 and 32.

    Level II-A (9 mm; 40 S&W; Lower Velocity 357 Magnum)
    Protects against 9 mm Full Metal Jacketed Round Nose (FMJ RN) of 8.0 gm (124 gr) at 332 m/s (1090fps); .40 S&W caliber Full Metal Jacketed (FMJ) bullets of 11.7 gm (180 gr) at 312 m/s (1025fps) and .357 Magnum jacketed soft point bullets of 10.2 gm (158 gr) impacting at a velocity of 381 m/s (1250 fps). Also provides protection against threats such as 45 Auto, 38 Special +P and some other factory loads in 357 Magnum or 9 mm.

    Level II (9 mm; 357 Magnum)
    Protects against 9 mm Full Metal Jacketed Round Nose (FMJ RN) bullets of 8.0 gm (124 gr) at 358 m/s (1175fps) and 357 Magnum Jacketed Soft Point (JSP) bullets of 10.2 gm (158 gr) at 427 m/s (1400fps). Provides protection against most other factory loads in caliber 357 Magnum and 9 mm.

    Level III-A (High Velocity 9 mm; Submachine Gun 9 mm; 44 Magnum)
    Protects against 9 mm Full Metal Jacketed Round Nose (FMJ RN) bullets of 8.0 gm (124 gr) at 427 m/s (1400fps) and 44 Magnum Semi Jacketed Hollow Point (SJHP) or semi-wadcutter bullets of 15.6 gm (240 gr) at a 427 m/s (1400fps). Provides protection against most handgun threats.

    Level III ( High-Powered Rifle)
    Protects against up to 6 hits of 7.62x51 mm full metal jacketed bullets (U.S. M80 Ball) of 9.7 gm (150 gr) at 838 m/s (2750 fps). Also provides protection against threats such as 223 Remington (5.56x45mm FMJ), 30 Carbine FMJ, and 12 gauge rifled slug.

    Level IV (Armour-Piercing Rifle)
    Protects against one hit from .30-06 Armour-piercing (U.S. Military M2 AP) of 10.8 gm (166 gr) at 868 m/s (2850 fps).
