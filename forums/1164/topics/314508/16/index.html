    Technically, a flick knife refers to a switchblade ie. a button to press to activate. That's what Cantonment told me the last time. Unfortunately, ICA guys have no idea what a flick knife is and keeps interpreting it as being able to 'flick' it out, which puts some flipper and BenchMade knives in that category. Irritating to have to run down to ICA then Cantonment. If you wanna be sure, you can always email Cantonment to check. A friend of mine did that before he ordered this huge-ass folder, and printed out the email to go to ICA. Funny thing was, ICA decided to clear the folder that day without the email. Heh.

    That aside, I'm clearing some 13 folders of mine with a mix from CRKT, BenchMade and Kershaw. PM me if anyone's interested.
