NLOS Spike

  • Sepecat
    22 May 10, 14:04

    There is a new version of the Spike.

    The NLOS Spike has a 25  km range and can be launched from vehicles, ships or helicopters. Instead of a fibre optic cable as used by in the SAF Spike LR  version , the 70 kg  NLOS Spike uses a  wireless link.

    A very interesting weapon especially for ground forces which can now call on a high precision army controlled  weapon to attack high value targets up to 25 km away.   No need to wait for an Apache or  F16. Various warheads are also available for NLOS.

    Imagine being able to call on the NLOS Spike to take out  a heavy tank when one does not  have a Spike LR readily available.

    There is currently no comparable operational weapon of this type in the world.  

    Leveraging on an already proven system , this cud also be a very cost effective weapon to posses.
