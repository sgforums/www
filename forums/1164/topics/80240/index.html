    Did my ATEC during ICT around 97' or 98'. It was a long time ago - but I remember seeing two of their 3 tonners driving into a ditch after our ATEC exercise - it was raining and conditions were muddy, quite a funny scene actually.

    We had a 2A grade - and was watched over by a certain foreign advisor as well. Quite an experience, since we were reservists going against full-time NS guys.

    Nothing really special about the aggressor company to me - just another unit playing "enemy". My unit used play enemy to a number of infantry units during my NS days, this was before the SAF formed a fulltime aggressor company. Playing enemy is a good thing - we get to see mistakes made by other units, and learn from it. My unit learned enough to get us the first redcon 1 grade ever during NS.
