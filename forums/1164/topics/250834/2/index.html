    Kota Tinggi complek or correctly "Pegarang fort" (sic). It had an AA battery, lookout towers plus protective pill boxes on hill ridgeline; lookout and ranging towers for the guns of Changi costal battery (so called johor battery) and intergrated with the other facilities on Tekong (Camp I and II) and P Sajahat (small island in between Tekong and Mainland). all part of the integrated defence for southern Malaya.

    From top of Pegarang, you can see all the eastern approaches out to some 30km (depending on visability) and can easily spot shells for the 'johor' battery as defence of fortress Singapore, worth a visit if they allow you to climb the hill (not sure now bcause part of RMN training facility??)
