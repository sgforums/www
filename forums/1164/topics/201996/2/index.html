    Firstly i will like to say that due to my inability to divulge any SOPs for tank crews, i can only contribute my views based on common sense and knowledge avaliable in public domain.

    The SM1 have 2 MGs and can fire HE and CAN rounds as mentioned earlier. CAN rds are mainly for anti-personnel use but can shoot at B vehicles if they are within the effective range of the CAN rds and that there are no friendly troops around. HE is effective for all B vehicles and APCs but not the MBTs. The two MG can be used against infantry and B vehicles.

    In my opinion these weapons are at the disposal of the tank crew to engage the enemy so its up to the tank commander's discretion to decide what is appropriate to be used to fulfil his objective and mission.

    For example, he can choose to order the gunner to fire a HE round at a troop carrying military truck then spray it with MG to kill those troops escaping from the wreackage. Or he can just fire the CAN round at it if at close range and subsequently spray with MG. These are just some examples of different combinations.
