    13m Fast Craft Equipment and Personnel (FCEP)
    The 4-tonne Fast Craft Equipment & Personnel (FCEP) is a high speed shallow draft landing craft capable of carrying passengers such as a platoon of troops in full battle gear or even a 4 tonne vehicle. It is designed to be able to beach or retract fully laden on typical sandy shores.

    23m Fast Craft Utility (FCU)
    The 18-tonne Fast Craft Utility is a high speed shallow draft vessel capable of carrying passengers or wheeled/tracked vehicle(s) of up to 18 tonnes. It is designed to be able to beach and retract fully laden on typical sandy shores.
