    Bhagavad-gita As It Is

    Bhagavad-gita covers five basic truths: God, the individual soul, the material world, action in this world, and time. It was spoken by the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Sri Krishna to His dear friend Arjuna, 5000 years ago. In this conversation Krishna transformed His friend from perplexity to spiritual enlightenment and each one of us is invited to walk the same path.


    Krishna Book

    Krishna Book is a summary study of the Tenth Canto of the Srimad-Bhagavatam. 5000 years ago Sri Krishna descended from the transcendental world to show us His eternal spiritual activities and attract us to join Him again. This book narrates those extraordinary transcendental activities of Sri Krishna.


    Sri Brahma Samhita

    Lord Brahma saw Sri Krishna directly and composed a prayer based on his realizations. Overwhelmed by this transcendental revelation, Brahma spoke these words glorifying Krishna and what he saw. Brahma's awe and love permeates the text while giving an overview of the workings of the cosmic creation.


    Nectar of Devotion

    This is a summary study of Bhakti-rasamrita-sindhu, the book analyzing the various stages of bhakti (devotion) as a methodical practice resulting in love of God. Discover all the intricacies of spiritual love, bhakti, in this book.


    Nectar of Instruction

    Here we will find instructions which will lead to a nectarian lifestyle. This book contains eleven short verses giving essential instructions on how to clean our heart and prepare it for loving devotion to Sri Krishna.


    Isopanishad

    The 108 Upanishads are considered the essence of all the Vedas, and Ishopanishad is foremost among them. Discover the essence of all knowledge in these eighteen enlightening verses.


    Teachings of Lord Chaitanya

    Five hundred years ago, Chaitanya Mahaprabhu gave the gift of love of God to everyone He met. This book , a summary study of Sri Chaitanya-charitamrita , documents Sri Chaitanya's conversations with the great scholars, kings, saints and mystics of His day.


    Teachings of Queen Kunti

    Queen Kunti, a tragic and heroic figure, emerges from an explosive era in the history of ancient India . Her teachings are simple yet reveal the deepest transcendental emotions of the heart and the deepest philosophical and theological penetrations of the intellect.


    Teaching of Prahlada Maharaja

    Prahlada Maharaja, a 5-years old boy was put into so many difficulties by his atheist father, yet was saved simply by remembering Sri Krishna. This book teaches the Science of God, as explained by Prahlada Maharaja.


     

    Teachings of Lord Kapila

    This book describes Lord Kapila's teachings to His mother Devahuti. Lord Kapila showed her the path of Sankhya -- the analytical pathway to a higher understanding of the world, the self, and the ultimate source of everything.


     

    Science of Self realization

    This collection of articles by Srila Prabhupada from Back to Godhead magazine covers knowledge of the soul and bhakti -yoga. This book also covers topics such as the goal of human life, spiritual teacher, reincarnation, super-consciousness, Krishna & Christ, and spiritual solutions to today's social and economic problems.


     

    Civilization and Transcendence

    In a series of questions and answers, Srila Prabhupada defines real progress as self-realization, i.e. to transcend this world and awaken our spiritual nature.


     

    Elevation To Krishna Consciousness

    Spiritual elevation is a simple matter of reawakening our original God consciousness. This book teaches how to see the truth of this world and follow the path toward Krishna , God.


     

    Matchless Gift

    The matchless gift explains how to obtain permanent freedom from material suffering. Here the steps to achieve it are laid out one after another.


     

    Coming Back

    Birth and death deal with the essence of our being. Reincarnation after death is a fact until we choose to break the bondage. Coming Back gives common examples from the Vedas that explains the truth of reincarnation and how to break the bondage of reincarnation.


     

    Perfect Answer, Perfect Question

    In 1972, Bob Cohen, a science teacher in the Peace Corps, began a search for spiritual enlightenment in India . A series of meetings with Srila Prabhupada transforms his life. Here is the transcript of the life-transforming meetings.


     

    Path Of Perfection

    The Path of Perfection is a series of talks by Srila Prabhupada on Bhagavad-gita. Srila Prabhupada deeply explores the philosophy of yoga, nature of consciousness, techniques of meditation, karma, death, reincarnation, and even spiritual ecstasy.


     

    Topmost Yoga System

    Although there are many types of yoga practice, the Vedic literature explains that no matter which practice we choose, success is only achieved when bhakti is present. This book explains what is bhakti-yoga and how to apply it in our practical life.


     

    On the Way to Krishna

    Every one of us is searching after happiness, but practically very few people are happy. This is because we do not know what real happiness it. This book describes the real happiness which could lead us to Krishna .


     

    Chant and be Happy

    This small pocket edition answers a lot of questions that may encourage us to Chant and Be Happy. Also includes fifty pages of exclusive conversations with George Harrison and John Lennon.


     

    Life Comes from Life

    This book is based on taped morning-walk conversations between Srila Prabhupada and his disciple T.D. Singh, Ph.D., an organic chemist. Srila Prabhupada's vivid analysis uncovers the hidden and blatantly unfounded assumptions that underlie currently fashionable doctrines about the origins and purpose of life.

     

    Prabhupada-summary of lilamrta

    In reading these accounts, the reader will be struck with Srila Prabhupada's personal qualities—his strength of purpose, his genuine humility, and his deep spirituality. Srila Prabhupada's life, as revealed here, is a ideal model that he set forth for others to follow.

    Cook Book -Kurma dasa-Big size
