    THE BLESSED SWING FESTIVAL

    OF

    THEIR LORDSHIPS SRI SRI RADHA KRISHNA

    JHULAN YATRA

    TUESDAY 9th AUGUST 2011

    Gauranga Centre 13 Cuff Road (Off Serangoon Rd)

     

     

    Dear Devotees,

     

    We welcome you this Tuesday evening, 9th August 2011, to join us in celebrating this exquisite festival where we will all have an opportunity to swing Their Lordships Sri Sri Radha Krsna and offer them flowers amidst sweet kirtan and wonderful association of devotees!

     

     

    5.00 pm – 5.15 pm  - Kirtan and Arati

     

    5.15 pm – 5.45 pm  - Class

     

    5.45 pm – 8.30 pm-  Swinging of Their Lordships, Offering of flowers

                                        and Kirtan

     

    8.30 pm – 8.45 pm  - Sayana Arati

     

    8.45 pm onwards-     Ekadasi Prasadam

     

    Your servant
    Devakinandan Das

     

       P.S We will continue the Blessed Swing Festival on Wednesday evening at H.G Selvam Prabhu and Arpana Mataji's residence, on Friday evening at H.G Anand Prabhuji and Anupriya Mataji residence and once again at the Gauranga Centre on the final and most important day, Saturday (which is also Lord Balaram's Appearance)! Be a part of this most life-changing and spiritually charged experience!
