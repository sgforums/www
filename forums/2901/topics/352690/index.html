Rath Yatra Festival in Puri, Orissa, India

  • Bangulzai
    21 Mar 09, 02:07

    The biggest and the grandest of all festivals, the highlight is the the sacred journey of images of lord jagannath of puri with brother balabhadra and sister subhadra from main temple to Gundicha Temple, where they remain for nine days. The mammoth wooden chariots carrying the three deities pulled by thousands of devotees present a spectacular scene The Ratha Yatra in puri is a spectacular riot of colour and noise.
