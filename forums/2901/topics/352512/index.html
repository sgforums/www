    Dharashiv : 6000 Hindus resolve to crush fanaticism

    March 18, 2009  ::  http://www.hindujagruti.org

    Falgun Krushna Saptami

    Dharashiv (Maharashtra): Dharashiv Ruled by the ‘Yadava’ dynasty became Usmanabad after Mughal invasion. Once again the fanaticism is getting stronger owing to attacks on Hindu Dharma and this nation. It is necessary to crush this attitude, mind-set and bring Hindus together and educate them on matters related to Dharma. Today, in the Hindu Dharma-jagruti Sabha held at Pushpak Ground, 6000 devout Hindus resolved, in the thunderous cries of ‘Har Har Mahadeva’ to crush every calamity fallen on Hindu Dharma.

    Inauguration of Dharmajagruti Sabha by lighting Samai by Digitaries

    The ‘sabha’ started with recitation of ‘Veda-mantras’ by Neelottama Jevalikar, Veda-murti from Tulzapur and lighting of lamp by H. H. Brah. Shri. 108 Neelakantha Shivacharya Mahaswamiji Upakhya Dhareshwar Maharaj. H. H. Dhareshwar Maharaj besides, Shri. Abhay Vartak, spokesperson of Sanatan Sanstha, Shri. Madan Sawant, special correspondent of Sanatan Sanstha, Shri. Vinay Panwalkar, chief of Dharma-shakti Sena and Shri. Ramesh Shinde, spokesperson of Hindu Janajagruti Samiti (HJS) for the State of Maharashtra addressed the meeting. Shri. Santish Patane, correspondent of staunch pro – Hindu periodical ‘Dainik Sanatan Prabhat’ was felicitated at the hands of H.H. Dhareshwar Maharaj. Shri. Vikram Ghodake, district-coordinator of HJS presented review of activities of HJS. The program was compered by Mrs. Sunita Dixit, advocate and Dr. Shripad Pethkar.
