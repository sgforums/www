    THE BLESSED SWING FESTIVAL

    OF

    THEIR LORDSHIPS SRI SRI RADHA KRISHNA

    JHULAN YATRA

    FRIDAY 20st AUGUST 2010

    Singapore Gujerati Society 13 Cuff Road (Off Serangoon Rd)

     

     

    Dear Devotees,

     

    We welcome you this Friday evening, 20th August 2010, to join us in celebrating this exquisite festival where we will all have an opportunity to swing Their Lordships Sri Sri Radha Krsna and offer them flowers amidst sweet kirtan and wonderful association of devotees!

     

     

    7.30 pm – 7.45 pm  - Kirtan and Arati

    7.45 pm – 9.00 pm  - Swinging of Their Lordships, Offering of flowers

                                            and Kirtan

    9.00 pm – 9.45 pm  - Ekadasi Prasadam

     

    Your servant
    Devakinandan Das

       P.S We will continue the Blessed Swing Festival on Saturday evening at Lakshmi Narayan Temple, Sunday morning at the Sri Veeramakaliamman Temple and evening at the Sengkang Temple, Monday at H.G Balakrishna Prabhu and Bhaktidevi Mataji's residence and once again at the Gujarati Society on the final and most important day, Tuesday (which is also Lord Balaram's Appearance)! Be a part of this most life-changing and spiritually charged experience!
