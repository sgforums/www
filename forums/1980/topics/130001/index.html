    Jedi Powers

    Emptiness
    Jedi equivalent to Rage, when you come out of Emptiness, all force skills are greatly increased. After a short time period, Force abilities are decreased until the Force User has a chance to rest. Any Force User in using the power is in danger until he has come out of it.

    Wall of Light:
    A Jedi creates a Wall of Light around a Dark Sider and strips him of his ability to use his powers. This power is defensible despite many Jedi's belief, however, several Jedi can team up and use this power making a dark sider incapable of defending.

    Dark Side Powers

    Rage
    DJ equivalent to Emptiness, when you come out of rage, all force skills are greatly increased. After a short time period, Force abilities are decreased until the Force User has a chance to rest. Any Force User in using the power is in danger until he has come out of it.

    Doppleganger:
    This is the ability to harness the force in order to create a "clone" of the user. The user may control the clone though the force. These creations are exactly like user save one aspect, No clone can touch the force. Only one dopplegangered clone may be created at a time and a user must enter a state of rage in order to create the clone.

    Feed on Dark Side
    Allows a dark sider to channel the dark side emanating from people with dark
    emotions and feelings, making them stronger.

    Force Lightning
    Corruption of the force that allows user to issue blue lightning bolts from their
    fingertips

    Transfer Force
    Allows user to transfer their life force into another being, causes escape from
    death. Any Jedi who uses this power to escape death immediately looses access to the Light Side of the Force.

    Inflict Pain
    Allows a Dark Sider to cause extreme pain in an adversary.

    Control Mind
    Allows a powerful Dark Jedi to take over the mind of someone nearby, making them slaves and puppets for the Dark Jedi controlling them, a Dark Jedi cannot take over and completely control more then one person at a time and great concentration and time is needed though he can alter the will of a small group for a short time.

    Create Force Storms
    Either storms of nature where the Force rages or Great destructive Storms created by Dark Siders.

    Telekinetic Kill
    This power allows a darksider to kill someone they are not even touching. The
    favorite methods of this is the old sith choking method.

    Force Scream
    This power is an involuntary reflex of a Dark Sider as a result of frustration from battling a Jedi. If a dark Jedi is being beaten and frustrated by a Jedi he may post himself doing this which greatly injures himself and all those around the area within 50 meters. A GM may also post a dark jedi using this power.
