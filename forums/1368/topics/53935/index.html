    Aries

    You are a dreamer at heart, dear Aries, and prefer not to think about the more material things in life. This explains why you tend to forget to grocery shop or pick up the dry cleaning; you're happiest if these sorts of things are done for you. Today, however, circumstances will force you to handle some of the more mundane chores of everyday life. We hate to bring you down to Earth in this way, but such errands must be done. You will feel better, by day's end, just knowing that you can be self-sufficient, even though you may not want to be!

    Aqaurius

    This is a day of untold opportunity, dear Aquarius. If you have been concerned that you have not been utilizing your creativity to its fullest potential, today you can put that concern to rest. Your creativity, after all, is boundless. You will finally realize it. Be on the lookout for any unusual opportunities, as someone or some opportunity you encounter could have a meaningful ripple effect for you.

    Cancer

    Today, dear Cancer, is a day to separate the wheat from the chaff. Or, in other words, to take a good long look at your relationships and decide who is a true friend and who is not. You are very loyal, which is certainly to your credit. But what is the point of being loyal to people who do not return the courtesy? You have given your so-called friends more than enough opportunities to demonstrate their affection. As difficult as it may be for you, it is time to let these relationships fall by the wayside. Look at it this way: this will free you up to spend more time with your true friends.

    Leo

    Whether you have acknowledged it or not, dear Leo, creation is the central theme of your life. If you are not an artist or writer of some sort, you should be! There are a tremendous number of creative ideas floating around in your head, just itching to be expressed in some medium or another. Why not give it a try? There's nothing to lose, and you will likely be astonished by what you are able to produce. See, your brain really does have two sides. It's time to use them both!
