Upturning the Downturn

  • Denise Pang
    22 Oct 09, 20:33

    When business drops bosses find themselves with idle time - but it shouldn’t be. In busy times you hear the cry of ‘not enough time’. So why, when there is enough time, aren’t we doing the things we couldn’t before? Being demotivated by bad business makes us all lethargic; bad for morale, bad for reviving the business. Now is the time for getting the business back into shape, training for the upturn, planning for the next expansion. Mentoring can get individuals re-energised, retrained and ready for the step forward. Now is the right time to do it.
