    Here goes:

    From the year 2006 onwards:

    - The number of modules needed to obtain a full Cambridge International
    Diploma in Business have been reduced from five to four.

    - To obtain a Full CID in Business, you just have to obtain a pass of two core modules and two optional modules.

    - Business Finance is now a Optional module instead of Core module.

    - Certification will be given for each and every individual certificates, including the core modules.

    - A new grading is given. Candidates will be graded as Pass, Merit or Distinction.

    (All of the above is applicable for both CID in Business at Standard & Advance Level)
