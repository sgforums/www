    This is a rough idea of how to break it up:

    1. Introduction (100 - 200 words)

    - define the basics of economics (eg. it is a social science)
    - give brief intro of how consumer demand creates markets for commodities by combining it with supply.
    - end introduction by specifying what issues you are going to address in the body of the essay (you can either rephrase/repeat the question asked, so that the reader understands what you are going to address later)

    2. Body (1500 - 1700 words)

    - list, define and explain the various theories on consumer demand
    - illustrate and discuss the difference between the 2 curves.

    3. Conclusion (100 - 200 words)

    - basically a summary of what you've written in the body.

    Hope this helps!
