    The Forbidden City Chamber Orchestra consists of 10 famous musicians from the China Conservatory of Music – Liu Shun (Music Director), Yang Jing (Pipa), Shen Cheng (Huqin), Zhang Zunlian (Erhu), Zhao Chengwei (Sanxian), Wei Wei (Liuqin/Ruan), Jiao Shanlin (Percussion), Yang Lin (Guzheng), Wang Hua (Dizi) and Luo Yuan (Yang Qin). 

    Date: 28 Jan 2012 (Sat)

    Venue: Esplanade Recital Studio

    Time: 3pm and 7.30pm 

    Programme: Kindly refer below for more details

    Ticket Price: Standard tickets at $35 and there are limited concessions for students, senior citizens and NSFs at $19
