    Programme
    Ives Holidays Symphony
    Vivaldi The Four Seasons, Op. 8

    The affable Taiwanese-American violinist Cho-Liang Lin adds a touch of cheer to this energetic and vibrant concert. He conducts the SSO from the violin in Vivaldi's The Four Seasons, easily four of the best-loved violin concertos, with infectious melodies and characterful portrayals which make them some of the earliest examples of "programme" music. Ives' Holidays Symphony, in celebration of four key American holidays, adds a distinctively American flavour to the evening.
