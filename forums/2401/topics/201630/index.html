    Yesterday at VCH is the stars-cladded night

    The honors awards concert starts with the presentation of Plaques and certificates to the various Prize Winner , for the Certificate of Participation, Distinction , Silver and Gold Awards

    First on the performance is Raffles Girls Symphonic Band with the Baton of
    Mr Takehiro Oura
    with the Inn of Sixth Happiness by Malcolm Arnold

    then Tanjong Katong Girl's School Symphonic band with the <> by Mark Camphouse with the Baton of Ms Sia Swee Suan

    Next our Community band heroes arrived ,in the form of Sembawang Symphonic Winds Conducted by Andrew Ng give us the repertoire of
    Noah's Ark by Bert Appermount

    Just before the Intermission ,we have the Temasek Junior College Symphonic Band (with Alumni) conducted by Mr Lim Ah lek performing
    Machu Picchu - City in the sky by Satoshi Yagisawa

    After the Interval ,Aichi Meiden High School perform the Ceremonial March by Takamasa Sakai

    then ,an excellent surprise from the ordinary schoolclothes boys and girls of Aichi Meiden which out of surprise they turn to the audience to
    perform the handclap beat for the American Graffitti XV by Naohiro Iwai

    there was cheers from the audience at their arousing rendition of American Graffiti ,so unique ,so nihongo in style

    Then the Maris Stella Wind Symphony(with Alumni)
    performed the Invictus by Philip Spark
    under the baton of Mr Lim Ah leck

    and Last but not least of all , our academic top brass
    fields in for us another rendition of the Macchu Pichhu -City in the Sky by Satoshi Yagisawa

    all in all ,the night was filled with musical surprise and technical passage
