    well regarding PT drills... all you need to know is:

    1. Commands
    everything will be given almost exactly the same, just the addition of "dengan lompat" (with a hop)

    Squad, dengan lompat, sedia
    Squad, dengan lompat, senang diri
    Bergerak ke kiri bertiga-tiga, dengan lompat, ke kiri, pusing
    Bergerak ke kanan bertiga-tiga, dengan lompat, ke kanan, pusing
    Menghadap ke hadapan, dengan lompat, ke belakang, pusing
    Menghadap ke belakang, dengan lompat, ke belakang, pusing

    2. Execution
    simply hop on the spot when either coming to attention and at ease or also when turning...

    remember that when turning (while hoping) either to the side or back... you do not swing ur arms around, just keep them locked by the side...
