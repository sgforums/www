    The NCDCC Cadet Uniform

    -NCDCC No. 4 should have their sleeves folded 4-5 fingers apart form the shoulder rank.

    -NCDCC No. 4 are to accompanied by the SCDF Belt for the pants.
    -Make sure the SCDF Logo can be seen on the belt.

    Civil Defence Related Badges to be put on top of the Left No. 4 Pocket
    - Maximum of 4 badges in a row,
    - SCDF-NCDCC, CD Knowledge, NCDCC Unit Best Cadet bage should be place in a Triangular shape.

    Non-Civil Defence badge should be placed on the right of the No.4 Uniform, just on top of the Velco Name tag.
    - Maximum of 4 badge in a row
    - Total Defence Badge to be placed on top of all non-CD Related badges

    Footdrill badges (CD Related)
    - To be placed on the left arm of the uniform.

    Civil Defence Badges
    - Fire Safety
    - First Aid
    - Camp Crafts
    - Shelter
    - Rescue
    - CPR
    - HAZMAT
    - USAR
    - Footdrill
    - CD Knowledge
    - NCDCC Unit Best Cadet
    - SCDF - NCDCC

    Non-CD Related Badge
    - Total Defence
    - Adventure
    - Swimming
    - Life-Saving (Sea)
    - SANA
    - National Heritage
    - Homefront Security

    Ranks For Cadets
    - CDT (Sec 1, No.4 Uniform)
    - LCP (Sec 1, No.4 Uniform)
    - CPL (Sec 1/2, No.4 Uniform)
    - SGT (Sec 2/3, No.4 Uniform)
    - SSG (Sec 3/4/5, No.4 Uniform)
    - WO* (Sec 3/4/5, No.4 Uniform)

    Ranks For Officers
    - OCT
    - CLT
    - OCT

    - LTA
    - CPT*
    - Maj*
    - LTC*

    * = Approval / Interview By NCDCC Promotion Board or NCDCC Commandant
