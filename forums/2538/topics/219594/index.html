    ok.

    National Civil Defence Cadet Corps = NCDCC

    We're formed in 2005 and is a part of the Home Team.

    We have a few similarities with our brother, the NPCC:
    - The way we put our badges (Non-NCDCC, NCDCC related)
    - Located in the Home Team Academy, next door to HQ NPCC

    Also We Do HAVE Differences:
    - Have SPECIAL Courses:
    ~ Fire-Fighting
    ~ Urban Search And Rescue (USAR)
    ~ Hazardous Materials (Hazmat)
    ~ Rescue
    ~ CPR / First Aid
    ~ Civil Defence Shelter
