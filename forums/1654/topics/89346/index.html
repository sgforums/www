    Any good places to recommend

    1. Changi Village (Charlie's Corner: western food and beer)
    2. Changi Village (nasi lemak)
    3. Siglap (Coffee Club, Thai Express, Cheesecake Cafe)
    4. Changi Airport Terminal 1 & 2
    5. East Coast Park (new hawker centre: fried carrot cake hippo like)
    6. Jalan Kayu (more north than east, along TPE)
    7. Simpang Bedok (milo dinosaw, roti prata)
    8. Bedok Central (but forever over-crowded, good for takeaways)
    9. Changi Village eat duck mee by rounding about the small carpark
    10. bedok blk 85 (bbq stringray, bak chor mee, prawn noodles)
    11. Geylang Lor 9 (frog porridge, beef hor fun, Taiwan BeanCurd)
    12. bedok blk 511 (mee sua)
    13. Geylang Lor 17 (dim sum)
    14. Still Rd - Black Pepper Crab
    15. Circuit Rd Hawker Centre - Kway Chap
    16. http://www.supper.com.sg/mt/archives/cat_east_food.html
    17. Old Airport Road hawker centre
