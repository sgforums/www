New Kallang Way Flyover

    Motorists can use the new Kallang Way Flyover from Sunday

    Motorists travelling between Sims Way and Nicoll Highway have a new and shorter route to try out, come Sunday morning.

    Motorists travelling on the Pan-Island Expressway towards Changi can make use of the new two-lane, Kallang Way Flyover to head towards Sims Avenue, Guillemard Road and Nicoll Highway.

    The flyover is located at the interchange between the PIE and the future Kallang/Paya Lebar Expressway.

    The Land Transport Authority says motorists using it could save nearly 20 minutes during peak hours and 10 minutes during off-peak hours.

    They also avoid four traffic junctions along Kallang Way, Aljunied Road and Lorong 22 Geylang.

    This flyover is the second of three flyovers costing a total of $256 million.

    The first flyover was opened last year September and the third flyover will open mid-2007.

    The three flyovers will form the 12 km Kallang/Paya Lebar Expressway that will stretch from East Coast Parkway to Tampines Expressway.

    And two-thirds of this expressway will be underground.

    Prior to the opening of the Kallang/Paya Lebar Expressway, several roads will be closed in the wee hours of the mornings of Friday, Saturday and Sunday for the placing of gantry signs and other works. - CNA/ch
