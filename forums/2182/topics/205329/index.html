    Temporary Closure Of Slip Road From CTE (AYE) To Upper Serangoon
    Road At Whampoa Flyover

    As part of our maintenance programme to apply protective coating, the Land Transport Authority (LTA) will be temporarily closing the slip road from CTE (AYE) to Upper Serangoon Road at the Whampoa Flyover near Exit 8B from 21 August to 26 August 2006 between 1.00am and 5.00am

    Motorists are advised to use the at-grade slip road to PIE (Changi) and use Exit 13 into Kallang Way, Aljunied Road, Upper Aljunied Road to go to Upper Serangoon Road.

    Directional and information signs will be placed well in advance of the road closure to inform and guide motorists.

    We will ensure that inconvenience to the public and motorists are kept to a minimum during the period of closure.

    A map* is enclosed for your reference. Please contact Mr Ooi Lye Hock at H/P No: 96772013 for further information if required.

    Temporary Closure Of Koek Road

    To facilitate improvement work to the pedestrian overhead bridge connecting Orchard Point and Orchard Plaza, the Land Transport Authority (LTA) will be temporary closing Koek Road from 20 August 2006 to 25 August 2006 between 12.01am and 5.00am

    Motorists using Koek Road to Orchard Rd are advised to use Kramat Road, Cuppage Road, Cavenagh Road to Orchard Road as shown in the attached plan.

    Directional and information signs will be placed well in advance of the road closure to inform and guide motorists.

    We will ensure that inconvenience to the public and motorists are kept to a minimum during the period of closure.

    A map* is enclosed for your reference. Please contact Mr Lee Hock Seng at H/P No: 92995771 for further information if required.
