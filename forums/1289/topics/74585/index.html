    Question:
    Why do I see an invalid user error when I try to view my friends or do other things on Friendster?

    Answer:
    This is an error caused when our server load is high. A very high user volume can cause our service to have difficulty performing certain functions and return an "invalid user" error or not show some recent changes or actions. There is nothing wrong with your account and no data has been lost.

    We are currently in the process of upgrading our hardware and should have this problem addressed very soon.

    Question:
    I am getting an error message, or nothing apears to happen, when trying to add a friend, send a message, or do other things on Friendster.

    Answer:
    People have been reporting getting error messages when doing things on Friendster such as Adding a Friend, Sending a Message, or Reporting a User, amongst other things. Or sometimes you do not get an error message, but nothing seems to happen. Friendster is aware of the problem and is working to correct it. Recent changes have allowed us to accept more traffic than before and usage is up considerably. This is causing a lag, or delay, in some features being carried out. In most cases the action you were trying to perform DID get done, and the results will show up soon, even if you got an error when you did it. We are sorry for this issue and hope to have it corrected soon.
