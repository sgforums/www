Bomb hoax caller jailed

    Nov 30, 2010 - Straits Times

    by Elena Chong

    A 24-YEAR-OLD man who made a bomb hoax call to the police was jailed for three months on Tuesday.

    Ng Shichang admitted giving false information to a telephone operator, saying that there was 'a terrorist and bomb in five minutes'.

    He made the call from a void deck at a block of flats in Choa Chu Kang Avenue 3 on Sept 26, shortly after a first similar call.

    A Community Court heard that the police traced the dishwasher's call to a public phone of Block 286.

    When police arrived, Ng fled but was detained after a short chase.

    A second charge was taken into consideration during his sentencing. Ng, who has a previous conviction, could have been fined up to $100,000 and/or jailed for up to five years.
