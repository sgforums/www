Singapore women ranked fourth? Sure or not?

Next
  • Clivebenss
    3 Jun 12, 20:16

    Shin Min Daily News - 31 May 2012 11:45 AM | Updated 11:53 AM

    Believe it or not, Singaporean women are the fourth most beautiful women in the world.

    Well, according to MSN Travel website at least.

    The reason for the ranking, MSN Travel said in an article, is because the ladies here can speak Singlish.

    In a tongue-in-cheek style, the article said: “You can’t beat a language that starts with sin”.

    In the MSN Travel ranking, women from Melbourne, Australia, are the hottest. This is followed by those from Rio de Janeiro in Brazil and Amsterdam, Holland.

    It lauded Singapore’s weather as “fantastic” and described the women here as “sleek, sweet, sophisticated”, donning stylish summer dresses” paired with “long cascading hair like waves on Siloso Beach”.

    Despite the gush of praises, some Singaporeans interviewed in a street survey have a different opinion.

    A 30-year-old manager known only as Mr Lin said that only foreigners might find Singlish cute.

    Twenty-year-old student Mr Bai also did not agree with the ranking.

    He felt that, in terms of looks, Singaporean women are not as attractive compared to the Japanese or the Korean.

    But at least one person agreed with the ranking, and it came from – no surprise – a woman.

    Ms Hong, a 24-year-old auditor, felt that Singapore women know how to dress up and “there are beautiful ones everywhere”.
