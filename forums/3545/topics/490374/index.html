    NETIZEN EXPOSES PERVERT MASTURBATING ON BUS, GETS FLAMED BY TROLLS

    Submitted by farhan on Sun, 18/10/2015 - 12:41pm

    A Facebook post by a brave female netizen was meant to alert the police to the disgusting behavior of an old pervert, who took out his private part on a bus and started playing with it in full view of everyone. But instead, her account was attacked by trolls who reported her video for nudity, harassed her and flamed her for posting up video evidence of the man's crime.

    n the video, the man could be seen dressed in a white patterned shirt and black trousers. According to the netizen, the man boarded the same bus 106 towards Orchard at 6AM. At first, she took no notice of the man, but soon noticed somebody looking at the man disgustedly and pointing out that he was doing something weird.

    She then realized that the man had taken out his private part, which was visibly erect, and was playing with it without bothering about other commuters on the bus.

    Feeling disgusted and offended, the brave female netizen took a video of the man's indecent exposure, despite knowing that doing so placed her in danger of being confronted by the man. She later put the video up on Facebook to shame the man and to alert the police about this incident.

    Shortly after putting the video online however, she was confronted by a netizen, who reported the video for nudity and forced Facebook to take down the evidence. She was also scolded for uploading the video by a few netizens, who she feels may be related to the old man.

    The netizen has since uploaded a censored screenshot of her video so as to inform other netizens to be wary of the old pervert. The police have been notified of the incident.
