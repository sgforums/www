Benches more practical than stools at lift landings

  • Clivebenss
    17 Apr 12, 09:36

    Apr 17, 2012 - ST Forum

    THE stump-like stools built near the lift doors at Block 169 Stirling Road are good for seniors who need to sit while waiting for the lifts ('Stirling Road block gets seats at lift landings'; last Saturday).

    However, each seat can sit only one person, and they tend to clutter up the lift landing.

    A more aesthetically pleasing alternative may be to build a bench against a wall or wide pillar nearby. This bench could be long enough to sit two to four people. Low arm-rests could be built at both ends of the bench to prevent people from falling over.

    The arm-rests will also discourage people from lying down or sleeping on the bench. The wall or pillar behind the bench will provide a back-rest for elderly residents to sit safely and comfortably.

    Ng Chee Kheon
