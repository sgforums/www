    By Khushwant Singh

    A former youth programme coordinator was jailed a year and eight months on Wednesday for touching the private parts of a 14-year-old girl.

    Cheng Chin Shen, 31, was then working for a non-profit voluntary welfare organisation that caters to the development of children and teenagers.

    He had pleaded guilty in December 2011. A district court heard then that the girl became a member in mid-2010 and visited the centre about thrice a week to study and play games with friends.

    Cheng befriended her and kept in contact through online and phone messages. On Dec 26, 2010, they were chatting about sex-related matters on Facebook when she accepted his invitation to visit his flat the next day. The following morning, she met him at coffee shop near his Jurong West home and they had breakfast.
