    Monitor lizard spotted 'swimming leisurely' at canal opposite KK Hospital



    STOMPer Mrs Chia spotted what appears to be a monitor lizard at a canal opposite KK Hospital. According the STOMPer, the creature was about 50cm in length.

    Similar creatures have been spotted previously at the Chinese Gardens, and Rivervale Crescent.

    Mrs Chia says:

    "Spotted this reptile swimming leisurely in the canal opposite KK Hospital.

    "It appeared to be 50cm or more in length.

    "I called the police.

    "The creature was crawling into a drain headed towards LTA (Land Transport Authority) when I last saw it."
