    About CW-X

    Wacoal Sports Science Corporation, headquartered in New York City, is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Wacoal International. The company's mission is to meet the sports performance needs of the active US consumer through the CW-X brand, the world's first truly anatomically-engineered high performance sports apparel. CW-X is a result of a forty year study by scientists at Wacoal Human Science Research Center in Kyoto, Japan on kinesiology, the science of human movement. The research was based on over 35,000 people were tested and injuries analyzed relative to warm-up and cool-down periods.
