    In late 70s and early 80s, national minimum wage was $250 per month. Overtime rate was 2.5 per hour. And for office workers if the management needed you to work during the weekend (Sunday) they paid you double salary and on holiday was triple salary. If you stayed work after 6 pm you could get your dinner allowance, after 8 or 9 pm bus fare reimbursement and after midnight taxi fare reimbursement. Office work was 40 hrs work week and in some companies even less than 40 hrs work week. More rest and quality time with families, relatives and friends. CPF contribution was 25 per cent for employer and 25 per cent for employee until after recession in middle of 80s....20/20 for employer and employee. National Productivity achievement was good.
