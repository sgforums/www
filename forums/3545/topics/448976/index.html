Elderly woman falls to death from 7th-floor flat in Bishan

  • Clivebenss

    Mar 13, 2012 - Straits Times

    By Lee Xin En

    A 64-year-old housewife died on Tuesday after she fell out of a seventh-floor flat in Bishan St 22.

    Police said that they received a call requesting help at 11am. Paramedics who arrived on the scene shortly afterwards pronounced her dead.

    It is believed that the woman was washing the window at about 11am when she fell.

    According to neighbours, her husband is believed to have been in the flat at the time.
