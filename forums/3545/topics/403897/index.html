    Creating a worse problem with recyclable bags?

    IN OUR bid to save the world from plastic bags, are we creating a worse problem?

    Those recyclable bags that have been on sale to replace plastic ones are proliferating most alarmingly. It looks like everyone's jumping onto the recyclable bag bandwagon - everyone's giving one away at the slightest of excuses.

    At any one time, there are four to five of these bags in my car and lots more in the storeroom. They look a lot more bulky than plastic bags and I wonder if weight for weight, they don't cause more harm to the environment in their manufacture and disposal.

    Why don't we go back to the rattan baskets of yesteryear? They are hygienic, totally biodegradable and organic.

    Lee Holey (Madam)
