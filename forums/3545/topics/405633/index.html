    188 people arrested for vice-related activities
    Posted: 18 July 2010 1704 hrs

    SINGAPORE : 188 people were arrested in an 18-hour islandwide raid conducted by the police.

    The raid started on Friday evening.

    Police said the 35 men and 153 women were arrested for various offences such as gang-related activities, immigration offences, illegal employment, peddling of contraband cigarettes, vice-related activities, illegal gambling and consumption of controlled drugs.

    The arrests were made in the Geylang, Joo Chiat, Jurong, Orchard Road, Selegie, Havelock Road and Boat Quay areas.

    Arrests were made at entertainment outlets and lodging houses, as well as along backlanes and forested areas. - CNA /ls
