    to anyone think Singapore is the most expensive city for commoners, ask yourself the very same question: are other cities like new york cheaper then the things you are buying everyday?

     

    any article or index that rank Singapore as the most expensive city has to be looked at carefully. what does the article or index use to calculate which cities are the most expensive.

     

    since every city is different, it is difficult to use the same method to calculate. a lot of index include the cost of car into the calculation. cars in singapore is way more expensive then other countries so even though our daily cost is lower, the index or article will rank us higher and reader will think that Singapore is indeed more expensive even though the things we buy is actually a lot cheaper.

     

    for eg:

    other cities:

    cost of goods:$5000

    cost of car:$5000

    total: $10,000

     

    Singapore:

    cost of goods:$100

    cost of car:$9,900

    total:$10,000

     

    base on the example, Singapore is as expensive as other cities but in fact our cost of goods is 50 times cheaper.

     

    the index or article could also be calculating items that expat buys like things that are luxury and imported from their home country. then the index has nothing to do with us.
