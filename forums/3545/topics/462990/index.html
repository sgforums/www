    Man arrested for slashing wife and brother-in-law
    By Imelda Saad | Posted: 16 December 2012 1406 hrs

    SINGAPORE: A man has been arrested for allegedly breaking into a house and slashing his wife and brother-in-law.

    The incident happened at about 6am on Saturday.

    Police had received a call about a man breaking into an HDB flat in Yishun estate.

    It was alleged that the 42-year-old suspect had also used a knife to slash both his wife and brother-in-law.

    The man fled the scene after the assault, leaving behind the knife.

    Officers arrested the suspect about 18 hours later in the vicinity of Telok Blangah. 

    A man will be charged in court on Monday.
