    Feb 8, 2011 - Straits Times

    By Vanessa Jalleh

    FOUR thousand grassroots leaders will be attending a Chinese New Year garden party at the Istana Lawn on Sunday.

    The grassroots leaders, together with their spouses, have been invited by the Prime Minister and his Cabinet Ministers to show their appreciation for their dedication to community service and contributions to building social capital.

    Entertainment for the event includes performances by the award-winning People's Association Youth Chinese Orchestra and the Tampines North Community Club's Philharmonic Winds Orchestra.
