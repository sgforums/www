    Aug 19, 2010 - Straits Times

    Elderly man dies after skid

    by Ted Chan

    A 90-year-old died in a freak accident on Wednesday after his tricycle skidded.

    Mr Choo Seng Kwee, a karung guni collector, suffered head injuries after hitting the ground at about 7.45pm.

    He was sent to Tan Tock Seng Hospital but died two hours later.

    Mr Choo was riding on the extreme right along Aljunied Road toward Upper Aljunied Road.

    Weather conditions were dry on that day and no foul play is suspected.

    Traffic police are appealing for witnesses, who may call 1800-547-1818.
