Security guard drowned after seeing ghosts

  • Clivebenss
    11 Oct 11, 18:52

    Shin Min Daily News  - 1 hr 27 mins ago

    A 55 year-old security guard was found drowned in a 2M deep swimming pool yesterday morning after his duty. Before the accident, he told his wife that he spotted two “white” spirits floating around and even instructed his wife on how to set up an alter for him in his demise. 

    This accident happened yesterday morning at around 6:20am at a condominium in Serangoon North Avenue 1.

    The deceased was 55 year-old condominium security guard Bai Xuan An.  At the time of drowning, he was not on duty and was wearing civilian clothes.  He was said to have gone to the condominium to look for a friend the night before and had left before dawn, but his body was found floating in the condominium swimming pool that same morning.

    Civil Defence Force arrived at the scene at about 6:35am and pronounced the victim dead.

    The deceased who was said to be a good father, had left behind six suicide notes.
