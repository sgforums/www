Power blackout at City Hall MRT Station

  • Clivebenss
    6 Jul 12, 14:22

    Posted: 06 July 2012 1401 hrs

    SINGAPORE: Parts of City Hall MRT station have been hit by a power blackout.

    One commuter told Channel NewsAsia that this happened at about 1.30pm on Friday.

    When contacted, SMRT said the blackout affected the platforms serving the northbound and west-bound trains.

    Trains are still running.

    It said its staff are fixing the problem.

    SMRT said due to the power trip, it could not alert commuters, but staff are using loud hailers to inform them of the blackout.

    SMRT said on Twitter that escalators and some lights are down.

    When contacted, Singapore Power described the blackout as an internal power trip at the station's end.
