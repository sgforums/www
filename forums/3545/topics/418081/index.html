    Passengers miss flights as computer system breaks down at Changi Terminal 1


    There is a big holdup at Changi Airport Terminal 1 Immigration, says STOMPer V, in an email to STOMP at about 11.20am today (Dec 16). Many passengers are missing their flights, as a result of what seems to be a computer system breakdown.

    Said the STOMPer:

    "Big jam at T1 immigration. Computer system down. People are missing flights."
