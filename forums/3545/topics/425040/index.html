    By Joanne Chan | Posted: 13 March 2011 2055 hrs

    SINGAPORE : A 19-year-old Chinese national is believed to have drowned at sea near Sentosa's Tanjong Beach Club on Sunday afternoon.

    MediaCorp understands that the victim was swimming with a friend when he began to struggle in the water.

    The friend then sought assistance from a lifeguard and other passers-by.

    Police said the victim's body was brought to shore by the lifeguard and pronounced dead at the scene by paramedics at about 5.20pm.

    The case has been classified as an unnatural death.
