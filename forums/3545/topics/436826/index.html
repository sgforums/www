    Aug 25, 2011 - Straits Times

    By Khushwant Singh

    The youngest of his six victims was only 11 years old; the oldest 41, and they were all molested between January and March this year.

    On Thursday, Jeremy Yeo Ming Che, 16, was placed on probation for three years for using criminal force to outrage their modesty. The part-time airport porter was also ordered to perform 60 hours of community service and his parents had to sign a $5,000 bond to ensure his good conduct.

    A psychiatric report tendered by the prosecution stated that Yeo has paedophilia, which refers to a primary or exclusive sexual interest in children aged 13 years or younger. As such, it was recommended that the teen should undergo the Positive Adolescent Sexuality Treatment programme run by the Ministry of Community Development, Youth and Sports. He should also attend mandatory counselling and supervision while under probation, the Institute of Mental Health psychiatrist had added.

    When Yeo pleaded guilty in July, the court heard that his hunting grounds were the void decks and lift lobbies of flats in Tampines.
