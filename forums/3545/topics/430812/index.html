Are water tanks safe from terrorists?

  • Clivebenss
    20 May 11, 12:33

    May 20, 2011 - ST Forum

    I AM concerned about the safety and security of the Housing Board's water tanks after reading the report on the discovery of a maid's body in a rooftop tank in Woodlands on Monday.

    Are our water tanks safe from intruders? In another case in 2002, a group of boys threw washing powder into a similar water tank. Looks like our water tank areas have easy access.

    Do the town councils and private property management committees have a plan in place to control access to water tank areas?

    Such reports would give ideas to terrorists to harm residents. Since we are living in a time of terrorism and Singapore is a potential target for terrorist attacks, are our water tanks really safe from sabotage that could cause a great number of deaths?

    What steps are being taken - or have been taken - to prevent such fatal intrusions?

    Joshua Selvakumar
