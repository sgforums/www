Relatives, friends attend Alice Goh wake

  • Clivebenss

    By Sara Grosse | Posted: 21 January 2012 1819 hrs

    SINGAPORE: Relatives and friends paid their respects on Saturday afternoon to Alice Goh, the late former deputy prime minister Goh Keng Swee's first wife.

    One of them was President Tony Tan Keng Yam who was accompanied by his wife.

    A memorial service will take place on Saturday night at Mount Vernon Sanctuary in Cherish Hall.

    Mrs Goh died on Friday at the age of 86, reportedly from kidney failure.

    She is survived by her son and two grandsons.
