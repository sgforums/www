Several parts of S'pore hit by flash floods

  • Lau bakkwa
    4 May 11, 20:51

    By Ewen Boey | SingaporeScene – 2 hours 22 minutes ago

    Flood in Paya Lebar. (Photo courtesy of @angeejin)

    Several parts of Singapore were hit by flash floods following a heavy downpour on Wednesday afternoon.

    Ang Mo Kio, Bishan, Balestier, Commonwealth, Serangoon Gardens, Paya Lebar and Yishun were among several places that were affected.

    Users of microblogging platform Twitter were quick to react, posting updates and pictures.

    @OngWeiZhong tweeted, "Floods at Commonwealth Avenue, claiming the bus stops. Wear your swimming attire at all times."

    Another, @pohui tweeted, "Heavy downpour at Bishand and AMK, AMK station flooded, Kallang River almost burst."

    @DewZW tweeted, "Flood at Balestier Road" along with a video.

    Yishun Interchange is partially submerged in flash floods on Wednesday. (Photo courtesy of @nerolijasmine)

    Earlier in the day, the National Enviroment Agency had issued a warning that flash floods could occur in low-lying areas of Singapore.

    Bus stops in manay areas of Singapore were also not spared from the the flash floods. (Photo from Twitter)
