    Philips & The Secret Little Agency new publicity this sunday !!!

    By Storywolf


    Philips & The Secret Little Agency change new publicity advertising campain after the whole Singapore was hopping mad for their super dumb guerilla advertising campaign featuring a video of a 'bear' along Ulu Pandan Road.

    The new campain is call "The Great Singapore Hunt". We will dress all the Philips marketing team as Bears and the creative team from the Little Secret Little Agency as wild hogs at Ulu Pandan Road, and let the public do some real hunting. This will be the first real hunt in Singapore since 1930s.

    However no guns are allowed for the hunt. The event spokenman - " We do not want the marketing team and creative team to die on the spot, as then we will need to bring in foreign talent to replace them, we want the public to have more hours of fun". Arrows, spears, slingshot are available for loan on the spot. Photography with the prey is available and for the hunter who brought down the prey get a free philip shaver - they can shave the "bear" and "pig" on the spot - including the hair of those inside.

    The news of the hunt already generate oversea enquires on the "Great Singapore Hunt" already generated a lot of inquires, looking possible of replacing F1 as the year hottest event.
