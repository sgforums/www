Grassroots organisations should focus on youth outreach

  • Clivebenss
    31 Jul 11, 18:17

    By Monica Kotwani | Posted: 31 July 2011 1807 hrs

    SINGAPORE: National Development Minister Khaw Boon Wan said grassroots organisations will have to focus on youth outreach in the future.

    Mr Khaw said this after a meeting with youths at Sembawang GRC on Sunday.

    Mr Khaw, who is also MP for Sembawang GRC, said getting senior citizens involved in grassroots activities has been successful.

    But he said a change in strategy is needed to reach out to youth. And that's because youth have multiple interests, and do not take to a top-down approach.

    Mr Khaw said: "You have to find, to discover what is it that interests them, and activities have to be organised around those interests. So the best way is that young people themselves come up with the ideas. We have some money, we'll support them; we'll provide the platform, they provide the ideas, and they provide the organisation."

    - CNA
