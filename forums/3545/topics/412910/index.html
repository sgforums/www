    Oct 11, 2010 - Straits Times

    By Jalelah Abu Baker

    A PRISON vehicle carrying inmates broke down in a tunnel on the Central Expressway, causing a short traffic jam on Monday morning.

    The hold-up eased once the prisoners were transferred to another vehicle and the broken-down one was cleared at about 10am.

    The inmates were being driven to the Subordinate Courts at Havelock Road from Changi Prison.

    The Singapore Prison Service confirmed that the breakdown occurred near the Cairnhill exit, and that an alternative vehicle was sent immediately.

    It declined to comment on how many inmates were present at the time, or how they were escorted.
