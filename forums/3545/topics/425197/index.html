Swiss student dies in condo fall

  • Clivebenss

    Mar 15, 2011 - Straits Times

    A SWISS student in his early 20s died last Saturday morning after apparently falling from his Aspen Heights condominium in River Valley Road.

    Mr Yves Patrick Biggoer was a final-year business undergraduate at the Singapore Management University (SMU).

    The police confirmed they received a call at 5.50am that morning and found an injured man on the ground floor.

    He was taken to hospital but died about 31/2 hours later.

    An SMU spokesman said the school is 'deeply saddened and shocked by his sudden passing'.

    In a statement to The Straits Times, the school described him as an 'attentive student and active participant in class' who 'contributed many interesting ideas and comments'.
