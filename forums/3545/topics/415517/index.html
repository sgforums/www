NTUC Club steps up security at Downtown East & Costa Sands

  • Clivebenss
    17 Nov 10, 21:08

    By Joanne Chan | Posted: 17 November 2010 2027 hrs

    SINGAPORE : Security at Downtown East and Costa Sands Resort has been increased, following the recent murder at Downtown East and the arrest of four youths with choppers at Costa Sands Resort.

    NTUC Club, which manages the two premises, told MediaCorp that security patrols have been stepped up, as more visitors are expected during the school holidays.

    The surveillance system has also been enhanced, and lighting installed in dim areas.

    Police have also increased their presence in the area, with more patrols and checks at Downtown East.

    Visitors said the recent incidents have not put them off from visiting the resorts.

    One visitor said: "Everything has happened, so there are bound to be more people patrolling. So they will definitely make it more safe."

    Another commented: "I have been here quite a number of times. It feels very safe here. Singapore is a very safe place after all."

    - CNA/ms
