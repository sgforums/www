    Mar 7, 2011 - ST Forum

    'Why not pick up litter that other people have left behind?'

    MR LOREN HOWERTER: 'I have lived in Japan, and I agree with Ms Charlotte Chu ('Clean and green: Why Japan beats top-ranking S'pore'; Feb 22). Singapore has strict laws against littering, but we should go farther. Why not pick up litter that other people have left behind? At a bus stop recently, a woman was sitting on a bench which had some litter next to her. I asked if it was hers, and she said no. A rubbish bin was about 5m away, so I cleared the litter. The woman looked at me as if I was crazy. Sometimes, when I pick up litter, people thank me, but usually not. Let us teach kids to actively take responsibility to keep Singapore clean, which is most efficiently accomplished by parents setting a good example.'
