    Dec 22, 2010 - Straits Times

    AN ELDERLY woman died on Monday, 16 hours after she fell while alighting from a private bus. The bus was dropping off Madam Tang Ah Yang, 68, near her home in Bedok, after a tour with a group of senior citizens to Jurong on Sunday evening.

    It is not known whether there was a guide present to help her get off the bus.

    She tripped and fell, hitting the back of her head against the pavement, but dismissed it as not serious, saying she did not need medical attention.

    Mr Tan Swee Hee, her younger brother, said friends who were present observed that she had appeared completely fine after the fall.

    However, they urged her to see a doctor since it was a head injury, and she was taken to Changi General Hospital by ambulance five minutes later. She died of head injuries at about noon the next day.

    The mother of four, who was described as generous and easy-going, was generally healthy and frequently went for walks with her husband, Mr Tan said. 'There should have been a guide when there were so many senior citizens,' the 60-year-old added.
