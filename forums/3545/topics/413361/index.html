Public-spirited man nabs fleeing snatch thief

  • Clivebenss
    18 Oct 10, 17:11

    By Mustafa Shafawi | Posted: 18 October 2010 1641 hrs

    SINGAPORE: A 40-year-old man, who tried to flee on Sunday afternoon after stealing a wallet from a female victim, did not get very far.

    He was stopped in his tracks by a public-spirited member of public.

    The man had snatched the 18-year-old woman's wallet from the table while she was having lunch with her friend at a hawker centre at Margaret Drive.

    However, the act was observed by 40-year-old Mr Eng who was seated nearby.

    Mr Eng managed to detain the suspect after a short chase.

    The suspect will be charged on Tuesday in Court.

    If convicted he could be jailed for up to three years and fined.
