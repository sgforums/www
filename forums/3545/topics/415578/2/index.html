    Marina Boulevard death reclassified as murder
    By Qiuyi Tan | Posted: 19 November 2010 2147 hrs

    SINGAPORE : Police have classified the death of a 36-year-old man at Marina Boulevard on Thursday as murder.

    The man - identified as a Chinese foreign worker - was found on a bench near the Marina Bay Financial Centre.

    Police had earlier classified the case as one of unnatural death.

    Police said the body had a cut at the back of the head, but there was no indication of a gang-related incident.
