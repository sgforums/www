    By Shaffiq Alkhatib | Posted: 18 November 2010 1329 hrs

    SINGAPORE: A 36-year-old foreign worker from China was found dead at Marina Boulevard on Thursday morning.

    The man had a cut at the back of his head.

    Police said there was no indication that the incident was gang-related.

    A passerby found the man lying motionless on a bench at around 6am.

    The police were soon notified.

    The man's wallet and other personal items were found near him.

    The man was wearing an orange top with dark coloured trousers.

    Police have classified the case as unnatural death.

    Investigations are on-going.
