TOTO draw at IRs: Is this a ploy to draw visit?

  • Clivebenss
    17 Jan 11, 16:19

    Jan 17, 2011 - ST Forum

    I READ with great disquiet the advertisement in the print media about the coming Singapore Pools 'Double Happiness' Toto draws to celebrate Chinese New Year.

    The ad put up by Singapore Pools - which can also be viewed on its website - indicates that the $5 million 'Reunion Draw' and the $10 million 'Hongbao Draw' would be conducted at its Livewire outlets at Resorts World Sentosa and Marina Bay Sands respectively. This is different from the usual practice of conducting all draws at its main branch in Selegie Road.

    Is this a marketing collaboration between Singapore Pools and the two integrated resorts to draw visitors to the IRs during the Chinese New Year period?

    My biggest concern is that both the IRs may utilise Singapore Pools as a vehicle to promote gaming activities since they are not allowed to conduct any promotion programmes for gaming among Singaporeans. Although Singapore Pools is a gaming entity, its main purpose is to combat illegal gaming activities - and channel surplus earnings to benefit the community - unlike the two IRs, for which gaming is a business.

    As Singapore Pools constantly reminds Singaporeans to be responsible gamers, likewise I would urge Singapore Pools to demonstrate to us that it is a responsible gaming provider.

    Teo Eng Hock

  • Clivebenss
    24 Jan 11, 13:08

    Jan 24, 2011 - ST Forum

    Toto draws at IRs above board

    THERE is no marketing collaboration between Singapore Pools and the integrated resorts (IRs) on the upcoming special Toto draws (Mr Teo Eng Hock, 'Toto draws at integrated resorts: Is this a ploy to draw visitors?'; Jan 17).

    Singapore Pools' Livewire outlets are tenants of both IRs and, like the other tenants there, we operate independently.

    Singapore Pools is taking the two special draws to our Livewire outlets for the benefit of our customers visiting the IRs. All draws are held within the premises of Singapore Pools.

    We observe our responsible gaming codes on all aspects of our operations and strictly enforce our rule that Livewire is open only to persons aged 21 and above.

    Phoebe Low (Ms)
    Manager, Media Relations,
    Public Affairs & Corporate Social Responsibility
    Singapore Pools
