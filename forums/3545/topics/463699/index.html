    SINGAPORE - The police are appealing for the next-of-kin of 76-year-old Lim Ah Kiat to come forward.

    Mr Lim, who was previously residing at All Saints Home, passed away at Changi General Hospital on Dec 20 this year.

    The next-of-kin or anyone with information on the deceased is requested to call the police hotline at 1800-255-0000.
