    Barbecue nuisance in HDB flats

    MY NEIGHBOURS hail from a different country and bring with them different cultural and social habits.

    One of these habits is to barbecue their food every evening on their balcony. The smoky, oily vapour pollutes the air. Smoke can be seen wafting up into the living areas of those living on the upper floors.

    It gets really annoying when laundry has to be re-washed and we have to put up with the lingering smell of food and oil in the curtains and rooms for days.

    I wonder if the National Environment Agency has any guidelines regarding the barbecuing of food on open balconies of flats.

    Linda Zhang (Ms)
