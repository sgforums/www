    According to Singstats Data, 85.65% of the resident population (Singapore Citizens and PR)  are Singapore citizens, assuming ceteris paribus (all things equal),

    Singapore Citizens being employed --> 1,990,700 * 85.65% = 1,705,035
    Singapore Citizens being employed (%) --> 1,705,035 / 3,102,500 = 54.96%

    Ok, we are not the minority, but we are not very significant, with only 1 in 2 jobs going to Singapore Citizens out of the jobs being offered.

    http://www.singstat.gov.sg/stats/themes/people/popnindicators.pdf
