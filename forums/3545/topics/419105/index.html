    Dec 29, 2010 - Straits Times

    By Vanessa Jalleh

    POLICE arrested a man on Tuesday allegedly responsible for at least four cases of indecent exposure in the Sembawang estate.

    In December 2010, the 20-year-old suspect prowled around void decks in the Sembawang estate, exposing himself and committing obscene acts in front of female victims, aged between 15 and 23.

    Using a description provided by the victims, police spotted the suspect, riding past a block of flats on Tuesday at about 5.30 pm.

    As the police approached him, the suspect tried to flee, but was subdued and placed under arrest.

    The suspect will be charged in court on Thursday for an offence of committing an obscene act in a public place which carries a maximum of three months of jail time or with a fine or both.
