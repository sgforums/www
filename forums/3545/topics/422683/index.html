    Feb 13, 2011 - Sunday Times

    The Little Red Dot has been making waves lately, with CNN ranking it second on its 'coolest' nations list. But do Singaporeans think they're cool? The Sunday Times finds out.

    By Ng Kai Ling

    RECENTLY, website CNNGo, a lifestyle and travel companion to CNN, ranked Singaporeans ahead of the siesta-loving Spaniards and the dreadlocks-donning Jamaicans as the second- coolest people in the world. At No.1 were the samba-dancing Brazilians.

    But do Singaporeans themselves think Singapore is cool? When The Sunday Times hit the streets to ask 200 Singaporeans what they thought was cool about their homeland, 91 - or close to half - either could not come up with an answer, or gave an outright 'no' to the idea of Singapore being cool.

     

    WHAT OUR POLL FOUND...

    We asked 200 Singaporeans what makes Singapore cool.

     

    Do you think Singapore is cool?

    YES 109

    NO 91

     

    Top five things that make Singapore cool

    1. Good transport system

    2. The diversity - multiracialism, multiculturalism

    3. Food

    4. Safety

    5. Shopping

     

    What is least cool about Singapore?

    1. Rude Singaporeans

    2. Too many rules and regulations

    3. Kiasuism

    4. Not enough entertainment

    5. Weather

     

    Coolest icon in Singapore

    1. Can't think of one

    2. MM Lee Kuan Yew

    3. Marina Bay Sands

    4. Gurmit Singh

    5. Hossan Leong/Kumar
