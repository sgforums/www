    SINGAPORE: Police are investigating a case of unnatural death which happened along Wilby Road in Bukit Timah on Wednesday.

    Police said it received a call at about 5.30pm seeking for assistance.

    When officers arrived at the scene, the body of an elderly Chinese man was found lying motionless at the bus stop.

    The 78-year-old was pronounced dead at the scene.
