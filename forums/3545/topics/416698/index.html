  • Clivebenss

    Dec 1, 2010 - Straits Times

    TWO youths, aged 12 and 14 years of age, have been arrested for trying to rob an elderly man.

    The 79-year-old victim lodged a report with the police on early on Tuesday morning, saying two unknown boys had attempted to rob him at Block 124 Ang Mo Kio Ave 6.

    While the victim managed to fend off his attackers, he sustained a swollen left eye and minor injuries in the process.

    Later on Tuesday, police patrolling Ang Mo Kio St 31 spotted a group of youths gathered around a barbecue pit, who fled upon seeing the police.

    Police gave chase and detained a 12-year-old boy, who turned out to be one of the two who had attacked the 79-year-old man. Follow-up investigations led to the arrest of his 14-year-old accomplice on Wednesday morning.

    The two suspects will be charged in the Juvenile Court on Thursday.
