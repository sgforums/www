MM Lee: All races treated equally

  • Clivebenss
    12 Feb 11, 16:22

    Feb 12, 2011 - Straits Times

    By Amanda Wong

    'ALL races are given equal treatment, nobody gets an advantage in Singapore' said Minister Mentor Lee Kuan Yew.

    Speaking to some 1,800 residents at the annual Tanjong Pagar Lunar New Year dinner on Friday, MM Lee said Singapore will progress and do well if everyone continues to be given equal opportunities.

    'In Singapore, everyone uses the English language, it is a common platform. It is also the language which has enabled us to connect to the world and get investments in.'

    MM also urged Singaporeans to stand united, regardless of race, language or religion.

    He said: 'Fair play enables harmony to be maintained. On that basis, we will continue to do well for many, many more years and our children will grow up in one of the most safe, secure and prosperous countries in this part of the world, if not the whole world.'
