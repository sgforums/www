    Molester jailed one more year

    By Elena Chong

     

    Christopher Neo Yong Heng was given another 12 months jail for having sex with a minor. -- PHOTO: SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE

     

    A MAN currently serving sentence for molesting a 15-year-old girl and slashing a 23-year-old woman was given another 12 months' jail for having sex with a minor.

    District Judge May Mesenas ordered Christopher Neo Yong Heng's sentence to run after he completes his 41/2-year and 12-stroke sentence meted out in June for two offences.

    The 23-year-old had molested a 15-year-old girl in August last year after he came to know her through social networking website Friendster. He had armed himself with a knife when he met her on their first date.

    While out on bail, he slashed a Chinese national at void deck in Jalan Rumah Tinggi while she was surfing the Internet on Nov 1.

    In the latest case, the court heard that the victim, then a 13-year-old student, was introduced to Neo by her friend in February last year.

    They chatted through an online instant messaging service for a few days before they met. He took her home from the Tampines MRT station in March (09) and had sex with her despite her protests.

    A second similar charge was taken into consideration.
