    my name is T.C.  and I am a senior citizen from Singapore. I want to tell you what happened to me during the last 4 years.  Up to summer 2011 I live together with my son Fook Yew and his family.

    Meanwhile the boys got old enough and did not need any more the attention and help of theirgrandparents.  That’s  when things started top change. Hazel – her name fits her very well. She must not walk, but can simply roll around.  Anyhow when she figured out that the kids did not need  me any more she started to isolate me in the apartment.  They were no longer talking to me and the only contact was their maid.

    In July 2011 I stumbled in the apartment, fell and knocked my head. My grandson Don arranged to bring me to the Changi hospital.  They found that I suffered from a brain hemorraghe, which should be treated immediately.  However my “beloved” FUCK-YOU decided that I had lived long enough and did not need any further medical treatment nor the also suggested rehab. Instead of that he dumped me in a small room without further medical treatment, probably hoping that I would not make it much longer.

    Luckily my older son informed my daughter who lives overseas. So she flew in from Europe took me out of that hell-hole and brought me back into Changi hospital as I suffered from an acute pneumonia. After I had recovered from this she brought me into a nursing home, where I get professional care.

    Since that time my “beloved” son FUCK YOU  never visited me once again. He even

    rejected to pay his share of the nursing home and medical expenses. So my daughter had to get to the local family court, which then forced him to pay.

    Right now I am still alive and can only communicate with the help of others. However I try to stay alive as long as possible, so that FOOK YEW at least must

    pay for quite a while. 

    FUCK YOU   FOOK YEW !!

     

    I look forward to hear your comments!

     

     

    T.C.
