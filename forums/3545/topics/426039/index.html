    Mar 24, 2011 - Straits Times

    by Elena Chong

    THE licensee of a hotel in Chinatown was fined $800 on Thursday for allowing a prostitute to ply her trade in a room.

    Teo Eng Heng, 40, admitted to breaching the Hotels Licensing Regulations at Dragon Hotel, Mosque Street, on April 24 last year.

    A court heard that police on rounds in Chinatown saw a couple enter the hotel. On checking the room, they found a couple.

    The man told police that he had met the 31-year-old Filipina at People's Park Complex and had accepted her offer to provide him sexual services for $100.

    Teo had failed to take steps to ensure that the hotel was not used for prostitution.

    A second similar charge was taken into consideration. He could have been fined up to $1,000.
