71 women arrested in multi-agency operation in Geylang

  • Clivebenss
    8 Sep 12, 19:16

    Posted: 08 September 2012 1910 hrs

    SINGAPORE: Police on Saturday said that 71 women were arrested in an eight-hour multi-agency operation on entertainment outlets in the Geylang area.

    The women were arrested for unlawful employment and immigration offences.

    The joint operation, which ended at 5am on Saturday, involved officers from Bedok Police Division, the Central Narcotics Bureau and the Singapore Civil Defence Force.

    Police said operators of the entertainment outlets were also summoned for flouting various public entertainment, health and fire safety regulations.

    Cash amounting to S$1,714, some entertainment equipment and gaming machines were also seized from one of the outlets.
