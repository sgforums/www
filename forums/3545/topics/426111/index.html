    Apr 21, 2011 - Straits Times

    Kedah murder: 2 charged with killing Singaporean

    A MAN and a woman were charged in a Malaysian court yesterday with the murder of Singaporean businessman Arthur Lee Wee Guan.

    Rajasekaran, 52, and Chen Jia Mei, 38, allegedly murdered Mr Lee, 59, sometime between 9am on March 20 and 2.30pm the next day.

    The duo are alleged to have committed the act about 8.3km from a hillside holiday destination in Gunung Jerai in Kedah.

    It was reported on April 8 that the Malaysian police had arrested two suspects for the murder.

    One of the two had been detained in Kuala Lumpur as he was suspected of being involved in other cases in the Malaysian capital.

    Mr Lee's body was found on March 21 by the roadside about 5km from the resort in Gunung Jerai.
