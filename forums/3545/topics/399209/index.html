    Don't let Balestier become like Geylang

    I REFER to last Wednesday's report, 'Geylang clean-up leads to vice in heartland', and agree with Holland-Bukit Timah GRC MP Christopher De Souza's suggestion to ban hotels from operating on an hourly rate in all residential areas.

    As a resident of Balestier, I am frustrated with the increasing number of KTV lounges and budget hotels in the area. Since the surge in such openings last year, my family and I have been often woken up in the wee hours by the sound of fighting. And it is unpleasant when men loiter around and gawk at women.

    Balestier is famous for its many great food stalls and heritage sites and I hope it will not deteriorate into a place where families will have to think twice about before visiting the area.

    Lee Siew Keng (Madam)
