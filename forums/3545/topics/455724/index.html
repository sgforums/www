1 injured in fire at East Village Hotel

  • Clivebenss
    16 Jul 12, 13:06

    Posted: 16 July 2012 1020 hrs

    SINGAPORE: Eleven people -- four children, five adults and two fire-fighters -- were injured in a fire at East Village Hotel at Marine Parade Road on Monday.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) said the injured have been sent to the Singapore General Hospital.

    The fire broke out at the hotel in the morning. SCDF confirmed that it received a call at 9.06am about the incident.

    Viewers who called the Channel NewsAsia hotline said the hotel, located at 25 Marine Parade Road, was engulfed in thick, black smoke.

    One motorist, Mr Tan, said visibility along East Coast Road was also affected by the smoke.

    It is believed that the fire started in the hotel basement.

    SCDF said it is trying to put out the fire with six water jets.

    It tweeted at about 12.20pm that the fire has engulfed the construction area in the hotel lobby.

    Cherie Hearts @ Eastgate, a kindergarten and infant care centre at 46 East Coast Road, has asked parents to pick up their children from the centre as the air quality in the area worsens.
