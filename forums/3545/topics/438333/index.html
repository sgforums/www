Take firmer stand against trans fat

  • Clivebenss
    21 Sep 11, 13:24

    Sep 21, 2011 - ST Forum

    I COMMEND National Development Minister Khaw Boon Wan for his plan to eliminate food imports containing trans fat.

    We should not follow the guideline of the United States' Food and Drug Administration of allowing trans fat content of less than 0.5g per serving to be declared as 0g.

    It gives such food products an unfair advantage against those that have true 0g trans fat.

    All trans fat content should be declared to at least two decimal points. Let the consumer decide on his comfort level for food safety.

    Singapore must take a firmer stand against manufacturers who negatively impact the integrity of the food chain in their pursuit of profits.

    Tan Choon Hong
