'No smoking' rule in coffee shop ignored

  • Clivebenss
    15 Feb 11, 08:25

    Feb 15, 2011 - ST Forum

    LAST Wednesday, I was at Ting and Ting Foodcourt at Block 494, Jurong West, around 7pm, and saw three customers smoking at a table in the "no smoking" area there. The coffee shop owner did not do anything to stop them.

    I hope the relevant authorities will step up their spot checks to ensure coffee shop owners enforce the "no smoking" rule.

    Also, it should be made compulsory for coffee shops to display prominently the relevant hotline number for members of the public to report instances of non-conformity to this rule. In this way, the authorities will be able to take immediate action against coffee shop owners who do not enforce the rule.

    Anthony Goh
