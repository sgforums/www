    -How do i ENTER a club? (just go to the bouncer and show IC or?)
    Walk in from the front door. If there is a queue, then u queue up. If bouncer ask for IC, show him. Quite common sense and straight forward.

    -How do i pay for the cover charge?
    Some place need u pay upfront, some place need u to pay when u are seated inside. some place wavie cover charges if u open many bottles. Generally accept different mode of payment.

    -What are standard drink(e.g $30(cover charge) with 2 standard drink)?
    Depends. U open many bottles, cover charge wavied. Different places different charges different rules.

    -How do i order drinks inside the club when its so messy?
    Bar counter for clubs. Waiter for places with live show or you can go to the bar counter as well.

    -Which club should I try out first?
    Up to your preference.
