Keep estate cleaner

  • Clivebenss
    12 Oct 10, 13:45

    Oct 12, 2010 - ST Forum

    I AM deeply concerned about the state of cleanliness in my HDB housing estate, which comes under the jurisdiction of the Aljunied Town Council.

    Despite the rise in service and conservancy charges this year, there has been no noticeable improvement in the general cleanliness of common areas such as void decks.

    Encountering pests such as cockroaches and rats is not uncommon. The extent of infestation is most evident after thermal fogging, which is performed once in a long while and always leaves the void decks littered with dead cockroaches.

    Most recently, the void deck of a particular block was scrubbed thoroughly over the weekend in anticipation of a visit by Member of Parliament Cynthia Phua. Otherwise, cleaning is an annual ritual.

    Clearly, the estate cannot be thoroughly cleaned only once a year. There is a need to maintain clean and hygienic living conditions at all times. Merely discouraging littering does not completely alleviate the problem as dirt would still accumulate.

    I urge the relevant authorities to step up efforts to upkeep the estate.
