Don't treat maids like slaves

  • Clivebenss
    14 Sep 10, 10:54

    Sep 14, 2010 - ST Forum

    I REFER to last Thursday's letter ("Timely reminder on treating maids right"). Singaporeans need to do some soul searching on how they treat their maids.

    It is very tough for maids to leave their homes and families for a foreign land, where they have to not only adapt to a new environment but also learn new skills quickly.

    As Singaporeans, we enjoy a high standard of living in a first-class nation. Let us not, in the pursuit of success and material wealth, forget to treat our maids like fellow human beings.

    Treat them fairly and with respect. Provide them with fair working conditions. They are here to assist us in caring for our family and household - not to be our slaves.

    Susan Tan (Ms)
