    Nov 29, 2010 - Straits Times

    by Elena Chong

    A FORMER Singapore Zoological Gardens veterinarian was charged in court on Monday with four counts of molesting a 21-year-old woman.

    Oh Soon Hock, 52, who is now working elsewhere, allegedly molested the young woman then working at the zoo between January and March 27 last year.

    Then head of veterinary department, he allegedly squeezed the woman's breasts and kissed her on two of the occasions at Mandai Lake Road.

    Oh, who joined the zoo in 1990, quit in April last year.

    Oh, also a traditional Chinese medicine practitioner, is represented by Mr Shashi Nathan and Ms Tania Chin.

    Bail of $15,000 was offered. A pre-trial conference has been fixed for Jan 10.
