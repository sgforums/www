    Man dies after assault

    A THAI national, who was believed to have been assaulted by an unknown person at Tuas Avenue 9 on Tuesday at about 3.30am, died in hospital on Thursday, around noon.

    The 35-year-old man was taken to the hospital with head injuries.

    Police have classified the case as murder and investigations are ongoing.
