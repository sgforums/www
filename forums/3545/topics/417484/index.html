    Dec 10, 2010 - Straits Times

    by Elena Chong

    A POLYTECHNIC student charged last week with outrage of modesty and theft was accused of three more counts of molestation and one of fraudulent possession on Friday.

    Jee Guang You, 23, a Nanyang Polytechnic student, is said to have caressed three young women on different days at the polytechnic library at Ang Mo Kio Avenue 8 in November.

    He is also accused of fraudulently having six pieces of women's undergarments, suspected to have been stolen or fraudulently obtained, at Block 360 Bukit Batok Street 31 on Dec 1.

    Jee, who was represented by Mr Jeevan John, was remanded for psychiatric assessment.

    He will appear in court again on Dec 24.

    The maximum penalty for molestation is two years' jail, a fine and caning. For fraudulent possession, the punishment is a fine of up to $3,000 and/or a jail term of up to one year.
