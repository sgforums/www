Two arrested for stealing S$40,000 worth of exotic stingrays

  • Clivebenss
    31 Jul 11, 18:15

    Posted: 31 July 2011 1809 hrs

    SINGAPORE: Two men were arrested for breaking into an aquarium and stealing 12 exotic stingrays worth S$40,000.

    Police said the owner found the sliding window of his shop unit in Serangoon North forced open and discovered the fish missing from the tanks.

    He made a police report and police tracked a 23-year-old suspect, arresting him near HarbourFront walk.

    His 32-year-old accomplice was also arrested at Rivervale Crescent.

    All the stolen stingrays were found in the residence of the first suspect and recovered.

    If convicted, the duo face a maximum jail term of 14 years.

    - CNA
