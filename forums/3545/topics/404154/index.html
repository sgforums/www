    Woman's body on ECP beach

    by Ted Chen

    THE body of an 84-year-old woman was found along the beaches of East Coast Park on Friday morning.

    Madam Tan Soh Hua was spotted lying on the sand by a passerby at about 7am, just about 40 m in front of the Goldkist Beach Resort chalets.

    Medics pronounced her dead at the scene soon after. She is believed to have drowned as she suffered no bodily injuries.

    Police have classified the death as unnatural and have ruled out foul play.
