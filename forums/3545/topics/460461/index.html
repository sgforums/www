    Published on Oct 23, 2012 - ST Forum

    MS ADA Chan rightly pointed out the different roles nursing homes and retirement villages play ("We need nursing homes and retirement villages"; Oct 10).

    She also suggested that Our Singapore Conversation include discussions on retirement villages and the increase of nursing homes as the pillars of support for our ageing society.

    This is necessary because discussions for these are not just quantitative issues, but carry along many other issues an ageing population faces.

    There are differences in lifestyles between the haves and the have-nots, but both need to be addressed.

    On the qualitative aspects, there are overall well-being considerations which extend beyond mere caregiving in nursing homes or retirement villages.

    If we can get our seniors to remain active during their silver years, the atmosphere in nursing homes and retirement villages can even be joyous ones.

    Geoffrey Kung
