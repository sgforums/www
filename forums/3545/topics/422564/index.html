    Feb 11, 2011 - Straits Times

    by Elena Chong

    A WOMAN who claimed that she was attacked with broken glass, bottles and knives at a pub was charged with fighting with three women on Friday.

    The three women were also charged with a similar offence.

    Doreen Yap Xiao Ping, 26, a manager, who was injured, is accused of disturbing the public peace when she fought with Ong Poh Eng, 30, Heng Ai Hoon, 30, and Ng Mei Fang, 28, at Ginza Pub along Sembawang Road on Nov 11 2010.

    Yap was represented by Mr Adrian Wee while Ong and Heng's lawyer was Mr Simon Tan.

    Ng told the court that she had engaged a lawyer to represent her. Their cases were adjourned. If convicted, they can be jailed for up to one year and/or fined up to $5,000.
