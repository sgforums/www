    LAST Saturday, I was walking past the Henderson Waves bridge and was shocked to see wild monkeys taking food from visitors.

    The visitors were there for a picnic and not to feed the monkeys. I do not think the area is suitable for picnics because of the presence of these animals.

    The authorities should put up signs discouraging picnics, and warn visitors to take extra care if they have children and babies with them.
