    Opposition MP Chiam says Dr Goh left behind a long distinguished career
    Posted: 15 May 2010 1352 hrs

    SINGAPORE: Opposition MP for Potong Pasir Chiam See Tong described the late Dr Goh Keng Swee as a practical man of visionary talent who left behind a long distinguished career.

    Mr Chiam said: "He was a 'jack of all trades' and minister of all in his time."

    Mr Chiam said he did not have the opportunity to engage Dr Goh in a debate in Parliament on some of his "unpopular policies which were hot topics in those days".

    He said Dr Goh retired in 1984, the year he won a parliamentary seat.

    "I give my respect to the late Dr Goh Keng Swee for the good values he upheld, such as thriftiness, perseverance, courage and diligence," Mr Chiam said.

    "My wife and I send our heartfelt condolences to Mrs Goh and her family."

    Mr Chiam suggested renaming the Jurong Industrial Estate to Goh Keng Swee Industrial Estate. - CNA/jy
