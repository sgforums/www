Freddy Lee, brother of former MM Lee, dies

    SINGAPORE : Freddy Lee Thiam Yew, the younger brother of former Minister Mentor Lee Kuan Yew, has died.

    He was 85.

    Mr Freddy Lee died at home on Thursday evening.

    The family said he has been suffering from cancer.

    Mr Freddy Lee was the former chairman of stockbroking firm J Ballas and Company.

    The former police officer is the second youngest brother of Mr Lee Kuan Yew.

    Mr Lee Kuan Yew has three brothers and one sister.

    Dennis Lee Kim Yew, a businessman, died in 2003 from kidney cancer. He was 77.

    The youngest brother, Dr Lee Suan Yew, is a medical practitioner.
