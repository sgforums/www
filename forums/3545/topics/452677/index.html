    ST Forum - May 22, 2012

    I AGREE with Mr Howard Lin ('Right foreign professionals always welcome'; May 12) that Singaporeans are willing to accept foreigners whose expertise is vital to our economic growth, but not those who accept jobs at lower wages.

    Such foreigners are willing to work for much lower pay, and their living expenses are also low because they rent rooms.

    While companies are eager to hire such workers, does the quality of their work match Singaporeans'?

    Singaporeans are not unwilling to upgrade their skills. But they do not want to take on an office job for a factory worker's pay, which is what employers pay foreigners.

    Thahira Begum (Ms)
