    Mar 17, 2010 - The Straits Times

    Maid hit teen, wanted

    THE police are looking for a 25-year-old Indonesian maid after she allegedly threatened her employer's daughter with a knife, and hit her on the head before tying her up with a telephone cable.

    The maid then escaped, probably through the kitchen window of the third-storey flat, where eyewitnesses saw her sitting on the ledge. The short-haired woman was wearing a red shirt and blue jeans.

    The drama unfolded at the Chestervale Condominium in Bukit Panjang at about 2.45pm yesterday.

    It is not known why the maid apparently threatened the teenager with the knife, but the girl tried to snatch it away, and suffered some cuts in the process.

    The maid then allegedly hit her on the head with a bowl before tying her up.

    The Secondary 4 student did not see how the maid escaped but managed to free herself. She then called her parents, who in turn informed the condominium management about the incident.
