    Late-night noise nuisance from military planes

    I HAVE been living in Jurong West Street 64 for five years. In the past six months, I have become increasingly irritated by the noise of military planes. This was especially so a week ago when the planes were still flying past at 11pm.

    I know military training is important and that there are airspace constraints in Singapore. However, the concerns of residents cannot be ignored.

    I have a five-month-old son and a three-year-old daughter and they are often awakened by the noise of the planes.

    I believe the noise is generated by planes returning to the airbase and not by outbound planes. My guess is that the outbound planes might be flying over the Lim Chu Kang area, while the returning ones fly over our area.

    Perhaps the relevant authorities could shed some light on this and on whether the planes could be re-routed? I also wonder if flying past at 11pm is allowed and if changes can be made to the timing.

    Woo Mun Loong
