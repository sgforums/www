    A youth was chased and hacked late in the night, causing serious injuries. The two youths who did this were arrested four hours later.

    The incident happened at the void deck of Block 1A, Eunos Crescent at 10:35pm on Friday night, May 27, 2011.

    After 22-year-old Ridwan was injured, he ran to the pedestrian crossing and wanted to cross the road.  A female pedestrian found him with his right hand bleeding profusely.

    She immediately brought him to the convenience store nearby to get some bandages and gauze from the female store owner.

    The store owner said: ‘I was shocked when I saw him all covered with blood.  But my shop does not have any bandages so I quickly asked the lady who helped him to report to the police and call for an ambulance.’
