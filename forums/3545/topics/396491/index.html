    HDB: Please understand, residents...

    WE HAVE explained several times to Mr Wee Eng Leong ("Can building plan be changed after vote?", March 18) that the lift shaft position was not changed after polling.

    The lift shaft plan shown in the polling brochure was indicative as detailed technical plans were done after successful polling.

    While the level of ventilation and lighting does not meet Mr Wee's expectations, it is incorrect to attribute this to the change in building plan after polling.

    In retrofitting buildings that were not designed with full lift access, it is inevitable that some disamenity will result, especially if a new lift shaft is built.

    Nonetheless, the lighting and ventilation of the staircases and lift lobbies on site must comply with the statutory requirements. In this case, HDB has also installed additional lighting at the ground floor.

    We seek the residents' understanding on this matter.

    Lawrence Pak

    Deputy Director (Upgrading Programmes Management)

    Housing & Development Board
