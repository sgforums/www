    STOMPer birdsama saw a schoolboy cutting his finger with a penknife and writing the words 'I FAILED TODAY' on a concrete bench at a badminton court near Fajar MRT Station in Bukit Panjang.

    He said:

    "I took this picture at a badminton court near Fajar LRT Station.
