    By Elena Chong

    TWO men were fined $2,000 each on Wednesday for fighting with each other along Clementi Avenue 2.

    Goh Choon Hong, 53, a lorry driver, and Koh Chee Beng, 52, a forklift machine operator, admitted to hitting each other near Block 350 on July 1.

    The court heard that Koh was riding a motorcycle along the main road when he sounded his horn at Goh, who was driving out of the carpark at Block 364.

    Goh continued to drive his lorry out of the carpark and cut into Koh's lane.
