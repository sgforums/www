  • BanguIzai
    30 Apr 14, 23:25

    To be placed after the word

    \  = for falling tone (a backslash)
    /  = for rising tone (a slash)
    _ = for a low level tone (an underscore)
    -- = for a mid level tone (two dashes)
    ''' = for a high level tone (three apostrophes)
