Missing old-time cinema ushers

  • Clivebenss

    Feb 7, 2011 - ST Forum

    WE WENT for an 11am show on Jan 29 at Cathay Cineleisure Orchard, but were late for the movie after being caught in a traffic jam.

    The show had already started screening when we entered the hall. It was pitch-dark and we had a hard time locating our seats.

    Our present movie theatres may be smaller, but I sorely miss the good old days when ushers shone torches and assisted patrons to their seats when the show had begun.

    Bennie Cheok
