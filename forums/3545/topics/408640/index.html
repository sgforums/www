    By Mustafa Shafawi | Posted: 20 August 2010 1210 hrs

    SINGAPORE : There has been a chain collision involving seven vehicles on the PIE towards Tuas before Clementi Avenue 6 on Friday.

    One of the vehicles involved was a YOG bus.

    The SCDF received the call at around 8.45am.

    It despatched two ambulances.

    Police said there were no injuries, and no one was rushed to hospital.

    The accident resulted in traffic congestion, with the vehicles involved taking up two lanes.

    But it was cleared within an hour. - CNA /ls
