    Jun 4, 2010 - Straits Times

    5 charged with gang robbery

    FIVE Bangladeshi men were charged with gang robbery of RM500,000 (S$214,600) from a money changer, 41, while armed with knives.

    Mohd Ali, 30, Muhammad Zahirul Islam Muhammad Aminul Islam, 26, Masum Hayder Tofazzal Hossain, 28, Salim Miah Ibrahim Miah, 32, and Md Milton Talukder Md Samsul Haq Talukder, 31, allegedly robbed Mr Zainun Sultan Baharuddin of the cash at a flat in Dorset Road last Thursday.

    Three of them are said to have been armed with knives at the time.

    They were remanded at Tanglin police division and will appear in court next Friday.

    If convicted they face a jail term of between five and 20 years plus at least 12 strokes of the cane.
