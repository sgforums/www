    About Space Battle Cruiser Armada MK VIII:

    The Space Battle Cruiser Armada MK VI, VII & VIII was a sealed top secret project for United Space Colonial Federations. The blueprints and the inventors went missing in the middle of the research and development and thus the project was shelved.

    During the fourteenth galatic war, year 2453, Space Pirates, known as Dread Mason, successfully captured a colonial outpost from the United Nations Space Federation and made it a Space Pirate Outpost. It was then known to the worlds that the Space Battle Cruiser Armada MK VII was used in the role and four such carrier class spaceships had held off an entire legion of fourty seven destroyers, eighteen cruisers, twelve battleships, eight spacesubs and six carriers from the United Nations Space Federation. Till now, the outpost was operated under the Space Pirates.

    It was rumoured that the MK VIII, the masterpiece of the same carrier had fully been equipped to handle an entire army by its own.

    About Space Fighters - Silver Falcon:

    The Close Space Combat Fighters are spacecrafts mainly utilised to attack other similar small spacecrafts. These other small spacecrafts can be the Space Bombers, carrying nukes, or Space Insert Fighters that forces itself into a spaceship and destroying it internally. Thus, the Close Space Combat Fighters either work as a protecter for these spacecrafts or used to destroy these spacecraft. The Hawkeye is the name of one such unit for close space battle. Though not a top class fighter, its ability to utilise bombs made it indispensible.

    About Space Insert Fighters - Black Vulture:

    The Black Vulture is a utility insert fighter, as it can perform roles such as repairing or destroying. Armed with the grappler, the Black Vulture is a hideous and notably the most feared insert fighters. These grapplers could easy destroy any close space combat fighter within its reach. Any battleship found surrounded with these vultures is considered dead. Also known as the Grim Reaper.
