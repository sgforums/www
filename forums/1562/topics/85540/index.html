    Apple-Cinnamon-Walnut Muffins

    Prep Time: 20 min
    Total Time: 40 min
    Makes: 12 servings, 1 muffin each

    1-1/2 cups all-purpose baking mix
    2-1/4 cups Bran Flakes, divided
    1/4 cup plus 2 Tbsp. firmly packed brown sugar, divided
    3/4 cup chopped Walnuts, divided
    1/2 cup cinnamon-flavored applesauce
    3/4 cup fat free milk
    1 egg, lightly beaten

    PREHEAT oven to 400Â°F. Combine baking mix, 2 cups of the cereal, 1/4 cup of the brown sugar and 1/2 cup of the walnuts in large bowl. Add applesauce, milk and egg; stir just until dry ingredients are moistened.

    SPOON batter evenly into paper-lined medium muffin pan, filling each cup 3/4 full. Combine remaining 1/4 cup cereal, 1/4 cup walnuts and 2 Tbsp. brown sugar. Sprinkle over batter.

    BAKE 18 to 20 min. or until wooden pick inserted in centers comes out clean. Serve warm or cooled.

    Bon Apetit

    Caramel Coffee

    Prep Time: 10 min
    Total Time: 10 min
    Makes: 6 servings, 3/4 cup each

    6 Tbsp. ground MAXWELL HOUSE Coffee or any variety
    1/2 cup caramel topping
    4-1/2 cups cold water
    3/4 cup thawed whipped topping (optional)
    2 Tbsp. chopped chocolate-covered toffee (optional)

    PLACE coffee in filter in brew basket of coffee maker.

    POUR topping into empty pot of coffee maker. Prepare coffee with cold water. When brewing is complete, stir until well blended.

    TOP each serving with a dollop of the whipped topping and chopped toffee, if desired.

    Hot Cocoa Marshmallow Cupcakes

    Prep Time: 15 min
    Time: 1 hr 15 min
    Makes: 24 servings, 1 cupcake each

    1 pkg. (2-layer size) cake mix
    4 cups miniature marshmallows, divided
    1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chunks
    1/4 cup milk
    1 tub (8 oz.) whipped topping, thawed
    1 Tbsp. unsweetened cocoa

    PREPARE and bake cake mix as directed on package for cupcakes in paper-lined muffin pan. Cool completely.

    MEANWHILE, place 2 cups of the marshmallows, chocolate chunks and milk in large saucepan; cook on low heat until marshmallows and chocolate are completely melted and mixture is well blended, stirring constantly. Cool at least 30 minutes. Gently stir in whipped topping.

    FROST cupcakes with whipped topping mixture; sprinkle with remaining 2 cups marshmallows and cocoa. Cover. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

    Storage
    Desserts frosted or decorated with whipped topping should be covered and stored in the refrigerator.

    Herb-Roasted Lamb


    Prep Time: 5 min
    Total Time: 4 hr 5 min
    Makes: 8 servings

    1 cup dry red wine
    1 cup extra virgin olive oil
    3 cloves garlic, minced
    3 Tbsp. fresh sage, chopped
    2 tsp. dried thyme leaves
    1 tsp. salt
    1 tsp. pepper
    1 butterflied leg of lamb (about 3 lb.)

    MIX wine, oil, garlic, herbs and seasonings. Pour over lamb in large resealable plastic bag; seal bag. Refrigerate 2 hours or overnight to marinate, turning occasionally.

    PREHEAT oven to 350Â°F. Place lamb on rack in roasting pan.
    BAKE 50 to 60 minutes or until meat thermometer reaches 160Â°F (medium). Let stand 10 minutes before slicing.

    Bon Apetit

    Raisin Bran-Berry Muffins


    Prep Time: 15 min
    Total Time: 35 min
    Makes: 12 servings

    1-1/2 cups raisin bran cereal
    1-1/3 cups flour
    1 Tbsp. Baking Powder
    1-1/4 cups frozen blueberries
    1 egg
    1/2 cup sugar
    1 cup milk
    1/3 cup butter or margarine, melted

    MIX cereal, flour and baking powder in large bowl; stir in frozen blueberries. Beat egg in medium bowl; mix in sugar until well blended. Stir in milk and butter. Add to cereal mixture; stir just until moistened. (Batter will be lumpy.)

    SPOON batter into greased or paper-lined muffin pan, filling each cup 2/3 full.

    BAKE at 400Â°F for 20 minutes or until golden brown.

    NOTE
    Keep berries completely frozen until stirred into batter. This will minimize bleeding and gumminess in baked muffins.
