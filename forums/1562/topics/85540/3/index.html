    Cinnamon Caramel Iced Coffee


    Prep Time: 10 min
    Total Time: 40 min
    Makes: 6 servings

    6 Tbsp. coffee, any brand
    1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
    1/2 cup caramel flavored dessert topping
    4-1/2 cups cold water
    Thawed whipped topping (optional)

    PLACE coffee in filter in brew basket of coffee maker; sprinkle with cinnamon. Place caramel topping in empty pot of coffee maker. Add water to coffee maker; brew. When brewing is complete, stir until well mixed. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

    POUR coffee mixture over Coffee Ice Cubes in tall glasses. Serve with milk and sugar, if desired.

    Top each serving with thawed whipped topping. Garnish with chopped chocolate-covered toffee.

    Coffee Ice Cubes
    Pour cooled coffee into ice cube trays. Freeze until solid. Use to chill iced coffee drinks without diluting flavor.

    Ritz Spider



    Prep Time: 5 min
    Total Time: 5 min
    Makes: 6 servings, 1 spider each

    12 RITZ Crackers
    1/4 cup peanut butter, divided
    48 small pretzel sticks
    Halloween sprinkles
    12 currants or small candies

    SPREAD each of 6 crackers with 1 tsp. of the peanut butter; cover with second cracker to form "spider's body."

    INSERT 8 pretzel sticks into the peanut butter layer of each spider for the "legs."

    SPREAD 1 tsp. of the remaining peanut butter onto top of each spider. Decorate with sprinkles, then add 2 currants to each for the "eyes."

    Mocha Java Chiller



    Prep Time: 5 min
    Total Time: 5 min
    Makes: 2 servings, about 1 cup each

    1/2 cup cold prepared strong coffee, any variety
    4 triangles (1/3 of 3.5-oz. bar) TOBLERONE Swiss Bittersweet or Milk Chocolate with Honey and Almond Nougat, coarsely chopped
    1 pt. (2 cups) coffee, chocolate or vanilla ice cream, slightly softened
    2 tsp. grated TOBLERONE Swiss Bittersweet or Milk Chocolate with Honey and Almond Nougat
    2 Tbsp. thawed whipped topping

    PLACE 1/2 cup cold prepared strong coffee and 4 triangles (1/3 of 3.5-oz. bar) TOBLERONE Swiss Bittersweet or Milk Chocolate with Honey and Almond Nougat in blender container; cover.

    BLEND on high speed until chocolate is chopped into small pieces. Add 2 cups coffee, chocolate or vanilla ice cream; cover. Blend on high speed, using on/off action, until smooth.

    POUR into 2 tall glasses. Top each with 1 Tbsp. thawed whipped topping and 1 tsp. grated additional TOBERLONE Swiss Bittersweet Chocolate. Serve immediately.

    Simple Oreo Cheesecake



    Prep Time: 10 min
    Total Time: 50 min
    Makes: 8 servings

    2 pkg. (8 oz. each) cream cheese, softened
    1/2 cup sugar
    1/2 tsp. vanilla
    2 eggs
    6 OREO Chocolate Sandwich Cookies, coarsely chopped (about 3/4 cup), divided
    1 OREO Pie Crust (6 oz.)

    PREHEAT oven to 325Â°F. Beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla with electric mixer on medium speed until well blended. Add eggs; mix just until blended. Stir in 1/2 cup of the cookies.

    POUR into crust. Sprinkle with remaining chopped cookies.

    BAKE 35 to 40 minutes or until center is almost set. Cool. Refrigerate 3 hours or overnight. Store leftover cheesecake in refrigerator.

    Bon Apetit

    Hot Spiced Tea Mix

    Prep Time: 5 min
    Total Time: 5 min
    Makes: 2 cups mix or 16 servings, 2 Tbsp. each

    1-1/2 cups lemonade iced tea mix
    1/2 cup sugar
    1 tsp. ground cinnamon
    1/2 tsp. ground cloves

    MIX all ingredients. Store in airtight container.

    FOR 1 Serving Place 2 Tbsp. Hot Spiced Tea Mix in large cup or mug.
    Add 3/4 cup boiling water. Stir until mix is dissolved.

    FOR 4 Servings Place 1/2 cup Hot Spiced Tea Mix into heatproof pitcher or bowl. Add 3 cups boiling water. Stir until mix is dissolved.

    FOR 16 Servings Place 2 cups Hot Spiced Tea Mix in heatproof container. Add 3 quarts (12 cups ) boiling water. Stir until mix is dissolved.
