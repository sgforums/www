    Goldfish - the dirtiest among all the fishes, need alot of space for it to stay healthy

    Do you know that they need 10 gallon of water per goldfish. For example a normal 2 feet tank is about 16 - 17 gallon. So, the max number of goldfish the tank can contain should be only 2. Anything more than that is really over crowding.

    Overcrowding will make the goldfish more prune to diseases.
