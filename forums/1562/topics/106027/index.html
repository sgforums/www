  • aH_v
    8 Dec 04, 00:17
    bismarck:

    Hope it's not too late.

    You need to do a number of things in check.

    1. Do not over feed. Left overs must be clean out and not relying on the filter to do it.
    2. Maintain regular water change at about 30% every 2 to 3 days.
    3. Service your filter regularly. If using external, once every month and change the medium every 3 months.

    Now.. your ill fishy.
    1. Isolate your fish.
    2. try using Pimafix and Melafix mixed.
    3. Do not feed the fish during these time.
    4. Service your filter.
    5. Clean up your tank.
    6. If you do have plants in your tank, check if they are rotting.

    Good luck.
