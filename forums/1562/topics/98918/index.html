Dun Stress, Stretch!!

  • fishing7
    21 Oct 04, 02:01
    Some maybe have seen this but i will just repeat it here.

    Stress, wat it that? More like a mental effect thinking of the result even though it may not happen.

    Eg.
    - You are given a task of organising a event for a big scale of people
    - You start to panick, worrying about the result. Thinking that u will definitely do a lousy job, or people commnenting that the event is a failure.
    - In the end, u stress yourself and start to show sign of rejecting the task
    - You Stress too much on the result, which may not happen

    Stretch!
    - You are given a task of organising a event for a big scale of people
    - By not worrying too much, u begin asking people for suggestions, going to the extend of calling people. Stretching number 1
    - You surf the web a little extra to find out more about happening places. Stretching number 2
    - You may even go down to the few places to test water. Stretching number 3
    - In the end, most people are happy with the event and thank you for that

    Common Excuses that may increase your stress level without u knowing
    1. No time
    2. Cannot Communicate well
    3. Why arrow me? or why is it always me?
    4. People dun like me

    You can do it... just re-prioritise
