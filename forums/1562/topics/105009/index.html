    Originally posted by laurence82:

    after reformating my c drive which contains my system files, will it affect in anyway my files in other hard drives? especially those word or excel files..
    i am assuming that u got at least 2 drives connected by SCSI or RAID or primary/ secondary IDE or SATA. drive C with operating system
    and MS office software installed. some files were created at drive C with MS office and saved in word or excel format onto drive D (for example).
    if u do a clean format and reiinstallation of OS at drive C, and intent to open up the files now at drive D when u reboot......no u can't. U still need to re-install MS office at drive C in order to open Files previously created in C and saved to D.

    If you are booting from drive D, and D also has a compatible or equivalant OS installed, u can open files saved in D, assuming that D previously has MS office installed too!
