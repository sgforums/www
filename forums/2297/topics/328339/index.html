    3:7 can be converted to 9:21

    Since James and Ruth together had 3 times as many stamps as peter, the ratio of Peter:James:Ruth is

    10:9:21

     

    Peter and Ruth=> 10:21 has 310 stamps

    So Ruth has 310 / 31 * 21 = 210 stamps
