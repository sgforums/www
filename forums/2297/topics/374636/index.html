    Dear students and teachers,

     I hope this will help our students to do well.

    1. Conjecture or MI involving trigo.

    2. Area of region by summing areas of rectangles and find the limit as number of rectangles tends to infinity.

    3. Sketch graphs of transformations, including f'(x). Be sure to understand the meanings of increasing/decreasing function and concave up/concave down.

    4. Given a problem description, formulate and solve a DE.

    5. Evaluate arbitrary vectors, or solve questions with a given diagram.

    6. Method of differences involving trigo and deduce an inequality.

    7. Rates of change (pure question or combined with DE).

    8. Solve a system of equations involving complex numbers.

    9. Hypothesis testing involving an unknown (e.g. population mean, significance level, sample size). Explain the meaning of p-value.

    10. Probability involving Venn diagrams. Independence of and/or mutually exclusive events.

    11. Sketch scatter diagrams or regression lines in relation to r.

    12. Equations of tangents/normals (e.g. whether it intersects curve again).

    13. Formulate a recurrence relation or find its formula.

    14. Sketch the graph of a rational function with unknowns.

    15. Geometrical properties involving complex numbers. Loci (involving an arbitrary complex number in a certain quadrant) related to the finding of max/min moduli or intersections.

    16. Solve an inequality (perhaps by a graphical approach), followed by a suitable substitution to solve a similar inequality.

    17. P&C involving cards, letters.

    18. Find volume of region bounded by lines and curves (to be sketched) by substitution or other techniques (e.g. by parts).

    19. Solve a system of linear equations.

    20. AP/GP question involving patterns.

    Thanks!

    P.S. This is the second version. I will provide some concrete examples in subsequent posts, so do look out for them! Thanks.

    Cheers,
    Wen Shih
