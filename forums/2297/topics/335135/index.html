    |PA| means modulus of the vector PA. this mean find it's length.of vector PA. direction is no longer important as either direction will have the same length.

    length of AP, |AP| = |1/4(b-a)|

    BP = BO + OP
          = -OB + OP

         = 3/4a - 3/4b

    |BP| = |3/4a - 3/4b|

    hence |BP| is 3 times bigger than |AP|
