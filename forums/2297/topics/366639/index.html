    If u start revising everything from sec 3 now, it would be fresher in ur memory when u revise it again in Sept/Oct for ur exams.

    Example u study now, when u study again the next time for ur exams, u can maybe just see the topic and ask urself what u know about it, jot them down then compare again with ur notes and further correct anything that u remembered or understand wrongly. This is better than starting to memorise everything from scratch.

    The purpose is not really to do pure memory work but more understanding as well since understanding helps u recall the facts. Don't just blinding memorise everything into ur brain and end up forgetting it directly after the exams since thats not the purpose of learning.

    Revising early also gives u time to find out the areas u are unsure of so that u can get them clarified early rather than last min before exams.
