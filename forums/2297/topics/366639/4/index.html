Those same IQ as you and revising you also no fight. Those same IQ and also not revising ok got fight. Those less bright than you BUT revising got fight with you. Those less bright and also lazing ok you beat them.

    Of the 6 catagories of students, you beat 1 catagory, fight with 2 and lose to 3.

    Suppose your fight turn out average aka the fighting chance one hor you beat 50% and lose to 50%.

    Aka finally you lose to 4 and beat 2. Aka you end up losing to 66% and beating 33%.

    Tell me what is the expected outcome?
