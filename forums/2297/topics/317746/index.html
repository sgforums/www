    source base have only about 5 types

     

    Inference "is the cartoonist a support of ..."

    Compare/contrast "How different are source A and B.'

    reliabilty "Does source D prove .."

    usefulness "How useful is source E in showing.."

    mixed question type."Source A and E are different in content, does it mean that  one of them is wrong?"

    You will need to master the skills from finding

    1. purpose PAMOV (problem, audience, messgae, intended outcome of speaker and verd)

    2. tone (bias, objective, one sided, negative etctec)

    3. Reliability enhanced (highest level!)

    you can ask your teacher about these skills and i am sure she will help you. i am taking my Os this year too and this format seems to work for me! in my recent SS midyears, i got 78 (: heh.

     

    if you wish to know more, just PM me (:
