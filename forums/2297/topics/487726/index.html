    SINGAPORE - Police are appealing for information on the whereabouts of Kyra Xue Ting Lefebvre, 10. She was last seen in a school located along Edgedale Plains on Feb 17, at about 3.30 pm. Kyra is about 145cm tall and has a mole on her right cheek. She was last seen wearing a white blouse, light green skirt and white shoes. She was also carrying a blue backpack and wearing white-framed spectacles. Anyone with information on Kyra's whereabouts should call the police hotline at 1800-255-0000 or submit information online at www.police.gov.sg/CrimeStopper. All information will be kept strictly confidential.
