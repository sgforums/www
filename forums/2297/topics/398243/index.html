    Q2

    girls not taking part=0.4

    boys not taking part=0.6

    0.4 girls+0.6 boys=480 pupils

    0.4 girls=240 pupils

    0.6 boys = 240 pupils

    total boys=400

    total girls=600

    total pupils= 1000
