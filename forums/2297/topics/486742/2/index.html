    You could check the websites of the respective JCs for more info. Without a pure science subject, they would only allow you to take 1 Science at H2 level and 1 Science at H1 level. A typical Science Combination would consist of 3H2 subjects (eg: Physics, Chemistry, Math) and 1H1 Subject (eg: Economics). This is known as PCME, which is one of the most common combinations. You would also have to take H1 Mother Tongue (if you didn't take Higher MTL at O Levels), H1 General Paper and H1 Project Work along with the 4 other subjects


    You could always try to appeal for 2 H2 Science subjects if you get A1 for Combined Science and if your L1R5 is good. But if that doesn't work, be prepared to consider other options
