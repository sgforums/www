    Hi,

      Expansion and validity range are basic skills; do look up details in your school's notes.

      Coefficient of x^n may be obtained by:

      1. looking at the pattern presented by the expansion;

      2. considering the general term; and

      3. knowing results such as

      (a) general term of x^n for the expansion of 1/(1 - x) = x^n,

      (b) general term of x^n for the expansion of 1/(1 - x)^2 = (n + 1) x^n,

      (c) general term of x^n for the expansion of 1/(1 - x)^3 = (1/2)(n + 1)(n + 2) x^n.
