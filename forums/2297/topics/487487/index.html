    I believe a C6 in English will allow you to enter many poly courses, i remember for poly courses the O level subjects requirements are stated.

    However, your overall O level results will be factored into consideration if you apply for NTU in the future after you graduate from poly.

    O levels takes up 20% of weightage for NTU application: http://admissions.ntu.edu.sg/UndergraduateAdmissions/Pages/PolyDiploma.aspx#addsr

    As for O level English, if you do not have B4 and above, you will need to sit for a simple Qualifying English test if you were to apply to NTU: http://www.ntu.edu.sg/Students/Undergraduate/AcademicServices/Matriculation/Pages/CriteriaforQET.aspx

    As for NUS, it seems that only A levels English/GP or equivalent can be used for exemption purpose.
