    y = [(x - a)/(b - x)]^1/2

    y = [(x-a)^(1/2)]/[(b-x)^(1/2)]

    dy/dx

    = [ (1/2)(x-a)^(-1/2)(1)(b-x)^(1/2)-(1/2)(b-x)^(-1/2)(-1)(x-a)^(1/2)]/(b-x)

    = 1/2(x-a)^(-1/2)(b-x)^(-1/2)[(b-x)+(x-a)]/(b-x)

    =(b-a)/ [2(x-a)^0.5(b-x)^1.5)]

    When x = (a+b)/2

    dy/dx

    = (b-a)/ {2[(b-a)/2]^0.5][(b-a)/2]^1.5}

    =(b-a)/{2[(b-a)/2]^2

    =(b-a)/0.5(b-a)^2

    =2/(b-a)
