    Many private diplomas have no market value at all ie employers will not pay you a few hundred dollars more compared to "O" level holders, and they will not give you higher position due to your private diploma over the "O" level holders.

    This is especially so when  the diplomas are given by the pte schools themselves ie the diplomas are not awarded by some international organsiations or foreign universities.

    Diplomas that are awarded by international organisations eg LCCI, ACCA (CAT) do indeed get higher pay and recognised by th employers.

    So, you will need to check the private diplomas whether they are really recognised by the employers and you MUST NOT simply believe the pte schools that all their private diplomas are recognised by the employers and that their schools are edu-trusted by the CPE and so all their pte diplomas are recognised by the employers. In fact, many employers in both the private and government sectors DO NOT recognise these private diplomas.

    i will say, go for a private diploma. no point wasting 2 yrs in ite only to spend another 3 years for a diploma. thats 5 yrs to get a diploma, when you can get a private dip in 1-2 yrs.

    BUT, make sure you do your reseach thoroughly before signing up for any of the courses.

    many of the diploma courses are awarded by local private schools themselves, or by some unheard of school overseas. these certs will definitely not be recognised by anyone.

    look for courses which offers certs from reputable schools, which are valid for application for further studies outside of the private school you got the cert from.

    e.g. MDIS offers diploma courses which only MDIS recognises. you can use the mdis dip to apply for mdis degree course, but no one else outside of mdis recognise the cert.

    another rule of the thumb. if you never heard of the school, that means your future employer would also have not heard of it. therefore, its a useless cert.

    go for courses which awards certs issued by wellknown schools such as RMIT, murdoch, university of glasgow, university of london etc. they carry more weight outside.
