    Go ahead and apply to poly if you're sure there's a course which fits your intended career. But don't go to poly just because you hate GP.

    Being great in your English helps GP, but GP isn't English, and there are many students every year who are still not great in their English, eg. China nationals, but because they have figured out the requirements of GP, they still manage to score As and Bs.

    Worst case scenario, you just only need to pass GP (quite easily done, as opposed to scoring A), and excellent grades in all your H2s will still allow you to enter many good NUS, NTU and SMU courses.
