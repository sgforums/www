    The UAS is used to decide which student gets priority of entering the course first, subject to merit. Thus, a student with a higher UAS will get priority of entering into the course than another student with a lower UAS. However, what grades you get also plays a very important role in who can qualify for the course. If you do not meet even one of the pre-requisites required by the course to enter, you basically have no chance of getting in even though your UAS is higher than the course cut-off UAS.
