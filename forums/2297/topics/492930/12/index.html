    By looking at Fe3+ + e -> Fe2+ 

    Increase in Fe3+ will cause +0.77V to become more positive.

    Then you factor in the equation

    Ecell = Ered - Eoxd

    Original Ecell = +0.80V - (+0.77V)

    New Ecell [After adding Fe3+] = +0.80V-(+0.80V) = 0
