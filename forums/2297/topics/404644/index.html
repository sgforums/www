    The foremost thing which you can do is to get your classmates' work and have a look at them. Unlike most people who will advice you to only get the "A" students' works, I will advice that you get some from the "A" students as well as the "B" students, and compare it with your own work. 

    Look at what you are missing that prevents you from scoring higher, and what is essential for an "A" piece of work. 

    Try to mimic their style, and from there, slowly change what is theirs into your own by adding your own "flavouring". You can just copy and paste whatever they do instead, but that will not truly make you better in English.
