    PGDE 

    CS1: JC Maths

    CS2: Sec Maths

    The above is possible, you will have to take 2CSes (same subject at different level for JC)

    However, MOE prefer Maths/Applied Maths/Stats graduate, as nowadays in JC, a maths teacher has to do many extra duties (it is not optional) such as assist students in research/project/competition. Many of these activities require Mathsmatical knowledge at U2 or U3 level. If you do not have a strong mathematical background in U, MOE may not find you suitable in teaching JC.
