    X : Y : Z
    6 : 8 : 12

    every 1 hour X paints 1/6 of the wall
    every 1 hour Y paints 1/8 of the wall
    every 1 hour Z paints 1/12 of the wall

    3 hours -- X paints 1/6 x 3 = 1/2
    3 hours -- Y paints 1/8 x 3 = 3/8
    together, they painted 7/8 of the wall

    Y + Z paint remaining 1/8 of the wall

    1/8 + 1/12 = 5/24
    1/8 / 5/24 = 3/5h

    3 + 3/5 = 3.3/5h

    On the first day,

    number of $8 tickets sold = number of $5 tickets sold = $1664 / ($8 + $5) = 128.

    therefore on the first day alone, they collected 128 x ($8 - $5) = $384 more from the $8 tickets.

    meaning on the second day, they collected $248 more from the $8 tickets.

    (from here on in i'm trying my best not to use algebra, any alternative method??)

    If they had sold equal number of $8 and $5 on the second day (48 tickets each), the amount collected more from $8 ticket willbe $48 x 3 = $144.

    For every 1 $5 ticket they sell less and 1 $8 ticket more, they will collect $13 more.

    $248 = $144 + $13 x 8

    Means that they sold 48 - 8 = 40 $5 tickets and 48+8 = 56 $8 tickets on the second day.

    Therefore they sold 128 + 56 = 184 $8 tickets.
