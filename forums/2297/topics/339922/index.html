    your usage of chain rule is wrong (although some would prefer to use quotient rule here)

    d/dx (2x-6) / (x^2 -6x +10)
    = d/dx (2x-6) (x^2 -6x +10)^(-1)
    = (2x-6)(-1)(x^2 -6x +10)^(-2)(2x-6) + (x^2 -6x +10)^(-1)(2)

    Try continuing from the above.
