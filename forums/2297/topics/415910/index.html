    PSLE results out Thurs

    THE 2010 Primary School Leaving Examination (PSLE) results will be released on Thursday.

    Pupils can obtain their results slips from their respective schools from 12pm onwards.

    Eligible students will also receive option forms from their respective primary schools to select secondary schools. These forms are required to be given to the pupils' parents.

    In every sealed Secondary One (S1) Option Form, there will be a unique S1 Personal Identification Number (S1 PIN) which allows parents to submit options for their child's secondary school online via the S1 Internet System (S1-IS).

    The S1-IS is accessible 24 hours a day through the Ministry Of Education's Secondary One Posting website at www.moe.gov.sg/education/admissions/secondary-one-posting/ starting from 12pm on Thursday to 3pm next Tuesday.

    Parents can also submit their child's secondary school options through their child's primary school from 12pm to 3pm on Nov 25, and 9am to 3pm between Nov 26 and Dec 1 on weekdays.

    The Secondary 1 posting results will be released on Dec 22, Wednesday.
