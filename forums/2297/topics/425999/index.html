    only took O level german before.

    there's a oral test, where they give you a text to read, followed by a dialogue with the examiner about a given topic. e.g. your future, your interests etc.

    listening comprehension exam. format is much like your PSLE exam.

    as for the written paper, there is a composition, some comprehension text, cloze passage.

    for A levels, at H1 level, format is around the same.

    for H2 level, its a different, with literature aspect, where you have to study german literature texts, german poems etc.
