    20 cent l__l__l__l__l__l__l__l

    50 cent l__l__l__l__l__l__l__l__l__l__l__l

    Black = Original

    Purple = The 20 coins

    Comparing the two, you will realise that 20 coins = 4 units.
    Thus, 1 unit = 5 coins.

    Amount of 20 cent coins at first = 3 units = 3 X 5 = 15 coins
