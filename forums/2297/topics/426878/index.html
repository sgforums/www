    If you go ahead with this route, you should retake your H2 Chem + H2 Bio 'A' levels every year (during your Poly years) until you score 'A' grades for both subjects. 'A' grades for both H2 Chem + H2 Bio, plus a good GPA for your BioTech diploma, will get you better chances of entering Biological Science in NUS/NTU, than either an 'A' levels cert or a diploma, alone.
