SG team wins International Biology Olympiad for the 1st time

  • Darkness_hacker99

    The Singapore team beat 236 students from 59 countries to win first place at the 23rd International Biology Olympiad (IBO) held here, reported The Straits Times. The four Singaporean students won four Gold medals at the Olympiad that ended today.

    This is both the first time the Republic has won first place, as well as four Gold medals.

    The medalists are Nol Swaddiwudhipong, Zhang Hui Ting and Mao Haitong of Raffles Institution, and Lim Yuan Wei of Hwa Chong Institution. Swaddiwudhipong is also the top Gold medalist in the Olympiad.

    The other team that also received four Gold medals came from the United States.

    Some of the world's most promising secondary school students participated in the competition. The IBO aims to challenge and stimulate these students to expand their talents.
