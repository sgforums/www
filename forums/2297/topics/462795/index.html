    The results of the GCE N-Level examinations will be released on Monday, Dec 17.

    School candidates may obtain their result slips from their respective schools from 2pm. Private candidates will be notified of their individual results by post. They will also be able to obtain their results through the Singapore Examinations and Assessment Board website, www.seab.gov.sg, from 2.00 pm on the same day.

    Students who wish to apply for the various courses offered by the Institute of Technical Education (ITE) can obtain the application forms from their schools when they collect their results.

    Students eligible for the Polytechnic Foundation Programme (PFP) and/or the Direct-Entry-Scheme to Polytechnic Programme (DPP) will also receive from their respective secondary schools, a copy of Form P and/or Form N, inviting them to apply for the programmes.

    N-level results released with 72.6% moving on to Secondary 5

     
    Published on Dec 17, 2012
    3:00 PM


    Normal stream students kept up their performance in this year's N-level examinations.

    Of the 12,510 Secondary 4 Normal (Academic) students who took the exams, 72.6 per cent did well enough to go on to Secondary 5.

    This year, 98.1 per cent of the 5,855 Normal (Technical) students also passed the exams.

    The numbers were the roughly the same as last year.
