    At the place where you got stuck, the equation has the form:

    A - ½ = B

    where A and B are complicated expressions. Just square both sides:

    (A - ½)^2= B^2

    A^2 - 2(A)(½) + (½)^2 = B^2

    Then can simplify and solve for n.
