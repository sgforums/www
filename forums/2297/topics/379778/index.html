  • LatecomerX
    24 Oct 09, 21:51

    6b - 3g = 110
    b + g = 84

    b = 84 - g
    6(84 - g) - 3g = 110
    504 - 6g - 3g = 110
    504 - 110 = 9g
    394 = 9g
    g = 394/9
    b = 84 - 394/9
    b = 84 - 43 7/9
    b = 40 2/9

    Hence, there are 40 boys, 43 girls, and 1 who is more girly than boyish at the farm (he or she took 9/5 apples, by the way).

    If all are straights, the difference should have been a multiple of 9 (504 - d = 9g), ie. "108 more apples".

    Originally posted by -Wanderer-:

    Ken had $20 more than Minnie at first . After Ken had given 1/5 to Minnie , Minnie had $64 . How much does Minnie have at first ?

    At first

    Ken        l____l____l____l____l____l________l     ( Big one = $20 ) 

    Minnie   l____l____l____l____l____l

    Now

    Ken       l____l____l____l____l______l     ( Big one = $16, since 1/5 is given away )

    Minnie   l____l____l____l____l____l____l__l  ( Small one = $4 ) 

    Hence, $64 = 6 units + $4

    6 units = $60

    1 unit = $10

    At first, Minnie = 5 units = $50
