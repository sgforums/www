    Truth is, having a JC H2 foundation in the Sciences (specifically Maths, Physics & Chemistry) opens more doors (than any other subject). For instance, unlike the Science courses in NUS, many courses in the Faculty of Arts & Social Sciences are open to anyone who makes the grade, regardless of their JC subject background. Meaning if you intend to take Economics, Psychology, etc in NUS, it may still be a better idea to study Sciences in the JC, so that the doors to NUS/NTU Science are still open to you, should you wish to change your mind.
