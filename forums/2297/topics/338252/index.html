    Go solo or form a group of up to 3 and show your moves at the GATSBY Styling Dance Contest Recruitment Road Show. Eight Shortlisted teams will battle it out at Zouk on 17 January 2009 and the winner will walk away with S$2,000 cash plus a chance to represent Singapore at the Asian Finals in Tokyo, Japan on 28 February 2009, against dancers from six other Asian countries.


    To participate, first log on to www.gatsby.sg to register. Then, record your dance moves in a video and submit it to Mandom Corporation in a CD.

    Alternatively, you can come over to the GATSBY Styling Dance Contest Recruitment Road Show. Professional videographers will be on site to record your dance performances, be it house, hip hop, R&B or street, for submission to GATSBY judges.

    Look out for a live hair styling demonstration by renowned hair stylist Jeric See, celebrity appearances by Chua En Lai, Sheikh Haikel and Georgbe Chan, fun games to be played and exciting prizes to be won at the Road Show!

    Recruitment Road Show is on December 6-7 (Sat-Sun) from 1-6pm at Orchard, Cathay Cineleisure. For more information, visit www.gatsby.sg or contact GATSBY at Tel: 6235 6700/ dance@gatsby.sg
