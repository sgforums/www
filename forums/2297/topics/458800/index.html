    Yes, but only if you score an A1 or A2 in your combined Chem + Phys science, then every JC will allow you to take H2 Chem + H2 Phys.

    Otherwise (if you only pass your combined science, instead of an A1 or A2), then only Millenia Institute will allow you to take H2 Science.

    And you're right, to be eligible to apply for Medicine, you need H2 Chem and either H2 Phys or H2 Bio. And in practice, you need A grades for every subject at A levels to stand a chance of a successful application.
