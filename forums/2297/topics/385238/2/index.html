    Informatics & IT School (also known as IIT) offers 6 diplomas -

    Cyber & Digital Security (CDS)

    Financial Business Informatics (FBI)

    Game & Entertainment Technology (GET)

    Information Technology (IT)

    Interactive Media Informatics (IMI)

    Mobile & Network Services (MNS)

    - all interesting courses and very relevant to industry. It depends on which aspects of IT you are keen on - there's good range to suit different interests. U can check out the website http://www-iit.tp.edu.sg/ and find out more about each diploma. Good idea to check out the blogs too. cheers
