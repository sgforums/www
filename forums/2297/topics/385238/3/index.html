    Originally posted by The man who was death:

    I have a place reserved at temasek poly for gaming technology. Would like a little info on what to expect from that course

     

    Heys! I'm in Game Entertainment Tech(GET). Hmm, what to expect... where do I start...

    Well, I can tell you one thing though, if you love the gaming industry, like reading up on gaming news sites and basically have a passion for gaming in general, you'll LOVE this course. Because of what you learn, the opportunites presented to you during the course and finally, the like minded classmates and friends!

    Oh, and you can expect to be learning programming, as it's quite important in the gaming industry, alongside Game Design. Programming languages include Java and C++(at least, that's what I've done so far.) Other than that, there's also 3d modelling and animation, although I have'nt done that yet so I can really talk too much about it.

    In addition to stuff like programming and Game design, you also get chances to play early builds of games that are'nt out yet as game studios such as Ubisoft and EA concact TP for game testers from GET.  I've been to one of these playtests too. Got a chance to playtest Assassins Creed 2 early last year about 6 months before the game was released. I can tell you, being inside a game studio is an awesome experience.

    Oh, other opportunities also include working as a volunteer at the Games Convention Asia, that one's really eye opening experience. You get to see people from all over the industry and even though it's a conference(y'know, talks and stuff) it isn't boring cos it's about games!

    Anyways, if there's anything else you would like to ask, feel free to reply! I'm always lurkin' around sgforum anyways.
