    Unless you drop out of poly, and enter it again (which seriously I will strongly advise again, since there is no guarantee they will take you back in again), there is no way you can repeat the year.

    If you have serious illnesses which cause you to miss the entire sem, it is possible to redo the sem. But if you are redoing the year because your grades are bad, then it is not possible.
