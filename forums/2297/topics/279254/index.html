    13 Sep 07, 18:10
    use ln(1+u) = u - u^2/2 + u^3/3 + ...
    and
    put u = -sin x
    which expands to -x + x^3/3! - x^5+5!

    sub the 2nd expansion in to ln(1+u) and u should get
    -x -x^2/2 -x^3/6 -x^4/6 +x^5/9 +7x^6/360
