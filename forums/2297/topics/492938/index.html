    FairPrice Share-A-Textbook: Donate your pre-loved textbooks at ANY Fairprice store from 9 Nov – 4 Dec 2016

     

     

    Q: What and when is the donation period?

    This is when members of the public donate their pre-loved textbooks for the project. All FairPrice stores (Supermarkets including FairPrice Xtra, FairPrice Finest, FairPrice Shop, FairPrice Xpress @ EssoService Stations and Warehouse Club) will serve as collection points during this period.

    This year, the collection period is from 9 November to 4 December 2016. All books collected will be sent to sorting centres for sorting before distribution.

    Q: What type of used textbooks can one donate?

    Fairprice welcomes all textbooks that are in the current syllabus (click here for list of books in current syllabus) and in usable condition (i.e. no torn / missing pages and no scribbling).

    Q: Will FairPrice take in assessment books, story books or encyclopedias?

    Yes they do, they will be sorted under reference category for the public to choose from.

    Q: When is the *public distribution day?

    Members of the public are welcome to pick up pre-loved textbooks on 11 December 2016 at any of the five distribution centers.

    * Students under the Priority Collection Scheme will pick up their textbooks on 10 December 2016.

    Q: What are the things to note on distribution day?

    Please be advised to bring along your own bags as they will not be provided on the distribution day.

    Q: What happens to the textbooks which are not collected during distribution day?

    Remaining textbooks will be sent for recycling.

    Q: Where can feedback or enquiries be channeled to?

    Feedback and enquiries on FairPrice Share-A-Textbook 2016 can be channeled to the hotline at 6888 1649 or email at fptextbook[at]fairprice.com.sg.
