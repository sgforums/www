    A hour and a half later, distance van travelled

    = 60km X 3/2 hours

    = 90km.

    Hence, distance between van and car = 90km

    Speed of car is 15km/h faster than van.

    Amount of time for car to reach van

    = 90km/h  /  15km/h

    = 6hours

    Hence, time taken for car = 6 hours.

     

    time taken for van = 6 hours + 1 and half hours = 7 and half hours

    Time which van start out

    = 0630 - ( 7 and half hours )

    = 2300
