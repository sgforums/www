    mean=9
    The variance is computed as the average squared deviation of each number from its mean.

    (biased)variance=[summation 1 to n (xi-mean)^2]/n
    =[(3-9)^2 + (4-9)^2 +...+ (35-9)^2)]/9
    =796/9
    =88.4

    (unbiased) variance
    796/8
    =99.5

    deviation=square root of variance.
