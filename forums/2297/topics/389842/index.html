    School A [] [] [] [] [] []
    School B [] [] [] [] [] [] [] [] [] []

    This year

    School A [] [] [] [] [] [] + 60students
    School B [] [] [] [] [] [] [] [] [] [] - 20students

    School A(This year) = 6units + 60students
    School B(This year) = 10units - 20students.

    6units + 60students = 75% x 10units - 20students
    6units + 60students = 7.5units - 15students

    Question :

    Last year the enrolment of school A was 60% that of school B.

    This year, the enrolment of school A was increase by 60 and School B decrease by 20. If the enrolment of school A is 75% that of school B this year, what was the enrolment of each school last year?

    Steps :

    1.   Draw the bar model for last year

          A  l_l_l_l_l_l_l

          B  l_l_l_l_l_l_l_l_l_l_l

    2.   Draw the bar model for this year

          A  l_l_l_l_l_l_l            + 60  =  3 units

          B  l_l_l_l_l_l_l_l_l_l_l  - 20  =  4 units

    3.   By comparison for school A,

          1 unit = l_l_l + 20

    4.   So, 4 units = l_l_l_l_l_l_l_l_l + 80

    5.   By comparison for school B,

          l_l_l - 20 = 80

          l_l = 50 students

    6.   Hence, the enrolment for last year was

          A l_l_l_l_l_l_l = 6 x 50 = 300 students

          B l_l_l_l_l_l_l_l_l_l_l = 10 x 50 = 500 students
