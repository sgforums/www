    There's only 5 Polytechnics in Singapore

    -Singapore

    -Ngee Ann

    -Temesek

    -Nanyang

    -Republic

    In decending order from Oldest to Youngest.

    I will be diplomatic and not comment on which I think it's best. (Every year, there will be Inter Poly Bashing, NP will say that they are better than SP and SP like wise will take a snub at NP)

    Rather than deciding the school, go to their individual websites and look at the courses and the modules you will learn and am interested in. I'm unsure if the course you are currently taking only allows you to go to certain courses, this you will have to check with the lecturers and helpers during Poly's open house.

    Do note their locations and if it's feasible for you (aka travelling time) as school can start as early as 8am. Wouldn't really make sense if you leave at Pasir Ris and train all the way down to Dover (though I have friends who do that )
