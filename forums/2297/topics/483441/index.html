    Yes, you can participate in the DSA-JC Exercise if you fulfill the age requirement for the level [i.e. 16 to 20+ for JC1 admission if you are Singaporean Citizen (SC) or Singapore Permanent Resident (SPR)]. If you are non-SC/SPR, you must be between 16 and 18+. If you are given an offer by a JC/Institution, you must achieve GCE ‘O’ Level examination results that meet the eligibility criteria for admission to JCs.
