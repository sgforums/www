    Short answer : Yes

    Personal recount is written as 1st person whereas narrative may be written in both 1st person and 3rd person depending on the marker. But I would advise you to always stick to 1st person for personal recount and 3rd person for narrative since u are probably taking the O levels. Grammar and tenses are the same for both qns except for the pronoun.
