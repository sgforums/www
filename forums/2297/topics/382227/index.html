    Hi Okmijnub, 

    There is a slight mistake in your workings.

    When dy/dx = 1, x = 3/2. Hence, y = 9/4.

    The equation of the tangent is :

    1 = [y - 9/4] / [x - 1]

    y - 9/4 = x - 1

    y = x + 5/4
