    For me.....just remember all the facts, go into the exam, choose the SEQ topic you most familar with, 

    1. Screen through question for GF/OF/Links (1 minutes) Question A

    2. Intro (2mins)

    3. GF1(5mins)

    4.OF (10 mins- try to choose those OF that are short)

    5. COnclusion (2 mins)

    6. Screen through question for GF/OF/Links (1 minutes) Question B

    7. Intro (2mins)

    8. GF1(5mins)

    9.OF (10 mins- try to choose those OF that are short)

    10. COnclusion (2 mins)

    11. Read sources (10mins +Understanding of issue)

    12. Question 1 (Normally inferences type) (10 mins max)

    13. Q2-4 for sbq, normally cross ref, usefullness, etc.....25 mins

    14,. check (5mins) Funish seq conclusion

     

    It may be rush with SS, but more practise with good knowledge of the content matter really help
