    TS, your question is not phased properly.

    Are you saying that while you are studying in studying in JC, can you take Add Maths ?

    JC does not offer add maths BUT you can take add maths as a private "O" level candidate while you are studying in JC.

    If your concern is that due to not taking add maths in "O" level, will you be able to cope with "A" level H2 maths (or H1 Maths) while you are studying in JC ?

    There will be no problem to cope with "A" level H1 maths.

    But there will be a lot of problems to cope with "A" level H2 maths if you have not studied add maths in "O" level.

    The advice is to get help on the pre-requisite add maths knowledge for "A" level H2 maths before you enter JC.
