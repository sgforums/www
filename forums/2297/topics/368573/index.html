    Hi students,

    I will be crafting 5 sets of O level Science (Chemistry) papers. Students who are taking this subject will benefit most from it. It comes with step-by-step solutions to the answer. Though it is not huge contribution, but I hope it helps students who really need additional papers to practice on.

    Students who are taking the revised syllabus (5116, 5118) and old syllabus (5152, 5154) can do this paper. Except for the minute difference between the syllabus, students taking the revised or old syllabus may still do the paper. Each set of papers contains 20 chemistry MCQ (worth 20marks), and structured and free response section (worth 65 marks).

    The core of the papers lies in the application of concepts, and in-lined with the use of critical thinking skills. This papers helps to prepare students for O levels exams through greater understanding and reinforcement of the concepts.

    Difficulty of paper will be set 3 out of 5 to stimulate realistic-yet holistic examination style.
