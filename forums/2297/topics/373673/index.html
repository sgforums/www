    1)

    a^2= a-2

    square both sides

    a^4= a^2-4a+4

    a^4= (a-2) - 4a +4

    a^4 = 2 - 3a (proven)

     

    2)

    a^2 = 2a -3

    multiply by a

    a^3 = 2a^2 -3a

    a^3 = 2(2a-3) -3a

    a^3 = a -6 (Proven)

    -2a^3 = -2(a-6)

    a^2-2a^3 = a^2 -2a +12

    a^2-2a^3 = (2a-3) -2a +12

    a^2- 2a^3 = 9 (proven)

    3)

    2a^2= 3a -4

    Multiply by 2a

    4a^3= 6a^2 - 8a

    4a^3 = 3(3a-4) - 8a

    4a^3 = a -12 (proven)
