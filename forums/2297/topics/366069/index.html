    Originally posted by davidche:

    I agree. Different subjects have different study techniques. Personally, mine is all about using mnemonics. Read notes before lecture. Listen during lecture. And going through 1 more time after lecture at the end of the day by creating those mnemonics & notes with key points/mind-maps. And most importantly, do as many practice questions as possible! Especially for subjects like Math. My friend told me his method once: All he does is read through once. Then just do as many questions as possible from workbook/tutorial. If he doesn't know anything, just refer to the textbook/notes on how to do it. The reason he does this (reading through once only) is because he says he would get "sian" if he keeps staring at the same facts all day long...
