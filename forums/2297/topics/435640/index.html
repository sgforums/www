    Originally posted by Jasmine ngjiamin:

    Pale green solution A---------- (excess aq NH3)----------> Green ppt------(expose to air warming)------------> Red brown ppt C

    What is A and C?

    Then,

    Red Brown ppt C------(solution PH1)-------> Yellow/brown solution B----(bubble SO2)----->A

    What is B?


    A is Fe2+ (aq)

    C is Fe(OH)3 (s)

    B is any soluble Fe3+ salt, eg. Fe2(SO4)3(aq) or Fe(NO3)3(aq) or FeCl3(aq).
