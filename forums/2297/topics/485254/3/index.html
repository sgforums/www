    The normal grades without moderation is this(shown below):

    As you can see, in order to achieve A1 without moderation, it'll require 75% marks. One of it could be the paper is too easy, so moderation is a way to filter and accurately grade the better students with the weaker ones.

    Similarly.. If the paper is too hard and very few people scored A1, the authorities may lower the boundaries so that a reasonable amount of student still can get A1.
