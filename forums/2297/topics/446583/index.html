    ...... Can 2=1 ? ......


     

    If A=B
    Multiply both sides by A, you get A²=AB.
    Subtract B² from both sides, you get A²-B²=AB-B².
    Factor both sides make it (A+B)(A-B)=B(A-B).
    Divide both sides by (A-B), you get A+B=B.
    Because A=B, therefore B+B=B,
    If 2B=B, then 2=1.

    Similar puzzle can also be seen at http://www.omardo.com/en/: Math Puzzle (2=1).

    Answer
    ↓
    The problem is when you try to divide both sides by (A-B).
    Because A=B, therefore (A-B)=0.
    You can't divide any equation by zero.
