    Different people have different methods of learning.

    For me I pay attention in class. Then understand the things that were being taught. Once I grabs the concept, I go hit the TYS. When I encounter question which I don't understand, I'll go back to my textbook to look for answers.

    If learning is a game, then these are the rules:

    1) Know what you're studying,
    - Understand the concept.
    - Use examples to help.

    How much understand is understand?

    To reach the standards where you can teach your friend. When you understand, you can easily teach your friends.

    2) Do the TYS.
    - Exposed yourself to the Maximum amount of questions that can be ask in different ways.

    There are more than 101 ways to ask a single question.

    3) Learn from you mistake from TYS.
    - Never be afraid to make mistake. When you made a mistake in TYS, then tell yourself you'll not make the same mistake next time.
