    1. Level of difficulty? It depends. Without A Maths, you are not allowed to do H2 maths in most of the JCs,  which is compulsory for all students in Science stream, which implies you can only qualify for art stream in most of the JCs. However, A Maths is not important for admission to Polys.

    2. Pure sciences. For polys, it does not matter much as combined science is sufficient.

    For JCs:

    Top two JCs: Without A2 for Pure Physics/Pure Chemistry, you are not allowed to do H2 Physics/H2 Chemistry.

    Other 1st/2nd tier Jcs: Without a minimum B4 for the pure, or a A2 for the combined, you are not allowed to do relevant science subjects at H2 level.

    Other Jcs: Without a pass for the pure, or a B3-B4 for the combined, you are not allowed to do relevant science subjects at H2 level.

    If you decide to aim for a JC, I strongly suggest you to take 3E4. Otherwise, 3E3 is sufficient for poly admission.
