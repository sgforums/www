    Anyone can advise how to do this qn (with workings)

    In a simple experiment of rolling a six-sided dice, the following events were defined:

    E = {Numbers bigger than 4}

    F = {Odd Numbers}

    Find the probability of Event E or Event F occurring. Show your workings utilizing the General Addition Rule.

    Hi,

      Follow the approach below:

      1. List the elements of the sets E, F, and E intersect F.

      2. Find P(E occurs), P(F occurs) and P(E and F occur).

      3. Apply the result P(E or F occurs) = P(E occurs) + P(F occurs) - P(E and F occur).
