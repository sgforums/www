    A heads up for those thinking of applying for this new "Veterinary Bioscience" NP course.

     

    On a stomp forum, preciouspurple11  pointed out that this new NP "Veterinary Bioscience" course is NOT for animal lovers who want to become veterinarian doctors.

     

    Quite the contrary, the course appears to be more for laboratory technicians who handle animals used for research and testing (ie. what animal lovers argue is animal cruelty).

    If you're an animal lover who wants to be a veterinarian doctor, it might be a better idea to go the 'A' level route, and subsequently to an overseas University to study veterinary medicine.
