    team A has 3/10 times 600 = 180 boys,              420 girls
    team B has 6/10 times 400 = 240 boys,              160 girls

    after transfer, team B still have same ratio of boys to girls. Hence, the number of boys and girls transferred maintain the same ratio, 3 boys to 2 girls.

    after transfer, team A has 180 + 3 units of boys, 420 + 2 units of girls.

    Since new ratio is 40% boys and 60% girls,
     3 x team A boys = 2 times team A girls
    540 +9 units = 840 + 4 units
    5 units = 300
    1 unit = 60

    hence, total transferred = 5 times 60 = 200 children.
