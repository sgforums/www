    Total possible marks = 20x6 = 120

    For each wrong answer, Jane loses the 6 points she could have gotten as well as 3 more points.

     

    Total deduction per wrong answer = 3+6 = 9

    Total marks deducted from wrong answers = 120-75 = 45

    Number of wrong answers = 45/9 = 5

    Total correct answers = 20-5 = 15
