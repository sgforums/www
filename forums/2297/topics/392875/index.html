    If you want to get into NUS or NTU general Science (eg. Physics, Chemistry, etc; you can teach as a career) , you will need to retake your 'A' levels. Maintain your A for H2 Maths, but get an A for H2 Chem and at least a B for H2 Physics. Then you can safely get into NUS or NTU general Science.
