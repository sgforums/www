    I did my o levels back in 2008.

    I don't know about now, but back in 2008, o level comprehensions questions comprises of 3 types of question. They are Literal, Inferential, and Evaluative.

    Literal questions are questions that test whether you read the passage or not.

    Sample Paragraph:
    Car drivers who break the law will face a $30 fixed penalty charge but will not get points on their licence. Lorry or bus drivers will face fines of up to $2500 for the same offence.

    Sample Question:
    What difference in terms of punishment will car drivers, lorry drivers, and bus drivers face if they are caught using a hand-held phone while driving?

    Sample Answer:
    Car drivers will be fined $30 while lorry drivers will be fined a maximum of $2500.

    Use your own words.
