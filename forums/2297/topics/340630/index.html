    The results of this year's GCE N level exam will be out on Thursday.

    The Ministry of Education says school candidates may obtain their results slips from their schools from 2pm on the 18th of December.

    Those who wish to apply for courses offered by the Institute of Technical Education can obtain application forms from their schools when they collect their results.

    Private candidates will be notified of their results by post.

    They will also be able to obtain their results through the Singapore Examinations and Assessment Board website, www.seab.gov.sg from 2pm on Thursday.

    GCE N Levels 2008

    This year's GCE 'N' level results are out!

    And almost all the students from the normal (academic) course are being awarded the N-level certificate.

    As for the normal (technical) course, 97.2 percent received the certificate.

    This year's cohort is also the first where nearly 100 Normal Technical students took up two subjects at the more difficult Normal Academic level.

    87 of them obtained at least a Grade 5 in these subjects.

    In all 73.2 percent of Normal academic students are eligible for progress to Secondary 5 N

    The top student from the normal (academic) course is Yio Chu Kang Secondary's Ao Cheng Kai

    He scored an aggregate of six points for English Language and five other subjects.

    This year, more than 17,800 students from the Normal Academic (11,820 students) and Normal Technical (6,028 students) took the N Levels examinations. --938Live
