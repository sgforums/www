    Originally posted by Hehe-Haha-Hoho:

    Please help me comment on the advantages of taking Triple Science and Double Science. Which will allow you to have a better score in O-Level. And which will be more useful. POA or Biology?

    Hi Hehe-Haha-Hoho,

    Biology will no doubt be a tougher subject to score than POA, but it will also be a more useful subject for you in time to come. 

    As for Triple Sciences and Double Sciences, I will make a short list of some of the advantages and disadvantages of Triple Sciences. For double Sciences, you will just need to swap the advantages of Triple Sciences with its disadvantages.

    Triple Science

    Advantages:

    - Broader content knowledge base

    - More options in choosing courses/subjects when you enter Poly/JC, provided that you can do well for all of them

    - Priority in entering the more prestigious JCs if you do well for all your subjects 

    Disadvantages:

    - If you have planned ahead to enter degree courses such as accountancy or electrical engineering, Chemistry and Biology will not be of a great help, thus two Pure Science subjects will suffice.

    - You will have to put in extra time and effort.

    Cheers.
