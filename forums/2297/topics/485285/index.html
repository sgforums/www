    It helps if you understand Chemistry deeply enough to be able to figure out the mechanisms for the inorganic chemistry reactions (at least for the simpler ones, excluding the more complicated redox ones). Try drawing out the mechanisms for the inorganic reactions that you're required to be familiar with, and once you get the mechanisms right, it'll help a lot in that you can recall the reactions more naturally, with understanding instead of blind memorization. Your school teacher will probably have no time to help you with these, best is if you can find a private tutor who is willing (and competent enough) to help guide you with these mechanisms.
