    When V=5

    10t-t^2=5

    t^2-10t+5=0

    using quadratic formula, u get...

    t = 5- 2[sqrt(5)] or t = 5 +2[sqrt(5)]

    The graph of V is n shaped. It hit V=5 at 5-2[sqrt(5)] and fall to V=5 at 5+2[sqrt(5)].

    Thus time interval for which speed = 5m/s or greater

    ={5+2[sqrt(5)]} -{5-2[sqrt(5)]}

    = 4sqrt5 ( proven)
