    The Secondary 1 posting results will be released on Dec 20 at 9am.

    Parents and pupils will be able to check the results through text messaging, if a local mobile phone number was provided during the submission of the child's school choices; or through the Secondary 1 Internet System; or by heading down to the pupil's primary school.

    Students are to report to the secondary schools that they have been posted to on Dec 23 at 8.30am.

    For enquiries, parents may call the Education Ministry's customer service centre at 6872-2220 during office hours.
