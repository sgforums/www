    Hi,

      Can you see that the rth term is a sum of r terms, i.e. sigma i (i varies from 1 to r)?

      Then the rth term is r(r + 1)/2 yes?

      Now, the required sum S = sigma r(r + 1)/2 (r varies from 1 to n). You will need this result:

      sigma r^2 (r varies from 1 to n) = n(n + 1)(2n + 1) / 6

      to obtain the final answer.
