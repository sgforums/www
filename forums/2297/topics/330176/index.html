    Prove that tan x = cot x - 2 cot 2x and hence show that

    2 tan 20 + 4 tan 40 + 8 tan 80 = 9(cot 10 - tan 10)

    * all units are in degree form.

    By substituting using the formula above, I got 2 cot 20 - 16 cot 160. However, I am not able to get 9(cot 10 - tan 10), although they add up to the same value.

    May I know how to further carry on from this? Thank you very much!

  • eagle
    10 Sep 08, 21:59

    Note that tan 160 = -tan 20 since tan (180 - x) = -tan x
    so 16 cot 160 = -16 cot 20

    2 cot 20 - 16 cot 160
    2 cot 20 + 16 cot 20
    18 cot 20
    9 (2 cot 20)

    but, from equation, 2 cot 2x = cot x - tan x

    Hence, 9 (2 cot 20) = 9 (cot 10 - tan 10)
