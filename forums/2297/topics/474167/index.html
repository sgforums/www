    Hi Sec 3 - JC2 students and other students,

     All the best for your coming promos or national exams! For Sec 4 and JC2 students, after your prelims it is time to practise O/A level Cambridge (Singapore / International) papers. I always advise my students to work on papers starting from 2012, 2011, ..... 2003 in reverse chronological order.

    You may also like to try a little of some other schools' tough prelim papers 2013 (if available) to prepare yourselves for challenging questions in the national exams.

    For Sec 3 and JC1 students, I advise my students to dig up the last 3 years of their schools' promos papers and try them.

    My blogs, which have just minor updates recently, provide Free resources and tips for you.
