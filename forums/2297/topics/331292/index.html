    first,  dice or die?

    1) total probability = both dice 1 + both dice 2 + both dice 3...

                                   =a x 1/6  + b x 1/6 +.....

                                   = a/6 + b/6 + c/6 ....

                                   =  (a+b+c+...)/6

                                   = 1/6

    where a, b, c is the probability for each die.

    2) 4/9 chance the white ball is chosen.

      a) 5/9

      b) 4/9

    3)  area of circle = pi x r^2

         area of circle closer to center = pi X (r/2) ^2

                                                         = pi x r^2/4

        hence, 1/4
