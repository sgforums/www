    Fe2+ in solid state, eg. Fe(OH)2 (s) is dirty green.
    Fe2+ in aqueous state, eg. Fe(Cl)2 (aq) is pale green.
    Fe3+ in solid state, eg. Fe(OH)3 (s) is reddish brown.
    Fe3+ in aqueous state, eg. Fe(Cl)3 (aq) is yellow.
