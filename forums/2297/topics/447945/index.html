    1st, you must have a good reason to transfer school (like handicap, unable to travel far distant, or got expelled)

    2nd, the school that you're going to transfer to, must have a vacancy. Most of the principals will simply brush you aside unless you have really good reasons.

    Chances of transfering school without exceptionally good reason is close to zero.

    For vincinity issues, you can try talking with the principals  no guarantee but... if the distant between your new house and your old school is really far away, then maybe chances are high. If not.. you'll have to continue in your old school and walk up early in the morning
