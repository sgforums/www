    From my experience in both attending classes at Tuition Centre and being taught by people (tutors) coming to my house to teach me, I would say that both has its pros and cons. Regardless of tuition centre or private tuition, what matters is the qualifications/certifications/achievements that the tutors have. As in, the tutor's own exam grades, the tuition center's certification in quality (if any) (for example), track record (how students generally progress when they attend their tuition), etcetera. Generally, going to tuition centre would enable the student to learn faster as there are many students in the class to identify many different doubts at the same time for the teachers to teach and clarify. On the other hand, having tutors to come to house and teach would enable the tutor to give a lot more attention and focus on the student during the tuition, since it's just one student to one teacher. It is usually quite hard to teach the Chinese Language to Primary school student, especially if Chinese language is not used at home. While the tutors teach him the Chinese language, it would be easier for him to learn the Chinese language if you learn the language along with him and encourage communication in Chinese language at home. Anyway, as to whether to go for tuition centre or private tuition, it depends on how much money you want to spend on this AND the qualifications of the tutors or tuition centre.
