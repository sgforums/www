    Hi,

    a simple calculation: for grp tuition, it's usually about $50~90 per subject, depending.

    Let's take $50, 3 subjects * 2 kids * $50 = $300 a month.
    $300 for private sounds more worth it if the tutor is good :)

     

    I have no experience in teaching Pri sch kids, but I will still say practice makes perfect. It's not just 1 or 2 questions per topic, or 1 or 2 papers preparation. It's at least 30 questions per topic plus at least 10 papers to be done on preparation (at least for A levels). Understanding is only 1 portion of success, the other is to practice and turn the understanding into application before honing it further by more practice.

    Most of the time, what differs between students is the amount of hard work willingingly put in.
