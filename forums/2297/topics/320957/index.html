    [quote]may i know the age limit for repeatees for JC2 for JCs? coz' my appeals failed..[/quote]

     

    To repeat in a JC, the principal has the power to accept or reject you.

     

    To apply as private candidate for 'A' levels, no age limit. You can sit for the or 'A' levels (or 'O' levels) on your 85th birthday just for fun if you like.

     

    But for Science subjects, if you repeat as private candidate, you have to sit for a Practical exam in lieu of SPA. (Unless you're taking the 'A' levels as a private candidate within 1 year after your JC, in which case you can (if you wish, if you're sure you did well in SPA) use your SPA results from your JC.)
