    Cost of Badge l__l__l__l                                               Total = $144

    Cost of Car     l__l__l__l_____$84_____l

    Cost of 6 units = $144 - $84 = $60

    1 unit = $60 / 6 = $10

    Total badge cost = $10 X 3 = $30

    Amount of Badges l___l___l___l

    Amount of Cars     l___l

     Cost of 1 unit of badge = $30 / 3 = $10

    Cost of 1 unit of cars = $144 - $30 = $114

    Amount which 1 unit of cars cost more than 1 unit of badges = $114 - $10 = $104

    Amount of badges in 1 unit = $104 / $10.40 = 10 badges

    Cost of 1 badge = $10 / 10 = $1
