    write the half equations and the overall equation for this reaction.

    1) Balance nitrogens by adding coefficients.

    2) Balance oxygens by adding water molecules.

    3) Balance hydrogens by adding protons.

    4) Balance charges by adding electrons.

     

     

    2NH2OH ---> N2O + H2O

     

    2NH2OH ---> N2O + H2O + 4H+

     

    2NH2OH ---> N2O + H2O + 4H+ + 4e-
