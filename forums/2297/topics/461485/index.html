    Hi! You are not alone! It is very common to fail some subjects. I saw a chem paper at a neighbourhood JC this year and it was tougher than NJC prelim qns 2011 in general.

    What is more important is your attitude. You have to cut down on playing and less computer and hp and other distractions.

    Plan a study timetable. Ask your good classmates for help in questions that you do not understand. Learn to ask questions and think a bit deeper.

    For chem, maybe you can get some neighbourhood JC lecture notes (eg. IJC, YJC) if you find your notes a bit cheeem. These notes are usually clearer and more basic. But they may lack the advanced stuff that RJC notes have. Or you can purchase C S Toh's yellow color guide book. Study the notes, ask questions, and practise your tutorials and some promos qns from past years. make your own notes.

    Most important is to be disciplined. You can make it!

    Or you can consider some tuition for your weakest subject.
