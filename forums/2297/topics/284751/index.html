    Following the reactivity series of metals,

    K
    Na
    Ca
    Mg
    Al
    Zn
    Fe
    Pb
    H (marker)
    Cu
    Hg
    Ag
    Pt

    Heating metal nitrates from Potassium to Sodium

    Results:
    Metal Nitrate (s) ------> Metal Nitrite (s) + Oxygen (g)

    Heating metal nitrates from Calcium to Copper

    Results:
    Metal Nitrate (s) ------> Metal Oxide (s) + Nitrogen Dioxide (g) + Oxygen (g)

    Heating metal nitrates from Mercury to Platinum

    Results:
    Metal Nitrate (s) ------> Metal (s) + Nitrogen (g) + Oxygen (g)

    Nitrite = NO2 (s)
    Nitrogen Dioxide = NO2 (g)
    Nitrogen = N2 (g)
    Oxygen = O2 (g)
