    Hi there, welcome to OSCAR !

    Are you looking for career guidance? Do you want to explore possible career options?

    Look no further, OSCAR@nie.edu.sg is the very website to help you find answers to some vital questions in career planning:

    • What types of career would match your interest and abilities?
    • What kind of work roles would suit you best in the world of work?
    • Which occupations would give you job satisfaction?

    If you are a visitor and wish to find out more about the world of work, our comprehensive and up-to-date occupational information on 300 occupations is accessible to you, free of charge, at the click of a finger.

    If you wish us to take you through a journey of self-discovery and self-assessment, you will need to be a subscriber.     Students need not subscribe if their schools have already registered with OSCAR.

    Accountants provide a wide range of business and financial services. They prepare, analyse and verify financial reports to give up-to-date financial information for management or directors to make decisions. Other services provided by accountants are auditing, taxation and advice on investment and liquidation. Travelling is necessary for accountants who perform auditing at the client's premises.

    Accountants generally strive towards attaining the Certified Public Accountant (CPA) designation, which is associated with high standards of technical competency in accounting, ethnical behaviour and productivity.
