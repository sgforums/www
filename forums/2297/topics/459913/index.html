    It's not about the 85% that matters.

    For maths, and for physics, the most important is your presentation of your answers. You may get the final answer correct, but if bad presentation takes away a lot of your marks, you will still not be able to score.

    So while you prepare for your O levels, take careful note on your presentation too.
