    (i)

    -AB bisects angle DBC, hence angle ABC = angle ABD = 45degree

    -angle ADB = angle CAB

    -Both triangles have DB as a common side.

    = > 2 same angles, and a common side, triangle ABD and BCD are similar triangles.

    (ii)

    DB^2 - DA^2 = DC*DA - DA*DA

                         =DA ( DC - DA )

                         =DA*AC

                          =AD*AB

    Note that AC = AB.
