    Understanding what each is saying is crucial as it defines the direction of your essay......

    Explain what John is saying. Then explain what Mary is saying. Here, it's like a chicken-and-egg story.....

    Then discus the merits and weaknesses of each person's viewpoints.

    Conclude by either agreeing with John or Mary OR not at all....... It's entirely up to you where the topic asks you to discuss.
