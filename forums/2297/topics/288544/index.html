    What is the towing force?

    Ans:

    F = ma

    a = (v-u)/t
    = (20-10)/8
    = 1.25 m/sÂ²

    F = 500 kg * 1.25 m/sÂ²
    = 625 N

    Total towing force = 625 + 500 = 1125 N
