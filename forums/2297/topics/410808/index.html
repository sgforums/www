    Regarding the electrochem qn, B is the correct answer. Precipitating Mg2+ out as Mg(OH)2 ppt lowers the molarity of Mg2+, thus making the oxidation potential @ anode more positive (as predicted by Le Chatelier's principle of system opposing the change). Since cell potential = reduction potential @ cathode + oxidation potential @ anode, hence cell potential becomes more positive.
