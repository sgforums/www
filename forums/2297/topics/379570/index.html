    Originally posted by bratorbread:

    may i ask wad are the pros and cons of writing one word topic essays?

    Hi bratorbread,

    The pro is that you will usually be able to write such essays in any form that you desire, be it narrative or argumentative. You will also be able to expand in many directions, as per your own desire.  

    The con is that some markers can be bias, and if you write something which they are not very fond of, they can mark you down for it, even if what you have written is logical and acceptable. Being able to expand in all sorts of direction can be a con too, as you find yourself stretching out too much in various aspects instead of focusing on a few aspects.  

    Cheers.
