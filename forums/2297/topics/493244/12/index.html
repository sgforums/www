    The JAE registration is now closed. The posting results are scheduled to be released on 2 February 2017.

     

    Applicants who are unsuccessful in getting a place in any of their registered choices will be posted as follows:

    • to a JC course with vacancies, if applicants have indicated a JC course as their first choice. This is subject to applicants meeting the net L1R5 aggregate of the lowest ranked students who were admitted to the JC course;
    • to an MI course with vacancies, if applicants have indicated an MI course as their first choice;
    • to a Poly course with vacancies, if applicants have indicated a Poly course as their first choice. This is subject to applicants meeting the course’s minimum entry requirements;
    • to a Higher Nitec course with vacancies, if applicants have indicated a Higher Nitec course as their first choice. This is subject to applicants meeting the course’s minimum entry requirements.

    The JAE posting results will be released in the first week of February through the following modes:

    • JAE Internet System (JAE-IS)
    • Short Messaging System (SMS) via applicant’s mobile number (if provided by applicants during JAE Registration)

    ACCEPTANCE OF OFFER

    FOR STUDENTS POSTED TO JC/MI

    Students who are offered a course in a JC/MI must report to the JC/MI on the next working day following the release of the posting results. Any student who does not report to the JC/MI on this date will be deemed to have forfeited his/her place. This place will be made available to other eligible applicants appealing for a place in the course.

    FOR STUDENTS POSTED TO POLYTECHNIC

    Students who are offered a course in a Poly should receive an enrolment package by end of February. Students are required to complete their online enrolment by the date stated in the package. Any student who does not complete the online enrolment by the stated date will be deemed to have forfeited his/her place. This place will be made available to other eligible applicants appealing for a place in the course.

    FOR STUDENTS POSTED TO ITE

    Students who are offered a course in ITE should receive an enrolment letter within 3 working days upon the release of JAE posting results. Students are required to accept the course offer online at www.ite.edu.sg by the date stated in the letter. Any student who does not accept his/her course offer online by the stated date will be deemed to have forfeited his/her place. This place will be made available to other eligible applicants appealing for a place in the course. For course and application enquiries, please call ITE Hotline at 1800-2255-483 during office hours (8.30am to 6pm from Monday to Friday).
