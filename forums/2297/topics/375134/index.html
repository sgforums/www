    Hi,

    Admission to JCs (L1R5)
    Aggregate of 20 points or below for L1R5:
    1. English
    2. Mathematics
    3. Science
    4.  Humanities
    5.  Any other O Level subject
    6.  Any other O Level subject

     

    Admission to Polytechnics (L1R2B2)
    L1: English Language

    1st Group of Relevant Subjects (R1): Maths or A Maths
     
    2nd Group of Relevant Subjects (R2) 
    (at least C6 for relevant subjects):

    Architectural Technology
    Science subjects, Art, D & T

    Business-Related Courses
    Humanities subjects, POA

    Science-Based Courses
    Science subjects, F & N
          
    Technology Courses
    Science subjects, D & T

    Best 2 Subjects (B2):   Best 2 other subjects

    Source: Damai Sec Sch's PT Meeting 2008

    Cheers,
    Wen Shih
