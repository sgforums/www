    Question: The number of bacteria in aculture grows exponentially according to an equation given by N = Z e^kt where t is the time elapsed and Z is the intial no. the population of bacteria is recorded to increase from 1 million to 5 million in 4 hours.

    Calculate value of Z and hence find k. (ans key: k = 0.402)

    Approach:
    At t = 0, Z = 1,000,000.

    At t = 4, 1,000,000 e^(4k) = 5,000,000, from which k can be solved.
