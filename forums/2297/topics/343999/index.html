    To add to how the JAE works.

     

    Students are ranked according to their L1R5.

     

    Only when there is a tie, in terms of L1R5 score, then your placement of choice (eg. 1st, 2nd, 3rd choice, etc) matters.

     

    Eg. If 2 students, Mr A and Mr B, both have the same L1R5.

    Mr A puts VJC as his 3rd choice while Mr B puts VJC as his 4th choice.

    There is only 1 spot in VJC left.

    If Mr A fails to get into his 1st and 2nd choice, and Mr B fails to get into his 1st, 2nd, and 3rd choice, then Mr A gets priority over Mr B to get into VJC.

     

    That's the one scenario you have to watch out for.
