    The twitter incident which happened last year. The students has a disability which allows the student to take the exam separated from the main group. Other students with disability will take the exam in a small room to facilitate invigilating process.

    Prior to the start of exam, the examination paper has already been layed out on the table for students. The student took a picture of the exam script (front cover) before the start of examination and post it to twitter. The examiner for that day failed to ensure mobile devices are kept out of the examination room.

    The rule states that any mobile devices capable of transmitting blah blah blah should not be brought into the exam hall (in this case the classroom which exam was held). Students cannot use mobile devices during examination too. This rule was not strictly adherd to because it was a small room. The usual practice is to ask student place their mobile devices away in their bags before the start of the paper.
