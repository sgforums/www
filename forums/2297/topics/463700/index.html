    1 year of TYS? I suppose that will only take you 1 day to complete. Or at most 2 days. Plan wisely. At this stage, just complete your homework, pay attention in class. If you have extra time, try out the topic-by-topic revision for each chapter that your teacher has covered. By the time you start doing the full exam paper for the 'O' level, you'd probably have seen all the questions before. Your school will also give you ample revision. You just have to follow through conscientiously and that will put you in good stead.
