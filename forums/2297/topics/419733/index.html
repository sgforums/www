    Note that if you are studying for 4 years in sec school, you need 20 marks to get that 2 bonus points, and if you are studying for 5 years, you need 21 marks.

    Under the LEAPS grading system, CIP is under service, where you can have a maximum of 5 marks from. And yes, you will get it by doing CIP for 100 hours or more.
