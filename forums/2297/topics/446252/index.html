    Joint Poly Admission Exercise (JPAE) are for first time poly applicants. For those who have been to a poly and wish to apply again, they will fall under Direct Admission Exercise (DAE). Your friend might have missed out crucial information for the admission officer.

    Read http://www.polytechnic.edu.sg/jpae/Overview.html

    Quote from the website

    "Ex-and current Polytechnic students are advised to apply through the individual Polytechnics’ Direct Admissions Exercises (DAE) instead of applying through the JPAE-ITE."
