    D7 can go into JC only if his L1R5 is very good ie L1R5 is = 12 or 13 points or so. In this case, the student will be required to retake and pass O Level English in JC1 before the JC principal will allow him to proceed to JC2. If he fails the O Level English again, he will be asked to leave the JC at the end of JC1.

    So, in your brother's case, he will not be eligible as his L1R5 is already 20 points.

    However, he can still appeal to the JC principal under special talent scheme ie if your brother is good in any sports or he is an inter-school or national player in any sports or games or he is good in arts or music. The JC principal can still accept him.
