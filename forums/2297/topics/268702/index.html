    Besides communicating well, you also want to give a good impression of yourself or your organization.

    Executives do not read letters words for word, they scan the piece of paper looking for WHO, WHEN, WHAT, WHICH, HOW.
    Lawyers read the letter line by line, carefully.
    Pen-pals look for WHO and WHAT gleefully.
    Mothers get excited just receiving the letter.

    So write according to your reader.

    You can choose to heck care because others do not bother.
    Thats your choice. There are no marks deducted.
    You only create a poor impression by a badly written letter.
