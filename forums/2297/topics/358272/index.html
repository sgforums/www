    in one minute, Tap X fill 1/x of tub

    in one minute, Tap Y fill 1/y of tub.

    From question ==> if both tap turn on, tub will be filled in z minute.

    ======>I fill the tub in z minute using both tap, and get this equation

    (1/x)*z  + (1/y)*z   = 1

    =====> simplify

    z(y +x)/xy = 1

    z = xy/(x +y)

    ======> This is the simplest way i can explain it online. If you still can't grasp, i suggest you find your teacher.
