     What is the difference between crossing over and chiasma?? When do they happen?

     

    Crossing over is the process while chiasma is the point which crossing over occur. Crossing over and formation of chiasmata happen during prophase of meiosis I. It is for exchange of genetic information between non sister chromatids of homologous chromosomes, leading to genetic variation.
