    Hi,

      In the syllabus, the key integrals involving trigo are the integrals of:

      1. sin² x,

      2. cos² x,

      3. tan² x.

      For the integrals of sin^3 x and cos^3 x, the following results are useful:

      cos 3x = 4cos^3 x - 3cos x,

      sin 3x = 3sin x - 4 sin^3 x.
