    1)

    Consumption in 1999: 3% = 3000, 1% = 1000, 100% = 100 000

    Consumption in 2009: 32% = 40 000, 1% = 1250, 100% = 125 000

    2)

    Area of forest base on 1st scale:

    1cm -> 4km
    1cm^2 -> 16km^2
    40cm^2 -> 640km^2

    Base on 2nd scale

    0.4cm^2 -> 640km^2
    1cm^2 -> 1600km^2
    1cm -> 40km
