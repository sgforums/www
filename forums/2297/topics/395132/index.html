    Originally posted by ZaiDancerz:

    discursive writing on'' tuition is a waste of time'' how do write?  i just want to finish this as soon as possible cause i still have 2 test to study...

    The first part of your essay should talk about things such as tuition in Singapore, how is it like, who goes for tuition and why, ...... other background information. You can also choose to include current debates or argumentative works regarding the tuition situation. (E.g. The tuition scene in the local context have been changing incessantly over the last decade, and....) 

    Depending on how your level of writing, you can either choose to have 2 parts next, one stating the pros of tuition and the other stating the cons of tuition, or goes in a fact - > counter-fact - > fact - > counterfact style.

    The latter style is only for very experienced writers with a good language command and control over the entire written work.

    At the end of the essay, just end off with your own stand with good reasons as to why you choose that stand. (E.g. Despite the sundry evidences pointing at the foible of tuition, the merit it holds far outweighs the former, and thus.... )
