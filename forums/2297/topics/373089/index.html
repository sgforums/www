    Greenview Farm is 840km away from Redbloom Florist.

    At 2pm, a lorry leaves from Farm to Florist at 80km/h.

    At 3pm, a motorcycle leaves from Florist to Farm at 60km/h.

    How far apart are the two vehicles one hour before they meet?

  • nightzip
    24 Aug 09, 00:25

    3pm : Lorry is 80km from Farm

    4pm : Lorry is 160km from Farm

    4pm : Motor is 60km from Florist

    Dist  apart at 4pm: 840-160-60 = 620km

    Hence, every next 1 hr, dist apart will lessen by 140km.

    So, they will meet at precisely 140x4hrs = 560km

    So, after 5hrs, i.e. at 8pm, the distance between two vehicles are left with only  620-560= 60km

    60/140 = 25 5/7mins

    They will meet at 8:25:5/7 hrs.

    So, at 7:25:5/7 hrs, 140x3hrs = 420km + 220km + 60km = 700km.

    They are exactly at 700km 1 hr before meeting.
