    HPLC is a university year two topic. It is not easy to explain the concepts to Sec 2 students. BTW, do you have any research mentors to guide you into doing the experiments? If not, then this project is unsuitable for Sec 2 to do it. A very mickey mouse way of explaining it is that you have a mixture, you pass the mixture into a HPLC, then the mixture will be separated into its various components by using some solvent, then you pass maybe one of the component into a mass sorter (mass spectrometer) and you find out how many % of the weight in the mixture is due to the weight of MSG. You get some computer print-outs. There are many variations and technical details in HPLC. Regards.
