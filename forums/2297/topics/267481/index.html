    We all know it is important to have a goal. A goal in mind helps you focus better and gives you a direction in life. In Homework Forum, we focus on...... yes... our Schoolwork.

    Post what are the subjects/modules that you are going to take in your upcoming exams, the grade that you are aiming for, and the date of your exams. Work hard towards your goal.

    However, please be realistic and positive towards your goal. Do not post "I aim for a F9/E8/D7 for my [put subject name]". It will serve no purpose but help you adopt a worse attitude.
