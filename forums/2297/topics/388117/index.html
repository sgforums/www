    Hi,

      In case there are students from H2 Maths wishing to know more about CIE F Maths, they should note that the H2 syllabus does not cover a substantial amount of F Maths content:
     
      - Roots of polynomials
      - Centroids and surface areas
      - Areas of polar curves
      - Reduction formulae
      - Second-order DE
      - Matrices
      - Further applications of de Moivre's theorem
      - Confidence intervals involving two means
      - Hypothesis testing involving two means
      - Chi-squared tests
      - Exponential distribution
      - Everything about mechanics (a lot!)

      Students before the H2 syllabus really picked up a lot more than students today.

    Cheers,
    Wen Shih
