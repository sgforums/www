    Hi Teenybean,

    I will suggest that you take up History. This is because as you have stated, you have confidence in doing well for both of the subjects, and that you are someone easily affected by the people in your surroundings. 

    If you are to enter the Geography class and do not get along well with your classmates, it will gradually affect your studies in Geography lessons. This will in turn have a negative impact on your Geography results. 

    If you are to enter the History class, you will have the support of your friends, though most importantly, you must work hard yourself.

    History is a lot about memory work and some interpretation. The good thing about it is that if you put in the effort and memorise and understand the events, you will be able to score high marks. The downside is that if you do no put in adequate effort, it is easy to fail it as well, as the questions are usually quite straight forward without multiple answers.

    I hope this helps.
