    Hi there!

    Whether to retake or not really depends on what you want to do after NS.

    If your grades allow you to go to ITE, then you need not retake.

    If you want to go to polytechnic to get your diploma, then yes, you need to retake. It really depends, you have to check with the various polytechnics for their minimum entry requirements. Most poly courses require a at least a D7 for EMaths, its part of the L1R4.

    If you have a N Level, then use your N Level to go to ITE.
