    1. iron is an essential nutrient for pregnant women. Doctors usually prescribe 350 mg FeSO4 tablets as dietary supplement for pregnant women. H/r it was found that iron tablets can cause poisioning deaths in children as little as 590 mg of Fe2+ can be fatal to a 12.5kg child.

    How many 350mg FeSo4 tablets wold it constitute a lethal dose to a 12.5kg child.

    ans: 5

    (Fe)2+ + (SO4)2-   --> FeSO4

    So, 350mg of FeSO4 would give you 0.35/[55.8+32.1+(16)(4)] = 0.002304 mols of FeSO4

    Since FeSO4 = (Fe)2+, there is also 0.002304 mols of Fe2+ in the tablet

    No. of mols of (Fe)2+ to cause death in a 12.5kg child = 0.59/55.8 = 0.01057 mols

    So number of tablets = 0.01057/0.002304 = 4.589

    Round it up to 5 since 4 tablets wouldn't be enough to kill.

    2. 25 cm3 of 0.02moldm-3 calcium hydroxide is neutralized by 25cm3 of 0.05 moldm-3 of hcl whats the conc of the salt formed in the resulting solution?

    ans: 0.0100moldm-3

     

    Ca(OH)2 + 2HCl  --> CaCl2 + 2H2O

    this is the reaction between calcium hydroxide and hydrochloric acid.

    so since the question already tells you the respective volumes and concentrations, the only thing you can find is the number of mols.

    No. of mols of Ca(OH)2 = 0.025 x 0.02 = 0.0005 mols

    No. of mols of HCl = 0.025 x 0.05 = 0.00125 mols

    you know that one mol of Ca(OH)2 needs 2 mols of HCl for the reaction to take place. but from what you have calculated, the number of mols of HCl is more than what you need. (you only need 0.0005 x 2 = 0.001 mols)

    therefore you CANNOT use 0.00125 to calculate for the number of mols of CaCl2(salt)

    Since CaCl2 = Ca(OH)2,

    No. of mols of CaCl2 = 0.0005 mols

    and the concentration of CaCl2 is therefore 0.0005/0.05 = 0.0100 mol/dm3

    Q1.

    Calculate the no. of moles of electrons ganied/lost by 1 mole of species, by using stoichiometry of the balanced redox equation. Final O.S. = initial O.S. + electrons gained (-ve value) or lost (+ve value).

    The O.S. of N in NH4+ is -3. The question probably gave you a list of N containing species to choose from.

     

    Q3.

    Group III elements often lack a stable octet in their compounds. Eg. AlCl3. Which is why Al is willing to accept a pair of electrons from Cl- to form AlCl4-. The reason they form dimers is to enable the atoms to have a stable octet.
