    Originally posted by bonkysleuth:
    1.7g/cm3 = ______kg/m3

    My answer turns out to be 1700 but I have the impression it's a wrong one. Can someone properly explain to me on how to derive on the right answer?

    1.7g=0.0017kg

    1cm=0.01m
    1cm*1cm=0.01m*0.01m=0.0001m2
    1cm*1cm*1cm=0.000001m3

    Therefore 0.0017kg/0.000001m3=1700kg/m3
