    Hey Ill let you in on a secret . Well its not quite a secret but its not well known either. To get into NUS is not as difficult as som pple think. You don't have to get strait As. Infact there are students in NUS who did not get an A too. You could get a few Bs and a C or two and with a B3 for G.P. you would still have a very good chance of getting into NUS.

    For new JC students who know that they are not academically excellent, my advice is  for the 1st 6 mths or so, pay 65% to 70% effort on studies and 35% to 30% on building an impressive CCA record. Getting a tutor would also help tremendously.
