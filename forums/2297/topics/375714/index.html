    Hi,

      To show that XYZ is equilateral, we show that all three sides are equal in magnitude.

      Vector XY = -3b, Vector YZ = -3a + 3b = 3(b - a), Vector XZ = -3a.

      Now we consider their lengths, i.e. XY = 3|b|, YZ = 3|b - a|, XZ = 3|a|.

      By the fact that |a| = |b| = |b - a| (because a regular hexagon is formed by six equilateral triangles), the lengths are equal and we are done.
