    Hi..i graduated from mjc last year. Lessons start at 8am everyday except on wedsday,9am. Wednesday is also cca day, lessons usually end at 12.30pm for your cca to start(about 1.30pm usually). For monday,tuesday, and thursday, you would not expect lessons to end early especially because it is very common to get lots of extraaa lessons. Ends around 5-6pm at most. Friday usually won't get cos it would be too tiring.
