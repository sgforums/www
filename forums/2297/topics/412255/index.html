    Hey there!NEVER ever guess or predict TOPICS for SS as you will NEVER know what the Angmos will set for SS.Study at least 4 THEMES,5 if you kiasu =D.

    Health Care(Britain & Singapore) + Understanding Governance(Principles of Governance,Controlling and Encouraging Population,Ageing Population &Traffic Flow)=1 Theme

    Conflict in Multi-Ethnic Societies-Sri Lanka & Northern Ireland(Causes & Consequences) + Bonding Singapore(Challenges to a Multi-Ethnic society & Managing Ethnic diversity in Sg)=1 Theme

    Managing International Relations(Causes of Conflict,Diplomacy,Deterrence,Iraq-Kuwait & Terrorism)=1 Theme

    Sustaining Economic Development(Globalization & Singapore's strategies to ensure competitiveness)=1 Theme

    Venice(Rise & Decline,Venice at its peak & lessons learnt)=1 Theme

    Basically,my teacher told our class to choose between globalization & Venice.Majority picked Venice as it was the LAST chapter covered before our SS preliminary exam and 99% chose Venice question while i'm the only one who chose Understanding Governance(Traffic Flow+Population) as i didn't prepare well for Venice ha ha.I would choose Globalization perhaps as it's much more easier for me to understand and memorize for SEQ.Well,it's your preference to choose which theme you desire,All the best to you too!P.S our teacher also told our class that the first 3 Themes are compulsory for us =D
