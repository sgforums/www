    2nd Raffles Community Leaders Forum

    What is RCLF?
    It is a platform for youth to gather and collectively engage specific community issues through facilitated discussions, community interaction and talks. Throughout the forum, youth participants will share their experiences and knowledge with others in the forum to deepen understanding and exchange ideas on the issue. The final product at the end of the forum would be an action plan detailing the contributions that the Forum participants can make towards ameliorating the problem and addressing the needs of the target community.
    In essence, RCLF seeks to encourage youth community activism, harnessing the spirit, creativity and dynamism of youth to play their part in our community.

    1st RCLF
    The Inaugural Raffles Community Leaders Forum was held from 7th to 11th March 2008 at Raffles Junior College (RJC). It was hugely successful and brought together 256 like-minded youth from 11 countries to discuss community issues. It is currently a finalist for the Singapore Education Award 2009 – Best Educational Event by a Public Institution.

    Special Interest Groups (SIGs) this year include:

    MEETING THE NEEDS OF THE ELDERLY
    YOUTH-AT-RISK
    ENVIRONMENTAL ISSUES
    VICTIMS OF ABUSE: OUTRAGE OF MODESTY
    UNDERSTANDING THE INTELLECTUALLY DISABLED
    URBAN POVERTY
    ANIMAL WELFARE
    UNDERSTANDING THE PHYSICALLY DISABLED
    VICTIMS OF ABUSE: DATING AND DOMESTIC VIOLENCE

    Who can apply?
    All youths from ages 17 to 25 with a genuine desire to make a difference are welcome to join us in our forum.
