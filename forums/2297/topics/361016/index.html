    (a) Since AF is parallel to BD, and AD is parallel to BC ( while F is an extend of BC, hence making both FB and FC also parallel to AD ), ADBF must be a parallelogram, with FB = AD. ( Extra notes: The opposite sides of a parallelogram are always of equal lengths. )

     

    (b) Since BC = 3AD, and AD is parallel to BC, triangle AED and triangle BEC must be similar triangles. As triangle AED and triangle BEC are similar triangles, all sides of triangle BEC must be 3 times the similar sides of triangle AED, hence EC = 3AE.
