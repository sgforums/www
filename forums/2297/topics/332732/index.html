    I shall try to explain both(I've done the sums before)

    1.

    as you've mentioned,1/3 h is 20 min and 1/2 h is 30 min.

    30 min - 20 min =10 min (difference between time taken)

    Now,for speed sums,a good way is to do ratio.Just remember,Speed is the reverse of Time and Speed is the same  as Distance.

    Let's do a ratio for speed first

          Speed

    90:96

    15:16

         Time

    96:90

    16:15

    16u-15u=1u

    1u - 10 min

    15u - 150 min

           =2.5 h

    2.5  x 96km

    240km

    Ans:240km

    2.

    In 1 hr, Ah Meng walks 4km.

    In 15 min,Ah Meng walks 1 km.

    Since he would have reached school 5 min earlier if had run at the running speed,

    it means that he would take 5 mins less to travel 1km (if he runs at the running speed)

    So,we take 15 min minus 5 min,

    =10 min.

    So at the running speed,he runs 1 km in 10 min.

    So,at the running speed he runs 6km in 60 min

    The running speed is 6 km/h
