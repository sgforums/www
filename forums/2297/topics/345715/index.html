                                              31 march 2006     31 march 2007      31 march 2008

    sales ledger balances         $12 600                 $16 410                   $11 240

    On 31 march 2006, it was decided to create a provision for doubtful debts at 4% of the outstanding debtors. This is to be maintained at 4% of the debtors at the end of the year.

    REQUIRED

    (a) prepare the provision for doubtful debts account for each of the years ended 31 march 2006, 2007 and 2008. [5]

    (b) state the amount of the net debtors that would appear in the balance sheet at 31 march 2006, 2007 and 2008 [3]

    (c) explain two reasons why mina kaur should calculate an annual provision for doubtful debts. [4]

    (d) state the effect creating a provision for doubtful debts will have on mina kaur's cash balance. [2]
