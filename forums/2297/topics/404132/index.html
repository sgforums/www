    From some MJ friends (in fact, general for almost all jc)

    Science

    BCME, PCME, BCMG, BCMH, BCMEL, PCMG, PCMH, PCMEL

    B - bio, P -phy, c - chem, m -maths, g- geog, h - history , el - english lit, e -econs

    + GP , PW and MTL (H1s)

    Or if take KI, is KI + CME, KI + CMG 

    Arts can be very varied

    Compulsory (Maths)

    Chinese Lit, English Lit, Econs, Geog, History, Chinese Studies

    choose 3

    Plus GP, PW, MTL

    if KI.,
