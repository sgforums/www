    Sounds like your daughter requires a tutor who is proficient at both A level Chemistry *and* A level Biology, *and* it has to be a tutor willing to give 1-on-1 tuition. This is difficult to find for both reasons : at A levels, almost all tutors (at least the good/great ones) specialize in one subject (ie. Chemistry, or Biology, but not both). And almost all tutors (at least the good/great ones), only give group tuition these days, because group tuition is more efficient and worthwhile, for both the tutor and the student (since 1-on-1 tuition will be very expensive for the student, to compensate the tutor for loss of earnings relative to group tuition). Sorry if I'm not directly helping you with your query, but I felt that you should know the truth of the situation.
