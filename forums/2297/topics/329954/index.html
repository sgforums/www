    if you wan you can draw models but i am lazy.. i'll just tell you the steps

    Amy: 4 units

    D: 5 units

    ________________________

    D:6 units

    Alice: 8units

    Comparing D on top and below, we know the LCM (lowest common multiple) is 30 therefore for the 5 units on top for D, you need to divide each unit into 6 parts to get 30. For Amy, you'll also need to divide each part into 6 = 24 parts

    For the D at the bottom, to get 30, you'll need to divide each unit by 5. for each of Alice's 8 units, divide them into 5 parts also and u'll get 40 units

     

    therefore the ratio is 24:30:40, in simplest form = 12:15:20
