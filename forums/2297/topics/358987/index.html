    Q1. Four Factories produced a total of 1398 chairs in a normal day. One particular day, Factory A produces 2 chairs fewer. Factory B produced 4 more chairs that day.     First , I will like you to imagine "1unit" as one of those boxes in model diagrams.

    Q1.

    On normal days,

    Factory A "1 unit" + 2 chairs

    Factory B "1unit" - 4 chairs

    Factory C "1/3 unit"

    Factory D "1 unit"

    Total = 1398 chairs = 3units +1/3 units - 2 chairs

    => 1 unit = 420 chairs

    Chairs produce on that particular day

    = 1 unit + 1 unit + 1unit + 1unit

    = 4 units

    =1680 chairs

     

    On a normal day, total chairs produced by factories B and C

    = "1 unit" - 4chairs + "1/3 unit"

     

    = 556 chairs

     

    Q2.

    Now, let ahmad's original money be "1 A unit" and Siti's original amount of money be "1 S unit".

    Once again, you will have to imagine it as a model diagram : P

    "1 A unit" + "1 S unit" = $100

    "2 A unit" + "3 S unit" = $275

    You will realise now that "2 A unit" + "2 S unit" = $200

    If you use it with the 2nd model ( ahem...equation ), 

    you will get "1 S unit" = $75

    And since Siti receive "2 S unit" from her mother, the amount she receive is $150

     

    Originally, ahmad has "1 A unit", which is $25.

    Every month he receive "1 A unit", which is still, sadly, $25.

    30% of $25 = $7.50

    amount of months needed

    = [$55 - $25 ( initial amount he has )] / $7.50

    = 4 months, including January.

    Hence Ahmad will have $55 in savings in April.

     

    Q3.

    Alice = 16km/h, Betty = 12km/h

    In 30mins, Alice travelled 8km, Betty travelled 6km.

    Hence Alice gained another 2km.

    Total distance ahead = 5km + 2km = 7km.

     

    At 2.30pm, Betty = 7km behind.

    At 4.30pm, Betty travelled 12km X 2h = 24km

    At the same time, Alice travelled only 24km - 7km = 17km

    Hence, Alice's new average speed = 17km / 2 hours = 8.5km/h
