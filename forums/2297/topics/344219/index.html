    If you want to be a Science teacher (like myself), you need to get a University Degree before MOE will hire you. In other words, it's recommended you take the JC route (because this path is more focused on getting into University). You can still take the Diploma route, it's just that you have to make sure you do exceptionally well in your Diploma studies, to get you a place in a University to do a relevant Science course; only after that can you apply to be an MOE teacher.
