    I must have made over two dozen posts across various Sg based student forums on this very topic already. I wish I'd saved the posts so I can simply cut and paste instead of rewriting everything. But I'll summarize here :

     

    1) Take H2 Physics instead of H2 Bio. (H2 Chem is of course, a MUST). This will open a lot more doors in the University for you. All Uni Science courses require H2 Chem, most require H2 Physics, very few require H2 Biology.

     

    2) Is Physics easier or Biology? Depends on the individual, of course! Some will find Physics so much easier. Some will find Biology so much easier. You know yourself best. But just as importantly, ask yourself which is more practical (ie. makes more sense) to study? If your goal is a University degree and financially feasible science-based career.

     

    To conclude, take H2 Chemistry, H2 Physics and H2 Maths. There's a good reason why this is the most popular subject combination in all JCs - because it's the best one for the JC student.
