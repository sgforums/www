    Tins A, B and C each contained some coffee powder. Mr Lim transferred 1/8 of the coffee powder from Tin A to Tin B, then scooped 1/8 of the coffee powder from tin B into tin C and finally transferred 1/8 of the coffee powder from tin c to tin a. Now there are 49g of coffee powder in each tin. How much coffee powder was there in each tin at first?

    working backwards
    tin C - 49g (since trf 1/8 out, now 7/8 = 49)
    7/8 = 49
    1/8 = 7
    8/8 = 56g (after receiving from B)

    tin A - 49g (after receiving 7g from C)
    before receive from C = 42g (which is after giving 1/8 to B)
    7/8 = 42
    1/8 = 6
    8/8 = 48g (before giving to B)

    tin B received 6g from A
    then give 7g to C
    49 + 7 - 6 = 50g

     

    A = 48g
    B = 50g
    C = 49g
