    Policy Process: Formulation and Implementation

    (a) How far is the control of traffic flow in Singapore achieved by the Park and Ride Scheme? Explain your answer. [12]

    (b) Here are three guiding principles of governance:

    • 'Leadership is Key'
    • Anticipate Change and Stay Relevant'
    • Reward for Work and Work for Reward'

    Is any one of these more important than the others? Explain your answer. [13]

    Discord and Disunity: Sri Lanka and Northern Ireland

    (a) How far was the Sinhalese-Tamil conflict due to British colonial rule over Sri Lanka (Ceylon)? Explain your answer. [12]

    (b) Here are three causes of the conflict in Northern Ireland:

    • divided loyalties
    • lack of voting rights
    • unequal employment opportunities

    Was any one of these more important than the others? Explain your answer. [13]

    The Iraw-Kuwait conflict of 1990-91

    (a) How far was the issue of oil production responsible for the worsening relationship between Iraq and Kuwait? Explain your answer. [12]

    (b) Here are three results of Operation Desert Storm (the Coalition operation to retake Kuwait):

    • destruction of Iraqi military forces
    • political instability in Iraq
    • environmental damage
    Was any one of these more significant than the others? Explain your answer. [13]
