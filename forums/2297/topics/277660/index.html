    In the qn below, <= denotes less than or equal to and >= denotes greater than or equal to.

    1. 1/(a-x) <= 1/(a+x)
    Multiply by (a-x)^2(a+x)^2
    (a-x)(a+x)(a+x) <= (a+x)(a-x)(a-x)
    (a-x)(a+x)(a+x) - (a+x)(a-x)(a-x) <=0
    (a^2 - x^2)(a+x - a + x) <=0
    (a^2 - x^2)(2x) <=0
    (a+x)(a-x)(2x) <=0

    Graph is a negative x^3 curve with 3 roots.

    After you have drawn the graph,
    Range of values of x which satsify the inequality above = -a<=x<=0 or x >=a
