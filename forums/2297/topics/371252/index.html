    cos 35 = BE/30

    BE  = 30 cos 35

    sin 35 = CE/30

    CE = 30 sin 35

    Volume of ABEC

    = 1/3 x base area x height

    = 1/3 x (1/2 x AB x BE) x CE

    = 1/3 x (1/2 x 40 x 30cos 35) x 30sin 35 

    =2819.07

    =2820cm^3 (3sf)
