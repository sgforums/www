  • zhlow

    choose poly if :

    -u have a particular liking for a certain subject ( eg engineering? )

    -u can work in a team or u are a project type person

    -able to withstand peer negative influence ( why i say so cos u have more free time to go to town if ur in poly)

     

    choose jc if:

    -u still do not know wad route u should embark on in life. ( unsure what u are good at.. engineering? business? )

    -self-discipline..and not procastinate. u cant afford to procastinate in jc. the work just keep piling up.

    -able to manage stress and can withstand long exam papers. eg. maths paper 1 n 2 is 3hours each.

    -a lvl is similar to o lvl BUT stress level x3 work load x3.

     

    concentrate in ur o lvl first cos u gt hell lot of time to consider after o lvls, do go for diff open houses to see wad diff instiution can offer u.
