    Q1.

    resolve both the forces into the horizontal and vertical components

    taking right direction as +ve : horizontal resultant force = 18 cos 25° - 12 cos 31° = 6.0275 N to the right

    taking upwards as +ve : vertical resultant force = 18 sin 25° + 12 sin 31° = 13.787 N upwards

    using pythagoras theorem, magnitude of resultant force = sqrt(6.0275² + 13.787²) = 15 N
