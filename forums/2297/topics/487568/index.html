    Police appealing for information on missing 11 yr old Singaporean girl



    Police are appealing for information on this girl pictured above who has been missing since Sunday.

    Nur Wafa Aliya Bte Sufizan, 11, was last seen at Blk 114 Ho Ching Road in Jurong West on Sunday (Jan 25) at about 2pm.

    The girl is described as being about 1.2m tall, slim with a light tan complexion.

    She was last seen wearing a black t-shirt and pink pants.

    Anyone with information on her can inform the police at 1800-255-0000. All information will be kept strictly confidential.
