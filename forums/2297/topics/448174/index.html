    You can always appeal to the various JCs (ask to speak to the HODs) to ask them to make an exception for you, if they allow you to demonstrate (eg. by giving you a test) that you're able to cope with H2 Chemistry and H2 Biology / H2 Physics (ie. the subjects required for Medicine).

    As a last resort, you can study in Millenia Institute (a 3 year 'A' level course), which allows you to take H2 Chemistry and H2 Biology / H2 Physics with only a combined science background.
