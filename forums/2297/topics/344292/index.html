    The Open Day is designed to give potential students and their parents a chance to have a look at the Singapore Campus, see some examples of student work, find out how we run our courses, meet our teaching staff and ask questions about the programs of study available.

    Potential students can get advice on:
    • How to enrol
    • The types of programs we offer
    • Career opportunities
    • Costs and payment options
    • Recognition of prior learning & work
    • Modes of study
    • Academic pathways
