    In every hour, the distance between Dick and Mark is increased by 80M-75M=5M

    In order to create a 6M difference in distance, they have traveled 6 / 5= 1.2hours. This is also the time taken for John and Dick to meet.

    Therefore distance between A and B = 100 x 1.2 + 75 x 1.2 = 210M
