    If you are not taking subjects such as Higher Art, Higher Music, Malay (Special Programme), Chinese (Special Programme) or Bahasa Indonesia, your L1R5/L1R4 will be:

    L1R5

    L1 – English/Higher Mother Tongue
    R1 – Humanities
    R2 – Math/Science
    R3 – Humanities/Math/Science
    R4 – Any subject (except Religious Knowledge and CCA)
    R5 – Any subject (except Religious Knowledge and CCA)

    L1R4 (for Millennia Institute)

    L1 – English/Higher Mother Tongue
    R1 – Humanities/Math/Science
    R2 – Humanities/Math/Science
    R3 – Any subject or CCA (except Religious Knowledge)
    R4 – Any subject or CCA (except Religious Knowledge)

    For poly courses, Humanities is a relevant subject for ELR2B2-A and ELR2B2-B courses but not for ELR2B2-C and ELR2B2-D courses.
