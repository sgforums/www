    For the record :

     

    "Phenyl" refers to the benzene ring alone. Eg. phenyl chloride = chlorobenzene.

     

    "Benzyl" refers to the benzene ring with an additional CH2 group. Eg. benzyl chloride = chloromethylbenzene.
