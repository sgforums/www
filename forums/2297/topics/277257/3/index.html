    a) Seriously you don't know?

    1 hr = 60 mins
    4 hr = 60 X 4
    = 240 mins

    4 hr 24 min = 240 mins + 24 mins
    = 264 mins

    bi) Average speed = distance/time
    = 374/4 hr 24 min
    = 85km/h

    bii) 1km = 1000m
    374km = 1000 X 374
    = 374000m
    1 hr = 3600 secs
    4 hr 24 mins = 3600 X 4 + 24 X 60
    = 14400 + 1440
    = 15840 secs

    Speed = distance/time
    = 374000/15840
    = 23.6 m/s (3 significant figures)
