    very long since i log in to help. is spoon-feeding allowed?

    sec 2 algebra i think only deals with only one variable (correct me if i'm outdated):

    let the price of a tshirt be $x.

    so the price of a cap will be $(14-x).

    96/x + 4 = 96/(14-x)                 <--- equating the number of items that can be bought

    simplifying the above (dont spoonfeed all the way), you will get:

    4x^2 + 136x - 1344 = 0

    divide by 4 throughout,

    x^2 + 34x - 336 = 0 (reducing to this equation means how u get to this equation using the information provided by the question to form an equation and simplify)

    solving, x = 8 or -42(rejected, how can price of tshirt be negative)

    hence price of tshirt = $8, price of cap = $6

    happy studying!
