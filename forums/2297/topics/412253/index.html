    y=kx^2, where k is a constant.

    Since y =10, we have:

    10=kx^2

    k = 10/x^2

    let the certain value of x be a.

    When y is halved, y = 5

    5 = kx^2

    5 = (10/a^2)x^2

    x^2 = (1/2)a^2

    x = (1/sqrt2)a or x = -(1/sqrt2)a
