    Photosynthesis

    DEFINATION: It is the process whereby light energy absorbed by chlorophyll is transformed into chemical energy which is used to synthesize carbohydrates from water and carbohydrates. Water and carbon dioxide is the raw materials while oxygen is released in the process.

    ·        Carbon dioxide + Water ------Light energy/ Chlorophyll ------> Glucose + Oxygen + Water

    6CO2 + 12H20 à C6H12O6 + 6O2 + 6H2O

    The glucose produced is either used immediately by cells during cellular respiration and cellular activities or converted into sucrose or starch in leaves, formation of amino acids, fats.

    Photosynthesize is important as it make chemical energy available to animals, remove carbon dioxide and purify the air, stored energy in coal.

    Limiting Reagents of photosynthesis are

    ·        Light intensity

    ·        Concentration of carbon dioxide

    ·        Temperature
