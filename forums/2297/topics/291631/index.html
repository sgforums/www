    f((x- 1/2)e^-2x)dx
    Break it up
    Let u = x-1/2
    let du=x-1/2 dx
    let dw =e^-2x
    let w= -2e^2x

    So basically fu dw= uw-fwdu (got it on paper but difficult to transfer)
    =f((x- 1/2)e^-2x)dx
    = (x-1/2)(-2e^2x)- f(-2e^2x)(x-1/2 dx)
    = (few more steps same as the uw-fwdu)
    = -1/2(e^(2x))x
