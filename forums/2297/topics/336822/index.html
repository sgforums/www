    Flawed question. Combustion implies the reactant has no oxygen. CO reacting with O is called oxidation, not combustion.

     

    So yeah, both A and B are correct, coz the answer is arguably flawed. Happens very commonly (even in the actual 'O' and 'A' level exams).
