    Well, if you talk about when I was around 20 years old:

    At what time do you guys wake up to go to school?

    I used to stay quite a distance from school so I wake up at 6am to attend class that starts at 8am

     

    On a average day, what kind of food would you eat?

    Breakfast: I tend to skip breakfast but if I do eat it’s coffee with soft boiled eggs or bread with butter

    Lunch: Noodles (Soup, Fried or Pasta) or sometimes I’ll opt for Western stuff like burgers, pork chop etc or you get the usual rice with some side dishes (meat/fish/vegetables)

    Dinner: Same as lunch

     

    Special Holidays

    I think ForbiddenSinner has pretty much summed it up.

     

    At what age people there starts to work?

    I would think girls will start work at around 22 to 23 yrs old while guys because of national service will start around 25 to 26 yrs old, assuming they complete their university degree.

    Of course there are the early school leavers who will start work as early as 16 yrs of age.

     

    What do you do to hang out on weekends? (Here we go to parties at clubs or at houses, most of the people love to dance and drink alcohol....)

    I do go clubbing sometimes when I was around 20 years old. But I stayed at home to game (Ultima Online & Diablo 2) and read much more.

     

    What do you think about your government?(Is it efficient?)

    I think our civil service is very efficient. The government departments run like clockwork most of the time.

    I think with every government, there is room for improvement.

     

    Well just for record, I’m female and am now past 30 :(
