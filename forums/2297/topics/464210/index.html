    The titles are "Understanding Advanced Organic and Analytical Chemistry" and "Understanding Physical Inorganic Chemistry" by Jeanne Tan and Chan Kim Seng published by WS education. You may go to Bras Basah Popular and check the books out. Or call up Popular bookshop first to see which branch still has the books. You can source from marketplaces around forums.
