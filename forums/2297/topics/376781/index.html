    Hi,

      For algebra, the following skills may be asked, so do find similar questions to practise:

      1. Expand algebraic expressions, e.g. x(x - 5), (3 + x)(x -1/2).

      2. Factorise algebraic expressions, e.g. x^2 - 5x,  
          x^2 + 6x + 9 which is (x + 3)^2,
         15x^2 + 6x - 9 which is 3(5x^2 + 2x - 3), then simplified to 3(x + 1)(5x - 3).

      3. Form simple algebraic expressions for word problems, e.g. Tom is x years old and his mother is twice as old as him; write down the age of Tom's mother.

      4. Simplify algebraic fractions, e.g. 1/(x - 3) + 1/(1 - 2x).

      5. Solve algebraic equations, e.g. x(x - 2) = -1.

      Thanks and good luck!
