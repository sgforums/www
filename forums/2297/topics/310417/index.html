    required to prove LHS = RHS?

    assume x <> 1, (which would give a division by zero error in the original equation anyway)

    replace the 2 one's on the left (1 in the numerator and 1 in the denomiator) with "(1 - x) / (1 - x)"

    i.e.

    numerator : (1 - x) / (1 - x) + (1 + x) / (1 - x)

    denominator : (1 - x) / (1 - x) - (1 + x) / (1 - x)
