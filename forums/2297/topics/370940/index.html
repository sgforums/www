    Yi Hao, it is great that you want to help, but please do remember to simplfy solutions for P6 and below questions.

     

    Price of a pack of Golf Ball with 30% Discount

    = 70% X $20

    = $14

    Ratio of Golf Balls sold at original price to ratio of Golf Balls sold at discount price 

    = 6 : 4 = 3 : 2

    Let 3 packs of golf balls sold at original price and 2 packs of golf balls sold at discount price be considered a set.

    Cost of one set of Golf Balls

    = Cost of 3 packs of original-priced Golf Balls + Cost of 2 packs of discounted-priced Golf Balls

    = ( 3 X $20 ) + ( 2 X $14 )

    = $60 + $28

    = $88

    Amount of sets of Golf Balls sold

    = Total earnings / Cost of one set of Golf Balls

    = $1056 / $88

    = 12 sets

    Amount of golf balls in one set

    = Amount of packs in one set X Amount of golf balls in one pack

    = 5 X 5

    = 25 golf balls

    Total amount of golf balls sold

    = Total amount of sets X Total amount of golf ball in one set

    = 12 X 25

    = 300 golf balls

    let total number of packet = 10 units.

     

    60% of total unit = 6 unit. 40% of total unit = 4 unit.

     

    30% discount = $20 x 0.7 = $14

    6 x 5 balls = 30 balls. so each unit has 30 balls.

     

    therefore, total balls sold = 30 balls x 10 = 300 balls.
