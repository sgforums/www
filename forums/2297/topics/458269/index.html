    Density = mass / volume

    mass = density  *  volume

     

    Let vol of air = V, hence vol of nitrogen = 0.8V and vol of oxygen = 0.2V

    mass of air = mass of nitrogen + mass of oxygen
    density of air * V = density of nitrogen * 0.8V   +   density of oxygen * 0.2V

     

    You should be able to solve from here :)
