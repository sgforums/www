    Hello, just wanna share my story, cause I used to be failing Lit and SS all the way through upper sec too, but in the end I (somehow miraculously) got A1 for combined humans (SS + e. lit). What I'm trying to say is, it's possible and you don't have to worry :) my advice is, work hard, consult your teachers and read good essays. And some more you used to get As (while I am just naturally bad at humans) so it could just be you need some time to adjust to the upper sec syllabus (or your school papers are crazily tough). For lit, what I did was read a lot of good essays and learn from them. and it also helps to be confident in writing, trust yourself and know that it's not hard, after all there's no correct/wrong answer so whatever you write will score some marks as long as it's logical! and for SS, actually memorising is key but I think it's also important to read the news for some examples you can apply to your essays. Hope my two cents worth helps and all the best :)
