    The GPA in each respective sem does not affect each other. Thus, you can have 1.00 for the 1st sem, 4.00 for the 2nd sem, 0.00 for the 3rd sem.

    However, the GPA for each sem will affect your CGPA. The CGPA is similar to your GPA, but it takes the grades of all of your semesters into calculation.

    Your final results are dependent on your CGPA.

    If you have 4.00 for all three sems of yours, your CGPA will be 4.00 when you graduate. If your buddies have less than 4.00 for any of the sems, it is impossible for them to get 4.00 for their CGPA, as all of the sems are taken into calculation.
