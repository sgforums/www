    If I am not wrong OasisBlue's first and second answer is correct while the thrid... I think is 24 tonnes.

    3rd Question:

    Bi[2]O[3] + 3C --> 2Bi + 3CO

    Since 3 moles of Carbon is used in the equation. We may use a direct proportion method.

    3 moles C --> 2 moles Bi
    36 tonnes C --> 24 tonnes Bi

    For the 1st question:

    Magnesium Nitrate does not react as it is already a salt[an stable ionic compound].

    Magnesium metal, Magnesium oxide and Magnesium oxide do react to form a salt.

    For the 2nd question:

    Direct proportion method

    1 mole of Bi[2]O[3] --> 3 mole C
    Therefore,
    2mole of Bi[2]O[3] --> 6 mole C
