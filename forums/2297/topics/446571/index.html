    f(x) = kx^3+5x^2-7kx-8
    if x-k is a factor, x=k
    f(k) = k^4+5k^2-7k^2-8 = 0
    k^4 - 2k^2 -8 = 0
    (k^2-4)(k^2+2) =0
    k^2 = 4 or k^2 = -2 (rejected)
    taking sqroot 4,
    k = 2     or k = -2 (rejected as k is +ve integer)

    *note tt square root always have + and - ans.

     

    f(x) = 2x^3+5x^2-14x-8=0

    = (x-2)(2x+1)(x+4)
