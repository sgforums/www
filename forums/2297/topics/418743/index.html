    More info can be found at the NUS, Faculty of Dentistry website. Do search under prospective students, you should find the answer to Biology or Physics there. The competition in recent years has been very keen. Applicants are first short listed based on academic results. Of course there are some who merit special considerations. Those who make it through are invited for a manual dexterity test and an interview. I believe that the final decision is made there. The only poly student (that I know of) who was accepted because he had excellent credentials and impressed at the interview. His story was reported in the local media. Most of the successful applicants had 'A' levels and had straight As. Then again most students took the 'A' levels... The IB student I know of had perfect score of 45. Hope this info is helpful.
