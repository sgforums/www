    Q2

    X~B(60,0.4) mean 24, var 14.4

    Y~B(70,0.6) mean 42, var 16.8

    X-Y~N(24-42,14.4+16.8)=N(-18,31.2) approximately

    P(X-Y>0) should be a small value
