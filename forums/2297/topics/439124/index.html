    comprain

    first para, intro yourself, say the reason/why u are writing this letter

    second para describe what happened

    third para say impact or consequences. state 2 or 3. (optional, if no have any)

    fourth para give suggestions, state benefits. also state 2 or 3 (rber to sound convincing)

    last para, por a bit, make tt person/organisation feel good (so tt would consider your suggestions)

    recommendation

    first para, intro yourself, say the reason/why u are writing this letter

    second para describe what u wanna recommend, reasons/why, (benefits also, if any).

    third para, por a bit, make tt person/organisation feel good (so tt would consider your recommendation)
