    If you have not done German language as a third language in secondary school, you will not be allowed to take it as H1 subject in  JC.

    Please refer to the Hwa Chong Institution requirement to take German language as a H1 subject in the URL link http://www.hci.sg/hwangcl/uploadfile/college-subjcomb2012/SubjectInfo&SpecialProgrammesInfofor2012.pdf

    It is to be noted that only the top 10% of PSLE students are allowed to take German language as a third language in secondary schools ie you will have studied German language for four years in secondary before you proceed to do German language as a H1 or H2 subject in JC.
