    Direct entry is only possible for all the engineering courses.

    Once u get admitted into the uni course, you will be sent a list of exemptions and u can choose to have ur 1st year exempted or to take the 1st year like the JC students.

    Those who take direct entry into 2nd year will have to take a few extra bridging modules so their timetable is abit more packed than usual but they do save 1 year of education.

    Those who decides to start as 1st years normally do so to strengthen their foundation as well as to score their A's since being able to be exempted certain content should mean you already know it well and is a good chance to fight for some As since its harder later on when everyone is on equal footing.
