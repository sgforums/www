    H.C.F. is the highest common factor of the 3 numbers.

    For 36, the factors in it are 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 9, 12, 18, 36

    For 48, the factors in it are 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 24, 48

    For 60, the factors in it are 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 10, 12, 15, 20, 30, 60

    In this case, the HCF will be 12, as it is the largest factor present in all 3 numbers.

     

    L.C.M., on the otherhand, is the lowest common multiple of the 3 numbers.

    For 36, its multiples are 36, 72, 108, 144, 180, 216, 252, 288, 324, 360, 396, 432, 468, 504, 540, 576, 612, 648, 684, 720

    For 48, its multiples are 48, 96, 144, 192, 240, 288, 336, 384, 432, 480, 528, 576, 624, 672, 720, 768, 816, 864, 912, 960

    For 60, its multiples are 60, 120, 180, 240, 300, 360, 420, 480, 540, 600, 660, 720, 780, 840, 900, 960, 1020, 1080, 1140, 1200

    In this case, the LCM will be 720, as it is the smallest multiple to be present in all 3 numbers.
