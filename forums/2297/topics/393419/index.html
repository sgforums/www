    Originally posted by coSituationde:

    What if I take 5 subjects, but fail the other 2 subjects?

    2 subject fails : 2 x 9 = 18 points

    How good are your English, E-Maths and Science anyway? A1? If it's C5 or C6, I say forget it, you won't be able to make it.

    3 Subjects C5 : 5 x 3 = 15 points

    Total : 18 + 15 = 33 points (Where can you go?)

    Ultimately, it will depends on what course you want to take in poly and which poly you like to get into which your O-Level results will greatly affects.

    Do take a look at the recent Cut-off Points (COP) for poly courses if you haven't taken a look at it yet and think whether can you make it into your preferred courses.

    The COP shown in the link below can only serve as a guide, no one knows exactly what is the exact COP requirement for 2011.  Depending on the popularity of the course, this year maybe COP of 10 for course A, next year maybe COP of 8.

    Click here to see view JAE 2010 COP

    Like what BuySellGames said, different course required different subjects, even if your L1R4 is good enough to get into a course of your preference, the school may not select you because you failed to pass the required subjects for that course.

    Some poly maybe lenient enough to allow you to get in but not without condition which is to retake the failed subjects while you are studying in poly. Failing to pass the subject after the retake may results in poly kicking you out of the course even if you have been through half of the course.
