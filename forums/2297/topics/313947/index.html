    first you need to understand 2 formuli

    P=V*I

    V= I* R

    Thus I = V/R

    What causes the LED to blow is the high current due to high voltage low resistor.

    If we assume 3V is the maximum voltage that can be applied without resistors, then the maximum current the circuit can take is 5W/3V  = 1.67A

    Subsequently the resistance of the circuit is 3/1.67 = about 2ohms

    With a 9V batt and a resistance of 2 ohms, the current is about 4.5A which is over the limit of the system.

    to reduce the current to 1.6 and below, you can use 9V/R = 1.6 and solve for R.
