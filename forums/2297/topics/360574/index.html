    Academic and Science Research are 2 different categories.

    Academic is for those who have top their Sec school for the past 3 years, and/or those who top competitions like New South Wales ( Higher Distinction ).

    Science Research is for those who have participated in uni-level science research for at least one full month, and/or taken part in big-scale science research competition ( e.g.SYSF ). In this case you will also need A1/2 for at least 2 Science subjects and also both E. Maths and A. Maths.

    And you do not need to worry, even if you get a crappy 20 points for your "O" level, they cannot kick you out once they have already accepted you in.

     

    MJC is alright, average JC.
