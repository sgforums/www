    A point B is 500 m due North of a point A. Rahmat walks from A on a bearing of 325 deg.

     

    Find Rahmat's distance from A when he is

    (i)due West of B.

    (ii) equidistant from A and B

    (iii) Closest to B

    (i) bearings of 325° = 35° anti-clockwise as shown:


    Hence, distance from A = 500 / cos 35°
    = 610.4 m


    (ii) Diagram is as shown:



    Distance from A = 250 / cos 35°
    = 305.2 m


    (iii) Diagram is as shown:



    Distance from A = 500 * cos 35°
    = 409.6 m
