    Let C be (h,k)

    Midpoint of AC = [(h+6)/2 , (k+1)/2]

    Midpoint of AC = E (2,3)

    Therefore [(h+6)/2 , (k+1)/2] = (2, 3)

    (h+6)/2 = 2

    h+6 = 4

    h = -2

    (k+1)/2 = 3

    k+1= 6

    k = 5

    Thus C = (-2,5)
