    Attaining A1-A2 for CCA allows you to deduct 2 bonus points from your ELR2B2, while attaining B3-C6 for CCA allows you to deduct 1 bonus point from your ELR2B2.

    However, the bonus points only affect your NET aggregate score, not your RAW score. It is your RAW score that determines your eligibility for polytechnic/junior college/etc.
