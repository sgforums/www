  • wee_ws

    Hi,

      Consider the fact that

      sin P - sin Q = 2 cos ((P + Q)/2) sin ((P - Q)/2).

      Another alternative is to consider these facts:

      1. sin 48° = sin (60° - 12°),

      2. the identity of sin (A - B),

      3. sin 72° = sin (60° + 12°),

      4. the identity of sin (A + B).
