    I recall there was an old thread here regarding how the L1R5/L1R4 is computed.
    This is the updated information I got from the JAE Booklet 2010 and the SEAB Hotline.

    Hope this helps those of us taking O' Levels this year.

    L1R5: For Admission into Junior Colleges
    L1 : First Language – English/Higher Mother Tongue
     
    R5 :
    Relevant Subject 1 – Humanities / Higher Art / Higher Music
    Relevant Subject 2 – Mathematics / Science
    Relevant Subject 3 – Humanities / Higher Art / Higher Music / Mathematics / Science
    Relevant Subject 4 – Any GCE ‘O’ level subject (except Religious Knowledge)
    Relevant Subject 5 – Any GCE ‘O’ level subject (except Religious Knowledge)

    L1R4: For Admission into Centralised Institute
    L1 : First Language – English/Higher Mother Tongue
     
    R4 :
    Relevant Subject 1 – Humanities / Higher Art / Higher Music
    Relevant Subject 2 – Humanities / Higher Art / Higher Music / Mathematics / Science
    Relevant Subject 3 – Any GCE ‘O’ level subject (except Religious Knowledge) or CCA
    Relevant Subject 4 – Any GCE ‘O’ level subject (except Religious Knowledge) or CCA

     

    As for the L1R2B2 computation, I didn't ask as I don't plan on going to a polytechnic.
