    Second part(destress)

    Why destress?

    The academic workload gets tougher as time passes.Even,a mugger like me,who enjoys studying may feel the strain of studying.It is important to balance your study/rest/friends/family time.Don't let yourself get too caught up studying especially during O levels and prelims.

    Other forms of Destressing other than the phone and computer

    Alright,you can play sports such as basketball,soccer with your friends after school,then go back to studying or vice versa.However, don't play too long and lose track of time,have a watch just in case. 

    Talk to your family members about your problems,maybe they can console you or guide you.Talking to friends may also help.

    Take a short power nap(I usually lie down on the table instead of the bed to do this,so as to not over-nap)

    Misconceptions(I will continue to add further)

    Many may say that completing TYS  for O levels is enough. That's not true.You must be exposed to questions from the prelim/MYE papers of the top-notch sec schools such as Catholic High,Fairfield Methodist,Nan Hua High.These schools have groomed their students to score 6A's and above repeatedly for the O' levels. Try to get the papers from your teachers,if that doesn't work,ask your friends.
