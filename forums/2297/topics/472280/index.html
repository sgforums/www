    Teacher of 10 years reveals reasons for leaving the profession

    Today's confession is from an outgoing teacher who reveals the reasons for finally deciding to call it quits after being in the job for a decade.

    Here is the confession in full:

    "The main reason why I left teaching after 10 years is because I felt I was merely a part time classroom teacher, but a full time administrator, event organiser and CCA teacher-in-charge.

    "For example, why do teachers need to call up event guests to ask them if they are coming for a Speech Day event?

    "Also, I left because I was sick of being part of the 'wayang' troop: Whenever a VIP drops in, the whole school has to put up a performance of having happy, enthusiastic students and teachers.

    "Thus, the VIP may thus think that all is well in schools when it may not be so."
