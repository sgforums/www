    Dear Libros545,

    I assume you're in JC now.

    It's like a formative assessment by your teachers and principal on your personal qualities and traits. It incldes academic, non-academic and a testimonial section. Don't worry about the CCA as all your CCAs will be listed down in the certificate.

    It's like a credential and will be helpful for scholarship and job applications. This certificate is part of MOE's holistics assessment report for graduates from secondary and post-secondary education.

    For more information, please refer to MOE: School Graduation Certificate.
