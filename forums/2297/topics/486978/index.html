    The results for the 2014 Primary School Leaving Examination are out on Friday, Nov 21.

     

     

    Here's how pupils can apply for secondary schools. The following information is from the Ministry of Education's website: 

    Students may apply for their secondary schools via the Secondary One-Internet System (S1-IS) from 11am on Nov 21, 2014, until 3pm on Nov 27, 2014.

     

    What is the Secondary One (S1) Posting Exercise?

    The annual S1 Posting Exercise is conducted by the Ministry of Education (MOE) to post Primary Six (P6) students from our mainstream schools who have passed their Primary School Leaving Examinations (PSLE) to a secondary school taking into consideration their PSLE results, choices of schools and available vacancies.

     

    Who can participate in the S1 Posting Exercise?

    All P6 students from our mainstream schools who pass the PSLE except those who have been successfully allocated a secondary school place via the Direct School Admissions – Secondary (DSA-Sec) Exercise.

    Successful DSA students who are eligible to opt for Third Language and/or Course Option can do so using the S1 Internet System (S1-IS).

     

    When is the S1 Posting Exercise conducted?

    S1 Posting Exercise is typically conducted in three phases, starting from the 3rd/4th week of November after the release of the PSLE results and ending with the release of the posting results in the 3rd/4th week of December. You may refer to National Examination Timetable for more information.

    S1 Option Phase – This phase is for parents to submit their child’s S1 options and/or secondary school choices after the release of the PSLE results.

    School Posting Phase – This phase is for the posting of students to secondary schools taking into consideration their PSLE results, choices of schools and available vacancies.

    Posting Results Release – This phase is for the release of the school posting results. Students are to report to their posted secondary schools on the next working day after the release of the posting results.

     

    How to apply for the S1 Posting Exercise?

    P6 students from our mainstream schools who pass the PSLE will receive a personalised S1 Option Form together with the PSLE results. Parents can use the Personal Identification Number (PIN) provided in the S1 Option Form to submit their child’s S1 options and/or secondary school choices online via the S1 Internet System (S1-IS) during the S1 Option Phase.

    For parents who do not wish to opt online, they may submit their secondary school choices at the child’s primary school during the S1 Option Phase.

    SINGAPORE: The Secondary One (S1) posting results will be released next Friday (Dec 19) at 9am, the Ministry of Education (MOE) said.

    The posting results can be accessed through:

    • Short Messaging System (SMS) text messaging, if parents have provided a local mobile phone number during the submission of their child’s school choices; 
    • S1 Internet System at www.moe.gov.sg/education/admissions/secondaryone-posting/; or
    • Going to the student’s primary school

    Students are to report to the secondary schools that they have been posted to on Dec 22 at 8.30am.

    For enquiries, parents can call the MOE customer service centre at 6872-2220 during office hours.
