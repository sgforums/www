    Depends on your Math skill. I myself was a Sec 1 Express -> Sec 2 NA -> Sec 3 Express student. Graduate in 2008 and now I am in Poly Year 2 (IT).

     

    I did have my A Maths but even my E Math near pass (That means fail, dude). My E Math on Sec 2 not hum tumph one ho, got some decent grade like > 70 but average in algebra, within top 10 ~ 15 on my class (My Sec 2 NA got a lot of Maths pro). Main thing is, if you take A.Maths, then you must prepare yourself good in Algebra for things like differentiation, Permutation & Combination etc etc. (Look, if you are poor in Algebra, then E.Maths can let you suffer some, and A.Maths will kill you.)

     

    Sure, I have taken a hard route to myself and that is it, but, you need to know A.Maths requires Algebra A LOT. And if you do not intend to take Engineering course in poly, then the use of A.Maths is limited. (I took an IT course, but if got A.Maths then got big advantage since in the 3 yr time, there will be around 1 maths module, excluding special enrichment like Diploma Plus.)

     

    PS: Unless you show true interest on humanities (History), I do not see the point getting Pure History since combine humans allows you to score very well in most cases.
