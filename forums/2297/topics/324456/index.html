    b)

    Gradient of QR = -1/3

    Using coords of Q, eqn of QR is
    y - 4 = -1/3 (x - 3)
    y = (-1/3)x + 5

    a)

    since the lines

    y = 3x + 7 and y = (-1/3)x + 5

    intersect at R, use simultaneous eqn to get the coords of R

    3x + 7 = (-1/3)x + 5
    (10/3)x = -2
    x = -3/5

    Sub x = -3/5 into any of the 2 eqn

    y = 3(-3/5) + 7
    y = 26/5

    so R = ( -3/5 , 26/5 )
