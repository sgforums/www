    Melting point will be higher if the forces of attraction between constituent particles are stronger. In case of NaBr there are ions which are strongly bound by electrostatic forces of attraction. However AlBr3 is covalent with discrete molecules between which there are weak van der Waals attractions. Ref: http://www.adichemistry.com/general/chemicalbond/ionicbond/ionic-bond.html
