    Hi

    For your first question, I think there is an error, for Consumer B, the number should read as 1 instead of 0 for Day 2.

    Let me try to answer your question.

    Consumer A is a plant eater and Consumer B is a meat eater. Producer X is a plant (leaves).

    B feeds on A

    A feeds on X

    In Day 1, there are two plants, one Consumer A and one Consumer B. ------ (2,1,1)

    During Day 2, Consumer A ate one plant. It was then eaten by Consumer B, the meat eater. ------- (1,0,1)

    During Day 3, Consumer B starved to death because there was no food left (no Consumer A left). ------ (1,0, dead)

    Summarise your answer based on the above thoughts and you should be on track.

    Hi

    In the presence of sunlight, the plant takes in carbon dioxide and releases oxygen through photosynthesis. Therefore, in box A, there will be more oxygen than carbon dioxide.

    In box B, the animal takes in oxygen and releases carbon dioxide through respiration. Hence, there will be more carbon dioxide than oxygen.

    As carbon dioxide is a greenhouse gas, it’ll cause the temperature to rise higher. Therefore, the animal is in Box B and the plant is at box A.
