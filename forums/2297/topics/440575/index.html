    Go to JC and try to work your way in from there instead. O-lvl grades are often, but NOT ALWAYS an indicative of A-lvl grades. 

    Psychology is not a course which requires straight As to enter, as many has misunderstood. In fact, you will only need straight Bs to enter it, though having a few As will help a lot.

    A lot of the private diplomas in Singapore are not recognised, and will require you to go either study overseas or study in a private university in Singapore. If that is the case, you may as well go overseas to study psychology in a prestige (or at least recognised) university.
