    Hi,

      Nearer the exams, students should

      - REVISIT old problems they have encountered and solved previously,

      - RECOLLECT what they did or made mistakes in, and

      - REINFORCE concepts and problem-solving strategies.

      Students should NOT do any more new, obscure, difficult questions that may erode their level of confidence.

      Tutors need not do everything that tutees ask them to; instead tutors can help tutees select appropriate questions suited to the ability of the tutee. Thanks.

    Cheers,
    Wen Shih
