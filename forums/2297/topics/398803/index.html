    There are certainly other cations the cyano ligands (ie. the -CN ion, with the negative formal charge on the C atom which is hence the dative bond donor) can complex with, but the ones listed in your SPA manual are just about the ones you need to know for your SPA. You're not required to memorize these colours (relevant data will always be provided), so don't worry too much about it.
