    1 Child Ticket: $4

    1 Adult Ticket: $5

    40% Adult Tickets, 60% Children Tickets

    For every 4 adults, there are 6 children.

    Price of 4 Adult Tickets + 6 Children Tickets = $20+$24

                                                                           = $44 (1 group)

    Number of groups: $5940÷$44=135

    1 group has 4 adults.

    135 groups have 540 adults.

    540 adult tickets cost $2700.
