    Hi, as to which CCA they are desperately trying to recruit, I cannot give you an answer simply becos I do not have knowledge of all the CCAs.

    Anyway, your chance of successfully appealing into AC is much higher if u appeal via Rugby, provided you are quite skillful in it such that you are better than the other ppl who tried to appeal via Rugby too. But u are forced to join Rugby as ur CCA. For drama, primary sch experiences are not very convincing, so u might have prob appealing via it.

    That being said, the best u can do is to get a senior in the drama CCA and ask for arrangement to "audition/try out", and get the teacher's recommendation if they want u. Most ppl who successfully appealed in have the the teacher's recommendation (the teacher will fill in a form to the sch). Some ppl even started joining the sch for CCA trainings in December holiday, just so they could get a "secure" place in the sch via appeal should they failed to meet the COP. But then again, you have to be very skillful in the CCA such that the CCA wants you. Even then, there is limited slots as to how many ppl each CCA can accept ppl who appeal, so the CCA might want you, but cant accept u cos theres no slot. 

    I know of a guy in a sports CCA who joined the AC for trainings in December (he's very skillful), and are almost guranteed the appeal slot regardless of whether meeting the COP or not, as the teacher-in-charge will do the recommendation to the sch and accept him (becos they want talent and win the A Div). But ultimately he scored more than 20 in his results and cant go JC LOL.

    Some sports CCA in AC are trying to lure ppl in some reputable secondary sch (like B Div champion) by asking those who are interested to appeal to go down to the trainings and see their skills. Then, the teacher will do the job for u if they want u, as long as ur O lvl score is below 20.

    Ok anyway, what Im trying to bring across is, to get the teacher's recommendation. Else if u just submit the appeal form after JAE results are released, chances are u will fail. Becos u did not meet the COP. Mostly they will only consider the appeal form after JAE if u meet the COP, and have good CCA record. But u did not meet COP, and ur drama portfolio is not good. Your highest chance is to get a drama senior number (preferably someone u know or of high position such as President or in EXCO) and ask to go down to the sch and joins their CCA trainings. Dont wait till JAE results are release. U can do so now. Then if they decide they want u, the teacher will fill in a recommendation form to the sch to accept u. But then again, u have to be very good in the CCA lah. Most ppl who successfully appealed into the sch via their CCAs of choice are very skillful in their CCAs, so the sch wants them so as to achieve good A Div results (and to prevent other schs from getting the talent LOL). As to how you are going to find the seniors number, u have to figure it out on ur own...
