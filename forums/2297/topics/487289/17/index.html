    Q1. No need. You can retake O level on your own without going to a private school.

    Q2. If you think you can juggle both Poly studies and private O levels, then give it a shot. You cannot combine certs (when calculating L1R5), you need to retake all subjects at 1 sitting. Not sure about DAE/JAE/etc, I specialize in A levels, not O levels.

    Q3. Yes, totally can. Of course, tuition always helps, if you can afford it.

    Q4. See https://www.seab.gov.sg/pages/nationalExaminations/GOL/Private_Candidates/registration.asp

    Q5. No documents needed. Registration is online, fees payment is via SAM machine.
