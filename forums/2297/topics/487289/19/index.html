    I am sorry for commenting without reading up on DAE. I myself switched course after my first year in tertiary level, via applying through JAE for the second time. However, that was from A lvl track to Diploma track. I was not aware that the procedure for Diploma-to-Diploma switch is not the same as A lvl-to-Diploma switch.

    I understand the concern here. I think now the only way is to, really, know what you really want to pursue, and appeal for it. Write in to the course managers, or even higher, to the director of the school, or even higher, to the principal of the polytechnic. You may want to write using pen and paper. It is always more sincere to write letter using pen and paper, instead of email.

    At the worse case scenario, you may want to opt for the Private Education track. It is (slightly) more expensive than the Polytechnic Diploma track.
