    Appeals have a small chance of success *only* if your L1R5 (or for MI, L1R4) score is zhun zhun exactly on the dot for that JC's cut off point, in your case, you need exactly 20 to have a chance of successfully appealing in. MOE won't allow MI to accept anyone whose points do not meet the strict cut-off criteria. You can still try, but MOE policy is MOE policy, so be mentally prepared.

    Moving forward, you have to seriously start considering private candidacy (O levels and/or A levels), and/or Poly courses. Every year, some students attempt to juggle both simultaneously (O or A level private candidacy, while doing a Poly course), with varying degrees of success.
