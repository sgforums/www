    Okay, figured out myself what you are typing.

    5/(a^2-1) - 7/(2a+2) = 3/(1-a)

    5/((a+1)(a-1)) - 7/(2(a+1)) = -3/(a-1)

    10/(2(a+1)(a-1)) - 7(a-1)/(2(a+1)(a-1)) = -6(a+1)/(2(a+1)(a-1))

    .= > 10 - 7(a-1) + 6(a+1) = 0

    10 - 7a + 7 + 6a + 6 = 0

    23 - a = 0

    a = 23
