    1.

    Original amount of red beads

    = [ 496 / 62% ] X 38% = 304 red beads

    Current amount of red beads

    = [ 496 / 16% ] X 84% = 2604 red beads

    Amount of red beads she bought

    = 2604 - 304 = 2300 red beads

    2.

    20% of local stamps + 50% of foreign stamps = 172 stamps

    40% of local stamps + 100% of foreign stamps = 172 X 2 = 344 stamps

    100% of local stamps + 100% of foreign stamps = 560 stamps

    => 60% of local stamps = 560 - 344 = 216 stamps

    (a)

    Amount of local stamps he has at first

    = [ 216 / 60% ] X 100% = 360 stamps

    (b)

    100% of foreign stamps = 560 - 360 = 200 stamps

    Amount of foreign stamps given away

    = 50% of all foreign stamps

    = 50% X 200 = 100 stamps
