    Practise some on your own first. Commas and full stops are not included in the word restriction.

    My old, outdated way of writing.

    Highlight all the important points (at least 15) and process the points in my mind to form a nice flowing paragraph. I will then use conjunctions and connective words to join the points together to form a sentence. Double check the grammer. re-read the summary again and make sure the teacher can understand and able to see the points without frowning.
