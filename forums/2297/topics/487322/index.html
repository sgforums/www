    Singapore has continually been seeing a growth in foreigner numbers, with a rise of about 7% per year most recently in both 2011 and 2012. The gov also issues Employment Passes, the various categories being P/Q/R/S-Passes. You can find more info here: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Immigration_to_Singapore#Policy (yay wikipedia!)

    We have also been very welcoming towards foreign corporations. Just look around shopping malls and you can see all the foreign brands: LV, H&M, Uniqlo, even McDonald's. Some companies even set up factories or plants here, like Seagate and Shell. These are only a few examples I can name off the top of my head.
