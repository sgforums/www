    Hello hellomeyou,

    I am a JC1 student who has just started my new school life for 3 weeks and I can tell you some stuff regarding the sciences.

    Firstly, I am sad to say that you cannot take H2 bio because the pre-requisite states that you need to have O lvl knowledge, whether as a pure or combined subject. In fact, H2 bio, chem and physics all require O lvl knowledge, which means you can only settle for chem and physics.

    Secondly, you must ask yourself whether you wish to have a science career (engineering,chemistry,medicine,dentistry) in the future. If you do, then you MUST take H2 chem and H2 physics. True that you will be slightly disadvantaged since you are from combined science, but it doesn't matter because if you have the passion and drive, nothing can stop you. However, you must check which JCs allow combined science students to take 2 H2 sciences. Just to add on, H1 science is useless for admission into a science course in uni.

    Lastly, all the best for your O lvls!!! Do not be overly worried about this now since you still got 2 months to think about it. But nonetheless, it's good that you are thinking ahead :D
