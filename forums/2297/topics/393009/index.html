    CCC/E, A for GP and PW = 70 points

    BBB/C, E for GP and PW = 70 points

    University score is calculated on 3 H2, 1 H1, GP and PW.

    Please do not be too worry even if your 3H2 are not very good as you can make

    up with the good grades in your GP, H1 and PW.

    For example, if you get AEE for your 3 H2 but you get A for GP, H1 and PW, your

    university score is still 70.

    Similarly, if you get CDD for your 3 H2 but you get A for GP, H1 and PW, your university score is still 70.
