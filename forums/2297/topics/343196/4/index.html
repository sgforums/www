    PAPER 1 (2½ hours) [carries 40% of the total marks for the subject]

    Part A carries 30% of the total marks for the paper
    Ten questions requiring short answers will be set based on a wide knowledge of
    materials, processes, tools, equipment terminology, graphic representation and
    interpretation. Developing technologies shall be excluded.
    Part B carries 70% of the total marks for the paper
    Part B of the paper will contain three sections of subject matter as follows:
    Section 1 – Tools and Materials
    Section 2 – Processes
    Section 3 – Developing Technologies
    Candidates will be expected to answer one question from each of the Sections
    plus one other from any of the three Sections.
    Candidates are free to make use of colour and other media for the
    communication of ideas in their answers to all questions.
    PAPER 2, DESIGN PROJECT [60% of the total marks for the subject]
    Completed over a period of two semesters
    Project themes will be notified to schools in January for examinations taken in
    November. The Project will be a personally identified design opportunity within the
    thematic topic set. The project will comprise two interrelated components:
    Part A The Design Folio
    Part B The Artefact
