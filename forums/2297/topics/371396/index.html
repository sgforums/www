    Hi,

      Let x be the price of tea and y be the consumption.

      Then the amount spent on tea is given by xy.

      Since the price of tea has increased by 25%, the new price of tea is 5/4 x.

      For the amount spent to remain at xy, we will then need the consumption to drop to 4/5 y.

      Thus, the consumption has to be decreased by 20%.
