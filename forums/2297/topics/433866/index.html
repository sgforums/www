    Elaboration is the giving the details or examples about the point you have given, can be found in the textbook. Linking statements is to use these details or sub-points to explain and describe the process that lead to the outcome in the question, in short, to answer the question.

    e.g. Competition from new trade rivals was the most serious reason for the fall of Venetian trade.

    Point 1 - Over indulgence in affluent lifestyles
    Elaboration - Nobles are addicted to gambling and lost their fortunes, indulged in parties and celebrations that lasted 6 months. etc
    Link - (Explain how these activities lead to the fall of Venetian trade) As the nobles indulged in an extreme affluent lifestyle, they spend less effort in their own work and in coming up with new strategies or ideas to improve their trading industry in order to stay ahead. Therefore, Venice began to lose a competitive edge over their rivals and this led to the fall of Venetian trade.
