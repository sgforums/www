    Originally posted by OXF0RD:

    FORGOT,all my science subjects are PURE! THANKS.


    If you want to keep the possibility of having a Science-based career / profession available (eg. Medicine, Engineering, etc), you're advised to take H2 Chemistry and H2 Physics (both!) in the JC.

    Very very few JCs allow you to take triple Science at H2 level, but if your JC does (usually only top JCs allow this, and then only if your O level grades are stellar), then you should go for it, assuming you're very keen in a career / profession based on the Sciences.
