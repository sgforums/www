    Usually, when school reopen, that's the time the English teacher will start drowning you with Situational writing resources, model articles, tips, and the marking scheme. Â 

    18/30 is consider average. You may have left out some important points in your writings, structure of the essay is not clear, and average is the choice of vocab you use, and a few grammar errors.

    A strong writing is a one with very clear structure, able to communicate information in writing fluently, and ability to use vocab words, with little or no major grammatical errors.

    I find it a good way to practice writing situation essay by "pretending to be eye witness".
