    TS says that question 2 is to be solved by integration by substitution.

    This question is a bit tricky.

    Do not be surprised that after du/dx, replace dx by [(Sqrt(4e^x+1)du]/(2e^x) , simplify to get e^x/2 du.

    Do another substitution ie e^x/2= (1/8)/(u^2 - 1)

    Integrate with respect to u

    Substitute back to get the final answer ie (1/12)[sqrt(4e^x+1)](2e^x-1)
