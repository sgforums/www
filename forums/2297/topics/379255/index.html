    A househould uses approximately 37.9726 m3 of water in a particular month. If the national average of water usage per month for a typical 5-rom HDB flat in Singapore is 19.1m3, how many percent above average is the water usage for this household?

    Here's my working

    37.9726 - 19.1 = 18.8726 m3

    %tage above average = (18.8726/19.1) X 100

                                          =98.809 %

                                         = 98.8%

    49.7% is the answer given. but i still think my method is correct. I mean, if it's 100% increase above average, the amount of water used would be about 38.2 m3, which is somewhere near 37.9726m3
