    Hi,

      First 2 results are double-angle identities, covered in Additional Maths.

      As for the 3rd result, consider RHS:

      1. numerator = 1 - cos(2u) = 1 - [ 1 - 2(sin u)^2 ]

      2. denominator = 1 + cos(2u) =1 + [2(cos u)^2 - 1 ].
