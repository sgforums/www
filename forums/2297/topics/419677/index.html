    you have to go and see for yourself how each uni awards its degrees, and determine which you prefer.

    NUS have modules which requires bidding, and you might end up taking crap modules if you don't bid enough.

    and by default, you do a normal degree course, and the top 60% of year 3 gets to stay back for honours.

    NTU goes by first come first serve when it comes to module selection.

    and it goes by a direct-honours program, where you will undertake a 4yr honours course.
