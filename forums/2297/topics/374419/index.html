    Tom saves $20 more than Ryan each month.

    At the end of a certain number of months, Tom saved $330 and Ryan saved $130

    The difference between this is $200


    If T saves $20 more than R each month,

    T will save $200 more than R in 200/20= 10mths

    330 / 10 = 33 (amount Tom saves each month)

    33+18= 51 (Tom's monthly money)

    51-20= 31 (Ryan's month money)

     

    So, Tom recieves 51 monthly and Ryan recieves 31 monthly :D
