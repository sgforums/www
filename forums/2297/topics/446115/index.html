    I suggest you seek out the schools that offer this degree first.

    1.Talk to enrollment/admission department, they will be the right one to advise you on your eligibility and if you qualify for any advance credits or subject waiver.

    2. Look at the course work required to complete this degree. Then head to the library and read up on some of the subjects you need to take in order to graduate with this degree.

    If the topics interests you, you will be more committed to accomplish this degree.

    Perhaps you can talk to career counselors on what kind of jobs you likely will land yourself in this area of training.

    Consider all your potential options, the last thing you want to do is change your mind half way through.
