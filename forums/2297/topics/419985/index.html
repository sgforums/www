    Dear DuoGreed,

     

    If your secondary school allows, you can repeat your O Level in your school. If they don't, then you'll have to go private school, higher nitec or appeal RP.

     

    If you want to defer from NS, you will need to be enrolled into a private school for a full-time o level course. Private schools like BMC, MDIS, citycollege, etc will help you with arrangements to defer NS. Private preparatory course leading up to O Level will cost around $6000 to $7000 depend on the number of subject you take. One point to note, you can't defer if you were to self-study.

    If you have an interest in a particular course in ITE, why not go to higher nitec? If you still have not decide which is best for you, then I will say go private and work hard from there again. I may not guarantee you that you can adapt to ITE culture so fast.
