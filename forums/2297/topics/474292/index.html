    ECG is a developmental process that facilitates the acquisition of attitudes, skills and knowledge to help students better understand themselves, explore viable education and career options, make informed decisions and develop plans to achieve their career aspirations.

    What is ecareers.sg?

    ecareers.sg is a web-portal designed to facilitate education and career development among students in Singapore. It is premised on the ECG Developmental Model and contains features that facilitate identity development while bringing students through the processes of awareness, exploration and planning. Some of the key features of the portal include career assessment tools, which allows students to discover more about their career profile, and a database of information on various occupations and education institutions (local and overseas), which provide students with accurate information to make more informed education and career choices.

    Access to this portal is available for students in Primary 5 and 6, all Secondary and Pre-University Students and for an additional 6 months after students have graduated from the MOE Education System.

    To find out more about the portal, please visit: ecareers.sg
