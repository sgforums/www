    Just finished 'O's on my part the previous year, as far as I know 'B2' does not exist and shall infer that you mean't 'A2'. For the New Syllabus of 'O' level English that you will be taking, it is the attempted bridge that MOE is trying to create for students to adapt better to General Paper in Junior College, so do not fear as much while you're getting 50+ because the teachers are new to it too as it was only introduced last year to replace the old syllabus.

    For Compo, read up as much as you can from other news websites on their different point of views on the issues(ISIS,Ukraine conflict,Ebola etc). Next, you may have heard this before, read up and write other people's composition. It works wonders given the short-span of time you have left. Lastly, for both Compo and Compre, just practice and practice. Theres really not much to say but to be consistent and hardworking from now till 'O's, and maybe you'll get your A.

    All the best anyways!

But for the summary when they tell you to take points from, lets say, line 15 to line 42, then make sure you get your info from there only. One foolproof way is to draw a very obvious line at line 15 and another line at line 42. liddat u confirm can see where to get ur points from.
