    a rectangular painting is to have a total area (including frame) of 1200cm^2. The painting is 30m and 20cm wide, find the width of the frame. answer is 5cm

  • FireIce
    29 Apr 13, 14:22

    let x be the width of frame

    (30+2x)(20+2x) = 1200

    600+100x+4x^2 =1200

    4x^2+100x-600=0

    x^2+25x-150=0

    (x-5)(x+30)=0

    x=5 or -30 (rej as length cannot be -ve)

    A pool 6m by 3m is to have a path surronding it. If the total area of the pool and path is to be 31m^2. Find the width of the path correct to the nearest cm (which is . of a m) (answer is 63cm)

  • FireIce
    29 Apr 13, 14:43

    let the width of the path be y

    (6+2y)(3+2y) =31
    18+18y+4y^2= 31
    4y^2+18y-13=0
    y = 0.63314 or -5.13314 (rej as length cannot be -ve)

    0.63314 m = 63cm
