    (1) logb (xy2) =m
    (2) logb (x3y)=n


    (1)    becomes logb x + 2 logb y = m
    (2)    becomes 3logb x + logb y = n

    2 * (2) – (1) : 5 logb x = 2n – m
    (3) logb x = 0.2 (2n – m) = 0.4n – 0.2m
    Thus
    (4) logb y = n – 3 logb x = 0.6m – 0.2n

    logb  y/x = logb y - logb x = 0.8m – 0.6n
    logb sqrt(xy) = 0.5 (logb x + logb y) = 0.2m + 0.1n
