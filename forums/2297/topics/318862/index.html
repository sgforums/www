    Expository essay can be used for economics. Such essays are intended to describe and explain the subject you choose. Be brief and precise about the topic that you are going to talk about in your paper. 

    1. Choose a way to disclose the information. Choose one of the methods described, for example compare and contrast, case studies etc.

    2. Analyze the researched information and summarize it into sentences that reflect your opinion. 

    3. How you present your own opinions and backing it up with the sources summarized into sentences is of main concern.

    4. Topic or key sentences and points are constructed based on evidence and research done by you. Form clear statements using simple language and  structure. Do not use redundant adjectives and adverbs.

    For the summary, write the key statement with the points you obtained from research. Whoever reads it should have the feeling that the subject is completely covered. Try to use references and quotes to cover the topic when appropriate to make your essay more interesting to the reader as economics essays can sometimes be quite dry to read at times.
