    for me, i prefer to use the wooden pencil one.
    i notice when i use the mechanical pencil compass,either lead broke or loosening of the tip occurs.

    if u wanna continue using the mechanical-pencil de, dun exert too much strength on the sharp tip.

    u can try this...when marking out arcs, draw ur arc lightly first, then go over it a few good times to make it darker.
    theres no fear of putting a tad too much pressure to ur compass, resulting in alterations of compass angles or tip loosening.

    some teachers are against this idea as it might result in 2 or more arcs being formed instead juz one accurate one. but i always like to challenge them...if ur good, ur good.
    theres no need to worry abt double arcs drawn if u hold on to ur compass well..
