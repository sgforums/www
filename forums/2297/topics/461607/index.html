    Question 19 

    (a)    Make use of the angle in a semi circle ie the midpoint of BP will be the

            centre

    (b)    It is to be noted that t = s and make use of angles in the same segment.

    (c)    At N, draw a line that is parallel to MP to meet the line AP at C

            ie NC will be s.

            At A, draw a perpendicular line to the parallel line to meet at D.

            Using similar triangles,

            TM/TB = ND/NA

            Since NC  <  ND  <  NA,

            r/p > s/u

    (d)   Since NC  <  ND  <  NA,

            s < u (shown)

    Note : The questions should have asked to show that s < u first before it asks to

               show s/u < r/p

    Question 22

    (a)      Use the AAA Rule to prove the similar triangles

    (b)      Use the ASA rule to prove that triangle PTS is congruent to RTS.

               Since the two triangles are congruent, SP = SR

    (c)       Use vectors and ratio theorem to show that RO=0.5(BS+QP)

               An easier method is to extend the line SP and BQ to meet at D to form

               triangles and use the ratio theorem to show that RO=0.5(BS+QP)
