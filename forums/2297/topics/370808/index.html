    Hi,

      1.  Press "Y=" to enter the recurrence expression.

      2.  The recurrence expression has to be rewritten as U_n = 2/9 [ U_(n - 1) + 3 ], since the calculator uses U_n as the subject on the LHS.

      3.  To type "U", press "2ND" followed by "7". To type "n", press "X, T, theta, n".

      Be sure to understand what "nMin" and "u(nMin)" in the GC mean too.
