    Wow I just did this not long ago... I'm also JC1 ^_^

    a/(1-r) = 16

    a/(1-r^2) = 64

    a/(1-r)(1+r) = 64

    16/(1+r) = 64 (sub a/(1-r)=16)

    1/(1+r) = 4

    1+r = 1/4

    r= -3/4

    a= 16[1-(-3/4)] = 28
