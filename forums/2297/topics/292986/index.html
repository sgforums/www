    for me i think chemistry was pretty manageable in the O levels
    but in the A levels, i think chemistry is much much tougher

    for most of the topics it's really memorizing (e.g. Organic Chem, Periodicity)
    but for those topics involving calculations and understanding, i suggest you read up and eliminate any doubts about them (e.g. topics like Ionic Equilibrium, Chemical Equilibrium). this way, you can answer any questions they throw at you since you understand the concepts well
    but most of all, practice and practice and practice and practice and practice...
    you get the idea
