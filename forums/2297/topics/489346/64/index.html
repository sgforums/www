    Yes, but the question states *general* decrease, so even though it increases slightly from Na to Al, but in *general*, electrical conductivity decreases from metals (LHS of periodic table) to non-metals (RHS of periodic table).

    For MCQs, choose the *best* option available, even if it's not totally correct. For this particular MCQ, the other 3 options are even more obviously wrong.
