    Cu2+ +2e- = Cu

    2 electrons (negative charges) are needed to balance the 2+ positive charge of Cu ion

    0.25Cu2+ + 0.5e- = 0.25 Cu

    0.125TiX + 0.5e- = 0.125 Ti

    TiX + 4e- = Ti

    so X = 4+
