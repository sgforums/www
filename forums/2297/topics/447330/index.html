    Another important aspect is to read this part: "investigate the role in fats in planning meals for a particular group of people".

    For me, I always make the mistake of choosing a vague/generic group, such as healthy people. This in turn leads into a very broad and incomprehensive content as there are a variety of healthy people ranging from casual gym-goers to athletes and from young adults to fit, senior citizens.

     

    Hence, it is important to really specify the target group, for example, I would pick, "Body-building athletes" to narrow down my subject focus, allowing me to speak in greater detail, bringing out a more solid content.
