    Business Admin is a very general course and is very versatile. The downside is that there are many business grads. But there are thousands of businesses in Sg.

    Logistics may be for quieter people who are meticulous and usually doing backend admin jobs. They are the steady workers who take instructions well. This is just a generalisation. My fren is in logistics and is not easy as clients want cheap service and high class service. And is subjected to global economy.

    Marketing is usually for those who are very friendly and like to meet people and do sales and maybe some PR.

    You may like to take some job questionnaire online or borrow some job survey books from library, or find out your personality type, eg. Myer Briggs Personality test.

    Pls choose your sch carefully, as some smaller sch are only out there to make money and do not really care about their students. If the principal runs out of money in your eg. 2year course, he may run away to some ulu place, for smaller sch.
