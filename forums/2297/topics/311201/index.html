    are you taking h1 art in school or private? if it is in school your teacher should be able to provide you with information and notes you need unless your teacher is anyhow one.

    i honestly didnt use my teacher's notes intensively when studying for my a'levels. i did most of my studying online and used my school notes and syllabus chart provided by moe to guide myself. the new art syllabus puts more focus on analysis and discussion rather than memorisation, so it can be good or bads news depending on what type of student you are.

    it is completely possible to do well for art theory with only a fundamental understanding of the art movement and the artist. mainly because there are just too many ways to interpret art so there isnt a right answer. if you can prove a point well you will have the marks even though in reality you dont know anything about the artist.  in a nutshell if you can turn bullshit into a credible essay, you are safe.

    oh and wikipedia is your best friend
