    Dan = 1 person
    Lian = Liking/love or whatever.
    Dan Lian. This actually means one sided love, it can be in secret or not. As long as its one party liking another without reciprocation.


    An = i dont know to explain. Think of it in english terms "kept in the dark"
    Lian - liking

    So its liking a person secretly.

    Both words can be interexchangable but changes the meaning of the sentence.

    I you, = one sided love on you
    I
    you, = i like you in secret.

    A person can dan lian and an lian at the same time.

    Dont think of An Lian = use before confession and dan lian = after confession.

    Just remember although interexchangable, they carry different meanings.

    Depends on who did the person confessed to.

    X confesses to Y that X likes Y, and Y doesn't like X, then it will be a one-sided love relationship.

    But if X confesses to another person that X likes Y, it's still liking someone secretly.

    In the last case, it's still liking someone secretly and a one-sided affair unless the other party also has a liking for him/her.
