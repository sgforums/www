  • Occam's Razor
    23 Oct 08, 10:54

    Lorms for 8m Q

    L1 (0-2 marks)

    • unbalanced
    • thin knowledge
    • very limited explanation, lacking evidence
    • inaccurate, general examples



    L2 (3-5 marks)

    • knowledge lack in depth
    • some points explained
    • incomplete evaluative statements
    • relevant examples, but lack of details


    L3 (6-8 marks)

    • good knowledge
    • most expected points explained
    • both sides of the argument are considered (balanced), with valid support
    • specific examples

  • Theguiltshow
    23 Oct 08, 11:12

    I take Core Geog too.

    Eg: "Soft engineering methods of coastal protection may be more effective than hard engineering methods of protection." Do you agree? EYA. (My school prelim question)

    Para 1: Describe at least 2 methods of soft engineering, with specific examples for both. State benefits of these soft engineering methods. (I actually named them all: beach nourishment, relocation of property, planting of mangroves and stabilising dunes)

    Para 2: Give problems associated with these soft engineering methods, with specific examples.

    Para 3: Describe at least 2 methods of hard engineering, with specific examples. State benefits of these hard engineering methods. (Again, I named all: groynes, gabions, breakwaters, seawalls)

    Para 4: Negative impact of these hard engineering methods.

    No need for conclusion. My teacher says you do not need to assess which is more effective, as long as you give both pros and cons for both, and specific examples in each paragraph. Then 8 marks :-)

    Hope that helps! I always get 7/8 marks for LORMS, btw ;-)
