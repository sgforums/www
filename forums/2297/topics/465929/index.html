    For students who wish to take up tuition (not all students benefit from tuition, it depends on what type of student you are, you know yourself best), better to start earlier than later.

    Every year, many J1 students look for tuition only after they've failed their Mid Yrs, in which case, it's an uphill struggle as there's hardly enough time to catch up to pass the Promos (bearing in mind you have at least 3 or 4 H2 subjects to juggle).

    As everyone is a unique individual; for student A, tutor A may be more suitable; for student B, tutor B may be more suitable. Some students don't benefit from tuition at all, others benefit tremendously. You know yourself best.
