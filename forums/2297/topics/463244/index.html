    Age is no issue. You can take the O or A levels even when you're 30 or 40 years old, let alone when you're only 21 years young.

    As for ITE vs private O levels, you can do both simultaneously, if you're willing to work extra hard. If your private O level results turn out good, you can immediately leave ITE and go on to a poly. If not, complete your ITE studies then continue onto a poly after that.
