    It shouldn't be a problem enrolling with your diploma. I was from lasalle doing diploma and I had schoolmates in degree program that enrolled into classical piano with a real estate diploma from Ngee Ann Poly. While some others got in with a barely passed A levels results. You just have to perform well during the interview and you should be fine.As for me I got into the course despite needing 1 more C6 for o levels. It's an arts school, not a business IT school with arts 

    However you might have to start off with an additional foundation year (1st year) if you do get into a lasalle degree program while those students with arts diploma can jump to the 2nd year of the degree program. But there's still a chance to jump to the 2nd year if you do very well for the interview and your portfolio is exceeding impressive.
