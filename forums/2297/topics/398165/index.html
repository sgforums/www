    For question 1), 

    80% of the remaining families choose Taiwan means that 20% of the remaining families choose Myanmar. 

    20% of the remaining families = 1/10 of all families

    100% of the remaining families = 1/10 X 5 = 1/2 of all families

    Thus, the 90 families who chose Hong Kong = 1 - 1/2 = 1/2 of all families

    Hence, the total amount of families at the fair will be 90 X 2 = 180 families.

    For question 2),

    2/9 of A is 5/7 of B.

    We will try to make the numerator same. Thus, we have:

    10/45 of A is 10/14 of B.

    Hence, the ratio of B to A is 14 : 45.
