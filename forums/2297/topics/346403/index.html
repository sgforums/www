    H2 and H1 Economics content have been reduced.

    The emphasis is more on applications and recent economic issues and the policies

    to tackle these issues and the strength and weakness of these policies.

    The examiners are not so concerned about the academic part of economics now.

    H2 students are not required to know the process of credit creation, the mechanics

    of the theory of comparative advantage, the theory of interest rate determination,

    the construction of a conumer price index and others.

    Yes, H3 econ is more cheem but this also makes it more fun.

    H3 econ covers the asymmetric info, hidden actions and characteristics, imperfect

    info, game theory with nash equilibrium, competitive, collusive and entry deterrence

    of the firm, more updated internatiomal trade theories Heckscher Ohlin theory,

    factor price equalisation theory, intra industry vs inter industry trade theories and

    discusses more detailed international trade issues and others.

    Syllabus of H3 econ covers many parts of the intermediate level of microeconomics

    and undergraduate level of international economics.

    Reading newspaper would be great as you can quote examples in your essays and case studies.

    And...length is not required for H2 Econ essays because we go for quality instead of quantity. For my A-levels, i wrote one page for each essay parts and i scored an A for my H2 Econs!

    Always write evaluative sentences and sentences that will satisfy the L3 criteria.

    Always seek help from tutors and go for consultation weekly (start at least 2-3 months before your As if your concept is clear enough. If not, start NOW!). Write an essay once every 2 weeks and submit to your tutor for him/her to go through with you. get him/her to write comments so you can improve in your essay writing skills. It's okay for you to keep practising on ONE essay question. Just try to aim 20/25 for each question before you go on to the next. =) it works. trust me.
