    There are two different ways for the houses to be placed



    Taking the difference of the ground distance,
    h / tan 25° - h / tan 58° = 40
    h (1 / tan 25° - 1 / tan 58°) = 40
    1.5196 h = 40
    h = 26.3 m



    Taking the summation of the ground distance,
    h / tan 25° + h / tan 58° = 40
    h (1 / tan 25° + 1 / tan 58°) = 40
    2.769 h = 40
    h = 14.4 m
