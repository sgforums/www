    Compound Interest:

    Principal x (1 + r)^n

    r = interest rate

    n = year

     

    You got your simple interest and compound interest mixed up, so you ended up combining both.

     

    Simple Interest:

    Principal x (1 + n(r))

    r = interest rate

    n = year (ie if your interest rates is per year)

    1 + 20r + 190r^2 + 1140r^3 + 4845r^4 -151r - 1 = 0
