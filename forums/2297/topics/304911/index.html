    Condition: at least one of them is lying.

    Case 1:
    both are lying, then black hair=girl, red hair = boy.

    Case 2:
    suppose first one is lying, black hair=girl. Since there must be one boy and one girl, the red hair = boy

    Case 3: Suppose the second one is lying, then red hair = boy. Since there must be one boy and one girl, the black hair = girl

    All 3 cases hold the same conclusion.
    (1) and (2) are satisfied.
    Answer: X
