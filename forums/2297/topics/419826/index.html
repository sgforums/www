    O Level Grading
    1-9

    7 - 9 = FAIL
    5 - 6 = PASS
    3 - 4 = OK
    1- 2 = VERY GOOD

    A1 - 75 - 100
    A2 - 70 - 74
    B3 - 65 - 69
    B4 - 60 - 64
    C5 - 55 - 59
    C6 - 50 - 54
    D7 - 45 - 49
    E8 - 40 - 44
    F9 - 0 - 39

    JC = L1R5 = 6 subjects including English and Maths (Below 20)
    Poly = L1R4 = 5 subjects including English and Maths (Below 18) (19 -  26 - lousier courses)

    D7 for English = NO JC (Selective courses in Poly)
    D7 for Maths = NO JC / NO POLY

    E8 for English / Maths = Retain. ITE also dun wan you.
