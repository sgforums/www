    Hello a1111967,

    Initially, Corraine, Alwyn, Keith and Zavier have 1026 Marbles in total.

    Since the question involves some transaction of marbles, i shall use the information given.

    If Corraine gave away 60 marbles, she would have 1/4 or what Alwyn has, thus, Corraine - 60 = 1/4 of what Alwyn has, which means 1 Unit for corraine, 4 units for Alwyn.
    Note that the "60" in the model denotes what has been given away by corraine

    If keith bought 150 marbles, he would have 1/2 of what Alwyn has, thus, Keith + 150 = 1/2 of what Alwyn has, which means 2 Units for Keith, 4 units for Alwyn(This is because from the previous step, Alwyn has 4 units, and now keith has half of what alwyn has).
    Note that the "150" in the model denotes the amount of marbles bought by keith

    Now if zavier doubles what he has, he would have the same number of marbles as Alwyn. From this statement, we can say that if he DID NOT double his quantity of marbles, he would have half of what alwyn has. Therefore, Zavier has 2 Units -- 1/2 of what alwyn has

    Given all these information, we can see that:

    1026 - 60 + 150 = Total of 9 Units

    the - 60 is due of corraine giving away 60 marbles, and +150 comes frome keith buying an additional 150 marbles.

    9 units comes from the model drawing (after the giving away and addition of marbles)

    1026 - 60 + 150 = 9 U
    9U = 1116
    1U = 124

    From the model, we can see that Corraine originally has 1Unit + 60 Marbles.

    Thus, No. of marbles Corraine have = 124 + 60 = 184 Marbles
