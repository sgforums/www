    Hi Lohhwee,

      Regarding the topic of P&C, always ask:

      1. Is the question about combination (just choosing, order is unimportant) or permutation (choosing, then ordering)?

      2. Which principle(s) should be used - addition principle, multiplication principle, mutual exclusion principle?

      It is really that direct :)

      You may read my examples on P&C at:

      http://wenshih.wordpress.com/articles-about-learning-maths
