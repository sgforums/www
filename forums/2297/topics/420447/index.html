    Hi,

      Do not get bogged down by names. Rely on logic, since maths is all about using that.

      Look at 4ab - 8b:

      1. Rewriting, we have (4b)(a) - 2(4b), so 4b is common in both terms.

      2. Then factor out 4b, that is, (4b)(a - 2).

      Finally, we simplify, that is, (1/4)(4b)(a - 2) = b(a - 2).
