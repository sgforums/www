    Can someone help me solve this question?

    Mary takes 6 days to sew a blanket. Jane takes 10 days to sew a similar blanket. How long would they take if they are to sew a blanket together? Express your answer in a fraction in its simplest form.

  • dkcx
    23 Aug 09, 16:03

    Mary sews 1/6 of the blanket in 1 day
    Jane sews 1/10 of the blanket in 1 day
    Together, they sew 1/6 + 1/10 = 8/30 in 1 day.
    1 / 8/30 = 3 3/4 days
