    POA can only be counted as a second relevant subject in ELR2B2-A and ELR2B2-B.

    There are ELR2B2-A, ELR2B2-B, ELR2B2-C and ELR2B2-D criteria used to calculate the cut off points for the different courses in the polytechnics.

    Go to the website of your preferred polytechnic and course to check which criteria is used to calculate the cut off points for the course.

    Alternatively, please refer to JAE 2013 for the details at 

    http://www.moe.gov.sg/education/admissions/jae/files/jae-info.pdf
