    Lifts wise, only 2 lifts to service the entire 2 classroom blocks (one is 7 storey while the other is 5 storey). In between lessons, the lifts are overcrowded (yea some girls taken lift from level 1 to level 2 -_-'). Walking the stairs is actually faster (and helps increase fitness too). Sometimes, it's better to take the lift down in order to go up. Yea it's that bad.

    But other than that the facilities are quite new. In my first year they had no aircon but now every classroom is air-conditioned (although some teachers and students complain that anything above 16 deg C is hot -.-). Oh yea, the handicap toilets are always occupied for some reason. Most of the time it's girls but it's an open secret that some couples have sex in the handicap toilets.
