    Originally posted by MyNameisNas:

    What are the criteria to achieve that maximum point?

    LEAPS I think. 

    L- Leadership

    E - Enrichment

    A - Achievement

    P - Participation

    S - Service

    In your CCA, if you're the chairperson of it, then you'll be awarded CCA points. Enrichment could mean if you're from performing arts and you participated in a concert. Achievement for performing arts SYF, for sports Nationals etc. Participation, you have to meet certain attendance requirements. And lastly, for service, your CCA should be involved in some CIP and from there, points will be awarded.

    21-24 points is A2. Above 25 is A1. Not too sure about the rest. 

    You have to obtain A1/A2 to receive that maximum bonus of 2 points. :) Hope this answers your question.
