    Q1    have your learned simultaneous equations?

       let x be the number of pens.

       let y be the number of erasers.

      x-y=520

      1/3 x=3/4y

    solve these two equations simultaneously.

    x=520+y

     then 1/3(520+y)=3/4y

    hence y=416

         x=936

    936/16=58.5

       therefore he needs 59 boxes.
