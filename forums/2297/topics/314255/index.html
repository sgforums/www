    a)
    angle abc = pi - 1.38 rad

    using sine rule,

    AB/sin0.7 = 10/sin(pi-1.38)
    AB = 6.56cm

     

    b) angle oab = pi - (0.7) - (pi-1.38) = 0.68 rad
    area of shaded area = area of sector - area of unshaded triangle

    = 1/2 (10^2) (0.7) - 1/2 (10)(6.56)sin(0.68)
    = 35 - 20.6244
    =14.4 cm^2
