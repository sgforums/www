  • FireIce
    17 May 11, 19:57

    it does not work this way

    let's say this JC the cut off point is 18, exclusive of bonus pts
    it offers 1000 places for this year

    900 students with 18 pts and below applied and got in. they do not need to use any CCA pts or other bonus pts

    the school will then consider the 19-pointers
    if u have 19 pts and A in CCA, u will be considered first
    after all the 19-pointers (with or with bonus pts) took up the seats
    there might be 10 left, which then the sch will consider those with raw score 20 pts
    again, those with 20 pts score A for CCA will be considered first, compared to those 20 pts but with B in CCA, and so on... until the quota filled

    it does not mean if u get 22 pts but A in CCA means u are eligible to fight with the raw 20-pointers
