    Buy the thick chinese compo books, categorize them into themes e.g. family, technology, environment etc. Highlight the useful phrases and memorize them!!!

    Ask your chinese teachers for notes and consult them when u hv any qns. When they see that ur putting in effort they will definately go the extra mile to help you. This applies for all subjects.

    Also buy and do your tys over and over cuz u know what kinds of qns will b asked n how to ans them to get the marks. 

    As fr reading n writing maybe u can consider borrowing pri sch chinese textbooks to practice. My friend did that diligently and his chinese improved by leaps and bounds. When it comes to language consistency is the key so try n integrate chinese into ur life whenever possible!

    Oh towards my chi Os my lao shi conducted daily oral practices before assembly so maybe u can go w a grp of friends to meet up w yr teacher. That helped alot as well.

    Hope this helps n all da best fr yr Os!!!
