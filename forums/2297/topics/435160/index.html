    Hi, can anybody help solve these questions without the use of algebra? My sis is in P6 and she doesn't have the knowledge of solving algebraic / simultaneous equations... Many thanks!

    1) John had 239 male and female fishes. 3/5 of the male fishes died and he bought another 14 more female fishes. As a result, the number of female fishes he had was 1/4 of the number of male fishes. How many male and female fishes were there at first?

    2) A jar contained some red and blue erasers. At first, the number of red erasers was 75% of the blue erasers. After adding in another 3 erasers of each colour, the number of red erasers becomes 80% of the number of blue erasers. How many erasers of each colour were there at first?

    3) In a classroom, when a boy stands up, the number of boys sitting becomes 4/5 of the number of girls sitting. On the other hand, when a girl stands up, the number of girls sitting becomes 8/7 of the number of boys sitting. How many boys and girls are there in the class?

    4) The ratio of $2 notes to $5 notes in James' piggy bank was 13:8 He exchanged 20 pieces of $2 notes for some $5 notes, after which, the ratio of $2 notes to $5 notes became 4:5. What was the total value of $2 notes and $5 notes in the piggy bank at first?

    Answers to any questions will be very much greatly appreciated! =)

    Thank you very much in advance!

    Q1.

    After:

    M: U U U U (4units)

    F: U             (1unit)

    Before:

    M: U U U U U U U U U U(10units, since 4units are left after 3/5 died)

    F: U - 14female fishes

    Therefore, at first there are all together 11units - 14fishes. which is 239.

    11units should represent 239+14=253 fishes

    1unit represents 253/11=23 fishes.

    No. of Male fishes = 10units =230

    No. of Female fishes = 1units - 14 = 23 - 14 = 9 (or 239 - 230 = 9)

    Q2.

    75% is 3/4. At first,

    R: U U U      (3units)

    B: U U U U  (4units)

    So 3units + 3 is 80% of 4units +3

    (3units + 3 ) x 5 is 80% x 5 of 4units + 3

    15units + 15 is 4 times of 4units + 3

    while 4 times of (4units +3) is 16units + 12

    Since 15units + 15 and 16units + 12 are equal, 1unit = 3

    R: 3 x 3 = 9. B = 4 x 3 =12

    Q3.

    When a boy stands up, no. boys sitting : no. of boys sitting = 4 : 5

    So in this classroom, ORIGINALLY,

    no. of boys = 4units+1

    no. of girls  = 5units

    Now when a girl stands up, the no. of girls sitting becomes 5units - 1, and this is 8/7 of 4units +1

    8/7 of (4units + 1) is 32/7 units + 8/7 which is equal to 5units -1

    So 8/7 + 1 and 5units - 32/7 units are the same

    15/7 and 3/7 units are the same

    15 and 3units are the same

    1unit = 5.

    no. of boys = 4 x 5 + 1 = 21; no. of girls = 5 x 5 = 25

    Q4.

    Originally,

    no. of $2 notes = 13units

    no. of $5 notes = 8units

    After 20 pieces of $2 notes are exchanged to 8 pieces of $5 notes

    no. of $2 notes becomes 13units - 20

    no. of $5 notes become 8units + 8

    Now (13units - 20) is 4/5 of (8units + 8)

    4/5 of (8units + 8) is 32/5 units + 32/5, which is equal to 13units - 20

    Therefore 32/5 + 20 and 13units - 32/5units are equal

    132/5 and 33/5 units are equal

    132 and 33units are equal

    1unit = 132/33 = 4

    Total value = 13units of $2 + 8units of $5 = 13 x 4 x $2 + 8 x 4 x $5 = $104 + $160= $264.
