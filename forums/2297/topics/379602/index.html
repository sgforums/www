    Hi Skillzkills,

    Do have a look at this.

    http://www.np.edu.sg/ADMISSIONS/TIPS/Pages/2009JAECOP.aspx

    The cut-off point for psychology is 10 points.

    As an additional side note, these are the cut off points for 2008 for psychology in NTU and NUS.

    http://admissions.nus.edu.sg/sprogramme-igp.html

    http://www3.ntu.edu.sg/oad2/pdfs/COP.pdf

    In NUS, psychology is a major under Arts & Social Sciences, with the 10th percentile at BBC/C and GPA 3.39, and the 90th percentile at AAA/A and GPA 3.82.

    In NTU, the 10th percentile is at BBB/B and GPA 3.49, and the 90th percentile at AAA/A and GPA 3.84.

    Cheers
