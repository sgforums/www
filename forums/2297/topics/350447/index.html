    how to find the units of c in this equation given that y has no units.

    C= squareroot (y * pressure/density)

    where y and c are constants.

    c = squareroot (y * pressure/density)

    Units of LHS = Units of RHS

    RHS: 1st term unit: Pa = Nm^-2
               2nd term unit: kgm^-3 

    Since y is a constant, it has no units.

    therefore, units of RHS = squareroot (Nm/kg)
                                            = (Nmkg^-1)^(1/2)
                                            = N^(1/2)m^(1/2)kg^(-1/2)

    Units of LHS = units of RHS,
    Therefore, units of c in this equation given that y has no units = N^(1/2)m^(1/2)kg^(-1/2)
