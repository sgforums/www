    Dear NN2H2,

    When the GCE O Levels results release next year, there will be an application date for various groups of student.

     Main stream O Level students will undergo JAE, while private students and A level students will undergo DAE. The application dates are slightly different but will be around the same period of time.

    Different poly have different application dates/date line. So it is very important that you refer to the polytechnic's website for more information when you get your results.
