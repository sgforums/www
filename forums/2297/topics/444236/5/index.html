    Originally posted by lanadelrey:

    Hi guys, i need advise on the environment ,culture and teachers in MI. thanks!


    If you're 'forced' to consider MI, it already means your options are limited, and you don't have much of a choice anyway.

    You probably can't find a suitable Poly course.

    You probably can't get into any other other JC (Science).

    So if MI's your last resort, it probably means it's your best choice available.

     Alternatives (choose a random poly course, take A levels as private candidate, study a private diploma, etc) are likely to be worse off than MI.

    On the bright side, MI allows you to take H2 Sciences with just a B grade for combined science (as compared to a A grade required by all other JCs). H2 Sciences are required if you intend to study Science in the Uni.

    Fair enough.

    But when I said "forced", I didn't necessarily mean your L1R5 is not good enough for decent poly courses. Because even if your L1R5 is very good (eg. below 10), but you don't already have a poly course in mind, it means you're not suited for poly. Poly is only meant for people who have already made up their minds on what they wanna do for the rest of their lives.

    If you're undecided on any poly course (ie. the exact career or profession you want, for the rest of your life), it means that you must go JC / MI / A Level route, regardless of how good or lousy your L1R5 is.
