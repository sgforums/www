    Pros and Cons either way.

     

    From the perspective of 'A' level H2 Chemistry, best options are either :

     

    1) Drop Pure Bio, take Pure Chem and Pure Physics.

    or

    2) Take Pure Chem, and drop Bio and Physics to combined Science.

     

    Chemistry is arguably the most important Science (in terms of eligibility for University courses, eg. Medicine), closely followed by Physics, then Biology as a distant 3rd place.

    Very few University courses require H2 Biology; many engineering courses require H2 Physics; and all life-science related courses such as Medicine require H2 Chemistry.
