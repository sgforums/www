    If all of your methods are correct but your final answer is wrong,you get all the method mark but not the answer mark. If only part of your methods are correct(some methods are wrong), you get only part of the method mark which is in your case your final method is wrong, you will most probably get ECF(Error Carry Forward) which means part of method mark.
