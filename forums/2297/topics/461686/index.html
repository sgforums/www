    Preparatory course for A level for local students are just the part time or full time A level courses provided by the private commercial schools.

    These so called 10 months preparatory course for A level are actually meant for JC students who do not do well and cannot repeat in their JCs. But the private commercial schools take in O level students with results that cannot go into JCs and polytechnics and sweet talk these students that they can complete A level in 10 months.
