    Hi,

      It would be best for you to check with your child's teacher.

      MSG: Mean Subject Grade. I believe the grade for each subject is assigned, based on the 'O' level grade format of A1, A2, B3, B4, C5, C6, D7, E8, and F9.

      L1B4: Score of English and Best 4 Subjects (I think).

      Expected L1B4: Based on the PSLE aggregate (I guess), the student is being seen by the school to be competent of achieving a certain score.

      If Actual L1B4 < Expected L1B4, the student is doing better than expected. It is, therefore, a good sign.

      It would be good to check with the teacher where your child stands, as compared with the rest of the Sec 1 cohort.
