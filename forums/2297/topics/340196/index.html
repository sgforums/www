    Hi,

      Hold on to your purse, because you do not have complete information yet.

      During the first meeting with the student, ask him/her to tell you learning difficulties and show you some of the work he/she has done in school. Have him/her to show you test/exam papers so that you have a better picture of how the child is doing in school. Have a word with his/her parents to understand his/her personality too. Establish a good rapport with the student before you begin to work with him/her. Have him/her to set goals for lessons with you as you move along.

      Hope these pointers help you to kick-start :)

    Cheers,
