    This will depend on whether you intend to pursue a career in the private or public sector.

    If you want to work in the private sector, you can consider proceeding to SIM directly. When you are in SIM, work hard & try graduating with first or second upper honours. I've seen people from SIM securing good jobs in the private sector.

    If you intend to get a public sector career, it is better to retake your A'levels and enter a local university.

    As for NIE, only pursue that option if you are interested in considering teaching as a career.
