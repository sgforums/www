    dividing (1) / (2) gives you tan (x/2)

    Originally posted by Isis:
    Can (tanx/2 sinx/2 + cosx/2-1 ) = (secx/2 - 1) ?
    Let x/2 be A.

    LHS
    = tan A sin A + cos A - 1
    =/= sec A - 1
    =/= RHS
