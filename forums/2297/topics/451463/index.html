    Hi,

      This must-know question is a classic problem of recurrences. Many schools adapt the question for their tests and exams after that.

      For part (ii):

      1. Use this fact:

      as n -> infinity, x_{n + 1} and x_{n} both approach the limit L.

      2. Substitute x_{n + 1} and x_{n} with L and rewrite the equation to look like the one you solved in (i).

      3. The conclusion is then obvious.

      For part (iv):

      1. Use the consideration given.

      2. Use the given graph to make the two conclusions.

      For part (v):

      1. Note that the 1st result in part (iv) demonstrates the convergence of a decreasing sequence.

      2. Note that the 2nd result in part (iv) shows the convergence of an increasing sequence.

      3. Consider, for part (iii), whether alpha < x_1 < beta when you apply the results.
