    (1+x)(a-bx)^12 = (1+x)(a/b-x)^12 (1/b)^12

    For (a/b-x)^12,

    coefficient of x^7 = 12C7 (a/b)^5 (-1)^7

    coefficient of x^8 = 12C8 (a/b)^4 (-1)^8

     

    Therefore,

    Coefficient of x^8 = (1/b)^12 [ 12C7(a/b)^5(-1) + 12C8(a/b)^4 ] = 0

    a/b = 12C8 / 12C7 = 5/8
