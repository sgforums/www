    Soha’s hidden talent!

    This cutie is doing some good work. Yes, we may have spotted her at her 'Bond' date with boyfriend Kunal Khemu, but when we get conversing with Soha Ali Khan on her future projects, we learnt that it's ‘Dil Kabaddi’ that's next in line. We were shocked when the actor told us that she actually played a lot of kabaddi while she lived in Delhi! "I used to play kabaddi when I was young. I was in Delhi at that time. But I haven't played the sport since I've come to Mumbai," she tells us. Though we don't put it past her, it's just a little difficult to imagine this suave, royal chick go hu-tu-tu... 

    Sanjay Baba's 'lost' spirit!

    Actor Sanjay Dutt is most upset about thehappenings in Mumbai... Who isn't? But the actor, we hear, has thrown away the bottle and done away with the 'spirit'! Yes, he has said No to alcohol! Sanju enjoyed his bubbly... but it's time to do some serious work. "Baba used to drink quite a bit, but has given up alcohol for the time being," informs one of Dutt's friends. Has it anything to do with the Mumbai massacre? "Well, it's more to do with his health! He has been trying to get back into shape. And it's important that he quits drinking for best results," we're told! So Sanju has forsaken the six-packs of beer for those six-pack abs. Good for him! 

    Hema Malini's zindagi ki kahani

    Actor Hema Malini is probably shocked at her own popularity. Her PRO claims that the actress' biography has been selling like hot cakes, so much so that volumes have been sold out and a re-print order has already been given. That's not all. We're told that the biography was recently translated into Marathi also, and that too has been lapped up by Marathis. Now, we're not so sure we believe this hot news! But if it's true, evergreen actress Hema ought to be inspired enough to pen an autobiography! 

    Neil is happy and gay!

    Neil is on a career high. He is so happy and busy with his work that he hardly has time to think about anything else. The dude - who is one of the hottest heartthrobs in tinselville - has no hang-ups and is not at all bothered about his single status. Rather, he loves to talk about the many men in his life. Before you jump to any conclusions, by men here we only mean to say, his contemporaries! Being quite proud about all the new guys introduced in 2007 he says, “They all are amazing and brilliant. Ranbir has already proved himself with ‘Bachna Ae Haseeno’. Imraan is very good in ‘Jaane Tu...’ and ‘Kidnap. Sikander Kher is a talented guy. Harman is again a dark horse. He was brilliant in ‘Love Story...’ and now ‘Victory’ is up for release. All these are nice guys.”  He continues, “There’s a dearth of new talent and we all are working hard and doing well. We have been appreciated in some way or the other and we are all happy doing what we are doing. I don’t think there’s any kind of insecurity in me or Ranbir or the others.”
