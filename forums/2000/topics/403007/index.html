    Manisha Koirala wedding: Bollywood actress Manisha Koirala getting married (marriage) today on 19 june, 2010 in Kathmandu, Nepal.  41 years bollywood actress Manisha Koirala wedding bride will tie knot with businessman Samrat Dahal, 33, in a traditional ceremony that will be low key and off limits to the media.However, Reception will be a majestic event at Hotel Soaltee Crowne Plaza in Kathmandu, where few thousands are expected to grace the marriage reception including Nepal President Ram Baran Yadav, Prime Minister Madhav Kumar Nepal, Maoist chief Prachanda, etc. Manisha koirala marriage photos are here with her husband samrat.

    Manisha and Dahal have been seeing each other over the past year and their wedding date was fixed by an astrologer a few months ago.

    She was reportedly engaged to ex-Australian ambassador to Nepal Crispin Conroy in 2001, but broke up with him soon. Last year too, the grapevine was abuzz that Manisha will wed American speaker and author Christopher Dorris, but that relationship too ended.
