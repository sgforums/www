    Sanjay Dutt, Neela Azim, Soni Razdan, Sadashiv Amprapurkar, Deepak Tijori and others..

    Traumatic images of the past haunt Ravi (Sanjay Dutt) to the point of making him mad: his sister Roopa (Soni Razdan) was forced to work as a prostitute and her grandfather refused to take her back to the family home. Just before he could rescue his sister from the Hell of prostitution, she jumped out the window and died in front of his eyes.

    Meantime, he earns his living as a taxi driver in Mumbai in the hope of finding the mysterious woman, who had made his sister panic. He helped his friend Gotya (Deepak Tijori), who want to get married to the prostitute Chanda (Neelima Azim). One night, Ravi meets Pooja (Pooja Bhatt) and falls in love her. But just after, the uncle of Pooja, sold her to the brothel owner, the eunuch Maharani (Sadashiv Amrapurkar). Ravi tries everything to save her from the claws of this cruel man...
