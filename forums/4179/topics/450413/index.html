Meet the world's friendliest crocodiles

  • CityMains
    9 Apr 12, 18:55

    In the Paga Crocodile Pond in Ghana, you can meet the world's friendliest crocodiles. They even let you sit on them.

    These crocodiles here are used to coexisting with humans. As such, tourists who visit this place are amazed when they can touch, sit on and take pictures with these animals.

    The pond itself is considered sacred, and it's a taboo to harm or kill these reptiles.

    It is also believed that the soul of every native of this village has a corresponding crocodile in the pond.

    According to local folklore, the death of most of the important personalities in the town coincided with the deaths of some of the biggest crocs in the pond.
