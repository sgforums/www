Daughter gets $14m wedding -- with 6 Ferraris as dowry

  • CityMains

    A rich coal mine boss in Shanxi, China held a large-scale 70 million yuan (SGD14 million) wedding for his daughter on March 18, with six Ferraris as her dowry.

    5-star Ritz Carlton, Marriott and Hilton hotels were booked for the wedding ceremony and three airplanes were rented to transport family and friends.

    A lot of famous celebrities such as Wang Leehom, Elva Hsiao, Jay Chou and Fahrenheit were reportedly invited to perform at the wedding. The wedding concert was hosted by famous Chinese TV hosts, Zhu Jun and Zhou Tao.

    It is reported that the bridegroom's father is a property developer in Hainan, China and the bride's dowry was 6 Ferraris.

    The coal mine boss, Xing Libin is rumoured to have assets worth tens of billions.
