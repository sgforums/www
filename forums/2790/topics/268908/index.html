    FOODS**

    toriniku = chicken

    unagi = eel

    gohan = rice

    tamago = egg ( someone using this noob ID TAMAGO is a real fool) kidding

    IKURA/SHYAKE = both is known as SALMON

    hourensou = SPINACH

    koshou = pepper

    iseebi = lobster

    hotate = scallop

    katsu-don = breaded chicken/pork with rice and egg

    kuro koshou kani = black pepper crab

    onakasuita = im hungry..

    beekon = bacon

    ***to tell someone that what you are eating now, you can say as the following:

    **ima unagi bento kutteru** = im eating bento now . ima refers to im *then* followed by ur food after that ends with kutteru...
