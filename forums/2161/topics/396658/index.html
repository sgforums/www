Presenting the Singapore Experience Awards 2010

  • BanguIzai
    15 Apr 10, 22:48

     

     


    The Singapore Experience Awards 2010 is proudly organised by the Singapore Tourism Board, and celebrates the best of Singapore experiences. This prestigious award honours outstanding individuals and organisations that have created a more vibrant and exciting Singapore in the areas of Leisure, Entertainment, Attractions, Business Travel & Mice, Education and Healthcare. 

    Visit     www.singaporeexperience.com to find out more about the Singapore Experience Awards, and you can also view the winners of 2009. Nominations for this years awards begin this May 2010. 

    Contact     stb_singaporeexperience@stb.gov.sg or 1-800-4AWARDS for further information.
