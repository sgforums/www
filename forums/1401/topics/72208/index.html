    Ray "Hercules Hernandez" Fernandez passed away yesterday apparently having died in his sleep. Dewey Robertson announced Fernandez' death at last night's Hall of Fame Banquet in Buffalo.
    No cause of death has been determined at this time.

    1Wrestling.com received email early this morning from Richard Jones of thepainclinic.net telling us about Robertson's announcement at the Hall of Fame Banquet, and we have confirmed through sources close to Fernandez' family that the news is true.

    Robertson had tagged with Fernandez early in his career in the Central States area. Fernandez wrestled for several territories in the 1980's and also had a run with the WWF where he feuded with Billy Jack Haynes.

    1Wrestling.com sends condolences to the family and friends of Ray Fernandez.
