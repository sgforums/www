    WWE UNLIMITED REPORT
    by Brian Richey @ 12:06:00 AM on 11/15/2005

    WWE Unlimited Report for November 14th - RAW IS EDDIE
    WWE Unlimited could be launched around 8:30. The screen said "Coming Up, WWE
    Unlimited." The format has changed to a special Eddie Guerrero theme for the
    night. The top section, 'Eddie Facts,' gave several facts about him
    throughout the night. The first was that Eddie won the WWE Championship at
    No Way Out on Feb. 15, 2004. The large, middle section, 'Fans Remember
    Eddie,' displayed the testimonials from fans that they have sent in memory
    of Eddie. The last section, 'WWE.com,' simply said: "Watch video tributes to
    Eddie Guerrero on WWE.com after RAW."

    Around 8:42 the sound from the crowd could be heard they cheered and chanted
    'Eddie, Eddie.' It cut out about a minute later.

    At about 8:54 they showed Eddie's lowrider on display to the right of the
    stage. The superstars began making their way to the stage, beginning with a
    group including Mysterio, Chavo, JBL, Triple H, and Chris Benoit. They
    focused on Benoit who was choked up and visibly upset. HHH consoled him.
    Many of the stars were wearing Eddie shirts or wristbands. The crowd, who
    had been cheering as the superstars came out, began to chant 'Eddie, Eddie'
    and loudly continued as the rest made their way out and clapped along with
    chants.

    Kurt Angle was shown and he said Eddie was one of the most loving, caring
    individuals that he has ever met. He said that when he was on, Eddie was the
    best in the world, possibly best ever. Eddie helped teach him about the
    business and said a lot to help him professionally and personally. He called
    Eddie a saint and said that he loved him and will miss him.

    Highlights from Eddie's match against Rob Van Dam from Backlash 2002 where
    he won his second Intercontinental Championship.

    Eric Bischoff says that he last saw Eddie while they were waiting for their
    bags at an airport last Wednesday. Eddie asked about his son and Bischoff
    asked him about his family. They showed each other pictures and then hugged
    and looked forward to seeing each other this past weekend. He said he will
    remember Eddie and that his thoughts and prayers are with his family.

    William Regal, with tears in his eyes, said that he hopes and prays that
    anyone who ever spent time with Eddie knows that they were blessed, because
    he was an extraordinary person.

    Highlights of Eddie and Rey Mysterio winning the Tag Team Championship at No
    Way Out 2005 against the Bashams.

    JBL said that he and Eddie were great enemies in WWE and great friends in
    real life. He started to get choked up as he explained how no one thought he
    could be a main eventer and Eddie stood up for him. He said he owed Eddie
    his career and they sold out the Staples Center in what was probably JBL's
    biggest match. When he was going through his divorce, Eddie sensed something
    was wrong and kept bringing him passages from the bible just for him. When
    he met his current wife, he thought Eddie was just as happy for him if not
    more so. He said the world was better with Eddie in it and then said to hell
    with the world because he was a better person because of Eddie. JBL then
    closed by saying thank you to Eddie's family for letting him be a part of
    his life and he wishes them all the best.

    Matt Hardy said Eddie was truly one of a kind and that he did everything he
    could to battle his demons and won. He said Eddie was a great person and he
    will pray for him and his family.

    Trish tells us to love what you do and go for your goals because thats what
    Eddie did as she watched him growing up and throughout her career. She
    thanks him for everything he did for the business and as a friend.

    Highlights of Eddie's WWE Championship celebration in his hometown of El
    Paso.

    The match continues as the ref does the count. The two get back in before
    the 10 count and HBK chops Rey in the corner. Rey misses a splash in the
    opposite corner and HBK hits a hard reverse neckbreaker for a two count.

    Christy said that she had a lot of touching conversations with Eddie. What
    really stood out was his love for the business and everyone in it. His love
    for his family was most touching. She finished with, "We love you Eddie."

    Booker T said that he and Sharmell's hearts go out to the family and his
    daughters. As a believer in God, Booker knows that he'll see him again.
    Sharmell said that she'll miss Eddie's smile the most and Booker agreed.

    Highlights of Eddie winning the WCW Cruiserweight title at Fall Brawl 1997
    against Chris Jericho.

    Jerry Brisco said he was fortunate to know Eddie and his family from an
    early age. Eddie proved himself to be one of the top Latino wrestlers in the
    world. Eddie wasn't a giant physically, but was a giant in what he stood for
    by becoming a success for those not huge physically and for his Latin
    brothers. His sympathy goes out to Vicki and his entire family. He
    brightened the world with his presence and was a shining light for many,
    many people.

    Ric Flair said he had a very controlled thing about him and had the ability
    to look you in the eye and be sincere. When he put his fist to his chest in
    respect, you knew that was real. He says Eddie will go down as one of the
    greatest wrestlers of all time and was a great man, father, and performer.
    He said that Eddie's legacy is so great that his stature will only grow
    larger as the years go on and that his daughters will no doubt be very
    proud.

    Highlights of Los Guerreros winning the Tag Team Championship at Survivor
    Series 2002 against Edge & Rey Mysterio.

    Teddy Long said Eddie was always there to do whatever he had to do to help
    the young guys coming in and brought his character to life with his
    personality and his comedic nature that made him a joy to be around.

    Footage of the 'security camera' at Playboy headquarters when Eddie was
    trying to stop the printing of then-girlfriend Chyna's Playboy pictorial.

    Shelton Benjamin said it goes without saying that Eddie was a phenomenal
    talent and everyday strived to better himself for himself and his family. He
    and Eddie actually got into some arguments and Eddie always said that he was
    doing this for his family and not to take that away from him. He said Eddie
    was a great talent and thanked him for everything.

    Highlights of Eddie's last match on SmackDown! against Mr. Kennedy.

    After the show the message "Thank You for Watching WWE Unlimited," appeared.

    I'm sure these tribute videos will be up on WWE.com sometime Tuesday if not
    sooner. Reminder that tomorrow the MSNBC show Rita Cosby - Live and Direct
    will have a tribute to Eddie at 9pm. I only had the chance to see Eddie
    perform live once in his first match against Batista a few months back at a
    house show in Raleigh. He did not disappoint and entertained the crowd the
    way he has ever since I first saw him as a member of the Filthy Animals in
    WCW. He truly was a great talent and one the WWE and the wrestling world
    will never be quite the same without. My condolences go out to Eddie's
    family and everyone in the business and around the world who was touched by
    him and saddened by his untimely death.
