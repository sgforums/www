    Congrats to Jeff. As of December 14, 2008 Jeff Hardy (Won the WWE Championship after defeating Edge & HHH in a Triple Threat Match in Armageddon 2008)  joined the ranks as the 7th ever Grand Slam Champion.

    Grand Slam champion in WWE refer to those that have won 4Main Titles in WWE.

    1) WWF/E Championship/ World Heavy Weight Championship

    2) Intercontinental Championship

    3) WWF European Championship

    4) WWE Tag Team Championship/World Tag Team Championship

     

    Currently, The list of Grand Slam Champions are (In Sequence) HBK, HHH, Y2J, Kurt Angle, Eddie Guerrero, RVD, Jeff Hardy.

     

    As the European Championship was discontinued on July 22, 2002, the number of future potential Grand Slam Champions is limited to wrestlers who have already held the European Championship. Hopefully WWE, change this ruling.

     

    The Good news is there are 3 Wrestler who are likely to join the ranks soon. They just need 1 more tilte to complete the the Grand Slam Championship.

    • William Regal and Val Venis are required to win either the WWE Championship or the World Heavyweight Championship.
    • John "Bradshaw" Layfield is required to win the WWE Intercontinental Championship.
