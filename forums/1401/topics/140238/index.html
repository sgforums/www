    STONE COLD STEVE AUSTIN SAYS "I COULD DROP HULK HOGAN IN A SECOND"
    By: Joey Styles
    6/30/2005 4:36:30 PM

    From the UK Sun:

    For months Hulk Hogan has been telling anyone who'll listen that he wants one last wrestling match with the only superstar in history as popular as him...Stone Cold Steve Austin.

    Now, in an exclusive interview with SunSport Online, Stone Cold has dropped his biggest hint yet that the all-time dream bout WILL take place.

    Austin quit the ring in 2003, due to a series of devastating injuries, and now enjoys a non-wrestling role in the WWE. But the 40-year-old revealed he still has "two or three" matches left in him - and one of those could well be the bout every wrestling fan wants to see.

    "I'd be happy to get it on with Hulk Hogan. I've heard what he's been saying - and that match is something that could definitely happen. If they can build it right, the money is right, the event is right and if the story can be told correctly then, yeah, I will fight him. I've never wrestled the guy before and I think it would be an important match because it would be the two biggest draws in the history of the wrestling business one-on-one for the first time ever."

    "Hogan's had great longevity and I've got a lot of respect for his career," Steve admits. Sometimes he's run his mouth and I don't think too much of a lot of what he's had to say but he's a smart man and he can see that this could be a great opportunity."

    "I know I could trust him to do it right - because if not I could drop that guy in a second!"
