WWE Survivor Series 2007 Results.

    Matches

    * CM Punk defeated John Morrison and The Miz to retain the ECW Championship (7:5[24]
    -> Punk pinned Miz following a Go to Sleep.

    * Mickie James, Maria, Torrie Wilson, Michelle McCool, and Kelly Kelly defeated Beth Phoenix, Jillian Hall, Melina, Victoria, and Layla (4:40)[25]
    -> Mickie pinned Melina after a Long Kiss Goodnight.

    * Lance Cade and Trevor Murdoch defeated Hardcore Holly and Cody Rhodes to retain the World Tag Team Championship (7:1[26]
    -> Murdoch pinned Rhodes following a Sunset flip powerbomb.

    * Team Triple H (Triple H, Jeff Hardy, Rey Mysterio, and Kane) defeated Team Umaga (Umaga, Mr. Kennedy, Montel Vontavious Porter, Finlay, and Big Daddy V) (w/Matt Striker) in a (4 on 5) Survivor Series match (22:09)[27]
    -> Matt Hardy was originally supposed to compete as a member of Team Triple H, but was removed following a kayfabe knee injury.

    * The Great Khali (w/Ranjin Singh) defeated Hornswoggle via disqualification (3:15)[28]
    -> The Great Khali won when Finlay interfered and attacked Khali with a shillelagh.

    * Randy Orton defeated Shawn Michaels to retain the WWE Championship (17:4[29]
    -> If Michaels attempted to use his Sweet Chin Music, he would have been disqualified and would not receive another title shot as long as Orton is champion. If Orton attempted to get disqualified, he would have lost the title.[30]
    -> Orton pinned Michaels following an RKO.
    -> Michaels used Sweet Chin Music after the match was over.

    * Batista defeated The Undertaker in a Hell in a Cell match to retain the World Heavyweight Championship (21:25)[31]
    -> Edge, who was disguised as a cameraman at ringside, returned from injury and interfered by attacking the Undertaker with a camera, followed by pulling Batista over the Undertaker allowing him to get the pinfall.
