WWE Live returns to Singapore on 28 June

  • FireIce
    8 Mar 17, 13:06

     

    Singapore Sports Hub confirms the return of WWE (World Wrestling Entertainment) Live on its Facebook page on Wednesday (8 March), a day after the news was leaked on its website.

    WWE Live will be taking place on 28 June at the Singapore Indoor Stadium. This year’s event features wrestling superstars such as WWE RAW Women’s Champion Bayley, Kevin Owens and Sasha Banks.

    Other superstars joining them include Cesaro, Sheamus, Rusev, Braun Strowman, and the ‘face of the company’ Roman Reigns.

    Tickets are priced from $48 for CAT 6 seats to $488 for seats by the ring, and can be purchased via SportsHubTix. WWE Starhub Early Bird tickets go on sale from 14 to 16 March while general tickets go on sale from 17 March.

    WWE Live was last in Singapore on 2 July 2015, the sold-out show featured John Cena, Dolph Ziggler, New Day, Lucha Dragons, and Nikki Bella, among others.
