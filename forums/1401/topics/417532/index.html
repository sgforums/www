WWE talent vs TNA talent

  • Jason316
    11 Dec 10, 00:36

    People like to knock on WWE for always having the same top wrestlers, etc. And they say TNA is the future.

    But isn't it interesting how at the moment, TNA is focused on getting old or ex WWE wrestlers while WWE is trying to build up more and more younger wrestlers. Swagger, Sheamus and now Miz were given a run at the top. And don't forget Nexus. Yes, they're young, inexperienced and untested. But at least they're getting a shot. Plus you won't know how good they are until they get that chance, right?

    So, on this basis alone, I think WWE is doing a MUCH better job than TNA in terms of talent development.
