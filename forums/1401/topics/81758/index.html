    BOBBY BILLIARD GIVES HER SIDE OF STORY RELATED TO HER RELEASE FROM WWE DEAL; TALKS ABOUT HUMILIATION, LONG PRACTICE SESSIONS, INJURIES
    By: 1Wrestling.com
    6/6/2004 11:24:52 PM

    Bobbi Billiard has posted a "live journal" update that gives her side of the story related to her recent release from OVW, the WWE developmental territory.

    Billiard says the WWE wanted her to say she left to "pursure other interests" and offered her a "lump sum of cash". She refused to sign the agreement because "that is a lie".

    Billiard says she was told in front of other people by Ivory that girls like her "**** your way to the top", and calls the experience humiliating. She also talks about the long and brutal training sessions that resulted in injuries that caused her to seek medical attention. She says injuries to herself and others caused doctors to question the OVW training program and the result was shorter sessions.
