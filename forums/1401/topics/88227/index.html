    Triple H vs. Eugene

    The Triple H/Eugene saga has finally reached its boiling point. No longer are these two RAW Superstars playing games with each other or pretending to be friends. Now, Triple H and Eugene are bitter enemies.

    According to Triple H, Eugene cost The Game the World Heavyweight Championship on more than one occasion. Now, the Evolution leader wants revenge. On Sunday, August 15, Triple H will step into the ring with Eugene, in an attempt to gain that revenge.

    Despite a lack of professional experience, Eugene is very skilled in the ring. If The Game is to get the revenge he wants, he will have to be on his game, as Eugene has shown to be no pushover.

    Will The Game get the revenge he is looking for? Or will Eugene pull off the victory of his young career? Watch SummerSlam, live on pay-per-view, to find out?
