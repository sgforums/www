    I think many of the matches are quite predictable for a PPV. As 'interactive' as this PPV is supposed to be, the results can be pretty lopsided, because anybody can vote as much as he or she wants, and because the developers can simply alter the results, and no one's going to notice. But I can already see the conclusion of some of the matches.

    Triple Threat Champion of Champions match:

    John Cena (WWE) vs King Booker (World) vs Big Show (ECW)

    No matter which title will be on the line, I'm already guessing that the original owner of that title will get to keep it. I think this sets up the big feud for Survivor Series (Raw vs Smackdown vs ECW). If you want my opinion of what happens, I'm betting that at the end, a big bunch of superstars from all promotions will run down to the ring and brawl, so the match ends in a DQ. As we all know, a DQ means no titles change hands. John Cena's title will probably be chosen. If a title DID change hands, I'd be immensely surprised.

    Umaga vs ???

    Wanna bet? Kane's going to get chosen. Whoop-de-freakin-do. We have to have a fued remember? Umaga sent Kane packing, and now Kane's back to kick his Samoan ass. Either way, I voted twice, once for Chris Benoit, and the other for Sandman. I've seen Kane around for a very long time, and truth be told, he's boring me. I'd like to see how a technical expert like Benoit beats Umaga. If Sandman gets picked, I'm betting they're going to drop a shocker and say that the match isn't under Extreme Rules, so Sandman can't use his Singapore Cane. At the end, he'll just grab it anyway and whack Umaga, DQing himself, and Umaga remains undefeated.

    Jeff Hardy vs ??? for IC title

    I'm pretty sure it's Carlito.

    RAW Tag Teams in a ??? match for the Tag titles

    Who cares? Seriously, who gives a flying fuck about the RAW tag team division? The RAW tag div is a joke. Spirit Squad? Cade and Murdoch? Haas and Viscera? Seriously, what the hell? And now, Cryme Tyme. Compare it to Smackdown, with teams like London and Kendrick, the Pitbulls and now, The Brothers of Destruction.
