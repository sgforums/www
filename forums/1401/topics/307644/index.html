    From wikipedia:

     

    In the final vignette Eric Young arrived and, after Young knocked over a glass, Shark Boy woke up and did a Stone Cold Steve Austin impression, telling everyone to leave and stating "and that's the bottom line 'cause Shark Boy said so!"

     

    On the January 24th episode of Impact, Shark Boy was "reborn" as "Stone Cold" Shark Boy,making the crowd say "what" and using these catchphrases:

     

    • "Shark Boy 24:7 says I just bit your ass"
      • The original Stone Cold catchphrase is "Austin 3:16 says I just Whooped your Ass".
    • "That's the fishing line, cause Shark Boy said so"
      • The original Stone Cold catchphrase is "That's the Bottom Line cause Stone Cold Says So".

    Shark Boy also signed off with Austin's signature of pouring a liquid ("Clam Juice") over his face and giving the audience the "middle finger salute" from the top ropes. His new entrance music & video are similar to Austins showing a shark instead of a rattle snake. It was announced that the parody of Steve Austin would only continue via the fans's response.

    Steve Austin said "only a cheap, second rate company like TNA would do something like this anyway".
