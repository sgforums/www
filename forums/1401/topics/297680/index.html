~WWE Armageddon 2007 Results~

  • HeartWong
    17 Dec 07, 11:54
    Rey Mysterio defeated WWE United States Champion Montel Vontavious Porter by count-out
    -MVP was counted out after refusing to get back in the ring. As a result, MVP retained the title.

    Big Daddy V and Mark Henry defeated CM Punk and Kane
    -Big Daddy V pinned Punk after a Samoan Drop.

    Shawn Michaels defeated Mr. Kennedy
    -Michaels pinned Kennedy after a Sweet Chin Music.

    Jeff Hardy defeated Triple H
    -Hardy pinned Triple H after reversing a Pedigree attempt into a cradle to win a WWE Championship match at Royal Rumble 2008.

    Finlay (w/Hornswoggle) defeated The Great Khali (w/Ranjin Singh)
    -Finlay pinned Khali after Hornswoggle hit Khali in the groin with a shillelagh and Finlay hit Khali in the head with a shillelagh.

    Chris Jericho defeated WWE Champion Randy Orton by disqualification
    -Orton was disqualifed after John "Bradshaw" Layfield kicked Jericho in the head. As a result, Orton retained the title.

    Beth Phoenix defeated Mickie James to retain the WWE Women's Championship
    -Phoenix pinned James with a Bridging Fisherman's suplex.

    Edge defeated Batista and The Undertaker in a Triple Threat Match to win the World Heavyweight Championship.
    -Edge pinned Batista after a Tombstone Piledriver from the Undertaker.
