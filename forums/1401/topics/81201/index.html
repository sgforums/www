    GOLDBERG HAD DINNER WITH BRET HART
    By: 1Wrestling.com
    6/1/2004 5:41:39 PM

    Bill Goldberg's official website has just been updated with a very interesting piece of news:

    Goldberg attended the Stanley Cup Finals game on Saturday night courtesy of the NHL, and following the game, he had dinner with Bret Hart. Goldberg and Hart again met together on Sunday. Goldberg's site promises to have exclusive information about the time the two stars spent together in the near future.

    As many may remember, Hart's in-ring career came to an end, as the result of a kick to the head he took from Goldberg during a match between the two in WCW.
