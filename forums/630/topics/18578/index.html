    Happiness Denied

    Prune the trees, tend the weeds
    Plow the soil, water the plants
    Feed the cows, herd the goats

    Mop the floor, dust the ledge
    Wash the clothes, vacumn the carpet
    Tidy your room, cook your food

    Give you surprises, buy you gifts
    Take you home, listen to'r problems
    Hug you more, piss you less

    Treat you nice, treat you fine
    Bring you out, bring you joy
    Make you happy, make you free

    Where does that leave me,
    Why am I so lonely then.
    Where's the light of love that I need
    A pale darkness of gloom
    Perpetuates within.
    So empty am I!
    Will anyone hear me?
    Save me please
    From this chasm of gloom
    Oh how I hate it
    All I do, all I do for you,
    Art thou satisfied?
    Why doesn't it show
    That you APPRECIATE
    and that you ACKNOWLEDGE my effort
    Yet you don't, all in your own world
    What about me?
    Lonely
    Happiness denied.
