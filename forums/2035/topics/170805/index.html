    Programme Highlights

    Orchard Road will resonate with activities which include the Dioramas Booths display at the Christmas Village; Foreign Booths displaying how they celebrate Christmas; Special appearance by well-known foreign artistes and performing groups at the Satellite Performance Stages; Singing Christmas tree, Christmas Floats; and A Grand Christmas Concert on Christmas day.

    Â· Grand Christmas Celebration Concert (@ Orchard Road)
    - On 25 December, a Grand Christmas Celebration Concert will be held on the road of Orchard.
    - Seven colourfully lighted floats will be featured at the concert.
    - Both local and international artistes and performing groups will be performing in the concert.

    The whole event will climax on the evening of Christmas Day 25th Dec with a Grand Concert and Street Celebration on Orchard Road. The organizers plan to have the stretch of Orchard Road between the junctions with Scotts Road and Bideford Road, closed to traffic. A stage will be erected on the road. The 7 floats will join the celebrations. Together with artistes, celebrities, singers, dancers and performers, both local and foreign, the audience on Orchard Road can join in rapturous celebration to make Christmas 2005 an occasion to remember for a long time to come.
