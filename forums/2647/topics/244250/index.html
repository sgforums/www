    This only applies to the fighting done in streets.

    Common tactics
    1)Hit hard.
    2)Hit where it hurts the most.
    3)fast and hard.
    4)exploit weaknesses and act on them.

    Scenarios.
    1)Cornered in a corner.
    2)Wide in the open.
    3)Sudden attack from behind.

    Actual Fighting

    This section covers a lot.

    1)Martial arts.
    TKD,and other martial arts. it is good to learn martial arts to protect yourself. The idea here is to learn moves and techniques to take your opponent before the opponent takes you down. Always be flexable in your attack by not restricting yourself to moves you have learnt.

    2)Fitness.
    it is good to have some exercise program so that you have the stamina...with the strength to take down the opponent.

    Now i will cover the scenarios.

    scenario 1, Cornered into a corner.
    This may happen without you realising it. It may be your friend or a group of people.

    If 1 VS 1
    if you have the courage to take him/her down, then go for it. Hit hard,if you want, go all out. but beware of counter-attacking if the opponent learned martial arts.

    If 1 VS many
    In this case, it would be wise to watch the suitation and act accordingly. if you cant handle them, RUN!

    run to somewhere public, or somewhere you are sure they can lose you.
    (i am not sure about this, but there is a chance that if you are attacked in public,there will be some others joining in to help/harm you.)

    Wide in the open

    This may be a case of scenario 3...which you may have to get up quickly and fight back. (i have never been in this kind of suitation yet, so i cant share my experience.)

    Kill or not to kill?

    This is a widely debated topic. especially in singapore.

    If the Threat level is so high that it endangers you and others, you may go in for the kill. Fitness and tactics matter a lot this time.
    There are no rules if you are fighting for your life. Keep that in mind and go all out according to your ability.
