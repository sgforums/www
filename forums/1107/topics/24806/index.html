    Welcome Ladies and Gentleman,

    Pinstrikers willl be hosting its first "Couple Tag Team bowling Tournment" tomorrow - 15/9/02 .I hope it will turn out to be a fun and enjoyable event. To help the games run smoothly, here's some rules and regulations:-

    PRIZE AWARD

    a. Apart from Terence's generous contribution of $100, all participating couple (less Terence and Ivy) will contribute an additional $10 for the prizes.

    b. The top prize (A total value of $90) will be given to the couple with the highest total score.

    c. There will also be a prize (Value of $25) for the couple with the most number of consecutive spares.

    d. Tokens of $5 will be given to every couple who achieve a score of 180 pinfalls and above in one game.

    RULES & REGULATIONS

    a. There will be a total of 2 games bowled each team. The gals will bowl first, with the guys doing the spare throw, and the reverse for the second game. There is also a warm up game before the actual event. This warm up game is shared within the couple. (2 person shared one warm up game)

    b. In order not to crowd the area and giving space to bowlers, each time round, only 2 teams will be bowling at the same time (if we have one lane only), or 3 teams will be bowling at the same time (if we have two lanes)

    c. To avoid distractions and disturbance to any bowlers, NO COACHING or guidance is allowed from the team members once the bowler step onto the bowling platform.

    d. Code of Conduct is adherence at all times.

    Last but not least, Thanks to all who has one way or another contributed in making the "Pinstrikers Couple tag team bowling tournment" a possible one. Good Luck 'Pinstrikers'
