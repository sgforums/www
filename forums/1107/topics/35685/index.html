    Here are the info I gathered from Cathay Bowl..........

    Parking shouldnt be a problem as there are 2 car park next to the building. The one on the right is free on sunday and the one in front is charging at $2 per entry.

    Rate - for walk in public, the rate is abt $4 but according to Chee Meng(person in charge) he will be giving us $3.4 per game for at least 6 bowlers per session and $3-$3.2 for at least 10 bowlers per session.

    Privilege card holder - We are invited to join their foursome league which is on every Thursday night at 8pm. Anyone who joined the league will be entitled to sign up for the Privilege card at a small fee and rate for bowling will also be at a privilege price of $2.8 per game. Further details will have to check up with Chee Meng again.

    Oil pattern - Christmas tree.

    Fellow PinStrikers, all in all, there is not much difference in the rate we'll be getting if we switched to Cathy bowl. The only challenge will be the different kind of lane condition we will be adapting to.

    p/s - will check out with Jackie on their oil pattern to do a comparison.

    Feedbacks are welcome.

    Attention - The new season will start on the 2nd March.
