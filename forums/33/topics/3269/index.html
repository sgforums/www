    Case 1: My senior secured a EDB-PLE Scholarship and graduated with a 2nd class-upper honours from the UK's Imperial College last year. He was from ECE , Ngee Ann Poly.

    Case 2 :
    I am a poly grad too. I secured a place in NUS as well as a govt. scholarship.
