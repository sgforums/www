    ok, here's my comments with regards to the new grading system.

    No, Prof Bernard Tan, the Dean of Students, couldn't give us a good reply. Were the student leaders informed of this change beforehand? Since the day I was in office, no. as far as i know, we weren't consulted at all. though the reaction this time is not as strong as the hike in U fees.

    some yr 3s lose out, undoubtly. the happiest of all would be the yr 2s. I'm a yr 1 now, and i can tell you that the new system screwed me up.

    i chose my CFM as graded as i was quite sure i would get a B in the worst case. after all, it's subject CAP which mattered to me, more than overall CAP. to my horror, the new system removes subject CAP and put everything on major CAP. i got a B for that CFM. had i known the change beforehand, i would NEVER have chose graded for the CFM. without that extra B, my CAP would have been higher.
