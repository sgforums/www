    Cuteness overload: NUS students de-stress through cat therapy

     

     

    What better to destress than to cuddle with some furry friends?

    Cat Safari Singapore and Love Kuching Project teamed up to give the students of National University of Singapore (NUS) the therapy session they deserve.

    Two 'Feline Therapy Sessions' were held on Nov 16 and Nov 24, where students get to mingle and play with the therapy cats from the organisation.

    This was for students to destress before their upcoming examinations.

    Cat Safari Singapore wrote: 

    "We are reaching out, an outreach project for students from NUS who are preparing for their exams this week, a channel for them to destress by the presence of the cats. It all went on well.

    "This initiative was a collaboration together with Love Kuching project & the committees from NUS catcafe."
