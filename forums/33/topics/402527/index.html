    Psychology Camp 2010: Cirque du Psycho

    Date: 6 – 9 July 2010 (Tue – Fri)

    Accommodation: NUS Eusoff Hall

    Camp fee: S$50

    Why you should join us!

     

    The annual Psychology Camp 2010 is here!

    Do you want to get to know like-minded peers and seniors? Or familiarize yourself with NUS? Join in the fun for this 4D3N camp!

    Also, there will be a CORS simulation session which will be useful for all freshmen! It will be conducted by friendly seniors who can also offer invaluable advice on the Psychology modules.


    Hesitate no longer! Download the application form from our blog and pass it to us during registration. If you have any queries, you may email us or drop us a message on our blog or Facebook group. We will address them as soon as we can.

    See you during registration!     J
