    [SIZE="3"]Our annual expedition is back this year with more challenges! We will be exploring the Northern part of Singapore, which consists of a few offshore islands such as Pulau Seletar and Pulau Serangoon. Normally inaccessible by other modes of transportation, we will explore these islands via sea kayaking. Along the way, you will be able to see Kelongs and activities along the coastal area, and even get close to the Malaysia shore.

     

    Get ready for a unique adventure and a splashing good time! Come join us for the expedition and experience the fun![/SIZE]

     

    [B]Route:[/B]

    Sembawang Park to Pasir Ris Beach

     

    [B]Date:[/B] 

    10th December 2011

     

    [B]Time:[/B]

    8am to 5pm

     

    [B]Price:[/B]

    $32 – NUS Students

    $28 – NUS Canoeing Club Members

     

    Note: You will need to possess at least a 1-Star Kayaking Certification before you can participate in the expedition.
