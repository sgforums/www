    Guess what fellows? The publicity of Cosfest 2003 will be starting at Chinese New Year! =) Preliminary details will be as follows:

    Event: COSFEST 2003 Natsu Matsuri (Summer festival)
    Organisers: Singapore Cosplay Club, NTUC Club, Quebec Leisure
    International Pte Ltd
    Date: 6th July 2003
    Theme: Natsu Matsuri - summer festival
    Location: NTUC Lifestyle World Downtown East event square
    Entrance fee: FREE
    Competitions: 1) Cosplay (all genres welcomed except yaoi, yuri,
    hentai)
    2) Original Character & Costume designing competition (simple costumes stand a lower chance of winning...plain everyday clothes costume will not be considered)

    Stage: 1)Band performances,
    2)dance performances,
    3)mass dance,
    4)EMCEE games

    miscs: 1) Artist square,
    2) costume exhibition,
    3) art exhibition,
    4) grafitti wall,
    5) SCC & Cosfest 2004 recruitment booth
    6) cosplay information display,
    7) centre of attraction(mystery)
    goodie bags for all cosplayers

    COSFEST 2003 aims at bringing cosplay in Singapore to a higher level by increasing public awareness and increasing the number of cosplayers.

    Anyone interested in setting up a booth, pls e-mail or call me at 96150158. Booth price is still under negotiation with NTUC. If there are a lot of people interested in the booths, the success rate of lowering the cost/booth is higher.

    Do you want to help us in promoting cosfest 2003? You can do a part by doing the following:

    1) everyday, tell 2 of ur frens who are not cosplayers about Cosfest 2003 and the website (will be released after Chinese New Year)
    2) tell ur relatives when during Chinese New Year visit
    3) set up a thread in forums inside Singapore and outside Singapore
    4) join us in other Cosfest 2003 promotional events between Feb and July (more details will be released)
    5) join the Cosfest ML for updates at http://groups.yahoo.com/group/cosfest/?yguid=91787970

    Any queries pls email us at cosfest@yahoo.com
    See you folks in Cosfest 2003 - Natsu Matsuri =)
