3rd July - COSMO Costume Competition

     ********************************************************************

     

    Presenting COSMO Costume Competition!!!

    This is presented as part of Mascot Parade. We are proud to bring you a brand new event segment so we hope everyone will come and give us support 

     

    We want to bring this competition every year, bigger, better and with more prizes, so hope that everyone will support us for our 1st event.

     

    Good luck everyone!

    Included below is a brief introduction to the Competition.

     

    Registration link is at the bottom.

     

    ===================

     

    General Info

    Date: 3rd July 2010

    Time: 12 pm to 8 pm

    *Registration is from 9 am to 12 pm

    Venue: Orchard Cineleisure

     

    Event Details

    • 4 categories to choose from

    o Anime & Manga

    o Comics & Cartoons

    o PC & Console Games

    o Movies & TV Shows

     

    • Participation Goodie Bag for all contestants

    o A pair of Cathay movie tickets

    o Limited Edition Animax T-shirt

    o SHINE Youth Festival Collectibles

     

    • 30 Seconds of Fame!

    o Each contestant is given 30 seconds on stage to impress both the judges and the voters

     

    • Voting System

    o All contestants will be given a number tag to wear during the event

    o Public can vote for their favourite contestant

    o Contestants can walk around during the entire event to garner votes

    o Voters are eligible for lucky draw to win a SONY PS3 (Contestants are allowed to vote for themselves!)

     

    • Grand Finals

    o The winner of each category will participate in the Grand Finals to become the Ultimate COSMO Champions

    o Each winner will get 1 minute on stage to convince the judges and voters on why they deserve to win

     

    • Attractive Prizes

    o Each Category will have 3 prizes

    1st Prize of Cash $500 and trophy

    2nd Prize of Cash $300 and trophy

    3rd Prize of Cash $100 and trophy

    o Ultimate COSMO Champions will win a pair of tickets to Japan and Ultimate COSMO Champion Trophy

     

     

    Programme

    09:00 am to 11:30 am Registration

    11:30 am to 12:30 pm Anime & Manga category

    12:30 pm to 01:30 pm Comics & Cartoons category 

    01:30 pm to 02:30 pm PC & Console Games category

    02:30 pm to 03:30 pm Movies & TV Shows category

    04:00 pm to 06:00 pm Mascot Parade + Results Tabulation

    07:00 pm to 07:30 pm Ashley & Alodia Performance

    07:30 pm to 08:00 pm Grand Finals + Announcement of results + Awards ceremony

     

     

    General Rules

    1. Contest registration is on a first come first served basis

    2. Closing date for registration is on 27th June 2010

    3. Contestant must be youths between 15 and 35 years old

    4. Each contestant may only participate in one category

    5. The character that the contestant is costume-playing must be from one of the four specified categories

    6. There is to be no obscenity on stage including cursing, swearing, inappropriate nudity etc.

    7. Contestant must be present during results announcement to claim their prize

    8. Any contestant who does not comply with the rules will be disqualified
