Ferdinand rumours rejected

  • bryanseb
    11 Jan 07, 20:51
    Anton Ferdinand's agent has rejected reports linking the defender with a move away from West Ham.

    The highly-rated defender's future at Upton Park has been the subject of intense speculation over the last few weeks.

    Reports have claimed that Tottenham and Newcastle are keen on signing the England Under 21 international.

    However, the player's representative Jeff Weston has rejected the rumours and claims Ferdinand is happy at Upton Park.

    "The reports about Anton are just pure speculation," Weston told skysports.com.

    "I don't believe there have been any offers made for Anton.

    "Anton is a West Ham player and he will continue to remain so until someone decides otherwise."
