    Here are the Harbourvale Routes :D (Currently there's an IEL being developed by Dragon, so I suppose you could link both systems up or something? The line runs from HL6 Central to CG2 Changi Airport. Harbourvale City is 200-300km northeast of Singapore, in the sea. :P However there's no need to link up both if you dunwan) I'm using this as an official template for my documentations too.

    The lines are arranged in chronological order of line's official opening.

    Harbourvale Line

    Termini: HL1 Harbourvale, HL16 Blackton Junction (future: HL18 Lower Ends)
    Rolling Stock: C100A
    Depot(s): Square Quays Depot, District Depot
    Length of line: 11km
    Duration between termini: 21 minutes
    Number of closed stations: Zero
    Random Fact: The Harbourvale Line was the first line in the city to be opened! :D

    Airport Express Line

    Termini: AE1 Stadium, AE4 East Harbour
    Rolling Stock: C100B
    Depot(s): South Coast Depot
    Length of line: 5km
    Duration between termini: Approximately 10 minutes
    Number of closed stations: Zero
    Random Fact: The Airport Express Line was intended to be a branch from the Harbourvale Line.

    North West Line [Under Construction]

    Termini: NW1 South Coast, NW10 City Square
    Rolling Stock: C100A
    Depot(s): Square Quays Depot, District Depot
    Length of line: To-be-confirmed
    Duration between termini: To-be-confirmed
    Number of closed stations: Zero
    Random Fact: The line was built under HLRT's supervision until HCRT took over, thus the monotonous designs from NW1 to NW6.

    Blackton Line [Under Construction]

    Termini: BL1 Townsville, BL13 Promenade
    Rolling Stock: C591
    Depot(s): Caroye Depot
    Length of line: NIL
    Duration between termini: NIL
    Number of closed stations: One, BL4
    Random Fact: The tracks in this line never diverge and converge, they are always 8m apart from each other. The middle portion of the viaducts may be used for recreational purposes like a park along the tracks, obviously with fencing beside the tracks.

    Evergreen Fields Line [Future]

    Termini: EF1 East Harbour, EF15 City Square
    Rolling Stock: C100C
    Depot(s): Lexington Depot
    Length of line: NIL
    Duration between termini: NIL
    Number of closed stations: Two, EF2 Lexington and EF13 Town Hall
    Random Fact: The current structure and route of the line was derived from an abandoned line with the same name. Old tracks can be found in the undeveloped Evergreen Parks district, 1km east of Evergreen Fields. Evergreen Parks was scheduled for extremely heavy developement and this district was supposed to take over Central as the city's CBD area, but plans were shelved after three newly built buildings collapsed in less than a month due to the marine clay in that area.

    Kegstone Line [Future]

    Termini: KS1 Lowland, KS10 Gateway
    Rolling Stock: C591
    Depot(s): Caroye Depot
    Length of line: NIL
    Duration between termini: NIL
    Number of closed stations: Zero
    Random Fact: The current Kegstone Line was an accidental line. There were no plans for this line at all, even after the Harbourvale Line fully opened from HL1 to HL16.

    City Line [Future]

    Termini: NIL (Trains can switch directions seamlessly at the following stations: CT1 South Coast, CT9 Lowland, CT14 Shorevale, CT22 Promenade)
    Rolling Stock: C398
    Depot(s): South Coast Depot
    Length of line: NIL, expected to be at least 30km long
    Duration between termini: NIL
    Number of closed stations: To-be-confirmed
    Random Fact: The City Line will efficiently cut travelling time, as passengers who want to get to and fro areas within the outskirts of the city, they can take this line without having to go through and interchange at the city centre.
