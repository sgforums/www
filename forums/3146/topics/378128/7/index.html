    The C151 with its smaller counterpart originally assigned to run the Osaka Subway Midosuji Line. The former can still get you a high score rating on this route(around Superb) in openBVE despite its slower acceleration than the C651. But overall, comparing the motor and run sounds, the C151 beats every train in acceleration and braking with its smooth transition of sound.
