  • jcqh
    19 Mar 10, 12:00

    Ok, something which you all have been waiting for - Version 1 is finally out!

    C651 Version 1.0

    Inclusive of:
    - Photorealistic exterior (Fully complete)
    - Photorealistic Interior
    - Self-drawn cab
    - Motor sounds

    Areas of improvement:
    - Photorealistic Interior (1/3 complete)
      - Vertical bars at each glass panel
      - Handgrips
      - More accurate adjustment of the overhead panels (some are slightly off)
    - Motor sounds (Needs development, sounds are not smooth)
    - Number of powering and braking currents.
      - Currently at 3P 4B, should be 4P 3B

    After 6 months of development (20/09/09 - 19/03/10), my "product" is now ready to meet the world!

    So... ENJOY!
