    Originally posted by Samuel Lee:

    What is the point for randomize train selection? Where to download C651 and C751B at the moment?


    It's to future-proof this route. If you would like to remove it, just modify the following:

    Find these lines:

    With Train,
    $Sub(0) = C151
    $Sub(1) = C651
    $Sub(2) = C751B
    $Sub(3) = C151

    Train.Folder $Sub($Rnd(0;3)),
    ;Train.Folder c151,

    and replace with:

    With Train,
    ;$Sub(0) = C151
    ;$Sub(1) = C651
    ;$Sub(2) = C751B
    ;$Sub(3) = C151

    ;Train.Folder $Sub($Rnd(0;3)),
    Train.Folder c151,

    That selection thingy is a kind of a trial. Subject to possible removal.
