    By the way, after driving the route from JUR to AMK, I hope certain things can be touched up in the new version : 1) The only gradient present was after leaving Jurong East Station. Here are some things I observed when riding the entire route throughout this section in real life : *There are supposed to be upslopes towards stations like Bukit Batok, Yew Tee, Woodlands (before the central siding leading to the station in the direction of MRB) Admiralty, Sembawang, Ang Mo Kio (slight upslope upon approaching the station southbound). *There is a very nice gentle downslope about 600m after leaving Bukit Gombak for Choa Chu Kang where the track levels up upon negotiating the first curve. From there after the curve its downslope for a long straight stretch of track that will eventually dive under Choa Chu Kang Ave 1 where it will rise upwards towards Choa Chu Kang. After leaving Choa Chu Kang there is another slight downslope where the train will cruise smoothly about 60+ km/h before loosing speed through the upslope towards Yew Tee. After leaving this station and running for 150m with a converging turnout there will be a very long downslope all the way to the Sungei Kadut Industrial area before the line rises slightly for the train to lose a little speed in order to negotiate the rather sharp curve.
