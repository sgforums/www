    Originally posted by FSJZ:

    Okay, but I still don't understand the technique of coding objects, I am better at coding routes. Those "addvertex' and 'setcoordinates' commands. '.'

    AddVertex adds a corner.

    AddVertex X, Y, Z are the axes. X is left and right, Y is up and down, Z is forward backward. It adds a "corner" of the face.

    AddFace adds a face that can be viewed on 1 side only. AddFace2 is not recommended, and it adds a face that can be seen on both side.
    AddFace 1, 2, 3, 4 adds a face which is linked by the vertices. This needs to be "linked" counter-clockwise.

    Hope it helps.
