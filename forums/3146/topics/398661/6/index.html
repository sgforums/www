    If anyone's wondering: Why didn't you put the RATIS as countdown mode? (4 mins > 3 mins > 2 mins > 1min > train arrived)

    Answer: The reason is because, openBVE .ANIMATED files can only read the train if the train is within 1km.

    So why not 2min > 1min? Because "if"s commands in .ANIMATED files are limited to 3, but it's possible, but it's better if commands are not so complicated.

    So why not count by timings? Because, timings aren't sooo accurate, player trains might be early.

    Source: Self-knowledge (Or something, I just trial and error stuffs)
