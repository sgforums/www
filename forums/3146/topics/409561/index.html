    Coastal Sands Line

    The Coastal Sands Line is a line operated by HMRT, where the line serves the busy Coastal Sands District and interchanges with the Airport Express Line, Evergreen Fields Line and the City Line. The line is denoted black on the system map of Harbourvale City.

    This line was constructed because HCRT decided to join City Line together with Airport Express Line and Evergreen Fields Line, as there was a gap there.

    Currently, the route has four stations, with a major interchange at East Harbour. This line is fully underground as there were lack of space above ground.

    [CS1] Coney Island (CNI)
    [CS2] Coastal Sands (COS)
    [CS3] Crestview (CRV) -- (Not In Service)
    [CS4] East Harbour (EHA)

    This route is currently being developed by Scania96, co-developed by yellowflip999.
