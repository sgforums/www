    And it's time to clarify all doubts now.

    The station in which all of you guys thought was some kind of depot after NW7 Blackton South is actually NW8 Caroye. I did not extend the route to NW8 Caroye due to the reason that the track layout there is extremely complicated, I'll need some time doing it. Besides, the section of tracks from NW7 Blackton South to NW10 City Square is extremely difficult to do, as NW9 Horizon is elevated.

    I do agree and admit that this is some piece of rushed work, I had to deliver my promise of releasing this route, I didn't want to make any of you guys feel that I'm somebody who loves to slack around and do nothing. Coding is tough work, I actually spent one whole day doing the PSDs itself.

    As for NW6 World Plaza, the station was planned to have 3D FHPSDs, but seeing the framerates drop dramatically after adding them, they'll be removed in the next release. Instead, I might consider the solution of the 1st Generation FHPSDs or simply place a yellow line to mark your comfort zone.

    The gradients on the section of tracks from NW1 South Coast to NW2 Westway is extremely steep and hard-to-imagine, but the steep gradients have to be there due to the reason your track goes under the Harbourvale Line Northbound track. There may be some modifications to this to change this. The steep gradients and curves are also a reason why I place a hard speed limit of 40km/h there, this section of track is not suitable for any typical train running speed from 50km/h to 100km/h.

    Other than that, I think all doubts have been clarified. Please keep the suggestions rolling in, thank you and enjoy the route for now. :)
