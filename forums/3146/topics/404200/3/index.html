    Here are some pre-release notes for you to consider when the route is released tomorrow:

    In order for the signs on the PSDs to be displayed correctly, you need to set openBVE's transparency mode to "Smooth". Here's how you do it. First, open up the openBVE application, and then click the "Options" tab on the left panel. Then under "Interpolation", there should be something called "Transparency". There will be a bar beside it. If your bar indicator points to "Sharp", drag the bar indicator to "Smooth". That should do the trick.

    The route goes from NW1 South Coast to NW7 Blackton South. All stations from NW1 South Coast to NW4 Lakeview have PSDs, the stations beyond that do not have PSDs. Although NW6 World Plaza and NW7 Blackton South are underground, PSDs are not installed in them.

    There are currently no reported errors as it is, if there is just one error or more, try redownloading the route package.

    I think that's about it, happy waiting! :D
