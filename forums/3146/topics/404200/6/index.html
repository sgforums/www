    Real life (Terms that should be used in openBVE):

    1G = 1st Generation = All underground stations on the East West Line and North South Line, except Changi Airport and Bishan.
    2G = 2nd Generation = All stations on the North East Line and Changi Airport.
    3G = 3rd Generation = All stations on the Circle Line and Bishan.

    openBVE (Terms that gives extra details of which version of the PSDs you're referring):

    2D = 2 Dimensional = Older version of 3G PSDs, seen at Bishan in North South Line for openBVE and South Coast in Harbourvale Line.
    OR
    2G PSDs, seen at Changi Airport in Changi Airport Branch Line

    3D = 3 Dimensional = Newer version of 3G PSDs, seen at Platform A of Stadium in Airport Express Line.
    OR
    1G PSDs, seen at some underground stations in East West Line for openBVE.
