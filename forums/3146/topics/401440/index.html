    NXL = North Xpress Line
    NAL = North Airport Line

    Yes, it's a remake of my previous North Xpress Line. This time, I recorded announcements myself, and somehow replanned the entire route.

    I referred to Google Maps and somehow traced out the map of both the lines so that they won't collide with buildings like the previous North Xpress Line did.

    Description of Route:
    It starts at an empty field where the future station will be in Boon Lay. From there, it turns up north, crosses the East West Line by above, and crosses the North South Line above in Choa Chu Kang. It then turns into Sembawang and makes a stop. The North Airport Line will have platforms but will skip this station. It crosses the North South Line again and make its way and follow TPE. Due to space constraints, the 2 lines will share tracks starting from there. The tracks will go underground immidiately after Sungei Punggol. The tracks will split to make another platform, and splits again to seperate the 2 lines. Both lines will have a stop here. The both lines will merge again so as to make trains easily return to Punggol Depot. They will split again and the North Xpress Line will make its way to Pasir Ris. Before it terminates, it will go above ground and will stop near the existing Pasir Ris East West Line terminus.

    The North Airport Line will continue all the way underground and stops below the existing Changi Airport Line terminus.

    Short Working Trips:
    Every morning, 5 C251s (NXL variant) will serve NAL which stops at Sembawang. It uses the NXL platforms throughout until after Sengkang station, which it will continue to Changi Airport station.

    For LTE, 4 trains will serve between Pasir Ris and will stop at Sengkang Platform C instead. Trains will turn back from there back to Pasir Ris.

    In the evening, both the LTE and morning SWT schedules will be on.

    Station Layout:
    Boon Lay Station
    4 tracks
    Stacked island platforms

    Platform A and B for NAL (upper level)
    Platform C and D for NXL (lower level)

    Sembawang Station
    4 tracks
    2 island platforms, separated by NAL tracks in middle

    Platform A and B for NXL (outer tracks)
    Platform C and D for NAL (inner tracks)

    NAL passes through the station under normal circumstances, but Platforms C and D will serve as an emergency platform for emergency uses.

    Sengkang Station
    5 tracks
    2 island platforms, 1 side platform

    Platform A and Platform C - Island
    Platform B - Side
    Platform D and E - Island

    Platform A and B is for NXL. Platform C is for short working trips and for trains to terminate there. Platform D and E is for NAL.

    Pasir Ris Station
    2 tracks
    1 island platform

    Platform A and B

    Changi Airport Station
    2 tracks
    1 island platform

    Platform A and B

    Works will start before the end of June Holidays. Train used will be C251, which will be remade too.
