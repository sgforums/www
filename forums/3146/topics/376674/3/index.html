  • kenneth1228
    3 Mar 10, 19:18
    Originally posted by flightsimmer:

    Hey!! just a random question, anyway to disable the auto door close function? i like to manual open and close the doors, more fun.. anyway to disable it? thanks!

    http://openbve.trainsimcentral.co.uk/common/tool_traineditor.zip

    Download this and extract it into your OpenBVE Folder.

    http://openbve.trainsimcentral.co.uk/common/tool_traineditor_source.zip

    This is needed for your settings for train editor, extract this into your TRAINEDITOR

    File. 

    When you finish downloading and extracting everything, Run TrainEditor in your

    OpenBVE Folder. Then click Open at

    the left side, then ignore the pop up of 'Do you want to save the data before opening

    another train', then go to your C151 folder and find the C151 train.dat file. Click on it

    and you will get the properties, go to Properties(2) by clicking on the tab on the top of

    the TrainEditor, look at the right side of the page named 'Device', you should see

    'DoorsOpenMode' and 'DoorsCloseMode', they were in Semi-Automatic for both, so

    change them to 'Manual', and you are done!

     

    Kenneth1228, editor of trains
