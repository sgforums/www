    Just 1 question.How do I do RM mode lights?

  • Bngauw
    5 Feb 11, 20:48
    Originally posted by Zameer Sufyan:

    Just 1 question.How do I do RM mode lights?


    Simple.

    First: You Need A Head 3 and Head 4. Where Both Heads are Mirror Image. And Head 4 is a CM Mode Head.

    Second:  Head 2. Colour the Whole Image in The Colour 0,0,255 For it To Be Transparent.

    Third: Make 2 csv File And Name Them Head1 and Head2. Then Edit Using Notepad and Add This:

    ;Head;,,,,

    CreateMeshbuilder,,,,

    ADDVertex,-1.8,3.95,0.108,

    ADDVertex,-1.8,1.7,0.008,

    ADDVertex,-1.8,-0.10,-0.008,

    ADDVertex,1.8,3.95,0.108,

    ADDVertex,1.8,1.7,0.008,

    ADDVertex,1.8,-0.10,-0.008,

    ADDFace2,0,1,4,3

    ADDFace2,1,2,5,4

    GenerateNormals,,,,
    LoadTexture, head3.bmp,,,

    SETTextureCoordinates,0,0,0,

    SETTextureCoordinates,1,0,0.6,

    SETTextureCoordinates,2,0,1,

    SETTextureCoordinates,3,1,0,

    SETTextureCoordinates,4,1,0.6,

    SETTextureCoordinates,5,1,1,

    SetDecalTransparentcolor,0,0,255
    ,,,,

    ,Into The File. For Head2, Just Change 

    (GenerateNormals,,,,
    LoadTexture, head3.bmp,,,)

    To

    (GenerateNormals,,,,
    LoadTexture, head4.bmp,,,) .

    Fourth: In Your 3131 Animated File, Add This:

    ;Lights;
    [Object]
    States = head1.csv, head2.csv
    StateFunction = value == 0
    RefreshRate = 1

    Into It.

    (Add On) Step 5: Don't Forget to Set Your OWN Animated Position. Or Youll end Up like C830/C751B/C951 Fan And Have the Head In the Middle Of The Carridge

    Simple As That. Should You Need More Help, Feel Free to PM Your Email Address To Me And We Can Chat on MSN.  Have Fun Editing! =)
