    1. That one i dunno how to explain

    2.That one i also dunno how to explain but there is a solution. Solution: Adjust the other rail's distance

    3.The idea of crossover/converge/diverge is to adjust the track distance/start a new track.

    For crossovers, you have to start a new track and adjust it's distance. Then when you want to end the crossover, put the command : railend xx

    Example:

    0,
    .railstart 1,0,0,1

    25
    xxxxxxxxxxx
    .rail 1;20

    50
    .rail 1;40
    .railend 1

     

    For converge and diverge:

    You just have to adjust the rail distance.

    Example:

    25
    xxxxxxxxx
    .curve -200
    .rail 1;15

    50
    .curve 0
    .rail 1;10

    Get what I mean?
