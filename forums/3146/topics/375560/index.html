  • SBS7322B
    15 Sep 09, 18:34

    Maximum acceleration of 4.8 km/h/s and top speed of 90 km/h. Don't worry. It will take a long time to accelerate from 80km/h to 90km/h. Especially when the ATC kicks in from the route developers, you won't be hitting 90km/h. I will also try to reduce lag by reducing the amount of faces to account for people using Intel family of graphic cards.

    This project is strictly copyrighted by Creative Commons Attribution-No Derivative Works 3.0 Singapore License.

    More info here: http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nd/3.0/sg/

    This is what you will expect:

    • Photo-realistic exterior as well as interior
    • Animated doors 
    • ATC-equipped (ATC functions are made from the route file, Ctrl+S to activate security system and Page Down/Up to select ATS/ATC).
    Completed:
    Tuning of the train
    Door animations and synchronised with doors opening and closing sound
    Sounds of the train, but more improvements can be made
    Exterior of the train

    In progress:
    Interior of the train

    I'm also looking for contributors for photos of the C751B cab. PM me if you are interested in contributing your photos of the C751B cab.

    Any more enquiries, feel free to PM me or post in this thread. =D
