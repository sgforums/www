    Put command:

    .railstart 3;0;0;1

    then

    .rail 3;10

    .........

    then when you want to end the crossover, put command:

    .railend 3

    EXAMPLE: (Som parts are extracted from the CCL)

    0,
    .wall 0;0;3,
    .wall 1;0;4,
    .railstart 3;0;0;1
    .railstart 4;20;0;1
    .rail 4;20
    .rail 3;0

    25,
    .wall 0;0;3,
    .wall 1;0;4,
    .rail 4;11
    .rail 3;2
    .announce ccl xt_CC2.wav

    50
    .wall 0;0;3
    .wall 1;0;4
    .rail 4;2
    .rail 3;11

    ..........

    Note: You just start the rail then adjust the distance
