    Yes, due to these PSDs were taken from Willis' CGL, I have no choice, but to follow that, which means, those PSDs were initally coded for first four PSDs, and only on that particular side, thus, in order to install the PSD to the rest of the doors, I have to extend it backwards. This is why the PSD has to be coded / declared at 21.6m before the stop point, and it is proven that the stop point of a C151, C651 and C751B should be at 152.45m after the start of the station.
