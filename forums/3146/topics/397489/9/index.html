  • Nickgoh45
    31 Jul 10, 23:55

    Great news: Update #3 has been released.

    Harbourvale Line v4.008
    -The tracks have been raised from 0m to 0.15m so as to meet Singapore BVE's standard.
    -The run sounds for the tracks have been changed to meet the C751B's standard.
    -The system map for the transit system has been updated.
    -The stopping points for almost all stations have been moved forward by 5m.
    -A station name now includes the alphabetical and numerical code for that particular station.
    -All platforms have been raised from 1.1m to 1.17m to be as of same height of the C751B. The platforms have been edited to give a 0.1m to 0.2m gap between the train and the platform for realism.
    -PSDs have been added at HL7 Square Quays.
    -HL12 Harbourvale Highway has been closed down for renovations. Thus there is a sign at HL13 South Coast to warn all drivers of the speed limit.
    -You no longer require to stop the train at HL12 Harbourvale Highway.
    -The announcement "This train terminates at Harbourvale" after HL6 Central has been moved closer towards HL5 Chinatown after users have complained that the announcement clashing with the usual default announcements.
    -The direction of sunlight has been changed, to make the sunset more realistic.
    -The background after HL3 Rivervale has been changed from the night picture to a dark evening picture.
    -Speed limit signs have been placed.
    -Stop signs have been placed opposite the platforms at the stopping area of stations.
    -More minor bugfixes.

    http://www.4shared.com/file/pb4qZz1x/HBV_Line_v4.html
