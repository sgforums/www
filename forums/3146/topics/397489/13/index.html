    The reason why the C751Bs are uncoupled is because they were positioned in RouteBuilder. RouteBuilder does not position everything precisely. Besides, I spaced all the carriages out a little because the whole train is extremely short and when all carriages are combined together, the whole train is only 125m long and does not meet the 137-138m requirement.

    In order to place announcements in your route, you need to code this:

    .announce anyroutesoundsfolder\anystationannouncement.wav

    This command actually finds the file called "anystationannouncement.wav" under the folder C:\openBVE\Railway\Sounds\anyroutesoundsfolder .

    Please do note that the directory of the file may differ depending on where you place your openBVE folder.
