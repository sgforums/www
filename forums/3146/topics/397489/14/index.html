    Whew, finally packaged this, it took me a whole lot of effort! :D

    Today's the release of the Harbourvale Line v4.015, here's the changelog for it:

    Harbourvale Line v4.015
    -All announcements have been replaced. The old announcements are kept as archives.
    -HL12 Harbourvale Highway has been fully renovated and reopened.
    -You now require to stop at HL12 Harbourvale Highway again. The announcements have been inserted into the route file again.
    -The number of A.I. trains operating in front and behind you has been reduced to zero for now, after users have complained of lags in the route.
    -The announcement "This train terminates at Harbourvale" after a few particular stations have been merged into the departure announcement as well.
    -The sentence structure of all announcements have been edited, and the chinese announcements have been done away with.
    -The system map for the transit system has been updated.
    -HHPSDs have been added at HL12 Harbourvale Highway.
    -The track section at the Headwall in HL7 Square Quays has been changed from a dark tunnel track type to an overground track type.

    AN IMPORTANT NOTE: You have to delete all Harbourvale City routes related folders (except for the Airport Express Line) before downloading and extracting this. This is because I have changed the organisation of the Harbourvale City Routes.

    Click here to download the Harbourvale Line v4.015.

    Thank you and have a pleasant journey. :)
