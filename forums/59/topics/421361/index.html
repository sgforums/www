    An enrollment letter will be sent to your residence. 

    In that letter, there are some forms for you to write - Particulars, study loans, CPF deductions ect. This is to facilitate and fastern the process when you come down to SP for enrollment 

    There is an online enrollment for you to do (In my year there is, 2 years back), using the info in the letter. -  First step. 

    The online enrollment will be the creation of your SP Ichat email account and some misc stuffs. It will also tell you about the appointment time at the end of it, for you to come down to SP for your final enrollment. You can get your Laptop at that time too.
