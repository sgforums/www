    Hi everyone or anybody reading this note.

    I am posting on behalf of the Singapore Polytechnic Cyclists. The club is holding a Round Island Cycling Expedition on the 19th and 20th of August. For those of you who are afraid of cycling because of the sun, well this is your chance! It is an overnight cycling trip where transport for food, drinks are included. That's not all, bicycles, helmets, water bottles are also provided!=) This ensure a safe and ease in cycling. T-shirt is also in the package for each participants. There are rest points for snacks and drinks. All this plus the night scenery of Ulu Ulu places PLUS the Changi Airport Runway stretch cost only $14!

    For registration venue, it is outside the SAC foyer from this coming week till the end of next wednesday. Time for registration is from 12p.m to 1p.m. Look out for the activity, you will surely regret if you missed this opportunity.

    This event is OPEN TO THE WHOLE OF SP STUDENTS. Anyone interested can join in the ride. Please pass this message to your friends in SP. Hope you people will support this event. Thanks! =0)
