    I think there are different type of training?

    A slightly lower weight like around 75-80% of 1RM weight for 8-12 reps give better muscle development while 85-90% of 1RM weight for 5-6 reps give better strength development.

    Also some people may opt to do a drop set on a lighter weight for max reps until failure for maximum training effect.
