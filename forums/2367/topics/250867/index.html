    Curling is the worst gauge for upper-body strength.Military presses, dips/pullups sound more feasible.

    Its like saying you scored 100/100 in Chinese but failed other subjects and call yourself academically well-rounded.

    But if you are obessed with curling, i cannot convince you to stop.

    Since you like arm strength this few compound movements WILL DEFINATELY give it to you.

    1)Dumbbell rows
    2)Chin-ups or Curl grip lateral pulldowns(alternate them on different days)
    3)Close grip bench presses
    4)Dips

    Google the exercises for pictures, it can be found.

    If you still wanna change your mind about training the whole body instead.Then i applaud you.
