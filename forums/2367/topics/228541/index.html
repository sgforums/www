    The 3rd HomeTeamNS Strongman Challenge is here again!

    If you have a high level of strength, endurance, determination and love challenges, participate in the HomeTeamNS Strongman Challenge 2007 and stand to win cash and prizes worth up to S$30,000!

    HomeTeamNS Strongman Challenge 2007

    Registration Fee: S$10

    Closing Date: 1 February 2007

    Preliminary

    Venue: Basic Rescue Training Centre (BRTC),
    Parade Square

    Jalan Bahar Camp 101 Jalan Bahar Singapore 649734

    Date:4 February 2007

    Time:10am (Registration at 9.30am)

    [Note: Participants will compete in 3 events- Tyre Flip, SCDF Red Rhino Pull and Log Walk. Results will be based on the combined best timing for all 3 events. Top 10 participants will qualify for the Finals, held on 31 March 2007 at Toa Payoh HDB Hub Mall.]

    Finals

    Venue: Toa Payoh HDB Hub Mall

    Date: 31 March 2007

    Time: 1- 6pm (Registration at 12.30pm)

    All participants are allowed to bring along their own safety equipment (eg. gloves, belts, wrist guards, kneepads and other gym wear apparels).
