  • sillyme
    15 Aug 07, 21:37
    I usually warmup by jogging slowly.

    For starters, set yourself a target distance.

    Each week increase the distance by 10%.

    Jog slow.

    It is good to plan one of your runs where you emphasise on your form. In short, a good form is that you "run tall". Imagine a string pulling your upwards. For more details, pick up a book on running.

    To really burn fats, you'd have to jog more than 30 minutes.

    But if you are a beginner, just concentrate on short jogs first.

    Most of all, learn to enjoy it
