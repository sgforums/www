    Split routines are fine as long as they are of the upper/lower body or push/pull types.Please no Day 1-biceps,Day 2-triceps that kinda crap...

    I may want to experiment with this push/pull split I came up with.

    Workout A(Push)

    Squats-4X6
    Lunges-3X10
    Incline presses-4X6
    Dips-3Xfailure

    Workout B(Pull)

    Romanian deadlifts-4X6
    Bent over rows-4X6
    pull ups-3Xfailure
    pullovers-3X10

    Train to failure only on the last set of the free weight movements.

    The schedule

    Mon-Workout A
    Tues-Rest
    Wed-B
    Thurs-Rest
    Fri-A
    Sat-Rest
    Sun-B

    The cycle starts again,basically you train every other day.
