    If you're gonna go to a gym, then there's no need to get additional equipment for home use, other than some mobility/prehab/SMFR stuff like maybe a foam roller(close to 0 gyms have this), resistance bands, etc

    If you've never done weight training in your life and are really weak, start with body weight exercises first - pushups, chinups, single leg squats/deads, etc. It'll improve your strength and conditioning so that you don't get an empty bar pinned down to your chest the first time you try to bench it.
