A Lift A Day-sample routine for beastly size and strength

  • Marco_Simone
    10 May 07, 17:59
    Mon-deadlift(10 sets)

    conventional deadlifts from an elevated platform-4x5
    rack pulls-3x5
    static holds-3x15-20 seconds

    Tues-military or bench press(12 sets)

    military or bottom position bench presses-4x5
    partial military or bench presses-3x5
    dumbbell overhead or bench presses-2x10
    lockouts-3x15-20 secs

    Wed-GPP

    Thurs-row(12 sets)

    rows-4x5
    one arm dumbbell rows-2x10
    pull ups-4xfailure
    static hold-2x15-20 seconds

    Fri-squat(11 sets)
    bottom position squats-4x6
    partial squats-2x6
    lockouts-2x15-20 secs
    lunges-3x12

    Sat-rest
    Sun-GPP

    Training parameters

    Rest:1.5-2 mins
    Tempo:2120
    Intensity:80% of 1RM for straight sets,125% of 1RM for supramaximal sets,1 rep short of failure
    Duration:should be in and out of the gym within 50-60 mins and that includes warm up,cool down,changing and bathing.

    You might think what the heck??? Only 40+ sets for the entire body during the entire week? I blast my biceps with 60-80 sets alone for the whole week! Ya,that's the reason why your arm size is almost the same as it was when you first started training.This was one of the ways the old timers a hundred years ago trained and they were much much stronger than modern day trainees with their Space Age machines,supplements validated by Ivy League university research...Give this program a try,you would be pleasantly surprised by the results.
