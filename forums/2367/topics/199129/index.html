    Exercises to build freakish forearms

    Isolations(Should be patronised the least):

    1)Wrist curls
    2)Reverse wrist curls
    3)Wrist rolls
    4)Wrist rotations
    5)Hammer curls, thx for exodus123 for the reminder.

    http://www.bodybuilding.com/fun/md55.htm

    Compound movements(Patronise this more):

    1)Upright rows/shrugs (Alternate them)
    2)Deadlifts
    3)Curl grip pull-ups(Palms facing you)
    4)Curl grip lateral pulldown
    5)Any form of rowing(Cable rows, barbell/dumbbell rows, dragon boat, kayaking etc etc)

    No 1 and 2 from compounds especially will also build a freakish and thick neck.
