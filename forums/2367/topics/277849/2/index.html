    Different. The cable crunches work the same way as traditional crunches. You're creating movement, not resisting it. You're using your core to pull down the cable, and then just letting it fling you back up once you're at the bottom. With the EAD, you're using your hip to bring the band down, and then resisting the back extension with your core, which is one of the functions of the core.

    Anyway, this is a pretty advanced exercise that most people probably don't need to be doing for a while.
