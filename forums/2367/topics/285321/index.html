    I can assure you, that most people despite being able to bench 90kg might not even be able to handle a 60kg military press.Simply put, their lower back is too weak to support.

    There was this case where i persuaded a guy to try military presses, he can bench 95kg but struggles with a light weight like 42.5kg with military presses.If you are sceptical about this, try experimenting it with a person who does bench press only all the time.

    I am not saying bench press is bad but rather over- prescirbed.I recommend doing/alternating both.

    Military press is this case is standing up with dumbbells or barbells.
