    The rules for ALL sports are the same.Soccer, basketball etc etc..

    1)Protein to recover
    2)Carbohydrates for energy
    3)Vitamins to keep immune system well

    Yoghurt, cereal, fruits.

    Meal 2

    Sandwich + grandola bar.

    Meal 3

    Roasted chicken + brown rice + 10 egg whites + fruits.

    Meal 4

    Banana and handful of almonds.

    Meal 5

    Cabbage soup with meat and other stuff + rice + a fruit

    Meal 6

    Glass of milk + sandwich
