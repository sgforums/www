    Today, i covered TPY Clubfitt.Photos were taken at 1115am+

    Equipment available

    "Cardio" corner:

    -Treadmills
    -Stationary bikes
    -Stepper
    -Rowing machine

    Machines:

    -Leg press
    -Lying Leg curl
    -Quad/leg ext.
    -Shoulder press
    -Vertical chest press
    -Pec Dec
    -Dips machine
    -Seated Row machine
    -Preacher Curl machine
    -Ab Crunch Machine

    Free weight corner:

    -1 adjustable decline bench(Can only vary the angles of decline)
    -Flat barbell bench press station
    -Incline barbell bench press station
    -Dumbbells from 1kg to 30kg.However, they do not have 27.5kg DBs.It jumps from 25kg to 30kg.The rest have 2.5kg increments.
    -Smith machine
    -Assisted pullups/dips
    -Power rack with a chin up bar
    -Lat pulldown
    -Rack of barbells(Not oly bb)
    -2 flat benches(Meant for using DBs)
    -1 adjustable bench(Meant for using DBs)

    Cons:

    -Can be rather cramped during peak hours.
    -Small toilet, hard to change
    -Aircon that is too damn cold sometimes
    -Many of the equipment is rusty and aged(This might deter some people but i dont really care)
    -As mentioned, DB jumps from 25kg to 30kg without a 27.5kg DB.The rest comes with 2.5kg increments

    Pros:

    -The instructor Daniel Tan.Reccomends practical exercises.

    -Friendly staff.Not so anal about trivial matters.Offered first-aid when my finger was cut.
