    Lets say you're doing a pull/push or upper/lower split

    To train back,all you need is

    FOR WIDTH

    chin ups for 3 sets

    FOR THICKNESS

    any form of rows for 3 sets of 6-8

    FOR POWER

    HEAVY rack pulls for 3 sets of 4-5

    The back is a very large body part

    The chins will take care of predominatly the lats,the rows will work mostly the mid back and the rack pulls will fry the traps and lower back.

    That's not to say,for example,chins only work the lats,they also,to a lesser but still very significant extent,other part of the back especially midback and traps.Same goes for the other 2 movements.

    Good luck!
