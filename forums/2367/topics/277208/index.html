    You will be in a world of pain when you do ITB foam rolling for the first time, even with a regular density foam roller. Tennis balls are for more advanced users who already have the major knots broken and need to get deeper past the guarding muscles, or at a more concentrated area. You will need something with sufficient stiffness so no, pillows obviously won't work. If your gym has a medicine ball or swimming noodles those *might* work, but they're a very very poor substitute. You should be foam rolling anyway so just get one. It's only $20.
