    I have been to the ones at Clementi,Delta,Kallang.

    Delta(5 mins walk from Tiong Bahru Plaza)

    pros

    -got 2 30 kg babies which I aim to overhead press by new year.

    cons

    -got a lot of ah beng poser types who diao people
    -not very accesible(have to take bus and walk 15 mins unless i wanna take 2 buses)
    -cannot do weighted dips and chin ups(there is a notice explicitly telling you not to bring the free weights to the machine area)

    Clementi(at Clementi central)

    pros

    -can do weighted dips and chin ups

    cons

    -got a lot of ah beng poser types who diao people

    -no power rack

    -dumbbells max is 20kg I think

    -the dip bars seem to be flimsy. When I use 1 plate,keep shaking...

    Kallang(Inside national stadium)

    pros

    -nice environment there(always go to stadium waterfront area chill out after I train,last time got Leisure park even better,go and chill at the food court)

    -got power rack

    -the people there are friendly,a few of the mat BB type are very nice people

    cons

    -cannot do weighted dips(dip bar over at machine area)

    -dumbbells too light

    planning to visit EVERY SINGLE clubfitt gyms around Singapore,next stop= Hougang,Toa Payoh,Jurong East.Watch this space

    went to Bishan clubfitt yesterday.

    I did weighted hammers,militaries and rack deadlifts.

    Pros

    -the pull up bar set up got hammer grip
    -got dips bar
    -there is a power rack

    Cons

    -the music is too loud and too distracting
    -the color of the wall-yellow-is very glaring
    -u cannot check your form when doing rack work

    As usual,there were the curlers and the tag team partners and they were all teens.

    2 teens in singlets using a single barbell and doing heavy partial curls together if you know what i mean.

    After completing the set,they walked around the gym with this "aint i impressive" look on their faces.

    I can only shake my head and carry on with my training.

    When I took out my dip belt,they probably had no idea what was that.They must be thinking:"How come this lifting belt got metal chains 1 ah???"

    Then when I did my weighted hammers,they thought I was an alien from Planet Zorocs.

    went to pierre's territory today hougang clubfitt

    The place is pretty new and big. Seemed to have the private gym feeling when I was there.

    The plates there are hexagonal and there are gaps in them to facilitate handling and loading-Good

    Facilities wise,similar to most other public gyms.Dumbbells are very light though.I think the max is only 20kg. I noticed there are more machines too compared to other gyms.

    As for the patrons,the same old story la...you know...

    PS: Next time if i wanna explore,not gonna do actual training in them coz the change of environment actually affected my training.

    If I wanna explore,I will go on a non training day and do some bodyweight movements.
