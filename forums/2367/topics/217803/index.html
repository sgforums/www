    Let me share with you.

    I started weight training in Nov 2005(1st anniversary liao!)

    For the first 2 months(Nov and Dec 2005),I used the Positions of Flexion methodology preached by IRONMAN Magazine's Steve Holman. Note this is a pure bodybuilding protocol with its main emphasis on muscle mass.Was on a 4 day upper/lower split. Medium reps(8-10) used.

    During this 2 months,I gained 1 in on my arms,dropped 1 in from my waist and gained 4 kg in overall bodyweight.

    Then around the start of 2006,I switched to a medium reps(8-10) full body 3X/week routine. I was on this for 2 months and I gained another 1 in on my arms and 2 kg in bodyweight.

    Only around late Feb did I switch to the low rep/low volume 3X/per week full body routine that I have been advocating to death and which I have been following till now. I have gained 3kg bodyweight and another 1.5 in on my arms with this.
