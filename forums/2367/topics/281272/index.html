    To supplement means to aid or assist.No dictionary defines supplement as miracle.Protein powder is protein in powder form to put it simply.

    These areas must be fulfilled first.

    1)Diet to feed the muscles adequately
    2)Good training for best results
    3)Proper rest for maximal human growth hormone release

    You can take all the protein powders or pills like a pumped lab rat but if these 3 areas are not satisfied.No results would be seen.
