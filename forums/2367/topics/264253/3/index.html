    First of all, i just want to say different people have different bodies, at different stages of fitness & different requirement. Saying from my own experiences, i was once a obese 90kg @ 1.70m

    At that stage, i would say for me, it is almost impossible to do interval run or sprint, let alone completing a 2.4km jog without walking. Even climbing stairs already makes you breathless. People who are in this stage is not suitable for any intensive exercise and should condition your bodies first like slow jogging. For me i condition my body about 1 to 2 months, 4-5 times of slow jog per week until i was able to complete 2.4km easily without walking. My weight drop to about 85kg and was stagnant for the 3rd month.

    It was then i begin to control what i was eating. Standard things like no fried food, no skins, no soft or sweet drinks, no junk food. The amount of food is the same but minus those mentioned meaning u eat more veg, fruits, water to fill up ur stomach. All these so call control plus the continue jogging, i managed to drop my weight to 80 (roughly abt 1mth 1kg) and remain stagnant again.

    As the jogging is beginning to stress my knees, i reduce to twice a week and i change to 2 sessions of gym overall workout plus weekend go swim. After 6 months, my wt is 72 now. My target is 68 4 more to go
    Hope my experience can help ...
