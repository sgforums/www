Let's talk movements not muscles

  • OPT
    30 Dec 06, 09:40
    When you are looking at selecting exercises don't base it on muscles that are being trained, rather look at movement patterns. If I am talking to a coach or training and they start talking about what muscles they are training and not what movements I know that they don't know much.

    here are a list that you should be thinking about.

    Horizontal Push
    Horizontal Pull
    Vertical Push
    Vertical Pull
    Quad Dominant
    Hip Dominant
    Accessories (knee/elbow dominant, etc.)

    There needs to be a balance between all theses movements.

    Think about is then post any questions.
