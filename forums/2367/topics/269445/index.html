    weight lifting plus cardio

    Weight lifting alone does help, but add in cardio for a faster weight loss. Weight lifting is for more muscles to increase your basal metabolic rate, which burns fats even you are 'resting' and recovering. Cardio burn fats only when you are doing it. A combination of both will give you a greater effect.

    Diet is also important, the types you eat, the amount you eat, and the time you eat.
