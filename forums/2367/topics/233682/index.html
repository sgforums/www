    26 Jan 07, 09:20
    cardio - none

    I think you should pair the exercises - you can get a lot more work done in a short amount of time.

    If you want to do a 3 day split then you will need to slip the following

    Day 1
    Horizontal Push, Horizontal Pull

    Day 2
    Hip Dom, Quad Dom

    Day 3
    Vertical Pull, Vertical Push

    Sample of how to pair exercises

    A1. Bench Press
    A2. Bent Over Barbell Rows

    You can rest 60 seconds between A1 and A2

    You also want to mix the reps/tempo/TUT(time under tension) week to week - one week do 5 reps next week do 10 then repeat.
