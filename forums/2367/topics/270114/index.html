    Ironically people who have lower back injuries are the ones who usually have strong lower backs. The reason is that the lower back takes most of the stress because of other muscles that are either weak or just inactivated...commonly the glutes are the main muscle that is asleep.

    Look for exercises that resist/prevent motion, and start slow. I can think of reverse hypers.
