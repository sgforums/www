    Hi All,

    After a successful inaugural AsterConference in Kuala Lumpur in October 2006 and
    on Asterconference ASEAN Roadshow in Jakarta, Manila & Bangkok in January and
    February 2007, the conference will now take the road to three other ASEAN cities: Singapore,
    Ho Chi Minh City and Hanoi. AsterConference is a dedicated conference on Asterisk, the
    powerful IP PBX software that has taken the world by storm. This conference will bring to each
    locality a panel of regional and local speakers to share their knowledge and expertise on the potential
    and benefits of what Asterisk can bring to a corporation of all sizes. Targeting both potential and
    existing user audiences, the conference will help the audience to have a wider understanding of what
    Asterisk is, its powerful and cost-saving applications, and hear testimonies of how companies have
    benefited greatly from it.

    The second league of Asterconference ASEAN Roadshow 2007 will take place in three cities:
    Singapore( May 7th 2007 ), Ho Chi Minh City( May 9th 2007 ) & Hanoi( May 11th 2007 ).
