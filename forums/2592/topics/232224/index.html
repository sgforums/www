    gakkoo school
    goro about, around
    hayai early
    hajimarimasu begin, start
    ji o'clock
    han half past
    terebi television, TV
    eiga movie
    rekishi history

    _____________________________________________________________

    Suzuki: Ash!ta gakkoo e ikimasu ka.
    (Are you going to school tomorrow? )

    Miller: Hai, ikimasu.
    ( Yes, I am. )

    Suzuki: Nan ji ni ikimasu ka.
    (What time are you going?)

    Miller: Hachi ji goro desu.
    ( About eight o'clock. )

    Suzuki: Hayai desu ne. Kurasu wa nan ji ni hajimarimasu ka. (Wow, that's early. What time does class start? )

    Miller: Hachi ji han desu. Demo kurasu no mae ni tomodachi to aimasu kara.
    ( At eight thirty. But before class I'm going to meet a friend. )

    Suzuki: Kurasu wa nan ji ni owarimasu ka.
    (What time are your classes over? )

    Miller: San ji desu.
    ( At three o'clock. )

    Suzuki: Jaa, sono ato uchi e kimasen ka. Terebi o isho ni mimashoo.
    ( Well, after that can you come over? Let's watch TV. )

    Miller: Nani ga arimasu ka.
    ( What's on? )

    Suzuki: Amerika no eiga ga arimasu. Mearii-san mo kimasu.
    ( There's an American movie. Mary's coming, too. )

    Miller: Jaa, sono ato rekishi o benkyoo shimashoo.
    ( Okay, after that we can study some history. )
