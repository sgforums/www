    here is how it to understand each other better , when u want to approach someone , want to get to know that person well and be friends

    ** konnichiwa , tomodachi ni nareru? watashi wa namae wa pikachu desu. 19 sai desu.. anata wa?

    konnichiwa = hi, tomodachi ni nareru = can i be your friend? watashi namae wa pikachu desu= my name is pikachu, 19 sai desu= im 19 this year, anata wa= and you?

    they will normally reply u as the following, ii desu, Mary(name) desu, 17 sai desu, dozo yoroshiku

    ii desu = ok, my name is mary, im 17, nice to meet you

    dozo yoroshiku / o ai deki ti ureshii desu = both is the same nice to meet you.

    hai / un~ = yes

    iie = no

    arigatoo = thanks

    arigatou gozaimasu = thank you (polite)

    daijoobu desu/ daijoobu dayone/ ki ni shinaide = never mind, no problem.

    shusshin wa doko desu ka = where are you from.

    doko ni sunderu no ? = where do you live?

    singaporu ni san deru = i live in singapore

    singaporu = (singapore)

    singaporu-jin = (singaporean)
