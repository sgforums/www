    The Yomiuri Shimbun

    A branch manager of Yamato Holdings Co., a leading door-to-door parcel delivery company, was arrested on suspicion of paying for sex with a minor, it was learned Tuesday.

    According to police, Naoyuki Emi paid a 17-year-old part-time worker 5,000 yen for sex in his car at a parking lot in Sagamihara, Kanagawa Prefecture, in early December.

    Emi, 49, became acquainted with the girl two years ago through a mobile phone dating Web site when she was a third-year student at middle school. He called her once or twice a month and had sex with her in exchange for amounts ranging from 2,000 yen to 5,000 yen, the police said.
