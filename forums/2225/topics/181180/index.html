    Corporate Profile

    A leading brand in the Asia Pacific, Pokka Corporation (Singapore) Limited was established as Pokka Japan's Asian flagship in 1977 and was listed on the Singapore Exchange in 1994. The company has subsidiaries in Singapore and Hong Kong and a significant interest in a contract manufacturer in Malaysia. The group's principal activities are the production and sales of beverages primarily under the Pokka trademark, and operating restaurants. It currently has 10 food establishments in Singapore, 19 in HongKong, 1 in Macao and 1 in Shautou.
