    Ugg is short for ugly, haha, but it does help. There are other versions on the market that aren't as bulky. I like chukka boots myself. Clarks makes a very nice and super comfy version. You may also like to try knee high socks (not cotton though) and moisturise, moisturise, moisturise. HTHs. For me, tights under pants really help, coupled with knee highs. It really helps keep me comfy in the winter. You can also get some silk thermal undies. I  have a pair from Eddie Bauer which I swear by. They really keep you toasty without the bulk. 

    As for shoes, canvas has an open weave and lets in the cold. Leather has no give and doesn't provide any warmth, imho. I like suede for winter.
