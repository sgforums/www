    I headed over to Kohl's this morning, armed with a 15% "Black Friday" discount card that they had mass mailed the week prior, with the intent of getting a few pairs of jeans. I picked out a couple of Lee jeans which were priced at $29 apiece (before discount) and proceeded to the cash registers, only to notice that the checkout line snaked all the way to the back of the store.

    Screw that. I wasn't about to waste nearly an hour waiting in line. I returned the jeans to the shelf and drove over to my favourite farming implement/hardware store, Mills Fleet Farm, to see if they had any good deals going. As I strolled through the clothing section, I noticed they had the same Lee jeans on sale for $13, so I bought them there, along with several hundred rounds of ammunition from their hunting department.

    After that, I headed over to Best Buy and picked up a number of Blu-ray discs of older titles for $8 apiece.
