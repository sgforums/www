    don't know when I'll be back again ....

    Yeps ... I'm returning home to Singapore this coming friday .... the past few years has been an interesting and exciting odyssey for me ....

    I've camped in below freezing temperatures, got caught in a snow storm, survived -59 celcius weather, got drunk in a cowboy bar, experienced a working farm complete with warm cow patties, had run ins with flower children, memonites, red necks, rig pigs and biker gangs, drug dealers, welfare bums, vietnamese gangs, korean gangs, indian gangs, hell riders ... as well as kind and warm and gracious people whose kindness towards me canceled out all the pricks ... people who'd invited a lonely foreign young man into their homes during thanks giving and christmas, who guided me and helped me navigate a foreign land when I first came here ... and the assorted friends who has crossed my path here, Sudanese refugees still bearing the scars of their country, chinese dissidents, and chinese princelings, the swedish and german exchange students, the farmboys geniuses who've never left saskatchwan in their lives ... the cranky leftist pot smoking arts profs, the ladies whom I met, in german class, in calculus 221, in geology lab, my student in cmpt 111 tutorial, at the residence, at alexander's bar, at the Pat's, at the overdrive, and the hose and hydrant, in the natural science library, in the arts library, in comm 205, cmpt 405, and my TA in geol 211 ... fond memories all ... thank you ...

    I caught trout, walleyes, perch, whitefish, pike ... witnessed auroras, ran over a deer (don't ask) ... ran over countless gophers (gopher suicides) ... swam in an icy lake, tanned on a nude beach (don't imagine it) ... survived a run in with a black bear ... trekked in the rockies, wandered around the coves of the pacific northwest, and the rolling prairies and fields golden with canola and wheat ... heard the wolves and the loons cry at night, got my piece of parchment, got a job .... got to see the best and worst of Canada, of humanity ...

    and ..... as I feel that it's only appropriate that a mod of this particular forum be based overseas as well, I'll be stepping down shortly after .... to make way for

    Jetta ! .....

    *applause ! ... *confetti ! ........
