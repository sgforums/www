    sujee cookies

    ingredients

    455g ghee

    340g icing sugar

    795g plain flour

    method

    1. cream ghee and sugar for 5 mins, add flour and knead into a soft dough.  leave covered for 4 hours

    2. preheat oven at 120 deg c

    3. shape into size of marlbe.  put on a greased baking sheet spaced well apart

    4. bake in moderate oven for 20-25 mins

    5. cool on wire rack and keep in airtight container

     

    recipe from s'pore cooking (leong yee soo)

     

    have fun!
