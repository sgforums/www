  • hatz
    3 Oct 08, 07:22

    geneva's great! love it here. so many places to go to and the piece de la resistance is obviously the lake, which i never get bored of. beautiful to run along it during warmer times and chill by during the cold seasons. i've only been here for 5 months so i've not experienced winter here yet, but i was looking through photos of my friends' and apparently it snows quite a bit here, but i doubt it's as much as what you get up there in toronto.

    being a singaporean, no place will ever meet our insatiable appetite for food, but there are many alternatives. geneva's condensed with people from all over the world and somehow everyone's been able to carve out a home away from home here, so it's pretty alright. i was talking to a colleague of mine today and he said that there's a jack's place right here in geneva, so i should go check it out. price wise, it's decent enough. much better than when i stayed in uk for 2 years. not only did that burn a hole in my pocket, it burned the whole damn pants as well.

    so far so good. not too bored with the place yet. one thing good about switzerland is that it's in the center of western europe, so basically i am a stone's throw from anywhere. paris, milan, amsterdam baby! if you're too bored you can always rely on their very, very efficient train network here to go whereever.

    yup so that's about it about life in geneva. where are you guys at?
