    Just go into the bank... you will need some id (like passport, medibank private, etc) to meet the point requirement.

    Please also apply for a Tax File Number which you can do it online (http://www.ato.gov.au/individuals/content.asp?doc=/content/38760.htm).  It's not compulsory for opening the bank account but it just helps you not to get taxed unnecessarily. 

    The 4 major banks here are:

    - Westpac (www.westpac.com.au)

    - Commonwealth (www.commbank.com.au)

    - ANZ (www.anz.com)

    - National Australia Bank (www.nab.com.au)

    Visit their website and find out which one suits you the most.  Good luck!
