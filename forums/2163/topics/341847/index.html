    the 2 places that you've mentioned are radically different....it all depends on what you like. if you enjoy the coastal scenery and love the sun-sand-sea, then GC is the place for you. there are also numerous theme parks at GC i.e. seaworld, dreamworld, movie world etc.

    Do the GoldCoast.

    Bring plenty of money and sleep as much as you can on the plane, so you can wake up early to take the bus to the Worlds.  Spent at least 1 day in each world.

    Bring Bikinis too so you can have fun at Seaworld too.

    And be careful of sandflies bites.  I ganna and my face swelled so big and I was so uncomfortable, I didnt enjoy much.

    Avoid those tours that means going to three places at once.  You wont have the time to enjoy it.

    Go to Sunshine Coast and visit the reefs. (thats what the biknis are for too.)  Rent a car and drive there in the early morning.  Laze at the cafes when you are tired.

    And if possible, get a service apartment, but avoid cooking, since you wont have time to finish all the cooking oil, soy sauce & condiments.  Even for one week stay, you are better off buying frozen stuffs to microwave and stay in when you dont feel like going out.

    Perth, I've been, except for the drive to Freementle market & the other drive to the oldest town near Perth & the koala zoo, there is not much there.
