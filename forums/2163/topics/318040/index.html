    The greatest earthquake of Chinese history estbegan at 14:58, May 12,2008 ,with its centre in southwestern China and its wave affecting all over China and countries around.

        Chinese army was soon started up to the aid the victims and up to now more than 100 000 soldiers have entered the damaged area. The international aid groups have also arrived there ,including the Japanese professional aid group. Every Chinese are donating their money and blood ,and the international donation is also going on . Human being always unites so strongly when faced with disaster.

     

         May 19 , May 20, May 21 are difinted as Memorial Day .During the three days China will half lower its national flag including every Chinese office abroad. For three minute following 14:58, May 19, all sirens in China will work.
