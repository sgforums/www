    Be on the safe side.  Go to the Australian Webpages to check on how to register yourself visiting there, as well as applying for a visa if needed.

    Then, if you are Singaporean, go to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs webpages to check on, and register that you are going overseas.

    And also, if you are male Singaporean, do check with the CMPB office or at least let them know you are travelling out of Singapore.

    Please print out your itinery and bring it along with your passport.  Cos you may need to show proof that you have a ticket home at the air ticks and custom counters.
