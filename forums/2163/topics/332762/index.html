    My favourite recipe for winters, cold nights. A pot of piping hot soup with garlic or crusty bread to dip in! Yum!                                               

    Minestrone Soup

    Recipe serves 2 - 3 generously.

    • 4 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 garlic cloves, smashed
    • 1 medium onion, sliced
    • 1 medium potato, diced
    • 2 small sticks celery, chopped (love the tops for the veggies, the bottom for the sweetness)
    • Half a carrot,diced
    • 1 can of diced tomatoes
    • 1/4 tsp dried oregano
    • 1 tsp dred basil
    • 1 chicken/veggie stock cube
    • 3 cups water
    • Salt & Pepper to taste

    *Note: You can also use McCormicks' Italian herbs found at most Supermarkets in place of the herbs above. Use 1 tbsp.

    1. Heat olive oil, then add onions and garlic. Sweat for 3 - 5 min or until onions are transparent.
    2. Add poatoes, celery, carrot and stir fry for 5 min, allowing the flavouring to mingle and oils to infuse.
    3. Dump in can of diced tomatoes, stir for 2 min.
    4. Add 3 cups water, stock cube, herbs, salt and pepper, and cover for 45 - 50 min to boil under low to medium heat.
    5. Taste periodically to ensure flavours and saltiness is well-balanced and add salt and pepper as needed. Stir periodically to ensure proper distribution.
    6. Serve with garlic bread, or any crusty bread!

    Very yummy.

    Esp sitting outside the front porch or balcony with a bowl of soup during those chilly nights. PHWOAR!
