    We played human musical chairs but alamak.....the wives beri pai seh to sit on the laps of other husbands leh.  One or two actually just stand in front of the guy when the music stopped.  kakakka...  Anyways, you can guess that the eventual winner is a husband and wife team.

    Then, we had dancing, some ballroom, some latin and some freestyling.  For a start, we formed a circle and each of us took turn to perform in the centre of the circle.

    It was a wonderful night.  Great company, great food and thank God for good weather to allow the party to happen.
