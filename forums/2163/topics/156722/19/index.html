    Say

    USD 3.50 = 1 US gallon

    At USD 1 = SGD 1.53

    SGD 5.36 = 1 Gallon

    2.63 US gallons = 10 litres

    SGD 5.36 X 2.63 US gallons = 10 litres

    SGD 14.10 = 10 litres
    In US:

    SGD 1.41 = 1 litre.

    In Singapore:

    Shell V-Power: SGD 1.793 / litre

    Shell 98: SGD 1.581 / litre

    Shell 95: SGD 1.514 / litre

    Shell 92: SGD 1.485 / litre

    **The assumption is you are in US and using US gallon, if you using British gallon which is 2.2 gallon = 10 litres, the US pump prices will be even cheaper.

    Therefore the petrol in US is definitely cheaper than Singapore.
