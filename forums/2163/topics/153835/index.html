  • ^spidee^
    21 Sep 05, 18:07
    Clothing

    -Long John (underwear that keeps you warm)
    -Windbreaker/jacket/jumper (preferably more than 1, thick and thin)
    -Long sleeve T-shirts

    Household

    -Heater (most homes come with a heater installed. Otherwise, buy a portable one, usually less than S$50).
    -Quilt (ensure that it's thick to keep you warm through the winter nights)

    Food

    -You'll find yourself eating more than you usually do in Singapore.
    -Chicken soup, pumpkin soup, winter melon soup, you get the idea...
    -Coffee, tea or hot chocolate.
