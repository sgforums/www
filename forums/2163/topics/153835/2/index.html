    Here is a list of what you REALLY need when you're in a cold country. There was once I thought the weather wasn't THAT cold and ended up shivering my way back. The weather changes during winter very drastically and suddenly. So you must always be prepared for it.

    -Moisturizing cream and lip balm! For boys and girls. Winter countries tend to dry up your skin and you'll get chipped lips. It'll hurt. Put alot of lip balm.

    -Long John!

    -Boots or thick shoes. Don't belittle shoes. They help alot!

    -Thick socks.

    -Umbrella. I am serious. No no. Not for raining. It's for snow and wind. Sometimes during winter, the wind is strong and it pierces your face. Bring an umbrella.

    -Gloves.

    And the rest are the norm. Sweater, coats blah blah blah. But you must wear at least 3 pieces of clothings underneath your coats! Even if it's alittle too warm, you'll be outside the cold more! So think about it!
