    So I have sort of settled down in SG....after X months…lawfully employed.  

    During the 8 months i have learned that due to the global downturn plenty of folks had wanted to come to work in singapore ........headhunters in Singapore are flooded with calls and resumes from the US, Canada, the EU other than the immediate Asian countries to apply for jobs in Singapore.

    Jobs vacancies posted are applied thru internet from all over the World. So if you think why companies don't response for overseas candidates because you are not the only person applied.

    Companies no longer have Human resource for recruitment, but Talent Recruiter most likely they are outsource within HR department.   

    In Singapore Salaries and experiences does not necessary come hand in hand. With the appreciation of SG currency Salary is negotiated at Price Point. Cost of living is pretty high in Singapore so do not assume your US salary is going to pay for living expenses in Singapore.

    On the good news to All Singaporean.....still there is a direction for Singapore organization to prefer to hire local Citizen first.....!!!! Because of excessive foreigners proportion.

    I met plenty of returnees from NY, L.A. Indian profession from US, and European...........some of them lived so closed together when i was in Los Angeles.......!    

    So if you are overseas and for employment reason have to return to Sing. If the Heat don't kill you....You probably can survived..... not too bad ...cause i have met lots of returnees already waiting for the next jump....heeee...!!!!

    Every morning I take the train to work….the first leg of my journey I am one of the few Singaporean in the train most are foreign workers……and foreign financial consultants.

    So how’s my work...............every morning I got into office.....I say ......."Ohaiyo to my  colleague.....than "Ang yong ha sa yo"  .....follow by Bonjourno.......than Sawadeeca..to .....and nanmastay to my working buddy.......and plenty of Malaysian ....Lah.......

    I work with my vendors also happen to be Filipinas, Malaysian, other asian.....

    So on a day 2 day basis I found myself have very limited contact with Local Singaporean….Did I say I return to Singapore!!!! The only time I am reminded I am in Singapore is during lunch time I go to the canteen and order my Chicken Rice.

    Thank God it still taste the same.....
