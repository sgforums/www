    I just checked Amtrak's website, and they do have services that run from Washington, DC, to Los Angeles. It's a two-day trip and the one-way coach class fare ranges from US$250 to US$400.

    Airfare between Washington, DC, and Los Angeles vary tremendously in price depending on the carrier and intended time of travel. Your best bet would be to sign on to any one of the travel planning sites like Travelocity or Expedia to see what prices they come up with for fares between those two cities. You'll generally get the best price by purchasing a return ticket, as a one-way ticket is probably going to cost significantly more than half of a return ticket.
