    I recently spent a week in northeastern Montana at my cousin's farm in Lustre. Our (my parents and I) visit coincided with an event they've held annually for 42 years called Schmeckfest (tasting festival). It's done to raise money to help run their Christian High School.

    They serve a meal, have a silent and regular auction of items made by members of the community and also items donated by businesses in the area. Entertainment is also provided by people from the community and members of the school.

    The food is all prepared by members of the community.
    They served 787 meals during the 5-6hr event.
