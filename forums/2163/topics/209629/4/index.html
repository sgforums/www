    Specially for udon!

    Made these last nite. Actually sonny bugged me into it. He loves it!



    Recipe:

    For the fillings:

    chicken cut in cubes
    curry powder
    onions
    potatoes

    method:

    season chicken with curry powder - set aside
    fry chopped onions till soft then add cubed potatoes
    see potatoes slightly cooked, add chicken and salt

    stir fry till meat is cooked. dish out and set aside

    hard boiled eggs - cut up

    Pastry:

    420g flour
    1/2 tsp salt
    180 g margarine
    1 egg yolk, beaten
    3/4 cup cold water

    sift flour into bowl and add salt. rub in margarine until mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
    add egg yolk and water
    mix to form a soft dough

    Final Stage:

    take a piece of pastry, roll out to a circle
    fill up with chicken fillings n egg
    seal n pinch

    heat oil for deep frying
    fry till golden brown
    drain on paper towel

    Good luck and enjoy!!! we did!
