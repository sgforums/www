    okay, here's the recipe for steamed chicken rice, in small portions,
    to make larger portions like a whole chicken, just use a conventional steamer,

    1. Marinate chicken thighs or quaters in light soy sauce, a bit of salt, sesame seed oil .. let it sit for around half an hour to soak in the juices,

    2. wash the rice, add salt, garlic cloves, sliced ginger, and pandan leaves if you can get them (frozen ones can be found at asian supermarkets ), plus oil and salt, put the steamer tray with the chicken on top, add a few loose pieces of garlic and ginger slices,

    3. press the cook button and your done ! ...
