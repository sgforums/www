    I think the most important things to consider when buying a car are:
    - ease of maintainence (including repairs and obtaining spare parts)
    - fuel economy
    - safety features (airbags, brakes etc)
    - alloy wheels (good for girls because they are lighter)

    You need to know what you need the car for. A 4 wheel (all wheel) drive vehicle is usually more fuel consuming than a 2 wheel. Try to compare about economy of different cars (no of litres per 100km). If you spend a lot of time in the car, you want a car with comfortable seats. Some of old Nissan car seats (esp the passengers seats) are not very comfy, not sure about the new one though, so check it out.

    The bigger and more powerful the car is, the more fuel consuming it is.

    Anyway, have fun and enjoy your car shopping. Hope you can find what you want soon!!
