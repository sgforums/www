    Got to Osaka and checked into the hotel a couple of hours ago. Went to the Shinosaka train station and got some dinner. Back in the hotel now. The hour-long flight from Minneapolis to O'Hare was uneventful, but the 14-hour flight from O'Hare to Narita was not enjoyable at all. The economy class seat was lacking in both pitch and width, and even though I got an aisle seat, it didn't help that the SOB next to me seemed to think the two-inch-wide armrest belonged to him. HIs shoes were off for most of the flight--which wouldn't be an issue if his feet didn't smell. The one-hour flight from Narita to Osaka's Itami Airport was fairly smooth, but by the time I got to Osaka, the strain from not getting any sleep for nearly 24 hours was beginning to tell. Will post some pictures later. Tired as hell now.
