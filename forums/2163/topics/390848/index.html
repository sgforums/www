    How do you guys celebrate? Do you guys get together with other Sgporeans or overseas Chinese to celebrate the day?

    Here, life goes on. The Viet community holds their Tet Festival at the Stadium. They have food vendors, Viet culture and a Miss Vietnam contest. 

    We have a small CNY celebration downtown on the 20th & 21st. They have lion dances, food, vendors, etc. Strange to see people other than Chinese among the lion dance troupe. 

    At home, I whip out the good old electric steamboat and we have at it.  This year, we'll have two guests.
