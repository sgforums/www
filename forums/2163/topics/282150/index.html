    Did you know that there's a foliage hotline fun by the US Forest Service?

    Call 1800-354-4595 and you'll hear information on prime viewing spots across the country. Or you can get online colour reports at www.fs.fed.us/news/fallcolors.

    For an overall look at what's most colourful in New England, check out the interactive map at www.yankeefoliage.com

    Leaves are turning in Virginia and viewing will be good through early November. Check ou the photos and get the lowdown on foliage festivals at www.fallinvirginia.org. Call the state's foliage hotline at 1800-424-5683.

    Foliage peaks in Pennsylvania in October. This snazzy website has live webcams set up at several scenic spots at www.fallinpa.com or call the foliage hotline at 1800-847-4872.

    For California's spectacular show, visit www.visitcalifornia.com

    Happy Fall!
