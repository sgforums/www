    If you are buying a car, you need to register it to a driver's license. Legally, you can drive on a Spore's license for 3 months, after this period, you need to get a WA license.  To get a WA drivers license, all you need to do is to rock up to any Metropolitan licensing centre to take your theory test. After you pass your test, you get your license as you have a full Spore license.

    Check out your Uni sports club for most sports.

    Diving, Ningaloo reef is good for diving. If you like abalone, Esperance is a good spot. Rock Lobster is plentiful in WA. Make sure you know your bag limit.

    As for Treking, I presume that you are thinking of the driving into outback for long expedition. . Go with groups and get a guide. Do not attempt to go into places where the road ends into the desert on your own if you do not know what you are doing. The Desert is hash place and people can get seriously hurt or killed. There will be some form of adventure club in Uni. Check them out.

    Tip: Try not to bring a whole heap of stuff from home. If you can get them in WA buy them here.

    Its cheaper to do an extra pair of glasses in Singapore.

    And medical wont come cheap, so you best be taking good care of yourself.

    1. Take those vitamin C fizzy tablets, throw into your orange juice in the morning.

    2. Change the dish washing sponge once a month, and remember to clean the lint off your dryer as often as you can.

    3. Avoid the casinos, if they become a regular nightly entertainment, you are sunk.

    4, Buy bread, have cellapane wrapper ready so you can wrap four pieces of bread per cellapane wrap and put into the freezer.  When you want to eat, take out a pack of four slices, microwave & toast the bread.

    5. Eggs, you can always boil six out of the dozen, mash the boiled eggs with mayo, chilli sauce & pepper and tupperware them in the fridge. You busy students wont have that much time to cook, so the eggs mayo, bread could become a staple.

    6. You should be bringing some stationary over, forget the A4 size papers, but do bring along some small siccors to leave in the kitchen and the laundry area.
