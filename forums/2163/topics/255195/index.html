    Don't miss out on the wonderful variety of food. You must try:

    Okomiyaki (I call it rubbish pancake. Sinful but oh so yummy!) You can find this at any street corner or food court.

    Ramen. Please please please visit any ramen shop. True heaven! My fave is Miso Kimchee Ramen. Not every ramen shop will have this concoction but you'll enjoy whatever they serve.

    Yakiniku. There are tonnes of yakiniku restaurants but if you can find the one called Sakai, it's cheap and oh so very good!

    Yakitori. This is the best!!! Cheap and good. About 60 yen per stick. You can find this anywhere. The best ones are small stands in alleyways. Yum!!!!

    Oh and since you're in Asakusa, you're near Electric Town. This is one of my fave places to go since I love electronics. You'll find many things here before they hit the world market. Electric Town is a test market for electronics.
