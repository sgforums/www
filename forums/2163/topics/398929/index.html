    If you're at Melbourne Uni, I'd suggest looking at suburbs that are along the axis formed by Sydney Rd, namely Brunswick, Moreland and Coburg, would make transport easier - the trams run straight to Melbourne along Sydney Rd.  I lived in Brunswick for about 7 years, and in that time I never experienced any problems.

    Forget Carlton and Parkville - prices there are absurd.

    Honeymouse is right about the groceries.  The nearest big Asian neighbourhood for you would be Richmond, but Box Hill's pretty good and not too far either.
