    i had only several boxes of stuffs so i used FedDex. it was fast and on time...no fuss at all.

    all the stuffs/boxes were in tact...only one of my plastic cookware had a small crack but they did warn me not to put any breakables in there.

    i also had eye glasses in one of the boxes but they survived the trip from singapore to US.

    i had a special rate...so it's quite inexpensive. i'd definitely use them again if i need to ship more of my stuffs over. but i would never ship big bulky items like furniture....so i've no need for international movers.
