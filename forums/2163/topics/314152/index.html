    Below are the info.

     

    Plans are well underway for the 11th Annual Upland Lemon Festival in Historic Downtown Upland. A project of Main Street Upland, this event celebrates Upland, past & present, including its role in the citrus industry. The Upland Lemon Festival will officially run from 12noon to 9p.m. on Friday, April 25th and 10a.m. to 10p.m. on Saturday, April 26th and from 11a.m. to 6p.m. on Sunday, April 27th.

     

    The festival offers something for everyone. Kids and their parents will enjoy the Children’s Fair, including a petting zoo, pony rides & climbing wall. In addition, kids can enjoy the many “make & take” crafts where only your imagination sets the limits. The now-famous Lemon Pie Eating Contest- a favorite of many “big” kids, is one the highlights of the Community Stage, along with performance by various youth and community groups
