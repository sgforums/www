    Yes, I agree that Northwest is not the best airline out there, but they do offer the most convenient routing from the Twin Cities to Singapore, i.e., MSP-NRT-SIN.

    The only drawback is that the flight gets into Changi at around 1 a.m., and departs at around 7 a.m.

    Their fares are also relatively reasonable. If I book a flight early enough during the off-peak travel season, I can usually secure a return ticket for a little under US$1000. In addition, each flight is long enough to yield sufficient FF miles for a free domestic ticket.

    Of course, with Northwest in Chapter 11 now, it's anyone's guess as to what will happen to them in the long term.
