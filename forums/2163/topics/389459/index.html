    1) They are driving a car that they are not used to. Usually people start their driving hoilday right away on their rental car, did not get enough time to be familiar with it first.

    2) Poor planning - people do not calculate the time they need correctly. They just blindly measure the distance. They did not consider the type of road, and take into consideration that they stop a lot to take photo and explore things. Thus they under estimate the time they needed. Also must take note of the day light time, for some countries at certain season - sunset start to set by 4 or 5 p.m

    3) Do homework on road to take. Some road in area like grampians are not really mean for normal travelling. There are some special road that are for slow nature observation.

    4) Some road oversea - can be very straight and have field on both side, esp when it is between 10+ to 3+ with the heat and the light - can lead to driver to be lost concentration and doze off. 

    5) Crosswind - usually you can feel it, if you driving on straight road easily. Usually it is curve or turning - which crosswind cause accident .
    6) Wild animals - kangroos & emu at grampian. Usually they start coming out in sunset. Outback - even have horses and camels.

    One advice is alway put a few days to stay in the city at the end to to use a buffer - so you do not need to rush, the most eat into those days.
