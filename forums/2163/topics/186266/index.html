    Yup, I'm finally in Seattle. Got in at about 11:45 a.m. local time after a particularly arduous flight seated next to an uncaring mother and her two screaming kids.

    It was raining when we arrived, and it's still raining now. Some call it gloomy, but I don't mind rain. Keeps everything nice and clean.

    The rental vehicle we were initially given had pulsing brakes which, while not a road hazard, made for an uncomfortable ride when coming to a stop. We returned the vehicle and the rental agency set us up with a better, newer vehicle (a 2006 Dodge Neon SXT). Drives great, but...

    ...the traffic here is RIDICULOUS. Once we were off the freeways, we spent more time stopped in traffic than actually moving. Fortunately, the sanity level amongst drivers here is pretty decent, and I did not see any middle fingers flashed.

    No images for now. Will report back later.

    MG
