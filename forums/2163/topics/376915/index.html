    Get PR or a visa that gives you work rights first. Otherwsie, most companies will not even consider your application unless you have exceptional talent.

    Try seek.com.au. Usually companies will want to interview you in person before a job offer so you may need to fly to Aus for interviews, although there are some who might do it over the phone. Also in Australia, they actually do check with your referees checks so make sure your referees are reliable and contactable. Another tip is to change your resume/CV to suit the Aussie workplace. There are many information not required in your CV, e.g. DOB, race, etc. You will also be required to respond to the selection criteria in writing, not merely sending in your CV with a cover letter. Check out the Aussie employment websites - they provide tips on writing a CV and responding to selection criteria.
