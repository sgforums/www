    Tips:

    1) Make sure the bak guas boxes are scattered all around in the luggage.  Preferably a softcase cloth type cheap Army Kit bag as luggage.  And secure your bags zipper with cable ties.

    2) Arrive at the airport during the early morning at LAX or during the late evening where the immigration and custom folks are going off shifts.

    3) And buy those $17-19/- a pack of Bak Guas in plain flavours vacumn packed at Bee Cheng Hiang.  10 pieces of bak gua to a box and if you buy more then 6 boxes, got give you at least 1 box free, depending on their promotion.

    4) Pack in Myjo Instant Mee Poks.  They are not available in USA, last time I checked.  And should be welcome with open arms by folks that will be missing them Mee Poks!
