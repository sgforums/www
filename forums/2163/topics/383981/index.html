    Hi Frizzleflow, are you looking for any temp job so as you are able to source for a more permenent position? Another stupid question, are you able to get the dole as it would make things a whole lot easier.

    There is another place to look for temp employment. I suppose you are currently living in melbourne. Go any centrelink near where you live and ask for a job network card. Also, ask them if they got anything on the cards and will they be able to hook you up with any government job network agency (refer to jobsearch.gov.au).  

    If they are able to hook you up with a job network agency, you will be ask to go down for a quick interview.

    Bear in mind, the jobs on the list are the sort of the things nobody likes to do. Put it this way, I got a job working as a cleaner in an abattior through this method. The job sucks, but it pays the rent and the bills and give you that little more confidence in getting the job that you want. 

    It works for me. Hopefully, it works for you.
