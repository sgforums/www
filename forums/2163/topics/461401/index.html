    Hey!

    I studied in Germany and I'm also living here now. I don't know much about medicine in particular but the quality of education overall is very good.

    I did study Journalism and I've had a really good experience. I managed to get a good job at the local newspaper.

    Even though a lot of German universities offer tuition-free education for internationals, you will still need a good financial backup. A scholarship would be the best thing if you can get one.

    I'd suggest you look and follow updates from universities at http://www.study-in.de/en/ but be sure to read more about studying in Germany and the student life there http://www.mawista.com/en/study-in-germany/

    You'll definitely have a great time studying here. I did, and I stayed here to live too!

    Good luck!
