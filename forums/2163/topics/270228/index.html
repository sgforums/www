    Originally posted by Fatum:

    Well, the pull and push factors for me would include:

    Pull factors:

    1. Parents. Realistically, though, this will eventually cease to be a pull factor.

    2. Food, though not to as great an extent as in previous years.

    3. Friends--at least those who have not yet emigrated from Singapore.

    Push factors:

    1. Better job opportunities/career prospects outside of Singapore.

    2. The year-round hot and humid weather.

    3. The escalating cost of living.

    4. The constant overcrowding and congestion. 6 million people? No thanks.

    5. The disingenuousness and callousness of the political machinery in Singapore whose policies seem geared towards self-aggrandizement and self-enrichment at the expense of the common Singaporean.

    6. The overregulation of society and related lack of personal freedoms and choices.
