    I advise you to do layering for now. You dun need a super duper warm coat for now. Just get one thick-ish one then wear a very thin T-shirt, followed by a long sleeve shirt, followed by the coat. That will keep you warm enough.

    I've ridden motorcycles in winter with that configuration (obviously I had woolen under short + a good coat) and I'm none the worse for wear.

    btw: I've found wool to be the affordable best!

    Disregard advice if you are going to norway, south pole, north pole etc.
