    1. Visit the Space Needle.
    2. Visit Pike's Place Market.
    3. Go on the Boeing plant tour in Everett.
    4. Visit the Bremerton Navy Museum in (duh!) Bremerton.
    5. Visit the Kinokuniya bookstore in downtown Seattle.
    6. A day trip to the Olympia National Park.
