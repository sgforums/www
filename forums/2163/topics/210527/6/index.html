    If anyone gets a chance to go to DC, fly in and out of Reagan National Airport (DCA). The airport is on the Virginia side of the Potomac, next to the Penatgon. If landing south, you'll fly a procedure known as the River Visual. You'll fly south along the Potomac and can see the sights off to the left.Great view, especially at night. It can be nerve wracking for us flying it, because we have to avoid 2 Prohibited Areas - White House and US Capitol.

    Was in DC a week and a half ago. Still freaking hot then. Walked around for 6 hours. Remember Smithsonian museums are all free. If you have time, go to the new Udvar-Hazy museum, part of the Air and Space Museum, near IAD. One of the retired Concordes is housed there.
