    One. You need to be a licenced distributor and has the contract to import & distribute the cargo.

    Two.  You need approvals to unload the cargo.

    Three.  You need approvals to transport the cargo.

    Four.  You need to engage security escorts for the cargo to be transported.

    Five.  You need to show proof of your cargo accepted by the buyer.

    Six. Unless you are licenced, have approvals and has the necessary personnels on standby at a high costs.  You are looking to be hanged from Changi Chalet.

    And oh yes....the bail amount for such cargo items is about a million dollars USD.  So until you can put up that kind of finances with another five million on standby.  Do not attempt to import.

    I suppose you are thinking of moving light stuff. For starters, your would need an end user certificate issue from any foreign government officials. This would allows you to transport your products from your source (Used/2nd hand products from your source). To bring them into Singpore, form a company, be a director, pay $1100 to the police to obtain your license. Using this license, pay 16 bucks apply for import license from license division for your consignment. This would alow you to unload your products from service carriers.  Must be store in secure location. Trasport under escort.
