The Kindness of Strangers ...

  • Fatum
    15 Nov 06, 07:16
    it snowed heavily last night, and the roads were horrible ...

    unfortunately, I drove Michelle into a snowbank this morning at a set of lights ...

    I was cursing and swearing; at the prospect of being late for work, and also at having to fork out some major molah to get a tow truck .... it was so bad her front wheels weren't even touching the ground ! ...

    I was wrong ...

    within 5 minutes, people were pulling up infront and behind me, offering to help ...

    first, a student type on a bicycle (yes, there are lunatics who bike in winter)
    then an east indian chap showed up,
    then an old lady who offered to help call a tow truck,
    then a CBC who turned out to be a colleague,
    then finally a chap turned up in a huge Dodge truck and a tow line and we pushed and pulled Michelle out of it ...

    I was amazed at how many people stopped by to help ... perhaps there's hope in human kind after all eh ...

    I wonder how many of us would do the same for others ? ... to stop by the road and offer assistance to a car broken down or stuck ? ...
