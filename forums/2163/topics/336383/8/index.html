    You're forcing me to recall math formulae that I've long forgotten.

    To convert °C to °F, the formula is: °F = (9/5) * °C + 32

    To find what -43°C would be in °F, we plug -43 into the above formula:

    (9/5) * -43 + 32 = -45.4

    So -43°C is equal to -45.4°F.

    To convert °F to °C, the formula is °C = (5/9) * (°F - 32).

    Easy, huh?
