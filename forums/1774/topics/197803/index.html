    CG Overdrive 2006 - Asia's largest CG conference
    Date: 14th - 16th July 2006 (Fri -Sun)
    Venue: Singapore Expo, Conference Halls A-F , Foyer 1
    Website: www.cgoverdrive.com

    About the Conference:
    The Conference will see 12 esteemed Computer Graphics (CG) Artists from renowned international studios like Pixar Animation Studios, Polygon Pictures, Bluesky Studio and Double Negative etc.

    These CG Artists, who have worked on Animated Feature Films or Movies like "The Incredibles", "Robots", "Harry Potter and The Goblet of Fire", "Stars Wars" and many more will be giving insights, through the various Artists and Knowledge Workshops, into the pipeline of animation production, concept art designs, illustrations, character animation, independent filmmaking and many other interesting topics.
    (For the full conference programme and Speakers' profile, please visit: http://www.cgoverdrive.com/conference.html)

    This is the first time Singapore sees such an impressive line-up of speakers, each an expert in their area of profession, to share and interact with the Asian CG enthuiasts and professionals!!

    Surrounding the Conference, a Festival will also be held, with the following segments:
    1. Exhibition
    2. Digital Art Gallery
    3. Digital Film Screening
    4. Singapore CG Village
    5. Recruitment Talks
    6. Software Clinic
