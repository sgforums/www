    Pao Quan is just part of 5 punches in Xingyi.

    The 5 are Pi (split), Beng (crush), Zhuan (drill), Pao (cannon), Heng (no idea how to explain this).

    Together, these 5 will form the wu xing quan (5-form punches).

    Towards intermediate, one will learn the 12 animals. Depending on which school of Xingyi, different animals will be learnt. 2 main schools: Shanxi and Hubei.

    Advanced learners will combine both wu xing quan and 12 animals into one coherent move.
