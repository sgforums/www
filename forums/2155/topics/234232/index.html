    C major scale

    C D E F G A B C

    D major scale
    -increase each note by 2 semitones:
    so C will become D, D will become E, but E will become F# since there is no E#, and so on...

    in the end you will get

    D E F# G A B C# D

    lets say you want to know the Eb major scale
    increase 3 semitones from C major:

    Eb F G Ab Bb C D Eb

    sharps and flats are added to scales in a specific order. you cant have a scale with # on F and G only.

    (Add # from top to bottom)
    F
    C
    G
    D
    A
    E
    B
    (Add b from bottom to top)

    this 'series/sequence' of F C G D A E B is very important in memorising scales.

    there is an alternative way of using your fingers to count what kind of # and b are present in each scale, but i forget hehe.
