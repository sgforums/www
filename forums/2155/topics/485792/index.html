    I was shopping at the ground level of Rivervale Mall & saw some things from a gift shop, as I intend to buy quite a number of same items so I ask the salesperson (an old auntie) if I can take some time to choose. She was quite ok at first & said no problem, their stuff were kind of like everywhere so that's why I asked her. I chose for a few minutes at a corner & was about to make payment, then this same auntie suddenly come over & shout "you mess up like that later who wants to buy" & grumble things like you can come later & they should not place these items here, etc. I just smile & said I have no time later & your items was already display like this when I came. I told her I have take my pick, would you like to make a count. She just grabbed my stuff & said "yes!' & stomp to the cashier. I was really taken aback & pissed off by her rudeness, her attitude seems to change 360 degree within minutes! So unreasonable & its not like I'm demanding or anything, I'm actually more polite than her! Anyway, since I have spent a few minutes choosing the items & I'm rushing to an appointment so I just ignored her, make a quick payment & went off. I will not patronise that gift shop again! Why is it so difficult to get good customer service in Singapore? Sometimes I feel that customers are even more polite than all these salesperson/shop owner. Always hearing stories of rude customer service from friends, etc, also.
