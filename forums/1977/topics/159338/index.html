    Calling all Action Figures and Toys fans! are there any more places in singapore selling japanese anime action figures? so far i only know these places.

    Bugis Junction - opposite kinokuniya.
    - Sells westen comics, superheroes action figures, toys and few anime figures.

    Funan IT Mall - Inbox 5
    - 2 small shop, one selling westen comic, superheroes action figures, toys and few anime figures. the other one just anime action figures.

    Cineleisure - Rapid Culture, 2nd floor
    - Sells lots of transformer toys and anime action figures.

    Simply Toys - Far East Plaza, Raffles Exchange
    - The raffles exchange outlet sells lots of movie collectibles and replicas.
    - The far east outlet sells lots of misc toys.

    Sunshine Plaza - 3 shops
    - Triangle Toys sells transformers and movie toys.
    - The other 2 shops sells anime action figures, one of the 2 shops, La Tendo has a larger selection.

    Action City - HMV, Plaza Singapura
    - Sells toys.

    Wheelock Place Dollz inc. 2nd floor
    - Sells dolls.

    Brick World, Plaza Singapura, Ngee Ann City (HQ)
    - Sell lego
