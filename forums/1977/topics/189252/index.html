Flea Market this Sat 6th May, at TimberLux

    Flea Day is organised by a group of fun loving professionals who believes in spreading environmental friendliness and love to our Mother Earth.

    Our objectives are to promote local music, arts and culture to our already stark and boring city. By doing so, we hope to gather those in the know and all aspiring creative individuals who wants to share their thoughts and ideas.

    With close to 200 vendors, you'll find an enormous variety of collectibles,... arts, crafts, comics, books, clothes, accessories, shoes, bags, designer labels, vintage items, retro furnitures, home decors, cds, records, toys to even our locally home-made cookies and kaya! That's not all, we also have stalls providing henna painting, manicure and quick foot massage services as well as food and drinks.

    For those music lovers, who wants to parade their jewel tracks from their beloved iPod, we have PODIUM! For 20 minutes, each individuals get to play their favourite tunes. So bring your iPod along and bring it on!

    We welcome all, including your pets.
    Cash Transactions only.

    Flea Day : Sat 06 May 2006 from 12nn-7pm at the TimberLux Centre (Green warehouse along 326 Havelock Road next to Riverview Hotel / opposite Miramar Hotel)
