    Well, they're back!



    Bender and Co are back in the first of many short animated movies of Futurama. beginning with Bender's Big Score.

    This 1.5 hour long DVD is going to be launched in America on 27th November 2007.

    (Wikipedia)
    Futurama: Bender's Big Score is an upcoming straight-to-DVD movie based on the animated series Futurama to be released in the US on November 27, 2007.The title is a parody of Shaft's Big Score. The movie is written by Ken Keeler, based on a story by himself & David X. Cohen, directed by Dwayne Carey-Hill and is set to include the Nibblonians, Seymour, Barbados Slim, Morbo, Robot Santa, the God space entity, Al Gore, and Zapp Brannigan.

    Returning guests will include Al Gore as himself; Sarah Silverman as Fry's 20th Century girlfriend Michelle;and Coolio, reprising his role as Kwanzaa-bot.The voice recording finished July 3.
