    For this management module I was doing, we had to interview two managers from any type of firm and learn more about their work.

    Other groups went to approach super-huge MNCs and interviewed the major power players in the industry. The then went on to make long-winded, bombastic presentations for my prof to review, complete with brilliant multimedia shows and so on.

    My group simply interviewed two managers from this small cafe where one of my friends worked part-time. We then made a short, simple PowerPoint presentation.

    We got A. Heheheh.........

    Not trying to brag... just that some people keep forgetting the time-honoured saying:

    Keep It Simple, Stupid.
