    papercut: Special semester refers to the 3-month break. In this break, you can choose to go back to school to take some modules (SS, GEM, etc). Two catches, though:

    1. You are not protected during the bidding process.
    2. You must pay extra to take modules over the break. Something like $500-$700 per module.

    They will send you an email detailing the special semester sometime in the next semester.
