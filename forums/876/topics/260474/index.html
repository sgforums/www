    Straight up rock & roll.

    That's what JET are about, plain and simple.

    The classic-rock infused sound of their 2003 debut Get Born was an instant World wide hit-yielding such infectious tunes as "Are You Gonna Be My Girl," "Take It or Leave It," and "Get Me Out Of Here."

    They went on to support The Rolling Stones and perform a rendition of "Rockin In The Free World" with Neil Young in Canada for Live 8.

    In 2006 they released their second album Shine On to wide critical acclaim. So far the album has produced the hit singles "Put Your Money Where Your Mouth Is", "Bring It On Back (UK)" and "Rip It Up (AUS)".

    The title song 'Shine On' was also just released as a single in March 2007. Also a brand new song titled "Falling Star" will be a featured in the upcoming blockbuster movie Spiderman 3.

    Don't miss JET "The Shine On Tour" Live in Singapore!
