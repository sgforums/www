    Also known as white, smack, junk, powder, putih, medicine or ubat

    Heroin is a powerful and highly addictive drug that comes in grains, powder or liquid. A common way to consume Heroin is known as "Chasing the Dragon".

    Withdrawal symptoms associated with the consumption of Heroin include aches and spasms, stomach cramps, vomiting, runny nose, watery eyes, loss of appetite and loss of body fluids.

    Drug Fact
    Opiates such as heroin come from the poppy plant. With colourful flowers & seeds, the poppy plant may look harmless and pleasing to the eye, but it is also the main ingredient from which drugs such as heroin, opium & morphine are produced. Poppy plants are also considered to be illegal in Singapore.

    Penalties

    Illegal traffic, import or export of heroin of more than 15 grams - DEATH.

    Possession and consumption of heroin - up to S$20,000 fine or 10 years imprisonment or BOTH.
