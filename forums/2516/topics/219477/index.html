    Also known as marijuana, pot, grass, joints or ganja

    Cannabis creates a mental dependence followed by physical addiction in the long run. It contains a chemical that affects your mood and the way you see and hear things.

    The effects of consuming cannabis are a loss of concentration, an increase in heartbeat, poor balance and co-ordination, anxiety or panic, depression, confusion or hallucinations.

    Penalties for offences involving Cannabis

    Illegal traffic, import or export of :
    Cannabis resin of more than 200 grams - DEATH.

    Cannabis mixture of more than 1 kilogram - DEATH.

    Possession and consumption of cannabis - up to S$20,000 fine or 10 years imprisonment or BOTH.
