    Also known as Special K and Vitamin K

    K is the street name for Ketamine and Norketamine and its dehydro derivatives (metabolised forms of Ketamine). It comes in a white crystalline powder, a liquid or a tablet and can be snorted, smoked or consumed in a drink.

    Ketamine is a hallucinogen which can alter your sensations, mood and consciousness and distorts all your senses.

    Penalties

    Illegal traffic of Ketamine - Up to 20 years imprisonment and 15 strokes of the cane.

    Illegal import or export of Ketamine - Up to 30 years imprisonment or imprisonment for life and 15 strokes of the cane.

    Possession and consumption of Ketamine - Up to S$20,000 fine or 10 years imprisonment or BOTH.
