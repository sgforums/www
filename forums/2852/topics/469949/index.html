  • zulkifli mahmood

    Once you reach JB checkpoint, after passing the immigration counter just continue walking towards JB direction. The bus bay is below the CIQ complex where there is a food court there. Ask the bus inspector about the bus that is going to KSL mall and wait for that bus. Make sure you have small change for the bus fare...I think about RM1 plus if not mistaken..ask the bus inspector. Insert the fare into the box like Singapore system. KSL mall is not far from JB and its not really that big. When you want to take a bus ride to return to JB, just wait outside KSL mall, there is a bus queue there that will bring you back to CIQ complex bus bay, the same place where you boarded the bus to go to KSL mall.
