    TROPICAL VILLAGE - AYER HITAM - JOHOR From Johor Bahru, drive along the old trunk road to Ayer Hitam for about one and a half hours, you will see a 12.8-hectare scenic wonder park, surrounded by verdant foliage. There are two towering 12 metre traditional kuda kepang, the tallest in the country. The entry fees will cost only RM6 for adults and RM3 for children, with the option of taking one's car or motorcycle on this stimulating sojourn for an additional RM6 and RM3 respectively. Inside the park, you'll venture a picturesque three-acre azure lake bordered by lush greenery, paddle boats and intricate figurines, and with a striking red bridge running across its crystal waters. Visitors can embark on an aqua journey around the lake in the 20 available two-seater paddle boats for RM15 per boat, for a half-an hour spin during weekdays and 20 minutes during weekends. Next to the lake is a brightly coloured playground, with swings and see-saws sculpted in the form of fruits such as pears, durians, watermelons, mangosteens and other tropical fruits; varied animal forms also dot this section. The playground also consists of toy train, pony ride, magic mirrors chamber and a haunted house that is sure to give kids a thrilling chill. These amusement attractions are priced at RM2 for adults and RM1 for children. http://www.fascinatingmalaysia.com/fmweb/website/html_new/destiny/tropical.html FACEBOOK http://ms-my.facebook.com/pages/Tropical-Village-Ayer-Hitam-Johor/182073601906422?group_id=0

    In 1990 while my reporter and photographer were assigned to cover the story on Pasir Gudang Racing Circuit in JB, I had followed them. So after we had completed our assignment, I requested to the person in charge of that racing circuit whether I could have a go on the racing circuit and experience the thrill personally. My request was approved and so I drove on the Pasir Gudang Racing Circuit with my Toyota Corona 1.6 cc car. Wow! It had looked easy to manoevre your car on the racing circuit but it really that easy like I thought. So many curves and sloping tracks. You really need to have that racing driving skill to drive on that racing circuit. I had a great time and it was very thrilling. The Johor Racing Circuit This 3.86km circuit in Pasir Gudang was developed by the Johor State Economic Development Corporation and promoted by Sports Communications Sdn Bhd in accordance to the standards set by the Federation International Motorcycle (FIM) and Federation Internationale Automobile (FIA). The circuit is built on undulating terrain with 12 challenging corners for the racers to test their skills. Besides that, it also offers excellent viewing for spectators to join in the thrills. The Johor Racing Circuit provides a world-class venue for international racing events. Among the events hosted yearly are the World Superbike, World Endurance, and the Johor Race Series for Cars and Bikes. In 1998, FIM gave an honour to this circuit to host one of the legs of the World Motorcycle Championship. It is one of only two legs being held in Asia. Despite its international status, this circuit is open to any motorsport enthusiasts to sample the thrill and spill of high-speed adventure. For more information, please call the Pasir Gudang Racing Circuit at +607-252 1313. http://www.virtualmalaysia.com/destination/the%20johor%20racing%20circuit.html Johor Circuit Pasir Gudang Race Track - Yamaha FZ1 - 1:50 http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QMZaZ_hQyNA Pasir Gudang Track 5 Laps =) http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MxfLZvEj8AI
