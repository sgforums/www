    Yesterday, after my wife and I went to Holiday Plaza to look see what's happening there, we ended up at Taman Merdeka (Independence Park). It was our first time there and we were surprised to see a Musical Fountain there on display for the public to watch and enjoy. We were there around 6pm and I think its more fantastic to watch it later in the evening. There is a small public pool for parents and children to enjoy for free. There is a free Wi-Fi too. There is no entrance fee to the park. There many people jogging, skating etcs.

    There is a large structure of The Royal Crown of Johor replica outside the main entrance of the Istana Bukit Serene. And there is a water fountain too. Many locals and tourists will also take photographs with two Istana Royal Guards sentry at the entrance.
