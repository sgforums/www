    I guess most mixed-rice stalls, whether Chinese or Indian, in Johor Baru and Kuala Lumpur are operated in this way. This is different from the self-service stalls in Singapore that are not in any way self-service. There should still be one at one of the top floors of City Square. Otherwise, try a new one at Nusa Bestari, probably in the same row as the white-coffee restaurant, at the same side as KFC and Maybank, opposite BestMart. They typically have twenty to forty dishes.
