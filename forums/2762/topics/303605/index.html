    Ways to improve timing:
    1) 4km~5km jogs
    To condition yourself better
    To get your brain and body to treat 2.4km as short distance.

    2) 2.4km full speed runs
    To get used to the 2.4km runs

    3) Interval training
    Improve overall speed by getting more used to a higher running speed
    Improve mental power

    4) High Intensity Interval Training
    Reduce bodyfat so as to be leaner
    Improve cardiovascular health and power
