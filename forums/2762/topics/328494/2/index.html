    Originally posted by tofushop193:

    ytd i went gym then i try to do planks for 35sec and 1 min rest and leg raise 35 sec and 1 min rest...for about 6 sets each...can this counted as HIIT???


    unfortunately, no.

    The main idea of HIIT is to bring up your training heartrate to 75-80% of ur max for an intense duration say 30sec, then allow it to drop to 50-60% for recovery duration, say 2 min.

    So, you sprint 30sec, slow jog 2 min is considered as 1 set/cycle/round/watever. Challenge yourself to do about 6 sets in the beginning. Beginners may not be able to handle a 30sec intense sprint. You may reduce to 20-25 sec, 1min 30 sec for recovery.
