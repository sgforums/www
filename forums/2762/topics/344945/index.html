    To improve your chin ups, you need to

    1) reduce your weight
    2) improve on your lats
    3) lastly, a bit of biceps

    To reduce weight, you can go for jogs/runs, or do HIITs (do google :D )
    Do interval trainings as well.
    These has the effect of helping you improve
    i) Standing broad jump
    ii) Shuttle Run
    iii) 2.4 km run

    To improve on your lats/back strength, do
    i) Lat's pull down
    ii) Barbell rows
    iii) Chin ups themselves, assisted if necessary

    To improve on your biceps, I guess most guys should already know :D

    Once you can do 1 chin up, you will improve very fast. Once you reach about 3 to 4, you might want to add 1 to 2 kg of weights to yourself and continue training. It will help you improve even faster.
