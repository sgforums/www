    You can try this method... determine your maximum heart rate using this formula (220 - your current age) +/- 10. For example if you are 20 years old then your max heart rate would be 190 - 210.

     Then after that, you go running, run at around 60-70% of ya maximum heart rate. DO NOT RUN at maximum heart rate. This is because when you run at 60-70% of ya max heart rate, your body tends to burn more fats for energy usage. If you exceed this range, your body will cross over to using carbohydrate/sugar as a form of energy, and thus your fats remains.

     Eating too much food that is high in carbohydrate/sugars will also make you grow fat, because un-utilise carbo will be converted to fats and stored in your body too.

     forget also about things like u-zap or what. because there is no such thing as pin-pointing which part of ya body you want to lose fat from. You have to exercise more, exercise by doing some cardio workout, this way, your body will sense the need for energy and will thus utilise some fat released from fat cells in the body. Even though u might be running, fats can be released from all different parts of the body, from face, arms, tummy etc, for usage.

     No point spending money to visit slimming centre or take pills. Exercise is the best form of slimming down, and at the same time u can keep fit too.
