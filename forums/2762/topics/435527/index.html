    You can't really do anything about it since there's only a few days left. But here's what you can try to do

    1. Work out in the gym.  Doing barbell squats in the gym for strength and explosive power.. standing/seated calf raise helps too. Spread your work out to say, 1 training day and 2 off day. Oh, and don't forget your lunges too.
