    My opinion:  Build your regime around a core set of basic compound exerises - chins, bench, squats, lifts, dips, rows.

    Generally for bulking, less reps more weights, for toning more reps less weights.  But for beginners, to avoid injuries, it's best to stick to standard reps of 12-15 and use appropriate weights while still able to execute the exercises in good form.
