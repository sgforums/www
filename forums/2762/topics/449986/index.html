How to create more space for pool goers at Fitness First Poo

  • ConcernedSGman
    31 Mar 12, 19:11

    Location:

    Fitness First @ One George Street

     

    Summary:

    Creates more space for members to utilise the pool and deck chairs by moving away towels on the chairs.


    Current situation:

    Swimmers at Gym pool would place their towels and shirt on the deck chairs while swimming.

     

    Problem:

    Leaving a unsightly and untidy appearance.

    Prevent others to use the deck chair by leaving it occupied.

     

    Other Perspective:

    Not all swimmers use the deck chairs before and/or after swimming.

    Other swimmers may not be able to swim, as there are no place for them to place their towel - 5 deck chair but the pool could accommodate 10 swimmers assuming 4 swimmers are standing by the ends of the pool and enjoying the soak.

     

    Suggested Solution:

    Setup a numbered towel hanger or numbered table with compartments for swimmers to place their towel.

     

    Weakness to Solution:

    Cost in infrastructure and setup.

    Taking up slightly more space in the pool area.

    How to make people aware to utilise the proposed solution?
