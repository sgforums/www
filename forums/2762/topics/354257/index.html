    1 Apr 09, 10:41

    i see lots of pple go gym only do isolated muscle groups, and light machine weights, then complain why they see little effect. Furthermore, they train only the upper body. They also spend a lot of moeny on powders to no effect.

    Why?

    Those that they train only target the small muscle groups, and not the big ones. Like that how to gain weight/strength? Not sure if I remember correctly, but ~>70% of all your muscles come from your back and lower body. How to gain when you only play with <30% of your muscles?

    Protein or weight gaining powders are not miracle products. With a correct diet and training regime, you don't even need them. With a wrong diet and ineffective training program, taking them is tantamount to throwing them into the drain.

    In short, fix your diet, fix your training program, then look to supplements.
