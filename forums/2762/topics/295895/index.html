The BIG Three workouts

  • eagle
    7 Dec 07, 23:45
    1. Deadlifts
    2. Squats
    3. Bench

    These three movements are given this term mainly because they work several muscle groups at once, and for overall strength and mass gains, they are unparalleled.

    However, quite a number of people do not like to do num 1 and 2. Reason:
    (1) is very hard
    (2) looks ugly while doing it

    My question is, does it matter that much if it leaves you better overall?

    Another fear that a lot of people face while doing it: they see others doing heavier, and they paiseh to do. Always remember. Everyone started out doing little, no one is a born superman. Start out with a goal in mind, and aim to reach that goal eventually.

    Ganbatte everyone!
