    1. Some people go to fitness centers to stay fit, while others want to get into shape. We all have our own reasons for doing so.

    2. If you are not consistent with keeping fit, then of course you will start to lose shape. How quickly or slowly that happens depends on how much you lead an unhealthy lifestyle.

    3. If you are new to this, then I strongly suggest using the SSC ClubFITT public gyms first. They cost only $2.50 per entry, and the facilities are good enough for most people.
