    I tried to lose weight for many many years too. I found out what works for me, instead of just jogging I did weight lifting too (even as a girl). It's very hard for girls to build muscle mass, so don't worry bout getting those body builder type of bodies... it won't happen. Instead you will tone your muscles, and more muscles = higher metabolism. Lifting the weights too is kind of cardio if you do lots of reps at medium weight. So now I alternate between cardio (jogging and the like) and weights. Also do crunches every day, this is very important because crunches tighten your tummy and make you feel like eating less.

    Instead of going on diet I think it's better to just exercise more as long as you don't eat junk food or anything. But after doing this kind of workout I find that I can actually eat more food and still maintain my figure. It's not about just how much you weigh but the percentage of fat that you have, so if you diet you'll be thinner but still have those flabby arms. To really look and feel good, do look into using some weights at the gym instead of just jogging.
