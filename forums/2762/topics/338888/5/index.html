    I'm not that strict.... and I don't advocate diets actually... To me, it starves your body of necessary nutrients...

    Just tweak your meals, eat less per meal but more meals, then ensure that you take less saturated fats, trans fats, but instead, ingest more monounsaturated and polyunsaturated fats.
