Feedback on my current weight loss plan

  • tintinspartan

    Hi, i am a Sec 4 student and i'm overweight. My current weight is 98 kg. I weighed more 10 weeks ago at 108 kg. I lost 10 kg in 10 weeks so far. It's a feat to me and i am still trying my best to hit my target weight of 75 kg by Year End. That is 23 kg to go.

     

    Ok, i want to share some things i did or learnt throughout the 10 weeks i am undergoing my weight loss programme

     

    Dieting

    I spend almost everyday outdoors. I always buy mineral water each time i go out as it is the most PRECIOUS liquid that our body needs. I dun usually buy those carbonated sports drinks or diet cokes. I do drink drinks that are high in calcium like Milo, Milk and Soya Bean. I welcome any advice on my current diet.

     

    I tried eating dinner before excercising and found out that i feel nausea while running. So, pls don't eat dinner before excercise.

     

    For food, i usually eat rice, chicken and vegetables.

    Weekday

    • Breakfast: 4 cream crackers, 150 ml of Milo, 600 ml of water
    • Lunch: 1 Serving of Rice, Meat (excl. beef), vegetables, 250 ml of Milo, 600 ml of water
    • Dinner: Same as lunch OR 1 plate of soup noodles with seafood and vegetables, 600 ml of water

    Weekend

    • Breakfast: (depends on what my maid cooks)
    • Lunch: Rice with meat and vegetables
    • Snack: 1-3 Cream Crackers in between meals
    • Dinner: Rice with meat and vegetables or Seafood Noodles

    Excercise Procedure (2 - 3 times per week)

    Procedure A

    • Start with 2.4 km run
    • Train muscles at gym (Lift Weights)
    • Walk to and from gym

    Procedure B

    • Nightjog and walk (4 - 6 km)

     

    Any other procedures that you want to reccomend are welcome.

     

    I hope you all can feedback about my weight loss plan so that i could apply them.

     

    Thanks.
