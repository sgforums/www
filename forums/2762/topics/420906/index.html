    For 2.4km, keep running and try to time yourself if possible, keeping in mind to beat your previous time (even 1 second is good!). Personally I just longer distances and since I enjoy running I do it frequently. Over time my 2.4km improved massively.

    On the other hand, there are those who advocate interval training..apparently it helps a lot but I haven't tried it so I won't know

    Pullups..practice also lah But since you can do 4, I would say do multiple sets of 2, so do 2..rest..do 2 again..rest. Try not to cheat, i.e. not doing it from a deadhang, kicking your way up, but I guess training frequency is key here, so aim to do pullups every day.
