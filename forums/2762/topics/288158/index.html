    Not in any order.Core training is kept separate from strength or mass training

    1)Military press(Targeted muscles are shoulders, triceps, back to stabilise and chest to a certain extent)



    2)Dips(Targeted muscles are the chest, triceps, some shoulders)



    3)Pull-up/Chin ups(Target muscles are the wings, shoulders)



    4)Bench press(Targeted muscles are the chest, triceps and some shoulders)

    9)Bent-Knee "Good mornings" (Hamstrings, hips and butt)

    List of motions achieved through these exercises

    Horizontal Push:
    Bench Press, Standing Cable Chest Press, Incline DB Press

    Horizontal Pull:
    Bent Over Barbell Row, Seated Cable Row, Bent Over DB Row

    Vertical Push:
    Military Press, DB Shoulder Press, Push Press

    Vertical Pull:
    Chins, Pullups, Lat Pulldowns

    Quad Dominant:
    Squats, Single Leg Squats, Front Squats, Bulgarian Squats

    Hip Dominant:
    Deadlifts, Romanian Deadlifts, Good Mornings

    Knee Dominant Extension:
    Split Squats, Lunges

    And one movement not included in the above exercises

    Knee Dominant Flexion:
    Reverse Leg Curl, Slide Board Leg Curl
