    Are You Left-Brained or Right-Brained?

    Some scientists believe we tend to use one side of the brain more than the other, and that being "right-brained" or "left-brained" can influence they way we feel, act, and learn.

    The left side of the brain handles math skills, logic, and your ability to talk and write.
    The right side controls non-verbal abilities, such as drawing. This side of your brain is imaginatvie, rather unorganized, and relies on feeling rather than logic.

    Take this true or false quiz to see which side of your brain you favor.

    1) I would rather read a book than draw a picture. true false

    2) I do much better in math class than in art class. true false

    3) I always wear a watch. true false

    4) I am hardly ever late. true false

    5) I make decisions based on careful thought, not because I have a "feeling" about it. true false

    6) When someone gives me directions, I like to have it written out rather than drawn for me. true false

    7) I like throwing things out. true false

    8 ) I squeeze toothpaste from the bottom of the tube. true false

    9) I try not to put things off. true false

    If you answered true to most of these questions, you may be left-brained: logical, analytic, and practical.
