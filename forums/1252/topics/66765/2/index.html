    alone and rejected

    she tried looking tragic,
    she tried astral projecting,
    sex, and black magic.

    except maybe one thing,
    just maybe...
    something to anchor their spirits....

    they needed a crane.
    the umbilical cord
    was in the form of a chain.

    and as hard as a kettle.

    just heavy gray metal.
