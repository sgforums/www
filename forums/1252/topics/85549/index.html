    so tired of walking
    so tired of this sh1t
    legs aching,head throbbing
    nowhere to rest my feet.

    dunno where i'll be going,
    dunno where i came from,
    eyes shut,jux keep walking
    i'll sleep tomorrow morn.

    how long more do i take,
    how far do i have to travel,
    before i find a place to rest my head
    before i leave this cruel world.

    everyday seems like a dream
    living between heaven and hell
    still bewildered by what it all means
    still waiting to leave this mortal shell.
