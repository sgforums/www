Kim Jong Il Receives Special Envoy of S. Korean President

  • ctstalin
    21 Jun 05, 21:54
    Pyongyang, June 17 (KCNA) -- Leader Kim Jong Il today received Jong Tong Yong, minister of Unification of south Korea, who visited him as a special envoy of President Roh Moo Hyun. Jong is leading the delegation of the south side's authorities to the grand festival for national reunification marking the 5th anniversary of the June 15 joint declaration. On hand was Rim Tong Ok, first vice department director of the C.C., the Workers' Party of Korea. On the occasion the special envoy courteously conveyed the personal verbal message of Roh Moo Hyun to Kim Jong Il.
    Kim Jong Il expressed thanks for it and asked Jong to convey his regards to Roh Moo Hyun and former President Kim Dae Jung.
    Then he had a cordial talk with the envoy.
