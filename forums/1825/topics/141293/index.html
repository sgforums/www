Iranian Baby Unbyol Blessed

  • ctstalin
    9 Jul 05, 23:01
    Pyongyang, July 8 (KCNA) -- An Iranian radio and TV broadcast a story about baby Unbyol at 8 p. m. of June 30 and in the morning of July 1, the prime times for news bulletin. She, the daughter of Esmaeil Babaei Ragheb, second secretary of the Iranian embassy here, was the 330th foreign baby to be born at the Pyongyang Maternity Hospital.
    Unbyol's mother said that the birth of her charming daughter was possible thanks to the Pyongyang Maternity Hospital and she could have an easy delivery thanks to the high level of technique and sincere devotion of medical persons of the hospital.
    The Iranian second secretary and his wife named their baby Unbyol in order to hand down long the profound care shown for them by the maternity hospital and the Korea-Iran friendship.
    He sent a letter to leader Kim Jong Il in deep reverence some days ago in the run-up to her first birthday.
    Kim Jong Il read the letter and blessed Unbyol's future, pleased to learn that they gave her a beautiful Korean name.
    Unbyol's father said that it was great honor for his family that his daughter received blessings from Kim Jong Il.
    Unbyol is now well known as a happy baby blessed by Kim Jong Il and a symbol of Korea-Iran friendship.
