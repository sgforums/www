    Cointreau Choc Shot
    Steaming hot orange cocoa.



    1 oz Cointreau
    hot cocoa
    (whipped cream)

    Pour the Cointreau into a shot glass of the bigger kind.
    Fill up with hot cocoa.
    If you feel that it is necessary, add a lid of whipped cream and decorate with an orange twist.

    If you want more chocolate with a lesser shock, you can make the same drink in a coffee glass, a wine glass, or even a mug, as in the Cointreau Choc.
