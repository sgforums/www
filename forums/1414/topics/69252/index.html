    Hi Marmalade,

    What a question to ask, "to stay faithful in relationship?".
    There are many reasons behind that "people tends to stray away in a relationship".

    It depends on
    -how close the relationship?
    -how did they get together in the first place?
    -how mature are both parties?
    -how is the person character?
    -how strong is one's determination, do they know where they stand?
    -how did either party treat the other party when together?
    -how did they appreciate each other?Take for granted?

    Humans are "cheap", only know how to regret when they lost something. And only then they realised one's importancy in his/her life. But for some people even worst, can forget the lesson learnt and start all over again (taking people for granted).

    Humans have so many desires in life, and can or ever be enough. Only those who really know how to cherish what they have now and what they are fighting for.

    This is life, this is human and this is what on one can change but only the person himself/herself.

    And that's why we have come across so many stories that teaches us how and must cherished what you have.

    Cheers!
    Meiyo
