    there were many shrines everywhere in Kuta..



    but mostly, Kuta is famed for their beaches.. and.. wow...



    and not forgetting that a tragic event happened there.. they have a memorial for the fallen which is still visited, and tributes placed daily.. the Bear went and said a silent prayer for the lost souls...



    near the end of the day, the beach is a wonderful place to be.. a romantic place for couples...



    or if you just want to be alone with your thoughts, deep in contemplation...
