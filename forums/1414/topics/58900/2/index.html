    Recipe for some kinda Minestrone.. with cheats involved...

    3 tomatoes
    2 large cans of crushed tomatoes
    2 cans of Campbell's Beef Comsomme (add 2 cups of water)
    1 packet Continental Vegetable dry soup powder
    1 bunch of spring onions
    1 bunch of celery
    2 cans of green French Beans
    1 kilo carrots
    2 chicken cubes
    1 oxo cube

    dice all vegetables and throw everything above into a pot, add salt and pepper, bring to boil and allow to boil rapidly for 10 minutes... simmer until all the stuff is tender..

    add water as preferred...

    cheat version of minestrone!
