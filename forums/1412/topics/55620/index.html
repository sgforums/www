    Time... the future. The universal forces of good have consolidated into a Federation called THE GALAXY ALLIANCE. This coalition of planets and star systems works for peace and justice in the universe, exploring new planets, helping those in trouble, and fighting the dark forces of evil.
    Unfortunately the universe is filled with sinister villains who beleaguer the outnumbered forces of the Galaxy Alliance. The most powerful of these enemies is KING ZARKON, supreme monarch of PLANET DOOM. Aided by his son LOTOR, and a formidable witch called HAGAR, Zarkon has spread terror throughout the stars, bringing back countless unfortunate slaves who he reigns over from the heart of his empire, CASTLE DOOM.
    One of the places which was conquered by Zarkon is an idyllic little planet called ARUS. Zarkon knew that Arus possessed a very special defense weapon; five fierce lion-robots which could combine into one great robot warrior named VOLTRON - but in a devious attack, Zarkon has Hagar magically neutralize the giant robot and hide its five separate parts in different places on the planet.

    Without Voltron, Arus was helpless. Zarkon invaded with an evil armada of spaceships and waves upon waves of robot raiders. KING ALFOR, the beneficent ruler of planet Arus, put up a valiant defense, but his forces were overwhelmed by Zarkon's superior numbers. Alfor himself did not survive Zarkon's ruthless invasion. However, the good king had a young daughter named ALLURA who did survive; protected by Alfor's most trusted counselor: CORAN.
    When Zarkon's fleet finally left Arus, this once lovely planet was but a sad shadow of its former self. Those who had survived the attack now lived in caves and dark forests, afraid to come out lest Zarkon renew his offensive. The once beautiful CASTLE OF LIONS, where Alfor had reigned, lay in ruins. Yet, unbeknownst to Zarkon, the Castle's secret chambers were still inhabited by two clandestine occupants, Coran and Allura, who lived on in hopes that someday the secret of Voltron would be recovered and planet Arus would be restored to its former state.

    THE RESURRECTION OF VOLTRON

    Years have passed since Zarkon's invasion of Arus. A team of Space Explorers approaches the planet. Young but determined, these explorers have been sent by the Galaxy Alliance on a special mission: to search for the secret of Voltron. The team of explorers consists of five brave young men: KEITH, PIDGE, HUNK, LANCE, and SVEN. Upon landing on Arus' barren surface, the team begins a careful investigation of the planet. After a close call with some of Zarkon's sinister surveillance ships, the team finally locates the ravaged Castle of Lions where they are met by Coran and his charge, Princess Allura, who has grown into a radiant young woman.
    All five explorers are smitten by the beautiful princess, but the real fireworks begin when the space explorers are visited by the GHOST OF KING ALFOR who declares ceremoniously that their arrival is a fulfillment of destiny. Through Coran, he bestows upon them the special knowledge that they need to resurrect Voltron!
    The five space explorers prove equal to the challenge, going through a series of dangerous adventures before recovering the five parts of Voltron - the Robot Lions - and bringing them together to form the incredible super-robot. Zarkon does everything he can to prevent the resurrection of Voltron, but to no avail. The indomitable young space explorers outmaneuver him and, with the help of the mighty Voltron, treat him to a humbling defeat.

    As Zarkon slinks off to plot his revenge, the cave dwellers of Arus emerge triumphantly into the sunlight. The royal castle is restored, the princess is restored to her throne, and the people set about the happy task of re-establishing peace and justice on their beloved planet.
    The space explorers, in turn, are constituted as a permanent VOLTRON FORCE taking up residence in the Castle which they use as a base of operations por protecting Arus and surrounding environs.
    Zarkon repeatedly returns to Arus, mounting one attack after another - but all are foiled by the mighty Voltron. In one of these encounters, Sven was badly wounded and was hospitalized on a neutral planet. Volunteering to take his place, Allura quickly proved herself a courageous fighter, earning a spot as a permanent member of the Voltron Force.

    The first campaigns Zarkon launches against Arus are led by a gruff general named YURAK, but after many repeated defeats by Voltron, Yurak is replaced by Zarkon's son, PRINCE LOTOR. An arrogant brute, Lotor has a personal motive for his attacks, enchanted by the beautiful princess Allura, he wants to capture her and make her his bride.

    These are the continuing adventures of space explorers as they battle the evil Zarkon and work for peace and justice throughout the universe.
