    umm actually the original Transformers are from Japan, Takara was the licensee holder to make the toys, US brought the series across the Atlantic and Hasbro was and is still the western hemisphere licensee for the toys. There are many Takara TF that Hasbro never made. Some of the US versions had differing paintjobs or plastic parts compared to metal. There were some crossover production pieces when the both Takara & Hasbro shared the same factory in HK.

    Stuff like Star Convoy, Victory Leo never made it to the US stores. In terms of colors, check out bots like Metroplex(Metroflex) and Fortress Maximus (Brave Maximus) for a better idea.
