    Lilo and Stitch (2002)

    Synopsis: The tale of a young girl's encounter with the galaxy's most wanted extraterrestrial. Lilo is a lonely Hawaiian girl who adopts a small ugly 'dog', whom she names Stitch. Stitch would be the perfect pet if he wasn't a genetic experiment that escaped from an alien planet and crash-landed on earth. Through her faith and unwavering belief in "ohana" (the Hawaiian concept of family), Lilo helps unlock Stitch's heart and gives him the one thing he wasn't designed to have--the ability to love.

He's too heavy to swim, and he was created to destroy large cities, so where does he find himself but on an island with no cities at all. He ends up in an animal shelter. From Lilo, however, and Lilo's sister Nani, he learns to care and comes to understand the meaning of ohana, family.
