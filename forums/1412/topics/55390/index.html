    Gatchaman 94, The OAV

    WHAT IT IS:
    A 3-part Original Anime Video remake of the 1972 Gatchaman series
    WHO PRODUCED IT:
    Nippon Columbia in association with Tatsunoko Productions

    WHEN RELEASED:
    Volume 1, "Gatchaman Vs. Turtle King": 10/1/94
    Volume 2, "Secret Red Impulse": 1/1/95
    Volume 3, "Final Count 0002": 4/1/95



    G-1: KEN
    Age 18. Hotshot pilot and leader of the Kagaku Ninjatai, Ken comes from a successful line of ISO agents. His mother was an ISO operative who retired when Ken was born (she died of illness when he was eleven), and Ken's father is none other than the leader of the Red Impulse squadron. Ken's latest incarnation has not changed much from the original save for a very short haircut and a quieter and more professional demeanor.
    Voice: Masaya Onozaka

    G-2: JOE
    Age 19. Second in command and chief gunner of the team. Joe is every inch the Sicilian bad boy who exists only to take revenge on Gallactor. Joe's father, Giuseppe Asakura, had been a small-time Mafia money launderer until he ran afoul of the Syndicate and was eliminated along with his wife. Joe initially trained on his own, but was recruited by Nambu (who evidently had Mafia connections). On his left wrist next to his team bracelet Joe wears a "promise bracelet," which won't come off until he has fulfilled his vendetta.
    Voice: Koji Ishii

    G-3: JUN
    Age 16. The Team's weapons expert, specializing in timed explosives. In her off time, Jun acts as the assistant manager of the Swan Lounge in the Bay Area Hotel, and works part time at the hotel as a swimming instructor. In the new novelization, Jun also took work as a model and a ballerina.
    Voice: Michiko Neya

    G-4: JINPEI
    Age 10. A sharp hacker who specializes in picking locks and breaking passwords. Jinpei's true name and origin are unknown. He was "adopted" by Jun when he was very young. He earns spare change working for Jun at her bar and doing odd jobs at the hotel. His interests are chiefly basketball, skateboarding and teasing his sister.
    Voice: Rika Matsumoto
