(My point of view anyway.) Although I think what you mean is that cannot buy from just anyone, and that is true! Afterall, there have been too many stories of shops playing dirty. However, I should think that generally all the stores selling the cards here in singapore is 'clean', so shouldn't be too much of a prob!

    For me, it's the set of cards that calls to you that you should get. When you go looking at the cards, just pick up the one that appeals to you the most... Take it to the counter and ask for them to open the deck and let you look through it... See if you can click with the cards. If can, then get... If cannot, then go find another deck that resonates with you better!

    If you are very into witchcraft, the Wiches Tarot should be an interesting deck to explore. Or you can try any of the celtic, egypt or legend based cards, but that'll depend on your preference of the legends. The Goddess tarot seems pretty interesting as well, perhaps is also worth a look. The Rider-waite is the classic deck used for beginners as it's easier to relate to.
