    Remember them?



    Matt Trakker and several friends create MASK, whose objective is to destroy the international criminal organisation Venom and its leader, Miles Mayhem. Matt and his team wear special masks to hide their indentities when on missions, which also provide them with special powers. Episodes last roughly 20 minutes.

    MASK Agents:
    Matt Trakker (Hunter) - the leader
    Bruce Sato (Magic) - the brains
    Alex Sector (Megabyte) - animals/computers guy
    Hondo MacLean (Sticker) - the weapons guy
    Buddy Hawkes (Clutch) - the mechanic
    Scott Trakker - Matt's son
    Brad Turner - the music guy
    Dusty Hayes (Powderkeg) - the marine specialist
    Gloria Baker - fighting and driving girl.
    Julio Lopez - languages and doctor guy
    Calhoun Burns - the builder guy
    Jacques LaFleur - the canadian luberjack
    Chief Nevada Rushmore - matt's childhood friend
    Ali Bombay
    Ace Striker - pilot
    Borris Bushkins - truck driver

    VENOM Agents:
    Miles Mayhem - head baddie
    Sly Rax - gangster weird accent guy
    Cliff Dagger - demolitions expert
    Vanessa Warfield - man hater girl
    Floyd malloy (Birdman)
    Bruno Sheppard
    Lester Sludge (the lizard)
    Maximus Mayhem - Mile's twin brother
    Nash Gorey - nervous electronics guy
