    It's a competition to create the lamest transformer you can think of!

    Quoted from the site:
    "Everyone remembers Transformers, right? Transformers were cool vehicles that unfolded and then reassembled into even cooler robots. Both the toys and the cartoons should be pretty familiar to anyone who was a kid in the eighties. Transformers were the coolest. But how did they keep all the Transformers so cool? By scrapping the really lame ones that no kid wanted to play with, of course!

    *Those* are the ones we want to see. In this contest, your task is to create the rejected Transformers, as in d1stantjourney's themepost of the never popular Mixotron. Poor Mixotron.

    Create a Transformer from an item that a kid would likely not be impressed with, vehicular or not, and please keep your entries to photoshop form- no cartoons for this contest.

    The rules of this tournament round are thus:
    Only competitors who are on their teams' roster may submit entries. There is a total of 72 hours to submit entries before voting begins. Captains are recommended to use the initial 24 hours to prepare a roster and to use the remaining 48 hours to work on and submit their entries. At least 5 entries must be submitted from each team. Quality is a must, so please submit your absolute best work.

    Good luck to all sites!"
