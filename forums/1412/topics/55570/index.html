    Girls and glam were all the rage back in the late 80s (yeah, only in the late 80s) ...and Jem and the Holograms were there to provide us with more than our fair share of all the fashions and strange lingo of a decade since past. What little girl didn't want to grow up to be just like the princess of rock, Jem? What little boy didn't want to grow up to be Rio, Jerrica's boyfriend and Jem's stage manager. Poor Rio...he's in love with both and doesn't have the brains to figure out they're the same person. Rio also had to compete for Jem's affections against Riot, the lead-singer of the Stingers who managed to look twice as effeminine as me and the entire cast of the Holograms combined.

    The Jem and the Holograms music group was owned by Starlight Music, a black sheep in the music industry due to their actual caring about causes such as charity and housing foster children. Constantly on top of the charts, their only real rivals were the evil rock group, The Misfits. Pizzazz, who has pea-green hair and army camoflauge paint on her cheeks, leads the group and will even break the rules to get on top.

    When Jerrica uses Synergy, a girl somehow connected to the star earrings she wears, she'll turn into Jem, grrl-rockin'-kinderwhore era-supreme pop star who's here to save the day!

    Jem and the Holograms
    They were constantly on top of the music charts, coming, seemingly, out of nowhere.
    They are owned by Starlight Music, and hold lots of benefits for their charity, the Starlight Foundation, which houses foster girls.


    Jem
    She's Jerrica Benton's alter ego, the lead singer of Jem and the Holograms. She also acts, travels, and other glamorous things that rock stars do along with dating Rio.

    Jerrica Benton
    She lives a full life. She's the President of Starlight Music and also runs the Starlight Foundation, a foster home for girls. At night she uses Synergy to transform into Jem. Wow! Rio, her boyfriend, keeps her company.

    Kimber Benton

    She's the youngest Hologram (20ish) and therefore has a tendency to act impulsively, but she has a large heart, and it really shows up in her music writing. She plays keyboards and guitar in the band.

    Aja Benton

    Another Benton (adopted) sister who plays guitar in the band. She may be hard-headed, but she is a real fitness fanatic. She used to be a Starlight girl and gives them free aerobic lessons.

    Shana Elmsford

    She plays drums and guitar in the band. Extremely loyal to her friends, she is a fashion designer and artist at heart. Most of the outfits worn by the band were designed by her.

    Raya

    She is the new drummer for the Holograms because she joined while Shana was gone. She stayed when she found out Jem's secret and became good friends with the group. She came from a large Hispanic family living in LA.

    Pizzazz

    She's the ringleader of the Misfits and Jem's arch-rival. She is a spoiled rotten daddy's girl, and will always have her way. She competes with Jem because Jem is at the top of the charts, and has Riot's attention.
    Roxy

    She is the toughest member of the Misfits. She is hard to the core. If she wants something, NOTHING will stand in her way. She is one uncontrollable person.

    Stormer

    She has a heart of gold, and she's the only Misfit with any real talent. She was actually friends with the Holograms for a while, but returned to the Misfits. She has a very low self-esteem though, and that is why she stays with the group.
    Clash

    She is the cousin of Video and does nothing but create problems. She is not a Misfit (even though she wants to be) but Pizzazz keeps her around to cause problems for the Holograms.
    Jetta

    She came from England to play Saxophone for the band. She is the newest member of the Misfits who joined when Raya joined the Holograms. She is a pathological liar, and loves to hurt people, so she fit in like a (bad luck) charm.
    Eric Raymond

    Eric, the manager of the Misfits, was once a co-owner of Starlight music, but Jerrica wouldn't let him. Then he waged war.
