    9. Sonicare Plus Cleaning System

    Failed the breath test? No problem, use Sonicare cleaning system to keep your teeth clean and your breath smelling like a bouquet of fresh daisies. This system cleans with a combination of 31,000 brush sweeps per minute and the gentle sonic waves help remove nearly twice as much plaque from between your teeth. Buy it here.

    Motivational factor: Having white teeth will encourage you to smile more often. Who knows, maybe she'll smile back. By the way, you literally feel like you came from the dentist after every use. All of sudden, brushing becomes a welcomed event.

    8. Compact Chrome Hair Dryer

    Hair dryers can make a big difference when it comes to how your hair looks and feels. This sleek, compact and stylish dryer will help you try different hairstyles that will show the ladies how trendy you are. Women love men who know how to take care of themselves.

    Motivational factor: No woman will spend the night over at your place if you don't have a good hair dryer that she can use in the morning.

    7. Turbo-Groomer 2.0

    When kissing your sweet lady, you want to tickle her with your fingers, not your nose hairs. Use the Turbo-Groomer as the perfect solution to remove unwanted nose and ear hair. This nifty product also trims eyebrows, mustaches, beards, and sideburns.

    Motivational use: You can always ask her to let you trim your initials into her "private patch."

    A woman loves a well-groomed man. You can bet that she'll be checking out your hands and fingernails before she lets you rub/scratch them all over her body. With this kit, you'll have eveything you need, from toenail clippers to a corkscrew/bottle opener.

    Motivational factor: You never know when she'll ask you to spend the night. Better yet, what if she asks you to spend the week? Impress her with your sophisticated cleanliness. This is an impressive must-have kit for all men.

Take a shower while listening to your favorite radio station.
