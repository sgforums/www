    5 steps to becoming a revolutionary:

    1. Be between 15 - 35 years old
    2. Have a burning passion to discuss social isssues
    3. Form a team of four
    4. Shoot a powerful 4 min video
    5. With your video, rally up people for your cause

    The Substation Moving Images is proud to present Reel Revolution, a new six week programme to challenge young people to bring issues up for discussion through the platform of video.

    Programme begins with an Introductory Seminar on 17 June. Teams will be briefed and mentored with a series of activities for knowledge building and skill development. At the end of the programme, four selected videos wil be used at the Revolution Powwow on 29 July for presentation and discussion by the teams that created them.
