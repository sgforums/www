    LG Electronics and Netflix Announce Plan to Stream Movies From the Internet to the TV

    LOS GATOS, Calif. and ENGLEWOOD CLIFFS, N.J., Jan. 3 /PRNewswire-FirstCall/ -- Netflix Inc. (NASDAQ: NFLX), the world's largest online movie rental service, and LG Electronics, a global consumer electronics leader, today announced they are joining forces to develop a set-top box for consumers to stream movies and other programming from the Internet to HDTVs -- bypassing the need to use a personal computer.

         

    The technology collaboration supports the Netflix core strategy of offering a multi-dimensional, or "hybrid," service that gives its more than 7 million members a variety of ways to receive movies and TV series for one, low monthly fee.
