German Film Festival 2006

  • FireIce
    4 Nov 06, 19:45


    It is now in its tenth year, the Singapore German Film Festival!
    We consider this a reason to celebrate! Together with our loyal and enthusiastic audience, we would like to present a diverse collection of the best German films from recent years and hope they will please the majority of viewers.

    With absolute abundance of outstanding new German films, selection was tough compared to previous years. Nevertheless, we hope we have succeeded to curate a programme that meets the highest cinematic demand, and at the same time, gives an overview of Germany's real life situation on celluloid.
