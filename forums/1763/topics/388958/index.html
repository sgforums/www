          SANTAU Opens 28 Jan 10  
       

    What do you do when the Devil whispers your name?
    Find out this January with Eng Wah Cinemas’ “Santau”.

    Featuring Mariani & the movie debut of “Road to Hollywood” winner – Putri Mardiana. Life is perfect for Halim –a high-flying career, love from his quietly supportive wife, Nina & a comfortable home with their daughter, Ecah. But evil lurks in the shadows & strange things start to happen when the house next door is vacated. Eerie apparitions start to haunt Ecah; bizarre behavior torment Nina. These incidents reach a tipping point when a hair-raising phone call threatens to rip Halim’s once peaceful life apart. From mysterious appearances to drastic changes in personalities, will Halim be able to save his family from the whispers of the devil?

    Cast:Bob Lokman, Esma Daniel , Farisya Fatin, Lisdawaty, Mariani, Putri Mardiana (Winner of ”Road to Hollywood”)
    Riezman Khuzaimi (Congkak, 2008), Zul Handy Black
    Directed by:Azhari Mohd Zain
