    Celebrating The Family showcases a special selection of two short films from nuSTUDIOS Film Productions, which touch on the theme of family. It will mark the world premiere of CASHLESS (2009), screened here with the short film documentary, In Our Own Words (2008). The filmmakers will be in attendance for a post-screening discussion. The event is part of National Family Celebrations '09.

    Synopsis:

    CASHLESS (2009)

    CASHLESS revolves around a man's reckless pursuit of his misplaced cash after he mistakenly transfers an important sum of money meant for his mother into the wrong ATM account. The film stars Danny Jow and features music from local bands The Lilac Saints and RustyNailz.

    - Official Selection for 5th InDPanda International Short Film Festival
    - In Competition for the 30th Durban International Film Festival
    - Official Selection for Projecto Videolab, Portugal.

    Click here to view the CASHLESS trailer: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nuxOJKZljII&fmt=18

     

    In Our Own Words (2008)

    What is the definition of normality? Two young kidney patients go on a quest to challenge society's perception of them. In the process, they discover their own ability to redefine what being normal is all about.

    - Voted Audience Favourite at The Substation's First Take screening (Oct 2008)

                                              
    For more details on the films: www.cashless.wordpress.com and www.inourownwords.wordpress.com
