    i must say, some ppl dont like this movie, some rate it ave...maybe some rate it as good

    storyline/concept:
    i must say this movie has a very refreshing storyline to the otherwise satuarated movie market of movies with similar storyline. ghost movies all have the same theme and same way of scaring ppl, like

    all is silence, main actor slowly approaches door/car/lake/bathtub/mirror/phone/etc and some moron with a bad sorethroat says 'hi' and everyone screams. and its usually because someone died eons ago.(all ghost thrillers)

    or it's americans blazing their way into country x and saving 1 of theirs while killing thousands.(behind enemy lines)

    it's diff frm other disaster movies or movies where 1 or grp of ppl are stranded in a certain place for sometime before someone saves them or they escape,etc. like the movie of this cargo plane crashing into a deserted island (tom hanks) and someone saves him. finally there's a movie abt the characters building themselves something to get them out of the place. interesting.

    cast
    really B list. so go reckon abt their acting skills.
    their acting seems abit ..i dunno what u call it, dry or forced. anyway they just lack the punch.

    even though it was probably scripted to have a rag-tag oil rig crew, it's now the usual bunch of zeros you would expect to see in a movie. it's just different.

    the movie itself
    it seems accelerated and inconsistent in terms of flow. suddely there's a makeshift plane after a few days of welding (like 3 days?)
    throughout the movie, it's obvious that the director or producer made good attempts to present it better by shooting frm brilliant angles such as showing a wide angle view that included the plane and a man standing on a distant tall sand dune,etc ( u'll have to watch it to get waht i mean)

    there's another scene where they show this guy being half-buried in the sand and being baked dry, that scene was rather nice;y crafted. seems to take cues frm "the day after tomorrow"

    there's 1 scene they 1 hot-headed guy tries to walk for help and another guy projects to him waht he's gonna expect if he really did

    like the using a compass to navigate and the area is surrounded with magnetic mountains and the compass will spin, etc

    the animation/special effects are really cool there..

    Warning: spoilers below!!!!

    there are some scenes where i have no idea what to make of it,
    either u call the producer ingenious/creative or just plain lame. depends on u

    eg1. they discover their so called engineer is a designer for model aeroplanes
    eg2. the same guy made everyone beg him to work after a scuffle.
    eg3. the chef even had the mood to create a menu from a piece of metal plate.
    eg4. they even sang "hey ya" by outkast while at it ?
    eg5. those nomads/whatever speak cantonese ? (ok fine, it's gobi desert and some chinese do speak cantonese)
    eg6. those nomads dont seem tanned at all ??

    personal rating. 6.5/10

    3.5 comes frm novel storyline
    the rest comes frm the cast,etc
