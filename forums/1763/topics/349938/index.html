    Blood : The Last Vampire (In Cinema June)

    The live-action Hollywood adaptation from the sensational anime of the same title, “Blood: The Last Vampire” is an action filled fantasy surrounding a vampire hunter named Saya (played by My Sassy Girl’s Jeon Ji-Hyun).

    [u]Synopsis[/u]
    Saya is sent to an American military base in post-WWII Tokyo on an undercover mission, where she finds the perfect opportunity for a grand showdown against the highest ranking vampire on Earth.

    When the anime was released in the US in 2000, it became a huge hit and spawned several manga sequels, novel adaptations, video games and even a 50-parts anime series.

    You can look forward to action sequences brought to you by renowned martial arts choreographer Corey Yuen who worked on such thrilling blockbusters as "X-men” and the "Transporter” franchise.

    Directed by Chris Nahon ("Kiss of the Dragon"), and produced by Bill Kong ("Lust, Caution”, “Fearless”, “House of the Flying Daggers” and “Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon”), "Blood: The Last Vampire" promises heart pounding sword fights and pulse racing action.
