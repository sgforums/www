    It is a 2005 Hong Kong martial arts-crime film directed by Wilson Yip. It stars Donnie Yen, Sammo Hung, Simon Yam, and Wu Jing. Yam portrays a retiring police detective who resorts to the use of questionable methods in order to catch Wong Po (Hung), a ruthless Triad boss.

    Cast
    Donnie Yen as Ma Kwan
    Sammo Hung as Wong Po
    Simon Yam as Detective Chan
    Wu Jing as Jack
    Liu Kai Chi as Lok
    Danny Summer as Kwok
    Ken Chang as Lee
