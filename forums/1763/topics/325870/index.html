    Call for entries: 2nd Asian Hot Shots Berlin 2009
    Presented by green chillies e.V. (Berlin)
    In cooperation with The Substation and Sinema.sg

    13th - 18th January 2009
    Deadline for submissions: 1st October 2008

    Asian Hot Shots Berlin (AHSB) is a newly established festival on the calendar that showcases a wide range of works from across Asia. Into its second year running, it aims to offer an alternative insight into Asian cinema, providing a forum for dialogue and cultural exchange between German and Asian filmmakers. Awards will be given in two categories (best feature and short film), decided by audience voting.

    AHSB is in cooperation with The Substation and Sinema.sg once again to bring the Singaporean entries to Berlin. Last year, eighteen Singaporean films were featured in total, including works by Victric Thng ("Locust"), Boo Junfeng ("Katong Fugue"), Nicholas Chee ("Becoming Royston"), Daphne Tan ("Man with Camera"), Jacen Tan ("Zo Gang"), Kirsten Tan ("Fonzi"), Joshua Lim ("The Olive Depression"), among many others. "Fonzi" and "The Olive Depression" were in competition for the Best Short Film Award and Best Feature Film Award respectively.

    We are now accepting entries for the 2nd Asian Hot Shots Berlin for recent Singapore short films, feature films, video art and installations completed in either 2007 or 2008.

    Deadline for entries: 1st October 2008.
