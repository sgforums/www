    Criminal Intelligence Bureau ("CIB" ) is one of the most secretive branches of Hong Kong Police. Its field agents use unassuming appearance and covert operations to conduct surveillance on targets like an "eye-in-the-sky" . What they watch becomes crucial intelligence leading to the arrest of criminals.

    CIB unit leader Matt (starring Simon YAM) receives an order to seek out suspects of a highly publicized jewelry heist. He leads rookie agent Bo (starring Kate TSUI) and a few teammates to conduct extensive stakeout looking for the elusive target. What they don't know is heist mastermind Shan (starring Tong LEUNG Ka Fai) knows too well to shake off the police. On the busy streets of Hong Kong, a game of hide and seek ensues...
