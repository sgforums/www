    Synopsis:
    Ollie is a drug pusher and on one of his usual runs he realizes that his customer Mike is his future brother-in-law. A tussle occurs between both men where now Mike realizes who the drug pusher is. Both men now have to struggle with their own conscience and that of each other. And also what it will mean for their future together.

    Mike
    - Working adult

    - "Family man"
    - The kind of person who means what he says
    - Any race welcomed

    Ollie
    - Typical teenager/young adult
    - Has to look younger than Mike

    - Any race welcomed
