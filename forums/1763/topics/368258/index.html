    It is an out and out feel-good love story about youngsters between the age groups 22-23. A man in his 30s starts to reminiscence his encounter with a girl when he was younger and told in a series of flashbacks. It is the usual boy meets girl, old fashioned romantic love story set in city of CHENNAI. The movie is directed by popular filmaker Gautham Menon and the music is by oscar award winner A.R.Rahman. Slated for 2009 release.
