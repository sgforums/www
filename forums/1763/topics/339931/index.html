    This one may be far from blockbuster material but it definitely has a dash of character on its own.

    Imho, The Visitor is a subtle story about human connection and its subsequent repercussions.

    Trailer

    Recommended for those who enjoyed Babel and Crash. Visitor is less explicit in the message it's sending, alienation lah, cultural acceptance lah so it becomes very hard to pin but me thinks that's the magic. A pretty quaint yet evocative piece of work. A similar, but more humourous approach can be seen in Miranda July's Me and You and Everyone We Know.

    Pity many movie goers will miss it because it's only screening on Cinema Europa, Vivocity.
