    Detailed Description...........

    From director Jeong Cho-Shin comes this sequel to 2002's smash hit Wet Dreams! Unlike its male-oriented predecessor, this outrageous teen sex comedy switches its focus to the perspective of three high school girls, each of whom are growing increasingly curious about sex!
    The film centers on the sexual fantasies of Sung-Eun (Kang Eun-Bi) and her gal pals Bang Sue-Yeon (Jeon Hye-Bin) and Kim Mi-Sook (Park Seul-Gi) in Korea circa 1991. But fantasy soon becomes a reality with the arrival of a handsome student teacher named Kang Bong-Gu (Lee Ji-Hoon). With his chiseled good looks and sparkling smile, no girl could resist him, that is - if he didn't have one fatal flaw. It seems Kang has an incurable disease; it isn't really fatal, but it might as well be. Poor Kang has a "gassy" problem that puts a major damper on his sex life! However, that little problem doesn't stop Oh Sung-eun from trying to snag her man! For those looking for a rousing American Pie-style comedy, look no further than Wet Dreams 2!

    Comin Soon on 14 July!
