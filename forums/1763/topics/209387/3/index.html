  • Gedanken
    18 Dec 06, 14:22
    My view's quite the opposite, e.

    First, the objective of CR was to bring it back to Ian Fleming's original concept rather than continue the path that the movies took, so if it's shattered the franchise it's done its job. Also, this is supposed to be Bond who's just come off his L-plates - think of this as the Bond version of Batman Begins. In light of that,

    - few gadgets and Bond girls who detract from the plot: good stuff
    - you get to see how Bond's formed his later approach to things: excellent
    - Bond has rough edges and actually screws up: even better
    - Bond spends more time working than getting laid: WOOHOO!

    The only thing that let it all down was the last 15 seconds - Craig could have played that last bit a lot more brutally a la Connery. I'd give the script a 4.5/5, although I'm still undecided about Craig being better than anybody else to play the role - my vote for the best Bond still lies with Timothy Dalton.
