    60 senior citizens enjoy free screening of Raintree Pictures' "881"

    SINGAPORE: Dozens of senior citizens were invited to a free screening of MediaCorp Raintree Pictures' latest film "881", escorted by the movie's leading actor, MediaCorp artiste, Qi Yuwu.

    Most of the 60 senior citizens came from PEACE-Connect Neighbourhood Link, a charity group that takes care of the elderly in Kampong Glam.

    The S$1-million film, which is about singers who entertain the dead during the seventh lunar month, was a hit with the audience.

    "The movie's very funny. It also shows how 'getai' was like in the past," said one of the senior citizens.

    "I was touched by the story of the Papaya sisters. I even cried," said another.

    The movie's theme song "One Half" is also getting rave reviews online.

    The music video has received about 18, 000 hits on YouTube and is among the top 100 most viewed videos.

    The movie "881" is now showing at all major cinemas. - CNA/ac

    Juz came back from the movie.

    I must say this is the best sg created movie i watched so far.. in terms of visual, audio, humour and humanity..

    laughed throughout the movie, but also felt the xin suan..

    some scenes were very touching..

    Qi Yu Wu went to sell his beloved pet (chicken) to raise money..

    Xiao Mu Gua dying.. hugging Da Mu Gua, cryinging and singing the hokkien song..

    Da Mu Gua went home to visit her mother but she kept the door closed.. and sang that hokkien song "ever since u left, u never returned.. i waited from dark till dawn.."

    the part when the papaya sisters kena assassinated and both were lying on the ground.. calling each other's name..

    Went to many Getais before when i was younger, but dun think i ever heard those songs in the movie before.. im curious whether the papaya sisters really sing at getais in real life.. they sing really very well, especially Wang Xin, her voice is very good. Never knew she has such a talent when she was with TCS.

    I feel that Da Mu Gua (Yann Yann) looks like Zhuang Mi Xue (Michelle).

    Tho Qi Yu Wu din utter a word in this movie.. but it's all gan qing xi on his part.. and i think he really played the role very well.. esp the part when he cried.. speaks louder than words.

    Initially I din really feel that this movie is worth watching.. but bcos of the advertising on tv, and my mum wanting to watch.. i went with my family.. and glad that I din miss it.
