    For Neil Gaiman and stop-motion animation fans!

    Explore a hand-made world here!

    Caution: The website takes up a lot of memory on the computer!

    Release date: 9 April 2009

    Plot: A young girl explores her new home and stumbles upon a mysterious secret door that seems to lead to nowhere. Without thinking much, she unlocks the door and discovers a whole new different version of her life. Soon, her adventure turns dangerous when her Other Mother wants to keep her forever. Equipped with only her determination, resourcefulness and bravery, Coraline must make it back to the other side safely.

     

    It's really amazing - they take you behind the scenes and you can see the production crew working on everything, from the characters to the scenery, created by hand. By golly, if I were rich enough, I would buy the whole thingy to display at home.
