    Hesher Trailer [2010]

    Hesher is a 2010 drama film starring Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Natalie Portman, and Rainn Wilson. The film was first screened at the Sundance Film Festival on January 22, 2010. Hesher (Gordon-Levitt), a young man with a troubled past, assumes the role of both mentor and tormentor to T.J. who has just lost his mother tragically. T.J. gets into a lot of trouble and a young grocery clerk named Nicole (Portman) steps in to protect him. Meanwhile Hesher begins to fantasize about Nicole.
