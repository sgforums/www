    HEAVY RAIN (SONY ENTERTAINMENT/2010)
    What is it about?     Heavy Rain is a chilling psychological thriller that revolves around a serial killer who calls himself the Origami Killer. The psychopath murderer would kidnap his victims and murder them after four days by drowning them in heavy rainfall, and then leave an origami as a call-card. On the other side of the ring, we have Ethan Mars; a doting father who is racing against time to save his kidnapped son before it's too late.
    Why it will work? Of all the games on the list so far, we think Heavy Rain would be the easiest to adapt to the big screen, and it will work very well too. The gripping storyline and the ticking time bomb factor would get one's heart racing, and if you like movies like 'Jigsaw' or 'Se7en', then what are you waiting for: go online and start a petition already.
