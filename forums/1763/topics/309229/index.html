    RULE No.1 rakes in over S$300,000 in ticket sales since its opening

    SINGAPORE : RULE #1 — Singapore’s horror movie which opened on 13 March — has so far raked in more than S$300,000 in ticket sales.

    The movie is about two policemen who answer calls regarding the supernatural while assuring the public that ghosts do not exist.

    Directed by Kelvin Tong, the movie stars Hong Kong actors Shawn Yu, Ekin Cheng and Singapore’s Fiona Xie.

    The S$2.8 million movie is shot entirely in Hong Kong.
