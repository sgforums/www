Filem Bagus! at Sinema Old School

  • blacklady
    21 Feb 09, 20:18

    Filem Bagus! is a special showcase of Malaysian independent films. A  showcase by Da Huang Pictures will be held at Sinema Old School from the 27th of February to 1st March 2009.

    Flower in the pocket by Liew Seng Tat will be shown on the 27th feb at 8pm. On the 28th, Sinema Old School will be screening James Lee love trilogy starting from Before we fall in love at 3pm. On the last day of the showcase, Apa Khabar Orang Kampung by Amir Mohd as well as Love Conquers All by Tan Chui Mui will be screened at 3pm and 5pm respectively!!

     

    For more information do visit www.sinema.sg/oldschool
