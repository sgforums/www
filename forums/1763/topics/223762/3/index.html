    Â• There was one day of location shooting, which was for the horses that were shot for the 'approaching sparta' scene.

The film was edited on an Avid, with an HD cut also maintained in Final Cut Pro The 3D was made using Maya, XSI, and Lightwave The 2D composites were made with Shake, Inferno, Fusion, and Combustion. Asset management was handled by custom software written in the Panorama development environment, made by Provue. Color management was handled by Truelight software. The working resolution for the film was 2K, at a working aspect ratio of 2.11 and a projected aspect ratio of 2.35.
