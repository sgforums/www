On Film for Japan

  • Lights on Shore
    23 Mar 11, 21:43
    The recent shocking series of events in Japan has left thousands of people in need for help. Lights on Shore is organizing a fund raising short film contest to support the communities affected by the quake. At the same time, Lights on Shore aims to stimulate increased interest and opportunity in filmmaking for the young and the passionate about the art.
     
    Everybody with a passion for filmmaking is welcomed to participate. Participating teams may be composed of up to 4 individuals. The deadline for team registration is April 30th, 2011. Works will have to be submitted by May 31, 2011.
     
    The short films must have a duration between 5 and 10 minutes. The theme of the contest is "Man against or with Nature?". Although there are no limitations on the genre, each piece of work will be screened for unsuitable or inappropriate content. To ensure that submitted works have been produced for the On Film for Japan contest, upon registration each participating team will receive two 'complexities' to incorporate in their story (e.g. include a scene with a man holding an umbrella).
     
    As part of the assignment, each team will also have the chance to contribute in the fund raising effort by selling tickets for the final event show in Singapore. Each participating team is asked to make a donation of SG$100 (or US$80). The proceeds from the registration and the sale of tickets for the final show event in June 2011 in Singapore will be entirely devoted to charity work for the reconstruction of Japan. All participating team members will receive a free ticket for the final show event in June.
     
    The work of participating teams will be assessed on the basis of the following criteria:
     
    • Online votes by the public (details on how to vote will follow): 70% weight on final score
    • Number of tickets sold by each team for the final event: 30% weight on final score
     
    The work of the top teams will be displayed this coming June at the final event show in Singapore, where the winner of the 2011 On Film For Japan contest will also be announced.
     

    To register for the contest, get yourself started as a film maker and make your contribution towards the reconstruction of Japan please fill in the online form at http://www.lightsonshore.com/id4.html
