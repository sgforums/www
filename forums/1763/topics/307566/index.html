Persepolis - Oscar Best Animated Feature nominee

  • Sinnerman
    14 Feb 08, 14:21



    SYNOPSIS: Nominated for Best Animated Feature Film in the upcoming Academy Awards, Persepolis is based on the same titled graphic novel written by Marjane Satrapi. This is a funny and poignant coming-of-age story of a free-spirited girl set to the shifting historical backdrop of her home country and Europe. Featuring the voice talents of Sean Penn, Chiara Mastroianni, Catherine Deneuve, Gena Rowlands and Iggy Pop, Persepolis is directed by Marjane Satrapi and Vincent Paronnaud.

    Title: Persepolis
