Asian Film Archive Presents: Cineodeon 2008

  • adsfghj
    1 Jul 08, 10:58

    The Asian Film Archive cordially invites students, especially those with a passion for film, to sign up as student leaders of Cineodeon 2008, a youth-initiated cinema chain project designed to help students learn how to programme and manage a film screening, under the mentorship of industry professionals.

    To equip these leaders with the necessary skills and resources to organise these screenings, we have scheduled a series of training sessions in which students will be tutored on film literacy, programming and promotion techniques, as well as the facilitation of post-screening discussions. The first of these preparation workshops is the one-day Cineodeon Boot Camp, where students will meet with teams from other participating schools, and also their mentors who will guide them in planning for their screening in August.



        Cineodeon 2008 Boot Camp: How To Organise A Film Screening
        Date:12 July 2008 (Saturday)
        Time:9:00am – 6:00pm
        Venue:Singapore Management University (SMU)
        60 Stamford Road, Singapore 178900
        Map:         http://www.smu.edu.sg/campus/images/smu_map.jpg

     


    Besides organising their film screening, outstanding Cineodeon teams will have an opportunity to present on their learning journey at an attendant event, Symposium on Singapore Cinema. Held in September, the Symposiumaims to showcase the achievements of student critics, jurors and programmers to the wider film community in Singapore.

    Interested students should form teams of 5 – 8 and send a courtesy email to daniel.i@asianfilmarchive.org. More information and the registration form will be sent to them. Forms can be reverted via email (info@asianfilmarchive.org) or fax (65431643). An acknowledgement email will be sent to the team leader and teacher i/c upon receipt.

    Registrations are closing soon so sign up as soon as possible!
