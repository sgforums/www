    We are currently looking for talent for our  short film .  The dates for production will be from 25th November - 8th December 2013 (4 filming days). Dialogue in English and Mandarin.

     

    It is a Fantasy Drama short film that will open up your mind, "What if you could go back to the past and change your future?"  The story follows an ex-con with a troubled past struggles to get his life back on track & slowly losing the love of his family. He is given a second chance at redemption when he was given a key that can open doors to his past and decides to mend his wrongs with this gift. Will he succeed or will he experience the effects of changing one's fate.
