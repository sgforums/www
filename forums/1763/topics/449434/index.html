My Girlfriend is a Cyborg - A surprisingly touching film

  • Dalforce 1941
    20 Mar 12, 20:13

    "My Girlfriend is a Cyborg" or in other translations, "Cyborg She", is a Japanese film directed by Kwak Jae-yong who also directed the popular "My Sassy Girl". 

    I have to say that at first glance this film seems like a typical goofy sci-fi comedic light hearted movie. It turns out that this wasn't the case with this film. Towards the end of the movie the sci-fi story transfomed into a more romantic and surprisingly touching film.

    This was rather unexpected for me. I recommand this film for those with two hours to spare.
