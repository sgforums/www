    Saw the new french(with english subtitles) movie DISTRICT 13 directed by Luc Besson shot entirely in france, You won't see the eiffel tower with lovey dovey kissing couples what you will see however will make you agree with me that it is well worth the money to watch this movie.

    The story line is abt this slum area in France full of arms and drug dealers, crime lords with armies carrying powerful guns like GPMG holed up in fortresses or terrorizing the neighbourhood within the slum area called B13 or district 13. The public outcry is so bad that the police have the whole area walled up and cordoned off to keep the criminals in.

    From the start of the movie to the end is non-stop action of machine gun spraying, running and fighting, jumping from building roof top to roof top, fast car chases and acrobatics, is a bit hilarous also.

    If you like Yamakazi and Bourne Identity this is the ultimate it's also a bit like the movie "bad boys"

    I give the movie a high 9 out of 10 Check it out.
