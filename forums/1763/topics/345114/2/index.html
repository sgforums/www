    CNY films yet to turn profit
    Thu, Feb 19, 2009
    The Straits Times
        

    By Boon Chan, Media Correspondent

    The star power of Fann Wong, Christopher Lee and Jack Neo has not been enough to make their Chinese New Year movies profitable.

    Although Fann and Lee's movie, The Wedding Game, and Neo and Gilbert Chan's Love Matters have made more than $1.6 million each at the box office since opening four weeks ago, the movies are unlikely to recoup production costs.

    The Wedding Game, directed by Ekachai Uekrongtham, cost US$1.5 million (S$2.3 million) to make, while Love Matters, rated NC16 because it deals with mature themes such as impotency and sexual needs, was budgeted at $1.3 million.
