    the movie title "Babel" is inspired by the story of the Tower of Babel.

    It was a tower built by a united humanity to reach the heavens. God, observing the unity of humanity in the construction, resolves to destroy the tower and confuse the previously uniform language of humanity. without a uniform language, humanity is unable to communicate with one another and therefore cannot build the tower.

    this pretty much relates to the movie, which centres around miscommunication with one another.

    btw, Babel is composed of two words, "baa" meaning "gate" and "el," "god." Hence, "the gate of god."
