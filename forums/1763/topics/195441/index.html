    This romantic comedy starring Jennifer Aniston and Vince Vaughn looks at the simple things in life that upsets relationships. The bottomline that this movie is trying to bring out, is simply to appreciate and respect your significant other. There's also a very strong presence of the 'Men are from Mars, Women are from Venus' ideology in the film, especially the 'Woman talk' that men shouldn't take at face value.

    I give it 4 stars out of 5.

    Out in a cinema near you 3rd August.
