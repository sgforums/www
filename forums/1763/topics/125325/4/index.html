    Hot damn, speaking as a Miller fan who read Sin City when it was first published, this movie is easily the best adaptation of a comic book I've ever seen.

    Mickey Rourke's Marv is very close to Miller's original portrayal, although I thought he could have been a bit more menacing when he spoke to Cardinal Roarke. Somehow, Clive Owen didn't fit my image of Dwight, but he still pulled it off well. Possible the most disappointing bit of casting was of Bruce Willis as Hartigan - the role needed someone older. Benicio del Toro's portrayal of the dead Rafferty was pretty off, though - he had a much more taunting tone in the original comic.

    The visuals, on the other hand, were absolutely spot on - the impossible slopes on the road into Sin City, the cars flying over humps, just perfect.
