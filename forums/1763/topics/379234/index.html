    The Viennale, a filmfestival in Vienna, is celebrated from 22th of october until 4th of november. The heartpiece of the festival is the tribute to the philipinish regisseur Lino Brocka, who was born in 1939 and died after a car-accident in 1991. In the seventies and eighties of the past century he established himself as a great name of the world-cinema. He produced between 20 years 70 films in a Hollywoodish manner. The movies of Broka will be shown in different cinema-theaters in Vienna.
