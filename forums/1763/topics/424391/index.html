    Z-Day or Zeitgeist-Day is an annual, global event day which occurs in the middle of March, each year. The goal is to increase public awareness of The Zeitgeist Movement and The Venus Project; organisations promoting a transition to a sustainable social system known as a "Resource-Based Economy".
    www.zdayglobal.org     
    www.thezeitgeistmovement.com     www.thevenusproject.com
     
    For the first ever Z-Day in Singapore, we're hosting a screening of the documentary- Zeitgeist: Moving Forward, followed by a discussion/ QnA. We hope that this dialogue will go a long way in improving the general awareness of society.
     
    Zeitgeist: Moving Forward was released on 15 Jan 2011 and screened in more than 340 venues, across 60 countries, in 30 different languages, making it one of the largest independent film releases in history. The Singapore screenings were held at Sinema Old School from 15-25 Jan. 
    www.zeitgeistmovingforward.com
