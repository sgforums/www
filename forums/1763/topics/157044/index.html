    Well...

    Basically, There are ppl born with special magic powers. These are known as the Others. Now, these others band themselves into Dark Others and LIght Others, the latter being the 'good' ones. One day both bands met on a bridge. Neither refused to give way and a battle ensued.

    The leader of the light others, seeing that both sides were so evenly met that there was no way for the battle to be resolved w/o a major loss of lives, decided to call for a truce.

    As part of the truce, they agreed that no party was allowed to try and influence new Others. New Others must be allowed to choose a side of their own free will. As part of the agreement, the Light others set up a group of others to watch over the Dark others, in order to ensure that they do not break the Truce. This is the Niight Watch. The Dark others set up a similar org called the Day watch.

    And so the truce had been kept for many many years. Both sides are balanced and the truce was intact. One day, however, one Other that is very powerful will arise. That other is so powerful that he/she will tip the balance in favor of one side or the other.

    The movie shows the rise of this OTher.
