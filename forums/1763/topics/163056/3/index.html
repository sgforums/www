    Watched it last night at Lido Cineplex. At exactly 12 midnight

    Following 2 paragraphs extracted directly from my blog...

    Ok what can I say? They really achieved their goal: "Have audience believe everything they see on screen"

    This movie is one hell of a animation. 5 million CGI hairs compared to Chicken Little's 76,000. With dinosaurs. If you read Digital Life yesterday, 2/3 of the whole movie is animation. This movie practically gave all the other animation movies a huge punch in the face. Seriously. Everything is so real, from the way the hair reflects the sunlight and its interaction with the surroundings. Facial expressions are perfect as well. I can't help but say it again, ITS VERY REALISTIC! Well of course, they used Shake for compositing

    The actors are great. But at certain part the editing can get a little messy, like when they show it in Ann's POV, when King Kong is running through the forest and everything was very shaky and camera is moving all around.

    I like the sound effects and music. It keeps you on yr toes and nobody can fall asleep as you are always wondering what will happen next, will King Kong win those dinos etc. Your heart is thumping and your mind is constantly spinning anxiously.

    And I CANNOT stand the creepy buggy part... URGH...

    And at the end of it... I can only say... Because of greed, people can even destroy the very thing they love and admire...

    Its a fantastic movie... Will bring you from laughing during the first 2 hours to crying at the last hour...
