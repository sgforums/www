    Dear Film Enthusiasts,

    Perspectives Film Festival is coming up soon! This annual film festival is brought to you by Wee Kim Wee School of Communication and Information, NTU.

    In its third year, themed "Breaking Through Reality", we will be screening five international films that play with the boundaries of reality! This film festival will take place from 25th Nov (Thu) to 28th Nov (Sun) at the Arts House. The line up are as follows:

    Thursday, 25 Nov

    Waltz With Bashir (90min/R21), 8.30PM

    Friday, 26 Nov

    24 City (112min/PG), 8.30PM

    Sat, 27 Nov

    Ten (89min/PG), 1.30PM
    Waltz With Bashir (90min/R21), 4.15PM
    Gimme Shelter (91min/NC16), 8.30PM

    Sun, 28 Nov

    24 City (112min/PG), 1.30PM
    Russian Ark (96min/PG), 4.15PM

    Tickets are priced at $10 (Adults) / $8 (Students/NSFs/PAssion Card Members) per screening. To find out more on how to get them, click here.

    Tickets are running out quick, so grab them fast! We hope to see you there!
