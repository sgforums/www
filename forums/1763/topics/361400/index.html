    Saw this movie at its sneak preview last weekend in 3D. IT'S SO FUNNY! And watching in 3D is definitely an experience. Although a little short (98mins), its pretty exciting with no dull moments at all. The jokes just keep coming at you, especially B.O.B who is just incoherent and full of nonsenses. Furry Insectosaurus is also my favourite cos i just keep laughing whenever he shows up wailing and waving his cute little insect craws with his crazy eyes!

    Definitely worth your money and time. I give it 8/10!
