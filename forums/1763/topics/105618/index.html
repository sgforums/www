    Movie Review

    I saw it last night.

    First of all, I have to clarify. It may be a show abt Vampires but its NOT scary at all.

    Surprisingly, its a pretty funny show as well. WARNING though, you may find some of the jokes too CORNY for your liking.

    I see APPLE everywhere. Is this show sponsored by Apple or something? Jessica Biel's character fights while listening to the IPOD (its very obvious ) Then theres the IMAC at the start and ITUNES near the end! and conversations on mp3 song listening and downloads in the movie itself!

    Hmmm...action wise. Plenty. Its got a decent amount of action involved. and oh yeah, Triple H is in there too. There's one corny scene of him wrestling.

    BABE alert? Oh the female lead for the Baddie Vampire is quite pretty. The teeth,jaws.Sexy (You may disagree...I just have this wierd thing with female vamps. ) Ahhh..and Jessica Biel. She's lovely.

    Storywise, nothing special.

    The OST sounds really cool though. It synchronises well with the action.

    Overall an entertaining action flick with a good dosage of humour.

    I'll give it 3 out of 5 stars.
