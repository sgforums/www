    Dunno whether this has been posted, but here goes..

    "Based on a cell-phone novel that moved 11 million people to tears, "Sky of Love" is a story about a regular girl's three stormy years in high school. While her friends are absorbed about their boyfriends, Mika is clueless about falling in love. She begins receiving phone calls from a boy she doesn't know and is gradually drawn to him. She finally meets him but their happy days soon pass as trouble is brewing on the horizon."

    Watched this in a preview screening yesterday...Those who are going to watch, warn you all first, it's definitely a sobfest movie. The storyline quite predictable but yet it will be tear-jerking. Guys will most probably feel bored and spend most of the time consoling their tearing girlfriends. It has a very typical Japanese movie mood...very tranquil and sombre. Movie is around 2 hours long.

    But personally, I like the movie a lot...so highly recommended if you want to watch some sentimental and touching love story.
