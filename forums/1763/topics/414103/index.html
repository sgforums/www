5 day Dance Film Workshop

  • T.H.E Dance Co
    28 Oct 10, 13:35

    London-based Brazilian dance film maker and video artist Gabriela Tropia will be conducting a 5 day dance film workshop from 13th to 17th dec. This workshop will focus on giving participants an introduction to the making of dance films and it will also allow them to create their very own works over the course of the workshop. The participants' works will be shown at a presentation on the 18th Dec at 2pm. This workshop so far is the first of its kind in the Singaporean dance + film community. $500 for 5 days. Bring your own equipment and get $100 off. Limited vacancies left. 

    Gabriela Tropia received her Masters degree in Dance for the Screen at the London Contemporary Dance School (LCDS) in 2007. She has worked on a series of short films commissioned by the LCDS for the Edge Dance Company. Some of her videos were screened at the One Minute Film Festival at The Place Theatre (London, 2007) and the Danca em Foco (Rio de Janeiro, 2007). In 2008, Gabriela Tropia's dance film, Tableleg (Campinas, 2006) was part of the Live Screen at Sadler's Wells Theatre.
