    K-20: LEGEND OF THE MASK

     

    In the fictional capital "Teito" where the aristocrats continue to thrive, there are rumours about a mysterious thief, who only steals from the rich and aids the poor. People call him K-20, (The Phantom Thief with 20 faces). Heikichi, a circus acrobat was conned and mistaken as K-20 and subsequently arrested by Detective Akechi, In the midst of clearing his name, Heikichi rescued Yoko, the Duchess, Detective Akechi’s betrothed fiancée. In the non-stop battle with K-20, the true identity of the thief may finally be revealed!

    Rating: PG

    Official Website http://www.festivefilms.com/K-20/

    Opens 19 February 2009.
