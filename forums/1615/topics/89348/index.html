    Make yourself a Tropical Smoothie!!!
    But what is a Smoothie?
    A smoothie is a delicious, nutritious meal in a cup. A smoothie made with fresh fruits is high in carbohydrates providing you with a high energy fuel while containing little or no fat. Combined with other nutrient-packed items like low-fat milk, light yoghurt, & fruit juice, it is a high powered drink.

    Since a typical smoothie contains both fruit and juice, it counts as 2 servings of fruit, plus the milk & yoghurt give you a serving of high calcium food. What a delicious way to get your daily requirements!

    In the Tropical Smoothie recipe below, I use local fresh fruits and other ingredients readily found in Singapore. If you want to get really creative, you can add a little boost like ginseng powder or wheat germ.

    Care to try a delicious smoothie? It's a great way to cool off after exercise.
    Tropical Smoothie - Serves 2

    Ingredients
    1 tub (150-200 g) Low Fat Yoghurt
    1 cup Hi-Lo Milk
    Â½ cup Orange juice*
    1 Tbsp. Honey
    1 cup Frozen fruit**
    1 Â½ cups Crushed ice

    Method
    Place all of the ingredients into a blender & blend till smooth.
    Pour into 2 tall glasses, garnish with a pineapple ring.
    Serve immediately.

    *Other fruit juices can be used to give it a different flavour. Try mango, guava, soursop or apple juice.
    **Slice up your favourite fruit like mango, banana, pineapple, guava. Freeze them for at least 2 hours. Fresh fruit can also be used if you do not have time for this step, but your smoothie will be more runny.
    Nutritional Analysis per serving
    212 calories; 3 grams fat, 9 grams protein, 41 grams carbohydrate,
    292 mg calcium
