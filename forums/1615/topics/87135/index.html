    In recent times, a growing number of people have sought information about nutrition and health. To meet this increasing desire for knowledge, Dr. Chen has toured the world, sharing the vital message of nutritional immunology. As a scientist, Dr. Chen has spoken internationally before numerous scientific organizations and medical universities. She believes that as scientists and researchers from around the world join together, their combined efforts will advance a universal goal - a healthier future for all humankind. Recognizing that the future depends on innovative new findings, Dr. Chen also contributes to scholarship programs for students pursuing studies in the immunological sciences.



    Through study and research, Dr, Chen has made nutritional immunology accessible and understandable to people of all interests and lifestyles. Speaking at seminars and conventions across the globe, she encourages people everywhere to share her message of knowledge and health. In addition, Dr. Chen educates the public through conferences and school presentations. Dr. Chen also utilizes literature to teach the relationship between nutrition and good health, making these important principles available to people everywhere. By request, Dr. Chen has contributed articles on nutritional immunology to several international magazines. As a company, E. Excel publishes a quarterly magazine, which features the latest findings in immunological research.

    The increasing demand for information about nutritional immunology led Dr. Chen to write two books. Nutritional Immunology, now in its third edition, offers an understandable overview of the relationships among the immune system, disease, and nutrition. Plant Foods and Nutritional Immunology: A Scientific Approach, Dr. Chen's most recent book, examines the relationship between nutrition and the immune system in more detail. Additionally, Dr. Chen has created an exciting and informative video entitled Nutritional Immunology: A New Science. Upon its release, it was immediately aired by Public Broadcasting System stations and affiliates around the country, and an increasing number of stations continue to broadcast it.

    Dr. Chen believes in serving the public, in building up our communities, and in contributing to a stronger, healthier society
