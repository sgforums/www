    Chicken and Cheddar Quesadillas

    This recipe serves: 4

    Preparation time : 15 minutes

    Cooking time : 10 minutes

    Ingredients
    4 large flour tortillas
    1 cup grated, low-fat cheddar cheese
    1 cup cooked, shredded chicken breast meat
    1/2 cup frozen corn kernels, thawed
    1/2 cup diced fresh tomatoes
    1/4 cup cilantro leaves
    1 lime, cut into wedges
    1/2 cup salsa
    1/2 cup non-fat sour cream

    Cooking Instructions
    1. Preheat the oven to 350ÂºF.

    2. Lay the tortillas out on a work surface. Sprinkle half of each tortilla with cheese. Top the cheese with chicken, corn kernels, tomatoes and cilantro leaves. Fold the tortillas in half and place them on a nonstick baking sheet.

    3. Bake the tortillas for 10 minutes, or until they are thoroughly warmed.

    4. Cut each tortilla in wedges and serve with lime, salsa and sour cream.
