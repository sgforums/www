A Truly Magical Christmas 2008

  • Blood_donation
    17 Dec 08, 15:39

    Want to save three lives this Christmas? Every hour, 15 units of blood are needed in Singapore.

     

    Come forward and Support A Truly Magical Christmas 2008 - a blood donation drive organised by the Red Cross Humanitarian Network (RCHN) - Singapore Management University (SMU) Chapter.

     

    Event details

    Venue  : Dhoby Ghaut NEL Station

    Date    : 19th to 21st December

    Time    : 12pm to 6pm

     

    You are encouraged to come forward and make a blood donation if you:

    • Are between 16 and 60 years old*;
    • Weigh at least 45 Kg;
    • Are generally in good health;
    • Have not had any symptoms of infection for at least 1 week e.g. sore throat, cough, runny nose, diarrhea; and fever for the last 3 weeks

     

    *16 and 17 years olds need to get parent consent form which can be downloaded from http://www.redcorss.org.sg

     

    For more information on blood donation, please visit www.redcross.org.sg or call 6220 0183
