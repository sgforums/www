    healthcare here in singapore is costlier than city like bangkok and KL, not so sure about HK's case. But I am sure you can quality healthcare here. Also, your employer should be able to enroll you in some group health insurance system. Also a visit to private practitioner in neighbourhood centre should cost you no greater than 25USD per visit.

    Depending on where is your office, you may want to choose housing nearer to your workplace. Singapore is a small island with 5M population, the public transport is filling to the brim. The traffic is very heavy during rush hours. Car ownership is very very costly.

    Living expense in low in singapore, food is cheap here. US2200/month for monthly expensive put you in the upper class living.
