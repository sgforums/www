    Recently awarded Zouk’s Favourite Resident DJ, at Zouk’s 19th Anniversary, djB’s memorable WeLuvHouse parties are reputed for the soul, grooves and happy vibes that come intrinsically with them. With an affinity for big room sounds that have hints of Tribal, Techy & Afro-centric percussive rhythms, he’s also spread the love of House music through 2 successful releases on the Defected label. Just having completed a slew of gigs in Vietnam, South Korea, Indonesia, Hong Kong and Ministry of Sound, London, djB’s gets on stage with percussion extraordinaire none other than Shovell who never fails to liven any night with his manic drumming and dramatic get ups!
