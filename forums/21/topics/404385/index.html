    SYMMETRY & VERTIGO presents ALEX M.O.R.P.H. & MARTIN ROTH on 16th July 2010!


    The World Cup will be over then but it's time for Symmetry to kick things up a notch. This 16th July, in partnership with VERTIGO & Flatline Productions, we are proud to host two of Germany's leading Electronic Dance Music artists, Alex M.O.R.P.H. and Martin Roth!

    Having had groundbreaking releases and remixes on Paul Van Dyk's VANDIT, Armin Van Buuren's ARMADA and many other major labels, these two artists soared in full force to global recognition. 2008 sees Alex M.O.R.P.H. landing in the DJ Mag's prestigious Top 100 while Martin Roth received Beatport's "Star of 2008", alongside Eric Prydz and Deadmau5.

    Having rained audio-fire worldwide over the past few years, Alex M.O.R.P.H. and Martin Roth will finally be making their debut in Singapore, this 16th July!

    Artists Biographies :

    ALEX M.O.R.P.H. (GERMANY) // VANDIT // FENOLOGY

    Silhouetted against the backlights and lasers, you can’t miss Mr MORPH behind the 1s & 2s. Trademark truckers hat uniformly in position, his emotive, eclectic trance soaring out and up from the speakers, he delivers one determinedly show-stopping performance after another. Once heard, once seen – he’s never forgotten.

    MARTIN ROTH (GERMANY) // VANDIT // LUNATIQUE

    By his characteristic strong DJ appearances on every continent & his unique distinctive style, Martin Roth astonishes the crowds with skills and unpredictable open-minded track selection what resulted in end of 2009 with an international residency at the Ministry of Sound club in London!

    EFFEN (SINGAPORE) // SYMMETRY // IN CHARGE RECORDS

    Ever since Effen literally went from bedroom to big room with his debut at Singapore's superclub Zouk in 2005, no one was quite prepared for what's coming next. 5 years on, one of Singapore’s fastest rising talent has his feet firmly placed in the Singapore club scene.

    DYZ (SINGAPORE) // VILLAINS

    Through his experience as a professional Audio Engineer, DYZ got the opportunity to work with well-known DJs such as Sebastian Leger, Dennis Ferrer, James Zabeila & Nic Fancuilli and the list goes on. He freely express himself through different genres of music ranging from Progressive House, Techno, Tech House, Electro tinged Uplifting House and Twisted Dirty House.
