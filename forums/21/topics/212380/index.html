ERDINGER OKTOBERFEST 2006

  • FireIce
    27 Sep 06, 15:21


    synopsis

    Erdinger Weissbier brings the festive spirit of the world's largest and most celebrated festival to Singapore. Attracting over 6 million people to Munich each year, the Oktoberfest is known for great food, entertainment and exceptional beer. Erdinger Oktoberfest 2006 will commence with a Street Parade and Keg Tapping ceremony at China Square Central, followed by four days of Oktoberfest-styled entertainment. The highlight - performances by authentic German band HÃ¼ttenpower, is guaranteed to bring the house down with original songs in both German and English.

    A mouth-watering spread of traditional German food, along with great tasting Erdinger wheat beer, will also be available at special prices. Ticket price includes a choice of a delicious 3-course meal and an Erdinger beer.

    DATE

    12 Oct - 15 Oct 2006
    12th (Thu) - 14th Oct (Sat), 5pm till late
    15th (Sun), 11am till 8pm

    Language: English

    TICKET PRICE (Exclude Booking Fee)

    Standard - S$31 (includes a choice of a 3 course meal and an Erdinger Beer. Subsequent food orders and Erdinger beer will be available at special prices.)

    Sale of tickets for each day's festival will close at 5 pm on the previous day.

    Tickets are available at the door at S$38 nett (booking fee not applicable). Subject to availability.

    Please add to above price $2 Booking Fee for tickets above $20 and $1 Booking Fee for tickets below $20. Charges include GST where applicable.
