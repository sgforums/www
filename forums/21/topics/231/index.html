    Shatec do teach you bartending. But the 3 years dip course does not concentrate on bartending alone. To learn bartending, you must actually have hands on training. Once you are an experienced bartender, you can check out some courses like the John Wyhte course Far East. www.iba.au.com

    After which for flair bartending, you can check out a Singapore flair association like www.overpour.com or international : check out www.barflair.org
