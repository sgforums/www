    About us 
    <Good News Everyone> is a friendly-progression driven, relatively new guild situated on US-Frostmourne (Oceanic, GMT +10, AEST/AEDT), Alliance side. We focus on clearing high-end content, both in WoTLK and into Cataclysm, in a competitive and friendly environment while ensuring that needs and expectations of raiders are met. 
    The guild founders are made up of members who have experience in 11/12 icc 25man, 8/12 icc10man heroic (Citadel and Bloodwing cleared). Many of our guild members are based in Singapore or Malaysia.

    Raids 
    We are currently recruiting members to fill up our main raid spots before starting our 25man raids. We will provisionally start 25man progression raids by first week of June, but in the meantime, 10man ICC runs will be taking place.

    Loot system 
    Loot council for important items with EPGP, with decay. Important items like trinkets, weapons or marks will be handled by the Loot council until they become obsolete, and EPGP will be a gauge to whether we should give the loot to a player or not. As we are a new guild, we will be starting with a blank sheet on the EPGP system. 
    The reason for the Loot Council and EPGP decay will be to ensure that the raid is being geared up in a balanced fashion, and to ensure that players more willing to rack up GP by upgrading themselves consistently will not be placed at a disadvantage, while punishing EP whoring for especially coveted items. 


    What we expect from you 
    People who are keen to raid and will do adequate research beforehand, communicate with the raid and raid leader well. 
    Be able to take criticism and be willing to improve. No one is perfect, but everyone should strive for perfection. And players who will self assess their own performance in raid and try their best to improve. We dont want people who criticize others and do not realize their own mistakes. 
    At least 75% attendence for raids. Higher attendence players will be treated with more incentives or will be able to join our ranks as a main raider 
    A proper internet connection, with a reasonably low latency. Frequent disconnections, especially during raids (resulting in downtime), will not be tolerated. 

    What you can expect from us? 
    Raiding incentives: Guild repairs for main raids, subsidised flasks and other consumables. 
    Transparency: transparency on the status of the guild bank. Constant updates and communication with raid members on any and all relevant issues pertaining to raids, PvE, and WoW in general, especially those affecting raiders such as guild administration and inner workings. 
    Willingness to take positive feedback from members, ensuring that their own realistic expectations of the guild are being met. 

    Interested applicants are invited to visit our guild website at http://goodnewseveryone.guildlaunch.com, where an application may be submitted, and will be reviewed promptly. Shortlisted applicants can expect to be either trialled in-game, or at least interviewed on Vent. Once agian, please take note that provisionally, the guild's first official EPGP activated raid will take place in first week of June.
