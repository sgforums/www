    Tier 4 is the 'new Tier 0', where you can obtain it quite easily. The purpose of T4 is to allow players that did not have the ability to obtain T3, to get equivalent gear.

    Players would tend use Tier 3 or Tier 4 to leapfrog to obtain Tier 5 gear.
