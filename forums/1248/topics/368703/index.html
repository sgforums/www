    Battle of the Year (BOTY) Asia is an established and regionally recognised platform for innovative B-Boy crews from all over Asiato showcase their spectacular hip hop moves and grooves. They gather together to compete against one another, with the top three winning groups representing Asia in the international finals held in Germany, Europe later this year.

    Singapore is privileged to host this regionally acclaimed event here for the first time. The best break dancers from Korea, Taiwan, Japan, China, Thailand, Malaysia, Indonesia, Cambodia, Laos and Singapore will stage their latest choreography in Singapore. Don’t miss the ground-breaking and heart-racing action “live” here in Downtown East!

    BOTY Asia Judges

     Bboy Born from Korea

    B-boy Born aka Yoo Hyun hails from Seoul, Korea. Born in 1986 the year of the Tiger, Born has come to be known throughout the world as the embodiment of the Classic B-boy with his ninja precise footwork, his comprehensive understanding and interpretation of the breakbeat and the music that became the soundtrack of NYC circa 1980's.  The precision angles and body lines in which he lithely and effortlessly maneuvers his body through is not only supremely mesmerizing but leaves the viewer with a distinct sense of deja vu and a sublime sense of nostalgia as if the spirit of a bygone hip hop era has bewitched the body of this young South Korean. 

    Since 2003 the artist known as Born has begun to compile a vast and impressive resume of wins at international competitions, participation in highly successful theatre shows throughout europe and has recently been recognized as an official ambassador or Korean Culture by the Korea Tourism Organization and the Korean Ministry of Culture alongside his crew mates the Rivers Crew.  Born is also a member of the World Famous Project Soul Collective, a grouped comprised of the top dancers from Korea, Project Soul is also the 3-peat World Champs at the UK Bboy World Championships. Born is also affiliated and is a member of Korea's Floor Gangz, Flavor Squad USA and the Mighty Zulu Kingz Worldwide one of the most prestigious and historically recognized B-boy crews as the crew and its members date back to 1973.

    A dynamic force within any structure whether in organized competitions or in choreographed stage pieces, Born continues to evolve and excel, always searching for inspiration on the dance floor, cutting through space with his mercurial footwork, surrounded by a circular wave of jostling bodies that always seem to form impromptu whenever and wherever the beat takes him.  He is a definite example and proponent within the true evolution of an artform, a dance form yet Bboy Born created a trademark Character all his own, deeply influenced by his blood and hertiage yet has not strayed too far from the original and the traditions of a deeply proud culture and people.

    Poe ONE

    Puerto Rican born Poe One lives in Los Angeles and is a member of the “Style Elements” crew. Poe is active as dancer, teacher, judge and also as event promoter in his country and all over the world. He has been member of the jury at several big international events such as the Battle of the Year or the Red Bull BC ONE 2005. Poe is well known for his excellent teaching skillz so he is also travelling the world holding dancing masterclasses and workshops.

    Poe One was in Singapore judging the BOTY Sg 2007 competition and he is definitely looking forward to judge and conduct the break dance workshop in Singapore’s first SHINE BOTY ASiA, 1st & 2nd August respectively at D’ Marquee

Leader Bboy Katsu, previously with "Mighty Zulu Kings" "Freshsox" and "7Dollers", is well renown world wide and has competed and won international competition. Not only has he competed, he is also recognized as Judge international in B-boy competitions.AllAREA, other members are also known top rank B-boys in Japan and internationally with their own standing, namely "Taisuke" who won 2nd place in BCONE2008; "Toshiki" won 2nd place at BOTY2006 under "Turnphrase crew".     FORMOSA Crew

    An elite team formed by all the top b-boys from all over Taiwan, therefore named "Formosa". Teamed up since BOTY 2004, with team members from five different teams, KGB, TBC, Happy Soul, and TC. "Formosa" represents Taiwan, a beautiful island. We care about our dearest love, our country, our people, and our island with gratitude and appreciation. We use culture to shape our pride and soul as Taiwanes Living in this beautiful island, we record every inch of this island step by step. Formosa will dance their name for the world to know, and let Taiwan hip hop dancing be seen and be respected in the world. Formosa has participated in numerous international competitions and was invited to perform in many countries. In Taiwan, we have also participated in many competitions and performed in dramas and commercials. Formosa has competed as a team in BOTY, B Boy Unit, Coca Cola Hip Hop Dancing competition, China Guong-Chou National Hip Hop Dancing competition...etc. A group of friends who dance together and who have worked their way to the world together is the best thing! Our team recruits B Boys from younger generations and we aim to train better B Boys... to allow younger generation a chance to come with us to see the world and to gain more experiences. Wherever there is an B Boy event, we will be there; to up our strength as B Boys and to open a door for Taiwan to see the world and to step onto stages all over the world.
