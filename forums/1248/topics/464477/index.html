    Hi Guys!!!

    We are going to organize a large scale flash mob to help raise fund for the World Toilet Organization(WTO)!

    2.5 billion people have no access to toilets and proper sanitation. They have no other choice than to defecate in the open, contaminating water sources and increasing the likelihood of contracting life threatening diseases such as acute diarrhea or cholera, which results in an annual death toll of 1.8 million young children and adults. WTO’s mission is to build up the proper sanitation for these people.

    So we are going to perform a large scale flash mob and film a video to attract people's attention to this pressing issue. Here are the details:


    Actual Flash Mob: 9th March 2013 (Venue and Timing: To be Announced!)


    Training Date:

    3) 23th February 2013 10am-12pm (Saturday)
    4) 28th February 2013 7.30pm-9.30pm (Thursday)
    5) 2nd March 2013 10am-12pm (Saturday, Final Rehearsal)


    There will also be a charity auction event with interesting and exciting prizes after the flash mob on 9th March 2012.


    If you could make it for at least 2 rehearsal sessions, the final rehearsal, and of course the actual flash mob, please join our team! You can make a difference! Let's enjoy dancing and the joy of giving!!!
