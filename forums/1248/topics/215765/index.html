    DANCING IN THE STREETS @ CHINGAY
    Flaunt your best moves, and get a chance to perform in front of thousands at Chingay Parade of DreamS!

    Join the PULSE HIP HOP KNOCK-OUT and win attractive cash prizes of up to $2,000! Registration is FREE; simply sign up now! Registration closes 20 October 2006. The preliminary round will be held on 29 October 2006.

    Outstanding teams will be invited to peform in one of the main items at the Chingay Parade of DreamS happening on 23 and 24 Feburary 2007.

    All teams will also have the privilege of attending FREE Hip Hop workshops conducted by a renowned dance instructor from South Korea.

    There are two categories in this competition:

    Open category is open to 13 to 35 years
    Under 18 category is open to 17 years and below

    One of the highlights in Chingay 2007 main parade programme where 12 P65 MPs, led by Mr Teo Ser Luck, Parlimentary Secretary, Ministry of Community Development, Youth and Sports (MCYS), will perform Hip Hop dance with 300 dancers!

    In this three-minute item, an 18m long float will sashay down the parade route accompanied by five trishaws. 300 outstanding Hip Hop dancers will surround the float and trishaws and break into a funky Hip Hop dance alongside the 12 P65 MPs. The MPs will perform one-minute Hip Hop choreography in this item and will distribute sweets to the audience and guests.

    Led by Mr Teo Ser Luck, Parliamentary Secretary, Ministry of Community Development, Youth and Sports, and Member of Parliament for Pasir Ris-Punggol Group Representation Constituency (GRC). The other participating MPs are:
