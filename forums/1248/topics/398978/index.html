    DANCE @ SWINGAPORE 2010
    Sat 19 Jun 2010, 10 am to 5 pm, Jitterbugs Swingapore
    Jitterbugs Swingapore's annual Dance @ Swingapore Day is an eagerly anticipated event amongst all dancers - old and new.  During this event, we open all our studios to run sample classes of all the dance styles we offer.
     
    From Lindy Hop to Ballet, Lyrical Hip Hop to Grrl Power, this is a chance to try out a new dance style ... or two! And to top it off, there is only one entrance fee for the day and you are free to enjoy the buffet of classes.  The classes run from 10 am - 6 pm, so with all 5 of our dance studios running, there are a lot of classes to choose from!

    Whether you are a newbie or a veteran to dance, you will find something new, so do come along - and bring a friend too!

    Fee:
    Early bird - S$10*
    At the door - S$15

    * Early bird tickets are now on sale and will only be sold from 2 to 31 May 2010.
