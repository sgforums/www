    SINGAPORE: Escape Theme Park at Downtown East will be operating its last rides on November 26.

    NTUC Club said this is because the theme park will be redeveloped to meet the changing needs of guests.

    Details of the redevelopment plans, the first phase of a revamp for the entire Downtown East, will be announced later.

    Escape Theme Park has entertained more than four million people since it opened in 2000.

    NTUC Club said a special promotion has been extended from November 19 to 26 to thank patrons for a week before the theme park closes.

    There'll be unlimited rides for two at S$10.

    Admission will also be free for those who purchase full-price admission tickets to Wild Wild Wet on the same day.
