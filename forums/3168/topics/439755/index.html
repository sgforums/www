10,000 participants in HomeTeamNS-New Balance run

  • QX179R
    16 Oct 11, 15:10

    SINGAPORE: About 10,000 runners took part in the HomeTeamNS-New Balance REAL Run on Sunday morning.

    Runners could choose between a 10 km and half-marathon (21 km) distance.

    Minister of State for Home Affairs & Foreign Affairs Masagos Zulkifli flagged off the 21 km race at 6:30 am and the 10 km event at 7:00 am at Changi Exhibition Centre.

    It's the only race in Singapore which has four punishing terrains - taxiway, road, sand and trail.

    The winner of the 21 km Men's Open is Onesmus Muindi from Kenya, who clocked 1 hour, 8 minutes and 9 seconds.

    First to cross the finish line in the 21 km Women's Open is 31-year-old Singaporean Qi Hui, in 1 hour, 21 minutes and 51 seconds.
