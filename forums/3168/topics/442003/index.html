    You meditate on something. Not meditate for the sake of meditation.

    Monks in the mountains meditate on the meaning of something.

    Businessmen meditate on how to sort out their problems. And so on.

    We either meditate haphazadly or in an orderly manner.

    Most or us use the haphazad way.

    No need to pay money to learn how to meditate in an orderly manner.

    Just sit down and choose one item to think about, and do your best to keep the thought on that item. Like why always at any place you work, you have at least one enemy. Just think about that over and over again. Any other thoughts come in, stop and go back your chosen thought. Be mindful of your breathing. Breathe naturally.  See that you are not disturbed. Just think on the thought, without any emotions. Slowly with practice, your will feel peace and be aware of the answer as well.
