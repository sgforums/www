NEWater Visitor Centre to close for renovations

  • QX179R
    16 Apr 12, 18:08

    SINGAPORE: The NEWater Visitor Centre will be closed for renovations from April 17 till June 22.

    When the centre reopens in June this year, visitors will be able to look forward to more exciting and interactive exhibits.

    These will showcase how NEWater is produced as well as other aspects of the Singapore water story.

    The NEWater Visitor Centre, which is located at 40 Koh Sek Lim Road, was opened in February 2003.

    The centre highlights the importance of water and how Singapore leverages on advances in technology to reclaim water.
