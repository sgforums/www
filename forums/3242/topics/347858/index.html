    Hi Guys, RAS would like to share with you something nice. As we all know that toilets, especially in coffeeshops, are always known as the dirtiest and a 'NEVER WILL' visit premises no matter how urgent you are.

    With our launch in November 2008, alot more coffeeshops owners are aware that the cleanliness of the toilet are important. They go through upgrading, some will renovate the whole toilet to give user a better facilities.

    I would like to share with you guys something great. Just this month, we had 3 coffeeshops owners taking part in our Happy Toilet Program (HTP) and we have already audit the toilet.

    I must say, the toilets in these 3 locations are clean, of course, you cant expect the toilet to have a cleanliness like shopping malls or hotels, but it is clean in the sense that it hits our basic 3 stars criteria. Coffeeshops are trying their best and I hope you guys can see it. Well, if you guys have the time, feel free to visit the coffeeshops toilet which is located at Block 1A, 2A and 4A, Woodlands Centre. The toilets are already certified. Feel free to comment here if u guys wanna share or have any doubts.. Cheers.
