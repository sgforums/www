    Which are the best beaches you have been to and why?

    For me, I find these beaches very good.

    Redang Island, Malaysia - Crystal clear water. The marine park is great for novice swimmers to get a close view of fishes and corals. A good place to learn snorkling.

    Palau, Palau - A remote chain of islands in the Pacifics. A good get away from the cities. Here, you get to see shallow beaches that can stretch from one island to another at a distance a few hundred metres away (Yes, that means you can walk on the water for a few hundred metres to reach the neighbouring island.

    Got a lot more. But these are the ones I find worth mentioning for now.
