  • Gedanken

    16PF = Cattell's 16 Personality Factor profile - it's a garden-variety personality instrument.

    MMPI = Minnesota Multiphasic Personality Inventory - a somewhat more heavy-duty instrument, used to determine if you're a nutjob, and if so, what kind of nutjob you are. Sections of the MMPI are also used as a screening tool for uniformed services or anyhting to do with firearms.

    STAI = State-Trait Anxiety Inventory - used to measure your level of anxiety, and to see if it's a short- or long-term thing.

    WAIS = Weschler Adult Intelligence Scale - put simply, an IQ test.

    I'm certified to administer all of these (amongst others), but the only one that I've touched in the last 2 years is the 16PF. Personally, I'd rather use Raven's Standard and Advanced Pattern Matrices instead of the WAIS to assess someone's level of intelligence - it's quicker and less culture-biased, and it tells me more of what I want to know.
