    All of Ditzy's referred articles are non-age-specific. IIRC, the DSM-IV diagnoses are only reliable for clients aged 18 and over, and this also applies to the ICD.

    Also, with DSM-IV, you need to make a point to ensure that you perform proper differential diagnoses - these are the items that state something like "the condition is NOT due to X....".
