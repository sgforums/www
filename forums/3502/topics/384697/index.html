    1)

    5/9 tables + 2/7 chairs = 44 furnitures ----- (1)

    9/9 tables + 7/7 chairs = 120 furnitures

    45/9 tables + 35/7 chairs = 600 furnitures ----- (2)

    ( This means 5 times the current amount of tables and chairs )

    From (1), 45/9 tables + 18/7 chairs = 396 furnitures ----- (3)

    From (2) and (3), 17/7 chairs = 600 - 396 = 204 furnitures

    == > 1/7 chair = 12 furnitures  ----- (4)

    4/9 tables are made of wood

    From (1) and (4), 5/9 tables = 20 tables

    Hence 4/9 tables = 16 tables 

     

    2)

    Original amount of girls = 5 units

    [3 units + 5] / [5 units + 3] = 5/7

    ==> 25 units + 15 = 21 units + 35

    ==> 4 units = 20 children

    ==> 1 unit = 5 children

    Original amount of girls = 5 X 5 = 25 girls
