    It seems I've come across this forum many years too late!

    I have been suffering from severe anxiety and panic attacks for over four years, with intermittent agorophobia because of how afraid I am of the panic attacks. I've tried all sorts of therapies I could find, but nothing has been a 'cure'. 

    There seems to be a big gap in affordable and effective therapies in Singapore, and I have been looking for support groups but haven't been able to find any that are still running. I would like to create a group of people who really understand what it means to go through or live with this disorder.

    Please let me know if you would potentially like to get involved. My email address is findingmywings3@gmail.com. If you are interested, please connect with me by email and I will update you with any progress and would love to share ideas of how we can get together and support each other.
