Rules of the Bus Trivia Challenge

  • Class 55 Deltic Fan
    19 Aug 07, 14:19
    01. There should be only one active question thread in the Bus Trivia Challenge forum any one time.

    02. Questions should be based on buses from Singapore and overseas

    03. Questions can be easy or challenging. No trick questions: there is a one point penalty for posting an impossible/trick question.

    A maximum of 3 answers may be sought per question. If an answer has more possibilities than that, any 3 correct answers from the over-all selection will count as a solution.

    04. Each player is allowed 1 attempt per question. The first player to post the correct answer sets the next question. (You cannot answer your own question.)

    If the top placed player is 10 or more points away from his/her nearest rival, he/she must wait 24 hours before he/she may answer the next question.

    05. If a question is not answered 24 hours after initial posting, the member who posted the question must offer clues to keep the game moving.

    If a question is not answered 48 hours after initial posting (i.e. 24 hours after a clue is given), the member who posted the question must reveal the answer to close the question. Then anyone except the member who posted the previous 'unanswerable' question may post the next new question.

    06. Before a new question can be posted, the poster of the previous question has to confirm the answer within 24 hours (failure to do so will incur a one point penalty, and the floor will be opened for a new question). The winner must post a new question in 24 hours.

    07. To give up a turn to post a new question, just say pass; a one point penalty applies per pass.

    If a new question is not posted 24 hours after confirmation of the previous correct answer, anyone may set the next question.

    09. Scoring system:
    One point for the first correct answer.

    One point penalty, or exclusion from play for one turn if no points to deduct, for posting an impossible question.

    One point penalty, or exclusion from play for one turn if no points to deduct, for failing to confirm the answer within 24 hours.

    One point penalty, or exclusion from play for one turn if no points to deduct, for giving up a turn to post a new question.

    One point penalty, or exclusion from play for one turn if no points to deduct, for failing to post a new question within 24 hours after the correct answer is revealed.
