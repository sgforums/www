GE: Candidates should have their hearts in right place, says

  • Clivebenss
    27 Mar 11, 00:50

    By Alvina Soh | Posted: 26 March 2011 2334 hrs

    SINGAPORE: Potential candidates in the upcoming General Election should "have their hearts in the right place", said Minister for Home Affairs and Law K Shanmugam said.

    Mr Shanmugam said this at an event in Nee Soon East, organised by the North West CDC

    "Apart from the heart, they must have the ability to connect with people and the ability to integrate, bring together the grassroots and they must have that desire to work very hard. Because it is hard work," Mr Shanmugam said.

    He added that people should not assume that having a diverse background means a candidate can connect better with residents.

    Mr Shanmugam said this in response to comments that the PAP's candidates are not diverse enough.

    The event, which aims to foster stronger community spirit and good neighbourliness, was attended by some 2,000 residents in Nee Soon East.

    -CNA/wk
