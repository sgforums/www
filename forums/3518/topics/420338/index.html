    Saturday, January 15 2011 · 4:00pm - 6:00pm

     

    Speaker's Corner (Hong Lim Park)

     

    Kenneth Jeyaretnam, on behalf of THE REFORM PARTY, warmly welcomes every Singaporean who cares about the future of our Nation to join him and his team on 15 Jan 2011 for a picnic in the park at this, our inaugural rally event.

    On stage, a mix of very special guest speakers and speakers from the party will talk about the key issues facing Singaporeans and outline the Party's concrete, alternative and viable proposals.

    Refreshments will be provided and there will be a display of memorabilia and publications, so you can find out exactly who we are, what we stand for and why we do what we do.

    This is the final stretch before G.E., come along and find out more about Team RP, get up close and personal with our candidates, our legal clinic team, our women's wing, our members and our CEC and see for yourself why our plans will 'Make it Right' for Singapore and Singaporeans.

    The RP Rally & Picnic will be a fun day for both young and old alike with a magician on hand mingling with the crowd, and a DJ, sound system and plenty of good cheer.

    Wear something yellow if you can or bring a yellow mat to sit on. Help us bring change to Singapore by spreading the word and telling everyone you know about this special day.

    So, make a date with Democracy. Make it there on 15 January 2011 and start the new year with REFORM!
