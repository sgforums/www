    Apr 23, 2011 - Straits Times

    By Jermyn Chow

    SENIOR Minister Goh Chok Tong urged voters in Marine Parade GRC to compare the People's Action Party's five-member team and the opposition's National Solidarity Party, rather than focus on the two youngest woman candidates.

    The PAP's Tin Pei Ling, 27, and NSP's Miss Nicole Seah, 24, have been the targets of netizens since they were fielded for the coming elections.

    'I am the leader of Team Marine Parade. I have four other very strong members in team Marine Parade. Likewise, you should look at NSP as a team,' said SM Goh during a walkabout of the constituency with his fellow GRC candidates on Saturday morning.

    The other candidates in his team include Mr Seah Kian Peng, Dr Fatimah Lateef and newcomers Brigadier-General (NS) Tan Chuan-Jin and Ms Tin.

    The NSP's line-up, unveiled earlier this week, includes businessman Ivan Yeo Tiong Boon, former one-term Nee Soon Central MP Cheo Chai Chen, project director Spencer Ng, warehouse supervisor Abdul Salim Harun and advertising executive Ms Seah, who is the youngest candidate contesting the May 7 polls.
