Polling Day is a public holiday

  • Clivebenss

    Apr 21, 2011 - Straits Times

    THE Ministry of Manpower on Thursday reminded companies to treat Polling Day on May 7 like any other public holiday.

    All employees covered by the Employment Act are entitled to paid public holidays, including the public holiday on Polling Day, it said in a press release.

    If an employee is required to work on Polling Day, he is entitled to an extra day's salary for the day's work, or be given another day off.

    Employees not required to work on that day are also entitled to one day off in lieu, or be given one day's pay, as per other public holidays falling on a Saturday.

    GE: Elections Department stays open on Friday
    By S Ramesh | Posted: 21 April 2011 2212 hrs

    SINGAPORE: Friday may be a public holiday but the Elections Department said it will remain open.

    In a statement, the department said it is doing this to assist candidates in filing their necessary applications.

    It will be open from 8:30am to 6pm Friday.

    It will also be open on Saturday from 8:30am to 1pm.
