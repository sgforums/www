    Mar 29, 2011 - Straits Times

    By Teo Wan Gek

    SENIOR Minister Goh Chok Tong has gotten his wish of having a strong candidate to understudy and succeed him in helming the Marine Parade GRC team one day.

    In a post on the Marine Parade PAP branch's Facebook page on Tuesday, MParader, SM Goh said that the People's Action Party team will usher in one of the two former military top brass - outgoing army chief Major-General Chan Chun Sing, 41, and Brigadier-General Tan Chuan Jin, 42, as a new candidate in Marine Parade GRC.

    He also confirmed that fellow MP Dr Ong Seh Hong will be retiring from politics.

    The post read: 'The good-hearted doctor is selflessly stepping down from Ubi for one of the two generals to understudy me.'

    The Straits Times, quoting party sources, had earlier reported that the likely new candidate is BG Tan and he will be introduced to residents this weekend.

    In a Facebook post last Saturday, SM Goh wrote that he had asked Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong to send him a 'strong candidate who has the potential to be part of the fourth-generation core team.'
