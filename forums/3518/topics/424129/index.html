SDA Secretary-General Desmond Lim quits SPP, joins SJP

  • tranquilice

    SINGAPORE: There have been changes to the Singapore Justice Party, a component party of the Singapore Democratic Alliance, following the Singapore People's Party withdrawal from the alliance.

    The alliance's Secretary-General, Desmond Lim Bak Chuan has quit the SPP, which is headed by Potong Pasir MP Chiam See Tong.

    Mr Lim has now joined the Singapore Justice Party.

    He was appointed as SJP's Secretary-General on Thursday.

    Mr Lim plans to contest in Punggol East, a Single Member Constituency in the General Election due by February 2012.

    "The reason I left SPP is that I wanted to contest under the SDA platform because I believe the SDA continues to be a platform for operational unity," said Mr Lim.
