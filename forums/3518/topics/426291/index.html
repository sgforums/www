    Mar 27, 2011 - Sunday Times

    by Carolyn Quek

    Mr Ang Wei Neng is described by PAP chairman Lim Boon Heng as being 'good at talking to people'.

    ANOTHER potential new face for Jurong GRC was spotted yesterday, joining two other newbies serving in the constituency.

    He is SBS Transit (West District) vice-president Ang Wei Neng, 43.

    People's Action Party (PAP) chairman Lim Boon Heng, who helms the group representation constituency, introduced Mr Ang as part of a possible line-up and described him as someone who is 'good at talking to people'.

    Mr Ang declined comment when approached. He was with Mr Lim at a team-building programme for Jurong grassroots leaders at the Outward Bound Singapore school on Pulau Ubin yesterday.

    Mr Lim, who is also Minister in the Prime Minister's Office, also commented on the other two new faces who are active in the GRC: legal counsel Desmond Lee, 34, who was present at yesterday's event, and unionist Alex Yam, 29.
