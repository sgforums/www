    Mar 27, 2011 - Sunday Times

    By Teo Wan Gek

    AFTER 35 years of holding the ground at Marine Parade, Senior Minister Goh Chok Tong is looking for a 'strong' new candidate he can groom to take over the helm of Marine Parade GRC.

    In a message posted on the Marine Parade PAP branch's Facebook page, he wrote: 'In this general election, I am planning for someone to succeed me in leading the GRC one day. I have asked the Prime Minister to send me a strong candidate who has the potential to be part of the fourth-generation core team.'
