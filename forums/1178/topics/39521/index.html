CANCELLATION OF POLY HEADSTART PROGRAMME

  • iwarp
    27 Mar 03, 14:30
    The Ministry of Education has advised schools to close till 7 April 2003 due to the spread of Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS).

    As a precaution for the students, we are cancelling the Poly Headstart Programme with effect from 27 March 2003. The second session from 31 March to 4 April is also cancelled.

    The staff will be calling you to ensure you are informed of this cancellation.
