    Which one is true.
    DA = Diploma Active points
    DP = Diploma Passive points
    NDA = Non-diploma Active points
    NDP = Non-diploma Passive points

    DA and NDA = Active points

    DP and NDP = Passive points

    You need 30 Active points, both NDA and DA. But as stated in handbook, you need 10 Diploma related points, so you will need at least 10 DA points.

    The rest of passive points is spread evenly between NDP and DP.
