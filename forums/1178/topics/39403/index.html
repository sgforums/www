    Note : This is only for those who are selected !!

    The Poly Headstart Programme will now run for 5 days in order to cater to the large number of students who are interested in the programme.

    Session 2: 31 March - 4 April 2003, 9am - 4pm

    Registration Venue: Level 1 Exam Hall, Tanglin Campus, 1 Kay Siang Rd
    Registration Time: 9am
    Please Bring: NRIC
    Attire: Casual

    Door gifts will be given on the first day.
    Certificates of Participation will be awarded on Day 5 of the programme upon full attendance of the course.

    Schedule of Events:

    Day 1: Centre for Educational Development
    "An introduction to Problem-Based Learning"

    Day 2: Centre for Innovation and Enterprise
    "Managing People"

    Day 3: School of Engineering - Electrical Engineering
    "Flashlight"

    Day 4: School of Information and Communications Technology
    "Computers and IT"

    Day 5: School of Applied Science
    "Challenges in Biomedical Sciences"

    All food is halal. Food, drinks and course materials are provided at no cost to the participant.
