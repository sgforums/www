  • catinthehat
    6 Feb 09, 13:52

    8.30 to 3.30 lah. half of this time is free time for you to work on your own and to do whatever you want. very slack one... enough time for you to go to Causeway point for lunch and back.

    When the updated student handbook for your cohort is out, read it carefully.

    RP emphasizes the following points:

    1. Self-discipline. You can learn as much as you want, or you can just learn the minimum to get by. If you want to score, you have to go all out to learn everyday!

    2. Independent learning. Don't expect info to be fed to you. You have to get it yourself by asking the right questions.

    3. Understanding. Getting info is not the end of the story but how you make sense of it. eg. You find out that SBS Transit is not going to raise fares. Is this good or bad? From whose point of view? 

    4. Teamwork & Communication. Everyday you work in a team and you present your findings at the end of the day. As some people might say, "talk talk and talk".
