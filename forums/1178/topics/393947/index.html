    (WSQ) Wireless Communication and Networking

    The objective of the course is to equip wireless communication engineer with the fundamentals and techniques of the latest wireless systems. It demonstrates the tools as well as the additional skill and know-how that the wireless communication engineer must acquire to fully discharge his responsibility in designing and optimising a fully functional wireless network.

     

    Programme Outline

    • Wireless Communication Techniques & Fundamentals
    • Present & Future Wireless Communication Systems
    • Wireless Network Performance Analysis & Optimisation
    • Digital Communication
    • Data Communication
    • Network Switching & Routing
    • Network Security
    • Satellite Communications & Wireless Broadband

    Target Audience

    Wireless Communication Engineers, Telecommunication Specialist, Network Engineers, Telecommunication Engineers, System IT Group Leaders/Managers and others in similar capacity.

    Certification

    Participants will be awarded the Workforce Skills Qualification (WSQ) Specialist Diploma in Wireless Communication and Networking issued by the Workforce Development Agency, Singapore (WDA) upon meeting 80% of the attendance and completing and passing all required coursework and assessment.
