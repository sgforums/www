    Show Title: MUSE  
    Date: 12 Nov 2008 (Wed)
    Time: 8pm
    Venue: The Republic Cultural Centre, Theatre
    Ticket Price: $10
    Ticketing: Call 6365 0410 or 6365 6945
    Artist/Arts Group: Republic Polytechnic Dance Groups featuring Hip Hop, Social Dance, Group
    Synopsis: Through a collective theme “Modern Dance, Indian Cultural Group and Malay Cultural Muse”, watch how the Republic Polytechnic’s Malay, Indian, Modern, Social and Hip Hop groups seek to redefine themselves in this showcase of original and innovative works. With choreography by respected dance-makers Fathurrahman Said, Kavitha Krishnan, Zaini Mohammad Tahir, Firhana Alsagoff, Louis Ng, Gin and Larry Liu, this varied programme reflects and celebrates the different concerns and approaches of the choreographers. Muse invites you to an evening of inspiring works that will leave you clamouring for more.
