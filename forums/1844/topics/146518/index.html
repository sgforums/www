    For basic training , you have to practise your strokes by finding a court with a wall. This way you learn to control the ball where you want to hit (flatly).

    If you are practising to play a game there are many exercises to work on. Strafting , that is side step , turning and forehand/backhand techniques of returning the ball.

    Do the practises and have fun while you are at it. There is no better game than playing the game more often and learning from the fun of it all.
