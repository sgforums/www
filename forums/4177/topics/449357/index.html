    Total Control Training Center presents
    10 DAYS FOUNDATION OFFSHORE TRAINING COURSE FOR ENTRY INTO THE OIL & GAS INDUSTRY
    13 – 27th April 2012, Tianjin, China (A USD 10 Million Training Facilities)

    In Collaboration with China National Offshore Oil Training Center (CNOOTC) &
    American Petroleum Institute’s (API) T-2 Production Safety System Course.

    This foundation course covers both Offshore Training & Process Operation Training and is a preparatory course which will allow participants to be familiarized on the Oil & Gas type of equipment and its facilities. It is relevant for companies & individuals as it provides a basic level of understanding, familiarization and hands on training on how the Oil & Gas type of equipments operate. Leading you to the award of Dual Certifications (CNOOTC Offshore Competency Certificate & Certificate of Completion for API T-2 Production Safety System Course).

    Who Should Attend
    Anyone who has an interest in the Oil & Gas Industry, Technical Staffs including Operators, Service Personnel, Instrument Engineers, Mechanical Engineers, Project Managers, Production Managers, and related engineering disciplines.

    *Travelling, Hotel Accommodation/Lodging Cost & Meals are not inclusive and will have to be charged separately, upon arrangement at the time of travel.
