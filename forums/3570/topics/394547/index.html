    Terminology for and within Forum

    "Forum" shall refer to the International Railways Forum.

    "Forumer" shall refer to a user posting in the International Railways Forum.

    "IRF" shall be the short form of International Railways Forum and can be used interchangeably with the full term "International Railways Forum".

    Purpose of Forum

    The Forum is for posting information pertaining to all railways EXCEPT the Singapore RTS, Sentosa Monorail and Changi Skytrain. These info should only be seen when stating examples, or when organising outings. All other discussion on these topics should be done in the Singapore Trains forum.

    For New Forumers

    We would appreciate if you chould do a short introduction of yourself here so that all of us can know a bit more about you.

    No spamming or clones allowed here. Constructive questions only.

    Other than official acronyms, please do not contribute your own jargons.

    KTM Antarabandar, the Singapore-Kranji Railway, the Singapore Steam Tramways and the Singapore Electric Tramways, and any other railways prior to any name change predating the first Mass Rapid Transit line planned by the Land Transport Authority, are not considered to be part of the Singapore RTS.

    Forum Etiquette

    All forumers shall have respect for one another. Respect must be earned, not granted.

    Trolling and/or flaming is strictly prohibited. If you need to speak to a person who has upset you, intentionally or otherwise, please use the Private Message system.

    No political and/or religious topics are tolerated within the IRF.

    Spamming is prohibited. This includes unconstructive posts, unnecessary bumping of threads and multiple threads of the same topic. Multiple threads means more than one (1).

    Advertising is strictly prohibited, unless it relates to an existing relevant topic. It should be posted within a topic only. New advertising threads will be deleted with the exception of any Announcement threads posted by sgForums Administrators.

    No illegal activities shall be discussed. This includes anything that breaks a law in the country the act was committed.

    No activities within any restricted railway premise may be elaborated, even with the permission of railway staff.

    A Forumer shall be addressed by his Username within the Forum. This is to prevent posting personal details which the other party may not consent to. Example: A user Qwerty123 shall be addressed as Qwerty123, or a short form which is agreed upon, officially or otherwise (eg. Qwerty), and not by Bob Tan, his real name.

    Disclaimer

    This readme may be updated from time to time. This is also considered to be Forum Rules. Should any disputes arise, the final decision must be a unanimous consensus between all moderators.

    Enjoy your stay in this forum!
