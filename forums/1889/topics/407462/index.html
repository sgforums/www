    The Coming of the Glorious Month of Ramadan

    >>  The Coming of the Glorious Month of Ramadan [6 August 2010]    (download)

    This week's khutbah discusses the preparation in welcoming the month of Ramadan.  

    · To emulate the examples of our beloved Prophet in his submission and thankfulness to Allah s.w.t.  

    · To prepare ourselves to welcome this blessed month of Ramadan.

    · To soften our hearts and cleanse our souls from all evil traits, from arrogance , hatred and enmity for others.


    Subhanallah!  All Glory be to Allah who has made our beloved Prophet Muhammad s.a.w. the most honourable, with the most humble heart among all mankind. A heart that makes him shameful of not submitting himself to God, although God has bestowed upon him the best of privileges and the loftiest of status. A heart that makes him thankful to God at all times, although God has assured him of his place in paradise. A heart that brings him to seek the love and pardon of His God, although he has been promised forgiveness by Allah s.w.t.
