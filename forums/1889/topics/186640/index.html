    In performing our obligatory daily five prayers while on the move, it may be difficult to find suitable locations to conduct our prayers. While there are mosques all over Singapore, sometimes the nearest one may be quite out of reach.

    I found this list of Mussolahs on the NUS Muslim Society website http://www.nusms.org.sg/default.aspx more than a year ago, back in late 2004. It details the locations of several mussolahs and suraus all over Singapore, as well as some details on them (where to take the wudhu, availability of telekungs, sejadahs, etc).

    Click on the following link to download the list of mussolahs:

    http://www.savefile.com/files.php?fid=7450055

    This list was made in 2004, so it may not be current. Some mussolahs may no longer be available, while others may have been set up.
