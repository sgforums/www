MUIS organises fund-raising for Indonesian quake victims

  • Honeybunz
    29 May 06, 17:46
    SINGAPORE : The Islamic Religious Council of Singapore (MUIS) is organising a special fund-raising effort in aid of the victims of the Indonesian earthquake.

    Donation boxes will be placed at all 69 mosques in Singapore from Tuesday until Friday.

    Donors can look out for boxes marked "Humanitarian Aid to Earthquake Victims in Indonesia" to make their contributions.

    The funds collected will be channelled to non-governmental organisation Mercy Relief.

    Donations can also be made by cheque.

    They should be made payable to "MUIS" with the words "Humanitarian Aid to Earthquake Victims" written on the back of the cheque. - CNA /ct
