    There are 2 types of analysis that are predominantly used to find and predict the fair price of any share.

    Technical analysis = This analysis usually focuses on the performance of the share based on the behaviour of its historical share price without the analysis of the underlying business and financial situation. It usually involves the use of charts to discover patterns arising from historical share prices and hence tries to predict future share price movements.
    Fundamental analysis = This usually focuses on the company's underlying business and financial situation. It will incorporate analysis of annual reports, analysis of the company's sector and the overall strength of the economy, when ascertaining whether a share price is fair or not.

    If one is to 'play' the stock market using the technical analysis, is it haram?
