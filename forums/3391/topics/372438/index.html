Fund Raising Table Tennis Tournament 29 August & 5 September

  • Y2kJericho
    18 Aug 09, 15:16

    For Schools

    If your school has a team of table tennis players, professional or non-professional, we welcome them to come to SAGE to play for a good cause in raising much needed funds to the Singapore Action Group of Elders, a non-profit organisation.
     
    We welcome also students who have the interest in table tennis and would like to try their hand out in a table tennis tournament that is friendly and fruitful.

    For Non-School Participants

    If you are participating as a team without any affilation to any school, you are most welcome to participate as well. Please sign up by going to SAGE at Toa Payoh, near the philips center, by walking in to 19 Toa Payoh West.

     
    Please do take note that as SAGE is a non-profit organisation, all proceeds will go to SAGE (Singapore Action Group of Elders), whereby the funds will be used to fund programs, job opportunities, social activities to aid the elderly.
     
    The details are as follows:
    Event Date: 29th August & 5th Septemeber, 2009 (Saturdays)
    Time: 7PM-10PM
    Venue: Singapore Action Group of Elders MPH
    Address: 19 Toa Payoh West
    Registration Fee: $100a (3 Persons per team)
    Closing Date: Tuesday, 25th August, 2009
     
    We hope you will be interested in helping us fund for a great cause.
     
    If there are any queries, you may direct them towards Singapore Action Group of Elders.
