    Years ago, my friend passed out of OCS and wanted to have a good life after that. He knew they were looking for some officers to send to Guards but there was also an opening in the NCC Training School (that was years ago). But how to get out of Guards and get into NCC School where there is a chance to see secondary school girls as part of your job?

    At his interview, they asked him where he would like to go. He said immediately, "Guards. But I really prefer Special Ops." Surprised, his officers asked him why. He said, " I like to hear men scream when I plunge my bayonet into their bodies and slowly pull their guts out...you know what I would really like? I want to cut their hearts out before they can even scream.."

    They sent him to NCC School....after a visit to the SAF psychiatrist.
