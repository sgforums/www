    i took out all of my wisdom tooth during the last 2 months of my NSF period.

    For 2 reasons:

    1. For the MC
    2. Because it's subsidised

    I remember doing it at Alexandra hospital. very professional. of course, very ex too. all in all cost me more than $1000. when i saw the bill i was so happy cos every cent was subsidised by the army.

    furthermore, it's even more expensive if you do it at a private clinic. can hit almost $400 a tooth.

    Plus, if you extract 2 wisdom teeth, followed by another 2, you can space the 2 operations out. all in all, you can get 2 weeks of MC. ain't that good?
