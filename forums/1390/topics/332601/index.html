    not all pple know this:

    NSF can go outside dentist, and then claim back 50% per receipt, but overall capped at $70 per annum. just submit receipt to chief clerk.

    e.g. 2 visits

    1st:
    receipt: $80.
    can claim back: 50% x $80 = $40.

    2nd:
    receipt: $70
    can claim back: 50% x $70 = $35.***
    (due to annual cap, so only claim back: $70-$40=$30)
