  • nightzip
    3 Aug 09, 23:08

    May have to check out the GOM, anyway, to the best of my knowledge, recruits are normally not given duties, but confinements & fatigue.

    Some BMTs actually let recruits have the experience of performing guard duty (using only baton & shield ofcourse), that is actually only performable from private onwards. The experience is good, but recruits can have encounters with "evil" spirits where many batches reported, then the recruit could have permanent scarr on the encounter.

    Guard duties is equivalent to COS. The basic job of COS is in orderly activities, and much lesser especially after 7pm (after dinner), there is only the standard switching on lights, ensure armskote & CQ room is checked by BDO and cleared, people all go off, or for dinner, lunch, etc, obtain BRO, update next day's guards, ensure everyone bookin, flag lowering etc.

    Guards - Pvt to LCP

    G3 - CPL - 3SG

    G2 - 3SG

    GComdr - 3SG - 2SG

    BSO - 2SG - 1SG

    COS - PFC - LCP

     

    Ok, most importantly is that 1st year units & 2nd year units are different. In 2nd year units, most people doing G3 & COS are normally LCP,CPL. But again, those that kena, is because they kena extras, thus for 2nd year units, the LCP & CPL will kena REAL extra duties, not confine in bunk and sleep all the way.
