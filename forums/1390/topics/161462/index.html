    Firstly, on the JPSDS, it's rather hard to get it according to one of my friend who is currently with the scheme... good academic results (B4 at least in english, maths and science), good CCA etc... so try hard...

    Secondly, commandos and guards are the hottest vocation of the scheme... spaces are VERY limited... my friend went for the scheme once he entered poly, he opted for guards but the recruitment officer told him that it's all filled up... he opted for armour instead... so pray hard

    Lastly, Your myopia is on the high side and this may compromise your chance to enter guards... with regards to the kind of missions they undertake, high degree of myopia may not be favourable... Also take note of your PES status, must be A or B... otherwise forget it...

    bottomline... nothing is absolute, exceptions could be made... why don't you call up ARC to check... unless you're very sure about your career choice at this stage of life, you may wanna experience army first before signing on... rem once you pen your signature, there's no room for regrets...
