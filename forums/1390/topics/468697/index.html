    Go to S1 Manpower Branch, find Chief Clerk to file an in-service training injury report to seek compensation claims (just like an insurance claim) and once approved, in future, all medical fees incurred in any govt restructured hospital will be fully borned by Mindef with the issurance of a Service Injury card.

    For ORD Reservists, Mindef will borne the fees only when it's an in-service injury and once the claim is approved, the issurance of a Service Injury (SI) Card.

    From the moment the injury report claim is filed till its approval,  most cases took an approx. few months, while a handful of complicated cases can take up to a year to be verified and processed.

    Service injuries are injuries sustained while serving NS.
    This also includes injuries sustained when reporting for duty or going home after duty.

    You will get FULL subsidy for medical treatment for such injuries, even after ORD.

    Remember to first report your injury to your unit MO. Also remember to collect and keep all documents relating to your injury and your treatment. These will help when you submit the injury report and seek claims.

    2) Where a workman meets with an accident while traveling as a passenger to and from his place of work in a vehicle operated by or on behalf of his employer and the vehicle is not a public transport.
