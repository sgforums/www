    You can't choose your posting. Or your next unit. The only thing is you can try for command school.

    And there's not real point in telling your PC that you want to be a driver. He can't really help you. I know, i was a BMT PC previously. We dont really have much control over your posting.

    If you're not Combat Fit but field deployable, then you can get to be a driver. But then there are other jobs available too that you may get, like storeman, quartemaster, medic and such.

     

    And if you're reason for wanting to be a driver is as dumb as so that you can get a Drivers Licence at the end of your liability then you should just go and fuck yourself. If you want it really bad, go to a center to learn. 

     

    And i don't bad mouth drivers. Drivers have a tough life, trust me, i've spoken to many of them. What do you think you'll be doing when you're between driving? Do you think you don't have to do duty? Standby duty? Wake up odd hours (like 3am) to drive breakfast for people outfield, then come back late at night???

    Its not all glamour for a driver ok. 

     

    And really, just to get a Drivers license??? Really, Go Fuck Yourself.
