    oops correction...
    there are 4 paramedic levels = correct....
    however there are only 2 spec levels.

    the flow is like this...
    current system:
    BMT/ OOC -> Combat Medic Course (Paramedic level 1 + combat phase)
    pass out as medics

    medics can go for SPEC II course (Paramedic level 2 + combat phase)
    pass out as 3sg

    regulars can then go for SPEC I course (Paramedic level 3 + combat phase)
    pass out...... 1 rank higher? not v sure.

    the super zai regulars, usually warrants, and even then only a few have this:
    Paramedic level 4 in CANADA

    for SCDF paramedics, they do not attend any of the combat phases, so they just go Level 1, pass out halfway, go back to unit, go to SMM again for level 2, pass out halfway etc...........
    until level 3 i think....

    most regulars will go for CMC then spec II, then go for some tour of duty depending on your luck - instructional/others
    after a while u will get promoted and/or go for spec I
