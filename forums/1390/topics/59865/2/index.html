    In this thread, i think people forgot to mention about the structure of Sispec.

    I'll just briefly highlight it here for those who wants to know.

    In a Sispec coy, there's no commissioned officers. The whole company are ran by WOSE.

    OC = SWO
    2IC = MWO or 1WO
    PC = 2WO or 1WO
    CSM = SSG or 1SGT or MSG

    So when you are in Sispec, expect old style military.
    Learn from the old birds and give them respect
