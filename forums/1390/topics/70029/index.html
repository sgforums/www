    Beside the seven combat arms, we have combat support like Medical, Supply & Transport, and Maintenance & Engineering Support.

    Under Maintenance,

    HQ MES - Maintenance and Enginering Support formation

    Direct and advises divisional and non divisional maintenance units up to battalion level in peacetime.
    Provides for training in OETI
    Participates in all or most equipment engineering projects

    Under MES

    We have

    3DSMB, 6DSMB, 9DSMB, GSMB, OETI

    DSMB - Direct Support Maintenance Battalion, divisional maintenance units.

    GSMB - General Support Maintenance Battalion, provides maintenance for Armour units

    OETI - Ordnance & Engineering Training Institute, trains all maintenance people.

    A Mech - Repairs Armoured and/or Tracked Vehicles

    B Mech - Repairs All Wheeled Vehicles

    C Mech - Repairs Specialised Equipment and Vehicles

    ATW - Automotive Training Wing - B Mech, C Mech

    AVTW - Armoured Vehicles Training Wing - A Mech

    EWTW - Electronics and Weapon Training Wing. - Armourer, armskoteman, people who deals with electronic stuff

    Ok thats all, I cannot go into the acronyms for stuff within ORBAT of units, or the vehicles types, and other sensitive info on our equipment.

    Otherwise, the MSD might be on my head. Most of these can be found in websites.

    For those going BMT

    BMTC - Basic Military Training Centre

    SectCom - Section Commander - Speciallist in charge of the section
    PS - Platoon Sergeant - Speciallist in charge of the whole platoon
    PC - Platoon Commander - Officer in charge of the whole platoon
    CSM - Company Sergeant Major - Company disciplinary personnel
    CQM or CQ for short - Company Quartermaster - Company Storeman
    Coy 2IC - Company 2nd in Charge - Officer assisting OC
    OC - Officer Commanding
    RSM - Regimental Segeant Major - Chief Honcho of all disciplinary personnel - watch out!
    CO - Commanding Officer - Commander of the battalion or training units

    SECT - Section - Smallest unit in army, varies from 7 people in infantry to 12/13 in BMTC
    PLT - Platoon - Consist of a number of sections
    COY - Company - Consist of a number of platoons
    BN - Battalion - Consist of a number of companies

    OO - Orientation Officer
    MO - Medical Oficer
    TO - Training Officer

    Air Force Related

    AETI = Air Engineering Training Institute
    A/C = Aircraft, not aircon

    Techs / Senior Techs
    AE = Airframe/Engine
    RC = Radar/Comms
    EI = Electronic Instrument
    AGE = Erm, can't remember. They either stay in workshop bang metal or they are arming the aircraft.

    DCC = Dedicated Crew Chief
    ADD = Acceptable Deferred Defect (Something spoil but still can fly)
    TFOA = Things Falling Off Aircraft
    BF = Before Flight (Servicing)
    AF = After Flight (Servicing)
    TR = Turnround (Servicing)
    WPT = Water Particle Test (Check fuel for water, one of the checks during B/F)
    FDT = Forward Deployment Team (They sit there and wait for something wrong to happen)
    AFLO = Air Force Logistics Order
    GAIR = Ground Accident Incident Report (Bus bang people)
    FAIR = Flight Accident Incident Report (Airplane bang bird)
    U/S = Unserviceable
    GS = Screwdriver
    FOD = Foreign Object Damage (Something not related to aircraft spoil the aircraft, e.g. metal wires)

    AMS = Aviation Material Specialist (Storeman)
    AVS = Aviation Vehicle Specialist (Driver)
    ACS = Aircrew Specialist
    AMO = Air Maintenance Officer
    AEO = Air Engineering Officer

    WSO = Weapon Systems Officer

    C3 (C cube) = Command, Control and Communications
    AOSS = Air Operations System Specialist

    ADA = Air Defence Artillery
    ADSD = Air Defence System Division (Chong Pang Camp)

    Airbases
    SBAB = Sembawang Airbase
    TAB = Tengah Airbase
    CAB = Changi Airbase
    PLAB = Paya Lebar Airbase
    Seletar doesn't belong to us anymore.

    ALS = Air Logistics Squadron
    AMS = Airfield Maintenance Squadron
    FSS = Flight Safety Squadron (I hope i got this right)
