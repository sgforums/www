    heyo, I'm now in commando leader cse. If you think commando bmt is shiong, don't even think about OCS, because to be a commando officer, you have to go through this:

    bmt -> leader cse -> OCS -> Commando officer conversion cse

    And trust me, leader cse is very, very tough (at least to me). Everyone I asked who became commando officers say that after the cdo leader cse, ocs is a cakewalk.
