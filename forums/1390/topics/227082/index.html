    Originally posted by chocochoco:
    http://www.sgforums.com/?action=thread_display&thread_id=226779
    MEOs/ATOs usually start off by being PCs/OCs/ Section Heads in maintenance bases. Being an officer, you would not be required to stay-in unless there is an exercise.

    You will have to go for a OJT term during your professional service term in OCS, in the later stages of your cadet training. You might be attached to a maintanence base for a short period of time to learn workshop and depot operations.

    MEOs are usually graduates with degrees, whereas most ATOs are diploma holders. This is what I know from my NS days.
