    Originally posted by jimmy89:

    Oh btw, OCS got rugby team?

    just a disclaimer: i replied this earlier, but somehow or another, it didn't register. if my reply DOES appear again, i'm sorry.

    to your question: no, OCS has no rugby team. in fact, unless substantial changes has been made since i left 1 year ago, there is no organised sporting teams under the OCS banner save for the AHM (Army Half Marathon) team.

    with regards to sports in OCS, which i think you seemed concerned about, most wings actually avoid contact sports. this is mostly due to their desire to avoid unnecessary injuries and the subsequent OOC (out of course) of cadets. However, if your wing does allow you to play recreational sports, chances are it'd be in the evening, and mostly limited to soccer and basketball. Furthermore, it'd be only towards the end of your service term/ pro term, when they start giving wing appointment holders more responsibility and flexibility to request such things.

    however, i recall air wing playing soccer almost every evening after their instructors leave. that was before they left for AFS (air force school)

    the mids (navy) wings are almost always studying, so if you DO make it to NDU, well, forget about sports. haha.
