    rank equivalence in OCS

    one's rank in ocs will determine wat rank one will turn out to be if one becomes OOC at any point in the OCC (sounds so similar huh? )

    OOC during pro-term 3SG or CPL (depending on how far into pro term; for inf, gds and cdo, haf to go ROC)
    OOC during service term CPL
    OOC during tri-service term PTE

    this is roughly the case. results may vary.

    but this does NOT mean a pro-term cadet is equivalent to a 3SG. a cadet is the lowest life form in the saf and has to follow the instructions of any other ranks other than the recruits!

    Handy Things to always have when in OCS

    1. lots of black tape
    2. perm marker
    3. lots of black string (for securing loose items)
    4. clear tape (the very broad kind)
    5. masking tape
    6. ziploc
    7. jackknife
    8. pen, pencil, paper
    9. telt (wrapping paper)
    10. febreeze - a spray tt removes odour from clothing - good when u need to re-wear no. 4 cos no time to wash!
    11. black rubber bands big and small
    12. materials for cleaning bunk and area.
    13. iron (yes, i brought my own iron in)
    14. starch (the spray kind)
    15. lots of hangers - the ones chosen as the standard kind for the plt
    16. lighter (to burn cock hair)
    17. kiwi and brush - accessible at all times!
    18. tupperware - i use it to keep the aide memoir in my SBO - we were told to bring the aide memoir wherever we go.
    19. spare batteries

    will add to the list whenever i can think of more.
