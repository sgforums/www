    Originally posted by acwire_2125:
    Can discribe more of the JST for the RSAF? And wad's the diffrence between JCC?
    i had my JST in Jungle Temburong way back in 1994. was an RSAF OCT then, service term.

    we are 'thrown' into the jungle into group of 3 and are instructed to built shelters, set traps (for iguanas, birds, etc), find water source/food individually. each to maintain abt 20m apart, eye contact for safety reason (possible animal attack).

    instructors would spot check now and then to see our achievements. if we were good, they would give us those animials we slaughtered and skinned during the practical lesson. if no good, receive only vegetables and fruits.

    after 3 days 2 nights, we set off for topo exercise in group of 6, looking for various checkpoints in the jungle.

    after the last checkpoint, this mark the end of JST.

    JCC...not to sure.

    I think training syllabus had changed over the years ler.
