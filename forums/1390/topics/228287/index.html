    Originally posted by peeehead:
    pass the first test then forever dont need to swim?
    Not necessarily. Depends to where you get posted to.

    My operational unit conducts quite siong swimming test:
    1. Attire no. 4, no boots
    2. Start off jumping into the pool from a 3m platform, with wooden rifle (not sure if its 3m... the platform in the Khatib camp pool)
    3. Remove wooden rifle. Thread water 10minutes
    4. Swim 10 laps, any style. No time limits
    5. Remove no 4 top, and bottom. use the no. 4 trousers to make a float.
    6. Swim back to the pool side with the float
    7. Viola! Pass!

    BTW, the failure rate is quite high.

    Originally posted by peeehead:
    and what are the requirements to pass the test?
    must swim by a certain time limit?
    In my days, you will pass the test if you swim 30m, any style (even non-standard styles). No time limits.
