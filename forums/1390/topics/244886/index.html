MINDEF sponsor up to 60 deserving WO/Spec for degree

  • BillGates
    27 Mar 07, 09:23
    Commissioning of Specialist and Warrant Officer Institute at Pasir Laba
    Posted: 26 Mar 2007, 1500 hours (Time is GMT +8 hours)

    Commander of the Specialist and Warrant Officers Institute, COL Kuek Joo Leng providing a brief on the Specialist and Warrant Officers Institute.
    Minister for Defence Teo Chee Hean announced that MINDEF will sponsor up to 60 deserving Warrant Officers and Specialists every year to pursue full-time university degrees at approved institutions. This initiative reflects the increased professional and technical expertise required by the 3rd Generation SAF and enhances the attractiveness of a career in the Warrant Officer and Specialist corps. Minister Teo was speaking at the commissioning of the new Specialist and Warrant Officer Institute (SWI) at Pasir Laba Camp today.
