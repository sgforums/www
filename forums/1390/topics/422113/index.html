    PES C9L3 - mostly got non-combat vocations like service medic, signalier, storeman and clerk.

    If be a storeman, that will go outfield to set up tentage, bring food rations for others, help out around officers, do arms duty, in charge of all the firearms in the store, then yearly go for rifle range to shoot to pass, no ippt, no soc.

    If be a signalier, that will go outfield to set up tentage, set up radio equipment onto rovers, help out around officers, yearly go for rifle range to shoot to pass, no ippt, no soc.

    If be a service medic, that will in medical centre care people, wait for them to come to you if they see doctor, help out minor injuries, yearly go for rifle range to shoot to pass, no ippt, no soc.

    Got camping out, can bring books to read when you have free time in your bunk but not when during field excercises or when officers expect you to be on alert for something, yes, you sure will be issued with a rifle.

    Basically just know your way around officers and specs when you finish your BMT and pass out from whatever courses they give you, as you will be more or less a helper in the command area, depends on where you go, mostly sure hang around main offices.

    Polish up on your EQ and stay out of trouble as officers can be strange creatures sometimes, not happy, they will make you sign extras (guard duties or other duties during weekends), but basically if you can work with them then no problem, just do what they say.

    CMPB checkup must declare everything truthfully.

    Yes fear of heights,insects and swimming must be declared.

    Normally for specific phobias like water,heights and insects, psychiatrist won't ask you to overcome.

    Unless It's social phobia or like fear of opposite sex that will impact your life.

    For example, my friend have stage fight. He shivers on stage that kind.

    Some people fear doing things in front of people, If you do not declare before enlistment, if when handling firearms infront of people, you have fear, It's troublesome.

    Psychiatrist will help you to slowly overcome those issues that affect your lives.

    If necessary they will write a memo to CMPB/Army psychiatrist.

    Some people are excused from NS like shizioprenic/autism and severe psyhosis.

    Their specialist will write a letter to CMPB MO.

    For example maybe if you have social phobia, you still can serve NS but with excused firearms and stay-in in camps.
