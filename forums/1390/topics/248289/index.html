    Originally posted by fudgester:

    If you have a rank on the sleeve, the top of the folded portion should be just below the rank.
    How can that be ?
    Which way is your fingers measured ?
    If 4 fingers from the shoulder, the upper part of the folded sleeves is probably covering a little bit of the formation sign already.

    I don't have the exact measurement, but there is about :

    1 finger spacing from the shoulder to the top of the formation sign.
    3-4 fingers is the size of the formation sign.
    1 finger spacing down to the top of the rank.
    2 fingers is the size of Lance Corporal rank insignia
    6 fingers is the size of Master Sergeant rank insignia

    So, if according to your measurement, when a PTE stand side-by-side with a MSG, it would be wrong.

    With the limited length of an arm, it should be acceptable to cover the lower portion of the rank when the sleeves are folded up.
