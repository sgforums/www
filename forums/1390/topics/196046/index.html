    Typically, there are lull periods and there are on the ball periods in every unit. The usual NSF typically might go through both periods.

    Depending on your vocation and department you are in.

    If you are in company line (combat), then your life will be determined by the necessary training regime and standby period - typically 4NTM. Surely will kenna LRI and other inspection. Also must assist for your BUC.

    But if you are in company HQ or HQ company, you might be busy throughout. If you are brigade or division level, then it can be different.
