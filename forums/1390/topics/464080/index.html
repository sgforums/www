    By right, OOC personnel will be transferred to BMTC HQ, under the control of the school itself (no longer the company).

    I think there's an admin lapse there, they should have handed you over to report to BMTC School HQ, they will have bunk etc. and miscellaneous duties for you while waiting for posting. Posting orders come from your superiors/units, they do not call or post to home.

    At no time, no personnel will be 'unit-less' or under nobody's command. There will always be a superior, an officer/sergeant in charge of you and responsible for your safety.

    You are right to get an off-pass. Its very important.

    If you cannot get a satisfactory answer, yes, call 1800-3676767 until you get a clear answer.
