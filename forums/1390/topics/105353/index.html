    Naval System Specialist

    Operating the RSN's advanced equipment and systems, Naval Systems Specialists are also able to maintain, troubleshoot and repair them. Fields of specialisation include Electronic (ES), Ordnance and Electrical Weapon Systems (OEW) and Ship Systems (SS).

    _entry requirements

    :: Male / Female Singapore Citizen
    :: 16.5 to 27
    :: Medically fit
    :: Good eyesight with normal colour perception

    _possess one of the following qualifications

    :: Diploma in Computer, Electrical, Electronics, Instrumentation& Control, Marine, Mechanical, Mechatronics, Microelectronics or Telecommunications engineering
    :: ITC in Electrical, Electronics, Mechanical, Mechatronics Engineering
    :: GCE 'O' or 'N' level passes in English, Maths and Science plus NTC - 2 in Electrical Technology, Electronics, Marine, Mechanics or Mechatronics

    _email address

    navy3@starnet.gov.sg
