    Unless there's been a radical change in the times, one of the more popular catch phrases you'll hear at Hendon is "can means can".

    If you're an NS man, what you want or don't want is of little, if any, relevance to Mindef. If you're slated to go to Commandos, you will. Just a few points of note:

    - For NS men, it's direct intake; if you've done your BMT somewhere else, you won't be transferring in.
    - PES A is the only hard-and-fast requirement - apart from that, if you've got a modicum of intelligence, you might be shortlisted, although I did have some company mates who seemed to defy this rule of thumb.
    - If someone asks you if you want to go to Commandos, chances are that you're shortlisted. Mind you, the answer doesn't matter; I asked if I had a choice and the sargeant said no, so I then asked him what the f*** he was asking me for. I got in anyway.
    - As a casual observation, the gung-ho ones often have a lower chance of making it to the beret than those who take it easy.
