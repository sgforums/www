    From my experience, it's better to see a specialist as a private patient ( if you can afford the $65 IIRC). This ensures continual care by the same specialist/consultant at hand, unlike a polyclinic or SAF referral which upon admission as a subsidised patient creates a situation where subsequent medical appointments have different specialists dealing with your case.

    If you go as a private patient to any government/govt-linked autonomous hospital, your fees for the subsequent visits are paid for by the SAF as long as you show your 11B.

    You can get the appointment through your MO's referral or you can go there yourself.

    Lastly, if you're well off, I'd recommend you head to a private hospital or ask around for reputable orthopaedic consultants.
