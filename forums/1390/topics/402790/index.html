    You have posted at the wrong place. This should be at the SAF forum and not speaker's corner. Anyway, just to answer your question..

    1) You can defer NS for your poly education. So, you can choose to complete your poly education first before you enlist.

    2) I think you meant NSF. NSmen refers to those who have ORDed.  For your date of enlistment, it depends on your luck. Some got called up earlier and some later. Typically should be a few months after your medical check up.

    3)Again, it depends on your luck. JC graduates willl enlist early in the year. Poly graduates usually enlist in June/Sept or Dec. 

    4) I think you should go poly first. You will most likely end up in the mono-intake group if you were to enlist with your current qualification.
