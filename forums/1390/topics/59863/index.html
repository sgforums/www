    first day...gong gong lor...dunno where the enlistment hall is(btw i enlist in CMPB)...the moment i walk into the CMPB entrance they ask me 'r u here for enlistment?"....i just nod my head with the sian look and they quickly paste the green sticker on me(i still keep tt sticker now..souvenir) and one of them usher me to the hall....then a guy at the counter ask me for pink IC and i just give...never even have a chance to see it for one last time..TADAH.....transformed into Green IC liao

    then i saw the packet with my 11B, dog tags and a pin number letter. on the packet it says...'G1-ARMY'..

    that time gong gong...dunno simi is G1 army..thot to myself..DIE lah..i thot i PES E...G1-ARMY sounds like GI-JOE kinda army leh....... i almost walk towards the offr to clarify until i asked the one beside me..u also G1-ARMY ah?? he said yes...then at least not tt jialat. As i am PES E , so the night on the day of enlistment can go home liao mah...but the oath taking offr tell us to greet farewell to parents...i laughed hysterically and refused to do tt...plain stupid i think!!! after the Oath that initiates me into this organisation....i am rightfully called a RECRUIT! then we are proceed to waiting room waiting for our units to pick us up..that clerk came late so we all sit there while the rest are deported to their units liao...felt like death sentence....can see some jokers trying to make the sad day appear fun..went around introducing themselves and so on.....until the three tonner send us to MINDEF...the heaven of SAF!!!

    Now..having gotten back my pink IC ...i am now a CPL(NS)
