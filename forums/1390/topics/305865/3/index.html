    bring uniforms (how many sets, depend on u, for me, i bring 4 sets for ICT and then slowly reduce to 2 sets for last ICT), plus SBO/helmet/full pack (if got) plus personel belongings, such as toothbrush/shower foam/slipper etc etc. if dun have SBO/Helmet/fullpack, i think they will issue u new sets. the emart there got sell these personel items as well, if u forget to bring. just remember to bring some cash and coins (for vending machines). bring dog tags/11B/IC/lock as well. Canteen only got one chinese stall and one drink stall and for 1st ICT, dun have much time for canteen break. Cook house food is quite ok though..For smokers, need to bring enough ammunition for one week. Also, no camera hp is allowed. so pls standby a non-camera phone.

    it is stay in and u get to book out on the first sat(usually evening) and then book in on sun night and then outprocess on fri evening (26 sep). usually no night off, cos alway got night training or early training the next day. be prepared to get up at 5am almost everyday. . And IPPT is usually on the first tuesday. be prepared for it. 2.4km route is two rounds round the camp. if failed, will get another chance to take before outprocess.

    also, i think it is better to drive(if got car) or take cab (share a cab, if u can find other who stay close to u), cos need to reach by 7.30am and consider the amount of things to carry, i dun think u want to take MRT/bus.. also, Alpha Company -stay at 3rd floor, Bravo -4th Floor, Charlier -5th Floor, Delta - 6th Floor. good luck if u kenna delta, although there is a cargo lift, which is very slow.
    btw, i think they will call u all up for a briefing in clementi camp around 3 months before the ICT. It is a half day event if i am not wrong.. they will let u know what to bring etc etc and if want to defer or got any questions, can ask during that briefing..
