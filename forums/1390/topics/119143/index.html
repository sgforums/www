    Static Line = The chute will be opened automatically for u the moment u depart from a plane..

    Anyway, back to the original question. If you take it back to WW2, Airborne and Parachute were not the same thing. Back then, Airborne meant you came in by glider, not Parachute. One detail of Band of Brothers that guys from the PIRs objected to was the song that the men sang while they ran. The actors sang "we're Airborne infantry", while the veterans insisted that the paratroopers would only sing "we're parachute infantry". Along the way, they stopped using gliders and Airborne and Parachute became synonymous.
