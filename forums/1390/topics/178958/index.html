    Many reasons..

    1) M16 was made under license. Meaning singapore can only make the m16 according to original specs and further modifications are not allowed as part of the license agreement despite if the modfication is actually an improvement.
    This severely limits singpaore's flexibility.

    2) Changing nature of warfare. SAR 21 is designed so that our fingers are not lmited by trigger guard. This makes it easier to handle if we are wearing MOPP 4, etc.

    3) M16 are running near (with some beyond) their useful life.

    4) SAR 21 designed with the typical Asian user in mind. So butt stock's shorter, etc.

    5) Shorter weapon, easier to wield.

    6) Singapore can finally make a rifle where they have full rights on, meaning can modify as they please. I personally konw there are 2 improvements on SAR 21 though I believe there are many that i dunno about.
