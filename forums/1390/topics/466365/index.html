    hey devil

    postings in scs after bslc (in fact every posting) is pre-determinded to a certain extend by your pre-enlistment checkup/psychometric test. it is only slightly affected by individual events (e.g. saying no to command school in bmt/medical reasons) ippt results usually only affect regulars' grading for promotion and not so much NSFs.

    infantry pro term, contrary to most views is actually damn fun. i went through infantry pro term myself, and after the initial 1-2 weeks of "sian-ness" due to what i heard about infantry beforehand, i actually started to enjoy myself. cos you know you go through actual shit unlike other vocations (or so everyone else thinks. haha just keep telling yourself your one more xiong, then when you finish you feel you accomplish more) and i was surpisingly proud of wearing my green beret afterwards. haha sounds strange right.

    all the best in your posting! everywhere has their own quirks and things people dont like.
