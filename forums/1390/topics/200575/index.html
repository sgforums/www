    Hey, don't sweat the small stuff. It's just a further reporting order.

    When I had my original checkup I was graded PES D pending medical review of a hearing problem (acute tinnitus) that I was having at the time. I was also sent an FRO for further evaluation, where I was graded PES B.

    Just come down with whatever medical reports your family doctor wrote for you. As for family history, you don't really have to bring down any supporting documentation unless if CMPB specifically requests for it.

    In the end, whether you worry or not is not going to affect your final PES status and future vocation. So why get anxious? Just go down and hope for the best. I can understand your hypertensive condition - but surely effective stress management is part of the treatment process?

    All that anxiety you're piling on yourself isn't going to help your hypertension at all.

    Good luck.
