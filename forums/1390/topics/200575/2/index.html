    Sigh... as I said earlier, it's just a FRO, they're not going to eat you up and spit you out.

    Of course you ought to be punctual for any medical appointment, and as with any appointment, it would be best to come five minutes earlier.

    If they give you a 1pm appointment, that means that they feel that 4 hours will be enough to deal with you. If it's not enough, they'll simply give you another appointment date to finish up whatever that couldn't be finished.

    I've said it several times, and I'll say it again: don't sweat the small stuff. It's just an additional medical review. Just give them your full medical history with whatever supporting documents that you have and just do whatever they tell you to do.

    Worrying about the outcome won't change the outcome a little bit. And seriously, what's the worst that could happen?
