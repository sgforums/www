    Hey I was a BMTC spec who did some time in a combat unit... totally different cultures I must tell you.

    I prefered being a unit spec... the office politics in BMTC, as well as the job scope (where you really bao ka liao) are a killer. I'd take the tighter, more gum with your men and relevant jobscope of unit anytime man.

    But no, it's not a waste to send NSF 2LTs to BMTC, they get the job done... most of them anyway.

    All I can say is that BMTC is a mixed bag... yes as an NSF commander there you are the king of Tekong but at the same time in many other areas the life is actually worse then being a unit spec. The whole BMTC feel for the commanders is extremely isolating, and you have far fewer close friends then you would get in unit.

    Not to mention while recruits might respect you, you have to clean their backside and run daycare for them... made me pine for the days where I was doing some real chiong suah in unit instead of the SITtest and field camp wayang.
