    20 Mar 06, 21:25

    Me , like some of the people who replied to this thread, had a mixture of equipment from 1G to 2.5 G. Because my NS and reservist spanned 2 decades ... 1982 to 1995. I still have my formation badges and pins from the Temasek green days. I still have my boots ( one pair) from 1982 ... still in reasonable condition , despite many years of service.

    Up till recently, I still had the first generation of camo no. 4 uniform from 1984, when the SAF were switching over from Temasek green. My parents were clearing my things in Singapore recently, and they reported that the uniform finally "gave way". The material was rotting and it was easy to tear it... so they recommended that I should scrap them. For those out there, if you remember the first camo version, it was distinguished by the fact that it was not sweat absorbent. You sweat and the shirt did not take up the liquid. Your sweat from your body ran into your under pants. After about 3 washes, the camou faded into a purplish white colour. It was probably very suitable for urban warfare camouflage !
