    Well, if you like policing work and ceremonial duties, the MP vocation will be enjoyable for you. You will attend a 10 weeks basic course (or Spec course if you are SISPEC graduant)where you will learn aspects of policing works,laws and your powers and how to use them responsibly, and weapons such as the 9mm semi-auto pistol. Trainers comprised of MP with various degree of ground experiences as well as Police Officers attached from HTA. Your potential posting after the course includes enforcement, and yes, this includes conducting raids(contrary to 105090's claim, is more frequent than your local drama on ch 8!), anti-riot etc ; investigation; intel; ceremonial; canine; KINS protection, etc. BTW, Provost no longer exist! Is now expanded into a formation and is now MP Command, and yes, all MP came from there.
