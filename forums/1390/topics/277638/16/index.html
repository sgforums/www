    Originally posted by Chelzea:
    cos after ns became 2 yrs, A level n diploma ppl may nt get their corporal rank. in fact my unit ,there is a directive for all man eligible 4 ippt to pass ippt to get cpl rank
    correct. the old academic quadification rank promotion rule for pte, lcp and cpl is scrapped. any men can get cpl rank if recommanded and pass their second year ippt. thats the new directive just out early this year. but most units will restricted still with a unit self-administered rank quota system and time period from the date of enlistment (doe). earliest lcp rank u can get is 7 months from doe, cpl is 12months for combat, 15months for service and tech (pes c and e).

    so now is all depending on ur unit s1 branch pple.

    the administrative compulsory promotion to cpl rank nearing ord for a lvl and dip grad is also scrapped. under the old system, even ur rank is pte but u have a lvl/ dip, u auto promoted to cpl by mindef cpc department when near ord. but now, this will ceased.
