    Possible to be officer, but almost impossible since you do not have at least a dip.

    Slow promotion, as in really slow. You will see your peers many ranks above you a few years down the road.

    Lower pay as in really much lower. The higher you go, the wider the pay gap. 

    Most important thing to note: Sponsorship to studies not guaranteed.

    You should know now why the recruiter does not speak to you clearly about everything.
