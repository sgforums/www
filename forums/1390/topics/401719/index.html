    Originally posted by DigitalArt90:

    I just got posted there, not sure how to get there. I live in Pasir-ris... Close to Ikea, Giant and Courts that side....

    On the first day I suggest you go there by Taxi.

    Else you can come to Tanah Merah MRT early in the morning. Turn towards the right. Go to the busstop that is going toward Changi Airport. There should be a long queue waiting for bus. There should be also a duty spec helping people to get onto the bus. 

    It only take about 10 mins to go to CNB. But the queue is long as all the personnels stay out.  when you go to CNB you need to alight  (change pass for your first day) and queue for bag check, then you need to take the internal shuttle bus, to get around the base. 

    They have take off the sign and bus timing schedule now.
