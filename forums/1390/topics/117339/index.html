    For those who thinking of AWOL. Ok I'll try to recall what I can about life in the Detention Barracks.

    You will be kept in a cell of either 1 or 3 detainee... You won't be sharing a cell with 1 person, this is to prevent any homosexual activities from happening. You'll be only sleeping on the hard cement floor with 2 blankets(used and dirty)

    5am. Wake up, brush teeth followed by breakfast consisting of 2 or 3(forgotten) pieces of bread with a mug of coffee/tea/milo. After that nothing to do till 8am or 9 am.

    8am/9am. Drill/Sandbag regime.(very tough)

    12pm. Lunch. Rice with 1 meat and vege. Then till 1.30pm or 2pm, another round of Drill/Sandbag.

    6pm. Dinner, like lunch 1 meat and 1 vege, brush teeth then wait for roll call(counting heads) then wait for lights off ard 9pm.(no more food till 5am)

    Repeat the above for the rest of yor days sentenced remaing..
    Good luck...
