    Heard that you have to go through a 3 months course before being posted to one of the 6 sub-units. Course might be a bit xiong but there is no more SOC and you'll have a lot of lectures and tests.

    After your course, you might be posted to guard the Istana , perform escort duties, conduct regular raids for contraband items in various camps, go and catch people who AWOL, guard the DB, investigate military crimes and many more. If you are lucky, you might get selected to join the Precision Drill Unit who will go through hell training and you parents might see you perform live on TV during NDP.

    Actually MP is quite fun lah, after your course it will be quite slacked unless you get a xiong unit. Oh and you get to wear those tight tight uniform specially made for MP and a white helmet with 'MP' largely printed on it.
