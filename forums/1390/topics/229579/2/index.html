    Can the Duty MO speed up PACES? No. That is like LMIS or it's replacement Enterprise System, managed by Army NMC/SMC. If the SNCO is unhappy, he can feedback to his unit's IT manager or brigade NMC. In any case, such feedback may reach the Formation HQ or G4/G5 Army person responsible much later on.
