    Anyway, Mindef is the one choosing the selected candidates to go for the VA selection test for Commando mono-intake, not you, the pre-enlistee.

    You have to do the NS Registration, CMPB medical checkup 1st.
    If you are PES A/B, only then you are generally in the large pool of pre-enlistees candidates where Mindef will pick and choose the selected few to go for the VA.

    And nowadays, with the education standards rising, the Commando Formation will usually prefer 'A' Level / Polytechnic graduates as a basic requirement for NSF Commando recruits.
