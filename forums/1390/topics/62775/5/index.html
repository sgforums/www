  • jacobs
    21 Jan 04, 18:34
    Well, i shall describe what i considered the three most siong experience yet. It was the "24km" route march around pasir laba and lim chu kang. We were there early that day, i think it was in the afternoon. We were at one of the hills in pasir laba, learning ridgeline fighting in prep for Ex. Lancer til evening. It rained, and our socks were soaked. Formed up at the startline, PLC SOC at 2200 or 2359(can't rem as we started alot of missions there). My PC has intructed us to carry 6x1.25l water bottles in our fieldpacks, but i only have 1.5s. So we start our route march, march up and down and up and down. I can only rem stopping at LCK cemetery, space(entrance to M203 and LAW ranges) and others are on the track along the route. At times we walked way more than 4km(we know as we walked alot of times) before we got to rest. We went up and down the hills at pasir laba like three times, going down was difficult and i think some guys lost their toenails due to being soaked in water for so long. From night we marched til 7-8am where the end point is... the top of elephant hill i think. During the last 12km of the march i was carrying one saw of my section mates suffering from a shoulderache for around 8km before i pass to my sergeant for the last 4km. We knew it was certainly more than 24km, when i asked my sergeant. He said it was 32km.

    Learnt a new meaning to slp walking. Prior to this "24km" route march, there was the "16km" route march. My PC instructions, 8x1.25l water bottles. Again i was carrying 1.5s, how heavy is a fully-filled jerry can? but it was at east coast park. Another was the Company mission exercise, FBO, "hor-lan -ded" in woodcutter's trail. I rate this three as the most siong i've been through so far.
