  • Fortiscause
    28 May 05, 09:27
    All NSF are entitled 14 Days Annual Leave per Calander Year (1 Jan - 31 Dec)

    If say you enlisted in 13 June naturally your Annual Leave will decrease accordingly. There is a formula to calculate that.

    You can carry the balance leave from the previous year to the new Calander Year once. (ie You have 3 days remaining in 2003, you can carry over to get 17 days in 2004. However if your did not use any leave at all in 2004, your 3 days will be forfeited).

    If you intend to take 1/2 day leave on a Holiday eve, I can tell you to forget about it. It will be counted as 1 day leave as according to GOM.

    And when you decide to clear leave, don't take more than 14 days continously within a fornight as that would include your Sat & Sun. I suggest that you take 10 days and take one day off. Don't waste your leave.

    Always talk to your Leave Clerk/Asst Chief Clerk/Chief Clerk. If you can't ask your Coy Admin Spec/Clerk to help.

    rgds,
    fortiscause
