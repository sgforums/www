  • Juiyong
    11 Sep 10, 01:33

    KungPaoChicken,


    It is likely to be C9L2 which is the same case as mine.

    My slipped disc occured at L4 and L5. Started off with degenerated disc but it will get worsen if you continued to exert pressure due to weights and postures. However, you can still go for non-invasive operation to remove the disc if the pain is unbearable even after painkillers and physiotherapy program.

    Since you are still in your NSF term, get a specialist from outside (not your M.O.) so that the downgrade process is faster. You will need to go for a MRI first and a downgrade recommendation letter from the Orthopaedic Specialist.

    Whatever it is, protect yourself. Slipped disc can stay with you forever (even if you go for an op, it might still occur again) and my advice to you is to seek treatment early.

    Take care

     

    Jui-Yong
