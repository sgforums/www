    Nope.

    ECG test have nothing to do with hair dye, unless your hair dye got some special ingredients in it which will affect your heart rate. Which I doubt so.

    Don't worry too much and just let them check again. Perhaps you were too excited on the day itself and your heart rate when up (Sounds funny to some, but it does happen), so they want to check you on another day instead. 

    There are many possible reasons, but you shouldn't worry too much. Being called to go for ECG test a second time isn't that uncommon, and many people turned out to be fine after all.
