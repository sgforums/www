    PES E BMT

    The new 4-week BMT programme will be conducted for PES E recruits. This programme will focus on vocational training as well as National Education, SAF core values, regimentation and discipline to prepare recruits for combat service support vocations such as clerk and storeman.

    Hi i'm from HRI as well. Actually you guys will only be in there for 1 month, to mix around and make some frens, listen to lectures, but doesn't exactly train u to be a clerk, cos that will only happen after ur posted to your new unit after the mod BMT. Where your new unit will be depending on your house location(only to a certain extent) and mostly, your luck. Then at the new unit u will be tasked to do things but mostly admin stuff. No worries! Its just like a normal 8 to 5 or 6pm admin job except for the pay and some %$#@ you will know when you are in. Somehow for me days in HRI are the best except for the short lunch time. Take care fellow recruit! :D StarryLight11
