    No, it's just a longer PT as compared to enhance, it's 8 more weeks now. Within the 8 weeks they will still include BCCT and probably abit of SoC and all the basic THT for SAR21 and basic first aid skill, 3km route march. HIKI event(PS, dunno how to spell) like range, field camp, sit test will only start after the 8 weeks of PT. In fact a lot of enhance NSF wish that they are in PTP cause they see those PTP got a lot more admin time than them (Used to see the platoon 3&4 in my coy rushing up and down . Which are enhance). But 2 month reduction is always a good thing though. Yes, PTP is more relax as compared to enhance because there is WAY too much time.

    And will my advancement be affected just because I am in PTP?

    Advancement as in going to SISPEC or OCS? No, only your PES (A still have higher chance than B to get into command school), IPPT (minimum a pass), Marksman, SITtest and SoC (fail still can go OCS, they whack you until you pass SoC in OCS) and attendance (If you keep chao keng get status you will have lower chance) will affect those. There are quite a lot of officers and SPECs in my unit are from PTP phase.
