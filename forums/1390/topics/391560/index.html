    1.5 wks of MC will OOC BSLC. If you are still combat fit (i.e PES A/B) you will recourse BSLC. Just tell your PC during your OOC interview. Most likely you will join the next batch of BSLC.

    After you OOC, most likely you will stay at coy line. If not get posted to Guard room coy to tank guard duty. If you are super lucky can get posted to HQ to help with admin. OOC during BSLC you have little chance of getting 3SG. If OOC during ASLC got a much higher chance.
