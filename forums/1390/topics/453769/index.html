    Originally posted by Troy Simha:

     

    but im partially deaf and i cant sit or stand for more than an hour how am i to do office work??


    Spinal Compression
    Type word documents 50 minutes, rest/ relax/ walk around 10 minutes.
    (Repeat from 8am - 5pm)

    Ergonomically designed office desk and chair.

    Partially Deaf
    Use of sight to read, use of fingers to type.

    Hyperhidrosis
    Air-conditioned office working environment,
    Buy Over-The-Counter at Pharmacy such as Driclor for hands, armpits and feet.
    Brand: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Driclor

    Dust Allergy
    Air-conditioned office working environment.
    Daily cleaning by office cleaner.

    Sunlight Allergy
    Use of artificial lighting such as LED/ fluorescent light in office working environment.
    Tinted office windows, some offices are installed with window blinds.

    Use of sheltered walkways within camp/ use of umbrella when walking around camp.

    Allergic Rhinitis
    Use of nose spray from Over-The-Counter at Pharmacy or from Camp Medical Centre.
