  • Ponders
    20 Oct 08, 14:53
    Official MP (manpower) GOM Only Officers CPT (or appt OC) and above are entitled to Military wedding in Uniform. Entitlement means, 1) drawing of stores for ALL parties, number one for himself, and his sword arch team (groomsmen) 2) using of military premises (usually OCS) for wedding photography plus other whatnots. There is NO restriction for other SAF personnel to use No1 uniform for wedding provided they do not infringe any law or SAF GOM (i.e. wrong usage of rank, using unauthorised adornments (e.g. swords for specialists, using tabs/badge not entitled to that personnel) However, the No 1 uniform is out of pocket and not drawing from store.
