    1st 2 weeks is deemed as confinement period.
    it means no book out during the 1st 2 weeks. read here <- click


    since u r clueless abt NSF and BMT, go to http://www.army.gov.sg/ 1st and then http://www.ns.sg/.
    Pre-Enlistees: http://www.ns.sg/iPrepNS

    BMT / SISPEC / OCS / vocational courses are stay-in camp for the weekdays. BMTC, being a training school is on 5.5 workweek basis. 5 days workweek is only when u get posted to a permanent unit after ur vocational course training.

    So, as usual for anything off duties, the answer is "please wait till u go unit."

    And even so, when u get posted to a permanent unit, must see ur vocation / camp is a stay-in or stay-out. if stay-in, must apply for permission of approval from the unit's chief clerk / s1 manpower branch. if stay-out, u r free to do anything during off-duty hours.

    Combat Fit

    PES A/B

    9-week BMT (Silver & Gold award for NAPFA)

    13-week BMT (Bronze award and below)

    PES BP

    15 or 26 week BMT (depending on BMI)

     

    Non-combat fit

    PES C1/C2/C9 - 7 week Modified BMT

    PES E - 5.5 days Residential Induction programme

    First 2 weeks of enlistment no book out, after that book out either on every fri evening or sat afternoon, depending on your company

    Post BMT

    If you are super fit and show leadership potential, you will go to OCS or SISPEC. If not, you will go for vocational courses before being posted to your permanent unit
