    USed to be in SAF dog unit during my NSF days.....

    Generally, one person will be assigned a personal dog.... i.e (ur registered dog.... ur "wife") u will be responsible for his/her health, grooming, training

    on top of that, u will also have a few "mistress" .... i.e... ur buddies "wife" as most ppl in dog units hav to work in shift, u will have to take care of ur buddies "wives" while the r on their off days and vice versa..... and if ur dog happened to attend B on certain days, u will have to bring ur buddies dog for ops

    if your unit have both guard and sniffer dogs, u may end up if one of each... i.e 2 dogs under your name....

    if there is a shortage of man power in ur unit, u may end up with more than one dog under ur name and vice versa.....
