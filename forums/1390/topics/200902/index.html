    Who of you have gone through it? You guys want to share tips?

    I'll start. Go for as many points as possible in the day shoot. Cuz the night shoot is a whole different animal.

    Also if you have the rounds, don't hesitate to "cover" for your friend by shooting his targets too. Especially when he has IA. He can cover for you when you have IA.

    For M16 shooters, better idea to use the big rear sight at night, It's hard to aquire the target at night with the small sight.

    Lastly, if you are a SAW gunner and can pull off the one squeeze one round ability, go for it. As you effectively double the number of rounds you can shoot. No one said you can't shoot twice or even three times as long as the target is up. And if you have "shot down" your target, you can then shoot the target of your buddy.

    As for the SAR shooters, I envy you for the scope but pity you on the mag change.

    My recent shoot, just as I was about to shoot, my target went down, shot by my buddy in the confusion (those of you who have gone thru the shoot will understand), I shifted to his target which was in turn shot by the guy on the other side of him! Thankfully I just had enough time to take that guy's target out before time expired.

    Man it was a lot of fun. Cross fire rules have gone out the window!
