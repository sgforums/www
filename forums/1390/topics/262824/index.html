    What does a RP do?

    Well, I've been doing on-job-training for the past 3 weeks, and according to what I have seen, done and experienced in my unit (Clementi Camp), the job of a RP is freaking simple.

    For my camp at least, all we do is guard the camp gate - either stand there holding rifle, or do admin work, which means, open gate, take down vehicle plate number...

    It's 2 hr do, 2 hr rest for now, but after the RP course, we expect to get a few more RPs in, which would allow us to do 2 hrs do, 4 hrs rest.

    My camp RPs work 8-5, and we have weekends off. And for some very strange and unexpected reason one of us has to do overnight duty, our sergeant major will give us one day off for it. Same goes for weekend duty (which is very rare.)

    Good, huh?
