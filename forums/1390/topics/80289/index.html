    constant and consistent training.

    2.4 is about speed hence tt is wat u haf to train - short but fast.

    interval trg helps alot. 300 m sprint (timed) + 100 m , repeated for 6 rounds, with rest between each round. o cos the timing for each sprint should be faster wif each round.

    other than tt is running about the same dist (say 3 km) and decreasing the timing progressively.

    many do not realise tt warm ups jus b4 the 2.4 is crucial too. tt is one of the purposes of the 20 min rest after the static stations b4 the run. make full and good use of it to warm up ur body and leg muscles totally. it will help.
