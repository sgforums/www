    Last time I was in the Obese batch, so field camp only 5 days 4 nites. Actually its 5 days 5 nites starting Monday, and we leave on the sat morning back to coy line.

    Super obese one do 3 day 2 nite field camp, but have 2 do the field camp twice.

    Field camp is probably one of the best things for me during my BMT, because after that, I was posted to Navy, and I no longer have to do field camp. Field camp really bonds ur section together, as u will be doing a lot of tactical things together. Also, it will jolt u out from ur comfort system. If u think being away from the comfort of ur home bed is hard, try field camp. Sleeping in the wilderness, with only a tent for ur roof really makes u appreciate the finer things in life.

    I remember wanting to shit on the first day of the field camp, but when i got to the 'sai kang' i saw that it was SWARMED with flies. My shit somehow immediately went back to my system, and i didn't shit till the 5th day. Once back to Coy line, the most popular place for recruits was the toilet. Lol. Coz need to shit, and quickly bath so can book out.

    Field camp is fun, because it builds u up, and really helps show a side of u u never know.
