    heh...field camps are the best topics nxt time if u wanna tell your grandchildren or friends that have not gone thru NS.. frankly speaking, field camp is nth to be afraid of (think of it as camping outdoors), just do wad ur instructors tell you to do, follow all field camp disipline, as for the skin condition, the earlier you let your instructors know, the better.

    Field camp is all about learning your IFC a.k.a Individual Field Craft. It teachs you how to adapt to war time conditions outfield, how to comouflage, how to survive in the jungle and how to perform basic recon and basic topography. Also teaches you fire movement, jungle movement / disipline etc etc..

    Overall no fieldcamp is the same, as each person has a different story to tell (save for the common story abt the instructor "torturing" them). So its best to experience it yourself. For no story is better then your our field camp experience..
