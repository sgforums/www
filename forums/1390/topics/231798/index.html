    First, stop talking to the NSF clerk, he can't do sh!t.

    Second, talk to the officer in charge, all ICT units are handle by a active brigade. The officer is usually a regular. Talk in a firm and assertive tone that u mean business and not a chow keng ginna trying to keng his way out of ICT.

    Third, go to ur school and request a higher rank person, preferable a professor, associate professor, someone with a Doctor something something to write a letter for you. Best, u can get a few of them to write. (I know it is usually the school responsibilities but if u are on good terms with ur teachers, prof, etc he can add in some weight into ur letter)

    Lastly, request the phone number of ur ICT CO, call him up and discuss with him.

    Note, the above are just recommendation, it is not a 100% guarantee it will approved. Approval is very much depends on ur unit.

    Furthermore, u SHLD prepare for the WORST CASE SCENARIO. i.e. u lan lan hab to do ICT. Then u have to make sure u can cover up for the lost time. U can discuss with ur ICT CO on compromise, i.e. allow u to take nights off or stay-out everyday during ur ICT. Most NS CO are also working ppl, so they tend to be more understanding and more approachable with these situations compared to the regulars.
