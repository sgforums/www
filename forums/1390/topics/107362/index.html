    I trained in Sungei Gedong regularly when I was active in the mid-'80s. My camp was at Stagmont Ring & the whole CCK housing estate didn't exist then, but imagine the noise racket our M113s (about 26 of them at BN level) made at dawn whenever we convoy-drove down to Lim Chu Kang & Sungei Gedong.

    BTW The Arty & Armour people who train there are often called "ang-sua peng" by the locals, due of the red dust or mud that forever sticks to the No. 4's. Or maybe due to the dirt or mud often left behind on the main roads by the mechanized vehicles.
