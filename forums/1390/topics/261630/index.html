    I'm getting the idea here that you're one of those hardcore bodybuilder types. I used to be somewhat like that before I enlisted so I roughly know what you're looking for. Sad to say, SAF gyms don't cater to hardcore bodybuilders.

    The machines are more training for endurance and physical fitness, rather than building great strength and muscles. In the army, it's more of doing 100 continuous reps of 5kg, rinse, repeat; rather than doing several sets of 8 reps of 100+ kg weights. You get the idea...

    Anyway, just my own opinion here, I feel endurance training helps more for combat fitness than hardcore bodybuilding. Just my personal opinions, it's helped me cope better in army training and exercises, than when I enlisted at nearly 100kg mass.
