  • yishang
    7 Jun 08, 12:36

    Most of the items they'll tell you to get after first 2 weeks, i recommend Nomex gloves and camo scarf for outfields! You'll hate the mosquitoes soon enough, and please gets lots of SAF Foot&Body powder, no prickly heat please, it's cooling but doesn't help much.. Foot powder absorbs ur sweat, best powder in SG!

Trust me! Sneak in some snacks if you can, undetected ^^ Don't need to bring extra CV wear, and alarm clock helps alot too... Have some spare HP batteries if you're the one who calls your gf or family daily. That's about all, you'll know what you'll need later on...
