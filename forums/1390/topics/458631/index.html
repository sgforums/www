    Source: www.mindef.gov.sg/nsmen

    Personal Equipment

    All NSmen are issued a set of personal equipment (PE).
    This gives the NSmen more confidence, convenience and potential for greater operational readiness. It also enables them to care for their personal equipment, thus ensuring better personal hygiene and better maintained equipment. Concurrently, NS administration is reduced, as the NSmen do not need to return such items after their training.

    Return and dispose off are for NSmen (reservists) who have completed their NS In-Camp Training (ICT) cycle.

    NSmen who have reached their statutory age (50 years old for officers and 40 years old for Warrant Officers & below) who do not require their PE items anymore, may return their PE at the following designated collection points:

    1) Army Logistics Base (ALB)
    601 Choa Chu Kang Rd
    Singapore 699817
    Tel: 6389 6380 / 6389 6381

    2) Any SAF eMarts outlets
