    Source: www.hometeamns.sg

    All the terms and conditions are subject to for Social Membership are as follows:

    1. Relatives/ friends of HomeTeamNS members who are *13 years old and above are eligible to join as Social member.

    2. Social members cannot apply for HomeTeamNS Family membership.

    3. Applicants must be Singapore Citizen or Permanent Resident.

    4. New Social Membership Application must be recommended and endorsed by a valid HomeTeamNS Ordinary / Associate member.

    5. Social members can make use of all the clubhouse facilities (except gymnasium, Archery Center & shooting Range) before 5pm on weekdays only.

    6. These exceptional facilities are exclusively for respective interest group members only.

    7. Social members can join the individual interest group: Fitness Workz, Archery Club, Gun Club, Entrepreneur Club and Golflinks (Renewal only) at a premium fee.

    (Please request for respective interest group application form.)

    8. Social members who wish to join the interest group/sub clubs must subscribe to 1-Year social membership and the expiry date must not be earlier than the sub-club membership expiry date.

    9. The interest group/ sub clubs membership is subject to approval by HomeTeamNS. No refund for Social membership fee will be made in any case, or in any event that the Sub club membership is not approved.

    10. Social members can utilise the swimming pool free of charge during off peak hours.

    11. Off peak hours refer to Mon-Fri (except Public Holidays), before 5 pm.

    12. Social members can participate in all HomeTeamNS activities unless otherwise stated.

    13. Social members (above 18 years old) are allowed to book any of the HomeTeamNS Holiday Chalets during non-peak period (outside School Holidays & Public Holidays).

    14. Social members (above 18 years old) can enter the Jackpot Room but not allowed to sign-in any guests.

    I declare that the particulars given are correct and agree to abide by the rules and regulations of the Social Membership.
