  • YI
    16 Apr 06, 19:34
    call pershub at 68642022 to confirm your minimum term of engagement. you can leave the service (subject to approval) as long as you had fulfilled your minimum term of engagement. if not, you had to fork out $$$ for the unfilfulled period. they can also tell you how much $$$ is needed if you are to quit at a certain date.

    if everything is okay.. you can proceed to get the early-withdrawal form and request an interview from your unit and squadron commander to have them "endorse" the form. if everything goes smoothly.. you should be out of the service three months from the date of submission of the form to pershub.

    disclaimer: this is the method widely spread around in the rsn. never tried b4 but had seen a great deal of people leaving successfully over the past years.
