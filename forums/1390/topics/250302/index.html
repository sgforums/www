    Maju camp holds IPPT on Weds, thursday, Sat and Sun too, but I not so sure they hold on Tuesday. Be warned about wed and thursdays, coz there are certain dates that the RT personnel will take their IPPT too, and it could get pretty crowded should u suay suay dio that day they taking. My advice is if u taking on weekdays, try to reach there before 5pm, as the RT personnel can only register for IPPT after 5.30pm onwards so as to let non-RT personnel take their test first. And if u taking on weekends, reach there before 4pm as the test starts from 4pm.

    You should book at least 1-2 weeks prior to your window close, because you never know what the weather is going to be like. For all u know, if u go on the last available date before ur window close, and if there is bad weather for a long time on that day, they will declare the test void, and you will be deemed to have not attempted ur IPPT. You will be charged and send to RT also. So, its safer 2 book earlier. Besides, if you fail, at least u can go take it 1 or 2 more time before your window close.
