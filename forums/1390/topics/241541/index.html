    Yup, you perform mostly clerical work. Arms handling not needed.

    Duties are still a must. Depends on where you posted, your duties will vary.
    For example, my case, clerks within my unit requires to perform:
    1.) Duty clerk. For all enlistees ranks. Under Chief Clerk Supervision.
    2.) Duty Mess boy. PTE to LCP. RSM Supervision.
    3.) COS duty. CPL to 3SG. Under RSM Supervision.
    Then unit outfield, all clerks also required to perform outfield duties (AKA sai gang)
