    16 May 07, 00:26
    Guys,

    Our SG fragmentation grenade has a 5sec fuse. It starts burning when the safety lever spring-off from the grenade cap-top. You should start counting-down when the grenade is thrown from your palm, not when the pin is being removed.

    5sec is just adequate if you are a good thrower with good reach of distance. The German's potatoe-masher was designed with a stick to improve the reach of the grenade in the open.

    Now that we have grenade launchers allocated at such proliferate rate in our fighting orbat, maybe, we should have differential fuse screwed on our hand-grenade so that we can choose the length of stand-off timing. ie., a shorter 2sec fuse for room-clearing as opposed to 5sec fuse for fighting in open.

    As a gentle reminder, as long as the safety lever is held in place, the grenade will not explode. Hence, it can be improvised as a booby-trap by tying the grenade with safety pin removed but retained in it's shipping container. Attached the free-end of the string across a road or a door that you expect enemy traffic; volia, you have an improvised anti-personnel IED. A common trick used by Thai soldiers and, no doubt, others in the region.
