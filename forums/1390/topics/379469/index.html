    sorry to disappoint u, but if you are planning to do it during NS. There might be a risk that SAF will not allow you to do this operation. Unless  you can prove that your operation are somehow related to the well-being of your body. From what I know, having an operation due to cosmetic reasons were often not accepted in NS. I got a mate, he went to have a lasik operation and gotten 1 week MC for that case. However, he was summoned back to the HQ for that he should not have done any cosmetic operation which induces absence from work during his operation phase. But luckily, back than, this rule was not really clear and people didnt know it even existed, my friend plead leniency to my co, and he was not punished. But my co did mention this during a parade, and if anybody caught doing so will be punished.
