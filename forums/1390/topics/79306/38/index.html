    when u get into your posting order to attend a course or posted directly to a unit, go to the respective camp medical centre to consult the mo there. ask the mo for further treatment for your case. to downgrade needs supporting medical evidence, ask the mo whether can refer u to the hospital specialist for more x-rays and reports. if got, an appointment will be made and u see the specialist. when at the specialist there, give more details, then request the specialist to write a memo to ur camp mo to downgrade.

    and no, complete bmt or whatsoever doesnt affect the downgrade process. medical is medical. pple last week of ord week oso can downgrade. even reservist oso can downgrade so long as u got medical reports, memos, documents, x-rays, hospital specialist reports... to show proof of ur present medical fitness.
