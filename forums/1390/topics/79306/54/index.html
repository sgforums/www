    in layman term, it's consistent pain at both knee joints.

    if u have the next appt with the specialist, pls tell him/her the situation at the camp medical centre. hence, pls request the specialist to write a much more hard worded memo to the camo mo, with the latest treatment/medication...etc.

    after getting this 3rd memo, arrange for a review at the camp medical centre. if even with this 3rd memo, the camp mo still reluctant to want to put up ur case statement for medical board. try to politely tell him, "sir... this is my 3rd memo, i think it's possible to pls put up my case for medical board. Let the medical board chairman decides..."   if u can come up words that r something better, then use yours.

    another delayed possibility is... keep on extension the lower limbs excuse for 1 or 2 more times, (either with the medical board pending meanwhile, or just continue the excuse alone). then when these 1 or 2 extensions ends, coupled with the memos, tactfully reason with the mo to pls submit ur case for medical board.

    anyway, u already a reservist. fyi, during my ns time i knew of a guy who had both knee replacement surgery done got a c2l2 permanent.
