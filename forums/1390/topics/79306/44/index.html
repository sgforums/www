    whatever it is... ur multiple problems... just tell cmpb doctor one by one specifically... dig up all supporting documents such as specialist letters/memo and show doctor....

    for ur respiratory problems like asthma n sinuses, it is considered common nowadays... so it is pes b category.

    for hand and knee fracture, i knew some guys with both knee replacement surgery done b4 get pes c2l2.

    BUT...

    other than that, as all u said above is preliminary medical symptoms... but no final diagnosis is given, thus, it is inconclusive to tell u any pes u MAY be in... e.g. blood test result? , x-rays? , hospital specialist report?....etc.

    fainting spells can be attribute to hundreds of possible medical conditions... e.g. low blood pressure, high blood pressure, hyperthyoidism, low blood sugar level...etc. same goes to back problems as well... e.g. muscle inbalance due to long term improper standing/sitting/sleeping positions? , slipped disc? , spinal bone spurs thus pinching spinal nerves?...etc.

    so without specific final diagnosis, there is nothing we can advise u on...

    ALL MEDICAL BOARD CASES NEED PROPER AND QUALIFIED SPECIFIC MEDICAL SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS AS EVIDENCE... AS ALWAYS, NO PROOF NO TALK !!!
