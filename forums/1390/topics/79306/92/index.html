    Hi, before answering, below is all purely my opinion, which may not be true.

    What I understand is that, as long as you pass out from BSLC successfully, no matter what PES you are in, you will still be able to get to 3SG, because you will already be CPL when you pass out.

    Most likely, you will be just temporary downgraded to PES C since you only consulted a MO, instead of Specialist.

    After you down-PES temporarily, you will be asked to visit the MO again for a further checkup, to see if you have recovered, and if you have, you will be upgraded back to PES B.

    It is up to MO's discretion whether or not to downgrade you. Do not argue or request from MO.

    That's all I know, hopefully someone with more knowledge may be able to assist you.
