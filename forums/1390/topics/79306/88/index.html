    Usually, if a person is excused from anything for more than 84 days, it's usually already scheduled as KIV Medical Board Review by the camp MO.

    If you are still in doubt about this MC, and whether are you schedule for the KIV, 1st) please call up your NSHRC and ask them. 2nd, please telephone the camp MO's direct medical centre office line and ask him about it. The MO's direct telephone number can be obtained from a clerk at the NSHRC.

    Last resort, try to get another stronger worded specialist memo from another specialist (govt / private hospital, your choice) as a 2nd opinion memo as a backup to support your case for the KIV Medical Board, and then book and go for another PES review at the reservist unit medical centre.
