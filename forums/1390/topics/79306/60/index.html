    a temp pes can be renewed again and again, or convert to perm, with reference of assessment of the condition based on the specialist memos.

    1) medical board will give the new pes effective from the date of medical board meet. find out from the medic for the date of medical board for the month, then after a couple of days later, can go medical centre and ask a medic to help u check for ur new pes status.

    2) no. at most u can request to do some other work in the coy line, or other rear areas like HQ coy line. same as att b light duties.

    3) no. either u have to inform the MO urself when u see him so that he can raise a request order for info from the hospital medical records office, or u get a specialist memo from the hospital on ur next specialist appt and then u pass it to the MO.

    4) find MO, ask him why. next, go to specialist and tell specialist the reasons given by MO. next, seek for his/her next course of actions. e.g.request for further investigations, better stonger worded memo, or request for a second opinion from another specialist.
