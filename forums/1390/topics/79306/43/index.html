    u MAY TRY a hospital a&e for a check test, since its just show 11b, saf pays mindset... at most, a&e doctor give u an excuse MC for lower limb activities for a couple of weeks + book specialist consultation follow up appt. for u. fastest way, since for internal hospital appt. booking, the referral waiting time is shorter and given priority.

    keep any a&e discharge summary as medical supporting documents.

    better ways:

    1. book and go back for an appt. with ur hosptial specialist for another follow up check, then get a stronger worded letter for further consideration for a downgrade below ur current c2l1 perm pes. faster way.

    2. go to camp mo, with any supporting documents e.g. specialist letter, memo, then complain to mo that ur knee conditions is worsening, here details there details, ask nicely for a referral letter to hospital, book hospital appt., see hospital specialist, get specialist letter. time = a little slow than no.1.
