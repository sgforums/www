    i guess u have to wait till the specialist referral appt to get a specialist memo, then report sick at medical centre for further review supported by the memo to request camp mo to refer ur case statement to the medical board. a month's wait isnt that long.

    any govt/private hospital specialist's memo/report is recognised so long as a final diagnosis is made n written in the memo. if u can afford, by all means visit private specialist, the waiting time may be shorten if u need further tests such as MRI or CT scan for better details to see if ur lumbar spinal is affected. x-ray doesn't see much, it is a very basic n primary tool to detect, then doctors will next order MRI/CT to further analyse in greater details.
