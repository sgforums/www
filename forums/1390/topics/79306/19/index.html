    Much depends on the pathology of the resected specimen. From your description, it sounds like a vascular malformation of some sort. After the operation, the pathologist (a specialist who will examine the specimen and diagnose the type of tumour or disease) will find out what the problem is. Depending on the diagnosis, the condtion may or may not recur. The pathologist will also be able to tell if the abnormal area has been completely removed by microscopic examination. Based on his report, the SAF MO will then be able to assign you an appropriate PES grading.
