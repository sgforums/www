    12 Jul 04, 20:52
    A note about OCTs...

    OCTs are kind of like LOBO MOs... either waiting for MOCC to convert to CPT, or just serving out the balance of their NS (short-liability disruptees). Most have the same civilian clinical experience as the CPTs, but may be short on SAF-specific medical practice.

    Tekong is a special place (I'm sure you all know that!). PTMC is a big medical centre, so it is a good place to put new OCTs, where they can be supervised. It is also a good place to put MOs who need more supervision than others (feel free to read between the lines).

    So, sorry, this means that some Tekong MOs are really great, and a few are "special cases" that can't be placed in solo medical centres!

    BTW, we also place these special cases in SCDF and Police... be grateful!
