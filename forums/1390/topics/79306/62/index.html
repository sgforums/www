  • Chua Hian koon
    4 Oct 08, 08:43

    In 1989, the eye M.O. tested my eyesight by asking me to wearing stereo polarization vision glasses and looking at a special board. Certain objects will appear to be higher or lower in height if the person has normal stereo vision. This is suppose to test for lazy eyes / cock eyes, the theory being that if a person has lazy eyes/cock eyes, certain stereo objects cannot see as only 1 eye is active. The board has certain objects that appears to be stereo even without wearing the special glasses, to weed out fake replies.
