  • eac
    3 May 09, 01:40
    Originally posted by Jimi_Goh:

    My specialist typed out a letter explaining my condition and requested me to be excused from ICT. Can reservist MO block this?


    It depends on the specialist memo and the type of ICT / ICT Activities schedule.

    The medical centre MO has the rights to agree with fully / agree with partially with the specialist. e.g. Full endorsement of specialist memo/ shorten the MC/ grant you alternative excuses recommended by the specialist...etc.

    If endorsed partially, you may still need to attend the ICT but doing light duties only (ATT B status).
