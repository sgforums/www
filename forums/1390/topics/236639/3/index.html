    Sounds as if roles and responsibilities have become a little confused in the Singapore Armed Forces.

    The British Army, Royal Navy and Royal Marines have just two WO ranks: WO2 and WO1 (the senior of the two). The RAF has just one WO (also known as Master Aircrew for aircrew). There's never any confusion about who salutes whom - WOs always salute officers.

    On a related point a couple of your SWOs recently attended the Joint Services Warrant Officers Course here in the UK at the Joint Services Command and Staff College.

    See the link below and the full pdf document at the bottom of the page within the link:

    http://www.defac.ac.uk/colleges/jscsc/news-archive/2006/news-from-the-joint-services-warrant-officers-s-course-jswoc
