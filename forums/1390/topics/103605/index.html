    During my time had 2 good friends who served as instructors in my BMT training unit. The 2 of them were senior term OCTs (at that time there was no differentiating of pro and junior terms and there were no tri-service courses). One of them was OOC in senior term because he could not qualify as a pilot (solo flying failed). But because he did his junior term OCS, he was deemed to have qualified as an infantry section cmdr. So he was posted to my unit.

    The other friend, suffice to say , he was a "jee-seow kia" ! He is a nice chap but just too playful. Finally, got up the a@@ of the wrong instructors and mentors in OCS and was kicked out. Then served as sect. cmdr in my company. Later in reservist, because he graduated from NUS as a lawyer, he served as local captain at the court martial centre !

    I know of 2 other chaps .. not close friends but acquaintances. One was OOC because he was charged and served 7 days DB for falsifying his IPPT results in his docket. This happened during junior term. He then served the rest of his NS as a clerk (Lance corp) somewhere in Mindef HQ. The other friend was OOC during junior term because he lost his rifle during a topo exercise in Mandai forest. Anyway, this guy actually became famous. You may have seen him on CNN Asia , as the CEO of a up and coming IT company !!!
