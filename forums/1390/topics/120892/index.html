    If you are an ASA or SA, the promotion depends on your educatioal level.ITE and 'O' level holders will be promoted to LCP around 6-9 months before ORD, whereas 'A" level and Diploma holders will be promoted to the rank of CPL.IF you are of a "scholar" ability, you might end up in MINDEF as a Project Clerk or Project Specialist.Though I'm not sure if these vocations still exist, or they pluck from the ASA ranks, these guys have a chance at becoming a 3SG aka Admin Specialist.Then again, if you're ASA, your boss can also recommend you for the Basic Admin Supervisor course, which goes down the same path as the former.

    Anyway, PTEs get $350, LCPs get $390, CPLs get $420, 3SGs get $560.When you reach the last 6 mths of service, you will get an increment of $15 for PTE and LCP.I'm not sure about CPL but 3SG's pay will rise to $590.
