    Originally posted by relackjack:
    hahah wth man? he is not trained as an AI during active times right? then how is it possible to become AI?
    Possible - I was from 2 PDF. My unit was a Guards unit. We started training on V200 vehicles when we were in our final reservist years. So during the course of our full-time NS and the 13 years of reservist training, my unit have deployed from helicopters, ships and armoured vehicles.

    Most units from PDF are defensive in nature. A small number are used for attacks - some with light armoured vehicles. Some units within are "seasoned" battalions doing their last few ICTs with the PDF, others are newly formed infantry battalions made up of other previously non-infantry trained personal - so they start from basics (2 PDF).

    However all this might change - starting with the new batches from 6 SIR, which seemed to specialise in defence, plus the ever changing organisation of the SAF. Already, 1 PDF units are transferred to the CADs.
  • lemon1974
    10 Nov 05, 23:49
    Originally posted by Shotgun:
    What does a PDF rifleman have to do? Eg for ICTs and their Ops role?

    Very skared later really kena that one... den really suay until lao sai.
    I am a technician during NS. Got call up to infantry reservist unit at 2PDF. Tot it will be very siong. But have tahan 3 years of ICT alrd..
    My unit make up of ex-store man/technicians/drivers/cooks etc.. All not from Combat unit, except for sergeant/pc/oc..

    Basically, rifleman just do a lot of defence exercise.. 1st ICT, basically do all the things that u learned before again.. stripe/assembly of rifles, introduce u to SAW/M203 etc.. go range...go outfield doing movement.. learn all the hand signal again.. chiong around in the forest.. go section live firing at second last day of ICT..

    2nd ICT... do back what u do in first ICT.. but in slightly faster pace.. go FIBUA as well. ALso, got 4 days 3 night defence exercise at Simpang where 1st two days, u will spend all the time,digging trenches, and filling sandbags.. You will see the SUN, THE SAND, THE SEA cos Simpang is at the yishun seaside...

    at the 3rd day, everything have to be ready and you will standby in your trenchs for attacks by enemy forces..Just wait in the trench, eat, talk cock etc..

    If lucky, the ememies go attacks other sections, and u just wait until exercise is cut. If you suay, the whole section/platoon, might be deploy to go patrolling and go reinforce other section/platoon and u have to run around in the darks... You will get little sleep during these three days. It is a bit siong, but just enjoy yourself out there.. Pray that u r only digging your own trench and not the command post or what...that is siong, even though "tobelco" are indented... 4th day is the packed up day.. unfilled all the sandbags, remove the iron picket from the trenchs, fill up the trenchs.. even though u feel shacked, u will do things very fast, cos finally can go back camp to bath..
    u will go back to camp from Simpang on thursday and friday will be spend cleaning up all the stores, which is big big headache... and sat outprocessed liao.

    3rd year is actually more relax... went for range (zeroing/practise/atp test) for the first week + one or two field movement exercise.. then spend whole saturday of first week in camp, preparing the things/stores for the defence exercise the next week... basically relax all the way in the first week. during range, just sleep while waiting for your turn.
    and we go through the same exercise for 4 days at SImpang and we finally turn operational in this ICT.

    Just dun worry abt it. Everyone is doing the same things and just do your best and for one thing, there is actually no rank difference between officier and mens in my reservist unit. My PCs/OCs/Sergeant, all do the same things as us. They dig trench as well. They move the logs. They clean up the stores. Oil the iron pickets..During the defence exercise, they are more tired than us but they still do their jobs well. We can be filling sandbags in the middle of the nights and our PCs just doing the same things as well.

    This is what touch us and motivate us to do our jobs even better. Rather than those officers who only know how to arrow people and do nothings.

    Dun worry, 2PDF is not that siong..
