    Not necessarily so. There is a thing called PDF, and that's where non-combat vocation people suddenly find themselves being riflemen.

    My cousin was an armourer during his time in NSF. He became rifleman during reservist. He loved it.

    I was in the S2 branch and was posted to rifleman during reservist after 2 in-camps. I'm a corporal but all Int NCO were sergeants and above. But I was given the option to stay by my S2 but I opted to go ahead with the transfer.

    In my PDF unit all riflemen were former storemen, cooks, etc. But my company all very on because they were quite proud to be riflemen. Kena best company 2 years in a row. Very good bunch. Mostly hokkien peng.

    Infantry quite fun lah. But I kena platoon runner, quite siong but still more fun than being stuck in a CP with all the officers around. More fresh air in a trench than a CP.
