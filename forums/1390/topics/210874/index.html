    Full time NSman Private Yeo Chang Wen Ambrose dies, found at foot of 4-story building
    Posted: 18 September 2006 1526 hrs

    SINGAPORE: A full time national serviceman was found unconscious at the foot of a four-storey building in Hendon Camp early Monday morning, and later pronounced dead.

    The Defence Ministry says Private Yeo Chang Wen, Ambrose, 20, was a driver in Headquarters Supply and Transport.

    The duty medical officer attended to him immediately on-site and Yeo was rushed to Changi General Hospital, and pronounced dead an hour later at 3.30am.

    Both MINDEF and Police are conducting investigating, and MINDEF and the SAF are providing all necessary assistance to the family. - CNA /dt
