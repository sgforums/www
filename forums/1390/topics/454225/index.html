    2. http://iprep.ns.sg/notices-and-timeline.html

    You will receive notices from the Central Manpower Base (CMPB) at different times before enlistment.

    There are 2 important notices you'll receive from CMPB, these are listed below:

    1. Registration notice: you'll get this notice when you reach 17½ years old. It will inform you to do your NS registration.
    2. Enlistment notice: this notice will be sent to you about 2 months before the Date of Enlistment (DOE).
