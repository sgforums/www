    You mean Tekong BMT? I'd to report back to Changi Beach at either 2pm or 5pm, depending on the tide, in my time. There was no jetty/fastcraft back in those days, only RPLs which must dock on the beach itself.

    For the same reason, Saturday bookout times were restricted to those hours too. As a result, some of my weekends totaled barely 21 hours (5pm to 2pm). Yet I considered myself lucky, cuz I stayed in the East. Imagine living on the other side of the island & needing an extra hour's commute each way!

    Spent those breaks just catching up on sleep, anyway. The biggest anxiety, as I recall, was hoping my washed No.4's could dry on time to bring back to camp. BMT life was already all-consuming enough; I didn't adjust towards taking nights-off, inc. on Saturdays, until vocational-course time.
