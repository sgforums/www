  • blitzonic
    30 Aug 07, 21:38
    No pics, but a quick review ...

    1. "COMBAT WORLD"

    1a. "Hardware Land" was where the static displays are. Very satisfying for me, asking & learning all about the latest equipment.

    1b. "Time Crisis" was a paintball arena or something inside a tentage. Skipped.

    1c. "Mortal Combat" was rained out when I was there. Instead of a "2-sided battle with the Tactical Engagement Systems", everyone was talking cover inside the training shed ~lol~ From what I surveyed, I'd mistakenly thought this was the paintball arena.

    1d. "Rambo 07" was small-weapons (SAR-21, M16 etc.) firing but wayang cuz it's all with blanks while aiming at tactical targets.

    1e. "Virtual Land" was a bit like Singapore Science Center, but with combat simulations (3D viewpoint of sniper, recee trooper, combat engineer, etc). Surprisingly no visitors when I was there, maybe it's located a bit out of the way.

    1f. "The Hitman" was live-firing with the SAR-21, but I missed it. Must take bus to the range, anyway.

    2. "ACTION WORLD"

    The daily schedule lists "Urban Operations Show" at 1pm, "Heroes On Parade" (marchpast?) & "Red Lion Freefall Display" at 3pm, "Homeland Security Show" at 7pm, plus fireworks at 7:30 during the weekends. Suppose to take place in a big parade square, but nothing happening when I was there.

    3. "FUTURE WORLD"

    Taking place inside an auditorium behind the big parade square. I think this is supposed to be a movie or something of what the future SAF & battlefields will look like. All I saw were flickering images standing outside (they were hosting it for the big shots) before I lost interest.

    4. "DOWNTOWN WEST"

    4a. "Yummy Land" got free samples of combat rats but, get this, they are also selling regular army meals (Vegetarian Set, Muslim Set, etc.) at $5 each!

    4b. "Family Land" was a playground for the kids, plus also introductory booths for SAF Yacht Club & the like.

    5. "MISSION WORLD"

    I confused this one with another &, together with the stop-star rain, ended up missing it altogether. My priority rtoo, after first scouting it on the AOH wensite. Damn! It's supposed to take you (but not sure what vehicle) on a ride deeper into the Live Firing area -- past Peng Kang, FoFo, Elephant Hill, E-Shape Knoll, Bajau, 106 Ramp) on a search-&-destroy. Damn! Damn! Damn!

    6. "BATTLE RIDE WORLD"

    The one I confused the above with, but at least this was satisfactory. Sit inside an armoured vehicle as it traveres through the BCTC village, meeting enemy fire, smoke bombs, etc. Tip: choose the Bionix or Light Strike Vehicle if you have a choice; the others not allowed to stand or smell the surroundings.

    7. "A.C.E. WORLD (Army Culture & Experience)"

    I overlooked this. But not too bad, cuz I was told the trips past Peng Kang & up to Fofo (& BCTCtoo?) was cancelled due to bad weather. The other two ferry stops are the old SAFINCO bunks & OCS.

    8. "XTREME WORLD"

    8a. "Fear Factor Land" is SOC, Flying Fox & Rappelling. No thanks.

    8b. "Hercules Land" is gym equipment & IPPT stations. As if. Skipped.

    Map reference: http://www.mindef.gov.sg/army/aoh/main.html

    I'm thinking of driving down again Sunday but then there's also Comex @ Suntec, leh. Some more Instana COG is nearby?
