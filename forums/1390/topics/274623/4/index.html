    For those who haven't been there: the new Pasir Laba Camp is magnificent too. A few places are quite high-brow, though not comprehensively on the whole as as SAFTI MI next door. Walk around & enjoy the sights, cuz I recall the whole place was under contruction & boarded up during the last AOH.

    If you're taking the shuttle from Boon Lay, note that the route traveres the rear of the army camps, entering first via a side-back gate into ITI (Jurong Camp 2?), then turning & winding past SAFTI MI, across the flyover (over the PIE), then into Pasir Laba.

    Cannot reveal more, for security's sake, but it's a big, big change from the days I slogged past the place when attached out to 5 SIR. Most of the wilderness is gone, but a few of the well-trodden treks are still there on the Pasir Laba side, so look out for them. The sudden sight of them had hit me emotionally during the last AOH, when ferrying from SAFTI MI to the rifle range/obstacle course. The route this time around isn't as long, but the revisiting was still nice. Going up to Fofo or BCTC, or deeper into the Live Firing Area, will trigger memories too.
