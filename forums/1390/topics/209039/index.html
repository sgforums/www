    Strange things found during NS days 1980s ...

    Animals
    1) Mouse deer in Macritchie Reservoir
    2) Tortises ( sorry forgot to spell ... you know the 4 leg kind with a shell !) at Seletar Reservoir ... many of them all crawling around, some with Buddhist markings
    3) Tiger Barb aquarium fish in stream in Macritchie forest
    4) Fresh water shrimps in stream in Macritchie forest

    Inanimate things
    1) Old British Army guard room documents in Nee Soon Camp. One was a report sheet for the Guard Cmdr to fill up. He was supposed to report guard strength, number of buglers and drummers !!! I don't know how old this report sheet was, but i thot they gave up on buglers and drummers long long ago.
    2) Nee Soon HQ armoury full of British weapons. Lee Enfield .303 rifle, British SLR auto rifle etc etc
