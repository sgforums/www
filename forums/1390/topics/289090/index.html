    obese BMT does not necessary mean that your chances of making it to command school is lower. its the same as the enhanced BMT or the PTP batches.

    the perception that obese BMT has a lower rate of recruits going command school is due to the inability to meet the IPPT criteria of command school.

    bear in mind that the minimum criteria for making it to command school is a pass in IPPT.

    obese BMT does not necessary means that it would be slack. i am sure there are batches of PTP or enhanced recruits whose companies is next to obese companies can testify that obese companies can get very tough too.

    i know during my year, an mild obese coy had to leopard crawl throughout the entire field due to some incident.

    so let us not jump to conclusions to hastily.

    obese BMT is structured for recruits to lose weight and gain their fitness. if they can clear their IPPT, and at the same time, meet the requirements for a command school, be it SISPEC or OCS, they will go to.
