    The SAF is not all that bad a place to be in, contrary to all the mudslinging, bitching and whining from the vocal majority but I must stress that it's all down to the luck of the draw. The superiors you get and your fellow colleagues will make the difference of having good or bad times.

    Given your polytechnic qualification, I would suggest that you go through your BMT first before you finalise your decision. Even if you can make it into the officer corp, I'd ask you to seriously consider your options again since it's not the rosy picture many here paint (Especially since most of them are NOT officers). Plus, as a farmer (even if you have a degree) your advancement prospects do not look good unless you're prepared to put in outstanding performances in your tours to up your "market value" with your superiors.

    I cannot stress this more: Consider if you're prepared to put your life to the draw. Your chances of getting good superiors and colleagues are not good given the selfish mentality of the kids nowadays.
