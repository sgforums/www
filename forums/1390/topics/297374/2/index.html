    Accept your fate & move on, Niptuckx. Like fudgester said, you may not adjust well to the Hokkien peng on the army side either.

    I do not know how the SCDF system works but if it's similar to the SAF one, mono-intakers enlist together with the idea of staying together throughout NS & reservist. In other words, some 22 years (until you turn 40). The only chance to formally "detour" out is after BMT, when the unit declines to retain you for whatever reason. Thereafter, the possibilities are all but fleeting: get downgraded, sign on with another military arm, work overseas after NS, posted to holding list for reservist, etc.
