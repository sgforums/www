    for 24 months service.....

    on paper:

    the yyyy is plus 2 yrs
    the mmm is the same.
    the dd is minus 1 day.

    e.g.
    090605 is DOE.
    080607 is ORD.

    ------------------------

    for 22 months service..... (have silver in napfa b4 enlist)

    on paper:

    the yyyy is plus 2 years
    the mmm is minus 2 months.
    the dd is minus 1 day.

    e.g.
    090605 is DOE.
    080407 is ORD.

    -----------------------
    just remember to do ur ORD FFI (book dental appt 1st then go do FFI at ur medical centre), and must dutifully complete ur unit's clearance forms, before u can step out of the camp main gate peacefully on that very much awaited last day.

    if u ORD on a weekend, then u collect ur pink NRIC on that week's friday or the following week's monday.
