    don't blame the RPs they are just doing their job. if their CSM/RSM finds out they let people in without proper documents, they are going to sign extras, not u.

    anyway Seletar RPs are one of the most on-balls RPs in the SAF. their guardhouse CSM (a MSG, don't know still there or not) is also very on the ball.

    just to brief u all on the documents allowed for changing of pass. they are;

    [*] 11B for soldiers
    [*] Pink/Blue IC for civilians
    [*] Driving license
    [*] Valid non-expired work permit for foreign workers
    [*] 15A aka temporary 11B issued by the unit if u have misplace your 11B.

    if u have none of the documents above for whatever reasons, u may use your non-expired passport to change for a pass.

    if u lost your 11B, it is advisable u make a police report asap and keep the police report with u where ever u go.

    alternately u can call up the contact person inside the unit and tell him to speak with the RPs or their superiors to grant u entry.

    depending on camps, some camps don't allow using camp passes to exchange for visitor pass.
