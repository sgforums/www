    Yup, combat-fit diploma and A level holders go to Enhanced BMT, regardless of what they got for NAPFA.

    A Level/Diploma:
    NAPFA Silver/Gold = 10wk Enhanced BMT
    NAPFA Bronze/Fail/Never take = 6week PTP + 10wk Enhanced BMT

    Others:
    NAPFA Silver/Gold = 8wk Standard BMT
    NAPFA Bronze/Fail/Never take = 6week PTP + 8wk Standard BMT

    The Enhanced BMT has additional modules to train and select the recruits for commander positions (SISPEC/OCS), otherwise same as Standard BMT.

    Can check out mindef website: http://www.mindef.gov.sg/arc/army101/army101_3100.html

    Anyway this system has been in place at least for the past 4 yrs liao (when i got enlisted).

    I don't think you need diploma/A-levels to go SISPEC? (Not sure abt this, maybe the SISPEC pple can clarify?) So O-level holders still stand a chance to crossover to OCS if they excel in their training.

    Hope this'll help clarify the various queries..
