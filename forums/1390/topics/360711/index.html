    Girls dont.

     

    People with missing eye, missing leg, missing hand, no arms no legs no need to serve NS.

     

    Son of PM do serve NS (Although he got himself into some trouble a few years ago, they are normally white horses and will become officer)

     

    CEO of a rich company also need to serve, it doesnt matter who he is, as long he is a Singaporean or Singaporean-to-be, with able body, will serve NS.
