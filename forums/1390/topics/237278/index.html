    Originally posted by aRcTiC_sTaR:

    ok some tips for u...
    there r some qns they r sure to ask...
    such as
    - why do u want to be a RSAF pilot? (confirm come out this qn n i feel that this qn alone has a lot of weightage)
    - if u make it thru, wat type of pilot u wanna be? (fighter, transport, heli)
    - if u dun make it thru, would u consider signing on for other vocations?

    n a lot of other qns i forgot... will post if i rem them.

    oh... n oso dun be too proud n overconfident.
    n oso DO NOT attempt to deceive the interviewers (normally abt 3 to 4 pple)
    for example, if they say ur ex-sch teachers say u were a very good student even though u noe they wont say that abt u, tell the interviewers they r wrong n they probably have the wrong info.

    be as truthful n sincere as possible, they have a factfile of ur info right from ur pri sch records.
    rem to greet them when u enter the room.
    observe ur sitting posture n hand gesture, sit straight, dun slouch.
    it's ok for ur hand to move a bit but not too much.
    speak fluently, dun slurr or mumble stumble.
    look into the eyes of each n every of the interviewers when u talk.
    juz be confident but not overconfident.

    hope all these will aid u.
    gd luck!
