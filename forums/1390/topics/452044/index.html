    Were you pes A-E before medically graded to pes F?

    If yes, you have to wait between 2-4 weeks for the medical board to process your pes F before getting a letter. Sometimes it may take 1-3 months for it to process finish too. You'll get a green certificate that states you're exempted from:

    A) Full time national service.
    B) Operationally ready national service.
    C) Duty to obtain an exit permit.
