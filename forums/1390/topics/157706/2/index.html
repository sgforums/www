    During my NS (1993-1995), were an ammo technician in Pasir Laba camp. In charge of issuing and receiving ammo from Unit training. When OCS come to return ammo, alway tekan those OCTs and their officers, arrow them to bring the cartridges here and there, ask them to check the empty cartridges.Tell them to help carry those unused ammo back to the storehouse in the middle of the night... Tell them that there are live rounds mix in the cartridges, and tell them that empty cartridges are short. Need to go back and find again the next day, else we will book them.

    Even though i am PTE for the first year, still can arrow them to do things, since this concern ammo.. They got no choice and have to obey and they dun even know my rank, cos i am wearing vest and slack..

    There was one time, when it is around 2 am in the morning,when they come back to return ammo after exercise and they messed up with all those unused ammo and we have to slowly check through. Then one OCT was shouting at me, asking me to hurry up cos he was tired and want to go back camp. I just tell him to shout up cos this is concerning ammo and i also tell the officer that if his OCTs make some more noise, i will make sure that the officer and his OCTs, wont go back to camp early... I dun mind delaying the returning process slowly, cos i can go back home to sleep the next day at 8.30am. They cant...
