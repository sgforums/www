    tks for advice...

    take ur btt before u enlist. den during ur 2 weeks confinement study for ftt. (u need to pass btt before u are allowed to book ftt.)

    and waiting time for theory test is around 2 weeks long

    PDL is valid for 6 months, before you need to renew again. $25 to apply, renew.

    btt valid for lifetime.

    ftt valid for 2 years.

    and driving lessons, school is around $80 for 2 hr session on weekends. private around $40-50 for 100mins.

    waiting time for school driving lesson  is 3 weeks. so u must plan your lessons very carefully. dun get confined over the weekend.

    den you need around 20-22 lessons before you can take the TP test.

    so if u want to get ur license fast, be prepared to chiong 3,4 or even 5 lesson every weekend.

    TP waiting time for school SSDC around 1month+. Private is at least 3 months, and can even be up to 6 months.
