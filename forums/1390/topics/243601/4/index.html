    Our battalion exercise always like this...

    Troop concentration. This is the time to slack off a little, cook some nice food, snatch a nap, etc, before the "siong" stuff begins. Some Advance party people (scouts, landing zone party) will be heli-inserted.

    Flying time. Everybody will get to fly.

    Not sure if it is the same, but for us, the CO will have a "Vanguard" company flying in first. The vanguard will move off before the rest of the unit is flowned in. They will secure all the major danger areas, trigger off any ambushes and engage any opforce partols.

    The rest of the unit can then move very fast to the objective with minimial contacts with the opforce.

    Then whack the enemy lor.
