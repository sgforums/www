    8 Oct 04, 21:43
    if you sign on combat specialist or officer, you will go thru combat training just as guys do.

    For Officers, your bmt will be at unit/ocs followed by your 10 mth term.

    combat specialist, bmt at tekong, specialist training at tekong... then go to unit.

    there is no difference for training between guys and girls other than a lesser physical requirements for girls. some trainings are exempted for girls due to their physiological differences.

    If you sign on service specialist, then you just attend 2-weeks ALL-ladies bmt.
