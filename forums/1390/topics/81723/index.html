    anyway also to sum up, since u will be enlisting tomorrow, the only thing u can do is to report sick immediately and hope to get ooc immediately. pending med board and all, u will prob stay around tekong. den depending on the final status, u may go for the pes c bmt. if the process is slow u may end up being posted out to a unit to do sai gang before going back for bmt. den whether or not u get posted back to the same unit will depend on wat kind of job u were doing there b4 going to the bmt.
