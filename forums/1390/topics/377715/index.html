    Originally posted by Xelement:

    can anyone teach how to pin the crest on the beret, how to season etc?

    First, force the crest on the inner part of the beret to create two small dents.

    Second, use something sharp to poke through the dents to create two holes.

    Third, put the crest in the front, with its two points through the two holes you made, and you just need to put your pin through in the inner part of the beret.

    As for seasoning, you could fold it in half to create the "fold" which you need, and place it under somewhere heavy such as your mattress. If you have no idea how to create the "fold", ask your seniors for help.
