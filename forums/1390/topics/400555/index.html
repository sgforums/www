    It is not impossible to get a job with PES F..... it's just that it may make it more difficult for you.

    A prospective employer would want to know why you got PES F, and you can't blame them for that. If the SAF, with all of its manpower constraints, doesn't even want to take you on as a low-cost clerk, then prospective employers would want to know why.

    Take note that you should fill out any and all employment forms as honestly as you can. While they may not necessarily conduct full background checks, any wilful dishonesty in your application that is discovered later on can be grounds for instant termination.
