    AN army regular was found dead in the Singapore Armed Forces training area in Lentor on Tuesday, said the Defence Ministry.

    In a statement released on Tuesday night, Mindef said First Warrant Officer (1WO) Rajendran Pachemuthu,51, was found hanging from a rope in the Lentor training area by two civilian contractors who were maintaining SAF training equipment.

    There was no SAF training conducted in the area in the last two days, Mindef added.

    The contractors reported the incident to Master Warrant Officer Ong Chin Sai, a commander from Engineers Training Institute, who headed to the site and made a police report at 12:20 pm.
