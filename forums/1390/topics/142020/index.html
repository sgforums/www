    in 30SCE u will become a plant engineer or field engineer.

    as a plant operator, u will learn to operate vehicles like tractors, excavator, shovel, forklift and dozers like those u see at construction sites to support enginner operations.

    it's a good life lah, no need to chiong suah, u move around in your vehicle and dig with your vehicle, as compared to field engineers they must run here run there dig by themselves.

    in 30SCE u won't be trained in other fields like what u mentioned above as they belong to other engineers' units. if u are interested they are,

    30SCE plant engineers/field engineers
    35SCE brifging engineers/amphibious engineers
    36SCE EOD engineers
    39SCE CBRE engineers

    u might still be able to be posted out, for instance u fail IPPT. but i recommend staying on, cos all your friends are there.
