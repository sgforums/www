  • Bus Stopping
    26 Jan 12, 20:55

    Well, judging from past experiences, u will be sent to redo ur driving course once ur temp excuse driving expires. During this period if you decide to get a perm excuse driving, depending on your location current (tpt hub or node?) and the reason you were posted as a driver (posted from tekong bmt, posted from mono intake bmt, ooc from bmt, etc), you will be revocated according to your PES status.

    Since u are PES A, you could end up anywhere! I've seen some being posted to RP, some as rifleman, and back to their unit (for mono intake). It really depends on why u were vocated as a driver in the first place since u are pes A! It's highly unlikely u will be revocated to storeman or clerk, as ur S1 will have to answer to why a pes A soldier is placed in a pes C vocation.

    Last but not least, do think about what causes u to be anxious. If u are afraid to drive big trucks (3,5ton), see if u can come to an agreement with ur FM/FSM/etc to place u on details requiring only either land rover or ouv, but u would still have to be trained for the vehicles. Or if the driving instructors are just too unfriendly, pray hard to see a better one the next time. Not all of them are harsh on trainees.
