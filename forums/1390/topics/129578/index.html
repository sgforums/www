    depends on if u are talking about drill or drill LESSONS.

    for drills, you do it as long as you are moving anywhere. eg even if your company is right next to the cookhouse, you fall in, march, then fall out just for the 10 metres -_-

    but for drill lessons, there are foot drill and arms drill, meaning without and with rifles respectively. for these, there are a total of 10+ lessons over 9 weeks of enhanced bmt.

    each lesson has about 2 hours allocted to it, in which you will have at least 2 water parades and a few more water breaks, how many depends on your CSM and PS and SC (your instructors, basically)
