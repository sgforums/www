    12 Jun 06, 10:43
    Don't have to bring too many things. During BMT, your bookouts will always be in Smart 4.

    You won't wear civilian clothes at all during BMT. So don't bother to bring any of those. Typically, they will provide all your necessary clothing. The only important things to bring is underwear (which they don't provide), toothbrush/toothpaste (which I think is provided, but you rather use your own) and washing powder (those don't have usually will go emart to buy).

    Also, recruits will get to buy spare PT attire at the emart, so don't worry about that.

    So only 3 items. Oh ya, bring a pen as well, in case.
