    im back guys. 2mths into my bmt and my BMI dropped to 25 ( i lost almost 10kg). next month is my POP day and i already went thru my SIT test and field camp. Left range n HE live throw n the bloody 24km march...

    Just back here to share my thoughts again..after these 2 mths..i've certainly became more physically fitter as well as mentally...but also realized that i do not like the regimental life in SAF at all... who enjoys waking up 5am in the morning everyday...wear smart 4 for lunch..yadayada the list goes on forever....

    My initial thoughts was to bloody sign on during bmt..but now i've changed my mind. screw it seriously..i rather work outside for afew yrs then get a tution fee grant to study in uni than being bonded to SAF for 10yrs+++.... Thanks to the bros who offered their advices to me..

    *I am on att C because of high fever due to the farkin super duper hot weather during the SIT test.
