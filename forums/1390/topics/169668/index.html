    You can volunteer to extend your ORD because of maybe ATEC or NDP Marching Contigent or probably ad-hoc projects that the unit is anchoring. This DO NOT qualify you for regualr pay. You only get your NSF pay.

    However, if let say you the extention requires you to extend more than the max limit (I think max. is 1 month) than you will be extending your ORD through a Short Term Contract Service for the duration i.e. 2 months or 3 months. Then, they will pay you regular pay.

    BTW, like mentioned before, it is not counted to your reservist ah.
