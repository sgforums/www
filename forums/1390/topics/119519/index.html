    Your profile looks quite similar to mine last year... In fact, the POP timings only differ by 3 weeks... I POPed around 25 March 2004 from BMTC School 2.

    I had an interview to be a scout prior to that (after I turned down all the command schools), and when I received my posting (maybe not so surprising after all), I found out that I was going to be a scout in an active infantry battalion.

    Just keep in mind: you might not necessarily end up as a rifleman in these battalions, but it isn't that hard for them to put you there too.

    For my battalion, there was an empty company which is oddly called "Support". In it are various platoons which do different very specialised jobs: in other words, a rojak company. At the end of BMT, this company was filled with us POPees from BMTC.

    Out of the 5 possible platoons, 3 have their own vehicles, which means that you will have a chance to learn driving Â— that is, if you end up in SP Coy after your POP.
