    You will not be convert to an officer because you got a degree, it doesnt work that way. Only if you excel during your Basic Section Leader Course in SISPEC and nominated to cross over to OCS, only then they will convert your career scheme from Premium Plan to SAVER scheme upon commission as an officer. Of course your bond and MTE will still remains. If you performed well in OCS and plus your good result/merit from your Diploma, you can apply for the Local Study Award (LSA) or SAF Academic Scholarship (SAS) for your Uni study and the bond will be added to your previous 1
