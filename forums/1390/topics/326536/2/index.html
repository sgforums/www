Therefore comparing to ATEC, ATEC is just the more simple and basic form.

    Blame it on your unit, blame it on your manpwer officer, blame it on your direct commanders who failed to show you the big picture and the need to defend singapore.

    I'm not a regular and I'm an NSF. There are 3 kind of people who enlist, the first category, people who are super positive about enlistment. the 2nd category, ppl who had no issue, neither positive nor negative. The 3rd group who are negative about it.

    People who falls into the first and second group probably will have a very positive army experience. We all had to serve so lets serve it like a man. Why sulk and complaint like a girl over some issue we cant change. Why not look positive towards it and there is always something to learn from it. Even there is nothing to look forward, the experience you get is once in a life time. No chance you will get to push to your limit, hurled by vulgarities by a commander and you cant even get back to him except for cursing him in your bunk, the brotherhood which was formed between you and peers. Outfield is the time when you know who is a true friend and you will never get that kind of opportunity in real life. Technically wise, you are able to run beside an IFV, sailed on a navy ship, participated in a artillery live firing and even just to set off an I chain or a simple demolition circuit.

    Once you are negative, life just sucks for you and i pity you. I dunnoe what kind of environment groom you to be so negative about NS. Maybe your parents? So you end up going to enlistment with that sulky face and you didnt perform well enough. You do not want responsibility and you opt not to be a commander. End up as a trooper in some infantry bn. Worst, you might be posted as a supply assistant or a clerk and there lead to a dreadful army experience.

    No one explain or let you understand that support is one of the more crucial thing in an armed forces and when you are talking about real war, support must be operational ready and efficient as the front troop cant win the war without combat support.

    When you find that your army experience sucks, you will end up convincing your sons that NS is a waste of time. Those people who had a positive experience will tell their son that NS is a positive for all singaporeans sons. This people will probably take up roles of NSF specialists and officers and the cycle continues.

    All i had mentioned above is just based on a simple theory and fact. you can choose to be happy or sad. its your choice as life still goes on. Just don sulk like a child and complaint. DO SOMETHING>
