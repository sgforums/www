    If I am not wrong, the category status applies to the lightning risk in that particular sector in question. Singapore is divided into many sectors, and each sector can have a particular status at any time, the most common being:

    CAT status clear: Negligible lightning risk
    CAT 1: Lightning detected within 1 km radius of sector
    CAT 2: Lightning detected within 2 km radius of sector

    Rarely used:
    CAT 3: Lightning detected within 5 km radius of sector
    CAT 4: Lightning detected within 8 km (unsure about that) radius of sector

    Till now, I have not heard of any CAT 5, but there could be.
