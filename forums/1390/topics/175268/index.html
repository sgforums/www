    not necessarily all who ORD will get the medal. as what bcoy mentioned above, just to add on,

    the SAF Good Service Medal is awarded to NSman who has attended at least 3 high-key ICT.

    the SAF National Service Medal is awarded to NSman who has completed 10 years of operationally ready national service (ORNS) and served a minimum of 5 high key ICT and 2 low key ICT.

    a clasp may be awarded to those who completed a further 5 years of national service with a minimum of 2 high key ICT and 1 low key ICT during the eligible period.

    NSman are also eligible for the SAF Long Service and Good Conduct Medal.
