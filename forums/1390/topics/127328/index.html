    as above..

    enlisted jan this yr, subsequently submitted my uni applications after receiving my results in march.. been called up for a couple interviews already.

    went for my SMU one last Fri, and have another one at NTU Mass Comm scheduled for 23rd, next Sat. problem is, my immediate superior, the SSG at STTS attached to my group undergoing a course has told me that SAF directives are that offs are only given to soldiers for medical reasons, and medical reasons only, that academical appointments or the like are considered for 'personal reasons/benefits' and thus leave would have to be taken.

    my point now is, isn't this friggin ludicrous as hell? its not like im taking the chance to meet my gf or go out to have fun, i have scheduled university interview appointments in black and white, interviews and appointments that would very much determine how the rest of my life shapes out to be. and for that, official further studies appointments, i have to apply for leave? esp when poor NSFs like us barely have 14 days a year?

    where is the logic in that, especially when all your friggin top brass, the CDF, the COA and the like, all HAVE to be SAFOS scholars or what not? you base the entire system on a unfair emphasis on education ( IMO. ), and yet penalise those down below precisely for trying to further their education, forcing them to take leave for university interviews.

    where is the sense in that?

    my SSG told me that he understands, but doesn't have a choice as its the directive. and that the leave forms would have to be submitted nonetheless with our future units when we get our permanent postings would decide wheter to grant us the days as off's or leave instead.

    is this true? because if it is, the army seriously needs to have a rethink about its double standards and policies. ( altho its not as if EVERYONE didn't already know that. ) how are you supposed to create a new age, academically and technically advanced army if u do not even support ur own soldiers in their academical pursuits? thinking army my arse.
