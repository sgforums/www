    Actually there's AETC (Armored Engineer Training Centre) where they house the one and only AEC (Armoured Engineer Company). No they do not belong to Armour... I'm the first ever PC in it hah...

    Organic Combat Engineers in Infantry units are called Pioneers. (Also in Armour units on 2nd tot). They perform basic Engineer tasks like Wire Obstacles (Triple-Con, 4-coil, 10-coil, Tangle foot...), Mine Clusters, and clearing of the same obstacles. Some lucky souls will also be required to build Battalion Command Post (BCP) ^^
