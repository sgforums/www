    i don't think many people are particular about this, but actually, it takes some skills to sew your rank onto your no.4 uniform beautifully.

    for example always buy ranks that have very little black camou pattern as possible, better if have none at all. especially for officers, epaulettes which have balck camou pattern covering the "singapura" wording or the rank itself are not very nice to look at. make it very hard of people to see.

    so anyway, anyone with experience or words of advice please feel free to share with us here.
