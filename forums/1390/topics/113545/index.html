    What would be the point of such a rename? It would largely be a farce whichever way you looked at it.

    Militarily speaking, there is no such thing as a Defence Force in the modern world. Any modern armed force with weapons that are offensive in nature like long range artillery is obviously not just a defensive force. Even the Japanese Self Defense Force is considering changing it's name to meet reality but can't do so at the moment due to constitutional requirements.

    Practically speaking, does a name change really matter? Everybody would know that the SAF would still be the SAF even if we changed it's name. Changing a name would be a nonsensical and costly exercise following the lines of other useless name changes like SPRING Singapore (formerly Productivity & Standards Board) and Agency for Science, Technology & Research (A* STAR) formerly known as (National Science and Technology Board (NSTB).

    All these name changes ran on taxpayer money and came out of these board's budgets, I think the SAF would have better ways of spending several million dollars.
