    Chamber explosion was due to missing CAM PIN not firing pin. With missing cam pin, the firing pin can still strike the cartridge and cause it to fire. Problem is that because the firing pin was so loose since no cam pin to hold it in place, chamber explosion resulted after a few rounds. The cam pin was found after a search was conducted for investigation.

    If it had been the firing pin that was missing, the rifle would not have been able to fire at all, and no explosion would have resulted... but why play around? Better not to fool with weapon components.
