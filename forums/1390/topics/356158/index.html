    no one in infantry uses the M4 or CAR-15. it was mostly used by armoured troops in the 1990s due to the confined spaces that is their workplace. they're all being replaced by SARs.

    the SAR-21 is more than adequate for most missions, as what you get from a bullpup rifle is maximum barrel length, overall reduced length of the rifle. hence theoratically speaking, the sar-21 CAN be argued to be more accurate, or at least more than capable of fitting the mission profile of anyone who requires an M4.

    The CAR-15s that the infantry and support arms used to have (carried by recce troops) had no rails, bells and whistles whatsoever.this is because last time round stuff such as ACOGS, EOtechs etc etc were simply too expensive to equip a normal conscript army. and i believe they weren't even invented yet.

    The reason why alot of naval boarding teams use the CAR-15 is due to the fact that they.. simply have not received the SAR-21 yet. (this is an opinion, not a fact.)

    besides, theres the SAR-21 P-rail MMS, so theres no need for us to buy any M4s.
