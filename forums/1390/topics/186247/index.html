    Hey bro,thats quite an informal way of making known your request.there is a much more convenient and easier way.Have you undergone ur medical check-up at CMPB.Have u been accorded a PES Status.A PES status is given after ur medical check up.After ur medical check up,you be given some leeway in time,before ur enlistment letter pops in.During this period,it is advisable to go to the Police Force Website,and click on Police Officer,and quick apply,it is advisable that u must be at least PES A or B,medically fit,minimum height of 1.62m,quite good eyesight,have a clean background.Are u mentally prepared for it?,Thats a sure way of signing on.but before all this,other than being posted by luck for Police NSF cus there r openings for all units,be it the army,navy or any other forces,our defence force is a conscript one.Loyalty to Country,Leadership,Discipline,Professionalism,Fighting Spirit,Ethics,Care for our Soldiers..and perhaps Safety and Team Excellence if you are in the Air Force.Be proud that u take on a role to serve the nation.Police,u probably report to PA,other than that,Mono intakes or others u report to CMPB or Hendon Camp,other than that the Army,u will report to Tekong,others U report to SCDF HQ or SOTC for other units.The Police has a BMT phase of around 5 months also.Do take in consideration.Try to take IPPT and get Silver or Gold in your tertiary institution
