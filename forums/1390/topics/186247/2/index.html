    K, let me explain how the system works...

    Police intakes are usually JC 2nd BMTC intake (march/apr batch) and Poly 1st intake (June/july batch)

    Number of recruits transferred to police, usually 30-40 per batch.

    How many ppl are transferred to Police is a request by Home Affairs to Mindef.

    There are agreemetns on the minimum number Mindef will transfer to Home Affairs and the criteria of ppl that is to be transferred.

    In short, you don't really have much you can do to choose your path to police unless you got connection. Another way you can do is sign on police and break contract... so your NSF is served in Police... expensive.

    But then, no harm trying to write them a letter... seriously, i dun think the letter will go far.
