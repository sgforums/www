    I am an old soldier ... so not current. But I believe that your unit or higher HQ will give you a briefing. Usually they will also issue a list of stuff you can bring and also instructions as to what you cannot bring or do. Standard SAF procedure.

    Personally, having been to India on business trips ... I would bring the following (if the SAF has not told you to do so)....

    1) water purification tablets
    2) mosquito net
    3) jungle hat ( scout hat)
    4) canvas trainers (sports shoe)
    5) some Indian rupees (more as a tip to villagers who are always keen to do things for you).
