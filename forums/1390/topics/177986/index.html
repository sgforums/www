    About 10-20% possibility..
    After posting out from OCS + SISPEC, a sizeable percentage of the cohort goes on to be staff officers or even service combat trained specs..yah..8+, 4+ months of training ain't that good

    Combat Medics: Be posted to some medical centres that doesn't require staying in..Though there maybe field covers that burn ur weekends or so

    Signallers: certain postings for signallers are 8-5
