    For Revocation, it is an administrative issue, so it is none of Medical Centre business anymore, thus please seek help from the NS unit's S1 Manpower Branch's Chief Clerk/ DyS1 Officer/ S1 Officer on revocation matters. 

  • vsclassic
    1 Apr 14, 20:31

    I understand there is a branch called CPC that handles unit posting...will they revocate based on a temp downpes? 

  • eac
    1 Apr 14, 21:06

    As all the NS units' S1 Manpower Branch reports to the CPC in CMPB, hence it is better to seek assistance from the NS unit's S1 Manpower Branch. 

    Usually, temporary PES personnel are often temporary revocation as either clerk or storeman. So if in doubt, it is the best to seek help from the NS unit's S1 Manpower Branch since it handles all personnel manpower administration. 

  • vsclassic
    2 Apr 14, 06:28

    Ok thanks eac for the valuable advice. But does revocation means being transfered out of the camp or internal transfer within the unit?

  • eac
    2 Apr 14, 10:14

     

    If the camp is a single NS unit camp, then usually it is internal transfer within unit for Temp PES and it is transfer out of camp for Perm PES.

    If the camp is a multiple NS units camp, then usually it is internal transfer within camp for both Temp and Perm PES.
