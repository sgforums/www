    You don't get to attend your POP since you are out of course already. Some when your MC overlaps your POP, that means you can start packing up, return the store items and bring everything back home. Most of the time, you will get the extension of your MC, since it's a fracture but not other medical condition, but I say a specialist have more power on that since they are the specialist and the MOs are not. You most likely will go to storeman or clerk, not very clear on that, heard that if your injury is on the right leg, you can forget about driver. Recourse date will depends on your superior, they can send you asap or they can drag all the way till the few months before you ORD.
