    agreed. most OC at ITD when was there (1985-87) were 'between jobs' being a regular you served 2 years active unit, 2 years school (includes BCTC, SISPEC, Service school and NService Training Centres), 2 years higher formation (as understudy like 2IC etc) or various combinations of this sequence.
