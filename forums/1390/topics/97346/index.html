    Our soldiers now no longer have their outfield meals packed in squat styrofoam boxes in styrofoam containers. HQ Supply & Transport (HQ S&T) has been phasing in new paper meal boxes and meal box carrier bags, with all camps SAF-wide now using the new items.

    In terms of both design and material, the new meal boxes and carrier bags offer significant improvements. The Â“cover-over-baseÂ” design and the material of the box are able to retain heat better and help to prevent contamination of the food. The box is also stronger with the 3-ply reinforced corner, and being made out of recycled, biodegradable foodgrade paper, is also more environmentally-friendly.

    The carrier bag is lined with five layers of insulating material like aluminium foil and foam to provide the same benefits, and has two carrying slings for convenient movement. Overall, handling and transportation is now much improved. Both the box and bag are also lighter.
