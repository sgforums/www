  • jianfish9
    21 Feb 07, 11:53
    Did my BMT at Tekong Bravo Coy in 2005. Felt like a crappy place. Just finished my A levels and straight away thrown into this hell-hole. Was in Enhanced batch but my fitness is really bad coz for the last six months never exercise at all once I cleared my Napfa test.

    Before I enlist, I thought NS was Bad but after I enlisted, I thought NS is even worse than I thought.

    Did not like the Specs and officers there. Who do they think they are? Got 3 stripes or 1 bar so what? My friends and I made an agreement that once out in the corporate world, if we see them, we will give them hell. Lol. My OC was good though. Was a Senior Warrant but then is not commissioned so ended up my company always last to book out and we had to complain to the 2IC of BMTC. We book out at 5pm on Every Sat.

    Luckily had good friends there, I guess going through hardship always create good friends.

    Anyway, Glad I Ord. Since my reservist is 8-5 guess I don't mind going back but not so soon in my career though.
    Personally, BMT is the worst place of my NS. Crap really. Glad I was there for 9weeks only.
