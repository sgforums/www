    Originally posted by Gackt247:
    Aiyo.. i have a headache as to how to PERMANENTLY get rid of fungi growth on the exterior surface of my rarely-worn parade boots. How?
    Abstain from keeping your boots in damp moist places.
    After wearing your boots for a parade, do allow the air inside to flow and ensure the inside of the boots are dry before keeping them somewhere.
    Also ensure the whole surface of the exterior boots is clean dry before keeping them.

    These are some of the preventive measures to cut down the growth of fungi.
