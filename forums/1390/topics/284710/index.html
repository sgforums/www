    You can see him, just to confirm your new status. Sometimes the medical dockets don't return to the unit in time for the ICT; I know, cuz I've used this loophole to draw out my own medical limbo in my time ~lol~

    The unit MO can write out a more-detailed excuse chit, if warranted. But the thing is, as I tried to say previously, once you report back you're usually obliged to follow the rest out to field training too. Dunno how the infantry side do it, but for combat support like Arty or SCE which are vehicle-based, you'll move out with the others & then it's up to your commanders & buddies on how best to utilize/accomodate you. (Many older reservists end up routinely doing this later on in their cycle, after obtaining downgrading but the unit still must operate at fullest strength possible, mah.) No more staying back in camp or following rations truck, like in active time.

    If you want to siam all these, then it's best to ensure that your CO know you have a legitimate excuse &, hopefully, he won't call you back as a result. Better yet, post you out altogther to the holding list, so as to siam doing a makeup ICT with another unit in the same workyear too.
