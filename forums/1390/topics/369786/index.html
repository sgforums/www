    Extracted: www.ns.sg/publish/NSPORTAL/public/all_services/index/Phase2_OverseasNotify/general_information/mindef_services__5.html

    www.mindef.gov.sg/imindef/mindef_websites/topics/nsmen/opsready/Mobilisation.html

    For Ops Manning:
    NSmen are excused from mobilisation only if they are overseas and have documentary proof to show this. Notification of overseas trips is not considered documentary proof for exemption from mobilisation.

    Those NSmen who fail to report or fail to do so within the period allowed by their NS unit will be regarded as absentees. They will continue to be regarded as such until they report to their NS unit to explain their absence. Disciplinary action will be taken against those NSmen without acceptable explanations. The effectiveness of the system and the operational readiness of your NS unit depends on you.

    NSmen who notify MINDEF / MHA are not automatically exempted from ICTs or any NS training and activities. They will have to apply for deferment or disruption from their respective NS units/NSSC if they wish to be deferred or disrupted.
