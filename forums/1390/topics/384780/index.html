    Hi shadez,

    If you drop out before you complete your Diploma course, you will be considered a "O"-level certificate holder, and thus will likely join the Mono-intake batches.

    You are not allowed to appeal to join those who are doing their BMT at Pulau Tekong, your period of serving depends on your NAPFA test results, and your allowance as a recruit depends on which Mono-intake BMT you entered. 

    Cheers.
