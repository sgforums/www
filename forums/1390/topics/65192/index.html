    whoa sorry to hear that bro but to be honest, its better to enlist in jan. if u're april batch, when its time to go for Uni, u gotta defer, and then when u grad from uni u have to come back n serve your balance out. that is even suckier. Its better to finish it off in one shot and then go study without any balance. i have a friend who defered to go study, and he was offered a job in the states, US$15k a month. after some time, he had to quit his US$15k a month job to come back n serve out his balance. so he quit a US$15k a month job to come back and take $500 a month. can u imagine how he felt? so this is actually a blessing in disguise.

    I also enlisted barely 3 weeks after my last A levels paper, but i dun actually see it as a bad thing. now i am glad that i have some time to recharge my batteries b4 going to uni, in fact because of the time i have inbetween ORD n entering uni, im planning to take 2 months to tour europe, something im sure my parents wouldn't have allowed me to do so back when i haven't entered NS yet. and im glad i will not have to come back and serve out any balance. Once im through im through with SAF.

    Anyway, enjoy your BMT. unit life is worse.
