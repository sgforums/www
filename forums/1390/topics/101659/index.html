    If you are going to work or study overseas, and this can be proved via a company letter or letter from a recognised school, you would then present this letter to CMPB exit permit office, and get an exit permit that would allow you to be away from Singapore and be disrupted from reservist duties.

    I have been away since 1995 on an exit permit as I have been working all these years abroad.
