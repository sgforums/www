  • wuming78

    you cant be a scholar if you dont have a degree. period.

    anyway, let's do some counting. a guy finishes JC and enters NS at 19; finishes NS at 21 and goes to Uni; finishes Uni at 25. If he so chooses to sign on then, he will be a CPT, because he has a basic degree.

    so there's really no big deal with that.

    on the other hand, a poly student finishes poly and enters NS at 20; finishes NS at 22. If he chooses to sign on then, he will be a LTA. And even for scheme A officers (without degree), they can get their CPTs in around 5 yrs, i.e. he will become a CPT at around 27.

    so there's no big deal with that too.
