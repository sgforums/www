  • the Bear
    13 Aug 04, 21:28
    there are 2 parts to that needle...

    the needle itself and the "yakult straw" plastic bit which covers the needle... after penetration into the vein, the plastic bit is pushed into the vein and the needle is withdrawn...

    that's the painful bit...

    also, you'll learn to give injections.. on each other also using sterile normal saline... the more you relax, the less painful injections are

    other than that, the rest are normal training..

    you'll be trained how to do basic triage, first aid, CPR and also basic knowledge in medicines and drugs.. also taught how to work in a medical centre, basic nursing and patient care...

    things that actually have use in the civilian world when you ORD
