    During my time in BMT ( I was in ITD), we were given forms to fill up. To mark our choice as to where we want to go. The instructors warned us that it was for show, as no one ever reads it apparently. This was in the 80s.

    On the sheet were infantry, guards, armour, armoured infantry, arty, engineers etc.

    I marked armour infantry, cos I figured that since infantry was likely to be the case for most recruits ( even if they went to SISPEC or OCS ) , might as well volunteer for armured infantry... but then get to ride on vehicles to go into battle !

    When my section leader in ITD saw my choice , he went berserk and started blasting me for trying to think this way

    I had opted in the form to carry on with "Guards" after my BMT when doing my BMT then in 7BTS as kana brain-wash by the Guards instructors. However, when the actual posting came out, was posted to Armour for trade course ...

    Towards the end of SISPEC, I volunteered for the PTI course selection and cleared all the entry requirements. Few days later, one of the PTI who was present in the selection interviewed me that I cannot join PTI as my name already "bonded" with Armour. Armour not willing to let go of me bcos I'm suppose to be part of a roving team to train recruits. The only way out is to sign-on as a PTI for me to "escape" from Armour. Of course, I never sign-on. One of my SISPEC company mate whom shortlisted for the PTI course eventually became a PTI, even went through Ranger course and I heard he is a 2WO now.

    In the end, I became AI as I was rejected to be a tankee bcos i got partial colour-blindness ...

    Now, I ROD liao ... no more IPPT, MOB and ICT ...
