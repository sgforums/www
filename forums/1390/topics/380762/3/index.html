    Field camp sucks because there are so many rules and such. So if you want to have fun , ignore the rules. Ok seriously, I always keep in mind something my sergeant told me, an offence is not am offence unless your caught. He told us lots of stories about things he only dared to do when he got to SISPEC cos he was shit scared during BMT. So my section, we got to alot of mischief and didn't get caugt. It was totally a rush and soo worth it. We cooked maggi in the wee hours of the morning. At 3am. The fires were very tactical and the instructors are asleep too.
