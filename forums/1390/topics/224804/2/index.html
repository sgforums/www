    nope MSCC are all done in house by OETI. We are taught a more intensive course during our 3 week command course which includes topo(manual and GPS), field maintenance work processes (spare indenting n such), infantry tactics (section movement such as 1up 2 up etc, firefight procedures, combing area, weps deployment, clearing obstacles), convoy tactics (moving out, Convoy proceedure, Anti Ambush/ anti aircraft drills, voice procedures, RALP, LOAC and some other maintenance stuff like QC and other stuff tts integral for maintenance deployments

    we finish our section comd. training we head on to train our men within our units under the ATTS system under the specific Bn SOP. the section will then go thru 2 evaluation periods, the section prof test and the platoon prof test before ORDing.

    anyway yep, the armt specs will end up in their respective 3,6 or 9 AMBs, no change is SAFTOE for logistics for now, juz the name change but certainly changes are coming along such as no more spec ranks for all techies, only comd. and section 2IC gets spec ranks....and some more coming along the way
