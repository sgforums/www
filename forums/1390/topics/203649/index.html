    Try to keep in pace to aviod falling back from the gruop. If you fall back few steps behind, you will find that you have to double or run inorder to catch up. You don't want that to happen, imagine that you fall behind about 10 metres than if will always seem that you are getting further and further away from the gruop.

    Beside fitness problem, one common problem that most guys fall behind are due to looking downward while marching. They might tend to slow down during that process and when they start to look up, they discover that they already slack behind quite a distant away.

    So, the key for fast march is keep walking and always keep in pace with the front.
