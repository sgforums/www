    Leave is pro-rated which means you will only receieve the amount according to how long you serve in that year.

     

    Let's say you enlisted on Sept 2008 and ord on Sept 2010.

     

    From Sept 2008 till Dec 2008,you should have about 4 days or so.

     

    From Jan 2009 till Dec 2009 you will have the full 14 days.

     

    From Jan 2010 till ord,you will have 10 days.

     

    So,in the end you will end up having 28 days for the 2 years(14 days/year).

     

    Leave cannot be brought forward so please clear it before the year ends.You can find out how much leave you have left from the clerks in S1 branch.
