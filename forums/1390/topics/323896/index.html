    NSFs just show 11b when register at govt hospital/polyclinics... will get full subsidy... even if u at A&E register.

    BUT if u want to see specialist, must got referral letter either from through polyclinic or camp medical centre.... then saf will subsidies full...

    another thing is even if got referral letter from above, but if u refer to a specialist in hospital by name, saf will not subsidies...

     

    only if need hosptialisation stay, need to pay a little bit... saf will still subsidies... a lot of the % of the cost of stay... like 80%?...

     

    for more info... refer to the Recruit Handbook in pdf format under the pre-enlistee section in www.ns.sg . there got list the co-payment list
