    Anyone has stories or memories to share??

    I start off first. It was our COC Parade for my DivComd. I was given a TOP to delegate the parking and sitting arrangements for the visiting guests.

    Anyway, there was this LTC who got out of his car and started to make noise to the ushers. I went up to him and asked whats wrong. Wah he shouted at me why his parking space so far. And he wants to park nearer to the Parade Square. I told him cannot as those are reserved. He kpkb so much.

    Hey, who know who I am? I am bla bla bla, he said. I said, Sir, there are over 20 over Col's and Generals coming to grace this event. Not to mention guests from the various ministries as well. I am sorry, base on the TOP, your rank is nowhere near them. I said this nicely of cos. He turned and walked away.

    Then as he climbed the stairs, he slipped from a step and felled. I was trying my very best trying not to laugh. Hahaha. In my mind, I was like serve you right for scolding me. But when I helped him up, he turned to me, reached out his hand and apologized to me over what happen earlier on. I was immediately touched.

    Maybe the fall brought him back to his senses....
