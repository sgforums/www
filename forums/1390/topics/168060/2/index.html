    Because the route march is so monotonous... I feel that it is actually more tiring than an equivalent long tactical march. I have participated in a long movement to contact ( 20 km) before , overnight, no sleep, engaging enemies a few times along the way ... and it was not so tiring as a concentrated route march of 24 km.

    I found it helped for route marches when:-

    1) You have a few songs in your head and you "sing along" mentally. Hopefully, it has a nice rhythm to get you going .

    2) Having water just before the route march helps... It allows your body to move vital minerals and nutrients around, and also avoids dehydration. Energy levels you will find much higher if you are well watered.
