    You'll ORD as per normal, whether fit or unfit.

    No extension whatsoever; unless you get charged and send to DB, only then will the ORD be extended as that's a different case issue.

    At most, after you ORD as per normal, you will be called back to a 1Day medical review at the medical centre as a reservist NSman.

    For more info about medical review, go to:
