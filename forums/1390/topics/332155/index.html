    Every month, Defence will sent Pioneer magazine to NSman and NSF man. I notice that my friend household will receive 2 copies of the magazine due to the 2 NSman in the house. I note that it is a waste of natural resources and public expenditure. Is it possible for the DEFENCE to generate only one magazine to the household with especially two NSman in the household.

    =============================================

    Dear Sir/Madam,

    1. We thank you for your suggestion and appreciate this opportunity to clarify.

    2. PIONEER is aware that there are households receiving more than one copy of the magazine. Thus, letters have been sent to such households annually to ask if they would like to continue receiving multiple copies or just one copy of PIONEER magazine. Not all have indicated that they prefer only one copy. But for those who have, we have stopped the subscription of other members of the household.

    3. You may wish to inform your friend that he may contact our circulation staff at 6373-1114 or 6373-1121 to stop his subscription at any point in time so that their family will receive only one copy of PIONEER magazine henceforth.


    Ms Adeline Wang
    Staff Officer (Special Duties)
    MINDEF HQ
