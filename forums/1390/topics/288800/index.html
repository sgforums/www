    I used to be a failure in SBJ 210cm .. but now I can jump 240cm...

    This is how I train...

    Every alternate days.. go to a step or a curb in the car park... do squat jumps... make sure u really squat all the way down before u make a jump again....

    Then try jumping up 5-6 steps of staicase... short staircase like the one outside a house... or steps in the garden... make sure u can jump to the top of the stairs... best is get someone to stand behind u for safety....

    You should feel extremely wobbly if u do this exercises for 30 mins... thats the treshold... relax for a day or two before doing it again...

  • ad3285
    31 Oct 07, 17:43
    Power-jump

    Starting position: Squat down
    Step 1: From squatting position change to half squat position.
    Step 2: From half squat position (like how u going to jump off for SBJ), do a high jump trying to reach as high as possible with your hands extended out.
    Step 3: After jumping, land and go back to half squat position.
    Step 4: From half squat position change to squatting position.

    Repeat 12 times per set. Do around 3 sets ^^
