    you were asking how to flee overseas...

    first, so long you meet the immigration requirements overseas, that country don't give a hoot whether you awolled. That is to say, be a PR or Citizen of that country, and live there happily. While serving NS, just apply leave and get exit permit.

    If you want to return singapoer, just get a different identity, but be forewarned, there have been cases where even ppl with different identity were caught, but were let go and banned from entering singapore as there are no solid case against them.

    also, if singapore come to find out that you are in a country that supports national service and at the same time has treaty with singapore.... singapore will request your extradition. So flee to country that do not have extradition treaty with singapore or does not support the idea of conscription.
