    I strongly believe you are in a "no-win" situation if you try to AWOL. I have travelled and worked in many countries (almost the whole of Western Europe, some parts of Asia, the US ...). The problem with going to these countries is that they have a strict immigration and work permit policy. No way you can get a job legally. So if you escape to these countries, you got to find a legal way to stay on. Otherwise, you will also be worrying about the local police catching up with you, besides worrying about being deported back to S'pore to face detention.

    So you are better off serving your NSF or reservist time. Look on the bright side... how bad can it be... weaker people have been through it ?!
