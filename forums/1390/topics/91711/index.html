    Sportsmen are posted officially to whichever units but are attached out to whichever organisation to train and play. bear in mind that this normally happens for downgraded personnel, very rarely do you have a PES A/B person in an Active unit being attached out for long periods (have heard of cases where some active personnel have been attached out for 1-2mths to play polo for their formation)

    if your soccer skills are exceptionally good and don't get accepted in SAFFC, you can always play for your formation and all that kind of thing. not attached out full time, but still get to FO at 2pm to train etc. etc.
