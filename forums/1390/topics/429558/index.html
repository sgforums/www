    1. You will be only bathing once a day. If you are lazy to wash, bring one and a half dozen, with the extras for emergency cases.  If you can wash your own stuff, 5 will actually do.

    2. 1st 2 weeks depend on your company schedule. Usually there is some basic training only (3km run is really basic). They cannot tekkan you yet (by right, though you can complain if it goes by left. Don't complain if you are in diver/commando BMT though.), so don't worry.
