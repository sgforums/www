    Sheares will have been given the instructions that the SAF has sent to the units. You can ask them for a copy and then show to your unit.

    A business process cannot be verbal when there is a procedure for purchase.

    Scenario 1 :-
    Sheares : How can we make Oakley available at a cheap price for your soldiers ?
    SAF : Simple, I will call all the units to tell them verbally to order through the unit.
    Sheares : Thanks !

    Scenario 2 :-
    Sheares : how can we make Oakley available at a cheap price for your soldiers ?
    SAF : Here is the list of address of all the units. Please you send a GOM to them.
    Sheares : thanks, I will do it right away !

    It cannot be like that, right ?

    There got to be some paperwork that involves pricing. SAF got to check that they are getting some genuine discounts. Then the person in-charge sends a circular to all the units, and the units puts this on the notice board. There must be an agreed format so it would make the processing easy, like unit, address, contact person, style and quantity, payment mode, etc. Ask for that circular.
