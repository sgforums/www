    Last time my WO taught us to walk with a slight bounce.

    This means that every step you take, the load of the full pack tends to follow gravity...ie downwards. Fight against it and you will have aches over your body.

    Therefore if you bounce a little, letting your legs absorb the downforce and push it up again. Your leg muscles stronger than your shoulder muscle. Let the legs do all the work.

    You must have strong legs though......
