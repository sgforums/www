    Non-competitive 5km reg fees $5 flag off 8.00am closing date Sun, 5 Sep 04

    Registration starts now. For more information, visit the official SBR & AHM website at www.safra.org.sg/sbr&ahm, email shearesbridgerun@safra.org.sg or call 63779860/ 865 (during office hours).
