    after some research on the web, i managed to find the answers to my own questions.

    Parachutist
    Candidates who complete the Basic Airborne Course conducted at the Parachute Training Wing are awarded their 'wings'. Commandos must complete 5 combat equipment jumps and 2 night combat equipment jumps, while personnel from other vocations must do 2 clean fatigue jumps and 1 night jump. Commandos wear this badge with a red backing.

  • Gordonator
    5 Sep 04, 14:59
    Senior Parachutist
    To qualify for the Senior Parachutist's badge, personnel have to complete a total of 25 jumps, of which 5 must be combat equipment jumps, 2 night equipment jumps and 2 tactical descents. As with all parachute badges, this badge is worn above the left pocket. All Parachute Jump Instructors are Senior Parachutists, and wear this badge with a yellow backing.

  • Gordonator
    5 Sep 04, 15:03
    Master Parachutist
    Master Parachutists have to complete a total of 60 jumps, of which 30 must be day combat equipment jumps, 10 night combat equipment jumps, 5 tactical descents, and 1 intentional water jump.

    Rigger
    The Rigger badge is worn by parachute riggers who have completed the Rigger's Airborne Course. Riggers have to do the same number of jumps as the Basic Airborne Course, and must be proficient in the packing of parachutes.
