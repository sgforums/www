  • baseturn
    24 Sep 06, 01:50
    Of course you can go overseas leave for your holiday during NS.
    Just go and fill in the leave form.
    In the form you must tell them where you are going for holiday.
    Before you do so, make sure you check with your commander first and get their approval.

    Don't bother hiding that you are going overseas, if you get caught you will be charged with either AWOL or exit permit violation.
    It's not worth it, just get permission and take leave.

    on the above points it is stated in the MP-GOMs... my notes are back in singapore but here's what i remember....

    To go overseas, service personnel MUST take leave OR off if the overseas travel is for non-service related reasons.

    Service personnel's leave or off does not equate to having an exit permit which must be applied separately.

    Some units insists that you apply leave even if you actually don't need to.

    On foreign MCs, there is a directive that permits service personnel to submit foreign MCs. But it must clearly bear issuing clinic, address and doctor's name. Doctor must be registered to practice in that country (duh).
