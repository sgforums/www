    For officers this might be interesting. Looking up my batch rep I found the list of OCS batches in Mindef's reference database. Over the years the batch descriptions have changed from BATCH, SMC, IOCC, to today's OCC. For example, 1/83 IOCC was my batch but in actual chronological terms it was the 38th batch to go through OCS. For those entering BMT, SISPEC or OCS, never forget many have gone before you, survived and done well.
