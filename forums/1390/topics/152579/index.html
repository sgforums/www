    Tip 1.

    Just a note to all serving, if your rifle butt is muddy, DO NOT kiwi it.

    For the sake of your poor Armt Specs, please gaffer tape your rifle butt or clean it.Kiwi screws hell with the Adrox anti-rust preservative oil and before you know it, your butt plate screw is not so easy to rust proof and your Armt Specs have to spend too much time de-oiling and re-oiling that they won't have time to fix the problems in your weapons that matter the most.



    Tip 2.

    Please do not drop your rifle on the ground unnecessarily, especially when you attempt to disassemble your rifle.Your PC and PS may like to slam the rifles on the ground, that is because they have not attended the Small Arms course.But if you can help it, DO NO slam.

    Thanks.
