    SAF Act allows MP WOSE to arrest a Commissioned Officer.

    SAF Act also allow a lower ranking WOSE to arrest a WOSE of a higher rank provided he has grounds that the higher ranking WOSE is in breach of any current regulations.

    When I was in the same rover with my OC (who is also the driver), we got a road check by a CPL MP near Jalan Asrama.

    CPL MP: (Sedia with stomping on the ground and salutes) Good Afternoon Sir, We are conducting a traffic enforcment ops, I need to see your 11B and LOA.

    OC: (Salutes back and searches his wallet for LOA) I got 11B and Camp pass but i did not bring my LOA which is in Camp.

    Then the CPL MP calls Kaki Bukit Camp to verify if the said NRIC (my OC) is issued an LOA and the reply was positive.

    CPL MP: Sorry sir, without LOA on you, you are not allowed to operate a military vehicle.

    My OC then took out handphone and call the rover that left few minutes earlier to return since there's another LOA'ed driver in that vehicle.

    CPL MP returns the 11B and camp pass, sedias himself with stomping onto the groud and salutes, thanking my OC for his cooperation.
