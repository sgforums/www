    http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PULHHEEMS

    a grade from 1 to 5 is assigned to each category, where 1 is excellent ability to 4 for little ability to 5 for no ability.

    the lowest grade amongst the 8 categories will determine ur PES status. PES A requires minimum 1 for all, PES B is 2, PES C is 3, PES E is 4 and PES F is 5.

    PES D is a temporary classification.

    Hearing is classified as a cateogry by SAF, and actually assigned a lower weightage, where someone with a grade 2 in hearing can still get a PES A, grade 3 will still get PES B or C.

    L codes are outfield deployability codes which are meant for seperate assessment from the PES C/E which is determined by the above processed.

    PES C2 and below will not need to fulfil IPPT obligations.
