    There is always a time lapse problem in SAF. There are two different departments doing their job in SAF, and they dont necessary update each other.

    Medical review is temp Pes E9L9 till Jan 2010. This means that they should put you on a medical review before they upgrade you. In feb, 2 weeks before the ICT then you will take action when there is no news from the SAF. 

    You should get a specialist letter when you visit your specialist for review, keep it as proof.  

    I have seen NS men walk in on the first day of ICT, and report sick and get three weeks of att C, forgoing the ICT straight.
