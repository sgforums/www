    I highly doubt you will find time to watch that TV-phone legally in Tekong.

    You will most probably be secretly watching it after lights out in your bed, which is what one of the guys from my BMT company did with his friend. They ended up getting caught by their PS, and had his PSP taken away for his BMT duration. His PS was kind enough to return it to him on POP though.

    Generally speaking, phones with TV function is legal, as long as it does not have a camera or any recording devices and memory sticks. If you have to plug something into your HP to watch TV, then you can forget about bringing it in.
