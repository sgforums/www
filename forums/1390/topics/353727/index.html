    The BMT duration is fix. If you miss certain activities due to MC its ok but you need to clear field camp, SOC, range, IPPT etc. If you do not complete most of the major activities or get too many days of MC, you might be Out of Cource (OOC) and have to return to tekong again for a recourse at a much later date.
