    Originally posted by erijazz:
    The Forest is a place I call 'HOME'
    I agree. In fact during NS (and to some extent during ICT) I was very happy to be outfield. Particularly in the forest / jungle. Usually it will be platoon or section level when in close terrain ... so we are pretty much on our own.

    For those going into NS first time, take the time to "enjoy" the jungle. Relax and soak in the atmosphere. Look out for rare animals (see if you can spot the mousedeer in Mandai forest for example). Look out for rare plants... some are edible (but check with your seniors first if you are not sure). The enemy (unless he is bloody sakai or something like that) is also uneasy with the jungle. The jungle is your refuge, your friend... can also be your worst enemy.
