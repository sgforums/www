    Hope this Combat Shoot FAQ is useful for those who haven't taken their yet. feel free to correct me or add on more tips/info.

    Introduction

    Combat shoot is only 1 day.

    For combat shoot, a team of 6 firers will make up 1 detail. The final score will be the total number of shots added up together. Each firer with get 3 mags of 5 rounds each for both day and night. So each person will shot 15 shots for day and 15 shots for night, total 30 shots per person. So a detail will shoot a combined number of 180 shots, passing is 90 shots, marksman is 144 shots. If u get marksman, everyone in the detail can wear the badge.

    the targets used are figure 11 and figure 12, the 2 biggest targets in range. So thereÂ’s no reason why u can miss.

    ThereÂ’ll be no IA detail so make sure u do all the necessary rifle cleaning, listen to orders carefully to prevent IAs and getting your rounds wasted.

    A feature of the combat shoot is the combat loading for your 3rd mag. No instructions will be given after you finished shooting your 2nd mag, you are to combat load your 3rd mag and continue shooting. I will touch on that later.

    You will take up foxhole with support position for both day and night shoot at the 100m point. This is the best position so make sure u do your best.

    For day shoot, you will be issued with 3 mag of 5 rounds each. All target will stay for 4sec before going down. ThatÂ’s more time than ATP so donÂ’t rush, take your time to shoot. The sequence of shoot goes like this.

    1st mag the 5 targets will come out in fixed sequence for all lanes. The conducting officer will tell u all the sequence so that u all can memorize it before hand.

    For example,

    Left figure 11, Right figure 12
    Right figure 12
    Right figure 12
    Left figure 11

    So as u can see, itÂ’s relatively easy to shoot the 1st 5 rounds, the purpose of this is to help u pass so try your best to hit all the 5 targets.

    After shooting your 5 rounds, unload check clear, wait for further instructions.

    2nd mag and 3rd mag, this is where the fun part comes in. firstly take note that no instruction will be given when u finished your 2nd mag, u are required to do a combat loading and continue to shoot your 3rd mag. Secondly the targets of figure 11 and figure 12 will come out in random sequence, take note of your lane number, if u are lane no.2 only shoot target no.2, if u are lane no.4 only shoot no.4 target. So meaning to say, please count 1st before u shoot.

    Example,





    Keep in mind that u will always have something to shoot at even though that the target might not appear in your lane. Your target might appear in other lane either to your left or right. So if let say you are lane no. 3, your target will appear in lane 2, 3 or 4.

    It is very important that u pay full attention when shooting. Always do a count and shoot the correct target. U can crossfire but it is strongly recommended that u shoot your own target.

    After u have finish your 2nd mag, quickly unload the mag but do not check clear, load in your 3rd mag and ease spring, your 3rd mag will be loaded. You have 6 seconds to do all this. This is the part where many people will gan chiong and screw up, so make sure u do it quick and good, to prevent IAs because if u IA, youÂ’ll lose your concentration and rounds wasted.
