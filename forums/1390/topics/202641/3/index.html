    okay... just to provide further info on the new approved changes...but until the official comprehensive document is out (GOM/Directive), all these info is only meant FYI at reading pleasure. only those enlisted in late 2005 batches onwards are eligible, these include:

    At CO's discretion through S1 Br:

    1)
    - All men eligible for CPL, regardless of education level. Time norm still apply: (earliest promotion time: Combat: 12months, Svc/Tech: 15 months from DOE.) ; at unit quota.

    - Reintroduction of CFC (Corporal First Class) [last seen in the 1980s?];
    criteria:::
    --> attainable from the 18th month from DOE (Combat/Svc/Tech)
    --> based on unit assessment and quota. e.g. commander's recommendation?
    --> only top 20% of CPLs in the unit are considered (even top 20% also still need to pick)

    2)
    PES E enlistees can be CPL in 15 months, same as with the PES C enlistees. Last time, PES E can be CPL only from the 18th month from DOE.

    3)
    Best Performing 3SGs are also be considered to be promoted to 2SGs earlier after completing the PS course.

    4)
    Best Performing 2LTs of the unit (maybe top 10%) can be recommended to CO to be promoted to LTAs a few months earlier. The rest of the 2LTs will still stay the same as present system: to be promoted to LTA only nearing their ORD.

    --------------------------- END ---------------------------

    Of course, even if the official documentation is published, it is still up to the unit to implement the changes. Eventually, it is the S1 br personnel who are doing the paperwork.
