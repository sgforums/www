    Originally posted by KungPaoChicken:

    Ok I think OETI means like engineering and technical Institute.

    How to like work myself, like perform accordingly to get inside there?


    It stands for Ordnance Engineering Training Institute.

    Honestly speaking,no one knows how they allocate the vocations.It all seems very randomized.

    From what I have seen,quite a number of my friends who did diploma in electronics/mechatronics ending up as technician during their NS.I guess their diploma might have influenced their posting but there is no gurantee that every person who takes the same course will end up doing the same job.

    During your BMT,you can voice out to your PC during interviews that you would like to be a technician after you POP...they will take note but they won't be able to do much about it.

    As for part time studies,if you are allowed to stay out and have the time,you can go for it.BUT,you must be mentally prepared that you might not be able to attend all the lessons or you might end up being late for some.This is especially true during periods where there are inspections/audit/excercises.If you need extra time off to study,you have to ask your OC(Boss) for permission.If they say no,then there is nothing you can do about it.Do note that the army will not sponsor your course,it will be entirely your own initiative to do part time studies.

    One word of advice as a NSF,please DO NOT miss out on a recall manning should there be one and use studies as an excuse.That will be asking for trouble as I have seen in serveral cases.If you do not know what a recall manning is,don't worry,you will be familiar with it once you are serving.

    Wish you luck in getting the vocation you want!

    PS:Only sure way of becoming a technician is if you sign on to be one!Lol!

    Originally posted by KungPaoChicken:

    Ook nice. Ya I paying for the ITE education myself. When is POP? After BMT at Tekong ar?


    Yes,POP is after your 3 month BMT if you go for the normal one.If you go for the obese bmt,it will be longer.If you go for PES C bmt(modified),it will be shorter.

    Please take note that you might not end up doing BMT in tekong.This happens if you kanna mono intakes which means you enlist directly into the unit to be a certain vocation.For example,if you directly enlist into a SIR,you will do BMT in their own camp and after you POP,become a rifleman for the 2 years in the same camp.Mono intake also includes Commando and Naval Diver.
