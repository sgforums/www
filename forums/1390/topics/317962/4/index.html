    Here's a wikipedia link on what a unimog is:

    The unimog that signals use is roughly the same but with the interior fitted out to accomodate a whole bunch of radios depending on the type of det you belong to.

     

    Only IC1 operators will get to learn driving,the specs don't do the driving.Specs are expected to be the veh com.So most IC1 operators that you meet will have a military driving license.Unfortunately, not many will clock the necessary mileage to convert it to a civilian one. Only signallers drive the unimogs due to the nature of equipment it carries.Only time the tpthub drivers ever drive the unimog is after all the equipment has been taken out and need to send the vehicle for maintenance.

     

    As for your question regarding IC2,it really depends on the type of unit you are posted to and at what level.In general,a division or brigade level signaller should have an easier life than a battalion level signaller.
