    Got your msg. Yes I do have other music, what would you be interested in?

    The inspection music this year is entitled Ceremonial Spirit, specially composed for NDP 2006 by the Sergeant Major of the SAF Central Band 2WO Ong Wee Hong. The drive-past is Fanfare and Salute by MAJ (NS) Tonni Wei, and this tune has been used the last couple of years.

    Tentera Singapura was played 2 times through and the SPF march played once was to represent the makeup of the GOH contingents.

    This are the titles of the music played-
    Contingents march in - All The Way
    GOH March in - Guard of Honour March
    Colour Party form up - Marching with Pride (Tri-service march)
    GOH Salute - Point of War
    PM's Salute - Salute No.2
    President's Arrival - Presidential Fanfare
    President's Salute - Majulah Singapura
    Inspection Tune - Ceremonial Spirit
    Drive-past - Fanfare and Salute
    GOH form up for march past - Marching with Pride
    March Past - Tentera Singapura and SPF March
    2006 Formation forge forward - Trust Me Singapore
