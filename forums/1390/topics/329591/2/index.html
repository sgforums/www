    An update on my initial charge that was issued to me.

    After numerous calls to ns.sg and "confirmation" from my ns unit, i was informed that i need not attend the charge for failure to attend RT.

    However, 4-5 days before the actual charge date (which was supposed to be cancelled), i had a "feeling" that something was not right and i better call them to double confirm. True enough, when i called in to ns hotline, the lady said that the charge was still on! I said this was not possible and told her the whole story. after a check, she found out that my unit DID NOT inform cmpb/nssc(office in charge of charging). 

    At this point in time, i wanted to scream at her. She put up an immediate request to my unit to check again and promised they will contact me the next day.

    The next day, no one called me.

    The following day was saturday and this time i was really pissed off. Called in and the person said he couldnt do anything as it was saturday and asked me to call back on monday morning 8.30. i told him the "cancelled charge" is on monday 11am. He said no choice.

    Finally on monday morning, with my uniform and boots ready "in case" i have to go down after all, i called in and after checking here and there, the officer confirmed that i no need to attend the charge. 

    I asked if i will receive any official letter, she said no. I asked how will i know if this time you guys got it correct and i wont receive another surprise? She said the conversation is recorded and i can always bring this up.

    Moral of the story - it's not that i want to say ns is really cock up. But this scenario really prove to me that if you want anything done right, got to do it yourself.

    So let this be a warning for anyone who thinks they are excused from IPPT/RT. ALWAYS check!
