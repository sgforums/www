    Flat foot is a very common condition,the deciding factor will be how severe it is.Most of my friends with flat foot ended up with PES B.Get your doctor to write a letter for you to give the MO if you intend to use flat foot as the reason to file for PES C.

     

    For the mental disorder part,even if you could get a referal from the MO to see a psychologist from the hospital,you will most probably end up having to pay for it yourself because you still have not officially enlisted yet and not under CSC(Civil Service Card)/11B.

     

    The good thing is that SAF also has its own MOs dealing with mental disorders so you might end up getting refered to a SAF MO instead and it's most probably free....that's if you want.Some people die die want outside doctor.
