    hmm..usually the instructors do the teaching of the skills..not management..More like they just give instructions, and let the cadet appointment holders carry out the duties...
    For teaching more in depth stuff, it usually goes to the WOs like the Weapons Team who conduct the lessons..For outfield, their will be an appointed instructor to be the exercise PC to teach the cadets the ropes..The Cadet Exercise PC will not do much, but shadow the instructor..

    The WSM does little, just goes around screwing people, doing the background logistics..Depending on wing, the AWSM may do more stuff than the WSM actually..

    The CQ is there to ensure that the stores are there, proper handing and taking over of stores, rations are indented for outfield, collect rations..Wing Comd seldom appears, but gives lessons occasionally..You dun really wan to see the Wing Comds a lot
