    My hunch is the unit will release you only on the test day. Particularly since since you're saying it's due on the last day of ICT anyway, i.e. outprocessing time (could be a half-day or early release). Your SI cell can easily make arrangements to discharge 1-2 days earlier, provided you've cleared all the IPPT & stuff. I myself has experienced this before, although it was to facilitate NUS-NTU reservists returning for the first day of semester term.

    You can try applying for deferment if the certification is directly work-related. If it's not, i.e. just a course you're taking on your own, you may have to resign to reporting for ICT (even if it means taking your textbooks out into the field for self-studying). *shrug* Suck thumb, loh. But it's really up to the CO, who must have persuasive evidence to dispense with you.
