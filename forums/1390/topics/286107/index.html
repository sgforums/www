    Hi

    During my time (ord in 96), we had to make our own. The material used is issued by SAF but was insufficient (one large sheet). Think some of the guys did get more from beach road.

    What i did was to blend with different materials e.g. using cloth from my camo scraf and camo net for tonners and rovers. The later are the best, material is sturdy and looks damn good Had to puck them from the tonners though *shhhh shhhh *

    Btw, we had to make camo jacket for the rifle and spotter bino/scope too ... that was a bitch!
