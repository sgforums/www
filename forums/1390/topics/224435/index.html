    The SAF.eMart is a revolutionary kit replacement system that allows servicemen to purchase their personal equipment and expendable items with their allocated credits via e-ordering with delivery services or walk-in purchase at any SAF.eMart outlet.

    Please log in to check opening hours at physical eMart outlets.

    __________
    Credit policy

    Standard Credit Allocation

    Army

    Active Combat Vocations - 260 Credit Points every year*
    Active Service/Technical Vocations - 170 Credit Points every year*
    Active PES E - 120 Credit Points every year#
    Active NSmen - 113 Credit Points every 2 years

    * With effect from 1 Sep 05 and subject to next top up date.
    # With effect from 1 Mar 06 and subject to next top up date.

    Air Force

    In-Service Combat - 226 Credit Points every year
    In-Service Non-Combat - 169 Credit Points every year
    Active NSmen - 113 Credit Points every 2 years

    Navy

    In-Service Combat - 210 Credit Points every year
    In-Service Non-Combat - 185 Credit Points every year
    Active NSmen - 105 Credit Points every 2 years

    The credit amount stated above is based on a standard credit allocation policy and there may be a slight difference in credit amount for different services.

    Roll Over Policy for Credits Allocated

    Unused credits will be carried forward for 1 year and, 2 years for In- servicemen and active NSmen respectively.

    Withdrawal Of Credits

    Servicemen will not be entitled credits under the following circumstances, and any unused credits will be withdrawal immediately after their service status is being updated in the manpower system.

    a. Posting to holding unit/inactive unit
    b. Posting to MINDEF Reserve.
    c. Completion of NSF/NSmen liability.
    d. Retirement or discharge from service.
    e. Change from uniformed service to non-uniformed service.
