    about your medical problems i do not know.

    shift hours can be 24 hours or 5pm-9am, 7-7, 8-5 depends on where you're posted too.

    you can be posted to places like tower, 203 sqn, bcp, hq etc..

    during the AOSS course the PT is only every wed morning 5bx which is self conducted and games hour from 3-5 also on the same day.. most of your physical training is actually done by playing sports during the nights which you stay in.

    weekends and public holidays depends.. if you're manning radar or dealing with air defence definitely you won't be on a mon-fri job scope. in fact every place an AOSS goes to seems to be on shift except for those instructors at Air Force School i think.
