    I wonder if you even read the thread. And I wonder what you are basing your claim on.

    Just for one last time,

    Hormat Panglima is given to CDF, service chiefs, and generals. Basically, all general grade officers. The band-in-attendance strikes the tune Salute No. 4, 5, or 6 respectively.

    Hormat Menteri is given to ministers. The band-in-attendance strikes the tune Salute No. 3.

    Hormat Perdana Menteri is given to the Prime Minister. The band-in-attendance strikes the tune Salute No. 2

    Hormat President to the president. The band-in-attendance strikes the National Anthem, or if on second execution after an inspection, Salute No. 1.
