    The SAF.eMart is a kit replacement system that allows servicemen to purchase their personal equipment and expendable items with their allocated credits via e-ordering with delivery services or walk-in purchase at any SAF.eMart outlet.

    The Army eMart outlet at Chevron will be opening from 27 Jun 07 onward. The address and opening hours are as follow :

    The Chevron
    48 Boon Lay Way Singapore 609961
    Tel: 68625700

    Tuesday to Friday: 11.30 am to 7.00 pm
    Saturday & Sunday: 11.30 am to 7.30 pm
    Monday: Closed

    From 1 Jul 07, there will be a 9 % discount for the eMart web-order.
