    After your Att C is over, look for your MO and let him check your finger. If it is still not fine, sound it out to him. He will likely give you Att B, so that you need not go through the SIT test and such. 

    In that case, you will likely have to redo everything with another company when you get better.

    Anyway, don't worry about shellscrape digging and GBC. They will not affect your chances of entering command school. A lot depends on your SIT test, and if you declare "10" for entering command school when the officer testing you ask you about how much you want to enter command school, you should be fine if you do alright for the SIT test.
