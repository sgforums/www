    haha my batch POPing in a few days. Actually u all enlist dont really need to bring alot of things. If u wanna bring the bare minimum, just need 6 clothes hangers, your underwear, soap and washing powder. Dont even need toiletries coz they provide, although u may want to bring in after a few weeks as you'll need to have one pack standby in your field pack most of the times. Bring lots of coins for the vending machine there too. You dont want to be stuck with just water for your 2 weeks there. Bring in some games like chess or reading materials (no porn) if u dont want to spend all your free time sleeping. If u kenna slack coy like me u can have up to 7 hrs free time a day excluding your mandatory 7 hr rest at night. And for smokers don't forget your sticks
