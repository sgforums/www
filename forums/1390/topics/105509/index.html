    Ok Guys, since many of us here are NS-men. Could we just summarise during our NSF day what is your unit standard field pack item? I believe every unit has different SOP. Well start from me 1st

    Pack 1 2 Sets of No.4

    Pack 2 1 set of PT-kit, 1 towel, 1 pair of socks, 2 pairs of undergarment & 1 sewing kit.

    Pack 3 Mess tin which inside include, soap box with soap, shaver, toothbrush & toothpaste, lighter, matches, C size battery, SAF powder & candle.

    Pack 4 Smaller mess tin which contain boot lace, kiwi & brush.

    Pack 5 Toilet Roll.

    Pack 6 1 pair of PT-shoes

    Pack 7 Lifejacket, Goggles & items use for rapelling.

    Miscellaneous items will be ET-stick & blade, ground sheet & combat rations.

    Well hopefully those guys who serve NS might share with us here

    there's a trick to packing all those items into that small field pack. make sure your ziplock bags are air tight. then roll up those uniform, admin shirt/pt shirt and towel into the respective ziplock bags, because rolling them up save space. sitting on them and squeezing out all the air helps too.
