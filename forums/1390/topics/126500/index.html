    You can still sign on JPSDS after completion of NS (NSF). After completion, you will go for your studies in Poly and after that, you will be a Regular in SAF. But you can't leave anytime. This is because you have a bond with SAF. If you want to leave anytime, you have to pay the liquidated damages to SAF before you are able to leave.

    If you are discharged due to poor performance, you still have to pay. If you are discharged due to service injury, etc, then u will leave without paying. Each case is case-by-case basis.
