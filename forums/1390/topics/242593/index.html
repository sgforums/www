    Originally posted by transcendence.:
    Hello.
    I am currently registered for NS. Posting date is April 10th. But while playing soccer, i injured my knee. How do i get exemption from army ?
    Sorry to hear about your injured knee.

    But unfortunately, this is what's likely to happen to you.

    You call CMPB to inform them of your injured knee, and they schedule another medical appointment for you. You go for it, and the MO classify you as PES D. This means you will not enlist for the moment. They will schedule another medical appointment for you again to determine the outcome of your injury.

    If you knee has healed, then you will be upgraded to PES A or B and will receive your enlistment letter again. If they decide to downgrade you to PES C, you will also receive your enlistment letter. However, you will be doing a non-combat BMT and subsequently non-combat posting.

    You will not get exempted from the army. I suggest you get a letter from your hospital or specialist clinic about your injury and call CMPB to inform them.

    To be exempted from NS means you need a PES F grading. PES F usually involves people who are mentally unsound, serious physical disability (one leg, one hand). I'm not a MO so i can't say for sure. But for your case, i am very sure you will not get exempted.
