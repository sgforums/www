    Welcome 8 Signals, Our Very Own System Managers
    from armynewsInteractive



    7 October saw the inauguration of 8th Signal Battalion, with MAJ Ng Chad-Son as its Commanding Officer. Chief Signal Officer, COL Lee Shiang Long, was the witnessing officer for this.

    8th Signal Battalion will play a key role in the Army's High Readiness Core, providing C4 support for a spectrum of forces. It manages the Army's Command and Control Information Systems (CCIS) and networks. By leveraging the latest commercial-off-the-shelf and military technologies and harnessing the IT skills of the new generation 3G soldiers, 8 Signal Battalion will fuse the power of technology with our Human Capital to accelerate the realisation of IKC2 capabilities in the 3G Army.
