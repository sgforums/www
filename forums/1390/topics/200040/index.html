    Mono intake is not bad. Mono intakes unit are the key to the success of the ARMY.

    You will enjoy the camaderie with your new found friends. Seriously speaking, gone were the days where the weak were bullied. Now everyone is trained to work hand in hand.

    I have seen how 1 SIR last mono trained. They enjoyed each other company so much that they work in cooperation and loyal to each other. They won the Best Infantry Unit for 04/05 Workyear.

    But of course, not all are the same. Maybe this batch you might find a few jokers. But overall, mono intake is not as it used to be like last time. You will bond with your superiors, friends and even the mess aunty.

    True training will be tough. But hey, this is not NCC or Boy Scouts. This is SAF.
