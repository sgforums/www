    430 SAF officers commissioned at SAFTI MI

    SINGAPORE: 430 Singapore Armed Forces officers were commissioned at the SAFTI Military Institute on Saturday evening, with Defence Minister Teo Chee Hean reviewing the commissioning parade.

    Among them, six from the Royal Brunei Armed Forces and one from the Royal Thai Army.

    The parade marked the completion of 38 weeks of rigorous training at the Officer Cadet School.

    The newly commissioned officers will assume combat, instructional or staff appointments in SAF units. - CNA/yy
