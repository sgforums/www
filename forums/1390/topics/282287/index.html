    If you try to read the directive carefully.... There are some conditions or regulations to be met:

    1. Your unit will try to match and give 5 day work same as regulars...

    2. Off-in-lieu can be given to NSFs who came back camp during weekends, but it is up to your commanders' considerations.

    3. NSFs fall under the Enlistment Act.... Not under the Employment Act... Only Employees which are employed by an organisation are entitled 5 -day work week... NSF is serving the their NS liability and on 24hrs standby...

    4. Regimental duties are regimental duties... Off-in-lieu is based on individual units... up to the commanders decision.

    5. You still must fight a war on a Sat n Sun... doesnt mean you only fight on Mon-Fri....
