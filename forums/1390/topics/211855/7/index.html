    Heads up from the P-032 batch(current).

    For future medics to-be, god bless you. Why? Currently the new course commander 2WO Tan Puay Ming decided to revamp the curriculum starting from P-033 batch onwards.

    Changes from current system(Tentative, from what I heard from my SGTs)
    -Running 2-8km on every even/odd days of the week
    -1 hour static training session per day
    -Eventual 24km Route March
    -Field camp
    -Drawing of rifles everyday during Combat Phase(Fall in 5AM)
    -Everyday night training(Till 9+++PM)
    -Outfield at Mandai when needed

    Hope this will help future medics-to-be prepare themselves mentally.
