    SAF pay you your salary 1st. December 10th pay you until December 31st.

    For example you enlist in December, you will get your pay of the (day you enlist in December until 31st) and (January up till 31st) by January 10th.

    So you are actually getting your December and January pay. Nothing to be happy about. You will not get salary one month before your ORD.
