    Runners in the SAF (Platoon, company, battalion (CO, 2i/c, etc) and upwards) are often so badly used.

    During active time, I saw a CO send his runner to go clean his bunk, cook food for him, etc. Even saw one runner sent up a tree to pluck rambutans!

    I would think the orignial idea of having a runner is to have someone relay messages when other lines of communications have broken down. Not have someone play "maid".

    I always kidded my friend that he should have told his commander that he'd send a fit sri lankan maid to take his place, and she probably be able to cook better meals than him.

    The sad thing is that often these runners are some of the better soldiers in the army and would make decent to good section 2i/cs.

    I think its time to change their designations to something else and give them some other real military responsibility than make coffee in the field.
