    Well if you are regular you will probably get what you request for.. But you will still need to take the psychomotor test and interview for tankee. You will need to pass it before you can be a tankee. Specs wise, if you never wear specs you will have a higher chance, but now very difficult to find people who never wear specs le. There are quite a number of tankee who wear specs too. But the main thing is the psychomotor test, if you cannot pass that, you will never have the chance to be a tankee at all. 

    When nearing your pro term, OCS side will invite people to go to ATI for the psychomotor test and interview. There are quite a number of people who fail the psychomotor though. Haha :)
