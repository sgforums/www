    I'm in a JC. Since most of my friends are enlisting in Jan, enlisting in April totally sucks. (can't find a better word to express myself). While you do get to find a job and gain experience, an early entry would mean finishing NS earlier, and by that time, you are more likely to know what you want and hence make better use of your time.

    People enlisting in April are either working or slacking.
    Slacking is totally detrimental to your brain and body. Working is probably the best way to pass time. And supposedly gain experience.

    While I cannot be absolutely certain, it does appear that people who finish early get to enter the Uni earlier...Which to me is definitely much more beneficial.
