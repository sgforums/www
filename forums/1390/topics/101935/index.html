    CCO = camp commandant office.

    It is a separate unit from the others in the camp. It's role is to provide logistical support to the units in camp or NRU visiting the camp.

    It exists to coordinate the pool of logistics so that foul ups dun happen.

    e.g.

    In tekong, both sispec and bmtc are to go thru CCO to book their book in/out ferry in a first come first serve basis. If BMTC controls the ferry timings, then naturally it would have priority over "good" timings. so on and so forth.

    Shared resources, like medical centre are under CCO's care cost accounting could be sorted out easily. Compared to Med Centre under BMTC then Sispec overuse it and deplete bmtc's resources.

    etcetc.
