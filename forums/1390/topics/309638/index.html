  • blitzonic
    8 Mar 08, 14:46
    Bring all your military gear, PT kit (for IPPT & admin wear), toiletries & a cupboard lock first time around. Plus some money for the canteen or emart; you'll likely have to contribute a few dollars to the BN fund too (to buy the styrofoam boxes & cordial drinks for outfield meals). See whether you've reported with the main body or an advanced party when you turn up; the difference being the CQs may not have taken over the bunks yet. Tompang your barang in your mate's car boot if things are not ready. If can book out the first night, then replenish whatever you forget to bring along. Or else buy from the emart.

    Originally posted by hao_hao:


    military gear includes all the standard field pack items tat we pack in BMT ?

     

     

    All the items, yes, but no need to be that strict anymore unless you're doing standby. (Yes, the latter can happen during ICT.) Reservist, what; just use common sense, lah. If you've lost something, try borrowing from your sibling or relative or friend or work colleague first.

    Each unit has its discipline standard, so put aside neatly at home the stuff you decide not to bring - in case you're allowed to book out on the 1st night (& replenish). Or else buy from the emart.

    BTW Your CQ will issue 'perishables' like toilet rolls, talcum powder, kiwi polish, insect repellent, unless the SAF has changed the system. Pens, chinagraphs & notebooks too, if you nag hard enough. Ditto groundsheets, ponchos & shoebrushes, though I understand these are not standard for every unit.
