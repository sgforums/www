    PDS - Precision Drill Unit, what you see on National Day with those rifle drills..

    SIB - Special Investigation Branch, something like civilian's CID, investigate any offences that occurs in the SAF..

    Dog Unit - Train dogs and MPs to perform attack, tracking, contraband search etc..

    ARP - Anti Riot Police, called when there's a riot which involves the SAF..

    ZPU - Zonal Provost Unit, not very sure what it does..

    DB - Detention Barracks, MPs posted there to jaga the inmates, aka Brazil team becoz of their yellow shirts and blue shorts..

    Motorcade Unit - Rides the motorbikes and book MID vehicles that breaks traffic rules.. Also provide motorcade escort to the President etc..

    GOH - Guard of Honour.. They train to perform GOH duties when the president or whoever host foreign visitors or head of states.. Also take turns to stand outside Istana..

    Plus, some are posted out to other units to jaga the security etc..

    All these from the best of my knowledge larh.. the terms or scope might be wrong..
