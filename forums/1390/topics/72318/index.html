    To my knowledge (for further enquiries, consult NathanG5)

    RP is normally at the guardroom, checking passes for illegal entry and exit ala security guards.

    MP meanwhile do further routine checks around the island to camps or uniformed personnel if they violate the SAF codes of conduct.

    RPs wear RP armband in smart 4 uniform. MPs wear their own set of old skool uniform
