    I had mine taken out during NS. 5 of them over 2 sessions (including one in-the-way pre-molar) plus tooth filling.

    - call your dental centre and ask to go down for teeth checkup + referral to wisdom teeth removal. dont be late for the dental centre appointment!

    - dental centre refers you to hospital or MMI, they will book appointment for you
    (but be prepared for last-minute changes when the dental officers are deployed due to ops requirements.)

    do at least inform your superiors out of courtesy that you are going for wisdom teeth removal, don't pull stunt. i had to change my appointment to a few months before my ORD after i have more-or-less trained my understudy a bit then can do take out teeth.
