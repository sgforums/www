    Hi BryanO,

    Aerobic Exercises are exercises where your body consume oxygen while you are exercising. They are low to mid intensity exercises aimed at building up your endurance, and includes swimming and slow jog.

    Anaerobic Exercises are exercises where your body do not consume oxygen while you are exercising. They are high intensity exercises aimed at building up your speed, and includes interval sprinting.

    I will suggest that you do not do both types of training on the same day, and alternate between the two of them on alternate days.

    Cheers.
