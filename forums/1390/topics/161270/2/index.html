    OFFICIAL policy on SAF items after end of reservist cycle....

    SAF items are official never "given" to service personnel but on long term loan.

    A serviceman are to keep their items till their liability for call-ups are finished.

    However, most items issue to them will be at their end of service life and thus can be disposed off properly.

    If the serviceman belives that the items can still be refurbished to be re-issued to other users, they may return the items to ALB.

    However, care must be given in disposing their helmet as this is a controlled item. meaning, if you think it is still usable, don't anyhow throw that it reaches on another person hand because possession of anti-ballistic equipment is in contravention of singapore police directives.
