    The GOM i read GOM-MP... about medical leave... was very vague and ambiguous on whether they can be called back.

    k, i read it somewhere in Jan 2005 when i was tasked to "research" on the kind of actions my OC can take... so i kinda forgotten how it is exactly phrased.

    Basically, it says like, [serviceman who has medical certs issued by private doctors may remain at home and rest until the MC ends.

    However, serviceman may be required to endorse the MC by MOs.]

    The ambiguity comes from.... "until the MC ends", but in practice servicemen have been asked to book in the night of their last day of MC.

    I think lately this practice has been changed to requiring them to book in next morning.

    "may remain at home and rest", it does not specify if serviceman must STRICTLY stay home and rest with provisions for meals.

    "may be required to endorse"... it never state if the MC has to be endorsed on the day the serviceman returns after his MC ends, or he can be called back to first endorse the MC and then use his MC.
