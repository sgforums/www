    @TS You can remove your wisdom tooth at zero cost. However, you will have to pay for your medicine. Eg. antibiotics and painkillers.

    The proper channel should be going to the medical centre and request for a tooth check. They will then refer you to a army dental centre. From there, you will be referred again to a hospital to get your wisdom tooth removed.
