    True, it is not fair. But suck thumb, this is SAF.

    But let's say you are the guy in charge of allocating duties. When your friends ask you for a better timeslot, of course you will try to give preference to them. This is how 'pulling strings' work. Trust me, it is not easy to refuse them. Even with a points system to allocate, there will still be some people doing extra duties. Afterall, I cannot ask a man to do half or one quarter or a duty. And there is no perfect system. People will always argue for a change in the system but I usually dismiss them because that system is simply advantageous only to them and not to everyone.

    When we were still under training, I was the Guard Duty I/C. It was a terrible job. It is not easy to be nice nor is it possible to accomodate everybody's last minute requests. Obviously I was not well liked. But I always tell people, 'You can scold me and my family, but you still need to do your duty...'

    Now, I am a spec who plans duty.
