    fighter boy or not, its largely based on your flying ability and also your character and personality. fighter boys tend to be type A personality, but then, there are a small handful i have seen otherwise.

    never heard that myopic degree plays a part in the aircraft type you fly. pilot trainees are all send for PRK surgery, which works the same as LASIK.

    it does not matter whether they post you commandos or tekong BMT. if you are interested to be a pilot, i would recommend you to just apply directly to RSAF as a pilot.

    if you clear the COMPASS test, interview and medical, they will automatically send you for air grading and the rest will be history.

    pursue your interest first, don't let the talk about how screwed up SAF can get so on and so fro. but do bear in mind its a 12 year bond.

    that's all.

    yup its pilot or nothing.

    If you don't make it, you go back to CDO.

    If you make it...they send you air-grading, if air-grading dun make it, they either send you back to CDO, or they send you to SISPEC and become Infantry/Guards Specialist.

    In rare case...they may ask you for air-grading again after sending you back to SISPEC. I had a friend who got that....
