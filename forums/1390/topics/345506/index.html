    The amount of subsidy received by Civil Servants & NSFs is capped at $10 per visit if they visit a private clinic, (e.g GP, neighbourhood family doctor). This subsidy is further capped at $350 per year for NSFs under the new Civil Service medical benefits scheme, the Medisave-cum-Subsidised Outpatient (MSO) Scheme.

    All NSFs enlisted on or after 1 Jan 1994 are placed on this new Scheme. 

    The same principle of co-payment and caps applies to dental subsidies at private dental clinics. NSFs can be reimbursed 50% of their dental expenses, up to a cap of $70 per calendar year.

    Since you are in Tekong BMT now, just submit the receipt* to your coy's office for the coy clerk to help you to claim on the Mindef Intranet's Personal Claims System (PCS)

    *within 1 month from the date of the receipt
