    The most garang story about MWO Raymond which won my respect.

    There was a batch of BMTC 3SGs attached to a mono-battalion.

    Because of the stupidity of some of the 3SG, ALL the 3SGs were punished and told to kiwi tonner tyres and sweep parade square (this obviously the men got to see it).

    Then polishing tonner tyre seemed to be that battalion's favourite punishment regardless the wrongdoing.

    The 3SGs called MWO Raymond. MWO Raymond first decided not to listen thinking that 3SGs were just being whiny. But after few calls, he realised that it is uncoordinated complaints and felt that unit's RSM gone abit too far and unchecked.

    He told the 3SGs a date, on which all the attached personnels are to pack thier stuff and duffel bag and book out without knowledge of that battalion.

    So the battalion woke up without any 3SGs in camp.

    MWO Raymond then called that RSM and tell him the importance of specs and that they should be accorded respect especially when men below them look up to them.

    After the talk, the 3SGs booked in 30 mins later. Apparently the instructino was to just wait nearby.

    Those 3SGs never got harrassed since.
