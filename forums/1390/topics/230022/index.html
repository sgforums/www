    NSF - full-time National Service
    NSman - after you ORD before you ROD. NSF enlist as boys, so they are deemed man when they ORD.
    Regulars are also full-time servicemen. Don't know at which stage they become 'man'.

    NSF and Regulars use just rank and name : eg 1SG Tan

    NSmen can use their rank if they wish to, and usually qualify themselves that they are not regulars by inserting (NS) after their rank. eg BG(NS) Tan
