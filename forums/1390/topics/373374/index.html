    I assume that you have interest in signing on as a pilot?NO,being in a PTP batch will not affect your chances of signing on.I was in a PTP batch and a few of my coy mates ended up as pilots too.

    To sign on as a pilot,you need to be eligible for OCS after your BMT,so the only things you should worry about are those factors which can affect your chances of going OCS(Eg.IPPT,SIT test,Peer review....etc).Of course,you need to pass whatever aptitude/medical tests that the air force ask you to go for too.

    If you do not qualify for OCS,then they won't offer you a pilot contract.
