    Sign extra/Confinement: Can't book out on saturday but you can book out on Sunday morning but have to book in again in evening so quite stupid

    SOL: Cannot leave the place at all, have to sign a book every 2 hours(that tentatively means you can't have a proper sleep) and you have to be in Smart 4

    You can also kena guard duties...etc

    DB affects your chances to apply for government jobs like MOE,MOH,SINGTEL...etc those government related jobs

    You can get charged for malingering which means you fake your sickness.

    The first time they charge you for DB can't be more than 5 days unless you really do something stupid like shoot someone in the leg...etc

    Subsequent offences will extend your db time.

    ORD date will affected though, for how many days you stay in DB,your ORD will extend.

    If you do get charged and wanna "lessen" the charge, go talk to your high ups, talk things out. Go higher up the chain, you may be required to write a statement of what happened...etc and they shall decide.

    Try not to lie but if you want to lie make sure they don't have evidence or you got support.

    Ask your parents if you need.
