    Personally, I have absolutely no interest in fighting a war. 

    However, i've given it enough thought to realise that should Singapore go to war, the option of 'running away', which most of us take for granted, barely exists.

    let's hypothesize.

    option 1: FLYING OUT

    For the ignorant, airports are key strategic installations that are usually very very high up on the target list for obvious reasons (go figure). Should a war break out, its very likely that changi airport will be one of the first places to be obliterated by enemy airstrikes or shellfire. Even if the airport is miraculously spared complete destruction, foreign airlines would be the first to fly the hell away, evacuating their own citizens, NOT desperate Singaporeans. Local airlines would clearly be grounded because SIA = government, and the government is never going to let AWOL SAF personnel fly off on its krisflyer+inflight entertainment+singapore girl aircraft. Those planes which are not grounded will be flying politicians, diplomats and the ultra rich out of the area. So unless you're a cabinet minister, or you have $100million to throw at the pilot, you wont be going anywhere. Even if, by some amazing stroke of luck, you manage to build an airplane out of cardboard and take off from the top of your HDB block, I'm quite sure your plane will be shot down because there's no way out of Singapore without crossing Malaysian or Indonesian airspace. And everyone knows that should Singapore go to war, it would probably be with those two countries.

    That said, , I dont think anyone will be flying out of the country except the occasional hero who plans on infiltrating paya lebar or tengah airbase, hijacking a fighter jet, and learning to fly it instantly.

    option 2: SAILING OUT

    In wartime, the seas will probably be blockaded  by the navy to prevent any amphibious invasion of the island. Civillian vessels will probably be used by the navy for logistical purposes eg. transport or hospital ships. Ship owners and crews would be the first to sail away if they hear of a war. And I really doubt the ability of an average person to swim to australia, india or thailand. Perhaps some might be innovative, and decide to row a bathtub. But I dont think the bathtub will be getting very far in the indian ocean or south china sea.

    option 3: DRIVING/WALKING OUT

    Most feasible option. Just walk/drive straight into the heart of enemy territory. Self explanatory.
