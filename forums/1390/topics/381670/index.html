    Wrong.

    In theory and written in GOM, you are not required to take leave simply for going overseas.

    But alot of Chief Clerk/S1 want a unit's personnel to take leave even if they are going overseas for a day "in case something happens"

    Anyway, if your unit uses computer to administer the leave system, you can't be deducted for leave on a sunday anyway so long you do not exceed 30 days in a row.

    But regardless, you should inform your PC/OC that you are going malaysia for the afternoon.
