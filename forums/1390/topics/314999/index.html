    During my NSF,I too have mob manning periods.

     

    For NSFs,it is roughly the same too.You see your unit code word on TV or get phone call,you report back to your same camp.

     

    Don't forget to acknowledge the phone message after you have listened to the message.Some people just switch off after listening to the starting.Of course,as NSF you will not be allowed to take overseas leave during mob manning period

     

    You have to bring your FBO too plus 11B,no different from NSmen.Depending on your vocation,you might end up having to do extra things like setting aside combat rations for distribution or set up ammo point if you are in the QM dept.

     

    Even more convenient if you are NSF since you will be in camp for most of the week but they usually call up on the weekends.
