    U can try a mix of Activities .

    How to tell what u need ?

    If your legs feel like rubber but your not out of breathe --> need more endurance

    If your legs feel ok but out of breathe ie chest explosion --> aerobic problem

    Some weight training is also beneficial , try some leg press , squats , hamstring curl and some upper body exercies to even out the muscles to prevent injuries and strenthen them

    I don't know how your 2.4 route is but for my camp is 3 laps of 800m. I train by targeting 3:10min/lap rest for 2min and run again aiming for the same timing. You need to train your heart to get use to the fast pace so your heart is able to pump faster in future running. And controlling your breathing plays an important role. Best is breath in and out from your nose for the first 1.5km or so and change to breathing in by mouth and out by nose and final 400, 500m use your mouth to breath in so can take in more air for final burst.
