    No, it is not too late to join at age 26.

    You will be assigned as how SAF needs you. May be back to BTMC as officer again, or may be in Gombak tomolo. 

    You dont really get to choose the area you are in as you are in the "training" line.

    (I dont like SAF officer/regular any way, especially BTMC officer)

    BMTC officer are considered low grade (You probably get B/C during your OCS to be posted to BMTC. You dont get to specialised training in other vocation, so you have to undergo the shit (ex: GCC) again if you get to be posted to guards. Support line (ME) you are out as you dont have an engineering diploma/degree. Promotion is also very hard because of the lack of cert.

    I suggest you put up the application and see if SAF still wants you. Dont wait.
