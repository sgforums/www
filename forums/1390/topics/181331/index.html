    Originally posted by yuko-ogura:

    i plan to retake my A lvls and study for it after the 2 mth BMT...
    Simple answer: No.

    BMT is the slackest period of training that you will go through.
    BMT stands for BASIC Military Training. Just knowing the Basics is not enough. If you are healthy (PES A or B), you will be sent for more courses after your BMT. Some of these courses will be tougher than BMT and may last longer than BMT. Sometimes, you may be sent for 2 courses back-to-back. Even if and when you get posted to a unit, you will not be slacking for some time.

    To be safe, don't plan anything until after you finish BMT.
