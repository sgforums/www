    SIB = Singapore Infantry Brigade; i.e. brigade HQ.

    Many staff functions to support brigade commander to run the operations of brigade. Brigade commander usually LTC/COL. Head of each function usually MAJ. Junior officers usually hold assistant posts.

    2LT who just got commissioned fresh out of OCS -- my guess is he will get to do plenty of dirty jobs.

    Many brigade exercises -- you won't be seeing him much.
