    Mono: Begin army life in Unit, make friends in your unit, stay with your friends throughout your army life (assuming there is no hip-cup) and end your army life with your Unit (Both ORD and ROD)

    BMTC: Begin army life on an island, make friends in BMTC, then POP, go to Unit, begin next phase of army life in Unit, make new friends in the Unit, when ORD liao can suay-suay kena post to another to a new reservist unit. Then......begin reservist in a new reservist unit , then make new friends again , etc, etc.....
