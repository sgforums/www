  • Fortiscause
    9 Feb 06, 09:02
    I agree with LazerLordz on the part where you need to get along with the S1 Branch Clerks. Among all the staff branches, they are the most useful to you as they can advise you on your welfare benfits, leave, discipline matters (pertaining to Summary Trial) and ORD Matters. Try not to find the Chief Clerk as she is usually swamp by work. If you want to talk to someone with a bit of weight one leh, try the Asst Chief Clerk (Usually a NSF holding the rank of 3SG) or the DYS1/AS1.

    Thumb of the rule, NO ONE IN THE UNIT CAN EXTEND YOUR ORD WITHOUR YOUR AUTHORISATION.

    It is a criminal offence to hold anyone NRIC, hence the unit is not allow to hold your NRIC and not return you upon ORD. However saying this, we do need you to complete your Medical and Dental FFI and the Unit clearance form to ensure that you do not suffer from any long term injury besides those prior ur enlistment and that all stores had been returned or paid for. IT IS NOT A MUST FOR YOU TO PASS YOUR IPPT/SOC TO ORD, but it is for your benfit if you can attain a GOLD for IPPT...extra $200 leh.

    BTW, go through the chain of command if you want to complain ya. At least you won't get further blacklisted.
