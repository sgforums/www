  • fudgester
    6 Feb 05, 11:18
    The way I see it......... extra != confinement.....

    I see extra as extra weekend duties..... You have to do the duty instead of someone else. So while you're being punished, in a way, you're also doing something good for someone else. Someone else gets to go out with his girlfriend and enjoy the weekend instead of you. Not much of a consolation for you, but you can take heart in knowing that someone else benefits.

    Confinement, on the other hand, affects only yourself. Someone still has to the the weekend guard duty.

    Signing extras for pretty trivial reasons are pretty common in the SAF. Heck, I once got two extras for not polishing my boots during stand-by bed. I was so concerned about cleaning my bunk that I forgot all about my boots.

    My advice to you..... Just take it like a man and accept it.
