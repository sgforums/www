    i dun see any part that ask u claim from employer. Claim from employer is same as asking them to pay you salary. actually for those 1/2 day or 1 day event, it all depend on your company on whether they still willing to pay you your normal salary and let you go to camp. if they willing to pay, then no need to submit make up pay.

    usually mob briefing is only 1/2 days on weekday.. if you are issue SAF100 , then u can claim make up pay before the briefing... for mobilization, no SAF100 will be issued and usually it will happen on sat or sun (so if you are  not working on weekend, then no make up pay claims).

    you fill in part 1 of that form. your company fill up part 3 (if u have detail, u can fill in also). and your employer must certified what is filled in part 3 is correct... then faxed it over. that it.
