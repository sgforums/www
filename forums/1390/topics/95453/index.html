    For info, you can either take Bus 177 from Bukit Bakot MRT station (NOT Bukit Gombak MRT!!!). The stand for 177 is IN the bus interchange itself but only runs in the mornings from 7am - 9am and in the afternoons from 4pm - 6pm. Check the timings again pls, it may have changed. Bus 177 goes all the way up the hill and you only have another 5min walk up to the main block.

    Alternatively, buses run along bukit timah road but that means you have to stop on the main road and trek up the hill. The little trek will take you anywhere from 10-15mins, its not as hard as it sounds. In the mornings there is a duty driver who takes his little Minivan and operates a pickup service. Dunno if this is still the case. Oh yar, the buses (these are the few that i can remember becos there are TONS)

    176, 178 (this can be taken from the main road outside Bt Bakot MRT) 171, 950, 951, 170, 66, 67, 190, c***b** too many to remember lah. Hop onto the SBS/TIBS website and find out.
