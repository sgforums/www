    A soldier stationed at CFB Petawawa, 160 kilometres west of Ottawa, has died while taking part in a Terry Fox Run on the base.

    Public affairs officials at the military base said Chief Warrant Officer Joel Sorbie collapsed during the run on Thursday.

    He was rushed to hospital in nearby Pembroke, Ont., where he was pronounced dead.

    An investigation into the cause of death is underway.
