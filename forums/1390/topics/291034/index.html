    For Recruits/Enlistees:

    There are couple of items which are allowed in the SAF as a form of personal recreation during free time in Bunk or for other purposes.

    Entertainment

    1- Magazines (R21/ Uncensored Pictures; Pornographic Materials Aren't Allowed; Maxim, NewMan, FHM are allowed Magazines)
    2- Story Books; Novels; Comic
    3- Games: Chess, Uno, Bingo, Reversi etc. (Poker Cards Not Allowed)
    4- PSP*
    5- DS*
    6- MP3/4*
    7- None Camera Phones (Common Knowledge)
    8- Loud Speakers (That is if you willing to share music with the whole Bunk)

    Toiletries

    9- Additional own towel (it could be just for cleaning of face in the morning; you can choose to buy the SAF green colour $2.50 in E-mart or bring along your own)
    10- Facial Wash
    11- Powder (Sometimes, there's no time for you to shower thus there's a need for powder bath)
    12- Shaving Gel/Cream
    13- Handy Plasters
    14- Ankle/Knee/Elbow Guards for people with previous history of injuries.
    15- Shower Form/Gel (which can easily wash off Camo-Cream)

    Rainbath shower gel washes off the camo cream quite easily followed by Dettol.

    Facial Scrub might help only to a little, since I've tried it myself, Biore isn't that good for washing off Camo-cream

    Food

    15- Three/Four In One Drinks (Must be inside sachets)
    16- Energy Drink Powder
    17- Protein Powder
    18- Food-Supplements
    19- Sweets

    Others:

    20- Cigarettes (Max: 2 packet per person)

    For Food, it varies depending in individual company;
    If there's history of Rats in that level of your company, than it's advised not to bring food in; You wouldn't want to see your items all in a mess.

    * - Must not have removable memory card inside

    Note: Expensive Items might be easily lost inside SAF since there are people who can't afford to buy such items* thus if anybody want to bring it inside, exercise pre-caution and don't anyhow misplace it.
