    hi... let me enlighten you

    once enlist 10 weeks BMT --> 2 weeks cross training(learn weapons, navigation skill, etc) --> 3 months Vocational training (depends what you kena can be signal, medics,weapon, demo/boat man, leaders 'sergeant') --> 3 months DOT/COT detachment ops training /company ops training --> RED BERET

    **sometime between 3rd to 6th month of ur life in NS you will be going for ur airborne course... trust me.. its fun!! defining moment

    as for wat comamndos do.. i leave it for you to come and experience yourself..
