    Hah, i went through that course before. To answer your original question, yes but you have to put in a request to the chief clerk/dy or your respective lao bans.

    You will go through the 1 week Admin Sup course at Khatib camp. Pre-requisite is that you must have attended the 1 day asa course. After that it will be roughly 7 months of OJT before you get promoted to 3SG. I heard it is alot shorter now though *shrugs*

    I am not sure about your reason of wanting to go be an admin spec though. If you are working in a S1/G1 type of branch, you are the first one to get arrowed for anything/everything. You will get to be some sort of ass-sistant chief clerk, but without any power. So essentially, compared to other ASAs, you will be doing the most work. I was lucky to get my pes status upgraded a few months after the course.

    Edit : Sorry, i just read. You are an E9L9 number 3 guy ? In that case, no, i am afraid. I have never heard of any Pes E personnels attending the various admin courses in khatib camp.
