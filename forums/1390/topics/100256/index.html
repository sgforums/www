    ok, here's how the initial pilot selection test goes:

    1) go thru a detail medical checkup, every station consists of a nurse and a MO, finally being endorsed by the chief MO.

    2) CPSS - Computerised Selection Test + IQ Test (abt 5 hrs): Pretty similar to the amp. test for those poly and JC guys but much faster and practical on flight simulation and a very tricky IQ test.

    3) Panel Interview: Pass the above two tests and u will come to the panel interview during ur BMT. The Panel interview will consists of at least 3 persons - psych. + fighter pilot + rotary pilot, up to 7 persons will be interview u. The interview is pretty straight forward, go in, answer a few questions (be confident) and then come out and wait, if u pass, the clerk will immediately give u the proficiency contract to sign and go to the next room. Else, bye bye.

    Pilot training is the top piority in NS, so even if u got selected for SPF, u still can go for pilot training, and even if u fail, u will still be back to SPF provided u can make it back to the next available intake.
