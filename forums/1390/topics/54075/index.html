    Ha ha... sorry dude, in fact, it's not button. It a pair of string tie on the SBO wif 5 beads on top and 9 beads below, wif a knot in between. It is use during nevigation for pacing of distance.
    1) For every 100m, pull down 1 bead from the 9s.
    2) When the 9th bead is being pull, the next 100m pull down 1 bead from the top 5s. This mean that u have traveled 1 km already.
    3) Nexts, push all the 9 beads up again and repeat para 1 until the 9th beads then pull the second bead down fron the top 5s to indicate 2 km traveled. Then go on and on.....
