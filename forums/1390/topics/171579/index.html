    1. Mortar can be used to provide fire support for REALLY REALLY close ranges. P

    2. It is easier to request for support that is a coy or bn asset, than to get support that is from a division asset. Not every sect comd or PC that requests for 155mm fire support will get it. But, requesting for mortar fire is easier since its a lower level asset.

On the offensive, u can use mortar to keep certain strong points pinned down or ducking for cover. Or even cut off the enemy's escape route for really decisive engagements, while the tuber artillery can pound the objective itself.

    4. Mortar on soft skinned targets is more effective and cost efficient. Tube Arty on mechanised, armored targets.
