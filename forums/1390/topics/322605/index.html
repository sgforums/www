    Channel NewsAsia - Monday, June 30

    1st Commando Battalion wins SAF’s best unit award for the fifth time

    SINGAPORE: For the fifth year in a row the 1st Commando Battalion has clinched the Best Combat Unit award in the SAF Day Best Unit Competition held on Sunday.

    And also showing their might in the air five years in a row was the RSAF 149 Squadron which grabbed the Best Fighter Squadron.

    But it was a debut win for the Navy’s RSS Formidable, which won the Best Ship award.

    The Formidable—class frigates are built for multi—dimensional warfare in the air, surface, and sub—surface domains and play a key role in the operation of the 3rd Generation SAF.

    As for the seasoned winners, the RSAF 149 Squadron has participated actively in major multilateral exercises such as Exercise Bersama Shield and Bersama Lima under the Five Power Defence Arrangements (FPDA).

    They also took part in Exercise Cope Tiger with the US and Thailand air forces.

    As for the 1st Commando Battalion, it’s an unprecedented 22 times that it has clinched the Best Combat Unit award. — CNA/vm
