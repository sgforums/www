    23 june to 30 june = 7 days
    month of june = 30 days

    $350 / 30 days = $11.667

    $11.667 * 7 days = $81.669

    $81.669 (month of june) + $350 (month of july) = $431.669

    MINDEF give u allowance 1 month in advance.

    Why u get only $424 instead? probably due to some subscription mag / insurance / company stuffs (company T-shirt) n etc.

    For detail informations, click on the following link: http://content.miw.com.sg/Mindef/Static/PayMatters/nsf_index.asp
