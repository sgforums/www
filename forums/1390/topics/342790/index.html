    From my own personal experience:

    Chances are that your friend went to the non-subsidized(a.k.a Private) clinic of the hospital for his first consultation.That might have been considered as a private consultation and therefore not valid for full/any subsidy,especially if he did it without a referal letter from a camp mo or another doctor.

    Most CSC(civil service card) holders including NSFs will be refered to the subsidized clinic of the hospital if they intend to make use of the subsidy.

    Relevant Source: www.cgh.com.sg/patient/outpatientcharges.asp

    You will be classified as a subsidised patient if your first visit is via:

    • Referral letter from a Government Restructured Hospital under subsidised status, A&E, Polyclinic or SAF, without specifying a specialist by name
    • Discharge from inpatient class B2 or C without specifying a specialist by name

    The rule is still the same.
    After government subsidies, the remaining charges u can bill to Mindef with ur 11b.
