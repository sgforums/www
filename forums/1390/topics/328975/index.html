    Institute of Higher Learning Scheme (IHL)
    (For NSmen pursuing their university education locally after ORD)

    The IHL scheme is an initiative to help maintain the unit's integrity in NS. It allows those who are pursuing full-time studies in local Institutes of Higher Learning i.e. National University of Singapore (NUS) / Nanyang Technological University (NTU) / Singapore University of Management (SMU) and polytechnics, to remain in their NS unit. To make it easier for IHL NSmen to fulfil their NS obligations without disrupting their studies, MINDEF has a standing arrangement with the 3 local universities on a common "blackout" window for NS call-ups during the universities' semester vacation breaks. ICT call-up will be confined within these specified "blackout" periods and the 3 universities will ensure a total "blackout" of all academic programmes during this period.
