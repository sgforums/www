    TS.

         Upon completing my GCSE O levels in Singapore, I elected to begin further education in the United States.

        I understand that you will be granted a stay of service if you are admitted to a Degree-awarding university and pursing at least the qualification of a BA/BSc . For the stay to be effected, you have to begin your course prior to turning 17 1/2.

       As for the 75K issue, that sum does not buy you freedom - its a deposit / insurance to secure your return from overseas education. The sum varies depending on your family's wealth, but if I recall correctly, it STARTS at 75K. Financial assets including property, bank bonds or the like may be utilized in place of hard cold cash.

     Utilizing this option had served me well in NS... at least I was more educated than many of my peers, more outspoken and could "work within" the system better than my O-A-ITE peers who really suffered.
