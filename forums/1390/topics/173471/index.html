    We Live....

    I think it is one of those play safe, no -ending type of rethorical phrase. You know the kind that smart alecs or politicians say. Example, " I think you know what I mean..." Basically this type of phrase plays on the audience ego. You can say some "cheam" phrase, no one actually understands, but then no one dare to admit they don't know.

    So We Live... becomes a very "cheam" phrase. So much so that a young sergreant may never dare ask the WOs what it means ...

    It also allows the WOs , what managers call the "wriggle room". If there is a courageous sgt asking what the motto means, the WOs can add more words to the end and end up with different phrases to suit different scenarios ( very SAF !) ... ranging from, Example, WE LIVE TO LEAD ! ( very macho !) WE LIVE TO EAT (lah!) ( sarcastic) WE LIVE TO DIE ( very obvious and inevitable - can also sound philosophical !) WE LIVE TO F*** ( pornographic - typical of the older generation of WOs) WE LIVE TO KILL ( ahh.... very garang but politically incorrect !) ... the permutations are endless.
