A New Home For 6th Division

    The spanking new Mandai Hill Camp will be home to several units of 6th Division. On 30 September, HQ 6th Division (HQ 6 DIV), HQ 2nd Singapore Infantry Brigade (HQ 2 SIB), 1st Battalion Singapore Infantry Regiment (1 SIR) and 6 Signal Battalion (6 Sig Bn) relocated to the new camp.

    HQ 6 DIV was previously located at Nee Soon Camp and 6 SIG BN was at Kranji Camp. HQ 2 SIB had been in Changi Camp since 1968 while 1 SIR had been in Guillemard Camp since 1969. HQ 2 SIB and 1 SIR are historically the first infantry brigade and infantry battalion of the Army.

    The various units held relocation parades at their respective former camps on 29 September. In the pre-dawn hours on 30 September, Commander 6th Division, BG Chin Phei Chen led his troops in a symbolic route march from Nee Soon Camp to Mandai Hill Camp. At daybreak, the Division First Parade was held at the new camp. During the First Parade, BG Chin lit a cauldron using a torch which had been carried all the way from Nee Soon Camp. This transfer of fire symbolised the transfer of life from the old camp to the new.

    Mandai Hill Camp will provide a good environment to facilitate cohesion-building for 6th Division.
