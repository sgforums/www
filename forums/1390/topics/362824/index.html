    Please go to www.ns.sg to know more.

    Singapore practices compulsory National Service only for Singapore citizens and 2nd Generation Permanent Residents.

    There is no volunteer scheme for overseas citizens to enlist in the hope of conversion to Singapore citizenship after serving NS.
