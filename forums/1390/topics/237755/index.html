    First things first, it depends on what appointment or rank you possess. A recruit who has just enlisted would literally have all his rights taken off him. He is considered the lowest of all. He is subjected to punishments and most of the comforts you were referring to are taken away from him.

    Lets say you got in SISPEC or OCS after your BMT, you would have more privileges such as able to go for canteen breaks as when you like. Having a fridge, oven or television(provided by you or your bunk mates of course) are privileges given to you but do not abuse your privileges or they would be taken off you. You could bring in your PS3 in. This only applies after you have been posted to a unit and have become a specalist or an officer.
