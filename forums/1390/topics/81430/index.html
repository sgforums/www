  • saabster
    5 Jun 04, 00:17
    CMPB MOs have a bad habit of telling recruits to try first and see how...... This is just another way of transferring the work to another MO to do the dirty work.

    Your Tekong MO is going to be pissed when you first present. Not really with you but the CMPB MO. If you bring ALL you investigations and a recent medical report and recommendations by your specialist, you will be put up for a medical board if your condition warrents one.

    Medical boards at Tekong are 2 weekly. Sometimes there is a backlog so it can take about a month. You will sometimes be asked to attend the board. In other cases, the case is so straightforward that your presence will not be required.

    In the interim, you will probably be put OOC. During this time you basically do everyones dirty work.

    You will finally be posted out when your new PES is determined

    Cheers

    "Combat" BMT alonegot a few types:
    1)Enhanced(the most shiong and longest duration of 10 weeks-i went through this one)all those air level and poly guys will go to this type usually held at tekong

    2)Standard-for those monointake batches i.e enlist direct into your unit not tekong,usually for less qualified people,duration is 7 weeks but Combat training conducted

    3)obese-4 months at tekong for those overweight guys

    4)standard recourse-"combat" bmt for recourse people i.e the whole company all recoursees,no fresh recruits.One of my friends went for this when he nearing his ORD date.So when he POPed,other companies shouted "POP loh!" those super lao jiao recourse guys shouted "ORD loh"!

    Non-combat BMT

    1)Pes C BMT:7 weeks right? most of the time do area cleaning 1

    2)Pes C BMT recourse:the whole company all recourse personnel

    2)Pes E BMT-i dunno much coz not it was not around when i was still serving

    So if you managed to downgrade to PES C and below,you will go for non combat BMT,but if your condition improves and your Pes status is upgraded,then all the best to you!
