    Originally posted by Gordonator:
    thanks for the answers. but let say this bugger didn't take his FFI and his ORD is next week. why will happen to him? can he just book his FFI at the last minute?
    well. one can only get back his pink IC when the S1 branch is satisfied that he is cleared by all the relevant departments, including the medical centre. this means that it is in the interest of the serviceman himself to arrange for an FFI as early as possible so that he can ORD without delay.

    of course one can safely say that his ORD date will not be affected even if he fails to complete his FFI - he will have to make an urgent appointment for it. but that does not mean that his superiors cannot make his life difficult for whatever remaining time he has with them.
