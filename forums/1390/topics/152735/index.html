    14 September 2005

    Press Release: Serviceman Collapses and Passes away

    Ministry of Defence

    A radar specialist from the Republic of Singapore Air Force, 3SG Perry Koh Han Min, collapsed suddenly while playing street soccer during his recreational time in Chong Pang Camp at about 9.10pm on 13 September 2005. The 23-year old full-time National Serviceman was given immediate medical attention and was evacuated to the Nee Soon Medical Centre, where he was further referred to Tan Tock Seng Hospital. He was pronounced dead at 11.07pm.

    MINDEF and the SAF extend their deepest condolences to the family of the late 3SG Koh, and will be assisting the family in their time of grief. MINDEF is conducting an investigation into the incident

    God Bless the soul.
