    Hmmm, I saw your usernick before.  You live in Woodlands, right?  Anyway, even if you dun, there's a shop in front of Nee Soon Camp (transit road, along sembawang rd) that opens up to 7pm that deals with army stuff.  Forewarn that you can't use credit there.  I usually do my shopping there seeing it beats going to beach road as I stay in the north.

    There's also a small shop (those "booth" kinda stores) that sell army stuff in pasir ris interchange.  Opens on friday to sunday only.  Great place to get ur stuff before booking in.

    And yes, chevron in boon lay way has an emart which u can use ur credits. 

    Hope this helps =)
