    NCC Cadets.
    CLT Trainee has a white stripe with the "NCC" wording, CLT has a white stripe and a shorter gold one with the "NCC" wording, Senior CLT has two white stripe and a shorter gold one with the "NCC" wording

    NCC Officers.
    Those undergoing training has two white stripes (similar to the one for CLT Trainee) with the "NCC" wording, they are known as Officer Cadet Trainee.
