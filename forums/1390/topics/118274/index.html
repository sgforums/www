    Anyway to clarify, a cadet earns his white bars as he progresses thru the course, starting with 1 white bar (and a white lanyard) the first day he steps into OCS...

    1 white bar = Tri-service term
    2 white bars = Service terms 1 and 2
    3 white bars = Professional term

    ... till he receives his black bar at his commissioning (actually before, haha).

    As for white colour singapura, its for all OCTs, not just for navy midshipmen.

    Originally posted by Gordonator:
    if a 1 or 2 white bar OCT OOC and is not given a recourse, will he be "promoted" to a PTE?
    Well actually they are already PTE (since they have already passed BMT) by the time they enter OCS.

    So if a cadet OOCs in tri-service term (1 white bar) or service term 1 (2 white bars), he reverts to his original rank of PTE.

    However, if he OOCs in service term 2 (still 2 white bars), he becomes a CPL.
