    FYI HQ Armour commands the various SAR units (active & reservist) as well as the School of Armour. Sungei Gedong has always been the main local training ground of all mechanized units, & in the last decade all of these outfits have relocated there - aka "Home of the Armour" - for easier access to their "backyard." Big difference from my days when convoys of tanks & M113s used to dirty/ruin the roads of CCK-LCK-Jurong during their journeying up to the northwest.

    Haven't been in that area for a while, but unless I'm wrong, the area is still quite ulu. (The Sarimbun Scout Camp nearby remains probably the most familiar for civilians.) Being allowed to stay out may not be that beneficial, cuz the daily commuting to camp will be time-consuming if you live far away.
