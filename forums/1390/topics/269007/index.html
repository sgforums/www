    21 Jul 07, 01:12
    Yes the music for this year's NDP is performed by the Singapore Symphony Orchestra, and conducted by maestro Tsung Yeh (of the SCO). All marches were re-orchestrated for orchestra by our own Singaporean composer Phoon Yew Tien (who also re-scored the present Majulah Singapura). He also wrote a few new marches like the GOH march and inspection tunes.

    If anyone's interested in SAF military music, keep a lookout for the SAF Band website come September/October. There'll be a revamp and one section that's been planned is for recordings of these music to be made available for download.
