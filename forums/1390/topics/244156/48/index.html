    Inform your PS/PC/WSM on your first day of reporting and ask them whether you should report to MO and ask for a revocation since you are pending excuse dust or should you carry on in training.     I was a Autotech B and WSM of ATW  (B and C) last i know was WO Suhami who is a very nice guy. Most of the other regulars at OETI ATW are all pretty friendly and approachable so just check with them about your condition and see what they say.
