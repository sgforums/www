    Lemme start by what I know.

    BRDG ENGR PNR(TRAINEE)

    Weclome to the Engineers family. I suppose Trainee, means u will be a trainee for the next X mths before officially begin as a Bridging Enginner Pioneer. The Bridging  Engineer Battalion (35 SCE aka 35 Bn, Singapore Combat Engineer)  is made up of a Boat Company and Bridging Companies. The Boat Company operates small boats to support and facilitate infantry operations in river crossing. And yes, it means you get wet easily. The Bridging Companies operates the M3G floating bridges to support and facilitate vehicular crossings over rivers.

    Both companies are equally exciting to be with, and i suppose, neither is more relaxed than the other.

    SEA TERMINAL OPR(TRAINEE).

    3rd Transport Company is made up of Sea Terminal Company  (STC) and Air Terminal Company. The STC operates the LARC-V (aka the duckie boats you see, abilet in green). They facilitate in resupply operations from sea to shore, aka form the Navy Landing Ship Docks to the shore. You will learn how to rig up supplies and probably how to operate the LARC-V.  There will be occasionally sea-faring ops which will definitely be more exciting than the usual duck tour boats.

    8 SIR. 2. Your vocation is RP(TRAINEE)

    Very likey, you will be trained in Protection of Installations (POI) duties and do POI Duties at Key Installations (KINS) such as Airport, Jurong Island and Sembawang Wharfs.
