    155mm gunner....

    I am sure the posting order is for 21SA located at Kranji camp. You will be trained in the operating and combat role of a primus gun crew. You will either become a ammo loader, a driver or ammo carrier for ur NSF duty.

    However, rest assured that the primus is a very modern weapon so manual labour is minimum. However, be warned that one Arty round is about 40kg and you WILL have to carry it.

    Expect alot of PT from th unit. 21SA works with the principle of ultimate fitness. So expect to get Gold in your IPPT by your second yr window. And yes expect alot of SOC.

    Your course will be about 10 weeks with an exercise GIant to determine which role suits you best. Drive well, and enjoy the course or it will be sad.

    And yes, you are going to Cavalier battery.

    Btw, i was a detachment commander there not too long ago so i know the sytle of the battallion.
