  • Rockhound
    23 Dec 07, 18:40
    combat medics... basically got 2 types one...i break it down for u k

    HQ MEDIC...organic to the medical centre..they are the ones that run the medical centre with the Senior Medic..Medic 2ic and MO IC

    they chiong with the Unit basically..setting up BCS

    Rifle Coy Medics(Battery Medics if in Arty)
    i shall say this in infantry context..

    3 Combat medics organic to each platoon with 1 Company Medic(Medic Spec) organic to the company...basically...this is the 1st line health care for the coys....
    they train with the coys....chiong with them..yadayada..

    Company line life for them...slack la compared to HQ medic...wat the coy do..they do lor....generally combat medics don't do guard duty...

    Company Medic..life..easiest..closest to encik...
    at most..check mosquito breeding area...teach soldiers how to wear condom before going overseas exercise... and handle medical emergencies within the coy(like soldiers attempting suicide and accidents.....

    All medics are required to be Medical Cover trained...how Medical Cover duties are allocated is up to the Safety Officer... Medic Specs....and MO...

    Medical centre duties...for me...i allocate a 3 unit system
    Weekday is 1 unit...Weekend and public holidays is 2 units.. Its a 24 hour thing basically...

    MOST IMPORTANTLY....CHIONGING IS THE EASIEST THING TO DO COMPARED TO RUNNING A MEDICAL CENTRE FOR A MEDIC...CONSIDER URESELF LUCKY IF YOU POSTED TO BE A PLATOON MEDIC
