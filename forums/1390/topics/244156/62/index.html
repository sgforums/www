    For those who are posted to 8 SIR, you're all gonna be trained as RPs (yes, as per what is stated on your posting) where you're all gonna undergo training and be redeployed to any of the camps/bases all over Singapore (not just limited to key installations).

    Every few months you'll be going back to Clementi camp for retraining.. Part of the new plan to keep RPs from "nua-ing" their way through the remainder of their 2 years.

    RPs who nua their way through, theoretically won't exist any more under this new plan.
