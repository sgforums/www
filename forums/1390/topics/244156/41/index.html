    For those who got posted to Sig Ops, below's a mini "FAQ" credited to dvcx

    1) What exactly is a Sig Ops and how does it differs from a Signaller?
    2) The duration of a Sig Ops course and if there would be a confinement period?
    3) What to expect of the course and my time in Stagmont infocomm2?
    4) What kinda unit would I be posted to after course?
    5) Is there really a need to bring in all those SAF-issued items?

    --------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    1) He say he doesn't know that there is a difference between the 2.
    2) There is unlikely to be a confinement period, weekend go home one, unless you get guard duty. the duration is about 7 weeks to 11 weeks, depending on the course you go into
    3) Inforcomm2... i think it is about 7 weeks? i can't confirm it, the course is physically more intensive than IC1 or IS. You tend to carry signal sets and run around (hes surprised C9L9 get posted there and say you might OOC )
    4) Unit to post to is a lot a lot lor, but chance is high is 3SIG, 2SIG and 6SIR
    5) Yes, it is required, although if you didn't bring on the first week, it is reasonable to assume the commander would let you off. So long as you don't be too much
