    5day workweek, stay in. at times maybe some night outs. should no more confinement liao.

    book in/out now can wear civilian, but usually at least got collar t-shirt, long pants and covered shoes.

    usually, tekong privates post to guards units are going to support company. will be further send for courses if needed/assigned, e.g. mortar platoon, ATGM platoon, pioneer platoon. u maybe going for GCC* with the mono-intake privates 1st, after that then going for the respective vocational course of a support coy's platoon assigned. moreover, u will be trained to use SAR21 rifle, hence got 300m rundown day shoot and 100m nightshoot range. so... u r both sar21 and m16 rifle trained.

    in bedok camp, u will be issued and wear green beret 1st, but since u r in guards camp and a combatant vocationalist, u will get additional $50 on top of the typical $100 combat allowance. (the extra $50 is even without going for GCC 1st...)

    *: after going though guards conversion course (GCC), u will then wear the khaki beret and the shoulder guards tab.

    in GCC, u will have heli-rappelling, 2km coastal swim with life jackets...etc.

    in addition, u may have to go for exercise requirements under the combat skills badge (CSB).

    then like a typical infantry battalion, got ATEC stage 1 and 2...etc. officially by mindef, a guards unit is an elite infantry battalion ("with wings and grills"). true to its motto, 'ready to strike'. Guards are under HQ 21 Division, for it is a rapid deployment force (RDF) division that go behind the enemy lines

    More info: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Singapore_Army#21st_Division

    a typical infantry battalion consist of a battalion hq, 3 rifle coys (alpha, bravo, charlie) and a support coy. support coy is of 4 platoons to support the rifle coys. like indirect fire...etc, which is mortar, pioneer, signal and anti-tank guided missiles (ATGM) platoons.

    no, not all 3sg lah...  a company made up is like the same that of BMT platoon and company, except platoon size is smaller a bit... a section is now enlistee rank: private, lcp and cpl instead of all recruits...

    ATEC is done during 2nd year of nsf mono-intake b4 they ORD. army training evaluation centre is an saf evaluation of the battalion in a simulated "laser pointer" device attached to the rifle in a 4 or 5 days war outfield exercise in safti lim chu kang area, whereby the entire unit will be evaluated on its war fighting capabilities.
