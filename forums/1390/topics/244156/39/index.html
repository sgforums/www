  • baikachuchu
    11 Jun 09, 12:27
    Originally posted by dkcx:

    For all NSF rank for spec is up to 3SG only. Internal promotion up to 2SG is possible in some units i believe but you don't get the pay so to me its still 3SG


    Yep you are right.It is called a local promotion(L/2SG).Usually specs can get up to 2SG if they are assigned to be platoon sergeants and go for the the PS course.The thing about local promotion is that your 2SG rank is only recognized within your unit and while you are still serving...if you go to another camp or ORD,you will go back to 3SG.

    Best part is,you still only get a 3SG pay but you got to do BOS duty like any other regular 2SGs!!!Lol!
