    mhcampboy you are totally ignorant so think twice before u make any comments or claims.

    Recce or Scouts are the elites of the Infantry. Only people of high security cleareance, non-malays and a minimum education level of diploma or A level are shorlisted for this vocation.

    You think they ride bikes only? let me tell you what they do.

    1)they learn to do a signaller's job. able to mend comms and operate it effeciently.

    2)They learn to drive or ride a bike in which bikers will really learn to do certain stunts such as flying up steep slopes and going through obsatcles which have a certain amount of risk.

    3)They go through CCAC course where they learn to administer IV by poking thick needles into one another's arm and also the ccac course which all medics have to go through.

    4)Target Recon where trainees are to learn on indentifying all military vehicles,
    tanks, weapons, helicopters,equipments.....

    Scouts have to be trained in this areas as they are similar to commandos who are self sustainable. During real ops, they dont have a battalion or coy following them, they work in groups of four and are left alone in the warzone.

    Now i will come into the conventional training.

    1)an average of 30-40kg fbo load. second only to the commandos.

    2)"FBO" Full Battle order swims and crossing water obstacles.

    3)Prisoner of war treatment and survival cost in which trainess are left in the
    jungle to survive with almost no usual outfield equipment and ration for 5
    days.

    4)Helicopter drills as scouts are also heli-borne.

    5) fast marches and long distance at overseas rough terrain navex amounting
    up to even 50km or more if trainees lose their way.

    6)Hell lots of Recconaisance missions, outfields and practisce of infiltration movements.
