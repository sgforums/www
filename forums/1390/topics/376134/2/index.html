    To clarify everything,

    The Sword of Honour is given to the best cadet. Infantry, signals, armour, arty, navy, airforce etc will have one SOH. SOH and SOM receipients are the top 10 percent of their cohort.

    Every officer will get a sword when he commissions. Is it sharp at the tip but the blade is blunt. SOH and SOM will get the same sword but will have an additional OCS crest on the sword.

    The SOH receipient will also receive a separate sword that he can take home for a few days but he has to return it to OCS. His name will be engraved on this SOH sword.
