    only when u r posted to / assigned to be a Driver vocation, then u go to SAF Driving School at Kaki Bukit Road there.
    All kinds of motor classes for military vehicles only...

    whats more, all $ for theory n practical lessons, n tests r fully paid for by SAF. not only that, u r also paid monthly allowance just to learn to drive military vehicles...

    at the end of ur 2 years as a military driver vocation, just clock a mileage of 7,000km + zero-incident record for a FREE conversion application to a civilian class 3 licence...


    wth... go to http://www.spf.gov.sg/abtspf/tp.htm#dept
