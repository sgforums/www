    My advice (i was BMT instructor)

    Is that you go Specialist now to document your condition.

    make copies of this document,

    Go CMPB to voice your concern.

    When you enlist, voice out your concern again IF you are PES A/B.

    If you are PES C, get a long term excuse letter (if applicable for your condition)

    Because if you go MO after you enlist. the process is VERY slow and involves lots of friction with your PC and Sgt.

    because your typical PC or Sgt will ask you to participate training if you got no excuse. MO will be unlikely to give you a long term status outright.
