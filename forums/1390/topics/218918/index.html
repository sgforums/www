    Here i m not comparing with ppl who go chiong sua or wat , if u mus , up to my understanding , combat units do gain offs if activated on public holidays , and their weekends r mostly secured to have a happy weekend.     The catch is that my work warrants me to be slack , however nt fully the case , if we r busy , manpower can definitely be very limited due to the fact we mount as a section . Sometimes we mount with 4 men , can u guys imagine that we hav to cover 24hrs with jus 4men ?

    SO to those who r similar with the above system , can u tell me is there any directives for us ?
