    I'm so sorry for you. cdos have to get under 9.14 for 2.4km. so Running is inevitable. that including 6km ahm, and 21km ahm. Ironman challenge I don't know got change or not.. but it's just some running, clearing some obstacles, doing some crawling around, testing on your weapon knowledge. very easy one(subjective) don't worry!

    BCT in PRC is just the tip of the ice berg, when you pass over to hendon, you still got turnouts, and that's when you get banished back to a recruit life again. so you'll get trained again and again. don't worry about not siong enough! :) it's true there's less(doesn't mean don't have) tekan, but the tight training schedule will be a challenge! it'll be around 9-10 months before you earn your beret.

    And that's only just turning operational.. :D more training comes and life gets hectic with endless outfields carrying heavier loads. all for Atec, which is basically a proficiency test of all combat units on their performance and etc.. part and parcel of NSF life.

    Lastly, you start with platoons of 60-70+ people, and it gets trimmed down before you get to hendon. we work in small numbers so don't worry about efficiency nor imtimidating factors.. either way, you'll be required to get it over with.

    all officers go thru spec course aka leader course. and only a handful like maybe 15% become selected. I don't know if they remained that way cos the last i know of is they cut down the number of leaders..

    Just try to adapt the military life first, before thinking so much.. :D
