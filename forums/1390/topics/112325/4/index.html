    ok here's what i did for BMT 2 years ago.

    before enlisting, if u didn't get a silver for your IPPT, u will go for a 2 months PTP in which basically u will do PT training like running, circuit training, gym foot drills, short route marches and lots of IPPT every now and then.

    then in BMT, u will be trained in:
    - Use of M16 (actually for those who go for PTP, it was taught already)
    - Foot Drills
    - BCCT
    - SOC
    - Route Marches
    - Swimming (for those who can't swim)
    - IMT
    - Range
    - 7 days Field Camp
    - 3 days SITEST (heard now no more liao)
    - IPPT
