    I still prefer Malay commands. More clear cut. Malay commands still have the Introductory, Cautionary and Execution parts...

    e.g.

    Compare "Ke Kanan...Lu rus!" and "RIGHT DRESS!!"

    The short pause in Ke Kanan Lurus actually prepares the squad for the drill they are about to execute, therefore u will see lesser cock ups, rather than using RIGHT DRESS which is so sharp and sudden.

    "From the right in twos..Number!"
    "Odd numbers one pace forward, even numbers one pace backwards, move!"
    "Right marker still, odd numbers to the right, even numbers to the left, right and left turn!"
    "Form three ranks, quick march!"

    You like meh. I've tried shouting those, sounds so stupid and silly.
