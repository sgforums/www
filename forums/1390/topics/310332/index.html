    Singapore, Brunei conclude bilateral army exercise

    SINGAPORE : The armed forces of Singapore and Brunei took part in a week—long infantry—armour exercise which ended in Brunei on Sunday.

    As part of Exercise Maju Bersama, servicemen from the 3rd Singapore Infantry Battalion and the 3rd Battalion from the Royal Brunei Land Forces simulated the conduct of military operations in an urban environment.

    Inaugurated in 1995, Exercise Maju Bersama is one of several annual bilateral training exercises the Singapore Army has with its Brunei counterpart.

    MINDEF said such exercises enhance mutual understanding, improve professionalism and strengthen the bonds between the two armies. — CNA/ms
