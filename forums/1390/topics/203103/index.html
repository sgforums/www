    IA Drills:

    1. IA IA no feeling : Put to safe,tilt and check. Take out magazine and snap it back,c-ock again. Slap magazine,push the charging handle,observe the bolt,release,tap the forward axis.

    2. IA IA Half Feeling : Half c-ock,take out magazine. C-ock and check clear

    3. IA IA Empty Magazine: C-ock two times,take out magazine and check clear.

    4. IA IA Double Feeling : Put to sae,take out magazine. C-ock 2 times,shake the bullet out.

    Assembling of rifle:

    Put the bolt back in the bolt carrier group,bolt cam,firing pin and retaining pin. Place charging handle and bolt carrier group back in the rifle. Check the safe by echoing 'Safe Mechism Checked',thn 'fire mechism check',squeeze the trigger and pull back the charging handle (Dun release the trigger),check for the 'thud' sound,echo 'sure check' Squeeze the trigger and close dust cover.
