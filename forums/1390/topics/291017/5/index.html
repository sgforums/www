  • dkcx
    17 Nov 07, 23:41
    Originally posted by stellazio:
    how to become logistic spec and how long does it take?
    To be a transport spec, just get your vocation as transport sup, go STC learn driving 3 months then 2 months of transport sup course, 1 year from enlistment date become spec.

    To be a storeman spec, just get vocation as supply sup, get posted to STC for i think 2 months course, wait for 1 year from enlistment and get spec.

    To be a maintainance spec, get posted to any maintainace vocation (A/B/C vehicle mech, small/heavy arms, comms), complete the 2-3 months course at OETI (a heaven place we all call Only Eat Talk Idle), post to unit for 2 months on job training, after that get posted to actual unit. From the people posted to each unit, a section commander and a section 2ic will be nominated from them base on performance and these 2 people will return to OETI for a 1 month MSCC course. Like transport spec, they will get their spec 1 year from enlistment.

    In the past, ALL maintainance personnel will automatically get their spec rank 1 year after enlistment. Thats how laserlord and i got our spec ranks :p

    All IPPT eligible are required to pass IPPT to get their promotion though. In the past there was no such need so people like me who failed IPPT, failed range etc still got my spec :p
