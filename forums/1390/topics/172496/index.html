    Don't worry...

    There are a lot of Zero Fighter Legends around... Those enlist as zero fighters and pass out as officers or Specs with 12 chin-ups...

    Chin-ups cannot be trained effectively with push-ups because of the muscle groups involved. The best way to train is.... keep doing it!

    I will give you a set of chin-up exercises which you can try... This is similar to the chin-up regime in BMT.

    Straight-arm pull

    Hang on the bar, apply strength to lift yur shoulders up a bit, and hold it there for 3-5 secs. Do this for 6 times. This helps to train muscle groups that make the initial lift in chin-up.

    Isometric Chin-up

    Use a chair or something to make you hang with your head above the bar, then hang yourself, hold in this position for 5 seconds. Do your maximum reps. This will train your muscles' endurance.

    Eccentric Chin-up

    Same thing, starts by hanging above the bar and release slowly down in 5 seconds. This trains the pulling power of the muscles group after initial pull.

    You can do this in your home with a doorway chin-up bar avaialble at Sport shops or do it in your neighbourhood exercise corner!
