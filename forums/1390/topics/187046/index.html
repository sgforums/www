    YOu can don't think about working for Government already.

    As for private sector, they can just write to Mindef to verify the info you provide is correct or not. Do remember finished NS and haven't got 2 different pay.

    So private sector don't really care about your NS and some are even happier that you are not liable for NS. But usually if you are exempted from NS, there should be a grave underlying problem.

    People i know to have been exempted NS (PES F).

    1. Very Low IQ
    2. Disabled
    3. Paralysed (not all cases)
    4. Congenital deformities (not all cases)

    I also dunno how true this is, but I have heard rumors people who have gone thru Madrashah education don't go NS.

    They are not officially exempted, but they never get enlistment letter either.
