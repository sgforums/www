    MP is not bo liow. This is wat MP does. They maintain law and order and frankly, they are helping the unit to weed out crminal and disciplinary elements. And frankly, raynjy, if it was a few sticks, nobody really cares because lite sticks are not control items. A couple of boxes on the other hand, can create reasonable suspicion that there was an attempt to misappropriate the items from camp store (reasonable means a 2nd or 3rd opnion will also have the same suspicion.) What that means is that when the MP showed the items to your superiors, they can actually dismiss it as legitimate or "no wrong done" on the spot and the MP will return the items to you thru your csm. If your superior allowed them to be confiscated, than they concur (at that point of time) with the MP suspicion. Now, stealing IS an offence under sect 43a of the SAF Act even if they are just lite stick. The good news is, they cannot convict you of 43a unless an investigation to prove your guilt is convened by your OC or CO. That will be your chance to prove your innocence unless they wanna just award your minor punishment then no investigation is required.
