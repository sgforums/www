    Disruption and deferment are 2 different things. What do you actually want?

    Disruption is to end your NSF service a few months before ORD to persue your studies and payback later while deferement is to delay the start of NS.

    Disruptions are mostly for uni students and is on a case by case basis depending on how much your unit manpower officer willing to fight for you.

    Deferment is granted to anyone who wans to persue A's or Dip and are below the age of 20 on the year of enrolment.
