    Before you enlist, at CMPB, they will give you a booklet.. detailing all the policies of SAF.

    They will cover you for injuries obtained while in Service and in cases of permanent injury, they will cover you after.

    For your case, you can't just write out an injury that happened 2 years ago and only want to claim against it now.

    They will ask many questions... like why you never bring it up 2 years ago, and even if you did not bring it up, why didn't you bring it up in the subsequent units you went.

    And of course they will ask why you can sustain such an injury for 2 years and only want to bring it up now.

    i dun really know what your situation is... but it will help if you elaborate what injuries has occurssed etc.
