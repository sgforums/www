    If the next ASLC course is a number of months away, chances are not high.

    Since you are not downpes or excused from anything, you will then be thrown into some unit and go through other form of spec training, and become a 3SG.

    Another possibility, will be that you will be thrown into some unit as a commander, and after 6 months or so, then they promote you to 3SG.
