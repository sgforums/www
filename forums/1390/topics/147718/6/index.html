    This was what I did in uncontrolled NavEx in Thailand.... Hope you guys can visualise what I'm talking about.

    Before going to sleep, we would hook our rifles to our waists using our belts. In addition, we would use our toggle ropes to tie the carrying handle to one of our wrists.

    We would then wear our Gore-Tex jackets over everything.... the rifle would be hidden under the jacket.

    In addition, we slept in pairs with the rifles positioned in between the two of us. The Gore-Tex jackets are big enough to allow us to do that.

    These precautions weren't foolproof... but anyone wanting to steal our rifles would have a hell of a time trying to do so without alerting us.
