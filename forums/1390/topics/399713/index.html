RSN Navy Open House 2010

  • ahtansh
    18 May 10, 11:04

    Date:
    22 - 23 May 10 (Sat - Sun)

    Venue:
    Changi Naval Base

    Event Description:
    This May, the Republic of Singapore Navy plays host to an Open House at Changi Naval Base.

    Highlights for the event:
    Climb onboard the formidable Frigate, the gigantic Landing Ship Tank and the nimble Patrol Vessel.
    Get up close and personal with Harpoon missiles, torpedoes and naval guns.
    Speed in ships on the open sea or relax on duck tours.
    Watch the Naval Divers in an exciting ship-storming display.

    Programmes:

    Naval Sea Displays
    Don't miss the exciting Naval Displays at these times. Grab a seat at the spectator stand or a spot along the North Wharf and East Pier.

    22 May: 10am and 3.30pm
    23 May: 11.30am and 3.30pm

    Ship Cruises
    Our Missile Corvette, Patrol Vessels and Mine Countermeasure Vessel open their doors at these times. Ballot for a ticket at any of the information booths. Recipients of the coveted tickets will be informed by their mobile phone about an hour before the start of each cruise. Do report to the ticket counter at the East Pier at least half an hour before cruise departure.

    22 May: 11.30am, 12.55pm, 2.10pm, 4pm and 5.15pm
    23 May: 9.50am, 1pm, 2.10pm, 4pm and 5.15pm

    Guided Tour of Frigate
    You can find out more about the Frigate and life on board from our sailors at these times. Ballot for a guided tour at any of the information booths.

    22, 23 May: 9.30am, 11.30am, 1.30pm, 3.30pm and 5.30pm

    Workshops
    Learn the skills of a sailor at the 'Navy People tent'. Come and find out how to tie some naval knots, make a pipe on the Bosun call, communicate with the Morse code, or recognise the maritime signal flags. There is also Unarmed Combat Demonstration by our Sea Marshals.

    Find out more about workshops timings.

    Getting There:
    Admission to the Navy Open House is free.

    Entry will be strictly via the free shuttle bus only. The shuttle bus operates daily from Singapore Expo Hall 1 from 8:30am to 4:30pm.

    Find out how to get there by bus, train or car.

    Visitors are advised to take public transportation to Singapore Expo. If you prefer to drive, you are advised to park at Singapore Expo as vehicles (including taxis) will not be allowed beyond the Tanah Merah Ferry Terminal junction without a valid pass.

    Contact Information:
    For enquires about the event, please contact navyopenhouse_2010@starnet.gov.sg.
