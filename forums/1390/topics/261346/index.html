    Yes, the police do keep a criminal register but, no, only certain employers (civil service, aviation, maritime, security) can ask them to help screen job applicants. Actually, I don't think a regular MNC have the power to do this, having heard of cases where overseas postings collapsed because the foreign country discovered so-&-so's background & then refused him entry!

    As far as I know, only those with really serious military convictions will be recorded on the register. Not so much the length of the sentence, but in relation to the offenses committed under the Penal Code. You can google for the info yourself but, offhand, they pertain more to deeds like desertion, insubordination, assaulting a superior, mutiny, etc.

    Hope this helps.
