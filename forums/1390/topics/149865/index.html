    to some extend... BMT phase is somewhat a joke now...

    well... i don't wish to elaborate much.. but in general.... it's like this...

    1. due to safety concerns... much training have been toned down... components of the 7 day field camp and held OUTSIDE the 7 day time frame.

    2. no more section based training during field camp.

    3. group training is a 1.5 day affair.

    4. non-tact break for 1/2 day.

    5. no need to pass ippt, range and IFC anymore to pass BMT.

    6. you can ATT B all the way in BMT and still no need to recourse.

    7. only way to recourse is like you OOT (OOC), early in the course. ppl who OOT like in 2nd half of BMT are given the discretion.

    well.. i have a feeling people is going to flame me for this post... but really... i personally witness how BMT have changed.... and it's now very frustrating to conduct BMT...

    recruits who do not put in effort will not differ much from those who does. yes, they don't go Command schools, but you think they care?

    and those recruits who does care would voice out the unfairness in the effort they put in just to differ....... so how am i, as their superiors, supposed to answer them?
