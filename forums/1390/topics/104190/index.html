    It differs from batch to batch, depending on how many divier officers they need that year. But you really need to be the best of the best to qualify, and it helps that you are a A lvl student. For 22nd and 23rd batches they are only sending 1 NSF diver to OCS, and competiton is extremely intense. But you may want to reconsider as combat diving course takes around 7-8 months, plus the 9 mths of mid ship man[ navy ocs], and the transition period, by the time you comission, you will only serve as a officer for at most 3 mths before you ord. And bear in mind that officers serve longer reservists as compared to men and specs, who oly serve 6 yrs of reservist.
