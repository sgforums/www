    ....&, forgive me for saying this, but (a) your pleas tend to be decidedly overstated & (b) it sounds more like you're just anxious about doing NS, that's all.

    Insomnia per se won't get you downgraded. It's never an illness in itself either, but always the result of some other problem. (Wholly treatable too, insomuch the doc will enquire about the specific problem of falling or remaining asleep, & then prescribe pills accordingly.)

    You'll probably be assigned a psychiatrist at IMH & he'll conceivably do the same, while at the same time playing counselor & hearing out your personal problems. But, as commented on in the other thread, your case lacks extensive past evidence so it's hard to see the SAF immediately reassessing your case all that sympathetically. But you can try your luck contacting MCC (Medical Classification Centre) or other CMPB branches to see about another medical checkup. (Google for the contact info yourself.)

    If you report for BMT as per usual, they'll more likely take things step by step. See your BMT MO, get a referral to a SAF psychiatrist, possibly get a temporary downgrade (to C1L3 according to feedback in the "All PES status related questions" sticky -- hey, why didn't you read there first?) &, if so, methinks almost certainly must recourse BMT.

    The usual chao keng advice apply -- your future job opportunities may be affected, if you do get "certified" a lunatic.
