    Hi Anthiadon,

    ORD refers to the Operational Ready Date. It is the last day of duty for a national serviceman performing full-time national service.

    PES is a medical grading. It ranges from A to F, with those who are of PES A being most physically fit, and PES F being unable to perform national service.

    Everybody will have to go through Basic Military Training (BMT), or its equivalent for those posted to SCDF, SPF...etc. instead of the army. Only once they have completed this training phrase, then will they know which unit they will be posted to.
