    Here's a update since I've already found my way there myself.

    For those who needs to go there for Medical Appointment.

    Go to Boon Lay Bus Interchange

    Take Bus 241

    Alight at the bus stop that stops directly at the opposite of Juying Primary School, should be the 8th Stop.

    Cross over the road, and walk forward, you should see the camp, than walk inwards..

    Clothes wise will be minimal polo t-shirt with jeans and sports shoes.. (For NSMan)

    There is some distance between the bus-stop to the camp entrance, and from the entrance to the Medical Centre..

    So wear something comfortable while you find your way there.
