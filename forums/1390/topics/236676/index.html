    do think carefully....

    don't sign on because of the monetary incentive because it will not last you through the times where the SAF throws it's s*** at you.

    sign on because you desire to lead this kind of life and want to experience a job that is not as routine as any office or desk bound job. that kind of passion would normally last you longer in the Army and u'll be less of a crappy superior this way.

    And signing on as a spec or Officer, technical, combat or cbt support roles will entail different areas of work... strongly advise you to talk to any Army recruitment officers... better still if you've not enlisted, wait till you get a flavour of SAF before commiting the next 6 to 10 years of your life/youth
