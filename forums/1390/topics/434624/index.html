    Hi all

     I need some advise as  to what to do re: a deferment application. The whole story is a little long, so please bear with me!

    I'm a Singaporean doctor who left for overseas for studies some time ago, and was working and completing some specialist studies part time up to the start of this year, when I was called back to serve my NS.

    Upon registering for at the CMPB, I was told that I would be enlisted as an MO due to my medical training. However, a week later I was notified that I could NOT serve as an MO; although I am registered in Australia as a fully qualified doctor, I am not in Singapore. Hence, if I were to enlist, I would have to serve in a non-medical capacity for 2 years (which would result in me losing my medical license). In order to be registered locally, I would need to work in a government hospital for 2 years under supervision. I was advised by a CMPB staff member to apply for get a job and apply for deferment.

    Fast forward 5 months - I have signed a contract with MOH to start in September, and have been granted conditional registration to work. I have also sumbitted my deferment application (through a CMPB staff member who is "handling my case"). However, despite all this, I got an enlistment letter for the end of August. The CMPB informed me that this was because I have not yet been deferred.

    I have recently contacted the staff member again, and all that I was told was that "the documents are forwarded to the relevant department, and the application is in processing and is still subject to approval". Now, it's been almost 2 months, and this wait is pretty frustrating, for obvious reasons.

    One, my wife and I have been literally living off our savings for the last few months (she has just got a job, thankfully, but visa issues have been a pain because she is a foreigner). Two, I am  receiving a lot of pressure from different departments to give confirmation of clinical practice. The MOH has confirmed my employment and informed my hospital that I am arriving in september (so that they can organise their staffing), the Australian Medical Board is wanting to check that I am doing clinical practice, and Sydney Uni (where I was doing specialist studies) wants to know I am resuming studies this half year

    Obviously all this depends on whether I actually get deferred or not. With barely a month left to the enlistment date, I need to know how/who I can speak to in order to expediate the process. I've asked the CMPB staff member I've been liaising with, and she doesn't give me any details as to what department exactly/who is processing my deferment. Any ideas what I can do/who I can contact to help?

    Thanks

    Sing Chee
