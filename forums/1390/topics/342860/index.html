    Criteria to be exempted from BMT Recourse (guidelines from HQ BMTC)

    PES A/B/C1 Personnel

    - complete any 2 out of the 3 items (IPPT, Live Range, IFC) as certified in the Soldier Proficiency Record Book (red book) by the relevant personnel (Coy PC)

    PES C2/C9 Personnel

    - since C2/C9 personnel are exempted from IPPT, completing either IFC or Live Range is sufficient criteria for exemption from recourse. (must be certified in Soldier Proficiency Record Bk).

    S1 Branch has to photocopy the pages, CTC it and fax over to HQ BMTC. Final decision to exempt you from recourse lies with HQ BMTC of course.

    Originally posted by grandmaster89:

    I am currently a REC and have served for a year. After my recourse in 2 months time, will I get promoted to CPL or to PTE?


    For PTE,confirm you will get it after your recourse.

    For CPL,abit more tricky.First of all,what's your type of vocation?Combat or Service?

    Secondly,have you gone for your vocation training(Eg.Storeman go for storeman course)?

    If combat vocation,then you can be promoted to CPL provided you went for your vocation course,served at least a year and PASSED your IPPT.Your OC must recommend too or no point.It is possible to jump from REC to CPL if all conditions above are fufilled.BMT must be completed too.

    For service vocation,to get CPL,you must have served at least 14 or 16 months,be vocationally qualified,pass your IPPT(if applicable) and your OC recommend.Also possible to jump from REC to CPL if conditions are satisfied.BMT must be completed too.If still have not served long enough or IPPT have not passed yet,then can be promoted to LCP first after your vocation course.

     If your vocation is the service 3SG type(Eg.CQMS),then will be slightly different.
