    31 Jan 13, 21:46


    Usually, you will enlist first.

    On your graduation ceremony day, you will be granted a 24hrs off pass to go there.
    Then you will book in on the 1st boat to Tekong immediately on the next day.

    @ http://iprep.ns.sg/enlistment-schedule.html

     

    Refer:

    1. http://www.ns.sg/nsp/web/esvcs/mindef/nsreg/enq-enlist-status

    2. http://iprep.ns.sg/enlistment-schedule.html

    3. http://iprep.ns.sg/notices-and-timeline.html


    4. Call 24/7 toll-free NS hotline at 1800-3676767 to speak to a customer service officer.



    You will receive notices from the Central Manpower Base (CMPB) at different times before enlistment.

    There are 2 important notices you'll receive from CMPB, these are listed below:

    1. Registration notice: you'll get this notice when you reach 17½ years old. It will inform you to do your NS registration.
    2. Enlistment notice: this notice will be sent to you about 2 months before the Date of Enlistment (DOE).

    Get up, keep fit, stay and maintain fitness and health always.

    If you are unfit, you will have to stay back on Tekong for Remedial Training (RT) and can only book out in the late evening of Saturdays on the last few ferries, whereas the rest of the people who are physically fit and pass fitness test book out early Saturday morning on the first few ferries to enjoy the weekends on mainland Singapore.
