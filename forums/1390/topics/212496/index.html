  • eugtay

    Yes, you only pay for the first consultation, all non admission follow up is covered by the SAF.

    I would advise you to make full use of this, i know i did . the SAF pays for all treatment and 75% of your ward charges, depending on your rank and what class ward you're in. I paid S$100 for knee surgery and 2 weeks in hospital, plus all the physiotherapy.
