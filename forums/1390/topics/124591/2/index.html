    Originally posted by snk86:
    well, lazerlordz, can explain what your vocation does? cause i really interested in arment spec...........
    Armament Specialists perform repair of Small Arms, Heavy Arms or Turret Arms.They have to prepare FFI (Fit For Firing)certification and maintenance reports on the serviceability of the weapons they are supposed to repair.

    Armament Specialists may be posted to individual units or be posted to Maintenance Bases where theywill do higher level repair jobs than those in the combat units.

    Small Arms: Your basic assault rifles, MGs, pistols, anti-tank weapons, mortars etc..
    Heavy Arms: Artilllery gun systems .

    Turret Arms: Maintenance of turret-based weapons like the M242 Bushmaster 25mm gun, CIS40/50 turret(HMG and Grenade Launchers), OWS(Overhead weapon system, a Typhoon gun.)

    That's about it..do note that regular Armament Specialists will be cross-trained in all the above mentioned.

    For more info..Army Logistics
