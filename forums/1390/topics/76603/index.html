    Hmm...a pretty exciting Pro-Term for them I'd say...

    You start with ur Pro-Term (and ur 3 white bars) with SOCJOT in Brunei jungles for about a week. River crossing and "Bukit Buang" (as some people would call it) then come back for ur Technical Term, handling the various comms equipment and learning essentially what the BSC/CSC people learn, only @ a higher level...Expect to stretch ur limits with line laying exercises and NAVEXs...

    Tactical term begins and be prepared instead for various MPXs, which ur skills and endurance will be tested, especially in the finale exercise in ROC...

    Due to the nature of some of the activities, heavy sleepers are advised to change their sleeping habit...(Can't say too much about why....)

    Hmm, posting can be to the former School, 2, 3 or X Signal Battalion as Node PCs, or to practically any combat unit (less CDO, they have their own Sig Officer I remember..there was a Cadet bearing his Red Beret in Sch tt time...b4 that was an Officer on course) as the BSO - any comms problem, CO will look for you...personally..

    There was a particular character which was posted to School after graduation...Hmmm, some of u here might have an idea who I'm talking about if I mention *flying antenna mast*...
