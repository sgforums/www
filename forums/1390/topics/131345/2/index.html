    I give a quick one:-

    Training wise, u will be learning and doing just the same as your PES A/B counterparts.

    The only difference here is your training is stretched into 5 mths (I think, I also forget liao)

    Typical day
    Each day begins after breakfast:-
    Area cleaning
    draw arms
    5BX
    Edurance Training
    Lectures in super cold lecutre hall after water parade
    Field training (if applicable that day)
    weapon training (if applicable that day)
    drills (if applicable that day)
    more edurance training / SOC / Aerobics / Swimming / Gym / Games (as in basketball, etc)

    Inbetween days there are IPPT reviews. U need to improve ur IPPT as u progress.

    Each day ususally starts at 5 something in the morning (when u wake up), ending around 10 at night (when u sleep).
