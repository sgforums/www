Focus Group Discussions on Strengthening National Service

  • eac
    12 Jun 13, 18:12

    The CSNS would like to invite you to join us in our focus group discussions that will be taking place from June to September 2013. This will be a time for you to come together, to share with us your thoughts on strengthening NS.  

    We are now opening registration for the first phase of the focus group discussions that will take place from June to July, where we will discuss broadly our experience with NS, and our aspirations for NS. If you are unable to join us in this period, we will be conducting another series of focus group discussions from August to September, involving further discussion on the key ideas identified in the first phase.  More details on the August to September sessions will be provided at a later date.

    For each session, participants will be divided into small groups of 6-8 participants to discuss and share their experiences and views, before everyone gathers for a large group sharing.  Each session will take approximately 3.5 hours.

    How to Participate

    Keen to participate in a focus group discussion? Simply fill in the form below and we will get in touch with you shortly. Do note that session slots will be filled on a first-come-first-served basis. Confirmed participants should receive an email within 3 working days.

    www.mindef.gov.sg/imindef/mindef_websites/topics/strengthenNS/participate/fgd.html

    We look forward to your participation!
