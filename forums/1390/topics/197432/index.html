  • Gedanken
    28 Jun 06, 12:25
    Originally posted by SpecOps87:
    So that means...the need for the weapon to be manually charged each time is due to in sufficient gas being forced back into the gas tube to help eject the cartridge and load another into the chamber?And that this has nothing to do with the ammo at all?
    It's a combination of the ammo and the flash suppressor. When you fire a live round, part of the air that's pushed in front of the projectile goes through the gas return tube. With a blank, the only thing being pushed through is the gas discharged from ignition from the round. Now that gas pushes the air in the barrel forward as well, but to a much smaller extent because most of that kinetic energy is lost when the discharge gas is compresses while pushing the air forward.
