    hmm... maybe i can help u out a bit here...
    after passing the CPSS, interview n medical checkup.
    u will proceed to Air Force School where u await the departure date to tamworth for Air Grading.
    ur CPL rank will have to be "demoted" to OCT, which means taking out ur physical chevron ranks on ur no 4 n putting on the OCT epaulette.

    Air Grading in Tamworth is roughly 1 mth plus minus a bit.
    if u pass Air Grading, u will then proceed to OCS for future training as a 2 white bar OCT. training will also include jungle survival training in Brunei.

    after ur OCS training, u will be back to Air Force School for further ground sch as a 3 bar OCT. once u finish ur ground sch, u will be heading to Pearce for Basic Wing Course of abt 1 yr or so.

    total training right from the moment u step into Air Force School is about 2 years. n yes... u will be OCT for 2 yrs...
    even the RP at AFS will try to pull rank on u cos u r OCT....
    gd luck~!
