    OOT, not OOC. OOC is for courses and not BMT.

    And yes, if you can get 1 more week of MC, you are almost certain to OOT.

    Anyway, they are asking how many weeks you are in BMT.

    If you have been there for 10 weeks, with 2 more to POP, down-pesing is a BAD idea, especially when you have been through field camp, range....etc.

    If you have been there for 3 weeks, and have not done much yet, then go ahead and down PES now.
