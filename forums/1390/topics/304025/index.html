    According to the dress regulation, NO.4 is combat fatigue so is meant to be worn long. They are designed such that the sleeves can be folded to be worn as smart 4, which is essentially a working dress equivalant to NO. 3. This is to facilitate most soldiers with no NO.3 dress.

    that said, unit can impose their own house rules regarding this issue to ensure soldier book out smartly and with clean fatigue.
