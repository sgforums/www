    So are you still in BMT?

    I suggest that when you report back for training, tell your PC and PS about your situation and try to get an appointment with the MO. With previous historical records from IMH, many of the commanders won't really want to have to handle your case, thus the possible cold shoulder from your OC. Try to explain your situation to your PC and get an MO appointment as soon as possible.


    Although I do have to warn you that many MOs don't really take anxiety as a very serious excuse. Simply because BMT is a high stress environment and most of the people who go in tend to ease into the system when they are immersed in it for longer periods of time. Also, Tekong MOs tend to be quite anal in an attempt to filter out the "kengsters" but make sure that you bring a copy of your IMH medical reports and present them to your PS, PC, your OC if possible and the MO that you are reporting to.

    As for the medical board, you can report back to the medical center to check on when it has been postponed to. Just know that once a date has been set, it is very difficult to change that date as the board has many cases to look at as well.

    As for your training, I guess once your PC is well aware of your situation, backed by the medical reports, he would pull you out of training till your medical board is complete. Prior to that, just be ready to do nothing all the way till it is cleared.


    If luck is on your side, the board may just be nice enough to grant you a down pes.

    May I know however, what is your current PES status?
