    It all depends on the medical condition that you got PES C9 for. Not all medical conditions can be upgraded just because you want to be.

    Modified BMT just means the PES C BMT which is modified from the usual PES A/B BMT.

    PES C9 are restricted to mostly all the logistics vocations like technician, driver, storeman, clerk, medic
