  • Culture counter
    22 Jul 12, 11:13

    There is no such thing as going back for BMT during reservist ICT. Those who are combat fir (PES A/B) who did not complete BMT after ORD, will remain as RECRUIT rank during reservist time. Their Service Certificate will also indicate the rank of RECRUIT.

    Very contradictory regulation for KPI(Key Performance Indicator):- 

    PES C to E personnel who do not need to pass ippt / soc, will usually automatically promote to CPL rank before ORD.

    But PES A/B personel have a KPI, i.e. to pass IPPT to attain rank of full CPL. Another KPI for PES A/B personel is of course to complete BMT, otherwise the rank will remain as RECRUIT.
