    The Commander of the Royal Brunei Land Force was given a Singapore military award on Thursday.

    Colonel Pengiran Dato Paduka Haji Rosli received the Meritorious Service Award in military from Defence Minister Teo Chee Hean at an investiture ceremony.

    The award recognises the Colonel's contributions to fostering ties between the Singapore Army and the Royal Brunei Land Force.

    The Defence Ministry said Colonel Pengiran has actively enhanced rapport between the officers of both sides through bilateral exercises.
