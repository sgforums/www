    I enjoyed my NDP duty alot. I was assigned to co-ordinate with the NTUC party. Was really fun, people in the NTUC was really friendly although all is kena arrowed to be there. The food was good; KFC, Pizza and stuff.

    Just sitting by the banks of Singapore river and eating lunch was really cool, I mean how many of us have ever done that. Started around 1pm and ended at about 7pm and got to clock one day off. What a good deal. So relaxing and can see girls. I wouldn't even mind volunteering for it.

    Definitely one of the better times in NS..
