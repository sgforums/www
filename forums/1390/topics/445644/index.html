    Several conditions have to be fufilled..

    1) Your S1 head is a nice guy, and actually makes the effort to do something about it.. Don't count on it though, they usually dont bother about this kind of things, or not, everyone will start requesting for camp change.

    2) Your camp does not need you ie: they have extra manpower. Which is unlikely if you're a clerk or storeman. Or else they have to transfer someone in to replace you which is troublesome and inconveniencing your replacement.

    3) A camp near the West side desperately needs extra manpower. But once you're there, you will have no control whether to stay in or stay out, and things may end up much much worse than before.

    Either way, changing camps is very unlikely to be approved and if that happens, just suck thumb and serve for 2 years.. Shit happens in army.
