    Haha.. 'Good salary' is highly subjective lor, what's good salary to you?

    Ok, just to give you an idea of what the average pay is like, this is from an Army Officer recruitment booklet that's been accumulating dust on my shelf:

    A Levels/Diploma:
    (Pay by rank)
    OCT (Officer Cadet): $1584 - $1695
    2LT: $1648 - $1807
    LTA: $1711 - $3201 (LTA 1 -> LTA 2 -> CPT 1)
    CPT: $3171 - $5017
    MAJ: $4227 - $6228

    Graduate:
    OCT: $2075 - $2555
    2LT: (Grads skip the 2LT rank totally)
    LTA: $2388 - $2823 (They go direct from LTA 1 to CPT 1)
    CPT: $2472 - $5017
    MAJ: $4623 - $6711

    SAFOS/SMS/Grad (Good Hons):
    OCT: $2555
    LTA: $3303 - $3432
    CPT: $3726 - $5446
    MAJ: $5431 - $7162

    As you can see, there are 3 distinct schemes depending on your qualification. Even with a degree, there is a world of a difference between a normal pass degree and a top honours degree/overseas scholarship. Of course ultimately, performance plays a part too.

    But i think the bottomline is, no point comparing yourself to others, cos there will always be someone who is promoted faster/getting a fatter pay packet than yourself. Just ensure that it is a job that you enjoy doing, and make the most out of it.
