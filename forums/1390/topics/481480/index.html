    SAF Group Term Life Insurance Scheme

    As a NSmen, you play a vital role in the defence and security of Singapore. Knowing that your loved ones are taken care of should anything unforeseen and unfortunate happen to you gives you the peace of mind to focus on your training and operational duties.

    The SAF Group Term Life Insurance is what you need. It gives you the right financial protection while you are protecting our country. You can continue with this comprehensive and affordable low cost life insurance even after you leave employment with SAF or ORD or no longer have NS liabilities, up to age 65. You also have the option to continue to be insured up to age 70 subject to prevailing terms and conditions.

    SAF Group Term Life Insurance policy is designed for SAF and MINDEF personnel as well as those who have committed to national service.

    For as little as S$4.21 a day, you can be assured up to S$1 million up to age 65*! If you are married, your spouse pays only S$3.29 a day to be covered for S$1 million and your children can enjoy up to S$600,000 coverage at the cost of S$1.97 daily. This small amount covers the following:

    Benefits
    Life Insurance Coverage    
    Death due to illness
    Sum Assured

    Total And Permanent Disablement (TPD) due to illness
    120% of Sum Assured
           
    Personal Accident Coverage    
    Death due to a non-SAF related accident
    Sum Assured + $25,000
    Extra Death Benefit of up to $100,000 more for Death directly due to SAF training or during SAF occupational activities including Ops Ready In-Camp Training
    Sum Assured + Extra 100% of Sum Assured
    TPD due to an accident
    120% of Sum Assured + $25,000
    Accidental Total and Permanent Dismemberment
    % of sum insured (according to compensation schedule)
       
    Additional Benefits

    Enjoy automatic renewal of $600,000 coverage up to age 65; thereafter, $100,000 coverage from age 66 to 70, subject to prevailing rates, terms and conditions
    Receive advance payment benefit of 50% of sum assured, subject to a maximum of $100,000 if it is diagnosed that it is highly probable that the insured person would pass away within the next 12 months.
    Receive daily hospital cash benefit
    Enjoy partial cash rebate of premium during good years
    Your spouse can continue to be insured if you were to pass away prematurely

    For more details, please refer to the SAF Group Term Life Insurance Brochure of the SAF Group Term Life Insurance policy.
