    I grew up in the Beach Road area. Orientate yourself like this:

    Next to Lavender MRT is the ICA building. Behind them, across the canal, is Crawford HDB estate, which has a major road (North Bridge Board) bisecting about one-third into the residential area. The hawker center itself is located at the "further" perimeter of this estate, bordering another major road (Beach Road).

    Stand outside the MRT entrance so that the ICA building is on your right side. The beeline between you & the latter is actually southwards on a compass, & the hawker center is further down the same direction too. Keep this overall bearing in mind as you venture forth: cross the canal bridge, cut across Crawford Court (i.e. HDB block nos. 460-plus's), cross North Bridge Road at the traffic right, walk down either the car park or HDB block no. 7 &, before you see the NPP post straight ahead, the bigger hawker center behind it will already be conspicious too.

    When you finish shopping at the army market, I recommend the Sarawak-style chilli noodles one floor down. Of course, you can then cross Beach Road (overhead bridge only) & go ogle at the Thai girls at Golden Mile too ~lol~
