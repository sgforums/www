    Source: www.ns.sg

    You can only call CMPB @ 1800-3676767 to ask the customer service officer to help.

    Please don't use the word 'cancellation of deferment'.
    If you say this to the officer on the phone call, he/ she will take it that you are dropping out of poly as a poly-halfway-dropout case as enlist you right now asap.

    Just tell them that you just want to apply for early enlist in the Oct 2011 batch.
    But let us tell you that it is a request, so which means it is subject to approval of CMPB, since this is a common repeated scenario every single year where people keep calling CMPB to ask them to schedule for early enlistment, as it's subject to vacancies (if any) for the month's BMT batch.

    Anyway, the typical enlistment of 1 or 2 months after graduation is very common. It doesn't make much a difference as most guys will just find a part-time or temporary job to do.

    April intake for poly grads = monointake into NDU or Hendon camp aka Red Berets.

    For Tekong, April batch is mostly a very late intake for JC students, most likely is for ITE grads.

    You just write courteously to CMPB to have you enlist into the very first batch.

    To do this, you have to book your PES review (medical checkup) to the very first month and do get Pes D(Temp Pes) then they can enlist u on april.

    Try get Pes A, then they can put u anywhere and let u go through any type of training.

    April should not be a problem by then. All the best.
