    Hey guys.... want to know a bit about the history of School 2 Ninja Coy? Well , back in my days, it was known simply as 2nd Echo Coy. There being a 1st Echo in School 1. I was a platoon sgt in 2nd Echo. At the time when I was ORD-ing in 1985, there was the beginnings of the BMT school revamp and the decision to avoid confusion over the 2 echo companies. So the CO then decided that the 2nd school should have the benefit of the more "kilat" sounding names, e.g. Ninja. This happened around May 1985.

    Needless to say, many of the instructors put in suggestions to the CO that there should be a camp competition to rename the companies in school 2. Accompanying the suggestion was a list of names, some of which were funny and some were downright rude !

    An old story from an old soldier. Sorry if this bit of info bored you.
