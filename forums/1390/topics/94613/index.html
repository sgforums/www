    21 Sep 04, 20:45
    So much out of the loop? Better be circumspect on what to reveal to you, in case you're a foreigner. A lot of your questions would've been clarified when you register for enlistment or undergo the CMPB checkup.

    1. Immediate after poly, do i go into NS??

    >>> Yes.

    2. What if i get a offer to a overseas scholarship?

    >>> In your case, you can only defer if you can commence a new educational course (local U, private school, overseas studies) before you turn 18. The common understanding is that the SAF wants you to do your BMT, leadership course &/or vocational training as young as possible. You can subsequently disrupt your NS, not sure about the appropriate timing but in my time you should've already served 2 years prior.

    3. Do i get a break like say 6 months before enlisting to build up on my physical powers or what??(now a few years no P.E my body is weaker then before >.< even though im in NCC..)

    >>> The call-up will be very prompt. The traditional format was quite systematic - 4 Tekong intakes a year; June & September serving the "Poly" batches while December & March the "A Level" one - & hence a 3-month slack is theoretically possible then. Nowadays you've got 5-month BMT's alongside the regular-scheduled versions. I hear the former is very gradual-incremental in its training, so if you can tolerate the regimentation it's probably more effective than any last-minute private exercising.

    4. How much time will i be required to spend on NS?

    >>> They're gonna make 2 years the standard stint.

    5. Can i request for a higher allowance in NS?

    >>> No.

    6. What are the daily routine do we go through?

    >>> Reveille, breakfast, draw-arms, area-cleaning, standy-by-area, 1st-parade, training timetable, teabreak, training timetable, lunch, mid-day parade, training timetable, teabreak, training timetable, send-arms, dinner, last-parade, book-out & book-in by 2359. If there's night training, supper will be indented.

    7. Do we stay bald for 2 years or 3 months?

    >>> For BMT only. But still crewcut-short when on-course (inc. at OCS & SISPEC).

    8. If we immigrate to other country, need to do re-service??

    >>> Depends on whether you've surrendered citzenship.

    9. Will i get deployed into combat if i got asthma?

    >>> Combat fit - yes (as in no Pes D to F). Combat vocation - maybe not; depends on severity. Overall, likely Pes B, as long as the condition is controllable.

    10. Will i be called up for reservist if i go for a full time course?

    >>> Yes. The ICTs will be timed during the off-semester vacations, if you're with a so-called "IHL unit".

    11. How long of re-service each year? Will i be compensated with allowance?

    >>> Up to 40 days a year. Note that the SAF workyear starts from April, meaning you could technically serve 2 ICTs in a calendar year. ICTs are categorized into "high key" & "low key" stints. You can claim make-up pay.

    12. Which one of the army classified "PES" is the safest? least injuries and casualties during training? Which one is the most dangerous?

    >>> Irrelevant.

    13. Do we get to train using paintballs?

    >>> No. This ain't NCC, bro.

    14. What kind of facilities are provided at Tekong?

    >>> A hundred times better than what I went through, with the old ITD Camp 1 & 3.

    15. (im currently got fire a few times live round with M-16 and get a marksmanship badge)

    >>> You need to score a lot more hits.

    16. How much lesser do i pay in tax liabilities?

    >>> Details are available at MINDEF website.

    17. I live pretty far from tekong....do they provide transport to and fro or what?

    >>> Only from Tekong to Changi Coast.

    18. Do all new NS recruits compulsory must go tekong?

    >>> No. Recruits also report to mono-intakes, SCDF & the Police Force.

    19. if during real war that time..Will there be a special army vehicle that pick us up..or do we take bus or mrt or taxi to reach the camp?

    >>> Trade secret.

    20. I heard they they will check private parts during NS... (Can i ask them not to check private parts during NS? im a shy guy..)

    >>> They'll stick a finger into your arsehole if you report sick with diarrhoea too often - fact! Army medical centres were also the only places I saw pictorial displays of sexually-transmitted diseases upfront. Quite gross.
