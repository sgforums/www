    Q11 What happens if I have approval of absence from Remedial Training?

    A11 NSmen granted leave of absence from Remedial Training session are required to do make-up training for each session missed, subject to a maximum of 2 make-up training in Phase 1 and 3 in Phase 2. Beyond this, any other missed Remedial Training session will result in the NSman being automatically discharged from the RT schedule He will be called back by his NS Unit for investigation into his absence. The NSman is liable for disciplinary actions if he does not have valid reasons for his absence. The NS unit will also reschedule the NSman for a new RT schedule.
