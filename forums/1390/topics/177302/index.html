    Originally posted by alwaysdisturbed:
    is it true dat after a nsf ord, he's still bound to military law for 6 mths?
    Yes and No.

    No is that you are not bound for things done after you ORD.

    Yes is that, whatever wrong things you may have done before ORD, they have up to 6 months after ORD to pursue you for it.

    It is what is commonly known as "statute of limitation".
