    Yes and no. Let's go with yes first.

    Yes you can remain longer than 6 months IF you fail your FFI (don't ask me what that is, go find out for yourself) or have an MC that spans into the start of your recourse (hence not allowing you to recourse and you'll recourse at the next intake). Or, you can TRY downgrading to a pes E and hence do not go for a recourse. Note: I am not encouraging you to keng for an MC or anything. I bear no responsibility for that.

    No you can't because it's almost IMPOSSIBLE to fail your FFI again. You will have to recourse no matter what. It's just a matter of when. Even if you run away from BMT in NS, you'll have to complete it in reservist. No joke bro.
