    Pass out from army side 8sir..?? ( During my time  Navy runs thier own Sea soldier course, hence all SS are from NAVY, btw i ORD-ed 1-2 years ago.)

    where are you posted to?? CNB or TNB ?? wads ur PES status ..?? if u are combat fit (able to bear arms), you would be a full fledged SS (sea soldier), u would be posted either to a land side plt or sea ward defence plt, land side would be your standard RP gurad house duites, sea ward would be something you wont get in army (piloting a inflatable boat and doing sea ward watch tower duites)...

    The duty routine would scare you, i belive SS is one of the NSF vocations in SAF where the stay in-camp period is the longest.... It works on a 7 day duty cycle system, would only 2 platoons to rotate between. (Eg: say u belong to plt 1, you would mount duty every thursdays to take over plt 2, and stay in-camp for duty form this thursday to next thursday, 7 days straight, and next thurday you would dis mount and be replaced by plt 2, you would then book out and enjoy your weekends, then next mon to wed, report to camp 8-5 for training, so before you think you would enjoy a 7 days off for 7 days of stay-in, there isnt.!! )

    Duty shifts you get during your 7 day duty days is quite alright, on average about 6-8 hours worth of duty per man every 24hrs cycle.(well dependent on manpower,deployment and planning....)

    since my time, the 7day duty cycle was constanly tipped by our superiors that it would be shorten to a 1 ,2 or 3 days cycle, but till i ORDed it has not been changed, what i heard from my mates that are still serving, till now, the 7days system is still in place.....
