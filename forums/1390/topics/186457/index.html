    19 Apr 06, 21:52
    Originally posted by woleile:
    hw do they choose ppl for the band, hw mani ppl get to enter everytime?
    Intake depends on the overall SAF manpower needs. When the other units are sorely lacking then the band gets a few guys, say, maybe as little as 3 per batch. However when the band has a shortage they'll get as many as 30. So it all depends on batch to batch, also whether you're a musician-infested batch, whether you are poly or JC batch etc.

    First of all get yourself selected for the audition about 2 weeks before your POP. if you entered your ECA as band during sch days chances are your name will be in the list. Just make sure your OC doesn't hide the list from you guys. Then, come for audition, play your best, and pray hard.

    I say that because everything really lies with CPC (personnel control) after that, how many guys they want to post to band or OCS/SISPEC etc.
