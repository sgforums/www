    The 3 most popular exercises for the SBJ are: double-hops-over-cones, star jumps ("jump as high as possible from squatting position") & tuck jumps ("jump to knee-touch chest as high as possible"). You need to do these regularly, in circuit sets of 12.

    my fren said that climbing stairs can help in your sbj.
    They work the thighs & calves (ditto duck walks), but personally I'm not a believer that they'll help for SBJ.

    The idea, instead, is to condition more the fast-muscle muscle fibers for that spring in your step. The exercises I mentioned train for that stop-start "explosion".
