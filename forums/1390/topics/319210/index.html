    Promotion for Enlistee rank men is prepared by the unit manpower branch, not cmpb. Unit clerk will 1st prepare the paperwork, gets approval from your immediate superior (e.g. PC), and the promotion is effective either on 1st or 16th of the month.

    If you are not promoted by the time period from Date of Enlist (DOE), it's usually admin lapse by manpower br, since the nominal roll is called up from the manpower info system by the clerks through its search function, not some auto-reminder for the generation of the nominal list. OR your immediate superior KIV your promotion lor.

    **Auto-promotion admin scheme by education level when nearing ORD is scapped wef early beginning of the year 2007, with around the same time of the re-introduction of Corporal 1st Class (CFC) rank.

     

    Earliest promotion effective date from DOE:

    PES A/B
    LCP: 7th month from DOE;
    CPL: 12th month from DOE (IPPT pass is needed).

    PES C/E:
    LCP: 7th month from DOE;
    CPL: 15th month from DOE.

    CFC:
    There are some criteria for CFC, something like:

    1) immediate superior recommendation
    2) vocation job scope/perfomance based, (with IPPT for pes A/B)
    3) unit manpower br approval, then followed by formation manpower br approval.
    4) Quota. e.g. some 20%-30% of the CPLs in the same batch, under the division/formation.


    so one of the morals of the story:
    make some friends with the clerks... dun belittle them... admin work is also crucial in the running of the operations, e.g. paperwork stuff still done by pens and words... appraisal work...etc.

    so the saying, 'the pen is mightier than the sword'.
