    30 Jan 06, 01:11
    Not true. Band people must be Pes C2 and above. in fact, most of the NSFs that get posted in are either Pes A or B. Standing one and half hours in a parade is no joke also, seriously. Plus, all the usual IPPT and BMI hoo-haa applies too, jsut like any other soldier. Go for your auditions at the end of your BMT. Your name should be in the list if you indicated band in the form that you had to fill up at enlistment. Come for the audition and if you're selected, you will get to know your posting together with everyone else. It's seriously a better life than chionging-suah. Trust me. Although you wll never get to be a spec as NSF, you will have most of your time doing your own admin.
