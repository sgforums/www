    SAF defence officer faints after treadmill run, dies in hospital

    SINGAPORE: A Defence Executive Officer working in the SAF fainted and died after completing a run on a treadmill on Friday.

    Mr Quek Meng Chua, 58, fainted after finishing the treadmill run at 8.20am at the Gombak Recreation Centre.

    He was given immediate medical attention and sent to the MINDEF Medical Centre where resuscitative measures were taken by a medical officer.

    Mr Quek was then transferred to the National University Hospital at 8.55am but he passed away at 9.35am.

    Mindef and the SAF have extended their deepest condolences to the family of the late Mr Quek, and will be helping the family in their time of loss.

    Mindef is investigating the incident. - CNA/ir
