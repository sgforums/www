    According to SAF Dress Manual....

    LTC and below, SAF crest.

    COL... that Crest with laurels.

    BG and above, pineapple. its a sword and baton symbol with Singapore coat of arms on it.

    On CDF... because he is representing the 3 services... he wears the Joint staff crest... if you look closely it resembles UK's Crest. It has a Sword (Army), Wings (Air Force), and Anchor (Navy). The difference between singapore and UK is that UK is topped by a crown whereas singapore on is by Coat of Arms.
