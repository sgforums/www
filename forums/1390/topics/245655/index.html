    I believe that some ppl have the misconceptions that the clerk vocation is necessary a easy job compared to the others (eg combatants). While i'm not saying that it is harder, it is certainly not as easy as what some ppl perceived. Just like all other vocations, the unit and appt that you are assigned to will likely determine the workload. If you are posted to an active unit as a clerk and assign to the manpower/intelliegence/ops branch, you may just feel that it is a underrated job.
    In peacetime, you'll need to be doing your day job in the office.
    In ops time, you'll be required to draw maps, manage orbat or running errands for your bosses.

    Anyone else feel the same as me?
