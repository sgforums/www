    Had this guy in my platoon during BMT, always smiling and cheerful usually. He was a bit weak, but was always willing to do whatever needed to be done. Hardly complained. Suspected he was a tad slow in thought.

    One day in the 3 tonner on the way back to camp after a siong outfield training, the PS asked him to sing a song. He insisted he didn't know how, when the PS suggested one of the army songs, he kept quiet for a bit... then suddenly there were tears streaming down his face. The whole platoon was a bit shocked. The PS hurriedly said never mind.

    Must have been the stress of being continuously pushed. I think for many people who go into the army without any preparation, it can get more than a bit overwhelming.
