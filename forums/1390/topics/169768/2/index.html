    Originally posted by kuekj:
    off topic: though I don't get to use my beret since I'm a svc medic.. how do I exactly do the beret? Read I have to tie some ribbons or something.. and where to put the SAF crest?
    Look at your beret, it has two holes.DO NOT put the crest and pin it through the two holes.

    Take the two threads as the center line.Then pin your crest on the left facing the threads.

    Later, tie the threads together or else they will stick out.

    Fold the beret in half and flatten it under a heavy object to acheive the desired shape.

    All the above instructions should be done with the guidance of a lao jiao, about to ORD soldier.Correct if there is anything wrong, it's been a while since I wore my beret..used to prefer the jockey Cap.
