    This is the issue with many people I've come across. They tend to compare technical/staff 3SGs against combatant 3SGs and this is an unfair comparison.

    The rank is there to help you do a certain task in your field of specialistion, be it commanding a section of Guardsmen as a Guards SC, running a manpower branch as an Admin Supervisor or being just a simple tech spec in a depot maintenance section (where the Section IC can be a MSG/2WO).

    Can we look beyond simplistic comparisons and try to move away from this notion that if you have not gone through SISPEC, your rank is worthless. If that is the case, why don't we simply close down ATI, OETI, IMOS, SOL, and all the other technical command schools? If there is no respect for your fellow Specialist just because he or she is of a different PES, then even as some combat Specialists distrust their technical or staff counterparts, the opposite will also happen as well.

    I have had experiences of Guards Specialists throwing their weight around in my depot and ordering my guys to perform certain tasks like transportation of mortars et al, heavy items that under SOP, they as the sending unit, are required to do by themselves. This was in spite of the fact that me and fellow Specs are there and nearly causing an accident to happen to one of the junior techs.

    If we did not have our rank, this chap could have caused a lot more trouble and he might not be easily rebuffed if the ranking tech on the spot was just a CPL facing down a 2SG.

    Lastly, everyone I knew never cared about the SISPEC issue. Our rank is rightly earned in our field of specialty, and it would be utterly ridiculous to rate a tech spec on the basis of an infantry leader.Sure, we may be not as proficient as them in combat operations, but can they do a better job than us at MIS or managing armament systems maintanence planning?

    Now this is not to say that we are a vindictive bunch, but there are reasons why non-combatants do require a certain junior commander's rank. In the end, the only factor that matters is mutual respect for the task that everyone does.But apparently some people have a huge chip on their shoulders.
