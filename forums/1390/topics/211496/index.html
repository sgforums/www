    24 Sep 06, 23:05
    That place is so hot that you can't stay in you tent after 6am in the morning. By the way, you sleep in a tent meant for 10 to 16 men.

    Night time is pleasant if you have time to laze around the make shift canteen. Beer is cheap but dun drink too much otherwise the hangover in the hot sun next day will be jialat.

    Cigarettes is very ex. Dun smoke if if can.

    Day off in Rocky (Rockhampton Town) is short. You probably have ONE day off to buy your stuff as you probably have no time during your R&R.

    R&R is usually a night stay. The "Educational Tour" will take up the whole day leaving you just a few hours at night to walk the town. BTW, it will be good if you can "inform" the clubs and bars when you R&R night is. Otherwise you may end up walking in a dead town especially on a Monday night.

    Next morning, you will probably be picked up at 7am to go to the airport.

    Enjoy your trip.
