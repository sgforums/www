    Answers:

    1) No, U just hv to complete BMT, go for CPSS, pilot selection interview, aeromedical check up,once u successful, then they will call u sign the bond (10 yr bond is effective only after u have earn ur wings, otherwise contract terminated.)

    2)It is base on manpower requirement , quota, performance. But they are allowed to appeal. Eg. if they have space for 10 fighter pilot, n they have 20 qualified for fighter, top 10 will make it, the rest stream to transport or rotary.

    3) Yes, But He/She is subjectted to conversion courses. 1 of them is C/IRPL (Commercial Instrument Rated Pilot License).
