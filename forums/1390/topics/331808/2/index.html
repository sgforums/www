    Defence Minister Teo in Australia, meets PM Rudd
    By Lau Joon-Nie, Channel NewsAsia | Posted: 24 October 2008 2300 hrs

    SINGAPORE: Singapore Defence Minister Teo Chee Hean is in Australia for a four-day visit from October 23-26 as part of regular ministerial exchanges between the two countries.

    He met Australian Prime Minister Kevin Rudd in Brisbane on Friday.

    According to Singapore's Defence Ministry, both leaders reaffirmed the warm and extensive defence relationship between their countries and discussed regional security issues.

    Mr Teo also conveyed Singapore's gratitude for the use of Australia's training areas by the Singapore Armed Forces.

    He also met his Australian counterpart Joel Fitzgibbon and Shadow Defence Minister David Johnston in Canberra.

    On Saturday, both Mr Teo and Mr Fitzgibbon will visit SAF troops taking part in the annual Exercise Wallaby, which is being held at the Shoalwater Bay Training Area in Queensland from October 3 to November 16.

    - CNA/ir
