    Atcually WSO is Weapon System Officer.To complete the course: need to do well in summex, IPPT gold, SOC below 8:30 and passing grade for all written or technical handling test is 75%

    Regulars get to choose what they prefer. For officers, they will get to learn 2 weapon systems - a missile system as well as a sensor system. The siongness depends on which weapons u are posted to. For I-HAWK,Rapier, and oelirkon guns, they are not man-portable system. Deployment is siong but after u are deployed most likely u will just sit there and wait for target. For Man-portable systems like Mistral or RBS-70, deployment time is within minutes and thus, u get to deploy, redeploy very frequently.
