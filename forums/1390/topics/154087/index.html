some things to share re: my 2 weeks confinement in NS

  • bismarck
    23 Sep 05, 00:34
    i made the mistake of forgetting to bring my watch to tekong and honestly i think it has helped me pass the time.,,ironic.

    because i dont have a sense of time so i dont really count the minutes that pass by during a training that i dun really like for eg, AGR or 30:60 etc.

    time in the 2 weeks seem to pass faster and faster as the days go by and many times i feel "eh it's breakfast again, the previous breakfast didnt felt like yesterday"

    during this informal chit chat session with PS and another sgt, he selected some of us to stand up and do a small intro of ourselves. then many of us openly admitted to PCC as a hobby, must to the surprise of my PS because he said nowadays we are so (cant find the right word)

    anyway i feel my platoon has rather 'innovative' forms of 'punishments'

    1 guy simply loves to lean on the chin up bar whenever we are there or lean on the SOC platform near the chin up bars so my sgts always made him shout

    " help help, the ( chin up bar, platform, wall or depending what he is leaning on) is falling! "

    and this guy who jokingly boasted to a sgt and tried to be funny was ordered to grab some pebbles from the chin up bars we were at and arrange the stones in platoon level, complete with platoon IC, sgts and PS.

    then during 1 lecture which quite a few were going to doze of, the OC who conducted the lecture dragged those he caught sleeping and made then sit on this raised platform and make silly poses that cracked us all up.
