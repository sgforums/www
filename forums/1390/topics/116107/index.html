    Most stylo command: "Tukar langkah waktu berjalan..... tukar langkah!"
    (Change of steps on the march).

    I say this is stylo because one of the PS in my BMT company pronounces it as "Ooogah chaka!"

    Longest command (I think): "Akan berjalan keluar dalam masa yang cepat..... Nombor satu contingen dahulu..... Bergerak ke kanan bertiga-tiga.... Kekanan pusing!"
    (Command to march past the parade)

    Weirdest command: "Akan memberi tiga sorokan kepada XXXXX.... Hip-hip.... Hooray!...."
    (Giving three cheers for CO/Brigade Commander/Division Commander during change of command parade)
