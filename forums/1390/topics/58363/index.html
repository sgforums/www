    about route marches

    its realli abt conditioning. stil rem my first 4km route march at bmtc. i nearly died. eventually moved on to do like 40 km route march.

    hence things like entire arm feel numb, breathlessness etc r all normal. it jus takes conditioning.

    some tips: make sure ur boots r tight when u do marches, else u can get blisters or even sprains. keep feet dry by using powder. if need be can wear 2 socks too. make sure ur underwear wont rub against ur groin alot when u walk else by the end of it u wil get bad abrasion there and trust me, its horrible. heng i never kanna b4. if need be wear boxers, esp if u r one who sweats alot. tighten ur fullpack straps such tt the weight rests on ur upper back rather than lower back. do ur warm ups properly b4 starting. during rests, sit down and prop ur legs up. dun drag ur feet when u walk. IMPT: sing and njoy (at least try) while marching so u wil be distracted from ur fatigue.

    soon route marches wil become rather njoyable.
