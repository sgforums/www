    First of all,have you officially revocated to clerk yet or are tyou temporarily doing a clerk job until they officially revocate you?Secondly,Redano pointed one important point.Estab Quota.

     

    If there is a clerk position that is empty in your unit,they will slot you there first.

     

    Your request for posting out is not confirmed like what the chief clerk said and it is very likely that CPC will ask him/her if there is any more clerk requirement within the camp.If there is a position open,they will most likely deploy you in that position within the same camp unless you provided them with a VERY COMPELLING and valid reason to have you posted out.

     

    My friend downgraded to PES E from an infantry company but he ended up as a clerk at the HQ even though there isn't anymore clerk positions and they were already over estab.Yes,he did request to be posted out....to CMPB infact

     

    Think of it this way.There are probably too many people in other camps who have downgraded to clerk.Other units have downgraded clerks,your unit has downgraded clerks too.Why go through all the paper work just to shift clerks when there is already enough?
