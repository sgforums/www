    (3) Have you found a new job already? How long did you take to find the new job after ORD, or you already secured a job before you ORD. How many resumes did you send?
    Job hunting started 3 months before I ORD. Secured a job 5 days before ORD. But switched jobs on the day I ORD to another dept of the same company.

    (4) If you have found a new job, how far are your ideals from your new job. In terms of position, jobscope & pay?
    Position was higher than what I used to be in SAF. Jobscope is also more complicated but more focused into automotive engineering. No need to do duty, no need to chiong any sua.     (5) Compared to SAF, how much welfare did you lose now?
    No more free medical. But current company covers my medical mostly with $3.00 co-payment everytime I see doctor. However, my current company gave me free courses to attend that helped me in my career even better. Something I'd never get in SAF.

    (6) What was your vocation in SAF?
    Autotech Spec

    (7) Were you very worried when you are going to ORD that you may be jobless for a long time?
    OF COURSE YES! But luckily I managed to stay free for 2 weeks when I started my new job on 15 July 2005.

    ( 8 ) Do you regret leaving SAF?
    Leaving SAF was 1 of my greatest agenda in my life. And I've accomplished. I will never regret that decision even if I were to be jobless for ages. What's more I'm young and can afford to run now. If I were to leave when my ex superiors are doing now, I'd be in greater trouble finding new employment.
