    (I think mods can put this as a FAQ in a sticky since I have encountered NSF and NSMen asking the same question over and over again..)

    MMI is inside DSO (KR) Building which is opposite NUH. You can take SBS Transit service 95 from opp Buona Vista MRT, Technopreneur Park bus stop, then alight at the second bus stop when you reach NUS. The bus stop is called LT27 Lim Seng Tjoe Lecture Theatre.

    From there you follow the covered walkway and the directional signs to DSO. Change pass with the security who will tell you how to proceed.

    --------

    Alternative way for those who want to take NUH LinkBus:

    Take NUH LinkBus to Main Building (1), alight at the bus stop. You'll see the MD11 in front of you. Walk to the objective. You'll see Medicine Faculty Museum, turn left, you'll see a cafe, turn right after the cafe and take the long flight of stairs down. As usual, change pass.

    -------

    For drivers, please prepare your cashcard. Please park at the School of Computing public carpark and cross the road, follow the first para on how to walk.
