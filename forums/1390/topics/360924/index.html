    While you are on status, its unlikely for them to sent you to any combat school for training. If you are revocated anytime before your excuse ends, it might be a logistic side vocation or else you'll be posted to a combat school to do saikang till you can join the training. Logistic side vocations like technician, medic, driver, clerk, storeman are all possible vocations. Vocation and education has little link except people with lower education normally end up in mono combat mono intake but anyone can be a storeman regardless of education.
