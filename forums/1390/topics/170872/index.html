    There will be a IPPT categorisation test held during the 1st 2 weeks of BMT. There will decide your weak stations and your 2.4 timing. Frpm the results, instructors will train you accordingly and focus more on your weak stations.

    IPPT table:

    Sit-Up
    Pass: 31 reps
    Silver: 36 reps
    Gold: 40 and above reps

    Chin-up
    Pass: 6 reps
    Silver: 8 reps
    Gold: 10 Reps

    Shuttle Run
    Pass: 10.8secs
    Silver: 10.4secs
    Gold: 10.2 secs

    Standing Broad Jump
    Pass: 216cm
    Silver: 225cm
    Gold: 234cm

    2.4km Run
    Pass: 12:19
    Silver: 11:39
    Gold: 9:44

    Basically Napfa and Ippt are very similar.
    Napfa SILVER = Ippt PASS
    Napfa GOLD = Ippt SILVER

    Which explains why SAF do not recognised Napfa bronze as it is below the IPPT passing marks.

    Hope these information is useful to you.
