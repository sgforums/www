    It used to be that any specialist/officer can apply for basic airborne/diver course.

    But due to over-demand and waste of money, nowadays it is reserved for:

    - Officers LTA going to CPT.
    - SSG or MSG going to JWOC (Joint Warrant Officer Course; pronounced J-walk)
    - Commandos' Currency Jump

    By right - "by right", you can apply with priority given to above, but usually get rejected one la..

    A few years back, I heard of 2 artillery NSF specs, their WO just apply for them, happen to have vacancy and they got their jumps!

    Anyway....usually u'll get rejected lar... if u're not a spec, its 0%, if spec, u got 1% chance.

    Outside courses to be recognised is impossible. A COMBAT jump is a military activity, means u're jumping for war...not for fun. There's no civilian organisation which can do that or teach you for combat..... NO outside course will be recognised for Jumps/Diving.
