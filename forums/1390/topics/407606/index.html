  • smrtbsbs
    11 Aug 10, 21:28

    Just my two cents:

    There are 2 routes to this:-

    1. If you follow what your OC said, there are 2 possible outcomes,
    -> You pass your BMT and POP. Like what someone who had highlighted, there were instances whereby a recruit POP even though he failed all FC, IPPT and SOC. In fact I know a few. And the OC really play a large role in determining whether you POP or not.
    -> You fail your BMT and you need to recourse.

    2. If you OOT, you will surely need to recourse.

    Now, for the recourse cases in both routes, if you do not have a PES downgrade, you will need to retake the whole BMT again. If you have a downgrade, then there is a chance that you take a modified BMT, or maybe you do not need to take it at all.

    Looking at the 2 routes, a 50% chance of retaking BMT is definitely better than 100% chance of retaking it. So unless you want to pia OCS and Spec, I would think it is better that you take route 1.
