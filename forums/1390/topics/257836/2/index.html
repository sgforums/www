    When I was in BMT, we did the practice grenade throw in some course area. (cos got place to duck, crawl etc).

    As i stepped up to go thru the procedure with my PC, he leaned towards me and said,
    "Lets have a bit of fun, When i say throw grenade, you throw it, but throw it towards the training shed. We see what your fellow recruits react"

    Just one incident in other wise boring day..
