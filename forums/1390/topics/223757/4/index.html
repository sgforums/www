  • android01
    19 Jan 07, 16:49
    All BMT recruits are issued with the LBV and the new fieldpack from this batch (Dec 06/Jan 07) onwards. The standard configuration of the LBV comes with the waterbag, as well as one water bottle pouch. I'm not too sure about the other pouches (magazine, grenade) though.

    Passing SOC hasn't been a requirement for passing BMT for quite some time now. However, they have just reduced the requirements even further. Previously, recruits had to attempt the SOC with the full rundown, with their time taken, even though it wasn't a requirement to pass. Now, they don't do any rundown anymore, not even the half rundown. The focus is now entirely on the techniques of clearing the obstacles.

    Good luck to all recruits from this batch onwards.
