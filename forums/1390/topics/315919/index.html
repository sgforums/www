    I think there are much more safer alternatives to upgrading though.A balanced diet will go a long way to helping your situation in a safe manner.If you can have the determination to get upgraded,you should be able to apply it to your diet too.With your current condition,you will most probably be excused alot of things even if you get upgraded.If you can't control your diet,that little amount of exercise might not help much at all.Just make sure you stay away from slimming pills,those things might do more harm than good for a person with medical condition.
