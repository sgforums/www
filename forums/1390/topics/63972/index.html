    im a RP from a certain airbase..the RP unit in Airforce are use as ground force in base..
    my unit name is FDS..Field Defence Squadron

    we do our RP course in the Airbase taught by our Military Police RSM n also rifleman training by our CSM..all under a course

    we do 24 hour duties spread out in an area as big as Toa Payoh after we graduate form the course..

    we have chiong sua training for 2 weeks after every 1 mth duty..
    we also have GOH training for NDP...
    there are time we still have to chiong a "sua" even on duty day..

    live firing of every single infantry weapon or organic weapon will be held..occasionally..

    n u will have major excercise every year..

    RP life is more relax then normal infantry..so dun worry..

    but if you know that you are a RP now..that means you will not be in FDS..
