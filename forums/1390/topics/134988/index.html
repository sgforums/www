    -non lao jiao-

    just my 2-cents:
    do consider some things, such as your reasons for wanting to go ocs....

    for many, the reason would be the higher pay and relaxed life after commisioning (albeit more stressed)
    if you are like that.... likely its better not to go... cos after commisioning you would have less than half a yr to enjoy those stuff... perhaps only 3 months if you factor in clearing leave etc.

    however if you are trying for officer out of pride and garang-ness.... by all means speak to your PC and go for it.

    however after everything... keep in mind that it will affect your NSF life, in the worst cases, you may either not like OCS/officer life, or not get into OCS in the end anyway...

    so, consider carefully
