    was making this list for my frens... so tought might as well post it here for the benefit of everyone... enjoy~

    heres all the essential stuff u will need to bring.. get all at beach rd so u will get the correct ones...

    and btw.. don't buy from the malay vendors... they charge too high a price.. go find tis stall with a big sign '133' .. its near the staircase by the toilet.. this chinese auntie sells her stuff quite cheap.. recommended by my sergeant too.. hee

    MUST bring stuff

    ziploc bags - small, medium, large ... at least 5 of each
    black tape x 3 - the ones u use to insulate wires .. buy the sansui brand cos theres more tape compared to the other brands like hunter or thunder... used intensively to secure items.. plus ur buddies borrowing from u.. ha ha
    blue tape x 1 - same as above.. for wrapping your dummy grenades
    clear tape x 1 - the large ones
    black rubber bands - small x 10, big x 1.. small ones for SBO... big one for helmet
    black perm marker - for labeling your army stuff wif your 4D number.. hee
    black pen - no pens of other colours are allowed!
    pencil
    clothes hangers - get at least 8 .. must be SAME colour
    clothes pegs - as much as u feel necessary
    washing powder
    slippers - to go toilet or bath
    toothbrush/toothpaste
    toiletries - soap, shampoo, facial foam.. watever
    shaver
    nail cutter
    underwear - lots .. unless u wanna wash everyday
    grey admin tshirt with army logo x 2 - they will give u two .. u buy two..so together four.. use some for standby (display set) and some for use
    grey singlet with army logo x 2 - same as above
    black shorts x 2 - same as above
    green army towel x 1 - for fieldpack.. get a slightly smaller one than usual so u can make a smaller pack no2 for inspection.. hee.. but DONT get a hand towel!
    home tshirt - bring 1 for sleeping so you dun need to wear the army ones.. bring 1 own shorts if u wan .. no need jacket... they will give u a veri gd gortex one
    orange cloth x 1 - they will give u one... one for fieldpack.. one for standby
    small key lock x 1- pass 1 key to your buddy .. also try to buy a better brand lock like yale
    comms cord x 1 - attach to SBO .. get the medium sized ones..
    rags or scotchbrite - for area cleaning
    lighter
    batteries for torch
    extra handphone batteries     - NO chargers are allowed! dun bring camera phone also
    empty yakult bottles x 2 - for making your dummy grenades
    coins and some cash - for vending machines, canteen breaks, emart purchases as your credit would not be here yet and no NETS at emart .. and for platoon fund if there is any .. dun bring too much cash tho.. $20 is sufficient

    Recommended optional items

    small L-torch - personal use.. u can buy maglite wif u wan... cos L-torch eats batteries too fast..
    cheap digital watch
    alarm clock - no need to bring unless u wanna OTOT .. cos surely got pple bring one
    reciever - for own entertainment at nite
    febreeze - when u dun have time to wash clothes
    dettol/lysol disinfectant spray - disinfect your personal stuff like boots, shoes etc .. and during periods where the whole platoon seems to be sick..
    snake powder - they will give SAF powder too..
    tupperware - for putting your writing materials and other stuff .. i use them to compartmentalize my drawer for tidyness..
    small thirsty hippo - your sergeant might make this a standby bed item
    magiclean wipper - for area cleaning to remove dust easily
    rifle sling - cos the one they give u is kana sai... unless u lucky and get a new one
    Pi Pa Kao - a must to prevent cough
    vitamin C tablets - boost immune system.. so will not fall sick easily
    glucose - for better tasting water and for energy during PT.. get the non-flavoured ones cos theres a possibility of trouble if u pour purple water (grape flavoured) out of your water bottle during water parade.. hahahahaha
    medication - like yoko yoko, zambok, medicated oil, sano plast, tiger balm, medicated plasters.. etc
    spectacle hook/strap - prevents your specs from flying off during PT

    optional items

    snacks - u can bring biscuits, cup noodles ... theres a hot water cooler at lvl 3 of each coy
    magazines - a hot favourite is FHM
    instant coffee, milo, cereal - up to u
    your own cup - for making your hot drinks at nite .. they din't give me any army mug.. so better to bring one
    your own fork/spoon - to eat your cup noodles

    Prohibited items
    camera phone
    chargers of any sorts.. hp, battery etc
    porn
    pirated cds/vcds
    camera

    Things to do before enlistment

    1- take the 2 empty yakult bottles
    2- fill to the brim wif sand
    3- cover completely wif blue tape
    4- tadaa... dummy grenades

    there are somethings that are not mentioned here like bicycle hook.. soap box..brush...etc ... that have to be standardised platoon lvl or whether u need it or not... so no point in gettin them now.. juz wait for further instructions.. [/b]

    ok. now another new set: useful things to bring when u enlist.

    1. ziploc bags of various sizes*
    2. plastic bags*
    3. extra handfone batteries*
    4. sano-plast (dunno how to spell; basically some cream or oil for muscle aches!)
    5. small torch (for use after lights off)*
    6. some washing powder (for clothes washing)
    7. clothes hanger (no need alot since they will probably want to standardise eventually)*
    8. pegs (as many as possible)*
    9. lock - usually not number locks as they will require you to pass one set of keys to your buddy
    10. slippers*
    11. tupperware for food like biscuits, etc.
    12. black tape*
    13. scissors/ cutter
    14. pencil/pen*
    15. clear tape
    16. perm marker - for writing ur name / 4D number on items
    17. snake powder - that is what they would recommend usually; you will get saf powder too.
    18. extra batteries for your receiver, torch, etc
    19. cloth for cleaning
    20. nail cutter*
    21. rubber bands (normal and those black thick ones)
    22. masking tape
    23. coins (for vending machine, payfones)*
    24. orange cloth (you will need to get eventually, but you can get it inside too)
    25. underwear (more! disposables are usually not recommended)*
    26. own clothes for sleeping if u need
    27. black string (for various purposes like securing items, tying your ID tag)*
    28. small mirror (you can get inside too; for applying camo)

    alot of essential outfield items you will pick up and learn after going outfield a few times. for me, my SBO always have these items

    1. black rubber bands, big and small*
    2. black tape*
    3. spare batteries for torchlight*
    4. ziploc
    5. pen/pencil
    6. writing material - the small black book - put inside ziploc*
    7. small mirror with camo*
    8. 2 x insect repellent tubes*
    9. spare black string
    10. jackknife*
    11. more than one torchlight
    12. half gloves
    13. mess mug* in plastic bag
    14. ear plug and whistle inside whistle pouch*
    15. a roll of the cord (... forgot wat its called. anyone?)*
    16. compass
    17. dummy grenade and magazine

    of course during bmt you wont have to have and you are likely not allowed to have certain things u want to get yourself (e.g. own torchlight, compass). this just serves as a guide.

    and a standard rule of thumb: always secure all your items! i secure things like the torchlight and even jackknife to my sbo.

    in both lists the * items are more important.
