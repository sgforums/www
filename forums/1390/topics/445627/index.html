    for more details, go read up at:

    1. http://www.ifaq.gov.sg/spf/apps/fcd_faqmain.aspx

    to sign bond means you sign contract to serve for 5 years as a regular instead of 2 years of full-time NS.

    break bond and you can expect to pay back several thousand dollars (compensation penalty + all the monetary allowances, regular salaries and bonuses) that is supposedly invested in you to groom you to be trained as a professional policeman.

    as being a professional policeman for 5 years, you are expected to be a full-time professional policeman to serve Singapore. only if granted approval by your superior, then you may register for part-time night classes to upgrade your studies, subject to approval since it's country above self. e.g. police duties comes first.
