    My advice for you, young man is ENJOY IT!! I'm not saying this from a "been-there-done-that" attitude or trying to belittle the whole experience, but really....it will be much better if you do. I was posted to 30SCE from Tekong and I know the kind of feeling - sian, everyday kena tekan like hell, outfield, outfield, clean weapon, clean stores, load stores, outfield some more.

    Life may seem very monotonous at times, and there seems to be no light at the end of the tunnel. But you know what? There is light. One day, you will walk out of that camp with your pink IC. Then the inevitable question that every NSF asks himself as he puts away his number 4 - what am I going to do with my life?

    My friend, go through it. Bear the hard times, enjoy the good times, become best mates with your platoon members. I look back on those days and realise that it is the only time in your life that you can "turn-off" - just do as your told and have fun. You get to shoot, blow things up, take long walks in the countryside and get fit. Seriously man, at my age you will be trying to keep the paunch away while slogging long hours in the office and being a slave to the corporate world - NS seems to be a welcome disruption. It will most probably be the time that you will be the most fit and handsome in your life. You have a good body, six packs and all. My six packs have undergone a hostile merger into one big pack.

    In short, I wish you the best and do take care of yourself. Don't dwell on the negatives, but instead focus on the good things. You are fit, you are alive and breathing, you will make the best of friends in there. Enjoy your adventure!
