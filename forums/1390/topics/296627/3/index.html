    Honestly, there's some good advice provided in this thread, so don't challenge them as yet (whether to boost your self-esteem or whatever). As stated, you'll definitely have two outlets early in BMT to highlight your predicament to (the PC interview; the MO when you report sick).

    Just don't expect quick resolutions. Your PC may have a quiet word with your DIs but training will otherwise carry on as usual, & peer pressure can be tough if you whine to much or don't carry your weight. You may not necessarily get to OOC before BMT finishes either, even if the MO sends your docket up the COC for further review/boarding. In other words, DON'T rely on your condition as a security blanket. This is a new stage of your life, & it's time to grow up accordingly.

    If the unit doesn't have an OO, there's always the SAF Counselling Hotline. Just keep in mind that neither source has any real executive powers; they can only provide a listening ear.
