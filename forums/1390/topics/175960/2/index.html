    During my BMT in Tekong, I was a recruit in Charlie Company in 2000. While, I was waiting for my posting after the end of my BMT phrase, there is a night stay alone in the recruit mass. I was alone playing the PS station and I can feel goosebumps poking out and I have those weird funny feeling which i cant describe.

    Soon, after playing for some time, I fell aslp on crouch. Thanks god, i was able to fall slp till the next morning. I remembered when i woke up, i still have thoose goosebumps as the whole room is quite dark and rather creepy because i was alone there.

    When i told my chief clerk that i stayed overnight in the recruit mass, he gave me a stunned and rather surprising look. As he recalled that the place is huanted as there are 2 occasions when the sergents had encounted some scary moments.

    1st occasion: The sergents were playing PS station in the mass late in the night where all recruits were supposed to be on the bed. There are some knocks on the door and when they opened the door, they saw noone at corridoor. It is quite unlikely that someone can run and hide from the corridoor. Something is mstery

    2nd occasion: Bcos Tekong is a old malay village before it becomes an recruit military camp. The incident happen when the sergents were resting in mass room and the radio suddenly switched to a Malay radio station, which is not explained.
