    For Artemis, I guess you are in either E, G or K company if i am not wrong.

    Anyway, towards the end of the BMT, your PC will bring up such cases to your OC and maybe to CO (if need be) to see if you qualify for "attended" BMT status.

    Attended BMT means you did go thru BMT but did not pass any test criteria. But you need not recourse BMT. To qualify, you will be accessed on attendance and personal discretion of PC/OC/CO. What matters is they will decide if you are ready for "next phase" of training. For the attendance portion, they have a formula to calculate your attendance. It's not simple Secondary school (50/50 days) but rather a weightage.

    Anyway, for your case, you might be a "re-bmt" case. Since you really miss too much.

    Your recourse might be the next intake since PES BP personnel can only go PES BP companies.

    For Ah-soh. Recourse is not always towards ORD date. It's really lots of factors.
