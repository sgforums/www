    You have to enlist.

    Show your specialist's letter to the MO when you report sick or during the medical examination following enlistment. Just because your specialist recommended deferment does not mean the SAF doctors will agree (I think it extremely unlikely). There are SAF specialists for such problems who will review your condition and your MO may refer you to them. The Chief of Medical Corps is an orthopaedic surgeon himself.

    Chondromalacia patella (CMP) is a very common problem and many of us have it at different degrees of severity (I have it too). Unless there are X-ray changes (very severe), you will not be downgraded. Physiotherapy will still be available in the army and it will be free as well. You may want to buy a knee guard and wear it during training...it may help.
