    guards usually do sentry duty on 2 hours rest 2 hour work basis. they may also need to do prowling depending on uints. the guardcom will brief u all on guard duties procedures and simple laws of engagements etc.

    everynight there will be staff parade whereby the duty officer (DO) will come down to the guardhouse to count strength, account for the arms and ammo and brief the guards on outstanding issues to take note. garang DOs might also give 1 or more turnouts (usually at wee hours of the night) to test the response of the guards.
