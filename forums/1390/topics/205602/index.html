    Take it easy man, its not so bad.

    They have the proper training method to train you how to swim. They won't toss you in the water and let you figure your way out.

    I'm sure it hasn't changed in 8 years, but they will typically segregate your company into 3 categories. A, can swim with the proper strokes. B, able to swim but strokes not refined yet. C, declare totally cannot swim at all.

    A means no need swimming lessons at all, you can lobo all you want during the lesson time, like sleep in bunk. This is dependent on your own company policy. I was fortunate to be A Slack time in BMT is so precious.

    B would go for training to improve strokes until you can swim.

    C, I think would really go shallow pool and to start from beginning.

    No need to stress, you're not gonna die. Actually swimming lessons are one of the most relax times in BMT I believe.
