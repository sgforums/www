    NS Recognition & Benefits

    To encourage the community to show support and recognition for our National Servicemen, the ACCORD Family & Community Council started the “We support you in NS” initiative. People’s Association (PA) and SAFRA have garnered the support of small merchants and retailers located in the heartlands or near camps to offer discounts and benefits to service personnel who show their 11B at stores displaying the “We Support You in NS” decal.

    Do visit our website regularly for updates on new promotions and discounts @ http://lifestyle.www.ns.sg/myns/ns-recognition


    For merchants interested in participating and other related queries, kindly email ACCORD@defence.gov.sg
