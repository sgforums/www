    20 May 05, 14:30
    There are 2 medals that you can get when you are a reservist:

    SAF Good Service Medal
    The SAF Good Service Medal may be awarded to an NSman who has attended at least 3 high-key in-camp training.

    SAF National Service Medal
    The SAF National Service Medal may be awarded to an NSman who has completed 10 years of operationally ready national service and served a minimum of 5 high-key training and 2 low-key training.
