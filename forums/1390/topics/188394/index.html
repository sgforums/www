    Introducing the Sea Soldiers. No longer will they be known as RPs or army vocationists serving in a naval base, they are now officially known as a new vocation in the RSN.

    They are
    - infantry trained
    - basic shipmanship trained
    - little bit EOD trained
    - little bit CBRE trained
    - POI trained plus rules of engagements etc

    they are also getting $150 for combat pay too.

    check out the cover story in this month issue of pioneer for more info.

    for those without subscription, online version can be found here.
