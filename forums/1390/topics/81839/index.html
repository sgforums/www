    Up to you. There will be no major difference between BP BMT and Pes A/B BMT.

    BP recruits are treated as PES B personnel only that they are obese.

    One difference is the ppl you get to mix around while in BMT.

    In PES BP BMT, you will be mixed around with ppl of ALL educational backgrounds.

    If you are PES A/B, your BMT will be according to educational level.

    However, JC/Poly recruits in PES BP will still get their chance to go OCS/SISPEC if you so desire.

    IF you passed NAPFA since your CMPB checkup, you will have to inform CMPB and automatically re-classified as PES B.

    But since you now reduced weight but have yet to pass NAPFA, then you stay as PES BP.
