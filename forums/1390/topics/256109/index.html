    Are you enlisted or awaiting enlistment?

    Most people sign-on way before entering NS, like JPSDS, coz they can get alot of money that way. However, once inside, they regret it but its too late. Too break the bond is too prohibitively expensive.

    There is a reason why SAF offer so much money to sign-on before you enlist and its bcoz most people would never think of signing on after being having the SAF-experience.

    However, some people sign-on after they enlist because they do find a career in SAF meaningful and worthwhile. However, they tend lose out on that big chunk of money that the SAF offer for those that sign-on before they even enlisted.

    So, if lets say if you are not under any scheme that give you huge monetary rewards to sign on before enlisting, then I recommend you enlist 1st, see your life there and decide about it later on.
