    Originally posted by ORIGAMIST:
    oh ?.... I thought he don't have to go BMT or something like that ... so at the next medical review, he will know his status ? By the way, there is Pes E and F, what are they ???
    PES E are for people with major health problems and will be normally assign as clerks or storeman depending on their problems. PES E people will go tekong for a 5 day induction programme before posting to unit to serve their 2 years. After that PES E people will ROD and will not be called up for reservist.

    PES F are for people who totally cannot serve at all like the disabled, people with mental problems etc
