    Let me try step-by-step instructions:
    1. hold the beret with crest facing your right.

    2. close the 'mouth' (elastic part) of the beret, with the crest facing away from you. Now the 'mushroom top' is divided into 2 halves.

    3. fold the half nearest to you inwards, over the closed 'mouth'.

    4. fold the other half over the previously folded half.

    5. place neatly folded beret under pillow when sleeping (or under any other heavy object) and let season.

    After a week, you will have a beautifully seasoned beret that will be the envy of your peers.
