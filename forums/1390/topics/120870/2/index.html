    ok here's some tips on how to take care of your beret.

    firstly, always get the right size for your beret. not too small nor too big or else u end up looking like mushroom. the average people wears size 7.

    after u gotten the right size, cut away the ribbon at the end of the beret. so that u won't look like a cock with ribbbon sticking out of your head and it looks nicer when put on the shoulder.

    secondly, remember to use your hands to brush away all those unwanted wool when u get your new beret.

    a good way to season the beret is to wear it often and carry it around where ever u go. putting the beret under heavy weights won't season it, it only helps to get the shape when folded.
