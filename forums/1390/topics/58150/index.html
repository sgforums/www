  • jacobs
    19 Oct 03, 17:39
    Unforgettable events quite afew.....

    1. Chiong suah times.... however brunei wasn't siong coz my unit did a 32km route march at pasir laba prior to brunei. We had gd combat fitness. It was the feeling, really super damn freaking low morale.... especially jungle village.... Then afew times at lim chu kang, first was section proficiency test. My friend got heat exhaustion under that hot weather.... really shagged out, body numbed still have to casuavec my section mate.. Second was recently, Platoon battlecourse at lim chu kang again... two guys fall out, hyper-ventilation. At the end of it, Casuavec again. The weather.....

    2.Punishments, i think this punishment is nothing to commandos though. There was a refreshment talk on chemical defence, and some of our guys were sleeping behind. Some not that cooperative, did things slowly. Our punishment? Walk and run around our parade square in Mopp4 (typo, i don't know the right spelling). Followed by standby bed in 5mins, we were preparing for alert red and our bunks were in a mess. When that Pl sergeant came, of course we didn't make it in time. So he say," nvm, i want your bunk to be the same as the day you enter army ". Everything went out of the door. Everything in our cupboard, our pillows and bed cushions weapons etc. Cupboards fall, mirrors break. It changed my attitude towards my superiors on that day. I don't care that much now. Although i have seen other platoons carried their stuff (cupboard and bed) to empty bunks and have standby bed over there then carry back....

    3.Of course there are times where we enjoyed ourselves, my friend coming back from outfield was so hungry that he took a empty tray and demand rice from the cookhouse aunty," ah boy, no plate leh ".
    But after spending so much time bonding, some had downgraded and/or posted out.
