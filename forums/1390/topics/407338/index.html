    WSO = Weapons Specialist Officer

    WSO (pronounced as wee-zo) Fighters are those who sit behind the fighter pilots in the cockpit e.g. on board the F-15 or the Apache. You're manning the machine's missiles / mini-gun.

    Try to prepare yourself well for the interview. It's one of the most critical filtering layer. Ask around among your friends and those before you what they were asked.

    Correction to last... only tandem seat fighters require WSO(FTR) such as F-16D and F15. Both aircrew in the Apache are pilots.

    WSO(FTR) are trained in navigation and operation of aircraft systems. Simply put, they are to do what the pilot is not doing at that point in time, to lighten the workload...

    The interview is relatively simple. They will ask general questions like why you sign up for this; why do you want to be a pilot etc. 

    Word of advice.. Do not say you sign up so that you are able to repay Singapore etc etc... They have been through countless interviews and experiences. They will know what is wayang...
