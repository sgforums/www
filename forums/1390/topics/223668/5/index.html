    Originally posted by TERENCEE:
    what are the badge i can achieve in bmtc?
    ippt gold/silver is a badge you can achieve during BMT. Whilst you can put the badge on as stated by MINDEF orders, it is not a norm for any recruit to put their IPPT badge. They will only do so after they have been assigned to a unit and taken the annual assessed IPPT.

    However, if you have achieved TKD, Basic Airborne before enlistment you may ask your PC if it's permissible to wear them. Even so, 1) you could prob only wear it on POP day 2) you may not want to have that added attention on you being the rare few in BMT to have confidence badges as a recruit.

    Cheers
