    Technican (For NSF, not regular) is for the following people:
    1) OOC personnel
    2) Obese personnel
    3) Personnel with conditional BMT passes
    4) Personnel who failed BMT
    5) PES C personnel

    Most of them will be posted to OETI to take a course there...... (For AETI and the navy one I'm not very sure about it)

    Technican is divided into several sectors --
    1) A Vehicle -- You are unlucky if u are posted to this...... It is for tanks.......
    2) B Vehicle -- For tunnels?
    3) C Vehicle -- For smaller vehicles?
    4) Small armour -- To repair weapons like rifies
    5) Comms -- For signal.......

    Normally you will have to wait for the course to start and that period you will be asked to do nothing except paperwork at either OETI, HQ MES which is besides OETI or they will attach you to some other camps temporary until the course starts..........
