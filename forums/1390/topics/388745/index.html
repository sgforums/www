    Originally posted by mfz:

    Your PES matters, but in an indirect way.

    You must understand that Military Band, being a service side, will be given less priority over Command Schools and other Combat units. Hence, even if you fail to enter Command School, it is likely that they will consider putting you into a Combat Unit badly in need of people first before thinking about putting you into the Military Band Side.

    If you are Pes C and below, you do not have to worry as much about entering Command Schools or Combat Vocations ( Though there are still a few combat vocations open for PES C ), thus in a sense, you will have a higher chance.
