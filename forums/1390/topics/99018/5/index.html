    As the movie implied. It means enemy aircraft sighted (supposedly a bane for field artillery). There's a drill the gunners are supposed to execute upon the warning, which was basically fanning out, sit or lie down facing the flight path & then firing the M16s into the sky. Is it practical/realistic? Beats me.
