    Hi guys, recently clear my new window IPPT and got 100 dollars. the timing was 11 ming 43 seconds, 5 pull up, 221 cm jump, 9.7 seconds of shuttle run and 36 sit up. I realized that I am very near to silver for this test... so i am confident of getting a silver with some more training

    My final aim will be gold which will be difficult because it requires 10 minutes. I worked it out, each lap has to completed within 1 min and 40 seconds which seems so difficult to me because that is like a fast speed. In short, you have to run 4 metres per seconds for 10 minutes to get gold.

    Another area of training i have to do is standing broad jump which needs 230cm. The rest are more or less there liao just need to maintain the ability to do gold standards.

    My strategy to achieve these targets are as follow

    -practise 2.4km run using my best effort and so gradually improve until hit 10 minutes.

    -drop my weight by a few more kilos to about 56kg through combination of diet and physical workout,

    -practise standing broad jump using square tiles at HDB

    -cross training other physical activities sometimes to maintain weight, general fitness to maintain my ability to do well in IPPT

    -practise other stations once a while to maintain the standards..

    I do use other methods before and read different methods by others but i realize that basically just training the real thing is one of the best method already. at least to me i think that is the logic.

    During the test, i will ensure i eat enough carbo food for the few days before and plenty of hydration to make my body optimal for running 2.4km. maybe at a energy drink also 1 hour before the run.
