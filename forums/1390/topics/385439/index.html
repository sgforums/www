    Hi iceangel89,

    I don't think you will get a chance to see the MO on the first day at your new unit unless you sound out your problem. It is important for you to show your report to your superiors, though there is no guarantee that they will allow you to see the MO.

    It will be better for you not to request to see a SAF Psychiatrist, as the MO may straight away assume that you are attempting to malinger. Instead, show him the report, and he will likely sound out to your superiors and give you some medicine to aid your condition. After a few weeks, if your superiors feel that there is no improvement, they may sound this out to the MO, who in turn will send you to see a SAF Psychiatrist.

    Cheers.
