    Icic, thanks for the information bro. Granted that autoclaving cannot be used in the field, but this device can still be used in a Medical Centre or Field Hospital Setting. Sydex and other chemical sterilization processes takes a long time to kill all possible pathogens and you have to clean up the instruments nevertheless. Sydex takes about 10 mins to sterilize instruments just for normal pathogens but 10 hours or more for spore like pathogens. Hopefully our enemies won't use Anthrax on us.

    What can be practical would be Flash sterilization of the instruments using this device within at the HQ level and putting them in a sealed containers for use when needed.

    But yeah, you are right that this device might not be practical at the battalion level during outfield, perhaps more at the HQ and medical centre level cos flash sterilization can help to cut waiting time and it's used more for orthopedic/emergency related surgical instruments where you have alot of bone/muscle tissue remants on the instruments and you want to reuse them asap on the next patient.
