    You have to hold the actual appointment for at least 1 ICT first. After confirm the appointment, you will be able to get your KAH allowance for the following years. It is pay once per annum apart for your normal ICT pay claim. How much it is depends on the rank and appointment you hold for example a CSM should get about $600 a year etc...

    Acting or stand-in appointment is not considered...
