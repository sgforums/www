  • Moxie

    I think there's a misunderstanding. I'm seen various numbers being thrown about, & the DSTA's official stand is that the SAR-21 is lighter than the M16.

    For accuracy's sake, I think it's instructive to note whether a 30-round magazine is included for each weapon when doing comparison.

    Also, the SAR-21 can be very heavily accessorized. There's the scope & laser pointer, of course, but also a detachable foregrip, tactical lights, 40mm underbarrel grenade launcher, amongst others. DSTA was also quoted in the media as saying the weapon ended up weighing "more than 8kg" when added with certain weapon sights (night vision) & additional sensors (underbarrel TV camera attachment to shoot around corners). The newer experimental version was supposedly "3kg lighter" with less bulky/lighter sensors.
