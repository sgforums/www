    Please call the 24/7 NS hotline @ 1800-3676767 to speak to a customer service officer.

    How should I inform Salary Services Centre of the change of my bank account if I am an active serviceman?

    You can visit the MINDEF internet website at www.ns.sg to update your new bank account via eSelf-Update. The change has to be updated by 25th of the month in order to have your pay/allowances credited into the new bank account on the next payday, 10th of the following month.

    As pay details are confidential and personal to the serviceman concerned, Salary Services Centre (SSC) does not reveal the pay details of servicemen to their relatives/friends. Please advise serviceman X to contact SSC directly at 6373 1429/1430/1432.

    Newly enlisted personnel will receive their first payment either on the weekly Friday payday or 10th of the following month, depending on when the recruiting agency notifies Salary Services Centre. However, the recruiting agency must inform SSC on time by the stipulated deadline so that SSC can create the relevant payroll data to process payment timely.

    Salary/NSF allowance being paid on the 10th payday is meant for the payroll period from the 1st to the last day of the current month. Thus, if you are going ORD on say, 20 March, you will receive only a pro-rated amount for 1 March to 20 March.
