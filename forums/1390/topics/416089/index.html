    You do not need to be super muscular or fit to get Pes A.

    You just need to be of a healthy weight and have no health problems.

    Some of the common reasons for Pes B are:

    1) Having a crooked back (Even slightly crooked counts)

    2) Wearing specs and having more than 250 degrees

    3) Previous injuries (Arm dislocated, leg dislocated...) 

    4) Health issues (Asthma, skin problem...)
