  • specfore

    TAG team is the team that controls and directs local movement of air assets at a pick-up site. Together with a specialist team of troops, they help choose and set-up (including clearing a safety zone) of a pick-up site, and coordinate the heli-lift or tactical airborne lift of troops and their support equipment (e.g. scout jeeps, ATGWs, mortars etc) from this pick-up site into a LZ/ LS that is behind the FEBA.

    TAG team mainly composed of ATC trained officers , WOs and sgts. Altho' there are a number of infantry/guards/ CDO chaps cross trained to do the job.
