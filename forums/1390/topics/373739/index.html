    Ur sec com and ps and pc dont decide where u go. They can recommend, but thats all. Sometimes if they dun recommend u can still end there. Ur posting is decided entirely by cmpb.

    They look at a few factors; namely bmt results (all the tests), peer appraisal, and commander recommendation. And this is the sucky part. They look at ur academic results also. So, if u and another guy have roughly the same bmt results, he will get into ocs or sispec if he got better academic results. Even if u are better than him, he still has higher chance than u. This fact is not spoken and never admitted by them, but its true. 

    And by the way, ur vocational assessment also counts. The computer test u did at cmpb after ur medical? Ya, dun screw that up or u MIGHT get into a shitty vocation.
