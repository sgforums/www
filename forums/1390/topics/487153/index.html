    Pioneer Magazine Cancellation
    Reservists: Call 6373 1114 or pnr_cir@defence.gov.sg
    NSF: Find Unit Clerk / Chief Clerk

    Just provide Name, NRIC No. & a Simple Reason for Cancellation.


    Template for Email

    Dear Sir/ Madam

    I, CPL (NS) ABC, S9XXXXXXZ, would like to cancel my subscription of the Pioneer magazine. Because if I want to catch up on MINDEF/ SAF news, there is already the online cyberPioneer webpage on the Mindef website. Thus, please refund the balance of my money paid to my designated bank account as stated in www.ns.sg

     
    Thank you.
