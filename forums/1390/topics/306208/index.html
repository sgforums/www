    When ur sergeant say, squad leader report strength.

    you sedi ya, then kablakang pu sing. then, ask your section/squad sedi ya, then diam them, then u move to ur right/left (to the sergeant), then go to him, say, good morning/afternoon,evening, sgt, appointment reporting to sgt, total str 20, present str, 17, reasons (for the 3) permission to carry on sgt, then turn back to ur platoon, and make sure nvr to back face your sgt. then senangdiri them. then u turn back, senangdiri yourself.
