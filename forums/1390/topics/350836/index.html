    U can keep continue emailing to NS PORTAL. They will have to give you an answer. For your case, if u really need to revocate, ur NSHRC has to discuss with NSPC before replying to you. it takes 1-2wks earliest. For all cases that are being forwarded to NSHRC, NSHRC has to reply to a system to let NS PORTAL PIC knows the update.

    I hv quite some experience in this as i have been revocated and now requesting to be posted out. Keep pushing thru NS PORTAL or if u can't wait, give ur unit NSHRC a call and talk to your chief clerk. Even so, they need time to pull out your data and check with the correct PIC. So it will take a few days.

    Hope this info can helps you.
