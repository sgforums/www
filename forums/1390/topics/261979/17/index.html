    Originally posted by lim123:

    hey anyone knows the difference between mono intake and double intake into guards camp?


    Guards mono intake is for the comparativelylesser educated batch. ie ITE grads, n level/o level holders. Which means they enlist as a whole from day one and ORD as a whole.

    Don't think it's called double intake. For the other one soldiers are sent to the guards unit after their BMT at Tekong/SISPEC/OCS. These mainly are the poly grads/a level holders/ Degree holders.

    My two scents worth. Pls do correct me if i'm wrong.=)

    Hi all,

    I do see a few questions here and there as I was reading through the lengthy topic on the commandos. Perhaps I can plug some gaps and answer a few questions with my limited knowledge on the commandos.

    First and foremost, congratulations to all who actually passed the Vocational Assesment. You'll probably be posted to Pasir Ris camp where you'll be in doing your BMT in SOTAC. Your instructors will all be specialist and you'll hardly see any other officers around other than your CO, 2IC. Life shold be fine although you might find the arduous journey sometimes.

    To answer the question on whether you'll get priority if you join BAC as a NCC cadet. Its a downright no. In fact, you'll probably have to do the pushing turning and rolling over once again.

    I would be glad to share any knowledge I have on the commandos as long as it isn't classified information.
