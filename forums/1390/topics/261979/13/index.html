  • Pitot
    13 Feb 09, 12:42

    Are you going for the VA at NDU?

     

    the schedule may differ but basically u do a barrage of pyscho and personality tests around 3 to 4 hour

    then ur medical which consists of a visit to the MO, a few tests (yes they will take ur blood, i got pricked on both arms) and then a sit in the hyperbaric chamber for simulated dive test to see if u can clear the pressure.

    then physical for chin up and SBJ and 25 m swim to gauge ur swimming technique.

     

    Interview nothing much, they ask about ur uniform grp history, fitness and also anything u want to know about the unit and if u are interested.

     

     

    Its an eye opener, u get to see things u prolly wont ever see again in ur life unless u enter NDU.

     

    fun la trust me.

     

    oh and lunch and dinner provided.

    if everything goes well, it should take around 12 hours.
