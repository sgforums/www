    Originally posted by S.s.k:

    You will most likely be with the Alpha Batch, made up of mostly JC students and some poly grads. Anyone know more about NDU and how many officers they choose per batch?

    Well, in the batch of about 80~ people, only 4 will go to OCS for NSF aka not signing on. They are chosen largely based on daily performance which includes performance, attitude and leadership qualities. But there are other factors taken into account like peer appraisal and physical factors. If you're deem as a potential candidate for OCS, you'll be selected to be a 'Leader' in the dive phrase. If not selected, you've a 99% likelihood not being able to go OCS.
