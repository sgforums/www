    Hello,

    I am sorry, but expressing interest does not really help much.

    For CDO, only 1500 people are randomly choose from mostly pesA and some pesB who met the requirement to attend a vocational assesment (VA). Only 250 of these 1500 are chosen to join the CDO.

    Here are some requirement for CDO,

    >165cm tall

    >55kg

    >less then 300 degree in each eye

    >Being only son will NOT effect your chance of being selected, please update youself.

    >Being smart or top student does not give you a higher chance. There are IQ test during the VA.

    If you are lucky, you might be chosen to attend the VA. Just do you best in every station and the interview.
