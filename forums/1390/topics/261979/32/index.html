    Originally posted by Maoning:

    So during NS, how to you get selected to get into Guards/ Commandos. Is it by random ?

    Well from what I know:

    Guards: Some will go in through the mono-intake (usually non diploma or A level holders) while the majority will be posted to Guards after tekong BMT, not exactly sure what the criteria is based upon but I suppose fitness plays a part (as Guards are pretty 'xiong')

    Commandos: Commandos is mono intake and they have their own BMT. You'd have to get a VA letter (they look at Security Clearance, MAPAS results, PES A/B etc) to go for an interview coupled with some physical and psychological tests. After that they will select the Commando enlistees from the VA pool. Apparently the chance of going from Tekong BMT to Commandos is close to or nil.
