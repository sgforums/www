  • rigaku
    5 Feb 10, 16:05

     

    the only way to get into CDO is if you are not in it is by signing on. No two ways about it.

    To sign on, you must first pass the Special Operations Force Selection which will be announced every quarters, you must check with your unit or for your case, your manpower side whether if there is any selection going. They are planning to ramp it up to 4 selections per year.

    The selection is 3 weeks long, and the last week will be a mini rite of passage, or formerly known as "hell week". Why I call it mini because if you are able to clear the selection and enter as their training company, your last week of your training phase rite of passage is way way beyond your imagination.

    For starters, once you cleared the SOF selection, you will now be put into either SOTF/CDO or SOTF/NDU. If you choose to be a commando regular, I think nowadays they won't want but you can still try. Another way is if you can ask to be a battalion regular, ie, less pay than Commando Regular Force or SOF, more sai kang but you still wear the red beret and still fight ATEC with 1st Cdo Bn.

    If your highest qualifications is Olevels, you can still try, but how successful depends on your results, whether if your results can still go for further education, if they deem okay, then sure by all means try for the Selection.

    In Army, everything must try, if never try, you'll never know whether if you ever stand a chance.
