    i guess depends on how he got his CPL.

    if he got the CPL from BSLC... then he will go ASLC if he sign on Inf/Guards Spec.

    if he CPL from his rifleman time or non-combat vocations.. he will have to go thru B and ASLC.

    basically if he got his CPL from a "non-leadership" training... he have to go BSLC first to sign on as a combat spec.

    my ex-CSM is a weird case, he was guardsman during NSF.. then ord liao cannot find job outside, then no guards opening for a period of time so he suck thumb sign on Inf spec..... now he is a Green beret with a guards tag.
