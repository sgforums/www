    It's best to call up your unit, for confirmation of the latest SOP. In my time, there's no need to apply to "defer" manning at all, but I must separately inform my chief clerk (phone) AND the Mindef Notification Center (phone, SMS, email, mail) if I travel overseas during the mob period. Even more so if you tio recall i/c, so as not to sabo your unit planning.

    Yes, you'll be excused from mob activation if you have passport proof of being overseas at the time. But pre-notification itself won't be enough; ditto if you're still here for whatever reason & didn't report. Chargeable offense.

  • blitzonic

    Yes, MINDEF has been using the ICA's EECUS ("Exit-Entry Control Upgraded System") to monitor travelling reservists for a while now. So much so, they did away with the need to physically surrender exit permits upon returning.

    But, apparently, the system can only "capture the actual departure & return dates of an individual when he returns from an overseas trip. This means that the individual's overseas trip duration cannot be ascertained at the point of departure. In addition, vital personal details such as overseas trip destination, expected trip duration & contact number are also not captured by EECUS." Hence the need to pre-notify MINDEF directly on the details of your trip. http://app.mof.gov.sg/cutwaste/suggestionview.asp?id=18635

    I dunno how much the new biometric passport can help track movement via EECUS better, if at all.
