    Ok....one reminder in case this thread goes outta hand..

    Ops Manning is a rotation of duty for different units to be on standby for emergencies or real operations.

    ie. At any time, there're a certain number of units on manning and readied for recall at X hour of time.

    REMINDER: Please do not reveal your unit name/date/time in public, that is classified information. You're not to publicise that your unit XXXX is on manning from when to when.

    Basically Ops Manning means, u're ready to be recalled....sometimes its just recall to test readiness and ensure that units are ready to move out, rarely u'll also be mobilised and move out for a 1-2 day exercise (super rarely happen)......or worst case, u can be activated in a real emergency (happen before, like Tekong robbers case, Ops Manning units move in)....or real war activation.
