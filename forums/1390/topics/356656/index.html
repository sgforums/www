    well hey guys. i know there have been a few threads previously regarding LBVs etc that our servicemen get, but they all seem to be dead. I thought it'd be nice for everyone to discuss the equipment that they have receieved over the years. Hopefully, MSD won't touch this as everything is widely available at beach road, and thus 'open source'. As this is my first picture post (and as i'm a noob), please be nice and tell me if you don't like it, or you want me to move it somewhere else.

    Just a brief introduction. I served my time in an infantry bn and just recently ORD-ed. my LBV might seem a little different from everyone else's, as there were leftovers from the trials given to the previous mono in the bn, and i took a sample. The quality's alot better than the current one that all the recruits in BMT recieved. I also zhnged it many times extra for personal comfort; namely to accomodate my bunsen cooker, cookies, 100plus, sweets, chocolates etc.

    I'd try and get everyone a close up of the new ILBV (integrated load bearing vest- digitised and armoured) from some open sources for comparison. This would probably be re-manufactured and issued.. 3-4 years down the road?thanks and have a good one! please add on to this thread if you can. i'm sure those nostalgic ex-nsfs overseas would love to see what we have now. i'd post more pictures in the coming days, once i get the hang of how to use my camera and photoshop.

     

     Back of my LBV. note the waterbag. its the SAF D&G issued one. the original SOURCE 3 litre ones were stolen quite some time ago.

    front of lbv.

    inside mesh.

    something cool i got from the older LBVs. magazine dividers! easier to separate the snickers from the mars bars.

    standard assault bag. issued to everyone. carried by everyone.

    back MOLLE of assault bag. useless to everyone.

    some random notebook i got for participating in a demonstration.

    inside of random notebook.

    uniform! no rank as yet. the store ic indented the wrong ones.

    well yeah! i finally got the links working. hope everyone likes it, and more importantly, contribute! i'd see what else i can take from my kit bag later. any requests? i hope to get a shot of the ILBV soon. let everyone see whats its like as opposed to what other army's have.

    cheers!
