    11 week. 4 basic eng, 7 weeks specialisation.
    But from what I heard, sign on don't get the auto enter CBRE specialisation. must complete 7 weeks specialisation of field engineer then go over to CBRE/EOD.
    Duration is quite long lor.
    But why sign on Spec? Officer would be better imo in terms of pay.
    If I not wrong, regular is $400 more.
    Road march in mop4, play sports in gas mask, run with gas mask.
    Weekends will be burnt, big events also have to go down, public holidays with big scale events also burnt.
    EOD is different though, the suit is 30+ kg with a little fan in the 'helmet' compartment.
    Then heard got quite alot of studying about bombs, how it can be activated, how to find the main charge, where is the fuse, all types of bombs around the world. How to determine if it's a fake or not.

    EOD means EVERYDAY ON DUTY. standby 24/7. Shopping centre call you, you go down.

    In both cases, the sweating in the suits is yucky. :D
