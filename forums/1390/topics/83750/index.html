    Actually, because you excused 40 days, it really depend what you do or did not do in BMT that will determine whether you pass BMT.

    As you may now know or dunno. IPPT, Range and IFC are not veto factor in passing BMT anymore.

    What MO is trying to do is maybe during BMT time you participate in all activities regardless if you pass tests. This way you NEED NOT repeat BMT.

    If you OOT/OOC in midst of BMT you WILL re-BMT.

    There are 3 outcomes from BMT.

    Completed BMT (passed all test + IPPT, SOC, IFC) = met all requirements and eligible for leadership training (OCS/SISPEC).

    Attended BMT (pass some, failed some, failed at least 1 of 3 in IPPT, SOC, IFC but overall 75% attendance of all lessons) = NEED NOT repeat BMT, not eligible for Commander. And block leave burned.

    RE-BMT = poor attendance and attitude towards BMT training. WILL REPEAT BMT.
