    For NSF Concession Card, you got to apply through your Manpower/Admin Branch. If you newly enlisted, you are entitled to apply for one through them. If you will be enlisting in April 2005, a concession card will be applied automatically by Personnel Service Centre.

    After obtaining the card, you got to make a refundable deposit of $3. From there, you can sign up any of the bus or mrt or bus&mrt plans.
