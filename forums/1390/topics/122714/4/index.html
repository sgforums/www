    Clerks can be posted to anywhere in SAF (includes Air, Navy, Army), regardless is that unit a combat unit or not.

    U only perform clerical duties, so really SAF dun really care if they put u in a combat unit or non-combat unit. U also do the same clerical stuff. The only difference is the job scope that u will be doing, i.e. Logistic clerk lah, Manpower Clerk lah, S2 branch Clerk lah, MT Line Clerk lah, Commander Runners, RSM runners, etc, etc. (Yup, clerks also got alot of types one)

    And as clerks, u will be following the unit's regime, i.e, if u kena a stay-in unit, u most likely stay-in unless of course, the unit's regime doesn't require clerks to stay in,etc (That is why alot of clerks are hoping to kena Mindef or CMPB where they dun have stay-in)
