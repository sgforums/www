    no official news from Mindef... so unable to comment.

    ===============================================
    L1 - Fit for field duties including front-line combat arms duties. e.g. combat 'hot' zone. Able to bear arms.

    L2 - Fit for field duties, but only in Unit HQ or rear areas. e.g. combat 'safe' zone. Able to bear arms.


    L3 - Fit for operational duties at HQ / Camp / Bases only. Not fit for field duties/ exercises.

    L9 - Suitable for peacetime sedentary duties at Bases. Not fit for operational duties even in Bases.
