    Originally posted by eac:
    absolutely no typo error... the mc is issued out and converted to a .pdf adobe reader document b4 printing by the medical info system.

    normal duties means normal duties under the pes e1l9 medical status.

    usually the normal duties part is to add on any excuses inclusive to accompany the pes status for more info as such: excuse heavy loads perm... excuse loud noise environment... excuse lower limb activities... excuse no4 uniform perm... etc.etc.

    and mostly likely u will be revocationed to a non-combatant vocation, but still the same reservist unit. for field duties... the l9 means that yes u still may need to go outfield but likely for a very short while. most likely u r stationed in base camp (rear end) while outfield.
    How can E1L9 go outfield when L3 is not even fit for field duties/exercises?

    PES E grading will be followed by the L-Code (Land Deployability Code) as follows:
    L3: Fit for operational duties at Bases only. Not fit for field duties/exercises
    L9: Suitable for peacetime sedentary duties at Bases. Not fit for operational duties even in Bases
