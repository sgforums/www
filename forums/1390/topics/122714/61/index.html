    This is called a Medical Board PES Review whereby 1 Chairman of Regular rank (MAJ) with at least a specialist in medicine/additional medical qualifications, and with 2 NSF doctors with CPT rank with an basic MBBS medical degrees to board you officially with an official PES grading endorsed by the Medical Board Review in the CMPB medical informations systems. The reason for 3 doctors to further discuss your PES and as well as other complicated cases is to provide a fair and just PES grading based on your specialist memos on a military standing.
