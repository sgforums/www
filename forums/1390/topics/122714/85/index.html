    First of all, you don't demand this and that. Anybody, even Doctors don't like this attitude. Instead, you are to request for. Doctors are professionals with a medical practising licence. Nobody is your servant. So, don't show "that stuck up face" at anybody, especially when you're there to request an "influencing memo".

    1) For this case, even with a specialist memo as the supporting document, the Medical Board will at most review this as a within mild PES C range status, with a possible permanent excuse heavy loads.

    2) A simple specialist memo from a public hospital will do. This is not so complex/ complicated enough to warrant a private specialist to write the memo. Don't waste money.

    3) Tinnitus, dependable on severity, e.g. mild/ moderate/ severe, will at most be given an excuse of loud noise environment (e.g. rifle range/ grenade throw) to accompany the PES. Nothing more to the PES.

    As always, please refer to this as the general guide:
    www.sgforums.com/forums/1390/topics/392446
