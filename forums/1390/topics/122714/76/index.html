    I've recently passed out of BMTC under PES C BMT due to my 6 months temporary PES status. Just wanna know, will I be recoursed into PES A/B BMT after the PES ends? Cos I saw somewhere saying that once a person passes his BMT, he will not need to recourse again.

    Hi Papercreme,

    If you remain as a Pes C, you will not need to go for a BMT recourse. However, if your Pes is upped to A or B, there is a very slight chance that you may need to go for a BMT recourse. 

    As for your letter, I will suggest that you look for your unit's MO first and show it to him, and wait for him to refer you to CMPB.

    Cheers.
