    temp downgrade dont get u a new vocation at all... u will still stick with ur coy within ur unit... e.g. combat man temp 3 months will temp attach to the coy hq or hq battalion for some other work... e.g. admin assistant... and all that...

    c2 defines the degree of the medical condition, while l1 is e deployment code. l1 still means u can still be send to frontline direct combat... pls read the very 1st page of the thread to understand in depth...

    e.g. a c2l2 signaller is under hq battalion estab. and operates at hq level normally. but if there is a need to send him to field, he will go to the combat zone defined as the secondary "safe" combat zone, as the direct combat frontline zone is further away...
