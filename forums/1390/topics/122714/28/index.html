    u posted in the wrong thread.... but anyway ill answer anyhow...

    no need to do anything.... mindef information system will know when u r grad from poly... the poly information system will auto update the mindef.

    u can stay in poly for the max. national limit of 5 years cos of repeat semesters without doing anything for mindef.

    the mindef will send u the e-online registration notification in ur final year, in it there will be a userid n password for u to register at www.ns.sg under the pre-enlistee tab.

    entry to ocs/sispec is not auto, its based on ur own merit and performance assessed by the platoon commander during bmt. even u r a jc/poly grad, u can be just another enlistee rank. eg. private, lcp, cpl.

    jc/poly grads will enter tekong for bmt, where most other non-tertiary educated will mostly go for the direct unit mono-intake.
