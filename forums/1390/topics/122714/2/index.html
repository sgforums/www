    1)If one failed the Napfa test, he will enter NS for the first 3 weeks + 2 weeks ? Then another few months for BMT?

    if fail NAPFA (IPPT), u will go for 1 month of physical training followed by 2 months of BMT. if u pass, u just go for the 2 months BMT.

    2)Then after BMT, i will be posted out to my respective vocation?

    that will depend on your final IPPT result and performance, if u pass and is of leadership quality, u might be selected for command school like SISPEC and OCS. if not u will be posted to a unit as a man. vocation depends on many factor like manpower, your PES status etc.

    3)For the first 2 weeks in NS, what kinda of life is in there? Is it tough training? or is it lectures and some basic training?[b/]

    lots of asmin work, physical training, learn basic military knowledge like marching, drills, ranks etc and lectures.

    a) since, I dont require IPPT, so I can eat and shake leg until Oct?

    Even though you dun need to take IPPT, doesn't mean that you cannot do simple exercises. It is still best to do some training.

    b) since I dont need IPPT, why still need Modified BMT ?

    BMT stands for Basic Military Training, it is not an exercise course. For PES A, B, BP ppl. IPPT is one of the passing criteria. You can count urself heng already that IPPT is not one of ur passing criteria.
    You will still be required to learn and be tested upon on ur field craft, weapon handling, etc.
    (Fire weapon need IPPT one ah )

    c) currently acceptable weight, will there be problem if I go in overweight?
