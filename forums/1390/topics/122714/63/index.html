    Please declare your medical conditions honestly and truthfully.

    It's within PES C range, with supporting documents such as medical specialist memo/ optimetist memo to be given to the CMPB doctor during the checkup.

    It's advised not to 'act garang' and ask for a PES A/ B when the doctor classified you as PES C/ E. You will end up getting your medical conditions worsened while serving.

    Yes, combat-fit command schools like SISPEC/OCS only accept PES A/B.
    However, non-combat specialist vocations such as logistics supervisor ("storeman sergeant")/ transport supervisor ("driver sergeant")/ arms technician/ administrative supervisor ("clerk sergeant") can accept PES C personnel.
