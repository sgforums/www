    1 Sep 05, 14:54
    K... if you are PES A or B... forget about 8 to 5 unless you end up in service side.

    So if you don't want to be frontline... that means service... so you need to get a rank of Third Sergeant (3SG) to get a pay of $500.

    If you want to get $500.... then best if you become a COMBAT (front line) Corporal. But you will not get this rank until you near ORD.

    Where to seek redress, they are plenty of redress avenues PROVIDED you have solid and valid reason you are treated unfairly.

    This would be things like, I am asked to work day and night because i am a chinese, or i am the only one in the entire company being asked to go for field camp while the company stays in bunk.

    If you are PES C, chances of getting 8 to 5 is very high. But same rules apply if you want to get $500 pay. This case only way is to get 3SG rank.

    As for going overseas, do you mean posted there or go overseas for training?

    For posting there, you MAY have the choice to say no. BUT if no one says yes, you will be randomly chosen. Overseas posting is the only other way you can get good pay without getting rank. A LCP to CPL may get aroudn $800-1000 monthly.

    For training there, you cannot say no. but you can don't go based on medical/personal reasons. For medical, it has to be, an injury WARRANTING a temporary downgrade, or you currently have severe contagious illness. Things like flu, fever, you still go.
