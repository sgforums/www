    Its divided into waves. The first wave will start at ard 5+pm. The last wave will start when the sun already set. It will be difficult for them to spot you since all of you are wearing helmet and all look the same. Even if you tell them to go to your rest point, they wont be able to find you too. 

    I was the first batch to do the 24km march in mainland Singapore. Rest time varies with the time your platoon/coy takes to reach the rest point. I suggest you to keep talking to your friends throughout the march. The toughest part is the last few km where most if not all will be too tired to talk.

    Good luck and enjoy your 24km since this is the last time you can do a route march with all your friends in Tekong =D
