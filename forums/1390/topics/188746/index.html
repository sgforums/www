    You can call up to ask if u are not sure, short list as guide:-
    1.) No.4 (duh...)
    2.) PT kit (inclusive of ur PT shoes)
    How many u must bring depends on urself and ur ICT period.
    3.) Beret (for parade)
    4.) Jockey cap

    Depnding on ur vocation and ur ICT nature:-
    3.) SBO
    4.) Helmet
    5.) Full-pack

    standard
    6.) toiletries
    7.) slippers

    Then the others I dun need to list liao right, u also know the "by left" theory.

    Driving in or riding in is usually not a problem unless the camp doesn't have enough parking lots. U can always call up to check. If u can drive in is the best, contact any friend that is driving in then carpool in. Usually, administrative matters and bunk settling in can take some time so if got car can dump all the barang barang in the car first, settle liao then bring it to the bunk.

    Oh, if u dye ur hair until golden lion or red rooster, remember to dye back black and go for hair cut, bo pian, army standard, dun wait until go in liao then kena screwed by the RSM.
