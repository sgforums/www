    Assuming ur rifle is empty, uncocked with the bolt carrier in the forward position.

    Remove empty magazine from M-16

    Swap and insert loaded magazine, check that its loaded. Slap the magazine into reciever hard.

    Point rifle down range, pull charging handle all the way back n release.

    Switch to safe, and press the forward assist a few times.

    Wait for command, "Firer watch ur front," or "Own time own target"

    Switch to semi, take aim, and squeeze trigger.
