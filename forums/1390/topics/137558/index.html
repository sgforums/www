    Originally posted by Littlemouse:
    changi air base as an RP
    Congratulations Littlemouse, welcome to Changi Air Base (CAB)

    Yes, for other RPs, its a 2-week course. But yur unit is Field Defence Squadron,
    so u will have to undergo 3-months of stay-in training. So bring ALL yur army barang barang on the reporting day.

    During the 3 months, u will go thru RP tutorials, telling u abt the laws of SAF and yur jurisdiction. You will also go thru basic infantry skills training like weapons handling, platoon/section live firing, chemical defence, FIBUA, foot drills...etc.

    After training, u may be posted to another Air Base depending on manpower requirement, but most likely u will remain at Changi.

    U will be performing RP duties in shifts, and dun expect 1 day, 1 day off. FDS does not have that luxury. The shift situation changes all the time, but the best u will get is probably 2 days on, 1 day off.
