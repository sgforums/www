    The ORIGINAL grand plan was BMTC is supposed to have washing machine and BMTC is designed to have a washing bay for all the machines in every company.

    But thanks to our "pioneer" batch and subsequent early ones.

    Lazy to wash, put everything inside. SBO, helmet, PT shoes, very muddy No 4.

    Washing machine obviously cannot tahan such load mah. So in around 2000-2001. The contractor gave up repairing and just withdrew all machines.

    To the threadstarter --> bring some detergent, how you wash your laundry depends on your bunk. If you are 2nd floor, you are in a bad/good luck.

    Good luck, you are above radiators and laundry dry within 3-4 hours. But bad luck, inconsiderate recruits above you will spit or hang wet laundry that will keep wetting your laundry.
