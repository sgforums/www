    STOMPer Cloud Leonhart is unhappy about the strict hair length for students and NSmen in Singapore. He wonders why it is necessary for NSmen to cut their hair short for reservist duty.

    "I am currently a NSman and go for my reservist duty once a year.
     
    "Every time I go for reservist duty, I have to cut my precious hair which I have kept to quite a long length and I feel my heart bleeding when I have to cut it.
     
    "I like to style my hair and I envy those Taiwanese actors and Japanese with long and nice hairstyles. 

    "From what I have noticed, the students in Taiwan and Japan are free to have their own hairstyles and can even colour them. They have long side-burns and long bangs. 

    "Their schools are open-minded and accept their hairstyles. The students and NSman here must have a slope at the back and short side burns. 

    "I am thinking that if we still cannot keep the hairstyle we want if we still young, how to have nice hairstyle when we reach middle-aged, when we have the high chances for hair loss and white hair. 

    "I don't mind going for reservist every year, but I hope the SAF will not be so strict in hair length and force me to cut slope and destroy my image. 

    "Hope Singapore will accept the hairstyle trends from Japan. Don't think that cool hairstyle is only for gangsters!" he said.
