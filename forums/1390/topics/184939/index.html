    You will need to go for your NAPFA test as soon as possible at the stated testing centres, otherwise you will be drafted into the PTP batch when CMPB enlists you for National Service.

    Also you will need to attain at least a Silver credit to miss out on the enlistment batching. If you attain your Silver for your NAPFA after the enlistment notice comes, you will need to inform CMPB and so they can reschedule your enlistment.
