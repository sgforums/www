    Its always a guard personnel to find his own replacement should he not be able to do his duty. Certain units will plan reserve but not all will so its up to the individual guards to do it. Since its tekong guard duty which is not counted at all by unit, its not going to be easy to find replacement.

    If you die die cannot find replacement, you will have to report for guard duty and see whether they can give you some slight straining duty to do.

    Getting MC etc won't be useful since being on MC means you still need find someone to replace you or else you are the 1 who get in trouble.
