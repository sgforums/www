    if u do not have any offs and will like to use the TP test as an excuse to take day off, i suggest u forget about it. u are not the first one i came across asking the same question. and i always tell them, why must the whole company training schedule be affected just because one selfish guy wants to take off for his TP test?

    and what if everyone in the company wants to take off for every petty little reason? today is my mum's birthday, can i take off? today my pet died, can i take off? u get the idea? i hope u understand.
