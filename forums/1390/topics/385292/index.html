    1. OoC rate is quite low actually. 

    2. Yes. 

    3. No SOC, no route march, a few out field training but very fun one, so don't worry. 

    4. Out of training is you are going to be placed in the next batch of trainees. Out of course means you will be posted somewhere else. 

    5. Don't worry, they will make you get a silver one. If not, good luck, even more training for you. 

    6. It is quite impossible to fail. Seriously. 

    7. No. 

    8. No. High chance you will go Gombak though, where you are stuck with guard duty for the rest of your 2 years. Suay will go Dog Unit or Detention Barracks. Lucky will go SB/SIB, where you will come to work in office wear and shake leg for 2 years. Even luckier will go LECC, where you will go raiding bunks, catching people, and get to wear the No. 1 uniform.
