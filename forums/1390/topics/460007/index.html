    1. Lodge a police report at your Neighbourhood Police Post/ Centre (NPP/ NPC).

    2. Submit the police report to Chief Clerk.

    3. Submit a new application form for a replacement ISAC card.

    4. Pay $80 card loss/ replacement penalty fee.
