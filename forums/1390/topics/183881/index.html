  • TooFree
    26 Mar 06, 22:22
    I agree with you that a proper level of regimental discipline has to be maintain at all time. Discipline and bearing of our troopers are certainly reflective of how our armed forces project itself to the rest world, and possibly deter any potentially aggressor.
    However, I am for operational discipline (OD) over regimental discipline (RD). Emphasis on the latter is no longer so much applicable over the former now as the battlefield strategy and doctrine have shifted away. Most importantly, operational discipline, readiness and teamwork win war, not regimental discipline.
    Allocation of resources (time) is another aspect. Largely because we are a conscript force. As a commander of any strike or frontline unit, rightfully, the allocation for OD and RD in a time constrain space, very much should be tilted in favor of OD.

    I am neither dismissing nor am I proposing to abolish RD but instead to give a more modern approach to warfare in the new era and how resources should be channelled more to training of troops where the resultant is a more impactful punch in the battlefield.
