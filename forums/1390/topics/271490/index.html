    ord date is set, unless u awol/ kena charged DB for more than a few days then ord date extend.

    when admission to hospital, already show saf 11b. so saf still pay, even discharged after ord date.

    ffi for ord is to be done in unit medical centre, and chop and signed by saf camp mo. official ffi form is also issued by saf medical centre. although the test r very minor and simple, only camp mo is authorised to sign the form. the ffi is carbon copied 1, 3 pages. one is kept by medical centre, the other is to be given to the unit clerk, in hand he exchanged for ur pink nric.
