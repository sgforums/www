    u dun necessarily need to have good fitness. it is a bonus not a must.

    You dun need to have extremely good academic results. But the intake will consist mainly of 'A' level or Diploma holders. There are exceptions as well.

    You will not have any chance to in commandos if you are not selected to go for its BMT in the 1st place. They conduct their own BMT. It is a different story if you are a regular. They will select people to go based on their potential: their situational awareness, IQ, etc etc. You can however volunteer when u go for your pre enlistment medical checkup.

    Good luck.
