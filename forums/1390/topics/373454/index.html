    From what I can remember... If he goes to the polyclinic and gets a referral from the polyclinic to a government hospital he may have to pay for the initial consultation, but as a subsidised patient. Subsequently, he can claim from the SAF under a co-payment scheme.

    If he goes straight to a government hospital and seeks a specialist, he will pay private consultation rates for the first visit, subsequent visits should be under SAF co-payment.

    I may be inaccurate with my information as it has been years, so perhaps people who are serving now might want to clarify, as I'm not too sure how the polyclinic system works?
