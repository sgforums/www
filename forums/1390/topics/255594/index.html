Navy base open house this weekend

    Date:
    26 to 27 May 07

    Time:
    9:00am to 7:00pm

    Venue:
    Changi Naval Base

    Event Description:
    To commemorate of the RSN 40th Anniversary, the Republic of Singapore Navy (RSN) will be hosting an Open House at Changi Naval Base.

    The open house will showcase the growth of the RSN and the capabilities that it has developed through the last 40 years, and provide a flavour of the direction it is taking as it transforms into the 3rd Generation Navy. It will provide visitors an opportunity to come up-close and personal with many of the RSN's warships and weapon systems. Visitors will also be treated to two days of vibrant entertainment, exciting interactive games and plenty of sea rides.

    Getting There:
    Admission to the Navy Open House is free. Entry will strictly be by the free shuttle bus, available daily from Singapore Expo Hall 1. View map of pick-up point.

    First shuttle to Changi Naval Base - 8:30am
    Last shuttle to Changi Naval Base - 5:00pm

    Visitors are advised to take the MRT or public transport to Singapore Expo. Drivers are advised to park their cars at Singapore Expo as no cars (including those in taxis) will be allowed to drive beyond the Tanah Merah Ferry Terminal junction without a valid pass.
