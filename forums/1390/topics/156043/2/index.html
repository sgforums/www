    Cdo education qualification.

    In Cdo, there used to be 2 intake per calender year. May/june and Nov/Dec. One intake for Diploma holder and one intake for 'A' Level students. In these intake, there are some with lower education levels.

    As for the SOF, It a different ball game. Cdo is not link to SOF. But Cdo will be given more opportunity to join them, basically because they are mentally and physical fit. Other will be from Army unit and Navy. (Air Force rare).

    3 phase of selections. 1st - Written Test under extreme temperature can last from early morning till late nights. - mental endurance (That's enough for you)

    2nd - IPPT test - physcial endurance. Gold.

    3rd - Attachment to SOF .......Inclusive of diving plaform....swimming in pool with no. 4 (cannot remember how many laps liao) After swimming, battle order run through trial, memory correct should be 20/30km. - Physical/ Mental endurance. (Pls note there will be some unsual activities late nights. Cannot disclosed liao)

    You wait for results..... Pass good/ failed go back to unit kena booo....

    If pass, intend training as SOF (You are still not in yet dun be happy)

    Most fundamental difference is that STAR is from the SPF while SOF is from the SAF... STAR would handle most of the domestic affairs (eg. supporting other police units, high risk arrest, criminal hostage rescue etc...) SOF wise, they would handle things of 'national/sensitive' aspect (eg. plane hijack, terrorist threats, classifed missions etc...).

    SOF are trained in a wider aspect as compared with the STAR due to their different mission requirements (not that the latter is lousier!!!). Extra things would be things like diving and parachuting (Static line, HALO and HAHO) etc...
