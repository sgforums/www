    I'm not sure the 1st batch of Primus-trained gunners have even ORD'd yet - have they? Even if so, it's unlikely that the weapon has been extensively introduced in the reservists - maybe in the near future, but it's still early days.

    Until then, the unit concerned may have to convert weapons. I myself joined another (IHL) unit after ORD, but my former NSF BN couldn't continue with the old M113 SP Mortars cuz they don't have it for reservist. The unit ended up spent its final "active"-time weeks familiarizing with the towed mortars, which consisted of field exercises & proficiency tests.

    It was mostly the same with past 155mm-howitzer units when they had to switch from the M-71 to the FH-88 to the FH-2000. FYI Reservists still presently train with the latter 2 weapons. The M-71's from the 1970s were supposed to have been phased out long ago, but some military watchers speculate the SAF still have them stored away somewhere.
