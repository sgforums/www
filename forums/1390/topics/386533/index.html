    There are usually many different enlistment batches for the different PES. If you have not gotten your enlistment letter yet, you might be enlisting in Jun in the new PES E BMT.

    Not everyone can enlist at the same time due to large number of people so some will obviously enlist earlier than others.

    You can check your enlistment date if its already decided at ns.sg but since you have not receive any letters, its assume that your date has not been decided and thus checking there won't give you any new information.

    For any further questions, please post in the Enlist/BMT FAQ thread and not start a new thread on this topic.
