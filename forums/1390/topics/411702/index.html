    Dear Bros, I went down to my DIV HQ this very evening to summit documentary proof for my exit permit application. (sailing as a navigational officer around the world).

    Cutting to the crux, I got into a lively exchange with one of the officer there who told me that any application for future exit permits after my current one might be rejected as I do not have a single ICT with the unit in their record. (FYI - I do not have any upcoming ICT to clear) He wanted me to call them up to schedule an ICT for me after my voyage so that 'they can answer'. I am also supposed to get my shipping company to set aside time for me to do my ICT. To say that I was surprised to learn of this new development is probably an understatement.

    When I called my HQ to seek further clarification, I was told that the absence of ICTs in my record will have no bearing on the success of my exit permit application if I have a valid reason.

    Any bros here, seafaring or not, with similar experiences to share?
