    If you came from a combat unit, you will most likely go back to your previous unit if they haven't ROD. SAF likes to keep these units together.

    If you were from school or some other non-combat unit then it's a little more of a crapshoot. Good chance you might end up in PDF if you are combat fit. Although if you are trained in a particular combat field, i.e. if you were scout instructor during your NS, you might get posted to one of the combined arms divs to make up for people downgrading etc.

    If you are on exit permit for studies, then you are on IHL and will go back to unit when you complete your studies.

    If you on exit permit, no worries, you shouldn't be liable for IPPT.

    So as long as you on exit permit, relax and enjoy the time.
