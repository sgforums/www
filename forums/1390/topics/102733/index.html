    Block force is set up to over-watch an axis leading to the engagement site. Basically, it is a section or platoon size force tasked to engage enemy reinforcements trying to reach the area of engagement, or like what the Americans are doing in Fallujah now, to engage enemy forces retreating from the area of engagement. Usually the support platoon will detach some elements including 84 or GPMG team , depending whether the attacking force can spare them , and together with some "lobo" infantry, they will form the block force detachment.

    There are bigger disposable missiles than the LAW (which is obsolete)... late Vietnam war era. I believe there is a new version of Armbrust , which some units are trialling.
