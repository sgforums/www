  • service_238
    27 Jan 09, 02:04

    Normally POP Block leave consumes 3-4 days of your annual leave. The rest will be given as off days.

    Examples:
    Enlist in Jan, 4 days block leave = 10 days of leave left
    Enlist in Apr, 4 days block leave = 6 days of leave left
    Enlist in July, 4 days block leave = 3 days of leave left
    Enlist in Sept, 4 days block leave = 1 day left.

    As for those who enlist in December.. I go back office check their leave cards first haha..

    The trend is usually the amount of leave left is almost as good as being enlisted on the day that u get posted to your new unit.

    All NSFs get 29 days of leave due to rounding up of leave in your enlisting year and ORD year.
