    The official requirement to "pass" BMT is,

    Pass IPPT, fieldcamp, BTP and 75% attendance. Then you will be awarded PASS BMT.

    If none of the requirements above are met but 75% attendance is, then you will be awarded Attended status.

    However, the COs so far have been letting people off and give them Attended regardless they met the 75% attendance.

    The only people who recourse now are those who OOT'ed early or those no awarded "attended". The latter, however, is rare.

    Because his unit COs will just train him on the items he missed in BMT proper.
