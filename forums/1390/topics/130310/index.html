    Originally posted by @BubbLeS~:
    Do all uniform personnel have to go thru physical training? I went to cmpb, there are many in uniform doing clerical work. What service vocations are there in SAF?
    The service vocations? I would deem the logistics/engineering fields as service side, although Combat Service Support (CSS) can also fall under this field.

    All uniformed personnel definitely have to undergo physical training, regardless of being a Officer (Maintenance Engineering Officer/Logistics Officer) or a Spec.

    Logistics is the key to victory

    For the logistics officer course, there are 3 routes to choose. You can be vocated under HQ MES (Maintenance Engineering Officer) or HQ S&T (MTO/DyS4/S4) or Ammo Base (Ammunition Technical Officer). Training for the officer course is as follows: 1mth TST, followed by a 4.5mth STI & II, and Pro Term lasting the further 3.5 months (I think the length of months is roughly there ) During Pro Term you advance to School of Logistics (SOL) for a 2 months course on basic logistics for all, followed by diversification into the respective fields - MEOs and ATOs go to OETI, MTOs & S4s go HQ S&T to do OJT.

    So which is your choice?
