    Though all male Singaporeans and Permanent Residents will be enlisted for full-time National Service (NSF) upon reaching the age of 18 years, those who are at least 16 and a half years old and wish to be enlisted earlier may do so under the Voluntary Early Enlistment Scheme (VEES). However, they have to obtain their parents' consent and be medically and physically fit.

    If you are above 16 and a half years old and wish to be enlisted for full-time National Service before reaching 18 years old, you may apply for VEES as follows:

    -Download and print a copy of the VEES application form from NSPortal under Pre-enlistee-forms-download.
    -You may also contact NS eService Centre at telephone number 1800-3676767 for any assistance.
    -Send the duly completed form to CMPB by post at the following address:

    Central Manpower Base
    3 Depot Road
    #02-09
    SINGAPORE 109680

    Upon receipt of your application, CMPB will reply to you for necessary follow-up.
