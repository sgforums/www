    First of all, after u hand in ur application form, dey will contact u to go to down to CMPB for a career talk if u havnt been to 1 yet.

    Wait for them to process everything and get ready to receive your money!

  • pika1987
    16 Oct 04, 23:10
    hi, i am under the JPSDS scheme for the Army...
    my vocation is Armour Infantry Leader, i chose to join Guards at first, but they say Guards dont wanna recruit regulars liao....
    my process of signing up is done, and i am getting $1000 per month, credited into my bank account. the transfer is done quarterly, meaning every 3 months transfer $3000...

    1st. u sign up, via internet, or personally at CMPB SAF Career Centre

    2nd. they will send a letter to ask u to go for an interview.

    3rd. medical checkup at CMPB, and determine ya PES status, see u combet fit anot, and see if u fit to join the vocation u chose anot. its the same as those enlistees one.

    4th. they will ask u to go down to CMPB for contract signing, but muz listen to briefing first. to let u noe the consequences if u break bond, and the benefits u can get if u stay in the army for a long term. they will also give u a form to gather information on your 2 guarantors, or surety.

    5th. after u find ya 2 guarantors, fill up the form and send back to CMPB. (they will give u a free envoplone at stage 4 also, dont need postage one.)

    6th. call the DXO and make an appointment to get ya 2 guarantors and u to sign the deed. so u can get the money, and SAF dun need to worry u run away, coz got 2 guarantors to find and get the money... again, the officer will tok to the guarantors abt the contract and their responsibilities.

    7th. the last stage! after everything done, they will send u a letter to ask u to SURRENDER ya PINK I/C...... to change to a GREEN I/C.... yup, now i only got green i/c, no more pink i/c..... and ya service status will be known as REGULAR....

    yup...... thats all!
