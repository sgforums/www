    hmm... good qn~!

    1. Identity Card (IC) / Birth Certificate (both also need)
    2. Family Particulars
    3. Educational Certificates (GCE O' / N' levels)
    4. Sec sch leaving certificate / testimonials / cca records
    5. letter of acceptance from polytechnic (JPSDS) / ITE (SISS)
    6. All polytechnic/ite results (if u sign up during ya course of study, otherwise unappciable)
    7. Medical Screening questionaire
    8. Parent's consent form... (sign on of cuz need parents to noe mah.. haha..)
    9. latest napfa results (valid for 1 year from date of test)
    10. certificate of service (NSmen or Ex-Regular)(if any)
