    yes. you can book for IPT directly and once u completed the IPT, your IPPT for current window will be consider clear, no matter you pass or failed. But pls be reminded that the IPT have limited slots only and usually need to be booked early.
    And sometime, even if u clear IPT or IPPT before reservist, your CO will still want you to participate in the reservist IPPT, just to make up the number. This happen in my ICT, when we got a new CO. All those who passed outside have to take the IPPT again, but then the CO is not concern about the results, but more on the participation.
