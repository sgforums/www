    I dun noe about the current SAF food that many of you guys have tasted. But to me, it sounded pretty tasty and good, as compared to my time in the '70s.

    Those times ...them cooks were NS enlistees.....who dun qualify for other vocations. They are mostly pai kias...... the one who toss numbers about when they dun like your asking why the food is so and so. And u noe wat....they do the cooking for us. Chief cook was a regular, Staff sargent or WOs.

    You can find worms, sand, in the vegetables, small sand pebbles in them rice, rice have the persistant light yellowish tinge and it smells too, for not being washed properly or not washed at all. Fish have the rotten kinda smell and taste too!! They gave lots of meat, vegetables, fish and fruits for lunch and dinner. Them cooks just dump em onto your tray. Take extras no problem, but throw away...kena take extra guard duty or worse DB!! Duty BOS or COS always patrol at the rubbish point to catch anyone throwing food....only bones and unedible stuff can be thrown.....

    Breakfast may be plain stale bread, yes stale, you can smell em..hahaha.... sometimes plain tastless bee hoon. And yes.....half boiled eggs!!!....I luv em...can take as many as you want...but have to finish what you take...no wasting of food! Coffee, tea? not too bad though

    Night snack, mostly red bean, green bean soup....Man I like it ...it is so so thick with so many beans in it!!!!!

    During my time, the above can be considered as food from heaven!

    That said, dun noe why NSF guys these days still wanna complain about army food.
