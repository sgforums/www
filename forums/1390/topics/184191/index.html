    Originally posted by normanlai:

    Try the following:
    1. Explain the rationale behind the purpose of the task
    2. Set small targets for them to achieve
    3. Figure out a reward at the end of a period of time.. say 1 week or 2.
    4. Get them going...
    5. Dun forget to praise them for their good job, or comment on their quality of work if it's less than ideal.

    NSF are still human beings afterall,... some praises will go a long way, for morale...

    As a superior to them (I suppose you are), you have to set your expectations lower, else their work will most likely be not up to par (I assume you are a good producer of work as well).

    Hope this helps..
