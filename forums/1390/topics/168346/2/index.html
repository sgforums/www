    i think the chances of that is rather low. if i recall correctly, SAF only revise the rank structure during thr early 90s. That was when warrant officer's ranks goes to the shoulder.

    The reason is due to the long wait for promotion in rank, and with a high chances of screwing it up over thr yrs, a lot of regulars quited the forces during this period (1980s to early 1990s).
    (to promote to a CPL or 3SG take u 3-5 yrs)
    (3SG directly to SSG take as long as 10 yrs)

    To them there was no incentive (money terms) as no promotion in rank means that ur pay will stalled for a very long time. When the money is no good, no one wants to continue. ( people got family to feed and stuff like tat) in the end SAF revise the rank structure to make it more attractive to the people. Resulting in 3SGs in the thousands~
