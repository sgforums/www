  • Rebecca Song
    27 Nov 10, 07:05

    Sure you can go out.  But do not be 'caught' laughing and generaly enjoying yourself. Go somewhere quiet for self reflection, enjoy the scenary at the lakes, like mandai lake, Chinese garden, etc. 

    If you need to meet with friends, go chit-chat.  Make sure it is only one friend, not a group.  Again do not be seen laughing and joking.

    Because long term MC means  you are depressed, and so act so.  Depression could be all types, the bi-polars get 'high' at certain times of the day, usually in the evening.  But the public do not understand.  And you get yourself misunderstood, you could be deemed as a malingerer.  Then it becomes difficult for you to explain your behaviour.
