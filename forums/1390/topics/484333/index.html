    Source: www.mindef.gov.sg

    328 SCE is a field engineer battalion that has the capabilities to overcome man-made and natural obstacles for manoeuvre of combat forces.

    Among its capabilities is the Combat Engineer Tractor (CET) which provides earth moving capability including dozing, levelling, spreading and filling, as well as lifting and recovery functions. It is operated by a two-man crew and has a rocket-propelled anchor to aid in climbing slopes of up to 45 degrees.

    Also in its arsenal is the AMX-13 SM1-Launched Bridge (SLB), which can overcome gaps of 14 meters and can be deployed within 15 minutes with just a two-man crew.
