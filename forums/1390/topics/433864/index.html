    Whether or not you are given leave is the prerogative of your commander.

    If he is feeling generous and wants to give leave, then good for you.

    If he is feeling stingy or you are needed badly in camp, then too bad for you.

    Before you complain, National Service Full-time is exactly what it is: you are a FULL-TIME soldier.

    If you want to do anything else on the side, then your superior may close one eye, but don't expect him to bend over backwards to help you.
