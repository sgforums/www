    Dont be bothered by these things. These adminstrations issue are supposed to be settle by your clerks. 

    The idea of house visiting is really to check on how well you are. And not to spy on your presence. They have make sure you are "fine" in order to clear of your medical FFI, else they will have to submit a lot of documents, incident reports, insurance claims to compensate the injury.
