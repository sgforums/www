    Originally posted by 01024G:
    What is stand by universe and so on?
    stand bys are usually conducted for the purpose of inspection - to ensure quality and standards are met. it could mean standby of operational equipment (making sure it is functional and operational), areas (making sure it has been cleaned properly) and so on.

    it has also been used as a tool for the enforcement of discipline. for such cases, standbys are conducted on anything and everything that could possibly be inspected.

    a standby universe can be said to be the mother of all standbys, as it means, literally, that the person conducting the standby can ask for any item or place or person to be inspected.

    of course, punishment will be awarded for noncompliance or failure to attain a given standard.
