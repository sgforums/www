    SISPEC = School of Infantry Specialists, also Suffer In Silence Plus Extra Confinement.

    anyway there are 2 courses in sispec, the basic being
    BSLC or Basic Section Leader Course, which, if im not mistaken, lasts for 10 wks and ends up with you being a CPL
    the more advanced
    ASLC or Advanced Section Leader Course, i think, lasts for 3 months and ends up with you being a 3SG.

    of course its also possible for one to be posted to the unit / other training schools of other vocations, where they will earn their 3SG ranks.
