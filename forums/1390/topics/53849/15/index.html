    [b]Your Enlistment Day - In-Processing
    (contributed by Ponders)

    If you have any medical or related letters, show it to your Platoon Commander during the first 48 hour compulsory interview. He'd most prolly ask you to bring it to Medical Officer for endorsement.

    As for what the process will be that day, you go to a building, surrender IC, get a piece of paper + green IC and ID tags. (piece of paper goes to parents).

    Then you go auditorium for a rehearsal of the swearing in followed by actual swearing in. Then you go lunch/dinner with parents then you send them off.

    From then on, you collect your equipment, then check the equipment, then try it for size. When you happy, sign. Sometimes you sign first then try the size. (see below)

    You happy liao and once everybody is happy with their equipment... you all go get new hairstyle ($2). Don't bother getting hair cut outside, Tekong barber says it has to be "approved" by them. quite stupid actually because he will say if trimmer cuts anyhair, he will charge $2, then he will press hard to make sure he cuts some hair.

    After new hairstyle it's most probably admin work already. fill up forms, declare drugs abuse, etc etc. You will be briefed on discipline, rules, etc etc.

    Equipment to be issued:
    WHEN you surrender IC, you will be given a black sling pack.

    At the company line (your bunk) you will be issued.
    1 field pack,
    1 black duffel bag with wheels.
    1 helmet + camou net
    2 pairs of boots
    5 green socks 2 white socks (which you won't get to use in school 2)
    5 sets camouflage uniform + sewing kit
    1 pair of running shoes
    2 t-shirt
    2 singlet
    2 shorts
    1 belt
    1 pair of garters
    1 jockey cap
    1 set if 2 mess tin
    1 mess mug
    1 green mug
    1 set of utensil
    1 toothbrush
    1 toothpaste
    1 soap and soap box
    1 dental floss
    1 toiletries bag
    1 toggle rope
    1 webbing (army bra)
    2 water bottle + pouches
    1 pair ear plugs
    2 tin shoe polish + 1 brush
    1 jack knife (victorinox)

    did i miss anything out?

    after 1-2 days, you will be issued
    1 or 2 set of camou cream
    2 insect repellant (burns)
    black rubber bands
    1 notebook
    1 thermometer
    1 Gore-tex jacket (suggest you write name on it, highly priced item as only PES A/B personnel are issued and not replaceable by credit)

    [/b]

    A NSF should familiarize himself with only the following, as long as he's not tasked for form part of a NDP contingent ;-) -

    FOR STANCE (with or without rifle)
    "Sedia" Attention
    "Senang Diri" Stand at ease
    "Rehatkan Diri" Stand easy
    "Kekanan Lurus" Right Dress
    "Pandang Kehadapan Pandang" Eyes Front
    "Kekanan/Kekiri/Kebelakang Pusing" Right/Left/About Turn
    "Diam" Steady
    "Semula" As you were
    "Keluar Baris" Fall out

    FOR MARCHING
    "Dari Kiri/Kanan Cepat Jalan" By the Left/Right Quick March
    "Dari Kiri/Kanan Cepat Lari" By the Left/Right/Front Double
    "Hentak Kaki Cepat Hentak" Quick Mark Time
    "Kiri/Kanan Belok" Left/Right Wheel
    "Berhenti" Halt

    FOR RIFLES
    "Rusok Senja Ta" Shoulder Arm
    "Turun Senja Ta" Order Arm
    "Hormat Senja Ta" Present Arm
    "Julang Senja Ta" High Port
    "Tatang Senja Ta" For Inspection Port Arms
    "Lepaskan Sepering" Ease Springs

    Okay, make that total 20 ~lol~

    A few of the above commands can be elaboratedly prefaced, paticularly with turns, in which case I told my less-familiar recruits to listen out only for last part. For example:

    "Bergerak Kekanan, Bertiga Tiga, Kekanan Pusing" Move to the Right, In threes, Right turn
    "Menghadap Kebelakang/Kehadapan, Kebelakang Pusing" Retire/Advance, About turn

    One more full command, except it's usually done at the end of a rifle-range shoot & thus completely word-associable:

    "Untop Di-perrksa Tatang Senja Ta" For Inspection Port Arms

    Verbally, the SAF style is to slowly "slang" the drill command out into:
    (a) the introductory part (the whole stuff at the beginning),
    (b) the warning part (dragging out a word to signal the impending 3rd part),
    (c) the execution part (sharp burst at the end word/syllable of the command).

    So, "Hormat Senja Ta" becomes "Hormat..Senja-a-a-a-a-ah..[pause]..TAH!" Even a Hokkien peng could predict what to do here, since he would already be in shoulder-arms position - & could phonetically recognized the command for present-arms (plus having countlessly rote-practiced the latter drill beforehand).

    STATIONARY DRILLS
    "Hormat Ke-Hadapan/Ke-Kanan/Ke-Kiri, Hormat" Salute To The Front/Right/Left
    "X-Langkah Ke-Kanan/Ke-Kiri, Gerak" Take X-Steps To The Right/Left (Max. 7 steps)
    "X-Langkah Ke-Hadapan/Ke-Belakang, Gerak" Take X-Steps To The Front/Back (Max. 3 steps)
    "Barisan Hadapan/Tengah, Dari Kanan, Ber-Dua Bilang" Front/Centre Rank From The Right, In Twos, Number Off
    "Akan Jadikan Dua/Tiga/Enam Barisan, Gerak" Squad Will Form Two/Three/Six Ranks
    "Dalam Bukar/Tutup Barisan, Ke-Kanan Lurus" In Open/Close Order, Right Dress
    "Bersurai" Dismiss
    "Be-Tulkan Barisan" Front Dressing

    MARCHING DRILLS
    "Maju" Forward (Done On Hentak Kaki Cepat Hentak)
    "Dari Kanan/Kiri, Belok Kanan/Kiri, Cepat Jalan" From The Right/Left, Right/Left Wheel, Quick March
    "Pandang Ke-Kanan/Ke-Kiri/Ke-Hadapan, Pandang" Eyes Right/Left/Front
    "Memberi Hormat Ke-Kanan/Ke-Kiri/Ke-Hadapan, Hormat" Saluting On The March To The Right/Left/Front, Salute
    "Hormat Memerima Barang Ke-Hadapan Hormat" Saluting On The March To The Front To Receive Articles, Salute
    "Ke-Kanan/Ke-Kiri/Ke-Belakang Pusing" Right/Left/About Turn
    "Tukar Langkah Tukar" Changing Step On The March, Change Step

    THE "MEMARAS" - SIZING
    This set of commands will size the squad/contingent in the way that the shortest person will be at the centre and the tallest at the sides.

    "Yang Tinggi Ke-Kanan, Rendah Ke-Kiri, Dalam Satu Barisan, Gerak" Tallest On The Right, Shortest On The Left, In A Single Rank, Size
    "Dari Ka-Kanan, Ber-Dua Bilang" From The Right, In Twos, Number Off
    "Nombor Gangil Satu Langkah Ke-Hadapan, Nombor Genap Satu Langkah Ke-Belakang, Gerak" Odd Numbers One Pace Forward, Even Numbers One Pace Backwards, Move
    "Orang Yang Di Sebelah Kanan Diam, Barisan Kanan Dan Kiri Pusing" Standfast The Right Hand Man, Ranks Right And Left Turn
    "Sekuad Akan Jadikan Dua/Tiga/Enam Barisan, Cepat Jalan" Squad Will Form Two/Three/Six Ranks, Quick March
    "Sediya" Attention
    "Ke-Kanan Lurus" Right Dress
    "Pandang Ke-Hadapan, Pandang" Eyes Front
