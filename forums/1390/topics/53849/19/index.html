    15 May 05, 14:47
    Originally posted by firewizard:
    er..for regulars...when can they book out? on weekends?..tks for answering my questions =)
    it depends on
    1. the job scope
    2. the unit requirement

    for example, staff officers work 8 to 5 - working you see. however, if you are with a combat unit which is currently in training phase, there may be a requirement for you to stay in, especially if you are doing your PC tour.

    you seemed very concerned about this booking out thing. if you intend to sign on, you must understand that you have to go by the requirements of the work, and this may mean staying in (whether its for an extended period or from time to time). however if you are just going to serve your ns, you can go by the "worst case" assumption (which is usually correct, since a NSF can only be deployed to that many vocations) that you need to stay in. and of course, book out during weekends.
