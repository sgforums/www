    SAF believes in using religions to bless our troops and Colours.

    Religious leaders are called in to bless during events such as OCS Commisioning Parades and SAF Day.

    The Colours in SAF were also blessed by the various religious leaders which is then handed over to the President and trooped.

    The consecration ceremony is carried out by the recognised religions in Singapore and they are as follows, in order of precedence:

    1. Hinduism
    2. Judaism
    3. Zorastrianism
    4. Buddhism
    5. Taoism
    6. Christianity
    7. Islam
    8. Sikhism
    9. BahaÂ’i Faith
    10. Jainism

    Similarly in OCS Commisioning Parades, these leaders are asked to bless the newly graduands. But i believe tt there're only 9 leaders.
