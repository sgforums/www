    I'm currently in the RSN. Since your pes status is E and your reporting venue is CMPB, you will go to your unit directly on your day of enlistment. Someone will pick you up and bring you to your unit.

    After CMPB that line is HQ RSN means you will be posted to any units under HQ RSN. Most of them are in Gombak, with one each at Maju and CMPB itself, and a couple each in Changi and Tuas. You are likely going to Gombak.

    Life is basically clerk job with essential office duties (open and close office aka duty clerk) and necessary camp duties (depending on which camp and whether you are called up or not)

    You just bring your beloved NRIC and your enlistment letter on your enlistment day. You will be given a bag, a notepad, a pair of dogtags and your 2 year companion called the 11B. You will sing the National Anthem and take the Oath at the enlistment venue.

    I have never heard navy clerks staying on their first day of work. You will get to go home on the first day so dont worry.

    MINDEF hotline is crap. You made the wise choice of asking in the forum.
