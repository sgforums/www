  • chanmalichan
    11 Oct 05, 08:27
    Originally posted by Arena:
    So may I know how do a person qualify to attend or undergo the ranger course? any pre-requisite?
    they accepts navy personnel?
    You have to inform your S1 about your interest in the (local) ranger course. Ranger are held in CTW.

    Once there is a course available for you, you will have to go throught a selection test. Unless, you're already a member of the Red-Beret.

    Army guys will have the opportunity first. Every intake, full house from the amry regulators.

    As for navy, you guys have your own professional and non-professional diving course conducted by NDU. If 'I' can remember, most Navy guys have this badge with Para wings. So far have no yet witness any navy guys with ranger tag. Unless it is a transfer of tri-service from Army to either navy or air-force. This is very rare unless you sign as a regulator after your NSF to go into other Tri-service. You will be able to 'display' all your tags but beret colour wil have to change. Chances very very rare.
