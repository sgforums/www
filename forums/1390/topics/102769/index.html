    YES you can indent vegetarian only for 1st and 15th of Lunar Month.

    But you need a lot of reminding to do.

    My recruit asked me about it, i had to do it for him because you cannot denied request pertaining to religious belief. (this person was practising a relaxed form of buddhism)

    First is to write a letter stating the name and particulars of the recruit, write a few copies. 1 goes to CQ, the other goes to Coordinator of the contractor.

    The recruit has to play a part as to remind us that those dates are approaching, so he tells me, i tell CQ, CQ tells the contractor. this preferbly 24 hours before the date.

    IF the 1st and 15th falls during outfield and combat rations are to be consumed. Two ways can be done about this, Remind CQ that there would be a need for vege ration on one day for one person. CQ will then indent from ALB accordingly. The easier way is to out-ration vegetarian food on that day if OC is not particular about recruit having fresh ration for a day.
