    But I'm sure we all had our suck-thumb experiences. Mine happened when I was in SISPEC, we were doing topo and going rather well when one of our detail members lost a radio antenna. The joker behind him wasn't doing his job in noticing stuff that he dropped... but how he lost it is still a mystery. After that no morale liao, but still completed that test anyway.

    The OC unfortunatley, was one of those kind of emperors that not only kill those responsible, but also their family and friends. Thought the other half of us were not directly responsible for the loss (and by virtue of our appointments as front scout and navigator) we still all kenna killed by him, ended up taking two confinement plus 1206 pay cut for the antenna for something we couldn't have prevented unless we were psychic.

    But you know the SAF, how they like to cover all bases. I guess we could have cut our losses by insisting that the loss was not our fault (being new to the SAF and stuck on Tekong, weekends were VERY important), but we decided to stick it out with the joker lost the thing, must be some twisted platoon ethos at work.

    Anyway that incident (on top of others) totally killed my passion for the SAF. Some people are go-getters, they believe in getting rank for personal achievement in the SAF and hence they might take this pretty well. But for me I came in with the naive idea that I would happily be my best for the system defending the nation, but that incident pretty much destroyed that motivation and I started to slack off in like any wiser NSF.

    I guess that made me into that Infantry Specialist who was always finding ways around the rulebook and slipping unoffical "cheat" tips to the recruits under me in BMTC to more TRULY prepare them for army life... as opposed to much of the wayang recruits often learn in Tekong.

    But oh well, that time is over. ORD LOH!
