    17 Mar 06, 22:22
    Originally posted by Serendipity.:
    Sea Soldier here. And i seriously have no idea what is it about? Is it gonna be slack? I seriously hope it will be tough man. Can anyone here enlighten me?
    Welcome to Changi Defense Squadron, alot like FDS, training will be at Changi Navel Training Base, RSS Panglima.

    I OOC'd from SSC8, you'll be from SSC9, the training is extremely physically extensive, very regimental, the bunks are unlike anything in BMT, they are in near perfect shape. Ultimately your job scope will basically be a "super" RP, 12 week course, get ready for lots of IPPT / SOC / Swim training. Min Silver to pass the course. You will learn M203 / SAW / GPMG, and some other theory stuff. After you finish the course, you will go for a 2 week on the job training, and then you continue with duty, at either Changi Navel Base or Tuas Navel Base. If you are from BMT you will be man under CPLs who will also train with you, by the time you go to the posting, they would've just passed out from SISPEC BSLC.

    Learn this name : SWO Balvinder Singh. You'll know why soon

    Course is the most fun you'll have there, but you'll get alot of fun exposure, foreign warships coming in etc, so its not so bad. Just get ready for a big culture shock coming from the Army.

    Enjoy!
