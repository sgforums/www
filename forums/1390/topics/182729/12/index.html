  • kuekj
    15 Sep 06, 22:03
    borntolive: erm medic now should be better la... you should have a good life being in SMTI, speaking from a fellow (though service) medic here..

    be positive about doing the IVs, it's practice makes perfect. There are quite a number of theory lessons you need to go through but once you get the hang of it, you should have no problems Furthermore, Nee Soon Camp has quite good cookhouse food at HQMC side.

    As combat medics you have the chance to be promoted to specs and go thru Combat Medic course spec II (heard some systems change right?) and you will learn a lot. My sgt told us.. in the SAF if you be a medic or be a driver, these are skills which can be used even after you ORD.

    By week 3 or week 4 you will be able to have weekly nights out depending on your platoon's performance. You have plenty of time during the nights out to go Northpoint, Sun Plaza, Thomson Plaza, Junction 8, etc.. of course not all the places within 3 hours la

    Anyway to get to Nee Soon Camp, you have the following options:
    1) Take 169 from Ang Mo Kio Interchange or Woodlands Interchange, or from the Northpoint bus stop at Yishun.
    2) Take 980 or 167 from Sembawang Interchange.
