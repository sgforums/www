    SAF MILITARY EXERCISES

    The Singapore Armed Forces (SAF) will conduct military exercises in Seletar, Marsiling, Pulau Ponggol Timor, Jalan Bahar, Neo Tiew, Lim Chu Kang, Jalan Kwok Min, Tuas, Upper Jurong, Punggol, Hong Kah, Ama Keng, Bedok Jetty, Kranji, Lentor Simpang, Sembawang, Mandai and Pulau Seletar from 11.30 pm on Sat, 26 Mar 2005 to 12.30 am on Sun, 3 Apr 2005.

    The SAF will be conducting military exercises in Pulau Pawai from Sat, 26 Mar 2005 to Sun, 3 Apr 2005, from 7.30 am to 12.30 am.

    The SAF will also be conducting military exercises in Pulau Senang from Sat, 26 Mar 2005 to Sun, 3 Apr 2005 from 7.30 am to 12.30 am.

    The SAF will also be conducting military exercises in Pulau Sudong from Sat, 26 Mar 2005 to Sun, 3 Apr 2005, from 7.30 am to 12.30 am.

    Blanks and thunderflashes will be used. The public is advised not to be alarmed.

    The public is also advised to keep clear of these areas.

    SAF ANNOUNCEMENT

    The islands of Pulau Sudong, Pulau Senang and Pulau Pawai are proclaimed manoeuvring and firing grounds. The public is advised to keep clear of these islands. The public is also advised to stay clear of the prohibited waters off Changi Naval Base and Tuas Naval Base.

    RSAF LIVE-FIRING EXERCISES

    The Republic of Singapore Air Force will be conducting live firing exercises around Pulau Sudong, Pulau Pawai and Pulau Senang from Mon, 28 Mar 2005 to Fri, 1 Apr 2005 from 7 am to 10 pm and Sat, 2 Apr 2005 from 7am to 1pm.

    Flares will be released during the night firing.

    The public is advised to keep clear of Pulau Pawai, Pulau Senang and Pulau Sudong which lie within the Live Firing Areas.
