    That FFI for cmd sch is common.Every recruit in a leadership batch even if it is PTP one also goes for it.My batch of 200 recruits in the company went for it at one go,lol!It is just a standard medical check up to say that you do not have any major medical conditions that will prevent you from taking up training in ocs/sispec.

     

    A more accurate measure of your chances would be IPPT and SOC.If you have a pass/silver/gold for your ippt and you managed to clear SOC then you got a chance but it is not guaranteed.

     

    Other factors would be the peer appraisal thingy and your SIT test results of course.
