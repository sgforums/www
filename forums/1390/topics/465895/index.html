    cyberpioneer is the official news website of the Ministry of Defence (MINDEF) and the Singapore Armed Forces (SAF). Managed by the Defence Media Centre (DMC) of MINDEF Public Affairs, its aim is to provide up-to-date news on the SAF and defence matters related to Singapore.

    Created to complement the PIONEER magazine, cyberpioneer also includes an online archive of all key feature stories published in the monthly, dating back to May 2001.

    The website you see now is the result of over a decade of evolution, with the latest makeover in October 2012.

    Accolades

    In 2012, PIONEER magazine bagged four awards.

    The May 2012 cover clinched a Silver Ozzie award in the "Best Cover, Association/Non-Profit, Business-to-Business" category at the 2012 Eddie & Ozzie Awards. This international competition is the largest in the magazine publishing industry and the only contest for all magazines and their websites. The Eddies recognise editorial excellence while the Ozzies recognise excellence in magazine design.

    The October 2011 cover and cover story "SAF never sleeps" were honoured with an Award of Excellence in the Magazine & Journal Design & Layout category of the APEX 2012 awards. The awards recognise excellence in graphic design, editorial content and success in achieving overall communications effectiveness and excellence.

    These two works also took home an Excellence in In-House Design award in the Editorial Design category of the 2012 American Inhouse Design Awards. Check out PIONEER's winning entry in Graphic Design USA's Online Design Annual.

    To top it all off, DMC won the Best Use of Social Media award at the Magazine Publishers Association Singapore (MPAS) Awards 2012. The awards, which recognise the best magazine publishing efforts and professionals in Singapore, is open to all publishers or producers of magazines in Singapore.
