    just to inform those who don't know, Officer Of The Day march is played at COG when the two lieutenants from the Old Guard (Pengawan Lama) and New Guard (Pengawan Baru) march up and down their contingents.

    They will march in this sequence.

    They will first march 4 steps forward, then they will about-face by their LEFT.
    Then they march 8 steps forward, then they about-turn by their RIGHT. They will do these '8-steps forward' procedures 10 times including the one mentioned above, with an about-face by the LEFT every alternate procedure.
    After which they will march 4 steps and then halt (2 steps). They will then face each other.

    Sorry if i wasn't clear. It's very hard to explain without seeing it yourself.
