    as mentioned above, PES is medical fitness, NAPFA/ IPPT is physical fitness.
    both are different aspects.
    e.g. you can have a very fit solid taut body (physical), but any of your internal organs/ immune system can be weak (medical).

    on medical checkup day, just bring any medical reports/ doctor memos will do.

    sweaty palms (hyperhidrosis) is still a combat-fit PES B, with excuse hand grenade throwing.

    a NAPFA silver/gold (applied to PES A/B only) will only give you a 2 months fitness cut, meaning you will have a reduction in NS time from 24 months to 22 months only. that's all it will do.
