    Block leave for BMT regardless of period of enlistment is always from the day of POP till the sunday of the following week. Even for those severely obese RECs who serve 6 months, they will have a short block leave inbetween their 6 months training to clear any of the offs and leaves they have for that 3 months before returning back to tekong for the next 3 months. This is to ensure all RECs POP/POC from tekong at about the same time and can enter training school together with as little interruption as possible.

    The number of leaves used in block leave regardless of enlistment month is also generally fixed at 3-4 days which is the REC's entitement for that 3 months in tekong.
