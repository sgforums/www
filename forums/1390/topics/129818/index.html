    *Nope we didn't go there to justwatch tripflare or any pyrotechnics, we were there for training.In fact, we didn't even know that we were to go there for jungle training as during the pre-briefing before Lancer, we were told that we would be only visiting and sight seeing Brunei and get in touch with their Brunei Cadet Force(BCF).About the Alarm mine that you all see, that's the one we learn to set up and disarm from RBAF personels.
    Originally posted by quivalen:
    so what exactly did you guys do? i notice a bruneian uniform in the tripflare picture and the bruneian chopper so i assume you guys did your training under the RBAF? care to tell us more?
    *Here's a list of what we did for the 10/11 days:
    1)We depart from CIA via SAF SIA chartered flight.My NCC was placed under
    Packet 9(Those who went thru JCC, (Commandos,OCT's & regulars will know
    about this).When we arrive at BIA, NCC was transfered 1st to Transit Camp
    (Jalan Aman Camp) while SAF personels were sent sumwhere else,i Don't
    know.

    2)From Transit(Jalan Aman Camp), we were den send to Lakiun Camp. For the
    first 3 days there, we joined SAF personels for Pre-Jungle Training made of
    Pacing exercise,Casevac Briefing and stuff,Navigation Exercise(the 5
    checkpoints,the last one dat i took was hard approximately 1 click(1km) from
    the 4th checkpoint),heli planning & deplanning drills,small animal & birds
    & bird traps,basic knowledge on jungle plants(datz where i know CB leaf)
    ,pyrotechnics practise(smoke grenade,pen flare and stuffs), visited
    Mount Biang(the locals don't refer to it as Mount But instead as "Hill",Had to
    go thru practical and demo in the mock up SAF "Jungle Village"before we
    were certified ok to go for actual jungle training. Oh ya our Instructor was
    JCC Training Cadre, Commando CPT Brandon.A lot of stuffs i forgot, sorry.

    3)On the 4th day,we were transfered from SAF Lakiun Camp to RBAF's
    Penanjong Camp and later in the afternoon To RBAF's Bangar Camp. At
    Bangar we got in touch with the BCF & RBAF

    4)Next day we were heli-lift with RBAF's Blackhawk to Landing Point,LP311
    which was located in Kg. meriam, 7km plus in temburong jungle itself. We
    were escorted by JCC training cadre, CPT Brandon from Commando, SSG Izil
    from Guards and RBAF's Officers,Wo's,Specs & OCT,Ranger. Down there we
    had actual practise of wat we have learnt from SAF plus new stuffs taught
    to us by RBAF's jungle training cadre. We had Night Navex, actual practical(
    make traps, shelter, plants identification and try out (we dig out wild
    tongkat ali also), tactical & non tactical jungle movement, learnt how to
    obtain water), jungle shooting using RBAF's M-16 A2 if im not mistaken with
    a couple of magazines of 20 live rounds each, Casualty evacuation exercise
    (CASEVAC),pyrotechnics demo & practical from RBAF's inventory. We were
    also issued with some SAF's MRE's. On the last day we did area cleaning and
    trekked out tactically of the jungle with our over 30+ kg load & issued
    weapon. The trek was 7 click(7km) where we had to go thru 7 small hills
    which was higher then bukit timah and crossed 2 river before we reach
    back civillisation.

    5)The final 1 & half days we spent our time for R&R where we went to
    Jerudong Theme park, Water Village, museums & short shopping trip.

    Basically that's all that i can remember for now on the training that i went thru. There's more to it dat i need time to recall. Im sorry if my post is too long. Just wanna help & explain a little bit.
