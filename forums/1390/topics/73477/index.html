    Okay, I've managed to find the following off the Ministry of Defence site:


    The parachutist's wing.


    Senior jumper.


    Master jumper.


    Rigger.


    Unit crest - this is not worn anywhere on the uniform.

    Currently qualified jumpers (who complete at least one jump every six months) wear the parachutist's badges with a red backing, unless they're parachute jump instructors, in which case they wear a yellow backing.

    Okay, the ones I haven't been able to find are the cloth badges for the No 4 (combat) unifrom, the embroidered badges for the No 1 (ceremonial) uniform, and the freefall badge (that's a huge one that I've only seen twice in my life).

    As for items 2, 4 and 6 on your list, I haven't come across them before.
