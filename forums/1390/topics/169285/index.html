    Hello, welcome to FDS. You will be posting in on next Monday, and will be going through a 12 weeks course, it's like another BMT, with live firings, route marches and defence exercise (i.e. trench digging). It is NOT a 8 to 5 job, but a shift work. You will take combat pay.

    In PLAB, it's not quite like standing at SFT/TFT. Trust me, you will have lots of experience, learn lots of things and yar, you will do things more than just standing gates.

    Once you graduate from course, you will know. Best of luck.
