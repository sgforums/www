    call up fax your MC to your unit
    report to ICT unit
    scan your IC when book in (In - process)
    check with your company CSM,
    find out the NS company admin Assistance (company clerk) report sick procedure, endorse MC with military medical centre MO,
    now no need to retrieve DOC CAD, everything computer linked.

    with Medical centre MC, there will state your condition, mostly follow outside doctor instruction one. (military MO would not want to have additional risk pressure)

    If medical centre MC, state attend B or attend C, depend of your OC,
    out process or not is all depend on him .

    if did not attend field camp , ICT no count, for high key.

    ICT not completed cannot claim outside pay, only service pay.
