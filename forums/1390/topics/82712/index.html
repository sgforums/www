    Your friend wrong lah.

    Recruits who enlist will get about $450-$600 depending on which period of the month they enlist.

    The first pay is always alot because it includes $30 (neccessities allowance). This is for you all the spend on whatever needed for you for NS, e.g. transport to Pasir Ris Interchange/SFT, bag, u-wear, etc. So that you wun have excuses of no $$.

    Then the pay includes $350 basic allowance.

    SAF pays you in advance, meaning... the pay you get on July 10, is from the enlistment date to July 9 PLUS July 10 to August 9.

    Usually this amounts to around $500.

    Thru the course of NS, you will have this "lump" payments after every promotion because your promotion back pay is paid up.
