    The Specialist and Warrant Officer Institute's (SWI) logo was unveiled by Chief of Army, MG Desmond Kuek on 12 September. The motto of SWI is 'We Live', which is the philosophy SWI will use to train and motivate the WOSPEC to live Our Army's values, the Army experience and to complete the mission.

    The logo shows the 3SG and 2WO rank insignias displayed above a book. The ranks show the commitment of SWI to the development of both the Warrant Officers and Sergeants, while the book reflects the WOSPECs quest for knowledge and improvement.
