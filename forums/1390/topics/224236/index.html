    Ok, l hope I can help to clear up the issue..

    Full time students MUST take IPPT no matter what. I went for NSmen briefing shortly after I ORD'ed last year, and we were told that its a must to take IPPT. If you study in SMU, NUS, NTU, or any of the 5 polys after you ORD, you need not take RT even if u fail IPPT due to the Higher Learning scheme, for the duration of your course in these institutes. For those like me who study in private schools like MDIS, SIM etc, you must take RT if you fail IPPT.
