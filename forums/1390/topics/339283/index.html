    Singapore Food Industries Limited

    Singapore Food Industries Limited (SFI) was incorporated in 1973 and has since grown to become a leading integrated food company in Singapore. SFI has evolved with the changing needs of the SAF, beginning with the supply of fresh rations to SAF cookhouses. In 1986, the company also took on the additional role of catering chilled cooked food to SAF cookhouses. In 1997, SFI took over the responsibility of providing freshly cooked meals on-site to SAF cookhouses when cookhouse operations were commercialised. Besides supporting SAF units locally, SFI has also built up her capabilities to support SAF’s overseas exercises and camps. The company has invested extensively in R&D to develop new recipes and improve food quality for the SAF. With her well-established world wide sourcing network, SFI is fully committed to provide uninterrupted support to SAF in all aspects of catering requirements.
