    My friend once mentioned to me that a WO from the ADF came down to his camp to give a recruitment talk...only the enlisted ranks were made to attend it.

    I do not have the full details but here are the parts that I can remember from him:

    1)PES A/B(They only mentioned riflemen vocation)

    2)TOE:About 5 years or so

    3)Highest rank achievable in contract: LCP(Lance Corporal)

    4)Pay will peak at about $1.2K,got a small sign on bounty bonus..plus a few other study incentives I think.VERY different from PP or Savers plan.

    Note that the above points are for the "men" contract only.I think it is aimed more towards those with below diploma certs.These points are sketchy so please take it with a grain of salt and confirm it with an ARC rep.
