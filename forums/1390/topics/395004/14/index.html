    Yes, you will be downgraded to within PES C to E range, depending on what's on the memo. (e.g. extent of diagnosis, degree of diagnosis/injury/illness/disease...etc.)

    The date of enlistment will either be brought forward or backward at most a couple of weeks to fit the batch cohort altogether.

    And please, health is more important than just a couple of weeks of time, so please show your memo to CMPB to review your new PES.

    By non-declaration of any pre-existing medical condition and if should you get injuried/ condition worsened/ faint/ sent to hospital, you will have to bear partial responsibility and fault when there will be a panel to conduct an investigation into this.

    Therefore, please call CMPB @ 6373 1340 to request another PES review (for Pre-Enlistees after CMPB Checkup).
