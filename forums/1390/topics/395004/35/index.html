    3 Oct 11, 23:07
    Originally posted by Steventan1961:

    Is it true PES C (Term) NSF pass BMT and gotten vocation post to unit go medical review if medical board give Term PES again and again your unit will not promote you not because you poor perfomance but because you no perm PES so they condemned you until you ORD.


    it's a myth...
    they don't care whether it's temp or perm. it's just simply by 2 terms, namely combat-fit and non-combat-fit.

    1st, rank promotion is time-based and conduct-based.
    e.g. combat-fit = minimum 7mths* for LCP, 12mths* + IPPT pass for CPL.
    non-combat-fit = minimum 7mths* for LCP, 15mths* for CPL.

    * = time starting from date of enlistment + passed BMT (PTE rank).

    2nd, when under proper procedure and no admin lapse, the chief clerk will generate a list of personnel to recommend for promotion due to the above 2 criterias, then pass the work to the manpower clerk, then he email your supervisor-in-charge for approval.
    3rd, if he approved, you promote. if he don't, you lan lan suck thumb.

    so in the end, it's your supervisor-in-charge who's playing your rank, provided that there is no admin lapse from manpower branch.

    1st, let's say that vocational postings are usually at random. the only thing you can do is this. when in tekong BMT, there's a monthly PC (platoon officer-in-charge) 1-to-1 personal interview to jot down notes in your personal military booklet. during that private interview, you can only express interest to him on the vocation/ formation you interested in, but please be informed that there's no guarantee/ promise whatsoever.
