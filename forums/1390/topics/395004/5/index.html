    1) Yes, the Camp MO has the prerogative. After the MC period, he have the options to extend the MC period and also its subsequent renewal basis.

    2) You can still use the same specialist memo and the accompanying hospital MC to get your camp MO to renew the MC extension.

    3) Please sound out when you have real medical case to your PC, since you are not malingering. Honestly tell your camp MO about the work you are doing now and thus request him to please give you temporary excuse status such as excuse heavy loads, excuse carrying heavy objects to further add on to your normal Att B (Fit for Light Duties) status. And just show your specialist memo to your PC to have his understanding on your medical conditions.
