    L9 then excused outfield if i m not wrong.

    Landcode has nothing to do with stay in or stay out.

    Landcode only restrict some vocations like combat roles or place of deployability.

    L3 L9 can clerk or storeman

    L2 can be driver technician and signaller

    L1 can be rifleman and  combat roles.

    But L3 means non-comabatant vocations.

    Unless excused stay permanent in by MO.
