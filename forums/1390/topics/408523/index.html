    1) BMTC school 4 is the latest School under BMTC HQ. Formerly there were 2 schools with part of School 1 located at Rocky Hill Camp which is further inland in Tekong as opposed to Ladang Camp, the camp at Tekong where the other 3 current schools are located.

    2) There usually is a break for Public Holidays, except in rare instances.

    3) Your PES Status will allow you to qualify for OCS. You're degree will help a lot too. In fact, as it is you are in a very good position for OCS. Basically its up to you to fuck it up. To bolster your chances, show proper attitude during BMT, push yourself in trainings and get a gold for IPPT, and do well for SIT TEST. SIT TEST being the most improtant to allow you to put a competitive bid for OCS.

    To qualify for command school, atleast an IPPT pass is a requirement, however, a better score would be more competitive.

    4) To be a fighter pilot, you need to sign on. There are no NSFs pilots. During the initial few weeks of BMT, there would be a recruitment officer from each of the three services coming down for recruitment talk. It is recommended that you sign on with him. To get in, you have to pass the Medical (for pilots, it is very stringent) and you have to qualify for OCS. Though, it is known that as long as you pass the requirements for a Pilot, they'll push for you internally to get into OCS.

     

    Good Luck.
