    Maybe I can help... without breaching confidentiality rules. You first have to determine what branch of the SAF you want to serve in. The reason is that if it is the air force, you are likely to encounter more "specialised " units. I can say this with some experience , as I served in one as a reservist.

    The army is less likely to accept you coming in from outside and requesting to join XXXX unit. They are likely to recruit from other units and when you have served some time ( e.g. 2nd year as NSF or you are about to ORD).

    You have better chance volunteering as a regular in the RSAF and RSN and serving with a specialised unit, than in the Army. Or if you have a special civilian skill and you get selected as a reservist in these specialised units.
