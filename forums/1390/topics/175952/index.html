    Bro,

    Any reservist training more than 7 days is considered as high key. If you ken a high key ICT, you MUST bring your FBO & SBO. That means your training programme will definitely include field excersies such as fire movement, attack and defence (for infantry).
    If you kena anything short than 7 days, most likely it will be quite senang one. Again depending on your training programme. It is always prudent to bring you FBO & SBO. You can also look at your SAF100 - it will mentioned something like this:- "you are to report to camp name in full battle order...."
