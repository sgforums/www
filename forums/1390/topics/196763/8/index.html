    Actually, the US Ranger tab is more for future leadership roles in the US armed forces.

    The US Ranger Regiment requires its members to pass the Ranger Indoctrination Program (RIP) or Ranger Orientation Program (ROP). Once passed, a soldier can wear the army ranger beret and Ranger Scroll/Sleeve Insignia on the uniform. (the Ranger Scroll identifies the solder as a member - ie. "vocation" )

    And in order to be in leadership positions, members must also pass the US ranger course as well. Most will do so for long term careers, and you will see both the Ranger Scroll and the Ranger Tab on the uniforms. Hence, the US Ranger tab is not a "vocation" per se, but for leadership requirements, as the school allows members of other units and foreigners to attend.
