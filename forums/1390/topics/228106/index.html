  • octega
    29 Dec 06, 12:00
    Do some simple training. Aim is to get ur body into motion so u will be less likely to get muscle cram, ache during ur BMT if u have not been exercising regularly.

    Be prepared to be scolded until "chow tou". One good training is to get a best fren and ask him to unleash the hokkien vocab onto u, remember to use the meanness and most hurtful of language (most likely involve private parts and parents). Why? To get urself numb, yes, totally numb and immune to scolding until the scolding from ur sergeants seems more like singing than scolding.


    Prepare mentally as well. Start to do pointless and meaningless stuff like running down stairs to touch a lamp post or changing from one set of clothes to another. Why? So u won't get bonkers from doing senseless and meaningless stuff during BMT.

    Get used to regimentation. Start to wake early 5am and do meaningless stuff like drinking lots of water then go eat breakfast then drink alot of water again. Then do some area cleaning, then clean the toilet, then knock it down 20. Drink water again THEN start do exercise. Do this everyday.

    Start torturing urself for other ppl mistake. For example, if ur mom forgets something, u do 20 sets of push up. Ur fren late for ur appointment, u do 20 sets of push up, basically, u do 20 sets of push up for other ppl's mistakes. Why? To get u used to SAF style of punishment, if anyone in ur platoon or company f**k up, u will get it even though u might not even be present or related to that f**k up person.

    Get in touch with mother nature, go downstairs and roll around in mud and grass, do it for 3 days and dun bathe during these 3 days only powder urself. Why? So u won't freak out during ur field camp.
