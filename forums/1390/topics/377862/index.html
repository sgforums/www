    1. If you fail IPPT beyond the 3 months in Tekong, you will have to go for re-BMT just before you ORD. As for the rest of the time outside of Tekong, you won't need to stay back on weekends, but your chances for promotion are close to zero if you are combat fit. But then again, it also depends on your unit's policy for IPPT failures.

    2. You will have to go for re-BMT, which will usually be near the end of your NS. I heard of people who managed not to go for re-BMT though, but don't get your hopes up too high.

    3. Yes, biscuits are allowed, unless you are from an obese batch.
