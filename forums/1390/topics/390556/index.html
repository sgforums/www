    Just to share, for mob manning, you can be scheduled to be called up EVEN the next day after you ORD. 

    Since you are entering uni in about July/Aug, calling you up in May will not result in any clash. I was even called during my exam period, though I manage to easily get "deferment" from the WO in charge for the days which falls on my exam dates.

    Mob manning usually ( over 90%) takes place on weekends, and most of the time you just need to bring your stuffs down and sign and go back. In fact, you won't necessary be called up even if it is stated that a certain week or two is the mob-manning period.
