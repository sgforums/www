    2 years to get your pilot 'wings' and your LTA rank. After that you still have to go for ops conversion in the squadrons.

    As for the passing percentage, let's put it this way, out of those people who pass the COMPASS test + interview + medical and go into OCS, it's rumoured that 90% of them get chopped along the way over the 2 years.

    Besides, pilots do not only fly fighters. There are transport and helicopters as well.

    As for A levels vs diploma, diploma will command a higher pay than A levels but the difference is not that great compared to diploma vs degree.
