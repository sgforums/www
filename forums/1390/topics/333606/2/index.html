    National Service Training Cycle

    The NS Training Cycle is a continuation of your active unit training cycle. Hence, you are expected to apply your military skills and knowledge when you return for In-Camp Training (ICT). The key NS milestone is the Unit's ATEC Evaluation whereby the entire unit will be evaluated on its war fighting capabilities. The year in which your unit is evaluated depends on your NS Unit's Training Cycle, which is different for Combat, Combat Support and Combat Service Support Units:

    • Infantry / Guards / Armour - ATEC Stage 1 (6th NS year), ATEC Stage 2 (7th NS year)
    • Artillery - FATEP (7th NS year)
    • Engineers - ETEC (7th NS year)
    • Commando - Evaluation (5th NS year)
    • Logistics - ATEC Stage 1 (6th NS year), ATEC Stage 2 (7th NS year)
    • Signals - Evaluation (6th and 7th NS years)
