    Smart 4:

    Take the number 4 top off first and lay it flat so it is easier to manage.


    1. Pull the sleeves from the cuff all the way up to the shoulder.

    2. From the bottom of the folded sleeve, start folding about three fingers length upwards towards the shoulder approximately 3 times, till only the cuff of the sleeve can be seen.

    3. Fold the cuff back down to cover the rest of the sleeve.

    4. Shove your arm into the sleeve. *It may be a little tight at first but it'll be alright after a while.

    Boot laces:

    1. Stick your foot into the boot

    2. Pull the laces till the boot is tight.

    3. Pull the laces through till only the length of the lace remains.

    THE KNOT

    4. Holding both laces in your hands, imagine tying a knot, only without the other end going through the hole. Essentially, if you pull on the end of it, the knot should come undone without needing to string any lace back through the hole

    5. carrying lacing up the lace through the holes made by your lace till there is no more loose lace.

    6. Pull the last bit of lace through the hole and tighten the knot.

    7. Tuck under your garters and you're set.

     

    Hope it helps =)
