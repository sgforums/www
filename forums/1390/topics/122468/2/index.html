    Ae86tofu, semi-auto sniper rifles like PSG-1 & so on are indeed good weapons for sniping. The reason why most snipers prefer bolt-action against semi-auto is because bolt-action rifles have less movable parts which reduces movement of the barrel when firing. Also the fact that the bolt action rifle is a follow through action, which means that the bolt does not eject the spent cartridge immediately after firing. Again this reduces movement of the weapon as compared to a semi-auto. Lastly, its all about the challenge of using a bolt action rifle against an enemy with superior fire power & coming out the winner.

    Ooh! I forgot, semi-auto rifles like the PSG-1 are quite costly as compared to a bolt action rifle.
