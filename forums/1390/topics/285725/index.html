    Originally posted by Samuelpsw:
    btw..how is the training like for pes Bp ppl..i sure wan to lose lots of weight during BMT..
    Basically, whatever Pes A and B people do, u also do, just that ur training is not as tough and spread out. For instance, while Pes A and B people may start immediately on 4km route march, u guys start off with 2km(just an example, may not apply now)

    And during my time, my coy(PesBP also) have 3 requirements that we must fulfil before we can book out each week. We just have to fulfil 2 of them. i)Lose 1 kg each week. ii)Do a certain number of chin-ups. iii)2.4km timing meet the requirement set by ur sergeant. Again, not sure if its still the norm now, it may not be so.

    Don't worry about losing weight. I weighed 88kg before I entered BMT, and after BMT, i dropped to 72kg. Provided of course, u don't go and snack too much and drink soft drinks. Stick to the three meals they give u every day and u shall be fine. During weekends, I would also eat whatever i want, but of course in moderation too.

    I'm previously from Apache Company in BMTC.. just poped a month ago tho.
    it was a mild obese intake then. but anyway.. to my knowledge the training is somewad progressive.. expect quite a number of endurance run (2km) and other PT like going to the gym for strength and circuit training.. however we dont really do exercises like the PTP/Enhance batch do. for instance we don't do AGR/Speed training etc.. the training programme vision is to cut down the weight basically, not really designed to let you pass IPPT with flying colours. Alot of work is needed cos if u happen to get into command school next time when u POP... it's gonna be very tough as the physical demands compared to mild obese training difference is quite a margin
