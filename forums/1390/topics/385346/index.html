    Hope I can answer your question.

    You should be under PES B2L3. And by right PES B2(aka C1) are considered combat fit, however need to see what level you are in (L what). L3/L9 not considered combat fit.

    Upgrade of PES is unlikely, as SOC itself are considered high risk activities that might caused trauma to your head, which can cause retinal detachment, unless you are willing to bear any consequences if something happen to your eyes after you up PES.

    Normally C1L3/L9 will not be under any combat units. Technicians, Service Medics, Drivers.. etc that are not of combat nature. (Unless you suay la)
