    to take a walk

    to take a walk down orchard road

    bypass mandarin

    to centre point

    where we had lunch at Macdonald

    and share a cup of lemon lime

    i hold her hands,look into her eyes

    and ask her to be my velentine

     

    now i'm confined in army camp

    i miss her so,my sweet honey

     i promise her,when i book out

    i call her out and date her out

    So I turned my way to the airport

    There was one guy, standing beside her

    I was so sad and departed

    Coz it breaks my heart, and filled with tears
