    Hi,

    I am new to this forum and I need some advice on my request to post out of my combat unit.

    I am a PES C9L2 NSMan and I have been permanently excused from carrying heavy loads.

    I have been suffering from back problems for the past 10 years due to the carrying of STB during my NSF days in the pioneer platoon. 5 years ago my back problem got so bad that I knew that was no way I could carry pioneer stores on my back and walk for 10-20km in outfield missions during ICT. I consulted a specialist and taken a few MRIs. I was temporarily downgraded in 2005 and then permanently downgraded in 2007 to C9L2 in 2007 due to my lower back problems.

    Since being downgraded, my active unit called me back for 3 ICT and 1 mobilization. For the 2 week ICTs, I had to carry all my personal equipment and other necessities required for a 2 week stay in camp back to camp and then out of camp after the ICT. All these heavy load caused me to suffer from acute lower back pain. The same is true for mobilization as we are required to carry all our personal equipment and report to camp. 

    To make matter worse, during the 3 ICT I attended after being downgraded, I have been made to to carry stores, ammos, go for parades as a necessary part of my duties. All of these aggravated my back problem causing me more severe pain and suffering.
    My back problem is now worse than before. I am extremely worried about going for ICT again because I am unable to carry all the heavy stuff that is necessary for ICT.
     
    I consulted my specialist again and went for another medical review with the MO. The MO said he understood my problem but he does not have the authority to post me out of a combat unit. The MO told me I that the proper channel for me is to contact my unit and that he will send me to the medical board and the medical board then permanently excused me from heavy loads.
    I then sent a email to contact@ns.sg explaining the reasons for my request to be posted out of the combat unit and was informed that my request has been sent to my unit and I will be contacted by the unit very soon.
     After 3 weeks when nobody contacted me at all, I sent another email to contact@ns.sg to follow up and the next day a warrant officer called me to inform me that I have been reassigned as a ASA 1 month ago and is no longer under the pioneer platoon but I still belong to the combat unit. I had to explain my problems and reasons for requesting to be posted out of the combat unit all over again. It was obvious that the warrant officer did not even bother to read my email and did not bother to handle my case even after 3 weeks of receiving my request.  
    After I have finished stating my case, the warrant officer stated to me that its a SAF regulation that a NSMan has to stay with the unit even he is downgraded to PES E. Only after I reiterated that I am in no condition to serve in a combat unit and after I insisited that I want to speak to somebody with the authority to handle my request did the warrent officer said she would send my request to the unit. She still added that most of the unit personnel are overseas in a exercise and there is no way she know when she can let me know the results.    
    All these seem like a pushing responsibility exercise to me. MO say he cant anything, warrant officer says request will be sent to unit after 3 weeks of receiving the request and then still stated nobody in unit is around to handle the request.
    I have a ICT on Nov 2rd 2009 and I dont have the time to wait until somebody is interested to handle my request. I am also very certain that the warrant officer will not be calling me back at all. I am considering to approach my MP but I want to do this only as a last resort.
    Can anybody offer me any advice on who or which SAF department I can call and highlight my problems. I just need to speak to somebody who has authority to handle my request and not ignore it.
    Thanks.
