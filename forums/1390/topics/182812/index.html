    Even though you will go tekong for BMT if you sign on as Officer, most of the time they will put you in the OCS intake.

    Because it will be easier for them to consolidate female trainers, etc.

    As for the intake dates, just be prepared to be called 2 weeks before enlistment.... you will then be rushed for medical checkup up and later rushed to Army logistics base for you issue items.

    If you sign on as specialist, you might be enlisted 2 weeks to 1 months before BMT and you will be in a unit first to "waste time" before you start your BMT training.

    what i mean is, they are allowed to recourse the basic qualification courses three times.

    meaning, if they get pregnant during BMT, they can give birth and return to BMT after.

    as for vocation change, it usually happens to courses where you will need to pass to get the vocation you sign on.

    eg, a lady signs on as guardswoman, she will need to pass GCC. If she fails 3 times (or more by discretion), she will need to re-vocate to infantry or she will be stuck in rank.
