    If you have letters from specialists, your MO will arrange for medical board. Regardless of which specialist you get, it is the Medical Board that will decide on PES grading. There are lots of orthopaedic surgeons in the SAF, including the Chief of Medical Corps himself. So if they disagree with the specialist outside, they can overrule.

    BTW, there is a secret book by which the SAF MOs decide on PES grading. The grading given reflects the kind of activity MOST patients with the condition can handle. Some people suay suay will have a condition that is not downgradable by the secret book but can be worsened by physical activity. I also suffer from a joint problem that got worse after NS.
