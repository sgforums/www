    if don go sispec then most prob will go where?
    Vocation training, at one of the Formation Schools (Arty, Signals, etc). The OCS & SISPEC cohorts will also attend courses there, only later. The difference between them & you is that you're obviously an "other rank".

    Will they post u to a unit close to your house wan?
    Not necessarily, depending on what you're trained as & which outfit you're deployed to (after the course). There's also a possibility of being retained by the said School to support its training cadre. You'll get a better idea of your options once you know which Formation you've ended up, eg. which army camps their units are based in. Some NSF "old birds" could even guess your probable destination then, based on their general comprehension of who has ORD'd or is ORD-ing.
