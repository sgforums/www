    Sembawang Camp has 1st Transport Batallion and STC (Supply Training Centre). Supply training Centre is a training school for logistics specs namely Transport and Supply (Tran Sup and CQMSes) 1st Tpt BN has Alpha,HMCT and Charlie Coy occupying the bulk of it. Larc V company is also there but it collectively under HMCT Coy..1st TPT HQ is also there... NDU is under the navy side of Sembawang Camp aka Terror Camp...There's a road leading to NDU...From the Medical Centre, its straight ahead.. That houses NDU and SWG.
