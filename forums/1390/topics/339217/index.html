    1) Enlisting when is not for you to decide, it's up to CMPB.

    2) Currently, I think the bunks are quite airy with enough fans compared to the BMT bunks in the 90s. But the sweaty smell can be quite bad as everyone is so smelly and sweaty had to squeeze into the bunk. It can be quite stinky at times. Depends on the bunk IC.

    3)There are cases of food/water poisoning but rare.

    4)Water parade is conducted before the start of any exercise, this is to prevent you from suffering any heat exhaustion. If you cannot finished it, it's up to you.
