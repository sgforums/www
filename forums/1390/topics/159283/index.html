    I have a friend who is physically fit but was diagnosed with this syndrome too few yrs back. It is a serious medical problem which requires you to stay out from strenous physical workout as it might lead to a cardiac arrest which will cause instant death. Most likely you will be posted to vocations where u will be exempted from any form of physical workout for example, Clerk or storeman. My friend,whom i mentioned earlier, was posted to clerk. Take good care of yourself friend and remember your health should be your main concern and most importantly listen to your body As for the enlistment letter do not worry, you will receive it sooner or later
