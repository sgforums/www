    ok let me help u out a little with regards to your quries. upon reporting to CMPB for your check-up, the following checks will be carried out:

    1.photo taking ( for your NS NRIC aka 11B)

    2.urine test

    3.draw blood for blood test

    4.declaration of tatoo if any.

    5.X-ray done for lungs

    6.eye test

    7.dental check up

    8.hearing test

    9.ECG or heart rate monitoring

    10.height and weight

    11.. Docter interview / visual check by SAF MO. ( expect to strip naked for 1-2 min, declare any medical condition to the MO)

    12.Upon completion on all the above, you will be directed to a computer lab to do your IQ test. Just follow the instruction on the computer.

    completion of your IQ test will mark the end of your NS check up. After 6-9 months, you will receive a letter from CMPB of your posting ie. Police/SCDF/Army/Navy. Im not sure of the reporting procedure for police and scdf. The letter will state the time and date for reporting and also the items to bring on enlistment day. If you are posted to the army, most likely you will receive your basic military training(BMT) in tekong whereas navy, you wil receive your BMT at RSS Panglima, IMOS at Changi Naval Base. Medics at CMPB can be quite "dao" and are rather unwilling to answer some of your queries for further instructions. Never to worry, specific instructions will be pasted on the walls around the check up area to guide you through the whole check up. Unless you lan lan bo bian than consult the medics there. hope all this helps and good luck!
