    in that case, it would be worthwhile bringing up your case with your PC, and eventually your OC. you may need to demonstrate to them that there really is no other avenue, and you did not end up in your present situation voluntarily.

    however you have to bear in mind that since its a stated policy that non-married personnel should stay in for your vocation. at the same time, a serviceman is actually not allowed to moonlight. hence you may not get what you hope for. in that case you may wish to ask for SAF's financial assistance.
