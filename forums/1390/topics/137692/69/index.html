    There is never stay in bunk in SAF. Any activity you are excused, you will be doing sai kang such as cleaning the drains, toilets, smoking area etc etc. There are also non physical base activities you have to attend if not you will simply fail ur BMT and have to recourse again later in ur service term.

    U will be issued with almost the same stuff as PES A/B except the gortex jacket which is 1 of the most expensive item army issues which is for PES A/B only.

    Enlistment dates for PES C is uncertain at the moment but unlikely to be ever before Feb-Apr since all the enlistment notice for that period all seems to have been sent out already. If you just got your PES means they will only start finding a slot for you now which should be in the middle of next year generally.
