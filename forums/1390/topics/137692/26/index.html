    Which list you referring to? I see so many list of things people bring till i also sian to search. Anyway, i would suggest just bring the required and don't bring too much other things. You can always bring the things you forgot after your first bookout. Just make sure you don't miss the items that you will sure need.

    On your first day on tekong after your family has left, you will have to go collect your duffle bag and field pack. After you found yours, you will need drag them to your company line and depending on where your specs prefer to do the size test etc at ground floor or at platoon level, in the end you need carry everything up to your platoon level. This is not so bad for those staying on 2nd story but those that stay higher up has alot more trouble as the more you bring the more load you have to carry. The issued duffle bag and field pack which on their own weighs alot already without the additional load of your personal items.
