    Originally posted by RaaRaa:
    Hi would like to ask these questions.
    1.) When is the first IPPT/Napfa in bmt ?
    2.) If lets say I get bronze or silver wat happens ?
    3.) If I get gold wat happens ?
    4.) And the items tested are the usual 2.4km and stuff ?
    TYVM!
    1) The IPPT cat test which is use to group people into different groups for AGR will be carried out on the 2nd or 3rd week. This result is not counted and only the following IPPT test after that cat test will be counted as your 1st IPPT test result if nothing has changed.

    2) You will still continue normal training but during the last 2 weeks or so when all activities are done, you can skip the IPPT test they conduct for failures and just rest in bunk.

    3) As far as i know, BMT IPPT results are not counted. For awards like gold and silver, you only get them from IPPT conducted in unit. BMT IPPT is more just to see whether you qualify for command school.

    4) All the napfa stuff excluding sit and reach
