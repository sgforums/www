    Originally posted by Meavdea:

    hey guys, im going into bmtc sch 1 at tekong on 8 jan 08. Im in pesA and just curious can i expect enough time spent on training such that there will be time for me to train up to be as fit as my peers or to the best i can. cos i haven been doin much training and hearing from my other peers whom are training ,im worried that my initial shitty performance will affect my opportunities. so hows it gonna be like? replies appreciated

    Jan batch should mean you are going into BMT directly without PTP. Direct BMT batch have a very pack schedule as 9 weeks is a very short period and alot of less essential stuff that the PTP batch has already cleared during their additional 4 weeks have to be squeezed into the BMT batch training schedule.

    Training might be tough in tekong if you haven't been actively training yourself and since alot of training becomes back to back, it might be hard for your body to cope well if you haven't been doing much training lately. I would advice you start some form of training yourself to get your body ready for what is to come.

    Regarding oppertunities, if you are talking about going to OCS/SISPEC a IPPT silver is required if i didn't recall wrongly and the rest depends on peer/spec evaluation, SIT test etc which is more how you behave and how well you show leadership capabilities than your real physical fitness.
