    Originally posted by chocolates-xed:

    1. anyone has any tips on field camp?

    2. if enhanced leadership batch means higher rate of command school, but how to show you not interested in command school? and if you dont want command school, is it confirmed you will be in chiong sua vocations? How to request for vocations like RP, MP?

    Hi. Field camp is the most xiong or perhaps the second most xiong in BMT. Tips well, Just keep your rifle safe. Tighten your blank attatchement and also take good care of your magazine. You can black tape the magazine if its allowed. In short, everything safe and not stunned. My personal experience, i smuggled wet wipes during my field camp. Hide it inside my ration pack. You can try it if you wish but beware of field pack inspection.

     

    They will ask you to express interest. Just indicate that you are not interested. Tell your sec com/ pc or even oc about it. Mp require interview. So that means you have to impress the Mp sargent when there is a interview.
