  • baikachuchu
    3 Sep 08, 08:33
    Originally posted by Rawr2008:

    Hi I am classified as PES C2L2, with excuse Run March Jump. What activities would i be doing since most activities in BMT would require RMJ i suppose?

     

    For eg. Route March. I probably will be excused no? If that is so, what will I be doing when the others do their Route March?


    Fatigue work like area cleaning or whatever other sai kang the COS for the day can think of.Ask you go cookhouse fill up ice box for use during PT trainings also can,ask you clean platoon office also can,ask you to help storeman pack up stores and clean the entire store also can.Basically,whatever you see those who fall out during certain training do,you also can do.

     

    What I said above is for those times where the rest of your company is doing a certain type of training that you will not be able to participate in.

     

    During route march,you will be excused.You might be assigned to ration party(deliver food and water) especially for the longer distance ones.Or else you just stay back at company line and do fatigue work until your ka kis come back.If really nothing to do,just sleep in bunk.

     

    Worst case scenario is that they make you do guard duty for that day which was what happened to my PES C friend.

     

    Overall you will still be able to participate in the following main activities:

    .PT Training(Excluding running stuff)

    .Range

    .Field Camp(No need route march there but just take tonner to camp site to set up tent)

    .Hand Grenade?(I think you still need to throw)
