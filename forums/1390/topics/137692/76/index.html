    Viper Company is just like any other company in BMT. Generally they are the same with some slight variations in policy (but this is due to the personalities of the commanders).

     

    And for your second question, the answer is Yes! There will be some performances by the SAF's Music and Drama Company. Mainly some songs, and dance routines and some audience participation segments. The MDC do have professional female performers (as well as their male counterparts- regulars and NSFs alike), but they performance is tasteful and not slutty and such. So you dont have to worry that your boyfriend will have "too good a time", if you get my meaning. These ladies are after all professional performers.

    This is part of the "Recruit's Evening", so that the recruits get to unwind and party with their mates in BMTC.

    Originally posted by Cheezerpup:

    so they have performances everyday for the last week of bmt? so no physical training anymore for the last week?

    The 'performances' are only for one night i.e. "Recruits' Evening", not for the whole week.

    They do passing-out-parade (POP) rehearsals for the last week or so of BMT, so yes, in a sense they do 'perform' everyday for the last week of BMT. By then there will be very little physical training, since most of their time will be spent on parade rehearsals.
