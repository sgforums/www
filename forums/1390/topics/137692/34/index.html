    The list is roughly accurate i would say. Most of the required stuff doesn't change and if you really did miss out anything, you can still get them on your 1st bookout. You will rarely get in trouble for not having things during 1st 2 weeks.

    The required stuff from beach road will be told to you by your PS or sect comm before your 1st bookout on what they want you to get. It might vary so just wait and see what they want.

    There will only be 1 2 week long confinement either during PTP or during BMT for those direct BMT.

    You will normally bookout on saturday morning and bookin on sunday evening. Sometimes weekends might be burn due to activities like field camp etc so there will be some adjustments here and there.

    Most units after BMT are stayin so if you get a vocation that lets you stayout everyday then you are 1 of the luckier guys out there. All combat units and certain logistics units are stayin.

    Normal life will definitely be disrupted especially for stayin personnel but its for you to balance your own time between friends and family during your bookouts and nights off.
