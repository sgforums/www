  • dkcx
    30 Oct 07, 20:50
    Originally posted by RaaRaa:
    Just curious from some post ago,

    1) What is CO's night ?
    2) What is POP ?
    1. CO night is a night about 1 weeks before POP that the whole school will gather and there will be performance etc to entertain everyone followed by a good supper. Each company is required to put up a performance which normally is chosen during OC night (similar sort of event but smaller scale held at company level). In a way its like a celebration to mark the nearing of your tour in tekong.

    2. POP stands for Passing Out Parade which is the day you finally promoted from a chao recruit to a chao private :p and it marks the beginning of your 'real' NS life.
