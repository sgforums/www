    I am not too sure about the effectiveness of push-ups for pull-ups but personally I found it close to useless. Lats pulldown in the gym can help. However, for me, the most effective way was just to hang from the bar. Get used to your body weight and sooner or later you will be able to do your 1st pullup. The 2nd will follow soon. I used this method and went from 0 to 8 in 3 mths even though I have been stuck at 0 for 4 yrs before that.

    As for 2.4, I ran about 3+ km? Get used to running more than 2.4km and 2.4km will seem relatively easier. As for timing, be realistic. Aim for a 11:00 to 11:15. 10:00 is close to gold timing itself so for you to aim for 10:00 is a very huge step. It represents a vision rather than a goal. 11:00 to 11:15 will be much more achievable and will net you a silver IPPT (around gold napfa standard) if you get the minimum requirements from other stations at your age grp (Cat X = <25 yrs old).
