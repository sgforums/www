    Turn out is more for guard duty bah. This is a command mostly given by the guard commander to gather the guards to check their alertness and training of some drills. When a turnout is given, everyone have to shout it out loud and rush down to the guard room office to collect their weapons before they hetaktaki and wait for the guard commander to give them commands.

    If the turn out if not done well, the guard commander can repeat the entire procedure again till he is satisfied. My best record was only giving 3 times turn out since myself as the guard 2IC and the guard com both felt the guards did too badly.
