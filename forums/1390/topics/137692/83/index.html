  • Forbiddensinner

    Originally posted by qwertywerty:

    if bring glasses in must bring the plastic black frame one? or will they provide it?

    You will have to bring your own specs. Its frame must be either black or very dark blue.

    You are free to bring in one with metal frame, though with all the sweat and scratches due to training and such, the frame may rust, so I won't advise on that.

    I will also suggest that you bring in spectacle hooks or band, so as to prevent the specs from dropping or "flying" off during your trainings.
