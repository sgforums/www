    Provost.

    The most hated and most unwanted soldiers in the army that brings law and order during peace and war time.

    They serve purposefully to apprehend soldiers who fail to follow the laws in the SAF during peacetime. Example, arresting deserters, awolees, preventing riots, guarding detainees etc.

    More importantly is their role during wartime to arrest rapists, thieves, deserters within the ranks and guard POWs.
