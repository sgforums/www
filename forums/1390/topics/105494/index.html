    C9L2 Recourse aka PES C Re-BMT.You will not be treated like recruits at all.You will be treated on a more equal footing as a fellow soldier and your PES status means you won't be going for 75% of the lessons, especially those that require physical or outfield activities.However,you may have to observe and just "be there" for the lessons instead of actively partaking in them.Quite a nice thing, this PES C recourse, you will have ppl with ranks from PTEs to 3SGs all around you, from different vocations and units all over the SAF.Enjoy it, take it as a release from the stress of unit life.Of course, if you are presently in a 8-5 vocation or unit, then the drag might be the stay-in part.But dont worry, most often than not, canteen breaks are "own time, own target".The next PES C recourse should be next end-March to early-April.
