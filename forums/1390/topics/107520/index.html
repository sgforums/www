    Hi Guys, just wanna confirm with you all, is it necesssary to take RT if I fail IPPT after my window closes, considering I'm now a full-time student studying in SG?
    As a principle, yes, cuz RT is non-residential like IPPT (& unlike ICT). You can apply to your Unit for RT deferment by submitting valid evidence, like an exams timetable, but must otherwise show why you can't be released to go down on weekday evenings & weekend mornings/afternoons. Note, though, that upon approval of deferment your Unit will book on your behalf the next-suitable RT schedule & inform you accordingly. Inconvenienced? Hey, working reservists - including overseas frequent fliers - aren't excused either.

    FWIW Best way to "postpone" the entire liability is to get medically exempted (6 months consecutive or 9 months culmulative) or being posted to MINDEF Reserve or to a Holding List (i.e. disrupted cuz you're overseas, etc.).
