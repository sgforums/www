  • gonegoose
    22 Jul 04, 12:08
    Besides SAF Film Unit, its parent unit INTEC has also other branches under its care that is staffed by NSFs.

    As for your qualifications, a related degree/diploma is def needed (and not just spending 2 yrs as AVA club president in Sec School/JC). If i can remember correctly, half of Ngee Ann Poly's FSV/multimedia grads are there. If you are from said institution, ask your seniors if they can recommend you (that's on the assumption that they want to or that you're good!) to their superiors. If not, just make an application through CMPB or INTEC.

    here's their URL

    http://www.mindef.gov.sg/safti/intec/index.html

    Also, more often than not, any NSFs in INTEC/Film Unit/DiTV have been downgraded in one way or the other. For a simple reason, if you're able and fit, you're supposed to be fighting!!! only if you are deemed unfit for combat then the organisation deploy you to other such places and not let your "talent" go to waste.
