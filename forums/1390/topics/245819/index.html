    1. Either you misunderstand what can be put in the cupboard or you shouldn't bring those things into camp at all.

    2. standby bed/area is different from standby field pack/sbo. This is where common sense comes in. The reasons for the two standbys are different.

    Standby bed/area is because they want to ensure cleaniness of the area and that you maintain your personal hygiene. Which is why you have to layout some standard items like toothbrush and shaver.

    Standby field pack/sbo is because they want to ensure you have all the necessary items to go for exercise or war.

    I hope this hints that field pack/sbo are not part of standby bed/area. They won't check your field pack/sbo items.
