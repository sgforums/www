Drink it with love

  • KumSioJui

    Middle-aged Japanese women have spent the last year swooning over the South Korean TV drama "Winter Sonata" and its male star Bae Yon Joon.

    This cup should send them into raptures. As you pour liquid into it, you can hear the show's theme song and a line of dialogue from episode 16, when Min Yeong (played by Bae Yong Joon) and Yu Jin (Choi Ji Woo) pledged their eternal love to each other at a church. You hear the words "You are the only woman I love. I would love to be with you even after my hair turns gray. I would love to have a baby with your eyes. For you, for our future baby, I offer my warm hands and strong legs to support you. I love you."

    The cup has a printed picture of the street and church.
