    Have you ever wondered how your dog sees the world? What he or she is looking at? What kind of visionary world your dog has? Well, Tomy has developed the world's first camera for a dog, so to speak.

    Just hang this camera from your dog's neck and press a remote to take a photo. You can also set a timer from one minute to 60 minutes. It has 8 MB installed memory and you can save up to 90 images. There is no flash, so do not worry about your dog going crazy from the sudden light.
