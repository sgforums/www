    When you first enter love, I believe you should always use your head more than your heart. If you give love so freely, you should expect to have your heart crushed a thousand times before someone will reciprocate. The other party has to feel like she has to, and wants to, work for your love and she's not ready to do that.. so why should you entertain that? I'm not saying you should be calculative.. just abit more guarded in matters of the heart.

    Let's put it this way - if you were a limitless water tap, people would probably just leave u on day and night. But if you placed a limit on how much water you gave out, people would suddenly realise how valuable you are and treat you accordingly.

    I would think it's best to retreat a little, show her that you're no interim boyfriend replacement but at the same time show her you've got the potential to be so much more.
