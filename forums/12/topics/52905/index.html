    I find myself in the same view as you.

    To both of us, we know that we have to balance ourselves between friends and bf/gf. We feel that it is equally important to contact our good friends regularly in order to maintain our friendships. It can be either going out together or chatting over the phone regularly.
    This is our view of good friends.

    But frankly speaking, most of the people doesn't feel the same way as we do. I feel that most of the people will have the opinion that good friends should be understanding enough to allow them to go out and stick together with their bf/gf all the time. I agree with that partially. It is logical that we should stick with our bf/gf most of the time but we should also spend some time together with good friends as well. It can be just a nice proper chat over the phone if there is really not enough time to meet up regularly.

    But some of the friends does not even willing to spend once a week to chat with good friends. They go out with their bf/gf every weekends and during some of the weekdays and then chat over the phone with their bf/gf daily. This is not wrong but we should always maintain a balance.

    This is not only to maintain a steady relationship with our good friends but also to protect ourselves if the bgr relationship doesn't goes well in the future. If we are together with our bf/gf everyday without some of our own personal time, we will feel that life is meaningless once the relationship is gone. This is because our bf/gf has becomes so natural in our life and we won't be able to find anything meaningful in life once they are no longer with us.

    It is a fact that good friends will always stand by you no matter what the situation is as long as it is within the conscience. We should not take our good friends for granted. We must always remember to spend some time alone with good friends no matter how busy we are. This is regardless of whether the good friends are of the same sex or opposite sex.

    Why is it that subconciously when we encounter problems and need help, and only then we remember our good friends. When we are happy, we only think of our bf/gf? This is a question that we have to think about to understand ourselves better.
