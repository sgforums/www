    Well, I'm not sure about whether will your DAE be accepted, partly because your science and math (which is important) is D7 but it all comes to what diploma did you choose when you apply for DAE. Of course, if you are accepted, be prepared for retaking O-level Math and Science.

    Anyway, regarding what you decision you should make if you are not accepted through DAE, I seriously think you should retake your O-level.

    Private Poly, in my view, is not worth your time (You are concern about time, aren't you?). Its expensive and its hard to compete with people who have a non-private diploma. High chances you will hit a non-private poly graduates or University graduates when you throw a stone down the street, so how are you going to compete with them? So, this option is out of question.

    ITE - Higher Nitec, in my view, its not a bad decision. You can at least get a cert that is more recongised than private diploma and if you work hard, you can still get into non-private poly. However, thats if you are not concern about time. If you are concern about time and you seriously want to get into a poly. You will need 2 years if you go through this route, whereas going through the route of retaking O-level requires only 1 year.

    Ultimately, if your aim is to get into poly or even better, into university, retaking your O-level is the best route. Yes, you may say its not 100% you can get into poly even if you retake your O-level, that goes the same for the route of ITE. However, I don't see the point why can't you get into poly if you work hard for that 1 year. My friend did it with L1R4 of 15, why can't you? And worst, he's first O-level scored worst than you, I remember is some where around 35 to 40 for L1R4.

    In the end, its up for you to decide which way you want to go. Remember, walk down a path which you will not regret when you look back in your later part of your life.
