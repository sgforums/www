The 2 qualities : Emotions & logic: each has its strong factor, but u must know how to balance them and know when to use each one at its fullest or half half. Logically think the problem thru,

    1)why did he break up with u? Lack of communication? Were u able to see this coming? The blow would be greater if u didn't see this coming. Is it his character type to play around with girls? Then u shouldn't be with him in the first place unless thats what u want too.

    2) could the problem also lie with u? Did u all really go into a relationship juz cause of boredom or really there was something and both of u decided to put in effort? Most pple do not approach love with such a viewpt and thought and think its juz the 'love at first sight' thing, which sad to say, give abit of time, the fuel runs out.

    3) Talk about this to your close frens and see what viewpts they can't say. Sometimes they can offer valuable insights which u can't see in ur current state of anger, sadness and frustration.

    4) The most important thing now is not to think of 'Why break up with me?' It leads to a state of personal bashing and leads to nothing.
