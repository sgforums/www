    he was as busy as usual, and went he walked out of his room, xiaolu whispered into his ears: " I am pregnant..."

    guess what Lu's response was? he was overjoyed and shouted out to the whole office: " MARRY ME Lu Tai!!!" he hugged her and the whole office was cheering and clapping, sharing their joy...

    xiaolu was very happy that lu proposed to her and that he was overjoyed abt her pregnancy.. xiaolu felt loved and finally, she is going to marry the man she loves most..

    the wedding has to be carried out asap before xiaolu's stomach start to be significant.. therefore, she quitted her job to do the preparations for the wedding as Lu was too busy to handle such matters...

    Lu and xiaolu both have dreams of being their own bosses, setting up a company of their own. so they were saving up all these years, and they have decided to start their own business after their marriage.

    their boss knows abt their intentions of starting their own business too, but he wanted Lu to help him in one last big business with a major client before he leaves the company. he agreed. and the boss gave them a very very big ang pow for their marriage and also to help them finacially for their new company..his boss gave them a ang pow of $10,000 dollars!!!!! and therefore he worked v v v hard to come out wif ideas for his proposal and also not to disappoint his boss.. everyday, he will work till 6am then he drives home, and every night, xiaolu will bring dinner to his office to dine wif him.. it was tough, but he assured her that this is the last time he is doing this as they will be bosses of their own company v soon..

    so, everyday, Lu works more than 16 hours everyday, and he started to smoke more and more.. while on the other hand, xiaolu was busy preparing for their wedding.. every morning, xiaolu will look at him falling asleep, and she was so loved. she can't wait for the day where she will be officially the wife of Lu.

    so, Lu worked for v long hours everyday, for a continous of one whole week without resting.. finally, he finished the whole project.. and finally he could go home and rest!!! hwoever, it is only less than 36 hours to his marriage!

    xiaolu and him has already made plans. he will come home and rest first, and she will proceed to the hairdresser and prepare herself first, and when she finishes, he will come and pick her up to the hotel for the dinner.

    that morning, xiaolu woke up v early as she was too excited.. she watched him sleepign soundly, and couldnt bear to wake him up to tell him that she is leaving the house already.. so she left the house w/o disturbing him...

    that afternoon, xiaolu was all ready but Lu still wasnt there to fetch her.. she called her house, but he didnt picked up. she called his hp, but he didnt picked up too.. she was worried.. she told herself, Lu will never leave her in the lurch.. she noes that he will come.. and they were get married happily..with blessings from the relatives and frens.. she waited and waited...

    she decided to go to the hotel herself. all the frens and relatives started to arrive but there is still no signs of Lu and his family.. she was really worried..

    then she received a call from Lu's family..
    "XiaoLu, Lu died in his sleep.. he didnt wake up when we woke him up, and we brought him to the doctor and was pronunced dead.. the doctor said his death was due to exhausion.."

    her heart dropped.. every relatives and frens who heard the stories cried.. their eyes were red..

    however, xiaolu told herself to be strong, and she wants to bring this child and gives him lots of love as this child is the last gift from Lu...
