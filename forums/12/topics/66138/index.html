    Ok to start with, I think I am more farmiliar with the 'down under'. Not only bcoz I have been the twice (holiday) but I have lots of friend who have studied there or lived there before. as far as Australia is concern, always choose the state uni. With all due respect, that doesn't mean that others are lousy. Put in this way, the state uni are just more recognised lor. I think I might be able to give you a rough breakdown;

    Tuition fee ~ $6k per semester depending what faculty
    Accomodation ~$150 per week - most would share a house with some friends and that would cut down the cost
    Transportation ~BUY a car. Its dirt cheap as compare to Singapore. Recent a friend just came back from Australia. He bought a 2nd hand car and drove for a year. Guess how much was the car? $1.2k and he actually manage to sell it off after 1 year for $800. And this car has never died on him for that year when he was driving it. Taxi are ex. More ex than Singapore. Otherwise if you live near the school then I guess you could take bus lor.
    Food ~ Better start to learn cooking. Dining out is not that cheap either there. Groceries and raw item s are cheap in supermarket though.
