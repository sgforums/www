    This revolves around the problem of the absence of intergrating into each other's life style.

    You see, what you are doing is level one in maintaining relationship: namely to hang out. Couple hanging out with each other is as good as hanging out regularly with a good friend - constantly finding new things to do and new places to explore. Don't get me wrong, its good stuff - however, to have intergrated lifestyle served a more lasting and fulfilling effect on the relationship.

    What do I mean by an intergrated lifestyle?

    I) A common hobby, goal and interest that both of you has AND to enjoy it together. For example, if you two are into music - certain days are definitely burn into the activities.

    II) Intergrated friends - hangs out with each other's cliche, whichever is more comfortable.

    P.S: You need to have your own life as well. A good relationship essentially needs a healthy lifestyle outside the boundary of your relationship.

    Cheers
