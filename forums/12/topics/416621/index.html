Lessons a 11-yr old girl taught us

  • Law8667

    A 11-year old UK girl Danielle Bradshaw asks to be amputee so that she can move on to pursue a greater dream and higher self.

    Read her story:
    11-year-old girl asks to be an amputee | Metro.co.uk

    This is what true courage is all about, and at such a young age too. Clarity of purpose of what she wants out of life and seizing control of her own life and destiny, instead of whining and complaining about fate and injustice of life.

    Do we too have something in/ on us that is not working for us, is burdening us but yet we are dragging it along and being too afraid to cut it off and move on in life? It could be a bad relationship, grief, fear, negative thoughts, negative habits etc. It is time to know what you want in life, make a choice and decision whether you want to continue to live life like that, or to be courageous enough to make the decision to transform your life in pursuit for the better.

    If anything is not serving you, get rid of it! You don't have to be a victim of life and fate. No one else can decide and determine your destiny unless you allow them too.

    "A man cannot directly choose his circumstances, but he can choose his thoughts, and so indirectly, yet surely, shape his circumstances." - Napoleon Hill.
