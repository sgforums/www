    My case has been on-going for 18 months and yet it is nstill ot resolved.

    My securities firm sold my shares wrongly 18 months ago and I suffered huge losses. I wrote in to them to appeal to return me the wrongly sold shares, however they rejected my appeal.

    Subsequently, I approached FiDrec for help. FiDrec took more than 6 months to hear my case under their adjudication process. The adjudicator dismissed my case, even though there are sufficient evidence that the company sold my shares wrongly. I was extremely disappointed, but I perservered and wrote in to Mr Prime Minister and MAS for help.

    MAS claimed that they are not able to help, which I simply cannot understand why. If they can't, who can??

    PM's staff provided me alternatives which could not work at all, either the legal institution do not handle disputes regarding stocks or the mediation fees is exhorbitant. He also suggested that I appealed to the CEO of FiDrec, of which FiDrec's answer was that the adjudicator's decision was final, so there was nothing they can do about my case. Subsquently, I wrote back to Mr Prime Minister because I really have no solution and noone else to turn to. However, it has been 3 months since I wrote to him and no reply so far.

    My case has dragged for 18 months and it was a tedious and long appeal process. However, I am not giving up hope. I really hope through this channel, I can get some response for my case. It was handled unjustifically and yet noone is able to help. I cannot believe this is Singapore and the system seems to just close the door on you. I feel so helpless even though I am a pure Singaporean and the Singapore Government has always claimed that they do their best for their citizens, but in my case, I do not get such treatment at all. What I get is no-reply to my emails and behind shut doors, and no solution and punishment to the securities firm for their wrongful deed.
