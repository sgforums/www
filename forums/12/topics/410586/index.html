    Good CCA records:

    1) leadership appointments in your CCA/ Class etc

    2) Performance in your CCA. e.g. Did you win any competitions, receive any awards etc

    3) academic-related acheivements. e.g. olympiads medalist.

    4) CIP. quality of CIP also counts. are you just a member, or do you played any role in organising the CIP activity.

    All these counts towards your portfolio and are reflected in your testimonials. The scholarship selection committee takes your school's graduation testimonial seriously when considering you for scholarship.
