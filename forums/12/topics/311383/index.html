    Between me and my gf, quarrels actually make our love stronger.

    When we have disagreement, we take time out first.

    Then we agree that we will both work to find a solution beneficial to both of us.

    Then we come clean with what is bothering us inside our hearts.

    Then we ask each other what do each of us expect from each other.

    Then we will work towards the solution.
