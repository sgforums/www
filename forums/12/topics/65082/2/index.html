    Most of the time begginers get the notion that if one eat alot is the fastest way to gain weight, however how much can an individual eat in a day?

    Thats where mass gainer offer so much flexibility in the sense that one serving (50g protien and 50g protien) is more easy to digest and isolate than probably 3 plates of chicken rice.

    However mass gainer alone is not the best option as you tend to lose out other beneficial aspects such as vitamins, fibre and complex carbohydrate brown rice and potatoes has to offer(Not to mention the inherent beneficial properties that nutritionist has yet to figure out)
    Generally mass gainer are most often use as a post workout meal to aid in speedy recovery.

    Remember its not the quantity that matters, its the quality thats the main concern.
    Try getting a little bit out of every food (again read up on the nutritional book) to balance up.

    If your frame is small and just begin on weight training, an ideal weight gainer of say 50 to 100 grams of carbo and 30grams of protien should be more than suffice.
