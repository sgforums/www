    Hi Music Mathers,

    The only sponsorships which I can find are for diploma holders to take up degree courses and degree holders to take up Post-Graduate Diploma courses. Note that they are only for regulars.

    http://www.scdf247.sg/index.php

    Click on either "Senior Officer", "Fire and Rescue Specialist" or "Paramedic", followed by "Career Prospects". 

     

    Since you have already chosen to enlist first and have received your letter of enlistment, I will strongly suggest that you try to think about which course you will like to take up in ITE, and read up on it in your free time if possible.

    Alternatively, you can choose to take up private diplomas after you have served your National Service, though they will be much less recognised than certificates from local polytechnics. If you really think that you will want to choose such a route, I will suggest that you try to get at least a private Bachelor degree instead of just stopping at a diploma.

    Cheers.
