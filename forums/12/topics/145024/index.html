    - High ISO up to 1600. Many raved about the night shots it can take well, relative to other digi cam. Apparently, it is best at handling night shots and dim light condition because of its high ISO.

    What i dislike about F10:
    - the screen is of poor resolution.

    - good battery life. About 370 shots per charge
    - comparing same shots to F10, the shot taken in the shopping mall seems to look brighter. But as i mentioned, it could because Sony P200 has a higher resolution screen than F10.

    Price wise:
    Sony P200 - $699.
    F10 - $560.
