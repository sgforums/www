    Again, you're thinking from your own perspective. When you ask them why they are behaving negatively, you are sending them a signal. You're telling them that you know that they are behaving negatively towards you. See if they have a job problem or personal problem then offer your help. Naturally, they won't tell you, but it's important to let them know that you're there to help.

    Not asking them, not finding out the problem is the easy way out. But it doesn't solve the problem. By not being candid, i.e. by not asking them, you're just avoiding the problem. Just probe once in a while, while not appearing to be pushy.

    What's so hard about monitoring? Again, tell them frankly that they are under your supervision and monitor. Tell them the target they need to reach and their responsibilities. It's their responsibility to hit the sales target.
