    To clear any misconceptions, UniSim is only offering PART-TIME degress, and the degree is conferred by them. on the other hand, SIM is offering full-time degree courses from other foreign universities such as RMIT, UOL and University of Buffalo. UniSim is just an extension of SIM. So its all up to u really. If you want full-time studies, then its SIM.

    I was an ex-student of SIM. I studied for a overseas degree there but due to personal reasons, had 2 drop out after only 1 sememster. However, the 4-5 months there was probably 1 of the best times of my life. Now, I am studying in another institution, but it really cannot compare with the time at SIM. Then somemore, SIM got offer snacks during tea-break, and their canteens are much much better than the pathetic 4-5 stalls I see in my current school's canteen. I guess its the friends I made at SIM, as well as the entire environment there that makes it special. During exams, its not hard 2 see the study benches, library and study rooms fully occupied. The students there are much more motivated than other places.

    So, if u plan to go UniSim or SIM, my advice would be to go for it! It will be a memorable 2-3 years of your life.
