    Inspired by AA and Dedicated back to you ...

    My Patch of grass~

    "Sometimes I sit down in a park... Don't know what I'm looking for... Though the world is so BIG.... The trees so small.... When I look at them... I know they've got families and more... Yet we cut them down.. Are they ignored...?

    Oh trees living everywhere... It's huge jungles we simply can't compare... But no matter where they are... They tell me of their stories... Through the sun and rain which have dried... And their friends just standing by... Through the hundreds of years they've seen... But the same old thing...?

    The same friends they never got bored of... The families they all knew of... Of the different weeping tales they've heard.... The happy laughing children's chorus... Through their roots they embrace us.... No weather too strong for them...

    Take me my little patch of grass... Show me how tall you'll grow up to be... With your daddy, your mummy and all the lovely and unkind ones.... You'll grow to be so simple... Yet beautiful...."
