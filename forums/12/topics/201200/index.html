    one thing few ppl know is, the shape of your chest cavity area affects whether you are going to be fat or thin or average. breath in, and see in the mirror the shape of the upwards curve of your bottom rib cage. if it is a deep semicircular curve (mesomorphic) you will tend to put on fatty weight much more. if it is a gradual upwards curve you will be tend to be of medium muscular build. if it slants more like the top of a triangle (endomorphic) you will tend to always be thin/slim and harder to put on weight.

    do not diet by skipping meals, when you keep not eating your body's metabolism slows down (goes into starvation mode) and breaks down your food slower. and then you will probably eat more after skipping some meals.
