    Though it's human nature to react to a situation or person, but sometimes, it's not the most effective thing to do. Being neutral to a problem or situation will allow a person to see and think more clearly about the root cause of the problem, and not be sucked into the drama, which may backfire and result in what you felt after your well-intended efforts.

    Often, difficult situations are needed for the person involved to learn from and grow as a person, and not for external parties to get involved and disrupt the learning opportunity meant only for the person involved. In such situations, a sincere and caring listening ear is all that is needed, not more non-neutral opinions.
