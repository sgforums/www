    Application Procedures
    Applications for the Notice of Marriage are to be submitted at least 21 days and up to three months before the date of solemnisation.

    Both parties must be present at the Registry with the following original documents:

    Singaporean - NRIC & Birth Certificate;
    Permanent Resident (PR) - NRIC, Birth Certificate, Passport & Re-entry Permit;
    Malaysian - NRIC, Birth Certificate & Passport;
    ID/Passport of 2 witnesses above the age of 21 years;
    Divorcee - Original or Certified True Copy of the Decree Nisi Absolute in addition to the relevant identification documents;
    Widowed Person - Late spouseÂ’s Death Certificate in addition to the relevant identification documents.

    Age Requirements

    21 years and above.
    If an applicant is between 18 and 21 years old, he/she requires the parentsÂ’ consent to marry. Both parents and two adult witnesses with NRICs must also be present.
    If an applicant is below 18 years old, he/she will need to call at Registry of Marriages to apply for a Special Marriage License.
