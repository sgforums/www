    9 Oct 10, 19:42

    Freedom from work, from family's conditioning, from societal norms one can add on infinitum. However, freedom to be entails the courage to be despite the rejection despite the odds - it presupposes one is an end unto oneself.

    In other words, one is self-fulfilled, a porous self and one can be and do but one never encroaches or uses something/another to be free.

    Lots of people want to be free but the erroneous stance is that something outside of oneself will do it. It wont and even when it happens it is a short-lived phenomena.

    The wise would reject nothing but wisely partake of what is and still be from that which one partakes of. The rejection or the disapproval comes in cos one is mired in conceptualized models. The self with all its conditioning, programming + defintions is the obstacle. Hence the divided, the inner split, the misery, the anguish, the anxiety.

    Let me reiterate - it is an inward process. Now everyone or almost all are trying to live from the outside IN.  Trying to live is not live - it is inherently flawed - one is waiting for something/someone to fulfil or conditions to be fulfilled.

    However, when one is intergrated from within it is different expereince altogether. Personality is added to one's centre it forms a crust. Individuality is from within and no crust forms. Indivisible is individuality. Reconciling or at least understaning these dichotomizing/polarising elements is helpful.
