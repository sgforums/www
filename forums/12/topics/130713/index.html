    Originally posted by m.c.king:
    Any tips on how to compliment someone without sounding sarcastic or misunderstood?
    1. be honest - state something that is evident, not lie or make things up
    e.g : your new shorter hairstyle suits you well (when person has haircut)

    2. specific - about what it is that you have noticed but dont go on and on. a line or two is good enough
    e.g. the way you use the colours on this page is very co-ordinated.

    3. say your feelings - about it
    e.g. i like the way you did the presentation today, the music bit in the introduction was very clever

    4. dont highlight yourself or anyone after the compliment.
