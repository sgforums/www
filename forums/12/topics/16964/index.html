    u got transport?
    if u got, i know alot of places

    take cab also can lah... can go, but the return trip u ownself settle.

    West Coast Park - muchiam pasir ris like that, very fun ah. near to a 7-11 also, the NUS hostels there.

    East Coast Park - cliche lah, but quite steady also ^_^

    Jurong Island - abit smelly but very nice views. very VERY VERY VERY few ppl.

    Jurong Hill - overlooks JI, got a very nice chocolate smell, very nice view as well :p

    Changi Coast Road - junction to tanah merah ferry station there. see aeroplanes, see stars (VERY CLEAR AH!), see commandoes (fit fit ah!) ^_^

    Pebble Bay - tanjong rhu there. overlooking the sea, can see nicoll highway. very nice place.

    Benjamin Sheares Bridge - below the grassy area there, very nice view of the singapore river and CBD skyline. very chio chio ah.
    confirm no ppl one.

    Kranji Dam - can see johor straits and johor night skyline. beware of crocodiles

    Punggol Pier - nice view of the sea, but quite crowded i think. coz alot of ppl fishing there. but still very nice.

    Seletar Reservoir Upper Zone - ultra ulu, bring stuff there will form mist, good for teh-teh
    the other reservoirs also have.
