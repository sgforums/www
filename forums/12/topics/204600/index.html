    I'm glad you are sick of working. Working in part-time jobs or full time jobs that doesn't pay well will only make one feel lousy. I'm glad that you have considered studying to widen up your future job opportunities.

    For a start, maybe you can start asking yourself what do you really want to pursue?

    Before stepping into the "uni study-life", think of what profession you want to set yourself into. Because the course you are about to take, is not as easy as those in poly. So you have to consider carefully and avoid taking courses you've no interest in or not good at, and that will spell disaster.

    From there, make a decision on which course you want to pursue. It doesn't matter if it's a local uni or a private foreign uni. It matters when you achieved what you really wanted.

    Never mind, take your time to decide on what you want to contribute to the society; on making a difference. Then from there, work out your plan and objectives. Once you've set your goal, go for it.
