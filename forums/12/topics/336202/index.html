    coffeeortea,

    Instead of doing inside out.. you do outside in approach.

    First.. you go look at the job you want. Meaning your final destination.

    Do you want to be a marketing director ?

    Do you want to be a financial controller ?

    Do you want to be global operation executive officer ?

    Do you want to be HR director ?

    etc etc.

    You go and look at the academic requirements for those kind of post. Your academic background is only a tool for you to get where you want to go.

    For you to excel.. you must be interested in those courses you choose.

    So take a look at the curriculum of the various subjects for each course.

    Compare them and figure out which one you are strong or weak at.

    Pick the one you think you have the highest confidence to do well.

    Go down to the library and look at the course books. Have an idea what the course will be teaching.

    Once you have all the information , you can make an educated decision.

    Please don't expect the forumers here to spoon feed you all the details, you need to be more resourceful than this to make it through uni, ESPECIALLY business related course.

    Good luck.
