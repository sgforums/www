    A simple break down.
    Things to spend daily/weekly:-
    2stroke bike
    1.) Petrol
    2.) 2T
    3.) ERP
    4.) Parking (Coupon)

    4stroke bike
    1.) Petrol
    2.) ERP
    3.) Parking (Coupon)
    (daily/weekly is because depends on the model of bike u are buying and the location u are traveling on a daily basis. example: Some bikes 2-3 days need to pump petrol if they travel to and fro from east to west daily.)

    Monthly
    1.) Installments (if u not buying one-time pay)
    2.) Parking (Season)

    Half-Annually
    1.) Mainteance (this is the changing of engine oil, spark plugs, etc) If you hoot ur bike jialat jialat everyday, this mainteance becomes a quarterly thing

    Annually
    1.) Insurance
    2.) Road tax (u can also pay half-annual)

    And all of the above depends on which bike you buy. For example, most sports bike pay a higher insurance premium compare to those Ah-pek bikes.
