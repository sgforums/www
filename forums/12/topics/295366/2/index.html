    I worked in an office IT department before, I can tell you that the IT department usually have full access/control of all office emails account.

    In IT context, you only need an auto-email-forwarding function to receive additional emails. For example, you can create a rule that auto-forward all emails sent to a Director's email account to his/her Secretary email's account as well. Then whatever emails the Director receives, the secretary will receive them as well.

    When it comes to email, the only way to ensure/include privacy is to use encryption, else you can always assume someone else other than the intended recipients will be able to read your emails.
