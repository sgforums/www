    In theory of appreciation in relationship, everybody belongs to three sides.

    (I) - Appreciation is not important. People who belongs to this category do not seek for returns at all. They do what they should from a duty of a gf/bf and seek near-perfect understanding to please their partners. Its an unwritten rule and they follow by them. They are often taken for granted.

    (II) - Appreciation is needed in a form of a minimal returns. Something like your credit card. You can have a bf who neglects you and you treat him rather well - the minimal returns of appreciation to you would probably be an sms saying that he loves your cooking, etc (although he may be spending his time with his buddy watching soccer and won't return until the next day). The woman can still function under this. In fact, alot people fall under this category.

    (III) - People who fall under this category value appreciation very much. These people could probably been under-appreciated before, or simply their ideal in love. They will do equal effort in returning appreciation and they understand this saying and practice them 'treasure what you have and whats around you.' Beware, because if you under-appreciate them - they will be dis-satisfied and could prove unseen problems and complication in the relationship.
