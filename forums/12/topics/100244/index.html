    One day, a teacher brought a student to a big field, filled with beautiful flowers and green grass. The teacher asked the student to walk straight through the field, and pick one flower which he thinks is the most beautiful, ".. but ur only able to choose ONE flower, just one, and bring the flower back to me" the teacher said. So the boy started walking across... a few minutes later, the student returned empty handed, and the teacher asked him, "why didn't you pick a flower?"
