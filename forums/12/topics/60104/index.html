    Hi everyone,

    Just felt like putting my thoughts into words and expressing how i've been feeling these days. I am currently still studying in a poly doing an engineering course. Engineering was never my first choice of study and neither was studying in a polytechnic. Due to my D7 english scored during the Os, i was forced to choose the not-so language based courses like engineering. I will not justify myself by saying i am good in english, but my standard of english has been at least good enough to secure a B4. I retook and got a B3. i hesitated to apply back into a jc as i thoroughly enjoyed myself during the first 3 months period the year before. As i was unable to come to a final decision as in to stay in the jc or not, i withdrew partly due to lack of confidence for i had not touched any of the relevant subjects for a year. I stayed on in my course and struggled till now, my final year. My overall including my D7 english when i appealed for jc that year was 18points. As to why i did not try business course in poly was the fact that i was embarrassed and afraid to appeal as a previous appeal attempt to stay on in jc was unapproved.

    I have completed my attachment just recently and during that period i did alot of thinking through. I felt that i was unable to contribute much to my company due to my lack of interest and lack of knowledge. Though i have managed to learn something and my bosses were saying i did alright, i just felt lousy that i could not make that extra difference. Everything in my life is going in circles. I could go on and on about everything in my life and i think it will probably just bore everyone of you. I am now going to end another phase of my life soon, and i have been worrying as to what i would want to take up next time, be it further studies or career. Though i may have an interest in business, i fear going into it only to find i am not cut out for it. I maybe be a jack of many trades but a master of none. I do not know what i am even good at. I really envy those who knows very early in life what they want to do in the future and what they are good at. For me, everything is jsut a question mark. I am sick of living my life this way and everything is basically crap right now. I have little friends whom i keep in contact with, let alone close too. I got back to Christ about a year ago and i seriously doubt my motives for going back to Christ. Sometimes i am so tired of everything i just wish i could land somewhere in timbuktu and lead a very simple life all alone.

    To most i guess it would seem that i am the result of everything happening to me due to my reinforced perceptions. But i can tell you, i've tried. Nothing seems right. To those who have been through this phase of life, or have overcomed it, do enlighten me with your experience on how you dealt with it, and to those who got nothign constructive to say, i suggest you shut your hole and save your sniggers to yourselves. I welcome healthy and constructive criticism though.
