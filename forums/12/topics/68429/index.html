    You can see the doctor too, but the window period for testing opportunity opens only after like in my earlier post 2-3 weeks after conception. This is the time when the hormones(hcg) caused by pregancy is at its peak and is released into the bloodstream.

    PTKs tests are on urine samples since the hcg is present in blood going thru the kidney. How accurate PTKs can be depends on the sensitivity of the test itself. Some PTKs test much lower values of hcg present thus providing more accurate results.

    Doctors test blood samples which is accurate to 99.9% since the amount of hcg contained in the blood is higher.
