    Sad to say the 'average' gaming slowly developed into full-pitch addiction, and i was playing for 8 hrs a day almost non-stop during weekends, and bout 4 hrs during normal school periods, though i have a slightly busy schedule from 3rd year polytechnic

Well i do noe tat comp game addiction is bad but its hard to control sometimes, especially for ppl like me who are repeat addicters
