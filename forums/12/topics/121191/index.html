Especially subfields like zoology, marine biology, agricultural science, they are number one, no doubt about that. Ask any university lecturers in australia, they will acknowledge that. Go to the auggie web and see for yourself. The no. of research articles published in international journals by all universities in each individual fields are listed. For biological science, UQ is the top. They have the Institute of Molecular Bioscience which attracts the best scientists in australia to do research in brisbane. UQ is geographically very close to the famous great barrier reef, and they own most of the research stations situated at the islands there, which makes UQ probably the best place to study marine science in the world. Of course, having said that, other universities have their niche area, if i want to do music, uni melb may be a good choice. So check out the facts, dun just hear your friend say this say that, their views may not be objective.
