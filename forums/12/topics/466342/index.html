At first I was a little startled, but maintained my composure. I told him that I have no idea as the teacher did not indicate to me of any incident today. He also said that he did not ask the teacher but it was his son that mentioned my son is the culprit. (It is my habit and practice to ask my son's teachers about son's performance in school everyday; whether he has drank enough water, did he create any problems for the teachers or did he enjoy the extra music lessons we registered him for.) He proceeded to ask if my son can respond to questions. I turned around, looked at my boy and asked if he has done what he is being accused of. He said no... Not able to get a yes from my son, he walked away not very much pleased. I drove off, rushing to pick my wife up from work yet thinking of the whole incident. When I reached my wife's office, I immediately called the school up and queried about the boy's bruised lips. Now, what the school told me was in total contradiction of what the father had accused my son of doing. Yesterday, I emphasis yesterday; morning - the class was having some music and dance lesson. So, all the children are dancing, jumping, laughing and I guess having some crazy fun time when my son, apparently from what the teacher had witness, accidentally knocked into this boy and he bit his own lips. there was little blood but a bruise nonetheless. The teacher who witnessed the incident also informed the parents of the boy yesterday about what happened that it was purely accidental. She said she has explained the whole situation in detail to the mother and she has acknowledged that the incident was an accident. So now comes my anger/frustration/pissed off/wtf mood. The teacher has already said it was an accident involving two 2 years old boys. The school explained to you in detail. So why are you accusing my son of deliberating injuring your son? You know the facts, you know the truth so what's with the finger pointing? Do you wish for me to pay for the medical fees incurred? I mean, grow up...
