    Definitely don't sign on if you are only unsure of what you want to do. It is a road of no return, if you regret signing on, there is no turning back. Also even worse you have a tougher time later, if i remember correctly there is an upper age limit too around 45 to 50 to which you can serve, after which you will be force to a career change.

    And at that time you will even be more unsure of what to do, for those with good qualifications, they get high pay in Army, while for those low ranking one, only enough to live by. If it is what you really want, you must be prepare to bear all that must come, even if you fail at what you choose, at least you tried, no regrets. What the point of living a life you don't like, just plodding the way thru. The main thing is do you have the courage to accept the consequences of your choice.
