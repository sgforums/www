    - Breakfast: 1 tau huey, 1 piece you tiao. 1 soya bean drink.
    - Lunch: Fried bee hoon with 1 egg.
    - Dinner: Spaghetti with 2 pieces mackerel (Ayam brand). 1 cup-a-soup.

    Recipe for vegies soup:
    Ingredients needed: 1 large potato, 1 large carrot, 1 large onion, 1 large celery, 1 large tomato, handful of anchovies, 1 cube chicken stock.
    Steps: Boil water. Throw in chicken stock. Cut everything into chunks. Throw into boiling stock. Boil for 3 hours. Add salt, pepper, shoyu & sesame oil to taste.

    Voila
