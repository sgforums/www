    Regarding your meeting up with her... You can TRY the 4 Pointers....

    1. Simple outing. Make your outing SIMPLE and SHORT. Like just a lunch or dinner... You might not be able to get everybody to come (see pointer 2), but at least it's a START... Some of them in the group might rush off for other things while you can suggest something or a programme for those who can stay back (and hopefully it'll include her..?)... (also see Pointer 3) The MAIN thing about this point is that when the outing is SIMPLE and SHORTER, the chances of turn up would usually increase relatively if the main reason for the people to come is just simply meeting up....

    2. Partial Attendance. Have you organise outings or gatherings before? Usually it's quite often that you can't get a big group of people to turn up on the same date and at the same time... And a few of them last minute simply can't make it.... As described in your post, many simply can't make it... If you can't get EVERYBODY, get SOME of them.... It's still a gathering? Ask her out on that... During the meet up, just explain why 'who & who' can't make it etc.... It's common....

    3. Partition of a day's programme... If you wanna make it a long hours gathering but the group can't afford the kinda time, DIVIDE out the programme... (refer to Point 1) Like if Mr 'X' says he can't make it... Suggest he just join the group for lunch or dinner... Explanation to the group would then be... "Oh.. You still can wish that the girl would be there throughout (if she's really interested in you and she's not occupied with other things...)....

    4. Take it as a 'yes'? It's quite often that people can't confirm their attendance at some gatherings... Take it as a 'yes' from them 1st and encourage them to come... Any last minute changes there'll be no obligations... At least you'll still manage to get your girl out there...?
