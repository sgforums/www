    Hello Timble. Most of the time we all want to have a desirable relationship, in which the guy should be caring, gentle yet manly. And the girl should be supportive and loving. But there's always a big difference between ideal and reality.

    Life is complicated. Lets face it. And why so ? Its not the world that presents itself that makes things complicated.. its how you want to think that makes complications even more... complicated.

    Take for example, your bf's 'vulgarity'. To you, its probably a matter of etiquette, or rather ur bf's lack of it. To him, its probably a freedom of speech, a way to express his thoughts and release tension. This is a 2-way ideal.

    Find time to sit down alone, and sort things out alone. Ask yourself what is the real problem that causes the 2 of u to have frequent arguements ? Are the 2 of u communicating effectively during the arguements ?

    Or is there the ego problem ? Because neither would want to really sit down and listen, enforcing your own theories.

    I feel the question ultimately is not 'Is love enough' ? It is 'Are we really listening' ?

    Just my 2 cents.. hope this helps a little at least.
