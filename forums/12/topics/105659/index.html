    When we see someone who has a beautiful wife, living in a bungalow, drives a benz, changes car every month, and is a very successful person, and we compare him to ourselves, it can lead to several endings, but lets stick to the topic.

    3) Hatred of world: You start to hate the world, blaming everything that its unfair, how he grew up with a golden spoon in his mouth, etc.

    This is one of the sources of Hate i guess..
