    Dear m_bear,

    Marriage is a wonderful thing. A heavenly union of two souls meant to be together forever. All these are part and parcel of marriage and fortunately, life too. Though, i may be young to advice you regarding marriage issues but this problem may be similar to pre-marital relationships as well.

    Anyway, m_bear , everyone goes through all kinds of stress. He need not give u a hug or kiss to show you how much he loves you or care for you. Maybe he is tried from work or you didn't drop hints to him that you need some TLC from him

    I know that it is frustrating and aggravating to yearn to talk to someone and not have them listen. You feel like you're talking to a blank wall but unfortunately, it's something that we all have to deal with sometimes. I'm sure that your husband is a sweet guy but when you're in a marriage you have to respect the feelings of the other person. If not, it's unfair to both you and your husband because you're losing out on the communication and it's communication that is KEY in a relationship.

    Just maintain everything like normal and give him a hug or kiss. He may or may not reciprocate the same thing back but you should know that he loves you and thats why you are in a marriage with him.

    Listen with empathy. Empathy is your ability to see things from your husband's perspective. Listen to your husband and tune in to his feelings. Whatever your husband is feeling is logical from his perspective. When you start to get it , you will know not only what your partner is feeling and thus, you will also understand why.

    Cheers
