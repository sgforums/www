    Dibilo,

    It may be love, it may Not be love yet, it's difficult to tell right now.

    You can't have the mindset whereby if you're Not in love, you won't make a move. Love is developed over time, with fondness, touching, & all those things that couples do.

    Usually, love is Not 1-sided. True love isn't about an all-encompassing love to the point whereby you smother your partner. It's built with a solid foundation, you're comfortable with this person to let this person see your faults, knowing that she'll accept them & love you anyway.

    You can fart, burp & dig your nose in front of this girl, & she'll still love you even if she teases you.

    And love is when even if she hurts you so very deeply, you can bring yourself to forgive her anyway.

    You're also in love if you can bring yourself to put her need for space above your need to cling on to her, i.e. you love her enough to respect her wishes.

    A whole lotta things make up love, it's complicated, it's complex, it's full of mysteries, but damn, is it wonderful.
