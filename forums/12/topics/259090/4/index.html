    Originally posted by sgGirlx:
    I tested it was negative.. Maybe beacuse of lousy brand? Getting another one tomorrow.. Can any tell me the symtoms of pregnancy?
    Sometimes test kits may give you false negative results.

    The test kit rely on the trace hormone in your urine.
    If there is not enough hormone to react with the chemical it will not show as positive.

    I suggest you go to a obstetrician/gynecologist to confirm this.
    There is nothing to be ashamed about.
    They will not report you to the police or tell your parent.

    The ob/gyn will probably do a ultrasound scan or perhaps a blood test to confirm if pregnancy is present.

    If you are not pregnant, please talk to the ob/gyn about contraceptives.
    You will be surprised how many options you have other than condoms.

    But remember that condoms can help prevent STD ( Sexually transmitted diseases).
    Oral or intrauterine contraceptives will not prevent STD.

    Symptoms of pregnancy differs with many people.
    Some experience it, some don't.
    So do not base your judgment on symptoms.

    Please do not delay the visit to doc any longer.
