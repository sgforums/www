    Everything, including paying early termination fees come to about $3k.

     

    At last, he replied an email saying she had harassed her family and refused to pay her a dime. Please note that this guy is a business man who claims to earn revenue of $10 million SGD annually. When he started off his company, Ms A had actually provided him a capital of $20k at least. During his cash flow difficulties, Ms A gave her ATM card to him where he withdraws the money to tide his business (another $10k), promising to return a few months later. And that never happens till date.

     

    When Ms A confronted him in his office, he kept saying he is still using the cable TV box but just refuse to terminate the account or return the setup box. And of course refuse to pay because he said she was also watching cable TV. Ms A replied she wants to cancel the cable TV but he just refused to return the setup box! As for the mobile line, he not only refused to pay the mobile bills, he even refused to do a line transfer. He also refused to return the M1 mobile broadband set.

     

    And in retaliation of claiming Ms A is asking him for money (when she is only claiming bills he had incurred and nothing more), he told the housing agent to kick her out of her rented room within 12 hours of notice. It was till then that she realized that the agent was in cahoots with him.

     

    Mr Scoundrel kept claiming Ms A used his money etc … but this is the type of guy who says ‘I will buy this for you … but you foot the bill 1st’ So at the end of the day, he went around claiming he bought lots of branded stuffs for Miss A while Miss A had to pay on her own for most of the branded stuffs.
