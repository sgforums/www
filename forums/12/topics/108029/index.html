    Anyone Loves music so much that they feel warmth from beautiful music and feel good whenever there is music? Anyone believes that music can be as serious as a passion that it is considered a religion for them?

    Anyone feels comforted when you are all alone but there is music? Music as a religion, is it possible? Instead of bible or quran, we'll have musical theories, instead of prayers, we'll have singing, instead of going to religious areas, we go to studios and compose songs, instead of fighting with other religions, we share our experiences and opinions of our genre. Sure, we may be splitted into many categories, like rock, pop, punk, rap, etc. etc. But we all have one thing in common, we love music =) instead of prayers when we meet supernatural beings, we sing soul music, like the reason by hoobastank, or the spirit carries on by dream theatre. instead of being officially converted like baptisim or circumize, which may be permanent, we could choose to leave whenever we like or so.

    To some, music may just be a passion, to some, music may be a job, but, to me, it is as powerful as a religion. Any other like minded people in here? The saddest show in the world would lose its impact if there isn't a sad song accompanying it. The most frightening show in the world wouldn't be quite scary if there weren't any haunting music with it. The most touching show would not make you break down and cry if there were no music. Where there is no music, there is no life. So, can music be a religion? Please add in your views here.. comments appreciated, no matter good or bad. =)
