    Hey peeps, perhaps you can share your sentiments and opinions here.

    Have you dreamt about a good friend of yours in 'that' manner before?

    'That' as in, the romantic, lovey-dovey kind of way, not the sexual innuendos manner.

    Just a few nights ago, I had a dream of this good female friend of mine. We've been friends for more than 4 years, and throughout this period, I've only consciously thought of her in 'that' way once or twice. The last time I thought of her in 'that' manner was... At least 3 months ago.

    In the dream, the furtherest we went in terms of physical intimacy was to hug and hold hands. She has lovely brown eyes, and in that dream, I can recall myself gazing straight into them!

    Frankling speaking, I was rather freaked after waking up. You see, she has been attached for the past four years, and I know her beau too. My principle has always been to not think about or woo attached women. Hence, this sudden dream about her proved a little too much to handle.

    She's a rather elegant and alluring person, that I grant you. Still, I can't help feeling a tinge of uneasiness.

    I wonder if anyone else here has a similar experience to share?

    Cheers.
