    hi,
    firstly, you need to bring your wife out to social activities such as some organised by CSC or etc. Let her mix with the people. Choose courses that allow her to mingle like cooking, dancing or even go with her to morning exercise which the CSC does on weekends.

    with that build confidence, and once the tongue loosens, things are easier.
    she will have chance to interact with other housewives with children and trust me, they will talk.

    secondly, at start of year, perhaps you can sit down with her and work out some goals. Start with something for the child, like knowing how to read the mulitpication tables, and then for you, to learn something, and then her, and lastly for the family. From here, you can inject some of what you want. but not big items just small ones. and then review half-yearly, and inject more of for the whole family.

    Do observe what she likes to do. for example, cooking, then you can enrol her as instructors in CSC or something, but one thing, don't just throw her there, be there with her and your child.

    Lastly, praise your wife when she does something nice in front of your child. People like to be praised. but when your wife try to be difficult, try to speak to her in another way, but infront of child, so she knows she can't throw her temper. Say something like "see, mummy disagrees with this, but baby know right? for the family to move, we baby, mummy and daddy must move in this way, right?"

    bottomline, this is a situation that has evolved over time, nothing can be changed overnight. Bring out your love and patience and make slow steps like building blocks to encourage yourselves. show your happiness and never never give up. what you have is a family that you as the head should never throw the towel in.

    Rem, a bad day only lasts 24hrs. - my favourite line.

    take care, and God Bless.

    bye.
