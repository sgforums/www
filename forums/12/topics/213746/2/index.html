    Depends on your situation.

    If you want to improve yourself and do well in order to go to SMU or NUS, then I would suggest you go to a JC.

    If you want to go to the technical side or get to the workforce early, then I'd suggest you take up a diploma.

    The competition is very intense between JC students. So if you're not doing very well in JC, you might lose your chance to go to a university to do the course of your choice.

    Although there are some others who managed to go into the course of their choice in a local university despite their mediocre results, these are either based on some exceptions or they are sort of, fortunate. But one should not depend on their own luck for this matter. Studying hard and SMART is always a must in order to get ahead in life.
