    3 Dec 05, 08:08
    Sounds like you love yourself less than your partner loves you.

    You may not have confidence in yourself as a lover, but what about in your partner?

    Do you trust your partner?
    Have you faith in your partner?
    Is your partner deserving of your love?

    If not in yourself, perhaps you should have confidence in your partner. At least in the choice he has made -- you. Out of kind and considerate intentions, you may doubt your ability to fulfill his expectations. However, you are also in effect questioning his decision in choosing you to love, and that may actually cause him more hurt than good. Is that the way you would repay him for all that he has done for you, all that he has given you?

    Try not to compare the love he has given with what you can offer. To paraphrase a movie quote: You are not perfect. He is not perfect either. But as long as you are perfect for each other, that is all it matters.
