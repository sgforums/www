    With people suggesting outing for AA and realisation that alot of people actually have much bottomed up in themselves, I've just done a little thinking..

    This is just at a brainstorming stage. Thought of organising outings (AA 'live' sessions'). Reasons being to better facilitate suggestion of solutions to individuals through verbal discussions (which would be more interactive and speedy compared to online). Of course, this would not replace AA forum's position as some people would still prefer to post online to their convenience and advices through the 'live' sessions would be restricted to advices given by only those who can make it for the 'sessions'. It can also act as a support group where people who's down in their life can listen to what others have to say and share, and to exchange numbers for future contacts if they want?

    The IDEA is for people to E-MAIL or PM me and have their identity a CONFIDENTIALITY if they find it necessary. Their problems (e-mail or PM) would be discussed over during 'sessions'. I've also realised that some people might not like their story to be posted out in the open. So here's some suggestions to cater for different cases...

    1. People who want their cases discussed but can choose whether to have their ID withhold. In the case where ID is withhold, the person might wanna use a representative nick so that I can address him / her.

    2. People who want their stories withhold from posting online. Their story would not be posted online but instead only the reply would be posted. Keeping as little detail in reference to the original story as possible. Only the representative nick would be mentioned.

    3. People who want their replies to be e-mailed back to them.

    With that, I should mention again that this is only a brainstorm stage.

    I would like to hear more opinions and suggestions, and whether forumites think this is a service which they can use?
