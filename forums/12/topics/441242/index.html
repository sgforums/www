    http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u6XAPnuFjJc

    You need three things to gain personal satisfaction from the work you do.

    Purpose - What meaning does the work you perform have? Does it make someone out there smile? Does it help your company in one of its decision-making processes? Think about it why you're doing the work you do.

    Mastery - Is your work mind-stimulating, challenging and gets tougher over time? Or is it dull and repeatative? If it's the same task all the time, you can try challenging yourself by setting goals for yourself (complete in shorter time, 5% increase from last month etc.). All you have to remember, is to strive to do better (or suck less) each time, no matter what it is - and don't forget to reward yourself if you do exceed your own expectations.

    Autonomy - Is there something stopping you from doing your work in a better way? Or you've been thinking about an exciting project but are currently buried under piles of unrelated work? Talk to your superior and see if you can work things out with him/her.
