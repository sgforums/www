    General school of thoughts:

    Some people believes The One comes by fate(yuan)

    Some believes in searching for The One (Active)

    Some believes in waiting for The One (Passive)

    Some believes in having one and making him/her The One

    Some believes in the law of probablity (Trying out and find out who's the best)

    There are many The Ones (One after another)

    Some believes in praying for The One to appear....

    ___________________________________________________

    Personally i didn't believe in the One theory.
    Any girl whom i love will be the One.
    I'm not sure how to phrase it, i just have this feel that there are many people who aren't practical about relationship.
    I mean...how do you qualify him or her as the One?

    Let's say you're age 20 and waiting for the one, one fine day at 11pm Christmas Eve. You bump onto him/her and got to know one another by chance. You liked him/her from the first time you guys bumped onto one another. You know that he/she is the ONE. Things went on smoothly till went character changes and the relationship started drifting apart.
    Everything was done and nothing could draw the two of you together.
    So the both of you broke up.
    The ONe left and got a new relationship after a few weeks.

    My point being: Your The One left and became another girl/guy's The One.
    Are you going to be single all your life since The One has left, and there is nomore The One.
    Or you'll be waiting for The Two to appear?

    Second point being: You think you believe in the One. Do you know what you're believe in? How could you be sure who is the One?
    I'm not trying to put off this thinking/concept/belief/theory, but i question this naive thought from many galfriends i spoke to. Many awoke from their thoughts and realised something very important.

    Third point: I don't believe in the One thingy but if i were to be more emotional and choose a ideal answer. I'd choose: having one and making him/her The One. Because i believe every relationship happens or a reason and making the best out of it, and making sure i'll leave no regrets, even i know that i cannot control the life span between two persons, it hurts to know it has to end someday. But i've got to make sure that Love truly exists in tat particular relationship and in every relationship...
    Why? Mainly because i believe in living to truly taste Love.
    The One is what i make out of...not what pops out before me.

    Fourth point: If you think he/she is the One now. Think again and again and again. After Five to ten yrs down the road, think again of what you've claimed those days.
    Maybe the One standing before you on the Aisle and wearing tuxedo saying wedding vows to you is your eighth or tenth! Haha.

    Who knows.....

    I'll end here and leave the rest for your thoughts. Don't be too sure of what you think. Don't take everything too seriously, yet do not fool around with things you cherish. Learning to Learn thru experiences is a gift in life.

    Cheers
