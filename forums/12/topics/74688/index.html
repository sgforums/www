    Recommended readings can be taken from books by Roger Arnold, John Sloman, Peter Maunder etc. The three I mentioned are just the more common ones around, but different authors have their different good points in different topics, so read widely.

    Also, don't forget to read up on current affairs regarding the economy of course... Read "The Economist".
