    There is two possible reasons:

    I) He doesn't want to go out with you and the reasons could lie anything between the declining stage of a relation to certain exisiting problems or external factor that comes into the picture and add a whole new meaning to your relationship.

    II) He feel bored/uncomfortable going out with you - also another hint that this relationship needs previewing.
