    seperate ways for the initial period....the duration of that seperation should be based on a case to case basis though...rule of thumb...if you've been together for about a year...put a distance between yourself and the ex for half that period...

    This is basically because when two people have been intimate and shared much of their lives together, they've developed a dependency (be it conciously or sub conciously) on that person. In order to completely sever the ties off that dependency, it would be best to use the old saying "time heals all wounds" because a wound is exactly what a break up is.

    Staying in contact with the ex right after the break up will not allow for the wound to breathe....and to heal itself.

    Add in an emotional heart and you get a very messy situation should the rational mind be put to test.
