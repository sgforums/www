    1. hold the mic so that the head is facing your mouth, dun hold the mic below ur mouth or by the side.

    2. when u need to breath while singing sometimes, try not to breath into the mic, when breathing, all u need to do is to lift ur head slightly or to the side.

    3. Sometime when u need to drag a note, its good practice to slow move the mic away from ur mouth as u drag the note.

    these are some easy tips to remember....
