    It appears that the actual presence of this ex is incidental. The heart of the matter is the effect it is having on the relationship between you and your girlfriend. This situation affects each of you in different ways, but you'll also have to consider how it affects the pair of you as a single entity.

    Talk to her and let her know your thoughts on the matter. I'd advise starting with what this situation is doing to BOTH of you - if you start with what it's doing to yourself, you could come out sounding selfish and if if you start with what it's doing to her, you could come off as condescending.

    As for the weird sh!t like the ex leaving stuff on her stable, that's something for the cops to handle. If at all possible, avoid direct action - it's too easy to do the wrong thing and end up looking like the bad guy.
