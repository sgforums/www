    I've only visited Sydney, but I've lived in Melbourne. The latter is romantic, a little more laid back, european charm.. If you *do* choose to go there, here are some places that are worth checking out :

    Melbourne CBD incl Chinatown, charming cafes / bars like Hairy Canary, Robot Sushi Bar (they proudly display a working Atari on premise), Gin Palace ... HardRock Cafe...
    Crown Casino
    Shops along Sth Yarra stretch, all the way to Prahran
    Gay/lesbian dance bars, which are among the most "happening" places at night
    St Kilda, the red light district..
    Mornington Peninsula National Park : snorkeling, swim with dolphins
    Great Ocean Road, Grampians & beyond : be prepared to spend a long time on the road, recommend u stay at least 1 night there (otherwise it won't feel like it's worth the trip)
    Phillip Island : penguins!!
    Yarra Valley wineries : u might find it abit boring if u're not much of a food/wine lover. Has a good hot air balloon tour. Be prepared for alot of travelling.
    Torquay (on the way to Great Ocean Rd) : Must-go for shoppers. Many Surf/swim-wear Factory Outlets. Roxy, No Fear, Ripcurl etc.
    Moorabbin : Must-go for shoppers. Another place that's full of factory outlet. Various brands, most notably Levi's & Canterbury
