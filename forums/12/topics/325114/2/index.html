  • Gedanken
    31 Jul 08, 14:16

    The purpose of life is what slips past you while you're mucking about trying to define the purpose of your life.

    Unless you've got a crystal ball, there's always the likelihood that your best-defined purpose today will be absolutely irrelevant by breakfast tomorrow.  Stay felxible and go with the flow.

    That's not to say that everything's got to be lassiez-faire either.  Rather than wasting time trying to draw up the Master Plan, get a couple of SOPs that will apply in most, if not all, situations.  For example, one SOP that's served me well for a long time now is that if I'm not interested in something, it may as well not exist; on the other hand, if something does get my interest, for that point in time, it's the only thing that exists.  Anything in between is a half-hearted waste of time and effort.
