    1. Recognise your emotional state. Know when you are feeling angry.
    This is the first step. Then know that you are hurt, thats why you are angry.
    2. Why are you hurt? Is your pride attacked? Degraded? Insulted?
    3. Do you fear? That you are made a fool. Do you fear losing something dear?
    4. Think! are all these hurt, and fears real. Or are they just an automatic response, without any substantance, just imaginary.
    How can you deal with them in another way. Take the steps to prevent a recurrence.
    5. By the time you go through the above steps, your anger would have fade away.
