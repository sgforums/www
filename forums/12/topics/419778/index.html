    i would discourage you from taking o lvl as private candidate.

    it requires intensive preparation, and you would need a good tutor to guide you through your subjects.

    and since your from normal tech, and had not touch on O lvl stuff for 2 years, most likely you will need 2 years to start from scratch again. this 2 years would be better spent if you decided to move on straight to shatec or poly, wherever your interest lies.

     

    option 3 also not bad, just anyhow hamtam, and try see whether can get into poly. you have limited time left before you are called up for NS, and you would want to secure a course for NS deferment asap.

    the 2 years of NS would make you stupid, forgetting everything you have learnt. so its better to upgrade yourself as much as possible before NS.

    and don't forget that you have a sell-by date. don't spend too much of your time to pursue full time studies. it would be harder and harder to promote yourself to potential employers as you grow older.

    imagine, being a "fresh poly grad" at 25, or if your good, "fresh degree holder" at 29, how are you going to compete with 20 yrs old fresh degree holders?

    also, another option is to try to pursue part-time studies during NS.
