    hmm, how old is the kid?

    there are 3 types of parenting, depending on the age of the kid.

    dictatorship parenting - for kids. because they don't know what's good for them, what's right and wrong, you decide for them

    advisory parenting - for teens, because they are learning to be an adult, you have to allow them to make certain decisions (things that won't screw up their lives, like CCA choices) without always telling them what to do. they'll automatically resent that. just be there to support them, when they face the consequences of their decisions, for better or for worse.

    consultation parenting - when your child is now an adult. Do make yourself available if they need to ask your valued advices about life. But do not make decisions for them, ever.
