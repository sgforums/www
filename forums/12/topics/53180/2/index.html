    Life's ordeals are meant to make one stronger and better.

    The question we ask ourselves when we encounter problems will be, 'Will these problems break us or make us?' Ultimately everyone face problems every now, what sort of mentality do we have to overcome them will be the key factor.

    Get your priorities right, settle your problems one by one, rather than accumulating them and one day you find it too much to handle. Having an optimistic attitude towards problems will ensure things go right at the end of the day. Start off today by telling yourself you do not cower in the face of problems. Find solutions to problems, instead of whinning over problems and procrastinating over what you can do or cannot do. Don't sit on them. Occassionally, some problems don't have solutions... then just throw them to the wind. Things will fall in place somehow.

    We all live only once.. do not live a life of regrets.

    For all who're troubled, take heart. Life goes on and it's you, yourself who can make the difference in your own life. The rest are bypassers..
