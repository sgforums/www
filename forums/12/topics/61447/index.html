    This is from my view points

    the most important factor is communication, and it's clear communication, not things that you make other people making inference. This will promote the trust, the understanding and reduce the number of fights and suspicion, because we get clear signals of the thoughts and intentions of each other. Will mean both people will need to open their ears and LISTEN (not hear), and both need to speak their views. You need to be able to accept opposing views too.

    the second most important factor is novelty. Both party should never spend their time solely for each other, but retain sometime for yourself and friends on a daily or even weekly basis. Do things that you would do alone, and learn new things to get more experience to share with your partner. It is to arouse the interest in your partner that you do not just have routine life with is all so predictable and lead to boredom of the relationship. This is why it's easy to for someone to come in between a r/s that's at least a year old, and especially a r/s that is even longer than a year.
