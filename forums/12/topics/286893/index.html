    Even if C has a bf, you can still be a better friend to her.

    Sometimes in life, we cannot expect what is given, to be returned to us 100%, especially in relationships.

    I always have this thought: We give willingly, no matter if the other party wants to receive them or not. Thus, we are not entitled to receive any returns from them unless they want to. In love, we give because we love them. However, whether or not they reciprocates our love, it is up to them. We cannot expect them to give their love back to us because in the first place, they did not ask us to give.

    Just like secretly admiring someone is sweet and doing things that you think will touch the person. So long as the person is happy, you will be happy too. In this case, aren't we forcing our Will on that person?
