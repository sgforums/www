    The more I touch you.
    The more I hear your voice,
    The more I fall...in love with you.

    How, for each encounter with you,
    You've made each one a greater experience than the one before.

    I find myself wishing you were there.

    Sends a tingle through my spine.
    Every hug you give,

    And the more I think of it, the more uncanny it seems,
    How, in such a short span of time,
    You've actually stolen my heart and made it yours.

    Thank you for all those wonderful moments.
    Thank you for your love, tolerance and care.
    Thank you for allowing me into your life.
