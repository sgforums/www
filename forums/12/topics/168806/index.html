    Feow, i'll leave the referring to CC to you.

    Meanwhile, i'll answer his question.

    1) Tank is too small for so many fishes.
    Thumb of rule - 2ft = 24inches. 1goldfish sized fish to 3-4inches. Nothing more, otherwise your fishes will suffer.

    2) Filter too weak/small to hold Bio cycle of your tank.
    You'll need a bigger filter area for your tank.
    No matter what, you'll still have to change your tank water all the time because the tank is small. (point 1)
    Aquarium shops have huge filter areas. You see tubs and tubs of water and huge filter sponge hidden underneath the shelf or elsewhere in the shop.

    Solution.
    Remove your tiny fishes or Change to a 3-4ft tank.

    Cheers

    thirdly, as said by some, if you are having too many fishes inside your tank, make sure you jolly well get hold of a super good filter which cleans the water clearly...there are some filters which cannot make it, be sure not to buy the wrong ones...
