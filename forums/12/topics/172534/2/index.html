    Hmm here's my input. What I can say is working as a teacher is not an easy task. Like regarding assessments, a teacher doesn't just teach one class, he/she teaches 3-4 classes. If each student is given 3pages of homework, guess how many pages the teacher has to mark? A class of 40 is 120 pages of work, 4classes is 480 pages! Imagine going through 480 pages of work everyday. What if it's composition writing? 160 stories to read through and correct? It's a tedious job.

    A teacher's job scope is not just limited to teaching. They participate in alot of administration work like in charge of various CCAs. With the CCA HoD pressing down on them, they have to use their already meager time to liase with the CCA coaches, collect coach fees, book locations for CCA usage, manage with the attendance etc. There's a lot of work to do.

    Every year a grade is given to each teacher by the school panel. The grading is dependent on the performance of the teacher throughout the year term. It will affect the amount of bonus the teacher will get, he/her ranking and it's like an assessment on whether he/she is able to cope on tutoring the current level or not.
