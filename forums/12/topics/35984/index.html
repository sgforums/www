    Confine the dog to his playpen lined with newspaper, or bring him to a pile of newspaper after his meal. If he does it at the right place, praise him profusely. Then slowly reduce the area covered by the newspaper. If he is smelling an area and you think he is going to do his business, quickly carry him to the newspaper and praise him after that. It will take some time though, so patience is the key.
