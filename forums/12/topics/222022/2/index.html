    no spell la.

    ppl are nicer there.

    want to buy clothes wholesale, go to Boh Beh. it is after the Stadium, can only access via tuk tuk or taxi. but it opens in the wee hours.

    other than that u can head to pratunam market, its not a shopping centre, its a MARKET. they sell alot of clothing, its located around the Bayoke Sky hotel. if you're taking the MRT, get off at Chit Lom, walk down to the juntion where there 4 faced Buddha is, turn right and walk down all the way past the bridge.

    the bad thing about markets is that they sell wholesale, so its cheaper if you get at least 3 of the SAME products. t-shirt can be bought for around 50baht each. 99baht each is the usual marked up price.

    other than that, food is cheap there.
    shopping wise, they can go to MBK or Siam Paragon/Square.

    MBK is a giant shopping centre.
    Siam Paragon is like the Paragon we have in Singapore, all the upscale stuff.
    Siam Square is not a shopping centre, rather more like an area, like Bugis. Students like to hang out and get cheap stuff there.

    any other info needed?
