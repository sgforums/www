One fine day, something casually knock against the wall you conjured and all sentry quickly goes back into action and you build a second layer wall to protect further.

    In the end, you discover that you have less and less space within your emotional area and you experience a choking effect.

    ***

    TO LOVE is natural.

    CloUdiSm Remix III state and acknowledge: Love brings up everything unlike itself. In simple, it means that love brings up everything - all the good, bad and ugly into surface. You can't love love's positive aspect because your deemed the seemingly negative traits as unloving. The truth is: its a whole package. You cannot reject some portion of the package because you didn't like it. If you force your way, it become more expensive instead.

    When we buy value meals in Burger King, we cannot remove the fries component just because we didn't like it. If we buy the burgers and drinks separately, after careful calculation, in fact, you are paying much more. The smart ones will change the fries to onion rings instead or cheeze fries when buying at KFC... but what if you are in Long John Silver?
