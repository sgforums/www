    Ever since after graduation and jobless for few months, i found my first job in Raffles Place.

    I find that i am very stressed working in this corporate environment. Go home late everyday with unfinished work which is unpossible to clear. It is so harsh, meeting the expectation of my bosses and supervisors.

    The hot weather and heavy crowd on lunch time drained my energy,and I always have this chronic headache problem at work. The headache is still with me now at home, Panadol seem useless.

    It is different from primary, secondary school and JC,uni where i had gone through in the past, where laughter is everywhere.

    Sometimes, I aslo have to bring work home to do. No social life.

    I can't imagine myself working in there until I am old or retrenched. I can't find any happiness in life, in Singapore. My friends aslo tell me they had the same problems and we talked about it. There is why Singapore has the among the lowest happiness index.

    I wonder if others have the same problem. Share your problem and opinion.
