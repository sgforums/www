    i suck at making life decisions. 

    every time i am in the midst of making major decisions, lots of things will crop up to confuse me.  ok, i admit i could be confused myself about direction and stuff, but who isn't at some points of their life.  i could be anxious to the extent of pacing up and down, being fidgety, unable to concentrate on the work on hand, delaying making the decision, avoid talking to people who are the stakeholders because i am griped with guilt, obligation and fear.  even to the extent of freaking out and running away when pushed to make the decision, hence leaving the other stakeholders flustered and angry that i did a u-turn on them.

    i tried to talk to people before making a decision, but that didn't help.  it only confused me.  i also tried keeping absolutely quiet and relying on myself solely to make the decision, however i usually get eaten up by the anxiety when left alone. 

    what can i do to help myself ?  i have lost many opportunities and time, and damaged relationships due to this.  i mean no harm to anyone, i just wish that i am better at this.
