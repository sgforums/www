    This is such a common classic case I will always hear from time to time. But regardless, here it goes.

    First of all: Geraldine MUSTN'T accept Simon's proposal at all - or at least the time being (But that doesn't mean she has to accept Francis's one) - probably if she did in haste - she will never know if she accepted it on the basis of substitute of company (until maybe later, such thoughts and feeling will be clearer)

    Secondly: She shouldn't accept Francis's proposal, yet - because Francis proposal because he is AFRAID of losing her - not because he feels that the time is right, love is strong, financially stable, etc and wanted to be with her till death do them part. This is unstable grounds - avoid if possible.

    Then what should she do?

    She needs time for reflect and ponder over what they really meant in her heart WITHOUT the interferance of their actions, words, etc. Which means these guys must respect her decision to remain alone for a period of time to think. Basically: this also allows the guys to think and cool down, at least they would be more rational in a way. She should go out with close girl friend, to shopping, etc and could probably seek their views too.

    After the period of rest - suggested time minimum 2 weeks - She should speak to the both of them, individually on one Vs one basis. About what she should speak leh... post a new topic or pte msg me. Then I continue my post from there bah.

    Love isn't a competition: its patient and kind. She wants to seek eternal happiness from a marriage - she has to make the correct decision! And in making the correct decision, she has to make that decision...

    I) Without exisiting problems. (Solve exisiting problems before moving into marriage and I mean solved it, not avoiding, leaving it, etc)
