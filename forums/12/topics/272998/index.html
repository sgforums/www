    Our journey in life is to seek out those true friends.

    Most of the people we encounter do not belong to this category; they are more likely to be transitory in nature, appearing like glorious sunrise and vanished as quickly like sunset.

    Your source of suffering isn't due to the natural shift of cycle in human relationship, but lies in your perception of possession.     You can't coerce someone to be your true friend if this person simply isn't.

    Why force a wrong key into the keyhole? The key will just crack inside as you turn it.

    Your situation is not depression. Just sadness and maybe upset, I guess.

    Maybe for some who wants to know more about depression, read on.

    When a person suffers from depression, he/she will have a big behavioural change. He/she don't have appetitie to eat. Listless and can't sleep at nite or only manage short sleep for weeks. To him/her there is no meaning in life. Everything is like the end of the world. In worst cases, some even have the thoughts of committing suicide. This can be cause by a failed relationship (breakups), financial problems, demised of a loved one, etc.

    In depression, you need help. You can't solve it yourself alone. Call someone to talk to. You need to pour out your tots and tell someone about your problems. A close friend will be very helpful. Parents may not be ideal and they may probably aggrevate the situation. There are some helplines like SOS to call.

    Perhaps you may want to see a doctor too. They can prescribe some medication to help ease your mind, like sleeping pills and relaxant. But medication can't perform magic.
