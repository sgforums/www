    Originally posted by [H]opes:
    in a relationship..without sex..everything cant work out?with true love..guys can forgo sex?
    Depends on the context, I'd say, though it might be unfair to label a relationship that couldn't work out without sex as without "true love".

    But first, why would a couple not have or even want sex in their relationship? If done with the proper frame of mind and if both parties knew perfectly well what they were in for, sexual intercourse could be a fun-filled and enjoyable activity. Is there a particular reason why one or both parties did not wish to have sex?

    If it is for moral reasons like the girl did not wish to be exploited and wanted to save herself till after marriage, then the relationship could work out without sex. Lots of relationships have worked out like that.

    If it is for physiological or psychological reasons like the girl was frigid or the guy was impotent, then things may get more complicated. Provided there is no exploitation involved, physical intimacy does help to strengthen the relationship. A sexless relationship would probably demand a great deal of restraint and understanding from the normal party, and would inadvertently place not a small amount of pressure and strain on the other party as well. That is perhaps the reason why many couples with such problems are often recommended to seek professional help.

    Not to say it is impossible, but the sexless relationship would probably have to be so strong and mature that it has transcended the need for sexual intimacy. Such relationships though, would be found more often among silver-haired aged couples, who probably wouldn't be asking such questions in the first place.
