    Salsa is one part of the Latino dances.

    Salsa is popular more because of its easy steps and fast daring routines. Easier to dance to any beat at most pubs, discos & anywhere with fast music.

    Think Shakira, Ricky Martin & some canto techno. Even Mambo No.5 once you got the beat.

    You know the Macarena? Similiar origins but quite more of a different moves. Macarena is co-ordinated while Salsa is more fun and unexpected dancing with a partner.

    Try the Salsa dancing with some reputable dance studios like Jitterbugs, Attitude Dance Studio and some more. Avoid the Cummnity Centres if you can.

    BTW, students or NS boys has discount rates for classes.
