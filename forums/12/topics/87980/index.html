    Spend some time everyday at least 5-10mins having a small talk with your grandma. At least tell her what you had for your meals and ask her about her meals. Old folks don't need attention, they prefers company. Accompany her to watch TV or have meals with her.

    It is better for you to show your appreciation for her this way while she is still alive rather than to lament and regret over it when she is not around.

    Anyway, I hate people who tells me about how sad or regretful they felt for not spending enough time with this and that before he/she die.     Do something about it and show your appreciation for your grandma while she is still around, don't wait.
