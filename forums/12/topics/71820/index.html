    hey..not bad..here's the breakdown.

    Melody: excellent! i think asian composers of slow nice mellow music are the best in the world. haha...really the melody's good. might wanna look into the arrangement of it to bring it out. otherwise, you've got the right tune.

    Voice: Great potential. Not quite there yet but its def a nice voice to listen to. the singer tends to drag or fumble his words when it comes to the 'L's and the 'R's. but its and asian thing to do so dun worry. a lil practice would help. for eg. your "act cute all the time" sounds real awful..."she's like a little..." sounds like "sheslikealirrle" basically your "little"s all sound like "lirrle" the "girl"s also sound like "gurr"s... the ang moh's have it easier cause their pronounciation is spot on and more distinct. dun worry though. practice would eradicate it.

    Lyrics: absolutely disastrous. if you're singing for a jc concert or some school play then its ok. but if you're thinking of releasing this as a single i suggest you work on the lyrics. some parts are good but "act cute" "She's the jovial but cheeky kind " "sweetiepie" "really sweet sweet smile" they just dun belong in an english song. I hate to say it but english songs should be atleast a A2 type of standard when it comes to songs. the lyrics dun have to include bombastic words but they must clique and at least retain a sense of proper english. Act cute is def not an english term.

    However. there are nice parts of the song esp the last verse?

    "She's like a little angel learning to fly
