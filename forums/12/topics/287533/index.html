    I think you haven't fully recover from the past. Instinctively, we tend to withdraw into our protective shell that serve as defensive mechanism but on the other hand the defensive mechanism serve as a barrier for others to reach us. Your friends probably do not understand this mechanism nor understand the pain that you have gone through. Don't be too hard on yourself. Do not point fault to anyone. We are victims of our circumstances.

    Do you know why people suffer from phobia (irrational fear) ? For example, someone was once bitten by a dog and from there day onwards, he has an irrational fear for dogs. It is the same thing that applies. The pain has been conditioned into our system leading us to falsely believe there all dogs are bad. The truth is not all dogs are bad. Our brain perception of things are NOT entirely correct, i tell you! On the other hand, fear isn't entirely bad, it helps us to be alert in times of danger.

    On a softer note, talking out help. It helps to bring acceptance and not denying the problem. Once we stared at our fear squarely and not fear the fear itself, we start to overcome it. The past is just memories and future is thought that constructed from the these memories. If you examined your thought properly, why are you fearing the pain when the past is not happening now from where the pain arises from and the future hasn't arrived yet !!!

    It is a good thing that you have a patient and compassionate friend to talk to. Human are regenerative creatures. Get well soon.
