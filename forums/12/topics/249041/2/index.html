    While I understand it's nice having your own group of friends, try to learn not to get too bothered about it. Just focus on your studies at the moment.

    This is one phase of life everyone has to go through. As you grow up, you'll come to accept that friends come and go. Only a few friends will continue to stay with you in the end.

    Transferring class may not solve your problem as you might face the same problem in the new class. How many times can you run away from the problem?
