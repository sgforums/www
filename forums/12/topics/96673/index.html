    Ask yourself:

    - What do I want from a relationship?

    - What do I expect of my boyfriend?

    - Why do I need to go into a relationship?

    - Can this relationship be maintained all the way till marriage?

    All these questions will help you to understand what you really want from a relationship. From there, you'll be clearer of the situation and be able to make your own decisions.

    Some comments: Your boyfriend adores you, and has the good qualities of a husband. But are you certain that he is showing his true personality, and not acting in front of you? Do you really love him for who he is? Are you certain that you can put up with this all the way till marriage?
