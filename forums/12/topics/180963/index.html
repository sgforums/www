    when a guy say "i miss you", it depends on what he misses you in (emotionally, physically or psychologically...and worse just say for the sake of saying to please you). know this first hand and foremost.

    missing a person will result in an action. either calling to talk over the phone or just hop over to your place and do something; either a simple coffee/tea session, reading a book (separately or together) or just enjoying each other's company, period.

    dunno what to do etc is a result of poor planning and perhaps a hint of being 'sianz' with each other. you don't get this kind of treatment during the first few weeks of courtship, do you? if you do, time to rethink if both of you are suitable with one another or work it out.

    to tell him you are disappointed, it can be achieved by plain tactful saying it out. never beat around the bush. just be straight about your unhappiness but be sincere and truthful. never have the intention to insult, injure the feelings or worse, get into a quarrel over this matter. resolve it peacefully.
