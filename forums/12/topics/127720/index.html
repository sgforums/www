    Dear InsidiousGG,

    The 1st thing that comes to mind is that there should be trust, & that this is a trust issue. However, human feelings come into play...

    Your friend is understandably insecure, who wouldn't be when there used to be deep (?) feelings between his girl & her ex. Whilst he may trust her, he may also worry that her ex may try to woo her back. This is supported by him giving her gifts, taking her out to movies, etc. Your friend's anxiety may be even worsened because she hasn't offered to bring him along.

    So, the suggestion is this, ask him to tell his girl about his feelings. If she loves him enough, she'll know what to do. He probably shouldn't insist that she stop being friends with her ex, but a reasonable request would be that she brings him along, so he can assess his competition.
