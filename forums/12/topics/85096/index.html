    Pri. Girl's Sch. i know of are RGS (in Balmoral), MGS (in Clementi i think), PLMGS (Serangoon), CHIJ (incl. T.Payoh, Kellock, Katong), St Anthony (in Bedok), St Margaret, St Joseph. Those sch. with affiliations are harder to get in cos they give priority to siblings & old girls. U have to do volunteer work to get in. If u aim for RGS, make sure your kid is very smart cos they expect the parents to provide tuition & monitor the kids' progress. They will stream the kids every yr. The clever ones get to go to better class. Also chances of getting into Triple Sc. harder cos everyone is so smart. Putting yr daughter in all girls sch. is still the best i feel cos nowadays the teens start relationship early. With less distraction, they can study better. My daughter doesn't interact with boys fr boys sch. nearby during her sec. sch. days. In JC, she has no problem getting along with the guys cos she is taught to be vocal, sociable & confident.
