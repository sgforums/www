    Most of the time when people enter a relationship, they only look at the good side of someone and ignore the bad side. This is what we call the honeymoon period. Once it is over we tends to realise that the person do have lots of bad habits. If their relationship is to last, both side must be able to accept each other bad habits and work together. If not they will blame each other and will try to force their partner to change. Once they realise that they could not change their partner, they will get angry and want to end the relationship asap.

    Is up to both side to make the relationship work but if either one side tries the easy way out by trying to change the action or habits of the other. Most of the time the relationship will end as there is no short cut in relationship building.
    It takes time and tender loving care to change someone, if you rush into it, it will only force the other partner to hate the change.
