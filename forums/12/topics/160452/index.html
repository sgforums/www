    Bear this in mind. A newborn should never be exposed to any adults/children for at least 30 days after birth as his immune system is still weak and needs to be developed.

    If this is the peak cold/flu season, avoid the common hospital if you can simply because there're going to be plenty of germs, bacteria, viruses floating in the hospital and bringing your baby there exposes him/her to them.

    If there's a hospital/specialist clinic for babies or pregnant mums, go for that.

    The principle is the less (sick) people you bring your newborn to, the safer it is for him/her.

    Hope it helps.
