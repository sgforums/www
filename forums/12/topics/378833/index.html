4 Powerful Ways to Stop the Pain of Emotional & Mental Abuse

  • Positive Parenting
    14 Oct 09, 22:53

    "The Deepest Scar are those we cannot See"

    • Has anyone told you how worthless you are so many times that you have stopped believing you matter?
    • Have you been brainwashed to think that a severed relationship must be your fault...or the kids failures in school or life is your fault?
    • Have you become drained emotionally & mentally by having your womanhood and motherhood attacked by name calling of being a lousy hopeless mother who amounts to nothing?
    • Or have you been criticized and made worthless at work by people of authority or your colleagues or school?


    One of the definition of Mental and Emotional Abuse is use of degrading words. insulting, ridiculing, name calling, imitating and infantilizing; behaviour which diminishes the identity, dignity and self-worth of the person.

    "A common misconception is that emotional abuse has to take the form of a partner yelling over every little thing, belittling or constantly criticizing a partner.

    Other forms of emotional abuse, can however, be just as damaging, and far less overt.

    They can include being disrespectful, discourteous, rude, condescending, patronizing, critical, judgemental, "joking" insults, and "revising" history."
