    Throughout our lives, it is normal to have affection for the opposite gender (other than your other half).

    The key is: How do you handle it?

    1) If one has a serious relationship problem with the other half, then perhaps it is time to move on.

    2) If one is happily in love with other half but also fancies another person, then he or she should think with the brain, not the heart.

    You can either:

    - give up all fantasies OR

    - be unfaithful to your bf

    If you choose the latter option, then there is something fundamentally wrong with your values, perhaps through no fault of yours. And in this case, you do not deserve your bf at all. Leave him for his own good then.

    If you still love your bf, there's no reason to have a fling. Stay faithful or live to regret it for the rest of your life.
