    People always say, "Love has no reasons", "Love is blind", "Love is making the other party happy", yadda yadda.

    We've all heard the idealists' version of love. And if you would read through AA, you would find many models and many interpretations of love.

    Possessive and jealous on one extreme, generous with giving and not asking for return on the other extreme.

    But what is love to you? Your own views on love, your own model of love.

    And at that, I want to pose an additional question:

    Does loving someone means you need to be in a relationship with that person?
