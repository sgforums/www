    I don't usually reply to threads as 99% of that in existence [in sgForums.com] are really ".........................................................".

    Kudos to you for having taken a very mature and objective viewpoint of the situation. I understand that you find yourself in a rather precarious predicament; not being a direct party, yet holding enough influence to stack certain odds. I am also glad that you have (at least, as evident from your post) extended unconditional support for and towards your sister.

    As far as my humble opinion goes, I think that ultimately it is for you to balance and weigh each guy against the other. Whether one finds superiority over the other (in your view), would be influenced by what values you (as the third party performing this balancing act) hold in higher regard, and equally, what negative traits you detest more - for example:

    Weighing the positive:
    BIL - long term relationship
    New Guy - fresh start

    Weighing the negative:
    BIL - deceptive
    New Guy - seemingly without morals or values (i.e. chasing before wedding)

    Of course you would find that such positive/negative values tend to veto each other; above all, you should be guided by your own raw instinct, whilst taking into the account the feelings of your sister.

    I would also add that long term relationships that have been broken by deception usually remain irrepairable - sometimes a fresh start is the only alternative; equally, something new may prove just as, or even worse than the preceding deception. It is for you to decide which holds greater truth.

    Personally, based on my own values and beliefs, I think your sister should stay with your BIL; she could prevent any future incidences by being more careful - it will be good reason for her to monitor their finances. I must admit that I feel that your sister, BIL and the new guy have more or less resigned to a number of conclusions:

    1) Your sister has more feelings (emotionally) for the new guy
    2) Your BIL recognises this; so does the new guy

    However, because of this new guy's sudden foray and very probably destructive entry, it sounds all too fishy for me to think him as a man of integrity and good standing worthy of your sister. Your sister probably has a little girly crush on him, and that can very possibly be attributed to the fact she is bored with your BIL - the so called "X* year itch" (*X = numerical value, some people say 2, some people say 7).

    Hence, my conclusion. I do not think your BIL is that great (in fact, I think he is no bloody good); rather, I think that the NG is absolute trouble - to the extent that your BIL is much better in comparison.

    Good luck!
