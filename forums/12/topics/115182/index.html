    use moisturizer that is oil free.
    or if u have sensitive skin.. stick to moisturizer that is oil free and alcohol free.
    this is because sensitive skin is very delicate and if you use products with alcohol content, it may irritate the skin and cause blemishes.

    clean your face regularly with oil free facial foam or facial wash. do not squeeze your acne or pimple or it will further irritate the whole area around the acne.. then it will cause more pimples and acne to pop out. then your whole face will end up with many pimples.

    just go to facials regularly like once or twice a month. concentrate on those acne treatment facials. these facials will soothe and calm your skin and then your acne will be better. but aftercare is very important. so invest in suitable facial foam and face masks. don't blot your face unnecessarily as it will cause more oil to be form.

    after washing your face, use alcohol free and oil free toner to tone your skin.. then apply one thin layer of the acne free cream (or pimple cream etc), then apply moisturizer.. then apply another thin layer of acne free cream. this will prevent the acne from spreading to the whole area.

    no matter how bad the acne becomes... u should never squeeze it. u haf to leave it to the experts to do it.
    if you have those acne or pimple without the "head".. it is recommended that you go see a dermatalogist to get a cortisone jab to make the acne disappear. just don't squeeze it!
