    Everyone keeps saying " forget the person". I mean, why should we forget the person we once loved? It is part of our life experience. Do you try to forget your pet which died 5 years ago because it brings sadness when you think that it can't be with you anymore?

    Do you try to forget a dead sibling, parent, grandparent (or anyone special to you)? No. You keep them in your heart.

    The more you try to forget that person, the more you will think of him/her. NO! Don't forget someone who has made a big impact on your life. You should thank him/her (or to those holy people, thank God) for crossing paths with you.

    Ask yourself: you will, and have met millions of people over your lifetime. Why is it that he/she is the only person to make such impact on your life? Admit it. This person is special even if he/she is no longer by your side.

    Use your experience to improve yourself. Make sure you don't repeat that bloody mistake that caused you to lose this person.

    What we can do is admit that this person will NEVER accept you again, no matter which god you pray to. People who ask for advice on how to forget a person are actualy in a denial stage. They refuse to believe that this person is gone forever!

    Ask yourself: do you think you would be able to cope better if you lose this person through his/her death instead of you being dumped?? If the answer is yes, you are still hoping for a reconciliation. As long as you still has this false hope, you will never be able to climb out of this abyss.

    Welcome to the real world.
