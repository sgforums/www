    It is not a sickness at all. It is just a simple character/personality issue. Open up your heart to the people around you, you got nothing to lose. Socialising is a skill that simply cannot be picked up overnight, but it is something that you are missing in your character/personality. I'm not asking you to be a fake person and pretend to mix around with people around you. Instead, if you are able to prove to people that you can charm them with your character/personality for just one time...then you can go ahead and say that they are boring. If you are unable to do that, then I have to tell you that the fault lies within yourself and you are simply a boring person to mix around with.

Take up the challenge to add effective socialising into your character/personality...it is afterall a life skill which will greatly benefit your social life and future career.
