    Well, when things didn't happened the way everyone expected, sometimes it's best to just way away from it to avoid further escalation of unpleasant events or negative emotions.

    You see.. people actually needs a space of their own. Even for a married couple, each of them would need a private space for their own retreat. Worst things happened, ie. saying the things you didn't meant to say.. or hitting each other.. etc, when you can't hold your frustration, too upset or simple not ready for something.

    The best thing to do when someone said they need time out, or cooling period, does not necessary mean a break out or any other things. It simply implies that he/she can't cope with what's going on right now and needs time to absorb.

    Best thing to do when someone retreat into this zone of cooling off, is to let it be.. don't jump to conclusion and stop pressuring him/her. Put yourself in his shoe and perhaps you can see some light. Cooling period can last from hours to a few months. So, do maintain your distance and not walk out of it nor to push forward.

    If it took too long (ie. 2x the cooling period he/she wanted), cooling period will starts to turn into ice age. This might be the time for you to take a small step forward.. testing the ice before you break it.
