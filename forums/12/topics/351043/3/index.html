    The more serious you are about love, the less valuable your love will be from your partner's perspective. Most of the time, being overly serious about love can lead to unnecessary jealousy and possessiveness....sometimes violent relationship.

    When love is in abundance and love is overflowing and easily available and freely displayed, there isn't really any reason left for anyone to earn, work for, pursue for love anymore. Usually that's when the relationship begins to slide down because either parties in the relationship no longer has anymore reason to pursue love.

    People are lazy, when things comes easy to them, they will relax and start feeling satisfied thinking that they had already attained love or even the highest level of love. But of course they were wrong, a relationship without a reason to pursue/earn love from one another is certainly a relationship which is doomed to fail.

    Don't you know that courtship is really the best part of having a relationship because that's when both parties tried to make each other fall in love with themselves...or attract the opposite parties to fall in love with themselves.

    There's probably no greater joy than knowing that you have the ability to make your partner to fall in love with you again and again....either consciously or subconsciously.

    And I believe we all want to be able to attract our partners to fall in love with us and also deeply wishes for our partners to have the same ability to make us fall in love with them over and over again.

    Be confident of yourselves and have fun finding love. Nobody will enjoy having a relationship with an insecure lover.
