    I must say that what udon said is very true.

    If your foundation sucks, then you seriously shouldn't even bother on working on your current syallabus. By all means go and dig out your lower Sec Maths textbooks and revise everything from the very start.

    I once tutored a Sec 3 boy who was EXTREMELY weak in his foundation Maths. He slacked all the way through his Sec 1 and Sec 2, and got demoted to the Normal stream as he had screwed up his Sec 2 streaming exams badly.

    When I first taught him (at the end of his Sec 2 year), he didn't even know how to do this question:

    x + 2 = 0

    He just stared at it blankly as though I had just asked him to buy a barrel of gummiberry juice from Muddy Murphy's.

    I spent the next few months giving him a crash course in Lower Sec maths. And I mean CRASH. He complained that he wanted me to teach him E Maths, but his foundation sucked so much that he really couldn't do any of it when I tried teaching him the Sec 3 syllabus.

    If you already can't solve x + 2 = 0, then how the heck do you solve this:

    2^(x+2) = 8

    Even so, the damage was too much for me to reverse. In the end, his mother fired me. No reason was given, but I suspect that he screwed up his final paper as well. I have no regrets - I did my job to the best of my abilities.

    You don't want to end up like this kid? Get back to basics.
