    Walking alone in a pit of dispair,
    I find myself scared from no one there.
    Every where I turn is the wrong direction,
    Self mutilation to face why waste perfection.

    I'm begging you down on my knees,

    For the hell I deserve is a devastating fate.

    Sleepless

    The heather has wither,
    but my heart still beats.
    Your shadow burns in my mind,
    you would never just leave me alone, would you?

    I drank my chardonnay,
    your reflection was dismiss,
    but your voice came back to me,
    How careless was I, to let you sneak through.

    I listen to the radio,
    your singing soon fade,
    Now I am finally alone again,
    I wonder why I would miss you.

    Sleepless unwillingly,
    A thought that revolutes inside me.
    A memory I cannot forget,
    The night is going to be long...

    Souless

    I still stand tall,
    though my heart is no more.
    I have never wondered,
    what is life without you,
    for my mind forbide so.

    Lingering in darkness,
    I search for my soul,
    I found it with you,
    imprisoned by your eyes,
    a prison I could not break free.

    You first stole my heart,
    and now you have kept my soul.
    How can I move on,
    when my mind is without both?

    Cyber Imagination

    Deep in the tunnels of my mind,
    images abound,
    Silky Hair and Sparkling eyes,
    tempt me like the devil.
    Was that pair of eyes yours?
    I sure hope it is,
    for none charmed me like it did.

    Words flow like streams of clouds,
    into my mind they went.
    fogging my thoughts no end.
    A vision is finally form,
    and how beautiful you look.

    I pondered, if this was you,
    but why the heck I care?
    This is how I want you,
    and how you will to me.

    Till we meet shall it perish,
    or maybe it will stay.
    "Nonetheless" I told myself,
    you were beautiful this way.

    You came into my life, loving me at first sight.
    But two years now, you had to make me sad,
    Sleeping with someone whom you have barely met.

    to make time go backward each time you frayed.
    You tell me now that you still love me,
    To forget about the things u did will not be easy.

    Fighting against my will to see your face,
    to be held by you and smile within your gaze.
    Sad but true, these will all be my illusions,
    just another day, pondering on your visions.

    Yes! You are there sometimes and for that I am grateful to you,
    But I need someone there full time and that you can not do.
    The time has come for me to let go and never to expect you to care again,
    People may come and people may go but my hatred for you will never end.

    Alone, her life, anew to start and always searching for a way.
