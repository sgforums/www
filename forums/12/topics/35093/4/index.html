    shall i compare u to a summer day

    Shall I compare thee to a Summer's day?
    Thou art more lonely and more temperate:
    Rough winds do shake the darlig buds of May,
    And Summer's lease hath all too short a date:
    Sometimes too hot the eye of heaven shines
    and often in his gold complexion dimmmed;
    and every fair from fair somtimes declines
    by chance a nature's changing course untimmed:
    But thy eternal Summer shall not fade
    Nor lose possession of that fair thou ow'st,
    When in eternal lines to time thou grow'st:
    So long as men can breathe, or eyes can see,
    So long long lives this, and this gives life to thee.[/u]
