    to lose weight depends on this simple equation:

    calories ingested < calories expended

    so.. eat less.. endure the hunger pangs at night.. or whenever.. have a fixed eating schedule..

    eat less = 70% of what you used to eat..

    soon you will not feel hungry anymore because your stomach actually shrinks... not shitting you...

    then exercise... increase your metabolic rate..

    there is no specific food you have to eat.. all those stories and tales of "banana diet" and "atkins diet" is bullshit.. they may work in the short term by tricking your body into something and then shedding the weight.. however, you will mess up your body.. usually permanently...

    eat a huge variety of food... make sure your meals are balanced...

    and a huge variety is NOT a huge amount of food.. they are different things... vary your diet to ensure you have enough nutrients from EVERYTHING...

    that is the only sensible and long term diet plan you can work with.. everything else is bullshit..

    good luck!
