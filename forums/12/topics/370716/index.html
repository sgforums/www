    If you want the results, you must work hard for it. There is no other way out.

    I can assure you quite a number of students who did very well studied until as late as 1am in the morning, and still have to wake up at 5am the next day to head to school.

    The most important thing is time management - how you manage your time.

    If you are playing computer/video games for more than an hour a day or watching television for the same amount of time, then I strongly suggest you cut down on all these.

    Many youngsters think it is "correct" to spend this amount of time on gaming and enjoyment, just because everyone else is also doing so, but it is actually supposed to be nothing more than a form of relaxation and enjoyment in your spare time.
