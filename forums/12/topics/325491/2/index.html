    Emotional issue is something that alot of people struggle with. Some take weeks... months and even years to move on. Some times its about controlling your own emotions.

    A female client of mine who is a successful, confident and attractive banker in her early 30s was caught in an emotional time warp for almost 8 years. She had broken up with this guy but wasn't able to move on even after he got married and had a kid. She simply could not get into another relationship and just buried herself into her work. Till she completed her therapies with me, she was constantly rewinding the past and her coffee/smoke sessions with her gfs are always about "what ifs" and "whys".
