    HI

    If you study in Australia, MBA (like Monash) when you complete the core units, you can still go to specialization, any field which you like during the course. Some people take MBA and after core units, they change their career. However, if you change your career, it means you have to start from beginning.

    Furthermore, after completing MBA, some people return to work, they find it hard to meet their expectations, or even hard to find a job because they get older and employer may want the younger. MBA will be more expensive to pay. Singapore is hard to find a job. So ask yourself how you are happy with your job now? Is it necessary to take further education? For knowledge, or promotion?

    You should ask yourself what you want for your career, what you like to do first, and who you want to be in future ... A master degree is a huge investment. Think carefully before you go for it. You should go to career counsellor to ask for advice. It seems to me you need to have a "need analysis' from them.

    Best wishes.

    J
