    On the otherhand, there are of course some teachers who can't be bothered to answer your questions, hence they keep trying to interrupt you so as to shut your mouth up.

    There is nothing much you can do on your part except to listen to them, regardless of which category your teacher comes from. You can try telling your teacher next time to listen to what you have to say first before they talk, but note that you will have to put it in a polite manner.     If it is the former, then good for you, he/she will listen to you, but if it is the latter, oh well, there is nothing much you can do.
