    http://www.abrsm.org/?page=exams/regs/international/2007/examGrades.html

    Exam Regulations 2007/2008

    1 Grades of Examination

    a There are eight grades of examination, numbered progressively in order of difficulty from 1 to 8.

    b Candidates may be entered in any grade irrespective of age, and without previously having taken any other grade in the same subject.

    c A candidate may enter for only one grade of Theory at a time.

    d Candidates for Practical Grades 6, 7 and 8 must already have passed one of the following qualifications:

    ABRSM: Grade 5 (or above) in Theory, Practical Musicianship or solo Jazz subject

    Trinity Guildhall, LCM, AMEB, UNISA: Grade 5 (or above) in Theory

    This must have been passed before the last date of entry for the Practical examination.
