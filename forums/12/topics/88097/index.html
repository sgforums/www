    Jun and Jen was a young loving couple. Jun was very charming and many girls couldn't help falling in love with him. Therefore, he became quite a flirt.

    As for Jen, Although there were a number of guys chasing after her, Jen had only Jun in her heart. She knew that Jun would leave her for another girl one day. But she continued to love him wholeheartedly.

    Jen loves rainy days. She loves playing in the rain,and whenever Jun wanted to join her, Jen would stop him.Jun then asked Jen: Why won't u allow me to join you in playing in the rain?Jen replied: Cos i don't want you to fall sick.Jun then asked: If playing in the rain would make me fall sick,then why are u still doing it?

    But each time Jen would just keep quiet and smiled at him.Although Jun was not allowed to join her, Jun felt happy. To him, as long as Jen is happy, he would be happy as well.

    However, nothing lasts forever, Jun soon fell for another girl, he even loved the girl more deeply then he had loved Jen. One day, while Jun and Jen were having their dinner, Jun told Jen that this would be their last dinner together. Jen looked at Jun, she knew that this was going to happen but she chosed not to ask any further. Jun then ask for a break-up, and Jen accepted willingly. That night, was supposed to be the last time Jun sends Jen home, but Jen kept very quiet, although deep down, she wanted to know the answer, but she chose not to ask. Just when Jen was about to step into the lift, Jun stopped her, he said, Jen, i'm sorry that i've let u down. But i'll never forget those days when i see u playing in the rain, those happy and unforgettable memories i had with you.After listening to Jun, Jen couldn't help it but cried. Jun hugged her tightly. He said, Jen, there's one question i wanted to ask you long ago. why was it so that when u're playing in the rain, u just wouldn't let me accompany you?Jen was stunned, she didn't know what to answer.Then,she said.. cos..(Looking into Jun's eyes) i didn't want to let u noe tat i was crying.
