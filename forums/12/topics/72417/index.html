    So what happen when you see? Your soul is happy, but your emotional spirt screams the pain - of someone so near, yet so far.

    Then what if you don't? Your soul longs and your emotional spirit still screams the pain - of someone far and still so far.

    Sounds like paragraph two has a better choice - at least your soul is happy.

You could say anything you want - do you lose anything? No. It's like playing Who Wants To Be a Millionaire and you are at the one thousand dollar level - even if you answered it wrongly, you don't lose anything.
