    I am a teochew myself and grew up in an extended family with uncles and aunties, grandparents. I remember hearing from friends during my time in Poly that their opinions of teochews are that they are a nasty lot of people.

    Your opinion may differ lah but to me it is very true.
    I am not sure whether this kind of things are common in common household or not but I would like to list out my unpleasant experiences with this lot of people as a kid.
    I was living together with this lot of people for a period of 12yrs. because you know in the 80s we still have kampongs in areas like lim chu kang, punggol.

    My parents and siblings are the only nice people among so many people I grew up with.

    My grandfather: very hot tempered teochew. unfortunately I was a torn in his eyes, he will scream and shout at me for no reason when he is in a bad mood and most of the time he is in a bad mood, he is like one of those cobras in attacking mode, once I do something that irritate him, he will pounce and the most nasty and condemning words will come out from his mouth.
    He likes to use Lo** B**, P* B** (famous and most popular teochew vulgarities)

    Grandmother (a teochew also): likes to bully my mother

    Youngest uncle: very narrow minded person, perhaps he is being
    Over pampered since he is the youngest kid of my grandparents
    I remember as a kid when I said the wrong words (not vulgarities); just saying I am not free when he wanted me to do something. Because of that he bear grudges with me from then on. Scolding me C***B** all that on occasions and never talk to me. So as a kid that was a threat and I actually lived in fright and try to stay as far away from him as possible.
    I also witness my self how he wanted to whack up my brother as a kid when my brother tease him. Luckily my other uncles and father are there and they restrain him.

    4th uncle: very nasty fellow also. Very wayward person. A beng in that era.
    Enjoy making fun and interrupting the young lady tutor my mother hired for us plus other nasty things he is capable of.

    These people are really full of flaws. I also have 6 aunties and some of them are already married by the time I was born.
    These people love to show off among each other and bad mouth about each other, creating rivalry among siblings.
    comparing and mocking at achievments of kids of siblings.
    Uncles fighting for family fortunes after grandfather passed away.

    Not that I am biased towards my father or whatever but frankly speaking, only my father is the nice one among that group of people

    I wonder whether this is common among Chinese household. Whenever I watch Chinese dramas for example (point of no return), people are always scheming against one another, all wanting $$ and power, love to show off their wealth

    ThatÂ’s why in the other thread I was asking how well it will be if we can become a truly gracious society instead.

    I never want to be like any one of them. I may not be perfect but I do hope I will never inherit of those nasty character or personality. I really feel sad for my mother having to put up with those nasty people.

    In fact due to verbal abuse as a kid I grew up with a lot of emotional related problems. E.g. low self esteem, severe anxiety, depression etc

    How about you people? Do you have similar experiences? Are your relatives like mine? I hope your relatives are much better than mine.
