    Another way is to postpone your studies. Find work with a good company that has money to send people on scholarship. Show it through your good work performance and get them to send you over. Not forgeting, as you age, there's more debts coming in.... e.g. marriage, house, car, utilities bill, baby etc and you better pray you don't fall sick overseas either coz the medical fees will bankrupt you if you have a terrible illness whatever on top of having to pay for school fees and accommodation, meals and transport.     Think carefully.....is it worth it??? At the end of the day, is the degree/diploma really that good??? would you turn out as any other local grad in terms of chances for a job?
