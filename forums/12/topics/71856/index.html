    No structured form for you to memorise and reprint them back to her - in love, if there are such solutions, I guess I would be writing books and people all over the world would be at least 50% freed from the love related-problems.

    Because there is no model answer, we usually follow the one that make the most sense to us personally and what I have got to say about your situations, I had already mentioned.

    Here's a good guide for you to go through:

    I) Things you didn't said before (kept it in your heart), it's high time to mention them now. Start from the past and begin from there. All the emotions, feelings, your vulnerable secret, your weakness, your flaws, your tears, you hurts, your everything - you pictured them into words and go through as if you are telling her a story about you, from your point of preception, someting that she never realise.

    II) Then seek to understand this story, again, from her side of the view also. She may then be very emotionally and say things she never would in normal days.

    II) Then, talk about future of your relationship. If there is no past, there will be no present. If present is screwed, future will be screwed. There must be a mutual agreement and saying about what you people see about your relationship. Relationship is like business - they don't fail until you call it quit or when you see zero future, zero market for your business. But there is a small gleam of hope - capture that.

    Keep communication flowing. This is not a trashing, chat nor a threatening session - this is about you and her - your inner self speaks.

    Good luck? You need them.
