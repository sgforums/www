    So what can you do, cultivate interests and join in interest group activities. That is a good way to start. Next learn to listen. Dont offer any answers espeically solutions. communications is more than just the offering of solutions. focus on the human elements in the conversations. on the joy the excitment, the details, the sorrow etc. Dont be too fast to talk either, pick up many topics, current topics of interest, know why you are interested in that topic and offer your views, learn as much as you can... then talk to your friends. surely some of them will share similar interests and you can have interesting conversations. Make it fun and dont judge. after the converstaion, if they had fun with you, they will more likely reply to your sms and want to meet you again bec they remembered in their mind that they had fun with you. from there on, bond with them not just on topics but just banter and banter.. bit by bit familiarity will make you many more good friends.
