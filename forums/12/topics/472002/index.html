    Hi, 

    I am Kelvin, the greysteppenwolf, owner of the site Greysteppenwolf's Singapore Mental Health Blog (http://greysteppenwolf.blogspot.sg/).

    I am here to announce a new mental health forum in Singapore, owned by a volunteer at CAMI (Caregivers Association for the Mentally Ill). 

    Common forum rules apply: no defamatory comments about others, hate speech, or information that intrudes on people's privacy. 

    In addition, please be reminded that a forum is no substitute for professional medical attention. Please do not rely on forum members to diagnose your condition. Only a qualified psychiatrist can do that. Secondly, forum members should not condone suicide, or ask anyone to commit suicide or disparage or bully those forum members who are suicidal. If you are actively suicidal, please call the SOS hotline or get professional help. Thirdly, forum members are not to ask a person to disregard a qualified doctor's advice, or to stop medication on his own. 

    These rules are only a rough draft and are amenable to revisions.

    Here's the forum:

    http://cami.org.sg/forum/forum.php
