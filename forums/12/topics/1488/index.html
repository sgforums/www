  • kentan
    7 Mar 02, 22:42
    your name sounds familiar, in software business.

    anyway, bankruptcy prevents you from being officially in any business until you are "discharged" or paid up whatever liabilities. Your debtor(s) can make you sell anything you own, house, car and get back whatever cash they can. If you find work as an employee somewhere, they will want a big chunk of your salary as instalments towards your debt.

    The only thing they cannot take from you is your HDB flat, which officially belongs to the government.

    Your best bet is to have a discussion with them on how to "restructure" your payments to them. They might agree to avoid getting nothing back. You will have to get a job or get a loan from someone else to pay them. This may stretch over years but you will be able to continue with your business.

    You should speak to a friend who is an accountant.
