    hey... i used to be really fat, but i lost 16 kg in 3 months.... haha

    blame track n field training

    anyway, just go running/swimming more often to raise your metabolism (one misconception is that running BURNS off lots of calories, meaning you can pig out. that's false, a 3km run would burn around 300 calories, equivalent to a bowl of maggee mee, which isn't really worht the effort.) however, aerobic exercise helps you raise your metabolism, meaning that even after working out, you burn more calories while at rest.

    diet- never skip breakfast. try to eat less for dinner, and never snack or have supper after 8pm. the idea being, u eat earlier in the day, your body burns it off while you go around doing your thing. eat too close to the night, all the energy released by the food is unused while you sleep, and gets stored as fat.

    personal reccommendation- pick up a sport. running after a ball is more exciting than just running aimlessly, thus is less mentally demanding. in fact, you might even find it enjoyable if you are sporty.
