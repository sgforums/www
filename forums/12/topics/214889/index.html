    The Guo Da Li is usually done before the wedding date.

    Keep to the basics since both of you different dialects. Set your budget 1st so its also easier for the parents to decide and work on. Anything extra, then its up to them to supplement if they so want those stuffs.

    Wedding Biscuits

    Roast Suckling Pig
    Canned Pig trotters
    Basket of oranges
    Ang Bao Money to the Brides mother.

    'Si Dian Jing' 4 pc jewellery. (Different dialect, different giver so you guys need to work out who to give.)

    Some jewellries, not akin to 'si dian jing'. This is extra, some dialect will give to bride to signify engagements. (This, you also will have to work out how.)

    Wedding Invitations. Since you dont have dinner, then find out how many cards are needed and thus send to them.

    There is some families that like to give toothbrush, Bowls and such. This you might want to budget with the mothers to know.
