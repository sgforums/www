    For swimming:

    They'll conduct a CAT test (categorizing thing), those who pass will never need to go to the pool again.

    Those who fail have to attend swimming lessons, while others get to slack in bunk, or do sai-gang. That's pretty much it. From what I remember, swimming lessons are still scheduled b/w 8 to 5, so you're not missing out on any night admin time etc, just less rest time in the day. No worries. =)

    PS: Passing criteria is to swim 50m in satisfactory breast-stroke. Do not have to be proficient, so long as you don't appear to be drowning (or struggling excessively)!
