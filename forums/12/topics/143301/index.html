    Hiyah Brightday,

    Usually, when my guy & I have dates, it doesn't mean we have to go anywhere special or specific I suppose we're very boring people, but what matters is that we both enjoy what we do. A typical date would include any or a combination of the following:

    1. He plans to cook a yummy dinner. Both of us then come up with a list of ingredients to buy, what accompanying wine or drinks, then we trot to the supermarket.

    2. We plan a movie day, & come up with what movies we want to watch, or to choose something from either of our collection. Then we plan our junk food menu & drinks for the day. We either go to the supermarket to buy the stuff, or call the fastfood chains that deliver (Canadian Pizza is a particular favourite with us)

    3. Petshop exploring. We pack our mini street directory, hop on the bus/MRT & off we go.

    4. Casual day out. We head to the theatre to buy tickets, then use the remaining time for a meal, & head to the library to read/borrow books, or quickly browse around for snacks. If we have more time, we go shopping for sports stuff (thanks to him, I'm starting to like more atheletic wear)

    There're a few more, but I'm too lazy to type them out Basically, you don't even have to go out to enjoy yourselves. It's fun to invite your date to your place & just cook together. Plus, there's plenty of privacy for more TMM
