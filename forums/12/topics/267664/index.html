  • jojobeach
    13 Jul 07, 04:00
    Before you give it an other chance.

    Please list down the reason why it didn't work out the last time.
    And why it should work out this time.

    Get your ex to also list them down.

    After both of you has the list.
    Please sit down together and go through each other's list.

    Discuss how you can improve and make this time round a successful one.

    When one party is talking..
    Do NOT be confrontational.
    DO NOT interrupt.
    DO NOT be defensive.
    DO NOT try to justify your past actions.

    Remember... you need to figure out what went wrong the last time. So you can make this time right.

    After both of you are comfortable with this ... and has confidence about the future together.. Please.. give it another try.
