    If you need to ask what is love, it goes to show that you are not ready for love, nor will be able to recognise it when it happens, and love will pass you by, leaving you with a lost yearning, a chance that seldom comes by again. Beware.

    If you know what is love, and yet can't seem to find love, it is only because your expectations of love may be beyond human possibility, a perfection that no one in this world will ever find.

    If you see a sexy girl and you want to possess her, make love to her and lock her up like a toy, that is most certainly not love, but lust.

    Love just happens naturally. It is an outgrowth of matured friendships that you cultivate. In time, you will feel comfortable with someone, and in that comfort, it becomes mutual, it eventually evolve into mutual exclusivity, to share a life together that only 2 can partake in with a future in mind, commonly called love.

    It will give you strength to face another day in our cruel and tough world, and a consolation at night in each other's arms for troubles and woes faced, and the joys you will find and cherish in giving oneself in truth and faith to another, that only the kind of love can share between 2 on the journey of life, not filled with roses, but a roller coaster of emotions to foster better understanding of each other to reach old age together hand in hand, rich or poor, good or bad health.

    Make friends with sincerity, and you will find and know love.

    Cheers.
