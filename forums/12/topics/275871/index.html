    Some reasons i can think of

    -power
    -greed
    -jealusy
    -love
    -revenge/hatred
    -self interest

    -those who are bullied want to redeem themselves
    -to gain advantage over others
    -cant accomplish tasks thru the "right" channels hence hv to use underhand means/taking convenient shortcut
