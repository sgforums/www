    Based on experience and common sense, she is embrassed by the unpleasant event that had taken place. The evidence that you mentioned "purposely sat far away from me" implies a negative notion of wanting similar thing to happnen again and perpectually and conscienciously stay away from you. From what I can observe "she would snub me and not be very friendly" is tells me that she is being defensive and yet unsure how to handle the awakward situation between both of you.

    Suggestion:

    1) Meet up with her in a quiet place, explain to her what has happened i.e. your friend had been rude to her by shouting sensitive things about her.

    2) Offer your apology by saying "hey I am sorry for that incident that happened, I know it might have cause you to be troubled".

    Optional: 3a) Confess to her that you like her and tell her why you like her and how long have you been having a crush on her. At this point, she will experience a mixed reaction of uncertainty and confusion. Persuade her to give you a chance to be in a relationship. [prepare some hand-made gifts i.e. stars, drawings, stuff toys, flowers (girls go crazy over flowers)], all you need to do is to make her feel special and praise her.

    Optional: 3b) Offer a meal as an apologetic gesture. If you feel that the time to confess is not right, you could offer her a meal for being placing her in an awakward situation.

    Outcome: 4a) If she accepts your confession and is willing to be in a relationship with you, you should cherish her. and post back in sgForum to tell us the outcome. However in the case where she rejected your love because she's unprepared or confused, do not force her. Act as if the conversation have not taken place and continue to be good friends. Try again nex time.

    Personal experience: I have a soulmate whom confessed to me when I was in sec 5. I rejected her love and pursue the girl that I loved. Now, she have become my soulmate and we read each minds other like open booked. Depend on how you define soulmate, my definition of soulmate is someone who knows what you are thinking without you speaking a word and know your thoughts and mind at a deeper level.

    Outcome: 4b) Reaffirmed the friendship. Tell her that in future if you have problems in schoolwork, ask if she is willing to help you. Or if you are the one who is better in schoolwork, offer your help to her during revision. Then wait for the right opportunity and confess again.
