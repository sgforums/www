    Cancel the maid work permit online or you can go to MOM-Work Permit Service Centre at Tanjung Pagar Complex about the matter. You should cancel the work permit so you don't have to pay for the levy. Go to MOM-WPSC (Domestic Maid Dept). Next time if you wish to employ a domestic maid, you don't have to go through an agency. You can do it online yourself and its easy since everything is online now. Once your application is approved by MOM, you can do all the necessary documentation yourself. If you go to Tanjung Pagar where MOM-WPSC is located, you can find there are many insurance companies to handle about the insurance coverage and security bond for your maid. There are clinics there too to handle pertaining to your maid medical checkup. Everything under one roof there. Good luck.
