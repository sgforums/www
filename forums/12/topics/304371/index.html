    Originally posted by Arena:
    i think the condition can be reversed 1 right?
    eat healthy, live healthy, exercise more
    no. but that said I know more people with it than I could shake a stick at and i couldn't say it really bothers any of them. it's usually a pretty easily managable condition (type 2, being the most common) it just requires getting into the habit of checking blood sugar levels regularly and having some insulin (and often something sugarry) around at all times just in case. but you can't 'cure' diabetes. (not yet anyway, I think there's a few clinical tests going on re using gene therapy to treat type 1 though but that's at least 20 or 30 years away from being in general use)

    anyway if it's getting to your friend a lot I think the key is to make sure she/he really knows about it, usually it's not that scary it just requires a few changes to daily life to manage it but if you leave the words 'chronic disease' hanging in the air it'll probably seem a lot nastier than it has to
