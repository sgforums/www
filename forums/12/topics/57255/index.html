    Your saggitarius needs focus on attention. Let me tell you your scenario happens to virtually almost all relationship (90%). All relationship will walk out of honeymood period eventually, regardless of how much attempt is to hold it. Under Love Life Cycle, good relationship generally stay within the growth stage.

    Its NORMAL for people to 'slack' gradually as their relationship advance - because motivation isn't as strong as previously when he has to chase you.

    Try to find life outside your boyfriend. One of the top few rules to maintain a good overall relationship is to have life outside your partner, for who wants a mate to be dull and so dependant on him/her? With decent social circle outside him, he has to constantly prove and motivate himself to work towards this relationship, which is doing good for you love.

    In theory explanation, the Law of Diminishing Effort state that partner will do minimal to upkeep a relationship, while maintaining security. If upkeeping a relationship and still feeling secured in your relationship means sms thrice a day against once in three days, if both still bring about similiar effect - he will do the latter.

    Being in a BGR doesn't signify anything. More effort must be work through to see it blossom. Have a good talk with him and let him realise this point of you. Communicating your thoughts effectively is one of the first step you have to do, at least allow him time to get to know the real you, instead of exploding one fine day without him knowing what's wrong.
