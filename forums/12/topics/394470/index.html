    Haha hi guys...virgin post here. How nostalgic to that of the MINDEF forum, where i see the same kind of 'trouble' being posted every week or after.

    In any case...there are just 2 approaches. One is to be brave to go straight to her to say, 'hi i am (blah blah), nice to meet you. notice that we are same lecture, care to be friends?' or something along that line.

    2nd, the passive approach, is to slowly get into her social circle via her cca, or any mutual friends etc. Either approach has its pros and cons, and i would say for the 1st approach, 90% is based on her 1st impression of you. Good, then lucky for you. If not, you know what to do. And within that 90% is based on many other factors...whether she is receptive and open, whether she is already noticing you, or she has no whatsoever reason to talk to a stranger. All in all its a gamble. So take things in stride.
