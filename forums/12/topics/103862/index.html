    Hmm... I try to be alone if I feel a mood swing coming on. I'll also try to let my close friends know in advance so they can siam (scoot away). hehe! Sometimes, I try to rationalise myself out of it. Sometimes, I indulge in a little self-pity then I realise how ridiculous I sound then laugh over it and it's gone. It's best to acknowledge it, say to yourself, 'OK, I'm feeling sad / depressed / angry / moody / frustrated / whatever because it's just a mood swing and it'll pass.' Then go do something nice like listen to music, watch TV, etc.

    Good thing about mood swings is... they pass. Just be conscious of it and make sure that when they pass, it's not like a hurricane passing by, leaving massive destruction in its wake! hahaha! Good luck taming da monster!
