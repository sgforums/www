    ok....get at least 6 hours on those real busy days....
    drink plenty of water... if needed, u can consider some health supplements...but it's best to consult a pharmacist or doctor regarding ur activites (brain-intensive or labourous) and ur diet (wat ur meals consist of...or why are u not having ur meals regularly.....) and they can identify wat u lack or wat u need in higher amounts and recommend the required type of supplements and dosage....

    the beginning stage (juz starting to juggle eberything) is tough....it's need adaptation and getting used to the routine....

    if in any case u need to make a decision among the 3 things....

    (unless it's a family ecnonomic crisis, then maybe work is more impt....u weigh them urself)
