    Please heed my advice -- DONT.

    I have also been an avid maplestory player in the past. But in the end, you would be left without nothing but loneliness and regret for your entire life.

    Knowing that your studies are already jeopardized by excessive gaming, I think you have already realised the negative impacts gaming has brought to you. As any other addiction, I know it's still very difficult to stop, but you must quit. Too many lives were already destroyed by gaming.

    I strongly encourage you to google "gaming addiction stories" and read some of them. Besides that, I find that those who lack determination often has not powerful enough desires to start with.
