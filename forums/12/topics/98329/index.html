    SYMPTOMS OF DEPRESSION :

    1.) Persistent sadness and low mood
    2.) Tearfulness
    3.) Feelings of hopelessness, pessimism
    4.) Feelings of guilt, worthlessness, helplessness and low self-esteem
    5.) Loss of interest or pleasure
    6.) Poor concentration
    7.) Reduced energy and fatigue
    8.) Suicidal thoughts and/or attempts
    9.) Biological symptoms like sleep disturbances, amenorrhoea, appetite and weight changes, decreased libido
    10.) Physical symptoms such as constipation, irritability, restlessness.
