    1. If a single Singaporean wants to own HDB apartment, they must be 35 years or above at the time of application.

    2. Occupant has no age requirements.

    3. Refer to number 1 if you do not wish to wait until 35 and do not wish to have a co-owner. Otherwise, you can sleep near the longkang.

    4. If a co-owner is not a family member (family nucleus), under the fiance/fiancee scheme you have 3 months to get married at the point after you had taken possession of the HDB apartment. If the co-owner is the same sex, or not eligible for marriage, you cannot apply. If co-owner and owner are not married within the 3 months, then HDB will re-possess the flat and maybe put many hantu inside.
