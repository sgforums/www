    cos,

    1) if the Guy-C really cant get Gal-A out of his mind, I would advice him to just confess to Gal-A and get her to make a decision. that way at least Guy-C knows that he had tried. and it is Gal-A's decision.

    2) If Gal-A decided to stay with her Guy-B, that will show Guy-B that Gal-A is serious with him and really loves him, and Guy-C should give up, cos the lady have made her choice.

    3) If Gal-A decided to give it a try with Guy-C, that will mean that there was a problem in Gal-A and Guy-B's relationship, and somehow the relationship is not strong or precious enough for Gal-A and Guy-B to put in the hard work to work it through.

    but then again, If Gal-A can leave Guy-B for Guy-C, what makes Guy-C think that she will stay with him when the going gets tough?

    Bottom line. if u are in a relationship and are unhappy, work that out first before considering anyone else. if it is really not meant to be, end it. but do not start a new one so soon. if the "guy-c" really loves you, he'll be willing to wait for you to pull yourself together.
