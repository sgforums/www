    U may still keep her photos if u want, and it will be unavoidable that u will think of her once in a while. Afterall, the both of u had good wonderful memories together before.

    Get involve in activities, get busy with work/studies, etc. I know it is hard to being single again. But do remember that u were single once before the relationship and u had got on fine. So u can make it through this period.

    However do take time to search ur own feelings. Since ur ex had left u for another guy, if there is an instance whereby she comes looking for u to patch up, would u take her back?
