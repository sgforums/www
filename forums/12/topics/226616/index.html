Letting Go [Is Never Easy]

  • Tiggerific
    20 Dec 06, 22:59
    I've decided to let you go
    You are,and will be very happy with him, that I know.
    I've sorted my life, which used to be a massive mess
    So now, I'd like to wish the both of you all the very best.

    It was something that I had to do
    But it certainly wasn't easy, that I'll tell you
    For you had grown to become someone to me whom the world meant
    Someone to me, whom God had sent

    As time passed I eventually,and albeit reluctantly, saw the light
    There was no point in carrying on, fighting the good fight
    It wasn't even necessary to do so
    And then it hit me: of you, I had to let go

    And so I'm walking away from it all
    Why keep charging repeatedly at a firm brick wall?
    It's what I had been doing all this while
    I'd failed to see the point then; I missed it by about a mile

    So I'll leave you to him; I wish you both a lifetime of bliss
    You I will but I'll try not to miss
    The best future is often based on a forgotten past
    But you I will remember, till I breathe my last.
