    try to figure out when u get these 'panic attacks' and try to figure out if there is something that triggers it.
    learn techniques to try to calm yourself down like breathing exercises etc.
    see if you can figure out what are the 'symptoms' of these attacks. (i.e do you get a headache before you get panic-ky? etc)
    at any point (while having the 'symptoms', during the 'attack' or after), tell yourself that although it feels like u might die or get a heart attack or whatever, you won't actually die or suffer from a heart attack.

    if it's really affecting your life, go see a doctor. most people dont need meds to deal with this as therapy is usually good enough to help
