    the age to start learning is 18.
    u have to past ur basic theory before u can get ur PDL to learn to drive on the road. the basic theory last forever.

    After that u can either start to learn to drive and take ur advance theory together at the same time. alternatively u can take ur advance first then learn to drive. the advance theory last for 2 yrs, which means u have to pass ur practical within 2 yrs if not u will have to re-take ur advance againz.

    The fees if u were to take in the sch is abt $1600+. That includes 25 lessons, but excluding theory lessons. any more u will have to pay extra. u will be taking extra lessons after that coz u can only book a practical test date after the 25th lesson and the test date will be abt less 2 mths away. but as driving needs constant practice so u just have to pay for the extra lessons lorz.

    If u were to take private, u just have to spend abt $800-$1000 but the test dates will be further away lahz..
