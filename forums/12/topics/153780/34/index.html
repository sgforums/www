    Actually, there're quite a few reasonable places for a budget of S$50/- for 2

    Ichiban (Plaza Sing): Don't go for the conveyor-belt sushi, just order the ala carte meal items. It's quite value for money, & yummy too.

    Shinbashi (Paragon basement): They have lunch sets that are very delicious. The decor is elegant, Not so "budget" looking, but prices are reasonable & the quality excellent.

    Din Tai Feng (Paragon basement): Yummy mainland china food that's quite budget priced, but the taste is great. I especially LURVE their dan4 chao3 fan4.

    Indonesian restaurant at Paragon basement: Very yummy tauhu telor & chicken korma. Just don't take their kambing soup, it's Not served piping hot. At that kinda price, you can order quite a no. of dishes, it'll fill you up.
