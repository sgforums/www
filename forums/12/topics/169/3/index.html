    Part 7 Memories..

    The next 3 months have been my most happiest time in my life. Sky has accompanied me almost everyday. He will be leaving in a month's time. We have lots of memories together. We went shopping, bookstores, dramas, movies, arcade, bowling, cycling, swimming and of course places for dating.that is boatquay. I enjoyed picking love peas with him. Its only the both of us. Although is a hot and sunny day, i think is rather romantic too. He promised me to pick all the love peas he had seen to me. In the end, he really did so. We end up picking 99 love peas and I had put them all in a small and cute bottle for remembrance.

    After that 3 months we spent together, it was like totally no news from him..before that he had mentioned that he need to pack his stuff for this period. However, I really missed him very much. The only thing I can do now is to cherish the times spent with him.

    I think of him everyday, and the things around me really reminds me of him. Its hard to except the truth that he's leaving me soon. But what can I do. If we both like each other, no matter how far is the distance apart, or how many years, we will remain true to each other. That's the only thing I can pull myself up. I just lead back my own life without him...

    Time passed really fast, the day has arrived. Sky called me the night before to meet me one last time before he left. We meet at Suntec Fountain and I had prepared a gift for him. Although is quite old fashioned, but I really take lots of effort to do it especially I had never done it before. I knitted a scraft for him, hope that he will like it.

    Sky was so touched when he saw the scraft. He hugged me and say "thank you". I looked at him and he pointed at my neck. Suddenly, I realised that there was a necklace on my neck. I had no idea since when it had been on my neck, should be the time when he hugged me and put it on for me at the same moment. I felt very happy as the necklace will be like him accompanying me everyday. I say thank you to him and we sat there looking at the fountain, enjoying the soothing music there, not forgetting the song, Nothings' gonna change my love for you'.. Although we remain silent, our hearts are united.

    Before he parts, he asked me not to send him off the next day. He do not want to leave sadly and also knew that I will sure be very sad seeing him leave. He said we would contact online when he reached there. That night, he see me home and we promised each other to count to 3, turned to leave and never looked back again.

    And so.... we part and goes our separate ways.....That night, my pillow got wet too......I cried till I slept... I dream of Sky and me.......going to school together...We had lunch and dinner together..We see snow together.........We cooked for each other............we spent times together...........
