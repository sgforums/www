    shirurinu, for me...it depends on how much I still love her. If the love is no longer there, then maybe it is really time to say goodbyes. But if I cannot live without her, then I will do/sacifice what I can to salvage the relationship. It is always best to salvage a relationship if possible. But there are some cases where you are powerless to do anything, that is the worst possible scenario that could happen.

    Imagine this by yourself, will the other party be happy if you forced him/her to stay with you? Or will she/he be happier? If you are confident that he/she will be happier with you, then you must hang onto that relationship. But if he/she will be happier without you, then it is time to say goodbyes.

    Hope it helps.
