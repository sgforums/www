    2. KTV + card game - Truth or Dare?

    KTV session with ongoing of 'Truth or Dare' card game. Participants would be divided into groups. Each participant would be asked to contribute 2-3 'dare acts' earlier. A 'loser' would answer the 'winner' 's question or face the 'dare act' either as stated by the 'winner' by earlier contributions or by drawing of lots. (to be decided later).

    Simple Card game rules :

    Each participant would be given a card. The person who holds the highest card value within the individual groups would be meeting up for '2nd round'. Card value being highest at 'A' and lowest at '2'. Highest from Spades, Hearts, Clubs to Diamonds.

    2nd round. No card value should be revealed. Within the participants for 2nd meet up, anyone can DECLARE to hold the highest card. Upon that, anybody within that group can CHALLENGE his/ her card value. Card value should be exchanged and revealed only within the 2 people. NO 'winner' would be announced. Only upon the CHALLENGE of a 3rd party would the previous 'loser' get to fall out and be excused from the game. (loser who fall out should not reveal the card value too, otherwise people can decide if its worth the challenge.) Game repeat itself until no one wants to challenge. 'Winner' and 'loser' fates would be as described above.

    Note. If within 2nd round, nobody wants to claim highest value. All cards would be opened. Highest card value being 'winner' and lowest card value being 'loser'. If nobody challenges a person who claims to be of the highest card value. The person would automatically be the 'winner' despite his/ her card value and all others shall open up cards. The lowest value holder would be the 'loser'.
