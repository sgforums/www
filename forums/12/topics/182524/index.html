    The so-called "sparks" actually make up chemistry between 2 people. Chemistry in a love context is kinda like its scientific counterpart. Like water = chemical bonds between hydrogen & oxygen, chemistry in relationships is about a guy & a girl coming together & forming a hopefully lasting & unbreakable bond.

    If something extreme happens (like in science, extreme heat is applied, etc), then the bonds may break, & hydrogen & oxygen hafta be by their lonesomes again until hydrogen finds another oxygen to bond with.

    Chemistry would refer to the compatibility (water can't be formed with hydrogen & sulphur) between the 2 parties, & the circumstances (like extreme heat applied) under which the relationship is formed.
