    You have to realise we as human deal with rejections day in and day out!!!

    when you are out in the streets, someone hands you a flyer, which you didn't take ( you are rejecting that person)

    when a sales person approach you to buy a product. "no thank you" (you are rejecting that person)

    when you flag a empty cab and it doesn't stop. (you are rejected)

    when you apply for any thing that didn't get accepted (you are rejected)

    when you ask a gal out, and she says she's busy, (you are rejected).

    Rejection is part and parcel of our life... but guess what you'll get over it!!! What matters is how you take it. like in the first few instances, if you are the one being rejected, what would you do? probably just shrug it off and try again. right?

    But what about the last example. if the gal rejected you, are you going to die? going to hide yourself in your room and never come out again? Of course!!!

    What i mean is of course you'll feel like "you going to die? going to hide yourself in your room and never come out again" but these feelings will fade. and what would you do? pick yourself up, pluck up the courage and try again! If not you'll never get anything out of life!!!

    Look at it this way. If you have met the love of your life and you know you two can be so happy together.... ; However because of the risk of rejection, you do nothing. and a year later she got married. while you two were chatting after her wedding she revealed that at that time (1 yr ago) she actually feels the same way about you and is waiting for you to do something, and since you didn't do anything, she took it as you were not interested!!!

    How would you feel then? won't you kick yourself and hate your cowardice? wouldn't you give anything to turn back the clock to make the move?

    What would you miss out in life, if you keep running from anything that may cause rejection?!
