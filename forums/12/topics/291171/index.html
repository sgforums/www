    Ok, let me ask u some questions:

    1)Do u have any CCA records that are outstanding? Such as President of Student Council, help to form a new club etc?

    2)Have u ever represented Singapore in any sports/games/tournaments and won medals?

    3)Do u do volunteer work such as helping out at old folks home, children's home at your OWN FREE TIME(not those school ask u go then u go type)

    4)Do u have any talents(play classical music, play violin etc)

    Reason I am asking all these is because although ur GPA is quite ok, it makes u no different from the others. What u need is something that can make u stand out from the rest. Remember, there are thousands fighting for that coveted spot at 1 of the 3 Big Unis, and there are competitions from JCs, Polys as well as those from overseas. You must be able to prove that u are different from the rest.

    Ask urself this: Are u willing to risk the possiblity that u may not make the cut for any of the 3 unis in Singapore, and then possibly spend 2008 working rathering than studying as u wait for the new school term to start(I am guessing there's only 1 intake a year?) in Aussie at 2009. That's the thing u should ask urself.
