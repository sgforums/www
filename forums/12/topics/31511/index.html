  • Yunhaier
    27 Dec 02, 19:25
    Life is like a piece of drawing paper. Everyone has all the equipment essential to create any kind of art. The lifespan of your life is the time taken to draw your art. Many people draw using dull colours - which represent their routine/bored life. Sometimes we drew wrongly - mistakes we made in our life. Some people correct it, others don't even bother to alter it because they feel its only a small mistake. When all the small mistakes adds up, its a hell lots of mistake. We see the badly drawn art and we often give up trying to correct it. Some people learn and change their art - there is always a invisible marking left behind by the pencils, etc. People sees it and learn never to do it again, although in reality many people repeat those mistakes because it is pretty naked to the eye and everyone overlooks it.

    Some people have branded, good drawing and painting equipments - represent wealthy family. But having good material doesn't make a good artist and often people think otherwise and end up with a shit art. Since they have the premium Equipment (money) - by making good use of it - they will have very beautiful art.

    Some people doesn't have proper equipment at all - represent bad family, ill-fated countries, etc. But anything can be used in the creation of arts to make it beautiful. Anything from Cans, breads to bras and bones. It is whether you want to make a difference - there are millions kinds of arts and each has its own beauty. If you chose to drown yourself in sorrow, you only have yourself to account for your miserable life.

    After the frame we drew - first 13 years of out life, we puts in colour. People who take the effort to do a good piece make meaning in their life. People who chose to give it up (committing suicide, etc) hands up the piece even there is still time. At rare occasions, the paper caught fire from the science experiment next table - represent people's unpredicted death and burned out your life - the paper. Sometimes we are lucky, they paper is only half burned -(represent handicap) - and if we still continue to make an effort to do a good piece of work, it will still be beautiful, despite the unfair disadvantage they received.

    Everyone has the chance to change, have the chance to correct. The eraser is beside you... have you chose to pick it up and attempt?

    Remember... everyone only has a piece of drawing paper - represent one life - We can't ask for another piece.
