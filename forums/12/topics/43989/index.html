    Well , in this story , we have this silly girl ... She is just a simple girl , having an average life. Basically , studying , going for religious activities , being with friends and seeking true love like many other typical singaporean girls. After four failed relationships , she had lose confidence in guys as they had gave her nothing but hurt , leaving deep wounds across her fragile heart.
    A boyfriend to her is someone who is going to share her happiness and sadness , someone whom she love with all her heart , someone who is willing to lend her a listening ear. He has to be the guy that gives her a whole new sense of direction in life which makes her feels like the most wonderful person in the world to have him. And that no matter how sad or sorrow she is , whenever she looks into his eyes and sees his wonderful smile will make her worries disappear. Not forgetting , someone she can trusts , be affectionate and committed to.

    Two years ago , she met this guy on a special occasion. It was love at first sight for the girl , she was mesmerized by the way he smiles , the way he speaks and his innocence which totally melts her heart. Anyway she manages to get his contact no. , it was the first time that she dares to get a guy's number face to face , especially he was someone she loves. They hit it off very well and as time goes by , she realised she had fallen head over heels with this guy. A few days later , they were together as an official couple as the guy gave her a chance to love him. This guy was very faithful , trustworthy , someone whom she can rely on which is nothing like the past few relationships of hers. Her life was full of love , care and concern. She vowed never to leave this guy for no matter what happens in the future.

    At the start of the relationship , the couple was really happy , enjoying each other's company and love. However , the girl was very possessive , she was afraid of losing him , she just simply gets jealous so easily that she creates quite a few problems for him. But the gal knew her mistake , therefore , she tries her very best to control her jealousy because she don't wish to create anymore troubles and add on to his burden. She did hurt him as a result of her ignorance and immature ways. When they went out together with their friends , she often left him alone with his own batch of friends as she don't want to be left out of the conversations that her own group of friends are having. She thought that he won't be lonely because he has his own friends to talk to , but she was wrong , he wanted her accompaniment more than anything else. Whenever the girl can't control her jealousy , she will ignored the guy as she does not know how to cope with her feelings. Perhaps this has caused the guy to be tired after some time as he had to coax the girl over and over again , slowly , his love for her started to get weak. She seems to feel it too , hence , decided to ask the guy about this. After his feelings were made known to the girl , she felt so guilty and sorry towards him because she does not have the intention to hurt him repeatedly. There was once she started to have a liking for another guy in her class , it was a minor crush which ended after a few days. Guilt overwhelmed her , she knew that it was terribly wrong to like another guy when she was still with her boyfriend so she told herself never to let any guy enter this relationship from then on. She was to remain committed to the relationship and stays faithful to her boyfriend

    Things began to get better between the both of them but it only lasted for some time. When the guy found a part-time job , she was so happy for him , she even accompanied him to the interview and gave him moral support. Meanwhile , she was concentrating in her 'O' levels examinations , both were busy with their own stuffs but they still make it a point to call each other now and then. After her major exams , they still went out together , enjoying themselves whenever the guy was not working. The girl felt that it was not enough , she felt neglected. Even though she had her friends accompanying her , she still missed the guy a lot , yearning to be with him more then ever. She complained to her guy that he had little time for her , this led to the frequent quarrels and unhappiness. As time passes by , the girl admitted that she was in the wrong after some self-reflections on her part , she apologised. She knew that her guy had to cope with the pressure in his work and pacify her off work , it must have been tiring for him. It was solely her fault , she blamed herself for it.

    About two months later , during January of this year , she started working full-time in suntec city and the time she spent with her guy is even lesser. But she still insisted on finding him every once a week , which was her off day , she did not take into consideration how tired and lethargic she was. The girl was contented even if they could only see each other for a few hours. Her tiredness and stress was being taken away every time her guy smiles at her , showing his concern for her , telling her how much he loved her. He was simply her everything , her pillar of support , her whole world. Without him , she felt as if she was only someone without a soul. After two weeks of working , the girl was told that she had to work continuously for the next two weeks for eleven hours everyday. She was devastated upon hearing this because she knew that it would mean that she had no time for her guy at the mean time. On the other hand , the girl had an stinking instinct that this is putting their relationship at stake. It proved to be correct , they drifted apart and he had fallen for another girl. She felt that her world had collapsed , but she still had some hope that things will get better. At that time , the girl's attitude towards the guy started to change , she felt very very exhausted , hence , started to talk lesser to the guy. The guy talked to her about the changes in her personality , she felt that she was in the wrong so decided to change for the better because of him. She was sure that her job resulted in all this changes so she sacrificed her job in order to save the relationship. On valentine's day , the guy told her that he was just testing her feelings for him and he don't like the other girl at all. She felt so relieved and happy to hear this but anger was also present at the same time. The girl did not tell him about her being angry because she fears that things might get worse.

    However , slowly , things started to change again. While what seems to be a nice guy , turns into a nightmare at times. He had problem controlling his temper. As the girlfriend , the girl tried her best to endure him and be tolerant. The guy seems to have double personality , really really nice at times and very bad-tempered at other. Over a short period of time , he started to change into another person. No more the caring , loving , faithful , loyal , good-tempered guy that he was at the beginning. The girl was extremely upset about this , she questioned him the reason behind this change , but he told her that partly he was influenced by his friends. She gets the feeling that he simply do not want to let her know the truth. After sometimes , she slowly more or less accepted this change in him , she wanted very much for him to be like how he was in the past , hoping that he would change for her , but he accused her for being demanding. The past memories of them being happily together still haunts her now and then. As a result of this , she lost her sleep , her appetite and things began to fall into monotone. He would not listen to her , slowly , the girl felt so dishearten and utterly disappointed. The guy even lied to her on some occasions , she told herself to give him more trust but , again and again , he hurts her deeply , till she lose hope in him. He dealt her another blow by liking two other girls and even went to the extend of telling one of the girls that he likes her. The girl no longer knows if he is still serious about her , she felt miserable , felt that her world has stop revolving. She felt the betrayal even though vows were not made between both of them. This girl only wanted a relationship with commitments , love and concern from the other party. She wanted to be there for the person she loves. All she wanted was the guy to be faithful and loyal to her more then anything else. But sad to say , its not within her control ...

    What can this girl do to save this relationship ? She e-mailed the above story to her guy but he did not seem to want to solve the problems in their relationships. When the girl told him her fears and her insecurity over the phone , he treated it like a joke and started to make some unwanted noises or started to sing. When its time to be serious , he joked around , giving the girl an impression that he did not take what she said seriously. The girl wanted to understand him better but he did not give her the chance to do so.
