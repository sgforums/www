    There's a reason for attraction. Your words, your behaviour and body language says it all. Attraction is not a choice but it's either the other party will be attracted to you or not attracted to you at all. But there's contributing factors and reasons as to why people are attracted to you.

    As for love, it cannot be defined simply because it is the longest-lasting power ever known in this universe. It was around since way beyond the beginning of time.
