    if it is msword, change your auto-save options ...

    MSWORD 2000, Tools > Options > Save > Save Auto Recover Info Every ....

    set this option to be 1 min ....

    if your pc hangs, right after the reboot, open up msword WITHOUT opening any other documents (just msword itself). you should be able to find your document there .....

    but, as with all ms products, reliability has never really been a strong point, so, caution ..... this may fail at times. better than nothing though ...
