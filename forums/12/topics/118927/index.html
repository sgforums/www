    If you are sick (Nauseous, vomit) within 2 hours of taking the first tablet, you should take the second tablet in the pack at once and contact your doctor or clinic immediately for another tablet

    If you are sick within 2 hours of taking the second tablet you should contact your doctor or pharmacist immediately

    1) Note : If you have taken several pills at a time, you may feel sick, vomit or have vaginal bleeding. Ask your doctor or pharmacist for advice if you are at all worried.

    2) Note : If you are sick, or have very severe diarrhoea less than 2 hours after taking this medicine, or if you have a disease of the small bowel (such as Crohn's disease) that interferes with the absorption of your food, the active ingredient of Postinor-2 may not have been completely absorbed. You should contact your doctor or clinic who may need to give you another course of treatment.

    3) Note : Like all medicines Postinor-2 can have side effects:

    Feeling sick (nauseous); about a quarter of women who take Postinor-2 feel sick for a short while afterwards

    Being sick (vomiting); only about 5 in every 100 women who take Postinor-2 will actually be sick.

    Postinor-2 may change your pattern of menstrual bleeding; although most women will have their next period at the normal time, some will have a later, or earlier than normal period. There may also be some irregular vaginal bleeding or spotting until your next period

    If your next period is more than 7 days late, go back to your doctor who will need to check that you are not pregnant

    If your next period is abnormal (too light, much heavier than usual, or especially painful) you should see your doctor without delay

    Some women get tender breasts, headaches, feel dizzy or feel tired after taking Postinor-2. These symptoms will get better within a few days.

    Don't engage in unprotected sex next time. If you are unmarried, better don't engage in sex at all.

    Good luck!
