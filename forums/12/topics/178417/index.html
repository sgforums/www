    subjects that u will be touching on are:

    1) Aerodynamics and Mechanical Controls
    2) Aircraft Propulsion Systems
    3) Human Factors and Error Management
    4) Airworthiness Administration

    This is only applicable to the Aerospace Engineering option in Temasek poly. Not sure about the other polys.

    In addition to that, the teachers will arrange for the students to take the relevant CAAS papers. I just passed one of the subjects and going to apply for 3 more subjects
