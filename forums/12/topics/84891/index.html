    When you attempt to love someone, yet lack the same love for yourself, you will find it difficult for this love to blossom beyond a certain level. Love always work in this way - to give and to receive. Not understanding the love for yourself will subconsciously drain you and have meaningless sacrifice, thinking that it will improve the relationship.

    In the end, you discover it is for spiritual growth if you gain that wisdom - about love being two-channel, not one.

    Allow me to share with you this: lack of self love will likely to attract negative relationship. Abuse of all sort is common. (This includes emotional abuse - taking for granted, inferior making, etc). Relationship may still flow within its complex dynamic, but this love can never soar as high as could have.

    To strengthen self love - you may begin by improving your overall self image, esteem and confidence. These three elements are condition essential for positive self love to take place and as you gain experience in relationship, you will gradually achieve it.
