    a sort of poem that i wrote when i sort of "broke up"w a gal
    I have cried,i have bleed
    till my eyes are swollen thru n my heart can bleed no more
    for one i hold so dear,i can't take it anymore
    she treats me like dirt n dust
    she throws me to the dogs
    my heart is now aching but it will soon ache no more

    I'm no doctor or studying iin the united states
    my dad is broke as hell and i gotta work
    to earn my place.

    thinking back,i never had a regret
    but it makes me so darn sad
    everything i do,i do for you
    but all i get is one tight slap.
    i stood by your side when you are down
    althought i gave crappy advice
    i certainly can't paint that well
    and i ain't a computer whiz

    all i have to say is that i have tried.
    well,tears once again flood my eyes
    i have never cried for anyone before
    salted water trickling down my cheeks
    and as it did,it told me no more,no more
    i deserved much better than this
    my heart ain't no inn
    you don't come n go as you please
    i think i real have had enuff
    you win.....you win.....
