    Asking about the cost of marriage is like asking about the cost of the car - it very much depends on oneself, how elaborate one wants the wedding to be, etc. I know of cases where weddings cost just a few thousand....and other cases where they were over S$100k.

    Mine was 6 years ago and we managed to break even from the angpows collected (fortunately for us, mainly due to some generous relatives). The angpows even managed to pay for our short honeymoon.

    To be complete, you'll also have to take in the costs not directly associated with the wedding - (eg. buy house?).

    The real cost of a marriage, however, is dealing with spouse and in-laws. This one is life-long.... oooooh, you'll find this out sooner or later, when the honeymoon/fairytale period ends.
