    You are at the bench of love.
    Many people are watching with excitement.
    You tried building a sand castle out from nowhere.
    The winds swept across your work,
    The sea waves crashes your work.
    Still you didn't give up building and creating your dream castle.

    To the person you wanted to share this with (Shim),
    Shim didn't appreciate it.
    You held on, because you feel that hope was at the end of the rail.
    Even the winds continued to howl,
    And the sea waves continue to plunder.
    You stand firm and guarded it with all your life.

    As the elements continues its onslaught,
    Your resistance is getting weaker,
    And your is strength wearing off.
    As you turn to the person you wanted to share this creation with,
    Shim never lend a hand.
    People around started to panic,
    Leaving one by one.

    Finally, the the sand castle was in ruin.
    Everyone around had left,
    Except Shim,
    Who still never learn to appreciate it.

    Enough of story

    The question to you people is, what if one day you are in the process of undergoing a great crisis (Relationship, financial, etc) in your life and simply NOBODY is there for you. Friends have left you, for they can't help or simply won't bother. Love ones stood there helplessly or had vanished into thin air.

    You are left alone.
    To cry...
    To despair...
    To feud...

    On your own - like a disability puppy that has lost both its hind legs. You have to move on, crippled by the crisis. This long essay marks the celebration of my 1000th post and I want you people to think about this: BECAUSE you people ain't the only one on the island. There are people around who WILL be there, be it whether it he or she the main actor, sub actress, extra, etc - There will be somebody around.

    To commit sucide is to let the God-like entity scorn at you...
    To let problems overpower you is to let the God-like entity laugh at you...
    To think that you are alone in this world... is to let me remind you that there are many people, in this forum, real life, on the bus, near the school, beside the kopitiam, etc - around to lend a shoulder, to lend a helping hand.
