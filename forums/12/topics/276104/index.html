    Don't panic...

    As mentioned in the replies so far, did any upsetting event take place lately? Have you been getting enough sleep? Are you stressed? Or do you have too much free time on your hands and too little to do?

    Sometimes we don't remember things because er, something like, when they are presented to us, we put it on low priority so we tend to not remember them. For a start, maybe you can try (I know this sounds stupid but it kinda worked for me a bit) reminding yourself to give more importance to certain things so you'll remember them better...
