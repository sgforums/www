    My dad was diagnosed with diabetes for over 20 years. It should be type 2. Being a diabetic, the only way he could stay fit & healthy is to take the medication and keep up with his doctor appointments.

    Here's the problem. He refused to take medication and will drink sweet drinks like there is no tomorrow. Worse, he's a heavy smoker. And he had been hospitalized 3 times. The last time was last year when he a a cut on his leg and the infection was so bad that there was it turn into a big hole. And the doctor said his sugar level is out of control. He almost got his leg amputated.

    No matter how or what me & my family try to do to persuade him to take his medicines, he just wont listen. Instead he raise his voice at us, just like how he had always been doing to his family all these years.

    After being discharge from hospital, he will start his medication but will stop after few months. He look really sick without the medicines. The most recent discharge, doctor even told him he needs to inject himself with insulin twice daily. That insulin thingy was a sign that his condition has worsen since he dont need that last time.

    I read about how diabetes can actually kill you if you fail to control it. Somehow, diabetes wont kill you instantly but will tortures you for years before it finally take your life. My late grandma (my dad's mum) was a diabetic too and she died from diabetes complications.

    My family have done all we could to help my dad but he wouldnt help himself. Since now he stop his medication again, we expect that he will land himself in hospital again soon. And I dont know how much longer he can last. Not to be rude but its a fact. He is coming 65 yrs this year. No medication, drink sugary stuffs daily and smokes heavily just make it even worse.
