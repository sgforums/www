    TS, you should be paying more attention to your self-esteem than the reasons as to why your Bf isn't "protecting" you as much as you think he should.

    What you need to know is this:

    Healthy Self-images do not bruise easily.

    It is a well-known psychological fact that the people who become offended the easiest have the lowest self-esteem.
    We are hurt by things we conceive of as threats to our ego or self-esteem.
    The person who has little or no self-reliance, who feels emotionally dependent on others, makes herself most vulnerable to emotional hurts.

    What you need to do is to build up your self-esteem, get a better and more adequate self-image of yourself so that you will not feel threatened by every chance remark or innocent act.

    A big strong person does not feel threatened by a small danger but a weak little person does. In the same way a healthy strong self-image does not feel itself threatened by any offhand remark.
    When a person has adequate self-esteem, little slights offer NO threat at all and they are simply passed over and ignored.

    Be bigger than such things. Have bigger fish to fry, as the saying goes.

    The person in hot pursuit of meaningful, rewarding goals and a calendar of important things to do has little time to obsess over trival slights and offenses.

    Most dumb, insensitive remarks are just dumb,insentive remarks - they have no hidden meaning and searching for it and being offended by it - is an utter waste of time.

    Don waste your time dwelling on them - you should just forgive and forget and move on.

    Speaking of which,
    The most effective method, and the 1st step you ought to take, to eliminate guilty feelings and reactions is to forgive.

    It is perhaps the most powerful and practical principle ever taught to build happiness, health, prosperity and wonderful relationship with others.

    The inability to forgive lies at the root of guilt, resentment and most other negative emotions. The refusal to forgive just one person can be in itself enough to undermine or even destroy your future happiness.

    You don't have to like the person. You just have to forgive her. Forgiveness is a perfectly selfish act. It has nothing to do with the other person, it has to do with your own peace of mind, your happiness, your own success and your own future.

    Perhaps the dumbest thing in the world is for you to still be angry or resentful toward a person who doesn't care about you at all.

    So for the sake of your own health and happiness, learn to unconditionally forgive every single person in your life who has ever hurt you in any way.

    That is the smart thing to do.

    Remember,

    To be wronged or robbed, is nothing unless you continue to remember it.
