    I feel that people from poor families are treated unfairly in Singapore. Especially for us guys. We have to go through army and serve NS. Since the market is bad, finding a perm good job is hard! Imagine that on top of all the trouble of being jobless, the army bothers you by calling you up for reservist.
    Thats where all the rich kids got themselves covered.

    Being rich their parents can afford them a better environment to nuture them. Most of them go through army halfway as they defer to go study University. The army cannot touch them and their parents pay for their education. After that, they go back to army and do nothing and when they ORD can get a better job because of their degree. For poor people, with only a diploma or less, they have to start at the bottom or even worse. Our pay sucks and even after working many years we might not be able to reach the level of the degree holder. If we study private U, we have to pay ourselves and cannot use our CPF money to do so.

    On top of all this grief, we still have all the crap about local ladies being judgemental of us guys. Give me a break and just admit that if the person is poor and lowly educated, he's not worth the time. Spare me the excuses.
