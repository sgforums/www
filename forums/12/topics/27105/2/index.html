    The important thing to consider now is to try and ensure that some money goes into savings after all the expenditures are considered, ie. monthly food, PUB bills, transport, loans (hopefully non by that time).

    Then with the savings, plan out what is necessary to be purchased. Excess luxuries should only be considered when the initial planned savings have been set aside and there is excess from previous months to be considered for luxuries.

    And even with that excess from previous months, it would be useful to set that aside as savings too. Things like a car can wait. Perhaps I might consider one after I get married and when we plan for kids (but that entire plan has to be shelved for now).

    In this time and age, cash in your own pocket (or in your bank accounts) is at least some form of comfort against the bleak outlooks of tomorrow. I learned a hard lesson.
