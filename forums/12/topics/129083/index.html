    "Living well is the best revenge."
    - George Herbert

    Your friend is a lucky man. Not meant to be sarcastic, but there are real-life examples of men whose wives cheated on them with their (the wife's) male friends, and those men are still living well with their new families.

    Your friend would be very foolish if he destroyed his life over a cause as unworthy as the ex-girlfriend. Invite him out for some drinks or whatever he usually likes doing with you and his buddies. (Alcohol is not very healthy, but it could be therapeutic for matters of the heart -- it does help to either numb the pain or release some of the pain out.) Sometimes male bonding might be useful, but in the end it all depends on him to reach out and accept the helping hand offered by good friends like you.

    Time is the best healer, and men generally recover faster from such setbacks than women. The only worry is that if your friend takes it too hard, he may not trust a woman easily again and could even become a little misogynistic, which does not bode well for his future relationships.
