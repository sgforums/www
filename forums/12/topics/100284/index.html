    girls express themselves through body language...so there are some ways to see if she's interested in you or not:

    1) when you're talking to her, look at her straight in the eye. hold the eye contact for 2 seconds: if she continues to gaze at you after 2 seconds, it means that she's quite interested in you. if she looks away, that's bad news for you. this is one of the easiest ways to test if she's interested in you or not.

    2) try to move in a little closer to her when you're talking to her. the point here is not to zoom into her like you're going to rape her. any girl, no matter how interested she is, will move back if you zoom in on her too suddenly. a small but significant shift in position will do the trick.

    if she's interested in you, she'll stay put. if not, she'll move back.

    3) when you're having a conversation, does she lean towards you? if she is, it's a clear sign that she's interested in you.

    4) does she play with her hair/earrings/rub her wrists when she's talking to you? that sounds like good news.

    5) if she's smiling and nodding alot when talking to you, that's a good sign as well.

    you might want to look out for these things as well, because they're signs of no interest:

    - eye contact never lasts for more than 2 seconds.

    - constantly turning away from you while you're talking to her.

    - always gives excuses to NOT go out with you.

    btw, being interested in you doesn't mean that she is definitely in love with you! remember that your goal here is just to check whether the interest is there or not, not anything else.

    when you know she's interested in talking to you, you can start making your moves
