    The bf not wanting to spend time with gf could well be that he has somehow lost confidence in himself. he might be thinking that he is not worthy of his gf.

    It also could be sign of mild depression. people tend to become more withdrawn, and lose interest in the things they used to enjoy.

    Many guys don't like to appear weak in front of their girlfriend. call it ego but it's a trait of many guys. At the same time, he might be dreading army life so much that he can't stop thinking how bad it seems. What's more, he can't share this with his gf cos he's afraid the gf might think he's weak.

    So, 2 possible reasons for him not wanting to go out 1) too worried about booking in again that it saps his energy and he's unable to enjoy his weekends, 2) he's avoiding you cos he's unable to be his true self and doesn't want to appear weak to his gf.

    It'll take time and positive experience for him to recover his confidence and to get used to army life. So the gf should give him support by lending a listening ear when he eventually gets to talking. Just listening will be good. Don't need to offer solutions to problems or give suggestions for this and that. Suspend your judgement, and just listen when he wants to talk. Reassure him that he is still the person he was and that that all he needs to be, and all that you expect from him.

    Understand that he is going through a difficult time and that avoiding you is not deliberate. You have to be very patient with him for now. He'll bounce back if he receives love and support from the people around him. It'll take time but with your help and the support from friends, he'll recover in time.

    Maybe also can check out this link:
    http://www.fivelovelanguages.com/learn.html
    Find out what 'love language(s)' he responds to so you can focus your energy on them. I would highly recommend reading the book as well. It's useful in all human relationships, not just with our partner.

    my 2 cents.
