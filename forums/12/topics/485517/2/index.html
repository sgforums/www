    hi, i'm new here and would like to share with you some thoughts of mine. 

    3 things you can do :

    1. Try it out

    2. Throw it out

    3. Think about it

    Change your nickname to say, happymax , hooraymax - whatever you like 

    when you have a headache, you think of .............dol 

    That is the power of advertisment ,it has a subliminal effect on our brains. That means, the more you use it , the better you remember it (like a habit )

    I remembered listening to a good audio sometime back about a coach asking his guest speaker who trains on memory and asked him - how long do you take to remember something? His reply was : Until  I choose to forget it

    In short, all of us have excellent memory , maybe some weaker but not bad memory like most of us think. 

    To get out of where you are now is to have a clear picture of where you want to go. Do you know where you are going ?

    If you do not have clarity on your destination, then naturally you would circle where you are right now and start to view things less objectively. You would have magnify your circumstances bigger than the possible solutions. 

    When that happens, you start to ask , why me? How come? Listen to love songs about break-up , etc (all these activities reinforce what you think about). 

    Blessings

    Sincerely, give your blessings to your ex- gf. This is one of the hardest for most of my clients. I know it hurts and all sorts of emotions surface up when a break up happens. It takes time but this has to be done.

    Clients who do not listen came back to me on a relationship rebound because they were still living in the past "shadow" without properly letting it go.

    Dateline

    Like your exams and homework, you have a dateline to submit , regardless of you do it at the eleventh hour or not. Set that date for yourself - by xx/xx/2014. Your thoughts, words and actions must be aligned.

    That means no more thinking, talking and enacting your previous behavior (example :when out with friends and you passed by a place you and her spent time with - and you said : You know, xx and I used to come here , and we do this............do that ........and you started smiling, recalling the nice memories )

    Nothing wrong with that as each relationship gives us that but you gotta retain the memories as that and move on in real life. The media and movies would tell you otherwise , haha.

    Talk to someone

    It's not true that men do not talk about relationships problem like women. That's what they do not understand us deeply, haha. This forum might be a safe and avenue for you to share your thoughts and let out your steam in a healthy way.

    I hope this helps and I think happymax sounds nice :)
