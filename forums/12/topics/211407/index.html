    Originally posted by lktlkygr:
    I oways kena rejected de..i tried 2 SAF positions b4 n failed.. Can anyone give me tips on passing the interview?
    when u send your resume and the recipent received and read it, and call you for interview.. this shows your resume pass and qualify. Most probably the problem lies on the interview part.

    When attending interview,

    make sure you're not late.

    make sure you're courteous at all times even to other passerbys

    make sure u reach there early so you will not feel panick or sweating.

    make sure u bring yr own pen along.

    make sure you bring a copy of your original resume

    make sure you present your true certs in a neat clear file (recommended)

    make sure you wear smart attire.

    make sure you don't wear any heavy make up.

    make sure your mouth don't have any odour.

    make sure you greet and offer firm handshake enthusiatically.

    make sure u present yourself professionally.

    make sure you think before answering any questions.

    make sure you're comfortable with 4 interviewers together interviewing you at one long table, each taking turns to ask you questions one after another

    make sure you answer relevant answers

    make sure you're on eyes contact with the interviewer when answering questions

    make sure your tongue is not tied

    make sure you speak confidently

    make sure you can name your own weak points and personality if they ask you (no one is perfect, some may think it's pathetic if you don't even know your own weak points, you can improve on your on weakness if you know it.)

    in my own opinion>> actually other than your paper qualifications, expected salary or past experiences, your character plays a very important part as well. Shortlisting a particular candidate for interview is in fact is trying to find out more about the candidate's personality and character.
