    Here are 2 anecdotes I want each of everyone of yall to read. Please give your take after reading.

     

    The Adult

     

    You are in your prime. Your career's smooth sailing. Everyone looks at you in such divine light. Things seem to be going your way. All along, you had no premonition of D-day: the day when things crumble and your world falls apart. Your boss always pats you on the back for a job well done. Triumphant, you strut around, thinking you are the greatest, the happiest man on Earth. However, your work performance suddenly plunged. You tried your best, pumped in your best efforts but somehow everything doesn't seem the way it was before. Now everyone casts a totally different look at you. They treat you as though you are nothing, but a piece of trash.

     

    The Kid

     

    A studious kid you are. Exemplary in conduct and eminent in status. You snagged remarkable grades, and are always the envy of your classmates. However, you knew you put in tremedous efforts to get to where you are today. You knew you deserved that recognition. So you walked around, head up high, knowing full well that this is the harvest of hard work. You were almost flawless in your work. However one day, you flunk one of your tests. Everyone, friends and teachers alike, look at you, a disgruntled expression on their faces. Now you are treated like trash. A phobia now lingers... do you still have the courage to put in even more effort to study?
