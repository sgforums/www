    We all have different views and values when it comes to love. Love between a guy and a gal. How we go by these values determine how the relationship goes. We all have our fair share of happiness and sadness, being whole and empty, being on cloud nine and ground zero.

    Despite all these, its in the past and we move on. We learn from all these and grow further. Through all these "trials and tribulations", we hone our values and refine our search for that "love" we deserved. Not the love we wanted, but the love that is meant for us.

    Wanting the love has an element of selfishness in it. By forgoing the selfishness in love, we can give our love wholeheartedly and without contempt to the people who deserved it. Whether they reciprocate in kind or not is beyond us. We give our best and do not expect the same back.

    It may be painful and torturing at best, but do we want to give pain or torture the people we loved? We don't respond in kind to those who do not know how to appreciate and value. On the contrary, we let them know such love exists.

    It is a pity too many do not know or do not want to practise such love for the world is cruel and realistic. The only thing I don't get it is why must we play by the rules of the world and not stick to our own beliefs and values?
