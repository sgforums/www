    Wega007,

    I realize you indicated in your opening post that you weren't keen on attending a community college,

    However, you could use a CC as a "stepping stone" for gaining admission into a four-year university. In other words, get admitted into a CC that will take you on the basis of your A-level grades, study there real hard for a couple of semesters to get a good GPA, then apply for transfer admission into the four-year university of your choice. More likely than not, a certain percentage of the credits earned at the CC can also be transferred with your admission, decreasing the amount of time needed to complete the four-year degree.

    Just a suggestion.
