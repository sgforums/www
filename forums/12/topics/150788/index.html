    If your friend wishes to avoid litigation, she could lodge a complaint with the Gleneagles Hospital public relations staff. Contact numbers are available at the website:

    http://www.gleneagles.com.sg/contactus.jsp

    Should the staff dismiss her complaint as a side-effect of medication overdose and aggravate her condition, she could also lodge a complaint with the Singapore Medical Council:

    http://www.smc.gov.sg/html/ComplaintDisciplinary.html

    All being said, however, you may wish to ask what your friend expects to achieve from pursuing such course of action. Compensation? Letter of apology? Disciplinary action against the doctor?

    To get what she wants, is it really worth the trouble?
