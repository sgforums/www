    hi autumn,

    your "career" problem appears to be two-fold to me. one would be having no idea what you wanna do, and the other would be being depressed over it. these two could fuel each other forming a vicious cycle. let's tackle this one by one shall we?

    firstly, congrats on getting ur business degree. if anything, at least this degree is general enuff to give u a wide range of jobs/industries that u can work in.

    you seem to need to know wat u really like before u'll even attempt to do it. many of us aren't that lucky. it's rather like the chicken and egg, do u do something first then discover you like it, or do u like it first and then go find that thing to do?

    there's no real answer to that question. different pple attack it differently. being in sg, most of us would think on the practical side. for some, no income = dead. so the answer to that above question for most would be to bite it and go look for a job, and hope that from there something will come out of it. joy, satisfaction, passion. i'm sure we all would rather do something we enjoy, but often wat we enjoy doing may not provide us with a decent living. so i suggest you start from there. just go apply for business related jobs, and get a job and "see how". if worse comes to worst, and u really HATE the job at least u will really know wat u dislike. of course hopping thru many jobs just to find out wat u hate is hardly good advice.

    the second part of ur career problem would be ur unhappiness or even depression. i fear it may cloud ur mind making u ask urself too man "ifs" and "buts". you mentioned and i quote "the answer seems to be that I dislike desk-bound admin jobs" to me that tells me you don't even know whether u dislike it or not! i'm not trying to discredit your statements about urself. wat i'm trying to say is that u being depressed about it would cloud ur judgement. u'll make excuses for yourself; a job offer may look good, but upon further thought, u think otherwise.

    maybe i'm coming across with the attitude of "too bad wat choice do you have but to go get a job even if u dun think u'll like it". life is about taking chances, there's no way u can say u'll hate the job until u try it. (within reason of course! i wouldn't say that about collecting garbage, but i won't be surprised if there are pple out there who enjoy that job)

    give urself a chance to learn to like a job. if u go in with an attitude that u have no choice and that u'll hate it but u have to do it, then u will never like it. it's called a self-fulfilling prophecy. if u tell urself something often enuff, u'll start believing it. so keep telling urself u can't do it, and after a while u definitely won't be able to. try not to get urself into that trap.

    as for ur quiet and shy personality, and feeling uncomfortable in large groups, i'd say just get out there more. seriously, humans are extremely adaptable creatures! u can always go with a friend u trust and if all else fails u'll have a back-up to talk to

    about that guy, if u treat him as just a friend and he knows that, then why do u have to decline going out with him? maybe it'll be a little arkward being with him becos u know he likes u, but if he's a decent guy he'll know where to draw the line and just go out as frens. maybe u could (if u haven't already) talk to him about these problems of yours and see wat he has to offer. as long as u make it clear to him that u rather be just friends with him and he's cool with that, then i dun see a problem in seeing him once in a while.

    i dunno the extent of ur depression, so i wouldn't know if u should seek professional help. but for now, be wary of a vicious cycle that could make u sink deeper and deeper into it.

    hope i've helped....
