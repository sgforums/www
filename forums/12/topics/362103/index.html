    Being Single for has made me happy for quite some time. Friends and the freedom of having to do many things without worrying over your special someone.

    But as time pass and your friends start getting hitched up you start to realize from time to time that you want a relationship.

    It has been about one year since the fateful day my 4years realtionship came to an end. No denying despite what she did, i still think of her. Honestly i was never that perfect nor good boyfriend to her. Maybe that was the reason when we broke up i never hated her. We make mistakes from time to time and i realize everything takes two hands to clap. If i were better, we might still be together. She has already moved on with her life and so did i but you know the nostalgic feeling when you visit the places that "we" once visited. Long relationships are hard to forget..

    Remember she told me one thing before we broke up when she apologized.

    " Love touches once and lasts a lifetime"

    Only recently i realize the real meaning.. When you really love someone truely, it will take a lifetime to even forget about this special person. With love there will be hate but one can only hate for so long but love forever...

    Life is really full of mysteries that many of us can never fanthom. Life gives us lessons on the little things that we took for granted. One can only get wiser through experience and time.

    Its these little things that have the largest effect over oneself as time pass by.....
