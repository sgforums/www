  • LazerLordz

    Why do the best suffer?

    Why do beasts and Man differ?Man can elevate himself to be above the riffraff of base concerns and politics.
    But thinking of enobled things like love and loyalty actually makes Man hope for the simple rough and tumble of the
    jungle.

    We want to see far into the future, but we struggle to place faith in things long-term in nature.

    Truly, it takes a giant in both heart and mind to believe that things will look up in the future.

    If a person can make you cry,laugh or place yourself into seemingly silly ideas, he or she is probably your soulmate.Not many people can do both.
    They know what makes you tick.Only they can see you for who you really are,and love you for what you are.

    Love is about the undying will and dedication to another person even through hardship, sorrow and initial betrayal.For the noble ones look beyond these mere concerns.

    Its very easy to love for the attractive stuff..Can you love the ugly side and the irregular frequencies for decades to come?

    I choose pure, simple, undying love.The affection that can survive the most crazy of events.For it will be strengthened
    by the passage through the catalyst of chaos.

    Peace and serenity await.

    What do you choose?
