    People should realise that it is ok to say "NO" to friends.
    I never lend my books/CDs to friends, I tell them the probability of me not getting them back is high, sorry!
    The same for money I cannot afford to lose.
    No point losing valuable money, as well as the friend.
    I will give the money that my friend needs. The amount that I can afford.

    I will also not be a guarantor for a friend's loan/HP.
    A guarantor is like a sucker.
    The borrower defaults, and the guarantor is liable.
    He absconds with the goods/money, you get saddled with a lawyer's writ.
    The lender will always chase the guarantor, because the borrower has already demonstrated that he is unable/unwilling to pay.
    Be a guarantor only if you can pay up the amount easily.
    Like a multi-millionaire boss being guarantor for a $10,000 loan of an employee.

    Have the guts to refuse.
