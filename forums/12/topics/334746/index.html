    If I were you, I will probably change job to another company like a software management or any other management position (if any?). Cos I think 5 years has probably given you sufficient exposure that you needed to move onto a different/better role in another company.

    What you need is to overcome your own defeated spirits, you can probably do that by picking up a book to help you learn more about the latest technologies out there that you will need to do better in your current job.

    OR

    You can probably try applying for some teaching jobs at some educational institutions, be it government or private...I'm sure your 5 years exposure in your industry would've made you qualified to teach.

    OR

    You can think about your talent...what talents do you possessed? Think about your hobbies, interests, desire....whichever activities you have confident in doing would be one of your strengths which you could further develop into a career path of your own.

    Life is full of opportunities, it's up to you if you want to spend the time and the amount of interests you have got in you to succeed.
