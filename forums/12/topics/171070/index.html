    How old are you btw? It's really quite meaningless to brood over small things...

    Let me use myself for an example... Because of 'certain things', my gf reacted in a certain way... Then I thought it really bother her, hence the way she's reacting (not reacting) to me...? Coincidentally, she needed to let me in on some information and I simply MISINTERPRETED IT because of the way she was behaving all these while..

    Hence I feel that in your case, you shouldn't really let the little things bother you too much... And if they really bother you, you should ideally have an 'established' (need not be formal one, but rather an effective one which in the style you deem fit for both of you...) conversation / discussion...
