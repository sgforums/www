    It works both ways my dear friend. fated to meet, freewill on what you can do with them.

    Relationship are born of two grounds - benefic/karmic. Benefic relationship are seen around - like your best friend or someone you feel so good with emotionally (or spiritually in some case) that hardly any conflicts would ever spring up or even if they do, they can't damage the core of your bonds. They existed because of the harmonious attitude we have foster in the past. (Or the present if such bonds ain't available and FreeWill is employ to consciously keep the relationship well and intact)

    Karmic relationship are usually our debt relationship. Because of our own personality flaws and attitude, many a times, life would force we to move to certain areas of our life we detest to go, face issue we subconsciously hated. Karmic relationship are steps for us to reached there. No possessive person will ever learn the destructive nature of possessiveness unless reality cuts and bleed them badly because of it.

    Ever heard 'Every relationship is a step to our final goal?' Each steps is hidden with lesson and there are times when we rejected the lessons and resist learning. History repeats and we rant about our ill-fated love life when in fact, the problem came from us. You may think 'Hey, but it's our partner not us...' CloUdiSm Remix III and studies has shown:

    I) We chose our partner not the other way round (First Law of Love).
    - You chose a jerk, you accept them, you accept what's in store for you.

    II) We attract similiar qualities exuded from our personality.
    - We are negative, we attract negative people.

    III) Complementary qualities keeps the relationship going.
    - The beauty of accepting, compromising AND more importantly - how we can complement ourselves to achieve things we never knew we had the power to. Conflicts is constructive not destructive.

    These three elements is my personal basis standard of a fulfilling, strong relationship.

    Cheers
