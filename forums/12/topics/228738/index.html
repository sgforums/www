    hey all,
    i just came back from a horrible new year with my family... my family is a mess. my dad and mum just can't stop fighting. they fought with each other since I knew them... and they really hate each other to the core... my mum especially.

    so anyway, for once in a very long time... i thought i'll give this family a chance again and spend the new year together as a family instead of with friends at parties... so we did and when I almost thought it was great and having fun... my mum did it again... she flared at my dad over nothing and ran away by herself...

    i think my mum has some personality disorder because of her old age... she's very insecure and suspicious of everyone, and always thinks she's alone and nobody cares for her... so more than often she stomps away in anger over the smallest thing so that we will go all over looking for her... its really painful, but we still care because she's my mum. it is even more difficult because I know she and my dad loves us, (that's probably why we still live in the same roof) and everytime they fight, it just never occurs to either of them that the fight doesn't just hurt them but all of us. but it does hurt, and it hurts even more that they care for us, so I can't just let it all go.

    you know i've always been escaping from all these and pretending to be indifferent, letting other siblings handle it. so i also spend very little time with my family. i try to spend as much time out of home as possible. i think i try to block it all out and pretend not to care so i don't have to handle the pains that come with it... but really, i can't lie to say it still doesn't annoy and hurt me everytime it happens...

    so for the new year, i'm just wondering... is it bad for me to be just blocking all these out and avoiding the family in general? is there anyway i can try to make things work? I've kind of given up, because I've tried for a long time, but it is really tiring and it just sucks the life out of me. I just wish everything in the family will be better this year, but really, i doubt so.

    ok i think i feel better just ranting... i just feel so alone in this, where so many of my friends have such great families... well you know what, i hope, this post, if anything, will help some appreciate their families a lot more.
