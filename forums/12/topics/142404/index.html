    Chronic procrastination is common. The following may be signs you're a chronic procrastinator.

      [list=]often find yourself working on things that you intended to do days before
      find it difficult to get right out of bed in the morning when it's time to wake up

      too often you find yourself late for appointments and meetings

      sometimes you have to pay fines because you pay bills and return items late
      procrastination has cost you money

      shop for birthday and holiday gifts at the last minute

      find it difficult to meet deadlines
      you feel anxious in social situations
      you feel you lack motivation
      you doubt your own ability
      feel tense when working
      give up when things are not going well
      have difficulty completing things you started

      you often believe you should be perfect and everything you do should be easy

      find it difficult to start working on tasks and projects; you find it difficult to start things on time and often you don't start things till the last minute

      you find it difficult to make decisions
      you have an intense fear of failure and a fear of others evaluating your abilities

      often feel you waste too much time doing other things than what you're meant to do
      have too many interests, hobbies, plans and projects
      you hoard things, you find it difficult to throw away things you no longer need
      have a messy desk and surroundings
    [/list]
