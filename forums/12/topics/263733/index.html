  • Detached
    26 Jun 07, 19:25
    And something additional just for your reference.

    3500 calories = 0.45 kg

    And your resting metabolic rate for your height and weight of 173/53 = 1465

    In short, you'd need 1465 calories a day to maintain your weight.

    So if you take in 1965 calories a day for a week, you might actually gain .45kg in a week.

    Remember this calculation excludes the amount of calories you're burning a day, if you're running/basketball/soccer kinda thing, adjust your diet plan accordingly.
