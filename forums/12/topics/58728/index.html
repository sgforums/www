    There are a couple of possibilities:

    The way you express your liking did not seemed to include that phrase 'I just want to tell you and no I don't want to chase you.' kind of strength or simply it isn't emphasised enough. She may feel that you may have the intention/motive to chase her.

    Together with the fact that she is actually the one minding, because once you admitted your feelings, she will be very conscious of your every move, words, etc. She could mis-read about you and deemed it as not-being yourself - which will indirectly cause the awkwardness in her.

    To save such situations, I only have two words: Act normal. When she is awkward, avoid being awkward yourself. Act normal, as if you said nothing or you had no feelings for her apart from good friends. Be generous about your situations and if people wants to alien themselves from you, don't let it affect you. Just continue to be yourself and eventually your action will lead them to be normal gradually.

    You have to induce back the old times, you can't expect it to appear from nowhere.
