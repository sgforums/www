    yes. there are many ways to overcome it. one of which is as heng described - deep breathing. there r other relaxation techniques too.

    anyway i also recommend going to the exam hall earlier - firstly so that u dun rush into the hall all flustered. secondly, to accustom urself to the environment and atmosphere. it helps in recalling wat u haf studied too.

    den u can also using imagination techniques - try to picture vividly in ur mind, u doing the paper well, and all is well and good. at the same time use deep breathing to calm urself down.

    avoid last minute studying - it may raise ur panic state if u find out there r things u haven touched or forgot. by tt time, it wont help much to cram too. rather than jeopardise the whole exam for tt small section u failed to remember or study, forget it! and ignorance is bliss in a sense - if u dunno u haven study, u wont panic.

    in the end the most impt is to prepare for exams early, so u feel prepared.

    good luck!
