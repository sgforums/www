    Dear sians00,

    You belong to the category where this isn't a problem at all, yet there is something about you that must be pinpointed and examined from a neutral third-party preception.

    I) Failure to know what you want - though man are visual animals, you truly lack the fixed nature of trying to get that 'one thing' you desire; instead you dissipate your energy and focus into many desires.

    II) Psychological state - in love, you fear to be hurt/rejection somewhere in your subconscious. Probably due to past experience or simply because certain media/parental/environmental/etc influence conjured this effect. Therefore to handle this insecurity in you, you have many crushes. Because even if you face rejection/hurt, you have other self-created 'pillows' to fall back on.

    I just want to comment on the last crush: The online crush is ridiculously silly - You will go past that phrase in your life soon. Trust me, your virtual impression of her and her real personality in reality will conflict because what you thought she would be is like a mirage. (If pics were exchanged, chances are, you fell for the looks and here's the catch - you are falling for three girls based on looks. Nothing wrong with that actually, but to decide, it's time to explore more than just surface appearance). Get to know them all, if possible, REAL LIFE, as a REAL FRIEND before you let your infautuation decide your choice.
