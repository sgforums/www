    No choice made is "forever"...so don't feel guilty about not taking up the new job just because it doesn't pay well.

    Do what is right for you "right now" instead, especially if you need the finances to help your family out.

    And in time, once finances are a little more stable, you can then think of maybe switching to other job opprtunities that benefit others. Priority is to your family.

    However, if you think that in the long run, taking the new job now would/can only lead to better things in the long run...and you feel that it will take a little bit of a struggle financially, but you can manage it....then go for the new job.
