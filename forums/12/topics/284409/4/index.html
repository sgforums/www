    Jealousy is part of our human existence emotion, a natural emotion all of us hv in our mind, it is an intergrated part of our life, there are two forms of resultant jealousy formation. The good and bad jealousy.

    1. Good jealousy resulted in motivation, competitiveness and also in love relationship. eg. because i am jealous about your richness, i make sure i get richer than you, so i must motivate myself to do well to challenge you. In sport, i am jealous that you run faster than me and get all the support, i make sure I will train and work hard to beat you the next time round. In Love, I am jealous to see you with another woman, why? because i love you.

    2. Bad jealousy resulted in bad thought of revenge, sabotage and even kill, it bring about a person mental illness in seeing other people good, it harboured the thought of destroy him or her, and when translated into action, it can be very detrimental to relationship such as frens, relatives, lovers or even life. As the saying goes, Even look can kill.

    So, instead of jealous about a person, we should try to envy him/her and try to emulate it or even better than him/her. But remember that in every envying, there seat in the corner of an unstable mind, a jealous envying.
