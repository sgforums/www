    When a girl loses interest in a guy and found comfort in another man - it time for a great reflection on yourself AND her personality.

    This girl is having her first relationship with a guy - the beginning stage of exploring what love is all about. And she began the discovery without having the 'wisdom' to understand what she really wants in a guy and what she is looking for. Obviously true for a first-hand relationship, because simply she has nothing else to compare and contrast, take note, etc.

    Lots of quarrel leading to breaking up and patch back again, etc - such cracks of a relationship cannot be mend. (Note: it cannot be mend). Going through it one time, probably it fated, this relationship could probably endure and move on. Anything more than that, chances are, the ultimate conclusion would have been declared since then, regardless of how many conscious delaying effort and tactics one/both are trying to make.

    And you just made one of the top ten mistake man often make in a relationship: but i realli did my best to fullfill whatever requirements she wants - the last thing you would do to keep a relationship running and alive with energy is to feed whatever your partner demands and wants, according to her standard and liking. Love gets dull and a woman would lose interest quickly. Under theories of the art of seduction, your action automatically opens her up to potential seducer and Poof! Comes a third party - Twice somemore.

    I understand three years is a long time and probably both of you have learn many things as a couple. You love her alot, although she not very faithful in her ways. Pushing your likely outcome into your subconscious, unless she tells you straight in the face, you rather avoid the obvious truth. Here is what you should do: face issues with yourself and weigh the pros and cons. Find the answer yourself, rather than to wait for time to provide you with one.

    P.S: Why regret if you knew that this relationship will turn out to be in way? There are many lessons for you to learn and if you don't realise the hidden morals behind it, you are just wasting the three years of being together. The fault of people is that they lead relationship and when it ends, everything goes with it. That's utterly foolish. You may lose the relationship, but don't lose the lesson that comes with it.

    Cheers
