    Dear AA community,
    I have been a Sgforum member since September 2006. I was doing a google search for something (name of the restaurant in Singapore) last year and it happened to lead me to this Sgforum where I came across AA.     It's always been my passion to be able to help and serve others who are in great need. AA is exactly the opportunity I have been looking for, an opportunity for me to reach out to help/advise/support/encourage whenever it's necessary.

    Before Sgforum, I was an active member of another forum which I signed up for exactly the same reason. I managed to contribute my thoughts and advice to people who needed it on that forum and during that time I managed to make friend with a woman who was suffering from depression.
