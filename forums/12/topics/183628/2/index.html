    it is not wrong for you to be indecisive.. after all u are a girl.. and a girl need security, love and loads more~ Not to mention girls are normally more emotional based and do make decisions based on that..

    i suggest looking into this 3 things before making a decision.
    1. Finance.
    It is best not to rely too much on loans or credit cards. ie:housing loans, renovation loans, car loans etc..

    Too much liability will cause a strain to your pockets and start a number of little squabbles, which will probably strain the relationship.

    And of cos.. an expensive wedding on loans, normally ends up in an expensive divorce too.. MONEY is always an issue.. like it or not.. People do fight, argue over money..

    2. Age.
    I wont say u are young.. but spending 40 - 60 yrs is not going to be easy. There are a numbe rof stuff that can be very unpredictable..

    I suggest that u might want to hold it back for a year or 2.. First.. to save some money... second, for u to meet and see more people.. third, to enjoy the little little things singlehood can offer u for a while more.. Until u reach 25 or so? By then u should be ready mentally and financially to settle down..

    There are many people who regretted marrying too young..

    3. Changes.
    Try not to have too many major changes in ur life within a short period of time/ same time..
    ie: Get married, buy a car, buy a flat, change ur job, fighting for a promotion.

    can be very stressful. Let it come gradually~ then things will slowly fall in place~ And u have time to react~
