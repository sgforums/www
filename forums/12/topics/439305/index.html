Cindy sometimes don't want to eat so much, she would give me her bowl of noodles or food to eat. Then sometimes, we joke a lot on the facebook. It was then I began to have feelings for her. There is this other guy, Norman who also seem to like Cindy. One day after finishing watching movie, I decided to go home like the rest of our friends. Then Cindy excuse herself to the washroom. I notice Norman still hanging around. I ask him what is he doing? He told me that he was waiting for his dad to drive over to pick him up. Apparently it sounded like an excuse. Then months later, Norman and Cindy are a couple. Again, I missed out, my heart is broken again.

    Then now there is this girl, Sandy. We got close and there is this other guy, David. After we group of friends hung out at a shopping mall, I notice Sandy and David stayed back, while the rest of us left.
