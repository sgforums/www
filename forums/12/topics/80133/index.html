    It is pretty advisable that once you are married, do not leave your wife/husband alone overseas for a long period of time. LDR hardly last - what more a marriage trying to manage a LDR test route. Not saying that it will fail - rather, it's too great of a risk unless you are so fringing` sure of the foundation of your relationship and that you are not going away anywhere more than a year.

    Relationship doesn't measure value through quantity of time. 3 strong years relationship could still be ruin over a period of 6 months wrong course.

    All I can say is do not jump into conclusion first. You are worried because there seemed to be a change in attitude. The only thing constant is change - 9 months? Minor changes are definite. Consider the following:

    I) Complete your study first. You can't do a thing when you are overseas apart from trying to minimise emotional distance gap. Things will be much worst if you perform badly or worst, failed to get your degree and had to repeat or similiar. This will produce strong conflict and probably deteriorate your relationship further.

    II) Talk to her regarding this issue. Since you have already been married, learn to express your thoughts and feelings regularly and especially when you encounter doubts and issues. She is your wife - not go through it together, learn to work mutually. Don't blame her nor trash things out - let her know what you long for (her company, quality time together, etc)

    P.S: Last thing you ought to do is to jump to conclusion and set blames. You been away for long, there are many things that could happen in her life during your absence. You know nothing, therefore things may not seemed like what it is.

    Cheers
