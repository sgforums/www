    I need advice. My wife has gone hysterical and I am desperate.

    My wife have been harassed and stalked by our neighbor with the help of the CCTV cameras outside their house. They have installed 3 cameras in 2011 after some loan shark harassment. One camera is in front of the lift, a second camera near the stair and a third on top of their door which is diagonally opposite of ours.


    The loan shark harassment did not last long and they still keep the cameras there but we did not really mind despite my wife's uneasiness. We were getting along fine with this neighbor, a couple in their 30's and 2 young children.

    Then sometime last year, the woman next door started to get unfriendly with us over some ridiculous accusation and we stopped talking to them. When thing got worst after continuous provocation from her to engage us in some verbal exchange, we got into some heated argument and her husband stepped in. We have managed to resolved the conflict and thing get quiet for a while.

    Recently, the woman next door started to harass my wife again. After each harassment, my wife was left shaken and have a stomach cram for hours.  Both me and my wife are over 50 years of age and my wife is not in the best of health.

    We believed that, with the help of their CCTV, the woman next door have always timed her appearance at their doorstep precisely, when my wife is at the corridor, coming or leaving home, alone or with our 4 years old daughter.

    We approached the husband next door, but this time, he also get hostile at us and challenge us to call the police or sue them in court.  All the while, we have not call in the police as we have known that this is a neighbour conflict, and the police could only advice us to settle it in a friendly manner.

    My wife was very upset and called HDB to enquire about the cameras, she was directed to the Town Council office and was told that an officer will look into this matter. My wife saw somebody visited the neighbor the following day and later, the officer from Town Council called my wife and told her about his finding.

    In his own words, the town council officer told my wife that the cameras will not be removed as the neighbor planned to extend their permit. She was told that only 2 of the cameras are real and the one pointing at our door is a dummy. He also told her that the neighbor offered to remove the dummy camera pointing at our door if she find it offending.

    After receiving the call, my wife was feeling so bad I have to rush home from work to see to her.  Later that night, I went to the Police Center and file a report against my neighbor. It was last Friday night.


    On the following Monday morning, I called the town council officer to enquire further on how long are they permitted to have those cameras installed and how many units are allowed.  I have insisted on knowing and told him that I have already lodged a police report.

    Reluctantly, he told me that our neighbor DID NOT have a permit to install those cameras at the corridor.  They are in the process of applying for a permit now and he has allowed then to leave the cameras as they are.

    I was furious, I have insisted that the cameras should be taken down immediately and he agreed. I have also follow up with an email, attached a copy of the police report to the Town Council, copy to the HDB branch office and chairman of the Town council. I have plea with them for some immediate action and also not to grant any permit to have cameras installed at our corridor, should the neighbor apply for one.

    That evening, when I got home from work, I stepped out of the lift and took few steps into the corridor, that woman started cursing at us loudly inside their house. Their door is always opened while we prefer to keep door shut. The Town Council officer must have notified them.  And they have has no direct view of the lift lobby. Its those cameras again.

    Today, it has been 3 days now since I sent that email. I have received auto-reply from the town council and HDB but no formal acknowledgement that this issue have been looked into. So I called the town council officer again and was told that he have notified the neighbor to remove the cameras and they have agreed. He will also follow up with a written notification to them but have no date-line on when those cameras will be removed. 

    Those cameras have been there illegally for more than a year now, I just wonder how long they will drag on ?  I took half a day of urgent leave yesterday afternoon and a whole day today as my wife is in a very bad emotional state.


    My question:

    Is there anyone here with similar experience to share on how you deal with them ?

    It appear that the Town Council is taking a very lenient view to this offence and have no intention to take any action against them. In fact, have allow them take their time to remove those cameras.

    Is there any cause of action I can take as my wife have suffered this mental torturing long enough. Legal action is too costly and lengthy,  and violent is not in our nature.

    Any constructive advice will be much appreciated.

    Thanks for reading this long winded posting.
