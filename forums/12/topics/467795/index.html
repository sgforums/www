    Once you get the final judgement for the divorce, there will be another "trial" for the division of matrimonial assets. It is not up to your partner to say what he or shes wants, it is up to both of you to agree, or put up your case through lawyers if an agreement cannot be reached. This will go to family court and the judge will make a judgement. Your agreement is subjected to prevailing law and conditions. Like if your partner can't qualify to own the HDB by him/herself, your agreement to let him/her have it means nothing and the judge will ask you two to sell and divide accordingly.
