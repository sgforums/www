    As some knew, i just had a hard time with my relationship. The time has come for us to split, but she is
    still holding on to her end. Anyway this is not the issue I wish to address.

    As I travelled, a book appeared, teaching me stuff which I should learn. This book has lifted my
    spirit and brought me back to my feet. For the singles who are feeling down, do ponder over the points
    a little, cheer up and bring yourself back on your feet.

    There are several chapters, but I will just summarise.

    1) Assessing yourself
    You are better than you think!
    Recognise your capabilities
    Don't be afraid to change
    Don't be afraid to try

    2) Facing Reality
    Don't Regress
    Stop the Pity Party
    Don't try to impress peope with Make-believe
    Adapt to the present
    Let our expectations be real

    3) Self Esteem
    Do not think you are the loser
    Think positive
    Never See yourself as inadequate

    4) Love and Friendship
    Do Something about that Lonely feeling.
    Make yourself Visible
    Don't Precipitate Rejection
    Just be yourSelf
    Accept What Cannot be Changed
    Stick with the Winners

    I will just keep it short for now.

    Perhaps some will flame me and question me about the points above. They are just
    merely lifted from a book, and I say it does help me alot. Might not be able to
    answer any questions regarding the points, but I will try defending.

    It's pretty hard for people to get over a couple's day alone. I had went through this
    and I know that feeling. For those who are single, cheer up. Life will get better, ok?

    : )

    Have a good week everyone!
