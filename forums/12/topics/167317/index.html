Aunt Agony Poll Statistic

  • Yunhaier

    This is the compilation to all the polls I have created. Suppose to do it once a year, but yunhaier had forgotten about it.

    Can put sticky if you wish (wink at MCsquare).

    P.S: This is only for fun... sample size too small and some bo liao polls not included.

    ***

    In a general poll of the attitude towards Love...

    About 28% of AA forumite feels that they are unlovable and that they could never see yourself getting attached, let alone get married. These people desire to love and be loved - however, they are lacking in confidence and self esteem.

    This is followed by a 16%, who feel the importance of having many possible partners before marriage and having the fun before being tied down. With the same equal percentage, another 16% of AA forumite believe love in a form of companionship and wouldn't mind co-habitation.

    In a general poll of whether one would resort to casting Love spell onto their fleeing partner...

    Almost half (47.8%) of forumite disagree with casting Love spell onto their partners, whose heart is fleeing. These forumites believe that Love is natural and Love is freedom, while one fifth (21.7%) of forumite would do anything to win back their lost love.

    In a general poll of the scenario that cannot be tolerated...

    About one third (33.3%) of forumite cannot tolerate the existance of their partners having a fark buddy. This is followed by 26.6% of forumites unable to accept their partners having sexual relationship with people they detest.

    In a general poll of whether one would confront their opponents in love...

    About two third (67.8%) of forumite would never confront their love opponents directly, while about one third (32.1%) of the people here wouldn't mind a face to face confrontation.

    In a general poll of whether one would want to know the absolute truth of his/her partner's inner emotional realm...

    One third (33.3%) of the forumites are interested to know, while the other two thirds (66.6%) of forumites believe in taking things naturally.

    In a general poll of the scale between Love or Parents...

    An overwhelming 86.3% of forumites would choose Love over parents, while 13.6% would choose otherwise. Most cite this reason 'Hoping one day parents will understand...'

    In a general poll of forumites' attitude towards match making services...

    An overwhelming 89.4% wouldn't attempt any match making services. Only a miserable 10.2% would say Yes. Most polled 'No' rather be monk/nun.

    In general poll of knowing 'The One' immediately without those long journey...

    Close to two third (63.6%) of forumites rather live life through and experience failing relationship before meeting up with 'The One'. Slightly more than one third of them (36.3%) prefers to know who is 'The One' immediately, without the natural search.

    In general poll of decision of relationship upon one knowledge of having fatal illness...

    28.1% of forumites will choose to keep mum and find reasons to leave their partners. An equal percentage (28.1%) of forumites would reveal the truth and hope that their partners will stick with them till death.

    Cheers
