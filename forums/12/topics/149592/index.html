    THIS DEED is made on the day of August 2005 between Her Name I.C Name hereinafter referred to as "the Wife"] of the one part and Name I.C no [hereinafter referred to as "the Husband"] of the other part.

    WHEREAS:

    I. The parties were married at the Singapore Marriage Registry on the _______ day of ___________ 1997.

    II. There are no children to this marriage.

    III. They are joint owners of an HDB flat at Apt Blk .

    IV. The parties have been living separately since sometime at the end of May 2005 when the Husband moved out of the matrimonial flat. Although he has subsequently moved back into the flat, they have lived in separate rooms and in separate households.

    V. The Wife has moved out of the matrimonial flat on _____ August 2005 and is contemplating commencing divorce proceedings.

    VI. Parties have agreed to enter into this agreement to regulate their conduct vis a vis each other pending the Wife's decision in respect of the divorce proceedings.

    NOW THIS DEED WITNESSES as follows:-

    The Husband and the Wife mutually covenant and agree with each other as follows:-

    1. The Husband and the Wife shall each live separate and apart from and free from the marital control (if any) of the other and neither of them shall molest, annoy, disturb or interfere with the other or his or her relations, friends or acquaintances or in his or her profession or business.

    - 2 -
    2. Upon the expiry of three years from the date of their separation, either party may petition the Court to dissolve the marriage and in the event that a party intends to commence such proceedings, the other party shall consent to the marriage being dissolved and shall execute such consent or other documents signifying his or her consent.

    3. This agreement does not preclude either party from commencing proceedings for a dissolution of the marriage on such grounds as he or she sees fit and at such time as he or she sees fit.

    4. The parties agree that both parties shall have the right of access to the matrimonial flat and to reside in the flat. The Wife shall have exclusive possession of one room and the Husband shall have exclusive possession of one room and neither party shall enter into the other party's room without consent.

    5. The parties shall continue to pay the monthly mortgage instalments of the flat until the flat is sold.

    6. The parties shall pay the expenses of the flat, that is, the utility bills, conservancy and service charges, telephone bills, television licence fees, SCV fees and any other charges arising from the use of the flat equally for the period when they are both residing in the flat. The Husband shall solely bear the charges for as long as the Wife continues to reside outside the flat.

    IN WITNESS WHEREOF the parties have set their hands and seals the day and year first abovewritten.
