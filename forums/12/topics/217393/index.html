    i think in a relationship, sparks will always be gone when you reach a steady stage.

    When that happens, (for some maybe 2-3 months time, some maybe up to 1 year), its time when both parties need to put in effort to maintain the relationship. A relationship does not survive on sparks alone, it survives on effort, care and concern and commitment amongst others.

    When you feel that the spark is gone, it would be nice to surprise your partner with little gifts or special holidays etc. It should spice up your relationship a little, provided that both still love each other. (Of course, when both hate each other, its another story.)

    Also, it is not very difficult to say "I love you" or to hold hands when watching TV or when simply slacking. Such small actions, actually to some research (Dont ask me where, I cant remember), does improve and maintain the intimacy of a relationship!

    IMHO, you need effort and not just the novelty of love.

    Cheers!
