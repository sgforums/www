    Signs to look out for:

    Behavioural changes
    - Finding it hard to go to sleep
    - Avoiding people or activities
    - Giving away personal possessions

    Physical changes
    - Neglecting one's appearances
    - Sudden weight loss or gain
    - Loss of energy

    Emotional changes
    - Moody, tearful, quiet, outbursts
    - Loss of concentration

    Verbal clues-
    Expresses feelings of despair, hopelessness or helplessness
    - Makes statements like 'I won't be around to face...', 'All my problems will end...'
