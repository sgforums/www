    If you feel life is boring, you could join some of the outings scheduled by the forumites, or you could join activities at your local community centre? Or perhaps just ask your friends out for dinner & a round of drinks. Some relaxed chitchat helps to loosen you up : )

    If you feel that your relationships have broken down, you're Not alone. Those of us in AA have gone through the same thing. It doesn't mean that you won't find somebody special, you just learn the lessons & move on. Enjoy your single days, it can be fun.
