    I was queuing for my courses and my driving instructor started his conversation with me. I can't remember how it begin, but it was definitely something lame.

    'Woah, later no go out meh? Night still young leh...'

    I was giving him that kind of look and replied his style, saying that I have to rush home.

    'Woah, so guai... see your pattern, aiyo...'

    'Huh what pattern?' I asked

    'Aiya... before your marriage, you must play hard. Go fool around you know what I mean?... don't be like me, regret when I am old.... '

    Then I asked him, 'then when do you think its the time to stop?'

    'When you feel the time coming lar...'

    'What if you don't or can't feel it?'

    There was no answer

    ***

    Some people felt regreted when they didn't fling around during the time when they were younger. And in fact, when they are married, this craving brew and may wreck much damage to their marriage. The theory explains: to play around before you are being tied down?

    Then my question arises: are you SO fringin` sure you won't be playing around even after marriage?

    This is not a question of time - this is a question of mindsets.

    Cheers
