    Career's your means to all of the other 3. Without a good and stable career, you would not see a condo, car or a stable relationship with your GF. Yes, you can get a good relationship without money, but at the end of the day, money helps. Money is like the grease that ensures your gears move smoothly. Your gears can move without money, but with more friction.

    When talking about the condo, it must be in reference to freehold condos in desirable locations. Any fool can buy a 99 yr condo in Woodlands or Choa Chu Kang.

    Lastly, a car's a liability in Singapore. I'm typically shocked at Singaporeans who purchase a car without owning a home. Unless your job, like you are in sales, requires alot of travelling, there is no real point in owning a car. It starts depreciating the moment you leave the showroom in your car.

    Although I put it in that order, you could possibly prioritise having a GF before a condo.

    I myself have covered 3 of the above, with the car still hovering as a possibility, my least priority. In addition, those that take a car loan to purchase a car is kinda killing themselves. End up paying alot for a vehicle that loses value day by day. They should be looking to pay the car in full or at least 70%. No point giving free money to the bank. If you can't pay that much, upfront, then perhaps you have no business owning a car, unless again, your job requires it.
