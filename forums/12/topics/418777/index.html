    Do you have any strong points outside of academic studies? Examples will be sports awards or extremely high rate of participation in community services. There are a few scholarships which are related to these areas. 

    Anyway, you can have a look here:

    http://student.brightsparks.com.sg/scholarshipproviderlist.php

    Aside from scholarships, another few possible options will be to take a bank loan, or sign on with SAF / MOE. 

    Taking a bank loan can be risky, if you cannot find a job soon after you finish your studies. Signing on means that you are bonded. If you break the bond, things are going to get very nasty for you.

    You can also try getting bursaries, apply for work-study scheme where you help out in the uni, or get help from your parents and use their CPF (You will need to pay them back bit by bit every month once you have graduated).
