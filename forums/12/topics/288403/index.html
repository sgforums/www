    Maybe you cannot see the benefits in the near future but let me tell you. If you have obtained a diploma in accounting, you can choose 2 options:

    1.) Do a UK or Australia degree in accountancy and work towards your professional qualification or

    2.) Take up ACCA and start at level 2 and complete your ACCA.

    If you are local, better listen to this.....there are thousands of foreigners and locals (China national, Malaysian and those who are switching from non accounting line to accounting) taking ACCA from either FTC, FTMSglobal or Singapore Accountancy Academy (SAA).

    You should take a walk down to their campus or go the the Singapore Expo during their Dec exams to see them there and they will be the ones who will compete jobs with you in the future. So if you are to drop your studies now, then I think it is not worth it.
