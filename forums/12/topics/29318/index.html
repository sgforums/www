    Chin up improvements can only be made by doing chin up. tat's the best and easiest way.

    i went into the army pulling only 1 chin up also. i managed to pull 13 after 6 weeks.

    first of all, you need to do chin ups on yr own. there are things called chin up trainers. so start with those first. these machine help you thru the counter weights. so you could be doing like 10 assisted chin ups per set.

    do 4 sets of yr maximum number you can pull. use the trainer for 2 weeks. slowly reduce the counter weight. must do this session everyday. can afford sat and sun off day.

    if you dun have the chin up trainer machine, get someone to support you as yr counter weight, but bear in mind the 'counter weight' is not consistant, so might be a lil more difficult. IE help by lifting you up slightly.

    after two weeks start on the real chin up bar. again you should be able to do at least 4 real chin up by yr 3 week first session on yr own.

    everyday, do 4 sets of yr max. alternate between 10 - 15 push ups after each set. you should be able to increase on an average of 1.5 chinup per week.

    Good luck.
