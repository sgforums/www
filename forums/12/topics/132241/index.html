When you talk about overwhelming (I'm personally prefer to use overcoming...) the 'differences', it would depends on what 'differences' you're talking about... And... It's often not just a MOMENT of 'overwhelming'.... As in... "YES!! We've MADE IT!!!" A relationship is a constant effort and journey... And cultural differences can often come with hidden natures...
