    Why changes take place and He/She becomes like a stranger so suddenly.

    Move on my child... Be grateful the person passes by.

    You are a better one in times.

    Changes take place and he/she becomes a stranger because you expect him/her to change when you don/t want to accept him/her as who he/she is.

    Unfortunately its so simple yet so difficult to do.
