    Well. We all define time as passing motion according to magnetism. Time is relative according to Einstein, so we do things with time as relevance. You know how long is 1 second, 1 hour, 1 day, 1 decade.

    Even when that person has left you (for reasons unknown), you can still feel the love. The love which is supposedly past once you two are seperated but yet it is there. Its like getting 101/100 for some test.
