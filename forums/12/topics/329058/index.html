    That's because you were too used to having him around in your life for the period both of you were together.

    Cry if you are upset and whenever you have reached your emotional threshold, your tears will eventually kill the love detachment off in your heart. And time will wash away all the lost, numb, meanigless and uncomfortable feelings from you faster than you think it should take.

    Moving on doesn't means forgetting about it, it means you are slowly rejecting him from your mind and you won't think about him as often as now.

    You can think less frequently of him to the point of getting use to your current life routine without the need to think about him.

    It's only temporarily, you can think lesser of him but you can never forget about him.

    You will eventually erase him from your heart when you have found someone better than him and your time spent with your new guy and the love you have for the new guy will overtake and replaced whatever remaining feelings you have left for your ex-bf.
