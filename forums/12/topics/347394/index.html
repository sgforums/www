    Someone plotting revenge?

    Its not hate, but hurt.

    That person has been hurt, by something grevious or something petty, usually the ego has taken a dent. This hurt generates hate, and the need to dish out tit for tat, or most likely, this for that. Revenge!

    Find out where the hurt is. Is it the ego, or pride, or loss of someone special or something valuable. Once you can identify the hurt, it will lose its potency. Note feeling the hurt, is not the same as acknowledging the hurt. The difference is in feeling pissed, as against saying " I was pissed by her remark." The latter leads the way towards pacification. The former snowballs.

    Identify the hurt to forestall revenge.

    As for retribution, forget it, as it is usually association. We are quick to attribute an unfortunate turn to  past events and say its retribution.
