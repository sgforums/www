    Ah, another long distance relationship (LDR) that came to an unfortunate end. Well, by now, you ought to realise a few things about LDR:

    I) Discipline of all forms must be instill, from both party, for without physical presence, the other party isn't here to cater to your possible needs. If not, the likelihood of breaking is very high.

    II) LDR is not your usual, common and normal kind of BGR relationship you are leading; everything must be twice da effort.

    III) Main reason why LDR fail is almost always because of the failure of (I) from one party, which result in the dating of other people, in consideration of a potential parter.

    P.S: Even if you were to propose - its utterly foolish to do it right after your come back. You just never know what has really happen during this period of time. By the way, if she can't even survive the test of this transitory separation, do you think she is in and ready for marriage?

    Cheers
