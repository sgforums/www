    1. Acknowledge that you do not have any more time to waste worrying.
    2. Come up with a detailed plan, breaking down into steps within the tight timeframe.
    3. Accomplish the various steps, sticking to the plan strictly.

    Alternately:
    1. Consult your prof on possible remedies (whether can extend dateline; convert it into research report instead etc).
    2. Go ahead with the part manufacturing. The more you delay, the worse you will feel.
    3. Realize that this is not the end of your life. Think positive. Focus on the solutions, not the problems.
