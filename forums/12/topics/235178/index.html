    The only effect of sit-ups is to strengthen your abs... which may indeed prevent your tummy from sticking out so much. But it does not address the underlying cause of a bulging tummy: too much abdominal fats. As soon as your abs lose strength from inactivity, you will get a tummy again.

    By all means do sit-ups, but only as a complementary measure. The best way to deal with abdominal fats is still through cardiovascular exercises like swimming and jogging.
