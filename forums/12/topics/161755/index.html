    Dear Laurence82,

    We hafta acknowledge the fact that we're Not superhumans, we can't help everybody. Some need time, & sink into depression for awhile. During this period, all you can do is to be a friend to them in need, & let them have a listening ear or a helping hand whenever they ask for it.

    Some prefer to wallow in misery & self pity. No matter how you may wanna help them, if they don't want to be helped, you hafta realise that whatever you do won't be able to change anything.

    Timing could be important, because when a person 1st experiences grief or failure, it takes some time for them to find their footing again. The kinda help you render is different at different stages.

    At the end of the day, you also hafta take care of yourself. If all of us could solve our friends' problems, then there wouldn't be the need for Aunt Agony columns in so many magazines...
