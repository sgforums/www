    I was facing this same exact situation, just about half a week ago. I feel for you, my friend.

    When a girl say "her feelings had faded", she really means it. There is really, no point trying to know what's her REAL reason behind the lost feelings. Third party invasion, or simply chronic erosion, it doesn't matter.

    Will knowing the real reason, get her back? Will she turn back?

    The real answer is no. She has chose to move on, you should, too.

    Yes, it would hurt inside, very much. I've chosen to move on, and forget her. I'm still hurting inside, but I know there's no more point in reserving the love for her. Why do you want to keep an unfinished burger from yesterday, when you can easily buy a new burger today?

    Go out, and do some sports. Cycling works for me. Get to know new friends, expand your social circle.

    And realise this: the predestined number of failed relationships has reduced by one. Perhaps now, it is at zero. Look forward to the relationship you'll encounter, sooner or later.

    Since you're heading into NS, it's a good time to re-orientate yourself, back into singlehood.
