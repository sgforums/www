    Usually you chose the people you like to love.

    There are ten... you like two.

    You ignore the eight and get to know the two better.

    You decide to love one. The one you decided on do not want to have a love relationship with you.

    You ask the other one that you love and the both of you decide to develop a love relationship.

    ===========================================

    There are ten, you have a crush on one.

    You decide to have a love relationship with the one.

    The one you have a crush on is not interested to have a love relationship with you but you continue to behave like you have a love relationship or you had broken off from a love relationship.

    There was no love relationship in the first place.

    ==========================================

    You have a crush on someone.

    You decided to have a love relationship with that someone.

    That someone decided to get to know you better.

    That someone decided after getting to know you, not to have a relationship with you but you continue to behave like you have a love relationship or you had broken off from a love relationship.

    There was no love relationship in the first place.

    ==========================================

    You have a crush on someone.

    That someone has a crush on you.

    The two of you decided to have a love relationship.

    When the two of you get to know each other better, one or both of you decide that you don't even like each other and break off from the love relationship.

    There was no love relationship in the first place because you two did not even know each other enough to start a love relationship!

    In the meantime, you might have become physically close and that causes a lot of problems...
