    Its risky to assume your feelings or even your thoughts about him just because you knew about his feelings for you. Many a times, its not what we actually felt - rather, its the constant thinking that made us feel subconsciously we may have a small crush on that person.

    THIS IS NOT LOVE NOR TRUE CHEMISTRY.

    The avoidance is probably because of your confusion and inability to handle or conclude things in your mind. Together with the 'time pressure' you are having from his deadline (end of the month), all these factors adds up together and create a little burden in your emotions and mind.

    Advice?

    Don't assume; take steps understand and realise what is all this about. To simplest way is to supply hope - you can remain passive and let him chase you, but you must feed him with hope; his own source of motivation. Probably he has felt that you has no interest in him, therefore, he could have given up.

    Comment how nice this shirt is, smile, etc, so as to tell him (indirectly) that you ain't avoiding him because you ain't interested in him. Agree for a lunch together (or even initiate one for a start to get him his motivation coming) and be yourself. With the little time you have, it should be sufficient to keep his confidence coming.

    Even if he has to leave the company - you have already lay the spell in him by then. Meanwhile, communicate and see how comfortable you are with him. Compatability of all kind (character-wise) very important - in simple words: TAKE your OWN time to know him. From there, you will know whether you have fallen for him and if he is suitable for you.

    Cheers
