    If Olive Oil and Waxsol don't work, ur earwax is prolly too hard, large and/or stuck to the skin of your ear canal. Ear candling works pretty well, but I find it's not practical ($$) and doesn't work well on badly clogged ears / dry ear canal conditions like mine.

    Try those metal ear diggers u can buy from old chinese convenience stores. Those are excellent. Be careful though, you'd want to coax the hard bits out but dun scrape your ear canal skin too hard & risk infection. Best to dig right after a hot shower. But whatever u do, don't use cotton buds.

    If it's too painful to remove, u might have to go to ENT. it's pretty cool, they have equips that will scope right into your ear.. then u can check out all that icky gunk!! If it's still too painful for the doc to remove it manually, u can opt to go to a private clinic + general anasthesia like I did many years back for a really nasty clogged ear + infection (partially coz of those bloody cotton buds).
