    I'm in my late 20s, and i had a resurfacing of acne problems about 2 years ago. Here's what works for me:

    1) Anti-bacterial cleanser from National Skin Centre. You can buy it off the shelve
    2) Toners that contain alcohol (non-alcoholic ones are too mild)
    3) Daily doses of vitamin c
    4) Avoid too much stress

    If you've got a serious case, see a dermatologist and get antibiotics. They can supress breakouts.

    For treatment of scars, try:
    1) microdermabrasion (mild, but doesn't cause redness)
    2) chemical peels (harsh, cause redness, but really peels)
    3) And if (1) and (2) doesn't cut it for you, well's there's laser resurfacing. But that would be overkill.
