Take for instance, you're going on a trip abroad, a field trip or a school camp, I think in those instances you will be so excited that you are eager to be there from the day before right??

    In statistical research, it has been proven that, changes needs to be done over time, getting better each time, and not immediate change. And you need at least 21 times of such change continuously before it will be a permanent change.
