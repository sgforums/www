    When the economy goes into crisis.. it doesn't matter if you graduate from poly, JCs or Uni, all will be affected.

    The problem .. when that happens.. degree holders will start competing with lower level graduates (Poly or JC) for the same job. Poly/JC graduates will have it harder as competition becomes more intense.

    If you are thinking about dropping out of your studies.. I suggest you don't. Just lower your expectations of a job prospect for now. And continue to strive towards getting your paper qualifications. Economy goes in cycles.. so by the time the situation improves.. you will not be left behind.
