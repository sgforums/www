    -- the following was written by me to all my friends last thursday... it really happened to me, and it sort of woke me up from some dreamland that if you take much pre-caution, you just can't be too careful... Hope you guys will be enlightened...

    You guys ever thought about your life?? about what you will miss if you're not around - as in you're no longer living?? Ever thought who will miss you?? Ever
    thought who are the people who will continue to acknowledge your existence in this world?? Ever thought of who you will wish you could see just before you close your eyes for the very last time?? Is your life a day in which you have lived by with some people who you consider them as friends, just because you hang out with them??

    Well, I think no matter how old you are, you ought to know who you will want to see for a last time and treat that person or people well... He/she could have been the very person who have been spanking you since
    you know what is good and bad. For all you know, this is the very person who have taught you the most important lessons in life.

    This is something that happened to me less than 6 hours ago. I was crossing the road and a car just hit me. I'm not injured though, as it was at a very very slow speed and the car hit my leg, but it's just a
    push, and I jumped off the bike, and had it land under the car, which sort of shocked the driver. I was not in the wrong too, I was crossing the road legally. However, it left me thinking what will happen if
    something had happened to me...

    People who knows me will know that I do a lot of reflection. Thinking about my actions and words, whether if they are good or bad, or have hurt someone... I used to recall the days when my dear parents who use to beat me and scold me.. those were always like every day. I think my dear brothers can justify that. For all the things that have done, I
    still respect my dear ol' papa and mama at home, and I do love them for all that they have done for my dear brothers and I. Even my dear brothers, I know we have been through a lot. We used to play together, and had our fights together, but in the end we still basically can't be seperated from each other for in us, there's each other.

    However on this day, I realized that even when you miss the people that you love and know you will be with them for your whole life... but the thing is you never know when your life will come to an end. Mine could have ended today... Even for all the precaution that you take... I did wait for the white man to come out before I cross the road.. but sometimes, things just happen to you. If I had been involved in a major
    accident, I doubt I would even have this chance to realize the life is so delicate. You might not even have the chance to tell your last wishes or even to see you loved ones the last time.

    I only want to say, when you still can cherish your own life and your loved ones, even they may be the ones who have hurt you emotionally or physically, just forget the grudges, and love them and cherish them
    again, for you may not even have the chance to do it.

    Last thing, Pa, Ma, Kor, Jun, I love you...
