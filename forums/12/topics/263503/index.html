    Originally posted by MooKu:
    How can you mend a broken heart?
    Every broken part in the heart can be mended by a precious memory. A precious memory of having loved and being loved before. A memory of caring and sharing. A memory of laughter, cries, anger, warmth, comfort, companionship...

    How can you stop the rain from falling down?
    You cannot stop the rain from falling down. Well at least the REAL rain. All you can do is to prepare yourself for it. On the other hand, where will the rain be if the sunshine is in you all the time?

    How can you stop the sun from shining?
    You are trying to contradict yourself, is it? Why stop the sun from shining? It is the one thing that radiates life and gives life. The one thing that shines through darkness and warm the cold. The one thing that reveals the hidden and the one thing that will lives on long after we all leave earth.

    What makes the world go round?

    Trick question. I will not answer you on the scientific part. Too chim. However, I will try my best to relate this question to the matters of the heart:

    Every heartbeat in you makes the world go round. It gives life to what you see, feel, touch, smell, hear. It gives colour to the otherwise dull enviornment around you. It gives a sense of 'soul' or being alive in every aspect possible. It gives you the incentive and initiative to take a step forward.

    I hope you can find something within what I have typed. It may not be a good answer, but its definately an answer, from my perspective.
