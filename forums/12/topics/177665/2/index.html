    I am insignificant.

    You are as insignificant as me.

    We can stand in the middle of a crowded street and scream at the top of our lungs and nobody will care.

    They will stare at us. They will laugh at us.

    They will discuss among themselves.

    They will forget about us.

    The harsh reality is, nobody actually gives a damn to know more about your life unless you have something of value to them.

    Remember that cute lady who rejected you?

    She is insignificant.

    She is probably better looking than you. She has better genes than you.

    There is nothing special about that.

    There is no reason to feel inferior.

    You are missing a point.

    Part of the reason why we are social beings is because we came to realize we have to depend on one another for survival.

    Therefore, the fact is no one in this world can tell you he has more right to exist than someone else.

    We are born with equal rights to exist despite the unequal abilities.

    Always remember that first and foremost.

    And we all depend on each other for love and unconditional acceptance.

    We spend our lifetime searching.

    How do you protect yourself from being abused by others in that process?

    By giving yourself unconditional acceptance.

    By loving yourself for who you are.

    And in that process, you will rid yourself of your vices and strengthen your virtues.

    When you have the ability to love and accept yourself completely. People will naturally come to you.

    Because they need to know the truth as much as you do.

    Take care.
