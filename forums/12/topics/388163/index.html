    This is just the first step. It will improve your confidence bit by bit.

    What you have to know is it is not by birth that we people are decided as introverts or extroverts, those deemed as extroverts are just people who got used to meeting people, etc, at an early point in their life. Then there are those "introverts" that realised they would be on the losing end if they continue shutting themselves out from the society, hence they become "extroverts" to remain competitive with their mates, even if they originally don't like interacting with other people. That is what you have to do. Stop shutting yourself out and let people get to know you :) In the process you can rediscover yourself, know all kinds of people, know what type of GIRL you like xP
