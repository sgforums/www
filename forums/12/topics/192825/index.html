    It's still possible to get a job in Singapore with a diploma, provided that you are not picky about it. Since you have a job, consider yourself lucky. You are still young now, and what you are doing now is just to gain experience. The pay is secondary.

    What you are going to do after 2 years of NS depends on the diploma and other factors. You definitely will have a lot to catch up after 2 years of NS. Industries changes and you will need to read up if you intend to have a job that's related to your diploma. If you don't want, you can consider a career with SPF, SCDF, Prison Service, etc. If you want to go university, you definitely have to read to revise and recap.

    What you are going to acheive in 10 years' time, depends on you. While education will help to a certain extent, the rest depends on you. You have to take the initiative and cannot wait for others to tell you what to do. You also have to be aware of changes around, be flexible and adapt to the changes. If you are not willing to change, what you acheived now will be the same as 10 years later.

    You have to ask yourself what you are interested in to know your ambition. Your ambition can be your job, or it may not be. Some people's hobbies are their jobs, and they love it.

    Your future is in your own hands. Your friends, your family only can guide you by giving you advice, and it's to up to you to listen and make decisions for yourself.
