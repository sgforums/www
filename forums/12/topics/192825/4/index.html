    If I may make an observation, lonestar, one of the key things that's going to keep you from achieving any of your ambitions is yourself.

    Read through your responses to other people here, and you can see that the emerging pattern is one of you finding problems with people's suggestions before you've taken the time to explore all options.

    If you are going to achieve anything worthwhile, it's probably not going to happen quickly, much less immediately. The least successful people I have ever come across all have one common characteristic - impatience. This impatience leads them to do one of two things:

    1) they either dismiss ideas without careful thought and examination (as you have done here), or
    2) they keep changing their plans before the last one has had time to produce results, wasting their time, effort and money in the dropped plan

    Do yourself a favour and get out of that dismissive habit. Go out, do your homework and carefully study what's worked for other people. Who knows, your circumstances may not be all that different from Sim Wong Hoo's when he started Creative.
