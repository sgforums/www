    GrimR's Did You Know Section. (Little snippets of information regarding the story.)

    -This is my second story. My first was written pretty badly and I didn't have the face to show it to pple. Maybe I should re-write it?

    -When writing, I visualised Mint as the person I have a crush on. She's from my school, but I never had a chance to even get to know her name. Have been hoping to see her in school just about everyday, but sadly, I never did. Well, only seldom...

    -Mint's house was 'created' from my visit to a friend's house. I simply added in more atmosphere. (Chapter 4.)

    -The pool hall mentioned refers to the one at Cuppage Plaza. I know the cue sucks etc... *shrugs shoulders* don't look at me. It just popped up my mind. So I just wrote what came to mind. (Chapter 1.)

    -Believe it or not, I actually used the old 'Bookworm' books as a source of inspiration in certain chapters. Can't help but
    feel the writing style in it is good.

    -Some of the chapter titles were taken from songs. Would you like me to list the few here?

    -Initially, a tragic/sad ending was planned. But I wanted readers to put down the story with a 'satisfying sigh', so I opted for a happier one.

    -I know this the one that had some pple criticising me. The shooting star! I was quite short of ideas at that time and so I thought why don't we add some exaggeration here. Doesn't it happen in dramas too? (Chapter 3.)

    -Writing the sad parts were pretty hard. Had to get myself into the mood, by listening to some sad songs.

    -Kate's little story was taken from Carrie Chong's reflections, on Perfect 10 98.7FM. I was listening in one night while writing, when I heard it and thought it would be good to put it in. (Chapter 10.)

    -In actual fact, I DON'T have a little sister! I'm the youngest in my family, and I have one older brother. (Chapter 9.)

    -Had quite a couple of late nights staying up to write. The more ideas I come up with, the harder it is for me to let go and settle down to sleep. But it's been worthwhile. ^_^

    -Clint, Mint, donÂ’t the two names rhyme?

    Yup this just about sums up all I want to share with you all about my story. Hopefully there will be another on soon. Who knows, check back 1 month later and you may just see a new post by me!
