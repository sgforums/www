    I think the appropriate way to use ProActiv is not to over use it.

    I learned that if you only use only a small amount of the cleanser and
    "GENTLY MASSAGE" it on your face, it does not irritate your skin or burn it lat all.

    The secret to using cleanser with microbeads is to use it really gently on your skin. Since the formula already has scrubbing materials (mircobeads), there is not need to use extra force into it.

    If the user like myself(before), insert too much force into the scrubbing, thinking that it will cleanse it more, is a total MISTAKE!!!

    And following the advice from the other replies, (THANK you!), Not using too much toner and the repairing lotion helps too.
