    I'm in my early 50s. Married with 2 children in the early twenties. My wife is working and things were fine and life was pretty ordinary. However life was not easy the last 2 years. Initially I took in my sick mother-in-law on a fortnightly basis but have since become permanent when she becomes bed-bound. I had to make my son give up his room for my mother-in-law and the caretaker maid. My wife had since shifted to my daughter's room and my son to my room. Business wasn't going well as my company had gathered a bad-debt of over a million dollars and legal fees mounting to hundreds of thousand dollars. In order to keep business going, I was without salary for almost a year. I had to depend on my wife's salary to upkeep the family. My wife had since become depressed worrying over her mother, our children (they are not too bad but somehow I find that it can never satisfied the mother). She would blamed me for encouraging my son to take up subjects that she deem he was not interested. I had thought it was the best for him since he was clueless after his o level. He went on to diploma and a private university on the subject I had encouraged . he wasn't doing very well and being a boy became interested in soccer often ending up late nights outside watching the EPL. She would picked on him for any things and often would end up quarrelling with him. My son won't give in and I'm caught in between. I would finally be blamed for encouraging him to study the subjects. I am liberal and would encourage them to study a subject they like so that in future jobs can become a hobby rather than a chore. Therefore I encourage my daughter to follow her dreams. She was good in drawing therefore went on to study game design. However upon graduating, she took on a job that was not related to her field. To make matter worse, she was not interested in her jobs and had since become very attached to computer spending all her time till late night on games and chatting. I got blamed again for encouraging her to select the subject. Due to work stressed, my health wasn't in good shape. I've been diagnosed with high blood pressure and mild diabetes and put on medication. My wife had always being sick every now and then (hereditary), therefore always blaming herself for not bring up the children properly. Every now and then she would bring up the subject of selling the house to downgrade as we are no longer young. I have often discourage her from selling as resale HDB is ridiculously high and that the money would be locked up in the cpf. At time I would lose my temper and tell her to go ahead. We haven't have any intimacy for 2 years since we sleep separately and I often get rejected even touching her. At time we end up quarrelling and she suggesting that I go look for another one. Talking to her becomes a chore. A simple conversation often end up with her raising her voice in a bad manner. For example, she complained that medical bill is expensive the this treatment and ask her why because I paid way lesser for the same treatment. She said she went in as a private patient while I went as a referral from the polyclinics. I asked why she didn't go polyclinic for referral and the conversation ended with her raising her voice saying why she should go to the polyclinic when her company is paying for it. I often go home after work and would sit down to watch TV and she would interrupted by saying that I have watched the whole day and she wants to watch the other channel. Otherwise she would comment that I should have pack the newspaper on the table when I see them in a mess or that I should go do some exercise instead. There's definitely something to say. I'm tired and sometime just don't feel like talking to her anymore. Due to frustrations from work and home, I often went alone to the coffee shop for a drink and 6 months ago, I met this Vietnamese beer lady. She was sort of fun to communicate with and I found myself laughing again. We often talk about anything and her life in Vietnam before coming to Singapore and I became quite interested. We become quite friendly and in fact, I found myself being jealous when I saw a male driving her to work. I do not understand as I have no relationship with her other than being the "other closed customer". I think I still love my wife who had sacrifice for the family. What is happening to me?
