    Death is but another passing phase.

    Try not to feel too sad over your friend's departure. The point is not to live the life we have over regrets, but to leave the life we had without regrets.

    Death within our social circle is a good reminder to us that life is fragile -- the pain of loss is natural. Rejoice in the fact that your friend's death has brought to those he left behind sadness, instead of happiness.
