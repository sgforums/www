    While 2 years is not a long time for a course, however, by the time the course ends, your daughter will be 10 or 11 years old. Imo, you might consider taking up the course in future when your daughter is older. If you are talking about a home business of selling cakes and cookies to people from home, you won't need a cert for it. Those Shatec cert is imo, more for ppl who are going to work in restaurants or hotel F & B industry. As for opening a small cafe, well there are alot of overheads to consider. Rental in Singapore is not cheap. On top of that, you will have to hire 1-2 ppl for cashiering/waitressing to assist you in looking after the shop if you do not want to be the whole time it is open for business. From what I see, cafes most peak period time is normally from afternoon until late at night(you have to at least be operating during this time to earn more money from cust). Again, this won't leave you much time to spend with your child. I understand that this is your dream, but ultimately, you need to decide where your priority lays in. You can always pursue it when your child is older.
