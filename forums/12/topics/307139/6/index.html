  • Yunhaier
    13 Feb 08, 01:04

     To those who believe whole-heartedly in fate, I say, take some of that belief out and learn to understand that self nurture makes the other half of the whole.

    Would you be able to seize that opportunity and make fate work towards your advantage if it 'finally arrives'? You see, that's only provided if you are conditioned and ready to receive Love in a manner that would allow you the option to make it advantageous for you.

    If not, fate is probably overhyped as a theory.

    In reality, many failed in relationship to obstacles, negative environment and most critically - to our unevolved self.

    In business, if you want to have good sales, you either have very strong branding or you must have a very good product. Of course, if you have both, your cash register will ring non-stop. When you apply that to Love, it simply means that either you are good at self marketing, or you are a good 'product', in which you could broaden your 'market share' and make fate will 'work' on a wider scope for you.

    One can't simply depend on luck, hoping for a consumer to walk by and buy your product; there must be some strategy to ensure a higher probability of success.   

     

    Cheers
