    Been too busy in the past, and missed out this post.. Dunno if you still want it, but this is what I learn about Schizophrenia in my Psychology class.

    Problems of is mainly

    - Perceptual
    Hallucinate by seeing and hearing things that are not there. Usually you will hear commands telling or something beckoning you to do stuff.

    - cognitive
    Delusions, often associated with thoughts of being followed, have some extraoridnary power or thinking of artistes falling in love with you.

    Subtypes
    ~ Disorganized
    Having thoughts that are odd and often have no links to each other, this is the most common problem. can just talk and talk lots of things out, but there is things may not associate to each other. Well if you can write a para of your prob as above, likely you don't fall into this type.

    ~ Catatonic
    This type is charaterised by one just stop doing things half way and freeze there, but one will still know the things that's going ard him. Just the mind didn't want to control the legs to walk so stop there.

    ~ Paranoid
    This one is a different from other subtypes. One can still have a trend of well-thought out ideas, and pattern of thinking, but has persucutary delusions. Like feeling annoyed, or fearful, or uncertain. Like who feels unsafe and suspect that someone is trying to stab you on the back kind of thing.

    ~ Undifferentiated
    This is more commonly known as multiple personality disorder like what fireice had mentioned earlier... One can just change from one character to another just like you switch the channels of the tv.
