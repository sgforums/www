    The first lecture is always like that. You pay 100% attention trying to absorb everything that is said. You end up mentally exhausted.

    You have embarked on tertiary education. No more like school days. Teacher guide you all the way.

    Now you are on your own. Even with the lecturers, class mates and study groups you are really on your own. They are there for you to use. Virtue vice, if you help your class mates, you help yourself also

    So from now onwards, prepare ahead  Read up on whats going to be taught before going to the lecture, so that you only spend part the time listening, most of the time making sense of what you have read, and asking question on what you don't understand.

    Remember you are on your own.
