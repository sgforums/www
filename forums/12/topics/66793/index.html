    We met on 3rd Jan 1999 in the schoolhall of our JC orientation. I entered the hall and she had her back faced to me. She turned around suddenly and her eyes captivated me...

    ****************
    Till now, whenever i asked her about it, she said she dun remember seeing me, but that image was always imprinted in my mind
    ****************
    She had the nicest eyes i ever saw. From den, i fell in love with the nicest girl in the world. And hence this story.

    I was a pretty good sportsman. Always been relatively better than peers of my age group. Until i met her.
    ****************
    I got to work immediately. Went up to her to borrow a pen. To write down her number... haha

    Nope, she didn't gave me her number. borrowed the pen, but couldn't ask her that directly. Only den did i found out that she was in my Orientation group. So naturally i got her number in the form of a contact list.

    I called her that night. Chatted for like eternity, and i asked her to go to school with me. She was quite popular amoing guys, but we just clicked there and then.

    We went for movies, dinners and everything else. I still have the ticket stubs and the receipts of the movie and dinner we had on our first date.
    ****************
    We became a couple on the 24th of Jan, 1999. Things became really sweet, the usual honeymoon period. We pretty much spend time together while doing our own things.

    We did pretty well for the O levels and we went to a better JC. Things begin to sour from then on.

    I was classmate with one of the prettiest girl in school. And that prettiest girl was a friend of my good friend... talk a bout right place, wrong timing.. .
