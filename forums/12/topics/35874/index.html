    A week ago, I met this girl in the forum and she started confiding to me about her problems.

    She's like almost everyone of us, loving someone that will not return. We talk for a couple of hours, long but true.

    Ever thought about this? When you decide to give someone a present but did not managed to send it to him/ her, can't take out too much courage to send it out?

    This happens to me. I bought a lot of present for him. I send out but all without a sender name. So definitely the person who receive it will not know who I am.

    The girl ask me," WHY do this? Let him know who you are. "

    But think about it back, a present given might not need to be from a person you have to know. All I need is only to let him know there is this 'santa' that knows every special days in his life, and give him presents providing him comfort and happiness.

    Knowing whether it's from me.. It's not important because all this will ended with this phrase.

    " I did it all out of love.."

    Enjoy your day everyone.
