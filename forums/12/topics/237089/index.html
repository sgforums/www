    It is only very important if you are looking to work for Government based Organisation like Stat boards or as a civil servant in one of the ministries. If you are planning to build a career in the private sector then having a private Diploma isnt that much of an issue but vary depending on each organisation as well and their demands.

    If i am not mistaken, Ministry of Manpower keeps a list of recognised bodies of educational providers both local and overseas, so does Ministry of Education as well as does local universities. The purpose of such a list is to help facilitate both government linked employers or local educational providers determine if the candidnate is likely to meet the paper requirement for the job opening or for further education options.

    Private sectors value experience and ability to succeed/sell/slog (The 3S) more than anything else depending on your field of employment, hence paper value may be considered secondary, however this also doesnt stop larger companies using headhunters to employ the most qualified person for certain positions as well.
