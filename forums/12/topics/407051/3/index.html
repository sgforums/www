    Patient with loneliness? patience with what one is being, what one is and of course, it presupposes one is accepting of oneself - warts n all. The wise would have beliefs but gravitate towards that beleifs that free them, unlimits them as human beings.

    The undoing is cos many erroneously assume that other-esteem is self-esteem. It is not, when the other/thing that made or caused one to feel ''good''/ ???""  collpases or disappears/ends where is the esteem. Self-esteem means that which one creates out of oneself from within - in this way, whether one is accepted/approved/validated by others/things becomes less important cos one is fulfilled in oneself; that which comes as approval, assent, validation is a bonus!

    PS - Theraphy works but sadly it is imposing that which society deems as normal. it is simply helping one to fit in. Beneath that, one is the same and that needs to be accepted or acknowledged cos no one is the same - all of us are unique. As long as that is unrecognised it is going to remain latent and situational/societal/neurotical anxiety are likely to be the mainstay and would inexorably surface in ...
