    When one tries to think ''positively'' or if one does so it is akin to escaping reality. It is being stubborn ( why stubborn - one sticking to limited beleifs, one's conditioning comes in, encroaches) and the wanting to only look at one side of anything of living that causes anguish and anxiety. Anxiety is always the fear of the future or what might unfold. Anguish is the guilt of what one may have been or done which is perceived as ''wrong'' by society or even by one''s folly.

     However, the ignoring does not mean the other that which is ignored is going to go away or ignore one. For eg, does the rain choose where to rain? In a muddy area something undesirable (merely a perception, a prejudice) like a swamp may grow and at the same time in an open garden a rose plant (also perception, also something told to one that this or that is nice) may blossom! However, if one is accepting of the reality of both phenomena - eg hot and cold, love and fear or left and right. one is free. It is the very acceptance that frees.
