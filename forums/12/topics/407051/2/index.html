    Anxiety is a by-product of unconsciously comparing oneself to others, to things, to ideas and hence the emotional stasis!

    Also, the disliking of what one is, what one does, the wishing and wanting and the conditioning of the past - all these reconciled, accepted , acknowledged one may feel better if not worse.

    Jettisoning preconceptuals models of life - one is free. it is the neurosis of projecting which is certainly incongruent to what is , or reality that adds to the split within.

    It is erroneous to assume that others, situations are causing them. it is one's unaccpetance of oneself, one's comparing, one''s conditioning. The more one is absent to one's ''self'' the more one is open to life and living. The barrier is that of imposing the 'self'' onto the canvas of life and the wishing and ... that is the undoing of many.

    Self means - what i think of myself, what others think of me, what life was, what life ought to be, what success must be, what it was ... the list can be piled Himalayan - imagine the repercussive ....especially when all these are used to prop oneself up!
