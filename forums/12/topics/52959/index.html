    Normally, I don't reply to family problem post because its very much often beyond our scope of control. However, I could sense your anguish and helplessness.

    When parents quarrel with one another, probably creating a scene and indirectly affect the child - it influence its psychological development and love definition. Most people don't understand the gravity of such impact and how much damage it actually cause to a child.

    Your parents ain't born into this world knowing how to be a parent. In the Ninth Law of Love - Marriage is not forever (Sad to say) - and its between two. You are old enough to think for yourself or even be a father biologically, so there are a few points I would like to comment.

    I) Since marriage is between two - the direct involvement would be your mother and your father. When critical problems occurs between them and when time passed until its time for them to divorce - let me tell you, take no side. Respect each of them because they bore you and that's about it. ITS THEIR LOVE LIFE and karma that they have to undergo. Come on, adults in their forties and fifties - they may still have to learn and grasp love cosmic lesson.

    II) You mum would probably need some form of support and though you may be right about your mum ranting about all sort of hell she could about your dad when they divorce - woman are still woman. Let her say what she wants - still take no side. However, be there when she needs you.

    III) Don't let this affect your attitude towards Love and Relationship. Probably it already has (subconsciously and consciously), however may I advice that you could channel all these bad negative experience you gain from your parent's marriage into a case study -and probably understand why their marriage not working, why they solve their problem in a certain way, character-wise, etc.

    P.S: Problems that exist in your parents marriage are very often not within your control. Your parents are way OLD-ENOUGH to know what they are doing. You may not share the same preception as either one of them, however, you certainly have the power to take good care of your own life.

    Cheers
