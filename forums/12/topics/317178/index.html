    Originally posted by jacqn:

    sorry.. but what is PBL learning?

    Problem-Based Learning.

     

    RP is the first institution of higher learning which adopts full PBL system. Everyday students in RP are given a set of problems to solves. Students will have to research on materials available(internet, library, given resource etc) to tackle for that day's problem.

    At the end of the day, students(in groups of 5) will have to do a presentation on that given problem. For the presentation part, there is no right or wrong solution. They're looking at your ability to reason.
