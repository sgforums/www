    try ASKING QUESTIONS... it may not be personal, at least not for a start.

    show some concern... e.g. How's your day? How was class/work?

    ASK OPEN QUESTIONS... general and objective enough for person to start talking.

    if person clams up or give you monosyllabic answers, try to ask CLOSED QUESTIONS. eg.

    Did you go the concert last night? Were you late for class this morning?

    next step - you share your opinions, or experience.

    example - yeah, last year when i was taking that class, that guy was tough on us too (share opinion). we had lotsa homework (share experience). sorry he gave you a bad grade (show sympathy).

    few rules to remember... 1) dont get too personal and ask about people family history. 2) share your opinions, not force it down people's throats. 3) be yourself but do it bit by bit.... better be safe than sorry. not many people can accept you at one go.

    see, not so difficult right? good luck !
