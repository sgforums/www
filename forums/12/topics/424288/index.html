    This is midlife crisis if you are in the thirties/forties.

    If you are in the twenties, start doing something before it really becomes a midlife crisis.

    For starters, you can plan about what to do and where to go next. 

    It need not always be about career or love life. Some people plan to travel to all countires of Europe, others plan to climb all major mountains in the world. Such aims will steer you forward, and give you something to look forward to. While you are working, you will be planning almost everyday after work what to bring along, what hotels to book, what you will need...etc.

    Do not plan something just for the sake of planning though. For those who I mentioned above, they will feel a sense of satisfaction after visiting another country or successfully scaling a mountain. If you are just doing something for the sake of completing it, you may end up feeling even more hollow afterwards.

    I believe there are forummers here who even planned to travel to certain countries in the world and try out some of the best delicacies which they have been looking forward to.
