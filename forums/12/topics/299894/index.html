    I am 30+ le, happily married for about 10yrs. However, my love life wasn't so smooth sailing before I met my hubby.

    My ex is a guy whom is 7yrs my senior. I was 20 when I met him. He is rich, decent and very kind guy who goes to church n volunteer alot. I was attracted to all his good points.

    We worked in the same company at that time. I was 1 of the youngest n prettiest girl there. I had many suitors but I wasn't interested at all. I juz became frens with my ex cos he stayed near my home n often offer to fetch n send me.

    We chatted everything n share our love life. I guess I felt v secure to hv some1 so muz older to lean on. I didn't realise that my ex n I started to click off after that.

    He had juz broken off wif his gf of 6yrs n I was his companion. He liked how innocent n sweet I was. I trusted him fully as we were so open abt our relationships.

    I thought that there was no reason that he shd cheat on me cos he is so old le. I respected him n even obey his instructions wat to wear. I was so naive n silly.

    We only went out after work til midnite.. I will hv to wait for his calls on wkends for a date. I cld not afford a hp at that time n wil always stay at home to wait for him. He usually picks me up v late evening.

    He did not show any sign of cheating. However, he misplaced his hp in my bag once n I got his gf phone number easily. There r many nos but I saw this particular 1 with a nickname which stored office, hp n pager n I guess something fishy.

    The next day, I page that number n a girl returned my call. As my ex had told me all abt his ex, I juz pretend that I am buying insurance frm her(she is an insurance agent). I met her n was surprised to see a contrast between the 2 of us. She is the next door girl while I am young n vibrant.

    That night, I called the girl again. I mentioned my ex name n she started crying pitifully. She told me that it was not the 1st time. I was hurt n angry. He had met her daily but briefly. He always send her back after dinner claiming he was v tired n met me after that. He chosed to spend most festival with me n leave her alone. He always hv excuses..I juz wondered y this gal can accept all these craps lor.. On his birthday, we booked a chalet. He did sneak out during lunch time claiming he had some errands to run n return to me.

    I confronted my ex then. I was sad but I was a v strong headed girl then. My ex denied all but he did not stop me from breaking up. I pitied the girl for spending 6yrs with this jerk.

    I did not eat n cried for 3 days. It was difficult for me. I decided to resign frm my job but my boss encouraged me to stay. My ex also started telling every1 that he was v sorry but he really did not cheat. I guess I was too stubborn to accept any explaination then. I moved on with my life n met my hubby. My hubby is also a nice guy n we got married v soon after that.

    If u were to ask me, if I married on impluse, I guess I was at that time. However, I really hv to thank god that I had made a wise decision. Sometimes, I do ponder if I shd listen n accept my ex explaination but then... I met him 5yrs after I got married, he still insisted that it was not wat I thought. The only acceptable explanation I can find for him is : he was torn between the 2 of us. He loved us both???

    I am sharing this story not to encourage pple to give a chance or break up. I juz felt that u hv to learn to thrash things out b4 jumping to any decisions. I love my hubby n we hv a wonderful family. I dunno if things wil b different if I chosed to give my ex a chance at that time. My ex is also a v proud person so he did not bother muz explainations 2.

    Nevertheless, I was really hurt badly n I always worried abt the trust in my hubby. I trusted my hubby alot 2 but I juz hoped I can put my past behind n start learning to build trust in men again. Lastly, I wish everyone the best in love
