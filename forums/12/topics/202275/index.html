    Picture this:
    A lot of gunpowder in a tight compartment with a single opening at the bottom.
    A stick is exposed to be used as a fuse.
    Ensure all parts of the projectile body is tight.
    Light the "fuse" and run the fuck away. (Please make sure you give yourself at least 5 seconds or maybe 10 to make a mad dash)
