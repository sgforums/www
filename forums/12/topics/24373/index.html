    There are a lot of ways of expressing your feelings to her. If you are the shy type, drop her hints along the way. For eg, be more attentive and caring to her needs, help her do things or buy things, ask her out more often, be there if she needs someone, or to the extent of "defend" her when she is harmlessly bullied by friends. Don't worry about your friends teasing you, coz basically that's your idea of letting her know.

    Listening, IMHO, is a skill that most guys lack nowadays. Pay attention to what she said, what she wants. She may like something or whatever food. Do all the things that please her. It will show that you actually care for her.

    When to reveal your true feelings to her is up to you, but do it at the right time. Observe her reactions and body language when you are with her. They can be useful indications whether she has accepted or irritated by your wooing. From there, then make the decision.

    Lastly, if you succeed, don't stop. Continue to be a gentleman and treat her nice. A good relationship doesn't stop at the "honeymoon" period. Remember that. Wish you luck.
