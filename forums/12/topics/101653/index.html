    Did you enter this relationship because you know what you want from it? Or did you jump into it just because she likes you, and you like her?

    Too often, many people jump into relationships without giving second thoughts. Why? That's because they're only looking for people who will love them, instead of people who will meet each other's needs. Such relationships will more often than not cool off very quickly.

    Ask yourself these questions before you go into a relationship:

    - What do I want out from a relationship?

    - What do I want from my partner?

    - WHY do I need to get into a relationship?

    This is important for both genders because it allows both parties to understand what they truly want/need from a relationship, which is essential for a long lasting relationship.

    I strongly suggest you and your ex-girlfriend to ask yourselves these questions. Perhaps you'll get a clearer answer.
