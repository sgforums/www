    I would suggest going to your friendly neighbourhood GP regarding this.

    If he feels that you need some more specialised help, he may refer you to a psychiatrist to determine whether it's really social anxiety disorder or something less serious.

    In the meantime, stick with breathing techniques. Go through your anxieties and ask yourself whether you really have a cause to fret over them. Try talking about your worries with someone you trust, or failing which, write them down and keep the list somewhere safe.

    Just remember - don't sweat the small stuff. And it's all small stuff.

    Like the first time do a presentation infront of the whole class.
    fist time to sing a song in KTV with some new frens.
    before exam/test of any kind.
    Meeting the girl you like for the first time.

    You mentioned that you are confident that you can do it, you tell yourself you can do it before the IPPT test. Infact telling yourself "you can do it" too much is not a good thing, this will only increase the pressure on yourself, the "you can do it" will become "YOU MUST DO IT!". When it's "you must do it!", you are under pressure already. So next time before you take a test, prepare well like you used to be, but do not think that you must pass it, keep in mind that it's also ok if u dont do well like you expected, u will be less stressed.

    IMHO i guess you are just the type of ppl that expect everything to be very ideal, to be exactly like what you expected. But in real life, we cant expect everything to turn out to be like how we wanted. You study hard, put in real effort, so you are confident that you can get at least 80/100 for an exam paper, that's makes you very sad when you get 75. But for those ppl who put in same effort, but he didnt expect a 80/100score, he's happy with 75 cos it's still A1.

    You feel nervous when you are outside, think that ppl are watching you, that's also the same as wat i said as above. You want to present yourself well in public, you want to keep the best image of yourself in everyone's mind, to your frens, even to strangers.
