    Its hard to fathom what 'act natural' really means, so let me try a bit.

    1: Hold the door for her, (yea, hold it, don't slam it infront of her, not a pretty sight)

    2: If you like, you could pay for her stuff, but make sure its a "First date only" kind of thing. Paying subsequently could bankrupt you(and also make you fall out of love pretty quick heh)

    3: Memorise a few jokes to make her laugh if needed. (Don't overdo it, she might mistake you for a clown)

    4: Remember her hobbies? Take or do or bring her there if you think she's feeling uncomfortable in her current situation, or refer to #3

    5: Look at her eyes when she talk, not anywhere else... (and listen. Remember to listen)

    6: Dress appropriately, don't wear long sleeved formal and all when she's dressed casually. (Take some time to let her know where you're going in advance as well)

    7: Oh yea, this is a big one. Unless she sincerely asks about your history, Don't.. i mean DON"T start telling her about your grandma, your grandpa, your achivements without asking. Girls don't really like to hear guys brag. Ask about her if you like, and perhaps she will ask about you. (But look at her expression, if she's bored when you're telling your NS stories... better do something about it dude).

    That's pretty much advice i can give you, so take care~
