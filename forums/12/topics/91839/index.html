    By reviewing your own situations, can a patch do any wonders for the problems/issue of your relationship, if that were to happen? You are just fighting to resist getting used to life without her and of course, with unsettled feelings, your craving to get back to the relationship is strong.

    We all understand and KNOW that irregardless of how sh!t a relationship will become in the future, we all do enjoyed a sweet past somehow or another in the past. But should we use such benchmark as a reason WHY a relationship should get back? All I see is a child denied of his toy and frantically trying all his ways to get them back - your relationship lack substantial reasons why it should return.

    You are unreasonable and she as well - which ultimately causes your break-up. So I believe you will be telling me that you will change and all that? In my experience (you could even look through all past aunt agony topic), such statement rarely secure the kind of vision you had for your relationship, now. Change is a short-term solution to relationship issue - to substain improvement, you have to evolve (and yes, there is a whole lot of difference between these two words). Evolving is a long term process - people don't get enlightened overnight. Ask anyone around and when they share with you their thoughts - you know where they are coming from. THAT is evolvement from their personal experience - change is talk and candleflame.

    I can say you are incredibly smart in a way - subconsciously, the method you use to try to re-woo her, works well against her. A Scorpio - intense, emotional and packed. And if you can probably win her, can you substain till the next rough tide that hits onto your ship? Do you even know what went wrong in the relationship, that leaves it with no choice but to break-up? It is MERELY personality conflicts? Or is there more?

    P.S: Take some time to cool off yourself instead of trying to cling onto the last desperate part of the ship. Continue your method to woo her back, I can only predict your next setback on the 3rd and 6th month of your relationship, again.
