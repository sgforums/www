    lack of sleep is only one of the contributing factors.

    i used to experience panic attacks too. made me miss a prelim paper. :X actually now I still don't really know what's the correct way to tackle it, except to be alot more prepared for tests and exams (instead of doing so only at the last minute). Sufficient sleep is important, and must be good quality sleep. Eat well also. And hmm... during panic attacks, have to try to calm yourself down by e.g. take a deep breath and hold it there, count to four (or ten), breathe out completely.

    some people say drinking something warm helps to soothe the nerves, but for me I find that drinking something icy cold and splashing my face with ice water or taking a cold bath helps.

    and the rest umm... the usual stress-relief techniques loh.

    during the onset of stress, TALK TO SOMEBODY!! the more you keep it to yourself, the worse your panic attack will get. Talking to somebody about the stress is a form of release.

    ahh eating well and sleeping well are the two basic things that you need to do. It's alright to indulge yourself in comfort food... IF they make you feel better. Otherwise, eating more fruits that have bite (i.e. crunchy!) can also help to alleviate stress and improve the way you feel internally.
