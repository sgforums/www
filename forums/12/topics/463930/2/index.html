    Originally posted by Zzzfrendzzz:

    then u explain it now?


    If u change CCA when year start, you attendance is refreshed in that CCA. Meaning you wont lose any CCA points due to the lack of attendance. However, assuming you took NPCC till June, then change to NCC, there's a chance you will get penalised for attendance %. Plus since you can only have 1 MAIN CCA, it means that if you changed in the beginning of the year, it does not affect any representation points and participation points you've earned so far. Assuming you changed your CCA only in June, meaning any representation points you get from JAN to JUNE would not be calculated but only participation points because after that when u change CCA in June, the main CCA would be the new CCA you've changed to.

    The time when u change your CCA affect how your CCA points are calculated. Please check with your PE HOD if you intend to change your CCA.

    Seldom do ppl change from 1 UG to the other though. But do note that you need not necessaryily restart totally (like being treated/restart like a sec 1) if you change from 1 UG to the other.
