    Cataract starts forming usually when you are 60 years old and above. You won't be cured of cataract even if you consume Vit A. You can only get rid of it by operation when the cataract has matured.

    Regarding your eye problems at night, maybe you would like an optician or an optometrist to check your eyesight again. It maybe because that your prescription for your glasses or contact lens is not up-to-date. The reason being that, in the day, our pupils constrict when it is bright. Thus, even if you have any error in your prescription, you won't feel any difference as your pupils are small due to the bright sunlight and thus, your resolution is good. However, when night comes, your pupils are large, thus, if there is any unwanted prescription not corrected, you may feel that your vision is slightly blurry, thus, resulting in a later response.

    However, you may consider a professional advice from your eye doctor.

    Cheers.
