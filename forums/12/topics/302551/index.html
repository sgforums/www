    Social interaction can be trained. Set time aside to be in a public situation.

    Set a goal that you will speak to 3 people and find out:

    1. What is their name.
    2. What is their favorite food.
    3. What is the name of one of their friends.

    The rule is you cannot ask them outright. Instead you have to find ways to get them to volunteer the information.

    This game has a time limit of 10 minutes. At the end of 10 minutes thank them for their time and leave.

    Doing this periodically will help you develop your skill in social interaction.
