    Put on the charm, the brightest smile and confidence - simply walk over, introduce yourself and let your intention be known. First step is nothing too hard, and if you do it right - most of a time it's a yes

    Get the number, call her - the local culture seems to prefer to sms in this situation instead, after few days... Keep the chat brief and ask if she would like to come out for dinner/tea...

    If she doesn't take bait, keep in touch with her. Wait till she is a little more comfortable with you in real life (you mentioned tuition) and then invite her to go out again.

    I normally prefer dinner dates for the first time, it's much quieter than movies or any crap of the same nature. Spend the time during dinner, to understand her better - keep it cool and funny, make sure she's comfortable
