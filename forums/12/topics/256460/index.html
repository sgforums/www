  • AndrewPKYap

    When you are young, it seems as if,
    That there is only one
    You rant at those that have a few
    One for the day, one for the night
    One for weekdays, one for parties
    and one for fighting with

    There you are seeking searching for THE ONE
    and there they are partying away with all comers
    as if anyone will do
    as if anyone they pick, they will be successful with

    If you are young and it seems to you
    That there is only one
    Let an old uncle give you some hints
    and clear the confusion in your head

    You see that people all around you
    you see your parents too
    that they have one and only one
    and what do you conclude?
    That there is only one for you.

    you cannot stand that one and you cannot have this one
    you are so sure that the one in outer space
    is the one just right for you

    the truth is that
    you can live a happy life
    and celebrate a golden jubilee
    with the one you cannot stand and the one you cannot have and the one from outer space

    if only,
    if only what you say

    if only you can be, become,
    become what, you say

    THE ONE

    the one for
    the one you cannot stand and the one you cannot have and the one from outer space

    It is all about YOU.
