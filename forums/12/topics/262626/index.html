    You birthday planners really need to learn alot more from those planners.

    1st: plan out the day when you are to celebrate. Make sure both the birthday person and you are available, at least. You can invite friends or relatives too.

    2nd: with your current budget, list out all possible activities which can be done. Chalet, ktv, posh dinner, concert, short overseas trip, etc, etc.

    3rd: depending on the repetitive sending of same gifts. GO OUT THERE into shops and malls and take a look. Find something of interest and value to purchase and to give as a birthday present. Be it a card, soft toy, ring, macdonalds, etc, etc. Make sure you explain why is that gift given instead of any other possible gifts.

    4th: add ingenuity into the birthday gig. Like learning guitar lessons and play birthday song for her. Get friends to play the music while you sing. Perform a short skit on how you two met and came to date each other.

    5th: if you are out of time. Find a place of tranquility and bring her there. Get a simple birthday cake and nice food. Preferably the food is prepared by YOURSELF. Find a nice spot, like beach, park, even void deck is no problem as the main focus is on the birthday.

    6th: Prepare a speech. Yes, a speech to show your love for her. To affirm her and summarise what she has done for you.

    7th: remember this gifts, venue, preparation, etc takes second place compared to you remembering her birthday and celebrating it with her. It does not have to be elaborate BUT it has to be memoriable.

    You are celebrating your loved one's birthday. At least try to put in some effort to prepare it. This shows your sincerity and meticulous planning.
