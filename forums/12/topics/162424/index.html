    You can refer the patients to the social worker for assistance. They will help the patients in this area. As a doctor, you should know that there's KDF which is subsidised by the government.

    The in house dialysis center is for patients admitted in for different illness that happened to have kidney failure. The number of machines there is defintely not enough.

    Contrary to what have been said, which ever method u chose, it is a torture.
    socially, emotionally, monetary.

    HD :
    - Compulsory dialysis 3 times a week.
    - Each session about 4 hrs. Must go to dialysis center.
    - can only make short trips out of Singapore
    - On the fistula arm, you are not to carry load of more than 10kg.
    - if fistula vein block or not throbbing, high fever, or anything must check into ER immediately

    PD :
    - Compulsary 3X a day
    - Can do it at home yourself. each session approx 3 hrs.
    - Stomach will looked bloated. Forget about slim waist and going for a swim.
    - you have to stockup cleaning fluid at home to perform the procedure.
    - If you want to go overseas, have to bring them along. together with some declaration.

    You took too lightly about the needling in HD. It is very painful. The first few sessions you are can take pain killers. After that you have to bear with it and accept it as part & parcel of your treatment.
