    I can empathise with your pain, for who amongst us have not suffered from crushing disappointments before? None. It's part and parcel of life, for we all have hopes and expectations, sometimes unrealistically so, thus the fall when our plans go apart...

    There is no way to remove the pain, no words, no music. No one else can remove it, for it will lies in our mind forever. But it is within our will power alone to put it aside, and concentrate on more worthwhile pursuits such as career or making new friends.

    The first step is to accept the fall, come to terms with it, and know that you have no immortal power to change events that you cannot control. But within your control is your will to continue on, if not for your sake and your future, at least for those who love and care about you, such as your family, relatives and friends.

    You have already faced disappointments, and know the pain, so spare them of such for they do have hopes for you, simple ones only, which is your well being.

    It's not the end of the world, at least not yet, for a bright journey lies ahead, if only you will open up your eyes. The majestic sun still rises, birds still sing, laughing children still play, lives still go on and had not stop. So too must you not stop, or dwell on the past...

    Good Luck.
