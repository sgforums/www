    Dear sand85,

    First things first. 5yrs is a long time. and any relationship can go still and stagnant after that amount of time.... you cant expect any relationship to remain full of sparks, excitment when you have been doing the same thing day in day out for the past 5,6,7 years, can you? Look at a happy old couple... do you think there's spark? do you think they love each other? the key is to find the spark. to make the effort to keep your love alive.

    it is up to the two of you to bring back the sparks, go do things you have never done, travel together, hike, camp, go fly kite, explore the jungle.. find new things to do... take up dance lessons together... these are some things you can do to try bring the sparks back.

    you have to try to get him to put in the effort too. it takes 2 hands to clap...

    that being said, If you are CERTAIN that you no longer love him (don't mistake this with being bored of your current lifestyle, because there's no more sparks), then you'll have to rethink the relationship. If you really have no more feelings with him you'll have to stop wasting his time and make a clean break.

    I know it is hard. I was in your exact position 2-3 yrs ago. I made the decision to break off. I kept telling myself, it is better this way then to wait and wait and on the day of marriage, realise I've made a mistake and leave him at the alter... so I chose to break his heart now rather than later. mind you it was difficult and heart-breaking seeing a person you once loved in so much pain. but I had to do it. if not I know it would have been worse.

    But before you make any rash decision, make sure of your feelings. do not regret your actions, there's no turning back!!!
