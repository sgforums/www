    Look, i think you have to acknowledge that you have a "facE" problem. You mention about having to face your CCA teacher, and that you felt JC1 newbies are giving u weird stares. Although I had not been retained before, but I know that when you are of a certain age, u got to be mature, especially if u go JC(not to say that those go Poly or ITE are childish). If they are going to be childish and talk behind ur back, or give u weird stares, then that's their problem.

    But ask urself this: Are u really bothered by the fact that they are staring at u, or because you yourself are looking at urself at a different way? If you yourself do not mind the fact that u have retained, then what others say or feel will not bother u. Doing JC1 again may not be a bad thing, in fact, it may be the catalyst for u to do well, and get to a good Uni. I myself just scrapped through the barrel of JC1, and it didn't get me any way, because in the end, my foundation was too weak and even though I could get into NTU, I couldn't get the course I want, and so I had to go to a private school. Look at things in a positive way, and u will have a whole new perspective on things.

    As for your CCA, I would suggest u join those type of CCA where membership is low, so that u can get a leadership position next year. No point joining a CCA which u like, but chances are that committee places will be hard to come by due to too many members. You, being the chairman of your CCA before, knows that having a leadership post will be more impressive than if u do not have. Sorry if I sound like the typical Singaporean-kiasu, but that's life, that's reality.

    I will not suggest that u pursue whatever is in your heart, but to pursue something that u know that it will help u in ur future endearvours, be it poly or JC.
