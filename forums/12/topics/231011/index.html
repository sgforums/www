    Humans are selfish by nature. It is natural for each individual to be worried about themselves first then about the people around them. Its a good thing and bad thing really. Depends on how you see and which line you crossed.

    Many of us put self before everything else is a normal response. How are we able to help others if we cannot take care of ourselves? What credibility can we provide or offer to others if we are unable to focus on our own issues first before trying to lend a helping hand to others?

    On the contrary, too much attention to ourselves may make us neglect the people around us. We may become self-centered as we try to protect ourselves (which is normal) from many uncertainties happening everywhere else.

    We have no control over other people and events happening around us. What we can control is over the choices we make and mature as we go.
