    I've been down that road before, and its just really painful. Liking a girl so much, yet knowing that you are never as important to her as she is to u. Hence after that I've taken a more relaxed approach to relationships. Have confidence in yourself, know what you are worth. If she doesn't like you for who you are, then what makes you think that she is going to learn to like you? Its a possibility, but its a gamble. If u don't feel really comfortable around her, or are constantly trying to impress her or find something to talk about/say just to keep the conversation going, then maybe she's not for you. If however you guys are fine, its just the matter of you wanting her more than she wants you, then turn inwards.

    Its a funny thing, but I've found that girls place more importance to personal traits (in guys) such as confidence, power, personality, and humour. Build up your confidence and your communication skills-go out there and let your hair down, relax and have fun, stop worrying so much about what pple think of you or what kind of image you project. Learn to laugh at yourself more, and let people laugh with you.

    Girls can sense these things. As long as there is a reason for her to respect and look up to you, she's going to come back to you..at that time, it will be for you to decide if u still love her the same way.
