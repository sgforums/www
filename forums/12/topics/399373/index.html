    Many problems in our daily lives revolves around relationships between couple, family members, friends, etc. Often, we get angry or disappointed when we don't get what we expect from them, be it love, attention, money, help etc.

    Have you thought if you're able to give unconditional love to people around you? And are people around you able to also give that? If everyone can do that, many of our life problems will be solved. :) "Love" simply means genuine care and concern for people, and doesn't just refer to romantic love. It could be family love, friendship type of love, love towards pets, etc.

  • Rainbow Jigsaw
    14 May 10, 21:43
    Originally posted by Mr Milo:

     

    it is best not to anyhow like that give....

    u will become emotionally drained

    therefore, see situations then give


    If you can truly give unconditionally, you won't even expect anything in return, so you won't be overly concerned about the outcome. If you're not that concerned about the outcome, then you won't feel emotionally drained. :)

    Reason that someone becomes emotionally drained is cos he/she is expecting the other party to return the 'favour', in terms of feelings or actions. when you expect something in return, it becomes conditional love.

    If you truly care about someone, you'll just do stuff to show your care and concern for that person. Whether that person reciprocates in actions or words shouldn't be of the greatest importance to you, as long as he/she feels better cos of your actions. Of cos, if you love that person romantically, if things are meant to be, then your actions will eventually touch that person's heart. If not, at least, you're slowly growing your caring side, which will help you in your own life anyway. It could also prepare you better for 'The Special One' meant for you in future.. :)
