    You may be low on serotonin in your body which, contribute to depression. Try taking up an exercise like yoga which, greatly benefits the mind, body and soul. Cultivate a healthy habit of eating right like eating dark leafy veg and fruits. A good diet helps with our system as well. Every time something negative or the feeling of being down invade you immediately do some deep breathing through your nostril and push the air down all the way to your diaphragm, held it there more about 10 counts then slowly release them out through your mouth.     Good luck
