    20 Apr 11, 00:20

    He got downgraded for the rest of his NS or temporary? A good psychiatrist will not necessary give him MC. Psychiatrist will have to diagnose what's the problem and give a treatment plan to it accordingly... He/She will then write a report and advise you on the path your brother will have to go.. he/she will then recommend to SAF on solution(s) for his NS. some solution may be download your brother to become a clerk instead? or storeman or something suitable for his situation.. All these will see what's the problem with your brother.


    There's no point keep giving MC to your brother. He can't be taking MC for the rest of his NS period. Anyway, whatever MC given apart from SAF camp medical centre, it will have to be verified by the camp Medical Officer. Which mean, even if the psychiatrist give your brother 7 days mc or even longer, your brother need to bring it to the medical centre to show it to the medical officer who will decide if it is 'approve' or not.

    You may want to consider trying Raffles Hospital where they have a counselling centre with some in-house psychiatrist... Mount Elizabeth and Gleneagles do have..

    One point to note.. private psychiatrist are very expensive where it can reach thousands of dollars.. do consider this.. Depression may only require counsellor, not to the extend of psychiatrist, so take note.
