    I was once a very very shy person.

    I don't go to hawker centres in my primary school, up to sec 4-5.

    Why?

    Because i'm afraid that the uncle/auntie will scold me.

    Yea, its silly, but that's the way i was.

    But now i'm different. I'm more out-spoken, more confident.

    Why?

    Because i know girls don't like shy guys, generally speaking.
    And shyness achieves nothing.

    How?

    I made steps to change. Its easy when you realise that the worse that can happen is to be rejected.

    Take steps, doing things you never do.

    Chat with a person who you're playing the same game with.
    Chat up a person in the lift.
    Ask the waiter/waitress for something, then ask how is their day.
    Offer the shelter of your umbrella to someone who needs it in the rain.
    Ask if the lady needs help with her groceries.
    Hold the door for the person behind you.

    eh, i seem to be doing a Singapore Kindness movement thing, but yea.
    Do the things above, and slowly, you're on the way to building up your confidence.~
