    All the talk about sacrifice, determination, hard-working, good EQ, etc are good in my opinion. But.....what is more important.....

    The people around u.

    You will be successful if the people around u are smart, helpful, supportive, etc. And u will be the opposite, if the people are stupid, petty, loser, etc.

    I have proven it myself. When I was around positive people, I was more positive and achieved and learnt a lot. Those people also helped me in many ways, directly or indirectly. When I was around negative people, I felt motivation-less, not-driven, scared of things. And they couldn't help me in many ways.

    Choose ur friends wisely.

    Shyness can be overcome through constant exposure and training.

    As for the definition of success, let me quote from a CNA interview with a famous Singaporean who was initially shy and whom many today regard as successful:

    Reporter: How would you define success, Mr. Sim?
    W.H. Sim: Oh... (smiles) "Success is getting what you want; Happiness is wanting what you get."

    It is not an original quotation, of course. Echoing Shutterbug's opinions, the definition of success (and happiness) all depends on how the individual defines it. If you have to rely on others to supply you with that definition, then most probably you'd never be happy with whatever success you may have.

    Know your strengths, work hard to the best of your abilities, soon you'd be able to define your own success and be happy to live with it. Good luck.
