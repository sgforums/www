    Hi Alex : )

    My guy also has Japanese blood, so when I read this post, I just HAD to finish reading it. And I also needed time to think about how to advise you as best as I can.

    A very normal occurrence when a relationship has gone past the initial "sparks" stage is when it becomes "normal" & "routine". It's like knowing that your partner is always there, & there's a tendency to take things for granted. What you have, is familiarity.

    Sure, starting a new relationship is great, & experiencing those 1st sparks of love always gives you a natural high, but to me, Nothing beats having a partner who knows your habits, accepts them even if they're annoying, & still loves you anyway. I don't wish to go through the problem of hoping that my annoying habits don't result in a quarrel, nitpicking (as with a certain ex of mine) on my faults, etc etc etc.

    Perhaps you're young, you've either just graduated or are about to graduate? There's still time for you to explore, experience, etc. Being stuck in a relationship where you're tired of the familiarity isn't going to help you, nor will it be fair to your gf. Perhaps when you've gotten a few years' work experience under your belt, & you've grown somewhat, you'll find somebody special to grow along with you. Then perhaps you'll understand why I don't wish to go through the process of looking for someone else & starting afresh. I'd rather re-create those sparks : )
