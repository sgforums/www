    Hi, from the above...I think it's a very clear cut. You see, as stated, both of you used to be close. The thing is.....you are starting to realise that she has excelled in areas which you think you are not comparable to her...e.g. looks, grades, interview techniques e.t.c. Well as time goes on..and these thoughts accumulates...you tend to start to become sensitive and 'angry' within yourself. You start to think why....why cant you be as good as her in those areas??

    Well...and when the above reaches its peak..you will start to be very sensitive...to whatever that person says......even though the words the person said doesnt really mean to 'hurt' you or 'show-off' to you...In the first place, this person MAY HAVE NEVER intend to hurt you from her action/words in anyway.

    Thus what you should do is: Have a heart-to-heart talk with her. Do not be embarassed to tell her what you think....and ask her how she managed to excel in those areas. If she is your good friend, she will share it with you. And dont forget...you have other areas which may be better than her. So, treat each other as sisters, and reach a win-win situation. This is just a common misunderstanding.
