    And if you manage to get MOM to justified it for you, by which your employer will have to paid you, he/she must pay your salary. But most likely you will loose your job too as your employer will have terminate your service.

All court charges will be fully borne by the loser(most likely your employer). Gst included. If he declare bankrupt, he will have to stop his business and all his asset under freeze condition till further notice.
