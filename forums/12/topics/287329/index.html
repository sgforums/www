    Why people quarrel ?

    We blame it on our nature but it is also within our control to prevent quarrel and to solve the problem in a peaceful manner IF we want to.

    These are some of the ways:

    It is important to regard others' point of viewpoint with utmost respect and try to understand where they are coming from, even if we do not agree with them. When we are sincere with others and don't simply dismiss their viewpoint because it differs from our own or seems unimportant in our eyes, relationships could have a better chance of flourishing into something positive and trusting, and everything just seems to fall into place. Respecting others is also about respecting yourself.

    If we are sympathetic, we will not be quick to blame others or be so stubborn in asserting our own opinion. Many potential arguements will not come to fruition. If each family member maintains his own rigid viewpoint without considering the other's feelings, an argument escalates that could have easily been avoided.

    Many arguments and fights are also started by unkind utterance that could have easily remained unspoken or been replaced with gentle words. Unkind words are sparked when we are not playing attention. Careless words can destroy relationships. If we use kind and gentle words, things will turn out to be better.

    Another way is to treat this marriage discussion like a business negotiation and not to get personally with it.
