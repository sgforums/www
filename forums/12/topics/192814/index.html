Are all of us growing apart?

  • Quirinus
    28 May 06, 11:53
    Everyone has experienced, or will experience at one point or another the famous Generation Gap between you and your parents. You and your parents will dress differently, talk differently and behave differently.

    But lately, I've been noticing growing gaps between people of our generation. Get on the MRT on any day and you'll notice this if you look around. There'll be youth in loose jeans and T-shirts (the casual look), guys in tight fit jeans and rocker style looks, girls wearing Jap fashions, guys in punk fashions, girls with Gothic black faces etc... Among friends, you have notice the fashionable chinese who listen to the latest Asian Pop and the conservative cheenas, you have the Mat rockers, and the tudung-wearing good girls, the punk indians and the quiet nice guys.

    Now, for me it's becoming harder to relate and to communicate. It's like people of our generation are becoming so different, so split. What the hell is happening?
