    Cheap stuff!

    It's very common for ransoms to be a fraction of the initial demand. But to do so, you must show difficulty in paying. Pay promptly and you will have the sum increased, or captivity prolonged. Don't show you are rich.

    Hope your local lawyer can be trusted. It's always locals who convey bribes eg in Msia you don't pay the driving tester directly, you pay the the driving instructor and magically you pass your test.

    About paying the right person, you will need to schedule payments such that both sides have motivation to continue. Eg pay x% after the first hearing, x% after the 2nd hearing, x% after release etc. Of course if you take the quick trial, you are saying you are not paying and game over. Taking the slow trial gives you time to offer and adapt.

    Good luck!
