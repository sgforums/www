    My advice is not give up without a proper fight; you came into this relationship and you did work hard to create a good relation, however you must alter your ways of doing. My suggestion is...

    I) Stop hinting, speak from your heart. Don't presume that by hinting she will get the message. Date her, find some suitable place and have a heart to heart communication with her.

    Four things you must Remember. Don't...

    I) Raise your voice to get message across - it never work.
    II) Speak round in bush or mention something not really that relevant between the both of you (e.g. You ex lar, etc)
    III) Avoid eye contact; always look into her eye.
    IV) Expect things will change, time is needed. Tell her you want to see her effort in trying to work for this relation (I said work for this relation, not as in change - alot of difference).

    II) After this talk with her. When she did try to do something for the relation, you must encourage and lead her on. She will feel better and would persist. Psychologically, this is called reinforcing.

    P.S: Always expect the worst, but tell yourself you gonna best your best.
