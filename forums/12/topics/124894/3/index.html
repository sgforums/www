    I concur with Devil1976 in that it's your friend's life to lead. To often, many get so involved in their friends' lives, that they expect their friends to take their view & follow it, & there's No other correct way nor alternative for them to choose.

    Whilst as friends, none of us would like our friends to suffer, & sure, it hurts to see them cry, we can't force them to lead OUR lives only, & to accept OUR views only, & to do wat WE wld do only.

    As friends, what we can do is listen & offer advice, but recognise at the same time, that it's THEIR lives to live, & THEIR lives to lead. We shouldn't be so narrow-minded to think that our way is the ONLY right way. There're other factors to consider, like the depth of her feelings, her character, & so on...

I've had friends in similar kinda positions, in which I think that what they're doing is only hurting themselves further, & I do let them know what I think.     And that's how I've kept my friends.
