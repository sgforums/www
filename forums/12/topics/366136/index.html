    Have you seen my Heart?

    I lost it on the road called Life...

    In between the sidewalks called Love & Trust...

     

    Has your life ever seem so bleak?

    Where you no longer feel pain?

    No longer feel fear?

    No longer feel that simple joy of even eating your favourite food?

     

    Would Life ever be found again?

    Would Pain be felt the same...

    Will one who lost his heart be given another chance to seek Love another time?

     

    Will one walk that road again...

    Feelings gone so long..

    One that no longer feel.

    Maybe one is already that..

    But yet that one never yield..

    For life was the only thing he has left..

    When everything else was gone.

    Not feeling the pain of suffering.

    Not feeling a single thing..

    Not wallowing in circumstances...

     

    Can you teach one to make his own heart again?
