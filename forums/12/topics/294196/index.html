    Hmm.

    Obviously work's going to be part of the game plan, but as for the rest, you're better off working out what you want to do before starting to do anything (don't take forever to work it out, though).

    Things are never going to be clear when all you've got in hand is a blank sheet like you have now. Start clarifying things with a few basic questions like:

    1) What are you interested in doing?
    2) From your experience, what have you found yourself to be good at doing?
    3) What kinds of jobs interest you?

    It's never easy just plugging on when you don't know what you're working towards, but at the same time hands-on experience will provide you with some knowledge to guide your decisions. Don't expect to have THE answer at any given time because your direction's most likely going to change with your circumstances. Put some thought into what you want to do (balanced against what you know you can do), and refine your ideas as you go along.

    As for option 2, the only person who's going to get any benefit out of going to see Adam Khoo is Adam Khoo.
