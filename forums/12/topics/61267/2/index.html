    Time does heal all wounds but the scars remain.
    The lover's spurn is like a knife searing and twisting in the heart.

    It happened to me. I managed to get over it. Some friendly advise (hope it helps) :-

    1. Pamper yourself. Boost your self-confidence by going for a new hair cut, working out in the gym ,etc

    2. Distract yourself by learning a new skill or picking up a new hobby, e.g. keeping a pet, photography, etc. This will help fill up the uncomfortable moments when you are alone and your thoughts tend to drify back to the pain

    3. Go out with your close close buddies. Talk about it if it makes you feels better. But don't swell on it. Remember that you have to move on and there are alot of fishes out there. Don't indulge in self-pity, it doesn't work and prolongs the pain.

    4. At a later stage, prepare yourself for the next r/s. Try to understand the opposite sex by reading ot making new friends.

    Last but not least, have to willpower to go on. Go read the lyrics of the songs, "I will survive"

    Take it easy and remember, move on.
