    DarkDestiny, you don't have to do anything.
    Just stay calm and find your own inner peace.

    Just be tolerant and let it pass.

    Your parents are probably concerned about your future.
    Perhaps they are worried about how a divorce may affect you.

    Hopefully you can see that different people have different personalities and marriage is sometimes about compromise.
    I'm sure you love your parents, and you want the best for them. So allow them to make their own decisions.

    Stay impartial and be tolerant; understand that your parents are going through a period of change. And even though it may not be what you want, it's really their choice.

    One day, you will still meet the right person for you, and there you will have your own happiness. And I'm sure you'll be much more aware of the importance of choosing a compatible partner.
