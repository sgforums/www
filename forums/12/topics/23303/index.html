    Maintaining a relationship is never easy. It is an learning experience. You have taken the first step to helping the relationship by reflecting upon it. There is no quick solution, as each relationship is unique.

    Feeling lost is definitely an uncomfortable feeling but it is also an indication that something do not work, not right. Knowing what causes the feeling may help you derive an solution to your problem. It takes 2 to start a relationship, it also takes 2 to solve their problems together.

    Where expectations are concerned, sometimes we get frustrated and confused because what happens in real life are so drastically different from our ideals, what we thought it to be. It may be a good idea to share with each other what are each of your expectations.

    Do not stay on the relationship for the sake of staying. Stay in the relationship because both parties would want to move the relationship forward.... pace it at a speed both are comfortable.
