  • LazerLordz

    Who am I?Can anyone enlighten me?

    If I were to leave this place tommorrow?Will you remember me?How would you see me?Am I a person who has touched your heart and made your life a better one?What do I deserve, the good or bad?Would you miss me when I'm gone?What would you say at my grave, if I could hear you?Will my spirit cry in silence after I hear you say the things you would say?Is there anything you want to say, anything at all?Search your heart and soul...

    Can I give you what you desire?I can.Will you take it?I may never know.Take the plunge today, be mine and I shall fly us away to a place where you will never feel regret or incompleteness.

    Am I mad in your eyes?Or am I just a very human soul?What would you say the day I disappear from your very eyes and life, never to be heard again over the phone.And my smile and rampant slackness never to be seen again?What's the price to be paid for happiness that is given to you wholeheartedly by someone who loves you with all his life, without even a single ounce of doubt?

    Heal me, teach me and most of all, do love me.
