    Though I believe that an online r/s can only be real once the couple take the initiative to bring it into the "real" life. It will never survive if both of you just rely on talking through the phone or on instant messenger. Besides that, you will need to meet her physically to judge if the chemistry is present. Not to say that you do not know that now, but meeting in reality will enable both of you to judge more accurately how suited you are for each other.

    So, I guess even as your family forbids you to meet her, you have to find a way to go to her. And how much you are willing to do is up to you. I am not sure of how old are you, but is it possible for you to take a trip to where she is? Or ask her to come over to Singapore? Once your parents have met her and see that she is real, maybe they will be convinced to let you stay in this r/s.

    Being in a r/s is always hard. A long distance one makes it even harder a trial. Add to the facts that it is also an online r/s...you have got yourself a huge hurdle to cross. But have faith and take everything as a test, knowing that if both of you manage to go through this safely and come out of it still as close as ever, it will be a very strong r/s.

    Good luck
