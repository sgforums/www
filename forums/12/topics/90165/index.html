  • ahkico

    We last yr, very spoon fed by our teacher... She always gave us ans to our workbook...

    Tis yr, our teachers refused to give us ans to the workbook, tellin us tat the ans can be found in ur lect notes, etc... But the thing is not all, n we dunno is correct or not...

    We lik model ans, then jus lik refer to it as a guide to study lik tat... Then my tis friend, she very darin... Lect teachin half way, coz everybody is lik crowdin round the lecturer, so she quicky took the model ans n brought it to photocopy...

    She lent it to me to bring home to copy the ans... I asked if we can photocopied for among ourself only... Coz is lik 20 sheets... How to pass the ans around? And we need to hand in our bks on fri... We got the ans on tues... She refused lor... She said cannot...

    I know i very bad, n in the wrong... but really for convience sake... So i went to photocopied it, n pass it to another 4 of my close friends...

    Supposed to give it to M, J, V... The last one, R, he very careless de... So at first, he was not included inside... Coz he ways drop his notes on the floor, we scared he drop it, wait we kena exposed...

    Then todae, M suddenly returned me the notes... She told me to pass it to R, coz she tot R don have... But we got photocopied for him already...

    I asked her why... M told me, oh... Coz i feel guilty takin tis notes... Is lik very bad lor... And then i got the ans already... Tat time discuss wif other friends liao... So u keep it lor... I don want keep it...

    Then i was lik... Ok... I so guilty for goin against my friend lik tat... N is meant to be among the 5 of us only, n no one else lor... Then we passed the notes to her on wed... She jus accepted it mah... She didn refused it or wat... But i think is true she got discuss the ans b4 i passed her the notes...

    True, she guilty, i also gulity mah... She makes me feel as if i m doin lik i have no feelin lik tat... At least jus throw away the ans if don want it lor... Return to me as though the paper is posion lik tat....

    Then another person, A, she and M quite close de mah... Then whilst M was tellin me the re rite, she so got mention A... She said tat A says, even if got photocopied the ans for her, she also wouldn't take it...

    Somehow or rather, is lik, M got tell A lor... And A so close wif the gal tat took the ans...

    I dunno... But am jus so disappointed in them lor...

    Haha... Now i suddenly feel as if i m in a dream... One by one, my friends around me all seems to lik change over nite... All from lik nice to not nice lik tat... Is lik from a true friendship, it becomes lik hmm... Hyprocriates lik tat lor... All behavin fakely in front of each other...

    Is lik i wake up n found myself in the same class wif the same people, but all of them becomes lik alien lik tat...

    Haiz... I have been feelin so bad over tis... Photocopyin without my friend's permission... Coz if found out we got the ans, we be in deep trouble so lor... Then kena tis kinda of attitude from my friend, M... So pek chek lor...

    I feel tat the most unhappiest thing is coz she returns me the notes lor... Mayb she tot can pass it to R... She didn know R don have... But if really lik tis, then wat for tak it the first place, n return it to me the next dae? Then mention bout A so...

    Actually i can tell she told A lor... Coz M is lik don know how to cover up lies de... Tat time we surprisin our friend for her birthday mah, so got somethings we got to cover cover, give her a surprise lor... But M will have the tendancy to let somethin out de... haha... Mayb is her gd or can be considered her bad pt bah...
