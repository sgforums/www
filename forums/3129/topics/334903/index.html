    The better food stalls in NUS(Personal experience)

     

    Engin Canteen:

    Nasi Ayam Panggam(sp?), otherwise known as the indonesian food stall. Voted best in engineering canteen.

     

    western food stall: best western food in NUS canteens perhaps. The teriyaki chicken hot plate@$3 is a huge hit. Incredibly guilt-inducing treat and MSG-laden(i think).

     

    Minced Meat Noodles Stall, great for their ...minced meat noodles and the meatballs noodles!

     

    Cai Fan: Cheap and sure way to fill the stomach@ 2.20 for 2 veg 1 meat and 2.60 for 1 veg 2 meat. Generous servings of rice

     

    Arts Canteen:

    Yong Tau Foo- Voted best stall in arts canteen, and possibly best stall in whole school. 2.05 for 7 pieces and noodles, very great taste and very great chilli. quality and satisfaction guaranteed. Otherwise, serving size of noodles not very generous.

     

    japanese- their daily special sets are decent and somewhat filling, however at 3.50 dollars I believe. Huge variety and it seems that the katsu curry rice is very popular

     

    Claypot stall- huge variety so you will never get bored!
