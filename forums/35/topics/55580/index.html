Why!

    God also said "You lose some, you gain some". But what did God give me? Oh, maybe he gave me an intelligent brain for me to score lots of ADs which i already scored. However, if i am really granted an intelligent brain, then why did i have to study so hard to score ADs. I can simply relax and score my ADs and not slog to get ADs. In the end, i decided. What the use of scoring ADs when you have a "worst-than-shit" face. Without self-confident due to ugly looks, what can my ADs do?? Get a better job? I believed Singapore employers not only go for quality but also partly looks too.

    So in the end, i decided to let loose of my studies. "Wo Bu Gan Xin". What sins have i committed to have deserved such a face? God, can you tell me? Tell me to ease my sorrow and shame. I am willing to exchange my life span in return for a better look.

I dare not speak up, i dare not lift my head high be it in school or outside. I dare not attend friend's party only to fear that i might be laughed at for my looks. I dare not strike up a conversation with any girl. So sometimes, i really feel like taking a knife and cut my own face to end it all.

    God, if you are really out there, can you please explain why you have given me such an ugly face??
