    canteen 1 got nice yong tau fu and quite nice japanese food and chicken chop.

    canteen 2 got nice yong tau fu, mango juice and fresh fruits. used to have nice western food but changed vendor liao.

    canteen 3 got nothing nice

    canteen 4 got best jap food and western food. especially bento set, fish and chips and chicken chop.

    canteen 5 - heard there's only one chinese rice stall and drinks stall.. nv eat there before.

    Other than the canteens in NP, there's also nice sandwiches at the Atrium, Four Leaves sandwiches.. i love the seafood sandwiches.. and chocolate exotic cake. Inside the library there's an internet cafe.. love the hot dog bread + egg and bubble tea..
