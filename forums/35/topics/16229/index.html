    >FAREWELL PARTY FOR ALL MERAHANS
    >
    >Dear ex-Merahans (students and teachers)
    >
    >Au revoir BMSS
    >
    >Bukit Merah Secondary school will be bidding farewell to its present
    >home of
    >33 years at Lengkok Bahru and be relocated temporarily at Raeburn Park
    >(present Gan Eng Seng Sec premises) in December
    >2000.
    >
    >We will all be returning to a brand new building at the present site in
    >December 2002. But to many of us, the "old" BMSS holds a multitude of
    >golden
    >memories. Hence to celebrate what BM means to us, we will be holding a
    >Farewell Party for all Bukit Merah students and staff, both past and
    >present.The details of the Farewell Party are as follows:
    >
    > Date : Saturday 25 November 2000
    > Time : 6.00 pm to 10.00 pm
    > Venue : Bukit Merah Secondary School premises at 6 Lengkok Bahru
    >
    >Tentative Programme :
    >
    > Lowering of School Flag Ceremony 6.00 to 6.30 pm
    >
    > Buffet Dinner & Public Concert 6.30 to 10.00 pm
    >
    >We have also planned a concert to reminisce the good old days. We invite
    >all
    >Merahans to be part of this concert. You may put up a solo / group item,
    >
    >give a tribute speech, showcase your musical talent or simply share with
    >us
    >what BMSS means to you.
    >
    >In order to preserve the history of BMSS, we have plans for a special
    >room
    >in the new school building where all the fond memories of the present
    >school
    >will be on display. We are in the process of collecting memorabilia such
    >as
    >photographs, tropies, awards, certificates or any other artefacts which
    >you
    >think would reflect the school's history and benefit the future
    >generations
    >of Merahans.
    >
    >Please be informed that a nominal fee of $10.00 would be charged per
    >person.
    >You may make your payment by cheque (payable to Bukit Merah Secondary
    >School
    >Advisory Committee) or by cash to Ms Tay or Mdm Beevi in the general
    >office.
    >It would be greatly appreciated if you could make a group payment if you
    >are
    >paying by cheque.
    >
    >Please confirm your attendance by the 10 November 2000 to be part of
    >this
    >symbolic occasion that will be documented in the history of Bukit Merah
    >Secondary School.
    >
    >We look forward to your suggestions and appreciate your help in ensuring
    >the
    >success of this occasion.
    >
