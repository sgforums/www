    Yeah i've been to my own division ECE's orientation camp and its fun. But since you're in AT i can recommend you to join the NPSU Freshman Orientation Camp. You can sign up at their booth during the NP lappy sale happening from now till 13 March. Alternatively, you can sign up thru the online form here: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/viewform?pli=1&formkey=dElDN0doQzNVY2hqQmxSNlJ4TkxTUEE6MA#gid=0

     

    Orientation camps in Ngee Ann in general are overnight camps (3D2N or 4D3N), with alot of activites you have NEVER experience in your secondary school camps, much cooler studd like group identity and awesome people you'll get to meet during the camps. NPSU FOC camps are for the WHOLE freshies batch so you'll get to meet people from other schools.

    I'm in Aerospace Electronics so can't help you there.. but from what i understand, AT students study the design aspect of Aerospace. And this year's 2nd year AT students has only 6 girls out of the total 120 students..
