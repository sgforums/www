    Hope I post this at the correct place. Anyways, even if you are not a Ngee Ann Student, I still wish to "promote" this campaign.

    In this project ( I&E ), we are actually working with Lasik Surgery Centre , who are also partners with NLB to collect unused spectacles for donation to people in developing countries who cannot afford to purchase spectacles even though they really need them.

    More about the campaign can be found here : http://health.asiaone.com/Health/New...23-116799.html

    Therefore, I hope you guys can support us.

    Donation boxes are outside library and Ourspace.

    We also created a facebook group for the project : http://www.facebook.com/group.php?gid=97797731572

    Brief Details :

    When : 3rd to 31st July 2009
    Where : Outside Library or Ourspace@72.
    What : Spectacles and Sunglasses

    Anything, metal frame, plastic frames and even Sunglasses.

    Do tell your friends about it. Thanks

    To those not in Ngee Ann Poly, there are also other donation boxes islandwide. Examples are at NLB and all Singapore Medical Group Clinics.
