    Official dress codes at other institutions

    Nanyang Technological University
    No singlets, shorts, mini-skirts, clothes with obscene slogans, slippers, or sandals without heel straps.

    National University of Singapore
    Students are advised against wearing revealing shorts, singlets, slippers and clothes with vulgar words or pictures.

    Singapore Management University
    No dress code.

    Temasek Polytechnic
    Students should have tidy hairdos, appropriate clothes and footwear. They cannot wear headgear which prevents them from being identified.

    Nanyang Polytechnic
    No clothes with offensive messages, singlets, mini-shorts/skirts, slippers, or torn clothing. They are also not allowed to dye their hair.

    Singapore Institute of Management
    No dress code. However, students are encouraged to be decently dressed.

    East Asia Institute of Management
    No slippers, singlets, shorts, spaghetti straps, mini-skirts, or sports attire. Security guards check every student before they enter the campus. Students are barred from the campus after their first warning.
