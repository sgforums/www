Joyous Life Release this morning

  • thubtenpematenzin
    23 Dec 07, 14:36
    Joyous Life Release this morning was slightly warm but the attitude of all who joined the ceremony of life release was with clear mind and heart filled with joy.We went to the Pusatan Makanan market and first purchased the fish from the butcher to save the life ,more than twenty large fish lives were saved and then we moved onto one cute baby turtle ,and some twenty five birds ,many numerous crickets,and some small fish also some twenty five frogs hopped and swam into freedom into the water of the park lake.The animal shared the joy and happiness with us and the birds sang as the flew to freedom,the frogs and some fish sat on the surface of the water to hear the Namgyalma mantra OM DHRUM SOHA OM AMRITA AYUR DADE SOHA which can purify their karma and save them from lower rebirth .We performed a small Namgyalma fire puja for those three sangha members who had passed away .We in the next fish release will print and laminate some Namgyalma mantra OM DHRUM SOHA OM AMRITA AYUR DADE SOHA for placing in the water and I recommend everyone who is connected with Buddhism to do this in oceans and rivers and lakes .first you print the mantra and then laminate it then gently place into the water and all the beings karma in that water is liberated.
    Om mani padme hung
