    I think you have the wrong idea. The Frozen Throne is a RTS (real time strategy) game, similar to C&C, Age of the Empires etc. These games can be played on PCs with or without internet connection. World of Warcraft is a MMORPG (Massive Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game). You need internet connection and a Blizzard account. After purchasing the game, you need to buy playtime. Its about S$40+ for 60 days. After 60 days your account will be frozen till you top up again.
