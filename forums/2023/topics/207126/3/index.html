    Actually, wakarimashita is past tense yeah?
    Arigato gozaimashita is also past tense. Though it sounds weird cuz in English there is no past tense for thank you.

    Wakarimashita is understood
    Arigato Gozaimashita is sort of like thanked you. The present tense is used before the act and past tense is used after the act.

    Present tense:
    Please pass me the tissue thank you(arigato gozaimasu).

    Past tense:
    "I have passed you the tissue."

    "Oh ... thank you(Arigato gozaimashita)"

    But most people do not care about that anyway...
