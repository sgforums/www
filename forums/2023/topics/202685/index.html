    ---------------------- Brief Synopsis (provided by Point-blank) ----------------------

    Kenzou Tenma is an extremely talented brain surgeon at the start of a promising career in a DÃ¼sseldorf hospital. Tenma has it all: money, esteem, a beautiful fiancÃ©e...It doesn't take long however until he realizes he's being used by hospital politics and that the management isn't interested in saving lives, but maintaining a positive image of the facility.

    One day Tenma ignores a direct order from above. He refuses to operate on the mayor, but instead decides to stay true to medical ethics and save the patient brought in earlier - a boy shot in the head, who survived carnage in his own home. Tenma pays a high price for his humanitarian actions: His girl turns her back on him, he loses his position in the hospital hierarchy. His career is heading for the imminent downfall. To make matters worse, it starts becoming evident that the boy Tenma saved was much more than he appeared to be. A series of murders throughout Germany follows, all bearing the signature of Johan Liebert, the child Tenma helped escape death.

    To correct his past error, Tenma must get to the bottom of the mysterious homicides, and investigate the truth of the Monster who is behind all of this. The kind-hearted doctor will soon have to discover that it takes one monster to defeat another...

    --- Comments by Parka ---
    The genre is mystery/thriller. The mood and atmosphere set by the anime is amazing. I have never seen any other anime that sets the mood this well. The anime truly lets you feel the eeriness flowing out from the screen.

    Couple with tight editing, each episode leaves you wanting more, until the last episode where the doctor faces the monster, ONE ON ONE.

    Fans of conspiracy theory might like this because the plot is one that's big enough to entangle all the key charactors in it.

    This is one of the most under-rated anime, but one of the best around.
