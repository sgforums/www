    The first episode is not bad, quite promising although the animation artwork leaves much to be desired. A lot of people will compare this to Initial D, and some of the styles quite resemble Initial D as well. It's relatively realistic (unlike in the game whereby you can turn at 320km/hr) and so far the speeds in the anime have not exceeded 240km/hr yet, and the unrealistic drifting is not portrayed so far.

    Still, it's only the first episode, and for those who want the english subs, currently I've only found one fansub, [OMFG] and for those who want the raws can download it from [Q-R] (Quantum Raws).

    Just a note, the english subs from OMFG are pretty basic, about those you can get in the 'official' DVD stock subs, and is not very accurate and only has subbing of voices, and does not have elaborate descriptions and meanings (like in Shuffle!), or additional subs on road signs and text in the anime.
