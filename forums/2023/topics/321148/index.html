    77 STAR is offering a 3 days Holiday Workshop Programme where you can get to learn the basics of Voice Acting, Manga Drawing and simple Animation Production.

    Here's the program line up:

    Day 1:
    Voice Acting:
    Learn simple breathing methods and voice projection.
    Animation/Manga:
    Learn foundational drawing techniques, take a short tour of the production studio where our animators work!

    Day 2:
    Voice Acting:
    Delve more into the art of voice acting through drama practice.
    Animation/Manga:
    Learn how to design your own manga/anime characters.

    Day 3:
    Voice Acting:
    Engage in actual voice recording simulation!
    Animation/Manga:
    Apply all you have learnt and create your own 4-Koma manga strip & make your own flip book animation!

    Date: 18th - 20th of June
    Total Course Fees: 100 $

    * Materials will be included
