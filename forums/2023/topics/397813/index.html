    Thought you all who has iPhone/iPod touch might wanna try this out. This app, Manga Rock, is a manga reader on iPhone/iPod touch. This was developed by my close friends who are current students at SMU. 

    This free app allows u to read all the manga from onemanga.com online. With in-app purchase at $1.99, you can download unlimited amount of manga onto your phone wile reader, as well as add manga to your favorites list, recent (reading history), tell your friends about a manga you love via facebook/email etc.

    This app received a 4/5 star review from 148apps.com, this is what they say about it:

    Manga Rock is a great way to view and download manga from onemanga.com, and every otaku should at least download the free version - Bonnie Eisenman, 148apps.com
