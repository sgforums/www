Anime: Guin Saga

  • xfool

    Base on a fantasy epic novel series that began in 1979,this fantasy tells the story of the mysterious warrior Guin, who has a leopard mask magically affixed to his head.

    The weekly television series will premiere on the Japanese public broadcaster NHK's BS2 satellite channel on April 5. Atsushi Wakabayashi (character designer of The Twelve Kingdoms, Yu Yu Hakusho) is directing the anime at the Satelight studios, while Nobuo Uematsu (Final Fantasy games, Ah! My Goddess: The Movie, Blue Dragon) is composing the music.
