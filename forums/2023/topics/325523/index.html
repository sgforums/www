Misia is coming to Singapore!~!~!

  • Sheepz228
    27 Jul 08, 21:28

    Singer MISIA, who celebrated the 10th anniversary of her debut in February, has announced that she will embark on her first concert tour throughout Asia. The tour will start in Taipei on August 30 and end in Hong Kong in early October, stopping along the way in Singapore, Shanghai, and Seoul. It is estimated that a total of 70,000 fans will attend the five shows.

    MISIA performed for the first time overseas when she held a concert in Taipei last September. She had become popular there due to the success of Fuji TV's drama series "Yamato Nadeshiko," which used her "Everything" as its theme song.

    Misia will be in Singapore on 30 Jul'08 for a special press conference to announce her concert. Let's all gather at the airport to show her a warm Singapore welcome. She will be arriving on flight JAL 719 on 30 Jul'08 at 5:00pm at Terminal 1. Please msg me or email me @sheepz228@yahoo.com.sg if you will be there!

    The next day on 31 Jul'08, she will be going to Zouk for the press conference at 10:00am. This will be a restricted event but fans are invited to wait for her as she enters and exits the venue.

    There will be special pre-sales at HMV Heeren on 2 & 3 Aug'08 from 1pm to 8pm. During this limited period, all tickets will have 10% off. Exclusive Misia merchandise with purchase of S$128 and S$148 tickets.
