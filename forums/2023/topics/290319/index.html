    Personal Comment: I stumbled upon this anime when I was browsing wikipedia for 90's show. But this is not the 1st time I watched the show. If I remembered correctly, I was 6 when this show appeared. I was too young to understand the show but I really liked it alot for some reason.

    Anyway, the show is a real collaboration. Its was a comic from Argentina and was later adapted into an animated series by Canada and animated and co-produced by Japan.

    Into the show, the show has the standard super hero super villain theme. The villain wants to take over the city at the same time kill the heroine by sending out monsters. Kinda like Pretty Cure theme. I like to emphasis on the word "theme" coz the story and genre genre is nothing like Pretty Cure or other super hero anime. The main villain is ultimate evil but the villain in contact with the heroine is a joke. As per standard.

    As for the life of the heroine Cybersix, as she is hiding from the evil bad guy, so she disguises herself as a guy by the name of Adrian and work as a teacher at a local high school. That is by day. By night, she let down here hair and auto look like a girl and don tights and high heels...... I know it sounds weird but believe me, its a good show. She befriend a co-worker at work as Adrian but the co-worker fell in love the heroine.

    So, 1 super heroine (check), 1 alter ego (check), 1 group of super villains (check), a monster per episode (check), 1 complicated love life (check)
