    About Digital Comic Course

    This course provides the support from the usage of computers and "Manga Studio" software to the drawing of manga. Manga can be produced easily with the help of this software especially for students who cannot draw well, having difficulty with frame spilt or colour tone gradation.

    The course is conducted in three different stages:

    Level 1 - Students will be taught on the basics of drawing, basics of manga production, basics of comic studio, story planning, plot creation, character setting, name creation, manuscript paper selection.

    Level 2 - Students will be taught on character production, background production, draft production, frame spilt production, pen drawing, finishing and effect lines, speech bubbles and text insertion.

    Level 3 - Students will get to learn print setting, web comic production, digital comic production, sound option setting, full colour setting.
