    Outro scenes of anime which leaves you... erm... like "Wow"?

    Before Black Lagoon came along, the outro scene of Gundam 0083: Stardust Memories was one that I always remembered and fond of.

    All 13 episodes shows the same scene of the earth revolving against the deep dark space, followed by the emerging light from the sun but it was the last episode that kinda cemeted it. Soundtrack by MIO (Evergreen) coupled with the scene was a perfect combo to illustrate a certain rejuvenation despite a sense of loss towards the end of the last episode.

    Now, Black Lagoon took over.

    Both series 1 and 2 featured the same outro but I watched it everytime till the end. U will see the outro focussing on Revy's legs (the main female lead)... as she walks thru a beach in slow motion. While at it, u will see bullets... gun magazine... her twin handguns, handgun vest dropped to the sand. The song "Don't Look Behind" by Edison provided a very fitting mood to the dark settings. When u think it's all over by the time she took out her boots.... she spuns ard and points the gun right at ya. Sui!!
