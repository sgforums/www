    Growing up is hard enough as it is, but for poor Daisuke has a bit of an extra burden to deal with. Not only does he have to struggle with his new romantic feelings for Risa [not to mention her rejection, ouch], his eccentric family, a news-hound of a best friend and a myriad of other issues - but he has an alter-ego to worry about to boot.

    Every male of the Niwa family has a special gene that, once they turn 14 is activated by any feelings at all related to love. Any time they see, or even think about, that special someone they are transformed into the legendary phantom thief - Dark Mousy. The only way to get rid of Dark and return to living a normal life again, is to have their true love return their feelings and love them in return.
