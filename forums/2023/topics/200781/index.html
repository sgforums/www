    Title : Ichigo 100%
    Volume : 19 End
    Story & Art : Mizuki Kawashita

    Preview english version:
    Click here Ichigo 100%
    Chapter 001 of 58
    "Strawberry Headline!"

    Story: Ichigo's story centers around a Japanese student who wants to make movies when he grows up. One day, is going to look at the sun on the roof of his school when he sees the most beautiful girl he has ever seen. What is more interesting is that he also saw her panties which had strawberries (ichigo) on them. He instantly falls in love, but he doesn't even know who she is. What will happen to this luckless guy?

    Review This manga is a unique manga for the shounen magazine that it is in (Jump). It does not have alot of high action or daring adventures, but I think it truly belongs because it is about a boy following his dream. The story is really original until about volume7, where the mangaka seems to start a formula of his own. Manaka has a fight with one of the girls, and a new girl shows up. THis doesn't really help Manaka out because the girls that hate him always come back. This cycle seems to be the only one present, but it is a huge part of the story. The other main problem I have with the story is that
    Manaka is such an indecisive bastard. He cannot make up his mind and in the process he ends up hurting the girls.

    Plus: The manga has a lot of interesting characters that help push the story along. The characters are each unique, but they all like Manaka. Manaka himself is a walking freudian theory. He is deep emotionally, and yet at the same time remarkably shallow. He reminds me of Shinji from Evangelion without all the super emotional problems.
    _

    One of my favourite romance genre comic right after Kutsura's I's and VGA, and A.I. Love You by Ken Akamatsu, Suzuka by Seo Koji.
    This comic have lots of drawings on panty and bust. But the central theme is on romance and love, it does capture some reality of being in love and struggles of it.
    I feel that it's a little deformed on artwork wise, but still nice.
    Love the ending!!!! Very close to life.
