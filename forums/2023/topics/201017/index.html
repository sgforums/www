    No More GANTZ Until November

    The most recent chapter serialization of the manga revealed preview pages to a second phase of GANTZ by Hiroya Oku.

    Just like the anime, the GANTZ manga is going to get a second stage tentatively called Catastrophe.

    Chapter 237 ends the first phase of the manga in a cliffhanger, and it is presumed that the second phase of the manga will resume the story where it halted.

    Therefore, serialization for the second phase of the manga won't start running until November.
