    Genre: Comedy, School Life, Ecchi,

    No. of Episodes: 12 Episodes

    Plot Summary: Takahashi Eriko was a recent junior high graduate looking really forward to going to an all-girls high school with her two best friends, Suzuki Yuma and Sato Ayano. That is, until she finds out that being around a bunch of girls isn't as comfortable as she thought it would be, and any ideas she had about high schoolers being more mature than junior high schoolers (the reason she was excited to go to high school in the first place) is blown away in the wind. Still, though, that doesn't stop her and her friends from still having fun, along with three new friends that also are new to high school.

    Personal Rating:

    Personal Comment: The tittle itself already suggest the type of anime this is. Its an anime about High School Girls.... But looking in deeper, its a little bit more than that.

    The story's main characters are six friends (all girls) . It actually starts out as 2 groups of 3 friends, they had a fight and suddenly they became friends... Happy Start. And each of the girls has their own weird personality. One is an actor wannabe, another is a hentai fanatic, another is a beauty queen.... you get the picture...

    Through out the show, the girls encounters normal everyday school life problems. Things from school medical check up to finding boyfriends to having too long pubic hair. And every time, something stupid and funny happens....

    And also, don't forget the teachers. A school anime isn't complete without the teachers. Since this show is in a girls school, alot of attention is focused on the male teachers. And since this is a comedy, they won't be normal... And they don't get their student's respect either.

    And since its a girls school, there would be lotsa ecchi scenes here and there. Stuff like wearing skirt too short to school and helping your friend trim their pubic hair... Do girls really do that...?

    Overall, this is not a big impacting anime and is not a rival to most school based anime. But its something good if you're looking for a quick laugh...
