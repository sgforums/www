Penguin Revolution (manga)

  • seow

    Yukari Fujimaru is the only daughter of a quite unreliable father, and she dreams of a more reliable future, as a public officer. Although she isn't completely aware of it, she has a gift: she can see the talent of a person in the form of wings on their back, and by chance she sees wings on the back of one of her schoolmates, Ryoko, who is actually Ryo, one of the male models of the famous entertainment company PEACOCK, in disguise. When Yukari's father loses his work and flees, Ryo will introduce her to PEACOCK as his manager, and a totally unexpected career starts for Yukari.

    Author: Tsukuba sakura.

    High quality art coupled with good plot, has very humourous scenes.
