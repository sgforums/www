    If you are familiar with Makoto Shinkai's work ie Voices of the Distant Star and The Place Promised in our Early Days, most probably you have already watched this anime a few times.

    This thread serves to promote this little masterpiece to those of you who have never heard of the name above or have not realised that this anime came out early last year in March.

    Or even for those of you who are still waiting for someone and wondering when and if you should move on...

    Sypnosis:

    "The title 5 Centimeters Per Second comes from the speed at which cherry blossoms petals fall, petals being a metaphorical representation of humans, reminiscent of the slowness of life and how people often start together but slowly drift into their separate ways.."

    The theme songs are very touching and fit the anime greatly...and if you have decided to watch it, pay attention to the ending part. The music video they put together is one of the most meaningful, breath-taking video that I have ever seen.

    And it won't take you much time...this gem only lasts for 1 hour and consists of 3 short episodes which followed the main character's life from childhood to teenage years to adulthood.
