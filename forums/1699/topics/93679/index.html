    Dear Users For M.I Forum,

    The nature of this forum is to provide Current/ Ex Students and Teachers a channel to express their opinions on various issues regarding the school (e.g Facilities, Environment, Food, Students etc...). In order to prevent any unneccessary misunderstanding or conflicts among any personnel, the below guidelines must be observed:

    1) All religious speeches, and speeches that would cause feelings of enmity, hatred, ill-will or hostility between different racial or religious groups should at all times be avoided.

    2) Defaming speeches and insults to any Personnel/Organsation/Republic Of Singapore Government should at all times be avoided.

    3) All users of the forum must be responsible for their own actions (speeches and comment). All statements made must be backed with facts and not baseless allegations

    4) Topic that results in argument or flame war would be given 3 warnings, the topic would be removed after 24HRS upon the issuing of the last warning.

    NOTE: ANY BREACH ON THE ABOVE GUIDELINES WOULD RESULT IN THE REMOVAL OF THE TOPIC AND IN REPEATED CASES, TROUBLEMAKERS WOULD BE BANNED FROM THE FORUM.

    We request your cooperation and understanding on the above issue. Should you have any problems or queries regarding the above, feel free to approach any of the moderators via a Private Message. Thank you.

    Prestroz
    For The Moderators Of M.I Forum, SG Forums
