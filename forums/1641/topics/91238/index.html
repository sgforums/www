    sixth day of working

    much time on this

    her? She has to
    be
    completely washable, but not plastic, have over 200
    movable parts,
    all
    replaceable and able to run on diet coke and
    leftovers, have a lap
    that
    can hold four children at one time, have a kiss
    that can cure
    anything

    from a scraped knee to a broken heart -and she will
    do everything
    with
    only two hands."

    The angel was astounded at the requirements. "Only two
    hands!? No way!
    And

    that's just on the standard model? That's too much
    work for one day.
    Wait

    But I won't," the Lord protested. "I am so close to
    finishing this

    no

    you

    her

    You thought of
