    Hello All,
    Dr Jeff Briggs, a world-renowned professional bowling trainer and academic will be in Singapore to conduct a series of clinics to help both bowlers and coaches as well.

    The free preview will be held this Sunday 22 June (4-6pm) at Planet Bowl.There will be an hour on the lanes and an hour in the auditorium. I'm hereby inviting all interested parties to come down for this free talk and gain more insights into bowling.

    Planet Bowl is looking to arrange with Dr Jeff Briggs to conduct his series of clinics in Civil Service Club later in the year. I'm using this
    opportunity as well as a chance to provide value-added service to bowlers, not just from Planet Bowl but from all over Singapore as well. This will be my vision for Planet Bowl in the next 2 years -

    "You don't just come to Planet Bowl to bowl, there are other benefits and value why you should be bowling here as well. We're not solely concerned about doing business and making money but we'll be genuinely looking into the wellfare and performance of the bowlers. Bowlers (members and non-members) who come to Planet Bowl for their bowling games currently enjoy reasonable rates, nice ambience and good tech & customer service.......BUT I'll be looking to add more value than just that." (as outline in my vision to GM for Planet Bowl)

    So.... bringing in a top professsional like Dr Jeff Briggs is the first
    step in adding vlaue to bowlers here in Planet Bowl. If you're free this Sunday, please feel free to pop by Planet Bowl for a talk that I'm sure will benefit your bowling game!
