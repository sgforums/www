Singapore Farm Tour 2009

  • Zhihui li

    Get away from urban surroundings and join us in our farm tour to get closer to the nature. Find out why goats milk is the healthier choice for you and your family at Hay Dairies Goat Farm. We will then proceed on to Jurong frog farm to learn about how to differentiate a female from a male frog and other interesting facts about frogs. Our last stop will be at Bollywood Veggies. Enjoy the countryside scenery while learning about the different kinds of plants and its uses in this beautiful organic farm. Transport will be provided. The tour will include snacks and drinks.
