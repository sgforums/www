    THE HEALTH RETREAT

    (5days/4Nights) June 7 to 11 (Sun to Thur), 2009

    At Borneo Highlands Resort, Sarawak (2 Nights)

    Website : www.borneohighlands.com.my

    &

    Holiday Inn, Kuching (2 Nights)

     

    Imagine yourself in a place where time and nature conspire to create an atmosphere of unmatched serenity.

     

    Welcome to Borneo Highlands Resort, one of the most exciting and unique environmentally friendly resort. Cocooned in a lush jungle and snuggled within the hills, it is a retreat one of its kind, offering an exclusive membership club and truly unique residential development by creating a sanctuary for you to retreat to in this region.

     

    Located just an hour's drive (60km) southwest of Kuching city, the capital of Sarawak, the resort stands at the height of 1,000 metres above sea level on the Sarawak-Kalimantan border and is nestled amongst the oldest and second largest tropical rainforest in the world.

     

    A resort created with emphasis to blend with nature, the resort offers boundless opportunities to experience numerous outdoors activities all year round in stylish comfort.

     

    Take pleasure in the finest natural wonders and find luxury in the bare basics. At Borneo Highlands Resort, it all about getting “back to nature, back to basics”

     

     

     

    Borneo Package inclusive of :

    - Welcome drinks

    - 2-night accommodations at your choice of room type

    - 2 breakfast, 2 Lunch & 2 Dinners (All meals are organic vegetarian)

    - Meeting / Function room with 4 tea breaks and basic meeting equipments provided.

    - Complimentary use of our Jungle Spa.

    - A guided tour to visit Kalimantan Border, Flower Gardens, Organic Farm, Horticulture Nursery & natural lakes.

    - One return transfer from the Kuching International Airport to resort.

     

    Holiday Inn Package inclusive of:

    • City Tour

    • Continental Breakfast only

    • Sarawak Cultural Village Tour (Optional)

     

    Cost of Accommodation

    Total cost exclude airfare is only S$700.00 (Include 6 meals at Borneo Highlands and breakfast only at Holiday Inn) + 3 Health Lectures by Mark Chan

     

    How to pay the Fees

    Write a check to be made payable to Balestier Rd SDA Church, and post it to 120 Balestier Rd, Singapore 329680.

     

    Contact Person

    For registration or enquires, please call Mark Chan @ 97438362 or email at markchan@adventist.org.sg


    Booking of Air Tickets

    The flight journey is about 1½ hours. All participants are to book their own air tickets by going online through JetStar (www.jetstar.com/) or Tiger airways. The fares are subject to changes, so you are advised to book early to get a cheaper rate. The flight details are as follows:

    7 June 2009: Singapore to Kuching at 15:05pm

    11 June 2009: Kuching to Singapore at 17:15pm
