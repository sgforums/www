    LEGO is all about creation. So STOMPer letsgolego thinks it's appropriate that some creative minds decided to create new scenarios with the LEGO characters from the Star Wars series.

    Said the STOMPer:

    "Don't like how the Star Wars stories played out? Then create your own story!

    "A group of creative students from an advertising school in Brazil have used LEGO Star Wars to create some new scenes that were not in the movie.

    "They called this project 'Make your own thing', and it was directed by Diego Mourão and Gustavo Dorietto, and the copywriter Lucas Mohallem.

    "Some of the other photos are from a project called Troopies365, where this guy took a photo of Storm Troopers LEGO figures in different situations every single day.

    "I love how out of place these LEGO characters are in these new scenarios.

    "Just imagine! Darth Vader having a BBQ!

    "And the Storm Troopers running from the green dinosaur (from Toy Story?) in the car are so cute!"
