    ~Chewy Cheesey Pizza~

    Ingredients:

    Crust:
    2 cups cake flour
    1 1/4 cup whole wheat flour
    1/4 cup olive oil
    1 egg
    1 cup water

    Mix all ingredients together. Knead on a lightly floured surface. Spray a regular sized, 12 " pizza pan with nonstick spray. Next, spread the dough to the edges of the pan, forming a lip around the ends. Set aside.

    Sauce & Toppings:

    1 tomato
    1/2 cup tomato puree
    1 garlic clove
    1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
    1/2 tsp. orgegano
    1/2 tsp. basil
    2/3 cup cooked rice

    In a food processor, blend tomato, tomato puree and garlic. Spoon the mixture over the pizza crust. Sprinkle the cheese and spices evenly over sauce. Cut the pizza into slices with a pizza cutter or sharp knife.

    Directions:

    Bake in a 325 degree oven for 25 minutes. Take out and sprinkle rice evenly over pizza. Return to oven and bake 25 minutes more.
    Yield: one 12 inch pizza

    ~Bacon Bites~

    Ingredients:

    3 cups whole wheat flour
    1/2 cup milk
    1 egg
    1/4 cup olive oil
    1 tsp. garlic powder
    3 - 4 slices bacon, crumbled up
    1/2 cup cold water

    Directions:

    Mix ingredients together thoroughly. Roll out on a floured surface to 1/2 - 1/4" thickness. Bake for 35-40 minutes in a 325 degree oven.

    ~Meatball Mania~

    Ingredients:

    600g ground beef
    2 tbsp. grated cheese
    1 carrot, finely grated
    1/2 tsp. garlic powder
    1/2 cup bread crumbs (whole wheat is healthiest)
    1 egg, beaten
    1 tsp. tomato paste

    Directions:

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Combine all ingredients together; mix thoroughly. Roll into meatballs - whatever size is appropriate for your dog. Place on a cookie sheet sprayed with non-fat cooking spray. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until they are brown and firm. Cool and store in the fridge or freezer.

    ~Frozen Fruit Treats~

    Ingredients:

    Lots of Fruit
    Fruit Juice
    Low-fat Yogurt
    Honey
    Cinnamon

    Direction:

    Place any fruit you favor in a blender. Some great ones are honeydews, cantaloupe, watermelons, apples, peaches, or bananas. Add some unsweetened natural juice (your choice - apple is good). Add honey for sweetening, and 1/2 tsp. cinnamon. Puree until well blended and smooth. Freeze in little cups or ice cube trays and serve as "popsicles".

    ~Spaghetti Dinner~

    Ingredients:

    1 1/2 lbs ground meat (beef, venison)
    1 pkg. spaghetti noodles
    1 can tomato sauce (no salt!)
    1 small can mushrooms
    Spices to taste (basil, oregano, garlic, no onions, except for human portion!)
    2-3 cups mixed veggies (broccoli, carrots, cauliflower, capsicums)
    Olive oil
    4 washed potatoes

    Side Dish: Baked potatoes

    Direction:

    Boil water & 2 tsp. olive oil. Add noodles when boiling. Boil just a few tablespoons water in another pot, add veggies, boil about 12 minutes and remove. Brown meat, seasoning with basil, oregano, and a little garlic.

    Add tomato sauce, mushrooms. Remove 3/4 of meat mixture into three dog dishes to cool. Add more garlic & spices (onions if you wish, for humans only) to remaining mixture to taste, and let simmer.

    Rinse noodles under cool water, divide equally into four bowls. Add remaining sauce to human portion. Serve with cooked veggies.

    Boil potatoes for 15mins, after removing frm water, use a knife to make an opening on top of it and add some minced garlic before baked it for another 5-8mins.

    Feeds 3 large dogs & 2 human adults.
