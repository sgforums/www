    Project INVEST - Parenting Course Schedule 2010

     

     

    Project Invest is a fun and experiential 4-session course for parents with children below 12 years of age who are keen to learn parenting skills and knowledge.

     

    Course Objectives: To improve communication and strengthen the relationship between parent-child.

     

    Topics:

    • Understanding child development and developmental needs of children
    • Parenting styles
    • Effective communication
    • Positive discipline
    • Parent’s attitude towards children

     

     

    Date:

    20, 27 April 2010

    4, 11 May 2010

    Time:

    7.00pm to 9.30pm

    Venue:

    Singapore Children's Society

    Family Service Centre (Yishun)

    Blk 107 Yishun Ring Road

    #01-233 Singapore 760107

    Fees:

    $10/person.  $15/couple

    Instructors:

    Ms Koh Wah Khoon, Director

    Ms Jessica Vincent, Counsellor

     

    * Course will be conducted in simple English

     

    For enquiries, contact the Yishun Family Centre at 6753 7331 during office hours or emailmryunos@childrensociety.org.sg
