    how long r u backpacking? and where - cold or hot places?

    usually bring about 1 wk's worth. washing can be done at the hotel or hostel. simple washing though.

    usually wear one pair of comfy shoes (for lots of walking) and bring slippers. or if the weather is fine, can wear sandals too.

    try to use credit card if possible. if canot, bring cash, but put them in diff places - e.g. in ur bag, on ur waistpouch, etc. but carrying too much cash is not advisable. or u can bring travellers' cheque too.

    accomodation will depend on where u go - city or rural. youth hostels are quite cheap and can be found all around the world. jus look them up in guide books or student travel agencies. there will definitely be cheap hotels too - those 1-2 star types. and some of them can be rather good too. do ur research b4 going. best, get opinions of ppl who have been there. even if its rural areas, if its touristy, there may be accomodation there, e.g. bed and breakfast type where the residence use their houses as "hotels" or even motels. for big motel chains tt have branches all around the world their motels can be rather nice - almost like a hotel (motels have ratings too, but cant rem wat it is liao). but for such chains it will be more ex too.

    as for skiing, unless u haf done it b4 u need to learn. so one dae may not be enuff.

    better check on the weather too and decide if u need umbrellas or raincoats.
