  • iceball
    21 Jan 08, 13:36
    Just now I went to kopitiam to eat.

    As I walk around, I saw many food stalls have rised their prices except for the nasi lemark stall.

    $1.90 for a set meal consisting of rice, chicken wing, ikan billis and egg. More worth it than the $2.50 meepok, fried kway teow or chicken rice.

    Morale of the story:

    1. Eat more nasi lemark, save more money and you can retire early.

    2. Continue to raise prices and everybody will go and eat nasi lemark, the cheapest meal in the entire hawker centre.

    3. If you want to be a hawker, set up a nasi lemark stall so that the poor people can afford to eat too.
