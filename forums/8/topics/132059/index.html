  • Tuatau
    16 May 05, 13:54
    1. Open Windows Media Player
    2. Tools > Options > Performance
    3. Click "Advanced..." under Video Acceleration
    4. Uncheck "Use Overlays"
    5. Click OK
    6. Use your favourite [Print Screen] button

    It worked for me. Hope it works for you, too.
