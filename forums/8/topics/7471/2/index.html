    As what FireIce said, there's NO orphanages in Singapore. And yes, we do have centres for troubled youths or kids. Those are under Student Care Service and it has a few branches in Singapore and I do know of the ones in Clementi and Yishun. They do welcome volunteers any time and hopefully, those interested are willing to go through a probation period where you can learn more about the kids there and learn to help them in long term over there.

    Other than these Student Care Service centres, there are a few children homes in Singapore. These homes house children from broken family and namely kids suffering from child abuse or negligence usually in a single-parent family. To know more about such children home in Singapore, those interested can call Singapore Children's Society or the Salvation Army. Presently, I am helping out at Singapore Children's Society Convalescent Home off Admiralty Road West, for those of you who wish to help know the number or to know more about this home, you can email me at pixie_gal@playful.com and I will be more than willing to help those who are keen to volunteer. We need tutors and helpers presently.

    Grace Haven is another children home but under Salvation Army. I have been there once and it's near Yio Chu Kang. For those who want to know more, can call Salvation Army to enquire.

    Well, all these single societies come under one organisation, National Council of Social Services (NCSS). NCSS does have information on old folks home and other nursing homes too. For further enquiries, you people can call NCSS alrite? Hope that I have provided you people with enough details. Ciao!
