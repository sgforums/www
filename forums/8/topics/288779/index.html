  • fox_hound_33

    Looks like another one of those Insurance linked policies(ILP).

    Did it say anything like in the first year 5% of your premiums will go into investments, second year 15%, third year 50% and from the fourth 100% ?
    Did it say it will put your money into some funds?

    My guess is that the policy has two components, one is the insurance part and other the investment part. The investment part is the one that gives the variable returns when the policy terminates.The insurance part is the one that provides for all those cover (illness etc) and gives your premiums back.

    My advice is this.................DO NOT ever sign up for anything if you are not sure of what you are signing up for!!!
    Dig up more info, ask your friend how EXACTLY the policy works.
    And if it indeed is an ILP......i would suggest to stay away from it. You can put your money to better use by buying insurance and investments separately.

    There is a lot of info on the net...Google your way through.
