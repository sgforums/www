    Entry Requirements (taken from SP websai)

    i) English Language 1 - 7*
    ii) Mathematics 1 - 6
    iii) 2 other subjects excluding CCA 1 - 6

    In order to be eligible for admission, an applicant must have also sat for one of the following subjects:

    * Additional Combined Science
    * Art / Art & Design
    * Art & Design (AEP)
    * Biology
    * Chemistry
    * Combined Science
    * Design & Technology
    * Integrated Science
    * Physical Science
    * Physics
    * Science (Chemistry, Biology)
    * Science (Physics, Biology)
    * Science (Physics, Chemistry)
    * Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)

    * Applicants offering SPM / UEC qualification must attain a minimum grade 6 for their Bahasa Inggeris (1119) / English Language.

    2005 JAE Cut-Off Points

    The Diploma in Games Design & Development is a new course starting in 2006, therefore there is no prior information about the past year's cut-off points.
