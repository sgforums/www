    Those rambo style knives really depend on the model. The camping ones can be found at sngarms. There are many low quality ones being sold at various unnamed shops around singapore. I know of one at peinisular plaza (or whatever the shopping centre next to funan is called, I'm not good with names).

    Those bayonets, stillettos and fighting knives are very hard to find in singapore. I heard Johor has tho I dunno where exactly.

    It is legal to own a knife (ie kitchen knife, camping knife, utility knife). But if found in your possession in public (pocket, bag, hanging from your belt etc), or your neighbours or anyone else felt threatened by your knives (you need not even be holding it), you can go to jail for possession of dangerous weapons.

    You'll need license for swords as they are counted as martial arts weapons. Those sold in shops like Ceasers are replicas and are considered display ornaments. If you want to bring in such "display ornaments", the police will still classify it as a sword (I dunno why).
