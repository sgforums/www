    Well, there's a community and resources availabkle about Tintin at Tintinologist.org. It's the place for resource and view discussions among the Tintinologist.org community around the world. Look out for me as I'm a member there.

    Tintin was created by Herge and first published in Le Petit Vingetieme on Janurary 10, 1929. Tintin adventures were published in that till The Black Island as German troops closed down the newspaper. Herge got Tintin on Le Soir till 1945, where Herge was arrested 4 times and Le Soir closed down in the midst of The Seven Crystal Balls.

    Raymond LeBlanc, social rights activist came to the rescue and revive Tintin by helping Herge start up Tintin's own Magazine and on September 26, 1946, Tintin Magazine was published.

    More info on Tintin and Herge are available at Wikipedia and Tintinologist.org
