    went to this restaurant at United Square, somewhere near Novena Square(right across the street)...they serve international buffet @ $29.80++ for dinner...very nice spread!! first, i tried the lobster miso soup...wah lau eh...the pot of soup is full of lobster...one of my fren had the sharks fin soup...while another 2 tried the mini buddha jump over the wall...

    next, i helped myself to the "shell" section...got prawns lah, got crayfish, lobster, fresh oysters, scallops with garlic and mussles...all damn shiok...there are also vegetables, potato salad and egg salad...the Vienna special lobster fried rice also not bad...black pepper crab, pastry soup, teppanyaki, sushi, sashimi, qing tng, and lots more!! too bad the dessert section quite limited, with cheese cakes, black forrest cake, mango and honeydew puddings, kueys and ice cream...

    can go try once in a while, butdinner is from 6-10.30pm...lunch available @ $22.80++ from 11.30-3pm
