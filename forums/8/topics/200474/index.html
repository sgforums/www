    REGISTRATION PROCEDURES FOR TANGLIN MALL 2ND HAND FASHION & JUMBLE BAZAARS
    (Every 1st & 3rd Saturday of the month, 3 - 11pm, Covered Walkway)

    Items Allowed To Be Sold:-
    - Antiques
    - Collectibles (e.g. stamps, phonecards, MRT cards, bank notes, Coke collectibles, etc.)
    - Curios
    - 2nd Hand Books
    - 2nd Hand CDs/ Records
    - 2nd Hand Toys
    - 2nd Hand Fashionwear (e.g. clothes, shoes, bags & accessories)

    Items NOT Allowed To Be Sold:-
    - Imitation Goods
    - New Items
    - New Bookds
    - New CDs/ Records
    - New Toys
    - New Fashionwear & Accessories
    - Food & Drinks
    - Goods that are similar or conflict those sold by Tanglin Mall shops

    Requirements (Submission & Registration):-
    a) Applicants must be 16 yrs & above
    b) Applicants must be a Singaporean/ Singapore Permanent Resident
    c) Applicants must register PERSONALLY with their NRIC (for verification purposes)

    Payment:-
    a) $70 payment strictly via NETS only
    b) Fees are inclusive of GST
    c) Fees paid are non-refundable

    Register for 2 & 16 Sept Bazaar at Information Counter (L2)
    (a) Submission Dates: 14 & 15 Aug (Mon & Tue), 10am - 8pm
    (b) Balloting Date: 16 Aug (Wed), 10am
    (c) Registration & Payment Dates: 17 - 20 Aug (Thu - Sun), 10am - 8pm

    For any queries, please call Information Counter at Tel: 6736 4922
