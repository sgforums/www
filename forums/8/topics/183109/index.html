    Runway Cycling 2006!!!!!
    -------------------------------------------------

    1stly, I admit that I started this new thread because I wanna reach out to each n every forumite around and not just those "members" of the "Hardtail/ Full suspension" bicycle thread! (http://www.sgforums.com/?action=thread_display&thread_id=169516&page=16 )

    SAFRA will be organizing a Mass Ride at the Paya Lebar airbase on 28 of May 2006(Runway Cycling )

    Registration will start online on the 20th of MArch 2006

    http://www.safra.sg/safraonline/events/eve_Index.asp?search_month=5&CURDATE_year=2006&search_cat=&search_clubH=&search_view=1&search_year=2006

    Alternatively, u can personally sign up for the event at any SAFRA club in Singapore (u have to pay $5 for rental of bike if u do not have your own, registration is free )

    The early birds will receive goodie bags at the event!!!

    Worried that u cannot keep up with the Roadies (road cyclists ) at the event?
    Fret not! This is a LEISURE event!!! U are free to cycle at your own pace!

    Besides bicycles, Kickscooters, RollerBlades and wheelchairs(for the disabled ) are welcomed too! In fact, an elderly man JOGGED alongside the bikes at last year's Runway Cycling!!!
