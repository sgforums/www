    hmm what to do if someone has fits:

    http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Epilepsy#Responding_to_a_seizure

    In short:
    1. Move him/her away from sharp objects or move objects away from person
    2. Cushion fall is possible
    3. NEVER place anything in the person's mouth
    4. If the fits comes in waves, lasts longer than 5 minutes continuously call the ambulance.
    5. Take note actions of patient during fits, how long fits last to inform to medic.
