    Check kouden in your Japanese dictionary and there should be something like "condolence money", "incense money" or similar terms.

    http://www.epochtimes.com/b5/3/4/10/n298440.htm

    There probably isn't a standard international term as the term is derived from local customs in Asian countries. Not sure if the angmohs have such similar practices as well.
