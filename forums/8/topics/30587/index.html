    Break because of habit refers to the termination of a relation because one/two party feels that this relation is no longer moving on like a relation - it has become a rountine and everyone is getting complacent and gradually this relation has become like a 'good friend' relation.

    What does that means? It simply means that both of you are so used being together that whether you both are together or not doesn't create much impact. She feels that you are just there as a bf, and that kind of need to see you diminishes. She doesn't want to get out of the relation because it has been going for years already, so people just stick with it - until when she finally feels that she has to move on, which is how she told you.

    Risk factor:
    Long relation (2 years and more)
    Finding that one partner doesn't really have that urge to see you - seeing or not or diminishing wanting.
    Have been taking things for granted
    Routine relation
    Due to life: both have to meddle with their own work/studies and the time spend seeing each other is very much reduced - even if there is a off day, the first thought may not be meeting him/her.
