    BAsically, in singing, there are 2 areas we are talking about. Techniques and the Voice itself.

    The voice itself, there is nothing much you can change, ever wonder why some people makes tremendous singing teachers, but never will succeed as a singer. Well not everyone has a voice as tone rich like Celine Dion or any of those black singers.

    So, if you think you can get past this stage, meaning you think you do have a voice with great tone and texture, then go on to learn techniques.

    One technique u must learn is to breath. Breath at appropriate places in the song, that I cant teach u here, u have to practice, u will noe ur breathing is wrong when you hear that you are cutting up the song unnaturally with ur breathing.

    Another is your diaphram. Sing with ur diaphram. One way to ensure that is to practice singing with ur hands holding your waist as you would hold when sitting pillion on a motor. There are more complicated techniques like singing out thru ur forehead and nose. but using diaphram to sing is good enuff but initially u may feel strain if u use diaphram.

    Sing into the mic, know when to draw ur mic away esp when u are singing loud or the high part of the song. Use your mic to do a crescendo or desecendo (soft to loud, loud to soft)

    that is the basic ones....

    Another impt thing of cos would be passion, cant be taught. Just like in "My heart will go on" by celine dion (though i hate the song), the part "near...far...wherever you are" speaks of a longing for someone...so when you sing, are you able to express that feel, when pple hear you can they feel that longing? hope u understand what i saying haha.
