  • Eikei
    9 Jan 04, 11:09
    "Sea Monkeys" are brine shrimp. They are salt water shrimp that feed on microscopic protozoa. The stuff u add in just regulates the salinity of the water and provides some nutrients for the shrimp.

    You can buy a bag of them at aquariums for around $2.50 but don't expect them to last very long. Some better aquariums also sell the eggs but getting them to hatch is difficult if the eggs are of low quality or have not been chemically treated to remove the external layer of protection.

    If its a gift, the set from Toys'R'Us is still the best cos it looks pretty good and has all the proper chemicals and food for the shrimp.
