"Click" - The most meaningful comedy ever!

  • shadez
    10 Aug 06, 20:37
    I went into the theatre with a soda and wedges on my hands and sat down to watch Click, a movie i viewed just to accompany my friend.(I dislike comedies...)

    When the movie started, i thought "Oh well, another unfunny comedy starring Adam Sandler...". No doubt there were scenes that made me laugh but i thinked that it was a lousy made summer comedy for the masses that consisted of "ass jokes", which there were plenty of and half-done jokes that were unfunny.

    Boy oh boy was i wrong...it was by far, one of the most meaningful comedy i have ever watched! The later part of the show almost made me tear and it delivered an important message: "Never take what you have now for granted or regret later.".

    Anyone who have not viewed this movie owes it to themself to give it a watch. It may start off bad but by the end of the film you will leave with a sad yet rejoiced heart.

    After the flick finished, many people in the same theatre walked out with a dazed look on their face thinking about the things that just happened.

    Watch "Click" NOW!
