Even when variety is allowed it only extends to wearing a polo t shirt or sport shoes. Yet for women it is vastly different. They can wear the same corporate uniform men wear, plus a mountain load more. Sleeveless blouses, sleeveless tops so little they look like form fitting tank top, miniskirts, leggings without any skirt over it, sleeveless short dresses, cropped pants, and very often slippers

Or somehow they got the company to ignore the dresscode on themselves altogether and come in wearing just a t shirt top and shorts. Men cant even enter an office in bermudas without receiving snide comments from collegues and told by boss not to wear like that, yet women are wearing hotpants-like bottom without being told off. And those men and women aren’t of differing position or roles, they’re the same position. Just today I saw a girl wear a longish button shirt without anything, skirt or pants or leggings at the bottom, posing as admin for the company, and everyone acted as if there was nothing wrong with her. If a woman went out to malls like that, I’d consider it abnormal, not to mention at work, and yet women now are being condoned and forgiven not just dressing informally but obscenely in the workplace, so what is this??

If all is to be equal, women should be subjected to the same long sleeve long pants and shoes rules. Yet they find that that isn’t enough and still go further and further with exposure and undercuts to the formality to their outlook, all the while still not letting men have any leeway to how we can dress. I can tell u a typical Bermuda looks way better than the casual skirt or torn denim skirt that women love wearing to work nowadays, and yet it isn’t allowed for us. Why is our rights to vary what we wear not respected, but that of women being allowed all the way? Have men not contributed as much as, if not more than women? Who are the ones drafted to serve NS, at the expense of their time, money and comfort? And the so called VWOs in charge of promoting equality only care for the privileges of women and ignore the unfairness men face. Is this becoming of a first world society?

    If women are to be allowed all these indulgences, then at least bermudas should be recognised as formal enough for the workplace for men, those which are around knee length and tastefully cut, not those baggy or slop looking ones.

    Ideally, men should be allowed the exact shortness of length of any kind of pants or skirt a woman may be allowed to wear. Why are our legs made taboo while women allowed to freely show them? Didn’t women coin it as slutshaming when someone criticises them for exposure? Why are they allowing every men to be subjected to the very same thing, day after day, while they enjoy their freedom? Ideally too, women ought to undergo drafting, to complete our nation’s vision of everyone going through a similar experience to boost national unity.
