    The Superior Race

    All two legs must be eaten!
    And so are those with four.
    But those who are pets to our master
    None of them will be call.

    All pets are right!
    And all non-pets are wrong!
    One is designate to be loved,
    The other deserves to die.

    Quick quick get in line,
    Dinner must be ready on time.
    All non-pets to the slaughterhouse!
    Quick quick get in line!

    Some hung their heads real low,
    Others tremble with fear.
    Some take one last look around,
    And bid their young in tears.

    Forward, forward to your glorious death
    To feed the superior race!
    Children of Adam and Eve marches on
    To feed the superior race!
