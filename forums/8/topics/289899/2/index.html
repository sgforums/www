    You can get Creed at cheaper price from Lucky Plaza and US market. Creed from CK Tangs are overpriced unless you need the packaging.
    Creed from Lucky Plaza (Beauty French Enterprise) is very cheap. I can get 1 bottle 120 ml tester bottle for $65. The smell is authentic as I have compared the smell from CK tangs sample with the tester bottle.
    If you want to get Creed from eBay, don't buy Green Irish Tweed or Silver mountain water. Most of GIT and SMW are fake in eBay.

    As for Lutens, I just can't resist buying Gris Clair. I love Lutens and they are going to release Chene soon. Plus e-space is having discount 15% minimum 2 purchase and minimum $350.

    You can always ask the sales assistant in e-space for sample. They always give unlike those from designer fragrance (ie CK, Issey Miyake, Angle...), they are very stingy with sample.
