    Long Island Ice Tea 3
    - 1/2 oz Absolut Citron Vodka
    - 1/2 oz Bacardi Limon Rum
    - 1/2 oz Jose Quervo Gold
    - 1/2 oz Triple Sec
    - 1/2 oz Dry Gin
    - 4 oz Sour Mix
    - 2 oz Coke

    Directions/Comments: This is the perfect Long Island Iced Tea from a Long Island native. I've served this drink many times and it is a hit in the Hamptons. Use the soda gun to allow just the Coke syrup to drip. Roll once and serve with 2 lemon slices. A good one should taste like Nestea.

    Long Island Iced Tea

    INGREDIENTS :

    30 ml. Smirnoff
    30 ml. White Rum
    30 ml. Tequila
    15 ml. Cointreau
    30 ml. lemon juice
    30 ml. sugar syrup
    Dash of cola

    PREPARATION :
    Build liquor ingredients over ice and add juice and syrup. Top with cola. Drop the peel into the drink.

    Garnish : lemon peel

    LONG ISLAND TEA
    Ingredients:
    - 1 oz Vodka
    - 1 oz Gin
    - 1 oz Light Rum
    - 1 oz Tequila
    - 1 oz Lemon Juice
    - 1 tsp Superfine Sugar
    - 4 oz Cola
    - Garnish: Lemon Slice
    - Glassware: Highball Glass

    Mix Vodka, Gin, Rum, Tequila, lemon juice, and superfine sugar in a shaker with ice. Strain into a cocktail glass highball glass and garnish with lemon slice.
