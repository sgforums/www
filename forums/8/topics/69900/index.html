    I did it myself in Melbourne. It was awful but possible to do it yourself.. you save at least $1000.

    This is what you need if you are applying for PR as a graduate.
    You have to get a graduate visa that gives you 6 months to get your paper work together. Australia doesn't give bridging visa to anyone who is holding a student visa any longer. Change of requirements for PR application since i got mine. So pls check!

    Letter of completion from your uni, doesn't have to be the bachelor cert.

    Transcript of grades for uni course

    Australian Federal police check -> need to d/l a form from Immi.gov.au

    Singapore police check -> S$50 plus palm and finger prints from Melbourne police station hq (near crown casino) you need an appointment. Download the form from sg police website. Look for Police Clearance form. 2 weeks.

    Certificate from professional body $300+, mine was from CPA, but if you're in IT you need to get it from ACS. This takes about 2 months.

    Health check, look at www.immi.gov.au for forms and call to make an apptmt. $300 approx.. then you have to go get your xray as well, it's part of the application price.

    All your documents must be certified. So make sure your documents is all in order. Submit your singapore police form as well even if the PR application form didn't ask for it. They usually use that as a method of delaying your PR approval.. to ask you to get it from SG.

    That's all i can think of.

    Put in for your graduate visa straight away.
    Make sure that you get a letter of completion straight away from your Uni.
    Once your results are out, contact your student office to get one.
    Then apply for your Professional certification.
    Health check -> this is valid for one year.
    Sg police check you can do it straight away.
    And don't leave the country if you can before applying.
    Make sure you send everything by registered mail.

    Their approval takes about 1 month for grad visa and up to 6 months for PR. This is if you put in your application during peak season which is in March.

    Forgot something else. Check that you have enough points. And with the points for English language whatever, you get the full 5 points. If you graduated from with a degree you don't have to do that English Language test to prove your language proficiency.
