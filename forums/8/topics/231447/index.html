    Originally posted by Megatop:
    Negative. You have to serve a minimum requirement of (1yr++ i think) before you can defer.

    Yah... and not everyone can defer after serving 1 year... Normally, only the following people can defer: those enrolled in NUS medical school, and those who take government scholarships (e.g. SAF scholarship, MOE scholarship, PSC scholarship, etc). If you're not in those categories, then it's rare to get deferment.

    Not being able to defer does't mean the end of the world. You can use the time during NS to read books and to pick up hobbies. You can also register for weekend courses (if you stay in-camp) or night courses (if you stay out-camp) to acquire additional skills and knowledge. I have friends who learned guitar in my camp, and someone in my company even formed a Japanese language learning-group....
