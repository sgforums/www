    The Empire Strikes Back absolutely blew me away when i first saw it as a boy, and i was hooked since then.

    it was the mix of futuristic technology (albeit "a long time ago, in a galaxy far, far away) and magic (the force) that really drew me in.

    Futhermore, The good guys were the underdogs and were constantly on the run to evade the pursuing bad guys. At the same time, they were trying to bring the fight back to the bad guys. I'm a guy who roots for the underdogs, and this element in the story is another reason why I like the series so much.

    There were one-on-one, face-off lightsaber duels, spectacular space battles, a plethora of interesting locales in Hoth, Tattooine, Dagobah and of course Coruscant.

    And the expanded universe is only getting better and better. The inclusion of the Yuuzhan Vong was a masterstroke; it really reinvigorated the series. Just when things were starting to slow down a little, the powerful new villians show up to throw the galaxy once again into mayhem. Shiok!
