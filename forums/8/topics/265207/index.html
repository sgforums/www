    It varies from person to person. Someone who is in tune with his/her own feelings will tend to have a greater ability to guess the feelings of another, because he/she will be able to read the signs. (Although, this is all just my own speculation, I suppose this is why introverted people can seem more empathic and sympathetic.)

    There's also something called "Cold Reading"; I find I can do it ok enough, but it takes a lot of practice, and you'll need to be very slick in talking (in case you make a mistake). See Wiki for details.

    Reasoning will not kill empathy or feelings. However, if one is too logical and direct, it will "kill" feelings, like you said. Sometimes, intuition just works best.
