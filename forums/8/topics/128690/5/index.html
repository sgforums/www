    When paying a 30 years loan, at the end of 30 years, the owner actually have paid 1.5x of the total HDB cost.

    I myself have opted for 30 years, the simplified calculations below instead of what "They" call "their" formular.

    I pay about $800 per month for a $180,000 loans.
    My HDB bought in at $255,000 - 4 room flat.

    I have to pay $800 for the next 360 months (30yrs) = $288,000
    $288,000 + $75,000 deposit fully paid up front = $363,000 total $ out of my pocket

    That's how much in total I have to pay. Bear in mind that I could never sell back at that price. Also HDB will say, "Block End" at not time frame for redevelopments. Either way, Pigon house is lossing end.
