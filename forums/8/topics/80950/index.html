    hmm, tw, haha, one of my fave, for the food and shopping..

    xi men ting is a place where u can shop..filled with youngsters' stuff....something like our far east plaza....u need to explore the place abit, bcoz i can't rem where they have wat......juz walk ard....
    there is a ah zong mian xian, which i feel is a must try, no tables no chairs..juz stand there and eat....there is a street where most of the shops are selling food..juz ask ard, got a shop with quite good mango ice..the other desserts can try also.... ~~

    u may want to shop at the taipei undergrd shopping mall, (taipei di xia jie), can exit from taipei main stn.....shd be got signs guiding u....
    if u're int to see whole of taipei at a glance, u may want to go up to the top of xin guang san yue, which is juz outside taipei stn, can ask easily for directions, i can't rem the exact entrance lah....
    have to pay for this of coz, i went up once, erm, didnt really find it fascinating.....

    nite mkts, my fave is the shih lin ye shi...it's within walking dist frm jian tan mrt stn, on the red line,...the food stalls have been relocated to a sheltered bldg opp the mrt stn, wif seats inside, it's kinda stuffy, so i usu buy wat i want to eat, then walk over to the nite mkt for my shopping...my fave there, the hao da da ji pai (chicken fillet), NT40 for a big big piece, my sugg is to share, so tat u can hv rm for other yummy food ~~, i ate the chou tofu also, but i anyhow pick a stall wan...so can't reccommend, then, the ai yu bing, something like ice jelly.....jian bao (fried bao), something like fried jiao zi..
    there're still some stalls inside the ye shi selling food.....stuff like fruits, and bing tan hu lu/strawberries/kiwi...etc etc...

    if u got a wkend to spare, u muz go to tamshui, see the sunset...and if got time to spare, take a boat ride to yu ren ma tou..i luv it there!
    tamshui is accessible by mrt...red line, final stn.....can shop, can eat there.....famous drink is the suan mei tang, famous food, ah gei, and u can buy the ah po tie dan and fish snacks......

    i've not been to the hotsprings, but my frens said it was an experience; it's at bei tou...

    theme parks.....hmm, i went to leofu village, erm, ok lah, got some really thrilling rides lor...like the da nu shen, xiao ao fei ying...tink they got a website wan...and there shd be a bus frm taipei going there direct......
    the theme park i really liked was ma la wan, which is somewhere nearer to tai zhong, it's separated to the water and dry section, diff admission of coz. but i liked both, my fren drove us there, so...hee, i dunno how to get there myself.....

    usually, i'll shop for books at their cheng pin bookshops...there's this really large one, which is open 24hrs, at dun hua nan road....can walk frm zhong xiao dun hua stn....

    these are juz wat i usually do when i'm there, my personal preferences...and there're some places i can't really rem liao..shall update if i tink of something.....

    in any case, enjoy ur trip!
