Disappointed with Personal Loan with UXB bank

  • t429
    14 Aug 07, 16:57
    I was in need of financial assistance, thus I called UXB customer service to enquire of possible loan to tide through some difficult times. I was told that I have qualified for all requirements except that I cannot have the cashline service that UXB has given me together with my credit cards.

    Thus, I went down happily on last Saturday's morning to cancel the cashline and apply for the personal loan. The staff there told me to keep the cashline service as it will not interfere the personal loan that I need.

    The staff even tell me that I can loan more than twice my pay. Thus, I happily keep the cashline service which I never use since day one, and fill up the loan application form.

    The staff submit the form for me and told me that I will receive sms from UXB about the approval. In addition, if revision is needed to the amount loaned, UXB Loan officers will contact me about it.

    Today, I received a sms from UXB, telling me that the loan request is rejected. When I called the customer service to ask why, I was told that it was because that I am having the cashline service.

    I was terribly upset. How can such a reknown bank like UXB have such poor service. It was great disappointment. I have UXB in mind for my future planning and this is what I get from a bank.

    I called at 1pm and told that the bank staff will get back to me. It is already 5pm and I am not having any update at all. I told the customer service officer that I do not want to waste another trip to the bank for a fault that belongs to the bank. They should solve it and do some serious service recovery.
