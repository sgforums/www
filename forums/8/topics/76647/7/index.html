    SINGAPORE : Following the collapse of Nicoll Highway on Tuesday, five SBS Transit bus services in the area have been diverted.

    They are service numbers 10, 14, 16, 70 and 196.

    In the direction of the city, these will now ply along Mountbatten Road, Geylang, Kallang, Jalan Sultan, Beach Road and Bras Basah Road.

    In the return direction, these services will travel along Bras Basah Road/ Stamford Road, Beach Road, Jalan Sultan, Sims Avenue, Geylang Lorong 1 and Mountbatten Road, until further notice.

    SBS Transit says traffic inspectors and patrol cars have been deployed to assist passengers at four bus stops along the affected stretch of road. - CNA
