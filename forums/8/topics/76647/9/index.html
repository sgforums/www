    Incident Along Nicoll Highway (Update on Traffic Diversion Plans)

    As a result of the incident at the Nicoll Highway MRT Station work site this afternoon, Nicoll Highway from Middle Road to Mountbatten Road has been closed.

    Motorists travelling from the Shenton Way/Robinson Road area towards the eastern part of Singapore should use the following alternative routes: -

    Shenton Way to East Coast Parkway (ECP)
    Fullerton Road to St. Andrews Road to Beach Road /or Victoria St to Ophir Road
    Stamford Road to the north

    Motorists travelling from the east towards the city should use the following alternative routes:-

    i) ECP
    ii) Mountbatten Road to Kallang Road to Victoria Street

    Please refer to attached map .

    In addition, there will be no ERP charge along Kallang Road from 8.30am to 9.30am until further notice.

    LTA initial assessment showed that the soil subsidence that affected Nicoll Highway was caused by the collapse of the temporary wall supporting the excavation site for the tunnels leading towards the Nicoll Highway MRT Station along Circle Line Stage 1. No motorists were travelling along Nicoll Highway when the incident occurred.

    The surrounding buildings and structures have been assessed to be safe for use by the BCA. LTA is monitoring ground movements in the area regularly. Works on the other Circle Line work sites are not affected.

    LTA is also keeping in close contact with the relatives of our staff and our contractor's staff involved in the incident. We are rendering them all the necessary assistance that they require.

    LTA will hold a press briefing tomorrow to update the media on details of the incident as well as repair works to be carried out.

    As a result of the incident, road closures have been effected at the following locations:

    Nicoll Highway, from Middle Road to Mountbatten Road
    Access to Nicoll Highway from National Stadium carpark
    Java Road
    Crawford Underpass

    Members of public can also refer to the LTA website for updates.

    Nicoll Highway Station is one of the six stations along Circle Line Stage 1 which is currently under construction. At the time of the incident, the contractor, Nishimatsu Construction Co. Ltd/Lum Chang Building Contractors Pte Ltd Joint Venture was carrying excavation works for the tunnel.

    Public Bus Services Diversion

    Six SBS Transit services, namely services 10, 14, 16, 70, 196 and 401, and CSS service 608, will be diverted until further notice.

    These services would be diverted to ply along Mountbatten Road, Geylang Road, Kallang Road, Victoria Street, Jalan Sultan, Beach Road and Bras Basah Road before resuming the original route towards the city.

    In the return direction, these services would be diverted to ply Beach Road, Jalan Sultan, Victoria Street, Kallang Road, Lorong 1 Geylang and Mountbatten Road.

    Although there is no mention by the bus companies, there is a possibility that services 16, 401 and 608 will not call at bus stops in the National Stadium area (Stadium Rd, Stadium Walk, Stadium Crescent and Stadium Boulevard) during the diversion period.
