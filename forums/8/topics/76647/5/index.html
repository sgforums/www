    A road collapse at Nicoll Highway affected electricity supply to the Marina Centre area this afternoon. Singapore Power said the Esplanade, Suntec City and Marina Sqaure areas were affected by the power failure. SP said the collapse damaged transmission cables supplying the area, resulting in the power failure. Supply was restored in 20 minutes via alternative cables.

    The Land Transport Authority has confirmed that excavation works at the Nicholl Highway MRT station worksite has collapsed. LTA said the incident, which happened at 3.30pm, caused a "major soil subsidence" along Nicoll Highway. There's still no word on whether anyone was injured.
