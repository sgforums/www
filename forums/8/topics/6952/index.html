    Kopi - normal coffee with milk and sugar
    Kopi si - coffee with condense milk and sugar
    Kopi o - coffee with sugar but without milk
    Kopi kosong - coffee with milk but without sugar
    Kopi o kosong - coffee without milk and sugar
    Kopi siu dai - coffee with less sugar
    Kopi kar dai - coffee with more sugar
