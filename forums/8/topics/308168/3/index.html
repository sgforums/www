    nehpyh, the kopitiam scene in your example is different from that of a blog.  As some of the other posters have pointed out, a blog is in a public domain and will be subject to scrutiny of others.  On the other hand, a conversation between Jay and Lucas (regardless of venue) should be considered made in confidence and not subject to public scrutiny.  On the other hand, one should always be careful when discussing such things in the public.  You never know whether the walls have ears.
