    THE CORE

    Seemingly a NICE IDEA for a PLOT with the Earth's inner core rotation stopping without knowing the reason.... Starting scenes were quite NICELY DONE... But excitements SOON dies down when the TEAM of "terranauts" ethusiastically builds their subterranean craft and went for their piloting practices... But BE BRAVE! For when the journey finally starts, be PREPARED for the WORST... An 'inner core travel' which basically won't have much for you to see... The team would be encounter faults and 'adventures' here and there just like they would in ANY other movie adventures which involves SPACE, plane in the air, ship or submarines... The ONLY REAL DIFFERENCE here is... You won't have much to see... No birds or fishes... No clouds or stars.... Basically it's just like entering a SIMULATION thing with a COMPUTER SCREEN which show MASSIVE WEIRD color map in front of you.... I think the audience would probably be having BETTER FUN playing their own 'simulation games'....

    When the 'end' draws near... Some 'things' begin to happen on Earth which caused MASSIVE DESTRUCTION... Just when I thought things are getting more interesting... The HEROs SAVED the day... But of course there ain't much of a crew left lah.. Just like your TYPICAL movie show (serial killer or whatever terror...), one by one dead earlier... The people on Earth CHEERED when the RADIATION RAY stopped... (this is the 'best' part, because I can't help laughing at that scene... )

    If ANYONE really plans to watch this... My ADVICE is, TRY NOT to fall asleep...? TYPICAL 'American Heros save the day' type of movie....

    TEARS OF THE SUN

    A 'WAR show' starring BRUCE WILLIS.... Quite a TYPICAL 'behind the front line' army guys movie... But still not bad in my eyes...? (probably being a bit bias here as I kinda like war movies..? >)

    TYPICAL 'American Heros save the day' type of movie too...? Worth a watch if you like war movies...?

    JOHNNY ENGLISH

    "Johnny English" starring ROWAN ATKINSON (your Mr Bean! and no! He's NOT DRINKABLE...) He acts as an AGENT in this movie, who can't really match up to his task...? GREAT INSTINCT for the crime! But don't have much of a BRAIN lah....? Talks much more than your TYPICAL Mr Bean....

    A 'MUST WATCH' for Mr Bean's fan or people just out to grab some LAUGHTERS...? "
