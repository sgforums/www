    Trance Republic's third event features Finnish producer/DJ Miika Kuisma. Having recently played for Ministry of Sound in Sweden, Miika will be bringing us the sounds of Scandinavian trance. Miika's interest in electronic music production began in 1992, citing influences from Oliver Lieb, Faithless, Way Out West, Sven VÃ¤th, Moby and early Eye-Q. Today, his main styles straddle dark, atmospheric trance, tech-house, and introspective chill-out music, often with his uniquely dark and aggressive underlying elements.

    Apart from his solo projects (DualSpin, Lost Method) he has also involved in several other projects collaborating with other producers. These projects include AR52, Fluid in Motion, Future Atlantis and Subliner. Producing for Joof Recordings, Hook-Bellboy Recordings, Freeride Records, Fragrant Music; Fluid in Motion & AR52's(Luke Chen, Miika & DJ Tab) recent release (Timegate/Hibernation) has enjoyed great reviews and is in the playlist of John 00 Fleming, Armin van Buuren, Dj Tiesto, Paul van Dyk, Ferry Corsten, Paul Oakenfold, Matt Hardwick, Scott Bond, Graham Gold, Misja Helsloot, Kid Vicious and more. Apart from original productions, Miika is also heavily engaged as a remixer.
