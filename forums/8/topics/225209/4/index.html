    Their methods of recruitment are similar.
    1, meet you for dinner.
    2, talk about a collegue who's young and earning big bucks and driving big car.
    3, say their company near where you're having dinner, so why not go and have a look.
    4, make you listen to their marketing plan, product etc.

    They have ways to solve whatever rejection you give. I mean really whatever! For the daredevils, you can try challenge them.

    And their teachings and culture may not be very correct according to business ethics. Generally its very sales oriented. Culture is something like "Do whatever it takes to close the sale."

    The senior people are so good that they can re-arrange whatever words/rejection to suit their needs.

    Example:
    Client "Hey you bluff me come down for dinner, actually want me to listen to your marketing plan/product/comapny!"
    MLMer "Why do you think so? If you meet a friend for movie, then after a movie your friend suggests going to dinner, you wun say your friend bluff you to dinner right? So now we meet for dinner, then I suggest we go to my company to take a look since its just round the corner, is this bluffing?"

    Think alot of MLM companies uses this method to recruit. Longfar, enyouth, certain agencies in nuskin, amway, coslab, dw, asayo, etc.

    And they wun tell you more at then end of everything if you have any questions. They'll just ask you to sign/comeback/pay money/etc.

    If you are ok with this way/company culture/teachings/ of doing business, then please go ahead.
