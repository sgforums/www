    Went into Malaysia with my landlord early yesterday morning.

    Have I ever mentioned that he loves old stuff?

    He bought me to this place called Cendol Factory.  And they do serve fairly good Chendol.  The place is pretty modern with Chendol green coloured walls and dark modern furniture with some old type furnitures for decoration.

    I was still suffering from the effect of my dentist trip in JB, so didnt take pictures.  I was eyeing a particular old style drawer unit there and landlord tells me.  He was eyeing that piece of furniture too!

    And when I was flipping thru the 2007 year Malaysian Her World Magazine.  It mentioned some old places and stuff that is no longer in Singapore!

    Landlord was telling me how he cannot find a particular Macdonalds ad from Youtube.  And I remembered a bit about it.

    There was a bunch of school kids supposedly going to the zoo (We all know zoo never had macdonalds outlets.) It started to rain and teachers and kids all cramp into a macdonalds outlet and had fun with some macdonalds kids meals toys.  When the rain kinda stopped.  The children all put on rain boots and duck yellow raincoats, and sang something like a duck song and lined up and walked thru the puddles of rain like little duckies following their teachers.
