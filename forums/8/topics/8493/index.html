    Is there really a beach in West Coast?

    Talking about beaches in S'pore, the most cleaniest ones are located on the offshore islands such as Sentosa,Kusu,St. John's,Coney,Hantu etc. As for those on mainland, the water is dirty and polluted with rubbish.

    To get to Kusu & St. John's, ferry service is available at WTC everyday. However on weekdays & Sats, the ferry leaves WTC at 10.00 am which is the only ferry. Ticket is $9.00 for 2-way.
