    The phenomenon that is the Weakest Link involves a group of strangers who have to work together as a team to win prize money of up to Â£10,000. But only one person can take the money away - the others leave with nothing as, round by round, they are voted off with the host's famous words ringing in their ears: 'You are the weakest link - goodbye'.

    Contestants take turns answering general knowledge questions, attempting to create a 'chain' of correct answers, as well as banking money as they go.

    At the end of each round the team votes off 'the Weakest Link', the person they think answered the fewest questions correctly - and therefore is most at risk of losing the team money. These 'weakest links' must endure the humiliating walk of shame - but it's their turn to get their revenge in the post-vote interviews!

    At the end of the show, the two remaining contestants face each other in the Head to Head: it's make or break now - only one contestant is the winner and takes the money home.
