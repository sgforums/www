    I appealed during PAE so i suppose it should be similar.

    Appeal online from the school's website (if they offer that feature) / Go to the school's general office to ask for a appeal form. Fill it up and give it back to them.

    You should be informed within two days or so.(supposedly it is successfully)

    Go back to the school to get a transfer letter.

    Now you will have to take the transfer letter to the new school that you are going to transfer to. Get the letter signed by the VP or maybe the Principal. They may ask you a few questions like why you want to transfer?...

    Now go back to the School you posted to , to submit the letter(signed). Finished.

    Things you need:
    A photocopy of your result slip
    Photocopy of CCA / CIP records

    Be prepared to answer questions , like why do you want to appeal to that school? *Important* Or maybe an interview depending which school you appealing to.
