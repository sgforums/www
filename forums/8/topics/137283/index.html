    Roti Prata ? Syed Alwi Rd , opposite Mustafa Ctr , that Prata nice . Gravy also good , except his tea-tarik not so impressive .

    Want to eat Pig Spare parts soup ? Jln Besar Rd , behind Eminent plaza , Zhen Wen Ji , soup Best in Sg !

    Prawn Noodles ? Try Upper Dickson Rd , off Jln Besar Rd , the kopi shop called Wen Hua , that Prawn Noodles Best in Singapore , JB and some say Batam ! Must also try the toast bread with Kaya and butter , better than Ya Kun !

    Balestier 933 Ba kut Teh also not bad , but pricing expensive .

    Ba Chor Mee ? Upper East Coast , Chai Chee Ba Chor Mee , good taste , but too much Vinegar at times .
