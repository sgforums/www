    yes yes....

    i used drafting pens and black ink...who says drafting pens are for
    technical use? my collection includes 0.1mm, 0.25mm, 0.35mm, 0.5mm up to 0.9mm and i keep them washed and dry when not in use. you can use ink on your drawings instead of pencil. i prefer pencil for a rough sketch and draft for what i want before applying ink over it. works everytime. can try charcoal pensils coz it can do more than a pencil alone....or you can combine pencil, charcoal and ink. i prefer my sketches to be in black and white but sometimes i use poster color and spray paint for special effects.....airbrush too expensive!
