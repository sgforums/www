    It is an offhshore Island near TaiDong, Taiwan. A Pacific Island.

    Worth visiting if u guys gg to Taiwan except in the winter.

    Got natural reserve where u can see wild or captive silk deer, snorkeling in the coral reefs, see different kinds of rocks craved out by the ocean waves etc.

    There is a famous hot spring. The water is hot due to the volcanic activity near the island seabed. It is said to be 1 of the 3 hot springs in the world due to seabed volcanic activity. The other 2 such hot springs are in Japan and Italy.

    There is 3 prisons on the island, with one still functioning. One of the 2 non-functioning prison is called Green Villa. Used to lock political prisoners which included Taiwan foul-mouthed politician Li Hao (who say Singaporeans descended from low class people) , Shi Ming De and current Taiwan Vice President Lu ShuLian. Now it is open to tourists and visitors.
