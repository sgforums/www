    Snobbish,

    You should talk to the manager, or escalate the problem. There's NO need to buy another ticket/seat.

    They should be able to replace a lost ticket, sometimes at a admin cost.

    Their reason for having 2 ticket is just a ploy to get you to spend more money.

    The original ticket has your worker's name on it.

    When any passenger check in at the airport counter, the customer service must verify the name on the passport match the name on the ticket before they will issue a boarding pass.

    Even if another person pick up the lost ticket and try to use it, he/she will not be able to pass that point, UNLESS he/she has the SAME NAME as your worker.

    There is no refund, is most likely your company bought a NON-REFUNDABLE ticket. But it can be replaced if lost.

     But make sure you get the ticket replaced BEFORE the flight date.

    It will likely cause your worker to miss his flight if you wait till the last minute to settle this problem.

    Don't delay.
