    24 Mar 05, 00:57
    Suggest you go find a map of JB first before going over, you can buy one easily at any book store. This way, you know where each place is.

    For first timer, going to city square mall is probably the easiest. After you clear the immigration, just walk straight all the way, first pass the mama shop, down the underpass, passby the petrol kiosk, then another building infront until you reach a big modern shopping center. The walk should be about just 5 mins.

    This way, getting back is also easy. Just cross the road and the check point is just in front.
