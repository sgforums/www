    Rat: The Other White Meat?

    POSTED: 12:59 p.m. EST February 18, 2004
    UPDATED: 1:07 p.m. EST February 18, 2004

    PHNOM PENH, Cambodia -- Rats are the other white meat -- at least for some people in northwest Cambodia.

    Fear of catching bird flu has prompted some Cambodians to give up eating chicken.

    The newspaper Light of Cambodia reports fresh rat is fetching a premium at local markets. Villagers catch the rodents in nearby rice fields and sell them for up 35 cents each.

    One vendor reports selling 130 pounds of rat meat a day.

    Apparently booze helps. The paper reports the rat meat is often eaten as a snack while drinking.
