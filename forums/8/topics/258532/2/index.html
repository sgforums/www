    Allow me correct this misunderstanding: Hanging DOES result in asphyxiation.

    Because when the condemned person falls through the trapdoor with added sandbags tied to his legs, the collective weight of the sandbags and his body will snap his neck. The angle in which the noose is draped over his neck, also acts to cause his broken neck to protrude against his windpipe totally blocking ability to breath.

    There's an excruciating pain when the neck breaks, neck muscles and tissues tear internally, and some severance of spinal nerves. Some loss of sensation can be experienced as such, but the brain can still feel suffocation while the body is partially responsive due to possible torn spinal column.

    Death in this method is brutal, and somewhat slow, because the body still twitches and squirms in pain and suffocation. Bliss only comes when the person blacks out from lack of oxigen. Brain death comes a several minutes later.

    Not pretty yah?
