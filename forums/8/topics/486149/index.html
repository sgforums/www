They told me to go to a barber to have my hair cut and return to collect my results only when my hair length is acceptable, otherwise I can only collect my results at around 5pm after everyone else (who can go in) has collected their results. Thus, I went to the barber shop nearby to cut my hair. There was quite a number of my schoolmates there whom were also forbidden entry into the school because of unacceptable hair length, that the queue at the barber shop was quite long.
