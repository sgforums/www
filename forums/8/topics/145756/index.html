    There is certainly nothing in principle wrong with interracial relationships; for we are all members of one and the same human species.

    In fact, I believe that each individual person is more than the sum of his genes; and this is could be why (if my belief on this score is true) a person of one race may happen to find members of the opposite sex of some other other race more desirable than are the members of the opposite sex of his own race.

    I know, for instance, that some Indian men find Chinese women very much more attractive than Indian women (in general); and, what is more to the point, I also know that some of the same men are given to finding Indian women the least attractive of all possible types of women.

    I think that even if it is true that some of these Indian men have the preference that they have for Chinese women (and posssibly also the aversion that they have towards the women of their own race) for a reason that partakes exclusively of naturalistic factors (so that their set of dispositions in this regard can be compared, in terms of their cause, with what is in some quarters held to be the cause of the fact that some Chinese men are unable to desire any women, except the women of their own race, or, at least, such women as seem to them to be indistinguishable from those whom they take to be the women of their own race); I think that even if this is true, that still would not mean, however, that the Indian men in question would be unable to muster the willingness to act in the teeth of their dispositions in this regard.

    On the Christian view, it is this ability to fight off our own inclinations (however they may arise in us and regardless of the series of factors, naturalistic or otherwise, by which they may be accounted for) that marks us off individually as persons who are each cast in the image of God.

    My advice, then, to any person who may be afflicted with some of the aforementioned tendencies (or with tendencies that are similar to these), would be as follows:

    So what if a particular ethnic group of women are more desirable, as some of your friends maintain, than are the women of your own ethnic group, and so what if you cannot help but feel yourself bound to agree with your friends in this regard?

    It is still open to you nonetheless to seek beauty on a higher plane and on the sort of plane where if you will but sincerely seek it there, you should certainly find it.

    For, is not the beauty of the Crucified Christ infinitely greater than any natural spectacle this universe can afford you, even that of the face of the most beautiful woman on earth?

    Let the pagans have their Aphrodite and let them sate themselves like animals with the taste and sight of her flesh; you, my friend, you, turn to the Living Christ!

    In Christ,

    Ben
