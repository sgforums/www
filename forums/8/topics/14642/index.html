    9 May 02, 18:16
    i dun like m series built-in rechargeable batt concept...oso pretty laggy compared to the previous series...

    PDA functions are almost similar throughout all brands...

    operating systems include Palm OS, Win CE, Pocket PC, Epoq (depends on which brand/model)

    now mostly above 8mb ram...
    calendar, address book, to-do list, memo, notepad are the basics

    other "extras" or differences will be...
    -size, weight, dimensions
    -external or internal battery (rechargeable or not)
    -handwriting recognition/graffiti
    -colour vs monochrome
    -expansion slot
    -Office tools like Word, Excel
    -connection to Internet via cradle/modem, infra-red, bluetooth
    -support mp3 or other music formats
    .
