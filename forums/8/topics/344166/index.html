  • Lee012lee
    14 Jan 09, 13:59

    Pte School - not only no govt subsidy but the pte sch wants to make a profit too.

    So, school fees at all pte schs are very expensive.

    Given your result - 2 A1's ie A1 in CL2 and Maths, you should enrol in ITE to learn a skill AND at the same time re-do the O Level as a private candidate ie re-take English, a Science subject eg Physics or Chemistry or combined science and another subject eg History, SS, Geography, POA, Art or Music.

    The objective is to do well in these 3 subjects and combined with the 2 A1s that you already gotten, you will be able to apply for some very good courses in polytechnic next year.

    The advantages of studying at ITE are

    1.  pay very low school fee ie approx $20-$30 PER MONTH

    2.  can ask ITE lecturers for help in the O Level subjects that you are re-taking

    3. can get NS deferment if you are due for NS

    4. can get bus/MRT concession

    5. can learn a vocational skill

    In addition, even if you pay $7000 to study in MDIS, MDIS will not guarantee that you will pass in your O level exam.

    BTW, pte schools have a very low passing rate ie L1R4 that is < = 20 points in O level exam. No one pte school has advertised the number of their students that have L1R4 that is < = 20 points in O level exam ie the percentage of students that have L1R4 that is < = 20 points in O level exam is very low.
