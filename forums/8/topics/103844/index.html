  • Qitai
    22 Nov 04, 17:10
    Cabin - whatever you can carry - there is some official weight limit for carry-on I think, but nobody ever actually checks them. For check-in, it depends on airline, fare type and which cabin you travelling on - check with airline concern.

    The list of to bring can be a lot and very little depending on what type of trip you are making and what type of person are you. Generally,

    (1) If you have electronic devices that needs recharging, check your adapter.
    (2) Bring a credit card if possible. If not, make sure you have enough spare cash in a major currency (E.g. US Dollar or Singapore Dollar)
    (3) Bring you passport and check if any Visa is required (Singapore usually do not need to worry about this)
    (4) Check weather and bring appropriate clothings
