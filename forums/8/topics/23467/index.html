    yes looks do matter. but up till when?

    i've always thought that when two strangers meet and both are single (well in these days don't even have to be single!) they will assess each other from first impressions. Looks are definitely a huge influence on first impressions. one can argue that looks is not a criteria in making frens but it FORMS that impression of a person.

    so i'm lead to believe that if someone finds another unpleasant in looks, it may create a negative impression. not that the other person is bad but the impetus for that first person to get to know the other is not as strong.

    however when the first impression is an OK one (to put it crudely, the other person PASSES the "test") then they proceed to get to know each other - and the start another chapter in their lives if possible.

    so to sum it up, looks are important only in the initial stage. when that passes i guess we can generally put looks aside and look for a person's "inner beauty".
