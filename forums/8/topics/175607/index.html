    I'm from ntu, the reason i chose ntu is becoz, it's near my house and has no need to bid for modules. nus u need to bid for ur modules and has become quite a chore for my nus friends esp during the start of the semester... somehow, there are still ppl who says it's very good to have bidding system... for me, I just dont see the point and im happy with the allocation system they have in ntu... it's quite hassle free...the argument is that in the bidding system if u have more 'buying' points, u have a higher chance of getting the modules u want... in ntu, if u are in courses like engine, they allocate u the core subjects, then u have to choose prescribe or unrestricted electives urself.. u just choose the ones u want, then the system will determine if u get it or not... there are those very popular modules that have limited spaces, so the system will normally give priority to more senior students. but what the heck they have hundreds of modules for u to choose, so if u dont get what u want this sem, u still got alot of good modules to take.

    bad pts about ntu? 1130-1330 very hard to find seats in the canteens. studying areas also very pack. and maybe the bus system is quite troublesome for some ppl, and ntu is quite large, so during hot days, can be damn hot and sweaty when u move from place to place. the halls in ntu are older than nus ones and has uncool names... like hall 1- 15. but of coz if u want to name all the halls, it's quite bo liao also... u need 15 weird names... having numbers just more convenient. other than that, it's quite a decent place to study... exams end earlier than the other unis but of coz start earlier too.
