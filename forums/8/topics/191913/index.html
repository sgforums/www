    source based questions are no kick...tt is...if u know how to do them...

    for inference...infer the purpose of the source first. if u can't infer the purpose, try for tone, provenance etc etc. but if u infer the purpose of the source LOGICALLY with evidence and explanation...u will get around level 4 which is like 4 - 6 marks depending on the question. i dun take history(but scored in it when i was lower sec) but SS n history works about the same way.

    for compare and contrast questions, dun just compare n contrast content. try to compare purposes and tones, complete with evidence and explanation. that will be around level 4, about 5 marks too.

    always remember to hav PEE in ur answer.

    P - Point
    E - Evidence
    E - Explanation

    cheers
