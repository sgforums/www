    Korean fantasy film that takes the creation myth of Mago, the mother of the creation of universe, and traces it through time from the fall of paradise to the corruption of modern man. The film relies mostly on body language with a minimal of dialogue, using 825 nude amateur actors (including women recruited from Seoul college campuses!) to convey a poetic meaning.

    Just watched this movie, lots of korean girls in their age 10+ to 20+ (also includes some teenage xiao mei mei) completely naked, bathing in the spring, running on grassland, climbing slopes etc... various activities. Nice figures, white smooth skin.

    Also saw the 12 spirits in the form of women such as Fire spirit, Wind Spirit, Tree Spirit, Moon spirit etc.

    Storyline is not easy to understand, so I just focused on the gals.

    Now showing at Golden cinema which is opposite Beach Rd army market. Rated R21.
