    In Canada, progressive income tax; meaning the more you earn the more you pay in taxes:

    0 - 29,950 (Federal - 17%) + (49.50% of 17% if you are living in BC, diffrent rate for different provinces). Total 25.42%.

    29,951 - 59,950 (Federal - 26%) + (49.50% of 26%). Total 38.87%.

    59,951 - above (Federal - 29%) + (49.50% of 29%). Total 43.36%.

    Above all, you pay additional federal 3% surtax for income above $65,000 a year.

    In addition, you pay 7% provincial tax (in BC) for some items that you purchase. The luxury tax kicks in at additional 3% for vehicles above $30,000, totaling 10%. In addition, GST at 7% for some items. So you could end up paying 7% for something or 14% - 17% for some other things that you buy.
