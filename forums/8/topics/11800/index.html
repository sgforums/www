    ********************************************
    Baking a cake and putting the ring in, or using the ring as a topping on the cream on top of the cake
    Writing "Marry Me" on a sandy beach

    Building a web page dedicated to her and propose to her on that page

    While hiking up a trail in the middle of the forest

    In a bath tub full of rose petals

    Asking her out for dinner at her favourite restaurant and proposing to her there

    Sending her roses every day for a month leading up to the day you propose

    Getting down on your knees at a busy road junction and don't get up until she says "yes" (make sure you are wearing something bright!)

    Writing a screen saver program with a "marry me" message and installing it in her office computer without her knowing

    On board an aero plane to your vacation destination

    On Valentine's Day in the restaurant and then buying everyone in the restaurant a drink after she says "yes"

    Pick her up from her office and arrange with the building administrator to "trap" both of you in the lift. Then propose to her

    In a karaoke lounge, sing her a love song and then propose to her after the song

    On a cruise ship against the ocean wind

    In an ice-cream parlor while having dessert

    Writing her a love letter expressing your heart felt love and devotion to her

    Bring her up in a hot air balloon and propose to her high up in the sky

    Bring her to the hotel banquet hall and then casually ask if she would like to have the wedding dinner here

    Put the ring in her birthday cake and propose to her on her birthday

    Put the ring in your mouth just before kissing her and pass it to her (be careful not to swallow it!)

    How to say it? Some examples... but make up your own more original =)

    "Sweetheart, you have been the most important person in my life since I can remember, I want you to know that I love you, and I'll love you forever. Will you marry me?"

    I think our relationship has come to a point of no turning back. I'm ready for the next step in life, and I want to take it with you. What I'm trying to say is, I love you, and I want to start a family with you. Will you marry me please?"

    "Darling, you are the love of my life. You've been my girlfriend/boyfriend, my confidante, my best friend, my lover, you're the person who cheers me on and supports me in everything I do. I'm so happy when I'm with you. Will you marry me?"

    "You're the most beautiful, intelligent, kind, wonderful person I've ever been with in my life. I can't imagine being another day without you. I want to be with you for the rest of my life. Will you marry me?"

    "There was a time in my life when I thought I would always be lonely. You changed that. You made me see there was hope and faith and love. If you hadn't been around, I don't know where I'd be now. I love you. I need you. Will you marry me please?"

    "We've known each other for a while now, and I feel a special connection to you. I think there's a reason why God let me meet you and fall in love with you. Honey, let's get married!"

    "Darling, I would like to ask you something very important concerning our future together. I really hope you'll say Yes, because you're the most important person in my life, and I love you more than anything in the world. Will you marry me please?"

    "Sweetheart, I've achieved everything a person could want in life. I've got a great career, great friends and a wonderful girlfriend. But there's one thing missing in my life, and it just wouldn't be the same without it. Do you know what it is? (wait for her to respond) I missing a wife. (Her name) I love you. Please marry me?"

    "Honey, I know we've had our rough spots, and I know I've acted really stupid sometimes. But I want you to know, that I've always loved you, and I can't live without you. I'm willing to try and work out all our problems together. Will you marry me please?"

    "What happened recently has made me think about a lot of things. About the fragility and preciousness of life. And I realise that you're the most important person in my life, and I want to treasure you, protect you, nourish you and love you. I want to be with you for the rest of my life. Will you marry me please?"
