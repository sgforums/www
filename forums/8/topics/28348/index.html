    In my room
    By Travis Wong

    In my room
    where I do sleep
    I've got sheets that need washing
    and so many pictures of you

    In my room
    where I do dress
    I've got clothes which are fading
    and so many pictures of you

    In my room
    where I do eat
    I've got snacks slowly rotting
    and so many pictures of you

    In my room
    where I do play
    I've got internet online gaming
    and so many pictures of you

    In my room
    where I do listen
    I've got my stereos blasting
    and so many pictures of you

    In my room
    where I do sing
    I've got my guitar strumming
    and so many pictures of you

    In my room
    where I do work
    I've got Pentium 3 running
    and so many pictures of you

    In my room
    where I do wake
    I've got an alarm buzzing
    and so many pictures of you

    In my room
    where I do stay
    there are many things
    that I'd love to say to you
