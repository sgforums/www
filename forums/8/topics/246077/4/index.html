    Platform melodies
    Some of the most common melodies used at JR platforms, played before the train door closing announcements are made. There are many more, but most of these should ring a bell if u have been to Tokyo.

    The first is one of the few in-train announcement chimes (this one is used on the Narita Express N'EX), and the last is the chime before platform announcements.
    http://www.freewebtown.com/tamago/JRmedley.mp3

    List of name of melodies:


    In-train announcement
    Towards Ikebukuro (Outer loop) towards Ueno and Tokyo Sta.
    http://www.freewebtown.com/tamago/JRanno1.mp3

    From Shibuya, towards Harajuku and Yoyogi (Outer loop).
    http://www.freewebtown.com/tamago/JRanno2.mp3

    Platform announcement
    "Arriving soon at platform 7 is the train to Ueno and Tokyo.
    For your own safety, please stand behind the yellow line, please.
    Ikebukuro, Ikebukuro.
    The doors at Platform 7 are about to close. For your attention, please."

    http://www.freewebtown.com/tamago/JRplat.mp3
