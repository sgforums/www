    I am an NTU student Accountancy who has almost finished his studies and gonna graduate soon. The situation is NTU is really rather interesting. Let's split this up first...first year, second year and final year girls....(All this applies to Accounts and Business Studies Faculty in NTU...so please DON'T Generalise)....

    First year girls go into NTU acting blur blur...join orientation and get to know lots and lots of new GUYS. At this point, I would give it a rough estimate that at this point about 30 % of all the girls are attached...either thru JC, Poly or Sec school even. Their Bf would be either in the Army, in NTU/NUS or overseas. This would leave a significant 70% of girls for "chasing".

    Thus the first year is the most "lucrative" for a guy who is unattached (better still if he is staying in Hostel !). Most guys go all out to chase girls in the first year. Most succeed....except for those either EXTREMELY blur (like me !)....or extremely shy or BOTH ! There are also some unfortunate guys who end up liking a girl who is already attached, and just sit and stare at her in tutorials everyday (this sounds familiar too)....

    By second year, the statistics would have shifted. Probably about 70% of girls are attached or WERE attached before...those already attached before coming to NTU either break up with their bf (an estimated 30%) or else continue their relationship (70%)....

    Those girls attached in first year usually stay so....this is according to personal observations. Also, the second year is when guys get a little more desperate if they have been "asleep" during the first year. As a result, more girls get attached....

    By third and final year, things have sort of stabilized. About 75 % of girls in ACC/BUS faculty are attached....and this includes girls who broke up so badly with theur bf that they are disillusioned about love (So this excludes them from being chased). This leaves a paltry 25% left for the remainder guys to chase....and believe me there are quite a few unattached guys still by third year ! (ahem !)

    As someone had mentioned, it is really more difficult to find girls after Uni, because most are already applying for flats ! ALso, work commitments tend to be very tight...and when one first starts work as an auditor (which most ACC grads do), it is a busy and hectic job, with not much time for socialisation.

    Thus, the remainder girls who are unattached throughout Uni will not find it easy to find a partner, and the same goes for a guy too.

    YET ! There are quite a few girls unattached throughout their ENTIRE University lives. Some reasons include: (1) Studies are more important - The workaholic girl who thinks about getting honours and a good job....guys are a secondary thought ! (2) Not ready for a BGR - Guess some think they are not "mature" enough....hope they think so soon cos some of these girls are quite good really. (3) Broke up before and scared of guys/BGR - Blame the ex-bf for instilling such thoughts in the girl....some have to wait till 2 years after the breakup to try loving a man again....

    Of the 3 categories, (3) is the easiest to tackle after graduation. Face it....(1) just wants to be a career woman and (2) never really knows when she is ready (perhaps NEVER ?).

    I would say category (3) girls are only about 3 to 5 % among the current graduating cohort of students from ACC and BUS in NTU. Of course, one might say that there are "more fish in the ocean" when one goes to work, rather than just the Uni batch of girls.

    This is TRUE....and it's a comforting thought to all unattched guys AND girls who have never been attached b4 or have felt disillusioned by love in Uni.

    So good luck to all of you and please.....learn to love yourself first if you wish to love someone else !!!
