    You can ask a question and give 2 options... Put a 'Prefer' or 'Are you' before it.... Like... Example...

     

    "Prefer A or B?"

     

    "Are you A or B"?"

     

    The next person would choose one as answer and give the next question....

     

    By default if no 'Prefer' or 'Are you ' is added in front, it'll mean 'prefer'....

     

    'Prefer' is asking if that next person prefer one thing over the other... 'Are you' is asking if that person is more of 'A' or 'B'....? If the 'Are you' is subjective, the answering partying can answer it closest to what he / she feels it is for him / her.... :)
