    26 Sep – Korean singer Paige of all-girl pop group, Astar, has gone under the knife to look like her boyfriend's ex-lover, according to a report on Daily Chilli's website.

    Paige, took the drastic option upon finding out that her boyfriend still loved his former girlfriend who had died in a road accident several years ago.

    "I found his photographs with the woman while cleaning up his stuff earlier this year."

    "Later, I noticed that he would always look at the pictures and cry. I realised that he still has not forgotten her," said Paige.

    "I love him so I can do anything for him," added Paige.

    The artiste chose to undergo the surgery at a hospital in Guangzhou, China, as she did not want her boyfriend of three years to know about it.

    "I want to see the surprise on his face when I see him in Korea later," she said as she is currently recuperating from the surgery.

    According to the report Paige's doctor revealed that the star had to change 15 parts on her face, her breasts and skin.

    The whole operation took 10 days and cost about $200,000 Yuan (SG $40, 716).
