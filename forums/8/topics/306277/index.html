    Army BMT enhanced intake for JC students are usually on Jan and April. These people only need to serve 1 year 10months. So lets just look at april batch, April batch enlistees will ORD in Feb. Minus away their annual leaves and off, they can start schooling in January. Anyway, its not compulsory to attend university's lessons. Some modules may require you to attend or else you may lose up to 10% of assessment grades but the university cant expel you.

    Generally in University like NUS/NTU/SMU, most students will start their courses in July/August. However, nowadays more and more students are taking the january intake cos there is no disadvantage at all. All ESSENTIAL modules are offered in every semesters. You will graduate in december, a period of time where there are much less fresh grads than June.
