  • anhydrouscoppersulphate

    29-year-old man drowns at Siloso Beach on Sentosa
    By Rita Zahara, Channel NewsAsia

    SINGAPORE: A 29-year-old Chinese national, here on a work permit, has drowned at Siloso Beach on Sentosa island on Friday.

    Clad in a pair of swim shorts, the man was believed to have gone swimming with a few of his friends but went missing around 3.30pm.

    His body was later recovered from the sea by a lifeguard and a friend.

    He was pronounced dead at about 4pm.

    Police have classified the case as unnatural death.

    Police say the victim cannot be named because the next of kin has not been informed.

    It is the fifth drowning this year on Sentosa.

    Last year, 46 people drowned in Singapore, two of who were on Sentosa. - CNA/ir
