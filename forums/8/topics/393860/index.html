    Originally posted by Fairyfairy86:

    how to start business without thinking through. if i win lottery, i will stop asking and start doing but all i have is my hard earned money.

    By the time you finish thinking and planning (that's if you finish because it will never be enough), others would already be way ahead.

    The key thing is the perception of money. You are extremely afraid that all your hard earned money will be gone; you can't afford to risk a single cent. And that's where differentiates the successful and the failures in businesses. There's no such thing as a totally risk free venture that will make you a tidy sum.

    The same thing goes for investing. I researched a lot and determined that I want to enter a particular stock, and told my sis so. She said will only buy if I can guarantee her will profit within 6 months. I told her forget it, don't buy. {There are other concepts here as well which I won't touch}.

     

    Another example: I started a finance blog which has about 100~200 unique readers a day now... Starting from US$1 a month or less from adsense advertising, it has grown to about US$20+, while I'm doing it to help myself think clearer before plugging my funds into any investments. My main risk is the time I took to set it up, because the opportunity cost of my time is quite huge; I can easily go outside and ask for much more. But I just went ahead and set it up.

    The key is, just do it. Learn and think as you do. Think too long and you won't go and do it in the end.
