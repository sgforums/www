    Erm.. by the way, i am a bit confused, what's the diff between government and stats board?
    Main difference between the 2 is that:

    1) Ministry is tasked with overseeing and developing of a certain function in the country, hence the duties, responsiblities and scope are wider and more generic in nature.

    2) Stat Boards are typically formed under a Ministry to carry a more specialized function e.g MOE oversees all Educational aspects in SG while the polytechnics oversee tertiary educational aspects pertaining to diploma level only.
