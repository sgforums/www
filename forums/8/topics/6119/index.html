    Trance itself splits into diff. cat. : u have deep trance(percussive driven, deep basslines and very melodic), progressive trance(trance which has very small parts of vocals here and there, a some sort of mid progressing into harder/faster trance music and very uplifting...makes u wanna put yr hands in the air), Goa trance(trance with influence originally from India!hard and consistant basslines)and I guess lastly is hi-nrg(high-energy) trance( wat they called peak-hour/floorfillers trance e.g: System F's "out of the blue"; Lost Witness's "red sun rising";Armin's "communication". Usually with lots of thumpy basslines, peaks/breakdowns...fast paced.)

    Recommended cd comps:
    -Global Underground: Sasha 'live' at Ibiza(progressive house to trance)....very melodic and funky....check out his infamous long mixing into the next vinyl.
    -Euphoria-level 3(mainstream trance to floor anthems....also for the budget-consious)
    -Slinky-technique(good buy....has a no. of trance mayhems)

    Those who are into progressive trance, Rennaissance is playing on the 11 feb. with Dave Seaman and Sister Bliss on the decks @Zouk!!Interested to join me.....email me. No kaki lah...so looking for ppl to join me.
