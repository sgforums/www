    Originally posted by ben1406:
    how does one sign on when he is overseas ? i really do want to sign up as one.
    use credit card or debit card lor.

    for those using DBS/POSB ATM to do funds transfer,

    1) from main screen, select "Other Transactions"
    2) if u see "Funds Transfer" on any of the buttons, select it
    3) if there is a "from which account" option, select "Own Savings Account" (unless u have a chequeing account then is Current A/C)
    4) if it prompts to what account, select "Other Savings Account"
    5) if it prompts for the account number, enter "0125019946". the dashes (-) are set for you and you just enter the numbers. Enter.
    6) Your receipt should come out after 10 seconds. Keep it.
    7) Come back here, click Premier, then Enter Payment Details.

    For bank wire from other banks, log on to your Internet Banking facility and look for funds transfer.
