    Sometimes, whatever he's done, I feel he's better off doing nothing.

    I can't be bothered also if fans out there think that this pork-forbidden idol is cute and they all bear the flaming desire to marry him.

    To tell you the truth, I find the entire Singapore Idol idea a joke. So what if it stimulates growth in the media sector, make some people with so-called talents hopeful and give some immature gals out there someone to crave for? It doesn't concern me at all.

    I personally feel that for every 5 Singapore Idol episodes, the quality is somewhat equal to that of 1 American Idol show. The ratio is that shocking. But that's the truth. And truth hurts to all the idol fans out there. When viewing Singapore Idol, I kept fidgeting and couldn't concentrate on what's shown. In short, I find no passion and absolutely zero purpose in watching people finding talents and recognising them in fellow people. Furthermore, I feel shortchanged. So what if they are spending the big bucks in making SI possible? I rather they do something more constructive. Having idols out there seemed to spread the message to the masses that there's an easier way to quick wealth.

    The answer is to put up a good show and appeal to the masses. Perhaps deceive them and make them all long for you like sexual beasts. Throw in some who will marry you on the spot, not forgetting being some shit ambassadors of some commercial stuff out there. Maybe then, you will have the fame, fortune, fans and a better sex life.

    So much for being an idol...
