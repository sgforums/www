    Its a nice place to go and relax

    Well.. depends on wat type of accomodation ye are looking for... Beach resort? Hotel?

    Beach resort wise.. try to get one along Pantai Cenang...Holiday Villa's a gd spot. V nice beach + view. If u want hotel.. preferably within Kuah Town.. unless u super rich go and book Andaman.. $500+ / night.. lol

    I went there late last september with my girlfriend. was raining for 3 days straight. Had the first 2 days of pure sunshine... anyway u don't have to worry about what to visit as there will be ppl distributing pamphlets regarding the 1 or 2 day tour packages they offer

    Make sure u choose the one which brings u to the Eagle Feeding event.. its v v v nice.. grab a gd camera along too.. v nice scenery. Do visit the cable car ride up to the summit of one of their peaks... V gd photo opp there... it will be abit chilly tho

    If u are into the history, legends and folklore of Langkawi, visit Mahsuri's Tomb...

    Makan wise... visit 'Fat Mum' homecooked seafood restaurant along Pantai Cenang... very close to Holiday Villa Beach Resort... SUPERB FRESH SEAFOOD AT LOW PRICE!!!!! MUST TRY!

    The locals there are VERY VERY VERY VERY nice!

    I'm making a trip up to Langkawi again this december for Langkawi International Maritime & Aerospace airshow...

    airfare up to Langkawi from Singapore via Silkair currently is $302 per person *subject at least 2 to go* for december period.

    Any info ye need, feel free to PM me..

    taa!~~~ Enjoy [/b]
