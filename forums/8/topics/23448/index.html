    hello pple!

    i'm new to this forum as u can see from the number of posts that i've made - or the lack thereof!

    Anyway from time to time i will daydream, or in the course of a meaningful conversation, come up with certain, hmm, how would you say it, ideas or theories, and i wanna invite comments. anyway that's wat forums are for

    so the first thought is:

    Why is it hard to explain why you love someone?

    Have you ever been stumped when someone asks you "eh brudder (or sister), SERIOUSLY, why u love him/her so much ar?"

    then u become glassy eyed and... words betray u.

    there's of course the usual answer - becos she's funnee, understanding, sweet, caring, *add more adjectives to this list*

    but is that the REAL answer? well, i dun think so and this is why:

    When you are asked a question, it requires the brain to firstly, understand it and secondly compute the answer. Even presenting the answer (usually in the english language for me!) requires the brain.

    However the brain can NEVER answer on behalf of the heart. and the heart is where love comes from. so the brain can never answer that question correctly.

    Even if the heart could speak for itself, i doubt any language in this world can even come close to expressing it's feelings. and it does not just limit to love.

    Now you know why words betray you when u are asked that question.
