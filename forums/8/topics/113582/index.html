    The crying girl beneath the rain forest tree...

    As her tears drop to the roots of the rain forest tree....
    The tree reaches out its branches to console the crying girl
    It stop a distance away...
    It was the same girl who had carved deep on it's trunk with a pen knife that morning.... the wounds still bleeding and fresh....
    After a moment the branch moved towards her forgetting about the pain it had
    The girl brushes off the branch not wanting to accept....
    If the girl accepts the tree branch she has to accept the bond of love .....
    The bond of love requires a change in her... she is unsure .....
    So while the girls ponders ... she kept on crying ....
    And the helpless tree stood there with the branch just a few centimetres away and kept it there......
