    7 Mar 03, 10:02
    It's a co-ordination thing..

    Just as you lean forward to almost tipping over, your legs must be tucked at the point of the explosive extension... also your arms must be at the point where they are about to swing forward violently...

    then violently extend legs and swing arms forward and upwards..

    in mid-air, try to get into the tuck position with legs forward and feet preparing to land..

    land with your feet not so far ahead of you that you fall backwards but allow the forward momentum to carry you forward in the landing so you don't fall on your bum

    should be okay... until i screwed up my back, used to jump about 2.4m like that..
