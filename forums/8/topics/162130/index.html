    1992: NTUC Pasir Ris Resort
    2000: NTUC Lifestyle World (Downtown East)
    2005: Simply called 'Downtown East'

    Located at 1 Pasir Ris Close (junction of PSR Dr 3)

    Notice that there are changes inside Downtown East (NTUC Pasir Ris Resort), for example, the main lobby located at the entrance has been converted to shops and the old swimming pool is the current site of Wild Wild Wet.
