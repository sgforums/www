    Tea generally has a cleansing effect. It is NOT a slimming agent and it is not meant to be used as a subsitute for exercise and a good diet.

    Try this: In the morning, before you eat anything, drink a cup of black tea like those Lipton sachets WITHOUT any sugar or milk. Use ONLY boiling hot water and infuse your tea for 2-3 minutes, any longer and you will have a real bad after taste. This has a cleansing effect and in the long term has a slight slimming effect but general due to its cleansing effect.

    BUT if you have gastric or weak stomach, dun try it.

    Also, whether its Oolong tea or Green tea, they are the same, its just a matter of degree of fermentation.

    TEA:

    Non-Fermented - Green Tea
    Semi-Fermented - Oolong tea (from here spin off grades like TieGuanYin etc..)
    Fully-Fermented - Black Tea

    So generally drinking any kinds of tea give you the same kind of effect, its just what kind you like.
