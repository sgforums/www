    1) Be very conscious of what you are eating. Only eat things that have nutrition value. Junk highly processed but unnecessary food like soya sauce, chilli sauce, soft drinks etc.

    2) Cut out EVERYTHING that tastes sweet to you e.g. cakes, soft drinks, sauces, etc.

    3) Always start your meals with fibre or protein first before you eat the carbohydrates. That way you line you stomach with protein which will slow down the absorption of carbs. Eat more fibre than protein than carbs.

    Try this 3 steps for 1 month and you'll lose weight without extra effort.
