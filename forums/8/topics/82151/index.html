    I using D-link also, but it ain't wireless. If you type http:// into your browswer link bar, it should take you directly to your router configuration module.

    Click on the configurations tab and access "port redirection".

    "select index to set" 1
    "state" enabled
    "protocol" TCP
    "internet" well known port
    "start port" 6881
    "end port" 6999
    "local IP address"

    Click on add or modify or both, make sure your entry ends up in the frame directly above you. Click on apply in the frame above you and agree to restart the router to let the changes take place. Please be patient. Once completed, those ports used for P2P should be configured nicely.

    If the above doesn't apply to your router, sorry, I don't know what does.
