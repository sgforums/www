    Not a survey, Just want to know how many SgForumers are particular about their dining etiquettes.

    2) Do you chew on your food in a very "loud" manner?
    3) Do you play with your food and utensils?
    4) Do you shake and cross your legs?

    For me,

    1) I am very particular about the sound I make, I try to make very minimal sound which makes me eat very slowly, Except for soup and noodles where sipping sounds are unavoidable. And I do find that noise annoying.

    3) I am guilty of this, I always have leftovers which I play with. When waiting for food, Occasionally I will play with the utensils. lol

    4) Yes. Habit already..
