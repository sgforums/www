    Err I am not sure if you know,
    but now Mount Ophir you need to pay for a ranger.
    Every 10 pax = 1 Ranger. Who will accompany your whole trip.
    I can't remember the cost, but you can find out by calling their office.
    Which the person above me have kindly provided the contacts liao

    They also charge overnite camping charge, PLUS rubbish charge.

    Now you can only camp at one place IF you are going up from Sagil side (not asahan side) and that's the elephant pool... so .. well may be kind of crowded if you are going on a weekend.

    Sigh that's why a bit sian sian about going Mt Ophir liao..

    By the way, sell a bit of KOYOK, if you are interested in Adventure
    at the Toa Payoh Central CC, there is an Adventure Resource Centre
    if you are a passion card holder, you can borrow books from there
    if you are not, you can enter to browse.. its open till about.. 8.30 pm on weekdays
