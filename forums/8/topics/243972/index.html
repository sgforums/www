    Basically, every poly is almost the same.

    - People don't give a damn if you're walking/eating/studying alone.

    - You will encountour some chio-bus/yandaos from a particular school.

    - So many choices of food because there's so many canteens to choose from.

    - Lively environment when there's a huge event like open house or live concerts.

    - You will get to know many people but not necessarily get close to them.

    - Slack when there's no assignment.

    - Very stressful when assignment is due or main exams are coming.
