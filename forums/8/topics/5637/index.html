    The reason i asked is because i think most associate themselves with terms without knowing its implications or definition. from what i've read so far, i think most here can be classified as heartlanders. There are two prequisites a person MUST have before he/she terms himself a Cosmopolitan.
    1. A globally marketable skill - you can get a job anywhere in the world
    2. A sense of belonging in a strange place - you must feel at home even in a foreign land.
    (after all Cosmopolitan means 'universal citizen')

    Just because one enjoys a foreign land (its education system/lifestyle) doesn't make one a Cosmopolitan. After all everyone can appreciate a good thing when he sees one.
    The ability to travel abroad or experienced overseas university-life doesn't make one a Cosmopolitan. After all Heartlanders are affluent enough for overseas travel too.
