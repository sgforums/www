    Very easy 1

    U wana embed to your blog?

    It worked for me dono if it can work for you

    Got 1 way to cheat.No need even upload

    Click the 'Favourites' link at the top to see your playlist

    At the right panel theres a 'do You Know' box there

    Take note of your playlist id

    Eg: http://www.radioblogclub.com/fav/0/XXXXXX/0

    If wana embed into blog copy the code below into your blog template

    XXXXXX&colors=body:#CCFF00;border:#CC0099;button:#FF6633;player_text:#663366;playlist_text:#7C0572;" bgcolor="#F3EEF4">

    See those red colour XXXXXX ? It stands for your playlist ID

    for eg if u see yr playlist ID = 345246 then just replace the XXXXXX with 345246

    Thats al

    Ps:Oh ya and if u are well versed in html you can change your player colour..etc.Sadly im not well versed in html enough so hvae to stick t this given colour
