    In 1 min, John complete 1/45 of the task and Peter complete 1/30 of the task.

    Therefore in 1 min, both of them can complete (1/45 + 1/30) of the task = 1/18 of the task

    Hence, to complete the task, you need 18 mins. (1/18 * 18 = 1)
