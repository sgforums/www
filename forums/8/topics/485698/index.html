    Sometime ago, I went shopping for DVDs and I must admit, due to work, I haven't been shopping for several months/years. I got 3 rude shocks of my life.

    1. HMV moved again from S313 to Marina Sq?!
    2. All CD/VCD/DVD shops in Orchard road closed down suddenly?!
    3. Even the CD/VCD/DVD shops in my neighbourhood all closed down?!

    I looked back in nostalgia in the golden years when HMV was a mighty 3-storey department store in Heerens, when Borders was a huge area shop in Wheelock place, when Tower Records was still open in Suntec. I spent most of my weekends at those places, how much wonderful memories I had of them....

    I still buy and listen to or watch CDs, DVDs. I still have a big collection of old movies in mostly VCDs.

    I support original, I don't download songs/movies. For me, a song album consist of a CD disc, with nicely-designed cover art, photo booklet with song lyrics. Not a digital file with no cover art or photo booklet.

    I still buy books. I must have hard copy of books, not e-books. I hated digital files, every time my computer crash, everything is gone. Or the file gets corrupted.

    Hope there are more like me to support the original and the CD/VCD/DVD will not become items we could only see in a museum.
