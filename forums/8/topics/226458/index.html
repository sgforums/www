    i see a lot of people with a lot of misinformation

    if you are a singapore citizen, even if you have a passport with one day left on it, the singapore authorities will not stop you if you want to go.. you have a right of exit and entry into singapore.. even with an expired singapore passport, as long as you can show that you are a singapore citizen, you have right of entry into singapore...

    however, if you are going to another country, you have no right of entry into THEIR country.. you are a foreigner to them and because of that, you have to abide by their laws... if they insist on the 6 month validity, they have the right to not let you into their country... heck! even if you have a brand new passport, they have the right to not let you into their country.. and you have no say in it at all...

    the 6 months validity is some international law thing.. or something.. everyone adheres to it... if the immigration fellas the other side lets it slide, then it's their right, and your privilege...

    figure out the difference between rights and privileges... and it all falls into place..
