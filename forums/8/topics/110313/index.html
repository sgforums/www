Finally, it is completed!

  • RETARDED_MORON
    1 Jan 05, 02:23
    Here is a treat for you people. My new sampler is finally done! I named it "Scoffing Sausages" just to be in theme with this website's title; it does not have any particular meaning. During the December holidays, I dedicated quite some time into practising my tricks and tried to perfect them as best as I could. Who could have blamed me? I have got too much time to kill. I then got my new digital camera around my birthday (and I am grateful) so I proceeded with the filming. It took roughly about 10 days to complete shooting all the clips required (I actually took off days in between, but that is beside the point). I jumped around under the scorching sun, I danced around under the pouring rain and obviously gained quite a few bruises too. Perhaps this is an obsession, but this is what I enjoy doing. Also, I did not have a tripod so I used a rubbish bin as one. I am totally serious.

    Enjoy all my hard work here: ftp://tricks:tricks@67.18.198.26/Scoffing%20Sausages.zip

    I hope no one sees this as an attempt to boast or show off my ability (not that I have any), but I just look forward to general feedback. In martial arts communities, people often post videos of themselves to see where they stand (http://www.trickstutorials.com for instance), and I am just merely doing likewise.

    For those of you who took the trouble to watch my video, I am sincerely thankful. Do please give me feedback.

    Happy New Year!
