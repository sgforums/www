  • Rock^Star
    22 May 07, 15:40
    If you're in HCMC, just stay for the most 3 days.

    Try walking Dong Khoi Street - a very rustic and charming street with French, Chinese and Italian restaurants.

    Go Binh Tanh Market - lots of cheap bargains.

    Try walking in the shopping malls around Dong Khoi.

    Try walking around Pham Ngu Lao Street - it's a backpacker's haven there.

    Must go to Cu Chi tunnels - Vietnam War guerrilla tunnels

    Try sailing Mekong River and see the life there. Then you will be brought to an island to have a lazy lunch and rest.

    If you're more adventurous, you can even go further up north to Hanoi, where it's colder.

    Vietnam is so fun!
