Of Days Gone By

  • Enigma
    23 Oct 01, 02:12
    I used to dream of childhood days
    Of carefree times and innocence so pure.

    I used to dream of good old days
    Of simple games and joy so vibrant.

    I used to dream of yesteryears
    Of soothing tunes and days so serene.

    Of days gone by,
    Of childhood gone,
    Of days of old,
    Of years that passed.

    I now see the harshest days
    Of needless wars and threats so real.

    I now see the violent times
    Of pointless battles and toons so dark.

    I now see the trying moments
    Of senseless deaths and terror so true.

    Of days gone by,
    Of innocence so stained,
    Of times so missed,
    Of blood that's shed,
    Of what purpose I beseech thee,
    Of what goal I implore thee,
    Of what gains they hope to seek,
    Of what insanity that plights them so.

    Of days gone by,
    I really yearn.
    Of days gone by,
    I truly dream.
