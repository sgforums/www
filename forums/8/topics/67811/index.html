$10m annual Toto Hongbao Draw on Feb 5

  • FireIce
    27 Jan 04, 14:46
    Singapore's $10 million annual Toto Hongbao Draw is back, and it will take place on February 5 at Singapore Pools' office at Paradiz Centre.

    In a statement, Singapore Pools said this year's draw will be conducted at 6.30pm instead of the usual 5.30pm to allow customers more time to buy their tickets. The draw on that day will close at 6pm.

    Sales for the Toto Hongbao Draw will start on January 29 from 4.40pm onwards.

    Those buying in advance will also need to enter for the draw on February 2, which is Singapore Pools' usual Toto draw.

    For customers who only want to enter the Toto Hongbao Draw on February 5, they can only purchase their tickets from 4.40pm onwards on February 2.

    Toto Hongbao Draw tickets are available at all Singapore Pools branches and outlets and Singapore Turf Club branches.
