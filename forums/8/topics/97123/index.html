    Looks to me like a configuration error.

    Try checking the following:

    >> HDD jumper settings
    >> CMOS/BIOS settings
    >> Do you have other storage devices installed which may be competing for attention on start up?

    >> have you checked your SATA cables or its connection sockets?

    >> FAT or NTFS ?

    >> Installing HDD in an existing WIndows XP platform with SP2, or installing HDD on a fresh Setup?

    You likely have overlooked something along the way. Otherwise, look at your motherboard, are there discolored or leaky capacitors?
