    Love is blind....

    You can't say, "i will allow myself to love him." or say that "i will not allow myself to love him" If the feeling is there, it's there. Emotions are something that we humans are not able to control, but you do have a choice to decide whether or not to keep the relationship.

    Are able to take-on the obstacles that you might encounter? Do you have faith that one day things will turn out okie? Are you willing to sacrifice? Is he? Is it worth all the trouble?

    No one can tell whether a relationship will work or not until you give it a try...

    Be true to yourself and your partner, don't end up regretting...
