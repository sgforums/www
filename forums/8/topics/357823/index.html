    In psychology, we learn that there are 4 stages of sleep and REM slp is the 4th stage. People will go through a cycle of sleep from stage 1 to 4 back to 1 and each cycle is about 1.5hrs. As each slp cycle pass, stage 1 and 2 sleep is slowly reduce till in the end no more and stage 4 or REM sleep gradually increases. More REM sleep actually makes you less tired if never remember wrong.
