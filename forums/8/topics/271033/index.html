    I believe their reason of not wanting to refund you is because on how their computer systems are programmed.

    You did overpay them but at the same time you still remained a customer. So most companies usually just credit your account and carry on. Usually they send a refund cheque after you terminated services and still having a credit balance.

    You wanting them to refund you while still a customer usually requires manual override.... so i guess it is human error/non-action on this part.

    In my opinion, you should first try to write in a formal letter to Pacific Internet and see what happens from there.

    As for bringing it to small claims court, it will be quite contentious on few points because pacific net did not "cheat" you of money. They admit the overpayment and that they credited you the $.

    Also, the fact that you were overbilled and you paid up is not going to help in the courts
