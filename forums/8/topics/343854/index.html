    100 mean 100D. It is a wrong way saying what correction. I think it should be -1.00D. D=diopters, used to measure power.1/(focal length in meters).

    astigmatism, bit more complicated, 0.5 (or 50 as you say) is not corrected as most of the time the brain can adopt to it, -2.50 may need correction. it is also important to know what is your axis. http://www.lensdesign.ca/toric.htm
