    he's not a magician. he's someone who happens to know the secrets of tricks... such people are known in magic circles as "tough cookies" or "difficult specs". they are the people who aren't really used to not having the limelight on themselves, and feel intellectually threatened or insulted at a performance, hence the wanton exposure. going to a free exposure website or party trick archive and hitting control-C doesn't make one a magician.

    An act is more than just figuring out how something is done. it's about appreciating the amount of work that has gone into making the impossible seem real, and enjoying oneself at the same time. it's about respecting the magician. the person who has worked for and practiced the effects that he now presents to you, not some guy who figured out the secret through some cheapo party stunt webpage or got uncle Bob to teach him the 21 card trick.

    Sometimes knowing the secret to how something is done simply spoils the effect for yourself... worse if you hand out the secrets to people who may not even want to know... then you are simply spoiling it for everyone.

    yes i am a magician, yes i can prove it, and yes i am proud of it.
