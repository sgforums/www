    Er - about relationships, it's not always completely about feelings. Just like in your family, there are times that you feel like you absolute hate somebody. Do you then disown that family member ?

    In a relationship besides feelings and commitment, you must know whether the two of you can stay together for the long run. Tue, it's not easy to know in the first few months of the relationship, but after (donno) one or two years, you should know quite well. Even then, there will be times that you feel that you are drawing apart. It could even be over something very trivial. The question you need to ask yourself is "How much does this relationship mean to the two of us?". The two of you have to ask this question. If both of you feel that you still mean a lot to each other, then you will definitely try to work things out. If you base your stay/separate decisions on feelings, then I'm afraid you will never have any lasting relationships. Problem arises when this relatioship means a lot to one party and very little to another. This needs more time sorting out. Comparing men and women, women tend to commit to a relationship earlier on than a man. Actually, this could be what is happening in your relationship right now. If he hasn't "really" committed, then the two of you should talk about where you think this relationship is right now, and (more importantly) where it's going. Sometimes, one or two talking sessions clarifies a lot of things between two people.
