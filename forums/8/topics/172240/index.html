    I don't go to warehouse sales primarily because
    1. location very ulu
    2. brands don't appeal
    3. even if brands appeal, stock is usually blah

    However I like store sales.
    1. go during off-peak hours to avoid long Qs.
    2. bring whatever store cards you have
    3. wear light, tight & stretchy. skirts preferred.
    4. footwear should be comfortable slides - easy to slip in and out
    5. bring shoulder carry or sling bags, roomy is better so you can consolidate bags

    I don't shop with company, even less so during sale where you go with intent to buy and not have to accomodate your friends.
