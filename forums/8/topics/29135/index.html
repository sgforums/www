The Global Fuze

  • Heckler1983
    15 Nov 02, 21:09
    Its true. The world we live in is a global
    fuse. It has a threshold. This fuse will blow
    and lives will end. Fortunately, we do not know
    when this fuse would blow. This delusion or
    ignorance is indeed a bliss. Threats to this fuse
    come, in forms of environmental harms, terrorism,
    armed conflicts, inter-national conflicts. New forms
    of threats wld come. Fortunately again, we do not know
    when, and how they would come.

    So what is the unfortunate phenomenon? Humans.

    We were designed to wait. To wait for things to happen.
    To react to situations.

    We were programmed to survive.
    Self survival is still the most basic instinct any living human can have. The implications are great. This program in us builds
    selfishness, suspicion and jealousy, u name it.

    But what about living things like plants, animals, they were programmed to survive and worse, to multiply, even by underhand means.

    This leads to a second unfortunate characteristic of human beings - intelligence.

    Our intelligence, together with the survival instinct complicates our existence. It is this feature of us, that makes us the catalyst of progress and development, at the same time, the primary threat to the global fuse.

    As the world ages, as living things which co-exist with us
    on this resource-depleting planet adapt to human behaviour,
    They too would become complicated, with time, they would evolve, so would us.

    Exciting? No one can picture a 30th century human and cockroach. How would they evolve, both physically and mentally?

    What do we do now then? Wait for the global fuse to blow? But we do not even know how the fuse would blow. Would we
    cease to exist because of depleting resources or because of a nuclear winter, or simply by some unimaginable extra-terrestrial scenario.

    With so many new threats coming into place. This fuse can never be removed. Imaginary it might be but still, it will be there.
    Meanwhile, we shall continue with development, and disasters,
    man-made or natural. And pray, that this fuse does not blow
    as long as we are alive... fortunately, all of us have a limited life span.

    alvin.
    (think too much)
