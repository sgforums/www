    So Much Pride
    -----------------

    When you are young,
    Love your parents and
    Be humble
    Remember what they told you

    When you are young,
    Stay healthy and
    Be polite
    And learn to keep a promise

    When you are young,
    Always keep what is given to you
    Write and derive the wonder from your gifts
    And be humble if you have misplaced it
    Go and get it back from those whom you think
    Can help you,
    Because when you show your anger, it makes them laugh

    When you are young,
    Be humble
    Learn to tell right from wrong
    And know your role then
    You will know your borders

    When you are young,
    Be humble and
    Never let anger get the better of you
    Learn to recognise it
    And stay true to yourself

    When you are young,
    Save your money
    Buy the things only when it is necessary
    Every brand new thing that you wear
    Has the power to pull out the thorns
    from other people's sight

    When you are young
    Be humble and
    Talk to as many people as you know
    Because appearances can be deceptive
    The better you know about them, the
    Better you can understand their character

    When you are young
    Be humble and
    Never accept gifts from strangers
    Because no borders have been established
    But from friends, yes

    When you are young
    Be on the look out
    Be afraid of hurting another person
    Be afraid of hurting yourself
    Be afraid of letting others hurt you
    Be afraid of hurting many people

    When you are young,
    Be humble and
    Put your best effort in
    Everything that you do
    And be afraid to say that you feel tired.
