    Research on Indian history has shown that there were at least two assassination attempt plans on Gandhi before Godse which were never executed, and this film revolves around one of them. The attempt, the motivations, the historical background, and the reason why it was a patriotic Hindu who made the attempt. Another daring and controversial venture written , directed and acted out by India's most well known actor and director Kamal Hassan

    The film that kicks off in the year 1999, but has as its storytelling arena the period between 1946-1948 -- attempts by certain lesser known individuals to assassinate Gandhi , well ahead of Godse's eventual, and successful, attempt.The movie which tells the story not about Godse but of one of these unknown individuals begins with an 89-year-old with a fictional character, Saket Ram (Kamal Haasan) on his deathbed and his grandson by his side. From there, the movie goes into a flashback to the pre-Indian independence days of communal violence

    The Positive aspects of the film is the way the film has been shot.Dialogue and storytelling revolves around controversial political undertones , events and how lawlessness and disorder can change an ordinary man on the street to a murderer.. The film's art director Sabu Siril needs kudos for recreating the atmosphere of the late forties , has given more prominence to details like marriage customs, creating cars, coffee cups, and cycles of that period. Kamal Hassan is once again brilliant as an actor portraying the assasin. In many sequences, his portrayal of dilemma of the assasin is excellent. The period is well researched, but and given priority to projecting the emotional trauma of the period. The film comes up with a satisfying climax with cameo appearance of the infamous Godse eventually pulling the trigger Gandhi instead of the main protoganist Saket Ram who at the scene of the event has no connection to Godse or his plans


    "Hey Ram" is certainly a remarkable achievement by the innovative multi faceted artist , actor and director Kamalhassan. With this film, he has attempted to attract a large segment of film audience abroad.It was already a big hit in french/europe movie festivals and fans flew over to India to congragulate the actor kamalhassan..The film, was simultaneously made in Hindi, French and English, . The rich production values and the technical excellence that has gone into its making matches the best of foreign films
