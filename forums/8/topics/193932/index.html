    I play secondlife

    Its different compared to other MMORPG game because there isnt one defined goal in the game. Its basically a community based virtual world where you can explore worlds and meet friends.

    For the more ambitious, you could learn to build items and houses, create clothes and sell them for their currency (which can be converted to real money if you want)

    Anyway to answer your question

    1. Its not necessary for you to buy land as they arent free. There are alternatives if you want to have your own place such as renting or joining groups. Some groups allow you to have free land.

    2. When you find an interesting place, dont forget to 'create landmark' that way, you can teleport to the place without flying there again and again. Its like a bookmark

    3. Dont worry about it, its like real life - people want to move around after a while. Eventually you'll find people to talk to A good meeting place is 'Ahern' - thats where alot of people are, but it gets kind of laggy though, for the same reason.

    4. Take your time. It took me about one week just to get used to it
