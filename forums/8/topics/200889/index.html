    With regards to the current Israel-Lebanon conflict, Mercy Corps is now preparing to aid the victims of the war.

    As conflict between Lebanon and Israel causes thousands to flee their homes, Mercy Corps is preparing to meet humanitarian needs of vulnerable populations: the elderly, the disabled, children, and the poorest of the poor.

    Fighting has displaced "hundreds of thousands of people," the United Nations said Tuesday. Mercy Corps staff in Beirut hope to begin distributing relief items such as bedding and cookware within days.
