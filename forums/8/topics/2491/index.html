    18 Jun 02, 12:42
    CMC (combat medic course) not so xiong lah. Its 11 weeks, first 6 weeks is paramedic training, not too different from the service medic and SCDF training.

    Next 5 weeks is combat medic training, more emphasis on field casulty management, and is more physical.

    You are required to complete 10 successful intravenous cannulation, with a huge 18 gauge needle. Notice i said successful. Be prepared to receive more if your buddy is a bobo "shooter".

    Last week is a 4?? day field camp. Then u graduate.

    Overall, a decent place to be in, although facilities are old. When i was there, usually 1 night off every week. Its a stay in course. If you put you mind to you, and try to learn, you will benefit a lot from this course.
