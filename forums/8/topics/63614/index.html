    Nowadays, we hear a lot of defects on our HDB flats. Sound like the quality of new and upgraded flats are getting worse. Here is the list:

    - Upgraded Windows Falling Off (http://straitstimes.asia1.com.sg/singapore/story/0,4386,224208,00.html?)

    Upgrading of flats are usually done on older precinct. A lot of these flats are occupied by old ppl or lower income families.

    - Ceiling & Walls Cracking (http://newpaper.asia1.com.sg/top/story/0,4136,40376-1067961540,00.html)

    Anymore, feel free to add to the list.

    Some of the comments/feedbacks heard are:
    - newly built HDB flats have a lot of defects/inconvenience (design, quality, etc).

    - upgraded HDB flats have poor quality .

    - some ppl speculate that the poorer quality of newly and upgraded flats might be due to the gharmen tendering policy of getting the lowest price.
    low price will get low workmanship, material and quality.

    - Heard some ppl giving comments on new flats and avoid to buy new flats. Ppl mentioned that getting a resale old flat is better bcos:
    => older flats are built with better quality (seldom heard any problem)
    => older flats are more spacious (compared to the pigeon holes of new flats nowadays, ppl find it suffocating)

    Anyone hear more things or want to comments ? [/b]
