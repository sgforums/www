    Ok let me briefly explain on this...

    Q1. Are there any Buddhas before Sakyamuni Buddha?

    Ans: Yes, but how many before him..I can't really remember muz check the religious texts.

    Q2. Why is he more popular and reconigsed?

    Ans: To put it simply, he was the one who attained Nirvana and spread the Dharma(word). Before him, there were many astetics seeking many different waysto attain some form of enlightenment.

    Some are very extreme cases, where these people actually endure physical pain to do so. The Lord Buddha ent experince all of them from fasting to physical torment as well as psychological ones. Until one day underneath the bodhi tree was he able to attain Nirvavna. His way is known as the MIddle Path. He preaches that for anyone to attain Nirvana, a strong body is as equally important as a strong mind.

    From there onwards he went on to teach willing disciples. To us Buddhist, the Buddha is not our God, he is our Teacher. The Dharma is not a relic, it is our Guide. Nirvana is not to become a god, but to be released from the numerous sufferings in the Cycle of Existance. Sakyamuni Buddha is the first Buddha to really expound the Dharma to the people and founded Buddhism. That is why he is so "popular".

    Quote:

    Man: Who are you stranger, you seem very different from the rest.
    Are you a God?

    Buddha: No I am not.

    Man: An immortal?

    Buddha: No.

    Man: A saint?

    Buddha: No.

    Man: Then what are you?

    Buddha: I am merely Awakened    .

    Buddhist everywhere view Buddhism as a teaching, although most will classify it as a religion. Buddha statues serve only as a reminder that Nirvarna is possible for anyone. Right now people worship many other Buddhas too, so it is not correct to say that one is more popular than the other. It is only human nature to believein things that really work for them, so which Buddha or Bodhisattvas answers to thier prayers, they will naturally belive in that one who does.
