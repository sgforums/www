  • Darkness_hacker99
    17 Dec 07, 05:30
    Primary School:
    No school fees
    Standard miscellaneous fees: $5.50
    Second-tier miscellaneous fees*: $5.50

    Secondary School:
    School fees after subsidy: $5.00
    Standard miscellaneous fees: $8.00
    Second-tier miscellaneous fees*: $8.00
    Autonomous Schools collect Autonomous School Fees, ranging from $3 to $18 per month, on top of second-tier miscellaneous fees~
    Independent Schools charge a separate scale of fees. The range of school fees for local students (excluding miscellaneous fees) as at 2006 is $150- $255 per month^

    JC/MI:
    School fees after subsidy:$6.00
    Standard miscellaneous fees: $11.00
    Second-tier miscellaneous fees*: $11.00
    Independent Junior Colleges charge a separate scale of fees. The range of fees for local students(excluding miscellaneous fees) as at 2006 is $225- $400 per month

    Poly:
    $2,100/year for Singapore Citizen(with tuition grant)
    $2,310/year for international student(with tuition grant)
    Not included miscellaneous and supplementary fees.

    ITE:
    For ITE, pls refer http://www.ite.edu.sg/admission/ft/fees.htm

    Source http://www.moe.gov.sg/corporate/education_system.htm
