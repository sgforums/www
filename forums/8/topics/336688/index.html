    SINGAPORE: A man has been arrested for threatening bank officers with a parcel which he claimed would explode.

    Eyewitnesses told Channel NewsAsia that a man entered the UOB Branch at the City Plaza building in Tanjong Katong on Monday afternoon at about 3.40 pm.

    He was said to have threatened that a parcel he was holding would explode, if he wasn't given money.

    As a precaution, the second floor above the bank had to be evacuated.

    A guard at the bank managed to pin him down and he was later arrested and taken away by police. - CNA/vm
