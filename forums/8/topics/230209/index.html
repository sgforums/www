    Try this:

    I learnt this in secondary school by an Indian travelling book salesman.

    1. Get paper.

    2. Roll into a tube/ cigar.

    3. Put horizontal.

    4. Get scissor.

    5. Cut vertical, about one third of the tube/ cigar length, cut halfway, don't cut until fall off.

    6. Grab the small part you just cut.

    7. Cut it open, horizontal, opening the tube. (The small part).

    8. Grab it. Extend roll/ tube/ cigar out. (Lengthen it).

    9. Twist abit.

    10. Insert back.

    11. You have a flower.

    I hope the above information is clear for you. It is a very basic.

    If you need further clarification, just contact me at my office number.

    Meat Pao.
