    Ah...not exactly true. Coz u have to sign up for the SCV package at $99/mth for 2 years. And charge to cashline. That means that:

    1. You definitely have to pay the 2nd year's subscription fee of $60.
    2. If you have use of the maxTV and maxOnline, good...otherwise it's $1188 a year!
