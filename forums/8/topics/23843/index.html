    Dim Sum... my no. 1 love!!!

    Try Red Star near Havelock Road... very popular and famous among Cantonese and Hong Kongers. Order from push cart. Sometimes, you have to run after a cart to get the dim sum that you want -- Very Hong Kong style. Usually crowded on weekends, have to wait for tables.

    Another place is Fook Yuen at Changi Airport Terminal 2. It's quite expensive though. Good service, excellent quality of food. Fine dining style whereby waiters will take order and serve you the food.
