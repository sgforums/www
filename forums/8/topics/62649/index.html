    the thing about hula hoops is..

    you do not have to shake your hips vigorously to keep it up. do not just move your hips, instead make sure your body follows your hips in the motion smoothly. given the right technique, 19.85% of the hoop will be in contact with your body at any point of time..

    get off to a good start by giving the hoop a smooth push and start your hip shaking motion at the same time..also, find out if you are more comfortable with the hoop going in the clockwise or anti clockwise direction..

    have fun hulaing..
