    i can give u names and brands but they might not effectively treat ur blemishes

    blemishes occur due to different reason for different ppl......
    could be ur diet.....or the nature of ur skin......
    probably the brand of make-up tt u use is not suitable for ur skin type....

    firstly, u shd identify the problem....usually not easy......need alotta trial and error......
    try to find out ur skin type......oily or dry.....
    do u need more cleansing or do u need more revitalising or moisture.........?
    u may approach skin care counter and ask them to do analysis for u.......or u can go see doctor.....nowadays doctors oso have dermatological products for facial skin problems.....
    try not to those beauty centres as they will most likely "pscyho" u to take up their facial packages and buy their products........very expensive....

    start with cleansing and perhaps scrubbing or exfoliating.....
    if make up is compulsory....keep it light so tt ur skin can "breathe" and renew....dab oil and shine using blotters....
    rber to remove make-up completely....impurities will clog the pores.....
    u can oso buy spot cream to apply on the blemish

    when u have the blemishes under control, u can start using masks to remove dead skin and replenish moisture......

    if u can afford, u may approach beauty centres and sign up for basic facial packages or courses.....jus for the regular cleansing and maintaining good skin.....

    as for brand recommendation, im not sure of ur budget and skin type.....but i think Shiseido Pureness series is pretty good for blemish treatment and control....a set of cleanser, toner, moisturiser and spot cream would cost $100~$150....
    Clinique 1-2-3 steps is not bad too.....the consultant will help u to determine ur skin type and recommend the suitable combination.......
    but take note to ask for the non-oily moisturiser (light yellow).....the make-up remover (cream cleanser@ $33) is very effective in removing make-up and not making ur skin dry.....if u can afford, can get the "Repairwear"....very soothing and replenish moisture......
    SK-II "shen qi shui" and the mask with pitera oso quite popular and touted to be very effective....but all these shd be the later stage....after the blemishes are under control.....
