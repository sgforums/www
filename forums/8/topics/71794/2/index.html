    Not just read English books. For one, Read English newspapers. Make sure you understand the arguments the writers make in the editorial and commentary pages.

    Also, when doing compo, before you write, think about your outline first. What are you writing about? Is it a descriptive story? or a balancing of arguments type of story etc. jot your ideas in short point form so that they follow a logical sequence, then write the actual compo elaborating each point. it'll help your compo flow better and make sure you have enough points to write about.
