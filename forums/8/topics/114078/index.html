    Do not go to the debt collector unless you really have no choice.

    How the debt collector collect debt. He would normally negotiate and ask the person to pay up, giving him installment plans etc etc.

    You should know that the fees would be 30% of the debt owned.

    Assuming the amount is 15K. 30% of the debt owned is 4.5K.

    If the debt collector manage to collect back say 5K for you, he would still take 4.5K as his fees. You get back only $500.

    Your choice.
