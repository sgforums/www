  • yangguizi

    The idea is make it compulsory for every boy in a low income family to enter these military schools. These boys will have to study primary school all the way up to secondary school in this military school. Along the way, they will learn to be disciplined like soldiers do.

    After their O'levels, they will be streamed accordingly. Those with good acadamic results can proceed to enter JCs or Polys of their choice, and will be freed from their military commitment, until their full time national service. The same goes for those who excel in other areas and those with excellent disciplinary records.

    As for the rest, they will be made to sign on as army regulars.

    This idea will address some important issues.

    Juvenile crimes and secret society problems. Harsh but fact, these low income family children made up a large percentage of those who entered secret societies and becoming a menace to public safety, not to mention being a bad influece to other kids.

    Disciplining them from young in a military school will prevent these youths from straying. Training them to be a soldier from young means these people will not end up being jobless after their full time national service. This will give them no chance to do crimes and become bookies. Also, this means SAF will have a larger pool of well trained military personnels, which is good news for Singapore Defense and the other normal boys who can have easier NS.

    Also, the military schools for primary up till secondary can be applied to girls as well. This will address the problem of rising number of Ah Lians and teenage abortion rate.
