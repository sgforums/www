    Xiu xing...is a practice where you abandon the worldly desires and troubles to clear your mind so as to practice a higher understanding of the world and realms.

    Lun hui...is a cycle of sheng (birth), lau (old), bin (sickness), si (death). When you die this life and you didn't xiu xing or do enough good deeds or own people favours, you'll reborn back as animals or humans to repay what you've owned.

    In Buddhism, the main point is to get out of this realm of birth, old, sickness, death & to get reborn again.
