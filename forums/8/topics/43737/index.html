    PET'S DAY OUT 3Sat, 3 May 2003 & Sun, 4 May 2003
    4pm - 7pm

    Stadium Cove introduced and held the hugely successful Pet's Day Out! and Pet's Day Out 2 events last year. Back by popular demand, Stadium Cove will present another fun-filled Pet Event on 3rd & 4th May 2003.

    Join us for 2 full days of competitions, fun, games and activities for both pets and pet lovers at Stadium Cove! Enjoy shopping with your pets at Singapore's Largest Lifestyle Village with a wide variety of unique and exotic lifestyle items from ethnic and handmade jewellery, arts and crafts, antiques, collectibles.

    Stadium Cove will also feature vendors with interesting products and accessories specially for pets.

    Programme Highlights

    Other ActivitiesTime: All day from 4pm - 11pm

    Re-Homing Scheme ("a-dog-tion drive")
    Come meet some furry legged friends of all shapes and sizes! You may just find your new best pal.

    Distribution of Goodie BagsGrab your goodie bags at Pet's Day Out 3 with something for both you and your pet!

    ExhibitionsThis would mainly involve the display of panels from the various societies in educating the public about the proper care and treatment of stray dogs, cats and other various animal related issues.

    MicrochippingMicrochipping is a relatively new way of identifying pets. It's a permanent form of identification that gets implanted under the animal's skin administered much like a routine vaccination. We will invite a speaker to expound on the benefits of microchipping.
