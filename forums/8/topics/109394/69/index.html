    Oldenlandia
    (Herba Hedyotis Diffusae)



    What it is:
    This herb is thewhole herb of Hedyotisdiffusa (family Rubi-aceae) distributed over Guangxi,Guang-dong and Fujian provinces. It is collected in summer and autumn, dried insunlight or used fresh.

    Property:
    Bitter and sweet inflavour, cold in property, acting on the heart, liver andspleen channels.

    Effects:
    Clearingaway heat, detoxi-cating, promoting blood circulation to subdue swelling, andpromoting diuresis.

    Used for:
    1. Malignant tumours of digestivetract,such as those of the stomach, oesophagus, rectum and liver, also cancers of the lung and lymphatic system.
    (1)For overabundanceof noxious heat and accumulation of phlegm- dampness in carcinoma of gastro-intestinal tract, itis often used toge-ther with Chinese salvia, flavescent sophora root, coix seed, sanguisorba root, etc.
    (2) For fire caused by depression of liver qi and accumulation of noxious heat in hepatic carcinoma, it is often used with Oriental Worm-wood, capejasmine fruit, rheum barb root, etc.
    (3) For accumulation of noxious pathogen andstagnation of phlegm-heat in the lung in lung cancer and lymphosarcoma, it isused in combinationwith prunella spike,Zhejiang fritillary bulb, kombu, oyster shell, or with houttuynia, subpro-strate sophora root,climbing nightshadeand Asian puccoon root.
    (4) For accu-mulation of tumour toxin in carcinoma of the colon, it is often used with sargentgloryvine stem,five leaf akebia seed,flavescent root,sun-plant,climbing night-shade, etc.
    (5) Foracute non-lymphaticleukemia, it is often used with honeysuckle flower, black nightshade, subprostrate sophoraroot, Asian puccoonroot and isatis root.
    (6)For nasopharyngeal carcinoma, it is usedwith subprostrate sophora root, sun-plant, selaginella , honeysuckle flower, dried rehmannia and others. It inhibits various tumours cells in laboratory and raises immunity of body.

    2. Appendicitis,acute tonsillitis, laryngopharyngitis, pulmonary abscess, urinary infection, jaundice due to dampness, dysentery,carbuncle, furuncleand snake bite.

    Dosage and ad-ministration: 50-120g,decocted in water for an oral dose. Orfresh plant is smashedto get juice for external application.Injection, extract andtablet can be made of the herb.

    Precautions:
    It should be used cautiously for pregnant women.
