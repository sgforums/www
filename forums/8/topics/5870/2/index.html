    There are some misconception about fat people that are very hard (for some people) to dismiss. There is this tendency in society to see fat people as lazy, unhealthy, unfit, etc. It doesn't matter if the person is actually healthy and fit, as long as he/she is fat, he/she is, kind of, branded as lazy.
