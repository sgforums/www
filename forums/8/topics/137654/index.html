    Just do what everyone has been saying since time immemorial: eat a regular, well-balanced diet with plenty of exercise. As long as you stick to the Asian staple diet of rice or noodles, you should have enough energy for exercising.

    Don't listen to those Atkins dieters who say that you shouldn't take carbs. That's pure hogwash. If anything, the fastest way to lose weight is through aerobic exercises (jogging, swimming, etc), and you need carbs for those. The only thing which you should cut down on is fats - you're aiming to lose 4kg of fat, anyway.

    Just take it slow and easy. Don't bother running 10km in one day and having severe cramps in your legs for the rest of the week. It'll be far better to run 2.4 km every day instead.
