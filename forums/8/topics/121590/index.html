    Hmm...When I alighted at Marina Bay I was pesterd by a bunch of ppl frm Ping Xian BBQ. They push a leaflet into me and mfriends hand and started selling to me how good their food was and all that. The woman even addressed me as "lao ban" (They desperate for sales?) In the leaflet it stated 100++ dishes plus free LIVE prawn, ice cream...Big deal..zheng fa is also doing it. They even offered to bring us to the place in the mini Van they had. I politely declined. So took bus 400 to reach the place and alighted at the bus stop.

    I would have eaten at zheng fa if not for the fact FI said something about chong pang. So I decide to walk a few more steps and qiao qiao and chong pang. The Lady Boss quite nice. She offered me free each person 1 can of drink plus Ice cream considering it was my birthday although she really cant lower the price. The food quite good. I like the chicken curry. It was cooked in Indian style(Bitter Gourd in it no potatoes).
    But the deep fried stuff(french fries.. etc etc) was a bit of a dissapointment, quite lao hong liao so must toast them on the bbq plate. The raw food not bad lar Overall, I think the customer service was better than expected...at least better than zheng fa...the lady boss really makes an effort to go and interact with customers and collect feedback on the food althought they cant really compete with zheng fa over the live seafood part. So If you want good service go chong pang. If you want good seafood then go zheng fa
