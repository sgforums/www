    2. Only Singapore Citizens and Singapore Permanent Residents are eligible to apply for tickets for the Actual NDP on 9th August 2012 and the NDP Preview on 4th August 2012.

    3. Ticket applications start on 11th May 2012 and end on 20th May 2012. Tickets will NOT be given on a first-come-first-served basis. All valid applications will be put through the e-balloting system.

    4. Each applicant must opt for either tickets for the Actual NDP, or tickets for the NDP Preview.

    5. Each applicant must opt for 2, 4 or 6 tickets. The chances of obtaining 2 tickets will be higher than that of obtaining 4 tickets, and the chances of obtaining 4 tickets will be higher than that of obtaining 6 tickets.

    6. Each applicant is limited to ONE application regardless of the mode used. The modes of application are the NDP website, NDP Mobile App, AXS and SAM machines & SMS. If an applicant uses more than one mode to make an application, only the application that is made latest in time will be accepted for e-balloting.

    7. E-balloting results are final and no appeal will be entertained.

    8. Successful applicants will be notified via SMS between 1st June 2012 and 5th June 2012 through their preferred contact numbers provided during their applications. They are to reply via SMS to indicate their acceptance or rejection of the tickets, and this reply is to be sent by 5th Jun 2012, 2359hrs to the same number that notified them of their successful application. Failure by any applicant to reply on the acceptance or rejection of the tickets before the deadline of 5th June 2012, 2359hrs may result in the tickets being forfeited.

    9. Applicants may receive SMS notifications or advertisements authorized by the Executive Committee, NDP 2012.

    10. Tickets for the Actual NDP and the NDP Preview are not to be sold for any gain, monetary or otherwise.

    11. Each ticket admits one spectator to the parade. Each child (inclusive of infants) must hold a valid ticket to be admitted to the parade.

    Application for NDP tickets closes May 20

    SINGAPORE: The application for tickets to National Day Parade (NDP) 2012 will end on May 20.

    Applicants may still do so via SMS text application @ 9156-1557, the official NDP 2012 website/SAM/AXS stations and the "NDPOnTheGo" mobile application.

    They may choose to ballot for two, four or six tickets to either the NDP preview on August 4, or the actual NDP on August 9.

    The organisers said the fewer tickets they apply for, the higher their chances of success.

    Collection of NDP tickets from Friday

    SINGAPORE: Applicants who have been successful in balloting for tickets to this year's preview and National Day Parade can collect their tickets from Friday (June 8-17).

    They can do so between 10am and 10pm, at the Float @ Marina Bay.

    Successful applicants must bring along their original photo identification for verification.

    If they authorise someone else to collect the tickets, the authorised person must bring his or her original photo identification, a photocopy of the successful applicant's photo identification and a signed copy of the Letter of Authorisation, which can be downloaded from the "Reach Us" page on the official NDP 2012     website.

    Organisers have reminded applicants that NDP and preview tickets are not for sale.
