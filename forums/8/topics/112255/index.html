    Care bear,

    If you are looking for the lens for the frogs / romeo / juiliet etc sun glasses frame then cannot use 3rd party one. It take a good optician to advise u against it.

    This is becos of the curviture of the lens (to accomodate the frame) to produce the "wrap around" feel that is unique for oakleys sunglasses. None of the 3rd party lens can actually "fit" into these sort of frame. Even if they can do it (by bending the lens to fit the frame), the focal point will not be the same and therefore when u look through the sunglass from the corner of ur eyes, u will get dizzy...

    Onli if u r choosing frames like wired range then it is possible.

    Please check.. before going ahead.
