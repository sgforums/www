    where? the blood bank near SPH and also in NUH..

    fill in some forms about medical history, behaviour which may cause HIV or Hepatitis.. weighed...

    they prepare you, get blood pressure, blood count..

    then if okay, they lie you down and then tie a tournequet, inject a LA near the vein.. then they stick the needle in, and you are advised to open and close your hand over the squeeze toy...

    after they take a pint (about 5 to 10 minutes), they'll seal up the tube and take samples for testing...

    you are patched up and given a hot drink and some biscuits...

    if you are an averaged sized person, you should not feel anything at all.. just don't try to exercise for a day...
