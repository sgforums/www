    For those who've never heard of or been to The Red House, it's a confectionery.
    It's in an old shophouse along Katong. It's along the row of shophouses where you find Katong laksa. The owners have been there since I don't know when... They serve normal kopitiam drinks and cakes. You pick and choose your cakes from this glass cabinet which you'll see when you first step in the shop. To the right, a glass cabinet holds old time memorabilia.

    The tables and chairs are the old rickety kind. It's not air-con but somehow it's very cool inside. And the noise outside just seems to get magically cut off when you're sitting in there and some kind of peace transcends the atmosphere.
