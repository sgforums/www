    This morning I donated $20 to police very UNWILLINGLY at my place!

    I stayed here for more than 5 years and have been crossing the road, just like everybody else.

    Then suddenly, this man in blue stop me in my stride while talking to an ah peh. He kept talking to the ah peh while making me wait. I got fed up and asked 'what's the problem'. He stopped his conversation and said I jaywalked and asked why I don't use the overhead bridge which is just a few steps away.

    Dang! I was loss for words! I suddenly realise that there is an overhead 'white elephant' which everybody has so well-preserved by not using it. But it just doesn't make sense to utilise the 'white elephant' just to cross the 2 lanes. Everyone ignores the 'white elephant' and cross the 2 lanes, so much more efficient!

    Nevertheless, from the expression of the man in blue, I reckon he and his organization must not have been paid well. So I grudgingly fish out my ID and UNWILLINGLY donated $20.

    To think that 3 to 4 persons have been activated to solicit for involuntary donation at the same place, I reckon their year-end bonus really need a boost.

    2 hours later, I went back to the same spot, I saw at least 15 students and ah pehs and ah sohs crossing the road and waiting in between the 2 lanes. Where are the men in blue? Not a single one in sight.

    Conclusion: I tio suay!
