The chronicles of my 3 wisdom teeth

  • fymk
    5 Mar 05, 14:39
    Got my final 3 wisdom teeth removed - 2 upper and the right lower wisdom teeth.

    The two upper teeth, which I self considered were the most problematic ones . They were growing into my cheek and I had 2 holes of an ulcer on each side to show for it. The right lower wisdom tooth was non problematic - never did me any harm. It is amazing how murphy's law kicked in while under sedation.

    In my highly sedated state and locally anaesthetized mouth , I could feel the oral-maxillofacial surgeon digging and popping out the upper teeth like he was picking flowers .

    Finally the last right lower tooth , the seemingly least troublesome one , gave the surgeon a run for his money. He probed , he pushed , he scrapped - I woke up alert and frightened, from all the discomfort that was travelling through all my pain receptors.

    I heard him talking to the nurse : "Give me the damn drill"
    He drilled - I started screaming - the pressure was overwhelming .
    He stopped and looked at me
    I went " duh pen fal"( my clogged up version of damn painful)
    He said to his nurse " probably disorientated" and continued
    I went " nur nur , duhhhh pen fal" (no no , damn painful)
    He said " let's drill again" while I was screaming Nooo but it came out incoherently to the version of OoOoOOoOoo
    After a whole eternity of screaming and crying in the dental chair with poor reflexes ( no thanks to the sedation) - I could feel him digging into my jaw ( yes my jaw ). I could see him all shadowy under my tears - he seemed to be in what i call a heave ho posture - heaving and hoing like european sailors of the old and the pressure was building immensely ....then he said "finally , damn stubborn tooth you have there".
    I went like ."eeerrrwwww" (oooooouch)
    He gave me his image perfect pearly teeth of a smile, and said " you are all done and no more teeth to pop out anymore"
    I would have said "wow what perfect teeth you have " but under the current circumstances I would rather opt to take out his teeth with his drill without LA for HIM.

    Now I knew why my previous oral maxillofacial surgeon did my wisdom tooth removal under general anaesthesia - the first resulted in a hairline fracture of the jaw on the left in an unconscious state
    Now the second - another hairline facture of the lower jaw on the right in a conscious state .

    However I am an optimist. Now I am looking forward to slimming down a few more kilos because I am having liquid diet like some overzealous dieting anorexic supermodel with a difference - out of necessity. Come to think of it , I will be saving on my telephone bills for the next 2 weeks. NOW DAMN IT WHERE IS MY CODEINE!
