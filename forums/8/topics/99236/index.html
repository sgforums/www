  • rumie
    23 Oct 04, 13:47
    pimple can't be treated, but prevented...

    once it's out, you can wait for it to subside, pay someone who's professional to remove it (don't, NEVER go to cheap facial centre at neighbourhood), or do-it-yourself (if you want permanent scars).

    i wld advice go see a doctor, either a neighbourhood one, or those at the singapore skin centre (but have to get referral letter from polyclinic).

    when you do see the doc, try ask from him this antibiotic called either:
    1. doxycyclin (sp), OR
    2. minocyclin.

    both cost about $1 per pill, an i alway get a month supply especially during exams period where i know outbreak is coming.

    if it's not outbreak, then it MIGHT be personal hygiene, lifestyle, or you're using the wrong product!

    personal hygeine:
    obviously, NEVER EVER touch your face at ANY circumstances, unless you're wahsing your face, of cos. if it's itchy, use a tissue.

    lifestyle:
    sporty-type and non sporty type will need different approach. but whatver it is, dont wash it too often.
    eat plenty of fruits and stay healthy.
    i've read about some food that can aggravate acne growth, well, read a lot but all say different things so cant give advice on this.

    using products:
    some people are just blessed with good skin and they can se cheap products on their skin.

    but for people like us, we really can't save on $$$ for products.

    you'll need to trial and error and find the most suitable one. i wont recommend you, but i've tried for years b4 i realised what is suitable for me.

    that's all, hope it helps.
