    SPCA NEWS LETTER

    SPCA is organizing Animal Day at Parco Bugis Junction on 7-9 Oct (Fri - Sun) 2005. They need your help to :

    1. Man Stall
    2. Face/Hand Paint Ballon Sculpture
    3. Design Posters/Badge making
    4. Emcee or Perform on Stage
    5. Think of anything else to add interest to the event

    The run in 3 shifts:
    1st shift : 11 - 2 pm
    2nd shift : 2 - 6 pm
    3rd shift: 6 - 9 pm

    If you can help,: Send Email to Selina at feedback@spac.org.sg
    or call: 62875355 Ext 21

    They need a FIRM COMMITMENT. And you would be dealing with Members of the Puiblic.



    UPCOMING EVENTS
    If you want to be contacted for SPAC events, Email your name and Email Address to : spca_fundraising@yahoo.com.sg

    SPCA Dog Behaviour Seminars - by Angie Tan

    "How to get your Dog to Walk Without Pulling" on 30 July 11am -12 noon
    "Toilet training" on 27 August 11am -12 noon

    Fee : $20. Most of the proceeds will go to SPCA
    Place: SPCA, 31 Mount Vernon Road
    Semina for People only, don't bring dog.

    Wine Tasting in Aid of SPCA
    Wine Tasting at Tudor Ballroom - Goodwood Park Hotel 15 July 6.30 - 9.30 pm.
    Ticket at : $60 available at SPCA
    Email to spca_fundraising@yahoo.com.sg along with your contact number

    SPCA extending Opening Hours
    Every Last Friday of the Month starting 29 July - SPAC Pound will open from 10 am - 8.30 pm. Drop by to adopt or purchase SPAC merchandise.

    Xmas Card Designs
    SPCA needs new designs for 2005 Xmas Cards. Submi drawing/illustration.
    Call Baldev at 6287 5355 ext 22 during Office Hours. Deadline 15 July.

    SPCA Merchandise
    Sale of SPCA merchandis at Causeway point Shopping Center, L1 beside Burger King. 16-17 July from 12 - 7.30 pm

    SPCA Adopted Cats now Microchipped
    Adopted Cats do get lost. SPCA introduced micro chipping. Cost will be absorbed by SPCA so adoption price remains at : $50 for male cat and $65 for female cat. This is the fee for sterilization, vaccination and deworming.

    Fun Run
    SPCA will hold the 3rd annual fun run at Macritchie Reservoir Park on 21 August. Go to SPCA web site for details.
    http://www.spca.org.sg/
