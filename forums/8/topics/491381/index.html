My mum and I received a suspicious phone call in the evening

  • BukLau
    25 Feb 16, 04:42

    So there was this singtel prepaid card number that called my mum first, saying he was from singapore power and and he was checking on the gas pipes. He asked if my mum's address was still the same and other not important stuff. Then he called me, at this point I was not aware that he had already called my mum. So I picked up and he said he was from DHL and he needed to deliver goods for my father. He said my father's name. I wasn't surprised that DHL would need to do this as my father regularly orders goods for his job, so I cooperated with the person. The call went on for quite long as I was playing video games and wasn't paying attention, so the person had to repeat himself several times. But basically, the whole thign went like this. 

    I picked up, he said he was from dhl and he is delivering goods for <father's name>. He says out my father's phone number and ask if that is my father or not, for "Security purposes". I said yes that is correct. Then, he said out my house phone number and ask if that is correct or not. The number was correct but I just said the phone number not working already(at this point i already suspicious but I was focused on the game). Then, he asks if anyone is at home. I said I'm at home. He then ask what time I will be home until because the delivery will be late. I said I'm not going out today. Then he said out my FULL address, EXCEPT the unit number. Then he said please repeat your unit number for "security purposes". I thought that even if this was a scam of some sort, he already know so much information about my family, what harm can unit number do? So I just gave, he said thank you, my colleague will call you when he is there. Few hours later I foudn out he called my mum also, so I called back just for fun. Turns out the phone was off and the number is prepaid.

    He also called my mum's office desk number. Even I don't know my mum's office number.

     

    This thing really got me curious. He put in so much effort, he found out my address and phone numbers, but what can he do with this information? I don't see how knowing phone numbers and addresses can harm us. I'm not rich either so I doubt anyone would wanna rob/kidnap me.

     Anyone has any idea what this guy is planning or what he could do with the information he has?
