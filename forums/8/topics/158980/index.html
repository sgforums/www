    Power slide and hockey brake are 2 different ways of braking. Power slide involve turning the direction you face and immediately jam the leg infront perpendicular to your movement direction. You will be sliding backward for a while with the other leg bend. Hockey brake is a sudden sharp turn causing both foot to point perpendicular to your direction of movement and thus stopping almost immediately.

    T-brake is my most common brake since I took out the brake pad. Everytime my friend will goes "pick up all those wheel tracks, what a waste".

    Actually can download a lot of clips from the net that show tips and techni. Search for inline blade tips.
