Berg is good, but lacks speed and can be forced off the ball. u need someone like Chelsea's Desailly with Stam. Someone who's tigerish, fights for the ball and marks tightly. ideally, i would say that they need a libero. someone who can launch an attack from the back with an incisive pass like Beckanbauer in his heyday. pity...i can't think of anyone with the class in that position today.

    Man U's real strength is in raw speed. Man U is probably the fastest overall attacking side i've ever seen. other teams merely have some fast players like Owen, but Man U's entire frontline and many supporting players are all very fast. Think Cole, Yorke, Solsjkaer, Giggs and Scholes. however, the fragility of Man U's attack is the inability to hold up the ball. since Cantona left, who can hold up the ball and create room for the rest? Sheringham maybe, but while he's elegant, he hasn't the kind of control Cantona had.

    if u look at all the last 8 in the Champions League, u'll find that there's no real outstanding side today. Man U had some claim to being outstanding last season by outfighting Inter Milan and Juventus, but look at this season.

    Valencia lacks flair, so does Porto. Lazio is a good side, dunno what happened to them. Chelsea is very good and so is Bayern Munich. However, both show weaknesses in their own leagues. Real plays well, but hasn't showed me something outstanding yet.

    Barca is terribly lop-sided. Granted, they are probably the best attacking side now. with so much flair that Man U pales in comparision. but look at their league record. 17-5-10. what does this tell u? they can win, but they can't avoid a loss. not the hall mark of a champion. indeed, the moment they lose the spark in attack, they lose. it's a certainty. i really wondered how come no one noticed before they got whacked by Chelsea.

I seriously can't think of how Man U can fail to get 8 pts from the remaining games. against Southampton and Watford, that should be six pts. just draw against Sunderland and Spurs and you can even lose against Villa and Chelsea. and i seriously don't believe that Man U won't be able to beat at least 2 of the above-stated last 4 teams.
