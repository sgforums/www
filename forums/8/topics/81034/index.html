    1 Jun 04, 01:39
    lie flat first
    then stretch arms straight


    next, ur upper body and lower body flap up at the same time...making ur body shape like "V"
    try to use fingers to touch toes



    retract to position 1....
    repeat

    if u want more challenging one, when u retract, ur head and hands cannot touch ground, ankle oso cannot touch ground.....then go up again.....come down both "ends" dun touch the ground.......repeat.....

    bery effective
    stomach pain for 1 week after i did 5 sets of 20 V-sits (tts 100!)during my training session when i was in sec sch.....

    not suitable for ppl with back injuries hor....
