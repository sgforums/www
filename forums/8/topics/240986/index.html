Mobile phone sales man attitude

  • batmanbegins
    6 Mar 07, 20:02
    Hi there,

    Earlier this evening when I was at Pearl's Centre located at Chinatown, I came across a shop dealing with mobile phones. The shop name only displayed M1 exclusive distributor. The unit no is #01-20 which is located just opposite the lift. At first, I asked them to show me a Nokia model phone. I was told of the price and I told them let me consider.

    A while later, I went back to the shop and asked whether I could test the phone first before I make any decisions! To my horror, when I said I need to consider more, they get very impatient and started to say sarcastic remarks. They said that if I need to go to the ATM machine to get cash, they can accompany me and they even offert to get my atm card and withdraw on my behalf. I was astonished!

    How come nowadays that shops stoop to this in order to get transactions?! The sales staff are really rude and it really put me off! I wanted to post this to a higher level hoping to highlight this event.

    I believe that no matter which customers, they have every right in making decisions without anyone interfering! They behave like rogues salesman! If a customer feels that the model is not to his liking, the shop can't pester and make nasty remarks! He think that everyone is like a fool where he can intimidate! The next morning, I will be going down to the shop and ask for the boss! Hope that they can give me a satisfying answer!
