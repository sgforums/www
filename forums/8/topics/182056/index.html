    Harddisk failure can be due to multiple factors:
    1. Motor on harddisk burnt
    2. Hardware conflicts causing buffer on harddisk overflow
    3. PCB on harddisk faulty
    4. Motherboard on PC faulty on the IDE connector to harddisk
    5. DON'T GET MAXTOR AS THEIR HARDDISK IS NOT RELIABLE!
