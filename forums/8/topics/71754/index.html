    7 Week Competition, commencing Feb 16th 2004 -
    Deadline: April 5th 2004 24.00 GMT
    The Brief :-

    An established space Corporation 'XX Corp', has enjoyed many successful years in the mining industry, collecting bulk loads of common fuels and ores from local planets and systems. However the industry is changing and there is now an urgent need to mine rare minerals in hostile enviroments, their current droids are very unsuitable for these tasks and so they seek to commission new droids along these guidelines.

    The droid has to have 3 'modes' for the 3 essential tasks it has to perform. It has to be able to transform into each of the 'modes' very quickly ie parts folding in/out etc (think transformers!).

    The 3 modes are :-
    1. Fly (To move between mining areas and drop off loads to the carrier ships)
    2. Mine (Devices for cutting through rock and a very stable locked down stance is required)
    3. Attack (The new mining areas are hostile but the enemies are as yet unstudied so particular weapons cannot be specified at this point, therefore fire power choices are up to you, ground mobility is also needed in this mode)

    Required for final Submission: (full details with all specifications will follow near the end of the comp)

    1) Concept drawings - (everyone must plan their droid out in 2d before you start to build anything in 3d!)

    2) Your 3D file and all textures - The transformations must be working in the 3D File, preferably with custom animation sliders (we will locate some tutorials for this) If this option is not possible then the file must contain the animation of the transformations with regular keyframes (no more than 8 seconds per transformation)

    3) 3 Beauty renders - One for each of the 3 modes, anything goes in these really, composition to show all sides, graphics etc but use plain backgrounds only as this competition runs for only a short time period. We don't want people to be building scenes or backgrounds, just concentrate on the droid.

    4) 2 final beauty renders showing various stages of the transformations between modes, again feel free to include all bits and pieces, graphics, drawings etc.
    1st Render to show transformation frames from 'mode' 1 to 2
    2nd Render to show transformation frames from 'mode' 2 to 3
