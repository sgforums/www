    Merry Meet Agenda,

    I used to use Sebastian Crude Clay & Keune Forming Wax. Both can style very well, however no matter what kind of clay or wax, expensive or affordable all will cause Hair Loss in the long run. If you notice the similarity between both clay & wax, they are either oily or too drying & cannot be washed off in 1 wash suffocating your hair. For past many years, i been using Keune Sculpting Lotion & Hairspray only, can style well, easy to restyle morever can be washed off easily, less harsh on hair & scalp. I also use Rusk Smoother leave-in texturizing conditioner.

    I know many of the youngsters these days like to use Gatsby Wax coz its affordable however i feel its very harsh on the hair morever when you sweat & it touches your face, soon pimple & blemishes will appear on your face.

    Money of coz not an issue but hair is, once hair loss begin there is no remedy to it even if you want to spend alot of Money. Losing your crown of glory can be a sad case to people of both sexes.
