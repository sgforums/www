    Happiness is all in the mind. I can have everything go wrong for me one day and still feel happy, on the other hand, everything can be going fine, but I can feel sad. It is really how you view yourself, how you value yourself, and you realising what you really want, and in midst of it all, able to take success and failure on equal strides. I am proud to say that most of the time, I am a happy person, no matter how much problems I have at the back of my mind. When I get sunk into a period of depression, I am thankful that there are always people around to help me snap out of it, and I snap out of it not only for myself but also for my friends. Even at the absense of my friends, I would know that since my family won't want to see me this way, my friends won't want to see me this way, and even I won't want myself to be totally engulfed in depression which I deem meaningless and unproductive. So why not smile your way through it and be happy and thankful about everything you have??? Quite longwinded i can see, heheheh
