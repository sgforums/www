    Her housing appliances were always haywire, her banknotes always displaced and her books were always disappearing, only to reappear on the same place days after frantically searching for it.

    It was not until 2 months into her stay in the apartment did she began to feel the heavy presence in her room which was disturbing her. She could not fall asleep like she could previously and had this feeling of being monitored closely and watched. She judiciously claimed that the person watching her is on the wall above her clock, which was directly opposite to her bed.

    At night when she was sleeping, she will almost always be awakened by a swirling noise in the water. She would wake up and see this black spot on the wall which grows bigger and bigger and would then spin in circular motion. She could hear disturbing voices of evil spirits and devils chanting in her ears. At times she would even feel her feet burning and though this may be quite a few encounters, she actually see her feet on fire. At this occassion, she began to scream, but her neighbors and her husband did not respond. They were so sound asleep as though the evil spirit might have cast a spell on them.

    She would be pressured both from the visions and from the anxiety. Her heart would weaken with every episode and she would not be able to sleep and concentrate the next morning. This would go on for several weeks, until towards the final day before the devil was exorcised from the house, she came to the devil face to face. She was threatened with her life and she could feel her soul being taken out from her body (feeling of a rollercoaster making a sharp descent). She fought hard and remembered the Bible verses and the Lord's prayer she was taught in church. She recited it several times, but with each time the pressure got harder and the noises got louder and much more irritating. She could sense more evil spirits surrounding her and making her go crazy. She would sweat buckets and her arms and legs would be paralyzed (no, not sleeping paralysis). She would be in a sitting position but she just can't move her arms and legs. It was as if she was being held down.

    She cried out loud that very final night and shouted out the prayer she was taught several times. The Devil at first went against her by slapping her and pulling her forwards, but towards the end, they decided to leave her alone.

    Daybreak, she called up the priest from the church she was attending, and her house was then blessed. There were still occassions when her electrical appliances still would auto-shut and on... however, she moved out the very following year.

    TOPIC: LICKING

    A young girl lived at home with her mother, father and her much loved pet dog.

    Being 16 years old her parents decided that she was now old enough to be left alone without a minder while they went out for the evening. Although there was some concern about leaving a young girl in a house all by herself, they knew she was sensible and would behave. And besides, she did have the pet dog to look after her and keep her company.

    The parents left, leaving phone numbers and supplies for the night. The girl was thrilled to be spending her first night alone and mainly watched TV until 11.00pm.

    Deciding she was tired she moved upstairs to her comfy bedroom to retire for the night. Her trusty dog following her all the time.

    After being asleep for a short while she awoke to a dripping noise coming from the bathroom. Not afraid, but curious as to what the noise was she lowered her hand down beside her bed to gain a little comfort from the dog. She felt the warmth of his soft tongue as he licked her hand, showing her that he was alright. Feeling assured she drifted off to sleep once more.

    Waking once again to the sounds of the dripping tap, or whatever it was, in the bathroom she instinctively dropped her hand down to pet her dog. Once again her dog offered up the loyal companionship that only a much trusted and loved pet can, and licked her hand and she once again fell asleep.

    For the last time she woke up again, that annoying noise was still going, she reached for her dog but found that he wasn't there. Wondering where her parents were at this time of night (it was about 3.30am by this time) and looking for her dog she walked out into the bathroom.

    A horrifying sight met her, the dog had been killed and was making the dripping noise, as blood slowly fell and pooled on the floor below. Screaming, she ran back through the house and attempted to call the police. The phone was dead, and she turned to suddenly see her parents also killed behind her. She looked down at a note written in blood, it read:

    "Humans Like To Lick Too My Dear".

    She then runs madly for the stairs only to see a black figure waiting with open arms to catch her.

    TOPIC: JAPANESE OCCUPATION, PENANG SCHOOL, TOILET SHRIEK

    There was a particular group or you may call it squad who were very likeable, since they were always helping people. Theirs is a group which bonds from friendship. The five stick together like glue, and their names (not real names) were Amanda, Betty, Cathy, Dorothy and Esther.

    Night fall and after the campfire, they got into their designated classrooms and put in for the night. There were about 15-16 girls in one classroom and about 12 classrooms at the east wing and the west wing were each filled up, to give you a rough idea of how big the attendance is.

    At about midnight, Esther decided to go to the toilet, but she was scared of the dark and were not allowed to turn on the lights since there was a strict ruling about lights out. Hence Esther grabbed her pen torch and awaken her four other friends who were sleeping next to her to accompany her. What they saw will forever stick in their imagination for as long as they live on.
