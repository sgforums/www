Inside their house, directly opposite from their entrance to the house, shone a green eerie light they have never seen before in their house. They were sure that there were no green light installed in their house before. Staying there for more than a year, and confident about their surroundings, they shook in fear and got more nervous. Spite all that, the place was dark and the switch to the lights were near the green light. There was nothing much they could do but to walk towards the light.

    Both knelt down in prayer as they thought that it was the existence of evil spirits. They prayed and prayed for half an hour, hoping that the light would disappear, but the light just kept right on shining. It did not flickered, it did not get dimmer or any brighter, it was just there. Both became weak on their legs and hands and started to jitter. What on earth could the green light be and what did it want from them? How did it get in in the first place and what will it do to them? Their prayers were still not answered and they finally decided to approach the light.

    Being the courageous of the two, Fatt decided to slowly check it out by crawling on his knees. Both of them crept towards the light, and Huang was still praying the Lord's prayer as they get nearer and nearer. There was a door leading to the source of the light. Opening the door, they sighed a big relief, as they saw a green translucent wrapper paper wrapped against the yellow bulb. It must have been due to the wind where the window was opened...

    Both of them sat there and started laughing at each other...
