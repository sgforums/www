    Steps in using IRC to download mp3 music

    1. connect to undernet, available in the list of channels already

    2. join mp3 channels such as #mp3cafe; #mp3oldies. You can find more channels by using keywords when listing channels

    3. type '@find [keywords of the song or artiste] e.g @find santana black magic woman

    4. a lot of the ircians there will 'private message' you. Cut and paste the message into the main channel. You'll be in queue to download the mp3.

    5. Wait until they send you the file. Careful of vbs files people try to send you. They are viruses.
