    PFC (higher pay) is given to soldiers with good performance. In an infantry section, there's a section commander (3SG) and a section 2IC. This 2IC is a lance corporal. You cannot promote anyone with good performance to LCP, as LCP is for those with more "significant" and "important" appointment, like signaller, MG gunner etc. So what SAF can do is to promote a PTE to a PFC.

    Anyway, the rank for PFC is quite unique, an inverted V, dun you think so?
