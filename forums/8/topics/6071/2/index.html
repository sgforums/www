    Cyberlove? I am still waiting for someone to prove it to me personally.*LOL* Cyberflirting? yes. Cyberlove? That's a no-no for me. I dun really believe in it yet. Yes, I have seen examples, but with my character, it is almost impossible. To me, falling in love with someone you have never seen in real life, or truly know irks me. I have met people on the net. And their personality is most of the time, a big difference.

    I am not saying that everyone is faking themself behind the screen. It's just that to me, the virtual world like it's namesake, is unreal. Eg. I have been emailing this gal from Sweden, yes, we got along quite well, we have mutual topics to discuss, I sent her cards on holidays, and sent her gifts for Valentine and her Bday. But that doesn't mean I am really into chasing her, to me it's more like a friendly kinda thing. I too do not have hope of her being whom she express herself to be when I met up with her, just so because, I think it is too unreal. To commit to someone I have yet even seen, is truly uncomprehensible to the logical me.

    But if say, I meet up with her, and then we fall in love, now that's a different story, some of you may still call it cyberlove. But to me, love only begins when it started, not before that. I claim to be a romantic perosnal, but I believe in real life romance, not romancing my Monitor. I guess I am just not romantic enough to believe in cyberlove, yet.
