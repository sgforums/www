    anger management.

    first u mus be able to recognise what triggers ur anger, and the feelings and sensations u experience once anger is triggered.

    if u are able to recognise them, then the next time u experience those feelings u can put a stop to them.

    how? there r several ways.

    u mus first understand and accept tt there is nothing wrong with being angry. the important thing is wat u do when u r angry. and that anger is external of you, hence u haf the ability to control it. the alternative is o cos to give up control to anger, which will then play u around.

    when u sense anger building up, try these:

    1. deep breathing
    2. slow urself down, almost deliberately, like stopping urself on the tracks.
    3. affirmation: keep telling urself tt u r under control and able to control ur anger.
    4. wear a rubber band, and snap it hard on urself - almost like shouting "STOP" to urself. of cos variations of these work too.
