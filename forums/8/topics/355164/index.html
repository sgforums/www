    the menu is not extensive due to budget and having to feed varied no. of kids from different races

    Childcare centres have different types
    playgroup, half day childcare, full day childcare
    so the kids eat diff things
    and oso depends on the auntie's mood and ingredients for the day
    usually something easy to prepare, easy to eat and easy to clean up

    breakfast is usually biscuits, sandwiches with butter/jam/kaya, buns with milo, horlick
    same for tea-break (for playgroups)
    lunch is usually fried beehoon, fried noodles, fried rice....or rice with vege, eggs, hotdogs, luncheon meat, fish fillet, fishballs, sotong balls,
    tea-break sometimes same as breakfast or if there is a good budget, u can expect paus, omelettes, ham/egg/tuna sandwich, carrot cake....

    no dinner provided at childcare centres.
    parents shd pick up the kids to go home and have dinner.....
