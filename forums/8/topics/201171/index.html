    Before you are quick to dismiss that guys or gals alike who surfed forum are generally losers, the 'unwanted' or even weirdos, let me tell you that I do have a fair share of gal friends who got hitched online and are now happily married. They are expecting a child next year. Similar articles of couple who met online have also proven that relationship works because of the uniqueness of forum environment where mutual understanding starts before prior to the process directed towards friendship-relationship-marriageship.

    In the process of sharing thoughts and ideas in a forum environment, somehow people with similar interests can gel up well or perhape these 'thoughts' or 'ideas' that you had shared in the forum are actully what the single and available forumites look for in their ideal partner-to-be. You can't stop cupid from shooting the love arrow can you?

    Certainly I cannot lump it all up and say that all users of forums are Mr Nice or Ms Kind. The onus is to be careful in your dealing and get to know the someone deeper before meeting up, preferably in group. So the next time before you jump to conclusion and formulate a biased perception on anyone online, try looking it from another angle and do not deny the person of a fair trial.
