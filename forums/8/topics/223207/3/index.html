    The idea is to let our cash earn more for us instead of us working solely for $. There is a limit to how much each individual of us can earn a month if we are drawing a salary from a job (which most of us are). Therefore, it make sense for us to protect ourselves as the single unit of production of income against death, or worse disability through insurance. Savings account interest rate is pathetic now at the rate of 0.25% P.A in my bank and thus we be losing out to inflation every year if we do not at least invest to beat inflation rate which stands around 2%-2.5% a year. Therefore, I feel that we should invest wisely and take calculated risk to enhance our wealth. As a rule of thumb, younger people should consider taking more risk than older people as time is on their side and they probably have less committment weighing on them. There are a lot of ways to do this but the worse thing is to sit around and let ourselves be consumed by rising taxes, GST and such without doing anything at all.

    Do talk to your advisers or bankers. They will be able to help you plan your needs carefully. Many people have little money for retirement because they did not plan well enough when they were young and at the peak of their earning power.
