    Cdi = (Cl^2) / (pi * AR * e)

    CDi=induced drag coefficient
    Cl=lift coefficient
    AR=Aspect Ratio
    e=efficiency factor

    AR = s^2 / A
    s=wing span
    A=Area of wing

    AR = s / c
    c=chord
