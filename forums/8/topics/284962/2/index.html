    Suddenly, one day the Atlanta police called him up and threatened him with legal action on his 'vandalism' of school corridor. The police officer in charge, LT Howard threatened him and made sacratic remarks. The evidence was a scratched video tape. Lt Howard kept pressuring him to resign, saying that the Emory will keep mute about his resignation. Thinking that he was going over to Georgia Tech as Dean, Sonnerfeld resigned.

    Soon, Emory President Chace started to leak the information to the press. He also called up Georgia Tech President to update him about the situation. In the end, Sonnerfeld deanship in Georgia Tech, subject to approval, was annulled.

With ta Atlanta Democrat State Representative(forget her name) and CEOs help, Sonnerfeld sued Emory to court.
