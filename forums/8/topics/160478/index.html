Review bus fare concession hours for senior citizens

  • dragg
    1 Nov 05, 13:26
    extracted from ST forums.

    I understand that the two transport operators have two different timings for concession rates to senior citizens.

    It's time the Public Transport Council recognises the importance of senior citizens and review the timings. If possible there should be no restrictions at all. This is necessary for the following reasons:

    1. Why penalise senior citizens who are still working? They are not entitled to the concessions outside those hours.

    2. With the need for family bonds, senior citizens may have to travel beyond the concession time after gathering with their children.

    More should be done to recognise our senior citizens. Without them Singapore will not be what she is today.

    David Soh
