  • Flight Cadet Officer
    22 Dec 02, 13:47
    A Aviation Class 2 Medical cert is required. Eyesights no more than 100 degees correctable 6/6. Height no lesser than 1.62m in order to reach the rudder pedals. Licence required in respective order of inital phase to final

    LICENCE

    Student Pilots Licence (SPL) - min. 10 hrs solo flight time, 5 hrs Instrument Flight Rules (IFR)

    Private Pilots Licence - Restricted (R) - Evaluation for final SPL phase. Theory requirements: Radio Telephony Theory, Radio Telephony Practical, Airlaw, Human Factors, Meteorology, Navigation, Air Rating. All these papers are required to pass.

    Private Pilots Licence (PPL) - 25 hrs Navigation flights with range 50 nm minimum. 7 hrs solo navigation flights with range of 50 NM. Final evaluation: Flight test, theory papers: advance theory consist 4 hrs test duration.

    Commercial Pilots Licence (CPL) - Before trainees can sit for the test, requirements are as follow: 10 hrs IFR, 70 hrs Pilot In Command (PIC) and 20 of which must be Cross Country, Total flight hrs required is 200 hrs.

    Airline Transport Pilots Licence (ATPL) - 1500 hrs Flight time required before trainees can sit for the 7 theory papers based on Airbus A320 Avionics, Glass Cockpit, Technical Specs, Engines Operational limitation and functions, Aircrew Management etc. (Class 1 Medical Cert required.

    Successful completion of the above licence would than enable pilots to resume training on Learjet 45s to gain Command time as well as Multi-Engine experience and Glass Cockpit Avionics with duration of 3 -5 months.
    Thereafter simulator training on the assigned aircraft such as A320, A340-300, 777-200ER/300, 747-400/412. Upon Completion, Instructors will conduct an actual flight on the assigned aircraft without passengers onboard.

    Upon Successful Completion of Simulator Check flight, trainees are now Commissioned as Second Officers and assigned to their respect aircraft in which they are rated on. Within few years of service, Second Officers will than be Commissioned as First Officers and than to Senior First Officers. To gain Captaincy, SFO are required to gain 7000 Flight hrs in which 3000 must be PIC based on Widd-Body Aircraft and Glass Cockpit Avionics.

    Captains are required to undergo Recurrent Check rides on simulators every 6 months to ensure Airline Standard Operating Procedures are still maintained, as well as medical checks.
