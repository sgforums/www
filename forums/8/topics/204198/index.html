    A few years ago when I just started learning piano I compared the rates between private and school and to me I found that private tutors:

    -are cheaper
    -more flexible schedule (can cancel here cancel there, shift around), school you miss one lesson have to usually produce MC, not always have makeup lesson
    -theory do at own pace

    etc.

    For me I found a younger teacher = less experienced but also cheaper, and probably more flexible than those teach many years already.
