    Been to Seoul numerous times, but not Jeju. OK la....looks and feels like Taipei to me. Grey, a bit aggressive/rude and lots of language problems. Shopping, transportation and food are affordable, but hotels are a killer.

    Before you go, check the weather to see what clothes to bring. It can get really cold and dry in Korea.

    I know many are influenced by TV and music. I personally have never watched a single Korean movie, whatever....but please remember that movies and real life can be two totally different worlds hor? The only place in Korea I've seen fashionable people was at MyongDong (the city centre of Seoul).
