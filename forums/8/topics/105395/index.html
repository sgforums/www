    Best to get some work experience and earn some money along the way.
    With all the time you have, chances are you will be bored if you spend all your time at home watching TV or going out with friends for movies and to hang out.

    Find some temp jobs that allow you to work in the mornings and afternoons.
    You will still have time during the night and weekends to enjoy yourself.

    Plus with the additional income, your choices of R&R will definitely be wider.
    On the other hand, maybe you might also want to consider saving / investing some of your money. In this day and age, you never know when you might need the cash next time.
