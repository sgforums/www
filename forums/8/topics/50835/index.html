    A crossed cheque (two parallel lines). The cheque can be banked into an account by anyone

    A crossed cheque with the words NOT TRANSFERABLE. The cheque must be banked into an account with exactly the same name as the name on the cheque

    A crossed cheque with the words ACCOUNT PAYEE (or A/C PAYEE). The cheque must be banked into an account with exactly the same name as the name on the cheque

    A crossed cheque with the words NOT NEGOTIABLE. The cheque must be banked into an account in the name of the payee, or their nominee

    An uncrossed cheque (also known as a cash cheque). The cheque can be cashed by anyone. It does not have to be banked
