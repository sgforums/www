Hurting someone I love

  • Luf
    23 Apr 00, 20:26
    Recently I did something very idiotic, I confess to someone I love that I feel hurt while doing something for her. That love is painful and I wish someone would dig out my heart so that it would stop the ache. That I thought of giving her the thing I done and leave her. I wrote her a letter telling her all these. It was written on the spur of the moment. Actually I had waited outside her house for 6 hours till 1am, then I decided to go back after a stranger persuade me to leave. It was my fault in waiting for her, coz I didn't check with her if she would be around. I wanted badly to give it to her personally. She was really happy to receive it, but after reading what I wrote to her, everything just crashed. She told me she understand how I feel and she do not want to be the source of my hurt. She do not want to be a burden to me. That I am very unsure of myself. From the bottom of my heart, I do love her deeply, but sometimes, I just can't control myself and do some stupid things, hurting myself in the process. I guess I deserve it but the last straw is when I confess to her my hurt feeling. I knew something like this might happen, that she will be hurt if she knows, and yet I have to let her know. The last thing I want is to hurt her and yet I just did it. I felt like a bastard.

    If only I could turn back time, I would not have done what I done. All is too late, now she want me to take some time out and think on my own. I had lost the inspiration in my life, the source of my joy.

    After doing some soul searching, I found myself, a rational self, and I knew what I want, it's her.

    I learnt that love is not everything in a relationship. It include love, care, concern, patient, understanding and communication. The hardest to achieve is understanding, misunderstanding is one major source of breakup, and to learn this part might require a life time effort. Yet it's a magical thing, something like research, knowing something new, and it's all the better becoz you are learning about someone you love. It's becoz I do not understand her which causes me to feel hurt.

    I will do what I can to convince her that I had sort out my thoughts, and I am certain of my feeling for her. I want her to be my one and only love.
