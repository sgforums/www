    the brackets glued to the teeth doesnt move your teeth. the brackets is to hold a flexible arch wire which moves your teeth by applying light pressure. the rubber bands hold the wire in place onto the brackets. your teeth may feel sore after putting on the braces, but not to the extent of needing anesthesia. its not like you are undergoing tremendous pain like an operation.
