    Normally, I would go shopping, and find new deals.

    Also must train the vocals and health. And actually, its not a long break if you must know.

    Before the recording of the albums, we need at least half a year or 10months of planning prior to the recording.

    There are still music videos that need to be done. And must learn the lyrics and melody at the same time.

    Also must choose what kind of theme the album will be. And choosing the costumes and outfit. Waiting for initial plans is such a torture.
