    usually fillers are necessary in anime because anime are afterall based on the manga.

    usually the author will take 2 to 3 months (assuming at his fastest pace) to come out with 1 book. but for anime, every week must come up with an episode. so they must let the manga be many many chapters ahead before they can do the anime.

    secondly, the author will of course must prefer the fans to buy his manga since he spent so much time and effort drawing them. so if the anime follow very closely as the manga, everyone will just wait to watch the anime instead of buying the manga.

    lastly, most of time the filler storyline will suck big time cos the author will keep the best ideas for his manga.
