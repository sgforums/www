    my chicken rice receipe is the best in singapore...dont believe try it!

    ingredients:
    - 1 whole chicken
    - 1 garlic
    - 1 ginger
    - 2 stalks of pandan leaves

    preparation method:

    1. rub some oil and salt on the chicken and steam it in a pot of water. steam for about 30 mins until chicken fully cooked

    2. collect the flavoured chicken stock (water) and use it to steam rice in the rice cooker. add in the ginger slices, pieces of garlic, pandan leaves. cook until you can smell the chicken rice aroma..

    3. cut the chicken into slices, add cucumber slices, tomatoes, spring onions

    4. serve with chilli and minced ginger/black sauce
