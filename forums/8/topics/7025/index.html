    Hi,

    With the Nokia 8850, you do not need any cables as long as you have a notebook computer with an Infra-Red port.

    The infra-red communication allows your phone to be used as a wireless modem. You can dial into an IASP (internet access service provider) by installing the drivers on the CD provided for your phone as well as the Nokia DataSuite. The cable which is said to cost $150 is more likely to be the NDS 2/3 kit which is sold seperately for the Nokia 51xx/61xx series phones. Without the cable or datasuite, the phone cannot hook up via infrared to a notebook computer.

    You cannot delete the ringtone from your phone unfortunately, but you can overwrite it with another downloaded or self-created tone.

    Hope this helps.
