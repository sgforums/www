    Winner of numerous awards in best picture and foreign language film category and dubbed into french and english , based on a true story , this movie revolves around a love story set against the political backdrop of the India-kashmir border dispute. A top military encrytologist gets kidnapped by a group of renegade terrorists in exchange for the release of an imprisoned mecenary leader, the wife of the encrytologist , is determined to make sure her husband is set free. This landmark movie in Indian cinema history features top notch cinematography, lighting and exotic locales ever seen in a movie and a groundbreaking soundtrack. The film is a mixture of heart-warming melodrama and an insight into the ideology and reasons behind terrorism.The film also showed the rest of India how far Indian films had gone technologically, and in their use of story telling.Incidentally it also marked the career of the then debuntant music composer AR Rahman
