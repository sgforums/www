    Originally posted by Devil_World:
    Any ex-students could give an insight to the details/structure of study?
    Part time and full time EEE, NTU:

    Its the same bachelor degree u got as those in full time.
    FULL TIME JC students need to study more modules than poly students. SO Full time JC students study 4 years ( include Industrial Attachment, IA -- 6 months ), while poly student which they called "direct entry" student 3 years ( include IA ). Note, for direct entry students, their ACTUAL course in NTU is 2.5 years ( U not in sch during IA, ok? )
    So, Part time students actually study 5 years. The part time students will take HALF the load of the full time students every semester, so 2.5 years become 5 years. Part time student dun need to take IA ( u are working anyway ~)

    AND, FULL TIME JC students need to take alot of modules like managements, HR, science etc . DIRECT ENTRY students only choose TWO of Electives modules ( like those management, HR ,sciense stuff ). PART TIME student dun need to take ANY general electives modules ( like those management , HR, science stuff)
