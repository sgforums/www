    Whether you want to tap your ezlink card or not when alighting depends on 3 types of cases:-

    1. You are paying concession fares.

    2. You are on a feeder or townlink bus.

    3. You are travelling more than 29 fare stages on trunk and express services.

    4. You are alighting at the end of the journey. (ie. interchange or terminal)

    In all the 4 cases, it's ok if you do not tap, cause the amount deducted will be the same. But, to make it a good habit, please tap and tap only when you are alighting.
