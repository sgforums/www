    Skeujin must be referring to Patpong, the tourist area - with pasar malam, go-go bars, live shows, cheap and fake watches, LV bags, DVDs. Prices are inflated though, need to bargain quite a bit. Dun be afraid to walk away - there are loads of the same stalls selling the same stuff, all open until 1am at least, so take your time.

    If you want to venture into go-go bars for a look-see, you need to know how it works, let me know if you intend to do so and I can give some advice.
