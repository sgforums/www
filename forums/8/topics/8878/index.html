    As we step into the next millenium, human are putting more emphasis on feeling. One particular feeling, which we always think about, is love.

    Finding the other half and spending the rest of your life with him or her is no longer a ritual that every human being must perform just to get on with life. Nowadays, marriage or even relationship formed must consist of love. A gal might indicate that she do not mind if the guy is poor or not good-looking, just as long as both of them have love for each other. Same to the guy, beauty or hourglass body is not the top priority.

    Its not that relationships build on love is not good. But come to think about it, do our grandparent have love before or after their marriage? Back in their time, there is no doubt that some may marry due to love. But most of them are match-make by their parent. They do not have any say in their life-long partner. They don't even know what's love is all about. They only know that they must marry and give birth to a boy in order to continue the life-line.

    If the matchmaking is successful, they will marry and live together for the rest of their life with or without love. In most cases, they learn to love and care for each other.

    Is love just a feeling or a process that we can learn? In our present society, it seems that we are stereotyping love as a feeling? "Oh, there is no love in us" or " I have no feeling for you" a guy might said just to cover the facts that the gal is not up to his standard or the society standard of a perfect wife. Vice versa, a gal might also do the same.

    However, if we are going to argue that love is a process, one might ask how we choose the one whom we can carry out the process. It's all boils down to feelings again. Feelings that are create by beautism.

    For a marriage or relationship to work, one must learn to love and care. If we are going to depend on feelings of love, you will never find the one whom you can spend the rest of your life with. The reason is simple. Feelings will fade as time passes. But with a little creativity, you can re-invent the process of love with magical touch that will last you for life.
