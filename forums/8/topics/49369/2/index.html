    I was at HarbourFront station on Friday at 9.50 am, that was the time the security guards opened the gate to the station. I went down to the station concourse and queue up to get the sentosa free admission leaflets.

    There were some people from the mass media interviewing public in the station, but I managed to "avoid" them. Then, the fare gates activated at 10 am sharp and I entered the platform to wait for the train.

    The train arrived at around 10.02 am and I took it to Punggol. I sat in the front seat in the first carriage where you can see the tracks and tunnel ahead of you thru the window. Some stations are very near to one another, they are just a 1 minute ride away. Examples are Outram Park - Chinatown and Chinatown - Clarke Quay. The ride on this train is smooth and fine with no delay at all.

    Alighted at Punggol station after about half an hour ride, the station is currently very deserted, the future LRT platform is on the 2nd level of the station. Walk to Punggol Rd, board service 82 to Punggol point and then back to the station.

    Entered Punggol station to take the train to HarbourFront (to enter Sentosa as I have got the free admission leaflet earlier), the train I board this time is not doing fine. The public announcement system in the train is not working, so there weren't any station stop and door closing announcements. Later, the train was too close to the one in front, about 50m apart, so the train had to be delayed at Farrer Park and Little India stations in order to maintain a safety distance from the train in front. Later at Dhoby Ghaut station, the train had to be manually controlled, and was announced off service upon reaching HarbourFront.

    Alighted at the station, walk along the causeway to Sentosa, but was told I cannot enter for free cause I was not issued the valid leaflet. (The person giving away the leaflet at the station had only handed me 2 leaflets instead of 3). The leaflet which was not handed to me is the valid free entry to Sentosa. As I didn't want to pay the $2, so decide to come at a later date.
