     Okamoto Singapore brings you the Okamoto Freedom Project, a range of products that helps Singaporeans do what they do best – each other. Our products are specially designed to help you find new ways to fool around.

     

    But the Project doesn’t just stop with our three products. We know there are even better ideas out there, and one of them could be yours. Send us your ideas and you could win a trip to Japan.

    Okamoto will be shortlisting ten of your submissions based on the cleverness of the ideas, how true they are to the Project, and what it costs to get them made. Once the top 10 are announced, members of the public will determine the winning product idea by voting.

    Instructions:

    1. Go to: http://www.okamotofreedom.com/suggest-a-product.php

    2. Submit your idea in 50 words or less.

    3. The top 10 entries will be announced on the 14th February ’12.

    4. If your entry has been shortlisted, get your friends to vote for you by “liking” your submission.


    Here's how the contest will run 

     

    24 Nov ’11 to 24 Jan ’12 - Submission of product ideas

    14 Feb to 28 Feb ’12 - Announcement of Top 10 and start of voting

    1 Mar ’12 - Announcement of winner
