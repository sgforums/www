    Singapore glams up as one of the world's fashion capitals, with Asia's biggest and most glamourous fashion and shopping event - The Singapore Fashion Festival 2005.

    The Singapore Fashion Festival will run from 24 March to 3 April 2005 and will feature haute couture unveilings and designer shows showcasing their newest collections, from luxury big names such as Roberto Cavalli, Versace, Givenchy, JPG, Kenzo to international streetwear brands like FCUK, Topshop and FOX. With close to 50 collection shows and many other satellite events, the event presents an eye-opening preview to the season's latest and trendiest collections.

    More than just a luxurious visual feast, it's also a good time to hang out at the hottest fashion parties, check out the unique exhibitions in town and take advantage of the exclusive shopping privileges held in conjunction with the Festival!
