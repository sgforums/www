    I think it is still quite bad for local grad rite now despite all those speeches and newspaper or news. One thing I see is employers are employing quite a lot of foreign talents to do certain jobs at a cheaper rate. This is usually becuz sporeans shun to do those kind of jobs.

    We can categorise job as doing job that sweat a lot or doing job that burn your brain a lot. Sweat a lot could be go inside those big ship tankers to do repairing. Burn a lot could be IT programming.

    One way to make oneself marketable and attractive to employers is to be able to do what those foreign talent can do and much more. This is to justify the pay packet.

    Example for IT line (I am in for 7+ yrs), it is no longer the case where you propose to boss to get vendor help and you just be liaison officer. The model is outdated cuz some boss want in-house staff to do it or at least maintain the system after the vendor left. Vendor quote exorbitant pricing and like held customer ransom cuz customer are now totally dependent on the vendor system. Maintenance contract can be very very expensive.

    For this case, if an in-house personel can do the maintainence which include making code changes to vendor system, he is retainable cuz afterall he only cost a fraction of what the vendor demand.

    Conclusion: the job market is quite different from last times especially with a lot of FT fighting with you for that slice of the pie. You gotta be better than them to survive.
