    the trick lies in the symbols

    realize that the symbols for all the numbers which are multiples of 9 are the same?

    the mathematical concept is this:

    let the number you chose be XY, where X is the first digit and Y is the second digit, hence the number is equivalent to 10X + Y

    the sum of these two would be X + Y

    subtracting X + Y from 10X + Y, one gets 9X which is always a multiple of 9, hence any multiple of 9 would give you your answer
