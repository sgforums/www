    If we take this as correct, then $1000 S$ is 23,800 - take the amount you want to change and multiply by 23.8

    Then write it down on a piece of paper and make sure you ask the forex people how much baht you get for the amount you want to change before you hand over the money. Then look at the amount they quote and compare it with the amount in your paper. A little differnce is ok. 100 baht is about S$4 so anything les then 100 baht should be no problem.
