    Officers from the Central Narcotics Bureau arrested 128 suspected drug offenders, including four suspected drug traffickers, on Tuesday.

    Officers first arrested two suspects in Jalan Bukit Merah after they sold 100 Erimin-5 tablets to an undercover officer.

    They then nabbed their supplier in Redhill Close and seized 20 Erimin-5 tablets, one gramme of ketamine and 117 cartons of contraband cigarettes from his residence in Bukit Batok East Avenue 3.

    Officers later arrested another suspected trafficker who sold 20 Erimin-5 tablets and one gramme of ketamine to an undercover officer in Ang Mo Kio.

    Among the suspected drug abusers arrested, 88 tested positive for drugs.

    Four will be sent to the Drug Rehabilitation Centre, while the remaining 84 were released on bail. - CNA/de CLICK HERE
