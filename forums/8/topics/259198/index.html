    Bio - understand concepts. Some need to be memorized, but those that doesn't need, make sure you understand the concepts for easy memorization.

    The best is to study everything. Don't spot questions. While almost all the time the teachers will be right, there are quite a few occasions that they are wrong.

    Sometimes, the questions can also cut across a few chapters, so even while your teachers are right about it, you never know about those other chapters.

    Rather, put them in the form of domains.

    For example, Education & Health is one domain. Then put the chapters that are related under this domain. A few chapters will be looking at Singapore's system, and a few chapters will be looking at another country's system.
