    Hi New,

     

    Becksondergaard or the maker of the 'eel skin wallet' is from Denmark. Eel skin is frequently regarded as the 'silk of the ocean' for it's slikiness and smooth supple texture. Comparing leather of the same thickness, eel skin is more durable.

    http://www.becksondergaard.com/

    Unfortunately, Becksondergaard does not make bags for men. As for their ladies range, they are mainly into classic and vintage design (just checked their website and I see they are starting to go into fashionable stuffs). If you google around, you can see other brands and design made from eel skin, men's wallet and bags included.

    Personally, I am very impressed with Becksondergaard's design and quality. It imparts the understated class and unique individuality that I constantly look for in a product. My ger shares the same taste and she has gotten 2 items (that I know!) and it is the de facto choice when we are buying gifts for frens ( if within budget).

    Dun worry, I do not have vested interests in the sales performance of the product nor am I the sales man.

    Check it out when you are in Taka :)
