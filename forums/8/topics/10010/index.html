  • no heart

    The first independent report on mobile phone service quality in Singapore has shown what most users have long suspected -- that newcomer StarHub has the poorest GSM cellular service around.

    Not only did it have the highest incidence of dropped calls -- when your call is disconnected prematurely -- it also fared poorly in getting calls through in the first place.

    SingTel Mobile was found to have the lowest incidence of dropped calls while M1 scored top points in the voice-quality category.

    Do you agree with the findings? What grouse have you against any of the phone operators? How do you rank them?
