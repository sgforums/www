    Sawasdee Krup(Men)/Kaa(lady)!!
    Just return frm there 3 weeks ago...here some tips.... Asia Hotel is one of the most convenient hotel as it is directly link to the skytrain and easy accessible to the city area... tips for u is to get the 1 day trip ticket cost 100 baht and buy a 250(forgotten) baht Subway Card(Ezlink alike) which deposit only 50 baht and definitely cost cheaper to travel around than buying the 1 way ticket.
    Never take Tuk-tuks (unless Desperate)= to Robert head unless u know the mim fare.

    First thing: Grap a map from Asia Hotel

    Go to Chatuchak Weekend Market (open only at weekend for tourist (reachable at subway Kampaeng Phet Station and not ChatuChak Station),
    SuanLum Night Bazaar (Live Stage Performance (Free) + Dinner + Drinking)(reachable by Subway, Lumphni Station)
    Palace (must wear pants +shoes + Bring Water) damm big(quite far near Khao San there),
    Chinatown (For roadside) Shark fin + bird nest and Zhu chao (also quite far by Cab),
    Patpong (can skip) now go down alot,
    khao San alot of Farang(Ang Moh ,sian)(pubs (1am) + flea market 1am - 3am),
    Thai Boxing is quite X (500 - 1000 Baht) tickets can be bought from SuanLuam Night Bazaar,
    Massage the cheapest (100 - 200) for foot and traditonal near Sukhumvit Station,
    See Tiffany or Tiger Girls show if interested,
    Watch movies there if u have time (cost only S$4) at MBK,
    It Stuffs - Panthip plaza,MBK...etc

    Never agree to let Cab or tuk tuk driver to bring u around even if they say only 100 baht to bring u around .... they can gain some benefit (fuel Coupon) for the shops they bringing u around.

    Thank you = Kop Koon Kruap/Kaa
    cannot = mee da
    no = mai da
    sorry = kor thod (kruap/kaa)

    that all folks .... think i too free liao

    [/b]
