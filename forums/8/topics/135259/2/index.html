  • storywolf
    6 Jun 05, 14:51
    Originally posted by zacken99:
    yo any kind soul here hv exp.in being a wedding dinner MC???

    any advise?
    Yep me last time alway do MC for people wedding dinner.

    The basic rule is to keep the MC role short and sharp - reduce any error. Remember people are there for the couple not to see a MC talkcock, unless cock up situation.

    The basic rule is to get to know the whole schedule of dinner from both Hotel and couple side, so that you will know what to do and say. Write everything down on a piece of paper ! Look at it if you have to , better than saying the wrong thing, even the best MC can go blank at times.

    Also avoid some chinese sentence - "Early give birth to noble son" which sound leave too much room to error ! I have see one MC poor chinese end up sound more like "Early birth to turtle son !

    Also have some preparation of some words for things that may go wrong. Yep champagne glasses can come falling down and break, well when that happen, the whole room will be in shock or total silence, well the MC must be step in to say some good things - like "drop floor, open flower " .... and turn the tense moment alway, also to quickly talkcock to give the couple or staff time to recover.

    As for games, best advice is to forget it. Althought the couple maybe game for it, the advice is enough things can go wrong, don't add more to it. It may be fun, but remember it a small stage, in the heat of the moment, people may trip, fall, or break things. A fun scene can turn ugly very fast. It is a grand occasion keep it that way, if need laughter, then hire professional clowns !
