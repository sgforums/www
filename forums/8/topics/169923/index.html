    Property Tax is one of govt's way of getting money from people without having to provide any service or product and to ensure revenue. Just like GST and income tax.

    There is no way you can escape from not paying property tax except you don't 'own' any. Same thing with GST and income tax. You need not pay any GST if you don't buy anything from any company or anyone who's GST registered. Don't earn more than $2K and you don't have to pay income tax.

    See it from this perspective. Govt own all the land in Singapore, you buy a flat/apartment on govt's land, so you need to pay off your property amount. You take loan from HDB/bank and they charge you interest for the loan. (You loan because you cannot pay up the entire sum immediately). You contribute to the economy by doing that. Govt earn, banks earn and you pay!

    Then, govt wants to fleece you more, so make you pay property tax because the flat/apartment/house you are living in is on the land which doesn't belong to you but to the govt. Unless you can elevate the property in the air and keep it moving, perhaps you need not pay property tax.

    Just like a car, you need to pay road tax as yr car is in contact with the road.

    All in all, these are avenues that govt has put in place to get money from people on regular basis.

    For TV and radio licenses, just get rid of yr goggle box and radio and you need not pay! I think the authorities will do spot checks at those premises that don't pay.
