    Back in SG & time to update

    Comfort: Very good; comfy spacious seats with ample leg room and leg rests etc etc.

    Amenities: lounge area on lower deck if bored, big TV at front of bus and overhead sound system, showed censored 'White Chicks' SG-KL
    In-flight meals also provided, chicken rice SG-KL and Subway sandwich KL-SG

    Service: Good attention by the stewards and drive was pretty smooth etc etc

    Connectivity: Route ends at Corus Hotel, we stayed there to avoid hassle but it's quite a central location to move elsewhere etc etc

    Quite a good experience overall, trip was abt S$90 both ways and most importantly, no pre-flight hassles lol. Check in on the dot also no problems.
