    TP...........
    That means you are taking it for CDS? The grading is like other subjects 60% pratical 40% theory.

    Practical includes class participation,attendance, online quiz(you'll be notified of this during lecture) and offline assignments.Finally a exploration report on a topic of your choice or pick 1 from a group of topics given by tutor.

    Theory consists of 1 tutorial and 1 lecture(usually held in the evening at business school)

    Oh, yes there is one exam but that's with the rest of your normal exam papers at the end of the sem.

    As for difficulty, its like any other subject; if you are enthusiastic about it and do your work with passion you can easily get a B and above. One important thing to note, is while the notes and stuff provided by lecturer is more than suffcient to get a good grade, it is recommend to do your own reading,web surfing etc.
