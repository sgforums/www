    The promotional event will last from 9pm to 12am tonight. As of now, over 200 members of the public have indicated that they will be attending the event on the bar’s Facebook.

    The spokesperson added that they have received “warnings” that many D cup ladies will be patronizing the bar tonight.

    The gimmick apparently did not go down too well with some women who found it distasteful and insulting.

    A 19 year old student who possessed a C-cup said she will not attend the event as it will “lower” her status.

    Dana Lam, the President of AWARE slammed the organizers for being “stupid”:

    “The organizers doesn’t seem to consider the safety and dignity of the lady customers who may be molested or harassed at the event,” she added.
