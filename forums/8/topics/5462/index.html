Unfortunately I knew that she would never go for a guy like me...never. So I sat and talked to her and her friends(wow, pretty).

Every time I wanted to talk to her about anything substantial, I choke up and stay quiet the whole time...the words...Talk, why r u so quiet?..came up many times...

To say that I am depressed is an understatement. after this post, I will go to the corner and cry for the guy that is still so much a part of me. He hasn't left me at all, I just had conveniently forgotten about him....I guess once a nerd, always a nerd. Scuse me while I go and cry.....
