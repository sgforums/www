    Relief for Life Insurance Premiums

    You can claim relief on the insurance premium paid on your own or your wife's life assurance policy.

    Please note that:

    No relief will be allowable to the wife for premiums paid on her husband's life policies.
    Premiums paid on children's life policies do not qualify for relief.
    For policies taken by you on or after 10 Aug 1973, the insurance company must have an office or branch in Singapore.
    The relief you can claim is up to 7% of the capital sum assured. However, if your total CPF contributions exceed $5,000, you will not get any relief for the insurance premium paid.
