    PS means Platoon Sergeant. In ncc, (for my school as far as I know) you become the leader during the second semester of your third year. I will be taking over as a leader this year. I will go for the leadership course, then get my sergeant rank. There are 18 people in my platoon. Different posts (duties) will be given to individual cadets. There are posts like medic, MP OIC (military police official in charge) PTI OIC (physical training instructer, etc. There are the 9 high post called the 'G9' They are:

    Unit sergeant major - In charge of the whole company (sec1 - sec4 cadets)

    Assistant sergeant major -  Assists the USM

                (The 3 OICs)

    PTI OIC - In charge of physical fitness

    MP OIC -  In charge of discipline

    PDS OIC - Only schools with a precision drill squad have this post. He is incharge of  

                        the PDS trainings ( go search deyi pds on youtube)

                      (the 4 platoon sergeants)

    Delta PS - In charge of sec 4 (his own platoon)

    Charlie PS - Incharge of the batch that came right after you (sec 3)

    Bravo PS - In charge Sec 2

    Alpha PS  -  In charge of the newest sec 1 batch ( what I am aiming for.)
