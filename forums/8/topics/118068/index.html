    hehe, I was still a kid and in Pri school starting from 1988(pri1).The rest they say is history! Nevertheless i could still remember what it was like when my grand dad brought me to school on a cold and frosty morning.Could still remember my Kindergarten was at AMK ave 4...cant seem to remember the name but its beside a very steep slope which i cant seem to find now, ...1980s Singapore used to have a lot of trees and forests.In Amk where i grew up most of them are gone now...the area where NYP stands used to be thick forests and there YCK CC i could remember is still there now together with the Hawker Center
    Attended computer classes there in the 80s when i was a kid No Intel computers, all of them were Apple and IBM>These were like Intel and AMD in those days.

    Could still remember the "stinggray sticks" which were tasty, Yan Yan and Ice Kachang.Watched movies at the NEW Crown and Broadway and the first movies i watched could still remember now were Aliens and Indiana Jones:The Last Crusade"

    also missed those 80s horror shows like FRIGHT NIGHT, Exorcist which my family members used to rent at the local video shops on VCR, yeap the VCR was the common media used back then though i knew LDs existed too but for the rich and wealthy!
