    Just curious, do you mean to say that you have migrated there or are you just studying there?

    If its the former, and if you have only been there for a short time, I can only say that the feeling will change. Slowly you will come to realize how different the environment (both physical and mental) is from Singapore, and most of the difference will be pleasant. You will come to appreciate the fact that you have the opportunity to do what, like TheNatureWitch said, so many many other people dream of doing.

    Then, I think 2 things might happen.

    1) You will realize that your loyalties and love towards somewhere that you have called home will change, and it will confuse, bewilder and scare you, because you do not want to be forced to choose like this, for whatever reasons.

    2) You will realize that its what you really wanted anyway, and your feelings for somewhere that you have called home have diminished so much because you find that there is so little to offer other than the odd plate of cha kway teow that you will think little of staying in that new country.

    Still, there is much to be enjoyed, and no point worrying so much. Or perhaps you have discovered that already?
