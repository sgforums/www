    Be careful.... from the looks of it.. the relationship is heading towards fizzling out.

    You are becoming too 'commonplace' for him (i.e. he's taking you for granted that you are always around) and he need something fresh or exciting to raise his interest.

    At this juncture, you need to establish 2 things:

    1) If he still cares about you at all (i.e. a phone call on his own initiative or he did something for you). *very impt!* Try to keep contact off with him for 1 entire week and see if he contacts you. If he doesn't, you better be on the alert that this relationship might fizzle out.

    2) Change of interest. Guys would change perspective after some period of stress or incident (army is an eg.) If his view of life is changed and you are not in there, you better be ready for the possibility of a split soon.

    I'm not kidding about this. Note Pt 1 above. That's very important. No matter what happens, a strong BGR would not run out of things to talk about or to do. TIredness is just an excuse. For guys, no matter how tired they are, for someone they like a lot, they would never give tiredness as an excuse. Only when there's a lack of passion will this happen.

    (Imagine, if a guy likes football very much, but he's so darn tired after a long day's work that he would rather die than lift a finger, however, if he's told that there's a free tix for him to go to a match and the next day don't have to work (weekends), you think he would dismiss it and say 'No lah, can't move, too tired lah, don't think can go'???
