    A Canister with a airtight lid.  A kg of SIS sugar. 2 stalks of organic vanilla in its pods.

    Put vanilla pods in canister, pour sugar onto them and keep in fridge.

    Tie a ribbon over it.

    And you have Vanilla Sugar.  Good for spooning into coffee and even Hot Chocolate for the whole family.
