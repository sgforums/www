    First few months is like that one. I had braces before also. First couple of months the braces caused ulcers (the metal part kept rubbing against my ulcer...yucks!). Had to see GP twice for the ulcer problem.

    After a few months, you will be ok... perfectly ok. Only feel a bit pain when dentist adjusts anything on your braces. Quite bearable though.

    Something to take note: Be consistent in your brushing and flossing. I know flossing is very very troublesome at this time -- you have to use the special kind of dental floss and floss it in a very different way.
    Also to note that you have to go for cleaning ever 3 months (normal ppl go to dentist every 6 months)

    If you cannot bear the pain in your gum, go to Watsons or Pharmacy to buy something to apply on the gum (always at the same section with the toothbrush, dental floss etc).
