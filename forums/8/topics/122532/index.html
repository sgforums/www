    depending on matt/glossy finish. a2 size cost ard 30dollars per piece if i didnt remember wrongly. i usually go to the one at bras basah.

    and if you want to print a2, you work should done in at least 200 resolution so tt the print results is decent. as always, work in the largest resolution you can manage.. then when necessary resave in lower resolution later...

    and xvii, once you start in tp you will drain your savings... so while you can, work and save up more now. you will easily spend one to two hundred a mth on materials/printing/etc.. but also depending on course.

    if you study visual comm, printing can be done in school but you have to invest in ultra good (and expensive!!) manual cameras, and hundred of rolls of film.

    if study animation, and need to do print outs, then will be the high cost of $30/A2

    etc. etc. just save up. or you might have to compromise work standards.
