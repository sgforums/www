    First of all, train up your proficiency in your guns, make sure your bullets hit your target - deadly shots of cause.

    2ndly, tactical thinking. The ability to use the surroundings against the enemy. Outsmart and outpace your opponent. For example, it would be really stupid to chiong upfront against a really good sniper(unless you have confidence in your gun), perhaps throw 1 or 2 flashbangs, go by the corner and try to flank him down.

    Take advantage of your rifle, it has the ability to shoot through most walls/doors.But of cause, reframe from using wallhack

    Be alert of your surroundings and make use of your hearing/radar. Know where your teammates are and one of them has been killed, you can sense imediately whether the enemy is backstabbing you or not.

    Always check for all lanjiao camping spots.

    Last of all, always be calm, don't panick. Even if you are the last person alive, and against 5 enemies, with a cool head, stealth and accuracy, you can finish all of them one by one - like a deadly assasin.

    Last but not least, the element of surprise. Don't be afraid even if you are on low health. There's always a chance of giving your enemy a headshot if you take him by surprise.
