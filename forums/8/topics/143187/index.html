    check the yellow page for those non-branded courier companies. If you want to use Singpost, consider the duration of transport, you wouldn't want to use shipmail and it takes 2 weeks to reach there right?

    A caveat in using smaller courier services is that they will not guarantee that your item will reach there on time or at all!

    Also surf Fedex or UPS website on how to fill in the courier form. In brief, most prob you will be declaring your item as sample or personal gift and writing there NO COMMERCIAL value or a few dollars. Read the websites and see how best to fill in the courier forms. Filling in the forms is basically the same for any courier company, big or small.

    If your item is fragile, make sure you use a sturdy box and not a fragile cigratte box type. Fedex and the brand names will give you free box if you demand for it. Though i don't think they have a box for your size. If you are not sending a gift and the appearance of the box is not important, write the tracking number, your name, your address, tel (use +65 ......), your reciever add, reciever name and reciever tel on the box. Use an oil based marker or pen.

    Lastly, don't let the courier man hurry you. Take your time to fill in the courier forms. You wouldn't want mistakes on it. The courier form comes in set of 3 or more pieces, make sure you keep your customer copy for tracing purpose.

    The courier form is free.

    I don't charge for the advice but if you can, make a donation to KDF or other non-NKF charities.

    All the best.
