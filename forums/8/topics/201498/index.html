    why?

    Croc's natural habitat: River/swamp
    Escape option :Swim to the river bank and run for your life.

    Shark's natural habitat: Deep sea/ ocean
    Escape option: NIL (unless a passing boat/ship rescues u...it's too far for u to swim to shore )

    Also, do note the hunting "habits" of both predators.

    Croc: Usually stalks the prey slowly first and only speeds up when it's quite near the prey. Also, they have a habit of mauling the prey and letting it die first before eating it. sometimes the croc leaves the "dead" prey unwatched, which gives it the chance to escape.

    Shark: Swims at full speed at the prey and bites it! Will not stop until it is finished with its meal. Seldom stalk its prey when its in the water. (might follow some ships in hope of catching a quick bite )

    Verdict: U will have a higher chance of survival during a Croc attack!
