    Anyone been there?

    I went there on a saturday, the queue was pretty long at the entrance but it didn't take as long as I expected. The queue inside however, was hell of a wait.
    This part of the year calls for frequent showers over Singapore. Majority of the rides are outdoors, and when the rain come falling down the rides are suspended.
    I remember waiting for 2 hrs for a go-cart ride.( Life Daytona, got wat I mean?) Despite being a one time ride, (the other rides are unlimited) the queue was long. When I got near the front, Argh! The rain came falling down. After it stopped the staff took their time to wipe the carts, sweep the route, etc. Then the rain started again! After this series of rainfall they test drive the route to make sure it was safe for riding. I finally got on the cart for a 5 min ride.
    I spent the whole afternoon there for just 3 miserable rides. There is one indoor ride, Alpha 8, I tried that, not bad, since it is the only ride not affected by the weather.
    Although my ticket allowed me unlimited rides, there wasn't much chance to enjoy it.
    It was still raining when I left in the evening. Instead of waiting for the rain to clear, I guess preparing dinner for my hubby will be time better spent.
    The rides are fun, I can see. Maybe I will be there again when the crowd is gone.
