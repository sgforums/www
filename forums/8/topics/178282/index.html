    Compressed formats does not affect file size of songs. Its the bitrate. Just use iTunes --> Preferences --> Importing --> MP3 Encoder --> (select a bitrate suitable to your file size)

    Then right click on the song you wanna reduce in the library and convert to mp3.

    Eg. If you have a 160kbps mp3 song, and you wanna convert to a smaller file size aka lower bitrate, select 128kbps as your encoding settings and convert it. A 160kbps MP3 file will be converted to 128kbps MP3 file.

    Converting between song formats reduce the quality
