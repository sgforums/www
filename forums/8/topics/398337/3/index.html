    It is perfectly fine to push for the preservation of the culture and lifestyle of an ethnic background. Absolutely nothing wrong with acknowledging one's historical roots and embracing it. To each his own. Nevertheless, how do we go forward from now? The modern society is getting more homogeneous in terms of the ethnic composition of its community. Globalization. A pre-requisite to the progression of the country, economy and its people relative to rest of the world. Can you deny it? Do you not want it? Do anyone not want it? A system of mutual consensus, tolerance and understanding is much needed to avoid conflicts arising from the differences of each ethnics/race/religion. The world is indeed not in its equilibrium state yet but mankind is still trying, or at least I do hope so.

    Singapore is a perfect example of the modern society. Indeed, the Chinese race makes up the majority of its population. But does that mean a single dominant race, regardless of what race, is the catalyst to a state of equilibrium? Will it be efficient and effective in the long run?

    What is the basis of language? Communication. The world would not mind, if they can understand Mandarin/chinese langauge as you communicate it. But the world is not Chinese. Singapore is not Chinese. For all your heart's content, embrace your language, and I applaud your passion for that, but do not impose it onto the world outside yourself. As much as you hate it, the world has settled for English to be the dominant language. Singapore, very much wanting to be part of the global set up, would have to follow suit. It is the only way forward. However, it is important to note that dominance here do not equal diminishing of the other languages. So for all the Chinese that one would want to speak, English has to be the dominant language in his/her life, else, it will be difficult for them to progress. On another note, living in a multi-racial country, despite the non-equal ethnic proportion, how do one live harmoniously with his/her neighbour who is of a different ethnicity  without a common and mutual basis of communication?

    In every type of relationship, communication is an important factor that has to be established. You are one person, living in a complex society connected to everyone else by relationships of any nature. How do you establish an efficient state of equilibrium for yourself?
