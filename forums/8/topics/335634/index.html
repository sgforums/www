The Morning Express?

  • Darkknighthuang
    1 Nov 08, 12:09

    When I first started listening to the Morning Express on the radio, I found it quite enjoyable. However after listening for sometime, I find them boring & not interesting anymore. Here’s why:

    1.       Besides just putting on songs, they are going through the same routine everyday and simply reading what’s published in the newspapers or something off the internet.

    2.       The report on traffic watch before 6:30 am, might as well be a recording. What kind of traffic situation do you expect at 6:20 am?

    3.       You have a guy who’s laughing at everything and sometimes over nothing while the other guy doesn’t (that tells me either 1 guy is not getting the joke, nor find it funny or the other guy is the only person laughing at his own joke) And when the younger guy does laugh, he sounds like a nerd. Ok, on a few rare occasions, they were funny but other times, you can tell their laugher is forced and so artificial.

    4.       One guy is always trying to show off his singing skills and it sucks.

    5.       The younger guy is always asking questions (sometimes naive ones) or pretending he doesn’t knows the answer to some simple questions.

     

    Sorry guys, as far as I’m concerned, the old chap can go fly a kite and the younger guy,… just GO.

     

    They call it the Morning Express. I call it the Morning Depress and I have been feeling better ever since I stopped listening to them.

     

    Note to FD & GO: Just my personal opinion. If the feedback is only positive, then maybe it’s just me and you guys are not that bad afterall.
