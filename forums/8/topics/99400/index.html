    My Itinerary used to be this when I visited the place.

    I start off with S$25 around Bugis Area.

    11.15am. Change money with money changer at Bugis Junction.
    11.30am. Board Singapore Johore Express.
    12.00pm. Reach JB custom. Board cab. Go to Johore Jaya.
    12.20pm. Reach Johore Jaya. Eat Ah Soon Bah Kut Teh.
    13.00pm. Walk over to Holiday Plaza. Buy stuffs, hang around, tea break
    14.00pm. Talk bus to City Square. Buy movie tix. Watch Movie.
    16.00pm. Hang around City Square. Buy Portugese Chicken for dinner tomorrow
    18.00pm. Take cab go Skudai eat seafood. Crab, Chicken, Beer, Fish usually.
    19.30pm. Take cab back to City Square Area. Board Singapore Johore Express.
    20.30pm. Reach Bugis again.

    I do almost every week back in Uni when I was really broke. That's how I spent my New Singapore Shares by the way, and I felt that that it was truly worth the money.

    Try doing this in Singapore without spending a hundred bucks at least!
