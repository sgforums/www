    Repost it here for other to read.

    ----------------------------------------------------------------

    I just received a letter from XXXXXX Business Pages and would like to share with all of you.

    Dear Sir Madam,

    To be included and to ensure your accurate entry for the 2007 edition, kindly verify your information on the second page.

    Please check your company details and amend if necessary, once completed and in agreement with the proposed advertisement contract, kindly sign, affix your company chop and return to us by Mail or conveniently Fax :

    Fax XXXXXXXXX before May 16, 2007

    Sincerely,

    ...............................

    On the 2nd page

    At top right hand corner :

    XXXXXXXX Business Pages
    Adertisement Contract : MX -XXXXXX
    Advertisement cost SGD 4,250 + GST per edition

    The Centre portion : (In Big big font)

    Dear Sir Madam,

    To be included in the lastest XXXXXX Business Pages, kindly complete, sign and affix your company chop & return to :

    A big Box with

    company :
    Address :
    Phone :
    Fax :
    Email :
    Website :

    ............................... ..........................
    Authorised Signature Company Chop

    ____________________________________________________________

    The reason why I bring this up is that. It appears to be just a normal request to update and VERIFY Company's info. In actual fact, it is a an advertisement CONTRACT.

    If you or your staff or colleague don't read the terms and condition properly and just chop, sign and fax back, then your company has entered into an Advertisement Contract of SGD4,250 + GST.

    Just a reminder.

    Patgoh
