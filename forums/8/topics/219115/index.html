    Hello Kitty Hide & Seek with Kitty And Friends Exhibition Singapore '06

    Im sure all you guys and girls in Singapore remember very clearly about the whole 'Hello Kitty Kraze' that struck Singapore a few years back but heres a better reason to go crazy over Hello Kitty all over again, from the 24th of November till the 2nd of December, Sheng Tai Toys and Sanrio bring you 'Hello Kitty Hide And Seek with Kitty And Friends' an exhibition at The Arts House on the history of Hello Kitty with displays of vintage collectible merchandise and memorabilia, images and Hello Kitty themed spaces alongside artworks, installations and animations by contemporary artists and design houses all over the world such as Rinzen from Australia, Buro Destruct from Switzerland and Power Graphixx from Japan to name a few.
    Interested? check out www.hellokittyhideandseek.com.sg
