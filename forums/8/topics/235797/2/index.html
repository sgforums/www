    For me,

    Primary school - There was a time the whole school were in marbles craze. Everywhere you see got people play marble, of course I'm into it too. Yea there were also a time where whole school into flag erasers. When I'm in primary 5 and 6 (Almost all in the same class as me in primary remained together in primary 6 except 3 i think, ya my teacher asked us whether we want to stay together and we all agreed), then at that time my class were into tamiya.

    Everytime after school end, we will go to the block next to our school and let our tamiya run in the drain (those shallow-type drain) below that block. If not, our almost all of our chinese classmate will get together and play block catching. Haha, I remember those time where we keep get scolded by the people living in the block for making so much noise and people keep complaining to our principal. We never care about it and still continue to play, even with principal at our back, we never got caught in the end.

    ----------------------------------------------------------------------------

    For sec school, not much memories, I think in fact none at all. Kinda boring, only talk everyday, if not make fun of our class chairman. Ya if really want memories is more than half of our class keep opposing our class chairman because he is very bossy.

    Then our class split into 3 groups - 'those who oppose chairman' faction, those who 'help shield class chairman and keep telling us to stop' faction and those who 'watch and laugh about it' faction.

    Then everytime the factions will complain about each others to our form teacher and our teacher will always become the mediator. The so-called war lasted 3 years (Sec 3 to 5). Come to think of it our form teacher kinda poor thing, for three years keep seeing the same crap.

    Our class don't like the chairman is because he is very bossy and he have been in the class chairman position from sec 1 to 4. At sec 5, he got pushed down from the chairman position by the 'those who oppose chairman' faction. How? We planned during sec 4 that when we are in sec 5 and teacher ask who want to be chairman for sec 5, we will nominate others and give all sort of reasons not to let him be chairman.

    Well, we thought its over in sec 5 since he is no longer the chairman but its not. He keep shooting/complaining at us and the new chairman to form teacher for revenge and of course we shoot back.

    Yup all my classmates from sec 4 successfully promoted to sec 5.

    I guess this is the only fun thing I had in sec school.
