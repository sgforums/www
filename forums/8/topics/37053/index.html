    4 Mar 03, 09:21
    Hmm... omelette...

    Slice fresh shiitake mushrooms and fresh button mushrooms... dry them...

    Bit of peanut oil, totally smashed clove of garlic.. hot.. saute garlic until fragrant..

    Stir-fry mushrooms for a little while then add half a can of beer into the wok.. cover and let the beer be reduced while the mushrooms absorb the aroma.. can substitute beer with white wine or stock, but somehow beer is good plus you can drink the other half.. cooking is hot work

    remove the mushrooms...

    Beat 3 eggs (not too much but pretty intensely to get air into the eggs).. add a little salt and pepper if preferred..

    Insanely hot wok, use some peanut oil... wok should be smoking like mad... add egg mixture and spread it around... add mushrooms into half-cooked eggs and cheddar cheese... flip one side over to cover the mushrooms and cheese and leave to cook for a little while more over a reduced fire... add some mozerella cheese on the top of it wait for it to melt a little.. lift the whole thing onto a platter and serve...

    that's the type of omelette i like to make and eat
