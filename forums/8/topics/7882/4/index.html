    I am not for Shearer or anything, but I would like to point out that Shearer is an out and out centre forward. That means that it is his job to wait in the box for the crosses. No, their main job is to stay in the penalty box and finish off whatever chances that come their way. Usually there will be one out and out forward in any team, the other one will be the foil.
