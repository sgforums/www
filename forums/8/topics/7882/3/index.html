    I am not for Shearer or anything, but I would like to point out that Shearer is an out and out centre forward. That means that it is his job to wait in the box for the crosses. Centre forwards are NOT supposed to dribble the ball past defenders or to create chances for other ppl to score. No, their main job is to stay in the penalty box and finish off whatever chances that come their way. Usually there will be one out and out forward in any team, the other one will be the foil. He will be the one doing the shielding, pulling defenders away, setting up chances..etc.
    Therefore there is nothing wrong with Shearer, that's the characteristics of his game. And good positioning is the most devastating skill that an out and out centre forward can possess.

    And if Shearer runs around aimlessly because Macca and Beckham kenna marked dead. The fault lies with them, NOT Shearer. Coz their job is to supply the crosses, Shearer's is to finish them. So no crosses means that they did not perform, NOT Shearer.
