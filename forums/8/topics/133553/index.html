    Your grade are very good for someone who are not interested in the course. With this grade, there should not be any problem for you to be accepted into any major university in Australia for a similar IT degree course.

    You can also try looking at business information system course. These course combine both business and IT units into a degree program. Hence you will study management and software engineering at the same time.
