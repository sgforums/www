    Hey guys...

    theism (dictionary) = Belief in the existence of a god or gods, especially belief in a personal God as creator and ruler of the world.

    atheism = not theism = Lack of beliefs in the existence of any gods.
    This is not the same as believing that there are no gods. Atheists don't believe in the gods that theists believe in that's all.

    That said, there are two other points.

    1) Since the qualification for atheism is only a lack of belief in any gods, atheists can have any number of personal beliefs. Some atheists believe in ghosts, some believe in tooth fairies, some believe in past lives, some just totally disbelieve in anything paranormal or pesudoscience.

    2) There are religions that do not postulate any gods, like Buddhism. As such, some buddhists are atheist (i assume there are some buddhists who believe in some form of god).
