    It is difficult to say whether COL has increased over the years for a few reasons. First, the present generation consume different goods, better goods, etc.

    Second, while salaries has increased, it does not mean we can all afford more things. The increased in salaries are COL adjusted ie what you can buy with $1 in 70's is the same as $10 in the 90's. Sad to say, people do not always accept this.

    Third, expectations. Increasingly, people come to expect more consumption in the present due to the availability of hire purchase, etc. This will generally increase prices because production fails to keep up and may not be kept up.

    Thus, it is for yourself to judge.
