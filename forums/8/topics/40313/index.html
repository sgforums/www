    ok...let me answer your questions...

    first of all, you can be caught even if you only have one stick of duty unpaid cigarettes...and it'll cost you $100, just for that one stick...the figure is hard to explain, but normally, you'll be told that the first packet is $100, subsequent packs are $50 each...

    if, let's say you are caught, and they let you go, dun thik that you are safe...customs officers will sometime conduct ad hoc operations at popular spots, coffee shops and etc...and if you did not pay the duty for the ciggy and you are caught, you'll be fined(this has happened before, no joke...some of the officers are hard up for cases)...even though the officers at the checkpoint let you off...to play safe, always insist on paying the duty involved, uness you are sure you'll be cautious enough to not let others know you're smoking duty unpaid ciggies...

    for smuggling in a carton(normally, the customs will call it "box") or more, you'll be handcuffed, interviewed, and they'll go to your house/workplace to do a raid, and subsequently, you'll be charged in court...

    how do i know? well, i was once a customs officer...
