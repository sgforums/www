    Start with the public information. For example, company profiles are public.

    If you knew this guy owns a company, then go down to RCB to check out the company and its information. Then you should get the guy's IC number and its full name. Or you may visit 1questnet.com. They have a whole lot of comprehensive searches on Singapore companies.

    Do a lookup of his name in the phone book, now you have his address and phone number.

    Go to google, key in his name, or address or stuffs.. if he has ever taken part in some online contest, or perhaps any event that has been showcased on the net, you should find it. Wow.. now you know his interests, and probably stuffs about him in his company if that was featured.

    Or, perhaps you only knew where he stayed, but you wanted his phone number. Just do a reverse looking for that in the phone directory!

    Nothing illegal here, these are all public information. In the US, properties owned by individuals are public information. Don't know if the same applies here...
