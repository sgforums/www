    But Icebaby0....I thought we already have recommended a lot of places....? And even asked for your interest, saying that its easier to recommend if we knew your interests...?

    Its better to book package tours as they are much cheaper. If you are booking hotels yourself, the best deals are at the outer areas....like Asakusa area.

    One thing about 99% of Tokyo hotels....the hotel rooms are about 2 times the size of our HDB bathroom (maybe slightly bigger). So get prepared to feel really claustrophobic. I tried booking expensive hotels before (eg. Prince chain)....but the rooms are still only slightly bigger then the cheap ones (not worth it). They are, however, super clean and efficient, with everything you need inside. If you're smoker....oooh....the room will be filled with smoke, giving you the feeling like you're in paradise.

    And the walls are thin too....so if you're there on a Friday or Saturday night, listen out for couples in the next room (if you're lucky enough).

    4.5 days is too little or too much for Tokyo....depending on yourself. Sometimes I wanted to get out of there in 2 days. Sometimes, I wanted to stay for 2 weeks. Depends on my mood lah.

    Witch_Cat.....for temples, its better to go to the "older" side of Japan, the Kansai region (Osaka, Nara, Kyoto, etc). Tokyo has a big temple at Asakusa....its famous for having the biggest something, but I can't remember what (lantern or some other kind of decoration or something). I don't know if there are anymore worth visiting.
