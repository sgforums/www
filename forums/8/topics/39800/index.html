    Some tips for u dude...

    1.Don't think before you dial the phone. See how fast you can dial the numbers, then count the rings to see if you can make them match the number of numbers you dialed : )

    2.Talk about her on the phone. Ask questions about her and the things she's interested in, because they're going to quickly become important to you. Spend the time on the phone getting to know more about her.

    3.Keep track of your common interests. You'll need to know those things later.

    4.Think about things you could do together based on your shared interests - but only after you've become more comfortable talking with her on the phone.

    5.BE YOURSELF. Don't try to be what you think she wants you to be. It's only fair that you are honest with her and with yourself. She'll respect you for that. Enjoy being yourself.

    6.Be light-hearted. Nothing is worse than taking things too seriously. Don't go gaga and "fall madly in love" with her on the phone. Keep things nice and simple - fun and friendly.

    7.Pick public places, the first few times you spend time with her. Do things where other people are around. It'll help her feel more secure and allow your relationship to move a little slower.

    8.Try to be unique. Choose things to do/places to go that aren't boring and expected. Forget movies - you won't know her any better than when you started. How about a picnic in the park?

    9.Don't go overboard, but send a card or a flower every now and again. Send flowers, that are special - roses are beautiful. Make a statement. How about a big, white orchid?

    10.Treat her like a queen. Respect her and if she gives you an inch, you'd better not take more than a half an inch. She deserves your respect.

    11.Do some interesting things after you know her better. Special outings will allow you to learn more about each other, but don't forget the rule

    12.Don't take things too seriously - I can't stress this enough. Don't let your hormones tell you you're in love. Life is long.. You don't have to get married at 18, and you surely don't have to experience sex at age 14. Take your time, have fun, relax, and enjoy getting to know this special young lady. Enjoy a simple relationship based on trust.

    Cheers
