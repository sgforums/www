    Taoism Form

    Taoism has many forms, sometime it confused people who want to learn it.
    But in general Taoism can be divide as:

    1. Taoism Philosophy 
    This is a group of people who learn Taoism from Tao Tek Cing.
    They use Tao Tek Cing as philosophical way of life.
    It is very common side of Taoism that has been spread into the West. 
    May be it is because there is no concept of worship, rituals and d

    ogma inside Tao Tek Cing.
    Because of that, any peoples be able to learn it whatever their religion.

    Taoist doesn't make this group.
    This group created because of people who learn Tao Tek Cing without affiliate with any Taoism school. 

    In learning philosophy, we must be carefull to avoid been entrapped inside intellectual perception. 

    May be we can understand that there are many type of sugar, even we know the chemical form of sugar, we also know that sugar is sweet. But it is different if comparing that knowledge with the taste that we feel when we eat sugar directly. 
    The taste of sugar could not been transferred just by the word.

    That is why in Tao Tek Cing / Dao De Jing, Lao Tse wrote: "TAO that been explained is not TAO."

    In other word: Sugar that been explained is not Sugar.

    2. Taoism Religion
    This religion followed by Chinese and usually called as Folk Religion.
    This group arrange by adherent and not disciple groups.
    (Please read topic: Religion & Spiritual)
    Because of that, the concept sometime deviates from basic Taoism that base on logical thinking way.
    Sometime this group entrapped on worship and superstitious thing because their concept base on faith!
    It is because of their limitation knowledge of Taoism. 
    But there are some peoples who look Taoism from this group! 

    3. Taoism Spiritual
    It is the nucleus of Taoism.
    All of Taoist master practicing spiritual exercises to improve their value in every aspect of life that can be divide as:

    3.1. Physical body 
    The target is healthy and long life.
    Increasing vitality and spirit of life by many exercise and doing healthy habit in every day life.

    3.2. Mental body & soul / consciousness / awareness / mind
    The target is become wise man, not just good man.
    Sometime Taoist reveals their wisdom in a book and today we know it as Taoism scripture. People learn it without join in spiritual Taoism school.

    It is the roof of Taoism Philosophy and Taoism Religion.

    In general our consciousness consist of
    a. Logical thinking way, ratio and intelligence
    b. Heart of heart, emotion and feeling

    Both of side is been progress, the final goal is enlightenment.

    3.3. Energy / Spirit
    This is the mysterious side of human life. 
    This is ‘something’ that still exists after our physical body death.
    Taoist has certain practice that makes us enable to reveal this secret. 
    What happen after our physical death?
    What is the condition and situation that we must pass by?
    Taoist practices certain meditation method as preparation to face any possibility in afterlife.
    May be it sound very weird but we must be train until be able to understand the condition of other dimension when we still life.
    By using this capability, we can prepare to face it better.

    The simple terminology that use in this scope is immortality.
