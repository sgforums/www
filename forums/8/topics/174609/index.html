    Question: Every year has at least one Friday which falls on the 13th of a month, and at most three such days.

    To prove: Any year could start with Sunday / Monday / ... Saturday, and it could be a normal year, which has 365 days, or a leap year, which has 366 days.

    So we have 14 categories of years: normal year starting with Sun till Sat; leap year starting with Sun till Sat. After we examine all these 14 types we find that a year has at least one at most three Fri. the 13th.

    Please note that "every year" falls to one of the 14 categories.
