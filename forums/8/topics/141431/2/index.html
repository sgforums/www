    Please allow me to offer my two cents worth of advice. I used to work in the private educational sector. (will not mentioned which one)

    Points to note when chosing a course:
    1. Reputation of private schools
    A lot of newspapers do report that the some private schools run into financial problems. The students are not able to claims for paid school fees and uncompleted course.
    2. Affiliated Universities
    Most private institutes will have oversea universities affiliated to their program. These allow their students to pursue the degree programs after completion of the diploma program. Look not only at the diploma course, ponder at which university you will want to study too.
    3. Tutor
    Look at the quality of the tutors too. I used to encounter complaint that the students do not understand the tutor's "indian" english accent.
    4. Facilities in the school
    Library, computer labs, classroom, study corner, and etc. Not very important do not add up to a friendly environment.
    5. Cost
    Any hidden cost apart from what the sales executive tells you.

    Hope the above information helps.
