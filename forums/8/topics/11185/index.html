    Disclaimer: These are weekend(Sunday/After Office hrs) rates. (ERP rates not included).

    Orchard:
    U can park at Lido ($2 per entry, no additional charges). Alternatively, u can try Singapore Power House ($1 per entry, no additional charges) or the carpark at Carlton Hotel (Cairnhill Circle, $2 per entry, no additional charges).

    MS:
    U can park at Suntec ($1 per entry, no additional charges) or Marina Leisureplex ($1 per entry, no additional charges).

    Bugis Junction:
    The carpark at Bugis Junction is ok. ($2 per entry, no additional charges).
