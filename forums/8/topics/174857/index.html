The Blood Bank needs YOU !

  • photo_seeker
    19 Jan 06, 22:43
    Blood stocks here are at dangerously low levels.

    The Blood Bank has only 500 units of blood, enough for 24 hours.

    It need to build up it's supplies to meet at least three days's needs. Singapore needs about 300 units of blood daily for transfusion.

    If you are healthy, aged 18 - 60 and weigh above 45kg, you can donate blood. Singapore must take along their NRIC and Non-Singaporeans their passports when donating blood.

    Donations can be made at Bloodbank@HSA at 11 Outram Road, opposite Outram MRT station. It is open from 9am - 6.30pm Tuesdays to Thursdays, 9am - 8pm on Fridays, 9am - 4.30pm on Saturdays and 9am - 2pm on Sundays.

    Call the Singapore Red Cross at 62200183 to make an appointment.
