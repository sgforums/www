    I enjoyed your review and having just seen the production, couldn't agree more. The show is magnificent and definitely one to see. I must say however, that I was thoroughly disappointed in Brent Carver's portrayal of Gandalf (others I have spoken to agree). He did not have the commanding presence necessary for the role. His voice came across weak and higher pitched than it should have and he stumbled occassionally on his lines. As you remark, every line seemed rushed. Apart from that distraction (and it was a big distraction for me), the sets were amazing, the costumes superb (particularly the orcs)and the story held true to the spirit of Tolkien. I was particularly happy to see that the sacking of the Shire had not been cut!
