    I know this forum mainly young people....my intention is to bring to attention that trading can bring in good decent money. But 3 things you have to master:

    a) A good trading system, be it technical analysis or fundamental analysis
    b) Money Management, this is like oxygen to divers, money is our oxygen.
    c) Mental strength and discipline is a must.

    You should start young...coz' now u have insufficient funds. but u can do mock paper trading, do it for a few years until you have the money. By then you should be quite ready to face the market. do not just jump straight in. It will kill you.

    I started pretty late and lost quite a bit. I regret not learning when I have plenty of time to while away in my youth. So if you are a young dude or dudette, learn from the sideline...understand what market is all about. Trade only when you are ready. It will be good if you are still a student. Coz' you will have no money to trade and will be forced to learn from the sidelines instead.
