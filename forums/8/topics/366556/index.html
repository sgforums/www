    There is a lie detector test in Singapore, but it is only used by the Singapore Police Force. On a side note, results from such tests cannot be used as evidence in court.

    During such tests, the interrogated person will be asked questions, and if he is lying, his pulse will go abnormally high, and that will be detected by the machine.

    This test is not extremely accurate due to the fact that some people panic very easily, and others know how to keep their calm even when they are lying.

    This test can be used for any cases by the police, as it is up to their discretion, though usually they won't use it if they can come up with ample evidence to deal with the suspect(s).
