    ***Ayu sings in tears for Nagase's birthday***

    Pop singer Hamasaki Ayumi (24) attended the ABU (Asia Pacific Broadcast
    United) Award Commemoration Concert located in Shibuya District's NHK Hall,
    Tokyo. On
    that day (Nov. 7), it was Ayu's boyfriend's 24th birthday. She was so
    emotional that she shed tears as she sang songs in the concert. Other
    artists representing other countries in Asia also attended. Artists include
    Kelly Chan (Hong Kong), Shinhwa? (Korea), etc... After Ayu's first song
    "Hanabi", Ayu reflected back on the times when she performed throughout
    Asia. She commented that the live in
    Beijing, the (fans') power was great. And at Taiwan's event, she was happy
    to see her fans cried when they met her. As Ayu sings her 2nd song
    "Voyage", she started to cry. Authorized people said they don't know why
    she cried. The live will be broadcasted in about 50 countries/regions.
