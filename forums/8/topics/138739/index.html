    SINGAPORE GAGA

    After playing to three standing room only screenings at the Singapore International Film Festival, Singapore GaGa screens at The Substation, 1-3, July 2005

    Friday, 1 July, 8pm
    Saturday, 2 July, (2 screenings) 4pm, 8pm
    Sunday, 3 July, (2 screenings) 4pm, 8pm

    Tickets $9, $6 (conc) from The Substation,
    45 Armenian Street, Singapore 179936
    Box office hours: Monday to Friday 12-8.30 pm (tel : 63377800)
    Screenings will be preceded by a short film.
    Friday's proceeds go to The Substation's Moving Images.

    SYPNOSIS
    The latest work by award winning filmmaker Tan Pin Pin is a whimsical portrait of Singapore through its music and sounds. It features performances of train announcements ("Eating or drinking is not allowed on the stations and trains"), Madrasah school cheers and by avant garde toy pianist Margaret Leng Tan amongst many others. Its a tapestry of montages, songs, and cinema verite vignettes lovingly stitched together.

    "A wonderfully textured mediation" - Ong Sor Fern, Straits Times

    "Singapore Gaga will have you swooning and guffawing through its stream of eccentricities" - Filmasia.net
