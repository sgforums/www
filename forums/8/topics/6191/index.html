    To be very honest I do not know what are the factors that "define" music as New Age. In fact, I know that I'm listening to New Age music only because the shelf on which I bought the CD read "NEW AGE". But what I do know is that New Age music seems to be able to tug on the heartstrings without the use of words. It elevates the listener into a realm of surrealism; one that is vibrant yet serene.

    And to add onto the list of recommendations, if you are into Fantasy stuffs (something like D&D settings), try some of David Arkenstone's work.
