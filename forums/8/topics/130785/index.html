    I'll be getting a new phone within 2-3 months from now.

    My requirements change from time to time, depending on what I need. Some major items are: price, size, brand, able to have chinese SMS, voice dial, performance (eg. any lag), battery life.

    For more detailed information, there is a very good local website with reviews for many phones. I say its good because they actually test the phones and give their personal opinions, which can often be different from what the manufacturers advertise.
