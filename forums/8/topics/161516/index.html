    IMHO, my ideal RPG is:
    - Good story line, but not fixed ending. for different decision u made should affect the ending of the story.(you play as the role of the main charater, that's why u should have the right to choose things.)
    - complex class/job system.
    - battle system that's complex enuff but not too troublesome to do things.
    - nice background music.(that's an important part)
    - takes you long enuff to complete the game.
    - in some part of the game, some kind of puzzle must be solved to pass certain area or get certain item. things like pushing box, solving riddle.
    - convenient to navigate around the old places the u have been before.(it's too troublesome to walk all the way to an old town for something) But u need to walk your way to new places.
