    (edited to add layman logic)

    Basically it works on 1 formula.

    X = ( X - 1 ) * ( X modulus 2 ).

    for the uninitiated what modulus does is to just throw back the remainder of a division. thus X mod 2 means remainder of anything divided by 2. In this case, the result set is either 0 or 1.
