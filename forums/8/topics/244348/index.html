    Diploma holders does get some advantage as compared to JC A level.

    Diplomas holder will be more in demand as they are equipped with practical and technical knowledge. Their salary maybe be range from $1600 to <$2000.

    Successful Diploma holder who are enrolled into University will have more module credit exemptions thus completing their basic degree in shorter time.

    One main disadvantage of getting a diploma is that the whole coure takes about 3 years in any Big5(NP,SP,TP,NYP,RP) polytechnics.

    On the other hand, you'll only need 2 years to obtain an A level cert.

    A Level holder are not as popular as Diplomas holders. Their salary usually range from $1300 to <$1800.

    Satistic have shown that 75*% who took A level when to University.

    A level holders does not have much module credits exemptions.
