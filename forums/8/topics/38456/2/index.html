    How's it going, y'all?

    I had very different board games to almost all y'all. and I don't think you guys would have heard of them. Besides, I reckon all of the ones I had or played on before have become computer games. They were...

    Cross of Iron
    Squad Leader
    Cresendo of Doom
    Risk
    Red Storm Rising (a 3D game based on WW3 between Nato and Warsaw Pact)
    A Line Across the Sand (a 3D board game based on the '91 Gulf War)
    Fortress America (a 3D game where everyone wants to attack America)
    Shogun (a 3D game where you fight to become... the shogun of medieval Japan)
    Axis & Allies (a 3D game on WW2)
    Dungeon (you choose various characters and explore a dungeon)
    Dungeons & Dragons (we actually made and painted props for our little fantasy games)

    They were 3D games 'cause they used figurines rather than flat pieces or tokens.
