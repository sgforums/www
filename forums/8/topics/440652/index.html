    Hi... Section 321 Capter 225 (punishment section 323 chapter 224) Whoever does any act with the intention of thereby causing hurt to any person, or with the knowledge that he is likely thereby to cause hurt to any person, and does thereby cause hurt to any person, is said “voluntarily to cause hurt”

    This law is very subjective because a punch is very subjective... if someone punches you but you don't are not hurt, it will not be brought to court... if you and your bestfriend fight and he accidentally punch you until you're bleed a little bit, would you want the police to arrest him? You can however file a magistrate complaint and the magistrate will review the case on a case by case basis...

    However, section 322 Cpater 224 (punishment chapter 325 chapter 224) Whoever voluntarily causes hurt, if the hurt which he intends to cause or knows himself to be likely to cause is grievous hurt, and if the hurt which he causes is grievous hurt, is said “voluntarily to cause grievous hurt”.

    Grevious hurt is classified as section 320 Chapter 224:-

    emasculation;

    permanent privation of the sight of either eye;

    destruction or permanent impairing of the powers of any member or joint;
    (f)
    permanent disfiguration of the head or face;
    (g)
    fracture or dislocation of a bone;
    (h)
    any hurt which endangers life, or which causes the sufferer to be, during the space of 20 days, in severe bodily pain, or unable to follow his ordinary pursuits;
