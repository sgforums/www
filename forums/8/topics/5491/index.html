    Hi Metalheads!

    Naw...I dun think you'll find many gals who listen to metal. In the first place the metal genre is not even mainstream amongst guys! Let alone gals!

    I mean metal is quite an extreme form of music (esp Black Metal) and its not really everyone's cuppa tea. I personaly took quite a while before I got used to the hard rockin heavy distortion sounds, plus incomprehensible vocals. Lyrics like this can be hard to swallow:

    And to Her dead reflection
    Twas as if Her pallor gleamed
    Like an angel's warmed by candles
    Where erotic stains had cleaved
    So demons dragged this libertine
    Lusts screaming for release
    Upon the flesh of maidens preened
    As canvas for caprice

    (extract from "Cruelty Brought Thee Orchids" by Cradle of Filth)

    Also, the image of metal has never been a respectable one. Its generally assoiciated with anguish, pessimissim, satanisam, sex, lust, violence, vulgarites etc etc to the extreme. In addition, the artists representing this genre are not very conventional in terms of looks, mannerism and image. Hardly a genre where "good" boys and gals would listen to if u ask me

    Lastly I would attribute the lack of exposure as part of this problem. If metal were given more time on local radio (which up till this point of time should constitute 0%), perhaps you might see more sg gals (and guys) listen to this form of music. Moreover, you can't really walk into a CD shop and ask the storekeeper "Hey! Can I have a copy of Dimmu Borgir's latest album"

    But ultimately, metal was born of the underground scene and should remain in there where it's sound can remain untouched by the influences of modern musical trends. Its a form of music not many would experience, but those who do will know the beauty behind the mayhem

    Heehee, must admit I oversteped the premesis of this topic a little (and I did have a slight biased towards Black metal in my arguments)! But the bottom line is there are not many gals who listen to metal, be it in sg or any part of the world!

    Love and Peace.

    (BTW...CoF's Cruelty and the Beast is a classic example of a great concept album! Do get it if u are a metalhead!)
