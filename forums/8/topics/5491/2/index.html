    lingirl and spooky:

    Hmm..afraid I do not really know how to define "Power Progressive Metal" (PPM), but I thougt it has something to do with the added keyboard effects. The keyboard plays alongside the usual electric and drums (plus the often heard but seldom seen bass). The effect is that power progressive sounds more "grand" than regular metal. The vocals in PPM are usually also clean and clear, and are of very good standards (they can easily give their contemporarys in mainstream music a run for their money). Also, most of their themes are not so dark.

    Perhaps you can call them "mainstreamed metal" cuz its easy for mainstream listeners to appreciate it, yet it does not compromise their metal roots.

    I got started on that genre by HAMMERFALL. Try out "Glory to the Brave", their first Album. Their second album (Legacy of Kings) is oso not bad...but I still prefer their first, in part due to the superb ballad of the same title 'Glory to the Brave'
