    Train your legs first. First at the side of the pool. Once you are pretty comfortable kicking for a long time without feeling tired, get a float.

    Use that float to keep your head above water. Swim for 2 laps and increase gradually, just kicking only. Practise for a few weeks. After that, learn how to pull up properly. Not doing properly can make you drink water, plus run out of breath fast. It can also slow you down a lot, because the pulling up motion can help you move a distance.
