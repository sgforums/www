    http://www.hko.gov.hk/gts/time/conversion.htm

    What we use everyday is the Gregorian calendar, the chinese calendar you are referring to is the Lunar calendar.

    From the site above, you can see for that selected year, the lunar calendar correspond to the gregorian calendar the dates.

    Simply speaking, what day for that day is what day for that day in lunar calendar.

    If you dunno how to read that calendar, I suggest you go to the bookstore and get any normal calendar, below the english number you will see chinese characters telling you is chu what for that day.

    There is no actual way to calculate what day will be what day on what calender to what calendar, however a search on google will present you various calendar converters so you just need to input the date to get all the info you need.
