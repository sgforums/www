    If you are not making any progress with officers at the counter, please insist you speak to the supervisors and keep escalating upwards into managements levels.

    Most complain I know of regarding HDB is that those frontline  customer service levels often stonewall people seeking help.

    As long as you are approaching in good faith, and seeking to pay your dues, you should not be turned away like a beggar on the street.
