    Then go overseas and convert to degree
    Make sure ur degree is 80% average
    Then u do the Australian GAMSAT or USA's MCAT....and see if u can get into medical school. U need to be at least a 65 %tile to get into the med school of ur choice.

    Psychiatrists - u got a powerful arsenal of anti psychotics to dispense out and alot of responsibilities pertaining to a person's mental state

    Psychology - only behaviour therapy , listening therapy etc No drugs
