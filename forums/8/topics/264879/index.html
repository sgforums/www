    Get at least a 0.75 carat .

    Diamonds - you need to look for cut , clarity , brilliance, color and something else I can't remember. Go for solid carats not cut up ones. A good 0.5 point solid will cost around 1.5k by itself - mastercut which affects brilliance , very small flaws, white to colorless.

    Then we talk about workmanship - platinum will cost more than gold. So opt for gold if you need to be thrifty.

    If you want small - no certification usually - u want big like 0.75 to 1 carat- certification given for insurance purposes . I like GIA certified or Debeers cert ( but I don't think there is one anymore). Don't let the jeweller hoodwink you about diamonds ....better to get certified and check it with another jeweller. Alot of fake diamonds around.

    Whatever you do , don't buy cognac diamonds - that is being cheapskate and it is shat.
