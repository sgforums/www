  • starviewer
    20 Aug 05, 18:52
    Hi,
    I think you are trying to set an encryption key for WEP right?
    Look for Wireless Settings -> WEP -> Encryption Key in your setup page
    and you are somewhere there.

    Just key in a password, and that password will be your network id.
    Other users will then need a password to use your network.

    Also, give your router a name. They call it SSID.
    After which, go to Control Panel -> Network Connections -> Your Connection ->
    Properties -> Configure -> Advanced

    Here you will see a SSID field and fill in the EXACT name of your router.

    Refer to your manual always. It provides the best instruction. Then ask what you don't understand in the manual.
