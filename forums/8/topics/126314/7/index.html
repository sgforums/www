    In a nutshell, below are the modifications made to latest editions of the textbooks on the Sino-Japanese war, as compared to the less distorted previous editions:

    1. "Comfort women" (female sex slaves of mostly Chinese and Korean origin who were forced to serve as "public toilets" for the Japanese army in the field) is now omitted.

    2. Forced Asian labour brought to Japan is now voluntary Asian labour.

    3. Death toll in Rape of Nanjing is now trivialised from figures to "many".
