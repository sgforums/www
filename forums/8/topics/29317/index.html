    To improve your pullups,train the abovementioned bodyparts twice a week

    Lats-pulldowns,rows,pullovers
    biceps-curls
    forearms-forearm rockers

    Sample routine
    pulldown 3 sets-12 reps,10,8
    pullovers -10,8
    bent over rows-10,8

    spider curls-8,6
    forearm rockers-2 sets to utter failure
