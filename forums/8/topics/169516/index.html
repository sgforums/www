  • zanza
    17 Dec 05, 23:28
    Personnally , i prefer a hardtail . If your buying your first bike , a hardtail would be a better choice as , 1. easier to control , 2. Cheaper , 3. Less maintainance. The trails at Bukit Timah don't require a full sus imho , the slight advantage u get from better control downslopes will probably not be worth the extra weight up the slopes . The downslopes are not that hardcore with hardly any 1-2 feet drops , no need full sus at all. At most u get better traction from the rear wheel. An average hardtail bike will be much cheaper than the full sus version and u can use the extra cash to get a better helmet which is always worth the investment.
