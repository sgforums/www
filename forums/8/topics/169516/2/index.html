    $250 +!

    2)Cycling Shoes (SPD types ) got 2 categories- Mountain and Road.

    Road Shoes - Meant for road bike Clipless Pedals (can clip in from only 1 side ) , not meant for walking as the soles and cleats wear out damn fast. Slippery to walk on if ur soles are made of Carbon Fibre. Cheapest is $80 (Gee Hin Chan bike shop ) . Most ex 1 I saw was $400 at Treknology.

    Mountain bike shoes - Meant for MTB Clipless pedals (can Clip in from both side of the pedals but Crank Bros Eggbeaters have 4 way entry ). MTB shoes have tough Steel cleats and their soles have tracks (like hiking boots ) to allow u to walk easily when wearing them. Can be worn regularly even when not cycling. I'm not sure about their prices though.. sorry...
