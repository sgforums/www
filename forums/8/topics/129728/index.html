    Actually, I think you're expected at this stage to know the differences liow....

    Anyway....

    A bachelor in Arts is a basic degree in the arts subjects - languages, history,
    geography, sociology and other humanity subjects.

    A bachelor in Science is a basic degree in the science subjects - physics, chem, maths, etc etc.

    Unfortunately, none of the above are directly business-related.

    For business degrees, there's Bachelor of Business, Business Admin, Business Studies, etc....different universities have slightly different names.
