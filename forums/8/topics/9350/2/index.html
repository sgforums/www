    You can refer to Straits Time Life! This Weekend 24 August 2000 copy about Singapore Beaches (if you still have one) and 31 August (?) 2000 copy about making-out in remote parts of Singapore (too bad I didnt keep a copy)

    ---------------------------------

    1. As reported in ST, Laguna Golf Green (off Xilin Avenue), the bridge over Sungei Ketapang.

    2. Changi Coast Road Cau-ser-rina (spelling?) forest.

    3. East Coast Park Area H, there's a mud track leading beyond the park, near Sungei Bedok.

    4. Tanjong Irau (Formerly a popular beach), you have to drive there by accessing Yishun Avenue 7 (near Blk 289) and onto a un-named track and follow straight.

    5. Punggol Point, the stretch of Punggol 17th Avenue leading to Punggol Marina.

    6. Changi Ferry Road (off Changi Coast Road) leading to Changi-Johor Ferry Terminal.

    7. Marina East, near East Coast Park Area A.

    8. Area near Tuas Checkpoint. Tuas South area.

    9. Changi Bay reclaimed land (this is where the new Changi Naval Base is to be build, but currently the problem is there's no way to get there!)

    10. Upper Peirce Reservoir Park (Great view to Ang Mo Kio)

    11. Lower Peirce Reservoir Park

    12. Lower Seletar Reservoir Park
