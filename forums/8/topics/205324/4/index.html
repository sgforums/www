    The salary you get depends on a lot of factors.

    As someone mentioned, it depends on the industry you're in. Companies in certain categories of industries have a high salary pay scale than others.

    All companies have their own pay scale, or put it in your terms, your starting salary. Before one joins the company as an employee, it might be good to find out about the pay scale, promotion prospects and compensation plans.

    People who have more experience are naturally paid higher relatively. They simply provide more value to the company. So if you want to have a pay increment, you should probably look at how much you're worth to the company.
