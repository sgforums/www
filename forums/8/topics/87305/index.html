House of Flying Daggers

  • Blueray
    27 Jul 04, 18:04
    Spoiler Warning!!

    The plot is set during the tang dynasty. The House of Flying daggers has been a thorn in tang government's flesh. Under a mysterious new leader, this organization grows even more powerful.

    Two captains were ordered to capture the new leader within ten days. They suspect that a pretty new dancer at the local Peony Pavilion is actually related to the old leader and begin their investigation.

    Director's direction: All of Zhang Yi mou's trademarks can be found in this film. Breathtaking and artistic scenery of forests, rivers and valleys are all featured.

    Acting: Andy Lau was quite disappointing. He fails to potray convincingly in this film. His expressions regarding the heavy burden of his task and emotions looked forced and unnatural. He also don't seem comfortable in delivering his lines.
    Takeshi Kaneshiro and Zhang Ziyi's on screen romance also failed to spark any chemistry. The bulk of this film is about the love and romance between the two. The lack of chemistry makes this journey painfully long winded.

    Trivial: The late Anita Mui was supposed to star as a supporting role in the film. That role is played by Song DanDan.

    My Verdict: It is not really worth spending money to watch this film unless you are a hardcore fan of Takeshi Kaneshiro or Zhang Ziyi(plenty of screen time for both of them) Wait for TV to screen it.
