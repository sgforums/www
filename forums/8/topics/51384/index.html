    Iranian Ladan Bijani died on Tuesday shortly after an operation to separate her from her twin sister, hospital officials announced.

    The conjoined twins, Laleh and Ladan, were earlier separated at about 2.30pm on Tuesday.

    But Raffles Hospital spokesperson Prem Kumar Nair said both had lost a lot of blood and are in critical condition.

    He said two teams of doctors were trying to stabilise them.

    Dr Nair told the waiting media to pray hard for them.

    Dr Nair said: "I have just been informed that Laleh and Ladan have been separated just a short while ago. However, they have both lost a lot of blood and are actually in a critical state. Ladan more than Laleh.

    "So there are two teams of doctors in the operating theatre that are trying very hard to stabilise them.

    "I have just been given the news and I do not have any further information. But I'll try and come back to you with some information as soon as I can. Please pray very hard for them."
