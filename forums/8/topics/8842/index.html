    Recently, I've been watching a Japanese serial called 'Back to the Original World' [literal translation]. In the show, the male lead dies of a heart attack at 00:00 01.01.2000, and gets transported back to 1995. In his 2nd life, he uses the knowledge of the future and became successful and rich, but inevitably failed to correct any mistakes he made in his 1st life. At the end of his 2nd life, he got transported back to 1995 for his 3rd life ... blah blah blah ... so basically, he keeps re-living the time period from 1995-2000.

    After some background knowledge, what would you do if you had the chance to re-live your life? Would you live it the same old way, believing nothing can change fate? Would you use your knowledge to get wealth and power? Or would you refrain from doing the things you had regretted? I thought that the show was very thought provoking ...
