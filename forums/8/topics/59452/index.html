    There are many possibilities for your system's erratic behaviour. BearBear may be right and your battery is flat. Also, it may be possible that your power supply is faulty and there is either a short somewhere, or your peripherals are not getting enough power. Motherboard shorts could also cause this problems.

    The first step you should actually take is to ensure it is not a software problem. So, reformat and reinstall windows. If the problem persists, you know that you definitely have a hardware problem. Check if there are any burns on your motherboard (highly likely), your cpu (not so likely), your power supply and lastly the other peripherals. Try removing all unnecessary peripherals (sound card etc, since you'll just be rendering 3D). If everything seems in order and yet the problem persists, as FireIce had mentioned, yes, jie ai shun bian.
