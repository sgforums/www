    Time to go

    HOST Frustrated wonders if her foreign house guest has outstayed her welcome.

    She says: "She's now in her fifth week at our place. She eats our food (bread, milk, eggs, fruits and whatever she can find in the kitchen), she gets our maid to do her laundry. Our maid has to clean up after her - does her dishes, cleans up the kitchen after she cooks."

    "Is this acceptable Western behaviour?" she asks.

    fishy throws in some philosophy: "An old uncle of mine has a saying - house guests are like fish, after three days, they begin to smell! Throw them out!"

    mug says: "I'm sure you know that to be like that as a guest in someone's house isn't a typical behaviour for many cultures. Just tell her to go."
