    SITEX is one of Singapore's largest IT and Technology Show

    Since its inception, SITEX has grown significantly in size and stature, spanning over 20,000 SQM in exhibition space last year. The 2004 presentation attracted 150 exhibitors and played host to more than 500,000 visitors.

    The 2005 edition of SITEX will be held from 24th to 27th November at Singapore Expo, Hall 4 & 5. The 4 days event aims to attract 700,000 visitors and will showcase new concepts and products of the IT industry.

    Visitors can look forward to exciting and dedicated pavilions such as; iEnvironment @ SITEX, which is a FIRST time ever joint presentation by SITEX and Temasek Polytechnic featuring Intelligent Living Environment.

    Home Biz Small Biz, a dedicated platform catering to SMEs and Home-Based business owners who are seeking infocomm products to enhance their business productivity.

    MP3 Gallery, a showcase of solutions packed with hours of portable digital listening. Totally fascinating with displays of the latest MP3 lifestyles gadgets and complementing accessories to meet sophiscated needs of music lovers.

    Home Entertainment @ SITEX, a galore of technology highlights to address the complex needs of urban homes today. Power packed with the latest home theatre and multimedia entertainment systems customised to suit varied room settings in Singapore.
