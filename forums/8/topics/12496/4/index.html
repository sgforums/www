    the game depends on the map. in ladder games where money and gas are limited like Lost Temple, zerg usually, in fact nearly always will go a 2nd hatch before making a spawning pool. sometimes the hatch is placed at the expansion, sometimes at the ramp. this build order has become popular especially after the v1.08 patch because the zergling rush is seriously weakened. and usually there is always an attack in the first 5-6 minutes. terran usually have marines/firebats/medics as the first wave. protoss is more variable---pretty difficult to use it on lost temple. zerg always get attacked, cos they go for the early expansion. if they don't, its easy to get locked up. these sort of maps require extensive scouting and reconnaisance because of the lack of resources.

    but in big maps like Big Game Hunters, its different. its much more brute force. if its 2v2 or 3v3, a common strategy is where one side will quickly triple or double-team one of their opponents. but sometimes people lock themselves in for a while. this can work because the resources are enough to last the whole game, and supply exceeds demand until at least the middlegame. as for 4v4, its always one word: RUSH.
    note that the player at 5 oclock in BGH is at a disadvantage in the early middlegame because terran can tankdrop at his ledge. the other bases cannot be tankdropped.
