Drivers!!! BEWARE of the new Red Light Camera

  • skygal

    Hi all drivers,

    The Singapore Traffic Police have upgraded all their red light cameras. Almost 92% of all the cameras have been upgraded all around Singapore.

    All the cameras are now using a new technology called the vehicle recognition system.

    No more road loop sensors (those black lines on the road) needed to detect vehicles speeding off the red lights. It is because all the cameras are now using Virtual Loops. The camera system will scan all the time to see if any vehicle passing in and out of the virtual loops during the red light and records all the information.

    These system significantly reduces many problems that once faced by the older capturing system and capturing performance increases by 50%. Now all the cameras are integrated with a high end computer system which are 24hrs online. These computers are installed with wireless modems which allow the Singapore Traffic Police download the captured images remotely from a Central Computer and quickly send out the summons.

    Hope you will drive carefully next time.
