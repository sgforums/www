    How Is Salmon Fish Oil Created?

    The best quality salmon oil is created by using a cold-press method of extracting oil from the head of the salmon fish. During normal processing, the heads are removed and the processing plant uses a flesh press to squeeze the oil out. Some companies add other natural supplements to the oil and some make pure, virgin oil with no other added ingredients.
