    Big Bubble Centre
    DiverCity Scuba

    You need to undergo the Open Water Diver Course first, it consist of 2 theory lesson, 2 pool session plus a dive trip over the weekends to Pulau Aur/Dayang or Pulau Tioman for your OpenWater Diver certification.

    Normally the schedule is like; Monday (Theory), Tuesday (Pool session), Wednesday (Pool session), Thursday (Theory and a paper test) and move out for dive trip from friday evening and back to S'pore by sunday nite.

    Normally should be able to clock at least 4 dive on saturday and 2 dive on sunday then set off after lunch time.

    The current market rate for OpenWater Course is about $450(Lesson plus Dive Trip and accommedation/food), if got more people enrol together might get a cheaper bargain.

    If u want to know more about diving, u can go to this site to find out more about the individual schools and clubs of the local diving community.

    FinsOnline

    There's even an online forum talking about diving 99.9% of the time. So u can hang around there.

    There are local courses conducted as well. But the visibillity of our open seas is not really impressive compared to those training grounds at Pulau Aur or Tioman. But u will be surprised with our local marine life.

    2 theory lessons (plus tests), 2 pool lessons and 4 open water dives are all u need to achieve the Open Water Diver certification. Maximum depth u can go to is 18m, of which why most people would go for the Advance Open Water certification next.
