    From Divers' Dream

    A typical day outing would depart at 8:30 am from meeting point which is currently at the WEST COAST PIER, which is next to the Republic of Singapore Yacht Club (RSYC). The local 'bumboats', which use for diving activities, will take about 45 minutes to get to the dive site. After a general briefing, it's time to get wet. One dive before lunch and another after. Will head back to the mainland when everyone has exited the water and should reach the jetty by 3 pm.

    Cost
    S$50 (with your own equipment)
    S$65 (include rental of BCD and Regulator system)
    These fees will include boat fees, 2-tank dive, weights and weight belt and equipment as listed above.
    What are NOT included are your personal gears of mask, snorkel and fins, wetsuit, lunch and water.

    Rental charges for equipment:
    S$10 = BCD
    S$10 = Regulator system
    S$10 = Shorties wetsuit
    S$10 = Mask, snorkel and fins
    Charges are for local rental only and on per day basis. Rental for oversea trips available. Enquire for the rates.
