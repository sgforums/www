    I have said it before, I said it again, I will say it again and i say it now.

    If you are planning to buy at Sim Lim,  DO YOUR HOMEWORK!

    Find out who are AUTHORISED to sell what you are buying.

    When you buy, CHECK that documents are ISSUED by them. Don't buy a camera from ABC Pte Ltd, but the stamp on the warranty card is XYZ Pte Ltd.

    DO NOT expect good discounts. The days of 50% off are over. SLS nowadays offer 10-20% below Retail Prices.

    Electronics now come with "Quick Set up guide" Usually it prints the CONTENTS of the box. Check this list with what you are getting.

    DO YOUR HOMEWORK, free gifts given by AUTHORISED shops are OFFICIAL from the brand. Do your research on what free gifts you will get.

    Yes, there are some blacksheeps in the building, but if you take steps to be careful. You will be safe.

    Lastly, PLEASE SUPPORT the honest shops in SLS. Its a long term investment. Here is why,

    Once they evetually die out, getting electronics at a bargain would be a thing of the past. You will end up having to pay things at full retail prices. Big Chain Stores will be laughing to the bank as they are ultimately the only winner.
