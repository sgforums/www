    Any Japanese food lovers?

    There's a Shima restaruant at Goodwood Park Hotel. Teppanyaki style cooking, its a "eat till you drop" kind which you could request the chef to cook for you after the first round. I like the solmon there, very fresh..you can order as much as you like. $40+ a person.

    Next I recommend is Arashi Japanese Restaurant at Paragon, I've been there twice.. the food is quite standard, likely is the ambience which makes me go back there for my dinner date. Price depends what you ordered, is btw range 15-20+.
