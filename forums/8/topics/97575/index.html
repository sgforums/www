    Regarding No 2. you will receive an email prompting you that you have received payment..you have to confirm-accept on that payment whithin a week ( i think) of receiving that e.mail... once you have confirmed-accepted it, the payment will then be put through to your paypal account and you can proceed to transfer to your bank account.If you dont confirm accept, you will not see the amount in your pay pal account. There are charges for transfer the amount to your bank account - I think its $1US per transaction if I can recall.
    You can leave the amount in there too if you dun wish to withdraw it and use the amount there for future purchases that you may have.
