    Donna Summer: Hello.

    (Big screen shows footage)

    RS: Wow! Apparently they'll wear anything we ask them to, eh! We'll go for a break right now, and we'll come back with the results in just ONE second!

    (Commercial break)

    RS: Lets get to the results. Fantasia, Simon said you suck. Donna Summer said you were good, but I don't think she meant it. Congrats!!

    RS: We'll get to the other 3 idols, but first lets see a performance by your idols. It ought to be fun to see if Fantasia, ONE of the BOTTOM TWO still be able to prance around gaily.

    (Idols take the stage, Fantasia prances around bravely, as if nothing has happened)

    (Commercial break)

    RS: Welcome back, who's joining Fantasia as the BOTTOM TWO? We'll find out in a second. Haha, I just love putting the whole America in suspense. Now, lets talk to Fantasia. How do you feel, having known that you have received the one of the two lowest number of votes?
