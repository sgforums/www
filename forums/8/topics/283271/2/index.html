    U can open a POSB MSA. If ur intention is to save $500 per month, this should be the account I guess. It's a monthly contribution account, means every month it'll auto deduct the $500 from ur account. Interest rate will be at 1%p.a. U can increase or decrease ur monthly contribution amount when u get any pay increment or pay cut at any period also. It's flexible, u can withdraw money from this account anytime but withdrawal can only be done over the counter. So unless u need the money really badly, I don't think u'll wanna join the long queue at the branch. U can put in any excess money, eg bonus, hongbaos... into this account at any time to enjoy the 1% interest instead of the norm 0.25%. Juz top-up via internet, fund transfer via atm or cash deposit thru the CAM or counter. But anytime that u do a withdrawal from this account, ur interest will be reduced to 0.45%p.a. for that particular month. U can close off this account anytime u wan, or the bank will close off this account if there's any 3 unsuccessful deduction from ur savings account. So overall, it's flexible, convenient and it helps u to save. Can view this account's balances thru internet banking but juz cannot transfer out only.
