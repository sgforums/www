  • sdaddy03

    The National University of Singapore Student's Union Business Committee would like to invite you to its first ever large scale beach party themed, on the 8th of February 2003 at Siloso Beach, Sentosa.

    Being an overnight event from 6pm to 6am, this beach party will feature some of the top local bands and DJs in Singapore. Local bands such as Farfetched, Kel, Eniale, Plain Sunset, Pug Jelly, Surreal and Vertical Rush will be performing from 6pm to 10pm. After which, top local DJs from Frontallabs.com, GarageSaleShop.com and Europa such as DJ Boy, DJ
    Gerald, DJ Janson, DJ Keishi and many others will be spinning through the night. In conjunction with our theme, attractive gifts and prizes will be given out on an hourly basis.

    Advanced tickets (inclusive of entry to Sentosa and one free drink) are priced at $25 (exclusive of $1 Sistic charge) at all Sistic outlets.

    We look forward to your participation in our event. Please feel free to contact Cheryl at 90269756 or email us at
    craveparty@yahoo.com.sg. You can also check out our website at http://crave2003.cjb.net.
