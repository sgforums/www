    I believe that any form of trade whether it be monetary or barter trade, a business registration has to be registered with the ROC. Unless you are solely surviving on the voluntary donations from people, than a business registration won't be necessary but still you have to register a non-profit organisation through ROC as well.

    Bottomline is, yes, you need to register whether be it profit or non-profit organisation. Check with www.acra.gov.sg , they have more information there.
