    Originally posted by contrarian:
    Can anyone recommend a tailor for shirts? I've not done it yet in Singapore and would like opinions on good shirt tailors.
    If you're looking for good tailor, can try Oxford Tailor at OUB Centre. I get all my shirts there, and trousers from another tailor at OUB (can't remember name). Tailor-made shirts are a little more costly ($60 upwards depending on material), but once they've got your exact measurements, all you need to do is to pop in and choose the fabric / colour each time. Trousers are more expensive ($100 upwards), some more they recommend handwash only, but for me no choice cos not easy for me to find size 28!

    Fabric quality are definitely better than the cheap off-the-rack ones, fabric designs more contemporary, don't have to queue at fitting rooms to try sizes, and can specify your design (wide-collar, double-cuff, trouser-cuff, pleats, etc). And they definitely last longer than the cheap shirts you find off the rack.
