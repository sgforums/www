    The crux of not eating in MRTs is basically to prevent:

    1. the proliferation of pungent food smell
    2. dirtying up the localised area
    3. basic courtesy to your fellow commuter

    If a person can do eat/drink WITHOUT violating the above 3, then i have no issues. Usually, examples are taking medicine, drinking plain water from a carrier bottle, baby drinking from a milk bottle, popping a sweet into your mouth etc.
