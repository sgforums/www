    Sept 17 (Sat) : Mid-Autum Nite Hike
    When: 6 - 10pm
    Where: MacRitchie to Upper Bukit
    Who: Everyone
    Cost: Free
    RSVP : By Sept 12 (Mon) - te4m4ct@yahoo.com
    What to bring : Water, mooncakes/snacks, torch light/lanterns(essential as most of the trail is pitch dark), insect repellent (for those with sweet blood)
    Attire : Sports wear and comfortable walking/hikingshoes

    Description:
    Come and join us for a Mid-Autumn Festival celebration - nite hike through a Singapore tropical forest! Along the way, you will encounter things that you would not have seen in the day, experience and explore the after-dark behaviour of wildlife, and immerse yourself in the sound of music! In the quiet of the night, all you can hear will be your breathing as you trek through the woods, no traffic, only the lovely sound from nature under the star-lit, full-moon sky. Don't miss this! Come experience an adventure which you always wanted! An experienced trek leader will be leading the hike.

    Meeting place : Double bus-stop in front of MacRitchie Reservoir Park.

    Note : As the start and end points are different, hikers are advised to leave their vehicle at home
