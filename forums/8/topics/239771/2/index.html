    when ppl say play 20 cents / 40 cents

    means when One tile (Yi Tai)
    if A give u win, he pay u 40 cents
    B and C pay u half price which is 20 cents

    When two tiles (Liang Tai)
    If A give u win, he pay u 80 cents
    B and C pay u half price which is 40 cents

    the highest in SG usually we play is maximum 5 tiles which is 3.20/ 6.40

    But when we say we play 30 cents / 60 cents
    the calculation abit diff

    means when One tile (Yi Tai)
    if A give u win, he pay u 2 dollar
    B and C pay u half price which is 1 Dollar

    the caculation are:
    $1/$2
    $2/$3
    $3/$5
    $5/10
    $10/$20

    but den when we play 40 cents/80 cents
    things are back to normal

    How to calculate tiles
    Example 1: During DONG (East) round, and u are sitting in Dong,
    If u pong Dong or get 3 Dong, u get 2 tiles
    If B pong Dong or get 3 Dong, he get 1 tiles
    If u get Flower with the number 1, U get 1 tiles
    If u get ani animals, u get 1 tiles

    Qing Yi Se (All same kind, like all SUO, all Wang, All Tong) = 4 tiles
    Ban Se (Same Kind + abit or many DA PAI) = 2 Tiles (which will become more tiles if ur DA PAI is in ur favour, if u got dong as ur da pai and its dong round, u get one more tile lor, unless u also sitting at dong position, u get 2 tiles instead

    HONG ZONG, FA CAI, BAI BAN = 1 tile

    Pong Pong Hu = 2 tiles
    Ping Hu (With at least one flower or animal) = 1 tiles
    Ping Hu (without any flower or animal) = 4 tiles
    Note: Ping Hu , u cannot use those DA Pai in ur favour as ur EYEs
