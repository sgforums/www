    National Skin Centre sees 80 to 120 people a month for tattoo removal

    SINGAPORE: The Singapore National Skin Centre said it sees about 80 to 120 people a month for tattoo removal.

    They include first timers, and those returning for repeat laser sessions, as it takes an average of eight sessions to remove a palm-sized tattoo.

    While getting a palm-sized tattoo costs about S$500, the centre said that removing it will cost some S$6,000.

    However, there are some limitations in tattoo removals.

    Laser treatment removes dark colours better than light tones. Also, the laser does not break the colour content of reds or greens as well as blacks and dark blues
