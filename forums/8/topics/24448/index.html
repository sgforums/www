    Well it really depends on your budget, i guess its the most important.

    There r a range of pixels u can choose, but personally, i would recommend 2.0 megapixels and above if u want to print out a decent 8R pic. Of cos, the higher pixel, the more exp.

    Than u need to know wat features u want, like u want movie mode with audio? 3 X optical? Lightweight? Propietary battery or AA batt? etc..
    But bear in mind that brands like Canon IXUS sacarifice features for lightweight! So its really up to u.

    Personally, i would give Canon all the thumbs up and stay away from other brands! Maybe except Sony if u got the money to spare.

    I personaly recommend Canon A40. Its a 2.0 megapixel cam, with 3 X optical, movie mode with audio and 4 X AA battery. Its not small and metallic like IXUS , but i find IXUS too small to hold properly. Moreover, with AA battery instead of Canon batt, u save money and last longer since u can always recharge rechargeable AA battery at low cost. And if u run out of batt, u can just buy one off the counter. Its overall a good cam for beginner or amatuer.

    It cost about 530? The place i bought is at Parasilk in Holland Village, they offer the best price, even better than SLS.

    If u want really good ones, i recommend G2. It only cost u one thousand only and it comes with lotsa stats.
