    personally, i recommend the UOB visa gold debit card. Visa is accepted by more places than mastercard. I am an UOB fan by the way.

    it boils down to 3 factors:

    1) Visa or mastercard

    2) bling factor. you want platinum, gold, or ordinary card.

    3) benefits. discount available for cards by different banks.

    UOB got the widest range of discounts, compared to dbs and ocbc, among the local banks.

    ATM-wise, although posb, dbs have the most atms, uob and ocbc ain't far behind too.

    cos of lower user-rate, the queue at uob/ocbc atm is much much faster than that of posb,dbs. 1 uob atm is faster than the 3-5 dbs atm. you can observe this at any mall or mrt station.

    also, uob and ocbc atms are interchangeable. so your atm coverage is quite considerable.

    as for the other banks, i wouldn't suggest it. their ATM are hard to find, and comes with limited discounts. also, depositing of cash might not be convenient.
