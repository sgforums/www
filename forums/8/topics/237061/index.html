    GCE A-Level Examination Results Release For Private Candidates

    Results for the 2006 GCE A-Level examination will be released on 2 March 2007 (2.30 pm). All private candidates can either use the password which has been sent to them through post or use their SingPass to access their results by clicking here.

    Private candidates can also appeal for their results through this website from 2 March 2007 (2.30 pm) to 12 March 2007 (11.59 pm). Candidates can appeal for any number of subjects.

    Appeal fees are chargeable as follows:
    (a) $168.00 per A-Level subject (inclusive of GST)
    (b) $100.80 per AO-Level or H1-Level subject (inclusive of GST)

    Please bring along your identification document, e.g. NRIC and Passport, to pay for your appeal fees (either cash or NETS) between 2 March 2007 (2.00 pm) and 13 March 2007 (6.00 pm) at MOE, Customer Service Centre (1, North Buona Vista Drive).

    The appeal request will be deemed as cancelled if payment is not made by the stipulated date.For clarifications, please contact the Ministry of Education, Customer Service Centre at 6872 2220.
