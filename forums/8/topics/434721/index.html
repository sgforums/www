    Clementi Jams:

    Below is the list of jams near Clementi area or Bukit Batok and Jurong area.

    Jams in Singapore(Clementi-Jurong area)

    1)Clementi Avenue 6 from PIE to AYE (Weekdays Morning only)
    2)Clementi Avenue 6 from AYE and Commonwealth Ave West to PIE (Weekdays Night only)
    3)Commonwealth Ave West to Clementi Avence 3to CP 20(Jam all the way to Clementi Avenue 6 or Clementi Avence 2
    or from Clementi Avenue 6 till Commonwealth West to CP 20)
    4)Clementi Avenue 2 goes towards Commonwealth Avence West from West Coast Road
    5)West Coast Road from West Coast Highway all the way to Clementi Avence 2 to Commonwealth Avenue West
    6)Commonwealth Avenue West from Boon Lay Way and Toh Tuck Ave towards Clementi Central before Clementi
    Avence 6
    7)Bukit Batok East Avence 3 from Bukit Batok Street 23 and Bukit Batok Central to Toh Tuck Avenue
    8)Jurong Town Hall Road from After Jurong East Central to AYE/Teban FlyOver.
    9)North Buona Vista Road/Buona Vista FlyOver from Dover Road to AYE

    The current road condition in Clementi is bad enough because
    1.There are a lot fo private condo buses parking in the bus stops---Eg,The Chevrons,The Botonia,The Infinti,The Carellia (spelling might be error)
    There will be 5 private buses at one time and CAUSED JAMS.
    2.There are railings as you had mentioned.
    3.The taxi did not obey the principle as there only wirtten MAX 3 TAXIS but it can go all the way up to 10 taxis
    4.There are some inconsiderate drivers who tend to park/wait the cars there as some might just buy food or deposit/withdraw cash so they park thier cars
    at LANE 1.By doing so,it had taken up the Lane 1 with the Taxis as well and Priavte buses

    and dont worry,it had been jamming there since 6 years ago in 2005 as I lived there so long I had get used to it.So with the New Clementi Bus Interchange,
    it will make no difference even if it skipped the current old bus stops and go to interchange straight away.

    Our conditions were like Nex@Serangoon.The road got 3 lanes.SAME AS OURS and jams all over.Anyway we did say we will ike to have 1-2 more lanes so it has 5 lanes but it is rather
