    Hmm...for starters, do not bring plastic or shopping bags. They make you look gay.

    Bring a utility knife.

    When they say bring 3 sets of clothing, bring 4.

    Bring all the optional items, if any, included in the list.

    Bring Torchlight.

    Poncho.

    Hiking boots are good.

    Bring sandals/slippers. For times when you just do not need shoes. Eg: going to toilet in the middle of the night, after shower, etc.

    Most important: an open mind, prone to new possibilities. If this is your first time camping, be prepared to face a little bit of hardship. But it will be fun!
