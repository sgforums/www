    Adult acne
    Mostly due to stress and other environmental and lifestyle factors.
    ie drastic temperature changes triggers oil production. eg: from the freezing aircon room to hawker centers for lunch
    Take note of such factors.

    Look on the bright side, you will not have wrinkles showing up asfast as your same age dry skin counterpart. Oil control products are cheaper than wrinkle /dry skin products.

    To avoid oily skin. The irony is that people over wash. Washing stimulate more oil production because you skin is trying to protect itself from the environment factors and maintain moisture. Its natural. Overwashing case the top layer to dry up and you'd end up with a lot of blackheads because of the dry skin flakes with are not effectively shedded off.

    Actually, just use clean tissue/oil absorbing paper to dab the oil off if you find your face oily (even at home). Don't think that oil is all bad, its just cause shine. All you need to do is just to make sure you cleanse well to prevent clogging up the pores.
    Depends on you skin type to discover the correct combination for your cleansing needs. For me, I like things with mild acid to rid of my top layer.
    And, do try to get a light gel moisturiser cause it helps prevent dry skin in our high air con offices/homes and thus reduce blackheads.

    Lastly matte look is out, matte radiance is in. ie Glow! So think of your skin giving you a natural glow...
