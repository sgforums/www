    If you just wanna know the basics, there're 20 teams in the Premier League. Over the season, each team plays the each of the other teams twice, which makes 38 matches a season. One game at home, one game away. You get 3 points per win, 1 point per draw, and none for losing. At the end of the season, the top 3 teams qualify for European Champions' League the following season, where they pit against the champions of the other leagues in Europe. The FA Cup, Worthington's Cup and European Champion's League are different competitions altogether.
