    Hmmmmm.....What is the meaning of a real friend?!

    To me, the definition of a real friend is someone that is there for you, through thick and thin, when you are up and down. Someone there to listen to your problems, Someone there to give you an unbiased advice. It doesn't matter how you meet that friend or how to communicate with them. The important thing is that they understand you and is true to you.

    Why should it matter how you meet the person?! Whether you guys are chatting on the phone, in person, or on the net?! The bottom line is you need emotional support. You need someone to understand you and be there for you.

    Maybe our definition of "real friends" are different. Mine are in the sense of emotionally, whereas your's in in the sense of physically. For me, even if that person are there with me face-to-face, I can't consider them as a real friend unless I get to know the person, they just can't become a real friend to me cuz they are there physically.

    Yes, being too involve on the net might cause some people to shut out their friends in real life, but that does not apply to everyone. I do admit that I spend a lot of time on the net in the forum on IRC, etc. But I never did shut out of friends in real life. I go clubing and have dinner w/ them at times. Why wonder if you can trust them or rely on them?! Just do it and if they disappoint you then change your views on them. You have to give them a chance before you can get to know the real person inside....
