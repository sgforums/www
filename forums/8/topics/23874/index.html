    oh ok....

    Well.... before break up, you really feel buay tahan about the other person that you must get rid of him/her. Then break up.

    First few days damn unbearable. Because you have to get used to not having someone call you or called somebody every other day. You suddenly don't hear the person's voice anymore.

    You will feel sad. You will regret, and ask yourself "have I done the right thing..." Then, you will want the person back. Chances are the person will not want to patch up. Then you feel even more painful.

    However, if the person agrees to patch up, you will go back to the buay tahan him/her stage and start all over again.

    First week is painful, but second week, you will feel much better(except for still a little pain for some people). But you will start to get angry with your ex over what he/she did that has caused the breakup.

    After dunno how long (varies from person to person), you will start to regret for doing what you did to your ex before the breakup. The guilt feeling will develop in you.

    dunno if this is true to everybody here or not. This is what most of my friends share with me. But for me, only first week feel unbearable... after that, I am ok, as if never been with the person at all. Life is short mah! Cannot feel depressed over one person for too long.
