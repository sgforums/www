    Many motherboards these days, can accept ECC memories.

    Leaving your system running for days is ok so long as you have the latest Norton Internet Security (Antivirus & Firewall) installed, and automatically updated every 1 hour.

    Periodically clear your Internet Browser's cache to prevent lag, also make sure your PC's interior has sufficient cooling (exhaust fans / intake fans) to ventilate - so that hot-spots don't develope.

    Never share your power outlet of your PC system with anything else like a fan, a hifi, a small TV, etc.
