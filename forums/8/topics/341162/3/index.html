    6 years ago, i used to be in ITE, and it's not the end lah.

    here's my experience:

    1) not many hot chicks, unlike poly......most girls are actually quite plain jane type.....of course once in a while see pretty girl, but don't get your hopes too high. That's what Japanese porn is for.

    2) life is quite slack unlike poly or JC, and the stress is not so much. The learning pace is stable and steady, good for a lot of people

    3) ITE students are not as scheming as polys, and especially, JCs. I can't imagine why any sane human would want to enter JCs. that kind of institutes are for snobs, kids of ministers, and animals

    4) quite good food. If you go ITE Dover, got one stall selling western food. Die Die also MUST TRY their CHICKEN CUTLET. The taste is totally legendary, and you will never find anything like that again. Botak Jones and Jack's Place, when compared to them, is like dog food. Pizza Hut and McDonald's compared to them is like dog poo. No kidding. Not sure if it's still around, though.

    5) if you go ITE, must conduct yourself with honor and discipline. Singaporeans generally don't have good opinion of ITE kids, so you must prove them wrong.

    6) ITE life is easy and free, so you must have discipline because it's very tempting and easy to slack your 2 years away there playing the whole time due to the environment.

    7) contrary to popular belief, there are few bullies in ITE and even the gangsters (if any) are pretty sensible
