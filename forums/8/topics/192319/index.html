    Some mispronunciations and misused words:

    * Salmon. Should be pronounced 'sammon', but instead pronounced 'sal-mon'
    * Almond. Should be pronounced 'armond', but instead pronounced 'al-mond'
    * Fruits, as in 'we're going to get some fruits'. Should be 'we're going to get some fruit'
    * Using the word Canoe to mean Kayak and Kayak to mean Canoe. (check the dictionary)
