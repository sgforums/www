    1st 3 months if cannot go JC can go Millenia Institute. Anyway, after this year, 1st 3 months won't be implemented. Don't need to worry about that.

    The advantage for going to JC for the 1st 3 months is you get to study the subjects at JC. You won't have a hard time catching up than those students who did not attend 1st 3 months. Another advantage is to make your O Levels better. Let's say you manage to get to Temasek JC in the 1st 3 months, and you choose Temasek JC as your first choice during JAE, you will get 2 additional points to your L1R5. For example, you got 8 points. 2 bonus points means 6 points for L1R5.

    Prelims only determine if you can get into JC or MI for the 1st 3 months, it doesn't determine your life after O Levels.
