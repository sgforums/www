    1. Auto-throw grenade : Press and hold q to switch to grenade, and release to throw grenade. Once you throw the grenade, you will automatically switch back to your gun. You can replace 'q' with any other key you feel convenient. Becareful not to mix this key with other useful keys such as 'g', 'e' or 'f' which are already in use. You can also replace the word 'hegrenade' with 'flashbang' or 'smokegrenade' if you dont use HE grenades.

    bind q +quickthrow

    alias +quickthrow "weapon_hegrenade; wait; wait; +attack; say Grenade Away!"

    alias -quickthrow "wait; -attack"
