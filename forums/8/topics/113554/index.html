    Hi fellow 'water-smoker',
    i dun have the exact address but i can 'guide' you there.
    Using the Park Royal Hotel as reference, @ the road intersection of Beach road (Towards Kallang) and Arab St (Towards upper serangoo road), the Cafe (4got the name) is about 5 shophouses away on the left hand side. You can look up www.streetdirectory.com.sg and look up Park Royal Hotel. study the road names and u shld do alrite.

    Wld like to recommend u a shesha plave i freq often. Its @ KTM railway station. If you have not been there b4, the makan there is gd. halal fd to be exact. A shesha store has been setup there as well. Quality and service there is much better than the cafe @ arab st. The owner has actually worked @ the a famour 24hrs eatery serving shesha in JB for a couple of yrs. I had been smoking his shesha since.

    Happy water-smoking!
