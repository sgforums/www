    Like some forumers above said, u gotta have a schedule. Create an overall goal. Split things into smaller tasks.. so that each time u complete a task, u'll feel a sense of accomplishment.. like u're closer to your goal. And be mindful of what would happen if u were to fall short of that goal (repeat year? lose friends? face parents? self-loathing? etc, etc).. and then find out what best motivates u. Is it encouragement? Fear? Friends? Family?

    Personally, I found that fear is the best motivator for me. The fear of failing, growing too old to learn, being stuck in Singapore for the nxt 10 years or more (NOOOoo). That's what's propelling me forward now at light speed. hehehehe.
