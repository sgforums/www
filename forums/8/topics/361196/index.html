    How about playing it in a league format, such that each team will have to play 8 other teams, excluding the other team from the same country as themselves?

    The winners get 3 points, a draw will results in 1 point each, and a loss will results in zero points.

    After all the games are played, we will add the points of both teams together, along with their GD, GF, GA, amount of W/D/L matches.

    The winning country will be the one with the highest total points, and in case of a tie, the following factors will be compared in the given order until one country has a certain factor higher/lower than the other country :

    1) Goal Difference

    2) Goal For

    3) Goal Against

    4) Amount of matches won

    5) Amount of matches drawn

    6) Head-to-head record
