  • ceecookie
    9 Jun 07, 19:27
    for those keen on driving.I can recommend 3 in 1 style of travel,Phuket - Krabi - Koh Samui

    Rent a SUV(recommend true 4WD,not AWD) and drive.
    There is a circular expresway - roads loop that u can take from outside Phuket Island to Koh Samui.

    There're be car ferries to take u from the mainland to Koh Samui.
    Also,u want to do pure offroading,head to the inner parts of Koh Samui Island,there are some trails(from sand to bare offroad style) u can take*.

    Strictly not recommended for Honda CR-V,Toyota Fortuner and AWDs

    Hi.For my plan(actually was Budget Rent-a-Car's suggestion),u start at Phuket.

    SUV charge depend on which rental and which model.
    I suggest you rent from Budget as they include a touring map that is quite detailed and show some suggestion on travelling routes.
    http://www.budget.co.th/vehicles_home.php

    Which SUV depend on ur needs.If u just want to travel normally(on road - light offroad),then AWD is sufficient.However,if u want to offroad extensively then full 4WD is recommended.

    The only true 4WD that Budget has is a puny 1.3l Suzuki Carribean.This one is if u reallyyyy want offroad because u can at most hit 110km/h on it.Est cost is B1570.00.

    AWDs like Honda CR-V cost est B2890.00 per day.

    Its seem that from your budget its best that you just take a tour around Krabi.This "package" is best with budget of 2k or so.
    5 days is recommended.
    To see photos of this plan,its at http://www.sgforums.com/?action=thread_display&thread_id=193740
