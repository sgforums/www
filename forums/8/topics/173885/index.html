  • Hydro

    Some 345 people were killed on Thursday in a stampede at the procession symbolising the stoning of the devil during the annual pilgrimage, Saudi Health Minister Hamad bin Abdullah Al-Maneh told reporters.

    It was the latest in a succession of stampede tragedies to hit the haj pilgrimage despite efforts of the Saudi authorities to avoid a repeat of disasters like the one that killed 1,426 people in 1990.

    "Many of the bodies brought to hospital are from Southeast Asian countries," one source added.
