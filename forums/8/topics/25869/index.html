Guns and Roses are back

  • Axl
    5 Sep 02, 00:25
    Guns and Roses resurfaced in stunning fashion during the 2002 Video Music Awards with a brand new crew after almost a decade in recluse.

    VMA saved the best for last when they unveiled GnR, a band they have been trying to get on the show for the past 4 years, to close out the awards nite.

    Still at the helm, Axl Rose belted out crowd's favourite Welcome to the Jungle, Madagascar and Paradise City in a 5 minute medley, barely enough for the bewildered fans to lap up, albeit a noticeably weakened vocal.

    "I don't know if 'soon' is the word" quoted Axl, referring to the long overdue release of Chinese Democracy, the album they are working on.

    Anybody still care if it comes out? I know I will.
