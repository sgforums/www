    Don't Turn Back
    ***************
    (by LexZEUS)

    I watch you leaving me,
    With assuredly on your face,
    With slow foot steps,
    Hoping that I will call you back,
    Just to kiss you goodbye,
    Or give you my last words,
    Or tell you how to behave.

    Don't turn back, my son.
    Just walk ahead and be sure.
    Use your courage like fingers,
    To create your hope and dreams.
    Use your faith as a bridge,
    When there is no road leading your way.
    Use your heart to judge,
    For you will face many to weigh.
    But don't use your mind too much,
    Surrender everything to God, The Lord.

    I watch my mother behind me,
    With smile on her lips,
    And readiness to let me away,
    For me to seek my own way.

    I cannot turn back now,
    I have attitude to train,
    I have experience to build,
    I have soul to enlighten,
    I have curiosity to answer,
    And a long history to sew,
    And to present myself to the world,
    For her teaching is not meaningless,
    And my faith in God is not disappointing.

    For the woman I love most
    *************************
    (by LexZEUS)

    I walked over a long bridges,
    Narrow in width, full of danger.
    Then I jumped to a river,
    Fighting the stream like a fish.
    In the grassy field I would fly,
    In the deep jungle I would survive.

    Then I spent my night in the wilderness,
    And closed my eyes near to den of lions.
    Early morning I woke up,
    Not having enough dream last night.
    Carrying my worn suit and sandals,
    I continued my long journey.

    I arrived at a rocky plains,
    Where there was no trees nor animals.
    On the top of mountain I could see,
    The most beautiful rose ever been.
    To the mountain peak I will crawl,
    To the mountain cliff I will climb.

    Just to take the rose, and bring it home.
    As a present for the one I loved.
    I will share her my joy and happiness,
    Without telling her the journey I have taken.
    For the most beautiful rose,
    I found it worthy to carry on.
    For the woman I love most,
    I will give her more than promises.
