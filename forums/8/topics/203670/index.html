    Originally posted by browniebaobao:
    [color=indigo]Poly is not under the charge of MOE if im not wrong.
    Polys are under the jurisdiction of MOE, but not owned or employed by MOE (like all primary and all non-private secondary schools).

    This means that the payslips of all poly staff, lecturers and tutors are not from MOE (ie. the polys are a private owned institution), but MOE can issue instructions ("guidelines") to polys that they must obey, or investigate them if any incident threatens the reputation of Singapore education system, or else (if the polys don't obey MOE, they) run afoul of the law.

    Singapore govt has (potential) control of everything in Sg, which is a double-edged sword, with both pros and cons.
