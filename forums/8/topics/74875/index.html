    He has earned his credits over the past years in many ways. Winning
    the Her World and Citta Bella competition 2002 in Singapore and the
    Malaysian Open salsa dance competition in 2003 are a few to name.

    He holds quite a few nick names from moves that he has done on the
    floor which have impressed the crowd time and again.

    He has also represented Singapore in the world's largest Congress
    held in Los Angeles in the year 2003 with Attitude Dance Studio.

    Together with him he carries more than 2 years of performance packed
    technique oriented knowledge, which for the first time he shares in
    classes that will be conducted by him, from the beginners.

    He was taught from scratch by Mr Gupson Pierre for 2 dedicated years.
    Apart from this he idolises Eddie Torres, Frankie Martinez and
    Fransisco Vasquez who have also influenced his dancing in many ways
    through their workshops and classes.
