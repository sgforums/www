    content and design is based on the website concept.

    for instance a online news website. would prefer to potray a serious, no nonsense look and will be more text based. it will also have less distracting graphics.

    on the other hand, for an advertising company's website, they would like to have an attractive concept and eye catching themes to entice the would be customer. and to show what creativity the company is capable of churning out.

    for designers like us. it is a challenge to work with all this factors and find a common ground to satisfy the needs of the website.

    one point to note. there is no way to please everyone when it comes to design be it art or web designing. it just boils down to tastes and preferences. there are bound to be ppl who dislike certain designs, layouts and interactibility. this cant be helped i guess :p
