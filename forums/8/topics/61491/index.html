    Source : http://www.singapore-expo.com.sg/calendar/calendar_main.htm

    Body Worlds - The Anatomical Exhibition of Real Human Bodies
    8 November 2003 - 8 February 2004 : 10:00 AM - 09:00 PM
    Hall 1A
    Public-Tickets admission. Please refer to show profile for tickets details. Last admission-8pm

    The tour starts with a human skeleton, followed by the locomotive system, the digestive system, special nerve and vessel specimens, and also the development of new life in the womb. Visitors are provided with precise insights structure of their body's interior. Approximately 25 whole body plastinates reveal the true-to-life spatial relationships between organs. Diseased specimens due to infarcts or cancer are also on display; a lung blackened by nicotine illustrates the effects of tobacco consumption.

    Tickets
    Adults :S$18
    Secondary and junior college students : $12
    Primary students : $10
    Free admission for :
    1. 6 years old and below
    2. the diabled
    3. 60 years old and above
    (concession granted upon presentation of valid Identity Certification)
