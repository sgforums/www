    Eating -
    Everywhere!

    Hotel recomendation -
    Stay somewhere near Ximen mrt station. Food and shopping very accessible.

    Northern Coast:
    Take a Guoguang (02-2558 3060) Bus from the North Station in Taipei on the
    Coastal line to Luodong.The Bus stops on Various locations on the Coast..

    If you are already in Keelung, then take a Keelung Bus (02-2432 3721) on the
    Fulong Line and alight at your destination stop on the Coast. (One departure
    per hour)

    Jinguashih: From the front of the Keelung Railway Station take a Keelung Bus
    to Jinguashih, On the way, the Bus stops at Jioufen and Rueifang.

    Jioufen:
    From the Exit #1 at MRT Stop: Zhongxiao-Fuxing, You can take a Keelung Bus to
    Jiofen and Jingashih.
