The Justification of Vice

  • Rexdriver
    19 Apr 05, 20:05
    Our PM has given his reasons for giving the green light to casinos. Very diplomatically, he has stated that he "hopes to bring all Singaporeans together, so that even though we may not all agree on this issue, we understand and respect each other's reasons and concerns, and can close ranks and move ahead."

    It would appear that the primary reason for the approval of the casinos is that it would boost the tourism industry in Singapore. Mind you, it is not that our tourism industry is presently ailing. The numbers are in fact up. It is just that our share of the Asian tourism pie has gone down. As an example, whereas tourists used to stay in Singapore for 4 days in the past, they now stay for 3 days. This compared to 4 days in HK, 5 days in London and a week in NY.

    Ok, so it would seem that casinos have a direct correlation with tourism in Singapore... No, now wait a minute, we cannot be sure of that, can we? Singapore never had a casino, so we don't quite know what impact a casino will have on tourism in Singapore.

    Or maybe casinos have a direct correlation with tourism in ANY COUNTRY. That would be a good basis to think that a casino would boost tourism in Singapore, now wouldn't it? Unfortunately, we do not have any feedback on whether the tourists stay those extra days in HK, London and NY because of a casino...

    So it seems that we're really just guessing and hoping that a casino would have a positive effect on tourism.

    Ok, let us move on to the next issue...
