Lessons learn from Bruce Lee life

    1. Bruce Lee was pretty much an ah beng. He fought in school and on the streets and was defeated several times. He than went to learn martial arts so that he could defeat his opponents and that is how he became a martial art expert.

     

    Lesson learn: There is nothing wrong in being an ah beng. Bruce Lee was a successful ah beng. Just that if you want to be an ah beng, you need to become undefeatable and 1 way to do that is to learn martial arts and than defeat all your opponents.

     

    2. Bruce Lee only became successful after he left his homeland China to go to America. If he had continued to stay in china, he will never become famous.

     

    Lesson learn: It is good to migrate. You need to leave your comfort zone in order to be successful.

     

    3. Bruce Lee challanged and defeated alot of top martial arts experts. That is how he became famous and got invited to film in martial art movies.

     

    Lesson learnt: Whatever you do in life, you need to challenge other experts in your industry and defeat them in order for you to become famous. Only when you become famous than will more opportunities open up for you.

     

    4. Bruce Lee became a national hero because when he defeat his opponents and in his movie, he always like to say "I am a chinese. Chinese people are not Dong Ya Bing Fu"

     

    Lesson learnt: In whatever you do, you should try to include your country in than you will be regarded as a national hero. For example, if you are a top fashion designer and you win in a international competition, say "I am a singaporean. Singapore is not just a tiny red dot". Than you will be regarded as a national hero.
