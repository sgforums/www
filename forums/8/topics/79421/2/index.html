    the story of the helen, the war, and Achilles vs. Hector were based on the Illiad by Homer.

    the death of Achilles, the Trojan Horse and the end of the Trojan War were based on the Aeneid by Virgil.

    its much more fun to watch the movie if u have at least some ideas of Greek mythology... like the Judgement of Paris (which teaches all men that when women argue, its best to stay out) and the Greek Pantheon and the roles which the Greek gods played in the war...

    it kinda explains why Paris, who was a weak, snivelling playboy who stole someone else's wife, could suddenly aim so well and strike Achilles' heels with an arrow.

    the story goes that Achilles was destined to die soon after he killed Hector and that fateful arrow was guided by Apollo as the Gods had all taken sides in the war by that time...

    in fact, Achilles' mother knew this and tried to prevent him from joining the war by hiding him away and dressing him up as a girl can anyone picture Brad Pitt in drag?
