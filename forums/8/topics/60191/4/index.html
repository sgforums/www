    she dunno how to make..

    there is an art in cutting, marinating and tenderising the beef...

    it has to be cut across the grain of the meat.. marinated with stuff you like.. but the important element must be juice from fresh ginger.. which tenderises the beef...

    you don't even have to marinate the beef as it is yummy, but if the beef seems to be on the tough side, a bit of ginger juice helps

    then before cooking the thing, you take a tenderising mallet and bash the heck out of it or you can use a small beer bottle to do it..

    when cooking steak, one of the best ways is an obscenely hot pan, or barbeque with a raging fire.. beer helps with the flavour and texture (serious!)

    you can do that if you are cooking it in a frying pan.. just pour about 1/2 to 3/4 cup of beer and let it reduce...

    should have tender steak.. after these steps..

    oh, the cut (as in what part of the cow) is important too.. some parts are to be used only for stews.. some parts for steaks.. make sure correct part
