    Hello Kitty:

    On a more serious note, I think we all know that age is but a number. In relationships, there is no right or wrong, should or should not. We would love to just follow our hearts, but we must also realise that there may be factors that may strain the relationship due to the age gap. Ask yourself, how do you feel when you are with the guy? Do you feel that you are going out with your little brother, and if so, do you mind that? Would you mind that you may have to wait a couple of years for him to grow up? Would you mind that people may make remarks that you may not like? Sit down and think of problems that may cause a strain on the relationship before you commit yourself. At least this way, you can be prepared to face the problems should any arise. I'm definitely not against such kind of relationships, if both parties can handle the situation well, I'd say Why Not?
