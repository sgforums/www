    My friend Ah Huat sells fried oyster at Bukit Batok Central. Ah huat once had a helper whom he had employed for years. This young man is Ah Lim. Ah Lim started working for Ah Huat since he quit school at 14. He had since learnt the ropes, especially Ah Huat's unbeatable skills of oyster frying. Later, instead of 1, Ah Huat had 2 woks frying at the same time to be able to serve out plates of fried oyster faster. Usually, Ah Huat at one wok, and Ah Lim at the other.

    Then Ah Huat's business deteriorate. Partly because the oyster farm closed down and Ah Huat had to buy frozen oysters that didn't taste as good. But most importantly, Ah Huat was beginning to drink too much. The store was hardly making money. Ah Lim, now married had not been receiving his salary for months. He tried to revive the business, but was scoffed by Ah Huat for being a busy body. Ah Huat told Ah Lim that if he wish to teach him how to run his business, then he should become a boss instead. Knowing that Ah Huat could hardly keep his business anymore, Ah Lim left to work for Mr Lee, the owner of another coffee shop elsewhere.

    Then earlier this year. Ah Huat employed Zhang Xiao Dong, or better known as the Cheena. Xiao Dong was came from Shangdong and came to Singapore to on work permit. Xiao Dong fries the best oyster. Now Ah Huat hardly needs to work at all. Xiao dong is cheap to hire and he fries 2 woks with both hands. Ah Huat skived even harder now.

    Earlier during a beer session, Xiao Dong reveals that his greatest wish is to open a store in Chinatown in the US. But like he said, Rome wasn't built in a day. He has to start somewhere... and thus he is working here now.

    Then last week, Ah Huat met Ah Lim, who is now a proud owner of his own fried oyster store. Fuming, he stormed towards Ah Lim's store, kicked and toppled his stove. "You quitter!" he said. You "fair weather hawker" he said. "I am doing just as well without you."

    Back at Ah Huat's wok, Xiao Dong is studiously frying oyster with both hands... and dreaming the dream that he will one day leave this stinking oily old place to fry oyster in the US.
