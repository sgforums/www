    A 14 yr old is NOT an adult yet (much as they may wish to consider themselves that). She is a teenager, learning how to make the right decisions so that she can enter adulthood prepared. She does not have the backing of sufficient experience behind her to make the informed choices. That does not mean she is stupid at all - but generally the decisions a 14 yr old makes will not be the same decisions she makes when she is 21. I am not saying girls who are 21 make better choices - but the same girl at 14 and the same girl at 21 would obviously have more life experience to base their decision on.

    Most guys at 21 are either preparing to get into university to form a base for a career OR starting on their career path. They are looking forward to a newfound 'man of the world' situation where they learn to be more financially independent, assertive and decisive. I think while men can appreciate beauty and 'cuteness' or personality in girls/women of ANY age, most men in their 20s are looking forward to having a more mature partner who are closer or understand the current life phase they are in.

    I reckon a 21 yr old guy who seeks out 14-15 yr old girls to be their soulmates (which I assume what a relationship is about, unless it is purely sexual) are either immature or still thinking like a 15-18 yr old, and not really prepared to move on to their next phase in life.
