    You can choose to repay the minimum monthly, which is usually either $100 or $200. I did.

    Also, this is one loan which does not have early repayment penalty. So, when you feel you have enough saving for other matters, pay it all off in one shot. This is exactly what I did. Had saving of 10K and debt of 15K back then when I quit my first job. If I had been paying $500/mth or $1000/mth, I wouldn't have the courage to quit my first job without a second job in sight. My friend choose to pay $1000/mth and could not quit even when he is extremely frustrated with his boss. In the end, he also did not clear his debt much faster than me as I manage to make a lump sum payment when I feel I had enough buffer for emergencies.

    And don't worry about the interest. This is the loan with one of the lowest interest after housing. Anyway, in a few years time, you will probably get use to having debts. Few are able to buy a flat without taking a loan.
