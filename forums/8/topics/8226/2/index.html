    To all:

    This is my childhood, a time which forms part of my life.

    I can't recall much beyond 4 yrs old. But I do remeber that I lived in the Chin Swee area then, I do recall that I am an energetic little devil who would wake up at 6 a.m. to go pester my mum for 20 cents so that I can go next door to buy soya bean milk for breakfast from dao hua pek. I can recall the daily morning rountine of following my mum to the market. I can recall spending the rest of the day in the house of my god-mother, often sneaking out to play along the corridor with the neighbour's kids causing her great worry.(Sorry mum)

    Toys, yes, I have toys. They were my everything then. I have the piece of rope which I use for my zero point, that I took 2 days to piece up with rubber band. I have my marbles, I have my bag of toy soldiers given to me by the gor gor who grew too old to play with them, I remeber I spent hours with those comarades of mine. "Ta Ta Ta Ta Boom" I would go on and on. I remembered my gloom when I was grounded. All I could do was to speak to my friends who were playing outside through the little slit under the door. It was still fun though, we were planning what to do tommorow. I remembered playing wrestling with brother chui, I would go over to his house, and we playing wrestling on his bed, if I got too tired, I would just sleep there, if he got tired he would sleep too, and when he is asleep I would explore his room, My exploration are normally quite fruitful, I found a smoking pipe which I posed later with in a photograph, a pair of sunglasses, a big T-shirt which goes to my ankles with the smiley face in front. Brother chui move out after he got married, did not hear from him much oredi.

    I remebered my excitment when I first discovered plastersine. I made quite a few piece of art out of them, my first piece of dog poo, my first snowman lookalike, my first Tv, which is just a square block of plastersine. *laffs* Yes, the TV, it cause great excitement in the block I was living in. My family bought this old TV from a friend which comes with it's own box and shutters, there were dials on it which goes "Tak Tak Tak" each time you turn it. I often got a caning for my constant channel surfing with the dials. My neighbours will always come to the house to watch TV. It is a magical moment, everyone's eyes will be transfixed to it, no sound, no noise, just wide eyes on the TV. I thin kthe neighbourhood noise pollution at night went down after the TV appeared.

    I remembered my ah mah, I missed her, it is a pity I did not learnt how to endear her early enough. but I remember her coming out to the corridor asking me to come back for dinner, I remember it well, for I caused her to fell once, she was weak, and all this walking in and out is not doing her good. All I could do then was to cry out loud on seeing her fall, I remebered trying to help her stand but I was too weak and she was too weak. So I just sat down by her side, while my firends went to call out the nieghbours to help her into the house. I am sorry grandma, I miss you.

    Then I remeber school, my first day at the kindergarden is a funny sight I remembered. Everyone was crying. Even the teacher was at a lost.     I remembered the old trees with roots in the air whenever I walked with my mum to the bus stop, I remembered the squariels on them. I remeber the old bus tickets I used to keep endearingly each time I had a bus journey. I always look with interest when the bus conductor punch a hole in the puch ticket.
