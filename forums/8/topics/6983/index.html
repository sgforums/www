    For the benefit of the doubt, paintball is a sport/game (hard to say whether it is a sport).

    Basically you are divided into 2 teams opposing each other. YOu are armed with a pneumatic gun which shoots balls filled with paint.

    Various games can be played. Most common is capture the opponent's flag and bring it to your own base to win.

    Something like a war game because you need strategy and tactics to win. to make things more fun, you gun isn't 100% accurate.

    Best part, is that you try your best to avoid being hit as it is painful to be hit. Depending on the distance, it almost always give you bruises. But masks are provided to protect your face.

    FYI: The paint is waterbased and easily washed off. My balcony is full of paint splatters and i dun bother to clean it. Waiting for the rain. eheh
