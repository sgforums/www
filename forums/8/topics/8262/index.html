    Okie Ticonderoga, here's the list of things to bring, but it might not be completed as i believe some of the things will slip my mind, i will just try my best to list......

    1) Shampoo, soap, shaver, tooth paste, tooth brush etc. (Those issue in army for display only, try not to use them)

    2) Many extra underwears + 1 or 2 piece of sleeping clothings (optional, as u may like to wear the army tee to sleep)

    3) Talcum powder...... (The best is the snake brand one)

    4) Handphone, wif extra battery (cos i believe u will miss yr home so much that u might want to call back).

    5) Slipper (for bathing purposes etc, but bring those ultra-light weight ones)

    6) Some other stuffs like torchlight, watch, alarm clock etc.....

    7) Washing powder (Yes this one important)

    Extra hangers .....

    9) Some medications like panadol, or 'Bao Ji Wan'

    10) If wear spec, best if bring extra pair, plus spec's hook or something.

    Think all these will be sufficent for the 1st 3 weeks confinement....... will add on if i remember others. All the best~!
