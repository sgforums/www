    previous year and to begin afresh.

    However, Valentine's day was actually created to remember the sacrifice of st valetine when he used to conduct secret marriages. It has since become a day to
    celebrate love. Hallmark has certainly
    made a tonne of cash out of it.

    The point I'm trying to make is that Valentine's day celebrates Love and everything good that Love represents, in that sense, every time you're with that
    someone special in your life... even
    if you ARE arguing with him / her or if you're just slacking about staring at
    each other... it can be a Valentine's day.

    Just a little footnote here, granted I don't contribute to the idea that Feb 14 is a day that you MUST buy flowers and you MUST have romantic candlelit dinners.

    It's all the festivities... join the
    crowd, soak in the atmosphere... wear silly
    heart printed hers and his t-shirts, that you will never in your right mind wear the rest of the 364 days of the year. Just go out and do it, instead of saying that it's all commercialised and ppl are just wasting money, because only on Valentine's day .. will people look at you and give you an encouraging smile or appreciative chuckle, when on any other day, they might just think you're some mad lunatic. It's Valentine's day! Quit gawking.. so go out and have fun!
