    Title : President's Star Charity raises S$3.8m for 47 charities



    Date : 31 July 2005 2233 hrs (SST)
    URL : http://www.channelnewsasia.com/stories/singaporelocalnews/view/160838/1/.html

    The President's Star Charity show has so far raised S$3.8 million, outperforming last year's figures.

    It was a closely-watched show as it is the biggest fund-raiser since the NKF-saga a few weeks ago.

    The President's Star Charity is backed by President SR Nathan himself and it caps the month-long President's Challenge charity drive.

    Last year, the President's Star Charity show raised nearly S$2.9 million to help 45 charities, which were selected by the President.

    That helped to bring the total President's Challenge coffers to more than S$8 million.

    By all accounts, this year is likely to match that amount.

    The President has said he is counting on the generous nature of Singaporeans to support the charity TV show and help the needy.

    And MediaCorp artistes left nothing to chance.

    They put up a spectacular song and dance - all in the hope of raising money, and providing help for 47 beneficiaries. - CNA /ch
