Actually frankly speaking I still miss my ship Eagle Centaurus quiet a lot, maybe it was my second time onboard, maybe it is because I am having the luxury of TV and Fridge. I never like to bit farewell, because it will only make the heart ache even more. Since it is already a certainty which you could not change, why not leave quietly and avoid more hurt to other people? The other reason might be because I am still able to access email and SMS onboard ship this time. I did spend the last few hours of my time in the Engine Room, so much work we have done on it, I will really hate it if other people mess it up after that.

No TV, no Phone and so small and miserable, the hotel in Hong Kong seem spacious compare to this. Pradeep and me were surprise that there is Internet access, but we regret greatly after that. It was very slow; can you imagine Pentium PC running Windows XP? Only manage to access one email after one long hour, and it is from my Guangzhou GF saying how good I am and I will manage to find someone better than her in future. Even my favorite Hardware zone website is block by Net Granny. I am very disappointed and all Raphael can think of is finding some food at this god forsaken early morning.

My China friend Dong told me that he is flying over to Singapore to join ship, but I never did receive any call from him until now. I manage to came across a beautiful flowerbed and took a shot of it, a very cute pussycat stare at me as if he knew I was a foreigner from a faraway land.
