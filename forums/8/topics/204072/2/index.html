    The brand name of fossil,which is fossil, is specially named to perfection.

    So if you ask a watch idiot whether he prefers a seiko or fossil. The choice is fossil.

    Brand name is very important, like many ad experts say.
    Now if you asked a WIS (Watch Idiot Savant), he'd say Seiko.

    Fossil essentially makes crap. Their movements are seriously aftermarket pieces slapped in fancy-looking cases. On the other hand, Seiko knows watches thoroughly, and they make everything on their own, right down to the oils used in their mechanical pieces.

    Bottom line - if you want a fashion piece but don't care if it's cruddy, go Fossil. If you want a real watch, go Seiko.
