    erm some 6 days job's pay lesser than 5 days job. And some 5 days job carry longer daily working hours compared to 6 days' job. For example, a 6 days job working hours is like 7 hours 20 mins excluding one hour's break time per day (6days a week thus making it 44 hours per week), whereas a 5 days job can be from 9am to 6:30pm and beyond (more than 8 hours 30 mins if excluding the one hour's lunch time).

    So if the two types of working days' total weekly hours were to be calculated and compared, it's actually around the same, that's what we termed as la chang bu duan in chinese
