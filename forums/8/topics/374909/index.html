    Try this tool called:

    VirtualDub Mod.

    A lot of editing methods in there.....

    You can play around, it's very simple, straightforward, linear, small sized, and will give you a good basis of editing skills.

    It's also very flexible.....

    It's open source.... and has a lot of add-on tools also...

    I was once a fanatic and I ran a lot of editings......my favourite was removing logos (like the TV logo on top corner), compressing with H.264, joining and splitting, audio editing, switching formats, etc.
