    If it makes you feel any better, the fallacy is with equating 1 month with 4 wks. Your employment contract says your salary is $xxx per month, not $xxx per 4 weeks. Small difference, but that extra 2-3 days over 4 wks is about 1 month over 48 wks, so according to the contract you signed up for you work that extra 2-3 days every month for that salary, or the extra 1 month over the course of year for 12 x monthly salary.
