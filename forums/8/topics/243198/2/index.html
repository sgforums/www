    I think you people probably enjoy a western style wedding more, like I do. Probably because for western wedding, you are only invited if you're a family or close friend to the bride and/or groom.     As for food, usually only 3 courses (Entree, main and dessert) and you need not to share with anyone. Of course you don't get the nice Asian cuisine like shark fins soup etc but it's more hygenic.

    There is also dance floor at most western wedding receptions so that the bride & groom as well as all the guests can dance the night away. I think it's more personal and nicer attending wedding where you can mingle with the bridal party.
