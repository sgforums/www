    Well here's my take on the Matrix Reloaded:

    The earlier versions of the Matrix failed due to imperfections in the system. As the Architect (the old man in the tv room) told Neo, due to 1% rejecting the Matrix, previous versions of the Matrix broke down, but the Oracle came up with a new way of managing this:
    From time to time, the imbalances result in one person chosen as The One to reset the Matrix and rebuild Zion. Maybe the machines themselves could not reset the Matrix, so needed to get a human to do that, hence The One.

    Thus the Sentinels invade Zion to force The One to make the choice that will allow humans to survive as a race, in a new Zion. This is the carrot that is dangled to ensure he make the choice which the machines want.

    Not sure how many earlier versions of the Matrix there were, but there were already 5 "The One" who had chosen the door on the right, which will allow them to choose the people to rebuild Zion with. Neo is different from them because of his love for Trinity which caused him to choose the other door, different from the previous 5 .

    The Oracle and the Keymaker are programmes written for the purpose of getting The One to restart the Matrix so that both the machines and humans continue to survive. The French guy is a rogue program, just as Agent Smith is now a rogue program, with a mind of his own, no longer following the rules that and the purpose they were written for.

    For most of the people, there is no choice, which is why the Oracle could predict what will happen. Only a few with the power have the choice: The French guy, Agent Smith, Neo. The rest just serve their purpose, human and program alike.

    The lines between the real world and the Matrix are blurring, so Agent Smith could copy himself into the real world to betray the humans, while Neo can stop the Sentinels.

    The "mistakes" in the plot are hooks to allow Part 3 to explain everything.

    The Matrix Revolutions will probably explain everything and bring a final end to the Matrix.
