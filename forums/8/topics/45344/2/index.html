    The 2 guys in white is The Twins...

    As for the maker of the matrix...
    What he is trying to say that history had repeated itself for the 6ixth time... The Matrix is a system has been repeating itself... The machines need energy to live on... but they cannot have tooo many humans... or they might juz be wiped out... thus... they created a story... a lie... "The One" to end the war... But in actual fact... in the matrix... "The One" job is to select few person to restart the whole real world and matrix... so that the energy source will not be wiped out nor will it grow too powerful.

    The old lady...
    she is the oracle... another program in the "mainframe". thus having living in the matrix 6 time... and being a programme... have the ablity to tell and read the future. Both the Keymaker and Serepah ( the white guy protecting the Oracle) are also programs.

    There might be some confusion about Agent Smith.
    Smith is no longer a agent... He was "freed" when Neo killed him... In terms of programming... he was deleted... however, in the storyline, programs like Smith (agents) can hide in the "mainframe" to prevent getting deleted. Smith somehow did that... AND also found a way to overwrite and copy programs. In another words, copy himself. Thus, he returned to the matrix and copied himself, trying to find back Neo for revenge and overtaking the whole "Matrix" programme.

    About the storyline, its basically very simple... Few points.
    - Neo is the The One
    - Smith is no longer a agent, able to copy, overwrite.
    - Smith able to take over a human mind.
    - Senitals are forcing into Zion, human race at risk.
    - Neo need to get to mainframe to stop them. To kill(hang) Matrix.
    - Keymaker is the only one that can show Neo the door.
    - Programs "defy" mainframes, like virus.
    - Neo is able to "foresee"
    - Neo meets the "creator" of matrix.
    - Neo is the 6th version of himself. History is repeating.
    - Love is changing this Neo. He made the choice to save Trinity and a chance that human can survive.
    - Neo saved Trinity, changed the history.
    - Biological change in Neo.
    - Neo able to control machines as though in the Matrix in the Real World.
