    Cost of A Lot
    Down Town Car Park Charges

    If you're having dinner at Carlton Hotel at Bras Basah Road, it's
    advisable to park your car in the neighbouring NTUC Income Centre or nearby Raffles City. That's because it'll cost you $6 to enter the hotel car park after 6pm, while you need to pay only $2 if you park in the other two buildings after 5pm. While hotel patrons can enjoy a $2 redemption, parking there will still cost $4. The only down side to parking at NTUC Income Centre is, you must vacate the car park by 10pm.

    If you're doing a quick pick and go, the cheapest car parks along
    Orchard Road are at Lucky Plaza, Ngee Ann City, Centrepoint, StarHub Centre, Le Merdien Singapour hotel and Plaza Singapura. Further south, Suntec City and Conrad Centenniel International hotel are also recommended. All charge $1 for the first hour. You can even get your Suntec City car park charge redeemed at Carrefour supermarket if you make a minimum purchase of $50 there.

    If you're spending longer than an hour, then you should not spare the walk and park at Shaw Centre, Le Meridien Singapour hotel and Plaza Singapura.

    Motorists should also be aware that Orchard Point charges $2 per hour after 11pm daily, including public holidays, and at Wisma Atria Shopping Centre, it's $15 per entry between 11pm and 7.30am.
