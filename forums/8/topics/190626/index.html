    To buy or not to buy?

    IS THIS is the right time to buy property in Singapore?

    Looking to buy asks if the upward momentum will sustain, especially with 6 per cent growth figures and the private property market warming up.

    Now is feels that this is 'the last chance to jump in before the prices begin to really shoot through the roof'. but of course adds: 'The world's most powerful are commenting everything is fine and dandy. GDP is at the highest, prospects excellent. What's the problem?'

    But MadMac disagrees: 'Look at the immense property oversupply all over Singapore and the planned and constructed projects. Why should prices go up even a single cent? Ridiculous... Don't fall into this trap!'

    www.expatsingapore.com

    Mother's Day ideas

    NETIZENS are sharing ideas on how to celebrate Mother's Day.
