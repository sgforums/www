    Traffic Police nab 298 motorists in Christmas operation

    SINGAPORE : A total of 298 motorists were caught for various traffic offences in a six-hour Christmas Day blitz on Saturday evening.
    Among them, 215 motorists were caught for speeding, 21 for drink driving, three for driving without a valid driving licence, and 35 for making illegal modifications to their vehicles.

    The operation also involved enforcement officers from the Land Transport Authority.

    It was conducted along Ang Mo Kio Avenue 1, Gambas Avenue, Clemenceau Avenue, Kallang Road, Stevens Road, Boon Lay Way, Hougang Avenue 3, Upper Changi Road East and Upper Bukit Timah Road.

    Traffic Police said there was a need to raise road safety awareness, especially during the festive season.

    It said fatal accidents due to speed-related factors fell by seven cases to 44 cases for the first nine months of this year, compared to the same period last year.

    It urged motorists to practice safe driving, and use alternative means of transport if they are tired after their merry-making. - CNA
