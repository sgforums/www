    So Msc or MEng depends on what you actually intend to do after completing your masters.

    What will be the various different career paths if I choose Msc or MEng?
    Msc is highly generic in nature, meaning its suitable for a variety of careers. Say, in the manufacturing industry, heavy industries like oil & gas, shipping, semicon, pharm etc etc.

    MEng is more specific, meaning its suitable for research oriented careers. Say, at AStar, NUS, NTU, biopolis, DSTA etc etc. A few companies do have R&D branches in Singapore like Seagate technology, semens , IBM ect etc. But you will find more R&D jobs in the government sector.

    So if you want research to be your career go for MEng, if not MSc.

    Btw, the curriculum for MEng and Msc is very different form one another. So better do your "homework" before deciding on one.
