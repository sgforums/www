    Fireworks on National Day will be from around 8pm.

    To view, can proceed to:
    PA Kallang
    Flats opposite Golden Mile C'plex
    Flats along Old Airport Rd
    Tanjong Rhu (Geylang Park Connector)
    Marina Promenade Park between Kallang Sea Sports Club and Benjamin Sheares Bridge.

    Take note that Merdeka Bridge is closed to public due to Nicoll Highway cave-in incident.

    If you are driving, the traffic police will be restricting traffic access into Tanjong Rhu Rd.

    To faciliate fireworks display, Mountbatten Rd between Guillemard Rd/Nicoll Highway and Old Airport Rd/Stadium Blvd will be closed from 7.45pm to 8.30pm for safety reason.
