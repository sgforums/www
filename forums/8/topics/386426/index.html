    Yes can DIY lah.

    No need special iron.  But at least the sheet of printing material called 'Iron On Transfers'.

    Silkscreen printing is the more commericalised version of the Iron On Transfer.

    You should be able to buy those Iron On Transfer sheets from Challenger or even Best Denki.

    And try to print your face picture in black/white charcoal style or sephia print to save your printer ink.

    Take note.  That pillowcase might lose its printing after a few washes.....
