    -liu bei, guan yu and zhang fei are not sworn brothers.
    -diao chan or cicada, one of the 4 tua chio bu, is a fictional character.
    -lu bu and dong zhuo fell out because of dong zhou's bad temper.
    -wang yun incite lu bu to revolt, but no diao chan is involved
    -lu bu is not a heartless all brawn and no brain grunt as in san guo yan yi. he is talented in other fields as well.
