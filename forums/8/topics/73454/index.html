    Last of three fugitive robbery suspects nabbed on Tekong

    SINGAPORE: Singapore authorities have arrested the last of three fugitive robbery suspects who had fled by boat to Pulau Tekong after committing two robberies in neighbouring Johor, Malaysia.
    The third suspect was nabbed some 56 hours after a manhunt for the trio first started on Thursday morning.

    He was found at about 4:45pm Saturday on the southeastern part of the island, near the beach where the trio's abandoned boat was found.

    No weapons were found on him and he is under police interrogation.

    The second suspect was arrested at about 10.30am earlier in the day while the first one was nabbed on Friday night. - CNA
