    Coming from a neutral point of view

    I'm comparing 2 magic shops that I frequently patronise.

    Magic Boutique and Magic Castle.

    Magic Boutique used to be my number one choice for getting high end products simply because she's cheaper. But the downside is that I always had to make my order at the end of the month, which is pretty hectic because if I were to miss the deadline, I would have to wait for another month to make my order.

    Magic Castle has fantastic before and after sales service. Jay(the owner) is also a superb guide having been in the business for more than 10 years. Whenever you order something, it'll will arrive within a week. However, some of the products are slightly more costly compared to Magic Boutique. That I can understand because Magic Castle is still a shop, so rent and bills are inevitable.

    I wasn't aware of Magic Castle's new website, only happen to learn about it after reading these few current post.

    I did my homework and was surprised to learn that every product I compared between the 2 shops, Magic Castle's prices for their members are lower than Magic Boutique. No doubt non-member prices are still a little higher than Magic Boutique's, I can tell that they've been lowered to a reasonable rate compared to the past. 

    And the 2nd ultimate surprised was that Magic Castle still maintains its fast 7 days delivery service.

    In my honest opinion, if Magic Castle keeps its prices the way they are now and maintains the quick delivery service, I will definitely consider joining the membership.
