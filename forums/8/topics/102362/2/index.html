    Profile

    Li Lin gained popularity with the lead role of Elaine Tay, a dedicated female inspector, in the hugely popular local English police series, Triple Nine.

    Known as an intellectual babe, this Pilates teacher was trained as a ballerina with the prestigious Royal Ballet School. She graduated from the University of Surrey, UK with a Masters in Dance Anthropology.

    Applying a fastidious, perfection-seeking zeal to all facets of her career, Li Lin has never been one to sit idle. Following the meteoric success of Triple Nine, Li Lin tried her hands at a Mandarin drama serial, Rising Expectations with stunning results. Her hard work was showered by numerous accolades received in Star Awards '97. The show garnered a nomination for the Best Drama Serial and she was voted as one of the Top 10 Most Popular Female Artistes.

    Playing the role of a fallen angel in Love Me Love Not, Li Lin won the hearts of audiences and critics alike with her sharp comedic timing, and in the blockbuster The Reunion, her role as the leading lady played a huge part to the astounding success of the drama. Needless to say, she is the darling of Singapore television, and one who will travel thus far, carrying with her, vast amount of experience and talents.

    To sum it all, Liping is an endearing actress who has won a place in every household in Singapore.
