Then tries to take your name, oddly enough shouldn't they have your particulars if they have the lucky draw form? I let her go on, then said I didn't enter in any lucky draw. She hung up without even saying bye. Haha.

    Second incident at 1:15PM today: "Good afternoon I'm Shirley calling from LaShell (spelling?)."

    Me: Okay? What can I do?
    Shirley: Remember when you entered in the lucky draws at supermarkets, restaurants and petrol stations?
    Me: Hmm, was it the one at West Mall?
    Shirley: Yes that's the one.
    Me: My lucky draw at West Mall?
    Shirley: Yes that's correct sir.
    Me: Well I've never been to West Mall in my life so I don't know how come there's a lucky draw under my name for it.
    Shirley: Okay bye.

    LOL

    Maybe it's legitimate, maybe it's not. You make the conclusion. I'd rather not risk having my personal particulars being used by a shell company or what not.
