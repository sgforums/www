  • Pangapda

    Kids of ages 5 and 6 in Kindergarten do not enjoy any privileges that Primary School going kids enjoy. Their monthly school fees are exorbitantly expensive (S$125)compared to a Primary School Education (S$5.50). They are charged Adult fare at cinemas and as well as when they visit Dentists. If they do not hold a usable Child's Ezlink card and taller than 1.2m, they will also be charged Adult fare.  Many rides in Themes parks are catered for those 1.2 meter and above and again, kindergarten kids are out. They are the forgotten groups that our society prefer to cast them out of the society system as if they are not useful or productive to society at all.
