    NaiveGirl:
    To give u a rough idea of SG Education system.
    Kids start schooling in kindergarten, which is about 2 or 3 yrs
    Afterwhich, they move on to Primary School whereby they spend about 6 yrs. At the end of 6 yrs, they will take the PSLE which will determine the stream that they will take -- Express or Normal.
    Express - 4 yrs; Normal - 5 yrs
    Next they move on to Secondary level, (by then they r around 13 yrs old). In the secondary level they will undergo 4 yrs (or 5) and take their GCE 'O' Levels at Secondary 4 (or 5). This is a very basic & important qualification in which I feel is very crucial in SG. Without the O's cert, it is very difficult to survive in SG (Please dun flame me, coz this is only how I feel)
    After which students can decide whether they want to start working or to continue studying.
    Now they will have to make the choice of where/wat to study if they chose the later path. There r several choices:
    1) Junior College (A Levels)
    2) Polytechnic (Diploma)
    3) Private Institutions
    4) Overseas Studies
    Usually for 1) they would complete in 2 yrs then move on to University for Degree and for 2) they will complete in 3 yrs and start working after that. Again this is depends on individual.
    Whew, hopefully this gives you a better idea of the SG education system.
