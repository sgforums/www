    There are no MUST-VISIT places per se, depends on what you wanna see.

    If you're into gothic lolita stuff, you could consider going to one of their er... 'black death' (rough translation) concerts. I went for one in uh, shinjuku i think (can't remember); quite a few guys dress up like metallica with face paint and all, then the girls are those classic lolita gals. The crowd's appearance can get quite scary, but ultimately they all seem harmless enough.

    Must go places so that you can tell your mom/dad you were really in tokyo

    Shinjuku + harajuku + shibuya => Shinjuku 2nd district (nii-chome, or kabuki-cho) is where you'll find all the love hotels. If you're looking for sleaze it's uh... in kabuki cho as well, but in a different part. It's your archtype of jap porn.

    tokyo imperial palace => just go wander around the entrance, and hope to get a glimpse of princess masako. Oh ya, you'll also see familiar stuff like OCBC around this area as well...

    odaiba => the island is so small, you can walk it in 4 hours. Can consider Fuji TV HQ, Toyota Building, + expo (but you probably can't enter)

    ueno park=> can consider going to todai for a visit; setting for love hina. nothing much here but plenty of parks and museum. nearby ueno park are plenty of those pacinko parlours.

    roppongi hills => where all the stars stay, but really an overhyped place

    Ginza => as its name suggests, where all the glitz is. you probably won't end up buying anything here. SONY hq is found here.

    there places pretty much define metropolitan tokyo.

    EDIT: Oh yes, the main building for japanese fashion is found in shibuya 101 building. It's pretty much almost an exclusively female shopping centre; if you wanna get those kick-ass boots + shorts + pink furry top + beret + long gloves japs always wear this is where you wanna be.
