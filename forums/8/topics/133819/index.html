    To solve:

    Crimson room = http://www.b-sting.nl/crimsonroom/solve.html

    Viridian Room = http://www.thonky.com/viridian/

    Chamber Room =

    First, under the chair seat there is paper.
    2nd, at the drawer, open the drawer there is a pen.
    3rd, at the tunnel there is a tube
    4th, use the pen on the paper
    5th, with the note, put into the tube and put in tunnel and press the red button.
    6th Then after that u will hear a sound that it drops back. Open the tube and u see a note
    7th, the note says "HELP*ME". Translate to numbers (i.e. H = 4. Blank = *) And use those number on the phone and u will hear the phone ring and then say okay.
    8th, put the tube with the note back into the tunnel. Go to the photoframe and shake it until it drops. (Hint: click the right, then left, the middle of the bottom). Take the money behind the frame
    9th, put the money into the tube and put into the tunnel
    10th, u will get a key in return to get out.
