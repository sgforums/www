    Originally posted by jusgal:
    guys can share on how best to study mah?

    so many theory....how best to memorise?
    No such thing as best way to study. You have to understand yourself to know how you will study best.

    First, find out your best study times. Is it morning, afternoon or night? Study during that period of time. Eat and rest well before that period of time so that you will be at your peak.

    Secondly, don't memorise theories. Link to experiences. For Chemistry, it will be quite easy. If you have done enough experiments in school, you will find certain topics easy to understand and therefore can remember. If there's no experience, try other ways. For example, write down the main points of that theory. Find examples to support it.

    Lastly, get a study mate(s). You help them, they help you, it will help you to memorise better.
