    You going for job interview soon ?

    Hmmm.... Most questions they will ask is work experience that you have, what kind of asset you will be to the company, any working hours problems, and then while asking, note your response. Not just the answers, but the way you answer so as to grasp as much of your personality within that few minutes of interview. So be prepared with answers to the general questions and if you are nervous and dont know the answers, remember that honesty is a preferred alternative than lies.

    I believe all interviewees should show a lilttle nervousness, even if you are fully prepared. It shows that you are eager about the job. Also, one killer question is "why do you want to work here/with this company? " Prepare your answer for this one well. Please do not answer with "to gain experience", "I think this job is easy", etc. You will be outta that door faster than superman.
