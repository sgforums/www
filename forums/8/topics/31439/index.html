    Hi! Actually, sleeplessness can be caused by many factors. It really depends on individuals.

    For me, I was not able to sleep well for 1 month in a row and I went to the doctor. He asked, "Eh, what did you eat huh? Do you know your intestines so heated that can burn holes leow?" Oh well, I was eating chilli padis as tidbits and forgot to drink more water and thus, causing intestinal heatiness and couldn't sleep.

    Then, I was facing so much stress at work that I couldn't sleep. Work stuff keep getting in my mind and the dreams and my brain just wouldn't stop thinking about work.

    The most recent one was when I was suffering from "rinithis" (dunno how to spell) and my brain just keep waking me up to breathe coz I kept stop breathing. My nose was totally blocked with thick mucus all the way til forehead and cheeks. Now after doing the "right" stuff, at least can breathe leow.

    As for you, I sense that you are generally healthy and just couldn't sleep. Perhaps you need to sit down and think carefully...... are you suffering from stress, did you eat something that is causing your body discomfort? I would suggest meditation about 20 to 30mins before sleep. Meaning...... just sit on your bed when surrounding is quiet and just concentrate on deep breathing and don't think of anything else. Maybe visualizing yourself to be somewhere nice or the sea or the sky...... anything to make yourself relax.

    If not, excercise in the evening (5-7pm) might also help you sleep better. Basically, keep a healthy and active lifestyle, should be o.k. Some people just don't need so much sleep. Do you feel tired in the day but just can't sleep? If you don't, then its o.k, you probably don't need so much sleep. I guess your biological clock is very tuned to the wake up timing leow.

    Still not o.k after trying whatever you can, please see a doctor coz it may be something else. Just like me, didn't know that my intestines were so heated. :p

    GOOD LUCK!!!
