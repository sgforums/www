    Simple:

    > Install an internal PCI modem card
    > connect a phone line into modem port
    > set in BIOS to powe-on by modem

    Done.

    Next time, just leave the power switch on at the wall outlet but leave PC off. When you are outside, call back and the modem card will turn on your PC.

    Most modern ATX motherboards has this Self-power On option in BIOS/CMOS.
