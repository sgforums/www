    There are so many outdoor activities I want to do but I just don't have the time for them.

    1. Take any buses from the bus stop nearest to my home and see where
    the bus goes to. If the journey's too long, I can drop at a town centre or
    a busy road I've never been before and reccee the area;

    2. Likewise but with MRT trains;

    3. Visit all the other libraries in Singapore I've never been to. Yupz, even if
    it's located at the other end of the island from where I'm staying. I
    realised that every library stocks different kinds of books - i.e. I might
    find books by an author that is not available in every library;

    4. Sign up for short courses - there are hundreds of them in CCs... so many
    interesting topics too;

    5. Go cycling at Sentosa, Pulau Ubin, Pasir Ris Park or East Coast Park;

    6. Learn how to swim properly and fight my phobia for the deep end of a
    pool;

    7. Check out ALL the shopping malls in Singapore. I patronize a few and
    there are so many new ones that have sprouted out I have not really
    gotten familiar with

    I am not a loner but I like my own company so it is relatively easy for me to pick any of the above items to do should I feel bored at home. But there's so much chores I can do at home leh........
