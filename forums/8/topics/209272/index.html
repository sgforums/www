    Saga seeds eh? Well, here are some tips :

    1) Buy yourself a glue gun but a big tube of strong glu (UHU) will do the trick

    2) Get a hard board of any colour of you choice (pastels will be nice)

    3) Draw a big heart and write the words " Happy Birthday My Love " or any sentences that you feel befits the occassion

    4) Stick every single seed that you have onto the board, filling up the heart area.

    5) Use paints for the words. Add glitter or puff paints for extra effect.

    6) If you have a pic of her or if you can do a sketch of the person, stick it right in the middle of the heart.

    7) Wrap it nicely and send it off to her

    Good Luck
