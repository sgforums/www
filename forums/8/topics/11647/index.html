    Hi Crysania
    Well for D&D, a nice and *CHEAP* way to stand out is to geta vintage dress, either from ur parents/relatives/friends etc,it usually looks very different from the spaghetti-strapped formals from celia Loe and that shop in taka(i forgot the name).

    I've been to quite a few, so I'll just list some of the stuff I've worn (which pple complimented me on).

    1.Dress from Celia Loe...don't do this one UNLESS u don't mind someone else coming in the same outfit. It will set u back abt 300.

    2.Vintage formal outfit from Mom, *free* Yay!

    3.A square sequinned top in fuschia from Chinatown,($40), and a white long slightly puffy skirt with tulle underneath from Plaza Singapura ($30).

    4.Mom's silk cheaongsam altered down to my size... cost me abt 20+ in alterations. (cheaper than making one!!)

    5.Black spaghetti top with diamond straps ($50), and a long ballgown skirt( $100) from Ooh La La! in Plaza Singapura.

    6.Long dress made in Holland Village by Shanny Dressmakers...approx 100+.Forgot exact amt.took abt 2-3 weeks..

    7. Vintage Victorian long dress at $100 from overseas vintage shop...u might find nice stuff from 'The Vintage Shop' in Plaza Sing/Pacific Plaza. Be prepared to spend at least 200 on a branded dress.

    OK. Now abt MAKEUP

    If ur on a lower budget, URBAN DECAY does makeovers for abt $30. (BUGIS). You have to make an appointment at least a week in advance.

    If ur going to Splurge, go to Andy Lee's Beauty Image in Wheelock. It's abt $120 for makeup. Pricey, but he is very very good

    HAIR

    Try Madgelene @ Soda Hairdressing in Hollang Village. She's the owner, and she gave me a fantastic hairdo for my prom for less than 50. I forgot how much exactly. Andy Lee even asked who had done my hair...so she's pretty good

    Splurge: Salon d Orient/Mahogany/ or of course, Passion. depends who u get at these places, they;re usually very good too, but u got to check what type of hairstyle u want first, e.g up/down, half-half, curls? straight, cornrows/braids, crimped...etc.

    And rem to bring whatever stuff u wan to put in your hair, e.g. clips, hairbands, flowers...etc.

    Hope this Helps Crysania, and Have a wonderful Prom

    aRi
