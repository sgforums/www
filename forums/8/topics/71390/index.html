    Originally posted by Ice79:
    heard that credit cards issued by singapore banks might not be accepted in some shops overseas, true a not ah
    Not true. The issuance banks may be local, but credit cards like visa, mastercard and even Amex are internationally accepted.

    The only drawback is when you lose the card, you will not have the replacement while you're overseas. I lost an OCBC Visa card before. I reported loss and was having difficulties telling the credit card company the name of the bank as they have not heard of it. Luckily, I brought enough cash. For the rest of the journey, I used cash. I only got the replacement card after I came home.

    If you travel a lot, try to use Citibank and Amex cuz they both have offices all over the world. If you lose a card, just call them - either the office in overseas or call back Singapore office. You will get your card within 24 hours and the replacement + delivery is free of charge.
