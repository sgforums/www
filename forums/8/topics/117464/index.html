    While most taxi drivers are honest blokes, constantly toiling and struggling so that they could earn a decent living for their familes.. their bosses bring home million dollar pay cheques, by giving out more taxi licenses to operators..

    Bear in mind that an increase in rental merely translates to more profit for the taxi companies, cabbies dont neccessarily earn more.. NOW, who own those taxi companies ? Most have to drive almost 24 hours a day so as to earn enough to feed their family.. To make it worse, taxi drivers got to tussle with road blocks, speed traps, parking fines, inconsiderate and demanding passengers every day..

    Nevertheless, i empathise with them for having to drive in our safe but totally zero courtesy roads to make a livelihood.. If taxi are owned by drivers personally, they take pride in their equipment.. However, THEY( the bosses) find faults with everything and therefore, ALL TAXIS ARE TO BE OWNED by them..

    So the next time you chance upon a taxi, always give them the benefit of the doubt, they could be on the road for 12 hours, but still finding passengers in order to earn more dough.. Be patient with them and practise courtesy so that it goes a long way to make us a truely singaporean with a big heart..
