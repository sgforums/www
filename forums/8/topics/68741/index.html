    privatising -> to remove an entity from a corporate business. Usually applicable to government offices, where the business will become a private company instead of a government subsidised office or company.

    corporatising -> to join a network of business under one company. Usually applicable to companies controlled by one powerful shareholder who dictates it would reduce cost to corporatise a sole business into a company.
