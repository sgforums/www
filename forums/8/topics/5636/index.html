    Hmm..... I would suggest that you should take a coupla days off, book a chalet at Pasir Ris / East Coast, and the time together.

    Then you can go swimming in the sea/pool, have a picnic together, or if you're at Pasir Ris, go riding for a distance into that park like place near the beach. There are a coupla tower like wooden structures in really nice, deserted sections, where you can just sorta like, lie in each others arms, looking at the natural scenary, and just while away the time in each others company. You don't have to talk or do anything, just sit up there, and well..... enjoy.

    Tons of things to do, watching the sunset, having a barbacue together, and poking fun at your other half because he has soot all over his face, and he just looks darn ridiculous/cute like that..... taking advantage to kiss him because he DOES look cute, that sorta thing. Maybe you can even make him look cuter by dabbing a bit of cream/butter on his nose and such.

    If you're the athletic and adventurous sort, then don on a pair of berms, a t-shirt, and walking shoes, and just well..... explore. You can try walking from say, the area around Tanglin Mall, all the way to Chinatown, exploring every nook and cranny that you've never seen before along the way, trying figuring out where you landed. You can cover Fort Canning Park along the way, landing up at the Aquarium, or try to figure out how to make it to Chinatown from Daimaru without going thru Boat Quey. Tiring, but fun if you know how to go about it, not to mention that its an interesting experience on its own.

    If you're into food, go eat something from every stall in Chinatown. (Sorry if I can't give exact details..... I know how to GET there, but I don't know the name of the darn roads..... )

    Hmmmm...... hope that's enough.
