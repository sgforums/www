    You should only burst the blister if your normal activities are going cause it to burst anyway. Back in the army I've seen cases of humongous blisters that would easily have burst anyway from normal walking, much less running and marching.

    So I made those guys sit down on a chair..... I would then get down on my knees and pop their blisters with a clean sterillized needle. After allowing the liquid to drain naturally (do NOT remove the skin), treat it as a normal open wound. Clean the region with antiseptic lotion and cover it with a small piece of plaster.
