    Dear Friends

    I am sure by now, most if not all of you would have seen the trailers on TV for the Fabric of the Nation project.

    This project was started by Ms Ong Hee Yah, a VP with CNA some weeks ago to rally Singaporeans together to stitch a patchwork as a rememberance of the difficult times we've been through as a nation, and how the bonds of social cohesive, friendship and rallying round each other have helped us tide over a very difficult patch in our recent history.

    So this is something that we all must be in........

    All the pieces of patchwork will then be stitched together by a couple of quilt masters and PM Goh will put his patch into the piece on 23 Aug 20003 to complete the piece. In total, they need about 3000 pieces.

    The Deadline for submission of patchwork blocks is 12 midnight, 8 Aug 2003 but you still can submit to the President's Challenge public outreach roadshow at Raffles City on 10 August 2003 between 11am to 8pm.
