    Both schools although art instituitions, generally have abit of a difference in their mindset.

    NAFA teaches u more on the local arts scene as NAFA itself is one of the foundations of the local art scene.

    As i have said, different people different mindset. U have people quiting NAFA n going to La sallle because they cant agree with the way NAFA goes.

    But you also have people who do it the other way round.

    Regarding lecturers attitudes, i dont find the lect's stuck up attitude in nafa.
    They are generally friendly and help you along the way.

    Of course, no one can help u if u dont help urself.
    Putting down ur work is another way to help u imporve. In this industry critics play a big part.

    But u cant really impress everyone.

    The foundation programme in NAFA is adopted from the famous bauhaus in germany, which is also the modus operandi of many art schools around the world today.
