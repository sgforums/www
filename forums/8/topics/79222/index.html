    people = who do you have at the job.. are they the correct people?

    product = what is the product being produced and everything about it...

    plant = your manufacturing process... does your plant have the correct infrastructure and all that to support the product?

    process = what are the processes in place which is: what are the things you have laid out, the steps you follow, to cut it short, your "SOP"...

    program = how to implement the processes you have put in place...

    that's the long and the short of it...

    i may be wrong
