    In Ultima Online, the 1st MMORPG (released 10 years ago, and still running strong today on both official EA servers as well as non-official private run servers), there have been griefers since 10 years ago. Griefers and PKs are two groups that overlap/intersect, but are neither mutually inclusive or mutually exclusive. One can be the former but not necessarily the latter, and vice-versa. But griefers are universally hated more than PKs.

     

    Griefing in real life also exists, for sure, in every strata of society. From a power abusing boss bullying a subordinate he doesn't like, to underworld gangsterism/mafia/yakuza, to a family member deliberately making life grevious/miserable for another (usually re-acting out issues of an inter-karmic prison that cuts across lifetimes, unwittingly and unconsciously), to political powers griefing political victims, etc.
