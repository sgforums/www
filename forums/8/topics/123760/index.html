    Inspired by a post on Something Awful.

    So, I'm writing up a little challenge for all us sg folks.
    Basically, you have to give me an answer to the following question.

    The question is: How many five-year olds do you think you could take
    in a fight? Let's say you were in a fairly large area - let's say a
    basketball court. You have to take down as many five-year olds as
    possible, barehanded. Rules of the game:

    -The kids are motivated something fierce. They'll get the Soylent
    Green treatment if they lose. They're not easily scared or de-
    moralized. Giving them the "mommy stare" or shouting is ineffective.
    You might be able to convince us of something that works, though...
    -They receive one day of training in group tactics. They're five,
    so they probably suck at it, but they will actively attack you.
    -You have no equipment besides a cup to stop the ol' One Shot KO.
    -It's Game Over when the toddlers overwhelm you. You set the number
    attacking you before you enter the arena, and they're all there at
    once - no wave attacks.

    So, tell me, how many could you take down? Five? Twenty? A hundred?
    Justify it, and make it funny. Note it. I'll pick what I think
    are the best ones and put them up in another note or on the forums
    for a general vote. Best post is crowned the Babyslayer and wins
    the fabulous prize.

    So, let your vicious sides run free. Unleash the fury. It's pure
    comedy gold.
