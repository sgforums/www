    I've never really had much experience with free web hostings. They are many reasons for such errors on loading.

    1) your html/java script is not properly debugged
    2) if you are using those WYSIWYG type programs, then they are not generating very good html codes. (Dreamweaver by Macromedia is one of the best)
    3) the server hosting your site (in this case xoom is not able to read your codes properly)
    4) if you did it using Netobjects... it's even worse, not the program, but that you cannot publish straight from Netobjects, you will need to do a local publish to your harddisk then use FTP programs to upload it to your site

    Geocities is not too bad, you could give it a try.
