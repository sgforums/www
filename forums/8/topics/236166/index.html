    Book place to stay from the internet..... then go get a bus from JB to Mersing. When your bus arrive, many tour people will come look for you to sell ticket for boat or Hotel in Tioman.

    I Scuba dive there quite a few times....... only see shark once. Lasted 5 sec, not even have enough time to bring up my camera to take photo. Shark won't bother people 95% of the time.... so, no worry. Good chance to see Hawksbill turtle around Tiomans water.

    Don't go till at least mid March...... earlier, and the sea might be too rough for your safety........ Enjoy
