    Apple-Pear Streusel Pie
    Prep Time: 30 min
    Total Time: 1 hr 20 min
    Makes: 10 servings

    3 cups thinly sliced peeled apples
    3 cups thinly sliced peeled pears
    3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar, divided
    2 Tbsp. Tapioca
    3/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
    1/2 cup flour
    1/4 cup (1/2 stick) cold butter or margarine, cut into pieces
    1/2 cup Walnut Pieces, chopped
    1/2 pkg. (15 oz.) refrigerated pie crusts (1 crust)

    PREHEAT oven to 400Â°F. Toss apples and pears with 1/2 cup of the brown sugar, tapioca and cinnamon in large bowl. Let stand 15 minutes. Mix flour and remaining 1/4 cup brown sugar in medium bowl. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in walnuts; set aside.

    PREPARE pie crust in 9-inch pie plate as directed on package. Fill with fruit mixture; top with crumb mixture.
    BAKE 45 to 50 minutes or until juices form bubbles that burst slowly. Cool.

    Citrus Pepper Steak

    Prep Time: 15 min
    Total Time: 45 min
    Makes: 8 servings

    1 cup orange juice
    1/2 cup A.1. Original Steak Sauce
    2 serrano peppers, stems removed
    1 boneless beef sirloin steak (1-1/2 lb.)

    PLACE orange juice, steak sauce and peppers in blender container; cover. Blend until smooth. Pour into small saucepan. Bring just to boil. Remove from heat. Remove 1/4 cup of the orange juice mixture for brushing onto steak as it cooks; reserve 1/2 cup of the orange juice mixture for serving with the cooked steak. Store in separate containers. Pour remaining orange juice mixture over steak in resealable plastic bag; seal bag. Turn bag over several times to evenly coat steak. Refrigerate 30 minutes to marinate.

    PREHEAT grill to medium-high heat. Remove steak from bag; discard marinade.

    GRILL steak 10 to 15 minutes or until cooked through, turning occasionally and brushing with the 1/4 cup reserved orange juice mixture. Serve with reserved 1/2 cup orange juice mixture.
