    To facilitate JP MORGAN CHASE CORPORATE CHALLENGE, the following bus services diversion will take place on Wednesday 27 April, from 3pm to 8pm in Suntec City, Marina Centre and Padang areas.

    3pm to 8pm

    - Services 100, 107, 130, 131, 167, 608 and C2 skip Connaught Dr and diverted via Esplanade Dr towards Collyer Quay. Services 167 and 608 will not call at the stop outside Clifford Pier along Collyer Quay.

    - Service 75 will be converted to loop service and operates between Bt Panjang interchange and Finlayson Green, skipping Marina Centre.

    5pm to 6.45pm

    - Services 10, 70, 97, 100, 107, 130, 131, 162, 167, 196, 608, 700 and C2 skip Esplanade Dr and diverted via Stamford Rd, North Bridge Rd, South Bridge Rd, Pickering St, Church St and Finlayson Green towards Raffles Quay.

    5.50pm to 6.15pm

    - Service 36 (in the direction of Orchard) skips Suntec City/Marina Centre and diverted via Rochor Rd and Beach Rd.

    - Services 97, 111, 133, 502, C2 skip Temasek Ave and Raffles Ave and diverted via Temasek Blvd, Rochor Rd and Beach Rd.

    - Services C1 and C3 skip Raffles Ave and diverted via Temasek Ave, Temasek Blvd, Rochor Rd and Beach Rd.

    - Service 56 will be converted to loop service, and together with service 195, skip Raffles Ave and diverted via Temasek Ave, Temasek Blvd, Rochor Rd and Beach Rd.

    - Services 77, 106, 171, 857 and 960 will be converted to loop services and loop via Bras Basah Rd, Nicoll Highway and Stamford Rd, skipping Suntec City/Marina Centre.

    Press Release:
    SBST: http://www.sbstransit.com.sg/whatsnew_25April2005L.asp
    SMRT: http://www.smrt.com.sg/pressrele_new.htm
