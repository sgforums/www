Why I feel proud being a Singaporean

  • caramon.
    8 Jul 05, 16:35
    Being a small country like singapore with no natural resources, what we achieved is no mean feats.. It does not happened by chance and as a citizen of this small island, I feel proud being part of it.. Before anyone starts to shoot off, I, like many of you here don't agreed some of the policy being carried out by the government but let's not forget to give due credits to those who make it happen as it's never easy..

    Singapore with only the size of a land area of 637 square kilometres and a coastline of 193 kilometres had steadily increased the land through reclaimation over the years, with marina bay being the newest project.. And it certainly doesn't seems to be stopping anytime soon..

    The ease by which we can obtain clean, piped potable water in Singapore at any time of the day masked the fact that water is a precious and scarce commodity.. To remind Singaporeans of the need to conserve water, many publicity campaigns have been launched.. Besides that, to date we have 13 reservoirs since 1964.. Add Newater and hyflux water treatment and we have our 2nd and third national taps.. It will be a grave mistakes for our enemy to place complacency against us as we are well prepared and we all know the name 'chilli padi' was not given to us without valid reason..

    Yes, we may be a small island, we may lack natural resources but someone out there are not giving up without a fight and stop it as it is and I sincerely hope citizens of Singapore will be proud of our progress and not migrate because of oblivious reason as there's always hope and solutions to everything, Singapore being the perfect example..
