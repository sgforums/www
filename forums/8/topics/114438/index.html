    Originally posted by the.raven:
    concrete got lots of compressive strength and shear strength, more than enough to withstand the bus traveling at 50km/hour.
    Concrete has to be designed with certain grades like; 35 N/sq.mm, 40 N/sq.mm, 50 N/sq.mm. 1 KN = 100 kgs.

    To overcome shear, the engineer has to add more steel reinforcement (stirrups) to stiffen the bending movement. Especially the cantilever reinforcement concrete structures.

    I am sure that the RC structures for Mrt are designed with at least 50 N/sq.mm concrete and area of steel 20% in proportionate of the RC structures size. Of course, the steel reinforcement has to comply to BS 4449.
