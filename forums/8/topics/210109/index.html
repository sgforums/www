    HK not exactly a cheap place to go, especially if you are going to stay at hotels and such. I find the hotel and food costs taxing after a while, not to mention transport.

    If its the first visit, 4 days 3 nights shld be sufficient if you just target those touristy places. 3 days is cutting a bit fine but still do-able but more tiring loh.

    Plan your route carefully, i.e. visit all the places on HK island together as I found to my horror, crossing between Tsim Tsa Tsui and HK Island is not cheap by train or bus. The cheaper alternative is to take the Star Ferry.

    Otherwise HK is a nice place and I look forward to visit again. Cheers and enjoy !
