    Try this game, it's very very fun:

    prepare 2 paper bats (roll newspapers into a thick bat and bind it with tapes)

    all players stand in a big circle. Place 2 chairs (or anything big enough) one in the north and one in the south of the circle, each 15 meters away from the center of circle.

    select 2 persons by random (O-bay-som). One person be the attacker, one be the defender (scissors paper stone ), each of them holds a paper bat. The game starts and the attacker will chase to hit the defender with the bat. the defender can only block the attack with the bat and cannot hit back on the attacker.

    Once the attacker hits the defender, their roles changes - the defender now takes the role of the attacker, and the attacker now takes the role of the defender.

    The chase goes on for the new attacker to chase and hit the new defender.

    Anytime in the game, the defender can go to any of the chairs and hit it then he will be allowed to pass the paper bat to anyone in the circle by hitting the person with the bat and throw the bat on the ground at his/her feet, the person who receives the paper bat becomes the defender now.

    there's no score for this game, no winners or losers. It's just excitement.

    If you like this game, you can try a more exciting version: play this game at a place with obstacles (such as canteen with tables or lecture room with many chairs)
    Everyone can go free to run about instead of standing in a circle, but they are not allowed to leave the place AT ALL.

    The rule is the same but this time THere is NO more chair at the North or South (they have no more uses in this version).
    the attacker will chase to hit the defender, and the defender is allowed to hit anyone in the place anytime and pass the bat to the New defender.

    it's very fun and I wish to play it again , too bad I no more schooling liao
