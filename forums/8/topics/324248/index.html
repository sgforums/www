    Celebrate National Day by Joining the Qoo Roo Talk Show!
    Comiqs.com will be launching the world’s first Comic Talk Show online application on 7th July 2008 to celebrate Singapore’s 43rd National Day. The host of the Comic Talk Show is a Comiqs cartoon character by the name of Qoo Roo. He is cute, crazy and funny! Qoo Roo will start the show by asking Singaporeans questions related to this year’s NDP theme, “Celebrating the Singapore Spirit”. Users will upload images of themselves and answer questions in comic strip format.

    Prizes
    As this is a contest of wits, a panel of judges will decide whose answers are most interesting.
    20 winners will win cutting edge Anti-theft mobile gadget software courtesy of Bak2U Pte Ltd.

    Partners
    Partners include high traffic websites such as SPH’s Omy.sg; top local lifestyle forum, Sgforumsc.om; top local lifestyle review website, Yebber.com, Asia’s blog advertising network, Blog2U.sg and Nexus.

    Rules and Regulations
    1.    Contest period from 7th July to 9th August 2008. All entries must be submitted within this period.
    2.    Contestants may only use their own photos to participate in the contest.
    3.    Winners will be notified by email. The results will be released on the 14th August on Comiqs.com.
    4.    Winners are required to produce their identity cards during prize collection.
    5.    The organisers and partners reserve the right to use the winning entries for any publicity or marketing purposes, as well as for the furtherance of national or public interest.
    6.    The judges’ decision is final and no correspondence pertaining to the decision of the results will be entertained.
    7.    Submission of entries signifies acceptance of the rules and regulations above.
