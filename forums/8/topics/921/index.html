    Well yes, schooling and education is important, and they go hand in hand with survival these days. Without education, you won't be learned. You won't understand things around you. You can't communicate with others, neither can others communicate with you. Won't that be miserable? It's like a cow talking to a chicken or something.

So long as you have a GCE 'O' Level cert, the route after that is optional and very much up to the individual. With the increasing number of degree holders nowadays, I really feel we should practise reverse psychology. What for study so 'high' and compete in the tight job market up there? Come on down to the lower ranks. It may not be so respectable or high-paying, but it is at least a decent job.
