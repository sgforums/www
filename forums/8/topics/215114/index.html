    P2P - Legal in Singapore only if you are using it to access non-copyrighted items. If you are using it to download copyrighted things, you are not only downloading, but also distributing as P2P has upload function. Note that distributing is a much much much more serious crime than downloading.

    MSN - As long as you received a copyrighted item or send it out, you will be guilty, even if you claim that it is your friend who send you just for private use.

    What is illegal for download or distribution?

    Anything that is copyrighted. So basically anything that normally you would have to pay to get them. Softwares, music, movie, even e-book!
