    First, in Singapore CNB officers don't do the 'tasting' thingy.

    It's stupid, 'cause overtime you'll become addicted to drugs as well, if you keep finding and tasting drugs.

    Second, the reason why Singapore drug-enforcement officers don't do that is because the amount seized is usually small. Any retrieval of drug from the seized packet is considered 'tampering of evidence', which in cases of 'capital punishment', where a small fraction of difference in the amount will determine whether the trafficker lives or be hanged, it is all the more of paramount importance that the seized drug is NOT touched at all.

    In other countries, be it US, Columbia or Middle East, the drug seizures come in TONNES. You can keep tasting it till your tongue dropped and still the weight is in tonnes. So there's the difference why in some countries the officers can do so, while in Singapore it is a no-no.
