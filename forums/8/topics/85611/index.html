    and when things hinder you. What shall you do? Complain about why life is so bad?

    Try not to think of "WILL i become successful one day"
    instead, change your mindset and think "WHEN will i become successful one day"
    It's the same thing as when you were still young and still crawling.
    It can't be "Will i ever learn how to walk?"
    It's "When will i learn how to walk?"
