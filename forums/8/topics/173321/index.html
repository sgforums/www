    Cheng and Leung split....finally...

    Jan 9, 2006
    The Straits Times

    HONG KONG - Blame it on work, not a third party.

    That is the reason given for Hong Kong's celebrity couple Ekin Cheng and Gigi Leung to end their seven-year romance.

    Cheng, 39, revealed on Friday that he had moved out of her apartment.

    Leung's manager Chiang Shing Chun told the Hong Kong press that the couple had drifted apart because of her busy schedule.

    Mr Chiang said she had requested for a lighter workload 'but there's no way' to reduce that, so Leung 'finally made the painful decision to call it quits'.

    Cheng and actress Maggie Siu used to be an item before Leung, 30, came into the picture.

    Then, rumours were rife that Leung was the third party who had come between them.

    But Mr Chiang said that 'when Leung got together with Cheng, he had already broken up with Siu. Leung was seen as the culprit for breaking them up because Cheng did not explain that to the public'.

    Leung has penned a note on her record company Warner Music's website announcing the break-up.

    Cheng's manager said he will comment on the break-up in a press conference today.
