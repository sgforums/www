    Seemed like a very interesting show to me from the trailers... About a group of 'heros'... Each possessing their own individual specialities and characteristics....

    1st half of the show is more about the characteristics of the individuals... Not much of 'action' and special effects until the 2nd half of the show.... Not sure if the effects would seems more impressive over the big screen (I think it should be quite likely so..). But though the story seems rather unique, the plot is quite a weak one. Level of special effects are somehow limited and just 'ordinary' ones... With the 'Jerkyl' somehow just like your green hulk, with the rest of the gang making up a bit of graphic effects here and there, human heros bringing out the 'legend'....

    Overall, show pace is a bit slow... Actions are moderate, special effects are limited and short-lived....
