    I'm not sure if this is true for the case you're raising, but I know people who "think" they still love their former partners. In fact, what they feel is that they miss those times - you know how it is - when they were still together.

    For times like these, I don't think getting over someone entails forgetting them. Conversely, I think that it is important to remember them, because once a relationship is dead, it can only live on in our heats. Remembering a person and the good times allows one to be able to stay friends and more without the baggage of love.
