  • viciouskitty74
    13 Aug 09, 12:19

    And since the prom is in December.  Might as well you go around to find out.

    There is seamstresses at Tanjong Pagar Market/Hawker Centre, they do fairly good work at creating chanel and gucci office wears, but they are very busy, (You can see from their shop how busy swamped they are.)  And I doubt it will be within your budget.

    I prefer going to the seamstesses at City Plaza to make a brand new garment.

    Katong Shopping Centre has seamstresses and tailors too, and they also have plenty of ready made garments they can alter to your sizes.  Though they are more traditional stuff like cheongsums and sarong kebayas.

    Chinatown MRT there, next to OG, also have a few floors of folks selling cloth and seamstress/tailoring service, so you and your friend should take some time walking thru there.

    There is a 'Just' shop I think they sell gowns at Chinatown Point.  Maybe you should go take a look first, before you decide on tailoring instead.

    BTW, if you must tailor, and you are on a budget.  Consider tailoring a really ultra simple gown in a interesting fabric and not a black colour.  So the gown can be worn onto other occasions.

    I got a replica Vera Wang Denim Gown in dark denim blue, for wearing to dinners, weddings & whatever event.  Either throw on a cowboy hat and boots for a theme company event, or a simple pashmina shawl for a Raffles Hotel wedding dinner event.
