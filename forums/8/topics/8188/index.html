    FnU,

    The ideal age to get married is when you have enuf money...nowadays, young adults trying to start a life of their own are facing the biggest problem of their generation: $$$

    If you wanna get married, you would probably want a flat (dun even think about house...hehe). The flat (new) would cost about $300,000. 20% downpayment means you need $60,000.

    Thinking about a car? downpayment minimum would be about $10,000.

    Getting Married? The whole package could cost $15,000 (average).

    Add that up, you need $85,000 cold hard cash.

    Now let's take the average income of a person aged 28:

    $3,000 take home pay
    $1,000 goes to each parent
    $200 goes to Handphone Bills
    $600 goes to insurance
    $300 goes to food ($10 a day)
    $200 goes to misc (movies, clothes etc)

    That leaves him $700 to save a month

    it would take him 85 months to save for a flat
    or 21 months to save for a marriage
    or 14 months to save for a car

    note: once any of these purchases are incurred, there are monthly payments eg car $600-$900 per month (excluding petrol $300 per month)

    Flat: furniture, utilities etc...(considering the monthlies are from CPF...)

    Thus, I think that your ideal age of 38 is quite close to the truth...

    Hey...what about kids??? we haven't added that in....
