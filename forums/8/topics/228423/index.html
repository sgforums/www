    Just something to share.

    The Sweden ship Gotheborg has arrived in Singapore. It will be docking in Singapore at Vivocity for 9 days, starting 30th December 2006.

    It's really a blast from the past to be able to see a wooden ship in modern Singapore. It's really beautiful, in my opinion, that is.

    The public can get their tickets at $10 to board the ship. You won't be able to get to the bottom 2 decks, which are the sleeping quarters and the engine room. But you'll get to tour the gun deck and of course the sun deck.
