    As you are new, I think it's ok for you to be nervous. You will be fine after a few weeks.

    The most important thing is to take the right order in the end and understand which course should come first.

    I remember once going to Pizza Hut in JP many years ago and I ordered a garlic bread and a pizza. The point of me ordering the garlic bread was to let me have something to munch on before the main (I was very hungry at that time) but guess what... in the end my garlic bread and pizza arrived at the same time which really defeated the whole purpose of having an appetitiser!!

    So it's important to remember the courses which one to come first unless the customers advise you otherwise. Entree/appertiser comes first then main course then dessert & coffee.

    Good luck!

    if they ask me what's inside the pizza or whateva stuff.. i'd point at the menu... den read out everything...

    den if any chinese or indian order super supreme... or other pizzas with beef, pls inform them that the pizza contains beef. tho it might seem troublesome... a few customers changed their order after i informed them.. cos hor.. they can't take beef.

    den got some customers... they're vegetarians... or when they order veggie lover only.. inform them there's onion inside.. chinese vegetarians cannot take onion.. some indian vegetarians cannot take onion oso..

    and hor when u key ur order, pls state customer's a vegetarian.. so they will take extra care not to touch any meat...

    anything not sure just say i'm not very sure, pls wait while i check with a colleague. best is to pull ur colleague over... den if u wan, u can explain to the customer saying u're new.

    everytime see an empty plate, clear. see empty glass, fill it (plain water only lar)

    and if u see customers feeding babies porridge.. with the restaurant utensils, pls tell the customer to pass u the utensils.. so u can dispose of it accordingly.

    in case its not halal. what we did last time.. when customer use the spoon or bowl to feed porridge, we throw the entire thing into rubbish bin

    and always suggest some stuff to go with the meal... except when they're ordering a lot lot lot... den dun.

    if they ordered soup, ask them if they wan puff pastry to go along or sth...
