    Channel NewsAsia - Friday, July 13

    15 July is deadline for GST Credits sign-up at ATMs

    SINGAPORE: Singaporeans who have not signed up for their GST Credits have until 15 July to do so at the ATMs.

    Those who miss the deadline would be required to sign up through the website or use the GST/1 forms available at any Community Centres, Community Development Councils, SingPost offices or CPF Service Centres.

    For those who have done their sign-ups via the website or ATMs, the payment will be made to their accounts within four working days.

    And for those who have signed up using the GST/1 forms, payment will be made within ten working days.

    Those requesting for cheque payment will receive their cheques within 15 working days.

    Singaporeans who require help to sign up can approach officers at the sign-up venues.

    Grassroots leaders will also continue to do walkabouts to help the elderly and low-income families sign up for their GST Credits.

    For more information, please visit http://www.gstoffset.gov.sg or call the CPF Board at 1800-2222-888. - CNA/so
