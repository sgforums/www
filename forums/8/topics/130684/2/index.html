    Lots of things to do and places to go.

    1) Golf can easily take half a day (with meals, washing up and showering)
    Try out diff golf course in the region (Malaysia, indonesia, Thailand). Scenaries at some courses are so therapeutic, it's good for the soul.

    2) Swimming, cycling and running. Just sign up for a triathlon and work for it!

    3) Work out at the gym every other day

    4) View condo showunits. There are so many condo launches lately. Every weekend can visit 2 to 3. Inspect their decor design and ask lots of questions about the condo

    5) Test drive cars. Each week you can go to one brand and try out ALL their models on the road.

    6) Organise Mahjong sessions. BBQs are too troublesome to organise, mahjong sessions are so much easier. Make it into a weekly gathering where the one with the least amt of chips would have to provide refreshments/meals for everyone the following week. No cash needed.

    7) Leave your cash and credit cards at home and go window shopping. Check out electronic stuff (say, cellphones) and ask lots of questions until you know the product inside out. Do it with other models of the same product. Try out and feel the products.
