    The Electric New Paper :

    Did KL teacher record school sex video?
    Steamy footage of students in toilet romp doing e-mail rounds
    26 September 2007

    A VIDEO clip of two students caught having oral sex in a Kuala Lumpur school toilet has been spreading via SMS and e-mail over the last few days.

    And it seems that teachers are circulating the footage.

    The person who filmed the students from the top of the toilet door is believed to be one of two teachers who caught the pair red-handed in the video, reported Harian Metro.

    Several teachers in Kuala Lumpur received the video clip of the two secondary school students, who are prefects.

    A teacher from another school who did not want to reveal his name said he received this explicit clip from a teacher friend, who in turn got it via e-mail.

    The teacher who got the clip said that the issue should have been dealt with by informing the parents and having counselling sessions with the two students concerned.

    He added: 'Their indecency should not have been made public to prevent them from being stigmatised and possibly getting depressed from this shaming.'

    The 2 1/2-minute clip shows the two students in matching school uniforms kissing and having oral sex in a toilet cubicle in their school.

    Their faces could be clearly seen.

    A male voice was later heard ordering the girl to button up her uniform as she cried and pleaded to be released.

    A male teacher could also be heard questioning the boy who didn't want to open the toilet door.

    The teacher called out about 10 times to the boy before dragging him out. The boy was heard begging to be spared, saying 'I don't want, teacher'.

    The male teacher scolded them, saying that they let down all the prefects in their school.

    On 25 May, Harian Metro reported that a gang of boys distributed a video clip of themselves molesting a veiled girl.

    It is understood that the video clip is now being distributed widely via SMS.
