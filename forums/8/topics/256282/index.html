    Its all a matter of preference, none is strictly 'better' than the other.

    I prefer classical acoustic (nylon strings) for my own reasons.. i find it:

    1) Feels more natural.. i like how it seems like the sound is at my finger tips, its more 'raw' in that sense.

    2) Nylon strings produce a thicker, more mellow sound, which is wat i like.

    3) Its much less hassle to set up.. in fact, no set up is required.. just grab and play. I hate spending time hooking stuff up, but no choice when im studio jamming. Very often my firends n I simply jam with acoustic guitars, including acoustic bass.. but our drummer feels left out
