    from the looks of it , u have oily scalp

    u only wash your head twice a day? man you're over -doing it . once is enough .

    what do u wash with? shampoo? u should only apply shampoo once a day , dont over do it as the hair scalp will over-react. ( stick to the shampoo you're already comfortable with )

    do you apply conditioner everyday? if not please get one ( i use SilkPro's ) and apply once everyday

    how do you dry your hair? blow dry ,brushing the towel against your hair or blotting it ( just covering the towel on your hair to absorb water and let it stay on for a few minutes ) . lets say u brush the towel against your hair, u are also brushing it against your hair scalp, and it may get damaged

    usually blotting of the hair is recommended as it doesn't harm your hair scalp since it does not apply any high amount of pressure on it .

    ...and do you get high amounts of dandruff?
