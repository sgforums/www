    it's easy ...

    Let say u want to find Solitaire by Clay Aiken ...

    So u type ..

    @find Solitaire

    OR

    @find Clay Aiken - Solitaire

    OR

    @find Clay Aiken

    Then the servers will message you IF THEY HAVE THE SONGS IN THEIR LIST.

    Look through all that appear ..
    Got something like this ..

    !AAA Clay_Aiken_-_Solitaire.mp3
    !AAA Clay_Aiken_-_Invisible.mp3
    etc..

    What you need to do is ..

    HIGHLIGHT (Left click your mouse and hold, don't let go. Drag from ! till .mp3

    from ! -- >>> !AAA Clay_Aiken_-_Soliataire.mp3 <<<-- to .mp3

    THEN LET GO OF THE MOUSE

    Then go back to the main mp3 channel and press CTRL-V to paste what you have highlight or right click your mouse and paste.

    And wait for the server to send you the song. Always bear in mind the slots available. You can see how many slots are available in the server when the server message u with the songs u want ..

    And u're done.
