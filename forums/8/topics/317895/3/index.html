    This is a  controversy topic, different societies, culture, religion, country and people view tattoos as different meanings. Some people might treat this as an art and a form of expressions, some might view it as a out of society action, some countires might view it as a culture or religion perspective.

    However in singapore, tattoos are widely associated with gangsterism or an action that defies society. Imagine you meeting your in-laws and they saw your tattoos, that's gonna be the end of the marriage for some.

    All to all you might like to have a small tattoo that exibits some style or tribal meaning in it so that people don't mistake you as a 70s or 80s gangster. And don't put it on too visible places, put it someway that can be covered away if need to be.
