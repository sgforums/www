    MinDef allows NSman to defer their ICT for the reasons stated below:

    1. On compassionate grounds, eg. marriage and honeymoon, wife's delivery, NOK illness etc.
    2. National respresentation at recognised regional and international games
    3. New employment
    4. New established business
    5. Examination for full/part-time studies
    6. Simultaneous call-up of 2 or more NSmen within the same department and who are employed in the same capacity

    To apply for ICT deferment, you have to type out a letter and submit to your NS unit via postal mail or fax with supporting documents, 10 weeks before the start of ICT. More info can be found in your NSmen handbook.
