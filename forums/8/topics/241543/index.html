    My solid diamond 0.50 carat is a V VS2, Grade E, good cut ( bloody jeweller/diamond cutter did not do an excellent job). It goes for about AUD$1.8k alone for the stone because it is small and because it is not a master cut . With workmanship and the gold , it costs even more.

    However if you are looking for a really good jeweller/diamond cutters , I know that Tiffany & Co uses Rand diamonds. Cartel supplier of Rand is always DeBeers. So you can actually request any reputable jeweller to purchase a stone from Rand. Would set you back by a few thousands but their cuts are excellent.
