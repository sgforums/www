    Ensure that the rate of evaporation of the salt solution (e.g. by boiling it) is on par with the incoming supply of infinite water. The conc , theoretically, should remain constant.

    proposed set up:-
    an enclosed vessel with steam outlet at the top and water inlet at the side. The vessel should have an internal float/ water level sensor tomeasure the height of the water level dropped after heating the salt solution. The diameter of the cylindrical vessel should be constant throughout its height. the level sensor should have the capability to calculate the drop in water level after boiling the salt solution and multiply itself by a constant (i.e set as the diameter of the cylinder base). The resultant volume calculated can be sent as a signal to open or regulate the flow of fresh inlet water supply so that they cann mix with the salt solution and thus keep the concentration constant.

    such set up and concept are widely employed in process plant and piping industries.
