    If you're taking 'O' level Biology, throw away your textbook and all your notes, just memorize everything in this Cambridge Revision Guide (can buy at Popular Bookstore Bras Pasar).

    http://www.cambridge.org/catalogue/catalogue.asp?isbn=0521648467

    Prelim may be a bit late, but from now to 'O' levels enough time to memorize the entire book and still get a good grade.

    I'm a Biology & Chemistry teacher in a secondary school in the East (and in my classes I get my students to use this book instead of textbook), and I'm also giving 'A' level / 'H2' level Biology tuition.
