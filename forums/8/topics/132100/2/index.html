    The name derives from the unity of Heaven, Earth and Man, which makes up the Tian Di Hui. Note that the "huI" word has a "ren zi tou". Triad=made up of 3.

    Tian Di Hui=forerunner of what we now call Hong Men. All the what tong what gao what siao ALL can be traced to this.

    In the past the Triad's main purpose was to overthrow the Qing dynasty. But after that they lost purpose in their organisations, and instead turned to illegal means to keep on surviving, which i guess has spread all around.
