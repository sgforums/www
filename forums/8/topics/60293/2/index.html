    your contract is a good source of info to answer most of your questions

    u mentioned 3 mths probation thereafter confirmation. if they did not wish to confirm you,

    what was the reason, not paying bonus is not a good reason, its usally performance related
    did u have an apprasial
    was in given to u in form of writing BEFORE your date of confirmation?
    the law states that if there is nothing in black and white to either confirm or dimiss you AFTER your date of confirmation, it is deemed that you have been confirmed...

    u mentioned 1 week notice, is that b4 or after confirmation?

    as for your questions on whether off day, time off is included, it depends...what happens in most coys is that you will be working during the notice period as per normal....then they will pay u back in $$ terms the value of your off days and time off,

    alternatively, some coys offset the off day by asking u not to come to work, but u still get paid for it...

    for payment of bonus, aws, standard practise is its paid to confirmed staff of at least 6 mths...
