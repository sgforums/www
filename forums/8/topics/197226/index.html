    Idol Chatter

    NO WAY is disappointed with the calibre of the current batch of Singapore Idol contestants.

    No way says: "I cannot believe they put these kids on national TV and call it entertainment. These guys are embarrassing - I've heard my mates do better drunk at karaoke."

    He thinks the saving grace is the judges; they are, he says, not too bad.

    Look at it this way, meanwhile, sounds like a gardener philosopher.

    "I used to have a tough time trying to get rid of the weeds in my garden every weekend. Well, that all changed. Why think of them as weeds? Think of them as plants you don't mind having. Voila."

    Just in case, you do not get it, he explains: "So, look at Singapore Idol as a comedy: Sit back and enjoy, William Hung of the American Idol series didn't do too badly commercially either."
