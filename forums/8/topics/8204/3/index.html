    As for the quality of post, I choose to ask myself a question, "What is quality?" Is quality a matter of words, verbs, expressions? Or is it the thoughts that transpires during the discussion? Or could it be the putting up of a good arguement? Or is it the ability to withstand the many many flames lashing out at you? Or....nevermind...I can go on and on and on...but I end up with on simple conculsion. A quality post is not a matter of a long essay, nor is it a matter of using latin. Nor is it about one's stamina on withstanding insults and throwing them back. To me, I conclude by saying, a quality post is one that can bring new light on a view point of subject, one that can express one's thoughts in simple short sentances which are easily understood, one that allows rooms for exploration and discussion, one that causes a person to generate interest in a topic which he/she would normally not be interested in. Such would be a quality post, for it truly allows a developement in a topic being discuss....such is the use of forums, to allow room for discussion and a place for the exchange of ideas.

    Note the word exchange, an exchange means you give something and take something back. Thus sharing a thought with all. In this light we often see 2 types of posts.

    1) The closed mind. I think I dun have to explain much on this. It is pretty straight forward. Simply a person is only interested in keeping to his own ideas and not bothering to take others thoughts into consideration. This is most of the time the ego at work. Where on refuses to believe that he could be wrong.

    2) The bargain writer. Basically what I meant is just that, such people just post a simplistic answer, one that does not really contributes to a topic, like "That is so funny, hahhahahaha." Finish. Now it is not wrong of them to do that, dun mistake what I meant. It is just that such post discourages quality posts from reappearing. By their sheer non-contribution in the long run, they kill the thread by not contributing discussion areas to the thread. Thus the dying of a thread.

    For the 3rd generation we are talking about, I dun think they dun post quality posts, just that their level of maturity and life experiences does not allow for much flow of ideas and unique thoughts on life. Thus limiting the scope and view they can contribute to a thread. But look at it this way, would this place not be a good training ground where they can learn and listen to the many worldly view that are available here? And as time goew on, when they are more mature, they would be able to contirbute more plus our posts added into thoughts when they post in future. This would be a great breeding ground for great flamers and politicians alike.

    And finally I would like to touch on lamers. *grins* In every generation we see lamers, from GBM, to shaolin boy, to pokemon(well he is not really that bad just that his area of expertise puts many woman off with it's disrespect.) to twinkle to Hugo. A lamer is a devil's advocate, and for a discussion to go on, a lamer is required to fan the flames, shit on our heads to stir some emotions. And that allows for an exchange of ideas.
