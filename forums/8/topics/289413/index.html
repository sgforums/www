    Alumni from the King Edward VII College of Medicine/Raffles College era (1905 to 1949)

    * Lee Kuan Yew - Founding Prime Minister of Singapore
    * Mahathir bin Mohamad - 4th Prime Minister of Malaysia
    * Goh Keng Swee - former Deputy Prime Minister of Singapore
    * Toh Chin Chye - former Deputy Prime Minister of Singapore
    * Tun Abdul Razak - 2nd Prime Minister of Malaysia
    * Benjamin Sheares - 2nd President of Singapore

    Alumni from the University of Malaya (Singapore) era (1949 to 1962)

    * Sellapan Ramanathan - President of Singapore and NUS Chancellor
    * Wang Gungwu - Director of the East Asian Institute at NUS

    Alumni from the University of Singapore era (1962 to 1980)

    * Goh Chok Tong - current Senior Minister of Singapore and former Prime Minister of Singapore
    * Shunmugam Jayakumar - former Minister for Foreign Affairs of Singapore
    * Yaacob Ibrahim - Minister for the Environment and Water Resources and Minister-in-charge of Muslim Affairs in Singapore.
    * Kishore Mahbubani - dean of the Lee Kuan Yew School of Public Policy at NUS
    * Wong Kan Seng - Deputy Prime Minister of Singapore
    * Tan Choo Leng - wife of Senior Minister Goh Chok Tong and former patron of the National Kidney Foundation in Singapore
    * Abdullah Tarmugi - Speaker of Parliament in Singapore and former Minister in the Singapore Government.
    * Balaji Sadasivan - Neurosurgeon and Senior Minister of State for Foreign Affairs in Singapore
    * Chan Heng Chee - Professor of Political Science and Singapore's Ambassador to the United States
    * Chew Choon Seng - CEO of Singapore Airlines
    * Ong Keng Yong - secretary-general of the Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN)
    * Subhas Anandan - reputable Singapore criminal lawyer and senior consultant at Harry Elias Partnership
    * Lin Hsin Hsin - reputable artist and digital media pioneer
    * Choo San Goh - prominent chorographer involved with the Washington Ballet
    * Arthur Yap - prominent poet and linguist

    The National University of Singapore era (1980 - )

    * Woon Cheong Ming Walter - former Nominiated member of Parliament and Singapore Ambassador to Belgium, The Netherlands, Luxembourg and the Holy See
    * Ng Eng Hen - Minister of Manpower and Second Minister of Defence in Singapore
    * K. Shanmugam- Senior Counsel and Member of Parliament in Singapore for Sembawang Group Representation Constituency
    * Davinder Singh - Senior Counsel and CEO of Drew & Napier
    * Vivian Balakrishnan - Minister for Community Development, Youth and Sports in Singapore.
    * Sha'ari Tadin - former Senior Parliamentary Secretary and first Malay graduate PAP Member of Parliament, Singapore
    * Low Thia Khiang - Secretary-General of the Workers' Party of Singapore and Member of Parliament in Singapore for Hougang constituency
    * Olivia Lum - entrepreneur, founder & CEO of Hyflux Group
    * Sylvia Lim - Chairman of the Workers' Party of Singapore and lecturer at Temasek Polytechnic in Singapore
    * Steve Chia - secretary-general of the National Solidarity Party and former Non-Constituency Member of Parliament (2002 to 2006)
    * Alex Au - independent writer who publishes articles on the internet under the moniker "Yawningbread" and prominent gay rights activist
    * James Gomez - Second Assistant Secretary-General of the Workers' Party of Singapore and Programme Officer of the Political Parties Programme at the International Institute for Democracy and Electoral Assistance (IDEA)
    * Jeanette Aw - Actress
    * Kelvin Lim - Singer and well-known blogger
    * Stella (Singaporean singer)
    * Tan Cheng Han - current Dean of NUS law school
    * Kaira Gong - Singer
    * Chew Sin Huey - Singer
