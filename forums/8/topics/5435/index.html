    Recently i watch quite a few movies so I am going to give all of u some reviews of those movies I have watch and u decide if that particular movies is worth watching it . HERE GOES>>>>>>>>>>>>>>

    MOVIE NO. 1 ( TITLE : BLUE STREAK )

    This is an action packed comedy starring Martin Lawrence which also acted in 'Bad Boys' with Will Smith . When my friend introduce this movie to me , my first though is 'Argg , not again , those meaningless movies like Beverly hill cops but when u actually watch it , I am sure that U will enjoy every minute of the show 'cause the twist is funny and Martin Lawrence can really act , i mean he's a funny guy and u will surely like the ways he handle his so call ' Tasks' assign to him . For those who have seen the movie , u know what i meant . For those who wants to relax and laugh it out , this is definitely a movie for u to lighten your mood .

    MOVIE NO.2 ( TITLE : 303,FEAR,FAITH,REVENGE )

    Actually , I dun intent to watch this movie mainly because it's a Thai movie . You will think that what a low budget film like this will do to scare the audience . It just that me and my platoon mates couldn't find another movie to watch during our nights off so we just bought our tickets and hop into the teathre screening the movie . ( anyway , it's Tuesday and it's only $5 per ticket so.. ) There are actually quite many scary parts in the movies and the movie is quite bloody . It will make u guess and guess who is really the killer which suddenly kills so many students in that Christian school and the person just can't stop killing . The moment u though things will calm down , another person is murdered . Well , if u think i am exaggerating , go see for yourself , it's worth the $5 or $7 you spend definitely !!!!

    MOVIE NO.3 ( TITLE : 007 The World is not enough )

    I just watch the movie yesterday with my good friend in West Mall and i won't recommend it to anyone here reading this . Although i will say that the plot is not bad but the whole movie including the acting really sucks !! The beginning part is o.k but as the movie flows , it keep getting worst and worst especially the last part , really disappointing compare to the last Bond movie . At least the last Bond movie got some good fighting by that HK actress ( forget the name ) . This one got pretty babes which can't really act well . I dun really like Denise Richards acting inside the show although i must admit she's sexy but , more good acting please !!!! The most disappointing past is the new BMW which is destroyed too easily . ( What the heck am i watching the show for !! ) So ladies and gentleman , any good comments about this movie which u will like to enlighten me ? PLs do 'cause i need it . The action is not enough , the acting is not enough , the trill is not enough , the explosion is not enough which leads to 'THE WORLD IS NOT ENOUGH ' Hee ! hee !

    Movie No.4 ( Title : Stir Of Echoes )

    Initially , I don't have high hopes for this movie after watching the previews and reading the reviews in ETC magazine but thanks to the boring Sat which is today and the fact that me and my friends can even get the tickets 5 minutes before the show starts and the FACT that the place is LIDO , i have no choice but to tell myself to enjoy the movie no matter how bad is it . The plot is quite similar to Sixth Sense but as the movie quote says' IT GOES TO PLACES SIXTH SENSE DOESN'T DARE ' , oh boy , it really means it !!! I won't say that this is a good movie compare to Sixth Sense but it got it's own taste for example , scenes that grip your heart tight and hair raising moments . This movie will also make u wonder who the murderer is although it's not as trilling as 303 . Why not give this one a shot and see if u got the same feeling as me .

    Movie No.5 ( The Blair Witch Project )

    Although i did not see this movie , what i get from my friends after watching this movie is not very healthy and encouraging so I decide to give this one a miss . For those of u who have seen it , u 'll know what i mean right .;-) Dun ever try to buy the Vcd 'cause from what they say about the movie , i am afraid that u 'll think that the Vcd is spoilt because of the shaky images and if u get headaches watching the movie , well , i dunno if u faint watching the Vcd . Anyway , I bought the soundtrack of this movie 'THE BLAIR WITCH MIX BY JOSH ' out of curiosity and i don't really like the gloomy music inside . If anyone of you want any samples of the tracks , don't hesitate to tell me o.k .
