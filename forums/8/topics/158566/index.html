    The trend in 1996 was Tamagotchi.

    Digimon: Digital Monsters started in 1997 but the device itself was pretty expensive so it didn't sell well.

    During 1998, the price was push down to $10.00 which created a Digimon: Digital Monsters craze is Singapore. Digimon: Digital Monsters cards started releasing.

    In 1999, the first Digimon: Digital Monsters anime, Digimon Adventures was released and a Digimon: Digital Monsters game for the Playstation, Digimon World was also released.

    In 2000, the sequel to Digimon Adventures, Digimon Adventures 02 was released. Sometime around here, a mange with a alternate storyline consisting of Yagami Taichi and his Veedramon was released. Digimon World 2 was also released.

    In 2001, the third anime for Digimon: Digital Monsters, Digimon Tamers was released. It introduced an entire new system of powering up for the Digimons whereby the Tamers would swip cards to power up their Digimon partners. This new system was later used in other anime and shows such as Masked Rider: Ryuki. Digimon Tamers wasn't a sequel of the Digimon Adventures series and its new system of powering up was uncomfortable and unrealistic to many, thus the ratings of the Digimon series started to go downhill.

    In 2002, the fourth and currently the final anime series for Digimon: Digital Monsters was released, Digimon Frontier. Digimon Frontier introduced the fresh idea of humans Digivolving into Digimons using Sprits. This idea swayed even more fans away. Digimon World World 3 was also released.

    Although anime series for Digimon: Digital Monsters has ended, this idea is not yet quite dead. The 8th movie for Digimon: Digital Monsters titled Digimon: X - Evolution was broadcasted this year with the DVDs releasing on 25th November 2005. Digimon World X or Digimon World 4 was released for the PlayStation 2 earlier this year. New Digimon: Digital Monsters Trading Cards are still constantly being released by Bandai, not to mention the latest Digimon: Digital Monsters Virtual Pet product called Digimon Accel.
