This reminded me of a scene I saw on TV in the 60s during the racial riots started from China Town and spreading to other parts of Singapore. I remembered my father and two elder brothers standing by outside our kampung house with parangs in their hand to protect the rest of our family members and our kampung house just in case we were attacked. I remembered going outside and standing outside the door and was scolded by my father and two elder brothers to get inside the house. It was quite a frightening experience. I also felt the anxieties and fears of my father and my brothers. Nothing happened to us and luckily too in my kampung, the Malays and Chinese habitants were neutral. Just two weeks ago, my wife and I from JB, visited Little India for an evening stroll and did some sights seeing along Little India. We went to Mustafar Shopping Centres and City Square Mall to shop.
