    optical zoom is wat is seen thru the lens. the real picture gets captured onto the ccd which is then interpolated into pixels. the digital zoom then enlarges the pixels, using again, interpolation.

    so while optical zoom is the pure picture, digital zoom suffers from interpolation TWICE... u tell me which one better?

    if u ask me photoshop's bicubic interpolation is much better than those offered in the cameras. i would NEVER use the digital zoom on a digital camera.
