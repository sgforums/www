    If your "O' level aggregate is single digit then go for top JC like Hwa Chong, RJC, VJC cos majority of scholarships given to them.
    If yr aggregate is 10- 16, still ok to go jc. Most prob. u end up doing Engrg, bio-tech, Computer, Business, Arts.
    If yr aggregate is 18- 20, then I advise u to go poly unless u don't mind doing Arts.

    Know your own strengths & interest. Are u good at Humanities or Sciences?

    To go JC u have to be very strong in Eng. cos must pass GP to get into U.
    U need to read widely for GP cos it covers topics like War, Economy, Edn. etc.
    I know of pple who are very good in Maths & Sc. but choose poly cos they are not confident in doing GP.
    Also in JC, u need to be actively involve in CCA, leadership, community services to gain points. U also have to do project work which is very time consuming. This means very long sch. hours. There are also tests every week. So u must be very good at time management.
