    These are Bollywood and Tamil flicks to look out for in 2006
    Out & soon to be Out now


    JOK (Tamil / Romance & Drama)
    Director: Krishna
    Cast : Surya , Jyothika and Bumika Chawla
    Music : A.R Rahman
    Synopsis : A down to earth ordinary guy keeps a account diary of his past college love life which becomes the turning point in his otherwise happy contented married life with another woman


    Bheema (Tamil / Action Drama)
    Director: Lingu
    Cast: Vikram and Trisha Krishnan
    Music: Harris Jayaraj
    Synopsis: A mercenary goes on an ordeal to protect the life of a helpless girl.


    V_V (Tamil / Suspense Thriller)
    Director: Gautham Menon
    Cast : Kamal, Jyothika and Prakash Raj
    Music : Harris Jayaraj
    Synopsis : A police officer goes in search of a group vicious killers who jump continents


    Godfather ( Tamil / Action & Drama)
    Director: K.S. RaviKumar
    Cast : Ajith and Asin
    Music : A.R. Rahman
    Synopsis : A lone vigilante seeks revenge for the death of his parents with three different personas in disguise


    Guru (Hindi / Contemporary Drama)
    Director: ManiRatnam
    Cast : Abishek Bachan, Aishwarya Rai, Malikka Sherawat,Madhavan,Vidya Balan
    Music : A.R Rahman
    Synopsis : Period Biography based on the life of India's biggest textile tycoon Dhirubhai Ambani.


    Lage Raho Munnabhai (Hindi / Comedy & Drama)
    Director: Rajkumar Hirani
    Cast: Sanjay Dutt, Arshad Warsad, Diya Mirza and Vidya Balan
    Music: Shantanu Moitra
    Synopsis: Sequel to the big hit Munnbhai MBBS, the Mafia don takes on the pretence game again in order to win the heart of a radio jocky lady this time.
