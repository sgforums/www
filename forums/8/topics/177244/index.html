    I will answer the questions part by part.

    First question on how to apply to a school after getting the results. You will apply online. It is still the JAE as usual. JAE will start one day after the results are released.

    Second question about the form. The form has 12 choices. You can put in any choices you like. It is not limited to poly courses only. You can put JC courses as well as ITE courses.

    Third question about where to submit the form. You will submit online. Follow the instructions on the webpage. After submitting, select the mode of payment. You will pay either online or going down to MOE to pay by cash or NETS.

    Last question about failing Maths. If you scored a D7 for Maths, JAE will probably be out for you. You may have to apply via DAE (Direct Admissions Exercise) if your JAE application failed. It is considered on a case by case basis. If you intend to apply for courses like IT and Engineering and Sciences, you MAY NOT get in. For other courses like design, a portfolio is required. No portfolio, and poor grades will not get you into the course. Again, on case by case basis. You have to re-take O Levels Maths after gaining entry to the poly. They will offer supplementary courses which are compulsory for you to attend. If you fail again, you will be out of the polytechnic.
