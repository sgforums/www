  • Schwing
    6 Aug 05, 23:36
    http://www.danga-bay.com/leisure.html

    has anyone been there before? it's located very near J.B...i was there with my wife, a friend and his wife...it's still undergoing some finishing touches, but the sea view was really captivating...i oculdn't say the same for the food at the Bayleaf though...it's pretty much like a food court, offering local delights like satay, ikan bakar(grilled fish) and even fast food (Mary Brown)...the sea view sort of made up for the average tasting food...

    after your meal, you can take a short tram ride around the bay, just for RM$1.00 per ticket...you'll get to see more of the bay, the theme park rides, kite flyers, the playground and you can even take a sea tour for RM$10 per adult (children RM$5)...

    if you're in J.B doing your grocery shopping, you might want to nip down to Danga Bay just to check it out...
