How to manage your credit cards bill

  • Gazelle
    21 Mar 06, 23:03
    The purpose of starting this thread is to share with you what I think are the most effective ways to manage your spending with plastic.

    a) Understand what credit card companies' intention
    Their main intention is to make you feel richer than what you really are.

    b) Use plastic when you can only benefit from it
    e.g. special discount and rewards point. However that should never be the only reason for you to spend money

    c) Beware of overseas expenditure
    Credit card company make loads of money through forex conversion. If you need to go for a holiday, try not to use you credit card whenever is possible.

    d) Settle you outstanding balance even before they are due
    Cash is king, the more cash you have the richer you feel. So the rule of thumb is, try to pay off whatever you have spent with credit card, so that you know what is your nett cashflow for the month. This is extremely important if you choke up big ticket items.

    e) Credit card are hassle and they cost money
    There is no need to carry so many carry card of the same purpose. e.g. why do you need to have 3 VISA when they all serve the same purpose? For me, get yourself, 1 master, 1 VISA and 1 Amex is more than enough. What is more important is still CASH.
