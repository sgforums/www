    Did anyone here know about Chanakya?

    It is considered that he is the Sun Tzu contemporary in India.

    Chanakya, the world's first management guru (300 BC) from India has worked out in detail on how to manage an organization. Some of the challenges that are faced by modern economists and managers are very well addressed in the book Kautilya's Arthashastra.

    "Radhakrishnan Pillai" is a well known columnist in Times of India publication on Arthashastra anecdotes. He is a management trainer and is associated with large organizations in improving their management practices. He has spent a decade decoding the knowledge of Arthashastra for the needs of contemporary management. He is in Singapore to conduct the workshop to address key business leaders in Singapore. He will also discuss the topic of "Wealth Creation and Wealth Management Principles of Chanakya" on 27th March for the benefit of individuals in Singapore who may be keen to interact with him about Chanakya.
