    Originally posted by technoboy:
    And where do I take the shuttle bus from NUS to Harbour Front?
    You have to go PGP first, then walk to the bus stop to take that shuttle, so a bit no point, unless you lodge in PGP.

    (There is a 'shortcut' to take the shuttle, but you must be very lucky. You can take the Science Park - NUS shuttle (the minibus), get off at Science Park II, then take the shuttle (which comes at fixed timing) to Harbour Front.)

    From NUS, there are a few ways to travel to Harbour Front.

    1. 97 at the AYE bus stop.
    2. 10 at Kent Ridge Terminal
    3. 10/30/143/188 at Heng Mui Keng Bus Stop outside Biz.
