  • UltimaOnline

    I give tuition for 'A' level (H2) Chem and 'A' level (H2) Biology.

    I can say this - for Chem H2 syllabus is almost the same as 'A' level. (certain previously 'A' level optional topics eg. Gibbs free energy, is now part of the Core, there are no optional topics in H2)

    But for Biology, H2 syllabus is totally different from 'A' level syllabus. 50% of the content is totally new (ie. brought down from University level, stuff you only learnt in the University previously). Specifically, the Molecular Biology and BioTechnology aspects.

    I personally much prefer the new H2 syllabuses. Much more interesting. (H2 level's Restriction Fragment Length Polymorphisms vs 'A' level's Sex Life of Plants).
