    I didn't find it nice at first too, coz I was expecting R&B, and he gave me lotsa rock instead. After several listens, manz, the album really ROCKS me upside down!

    No idea David Tao can rock so damn well, and his R&B still maintains his usual standards. Most of the songs are great, I quite like the remake of Yue Liang Dai Biao Shui De Xin, reminds me of the stuff he did for Ye Lai Xiang in the previous album. His music has more depth now, no longer using pure R&B to appeal.

    Gong Bao Ji Ding is one helluva funny song, so is My Anata. Melody is for his ex-gf of the same name. He initially wanted to dedicate the song to her when they were together, but ironically he only finished the song after they broke up.

    Wait till I give it a few more listens, think I'll like it even more. Sorry Jay, you gotta wait a while more in my CD rack before I get back to you again in a few weeks' time. Shin Yue Tuan, you too...So sorry!
