  • ditzy
    10 Nov 04, 17:34
    First thing you need to train is your coordination. If you can coordinate your arm movement with that of your legs, half the battle is won. To jump far, your arms have to be in front of you once you lift off, this is to provide the extra momentum that will get you forward.

    Next is your calve muscles, find a staircase, then tiptoe on the edge and try to push yourself up using only your toes. Work those calves, strain them, make them work.
