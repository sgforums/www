    Steps to take:

    Be gracious with her character (esp if she's selfish)
    - Always be nice to her, and don't pass comments which she doesn't like. Try to give in to her demands, and whenever you go out, ASK her where she likes to go. Avoid making her unpleasant with your actions.

    Be gracious with your wallet.
    - Pay for everything in advance, but never don't ever refuse to take her money if she insists on paying (You'll be the fool.)
    - Shower her with presents on her birthdays, christmas. Some girls love flowers but not all, and roses do make dinners romantic.

    Be gracious with her friends.
    - Do not attempt to show that you like to be alone with her for the first few times, they are usually shy. Try being friendly with her friends but never get too close to them.

    Be gracious with her family.
    - This depends on individuals, if you managed this stage, always try to show respect to her parents.

    Last thing - girls really like to have everything done smoothly. So if you ask her on a movie, make sure you get the tickets before she arrives. If you drive, make it a habit to drive her around, she'll feel more comfortable if she's going out with you and cross your fingers and wish your vehicle won't breakdown, especially in expressway.
