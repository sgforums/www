    YAM PASTE MOONCAKE WITH CRISPY SKIN
    Crown Prince Hotel

    When we consumed it (on the same day of purchase), it was still warm!

    The skin was crispy, but not light enough.

    However, I liked the yam paste filling because it wasn't so sweet that it was sickening. It was a light-sweet taste.

    The next day, it wasn't as crispy anymore. In fact, the inner layers of the skin became kinda soft. Box says to enjoy the best of the mooncake, bake it lightly in an oven for about 6-8 minutes.

    Rating:

    SNOWSKIN, SINGLE YOLK with WHITE LOTUS PASTE
    Ritz Carlton Hotel, Summer Pavilion

    The skin was thin and chewy, unlike snowskin mooncakes of some shops which have thick and spongy skin. But I think I'll like it better if the skin was slightly thicker.

    Lotus paste was not too sweet (again, I like!). It was sticky and very fine.

    Ahh.. I don't know how to describe.

    Heavenly!
