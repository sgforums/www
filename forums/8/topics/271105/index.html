  • ouchtthurt
    31 Jul 07, 17:07
    Aiya mian kia lah. I remebered me goin to that place like so many times. But that was long ago. Haha. Those stupid secondary school days.

    In retrospect I can give you a summary.

    Weather: Rains alot. Most of the time alot of activities have to be cancelled because of rain.

    Living quarters: Double storied bunk style. Think the upper storey is made of wood. Then got cracks which allow you to see below. I think also got gate. Then below can hang clothing. That time got one of my friend jump around, then ganna snagged by the clothes line. Need stitching on the nose.

    Toilet: Considered darned nice already. Very clean actually.

    Water activities: Mainly kayaking. The kayaks there are relatively unstreamed-lined, and its quite hard to paddle. The water surrounding bathera can be quite choppy, but still managable. There are houses there, kelong with dogs. The dogs can be quite defensive if you get too close. The water sometimes got dead fish. Last time, I also remebered got dolphin and flying fish. Dunno whether now still have. If you set off during high tide still ok, but if you set off during low tide. The entire slope area is like swamp like that. Dam farked up.

    Land activities: Rock wall, high-rope confidence course, low rope course(very very very muddy, its just beside the rappelling tower cum flying fox, so dont fall down)

    General environment: The place is not at all scary. Though there are some banana trees there, and pls dun shine at them. There was an incident last time...

    The canteen serves really good food, and sometimes the canteen got bats flying around, so beware. Camp Christine is the Girl Guides Camp I think. Its just beside Bahtera. Then the Sarimbun Scouts camp is there too. Dunno whether its still there not. Haha.

    Bring repellent, cause there are mosquitos in the night. It can be quite cold too. The night is dam fun. Go for solo walk if you can. If you can venture to the area behind the multi-purpose hall. There are stairs that will bring you to a lecture room of some sort. Go look in the night, its forbidden, but everytime look inside like got some green light.

    Dun ever ever run when it has just rained. Especially the bunk area.

    Oh yah, if you want you can take a stroll down to the 3SIR's FIBUA training facility. It looks like a ghost town.

    Anyway, I like to ask, why would you be goin tare tml? Tml school holiday liao ah?

    Hope my information was comprehensive enough. If you got questions just ask. I will try to recall.
