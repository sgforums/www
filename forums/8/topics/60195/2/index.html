    Ribonucleotide reductase reduce Ribonucleotide diphosphates to Deoxyribonucleotide diphosphates.

    Catabolic end product of Purine is Urate. Accumalation of Urate(Uric acid) due to impaired excretion, accumalation due of Phosphoribosyl pyrophosphate(PRPP) as a result of HGPRT deficiency, Glucose-6-Phosphatase deficiency as in the case of Von Gierke's disease all result in PRPP accumalation.

    Uric acid production increased in tremendous concentration and solidify as Sodium Urate (Tophi) crystals at the joints and in some cases Kidneys. This is known as Gout.Inflammation of joints caused severe pain and discomfort to Gout sufferer.

    Allopurinol is used to treat Gout as it is an analogue to Hypoxanthine and when catalysed by Xanthine oxidae converted to harmless Oxypurinol.
