    Employee 1: A driver who is an expert  and delivers his goods with accurate precision but has zero knowledge of other work in the company

    Employee 2: A milder scaled down version of employee 1 but not as proficient, slower but knows more work relating to the company. he may not be as proficient as employee 1

    I would rather have employee 2 because he/she knows the bigger picture of the business. When employee 1 is not around, i can still use employee 2 to keep the business running though not in top form. But when employee 2 is not around, i cant depend on employee 1.
