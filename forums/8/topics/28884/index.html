    I'm using 7210 nokia hp currently.

    Its features & looks are totally different from those existing hps.
    Many reviewers said the press buttons are wide apart & will be difficult to use.
    However, I'm quite comfortable using the unique key pad, no trouble at all.

    The bad feature is once the screensaver is activated with the light off (to reduce battery enegry) you can't even see the colours of the wallpaper & also whatever on the screen unless u are standing under the sunlight or if you press any key button.

    The downloads using WAP is fun but can be costly.
    Haven't tried the MMS yet cos no point using at the moment.

    Furthermore, it lacks cam feature. If you can do without cam feature, then buy 7210, otherwise wait for the new nokia hps with cam feature to be introduced next yr Q1.

    I'm still quite satisfactory with 7210 though it has a few bad points.
