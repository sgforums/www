    You can try diamond push ups while positioning/resting your legs on a sofa [somewhere soft so that your feet will feel comfortable]. Then straighten ur legs and positioning ur body at a 30 to 45 degree angle. Do at least 30 times. If can do 3 sets with each recovery timing around 2-3 minutes maximum. That's all.

  • gentlerock
    29 May 05, 18:38
    Originally posted by digicharat:
    what about abdominal muscle?
    how to train and get rid of the little fats around it?
    You will need to.....

    (1) Do lots of cardio, which is the only way to healthily get rid of fats in your body, so that they don't cover up your abs muscles. Different kind of cardio is effective for different kind of bodies, but running is basically generally the best. This has to be coupled with careful food intake (no point if you're drinking beer or eating lard).

    (2) Do strength exercise for the tummy (crunches, sit-ups, machines, etc). These will enlarge your ab muscles so that they show more clearly.

    Some things you will need to note are:

    (1) There is no such thing as building or slimming only a certain part of the body. Everything in your body is complementary and will often have to be changed together, more or less.

    (2) There are some people (not many) who have great difficulty achieving body muscular definition due to genetics.
