    cross breed between hamsters or breed hybrids is dangerous and harmful to the hamsters, especially roboroviskii is so small and sapphire winter white is so big. The sexual organ of robos is suitable for giving birth the same breed, and if the pup happens to take after the sapphire winter white, the robo mum would have problems giving birth, resulting in hard labour, which might most probable cause death. Even if there is successful birth, the offsprings are of high mortality rate and highly infertile. If you have doubts of wad I say, perhaps can find out more from other hamster or pet websites. Since you keep hamsters, suppose you love them and had their interest at heart
    As for the hamster that killed people, its not a hamster. The reporter got the facts wrong. Its actually a lemming which do resembles a winter white. The victim does not die of any virus, but due to allergy which is very rare case.
