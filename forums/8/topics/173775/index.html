    assume 'z' is total saving in his bank account,
    z = z(3/5) + z(2/5) ----- rite?

    "After spending 1/3 of it, he had $60 left"
    ---SO, u can play around with that $60, and also the 3/5 of the total saving

    ...
    $60 = [z(3/5)] - [ 1/3 x z(3/5) ]
    $60 = [z(3/5)] - [ z(3/15) ]
    $60 = z (3/5 - 3/15)
    $60 = z (9/15 - 3/15)
    $60 = z(6/15)
    z = $60 x 15/6
    z = $150
