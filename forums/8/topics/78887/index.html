The New Cow Theory

  • Hydro
    10 May 04, 11:10
    After being dumped repeatedly by men, Jane Goodall comes up with the New Cow-Old Cow theory: New Cow is short for New-Cow Theory; which is short for Old Cow-New Cow Theory, which, of course, is short for the sad, sorry truth that men leave women and never come back because all they really want is New Cow.

    It's basically an all-men-are-bastards book with plenty of examples of men dumping women and all the excuses they come up with when dumping women. Again, it's not a story I can relate to myself but I am sure, for anyone who has been dumped and then especially for another woman, this must be a very therapeutic book to read.

    It's very light reading and perfect when on a plane (like I was for half of this book) or in any other surrounding when absolute concentration is not required. However, I didn't find this book all that good (not that I expected to) - and it's not a book I'd recommend reading unless you need something so light you might just as well have watched TV instead.
