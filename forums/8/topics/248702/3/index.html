    Just be realistic with everything you do. Getting married is not all about the wedding only, it's a love union between you and your partner. Because you see others have a glamorous wedding, you don't necessarily have to have a wedding like them.

    Why stress yourself to save money for an expensive wedding (1 day event) instead of trying to save $$ to provide your partner a comfortable life after you're married?

    Money is one of the causes of marriage breakdown. So if you really love each other, focus on how both of you can maintain a healthy and happy marriage rather than the wedding itself.

    Just do what you can afford.
