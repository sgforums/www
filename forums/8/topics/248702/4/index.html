    if really must have dinner, then maybe have in a restuarant - $400/ table onwards. 4-5 star hotel dinner min. $800/table onwards

    pingjin - gotta check with yr future in-laws, can be from a few hundreds to a few thousands.

    cakes & angpows for helpers (jiemei, xiongdi etc) - $1000

    photographer for actual day - $600 to $1200

    videographer for actual day - $1000

    suits & gowns package is from $1600 onwards, designer ones at least from $4000 onwards.

    honeymoon can settle for neighbouring countries like Langkawi, Bintan, Koh Samui, Bali or Phuket/ Krabi etc. those places are good for honeymoon.

    HDB flat - downpayment can paid by yr CPF. It's the reno cost & furnishing that kills.. $5000 onwards (for premium flats), $20000 onwards ( for standard flats).
