    if you are thinking of opening a USD Account in Singapore banks just for Paypal.. forget it.

    Because Paypal will pay you according to the country you are in.

    So when you want them to remit the $$ to Singapore, they will always convert to SGD first before remitting.

    USD accounts in Singapore have the following restrictions (typical)

    1. minimum balance of US$1000
    2. charge per transactions made (withdrawal or deposit, 0.2% at least minimum $10)
    3. Receipt of TT is usually $10 deduction.
    4. inter-account transfer $5 or a commission.
