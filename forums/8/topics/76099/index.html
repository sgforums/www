  • Larry

    There are basically 2 types of blades to look at:

    1) Hard blades
    - Hard plastic casing on the exterior
    2) Soft blades
    - Softer canvas material

    If u are looking for comfort and more into leisure blading often, then u might wanna consider a pair of soft blades.

    K2 blades from Skateline is quite good. I own a pair of Citrus (already out of stock) and they are soft blades. Very comfortable and provides a good speed. I paid about $400 a yr back for them. If u are looking at about a $200 budget, u can try checking out Soloman's blades. The website FI has provided does import Soloman blades as well.

    Enjoy!!
