    Your resume should consist of your cover letter and your resumes.I suggest u go to Books Kino to browse through some examples regarding the format of the resume itself. They got several books which explains how a resume should be typed out and phrased. Do spend some time at the bookshop or library to get a gd feeling how to write your resume.If funds permit it, its gd to purchase 1 book on resume writing and other on gd phrases to use.

    Alternately if u have any good frens working in HR, you could ask them for some resumes to reference.

    Try to use clear concise words, more powerful words like "led my project team", "expertise and demostrated skills in ...." Don't go and type in long phrases or sentences. Short and sweet is the key.

    There are several types of resumes format, chornological,functional,combined etc

    If your are fresh graduate, starting with the combined or functional format will be suitable as the chornological will not suit u unless u had any form of prior work experience( unless you did work before in small companies doing sales or proper work)

    DON"T ever go and write a cover letter which can be used for ALL sorts of job positions you are applying. The HR hates it and it speaks volumes of your insincerity and lack of effort.

    first there are short one page ones and there are multi-page ones like a booklet, but that'll be more like a CV i suppose,

    here's what you need:

    personal particulars (standard stuff lah ... name, IC, address, contact etc)

    followed by education, the institutions you've attended and the qualifications you got out of it. start from highest attained to lowest, can stop at O levels, no body will be interested in your PSLE lah .. put them in a column with date from when to when on the right side

    next will be job experience, same format, position followed by a one sentence description of your job scope

    and a section on skill sets

    e.g. programming languages
    java (including JSP)
    C/C++
    prolog
    haskell
    perl

    blah blah blah .......

    lastly, name, position and contact of your referees, if any

    that's roughly it ! ... not really a fix format, but here's what they'll expect ....

    hope this helps
