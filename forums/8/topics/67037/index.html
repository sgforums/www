Somehow the two medicine "clashed" and I started to feel very giddy and high. It's a very scary feeling. I quickly lie down on my bed. Then, I saw some "people" kneeling around my bed. They were either talking or praying, very loudly. They all looked quite scary (all very pale looking and dress in white).
