    Causes:

    1) Your motherboard may have leaking or swollen capacitors
    Solution = change mboard, cpu and ram

    2) Your Power Supply Unit may be failing
    Solution = replace the PSU

    3) Your sound card or the PCI slot which sound card is in is faulty
    Solution = replace sound card or mboard

    4) Rarely but can happen, your RAM may be failing or faulty
    Solution = replace all RAM
