    Title : Man injured in fight at Lucky Plaza, police looking for suspect
    By : Chio Su-Mei, Channel NewsAsia
    Date : 10 Nov 2007 0152 hrs (GMT + 8hrs)

    SINGAPORE : A man sustained injuries on his face and arm following a fight in Lucky Plaza on Friday afternoon.
