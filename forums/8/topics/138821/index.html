Major Bus Services Diversion in the City today (25 June)

  • tranquilice
    25 Jun 05, 08:44
    To facilitate NDP rehearsals, there will be major bus services diversion in the Suntec City/Marina Centre, Padang and Singapore Indoor Stadium areas today (25 June) as well as the following days:

    Saturday 02 July
    Saturday 09 July
    Saturday 16 July
    Saturday 23 July
    Saturday 30 July
    Tuesday 09 August

    The bus services diversion will take place for 21 hours, from 2am to 11pm.

    The 22 SBS Transit services affected are 10, 11, 14, 16, 36, 56, 70, 97, 100, 107, 111, 130, 131, 133, 162, 195, 196, Express 502, Express 518, CityBuzz C1, CityBuzz C2 and CityBuzz C3.

    SBS Transit services route diversion details http://www.sbstransit.com.sg/whatsnew_24June2005-1.asp

    SBS Transit services route diversion map http://www.sbstransit.com.sg/whatsnew_24June2005L.asp

    The 10 SMRT services affected are 75, 77, 106, 167, 171, CSS 608, 700/700A, 857, 960 and 961.

    Take note that services 75, 77, 106, 171, 700A, 857 and 960 will not go to Suntec City/Marina Centre from 1pm to 11pm.

    SMRT services route diversion details http://www.smrtbuses.com.sg/misc/new/NDP2005-General%20poster.doc

    SMRT services route diversion map 1 and map 2 http://www.smrtbuses.com.sg/misc/new/MAPS-NDP2005-BUSDIVERSION.ppt
