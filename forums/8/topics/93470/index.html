    For more secure long term future, best to take University if you can. Having a degree is a great advantage compared to diploma. However, it may really depends on what you want to do eventually. I can say the following should be generally true (not always, just generally)

    (1) University average pay over a life time can be significent higher than diploma holder (Note: avergae - exact pay depends on individual capabilities and fate)

    (2) Diploma holders work tends to be more hands on, whereas degree holders work tends to be more administrative (E.g. Project management is more administrative than hands-on)

    (3) Public sector, a huge employer in Singapore tends to emphasis a lot in education qualification. If you ever intend to work there, a degree is almost a must to hold any important post or a high pay. Private sector can be more flexible but it really depends on individual employer.

    (4) No OT pay for degree holders usually.
