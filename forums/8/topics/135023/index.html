  • mochou
    1 Jun 05, 19:05
    Method A:
    - put mee in boiling water and cook until how soft(i dun like too soft) i want it to be.
    - pour all the hot water away and fill cold tap water(this will wash away watever chemical cover on the uncooked mee and make the mee more elastic)
    - put the mee in a bowl(drained all unwanted water away)
    - cook the soup with the flavour and add egg or other stuff if wanted.
    - pour the boiled soup inside and EAT.

    Method B(fried noodle):
    - put mee in boiling water and cook until how soft(i dun like too soft) i want it to be.
    - pour all the hot water away and fill cold tap water(this will wash away watever chemical cover on the uncooked mee and make the mee more elastic)
    - put the mee in a bowl(drained all unwanted water away)
    - put the flavour(powder,half pack should be enuff)into small amount of water and stir.(use it later)
    - heat up the pan with some oil and then add the flavour(oil) with it.
    - throw the mee in and fry, watch "out for shooting oil" cos the mee got water.
    - pour in the water mixed with flavour powder and keep frying
    - add egg to fry together if wanted.
