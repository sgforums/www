    when got time...

    1) rinse the straw mushrooms and bok choy,
    2) cut the boy choy and slice the sausage.
    3) chop onion and fry it with oil, put aside
    4) use remaining oil fry the egg and sliced sausage.
    5) half boil about two and half cup of water
    6) throw in noodles
    7) stop the fire after while
    8 ) drain the water away
    9) half boil another two and the half cup of water
    10) put in seasoning powder and seasoning oil. and stir
    11) put in noodles when water's boiling hot
    12) when the noodles is about 3/4 done, throw in the boy choy, straw mushroom and vege garnish
    13) when noodles done, pour into a bowl
    14) Arrange the sausage and egg on top on of the noodles.
    15) garnish fried onion on top.

    Method A...

    1. boil water
    2. water boiling bubbly, throw in choy sam or any veges
    3. veges done.. remove
    4. throw in noodle and boil
    5. remove noodle and put in bowl.
    6. seasoning i use my own version of chopped spring onion, sesame oil, soya sauce and then mix the noodle then add the veges... ahhaha

    Method B...

    1. boil water to cook noodles.
    2. open a can of campbell cream of mushroom and boil it then add in the noodles.. yummmy...

    Method C...

    1. boil water to cook noodles..
    2. drain noodles and put into a bowl
    3. add fresh kim chi and mix (for dry kimchi noodle) or add boiling water for soup kim chi noodle...

    Method D...

    plenty of time.. then will add seaweed, veges, mushroom.
