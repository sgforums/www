    Yes you can.

    You just need to pray hard that they are not doing spot checks on that day and you will be fine.

    Oh, and if there are a number of people 'tenting' there, join them. It is good to be caught together than to be caught alone, because you could then use the excuse that you didn't know you needed a permit to camp there as many people are also camping there.

    On legal issues, if you can't book a certain area for tent pitching, that means you are not allowed to set up a tent there.
