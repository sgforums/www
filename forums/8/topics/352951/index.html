    This is serious and no laughing matter. I know of people who claims to have imaginery friends. The sad thing is that they are often ridiculed and denigrated.

    I believe that imaginary friends are grown out of loneliness by people who create an ideal of a person or role model that he/she can find solace and comfort in. These people faced problems at home or at work and have problem interacting with real people-could be a number of issues like family divorces, bullying, etc.

    They must not be avoided. They are not mad. They are as human as you and I are. Only friendships and patience can draw them out, for in their mind, these friends are real and will either turn to angels who may help if he/she had real friends or proper directions, or devils who may harm when he/she grows up bullied or perceived slights.

    It hurts so much to see a child of only 10 talking to an imaginary friend, and being bullied by real peers as a fruitcake. It will only drive them deeper into a delusive world. He/she, is only one of us, a flawed human, with similar hopes and dreams......Please help him/he, not make fun....
