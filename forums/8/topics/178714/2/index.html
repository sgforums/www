    Why do you live? You live because your heart beats within you, your blood is flowing, and there is enough oxygen in the air around you to keep that system going. You live because you are lucky to have enough food to eat, enough water to drink, a shelter over your head to keep the elements away from harming you. You live because you do not live in a war torn zone, no mines/no bullets/no enemies waiting in fear to kill you anyway cos if they don't they are dead. You live because your basic needs for living exists.

    And because it exists (because for whom people that these basic needs are a struggle, the question THEY ask is "how do i continue to live?"), you will ask this question WHY you live? And often, they can find their answers in... WHO you live for, What you live for, and HOW you live.

    And for many of THESE people, when their answers to who, what and how have been found unreliable, they tend to fallback to the Why again. cos its the safest rhetoric question around.

    Who do you live for? I live for my family, my loved ones, my cats that I need to take care of, the kids that I work with

    What do you live for? Simple things in life ba, fish and chips takeaway on my dining table right now for a cosy comfortable dinner, trips to beautiful and not so beautiful places that amazes me how big and different the world is... and how small when people's hearts touch each other

    How I live? As fully as I can... and that means, lots of good food!
