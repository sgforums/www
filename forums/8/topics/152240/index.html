  • casshern
    11 Sep 05, 21:05
    this morning at about 2.30am i went in to JB with one of my friend to pump petrol / learn more about JB. tot of learning the place better so next time can go in myself to pump/supper because i have never drive in before

    everything is SOP, pump petrol, wash car, drink milo.

    on the way back we are supposed to get an Exit stamp at the JB customs side. when it was my turn, the cabin was dark and smoky and i was like "huh? how to stay in there and chop passport?"

    so i happily took my passport and went zooming across the causeway so i can get some sleep back home. upon reaching SG custom side, the customs officer in the brightly lited well vendilated cabin flip thru my passport and ask me " why u dun haf the exit stamp? "

    me: WTF??! why dun haf? okay... nvm what can i do?
    officer: u can continue back home but u would face problems the next time you return to JB, so i advice you to do a u-turn and go back for the exit stamp
    me: huh... wah lan heh... sian ok den i think i go back to avoid trouble
    officer: okay den i will scan ur passport to affirm your entry back into SG
    me: okay thank you very much ah!

    so i made a u- turn down the road and went back into JB ALONE! my friend was already zooming home on the BKE. At the SG customs i told them what happened and the SG custom officer at the entry side was giving the "WTF they trying to do" kind of look when she flip thru my passport. so ask me to carry on and i don't have to pay the toll charges with my cashcard.

    i went on to the JB side and again i told the custom officer what happened. she gave me the thumbs up to carry on to get what i needed and i went down to the lane for the JB toll. and this is what happened

    me: hello bang, me need to do a turn to get the exit stamp, your customs side forgot to give me the stamp
    CBK: 2.60 ringgit
    me: huh? why i need to pay again?
    CBK: 2.60 ringgit (give dulan look)
    me: but i only need to do a turn to get exit stamp
    CBK: ya, 2.60 (voice turn hostile)
    me: can you please explain why do i still have to pay when your side failed to do their duty?
    CBK: THAT'S YOUR PROBLEM LAR! 2.60 RINGGIT!
    me: why shd i pay?! i paid just now coming in and now i have to waste my time and money to come back cos your customs din do their job?!
    CBK: OIE! 2.60 ONLY YOU CANNOT PAY IS IT??!?!
    me: the point is not about 2.60 but i want an explaination why i have to pay when you fellas failed to do your job??
    CBK: OKAY NVM YOU GO YOU GO !! (took out a board and want to take down my vehicle number)
    me: hey look, i demand an explaination on your behaviour
    CBK: (keep quiet)

    den the cars behind started horning so i suck thumb haf to cross over. so i did a turn and made sure i got my exit stamp this time rd. being a first timer i lineed up in the "coaches/cars" line when i could go take the left most motor cycle lane and filter off to the car lane. so i sibei suay kana stuck in the bus lane for 20mins before i filter out to the car lane.

    mood turned worse by the min and wasn't made better by the jam...

    my question is now that they haf my vehicle number taken down, what would actually happened?
