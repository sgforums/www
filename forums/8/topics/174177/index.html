    1) add another layer on top of the picture1 that you want to place the face on.
    2) from there other picture 2- you can choose to use clone or select cut and paste to pic1.
    3) go back to the pic one layer of the head, you need to blow up in to pixel to remove all the edge of the head nicely. Then, u need to create some shadows. Next is to adjust the head skin tone & lighting to match the body and neck skin tone. As you most properly still have to recreate the effect of the lighting and how shadow fall on the face to match the lighting angle and shadow.
    4) finally merge the 2 layer into 1. Then use the smurge tool to merge the neck area better, do some casusian blur and sharpen it again.
