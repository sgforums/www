    I think the card better than Amex plat is the Amex titatnium card. Ive seen it before in the newspapers and its prolly the one with unlimited credit. And it is made of titanium. Anyway I got a couple of cards and theyre all for different purposes.

    I mean each card has SO many priviledges that you absolutely cannot remember everything. Thats why when you wanna buy stuff, you go shop around everywhere and anywhere and see which shops accept which cards, and they will put up signs displaying the cards they accept. Its best to have a few different cards so that you can gain the most benefits when you shop.

    UOB Platinum - Its now called UOB Preferred Platinum card. I got the original plat card a while ago and just lazy to change it. Anyway UOB offers a huge list of dining priviledges, you get 11% discount at Cathay cinplex and also discounts at a wide range of clothing and accessories store. It also covers travel insurance if you pay for it using the card.

    ABN AMRO  Platinum - This card belongs to a Dutch bank. It doesnt have as much priviledges as the others (it has the usual dining priviledges at some places but quite limited), but it does have one unique priviledge. With this card, you are enittled to the Comfort Cab priority queue and you get to call a priority line when you need a cab. The cabs come fast and you do not need to pay the service charge for calling. 

    Citibank Platinum - This card is probably the other "must have" card besides UOB platinum. It offers a huge range of priviledges as well for many dining and shopping places. Travel insurance is also covered. You get to sign up for a service that helps cancel all your cards if they ever get stolen. I guess the other cards should have such a service also. Citibank platinum allows you to cut queue at Golden Village because they have a priority queue for you. No discounts though. Just very good for buying movie tickets on super crowded weekends.

    Amex Plat - I think this is my favourite card. Its not the most widely accepted but i think it offers the best deals. It comes with a Feed at Raffles card which offers superb deals when you dine at the Raffles Hotel and Swissotel. Membership to Feed at Raffles costs over 600 SGD if you sign up externally. Amex plat gives it to you for free. This card allows you to cut queue at Cineleisure and The Cathay using the priority queue, and gives you the discount that UOB card offers. This card gives you priority entry to MOS. It probably has the best travelling insurance. Many shops offer 5X or 10X rewards points, and these rewards points are for life. No expiry unlike the rest. And it looks the best among all the cards abovementioned.

    Its better to check out the websites to get details on which cards are better for you. Generally its good to have many cards as long as you can control your spending and pay your bills every month when the statements arrive. Its the best way to reap rewards when you gotta spend money.
