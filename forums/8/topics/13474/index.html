What the hell is vanilla?

  • pulse
    5 Feb 02, 11:38
    I hate vanilla. There, I admit it, I freely and passionately confess it. I hate vanilla. I mean, what the hell is vanilla? Until a few days ago, I was under the full but crazy impression that vanilla plants grew on the planet Mars and little green men in tuxedoes harvested them. Mind this though, I am still not fully convinced that vanilla is really made by the vigorous stirring of ordinary milk powder. There is nothing ordinary about vanilla!!!

    Take chocolate and strawberry for example. Both are nice, courteous flavours. Simple and straightforward with absolutely no airs. Unlike that great pretender, of course, VANILLA!!! I am convinced that people only resort to vanilla if an ice-cream parlour has inadvertently ran out of sultry chocolate and delectable strawberry. No one in their right minds would make vanilla their first choice. No one has vanilla as their favourite flavour.

    I am not saying that such people do not exist. There are always a few oddballs out there. It is what makes our little world so cute and interesting. If you do know some of these aliens, however, someone who goes up to a Starbucks counter and cheerfully announce, "A cup of hot vanilla, please.", I want you to ask that person this question.

    "What the heck is Vanilla?"

    Eight out of ten of these aliens will simply stare blankly at you. The moral of the story being, NO ONE KNOWS THE ANSWER!!!

    If the person still proceeds with the order, then it is high time for you to wander into the sunset and search for another friend who has less suicidal tendencies...
