    A standard barcode has two parts.

    One part is the lines we usually noticed. It's nearly impossible to decode using the human eye.

    The other part is denoted in numbers, it is at the bottom of the lines. The numbers desribes the product, e.g. manufacturer code and product code. The cash registers are programmed with a database of these numbers and its associated price.

    Unless you are familiar with these numbers and its actual product, it is also not possible to determine the price just by reading the numbers.
