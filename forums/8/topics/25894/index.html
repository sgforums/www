    Newater makes 4D crowd happy

    NEWATER was the toast of punters last weekend after it inspired them to pick the first-prize number in Saturday's 4-D draw - 6600.

    Eagle-eyed Singaporeans, whose love for a flutter on any new development is legendary, spotted the number on the 350 ml bottles of reclaimed water.

    It forms part of the toll-free hotline number - 1800-284-6600 - for the Public Utilities Board, producer of Newater.

    Never mind that it is in tiny print, just 1 mm tall, diehard punters found it.

    A $1 bet would have reaped $2,000 or $3,000, depending on whether a Big or Small bet was placed.

    Singapore Pools would not say how many bets were placed.

    But at least one group in Bukit Timah constituency was celebrating its windfall at a community dinner there.

    Housewife Irene Lim, 52, said: 'My neighbours were all talking about it.

    'They told me a lot of them had struck the first prize because of Newater.'

    They were inspired because the Cantonese phrase yao soei, which means 'have water', also means 'have money'.

    'Who would have noticed such tiny numbers?

    'They must have really good eyes,' she said.
