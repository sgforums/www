    How to get your tickets

    APPLICATION (June 4-13)
    SMS

    For tickets to the NDP: Key in NDP for the parade or PREVIEW for the NDP preview, followed by a and your identity card number, including the letters in it, or if the person is born after 2000, the letter T and his birth certificate number, followed by a .
    Key in SC if Singaporean; PR if permanent resident.
    Send the text message to 39892 to apply for two tickets; 39894 for four tickets; or 39896 for six tickets.Each SMS costs 20 cents.
    Telephone

    For the preview, call 1900-112-1391
    For the NDP, call 1900-112-1392Follow the instructions. Each call costs 20 cents.
    Online, SAM and AXS machines

    Visit the NDP website at www.ndp.org.sg
    Submit a form at any AXS or SAM machine.
    RESULT (June 24-30)

    Successful applicants will be contacted at the number they have given.
    COLLECTION (July 2-11)

    At New Millenia Walk, Level 2, between 9am and 9pm daily.
    You must show your identity card, birth certificate, passport or driver's licence.
    To collect on someone's behalf, take along his identity card, birth certificate, passport or driver's licence.
    Photocopies will not be accepted.
    For general enquiries, call 1800-NDP-2004 (1800-637-2004)
