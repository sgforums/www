    LCCI Diploma in Accounting and ACCA are two different standard

    qualifications. One is a diploma and the other is a professional accounting

    qualification.

    Passing rate for LCCI Diploma in Accounting is much higher than that of ACCA.

    The passing rate of ACCA is very low ie 10% - 20%.

    Many students cannot pass through level 2 after many tries and then they give up.

    However, once the students passed all the ACCA exams, they are highly sought

    after by employers and many of them hold high accounting and finance positions in

    the companies, MNC, banks and so on.
