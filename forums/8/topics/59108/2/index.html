  • liyan
    12 Oct 03, 00:11
    when i was doing my final year project and nothing went right from the beginning. at first things were tolerable, then slowly approaching the last 4 weeks of the the 15weeks duration of the project, i started to be very upset. the project was leading to nowhere...

    i tried my best, it was not good, but it was the best i could do. but nothing was accepted by the lecturers. i propose my ideas, they threw them out.

    whenever i'm alone, i started crying. then laughing. then i start to stare into space, begin to talk to myself. i dunno if i would be in time if i start to redo everything, i dunno if i should try to save the proj, or start all over again with around 4 weeks time left. then i cried again and again.

    no one could help. i could not approach anyone. most of my friends start to shun me. i dunno why then. a few friends keep trying to talk to me though. i only talked to one of them. i was lucky she was with me throughout.

    proposals still got threw out. the 4 lecturers all have different opinion. i have no idea who could help me correctly. when deadlines neared, i started to shiver whenever i sit in front of the computer. i could not sit still and i keep squirming in my computer chair.

    a few friends never allow me to be alone. i went to sit by bedok reservoir and each of them want to come with me. when i sit to eat alone, people keep coming by to want to sit down. i keep telling them "if you dun mind, please sit at another table". if they refuse, i get up and walk away.

    sigh...really sorry to all of them.

    when my proj was finally submitted, i could not recall how i lasted through the last few days. but remembered that i was crying like mad. i must have cried 8, 10 times a day.

    and if u ever have a friend who seems to be really depressed, dun assume it will work if you ask them to "snap out of it". being depressed to the extremes is an illness. if someone is having a fever, you dun go to them to ask them to return their temperature to normal right? because that cannot be done.
