    Clear cookies, history and cache.

    For IE:

    Tools > Internet Options

    Click Delete Cookies
    Click Delete Files. Tick Delete all offline contents box. Click OK.
    Click Clear History
    Click OK.
    Close IE. Open it again.

    For Firefox:

    Press Ctrl + Shift + Del

    For Opera:

    Tools > Delete private data

    For Netscape:

    Tools > Options > Privacy
    Click Clear All
    Click OK

    For Avant:

    Tools > Clear Records > Clear All Records

    For Maxthon:

    Tools > Clean System > Clean All

    For lazy people, use this: ATF Cleaner OR
