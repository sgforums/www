    SINGAPORE : A 40-year-old man has been found dead at the foot of a block of flats in Tampines and police later found the bodies of a woman and two children in the 12th storey flat where he was living.

    They are believed to be his wife and children.

    Callers to the MediaCorp News hotline said the body of the man was found lying face up at about 8 am on Monday at the foot of Block 470, Tampines Street 44.

    He was wearing a long sleeved beige shirt, a red neck tie and blue pants.

    Police later established that he lived on the 12th floor.

    When they entered the premises at around noon, they found the bodies of a woman aged about 39 years, a boy aged about 12, and a girl of about four.

    They were lying on a bed next to one another.

    They were all dressed as though they were about to go out.

    Channel NewsAsia understands handwritten notes were found in the house.

    Police are investigating. - CNA
