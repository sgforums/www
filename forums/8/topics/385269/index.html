    Well if you are looking for a classic, undeniably great strategy game based in 17th century Europe, look no further. Uncharted Water 2, developed by KOEI, was made in 1995 and is a true tribute to the enjoyable, easy games of the mid 90s era.

    Play as one of several characters and increase you fame and seamanship, along with several other attributes and manage a huge crew. Get more money and better ships, be a merchant and trade with ports for profits or be a pirate and make your money by stealing your money.

    Visit the hundreds of world-wide ports and upgrade features of your ship while managing crew size and food supplies.
    Quite simply this game has it all and is clearly one of the best KOEI entertainment-strategy pc games. This game will keep you interested for plenty of time and can be play over and over again. Save games at anytime so you’ll never be tied down by the game so long as you can manage your time well. Oh yeah, there is also an amazingly complex battle board where sea battles are fought. Moreover, trinkets, potions, remedies, armour and swords can be bought and sold to improve your hero. HINT:
    Visit pottery shops at 2:00 am in the morning and you’ll find that shop keepers have
         
    better merchandise to offer.

    This game was made late in the mid 90’s streak of amazing games released by KOEI and is truly one of the best. I highly recommend it to anyone looking for a rarely Europe based game found throughout KOEI games. Although there are all kinds of aspect to manage and great depth to the game, it is not overly complex and is made even better with easy to manage and control game play.

    If you like this game you may want to check out the Romance of the Three Kingdoms series and the Genghis Khan series also by KOEI, they are wicked games that’ll keep you coming back for more time and time again. If you do download this game and have never played it before, I recommend using Catalina, she gets the best boat of the start and in my mind has the most interesting life-story line; which are loosely followed for each character, not to mention she has the best, most well rounded skill set to begin with too. The English guy is good too, but his starting conditions are very difficult to deal with, I’d recommend playing as him once you’ve developed some decent skills. I hope you enjoy this great game!
