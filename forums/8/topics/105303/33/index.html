    Press Release - Current
    30 November 2004

    'The Gift of Life'
    - SMRT organises third public blood donation drive at Somerset station

    SMRT Corporation Ltd (SMRT) will be organising its third public blood donation drive tomorrow at Somerset Station from 11am to 5pm.

    This is the fifth and final blood donation drive for the year organised in support of the Singapore Red Cross blood donor recruitment programme. Earlier this year SMRT had organised two staff donation drives and two public donation drives. A total of 631 units of blood were collected.

    "We are expecting a good turnout of donors tomorrow, as it is a weekend and Somerset Station is centrally located," said Vincent Tan, Vice President Rail Operations and Chairman, SMRT Blood Donor Programme organising committee.

    "SMRT has been very committed in its efforts to promote blood donation. We hope that this will encourage more Singaporeans to come forward to give blood and help save lives," said Ms Cecilia Tan, Director (Blood Donor Recruitment Programme), Singapore Red Cross.

    SMRT hopes to collect 220 units of blood from tomorrow's event. Each successful donor will receive a 2005 calendar diary as a token of appreciation from the Singapore Red Cross.

    SMRT's Corporate Volunteer Programme

    SMRT's corporate volunteer programme gives staff an opportunity to volunteer their time and effort to help the needy and less privileged. This year, the company has adopted the Geylang East Home for the Aged (GEHTA) and welfare organisation Beyond Social Services, in addition to its support of the Singapore Red Cross.
