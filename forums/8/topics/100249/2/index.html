    the best way is to learn it through experience.

    hang out with your friends who know how to joke[or always make you laugh]. note down what topics they always focus on, and try to make up some of them yourselves.

    if you can't make them up yourselves, then just copy their jokes. i'm sure you have more groups of friends other than the usual group that you hang out with.

    if you're with guys...the best topic to joke on are 'yellow jokes'. (ie. dirty jokes) 'yellow jokes' are easy to start and easy to understand. besides, the possibilities of 'yellow jokes' are endless!

    if you're with girls...the easiest way to joke is to make fun of them. if she likes to sleep in class, call her a pig. if she likes to eat, call her a pig. if she's thin, call her a bamboo. from there you can get alot more relevant jokes on her.

    eg. if you nicknamed her, "bamboo", you can make fun of her by asking her how much she charge for hanging your clothes. when she asks why, tell her that it's her job as a bamboo pole.

    experience is the best teacher...the best way is to watch how other guys do it and learn from them. tv shows are a good place to learn too, especially the taiwan variety shows ===> with wu zhong xian/ kang kang as the hosts. they make great "yellow jokes"

    but if you want to become a joker, you have to make it part of your habit. don't just pop a joke suddenly after being silent for 45 mins, and then another one after 2 hours. she'll only think that you're siao.

    i hope that helps!

    btw..before you tell a joke, ask yourself whether it's funny or not to others. sure, it seems funny to you that a whale has a kkj, but do other people think it's funny as well?

    another tip is that when you're telling a joke, make your language simple and easy to understand for others. jokes that need a bit of thinking are not a good choice too, because most people are WOLS.

    i think an alternative you can pick is to ask people riddles or dumb questions. riddles are easy to remember and hard to guess. dumb questions are great to use and very difficult to guess.

    riddle: a part of our body has the ability to expand or contract 6 times its normal size. what is it?

    ans: the pupil of the eye, not our kkj.

    dumb question: do you know that some canibals are vegetarians?

    ans: because they eat 'vegetables' (zhi2 wu4 ren2)

    hope that helps!
