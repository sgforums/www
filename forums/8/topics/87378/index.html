    Item that is NOT recommend: Creative MuVo2 (1.5G)
    CNET Users review: 89% thumbs up, 11% thumbs down



    Bought this mp3 player about a month ago. Sux big time! The battery life is shorter than what Creative claimed. I think the battery life is around 4 hours and that is despite me turning off the backlight function.
    You will have to recharge it every 2 or 3 days, assuming you are a light user.

    2 days ago, the wire of the earpiece broke off totally! KNN...it's not as if i use it to tie a horse and pull around with.

    When you record songs thru the USB 2.0 cable, the battery gets drained really fast! You can't even transfer 50 songs without the battery dying out on you.

    MY REVIEW: Poor battery life (worst i have encountered in my whole fricking life). Small buttons for control are unsuitable for users with big hands. Inferior earpieces given.

    The good thing about this piece of trash is the size. It is smaller than most hard drive mp3 players (including ipod) and will fit in easily into pockets.

    Overall: 4/10
