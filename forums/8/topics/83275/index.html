  • Chin Eng
    22 Jun 04, 16:37

    Depends on what you want to do.
    Was there last week for a family vacation. Shopping is always good. Now with the BTS (Bangkok MRT), getting around is far easier and faster.
    Don't miss Chatuchak Market. Contrary to popular beliefs, it is NOT a weekend market. It is just that ALL the shops are opened during the weekends. Most of the shops are opened all the time. Stuffs are really cheap. Ornamental things (like wooden chopstick sets) that are sold at Takashimaya Basement 1 can be bought at a fraction of the cost.
    Accessible via BTS.
    There is always Mahboonkrong. Get off at National Stadium BTS. 8 floors of shopping and entertainment. Cheap (and nice T-shirts) available for not more than 199 baht (about $4.20) even for XXL size. Plenty of mobile phone peripherals.
    River Cruise at 75 baht (get off at Saphan Taksin), something like river taxi. The price of ticket is for a whole day indefinite number of stops at the specific piers. Can connect to Royal Barge Museum.
    Imperial Palace (get a cab - cheap and always metered). Entrance is 200 baht (I think).
    Food - any reputable restaurant is still cheaper than in Singapore.
    Take the tuk tuk only if you are in a group of three, girls don't do this alone. Tuk tuk drivers can be unscrupulous.
    Patpong - be very careful. Ahem!
    Have fun, happy shopping and eating.
