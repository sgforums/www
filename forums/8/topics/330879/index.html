    It's red tape basically. If you have lodged a Police report, look up the investigator in-charge of the case [his name and contact number will be at the bottom left-hand corner of the report together with a contact number]. You can ask him directly yourself [although you might get the same reply] but if you do, you can tell him to liaise with MOM about your maid's mode of exit.

    But from past experience, it should not be an issue lah. Either the investigator will tell you himself [you should have a right to know since you are the employer anyway], or you can try to get him to inform MOM. He should be able to do so by putting up a memo.

    Hope this helps.
