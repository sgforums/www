    The Civil Service is classified into Schemes of Service, each of which has its distinct job characteristics or functional areas. There are minimum educational requirements for entry into each scheme to ensure the quality and calibre of recruits into the Service. Officers in the same scheme share the same salary, benefits and career progression structure.

    There are 5 primary schemes in the Service currently, and many other specialist schemes to cater to various career needs and aspirations.

    Schemes of Service Entry Qualification Gross Monthly Salary*
    Management Executive Scheme University Degree Up to $2,550; or
    Up to $2,850 (for outstanding candidates)
    Management Support Scheme Polytechnic Diploma Up to $1,730
    Technical Support Scheme Polytechnic Diploma Up to $1,850
    GCE 'A' Level $1,271
    GCE 'O' Level $1,113
    Corporate Support Scheme GCE 'A' Level $1,176
    GCE 'O' Level $1,092
    GCE 'N' Level $1,008
    Operation Support Scheme Passed Secondary 2 $885
    Completed Primary School Education $818

    *The salary range does not include increments for NS, work experience etc

    from: https://app.vog.gov.sg/StaticContent/StaticPage.aspx?url=Rsc_Benefits.htm
