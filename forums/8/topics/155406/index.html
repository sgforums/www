    Time is GMT + 8 hours
    Posted: 29 September 2005 1709 hrs

    NEA reminds public smoking ban to be extended

    SINGAPORE : The National Environment Agency is reminding the public that smoking is banned at more public places from next month.

    These include bus interchanges, public toilets and public swimming complexes.

    Signs will be placed at these locations and enforcement action will be taken against smokers who breach the ban.

    Members of the public can provide feedback on smoking offences by calling the NEA call centre at 1800-CALL-NEA (1800-225-5632), or sending an e-mail to contact_NEA@nea.gov.sg. - CNA /ct
