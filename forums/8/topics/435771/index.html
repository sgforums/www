    Security Checks

    To ensure safety and security at the event, security checks on persons and belongings will be conducted as a condition of entry. Walk-through metal detector gantries for persons and x-ray machines for personal items will be used. The public is advised to minimise the amount of personal belongings brought into the venue to facilitate quick security clearance.

    Any container (100ml capacity or larger) containing liquid, aerosol or gel will be subjected to additional checks. Canned drinks and canned items will not be allowed into the venue. Each ticket holder will receive an NDP Fun Pack containing amongst other items, 2 bottles of water, 2 packet drinks, and light snacks. Prohibited items, including keychains and lighters shaped like a gun, pistol, bullet or grenade, need to be discarded before entry is allowed.
