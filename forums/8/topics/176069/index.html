    Hey Stanny,

    There are some backpacker guest houses opposite Bugis Junction, but its only one or two of them......above coffee shops. I don't recommend you stay there (I suspect they're not very friendly).

    If you want cheap hotels, do a search for Hotel 81 or Fragrance Hotel on Google. They are more expensive than backpacker joints...but still quite affordable. Be warned though....that the lower floors of these hotels are used for "short term use". Overnighters get to stay on the upper floors.

    Most shops are closed on Reunion Dinner night and on the first day. But many should be opened from Day 2 (I think).

    As for pubs for single, I've been out of the scene for some time, so sorry I can't help on this one.

    Enjoy yourself here!
