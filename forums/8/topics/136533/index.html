    There are a few websites that explain the musical terms, but the one below is quite easy to understand (it is meant for guitarists, but most musical terms are interchangeable between instruments):

    http://emedia.leeward.hawaii.edu/frary/glossary.htm#DAnchor

    Most music scores have recommended tempos with a metronome setting marked at the top left corner of the first bar:



    The number indicates the number of beats per minute. The example above recommends a tempo of 116 crochets or quarter notes per minute.

    If you do not have a metronome, you could visit the website below to get a rough idea of the tempo.

    http://www.metronomeonline.com/

    Hope that helps.
