    Can we have some more logical stuffs please? Read this from somewhere... Rather interesting

    It is commonly regarded that time is the 4th dimension.

    We are 3-dimensional beings, hence we are more familiar with how 1,2 and 3 dimensional objects interact.

    For example, we know that our 3-dimensional space can contain an infinite number of 2-dimensional objects. ie. Lots of 2-D shadows can be cast in our world. Similarly, an infinite number of lines can be drawn on a 2-D piece of paper. However, the line on that piece of paper is unable, on its own, to move to another position on the paper. Same for the shadow. It does not change, unless something changes in the 3D world, like when the object casting the shadow moves or the light source moves.

    If we extra-polate what we know from the above, it suggests that if there exists a 4th dimensional creature, living in his 4D world, he can easily observe our entire 3D world, and cause a direct consequence in our world. ie. If he shines his 4-dimensional torch, he can cast a 3Dimensional shadow in our world.

    Going a bit further, note how a 3D creature moves around in a 3D world. When we walk around, we know that we our physical location has changed. However, when we moving a a piece of paper with a drawing on it around, would the drawing know that something has changed? Unlikely. This is because the drawing still confined in the same piece of paper.

    All these lead to my following beliefs:
    1. A higher dimensional being can manipulate our 3D world. Perhaps God is such an entity.
    2. If time is the 4th dimension, the higher being can just shift or change our position in time as simply as us moving a table from a location to another.
    3. We are 3D creatures.. we can only observe the consequences of a change in time (like us getting older) but we cannot force a change in time, since it is outside of our dimension.

    Answering the original posters question, I can suppose if our whole time-space could be on a 4D truck that's driving around in the 4D world. In that case, our position in time is constantly changing. When the truck moves forward, our perception of time moves forward. When the truck stops, all time freezes, but we wouldnt even have a clue. So if the truck reverses in time, then we will move backwards in time.. like a rewinding video. Again, we will not be able to observe a move back in time, and it will feel perfectly normal to us even if everything is going backwards.

    So in that sense, time travel is definitely possible, in all directions, but too bad we, as 3D creatures, cannot control or initiate a change in direction of time. A higher dimensional being could, if such a being exists and wants to.
