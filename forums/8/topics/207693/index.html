    WATERFEST BY THE BAY 2006 is back again. Here are the details:

    Event Name: WaterFest By The Bay 2006

    Venue:
    Kallang Basin (Opposite National Stadium, next to Oasis Restaurant)

    Date of event:
    2nd & 3rd of September 2006

    Time of event:
    Day 1 (2nd September) - 10.30am to 7.30pm. Day 2 (3rd September) - 10.30am to 6.30pm.

    Event highlight:
    - Freestyle Moto Xtreme Demonstration (3 shows per day, including a show with Pyrotechnics show on 2nd September, 7pm)
    - Doggy Contest
    - Bike Trails Demo
    - Skateboarding Competition
    - In-line skating Competition
    - Bayfront Jam Concert with "Kruger featuring John Molina, Ronin, Electrico)
    - Beatbox Show featuring DJ Koflow
    - Street Fusion (World's First Hollaback Crew) show
    - WaterFest Urban Race
    - Subaru Action Asia Challenge
    - Free Boat Rides like Jet Ski, Banana Boat, Power Craft, Paddle Boats, Aqua Scooter etc.. (2 times more then last year's)
    - Wakeboarding Demonstration
    - Sunset BBQ
    - Stage Games
    - Childrens's Carnival Arena with Giant Bouncers, Dunking Tank & Rodeo Bull Machine (Rare in S'pore)
    - WaterFest By The Bay Photography Contest

    Here are the prizes for the Photography Contest:
    Prizes-
    1st Prize: Motorola Handphone + 1 DVD Player
    2nd Prize: Motorola Handphone + S$50.00 meal voucher from FlowerBed Kitch & Bar
    3rd Prize: Motorola Handphone

    Please refer to attachment for the contest forms as well as the rules & regulations for the Photography Contest.

    This year's event is in line with spreading the news to Singaporeans to enjoy our Waters through water sports & also PUB's msg of enjoying the water we drink, since the Kallang Basin is converted into S'pore's latest & largest reservoir.

    The contents this year is so much more & exciting compared to last year's. For more information about the event, please log onto www.waterfestsingapore.com
