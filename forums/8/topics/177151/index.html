    Push ups won't affect your height. But a hard gym work can do it. When the body still grows, this is possible thanks to the clefts which are on each end of the bones. The bones get longer this way. When the body stops to grows, these clefts "close" themselves. When lifting heavy weights, the weight can press on these clefts and close them and the bones can't grow. Thus affecting the height.

    Guys who still grow should only lift weights and work with weights which are not more then the growing bones can bear.
