    Imagine yourself in the following 3 situations and work your way through these scenarios in the order presented.

    You may not be as wealthy as Bill Gates, but you do have all the money you need, now and in the future. What will you do with it? From this moment forward, how will you live your life?
    You have just come back from a visit to a doctor who has discovered from your lab reports that you have 5-10 yrs to live. In a way you are lucky. This particular disease has no manifestations, so you wont feel sick. The bad part is that you will have no warning about the moment of your death. It will simply come upon you in a unpredictable instant, sudden and final. Knowing death is waiting for you sooner than expected, how will you change your life? And what you do in the uncertain but substantial period you have remaining?
    Again, you've gone to the doctor, but this time you learn you will be dead within 24 hrs. What regrets and longings do you have? What are your unfulfilled dreams? What do you wish you had completed, been, had or done in this life that is just about to end?
