    If the tiny bits of fibre come from the fabric itself, there's not much you can do. It's probably from too much friction or you didn't turn the clothes inside out when you wash it.
    Maybe use a masking tape to get them off, if they'll come off at all.
    Otherwise if you can do it without damaging the fabric, carefully use small scissors and trim the more obvious bits away.
