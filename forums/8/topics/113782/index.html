    ok. Answer:
    1). Assuming uniform beam.
    so diagram is

    ----------------------------------------------------------
    | |
    ----------------------------------------------------------
    o 3m | 1.5m o
    v
    30kg

    there the supports are 4.5m apart.
    the overall weight of the beam is supposed to act at the middle of the beam (i.e. c.g. of the beam).

    Assuming simple supports, therefore, the moment of the weight of the beam w.r.t. the right support is 30(10) x1.5m= 450Nm (assuming g=10m/s2)

    therefore for someone standing on the right of the right support, the length of overhang before the beam tips over is 450Nm/70(10) =0.643m
    or just use 45/70=0.643m.
