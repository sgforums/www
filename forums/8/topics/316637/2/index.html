    I go to Bangkok every year for my tailoring pilgrimage. In the multitudes of tailors in Bangkok, you have to find the tailor that is really good. Some advice on looking for tailors in bangkok is:

    1) never go to those that says "24 hours guaranteed" tailoring, fast no good.

    2) Those who allow you to have 2 or 3 fittings before embarking on the tailoring it.

    Of cos, there is the issue of workmanship and style and materials. That you need to find out yourself which one suits you best. Dun be surprised that there are some shops in Bangkok that is pretty expensive by their standards and refuse to bargain becos they know their standards. Anyway I found my regular tailor in Bangkok liao. Tucked in one corner by the chao phraya river. Not the cheapest in BKK but really good workmanship.
