    How's it going, y'all?

    I'd have to say that taichi is a really practical martial arts style. If you're one of those who say the slow moves look silly or it looks like a boring style, then it has actually served its purpose of fooling you already. Practitioners are taught that there is no point in practising those hard and fast moves because you cannot focus on the little mistakes that you'll naturally make as you pratcise. Thus when you move slowly (and surely), you'll be able to perfect the move. Finally, taichi is the martial arts that spawned other similar styles like aikido. I've done taekwondo, kendo, winchun and taichi. Still doing the last one, and it's still the most practical one out of all of them.
