    1. Have a sense of humour and don't treat everything your friends say seriously. Sometimes they may say things that are offensive, but they don't mean it.
    2. Know right from wrong. Know what you are doing and always think of the consequences.
    3. Don't ever get addicted to a game that you wake up at 3am just to play it.
    4. If you dunno, ask. Clarifying is better than doing the wrong thing.
    5. Learn to manage time well. Balance work and play.
    6. Don't try too hard to make friends, ppl will view you as an attention seeker instead.
    7. Don't be afraid to try out new things (except smoking, taking drugs of course...). Secondary school is when you start going out with friends to explore. Bowling, ice-skating, etc are good activities to try out. Healthy activities. LAN gaming is unhealthy if you feel the urge to play everyday.
    8. Don't ever try anything funny with the teachers and admins. Stay out of trouble. And don't always think online ranting is harmless.
    9. Look on the positive side. Participating enthusiastically in school events is better than brooding over the time wasted.
    10. Making friends? Be yourself.
