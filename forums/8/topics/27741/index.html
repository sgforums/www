    Originally posted by phreak:
    5 boys, 4 girls and 2 Ah Guas are to sit on a row of chairs.
    In how many ways can this be done if
    - the girls must not sit next to each other
    Ways that 5 boys and 2 Ah Guas can arrange themselves in a row - 7!/(5!2!).

    Now... for this row of 5 boys and 2 Ah Guas.. ie 7 males.. there are 6 slots in between them to place the girls, plus also 2 more at the 2 ends of the row. That means there are 8 slots to place the girls... from which we must choose any 4 - 8C4.

    So, the answer is 7!(8C4)/(5!2!). This answer is valid when we don't care about the identities of the 11 people involved, but only their status, as in boy/girl/ahgua.

    If we care about their individual identities, then the answer should be 7!(8P4).
