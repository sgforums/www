    If you face this problem.. how would u handle it?

    Mary likes Jack and he knows that. In fact Mary is absolutely in love with Jack. Jack thinks that she is an excellent girl but for some reason the romantic feelings seem to escape him no matter how hard he tries to love her back.

    However, they are still good friends, Mary accepting the fact that he still cannot fall in love with her or maybe living the hope that he may. Jack has been an absolute gentleman by telling her how he feels and being honest to not only to himself, but also to her. They spend much time together, and are truly happy as friends.

    One day, Mary gets into an accident and goes to ICU in a very critical condition. The doctors basically write her life off, unless she is "very strong".

    Of course, Jack rushes to the hospital (almost forgetting to wear his underwear -ed) and holds her hand by her bedside. He knows that the doctor says whether she lives depends on her will and courage.

    While holding her hand, she opens her eyes and whispers to Jack, "do you.... love me?"

    If you are Jack, what will your answer be?

    Consider these situations:
    If you say no, she may lose faith in living, and give up. Of course, you will not be blamed for her death.

    If you say yes, she may also die. But it will give her the strength to live and -may- possibly recover.

    For the sake of this argument, let's just say u said YES, i love you, and she recovers. Wat happens next? she's well and fit now, and she THINKS u love her, but u actually don't. you feel everything good about her.. EXCEPT love.. then how?
