    I just learnt I have Sound->Colour synesthesia, seeing music as a bunch of...things in my mind (or mind's eye). Sometimes it appears as a bunch of poorly drawn, scraggly animation, othertimes it's a vivid picture for the duration of the sound. To make it easier to imagine, a bass drum in a part of a song appears as a grey splot of paint, but changes in intensity, shape and size depending on whether other instruments are also playing at that time. A drum roll usually brings up an image of many Uno-stacko/Jenga blocks rapidly being stacked up before tumbling over (on the cymbal crash at the end of the roll).

    The best representation I could show, just that music/sound instead of taste is the trigger of the visuals: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yyah49_Oz78

    I'm curious to know if anyone here also has synesthesia :o, maybe of different types. I don't know anyone around me that does.
