    Thats an interesting question....

    I believe it really depends on what your aim is for coming into the forum.....some come in here to put their sincerest thoughts down.....some destress and yak away their problems....some to be an alter ego that they cannot be in real life.

    Flirting....making new friends.....its all part of this forum we deem as free speech.

    Someone asked me whether my words in the forum really reflect my personality.....I'd have to say that it really depends.

    To a person who is in the depths of stress....the forum is a place of consolation...to vent his frustration at all he could not do. To say silly things that he otherwise would not mean in real life.

    To a person who is happy....the forum is a place to share his joy, and spread the warmth to everyone who wants it.

    To a person who is neither happy nor sad....the forum is a place to see what are the interesting happenings that could spice up his life.

    We write so much in the forum.....yet without the person's body language....a smile...or a dejected face....how much truth is there actually?

    The person could be posting happy messages as a way of relieving sadness...just as sad messages can be a way for a happy person to attract attention. And that is why I always believe in understanding how people truly feel, to snatch a glimpse into their lives, be it forum friends or otherwise.
