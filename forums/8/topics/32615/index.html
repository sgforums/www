    The Chocolate Box

    B1-02, Raffles City Shopping Centre, North Bridge Road
    10 am to 10 pm daily; tel: 338-9707
    $34.50 for 24 pieces (or $10.30 per 100 g)
    Ratings: * * * * *

    This shop offers a wide variety of pre-packed chocolates. You can also choose to package your chocolates in a clear box wrapped with ribbons, or in a decorated metal tin.

    The clear packaging is enticing as each chocolate is displayed as a feast for your eyes, but have a pair of scissors ready to snip off the ribbons which are tied too tightly.

    Exotic varieties include green tea and mango truffles, on top of the classic dark, white and milk ones. There is also a variety of liquor truffles like Tiramisu, Grand Marnier, Baileys Irish cream and rum and raisin. They are designed by a Swiss chef but made in a factory in Singapore.

    The dark chocolate scored an ace, with its bitter-sweet flavour and smooth texture.
