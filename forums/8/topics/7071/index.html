    Listen and pay attention. Simple as that, the problem with us man is that we seldom listen, no I am not saying that we have shorter attention span. Just let her let off some steam, just let her complaint, all you need to do is listen, and empathise by sharing similiar situations you have had with her, but not the solution. She will find you a different man from the rest.

    Let her be special, do things that you would never do for another gal for her. And let her know subtlely. Say if you never give flowers to girls, then give her a stalk of rose on your date with her. Pay special attnetion to her, be sensitive, feel her every emotion, and be there behind her, when you are needed, made her your queen.

    Method 3

    Surprise her, and make it often. Be the sugar and spice in her life, bring her out to an exotic place for dinner, spomewhere she never went before. Appear at her office door, at the most unsuspecting time, with some ice-cream. Make sure you are alway interesting, by exposing your different sides to her, it will help to keep her interested, she will not find you boring.

    Method 4

    Be yourself, be what you truly are. For you can win her heart by playing actor, but you will most probably not keep it for long, if all you have is a facade. Let her like the real you, not the prince in shiny armor you dress up as. Let her see your ugliest moments, share your faliures with her, this way you are more real, and that is something mature woman look out for.
