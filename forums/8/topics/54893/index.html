    aiyah.. circular calculation....

    divide your number into 2.. let the first 4 digits be x and the last 4 be y

    therefore your number will be 10000x + y

    follow the steps in the "mystery formula"

    step 1:
    key in first 4 digits: x

    step 2:
    multiply first 4 digits by 80 to get: 80x

    step 3:
    add 1 to the result to get: 80x + 1

    step 4:
    multiply by 250 to get: 250(80x + 1)

    step 5:
    add the last 4 digits to get: 250(80x + 1) + y

    step 6:
    add the last 4 digits again to get: 250(80x + 1) + 2y

    step 7:
    subtract 250 to get: 250(80x + 1) + 2y - 250

    step 8:
    divide the whole lot by 2 to get: [250(80x + 1) + 2y -250]/2

    simplify the result in step 8...

    [250(80x + 1) + 2y -250]/2
    = [20000x + 250 + 2y - 250]/2
    = [20000x + 2y]/2
    = 10000x + y

    which is your mobile phone number!
