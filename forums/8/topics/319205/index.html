    I think all kinds of media can somehow hace impact on youth Violence. For the radio, the problem only lies with the channels which plays modern Engligh songs which may contain words of vulgar. Our newspapers and TV news programmes are controlled by the government so there no problem with the news. I don't know weather the internet is classified as the media by you, but I must say that the internet is the most serious type of media that has impact on youth violence. In the internet, you can find all kinds of information, people and there are many things that you can do. In the internet, you can also find information telling you to fight.     Q2: What are some ways that this problem can be solved?

    I think the only way to solve this problem is to instill the right values in our teenagers. Also, we need to control the media to teach them the right values. However, I feel that some teenagers are violent because they do not have a purpose in life. What we need to let them have is a purpose in life. I also think there is another reason contributing to teenage violence, which is because our teenagers are under too much preasure from their studies. Preasure from their studies may give them a hot temper and therefore become violence. Of all, I think we should let them have more exposure to whatever is happening outside. Bring them out of the house or classroom. Let them look at what is happening outside, and make sure they spend their time fruithfully outside by observing, instead of throwing them with worksheets As a teenager myself, I always take every opptunity to observe anything I can, from buses to trains, and how things are managed, whenever I am outside the house. This will possibly let them find their purpose in life. For me, I will want to improve our transport system when I grow up. Somehow, youth violence is related to the culture nowadays. Take a ride on a feeder service on a typical weekday afternoon and you will get what I mean. You will see students playing loud music on their handphones and talking loudly as well. And look at our adults to compare how they behave. Some of our violent youths may have learnt violence from all these. For me, I do not. I am mature enough to type in proper English. While some are not mature enough to do so. We must train them to be mature enough. We should kick their habits of talking loudly and typing in improper English. I can also infer that our youths are inmature from their childishness. Its all related.
