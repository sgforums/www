Woman Arrested For Selling Fake Vouchers

  • Urroh
    18 Nov 06, 13:04
    A 20-year-old woman was arrested today at 11 am for selling fake supermarket vouchers on-line.

    On 31 Oct 06, at about 8.10 pm, a manager of a supermarket discovered that one of his customers had used two pieces of fake vouchers valued at $50/- each for a purchase. According to the customer, she bought the vouchers through an online auction site. She had paid $170/- for 4 $50 voucher.

    Since then, Police received a total of seven similar reports.

    After investigations, police managed to establish the identity of the suspect who sold the vouchers. The unemployed woman was arrested from her home this morning. She will be charged in court on 18 Nov 06 and remanded for further investigations.

    PUBLIC AFFAIRS DEPARTMENT
    SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
    17 November 2006 @ 7.30pm
