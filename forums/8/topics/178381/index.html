    In terms of ethnics from my own experience...

    Ethnics
    1. Govt doctors are paid a fix salary, therefore there is less conflict of interest. Private doctors salary depends on how much money they can make out of the patient + number of patients. Therefore they may try to spend as little time as possible to diagnose your condition (higher chance of misdiagnosis) to maximise the number of patients seen per day. i.e give wrong medicine/treatment due to misdiagnosis and ask patient to come back if condition doesn't get better. Another possible trick to make more money is to give less medicine and ask the patient to come back for another visit if the condition doesn't get better.

    2. I think that govt doctors are more accountable because they have to report to their superiors for those junior doctors and follow standard procedure. Private doctors that run their own clinic mostly do what they want?

    Note: These are just my own observations from my personal experience that i wish to share with everyone. They may or may not be true.

    If anyone has a bad experience with private or govt doctors please share your experience here.
