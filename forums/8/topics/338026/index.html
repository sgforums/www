    I'm 21 this year and I've been using Minoxidil which is available in pharmacies for a month now. It costs about $60 a bottle and the first bottle I bought has last me nearly one and a half months.

    I'm going through the first phase atm which is the shedding of hair on the affected areas, but it is a sign that the medicine is working.

    Anyways, the initial first two or three months will be the shedding of hair, which will make your balding worst but you need to keep using it.

    After the shedding phase, new baby hair will grow out of your scalp

    I hope this info was what you needed and maybe you'll proceed with the same treatment as I am, with the same good results, good luck :)
