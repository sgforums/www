    Ultra spoliers

    Okay, don't expect too much Matrix action, most of it takes place in the real world.

    And no mind-bending kung fu fight sequences, just the one at the end vs. Neo and Smith which I think is pretty okay by Matrix standards, but my favourite two fights are still the Burly Brawl (100 Smith fight) and the CS_Mansion fight in the mountains. Most of the bullettime FX is in Reloaded I'm afraid... there's just one reconizable BT in Rev.

    Okay, Morpheous and Trinity go into the Martix to save Neo, who was trapped in there after he discovered he could use his The One powers in the real world as well as the matrix. Apparently, The Matrix is also connected to the real machine world. Neo can sense the machines in the real world like he sees Matrix code, even after he is blinded by the Smith/Bane guy in the real world. (Ironically Neo does not really have his Kung Fu moves in the real world)

    In the place of the usual stylish sequences we get most of the visual blowout in the machine invasion of zion, which is a massive one-hour battle swapping between two parties... Morpheous and Niobe rushing home in the hovercraft hammer, and the main war in Zion.

    Trinity and Neo on the other hand, will head on different directions towards the machine city in Niobe's ship, the Logos. In prehaps the cheapest main character killoff in the trilogy, Trinity kaputs when the Logos crashes.

    Smith takes over the entire matrix (including the Oracle) and threatens to destroy the real machine world as well. The machines are unable to stop him, hence Neo comes to the machine city and talks a deal with them. End the war and he'll stop Smith. One Smith clone now has the same One superpowers after copying the Oracle, in fact, he's more powerful then Neo.

    So the machines plug Neo back into the matrix and he fights Smith in the final battle where Neo gives up his life in order to defeat Smith and Reload the matrix. This time however, there is peace between the man and machine and everyone knows the truth. Those who want out are allowed out.

    The war is over... for now.
