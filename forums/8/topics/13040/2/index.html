    I think the whole point of the whole proposal is to prevent such a thing from happening. Frankly speaking, my PSLE was so many years ago that I have no idea what's my expectation then. I wanted to go to RI, but was afraid that I might not make it because I was from a neighbourhood primary school.

    Imagine this scenario:
    A student who has consistently been the top student in a below average school. He thinks he's good enough, so put down all the top schools as his choices. In the end, PSLE only got 250 => failed to make it to ALL his choices. So what will happen? Chances are, he will be ding-dong-ing along and end up in a neighbour school near his house. If he had been allowed to select after knowing his 250 score, he can make a better choice by omitting all the top schools and going for maybe an above average one like Anderson Sec.
