    Don't think you need to apply for a Fire Safety Certificate for roadshows lah... The landlord or Strata Management of the mall have it and it's a must have for the mall to commence operations and occupancy...

    The undertaking of Fire Safety will be by the mall's Fire Safety Manager (FSM) for any form of fire safety preventive measures taken during the roadshows like provision of suitable, adequate and capable fire extinguishers, non-choking of access passages and also not overcrowding the area are some pre-requisites...

    The thing your friend need to check is with Public Entertainment Licensing Unit (PELU) for any possible permit required for the roadshows...

    For more information, can check on http://www.scdf.gov.sg

    Hope this information helps...
