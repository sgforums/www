    Batch I Mk I are a mixture of Voiths and ZFs without the intercooler (as said by someone else earlier in the forums). The Habits that had been burnt before are all ZFs from this range of regos.     Batch II Habits are mixture of Voiths and ZFs with intercooler. It is known that certain buses from this range got a pair of "horns" protruding from the bumper and some of these buses in this range have different blinkers from the original versions
