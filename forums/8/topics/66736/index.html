    Of course taking cab the fastest! If you are taking buses, then follow these choices:

    1. Board service 147 along C'wealth Ave West, alight along Serangoon Rd (between Tekka Centre bus stop and Boon Keng MRT station), transfer to service 23 to Temasek Poly or Tampines interchange.

    2. Board service 166 along Dover Ave/Rd, alight opposite HarbourFront Centre, transfer to service 10 to Tampines interchange.

    If you are only getting to Tampines Ave 2, then board service 106, alight at Lucky Plaza bus stop, transfer to Express 518. (Note that Express 518 do bring you to Temasek Poly too although the fare is more expensive)
