An open love letter to my best friend

  • Gaudio
    28 Sep 12, 02:42

    Dear Michelle,

    There're some things I really want to say to you, to express to you. Aside from simply coming up to you and saying them, there really is no way to tell you how I feel. So I won't. My ideas, due to the poor means of communication words provide, might be misunderstood and cause our relationship to become uncomfortable. So I've decided not to tell you. And I'll compose and publish my letter here.

    I love you. Not in a friendly way, although I think we're great friends. And not in a misplaced affection, puppy-dog way, although I'm sure that's what you'll call it. And it isn't because you're unattainable. I love you, very simple, very truly. You've always been such a wonderful, protective and supportive person to me, you've been with me and been there for me ever since I met you, be it through love, pain or confusion. For that, I'm eternally grateful. I know you think of me as just a friend, and crossing the line's the furthest thing from an option you've ever considered. But with you, I feel that there're emotional things that'll not change, even if our two seperate lives do change on their own. I'm feel a special comfort when I'm with you, I could curl up next to you and fall asleep quite easily. Your faults and unattractive behaviours, they're spicy and real and they make up part of who you are. I love them.

    I know if you see this, it will probably queer our friendship but I can't take this anymore. I couldn't allow another day to go by without getting it out there, because I've never felt this way before, and I like who I'm because of it. If you ever ever see this, and if bringing this to light means we can't hang out anymore, then that hurts me. But I know, I know some part of you is hesitating for a moment, and if there's this moment of hesitation, that means you feel something too. And all I ask is that you not dismiss that, at least not for ten seconds and try to dwell in it.

    Michelle, there're all kinds of love out there, but this is my one and only life. You have no idea how much you've changed it forever and moved me. Without you, I wouldn't even be a fraction of the man I'm today. I would risk this friendship for the chance to take it to the next plateau. Because it's there between you and me, you can't deny that.

    I want to tell you these things. Perhaps someday I'll tell you. Perhaps someday you'll see this, and I'll never know. Regardless of what happens, I want you to know that I love you and I'll be there for you above any other person. I count myself among the luckiest souls in the universe for meeting you.

    Love, 

    Ian
