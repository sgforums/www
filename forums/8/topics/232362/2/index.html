    19 Jan 07, 12:01
    Starhub has mobile phone plans that offer free IDD 018 calls to several countries including Malaysia. You only pay for the local call costs, which is billed on per second basis, and can count towards your free local talktime.

    Bear in mind that 1) you HAVE to sign up for a plan that supports 018 free calls.. and 2) Always dial the 018 sequence manually.. I've come across a rare case in the past where a customer saved his friend's number in his phonebook using 018, but when they called his friend via the phonebook, he was charged premium 008 rates. There was this huge investigation and turned out to be the phone software fault. So be careful.

    As for the Phoenix card, you might have to hop down to a nearby 7-11 outlet etc to check out the card's rates. It's still cheaper than the mobile phone calls, but it's only practical to use it from a land line.

    hope it helps.
