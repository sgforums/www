    Firstly, find out more abt the company( History, Profile, Services etc) before going for the interview. Prepare few sets of questions regarding the company or the position u r applying......

    Be prepared for the Popular Personal Questions like:
    Please describe yourself!
    What r ur good points?
    What r ur bad points?
    What u usually do/go after working hrs/weekends? (do not mention clubbing unless u r interviewing for a sales position in Asia Pacific Brewery or something similar.)(dont mention sleeping or slacking coz u will give them a bad impression of being lazy)

    As for the questions regrading the job depends on which industries u r going to.

    A friend of mine went for an interview few months back.... The first part of the interview was conducted in a group of 3. Each of them was given a computer.....(sounds like a IQ Test or something)
    The same question was given to the 3 of them and they only had 3 mins to type whatever they can. The question was " WHAT IS YOUR GREATEST MOMENT IN LIFE" 3mins countdown starts now!!!

    Be prepared to be interview by a group of people at a time......
    Be prepared to be bombard by questions even thought u have not finish answering the previous one.....
    Do not get panic in this situation... They just wanna see how u react in a situation like this.
    Remember the sets of questions prepared before the interview? Now its your turn to ask them when they had finishing asking. By asking or talking more to them will make them feel that u r sociable n really interested in this position.

    All the best to u
