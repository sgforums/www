    Smoking habits amongst house physicians working at Jinnah Postgraduate Medical Center, Karachi, Pakistan.

    Piryani RM, Rizvi N.

    Jinnah Postgraduate Medical Center, Karachi, Pakistan. r_piryani@hotmail.com

    The objective of this study was to estimate the prevalence of smoking among house physicians working at Jinnah Postgraduate Medical Center, Karachi, Pakistan. Sixty-four (32%) of the 200 house physicians were smokers; 50% (36) had started smoking in their twenties and 44% (2 in their teens. Seventy-five per cent (4 of the smokers were influenced by friends, 20% (13) by cigarette commercials and the remainder by their parents. Ninety-five per cent (61) smoked during duty hours. The frequency of smoking among young doctors was higher than the overall prevalence of smoking in Pakistan.

    PMID: 14959980 [PubMed - indexed for MEDLINE]
