    Cess is a tax levied at the rate of 1% on all Cessable items sold by tourist hotels, tourist food establishments and tourist public houses. It is collected by the Singapore Tourism Board (STB) under the Singapore Tourism (Cess Collection) Act. The Inland Revenue Authority of Singapore (IRAS) is the agent appointed by STB for the administration and collection of Cess.
