    from kranji mrt station...?

    go kranji, cross the overhead bridge and hope on to 160 or 170 (blue plate). u can take 170 or CW1 as well but not recommended.

    when u reach the checkpoint, tap u card out to suspend the ride, clear customs and tap the card to resume the ride when u board again. same thing happens at jb. u can choose not to reboard at jb, it is ok not to resume the ride, cos ur fares are properly calculated....

    if u board sbst buses, u can take any sbst bus on each segment of the journey, means u can be taking a 170 to woodlands customs, and take a 160 bus across the causeway because it came first, then from there u can reboard according to ur destination.... if u board 950, u have to wait for another 950 to come for each segment of ur journey if u wan ur fare to be counted as that of one journey....
