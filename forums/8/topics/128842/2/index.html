    Don't buy low flr flats cos
    1) U will have pest problems (cockroaches) cos of the rubbish chute.
    2) noise pollution- many Malay weddings/ funerals held at void deck
    3) No view- pple more willing to pay
    4) Very hot cos u get blocked.

    Don't think u can make much unless it is first flat & u get subsidy.
    Anyway if u make from the resale, u still have to pay high price for your next flat.

    It is ok to buy exec HDB if u intend to have a big family, then save u the hassle of upgrading.

    Buying a flat u shd look at location- proximity to MRT, mkt, schs.
    Also check the facing- don't have afternoon sun cos very hot.
    If possible don't get too near mosque, church, sch. cos congestion
