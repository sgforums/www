    the math of prime numbers...

    say, they have a parasite or predator which also goes through their long life-cycle....

    the animal kingdom is tied to seasons which is annual cyclical....

    if the parasite goes through a 2 year life cycle.. then the cicada will try to avoid meeting the parasite by not having a life cycle not divisible by 2....

    they'll meet every 6 years if the cicada evolves into a 3 year life cycle...

    with a 17 year life cycle, and if the parasite increases its life cycle accordingly, it'll be left behind with a 16 year life cycle...

    they'll meet only at 272 years (16 x 17) by that time, the parasite will probably have gone extinct...

    same thing happened with the 13 year cycle species... it's 156 years (12 x 13)
