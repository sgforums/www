    Adding to Fugue's comment.

    What is effective communication?

    It is about letting others understand what you want to say.

    It is also about understanding what others are saying.

    Generally, it is not about using bombastic words or that the grammar must be 100% accurate or super beautiful sentences. You will notice that some forumites are very strong in their english language but not many understand their posts. In this sense, they are not communicating effectively. However, if all the forumites are lawyers and english teachers, then it is perfectly alright for them to use super chim english.

    I believe you will do fine in NP business, judging from the english you type. Don't worry about presentations, the course is supposed to teach you how to do them. As for report writings, just use simple sentences will do - read some business articles (ignoring the business jargons at this moment) and you will know what I mean. AFter your Os, should decide to take the NP biz cos, rEad business articles frequently to improve your business english.

    Most importantly is whether do you see yourself working in the business line, aka marketing, finance, accounting, logistics, human resource, etc.

    If you are indian or chinese, I suggest you up or at least not ignore your mother language.

    Hope it helps and if you still have questions, keep on posting.
