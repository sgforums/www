  • Ludacris

    basically just ta' let ya'll know whadd ah' mean. geddit?

    da hood - neighbourhood
    frontin' - engaging in a violent action, usually physical
    dawg - friend
    homie - friend
    nigga - friend (singular)
    niggaz - friend (plural)
    chronic n' s'hit - marijuana or weed
    toke - to inhale marijuana smoke
    bong - a device filled with water to smoke marijuana
    cribs - house
    ho' - a lady
    mothaf'uckin - extremely
    bling-bling - valuables, money
    cheeze - money
    mofo - motherf'ucker
    pimpz - people
    wudup - hi

    how to use it

    in any english word ending with a "d", substitute it with "oodt"
    hood = hooodt or hooudt

    in words ending in "ore," eliminate everything after the first "o" and add an apostrophe
    i won't tell you again, please shut the door = i aint tellin you no mo', shet de do'

    for words with 2 identical consonants followed by "er," eliminate the "er."
    that negro was larger and was holding a pistol = mah nigga was bigga had his fingah on yo' trigga

    most "er"s are dropped and replaced by "ah."
    tower = towah

    in the case of a plural, "ers" is replaced by "az."
    niggers = niggaz.
