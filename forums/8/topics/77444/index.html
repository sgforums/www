    27 Apr 04, 20:48
    genting highlands - easy and convenient, cause it's a popular destination. lots of travel agencies organize trips

    cameron highlands - used to be hotter than genting, but given the development at genting, this is no longer true. highlights include tea, vege and flower plantations

    fraser's hill - nothing much. just a place to relax. it's troublesome to go up, cause the road can only accomodate one vehicle one way. so, there is a "up" and "down" schedule. and i believe travelling up and down stops at 7pm. less developed, thus attracts those who don't like highly developed places. stayed at this villa once (yes, a real english style villa - built by the english themselves). it was B-E-A-UTIFUL!! fire-place, wooden flooring, window seat, strawberry plants and a lovely garden

    pangkor island - island getaway. best hotel (highly rated in the world as well) is pangkor island resort. rates start from RM1,000 and above, i believe. more affordable and also a very nice hotel/resort is pan pacific, pangkor island. there are other smaller ones ....... damai laut or something like that.

    redang islands / perhentian islands - just came back from perhentian islands. these 2 islands are near to each other (30mins boat ride). the water is great, the marine life is just beautiful. this place is only is you enjoy the beach and getting a tan
