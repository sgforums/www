    For a guy and a girl to remain just close friends is not impossible, just very rare.

    It is difficult to say that when you just become friends with a member of the opposite sex, you have it in your head that you will just remain friends. Attraction may sometimes grow slowly, so after a period you feel yourself being drawn to a friend, especially if you have been spending a lot of time with him/her, be it talking on the phone, going out etc. Some people are uncomfortable remaining friends with someone whom he/she has tried to have a more intimate relationship with. It is in their minds that if we cannot be bf/gf then we should stop wasting our time. Othertimes it is just the awkwardness of it all. When you are with that person you find yourself being mindful of what you say or do so you will not give the other person anymore wrong impressions. In the end it becomes easier to just drift apart.

    I have a female friend and we have known each other for almost 3 years now. Our friendship can be considered quite close, although it is a on/off thing. At times we can talk to each other everyday, sometimes through the night. Other times we do not call each other for weeks even. We have shared secrets, experiences etc. She even admitted that she liked me once way in the beginning, but at that time my heart was thinking of someone else. I have tried to make our relationship more intimate, but she just laughs it off. Till now it is more like a joke between us. Honestly, I have not gone out with her 1-on-1 before. Tried many times but it is mission impossible. She always cancels at the last minute or forgets about it. Oh well...

    If you do manage to have a close friendship with someone of the opposite sex, treasure it. It really is a wonderful experience.

    Just my two cents.
