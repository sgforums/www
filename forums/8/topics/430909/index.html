    Hi there..

    If you are joining SMU, be prepared for lotsa mugging and fluffing. There will be shitloads of projects. Interesting and fun sometimes, depending on your groupmates.

    You won't be bored in classes. Laptop is right in front of your face all the time. For most modules that is. Haha.

    Campus life is pretty not too bad. The activities, events, fellow undergrads, location.

    Personally, I enjoyed my 4 years there. The emphasis on being vocal definitely helped me and I'm thankful for that. Haha. I like the learning atmosphere as everything is quite techie. Plus air-conditioned everywhere! And I'm one who can't study if I feel stuffy and uncomfortable. My only complaint was probably food. Blardie expensive everywhere.

    No problems with finding job after graduate. Well at least for me and the SMU people I know. =)

    Good luck and all the best! Don't mug too hard. Enjoy uni life more. No point chasing grades too much. Haha.
