    Phoon Huat got two types of courses. One is demo type where you mostly get to see only and minimal hand-ons. Good if you have some background skills. Available main in shops.
    Another is the workshop where you really get to bake and learn. Held at Sim avenue or Sim drive, their HQ.
    Got the info ard 1/2 year back when i called them, nt sure if there is any changes.

    If you learn from people, you may learn something which may not be found in the receipes. eg how to separate eggs, flour need to be seived b4 used, when making dough, egg to be added last, letting the dough to "rest: in fridge.

    For oven get some thing which is manageable size. It shd cost ard $300 plus. Tefal is a good buy.

    I do bake, learnt from mum the basic but the tips from a bakery owner really improve my techniques.
