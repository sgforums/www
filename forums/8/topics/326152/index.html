    Started at Mt Faber lor ...
    HarbourFront MRT Stn Exit D. , walk along a trail crossing Henderson Waves and the Alexander Arch, after which was the Hortpark itself. .The whole trail was mostly uphill. The view from Henderson Waves itself was nice, think u can bring a camera along.

    I think u need to be a nature/plant lover to enjoy the Hortpark. U can still continue to trek towards Kent Rigde Park from Hortpark. Anyway, at Hortpark, there are vending machines around to replenish urself ...
