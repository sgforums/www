    I think its important to choose a general diploma.

    Try not to over-specialize at 17. You may not know what you really really want now. It's alright to do an IT diploma, but if you went to do a diploma that specializes in a specifc field in IT.... say security, you may find that you may 1) realize later that you do not like it. 2) Realize that suddenly the demand for such a professional drop in a couple of years time 3) Find that there are limited number of universities that recognizes and give your diploma advance standing.

    So, do a something that is more general. It gives you the space to move later on.
