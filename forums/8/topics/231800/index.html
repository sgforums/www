    Originally posted by Fog:
    like starving for few days in a row only drink water or do tremendous amounts of exercises. is it healthy dieting
    Like I mentioned in other thread, to starve yourself is not a good and healthy way of dieting. Everytime you starve yourself, your body remembers it so as soon as you start eating, your body will try to absorb the food and store it as fats. In the end, you might end up piling on more weight than you lost for the few days.

    If you want to do exercise to lose weight, just make sure you do something you enjoy and sustainable. When you exercise your metabolism increases, and hence can afford to eat more without gaining weight (as long as food intake < energy burnt). However, as soon as you stop exercising, your metabolism slows down and you'll soon put on weight again.
