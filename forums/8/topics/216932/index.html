    Saying

    "Hello A, I would like to introduce my good friend B"

    would make them feel shy and more conscious about themselves. dun have to be so formal la..

    find something common between A & B. (e.g interests, work place, religion, school, favourite food)

    Lets say A & B both like flying kites.

    Introducing A to B.....

    "Yo A, my friend here also like flying kites leh"

    Than juz leave them to talk about flying kites la. Usually the talkative party would start asking lots of questions. I've seen pple whom I've introduced to each other talk for hours on the phone after meeting each other for the first time. Common interest !!

    If both of them are shy, than maybe u start talking about kites and they would most probably start talking. Some people feel more comfortable when someone they know is also in the conversation, instead of just talking with someone they hardly know.
