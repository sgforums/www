  • FireIce
    5 Mar 08, 13:49

    occasional leisures as in... to dine in a fancy restaurant or have a drink or two at MOS or watch movie?

    food wise, if normal food centre or coffee shop (we call kopitiam), would be about S$5 for a meal (including drink)
    transport perhaps S$4 to and fro

    so give and take a little, daily expenses will be $15 (for 2 meals and transport)
    one month, minus weekends when u do not need to go out, would be 22 days x $15 = S$330

    groceries, toiletries, daily necessities = S$100 a month
    leisure activities = S$100 a month

    sometimes u need new clothes/shoes... maybe only S$100 every 3 months...

    so it's not hard to survive... just have to adjust your lifestyle...
    look out for places with economical food and stuff....

    have some savings for rainy days also...
    S$100 a month is not difficult
