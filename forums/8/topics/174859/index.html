    Fiddling with networking on your PC, can give you technical headaches.

    Go buy an external USB HD housing. Take out your hard disk, if it is in C drive configure jumper so that it is slave, plug into external hard disk housing, turn it ON and plug USB cord in. You can then transfer whatever you want.

    Or, you can buy the external USB hard disk housing along with a 250Gb hard disk and use it to transfer and hold your files in.
