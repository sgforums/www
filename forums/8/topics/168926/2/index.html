    Positive news; Chinese food is very cheap here compared to your Chinese takeaways. Living in Singapore, you need not have to cook at all. Just a US$2 can get full and satisfied on a hot meal in Singapore!

Hence expect to lose weight easily in Singapore as most of your diet will consist of more carbohydrate than protein.

    Not much cheap buys in Singapore compared to the Asia region. Would sugg you travel to parts of Southeast Asia for cheaper buys. However, if you want expensive stuff, you can buy cars and apartments in Singapore. Cars in Singapore are <10 yrs old and cost a lot of $$$ in comparison with the same model and make available in US.

    Anyway, if you come to Singapore, you have to travel to the neighboring countries here to make your stay worthwhile. Singapore gets boring after a while.
