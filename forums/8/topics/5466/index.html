    Oh manz... a topic I thought nobody would bring out. Maybe I can shed some light on this issue, since I've been in the same situation before... painful experience... but at least it's over and I've got a wonderful girlfriend right now.. so no complains really.

    I had this really terrific friendship with this girl. As mentioned by MySubterfuge, the two of us were great pals, confided in each other, shared our problems, stuck by each other through thick and thin... It all started out purely platonic of course, then something stirred inside me. Emotions started getting the better of me, and I found myself inevitably falling for her. I approached her, popped the question. She didn't reject me outright, saying that she was quite confused too, and needed time to sort out her thoughts. Finally after 2 weeks, she decided it wasn't going to work. Of course, I cried my eyes out... when pubbing.. smoked... basically did everything I shouldn't have done near my exams, and I suffered the consequences of my foolishness. Flunked one subject and scrapped through the others.

    Of course, things got rather awkward, and we would avoid each other. Stop calling each other, it even went so far as to avoid going out together with our other good friends. This lasted for almost a month.

    BUT.. things did not end that way (and no.. she isn't my girlfriend now either)... being matured and sensible people, we decided to end this foolishness, as it was apparent that it's causing our other friends a bit of unease, as well as the fact that THIS IS REALLY A DUMB AND CHILDISH THING TO DO... (not shouting at you MySubterfuge)

    I started calling her again, she didn't ignore me, we would make a few exchanges initially, as I guess the awkwardness was still there.. then things slowly started picking up again. We started yakking and complaining to each other again, and before we knew it, we were, and are good friends again, almost back to what we were before the entire sorry episode. I stress on the word almost, but I have faith that we will once again reach the level of friendship we once had.

    Of course, what happened to me, need not neccessarily happen to you. But it's a sign that it is possible for you to salvage the friendship.
