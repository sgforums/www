    Just a little informal survey for the users of this forum:

    What is it that you hope to achieve in visiting an internet forum such as this?
    I understand that some people are merely killing time, by knowing more people, dispensing free advice for those who seem to need it. Some people just want to share some problems they have no solutions to, or maybe they just need affirmation in what they already believe in. There are also some who are curious to understand the general view... (like this survey) on a variety of subjects, especially the more taboo ones, (one clear eg, being SEX). Or like fatnugly like to screw pple on the net in order to kill his time and for shoik.

    There are some inconsistencies, because on one hand, there are some very trusting people who take every word others say for God's truth, and there are those who are looking for serious relationships here.

    And on the other hand, there are some people soliciting for casual sex here.

    I think that generally, it is not encouraged since we cannot reach consensus where such practices (sex) are concerned. One man's fantasy is another man's sexual nightmare, and the internet forum just isn't the place to facilitate such matter, also because we cannot ensure the protection of minors. Besides, what happens outside the forum is beyond the control of moderators.

    I hope to hear some comments and some suggestions as well, as to understand what are the expectations of forum-users.
