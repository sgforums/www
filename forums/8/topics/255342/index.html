    A pretty basic example for you.

    Dear

    It is with great reluctance that I submit this letter of resignation from my position as , effective from the date of this letter.

    It has been my honour to work for such an outstanding organization. I am extremely grateful for all the guidance rendered to me for the past X years and have enjoyed my working experience.

    Thank you for the opportunity to serve .

    Yours sincerely
