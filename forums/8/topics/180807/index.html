    schnazers are prone to having skin problems so use a mixture of medicated and regular dog shampoos wen giving it a bath n do it regularly at least once a week or wen nessacary. i reccomend bio groom shampoo. plush puppy smells nice but is over priced n overrated.

    i hope u r feeding it dry food, a pup requires bout 2 - 3 feeding times a day. each times just put a handful of pellets into the bowl will do. as he grow older. switch to adult formula pellets i, increase the amount of food n feed only once a day. always keep fresh water available at all times. if no one is home to change or top up the bowl. get the bowl tt can acttach a upside down mineral water bottle tt will self refill.

    keep your dog entertained with chew toys. raw hide bones r good for cleaning its teeth and a diversion from chewing up yr house during its teething period. try to brush its teeth during bathes too. use a soft toothbrush and toothpaste (regular human brushes will do fine and i use kids flavoured toothpaste so tt its like a treat)

    wenever you bring it outdoors. make it a habit to check for ticks. n do NOT play with your pup for hours on end. ignore it if you must. pups r naturally playful n attention seeking. they dont knw wat the meaning of tired is until they suffer from fatigue and in most cases its fatal.

    anythg else pm me...
