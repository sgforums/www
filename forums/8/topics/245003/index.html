    i take it as it's a new pair of boots?

    firstly, tab ur brush multiple times on kiwi, then brush lightly over boot. place quite a thick layer and leave for 3mins to dry while concurrently doing the other boot.
    after it has dried, use ur brush to brush it all off.

    repeat multiple times and it'll start having a soft shine. keep doing this and in the future, all it needs is 1 layer and it'll start shining already

    to bring it to a mirror shine, use a cloth, dab a little bit of water, and keep rubbing the area where u want to mirror shine in circular motions. repeat multiple times.
