    16 means you're taking Os this year. If so, time to shut every disturbing persons, thought out of your life and head and focus on the subjects
    that will land you in a job first (school later on).

    If you cannot find peace and support at home to do your studies,
    find a quiet and conducive place where you can concentrate.
    Never at yr bf's house!

    Of all the subjects, it's unlikely you can excel in all. So don't let those
    that you can't make you give up hope on those that you can!

    Say you have 7 subjects and only 5 you can do well. Then, concentrate
    on those 5. But don't drop the two most important subjects - english, chinese.
    Next is maths, science, economics. I say that because I was science student
    and I hated useless geography, history, literature. But if you're into the arts and fashion, modelling, english, chinese, literature, maths, economics look good.

    In life, we can pressure ourselves to quit (because we're scared we're going to fail) but there's the other equal 50% chance that if we pluck our confidence and guts to work hard and go for it, you'll be surprised at what we can achieve.

    Never say quit. Never say die. Those who succeed are often those who focus and seize 100% chance of their confidence, guts, determination to succeed. There is no room/chance to worry before the war. Worry only for the results after the war is fought. Just make sure you're determined to win and you give your very best in the preparation and during the fight.

    And if you do your Os well enough, consider taking 1 year break from school and try working in an area you've always wanted. Go back to school 1 year
    later. You'll be stronger and more matured to handle your further studies.
