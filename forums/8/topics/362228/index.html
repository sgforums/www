    Even if you don’t have cable TV, you’ll probably know the names of Aska Yang, Yoga Lin and Jam Hsiao or even bought one of their albums. They are all alumni of Taiwan’s version of American Idol, the singing contest and reality competition known as “One Million Star”

    Now StarhubTV, which broadcast One Million Star regularly on it’s E City channel is organizing a contest to find the best singers from Singapore to PK Challenge the contestants in Season 5 of the show in Taiwan.

    PK Challenge is a exciting part of the contest where newcomers can challenge the remaining contestants midway into the show, take over their place and eliminate them if they prove to be a better contestant during a direct one on one singing challenge.

    Wow, another singing contest along with Singapore Idol, this time the winner might become famous in Asia region because the show is broadcast in many countries. Who will represent Singapore and PK the Taiwan contestants?
