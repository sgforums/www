    Which brings to mind this concept of '5 Levels of Communications' which I read abt a long time ago:

    Level 1: Casual Acquaintances
    At this level, you are only saying "Hi"s and "Bye"s and the occasional smile to the person. Nothing further.

    Level 2: Colleagues
    At this level, you get together and begin to exchange information abt a certain topic. For example, may be work-related or abt a common hobby. However, the info exchanged remains strictly factual.

    Level 3: Good Friends
    At this level, you begin to share your personal opinions on some things. Eg. 'I feel his action is simply irresponsible.' etc etc etc. You let the other party know your point of view on things (willing to risk a challenge by the other party abt how u feel).

    Level 4: Close Buddies
    At this level, emotions are involved. You are willing to let the person understand your emotional side. Topics may be of a more personal nature. Eg. "My sis simply dun listen to my mum's advice to quit smoking. Sad man..."

    Level 5: Intimate Companions
    At this level, some secrets are openly shared between you and your partner. There is no inhibition nor restraint when toking about any aspect of personal life. Much like soulmates for life.

    Understanding these 5 levels will let you have a better understanding of the kind of relationship u wanna maintain with each person.
