    SINGAPORE : Power supply has been fully restored after a 45-minute blackout hit many parts of Singapore on Thursday afternoon.

    The Energy Market Authority said the areas affected included Bukit Batok, Chua Chu Kang New Town, Crawford, Jalan Eunos, Mandai, Pandan and SengKang.

    It said the power supply was disrupted at 3.55pm due to the tripping of two power generation plants at the Senoko Power Station.

    It was fully restored by 4.40pm.

    Callers to the MediaCorp News hotline said traffic lights in the areas were also affected. - CNA /ls
