    I started with G,C and F. Get the chord from the internet. It's very easy, just type in "Guitar chords" or "basic guitar chords".

    Example: G chord ------------ the numbers=the fret you put ur finger on
    E----3--- B-------
    G--------
    D--------
    A----2---
    E---3----
    Strum and hold the chord.. make sure you get a clear sound from each string.. Try pressing a little harder if you can't get the sound.

    After you can remember this chord learn another maybe a C.. do the same thing and when you can remember this chord try swtich between the 2 chords. Then try the F chord.

    Once you learn the 3 chords you can play a song.
