    Originally posted by Me0w Me0w:
    price not problem....
    Try visiting
    http://www.femalebridesonline.com/main.php
    Lotsa info there. Can even take part in their forum where all the brides-to-be like to share information with one another, such as which is the best bridal shop, where to get wedding cakes etc.

    Also visit this
    http://www.singaporebrides.com.sg/

    Do you want a lot of pictures? Do you like to have a lot of nice pictures? Whichever bridal shop you go, try to bargain for more pictures. They may have a lot of catch like... give you more choices of traditional outfits for photo taking, give you additional evening outfit for photo taking, more locations for outdoor photography etc. All these sound attractive.

    No matter what... you must remember to bargain for more pictures. The standard is 15 to 20 pictures per bridal package. You may think this is enough. But actually it is not. The photographers will actually snap more than 100 photos for you, but if the package only includes 15 pictures (for example), you can only choose 15 photos out of the 100 that were taken to be put in your album.

    The problem is: usually after taking the photos, you will find all the photos very nice (cuz most photographers are very skilful and professional). Then they will persuade you to develop more photos by paying another few thousand dollars (on top of the few thousand dollars that you have already paid for the package).

    As for which bridal shop is the best, depending on what you want. (ie. do you emphasize more on the gown design or the photo?)

    If you want good photos, you may want to try Dang Studio. The photographer they use is Andrew Choi (one of the most famous photographers for bridal photos).

    If you want nice gowns, try to go for Made-To-Measure kind of package. Then browse through all the bridal magazines to find the design that you like most. Show it to the bridal shop designer. You can find many nice designs in this www.theknot.com

    Let me know what else you want to find out.
