    I was astonished having readth the above-mentioned ST 29-May 2009 and here's my views.

    Certainly made to look stupid of our ministries having to take some french-fries from some distant experts rocking comfortably at their own office.

    Mannn..., what we need is to apprehend all Fliers distributors and procurers of fliers.

    " Second-grade to Ah-Long "  They are everywhere and everyday.  Invasions of letter-box, spruced on the floor mixed with damp drains, stuck at your door posts or grills and worst not, dumped into opened doors and windows.

    How many trees have gone to support the crimes of this so-called business glimicks? since our nations opts for green enviroments.  

    Its not all right, if business-leaders insisted this's a free-world business and enterprisings. With those disgusting adulterated photographs of house agents sickens to the core beyond repairs. Why wouldn't they sell their house as well as their parent's house instead of conning others?

    The government should have already looked into those obvious Fliers-issues seriously by now instead of having an alien, who doesn't eat char-kuay-teow yet telling us law a must to curb litterings.    

    Failing to awakes our cash-box and coozy lazyTown-Councils to apprehand all this fliers-nuisance whom had done numerous distructions to our letter-box with their poky chop-stick tactics.  

    Can the law do something about this? rather than aiming the barrels at poor smokers who, had contributed painfully and forcefully to our country's economy and a also a generous tax-payers to our lovely IRAS.
