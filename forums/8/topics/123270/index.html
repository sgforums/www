    * Business Profile (Business)
    For Sole Proprietorships/Partnerships

    Details in Report
    o Name of business firm, registration no., date of change of name
    o Commencement date, renewal date, expiry date
    o Place of business, date of change of address, status and effective date of status
    o Principal activity(ies) and particulars of manager(s), existing owner(s), withdrawn owner(s) and branch(es)
