    For those of you who are using ADSL connection, are you aware that in the event that you wish to terminate the ADSL plan, you need to pay a termination fee of $40+?

    They call this the 3rd party (which is singtel) charge. This is because to have ADSL in the phone line, Singtel needs to activate a special line called the B-Access. In the event that you don't need the ADSL anymore, the service provider will inform singtel to disable the B access and singtel will charge the service provider for this, which the service provider will charge you.
