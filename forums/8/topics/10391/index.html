A cold and dark Saturday

    A cold, dreary Saturday night....

    A guy sits alone at the bar.....staring at a glass, wondering about the direction of life.

    Just when he is about to go, braving the pouring rain, she steps into the bar.

    His eyes flick open from their original half-closed state. As she walks past...he can sense the confidence exuding from her.

    As she glides past....he catches a whiff of her scent....it is feminine and distinct, enough to invoke many memories..but at the same time is not overpowering.

    Large, expressive eyes and warm luscious lips....he is totally mesmerized as she sips at her light drink.

    Digging his hands into his black leather jacket, he reaches into a pocket and retrieves a handphone. He types some words in.

    Gathering his confidence, he gets up and strides towards her table. His shoulders are straight, his footing is solid. A little bit of stubble adds to the manly touch.

    Her eyes slowly take into focus this man. She notices his broad shoulders, his short tousled hair, his prominent jawline. The way he walks indicate he is a man sure of himself.

    She notices the light in his eyes, the way his lips curl up, his strong aftershave, and takes in the manly and authorative scent.

    He leans over and whispers in her ear, "Have a look at this." Passes her his handphone.

    She looks at the screen....and can't help laughing.

    On the screen is a sleeping pig....with the words..."hand drawn and 100% pure originals".
