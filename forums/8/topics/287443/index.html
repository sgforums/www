    PAYM Policy Forum Series - Can I afford to retire in Singapore?

    Concerned about the latest CPF reforms?
    Considering early retirement? Retirement planning for your parents?

    The Bishan-Toa Payoh CC Youth Executive Committees have come together to organise a policy forum titled "Can I afford to retire in Singapore?".

    The forum will be chaired by Mr Hri Kumar Nair, Member of Parliament for Bishan-Toa Payoh GRC, Board Member of People's Association and Panel of Advisers to the PAYM Central Youth Council. Mr Nair will share his insights on the current CPF measures to help Singaporeans be retirement-ready.

    Joining Mr Nair will be long time veteran in the social service scene, Mr Gerard Ee, who is the Chairman of Council for Third Age (C3A). Hear Mr Ee speak on schemes and programmes which C3A offers to senior citizens to lead a meaningful life after retirement.

    Come also to learn ways on how to plan for retirement and help enrich the lives of your parents and seniors around you. Mr Chua Chew Yong, 69, a retiree and an active senior citizen, will also share his experience in planning for a meaningful golden age.

    You are cordially invited to participate in the forum and share your thoughts!
