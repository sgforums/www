    reptile.. the crocodile hotpot



    Ingredients :

    Crocodile rib
    Tomato /Ketchup
    Chicken stock
    Light soy sauce
    Sugar
    Salt
    Ginger
    Corn flour
    Cherry liquor

    Directions :

    1. Pour the chicken stock into a pot, add ginger and rib, place the pot on low heat.
    2. Simmer until the ribs are tender, season with light soy sauce, tomato ketchup, salt and cherry liquor.
    3. Dilute corn flour in water and pour in stock. Stir in for 2 minutes and then serve.
