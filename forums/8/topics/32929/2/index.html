    If it is only a textbook, then jumbo box cannot. too big. Jumbo box is by DHL. If you want to send to US, UPS is a good choice but if they collect from your home, there is a surcharge. DHL may be a good choice and so is FedEx. They both are about of the same rates. If the delivery address is a outskirt of the city area, then FedEx charges more for delivery to outskirt area using 3-party courier company. DHL is more reasonable in such way then. You can use your own packing. The rates is by volume metric.

    Try not to use SingPost (Speedpost). It may be slightly cheaper but when they deliver until US, it is being delivered by the post office in US.
