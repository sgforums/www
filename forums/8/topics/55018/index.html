    News Report - http://www.channelnewsasia.com/stories/singaporelocalnews/view/46924/1/.html

    A train had stalled just south of Outram Park station at about 2:30pm due to an electrical fault.

    But services along the rest of the NEL line went on smoothly.

    SBS Transit said about 60 passengers alighted at Outram Park after attempts to push the stalled train to HarbourFront Station failed.

    Shuttle services were provided till normal train services resumed at 4pm.
