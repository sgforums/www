Man robs Joo Chiat bank with kitchen knife

  • octega
    16 Oct 04, 23:50


    ARMED with just a kitchen knife, a man ran into Hong Leong Finance in Joo Chiat on Friday and made off with about S$10,000.

    The robber is still at large, but it is understood that the police have recovered footage from a closed-circuit security camera, which captured the robbery.
    Advertisement

    He was wearing a cap and a short-sleeved polo shirt. He was also wearing a glove on his left hand and wielded the knife in his right.

    At about 3.35pm, he ran to the bank counter and leapt over it the moment he entered. Shouting 'Robbery!' in Hokkien, he forced open one of the drawers below the counter, and grabbed a wad of cash before leaping over the counter again and running out. The robbery was over within a minute.

    The five employees who were in the bank at that time were unhurt. Scores of policemen turned up at the scene and searched the vicinity for the robber, but he is still at large.

    The police are looking for witnesses. Anyone with information should contact them on 1800-255-0000.
