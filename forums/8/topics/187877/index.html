    It turned out that the facts are as follows. A Shanghaines women was talking to a man. The man went to his car. The car moved off very fast with the woman clinging on to the door on the driver's side.

    She said that her clothes was clamped on the door.

    A witness sitting at the coffee shop said as follows:

    "I saw the woman running next to the car and the car door suddenly open and the woman fly and roll on the road a few times. The car then stop at a short distance and then drove off."

    The woman's sister also from Shanghai said that they have the driver's hand phone number.

    All this time the woman was sitting on the road. She had a short mini skirt made of jeans material on and a light coloured spagetti strap blouse.

    When the Police finally came after a call was made to 999; it was a policeman on a motor bike... traffic police.

    He asked the sister to contact the man on the phone. She did and the man over the phone asked her why he must come back to the accident scene.

    She passed the HP to the cop but he was busy on his walkie takie. By the time he tried to talk to the man, he had hung up.

    When the victim's sister called the man again, his mobile was off.

    It seemed as if the woman's left leg was dislocated at the knee as the people there could not move her even to sit on a chair and she remained on the road.

    When the ambulance came and tried to move her, she started crying. The paramedics asked if she lost consciousness and asked the witnesses what happened.

    She was taken to the hospital and her sister went with her in the ambulance.
