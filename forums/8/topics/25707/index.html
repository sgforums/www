  • dr00|
    2 Sep 02, 09:45
    Do u think that different dialect group of ppl lives r normally concentrated in different areas? For example: Most Cantonese and Hakkas either lives in the Central or western side, seldom on the east, while most Teochew r living in Hougang area. What dialect r u, and what do u think?
  • sohguanh
    2 Sep 02, 09:54
    I think this has to do with some history. Back before HDB start building flats, most people living in village/kampong pertaining to their own dialect groups clan? So when HDB start to chase people into the flats, most of the same dialect people move into those flats. Hence we could see a 'sort of' dialect group prominent in that areas?

    As time goes by, those early people have kids and they grow up marry and start to move out from that area, dialect presence begin not to be so obvious. However, roots run deep and some parents prefer their sons/daughters to live close to each other. Hence in another way, the dialect grouping is still there.

    AFAIK, from my era onwards, most married who get new flats are assigned Sengkang, Punggol, Sembawang, Admiralty etc. I think the dialect grouping is getting less concentrated. Of course, new era people trained in English,Mandarin courtesy of our education system, some may just forget their dialect language already.
