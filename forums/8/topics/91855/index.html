    "How far do you agree that the motives of the 2 superpowers in intervening in the various arab israeli conflicts in the period 1956-1989 were essentially the same? (A levels october november 2003)"

    1. Suez Crisis
    2. 6-day War
    3. Yom Kippur war

    Suez Crisis: Nasser does arms trade with USSR's Czeckoslovakia, Americans pissed off, withdraw 40 million $ Aswan dam project. Nasser pissed off coz he has no money, nationalises the Suez Canal (promising Brits and French compensation). Brits and French pissed off, secretly plot with Israelites to attack Sinai; Israel invades while Brits and French move in under the pretext of "defending the Suez from fighting". Brits, French and Israelites successful, but USA + USSR + world condemns the attack in UN, so this is an intervention. I.e. WHat were their motives?

    Yom Kippur War.
    New egyptian president Saddat feels like spilling more Israeli blood, encouraged by PLO's pleas of anti-israel. Moves forces to Sinai's borders. Lebanon, Jordan and Syria also mobilise invasion forces. Israelis get pissed off and launch preemptive strike and WIN more territories. USA acts as mediator and brings PLO and Israel to negotiating table. USSR is not involved again.

    So i am like "wtf" to the qn
