    have to warn you guys.

    becareful if you order items with big packaging.

    i ordered a 3.3kg item which was a $135 anti glare filter.

    vpost wanted to charge me close to $140 for the freight.

    they based their charges on the higher of weight or "volume weight".

     

    they use the formula of packaging as volume weight

    [length  X  width  X  height (cm)] / 6000

     

    my package which was 61cm X 44cm X 31cm / 6000

    ended up with a volume weight of 13.9 kg, despite only weighing only 3.3 kg.

    shipping cost ended up to around SG$131.59 and my item only cost US$95.
