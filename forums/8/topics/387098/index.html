    Resorts World Sentosa IR Will First Open on 20 Jan 2010

    Resorts World Sentosa (RWS) will be the first Integrated Resort (IR) to open in Singapore as they announced in their latest press release that they will be opened on 20 January 2010 (Wednesday). As part of their promised phased opening, the Resorts World Sentosa will open its doors beginning with four of their hotels.

He claimed that they have been single-minded about their aim and did not have have to deal with any distractions or make any excuses, in a not so subtle reference to Marina Bay Sands delayed opening.
