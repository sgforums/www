    Lets not say COE and other blah blah blah...

    Juz consider monthly expenses:

    Season parking : $65/mth

    ERP: $4/day (approx) = $80/mth

    Petrol: for $1.9/litre of petrol = $160 /mth (light travelling and to and fro work)

    Parking: In Orchard, HDB carpark $2/hr..if u go shopping+ movies, approx $10 for 5 hrs. So 1 mth, 4 times = $40/mth

    so 65+80+160+40 = $350 (min)

    If you take car loan, paying $400 monthly installments
    Factor in those occasion parking , erp summons etc..

    Monthly, u have to pay $750(min)

    Other expenses:

    Road tax $400/6 mths
    Insurance $800/yr
    Servicing, maintainance: $800/yr
    Occasional paintjobs $100 - $150/visit

    No doubt fuel price gonna increase again...

    If MRT,

    1 trip max $2..
    Give you 1 day, 2 trips + bus transfers = $6/day
    30 days = $180

    Occasionally late night movie+ suppers, nightrider $3/trip or at most cab mid-nite = $40. 4 times per month, $160

    So total, $180 + $160 = $340 (avg)

    U still can save $410 per mth.

    That's for my comparison...dunno abt others...cheers
