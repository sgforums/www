    The discussion about Linghu Chong and Dugu 9 Jian can get pretty interesting, when we talk about the idea of form vs. no form.

    Think for a moment. Dugu Qiubai mentioned wu2 jian4 sheng4 you3 jian1. Compare this with the Taichi sword, which is supposedly also no form! Who will win? In Xiao Ao Jiang Hu, we read of Priest Chongxu, who was from .. tata .. Wudang, wielding the Taichi sword.

    Also note that Dugu 9 Jian is about exploiting the enemy's weakness. In the case of Dongfang Bubai, the guy was so fast that Linghu Chong couldn't react fast enough to see the loopholes.

    There is a stance in the 9 Jian called po4 qi4 shi4 (break energy stance?). Think for a moment. Can Linghu Chong defeat Duan Yu shooting "lasers" with his 6 Mai Sheng Jian?
