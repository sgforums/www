    I agree! The shows you list appeal to a great portion of the population, young and old.

    However, the feedback from the industry is that a local animated sitcom will not be succesful because the majority of (adult) Singaporeans feel animation is for kids and these viewers will never accept a Simpsons or Family guy-type show featuring local characters.

    I think the industry feels it would only make sense to make a show if it appeals to the majority of viewers, young and old, all races, lower-, middle- and high-income groups.

    My conviction is that a sitcom that looks like South Park and plays like the Simpsons will get good ratings. I hope to prove it by getting people excited and expressing their interest in seeing made and aired in Singapore.
