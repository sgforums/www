    This is a tough topic, because we are talking about things that cannot be experienced through the 5 Senses of the world population (meaning that one person may have an experience and others may believe or call him crazy, or people of one religion have a certain belief about them, but people of another religion will believe differently - but each will swear that their beliefs are the real truth).

    Here are some general references from the Wikipedia.

    SOUL
    http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soul

    SPIRIT
    http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spirit

    GHOST
    http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ghost

    ETHERAL BEING
    Should be another E in there. "Ethereal". Also known as "AEthereal".
    Technically, an ethereal being would be any of the above three, but is usually not associated with the word "soul".

    Spirit & Ghost are similar. People usually use the word Ghost to mean a harmful or naughty Spirit. A ghost is always a spirit, but a spirit is not always a ghost. A ghost is generally thought of as the soul or spirit of a person that was once living, now deceased, but not at rest.

    A Spirit is a non-physical entity or ethereal being, etc. It does not have to be from a "person" that was once living.

    There are many more terms and words that can be used for a lot of different things - it's quite a big subject.
