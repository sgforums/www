DEAD BODY - The Poem

  • Devil1976
    29 Aug 03, 12:56
    I hang on to this dead body...
    Regardless of my rights....
    Regardless of my senses,
    the thing which made me bright...

    The body which has once given me heat...
    The indication of life....
    The touch of coldness now... Sends shivers up my spine...
    The feelings, the emotions...
    The memorial ties....

    The body which has once took the truth,
    spoken me lies....
    The thing... My mind....
    Empty...

    Numbness within me....
    Dead body.
