    Envy?

    I seldom envy other people. Why think so much? I don't have that kind of energy to envy. If they are successful, that's good for them. What's the point and why I envy other people for?

    I envy people who are born into a royalty/ good family/ good environment.

    Can you choose to be the son or daughter of a president? Of a tycoon?

    I know deep down this is the only thing I cant change, nor can anybody else change theirs, and so, for me this is the only thing which truly separates whether somebody is luckier and thus more enviable.
