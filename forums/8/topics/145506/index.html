    I don't really see the fuss and the need for corporates to be 'charitable'.

    Any profits they make will ultimately go to the shareholders. Hence, at the end of the day, it's more logical and reasonable for these people to donate at their own goodwill.

    By having corporates to donate, these shareholders are indirectly being forced to donate also - not exactly that all would agree to such donations being forced unto them.

    Of course, with such, only then does it make logical sense for corporates to donate if it means they could use them as a form of marketing or tax avoidance.
