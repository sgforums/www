    Fortune telling implies a pre-determined destiny, and I am not to subscribe to the hypothesis that one's destiny can be determined by a few criss-crossed lines on his palm. However, the danger is there - some of these predictions may actually become self-fulfilling prophecies when you start to live your life under their influence.

    Even if these predictions were true, knowing what will happen to you in the future would only unnecessarily burden you with worries over a fate that you cannot avoid.
