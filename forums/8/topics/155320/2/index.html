    I am not really for this system. 'O' levels is a benchmark recognised by all commonwealth countries, in other words, there is no basis for one to progress to a Polytechnic if you haven't hit the benchmark.

    What if the student fails his/her Polytechnic education? It is not unusual. And so what is the student left with? PSLE?

    Regarding the point about Polytechnics not having enough students to fill the intake, thats obviously off the mark. The numbers show otherwise.

    Even ITEs require a certain level of proficiency in 'N' subjects before allowing entry. There must be a check and balance mechanism built into this whole scheme to ensure not everyone cruises into a Polytechnic easily.
