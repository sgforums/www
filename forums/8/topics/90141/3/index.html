    Hey hey people, cool it.

    What's the best among the 3 uni in Singapore? Like some have pointed out, each has their strengths. In Singapore, as least we all know that all 3 are recognised and branded in a way so there's no issue of doing a degree from some koyak, lousy nameless Uni that nobody recognises or accredits.

    Check out what you wanna do for starters.

    NUS is a comprehensive Uni - meaning got all the faculties like engin, science, arts, medicine etc.

    Programs I feel are more general - eg. NUS business admin more general compared to so called specialised business degrees in NTU.

    NTU so far is a technical uni hence the name technological. PRograms offered are more technical and specialised. That's why you got the Mass Comm, Engin and the business programs are specialised a particular field eg. Accounting, Marketing etc.

    BUT - NTU is now moving towards being a comprehensive Uni like NUS - New faculties have been set up - now they have biology, arts and humanities, media and design and of cos later on, even the sciences (chem, physics, maths)

    I don't think NTU will have medicine ... not that I know of anyway.

    Regardless, I think our education system really gearing towards the US model - note the various collaboration with numerous top US Uni (applicable to NUS and NTU) Even SMU works with Wharton business school in US.

    SMU new kid on the block - hard to determine their worth but judging from the raves review, they don't sound too bad. They speak of students being more outspoken.... take it a pinch of salt... this could be due smaller enrolment numbers making it easier for such interaction or maybe they are really just good. Only time can tell.

    Hope this sheds some light. If you wan more info, you got to be more specific about your interest ie courses etc. cos I don't want to bore others with my long long message.

    cheers
