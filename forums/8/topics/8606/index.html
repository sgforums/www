    Dear Purr and Callan,

    After going through the two ends of the spectrum in morality, I learned that the world is just not black or white, but shades of grey. I know that a few years back, after a fallen from grace. Good and Bad are merely labels, since on a superficial level, morality is a code which is conditioned by the society at that particular point of era.

    nowadays, there exist so many conceited people who go about in society, pretending to be good, who are in fact naive and tedious. I begin to think it shows rather a sweet and modest disposition to pretend to be bad. I would not say that I am a decent guy, since I have been bad before. I would just say that I am a simple person with so and so values, and not morals. If you pretend to be good, the world takes you very seriously, and once you fall, you are condemned forever. If you pretend to be bad, it doesn't. Such is the astounding stupidity of optimism. I am afraid that good people do a lot of harm in this world by labeling evil to such extraordinary importance. It is nonsensical to split people between good and bad. People are either charming or tedious.

    I think I have not made clear what I meant by "nice" and "jerk" so far. Well, I mean a guy who is "nice", is an altruistic person who cares for the other people, and do things that helps other people without expectation and a jerk is a selfish guy who cares for himself and not for the people around him. It is a relative concept.

    Have a good day.
