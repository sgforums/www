    Interesting point.

    Singaporean society is not one that accepts failures. Taking a historical perspective, being a small island with nothing going for it expect its people and good geographic location set the path that Singapore is currently upon. The lack of a social welfare system has driven that point home further - if you are out of a job, you are on your own. Not that I am advocating a dole system in Singapore - the system has major drawbacks. However, it is in the national midset that failure is diametrically opposed to survival, rather than to success.
