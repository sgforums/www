    National Education Show: Saturday 17 July 2004
    NDP Preview: Saturday 31 July 2004

    Roads Closure

    Stadium Road, Stadium Drive, Stadium Boulevard, Stadium Walk and Stadium Crescent will be closed to traffic from 2.00pm to 10.30pm on these 2 days and on National Day itself. In addition, Mountbatten Road between Guillemard Road/Nicoll Highway and Old Airport Road/Stadium Boulevard will be closed to traffic from 7.45pm to 8.30pm to facilitate fireworks display.

    Bus Services Diversion

    Bus services 16, 401 and 608 will be diverted to ply Mountbatten Road from 2pm to 7.45pm and from 8.30pm to 10.30pm. Bus services 12, 14, 158, 196, 401 and 608 will be diverted to ply Guillemard Road, Tanjong Katong Road, Dunman Road and Old Airport Road from 7.45pm to 8.30pm. Bus services 10, 16, 31, 32 and 33 will skip Old Airport Road and Dunman Road, and will be diverted to ply Guillemard Road and Tanjong Katong Road from 7.45pm to 8.30pm. Bus service 11 will not operates from 2pm to 10.30pm.
