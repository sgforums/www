    2 Feb 06, 13:15
    Originally posted by tt3:
    dun keep them... die veri easily...
    Yes and no to that

    I wouldn't recommend rays to beginners because they will just kill them unintentionally.

    They are sensitive fishes so you have to ensure the water temp, pH are kept at the correct levels and really watch the nitrate level as well.

    Also, they are sensitive to alot of aquarium chemicals out there. Small doses can kill them. There are only a few items out there that can be used.

    They can tolerate a little salt but please don't use too much and dissolve the salt before pouring it in because the salt crystals can 'burn' the ray.
