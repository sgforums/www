    Before I started any of the industrial attachments I did while at university, we had to sign a placement contract, which stipulated exactly what we were expected to deliver to the organisation and vice versa. The contracts always contained an exit clause in the event that obligations were not being fulfilled.

Personally, I figure market rate would have been between $5500-$6000 a month, but what the hell, I was short on cash at the time.
