    take me away from this world of dreams,
    lead me to where my heart would follow,
    for there is no hope in dreams with a heart so empty.

    if this is not the end of oblivion,
    then i shall live everyday,
    as if my life would end on this very day.

    losing myself to the endless time currents,
    drifting further away from the world of reality,
    despair is all i can seek comfort in.

    darkened may my heart be,
    yet flames arise within the burning ashes,
    with an impure heart i have, risen again have i...

    guide me,
    embrace me,
    take me away on those angel wings of yours,

    there is only solitude in the love of your warm and comforting heart...
