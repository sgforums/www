  • orange
    4 Jun 00, 12:44
    I wonder if any of you have had this experience before. Today, I ordered some chicken rice at my neighbourhood coffee shop. I've always patronised this stall because of its delicious food. While I was waiting for my food, I noticed that the hawker blew his nose into his right hand, gave it a cursory wipe on the tablecloth nest to him and continued chopping the chicken I ordered. I wanted to cancel my order there and then but he was holding the chopper in a menacing manner and I did not want to find out what he would do with it if I were to cancel my order. Needless to say, I lost my appetite after that.
    Another time, I was having a meal of vegetable rice when I bit on something hard. On extricating the offending morsel of food, I found a rusty 2-inch nail.
    Do you think such "extra ingredients" add extra taste to the food. Any similar experiences to share? Any interesting items, e.g. cockcroaches found in any of your meals before?
