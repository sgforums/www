    ccas can really enrich your life if you join the right one.

    you can make friends who can help you with your studies. the people in your cca will not be in the same class as you and you guys can share notes. these friends can also be your pillar of support in times of need. ccas can definitely open up new opportunities for you to meet new people which will make your life in school more interesting.

    there are also many other skills you can learn from joining a variety of ccas, be it sports, performing arts or clubs and societies. there is always something you will learn from joining a cca or two.

    but if you join the one not suitable for you, all you get is unhappiness and stress. it boils down to your personal preferences and whether you can cope with your ccas or not.
