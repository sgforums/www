    "Overall a great feature-packed phone"

    Pros and cons:

    The pros:

    - 65k-color TFT is absolutely brilliant.
    - The resolution for the built-in camera may not be great, but it is decent and enough for everyday use (MMS, email, background... etc.).
    - Decent battery life considering that it has a TFT screen. The phone lasted me around 3.5 days on heavy usage (playing with camera, games, MMS and WAP).
    - Light, beautiful phone design. The self mirror is a great addition.
    - Easy to use

    The cons:

    - Caller display/profiles can only be used if the numbers are stored in the phone memory, and cannot be used with the numbers in SIM memory.
    - The phone hung on me twice already while browsing via WAP. "Hung" meaning I had to take the battery off.
    - Each IrDA connection doesn't last very long so you have to be really quick when transferring files from the PC.
    - Menu response a little slow.
    - Lack of games.
    - No Java or WAP 2.0 support.
    - Cannot pick up/hang up calls with the flip. Must press ANSWER button and END CALL button.
    - Cannot reject unwanted calls without flipping the phone open and pressing END CALL button.
    I am being a bit picky with the cons, but overall the phone is tremendous! Just to let you guys know that the phone memory is actually a lot more than 650kb. It is more around 1,630kb.
