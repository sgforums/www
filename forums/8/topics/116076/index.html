    *Free outgoing talktime is valid for use at any time of the day. Maximum outgoing talktime represented is calculated based on prevailing Super Off-peak rates only. Peak: 9am-9pm (daily) 21cts/min. Super Off-peak: 9pm-9am (daily) 10.5cts/min.
