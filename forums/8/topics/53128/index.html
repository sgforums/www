    SEEMINGLY quite an interesting horror.... The plot seems rather confusing from the beginning but actually it's rather straightforward and SIMPLE... Think the director's just trying to place things in such a way that it might seems mysterious and perhaps a little 'deep'...? Bogus....

    Personally I don't find the plot very interesting.. If you're just in for some 'frights' without bothering about what the he[/i]ll is going on.... Can very well go for it......
