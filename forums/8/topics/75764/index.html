    go to picture you want in any website (must be freely available in internet)

    rightclick (if you're using MSIE) on it and choose "properties"

    then see the "address (URL)"? highlight and copy that...

    whack it in the box... then highlight it and click the "Img" button on the top of the box

    click on Preview button at the bottom to check...

    if okay, hit Submit

    voila!! done!

    p.s. colour words: highlight the words you want coloured, then click on the "font colour" and choose the colour.. that's all!
