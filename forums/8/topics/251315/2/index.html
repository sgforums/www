    hi all,

    i'm new to sgforums.com. But i would like to share a thought or two with everyone. the parking lot which is behind the kuanyin temple at waterloo st is free on weekend. But on any other day you need to put a coupon to be safe. well, that area is frequent with carpark warden. So just be more carefull next time. But most of the time, churches and mosques will apply to URA or TP for parking space which is outside the church/mosque or near the residential area. Like for example, I was delivering cake to one of the church in bedok, if i'm not mistaken, the address is 82 bedok road. I notice on the church noticeboard, an application for parking space outside the church to TP. The approval from Tp was also pasted. The church/mosque will indicate the distance from which lamp post till which lamp post. The tp will decide whether to approve . Things to consider would be the safety of the people on the road and make sure there is no complain. The church or mosque people have taken this initiave to apply for the carpark as they know that they will be many people coming to church on sunday and mosque on friday. By the way, the approval will usually take about 2 weeks.
