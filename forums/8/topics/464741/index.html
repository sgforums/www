    Hi! It may be a good idea 2 or more pairs of leather shoes that are resolable. Also get sizes with more volume to accomodate insoles with sufficient cushioning you require. Also buy shoe trees to go with them. Leather shoes breathes well, it also stretches and mould to your feet as you wear them, making the more comfortable. Having more than one pair allows the shoes rest so that the uppers do not wear out prematurely. Shoe trees keeps the shoe in tip top shape. Find shoes with leather outsoles that are stitched so it may topied with rubbers soles and heels. Stitched soles are more durable than glued soles. Instead of changing shoes, changing out soles and heels make better econimic sense in a long run. Shoe insoles you can choose leather, eva, sorbothane, polyurethane or silicone types. Some may have rocker or heel and arc supports, improving gait and evening wear patterns. Lastly get a shoe brush with shoe conditioner and wax to keep the shoe looking new! There are some people who claim to have worn the same pairs of shoes for 20 over years!
