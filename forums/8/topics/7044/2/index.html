    Ok, my turn (finally !! Saving the best for last hehe)

    I have not carefully read all the posts in this thread so if i do repeat some points you guys have already brought up, please bear with me.

    This topic is difficult to present in a clear way ... let me try ....

    Sexual Infidelity

    This is about having a sexual relationship with someone other than your loved one (stead or spouse).

    Humans have sexual needs. Some people can control their sexual needs well but some can't.

    those who can control their needs

    people who have the strength of character will not engage in sexual infidelity even when seduced. They can resist temptations even if their spouse are not able to satisfy them. Their sense of responsibilty is stronger than their sexual desires.

    those who cannot control themselves

    These people do not have a strong strength of character. They will either look for sex voluntarily (look for prostitutes or go for one night stands) or give in to seduction and temptations (motivated).

    Hence there is a need to understand the cause of his infidelity. Is it a "voluntary" act or a "motivated" one ? I will say that the former cause more hurt than the latter.

    Emotional Infidelity

    colette has mentioned the Net as a possible medium of emotional infidelity. And there was once a case in Singapore where a husband had sued his wife for having extra-marrital affairs with a person on the Net. The Net is truly a dangerous place ! Perhaps there may be "virtual mistresses" in the future ! And the wife will not go to the mistress' house with brooms but she will send trojans and viruses to the mistress, hehehe :p

    Emotional infidelity is much more complex then Sexual Infidelity because one cannot be "seduced" or "tempted" to betray his loved one emotionally.

    As a result, emotional infidelity is more of a "volunteered" one. While sexual infidelity can be more of a "motivated" one (unless the person goes around asking for sex or look for prostitutes, then it is definitely a "volunteered" one)

    Hence, it is more difficult for a someone to engage in emotional infidelity than sexual infidelity.

    Moreover, an emotional relationship is more difficult to start and maintain than a pure sexual one as the latter has more direct and "tangible" benefits.

    In addition, a pure sexual relationship is easy to break up since it is a "physical" relationship. As long as the 2 parties are not going out together anymore, they cannot commit to sexual acts. However, an emotional relationship is more difficult to resolve. How many times have you heard of a person still missing his ex even after years ? Physical separation does not ensure the end of the relationship and worse still, one can never be sure when the relationship will end, not even the offender himself.

    When it comes to the "trust" issue, emotional infidelity will definitely result in a greater loss of "trust" than a sexual one.

    let me give you 2 examples :

    example 1:
    When your loved one is by your side physically, you know he can't commit sexual infidelity (cos he is beside you) however, you can't be sure if he is thinking of someone else.

    example 2:
    When your loved one is making love to you, you also can't be sure if he is actually thinking about another person. Perhaps he is imagining you as another person.

    This 2 examples illustrates why emotional infidelity will result in a greater loss of trust.

    conclusion

    I will think that emotional infidelity will incur more hurt because infidelity is all about the betrayal of "trust". Since emotional infidelity will lead to a greater loss of "trust", it will incur more pain and do more harm to the relationship.

    I hope I have made my post clear to all
