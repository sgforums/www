    i have 2 bank accounts, one for expenditure and one for savings. i used to have 1 bank account but i feel it is not good enough cos i cant see the money saved monthly. besides it is very easy to swipe the card away without realising that you have overspent and used your savings to pay for the purchases.

    so i got my 2nd bank account and had my salary credited to this account to be used for expenditures. i set a monthly fund transfer arrangement to transfer my savings to my 1st savings account. this way you force yourself to save the money once you get your salary. usually you have to keep min $500 for the expenditure account to prevent the $2 deduction so bear in mind not to spend til there is less than $500. calculate beforehand the savings that you can really save so that you wont be dry before your next salary comes and end up withdrawing your savings.
