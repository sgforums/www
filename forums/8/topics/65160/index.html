    ok, for easier reading, i have decided to consolidate all the countdowns in this post..

    1) The Nation's Countdown 2004 - Sentosa, Tanjong Beach
    12 hrs of of live performances, songs and dance sequences, foam party and an fireworks at midnite!

    Adult - $26
    Price includes Island admission, re-entries into event & 1 drink.

    2) Paradise Countdown 2004 - Suntec
    Some lighting effects, balloons, dancers in costumes, DJ Steve Lawler, DJ Dave Angel...

    Presale (8 to 30 December) - $35
    At The Door (31 December) - $45

    3) Ministry of Sound Essential NYE 2004 - Singapore Expo
    Superstars DJ like Dave Seaman!, Darren Emerson, Lisa Loud, Leeroy Thornhill...
    Supporting acts like fire-eater, jugglers, break-dancers, percussionists, stilt-walkers, etc.

    Pre-sale tix - $35
    Day-sale tix - $45
    VIP - $80

    For a smaller scale countdown,

    4) Downtown East - D'Marquee
    Not really sure of the programs, should be able to see some local stars.

    Showtime: 8.30pm - 12am
    Dance Party: 12am - 4am
    Admission: FREE

    5) PartyX - Boon Lay Place
    Cheerdance Competition, Live rock bands, Foam party, Rocket Pyrotechnics.
    Free shuttle bus from Boon Lay MRT. starts from 630.

    6) Countdown - Padang
    Stars include Alex To, Landy; eye candy, Cyndi Wang; and local talents Rui En & Dreamz FM. 8pm to 2am

    Tix: $33, $19 and $13-sold out

    7) The Arena - Raffles The Plaza Ballroom, Level 4 Raffles City Convention Centre
    Featuring DJ Leomeo, DJ Sassy and DJ Lisa.

    Arena Early Bird - $59 (includes 2 beers/wine, 1 shooter)
    Arena - $79 (includes 2 beers/wine, 1 shooter)
    VIP - $195 (includes 2 beers/wine, 1 shooter and one champagne)

    8. DJ Leomeo - Somerset's Bar
    Somerset's Bar will close it's door this 31st Dec.
    spend the last nite there with DJ Leomeo while he plays the best jazz music ever.....
    sob, i will miss this place.

    9) Salsa New Year's Eve Party - Chijmes
    Featuring Ireson band, mexican pianist-composer, Alina Ramirez and DJ Yujin to spin Deep Latino, tribal and soulful vibes!
    FREE.
