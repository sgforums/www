    New Year's Eve party at the Padang cancelled
    Source : http://www.straitstimes.com.sg/news/story/0,4386,227359,00.html?

    REVELLERS looking to ring in 2004 at the Padang now have to look for alternative party plans.

    Countdown @ Padang 2004 has been cancelled, according to an ad placed by its organisers, TES Entertainment, in The New Paper yesterday.

    When contacted at the company's office, staff member Victor Kwek said the 'business decision' was due to 'unforeseen circumstances' involving partners and sponsors.

    It's not due to a lack of ticket sales, he added, declining to reveal more information until his managing director, Mr William Lim, who is overseas, is back in Singapore on Tuesday.

    Mr Kwek was unable to say how many tickets have been sold.

    The outdoor party is billed on the company's website as 'a fusion of concert and party, involving international celebrities'. These include Hong Kong pop singer Alex To and rap group MP4, and local acts like Rui En and Dreamz FM.

    The sponsor listed is clotheswear brand Hang Ten.

    Tickets which were priced at $32, $18 and $12 will be refunded at the main TicketCharge office at Le Meridien Hotel.

    Recent projects of TES Entertainment, a concert promoter, include Hong Kong singer William So's concert in August this year and a concert in March by Hong Kong stars Flora Chan, Gallen Lo and Louis Koo.

    But those looking to party away on New Year's Eve still have other alternatives.

    Other bashes include Party On, Singapore, at Downtown East, featuring performances by Wendi Koh and Anita Sarawak; and one at Suntec City, organised by Paradise Media, which features DJs Steve Lawler and Dave Angel.
