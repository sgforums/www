    Estimate the basic total costs you will need and change as much as possible here. Then bring some SGD along (or better still, USD if you have) and change the rest there.

    The SGD is not an international currency and is rare in Korea - exchange rates are not fantastic there (but not very far off from here too). Remember that in Korea, changing money is not like here - they don't have money changers in every street corner like we do. In fact, I don't remember seeing any money changers outside Kimpo and Incheon airports.

    Having a credit card will help a great deal when dealing with such dilemma.
