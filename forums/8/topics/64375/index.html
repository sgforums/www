    Hopefully of course no one wishes to regret on a decision they have made, but it is very seldom the case. At both ends of the career and relationship paths, there is a a very bright end possibility and a very dim dark end possibility. Even if we have made the choice and embarked on that path, along the journey through the years, things may crop up against our will, our pre-emption, that cause us to realize the alternative route would have been better now already.

    High chance of path changing for many of us, as things seldom stay constant. Not regretting for a better possibility is rather impossible as who wouldn't? And the fact that no one knows for sure what lies ahead in their choice, is also a reason why not regretting is impossible.

    I would think the only best thing is, choose carefully. If regrets come about in the future, be glad that you at least took a path. (In short, cherish the path you took, the experience and memories you got. Regret is nothing to be afraid or avoided.) Many others never even took effort for both paths, these are the ones who are the really pitiful ones.
