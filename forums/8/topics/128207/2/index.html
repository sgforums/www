    Disturbed, he ordered his driver to stop and he got out to
    investigate. He asked one man "Why are you eating grass?"

    "We don't have any money for food," the poor man replied. "We have to
    eat grass." "Well, then, you can come with me to my house and I'll
    feed you", the lawyer said.

    "But sir, I have a wife and two children with me. They are over there,

    under that tree". "Bring them along," the lawyer replied

    Turning to the other poor man he stated, "You come with us also."

    The second man, in a pitiful voice then said, "But sir, I also have a
    wife and SIX children with me!" "Bring them all, as well,"
    the lawyer answered.

    They all entered the car, which was no easy task, even for a car as

    turned to the lawyer and said, "Sir, you are too kind.
