    the very controversial book...written by Singapore based China journalist, Jiu Dan...
    the book depicts the dark side of Xiao-Long-Nus..china girls who come to spore in hope of a good life and perhaps a little fame and wealth..
    since the launch of the book...chinese (as in china-born chinese) have flooded the forums of many papers and magazines..
    jiu dan personally replied to some of the letters..
    while supporters of the book fend for jiu dan and flame the negative comments fiercely..

    i read the reviews and some of the letters before i got my hands on the famous book..
    but somehow..i still manage to struggle through the book wif a strictly open mind and being as partial as possible..not letting the reviews and comments interrupt my own interpretation and comprehension of the gripping story line...

    i must say..Jiu Dan is very observant..and very critical in her words..when describing the plight of the Xiao-Long-Nus...
    who came on the intention of seeking a better life...wif hopes to marry "jin-gui-xu" and to gain permanent residence here...
    however..it was not all tt they expected...so many turned to selling their bodies to make quick and easy money...
    some were more pitiful..as they were conned and made use by sporean men...

    i feel pity for some (those who got conned and suffered..and chose to prostitute themselves..goin bac china empty-handed is very "no face"..)
    i feel happy for some (they worked hard and saved money and set up small legal business)

    all in all..
    i think this is a relli good book..
    and those praises and flames tt followed are equally interesting..

    those who are up to it (i must say..ur chinese muz be quite good) shd give this book a try..

    (the publishers are working on translation to english..im not sure when will it be out though..but worth keeping a lookout..)
