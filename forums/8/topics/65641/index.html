Tribute to Anita Mui (1963-2003)

  • Nata|ie
    31 Dec 03, 11:23
    I'm an avid HK entertainment scene fan.

    I heard the news of this superstar's passing on the way to work at about 12.30pm yesterday. It took me quite a while to recompose myself. She's an idol to me regardless was it her music, movies or her attitude, to me she's the best entertainer and really down to earth artiste that Hong Kong has ever had.

    Her, being at 40, did not marry. I read the news doing her tribute and listened to the radio this morning. Can't help but tears came down.

    I actually thought she was handling it quite well, doing her chemos and stuff. To pass on for her, was an end to her suffering. The Madonna of Asia, have left many great memories in her friends, family and fans.

    I do not call myself a fan of hers. But my mum and I admire her attitude towards people and her work. She's really different from other superstars. She have this very approachable character and never say die attitude.

    Though she did not win this battle against cervical cancer. She did not give up. She passed on, but her lessons for me remains deep in my heart.
