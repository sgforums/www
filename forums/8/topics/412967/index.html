    what sound do you like?  that is the main reason why people have different guitars.

    once you have decided what sound you want, go have a feel of the guitar : is the action too high or low? it the neck comfortable for you?

    after all that, then you look at the cosmetic side of the guitar.

     

    fender strat has its own sound, the les paul has a fat sound... etc etc

    and within the brands, they have different guitars with their own sound.

    so choose your sound, then feel it, then choose the design.
