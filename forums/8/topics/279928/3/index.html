    Originally posted by january:
    then when u go about doing your daily stuff like working or studying, do you remain in state of bliss or no feeling?
    No. Jhanic bliss may have positive effects into daily lives, but as far as I know, jhanic bliss cannot be sustained out of meditation. I had a forum friend (longchen) who said when he was young, around 16, he was able to attain incredible bliss.. thats when his interest in meditation began. And sometimes the bliss would last for a few days, but he would then seek to attain the bliss again and again. But he also eventually realised that the jhanas by itself aren't going to help much (in terms of insights).

    This is because jhanic bliss is a shamatha kind of achievement, that means that arises from strong concentration, absorption, one-pointedness.. so jhanas are temporary and cannot be sustained outside meditation, though you can train mastery over it, such that you can enter it at will. (I cant)

    But the bliss that arises from insights might be a little different...
