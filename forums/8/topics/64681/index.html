Repair cost in Singapore is very high

  • hoganrock
    19 Dec 03, 10:40
    Extracted from S.C.S

    Currently, many big organisations are over charging $$$ their after sale
    service for their products. A technician fee if you bring in a product for
    service or repair can easily cost u $60 to $120 or even more. There is also
    evaluation fees if you decide later that it is not worth repairing or due to
    some reasons you do not wish to repair it and these fees varies from $13++,
    $20, $50, $60 and maybe even more.

    Try secretly visiting these service centers and you will noticed they are
    packed with people bringing in their products for repair, especially on
    Monday morning. I believe they are making more money on their after sale
    service then on selling their products. Some organisations even sub their
    after sale service or repair center, without telling or letting you know.
    Thinking you are paying for premium service, but in fact not.

    Many also priced their service or parts so high that you rather buy a new
    set then having it repaired. The word reliability just cannot be found
    anymore. It doesn't mean you buy the best or top model and brand of the
    product and play the extra premium price and you are safe, don't be too
    happy you are not safe after all. You are buying just the same as any cheap
    ordinary brands or models, once your product breaks down after the one year
    warranty period. These organisations will be looking at getting more $$$
    from you, regardless whether you bought the best model of its class, it just
    doesn't matter these days.

    Quality and reliability are words of a past, could this have to do with some
    reasons that all these products are being manufactured/assembled in some
    ways or another maybe some parts from China? Go to second hand stores (along
    Kg. Kapor Rd) you will be amazed to find products models that are not very
    old and branded up for sale, for example many branded refrigerators and in
    certain days you will find invertors types up for grab. Someone just thought
    repairing isn't worth the while and have either traded in or given away to
    some repair shop.
