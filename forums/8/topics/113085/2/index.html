    Firstly, I'm an eurasian with Singapore citizenship...

    Hence, I started off like you - not knowing what's there to do etc.

    Do what the locals do...

    1) Shop till your drop... well, it depends on what you wish to buy, there's always the best place to get it... for example, sports stuff ==> you would expect things to be less pricey at Queensway etc.

    Orchard would be quite general I would say...

    2) Eat... try all kinds of local food... I was dared to try 'Sambal udang' (a malay dish) many years back and I cried eating it... man, it's fiery hot.

    3) Observe the people around you... I spotted the first she-male in 'Orchard towers' and she's really gorgeous until she opened that mouth of hers... Then only, I realised that harsh truth.

    4) The others...

    Movies, Bowling, Skating, Clubbing, Singing, Sports - Volleyball games at Sunset Bay(Sentosa), Swimming, Online Gaming, Chatting(MSN), Reading (I'd love Borders for that), Soccer (Watching/playing/betting on it)

    As for place of interests... they are expensive. I would suggest not visiting the zoo... Sentosa is fine, but cost wise, it's high.. Hence, you shall decide...

    As for downtown east, that would be themeparks - Escape and Wild Wild Wet... again, cost price...

    Anyway, enjoy your stay here... have fun!
