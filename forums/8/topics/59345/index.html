    Consider your cost: Store rental and overheads, copyright fees, marketing and promotions of medchandise, staff hiring and salary etc
    Miscellaneous costs: Loss of goods thru shop-lifting, outdated mechandise (you need to follow latest movie trend to survive in this fickle, fast changing movie industry), inflation, currency exchange rate etc

    You profit margin will not be a lot and you working hours will be long! Are you up to it?
