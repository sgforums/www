    Quick fix for TV problem
    I CAME back from an overseas trip to find my family ready to throw away our TV set after experiencing the problem described in the letter, 'Day the TV took on a purple and green hue' (ST, July 6).

    Mr Henry Tay's TV set probably just needs to be de-magnetised. Here's how: switch off the power supply to the TV at the power socket or use the power switch on the set - switching off using the remote control won't do. Then leave it off for a couple of hours. No need to seek compensation from Singapore Power.

    What probably happened after the Big Blackout was that, once power was restored, electro-magnetic waves were created in and around the house as various appliances started up. This caused the TV to become magnetised, giving off the purple and green hue.
