  • Liew

    Currently NTU's animation club, AnimeWorks, is screening some anime movies in NTU's Chinese Heritage Centre (the very oriental-looking building overlooking Yunnan Gardens, has a fountain in front. Take BUS 179 from Boon Lay MRT).
    Those interested can come down for the shows over the next 2 days from 7-10pm.

    The shows:
    20th Feb (Wed) - Mononoke Hime,
    Metropolis
    21st Feb (Thu) - Ah! My Goddess Movie,
    One Piece The Movie 2

    Cost:
    $2 for both days (public)
    Free for VAS members

    Isn't very often for us to see anime on screens bigger than 29-34 inches, so come on down, especially for those living nearby!!

    AnimeWorks website: http://homex.coolconnect.com/member3/ntuanimeclub/index.html
