    Originally posted by limywv:

    omg sians POSB for those under 21 years also must maintain $500 ?

    No such thing as under 21 maintain $500. All ages also must maintain $500 Monthly Average Balance (MAB), else $2 svc charge.

    If your funds are tight, then open a POSB account as their MAB is $500. If you have more funds, consider a OCBC or a UOB account, cos their queue at ATMs is always shorter than POSB/DBS ATMs.

    U can use a POSB ATM card at a DBS machine, and vice vesa.

    U can use a UOB ATM card at OCBC machine, and vice vesa. The first 2 withdrawals in the monthly is free (If u use a UOB ATM card on OCBC ATM or vice vesa), subsequently, a svc charge of $0.15 is applicable per withdrawal.

    Every bank will have their fair share of people critising them. Personally I prefer using OCBC's banking systems.
