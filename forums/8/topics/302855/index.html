    after reading other (normal) people's blogs, i find a common characteristics in the things they post.

    01) Pictures of food.
    I'm puzzled why people like to post what they eat for breakfast, lunch, dinner, supper etc on their blogs? I mean, normal readers won't be interested (don't care) to read about what you eat (at least for me).

    02) Posting many pictures in one single post.
    it's not advisable for one to do that cos u know why? if you post too many pictures, normal people won't have the patience to scroll through them one by one and look at all of them (unless it's pictures of cute girls taking pictures of themselves in bra and panties). in most cases, people will just scroll down quickly.

    03) Writing a long essay.
    Yes, if you write too long a story, people don't have the patience to read them at all.

    04) Writing a long essay without any pictures.
    that's even worst, alway accompany your post with a picture (but not too many).

    my humble 2.5 cents.
    feel free to discuss and add on. ^_^
