McD SG to launch SG50 Hello Kitty Collectibles

  • FireIce
    10 Jul 15, 12:50

     

    Good news for Sanrio and Hello Kitty fans. According to a report from TODAY online, McDonald’s Singapore will begin selling this exclusive SG50 Hello Kitty collectibles starting from 27 July later this month.

    A total of 6 Hello Kitty characters dressed up in iconic Singapore culture and heritage will be available, with one design on sale each week in celebration of the nation’s Golden Jubilee. Priced at $5 each with a purchase of any Extra Value Meal, customers can donate the meal to Food Bank Singapore by letting the counter crew know if they only want the toy. In exchange, food vouchers will then be given to the group’s beneficiaries.

    Image source: McDonald’s Singapore

    The entire SG50 Hello Kitty Collector’s Set is also available at McDonald’s Online Store (unavailable at time of writing). The set will go on sale earlier starting on 20 July at 11am. Priced at S$80 per set, customers are limited to three sets per online transaction and while stocks last.

    McDonald’s Singapore has also announced that they will also be selling a special SG50 ice-cream cone and limited edition SG50 packaging for 3 food items on the menu. The Love SG50 cones will come in red colour and available at 50c each from 7 to 9 August. The limited edition packaging for McSpicy, Twister Fries and Filet-O-Fish will be available from 23 July.
