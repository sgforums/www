    A "poem" dedicated to the recent issues and opinions cropping around on finding Mr Right.
    As food for thought, heres the general opinion flying around in "poem" form.

    Handsome then too poor
    Rich then not handsome

    Intelligent then too opinionated
    No opinion then no goals and direction in life

    Successful then too full of himself
    Not successful then no security

    Fun, out-going and very social then too much of a flirt

    Very tall then too fat
    Not fat then too short

    What I want is MR RIGHT! PRINCE PERFECT from fairy tale. But no perfect in Singapore.
