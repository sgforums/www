  • Gedanken
    24 Jun 07, 19:56
    Why jump straight to a DSLR? Get a decent prosumer camera first, say something like the PowerShot S5IS. It gives you decent zoom, image stabilisation and all the whiz-bang goodies, but at the same time you get manual override for all of the settings, so that you can get a hang of the finer points of photography. Once you get a handle of taking decent photos, you're ready to move up to DSLRs where the learning curve gets steeper.
