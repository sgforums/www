    Providing sgForums for free for the past few years, coupled with the lack of donations, have resulted in me being heavily in debt. As a result, we will have to start charging users to access sgforums. User's will use a credit card or bank transfer to top up their account and they will be charged per post. Our tarriffs will be as follows:

    1st 10 messages, free.
    next 100 messages, $0.05 each.
    subsequent messages, $0.02 each.

    The money we receive will go towards settling our debts, and paying for server upgrades and webhosting costs.

    We hope you will continue to support and stay with us.
