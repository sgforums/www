    Well, I do not think this is applicable to shopping malls in Jurong.

    Previously, Jurong had only Jurong Point. Now, there is JCube, JEM, Westgate and Bigbox. With three new shopping malls already opened and one new shopping mall opening soon, the shopping experience in Jurong is becoming more exciting as there is now competition among the shopping malls in Jurong, unlike previously when Jurong Point was kind of a monopoly here.

    Meanwhile, at Jurong Point, things are becoming more interesting, as JP upgrades itself to be on par with the new shopping malls and remain attractive in the increasingly competitive shopping scene here.

    As someone who goes to Jurong Point almost every week, I have witnessed how Jurong Point was affected whenever a new shopping centre opens.

    • JCube only affected Jurong Point in its first few weeks of opening, probably because people want to check it out, and the crowding level at JP was just as high subsequently. 
    • JEM almost affected Jurong Point big time, as it has more shops and more brands, but the impact was greatly reduced with the negative news reports on JEM. Similar to JCube, the crowding level at JP was reduced only in the first few weeks when JEM opened.
    • Westgate, on the other hand, is a big rival to Jurong Point, and has been affecting the crowding level at JP ever since it opened. 
    The opening of Westgate seems to have triggered Jurong Point to renovate and improve itself to make it a better shopping mall. There has been many additions and alterations at Jurong Point recently. For instance, the floor spaces at the second and third storeys of JP1 were expanded, and the "marketplace" area of JP2 was revamped and given some china theme. Right now, there are some works ongoing at JP2 basement one and level one, and JP1 level one. With so many changes happening at Jurong Point, I would say that Jurong Point is actually becoming more interesting, instead of getting boring.

    In short, the shopping malls in Jurong are actually becoming more exciting and interesting now, instead of getting boring.
