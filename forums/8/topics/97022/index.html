    Posh craving chocolate cookies

    Victoria Beckham is craving gluten-free chocolate cookies during her pregnancy.

    Victoria, who is expecting her third boy in March, has been buying six packets of the chocolate cookies from supermarkets near the couple's new home in Madrid.

    Posh has had to go for chocolate gluten-free cookies because of an allergy to wheat reports The Sun.

    A source close to the singer said: "She has had a real craving for gluten-free cookies. They have chocolate on one side and she absolutely loves them.

    "Vic eats what she wants when she's pregnant. It's a real treat for her because she's usually so careful about her food.

    "David also loves it when Victoria is pregnant because he enjoys cooking healthy meals for her."

    Meanwhile David's eldest sister Lynne has also just discovered she is pregnant with her third child.

    Victoria's brother Christian is expecting a baby next year with his girlfriend and Posh's sister Louise has just given birth too.

    The source added: "Victoria and David are really excited about the new baby and are trying to come up with a Spanish-sounding name."

    Real Madrid star David is currently in Britain preparing for England's World Cup qualifying clash with Wales on Saturday. It's only the England skipper's second competitive game at Manchester United's Old Trafford since the club sold him to Spanish giants Real last year.
