    There is *NO* missing dollar. In the left-hand-side of the equation, we have the manager keeping ($30 - $5) or $25. On the right-hand-side of the equation, we have the men paying ($9 x 3) minus $2 pocketed by the waiter, which is $25.

    ($30 - $5) = ($9 x 3) - $2
    $25 = $25
