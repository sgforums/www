    Kiite = ( Listen )

    motto yukkuri itte = ( speak more slowly )
    *note* very useful when you need someone to speak slowly..

    *SUBJECTS*

    Eigo = ( English )
    nihongo = ( japanese)

    Eigo umai ne = ( you are good at English )

    umai = ( nicely done ) also known as good / nicely done..

    Demo, eigo sugoku umai-ne! = ( Your english is so good ! )
    *note* *very useful*
    when in JAPAN, you would find it rather difficult to interact with any japanese. so when you try to speak to them in japanese **it shows the japanese that u r trying ur best to speak their languages.** JAPANESE loves to see foreigners try their best to speak their language. they loves to see you make an effort speaking their dialect.**
    by the way, foreigners who speaks even a little japanese will be constantly praised in ways like this. this is just one reason its good to make an effort..
