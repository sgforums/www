    Violet Sanford (Piper Perabo) heads to New York to pursue her dreams of becoming a songwriter, and the last thing she wants to do is work at the rowdy but popular hot spot Coyote Ugly.

    But life isn't as easy as it was back in New Jersey, and Violet has to do what it takes to make it in the big city.

    The title refers to an actual bar run by female drinkmasters in New York City's East Village, where scantily clad bar babes (Tyra Banks, Bridget Moynahan and Izabella Miko) hang from the rafters and serve swarmy patrons.
