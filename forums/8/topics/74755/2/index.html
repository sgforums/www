    enterpruener ? business ? most of the posts i saw there are nothing but self-employments.

    wonder how many of your are expose to Robert Kiyosaki's cashflow quadrants ?

    Owning a shop, running a online sales job are all basically fall into the "S" quadrant. I don't want to explain the whole book on Cashflow Quadrant here.

    Just one simple question is this, can your "businesses" continue to run if you go on a vacation of 2 weeks, 4 weeks or 8 weeks ? If not, then that is a "S" for you. Instead of selling yourself to a job (Employee), you bought yourself a job.

    A true business uses the concept of System & Leverage. It should run on its own where you are or not around. I don't see this around in all the above postings.

    Another comment I concur with some other views here is everyone is certainly afraid of risk. Yes, fear is what keep people out of doing things people cannot see....just like not everyone can be Christopher Columbus....setting sail into the unknown.

    I myself have my home based business and also coach others in starting/running their home base business. I am also into Options trading....so risk and risk management is part my daily agenda.

    Do message me if you need some private conversation on starting businesses.
