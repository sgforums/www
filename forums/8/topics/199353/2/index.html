    A trip to NSC under subsidy costs around $40 back in year 2001...

    You must determine what causes your acne - whether is it your hormones or is it plainly a skin condition, or is it the environment that causes it - before choosing a method to follow out of so many suggestions.

    Find a facial cleanser that suits your acneic skin, and STICK TO IT.

    If your skin is oily, blot it once a day. Do not over-blot as it will cause your skin to produce even more oil.

    Change your pillowcase regularly (or put a towel over it and change the towel).
    Ensure your living environment is clean e.g. free from excessive dust.

    To try to salvage the harm that has already been done, remember to take Vitamin C as they will help in your skin renewal and scar healing. Some flowerpodders recommend eating a kiwi a day, as kiwis contain a high amount of Vitamin C.

    DOXYCYCLINE : side effect is nausea, so always take it after food, and not just a bit of food. Better to be a meal. If you still experience nausea, umm, I used to take anti-nausea tablets like metolon or that small yellow tablet.
