    Originally posted by coldzero:
    May I know after I have graduated and got a degree on the course, where should I proceed on to establish my career?
    After you've graduated with a Psychology degree, it's advisable for you to pursue an honor and then a masters in Psychology. Because in order to become any form of Psychologist, you need at least a masters in Psychology.

    Once you get a degree, you can work as a social worker. Of course, being a social worker requires compassion and love for people. Psychology is a course that involves loving people. That's the reason one studies Psychology - in order to help others overcome their problems.

    You can also work under Human Resource et cetera et cetera...

    You may like to check out this web site for more info:

    http://www.singaporepsychologicalsociety.org/

    Oh, by the way, in order to become a fully recognized and certified Psychologist in practice, you'll need to be affiliated to SPS.
