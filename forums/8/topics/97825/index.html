    OCT 13, 2004
    Sheikh Haikel, Cheryl Fox nabbed in drug bust
    IN LAST week's drug bust that uncovered several big-names in high society, Sheikh Haikel and Cheryl Fox were also arrested by the Central Narcotics Bureau (CNB), The New Paper reported on Wednesday.

    Having submitted their urine samples, they are now out on police bail.

    Haikel was arrested together with Marx Oh Chee Wee, the director of events company Zero Event Concepts who has since been charged with drug trafficking.

    'Yes, I assisted in the investigations but they released me a few hours after. I was at the wrong place at the wrong time,' he said.

    Fox was called in by the CNB for investigations after Thursday's raid. She had been seen dining with Oh the day before.

    Haikel and Fox will report to CNB when the results of their urine tests, which are expected by next Friday, are out.

    Since the raid, Guiga Lyes Ben Laroussi, marketing manager of Bobby Rubino's restaurant, and his girlfriend Mariana Abdullah have also been charged with drug trafficking.

    Five others have been charged with drug consumption. They are Jeremy Mahen Chanmugam, director of Zero Event Concepts; Nigel Bruce Simmonds, editor of Singapore Tatler; Francois Fabien Mermilliod, a French chef at the Flutes At The Fort restaurant; Hamden Mohd, a technician and Andy Ng Kwang Thiam.
