    Isn't school uniform a form of identity?

    I am a secondary school girl studying in the east.

    Before I could take out the student fare of 55 cents to pay, the bus captain told me to pay the adult fare of 90 cents.

    The reason? I did not have my ez-link card to prove my identity as a student.
