    Assuming the father has relatives whose blood group is O, the possible baby's blood group is: 25% will be an AB, 25% will be an A, 50% will be a B.

    Assuming the father has no relatives whose blood group is O, the possible baby's blood group is: 50% will be an AB, 50% will be a B.
