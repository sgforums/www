    This is a reply from NUS Admission Office to the enquiry whether a student without Project Work will be able to be admitted into NUS.

    "Though it is a requirement that students presenting the Singapore Cambridge GCE 'A' Level examination results for admission to NUS must submit PW scores, we realise that there could be some students who do not have the opportunity to offer the subject. Such candidates will not be at a disadvantage and will not be required to submit PW scores for university admission. Instead, their university score will be computed based on their performance in the Singapore Cambridge GCE 'A' Level examinations. In addition, you may submit information on any exceptional talent / quality such as achievements in the area of the arts or sports at the national level, holding key management positions in organisations or is a volunteer in social organisations. This exceptional talent / quality will be taken into consideration in your application for admission."
