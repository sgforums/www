    DId average singaporeans know why singapore employers like to employ foreigners??

    from what i know, eg a zhichar shop that employ china nationals cleaners, counters service staff, get kickbacks for every foreigners they employs. the kickbacks could go up to $1000/workers. That is almost equivalent to 1mth of such foreigners pay. in another workers, these workers get to work for them free for 1 mth.

    The so call foreigns workers pay a handsome fee of up to 30000- 50000 RMB to middleman in china to get them working in singapore. with a middleman in singapore, they would arrange jobs, permits to allow them in legally. these middleman fees will be split among china middleman, singapore middleman and the employers alike. that is the incentives these middleman waive at the singapore employers to tempt them should they meet the minimum requirement of singaporean to foreigners ratio.

    If the local authority do not seen or failure to address these problems,

    singaporeans opportunity to work will not be just the problems of whether our productivity is high, but we also have to pay $$ to employers to employ us should this problem persist.

    shame!!
