    Type of Bonus Points
    1) For applicants seeking admission to JC/Poly/ITE courses and with the following CCA grades
    a. Grades of A1-A2 2 points
    b. Grades of B3-C6 1 point

    2) For applicants seeking admission to JC/CI courses and with grades of A1 to C6 in both their first languages (i.e. English and a Higher Mother Tongue). This is provided that these choices come before any Poly/ITE choices. 2 points

    3) For applicants provisionally admitted to a JC/CI if they choose course(s) from the same JC/CI as their
    a. 1st choice, or
    b. 1st and 2nd choices.

    or

    For applicants from feeder schools if they choose their junior college course(s) as their:
    a. 1st choice, or
    b. 1st and 2nd choices 2 points

    4) For applicants who had applied for the LEP (Chinese) or LEP (Malay) and have been selected for the programme. 2 points

    For (1) to (3), it is limited to a maximum of 4 Bonus points only for these 3 sections.

    For (4), you will be given 2 bonus points for all choice of courses in the JCs offering LEP (Chinese) or LEP (Malay). This is not affected by the limitation stated above unless you are eligible for all 4 sections.

    If you are eligible for all the type of bonus points, you are limited to a maximum of 6 points only for all 4 sections.

    For Section 3, I am not sure whether it would still be in effect from next year JAE 2006 because there is no more 1st 3 months from 2005 'O' level takers onwards.

    As for the Cut-off points (with bonus points) for entry into JCs/CI, it is given under Annex B of the JAE Booklet for 2005.
