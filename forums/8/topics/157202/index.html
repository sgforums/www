    Hi,

    Can I give some suggestion?

    For later, I think u can try improving his health by giving him soaked dog food mixed with a bit of shredded meat. Give him good brand like Innova. And soak it for 15-30 mins so he won't need to chew so hard. The added shredded meat, or liver, is optional. If he doesn't have any appetite, the aroma from the meat can help make him start eating. After a few times u can gradually reduce the meat altogether. Or if not, u can get a half-wet or wet dog food, usually in cans. They have much better aroma than the dry ones and ur dog should like them better.

    It's not so good to give ur dog table food coz it's hard to give balanced nutrition and/ or it's hard to always supply something he will always eat.

    I have a very choosy dog who was very skinny and he ate very little. Gradually I found out how to solve this. By the method above. Now his weight is normal and in fact slightly above average. Also I found out he won't like to chew hard dog food.

    Asides from food, u should pet him and touch him many times. And if possible don't confine him for long.

    Finally bring him out for walks. A beach will be perfect. You can go in the afternoon when the sun is not so hot and walk with the dog there. Don't make him too tired (you can see from the breathing). When it's enough, go home and give him dinner. He will have much better appetite because he just did sports. And plus it's a leisure/ entertainment activity as well.

    With the vet's help, plus regular eating, lotsa love and petting, and beach walks followed by meals, Im sure ur dog will see an improvement of health and happiness. Don't forget the vet too, in case he has any conditions/ illness.
