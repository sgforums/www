    Bollywood -
    its the name given to the Industry after the great actor Amitabh Bachan, who popularyl is referred to as Bachan..B for bachan , and it became Bollywood..In fact Bollywood is led by his multinational ABCL corporation
    Another reason was the fact that everything in this industry is just like Hollywood, big and flashy..Gorgeous babes and hunks..trendy costumes and an increasing emphasis on commercial value, means of making big bucks...Almost everyone here has that Las vegas attitude and tend to follow watever's hot and trendy at the moment..It mainly consists of North Indian actors, directors and technicians..most of the time talent and innovation takes a backseat here..what they lack , they make up for it in terms of flashy stuff

    South Indian Film Industry -

    This industry has no name, but by far its better than Bollywood in terms of churning out quality movies but never got the recognition it deserved..This Industy had done a lot for the recognition of Indian cinema internationally..This is where some of the best talents come from, like Director Maniratnam, composer Ar Rahman, and whole lot of others..Over the years, especially during the 80s and 90s, they have churned out genre defying movies which set a new standard even the infamous Bollywood tended to follow and replicate....In fact they tend to lead Bollywood itself....Even a lot of artistes do crossover from Bollywood to make movies here...And artistes here when they crossover to Bollywood make a huge impact .In this Industry, everything is done fast and efficiently..though the actors or actressses do look just as good ( as their Bollywood counterparts.. in terms of performance and technically, they have set an example to Bollywood...its here that new concepts, ideas,techniques and technologies pertaining to film making have been introduced and evolved
