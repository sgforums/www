    I explain more here.

    Services 85, Express 501, Express 511 and Express 512 will be terminated on Sunday 27 July 2003. Their last day of operation is on Saturday 26 July 2003.

    Service 97 route between Jurong East interchange and Fullerton Rd remains unchanged, after that, it will proceed to Suntec City/Marina Centre to make a loop. The service will skip the part between Hougang Central interchange and City Hall MRT interchange.

    Service 111 route between Ghim Moh terminal and Dhoby Ghaut MRT station remains unchanged, after that, it will proceed to Suntec City/Marina Centre to make a loop. The service will skip the part between Hougang Central interchange and Dhoby Ghaut MRT station.

    Service 106 route between Bukit Batok interchange and Dhoby Ghaut MRT station remains unchanged, after that, it will proceed to Suntec City/Marina Centre to terminate there. The service will skip the part between Serangoon interchange and Dhoby Ghaut MRT station.

    Service Express 502 route between Boon Lay interchange and Le Meridian Singapore remains unchanged, after that, it will proceed to Suntec City/Marina Centre to make a loop. The service will skip the part between Sengkang interchange and CTE exit into Orchard Rd.

    Service 81 route between Tampines interchange and Serangoon MRT station remains unchanged, after that, it will proceed to Serangoon interchange to terminate there. The service will skip the part between Serangoon MRT station and New Bridge Rd terminal.
