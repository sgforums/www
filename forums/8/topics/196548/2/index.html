    Debt agencies can make sure you cannot get another job. It's easy to track you down to your new job in Singapore..one of their usual tactic is to go to your new office and make a scene asking you to pay up. They will make sure your new colleagues and employer know that you owe them money...

    In addition, they can submit your data to credit agencies, so you will not be able to apply for credit cards etc..

    Think carefully...
