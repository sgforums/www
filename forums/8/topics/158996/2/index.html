    Actually rock music has been very popular in Singapore since the days of Jimi Hendrix, Rolling Stone and Led Zepplin etc. The reason why this rock movement is getting more noticable is because of our goverment's effort to create more outdoors venue for band performances. in the old days, the only public live preformance in Singapore is the Hungry Ghost Gei Tai. While today, we have Youth Park, Substation, Esplanade, and many music festival.

    Beside that, music (MP3) and musical instrument are just getting cheaper these days and to learn to play music, it is as easy and cheap as buying an instrument and download materials from the internet.

    Why kids like rock? Because Rock is always associate with being Rebellious, and it has a very cool image amoung teenager.

    Minus the image thingy, music is universal langauage which help to bring people from of all colors, race and religion together.

    Honestly speaking, there might be more band competition these days, but I would say that the quality of the bands are much better in the old days. You dont get to find many 3 chords music in the 70s and 80s right?
