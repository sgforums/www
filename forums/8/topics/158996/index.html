    I have been involved in the rock culture in SG ever since i was in P6

    I was in a local rock group, Acid9, and the only exposure we had was local jamming gigs at Sunset Bay and the Loft.

    Rock in SG, presumably, started with the local MAT ROCK bands like Sweet Charity and Lefthanded. They started the long, frizzy hair in the 70s ala the mullet in the western region.

    Lasted from 70s to 80s, mellow rock bands such as the Bambinos and the Crescendos followed the success of their counterparts in the west , The Drifters, Budy Holly.

    Then during the late 80s and early 90s, came the revival of Pseudo "Hair Rock" thanks to Douglas O and his band, Energy. Tania followed quite soon. They are the LIVE BANDS type that paved the way for current LIVE BANDS in clubs such as the-now-defunct Europa.

    Then, came the pioneer of Heavy Metal in SG with the birth of Axed Ministers. They are indeed the pioneers. Carved out a niche in Japan and now, they are covering gigs in Japan.
