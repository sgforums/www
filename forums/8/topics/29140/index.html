    But whatever your inclinations,
    shopping is an important part of a
    relationship with a woman.
    When I first get to know a girl, I always make it a point to allow her to
    suggest what we should do ? be it having dinner, going to a club or watching a
    movie ? because I want to look like a caring and sensitive guy.
    But there'll come a time when this approach wears off and I'll be sitting in
    front of her and she will be asking me
    this question: "So, what shall we do today darling?"
    I've become quite an expert on this and can tell you with some authority that
    this is a trick question.
    And it becomes trickier when she
    follows through with: "How come I'm the one who is always suggesting what we
    should do?"
    Before you could tell her it is a man's thing to allow women to make these
    decisions, she'll say: "Why don't you for once decide what we should do?"
    As an expert, I keep in my memory a standard list for moments like this.
    I always know what she would say but to make sure there is communication in the
    relationship I'll start off with: "Let's go to the movies."
    To this she'll say definitely: "There is nothing interesting showing right now."
    Then, I'll suggest: "Let's go to a club" and she'll go: "We did that last week".
    Sometimes, I'll toss fishing into the
    list to try my luck but I risk bodily harm for doing this.
    But when I say: "Let's go shopping,"
    suddenly I'm smarter than Albert Einstein. She'll say: "That is such a brilliant
    idea."

    always a high chance that I'll end up in the lingerie department holding a pair

    If I don't look stupid doing that, then I usually look like some kind of sex

    It's not like after buying one, they go around in the office with their skirts

    The only reason I make sure that I put on decent underwear every day is because

    Like if I am in a bank that is being robbed and everyone is made to strip to

    Can you imagine what the headline would read the next day, if you are

    But guys, if you really love a girl and want to make her happy, go shopping with
    her. Usually a dinner, movie or a romantic cuddle after this is well worth the
    ordeal.
