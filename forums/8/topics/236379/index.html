    Originally posted by Xephone_xenon:
    Any good ways to improve the language english.

    I read quite alot. But i dont see it helping me much.
    You have to try to apply the new words you see.

    There's a book by Barron's, written by Murray Bromberg and Melvin Gordon. The title is "1100 Words You Need To Know". Should come in handy for you if you would like to expand your vocabulary.

    There is one assignment for each day of the year, and for each assignment, they first give you a short passage containing new words for the day, so you can have a glimpse of how they may be used.

    Then there'll be sentences with blanks for you to fill in with the correct words, and lastly you'll be asked to match definitions to words.

    At the end of each page, there's also an idiom and its explanation...

    Grammar-wise... I don't know, does it come naturally as you read more (good) books?

    i would say, read and read and read...

    proper stuff that is...

    one of the best things to read, i would say, is the Asterix comics... although a lot is lost in the translation, the translators have done a great job with the stories...

    you will slowly pick up the words as well as how you use them when you start to discover that each line, each name and everything they say mean more than just what is written... just like their names..

    Asterix, the hero, comes from the word "Asterisk" which is usually used in books and documents to denote something special.. because he is the hero, he's intelligent and the star of the series... that is, he's special..

    Obelix, comes from "obelisk" which is a huge rock carving that stands up.. he is also a menhir-carver and deliveryman.. menhirs are obelisks.. and he is also a rock which Asterix can depend on.. he's also a big man.. someone dependable, a rock...

    then there's Cacophonix, the bard, whose name comes from "cacophony" or noise.. he thinks he's a musician of exceptional talent but his sonnets and songs drive people away.. in other words, he is a "noise maker" or a source of a cacophony...

    go read the comics.. and look for the hidden meanings in the stories... the meaning written there is funny but the hidden meanings are funnier.. which is the genius of this series..

    good luck!
