    Yo bismarck! Your swordfish suffering from dropsy. You can see from the photo that the fish's scales are raised. This is a symptom of dropsy. It is a infectious disease caused by bacterial infection. Largely due to poor water condition. You should go add some liquid medications to avoid spreading the infection to your other fish, and you have to change water and clean up the pump liaoz. And throw away the carbon from the pump! The only purpose for the carbon is to keep the water crystal clear, but you have to change the carbon often, cause i can bet you the source of the bacterial infection is from the carbon. I learnt the hard way b4, now i dun use carbon at all, i use those cotten wool instead, and change it when it gets disgusting. Finally, if you think your situation is too jialat liaoz, and the meds not working, you should do the old fashion way and add salt. Cover the tank when you do this hor, the fish, expecially swordfish tend to jump when salt is added.

    Hopefully this helps! GOod luck!

    Oh yar bismark! You can do one final thing, you can buy a heater and raise the tank temp. to around 26-27 celsius. Since fish are cold blooded, the higher temp. will increase fish metabolic rate, boost immune system blah blah blah. And if you really want to help those fish that i said is cannot make it, u can give them a salt bath. THis means that u prepare a pail of water, put LOTS of salt in it, put the fish in for about 10 seconds....the fish will be very uncomfatable, and will find hard to breathe in this salt water and will struggle and jump. But only use the salt bath if you are VERY desperate, i only do this if the fish is very important to me, or cost damn a lot. And this method is not 100%, the fish can die from stress by being in the salt bath. Salt baths especially good for fungal and external parasite infections.
