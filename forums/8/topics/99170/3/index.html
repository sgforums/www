    Mooku, my hair started turning white when I was in Primary 4. It was horrible. At first, it was just a few obvious strands but then when I started Sec 1, my mom's hairdresser suggested that I dye my hair, which I did. That made things worse because my whole head of hair turned white after a few months of dyeing!! :~(

    Now, I take advantage of the situation by colouring it a Dark Brown with reddish tints. White hair takes to colour much easier than black in a way. But also, the colours tend to become more extreme and obvious so you have to choose the shades carefully. Once, I tried what looked like a nice reddish brown only to have my white roots turn purple! HAHA!

    Now, I still find it tedious to have to touch up my white roots every 2.5 weeks or so. My hair grows too fast. Good thing is, these days, the permanent do-it-yourself colouring kits available at Watson's and Guardian Pharmacy are really easy and effective. Also, they're not that costly and definitely cheaper than going to the hairdresser's. Plus, when I'm waiting for the 35-40 mins after applying the hair colour, I just sit at my computer and play some computer games or watch TV / VCD's.

    If we ever have war, then die... no hair colour, I'll soon become 'bai fah mo nu'! hahaha!
