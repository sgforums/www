    The Curse of the Black Pearl....

    Set in the Caribbean Sea in the 17th century, this is the story of a gentleman rogue of a pirate, Jack Sparrow (Johnny Depp), who teams up with the daughter (Knightley) of a governor (Pryce) to stop the evil plan of a ship of dangerous pirates (led by Geoffrey Rush) who are trying to reverse an ancient curse that leaves them stuck between life and death, with the light of the moon revealing their skeletons, like some kind of undead monsters.....

    For those who loves adventures of pirates and the battle of ships and cannons.... The adventure takes a resemblence with past pirate movies, blended with its own mix of fantasy by the 'curse'.... See how legendery hero fights for loved one and dignity.... It's not a bad choice if you're all out for adventure... And the plus point is.... It have been probably quite a while before a movie about pirates (not referring to animated ones) has been released...?
