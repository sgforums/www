    Just to share with you what my lecturer said during one of his lectures.

    He told us that guys cannot

    1) decide on a place without asking the lady's opinion. It is being authoritative. The lady will feel that she is not significant enough to make decisions.

    2) keep asking where the lady wants to go and won't budge until she names a place. It's a bit wishy-washy to do that.

    He said that a good way will be coming up with several alternatives (descriptions of the different alteratives as to why he chose them would be better - by me) and allow the lady to choose.

    In this way, the lady will feel important to be part of the decision process and not being pressured to brainstorm of the different places to go.

    He even shared with me his experience.

    He took a lady out for a date. They went to several restaurants. Each time, he stopped to ask the lady if she wants to eat in that particular restaurant. The lady said, "anything is fine with me" However, he still carried on until they came to this restaurant. Again, he asked her whether this is to her liking. She gave him the same reply and he decided that they have their dinner there. The lady was delighted. She told him that it is her favourite restaurant. She asked why he decided on that. He revealed that it is because he observed that her feet were pointing to the last restaurant and not to the others. It is an indication that she prefers that to the rest. The lady was impressed. This goes to show that observation is also important.

    I tried to use this 'feet pointing' theory on certain things and I realised that it doesn't work at times. ehehhe
