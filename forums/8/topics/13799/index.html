    Since you would be driving a Class 3 vehicle, you would of course need a Class 3 license. I asked one of the taxi drivers during one of my rides and he says that other then the Class 3 license you need to take the Taxi Driver's License as well. However if you are married already, you can apply to take the test anytime regardless of your age. But if you are single, then you would have to wait till the age of 30 or 35 (couldn't really remember) before you can actually be allowed to take the test.
