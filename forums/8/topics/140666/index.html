    Getting a degree has become too common in Singapore. Eventually one out of four Singaporean will be a graduate. Given that there are no natural resources in Singapore, the quality of our workforce become the driving force of the economy.

    The purpose of education prepare you to work in the society. Eventually, it drives down to your altitude in your work to remain viable to your company. If you are in demand, you can join another company.

    .... Isnt this what everything about? About money?

    Be positive, fully assert yourself in your work. Learn and experience new ideas from your work. In the new economy, no one can promise you a life long career. But the experience and knowledge which you have learned will help you to be employable.
