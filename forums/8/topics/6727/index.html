    You might have seen some of our regular members with titles like 'DaDDy's LiTTLe GiRL' or 'Lover Of Veiny Arms', these are examples of custom titles.

    So you want your very own title? Read on!...

    Everytime Chief gives someone a title, he has to go through a little hassle involving the editing and uploading of configuration files. This takes up a little of his time and he finds it a little irritating. But I have convinced him to my idea of having forum members who want titles to show their appreciation by giving a little BACK to the forum.

    My idea is this:

    If you want a title, simply introduce a friend to sgForums.com. That's it!

    So how does this go? Simple:

    1) Post a reply to this topic and introduce yourself, this introduction should include your name, age, gender, what you do (work/school), likes and dislikes and anything you can tell us about yourself, ALSO, include the name of your friend whom you are introducing.

    2) Ask your friend to register and read through several topics in the forum. Your friend will have to post at least 5 messages in the various topics. These messages should have some THOUGHT behind them and not be nonsense simply for the sake of reaching the minimum number of messages.

    3) After posting his/her 5 messages, your friend should then reply in THIS topic with an introduction of him/herself, and name YOU as the person who introduced him. Your friend will then CHOOSE A TITLE for you, we know we cant enforce this but lets play fair, you should not tell your friend what title you want for yourself.

    SIMPLE?

    Because we don't want to overwork our dearest Chief, only the first 15 people who respond to this thread are eligible. Also, we reserve the right, at our sole discretion, NOT to give the title out, this is in case there is cheating/abuse of the system.
