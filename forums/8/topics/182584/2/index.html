    In White Wolf's World of Darkness, vampires descended from Caine, who slain his brother Abel. He was made to drink blood and only blood by God for his crime (the first homicide). Caine was taught the art of vampirism by the First Woman, Lilith. Caine is Cain in the Bible; Lilith is entirely fictional.

    As for Dracula, he was originally a prince of Romania (Wallachia[spelling?], to be exact), who somehow turned into a vampire after his death. He was already rather bloodthirsty (pardon the pun) and cruel in his method of dealing with people who were against him (impaling them on wooden stakes)...

    Oh, if you watch this Hong Kong drama series (My Date with a Vampire II and III only; vampires in the first part does not seem to share the same origin), the vampires are descended from a powerful vampire called Jiang Chen, or vampire commander (as he is subtitled, although the 'Jiang' is tone 4, for 'general' and not tone 1 for 'vampire'...)

    This Jiang Chen, in turn, belongs to a group of people called Pan Gu. Yes, that is the same Pan Gu in Chinese Mythology who created the world, only that in the drama series, Pan Gu is not a single entity but a group/tribe of people.

    There are also two other members from this Pan Gu tribe which appeared in Part III, both of whom are considered deities, and one of whom is even the image upon which Man was created (by the goddess Nuwa).
