    1. Do you have dream?
    2. Do you plan something concrete for your dream?
    3. Do you execute your plan?
    4. Do you persistently execute your plan?
    5. Do you review your plan?
    6. Do you accept critics?
    7. Do you learn something from positive and constructive critics?
    8. Do you have strong technical knowledge and skill?
    9. Do you believe in marketing and customers are the most important?
    10. Do you have good relationship with people?
    11. Do you have the influence to convince people?
    12. Do you manage people well so people work well under you?
    13. Do you reward and punish your subordinates appropriately?
    14. Do you continually upgrade yourself and adapt to new environment?
    15. Do you believe in yourself, rather than pure destiny?
    16. Do you truly understand yourself - your abilities, strong points and weaknesses?

    If your answers to all the questions are yes, you might have the chance to succeed.
