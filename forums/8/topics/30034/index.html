    Scenario I

    You and your gf/bf have been together for a 4 years, very loving couple. He/she is the most suitable guy/girl for you that he/she feels nobody could take his/her place. During your 4 year anniversary, he/she went to get present for you, wanted to give you surprise. You waited at the destinated spot. Waited and waited... but he/she never appeared. Later you receive a call from his/her mum... saying that he/she had been in a car accident.

    You rushed down to the hospital to see him/her and few days later he/she woke up. Doctor told you that his/her memory bank has been damage and would lost part of his/her memory. To your dismay, he/she totally lost memory of you and other major events taken place in his/her life. When he is discharge... you tried to recollect his memories by bringing him/her to old place, meet old friends, etc. To make matter worst... he/she falls for your good friend, who once had a crush for him/her before you both were together. He/she totally treat you like a causual friend, but really different when interacting with your good friend.

    You give up and decide to let them be together. You were hurt and this guy/girl is always by your side. You were touched and decide to give him/her a chance after 2 years. Together for about 3 years, he decide to marry you. He propose and you considered first. At the very same moment, your ex woke up and suddenly recall his/her memories after so long. He broke with your good friend and come looking for you.

    WHAT WILL YOU DO?
