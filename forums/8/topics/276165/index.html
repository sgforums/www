  • SBS3625D

    I was taking bus this morning and I saw an old women trying to cross the road. She saw a car coming towards her and as the road was busy, she tried to run across, unfortunately she was probably stiff in the joints and she could only attempt to hobble and hop to the kerb. Fortunately she got across just when the car sped by.

    For once I could not form an opinion on what I just saw. I could not say the old woman was playing with her life because the nearest traffic light is near for me, but not for her. There were no overhead bridges, and even if there were any I doubt very much if she could/would climb. So there was no option but to risk life and limb to cross the road.

    And I find this really sad. I can't find solutions to this perennial problem and I can see all of us coming to this one day.
