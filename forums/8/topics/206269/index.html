    Oh that one. You have to do still frames manually at every part you want a 'ghost' image to appear. Set the still frames to around 50% opacity and place it at the layer on top of the main video.

    FYI, the video editing software you use must support layering. Basic ones like iMovie and Windows Movie Maker do not support layering. Practically all advanced editing apps will have.
