    Hello....the top runner will be placed in the middle. This is deliberate. That's why u see the winner from the middle lane mostly.

    I don't know whether it's for the V shape or not. I thought it's just to center the attention to the middle/ center and place the best runner there.

    It's similar to tennis or badminton where the main center court will hold the match of the best players, and the side ones hold matches of other players. And when it's final, only the main center court is used, not one of the side ones.

    It's all to direct and center the attention.

    I suppose it won't be nice and kinda weird if u have to look at an extreme court and ignore the center court in front of u and all the rest of the courts.

    I think this principle applies to many sports, including running, swimming, etc.
