  • lookslikegod
    27 Mar 09, 12:08

    Hi, since you are travelling yourself, it might be nice to get yourself lost in Singapore.

    Boosting the efficient transport system, you can do some exploration, get yourself lost in the Housing estates, check out the different paint scheme and different build of the flats.

    The zoo, night safari, bird park are all nice places to go too.

    You can always do the oh-so-popular shopping at the local shopping scene. Get a taste of what it is like to stay so near to the city centre that shopping isn't exactly a chore. Check out the girls, do some drinking at pubs.

    Check out your bargaining skills at Sim Lim Square, hunt down for the local night market (Pasar malams). Lots of things to do.

    Food wise, don't have to go to the popular places like newton circus to eat. There are many other much better food elsewhere.
