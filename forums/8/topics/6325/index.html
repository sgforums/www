    Are you content with what you have now and where you are going? I'm not just asking about the snap shot of how you feel now, rather your feelings on the stage you are at now in life.

    Which got me thinking about how little I have achieved in my past 20++ years of life. Almost long enough to experience a mid life crisis. So, I would like to hear from anyone, whether they feel the same way, and the reasons why.

    Come on, this is not in any way just thread for complaining. Say what you want, write what you think, and pray that we all will work to remedy/ improve things.

    Ah..nice thread for some self reflection

    To be honest, yes, I'll say I am happy, or at least I think I am. Though I must admit life has not gone my way all the time, but I overall, I can't say I do not wake up every morning looking forward to a brand new day.

    In terms of accomplishment I feel I have done little too. But is that a valid reason for me to be unhappy about? I mean I accept the fact that not many of us here can achieve something big in our short lifespan. Sure, I would like to have the opportunity to do something on a scale that would send ripples through the world; something that would earn me recognition and respect in the annuals of mankind's history. But I am realistic; such opportunity may or may not knock during my lifetime. I still interact with the world, doing a small part that may one day fit into the big picture - or become the big picture itself. But till that time comes, life still goes on...

    I do have regrets in life and such, but will regretting over a past issue change the way you are today? I think not. What's important is that one should be able to learn from that event, so that you would be wiser when making a decision in the face of a similiar one. I try not to regret, as its not very constructive use of time.

    Well, it is tempting to go on rambling about this and that, but I doubt many would be interested to read it anway

    Mabe its just me, but I feel that being happy or sad with your current state of life, time still ticks; events still transpire; everything still moves.

    It may be an attempted self-delusion on my part, but it has served me well. At least I can see optimisim in everything I do. Hence I am able to look forward to the challenges each new day brings, with a radiance that matches the life-giving sun at the dawn of day
