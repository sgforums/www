  • shade343
    7 Mar 06, 20:19
    I noticed that the recent trend for certain mods in sgforums is to close topics on certain subjects and refer them to the relevant forums. Although I do not find anything wrong with that, I must say that it greatly inconveniences the threadstarter.

    People post in chit chat and the popular forums because such anchor forums receives a substantially higher amount of viewership as compared to the other relevant forums. This means that the chances of getting a response is higher for the threadstarter.

    Secondly, the relevant forum may have already gone into a state of decadence and posting in such forums merely serves as a waste of server space.

    Since chit chat is supposedly for anything under the sun, why not allow threadstarters the freedom to post whatever they want there? It would certainly be a boon to threadstarters for them to leverage on the chit chat forums viewership and activity ratings.
