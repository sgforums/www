    wacky cake

    1 1/2 cups (180 g) whole wheat flour
    1 teaspoon baking soda
    1 teaspoon salt (optional)
    1/3 cup (28 g) unsweetened cocoa
    1 cup (200 g) sugar or other sweetener, to taste
    1 tablespoon vinegar
    5 tablespoons (75 ml) oil
    1 teaspoon vanilla
    1 cup (240 ml) warm water
     
    1. Preheat oven to 350F (180C). Grease and flour an 8-inch x 8-inch (20 cm x 20 cm) square pan.
    2. Place flour, baking soda, salt, cocoa and sugar into the Vitamix container and secure lid.
    3. Select Variable 1.
    4. Turn machine on and slowly increase speed to Variable 10, then to High.
    5. Blend for 5 seconds. Remove lid plug.
    6. Pour vinegar, oil, vanilla and water into center of flour through the lid plug opening. Replace lid plug.
    7. Select High.
    8. Slowly turn machine on and off 5 times or just until mixed. You may need to mix in the last bit of flour with a rubber spatula. Do not overmix. Pour into pan.
    9. Bake for 25 minutes.
