    Vinyl plates will not rust but they are twice as bulky. Bulky means you cannot load as many plates and the diameter of the plate is also bigger so you have less range of movement in certain lifts. After about 3-5 years the vinyl will crack so shorter life time but they are cheaper. I suggest you go with rubber coated if you want to reduce noise. Otherwise cast iro on a piece of carpet is good too.
