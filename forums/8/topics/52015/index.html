    Agreed!
    Skid Row had 3 main albums, skid row, slave to the grind and subhuman race. The first one was abit raw, but regarded by many to be one of their best which included hits like 18, youth, big guns, sweet little sister etc. Slave had a more polished feel to it with wasted time as my personal favourite. Subhuman perhaps coming after a long layoff could not really find the same magic anymore and was at a time were rock/metal was on the decline and perhaps more experimental in nature.
    Sebastian Bach was considered by many as the best vocalists in the industry as well. Too bad they splited.
