    ^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

    JOINT ADMISSIONS EXERCISE (JAE)

    The Joint Admissions Exercise (JAE) is conducted annually by the Ministry of Education (MOE) to alow holders of Singapore-Cambridge GCE 'O' Level Certificate to apply for admission to courses offered by Junior Colleges (JC), Millennia Institute (MI), Polytechnics (Poly), and the Institute of Technical Education (ITE).

    The JAE 2007 registration will be held over a period of 6 calendar days (including Sunday), starting from 3.00 pm on the 1st day of registration until 4.00 pm on the last day of registration. The exact registration dates will be published in the media together with the release of the examination results in the second week of February.

    For students with 2006 GCE 'O' Level Examination results:

    You will need the following before registering online:

    Form A
    JAE-PIN (Found in your Form A) You don't need SingPass.
    Completed course application worksheet. (Found in your Form A).
    For Singapore Citizens/Permanent Residents without 2006 GCE 'O' Level Examination results:

    You will need the following before registering online:

    Valid SingPass
    Completed course application worksheet, click here to download the worksheet.
    Alternatively, you may register personally at Ministry of Education, Customer Service Centre (MOE-CSC) at 1 North Buona Vista Drive during the JAE 2007 registration period. Applicants are required to bring along their original identity card, GCE ' O' Level Examination certificate/results slip and Secondary School report book.

    Important to Note for all online applicants:

    1. If you have completed your worksheet and logged on successfully to JAE internet system (JAE-IS), you should be able to complete your registration within 15 minutes.

    2. Your online registration is successfully submitted only when you receive the Verification Slip on the screen with the message at the top of the screen:

    Your JAE registration has been submitted successfully.

    This is a copy of the choices you have submitted. Please print a copy for future reference.

    3. To ensure other applicants can enjoy speedy and smooth access to JAE-IS, the system will logoff automatically if there is no activity for 10 minutes continuously.

    4. An application fee of $7 will be charged for the first application, and $10 for amendments to the submitted application. Only one amendment is allowed. All JAE applications fees incurred will be collected from the applicants by the institutions (JC , MI , Polytechnic or ITE) that the applicant is eventually enrolled in. You do NOT need to make payment now.
