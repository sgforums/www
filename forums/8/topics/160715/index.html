    hey everyone! this is the correct answer! Fatum also has the correct answer. yeaps yeaps. i've discussed it with my boyfriend just now. AND IT IS LOGICAL. let me rephrase this answer...hopefully you all will understand. ^^

    ok...

    let's say A wears a green hat. all 3 hands are raised.
    in B's view, he can see that A is wearing a green hat. therefore C is raising his hand due to B's red hat. therefore, B can deduce logically that C is raising his hand towards B's red hat. so B will leave the room.
    C himself would also see that he is wearing a red hat (same viewpoint as B) and leave the room.

    next scenario.

    let's say C is wearing a green hat.

    All hands are raised as well.

    In A's view, he is raising his hand because he saw B's red hat. he also takes notice of C's green hat. A can then safely deduce that B is putting up his hand because of A's red hat.
    In B's view, it would be the same as A's. they would then have already leave the room, having known for sure that both of them (A and B) are wearing red hats.

    HOWEVER.

    A and B did not leave the room. they may be hesitating because they do not know for whom is each raising their hands for.

    therefore, C can safely deduce that C himself is wearing a red hat in order to cause such hesitation.
