    bank interest is actually annual interest, calculated on a daily basis..

    eg> 0.02% / 365 days * amt u have in bank on day 1 n the cycle repeats.... until u have all 365 days, n u add the sume up, n u get the annual interest.

    savings plan interest> depends on whether u're talkin about the ones offered by banks or by insurance companies (endownment plans)

    for the former, u give up the interest the moment u withdraw.. at least that was wat i read the last time i saw the brochure..

    for the latter, u will still gain interest on wateva u have in the acc.. so when u use ur bonus received to pay off ur premiums, u lose out on the interest that would have been urs, if u continue paying ur premiums...

    eg, premium was 1k, bonus was 2k, $$ in acc was 3.5k

    u used ur bonus to pay off ur premium so acc was still 4.5k, but bonus becomes 1k.. interest is 7%.. go work it out urself.. total amt in acc without interest would be 5.5k

    if u paid ur premiums, that would be $$ in acc = 4.5k, bonus = 2k, total amount in acc = 6.5k..
