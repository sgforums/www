    In celebrating the close friendship between man and pet, Stadium Cove brings the pets for a day out at Stadium Cove!
    Take time out from your busy lifestyle and spend a relaxing day with your pet, and showcase your very pride and joy.
    What? : PetÂ’s Day Out! Event on 13 & 14 April 2002

    Time: Saturday (13th April) 4pm-8pm & Sunday (14th April) from 2pm-7pm.

    Where? : Stadium Cove (outside Singapore Indoor Stadium)

    HOW TO GET THERE :

    a free shuttle at the bus stop opposite Raffles City, along Bras Basah Road. Stadium Cove is about 5 to 10 minutes drive from Orchard Road.

    In addition, bus numbers 11, 16 and 608 will bring you to the Stadium Cove area.

    Entry fee: $8 for all members of the public attending this event.

    In return, you will receive a goodie bag. Part of the proceeds collected will go towards the animal associations.

    Registration forms will be available in the newspapers or on site.

    All collected funds will be distributed equally to the five participating societies:

    -Cat Welfare Society
    -Metta Cattery
    -Action for Singapore Dogs
    -Animal Concerns Research & Education Society (ACRES)
    -House Rabbit Society
    -Singapore Kennel Club

    This event will also serve as a platform for an adoption drive for unwanted animals.

    Programme highlights
    1) Pet/Master lookalike contest

    2) Dress Up Your Pet Contest/cum fashion show (Pooch Parade)

    3) Fattest Cat Contest

    4) Educational animal care drawings
    Children can participate in an on the spot colouring and painting contest, revolving around an animal theme. The best picture wins. All entries will be displayed

    5)Dog race
    Special race lanes/obstacle courses will be set up and owners can sign up their dogs for the above event. The top prize will be a supply of dog food/ F&B vouchers.

    6)Rabbit Race

    7)Strike a Pose!
    Capture your pet in their most funny expressions or pose, and send their pictures to us. The winning entry will receive a mystery prize.

    Dog Obedience Shows
    The Singapore Kennel Club will be invited down to showcase their dog shows or highlight their own internal events. In addition, they can hold their own promotional activities.

    Side Activities
    1)Fund Raising
    Award winning dog groomer Augustine of Pet Stylist will give your dog a beautiful coat of fur. All proceeds from his skilful hands will go towards the welfare societies.

    2)Adoption Drive

    3)Distribution of Â“Doggie goodie bagsÂ”
    for the pets! The goodie bag will include samples of certain brands of pet food, and the latest range of pet accessories. These goodie bags will be distributed upon registration.

    4)Sale of Pet Merchandise

    5)Exhibitions
    This would mainly involve the display of panels from the various societies in educating the public about the proper care and treatment of stray dogs, cats and other various animal related issues. The aim of this exhibition is also to increase awareness of the plight of these stray dogs and cats and to make us a more humane society.

    See you there...
