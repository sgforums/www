    Laptops are NOT suitable for gaming, reasons being as follows:

    >> graphic chip that comes with it does not have sufficient memory
    >> if graphic chip has sufficient memory, internal cooling not sufficient
    >> graphic car/chip NOT upgradeable
    >> limited RAM size support in all laptops
    >> power supply can't cope higher performance graphics chips
    >> entire laptop not scalable

    Not a common practice to use laptops as gaming platform.

    Gaming platforms are mid-size to large rigs with liquid or gas cooling for it's CPU, and multiple fans for ventilation. Power supply tops at 600w.

    Therefore, the laptop is best used for schoolwork, office work, and popularly, as mobile email checker.
