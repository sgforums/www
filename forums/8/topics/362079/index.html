    Where do you place your stuffs.

    Office table 1.

    From left to right.

    The A4 papers in a tray, the printer, the office phone, notepads, calendar, hp/pda/laptop charger startions, monitor screen, keyboard, mouse pad, pnes & pencils in a cup & a box of tissue paper to screen priying eyes away to what your right hands might be twiddling.

    Because this table opens up from the right.....so I keep my right open to handle anything incoming so there is no trays there.  People just will slide their stuffs onto my table on the right and I will know they are new stuffs.

    I also do not want people to access the use of my phone. To reach that phone, they have to be physically standing in front of the monitor.  which makes it difficult for them to escape if I caught them snooping.

    Office table 2.

    Similar positioning, except the phone is on the right, because I know people needs to access the phone easier. Dictionary, street directories, notepads and pens are there too, so others can easier jot down phone messages or refer to stuff without me needing to be physically there.

    But the fax printer is still not accessible to others.  So I can control the confidential faxes.

    Home study table.  (Getting a new set)

    Will be similar to the above, except there wont be a pen/pencil stand nor note pads.  Just the scanner/printer/scanner.  Two portable hard drive connected to the laptop on the left.  The right is for thumb drives and mousepad.
