    Assuming both the chin up bars start at sea level. Theoretically, it will be easier to do chin-ups at the 5 m high bar because you are further away from the center of the earth. The further away from the center of the earth, the less you weight and thus easier to do chin-ups. But because the difference is only 4.5m, the decrease in weight is not noticable at the human senses level, thus physiologically, you feel that you exert the same strenght at both 2m and 5m.
