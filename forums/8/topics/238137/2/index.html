    I think its a test on whether your "friends" are true friends or not by sending them festive greetings and testing if they do reply.

    If the receiver is too lazy to type a simple "thank you, have a happy CNY yourself", how can you rely on him or her in times of needs?

    On to my test...

    This CNY I personalised all the CNY greeting sms i sent out. I typed their names plus specific well-wishes or thanks that are suitable for my friends. I didn't use those canned chinese sms greetings at all.

    The response rate is not bad, about 70% response and out of this 70%, about half, i.e. 35% responded with personalised replies.

    These 35% I will remember as TRUE friends and I will die for them.

    The 35% who replied to me using canned replies, I will treat them as hi-bye friends.

    As for the 30% who did not reply, I will give them one more chance at the next holiday. If they still don't bother to reply, I will delete their names from my handphone.

    All in all, i sent out about 50 sms. Took me at least 4 hours in total to think, type and send.

    Anyone else tried sending personalised sms this CNY?[/b][/u]
