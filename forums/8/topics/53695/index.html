    Another Bus 76 Commotion on route to work........

    Today 31 July 2003, I had seen another commotion on bus 76 as I was on board that bus on route to work at around 8++am(why accident alway happen in the morning?). I talked about this before but this time round it is a different. This time it is more violent and crude, luckily nobody injured.

    There is a young short voluptuous short student lady (20++) board the bus 76 and she is late for school event/activities. Her friend called her up and she told her she's on the way now. Don't know what her friends talk to her and she suddenly shout,"don't cry mother cry father(in hokkien). I'm on my way already(in mandarin). It's the bus stop after the school right? I know!!! The bus is very crowded and I'm taking bus 76. Wait for me!" I forgot to tell you that she's standing very near to the front door and clang on the pole trying to maintain her balance while the bus maneuver the multiple "S" curve along paya lebar road.

    After a while, she alighted from the front door and sprinted to her destination(I don't know where) like an olympic medalist holder, making angry faces(stretching and shrinking her face like a puffy fish) along the way. An 30+ man walked past her, trying to minick her face and carrying on his way. Suddenly the olympic medalist holder girl made a brake and chased after the 30+ man scolding him and kicking him. Luckily like a kung Fu fighter trying to block the enemy's attack, he used his legs to block her attack like a stork trying to balance on one leg. She stopped the kicking and scolding, turn around and carrying on her way to her destination. That 30+man used his finger to curse and swore at her and called her "Siow". And that man hastily increased his pace which turn into a run.

    What a day!

    Why do I have to see this commotion on bus 76 every month?

    Sigh!
