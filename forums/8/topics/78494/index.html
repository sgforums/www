    A woman and a baby were in the doctor's examining room,waiting for doctor to come in.

    The doctor arrived, examined the baby, checked his weight found it somewhat below normal, and asked if the baby was breast fed or bottlefed.

    "Breast fed," she replied. "Well, strip down to your waist," the doctor ordered.

    She did. He pressed, kneaded, rolled, cupped, and pinched both
    breasts for a while in a detailed, rigorously thorough examination.

    Motioning to her to get dressed, he said, "No wonder this baby is
    underweight. You don't have any milk."

    "I know," she said, "I'm his Grandma, but I'm glad I came."
