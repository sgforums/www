    When do we call bao under what situations?
    [b]
    1. when the guy has pong/eat of 9 tiles of a same colour, and your discard of that colour result to his winning, you are bao-ed hahaha. provided that he win with one-colour.
    2. when someone has already 4 fans on the table (be it flower, animals or pong of big tiles). if your discard tile added his win from 4 fan to 5 fan, you are bao-ed.
    3. pertaining to point 2, if the guy has 4 fans and ur discarded tile added it up to 5 fans, though he did not win, you will be bao-ed if he self-pick.
    4. you will also be bao-ed if your discarded tile is 4 tiles away from the last 7.5 tiles AND the discard tile is not found in the sea.
    5. lastly, you are bao-ed when someone win your discard tiles while you are xiao xiang gong or da xiang gong.
