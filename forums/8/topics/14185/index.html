    Where: Asian Civilisations Museum, Empress Place
    When: March 20 to April 7, 11 am to 6 pm daily
    Free admission

    The largest fashion exhibition ever in Singapore pays homage to French designer Jean Paul Gaultier. And don't worry about getting dressed up. Visitors to this interactive exhibition have the chance to select Gaultier outfits, wear them, accessorise, put on makeup, spray on perfume, walk down a runway, and even have their photograph taken to remember their model moment. Gaultier's haute couture collections from 1997 to 2000 will also be on show.
