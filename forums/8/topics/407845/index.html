    so i know lucky plaza well known for those chop head ah beng shops... but i went to look look see see anyway, if buay gam, can always walk over to ngee ann city Best Denki upstairs of Takashimaya department store.

    then the ah beng man buay song me, at first try to divert my attention, ask me want to buy or not. if dun want buy, dun come n chup siao them do business.

    then i said ok later...den i quickly tell the ang moh couple go to superstore got better deal, the package everything include...not like these cut throat shops they charge seperately for charger etc.

    den i also say, if buy from best denki, can claim GST. then i think the ah beng n his colleague very buay song me already, like going to whack me up. den i still keh kiang say u people r con men...

    then before things turn ugly n i really kenna whacked, i quickly ran out.... n when i look back, i saw that beng fella diao me n i think he went to tell his friends from other shop to watch out for me...


    so next time i dun dare walk lucky plaza liao...hehe. anyway that ang moh couple dunno how...i hope they made the right choice... old people also want to con...kanina...i cannot stand. give singapore a bad name
