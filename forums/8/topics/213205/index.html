    SINGAPORE : The body of a man was found floating in the Singapore River, near the UOB Plaza on Monday morning.

    The man, who did not have any identification papers on him, was believed to be Indian and in his 20s.

    Police said he had no visible injuries.

    The man was wearing a black polo T-shirt, blue jeans and a sports watch with a blue and black strap.

    He had shoulder length hair and a beard.

    Police has classified the case as unnatural death and are appealing for eye witnesses to call their hotline at 1800-255-0000. - CNA /ls
