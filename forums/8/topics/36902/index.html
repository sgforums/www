    Hi aixuan,

    Actually, at this junction it is not easy to think clearly as to which option is the best . It will be more clear as time passes & you being able to accept the fact.

    Anyhow, if you're going re-do your "o" then go straight to Aust. you wld need to do the foundation cse/"A"/ Diploma cse still b/4 you cld go on to a Uni. That means it is a duplicate effort or a waste of time if you are sure tt you want to go to Aust. after re taking yr "o" .

    U can't get into Aust Uni. with just "o" levels u need at least the following:

    - Aust . foundation study
    - "A" level
    - Poly Diploma.

    As to you Qs on Re do " O " - I can't answer since I am - not sure of the current system. May be current " A" level or Uni students can help you. Probably u can do it as a private student.

    If u seriously wants an Overseas education in Aust. & still wish to re-do "O" . U can go to Poly after re-doing "O" & the proceed to do a 2 yr cse after yr diploma cse ( provided same major for the Uni cse- to get exemptions)

    BTW don't be a afraid of maths , I got "D7" first Time & got an A2 when I repeat - a matter of prac. get a good tutor . Pls be advised u need maths in most cses in Uni later -hence need to overcome yr fear now . Maths is No big deal really - lots of Pp can attest to this fact.
