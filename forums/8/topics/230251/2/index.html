    Those the bring back the fondest memories.

    Katong Shopping Center - Used to buy computer games there. In those days, games come in diskettes. A game like Hero Quest fits 10 disks! They pack it in a plastic bag with a manual. Pirated of course, but in those days nobody sells originals in Singapore.

    Upper Serangoon Shopping Center - I take bus number 24 from my secondary school there. On the highest story, there's a shop that sells computer games. It is still there today I believe. The arcades have Street Fighter II Champion edition. And on the ground floor, there are shops that allows you to play games at $2 an hour. There's Neo Geo and Super Gun.

    Katong Complex - I hang around this mall, in a music shop called Smash Top 40 Music Mart for hours every friday. Its a really tiny place that sells rock music casettes. I bought Metallica and Guns & Roses there.

    That's back in the late 80s and early 90s. Where I mis-spent most of my teen and childhood...

    I still visit both malls. When I am there, I sit at the coffee stalls, and think about myself. In my humble opinion, the worst malls today are the best malls that I have ever gone to...
