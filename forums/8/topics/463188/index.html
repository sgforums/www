    We were at the FU Lou Shou complex /albert complex to sample some of the typical local hawker food. It was 2pm just after the lunch crowd. We order our food and ate. As we were about to finish. An old English speaking Chinese man approached our table he was about early to mid 70s. He askedvery pleasantly ," could I have those two left over fish balls and that left over kuay teow please?" I said " sure yes!" without thinking and then realised he wanted our left overs! It was shocking because here was this old man who actually spoke to us in English and wanting to eat our left overs. I promptly bought him a new packet of food and also a drink for him . I gave him the new packet of food and saw that he wanted to keep it in his bag and continue eating the left overs!!!! What's happening to my Island Paradise?
