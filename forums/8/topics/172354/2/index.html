    A point to note is: the most important components in a audio system
    are, in the order:
    1) speakers
    2) amp
    3) source : CD, tuner, etc
    With good speakers, even with avg 20W amp, it still sound good
    (except slightly weak bass when u turn on the volume).
    compared with lousy speakers and good amp.
