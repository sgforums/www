    However as we walked out of the estate he kept following us...and when we crossed the road he did so too...and he sounded like some gangster so we decided to take a cab away from him. He even tried to chase the cab by running on the road. We could hear him shouting the plate number into his phone..

    But my father didn't think so. well he reckons that the guy was too bored, and decided to cause some trouble. He said that because the guy didn't look like a madman, and he did stuff that didn't enable the police to arrest him...

    so if the condominium wanted to get rid of him, how could they do it?
