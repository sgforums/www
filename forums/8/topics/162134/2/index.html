    Ok ok ok... .this is my 4th DAY!!!

    You guys are great, thank you all for the support, I really appreciate you all.

    THANKS!

    Now, the morning urge to smoke is much lesser, but as someone said; would likely take 1.5 weeks for ill temperaments to fade away. Meantime, my employess, friends, and wife are mindful of this IRRITABILITY issue. hahaaa.. I lost my stylus yesterday - wanted to throw the damn PDA into the sea! That was my second stylus! Damn troublesome PDA phones! I should NEVER buy O2!

    So my digital music production equipment just came last night and set up, now I have piles of Owner's Manual to get lost in. And nope, I don't fancy no church organ. Actually, this synth has everything inside - every known real and synthesized instruments are inside. Amazing technology! Just wonder why these ingenius people didn't dream up a remedy that can erase the Nicotine Urge with one tablet.

    Just in case you guys are wonderin what it is I bought - it's the YAMAHA Motiff ES8 workstation. Very engaging - started playing it last night, and not only smoking was forgotten, I even forgot my wife....

    Well, there;s more to come; my 6 string bass, a drummer's throne as a seat, knick knacks here and there... then some books on scales to practice - actually brush up as I'm not totally new to music....

    I look forward to one full week without them friggin ciggies, and THANKS again you people. Tell your friends who are still smoking; things smells and taste different when you stop smoking, and after climbing up a flight of stairs, your heart doesn't sound like African drums. Stamina is a lot better, but I have to be wary of my impatience on the road while driving, and my temper - wait kenna wack by Ah Beng truck driver...

    Oh one funny incident to share: drive to Funan in my Jag, parked it and went shopping around. Came back to carpark and went looking around for 30 minutes for a mini-van instead of my Jag. Haaaahahaha.. nearly called the Police, then realized the key I was holding has the Jag emblem.....walao damn embarrassing, lucky I was alone....
