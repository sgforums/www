    if u're not 18 yet, they will not call u up. the school will send the nominal roll to cmpb. this applies to govt institutions like JCs and polytechnics. this applies even if u've been medically classified.

    if u're nearing 18, go to cmpb and enquire with the counter. they will confirm with u ur status.

    u can defer for any courses up to diploma or 'A' level (or its equivalent) in recognised local institutions like polys and JCs, and once your place is confirmed, the deferment is automatic. if for some reason they screw up and wan to call u up, go to CMPB, explain and show them a proof that u're a student of RP (get it from ur AA office), they will defer u straight away.

    private schools generally are a no-go, u need to check with cmpb whether it's recognised.
