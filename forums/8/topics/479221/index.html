    Originally posted by ItsNicoleKam:

    How to tell if my string is steel or nylon :(

    If the string taste metalic, its steel. If it taste bland, its nylon.

    Similarly, to test if your guitar is classic or acoustic, you simply measure the width of the frame where the strings are attached. If the strings are very close to each other, its acoustic and the strings are generally steel. If its quite spacious, then its classic and the strings are nylon.

    Acoustic guitars are more for strumming. Classic guitars are better for plucking.
