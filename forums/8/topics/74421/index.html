    I did it last year, it feels great not to wear glasses or contact lens anymore! My eyesight is still 20/20!

    Although they say your eye sight can worsen after a few years, the chances are when you do it when you're in your 20s, in your 30s you start having far sightness as well, thus it counters acts against short sightness.

    Besides, a correction is only a few hundred. An operation per eye is around $2500. Inclusive of doctor fees and other fees, total cost for both eyes is $5500.

    Each year you spent $250 on contact lens, $100 per pair of glasses , $100 on solutions and tablets for cleaning and storage of contact lens each year, for 10 years = $(250+100+100) x 10 = $4500.

    Thus, I guess you pay $1000 for comfort and convenience. Heh...
