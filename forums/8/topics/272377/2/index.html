    Ashoka , Prakrit Imperial title: Devanampriya Priyadarsi, "He who is the beloved of the Gods and who regards everyone amiably") (304 BCE Â– 232 BCE) was an Indian emperor, of the Maurya Dynasty who ruled from from 273 BCE to 232 BCE. His empire stretched from present-day Afghanistan and parts of Persia in the west, to present-day Bengal and Assam states of India in the east, and as far south as Mysore state.
