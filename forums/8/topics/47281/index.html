    If you want this, you will first need to get rid of the excess fat around your tummy. After that you will need to do crunches and not just normal sit up which is actually useless. Not sure if you know what is this exercise but i try to describe it as best as i can.

    For this it is sort of like sit up but quite different, you lie down in the normal sit up position first.Then from your lying position, use your hand to cup your ears. Nvr put your hand behind your head to give support cos if u do so when your raise your body, you will be using your arm muscle to life your body and not totally on the abdominal muscle (abs) which you should be isolating to do the work

    Well after you have cupped your ear, slowly exert your stomach muscle to raise yourself, also try not to use your leg muscle to help in this.You do not have to raise yourself too high. How to gauge on this you may ask. Well if all the previous steps r done properly, you will feel your stomach muscle crunching up together and the pressure of this effort is all concentrated on it. Thus this isolate the effort all on the abs which will allow it to build up faster.

    However this is only for the upper abs, for the lower abs and the side abs, you will need to do inverse crunches, which is lying down totally and then using your lower abs, raise both legs until u can feel the same sensation as when u r doing the normal crunch. For side abs, you basically do normal crunches but at the same time of raising your body, do a twist to the left or right alternatively.
