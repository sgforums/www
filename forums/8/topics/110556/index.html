USB 2.0 running at 480 mbits per sec transfer rate, however has to share resources with whatever peripherals which may be also connected to your system (e.g USB mouse and USB keyboards, USB modem, USB camera, USB printer, USB scanner etc) So if you got a 'crowded' PC, most probably your performance expectation of the connected USB 2.0 devices may be affected, provided all your connected USB devices are runningat the same time.
