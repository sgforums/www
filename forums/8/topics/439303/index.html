    $20 is the minimum available balance you need in your bank account. There's also another balance which is the Ledger balance, which is not an updated version of balance in your account. Ledger account balance means the transaction that has been performed but not yet send through.

    Exmaple would be

    Available balance: $19.90
    Ledger balance: $250.90

    In this case, you will not be able to withdraw your money because it's below $20.

    Sometimes, even if your bank have $20.10, your bank may not allow you to withdraw that sum of money.
