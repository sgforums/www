    I went for steward interview at a hotel around 10.00am in the morning. I saw a long queue waiting especially for the stewardess part
    (got fat, old, skinny etc).

    after one hour of waiting, i managed to register myself and was called to a room where we will catch a video clip about how to become a steward/stewardess.. blah blah blah.. this round, i waited for 2 hours then went to the basement for the actual interview..

    there are three rounds of interviews...

    ten person went to the first round where we must introduce ourselve and what is the reason to join SIA...

    I was at the fourth one to introduce myself. the first three did very badly as they only tell how are the good benefits they going to have... then when my turn, I just said, I believe everyone here including myself have this passion to join SIA. before i talk more about myself. I wish everyone here the best.. SIA is a place to prove that our service in singapore is excellent and will be world class standard.. blah blah blah... guess what? four person including myself were enlisted only. the rest were rejected on the spot. So realistic..

    but then, i did bad in my second round... they asked those scenario how will you react if something happened..

    Although i failed but i am glad that i take up my courage to go interview
