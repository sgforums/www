    how much are they?

    you may have to pay GST for them..

    if you are 18 and above, you have GST relief..

    - for less than 24 hours, $50
    - for between 24 hours and 48 hours, $150
    - for more than 48 hours, $300

    which means if you have been away from Singapore for less than 24 hours, and you buy about $200 worth of stuff, you have to pay the GST for $200 - $50 = $150 of the stuff you brought back

    which is $150 x 5% = $7.50 to Singapore Customs (which is different from ICA, the fellas checking your car and passport)

    if the stuff is considered Health Supplements, and not controlled by HSA, it will be treated as normal things which does not need a permit... if it controlled or listed by HSA, it is best to as HSA about it..
