    Replacement of lost Student's Pass

    Do I have to report to ICA if I lost my Student's Pass?

    Foreign students who have lost their Student's Pass and/or the Visit Pass and Disembarkation/Embarkation card are required to apply for a replacement within 14 days from the date of loss. The student is required to call in person at ICA Student's Pass unit with the following documents:

    Documents Required

    - Valid travel document and a photocopy of the personal particulars page;
    - A letter from the school stating that the applicant is currently a registered student;
    - Form 16 duly completed and signed by the applicant; and
    - Police report (original)

    The processing time is 3 working days (excluding the day of submission and collection).

    (source: Loss of Student Pass)
