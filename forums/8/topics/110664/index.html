    depending on which level u are in and wat sort of event.......

    are u the full-time employee of the event co. or like those part-timer tt oni help out on the day itself

    the full-time ones will be in charge of everything from planning, looking for suitable premise, finding sponsors, sending out invites, advertising, arrange for food or refreshments, hiring temps to help out, sounds and light system blah blah blah.....alotta big and small things to be taken care of.............
    major events can be planned almost a year ahead......others roughly 3 months of preparations.......

    full-timers' working hours are "fixed" but OT is common bcos u can't get the right person at the right time and u have to wait loh.......and some things can oni do on weekends......so ur weekend have to be burnt.....
    u oso need to be outspoken, good at networking, know where and who to pull strings, resourceful......
    be prepared to meet problems and delays in schedules.......this always happens......mus have backup plans or know the ways around it to prevent further delays or spoilers.....

    event executive.....usually is the lower level one....jus coordinating and executing accordingly, most active on the day of the event itself.....minimal participation in the planning and preparation stages.....
    there would be a training or briefing session prior to the event to let u know wat the event is abt and wat u will be doing/where u will be stationed.......
    such ppl have to be outspoken....have to dare to approach ppl (like those credit card, starhub roadshows) and talk and introduce the product.....
    u get to meets lotsa ppl, face rejections, nasty remarks and all tt.....it's a good learning experience......be prepared to stand or walk whole day oso.....

    there are many types of events......roadshows are most common.....concerts.....product launches......fashion shows.......charity gala......company D&D......
    it's bery good exposure and a good opportunity to widen ur network and gather contacts so in future u know who to look for to get wat......

    salary-wise, quite standard lah....see ur qualifications, which level u are in......but most ppl are not doing it for the money....it's for the passion of making things happen, meet ppl and see ur hard work become a success......
