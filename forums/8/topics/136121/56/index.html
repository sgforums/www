    TOKYO - A MAN has confessed to killing three people, including a 14-year-old boy, by forming fake suicide pacts through websites that contained information on group suicide, according to a newspaper.

    After killing the woman, he searched for more victims through suicide websites and killed the boy and the university student. He allegedly dumped their bodies in nearby mountains and a dam.
