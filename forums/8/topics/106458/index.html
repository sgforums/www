    She sits at the window watching the fog as it rolls over the landscape. Her thoughts are as thick as the air. Always, always it seems, it is him she is thinking of.
    Her mind wanders aimlessly over the past few months. She pulls lint balls mindlessly from the sofa cushion and then stretches her legs out before her. His eyes. Oh his eyes that have such a sparkle and a gleam. She smiles.
    Even in the lonely moments when he is not present she feels him. It's as if he is a part of her and she takes comfort in knowing that he will soon be home. In her arms, with in her sight and she will know the sound of his laughter, the feel of his touch that makes her skin tingle, the warmth of his embrace and the firey passion of his kisses as they settle down for the night. Again, she smiles.
    It wasn't very long ago when she was trying to imagine life without him. Events had kept them apart. She had gone about her days wearing a mask to disguise her true feelings of loss and despair. Nothing made sense to her in those hours and minutes without him. Try as she might she could not forget about him. She felt him in the air, saw him in the blue of the sky, heard him in every song that played on the radio, cried through each love story that spoke of what might have been. Understanding eluded her as the feeling of something missing could not be fulfilled with in her.
    Until one day there came a knock at her door and when she opened it he was standing there looking for all the world like a man coming home. In that moment she knew what had been missing. She knew with a sudden clarity that this was her destiny. This man with whom her heart had claimed months before was what was missing! It mattered not where he had been for he was here now and she knew it was fate that had brought him to her door.
    They talked for several hours that day. He was still the most handsome man she had ever seen and when he was ready to leave he took her face in to his hands and placed the softest kiss upon her lips. It became the beginning of what was meant to be and as her mind replayed the moment he became hers she heard the soft crunching of his tires in the gravel drive. The sound of his truck door opening and then closing. Soon his key would turn in the lock and with in moments his face would appear in the door. She was awash with anticipation as she sighed a deep sigh of contentment and pure joy!
