Sentosa LRT

  • tranquilice
    30 Mar 04, 14:30
    Sentosa LRT, currently named "Sentosa Express", is now ongoing. The pillars of the viaducts near the Sentosa ferry terminal have been constructed, following will be in other areas.

    "Sentosa Express" will be elevated and will runs from HarbourFront MRT station to Palawan beach via the Sentosa causeway, taking a total of 6 minutes. According to source, it will have 3 stations in Sentosa, which are near the ferry terminal, near the Merlion and Palawan beach near Palawan monorail station.

    You may have noticed that "Sentosa Express" will not cover most parts of Sentosa like the monorail. These places not covered by "Sentosa Express" will continue to be covered by existing internal bus services.

    The line is expected to complete by April 2006 and will fully replace the Sentosa monorail.
