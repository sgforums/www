    in spore...there exists a grp of ppl in the local discourse called Ah Bengs, Ah Sengs, Ah Huays and Ah Lians...the former and the latter 2, differentiated by gender of male and female respectively...
    they seem to be ard for a very long time..and constitute observations tt are unique to thie island...

    Ah Beng, Ah Huay, Ah Lian, Ah Seng -- all there are actually shortcut names in hokkien...
    a chinese name usually has 3 characters...the first his/her surname and the other 2 constitute his/her personal name...
    the chinese oso have dialect names...
    sometimes..out if convenience...the individual weill be addressed by one his his/her dialect character, prefixed by "ah"....
    therefore...Ah Lian and Ah Beng were also similar forms of addresses by either kin or close fren...

    however..."ah beng"..in 2day's context...no longer refers to a form of personal address...it refers to a grp of ppl with a set of characteristics...

    some ppl see the squat toilets as solution for dirty toilet seats...apart frm straining the old legs...crouching in a cubicle is juz so undignified...
    but, of cos...we muz consider ppl who are predisposed to squatting, like the ah bengs..who squat anytime anywhere cos they somehow think tt it's a manly pose tt zaps the babes...mainlu the ah huays (thus the ah beng love talk: if u relli lerf me, squat wif me till the end of time)...it does not help tt many rock stars also adopt the squat on their album covers...

    ah bengs can be classified according to charateristics:

    style -- ah bengs are certainly not trendy...trendy itself is another mythologised concept...however.."to be trendy is to do wat everyone else isn't"...as such..ah bengs are assoc. wif the style tt belong to the masses...

    bad breeding & manners -- ah bengs squat on toilets...they squat anywhere...they haf bad breeding..

    can be rich -- they belong to the upper class...

    not well-liked -- obviously...

    born ugly in behaviour -- "predispositioni"..."breeding"...

    a homogeneous group

    ah beng thus become a multiple personality of all sorts...
    however...in bringing 2gedder all these statements...we can see tt in reality, no such person actually exist to have all these qualities...
    as such...ah beng here exists as a myth thru which discourse feed its matter...
    ah beng is appropiated so tt a caricature and stereotype can be created...however...this stereotype needs a name...thus "ah beng" bcomes the convenient label tt can mean anything tt is socially distasteful...

    thru the various discourses tt haf constructed the negative image tt is the ah bengs and ah lians...these labels themselves become reified to bcome labels to tok abt other things...
    as such...u can have a taxi-driver criticising the ah beng wo disturbed his daughter n oso criticising his own daughter as dressing like an ah lian bcos she wore a pair of shoes wif a flower in front...it thus becomes a label tt a person can criticse on things tt he/she does notlike...

    in this sense...ah beng and ah lian becomes "overdetermined"...it is filled wif infinite meanings tt is not its original but can distort the original...
    the manifest content of ah beng bcomes a condensation of multiple constructions, which can oni be seen in its "latent content"...but it is oni thru work of deconstruction and relation tt its infinite meanings can be exposed....

    the intentions to snub at ppl of a lower class is reflected thru his tone tt pts out the foreign invasion of the ah bengs and his relatives..of a place tt is actually trendy until the invasion took place..
    ppl who do not conform to a certain standard of behaviour e.g. squat in public or dress in certain manner...are all criticised...
    all these comments arise from an intolerance of either non-conformists behaviour and appearance or of a snobbery of ppl who r not of a certain social status...
    this snobbery or intolerance is grounded in institutional phenomena...the larger relations at play are the existing social classes or statuses and the boundaries tt delimit wat is acceptable and wats not...

    the power network tt underlies these relations lie unexposed as it is displaced from the text confronting a reader-consumer who is not aware of its mythical content...as such..the reality of things is also displaced...
