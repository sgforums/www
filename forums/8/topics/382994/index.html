    I was unfortunate to have dropped my new EZ-Link card (with quite a large stored value in it). Prior to the lost, I have submitted the EZ-Reload application about 2 weeks earlier.

     

    I called TransitLink office to report the lost and also to haul the application. They assured me that everything would be settled on their end.

     

    To my surprise when I received the letter from TransitLink a few days ago, informing me that my application was successful. As the letter states that the facility needs to be activated before it could be use, I ignored the letter.

     

    I got a shock when I looked thru my bank statement and discovered that an EZ-Reload amount has been deducted from my GIRO account for my lost card. This contradicts to what was stated in the letter. I called up TransitLink immediately. The lady on the receiving end confirmed that my card had been reported lost, but was unable to provide an explanation for the deduction. She was apologetic and told me that she would escalate this issue and process with the refund. The refund process would take approx. 1.5 months!

     

    Lesson learnt? Always look through your bank statement unless you are charitable to all organisation (and in this case, you can always “donate” to me.. *lol*)
