    My theory...

    1) Letters are meant to be for another doctor to read.

    2) You might have a condition that would bring you into self-denial and not seek medical condition at all.

    3) The doctor writing the letter might not know your problem. So he writes a letter stating his observation and request the other doctor to review. The doctor don't want you to speculate your own problem.

    5) Prevent you from "planning your assualt" on the next doctor.
