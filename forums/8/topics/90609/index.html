  • X-men
    23 Aug 04, 11:42
    The wait for our Olympic medal continues as Li Jia Wei failed to win a bronze medal after losing 1-4 to South Korea's Kim Kyung-ah in a women's singles table tennis match on Sunday.

    On Saturday, when unseeded North Korea's Kim Hyang Mi beat sixth-seeded Jia Wei 3-4 after she had lead 3-1 to advance to the final, some how I have a funny feeling that she might just end up the same path Jing Jun Hong took 4 years ago which is to come home empty handed.

    Nevertheless, Jia Wei has tried her best and I'm thankful for what she did. Maybe its the pressure that got better of her, maybe its the eagerness to win a medal made her lose her cool. Maybe even the shouts from the North Korean with every points she took scares her...

    When the tv did a close up on the 2 gals, you can see that Jia Wei some how lack the 'killer' look on her face. Maybe she needs the killer instinct to beat her opponents.

    I don't understand why Tan Howe Liang can do it 44 years ago where training and sport facilities were not as good as the current days. Why can't we win an Olympic medal now even with foreign talents.

    Our swimmers which we can be proud of in SEA games, 'drowned' in the Olympic pools. Finished at the bottom half of those taking part.

    Sailers also performed badly even though we have an asian game gold to be proud of courtesy of Dr Ben Tan.

    Soccer... hopeless?? Goal2010 is way too high a dream when we can't even win gold in SEA games.

    With sports schools, foreign talents...What else can we do to win medals. Are we just to busy making money to pay loans... (now have to make babies ) that we can't concentrated on sports?
