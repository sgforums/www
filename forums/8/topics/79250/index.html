Free food with choice of pillows.

  • Hydro

    FROM toys to pillows and free food, some Singapore hotels have come up with novel ideas to woo guests in a bid to regain ground lost when the industry was hit by Sars last year.

    According to figures from the Singapore Tourism Board (STB), the average occupancy rate declined from 74 per cent in 2002 to 67 per cent last year. More significantly, the revenue earned per room went down 18 per cent from $94 to $77.

    Advertisement

    Since last month, Swissotel The Stamford has provided a play corner with games and toys in its rooms for guests travelling with children.

    Also looking out for children is Shangri-La's Rasa Sentosa Resort. Those under 12 eat for free at buffets, as long as they are with one paying adult.
