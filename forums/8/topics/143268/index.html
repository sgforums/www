    My colleagues and friends (who own Parrots) tell me that parrots are territorial. I don't think they like "intruders" or changes. Your parrot will be very possessive so anyone or thing coming near you or between you both (you and parrot) will trigger some reaction from the parrot. I was also told parrots can get depressed and suicidal.

    Did you do some homework before buying it? You may have to give one up. I don't think they can/will get along. Sorry to say that.
