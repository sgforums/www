    Do you remember the names Toh Hong Huat and Keh Chin Ann? In the late 80s, you would not have passed by a public place without seeing their faces. The two primary six boys were best friends from Owen Primary school who were last seen at the middle of the day in 1986 - and to this day, their whereabouts are unknown. The case of the two missing schoolboys are some of the most (in)famous cases involving Singaporeans that remain unsolved today.

    Toh Hong Huat and Keh Chin Ann were just ordinary school boys who came from a very humble background. When I first heard the news about this case, I was emotionally affected. One of them was just living opposite my block ( my old home in T P) and I had seen him before as a little kid growing up.

    School was just a short walk away. At 12.30 pm, they were last seen together. Then suddenly…never again till this day! Their books and bags were left near a tree. NO ONE …yes, not one soul around that spot, saw anything that could help the police to solve the case.
