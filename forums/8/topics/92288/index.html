    The paper "may" seem useless. The time "may" seem wasted.

    Perhaps the real value of a tertiary/uni education is the journey that you took to reach that stage of receiving of your diploma/degree.

    Education is a lifelong journey. It is never wasted on anyone even if it does not apply to their current job portfolio ...
