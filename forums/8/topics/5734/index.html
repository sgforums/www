    Here is what I have understand thru interaction and my own understanding of love:

    1)Love is something when you take away all the hatred, all the pain, all the beautiful and all the memories, yet you are concerned about the other person and still feel for the other person.
    2)Every now and then, even years later, something reminds you of the other person - a certain smell, a few notes of a song, a place, a few words and some item which trigger your thoughts - and suddenly you feel just as bad as the day the other person had told you he/she never wanted to see you again. If you want to know the honest truth... you never completely get over a person you really cared about.
    3)The way I see it, people are either meant to be together or they're not.
    4)What makes a relationship is not whether how smart, good-looking or any kind of good qualities that one must possess, all you need is X-factor, that thing which allow you to attract the other party.
    5)If love, whether human or divine, was the solution to the person's problem, it was not the one that could be given; it had to be bestowed by a gift. That is the most difficult part for a person in his/her search for a relationship. If love is ever a choice, who would want exquisite pain.... *sigh*
    6)Romantic love is a dream where we wasted half our lives searching for, and realize in the end, we came here alone since we are born and in the end, leave this world alone as well.
    7)There are 2 tragedies in our lives, one is not getting the r/s which we want, and the other is getting the r/s we want which ended in realizing the other person is not the One. I always prefer the latter. What about you?
    We do what we must in life... Sometimes we can choose the path we follow. Sometimes our choices are made for us. And sometimes we have no choice at all. Love is something that we try to seek the path, but never know where we are all the time...
    9)It is always surprising how small a part of life is taken up by meaningful moments. Most often they are over before they start even though they cast a light on the future and make the person who originated them unforgettable. So much is those fast going romances, where you find a moment that you love someone and then he's gone from your life and it is a flash of the moment.
    10)If you love someone, set her free. If she comes back to you, she is yours forever, and if she doesn't, it is never meant to be.. It is something sad yet, we have no control over these things...
    11)I wish that someone could come into my life and love me, then set me free from the shadows of the past and push me to higher heights of life. But love is not about trying to get someone to relieve your pain, but to make you a better person by loving someone.
    12)Love is the wierdest thing.. At one point, you think you will not love this person, and somehow, fate makes a twist and you realize that this person is not that bad afterall, and then you decide to love this person.
    13)Love comes when a person least expects it... So, don't bother to search, it will just comes to you when you don't bother about it...
    14)Love & Lust belong to Desire, and Desire is always cruel.
    15)"You are mortal: it is the mortal way. You attend the funeral, you bid the dead farewell. You grieve. Then you continue with your life. And at times the fact of her absence will hit you like a blow to the chest, and you will weep. But this will happen less and less as time goes on. She is dead. You are alive. So live." from Dream to those who cannot get over with losing their loved ones to Death...
    16)Love takes hostages.It gets inside you. It eats you out and leaves you crying in the
    darkness, so a simple phrase like "maybe we should just be friends" or "how very perceptive" turns into a glass splinter working its way into your heart.
    17)When you wake up from your dream the next morning, realize that the other person is no longer by your side, you will be feeling pain and sorrow for the loss, and wonder why you are still in the same position as before. Love is a double edged sword, it heals and yet hurts at the same time.

    Such are my thoughts to love, will add if I have something new in my mind....
