Bad experience with a plumbing service

  • Black-night
    18 Jun 08, 14:10

    Recently saw an ad in The Cats Straits Times page about some plumbing services...It mentioned that they will arrive within half an hour. The ad is as shown below under plumbing:

    Clear all chokes with hijet auto
    machine, replace toilet bowl, basin,
    water heater, dustbin, cistern,
    taps, ceiling leakage, door lock,
    apply permit, etc.

    Fast & reliable(24-hr)
    no obligation.
    Arrive within half an hour

    Paid for the service $200 and mentioned that there was 1 year guarantee. Due to some clogging of the pipe, the person mentioned that it needed one night for the chemical to dissolve the solids stuck inside.

    The next day, tried to call the person up but he refused to. No matter how much I tried.

    Went to CASE to make a complaint then realised that the business does not seems to be registered and cannot be done.

    So, just wanna let people out there know that there are illegal business who are operating, they can happily advertise and yet not be penalised in anyway since they are not formally registered, no add, yet nothing can be done about it. It sucks but it is an important lesson to be learnt.

    So, do take serious thought and consideration even deciding to purchase anything.
