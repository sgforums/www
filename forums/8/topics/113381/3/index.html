    when tv ads often sang "jiang yi jiang, ting yi ting, da jia xue hua yu..."

    when bus passes come in the form of flimsy cards and you have to queue at the bus interchange every month to buy the stamps....

    when S$ and ringgit can both be used in Singapore...

    wah... too many.... can't list everything here..... nostalgic times....
