    I remember the primary school workbooks back then..... they were made of glossy and really flimsy low-quality yellow paper. When I started using pens in Pri 3, I remember having to be careful not to apply too much pressure. No point tearing the pages for nothing.

    I was really young, but I remember that the 80s up till the early 90s were the golden era of cartoons. Bionic Six, GI Joe, Ghostbusters, He-Man and The Masters of The Universe... Those were classics. While some of these cartoons have been remade recently, they still lack the spirit of the original.

    My favourite cartoon of all time though has to be MASK.

    I remember having one of the toys: a green motorbike called Condor which could transform into a helicopter. My brother had Firefly, a 4WD vehicle which could turn into a plane. My cousin had the big bad boss..... a purple helicopter which could turn into an airplane.

    We would spend hours battling each other.
