    for my time...entitled to 14 days per year....normally after BMT...there will be block leave for U....meaning U can take a few days off before U report to your new unit...how many days differs....anyway...block leave is also covered under the 14 days per year thingy...so....

    During your NS....U might be given off days and so on...the difference between the 2 is that during off days...U are still recall-able by your unit and U cannot leave singapore during off days...even if it's just across the causeway....leave is more flexible in the sense that U will not be recall-able(unless maybe during exceptional cases) during your leave period...and U can leave singapore provided U clear the necessary paperwork like your exit permit with your unit S1....

    so assuming U kept all your leave and offs until U are close to ORD...then U can start to plan clearing leave....clear your off days first then your leave....because off days can be forfeited...leave cannot....
