    I too started out paying for folio and ended up with no jobs. so now i'm freelancing and getting photos done myself.

    agencies,if "reputable", they take u in, and u dont have to pay as they'll send u for assignments, whereby u get more pix to build your folio upon.

    alternatively, actively source out fotographers and once you have a couple of "decent" looking shots, show them to the agencies.

    Read more magazines and check out which agencies the models are from.

  • supercharger
    12 Oct 04, 19:21
    Ya I agree, if the agency is reputable, then you dont have to pay for photos but usually unless you are talent spoted etc.. otherwise you will still need some photos to start. I used to make money shooting people! hahaha and I can tell you that the problem with the smaller agencies are that they often struggle with assignments and their contacts for jobs are few. So they have one of several approaches to marketing the agency... they want tons and tons of pics and hope that one of the fresh face girls will interest some client to bite. But since they seldom have any official tie up with photographers and ad agency and etc... and they have small budgets, they will often ask that the girls get their own portfolio or that they pay $ and the agency will engage a photographer to shoot them.

    anyway the competition out there is really quite intense so unless you are really pretty or that you have a rather unusual or interesting look etc its hard to get into the larger agency. Oh the smaller agency usually end up getting the girls to do jobs like show openings etc instead of just shoots

    So instead of paying the agency to shoot a portfolio for you of which the quality and variety you cannot really dictate, you might as well engage your own photographer but again it will be costly.

    But you have to start somewhere.

    all the best
