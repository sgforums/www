On NSmen in Taiwanese uniform

  • no heart
    29 Oct 00, 14:25
    Singaporean soldiers training in Taiwan wear the uniform of Taiwanese soldiers. If China attacks Taiwan, how will it tell the difference between soldiers from Singapore and Taiwan?
    This was a question put to SM Lee Kuan Yew by a foreign correspondent from Bloomberg on Friday night.

    Mr Lee replied: "We wear their uniforms but not their insignias. We do it so as not to appear outlandish or offensive to their local population. If we are caught up in a war? I suppose we could be mistaken and be shot."

    He said to laughter from the audience: "Our NSmen know, their parents may or may not know."

    He said it would be useful for all sides to know that there is a third party involved so that some advanced notice could be given. "So that if we get out, then obviously something is going to happen."
