    Just go down with your old passbook and queue with your NRIC.

    Try to instruct them to print out the previous transactions statement and give you a new book.

    You can also opt to use your current passbook on the machines and print out the transactions before getting a new book.

    Do enquire about changing your atm card too.  The new card can be collected on the spot.  Dont have your name inscribed on it one.

    Do consider also getting the DBS Master Debit or the Go card.  Lots of new functions as well as discount or privilege function.
