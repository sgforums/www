    Time is not everything. No doubt 3.5 yrs is quite a long period that u've been together. But dun let this be a measurement of the depth of this relationship. And it should not be the reason that u shld stay with him.

    The crucial issue is: Do u still love him?

    U have to consider the fact tat u juz started out working. All those changes and the new things that u are exposed to rite now will not stay fresh and fun forever. So if the reason u wanna breakup with him is coz u wan to go out and "see the world", do think through carefully. Perhaps u can both compromise, let him take u pubbing or do something new for a change and bring the sparks back to yur relationship. Afterall, this may be juz a temporary phase u are going through.

    As for the quarrels, have u ever examined the cause of it? Some couples are simply argumentative, quarreling over little things that does nothing to hurt the deep feelings they have for each other. Some even enjoy the "sparks" that flew during the arguments and esp the apologies and making up after that. However, its tiring (I know coz I used to quarrel a lot with my ex, at least once per week) in the long term. So look at the reason behind this. Is it due to the clash of personalities or difference in values. And then start resolving from there.

    As a relationship develops, there's bound to be some point in time when u feel tired and want to give up. But remember this, a relationship does not only take love to survive, wat ultimately matters is tolerance. Dun give up, put in some effort and see how it goes first b4 taking any drastic actions, ok?
