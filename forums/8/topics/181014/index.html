    If you had a box... now this box you can't open but you are told there may or may not be a RED ball inside. Now some people lets call them group A would have faith that there was indeed a RED ball inside and that it was as simple as that.

    Now you would have another group of people that would try to measure the box , weigh it , pick it up and shake it, analyse what the box is made from, perhaps even pick at the tape holding the box together. Lets call them Group B.

    A would declare that the mere attempt of scientifically studying the box would undermine the "teachings" and very idea of the ball in the box, and say that it shouldn't be done. B would of course disagree and claim that only scientific knowledge can answer the question of is there a ball in the box.

    So why would both groups disagree with each other, after all you can't open the box so it is very hard to proove whats inside, which means nobody loses...... or do they. what happens if group B prooves that there is nothing in the box using what ever manner... what then?? does A simply ignore the evidence and carry on believing or do they pack up and go find a wardrobe that may or may not have a coat in it.

    So you have the believers A and the non believers B you also have another group that do believe there is somthing in the box but the think it is a blue pyramid. group C shall we say.

    What I am trying to say is until it is proved that that there is or not somthing in the box whats the point in arguing and fighting.

    but maybe we already know why we fight and argue about a subject such as religion... the only group with somthing to lose are those that believe.

    PS. if there is nothing in the box....Who made the box???
