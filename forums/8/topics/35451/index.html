    When the NOKIA 8250 was first introduced, it was marketed at a crazy price of $950, with a Two Year Plan.

    Three years later, it is offered at only $75, with a Two Year Plan.

    Most phone companies try to recover their R+D costs from the first few months of the product launch, from the keen interests of buyers, who have the urgent desire to be the first owners of a new product.

    This is the price to be paid for "exclusiveness".
