Bad Experience with UOB services

  • Degenhard
    3 Aug 09, 17:40

    my wife and i moved from Germany to Singapore since Jan 2009. we are just wondering if any of you have ever encountered bad services from both UOB banking and Credit card service.

    recently i had these problems:

     

    two months ago, i wrote to UOB Credit card centre asking for the urgent credit card approval due to my 3 weeks trip to USA. my wife (a medical doctor) and I (Manager) are both overqualified for the platinium card. two days upon submission of my application, i received call from the UOB CCC (Credit card centre) saying that the my application has been denied. when i asked why? the caller says she cannot disclose the reason. However, a week later they send me another letter saying that they have overlooked my qualification and asking me to send in additional documents to support my application. well, i did it and now it's been 1 month over, I receive neither call nor credit card from them at all. On the other hand, I have to say that i am very satisfied with America Express, they listen to me carefully and make a special arrangement so that i receive the card within 24Hr. at the end i selected Amex.

    two days ago, my wife and i went to UOB wanted to open safe deposit box. to my surprise, we're required to keep a fixed deposit of S$10,000 in UOB before we could open a safe deposit box account. of all the banks i asked, none of the require us to fixed deposit S$10,000. btw, their safe deposit box is the most expensive among all other banks.

    My wife and I have decided to close our UOB bank account.

    Curious to know about others' opinions.
