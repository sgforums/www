Since you are still at a young and tender age where your perceptions and views on issues are slowly being formed, I would advise you to take a more passive approach and take small steps at a time. Equip yourself with more knowledge and ALWAYS take a look at both sides of the argument. A lot of people think that being political is only about adopting one view, campaign it as THE only view and think they are being very smart. These people are dumbasses and there are too many of them so please don't become one.

If you really feel a need to get involved, take on political roles that are more within your grasp for example, student government in your school (no matter what they say about prefects of councillors, there's a lot to learn from it). Good luck with that!
