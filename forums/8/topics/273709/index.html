    I am perplexed by the sheer number of Eco posers in my office. FYI, I work in a Market Research MNC agency as a Quantitative analyst. A great deal of my job involves data analysis, cross referring hordes of data, checking data integrity and all. As such, I would always prefer to print out my data in paper so that I can better analyze the data and spot mistakes. However, I am always being scolded if not told off by my seniors or some dumb secretaries for printing the data out on paper. Common, how I want to print and use the office printer paper is my own prerogative. After all, itÂ’s my work anyway. Besides, I would recycle the paper by printing on the other side some time later.

    I believe an Eco fighter is one who exercises Logic and Good common sense in executing his/her work to deliver efficiency and be green at the same time. An eco-poser on the other hand, is an attention grabber who prefers to abandon good business logic and efficiency in a bid to display how good an eco-activist they are, and in the end deliver more damage to the environment.

    Another example would be the deliberate switching off of this huge mega server computer where analyst withdraws and extracts data from the system. Some Eco-posers chicks will at the end of the day, stupidly switch off the computer. Common, dumb woman, to reboot and restart the computer takes 30minutes!!!! Simply put, these eco posers are not exercising logic because the amount of electricity needed to reboot the system would consume at least 5 times the electricity consumption if the computer were to be simply in sleep/standby mode overnight. This is also precisely why Changi Airport turns on the air conditioning 24/7 because it will consume a lot more electricity if the conditioning system were to be turned on and off.

    Another incident would be about the claiming of Cab fees. My company policy allows employees to claim for cab fee after 9pm. For goodness sake, most public transport services are still operational during 9pm. It is illogical to grant cab fee claims so early in the night. In doing so, it simply encourages employees to take cab at will (producing more pollution) and be lazy bums who will not use the public transport even when it is still not so late in the night.

    A more illogical time to accept cab fee claims would be after 11pm. However, I am surprised that these eco posers remain quiet in voicing out their opposition to this 9pm rule. Why the silence? Because Eco-posers are folks who do green stuff for show, but when a certain green measure takes on a severe toil in inconveniencing them, they will be as mute and meek as a sheep. You will be surprised that these green posers go way up the hierarchy to director levels.

    I urge all of you office folks out there, to stand up against Eco-posers. And if your office has established a green team (or something similar), please make sure there are no eco posers in it.
