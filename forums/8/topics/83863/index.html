    Howdy...

    Interested in studying in Australia, aye? Well, I'm doing me Masters in Education in Adelaide. The Ivy League equivalent in Australia would be the Group of Eight. If I'm quite right, they're basically the state universities like Adelaide, Melbourne, NSW, UWA and Queensland. You should be applying to them directly or you could apply through IDP at the RELC building near Shangri-La Hotel in Orange Grove Road (I think). Heaps of options for youse, mate.

    For the courses that you were interested in, I'd say Adelaide is pretty good for Mathematics 'cause we've got a professor who works kinda closely with Steve-o Hawkins himself. For the triple-E course, I've got no idea, mate. But then again... not many people come to adelaide 'cause they find it really boring compared to Singapore. Well, I've grown to love it and I can't stand them city folks up in Sydney (bleah!) and Melbourne (bloody Victorians...).
