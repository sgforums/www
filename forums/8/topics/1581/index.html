    Well, I have to disagree with you on the part about platonic relationship (for argument sake) eventhough u're better than USB.

    The point is that have you ever wonder why some things are meant to be that way? Not every close friend you have are to be the ONE. Let me try and put it another way (I know sometimes I can't express myself properly, but I'll try my best).

    To have a successful relationship, it takes faith and commitment, if either party lacks one of these components, it's not gonna last long. The reasons why some people would prefer platonic relationship over BGR are as follow:

    Firstly, they are not ready for serious relationship. Not because they are immature, but because they are mature enough to realise that it is not times yet or perhaps they are not ready to commit and do not wish to hurt the other party. Perhaps both party do not want to be tied down at the moment because they have some other commitment and realise they cannot juggle two things at a time (in my case).

    Secondly, the person might prefer the freedom that comes along with platonic relationship, so don't want something more.

    Thirdly, why can't two person of opposite sex be just friends? Why must it lead to something more? Sometimes it's not that we put barriers all over to protect ourselves but to preserve the friendship. We take calculated risk in that we don't go for every close friend. Even you don't do that

    And about love being simple, yes, I agree. And if one party should realise that the other party is his/her soulmate, then it was already meant to be in the first place. Just remember that not every friendship is like that.

    If you don't like the person, you wouldn't be close to him/her in the first place.

    About the brother/sister thingy, I would not say it's the same as platonic. It's another kind of relationship that can develop into something more, but not necessary so. Might be something superficial or a disguise though, like you said, someone hoping for something more.

    The kind of love you feel for your close friends are easily confused with the BGR kind of love. Sometimes, the feeling can be more intense than that of BGR and that's when you realise that the one you're with rite now is not the ONE but your close friend is. This kind of problem would be quite sticky and personally, I wouldn't know how to get out of this one.

    By the way, you might think that I'm contradicting myself somewhere along the line, but what I'm trying to say is that, it is possible to have a platonic relationship without going further. Not brother/sister though, to me, there's always somthing fishy about it, it's like a childish kind of relationship.

    Maybe difficult since we always get confused about our feelings, but not impossible altogether to stay the same.
