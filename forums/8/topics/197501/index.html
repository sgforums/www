    1. Take bus 169, after the bus pass Yishun JC (there's a hindu temple beside Yishun JC), be sure to alight once u see MacDonalds at the traffic light junction.

    3. Walk approximately 1-1.5 hours from Yishun mrt to the camp's main gate

    4. Take the cab from Khatib/Yishun mrt, u reach the camp's main gate within 10 mins, at most only 5 bucks
