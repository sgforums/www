    From what I can recall, below is the function of Cc & Bcc

    If u send an email to another person in the Bcc field besides the recipents in To & Cc fields, the recipents in To & Cc fields won't know whoelse u send the email to in the Bcc field as the name will not be shown in the email.

    Another thing to note, if there r 2 recipents(Eg. John & Peter) in the Bcc field, John won't know that Peter receive the email also & vice versa
