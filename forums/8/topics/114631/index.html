    28 Jan 05, 09:25
    What tax will definitely increase every year? Tobacco tax lor.

    The demand for cigarettes is inelastic so the Govt could tweak the tax to help balance the budget by a bit.

    Possible reasons:
    1) banning cigarettes has lots of ramifications - tourism suffers, and FDI drops 'cos no one wants to set up business/factories in countries which do not allow cigarettes.

    2) The tobacco lobby is strong - big ciggy manufacturers fight hard to resist the bans. Sgp is a small country so it cannot resist them. Think chewing gums.

    3) No justification to ban ciggys 'cos they theoretically do not hamper a worker's productivity or judgement, unlike heroin. The smokers in the USA will take their govt to court if the latter dare to ban ciggys totally.

    4) The percentage of smokers in this country and the world is really huge and growing. It is too late to impose a total ban on cigarettes.
    People will find ways to get their fix - ban or no ban.

    5) Govts around the world earn a substantial revenue from cigarette-related activities. No sane govt will want to kill this golden goose.

    6) They countries in the world are waiting for "somebody" to start the process of banning cigarettes. Trouble is, no one will be the fool.
