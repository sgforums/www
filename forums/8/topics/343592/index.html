    Just bring along your identification documents to any bank and request to open an account lo. Do take note that an initial deposit of $100/500/1000 (more details available on banks' websites) may be required to open one, and from what I was told, you can't open more than one account with a bank, so if your current account is with DBS/POSBank, open a new UOB one.

    Since you're 17, and it seems that you're planning to shop online, I would recommend applying for a debit card at the same time as well. For that, once again, you can read up more about applying for one on the website of the respective bank you're opening an account with.
