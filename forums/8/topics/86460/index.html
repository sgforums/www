    It depends on how much devices/pheripherals you are running on your computer. But i think a rough estimation would be about an average of 200watt to 250watt for today's home computer/pheripherals/monitor. Even though you have a 450watt power supply, it is most likely that it will not be running at full load always.

    If the rate($) of electricity in SG is 0.158/kWh

    250W = 0.25kWh
    30d = 30 days
    24h = 24 hours

    Therefore,

    0.25kWh * (30d*24h) * 0.158 = $28.44/month
