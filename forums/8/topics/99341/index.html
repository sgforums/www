    Imagine you are slapped by you GF cos she came upon your stash of porn vcds when u told her u have kicked the habit (apparently u only kicked it under the bed).

    Lets take her hand's mass to be 1kg and the temperature of the hit area to be increased by 1 degree celsius. Take mass of tissue to be 0.1kg and its specific heat to be 3.8kJ/kg.degrees celsius. Calculate the velocity of the hand prior to impact.

    We take her hand and affected area as a system. It is a closed system cos amount of mass remains unchanged. We assume that there is no significant energy/heat loss/gain by the system to/from the surrounding air, therefore the system is adiabatic and no work is done on or by the system. The height of the hand doesn't change during slapping therefore potential energy change is zero. We assume the hand is brought to halt after hitting and the face doesn't move upon being hit.

    Therefore, change in kinetic energy = mass*specific heat*(Delta T) = 0.38
    Therefore, velocity is found to be = 25.2 m/s
