    Hey I'm doing animating stuff using flash too. Just experiment it.

    Here's how I do it :
    -Draw the animation on paper.
    -depending on the type of action I wanna do, I usually draw 5 to 10 frames for 1 sec.
    -I scanned the pics in black and white. (lines only)
    -import the pics to the FLA....but..........
    -I convert the pics to vectors (bitmap tracing) so the size will be drastically reduced. I also add the colors in the vectors.
    -expanding the vectors will not pixelate the picture....cos it's vector liao.
    -so if you convert the vectors to movie clip symbols and place them in sequence...you got yourself a cartoon liao. the SWF will be small size and still rulez.

    For simple animation like talking, walking, eye rolling, hand movement, you can "cheat" the animation and obtain smooth movement with minimal drawing.

    - Draw your character but in seperate parts. Eg. the head, eyes, upper arm, lower arm, palms, body, etc....
    -scan in B/W, import, vectorize and color them in flash.
    -assign the parts into different layers.
    -add motion tween to the layer you want your char to move.
    Eg 1.
    you tween the right upper arm left then the lower arm from right to left to create a smooth effect that looks like the char is doing karate chop.(eg only....needs you to experiment it to get it correct)

    Eg 2.
    you tween the eyeballs left and right and left again to make the character looking around.

    Blah blah....

    Wish you goodluck dude........update us once you've got you first animation......(tips: do watch alot of cartoons to see how they animation their stuff)
