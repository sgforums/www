    It’s in your hands to empower more than 310,000 people in need!

    TrueHearts Show returns for 2009, to raise funds for people with needs in Singapore. The show will be telecast live on 7th June 2009, on Channel 8 from 7pm-10pm.

    The show aims to highlight the plight of people with needs in this current economic climate, inform others on the avenues of help available and to rally Singaporeans to aid others in their community.

    For more information, please visit www.comchest.org.sg.
