    valentine's day has always hold very little meaning for me. never believe in celebrating love in this manner. to me it has always been kinda silly. walk along the street and see girls smiling and holding ther trophy bouquet, teddy bear etc and couple dinning. really, how unromantic.

    i like to be loved and like to be surprised but best when i least expect it, best when its real and simply. like chum driving me to east coast and we just lie on the beach counting stars and listening to the waves and the time when we just knew each other for a while and one night, he suddenly and secretly stuff a lovely necklace with a beautiful star into my hand. the feeling was magical and sweet. a special connection between us two. and these surprises never stop, like recently chinese new year, he came home on the eve with a cute teddy pajamas for me! and all these is love to me.

    but i am not all that cynical cos i do have wonderful valentine's day that were spent with girlfriends (ha, when we were all young and boyfriendless). and when i was younger i was also excited about which boy will send which girl flowers on this special day in school. and those were the days. i was the center of attraction receiving bouquet and gifts from some boys. it was kinda nice to know that someone likes you and yet so embarrassing. fun but nothing like what i have with chum.

    writing about chum will always remind me of all our wonderful moments,
