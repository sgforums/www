    a good presentation dun need cue cards....
    ur powerpoint/transparency/wateber u are projecting shd consist of main points and u, as the presenter shd go point by point, elaborating on them (by memory....it comes easy when u REALLY know ur stuff)

    main eye contact with the audience....if possible....walk ard the room a little (but dun wander too far hor)

    intonation and speed mus be grasped well....
    when stressing impt points, take note to speak a bit deeper and louder.....
    dun tok to fast.....u oni get ur tongue tangled and the wrong words come out....

    hand gestures help too.....

    if u are up to it, throw in a bit of humour at the right places.....the audience need a giggle to keep awake.....

    rber to drink sufficient water before the presentation....not too much until u wanna pee in the midst of it.....

    take a deep breath b4 u start.....make sure u memorise ur stuff well.....keep the flow.....communicate with the audience.....before u know it....it's over!
