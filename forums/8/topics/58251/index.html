    Dear all,

    You might have heard something about the club organising eye screening for the elderly. We are pleased to say that it is coming true and it is called Project I(eye)-Care. What is Project I(eye)-Care about?... The attached doc says it all...

    Status of the project thus far... Funding for Project I(eye)-Care has been secured from the Singapore Eye foundation and North-West CDC. The necessary eye screening equipment has been secured from the Singapore Women Association. There will also be volunteer Optometrists and nurses joining in this project. Four old folks home in the North-West CDC have been identified for this project.

    The first eye screening will commence on the 12th Oct. We would like to ask you to help out on the day. We need volunteers to help setup the screening area and escort each elderly patients during the eye screening. The details are as follows:

    Date: 19th Oct 2003 (Sun)(supposed to be 19th)
    Time: 12 pm - 6 pm
    Venue: Villa Francis Home for the Aged
    9 Mandai Estate Singapore (729906)

    Directions to reach there!

    Please use the following URL to see the map.

    MAP

    The home is located at about the postion of the star along Woodlands Road. If you are travelling towards the causeway, the road is on your right across from the road into Sungei Kadut.

    You can access it from KJE, exit Woodlands Road towards Johor, or SLE, off at Mandai Road, and then turn towards City on Woodlands Road. I recall that there is a sign on the road indicating the location of Villa Francis home.

    If you zoom in on the map, you will see more details.

    For those bussing, the following services run along Woodlands Road: 170, 178 , 925 , 925 , 927 , 960 , 961 , 961 .
