    Yes, an evolution. I am not an expert in Bahasa Melayu but my understanding is that much of Malays residing in Central and Southern Peninsular Malaysia, including Singapore and the Riau Archipelago, converse in Bahasa Melayu-Riau. However I am not too sure if the Bahasa Melayu used during the Malacca Sultanate Age is the Bahasa Melayu we are using today. It may have been slightly different.
