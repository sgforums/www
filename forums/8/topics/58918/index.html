    Knight Ridder Newspapers
    Oct. 6, 2003

    After wearing a vial of Billy Bob Thornton's blood around her neck and having confessed to a fascination with mortuary science and collecting knives, Angelina Jolie is well-known for her unusual tastes.

    Even hardened Lara Croft fans may find her latest admission a little unpalatable, however; the actress has revealed that she likes eating creepy crawlies, reports Hello! magazine.

    "I developed a taste for the stuff while I was in Cambodia," explains the 28-year-old UN goodwill ambassador. "I've eaten cockroaches and bee larvae and crickets. You can get them with peanuts inside or with guts. I like them - they're really meaty and high in protein."

    Jolie has been making regular visits to Cambodia since she adopted a little boy, Maddox, who was born there. She became enamored of the war-ravaged country while making the film "Tomb Raider" and has since donated $1.5 million to help set up a wildlife sanctuary.

    Angelina is currently sampling the local delicacies of Morocco, where she is filming the historical epic "Alexander" with Colin Farrell and Anthony Hopkins.
