    Another point to add from my previous experience. OEM license ties with your PC motherboard, therefore once your PC motherboard is fried or burn out for whatever reason, your OEM license goes with it, since OEM license is not transferrable. You are REQUIRED to purchase ANOTHER license for the replacement PC.
