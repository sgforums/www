    we do it when she was really young..less than 1 week she learn although still got mistakes but now, she know only on newspaper unless the paper is 'full' or someone forget to put but she still do it at the same area..

    put newspaper at the area u wan your dog to pee/shit...cover a bigger area at the start.

    then when the dog do it at the wrong place
    1. Scold your dog or say 'no' loud and firm. This only applicable if your dog still at the area..if it moves away liao, the effect no so great..

    2. W/o letting your dog see, just use tissue or toilet paper, clean up the area nd put the tissue at the newspaper..

    3. Bring your dog go there and smell..

    Must be firm loh...if your dog pee/shit wrong place, must make the dog know 1. If not u not persistent, a while he forget liao.

    My dog will turn round and round when she wan to pee/shit. If u know how yr dog behave, can quickly bring to the newspaper.
