    I believe the answer for your question lies in the 21 gun salutes. The 21 gun salutes is spread out across the during the parade inspection. The first shot of the 21 gun salutes start after the parade commander ask the president for permission to inspect the parade and upon given the permission, the first shot will be fired as the president take the first step. The subsequent shots will be spread out across as the president conduct the inspection.

    So, if the president stops to ask question, the gun salutes will pause, it is a signal for the 2 officers in front to slow down. There is a lot of coordination for that.
