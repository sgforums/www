    Some point to consider when buying mp3 player:

    1. Internal vs External Batt
    External batt (User changable batt) is better.
    firstly,when run out can change on the spot, music continues.
    Secondly, widely available and cheaper compare to proprietry batt when the lifespan is depleted.
    Lastly, better flexibility at charging batt. Internal batt normal charge by connecting to the USB, so if you intend to change your mp3 content, u will have to charge the batt which could be partially discharge...this will shorten the lifespan.

    2. 1.5GB vs 256MB Memory
    Go for solid-state(non movable part) memory as it is more durable. 256MB will provide decent space for your mp3. Any capacity higher tends to be in range of microdrive which will subject to wear and tear. 128MB will be the bare minimum...

    3. Additional features?
    The only features i find useful will be the player double up as a thumbdrive. Therefore check how the player can be connected to a USB port, does it require any other accessories to connect, best if not? voice recording and radio dun often work well, so if you are considering these features u odd to test them out.

    cheers
