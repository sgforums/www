    Actually, it is widely accepted that Cellular signals will remotely affect on-board navigation.

    This remote chance is further reduced by the amount of metal between you and the avionics equipment.

    However, since cell phone manufacturers produces their fone that may emit an unexpected electro magnetic wave. Airlines want to be on the safer side and ask you to switch off.

    Usually only the person sitting directly on top and his immediate surrounding can affect the avionics.

    Moreover, they only affect the electronic avionics. Pilots are trained to use manual navigation. So there is still redundancy
