While I still cringe at every of his kicks, he still lives up to his reputation as a brilliant shot-stopper.

    Cole really should have put the flick-on from Stam away. Yorke was not getting chances and I was thinking of putting in Sheringham for Yorke as early as the 35th min, though a half-time switch would be better. Man U was giving away possession too casually and they needed someone like Sheringham who can hold up the ball. Which is why Cantona is still revered as one of the best Man U players now.

    Throughout the whole match, I failed to see Man U string more than 5 passes together without losing possession. This is something very unusual as Man U is famous for being a passing team.

    Can they beat Real at Old Trafford? I still think so, if they wake up and fight. There were too many occasions where Becks was too involved with defending R Carlos and unable to make his usual runs. More options for Giggs when he makes his runs too. As the commentator said, it's very exciting watching Giggs run with the ball. However, Giggs needs something to do with the ball. He can't just run and run without someone to pass to.

    At top form, I would say that it's a toss-up between Barcelona and Man U for the title. Now? Man U doesn't deserve it from the Real performance. Barcelona would probably have walked away with a win. Chelsea is a good side and here's hoping that they can take advantage of Rivaldo's injury and come out with something good.
