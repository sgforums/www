    Though i only watched 1 episode of Gundam Seed, I would prefer Gundam Wing over it. Wing is not just about Gundam...it portrays the lives of these 5 teenagers who had to battle against the enemy as well as questioning their own roles and actions in the war...If you had watched Wing, you would be familiar with the part where Quatre went depressed over the death of his father due to the colonial war and started to cause mass destruction with the Wing Zero MS. Gundam Wing itells the negative effects of war, which is illustrated to us through the experiences of these 5 teenage soldiers..I had nvr seen an anime so 'emotionally' invoking, especially when the anime is reaching the climax as it is reaching the end.

    Speaking of Gundam, i felt that the gundam designs in Wing were relatively better. You could see that the design of every gundam blended well with the characteristics and personalities of their pilots.

    Songs like "Just Communication","Rhythm Emotion" and "White Reflection" are very good but pale in comparison to the ones in Seed. Seed's main theme was ok but still not up to par with the ones in Wing.

    This series of Gundam is one fine anime to watch if you "know how to watch"...hands up Gundam Wing!!!
