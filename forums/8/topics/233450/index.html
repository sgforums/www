    Has anyone tried a 50 day fast?

    Last year, I read a book on fasting by a writer called Bragg, which mention that Jesus Christ, and Buddha fasted for 40 days.

    I then tried fasting for 40 days without eating any food. I just drank water, coke, pepsi cola. After 40 days, I decided to try for 50 days.

    At the end of 50 day fast, I was thin as skeleton. I was crawling on the ground, instead of walking. I kept fainting, vomiting, nauseating and hallucinating. I feel like I was almost dead. I lost 20 kg.

    After my fast, I quickly drink a few cans of Essence of Chicken. I also ask people to help buy chilli crab, roasted duck, roasted chicken, cereal prawn, fish and chips, for me to celebrate the end of fasting.

    But I could not eat a thing. I push myself to swallow but just vomit out everything, as my stomach and intestines are in hibernation and no longer working, after a long fast. I also could not excrete. I constipated.

    I could not eat nor excrete. It took me almost 2 months to recover to my normal appetite.

    Never try such a fast. It is like committing suicide. Very dangerous to human body.

    Has anyone tried fasting? Care to share your experience?
