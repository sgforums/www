Where to get cheap stationery?

  • Revolution
    23 Jul 00, 15:25
    Allo!

    If you're a poor student like me, you probably hope to find ways of saving on the essentials of school - stationery such as pens and paper.

    The other day I got a recommendation from my friend about a web site called paperworm. Never heard of it before so out of curiousity decided to go and see how its like and whether the prices are good.

    Turned out that paperworm is actually an online stationery retailer, or e-tailer as ppl who know the lingo would say (or at least they think they know the lingo). Not a bad site, cute worm on the front page.

    the prices there are quite good, on a whole. They even have things like ink cartridges and batteries at very competitive prices (definitely much lower than what you get at your neighbourhood store).

    for example, you can get a pentel highlighter for only 60 cents, and a stabilo eraser is only 15 cents! Quality notebooks like Azone ones go for 95 cents, much cheaper than what popular sells.

    the best thing is that they don't need you to pay by credit card. Just order thru the net and pay cash or cheque on delivery... no need to be afraid of sending your credit card number on the net.

    And they are able to deliver to your doorstep by the next working day.

    i'm not affiliated to them, but just wanted to let you all know that there are cheaper sources of stationery for us students than our school bookshops and campus co-ops.

    let me know if you find any other bargain sites!
