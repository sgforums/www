    jiao che is actually "jiao ta che"....which is politically right.....
    in hokkien is "ka-tak-chia"....
    but ppl lazy liao.....become 2 words oni......

    strictly speaking....dialect overrides the beijing chinese we speak in general
    beijing chinese itself is a dialect....but is chosen to be the mainstream chinese in china well as other chinese-speaking countries becos tt time when the cheng hoo is having headache whose dialect shd be "superior", beijing is the main economic and political hub, so they pick beijing dialect as "pu tong hua".....

    with globalisation, cultural exchange and china ppl eagerly picking up english.....they have started to call taxi "de shi" (translation).....sofa as "sha fa"....
    eben SARS oso they call it "sha si"....

    language evolve as ppl interact and learn from each other, borrow from each other......so there isn't any "righ language" or "superior language" in a linguist POV....

    it's the ideal to have a common language across the globe.....but it can oni be an "ideal" so to speak..............
