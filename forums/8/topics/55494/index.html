  • Upscale

    2nd Sergeant Hu En Huai, a 19-year-old full-time NSman passed away on Thursday at the Singapore General Hospital.
    Sergeant Hu was a Guardsman from the 7th Singapore Infantry Batallion.

    He had collapsed earlier in the afternoon during a training exercise at Pulau Tekong.

    He was given immediate medical attention before being evacuated to the Singapore General Hospital by helicopter.

    He died later in the hospital at about 6.15pm.

    The Defence Ministry is investigating the circumstances leading to the incident.
