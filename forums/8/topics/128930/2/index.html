    If you're travelling on Sheares Bridge towards Changi Airport....somewhere near the top (middle) of the bridge, there is an exit on the left (which is the usual case for highways) called "Rochor" (pronounced "Roh-Cho").

    Exit there by turning your steering wheel gently to the left. Don't forget to tap gently on the brake pedal too, if your car is moving too fast.

    After exiting, you will go downhill. Keep to the left lane....and at the first left turning you see, turn left by turning your steering wheel. You will immediately go through a ERP gantry point....and right in front of you is the Fountain of Weath.

    Then you're in Suntec liow lor.

    Look out for signs pointing you to the carpark (there are so many....but all lead to the same underground carpark).

    Remember to switch off your engine before you leave the car.
