    Better to use AYE 'cos although further, its less confusing. From NTU, take the PIE to Tuas, then connect to AYE towards city.....just go straight till Sheares Bridge....exit to Rochor and PanPac is right there just after the exit (on the left).

    If you have to use PIE, then exit at Stevens Road, then go towards Orchard Road....then straight to Marina area.

    Suntec City carpark is so big, with many entrances and exits....all leading to the same hug carpark underground - just remember where you park because its not easy to find a lost car there, due to the sheer size of the carpark.

    But if you go to PanPac, you can park at Marine carpark too (just beside PanPac).

    All carparks in those areas are wider than the typical HDB type, do don't worry.
