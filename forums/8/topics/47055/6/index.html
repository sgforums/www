  • the Bear
    3 Jun 03, 10:27
    almost foolproof way of measuring...

    wash the rice...
    water level should just cover the raw rice and a bit more.. put your hand in, palm flat on the rice... if the water just covers about 3/4 to 7/8 of the back of your hand, it's enough.. more than that, your rice is soggy.. less, it'll probably burn..

    put the thing into the cooker, press "cook" and cover.. you're done

    oh, to get fluffy rice, use wet bamboo chopsticks to stir up the rice just after the cooker automatically shuts off...
