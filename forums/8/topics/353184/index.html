     I was to a recent career fair where i picked up some Astar brochures. This is what one of them says:

    "Fresh post-docs will receive a total annual remuneration package ranging from US$32,000-US$48,000. More experienced researchers will receive a higher remuneration commensurate with experience, training and track records".

    So if you are fresh Phd expect to get about S$4000-S$6000 per month.
    And if you have some experience you can get more than this.

    I also noticed that there also seems to be a lot of interdisciplinary positions available. Eg Bioinformatics where engineering modeling and simulation techniques are applied to proteins and such.
