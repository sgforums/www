In britain long ago, we called the French wogs, then the Indians, Poles and now the Middle Easterners and Muslim Britains (although it is not allowed today; hence why Asia seems to be the place where there is no constitutional ban on the insult). In Australia we called the brits wogs and poms. It has many contexts and usages.
