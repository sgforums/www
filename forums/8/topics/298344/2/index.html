    • So how do I know if it's not due to my phone? Well, cos when I connect via my wireless LAN from home, it's fast fast fast (based on my average Starhub broadband sub via Apple Airport).

    Issues:

    • I kept getting 'connection error' when making voice calls. I.e. cannot call out.
