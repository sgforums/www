    Drive slow.

    You see, the penalty for driving too slow is only 6 (? or 8 I think) at its maximum. That is if u drive too slow 4 times! First time cannot minus marks. The rest of the times, 2 marks each.

    But the penalty for speeding is WOW!.. and when u are fast, u tend to make all sorts of mistakes.

    The idea is to look at the penalty chart to see what sort of mistakes can be made and which can't, so that you can perhaps work out a strategy on how u can sacrifice some parts for some parts else. For example, what I did was, I sacrifice 6 points for driving too slow, so that I can save myself from

    a) Not breaking in time for the lights
    b) Not having a chance to cut out to another lane
    c) killing old people crossing roads.
    d) many many others.

    There are other stratagies you can derive based on your driving skills. Think of it this way.. "I know I normally had to rush for the blinking green lights because I can't turn in time.. so, lets say I purposely drive very very slowly so that I can force myself to stop at every junction then I am safe!!!"
