    This is a reply from the Association of Banks in Singapore to the Forum Page recently (Straits Times 8 Sep: http://straitstimes.asia1.com.sg/forum/story/0,5562,338810,00.html):

    "...However, a written request from customers is not required when a card is sent under the following circumstances and conditions:

    When the card is a renewal or replacement of a card of the same kind being renewed or replaced, or

    When the card is an additional card to an existing cardholder, provided:

    1. The bank discloses to the customer the fees and charges, and terms and conditions associated with the additional card.

    2. The customer is not liable for any amount charged to the card until he has accepted the additional card.

    3. No additional credit limit is granted to the additional card.

    In Mr Leong's case, it is possible the card sent to him is a renewal or replacement card. He should check with his bank whether this is the case.

    Ong-Ang Ai Boon (Mrs)

    Association of Banks in Singapore"
