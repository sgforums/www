    Features the same voice as Super Toy Teddy from the movie "A.I.: Artificial Intelligence"
    Over 200 seconds of speech!

    Batteries included!



    Cute & cuddly, beautifully crafted in luxurious plush fur, this Teddy is the large 15" tall version with articulated arms and legs. This Teddy is most desired by collectors.
