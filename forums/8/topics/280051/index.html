    Camping

    Whether you are having a slumber party or just gazing at the constellations, Singapore's night skies can now be enjoyed from the cosiness of your tents.

    Camping can be done at any of these parks:
    Changi Beach Park

    East Coast Park

    Pasir Ris Park

    Sembawang Park

    West Coast Park

    Pulau Ubin

    Except for Pulau Ubin, a permit is required if you're planning to camp on a weekday. Weekday camping is also limited to designated areas at the parks. You may wish to click on the links below to see the designated camping areas on the maps.

    Changi Beach Park

    East Coast Park, Area C, D

    Pasir Ris Park

    Sembawang Park

    West Coast Park
    Registration for a weekday camping permit is quick and fuss free. All you need to do is to set up your tent and our park ranger will approach you to scan your identification card. Once scanning is completed, the park ranger will print out a verification slip, which will then be attached to your tent. Scanning of the identification cards will be done on a daily basis.

    Camping permit is not required if you are camping on weekends and public holidays.
