    Hey guys, my $0.02 worth here!

    Every Wednesday at Zouk is Mambo nite (i should know because i go pretty regularly, haha)

    *Entry for ladies is free all nite long. Gentlemen have to pay ( not sure about the charge though.. but think it was mentioned already)

    *Entry to Phuture and Zouk itself is free. But you have to pay and be at least 21 years old to enter Velvet Underground.

    * Zouk spins alot of retro, which i personally do not really like because everyone dances the same there. Later on they spin some top 40 hits.

    * Phuture plays R&B, hip-hop stuff. But the music mix tends to be the same fare every weds. eg. "milkshake" by kelis, "where is the love" by black eyed peas. That kinda thing.

    Anyway, will be headed there tonight, so if you need anymore info, feel free to PM me!
