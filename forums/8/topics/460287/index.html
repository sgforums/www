    Whether it's your home or office, it’s always to have good firefighting equipments with you. Standard fire fighting equipments protect people and businesses from fatal fire accidents. It will keep potential fire related hazards out of bay. Fire fighting equipments at home and office should be checked regularly, and regular inspections and servicing should be done to keep them intact. Inspections are done to ensure the equipments functionality, and to maintain all the other vital aspects of fire safety management. Fire fighting equipments include fire detection and alarm systems; emergency and escape lighting systems; sprinkler systems; fire door automatic release mechanisms; portable fire extinguishers; gaseous, foam and powder extinguishing systems; smoke control systems including systems using pressure differentials; evacuation lifts for persons with disabilities; fire hydrants; hose reels; fire mains; fire doors, and other related things. Trained personnel and fire safety training  instructors would know how to check fire fighting equipments.

    Fire fighting machine testing plans include complete testing of new fire safety equipments, maintenance of minor parts and refills. Comprehensive plans for the testing of fire fighting equipments will include the charges for all the services mentioned above. However, if the equipment is damaged by the user third parties or natural calamities, extra charges will be added to the maintenance plans. Replacement of all major parts is chargeable under all the plans.
