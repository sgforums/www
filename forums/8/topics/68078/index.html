    Pheresis is a special type of blood donation that allows a person to donate just one needed part of his or her blood, for example, platelets to prevent bleeding or plasma, the fluid part of the blood. Read on to find out more about this special gift of life. . .

    As part of its continuing efforts to excel in quality donor care, HSA has expanded its apheresis service via its new and fully dedicated Apheresis Suite@HSA.

    Unlike regular whole blood donation where it takes only 20 minutes to complete the donation process, apheresis donation can range from 45 minutes to 2 hours. Each apheresis donor can now enjoy his personal choice of the latest movies and full terrestrial networks via the individual TV monitor attached to each apheresis donation couch.

    In addition, the new Apheresis Suite provides a more comfortable and relaxed environment for its regular donors who not only sacrifice more time at each donation, but also make more frequent donations of up to 6 times a year.

    The expansion of the apheresis service via its new fully dedicated service centre is part of HSA's on-going efforts to upgrade its donation facilities so as to encourage voluntary blood donations.

    www.hsa.gov.sg
