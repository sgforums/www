    The A xx services go directly to the airport. The seats are also wider and the leg spaces are larger. Hence they are more expensive.

    The E xx services go to the various parts of the airport, including the passenger building, catering centre, cargo centre, maintenance centre, Expo, Tung Chung town centre etc etc etc before going towards the city. The E xx services are for the locals, so it's cheaper.

    Normally tourists will prefer the A xx services because it's more direct and more comfortable. But locals will prefer the E xx services because it's much cheaper. For me, I live in HK Island and I have a choice of A11 or E11. I prefer the E11 because E11 costs only $22 but A11 costs $40. (I would rather spend an additional 15 to 20 minutes "touring around" the whole airport and save half the cost.)
