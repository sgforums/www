    Well... If the weather is sunny, and there's no air pollution, you can stay in HK Island and Kowloon for your whole trip.... (HK Island and Kowloon already have many many attractions and places to shop and eat.) If the pollution is bad, you can go to the far-away places which everyone mentioned... (Lantau, Disneyland, Shatin, Shenzhen, Macau, etc)

    For the far-away places, it's advisable to choose only one or two... because transportation is expensive in HK and travelling time is long. (If you wanna go Macau or Shenzhen, you still need to consider the time taken to clear immigration... Waiting time of more than one hour at checkpoints are not uncommon.)

    If time is limited, just focus on HK Island + Kowloon (+ either Lantau Buddha and/or Disneyland).... Next time, you can always take Tiger Airways directly to Macau or Shenzhen.
