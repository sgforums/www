    Originally posted by KTE:
    There is also another type of marketing called network marketing. But always beware of "working" for any company which promises you big bucks by convincing you to purchase their products.
    Network marketing and multi level marketing are the same. Both of them are direct marketing selling directly to consumers without overheads such as rentals, advertisements, middlemen, delivery costs, staff costs, CPF etc

    They use ordinary people and by word of mouth to market the products. No sale, no pay which is a fair system instead of employing sales people and incurring the overheads
    above, no sale still gotta pay, so companies sometimes go bust.

    That being the case, they can actually market very good superior products and pay handsome commision to distributors because of the tremendous costs savings.

    In the past, pyramids and fly by night companies masquerading as MLM companies exploited this advantage and conned people.

    Eversince the law was ammended in June 2000 and reviewed in Jan 2002, legitimate MLM companies can now operate in Singapore subject to very strict guidelines inter alia namely :-

    1) It must not be touted as a get rich quick scheme.

    2) Distributors must not be coerced to be loaded with goods which they cannot move.

    3) Goods must not be too expensive which one would not purchase in any ordinary circumstances.

    4) The companies must be transparent in their payments to distributores and must be able to declare the average income of its distributors.

    5) Consumers are protected with 100% money back guarantee within 60days from purchase.

    Below are my own personal opinion why the goverment suddenly allows MLM for the following reasons :-

    1) There are many self made millionaires through MLM all over the world and this is to give Singaporeans to have more opportunities.

    2) As this is a virtually little initial investment start up, many people can make a living, especially those that are retrenched or are unskilled.

    3) Since many MLM companies are global in nature, eg Nuskin, Pharmanex, Amway...etc, it is now for Singaporeans to gain foreign income by networking overseas too. The fear that with China's entry into the WTO, foreign investments will shift there and many Singaporeans will be unemployed and the economy will suffer. In the past, Singaporeans had suffered huge financial losses in China, now its our turn to bring income back to Singapore by our marketeer entreprenuers to go there and bring money back without having to have huge financial resourses to start off and thus carrying a little risks. Sounds simple? Yes and that is why many people do not believe in the power of network marketing.

    Many people will only make a few hundred dollars or 2-3 thousand per month. However, with a great sensible marketing plan, fortunes too had been made.

    As said earlier, like any businesses, money do not drop down from the sky. Some work and ingenuity is required. However, since it is almost a no risk venture, it is worth considering isn't it?

    Please ask for a business plan and training syllabus before you join any company. Mark my words. Training is crucial to your success as there are many people who made it, just copy what they do through training. What for reinvent the wheel and waste all the resources until you give up? If they only ask you to sell sell sell and recruit recruit recruit, then you can't make much, although you can make some pocket money. How many bottles of products can you sell a day? How many people can you recruit to help you sell? Limited, isn't it? Anyway, Singapore do not allow companies to pay on recruitment as that will be pyramid and pyramid is illegal. In otherwords, goods must move. So those you've recruited must also sell. How many will do that. Most of them just go to sleep, don't they?

    One more important thing I must stress. When someone invites you to join him, ask for his pay cheque to see how much he earns. If he himself made little, how is he gonna teach you how to make more? If you want to join, lookout for a succesful upline, then he can teach you how to be as successful as him and with some ingenuity on your part, you may even make more than him, right?

    You don't see Bill Gates selling computers don't you? He builds business. So to be very successful in any business, building it with a plan is the key. If selling and recruiting being the only activities, then one will only be a salesman and that will not bring you financial wealth. Make sense? DuH
