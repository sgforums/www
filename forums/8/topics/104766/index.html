  • subzero_baby
    29 Nov 04, 19:46
    for my experience is quite painful. Firstly I had to remove all 3 of them as they were so called the "offending tooth". Meaning, they cause pain and swollen gum. Went for a x-ray, then schedule to see the specialist. Doctor then told me that my low wisdom tooth is more dangerous and it's really near to my nerves, and the upper one is just as close to the nerve.

    Operation costed about $3.5k. duration of operation is about 30- 45mins. I was put to sleep and they had this tube stuck thru my nose into the throat. Woke up with a very bad sore throat and sinus, not forgetting the 15 stiches i have in my mouth. Couldn't eat solid/ hot food for about 2 weeks.

    After the operation, which your doctor will also tell you that you might not be able to taste (because the nerve damage) and you will feel numbness in the lower jaw. But all these are on individual problem. some ppl might not have this problem.

    As for the big hole in the mouth, it takes about 3 months to be closed up, depending on the size of it. Mine was quite big, they had to cut up the tooth before they can extract it. But they will nicely label the bottle and put your teeth inside for you to bring home. I still keep mine till now... (just few months old lar)

    my advice is that don't extract all at one time or more than 2 at one time unless it's really a must. For my case is that all 3 of the tooth has to be extracted by operation, so each time to book the operating theater, doctors.. cost alot, at least a few thousand. so i rather do all 3 at one time, but really really very painful and miserable, especially during the recovery time.

    The antheseptic is injected onto the gums. Its the most painful part of the whole operation.And NO, you do not fall asleep. You are awake when the dentist tries to extract the tooth. so you will feel tension and strong force on your gums. You'll hear cracking sounds...similar to that of buildings collapsing.

    But its all over in less than 3 mins.

    It ends with the stitching and you gotta see the doc again after a week to remove the stitches.

    Pain after the extraction varies with individuals....some will feel pain, others won't. I for one felt slight pain during the first 2 days.
