    Just came back from the Standard Chartered Marathon.

    Feeling a bit tired now after opening and erving over hundred bottles of drinks.

    My drink station is at the 26km check point.

    Here are some interesting notes.

    There were 2 barefooted runners. Simply amazing.

    I saw some runners with bleeding nipples. Don't ask me how or why. I didn't ask them how or why.

    Most ladies that participated in the run were quite pretty. Hmmmmmm......Or is it just me?

    When the first horde of people came it, we didn't have enough opened bottles to serve them. I think we underestimated the size of the group. Now we know where we were issued more than 5000 bottles of water, premier distilled drinking water. No Sodium, no calories, and no blah blah blah.

    Most elite runners were foreigners, from Kenya and other places.

    The last runner reached the finishing line with a time of over 7 hours. It may seem long to you, but hey, it's a marathon.

    Interesting, and sleepy, day. I didn't sleep last night.
