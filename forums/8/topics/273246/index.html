    Boy, 16, allegedly rapes girl, 10, then punches her mum
    09 August 2007

    HE lived just 1km away from them.

    But the 16-year-old Kota Bahru, Kelantan, teen was in no way neighbourly.

    The school dropout allegedly raped his 10-year-old neighbour while her mother was not at home, and then assaulted the mother when she arrived home before he could escape.

    The attack happened on Monday, reported China Press.

    The girl was home alone with her 8-year-old sister, while her mother was at her school processing her bursary application.

    The girl was doing the laundry when the suspect barged into the house and locked the door from inside.

    He then reportedly forced the girl into one of the bedrooms where he raped her.

    During that time, the younger sister was left to play in the living room.

    New Straits Times reported that the victim's 46-year-old mother returned home at 1.30pm and heard her daughter's screams.

    She broke down the door to find the girl semi-naked on the floor with the teen.

    The teen then punched the mother on her face several times before running off.

    He was arrested at his home an hour later by the police.

    Over in Jinjang Selatan, Kuala Lumpur, another young girl had also become the victim of a bad neighbour.

    NST reported that the 12-year-old girl ended up being raped by her 68-year-old neighbour last Thursday, after he asked her for help in taking care of his paralysed brother.

    The unsuspecting victim had agreed, thinking the male suspect was a 'nice old man'.

    But after following the man into his house, she was forced to go into a room where she was then allegedly raped.

    After the attack, the man warned the victim to keep silent about the incident.

    The traumatised girl kept mum but confessed to her mother the next day because of the pain in her private parts.

    A police report was made, and the man has since been arrested.

    And in Penang, a 26-year-old woman was raped when she fainted after receiving a tap on the shoulder from a mysterious man earlier.

    China Press reported that the victim had just withdrawn money from the ATM machine on Monday morning and was walking to her car when a man tapped her on the shoulder.

    The next thing she knew, she was in a hotel room with the man, and it was already evening.

    The man who tapped her claimed that he had raped her while she was unconscious, in 'punishment' for her seducing someone else's husband.

    He then drove her to the bus station before escaping.

    The woman made a police report the same night.

    She denied the man's allegations and suspects she's a victim of mistaken identity.
