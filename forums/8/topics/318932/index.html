    My Ambition

     

    Ask me about my ambition and I dare lay a wager that many would dismiss it as one of my wishful thinking. One which many are skeptical that I possess the qualities and prerequisites to carry them out. Do not start batting your eyelids and cast me that doubtful look. But yes, I want to be at the helm of a human rights group. Or maybe charity and welfare organization.

     

    Riffle through the newspapers and be baffled by just the countless number of people who are not provided proper avenues to voice thier displeasure or entitle privileges they should have been given. Very often, we would hear of stories involving foreign immigrant workers having to put up with the sordid conditions at their quarters. Faing the prospect of being repatriated to their hometown if they are too frenetic about their living conditions, all they can do is to keep mum about the torturing conditions they are subjected to. Do they have a channel where they can turn to for assisstance? Even if there is, are they aware of it? This of course, is still an immensely shallow perceptin of the lack of avenues advocating human rights. Human rights group usually have a particular specialization. They can range from ensuring people in the aftermath of a disaster, receive the necessary aid needed and maintaining their quality of lives, to putting the issue of women's rights, etc under the microscope.

     

    The very reason why I yearn to be in this line is that I want every living soul on Earth to know that, be it that they hail from an impoverished or affluent family background, they have with them a precious asset : Human Rights. I want to be able to empathise with others, bring respite to their fears, agony, etc and most importantly let them now that every individual has a say in matters concerning them. They should not be, at any instance, be deprived of the key to the privilege that is human rights.
