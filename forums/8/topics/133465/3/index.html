    Kantian

    This modern theory was derived by Immanuel Kant.

    Utilitarianism
    The rules of right and wrong should above all else acheiev the greatest good for the greatest number of people, even though paticular individuals may be worse off as an effect. Puts whats good before whats right.
    This modern theory was derived by John Stuart Mill.
