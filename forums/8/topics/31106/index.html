    mysingtel ACCESS FEE QUESTIONS

    Find solutions to the most frequently asked questions regarding mysingtel Access fee charges.

    1. Why are you charging for the service now?
    SingTel introduced mysingtel in January 2000 to encourage those who have yet to be acquainted with the Internet and its search tools to discover the benefits of web surfing. A lot more people are now familiar with surfing the Internet, and have moved up the Internet value chain to a dial-up or broadband subscription. In order to sustain the service, there will be an access charge of 2 cents (with 4% GST, 2.08 cents) per minute for mysingtel.

    2. When will charging take effect?
    The charging will take effect from 1 February 2003 (12 midnight).

    3. What do I have to pay for using mysingtel from 1 February 2003?
    With effect from 1 February 2003, users of the mysingtel service will have to pay the current local toll charges AND a per minute access fee of 2 cents (with 4% GST, 2.08 cents).
    Source : http://www.mysingtel.com.sg/help_accessfee.html
