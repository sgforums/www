    casual greetings will be hi, hello etc. we seldom go around hugging one another when greeting. for business will be standard hand shake. we don't speak only english. we speak a mixture of english, chinese, tamil and malay. some say singlish, some say leh, some say lah.

    busniess. usually as welcome for clients, they'll be invited to local restaurants featuring local fare like chinese or peranakan food. casual business talk can be done here. depending on the client, it can also be done over at the pubs, karaoke or golf. serious business discussion will take place in office meeting room.

    singapore is a veryyyyyyy small country, and is a city itself.
