    Things have changed since when I enlisted about 3 years ago...But this was what happened then...if I can still remember..

    1)You take the oath
    2)You take a bloody long bus trip to Keat Hong camp to collect your stuff(boots, uniform,shoes,shirts, shorts,etc) in a very heavy ali-baba bag

    5)Bring you to your bunks
    6)Learn to wear and then wear the uniform and report in
    7)etc.....

    Ahh.....fond memories....(I was posted to bloody Tekong that time, when the policy of all BMT in tekong was not implemented yet...I was fat, that's why. But army made me lose 23 kgs...I like that!)
