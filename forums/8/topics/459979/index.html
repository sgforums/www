    Please bring a very good and durable torch light.

    My suggestion:

    Bring a small bag that you can carry anywhere u go (i.e. when you leave base camp for expeditions). Yes you will be carrying a very big camping bag issued to you but that one usually is to carry GROUP stuff such as food and messtins etc.

    The small bag can put

    -Small face towel (can use for washup and piillow)
    -Torchlight
    -Small water bottle(Emergency although they will let you carry 2kg jerry cans)
    -Sanitary Pads
    -Powder ( Preferably prickle heat )

    Okay the reason for the face towel is stated already but during my OBS, we setup the tents at a very rough tree area. the ground is super uneven and full of rocks and near red ant nests.. So It can act as a head pillow and a blanket in the early hours cause it will be really chilli.. Morning washup can use also =D Multi-functional face towel


    Good torch light (bring a spare one in your personal small bag) is because we had to pitch the tent when it was really pitch dark because we were behind schedule... IT'S REALLY SUPER DARK NO JOKE cause it was in the forest.. I walk out abit lost already LOL Don't drop the whistle they gave you.

    Powder we used it and spam on the entrance of the tent to prevent/reduce red ants from invading us... really painful cause I slept beside the entrance..

     

    Hope this helps! Remember have fun but SAFETY FIRST!
