    A singaporean family out for a walk in a HDB heartland.

    Dad, Mum, pre-teen daughter and son. A typical state designed Singapore family taking an evening walk down the road. 10 meters ahead of them a gay couple is taking a walk too, holding hands, caressing, probing each other, giggling away in careless oblivion. The wholesome family is a bit embarrassed.

    Dad pretends to look at the road. Mum tries to divert her daughter'sattention by pointing out to something. The son stares at the gay couple with curiosity.

    Along with the cigarettes and chewing gum on the shop shelf are adult sex toys, porno figures and 3 D signs. The entire street is alive and buzzing with gambling kiosks, sex tourist guides and gay couples in close embrace and passionate holds.

    The family is now clearly embarassed.

    A few more steps and that brings them to a group of young hookers,clad in tight jeans and short tops. They start to tease the mum and daughter while throwing suggestive sounds at the dad.

    For the first time during the walk he smiles and winks. A gigolo makes a suggestive offer to the mum making her daughter blush.

    The mother cannot take this anymore. " Lets go back" she says in disgust.
    No ! says the father with firm authority. " You all can go back.

    I am going to the casino".

    Me too says the son. So does the daughter. The mum then
    has the last say " lets all go to casino."

    They all recalled the daily mouth watering true stories in the Straits TImes, about how people have made huge piles of money at the casino, driving mercs and BMWs, owning condos and going on hollidays with their families, all thanks to the casino belt. It was mandate from heaven as ST put it.

    Off they go to the casino, happily waving their ATM cards and credit cards.
