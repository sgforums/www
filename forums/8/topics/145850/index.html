    Location: Marina Bay, the park opp the Buffet chains

    How to get there: On Aug 8, IF you get there bef 1830,

    You can board Sv 400 from Anson Rd (Tanjong Pagar MRT)

    Aft 1830,

    You can board from Chinatown MRT (People's Park)

    Otherwise,

    can board 400A from Marina Bay MRT

    Note that 400 will not call Marina Bay MRT

    The bus will alight your @ Marina Blvd, from there you just walk in straight to the end of the road
