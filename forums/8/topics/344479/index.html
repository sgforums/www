  • ditzy
    17 Jan 09, 11:03

    Kovan mrt area where there are currently all the condo developments going on up to simon road, used to be very old shop houses which were gutted by a big fire in the 80s, and never rebuilt.

    The big longkang that snakes along old tampines road and goes underground at simon road used to be open, and a five foot way very lok kok hawker centre use to occupy the area along simon road. There used to be orh lua, wanton mee, and hokkien mee. The hokkien mee has relocated to a corner shop at the old airport road hawker centre.

    The macdonald's branch along hougang st 21 is close to 25 years old, along with the mee siam, yong tau fu, carrot cake, sugarcane, and coffeeshop stalls nearest mac's. Special mention goes to the wanton mee facing the old interchange, they still use ketchup.
