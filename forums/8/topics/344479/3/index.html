  • sLoW MiS
    2 Apr 09, 16:24

    Hi,

    I used to stay at Kang Kar when I was much younger.

    There was a kampung there and yes it used to be a fishery. Fishermen sell their harvest there. Its bustling with activities when it come to the early morning when the fish trading begins.

    There was a bus terminal where the buses like 153, 82, 85 used to be there. 

     

    Have lived there until the government wanted back the plot of land for development in 1985. That was when all the fellow villagers moved to the surrounding HDB like Hougang Ave 7 and Ave 10

     

    The then Kang Kar is now occupied by part of punggol park and Serangoon Secondary school and the long stretch of road down to Seng Kang.
