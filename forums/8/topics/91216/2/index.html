    if you're not going over the peak period, can try priceline... i got a 4-star hotel room at tsim sha tsui east for SGD50 per night (excluding taxes)... my cheapo singaporean strategy is: start ridiculously low, they'll tell you this is too low but hey, it doesn't cost anything to try! and then slowly work your way up till your offer gets accepted... i bought my air ticket there too but forgot the price. think it's ard $200+ or $300+.
