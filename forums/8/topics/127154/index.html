    When my grandmother died, there were the usual funeral rites conducted in the temple, including the chanting of prayers, etc. The whole ceremony lasted for 2-3 hours......and towards the last part, the monk actually drew some numbers and said that my grandmother was helping by giving us those numbers to bet with.

    Yes, and the monk also asked my uncle (grandmother's only son) to shout "huat ah!".

    We felt that it was a bit silly, but what the heck....this was at a funeral, so we just kept quiet and my uncle just tried to comply without looking too stupid.
