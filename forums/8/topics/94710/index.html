    i wanted to go to the one at middle road too, but the nice student counsellor from the japanese embassy recommended this school instead.

    she says she's a language teacher herself, and feels that the JCS methods not very good. i nvr attend b4 lah so i dunno. that time wanna go, miss the enrolment by 1 week n 1 lesson, the puborkias dun want let me cut in. 1 lesson nia ge gao meh nahbey cheebai muchiam i going to miss anything like that.

    she also recommend against going CC to learn. she went to a few once for fun, quality sibuei jialat. stay home learn also better.

    one thing i noticed at both schools. ratio of tapor to chabo = 1 : X where X > 5

    the school is near int plaza, which is near alexandra. (my current office oso near alexandra, a bit more left nia)

    anyway whichever school you go to not very important lah, language this kinda thing must use often then can train one. if not u everyweek speak 3 hours of jap nia, how good can u get?

    b4 i enrolled i had already been teaching myself for about 2 years. just pick up a book to read, can be comic or novel. and get a dictionary. learn alot faster than flipping textbooks. u'll recognize most alphabets within the week, after that it's just a matter of remembering words and understanding sentence structure and tenses.

    must complement together wif either anime or j-drama. so you can recognize how words are pronounced, some words like chinese, got hi/lo tone one.
