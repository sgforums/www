  • jliu

    I have come to the conclusion that our jobs are basically divided into two categories: White Collar and the Blue Collar.

    White Collar: Primary office work, involve the use of computers. Bright office lights, keyboard tapping away, if office is near public assessed areas eg banks, then even more busy. Cons about office work: Too much computer usage and bright office lights can make my eyesight poorer.. PRO: 8 to 5 job, job time is predictable.

    Blue Collar: Factories, machines, dealing with those, mechanics etc, manual labour etc, very dangerous, very noisy. FUMES, EXPLOSIVES etc, PRO: Higher salary CON: RISKY work conditions, shift work.
