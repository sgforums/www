    * The registrants should call in person at:

    Counter 1, IC Section
    Citizen Services Centre
    3rd Floor, ICA Building
    10 Kallang Road
    Singapore 208718

    * The registrant should then obtain Q-number and wait to be called.
    * The original copy of the police report should be produced if available.
    * Alternatively, Statutory Declaration should be produced in lieu of a police report. (You may wish to download the declaration form)
    * Once the replacement registration is completed and payment (S$100 for the first loss and S$300 for the second and subsequent loss) is made and a receipt will be issued.
    * This receipt must be produced when collecting the replacement IC in 1 or 3 months' time.

    (from ICA Website)
