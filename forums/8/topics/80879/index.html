    Below are the rides descriptions i had extracted out from the flyer.

    Rides:

    1.) Ular-Lah
    This water slide extravaganza features the 1st raft slide in South East Asia. Each raft seats 6 ppl & u can expect high -banked coprners & 360' spins as u race to the finish line.

    2.) The Waterworks
    Twist & turn thru the smooth curves of the super flume tube sildes before splashing into the pool. There are 2 superb slides to choose from. Recommended for families who are looking for more than just a little excitement.

    3.) Tsunami
    Tsunami tidal waves are great fun for the whole family. The controlled timings for the tidal waves & the simulated natural waves make this pool a great place for everyone to cool off.

    4.) Jacuzzi
    Enjoy a wonderful water massage in the Wild Wild Wet Jacuzzi; just sit back & relax, let the soothing bibbles rejuvenate your bosy. It's a wonderful way to wind down.

    5.) The SideWinder
    Enjoy the ride of your life as u rush down this slippery wedge at breathtaking speeds on inflatable rafts. And then do it all over again, backwards.

    6.) SkyRider
    If u need to dry off a little bit, hop onto the SkyRider monocycle & enjoy the aerial views of the rides and pools at Wild Wild Wet. It's great fun for eveyone!

    7.) Yippee!
    Wild Wild Wet even caters for the half pints. Yippee! is a safe play area for toddlers that will keep them entertained for hours. Seperated from the Professor's Playground, Yippee! is a shallow wet area loaded with soft play equipment.

    8.) Professor's Playground
    Kids love to soak with mum & dad, and this interactive play structure allows them to do just that. Hidden water cannons, fountains and cascading waters from a GIANT bucket provides hours of fun and frolics for the little ones.

    9) Shiok River
    The perfect ride to help u relax; drift around this tropical canal on a giant inflatable tube and watch the world go by.
