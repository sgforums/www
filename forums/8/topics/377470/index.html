Open Quiz - Interested to prove yourself?

  • A.T
    1 Oct 09, 14:46

    The TATA Group has created an online quiz for all residents of Singapore called TATA Crucible. I have been trying to promote it for the past few years, to use it as a platform to encourage our community to participate in lateral thinking and building a pool of peers who are interested in acquiring more information and knowledge about general stuff, which I think improves the way we streamline our thoughts and thinking process.

     

    Sadly I don't see many locals trying their hands at it. Somehow it makes me feel that if it doesn't involve big $$ and publicity /fame, then locals won't even attempt or consider participating. And even if I do see that few, most get the majority of the answers wrong. It is frightening to see how our society has evolved.

     

    A.T
