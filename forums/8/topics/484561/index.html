    This year's National Day Parade will celebrate the key ingredient in Singapore's foundation - its people and the theme is 'Our People, Our Home'.

    This was unveiled, along with this year's NDP logo, which comprises red speech bubbles, one of which contains the crescent moon and five stars of the flag.

    Said parade executive committee chairman Colonel Wong Yu Han: "Our team wants to create a parade that will deeply engage fellow Singaporeans and inspire the next generation through active participation. We want to celebrate the can-do attitude and caring spirit that hold us together and make Singapore our home."

    This year's celebration will be held at The Float@Marina Bay, and is made of three segments - the pre-parade, parade and ceremony, and show.
