Love Letter for your country

  • Ronald Tan
    19 Jul 09, 01:26

    “The times change quickly. Recession, disease and despair. The doom and gloom can overcome us, while much of what we do is whine and complain that nothing can be done. 

     

    No more. Not for us.

     

    We are people on a mission. We believe that instead of sulking over the state of affairs, our words can be put to better use. We think we can gather a whole bunch of messages from like minded people, filled with positivity, can bring a little light back into our nation. 

     

    In short, we want to pen the biggest love letter to our nation that has ever been seen. 

     

    Word by word, we know it can be done. 

     

    I am sure all of us have written a love letter for your love one before.

    But have you ever written a love letter to your country?

    Join us, won't you?”
