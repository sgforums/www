    synopsis

    NEMO IS BACK!!! He was the little fish that broke box office records! And now you have the chance to see Nemo....live....in a spectacular ice show.... that captures all the adventure....all the courage....and all the spirit of the movie that stole your heart!

    Disney On Ice presents Disney/Pixar's Finding Nemo brings Nemo's underwater world to life in a creative spectacle of color. Audiences will be submerged in a comical adventure with Dory, Marlin and Nemo. This animated world "down under" comes to life in a brilliant rainbow of color, set to an energetic musical score. Gliding smoothly across the ice, skaters costumed in many forms of ocean life perfectly emulate the natural flow of fish through water. A digital projection screen combined with aquatic lighting help create the depth and vastness of the ocean that envelops the audience.
