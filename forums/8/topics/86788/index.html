    question esp for ladies. Guys also can answer, if you have such encounter.

    A lot of beauty salons like to do pushy tactics and insist the customer sign up for 10 sessions of facial treatments. and then push the products to customers. Usually the products not cheap one. For example, Mary Chia's products will definitely cost more than a couple of hundred.

    Other kind of businesses are also very pushy. Wang Yang (the foot reflexology) is one of them. Everytime I go, they want me to sign package. Once I signed package cuz I decided to have the foot massage more often. Each time I went, they would try to sell me their products. Last time was the Royal Jelly that cost more than $100. A few days ago, I went for a back massage cuz I have been having stiff shoulder/back and headache for days. They tried to push me the magnetic belt that cost $218!!!!

    So far, the most pushy beauty salon is Jean Yip. Never go to them for hair treatment. They will tell you one treatment is not enough & to take up 10 sessions. Once a few of them gang up and tried to persuade me. Even told me to use my credit card if I don't have the cash.

    Now my tactics with these sales people are:
    1 ) Tell them that I am using my husband's credit card. If I anyhow spend, I have to answer to my husband.
    2 ) Don't look at them at the face. Just say "Don't need. Don't need. Don't want".

    Any other ways to share?
