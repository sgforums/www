    Since you're a poly grad, when you go to the Big4, you're probably to be one of the lowest lifeforms there, below the fresh grads, and the MBA associates. Not likely to be in any IB deals, but just data-crunching.

    It's much better to work in a small company than in a large set up particularly when you get to learn more things at a more comfortable pace too.
