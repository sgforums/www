End of the road for shopkeepers.

  • caramon
    6 Jan 05, 14:01
    The 65 year old man and his wife ran the hawker stall for 6 years.
    Start work at 5.30 AM and close shop at 10 PM. That is over 17
    hours in a 24 hour day. 7 days a week, keeping the makan shop
    open.

    End of the month they have to borrow money to make ends meet.
    Not much, just 500 dollars or so from friends and relatives. Soon
    enough, they ran out of friends and relatives willing to loan them money.

    Their health was in bad shape. The man suffered from high BP and
    moderate diabetics. The woman took an afternoon nap sitting on
    a stool in front of the stall when business is almost nil.

    All this for what ?
    They were paying 6000 dollars a month as rental for the stall to
    the coffee shop owner. In 3 months arrears, he took action. Asked
    them to sign over their 3 room HDB flat as security or he said he
    will take them to court to get back the 18,000 dollars owed to him.

    Luckily for the couple, one regular customer whom they had approached
    for a loan, decided to take the matter seriously. He made them sit down
    one afternoon along with a couple of his church members. Here is
    what he roughly told the aged couple. He had a calculator in front of him.

    " You have been running this stall for 6 years, every day, every week
    for 16 hours non-stop, working like dogs. You have paid almost half a
    million dollars as rent to the coffee shop owner during these six years of your old age life.

    What you have now is a debt for 18,000 dollars for all this effort.
    Close your shop, stop your dreams and kill your hopes.
    The system here is heavily against you. It will make you work like a dog and suck all your blood till you have nothing left. Just pack your
    things and go. Me and my colleagues here sincerely advice you
    to close shop and stop feeding the landlord.

    This is the advice we have given to over 20 stall holders who could
    not see how the odds are against them due to their ignorance.
    Live your old age with what little you have left."

    Last week the couple tearfully closed their shop and bid goodbye
    to their fellow stall holders. It was not easy, as they looked around
    their familiar surroundings for the last time.
    Yes, everything was over for them. They knew that. Their great
    Singapore dream was miserably over. The end of a long, tiring and utterly hopeless road.

    Somehow, in that agony of theirs, they must have felt a sense of
    ecstacy, as they walked away for the last time from the place.

    Another case:

    The man aged 40 something running a hawker stall selling
    fishhead curry. Slog for over 10 years. Business was good
    then for him and he could pay the rental of 7 K plus.
    Then Maxtor and other MNC plants in the area closed and he lost business income dramatically. That was in 2000.

    He has been running the business since then, hoping for better
    times and paying the rent from his savings. But business has been going bad to worse and now, The landlord is after him as he owes
    over 42K and filed a case against him plus closed down his stall.

    Desperate, he got a contract to supply mid day meal packs
    to some Sembawang Ship yard employees. Profit margin was
    very low. He hired a cheap foreign worker to help him pack
    the meal packets in his flat. But the worker was an illegal.

    He was arrested for hiring an illegal worker and spent
    3 months in jail. He lost all and has a police record now.
    Saw him yesterday with a close cropped head. Only then he
    tells me about his prison stay.

    I cannot under any circumstances say that this man is lazy,
    stupid or lack business sense. He is an extremely hard
    working person, knows his trade and willing to take risks
    even to succeed.

    If he can fail, then it cannot be due to his shortcomings.
    It was because in today's condition it is almost a losing
    game for most businessmen. So imagine the fate of others
    who are less able than him !!!.
