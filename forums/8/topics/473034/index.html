    Hi!

    I am Eng Kiat, graduated this year from Diploma in Green Building and Sustainability from Temasek Polytechnic.

    Director's List awards the top 10% of students based on academic performance within the course cohort. It is awarded annually for each year of study in the polytechnic. So if you do well for all 3 years of study, you can get the award once each year. It is a recognition of your hard work and excellent results. I hope this is informative enough for you. If you need any information feel free to PM me.

    Personally, I have achieved Director's List for all 3 years of my study in Temasek Polytechnic and graduated with Diploma with Merit Award.
