    JAE - Joint Admissions Exercise
    JPAE - Joint Polytechnic Admissions Exercise

    Usually, JAE is for current O Levels students who have just received their results.

    JPAE is usually for those who are considering options (such as the sponsorship by SAF is done using JPAE) or for those who are from ITE/JC.

    JPAE are also for foreigners, but they are usually held on a later date.

    Do not confuse PAE with JPAE, PAE are for students who have done well in their prelims, and not O Levels and can enter the first 3 months for JC. This will be scrapped from 2008 onwards.
