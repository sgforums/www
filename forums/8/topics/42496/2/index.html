    How many things have you done in your life that has particularly left you feeling guilty all these while?
    Up to now, i can still strongly remember 2.
    These 2 incidents happened to me when i was really young, in my primary school days.

    (1) Once, we had a class outing. A picnic. It was a potluck thing and everyone has to bring a food.
    There were lots of fishballs that day, could be becoz it's the easier to prepare.
    I brought fishballs too.

    But, i'm not the one who fried it. My mom did. I can still remember that she woke up at ard 6am in the morning, preparing and frying the fishballs.
    Since there were too many fishballs that day, no one touched mine. I remembered tying them up in a plastic bag and threw it into the rubbish bin without thinking much.
    I only know that my mom will be disappointed to see me bringing back all of them.

    But after that day, i felt sooo guilty. I can feel the pain in my heart as i can still remember my mom standing at the stove, early in the morning......preparing the fishballs for me.

    (2) My mom used to force me to bring water bottle to school. There was once where she filled the bottle with ginseng water instead of plain water. Instead of appreciating the time and effort she spent on cooking and money on it, i got soo angry with her.
    After school before boarding the school bus, i poured the whole bottle of ginseng water into the dustbin. I still answered my mom happily that i finished drinking every bit of it.

    Sigh. Guilty guilty...
