    Just wanna share with you all the experience....

    I was out with my friend for dinner. On the way to meet her, I already felt discomfort in my right tummy. I thought it's my pants or girdle that was giving the problem. I still went ahead and ate with her till late at night.

    The discomfort which has become pain did not go off. I thought, it should go off after a night's sleep. But in the morning, the pain was worsened for a while.

    I went to work as per normal. I had a meeting early in the morning at night o'clock. At the start of the meeting, I had a pain in my right tummy, the same spot that was giving me the discomfort. So painful that I couldn't focus on the meeting for a moment.

    I still stayed in office and did my work as per normal, despite the pain. I thought it could be some muscle ache due to my sleeping posture. I told myself to go see a doc after work. I had initially planned to see my company doc during lunch, but ended up having to attend a lunch meeting. In the evening, I went to a GP near my place, hoping to get some pain killer to ease my muscles. The lady doctor pressed my stomach. There was this spot on the right side that gave a very very sharp pain when she pressed it. She said that it was definitely not muscle cramp or anything. She said that that part is either my right ovary or appendix. But she refered me to KK hospital anyway, cuz she was positively sure it was ovary. I almost cried when I heard that. From a mere muscle ache (which I thought), I ended up having to rush to the hospital.

    Was at KK hospital 24 hr clinic. OMG! Waited for three hours. Couldn't move my right leg anymore, after the GP pressed real hard on my painful part. Finally saw the KK doc who told me that ovary not so high one. Cannot be ovary. Quickly sent me to SGH.

    When I was in SGH, I went for two x-rays, blood test etc. Then I was warded for observation. By the time I lied down on a bed, it was 1 am. I tried to sleep. So tired liao. But every hour, somebody would come in and kah jiao. Either a nurse wanting to take my temperature or a doc want to take my blood. In the end, whole night didn't sleep. Before morning, a doc came in pressed my tummy and decided to operate on me. She said it is 90% appendicitis, even though the x-ray couldn't tell anything. She said that the op will remove the appendix and avoid future problem of appendicitis. Also with the op, they will be able to confirm what the real problem was if it was not appendicitis.

    Just wanna share with you all cuz this problem is so common. Dun be like me. Blur blur, thought it was some muscle problem.
