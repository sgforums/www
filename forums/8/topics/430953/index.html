    Is Singapore boring? CNN list sparks debate

    FAVOURITE pastimes - going to the shopping mall or watching a movie - have been declared among 'the top 10 most boring things to do in Singapore'.

    The list, published last month on website CNNGo, a lifestyle and travel companion to CNN, is compiled by one Ms Elaine Ee, described as a writer and editor of 15 years. Social observers and veterans of the entertainment industry such as nightspot Stereolab owner Michael Lu, say that entertainment options are available but people just have to be open to seeking them out.

    must everything be political?

    anyway, most people say "Singapore is boring" because they are pretty much cynical and jaded...

    they would go to other countries and do whatever they can actually do here.. the difference being they're "on vacation"

    it's a mindset thing..

    i see most of the people in my office go to HK and they don't do anything other than eat and go shopping.. they've not seen The Peak and wandered around the place, they've not been to Ngong Ping and climbed the giant Buddha, they've not done much other than to go to various shopping places and bought a lot of shit...

    and this was "fun and exciting" to them...

    they could have done the same at home...

    the same went for people who travelled to the UK, Bangkok.. wherever.. they just went and did the same things we would be able to do here without taking in the local sights during their travels...

    so... it is really "boring"?

    or are they just tied to a job where they cannot take a week off to explore and find out the really cool places and do really cool things?

    i've not seen enough of Singapore to be bored and i've lived here most of my life!
