    Nikon, Canon, Minolta are all good brands because they are Camera Base Company, but does not means they won't produce lemon. New comer company like Sony, Philips .... They are catching up fast.

    Generally 5MB is what you are looking for. If you plan to carry along with you all the time, a small size one. Consider Battery also. Carry a second battery is what most are looking for, cause these little device eats battery power a LOT.
    Consider a big Memory cards when buying. 512MB & above, cause for digital owner's tends to take a lot more picture (No Cost involve mentaility) & always takes at the highest picture format. No point buying a car & always drive at 3rd gear right??

    Be aware of what type of picture you mostly picture. For Landscape lover's, minimum 28mm (Equivlent in 35mm Film format ok)

    For people lover, 35~200mm are good. 35~100mm will feels a bit fall short.

    For general photograhy, 35~50mm good enough liao. I trust my legs are 1000x better zoom than any zoom lens you can buy. (Always goes closer, it'e much sharper then your lens. )

    I also notice new comer in photography loves huge zooms. Just because shy lar they no confident lor. Then after few years of photo taking, they like huge wide angle & stay at wide angle sometimes. I personally loves 20mm & 24mm in film format. The other range I loves is 50mm F1.4 if you know what I am talking about.
