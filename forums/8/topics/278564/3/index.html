    FYI , Keeping Hermit Crabs as pets is internationally recognized. Even those ang moh also keep . But if you can't keep pets properly , see them wan keep just becuz its cute but don't want give them the additional attention , i suggest you DON'T keep any pets , less you give them insufficient attention. In case you don't know , keeping Hermit Crabs isnt as easy as it may seem.

    A fellow guy said that Hermit Crabs needs dark shelter when it molts , otherwise it will die. In the wild , they moult underground . If your tank is large enough , you can fill your tank with sand deep enough for them to bury themselve and molt. Otherwise its best to give them a dark shelter.
