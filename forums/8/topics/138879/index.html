    The trick to answer surd questions like these is to take out the perfect squares from the surds of big numbers and leave the surds in lowest possible terms.

    e.g. SQRT(50) = SQRT(25*2) = SQRT(25) * SQRT(2) = 5 *SQRT(2)

    Here's the worked solution to your question:

    SQRT(50) + SQRT(2) - 2 * SQRT(18 ) + SQRT(8 ) =
    5* SQRT(2) + SQRT(2) - 2 * 3 * SQRT(2) + 2 *SQRT(2) =
    [5 + 1 - 6 + 2] * SQRT(2) =
    2 * SQRT(2)
