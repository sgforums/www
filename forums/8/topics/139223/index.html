    Suggestion:

    1. Discipline the son with caning everytime he steps out of line. Don't do what other moms do and simply branish the cane. USE IT.

    2. Ban him from going out to Shopping malls or playgrounds until he learns to behave himself. Without an incentive, he's probably not going to bother anyway. If he starts to scream or rebels on this point, apply suggestion 1.

Since his attitude is becoming a problem, this option will give him some responsibility. Again, if he starts to cause trouble, bring out the cane.
