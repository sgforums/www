    ok I'm here to leave my 2 cents worth.
    First of all,I must clarify that I am a Christian, not a staunch Christian, but I am a Christian. And although I'm not from CHC, I have nothing against them. Secondly, I have nothing against pastors, 1 of my best friends married a pastor in US...And like many people, I do not see the big deal about the dress matter...

    I'm an avid music lover, I tune in to almost all channels of radio all day and visit record stores on a daily basis. From a music-listener's point of view, Ho Yeow Sun's albums should not be selling as well as it allegedly does! I seriously cannot remember any 1 of her songs! It's not that I'm bias or what....like say, I don't like 5566 but I know certain songs are sung by them...I may not be a fan of Alan Tam or Gigi Leung, but at least I can name some of his songs....and so on. Of course you may argue that these songs are very much requested on radio or something like that but if HYS cds are selling like hot cakes and she's the best selling artiste in the region, does that not mean her songs are so wonderfully fantastic and that everyone wants to request her songs on the radio?

    Secondly, her voice is really mediocre! She sounds exactly like the girl next to me in church every week-- and I do stand next to a different girl every week! It's ridiculous to indicate that her vocals are so powerful that people will just sit up and pay attention. Everyone knows you have to have a unique voice (esp if You're Singaporean) to make it in the music scene :think: Sun Yanzi, Ah Du...

    Thirdly, I have seen her church members(I may be wrong) in action during the period of the last album. The only reason I noticed was coz' their actions were so weird.
