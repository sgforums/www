    Hi, I'm pretty new here in this forum, but have found most of the discussions pretty interesting and enlightening.

    I think that whatever the breakup strategy, the main thing is to make a clean break. Years ago, when breaking up with my ex, I really wanted us to remain as friends (it wasn't offered as a condolence, I was sincere). But I realised that by wanting to remain as friends, things will become worse as it will only leave the other party with false hope. So to save both parties from any misunderstandings, the best thing is to make a clean break, once and for all.
