    This is Shawn, the official blogger for the Asian Festival of First
    Films; an annual film & documentary festival to celebrate excellence
    for first-time Producers, Directors, Cinematographers, Writers, Actors
    and Documentary makers from the Asian Diaspora. This festival is held
    in Singapore for the 2nd consecutive year and commences on the 29th of
    November and ends on the 6th of December.

    My purpose of contacting you is to request a link to the blog for the
    Asian Festival of First Films on your website. Your aid to help
    promote the AFFF will be beneficial to us as we are trying to gain the
    support and the possible future participation of budding filmakers and
    most importantly, to gain more exposure for asian first films. We are
    building a platform for Asian films to gain international recognition
    through other internationally recognised film Festivals.

    In return, the AFFF blog can in turn promote your blog/website and aid
    in the promotion of the various asian film festivals.

    Main Website: www.asianfirstfilms.com
    Blog: www.afff2006.blogspot.com

    Thanks!
