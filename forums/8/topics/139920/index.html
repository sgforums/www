  • Tuatau
    2 Jul 05, 10:23
    There is such an alert feature within Outlook, but you've got to let the application remain open for it to work.

    1. Open Outlook.
    2. Tools > Options > Preferences > E-mail Options > Advanced E-mail Options
    3. Under "When new items arrive in my Inbox", check the desired alert options.

    Now you could minimise Outlook, let it run in the background while you carry on with your work on other applications.

    HTH.

  • Tuatau
    4 Jul 05, 09:10
    No problem.

    1. Click the "Start" button at your taskbar and open Control Panel.
    2. Open Sounds and Audio Devices.
    3. Under Sound, change sound for "New Mail Notification" under Program Events.
