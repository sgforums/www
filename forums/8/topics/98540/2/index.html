    I spent almost the whole of last year playing golf EVERYDAY in US. Here's my reasons for playing golf.

    1) Addiction of the golf course. Diff golf course offers diff challenges. Seeing greenery and scenic surrounding as compared to concrete jungle is very inspirational. Imagine aiming the balll towards the ocean while having wild deers and ducks watch you drive the ball into the sunset, that feeling is amazing!

    2) Getting a kick out of every hit. If the stroke is correct, you will find that the golf ball just flies beautifully and you get a kick out of it. Achieving that kind of hit is very satisfying every time (kind of like launching a rocket into the sky feeling, if you know what I mean.)

    Playing golf in Asia's unpredictable weather is frustrating. However, when you do play golf in perfect weather (i.e. Sun's shining but not hot with slight chilly wind and low humidity), that feeling is incredible! Also, you can dress up for a golf game and you would feel really good. No perspiration!
