    SINGAPORE : A second policeman was sentenced to 12 months' jail for engaging in oral sex with an underaged girl.

    Last month, a police sergeant was sentenced to two years' jail for the same offence, but his jail term was halved by Chief Justice Yong Pung How.

    Lawyers said this had effectively set a new benchmark for sentences.

    The police corporal cannot be named to protect the identity of his victim, who was 15 years old at the time of the offence.

    The sentence was handed down by District Judge Toh Yung Cheong on Thursday.

    Subhas Anandan, Defence Lawyer, said: "There is a lot of similarities between this case and the one that had appeared in front of the honourable Chief Justice. Both were policemen, the girls were 15 years old, so there were a lot of similarities. So I did not expect anything less or more."

    But it was still a shock for the family of the policeman.

    His mother and pregnant wife, who were in court, both broke down in tears when the sentence was handed down.

    His wife, who is also the sister of the victim, is expected to deliver their first baby at the time of his release. - CNA
