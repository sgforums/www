    To score well on Animal Farm do the following:

    1) You have to know and understand the concepts of 'communism', 'idealism', 'democracy' very well. Must be able to explain them well in your answers.

    2) Draw parallels and differences of actual politicians (past or even present) to the story. Relate their way of governance to how their countries turn out.

    3) Compare how people's lives are affected by the various policies with reference to how the farm animals react to the rules imposed in the farm by the pigs.

    Sure to get A1 if you follow these steps!
