    SVIP & Grassland are using 26-seater coach, which all the tour agencies are using. You can try Transtar First-class coach. It is 16-seater coach, which is sort of first class seat on a aircraft. The price, of course, is also about a first-class seat.

    For hotels in Genting Highlands, it depends on you budget. Genting hotel is most expensive while First World Hotel can be the cheapest. But of course, if room size n service are important to you, try a moderate hotel. The moderate ones are THeme park hotel, highland, etc.

    Try to take Morning coach, then you reach there mid-afternoon. It is wasting half-day but better than taking night coach whereby you reach genting very early morning and if you check-in hotel immediately, you will be charged a day. If not, wait till about 1-2pm then you check-in.
