    Many a Forumite has expressed concern that with the Govt's increasing emphasis on the main languages, the us of hokkien may just disappear altogether. But did you know that the Govt has been actually using hokkien oso, particularly in the Ministry of Environment's hygiene rating of hawker stalls and restaurants?
    What ENV's ratings mean:-

    A = Eh sai
    B= Buay pai
    C= Chin chai
    D= Lao sai (because the 'D' here stands for Diarrhoea)
