    Maybe it's more effective to check with the credit card company, look out for stickers on the restaurant doors and look out for this kind of promo in the credit card newsletters.

    Most of these promotions aren't attractive at all. It's usually like "5-10% discount off the bill. Not valid on promotional items and set meals". You can't save much in this kind of promotions. Besides, you must make an effort to go to a specific restaurant which has such promotion. Not worth the effort.

    However, a few of the credit card offers and benefits can be very attractive. Eg. the UOB Lady's Card entitles the cardholder free use of facilities in California fitness and St Gregory Spa. The membership fee of the credit card is either free or costs less than $100 a year.
