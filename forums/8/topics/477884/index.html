    Alternatively, you may want to change CCA. 

    When I was in secondary school, I know of peers and juniors who change CCA halfway, in sec 2 and sec 3. At sec 4, when our PE HOD let us see our CCA points, it turned out that their CCA points is either the same as mine, or higher.

    Your CCA points will be affected if you do not attend CCA sessions. On a separate note, I know of someone whose CCA points was completely wiped out because he was absent from CCA for many times. 

    You should consult your school's CCA HOD (in most schools, it is the PE HOD) on this matter.
