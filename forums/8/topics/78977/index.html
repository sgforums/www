    When you say you underachieve, that obviously means u know you're capable of much better. Then all the more u should put in more effort, work hard, work smart. When you're passed over for promotions, sit down and honestly examine the reasons why, and not blame others for not giving u chances. Perhaps your colleague is able to maximise efficiency and conribute value-added work. Perhaps you're passed over because u dun display the right employee attitude and hence not performing optimally.

    When u say people dun give u enough recognition, perhaps you're expecting too much reaction from a small effort. Although most of time a person with a good attitude and character who puts in effort usually gets his/her desired partner, but there's is a unnervingly large minority of cases where the results point the other way. But pls do not lose heart. My advice is very cliche, you've probably heard it a thousand times, but still it's the old advice that works: dun give up hope, cuz when u fall down and u dun try anymore the chances of success is definitely ZERO. get up after u fall down, stand and fight again, despite the odds, despite the pain, at least u know there is still a chance to win!

Go borrow more books to read today. Go attend some upgrading courses. Think smart and above others. Give 100% when others are only giving 80%, and then give 120% when the others are giving 100%. Life will be very busy and hard when you're young, but that's the only time u have to push yourself to achieve.
