    Let me state my position first: I am a selfish person.

    This being the case, naturally I would place emphasis on my happiness above other things. After all, its my life.

    But of course, our state of happiness depends not only on ourselves, but also on the surroundings around us. I mean we cannot claim to be truly happy if we are successful and our family and friends are suffering around us. This is even more so if their burden was a result of our actions.

    I guess when I make a decision that involves my own "happiness" as well as that of others, I would have to place myself as the centre of focus and consider the rest as "factors" before reaching my decision.

    This is because when we make such decisions, whatever the outcome is, we have to LIVE with it. Thus, if reasoning and gut feeling tells you that you can, by all means carry on, even if it means hurting others around you.

    Its somthing like whether the ends justify the means. But I think its more of a question of how much of our conscience we are willing to betray for happiness.
