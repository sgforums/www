    hi there, im rather lazy to type down but i just want to share with everyone something...

    As you know, each time you visit the dental, either they get ur tooth clean, or extraction or anything, you would go through the whole process includes Injection~

    I know that some of you are afraid of injections. Like me, im also scare of injection~ They inject to numb your gums so that you wont feel any pain during the whole surgery~

    If anyone is scare of the pain*injection*, you could request for a candy~ literally means , the dental officer will apply a sweet gel around the gums which he /she is gonna inject you~
    **the dental surgeon will put the gel on a small cotton bud and rub it around your gums to numb it~     After applying the gel , you would rest bout 1min then you can actually feel that your gums are numb, then only after that they would jab you to numb your gums longer, You wouldnt feel pain anymore..

    I have visited the dental a couple of times, and have requested for the candy treatment, i think it would be useful to most of you if you are scare of injection.

    P.S
    SO next time , if you visit the dental, you can ask for the candy treatment~ But note: Not all dental clinics have this special treatment~
