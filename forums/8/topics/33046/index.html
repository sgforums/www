    Don't mean to dig out old threads but I managed to catch the show last sunday and like to share some views on the movie because today's chinese paper comment on it again.

    Agreed that the plot was weak, nothing much to talk about. Simply a task to kill the Qin emperor.
    Agreed that some scenes are really funny esp with broken sword (Tony's role) comical expressions.

    What I like is the coulour used.When nameless (Jet's role) tells his side of the story, the characters are in red, when the emperor guess what had happened it's in blue What really happened was in green When back to current time it's all in white.

    It's not like the previous movies where, when there's more than one side of the story is to be told... all will be of same coulour, same scenes just different endings which might confussed the audiences.

    Something drawn from the movie, the person who knows you best might not be your best friend but instead he/she can be your worse enemy. Someone who appears to be a tyrant might not always have an evil intention.
