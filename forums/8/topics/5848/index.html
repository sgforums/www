  • badboy
    31 Jan 00, 18:14
    Normally I'd take Vodka Sprite, or Gin Coke, both are quite nice and relatively weak.
    As for SOS, I've not been there. But if you want a few recommendations of cool places, well, here goes:

    1.) Club Samsara. Along Mohammed Sultan, 1 of the few there with age limit at 18. The crowd is young, and mainly retro is played. It's rather happening, though it's kinda small, coz the place is long and narrow.

    2.) Zouk. Well, need I say more?

    3.) Lan Kwai Fong. It's a really authentic place if you just wanna find a place to chill out without so much dancing. There's even food there.

    4.) Venom. At Pacific Plaza. It's a rather happening place too, but well, unless you're the alternative type, don't go on Sundays. ( designated gays' night)

    5.) Sunset Bay/ Pink Flamingo. Sentosa, Siloso Beach. This is what I find very refreshing for a change. The crowd there is very fun-loving, and the stuff are very friendly too. After partying, you can still spend the night at the beach. Really wonderful.

    If you're looking for more places, try searching at www.catcha.com.sg . Search under Entertainment, and click on nightspots.
    That should give you an almost exhaustive list of hangout places in Singapore. Cheers to partying!
