    Cathay is better for the service and aircraft type.

    For Cathay : You be flying most probably a Boeing 777 to Hong Kong and either A330/B777 to Seoul.

    All of CX's aircrafts has Personal TV in every seat, you won't get bored. Service is not bad.

    For Thai : Prepared to fly their sucky Airbus A300 to Bangkok and change for either A330 or B777 to Seoul.

    There are no Personal TV on board , if you choose Thai, bring some sort of entertainment along. Service is better then CX if you ask me.

    If you are on budget , fly Thai.

    Actually another way of getting there , direct and price is reasonable is Korean Air . You will fly the Boeing 777-300 with PTVs. Service is good. Hope this helps.
