    In such case, you can contact the bank asap to tell them about this error. They will check with the other party (the wrong account holder). If the wrong account holder say that the money is unknown to him/her, the bank will credit back into yours.

    However, if the wrong account holder insist that the money is yours, you can make a report to the police and/or bank. In such case, the police/bank will investigate things like, is there any contract, etc that you have to transfer a money to him or not? if not, then he should not accept such amount. Then the money will be transferred back to u.
