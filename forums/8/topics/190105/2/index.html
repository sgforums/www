    I'm assuming your a freshie.

    Yes the 1st few weeks of poly life is incredibly fun and slack and what-nots. But remember the lectures and tutorials and labs are there for a reason and if you're smart enough you can get your minimum of 75% or 80%(usually business schools) attendance.
    The 1st year of poly is the best time to pull your GPA up above 3.5. Once you get into year 2 and 3 subsequently the modules are gonna get harder and if you're an average person like me(heeh) the A's are going to get hard to attain.

    So my advice to you would be to enjoy and study smart at the same time. Those 3 years will go by in a flash. Trust me, i still couldn't believe i'm having my graduating ceremony next month.

    you'll have lots of fun during the 1st few months. the whole class will eat together, pon lessons today, play together, organise chalets & bbqs together and chiong together.

    after a few months, the class will be divided into different cliques. after 1 or 2 semesters when the projects become stressful, the blame game will begin and politics will start and ppl will start to take sides. by the end of your poly life, you'll only be hanging out in your small clique

    work hard during your 1st year and try to keep your gpa high, above 3.5 if possible. its gonna be tough from the 2nd year onwards.
