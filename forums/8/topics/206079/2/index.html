    Originally posted by aRcTiC_sTaR:
    Personal bar as a hobby.

    Has anyone tried Tanqueray gin? My friend recommended Tanqueray over Gordon's, citing that it tastes better. Seems that Gordon is more popular because it's cheaper.
    Tanqueray is considered a premiere brand. The next premium is Bombay Sapphire.

    It is good to Start with Gordon's or Beefeater and work from there.
    I personally prefer Gordon's for the hint of sweetness and blackthorn fruit.
    The reason why you should start with Gordon and Beefeater Gin is because :

    Cheaper
    Characteristics and taste is milder and more generally accepted
    Good for cocktail mixes.

    Premium Gin are good for :
    Premium Martinis/cocktails
    Drinking Neat
    Drinking On The Rocks

    If you like Gin, you may then try to get the premiums. I find Bombay Sapphire delightful. But Gordon's will have the 'punch or kick' of Gin.

    Cheers
