Are you scared of dogs?

  • tranquilice
    21 Feb 04, 13:30
    She was attacked by 20 dogs

    An 8-year-old girl's horrific experience

    ALL this Taiwanese little girl wanted was to have some fun with her classmates, but she ended up being attacked by stray dogs.

    The 8-year-old's ordeal ended only when a granny came to her rescue and chased the dogs away.

    By then, the girl had already sustained multiple wounds.

    Though minor, they required 200 stitches at a local hospital, reported Taiwan's Apple Daily newspaper.

    The incident took place at an empty field in Tainan at around 5pm last Thursday.

    The little girl had cycled to the field to play with two classmates.

    Before long, they found themselves surrounded by more than a pack of 20 dogs.

    Terrified, her two classmates quickly ran for their lives.

    But the girl, whose surname was given as Zhang, was too frightened to move.

    As the dogs moved in on her, the girl crouched to the ground and started to cry.

    Her screams of help failed to frighten the strays, and they began to gnaw at their 'prey'.

    Meanwhile, her classmates yelled to a 75-year-old woman they passed in their flight to safey: 'Granny, granny, there's still someone behind!'

    The sounds of the dogs howling and barking could be heard from a distance.

    SHOCKING SIGHT

    Madam Wu Zhou Mei Zhen, who was had been attacked by dogs before, knew something was dreadfully wrong.

    She picked up her pace and went towards the noise.

    A shocking sight greeted her.

    Several dogs were bent over, biting a girl.

    She picked up some soil and stones, and threw them at the dogs.

    Recalled Madam Wu: 'It was so scary. The dogs were not afraid of me. I was frightened to death but the girl's cries gave me courage.'

    The feisty old woman continued to advance, and shouted at the dogs.

    The dogs bared their teeth at her menacingly, but she didn't let up and continued to throw stones at them.

    Eventually, they ran away.

    By then, the little girl was already badly hurt.

    Her clothes were ripped off, save for her underwear, and her body was covered with blood from numerous wounds.

    Madam Wu quickly signalled to a passerby to call for help and the girl's parents.

    It was only later that she discovered that she had dislocated her right shoulder from the exertion of throwing stones at the dogs.

    At the hospital, the girl had to be treated for four hours, and warded six days for observation.

    She could only say 'I'm very afraid of dogs' when asked about the incident.

    Her worried father, Mr Zhang Xiao An, said: 'Please, don't let such an incident happen again!'
