    This games consist of 5 teams and a facilitator. The aim of the game is to earn as much money as possible.

    The teams are divided with each team having an equal number of participants.

    Each team will decide which department they would like to be in, namely:

    Finance
    Human Resource
    Research And Development
    Marketing
    Customer Service

    After the teams have been decided and agreed on, each team moves to 5 different areas. Each team will not know what the other teams are discussing about.

    The facilitator will now start the game by walking around and asking each team to select either an "X" or "Y". The facilitator will do this 12 times.

    Depending on the choice each team make, the following results will occur:

    1 selects "X" 4 selects "Y"= All who select X earns $40. Y loses $10

    2 selects "X" 3 selects "Y"= All who select X earns $30. Y loses $20

    3 selects "X" 2 selects "Y"= All who select X earns $20. Y loses $30

    4 selects "X" 1 selects "Y"= All who select X earns $10. Y loses $40

    5 selects "X"= All teams Loses $10

    5 selects "Y"= All teams earn $10

    After every 3 rounds, the facilitator will call for a department meeting. Each team will appoint a member to represent them in the meeting. During the meeting, department heads are allowed to discuss their opinions and ideas and, if possible, work out a solution.

    The maximum amount the company can earn is $600. 5 teams X 12 =$600

    To those who played it before, how did you feel after the game? angry? Sad?
