    Vampires are known as the Kindred. Story goes around (i'm not sure whether real or not, qin doe doe yan liang) that when Cain killed Abel, God curses Cain that no food grownth on earth would sustain him. Only blood that floweth from his brother's body would sustain him. God than set a mark on him, stating that no one would be able to hurt him, thus rendering him immortal...for not even death can touch him.
    From then on, he preyed on the descendents of Seth, the 3rd son of adam and eve.
    Those 'embraced' into darkness are known as Cainates and possess a thirst for blood.
    To note there are 13 different vampiric clans around the world, each clan masquerading as humans.
