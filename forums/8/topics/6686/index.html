    Loneliness comes to me,
    when reminiscence and nostalgia struck me,
    when love lost can never be regained,
    when pain and sorrow dominates,
    when I started to realize that I am going nowhere,
    when I realized that I have to walk my own destiny, and the road was meant to walk alone,
    when I received news that my friends are getting hitched and married, and I am still single and available,
    when I could not find answers to abstract questions,
    when I wished that I am feeling in love again but knowing that someone would not share the same feeling,
    when I feel the envy of the lives of others,
    and finally, when I feel like giving up...

    Yet, for 25 and 5 months years of my life, I spent 23 years of loneliness, and that 2 years of being with someone still remained a deep and unforgettable part of me, and when I think of the happy times, I could still thank her (or both of them) for making them beautiful and sweet regardless of the pain both of them left for me. I could only find consolation in the following words,

    Yin1 wei4 you3 qing2 (because there is love), I still believe and wished that there will come a day where I could be happy once again....
