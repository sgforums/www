    I am using SingNet BroadBand Unlimited 512k. The modem is Aztech DSL100U. I got it free from SingNet.

    Sometimes it will disconnected only when you are not uploading or downloading anything (surfing websites, etc). I called Singnet with regards to this problem.

    They said that to provide better service to all subscribers, they will automatically disconnect the service if they do not detect any uploading or downloading. All you have to do is just to reconnect back. But don't worry, if you are using the service for uploading and downloading, they will not disconnect it for you. The period allowed before disconnected may varies with the number of people using at that moment, etc.
