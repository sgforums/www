    $2 - entrance fee which you buy when you alight inside sentosa.
    $1 - for shuttle bus service
    $2 - for each private vehicle driving in.
    $15 - apply for islander card, unlimited entries for a year.

    so if you take bus from tiong bahru or world trade bus interchange, that is $3.
    if you drive in, that is $4.
    if you wanna walk in also can, that is $2.

    As far as I know,

    When you board the shuttle bus, you pay $1 onboard, when you reach Sentosa, you have to get down the bus and proceed to the ticket counter to buy the admission ticket at $2.

    The Sentosa Splendour and Sentosa Classic Plus costs $29.90 and $19.90 for adults respectively. The Funseekers card (season pass) costs $32 for period of 1 year entry.

    The charge for glass cabin cable car is more expensive. A ride for normal cable car into Sentosa costs $14.50 for adult (inclusive of admission fee)
