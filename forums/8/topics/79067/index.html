    no.. why would job agency charge the applicant?

    usually it is the job agency will match the job for you. Then they will send you for interview. if all goes well.. and you have gotten the job. then once you start work, the job agency will charge the commission to the company who is employing you. You don't have to pay a single cent.

    The company pays the commission to the job agency because they engaged the service of the agency and the agency had helped them to find a suitable candidate for the job. So the company don't have to advertise in the papers etc.
