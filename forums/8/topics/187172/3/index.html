    actually, tat's the thing about credit and debit cards. it's the balance between the power to spend and exposing ur credit card details to fradulent users. in singapore, credit and debit cards have the same coverage and usage, the only difference is the strict credit limit imposed on debit cards.

    overseas, debit cards function quite differently from credit cards, and have even tighter restrictions altogether. my line, we deal with uk credit card transactions and online payment solutions, it's a daily thing to look for fraud, and to verify if a particular card is flag as stolen before allowing money to leave our side. just a few days, there is a big case of someone in sweden using a stolen credit card (in fact he used 8 cards then finally 1 got through), try to use for online transaction with multiple accounts from the same IP (stupid!), but before he could request for bank wire to get hard cash, it's flagged and now is a police case liao.

    tat's why, if ur card is spolit, call the hotline, disable immediately, they will send u a new card FOC. no questions asked. if u ever found the card, snip it into two. settle.
