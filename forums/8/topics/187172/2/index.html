    Some Info:

    Scenario 1

    When applying for a debit card and you don't have an existing account with the bank, you will be asked to open 2 accounts:

    1) Current Account - This is where your money is deposited, is a statement account and ATM card can also be requested.

    2) Debit Card Account - Linked to your Current Account

    When you swipe your card, draw cash, purchase things online etc, the amount will be deducted from your Current Account immediately, just like NETs.

    Scenario 2

    When applying for a debit card and you have a existing with the bank, you may link the Debit Card to your existing account.
