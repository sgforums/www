  • jeffning
    22 Apr 06, 23:20
    Originally posted by askxyz:
    What are the differences b/w a debit card and a credit card?
    Debit Card
    No minimum income required. If have, min SGD18,000.00 p.a.
    Use what you have in your savings or current accounts.
    No tendency to overspend due to limitation of your account balance.
    Can used for online and/or offline payment.
    Lots of perks and benefits/rewards.
    Spend now and pay now.

    Credit Card
    Subject to minimum income of SGD30,000.00 p.a for a generic card.
    Have finance charge of late payment and interest charge.
    Uncontrolled spending could lead to bankruptcy.
    Lots of perks and benefits/rewards.
    Widely accepted as online payment.
    Spend now but pay later.
    Pay minimum amount on the statement.

    You can refer to credit and charge card regulation for details.
