    Basically, U can request for HFC (Hold For Collection).
    Call up the Bank's Hotline and check if ur card is already embossed / ready. Let them know u wish to do self-collection. U may then have to go down to the Card Centre to collect, along with ur NRIC as proof of ID.
    I would not advise collecting the card from the branch as there's a possibility of delay n loss of card when the card is being transferred internally from Card Centre to the Branch.
    Call the 24 hotline friendly line of the Bank. They will be able to provide the necessary assistance.
