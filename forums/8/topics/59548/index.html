    Cost cutting is not the way out. One has to reduce expenditure and cut cost, then can really save:

    Here's a few drastic cost cutting measure:

    1) Use one bowl, i cup & 1 set of utensils for all your 3 meals in a day so that there isn't much to dishes to wash. Just rinse after each meal and you can reuse. Save money, time and dish washing liquid

    2) When go shopping and something catches your fancy, remember to ask yourself if you truly need that item and how many time you would use it. then ask yourself, what you think the cost price is and compare with the selling price. Are you willing to let the store earn that kinda money from you??

    3) Don't donate your change on Saturdays for flag sellers, donate to your own piggy bank. You'll save a lot more.

    4) When printing reports for self-reading from the web or whereever, remove margins and reduce fonts so that you can squeeze as many words on one paper and save paper.

    5) Use both sides of paper when printing.
