    Beware of Ransomware

    What is ransomware?

    Ransomware is a type of malware that prevents users from gaining access to their computers until a ransom has been paid.

    How to protect yourself from ransomware?

    1. back up your files.

    2. use anti-virus.

    3. verify email sources.

    4. patch your system.

    What to do if you are a victim of ransomware?

    1. disconnect computer from network.

    2. never pay the ransom.

    3. seek help.

     

    stay vigilant.
