    The most important lesson to be learned is to take care of ourselves when we are well, and treasure your friends when they are still around.

    One example:

    I had a good friend in sec 4. His name is Yanxian. He had missed 2 yrs of school because of blood cancer. However, he recovered well enough to do the sec 4 curriculum.
    Near to the O levels, however, his illness took a nosedive. He had to be hospitalised, and undergo chemotherapy. He recovered well enough to take the O levels, although he cannot sit in the same hall as us because his immune system was too weak.

    I seriously thought he has recovered, and will be on the way to recovery. In my first year of JC, I had called him a few times to catch up. He sounded very well. After that, because I was really very busy with work, I did not contact him for a very long time.

    It was after my A levels before I called him again. This time, I was told by his sister that he had passed away. His family knew I was his good friend, but they did not contact me because they do not have my contact details. I was really shocked and sad that I have lost a good friend.

    Although I have not visited his ashes, I still miss him. I wished I had kept more in touch with him...
