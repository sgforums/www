    Just finished reading this really mind blowing book....

    called "Shantaram" by Gregory David Roberts

    This is what it says st the back of the book...

    In 1978 Gregory David Roberts - a young writer and activist - committed a series of robberies while addicted to heroin, and was sentenced to nineteen years in jail. After two years, he escaped from a maximum - security prison in broad daylight and became Australia's most wanted man.

    Hiding in Bombay, he established a medical clinic for slum-dwellers,and worked as a countefeiter,smuggler and gunrunner for one of the most charismatic branches of the Bombay Mafia. Shantaram is based on this 10 year period of his life.

    The result is an epic tale of slums and mansions,romantic love and prison torture,philosophers and assasins,mafia gang wars and Bollywood films A gripping adventure story,Shantaram is also a superbly written mediattion on good and evil and an authentic evocation of Indian Life.

    Its is an amazing story of an amazing life...I am eagerly waiting the release of his 2nd book which hes writing now...that will go into the next 10 years where he escapes capture twice, is finally caught in Germany and finally extradited to Autralia to serve out his remaining years of sentence.
    Hollywood - Johnny Depp has recently optioned the book and the author is also now working on the screenplay to..."Shantaram"
