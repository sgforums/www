    Hi...

    Just now I was dreaming. I was in living a place where nearby there was a development for a resort of some kind.

    It was quite annoying, there was construction noise. After a while, the construction was completed. Then I remember me and my family went there and check out the place. Quite nice.

    The resort was unique and neat, the interior design was good and modern, and it was spacious. There were many rooms, nice gardens, it was beautiful. Guests could stay there for 3 days at a time. We explored the interconnected rooms and sections.

    Me and my family, together with the family dog, was exploring the newly completed place.

    Some people were there also, I assumed they were doing just what we were doing, looking around, people who were residents nearby the resort development.

    This was a dream, I woke up a few times in the middle of this dream.

    I remember thinking to myself, oh ok so this is a dream. I had full awareness of this fact.

    In between waking up and continuing the dream, it became a little weird, I was almost like watching a movie. I watched myself and all the people and all the surroundings, without being able to do anything. I remember I was thinking to myself, hmmm this is a dream, I can do anything I want, let's do something wild and crazy, Im a king here. But no, I couldnt do anything, no control. I was unable to break away from the script, whatever the script was.

    Meat Pao.
