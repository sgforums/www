    Mahathir hospitalised after heart attack

    Posted: 09 November 2006 1208 hrs

    KUALA LUMPUR: Former Malaysian prime minister Mahathir Mohamad was hospitalised after suffering a heart attack on Thursday.

    His son, Mokhzani, said that Dr Mahathir suffered a mild heart attack and that his condition was stable.

    Dr Mahathir, 81, was admitted to the government's National Heart Institute, a premier heart hospital, "in the early hours of today," said hospital spokeswoman Alawiyah Yussof.

    "He suffered a mild myocardial infraction," she said, a reference to a heart attack. Other details will be released shortly, she said. - CNA/ir
