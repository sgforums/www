Poem about life in the beginning

  • jliu
    8 May 01, 21:10
    in the past, many years ago, people left an advice before there was pollution, before there was cities and before there was such thing as war..there was never any history to study and people followed the same advice:

    life is about giving
    water to a tree
    , attending to every laid out thing
    and earning a fair share
    of money

    be kind and hope the trees
    will bless your temper
    the same for the hills and rivers

    give praise and ask someone
    to watch for you
    because you are incomplete
    .
    .
    Brace up your character
    with a desolate moist lotus

    be sharp and wait for spring and
    hope that there will be fabulous
    rain

    Clouds are between the sun and
    the moon
    and blue-green clouds are the
    most beautiful clouds..why?
    because they are storm clouds
