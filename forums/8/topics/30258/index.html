    Hues of gray surround - different shades of gray
    as the monotany - uniform, lack of variety
    of it all envelopes like a vortex devoid of colour. - the above two things wrap like a sucking whirlwind that is in black and white, no colour.
    Between stops of seclusion and solitude - in between these two "things" seclusion (to set apart) and solitude (being alone)
    lies a fragile soul looking for direction. - self-explanatory

    Ever seen a rainbow in monotone? - monotone, having only different shades of one colour in this case. it can also refer to sounds
    Surfing toward the horizon constantly looking - self-explanatory
    abreast, ahead but never behind, - left right front but not back
    escaping the vortex of bleakness it flees. - running away from the sucking thing described in paragraph 1. bleak = gloomy

    Spectrum lacking light - A light that doesn't have a spectrum. Hence, without colour.
    pricks gently yet disturbingly. - describing how the light affects the subject.
    Translucent shapes amass - transluscent, not clear, semi-transparent; amass = gather
    before the direction seeker as a tease, - the shapes seem to tease the subject, just playing with it.
    doing not much more than to dim any consciousness available. - self explanatory?

    As it plods, it seeks.
    It seeks [b]Col    our, Depth and Substance.[/b] - plod, to move ahead with a heavy foot, labouriously, with difficulty.
