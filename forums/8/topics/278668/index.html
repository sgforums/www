    A shoes manufacturing boss ponder whether he should enter African market. Hence he send one of his marketing excective A to Africa to do research.

    A went and came back to report to the boss. "Boss ah, I dun think there is any market value in Africa. cos nobody wear shoes anyway. Most dun wear shoes at all!"

    The boss remained unconvinced and decided to send excective B to go to Africa for a second round of research.

    When B returned, he reported to the boss. "Boss, I believe there is Huge market value in Africa." Susprised, the boss asked why he think so. "Because since most people in Africa dun possess any shoes, if we market our shoes there and set a culture of wearing shoes, there will be a huge demand of shoes."
