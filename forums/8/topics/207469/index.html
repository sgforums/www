    3 months ago, 14 May 2006, i accepted a night shift job from a job agency in international plaza. The job agent is quite arrogant, he just implying things like i owe him a living. Anyway beggar cannot be chosers, i been jobless for close to 4 months before that.

    Before i sign the contract, the job agent told me it was a 2 months temp to perm night shift job. Then i look at the job it was valid for 6 months instead of 2 months. I was surprised and ask him why is the contract valid for 6 months, the job agent behaved impatiently and says their is a reputable job agencies, they say after 2 months if my enginner if satisfied the company will sign me up. I admit i was so navie and damn stupid, i sign the contract.

    2 months later at 16 July, i ask the HR department why i am still not converted to perm staff. They says my contract is 6 months, i ask my direct engineer and he was surprised. He says you didn't know your contract was 6 months.

    I was shocked and furious, i confront that agent and he just says" if you think it is too long, give us 1 month notice and quit." This is my encounter with a job agency and it is really a very big agency. I just want to share my experience, i admit i was downright naive and stupid and i urge you people out there to try to find a job by yourself first.
