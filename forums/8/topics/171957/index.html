10 new compressed natural gas (CNG) buses for SBS Transit

  • ^tamago^
    1 Jan 06, 09:29

    SBS2995S


    SBS2996P

    10 more Volvo B10BLE-CNG (Volgren Cr222L bodywork) buses have been commissioned by SBS Transit. These buses are parked in Soon Lee Bus Park, and will be under their charge.

    These buses have been acquired by SBS Transit in 2003 but have since been laid up in the depots while fuel consumption and feasibility studies were being done on the 2 identical buses, SBS2988M and SBS2989K, since 2002. They were initially deployed on Jurong Island Services 711, 712 and 713, before being put on Service 105 after SBST decided not to continue the JIS service.

    These compressed natural gas (CNG) buses are expected to be deployed on Services 66, 105, 143, 174 and 243.

    The registrations of the 10 buses are:
    SBS2990E
    SBS2991C
    SBS2992A
    SBS2993Y
    SBS2994U
    SBS2995S
    SBS2996P
    SBS2997L
    SBS2998J
    SBS2999G

    This post is for general information purposes.

    Look out for 150 new Volvo B9TL double-deckers coming our way sometime near Chinese New Year.
