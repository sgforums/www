    The lack of privacy that comes along with having a social networking website account has finally hit me.

    My mum's ex-colleague's (also my mum's good friend) daughter, who is my friend on facebook, saw my ROM Solemnization pictures as well as the wedding photos on facebook.

    Nothing wrong with that right?

    Of course nothing is wrong, unless if the people decide to spread the word and it got back to me. What do i mean by that?

    Above mentioned friend, saw the pictures and alerted her mum, who IMMEDIATELY (seriously, no joke, it was IMMEDIATE.) picked up the phone and called my mum to ask her about it.

    auntie: "eh, ur son got married already? how come never invite us? is he going to invite us for the wedding?"

    mum: "orh no la, he registered his marriage with his girlfriend so they can apply for a new flat.. as for the wedding photos, they're just models only..."

    see? with facebook (or the internet, for that matter), you'll have no privacy. Anything you put online, people will just SPREAD THE WORD.

    Don't get me wrong, I'm not having a problem with people talking about things that I post online. I put these stuff like my wedding pictures on facebook and on my blog, so as to share my happiness and my joy with my friends. It just gets irritating when people get so EXCITED and keep talking about it, until it gets around to me knowing it.

    If you see something I post, and you find it interesting or JUICY (you know what I mean..), I would really appreciate it if you just kept it to yourself or at the very least, don't go around the world screaming about it and making a big issue out of nothing at all.
