    Hi there,

    I am currently living in Kovan region and have just purchased the Draco STB for my living room.

    I do not have a cable point, and thus I have to use my rooftop antenna.

    HDTV, Digital Signals will be able to be received as long as there is a signal VS analog whereby a clear image will only be present if there is a strong signal.

    With regards to your area, the broadcast station is at bukit batok so there shouldnt be a problem in receiving programmes.

    I live quite a distance from Bukit Batok and I am already receiving a 95% signal most of the time.

    Also note that you will not be able to use the coaxial cable which is connected to your cable point (if u are living in a HDB). You have to either connect the STB to your rooftop antenna or the digital indoor antenna provided.

    Hope this resolves your question!

    Hi guys and jeez.Just bought my Draco STB + ANT from someone selling his brand new unopened set.

    Here's a mini review by me.

    ANT 100 satellite comes with it.A small photoframe like object,also around 4X6 or so.
    HD5 is clear and uncluttering with good placement of the ANT,u need to place the ant near windows.However to view the other digital channel ie Digital 8,u need to have really good placement of the ANT.I found that when i place the ant somewhere near the window HD5 was ok and uncluttered.However other channels will start to lag.I ended up putting the ANT at the window,propped by the grill.

    I agree on the agreement on HD5 being better than 5.However Digital 8 and 5 and CNA was noticability of poorer quality than my coaxial analogue.

    Also,a little audio glitch.When u connect the optical cable to the STB for surround sound,it will work but the regular audio transmitted by the HDMI cable was not unmuted.I had to put the volume of the STB to 0%

    Also,another glitch is that even though the STB is on standby,the audio signal in the connected optical cable remains and my subwoofer did not auto standby and remained on instead.I had to manually power off the sub
