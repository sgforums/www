    I understand how you feel.

    It's a feeling of having experienced major failure. It makes you have doubts about urself. It makes u think that you, could in fact be, a failure. As a result you feel depressed and u feel low.

    Following this, a challenge faces you. Because of that past experience, you face this challenge with doubts, with much non-confidence, with fear.

    I have to remind you, that whatever happens in life it's how you see your failures, and how you get up back again from them, which will shape ur future destiny.

    When people are succesful and everything goes smoothly, people party. When a failure strikes, people are taken aback. They think, they ponder. They spend time thinking on the whys and hows, and try to avoid failure again in the future. They learn. They dont do the same analysis or learning when they are succesful.

    People remember pain more than pleasure. People want to avoid pain more than wanting to gain pleasure.

    When you are faced with a failure, how u think and how you learn from it, will determine what kind of person you are. In this life.

    It will determine how you face the next challenges. Will you be full of doubts and fear? Or full of confidence, determination, belief, and action?

    Learn from it. Use it as your advantage. It can really be your gift.
