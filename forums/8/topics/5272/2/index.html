    bochup.... it seems rinoa said she is hibernating... but i can give you an estimate cost for University studies in Western Canada. THings are more expensive in the east.

    basically... you are looking at about CAD $1800-3000 per month, everything included.
    This $1200 range depends on your lifestyle and uni you are going to.

    It's whether you want to live alone (more expensive), or sharing with somebody. Type of food you eat, whether you want to cook for yourself or "dabao" everyday.

    The biggest portion of the cost is usually the rent and Tuition fees. Rent is anywhere from $250 to $850. Cheap means old house, less facilities, pay your own utilities. Expensive means the opposite, but mostly you still have to pay you own utilities.

    Tuition fees depends on the Uni. Lets focus on the 2 unis in Vancouver. One charges around $250 per credit hour (SFU). The other charges around $410 (UBC). This is international student tuition (locals pay around $75). A student is considered full time upon taking 12-15 credit hours per semester.

    Movies $5-10 (depending on theatre and day)
    Bus $75-150 per month (unlimited)
    Groceries at least $100/mth
    Petrol $0.60/ltr (average but rising fast)
    Telephone $25/mth
    Cable $35
    Insurance $36
    Internet $10-50
    Electricity $40 (year-round average expect up to $100 during winter)
