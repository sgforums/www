    Halo Everyone!!

    I just returned from my 11 day trip to the south island yesterday night.

    Whoah!! Now I know what people mean when they say something took their breaths away. The experience was simply breathtaking!

    We rented a car and drove from places to places, staying in different motels/hotels nearly every night. We spent half our time driving from point A to point B, taking the car to over 2000 km through our stay there. Though, the time and distance was all well worth it. We get majestic mountains, beautiful lakes and lotsa sheep to greet us around every bend!!

    New Zealand - definitely a place to see and fall in love all over again in.
