    it's email now between friends who are overseas as it's faster coz that cuts down on the waiting period and the constant checking of the snail mail mailbox but snail mail has its advantages too. you get to hold the letter, you can re-read what's written over and over again, it's portable and if you're lucky to have someone who's a little creative, there'll be doddles all over, it has a different feel to it compared to email. the choice of paper, its print, texture, colour all convey a certain mood or affection and to see the handwriting, well, that should put a smile on my face and most important of all, it's a precious record of what has transpired between us, its physical existence assures me that once, i shared something special with someone. with email, it's special too even though there's no record and the daily exchanges of correspondence sure beats the long wait between people who are not in the same country, it helps greatly in maintaining a friendship or relationship. i never thought that i would ever say this, considering that my longest crushee has urged me to get Internet services for 3 to 4 years before i eventually did so last year [no wonder i never made any headway with him ], the Internet is a great tool and i'm thankful for it. oh, ICQ is most wonderful too.
