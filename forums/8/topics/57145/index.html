World Animal Day 2003

  • FireIce

    Get ready for WORLD ANIMAL DAY!

    It's soon going to be that time of year again where we celebrate a very important day - World Animal Day!
    This year SPCA will be celebrating World Animal Day at the very popular shopping centre, Parco Bugis Junction, (the stretch along Coffee Bean and Tea Leaf) from the 3rd -5th October (Fri-Sun) at 11am - 9.30pm.

    # There will be an extensive exhibition featuring:
    1) How to take care of cats and dogs.
    2) Benefits of Sterilisation.
    3) The sad facts of Animal Abuse and how you can help
    4) Functions of the SPCA.
    5) Small Mammals - Victims of Consumerism.
    6) Don't tie your dogs up.
    7) Compassion for stray animals and much more
    8[/i]) Pictures and details of animals currently up for adoption at the SPCA.

    Other animal welfare groups will have their exhibits and brochures at this event promoting their respective causes: Cat Welfare Society, House Rabbit Society, Animal Welfare League, ACRES and Wildaid Shark Conservation, AVA and The Nature Society.

    # DJs from WKRZ 91.3 will be on hand to add to the festivity and you can look forward to exciting stage games.

    #Veteran SPCA volunteer, Christopher will also be hosting games where you can win attractive SPCA t shirts

    # Pet Care advice by SPCA pet care counsellors,. SPCA pet care leaflets and brochures will be available for all to take at the pet care corner

    #Face and hand painting

    # Animal Balloon Sculpturing

    # Mural Board
    Write down your special wish for animals and pin on our mural board.

    # Arts and Craft Corner

    # Sale of SPCA products : t shirts , posters, cards, car stickers, badges, bookmarks, mouse pads , fridge magnet, Mulberry books.
    SPCA DESK CALENDAR 2004
    SPCA DIARY 2004
    CHRISTMAS CARDS

    # Become a Friend of the SPCA by becoming an SPCA member.
    Sign on at the Pet Care and membership booth

    # Free SPCA posters will also be given out . A must for your bedroom wall, it teaches us a very important message that a pet is a lifetime of commitment . Only one poster per person.

    # The highlight has got to be the special guest puppy from the SPCA pound who will be making a one hour guest appearance on Saturday and Sunday.

    # Visit the Science Diet stall for free samples of cat and dog food.

    # Don't forget to buy the boardgame that every animal lover must have - THE PET RESCUER! It makes an excellent gift for children. Besides being fun, it teaches them about responsible pet ownership with important animal welfare messages (eg. A Pet is a Lifetime of Commitment) peppered throughout the board game. Only $9.90!!!

    # Pick up a cookie box or two for the upcoming Christmas celebrations. Three wonderful and yummy flavours to choose from , Cashew Nut Crunchies, Almond Sugee and Pineapple Tarts. The money raised from the sale will go towards the animal welfare cause.

    Remember to keep this day free and book your family and friends too.
