    From Tuesday 22 July 2003, the through train service between Boon Lay and Changi Airport will be terminated and replaced by a shuttle service between Tanah Merah and Changi Airport. Average frequency is 12 minutes per train. The frequency will be shortened to 7 minutes per train in the evening between 7 pm and 10.30 pm.

    Also, from Sunday 27 July 2003, Tibs bus service 989 will no longer serve Changi Airport. It will be integrated with service 185 to become a new service 985 plying between Choa Chu Kang and Kallang MRT station.
