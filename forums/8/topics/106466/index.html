    i asked a Christian friend this question before too.

    She told me that is because doesn't mean you are Christian or you pray, you will have a smooth easy life. It is not like a magic 'get rid of my problem' way out by praying. Praying just makes you feel more at ease that you are not alone, you are releasing it out to God, your creator. He may not seem to help you solve it in the most direct way, but He probably has a different plan for you from what we may all think. Perhaps He feels you ought to go through this to learn something from it, or to make you better or stronger. The returns is far better than just helping you miraculously get past it.
