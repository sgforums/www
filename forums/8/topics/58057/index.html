The Pursuit for Good Chicken Chop.

  • Hydro
    26 Sep 03, 15:19
    There I was just woke up from my sleep, wondering what should I have for my Brunch ( Breakfast cum Lunch). I always sleep very early about 3 am in the morning, that is why I always wake up late about noon. Decided to have western food again, I am a western food lover. Once I went to People Parks looking for a stall named Chicken Factory after my blood donation at HSA, I was so disappointed when I could not find it and have to eat Yong Tou Fu instead. Just cross the overhead bridge across the PIE and Geylang East Central is within my reach. There is a lot of Coffee Shops selling nice food here, but my favourite is the Hananiese Western Food at the Blk 117 Hawker Centre. I always order Chicken Chop when I first came across any new stall, if they canÂ’t cook the Chicken Chop well, you can forget about the rest of the menu. It is hard to find good and cheap western food these days, all I know of is the one at S11 near AMK bus interchange and the other one near my house at Circuit Road Blk 79A Hawker Centre (Actually it is more famous for it Satay Steamboat, but I donÂ’t enjoy steamboat so much, so I havenÂ’t even try it after twenty over years of living here). What I like about the Chicken Chop is the creamy sauce he put over the chicken, the nicely cook fresh vegetables is something you would not find in most of the hawker centre western food stall.

    The uncle selling it had know me so well as I had become a regular customer. He always offer his seat in front of his stall to me whenever there are too many people and I could not find a seat. He always like to sit down and chat with me whenever he is free. Today he told me a joke, there were this couple who came for their Lunch. The girl order a Chicken Chop, when he brought the food to the table both of them offer to pay for the food. So he took the money from both of them and they were both shock, he laugh and say that it is nothing wrong with that because one pay for the food and the other offer to give him tips. But it was just a joke, he did return the right amount of change back to them. Always crave for a good meal of western food, if you happen to know any good lobang care to share with me too.
