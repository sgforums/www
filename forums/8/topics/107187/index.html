    Shopping :

The Tangs Card only need a min mthly salary of $1800, if I'm not wrong.

    Eating:
    Not worth it. They usually give you very very expensive restaurants like Equinox. If you really want to pay less, why not go to a less expensive restaurant without discount. It works out the same way anyway.

    Anway, OCBC card gives 15% discount at Marche (I just found out).
