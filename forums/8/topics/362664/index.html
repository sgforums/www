Continue this everytime you meet her, and you should be able to get her hp no. by the 3rd time you meet her. If she doesn't remember you, then start a conversation everytime you meet her, and by the 6th time you talk to her, ask for her e-mail. Only ask for her hp no. when you think the 2 of you have converse enough.

    In this case, you only need to look average at least. It is the 'best' scenario, but you will need quite a bit of luck.

    All in all, I will say that appearance matters a lot, so make sure you put on some gel and try to look neat/cool when you decided to try approaching her. Btw, everything above is read up by me, so don't blame me if they don't work.
