    Singapore Sweep top prize is now $2m

    WHEN tickets for the next Singapore Sweep draw go on sale from Jan 8, the top prize will be worth $2 million, up from the current $1.5 million. Each ticket will cost $3, $1 more than now, but fewer will be printed, improving chances of winning.

    The second prize will be worth $400,000, an increase of $50,000.

    Three new prize categories - Participation, 4D Classic, and 2N2 Special Prizes - are to be introduced and the existing 3D Classic, Delight, and Extra Prizes will be scrapped.

    It is the first major overhaul since Singapore Pools raised the top prize from $1 million to $1.5 million in 1995. The total value of prizes will rise from $3.75 million to $4.3 million, while the number of tickets printed will shrink nearly 23 per cent from 3.5 million to 2.7 million.

    The new tickets go on sale on Jan 8. The draw is scheduled for Feb 4.
