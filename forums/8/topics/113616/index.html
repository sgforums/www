    Jane use to be rebellious, and throw tempers easily at home.. she was sad that her parents wasn't earning enough money so that they can have a better life.. being the eldest in the family, vacation means working part-time to substain what-ever little money she earn to contribute household expense..

    She was used to being left out from her peers, where most of them can have anythings they want in life.. be it a nitendo, barbie dolls, or rotting at home during school holidays.. She hate about everything, and lament why life was so un-fair to her...

    She had never been to any country before, not even JB.. passport to her was redundant.. Home was a small 65sq m, which she have to squeeze with her younger brother and sister.. It doesn't give her any room of privacy, and she often cry herself to bed..

    When she was in JC, life took a turn for the worse when her dad die of cancer.. she was in a cross road , whether to continue her studies or start working.. she knew there was not much choice for her..

    When her teacher learn of her plight, he introduce her a part-time job as a property agent.. he encourage her and was always around whenever she need a helping hand.. Through her hard work, she finally manage to complete her JC and was also the company's top housing agent..

    Today, although she boast of a landed property in CBD area, she remain humble.. speaking through a seminal, she said.. " If im not strong and independant, i could end up in a night-club or worse, be a prostitute.. Im happy that my teacher was always there for me, and i was given a chance"

    I don't regret the path in my life.. thou tedious and broke, it taught me the real value of money, and bring out the character that i have today.. I have miss out my precious child-hood, But i certainty did not miss out what i have achieved today..
