    When you live longer that will be a tough question to answer. Regardless what the inspirational texts say about friendship, I think in real life that is pretty hard to achieve.

    Lots of people drift apart. Many just stop contacting each other when it is busy. Perhaps its just a fact of life, and that they are not the "friends" in the definitions that everyone has.

    Sometimes you think that the friends you have will last forever, like when you are in primary school, but you find that you lose contact soon also. I guess you can't keep in contact with everyone all the time. A few very good friends are what you need, in my view.

    Well, despite my more conservative view, I would like to think true friendships exist. You see so many people with friends that they know for such a long time. Just your luck if you meet someone in this short life whom you can click and be friends with and stay that way.

    One lesson I learnt is, you should try to actively do the things that marker posted. BE a good friend rather than wait for someone to do the same to you. Its not as easy as you think.

    Happy friendships.
