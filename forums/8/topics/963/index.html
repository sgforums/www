    Let me start off by saying that this posting is not intend as a flame bait or to offend anybody.

    I remembered that I once posted in a thread saying that the Original Sin refers to the sinful nature inherited, rather than the actual sin of eating from the Tree of Life itself. It is this sinful nature that leads us to sin, and we all know that the wages of sin is death. Somebody asked me if I can find any verses to support my view. So far, I have found a few.

    ROM 7:5 - For when we were controlled by the sinful nature, the sinful passions aroused by the law were at work in our bodies, so that we bore fruit for death.

    GAL 5:16 - So I say, live by the Spirit, and you will not gratify the desires of the sinful nature. (Gal 5:16-25 all talks about this sinful nature)

    GAL: 5:24 - Those who belong to Jesus Christ have crucified the sinful nature with its passions, and desires.

    I have also found few verses which seem to suggest that for those who died without having the chance to know Jesus will be given a chance even after their death. But bear in mind that this is different from those who knew but rejected.

    JOHN 5:25 - I tell you the truth, a time is coming and has now come when the dead will hear the voice of the Son of God and those who hear will live.

    JOHN 5:28 - Do not be amazed at this, for a time is coming when all who are in their graves will hear his voice and come out - those who have done good will rise to live, and those who have done evil will rise to be condemned.

    John 5:28 seems to suggest that those who died without knowing him will be judged by their deeds.

    Any comments?
