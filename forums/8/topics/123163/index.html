    In Queensland/OZ it goes something like this:

    1) 15 bucks to take a 30qn basic theory test to obtain your learner's licence. If you fail, come back again tomorrow, spend another 15, and so for and so for.

    2) 20 bucks to get your learner's licence made if you passed the theory.

    3) Start off with driving lessons, an average of 35 bucks by the hour. You can drive on the roads with a learner's but you need a qualified driver on the passenger side.

    4) A minimum of 6 months before you can take your first practical driving test. 100 or so bucks to make arrangements. If you fail on monday, come back on tuesday, foot another 100bucks and take again. You can do the above for as many times till you pass(or run out of cash). No time limit or gazetted periods.

    5) Get your provisional licence after you pass the practical. 1-2 years later apply for an open licence for free.
