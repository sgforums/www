    King of Fighters 97, 98, 99, 2000, 2001, 2002, 2003, + hacked versions
    Street Fighter Zero/Alpha I, II, III
    Daytona (Original) - Expert Course

    There are a couple of other car racing games that are even better than Daytona, but due to lack of opportunity/availability, never got around to playing them much.

    Two of such car racing games can be found in the Plaza Singapura arcade. The more popular one always has 12-18 guys surrounding it, with huge speakers blasting away beside each steering wheel.

    The less popular one though, is the one I find amusing (I can't recall the names for both games). Whenever you screw up while driving (eg. kena crash), your 'girlfriend' (seated beside you, in the vehicle commander's seat) will exclaim, "What are you doing?!?" and the animated graphics will show her thumping you with both her fists, (while you're driving at dangerously high speeds!) and you (ie. the driver) can be seen to be defending yourself against her assaults by raising one hand to block.

    Damn funny. Great graphics and a terrific thrill ride too, that game. Too bad it's not commonly available in the other arcades.
