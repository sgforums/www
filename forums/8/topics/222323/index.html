    hook, very simple...

    get a reactive ball, deliver so that it is rotating about 90 degrees from the direction the ball is going... when it reaches the end of the lane, the forward momentum and the rotation would turn into a curve...

    go to this site => Tenpin Bowling for videoclips of how to go about it..

    frankly, i prefer the helicopter spin.. hook is very dependent on the lane conditions while the helicopter spin takes that variable out of your game..
