Views on Relationship

  • Luf
    24 Mar 00, 18:09
    Sometimes I feel that even if you are with someone now and love the person with all your heart, things might not always be the same. The person might have a change of heart and stuffs. Life in unpredictable.

    To me, I feel that if I love someone now, I will do what I can to get her. Of coz I dun mean that I will neglect her once I get her, but I think it is sweet to go after someone, the feeling of uncertainty, the feeling of hope, the feeling of being ignored. All these made up the experience of life. It might not be sweet everytime, even in a relationship, nothing is definite. In fact, the phase of courtship got it's pros and cons. Firstly, you are not commited and you are not responsible to anyone, and the girl also cannot expect too much from you, since you are not commited. Hence less pressure. At the same time, there is someone whom you can indulge your time in and care for. It is nice to care for someone whom you like. Being attached would hopefully infer to someone thinking of you constantly. That you got a soulmate whom you can share your sorrows and your joy with. Someone who understand you, who knows what you want and be there for you.

    No matter what, I still feel that life is meaningless without love. To be hurt and to recover is what a person would usually go through. Even if I know I would fail, I would still carry on for what I believe in, coz in this world, nothing is definite.. not love at least.
