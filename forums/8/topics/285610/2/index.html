    if u want to transfer to another bank, example POSB/DBS to UOB or OCBC or any other bank, and u want the money to reach in 1 working day, you can approach the counter staff to request for a MEPS/TT. Charges apply.

    Alternatively, approach your bank and get them to issue a cashier's order (cheque) to the receiving party's name. Then give him the chq. Charges apply for 3rd party payee.

    The fastest method is to withdraw cash and deposit into other party's bank using their Cash Deposit Machine. Free.
