    All companies in singapore have a Co. registered nos. Whenever u file complaint, use this nos, it is link to IRA, Inland Revenue, ie income and corporate tax, all your salaries is taxable unless stated by IR to be deem not neccessary, all income must be declare, it is the duty and the law of IRA that the Company's HR or representives, must submit all salaries+bonuses and other related incomes within the company, given out to the employees. Any non submission or false entry shall be classified as attempt to cheat and evasion of taxes. Any discrepancies shall be audited both internally and externally.

    Therefore it is the liability of the company management if they fail to paid out the salary to u within 7 days after your resignation or whatever, fail to report to IRA, fail to reflect on their account leading to audit discrepancies and finally fail to compliance with the contract of or for serivce.

    If nothing is written or stipulate without a contract of or for service, the employment act shall supersede in the eye of the laws in Singapore.
