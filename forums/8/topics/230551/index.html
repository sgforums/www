  • ndmmxiaomayi

    RP - 50% test, 50% daily work.

    Add the grades together divide by the number of modules you took = GPA.

    For example, after 16 daily work and 4 tests, you have a B for all 5 modules.

    B = 3 points

    5 modules = 15 points.

    GPA = 15/5 = 3

    It's cumulative...

    Depends on your poly. For my poly, here's how it works.

    A = 4
    B+ = 3.5
    B = 3
    .
    .
    .
    so on

    So your GPA for this 5 modules is

    (4 + 4 + 3 + 3.5 + 2)/5 = 3.3

    And say you have more done 4 more modules in the next semester, say 9 with grades like A, B+, B+, B

    GPA = (4 + 4 + 3 + 3.5 + 2 + 4 + 3.5 + 3.5 + 3)/9 = 3.89

    It's cumulative... (at least for RP)
