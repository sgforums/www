    My personal way of controlling anger is to lift myself out of the situation mentally. The whole thing of getting angry is because we tend to involve ourselves too much emotionally. It is always important to think logically at the point of "blowing up"...Does showing my anger solve the problem?

    Normally it doesn't. It'll probably make matters worse, and strain the relationship between you and the person you are angry with. Really not worth it. Up till now, I have never had a time which I think to myself..."Hmm, I'm glad I blew my top!"

    If people challenge you to throw your temper, the more you should remain calm. Hum a tune in your head, smile, and think that it is just a waste of time and energy to deal with this sort of people.
