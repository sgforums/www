    Why original games cost as much as they do ?
    Well, it is true that original games have a higher price tag due to the need to cover up loss due to piracy.
    But there are other factors.

    1. Research and development costs are high.
    It requires an average of 2 million to do the research, writing, designing and programming. The costs includes, Wages, Equipments....

    2.Packaging and marketing costs are high too.
    Without the makers' advertisment on gaming magazine, press conference about the game, would anyone know about the game at all ???
    In addition, money is required to provide the beautiful packages the games are in, to manufacture the wondeful CDs and to ship the games to different locations.

    3.License to make and sell.
    You need a license to make a game. Some times, when the game involve the ideas of another company, (example : Wolverine from Marvel Comics), you need to pay to that company for the right to use the characters.
    In addition, you have to have a license to ship games to different countries and satisfy their censorship laws. You need a license and a decent shop to sell games.

    4. Further Improvement to the product.
    When the game is sold, the company still need to please the consumer by researching and releasing Patches, Webpage, Strategy guides, walkthroughs....
    Games like Starcraft have their own world-wide gaming Server and therefore requires money.

    Therefore, original games will never be able to compete with Pirated counterparts.
    While pirated ones can go for $3 each, the best price original softwares can go is around $30. (Which is what original games are going for right now...well some of them.)
