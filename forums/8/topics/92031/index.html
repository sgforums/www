    One of the greatest musicians the world has ever known, Kitaro has launched a massive Asian tour. He is set to take Singapore by storm this coming, September, where he will perform a one-night concert only that is not to be missed.

    It could be said that Kitaro's music is wordless poetry. The GRAMMYÂ® and Golden Globe-winning composer and multi-instrumentalist's lush, meditative soundscapes illuminate vast worlds of the imagination, at the same time that they utterly transcend the boundaries of geography and language. Fusing electronic wizardry, traditional Japanese forms, and pop-inflected Western idioms, Kitaro has won global acclaim with a signature sound that seamlessly marries the age-old and the space age, earthly splendor and the ephemeral. "We have music so we can feel the Universe," he has said, and, over the course of his extraordinary, more than three-decades-long career, millions have done exactly that through experiencing his exquisitely realized sonic artistry.

    His debut solo album, 1978's Astral Voyage, was a sensation in Japan, generating a sizable cult following for the atmospheric pieces that critics dubbed "sound pictures" and "mind music." Kitaro's fame skyrocketed in 1980 with his soundtrack for an hour-long documentary about the ancient overland trade route from Europe to Asia, Silk Road. The album is considered an all-time instrumental classic, and the show was so well received, it evolved into a hit staple on Japanese National Television for five years running.

    In the following years, Kitaro earned a number of Grammy Nominations for Mandala, An Enchanted Evening and Gaia Onbashira. The accolades were made official when after an amazing six nominations, Kitaro captured the prestigious Grammy Award in the 'Best New Age Album' category for Thinking of You. Kitaro notes, "The Grammy Award was always an incredible dream and to actually win was truly a dream come true."

    Kitaro's music is renowned as well for its messages of peace and spirituality, both in a collective sense, and as directed to the heart & soul of each individual listener. Long recognized as one of the most acclaimed recording artist in the Orient and America, Kitaro has always taken his music far beyond the borders of his native Japan. As a composer and multi-instrumentalist, he has reached millions of people on every continent with his stellar recordings.

    You can expect him to perform his signature tunes at this Asia Tour Concert in Singapore.
