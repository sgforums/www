    today i had this sudden thought, i miss melb life very much. it just came out of the blue.
    6 weeks after returning from Melbourne for my ITP,I dont seem to feel anything for singapore anymore. in other words, i dont like staying here for the rest of my life. i very much miss the life i had in melb (6weeks). I miss every moment of it, the cooking, buying of groceries in Safeway( name of a supermarket we always visit), swanston street (the street where the Uni and our apartment were located and many other shops), yarra river, crown casino, russell street, great ocean road, etc.
    i miss the cooking, the climate, the culture,the lecturers taking us for the ITP,the fact that i had to make every cent count, wash yr own clothes, having to stay in a small apartment and sharing it with 2 other occupants which are complete strangers(same poly diff course) though eventually getting to know them well.

    The fact that you are able to live well and independant of your parents where help is a 7hr journey away is nice. abit like you are in ctrl.

    there are also other things i miss abt melb that i cant put into words or describe properly.

    i miss the place there so much that i dont want to stay in sg and migrate there the moment i turn 21 or something.

    if u ask me abt what i miss here, i would say nothing.
    not that i dont like some ppl because of conflict but it's i dont miss sg as a whole. at the most i miss the food at home that's all.

    in the last 2 days in melb, i dont seem to be eager to come home at all. in fact, i was slightly sad and i found the plane right home very uncomfortable and long. way longer than going there.

    i just dont seem to miss singapore anymore.
