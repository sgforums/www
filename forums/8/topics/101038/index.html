    Nov 4, 2004
    Nicoll Highway hero hailed

    Mrs Sally Heng barely holding back tears last night as she received the Medal of Valour from President S R Nathan on behalf of her late husband, construction foreman Heng Yeow Pheow, who died in the Nicoll Highway disaster in April.

    Wearing a black dress, Mrs Heng shook the President's hand, then turned and bowed towards Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong and several hundred people who attended this year's National Day Awards ceremony in the Suntec City ballroom. Others present were Senior Minister Goh Chok Tong, Minister Mentor Lee Kuan Yew and senior Cabinet members.

    Nine Singapore Civil Defence Force officers also received the Medal of Valour for their rescue effort. Former director-general of education Wee Heng Tin received the top honour - the Meritorious Service Medal.
