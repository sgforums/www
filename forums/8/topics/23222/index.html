    Ok Jason, some answers to your qustions :

    1) It really depends as not all banks are totally linked up for funds transfer now !
    You can of course transfer funds from eg. DBS to POSB and vice versa.
    However, from say POSB to UOB or POSB to OCBC may be a problem as they are not interlinked yet !

    2)Unless the person who transferred the money gave you his account no., there is no way you'll know who transferred you the funds as all you'll get from your bank notification is a bank reference number which may or may not reveal the identity or a/c number of the depositor !

    3)Giving your bank account number will be no problem, but instead "hit the jackpot" as there are known cases some bank employees depositing huge amount of monies from dormant accounts by error to a wrong acount number.
    Wah, what a windfall !
    Anyway should this happens, they will rectify the correct amount before you can even detect the major error !

    What I would suggest is depending on the bank that you have right now, perhaps open one each say POSB, UOB and OCBC, so that anybody can deposit to your respective account without any problems.
    It may sound complex and confusing, but all that is required from you is to get these depositors to inform you by email of the date and approx. time of deposit, and the bank that they'd used to deposit the funds and to which a/c(bank).
    So, end of the day, you have the funds in your respective a/cs and problems solved.
    However, it will be some time before all banks allows immediate transfer through all the local banks in S'pore.
    For the time being, I think you can take the above option, or as per my earlier suggestion get yourself a PO box, and accept cheques payable to your site.
    Hope my suggestions will help you solve this payment option problem.
    Good luck !
