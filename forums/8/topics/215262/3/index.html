When I was in the Army I'm sad to say that many of the Malays in my company felt that I was a heretic of sorts just because I'm was a A Level student and they had less education.

They were the ones who told me about all the backstabbing and politics that goes on behind my back. But as for the rest... they backstabbed me and whispered behind my back that I was snooty and arrogant just because I had higher education than them.
