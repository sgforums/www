    Children's Day

    They bring laughter, they bring tears, they bring joy.
    Indeed, parents have the greatest gift from God,
    None other than adorable children whether they be girl or boy.

    This special tribute I pen for the lovely children of all nations
    For it is Children's Day come 1st October 2001 and time for a celebration.
    Without these lovable ones a family is not quite complete
    So parents, let's give each and every one of them a nice treat.

    In celebrating Children's Day spare a thought for children who go hungry,
    And whenever we can food and clothing we should offer to them for free,
    But above all we must give them the most important thing which money can't buy,
    Yes....LOVE and lots of hugs and kisses which we should not fight shy.

    A home with lovely children is a sanctuary for Daddy and Mommy.
    They are angels and can at times be so cute and funny.
    I wish all you lovely children a Happy Children's Day 2001.
    I love all of you even though my wife, at miscarriage, lost our only child and till today we have none.

    copyright Raymond Anthony Fernando
    A fitting tribute to all children of the world
