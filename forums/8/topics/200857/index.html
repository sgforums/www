    Originally posted by Jp1:
    all same la. i dont care brand as long useable and last can liao
    Depends on your needs and usage. The wenger multipurpose knife has a better scissors spring that is durable and does not jam. The victorinox one tends to jam and the scissors joint gets loose easily.

    Furthermore, the victorinox has a very durable cutting edge that can take many years or careless abuse and still not need sharpening.

    A cheap multipurpose knife tends to jam and you have to fight with it to open the utilities after sometime even though the joints are well oiled. The knife is also dull upon purchase and loses its anti rust coating upon sharpening.

    As a gift, the victorinox is the most recognisable and well received knife as compared to its close rival wenger and if you give a cheap brandless one, dun expect any calls of thanks from the receipient.
