    Originally posted by Jfund:

    PedoBear, how do i hack the logic controller? =x

     

    first of all, i am assuming your digital clock are those 4 digit LEDs types

    |¯ |  |¯ |  |¯ |  |¯ |
    |¯ |  |¯ |  |¯ |  |¯ |
     ¯    ¯     ¯    ¯
    each digit is made up of 7 LEDs; 4 digits x 7 LEDs = 28 LEDs

    basically, there is a controller chip in the clock that is controlling the 28 LEDs to light up in the correct sequence to display the time.

    open up the clock, look through the PCB, if you can see the chips then you are in luck, you just need to locate the chip that controls the LEDs and reprogram it to only display 12:17 instead of the time.

    some chips are cemented to the PCB with those black and hard stuff to prevent tampering, if thats the case, the chips cannot be reprogrammed.

    assuming you can see the chip, there should be a brand and model number on it. google the brand and model to get the chip's specs and programming guide.
