    I believe the NS scheme in which u sign ON is a 6 year bond inclusive of NS.However if you take the Joint SAF-Poly scheme it is a 4 year bond includesive of NS.The thing here is you can only sign on once you are in your vocation.Meaning if he is already posted to the Navy for NS then he can choose to sign on.But the Joint SAF-Poly you get to choose your own branch.
    As for the navy and give time to you well have to see the time table.On average if he serves on a ship he will be gone for anywhere between 5days-2 weeks stretches after which he is free for the next 2-4 weeks before he should set sail again.But it all depends on the timetable of which he will be given months in advance.
