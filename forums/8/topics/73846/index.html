    Treatment for all who need it

    WE WOULD like to thank Mr Philip Teh Hock Seng for his letter, 'No money, so man hurt in fire left without medication' (ST, March 1, and assure him that medical treatment is always provided to patients at the Emergency Department of Tan Tock Seng Hospital without question of payment, as treatment of the patient is our priority.

    In addition, patients who are unable to pay their medical bills are provided Medifund assistance for treatment or medication.

    In cases where medication is not needed urgently and patients do not have cash or a credit card to make payment, they may choose to purchase the medicine at a later date.

    Our hospital continues to face a substantial amount of bad debts from patients who can afford to pay, but do not settle their bills. We therefore seek Mr Teh's understanding of our situation.

    We want to assure him that we will investigate his colleague's case. We would be grateful if he could ask the patient to contact our Quality Service Manager on 1800-2529913 to provide more details of the case.

    CHRISTOPHER TAY
    Chief Operating Officer
    Tan Tock Seng Hospital
