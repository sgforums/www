    For private you will only gain if you can pass 1st attempt. If you need > 1 attempt then the cost can actually come quite close to SSDC cuz you will take up more remedial lessons from private and then the waiting time for next test could be 1 - 2 months later compared to 1 - 2 weeks for SSDC.
