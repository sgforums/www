    AboutAGal, it is impossible to create high resolution image from low resolution image. The information scientists call it entropy.

    You can only go from high to low res, not the reverse.

    Of course Photoshop can help you to a certain extent but there is a limit.

    In Photoshop, open your image, click Image, Image Size, Change the pixel there, remember to select resampling (choose bicubic for better result).

    Anyway bear in mind, the result won't be satisfying!
