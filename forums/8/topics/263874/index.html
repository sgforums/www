    There is not much harm in either way. But it's better to let it charge for 8 - 10 hours when you are going to charge it for the 1st time. This is to let the battery and mainboard register the same battery level. Otherwise you might see that although the screen said it is fully charged, but when you switch on the device for a few mins, the battery level will drop very fast.

    Also, for Lithuim Ion battery, it is better to charge it when the battery is about 25% - 50 % left. This will prolong the a normal battery life span to about 3 - 6 months. For Nickel battery, the best is to let it drain till it is empty before charging.
