    Once, there was a little boy growing hungry. He found a little pot by the street, but refused to be reduced to begging with it. So he filled the pot with water, found some twigs and started a fire.

    Then he began stirring. A middleage women walked by with her basket of vegetables she bought from the market.

    "What are you doing little boy?" She asked.

    "I am making a bowl of magic soup. It doesn't look much, but drink it if you wish, and savour the magical taste that you will not forget in whole life..."

    "Oh... let me try" her fingers were almost in the pot when the little boy said again.

    "But its a waste, it will taste better if it had a little of those vegetables you have in your basket." the little boy said, as he pointed to the aunty's basket.

    "Oh.. then let me drop some of these in'.. the aunty said as she emptied a few strands of kai lan, kang kong and choy sum into the pot.

    Then she sat and waited while the soup boil.

    ------------

    And more inquisitive idiots walked by, threw in fish cakes, ngor hiangs, cattlefish, fish balls... then sat and waited while the soup boil. At the end of the day, there was a great bowl of magic soup.

    ... and that's the yong tau fu of today.
