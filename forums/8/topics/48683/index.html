Just adults and either one or two teenagers. Besides, it didn't make much sense cos who would play marbles late at night and in this day and age? So my question is, is that really true? Do estate developers really have marbles inserted within a housing block itself, and if it does, is it common to every estate, or only selected ones? At the time, I told myself that there really was a kid upstairs just to reassure myself but I have to say, I'm really glad I no longer live there. Anyway, if you guys have time, I hope you can reassure me further with whatever you might know or find out.
