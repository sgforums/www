    Fish. Preferrably "batang" or "taipan"
    cai xin
    ginger
    garlic and onion
    kang2 hu3 kia4 (ikan bilis)
    yellow bean
    pork bone (ka1 dang2 guk4. this bone from the side.. very juicy one. ask from the pork seller)

    1. slice fish.. season with corn flour. put one side
    2. cai xin. wash.. dry them. put one side
    3. slice ginger. put one side
    4. garlic and onion. slice. fry until brown. drain away the oil. put one side.
    5. clean the ikan bilis. remove the black stuff at the gills.
    6. boil ikan bilis, yellow beans and pork bone. add salt.
    7. after it boiled, turn heat down.. keep it there to boil until soup gets tasty.
    8. watch tv. (a watched pot never boil)

    9. off tv. bring the seasoned slice fish in.
    10. drop ginger into the soup.
    11. turn up fire.
    12. drop fish to heat in the soup. the moment it turns from bloody to white, take it out. put in bowl.
    13. drop vegetable into soup. cook for a while.
    14. scoop soup and vegetable out, put in bowl that has fish in it.

    that's it. If you want the milky soup instead.. pour condense milk between step 8 and step 9.
