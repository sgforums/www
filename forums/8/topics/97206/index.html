    A Spyware, is a software that only James Bond will use on missions.

    But seriously, it's basically a bug. Technically, it's regarded as a Marketing Application. Spyware mainly monitors your online activities, taking note of sites you frequently visit, and can potentially slow down your internet connection bandwidth.

    Examples of spyware are things like screensavers, fancy mouse cursors, wallpapers that changes by itself ( because you're linked to a server that provides the wallpapers ), a popular one is Kazaa which shares your system's files with the World Wide Web. Forget privacy if you're using Kazaa..

    Remember this; Anything FREE on the Internet, is Spyware. Having spyware onboard, means you can forget Privacy.

    Spyware, can also act like a virus magnet. So good luck, if you don't have a firewall and antivirus installed that is Up To Date.
