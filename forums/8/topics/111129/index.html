How do people fall in love? Love hurts yet pple still sink!!

  • LadyAngel
    6 Jan 05, 00:12
    Well.. As usual, this is purely my own opinions.. so shoot me down whenever u feel im wrong.

    I believe that most relations revolve around these 3 steps:

    1st, Attraction: It is essential because without Attraction people won't even make the first step to get to know another person... and if you dont even get close to a person.. how can you love her? However, this stage alone isn't love. Probably just a crush or what. Attraction can be either physically, intellectually, emotional or financially (the worse reason, but yet sadly, it happens.).. and many other reasons.

    People find themselves attracted to another person because they believe that they find something in that person that is important to them. People usually mistake this for love (Especially the physical attraction part). I believe this is because they tend to be "in love" with what they see in that person (whichever area they are attracted to) and belive that it is love to the ENTIRE person.

    2nd, Love: This is the part after attraction, where people really spend the time to get to know the other person.. and realize that it is more than just the initial attraction to that certain aspect of that person. They start to enjoy each other's company and hence, grow to love and like that person.

    However, passion only lasts 3years... but i believe love lasts longer.. however, most people who do not fall in love, or fall out of love.. still tend to stay together due to the next reason

    3rd, Attachment: Stuff like "we've been together so long...", "we've had such wonderful memories", "without him/her my life will be incomplete" aren't reasons of love.. but rather, attachment.

    Even when pple fall out of love, they stay together becoz they are attached. Imagine a story book... not every chapter is the climax, not every chapter is funny or nice.. but some chapters just help to progress the plot, or give closure. If you take it out, the story doesnt really have any meaning, or cannot continue. That is the same case, i guess... The person isn't the love of your life anymore, but u just can't let go.. coz he/she has become such a big part of your life.

    At the first and third stage, pple often get hurt, coz in the first stage, they tend to be too focused on that aspect that they love and get disappointed when they learn that a person is MUCH MORE than just that! But once they get to the 2nd stage, they're safe. Some pple either just skip to third stage, or after 2nd, go to third. People get hurt here too.. coz the love decreases and then, becoz of all the wrong reasons, they stay together, being under the impression of love.. and tend to neglect the other party, not treating him/her the way a lover would.

    Well... what do you guys think? Am i right, or am i just being biased again?
