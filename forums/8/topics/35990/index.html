    narz believes positive corporate cultures can be cultivated.

    Management has to set examples by encouraging sharing of information by not being selfish themselves.

    Instance One: Before implementing policies, management should take time & pain (it can be a great hassle) to explain the reasons behind the new initiatives. Staff can thereby take this opportunity to share insights on the feasibility of the policies before it is actually implemented. Many typical local firms go by the top down management approach resulting in a lot of resentment and resistance.

    Instance Two: Management can motivate staff by simple mentorship programmes whereby experienced staff can mentor inexperienced personnel when undertaking challenging projects. Set targets to be met by mentors & mentees & both parties will be driven to share & communicate more with each other.

    A simple aspect to bring about nformation sharing is: Communication. Think of ways to enhance communication among staff at various levels and information should flow better.

    Think of it this way.
    Positive culture incorporated (by sharing & enhanced communication)
    employers & employees exchange valuable information that would aid them to work more efficiently & serve their ultimate pay masters (customers) better
    customers are happy, business improves
    business expands and needs more management staff
    more people get to climb to the top as more higher positions are required
    more $$$ earned eventually.

    This is why narz strongly advocates the marrketing approach to business. The customers are your paymasters, not your bosses.

    Of course, reality is that good management is hard to come by... each company's manangement would have their flaws & weaknesses. The key aspect is to recognize that they are not perfect & constantly improve their styles of managing. Only can then employees be led towards a brighter tomorrow for themselves too.
