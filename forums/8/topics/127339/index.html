    EC = quasi-govt, but is still built by private developers.

    main diff is u get subject to rules and regulations similar to HDB, eg. 5 yrs cannot sell, within 10 yrs cannot sell to foreigners. there is a household income ceiling of S$12k pm vs S$8k for new HDB vs no income limit for private (of cos!). plus u get $30k grant for EC if u form a family nucleus and are first timers, like for resale HDB.

    both EC and private can use CPF to pay instalments.

    but downpayment of 20%, only 10% can use CPF. These apply to both EC and private.
