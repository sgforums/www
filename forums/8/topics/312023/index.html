    1) buy a $9.90 tent from carrefour

    2) buy a box of $2.90 mosquito coils

    3) buy a packet of $1.50 wet tissue

    4) but a packet of $0.90 cracker & a $1.00 bottle 1.5lt mineral water

    5) take bus 29 from Tampines Interchange & get off at bus stop outside SAF jetty at Changi @ $1.10 each

    6) walk towards right

    7) take a pee or pang sai and freshen up at the last toilet @ FOC

    8) walk right some more

    9) look for that tall oak tree with cushy cover

    10) set up $9.90 tent making sure the tent door faces the seas

    11) burn 4 sets of mosquito coils and place at 4 ends of tent

    12) scatter crackers around tent. you would be alerted if anyone comes close to your tent

    12) close tent door after checking surrounding again

    13) begin business

    14) after business, wash briefly with water from 1.5lt of mineral water

    15) pack up tent and throw it into nearby dustbin. Do not litter.

    16) proceed back to last toilet and wash up thoroughly. Bathing facility is available

    17) cross over to opposite side of road and take 29 back to Tampines.

    Above package should cost approx $20 and would cover basic needs only.
