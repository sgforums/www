    4 May 04, 19:50
    As a junior prison's officer , you will most probably be in charge of escorting the prisoners to the Dispensary (clinic) or to Visit rooms , and basically anywhere else they have to go.

    The atmosphere is indeed depressing , you have to use your keys every few steps you take and you must be very alert to fights etc.

    You will also be required to supervise inmates working as cooks or plumbers or electricians(yes they do work as these). Your job is basically to follow orders from your superiors and to ensure the safety and well being of the inmates.

    In certain institutions , you might be required to be a Personal Supervisor to some inmates. You will be there closest friend , you will help them reform , you will help them with family troubles and certain matters regarding their cells or dorms.

    Once you settle in , some inmates will become your friends(yes , people are friendly in there) and you can chat a little sometimes.

    It all depends on your attitude , if you see this as a chance to help reform the prisoners , to give them a second shot at life , this is the job for you. If you just want the money or are just blindly signing up , this is a rather bad choice.

    The environment is very strict , but don't be too intimidated , the prisoners may try to take advantage but try to draw the line somewhere , ultimately 90% of them are nice people , believe it or not. If you treat them with respect , 90% of them will do the same.
