  • NewBWithACause

    Well, lets look at the different attributes.

    Power: Well, Batman is just a man, while spidey has his multiple super powers (i.e. Strength, spider sense, wall climbing)

    Advantage: Spiderman

    Gadgets: Spiderman's only consistent gadgets are his spinners. He can whip up some nice stuff in a pinch, I doubt he will ovetake Batman in this category.

    Advantage: Batman

    Fighting Ability. This is kind of like Martial Arts vs. a super powered acrobat. Spidey's power make up for his lack of Martial arts skill.
    Advantage: draw

    Resources: Gotta go with the billionare over the poor photographer.

    Advantage: Bat

    Intelligence: Both these guys are super smart. Batman is a detective with tons of cool crime solving gadgets. Meanwhile, Peter Parker is the poor college kid who makes all his inventions by stealing stuff from the University. Since this is basically the way all cool inventions get accomplished, I'll go with spidey.

    Advantage: barely Spidey.

    So far, we got ourselves a draw. There is only one way to end this. Villian Bantering Ability. Spiderman is unbeatable in this category. The dark knight is more of the silent avenger.

    So in the end, Spidey takes the fight in a bantering rout.
