    That was an old song by Liu Wen Zheng called "Chi Dao" (late). The story was just the opposite, a guy telling a girl that she was too late, he already has given his heart to some other girl.

    Think the version you listened to should be the recent cover by China Dolls.

    ++++++++++++

    "ni dao wo shen bian, dai zhe wei xiao
    dai lai le wo de fan nao
    wo de xin zhong, zao yi you ge ta
    wo, ta bi ni xian dao "

    (you come to my side with a smile
    with it comes the troubles
    I've long had another girl in my heart
    she came earlier than you did)
