Legitimate MLM companies after submitting their marketing strategies and adhering to stringent controls and approved by goverment were made legal. Usually these legitimate companies will be a member of Direct Selling Association Singapore. So one can make checks to see if they are legal or not.

    Unlike pyramid selling, MLM marketeers do move products and products are also priced to be acceptable by the market they are targetting at. The law prohibits coercion and hard selling. The law also provides 100% money back gaurantee if products are returned up to 60 days from date of purchase to protect consumers.
