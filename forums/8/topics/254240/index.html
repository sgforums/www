    Originally posted by orionlight:
    how is it played?i'm quite confused
    -Pick one case at first, that's YOUR case. Can only open on 2 occasion:

    1) When you accept the DEAL which offered by BANKER, but you won't get to keep the money in it.

    2) After you have opened all the suitcases and then you can open yours and whatever the amount you get to keep.

    - Then every turn, open case by case; its better to open cases which have low values as it increases the probabilty that your case has a high value!

    - After about 5 cases have been opened, the BANKER calls and then offer an amount for YOUR CASE.

    - Can choose to DEAL or NO DEAL.

    - If NO DEAL, then continue opening remaining cases. If DEAL, then you take the amount that the BANKER offers, but lose the CASE amount which can be the $250,000

    - The BANKER will offer higher if the cases that has opened contains lower amount; but once the higher amounts have been opened, the offer for YOUR case drops very fast!.
