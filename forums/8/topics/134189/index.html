  • gentlerock

    There are two main ways to write a cheque:

    - crossed cheque
    - cash cheque

    For crossed cheque, your name is on it. So even if you drop it, no one will be able to do anything with it. When you deposit the cheque to the bank, the bank will put the money in your account.

    For cash cheque, it represent (and is equivalent) to cash. You go to the bank and exchange it for cash. If you drop it, anyone else can go to the bank and exchange it for cash too.

    Keep your cash cheque carefully....
