    Asure,

    "stages of life" is different from "level of maturity"

    I never talk about "maturity" i talk about "stages" of life. Like which stage you are in .... experimenting with BGR, searching stage, enjoying life stage, settling down stage, thinking about marrying stage. And all these stages are not directly related to level of maturity.

    In Singapore, these "stages" of life is well well defined. For example, when someone tell you he is 12 years old, you will ask "PSLE ah?"
    When someone says he is 16 years old, you will ask "O LEvel ah?"
    When someones says he is 18 years old, u will ask "A Level ah, when you go Army?"
    When someone is 23, you will ask "Uni ah ? or working got gf or not?"
    When someone is 35, you'll ask "how long you married?"
    Know what I mean ? And all this has nothing to do wiil the level of maturity.

    My point is, 2 persons who are at different stages in their life, is hard to be together. You may be thinking about marriage, setting up a family, but your gf still wanting to have more fun in life, wanting to enjoy and not to settle down.

    And age, I feel, is a good INDICATION of a person's stage in life. So if the stage of life a person is in matters, age being an indicator, matters.
