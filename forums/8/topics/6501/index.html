    Age matters or not ? I think it depends on which country.

    Correct me if I am wrong.
    In country like US, people do not go through the same stages in life. So a 17 year old may be more mature than a 22 yr old. All depends on what you have experienced in life.

    In Singapore, most of us go through the same stages. Primary school, Secondary School, JC or Poly then Univerisity.
    So in Singapore, a person's age will be a good reflection of which "stage" he is in his life.

    I as a Singaporean, think that age does matter in our society. A guy who is 25 may be thinking about settling down whereas his gf who is 20 may still be in the "experimenting" stage of her life. 2 persons in 2 different phases of life, how to be together ? They just live in 2 different worlds, different expectations and ideals. But this gap narrows when both grow older. For example, this gap is much smaller if the guy is 40 and the woman 35. Similarly, this gap is very big when one is young. For example, the guy is 17 and the girl 12. But in these 3 examples, the age difference is still the same .. 5 years. Hope you get what I mean.

    It is the stage of life one is in that matters. And age, to some extend is a good indication of the stage of life a person is in, especially in a rigid society like Singapore. So ? Age matters, just how much.
