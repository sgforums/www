    (taken from the SPCA wesbite)

    Event : World Animal Day 2002
    Date: Sunday 29th September 2002
    Venue: The SPCA, 31 Mount Vernon Road. (off Bartley Road)
    Time: 10.30am-3.30pm
    Parking will be made available along Bartley Road

    It's soon going to be that time of year again where we celebrate a very important day - World Animal Day!
    This year SPCA will be celebrating World Animal Day at the SPCA on Sunday September 29th .

    You are all invited to our open house.

    There will be:
    -- Exhibitions on the work of the SPCA, responsible pet ownership, animal abuse, sterilisation of pets and lots more.
    -- Stage games : with DJs from WKRZ 91.3 on hand to add to the festivity from 12 noon - 2pm.
    -- Face and hand painting
    -- A Pet Care Advice Corner where you can get information and advice on how to look after your pets.
    -- Sales of gifts and stationery : look out for the new SPCA 2003 desk calendar , new t-shirts and dazzling new Christmas card designs.
    -- Other animal welfare groups will be there to create awareness about their respective causes: Cat Welfare Society, House Rabbit Society, Animal Welfare League, ACRES and WildAid Shark Conservation.

    The highlight has got to be the animals in the Pound awaiting adoption. Come and give them lots of cuddles and get a Polaroid shot taken with them for just $3.

    A free SPCA poster could be yours to take home . It is a collage of our favourite animals and a must for your bedroom wall.

    Check out the jumble sale for some good buys ranging from clothes to books.

    It will be fun for the whole family !
    In the meantime, remember to keep this day free and book your family and friends too.
