Exotic recipes: Wasp/fly eggs burger

  • Harris_JeyaRaj

    Ingredients:

    Fly or wasp eggs (looks like miniature rice)
    Beaten chicken eggs
    Parsley
    Salt and pepper
    Burger buns

    Mix the wasp or fly eggs together with the beaten eggs, salt, pepper and parsely and stir well...sccop and put on flat frying pan shaping them into rounds
    Fry till cooked and slot them into burger buns. Eat and enjoy

    This kind of insect foods are actually eaten in the world if u have doubts check them out on the internet....
