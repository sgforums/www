    Tips for those who seriously have problems with house lizards.
    Keeping a clean environment is very important. If your home have ants & insects esp small files, the lizards will always be there for you. Food is the main attraction for all pests/lizards, they love ants, insects, small roaches & sometimes your leftover! take note those of u like to eat snacks/tibits every where at home & lazy to clean up.

    Lizards hunting area:
    1) HDB or house light that is turn on throught out the night til morning, attract moths/flying insects. Well u can't do much if it is a HDB light - just spray lotsa insecticide around the area - last for weeks or a month.

    2) Ant nest, have u ever wonder why no matter how u clean up daily, spray insecticide around your home area, but they still keep coming back after a while??? There may be a nest inside or somewhere near your home! same goes for the roaches! Try to seek out their nest if possible or call the pest expert if u don't how or no time to DIY.

    3) If your home is clear of ants & roaches, there will be fewer lizards. Now for your home rubbish & food. clean up the rubbish area, esp rubbish left over the night attract lizards! lastly keep all food properly in containers. Good luck
