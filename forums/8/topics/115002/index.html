A very nice gesture from The Coffee Connection

  • JWorld
    30 Jan 05, 01:14
    Dear forumers

    I would like to share with you what happened to me at The Coffee Connection at Paradiz Centre today.

    My day started off with a quarrel at home, which affected my mood and performance at meetings during the day. After the meeting, I went to Paradiz Centre and decided to find a place to sit and have a drink. I needed a place to reconcile my thoughts. My mood had been awful and I had no shoulder to cry on when I needed one.

    I entered The Coffee Connection outlet at Paradiz Centre, sat down and ordered something. Just then, my friend sent me an sms to comfort me about the morning's quarrel that I had. Tears started rolling down my cheek, non-stop. When the waiter served me my food and drink, I turned my face away so as to hide my teary face.

    After a couple of minutes, I stopped crying and made the payment with my credit card. As I was slowly enjoying my drink, a waitress came by and gave me a slice of fruit cake and said that it's on the house. For a moment, I thought TCC was having some promotion to give away free dessert. But I found a note, wrapped in the serviette, with the message written as follows:

    Dear Miss

    Just a little something to cheer you up.

    From:
    Staff of TCC

    This is the first time in my life I have ever encountered this. I wonder what I can do to return such a nice gesture.
