    den write simple, easy to read, no mistakes, no fuss. u shld read more model essays, follow their style to start with.

    storyline muz be clear, got proper beginning and ending. preferably start with an introduction, den problems den solve em, end with happy ending or pleasant surprise. keep everything in a well defined structure.

    if they can't find fault with ur essay, they can't penalise u even if they dun like ur story.. juz not give u distinction only.

    the english exam basically is not a literature contest, its to examine ur competence in the english language. how u present ur thoughts and ideas in english. if its basic and clear, they can only suck thumb.

    dun write insults or vulgers, as far as the story goes, u're last angel on earth.

    if not enough words, describe the events in more details. e.g. rather than saying opening the door, describe the colour, the sound and the feeling when opening it. make sure its relevant though, dun feel horny when opening a door in a haunted mansion with ghosts flying around.

    impt is don't add too much to complicate things, juz fpr the sake of adding some words. e.g. suddenly gundam freedom smash into the mcdonald.. blah blah. which is not related at all.

    once u can write a simple essay, error-free, den think of plots and twists to make it interesting. otherwise, keep it simple. keep it real. don't write far fetched stories like alien adduction or wad.

    dun go and pull off a stunt like writing a story memento style. confirm die if cannot succeed.
