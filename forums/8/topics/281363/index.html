  • Jezmeister
    23 Sep 07, 02:19
    best way I've found to learn it - stand up loosely, feet shoulder width apart etc, put a hand on your stomach to make sure you're using it - your shoulders shouldn't move at all, and go up and down scales but on every note take a deep breath and do a very over the top 'oh' or 'ah' sound for it and strongly force the air out without moving your shoulders, so it effectively seems to be coming from your stomach. you'll probably only manage a few scales before it starts to ache or hurt a little first time but you'll get used to it after a while (by that i mean stop and do it again later not carry on when it hurts) - after a while you'll find it gets easier and, hopefully, you'll be able to translate that technique into controlled breathing and singing for your songs
