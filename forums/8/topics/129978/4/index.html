    Yes i agree. Buying property is abt Location, Location and Location.

    Let me share with brothers my personal experience.

    I am currently trying to get a resale flat (I am single) near Hougang Kovan. So far have viewed a few flat.

    1st flat - 15 min walk to Kovan MRT. Didnt ask age of the flat. i think it is around 18 yr old. Good done up and high floor. After a few week, flat is still unsold.

    2nd flat (abt 5-6 min walk to MRT), 20 yr old flat mid flor and very basic renovation. renovation looks a bit old. whilst still considering the offer price, it was sold within 1.5 week.

    2nd flat (3 min walk to MRT). Also 20 yr old flat 5th floor. Heard no renovation. I am very keen on this one since i go for location. before i could arrange for an apptment with the owner, it was sold in one day. The buyer has already issued deposit cheque on the 1st viewing.

    The moral of the story? buying property is all abt location. the 3 flat that i view is located just within walking distance of each other (hence the available/surrounding amenities is the same). therefore the deciding factor appears to be walking distance to the MRT.

    My point is that if you just want to buy it for staying and that you don't intend to sell your flat in future or due to really tight budget constraint, by all means go ahead to get the Jurong West flat.

    But a note of cautious - shd u decided to sell your flat one day, it may take months before u manage to find a buyer.

    A poorly located flat (even though cheap may not attract buyers).
    A good location (even though expensive) will attract lot of buyers.
