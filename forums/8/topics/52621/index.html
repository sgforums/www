camera review of panasonic lumix DMC-FZ1

  • bismarck
    20 Jul 03, 22:02
    bought it more than a month ago.
    specs and personal review

    2.0 MP ( yes 2.0)
    12xoptical zoom,3x digital
    image stabilizer
    burst mode of 4pics/sec ( ard there)
    leica dc vario-elmarit lens

    2.0MP ? more than enough for me. images taken at 1280 X ???? pixels are 700kb in size

    12x zoom ? excellent

    image stabilizer ? crap function

    burst mode ? cannot use flash

    leica lens ? heard it's good, the pics look so-so ( my skills sucks)

    has a idiot-proof mode for users. otherwise it requires abit of explanation before getting someone to take photo for you. ( there is a green dot that denotes whether or not the image is in focus or not, if yes the green dot doesn't flash)

    the flash is pathetic in design. when taking a photo at close range,the giant lens blocks the flash a little. at the bottom of the image,there is a shadow believed to be caused by the lens.

    rather automatic.cannot select aperature nor shutter speed.

    image del function allows u to either del all images or select a few at 1 shot. but it re-confirms with u whether u want to del the images. DUMB!

    has good lighting options. you can adjust the settings for the lights u are using in the room( warm light, cool light,etc) and can choose between ( cloudy sky, bright daylight,etc)

    set-up functions allows u to change the lcd's brightness,re-format the memory card, reset the file number it names the images( can reset the 'counting' to p0000001.jpg)

    proper hand-grip

    comes with lens-hood + lens adapter

    comes with a pathetic 8MB sd card.

    summary, rather ok camera for basic users who want to jump to manual cameras. includes several light settings which u can choose depending on the lighting conditions.
