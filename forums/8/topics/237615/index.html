    That's because the past participle of "come" is still "come", whereas the past participle of "strike" is "struck", hence "had strike" is grammatically incorrect. "Had struck" is the correct past-perfect form.

    Hence, "had struck" comes earlier in the timeline than "has struck", which is the present-perfect tense, while "had strike" is invalid since it is grammatically incorrect.
