    The armies of the Angaraks are amassing their troops for another war against the West, the Nadraks looking for a new alliance to forge which proves most profitable, and the Thulls scurrying away, trying to avoid being used as cannon fodder.
    Murgo's already send raiding parties into Algarian territory, and the Malloreans cross the great sea to get onto the western continent.

    In the West, the Drasnian Intelligence Agency is quickly gathering information on the upcoming attacks and the Chereks prepare their fleet. The Murgo's near the south of the Algarian plains are already suffering heavily losses from the sabres of the horsemen.

    Which part will you play in this war? Or will you seek to avoid it at all cost to save your home? Will you be a coward or a hero? A farmer or a thief? The choice is yours. Play Prophecy the MUD.
