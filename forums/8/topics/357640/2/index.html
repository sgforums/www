    I live here in Singapore for the past 10 years. As you, i have also wondered why so much indians gather there on weekends. I don't know the answer until few months back. Though, i am Indian, we are creamy (call ourselves) layers who does white collar job and would hardly go to Little india except to buy our grocieries. But, the people who gathers there are blue collar workers, Supporting my friends business, i had a chance with them to interact and most of them live in the dormotories in Tuas, Jurong. Most of the week, they eat their self prepared food (though, it will be horrible, they have no choice), during weekends, they can spend few dollars on so called good food from little indian restaurants and most importantly coming there to interact with their own villagers who work here. The reason for gather is to pass on information back to village friends, family , to send electronics good and to gather information about the happening in their and neighbouring villages back in India.

    Though, it may seems to be a gathering, if you notice, there will be small pockets of people, most of them are from same villages and neighboring villages.

    It is true that it can be irritating when you want to glide through their staring eye (if you are with your wife or girl friend), but, we have no choice unless avoid weekend evenings. This is not the case in India, but, here it is different, because too many singles at a same time.
