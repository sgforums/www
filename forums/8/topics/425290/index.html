    Private diploma focus mostly focus on the core disciplines stuff i.e. main modules of the course, and reduce the unnecessary modules (communications, etcs)  to the minimum. You'll only be taught the main and most important thing that you will need to know.

    Mainstream polytechnic covers more modules and also more skills development (problem solving, communications, projects works, fyps) modules, that's why it takes about 3 years to complete.

     

    Personally, if you ask me, I would choose PSB. Please search our previous threads on PSB and MDIS and you will be able to find out more.
