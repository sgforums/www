    29 Jul 04, 11:28
    watch your purse, don't let it swing out when you walk beside the road. a motorcycle may swing by and grab your purse/bag.

    watch your gold stuff, same as above.

    DO NOT put your purse or handphone on the table when eating, especially at mamak stalls/chow kit (also known as Petaling Street) area. someone may run past and grabs it off your table. kinda hard to chase after them.

    and of course, pickpockets ...

    get ready some coins so that you can put your stuff in the lockers when at sunway lagoon.

    KLCC is for branded stuff (which btw, is about the same price in singapore. Zara in singapore is cheaper, so is MNG).

    Mid Valley for basically a lot of things, mainly for families.

    Bukit Bintang area for branded stuff and budget stuff.
    Lot 10 for branded stuff
    Starhill for branded stuff
    KL Plaza, Bukit Bintang Plaza and Sungei Wang Plaza are connected, lots of gals' stuff, kinda like Bugis concept
