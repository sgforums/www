    yes, that's where i got my nick.
    churrascaria means barbecue in portuguese.

    my first nick in this forum was scaria....
    but i dun like it and register another churra instead and has been using this ever since.

    it's a buffet style of ARD $40+++ per person, meat plus salad.
    if u're having salad only, it's ard $25+++.

    a passador (meat waiter) will come to your table with one type of meat and slice pieces of them onto your plate.
    they have 12 different cuts of meat (beef, chicken, pork, lamb, fish) and a nice selection of salad.
    so, just eat a bit of every 12 cuts, u will find urself pretty full after that.
    leave some room for the dessert coz they have some nice dessert too.

    overall, a pretty nice place with good food.
