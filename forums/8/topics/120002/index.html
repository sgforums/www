    Very healthy right? But one day, in the midst of his usual jogging session, he felt a severe chest pain. He felt like throwing up, but couldn't. He could not breathe well . He quickly rushed home and told his spouse to send him to the hospital. His spouse at first thought he was just panting after jogging too much. But still drove him to a nearby GH.     The second one was my friend's husband. He worked in a highly stressed environment and smoked a lot, mostly to relieve the stress. No exercise, cuz no time.

    One day, while my friend, her husband and the children were at home, her kid suddenly went to the kitchen to tell her that the father was panting. My friend's husband refused to go to the hospital cuz he was concerned that if both adults were away, nobody would take care of the kids. He thought he could just rest and the pain would go away. That was before dinner time. He was in pain for a few hours. It was only late at night, probably past midnight, that he couldn't take the pain anymore and his wife, my friend brought him to the hospital. The doctor diagnoised that three of the vessels were blocked and he has no confident that my friend's spouse could survive thru. But he did survive after the op. However, the nightmare is not over. His heart is so damaged that he had to constantly take a lot alot of pills every day to sustain his life. He was only 36 while he was having the attack.
