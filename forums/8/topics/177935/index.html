    NUS/NTU

    To apply to NUS and NTU, you need to have:
    1. At least two (2) H2 passes
    2. Attempted General Paper (GP) or (Knowledge & Inquiry [KI]) in the same sitting
    3. A minimum of Â‘SÂ’ grade in Mother Tongue Language (MTL)

    NUS and NTU will select you based on your grades in:
    1. GP (or KI)
    2. PW
    3. Three (3) H2 content-based subjects and one (1) H1 content-based subjects (at least one has to be a contrasting subject)

    You can choose to include your MTL grade if you have done well in it.

    SMU (this one is more important)

    1. Good passes in at least three (3) H2 subjects
    2. Attempted General Paper (GP) or Knowledge & Inquiry (KI)
    3. A good pass in Mathematics at H1 level or equivalent
    4. A minimum of "S" grade in Mother Tongue Language
    5. SAT Reasoning (an advantage)
    6. Interview (for shortlisted candidates)
