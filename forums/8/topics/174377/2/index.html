    How old are you? Primary 3 science. But tertiary education explanation.

    Kingdom: Animalia (Animals)

    Animals can be split into two groups: Vertebrates and invertebrates

    Invertebrates can be split into eight groups called Phylum, namely:

    protozoa, coelenterata, platyhelminthes, nematoda, annelida, mollusca, arthropoda and echinoderma

    Arthropoda can be split into 4 groups called Classes, namely:

    crustacea, myriapoda, arachnida and insecta

    Insecta means insects, and is grouped under the animal kingdom. Since ant is an insect, it is an animal as well.
