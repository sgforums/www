    Very possible.

    I have a good friend who was my JC class mate. We used to go out together one on one (for movies, shopping, swimming etc.) during our JC days, but there was no physical contact between us, and no affection other then the normal "friend" affection. I treated her as a 'normal' friend and both of us are comfortable in the friendship and have no intention (and no urge!) of going beyond.

    In time, we got into our own separate relationships and got married... and we attended each other's wedding. We both have our own children now but we are still keeping close contact. Both our families will meet every now and then. Her children and my own children are also becoming close friends.

    So to answer the TS question, yes platonic friendship is possible, though admittedly rare.
