    1. Chua Chua Kang, Ang Mo kio or Tampines

    2. You can only play cards manufactured by Konami. Either English Asia version or Japanese cards. I go for japanese cards as the quality is much better. Japanese cards are also cheaper and the foils are worth much more.

    3.Yes. Every year there is a Worldwide Asia tournament organised by World of JJ.

    4.See answer in 1.But the best players can be found in tampines

    5.I spent $200 on my deck. Cards like Jinzo, Chaos Soldier, DD warrior Lady, reflect bounder and Lightningf Vortex is a must. If you lucky you only need to spend $50 on the boosters and strike foil every time.

    6.Tournament format. 8000LP best of 3 games is winner. Occasionally we play ante.

    7.Dont waste your cash on Starters. Just get the boosters and construct your own deck.
