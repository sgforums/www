    RISE OF VENICE
    political reasons: say this(tho it sounds stupid) LPPPENG
    L-Leadership(capable and commited)
    P-Policies(were practical)
    P-Peace(citizens involvement in this..)
    P-Power(had effective checks on power)
    E-Electorial system(was fair)
    N-Navy(was strong)
    G-Government( practiced aristocratic republic)

    economic reasons? say TAPD.
    T-Trade monopoly
    A-Adventurous spirit
    P-Provision of trade services
    D-Diversifying into manufacturing

    social reasons? our favourite word- LAR!
    L-Loyalty of Venetians
    A-Attracting foreign talent
    R-Respect for different practices

    FALL OF VENICE
    remember this sentence. Can Duck, Why Run?! Oh i see!!!(oiicc)
    (external reasons) (internal reasons)
    External reasons:
    C-Competition from new trade rivals
    D-Discovery of a new sea route
    W-Wars with Ottoman Empire
    R-Rise of larger states(eg. France England and Spain)

    Internal reasons:
    O-Over dependence on merceneries
    i-incompetent military leaders
    i-insensitivity towards neighbouring countries
    c-complacency in Venetian society
    c-corruption in the government

    alright now lemme help u guys with the structured essay.
    remember this format for EVERY single paragraph in the essay.
    P E E L
    P-point
    E-elaboration
    E-example
    L-link

    eg: (P)Venice had capable and commited leaders. (E) They were willing
    to place state interests above their own personal interests.
    (Eg) Despite being partially blind, Doge Enrico Dandalo led a Venetian
    fleet to success. (L) With such high level of commitment, the leaders
    in Venice were able to provide good vision and direction for the city-
    state and this allowed Venice to make wise decisons leadind to its rise.

    yup thats abt it. for every point u can use the PEEL format. ok?
