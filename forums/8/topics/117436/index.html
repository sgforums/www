    THESE Vietnamese sisters had been resigned to marrying farmers in their country.

    Miss Ha Kim Amh and her younger sister, Miss Ha Kim Ngoc, never dreamt of marrying a foreigner.

    But now, the Ha sisters want Singaporean husbands.

    They are looking for the perfect match with the help of Mr Mark Lin, managing director of Vietnam Brides International Matchmaker.

    Speaking in Vietnamese, Miss Kim Ngoc, 20, said: 'Our cousin kept encouraging us to get Singaporean husbands. She married one and she kept saying he was a good man.'

    Two years ago, her 24-year-old cousin had been recruited as a Vietnamese bride by Mr Lin's agency.

    The Taiwanese, who migrated here 10 years ago, has agents in Vietnam to look for the brides, and organises matchmaking tours to Vietnam for his Singapore customers.

    The Ha sisters' cousin and her 35-year-old husband, who earns $1,500 a month as a warehouse assistant, have a 9-month-old son.

    The family has visited Vietnam twice since they got married.

    Miss Kim Ngoc said: 'We can see that he loves my cousin very much. She also said he does not drink or beat her.'

    Last year, the sisters decided that they wanted to marry Singapore men too.

    With their cousin's help, they contacted Ms Pham Ngoe Anh, Mr Lin's chief agent in Vietnam.
