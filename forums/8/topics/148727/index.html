    LEO MAN

    He likes to comb his hair backward, opens his forehead showing a dignified facial _expression. His eyes sparkle, but show some slight laziness. He walks firmly & slowly like a lion - confident & ego proud. Outside, u may think he is a kind & gentle person, but inside he is a strong & secure person. If he is frightened, he will react & respond right away. His words always seem normal but mostly imply "order & demand". He will not talk fast, or cannot talk fast, neither walk fast. In a crowd or at work he will act normally, but not for long u will see him standing out of the crowd & be the center of attention with his words, or his action. U might think he is a shy guy, but deep down inside he wants power over his family & friends. He just waits for that right moment. Do not take what's his, & do not order him, or else u will see a fierce lion. He respects elderly & senior, but will never bow down & accept being like a loser for he will rather die than lose his dignity. He talks bluntly even on an occasion that he should not say such things. He is a compassionate guy & always looks at other people on a bright side. He likes people to listen to him. Though he has such a blunt & bold personality, he could easily reach his goal without making any enemy. Once u disagree with him on something, he can be very upset like a rainy storm on a summer day. It will only last a short time, then he will be back as a cheery, merry person again. He is a bright & witty guy, & he will not put any efforts on something that he thinks will not work & waste his energy. He is a good planner & can manage his job assigned well. When he gives order, he expects them to be carried out exactly. He is the leader type that the followers love. He can give other people advice & solve problems for other people well, except his own mess. He can be easily hurt by other people especially if u don't trust or respect his ability. U could compliment him sometimes & make this lion be your kitty with no difficulty. He is not a good judge for he listens to many people & tends to believe all sides. U can hardly see a Leo man with no woman by his side. If u see him alone, he could already have a loved one in his heart, or just broken up with one. Because he is very proud, he can change many girlfriends. He will do many things for the woman he loves, but losing face is not one of them. Leo man cannot live without love, because for him Love is a ray of Light. He likes people to rely on him, it makes him feels "in power". He may complain if he is asked for favor, but deep down inside he is happy that u asked him. If u offered to help him, he will refuse u right away. When he's broke, he can still find money. He is not careful with his way of spending for he has fun spending money & is happy to buy what he likes. He lets other people borrow money from him easily even if he has no money, he will run to his friend to borrow money for u. He likes first class, first quality of everything. He can work hard like a mad dog, & sometimes can be lazy like a lazy sleepy cat. When he works, he is very serious. When he parties, he can be a party animal. If he asks u out, u will sure to have a fun & jolly time. He will take chance with his love life, so if u know how to handle him, u will win. If he is your loved one, it won't be a romance novel. U have to be ready to calm him down when he over-reacts to small matters because your cool stability will control & ease his mood. If u can't handle or understand him, your relationship will be like a demolition zone, a on & off relationship till all your friends grow tired to hear about your breaking up & making up. Beauty is always in the eyes of the lion. U have to be dignified to walk with the lion king. Your looks is part of his image & ego & he is very proud about it too. If u want his attention on the first time u meet, u better be astoundingly attractive. If u have a first conversation with him, u have to show him how much u adore his thinking. He likes to talk & not knowing that he likes to talk about himself.
