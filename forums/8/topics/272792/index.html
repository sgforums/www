    Person believed to have fallen onto train tracks
    A person is believed to have fallen onto the train tracks along the East-West line, near Tampines MRT station, said a witness.
    The westbound train was travelling from Boon Lay to Pasir Ris.

    A witness at the scene told straitstimes.com that authorities are now trying to recover the body, which is believed to be underneath the train.

    The incident caused a disruption in service along the East-West line. The westbound service has also been suspended.

    The victim's gender and other information are not confirmed as yet.
