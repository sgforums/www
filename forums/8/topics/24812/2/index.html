    The Singapore White House.





    This house has many stories and is famous for Singapore's ghost hunters to explore.

    The house is big. We spent about an hour inside looking around and checked
    every room. Not much furniture there, so probably the guys who came long before
    us took everything away.

    Since we didn't have any "encounters" we left the house.

    Weird and scary enough, we never thought that "happenings" would
    happen only after we left the house! Our bicycles which were left just
    outside the entrance of the house were all toppled! It could not be the wind and
    and there were not a single soul in sight. The area around the yellow house was
    dead silent. And the worse part, there were scratches on the seats of ALL our
    bicycles! The scratches look as if someone had used his nails to scratch our
    nails. Believe me, we went back to Tim house together and stay over there as no
    one dared to go home alone by himself that night.

    This story is one of a successful man who from young without any
    education became successful with his own hard work. However it seems that he was
    not blessed with the fortune of having a family. Although rich and successful,
    he was lonely-without any wife and children. The house above was a grand one
    sitting on freehold land (that is why the government could not claim it).
    However it was purely a grand house and never a home. He had no one to share his
    success with. And on one night, for some unknown reasons he took his own life by
    hanging himself and from then onwards, without any wills written before his
    death, he left this world and the house alone to stand.

    Because the man did not leave any will behind, all his properties and this
    house were left untouched. Without any maintenance, the house lost its glamour
    and looks no more than a dilapidated haunted house.

    DuH
