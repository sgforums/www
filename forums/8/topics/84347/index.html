    try our heroes...

    Asterix (or asterisk).. he's the hero.. something special.. when you read a document or a book, something to point out a special fact used to be marked with a sign.. *

    the sign is used as a point where you know that it is something to be taken note of.. sometimes there will be a footnote at the bottom of the page..

    therefore Asterix is special..

    Obelix (or obelisk).. which means a stone monolith, usually tall.. something like the menhirs he carves and delivers for a living.. he is also strong and dependable.. a rock which Asterix can depend on as a friend...

    Getafix (get a fix)... the druid.. who provides the village with the magic potion.. or their "fix" of potions...

    Cacofonix the bard (from the word cacophony)... his "talent" is taken for noise.. which is what a cacophony is.. a jarring noise...

    have a read and know what these things are

    they add to the genius of the series which was not written in english, but so well translated!
