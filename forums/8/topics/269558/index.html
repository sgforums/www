    The Beginning Of the End? (Chapter 1)

    Taking a last look at his family, he felt a sense of regret.Private Bill Curtiss had never experienced this worry before.He felt a pang of utter fear and hopelessness.He was serving in the Air Force in Baghdad as a pilot.As the last call for Flight 453 ended, he tore his gaze away from his lovely wife,Marsha and his wonderful daughter Kristinne.

    He proceeded to the gate and soon disappeared around the corridor.As the Boeing 747 taxied down the runway, Marsha and Kristinne whispered an almost inaudible prayer for Bill.

    In the aircraft,where the jet engines shuddered and groaned as the pilot cruised down to the taxiway of the Texas airport.With a feriocious roar,the throttle on full revolution, the aircraft roared towards the runway.Like a graceful eagle, the aircraft lifted into the air and soon disappeared from view.

    High above the skies,Private Bill started on his steaming hot lunch and glanced over to other servicemen who were taking part in the war against MEC.

    It was well into midnight when the plane touched down in Oman,Pakistan.With a shudder, the plane groaned as the brakes were applied harshly.As the aircraft taxied to a stop, 4 , almost invisible people were waiting impatiently

    The person in the middle of the four, lined up in a linear position held a loudspeaker and barked menancingly"All Pilots to the bunk please!".He motioned towards a small ,yet cosy building.

    Pilot Examination.(Chapter 2)

    Sunrise.

    The Commander was designating servicemen towards their respective platoons.Private Bill was immediatly cleared to join Alpha platoon and rushed into the crowd of Alphas without further ado.

    Later that day, the sun was high up and looking gallently towards the yellow earth.

    The Commander hastened the Alphas into attention.Bill Curtiss was assigned to a F-15 Eagle, which was quick and agile when it came to a dogfight.Equipped with laser digital warning system and AGM missiles,it was a deadly machine.He teamed up with Flight Instructor Ross Wingley and shook hands.Without a word,they both climbed into the cockpit and so began the first day in Pakistan.

    At the end of a long and eventful day of bomber training, Bill hastened into his bunk.He shook hands and acknowledged his neighbour Private Jim Weiss, who was assigned to a F-14 Tomcat.

    Lurching off into an eventful sleep,he tossed and turned,with loud cackling, planes being shot down being replayed over in his dream.

    Regeneration. (Chapter 3)

    As months flew by without incident,Bill was now a corporal.He knew how to operate the aircraft and soon , became an experienced pilot.

    He was lurching off into sleep... Loud Cackling filled his dreams yet again......

    An Air Raid siren sounded impassively.A night raid was materializing and the radar had picked off 15 airplanes swooshing overhead .Patriot missiles were deployed and pilots were hastened towards their planes.

    Bill jumped into the cockpit and switched on the engine.His Co-Pilot, Ross Wingely mounted into the plane, switched on the AGM missile system.With a loud roar, the Eagle screamed down the runway and took off into the night

    "Alpha-34,Confirm position and status."A voice materailsed over the intercom .

    "Yeah, i hear you buddy".."Loud and clear".Position 56 Km away from Base Gerring"!.Turning to Ross, he gave a thumbs-up and started for the clouds.

    Suddenly, it seemed that the night sky was exploding with MEC airplanes.The Eagle sidebanked, dodged a MEC plane.He decided to engage a MiG-29 which was concentrating on attacking the airfield.Bill flicked the missile fail safe system from neutral to Active.Locking his target, he pressed on the trigger.A loud swooshing sound followed by a stream of red light issued from the lethal, fired AGM now bent on engaging its target .Bill and Ross waited with bated breath

    It hit home with a loud explosion.Cheering, Bill started for the skies, rejoining his comrades in battle.

    When the threat was finally removed, Bill landed and reported to Ground Control and the Commander that he had shot down 4 planes .Without a moment, he dashed straight into his bunk and fell asleep almost as soon as his head touched the pillow.
