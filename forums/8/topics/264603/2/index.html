    A lot of mention about Audio-Technica and Sennheiser here..so let me give you guys and TS my opinion on these 2 brands cuz I both got use before..I currently using Sennheiser PX-200 priced S$115.90..last time was a Audio-Technica ATH-EM5 aluminium clip-ons priced S$59.90..

    Audio-Technica: It is relatively cheap and sound quality is actually not bad..but it doesn't have much bass..so if you are a bass freak, I would recommend steering away from Audio-Technica (AT)..what AT provides is a very good mid-range..and mid-range is important because that's the spectrum where the sound of most instruments and human voice reside in..Not much treble though..AT very easy to get..Popular is one place..HMV also got some selections of AT headphones..

    Sennheiser: Sennheiser is really quite good..better than my old ATH-EM5 in terms of bass and treble..it also has a good mid-range..but not as good as my ATH-EM5..that's a trade off for better treble and bass..You won't regret it if you buy Sennheiser because they offer really good sound quality at not VERY expensive prices..

    P.S.: If you really sound freak and want amazing sound quality, there's a lot of other brands out there..there's Shure, Grado, Crossroads..blah blah blah..Bose is a little overrated..personally went to test out their new inner-ear headphones..not VERY great..but overall I would say it's good..

    To TS..if you budget is only 50 bucks and you are no bass freak, get AT..worth it..but please take good care of it..AT stuff quite fragile..my ATH-EM5 aluminium clip-ons already spoilt twice..had to get it to their head office to geddit fixed..
