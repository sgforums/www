    Actually its the guy's fault. Even though she initially expressed to him she didn't need him giving her lifts from work, the fact that he PROCEEDED to do so meant 3 things.

The question is, why did he not feel the need to send her back on the day he wanted to go out with friends when he oblivously saw the need to do so every other day?

    3. The fact that he fetched her home everyday created a trend, a routine she could count on. This is prima facie of a right that he himself gave her. By withdrawing that right, not for an emergency but for drinking with friends, he was taking away what was hers through habit. Classical Pavlovian behaviour.
