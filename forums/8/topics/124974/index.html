    This particular joke won an award for the best joke
    in a competition organized in Britain.

    A MBA and an Engineer go on a camping trip, set
    up their tent, and fell asleep.

    Some hours later, the Engineer wakes his MBA
    friend. " Look up at the sky and tell me what you
    see." The MBA replies, "I see millions of stars."
    The Engineer asks "What does that tell you?"

    The MBA ponders for a minute : "Astronomically
    speaking, it tells me that there are millions of
    galaxies and potentially billions of planets.
    Astrologically, it tells me that Saturn is in Leo.
    Time wise, it appears to be approximately a quarter
    past three. Theologically, it's evident the Lord is all-
    powerful and we are small and insignificant.
    Meteorologically, it seems we will have a beautiful
    day tomorrow. What does it tell you?"

    The Engineer friend is silent for a moment, then
    speaks. "Practically...Someone has stolen our
    tent".
