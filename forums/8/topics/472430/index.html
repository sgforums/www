  • Summer hill
    24 Jul 13, 18:31

    1000 per month means stale biscuits everyday, public transport everyday.

    Let me do calculation.

    Lowest possible rent: $600/month

    You have $400 left to spend.

    An average 1 way bus trip cost $2.50. You stilll need to take the bus at least twice a day. 5x30, you have $150 gone.

    You are left with $250.

    Not forgetting school items [like pens] which needs money out of your pocket, average at around $30/month.

    You are left with $220.

    Most landlords won't let people who rent their rooms cook. You need to eat out. If you spend $2 on a meal, 3 meals a day. $6/day. 6x30, adding up to $180.

    Remember, you cannot even spend a few cents more on each meal, or for example [$2.50x3]x30= $225. You will go over the budget.

    You are left with $40. 

    What are you going to do with that $40? When a foriegner falls sick in Singapore, a trip to the doctor can rake up to $50, a specialist $80+++
