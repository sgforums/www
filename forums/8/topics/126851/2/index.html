    If your company deducts 20% of your pay to CPF, then you shld be receiving a FORM IR8A from your employer.
    You have to check if your company is auto-inclusion listed company. If so, they will file on your behalf as you juz need to log into the web and check to make sure all the details are correct. All your allowances, bonuses, OT pay are included inside the FORM IR8A.
    If not, you have to file thru internet with the FORM IR8A. All the details are enclosed on that form, you juz have to key in manually.

    Unless you have other sources of income, or claiming relief, you have to let them know by clicking on the respective boxes.

    Hope that helps.
