    Originally posted by LoVeMeLoVeMenOt:
    so wat is a centrio pentium M huh
    A centrino is just a brand name used by intel to promote their line of laptops. It consists of a pentium m processor(which is a improved version of the current mobile pentium processors, enhances battery life) and a wireless lan(802.11b). With the combination of these two things, it forms what intel promotes as "Centrino".

    Effectively, there's not much difference in battery life between a centrino and a non-centrino laptop. (what i found out)
