    1st Section
    3.0mol

    2nd Section
    1.0mol
    600K

    PV = nrt
    For 1st section
    3.0 x molar gas constant x 400K -> x joules
    For 2nd section
    1.0 x molar gas constant x 600K -> y joules

    x + y = Total Energy.
    So, Total Energy = nrt again.

    (x+y) = (3.0+1.0) x molar gas constant x temperature.
    New Temperature = (x+y) / ((3.0+1.0)xmolar gas constant))
