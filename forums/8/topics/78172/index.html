    Using Bittorrent is simple, just click on the torrent link that contains the file you want to download, choose where you want to save the file to and after that a download dialouge should appear. All you have to do is wait.

    Bittorrent works like this, the more you upload, the faster your download speed. But of course, the speed will also depend on how many people are downloading the file and how many people are seeding the file. The more people downloading and seeding the file, the faster your speed. Seeding means people who have already completed their download but comes back to help distribute. Since Bittorrent downloads in parts, which means you may download the end of the file first, the middle first or even the front, it depends on the users at that time, you'll need Seeds to complete you download, without Seeds the most you can download to is 99 % or less. Oh ya and if there are no people downloading it and no Seeds, you can forget about downloading anything.
