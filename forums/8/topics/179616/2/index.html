  • BillyBong
    26 Feb 06, 08:16
    Pick up a book on NZ by Let's Go, Lonely Planet or Eyewitness: Top Ten.

    They give you a breakdown of the places to go, their various costs involved, and food to eat. Eyewitness guides give you an excellent historical breakdown of each place you visit, saving you the trouble of a tour guide's explanation.

    For good pictures, try National Geographic. Lonely Planet has some decent colour pictures as well. There are also budget travels under some books that offer you camping on a shoestring, hiking or cycling tours. NZ is perfect for all of them.

    The coastline of North Island is fantastic, especially from 90-mile beach around Cape Reinga and beyond. But it is highly recommended to start in the South.
