    a) Longer Staple
    Somethings are just made that way. Smaller staple are made for portability.You can have longer staple, but it may not reduce the chances that you will not run into an empty one when you need it most urgently, because ultimately it still needs to get refilled, and as long as the person who refills it never does the job, you still end up with the same problem.

    Besides the bigger the staple the heavier it is... and is precisely what the Americans have. Big, huge staples which you can't carry around freely to the photocopier without finding a space to put it. While people are admiring why I have all the essential stationery in my pencil case, it's a good thing, to me, to have miniturization in our society.

    b) Waterproof HP
    I like the idea, but it's not for swimming, and more for protection against rain, and accidental drop into pools of water. It's already available in the market, as Fireice mentioned, and I have seen it too. However, I don't see the point of being stuck with a phone call when you're in the middle of the pool or when you're bathing. You would be in the middle grasping for air, and taking a call. Maybe you are a busy person who needs to get hold of every call.

    c) Bicycle Stop
    I believe they had piloted a program in Bukit Batok several years ago. I'm not sure with the progress at the moment, and the evaluation, maybe you may want to check it out with the GRC. =)
