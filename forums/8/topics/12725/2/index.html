    Hmm, went to Bedok Funland the other day. Nobody was playing VF4 at all.

    But everyone was trying out KOF 2001. Wow, there seems to be five or six new characters, and a whole lot of old ones being brought back.

    One thing I like about KOF, each new installment is usually better than the old one. All the characters are more well-balanced (in KOF 97, Iori was way way way too powerful).

    As usual, my two favourites, Ralf and Clark, are consistently useful. I have rather slow fingers and I can't pull off those ultra-fast combos, so I like to use them. Besides, one heavy punch from Ralf or Clark is equal to a three or four hit combo from other flashier characters.

    Sometimes when I get challenged, usually my opponent will act very seh after pulling off a 7-hit combo with special. But then I just punch a few times, slam a bit here and there, and I do much more damage by pressing one button occasionally than all that fancy finger slapping. I know Kyo and Iori and some other characters have impressive combos, but the amount of damage they do seems to have been reduced substantially.

    And oh yeah, Goro Daimon is back, HA HA HA!!!
