    there's lot of effects u can do.

    washing type:
    enzyme wash/stone wash(make washing effect, on the jeans)
    bleaching(make the color light.)
    tinting(tint yellow,brown,orange tone will make the jeans look old, got "rusty look", if just slightly yellowish make it look abit old, like put very long. also can tint red, green, but will not be very natural.)

    non-washing type:
    Sand blast(where jeans got one patch lighter color on the thighs)
    whisker(the horizontal lines beside ur zip)
    abrasion(make the bottom and pocket mouth a bit torn.)
    cut hole(got a hole with the white horizontal cotton strings across).
    wrinkle fixing(make part of jeans got wrinkle effect.

    our factory in china does all the things i mentioned, except garment dye.
    the factory in sg run by my dad and his partner do only garment dye and washing only. Cos main cost of sand-blast, whisker, abrasion etc are labour cost, which is high in sg, cheap in china. For washing and garment dye, the cost are mainly steam, chemical, water, electricity. which is also cheaper in china, but the differece not as big as labour cost.

    u can actually DIY. just use Sand paper, dun buy the type which is very rough.
    that's how we do whisker.
    for sand blasting, u can use sand paper to do it also, similar effect but not as natural as sand-blast.
