    Update: New prize added for every submission and deadline extended! Read on to find out more. Users can submit as many entries as they want!

     

    Comboutique Asia (http://www.comboutique.asia) in partnership with SGForums is organizing the very first 'Design an SGForums T-shirt' online competition. So crank those creative juices and show your support by helping design some funky tees! Here are the details.

    Prizes
    The top winning entries selected will:

    1. Be sold in SGForums' merchandise shop (http://shop.sgforums.com)

    2. 1 top prize of S$100 to spend to buy your own personalized swag like t-shirts, mugs etc from Comboutique Asia or SGForums' own shop!

    3. 4 runner-up prizes of S$50 to spend to buy your own personalized swag like t-shirts, mugs etc from Comboutique Asia or SGForums' own shop!

    PLUS...

    *NEW* 4. Every entry gets a 6 months SGForums Premier Membership for free! Note: To avoid "CMI" (rubbish) entries just for the Premier Membership, SGForums reserves all rights to disqualify such entries and not award them with the Premier Membership. 

     

    How to participate

    Step 1: Go to http://shop.sgforums.com

    Step 2: Click on the 'Design your own t-shirt' tab

     Step 3: Pick a color of t-shirt and go crazy designing the most awesome SGForums t-shirt you can think of. There are no design rules i.e. you don't have to use certain colors, logos, pictures, text etc. It's free play!

    Step 4: When you're done, click on 'email to a friend'

    Step 5: Enter your Name, a valid email address in the message box, enter your SGForums username. *IMPORTANT* In the 'your friends' box, enter the email address contest@comboutique.asia and then hit 'Send'.

     Rules

    1. The closing date for the contest is 31 July 2009. Update: Contest has been extended to 30 Aug 2009.

    2. Selected entries maybe put up for voting to determine the final winners.

    3. All entrants acknowledge that their submissions and all intellectual property associated with them maybe used by SGForums or Comboutique Asia for merchandising sales in the future.

     

    That's it! Start sending those designs in!

    The Comboutique Asia team.
