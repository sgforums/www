    It's perfectly alright for u to buy a dog. But ask yourself this: Are u willing to be a commited pet owner? Keeping a pet is a BIG RESPONSIBILITY. Be it a cat, dog or hamster..its still a LIFE.

    I must confess, i wasn't responisble enough. I used to bring him down for walks daily in my old house. But when I shifted house, there's no longer places for him to run about outdoors (I stay in a busy part of S'pore, heavy traffic ) so i stopped taking him for walks altogether. As a resut, my dog grew fat n unhealthy from lazing around all day.

    Besides, u should take your pet for a checkup at the vet's every half a year. It's to ensure that ur dog is fit n healthy. I didn't do that, and I deeply regretted that now.

    Do not assume ur dog is healthy just because he LOOKS healthy. I've learnt that lesson the PAINFUL way.

    Although I cannot erase the guilt of neglecting my pet dog, I've learnt something from my loss.

    BE RESPONSIBLE...

    OR DO NOT KEEP A PET!
