    Hi, if it is possible, try to get them sheltered. The kittens are very dependable on their mother now, if you euthanized their mom, the kittens will probably follows as well.

    Let nature take its course, don't interfere by killing the mum, we won't know whether the mum will die or not or just feeling signs of weakness. But what you could do to help is to give them a place where they can rest, shielded from hot sun or rain and thus allowing the mum to recuperate.

    Try feeding her milk instead, doubt she has the strength to chew or eat the fish. Start with something light and hope she can recover.
