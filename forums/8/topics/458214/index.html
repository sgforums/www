    (i)  Processing Fees for First Time Application / Subsequent Renewals

    The passport fee is:
    • S$80 for applications made at the Counter
    • S$70 (after a S$10 rebate) for applications submitted online or by Post/Deposit Box
    (ii) 

    Penalty Fees for Loss of / Damaged Singapore Passport

    If your current passport is lost or damaged, you are required to pay, in addition to the passport fee, the following non-refundable fee:

    • S$50 for the first replacement 
    • S$100 for subsequent replacements  
    (iii) 

    Payment Mode

    Payment may be made via the following modes:

    • Visa / MasterCard Credit Cards or Visa /MasterCard Debit Cards by completing the relevant portion in your application form. 
    • Applicants who apply by deposit box can also pay by NETS/CashCard using the NETS/CashCard machines available at Levels 1 and 2 of ICA Building

    When you want to renew or apply new passport online, you must have a credit card to pay. However, when you apply personally at ICA headquarter at Lavender Road, you can pay through nets during application. However, when you don't have enough cash to pay during application, you can pay cash or nets when you collect your new passport at the customer service counter. You can also apply or renew your passport at any Post Office but you must pay in advance either in cash, nets or by credit cards. I'm staying in JB for the last seven years now. I've renewed my passport and applied new passports many times. Usually, I go to the ICA Headquarter to do those tasks because it is faster than when I go through the Post Office. And I don't have any credit card for payment for online passport application or renewal.
