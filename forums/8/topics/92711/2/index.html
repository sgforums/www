    Originally posted by fett:
    hi, can send me your info concerning your trip to taiwan? always thinking of going back there since my ns days.
    Get a lonely planet guide. A lot of info can be found there. If you can speak mandrain and read chinese back packing in Taiwan is not a problem. The people are friendly and will gladly assist you if you need any help.

    The best part of my trip is in Hualian Taroko Gorge. Thats something you shd never miss if you go to Taiwan. Basically I made a round Island trip starting from Taipei, Hualian, Kenting, Gaoxiong, Taizhong and back to Taipei again.

    The east coast is rugged and scenic. In the west coast you can try farm visits. Dont miss the night market in Taipei or Gaoxiong. If you are more adventurous you can try the fermented toufu, which you can smell miles away.

    I missed Alisan and Sun moon lake because of time constraint. You may want to consider to go Hualian, Taroko gorge and cut across the central mountains to Alisan and Taizhong.

    The lao ban niang at the local motels or lu she are good source of information. Ask them for local attraction and places to visit. Get a travel companion to share cost and company. Travel light. What you can in SG you can get there as well.

    For further info, you can visit the Taiwan representive office in Singapore.
