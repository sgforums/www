    Ladies' Street @ Mongkok is bigger than the other one @ Temple Street . If you wanted to buy branded stuffs , Causeway Bay lots of shopping centres .

    Besides Lan Kwai Fong , there's also pubs at one of the backstreets somewhere near Carnavan Rd (Tshim Sha Tsui area) .

    Also must try the steamboat @ Carnavan Rd , one is named Tao Xiang (cheaper & more varieties) , the other is You Gu Qi -- english direct tranalation means 'Got Backbone' -- this one is more like Hkg secret society teahouse type , a bit rowdy but the soup base there is fantastic .

    The Peak and Celebrity walkway of Fame is a must go . Would prefer Ocean Park rather than Disneyland (regretted going there).

    Must go to Xu Liu Shan to try their different kinds of desserts , not to forget other milk desserts @ Yishun Milk Company . Also you must try the roasted goose in hkg .

    Drinks there @ roadside stalls are cheap , pubs still consider a bit cheaper than sgp pubs .

    Public transportation there is very convenient & user friendly and you probably do not need to take any cabs at all . If you have spare time , you might wanna go to Shenzhen (a very nice place to go also) and stay for a night or two .

    Overall , Hongkong is a nice place to visit .

    Staunton street has some nice western restaurants. Jesper is what i would recommend. A little pricey. but food is good.

    Lan kwai fong is well known to everyone for its pubs and clubs.

    Causeway bay is like orchard road. Shopping for branded stuff. Kwoloon and jordan are the places for the "not branded" stuff.

    26 Aug 06, 08:25
    Victoria Harbour - Enjoy the night view of Hong Kong
    Mongkok's Ladies Street - Plentiful of shopping and snacks
    Tsim Shai Sui's Temple Street - Plentiful of pasar malam stalls but prices a bit steep, need to bargain
    Tsim Shai Sui's Chungking Building - Lots of concept shops and some have unique and exclusive stuff
    The Peak - Enjoy the Peak Tram up to the top and visit at night, can see very nice and cool night view of Hong Kong
    Madam Trussard Museum - Wax figures of several celebrities
    Celebrity's Walk of Fame - See and touch the Stars on the ground and the bronze figure of Bruce Lee
    Lam Guai Fong's SOHO - Superior clubbing experience
    Lantau 360 - The cable car ride from the MTR station and catch a high view of the Giant Buddha
    Lantau Island's Po Lam Temple - Enjoy the delicious vegeterian spread that makes you come back for more
    Lantau Island's Giant Buddha - Walk up the steps to the foot of the Giant Buddha, pray your respects and get blessings
    Ocean Park - Watch the famous dolphin performance and have fun inside the theme park, remember the Super Roller Coaster
    Disneyland - Experience the Disney magic in a small scale and grab your favourite Disney character's souvenirs

    Remember the roasted pigeon, tim sum, desserts and Lao Po biscuits from Yong Loong...
