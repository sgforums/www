    once the laser shoot finish, they immediately dripped in alot of eye drops, then later use some kinda sponge thingy to wipe away the excess on my eye (or maybe is to clean off the chaoto parts ), then later use needle to push the membrane back on again, more eyedrops again, then the sponge thingy come and rub against my eye that time. u know ah the instant b4 the membrane fell back onto my eye, i was able to see all the veins and nerves on it leh
