    Anyone read the papers these few days? Regarding our local theater and arts awards?

    Quite some of the male artists went in skirts or dresses or skirt-like garments. Is this a hint of the future of male fashion to come?

    Personally, for some time now, I have felt strongly that male fashion would evolve towards the trend of skirts, dresses and skirt-like garments. A while back I talked about several top Italian fashion houses planning to launch a line of men's skirts.

    Looking back at the history of men's fashion, as late as the early 20th centuary, men were still wearing long flowing garments, and boys were still wearing petticoats and pinafores. For men's fashion to evolve towards pants, then slowly back to skirt or dress-like garments, is actually a natural thing to happen. I would theorise that any such garments for men would have a bolder, simpler and more chiseled cutting and design, to make it different from the feminine kinds of such garments.

    Personally, I still think trousers and pants would be more convenient to wear, as we can have lots of pockets(esp for ppl like me who has no wallets and has a lot of junk) and also it is easier to do stuff like ride bikes n climb walls n stuff.
    But, for some high brow event that requires classy dressing, if skirts and dresses were to become the de rigour for men ONCE AGAIN, I certain wouldn't mind too much. after all, masculinity is much more than just about the clothes I wear.
