    Two kids fall off ride at theme park, suffer serious injuries
    By Rita Zahara, Channel NewsAsia

    Two girls fell while they were on an indoor roller-coaster ride at the Escape Theme Park at Pasir Ris on Friday.

    A 9-year-old girl and her 11-year-old sister had gone to the theme park with their mother when the accident happened at about 11.30am.

    Channel NewsAsia understands the girls were thrown off the ride about 3 metres above the ground.

    The two girls suffered serious back injuries and are in critical condition.

    They were sent to Changi General Hospital.

    The younger of the two was later sent to KK Women's and Children's Hospital.

    It is believed the cause of the accident could be due to a faulty safety bar.

    Police are investigating. - CNA/ir
