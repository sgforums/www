    This is something that happened to me today. Just wondering what fellow Sgforumers will do in light of a similar situation...

    You're waiting for a taxi on a deserted road. After 15 minutes have passed, you finally call for a cab. He sounds very persuasive and tells you of the time where similar situations have occured. The driver does not arrive till 15 minutes later, and in the meantime 2 empty cabs pass by.

    If you've already given your word to the first cab driver that you will not cancel his booking... Will you flag one of the empty cabs that pass by and cancel it anyway? Booking fees are an additional $4.

    Just thought it would be interesting to see how different people reason.
