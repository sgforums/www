Kung fu star Jet Li escapes killer tsunami in Maldives

  • Lim Li Hao Matthew
    29 Dec 04, 12:38
    Chinese action star Jet Li escaped with minor injuries when he was caught up in the weekend disaster that saw tidal waves swamping the hotel where he was staying in the Maldives, a report said.

    Li was on holiday with his wife, four-year-old daughter and friends at the Indian Ocean tourist paradise when the disaster struck Sunday, the Singtao Daily reported. At least 52 people were killed on the islands.

    Li, 41, who starred in films including "Shaolin Temple," Hollywood action flick "Lethal Weapon 4," "Romeo Must Die" and most recently in Chinese director Zhang Yimou's "Hero", suffered minor injuries to his leg, the report said.

    Li grabbed his four-year-old daughter when their hotel room started to flood and escaped, the newspaper said, citing a spokeswoman from his management company, China Star.

    Li, who could not be reached immediately, contacted his family and friends Monday and confirmed they were safe. They planned to fly back to Hong Kong as early as possible, said the spokeswoman.
