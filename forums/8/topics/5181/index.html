    tattoos....I love them but the normal barb wire and dragon things etc...are really everywhere...a bit boring...beutiful, but still ho humm.

    The whole point of getting a tattoo isn't to be with any "in" crowd. It isn't to gett it to follow everyone else. It is a sign of individuality or a sign of an indivual group...When you see people with a certain tattoo and go and get it yourself, then someone else does the same...the tattoo loses it's reason for being there in the first place.

    It's an individual taste...used to express something...something about yourself, about your group, or about your beliefs.

    That's why I'm sure I'm the only guy in Singapore with a big ass star tattoo on his arm. I have only seen one guy with a star on his arm and it's so small you can barely tell it's a tattoo...more like a mole.

    It says something about what I aspire to and my views on life man. AS an artist...and because I'm an artist, I have an unprecedented amount of creative decision making ability that noone else can really fault...
    ha ha...the perks of being an artist: Do anything crazy and noone can say anything...!!!:-)

    If you get a tattoo, make it intentional. Dun let it be half fucked...or else it will seem like you did it half fucked...and do something that says something.

    Is my star cool?...To me, yes because I know the reasoning beind it...but to other people it's a stupid thing to do (Why a star..Why not this or why not that)...and I always unintentionally scare old folks in the lift...can't help it...
