    24 Jul 07, 04:15
    u can try pasir ris park, there got 2 types of fishing pond

    1st type : super hungry fish (they have nothing to eat, so ur dad can have the fish biting the bait in no time BUT the fishes don't have any strength to fight, thus if you dad is a pro in this area, u might want to consider trying out the compeitive pool which is the 2nd type

    2nd tpe: being feed well, fish don't bite the bait easily and they are considered average when it comes to fighting to reel the fishes in, thus if you want, try taking your dad there, it should be easy to find, thus ur dad should have no problem going there, and it's safe for you as well.

    rods can be rent from there, but advice is to bring your own..

    if you're new there, and not used to sun, u might sweat a lot, so do take note to wear those clothes which are not tight fit, no high heels...no short or mini-skirts..coz u will be sitting on either the floor or the bench..so u don't want to expose ur undergarment to the other people seated opposite you..

    btw, if u are first timer: do get prepared to be bitten by mosquitoes if you both go to natural places like parks etc, but the only exception will be Bedok Jetty as it's so windy there that mosquitoes can't buzz there unless in the late night to midnight time..

    hope this my comments helps..
