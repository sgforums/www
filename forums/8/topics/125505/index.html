    just graduated from sp last semester just wanna give my insights..

    ure first ever semester...more often than not....

    1.You are overcome by suakooness...
    2.Unsurprising that u might get lost in ure first week...
    Year 1
    Poly life is pretty relaxed...throughout my 3.5 years in it....teaching staff teaches/treats u like adults...the best advice a lecturer gave me during this time was...'Poly is unlike anything u ever experienced b4...in primary school, ure parents look after ure studies....in sec school, teachers look after ure studies....over here, u will learn that onli u are responsible for ure diploma...u either slack and waste ure time...or u work for good results'...Anywaes..classes were ok....first few weeks...attend everything...sooner or later skip here and there...and i think if i remembered correctly...it was the first and last time i played 3 hrs of carrom straight(exhausting leh).....get a cca if u want...if best...enter dragonboat for all u guys....training is tough...but u wont regret it....i played rugby throughout poly and was a member of the gym and fitness club(unofficial cos i didnt want to get tied down with watever meeting and pay their membership...)gerls basically enter anything u want....got a whole load of CCAs there....

    Year 2

    guess wat...voluntarily retained myself to pursue some other things on that year(failed maths...but when it was my last semester...i regret alot...cos my frens all grad and left me alone....but it was bearable)Year 2 is when u prolly pro in alot of stuffs...u should by now noe wat classes can skip...and noe the importance of tutorials and practicals(never skip them...lectures are ok...but these 2 are super important.)...It doesnt hurt to make frens with the right group of ppl...namely...

    1.Teaching Staff
    2.If can..the module coordinators..
    3.lab technicians if ure course has any practicals...

    and for all u kiddies out there....attachment is a bitch...hopefully u get somethin work/course related and ure superiors are nice...if u never work b4...prepare for a culture shock..my first attachment became an IT Exec trainee(repair guy..fix dumb idiots computer problems) in NTUC CLUB....2nd attachment made 'coffee'(Basically arh) at Singapore Tech(aerospace)(WTF)

    Year 3

    U should now have the mentality of 'Big fry' now and should be damn matured although i seen some still acting like freshmen...u should now be doing ure Final Year Project...My advice for all u kiddies......keep group members to a minimum and do it with someone u trust(too many heads not good in my opinion)....I've seen groups self-destruct midway through the project(some even when starting it) due to selfishness of group members....immaturity...ego...character clash...pure laziness(the 'Aiyah slack arh...can do later'Attitude')

    watever it is....ure initial planning stage of ure FYP should be over by ure 1st term...so u got time to study for ure exams.....after the 1st term...u should be able to do the 'manual' work on ure fyp and type reports...in my opinion...FYPs were a nuisance if not done with quickly...

    I wont lie to all u kiddies....polylife is easy if u play ure cards right....slacking is fine by me....but importantly....DO NOT FORGET THE FOLLOWING:

    1.Do ure damn F-ing tutorials...
    2.Be thick skinned abit...dunno u better f-ing ask....forget ure ego
    3.Dont slack for 3/4 of the semester and mug for 1/4 semester....really not worth the sleepless nights
    4.Exams are easy...provided u practice..practice...and practice...(my method was write watever i am supposed to remember again and again and again..greenpeace will prolly hunt me down for wasting so much paper)...
    5.For all u maths weaklings...due to the fact that they provide formula books...please please practice...maths is easy...really...no joke....

    i wonder if i forgotten anything........will update later...
