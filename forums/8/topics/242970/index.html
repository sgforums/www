    +1 is actually to increase post count on those stupid threads or threads which are going to be banned. Remember, you can only +1 in lame/stupid threads.

    The only place you can +1 the most is Bar forum. Chit chat forum and Aunt agony and Speaker's Corner is NOT a Place to +1.

    Example: When other start to +1, maybe you can try to follow +1.

    Owned/Pwned is actually used when something or a plan Back fired or defeated.

    Example: I pour petrol in his car wanted to burn him, but instead I am pouring on my car.

    Plan back fired = Owned/pwned.

    Flaming = When both parties have different view on a topic and gets heated up. Both parties are swearing each others.

    NPNT = No Picture No Talk
    GPGT = Got Picture Got Talk

    You need supporting details like a picture to prove what you are trying to say.

    Example: There is a c0ckroach on my rice!
    Reply : NPNT

    Means: you need picture to let people believe what you say.

    TS = ThreadStarter = Person who start a topic.

    Feel free to ask more..
