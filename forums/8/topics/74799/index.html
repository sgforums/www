    The man found dead in the boot of a red Mercedes in Boon Keng Road on Friday at Block 6A carpark was a scrap car dealer.
    Family members say 56-year-old Bock Tuan Thong could have been a victim of a robbery as he usually carried large sums of cash and wore a $15,000 Rolex watch.

    The victim's wife, Tan Ai Wah, could not contain herself when she claimed his body on Saturday morning.

    She was the last family member to see him alive after her husband had dropped her off at her factory in Ubi at about 11.15am on Friday in the red Mercedes borrowed from his brother.

    Madam Tan said a family friend who had phoned her husband later overheard him in a midst of a quarrel.

    She said: "He said Bock was distracted, and his tone sounded as if he had just scolded someone."

    Madam Tan said her husband is a friendly and easy-going person, who would not lose his temper easily.

    "I cannot understand his death," said a family member.
