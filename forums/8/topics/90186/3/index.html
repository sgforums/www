    It'd have been better if they had adapted the excellent storylines from the BlackHorse comics or from the AVP novels, instead of coming up with a new script.....

    And I think it would have been cooler if they had made an animated movie of AVP rather than a live-action one. If Fox studios had handed the franchise to those Japanese animators who worked on gritty sci fi anime classics such as "Ghost in a Shell" or "Jin Roh", I think the results would have been great.

    An animated movie would have allowed for far greater scope (at lower budget) for which the scriptwriter can set the movie in (i.e. can set the whole thing in a different planet, with lush settings, can even have longer and more visually exciting fight-sequences without blowing the budget).....
