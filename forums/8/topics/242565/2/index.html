    Why not take a trip down to Robinsons or CK Tangs and take a look at their shoes.

    I am assuming that thread starter is working in a white collar job.

    There are 2 shoe (pun) of thoughts. The european styling which is slightly tappered than the american ( flat front shoes)

    Personally i prefer a european cutting as its less common and it looks more formal. taste may differ.

    Down to the shoe brands.Yes you can get your Bata, Pedro,Primevera shoes.Robinsons & Tangs also offer quite a gd selection of affordable shoes like Hush Puppies, Passporte and Renoma or Braun Buffel.

    i own 2 pair of shoes, 01x pair of Braun Buffel , 01x pair of Mario Fagli and 01x pair from Bruno Magli. yes they are pretty expensive, ard $280 , $200and $400. but they are going gd after 2 years. no hard core leather wear and tear.

    Of course you have to do your part, i apply shoe cream and carbuna wax on a weekly basis, and its been serving me well.

    Lastly, the sound of a pure leather sole echoing the corridor is rather empowering =)
