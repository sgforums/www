    Hate and love.... extreme feelings they are.

    They are similiar yet different. Let me explain, it is the source or shall I say motivation that is the same, but the effects that are different well, at least most of the time.

    Love originates from you, it's motivation is an object or a person of desire. When you are in love, your actions are guided and driven by emotions, it does not really make sense and sometimes even defy logic.

    Hate originates from you too, it's motivation is an object or a person of detest. When you are in hate, your actions are guided and driven by emotions again, it does not really make sense and sometimes even defy logic too.

    The above are similiarities. But what are the difference? The effect of love and hate are on two different specturms. Love makes you care, makes you sensitive, and overall good feeling is infuse in you, you long to see the person you love, you are happy just to see him/her, it brightens up your day. Hate makes you disregard the person, makes you irritated by him, and overall feeling of disgust is infuse in you, you dun want to see the person you hate, you are irritated just to see him/her, it darkens your day.

    But of course there are effects that are the same too. Both love and hate could motivate, it could motivate a person to perform in extreme circumstances, it could drive the person to prove himself, and these are the positive effects of both love and hate. It could also cause a person to lose hope, cause him/her to sacrifices/kill himself/herself because of that person. Negative aspects of it's effect are the above.

    Ultimately they are both emotions, and it is a matter of perspective, of how you can use them to your advantage.
