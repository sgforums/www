    Body parts found near Orchard MRT station

    POLICE said an off-duty cleaner made a grisly find near the Orchard MRT station just after lunch time on Friday - three red bags containing a woman's head and limbs, all stuffed into a blue-and-white sports bag.

    The head, with long hair, was in one bag; her legs and feet in another and her arms and hands in a third bag. no information about the torso is available.

    Police are at the scene, which has been cordoned off. The bag was stashed next to a flower bed along the perimeter of the park just next to the Orchard MRT station.

    A police spokesman said they received a phone call about the grisly find after noon on Friday but could not provide further information.
