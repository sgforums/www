    My friend is a coporate RM with one of the overseas bank. Not mentioning which one, but of those that has a strong presence here as well.

    They have a basic salary, ard $2500 + handphone allowance + taxi vouchers + entertainment expense.

    They need to rake up sales according to what their managers set for them ( if they are rookie, high performer or ppl who need to work on their sales etc) usually it will be ard $30,000 to $80,000 per mth.

    Usually its achieveable as they have a existing set of portfolio for them to work on. So its kinda of selling new products and services to the clients.

    Payouts are by quaterly basis. If you been a consistent worker and smooth talker, a mthly average of ard $5000 to $8000(taking into account the quaterly payout) is usually possible before CPF.

    The rewards are high no doubt, but so are the pressure to make u perform. 3 mths is the norm the grace period they will allow anyone not performing to pull up their socks.Usually once your out, you out of the entire banking business.

    A diploma at least, relevant studies in BnF is gd, but quick wits and being street wise will get you further
