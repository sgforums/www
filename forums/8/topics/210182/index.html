    Flag

    Main article: Flag of Sicily

    The regional flag of Sicily, recognized since January 2000, is also the historical one of the island since 1282. It is divided diagonally yellow over red, with the trinacria symbol in the center. "Trinacria" literally means "three legs" and is representative of the three points of the island. The head shown on the Sicilian trinacria is the face of Medusa. However, before the Roman Empire, the head was the sun. The trinacria symbol is used also by the Isle of Man.

    source: Wikipedia
