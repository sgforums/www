    Have lived my wholelife in these areas..They have changed a bit compared to 10 years ago. But it has a rich history and culture

    1)Hougang ave 9 Hougang mall ,Hougang green, Hougang point has been around since 90s for HDB dwellers.Previously they were not so densely populated and not well known too..Witht he start of NEL line, they got more exposure..This area is near to Kovan. Hougang MRT is found here

    2)Kovan has been revamped with the new Heartland Mall & Kovan MRT station. Impressive compared to before when the area looked like a ghostown in the 90s. The older one was Kovan centre since the mid 90s. Can find many churches and small private schools here.Some of these schools u may never heard before like Our Lady of Lourdes, Parry and Serangoon Sec. They are tucked inside a huge maze of private property probably the biggest concentration in Singapore. Most of these schools may be privately owned and run or very old and been around since pre independence Singapore

    3)Serangoon Garden area has been revamped as well...This huge private property area is part of Kovan one of my fave and one of the oldest well known of all these areas...it has Chomp Chomp and so many things to do here...Rich people stay in this area..most o.It has a rich social air around it....Life here is good because it had a relaxed cosy feel similar to other countries HDB dwellers from the nearby Hougang and Serangoon frequent this area as well.

    4) Serangoon North well known for its residential pet shops. Other than that there isnt much to do here. Many old schools here as well. Like Serangoon and Nanyang JC. Mostly for HDB dwellers
