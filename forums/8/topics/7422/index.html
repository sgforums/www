    A few months ago there was substantial coverage in the press about the advantages of knowing CPR. Free lessons were held at various locations. I'd like to take a simple survey among forum members, in the form of the following questions.

    Are you proficient in CPR?

    Where and how long ago did you learn it? Have you taken any refresher courses?

    Have you ever had the opportunity to perform CPR on an actual human being?

    Do you think you could perform the life-saving procedure correctly and confidently, if an emergency situation involving your loved ones occurs tomorrow?

    Would you volunteer to perform CPR on a stranger, if his/her life is at stake?

    Are you aware of the various dangers associated with the act of CPR?
