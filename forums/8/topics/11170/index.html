    16 Feb 01, 09:28
    Bangkok(Thailand):

    A nice place to shop. Local goods are cheaper than what we can get in Singapore. Roadside stalls have interesting and exotic local dishes, but ain't for the weak-stomached. Restaurants' food prices are very cheap. The World Trade Centre there is much bigger than what we have in Singapore. The BRS(Bangkok Rail Systems, if I remember correctly) is worth a ride. Quite different from Singapore's own MRT. Leather goods are cheap too and you might wanna check those items with exotic skins out. Night activities are abundant. U can go and watch a Ah Gua Show or Tiger Show if u want and can take it.

    Tioman(Malaysia):

    Food is similar to Singapore Zhi Char, unless u wanna have some western food, but sure taste better than a lot of places I have tried on local grounds. Snorkelling near the Coral Islands is no longer that interesting as a lot of corals are dying due to pollution. The fishes however, are most interesting when it comes to feeding them.
    Not much of night recreation though.

    Been to quite a number of countries.

    Fave: HongKong, US.

    Average: Japan.

    Below Average: Bangkok, Malaysia, China.

    I think i know why so many people like HongKong. It's really like we are at home, in Singapore. There's the people rushing off to nowhere, the shopping arcades, the MRT, the high-rise flats, etc. Wouldn't mind going there again. Pretty girls all around.

    US, well, becoz of the friendliness i have received from the people there. I often thought most of them were racist when i was younger. Well, they were not. Disneyland was an experience i won't forget.

    Japan, was too boring for me. Walking around, freezing in the cold. Liked the sceneries and well...the girls too. =)

    Bangkok - too hot. too dusty. too many traffic jams. Girls weren't too bad. lol.

    Malaysia - well, you know.

    China - Hmmm, imagine staying in fujian, in a kampong house, where it's lights-out at 6pm. Yeah haha no amount of pretty girls would overcome the feeling of boredom...and well, there aint any pretty girls there at all.

    Best bet for a holiday - HongKong, US.
    P.S: Not honeymoon though.

    The ultimate place i want to go:
    Moon.

    Have anyone travel alone before? I did. One time I had a big fight with my parents and i just walked out. I ended up on a bus to thailand, it was lonely at first but later it got better. I made friends with a swedish backpacker and we were getting pissed drunk on the streets, i knew it's like a kid to walk out of home like that, but it was a good time to be out of touch with everything familiar, and sort my thoughts out. I met nice friendly people, nice friendly girls it's a rubbish littered country but it always keep me running back, not for the girls but the water and islands.

    The 2 most likable place for me will be Thailand and Australia, I enjoy being close to nature and driving in all these places. I get to see the culture, people and food. It's better than visiting something like a "tourist attraction" which is jam-packed with tourist rushing for rides.
    In Australia, i met up with the same swedish guy, rented a old caravan and we went driving. The whole point of the trip was about getting lost, that's what Curly(swed) told me, we drove miles and miles, when we get tired, we start camp and eat, get our heads down. The whole experience of the journey was about liking it and not fighting it. thats what i told curly, the land was so vacant, and as far as your eye could see.. i really thought maybe if we drive far enough, we get to Singapore. haha..

    I like to go back sometime again.. if anyone else had better places, please tell me
