As for real life situations .. just imagine someone fighting u as u do ur pattern. The patterns in taekwondo are actually conceived as a combat simulation. For example, ur high block in yellow belt pattern is for blocking a person wielding a dagger from stabbing ur head. green belt's knife hand block is for blocking a punch aimed at your mid section. bla bla bla.. if ur well versed in ur patterns when u fight you'd be subconciously using it in ur fight. thats the basic of all martial arts.. imprint the self defense techniques into your subconscious so that u use it without thinking. then when ur actually fighting u'll be on autopilot.. not much time to think during fighting.
