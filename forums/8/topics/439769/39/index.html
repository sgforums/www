    Just came back from CoCo Ichibanya @ 313 somerset.

    The curry was great. Taste better than many of the indian curry out there.


    The level of spiciness can choose Level 1 to 5. 
    Amount of rice can change.

    I had the Pork cutlet + cheese set meal (Choice of 3)= $18
    - pork cutlet +cheese
    - mini salad
    - coke
    - a scoop of ice cream (i think it's walls?)


    My gf had the Omelette Chicken Curry (choice of 2*)= $18
    - omelette chicken curry
    -a scoop of ice cream (i think it's walls?)
    -drink she choose tea (they give1 tea pot + milk cup)


    *drink is standard choose from menu,
    changeable between ice-cream or mini salad

     

    Review:
    Ambience is good because we go at non-peak hours. Seats are comfortable.

    Waiters/waiteress are slow in clearing dish (probably it's non-peak hours)

    Cleanliness = average. They provide you with disposable straws wrap in paper. So you need to tear the paper.

    Food serve time: +/- 7min (non-peak hours)

    Service staff: international crew, manager is pinoy 

    Chef: Japanese

    Damage: Price listed on Menu + 7% gst +/-

    The curry is not so atas till the extend of premium. The curry is only slightly above average when compare to other competitors. NTUC sells similar pre-made curry at much more cheaper price.

    Personal comment: You pay $18 for the curry at CoCo Ichibanya, where I can buy pre-made curry of similar quality at any NTUC for less than $5.

    Will go back to CoCo Ichibanya @ 313 Somerset during non-peak hours to chill. Good for people who doesn't have regular eating timing, I prefer such timing to eat because no need to squeeze.
