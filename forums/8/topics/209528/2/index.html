    This song which has a poignant touch to it, was composed in Budapest by Rezso Seress in the 1930's when Hungary was under the Nazi occupation (lyrics were later added by Laszlo Javor). It is rumored that this song was 'cursed' and had caused many people (esp ladies) to commit suicide while/after listening to it during that time. Much of that is urban legend, and certainly there were plenty of reasons to commit suicide in Eastern Europe in the '30s without using a song as scapegoat. There is also a fiction novel and nice german movie ("Ein Lied von Liebe und Tod") based loosely on this scenario.
