    7 Dec 06, 19:24
    Hi,

    I was from the batch that entered RJC using O level results (not IP).

    Something around 7-4=3 points should be sufficient (the cut off varies from year to year). Also, I noticed some misconceptions from earlier threads. Some threads mentioned subtracting 6 points from your raw L1r5 score. I believe the maximum number of points you can deduct is 4 points.

    I suggest you try to get into RJC by working to obtain at least 3 points (7-4) instead of banking on your outstanding cca. That's a possible option, but you must realise that RI and RGS have their own pool of CCA talents which will fill up the places in RJC, so using this route is not foolproof. Besides, assuming you appeal your way in using CCA, you would have to remain in that sport (you might want to try something different in JC).

    And about rjc students being elitist, our teachers dont point a finger at us and say, "Be sure to spew harsh words to others." Every school has it's own fair share of students who display undesirable behaviour. Entry to the school does not automatically make you a rude and condescending person.

    Hope you make it.[/u]
