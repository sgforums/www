    Okay this are the answer that i believe is right:

    1) in marketing, is the 4Ps or 4Cs better?

    Ans:There's not right or wrong to this question but i think the 4 Ps are more used widely.

    2) the 4Cs consist of Consumer solution, Convinience, Communications and Consumer Cost.
    can anyone explain to me what Convinience, Communications and Consumer Cost are?

    Ans: Actually the reason why the 4Cs are widely recognised to be used together with the 4Ps because it's bascially the same.

    4Ps = Product, Price , Place, Promotion
    4Cs = Customer Solution ,Customer Cost, Convenience, Communication

    Convenience has the same meaning as Place (ur ans is correction)
    Customer cost same as Price (Where how much the item is offordable)
    Communications (What kind of message ur item has given to the consumer)
