    The way I intend to study mine is:
    - Know what marketing concepts are available (application)
    Such as:
    (a) Pdt concept
    (b) Production Concept
    (c) Sales Oriented Concept
    (d) Customer Oriented concept
    (e) Societal Marketing concept (I think)

    - How u apply marketing to a marketing plan? (Cos marketing plan u must know marketing) Such as:
    (a) Internal Environment
    (b) External Environment (Macro environment)
    (c) Core Capabilities
    (d) Market Trends/Market Research (such as primary research/secondary research)
    (e) SWOT analysis
    (f) Segmentation
    (g) Marketing Strategy
    (h) Marketing tactics/mix (4 Ps or services 7Ps)
    - Then read up on the rest such as relationship marketing
    - Buyers and Sellers behaviours?
    - Understand how to apply the marketing mix
    (i.e. (i) Pricing strategy will depend how u wanna price ur items. As luxury item/Affordable/Cheap to enter the market place. (ii) Product strategy - is it a niche product/differeniated product. Or you just wish to product more for economies of scales sake.
