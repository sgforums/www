    Funkey! card is the cashless payment system implemented at whole of Downtown East since 2004. It is a stored value card that you can use to make your purchases throughout Downtown East, except MacDonald's and Burger King...

    For every $1 you spent using the card to pay for your purchase, you will earn three Funpoints... With the accummulation of the Funpoints, you can use the Funpoints to exchange for some premiums at the Funkey! Booth...

    The Funkey! card can be kept for use up to five years from the date of issue... And everytime, you just need to top up your cash into the card...

    Wild Wild Wet and Escape Theme Park use the Funkey! card as their mode of payment...

    At Escape Theme Park, for the admission rate of $16.50 per adult and $8.30 for child aged between three to twelve, you can enjoy unlimited tries on all the rides and attractions, no need to pay anymore, it's a price for all...

    Lockers are available using a dollar coin based on single-use basis...

    Likewise to Wild Wild Wet, for the admission rate of $12.90 per adult and $8.80 per child aged three to twelve, it covers for all the slides and attractions inside the park, inclusive the free use of tubes and life jackets...

    Lockers are available using Funkey! card at $2 per single-use basis...

    Operating hours for Escape Theme Park is from 10am to 8pm, with last admission at 7pm, on weekends, public holidays and gazetted MOE school holidays...

    Operating hours for Wild Wild Wet is from 1pm to 7pm, on Mondays, Wednesdays, Thursday, Fridays (Tuesdays are closed for maintenance) and from 10am to 7pm, with last admission at 6pm, on weekends, public holidays and gazetted MOE school holidays...

    For more details, visit their websites:
