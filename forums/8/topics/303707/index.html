    Hey guys, my advice on aerial is,

    - push off hard on the right leg (if you're going aerial to the right) and make sure you extend your pushing off leg fully (really point those feet)

    - make sure you you don't dip down your head to the ground, aim up

    - use your arms as well in the swinging of your body to go around

    - do a video recording of every attempt so that you can monitor your progress and to compare with the normal aerials (spotting mistakes)
