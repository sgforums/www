    i have worked in a small shipyard b4 so my experience may not be relevant. however it must be understood that a working environment is only dangerous when the people who should be responsible for the safety are not doing their job well.

    working in a dirty environment can be bad for your health but if u take the necessary precautions u should be okie.

    the money is definitely good esp if u can do ur job well. we live on an island and being surrounded by water, there is a reason why we have become a world class sea-port.

    do look into it and decide well.
