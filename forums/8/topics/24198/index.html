    I think besides getting the guitar (which should be assumed since u wanna learn!) first thing to do it to get ur fingers used to the strings. your fingertips will harden with calluses and it won't feel painful after a while.

    the next thing is to get used to spreading ur fingers and pressing just b4 the frets.. and also coordinating ur fingers one seperate from the other. one good initial learning exercise (altho BORING) is to constantly change between the C and G7 chords. just strum each of these chords twice or three times and change and do it repeatedly. also can practice your right hand (strumming/plucking hand) so that u can coordinate with ur left.

    i assume u are using a right handed guitar. most left handers will also learn on a right handed guitar.
