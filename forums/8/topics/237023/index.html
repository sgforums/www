    This cannot be generalized. Some kids can be reasoned, and they respond positively to reasoning or verbal rebukes. For these kind of kids, there is no need for any corporal punishment.

    However, some kids simply cannot be reasoned and/ or cannot be bothered with verbal rebukes. For these types of kids you gotta speak their language... The Rod! (Kids that run around inside the MRT inconveniencing and endangering fellow passangers are probably the ones that understand only the rod, but whose parents firmly subscribe to the "No Cane" rule)

    It is dangerous to argue for or against the use of the rod, without considering the character of the kid(s) concerned. I think its more important that even if you do cane your kids, you do it not out of anger, but out of a desire to make the kid into a better person.
