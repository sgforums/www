    Well, it depends on what you want to play. Below are the Classes WoW has to offer

    Warrior
    The 'tank' of a group. Basically you are the front man for any group in WoW. You have to hold the mobs from attacking the rest of the party especially the cloth wearers. I can't talk much about PvP coz I never played a warrior before but my Frost Mage always own the warrior

    Druid
    Hybrid Class and really nasty after Burning Crusade came out. A Feral Druid can now kill faster than a Rogue and Tank better than a Warrior and they heal pretty well too. In fact the Feral druids in my guild have more hit points and armor than our Main Tank though the Main tank have better items.... go figure.

    Priest
    The healer of any group. You can be heal spec or shadow (damage) spec. Seriously the shadow priest I think is lesser warlock but nasty in PvP coz a priest can shield and heal. I don't think a Priest is that effective if you want to be a DPS class and concentrate on PvE. Priest damage spells uses up way too much mana. Still, if you wanna play a priest, you have to go Shadow Spec till your guild starts raiding end game coz it's really a pain questing alone being a Holy priest

    Paladin
    Also a hybrid. I always call using a Paladin playing WoW in easy mode
    Wears plate, can heal and bubble up and can clease. Basically this means a pally have high armor, does decent damage, can heal like a priest and get out of traps easily like a druid ><

    Rogue
    Very fun class to play coz you can stealth and spring surprise attacks. Does crazy damage and if you are good can keep the mob/player stunned so long they can't even hit you.

    Hunter
    Fun class to play but I find that you need skill as a hunter. (Sad to say I think alot of hunters on my server sucks shit) You need range as there is a dead zone where you can't shoot arrows. Though you will go to melee but hunters do most dmg with bow or gun. You get a pet too for additional dps. You can also set traps. As a mage I hate hunters especially if they are spec Marksman with Silencing Shot ><

    Mage
    Super fun class I'm currently a mage. Nice DPS and you can really burn through mobs while questing. Have alot of area of effect spells. I'm Frost spec so I do less damage than a Fire mage but have better survivability. Other thing about mage is that it makes you lazy coz you can teleport to major cities instead of taking the 'public' transport.

    Warlock
    Reminds me of a hunter with a pet but you use spells instead of a weapon. You really DOT people to death. I tend to think of locks as a 'sucide' class. You either survive or you die coz the lock have no survival skills to get them out of sticky situations except maybe for fear which is a 2 edge sword.

    Anyway for me, I have only played the following classes seriously.
    Hunter, Mage, Priest & Rogue

    Mage is my fav. (So good for farming and moving around :lol
    Hunter is fun coz you can aggro everything and then Feint Death
    Priest ... kinda regretted creating her coz in a party, what I see all the time are the health bars of the party ><
    Rogue is fun for ganking
