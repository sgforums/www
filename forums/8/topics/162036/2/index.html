    COMBINED SCIENCE: 2 sciences total, studying each Science for each semester of the year (1 semester teach 1 science subject)

    PURE SCIENCE: 3 sciences or 2 sciences, studying all of them simultaeneously all time of the year. (1 semester teach 3 or 2 science subjects)

    PURE SCIENCE COURSES:

    3 SCIENCES: Triple Science

    2 SCIENCES: Double Science

    STRESS LEVELS:

    PURE > COMBINED

    CONCLUSION:

    PURE = Requirements: 60% overall percentage, above 65 for Science

    COMBINED = Requirements: Just pass.

    TRIPLE SCIENCE = Mad nerds who are glued to their desks
