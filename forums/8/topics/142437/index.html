    I guess in the beginning, it might be 'fun' or 'interesting' or provide a breath of fresh air because of the cultural differences, the different looks and all that.

    But after the intial euphoria is over, wouldn't you want to get to know him / her better? That's when you do need to communicate to be able to know each other on a deep level.

    Sooner or later, frustration would also set in when you realise that it's a task even to make each other understand each other's simplest needs. Also, even communicating simple things like, 'What would you like to eat?' becomes a challenge. Playing charades for a long time gets to you.

    If you can't even chit-chat with each other, how would you know what you have in common and all that anyways?

    So, my answer is nope! Open Communication is BIG for me!
