    me Samsung user.

    Firstly, as said by the others, pair up bluetooth adaptor (gota buy if u dont have), then, download and install this file http://sg.samsungmobile.com/en/phone/manual_software/manual_software.jsp?page=4. Choose Samsung PC Studio PIMS 2.0 (Win 98/ME/2K/XP) (which m using)

    After installing, activate the bluetooth from your PC and HP. Then open the bluetooth programme.

    Then open the file u installed from Samsung website. Go to FILE > SETUP Then choose which port the bluetooth is using. (check by seeing the blouetooth programme window) then choose speed as 115200. then press OK.

    if nothing happens, choose another Port, close PIMS and reopen.

    good luck!
