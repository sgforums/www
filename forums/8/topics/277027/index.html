    It really all depends on whether you aspire to be your own boss or not. Most business owners that I've talked to started out from a humble beginning. They love the control over their own profits and losses, whereas a salary based individual has totally no control over their rise or fall of their income. But of course, having a salary means that as long as you don't screw up, not corrupt and always on time for work, the money is yours.

    Well, in both aspects, they do provide income, but the question of quality time which I believe you are interested in. Everything concerning a salary based worker is always at the mercy of the management,eg salary,bonus,leave and etc. But being your own boss is different, you schedule your own time,leave,how much income you want really all depends on you.

    As for me, I'd love to have both time and money, and this is only possible if you work out your business plan well enough to enjoy both the former and latter. No risk , No gain . Whichever path you choose, I wish you all the best !
