    FEEL FREE TO ADD ON

    CONTACT LENSES

    Advantage :

    1. Looks better?
    2. Better focus compared to Specs. (No focus distance, less strain on eyes) eg. In FireIce's case, eye-sight stabilises...
    3. Less restrain when doing sports activities.

    Disadvantage

    1. Easier to get tired.
    2. Have to clean (if you're wearing focus day & night) or put on and take off every time ya use it.
    3. Cannot overheat.
    4. Might get eye diseases from it.
    5. Cannot go too dry places. eg. strong air-con.
    6. Limited wearing hours.
    7. Normally cannot go to sleep with it.

    SPECS:

    Advantage

    1. Some feels more natural & comfortable.
    2. Don't get tired so easily as compared to contacts.

    Disadvantage

    1. Additional weigh.
    2. Might be hindrance during sports.
