Toddler survives 80ft fall

  • kiri
    9 Nov 04, 13:24
    A two-year-old Romanian boy survived an 80ft fall down a rubbish chute from the top of a nine-storey block of flats.

    The toddler, from Petrila, Hunedoara county, was playing on the staircase when he heard noises coming from the rubbish chute and went to investigate.

    He leaned over the chute and fell into it, sliding almost 80ft to the ground floor, reports Libertatea newspaper.

    His landing was softened by the heap of rubbish at the bottom which fortunately hadn't been cleared for several days.

    Doctors from the Emergency Hospital in Petrosani said the boy was stable after breaking his left leg and arm.

    But they said it was a miracle that he had not been more seriously injured or killed.
