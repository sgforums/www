S'pore Ain't All That Bad Either.

  • Terence
    3 Feb 00, 02:17
    I've been accused of, among other things, being an unfair critic of our lovely tropical island. Just to even up the views, I thought it would be a nice self-reaffirming exercise to think of the things that make Singapore such a warm, cosy and generally beautiful place to die in.

    and none of that good government, low crime-rate crap.

    1. The empty and lighted expressways that stretch into the dark night. Can't get more existential than that.

    2. Being able to go to Chinatown for a 'traditional' meal to feel 'ethnic' and then hopping down to Holland V to feel cosmopolitan.

    3. Where else can you bitch and complain which such flourish?

    4. The small but growing number of street buskers.

    5. The fact that everyone from doctors to lawyers to hawkers and shopkeepers all wear shorts and slippers to Orchard Road. In a way there is very little visible class distinction.

    6. The encouraging and exciting growth of the theatre scene.

    7. Substation

    8. Cheap Food!

    9. Its a country where I have friends from different races and religions. Plurality of cultures.

    10. So close to beautiful diving spots around Southeast Asia

    11. vibrant night life

    12. how my aunty-type neighbours keep asking me if i've eaten. 'jia pah buai'?
