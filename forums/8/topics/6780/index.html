    Lately, I coloured my hair, but when I met some friends who did not meet each other for a long time, I was teased by them coz they thought that coloring hair is out of trend now. Last year, when there was a fashion for colouring hair, most of my friends around also joined the trend and coloured their hair too and they even persuaded me to go colouring with them, but I did not. Some of them already coloured their hair black when the trend was over. They are such high-fashioned gals, in their eyes. I usually am the one who can't catch up with fashions. I admit this frankly. Anyway, I like the colour of my hair now. So I am not angry with their comments, just laugh it off.

    This is only a small matter, and I don't bear it in mind, but it brings me a thought about fashions. Living in a modern society, fashions more or less affect our life. It can direct us to follow a certain style of conduct.

    Being regarded as the old-fashioned one, am I really insensitive to fashion? It may not be the only reason. Where do we get the information about fashion? Most are from mass media, especially the fashion magazines. If you get a fashion magazine on hand, it can tell u what is up to date in fashion: just follow it and you will be the most "IN" person. Nowadays, most of us young ppl like to go after trends and follow the ideas from the fashion magazines. Especially for the ppl aged between 14 to 20. Some of them just follow the fashion blindly, That is the reason why youth has quite similar taste in fashions and are similar in dressing and conduct. No offence to anyone who is keen on fashion, after all, not everyone can try the most " IN" things. for example, guys wear earrings. Only the young guys dare to try it. The younger ppl are more willing to try the new things. Maybe age is another reason for me always being out of trend. For the people of twenty something or older, they may be more choosy at following the trend. issit?

    Actually, fashions relate to the global culture. We can't talk about fashions without mentioning global fashions and mass media. As we know that Japanese culture affect us so much nowadays. if someone interest in this, I would like to hear more about it.
