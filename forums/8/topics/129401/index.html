This is what i ask before in another forum and someone told me what to do , you can try to figure out if not you got to ask someone who is gd to help you out. Gd luck

    A. Try to acquire another HDD with OS installed*.

    B. Connect the newly acquired HDD into IDE1 as Master, then connect old and new HDD into IDE2's Master and Slave.

    C. Boot up and backup all the files you need. (Burn into CD/DVD or Transfer to another HDD)

    D. Reformat and do a clean install for the HDD that has the damaged OS.

    *You may have to reinstall the OS while the newly acquired HDD is connected to your com, some hardware-to-software conflicts might prevent boot up.
