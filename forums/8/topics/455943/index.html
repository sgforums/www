    Hello every, I'm a beginner in here. Welcome to give me some advice.

    Evernote:

    I put a lot of important idea,meeting draft and other important information in Evernote on my computer.

    The Evernote app on the phone let me able to see these notes anywhere.

    Google Play:https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.evernote.world&feature=search_result#?t=W251bGwsMSwxLDEsImNvbS5ldmVybm90ZS53b3JsZCJd

    Official Site:

    http://evernote.com/

    Dropbox:

    Although we can connect Android phone with a USB file transfer, but unless a large capcities file transfer need to use USB. I usually use Dropbox to sync file quickly and efficiently.

    Or if you are not in front of a computer and you need to download the file into your phone, you could use the app to transfer the file to your phone quickly.

    There are so many cloud file space apps in the Play Store, but I love the sync speed and UI of Dropbox.   

    Play Store:https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.dropbox.android&feature=search_result&hl=en

    Official Site:

    https://www.dropbox.com/

    TED Air:

    TED allow me to follow the trend of technology all around the world. And the TED Air apps not only a useful app for widening our horizons but a good device for learning anothor language.

    We can change the language setting to Chinese or English, and it would show the subtitle of selected Language when we watch the TED videos.

    Play Store:https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ogqcorp.tpa&feature=search_result#?t=W251bGwsMSwxLDEsImNvbS5vZ3Fjb3JwLnRwYSJd

    http://www.ted.com/
