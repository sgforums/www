    The Singapore Fireworks Celebrations 2007 will be held on 17 and 18 August at the Marina Bay.

    To celebrate her 42nd birthday, Singapore will be inviting friends from all over the world to be part of the festivities. World-class producers from Europe and Asia will stage spectacular pyromusicals that will set the night skies ablaze with beautiful fireworks accompanied by stirring music.

    For the first time ever, spectators will be treated to the magnificent panoramic view of the fireworks displays and SingaporeÂ’s signature city skyline from the brand new floating stadium at the Marina Bay.

    The event will open in the evening with a festive carnival featuring a wide array of arts and charity stalls, entertaining street performances and many more exciting activities in the Marina Bay area.

    The celebrations at the Marina Bay will continue into the night with the fireworks shows followed by more late-night revelry around the Bay area.
