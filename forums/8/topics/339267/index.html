    Why Men are Childish

    Wednesday, October 08 2008 @ 07:49 PM PDT
    Men are childish because of women, or Darwin, or maybe both. 

    Women love children. This is good, because the human species would not have survived very long if we men had to take care of them. 

    Women seem programmed for it. It's reasonable to think that perhaps there is a genetic component; that this behavior is hardwired. 

    It's deep, that's for sure. Many a husband has found, with the arrival of his first child, that he is now running a distant second. Every eye movement, facial gesture, smile and even belch or burp is greeted with enthusiasm by the woman he married. An intelligent woman, able to discuss affairs of the day, scientific and intellectual, suddenly becomes perfectly happy to goo-goo in mindless abandon with the child. 

    It's probably an evolutionary thing. It makes sense that the babies who survived had mothers who were fascinated by their looks and behavior and both the mothers and the babies evolved to mutually reinforce this as a matter of species survival. 

    As women evolved to be fascinated with childish behavior, men were evolving as well. It's also a matter of species survival that women have to be attracted to men at least long enough to produce children. What better way to attract women than to plug in to attractions they already have? 

    So men evolved to have childish behavior and women evolved to mother their men. 

    At least until the first child comes along.
