    Hi, newcomer here

    Here to share some story.

    Me and my friends, during chalet, decided to go to OCH around 2am. Around 7 people went. Armed with a camera and videocam, we went there..However, due to the lobby being EXTREMELY cold at that point of time, and most of the people unwilling to enter, we left. But then me and the camera man guy, together with another guy, went to see the other building, above the hill. After filming, we watched the tape at the chalet. Here's how it went

    *Actual scene*
    Videoman: "Ok ah..here is the hospital"
    Friend: "Eh, on night vision, cannot see"
    Videoman: "Night vision..on"

    Then we could see the hospital building clearly. My friend proceeded to use our flashlight to shine at each window, asking every time,"Anything?"

    The flashlight, as you all may know, will appear bigger and dim on the building wall. As my friend shone through a particular window..around the 4th-5th storey, I swear, i saw an old man there, looking at us. In a instance, that guy was gone.

    As we watched the video..at that part where i saw the old man, where the flashlight shone..something happened. As the flashlight moved away, another beam of light split away from the window. It was like a bright tiny laser, intensely white. It began hovering around the left part of the building, while the torchlight was moving right.

    At first, we thought that we had two torchlight..then we realised, we only had one with us. Immediately, the videoman stopped the video and deleted it,(cause he very scared of such stuff one la~)

    The next day, me and my friend went to the hospital...in the..morning -.- cause it was the last day of the chalet already, and no one wanted to come with us at night. If you all want, i have some interesting pics taken at the hospital around 10am.

    However, I'm off to a party, if interested, please let me know, i'll post when i get back
