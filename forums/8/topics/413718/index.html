    Hi guys, I feel the need to raise the attention of the public regarding 'AUDIO HOUSE'. This afternoon, I went to Singapore Expo with my Father with the intention to buy a '40' inches Samsung LED. TV.

    We walked to Samsung kiosk where there was like an aution going on there.The salesman at the front was using a microphone saying '40' inches Samsung LED TV was selling for S$1,499 with freebies which are as below.

    1) A cheap looking HDMI cable which they claimed is S$120.

    2) A really cheap looking net book without a single brand on it.

    3) Free original wall mount

    4) Free delivery to my house

    We got the white card and proceed to the back. Before we can ask any questions, Salesman A took out the invoice and demanded us to make payment quickly before it's gone. I say ok but before making payment, I'll need to take a look at the netbook and the specs. Salesman A says I can't. (little fishy here) I demand again and he finally flash the cheap netbook infront of me and I took a closer look... damn it's looks damn cheap without a brand on it except made in China printed on the back of the netbook and he claims this brandless netbook is very expensive and came with high specs running on some cheap weird looking operating system. It's not even Linux, Windows or Mac. (umm... maybe it's better than my iPad I guess haaa...)

    Next he passed the invoice to my Father and my Father saw the price printed on the invoice was S$1,599 instead of S$1,499 as claimed. We asked Salesman A, did you make a mistake? Kindly check please. Salesman A came back and says there is no mistake. I demanded he check again and he seems pissed off. He came back the 2nd time and says his boss told him S$1,499 is fine but no netbook. At this time, my Father and I started to raise our eyebrowns and feel something fishy is going on here. It's totally different from what we've heard from the salesman infront with the microphone.

    Here come Salesman B (An award winning retard). He came over and was pretty rude (Cheap low class ah beng) and insisted both of us heard wrongly. He told us everybody heard S$1,599 and only the 2 of us heard S$1,499. I asked him who is the everybody? Please ask the person to come over and he kept quiet. Without any hesitation, both my Father and I walk out of this so called 'GOOD' deal. IMO, we felt this is a scam and they are trying to con us.

    After this incident, I'll never ever step foot into AUDIO HOUSE again because they've tarnished whatever little reputation they may have. Of course I'll warn my friends as well.
