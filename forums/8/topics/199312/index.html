    Well , I used to be from NUS. Just graduated from there a few months ago. I will say there is not much of a difference in the lifestyle of NUS and NTU, whether you enjoy uni life is very much up to yaself. That is if you want to be more active and participate in CCAs or hall activities if you gona stay in campus or just go to school attend lecture , tutorial and do project. Undoubtedly , the latter will make your uni life dull and uninteresting.

    Since you are going into NTU , do apply to stay in one of the halls and join their orientation to get to know people. Then perhaps join some committees or participate in some sports to have some fun outside of your normal school curriculum. This is quite a fail proof way to enjoy yaself in uni.
