    Males or females of Asia especially those of us that live in the tropics with the sun all year round should clean their face at least twice a day just like brushing our teeth twice a day.

    After reaching home, first thing first is to clean off the dust, muck, make-up or whatever on our face with an oil-free base cream/lotion. (Johnson babyoil or Nivea lotion works well). Use circular motion to work the lotion around the face starting from the chin working upwards all the way to the forehead. Then use a damp cotton to clean off the lotion.

    Next splash with lots of cool tepid water. Then apply foam cleanser to face. But try not to spend too long or too much on face for some foam cleanser can be very drying hor even the expensive one as it stripped off our natural oil. As usual splash off the residue with lots of cool tepid water.

    To tighten pores and if you are not lazy squeeze a bit of lemon juice into a bowl of water and immerse your face in it for a few seconds. Smells good too

    Ok now your face is clean and relax
