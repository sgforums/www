    Yang Chew Hwa / Yeong Chin Hwa are not so-called English names.

    They are english romanization of dialect names.

    Since you are born with Yang Zhen Hua, just stick with it. It can be written in English as Yang Zhen Hua.

    Unless if you want to adopt a dialect name, that is up to you, but first u have to check what your dialect ancestry is, and then how to change it properly.

    But I will not do it for that reason if I were you.

    For an English name, I just recommend "Jimmy" for you.

    Zhen = Jim sound.

    Jimmy is short and friendly.
