    Ocean's not too bad... Got a real 'clean' tenor voice...

    Recommended Tracks in his album :
    1. Xiu Xiu Xiu,
    2. Gu Dan Bei Ban Chiu,
    3. Wu Wei Xiong,
    4. Peng You Zui Jin Hao Ma, &
    5. Shang Xuan Yue (Version 2004)

    The lyrics of his songs are nicely written and I do like the lyrics of the first 2 recommended..

    Shang Xuan Yue was previously sang by Andy Hui (Xu Zhi An) and Ou De Yang's version has a totally different feel to the former.

    The album also includes 3 Minus One (Karaoke) track. Not too bad though if you are thinking of buying it..
