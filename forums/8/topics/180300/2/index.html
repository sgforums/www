    depending on the model, the price varies for oakley frames and sunglasses i've seen prices at 100++ to $800-$900++

    if you want oakley prescription lenses, you'll need to pay at least $400++ which is inclusive of $15.75 for the sending of the frame to USA
    lens price can go even higher denpending on the frame you choose, the harder to make the lens , the more expensive it would be, maybe $500-$600++ for the lenses alone

    normal curve lenses have distortions on the side, for people who need curved lenses on their sunglasses , oakley lenses have less distortion so people who need better vision for sports can still go for curve lenses

    and for those who actually bother to read the warranty card , it states that its a 1 year warranty for manufacturing defects

    beyond that its a case by case basis, and if you get charged for repairs after warranty period you get charged much less if you have the warranty card as in $30 instead of $60 so don't throw away your warranty card even if the warranty period expires!

    not sure if its for all models but i've seen it written on a card that original oakley sunglasses can go to the service center to exchange for free pair of ear sock or something in case of wear and tear within the warranty period
