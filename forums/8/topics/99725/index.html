Firefighters stumped by blaze next door

  • kiri
    26 Oct 04, 18:58
    A group of firemen were forced to watch helplessly as a house burnt to the ground next to their station.

    Bricks and rubble sent flying by an explosion blocked the door to the garage where their fire engines and water hoses were kept.

    Fire fighters ran out of the station after hearing the explosion but could do nothing to save the burning building just 60 feet away.

    Fire investigators are still not sure what caused the fire to break out in the house in Welmbuettel, Germany.

    But they say exploding gas pipes sent pieces of wreckage flying more than 160 feet.

    Police spokesman Guenter Santjer said the owner of the house had been lucky as he was away hunting at the time.

    "He was extremely crestfallen when he came back to find his house had been razed to the ground, but realised he had had a lucky escape," said Santjer.

    The fire was eventually put out two hours later by a fire crew from a neighbouring town.
