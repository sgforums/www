    Bored in Singapore?

    F.ROAN.I.R thinks life in Singapore is boring. He says: 'Other than window shopping, eating, watching movies and having barbecues, there's nothing much to do.'

    Candyger agrees. 'The only thing you can do here is either shop or play sports.'

    However, lalala1999 says that if he lived in other countries, he'll probably be doing the same things too.

    FarmerBrown also came to Singapore's defence.

    'Would you like demonstrations held at your doorstep and robberies in your neighbourhood?'

    Jayellefull would prefer it if 'there was more natural scenery around'.

    Support our athletes

    SYNAPSEMAN read with interest a letter written to The Straits Times asking 'Why send swimmers to Games?' (ST, March 23). He disagrees with this 'sure won't win, why bother?' mentality.

    He says: 'Many of our swimmers are rookies, that alone makes their efforts highly commendable.'

    Smallbully was also impressed with their performance. 'If you don't even try, you'll never have a chance of winning.'

    Shuuji considers it an achievement for the swimmers to have made it to the finals. She writes: 'We've proven that we're good enough to get into the finals, so who knows, maybe we'll get a medal next time!'
