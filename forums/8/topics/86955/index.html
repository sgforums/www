    From the Strait Times Webby:-
    A CARICATURE of a gunman, with what appeared to be gibberish written in Arabic script, sparked off a minor commotion in the heart of Chinatown on Saturday afternoon.

    The crudely made poster, measuring 50cm by 15cm, was pasted on the external wall of the sheltered exit to Chinatown MRT station at Park Road.
    Advertisement

    It drew a crowd of passers-by wondering about the significance of the poster. A cordon was put up around it by the police.

    Some police officers were spotted dusting for fingerprints on the poster and taking several photographs of it.

    Police are taking the matter seriously and carrying out investigations.

    Those responsible for putting up the poster might well be committing mischief or vandalism.
