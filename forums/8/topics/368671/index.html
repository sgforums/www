Volunteers needed for Special School Camp

  • ShrodingersCat
    18 Jul 09, 08:58

    The Starlight CLub is a volunteer organisation with a focus on providing social support with youths with special needs and youths at risk.

    For our current project, we are looking for 22 volunteers for the Spastic Children's Association School (SCAS) School Camp.

    Date: 8 August 2009 (Saturday)
    Time: 8.30 am - 6 pm
    Venue: Meet at SCAS. Bus will bring everyone to East Coast park.Volunteers to leave from East Coast Park.

    The children who will be attending this camp are between the ages 7 to 11 with different types of special needs. Some of them will be using the wheelchairs and/or walking aids. There are also some who may be non~ verbal and communicate using gestures.

    Activities include playing games, building big sandcastle together, bowling (wet weather program).

    There will be a volunteer briefing prior to the camp. Please let me know if you prefer the briefing to be: (Weekday Evening or Weekend Morning)

    Volunteers would be assigned as buddies to the children to help them to whenever it is required:
    1) Assist child in packing belongings/tent
    2) Play games
    3) help to carry child/ help in walking when necessary
    4) help to accompany child to toilet when necessary
    5) help in feeding when necessary

    I understand that the time is quite short and would appreciate if those interested can just send me a PM or email thestarlightclub@gmail.com !

    If you are interested in further volunteering opportunities, you can also join our Facebook group!


    Thank you everyone!
