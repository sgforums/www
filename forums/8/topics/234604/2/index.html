    been through this myself

    mostly they asked questions on what u have done in school that requires certain skills... for example they'll ask wat u like most about school, how do u deal with conflict when working in a team, give some examples of cases where u played a leadership role, why are u interested in this industry, how do u think u can contribute to the company, what do u think will be some challenges in stepping out into the workforce, etc

    for sales/marketing jobs, most likely the focus will be on communication/persuasive skills... sometimes may give u some prop and tell u to try to sell it to the interviewer

    don't be surprised by the questions... sometimes they will ask u questions on the industry/company that is quite unrelated to the job that u applied for

    good luck!
