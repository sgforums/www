    'Shrinking' homes

    NETIZENS note that new HDB flats have become smaller.

    Sticky issue

    STICKERS pasted on doors and gates of HDB flats by locksmiths as ads are a nuisance to some, but others have no problems with it.

    "Stickers are really too much," says undiscern, as it is "so difficult" to remove them.

    But zhihau writes: "I understand that peeling off the stickers is annoying. But locksmiths also depend on these ad methods to survive."
