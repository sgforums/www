    Hi friends, thought u peeps maybe interested in photoshoot opportunities with prizes or just a dayout. Heard it will be a very good event. Watchout for the fireworks coordinated display with Freestyle Moto X demonstration from the Best MotoX rider in the world.

    Event name: Waterfest By The Bay
    Venue: Marina Bay
    Time: 11am - 9pm
    Date: 15th & 16th October 2005

    Event Highlights:
    - Free Boats, Jet Ski rides
    - Static display of various crafts on land & mega yacth berthing along side Marina Bay throughout the event.
    - Water Sports Demonstration like Jet Ski, Wakeboard etc..
    Watersports demonstration time for 15th: 2pm, 3pm, 4pm, 5pm.
    Watersports demonstration time for 16th: 12pm, 2pm, 4pm.
    - Beach Volleyball "Quicksilver Team Cup" in Asia's first man made volleybal court on land, with International professional competitors like USA etc..
    - Canine Show (High jump & obsticle challenge)
    - Bayfront Party with Singapore's top bands
    - Freestyle Moto X demonstration from the Best MotoX rider in the world.
    (Special show on Saturday 7pm that includes fireworks display). MUST WATCH!
    MotoX demonstration time - 15th Oct, 1.35pm, 2.30pm, 4.30pm, 8.00pm with fireworks.
    MotoX demonstration time - 16th Oct, 1.30pm, 3.30pm, 5.30pm.
    - Photography contest

    Prizes for photography contest:
    1st Prize: S$500.00 photography voucher
    2nd Prize: S$200.00 photography voucher
    3rd Prize: S100.00 photography voucher

    Entry forms can be obtain at venue (registration tent facing waterfront), or log on to the official event website: www.ssc.gov.sg/waterfest for the official entry form.
