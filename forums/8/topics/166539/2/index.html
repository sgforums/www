    Anyway, it is interesting to note that the Misuse of Drugs Act has no heading in the law that says

    " Be it enacted by the President with the advice and consent of the Parliament of Singapore, as follows."

    Under Article 60 of our Constitution, any law to be deemed valid in Singapore is required to have this header.

    Every statute enacted by Parliament is required to have this header and it is highly debatable whether this law, without an authorising header, is actually even Constitutional.It's important that this issue be addressed, because we have been arresting and executing people under a law that does not even have a header that should basically empower the Act.
