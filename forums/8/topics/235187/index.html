    Mother Sentenced For injecting Faeces Into Daughter

    (Oklahoma City, OK) -- An Oklahoma woman will be behind bars for the next decade-and-a-half for carrying out the bizarre crime of injecting her young daughter with feces. Sarena Sherrard pled guilty this week to the faeces shooting in 2005 after getting caught on video tape doing it at an Oklahoma City hospital. She is said to suffer from Munchausen Syndrome by Proxy, a condition where parents try to gain attention by harming their children. The child suffers from hearing loss but is now four-years-old and has mostly recovered. The judge said he was sentencing Sherrad to one year behind bars for each of the 13 times the little girl was hospitalized and then added two-and-a-half more years because the abuse went on for that long. Sherrad is also not allowed to have unsupervised contact with children or the elderly and will spend more than a dozen years on probation after her release.
