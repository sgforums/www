    Faith

    The farther we go, the more the ultimate explanation recedes from us, and all we have left is Faith.
    -Vaclav Hlavaty

    Faith, like a jackal, feeds among the tombs, and even from these dead doubts she gathers her most vital Hope.
    -Herman Melville

    Faith is like electricity. You can't see it, but you can see the light.
    -Anon.

    Hope
    Fear can hold you prisoner,Hope can set you free. -The Shawshank Redemption quotation

    We must accept finite disappointment, but never lose infinite Hope.
    -Martin Luther King Jr.

    Of all ills that one endures, Hope is a cheap and universal cure.
    - Abraham Cowley

    The Future
    When all else is lost,the Future still remains.
    -I forgot who said this one...

    With each sunrise,think of your life as just beginning
    -I forgot who said this one...

    The Future belongs to those who believe in the Beauty of their Dreams -Eleanor Roosevelt.

    Perseverance

    History has demonstrated that the most notable winners usually encountered heartbreaking obstacles before they triumphed. They won because they refused to become discouraged by their defeats.
    - B. C. Forbes

    Geology is the study of Pressure and Time,that's all it takes,really,Pressure and Time. -Ellis "Red" Redding(Morgan Freeman) in The Shawshank Redemption(You won't really understand this quote unless you watch the show)

    Patience is bitter,but her fruit is sweet. -German proverb

    Adversity

    It is during our darkest moments that we must focus to see the light.
    -Taylor Benson

    And when it rains on your parade, look up rather than down. Without the rain, there would be no rainbow. - Jerry Chin

    Adversity draws men together and produces beauty and harmony in life's relationships, just as the cold of winter produces ice-flowers on the window-panes, which vanish with the warmth.
    - Soren Kierkegaard
