    if your monitor or printer cannot handle the resolution of the picture, it's going to look distorted... imagine, a 2048 x 1536 picture to be crammed into a monitor that can only handle 1280 x 960, it's going to be distorted as the monitor tries to reach something it cannot.

    so, see what you are going to use if for and what type of media you plan to use your pictures on...

    if your media, like your monitor cannot handle the high resolution, you can use things like photoshop or ACDsee, to step down the resolution, so it'll look better...

    hope this helps
