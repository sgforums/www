    Try Goldheart (jewellery shop). They'll tell you what to do and give you a little leaflet on aftercare. They should give you some kind of cleansing solution or gel. If they don't, ask them what you can pick up from guardian or any pharmacy. I used surgical spirit and it worked perfectly for me.

    If I remember right, you need to clean the area twice a day and gently move the earrings back and forth alittle. Depending on how fast you heal, it might take 3 to 6 weeks before you can remove the original earrings they use to pierce your ears. And for 6 months after that, I remember having to make sure my earrings weren't removed for more than 24 hours at any time so the ear holes don't close up.

    I think Isabella (costume jewellery counter) does ear piercing too. Don't know if they provide instructions on after care though.

    Good Luck
