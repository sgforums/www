    Would like to recommend the Singapore Zoo!

    Actually just been there recently on a weekday with spouse and son and is really quite enjoyable. Guess many of you people have not been there for ages ...my last trip there was almost 10 yrs ago. As i was discussing with my spouse, in our life time, there will only be certain ages when we go to the zoo. the first would be when we are pre-primary, when our parents will bring us there. The next time will probably be some years later in upper primary, for example, say after examinations and going for class excursions. Then there will be a lag of at least 10 years till we are young adults dating.....then zoo will be one of the places to go as well. ( after being sick of orchard road, suntec, marina etc) Finally as marriage went by and start to have children, this will be the time we visit the zoo again, with our kids.
    In fact both of us each has this similar experience, which i think many of you will agree.

    Coming back to the zoo, i must say it has changed tremendously. Admission charges is 13+ per adult but if you use nets, got 10% discount. Think children's price is half of that. there are many new attractions, with tram ride as well. If you have kids, there is a very interesting playground with a large pool area for kids to soak themselves in. Also have the animal farm which is very educational for kids to learn about domestic animals.

    Think since the Night Safari opens, much attention has been focussed on it rather than the Singapore zoological gardens. Go there, and you will be pleasantly surprised..
