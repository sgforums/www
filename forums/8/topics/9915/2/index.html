    You people wouldn't mind at all if a total stranger added a few random thoughts now, would you? I don't have any paper tomorrow, yet my mind is floundering hopelessly in the abyss of abeyance... boredom breeds insanity. Am currently fighting the urge to look over my shoulder just to check if my stuffed pooh bear is sneaking up on me, or if there's a face at my window... despite the fact that I reside on the 29th floor.
    I feel so insignificant... my life is like a walk along the beach... i leave footprints, but the tides of time wash them clean away... I look up, but spot nothing except the horizon... so... empty... so distant.

    Footsteps. Mom opens door. Mom demands to know why I'm spending so much time on the computer. Wonder what she would think if I told her i'm thinking about my life? I say nothing. Mom closes door.

    Thoughts drift... now thinking about colours. Colours are nice. Colours... they represent so many feelings... so many ideas in a non-verbal sort of way. Purple, so mysterious, or passionate red, or the unmistakable purity of the colour white... Wonder how humans automatically came to associate them with their corresponding emtions? If I had some artistic sense in me I'd represent Life on a sheet of canvas as a swirling mix of these colours, probably with varying intensities of the individual hues along an axis labeled Time. Hmm... looks like a Paddle Pop. Yum.

    Again I think. What's the point of this little passage? Can arrive at no definite answer... Quite intersting actually, that I can, at my own will and fancy, communicate through a thin copper wire to so many separate entities out there with whom I assume I have nothing in common save for Newton's Laws of Gravitation... and maybe Motion.

    Think I should sign off now..... best of luck for any upcoming papers you all might be sitting for.

    cya.
