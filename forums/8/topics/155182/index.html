    FYI, to buy flat, you need a family nucleus. If you are buying as a single you have to be 35 or older. At 25, sorry you cannot buy without a spouse.

    First-time buyer (with spouse) of RESALE HDB flat gets grant - Either $30K OR $40K
    You get $40K if you buy a flat that's within 2 km from your parents or in the same town
    Otherwise, you only get $30K.

    As a first-time Single buyer (must be >35 yrs old), you get only $11,000 grant

    However, you have to fulfill the first criteria on either getting a spouse or >35 yrs old before you can buy flat and get grant.

    Clear?
