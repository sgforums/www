    That's sounds a tad too hyperbolic. But your teacher's point is to more adjectives and imagery to bring a point across, rather than telling it straight out. The purpose is to enrich your writing and make it sound more interesting. For example, instead of saying, "It is morning", you can say, "The red sun rose like a ball of fire over the far hills." This applies more for descriptive writing such as recounts and narratives rather than expositions or information reports.
