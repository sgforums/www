    how many of you are close to graduation (from any institute, sch, poly, jc, u, kindergarden, etc), how many have already graduated?

    for those that've already graduated, when did the graduation mood struck? was it after the exams? before the exams? during the exams? during the start of your last semester?

    isn't it exhilarating?

    after 20 years in the bloody education system, 2 decades worth of being stuck in a lower caste of society, especially when being in a tertiary institute, where you're expected to act and think like a working professional, and yet treated like some secondary school kid that has just discovered the wonders of pubity. you are supposed to excel and be unique, create your own world, and yet you are told to conform. your works are not recognized because your mentors are industrial rejects that are cannot see beyond their own worth.

    you try to find a job but you're still a student! even though you've already learned all that you need to learn, you're still not given your due recognition, you're given menial tasks and minimal wages, your very existence exploited to the very ends. you're screaming to do what you've been taught to do, what you've always wanted to do, but you're relegated to the unwanted tasks, the unglamorous, unexciting things that everyone else avoids because there is no recognition.

    the final exams and assessments are near, work is piling up like mad, thesises, essays, projects, you're not supposed to be able to breathe!! you're to stay home everyday and mug and work and draw and type till you develope carpal tunnel syndrome and lose the use of your arms!!

    and when you look up, you see that bright glimmer of light that is graduation. it's better than chocolate.
