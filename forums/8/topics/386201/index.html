Watch out for Donation Collectors!

  • Shotgun
    28 Dec 09, 03:12

    There are scammers going around these days that approach people on the streets and shopping malls trying to solicit donations in the name of dubious charity organizations.  Watch out and be careful not to be scammed by them!

    Firstly,  check that they have a license to collect.  On the license, there must be the volunteer's name and NRIC.

    Secondly, they must either be wearing their NRIC or be willing to show it upon request.  If they say they "forgot" or whatever, be careful.  It is likely to be a scam.

    Third,  note down the "organization's" name, and contact number (if u are really anal). There are a lot of a ah-kow and ah-neow "charity" organizations popping up these days claiming to solicit funding for some children's humanitarian relief fund everywhere.  

    Finally, if you are in still doubt, just don't give.  Or ask that person to bring a colleague who has brought an NRIC along and donate to him/her instead.  

    Remember, this is not about being ngeow.  It is about preventing cheats and liars from benefiting from the goodwill of people in the name of "charity."

     

    Be on the look out for one particular women who hangs around Vivocity doing just this.  She is about 1.6m tall, about 24-30 yrs old, Chinese and usually wears a pony-tail.  She speaks mandarin in a very heartland fashion, and can speak English as well.  Complexion is very slightly tanned.   She operates alone, and claims to be a volunteer collecting donations for children's relief fund based somewhere in Telok Blangah.  

    When you ask her to show her license, she will just show you a plastic hard folder that shows an "official-looking" letter stating the purpose of the collection but NO NRIC details or collector's details.  When you ask her to show her NRIC, she will say she forgot to bring it.

    Note down what she is wearing, what bag she's carrying etc, politely decline and inform the information counter or security about it.  

    My friends and I had a run in with her once and exposed her.  She took off running like a bat out of hell and disappeared towards the bus stop.  So do watch out for her.
