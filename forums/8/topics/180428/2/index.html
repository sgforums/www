    Ok, from the teachers viewpoint, our contact with MOE HQ is usually through official channel, which means through the office clerks, which means the principal will be informed and has to approve (principal's signature). Of course, in situations such as this in which you obviously have to bypass the principal, you could either go one level up to cluster superintendent (the one who supervises all the principals in the cluster; but your school does not seem to be part of a school cluster system), or directly to MOE HQ.

    In cases like this, the only way is to go straight to MOE HQ; it is very difficult for a teacher in your school to help you, because he/she would by right have to inform the principal, who would then attempt to silence this complaint before it reaches MOE.

    So you have to do this yourself, with your parent's help, of course. Send an e-mail as well as a feedback submission to MOE giving them brief details (but don't give the name of your school or principal yet), and asking them who in MOE should you speak to regarding this.

    http://www.moe.gov.sg/corporate/contact_us.htm

    Feedback submission :
    http://www.moe.gov.sg/corporate/feedback.htm
