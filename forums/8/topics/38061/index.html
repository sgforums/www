    All SingNet (dial-up and broadband) subscribers are pre-enabled to use Outdoor Wireless Surf as long as you have a notebook/PDA and a wireless LAN card. Simply set the SSID to 'singtel', log-in with your userid and password when prompted, and you are ready to go!

    What you need to get connected
    1.
    A SingNet (Dial-up or Broadband account) or SingTel Mobile GPRS account.

    2.
    A notebook, or PDA that meets the requirement of Win 98SE/ME/2000/XP/CE.

    3.
    A (Wi-Fi or IEEE802.11b compliant) Wireless LAN card.

    4.
    Install driver on notebook/PDA

    5.
    When at hotspot, customer must change SSID to 'singtel'. Some Wireless LAN cards such as Orinoco are able to scan available SSID at location.

    Getting Started
    1.

    Launch the Internet Browser and request for an URL.

    3.

    For SingNet customers

    -
    Type in your SingNet Dial-up / BroadBand User ID, followed by @singnet and your corresponding password

    For SingTel Mobile GPRS customers

    -
    Your login ID is your mobile phone number, followed by @stm
    For eg, 91234567@stm

    -
