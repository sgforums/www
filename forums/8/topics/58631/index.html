    I was from 4SIR Recce Scout.

    Bascially you get to learn bike if u are a signaller in ur scout team (4person to go into jungle, while driver of scout team will hide with vehicles). (which mean that once u reach ur destination, u are suppose to get off from ur bike, with ur signal set and get ready for the mission)
    Bike, just like the scout jeep is used for transportation purpose.

    Like what others tell u earlier, scouts is really siong. First to leave camp for exercise, last to return. Cannot be contacted by enemy and must run like mad if enemy chase you. (dont get caught or else kena POW).
