    Well, I for one have always believed that revenge is never the answer...look at the the great Shakesperean revenge tragedies...when things go wrong in a relationship, it's always sad, especially if the breakup was a rough one. But if you ever truly had feelings for your ex, I don't think you'd want to hurt him/her in that way, right? Besides, it would be awfully juvenile to want to "get back at" someone like that. Although it;s understandable how or why some people would...after all, everyone's human, and the kneejerk reaction to being hurt by someone would probably be to want to hurt that person back...I think it all stems back to when we were young...I remember seeing parents telling their kids to "beat the floor" after they fall down...kind of to cheer the kid up, but I think inadvertently it plants the idea that the solution to alleviating pain is to hurt someone or something else back...the whole idea of revenge is rather barbaric, isn't it.
