    here is the proof. to make it easy, i don't consider whether you pass your birthday. everyone is older when 1 jan 2005 hits.

    1. First of all, pick the number of times a week that you would like to
    have chocolate
    (more than once but less than 10)

    define x:= chocolate number. we will see this number as the first digit.

    2. Multiply this number by 2 (just to be bold)

    2x

    3. Add 5

    2x+5

    4. Multiply it by 50 -- I'll wait while you get the calculator

    (2x+5)*50 = 100x + 250

    5. If you have already had your birthday this year add 1755 .... If you haven't, add 1754.


    just assume everyone passes the birthday, so +1755
    100x+250+1755 = 100x+2005
    now you see something...


    6. Now subtract the four digit year that you were born.

    100x+2005-birthyear = 100x + age,
    as x is single digit, after multiplying with 100, you will see it as the third digit. Age is even simpler, 2005 - birthyear definitely gives you your age.

    Easy.


    You should have a three digit number.
    The first digit of this was your original number (i.e., how many times you want to have chocolate each week).
    The next two numbers are YOUR AGE! (Oh YES, it is!!!!!)

    proof given as above

    Apparently it only works in Year 2005.


    to make it work for other years, you got to change the +1755 to +1756 (2006), +1757 (2007) and so on
