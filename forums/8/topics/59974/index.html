    Media Asia's Lunar New Year film MAGIC KITCHEN (lit. MO WAN CHU FONG) held its opening lens ceremony yesterday. In attendance were director Lee Chi-Ngai as well as stars Andy Lau Tak-Wah, Sammi Cheng Sau-Man, Jerry Yan Cheng-Xu (right with Lau and Cheng) and Nicola Cheung Sun-Yuet. Describing herself as "not much of a cook in real life", Sammi Cheng revealed that she is taking cooking lessons to make her performance as a master chef more believable. Since the event was held in a kitchen, reporters could not resist bringing up Andy Hui Chi-On's infamous "kitchen declaration". In response, Cheng reportedly blushed and said she had nothing to say. She also had no comment when asked if she would be spending the Mid-Autumn Festival with Hui.

    As for Jerry Yan, the Taiwanese idol told reporters that he will be speaking Cantonese in the film. Yan: "I'm very happy and excited to be working with Sammi and Wah-Jai. I hope to do my lines in Cantonese for the film so I'm studying very hard."
