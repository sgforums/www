    My observation ...

    International Players
    1) HMV - over-priced but good place to find those rare titles
    2) Borders - better-priced than HMV and a good place to get those US-import stuff. But unfortunately, the place is too small.
    3) Tower Records - over-priced but used to be good in finding rare titles but now they are shrinking their operation in SG. sad...

    Major Local Players
    1) Gramophone - started in Raffles Place (I think). seem to be flushed with cash as they acquired CD Warehouse and started expanding their operations. Pretty decent place to shop if you want normal titles. No surprises here.
    2) Sembawang - started in a little shop in Sembawang Shopping Centre (hence the name). Boring place to shop. Limited range of titles.
    3) That CD Shop - started in Tanglin. Over-priced. Perhaps they need to recoup what they spent on those expansive listening stations.
    4) Music Junction - once a local great but now seem to be dying...
    5) CD shop in Pearl Centre (don't know their name) - used to be my personal favourite as they have the cheapest range of Chinese and Cantonese titles. However, the place is too small and cramped. Recently, they brought in a lot of made-in-China stuff which I personally think it's a mistake.
