    Dear Sir/Madam,

    I am disgusted to know that a blogger by the name of XiaXue is a part-time writer for your paper. Being a regular reader since TNP was born, I was sad and disgruntled by this piece of news. Not that I have anything with that particular Miss XiaXue, but she doesn't come across as a person who can contribute anything intellect to your paper.

    From her blog, I actually 'sees' her as not much of depth/analysis and personality as a person. You could say I am in awed, shocked to know she's hired. Is that the minimum criteria for joining the TNP as a writer? I hope not.

    Besides being shallow, she's also not humble as you can see from the title of her blog. Does she really think she is God's Gift to men? How can a person like her be a good role-model for young singaporean? Therefore, I would like to appeal to you, Sir/Madam, please spare us the agony. I am sure there are better candidates around than Miss XiaXue, and I hope It wouldn't be long before I get to enjoy your paper like I used to.

    Your sincere reader,
    Caramon
