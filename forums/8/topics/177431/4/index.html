    Btw.... Here are some basics you should know as a temp staff.

    1.) If you are a monthly paid worker, your maximum working hour per week is 44hrs. Anything beyond that, you are entitled to 1.5x basic hrly rate.
    2.) Work on a public holiday or off day, you are entitled to 2x basic hrly rate of pay.

    3.) You are entitled to 1 off day every work week.

    4.) If you are a hrly paid worker, you too is entitled to 1.5x hr rate if you work beyond your scheduled hrs or over 44hrs in a week. ( this abit grey area, better to check with employer before starting work)

    5.) All workers, including temp staff and part-timers ( except where the worker is a full time student in Sec sch or JC) are required to contribute to CPF.

    There are many more information available, better to read up on the relevant Acts to protect yourself.
