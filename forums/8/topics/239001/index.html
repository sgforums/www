    A repeater like its name, repeat. Repeat what you ask. Data. Since you live in a 2-storey house and is not willing to buy a router, you will need a repeater. The repeater will repeat data such that your data will not go corrupt or get lost half-way down the cable while transferring.

    A router is just another piece of hardware that does routing. A router specializes in finding the best route between 2 networks. This will ensure that data will reach its destination faster.

    Depending on how you set a router up, it may act as a gateway, firewall or just what it does - routing.
