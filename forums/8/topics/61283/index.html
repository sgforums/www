    Originally posted by birdbird:

    Novotel is opposite Siam Square. Just walk out, walk over the overhead bridge and the malls are there. On the other side of Novotel is a lane that looks about like Holland Village. Maboonkrong is 1 sky train stop away from Siam Square.

    Siam Square is a large shopping mall with mid market goods. Maboonkrong sells the Far East Plaza kind of things.

    Take the sky train if you want to reach your destination fast and if you want to go to areas that the sky train cannot reach, take their boat on Chao Praya river. The jam at Bangkok don't end so its usually faster to hop from train to boat, boat to train and only when you don't have a choice, take a cab.
