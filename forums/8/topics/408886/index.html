    Dri fit usually usually 100% polyester.. and have 'wicking' to remove perspiration away from the skin to evaporate it faster...

    Usually your clothes don't remain wet with perspiration so if you are doing sports over a longer period of time (like trekking for hours).. this might prevent you from catching a cold from wearing wet clothes.

    But a lot of differences in quality between cheap dri fit and expensive ones...
