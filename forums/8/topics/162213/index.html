Child Aid Concert

  • ^spidee^
    10 Nov 05, 17:32


    For the first-time ever, Singapore's top young musicians are putting on a concert in aid of their less-fortunate peers.

    ChildAid will raise funds for The Straits Times School Pocket Money Fund and The Business Times Budding Artists Fund.

    A showcase of Singapore's young musical talents, ChildAid will feature award-winning talents such as pianist Abigail Sun, violinist Loh Jun Hong, jazz singer Nathan Hartono and winners of The Straits Times School of Rock competition 3dash1.

    Other highlights include the high-energy Jazz Kids, ballroom dance champions R. Reynita and Ashton de Silva, teen opera singer Janani Sridhar and Singapore Youth Festival winner, the Victoria Junior College choir.

    Tickets on sale at Sistic outlets at $10, $15 & $25.

    Location: NUS University Cultural Centre Hall

    Time: 8 December 2005, 8pm
