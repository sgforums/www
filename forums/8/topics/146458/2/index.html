    One way to do it in comfort is at Al Dente Esplanade, an Italian eatery that has an alfresco dining area on its rooftop.

    The restaurant offers two seating times for two different experiences. The earlier seating from 6 to 8pm will allow diners to catch the fly-by show. It is priced at $55 per person.

    The more magical experience, however, will be the later seating from 8 to 10pm. Priced at $75 per person, this will include some spectacular fireworks that mark the end of the parade.

    Both prices include a four-course dinner that features dishes such as flower crab bisque, grilled beef tenderloin and oven roasted butterfish.

    Reservations are a must and the restaurant requires prepayment while booking.

    Al Dente Esplanade, 8 Raffles Avenue, 01-13 Esplanade Mall, Tel: 6341-9188
