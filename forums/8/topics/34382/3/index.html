    Rumours of Banana Tree Spirit

    In every season, the banana trees are ready to blossom and their purple jantongs (hearts) which contained their flowers would start to appear. Soon the flowers would burst through and transform themselves into little green fruit, as small as a baby's fingers. These would grow quickly and within a month there would be beautiful bananas on long stems ready to be cut down and hung up to ripen.

    In the Kranji Banana plantation, there is a Pisang Raja tree - King of Bananas. The flesh and the skin of this particular species of banana was the color of gold and it tasted like no other banana. But one day, the Pisang Raja tree has been vandalized. Tied tightly around the jantong were strands of cotton sewing threads. They were of seven different colors. Why would anyone want to do such a thing? Somebody from the neighbourhood was trying to force the spirit of the Pisang Raja tree to grant him a special favour. By reciting jampi-jampi (magic verses used by witchdoctors) and tying threads tightly around the jantong, the flower buds would be deprived of moisture and would die. The threads would only be untied if the tree spirit agreed to grant the person the favour. Because of the tightly wound threads around its jantong, the tree spirit could be heard to groan in pain.

    There the culprit near the Pisang Raja tree, prayed out loud "Spirit of this tree! I command you to give me the first prize number in this week's lottery! If you don't, I shall destroy your jantong and you shall die!"

    Two days later, he won the lottery and moved out from the HDB flats into a condominium. On the same day, the Pisang Raja tree died because the thief didn't keep his promise and untie the thread. And the revenge from all the banana tree spirits from the whole farm have come into the HDB estate, in search of the thief.. We don't know what had happened. But all the people hastily moved out from the HDB estate as if they had just vaporized overnight.
