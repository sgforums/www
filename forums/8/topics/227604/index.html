    The Citibank SMRT Visa Platinum Card combines the best of credit card privileges and the convenience of an ez-link card. Earn up to 2% rebate as you tap and go. The top-up amount will be directly charged to your Citibank SMRT Visa Platinum Card. What's more, you'll earn up to 2% rebate with each top up!
