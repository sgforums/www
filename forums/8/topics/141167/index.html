Link between psychological problems and demonic possessions

  • About A GaL
    9 Jul 05, 08:37
    I was reading an article on the internet about the alleged 1949 exorcism of a boy which was inspired the movie The Exorcist. The article suggested that There may be a link between metal disorders and so called demonic possessions. We know mental disorders are real and science has progressed to explain why this phenomena exists.But the other phenomena demonic possessessions are still speculative.

    The 2 could be related or one causing the other. Demonic possessessions could be real but what causes them could be psychological too. Everday our minds are getting bombarded with tons of informaton around us. The sights and sounds we experience and the corresponding thoughts could somehow cause a mental imbalance in our brains. Society , upbringing and instilled values particularly superstitions could also play a huge part too.When the film The Exorcist was released back in the early 1970's, hundreds and hundreds of cinema-goers believed themselves to be possessed . In most cases demonic possessions seem to resemble symptoms of mental disorders and the the alleged exorcisms seem to closely resemble ancient treatments for mental disorder particulalry the power of suggestion before modern medicine came about

    Seizures and convulsions are symptoms of epilepsy. Personality changes can indicate hysteria, or schizophrenia, or other psychological malfunctions. The point of the issue is not to debunk any theories regarding religious beliefs or the medical profession. A thought which lingers in our minds is te 2 phenomenon could be the one and the same. The devil, as they call it which can enter our bodies and minds could well be a inherent psychological problem which manifests itself under the right environmental situations as a demonic possession, a term and ideas which were conceived by pseudoscientists with a religious agenda to the belief that there is a constant war being waged between God and the Devil for our souls.
    If the above points are valid, there could be truth to the saying afterall that there is the devil in everyone of us and it could very well be real
