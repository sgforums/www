    5 Steps to Becoming a Happier Person

     

    Finding yourself stuck in the doldrums? Perhaps it's time to make some lifestyle changes. If you your goal is to be happier this year, here are some simple steps you can take:

     

    1 Cultivate a passion

    Apart from work, find a hobby or interest that you enjoy doing, or pursue something that you've always wanted to do. Having goals will keep you driven and motivated.

     

    2 Be connected

    You are an individual and you are unique, but the happiest people are closely connected to something bigger than themselves, be it family, a club, or a community of some kind.

     

    3 Be brave

    Change is said to be the only constant in life, so what's the point in resisting any that may come your way? If you fear change, letting go of that fear wil do you much good.

     

    4 Avoid introspection

    Focus outward and you may find more fulfilment in life. With regard to your relationships with others, instead of considering what they can do for you and how thwy will serve your personal goals, think of what you can offer to them instead.

     

    5 Live in the moment

    This is clichéd, but it's true. Happiness is not a state to arrive at, but a manner of travelling. You may look forward to significant events that you think will make you happy, but it's the journey that matters.

    Remember to revel in the small things and appreciate everything you have - there are many out there who are happy with much less!

     

    Classified Connect, The Sunday Times, January 12 2014, Pg C5
