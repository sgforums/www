    4) Depreciation of equipment + fixtures and fittings @ S$ 10,000.00
    5) Other items like bags etc. per month @ S$ 2,000.00 per month.

    Grand total S$ ( 300,000.00 - 30,000.00 - 10,000.00 - 18,000.00 - 10,000.00 - 2,000.00 = S$ 230,000.00.
