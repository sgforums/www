    Phase 5 (Chinese Cemetery)

    Phase 5 of the exhumation programme covers the Exhumed Remains Section (ERS) of the Chinese Cemetery. The graves in ERS date back to between 1947 and 1975. Please see the map for location of graves.

    Exhumation has commenced on 17 July 2017.
