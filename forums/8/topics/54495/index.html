Love be...

  • Ihavenomouth
    9 Aug 03, 19:22
    If Love be the fruit of joy then love be not just a toy
    That fancies restless souls to sleep
    Love to me more then I plead

    If Love be the bonds of trust then how strange it surely must
    Of which I would then not doubt
    That Loving freely I not flout

    If Love be the passion of fire that Love does mean more than desire
    Then in haste I would gladly break
    These chains that causes my heart to ache

    If Love be the poison of pain venomously coursing through the veins
    Then Love be the epitome of death
    For Love I take my last breath

    If Love be destined for me if eyes once blind could only see
    If truth does hide from me it seems
    I end my night of illusive dreams

    If love be alive and breathe the air then for Love pray it be fair
    For Love would then plainly see
    That Love has taken life from me
