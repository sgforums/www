    "Freak Economics Macdonald Big Breakfast "

     

    "I always ponder over the frenzy people accord to ordering Big Breakfast Macdonald meal compared to the other breakfast meals offered at Macdonalds.



    People go gaga over the BiG breakfast meal because it is percieved as "BIG" compared to the rest of the meals. In a Big Breakfast meal, we have 1) the eggs, 2) 2 slices of bread, 3)one hashbrown, and 4) a piece of chicken sausage. These items are displayed spaciously on the plate. Now, this Seems a Feast!!!!.

    But on hindsight, you do realize that, you could assemble the items 1), 2) and 4) together and it is actually a burger. In fact, a sausage egg macmuffin burger if you call it. So in reality, there is nothing "BIG" about Macdonald's big breakfast meal. It is merely about presentation and about decieving the eyes."
    Source: http://www.bigagapekid.blogspot.com/
