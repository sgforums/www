    Welcome to Queensland XD

    250AUD per month is really a tight budget as usually cheap share house is about $110 per week for a single room in Sunny Bank area (nr Griffith), and 160+ for single rooms near UQ.(St Lucia)

    If you are really on a tight budget, my suggestion is, find a tempory accomondation first, then rent a whole house near the uni or close to transport and sublease the rooms to other students. Many students do this to save cost on accomondation.

    If you can read chinese, you may want to check out the brisbane shared accomondation forum on ozyoyo.com and find your first accomondation there. 

    I do not suggest Unilodge or whatever accomondation options provided by university. The pricing is unreasonable for the quality of the room and very noisy also, most are exchange students who party until the am. I stayed there before( Griffith Village) and moved out in a month as i really couldnt stand the noice.
