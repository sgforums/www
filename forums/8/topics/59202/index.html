    swanston street is along the main street of melbourne city so u dun have to worry abt ATMS cos there is ANZ bank, commonwealth bank and bank of melb branches there.

    swanston street is quite long but there are 7-11s along the city area so u dun have to worry. there is also a express supermarket there called coles express n also a small chinese grocery shop all within walking distance. as for fast food, in the city there is KFC, Mac, Burger king/ hungry jacks and also red rooster

    for food n transport, it depends on how long u will be staying in melb. public transport are divided into different zones. city area is zone one and for a daily zone one ticket, it is $5.20 which entitles u to unlimited rides in zone one the whole day. a weekly zone one ticket is $22.90 which gives u unlimited rides in zone one for the whole week....both tickets include travelling on the bus, tram and train. (i m assuming u r going for only a holiday n so wun be venturing into the suburbs but will only be hanging ard in the city)

    as for food, unless u r going to splurge...otherwise, eating at the usual eateries along chinatown or "foodcourt"...budget abt $10 per meal, inclusive of drinks

    hope this helps u!
