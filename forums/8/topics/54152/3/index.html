    My two cents,

    We consume food (calories) and also burn energy (calories). Any excess calories convert to fat. If there are shortage of calories, the body will burn fat. It is the laws of physics.

    Wanna lose weight, have to consume less food..... and at the same time, do exercise to burn more energy.

    After exercising you will feel more energized not tired and will sleep better at night.

    My advise is cut down on fatty food and cut down on sugared flavoured drinks like soft drinks, coffee. Reduce a bit your grain intake (eat a bit less rice). If you must snack eat healthier snacks like roasted nuts (unsalted) and sugar-free sweets. Eat more veges and lean meat/fish.

    Stick to a exercise regime, cardio group workouts like Kickbox will be ideal. If in a class, you will be more motivated. But to really lose weight at least 4 times a week.

    If you stick to it, in 6 months time confirm lose weight ....
