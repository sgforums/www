    FYi Redang is the island where the Richie Ren and Sammi Cheng movie, Mo Mo Char was filmed, Laguna Beach Resort has the original Mo Mo Cha cafÃ© still there, but kinda ex price is $964 while Berjaya is $646 for 2
    Berjaya is 5 star and Laguna is 4 star.

    Berjaya is on a beach by it's own. Laguna is at Pasir Panjang sharing the beach with about 8 other smaller operators. So, one is more quiet and the other more happenings.

    You should have the itinerary when you book the tour.

    Typical 3 day 2 night stay have you arriving at Noon. All meals provided. And then there are three snorkling trip usually, afternoon of arrival, next morning and afternoon. You can choose to go along with the snorkling or skip them. Recommended snorkling trip to go will be the one going to the marine park. You can see a lot of fishes and corals there. The rest will depend on if you like snorkling or not.

    I went to Laguna in August and went for only two snorkling trip, skipping the first one. Spend the rest of the time strolling on the beach and admiring the Ang Mos. Afternoon have some Ang Mo go naked when everyone is out snorkling.
