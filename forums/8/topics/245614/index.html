    okay.. here's the long and short of it...

    if she is Malaysian and is a Singapore PR or holding a Long-Term Pass, she can still enter Malaysia as long the Malaysian side allows her entry, probably with her MyKad or something.. ask first...

    but she will have to get a new passport before she is allowed to return to Singapore unless you guys find her passport and give it to her...

    if she is Singaporean, and still attempting to use the Restricted Passport (which many call "Malaysian Passport" ) there is no way in hell because that passport was phased out and not recognised more than 10 years ago...

    if she is Singaporean and want to go to Malaysia for a day, rush down to ICA Building NOW with photographs (you can take photos there too) to the Document of Identity Section and make a Document of Identity... good for 1 trip.. will cost you around $15
