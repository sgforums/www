    A dilemma that had been asked since the dawn of civilisation, which there is no right or wrong answer.

    Many will give arguments and conditions to the question posed, all equally logical and rational, but never a right or wrong one. It is a dilemma that one lives with, responsible for the decision he had to take, and no one else have a right to accuse or praise, but to ask themselves, what would I have done in his/her place....

     

    For myself, without any preconditions or senseless deep arguments, i would jump and attempt to save BOTH, or die trying. There may be a chance, a slim one perhaps, as it had happened in many crisis before, for a miracle that I may succeed.

    But if no miracle, then it is better to die in an attempt trying to save all, than to live with a haunted memory for life, of what could have been.....

     

    May no one needs to face such a terrifying and horrible dilemma..
