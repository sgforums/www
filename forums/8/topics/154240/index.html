    Warriors In Peace
    Singer: Jolin Tsai (Chinese version)
    Music composer: A.R.Rahman
    Music performed by: London Symphony Orchestra

    Wonderful rendition by popular Chinese singer Jolin Tsai...starts off with beeping sounds and Jolin's vocals, followed by ARR's trademark sitar and the tambourine, then eventually settling down to soft percussions...the song has a serene quality, interspersed by aggressive musical pieces...it transports you back to the times of Emperors, skillful Warriors and masterful Swordsmen...a powerful melody backed by a grand orchestration...brilliant!..
