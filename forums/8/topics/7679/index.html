    Dear MayaJade,

    Originally, I was thinking of reserving my 700th post for the Singles thread if it ever hit 699. But your thread was really so alluring that I decided to stop writing my 1st year report for a while and write down what I think about the issues. So I will make it very long since it is my 700th post.

    I always believe in dreams. They keep me alive, and it is the only thing which keeps me going. If I have to put it in thoughts, my dream is in an ascending order : the longing for love, the search for knowledge and finally having the faith that there exists altruism and hope for this world. Believe it or not, I once dream that I would be a martial arts instructor by nite and car mechanic by day, and I would riding my motorbike with my long hair dream gal sitting behind me. Now, it become so different.

    It is always a test to one's life that my dreams would be challenged. I know that I made many dreams come true in my life, and what really hurts me most is to be no longer part of the dream which I originally started. I recalled that I once started something really big that in the end, I have to be forced out of that dream by unforseen circumstances and perhaps my own folly. Yet it is always surprising how small a part of life is taken up by meaningful moments. Most often they are over before they start even though they cast a light on the future and make the person who originated them unforgettable. Everyone should have legends, they allow us to dream. In the same light, I think that dreams have dominated most part of my life. Because I have been freed of some dreams which I once wished to be, I have to start new dreams and I manage to start new ones now, perhaps in other things which I seek to fulfil now.

    I have always believed that life is a dream, love is a dream, and half our lives wasted, just for one dream. A flicker of yesterday, a spark of today, a man's rise and fall, only till death do we recall.

    Having dreams and achievements based on ambitions are not something really worthwhile. If I will to look back, I find myself making so much achievements and now I will be moving even more. But as I go along, I realise that it does not really matter how much achievements, fame and glory I do own. If you ask me, I am willing to give up all my dreams and achievements to be with that someone. I find that the more achievements made me feel more lonely and difficult to move on and more incomplete as a whole. Perhaps it is the longing for love. Yet, this dream of mine is never within my dream. If patience is really the issue, then I have been very patient till the point now, I have decided to stop seeking. It is love what matters to me most. If you ask me, I feel that I managed to achieve my dream for 2 years in my life, when I was with my ex-gfs, and they give me the encouragement and inspiration to make dreams come true. Yet, they do not realize that they are part of my dreams and for them to leave me, it was like taking away my dream as well and that is what hurts me the most.

    I have goals and fulfilled all of them, after looking at my own little list of goals. I think that to dream is to start with small goals. I remember telling myself a year ago, that by today next year, I must know how to dance, work in the bar and a couple of things and I made it. If you call that small dreams, I guess I did manage to make it work for myself.

    So, what more can I say? I will encourage people to dream, and it is like seeing the stars in the sky. Sometimes, when I am walking home in the middle of the night, and since the town I stay gets very dark, I will look up in the sky and find the constellations hanging in the sky. It made me feel so minute and yet, it give me hope and most importantly, it give me dreams to carry on the next day.

    Do take care.

    yours,
    the prodigal
