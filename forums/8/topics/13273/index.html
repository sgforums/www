  • Rapture
    22 Jan 02, 23:05
    I do Zoids modelling. I have 30-40 Zoids collection in my room.

    I can advice you to go buy spray paint, those which are meant for cars. Get about 20-30 cans of different colours, especially those metallic ones - metallic red, metallic blue, silver, sparkling gold, ash green etc.

    Arrange your model parts wide on newspaper and spray paint away !
    In the end when you assemble your model, the colour is much more nicer than those colours you buy from hobby shops!
    Really u can trust me on that - they look more solid and metallic!

    With spray paint, you can also layer different colours , example spray paint brown for the 1st layer, and silver for the second. The resulting silver is much more sparkling and beautiful.

    After I used spray paints, I really find that those toy models displaying at hobby shops really sucks and ugly!

    And what's more, 30 cans of spray paint is 60% cheaper or less(cost u about $ 120) than getting an airbrush with 30 colours ( which is more than $240).

    Go try it.
