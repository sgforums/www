What I observe is that this premise leads us to such unproductive questions as why God permit so much misery and injustice, the exploitation of the poor by the rich and all the other horrors which He could have prevented.. God was invented to explain mystery. God is always invoked to explain those things that you do not understand. Now, when you finally discover how something works, you get some laws which you're taking away from God; you don't need him anymore. But you need him for the other mysteries. God is something which man crafted to ward off the fear that lies in his heart against the natural forces.

    Though you may not think what I believe, but it does not mean that you are wrong. We are entitled to our own opinions and beliefs in my conception.

    Of course, religion has seriously impeded science in the past millenia, particularly during the Dark Ages for Christianity. Every great scientific truth goes through 3 stages.
