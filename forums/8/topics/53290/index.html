    First of all, different places in sg get PUB water which pass through different system.

    Landed property get PUB water direct from the PUB pipe. This is normally safe to drink direct from tape, although the quality of water differ from place to place say jurong as compared to marine parade.

    High rise building (HDB flats and Office Towers) say 5 storys and above get water from the roof top storage tank. The roof top storage tank is inturn feed by the storage tank at the ground floor. The trouble start when things start to get into the storage tank. It was reported that in Bukit Timah area, swerage water seeped in to the storage tank causing many office and reisdence sick. At one time some boys poured washing powder into the HDB roof top storage tank. At another time, a mudered lady was damped into the storage tank.

    Also for new high rise building, the PH value of the water may be way out of the WHO / PUB standard. This could be due to the low usage and the interaction of the stagnant water with the pipe lining.

    Therefore, do listen to the office Ah Soo to boil the water or drink from the drink dispenser for your own good.
