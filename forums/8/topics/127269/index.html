    Originally posted by NeonTetra:
    so will my turtle drown if i put it inside a deep tank of water?
    I'm not sure of the species of your turtle.. As in if it is a 'true' aquatic turtle (like the pignosed), it is ok to put it in a deep tank of water with no dry area as long as the tank is not filled to the brim, thus allowing it to swim up to the surface to take gasps of air from time to time. These turtles are able to 'breathe' oxygen in the water like fishes, and they only need to supplement this with gasps of air every 5 min or so.

    However, if your turtle is actually a terrapin or tortoise, u need dry land for it to climb onto, otherwise it'll have a hard time struggling to keep his head above water to breathe.. these fellas can't hold their breaths for too long underwater.
