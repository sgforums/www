    Depending on your usage, you might need to do servicing every one or two years.

     

    I use my aircon very frequently (about 7-8 hours a day).

     

    I normally ask my technician to chemical wash my unit every year. He will strip down the whole a/c unit (except the pipes connected to wall), spray chemicals on blower and the fins, then spray it with water, the whole process normally takes around 2-3 hours.

     

    The slime starts to form after one year, if I don't chemical wash it, in 2 years there will be green slime all over.
