    Good that you really practise 1-2 hours a day. Do that consistently, and you'll definitely see results.

    Also, don't need to always follow the pace of the teacher. If you really mastered what you'ved learned (notes correct, rhythm correct), you can go try other songs ahead.

    Actually, i think once you hit higher grades(near 8 ), it would be much more fun and inspiring experience to be able to play the songs you hear on your CDs(ie Mozart's Rondo Alla Turca, Chopin's Nocturne - take them as a challenge cos it's possible)...the middle grades were actually the toughest phase for me. Depends on each individual perhaps...
