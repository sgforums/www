    Like my laksa partnership with my brother, every year, apart from your personal income tax declaration statement, you need to pick up an extra form called Form P to declare the tax for your business.

    The tax in form P you have to declare is only 4 lines. The takings of your business for the year, the expenses for that year, and allowable deductions. The final amount is your profits. That's it!
