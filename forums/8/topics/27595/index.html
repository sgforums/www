The Time Is Not Too Long.

  • The Webmaster
    15 Oct 02, 09:39
    Friends and countrymen,

    Terrorism has arrived at our doorstep. This is in the words of our Prime Minister published just today. We have to be vigilant and realistic. To those who countinue to indulge in merry-making without giving a second hoot to the situation within and around us, I say to them that the time is not too long when we shall witness atrocities right in our midst.

    Ironically, we can no longer afford to lose respect for the criminals who have very little respect for human lives. They are profound in their ways and tactics, and are probably just as shrewd as the American FBI or the British MI6.

    Wake up! The Angel of Death is already at work around us. The time is not too long when some of our loved ones may be snatched from our sides. It seemed farfetched years ago, to some it remains so.

    Remember, what is to happen cannot be avoided - the inevitable happens.

    Can we complain any more about bus fares and hikes when the day comes when our homeland is reduced to rubble?
