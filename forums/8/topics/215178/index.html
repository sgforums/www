    Basically, some one is using something to earn money from and they are the creators of it, they have intellectual rights over it.

    So lets say Pocahontas is actually a real life person. What disney did was to animate it and create character with likeness to that of pocahontas. The intellectual rights Disney has isn't on Pocahontas but rather on the character they created and related works.

    You can draw your own Pocahontas and not infringe on their rights so long you do not ride on what they did.

    How you decide whether you ride on what Disney did is very vague, best is you make enough measures to avoid any infringments. The rest is up to the courts to decide.
