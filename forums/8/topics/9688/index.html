    A WOMAN gave birth to a baby girl in a Geylang hotel room and then abandoned her newborn.

    Ambulance officers found the baby in the room on Sunday with its umbilical cord still attached.

    Baby Sara, as nurses at KK Women's and Children's Hospital call the abandoned baby girl, is doing well now. Her weight is a healthy 2.6kg. -- SHIN MIN
    The manager of the hotel, Madam Ong, said she knew nothing about the baby's birth until the paramedics showed up.

    The room had been occupied by a Mandarin-speaking, middle-aged man for four days, she said.

    Neither this man nor the baby's mother could be found, but the police are looking for them.

    The baby is doing well now at KK Women's and Children's Hospital.

    Baby Sara, as the nurses call her, weighs a healthy 2.6 kg and appears to be a full-term infant.

    But a hospital spokesman said she has been put on an intravenous antibiotic drip to remove any possible infection during her birth.
