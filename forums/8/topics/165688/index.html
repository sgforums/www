    Xian is a great place to visit for the history.
    Other than the famous Terracotta warrior site, you may want to do a walk on the city walls. Apparently, it's the only city with an ancient city door (chen2 men2), not expensive to go in.
    The other nice place to go to is Hua Ching Tze (pinyin not exact) the place when Yang Gui Fei bathed in. It's now a very very nice park. Also in there is the little hut/house where Chang Kai Shek was almost shot. There's a window (now totally protected with reinforced glass) that a bullet went through.
    There's Shu Fa Jie (or calligraphy street) where one can purchase brushes, ink slabs, books....

    Food? Try the dumping feast at the city center. Lots of Muslim food too.

    Not been to Chengdu.
