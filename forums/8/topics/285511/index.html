    Renowned actress and comedian Lydia Sum Tin-ha is reported to be in serious condition in a Hong Kong hospital's intensive care unit after collapsing in her home on Thursday.

    South China Morning Post reported that the semi-conscious Hong Kong actress was rushed to the Queen Elizabeth Hospital.

    The 60-year-old actress, fondly known as Fei Fei, was later transferred to the Queen Mary Hospital.

    The Standard reported that Sum suffered from pleural effusion, which is a complication of liver cancer.

    Police officers have been assigned to patrol the intensive care unit at the hospital to keep the media at bay.

    Sum, fondly known as Fei Fei, (inset) was rushed to the Queen Elizabeth Hospital after she collapsed in her home in Hong Kong on Thursday.
