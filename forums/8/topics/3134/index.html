    From what I know (I've a relative in the army), they have a right to hold you back if they can account for it e.g. if they say that your job has not been done well during your period of service and you are responsible for certain serious matters, etc. But I think this applies only if the matter is of great importance or might affect the security of Singapore or result in some big undesirable consequences.

    However, if they want to extend your service because of your mc, I think it's not likely that they will succeed because when you were enlisted, you were supposed to be certified by the MO (at CMPB) that you were fit to enter the army. So, logically, any medical leave you take during your NS days should not be an reason to extend your service.
