    In last Sunday's issue of Life! from the Straits Times, there was a special report on SARS.

    A student from XXJC was interviewed... and my, was I shocked by her comments.

    She mentioned that her parents made her bring a water bottle to school [I think because of the reason that water coolers aren't exactly safe at this point of time]. And what did she say? "It's so embarressing!" And she also said that luckily she did not have to bring a lunchbox to school.

    I hope her comments don't represent the general population of teenagers nowadays... because if it does, it only goes to show how shallow the Y-generation has become.

    And by the way... is this a result of Singaporean education?
