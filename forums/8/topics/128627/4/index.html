    The story behind my nickname is 9 years in the making and has a long story behind it.....

    One day while I was in Sec 1, I was eating Chocolate Fudge Cookies. A friend of mine who was passing by saw me and, in a moment of childishness, started calling me Chocolate Fudge. Then the word was spread and everyone started calling me by that name.

    As years passed, people found it more and more tiring to call me by thyat long nickname. Gradually, my nickname was shortened to just 'Fudge'.

    Eventually I wanted a cooler-sounding nickname. At that time (in 2000), the Napster scandal had just broken out and everyone was talking about it. I thought it would be way cool to add a '-ster' behind the 'Fudge'.

    That's how I got 'Fudgester'.
