Happen that my friend L was there too, and he told me that he was interested in S. I knew K is still in love with S and due to my friendship and love debt, I decided to step out and help them instead. But S was very determine, she start to ignore L and try to get close to me instead.

    C and I knew each other when we went out for clubbing, that time she was harrass by another guy which she doesn't like and hint me to save her from that guy. We have that kind of connection which we knew what the other think just by some simple body language. But I don't know why, everytime we went out together, something bad seem to happen. Once was my cousin car break down and we are stranded on our way back home. Once was my cousin got drunk, pick up fight and we had to send him home instead. If our relationship really bring that much bad luck, I felt it is better for us to stay apart.

    I really don't know what to do, I really don't know who to choose, I guess I will let nature take it course.
