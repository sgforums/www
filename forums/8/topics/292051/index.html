    Originally posted by Y_Shun:
    hmm then from MI/POly which one is easier to get into Uni
    If you want to study Arts and Social Sciences, go to MI. If you study hard, get a good A-level certificate with some As and Bs, and a good GP grade of B3 and above, it's easier.

    However, if you're going for business/engineering related disciplines, I'd suggest you head to a Poly, but make sure you're in the top 5-10% of your cohort, then when you go to Uni, you can skip a year or two. It depends.

    I'm not from an Engineering background, so that's all I know about that.

    Of course, if you want to save money, just go MI Science and piah your way through and be independant.
