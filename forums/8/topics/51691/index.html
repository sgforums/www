    sgForums.com 2nd 8-Ball Championships 2003

    Venue: MONSTERCUE 2 @ Meridien Hotel #02-17/18

    Date & Time: 3 August, Sunday, 1330 - 1800

    Prizes
    1st Prize - $60 cash + Pool cue with case worth $69 + 1 year sgForums.com Premier Membership worth $20 + Trophy

    2nd Prize - $40 cash + Pool cue with case worth $69 + 1 year sgForums.com Premier Membership worth $20 + Trophy

    3rd Prize - Pool cue with case worth $69 + 1 year sgForums.com Premier Membership worth $20 + Trophy

    Registration Fee
    Pay only $15 & get
    - Free 3 month sgForums.com Premier Membership
    - Free Monstercue VIP card (discounts for playing time & pool accessories like cues and chalk at Monstercue outlets)
    - A few fun-filled frames of pool + a chance of winning!

    Rules
    World Standardised Rules.
    Referee's decisions are final. There will be an explanation of the rules beforehand to all players.

    Tournament Format
    "Double Elimination" format at the beginning so you need to lose 2 games before being knocked out, followed by best of 3 knockout.

    Eligibility
    Only sgForums members are allowed. Only amateur players allowed. No professional players allowed.

    Registration
    Players should arrive by 1300 to register. At 1330 SHARP if there are still players who have not arrived, they will forfeit their spots and anyone who is present can register for the empty spot. The competition will begin at 1400.

    Invitation
    This is not a serious tournament!!! It's just for fun, more like an activity for members to make friends and know who the other forumnites are So if you play pool, even if you're only a beginner, feel free to join us! Please! Also, supporters and birdwatchers are welcome

    How To Join?
    All 32 slots have now been filled up, but there WILL be participants who do not turn up, so just turn up and get on the waiting list and you might get a slot.
