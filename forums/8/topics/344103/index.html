    Lots of bad service in Singapore:

    1) Hairstylist just cut the hair according to what they want and ignore the need and requests from customers.  some even quarrel with the customers when customers voice out.    Hairstylist sweet talk to customers to con customers, used free samples on customers hair for own testing and still charge full rates, cut a hole in the hair.  Such people are Peggy from Super Cut - Goldhill Plaza, Daniel from Chapter 2, Bugis, Kary from Mix Salon (a small neighbourhood salon).

    2)   Bad service from STarhub, Singtel and M1

       Starhub knew that particular model of cable tv box not working effective still went ahead to issue to customers.  As a result, customers can't watch cable programme.   Technician came to check, confirmed this particular model has many sets returned because of defect.

    Starhub refuses to let customers terminate contract when both their GPRS and 3G internet broadband is giving lots of problems everyday.   They don't seem to solve the problem.

    Singtel overcharges bill.   The customer service of the Singnet broadband staff really suck.   Talked nicely to them but treat customers like shit.  Shouted at customers and upon request refused to pass call to supervisor or other colleagues when they can't help with the problem.

    M1 agreed to pay the balance bill paid upon termination in the next bill.  Instead, of paying the balance, they ask the customer to pay instead. 

    3)    Sales staff - Discrimination against local customers.    Sales staff prefer to serve the angmo first before the local customers when local customers are there first.
