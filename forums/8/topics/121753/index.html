    All my suggestions are for off the beaten track..

    Go up north to Shire of Dandaragnan.Nice beaches and literally crowdless if you want a good picnic by the beach
    Visit the Pinnacles.
    Port Bouvard, Mandurah.Nice houses with jetties, good for fishing trips
    Cottsloe and Scarborough Beach
    New Norcia, a Spanish monastic town.
    There's a Caesar's in Fremantle that sells the best pasta I've eaten.
    Explore the whole of Kings Park and the surrounding Botanic Gardens.Especailly the Federation Walkway that is a treetop walk.
    Try the apple strudel at Corica's , along Newcastle Street, Northbridge(just north east of Hay Street Mall)

    If you have a few days to spare, drive south to Albany or Denmark or up North to Pinjarra and Carnavon, Monkey Mia..Those are best done with a SUV and be careful on Australian outback highways, kangaroos and emus regularly are seen to have been knocked down.The outback is not lit at nite, so drive slowly.

    Have a nice trip!
