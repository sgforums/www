    How to get to Changi Boardwalk?

    By bus
    Take no.29 from Tampines or Changi Village and alight at Netheravon Road (near Changi Beach Club). Hmm.. if you guys know where's Changi Commando Camp. Its actually pretty near there. Just walk straight up say, if Changi Commando Camp is on your left.

    By cab
    Some cabbies do not know that place actually exists. So you have to let them know its near Changi Commando Camp or the old Changi Hospital.

    By car
    Drive along Loyang Way (i think), and turn left to Andover Road. Head straight up and you'll reach Changi Beach Club. The Boardwalk is just smacked near there.

    PS : There's a Boardwalk cafe in Changi Beach Club which is open to public. Food is decent only.
