Delicious Sushi/Sashimi at Takashimaya!

  • UltimaOnline
    14 Apr 06, 01:48
    Inside the Cold Storage at Takashimaya (Ngee Ann City), there is a sushi / sashimi section. The normal salmon sushi there (75 cents) is not very good. But their "Salmon Belly" sushi which costs $5.50 to $5.80 per pack of 5 pieces, is very fresh and delicious!

    How to tell the difference? The normal salmon sushi is all pink. The "Salmon Belly" is pink with white stripes. It's located at a different section from the cheaper normal salmon sushi. If u can't locate it, ask the auntie on duty there for help. Tell her youwant "Salmon Belly" sushi, or sashimi (which is more quantity, and so more expensive than sushi).

    I usually also buy a pack of fresh salad from the Cold Storage, plus the sushi, then head over to Mos Burger's nearby to buy ice-milk-tea-without-milk and their potato wedges (about as nice as their french fries), to makan together with the sushi.

    Try the "Salmon Belly" sushi; it's a little more expensive than the normal salmon sushi, but it's a lot fresher, more delicious, and certainly more worth it!
