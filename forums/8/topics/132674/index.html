    Originally posted by silv3rgal:
    Will you still wanna carry on with this friendship knowing that it'll never come to a good end?
    If it is friendship and remains only friendship, then you could have the best of both worlds -- no strings attached, yet you can still enjoy his company if you still want to.

    However, from your example, it may not be just friendship. It may lead to something else, something that does not have a good end, and that's where your rationale can save you.

    Think of it like taking drugs. You feel great when you take them, but you know they'll eventually destroy you. The problem is the more you take them, the more you want them. The deeper you're addicted to the pleasure they give you, the greater the pain of pulling yourself away from their clenches. So the best way to save yourself from pain is to abstain from pleasure offered in the first place.

    There is a Chinese nugget of wisdom "Short pain better than long pain". The same principle applies. Always protect yourself before indulging.
