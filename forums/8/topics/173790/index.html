    Polytechnic Graduates
    The following polytechnic diplomas are acceptable for admission to SoC undergraduate programmes. Advanced placement is based on the relevant polytechnic diploma subjects a candidate has read. Candidates with either the business or computing-related diplomas are classified under Category 2 and are required to take a placement test on admission to Computing programmes.

    Category 1: Computing diplomas
    i. Business Informatics (NYP)
    ii. Computer Information Systems/Programming & Systems Analysis/Information Technology (SP)
    iii. Information Technology/Computer Studies(NP)
    iv. Information Technology (Mobile Computing) (NP)
    v. Infocomm Technology (NP)
    vi. Information Technology (NYP)
    vii. Information Technology (TP)
    viii. Multimedia Computing (NP)

    Diploma holders from the above eight programmes admitted to either the four-year or three year programmes in SoC will be granted advanced placement credits totaled 38 MCs as follows:
    (i) 8 MCs from the University Level Requirements (one Humanities and Social Science
    GEM module and one Breadth module).
    (ii) 12 MCs from Unrestricted Elective Modules.
    (iii) 18 MCs from CS1101(5), CS1102(5), CS1104(4) and CS2102(4) from programme requirements.

    SO ONLY THE EIGHT DIPLOMAS ABOVE WILL GET FULL EXEMPTION W/O NEED OF PLACEMENT TESTS
