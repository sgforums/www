    Steps of disposing Energizer batteries in a proper manner:
    1. Remove the 2 AA size batteries from the pack
    2. Take out the used batteries from your discman/remote control/electric shaver...etc
    3. Replace the old batteries with the new batteries.
    4. Put the old batteries into the pack from the new batteries.
    5. Test whether the new batteries are working perfectly well.
    6. Go back to the store which you have bought the new batteries.
    7. Tell the person who has sold you the batteries, " Uncle/Auntie!! your batteries cannot work one lehz?!?! Can you please change another pack for me??"
    8. Leave your used batteries with the shop-keepers and take your new pack of batteries.

    This is the correct way of disposing batteries
