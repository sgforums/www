Yes. I just said it is not education you are getting but some memory excersice cuz u have to take exams and tests... after which you would have probably forgotten.

"Augustin, wake up your stupid business idea and start attending classes!! diploma is your future!!" wow...... in other words my lecturer was trying to say that i should study harder and later on find a job and work for someone else and shut up........ he just forgottened that times are hard and you might get retrenched.... so what do I do? study even more harder?? Goodness!! when will this stop??

    To cut my story from here, I say that the only way to make it in life is to own your own life.... be your own boss.... people WILL say you are crazy but thats life..... you want success, then take the road less travelled....

    Successfull people dont do different things, they just do things differently.
