    if u don't have a lot of luggage to hog around, then i recommend staying at some of japan's common-place lodging (besides ryokan). they have the capsule hotels (3000 - 5000 yen per person), the oedo onsen at odaibai is another popular night stay, where people can can go for outdoor onsen and overnight stay(5000 yen). all don't need bookings. these experiences are quite unique. else there are quite a number of good japanese budget hotels (such as alpha-1) which goes for 5000 yen per person per room. yahoo japan offers a good avenue for these searches (but they are in nihongo)
