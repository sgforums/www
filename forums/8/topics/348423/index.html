    each trick is around $30-40 and some needs a prop (or what me magicians call, a gimmick) Normally, a trick with a gimmick (if taught) costs far less coz the gimmick costs quite a lot (normally below a 100). I have taught quite a few strangers for that fee and back then, when Internet and P2P were still new (Napster era)...I have downloaded quite alot (more than 500GB) of Magic tricks Revealed by some of the leading magicians back then like Jay Sankey, Brian Ochab, John George and alot of former Ellusionist magicians (who are right now not performing for being under fire for exposing their secrets)..Normally, i burn them to CD to those who require them at $200 a pop..and 4-5 years back, magic was quite in the trend due to the emergence of David Blaine,Criss Angel and such..I managed to fund my handphone, ipod and PS2 directly from selling burned CDs.

    For beginners, i recommend u practice card flourishing for display performance..took me quite a while to master card flourishing..u may need DvDs to help though..then u can start off with easy card tricks like 3 card monte, using a Svengali deck and maybe hovering card.. I still have quite alot of Bicycle cards mostly ghost deck, svengali decks, gaff cards, Black tiger and blue ice..
