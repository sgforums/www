    Coming from a multimedia background, with some knowledge on game design... Let me give your my 2 cents worth of nothing

    3D animation/programming ain't as simple as it looks, unless you are really fervent 'bout it and dead hardworking... you will find yourself struggling with it halfway in the course..

    It's really not an easy road to take - from storyboarding, character design, logic planning etc to modeling, animating and coding... it can really drain a person

    You are right to say that there isn't any option to further your studies in Singapore yet; and overseas education do cost some money even if you're on a scholarship..

    I, however, will advise you to go for the diploma if you're truly interested. But be warned, that many poly students went into their course with so much passion and all... yet lost it all halfway through it...

    It's not just about interest sometimes... It's about discipline...

    Good luck
