    Sent to the mechanical workshop.

    Did dirty and greasy hands-on repair on valves and pumps together with banglas.

    Refused to do hands-on after some time.

    Sent to another department.

    Did data entry and file packing.

    **Conclusion: Employ undergrads to do sai gang (dirty job) for a mere $500-$600 per month. Freaking cheap labour compared to employing a part time worker for $1000+ per month. Main reason why many companies love to take in attachment students.

    Some companies (very few) do treat attachment students seriously. They give them proper work and training. Some even offer the hardworking students scholarships. This is the correct way of handling attachment students. However, in today's economy where job seekers exceed job vacancies, the companies don't give a damn whether you want to go back to them after you graduate. There will always be many others who would want to have the chance. Sigh...
