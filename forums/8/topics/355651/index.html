    Dont waste money on singing lessons unless you hope for a chance to be recognised. Then you go for that, then join singing competition, any place that can showcase yourself... Been good in singing not going to land yourself being singer. You need to be good in other respective areas... And also the lucky chance to be like by the professionals in the music industry.

    Otherwise, save the money for karaoke.

    Step 1: to listen to the songs for a few times, remember the tunes.

    Step 2: in karaoke, try to get the tunes and tempo right, record yourself and listen to check.

    Step 3: ok, now your tunes & tempo nearly there correctly, it about your own feeling, how happy, how sad you feel then apply them in your singing.

    Vocal Teacher like to teach breathing & dan tian. But actually, when we speak, depend on emotions, we used dan tian already. And when you are out of breath, you naturally will breath.
