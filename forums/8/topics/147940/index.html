    A Tribute to Minister Mentor Lee Kuan Yew,
    For he, who dedicated his life to Singapore.

    http://happybirthdaymmlee.com

    (to make it easier to read & remember - HappyBirthDayMMLee.com)

    This site is dedicated to celebrate Minister Mentor Lee Kuan Yew's birthday on 16 September.

    This is a Project by Ordinary People of Singapore and we ask you for your assistance to carry this project if you love him and understand the role he has played and is still continues for the development of Singapore and make it/keep it a Peaceful and Harmonious place to stay.

    We as Ordinary People of Singapore have thought and come up with this way of honoring this Extra-Ordinary man.

    We have not got any support from the Media. Why such a project by Ordinary People does not get their coverage which it should. What we are doing is Honoring our Great Leader which is everyones responsibility.

    We wish him long-healthy life to guide us (citizens of the world) in our journey to be the role model to the world, of a Lively HARMONIOUS Society - SINGAPORE.

    He is the one mainly responsible to have brought us to be here, not forgetting all those who assisted him to achieve this.

    They - The First Generation, have done their part in assisting him to bring us to this position.

    NOW - The Next Generation, have their part to play by honoring him.

    What we ask from all those enjoying benefits from the hardwork and dedication of MM Lee is to write in the Best ways they can to honor him.

    Is it too much that we are asking ?

    It is our humble request to YOU to promote this website at your home, among your friends & relative, your neibourhood, your work place.

    Please promote this website in the way you can

    HappyBirthDayMMLee.com

    http://HappyBirthDayMMLee.com

    We hope to compile a book of selected messages and present it to our MM Lee.

    Best Regards,

    musaKazim.
