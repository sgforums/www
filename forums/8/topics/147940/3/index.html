    of a Lively HARMONIOUS Society - SINGAPORE.

    He is the one mainly responsible to have brought us to be here, not forgetting all those who assisted him to achieve this.

    They - The First Generation, have done their part in assisting him to bring us to this position.

    NOW - The Next Generation, have their part to play in honoring him.

    We as Common People of Singapore have thought and come up with this way of honoring this Extra-Ordinary man.

    What we ask from all those enjoying the benefits from the hardwork and dedication of MM Lee is to write in the best ways they can to honor him.

    We have not got any support from the Media and we Don't Care for it, we are sure they will be questioned of their heedlessness. Why such a project by Common People did not get their coverage which it should have been. What we are doing is Honoring our Great Leader which is everyones responsibility.
