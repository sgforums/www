    nikar.
    1. don't change the layout! when they settle themselves, they know where to go for food and where to sleep. sometimes, they would already make their own slping/urinating area. hamsters cannot see far. they use their sense of smell mostly.

    2. hamsters are nocturnal.

    3. it is alright if you keep one. just don't keep it as display lah.. play with it at least 10mins each day.

    4. Vitacraft fruit/nut/wildberries sticks allow hamsters to bite and chew their food. sometimes, when ur asleep, the hamster may bite the metal wires of the cage. u surely cannot sleep one.

    side note : it is VERY important to allow hamsters to gnaw/chew on something. if you do not allow them to chew, the teeth can grow and pierce thru the gums and then... yes, the brain.
