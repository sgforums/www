    hamsters in the wild will eat anything.. they mostly eat seeds but will eat things like insects and worms for protein..

    very young hamsters, look around.. they are about the size of the old 50c coin

    soft sand, you can get at the pet shop too... the good ones.. they sell it as chinchilla sand.. the dirt and stuff will stick onto the sand and the hamster shakes it off.. i bought mine, a 2 litre bottle of the sand for $12

    just put some (about 1/2 cm deep) in a small dish (a shallow container they give at the hawker centre is best) and drop the hamster in it.. the hamster will know what to do if it's smart enough

    most of all, figure ALL the things out before you get a hamster
