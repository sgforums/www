    Hi all,
    If you are a single working adult, please kindly help to take this survey, thank you!

    Purpose of survey: For Poly group project. Project theme is "Dream Home".
    Goal is to design a housing project based on wants and needs of target group i.e. single professionals.

    Survey is meant to be as anonymous as possible.
    You do not need to include your name.
    Please send your response by PM.
    Thank You for assisting. :)


    1. What is your age and current job? What is your typical working day like?
    2. What type of house do you live in? Are you the owner?
    3. Are you going to buy your own home in the future? For yes/no, why?
    4. What type of house do you want to live in? e.g. HDB, condo, bungalow, etc
    5. Which part of Singapore would you like to live in and why?
    6. What amenities are most important to you? e.g. school, shopping mall, park, etc
    7. What attached facilities should your ideal home have? e.g. swimming pool, basketball court, gym, etc
    8. How should the interior of your home look like? e.g. colors, furnishings, etc
    9. What is the mood/atmosphere you want inside your house?
    End Of Survey
