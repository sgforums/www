    Dressing:

    - Price of clothes are usually cheap. Brought from pasa Malams or penesula plaza. Coz Ah bengs are usually poor.

    -texture of clothes are mainly glossy,

    - color of clothes loud colors like lime or yellow

    - Type of pants are usually baggy and loose, some bell bottomed. Baggy silver glossy pants are typical of an ah beng's dressing

    - Type of clothes include tight fitting sleve ones from pasa malam. Sometimes comes with a zipper with a huge zip shouting for attention

    - accessories wallet with chain, hairband, highly visible necklace that even a blind man can see it and sometimes piecings and earings

    - Brands ( some are fake brands ) like Spitfire,Fubu,Diesel, tribal, FOX,BLIND, NO FEAR, NY ( new york)

    Not saying that everybody who dresses like that is an ah beng. Juz saying that these are typically the stuff ah bengs go for...
