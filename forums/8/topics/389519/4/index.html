    Originally posted by As romanista2001:

    but only the more expensive auto watches can calibrate mah right ? if i'm not wrong, the basic Seiko autos cannot do anything to it leh.

    Nope - most mechanical movements can be regulated (it's not "calibrated" by the way).  The level of accuracy to which a movement can be regulated varies according to design and grade of materials used, as is how long the movement will actually maintain that level of accuracy before requiring regulation again.  Obviously there's a cost involved in getting a watchmaker to regulate the watch, so you'll need to weigh that cost against the value of the watch.
