    I've always told myself that i needed a holiday, to get away from it all.

    Who would've known that I was on a 2 day cruise, sitting at the deck looking at the sunset and later at night the beautiful stars... cruising to Indonesia.

    Waking up and being fed, the chefs cooking for us daily...

    Heading to the deck to breath fresh air whenever you feel like it... No TV, no newspapers, disconnected from the world except for the internet terminals.

    Lying in bed, the ship rocking gently... Now i'm using the internet at 0545 Indonesia time. No one to bother me... in fact no one has been bothering me since we set sail.

    Could this be a holiday in disguise?

    All I can say is, I'm not fully optimised yet. Maybe my holiday will end when that happens, but its been almost a week... I still want a real holiday!!!
