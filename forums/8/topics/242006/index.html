    Bg65 is just like a type of your string. I mean there r Bg80, BG65 etc.
    It's a yonex string. Unless you're really competitive, than the string type matters...if you're playing for leisure, i doubt the string will matter at all.

    As for rackets, it's not the lighter the better. It's the balance of the racket and it's ability to generate power. With today's technology, creating ubberly light rackets is cheaply possible, but who wants to play with a racket that feels like a feather...

    And today's more popular rackets by Yonex are all head-heavy based...and most of the are not light at all.
