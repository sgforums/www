    At times, our fellow citizens may have disappointed us, such as queue cutting, 'chopping' seats with tissue paper, overcrowded MRTs, whines and gripes, etc, but still, the Singapore spirit lives within us.

     
    It is something that had developed the night we were kicked out of the Malaysian federation, and passed on from generation to generation - a spirit that is deep inside each and everyone of us singaporeans, and yet being a metaphysical spirit, we know not how to express it in words, but only in action, as an entire nation and not as a self centred individual.


    Even though we may be a democratic nation, this spirit is socialist at heart. It is love, honour, integrity and hardwork.


    Love
    ----
    We singaporeans love our children. Our previous generation were poor and lived in attap huts, but yet they gave birth to us, provided for us, sacrifice much and help us become a middle class affluent society, when everybody else, espacially our neighbours,had given up on us all as a hopeless case.


    We love each other enough to contribute taxes, so as to fund social spending for all, helping the poor and all to get educated enough to be relevant for employment, a means out of poverty, as well as contributed sons to the military and civil forces to defend and protect everyone, even though we have no natural resources our own to give us wealth. We had only each other.


    We love ourselves, enough to be confident that we can provide for our brides and bridgrooms with a shared future of homes and children.


    Honour
    ------
    Our men serve, protect and defend our nation with honour, willingly take up arms and bear hardships on behalf of our women and next generations. We faithfully keep to our national pledge and meant every word of it in our daily lives. We live by the law, follow the laws and keep to it as a civilised nation.


    Integrity
    ---------
    We cheat no one and are generally corrupt free, from our political leaders to the roadsweeper. We earn our money honestly, no more and no less, and had even been grudgingly admired by other nations for people who cannot be bought.


    Hardwork

    -----------
    We remain gainfully employed and seek no handouts from anyone else. We all work hard, in schools and at work, even till other nation feels we are too hardworking. We work hard to survive and to provide, not only for ourselves, but for those who are struggling behind in the race of life, to help uplift them and bring them up to our level. No singaporean loafs, even the housewives work hard to bring up our next generation.


    This is you, written on your behalf, from your own heart despite the cynical exterior you and many exhibit. A Singaporean. And this spirit that lives within you.
