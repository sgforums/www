    I myself thinks that the current path that mankind is currently heading into is a path of no return, to put it simply, a path that leads towards depleting the world resources and ultimately our own extinction.

    We spend too much time working, then earning the so call money(which some say is the devil's gift), and ultimately we spend it. This is the way economy works now, where everyone had to contribute in one way or another by working and also by spending back the money back to the economy.

    But had anyone ever questioned do we really need the stuff that we're buying? Do you still remember the movies or dvds you're watched years ago? do you still play playstation one? do you still use iphone 2G? do you still use windows 95? All these products is basically the same, what i mean is windows 95 is the same as windows vista, and iphone 2G is the same as iphone 3GS, they're just different generations and you just pay a money to have the 'latest' version which speaking relatively, it's actually the same. We work, economy moves, shares rises, property rises and resources get used up faster and faster. Oil is going to be used up in another 80yrs time(just one generation) and sadly no reliable alternatives is available, and shockily, even if there's alternative, our buildings, infrastructure will not be equipped to tap into this new source, so we're definitely DEAD within the next 200 years(which is amazing short given the amount of time we've evolved as human) 

    I myself feels the correct way is to cut down on technological advancement, lets face it, we don't need a iphone every year (if there's no one new model every year, we'll always own the latest model), we don't need a new movie per month, we don't need new games every month (we'll forget how to play it years from now anyway) etc. We should build a world whereby everyone works maybe 1-2 days a week (if everyone in the world do that, it's basically the same as working 5 days a week), then we can spend the rest of our time talking, playing simple games, or just relaxing instead of participating in the vicious cycle that we're currently in. The world will be moving slowly if thats the case, but nature is a slow machine, no point rushing here and there when life itself tooks it own sweet time. Life must be moving slowly in order to be appreciated.

    But given the way nations and companies of the free world market is being lay out currently, where 1 major world power is check by another major world power and companies is being checked by their competitors. NO ONE can stop this cycle, america can't be the first to stop moving their economy with the world's other power still powering their economy on, countries can't be the first to cut down "significantly" on their natural resource output without risking falling behind their competitor countries, no one can stop it now. It is a joke even thinking that countries will work together for the sake of mankind, cause lets face it, they'll need to earn points for their own political reasons back on their own country, no point worrying stuff which is generation ahead when their own office term is til the next election which is only 4-5 years to go. They would only want the market to move and economy to prosper so they can remain in office.

    So lets face it, we're definitely DEAD, so don't bother having kids cause i can almost assure you they'll experience a world war that makes WW2 seems like a kids game in their lifetime, good news is, you and I are safe, since we'll not be alive when that happens.
