    i went shenzhen once every year. i stay with my cousins in long gang which is 80km away from it .. i tell u some experience la..

    actually shenzhen now is about 7 years back of singapore. when u go there, must bargain. their items usually sell 3-4 times the normal price(1.5 -2 times for electronic product).. so if u see this bag cost 200 dollar, u can actually get it at $50. depends on your bargain skill, tatic, ect ect.

    you also can go to asia's biggest IT trade centre (aka shenzhen simlim. but sim lim is the best in S.E.A). more than 3000 companies there do IT trade in just one building. competition is so tough that u may even get really cheap prices out from them. be sure that u ask about warranty... local or international. and also ,just take extra precaution to look out for immitation products.

    The food there taste great too! many different (hokkien, teo chew ect ect...)chinese culture food restrauant to choose. just bear in mind that hot( as in chilli hot) isn't commonly serve there).

    if on the street u see people with limps cut off and begging for money, dont give them. my cousin says that government has given food and place to stay. they did that cos they just want money. they dont deserve at all too... is money more important than health?

    most of theshopping mall there very squeezy one, so keep your belonging tight as well.
