    Originally posted by FireIce:
    yah.......
    but i wear contact lens for so long liao hor.....my degree sort of stayed constant.......
    Well, that depends the type of lenses you wear then.
    Soft lenses won't stabilise your shortsightedness coz it is soft, and it conforms to the shape of your cornea.
    Rigid Gas Permeable lenses will be able to stabilise your shortsightedness by moulding the shape of your cornea in a way.
    However, when we grow older, our shortsightedness will sort of stabilise. Even if there is any increment, it will only increase by not more than 50degrees.
