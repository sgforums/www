    to have a .com.sg in your domain name, it has to be attached to a local and registered company name.

    however, you can always register a personal domain name e.g. ahwei.per.sg with sgnic. but sgnic no longer allows domain name parking. their responsibilities are narrowed down to being a domain name registrar.

    be it a .per.sg or .com.sg, to park your domain name, you will have to do so with private domain name hosting company. e.g. 8to.com.sg

    lastly, you can be anyone to register a .com or .net. there are tons of registrar which allows registration and domain name parking out there. e.g. register.com
