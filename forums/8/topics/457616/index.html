    Sembawang Hot Spring, located at Gambas Avenue between Woodlands Avenue 12 and Sembawang Road, is the only natural hot spring on the main island of Singapore. At a small side entrance of Chong Pang Military Camp, it lies in a shady wooded area about 100 metres off Gambas Ave. It's open to public and free entry from 7am to 7pm daily.
