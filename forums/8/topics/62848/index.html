    Ok, anyway, the power box you're talking about is a power supply. This power supply is connected to the motherboard or mainboard and it powers various devices like your CPU,fan,harddisk,etc. When u press the "On" button to switch on your computer,it actually signals your motherboard to start things up, i.e,CPU runs,botup screen appears. The ventilation fan in your power supply will only be active when your system is turned on. This is what i found out. I don't think you can verify the power supply that way.

    Anyway, your problem may be some loose connections like the power cord connecting to your power supply.
