The lost soul was caught in a dilemma and could never made up its mind. The kind-hearted God then transformed a scenerio of Ah Teck's Fruits Stall right in front of the soul's eyes. 'Look carefully', he said.

    Apples were the hottest selling fruits off the shelf especially the reddest though it cannot guaranteed it might be the juiciest of them all, whilst Oranges were a close-knit, happy and contented bunch of fruits. God then carry on to sum up with two abstract equations; left to right, non-reversible.

    1. Oranges like Apples
    2. Apples may or may not like Apples

The soul was then transformed into a dull-looking apple.
