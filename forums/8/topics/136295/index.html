    it's got to do with the Old typewriter. those with mechanical arms that snap themselves to the paper.

    in short, the layout of QWERTY keyboard is to seperate the common keys AEIOU, RSTLN so that their arms will not be entangled if the typist is a fast, or touch-typist..........sooooo......when they evolved to computer keyboard, the layout remined because people were already trained that way.

    Therefore, if Bill Gates come out with a ABCDE layout, it will still work. Just need 5-10 years to become the default layout.
