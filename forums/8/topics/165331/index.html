    Young boy, start by learning & understanding Time Scales or Time Signatures.

    See how well you can keep up by tapping with your hand to a metronome.

    Listen to all sorts of music styles, and note the drumming styles involved.

    Ask yourself also, can you practice conveniently, without causing noise pollution?
