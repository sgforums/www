    OAV, (sometimes quoted as OVA), means Original Animation Video. This is a series that is not used for screening over TV. OAVs are not movies, even though some of the OAVs have the length of a normal movie. An OAV is a series of episodes. OAV usually has more gore, hentai, more serious content, not suitable for TV screening. OAV targets audience who usually fall in the category of fans or adults.

    Seisouhen is the second OAV, spanning 2 episodes covering the brief summary of what happened after the manga's Jinchuu (Renzhu) Arc.

    Each episode is around 27mins. Thus it is less than a movie, so you wonderful dreamers out there. THIS is not a movie.
