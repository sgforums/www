    Beware of FONNY!

    --------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    dl4akb04-08-2003, 07:34 PM
    Well, somehow I expected what happend today. More than 4 weeks ago I returned
    a faulty RAID controller card to FONNY. The card was less than 6 months old and
    fully covered by warranty. I was told, that they can not provide an exchange
    and the card has to be sent in to their distributor as it was not new. Fine,
    no temporary solution available. I was forced to buy a new card to be able to
    get access to my data on the harddisk including important work files. They
    promised me to give me a phone call as soon as they have more information.
    I gave them my business card.

    So far so good. Today I found time to go to SLS and inquire the status of the card.
    The one staff at FONNY took a look at the receipt they gave me, then looked in
    their shelves only to tell me that the card would be still with the supplier.
    Well, I just asked for the name and the phone number of the "supplier" and what
    the heck, the same time I saw my box at the shelf behind the desk!!! This
    damned MF was lying directly in my face. I asked him to give me the box and the
    card was still the same. Untouched for more than 4 weeks.

    No wonder that this guy disappeared somehow fast and left his colleague talking
    to me. I was already quite pissed of and asked him for an explanation... The only
    thing he could tell me was that he did not sign the receipt from 4 weeks ago and
    the guy who did it will maybe come in 1 hour! I asked him to call his colleague
    and the next lie: he would not have a mobile phone. Unbelievable! Now that was enough.
    I asked him if he is the boss and as he declined I asked where I could the boss.
    No answer. He said he doesn't even have the phone number or name of his boss!!!
    If the shop would burn down he could not call his boss?! Bullshit! So stupid
    liars there... I took the broken card together with the receipt and left.
    This case will go to CASE!

    So beware to buy anything from FONNY. Their warranty is not worth the paper they
    print it on.
