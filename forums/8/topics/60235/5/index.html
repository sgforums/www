    Cow 'makes nuisance phone calls'

    A Belgian woman being plagued by nuisance phone calls discovered the culprit was a dairy cow.

    The farmer had rigged up the animal's milking machine to call a mobile phone if it malfunctioned.

    But he accidentally programmed in Caroline Lenaerts' phone number.

    Caroline, 26, from Ravels, told the Het Laatste Nieuws she received strange calls for two weeks until the mystery was solved.

    "I could only a hear a signal, no voice. It was no pleasure at all. Once it woke me at half past four in the morning," she said.

    The paper says the telephone company eventually traced the calls to the farmer in Poppel who told them about his alarm system.

    Story filed: 19:59 Tuesday 29th January 2002
