  • binZ

    Today 19 June 05, around 1718 hrs. after topping up fuel at the SHELL station near my house, I proceed to make a a U- turn at a traffic junction.

    At the point of time, there was a SBS bus, service no, 181, Car plate no SBS 304A that is proceeding along the road with the speed of around 40km/hr.

    As It was a safe distance of at least 150 metres away, plus it was travelling slowly, i proceed with my U turn.

    While i am U-turning, that bus driver start to on his head light, and flashed his high beam at me several times when we are still a goodly 100 metres away and in no danger of any collision.

    I thought that the driver might be overly cautious and thus just proceed on the road to Jurong west st 25. But when i reached the traffic junction outside Jurong Police Division, there was a red light and i stopped my vehicle. That same bus start to flash his high beam at me again. it is for no particular reason that this bus flashed at me. I dun think it is wrong for me to stop at a red light? From there onwards, this bus driver keep his high beam at me all the way.

    And i stopped at the road side and on my hazard light to wait for the bus to go past me to take down the bus number, the bugger delibrately stop behind me and expect me to move off. After he U turn at the small roundabout, he still got the check to stare at me.

    pissed me off.. will go and complain to SBS! If im not happy with the answer, i write to the papers!
