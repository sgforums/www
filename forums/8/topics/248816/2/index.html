    Originally posted by Chuichi Koshiramaru:
    Why must hatred must always exist in the human heart? Why can't we all get along? Can't everyone just tolerate each other?
    Easy to say sia... I believe everyone will opt for a peaceful life anytime BUT, there is a limit to tolerance right?

    Some of us get riled easily but some of us can 'loon' very long. But everyone will still feel anger especially when being accused for doing or saying something that is not said or done. We can also get angry with insults that we don't deserve. Naggy people will always drive us up the wall. Dishonest, insincere and liars make our blood boil. Being made to feel like a blardy fool will also cause us to react defensively.

    There are thousands of ways PEOPLE can irk us to mild to extreme anger. It is at that stage when we feel 'hate'. Hatred for the cruel words/actions, hatred for the wrongs done to us, hatred for the wicked, the snobs and the evil doers.

    In a way, hatred is not totally bad. But to carry deep resentments in the heart is bad.

    We are not living in la-la land. There are people we can get along well for many years. But there are people we best disassociate with because of the vast difference in beliefs, goals and train of thoughts. Through mankind's instinctive sense of preservation, it is natural to avoid any form of conflicts. Some do it by shying away, others by bull-dozing their authority over others.
