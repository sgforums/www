    I have been told to clear my one particular set of my work (which I really hate to do) and suddenly a dead line is set. This set of work started in 2003 and have been on & off till now. Suddenly warnings and threats are coming in from my boss that I will lose my post if I dun hand over my work on time.
    Everyday since last week I get emails from my boss threatening that he will take drastic actions against me to remove me from my post, and he is very disappointed in me. I was really puzzled. I have worked very hard and was promoted twice in 3 years. My boss says I have been complacent bcoz I was rated very highly by my management. Ok, I tell myself, its my fault I have not committed myself so this is the time to go full throttle.

    I have been forcing myself to work for 12 hours everyday this week, handing in my work but the initial part have been rejected by my boss with nasty remarks and tasked with more questions & directions. Furthermore I have to come up with the programme of a full day event I was tasked to organised for my department.
    I am really pushed to the edge, mentally very drained, utill I suddenly felt there were only two options left:

    I kill myself or I kill him.

    Losing this job could be equal to the first option as I have a lot of things to pay. But know I won't kill myself bcoz I know death can stop one thing - my insurance premium. Also I tell myself the 2nd Option is worse as it is not worth it to commit such a crime.

    In the end, with my colleague's help, I handed in over 90% of what my boss wants to see by 5 pm today. For the first time this week, I left office on time. I dunno what will happened when he gets all my work on Monday. After I left, he sms me to attend a meeting tomorrow with my other bosses. I lan lan agreed.

    This is the first time in my life I felt so scared of losing all everything bcoz of my job. I have a study bond so I can't leave. I really regret not applying for the transfer I wanted since last year.

    I know no one can help me. I just pray hard I survive this episode. I am really stressed out and although I am at home now, the work stress almost the same as I left my office.

    Show me the bright side.....and I am just glad I have sgforums to pour everything out.

    5 Feb 05, 07:38
    Make sure you save all the threatening emails. You might need them one day.
    Also, you might want to try and find out if anyone has been carrying tales behind your back resulting in this situation. 2 promotions in 3 years is bound to cause some green eyes. I've seen this kind of thing happen before. Be very careful of what you say it in the office. It can travel to your boss, with some creative editing.

    If you need to say anything to your boss, or report on anything, try to make sure you do it in person or email, never let anyone else carry the message for you. It took me a while to realise that every time I ask a colleague to pass a message, or even the boss's secretary, the message would inevitably get edited, in a negative way.

    Real life example:

    Me: hrrmm...ok, this might take some time, I'll probably need until lunchtime to complete it. Let boss know.

    What message bearer told boss: He said last minute give him very rushed, he cannot finish it on time. I think there is something wrong with his system. There is room for improvement.

    Worse, I'd found that some will even claim credit for what I did.
