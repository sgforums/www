    (by brand)

    NOKIA
    no doubt the besr-selling household brand...user-frenly..easy navigation...cool design...
    hot favs include N8210 (no longer in distribution thought), N8250 (blue is cool...the oni 8x series with composer) and N8850 (premium model of nokia, enuff said)
    affordable one will be N3310 or N3330 (if u need WAP)...got composer, SMS chat...
    if u are more tech-savvy..can take a look at N9210...mini-notebook, palm, fone...all in one..with colour somemore...
    for SMS fanatics...get N5510...full qwerty keyboard for SMS n gaming convenience...can load mp3..integrated radio
    N8310...errhhh...not relli recommended...the 1st batch is full of problems...thought the radio and GPRS are the selling point...(but, do u relli need them?)

    ERICSSON
    bery trusted..kopitiam towkay's handy 688...drop from 3rd floor oso no spoil...okok..tt was like...last millenium??..
    the new ones...T-series...from T28 onwards...all the models are similar in design and features...but ericsson is quite famous for its auto-off probs and fone kena "hang"...most established foundation and network in 3G though..(T6..lets keep our fingers crossed on this...

    SAMSUNG
    samsung has the most number of features and functions compared to other brands...WAP-enabled ones like N200 and A200 are a big hit with samsung loyal supporters...light and small...however...accessories are difficult to find...often have to wait for some time for shipment to come in...composer, games, voice-activation are a few of the popular functions...

    SIEMENS
    navigation not as frenly as nokia...but has its own pool of supporters...but they got weather-resistant+WAP model...not bad..
    S40, SL45 and S45(new) are the high-range ones...got WAP and/or mp3...however..they are battery-suckers...

    MOTOROLA
    the V-series...bery problematic..oni for gentle users...the flip thing spoil bery easily...a bit too rough and the connector broke liao then tts it...repair not worth it...might as well buy a new fone...but their selling point is small and light and handy...but i dun understand y they mus always have antennas...
    those gin-nah ones like T190, T189 are quite cute...and affordable oso...but more popular with young ladies...cos...errhhh CUTE loh..

    PANASONIC
    GD90, GD92, GD93....auto-off prob...LCD flicker...tok halfway the whole thing shut off...are the common complaints at their service centres...no doubt interface quite cute...multi-colours...composer...

    ALCATEL
    not navigation-frenly...limited features oso...501(WAP) is not bad...but the shape a bit farnie...not bery popular in singapore...

    tts abt all...
    thanx fer reading and may u choose the best for u and ur pocket
