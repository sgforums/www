    24 Dec 06, 12:51
    Agreed that you should approach Recruit Express at ur own risk.. many of the interviewers are not at all famiilar with the industries and jobs their clients are offering and will often ask stupid questions / make stupid comments. They are, however, very good at getting you entry-level temp/part time jobs.

    I interviewed 4 times for the same job at Recruit. The 1st 3 times I went, the place was chaotic.. the overly made up female interviewers asked me all sorts of odd questions. The 4th time, I got lucky and spoke directly with an assistant manager.. once he heard my phone voice, he got me an interview, asked the right questions, and sent me straight to Starhub. I got the job and became a star performer.

    However after I changed jobs, I realised that the pay was actually considered below average for call centre jobs of that nature/workload.. Recruit deducted a large portion of my salary each month.

    I have, however, had good experiences with Adecco (they fought for my friend's rights when her employer tried to twist around the contract), GMPrecruit and Kelly Services. They're very professional, and they offer serious jobs with decent pay. I got my current job thru GMP, drawing above average pay for this job/industry and very happy indeed.
