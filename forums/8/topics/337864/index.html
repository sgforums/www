"No E-" Day - Proposal

  • Schahryar
    20 Nov 08, 22:42

    This may sound stupid, but I wrote a post in my weblog last month about a plan in my life. The article called "No E-" Day, and I suggested in this post to avoid of using any electronic services and devices like computer, internet and mobile for one day.

    I was talking to some friends days ago and almost all of them were interested to do that at least once a year.

    I thought this idea can be shared through the net and maybe there are many people to have interest about this "No E-" day proposal.

    I think nowadays everybody needs that as we are going to be addicted more and more to modern technology.

    So, on "No E-" day, you should follow this ToDo list:

    - Turn off your computer
    - Don't use Internet
    - Turn off your mobile
    - Don't watch TV
    - Don't play video-games
    - Don't blog!

    What do you think? Would you like to do that?
