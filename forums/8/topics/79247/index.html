    Here are some recipes from years of experience starting from my great great grand father, try it and you might come back for more.

    Campbell Maggi

    Boring of eating the same old Instant Noodle everyday? Want to impress your dream girl with dinner for two? Why spend so much time trying to cook Italian pasta when you can substitute it with something faster.

    Ingredients:

    Two Packet of Maggi Instant Noodle
    One Can of Campbell Soup
    One Household Candle

    Method:

    Put the can of Campbell Soup with one can of water in a cooking pot, heat and stir until it simmer (You will be able to see small bubbles floating out to the top of the soup). You can put the Instant Noodle in and cook it with the soup for three minutes or you can cook it separately and put the soup afterward. Light the Household Candle at the center of the table to create a romantic atmosphere; you might score extra points if you play some light music in the background. See, cooking is not that hard and you manage to did it in less than ten minutes.
