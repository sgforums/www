  • Larry
    6 Sep 04, 10:23
    Hard blades are those type of blades that are covered with plastic on the outside, with cushion on the inside. This would mean that the shape is generally fixed, becos of the hard casing outside.

    Soft blades are those blades that have some or no plastic covering on the outside. It's more comfortable as the soft material allows shaping of the blades to ur feet (after some time). However, if u are considering moving towards blading on the streets, then getting hard blades is a better choice. If u are those ECP leisure bladers, then u can consider getting a pair of soft ones.
