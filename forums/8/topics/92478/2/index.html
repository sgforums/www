    It's important to get a decent pair of comfy blades cuz if they dun fit well or a re poorly made, they'll hurt ur feet if u wear em too long. + they aren't durable & u'll find urself shopping very soon for a proper pair anyways.

    Shops I'd recommend are:
    - Hvper @ East Coast 64464648 : highly recommended, good service n range
    - Skater's World @ Queensway
    - Inline Culture
    - Skateline

    As for brands:

    Razor has some very decent ones that cost $129 - $179 but i dunno if they're still to be found, A lot of skate rental shops bought up the stocks to use as rental blades. Fila, Rollerblade & Salomon make a whole range of quality skates too depending on ur budget. Salomon is abit exx tho, like Nike; nice to see but more exx. Personally I use Rollerblades .... no complaints at all.
