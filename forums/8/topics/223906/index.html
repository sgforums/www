    How bad is your acne? How old are you? It could be just a phase (hormonal change) that you are going through and would get better after you reach certain age.

    I personally don't find the acne cream helps. The cream is usually very strong and tend to burn your skin.

    If it really bothers you, perhaps it's worth going back to see your dermatologist (skin specialist) to ask for something else.

    I mentioned in one of my earlier post that I used to have acne problems too and had tried trying cream and antibiotics but didn't work. So I went to see the skin specialist and was put on Roaccutane for 6 months and it has been good since.

    Roaccutane is very effective in terms of acne problems but can be expensive and it has some bad side effects such as depression, affecting your liver function causing cholesterol fluctuation, dry skin/lips/nose bleed, get sunburnt easily (advisable not to expose yourself to the sun while taking the medication), cause damage to fetus (therefore if you are a female you have to make sure you are not pregnant and not going to fall pregnant). Even after you completed the course, you need to wait at least 6 months or so before you consider to start a family.

    If you do decide to go on Roaccutane, make sure you do a liver function test (just a blood test) before starting taking it and every 6-8 weeks after started taking it. The specialist in Singapore may not get you to do it but make sure you insist on it as it is a very strong medication and can affect your liver.

    Good luck!
