    These are new:

    Bukit Timah -- created from parts of the old Bukit Timah GRC
    Joo Chiat -- created from East Coast GRC
    Nee Soon East -- created from Sembawang

    These remain the same:

    Hougang
    Nee Soon Central
    Potong Pasir

    This one grew:

    Macpherson takes two districts from Marine Parade

    These two shrank:

    Ayer Rajah gives one polling district to West Coast GRC
    Chua Chu Kang gives one district to Hong Kah GRC and two to Holland-Bukit Panjang GRC

    These are gone:

    Boon Lay -- goes to West Coast GRC
    Bukit Gombak -- split between Holland-Bukit Panjang, Jurong and Hong Kah GRCs
    Kampong Glam - now part of Jalan Besar GRC

    GROUP REPRESENTATION CONSTITUENCIES

    These are new:

    Holland-Bukit Panjang GRC -- formed from parts of Bukit Gomabk, Bukit Timah, Chua Chu Kang, Kreta Ayer-Tanglin, Sembawang and Tanjong Pajar
    Jurong GRC -- formed from parts of old Bukit Gombak, and parts of old Bukit Timah and Hong Kah GRCs
    Pasir Ris-Punggol GRC -- formed from parts of old Cheng San and Pasir Ris GRCs

    This remains the same:

    Toa Payoh-Bishan

    These have grown:

    Aljunied -- takes in parts of old Cheng San and Pasir Ris GRCs
    Ang Mo Kio -- takes in part of old Cheng San GRC
    East Coast -- takes in part of old Aljunied GRC
    Hong Kah -- takes in parts of old Bukit Gombak and Chua Chu Kang
    Jalan Besar -- takes in old Kampong Glam, and parts of old Kreta Ayer Tanglin GRC
    Marine Parade -- takes in parts of old Aljunied and Ang Mo Kio GRCs
    Tampines -- takes in parts of old Pasir Ris GRC
    Tanjong Pagar -- takes in parts of old Kreta-Ayer Tanglin GRC
    West Coast -- takes in old Boon Lay, parts of old Ayer Rajah, and old Bukit Timah and Hong Kah GRCs

    These are gone:

    Bukit Timah GRC
    Cheng San GRC
    Kreta Ayer-Tanglin GRC
    Pasir Ris GRC
