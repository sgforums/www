    understand the difference between studying in a poly and a jc 1st.

    in JC, you're just doing the same things you did in pri and sec sch; general subjects, albeit more advanced ones, but still do not serve you any good in a working environment.

    lastly. a degree is not necessary. neither is it a means to an end.
