    well, the Olympics draw near again, and who will be watching?

    not me.. coz the Olympics have lost everything it stands for...

    just look at the IOC.. until 2001, the presidency was held by the right hand man of Franco! if you look at the members of the IOC, you'll see corruption, criminals and basically the dregs of humanity holding court...

    the delegates of the IOC are not appointed by the countries they represent but by the IOC themselves... more than ever, the members show themselves to be ruthlessly corrupt and the most spectacularly blatant corruption happened in Salt Lake City for the 2002 winter olympics..

    well, remember the Ben Johnson incident? according to a few investigations, it was a screwup that he was tested positive... he wasn't supposed to be... and according to the investigations, most of the field of that fateful day was on drugs... including Carl Lewis (this allegation was proven when Dr Wade Exum(a anti-doping official who resigned in disgust at the corruption) revealed documents showing that about 100 athletes were allowed to go on after being tested positive while the USOC covered everything up)

    apart from that, did anyone remember the Olympic games in Korea? i personally remembered watching boxer Roy Jones beating Park Si-Hun half to death yet losing the bout on POINTS! a fix was never so blatant!

    well, the Olympics may still have the sheen of "The Greatest Sporting Event" but to those who scratch the surface, we see that it is just a facade... and the inside is rotting with corruption and worse..

    will you be watching it?

    i won't...
