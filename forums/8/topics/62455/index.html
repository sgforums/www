    Hi...Well, there is actually no SIM logo 'chopped' onto the UOL degree, only the indication that the degree was awarded via an External program. Degree is the same for all institutes SIM SIC or PSB.

    Slight misconception down here, there are no definate 'textbooks' in the UOL program but 'Essential Reading Materials'. The materials that they're sending (it's actually UOL-LSE that's the one doing the sending) are "Study Guides" which are in no way a sub to the essential reading materials...They ship all the way from London...and are means of allowing the student to grasp the basic concept of wad to look out for in this sub..

    UOL lecturers only fly in once per year from London to conduct Revision Workshops after the Mock Exams, which is another exclusive feature to SIM...
    These exams are the closest thing u can ever get to a real UOL exam...
