    The Pilates Method Today
    The Pilates Method is not just a series of exercises, but a complete approach to developing body awareness and an easy physicality in day-to-day life. It requires commitment from both the teacher and the client as it takes time to improve and change the body's postural and alignment habits.

    Osteopaths, physiotherapists and general practitioners are now recommending Pilates as one of the safest forms of exercise today.

    It is beneficial for:

    General fitness and body awareness

    Injury prevention

    Improvement of technique for athletes and dancers

    Remedial and rehabilitation work

    Pregnancy: ante and post natal

    The elderly

    For children from 12 years old
    Pilates helps to:

    Increase and create a balance between strength and flexibility
    Create an awareness of and strengthen dynamic stability
    Improve coordination
    Release stress
    Improve posture
    The Pilates method is taught as matwork and/or studio work.

    Pilates matwork is usually taught in a group and offered in many dance, fitness and health centres. Some Pilates studios have a separate space for teaching matwork classes. Pilates has grown increasingly more popular over the last decade and as a result many exercise teachers incorporate Pilates exercises in their classes. However, without the appropriate training and qualification the teacher is not necessarily a Pilates teacher. Pilates is a holistic and unique approach to exercise, enhancing a philosophy of principles and fundamentals, which set the method aside from many other forms exercise.

    In a Pilates studio one uses specialised spring resistance Pilates equipment, which tones, mobilises and strengthens the body and places little stress on the joints. Studio sessions should always be taught under supervision and the class should be tailored to the individual's need. It is standard that a client fills out a medical history form before the start of the first session and gives the teacher all the relevant medical information that is asked for.
