How to unlock 20% net speed hold by WinXP-pro

  • Qkool
    24 Jul 04, 20:06
    WinXp had secretly limit 20% of our broadband speed.
    so we had to cancel the 20% .
    *because i using WinXp-pro,so i only remember the unlock for WinXp-pro only. sorry for WinXp-home.

    frist, close all program.

    press [start] > [run] > enter 'gpedit.msc' > [ok]

    Look on the left-top under [Computer Configuration] , press 1 click to [Administrative Templates] .

    Look at rightside of the pages, you will see [Network] ,2X click on [Network].
    now same area, look for [QoS packet scheduler], 2X click it.

    now you are very near to finish.

    look for [limit reservable bandwidth] , one click on [limit reservable bandwidth]
    READ the desciption which wrote on the middle
    (just to understand what are you doing).

    After that, 2X click on [limit reservable bandwidth], you will see
    @ Not Configured
    @ Enabled
    @ Disabled

    press [Enabled], and set your bandwidth limit % to [0%].

    Press [apply] and [ok] .

    [RESTART] our pc.
