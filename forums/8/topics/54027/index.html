    actually, energizer vs duracell nearly totally depends on the capacity since both are 1.5v alkalines.

    if you didn't know, there are many versions, types and makes of energizer batteries made in different countries. this results in varying capacities. same for duracell.

    a typical energizer/duracell would last around 3000mah.
    a mid-range rechargeable battery lasts about 1600mah or so.
