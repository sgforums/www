    I should add that RMIT is a good uni, as far as BBBA is concerned I'm not too sure. On campus learning is definitely a big booster in terms of CV, there is a stigma (and it's true) that distant learning isn't half as effective or efficient. I'll say for sure that HR @ Big4 don't take too kindly to any distant learning courses, I have a friend who's from UQ on site finance and he landed a job as a team leader in portfolio mngment (big $$$); another friend of mine who was very insistent that distance or not employers couldn't tell, did a LSE course back home and is still unemployed as we speak, has been for past few months now. If you can, go overseas, it's a good experience and will increase your sellability, even more so if it's a top uni, if not, I'd rather you aim to go into SMU or SIM, which I feel are equally good unis when compared to overseas business schools. I'll go to the extent of saying that they are better than NUS/NTU's business schools for sure, and you definitely will have a future provided you play your cards.

    Alot of people here feel that paper qualifications do matter, and I'll say for sure that they do, they increase the chances of landing you that interview, and that's it. Once inside, it's up to you to prove yourself. Employers look for experience and past results. If you put a Monash Biz grad & RMIT Biz grad together, no doubt the Monash Biz grad will get the interview, but if the RMIT Biz grad has good experience, they'll be on equal standing, or the RMIT grad will have the upperhand. Either way they'll probably call both anyway, so I'll just say for sure now, that don't worry too much about your degree, think more about your skills, experience, past results, and what you can bring to the table that will have a company hire you.

    Paper qualifications matter when you're starting out to land that interview, beyond that, not really. What is more important is you don't f uck up when you're working, then, even if you're from harvard, no one will give 2 sh its.

    Good luck and all the best
