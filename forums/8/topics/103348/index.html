    Hi, u can't compare Singapore water to any places in the world. Singapore water is pretty unique to me, ok, the vis is terrible, usually around 1.5m or best at abt 9m, plenty of slit tat u may feel u r doing a nite dive at times, current changes and pick up very fast. However, if u dive in Singapore often, u will be a very good diver as u can handle these situation easily, and there's are these sayings - "If u know how to dive in Singapore, u will know how dive anywhere in the world" and "Singapore's water trained the best diver in the world". Dun take Singapore water for scenary, treat it as a training. To be a good diver, its not to see how much dives u log or years of experience, but to see how much u learn from each dive! If u always dive in clear and safe water like Tioman under swimming pool condition, how can u handle strong current and no vis condition?
