    Okay, I wasn't a great fan of the PC/PS game but I did play the first two series (2nd one sux)

    As for the show......The opening I already try so hard not to laugh......There we see a large-breasted heroine having a gun battle with one of the robots and doing all the somersaults.......pretty much like the game itself....and wow! the bullets are mah cham like unlimited one.

    Action wise...dunno leh....like any typical action movie loh...all the explosions, bullets flying, daredevil stunts, jumps and falls...etc

    Story-wise......beginning quite okay but the later part quite sian.....a bit like game story one.

    As for the character....Anglina really look like Lara Croft(the main character in the game) The well toned legs, sausage lips the the "enchanced" bust. :p The Butler is younger in the movie than in the game....the whiz kid I din see in the game though. The house looked like the one in the game too.

    Conclusion: I've seen better action movies...and more meaningful ones too....this one is just milking the Tombraider fanchise that is popular only in the gaming world of the US. The action is so so only and the computer effects are nothing special too. There's just nothing in the movie that cuts above the rest......I want my $8.50 back!!

    But if you are a Tombraider fan.....you cannot miss this.

    2.5 out of 5
