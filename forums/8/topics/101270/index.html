    To learn Martial Art? What is the intent? For self defence? For self defence, there are various forms. Kickboxing is one of the sports that teach self defence?

    kops21 have practise TKD during younger days, and is exposed to various forms of martial arts, physically tested it or through friends(martial art kaki) experiences.

    Different Martial Arts are strong/trained in different parts of the body. IMHO, for people that are 20 yrs old and below picking up a sport that focusing of legs, should still be okay, as their flexibility is still fine. For folks after that age, your bone structure is stiffer and it is harder to train. Going for sports like Karate(focusing on hands and reflexes), should be more easier for those that are not a "spring chicken".... can u imagine asking someone that is 40-50yrs old picking a martial arts that are physically challenge. These groups of people are normally more comfortable with Qigong.

    Everyone has his requirement, depending on your age and physically attributes and fitness should choose a sport that suit him/her accordingly.
