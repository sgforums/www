    When i was a wee little boi... my cousin and i used to spend our weekends attempting to catch the birds.

    I think we had a couple of successes but i only remember one clearly. Basically, i prepared for the weekend by scaterring grain on the killing ground every single day for 5 days. Then on Saturday, we laid out trap. It consisted of a rectagular plastic basket, a long piece of string, and a couple of slippers.

    Basically we would suspend the basket above the bait using the string, and then we tied a butterfly knot to hold the basket in place. We used the slippers as extra weight so the basket would not bounce up when it fell.

    Then we hid behind some curtains and waited. Bird came. Ate grain, we released the knot, basket came down and there you go. Bird in a cage. had a few unsuccessful attempts but we got there in the end.

    You could try the stick propping up a basket trap as well, but i think you need a big basket for that to work since the birds will usually just stick the necks in to peck at the food. This means that they will usually have time to escape before the basket comes crashing down.

    Or you could just buy a mouse trap or an air rifle (if that is legal, of course)
