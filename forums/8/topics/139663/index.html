    R u intending to play ur ipod in ur car?
    If so, tune ur car's fm to a free "empty" channel, eg. 88.7fm;
    plug in iTrip to ur ipod, go to the iTrip stations & select the same 88.7fm. Once u select, ur ipod will start playing. Play into abt 2 seconds, "PAUSE" it.
