    Originally posted by Seifer:
    You must set ur phone language to Chiness first. Then when u type a message, u will specify in hanyu pinyin.

    follow the following steps..

    1. Press MENU.
    2. Go to SETTINGS.
    3. Choose PHONE SETTINGS.
    4. Go to LANGUAGE.
    5. Choose CHINESE (Jian Ti Zhong Wen) dun choose (Fan Ti Zhong Wen) it cant work.
    6. Go back to main. got M1-GSM logo.
    7. Go to MESSAGES.
    8. Choose WRITE MESSAGES.

    you are now in Hanyu pinyin mode..
