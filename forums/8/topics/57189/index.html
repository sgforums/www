Sentosa Holiday Chalets suck!

  • BillyBong
    16 Sep 03, 01:51
    I recently booked a Sentosa holiday chalet and completlely regreted it. Please do not...i repeat do not book the Sentosa Holiday chalets under any circumstances....

    What you see on the internet is a far cry to what you actually get. In fact, to sum it up in one word, the place is a dump.

    1. The floor was so sticky i had to mop it b4 my guests arrived.

    2. The price for a 12-bedder cost an astonishing S$208/-!

    3. The list of items i was given included a VCR among other things. I was not given one and there was no effort by the staff to provide a replacement during my stay despite my bringing the matter to the duty staff. In fact half the bed sheets were dirty after they were supposedly clean and packed!

    4. The BBQ pit was in such a state of filth that i was glad i ordered catered food instead of BBQ food.

    5. The chalet that was given to me stated an isolated block by itself. I was indeed given a single chalet blk without other sentosa holiday chalets adjacent. Instead i had the people's association chalets joined to my unit!

    There is apparenly alot of misrepresentation on their website prior ot my renting the unit. While i did not expect the holiday inn crown plaza standard, i was at least expecting a clean place to stay.

    The dejected look of the place summed it up so much i actually stood outside the chalet wondering if i'd been conned.

    Though they claim they're doing a face lift soon, i doubt so much as a paint job by their administration.
