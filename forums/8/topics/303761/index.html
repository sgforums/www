    Top investment banker at a top investment bank you'll need:

    At least a degree from an Ivy league or a school of an equivalent standard, and then some.

    An IQ of 160, technically you'll need to be a genius.

    Basically the top big name bankers are the cream of the crop, maybe only the top 1%. Good luck.
