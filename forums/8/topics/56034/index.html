    By Tamudder Goose



    Pussycat, Pussycat
    Where have you been?
    I've been to Geylang
    Lorong Seventeen.

    Pussycat, Pussycat
    What did you there?

    I went to chase kway, lor!
    You donno, meh?



    Three blind mice! Three blind mice!
    See how they vote! See how they vote!
    They all voted the gah'men prat
    Just so they could upgrade their flat
    Have you ever seen anything quite so sad
    As three blind mice?



    Mary had a little lamb
    Little lamb, little lamb
    Mary had a little lamb
    Who was a nincompoop
    He followed her to the food court
    The food court, the food court
    He followed her to the food court
    And now he's kambing soup



    Kookaburra sits on the old gum tree
    Merry, merry king of the bush is he
    Laugh, Kookaburra, laugh
    Kookaburra gay your life must be
    Kookaburra just can't sing no more
    Kookaburra's jailed in Changi, lor
    Gay, Kookaburra, gay
    Is against the law in Singapore!

    Little Miss Muffet
    Went to a buffet
    Hoping to eat tau hway
    But along came a waiter
    Who quietly told her,
    "Today only got teochew muay!"



    Hush-a-bye, J.B.
    In parliament
    Skarly you're sued
    By the government
    They won't panchan you
    Though you are small
    Down they want J.B.
    Party and all.



    Humpty Dumpty
    Sat on a wall
    Humpty Dumpty
    Had a great fall
    All the king's horses
    And all the king's men
    Couldn't put Humpty Together again.

    So how now like that?
    It's damn obvious, lah.
    They scooped up poor Humpty
    And made some orh luah.



    Jack and Jill
    Went Canning Hill
    To marry for a flat
    But soon, of course,
    They both divorced
    And the HDB took it back.
