    sth to do with binary ba.

    100 gives u a 7 bit binary number ma.

    assign 1 rat to a bit, eg rat 1 is assigned to the msb, rat 7 assigned to lsb.

    den write down the no of each bottle in binary.

    den, let's take 17.

    the binary for 17 is 0010001. den make rat 3 and rat 7 drink the wine. i.e if a bit is 1, den the rat assigned to that bit must drink the wine.

    do this for all the bottles lo.

    after 1 day line the rats up again, in their bit order.

    dead one u put 1, alive one 0.

    den u'll get the number in  binary.

    convert back to decimal and voila!
