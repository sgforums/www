    Old man staring very hard at young man. young man staring in another direction.

    OLD MAN, " you definitely remind me of some one I used to know, his name is Chen Ah Du, Who is Chen Ah Du to you?"

    Background music: dramatic sounds.

    YOUNG MAN, "Chen Ah Du is my father."

    Background music: dramatic sounds again.

    Young Man still not looking at old man, staring even harder at empty air, Five seconds later.

    YOUNG MAN, "I am his SON!."

    Background music: Even more dramatic sounds.

    dud.......

    1) Actors would always say their "important" lines not making eye contact with one another. One would be staring and the other staring at another place.

    4) However deadpan the dialogue is, some one in the editing room feels that a bit of dramatic music would make it better,

    what type of audience is this dead pan show aiming at?
