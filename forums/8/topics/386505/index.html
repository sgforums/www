  • ShrodingersCat

    Well.. I think first, you need to really enjoy singing.

    Enjoy the music, enjoy the feelings, enjoy sharing music with your friends and don't think of singing as a performance, as something you are 'showing' to people, but just as a part of yourself. Have a GOOD TIME!

    Don't be a 'wanna be' - just BE someone who loves to sing, and enjoys the expression of music. Everything else will come along~ whether it is joining a music school or a competition.

    Then it wouldn't matter if you are good or bad (and since people have praised you, at the very least, you can carry a tune!) and you lose the consciousness and awkwardness that accompanies a  'performance'.
