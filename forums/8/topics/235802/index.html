    Learning should be two way.

    The receptive end (aka, layman term = student) and the giving end (layman term = teacher) are learning from each other.

    The "student" gains knowledge from the "teacher", while the "teacher" also gets feedback from the "student" and learn more from the "student".

    Not the "one-way ticket" teaching method that Singapore is imposing now.
