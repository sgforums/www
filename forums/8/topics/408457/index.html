Young Designers Awards 2010 @ Heeren

  • yapss
    18 Aug 10, 14:53

    The Heeren presents the Young Designer Awards (YDA) 2010 - the search for the next front runner in design and the fashion industry!

    YDA is back for the 2nd year running in a 3 weekd long competition happening at the Heeren Shops. 

    Fridays 27 August, 3rd Sept and 10 Sept  (Flea Market)

    Saturdays 28 August, 4 Sept and 11 Sept (Grand Finals)

    Open to all students in Singapore, each 4-member group must go through sounds that will be carried out in this format: 

    Contestants will be judge on various challenges that will test their fashion savvy-ness, their ability to spot and emulate trends as well as their ability to sketch creative and trendy looks. 

     

    On top of that, a Flea Market Festival will be held at the outdoors of The Heeren (area in front of Spinelli's) on 10 September from 5pm to 10pm. Semifinalists will be given the opportunity to display their very own creations and sell to the public. 

     

    Top teams stand to win up to $1000 worth of Heeren Shops vouchers.

     

    For more information and participation, email ann@punch.com.sg with the title'I am a Young Designer'. 

    See you there!
