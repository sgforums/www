    First of all thereÂ’s not much a different on Export, Import or Parallel set hps as you call it. Mobile phones are class in two cat Asia & Euro set only.

    Box Packaging.

    Secondly, for TEES as in Telecommunication Equipment Standards most mobile phones in SG is Type Approval by IDA for local use with or without IDA stickers is fine; unless specific hardware with system bandwidth like: iDEN 800, PDC, WCDA or CDMA 800 MHz...etc.(Canada, Japan,US, Korea). The Fact is all Equipment Hardware Manufactures does not label their hardware for country specific approval, IDA's label are purchase by appointed & authorize distributors at IDA office with the necessary documents and will do the labeling of IDA & warranty seal before the sells of goods for the respective country market. Warranty seal are provided by the respective appointed service center.

    As for Warranty and Insurance terms; it cover against defects in material or workmanship, covering repairs in Mattel's workshop, including cost of parts and labor for a validity date of 12months from the date of purchase.

    Exclusions: Warranty does not cover repairs in case of breakage or misuse, normal wear and tear. Also it will be dim void if Unauthorized repair or installation contrary to recommended procedures, Accidental damage, due to water and moisture, negligence, or electrical hazards, Unauthorized modifications to the Product or repairs made by a third party, in terms of both software and hardware, Use of non-original spare parts and Cases of force majored.

    Case to case basic; most appointed service center like NPC, Accord, ServiceOne will acknowledge your hardware as long there is a untamper local warranty seal, with or without warranty card. All service center, can identify your hardware equipment country of origin, batch number, IMEI & warranty period be it Asia or Euro set.

    Last, the price different between Asia & Euro set; most Euro set are cheaper because the are imported from another country like UK, Dubai, india..etc hence they do not support certain Equipment setting & languages. Also the importer can slash prices compare to the stand RRP set by phone distributors.
    Certain parallel Asia set which comes from country like HK, Taiwan, Thailand...etc. tends to have a very different system & language setting. Most of these set does not carries a local warranty seal or card; which is why the selling price is cheaper.

    As such, the above captioned should answer most of your quires; should you require additional info; you know where to get me!
