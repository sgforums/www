    Dust mites are micro organisms that are actually good bugs. They clean up after our shedded dead skins. They don't suck blood. The only problem is that too much of them can cause allergy and other respiratory problems.

    Bed bug are different. The word bed bug is a misnomer because they don't infest only the bed but also other areas. Because they are commonly found on bed, hence 'bed bug'. Once saw one on a bus between the top of the seat lining. I quickly came down the bus. It is not just bed they infest.

    Gone to the net and did some checking. It seems like bed bugs originated from trees where they feed on the blood of bird, bat and other animals. So when they infest the home of human, they are usually found in dark and wooden areas. So the word 'bloody wood bug' would seem more appropriate.
