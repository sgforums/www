    From Sunday 15 Feb, feeder services 321 and 326 will be merged to become trunk service 113, while feeder services 322 and 327 will be merged to become trunk service 112.

    Do you think this is an appropriate decision by SBS Transit? Shouldn't the feeder services be merged into intra-town services as the new services are plying only within Hougang town?

    Note: Feeder services and intra-town services connect between neighbourhood of a town to the town centre or another neighbourhood, while majority of trunk services connect one town to another.
    Feeder services and intra-town services charge fare of 63 cents using ezlink card regardless of distance travelled, while the fare of trunk services increase with distance travelled.
