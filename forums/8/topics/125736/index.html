    Anyone remembers those times around 10 years back when fewer guys had dyed hair and maybe its just simple dyed single colour Golden or brown....most of them emphasised on hair cut and styling using the humble gel...They had long fringes combed up and thick front hairlines which bulge out.......Either super long and thick sideburns like John Travolta. e...or sideburn with varying degree of texture and thickness mildly resembling the 70s clubbing scene

    2nd type there was somehow this Leonardo Dicarprio/Ricky martin hairstyle which used to be common in the early 90s teenage guys which somewhat disappeared when come to the millenium....very long fring combed backwards and flat or pointing short fringe....No sideburns at the side and no scrapes...all uniform and medium length hair..those hairstyles were actually retro borrowed from the 1950s/60s...it appeared again in the 80s and somehow went fashionable in the 90s.....and there was the rocker hairstyle like Kurt Cobain's for those into rock music....The hair is grown long to the shoulders and unkept which were popular among non chinese guys at the time together with goaties or beard..or tied up into a ponytail...back in sec school days, its always the malay and indian guys who sport this look and get caught by the diciplne master often....

    The 3rd type used to be American type Marines kru-cuts/scrape with some logos "imprinted" at the back of the head like the NIKE tick.. which is actually a bald spot shaped like the desired logo..
    dont know how they manage to do that in the barber shops but a few minority had these wild haircuts.... Definetly those were wild times for hair....and most of the guys always cut at the malay barbers instead of saloons now...

    Back int he 90s it was more of a caucasian trend while nowadays its more J-pop/Ah gua "Butch" trend which i think suck......
