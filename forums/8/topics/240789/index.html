    hydrogen is dangerous.. it's helium...

    and no.. they rise and the balloon expands due to the drop in pressure up there..

    P1V1 = P2V2

    P1 = sea-level pressure
    P2 = pressure as it floats up..

    as it goes up, the pressure decreases therefore P2 drops.. V2 must increase as P1V1 is a constant...

    as V2 keeps increasing, there is a limit to the stretch of the balloon.. once the limit is breached, the balloon explodes...
