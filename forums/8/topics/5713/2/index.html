    Heh heh well it is interesting to see that most pple play console RPGs over anything else... I fear the old pencil and paper RPGs are perhaps dying a slow death.

    It would be hard to consider console RPGs true RPGs as the only character development you get is from levelling up.... In console RPGs the only way for your level 1 character to kill a say.... dragon would be unless you 1) cheated or 2)it was event driven, meaning it WAS supposed to happen

    However in pen & paper RPG, a level 1 character(or a group) would be able to kill a high level monster. Not by direct combat but perhaps by conceiving some feasible idea(i.e. luring the monster into a trap).

    Therein lies the magic of pen and paper RPG, coz you are not bounded by a preprogrammed environment(unless ur DM sucks that is) You choose how your character develops and how u choose to interact with your group member and NPCs. Almost anything is possible in pen and paper RPG(again depending on ur DM) where your imagination is the limit!
