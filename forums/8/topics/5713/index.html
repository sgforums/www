    As you all can guess...I'm very bored...

    Just wondering what do most of you think of the computer/video RPGs in the market these days.

    I can consider myself growing up on RPGs, back to the days of the good old pen and paper Dungeon and Dragons to computer RPGs like Might and Magic, The Bard's Tale, Ultima, Wizardry etc etc.

    I've tried out the "new generation" of RPGs like Diablo, Final Fantasy VII, Balder's Gate etc etc, but somehow I just have this feeling that they do not make RPGs like they used to. In fact I would only consider Balder's Gate a true RPG, though the former two are classified as such (I'd say they are more like Arcade and Adventure respectively). Worst still were the updated versions of the old series, who come across as games who are trying to capture the new market, yet trying to retain their traditions. Unfortunately they fell flat on their faces on both accounts. In fact, you can say they are the worst of both worlds.

    Perhaps its just me who's not accepting the change to my beloved computer gaming genre; or it could be just that the new RPGs are unable to reproduce the same magic that enchanted "old gamers" like us. Either way, I've already moved on in life away from RPGs onto other things. But I do keep track on what's going on from time to time

    Well...just thinking out loud.
