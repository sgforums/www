  • nismoS132
    21 Mar 05, 22:19
    there is no intel is best or amd is best thing, they each have their pros and cons, depending on your needs.

    intel chips may be hotter, but they have a built in auto safe where it will shut down if it's over heating. amds don't. and these are the kind of things you don't want to happen halfway through a render job. you can compensate by using alot of fans, or even a water cooled system, but there's no guarantee that it won't break down, and once your amd is fried, that's it. kiss that deadline bye bye.

    the newer amd64 chips don't seem to have much of a overheating problem though, haven't really used it to the max yet. see how bah.

    if you're building a server, intel's xeon chips is still currently king, compared to amd's xp or mp chips.

    but for the general user doing games or office work, none of the above matter. price is king. go for amd. just remember to stock up on the fans. 1 in 1 out.
