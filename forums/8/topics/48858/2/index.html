    when the time comes, a person has to choose.. is their commitment to the family that gave him life, or to the one he is planning and going to start a life with...

    that is one of the big choices in life... parents do not make it easier with their demands... and if the demands are too great, the person will end up with neither...

    Love as shown by the parents, MUST include letting go... recognising that the child WILL leave the nest sooner or later.. if they seek to hold on to the child and keep treating the child as one, instead of an adult who is an individual, there will always be that unspoken chain that holds and strangles the child...

    then, what commitment can that child give to his spouse? when he is held back by the parents?

    strangely, if the parents let go, the child is free to choose, the commitment to his spouse will be strengthened, and love for his parents and to his spouse's parents (if they do the same) is also strengthened...

    how many parents do really let go? how many have the courage to do the right thing? how many children's lives are ruined because of parents who do not let go?

    i wonder...

    love between 2? that's the foundation of any family.. that is where it starts... if a couple find that, that is where everything starts.. and any good parent would know that this is where it started for their own family... would they squander this most precious commitment between 2 for some selfish hold on their child? unfortunately, too many think so..

    alas...
