    Firstly, a business must be sustainable. The dotcom bubble burst becos most dotcom owners just wanna go into the industry and try to earn a quick buck, sell the business away then get out, there is no committment to make it last.

    Secondly, unless u can get a product that is totally new, it is almost likely that whatever u sell, u will have competitors. The idea is to do things differently and to serve the customers better than the competitors do. Think DELL. When they came into the computer market, it was well saturated with giants like HP, Compaq and IBM etc...but look at where they are today, making these big guns scurrying and panicking. Why? becos they did things differently. They cut down costs by selling directly to consumers, of cos there are many gritty details i wun go into here but the idea is to serve ur consumers better than ur competitors.

    Thirdly, understand ur TARGET consumers. Understand their values and beliefs, it will affect their purchase behaviour.
