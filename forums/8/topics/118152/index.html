    Hi all,
    In general, there are two types of claim in insurance, namely reimbursement and compensation

    Reimbursement normally works for medical expenses. Regardless of how many insurance policy you have, when comes to medical expenses claim, you can only claim up to how much you have spent, after minus all deductable and co-insurance.

    Some medical insurance has hospital income. good luck to you. if you have 5 insurances which has S$100 daily hospi income, u can claim S$500 per day in total.

    Compensation works for life, critical illness, disability insurance. if you have 3 life insurance with sum assured of S$50k each, when you die, your wife will get S$150 in total. same goes for critical illness and disability insurance.
