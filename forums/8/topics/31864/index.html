    I did not know this until i read the papers. Cash cards are valid onli for 5 years.

    CASHCARD users can now find out when their cards expire by sending an SMS.

    All they need to do is key in an SMS message with the word 'expiry', followed by the card's 16-digit identification number and send it to 66888.

    Advertisement

    They will receive a reply via SMS in a few seconds which states the month and year that the CashCard will expire.

    Besides this, expiry dates can be checked by calling the Nets Call Centre on 6274-1322 or taking note of the warning messages on the point-of-sale terminals. These can also be checked at www.nets.com.sg

    In a press statement, Nets advised those who have CashCards issued before 1999 to check their card's expiry date.
