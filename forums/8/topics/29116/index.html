    for ur question, erm... it is not true that if u have a toothache means that ur toth is gonna drop.. this is a myth given by those mothers who are trying to scare their kids off from eating too much cold stuff..

    erm.. there are afew reasons on why u will have a toothache.. let mi guide u one by one

    1) Smoking.. Do u smoke? or rather, do u smoke regularly or "heavily"? Smoking harms ur gum in which will affect the postion of ur tooth. The gum will lose the role of holding the tooth of yours eventually and thus, the tooth wont have a secure place to stay intact. The movement of ur tooth will cause some pain in ur gum and thus, a toothache has occur..

    2) Cold Stuff.. erm.. Do u drink alot of cold drinks or eat alot of cold food? Do you know that both of the above mentioned, can hurt ur tooth and causing it to have a toothache?

    3) Infection.. erm.. Do u brush ur teeth the proper way? Improper way of brushing teeth can lead to improper cleaning of ur mouth. When a tooth is not clean properly, it will secretes some acid into the gum and thus, causing the tooth to ache due to the fact that that particular tooth has low in acidity level.

    4) Wisdom Tooth.. Have u notice if there is any new tooth growing out at the side of ur tooth? If u do, it means that u have had a wisdom tooth. The arise of the new tooth is pushing and fighting for a place to grow.. Thus, u will gradually feel the pain.
