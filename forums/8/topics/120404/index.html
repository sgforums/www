    Dear Deluded ST staff,

    i welcome the decision of STI to go subscription-based.
    This provides me with one less bookmark on my buldging list of sites to visit and perhaps more quality time with my family.

    As far as e-marketing is concerned, i have to commend the amazing ability by your marketing team to piss off so many Singaporeans with the go subscription decision. In fact, no one i know is willing to fork out a single cent for STI.

    And like most existing subscribers, i laughed out loud when you sent us an email saying "We believe that we have a good and valuable product that users will want to pay for". For a change, ST journalists impressed me with their humour. Haha!

    By slashing your price by 50%, you have proven to us that your 'valuable product' is not really that valuable.

    By the way, kudos to your marketing team! In the event that i should need to do an analysis on a failed e-marketing campaign, i would consider using STI as a case study.

    Good luck lads! You will need them!

    Regards,
    From an EX-STI reader
