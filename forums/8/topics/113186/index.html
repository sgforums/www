    Been there....tt place basically nothing much...just total laid back and do nothing..enjoy the sea, the beach etc.

    activities u can try include island hopping (they will bring u to many kelongs), sunset cruise.. outsea night fishing...jungle trekking..u can also play beach volleyball there.. the beach is clean...the sea not too bad...snorkelling is great....lots of coral but not so much marine fishes. careful of the sea urchins which is everywhere..

    the accomodation is great. u can just opt for the cheapest room in the island and still enjoy reasonably good accomodation condition. One thing to note the water in the room has some weird smell.

    the ppl there are generally very friendly, be prepared for 3 man holding guitars welcoming u to sibu island when u first step on the island.

    overall i gave it 3.5 out of 5.

    Yeap the island is rather boring. Other than diving, there's nothing much to be done. No commercial set-ups, other than several villages over there and the resorts. Basically you'll either be lazing around the beach or be doing a little exploration of the island.

    I recommend Pulau Rawa. The waters are as clear as Sibu and Tioman. But in Rawa, you don't have to go diving to see corals or fishes! The fishes are plentiful, and so are the corals. You'll be able to see lots of corals by juZ snorkelling, no oxygen tank needed! Heard that it has undergone renovations, so the facilities there are now much better.
