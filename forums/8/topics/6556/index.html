    another important ingredient for a vibrant for arts scene is a critical mass. suffering the curse of a small population, the ardent arts-patronisng core is too small to substantiate any profit nor to provide critical feedback.

    its heartening to see the number of aspiring artists(es) here but careful soul-searching must prevail. the expected excitment and joy from pursuing an artistic existence must be measured against talent possessed. its all well and good to want to be an artist but as artists what do we have to offer the world which is already not there? do you have a voice that is unique, not only unique but relevant to a discerning audience? the arts is not a soft-option but the view prevails and continues to lures wide-eyed aspirants into an existence that may ultimately lead to disillusionment. the love for the arts, at the end of the day, must be distinguished from talent for the arts.
