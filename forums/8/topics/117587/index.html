    I think M should be OK.

    Dont buy S, coz it may be too small or too tight-fitting.

    For thin guys, it is good to buy a slightly bigger size, to project the image of a good body / hide the thinness. If it is tight, it is not good as it will show the thinness of ur body.

    And secondly, as guys grow older and gain more weight in the future, a bigger shirt will still fit, but a fitting shirt will become too small. Most guys gain more weight as they grow older, not lose weight. So a slightly bigger shirt will be good.

    Hope this helps.
