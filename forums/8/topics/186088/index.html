    It may be Irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), a very common problem amongst young adults, characterized by a stomach discomfort usually aft meal. It's a functional disorder (i.e. means it's not caused by any disease or organic obstruction like tumour) where the large intestine (colon) is sensitive and react strongly to a variety of stimulus , e.g. certain food like coffee, tea or spicy food and negative emotions like stress, anger & anxiety.

    Do u noticed a change in ur bowel habits and change in appearance of ur stools? The topic of one's bowel habits is a seldom discuss topic for obvious reasons but paying attention to them may actually save ur lives, given that the digestive tract is a common site for disease
