    Homebody's approach is a rather safe option, although it does have the disadvantage in that you could feel that you are attempting to hide some part of yourself. That seems to be the underlying problem in your initial post.

    I'm not sure what support groups are out there, but I suggest you source them out. Look at it this way - misery loves company. At the very least you will have people who have a good understanding of what your issues are, and who may be able to offer you some relevant advice.

    That said, at the age of 15, be aware that your sense of identity is undergoing a huge amount of change, and this has a number of implications. One is that your sexual orientation is far from being set, so you may be reading too much into things. Another is that whatever the nature of your sexuality is, you are in the process of fitting it in with all of the other parts of your personality, which ar themselves in a state of change. This is a very confusing and unsure time, and as a general principle, it is best to take your time in reaching any conclusions - the answer you reach today could well be inapplicable tomorrow.
