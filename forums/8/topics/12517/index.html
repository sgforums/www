    Virgin mobile's costs are exorbitant...
    maybe a simple comparison using singtel's charges as a benchmark

    singtel's max charge for excession is
    $0.20/min for peak hours... not to mention offpeak charges are lower.
    excess SMS's go at $0.05 an SMS

    virgin's normal flat rate for all calls is $0.20/min, which is the same as singtel's max excess charge, not to mention the $0.20 connection charge to add.
    they're SMS goes at $0.10 which is twice of singtel's _excess_ SMS,

    which makes virgin mobile extremely expensive...

    the reason why i used singtel as a benchmark is because Virgin is leasing Singtel's network-- buying 'bandwidth' from them. since virgin mobile has to earn money in the middle, virgin can never be as cheap as singtel, although they're services are comparable (since the network is the same)
