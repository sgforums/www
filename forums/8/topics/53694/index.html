    On Monday 7 July 2003 morning 8am++, I witnessed a commotion on bus 76 as I was on board that bus enroute to work. The Crowd is tremendous and the bus driver had stand up and shout "Oei, pls move back!!!" as there are lots of "statues" in the bus who refused to budge. There was an old lady who clinged on to the pole tightly which was near to the front door. She "zapped" her e-zlink card(Cash card for paying the bus fares) and there was a "de de de" meaning error. Then she zapped again. Error. Then the young 20+ male bus driver told her to zapped again. ERROR. Then the old lady said that she has enough money, how come there's an error. Then the bus driver told the old granny whether she had zapped the card or not and she said she did. The old lady patiently zapped again and again. There is another man seated 2 rows
    in front of me at the left hand side on the seat advised the old lday
    not to zapped again as it will double charged or start a new journay
    fare again.

    Then the old lady snapped back in mandarin and refuted the driver(very long)

    "Which eyes did you see me that I did not zapped the card. Don't
    anyhow say ok? You did not see my zapped the card and said that I did not zapped the card. Don't think that I'm dressed like that I don't have money. Not every Singaporeans who dressed expensively have money. Those who dressed raggedly have the most money. People so eager to kidnapped me for ransom ok! My sons and daughters are university graduates. You despised me is it because I dressed so simply is it? You look down on me because I don't have much education is it? I have lots of cars at home. My sons and daughters are selling cars. You young people don't anyhow say ok? I will call people to beat you up. You want to one on one is it? You come down from your driver seat and we go to one side and fight it out................"

    And the rattling goes on. My stop is here and I alighted from the bus. At time the bus is very crowded and how can the bus driver see whether the old lady has zapped the card? Ahysterical old lady or just a pure misunderstanding? Is the driver's fault or the illiterate old lady fault for "zappng" the card too fast?
