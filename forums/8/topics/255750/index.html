    Welcome to SGF Massive Online Text Game (SGFMOTG). Yunhaier will be your game master for this entire month as the game progresses.

    I will explain the rules below.

    Rank is based on: Fledging (Junior), Specialist (Member), Elder (Senior Member), Elite (P-Member)

    Level is determined by the level of years joined SGF

    Discipline: (P-member with post greater than 20k or senior member with post greater than 30k has ability to choose discipline Â– all abilities subjected to level of general)

    The following discipline available is as follows:

    Marshal: Improve troopÂ’s offense advantage
    Tactics: Chances for first strike Â– a bonus initial strike (Cancel out if both have first strike). First strike does not count as attack.
    Magician: Deal random elemental damage to enemy troops
    Skirmisher: Immune to first strike; concealed when assaulting realm.
    Calvary: Always succeed in fleeing, unless annihilated outrageously
    Cleric: Small chance of summoning avatar (Immune to magic, first strike and troops have no vulnerability).

    Players are allowed to name their own troops and decide what kind of troops they desire. Troop size is based on post counts. Troop size is calculated based on the first indication that forumer wants to play this game and I will take the figure (rounded off) from there. Note that once you are into this game, your troop size is fixed Â– it cannot grow even if your post count increases.

    People with less than 100 post count cannot participate in this game. Sorry.

    Type of troops includes Melee, Ranged, Ariel

    Melee units enjoy 25% advantage against Ranged units, but suffer 25% vulnerability against Aerial units.

    Ranged units enjoy 25% advantage against Ariel units, but suffer 25% vulnerability against Melee units.

    Ariel units enjoy 25% advantage against Melee units, but suffer 25% vulnerability against Ranged.

    ***

    Combat:

    Damage is based on troop size, level of general and combat roll done by me.
    General with less than 10% of original troop will always attempt to flee - Calvary discipline will always succeed in escape. A roll will be made to see if you succeed. If failed, entire troops will be crushed and player will be eliminated.

    There are Four realms to serve (top four forums). Lord of each realm goes to the highest level general (or largest troops if same level).

    The lords reserved the right to banish any general/s via PM to me. Any general desire to betray their realm to serve another can indicate via PM to me. (Although a traitor tag will be marked beside your name)

    Higher level commanders can Â‘mobilizeÂ’ lower level generals without their acknowledgment when invading other realms. (Of course if they die in course of battle and get pissed off about you using them in battle without their knowledge, you have to account for them). Lord of the Realm can mobilize everyone serving the realm into combat. (Therefore there can be passive players, completely being manipulated by the likes of higher levels General)

    For example yunhaier is a level 6 general and I can take general/s of level 5 and below with me into battle, but NOT those same level as me, unless I am the Lord of the Realm.

    All battles intended must be initiated via PM with me and telling me WHO the people involved. I will reveal the attack, but only general with Skirmisher discipline are concealed.

    E.g. (via PM)

    Yunhaier will lead an attack on Bar. I will bring the following people with me:
    Mooku
    Bontakun
    Sunrise

    Generals will fight until they win, fled or dead.

    Death is permanent in this game. Killing one general earns you one head trophy.

    No alliance/assistance between realms is allowed. Generals can only serve their own realms without getting involved in other peopleÂ’s battle. Unless they change their allegiance.

    Fled general can still play the game - they still remained within the realm. In case where everyone is dead defending a particular realm, the realm will be destroyed, although Gold will be transferred to the invaders.

    Every realm has their treasury: Gold Pieces (calculated based on activity rating of forum). Every successful raid will steal gold worth total level of generals participated, plus 1 gold per 10000 troops alive. Currently:

    CC: 1120 Gold Pieces
    Bar: 493 Gold Pieces
    AA: 345 Gold Pieces
    OBF: 324 Gold Pieces

    After battle, surviving generals will restore back to original troop amount.

    Victory will be for realm with the most gold as of 20 June 2007. Until 25 May 2007, no battle is allowed. By default, CC is cheating, therefore, it will win unless other realm/s can prove it otherwise. Players can choose to recruit anybody into this game, serving any particular realm, but I will only consider them in when they PM me with the following information below.

    ***

    Anybody with more than 100 post count can play. To join this game, PM me the following information:

    i) The name and the type of troops you desire. (E.g. I named my troops 'Vampire' and I want them to be 'Melee')

    ii) If your a P-member (Rank: Elite) with post greater than 20k or Senior Member (Rank Elder) with post greater than 30k, you can choose discipline. (E.g Marshal Discipline)

    iii) Indicate which Realm you wished to serve (Four Realms include the top four forum in SGF: CC (Chit Chit), AA (Aunt Agony), Bar or OBF (Original Babe Forum).

    Cheers
