    Declaration of a confident lady here! & what do I think that makes me one?

    Well, to me, there are differences between intrinsic & extrinsic displays of confidence.

    Intrinsic- I am able to "pyscho" myself that I am capable of achieving targets or aims set out and that I shall not be beaten by circumstances.

    Extrinsic- More importantly, I am able to transform those intrinsic thoughts into action and get what I want. No matter how long it takes or how difficult it gets. So far, I have been able to get what I want out of life and I have no doubts about my future too.

    Of course, there are bound to be certain things that I have not been able to accomplish as yet but the important thing is I do not let it get to me and deter me from achieving new goals or targets.
