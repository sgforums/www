    IMHO, oakley sunglasses are expensive due to several factors.

    1 is their lenses. they aren't any cheapo lenses that you can find on the streets. they filter out all glare and uv all that shit effectively. those cheap sunglasses you have, may in fact harm your eyes. reason being that they don't filter the uv but darken your vision, causing your iris to enlarge allowing more of the harmful uv rays into enter your eyes.

    2 is their life time warranty. in the 1st year u can exchange ur Oakleys for free if they are damaged. from there on for a mere 30 bucks u can have a brand shd you spoil them (same model though)

    3 is their durability, if you notice that in their plastic frames Oakleys refrain from using screws and other detachable parts. even in their metal frames there are minimal screws. the oakleys are made to last for a life time.

    the brand factor is another, but i believe that Oakleys are good investments and they really protect your eyes, esp if you are the sporty kind always in the sun.

    p/s: i think you can actually wear the sunglasses at night cos the lenses would not darken your vision like those conventinal sunglasses. i tried, but remove them after a while cos i don't want to look like a goon....
