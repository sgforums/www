    i have used n620 before..

    i would like to comment on the sms. When you sms halfway and people call, your whole sms is erased. And after you sms, if the sms fails, the whole sms is also erased away.
    And there's no different profile to choose from like nokia phones..

    And for the ringer, although it's polyhonic ringtone.. the function is not very nice. It's either you select vibrate ringing, or ring with a tone.. If you select both, the phone will ring with the tone first, den vibrate without the tone. So it's totally out of point. It's good if the phone ring with a tone, at the same time vibrates..

    1 thing nice is the 3 or 4 shades of grey for the display, but nowadays, phones are all with coloured screens. And the polyphonic ringtone is nice. But the composer is kind of hard to use.
    I've sold this fone after using for 3 months as i can't tolerate the functions that it has..
