    Not sure if it is exactly the same in computing, but should be fairly similar.

    In science you have to discuss with supervisor to write a thesis proposal.

    Thesis proposal = background of research area, your specific research interest (may need a hypothesis), techniques, current results and future plans/timeline of experiments.
