    Housepours = the liquor that avail in the club as basic/standard spirits.
    e.g when you get a club entry ticket with a entitlement of one drink. You are going to use the coupon and order a drink.
    You want to order a Belvedere vodka cranberry, the bartender will tell you that that they use Absolut Blue.

    - Absolut Blue is their vodka housepour for all coupon or standard drink.
    Other than Absolut Blue is considered premium spirits which is additional of $2 or more.

    Housepour has six kind of liquor.

    -Tequila
    -Vodka
    -Bourbon
    -Whiskey
    -Cognac/Brandy
    -Gin

    Mixers are

    - Juices
    - Carbonated drinks

    Housepour mix is considered only one spirit(housepour) and one mixer to form a drink.
    e.g Vodka Redbull, Tequila Orange, Brandy Dry(Ginger Ale), Whiskey Coke, Gin Tonic, Bourbon Coke.

    Or Housepour neat. (You should know what this is.)

    Cocktails consist of more than two liquid in a drink.
    One spirit and two juices
    Or Two spirit and two juices and more.
    e.g Around the world, Long Island Iced Tea, Mai Tai, Hawaiian Blue, Blue Lagoon, Grasshopper, Lychee Martini, Cosmopolitian, Metropolis, Manhattan, Margarita, Pina Colada...etc.

    Shot could be one spirit
    or Shot cocktail.

    Cheers
