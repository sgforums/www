    US is split into a few time zones, but the main one will be ET, CT and PT.

    For US timing,
    ET - Eastern Timing (The time zone along the East coast of United State), it is 12 hours less Singapore time (for daylight saving time).
    When daylight saving is off, it will be 13 hours less Singapore time.
    If it is 5th May 2005, 6am ET (Daylight), it is 5th May 2005, 6pm Singapore timing.

    CT - Central Timing (The time zone along the central part of United State), it is 13 hours less Singapore time (for daylight saving time).
    When daylight saving is off, it will be 14 hours less Singapore time.
    If it is 5th May 2005, 5am Central (Daylight), it is 5th May 2005, 6pm Singapore timing.

    PT - Pacific Timing (The time zone along the West coast of United State), it is 15 hours less Singapore time (for daylight saving time).
    When daylight saving is off, it will be 16 hours less Singapore time.
    If it is 5th May 2005, 3am PT (Daylight), it is 5th May 2005, 6pm Singapore timing.

    Hope that answer your question.
