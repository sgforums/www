    Sometimes you can't ignore the lumps too. You may have a preset criteria which your ideal fits, but how often is it that you'll meet your ideal? Sometimes you may have to adjust your expectations a little over time. You may like this person, but you may not necessarily like everything about that person. That person may just get sifted and neglected because he is not the "perfect size" lump.

    However, over time, he can still evolve into a lump which you eventually will choose. Eg. a overly possessive guy may become wiser as he grows older and he will not be so stubborn upon this aspect. He may now becomes your perfect lump, but the problem is he has been sifted from the pile earlier on, hence the you miss out on him until you next meet him again, but by then he may have become someone else's lump.

    Just remember that ideal is ideal, best left to be an ideal, and not to be pursued too zealously. Sometimes an ideal or a dream is best left that way - you'll always be hoping for it, but somehow it's so near yet so far, you'll never be able to possess it, but its perfect image will be forever etched in your memory. Once you get hold of it, you may realise that the picture you painted previously may not appear what it really is.
