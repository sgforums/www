    I from Creative Media year one.

    Join the course only if you have genuine interest, cos you need to do drawing, software and alot of technical stuff along with the creative ones. The course quite taxing in terms of amount of assignment and all, definitely very jialat if you not interested.

    As for Games Design, they do make board games in first year bah. That i think is for them to learn how games work and all. My friends sometimes slack off by playing DOTA in class, so yeah. Anyway, hope I answered any questions you may have about the course.

    Before I forget, these two courses along with most design courses don't have examinations... cos the assignment already counted as your examination marks.
