    Chocolate Slut & Entourage



    Since its opening in 1991, Seah Street Deli has been reputed for its typical laidback All-American cosy ambiance and the generous servings as well as its friendly service.

    It is the place to stuff yourself with huge sandwiches, giant hotdogs and burgers. This time though, the desserts take centrestage what with the chocolate slut leading the way. Take no offence, for chocolate slut is actually a combination of fresh cream, marshmallow, chocolate chips and milk chocolate that reclines on a base of Oreo cookies and topped off with dark chocolate. An irresistible temptation indeed.



    Hot on the heels of the chocolate slut is the orange carrot cake adorned with a cute orange marzipan carrot and infused with flavours of tangy orange, zesty pineapple and spicy cinnamon. The strawberry cheesecake, a tribute to all things American, consists of Philadelphia cheese and high-bred wild strawberries and puree mara des boir from France.

    These desserts are served in individual portions suitable for diners as well as convenient for take-aways.
