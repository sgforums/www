Captain Of The Ship

  • hoganrock
    11 Oct 03, 20:20

    Long long time ago, in a far far away galaxy, there is a wooden ship with lots of crews under the famous captain who sailed through the pacific and the world to find food and treasures for the crews. They earned alot and they are tremendously happy. One day due to unforeseen circumstances, the food and treasures are suddenly running low and they are hungry. So they have to save every penny they had acquired to curb unnecessary spending.
    They spend their money wisely and will question every thing they spend whether if it is worth it.

    Unfortunately, one of the wooden floor planks broke and start to leak and water gushing in. The broken 'plank' is broken into many pieces and can't be repaired anymore. The captain told the sailor to find a good floor 'plank' in the ship to cover the 'hole' as we could not afford to buy new one. There is no extra or good working plank in the ship. Then the captain told the sailor to get a good working floor plank from one of the 'existing' floor plank and replaced the 'hole' that is leaking with water. Well, guess what?

    The sailor did remove the 'existing' floor plank and replaced the 'hole' but the problem still exists. The hole has been patched but it still leak. It leaked from the wooden floor plank that has just been pulled out to replace the 'hole'! The Captain just shrugged his shoulder and said what to do?

    Guess the morale of the story. It applies to our real life as well.
