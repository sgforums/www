    - do a manual ntwk selection from your own hp; select singtel/m1/starhub.

    if not..
    - remove sim; take a tissue and wipe the gold magnetic part of the sim card.
    - insert it into another cellphone; monitor for abt an hr or two

    if it doesn't; it means fault lies with the sim card.

    - if fault = cellphone; pls refer to phone manufacturer.
    - if fault = sim card; walk-in to your nearest telco shop to get a replacement sim card.

    there're only 3 main possiblities regarding LS (line status)
    - network, cellphone and sim card.

    the other rare possibility would be bugs (in your cellphone system).
