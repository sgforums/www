    Originally posted by Kimosabe:
    Herpes???!! What is that?? Isn't that STD??
    Not all infections caused by herpes are sexually transmitted.

    In fact, cold sores are also caused by herpes viruses and such infections can be transmitted without sexual contact.

    Cold sores can lead to complications, and, when they do, the complications in question cannot always be treated by antibiotics.

    The infection you are thinking of is probably genital herpes.

    Genital herpes (or a herpes infection of the genitals) is almost always contracted by sexual contact.
