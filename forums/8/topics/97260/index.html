    You got nero burning rom right?

    Under "File", select "Open".
    Look for the folder with your iso, or cue file.
    Under "File of type", select "Image Files(*.nrg,*,iso,*cue).
    Select the file from your folder, and click "open".
    Now you are ready to burn the image onto a cd-r/w.
    Watch your movie by playing the cd-r/w.
