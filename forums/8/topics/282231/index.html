    Hi welcome. I am one of the Moderator at GCE A N O Level.

    English (Paper 1 and 2, and Oral) - $80

    Chinese (Paper 1 and 2, and Oral) - $50

    Combine Humanities(SS and History) - $70

    E Maths(Paper 1 & 2) - $70

    Science(Chemistry, Physics) - $87

    I am not too sure if D&T is offer to Private Candidates or not.

    Add Cambridge fee: $60
    Add fee for local subject: $30

    You'll received your Entry proof by the Early July of the examination year for private candidate.

    In that Entry proof, It'll state

    1) The subjects which you are going to take
    2) The Date and Time
    3) The school which you are posted to
    4) The address of the School
    5) Your index and centre number

    Please Note IMPORTANT:
    For Practical

    If you didn't take practical at O level Science at sec5, you cannot register for Private O level Science subject.

    If want to take Practical at O level as a science subject, you must have taken the Practical paper before OR you are in a private institution which is conducting practicals.

    ORAL
    You'll be taking the oral at the school which your are posted to, same for the practical.
