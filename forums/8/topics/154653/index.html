    Weight-training exercises

    (Please dont give me that crap that muscle bulk will deprove your agility, come on i have even seen champion triatheletes/joggers doing weight training everyweek and it helped them)

    http://www.exrx.net/WeightExercises/Quadriceps/BBSquat.html -Back squat

    http://www.exrx.net/WeightExercises/Hamstrings/BBGoodMorning.html -Barbell good mornings

    http://www.exrx.net/WeightExercises/GluteusMaximus/BBDeadlift.html -conventional deadlift

    Deadlifts and squats helped to build explosive strength and you need "explosiveness" during broad jump, actually olympic lift variations build explosive strength better.

    Plyometrics exercises:

    http://www.exrx.net/Plyometrics/StairJumps.html

    http://www.exrx.net/Plyometrics/HurdleJumps.html

    All this exercises require some "explosiveness" to perform
