    Please bear in mind that the zombies (as depicted in "28 Days Later" and "Dawn of the Dead") are not the traditional kind of undead....they're not slow moving dullards who are afraid of relics or supernatural stuff like garlic, silver, salt, crosses, holy water, 'fu', etc...you can't even go to a temple or church to hide from them (in fact in one scene on '28 Days Later', the pastor of the church himself became zombified).

    The zombies we're talking about are caused by infection, either thru' biting or blood transmission (therefore religious relics don't ward them off). They are also superhumanly strong and can run very fast. Plus they seem to have enhanced senses (very good hearing...and can see in the dark). Also they seem to be able to last a long time without needing to drink water and eat food. Furthermore, the only way to effectively kill one of them is to put a bullet thru' its head.....bullets thru' other parts of body not so effective...

    The only weakness they have is that they are not that intelligent (can't drive, can't use weapons and can't unlock doors!!!!).
