SSC Launches Great Singapore Race

  • wuming78
    7 Dec 03, 11:17
    The Singapore Sports Council announced today that it will be launching an annual sports extravaganza called The Great Singapore Race, which it claims will be comparable to none in the world.

    SSC Deputy Vice CEO Mr.Runnathurai s/o Marathonasamy said that the Great Singapore Race will be an endless marathon during which participants are required to gather five Cs during each lap of the endless number of laps.

    To ensure greater competitiveness, race participants will be divided by, um, race. The SSC denied that participants from minority teams will have hurdles placed in their path. Rather, the SSC says that in all lanes, obstacles will be strategically placed to ensure only 15% make it to a certain degree.

    However, to provide encouragement, the especially talented such as scholars will run in express lanes, alongside white horses who are expected to add some colour to the competition.

    To improve the standard of the race, international participants with talent will also be invited to participate, and in the interests of hospitality, may feel free to overtake local participants at any time.

    The chosen One however will be in a solo race. There will also be one Supreme umpire and one firing gun. To ensure orderliness, officials dressed in white will stand on the sidelines to motivate participants and hand out recycled water at market prices.

    Participants who are not satisfied with the rules of the race or express criticisms will be disqualified. Further, Mr. Runnathurai said that all unfit participants should quit the race rather than continue and be a burden to all. However any other participant who leaves the path prematurely will be deemed a quitter.
