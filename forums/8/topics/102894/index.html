    You will not find the topo map of singapore any more, what you have is just the Singapore road map, ( minus contours, height and lots of other information). Can get at popular or other big bookshops. Have both laminated and non-laminated. Not sure if $ 25 for laminated or non-laminated, but non-laminated less costly.

    The Topographical Map of Singapore is produced by the Mapping Unit, Ministry of Defence, but they have stopped selling the Topo Map as well as the Locality Map of Singapore. However, they are still offering the Road Map of Singapore for sale. The address and contact number of the Mapping Unit, Ministry of Defence, Singapore is 200 Airport Road, Paya Lebar Airport, Singapore 534257, Tel. No. 6488 3202. Alternatively, you can contact them at their email address at SMU@Pacific.net.sg or SafMu@magic.com.sg
