    North East Xtreme Challenge 2005

    Have you always wanted to take part in the reality television show "The
    Amazing Race" ? This is your chance! The Youth Executive Committees (YECs)
    in the North East Region will be organising "North East XTreme (
    N.E.X.T)Challenge 2005", a modified version of the widely popular reality
    show.

    Youths will have to pit their wits to overcome challenging obstacles and
    quizzes during the race so as to emerge as the winning teams to win
    incredibly attractive prizes. Of course, during the race, they also will be
    visiting culturally rich places to enjoy the picturesque beauty of the North
    East Region. If you are looking for adventure or simply wish to spend an
    unforgettable time with your friends, wait no more!

    Date/Time?

    The "North East XTreme ( N.E.X.T) Challenge 2005" will be held on 26 June
    2005, 8am at the Kovan Hub. Youths can participate in teams of five members
    and registration forms may be downloaded at www.paym.org.sg.

    What do you stand to WIN?

    Champion - $5,000 worth of cash and prizes
    1st Runner-up - $3,000 worth of cash and prizes
    2nd Runner-up - $2,000 worth of cash and prizes
    3rd Runner-up - $1,000 worth of cash and prizes
    5th to 8th Place - $500 worth of cash and prizes each
