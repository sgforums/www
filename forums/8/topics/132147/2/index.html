    Originally posted by Hellraiza:
    i was refering to wad the actual words "xiao ao jiang hu" mean
    "Xiao Ao Jiang Hu" literally means "laugh proud river lake". "Ao" rather than meaning "arrogant", should be taken in the context of "carefree and unaffected". "Jiang Hu" is a term for the pugilistic world in this context, but also refers to the triad underworld in modern usage.
