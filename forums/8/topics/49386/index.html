    How to get to Sentosa by MRT from 20 June 03 after 10 am:-
    Take the North East line (NEL) and alight at NE1 HarbourFront station, use the exit which bring you to the food centre and bus interchange, walk up the stair to the interchange and board the shuttle bus there.

    If you are taking the MRT from other lines:-
    From the East-West line, transfer to the NEL at Outram Park station
    From the North-South line, transfer to the NEL at Dhoby Ghaut station

    Or, if you are still alighting at Tiong Bahru station, then you may board service 85 at the bus-stop which has the former Sentosa shuttle, and alight at HarbourFront interchange and transfer to the shuttle.
