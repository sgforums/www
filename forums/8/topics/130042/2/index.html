    First hand experience and advice from an O level student who scored very high last year

    These are the top 6 advices if you are a lazy student n procastinator like me

    1.) NEVER EVER procrastinate
    2.) Revise ur work regularly
    3.) Don't sleep in class (i always sleep in class)
    4.) Always useful to start preparing early
    5.) Be attentive in class and ask questions to clear your doubt.
    5.) No such things as last minute study. The few weeks n days left before ur Os are meant for revising and to clear any doubts.
    6.) Try and do all your Ten Year Series. If you can solve most of them, you should not have any problems scoring As/Bs
    7.) Have a target/goal that you want for the outcomes of your results and MAKE SURE you WORK TOWARDS YOUR GOAL

    Start taking actions early and don't regret like me after u get back ur results.

    Anyway you can try buying the book 'I am Gifted, So are You!' by Adam Khoo. Its really helpful as the book teaches you the correct methods of studying.I personally find it very useful n inspired after reading.. but guess wat? My cousin gave it to me as a Xmas present after i finished my Os
