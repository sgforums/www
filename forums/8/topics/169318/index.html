    $0.50 renewal fee per book. renewal is usually done by re-borrowing a borrowed book at the self-check machine. the fee is charged to your library account which you can settle at the payment machines.

    you can pay with cashcard or ez-link card when you scan your identity card at the payment machines, which shld show all outstanding fines and a prompt to pay.

    note that you can use NETS at the library information counter should your outstanding due exceed $2.00.

    yes, you can owe the library money indefinitely though your borrowing privileges will be suspended once you exceed an amount in your library account (should be $2.00, not $5.00) until you settle the outstanding amount. the maximum fine for overdue borrowing will not exceed $5.50 per book.
