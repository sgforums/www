    I support MOE to come up with cookhouse system.

    Reasons:

    1. Ensure students to eat healthy food and eat the correct amount.
    2. Overweight students will be controlled in their amount and type of food, as the cook house staff will give out food according to special tags assigned by the school.
    (Eg: Overweight students wear red tags, Normal students wear blue tags and Underweight student wear green tags.)
    3. No need to bring pocket money, so that students will not overspent in school.
    4. Ensure students do not eat unhealthy food as school canteens sometimes sell "illegal" food.

    The cookhouse operator need not to be SFI, it can be NTUC Foodfare, which could cut cost, to lower down the cookhouse cost.
