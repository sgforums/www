    Use "green" abrasive paper to sand the brake surface, wet first.

    Use the more abrasive ones for brake pads.

    Note: NEVER use those "black" or more abrasive sandpapers on the brake surface as it will reduce brake modulation and make slowing down smoothly a pain.
