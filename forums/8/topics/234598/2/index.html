    Now just solve 1g/cm3=(1/1000kg)/[(1/100)m*(1/100)m*(1/100)m]
    =1000kg/m3

    1gcm-3

    This means, in a space measuring 1cm by 1cm by 1cm, the mass is 1 gram.

    In 1m-3, the dimensions are 100cm by 100cm by 100cm.

    Therefore, mass in 1m-3 = 100 X 100 X 100 = 1000000 grams

    Therefore, 1gcm-3 = 1000kgm-3
