    Yahoo mail, is Yahoo mail. There's no need for SMTP, unless you're using a SingNet email Account or Starhub email account.

    If it is Yahoo email, your email sending problem is probably due to an infection in your PC. If you're using SingNet, SingNet email account can't send email via Starhub's SMTP. If it's Starhub email account, your settings could be at fault. Call them to step you through on settings.

    Some times, a serious infection can cause email sending issues on both SingNet and Starhub accounts; both ISPs will scan your emails for auto attachment of viruses.
