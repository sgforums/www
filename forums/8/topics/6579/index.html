    Everyone seem to be overseas nowadays huh?

    The majority of the Singapore youths tend to have a rather narrow view of issues in life. They tend to be more interested in local and personal issues. For example, NS guys will talk about NS 24/7. Some people do not seem to realise that the world is so much bigger than that they've been living in. There's a sense of restlessness about youths these days. They are hardly motivated by anything in life and live life on a day to day basis. As you grow older and gain more experience, you realise that you'll have to make things happen instead of waiting for them to occur. There's a much wider diversity of life outside the homes, schools, countries etc. A desire to learn seems sparse too.

    In response to prodigal's thoughts:

    1) relationships:

    Due to the materialisation of our world, everything turned unseemingly cold. Relationships are harder to maintain due to stress, insecurities, temptations, mistrust etc. When problems arise, people nowadays are reluctant to face up to them and solve them. They simply give in. Thus the break down of marriages etc.

    2) Virginity

    I've posted in the virginity thread too, so read that.

    3) Tolerance to anormalies

    Some people say that its due to the world 'opening' up. We do have to acknowledge the fact that all humans are individuals, separate entities. They behave in different ways and that's what makes the world so interesting. However, I do not think that that's the full and only reason. People are more insecure these days. They do not want anyone to see that so they hide their innermost feelings and put on a false front. When they are in the company of people who do not mind the anormalies, they'll behave in that way too i.e. they do not want to 'lose face'.

    All in all, its not the changes that we should be focusing on, but instead, the adaptations that we make in response to those changes that are important.
