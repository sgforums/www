    SINGAPORE -- A Singaporean woman was killed when she was hit by a subway train, police said on Sunday.

    Neo Shiea Chee, a 25-year-old teacher, was trapped under the mass transit train at 6:37 pm on Saturday and pronounced dead 18 minutes later, said police spokesman Ang Poon Seng.

    Police were investigating whether the woman jumped in front of the train or was pushed onto the tracks.

    'In less than five minutes police arrived at the scene and found the girl trapped underneath the train,' Mr Ang said. 'We still do not know how she got trapped there.'

    Police are investigating the incident.

    Witnesses said the woman was nearly severed in half at the waist by the train.

    The train's 600 passengers, all uninjured, were evacuated within minutes of the accident, Mr Ang said. -- AP
