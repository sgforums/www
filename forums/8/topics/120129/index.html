    The demand of exec. HDB isn't very high cos there are oversupply of new flats & many are now buying 3- 4-rm cos economy not good.
    For the price of an exec., u can get a 99 yrs condo. with facilities.
    If your friend gets a good offer, then don't wait any more cos all prop. 99 yrs. start depreciating as the prop. ages.
    Freehold prop. are still better cos they appreciate faster.

    Majority of pple loses money cos they bought during the boom period.
    One of my fren recently cut lost (she lost over $100K), when she sold her 99 yrs. condo & upgrade to freehold.

    Downgrading from condo to HDB will save maintenance fees & utilities etc are cheaper esp. 3- 4-rm. Ask your friend whether she is willing to take the loss.

    U can check with some prop. agts. for the latest HDB prices transacted.
