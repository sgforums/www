Others say they traveled eons in space to populate the earth. The theories are endless, but locked inside a top secret government vault is the answer. I will complete the puzzle for you in the form of a simple story, then you can be the judge of what you want to believe.

    A long time ago, beings from another world visited the Planet Earth for the first time. Long before the others. Yes there have been others, even now in our time. But it's the ones that got here first, that holds the key to the puzzle. They were a group of beings with enormous knowledge and power. Lets say they had more total knowledge and power than the population of 1,000 Planet Earths' combined.

    First they seeded the Earth with plant life. It doesn't matter how they did it, or how long it took. They could do it. They could do anything they wanted. They were like gods. Gods with knowledge and power far beyond anything we can imagine.

    Like an experiment, they waited to see what happened next. As a result of this living plant life, other forms of life developed. Including human beings.

    Then they left. Just like that. You see, these peaceful beings even with all their knowledge and power, had enemies. These loving creatures who traveled to distant worlds, creating life, had enemies who could destroy life. In order to preserve their new world, and to defeat their enemy, they elected two beings to stay behind on planet earth while the rest left to fight the enemy somewhere else far away from this planet. Then, something horrible happened to the aliens that left. Caught in a terrible battle beyond description, they all died. The two beings left on planet earth had no choice. They were here to stay. They had to multiply their kind. And they did. They built a race of supreme beings. They lived side by side with the life they created, but chose to hide from the developing humans. As the human race was developing, the aliens were living among them watching. They did from time help us out. Can you think of a few things on this planet that came from their help? Sure you can!! The pyramids for example. They also helped us fight the plague in the middle ages. They gave us the industrial revolution! Hard to swallow? Don't want to believe it. It's your choice. There's more! Its my job to find things out. The Government has some of these beings at a secret location. Here is what I was able to find out.

    As the alien race developed, they had a disagreement amongst themselves, and split into three groups. The first group wants to give the humans substantial knowledge, with no strings attached, then find a way to leave this planet. Perhaps to create life somewhere else. The second group wants to give the humans substantial knowledge, only if we can prove that we can handle it. Then they would join the others in some far off galaxy. The third group has no use for human kind. They don't like us. They are the one ones who stay to themselves.

    From time to time, other alien races visit Planet Earth. The aliens already here have a way to cloak the minds of other beings. Human or otherwise. So when the other alien races visit, they do some experiments, perhaps an abduction or two. They find us uninteresting, and of no value, so they leave. The third group of aliens listed above, let us remember some of the encounters, just because they don't like us. Thus all the stories of painful abductions and close encounters.

    When the government saved the life's some of these visiting creatures from a ufo crash site, the creatures were so grateful they decided share their friendship with us. The aliens living among us knew that saved aliens were not sincere. They were using the humans and were up to no good. No one really knows what took place, but a small group of the Planet Earth Aliens contacted The President of the United states along with several selected heads of governments. They exposed the bad ones for what they were, and killed them. Only a selected few human beings really know what truly happened, but what I am able to gather is this:

    They tell us that another asteroid is coming. Much bigger than the last one. Yes they were here at that time in our history. They managed to survive, but they feel that the next encounter with a giant asteroid will destroy more life than the last one did. If the aliens give the human race the knowledge to expand our abilities without destroying ourselves, maybe there is a chance for survival. When will the asteroid get here? The answer is known only to a select few. But it will happen without warning. What do the Planet Earth Aliens look like? For reasons I would rather not discuss, I will only tell you this. They look somewhat like human beings, except they have very large deep set brown eyes. They live among us, Lets leave it at that. They tell us to be kind to each other. Love and show compassion to others less fortunate than ourselves. They never die from natural causes. They can be injured but heal quickly. That's it.

    The rest is up to you.
