    You need to take the train to Paya Lebar mrt station.

    From there take the circle line and head to Stadium.

    At Stadium, alight from MRT then from there, take a cab to Boon Lay MRT.

    At Boon Lay MRT, hop on to the train and alight at Jurong East station. Take the red line all the way until you reach City Hall.

    From City Hall, you need to walk through Citilink mall, until you see a sign that tell you where marina square and esplanade are. From there you'd know how to get to Esplanade library. You just know it.

     

    Yep, going to that library sure is expensive, but they have many valuable books there, so it's worth the trip and $$$. Have fun and good luck.
