    Setting up an online business is not as easy as it seems. However, compared to retail store, it's considered as affortable and managable concept.

    Before you kick start anything, the FIRST step is to always: Brainstorm Business Idea
    The first step might seem obvious but not many stop and think about what business they are doing. What products or services are you going to sell? Who are your competitor? How much should I charge? These are all questions you should be answering before moving on.

    The next step is to: Incubate Your Business Idea
    What does it means to incubate your business idea? It is to move on from your planning stage in the first step to executing your ideas. Your business could worth a million UNTIL you actually start taking the first step to start it. Think about platform where your products/services can be sold.

    Phycial/unwanted products could be sold at Gumtree, Qoo10, Carousell, Ebay, Facebook, Instagram and many more! (Website like a .COM might not be suitable unless you're trying to establish a brand)

    Likewise, services could be sold at Instagram, Facebook, Website, Groupon and many more!

    The last step is to: Tectical Planning
    You might have the best website or facebook page set up now but wouldn't put a single penny until its being displayed right infront of your targeted audience. Thus, the last step is tectical planning. Plan how you want to promote and advertise!

    In conclusion, use this BIT concept and you might find yourself making money within a month from starting your very own business. Cheers!

    A 19-year-old woman was arrested on Tuesday (Nov 29) for suspected involvement in several cheating cases on the Carousell online marketplace, the police said in a press release on Wednesday.

    Police investigations are ongoing.

    The suspect may be charged with cheating, which carries a fine and up to 10 years' imprisonment.

    The police advise members of the public to be careful when buying items from strangers online.
