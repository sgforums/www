    Actually there is no one RIGHT way to sell, cuz the MOST IMPORTANT is YOU need to TAKE ACTION.

    Everyone who wants to sell needs to START somewhere! Whether its Carousell or Gumtree or watever platform, my friend you need to post your product and get IT IN FRONT OF PEOPLE. 

    Truth is there is no telling when you will make that FIRST sale or that BIG sale! BUT you will never know if you don't DO SOMETHING!
