    Tips for Mothers Day! Cards, Restaurants, Flowers, Pastries, Gadgets!

    Hi everyone, people have come to me asking me what to do for Mother’s Day so I’ve compiled a list of special things that I do to appreciate my mum on Mother’s Day. Hope it helps!

     Tips for Mother’s Day

     

     1. A Heartfelt Card

     A card is the bare minimum to give our Mums for all the hard work she’s done to bring us up – homemade if you have the time!

     How to make Absolutely Beautiful Homemade Cards - Google Martha Steward Mothers Day Cards!

     Alternatively, if you have no time, you can buy a card from Popular and write in it (Not Recommended! ï�Š

    What to write in the card? Some tips:

    • Show your appreciation

    • Be Sincere and Genuine

    • Address any deep-seated issues between you (Ouch!)

    • Something that she will remember and treasure forever

    • Reminiscence about good times (you can also attach pictures and polaroids for them!)

    • If you have kids, get them to do something fun like put their handprints in them!

     

     2. Spend Good, authentic time with them

     What Mothers want most is to spend quality time with us. Nowadays, everyone is so busy with our careers, our own families that we do not really spare much time for our mums. A good way to show our appreciation of them is to SPEND THE WHOLE DAY WITH THEM. You do not have to bring her out to posh restaurants; in fact, your Mum might appreciate it more if you could stay at home and show her your love!

     Some ideas that I have used in the past:

    • No work day for Mummy! Clean up the house, wash the dishes, cook breakfast, lunch and dinner for her…do all the chores

    • Offer to help her shift around furniture to give the house a new look (this can take up the whole day and it’ll be great fun!)

    • Watch TV together

    • Sleep together for the first time in a long time

    • Do up each other’s hair…come up with your own activities, the list is endless!

    • If your mum likes shopping, bring her out shopping and give her a treat!

     Nice Restaurants to go to:

     Google citynomads, ladyironchef,thediningtable Singapore!

     

     3. A Bouquet of Flowers is a MUST

     For some reason, Mums really love flowers. You should choose a bouquet that suits your Mum’s personality. Surprise her with a fantastic bouquet of flowers! I know my Mum just goes absolutely crazy over a beautiful bouquet. You can consider flowers from a number of shops like Far East Flora, but the cheapest and best I’ve ordered from is a small little-known online flower retailer: Floral Garage Singapore , which has excellent customer service. However, for those who prefer really posh and AWESOME bouquets, you can consider Floral Magic!

     Here are some big and reliable flower names:

     Fareast Flora, Wonderful Flora, XpressFlower! You can google them to view their websites!

     Mum can place these flowers in a vase and leave them out for the whole week, brightening the entire house.

     Surprise your mum with appropriate bouquets, and brighten up her day ï�Š

    “Flowers always make people better, happier, and more helpful; they are sunshine, food and medicine to the mind.”

     

    â€• Luther Burbank

     

     4. Home Made Cakes, Cookies, or Desserts!

     This is one of my favourite things to do for my mum. You can find any number of delicious recipes for the type of cakes your mum likes. Banana Cakes, Chocolate Cakes, Fruit Cakes, Muffins, Pies, Ice Cream, there are so many different things you can do!

     

     Lastly, remember that no single act can make your Mum happy, make sure that you show your appreciation and love for her through your daily actions and words, and she will be happy.

     

     5. Appliances/Gadgets (for Homemakers)

     For mothers who are housewives, I think some gadgets and appliances would be of superb use to them! Probably a decade ago, domestic helpers or homemakers had to slog it out by hand-washing clothes for the entire family. Washing machines have certainly come to save the day. Value for money appliances from courts and Harvey Norman such as a dishwasher, clothes steamer, multi-purpose vacuum cleaner, would be a great gift! It would make their life so much easier. Of course, apart from getting her stuff to aid her daily chores, you could also get her something she could relax with, like a massage chair from Osim!

     Parisilk do have some very attractive promotions from time to time. Do check them out by googling them!

    For mothers who drive, getting them a GPS would be a good idea too! I’m not trying to be a sexist by saying that females have bad sense of direction, but it could be a great friend on the road. I’m using a Garmin nuvi 1460 and it comes with lifetime maps updates! For a country like SG where the roads are always changing, its certainly a good buy. Even a mini vacuum cleaner to clean the interior of the car would be a great gift. Try those HomeFix! The staff are usually very knowledgeable.

     

    6. Exercise Equipment

    Mothers who are working or staying home as homemakers are sometimes too busy to exercise. If they are not too busy, they tend to find excuses. I recommend you guys to head down to JMALL. You can google it, it is a great online store with value for money exercise equipment. This will be an excellent way to ensure she maintains her physical fitness!

     

     HAVE FUN!

     Hope you enjoyed my list!

     Feel free to add any suggestions/opinions to this thread!

     

     HAPPY MOTHERS DAY!
