    Let's Learn Hokkien!

    The difference between:

    1. Deng Bai
    2. Gu Zah
    3. Gor Zah

    All three mean "last time", but denote different duration of time past.

    Deng bai is not long ago, macam a few days ago.

    For example: "Deng bai, wah ki buay cai eh si zun" (Last time I buy vegetable that time).

    Gu Zah is many years ago, a faded memory.

    Eg: "Gu Zah mata cheng teh kor eh si zun" (Last time policeman wear shorts that time).

    Gor Zah is ancient history, older than your grandparents.

    Eg: "Gor Zah di deng sua eh si zun" (Last time in ancient China that time).
