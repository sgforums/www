    incident one...

    a few of us were chatting thru the nite, and it was past 2 in the morning...feeling tired, i told the rest of the guys i'm going to wash up and go to bed...i took my toothbrush and proceeded to the toilet, which was on the 2nd floor...(tg gul camp ITC)...as i walked up the stairs, i can feel the hairs on my back standing on ends...i quickly brushed my teeth and went back to my bunk on the ground floor...the next morning, a few of my bunk mates claimed that they saw a white figure wlking all over the bunk ard 4 plus in the morning, i asked if it was the rest of the guys still chatting, but they said the went to bed shortly after me...

    incident two...
