    There are mechanical movements and quartz movements. Your average Casio uses quartz movements. Mechanical movements can only be found in very high-end watches such as Jaeger-le-Coutre and Franck Muller. Watches combining analog and digital display can easily be found, for example, from Casio's G-Shock range.
