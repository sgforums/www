    actually there is no need to panic. i have been in a couple of such situations before.

    if you are really worried about it, and have the reciept, go back down to the shop and explain it to them and they can immediately charge back to your mastercard/visa (doesn't matter if it's a debit or credit card). i haven't been to a restaurant where they have put up too much of an issue with it.

    otherwise, just call your bank or download a form online (if you're from DBS, not too sure about other banks) fill in some details and send it back to them. you don't even need to call up the restaurant, the bank will sort it out for you. it usually takes 4 weeks to clear though.

    hope that helps.
