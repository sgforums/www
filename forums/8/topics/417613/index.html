    Many people are unaware.

    In Singapore, there is already a program called,

    Advanced Medical Directive.

    ( AMD )

    If you go to hospitals, sometimes you can see the brochures about it. You can pick up one and read it.

    Basically,

    It means if someone is sick to the point of incurable, and there is no medical procedures which can realistically cure his ilness, and the person is at a very suffering / almost dying state, then the person can instruct the medical personnel to stop any unnecessary procedures and just focus on pain reduction and feeding.

    This is my layman language and understanding.

    It is something like that.

    And as the name implies , Advanced Medical Directive, means that this instruction can be given at an advanced / prior phase.

    So you can sign it while you are still healthy.

    So,

    I believe this is a good solution.

    This is not exactly Euthanasia, but it has sufficiently and adequately adress the spirit of it.

    This is much better than Euthanasia, in my humble opinion.

    Because the difference is,

    In Euthanasia,

    There is an active action to squelch human life.

    This brings moral hazard.

    Meanwhile AMD does not do anything to actively to squelch human life, but just to withdraw certain procedures, and then passively let the human pass away to the afterlife.

    So in the ideological and philosophical spectrum, I believe AMD is a bit more centrist and prudent, while Euthanasia is more left-wing.

    AMD should be enough.
