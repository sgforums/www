    First of all, is all depends on the traffic conditions on that day. Any ERP or CBD or Peak surcharges or not is a factor to consider.

    When the vehicle is moving in fast speed and non-stop (via expressway), the meter will not "jump" that fast. If the taxi is on the roads and traffic junctions are a lot, then the meter will "jump" a bit fast.

    All meters are being certified by LTA. Therefore, the driver cannot con you. And also, if you are in a hurry and need to take a cab for such long distance, do think of the 2-3 dollars.
