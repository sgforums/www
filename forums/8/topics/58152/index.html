    Here's a formula to impart and curb spendthrift habit:

    If you go shopping with a bf/gf, challenge each other to think the same way and make this into a game to see who can resist the appeal of buying the longest! (it's very mentally exhausting but very fun one!!)

    1) When you see something you like and you can afford, ask yourself if you really need it.

    2) If yes, then ask yourself if you are willing to let the store earn the kind of money (i.e. the price) from you. If no, don't buy.

    3) If yes, look in your wallet to see got enough cash. If no, look elsewhere to compare prices and don't buy from the store.

    4) If wallet has enough cash, ask yourself if you can tolerate the thought that yr wallet would be outta cash after buying. If no, keep you wallet in your pocket/handbag. (Don't consider using credit card or nets, your bank account would diminish and the cards company earn commission from you.)

    5) If yes, ask yrself if I should reserve the money for other things instead. If no, don't buy.

    6) If yes, don't buy. If no, also don't buy, coz this though process will tire you out and you will not have the interest to buy the item anymore.

    7) Repeat step 1 for every other item that catches yr fancy.

    At the end of the shopping trip, the loser (the one that bought an item) will have to endure being kissed non-stop by bf/gf for a good 30 min!!
