    After the coming Vesak Day holiday, there is going to be a real dry spell in terms of holidays for the next 7 months. The following are some holidays I think the S'pore government should declare:

    (1)Singapore would not be where it is today had it not been for the efforts of our workers. Instead of just having labour day to recognise their sacrifices for the country, we should have nation-wide holidays for each of the different porfessions to show our respect for them, no matter how lowly we perceive their jobs to be, eg, carpenter's day, shoe salesman's day, karung guni man's day, odd-job labourer's day, etc.

    (2)We should have holidays to commemorate the birthdays of our president and MPs. Even Malaysia has holidays on their sultan's birthday. We could have holidays for Nathan's, PM's, SM's birthday. This list will include all other MPs no matter how insignificant and quiet they are in parliament.

    (3)We should have holidays to commemorate important events in S'pore's history, eg, anniversary of British surrender, anniversary of Japanese surrender, etc.

    (4)We should have holidays to coincide with dates on which computer viral attacks are expected. As much of our economy is dependent on computers,and banks overseas have been known to declare bank holidays on such dates, why can't we follow suit? We could have holidays on Apr 26, any Fri 13th, etc.

    If enough of you support me, I will formally petition the govt with this request. Alternatively, I am willing to speak out at Speaker's Corner at Hong Lim Park,
