    What u need to know is, whether prostitution is a luxury or not, and whether we can contribute good reasons to say it is a luxury, is NOT relevant to the supply curve elasticity.

    Supply curve elasticity is NOT affected by whether the good/ service is luxury or not.

    Supply curve elasticity means how much supply is changed if the price change, and it is dependant on time (less time=more inelastic, more time=more elastic) and capability/ capacity (if excess capacity, can produce more quantity quickly=more elastic. Full capacity=more inelastic). It is not related to the goods/ service being a luxury or not. Unless if u are talking about the demand curve elasticity.
