    Finding love is part of life. One will find four people in this process.

    The first person is you,
    The second person is the one you love most,
    The third person is the one that loves you the most,
    Finally, the forth person is the one that spends the rest of your life with you.

    Firstly, you will meet the person you love most.
    And you will experience what love is like,
    after having experienced being loved,
    you will begin to discover who loves you the most.
    Once, you have experience to love and being loved,
    you will finally understand what you need,
    and find a person that suits you to spend the rest of your life with that person.

    However, in reality, very often these three people are not the same.
    The one you love, is not the one that chooses you,
    The one that loves you most, is not the one that you love most,
    and the one that spends the rest of your life with you, is neither the one you love nor the one that loves you, the person just shows up at the right place at the right time when you need someone.

    Which person will you be in someone's life?

    No one has a change of heart for no reason,
    when someone loves you, he really does.
    When he does not love you, he really does not.
    When he loves you, he cannot pretend he does not.
    Similarly, when he does not love you, he cannot pretend he does.

    When someone chooses to leave you,
    you must ask yourself if you still love him.
    If you do not love him, do not ever hold on to the relationship for the sake of ego or pride,
    If you still love him, you should hope he feels happy.
    Hope that he is with the one that he loves most and do not stop him.
    If you stop him, it means that you do not love him anymore.
    And if you do not love him anymore, what right do you have to say about his change of feelings?

    Well, that's love.
