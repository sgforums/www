    Hi, I am currently staying in the States and have been sending packages back home to Singapore and receiving packages. If you are shipping those items from US, you can check out the rates from

    http://www.usps.com

    The weight is in pounds and the cost is in USD.

    You can consider to purchase insurance for your package as your items such as the hard-disks are quite delicate. As for the inspection, it is really depend on luck. I got one of my packages cut open and it damage one of my CD cover.

    If you are using economy shipping, the shipment will take about 4-6 weeks time, however, it really depends on situation. I had shipped a 7lbs package for my friend and it took more than 7 weeks to reach.

    Sometime, it may be better to spend a bit more to get your items fast and in good condition. Well, it depends on you.

    Hi Lupid, I cannot be 100% sure if it will be safer to deliver by air than by sea. Please don't quote me on that, but I guess you will not like to wait for 6-8 weeks and worry if your items will arrive in good condition or not. Just a question, are you buying the items off ebay or other website suppliers?

    If you want to send by economy shipping, you can ask the suppliers to put more foams or bubble bags (but that may cost a bit more) to protect your items from hard knocks. However, you know that for hard-disk, they have the reader (a needle-like part that reads the data), that is the delicate part.

    As for the parcel inspection, it just happen randomly, my friends got their parcels open and their "bak gua" was confiscated by US customs.

    As for the issue of privacy intrusion, you see, when we want to ship a parcel to overseas, we need to fill in a Custom Declaration and Dispatch Note, it got a small print that says "The item/parcel may be opened officially". We also need to put our signature on that form to declare that the item/parcel does not contain any dangerous article prohibited by legislation or by postal or customs regulations.

    Another alternative shipping method will be by UPS or Fedex, however, that may cost more. You can check out more details from their websites.

    Hope that these information will be helpful.
