Walking with Dinosaurs - Debut on Central 9pm Tonight

  • Ande Tan
    19 Jul 00, 18:54


    Tonight, there will be a BBC documentary production: "Walking with Dinosaurs" on Central at 9pm. It takes us back to the Mesozoic era, where the dinosaurs roamed, from the late Triassic period, to Jurassic period and finally the Cretaceous Period in all 6 episodes. Different dinosaurs plus prehistoic marina creatures that survived during the different era will be featured, their lifestyle, migrating habits, hunting tactics and so on are described in details.

    Furthermore, the computer animated realistic CGI are used to bring the dinosaurs back to life, and with mechnical models being used for closed-up.

    Episode 1: New Blood

    220 million years ago(Late Triassic)

    Episode 2: Time of the Titans

    152 million years ago(Late Jurassic)

    Episode 3: A Cruel Sea

    149 million years ago(Late Jurassic)

    Episode 4: Beneath a Giant's Wing

    127 million years ago(Early Cretaceous)

    Episode 5: Spirits of Silent Forest

    106 million years ago(Early Cretaceous)

    Episode 6: Death of a Dynasty

    65 million years ago(Late Cretaceous)

    Do tune in and I hope to hear your feedback of the documentary.

    Thanks!
