    what's OLL and PLL? i've nvr even heard of these terms haha but i can do under 3min using the cross method. toys R us provides the solution, for all those who duno how to do it.

    i'm interested in other quicker methods but i can't really understand the sh it i found on the net lol.
    OLL = orientate last layer (make the top colour of all the top layer cubies the same) and PLL = permutate last layer (put the last layer cubies into the correct positions)

    for a quicker method, try fridrich CFOP system. basically, its cross (same as beginner method), then corner-edge pairing x4 (fridrich f2l), then OLL, then PLL. 57 OLLs and 21 PLLs for you to learn, nothing to understand really.. just need to memorize them. for F2L i suggest you going to youtube and searching 'fridrich f2l tutorial'
