    Under the agreement, Huawei will provide its innovative distributed Node Bs to help StarHub deliver the full capabilities of HSDPA (high-speed downlink packet access) across the island this year.

    ''We have been impressed by Huawei's 3G technology roadmap, especially their ability to deliver next-generation wireless applications and services quickly. We are confident that Huawei's solutions will help us deliver an enhanced 3G experience to our customers,'' said StarHub's Head of Network and Wholesale, Mr. David Storrie.

    Huawei is a leading pioneer in the field of UMTS/HSPA solutions. It was the first to introduce the distributed Node B solution which allows fast network deployment at lower Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) for its customers. It was the first to support full-performance HSPA with 14.4 Mbps HSDPA, and the first to roll out its 'Iub IP' transmission solution which has proven to save transmission operating expenses (OPEX) of up to 80 percent in the deployment of HSPA networks.
