    I've to quote an old text:

    Humans' destiny in life is based on a trinity of luck, namely:

    Tien Chai (Heaven Luck)
    This is the luck that we're born with. Also known as 'Karma'. We can only know and learn about it thru divination methods like Purple Star Astrology, Four Pillars...etc. But we cannot change it.

    Ti Chai (Earth Luck)
    This is the luck created by the surroundings around us. Good/Bad energies around us can affect us. This is actually fengshui. By enhancing our earth luck, it will lessen the effects of Tien Chai if it is not good. Fengshui does have effect in our life. Just that we can't see it.

    Ren Chai (Mankind Luck)
    This is the luck we created ourselves thru hardwork, positive attitude...etc. Your own doings.

    In short, our destiny depend on each of the three kinds of luck. Lacking in one will have effect in our life. For example, even if your heaven luck is very good, your house's fengshui is very bad, you will still experience misfortune, but maybe not to its full effect of bad fengshui because your heaven luck will "overwrite" some of the bad luck, hence lessening it.

    Fengshui has a considerable effect on your life but it's not everything.
    Even if your heaven luck and earth luck are good, you still have to work for your future! If you don't do your part, whatever good karma and good fengshui also no use.
