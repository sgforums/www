    You must note the person's ratings. For me i have a rating of 47 which means 47 different individuals have bought from me or i bought from them, and i am rated a good.

    People with high ratings are the people you can normally trust more. People with low ratings such as 1 or -1 are normally not trustable.

    As for me, i have done over 60 transactions, and only 2 failed. The items didn't get thru.. Perharps the postman ate the items.

    If you bought something from someone, pay 50% first, than insist on registered mail if you don't have a meet up. So the person cannot accuse you of not choosing registered mail, and the item got lost.

    If you paid, requested for registered mail, but didn't receive the item or a reply after a long time.. Something must be wrong.

    Call the police. There are similar cases.
