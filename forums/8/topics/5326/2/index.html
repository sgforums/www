    What is interesting about the other person that will be an attractive trait?

    There has been this cliche, opposites attract. But I'd like to add that its the similarities that hold them together.

    What kind of person would attract me most? Its like asking myself, what do I want to improve in me that I want to have, as well as what about myself that I like. All these qualities in the other person add up to what attracts me to the other person.

    I am not perfect, it is my strive towards perfection that is what drives me on. To be that Mr Right first before looking for Miss Right. Along the way, what would turn my head would be to meet a person who is also working to be Miss Right. To be able to accept me for what I am, for who I want to be, for who I am going to be, and for what I was before. A person who will let me into her world and for me to know and understand who she was, who she is, who she wants to be, and who she will actually be. In short to share her life and dreams and to dare to take a chance to be vulnerable with me.

    Together, we want to achieve is that we will be right for each other. And if along the way, things do not turn out the way we would like it to be, we will part having been a better person, and also contributing to the betterment of another. And when things turn out great, keep repeating the successes that have worked and keep it running till the end of time.

    Some of the traits that I have actually sat down and thought about:

    Strength of character to say YES to the right things and NO things that will harm, wisdom and the courage to take action , compassion not only to me but to people, loving and unselfish, humility and willingness to be corrected, as well as loving enough to correct me. Proud to be a woman, and takes pride in me being her guy. Confident, understanding, tactful, joyful, patient, listener, responsible, fun, charming, to be my better half, emotionally stable. Intimate(emotionally, intellectually and physically), passionate about life. sense of humour. A wonderful laugh (making loved one laugh really makes me happy). Adventurous spirit. Spontaneous.

    Someone who wants me and yet doesn't need me. This is says a lot about her. That she wants to be with me because she has chosen to be with me, and not the other way around. A person who is independant and can stand on her own. I make my choices in my own life, but it also takes two hands to clap. We must both choose each other

    But this also has its own drawback. She can choose leave and I would be standing alone.

    ME? I have some good points and some not good ones, most of all I want to improve myself day in and day out to be the best gift to the one I choose to be with.
    Because I know that what is most impt about having attractive person, is to be an attractive person to her first

    The sexiest thing a person can wear is their personality.

    At the end of this, I wonder if I shared anything relevant to the topic, but it did get me thinking about the one that I love....

    thanks for your time anyway..
