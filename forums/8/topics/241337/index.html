  • niwde1982

    was confused and dilema whether to hold the rom and actual day on same day or different....

    was comparing the pros and cons.....

    pros:

    1) same days mean u dun need to spend extra money on rom celebration.... save $$$...

    2) rom date and wedding date same

    cons:

    1) customary wedding needs to be on an auspicious date. it mustn't clash with parents and some of the important relatives. therefore, the date we hold our customary may not be the date that we want or the date symbolic to both of us, i.e birthdays or anniversary.

    2) very rushing, some hotel doesn't even allow rom to be held concurrently, say 15min before the dinner. however they do allow ceremony to be held earlier say 6pm prior to dinner. but relatives and friends have to arrived early and thereafter the ceremony they have to wait in vain at the lobby maybe for 1 hr plus or so.(hotel normally dun allow pple to stay in the ballroom as they need to prepare the wedding dinner). As they expect flow of pple to start coming in by 7pm.

    3) quite meaningless to hold tea reception in the morning when both of us are not yet officially husband and wife. it would be better if both of us have already been officially married before the tea reception.

    so for those planning marriage soon or already been married... what do you think? please do share your experiences..........
