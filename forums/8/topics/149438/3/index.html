    I used to draw vignets (a kind of drawing), and calligraphy (latin letters), and best of all, carve pencils with cutter. The pencils will look like mini totems. Very nice. Maybe u guys still in school can try it too.

    Other than that, yeah, chit chat. Or look outside the window. The school has wide open spaces and very quiet, a bit breezy sometimes. My class was on 2nd floor, so could see far far away. Just grass and sky, and some trees. Very nice. My mind would slow down and time pass more quickly.

    If my seat is strategic enough, I would massage a girl seated in front of me. Somehow that would get rid the boredom very quickly. But not too obvious please, I don't want too many people looking. I only massage when my seat is strategic, like at a corner or back.

    Those, if the teacher is in.

    If the teacher isn't in, we change seats and sit close to friends we like. If no teacher for long, some guys would bang the tables and create very nice music (percussion) with lotsa beats from many tables. But this won't last long coz the sound would be too loud and someone would come.

    I think once I got so bored I pretended to be sick. I lied on a bed until the day is over.

    Once I went out from class and didn't return until an hour or so. But strange, I dont remember what I was doing.
