    Alex
    In Maslow Hierachy of needs..self actualization is the last stage of needs provided all the biological needs are met...and to me to maximise one's own potential is very important...As Maslow said.."If a man can be, He must be".....And there are seven needs in Maslow theory...namely
    1.physiological needs
    2.Safety and security needs
    3.Belongingness and love
    4.Self-esteem
    5.Cognitive needs
    6.Aesthetic needs
    7.Self-actualization
    1 being the most basic that needs to be fulfilled before progressing upwards to self-actualization.

    I admire guys who are willing to fight for his own interests and rights...making his potential to the maximum...in other words, intellect and talented and cultured guy will impress me better than those hunks...with good looks and no brains...
