    Once upon a time....

    There was a girl named Joyce. She got a good friend named Eunice. Eunice introduce her friend, Wind to her. Joyce get to know Wind and also Wind's friend Star. She enjoyed chatting with Star very much and everyday they will met each other online and chat at night.

    After chatting for few months, Star realised that he had fall in love with Joyce. Joyce in another way, enjoy chatting with Star's as he really understands her well....one day she ask him to meet up. However, Star rejected. Joyce did not ask him the reason but she never ask him out again. So..they continue chatting every night in this way....

    Till one day....Joyce logged onto the net...she waited the whole night.......she didnt see his name online.....she thought he might have his reasons......next night, she logged in again...and he didnt appear again............after one week..........of waiting........she then realised that Star has sent her an email few days ago..........there's only a word in it.. "goodbye"

    Joyce quickly called Eunice to ask for Wind's number. Wind then told her that Star has migrated. Joyce was shocked! She has never heard Star mentioning about migrating.

    "He had actually wanted to tell you how much he loves you himself...but he can't do it." Wind replied.

    Joyce was in tears, all this happened in such a short time...

    "What? he loves me?"Joyce was stunned...

    "..............he's a mute.. ...I guess its not easy for him all the while...." Wind looked down saying these...and left.........
    That day, Joyce cried....

    Until today, Joyce was still hoping that Star will appear again.......
