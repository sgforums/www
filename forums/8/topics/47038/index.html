    Think about if u sign on, and after 10 yrs, u find it is not suitable for u and would like to find a job outside. Do you even think outside employers will find your 10yrs in the regimental forces as good working experience.

    Last time my inchek, a staff sargent told me this too, said that he signed on around 1988 when the economy was doing really bad and he wanted to have a stable income. Now after his contract ended in around 1994 which is the boom time of Singapore's economy, found he could not find a job outside that will recognize his working experience in the army. Thus in the end he ended up signing on again. This was his advise to us to think carefully when there was a talk to pycho us to sign on.

    So think really carefully, dun go in just for the money.
