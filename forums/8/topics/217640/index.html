    I was very prone to mouth ulcer in the past. My ulcer usually takes 3 weeks to recover and I can get up to 3 on each outbreak. For years, I have been seeking help from Internet and doctors.

    Until, I met a doctor fluent in both western and chinese medicine. The first question he asked me " Have you taken sufficient water and green leavely vegetables? "

    The question strucked me directly as I seldom drink water and dislike green leavely vegetables. Since then, I begin to drink more water each day and taking more "green" vegetables. I also follow my dad's advise to take cold-water bath rather than heated-water bath. To my surprise, the ulcers recover fast and seldom return anymore. As such, I am taking this opportunity to share with those who happen to come across this Post. Past this to your friend who in need.
