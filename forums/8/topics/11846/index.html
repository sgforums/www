SMS To Get Your Drink!

  • Churra
    7 Jun 01, 23:58
    Newsroom bar has just introduced the first-ever SMS drink order service using BuzzCity Mobile Club. No more squeezing and elbowing other thirsty patrons at the bar to shout your order to the bartender. Now, you can display those matrix cool moves, slide your ever-trusty handphone out from your pocket or handbag and SMS the bartender at 97BUZZIT (9728994.

    Once your SMS has been received, it will be captured and displayed on the television screens facing the bartenders. (Also a good way to pick up one of the bartenders or bartendresses!) When your drinks are ready, you will be notified by SMS.

    Mr Peter Wong, owner of Newsroom Bar said, "Since introducing this SMS order service two weeks ago, there has been an increase in sales."

    For a limited period, you will also enjoy discounted rates on drinks if you order through SMS. This service is available to all mobile phone users regardless of their mobile operator. In addition, there is no need to pre-register for this service.

    Without a doubt, other bars will soon follow suit. After all, who can resist the lure of following the pied piper?
