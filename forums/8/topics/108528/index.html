    THE COOLIE
    =========

    Keeper of peace, knight of the land,
    Giving fellatio to knights of high stand,
    Living to work, not working to live,
    Parading for people to see and believe,
    Knights in their armour, with no defect,
    Reeking of having too much self-respect,
    He gets what he wants, he will have his way,
    All else who signed not have nothing to say,
    They are but infidels, slaves they must be,
    Nothing compared to these knights of the free,
    They have to work, sign extra duty,
    Use and abuse, these coolies are free,
    I was among them, I slaved to the max,
    Strange for my father he paid much more tax,
    Tax, to feed bums who call themselves men,
    Swearing to work hard, this islet defend,
    Judges they aren't, yet judgements they lay,
    Unto the coolies who slog everyday,
    We are but coolies, we work without pay,
    When you instruct us we humbly obey,
    What more do you want, oh great Sir Knight?
    Thet we sign extra for your personal delight?
    You get the credit for the work we do,
    And then you make us celebrate with you,
    Annual parties, dinner and dance,
    Squandering taxes at every chance,
    Knight, oh great knight, have you no heart?
    Think of the peasants who truly work hard,
    They who are taxed, so in war you die,
    Yet you use tax money just to get high,
    Impeccable disgrace, villainous swine,
    Giving us extra, making us sign,
    We are not villains, we don't get paid,
    Yet we slog for you, worser than dead,
    Our folks pay you to defend this land,
    Not just to sit back and simply command,
    Continue to sing, you heinous green thing,
    Outside in reality, I will be king,
    You bow before me, overpaid beasts,
    Underworked fungi, living off yeast,
    Ironic how you mongrels command,
    Giving out extra from the palm of your hand,
    For now I'm a coolie, a dog you may kick,
    Don't fear, Sir Knight, soon my balls you will lick.

    Composed by an NSF...
