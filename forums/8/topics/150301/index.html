    You toil from morn till dusk for us,
    You teach us right from wrong.
    You help us do our arts and craft
    and even sing a song.

    We learn to read our nursery rhymes
    and write our ABCs.
    We learn how a little seed grows
    from seedlings into trees.

    You teach us how to love and share -
    We learn to laugh a lot,
    to stop and smell the flowers,
    to give the best we've got.

    As I journey through this life
    with the knowledge you impart
    Never forget on this Teacher's Day
    to say thank you from my heart !

    Wishing all teachers and would-to-be teachers, a
    Happy and Pleasant Teachers' Day.
