Chingay 22, 23 Feb 2013

  • FireIce
    18 Feb 13, 13:10

    There will be road closures and traffic diversions on Feb 22 and 23, for the Chingay Parade Singapore held at F1 Pit Building Area, next to the Singapore Flyer.

    Traffic will be diverted at Republic Boulevard and Raffles Avenue towards the city, as these roads will be closed from 6pm to midnight on both parade days.

    Bus services 56, 75, 77, 97, 97E, 171, 195, 960, 961C serving the bus stop before Temasek Avenue towards Raffles Avenue will not be affected. The Singapore Flyer will also still be accessible to the public.

    Electronic Road Pricing (ERP) gantries on roads leading into the Central Business District will continue to operate as per normal during the road closure.
