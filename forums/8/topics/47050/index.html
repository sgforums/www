A look at Revelation 6 provides us with yet another astonishing insight. In verse 5-6 of the chapter, " A quart of wheat for a day's wages...." a day's labour and yet unable to buy enough wheat tells us the amount of hardship one has to go through inorder to earn a living. With economic downturn and increase in unemployment as the backdrop, how relevant is this prophecy to us ? Animals killing a fourth of world's population how can it be? But once again, we are brought to attention that the current killer diseases that has emerged lately came from animals. How can it be so?

The Bible warned its readers that these are the signs and indications of the second coming of the Lord Jesus Christ which marks the final episode of world history ending with the Judgement day............Coincidental or fufilment of biblical prophecies.....You decide?
