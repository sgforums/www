    Why not we play suka suka one? those take part in Tournament go by the rules. Other players who wish to spar with each others only, then play play no international rules.

    The basics: 8Ball
    - Black 8 ball cannot be pocket in until you completed the 7 balls.
    - Black 8 and white ball cannot be pocket in together.
    - Tyco ball is consider as fault1. Meaning when you aim to enter a specific hole, it didn't enter, instead entered another hole. Fault1, player drop his turn.
    - Hit wrong ball. For example, you should be hitting strips, and you hit solid full color. Consider fault1 too.
    - If white ball missed your color you are hitting, consider it foul. Foul, player will lose his turn and gave his opponent a chance to select the new position of the white ball.
    - If white ball entered any hole or fall away from the table, it is counted as Foul.
    - If player or any object belongs to the player including the cue player is holding touches any ball excluding the whiteball. is considered as Fault1.
    - At least 1 leg must touches the ground.

    Basic Rules in Pool 8Ball.
