    Come and watch our first pool tournament! It promises to be an exciting and fun-filled event with 32 players! Bring food! Bring friends! Bring alcohol(?)! See you there.

    sgForums.com 1st 8-Ball Championships 2002

    Venue: MONSTERCUE 2 @ Meridien Hotel #02-17/18

    Date & Time: 1st September, Sunday, 1400 - 1800

    Prizes
    1st Prize - $60 cash + Pool cue with case worth $69 + 1 year sgForums.com Premier Membership worth $20 + Trophy

    2nd Prize - $40 cash + Pool cue with case worth $69 + 1 year sgForums.com Premier Membership worth $20 + Trophy

    3rd Prize - Pool cue with case worth $69 + 1 year sgForums.com Premier Membership worth $20 + Trophy

    Registration Fee
    Pay only $10 & get
    - Free 1 month sgForums.com Premier Membership
    - Free Monstercue VIP card (discounts for playing time & pool accessories like cues and chalk at Monstercue outlets)
    - A few fun-filled frames of pool + a chance of winning!

    Rules
    World Standardised Rules.
    Referee's decisions are final. There will be an explanation of the rules beforehand to all players.

    Tournament Format
    "Double Elimination" format at the beginning so you need to lose 2 games before being knocked out, followed by best of 3 knockout.

    Eligibility
    Only amateur players allowed. Friends of forum members can join too. No professional players allowed. No personal cues are to be used.

    Registration
    Players should arrive by 1330 to register. At 1400 SHARP if there are still players who have not arrived, they will forfeit their spots and anyone who is present can register for the empty spot. The competition will begin at 1430.

    Invitation
    This is not a serious tournament!!! It's just for fun, more like an activity for members to make friends and know who the other forumnites are So if you play pool, even if you're only a beginner, feel free to join us! Please! Also, supporters and birdwatchers are welcome

    Thanks
    Many thanks to Monstercue for agreeing to host this tournament & sponsoring the pool cues with cases, the trophies, and the VIP cards. They have just opened a new outlet at Bukit Timah Plaza so watch out for their special offer to sgForums.com's members soon! If you have the time, do check out their website: www.monstercue.com.

    How To Join?
    Our registration is full. Apologies to all who wanted to play but did not manage to get a place. However, if any of the players do not turn up by 1400 on the day of the tournament itself, his/her spot will be open to the public. If you want to come down and try your luck, do private message me as well so I can put you on the queue.
