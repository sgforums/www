    Maybe you should avoid taking too much coffee in the morning too...cos excess caffeine produce similar effects...

    However....hmmm.....I am not trying to scare you...but if it occurs rather frequently even when you are not doing anything....maybe jsut sitting down doing work......it is usually not a very good sign....cos if I didnt rem wrongly....numbness in arms are warning signs of stroke....
