    What is the purpose of life?
    Life is to be born then to die ninety years later.
    Hence, the purpose is to lengthen age to infinity.
    But it seems unlikely, so I have to accept eventual death.

    While waiting for my death
    I realize that to live requires money
    So my first goal is to earn
    With it, I can last my life until death

    With the money
    I look around to see what is affordable
    I think, I do, I wait
    Finally I die,

    With death, I will be permanently gone
    Without body
    Without memory
    Nothing to do
    Nothing to think

    Such is not as good as living
    But it is not as if I have a choice
