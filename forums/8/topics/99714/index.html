School of Rock opens

  • kiri
    26 Oct 04, 16:39
    A real life school of rock has opened its doors in Argentina.

    The Palermo Sounder School in Buenos Aires teaches students rock music alongside traditional subjects like maths and geography.

    Other subjects include history and analysis of rock and pop music, how to organise a band, rock and roll clothes' design and artistic production.

    School spokesperson Ariel Garbarz said the school was inspired by the School of Rock movie and also by the 1980s TV series Fame.

    He told Clarin: "We want to educate quality musicians who will help revive the name of Rock and Roll and will also change the world."
