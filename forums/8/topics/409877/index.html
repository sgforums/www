Sembawang Pajama Party - 10 SEP.

  • Argyle
    2 Sep 10, 18:34

    I'm part of a small organization called the Singapore Regional Recreation Committee. We're a small social group comprised of members of the US Navy community in Sembawang. We're a private organization that sets up events, usually for the local community here, but we're looking to open up our doors to try and meet some new local people.

    On 10 September, we're having a Pajama Party at a place known locally as the "Aussie House" on 203 Lagos Circle, corner of Canada Rd and Lagos Circle (Google Maps Link). Party starts @ 1800 until late. We'll have a DJ playing American music, food and drinks (alcoholic and non-alcoholic) for a small fee, games and prizes. We'll have a Wii set up on a projector as well. If you'd like to come, feel free...there's no cover. I'll be wearing my favorite pink pajamas, but if walking around in your pajamas makes you uncomfortable (appropriate pajamas, not lingerie please!) you can dress normally. We'll offer a prize for the best pajamas. We'll have changing room available too. Come out, dance, eat, meet new people and have a good time!

    If you have any questions, need directions, or want to RSVP (not necessarry, but awesome if you do), contact me via the board, or e-mail me at SembawangSRRC@gmail.com

    *** Note: this is not a professional event, so please don't expect it to be a high-class affair...leave the suits and gowns at home ;-)
