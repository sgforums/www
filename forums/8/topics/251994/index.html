    Backpacking is safe, as long as you exercise common sense in looking after your own security, and your belongings. It's good to at least have a partner on the trip who can at least help to look after your big backpack while you visit the toilet.

    Seriously, it's good value for money kind of travelling and it's the only way to really mingle with the locals, get to know how the average person lives, etc. since package tours will only take you to the touristy places of interests and shopping malls.
