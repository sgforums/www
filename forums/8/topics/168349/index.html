    I was from SIM-UOL. So maybe i can answer your question.

    I have not seen anyone studied the first 2 years in S'pore and last year in London. But, there are cases whereby some students do exceptionally well and are awarded scholarship to London - LSE - London School of Economics (LSE is one of the best Uni in the world, top 4 or 5 at least)

    The one that can do the last year overseas is probably RMIT. RMIT students are encourage to study for one sem ( i think) in RMIT Australia. This is not applicable to UOL.

    Those who complete in Singapore will have a cert that says Degree awarded EXTERNALLY. Other than that it is all the same. We take the same standard of paper, as those in London.

    Hope this help. Call SIM if u got any queries, they quite friendly..
