    The Art of Music Pte Ltd, founded by Mr. Andre Tan, is a music haven supported by a dynamic team of dedicated music professionals from the local music industry. Spreading their passion for the performing arts, the school offers an expansive list of courses to serve your musical needs. The Art of Music is here to nurture that musician in you.

    Teaching with passion, learning with joy!

    Our team of qualified and dedicated mentors will introduce students to an exciting journey of developing their personal musical style. We aspire to lend a breath of fresh air to music appreciation in a fun, experimental, and encouraging atmosphere.

    The following is a list of workshops we provide:
    • Pop Vocals
    • A Cappella
    • Beatbox
    • Hip-hop Dance
    • Pop & Jazz Guitar
    • Song & Lyric-writing
    • Music and Audio Production
    These workshops are available as a single-session, as well as a series of regular sessions.

    Some of our in house instructors are:
    - Sugianto (Project Super Star Finalist ‘05 and Taiwan's One Million Star Contestant [Representing Singapore])
    - Charles Wong (Champion Beatboxer and Judge of Beatbox Asia Championships ‘08)
    - Mizaki (Lead Guitarist of Superband Finalist, Lucify)
    - Mark Shi (Local music producer and Song-writer)
    - Zahiah Jumat (Noise Singapore 2005 - Judges’ Choice)
    - Tay Kewei ( Founder of Pop-Jazz band Project DIY, band member on world tours with David Tao, JJ Lin)

    For more information on our packages and lessons, feel free to visit our website at www.theartofmusic.com.sg or contact us at 6737 2700
