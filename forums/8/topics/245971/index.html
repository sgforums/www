    When I read self-help/motivation/success books, I always ask "How?"

    tue wif morrie teaches you to question many things in life, what is true to you? what is important to you? is doing what you do to chase what you want really what you want? be forgiving, etc etc.

    I always ask "How?"

    How do you forgive somebody? To put it in an engineering way, what must happen before it is forgiven?

    What is your purpose in life? HOW do you find it? HOW do you know this is the true purpose/the most fulfilling for you?

    What is important to you. HOW to find what is important to you? At my life stage, money is important, so is career. Family will take a backseat now. My friends and I consider myself a rather realistic but amibitious person. But HOW do I know what is truely important?

    Do one really have to loss something, a love one, an entire career, a healthy body, etc before one will know what is important to him/her?
