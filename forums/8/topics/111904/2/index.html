    A job agency is also known as management consultant, employment agency, headhunter, etc. Many fancy names for the same low-class faeces.

    When companies want to employ people, it is sometimes more convenient to outsource the function to these job agencies. The companies will tell the job agencies what jobs are available in their company, what kind of people they are looking for, what their budget is, etc. The job agencies will then look for the people from the public lor.

    In return, the job agencies will get paid from the companies when a candidate is successful (usually a fraction of the salary).

    No, if you go to these agencies, they will not help you to find jobs. But if they happen to have some vacancies already, and you appear at the right time, they will recommend you to these jobs.
