    Bus rams another at Toa Payoh Interchange injuring one passenger

    By Chai Ping/Ong Chung Hui

    A bus crashed into another at Toa Payoh Interchange this morning.

    The impact shattered the windsheld of one bus while the other suffered a slight dent to its rear.

    Passengers were boarding Service 238 when the second bus operating the same service rammed its back.

    10 passengers were on the first bus.

    A woman who was carrying her son suffered slight injuries to her head when she fell.

    She was brought to Singapore General Hospital.

    None of the other passengers were hurt.
