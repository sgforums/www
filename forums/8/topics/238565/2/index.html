    I've been using the N80 for more than 6 months now and I must say that I'm not very satisfied with it.

    Firstly, though the camera is 3 megapixels, the picture quality is not very good due to the lack of autofocus, and you can forget about taking outdoor night shots as the flash is pathetic.

    Secondly, the Symbian OS is very unstable in my opinion, and prone to hang or restart on its own rather frequently without warning.

    Thirdly, the battery life, at 3 days max, is quite poor.

    Lastly, for some reason the antenna is rather inefficient, and during calls it would warm up rather alarmingly. Goodness knows how many of my brain cells have been killed by the resulting radiation.

    Hence, my most sincere advice to you is to buy any other phone than the N80, and try as much as possible to avoid Symbian OS ones.
