    Ok, Jurong Kerchil areas as I have described, has many makan areas. One of the famous Boon Tiong Kee is there , famouse for they chicken rice and price tag . Along that row, you'll find also the Al Ahmin, 24 hour muslim makan place, oh, bikers will love this place... lots of malay bikers hang out there. There's 2 pubs and a wine bar there too round the corner, facing the field and bukit timah food centre. Note, wine bar sells quality wine from around the world... Oh, around the petrol station in Jurong Kerchil, there are a few makan places, seems one of the prata shops pretty good.
    For hill view, check out the quiet spots at Jalan Remaja, opposite Lam Soon Industrial building. You'll find another branch of Al Ahmin, Balik Kumpong massage hut, very professional, very clean and afforadable, and Gone Fishing cafe. The owner's Kenny. I hang out there... you can bring your own book or get his, see the artworks there. And play your own CDs as well.
