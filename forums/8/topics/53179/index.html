    Originally posted by pineapple_pie:
    hey how you all know that ppl are trying to hack into your pc?? i don noe leh .... can tell me more in details?
    well, we are using a firewall program which detects if there are people trying to connect to your pc without your permission. Even if you do not have valuable information for them to take, it may also results in some other nasty things if they are malicious.(which i think most are not,most just do such things to test their skills for fun).Basically if they find a way it, they can do almost everything on it as if they are the owner.

    Eg, they are able to install something in your computer to make it do works for them, eg being suddenly your computer is being used to host porns. For certain things like stealing information, they can either get it directly from your hard drive if u store the info there or they can install programs like key loggers that will be able to remember every key stoke u key in on the computer. Example if u use internet banking often ,u will need to key in passwords, that program will be able to remember all your keystoke and send a report back to that intruder. Based on that, they are able to analysis that data and get your passwords, user ID and so on.

    Basically the longer you keep your pc and your internet connection on, the more time they have to keep on trying to break into your system.If so a firewall is your first line of defense against them. To use a firewall does not really require you to be that computer savvy, that goes for me too. I just brought the program, put in the CD, install it and it will automatically go to work. Of course it works 2 way, protect people from intruding and also to prevent your computer from sending out info to other parties. Thus your program may prompt u on what kind of connection u will allow to other parties. To check if there are intruders, just just have to open up that program again and it will show u how many blocked intrusions there are that day.

    I m using a norton firewall program, if u want to have more info on it ,u can refer to the link below. This is a pay to use program but there are also certain other firewall program that u can download for free usage from sites such as ZDNet.com.
