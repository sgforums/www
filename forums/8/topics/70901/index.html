    Try plugging your cable directly to the modem, turn off the modem 1st.

    Disable your internet connection using right click at the network connections folder.

    Turn on the modem and wait for activity light.

    Enable the connection with right click again.

    Ur IP and subnet mask should be there to see on the bottom left corner of the network connections folder.

    Open ur browser to check if it connects to internet.

    Tada!

    The above instructions should work well with windows XP and SCV connection.

    Good luck.
