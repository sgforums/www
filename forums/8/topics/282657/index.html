    HDB Pet Regulations
    Dog Breeds Allowed In HDB Apartments

    AVA assists the HDB in ensuring that dog licences are only issued to dogs that qualify to be kept in HDB premises. HDB apartments are allowed only 1 dog of a toy breed or its cross (that stands at 40 cm at the shoulders and weighs 10kg or less).

    The breeds approved by HDB to be kept in HDB apartments are as follows:

    1 Affenpinscher 32 Little Lion Dog
    2 Australian Silky Terrier 33 Maltese
