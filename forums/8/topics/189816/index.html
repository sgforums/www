    Originally posted by photo_seeker:
    Can someone also advice me how to fully save all 400 contacts that I've stored in my phone memory. Will I be able to restore the back-up from my 6670 to my New N70 or do I need to do manual transfer?
    Bluetooth everything over if u have the 2 phones in front of you. Options -> Mark All -> Send -> Via Bluetooth.

    Or you can copy all to your SIM card. Go to Options -> Mark All -> Copy -> To SIM directory. You will lose all the special details like Email Address, Address, 2nd Mobile Number etc. if your SIM card does not support it, or they'll be stored as duplicate entries.

    For me, I bluetooth back to my iBook so the backup is always there for any phone to load the contacts list.
