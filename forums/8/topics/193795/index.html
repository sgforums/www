    WE REFER to the letter 'Desperate Housewives on TV Mobile not good' (ST, May 25) by Ms Rupa Balaguru.
    The hit drama series on MediaCorp TV Channel 5 has been in simulcast on TV Mobile since the first season and was well-received by viewers.

    However, with Desperate Housewives' second season, compelling issues have brought new twists to the storyline and some of these may not be suitable for young viewers.

    We have thus dropped this series from our programming schedule and instead replaced it with Diva on a Dime, an entertaining series that will cater to viewers of all ages.

    We would like to assure Ms Balaguru that we take viewers' feedback seriously and constantly review our programming line-up to ensure that it meets the needs and preferences of our viewers on the move.

    We thank Ms Balaguru for her valuable feedback and hope she will continue to support TV Mobile.

    Kim Wong-Nathan (Ms)
    Programming Director
    TV Mobile
    MediaCorp TV
