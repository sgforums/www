    The concept of reasonable violence is debatable . The irritating person who pisses you off may think he is right just as you think you're right . If every self-righteous person uses these so-called justifiable violence , what do we get but chaos .

    Even if it is clearly justifaible , there is still the level of violence to use . If a person is verbally violent should you reply with a punch (physical violence) or shout back .

    Should we deal with the situation with a higher intensity of violence or just the bare minimum . For example if a person punches you , you are justified to block and punch back . But suppose if you knock him to the ground , are you justified to kick him till you're satisfied ?

    No.Because then the violence you exhibit becomes unjustified .
    But don't get me wrong . There are certain circumstances where violence is indeed justifiable like protecting a person from a bully .

    But in cases where the boundaries are not so clear-cut , how do we know in the heat of the moment ?
