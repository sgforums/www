    rollin down the mountain right?

    Aniway, i was sitting in the middle of my mum and bro

    in front of me. So i pushed back with my legs and covered
    my family with my hands and saw chaos....

    Eventually the van did stop rolling, reasons which escapes me
    at that moment. All i am concerned of is to get every one out
    quick. My family was safe but my aunt flew out of the van
    with her baby through the window.....She did not put on the
    safety belt... and she was carrying her baby....

    Well i saw her dying in front of me with a shard of glass in her
    throat. The driver which , tragicly is her husband, saw her

    How we got to hospital, i forgot but luckily everyone was safe,
    save the baby. Michelle(my baby niece) , fractured her skull

    lucky that her bones are soft and still growing, so with
    luck, she may still heal... Well she is alive today. Still a
    beautiful child. But she has problems learning things, and
    interacting with people.

    Well, want to know how the accident happened?
    When i closed my eyes, we were driving on mountain
    roads. But the accident really happened after we left
    the mountains. With the rest of us sleeping, no one
    noticed my uncle dozed off on the wheel. And he knocked
    against a railing on the side of the flat road. He woke
    up, jerked the wheel to the opposite direction, saw the van
    turn and turn....and his wife dying, his baby crying with
    his own folly.
