    July on the Chinese calendar (roughly beginning end of August) is considered Ghost Month, during which travel is discouraged, so this is considered a good time to travel to Taiwan since this is a low travel season, travel is cheaper and there are many festivals and interesting religious ceremonies to take in.

    October and November are the most enjoyable months in Taiwan weather wise (with reduced humidity), so these are considered good months to travel to Taipei. Taiwan is a subtropical climate, so the summer months can be uncomfortably humid and travel might be uncomfortable.
