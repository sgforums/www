    oki, it's just another motorcyclist who was knocked down by a lorry...
    normally, i would have just neutral feelings about it, well, when a man
    needs to go, he has to....

    however, this person was from my school, my course, my batch. Although i
    don't know him at all, apart from seeing him during lec or lab sessions, i
    have never talked to him, smiled at him or any minor eye contact...

    i'll never get the chance to do it anymore.......

    most importantly, he was sitting beside me when we took a paper on sat, he
    was still alive and kicking, so vibrant. I even caught a glimpse of his
    matric card, saw his photo. commented in my mind that how short his hair was
    as compared to now....

    AND it was the exact same photo that i can relate to immediately , printed
    on the front page on the local evening tabloid, with the headline "motorist
    knocked down by hijacked lorry after exams". i dropped my maths notes, and
    came to a halt......

    My worst fears came, HE was that person..

    i have no right to be sad, cause i don't know him, but i HAVE the right to
    be angry, not because of the person who knocked him down is a bastard and is
    a criminal because of drugs and VCD. That asshole was supposed to have an
    operation done, but he escaped, hijacked a lorry and in a moment of
    insanity(god-damn this asshole, why didn't you just get your damn op
    done???), jammed on the stupid accelerator, knocked down this lec mate that
    i have never even talk to.....

    and to glamourise the story, the asshole jumped down from a building later
    after commiting the stupid act....

    and my lec mate wasn't even given a choice to choose whether he wants to
    save himself......

    and now he is gone....

    F***, we all in the middle of our exams, AND HE DIDN'T EVEN FINISH IT....and
    he will never know his results.... we struggled so hard, just to get the
    damn degree, to join the stupid workforce and so on......


    i'm not trying to act concerned, kick a big fuss about it, or to feel sorry
    just because everybody does so, nor that i'm trying to pretend to be a
    philosophical yuppie
    i just feel how human lives can be so cheap and worthless, it could end any
    time, any place, anywhere, any how....
    and i'm just wasting my life here, killing myself with all the cheap alcohol
    that i can lay my hands on.....

    through the mountains and the valleys
    i arrived at my sacred ground
    an ascetic welcomed me in
    he has waited for a lifetime

    an holy ground
    the time has come
    for me to enter into a new world

    i dream of my holy shell
    when shall it recieved me...?

    tuts goes on, lec goes on, exams goes on, work goes on, life goes on,

    soon this incident may just become our part of our distant memory, long
    forgotten.....

    a new life starts every second....
    an old life ends every second....
    when will it be my turn to start the end....
