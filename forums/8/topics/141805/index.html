    NKF CEO T.T Durai has withdrawn his defamation claim against Singapore Press Holdings and its writer Susan Long.

    Mr Durai said he would also drop NKF's defamation claim against the two parties.

    Speaking to reporters after court was adjourned, Mr Durai said he dropped the case as he did not want to proceed with the matter any further.

    Mr Durai said this decision was taken in the best interest of NKF.

    Asked by reporters if he would resign, Mr Durai said that he didn't think that this was necessary.

    The CEO is expected to appear again in court tomorrow afternoon when the court will decide on the issue of legal costs.
