  • january
    5 Jun 05, 23:55
    i had this idea pop up suddenly it goes like this and i need you all to spread ...... it throughout the internet world, then spill it to television, and other media format

    i think our current aim as individual and collective human race is to discover anti ageing knowledge, to cure common ageing disease because no matter how rich you are, no matter how wise you are, death will befall on you as long as we have not discover techniques to increase human lifespans greatly.

    there are already many organisations in this field of preseving age and fighting common death but to wait for them to achieve results while we watch the star wars episode III is wrong. we need every human brain to cooperate to see if we can develop the technique. if we have the whole human race working towards these comman aim, then our chances of getting it will increase very large or at least we can discover if it is possible in the first place to not die.

    but this aim has many obstacles that i can think of offhand and i propose the following. the whole planning process will be difficult thus i require the help of all human race and the first step is for you readers to propagate this messages around the globe to gain momentum.

    some of the things we have to settle first before embarking are
    stop capitalism
    stop enjoying life
    stop violence
    stop religion
    stop politics
    stop fighting for territory, national pride
    stop building your personal ego, fame, power, money
    stop materialism
    stop educating non relevant stuff
    provide basic utilities to the poor and sick or transport them to safer environment and try to incorporate them in the project
    stop philosophing because it is less important than ending natural death
    stop competition between nation, states, schools, races, let all tune up to the common aim
    divert all resources to this project
    reduce global income gap, we cannot have the rich people spending luxury life while the majority work on this project

    much fine tuning is still needed but the first thing is to spread and i got a hunch the common sense of this project will provide the drive and engine to keep this alive.
