    Sameera Reddy features in a video game
    By IndiaFM News Bureau, June 7, 2006 - 05:37 IST

    Download image as wallpaper

    Sameera Reddy has become the first Indian actress to feature in a video game. The game designed with mobile phones will show Sameera as a warrior princess who fights savage warriors. The action takes place in an ancient Indian city where she fights the tribes trying to attack her father’s kingdom. Looks like the Reddy girl is all set to become the Indian version of Lara Croft!

    The game is named Sam’s Mission and includes special effects and fights. The actress, who claims to be a gaming freak herself, says that she even carries a portable Playstation when going on sets. In fact it was her fascination for video games that made her accept this project.

    Sameera says that she has been working on this since one year and the demo version of the game has turned out great.
