    Why not soak in the greenery and nature with your family or friends at Gardens By The Bay (Rhu Cross), all for a good cause! 

    Bare Your Sole is an event that encourages all ages to come down and walk to raise funds to help the elderly in Singapore. The funds will go to Habitat Singapore, "Project Homeworks" where we clean up homes of the elderly poor because EVERYONE deserves a proper, living environment.

    It's a great cause to support and you can check out more about the event details on the FB page or visit their official website!

    Use this opportunity to do our part to society and at the same time, spend quality time with friends and family! :D

    Hope to see you guys there!
