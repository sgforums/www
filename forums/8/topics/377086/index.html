    To clarify first.

    N level with full passes.

    Normal Academic or Normal Technical ?

    If Normal Technical, it is not advisable to do private "O" level as the gap between NT and O level is very large.

    If Normal Academic and you are willing to work hard ie to spend a lot of time doing past year exam papers on top of attending the private "O" level classes and working part time in the day, it is possible. However, you need to realise that you will have give up most or all of your social life and you need a lot of physical and mental stamina too.

    ITE is  REALLY NOT it is the end. Do not be misled by the image issue of ITE. ITE can really teach and you can really learn a skill that can allow you to get a job easily.

    As compared to ITE, a poor "O" level result with all F9, E8, D7 will not get you anywhere or even a proper job.
