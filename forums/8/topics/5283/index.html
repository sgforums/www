    I'm addin my 2 cents worth from a guy's point of view.
    I have to say in this kinda situation, it very much depends on individuals and the couple involved in this dilemmma.
    Sure i agree wif Jerrick's point abt not being able to guarantee that the guy will not rise in status, but does everyone thinks the same?

    The age of the couple involved affects the outcome of this issue greatly.
    Young couples - "Yao ai qing bu yao mian bao"
    (Direct translation:Want luv dun wan bread)
    They're young, looking for fun, not in the society working yet, so this doesn't affect them one bit.
    Mature couples - maybe from 21 onwards? Things are different already, does this guy have a career? Good prospects? Ambitious? Able to support the relationship financially?
    Fail any of this criterias and I can guarantee u won't get too many girls from 22 onwards.

    Very importantly is not what her parents think, but what she thinks of you. Does she trust you? You should constantly show her that you're tryin to upgrade urself even when ur status isn't the same as hers. There is no 'appropriate action' on her part, only the guy's.

    Whether it will affect the relationship adversely depends on the girl's character.
    More than often, sadly, it does.

    Naturally, the guy will feel some kinda stress in many ways. Other people's views.
    How people look at them n stuffs.
    I have a friend who lives in normal HDB flat like many comin from a normal family and he's got a gf who lives in a private house.
    He rides a scrambler and have some difficulties upkeepin it.
    She, on the other hand, drives a Rovers Convertible.
    U shld imagine how it feels when the girl's drivin ye ard in HER car.

    It's a realistic world out there nowadays.
    We can't have everything we dream of. A guy can only show how capable he is to be successful and not ponder over issues of whether or not in this kinda situation, the girl and her family will accept him.
