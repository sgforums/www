    (5) An experienced street fighter may have weapons.

    (6) An experienced street fighter will be able to keep you flinching or covering up.....and you will have no balance, and can't catch your tempo, to counter-attack.

    (7) If the kick is not hard enough, it will not knock the person out. He will be able to ignore the pain and continue attacking you.
