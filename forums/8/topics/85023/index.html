    Jill, a 20+ year old girl, is at a marriageable age. She's been in a relationship for more than 2 years with this guy who is not fantastically good looking (in fact, very average looking).

    About the guy (Francis)....
    Francis is the only son of a businessman who owns a manufacturing business, with branch offices in various locations such as JB, Penang, Shanghai, Suzhou, Singapore and a few other places. This businessman is very old fashioned, which means that he will let his only son inherit the whole business one day. Francis does not have a mind of his own. He will listen to whatever his parents (esp the mother) says.

    About Francis's mother...
    She only has secondary education. Very old fashioned and superstitious. She believes that all girls should do ALL the housechores and listen to the man. She literally told the Jill she will think that Jill is not well brought up by her parents if she fails to do the housechores. She also told Jill that women at any time are one level lower than men.

    Also, she told Jill that the latter should not mix women's clothing with men's when doing the laundry. Her rationale is: women's clothing esp the pants and shorts will bring the men bad luck.

    Jill, being a graduate, of course, buay tahan the future mother-in-law. But she really wants to be with Francis. After all, Jill and Francis have been together for quite a long period. They have been doing things together on their off days or weekends and they do enjoy being with each other.

    Now Jill and Francis are talking about getting married. Francis, being a filial child, wants to live with the parents after they get married, even though he is fully aware that his mother could be a nightmare for Jill.

    What do you think Jill should do?
