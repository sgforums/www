    depending on ya hair texture and face shape. It makes alot of difference when u take that into context.

    Guys with straight hair looks pretty good in short spikey styles i think. Cos long straight hair is totally out of fashion. Machiam girl.

    Guys with wavy (not curly!) hair should check out the long styles with flowing locks. Very appealing since they dont have to go perm their locks to achieve that look.

    Curly hair should just be botak. Like black guys... so ultra cool. basically curly hair when long looks like a afro. when short have no body.

    now to face shape.

    If u got round face, get the long hair as it helps to "slim" your head down.

    Oval shape face is the most desirable as it can go with any hairstyle!

    Square face (those with strong jawline) , up to u, if u think ya jaw lines r sexy, go for short crop. But if u think its ugly, long hair to cover it up.

    Certain face features r essential too, like if u got high cheek bones, definatly go for short hair to show ya cheeks out, not many people got nice cheeksbones.

    Hollow cheek (like super model type undergoing stravation), again definatly short as it shows the contour of ya face.

    Fat cheek, go with long hair as it helps to cover up the "bufffiness".

    High forehead should go with hairstyles with fringe as it help to shape ya face.

    Those with protruding jaws should have long hair to help cover it up.

    Big eyes should go with short hair as it helps to "illuminate" ya eyes.

    These r the following tips to help u choose!
