    Let me share my experience wif u guys abt doing recording in the studio. The orchestra which I used to play in decided to come up wif a CD. It's only then I saw the wonders of sound editing...

    1) Using a mixer, a single person can play a part from his music score, stop the music at the point. And play a new part OVER the point where he was previously playing, mixing the new sound into the old one. Thus giving rise to the new meaning of a 'One-man band'

    2) He also can stop at any point, take a break and then CONTINUE from the point that he had stopped playing.

    Actually all these processes need not be done on a very high tech machine. A modern home computer can do all that. Believe me, I've done all those stuff mentioned above during my school project...
