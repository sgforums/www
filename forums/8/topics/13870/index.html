    the course tt NUS offer is not called Mass Comm, but something similar...

    "Information and Communications Management"
    most ppl call it ICM
    offered in FASS

    there is a "brother course" over in SoC called Communications and Media

    in SoC, the ppl mainly focus on technical parts such as programming and design..

    can say tt is slightly more specialised than Comm Studies in NTU
    note: Mass Comm and Comm Studies are 2 different scope
    in ngee ann is Mass comm, in NTU is comm studies (straight hons)
