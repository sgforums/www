    Just found a fantastic recipe for instant noodle.

    Ingredients

    1)Indomee. (Tom Yam flavour dry)
    2)Sesame Oil
    3)Bar chor
    4)Soy Sauce
    5)Chili sauce (Indofood brand)
    6)Vinegar

    Season bar chor with soy sauce and sesame oil.

    Bring water to boil. Add noodles, bar cho, off fire and wait for 2/1/2 mins. Drain away water.

    To prepare the sauce, empty all the packets of seasoning on plate, add 1 teaspoon vinegar, some chili sauce, and mix well. Add noodle and bar chor, then stir well.

    Enjoy! ... remember, u heard this from the laksa man!
