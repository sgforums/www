    Dear Revelations,

    When I was younger, I believed that I should fight for my love. Even if the gal say no, I would persist until one day I know that it's time to give up, and move on with my life. As I grew older, after experiencing two beautiful yet heartbreaking love lives, I realized that there is no fight for love at all..

    It does not matter how hard you fight, because if you do not possess the x-factor which women wanted, you can never make it work, because a r/s needs two hands to clap. So, what about waiting for love? I don't know whether it would also good to wait for love.

    I would specify it in a very simple example : suppose I like this gal, but I know that she is not interested in me as a bf, but as a friend, and the circumstances at this time do not permit it, I would not fight at all. My reasons are very simple :

    (a)If you like (or love) someone, u have to let her go, give her the space for her to do what she wants.

    (b)Perhaps, if insistence on love would lead to the destruction of friendship.

    I can tell her what I feel, and also tell her that I would not make any further advancements, and just be a good friend. If something would happen in the future, then let it happen. If there is nothing in the end, I would wish her well and hope that she would find that person she love.

    I think ultimately it is not what I desire which is important, being sensitive to her needs and understanding to things around would help. So, my answer is : let nature takes it course...

    Yours,
    The Prodigal
