    I use to face this problem so now I only paint the surfaces that needs to be paint, joint parts dun paint or join already then paint. Remember if you want to paint over a joint part, 1) Wait until it is completing dry 2) The colour of the paint must match (Be careful, usually I only leave a small area for the joint parts such that if the colour really does not match, it is still not noticable).

    Yup and I agree too to paint it before jointing them up. It looks better I did once the reverse on a F14 Tomcat and it turn on a mess.

    A tip I learned also is: If you are just doing for display and not building the model in a landscape/scenario.
    Then use a small black pen about 0.1 to 0.3 tip to do highlighting on the grid lines this brings out the details and look better but dun over do it.
