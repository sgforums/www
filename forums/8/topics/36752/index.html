    As for Australia study - lots of problems you may face.

    I did my degree in Curtin ( didn't manage to do well to do it in local Uni - but manage to got into NTU for its Msc - master degree cse ---- Funny Eh ??? That's Life ) I prefer poly actually more practical nowadays. More value than degree I feel.

    FYI, for your case you can do at least a intensive foundation year b/4 you can go to a Uni (provided you pass & meet a min. std) . You still need to do maths & I heard it is even more difficult than S'pore maths or the same at least. Education std there is pretty good , I going to send my children back for study from Kindergarden to UNi . if time comes.

    But, I guessed it is easy for you to say you want an overseas study - first so expensive abt $30 per Yr total $90k at least just Uni only without foundation / pre-Uni yr. And, homesickness are you mentally strong enough to be indepedent - cook, wash your own clothes , look for & rent a hse or room, go to a doctor when sick etc Many ??? to take care not a bed of roses .

    For me I am a guy - I love the life there ,love to cook , make friends easily including the local friends - chinese thou' - all these help to avoid/solve the above problems. I join the students councils

    So think carefully - take a step back & Ponder what you do best ?
