    6 Feb 06, 04:49
    There are many fun things also..... At one time, during confinement weekend. We decide to have Korean BBQ in the Bunk. We brought an ultra thin mess tin as a stove... smuggled some fresh meat and started BBQ.

    Guess what ... It was not a good idea.. It seemed that the oil and tha tin couldn't take the heat.. and the whole mess tin was ablaze with fire. ... Imagine the amt smoke created... Especially pouring cups of water help spread the fire.... what WTF... imagine the comotion, swearing and bewilderness. Anywhere, We were able to put out the fire by covering the fire with wet blankets and tons of water on it.

    To make things worst... the OCS trainee on inspection round came.... who notice the whole lenght block was missy than usual. I think we held the world record of fatique duties of clearing the mess.... actually we dumped the whole mess out the rear window and hose the floor to cover the carbon smell.

    The Inspection team came up.. and asked if anything wrong and why the floor is wet. We wayang all the way.. saying that one of my bunk mate was possessed and when wild and we had to drouse him. Then one of them enquire about the Carbon smell. We said we were also curious why and that we had notice it the whole night... They looked puzzled and left.

    As the inspection team was walking away... We understood why... KNN the whole entire blocks of barrack was heavily misty and very spocky... maybe they were spooked too.. while coming here... damn lucky... then again it was actually smoke.

    You see... the inspection team have to walk across a make-shift wooden bridge over a small river in thick vegetation to get to our block. While the fire was going on one of my bunk mate was "watching the water".
