    In my opinion...There are 2 different kinds of guitarists
    1) The Technical(steve vai, John Petrucci,malmsteen)
    2) The Melodious(Joe Satriani ,Jimi Hendrix,Tom Morello,
    Stevie Ray Vaughan)

    Technical guitarists have amazing abilities in terms of executing their guitars...Songs they produce are prolly just pure shreadfests or insane tapping that u dont really hear on ur radios stations.They incoperate many many techniques in their songs that when u hear, it seems as thought it's impossible to be played by a human being...The songs however, doesnt seem to "flow" just right...

    Now,Melodious guitarist are the ones where i call "Has The Ability To Make Their Guitars Sing". Dont get me wrong here, that doesnt mean their songs are filled with power chords and can be easily played. Their music is just soulful and "flows" with the rhythm.

    Prolly Paul from beyond doesnt have much tech abilities or wad not.But if he can make his riffs or chord progression sound gd, he's a gd guitarist in my eyes
