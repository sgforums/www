    Dear Customer,

    We would like to alert you on a recent widespread security threat of
    W32.Mytob@mm and its variants that send e-mails to addresses that it gathers
    from Internet user's Windows Address Book on the computer.

    E-mails initiated by this type of virus always come with an attachment or a
    link.

    If you receive e-mails purportedly originating from Pacific Internet Limited
    stating any of the following listed e-mail addresses as senders, it is
    highly possible that these are virus-initiated emails originating from an
    infected machine.

    register@pacific.net.sg

    support@pacific.net.sg

    webmaster@pacific.net.sg

    Please do not open the attachment or link indicated on the body of the
    message of the email. Delete the email and scan your computer thoroughly
    with an updated anti-virus application to ensure it remains uncompromised.

    Should you have received any suspicious emails and wish to verify its
    authenticity, please contact our 24-hour Technical Support Hotline at
    63366622 (please choose option 2).

    It is highly recommended that you visit
    http://www.pacific.net.sg/article.php?id=417687 to understand more about the
    W32.Mytob@mm virus.
