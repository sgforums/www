    Mid year 2005 total population is 4.351million
    Mid year 2005 resident (S'poreans & PRs) population is 3.554million
    (source: http://www.singstat.gov.sg/keystats/hist/population.html)

    Therefore non-resident (FT, student pass, etc) population is about 797,000

    Also Resident (S'poreans & PRs) working person's montlhy income (incl AWS, bonus, commission, allowances, tips, etc but excl employer's CPF) distribution is as follows:

    S$
    0 - 499 3.7%
    500 - 999 9.5%
    1000 - 1499 13.4%
    1500 - 1999 13.0%
    2000 - 2999 21.0% - Median is about S$2,497
    3000 - 3999 13.5%
    4000 - 4999 7.9%
    5000 - 5999 5.2%
    6000 - 6999 3.2%
    7000 - 7999 2.1%
    8000 - 8999 1.7%
    9000 - 9999 1.0%
    10000 and above 4.9%

    Average resident monthly income based on extrapolation and assuming that those earning S$10,000 and above earns, on average of say S$12,000 is approx S$3,308 per month

    The $2,193 number in the above post includes non-resident working population.

    Therefore, on average, non-resident monthly income is lower than that of residents.

    In summary, the average working resident in S'pore earns about S$2,497/mth.
    Working residents in S'pore earns, on average, about S$3,308/mth. Note the different context.
