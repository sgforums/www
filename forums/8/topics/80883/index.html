    Originally posted by BonJovi:
    I am wondering how to format C drive with Windows XP. After formatting how to re-install it back?? urgent....thx in advance..
    Here's what you can do. Don't forget to thank me if you succeed.

    1) Back up all your data
    --> the usual stuffs by using CD burner or partioning your harddisk.

    2) Restart your computer
    --> when the screen loads, hit the DEL button to enter the bios
    --> the bios is like a menu with lotsa of configuration and has a blue background

    3) Change the boot sequence
    --> a boot sequence tells your computer which drive to load when it starts up
    --> In the menu, enter the bios (it should be the 3rd or 4th option) different motherboards differ
    --> the default boot sequence usually starts with your A Drive then your C harddisk drive.
    --> Change the A Drive to CDROM by using the plus and minus key of your keyboard ( located at the farthest right end of your keyboard above the enter button)

    4) Save it
    --> Self explainatory. Save settings and exit.
    --> Insert your windows XP disc in before you exit.

    5) Restart and Boot
    --> your computer will then restart and it will boot starting with your CDROM
    --> follow the instructions given onscreen

    BINGO !
