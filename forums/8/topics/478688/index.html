    About OneInbox


    OneInbox is a secured platform for you to receive Government letters electronically (e-letters), in place of hardcopy letters. Besides being an eco-friendly initiative which enables you to do away with hardcopy letters, OneInbox also offers you:

    Anywhere, Anytime Access
    You can access e-letters from the Government anytime and anywhere, even when you are overseas. All you need is your Singpass and an internet connection.

    Your Choice of e-Letters 
    You can choose to receive e-letters from all participating Government agencies, or only some e-letters. Simply log-in to select the e-letters you want to receive. For a start, you can opt to receive e-letters from the Ministry of Manpower (MOM), Central Provident Fund Board (CPFB), Housing & Development Board (HDB) and Inland Revenue Authority of Singapore (IRAS). More e-letters from these and other Government agencies will be added to OneInbox gradually. Keep a lookout for them!

    Forward, Download or Print Your e-Letters
    e-Letters from the Government will be retained in OneInbox for up to 18 months. You can forward the e-letters to your preferred personal email address, download or print a copy of them. 

    Email Alerts
    You can choose to receive email alerts when new e-letters are delivered to your OneInbox, or email reminders if you have not read your new e-letters after 7 days. You will never forget or miss your important Government letters again. 

    Secured Mailbox
    Unlike ordinary email accounts, OneInbox is free from spam and the e-letters stored in OneInbox are secure. Your confidential e-letters from the Government are safe with OneInbox.  

    You can access your OneInbox account by visiting www.oneinbox.gov.sg and logging in with your SingPass, or from your eCitizen (www.ecitizen.gov.sg) portal.
