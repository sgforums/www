    some ppl are not loners by choice. That is quite correct, ela.

    While one may argue that ppl can change, it is also true. But such a change is never easy, and demands a lot of will and mental endurance to. Due to social pressure, family, romance, peers, job, education...all these shape us into what we are today. To even change ourselves, is beyond us, frankly speaking. It is only the things that happen around us, to us, that continually shape us, and slowly we adjust to the changing human/social environment, we change. For ppl like that, perhaps the best we can do for them is to accept them for what they are, and not give them unnecessary pressure.

    loners themselves should also seek what's best for them, not at the expense of other ppl of course. for example, being rude, being rebellious, unreasonable etc. Accepting of self comes top priority, and not seek to change into another person. A loner has her own set of troubles, a very popular person has her own set of troubles.

    Live life as it is, because life doesn't turn out the way we want it to.
