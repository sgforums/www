    Before I proceed on with my essay, I wish to clarify and state clear certain things. First of all, this essay is meant for adults above the age of 21 with discerning taste and who appreciate the state of non-conformity. Secondly, I take no responsibility for whatever might happened to the readers after going through my essay. As I have mentioned in the first 2 lines, this is meant for ADULTS! I am sorry, I mean, THINKING ADULTS. Thirdly, I hope my piece of work is academic enough for I have no inclination to cater to the blood and gory side of cheap entertainment. I stress once again that I do not promote the usage of suicide and it is meant purely as a discussion topic. If you are easily offended by the topic, I suggest you click on the back button of your web browser and look at other delightful threads in the forum.

    Thank you. With this out of the way, I shall proceed on with my essay, that is, suicide and the ways to go about doing it. In this stressful society of capitalism ran amok, people are confronted with problems on a daily, even hourly basis. We stress about our work, study and life. We worried about our family, friends and loved ones. Crisis are occurring at an alarming rates and we often feel besieged and yearn of an easy way out of this miserable existence. Of course, suicide is outlawed and out-moral. The courts dissuade us from attempting it with fines and jail while the moral crusaders denounce suicide as a cowardly act that is sure to be condemned by your makers and the gods they promote. We are urged by society to meet our problems with stoic brazenness and courage in the face of adversity is highly publicised in the media. More often than not, it is usually love angst teenagers who resort to this measure of ending their problems once and for all. And that, my friends, is something I do not like or encourage at all!

    Suicide is for adults pure and simple. They are for individuals who have discovered that life has no more meaning for them, and that the problem facing them requires a much drastic solution and that all pros and cons have been measured. In short, suicide is a rational action of THINKING individuals. What I have here now with me is a list most potential suicide cases went through. Most people have different priorities in life and this list is not the definite list to suicide. However, I believed it fulfilled most individuals who are taking this route.

    1. Ask yourself this question first. Is it true that life has no more meaning for you? If you cannot convince yourself with your answer, I suggest you stop now.

    1. Is the problem really insurmountable? If not, I would also suggest that you stop thinking of suicide as an option.

    1. Now we come to the guilt part, the consequences of your actions. Can you really bear to see your loved ones agonised over your death? Is it fair to them that they have to deal with your problems after you are gone? If you think you can answer all 3 questions in the positive, congratulations my friend, we can now proceed to the crux of my essay.
    Euthanasia, the act of merciful killing, is a hotly debated issue in society today. I will not go into the nitty gritty details nor debate anyone on it. Suffice to say, what I have here is for people looking for a way out of their existence and the various ways to go about achieving it. I do not endorse this action nor do I condemn it. All I am doing it listing out in dry terms on how best to achieve it in a painless way.

    I listed the ways of killing oneself under 4 major options: the crash and burn method, the toxic and poison method, the silent razor method and lastly, death by drowning.

    THE CRASH AND BURN METHOD.
    The most popular method to date. This form of suicide encompasses jumping, car and pedestrian accidents and any method that involve brute force. Needless to say, this method of suicide brought about unnecessary blood and gore, suiting those with attention seeking tendencies. The few drawbacks of this are it hurts at the moment of impact. Of course, if you can overlook this short moment of uncomfortable feelings, it would be indeed a terrific way to go. Highly recommended for the non-squeamish individuals.

    THE TOXIC AND POISON METHOD.
    Very popular with the females individuals. One of the reasons could be the relatively painless way of death and leaving the body and face intact for public consumption. Conventional methods include the quintessential sleeping pills, the monoxide in the car and other lethal poisons like arsenic and rat poison. Of course, the drawback for this is that poisons are hard to come by in Singapore and they do not guarantee a painless way of dying. There have been cases of suicide cases devouring large amount of rat poison, who unfortunately enough was discovered in time, and even more unfortunately, had to suffered through the horrendous process of stomach pumping. Ditto for sleeping pills and carbon monoxide, not necessary a sure proof way of dying. Individuals are highly advised to plan with great foresight before attempting this version of suicide.

    THE SILENT RAZOR METHOD.
    Yes, the popular image of blood filled bathtub is bound to fill oneÂ’s mind when one thinks of razor. The silting of sharp metal across the veins had accounted for many a females life and it can be a rather painful way to goÂ…until you have less than a quartz of blood left. Strictly for the voyeurs out there, who loved to look at themselves as they bleed slowly to death. Death by hanging also belongs to this category. Instead of a cold metal at your veins, you have a thick rope caressing your neck as you suffocate to death. Relatively quiet way of going. But can be rather unpleasant, for suffocating is can induce a lot of pain and struggling as you gasp for air.

    DEATH BY DROWNING.
    Self explanatory. Very, very horrible way of dying, for water entering your lungs IS a very painful experience. Not recommend for the swimmers out there. Your will to live might just kick in during the crucial moment, unless of course you tied a large object to your below.

    There is undoubtedly many ways to skin a cat, as the saying goes. The sky is the limit and why limit to youself to those options shown above. After all, this will be your last chance at exploring your creative self. There will be people who are probably not too happy with my essay and think that it is in bad taste. I prefer to take a more sanguine view about this. I will not go into the right and wrong of suicide and pointing out the ways to kill oneself is but an interesting exercise in the grey matters.
