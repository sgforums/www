    Bringing childhood memeories, let me see what I did

    Armed with really short boyish hair, I went to conquer the boys kingdon, If he is He-man, I am She-ra, and Thunder Hawk is one of my favourite Mask Crusaders with his flying car in the air waiting to compete all his enemies.I watched cartoons like Saber Rider and also Inspector Gadget (which ironically turned into a movie which I have not even watched)Yah, hopscotch, one of my favourite past times playing with my male neighbour

    We played erasers when we are bored,all we have to do to keep ourselves entertained is two person engaging in having the eraser on top of each other, the one that is on top is the winner,,,and we are really so into it..that we even brought erasers of different countries to compete and I was conferred the title "Queen of Erasers".

    I also went to the fields to catch grasshoppers with the guys, green ones, multi-colored ones, big or small and we will frighten the girls the next day with the "prey" that we caught.

    Went to Van Kleef Aquarium and also Labrador park for excursions and also Camp Christine for our Pr.6 camp...I simply love school life back then..so carefree...

    I read lotsa books like fromm Bookworm club and was called the "Walking dictionary"..absolute nerd then...Those were the days where I will spend time in to Eco garden with the teacher in the Science club...catching water boatman, pond skater, as well as dragonflies with our newly bought nets..boy we really look like vicious predators to those insects in the pond community.

    I loved playing "Jia Jia Jiu" with my neighbours roleplaying that we are adults having a familly and I will be the mother and my brother and sis will be my kids and again my male neighbour being the father..( we are always constantly teased bu the sdults that we are an item and that makes him cry.. Dunno why he cried )...and we like to play the board game "Risk"..Rol the dice and win the continent!It really looks so easy without have to put up a war...

    Those stick on luminous "earrings" are so popular at that time, glas with no earholes will buy those to put on the earlobes, fore head. And also those small little beads of scent awed us gals so much....
