    its a progressive tax structure, meaning the more u earn, the more u pay. but even at the top tier, u only pay up to 20%.

    if u earn <$22k pa, u pay 0 tax.

    and dun forget u get to claim for reliefs, such as personal relief, CPF contribution rebates etc... so the chargeable income is likely to be much less than ur annual income.

    go to the IRAS website to check the rates: http://www.iras.gov.sg/ESVPortal/iit/iit-se-a1.1.15+tax+rates+for+resident+individuals.asp
