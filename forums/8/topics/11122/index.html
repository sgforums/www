    Dear Mishima,

    I dunno what I can offer to u. I'm basically an average guy out here. I'm very practical and down to earth. Most things I believe must have some grounds in it, if not, there must at least some intuition for my belief.

    Then how can someone who's characters that qualifies him to become an atheist to ironically believe in God? It came no suprise if u listen on.

    I was brought up in a Christian kindergarten.
    Even though I'm not a christian at that time, I did everything pretty much like a Christian does. I remembered praying a lot, singing a lot more and saying grace before every meal. I was told a lot of stories which I thought were merely fairy tales. Later things start to fall into places when i got hold of a bible during my sec sch times. Those stories
    are from the Bible! 'duh' u may say... but how was i to know as a 6 yr old kid then!? The more I read the Bible, the more I recollect those stories I heard during kindergarten time.

    As I grow older, I felt a tug in my heart, that told me I shall go to church. I dun have anyone whom I felt comfortable going church with. At that time, I thought this person would probably be my girlfriend. Then I imagined how perfect it would be to be have the company of my dear one down to church.

    I meet this sweetest girl in my life. I took a liking for her and soon realised that this feeling is mutual. We hit it off very well, and I felt I've known her for a long time. The times we spend together are most memorable.

    Although her parents are non-christian, she attended a church. Every time she does that, she has to secretly do it, for her father, esp, has violent objection to that.

    She never mentioned about going down to church with her. She's least bothered by it. Then I thought, I've found the woman who I truly love, and she's willing to accepts the person I am.

    I made known to her that I wish to go down church with her. Not for her, but because I truly wants to. Because she can't attend church whenever her father is around, she has to wait for the time when he's not. So she has not much chance to go to church.

    I remembered clearly, once, she makes a call and invite me to go down church with her a few hours before the service.Just enuf time for me to prepare my trip down. Probably her father make some last minute decision to go out. I admire her faith, that's unwavered even when facing such objections.

    There were times when things were bad between us. But the very next day, I wasn't feeling hurt anymore. We started talking again, and she told me she has prayed for me, and ask God take away the hurt feelings and forgive her.(dun ask what it is)For one thing, I am touched by her, but more importantly,I could really felt God working tangibly between us.

    I think it's easier to feel God when u are in love. God is getting real.

    I dunno how to write the second part, for it's an abrupt change, almost hard to believe.

    I spend my quiet time with God. I ask whether this girl is the one in my life. Is she going to be my wife? No answer.

    But somehow, almost illogically, the intuition came to me. She's leaving me. I couldn't believe and did not want to believe.
    The feeling is stronger that I could not bear anymore. One day we were on the phone, and I asked her, "do u have anything that u wished to tell me but do not dare to". She told me what's on her mind.

    Well, my intuition was right. She is leaving me. I guessed most people would have felt heart-broken when someone you love so dearly want to end a relationship. I couldn't believe myself, but I stayed so calm. We ended our conversation by praying for each other on the phone.

    For many weeks I din felt any heartache over the loss of this relationship. I spend my time with God, this time asking why am I not feeling hurt by it. Progressively, I start to feel the pain in my heart. It was so bizarre, that when u break up with a girl, the pain comes a month later which is still strong enuf to make me cry. I thought, other than God who else is helping me to pull through all these?

    I asked God whether she's the one for me. The answer is no. And God provide the comforting hands to let me accept the consequence of his will.

    Although we ended our r/s, we are still good friends. I still love her, though it's not the kind of guy-girl love. It's the kind of love that a brother would have for a sister.

    Mishima, I can talk a lot a lot about God, but I really can't finish the things that God has done. To me God is always loving. As to which religion God is in, it really doesn't matter. It is between you and God.

    Slow down ur pace, calm yourself down take in a deep breath and u will learn to recognise what God has in stall for u.

    Seek and you will find.
