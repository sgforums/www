    How remarkable dat life is so mundane. But we all relish it. Yes, we complain sometimes, but we are not really complaining... life is good. life is.

    We live different lives, but essentially the same life... some richer, some poorer, some quicker, some slower, but all cycles...

    No one escapes the cycle of life. No one can say they are not born, bred, and dead. Eventually. Not many can refuse to grow up, not many can refuse to fall in love, not many can refuse to procreate, not many can refuse to raise their children, not many can refuse to work, not many can refuse to grow old... and maybe fat, and none can refuse to die.

    We live our own lives, yet my life and ur life is not really dat much different. We experience the same things over and over again, through the ages and history of mankind. The form of it may change, but essentially the same.

    How wonderful -and repetitive- life is. Wonder if anyone here is tired of it?
