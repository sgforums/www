    8 replies to this thread, and we still dunno which floor you live in.

    Alright, most hdb blocks, regardless old/new,  the first 3 floors from the ground usually get their water supply from the underground mains, not the roof tank, hence water pressure should never be an issue.

    Almost, all old/new flats have their roof water tanks upgraded with a booster pump, there is almost no chance of the booster pump breaking down, because of redundancy (got 2, not 1).

    You gotta check if your water pressure is constant throughout the day, because some boosters work on a peak timer, and not on-demand.

    Do a simple test with your main valve, find out how much flow you have in its fully shut, and fully open position.
