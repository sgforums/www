    Siliva Meet - 30th May 2003











    Review - The first ever Singapore Car modeling meetup was held on the 30th May 2003, the AF SG Silvia Meet. It was attended by 11 local modelers with 15 different types of Silvias with different themes. In my opinion, it was considered a successful first meetup.

    Evo Meet - 31st August 2003











    Special Build - Cwest Evo by 2 local modelers





    Review - The second car modeling meetup was held on the 31st August 2003, the AF SG Evo Meet. It was attended by 17 local modelers with a extremely wide variety of Evos. So far till now, this meetup still holds the record of the most modelers attended and the most number of car models in a single meetup, a total of 33 Evos! In my opinion, it was the best local car modeling meetup ever organised. It still brings back memories after seeing these pictures again.

    SR Garage Rotary Meet - 30th November 2003








    Special Theme Cars


    Review - The 3rd and last car modeling meetup of 2003 was held on the 31st August, the SR Garage Rotary Meet. It was attended by 14 local modelers with 23 different RX7s and RX8s. In my opinion, the overall standard of the cars' workmanship had improved greatly with the addition of a very special theme which incorporated bike designs to the cars.
