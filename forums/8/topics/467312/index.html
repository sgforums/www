    Hi everyone! Remember the first time you went to the Science Centre? Can you recall the fascination and wonder you had when you first walked through the exhibits?

    In conjunction with its 35th anniversary, the Science Centre Singapore is currently running a photo competition inviting visitors to submit their past photos taken there. The photos can be taken last week, last year, or last decade. It doesn't matter as long as it sparked something in you when you were there. If you have such a photo, you can share the spark by submitting the photo through their Facebook Page    , or by uploading it via Twitter or Instagram with the hashtag #ShareTheSpark. There are quite a number of prizes to be won like membership passes and tickets to visit the Science Centre or Omni-theatre. 

    Here is the contest page     if you are interested in participating, or feel free to reply to this thread if you have any questions :)
