    No lah....G2000 is a Hong Kong-based low-end fashion franchise that managed to expand in Singapore. Their stuff are standardized from templates and cannot be compared with the mid-range shops like Zara/Topman or the higher end ones like those u find in Paragon.

    Caserini and Domanchi are located at 2nd floor of Centrepoint shopping Centre.

    Domanchi shirts, size 1 and 2 can be rather tight fitting. Price range fr $49 onwards. Their fitting range is by number 1-4, rather than by roman numerals like S-XL or neck measurements like 14.5 - 17.

    Caserini shirts are mainly European imports. Prices of 2 for $69 are reasonable, but u won't be able to find a slim fit shirt there cos they are mainly US/Euro-sizes.
