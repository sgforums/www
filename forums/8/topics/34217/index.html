There are a lot of greedy MATHER FARKING BESTARD vets who live a fat, rich lifestyle in their bungalows driving big cars because of all the sick animals in Singapore!     Remember, vets specialise in vetrinary (animal) medicine. They are NOT nutritionists or animal food experts, no matter what they claim!!! Of course, we expect them to know something, and they generally do. But have you noticed that most vets only carry 1 or 2 brands of food? And that's the only thing they will recommend because they earn commission/profit from it!

    The question is: DO U REALLY LOVE YOUR PET/COMPANION? If you say U do, avoid feeding your dog or cat ANYTHING that the animal can't find in the WILD (no people, no human food sources, rubbish, left-overs etc around lah).

It may take awhile but your dog may have a chance to lead a happy, healthy and relatively PAIN/DISEASE FREE life; and both of you will be happier. It costs less also in the long run - lesser vet bills, lesser fallen tears and suffering in your heart.
