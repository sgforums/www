  • Illegal Operation
    22 Jul 00, 11:47
    That's because long time ago, during the early era of the typewriter, the keys were not as active as they were now.

    In fact, if you arrange them alphabatically, people tend to know where all the keys are fast enough. They typed so fast that before one key comes up, another goes down. The mechanism tends to get stuck when this happen. So, to solve this problem, they arranged the keys on the typewriter in the most unconvinent way.

    Eventually, when the computer got popularized, some tried to rearrange the keys to make typing common words easier, but many were used to the QWERTY system. The more convinent system was seldom used as a result.

    So there you have it.
