    Oh, christmas themes, is that what you mean?

    Firstly, you could think in terms of places...
    For example, Under the Sea: have gifts that are related to animals or colours found there, like dolphin earings or necklaces (which i just bought from mini bits at the heeren )
    or Forest : green colour related stuff, or deers (could be soft toys), birds, etc.

    Another type of theme you could try is a People theme,
    like maybe Gypsy: scarves, those big hoop earrings, scented candles..
    or even African: wooden carvings of animals maybe?
    or Greek: aromatheraphy, books, etc.

    Yet another theme could be Adjective related.
    Like Bright: get things in VERY bright colours, like maybe a red bandana...yellow calendar? or nice colourful pens. or even a lamp! =P
    or even Sexy like panties, stockings for the girls, and ... i wont know about guys

    Or another one maybe Item related...
    Like Thread: Anything made or uses thread, like shirt, skirt, or even wind chimes which use it.
    Maybe Water: Bottles of bubble bath, soap, shampoo, scented oils.. or yes, once again, related to the colour blue. or scarves which are "flowy" like water...

    lol...i'm a girl...so the examples i gave are... -girlish- . Hmm... i just thought of another idea... maybe a "His and Hers" theme? like getting things which are VERY noticably guyish for guys, and girlish for girls, like shavers and earrings respectively? =P

    Just think in broad general terms of Items, Adjectives, Colours, Places, Time (in history), Plants, Animals, Cultures first, and then zero in one a more specific one.
