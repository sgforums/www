    I am so upset

    I don't really understand angmoh's english. I didn't pay attention to it. When I watched TVs, movies, cartoons when I was young, I would somehow automatically 'shut off' my ears and just relied on the subtitles.

    And this made my english listening skill very poor. I don't have problem reading or speaking english, but when come to english listening from angmoh. I am completely helpless.

    Luckily singapore english is not a problem to me, because it is kind of blend flavor of english and local accent.

    The real problem came one day is I worked in a company where a lots of interaction with angmoh. Then I became dumb. I was so scared to talk to them. Because I was afraid that I talked the different topic from what they were saying.

    I felt so inferior. I trained myself very hard, start to 'opening up' my ears when watching TVs, movies, radio. I am becoming better. Now, 80-90% of the work related conversation are good to listen. But when angmoh talk about their life stuff, I become dumb again. Those words are so strange to me. I couldn't really understand.

    Do you have the same or similar problem?? I am so upset now.
