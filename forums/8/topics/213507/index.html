  • viciouskitty74
    7 Oct 06, 01:06
    For those on a budget.

    Army Market on the 2nd floor of Beach Road Market.

    Great for finding army backpaks. But remember to buy a pair of those strap wraps. Ask the aunties and uncles there and they will be able to understand.

    Those strap wraps are basically cushions so that the backpack straps wont charff your shoulder skins.

    The water bottles with filters are harder to find, try those specialised backpacking sports shop. You will find yourself being able to lug around less, especially on foot & bicycle adventure trips. Trust me, they are costly, but a true life saver when you can confirm whether the water is safe to drink. Put the water in the water bottle, squirt them out onto a tin bowl (can buy at army market) and cook the river water before drinking.

    And bring along lighters & matchsticks kept in ziplock bag. Trust me. you will need it.
