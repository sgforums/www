    Hiya daniuge,

    Fondue's like a soup made from melted cheese and chocolate. The original dish is the cheese fondue and comes from Switzerland. You usually eat it with bread by dipping it inside the melted cheese. Some receipes have included meat and seafood. The chocolate fondue, on the other hand, is more of a dessert where you dip fruits like apples, kiwi fruit, strawberries and bananas.

    There're a couple restaurants here that serve fondue. I remember one that shared the same strip as Mad Monks, but I think they've closed down or something. Anyone care to clarify? another place I know is at Mohd Sultan, a restaurant called Pacific Breeze that serves fusion food by the river. They've got the chocolate fondue. Another place that serves cheese fondue is The Tavern that can be found in Siglap and the mouth of Mohd Sultan. Cheese fondue is quite pricey by the way...

    Bring it on!!
