    crossing 2 lines at the top left hand corner means to be bank into a/c ... cannot be encash over the counter..

    if bearer is crossed n also crossed at top left hand corner... means must be bank into the stated person a/c.

    if a/c payee only non-negotiable is stamped then must be banked into the stated payee a/c even if the bearer is not crossed.

    if top left hand corner is crossed but wan to bank into another a/c (that means a/c name n payee is different) then must c if the bank wants to accept anot.
