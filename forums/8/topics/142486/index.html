    From Channelnewsasia.com

    SINGAPORE : Just 24 hours after NKF's Board and CEO TT Durai stepped down, 55-year-old Gerard Ee has been named interim chairman and acting CEO of the National Kidney Foundation.

    Mr Ee is no stranger to charity. He is even known as Singapore's own 'Mr Charity', serving as President of the National Council of Social Service since 2002.

    Mr Ee, a former Nominated Member of Parliament, also sits on the Council on Governance of Institutions of Public Character.

    Mr Ee heads the Automobile Association of Singapore.

    He recently retired from accounting firm Ernst & Young. - CNA/de
