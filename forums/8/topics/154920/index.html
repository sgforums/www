    JC need L1R5, which is your English and relevant 5 subjects. You only need L1R4 for MI. However, when your L1R4 adds up, it must be less than or equals to 20 points. Minus away your CCA points. For JC, L1R5 points must also be 20 points or less.

    Example:
    English : B4
    Chinese : A2
    E Maths: A2
    Combined Humnities: B3
    Chemistry: B3
    Sub Total: 14 points
    CCA points : 10 which is grade B4

    For CCA grade A1 to A2, you can minus away 2 points from your L1R4. For CCA grade B3 to C6, you can minus away 1 point from L1R4.

    Therefore, total is 13 points.
