    Originally posted by Catknight:

    Wonder if you're allowed to build a wiccan temple here in SG..is it allowed?

    You will have to get a permit first, though whether it will be granted or not is up to the government. Wiccan is not out-lawed in Singapore, so generally speaking, it is possible for you to build a temple here.

    But your biggest concern will be the financial cost. Even in the US, there are not many wiccan temples there nowadays, as you will need sponsors or donors to maintain and upkeep it. In Singapore, it is even worse, given that there are barely a handful of followers here. You will not even be able to raise enough funds to buy down a piece of land for the temple.
