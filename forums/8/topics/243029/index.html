    I am writing this to share my experience to those who have the same problem as me. Since secondary school, I have been having blocked ear, and it got worse whenever I catch a cold. It affects both my speaking & singing especially when I can hear my own voice from within. My hearing is not that sensitive since then. I hate the distracting voice from my inner ear especially when speaking to people or humming a song I like.

    Try this out to see if you find this distracting: Close your mouth and hum any song that you can think of, do you hear your own voice from within your inner ear ? If you does then you are probably experiencing what I had once experienced.

    Doctor diagnosed that I might have sinus and that what I am going through is one of the symtom of sinus. I took the cold medicine and use the nasal spray prescribed to me but the medicine only stop my running nose but my ears are still blocked !

    I was getting desperate for help. I seek internet to see if anyone recovers from it but none actually does except maybe through surgery. I have even tried ear-candling, although it only clears my blocked ear but it is only temporarily as it came back less than an hours later. Turning to last resort, I heed my mum's advice by refraining from taking cold drink for a week and only consume hot/warm stuff. And guess what ? After just about 1 week plus, and my blocked ears symtom is gone and I am no longer hearing my own voice from within. My hearing is back to normal.

    If you are experiencing what I had experienced, try this out for 2 weeks to see if it works for you. It works for me and I certainly hope it works for you too.
