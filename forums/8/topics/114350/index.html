    Airline.....

    I was taking a domestic flight from one state to another.
    Supposed to reach there before noon so that I could have lunch in the 2nd destination. Woke up very early for the morning flight (at 4 am). Arrived in airport at about 6.30 am.

    The flight duration was 2 hours only. As I woke up so damn early and slept so damn late (about 12 midnight) the night before, I was really sleepy and dozed off the moment I got on the plane, even before take off.

    After the plane took off, I sort of heard some annoucements by the pilot. It sounded like bad news, cuz the pilot said something like... "Today is not our day". I thought it's some usual annoucement, probably some bad news on the weather or delay in arrival time. So I pushed now the back rest and continued with my nap.

    Just then, the passenger next to me told me to lift my backrest up as we were landing soon. He said that we were going back to the original destination, where we boarded our plane. I said "Har? What?" He said the pilot said there was something wrong with the aircraft -- Mechanical Fault!!!

    I was like... OMG! Didn't they detect the problem before takeoff??? Then a couple of Singapore travellers said "No wonder SQ is known as the best. It is known to be using new aircraft only. After a few years, they will sell away to the not-so-good airline (which I think they are referring to the airline we were taking)".

    When we were at the airport once again, there was nothing to do except waiting. I practically spent 1 whole day at the airport . I walked all the shops there and spent the whole time walking from one end of the airport to the other except during lunch.

    After lunch, the airport announced that they were not able to repair the aircraft. This means that the damage was really bad and my question of "Why didn't they detect this earlier?" popped up again.
