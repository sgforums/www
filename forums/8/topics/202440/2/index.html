    Originally posted by markzz:
    If u noticed, the dressing of teenage heartlanders around singapore differs to the various towns they live in!
    (note: this is applicable only when the residents are in their home town )

    Here's some examples:

    Tampinirians (Residents of Tampines ):

    1)sporty T-shirt
    2)Surfer shorts
    3)Sporty slippers (eg:Billabong )

    Yishunites ( Residents of Yishun ):

    1)Bengish (or Lianish ) T-shirts , sometimes with Dragon prints
    2)Soccer shorts for boys, hot shorts for gals
    3)footwear varies (Skate shoes, cool slippers, Platforms, etc.. )

    ---------

    how abt those staying in Bedok, Geyland, Aljunied?.

    Hougangers (Residents of Hougang ) and Toa Payoreans (Residents of Toa Payoh ) :

    1)"No Fear" or "OP" T-shirt
    2)bringly coloured shorts
    3)One colour Rubber slippers(used to be a trend to wear different coloured slippers on each foot )

    Jurongees (Residents of Jurong ):

    1)Collared short sleeved beng shirt , polo tee or Soccer Jerseys
    2)Demin shorts or cargo 3/4 pants
    3)Footwear varies

    Kallangan (Residents of Kallang ):

    1)Giodano or Bossini T-shirts
    2)Sport shorts or elastic shorts (worn mostly as sleep wear for most people but Kallangans sometimes wear them when outdoors in Kallang )
    3)common Slippers

    So, I would like to announce...

    after my observations in the various towns....

    The Jurongees are the most fasionably dressed of the lot!

    P.s: To tell if a guy is not from your town, he is usually spotted in your town wearing:

    1)Nice T-shirts
    2)Long Jeans
    3)Covered shoes
    4) will be carrying a bag!
