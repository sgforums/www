    there is almost no way you can prepare yourself mentally for army except to just stay open and learn to have a positive attitude cos its very easy and almost 95% confirm to have negative attitude inside one.

    you can also accumulate injuries now so you can get a combat unfit status.

    physically all that running and pullups are only for IPPT-wise fitness. (we call it admin fitness) but if you're in combat there's a whole new type of fitness called combat fitness

    its about carry weight (rifle like 4 to 5 kg, fieldpack like 20kg) its carry load in a very uncomfortable manner thats hard to train... don't worry actually i think the physical part isn't something to worry about cos it will always be within your limits (though at times at the extreme limits)

    the really hard things to prepare for are depression like issues. a lot of ppl lose their girlfriends inside.. see if u can handle or not lah...
    like i say... stay positive.
