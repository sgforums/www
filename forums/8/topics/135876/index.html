    1) How much is the Air Fare, Ex SIN - orlando per adults?
    The typical round-trip air fare for SIN-ORL-SIN is between S$2,000 to S$3,000 per adult, depending on the time of travel. Check out zuji.com for indicative fares.

    2) How do we go to Florida Disney Land from Orlando Airport? Is there a free transfer by the Walts Disney from the Airport to Disney or we have to take cab by ourselves.
    There is a free Disney's Magical Express service for guests of Disney resorts. Other visitors could rent a car or take a shuttle bus service. Taxi fares are too expensive to be considered a viable option.

    3) I plan to go there during the 1st week of Jan 06, is it good if i go during that period.
    It is not a bad time as most American kids would have gone back to school after Christmas, but the operating hours for the parks, parades and special events are shorter, as compared to summer hours.

    4) Is it safe if we book the hotel rooms through Intenet by credit card? Will there be a possibilitlty that their system does not have our particular when we reach there
    It is generally quite safe and reliable to book hotel rooms in U.S. through Internet. To stay on the safe side, book through a reputable online agency or with a reputable hotel.

    5) Please advise how much do you all spend for the air tickets and accomdation when any of you all go last time?
    It may be quite expensive if you travel by yourselves. Your total damages could go up to S$4,000 or more per person (the air fare could already take up more than half of that). Try to book through a travel agency which provides a "free and easy" itinerary at Walt Disney World. There are other theme parks such as Universal Studios and Seaworld in Orlando, too, so you might wish to visit them as well while you are there.

    Have fun.
