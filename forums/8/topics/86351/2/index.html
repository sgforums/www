    There are two kinds - gas or electric.

    For the gas dryer you will need to have piped gas supply. So if your home is not fitted with this source you will have to go for the electric ones.

    The electric ones are usually cheaper in price and there are many choices in the market. There is only one supplier for gas dryers - Singapore Power/Gas.

    I did some calculations back then for my mom - the cost of usage for both the gas and electric types are quite similar. But it seems the gas one becomes more efficient after say 5 years down the road. Gas supply is currently cheaper than electricity supply.

    We have found that the convenience of a dryer has out-weighted all other considerations. Especially for me - use to have to help my mom do the washing and then bring out the bamboo pole and then hang the clothes and than put the cloth peg on the clothes and than bring out the pole...And than when it rains rush to the window to take the clothes back in again. Now - we even wash and dry our clothes at midnight.
