"Battle Angel" preview

  • last boy scout
    9 Feb 06, 23:00
    Battle Angel (2007)

    Directed by
    James Cameron (Aliens, Terminator, Titanic)

    Writing credits
    Yukito Kishiro (graphic novels) and
    Laeta Kalogridis (screenplay)

    Set in the 26th century, the story takes place 300 years after a societal collapse caused by a major war. In that society, it's a technological dark age following a pinnacle of achievement far beyond where we are right now. Cyborg technology is a way of life. People are augmented a lot as workers, so being a cyborg is not unusual. The main character is a cyborg. She has an organic human brain, and she looks like she's about fourteen years old. She has a completely artificial body and she's lost her memory. She is found in a wreckage and reconstituted by a cyber-surgeon who becomes her surrogate father.

    The main character, even though it's a live action film, will be done with CG animation and will be shot in 3D using the stereo imagining system that James Cameron had been developing for his documentaries.

    Will include roughly 1,500 visual effects shots.

    James Cameron confirmed in an interview that this will be a combination of the first three books in Yukito Kishiro's series of graphic novels. In another interview, Cameron also said that should this film be successful, he hopes to make another two "Battle Angel" films.

    By the intended release date of Summer 2007, Cameron hopes that around a thousand theaters across North America will have been equipped with special 3D digital projectors that will be able to screen the movie.

    Based on the Japanese manga, "Gunnm". The comic was released in North America under the title "Battle Angel Alita".
