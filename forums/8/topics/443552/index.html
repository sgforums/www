    HOW & WHERE TO TOP UP YOUR FEVO

    Topping up your FEVO Prepaid MasterCard® purse:

    Online
    To top up your FEVO Prepaid MasterCard® purse online, visit www.fevocard.com and log in with your FEVO card number, FEVO Password and CVC to access your FEVO account. Click on the "Top Up FEVO Balance" link under Account Details and follow the instructions to top up your card. The minimum top up value is $20. The maximum value that you can have on your FEVO at anytime is $1000. A fee will be charged for each top up. Please see the Charge table for current fees. 

    AXS stations
    You can now top up your FEVO Prepaid MasterCard® purse at AXS stations island-wide! Click here for a step-by-step guide on how to top up at AXS stations. 

    Topping up your FEVO ez-link purse:

    To top up your FEVO ez-link purse, use the top up machines at MRT stations or top up through other ez-link top up channels such as DBS/POSB ATMs, AXS stations, at 7-11 convenience stores and more. If you choose to top up your ez-link purse with a debit or credit card, you can do so online at www.ezlink.com.sg.

     

     

    ENJOY GREATER CONVENIENCE WITH SINGPOST TOP-UP POINTS!

    More FEVO top-up points are available for our loyal customers! You can now top-up your FEVO Prepaid MasterCard© using cash or ATM card at all SingPost outlets. Please note that an administrative fee will be charged for each top-up.
