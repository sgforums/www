    Here are the steps for you to enable ur Zen Micro to display Chinese Char but before it, it's recommended for you to upgrade you Zen Micro firmware to version 1.02.05

    1. Go to your Control Panel
    2. Select Regional and Language Options
    3. Go to the Advanced tab
    4. See the Language for Non-Unicode programs
    5. Select Chinese PRC
    6. The system will prompt you to restart.

    Just do it and you can transfer your favourite chinese songs to your Zen Micro. Dun worry about the Chinese PRC settings. Your computer will still display in English language. That's for non-unicode programs.
