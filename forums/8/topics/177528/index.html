    here is a provisional copy of the n91 phone manual (pdf, 2.1mb). read it to understand more of its features.

    interesting points:
    1. it's amazing they are sticking with BL-5C used in 7650 and n70, and not the higher-capacity BL-6C originally designed for QD.
    2. you cannot remove the back cover of the phone while the n91 is on, it will stop the microdrive.
    3. only 802.11g Wi-Fi is supported. WEP (up to 128-bit), WPA and 802.1x authentication is also supported, thus we can use them on certain local uni networks.
    4. music player supports AAC, AAC+, eAAC+, MP3, WMA, WAV, 3G2, 3GP, 3GPP, 3GPP2, MPEG, MP4, M4A, DCF, ODF, ASF and M3U. equaliser is present.
    5. macromedia flash player, svg player and realplayer is pre-installed.
