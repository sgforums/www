    the other day i go my amah house during CNY n then she cook fried rice, very rice wor! so i ask her how she cook one n she tell me, n she also said dont tell anyone coz its her secret recipe.

    my amah will cook this special CNY fried rice once every year during CNY.

    ingredients r
    dried scallops
    dried mushrooms
    1 can abalone
    wax sausages
    bak kwa
    shrimps or diced prawns
    egg
    spring onions
    iceberg lettuce (not chinese lettuce)
    garlic
    pepper
    soy sauce, dark sauce, oyster/abalone sauce, sweet sauce, msg

    ok first of all u have to soak the dried scallops n mushrooms overnight or half a day so it will soften up. after that DO NOT throw the water away, coz u need it to cook rice.

    ok after the scallops n mushrooms had enough of soaking, take the water n pour it into the rice cooker. then open a can of abalone n pour some of the salty water into the rice cooker as well. Add in rice n cook rice as per normal.

    After the rice is cook u will have to take the rice out n put on a big bowl or plate n leave it in open air for it to dry up a bit, about 2 hours. So that when u cook the fried rice it wont be soggy.

    ok now u prepare the ingredients. u break up the dried scallops into pieces n dice the mushrooms. cut wax sausages diagonally. dice the bak kwa n abalone too.

    after everything including the rice is ready...heat up little bit of oil in a wok, throw in minced garlic, n then add in the wax sausages n bak kwa. u dont put so much cooking oil in the beginning coz the wax sausages gonna leak out fats. then add in the shrimps, mushrooms, n dried scallops. then add the rice. then u dig a hole in the pile of rice n pour in the egg, n cook the egg in the centre.

    after the egg is semi cooked, u can continue stirring. now add in soy sauce, dark sauce, oyster or abalone sauce, a bit of sweet sauce n pepper.

    then add in the spring onions n finally, after u off the fire, add in diced abalone. the reason u add diced abalone last is because if u add too early, the heat will overcook the abalone n make the texture rubbery.

    finally, after u serve the fried rice on a plate, garnish with shredded lettuce.
