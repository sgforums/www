PAP government has proven ability in tough times: Lim Hng Ki

  • Cindyfeh
    22 Apr 06, 00:55
    http://www.channelnewsasia.com/stories/singaporelocalnews/view/204260/1/.html

    SINGAPORE : Trade and Industry Minister Lim Hng Kiang says the PAP government has proven its ability to steer the country through difficult times, like the economic downturn in 2001 and SARS.

    Said Mr Lim, "We have shown that we have been able to get through these rough patches, whether it is 2001, whether it is SARS in 2003. I think there is a growing confidence not just among the business community, but among the population."

    The minister, who leads the West-Coast GRC, says his team is geared-up for a fight.

    Said Mr Lim, "For West Coast GRC, all my team members have been proven. In 2001, I had three new candidates, so I carried the burden of three new candidates. This time round, my whole team is a proven team. We go in much stronger than in 2001. This time round, I am reinforced by Ayer Rajah, and Ayer Rajah is a very strong single constituency added to my GRC. So I can't ask for a better combination of factors."

    Mr Lim, who was at a ground-breaking event for a S$450 million nutritional powder plant in Tuas, also gave his assessment of Singapore's economy.

    He said, "Right now, the numbers are working out as we projected. We expected a much stronger first half than the second half. But at the same time, we don't expect the second half to be much slower because the local economy is doing well." - CNA /ct
