    Hi, Phuturistic,

    Well, I think it is a normal thing for a guy to talk about his ex-gf, whether to his friends or to you. If he can freely talk about her to a gal, it means that he is in the process of recovery, in the sense that he is leaving it behind.

    Sometimes, the gal would take it that the guy is still living in the past upon the mention of the ex-gf. Perhaps, the guy would like to tell her that he would want to repeat the same mistake. The best thing to do is to talk to him about it and tell him how u feel. Don't leave it until someday, you break down and decided to jump to extremes for a breakup. It is sad if things would ever reach this state.

    Sometimes, the guy has not gotten over yet. It is up to you to help him to tide thru it, to re-assure and help him to get over the period of pain.

    Remember, u are now with him, and his ex is no longer with him. Why be jealous with a person who is no longer with him? It is analogous to being jealous to a dead person...

    Hope you will sort it out.
