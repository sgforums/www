    magazine (print media) is pretty restricted sensual experience form of media...u cant do roadshow, cant be interactive, and have oni texts and pictures to convey messages...

    i can only list u some of the contest concepts i came across..u can review them and take ur pick

    -- count number of xxx(the sponsored product) in the picture...

    -- spot the difference
    (teens love photo-hunt rite?)

    (get THEIR creative juices flowing)
