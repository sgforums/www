  • nonameguy
    1 Jun 07, 16:19
    In any case it is not easy to get D7 for GP. I am from NYJC and I know most people there keep failing or borderline passing their GP until the A levels, and when they take their A levels they do a lot better. None of the people I know of have failed their GP. As long as you are not a foreigner your English should be decent enough to pass. Just predict what questions will be asked (it's never hard to do that) and memorise as many points as you can for those questions. Then during the exam dump all the points into your paper. That should be enough to get you at least a C5. In any case you will want to get a b4 for english, cause the english module in uni (at least in NUS) can be very time consuming.

    Since you are still in J1 I highly recommend you to improve your english while you can. Read more story books. Read newspaper. Even reading New Paper can help your english.
