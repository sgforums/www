    Cointreau Aura... is it the same as the regular Cointreau? The picture sure looks like it.

    Regular Cointreau is a hard liquor, like your normal vodkas, whiskey etc. The difference is that it is an orange liquor (derived from oranges).. making it naturally citrus and sweet.

    Comes in a squarish bottle, which gives it a classy look and is different from the regular liquor bottles... and it goes well with mixers like tonic water, sprite or 7up. One of the main ingredients for mixing B52.

    If you need more info on Cointreau, try searching this website.

    www.cointreau.com under the international tab.
