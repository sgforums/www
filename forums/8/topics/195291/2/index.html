    some things my tracks instructor taught me last tiem.

    this apply to lane no. 8 and minus 15 m for every inner lane hencefort.

    1 round = 460m

    10 rounds = 4.6 click

    15 rounds = 6.9 click

    20 rounds = 9.2 click

    1st 3 to 5 rounds slow jog steady pace = warm up effect

    gather momoment breath steady. faster pace. foot spring effect.

    when tired back to slow jog steady pace

    rounds count is better then time tracking. time tracking tires easily. round counts gives a more substaintal goal to achieve.

    think something inspiraational when running. or use an mp3, if your mind is not strong enough. or, try to enjoy the run. if you can.

    Do not stop. will tires easily. treasure the momentem gained.

    Do 15 rounds for 4 consecutive days. do 10 rounds on the fifth day. or 15. if you are up to it.

    rest for 2 consecutive days. and do it again on the 3rd day.

    when 15 clicks are no longer a kick, when the day comes. try 20 clicks
