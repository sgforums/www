    I believe in fate but I also believe that we can change our fate if we truly want to.

    Heaven has put us on this Earth under different set of circumstances...some desirable some not-so-desirable. The Almighty has already set a path for us or perhaps even drawn a roadmap of our lives for us to follow......but in the end, He has allowed us all free-will to change our destinies if we try hard enuff.

    I believe that a person who resigns himself to his fate will certainly fall under its sway....but for a person who chooses to fight his fate, he has 50% chance of either failing or succeeding in changing his destiny. If you dun like the circumstances that fate has handed you, why not fight and try to to win? You have 50% chance of succeeding...why not take it?

    I don't believe in karma...why should I shoulder the blame of a previous existence that I know nothing about.......
    I believe I have only one life in this existence and it's my responsibility to do my best with it, even though I'm but an imperfect creature.
