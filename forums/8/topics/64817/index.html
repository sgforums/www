    okay.. got that off my chest...

    men and women are fundamentally different... both sides have to learn that... live with and accept the differences and support each other in times of need..

    this is pretty typical:

    a man figures out his problems during his "fire-gazing" time... where he stares blankly at the fire (in the past) or the tv these days... then he does something about it...

    a woman shares her problems, talks about it, complains and all that with people around her, then they offer empathy and support.. and she will come to a conclusion and do the necessary...

    if this is so, then the man must offer his support when his lady is in need, and the woman must give her guy his space when he needs it...

    both sides will suffer if the woman nags at him and complains that he is shutting her out.. he isn't... he's just trying to deal with problems his way.. it would be good to give him his time and space.. then be there when he comes to her...

    and again, both will suffer if the man is irritated and lashes out when his lady keeps talking to him about her problems.. she is just trying to deal with them her way with him... he should feel honoured and offer a ear to bend, a shoulder to cry on and strong arms to hold her.. she needs support at that time.. and who better than her man to give it..

    understanding the ways of your partner is paramount in a relationship..
