    Originally posted by dark_snowboy:
    i mean the pictures from phone to card, is it possible within the phone itself?
    Yes it is possible. From Gallery --> Options --> Move/copy to folder and you can select memory card as your destination folder.

    If you intend to do more complicated stuff like moving multiple files and tired of Gallery's loading time, you can download a more flexible application called FExplorer

    Just for your info...
    .jar files are usually Series 40 games (6610, 3200, 6230 etc). .sis files can only be opened by Symbian phones (6670, 3230 7610 etc). Difference is the .jar will be smaller screen size and i suppose simpler games. Installing .jar files on Symbian phones will show on top left of the screen filling about half the screen size only.
