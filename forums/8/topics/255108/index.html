    Direct poly admission exercise starts July
    STUDENTS with special talents and achievements, seeking direct admission into Singapore's junior colleges (JCs) and polytechnics for the next academic year, may start applying from June and July respectively.
    .
    The first direct polytechnic admission (DPA) exercise starts on July 11 for those who want to confirm places in polytechnic courses before taking their GCE O-level examinations in October.
    .
    The DPA gives the polytechnics greater flexibility to select students based on their talents and not just examination results.
    .
