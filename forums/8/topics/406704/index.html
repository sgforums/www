    Your signature is not needed. Only your credit/debit card is needed.

    Sometimes your card is not needed. Only your card number is needed.

    Once the card is swiped through the reader, your account is billed with the transactioned automatically. The transaction can be billed to  your account manually, if your card  number is given in writing.

    The onus is on you to prove you did not do the transaction, therefore the signature works against you. Anyway even if you did not sign, i.e. you gave your card number, you are by contract liable for transaction done on the card account.

    Once you have a credit/debit card, you have a hole to your money that makes it easier for the banks to take your money to pay the merchants. You provide the security by carrying the hole guardedly.
