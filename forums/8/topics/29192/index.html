    Yup. Came up with this myself, because I always wonder why I am so unlucky.
    The Success Luck Theorem states that you will be successful/unsuccessful given the amount of luck and ability you have, together with other forces in the world.
    In equation form:

    50%Luck + 45%Ability +5%Other forces=Success.

    If you fall below 50%, you are doomed to fail. But note, NO one has NO luck, or zero luck.
    To give an example to illustrate my theorem, suppose a man playing jackpot. He doesn't need any ability, but when he has the 45% luck and 5%Other forces, he is going to strike jackpot. Let's say that he has 50%Luck and 5%Other forces, he is going to strike it BIG!
    Compared to the guy playing blackjack, the bJ guy would have luck, ability and other forces to ensure him a success.
    Ever thought that you are unlucky? Nope, you are not.
