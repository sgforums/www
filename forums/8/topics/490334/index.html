Your complete guide to Halloween this year

  • FireIce
    14 Oct 15, 11:41

    It's that time of the year again, where you can put on your scariest outfit to haunt the parties along Clarke Quay. We have put together some of 2015's most fun Halloween events that will send chills down your spine.

    Scare Events

    In case you still haven't gone, the fifth edition of Halloween Horror Nights is all about bringing local ghost stories to life. There are creepy Chinese vampires, a cursed kampong and some hungry ghosts in these four haunted houses and three scare zones. Until Oct 31

    Up the ante with Changi Revisited - The Hendon Horrors, which is set within the grounds of the infamous Old Changi Hospital and the nearby Changi Barracks. Bring your friends to solve a murder mystery play at the fictitious Hendon Hotel. Oct 23-25, 30

    Bugis Street will be transformed into a scare zone where monsters and ghouls will haunt its alleys. There is also a party and a flea market. Oct 23, 24, 30, 31

    The Jurong Spring Youth Club is holding a haunted house event called The Missing 4. Participants must come in groups of four too witness the "horrors" in the house. Nov 7

    Parties

    The White Rabbit  is having a garden-themed Halloween bash. The best dressed monster or ghost may win a two-night stay at the IZE Seminyak Bali. Oct 31

    1Altitude and Altimate are holding a Hell's Gate party with a 360 simulation featuring the protagonist Altimasia fighting demons and spectres. Those in costume enter for free. Oct 31

    Kilo Lounge's After Dark Presents: A Halloween Special. The club's sixth, seventh and eight floors are decorated according to the yarn they have spun us: three wives killed themselves to be immortal with their husband, and the house is cursed. The DJ line-up includes Stefano Ritteri, Maurice Simon and Oliver Osborne. Oct 30-Nov 1

    Party at Zouk's Witch Hunt event. Dress like their fabled witch or come in costume. The prize pool includes trips to Tokyo, Hong Kong or a staycation at W Singapore. Oct 31

    Test your scare knowledge at The Wallich, which is hosting its Halloween Special Quiz Night and After Party. Costumed guests get a free shot. Oct 31

    Attica is having four themed parties—one for kinky folks, one for singles and another two for vampire and zombie nerds. Oct 28, 29, 30, 31 respectively

    Bang Bang is hosting a Mad Max Halloween Party. Dress as the heroine Furiosa or come as the guitar-playing Doof Warrior. Oct 31

    Singapore's Halloween Pub Crawl includes visits to three Halloween themed bars and tasting special drinks. Oct 31

    Ce La Vi is bringing Cirque Le Soir this Halloween. Expect sword-swallowers, acrobats, clowns, magicians and more. Oct 31

    Food & Drink Deals

    Catalunya rolls out four special Halloween drinks like Toil and Trouble, Red Eye, Witches’ Brew (that contains eyeballs) and Potion Royal. There's also a Weekend Bingo Brunch with similarly themed cupcakes and tarts. Oct 26-Nov 1; brunch on Oct 31 and Nov 1

    Senso Ristorante & Bar has a Halloween brunch that consists of Pumpkin Risotto cooked with pecorino cheese and Trumpet of the Dead mushrooms, among other dishes. Diners get free flow of Prosecco and other themed drinks Oct 25

    The Swiss Club is having a Champagne Brunch and a Night Feast. There will also be a best-dressed contest. Oct 31

    Movie Screenings

    Golden Village is having a Halloween Horrorthon with films like The Visit, Crimson Peak and Goosebumps. Oct 23, 24, 30, 31

    The Projector is screening horror classics like The Devil's Backbone, Scream,  The Texas Chainsaw Massacre and The Conjuring. Oct 27, 28, 30, 31 respectively
