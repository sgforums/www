    What are those check marks next to my messages?

    We are glad you asked. These green checkmarks are actually very important since they are indicators that acknowledge the delivery of a message. Here is what they mean:

     - message successfully delivered to the server. 

     - message successfully delivered to the phone of your chat partner. Note: this does not indicate that message was read, just that it was delivered. 

    Also note that in a group chat you will only see one check mark indicating that the message was successfully delivered to the server.

     

     

    What does "Last seen at..." and "Online" mean exactly?

    "Online" and "Last seen at..." are timestamps that tell you whether that contact is online, or the last time they were connected to WhatsApp.
     

    • "Online" means that contact has WhatsApp open and is connected to the internet. However, it doesn't necessarily mean they have read your chat.
       
    • "Last seen at..." refers to the time the contact left WhatsApp. You can also think of this as "Went offline at..."
