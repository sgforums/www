    Sung-hyon moves to Ilmare and receives a strange letter. The date of the letters he receives is 1999, tow years ahead of the time. What was written in the letter comes in reality. He receives another letter saying, Be careful, there will be a lot of snow in January, and everyone will have flu.

Un-ju has not been communicating with her boyfriend for a long time, and Sung-hyon tells Un-ju something.
