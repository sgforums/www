    Can anyone recommend me a good salon. Budget of about 35 and below cut + wash ? I want a salon that specialises or can cut a more "caucasian" hairstyle. Like those simple brush back hairstyle for medium length hair.

    So can anyone recommend to me a good salon that isn't the typical only know how 2 cut the latest japanese hairstyles salon ?
