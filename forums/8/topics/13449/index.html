    I'm an MP3 user and I've frenz using MD player so let me give you some tips.

    MP3 Player
    ==========
    Adv:
    -Lighter and compact (unless it the creative jukebox)
    -unshakable
    -fast ripping from CD (my player comes with a powerful software)
    -can store song downloaded from the net (Audio Galaxy or Kazaa)
    -can act as a "diskette" where you can upload non-MP3 files to your player and transport to other PCs with USB ports.

    Disadv:
    -Needs a PC to upload and download
    -Limited playtime. 1MB is 1min so a 64MB player can hold about 1 hour song. My player with 96MB hold about 1 and half hours. Unless you must Creative Jukebox with 5000MB memory, then I think you can ignore it.(you have to carry a whole suitcase of CDs to match that playtime)
    -some players have lousy amps so the sound not so good. (lucky my still ok :p )

    MD players
    ==========
    Adv:
    -compact
    -recordable disks so you bring your whole library of MDs to listen unlike MP3 players were you must select carefully.
    -most amps are good
    -almost unshakable (98%)

    Disavd:
    -More exp due to the discs.
    -long recording times. There a technology that doubles the recording speed but you only save half the recording time, still no fight to mp3 ripping which is done in a flash.
    -battery eater

    So I give you all the adv and disadv...you decided loh. Hope it helps
