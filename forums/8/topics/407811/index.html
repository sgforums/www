    Two events in a roll reported in Straits Times (7Aug0) to create the so called records by the the records-crazed Singaporeans. What a waste of precious resources and time!

    2)gan eng seng primary school setting world records for making the largest quilt

    Other silly YOG related records:

    1. Largest display made of paper boats (177,140) - Gan Eng Seng Secondary School.
    2. Fastest timing for passing of rubber bands (10 people passed 40 rubber bands in one minute) - East Spring Secondary School.
    3. Largest human formation of YOG logo (1,300 people) - East Spring Secondary School.
    4. Largest YOG logo made of cans (5,000 cans, 8m x 4m) - Orchid Park Secondary School.
    5. Largest display made of recycled batteries (4,000) - Bukit View Secondary.
    6. Most number of people doing frog jump (300) - Fuhua Secondary School.
    7. Fastest in passing water from cup to cup (33l cups in one minute) - Choa Chu Kang Secondary School.
    8. Most number of paper origami eagles folded (50,000) - Yuying Secondary School.
    9. Fastest in passing of balls over 50m (147 balls in 10 minutes) - Yio Chu Kang Secondary School.
    10. Most number of 400m rounds run in one hour (42 rounds) - Guangyang Secondary School.
    11. Longest batik (202m x 0.85m) - Mayflower Primary School.
    12. Largest logo made out of hand-held sparklers set (248 participants) - Fajar Secondary School.
    13. Largest quilt (12m x 9.7m) - Gan Eng Seng Primary School.
    14. Most number of people bouncing table tennis balls (650) - Fuhua Secondary School.
    15. Largest map of Singapore made with postcards - Ngee Ann Secondary School.
