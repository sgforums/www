    Originally posted by Ah Chia:

    Rent out room have to pay how much tax?

     

    http://www.iras.gov.sg/irasHome/page_ektid2378.aspx

    Example:

    If your house can fetch a rent of $1,000 per month,
    AV is $1,000 x 12 = $12,000
    Property Tax is 10% x $12,000 = $1,200 per year

    If you are living in your house and qualify for the owner-occupier's concession,
    Property tax is 4% x $12,000 = $480 per year

     

    That is why people prefer renting out rooms than renting out the whole place, unless the rent is high enough to cover the tax too.
