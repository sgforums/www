    hello, i'm a JC student. the reason as to why jc students pay student fares is simple.

    JC students are ALL in a 2-year range. afterwhich, they may quit aft As (not suggested) or go to a local uni (where they pay adult fares).

    try to look at it this way. see "how near you are to entering the workforce". a jc student, upon embarking jc education has abt 6yrs (gals)/8yrs (guys) b4 they start working if they take the jc uni route. when these jc students are in uni/NS, they are already paying adult fares. thus, they pay 4yrs/6yrs of adult fare b4 they start working!

    a poly student, if they start work aft obtaining their diploma, has only 3yrs (gals)/5 yrs(guys) b4 entering the workforce. they pay 3yrs/5yrs of adult fare b4 they start working!!

    poly students are at an advantage? it's evident.
