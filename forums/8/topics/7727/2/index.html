    The Meaninglessness of Sincerity

    i once asked someone what this whole "sincerity" thing was all abt but never got a good answer out of it. what is it to go after a girl "sincerely" anyway? maybe i'm being naive but is there another way to go after a girl? you mean some ppl like a girl and wish they could be more than frens but do it "insincerely"??? what does that even mean??? i would be most grateful if someone could enlighten me.

    which leads to my next point. assuming we know what it means to be "sincere", then is bugging a girl all the time showing your sincerity? i've heard of cases where the more you persist with a ger, the higher the chance you'll succeed with the girl. of cuz wif other gers, the more you persist, the more they hate you and tell their frens abt you. but why should it be that for some ppl, "sincerity" is equated with how much you bother the girl??? i really, honestly don't get it.

    if a guy leaves a ger alone after she has shown no interest in him, does that mean he is not being sincere? i thought the guy was showing a good measure of respect for what the ger wants. you mean if i dun respect her wishes and persist some more, that is a measure of my sincerity??? goodness. plse tell me that sincerity means more than this. if a ger doesn't show interest, isn't it only prudent to forget abt her and move on? even if the guy moves on, does that mean he wasn't "sincere" in the first place? you mean if one is "sincere" he should never be able to get over a ger? or is it that if a guy gets over her too quickly then he's not being sincere? double goodness. i always thot that sincerity meant that you like the girl for who she is and really want to see if things can work out between the two of you. no one can be certain what the outcome will be. but then that's the chance he's willing to take. it's a serious matter and that's what he really wants to do, to give things a try. but then this would be true of every person who goes after a girl right? so what is so special abt this thing called "sincerity"? everyone is sincere one what!
    but if this is the case, then "sincerity" in courtship has no meaning. because everyone is sincere to some extent. but why should the "extent" of sincerity count for anything at all?

    methinks that if you equate sincerity with how much (or how) a guy is willing to chase you (the "extent" of sincerity), then i wonder whether it's a measure of his liking for you or a measure of how much you want your own ego stroked instead. sincerity shouldn't be (though i know for some ppl it is) a big concern at the initial "chasing" stage. it (i.e. sincerity) should only become apparent when (or if) he is willing to work thru the r'ship and all its problems and ups and downs. isn't that the true measure of sincerity? and so if you know a guy is like that, that he has the kind of character that is willing to see things thru and work things out thru thick and thin, then that's a measure of his sincerity right there. he just is that kind of person to be serious abt a r'ship. so shouldn't we move beyond the surface level of what "sincerity" means already? if sincerity is just abt how much you'll do for a girl at the start of the r'ship, then that's a very poor measure of the guy indeed. which guy wouldn't put his best foot forward and give it his all? so the one who can convince you the best is the most "sincere"??? triple goodness. once you get to know the guy, you'll know whether or not he's the kind to play around or the kind who will flee when problems arise or whether he will stick by you no matter what. the true measure of sincerity to me is whether he can last the long haul. see after 2-3 yrs whether he will still do the same things he did when he was first courting you. most guys will not. if you think otherwise, you must know really amazing guys. stick with them then.

    my point is this: tying sincerity with what or how much a guy is willing to suffer for you or do for you at the start of a r'ship is not a sure sign how much he'll treasure you later on. and yet some gers place so much emphasis on this fact. in the end it's all a game isn't it? who impresses you more at the start. how peculiar human beings are to place so much hope on something they know will not be indicative of things to come. in other words, a show of "sincerity" at the start of the r'ship is pointless, meaningless and empty, if you think that that "show" points to how much he likes/loves you. and yet some guys are being picked over others precisely on this score.

    if you think all this is obvious, and that there will be other factors that girls figure into their assessements, then why is it that "sincerity" still figures so much in the evaluations of some gers?

    "oh, but he sent me a 100 roses every day for the entire month of february!"

    "he didn't have to call me. i was overseas yet he spent so much money calling me every night. and he lost so much sleep even tho he had to work the next day."

    "oh i told him abt this really expensive watch i really liked and he bought it for me! and i don't even know him all that well!"

    "he made me these little cards to tell me how much he missed me. and he writes such wonderful poetry!"

    aren't all these signs of what one might call "sincerity"??? of cuz if there is no chemistry in the first place these little signs lose their meaning. but even if the chemistry is there, what meaning can one find in these signs? if a ger likes two guys and one does more for her than the other, she'll prob pick him. but why??? cos of how he makes her feel? he makes her feel special maybe. in the end really, all "sincerity" amounts to is making the ger "feel" that she is special (tho i think this is a really impt thing to do, it shouldn't be equated with sincerity). it's a fleeting, momentary thing. whether or not she really is special we will only know in the long run. cos once the "feelings" are gone and problems come around, we'll see whether the "extent" of sincerity now is proportional to the "show" put up at the very beginning. what i'm trying to say is that "sincerity", a concept for so long restricted to what a guy would do at the start of a r'ship, should instead (and more properly) be a concept used only in the long run. at the start, everyone is "sincere" (and so the word has lost its meaning) and the only thing that matters is how you make each other feel. sincerity should be taken out of this context and put to work in the longer term. it shouldn't be abt how much passion there was at the beginning (altho passion is impt, it shouldn't be equated with sincerity). sincerity should, instead, be abt how hard a person is willing to work at the r'ship down and thru the years. but since you can never tell at the outset whether someone will stick by you thru all the ups and downs, "sincerity" can never be spotted beforehand. you can only gauge it by how a person handled previous r'ships (and even then it doesn't mean that your r'ship wif the person will end up well), not by how much he is willing to do for you now. sincerity then can only be appreciated in retrospect, as it were, thru the rear view mirror. hence, liking a guy at the very beginning because of his "sincerity" is (as the title of my post says) meaningless.

    quadruple goodness! so cheong hei! i go for lunch now....
