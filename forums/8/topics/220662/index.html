    The fact that you signed OTP, paid deposit means you have agreed to engage that agent to help you sell your property, regardless if you have signed exclusive to sell' with the agent. Furthermore you have gone for your 1st appointment.

    You should complain to the agency that the agent represents and delay commission payout until all matters regarding the case is resolved. However, you still need to pay commission to the agent whether you like it or not as he is responsible in helping you get a buyer.

    Usually you will encounter 'uncontactable' agents when the agent has too many deals and doing too big a scale. He/she will have an assistant to screen phone calls.

    Rule of the thumb:

    1) Never engage the service of very popular agents who boast of doing a lot of deals. They won't have time for you and see to the details of each transaction.

    2) The level of trust and ease of communication you have with the agent is what counts most.

    Not advisable to change agent since you have gone through 1st appointment. Just go through the 2nd appointment with him and move on.
