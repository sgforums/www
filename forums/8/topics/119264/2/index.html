    This is what I got from reading the SP Prospectus 2005/2006 under Admissions and Courses.

    I'm not sure whether you want to apply for Class Transfer or Course Transfer, so I will post both.

    Application for Class Transfer
    Requests for class transfers may be considered subject to individual merit on each case and available vacancies. Applications must be made before the commencement of each academic semester through application forms available from the Admissions Office, the respective academic school office and via the Internet at www.sp.edu.sg/departments/asd/forms.htm. The student must continue to attend the class to which he has been originally allocated pending the result of his application.

    Application for Course Transfer
    Fresh applicants wishing to transfer course may appeal be writing in to Admissions Office. Such appeals may be considered and are subject to indivdual merit and vacancies. These must reach Admissions Office by 30 April 2005.

    For current Singapore Polytechnic students, only those who have sat for and passed the semestral assessment may apply. Requests for course transfer may be considered only at or before the commencement of each academic session and is subject to individual merit and vacancies. Applications should be submitted by 28 May 2005. Application forms are available from the Admissions Office, the respective academic school office and via the Internet at www.sp.edu.sg/departments/asd/forms.htm. The student must continue to attend his original course of study pending the result of his application.
