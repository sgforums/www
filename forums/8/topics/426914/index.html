  • Gerberad
    5 Apr 11, 16:40

    i know at woodlands industrial, there a few of those multi level warehouse shaped like multi storey carparks, where u can get a space for $500-$800 a month. and at that industrial is where all the wood/metal/electronic works are located at. so nobody will find u noisy or dirty. there is 3 carpark slots for each warehouse u rent, and it is 2 levels. which is good cos u can do all the industrial on first floor and office on the 2nd level. there are canteens nearby. basically in my opinion i find all the amenities there kind of suitable for your works.

    but if u are more of the artistic side, i think maybe u should find a space in town, like tanjong pagar or the industrial park near vivocity, i think they go at the rate of $100-$150 for 3 days with aircondition and the room is like the size of a 5 room hdb flat. it is not as dirty n dusty as woodlands, and its near exhibition areas in town and museums so it would be easy for u to transport your works to exhibite.

    in my opinion, and i am not trying to offend other forumers, i do not think universities, community clubs, hdb, private housing areas are suitable as these are the places where children are walking around and people are living, so i guess people will not tolerate noises and dirt and it poses danger to their living too.
