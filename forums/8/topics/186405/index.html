    Not on company time

    SURFING pornographic sites while at work is a definite no-no, say netizens.

    The discussion started after the MP for Potong Pasir, Mr Chiam See Tong, fired former taxi driver Lim Kian Heng for such an alleged misdemeanour.

    Mr Lim is now apparently trying to run against Mr Chiam in Potong Pasir.

    soulhacker says that people should refrain from visiting pornographic websites at work, especially out of 'respect' for female colleagues.

    pkchukiss says: 'When you are on the job, you make sure that you perform the job that you are hired for, as you are on the company's time.

    'Checking e-mail and surfing websites when you have work to do is unfair to your employer.'

    All for you, dad

    AFTER getting lost on a snowy mountain, the Singaporeans who went to Japan to find an elusive karate master have apparently tracked him down. Well, maybe not because there are doubts that he may not be the man.

    Still, netizens who initially laughed at the group are now singing a different tune.

    Rogue thinks the group should be lauded for fulfilling their late father's wish.

    Another netizen agrees: 'The children are being very filial by doing so much to fulfil the wish of their parents. I would do the same, if it were within my means.'
