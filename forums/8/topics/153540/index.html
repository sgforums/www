    Current POA format is:
    Paper 1
    30 MCQ questions 1 hour

    Paper 2
    Section A 4 questions compulsory
    Section B 2 question choose one.

    What working that you need to do on other paper

    You can use calculator for calculations, other than that theres no need for rough working unless you count accounts that the questions do not require you to open. In this case, you can just do them on the actual answer paper. No bonus marks, but no minus marks oso..
