    best worst

    router switch hub

    if you just wanna share files, share printers, then it's ok to go for a hub. if you want slightly faster performance, pls go for at least a switch (games, internet)

    this is due to the fundamentals of how a hub and a switch works. a hub connects the pc's one a a time (one to one), whereas, a switch connects the pc's all together at once (one to many)

    and thoreldan has brought up a good point, it's easier / more convenient to share your internet access with a router
