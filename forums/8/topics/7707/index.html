    I dream of being a sports journalist and its ne more week to sleepness nites and here's my predictions for Euro 2000:

    Group Stage

    Group A
    I feel this is the toughest group with England, Germany, Portugal and Romania. It will be a very close and my predictions will be Portugal topping the grp and Germany second.

    Group B
    We see one of the host country here with Belgium, Italy, Sweden and Turkey. I do expect a few suprises here and the team that will go through are Belgium topping it and Italy second.

    Group C
    "Easiest" among all 4 grps with suprise team of the tournament Slovenia. Joining them are Spain, Norway and Yugoslavia. Most prob Norway will top this grp and Spain getting second.

    Group D
    Another grp of death with World Champions France and co-host Holland in this grp. Not forgetting 96 finalist Czech Rep and also 92 fairytale team Denmark. However I think France could go through by winning this grp and Holland second.

    Quarter Finals

    Portugal Vs Italy: This could be a mouth watering match with the mini battle of Figo against Maldini. Tough to decide as I'm a fan of the Azzuris but I'll say Portugal will go through.

    Belgium vs Germany: I'm no big fan of both these teams but by the looks of things I'll say Germany to go through.

    Norway vs Holland: A mini battle of EPL vs Primera Liga with the amt of Nowegians playing in the Premier League and Dutch players in the Barcelona side. I predict Holland to win this.

    Spain vs France: This match could go to extra-time or even penalties. This is one match that is super hard to predict but I guess France has the upper hand in this one.

    Semi-finals:

    Germany vs Holland: Wow! What a match this would be! But with the home advantage I'll say Holland will reach the finals.

    Portugal vs France: Some ppl by now may say I favour Portugal but for this match I'll say France will win.

    Finals:

    Holland vs France: Wow!! It may be repeat of the group D match but it still promises to be a cracker. But my guess for Euro 2000 champions is Holland.

    This is just my predictions and I may be wrong, as in my heart I always hope for an England vs Italy final. So who do u guys think will win it this yer?
