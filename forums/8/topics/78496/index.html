    At Bangkok, take the skytrain, alight at Nana. Then walk along the nightmarkets that extends until Asok. A fake prada wallet usually quoted at 2000 baht and above can be bought at 300baht. Just ask nicely and when rejected, pretend to walk away. (500 baht = S$1

    If you are a guy, drop by Patpong 1 and 2 for thai gal show. The gals are usually prettier on the 1st floor but some are trans, so better ask so you don't pay for what you don't want.

    If you want food, drop at Saphan Taksin, then walk out to Yaowarat street, Bangkok's Chinatown for a claypot full (around 4 small bowls) of braised sharks fin with beansprout for 200 baht. For desert, drink birds nests soup with dang gui for 300 baht.

    Cheap and nice food can be found at shopping center food courts. A bowl of noodles cost 50 baht. The food court works on coupon system. You buy like 200 baht worth of coupons then return them what you cannot finish. I like the duck noodles at world trade center at Chit Lom

  • Larry
    19 May 04, 09:30
    Koh Samui? Not bad a place for relaxation. Most people would go to Chaweng for accomodations as its the most developed part of the island. The whole island is about one-third the size of Singapore, so u can actually tour the whole island in less than 3 hours. If u are willing to spend on accomodations, try going to Tong Sai. The beach and water there is beautiful. But very expensive too. Be sure to visit the Grandfather and Grandmother rock in Lamui. U will be in for a surprise.

    Bangkok is quite ok for a shopper. The night market, (Chatuchak) is about the size of 9 soccer fields (At least this is what the tour guide told me the last time I was there.....) but the variety tends to repeat once u go through a few rows of the shops.

    Also, visit the river that's full of catfish. It's a rare sight when u throw bread into the murky waters and see all the catfish surfacing. Oh yes, don't miss the theatre that hosts transvetite shows as well. Some of them are very pretty and I did have some photo shots with a couple of them.
