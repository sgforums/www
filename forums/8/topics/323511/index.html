    Some interesting facts I found on Google about four-leaf clovers:

    "Approximately 1 in every 10,000 clovers has the four-leaflet form." - Wikipedia

    "Legend also holds that if a lady hangs a four-leaf clover on her door, the next man to come in will become her husband." - Wikipedia

    "The current record holder is a Pennsylvania prison inmate, George Kaminski. While serving time on a kidnapping conviction for the past 25 years, Kaminski has gathered 72,927 four-leaf clovers. He found them, one at a time, hidden in the grass of prison yards.

    Kaminski was not available for an interview, but a caseworker at the minimum-security facility in Mercer, Pa., relayed a few questions and answers by phone. Kaminski said he started finding the clovers to show a younger inmate who was depressed that 'you can do anything you set your mind to.'" - The Washington Post, March 17, 2005
