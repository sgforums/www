    For transferring of whatsapp messages:

    1) Go to your old device's whatsapp, menu > settings > chat settings > backup conversation. This will take ~2mins. After its done, ensure that the last backup date written is the point of time where you press backup. 

    2) Plug your old and new device into the computer (fastest way)

    3) On your computer, choose internal memory for your old device.

    4) Look for the whatsapp folder and copy/cut the whole folder (depending if you want to maintain a backup on your old phone or not, but you will not be able to get access to it once you login into your new phone)

    5) On your computer, choose internal memory for your new device and paste the whole whatsapp folder here. (Whatsapp will only read from internal memory, it will not work if this folder is in your SD card)

    6) Download(Or login) whatsapp on your new device.

    7) After successfully confirming that you are the account holder, whatsapp will prompt you with "Back up found, do you want to restore?" (something along this line). Choose YES. 

    8) And wah-lah, your old whatsapp messages are all on your new devices. 

    This steps also will transfer the photos sent to your old phone via whatsapp, however in the personal chat/group chat, the picture may or may not seemed to blur out due to different file path. However, you can still find these photos in your photo albums. 

    As for SMS, different brands provides with their own internal brand transfer. Since both your old and new devices are samsung's you might want to head down to any of their samsung shops to check out with their brand ambassador. Hope it helps!
