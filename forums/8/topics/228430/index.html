    The curtains came down on Neptune Theatre on Friday evening, bringing an end to 34 glorious years of entertainment.

    As the dancers prepared for the final dance, many took the opportunity to capture a few memories to take home.

    Walking down memory lane, the guests present will be the last to experience Singapore's largest theatre-restaurant ever.

    Since its heyday in the 1970s, the Neptune Theatre has been home to the longest running topless revue in Singapore and the finest Cantonese cuisine.

    The building, located at Collyer Quay, has been slated for redevelopment.

    The building was the starting point for many of Singapore's entertainers and has hosted hundreds of events attended by over 10 million Singaporeans.
