    I find that a lot of my friends' mother do not allow them to donate blood too. Ya, maybe they just don't know that donating blood is quite harmless. It may be difficult to change their mindset, but it could just be one of their ways to protect their beloved children. My mom is very skeptical about this too. But I am glad I managed to convince her that I will be fine after the blood donation.

    A lot of people claimed that donating blood is not painful at all. I used to doubt that fact. It just seems so unbelievable especially when you see all those frightening equipments, needles and bags full of blood!

I looked away when the nurse was about to insert the needle into my hand. I was expecting a very sharp pain. Half a minute passed, I still don't feel anything. I turned around only to find that everything was in place!

    So to those who hesitate to donate blood in the fear that it will be a painful process, please don't. Do your bit if you can.
