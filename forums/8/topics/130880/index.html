  • oxford mushroom

    It is well established that both romantic love and drug addiction are very similar in terms of the neurological activation pathways. Both involve the release of dopamine in the nucleus accumbens and frontal lobes. Other stimuli that can produce release dopamine and sensations of pleasure include alcohol, cocaine, methamphetamine, heroin, nicotine, marijuana, other drugs and by compulsive activities such as gambling, eating, and sex.

    Perhaps the day will come when we can treat a broken heart by using a drug to simulate the release of dopamine in the nucleus accumbens (an area deep within the brain). In fact, when that day comes, will we still need romance? Why bother with flowers on Valentine's Day? Just pop a pill....
