    Hi, I'm a 27-year-old housewife who has been travelling with my husband around Asia for the past year. We usually stay in a city for 2-4 months then move on to another, due to the nature of his work.

    As such, I've been unable to find a job after resigning from my job in Singapore. Call it the Singaporean Curse, but I am someone who enjoys working very much. I've tried to keep myself occupied by learning a fourth language and attending classes on yoga, knitting, flower arrangement etc. but these are more like time-fillers than activities that give deep fulfillment.
    There is still a void in my heart that I cannot seem to fill.

    While I do enjoy being a housewife and caring for my husband, I resent waking up everyday and not having goals. I've considered free-lance writing, and community work...but these have not come to fruition due to the short period of stay in each city. I've only managed to do some free-lance translation work, but that is not frequent enough to keep me occupied. Worse, moving from city to city means that I've not been able to build up an immediate circle of friends. My husband travels very frequently (average 2 times a week) which leaves me very lonely.

    We don't plan to have kids for another 1-2 years, so I'm likely to have plenty of time on my hands till then.

    What do you girls do to fill up your time? HELP!!

    We stay in serviced apartments, so there's not much housework to do (save the occasional laundry. His shirts and suits are sent for dry-cleaning). Even when he is around, we usually eat out, as we enjoy exploring new food joints. So I don't even have to cook. I tell you....I'm a bum lah.

    There are perks to being a travelling 'tai-tai'.....but there's also an emptiness (due to lack of goals) and strange loneliness that few people can understand.

    Sigh... I just want to be more useful to society... and derive some kind of self-fulfillment...
