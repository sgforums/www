    Subject Combination. When you are in upper Sec, you will be given a choic to choose the subject you like.

    For example me, I have:

    Combined Science(chem,bio)
    Combined Humanities
    E Math
    Chinese(Fixed)
    English(Fixed)
    Principles Of Accounts.

    What you can choose is

    E math or A math,
    POA, D&T, F&N or Art
    Pure Sci or Combined Sci.
    Geography or History.

    For my school they say,"If you want POA, you must also accept Geo,Sci(Chem/bio).

    So it is very Important that you are in a good class when you are lower sec. For me, I am in the lousy class and therefore doesnt have the rights to choose the subject which I want.

    My $2 worth

    If you like business sort-of thing, POA is the right subject.
    If you like energneering, D&T is the one.
    If you like cooking, without saying is F&N
    Have art talent, Do Art.

    As for Science, Get the Pure ones.
    Although Pure and Sub science have 2 differnet name, they actually have chapters in common. For Pure Science, it's just a few more details.

    As for combined humanities, one of the subject is Social Studies(Complusory), and the other is Geo/His. In my opinion, Geo is more easier as compared to Hist. If you liked Social Studies in lower sec, mostly you'll like Hist. As for Geo, if you to know about global warming and stuff related to Farming and technology, factors concerning about plantation arigculture, this is the best.
