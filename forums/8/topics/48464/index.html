    What the producers say......
    In this adaptation of the long-running Marvel comic "The Incredible Hulk," research scientist Dr. Bruce Banner's (Eric Bana) failed experiments cause him to mutate into a monstrously powerful and savage green-skinned behemoth whenever he loses control of his emotions. Jennifer Connelly plays his love interest Betty Ross, Nick Nolte plays Bruce's dangerous father, and Sam Elliott plays Betty's father, the tough-as-nails General Thaddeus "Thunderbolt" Ross.

    What skeujin says...

    To sum the entire movie in one word... i would use the term BORING! True to my previous post movie review, Stan Lee has gone senile and added another flop into the laps of his adoring fans.

    I cant tell you when i have ever sat through a movie and wished that i could change the channel.... hmmmm.... then again... yes i can, Daredevil!

    This movie is total crap! The budget must be so low that they cant splurge on the special effects and have to put the audience through mindless garble and flashbacks over and over again.

    Ang Lee's direction of the movie is totally dissapointing, trying to put forth a comic book style of story telling by adding windows of actions throughout the movie only adds to the headache by trying to keep up with the useless banter by mixing it with un-coordianted shots. Go back to china dude!

    Even the origins of the hulk has been twisted to add another charecter that has no belongings in the comic. (more crap to reduce $ spent on CG).

    Even the CG is of such low quality that i could swear that i was watching a muscular version of shrek goes to hollywood.
