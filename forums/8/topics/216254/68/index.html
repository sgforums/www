    Stir-fried Beef Udon, Chit Chat style

    Ingredients:

    1 egg
    1 handful of udon
    1 small chilli padi
    1 banana
    1 piece of beef, sliced
    some oil from pork pork

    Method:

    Fry chilli padi lightly in oil produced by pork pork.
    Add in beef, fry until cooked.
    Throw in udon noodles.
    Crack the egg over udon.

    Remove from heat.
    Add banana slices to garnish.
