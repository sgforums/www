    Tomorrow will be the last day of operation for Hougang Mall's Zone X outlet...

    When it first opened 8 years ago, it far won the then arcade at Hougang Plaza... It initially moved from being mainly ticket redemption outlet, to one without ticket redemption machines few years later... 2 years ago, it underwent a renovation where by 1/2 of the arcade is taken away by Asia Mall Management and it opened Pretty Pix to house NeoPrints Machines and Toy Catchers... Come 21st March, it will be the centres last day of operation... Zone X, Pretty Pix will close for good... The staff will be relocated to different centres and temporary staff will be dismissed...

    FYI - NTUC Club have already closed. They're clearing their furniture thru a sales

    From my understanding, the whole area currently housing Zone X, NTUC Club and Pretty Pix will be torn down to make way for food establishment...

    My purpose of starting this thread is to find out if there's any supporter for this outlet despite it being neighborhood one...

    Personally I'm sad to see it go... Afterall, I've been a regular at this arcade for as long as it existed and the bond with the staffs is present... My best impression of Zone X friendly customer service also begins there...
