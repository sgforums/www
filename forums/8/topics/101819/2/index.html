    hey, looks like u've already gotten some sound advice. But i juz like to intervene and be a busybody sometime
    Here's my take in this:

    To attain a flat tummy, u needa do 3 main steps:

    1) Cardio-vascular activity(eg jogging, swimming, cycling, etc)
    I recommend jogging as u support your own weight, hence adding to the intensity.

    Pointers/tips to look out for while jogging.

    -As pointed out before, try to jog/walk for at least 20 mins and above. Below that and ur body is using glucose as the respiratory substrate, hence there's no net loss in fat volume. Try to maintain for ard 30 mins, tt way, ur body will start metabolising lipids to use as energy.

    -Wake up early and jog in the morning. Studies have shown that jogging in the morning tends to use stored fats and glycogen rather than those in ur blood. Moreover, doing cardio in the morning will kick-start your metabolism, and rev up ur energy level throughout the day.

    -Find a jogging partner for motivation and support.

    -Remember to hydrate yourself before, during, and after the jog.

    2) Strength/Resistant training
    Doing crunches, situps etc will strengthen ur abs. (though much is dependent on your genes)
    Here are some effective exercises you can try to strengthen your abs
    Change ur routine every 4-6 weeks to avoid your muscles from adapting to the intensity and exercises and thus the plateau-ing effect.

    Note: doing ab exercises will NOT give u six packs, the ONLY way for u to see ur six packs is to reduce your body fats.
    However, strong abs support your spine and protect your back.

    3) A healthy, low calorie diet
    In a nutshell, eat a balanced diet.
    Rich breakfast, moderate lunch, little dinner.
    Eat in smaller proportions. I think u shld noe wad is healthy and wad is not.
    Try not to eat after 6-7pm, as after that your metabolic rate falls, and ur body will convert the excess glucose to fats. And for guys, the most amount of fat storage is in the adipose tissue under ur tummy. (trivial: for females, it's the hips and butt, hence the hourglass shape)

    Hope this helps. If not, whatever.

    Cheerios,
    mythoria
