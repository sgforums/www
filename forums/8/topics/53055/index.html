    I would strongly disagree.

    For anyone who walked in and expected to see a replay of T2, it would certainly be a big disappointment. However, note that the intention of the script was to take the focus off the terminators themselves and in instead shift it to Judgement Day.

    The story has now changed fundamentally, in that the assumption in the first two movies was that Judgement Day could be prevented. Along the aspect of the storyline, this is an interesting development that open up a whole lot of new possibilities.

    Stepping away from the numbed action-flick watcher's role and into that of the movie aficionado, one would have to appreciate what had been done with this movie. They took a movie that worked very well, and while the overwhelming temptation would have been to simply repeat the formula, they took it in a different direction and made it work. That was one hell of a ballsy move.
