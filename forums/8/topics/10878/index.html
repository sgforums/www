    Eight ball is when u arrange the balls in triangle shape and then finish potting either stripe or colour to take the black. Usually played at pubs.

    Nine ball is played with nine balls numbered 1 to 9 and arranged in a diamond. Opponents have to pot balls in sequence or use available numbered ball to pot the number 9 ball.
