    The number of spells you can learn is dependant on your intelligence. That also means that any magical item or potions that increase your intelligence will increase the number of spells you can copy into your spellbook.
    So, if you find that you cannot learn anymore new spells in a particular level, just pop a potion of genius (or something similar) and you can then learn the spells.
