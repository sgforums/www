Sick and down with a cold

  • loudmonkey
    21 Aug 06, 04:06
    Started sneezing this afternoon, on Orchard Rd, after I came out of the station to take a bus.

    I didnt sneeze in the bus, but once I got off the bus, I started sneezing again.

    A few later hours later the yellow mucus came about. Mum said I had eaten too much heaty stuff, and she is probably right.

    My face feels achy, my right nostril is blocked. At times when I blow too hard, my right ear gets affected, and I cant hear very well.

    I am not having a fever, but a headache on the right side of my head keeps throbbing.

    This continued into the night. I tried getting some sleep, but couldnt. My face was aching, my nose was blocked, and then, when i sneezed again, it felt as though my whole body was trying to get rid of needles hiding in my body. It was painful to sneeze.

    I am close to finishing my box of tissues. If I need more, I have to get it from my parents' room, who are asleep, and I dont want to wake them up.

    I drank more water than usual, but that only meant more toilet visits.

    Now I am awake only because I cannot sleep, or I had tried to sleep but couldn't. Normally when I read a bok by bedside I could sleep pretty quick, but this didnt work this time round.

    My TV screen was now showing the MTV awards, and on Channel 8 there is Xiang Yun and Huang Wenyong hosting the Huang Jin Nian Hua Show, definately not for me. So I switched it off.

    I tried switching off the air conditioning, to see if the nose will stop drooling the mucus. It did, but my nose gets blocked, and then i have to blow hard. When I switch on the air conditioning, my nose started to drool again.

    I am going to get an MC tomorrow.

    Thanks for reading my sick diary.
