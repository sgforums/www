    *** Currently, this scheme is available to UOB/DBS/POSB Visa/Master Credit Card member (minimum purchase of $500). You can choose to pay over 6 or 12 months.

    *** For instalment payment, we need to add 6% to the cash price.

    Example:-
    If a phone cash price is $1000, the monthly instalment amount (based on 12 months scheme)
    will be:-

    Cash price + 6% > $1000 + $60 = $1060/12 months
    Therefore the monthly payment = $88.33

    *** Debit Card is not accepted. Only Credit Card is accepted at the moment

    *** If I'm not wrong, your bank account balance will have to be above the remaining liability of the installment, and they will not be available for you to withdraw. But I may be wrong, maybe you can tell us.
