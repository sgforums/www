    Hi,

    Dickson Hotel is OK if you're expecting something basic and affordable. Cleanliness should be OK lah, for most hotels nowadays. I've never stayed there, but I have overseas customers who have. They've just renovated it maybe 2 years back. Just opposite Sim Lim, its within walking distance to Little India and lots of food.

It shouldn't affect your family though, unless they're really the conservative type....because I didn't see any sleazy people hanging around the lobby during my few times there.
