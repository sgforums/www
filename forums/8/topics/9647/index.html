And the feelings more often than not will be corroded in someway or the other, either by his reliance on the lady, or by the lady's longingness of a more stable relationship, the wish to have her loved one beside her when she is feeling down or when she sees another couple lovingly walk past.

    But don't take my word for it. It has all been encouraging posts in this thread and I feel the reality of it all is that it is more negative than positive. And the onus would be on the lady to maintain the relationship, especially for the guy who is going to go through BMT.
