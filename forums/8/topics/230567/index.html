    Basically, a warrant is a financial instrument that gives the holder the right,but not the obligation, to buy the underlying security at a fixed price(strike price).

    Call and put warrants usually have a fixed life span as defined by the issuer. Warrants are favoured by speculators as it provides them greater access to leverage.

    It is considered risky because it is a form of investment catered to more sophisticated investors.
