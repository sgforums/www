    KoC Tips & Tricks

    Selling weapons to buy upgrades, the "Hidden Sell"

    Selling off weapons is a risky bussiness, especially when you have to sell off large amounts which will load your account up with unseen amounts of gold for your enemies to steal. It would not be uncommon if you were attacked just when you sold off and lose tons of gold. Therefor, use the "Hidden Sell" technique. Simply wait untill rank update time (exactly 5 past the hour) to sell your weapons off. At this time, attack pages are not showing up for a few moments. (You get the "Please wait... Rankings are being updated" warning). Now you are invissible to others. Only enemies that have you logged in their attack log would be able to attack you. So to be completely safe, use 2 browser windows. One for selling of weapons, and one for buying the upgrades.

    Rush Attack

    When you buy upgrades, it is best that you buy them with your TBG (Turn Based Gold) or with gold from your attacks rather than from banking. This isn't always so easy. The first upgrade(s) you should be able to purchase just from saving your TBG. But sometime it will just not be possible anymore, so you'll have to use saved gold from attacks to buy the more expensive upgrades. There is one danger, getting attacked yourself and lose your money. To prevent this, use the "Rush Attack" technique. Simply open about 6 Kings of Chaos windows and log in to your account 6 times. On the first window, go to the Armory where you buy your upgrade. On the other 5 pages, go to the attack page and find a suitable target for every page (so you have 5 targets). When you found a target, go to the target's statspage and click 'Attack', no fill in the 15 turns you use to make your attack but don't attack yet. If you did this for all five pages, simply attack all five on a row. Now go to your armory page and buy the upgrade. You can do this with more then 6 windows if the upgrade is more expensive but keep in mind that the more windows you use, the more errors that could come up. Also it depends on the memory of your PC and the speed of the KoC servers how many windows you can open up. For the most expensive upgrades, this technique can't be used anymore. You'll have to bank for those.

    Mass-attack safe

    Mass-attacks are large scale coordinated attacks launched by alliances to damage the enemy's defense weapons and kill the enemy's soldiers, spies and sentries (which reduces your TBG). There is a way to protect yourself from this event. The casualties calculation (of soldiers) is based on the amount of soldiers fighting when you are attacked. These are your trained defense soldiers/mercenaries and untrained soldiers/mercenaries. To keep this amount as low as possible, just train all your soldiers to attack specialists and keep only as much defense specialists as you have defense weapons (or a little bit more in stock). And the best part is, if you suspect to be mass attacked are a mass attack is going on, you can quickly make all your trained defense soldiers untrained again and train them to attack specialists. This way your weapons are not used and will not be damaged.

    Another technique is to only use trained defense mercenaries to arm your defense weapons and train all your TBG producing soldiers to attack specialists. This way you won't lose any gold producing units. (With this technique you won't be able to protect your weapons from being damaged though)

    Also always keep your spy/sentry force small to prevent many casualties but keep your Spy and Sentry Rating high enough by making sure your spies/sentries are equiped with tools.

    Stealing maximum amount of gold

    To steal maximum amount of gold from someone, always look for an army size equal or larger then yours. Also always use 15 attack turns. A nice trick is to make your attacks always on/after 30 min past the hour or 3 min past the hour. This is when the gold comes in and you'll be the first to steal it.

    Buying mercenaries

    Whether you buy attack, defense or untrained mercenaries, always know that the more you buy, the more casualties your army will substain when attacking or being attacked upon. No need to make sure 25% of your army are mercenaries. Buy just enough and just not too few...

    Probe Attacks

    Whenever you failed to spy on an enemy or were unaible to recover his/her Defense Action, launch a "Probe Attack" to know his counter damage. A probe attack is a 1 turn attack upon your enemy just to know his counter damage. Counter damage is not Defense Action! It is the actual damage your enemy's forces inflict when defending. The enemy's counter damage can be found in attack reports or your Attack Log. This is roughly estimated to be 2/3 of the DA. Probe attacks also have a cost, you'll have damage to your weapons and casualties like the result of any other normal attack.
