    I have been hearing those marbles dropping and furniture shifting sound often since I was a kid but I don't think it has anything supernatural about it. In fact i think u can hear it all the time its just that the house have to be really really quiet and that often happens only late at night when everyone is asleep. If u are alone at home in the day and u keep really quiet u will also hear a lot of funny noises and once your imagination starts to run wild, u will freak out.

    guys don't be paranoid la ...those marble sounds u used to hear is probably really just the children upstairs playing with marbles lah..thats why u only remember hearing it when we were younger when marble games were still quite common. another reason i can think of is becoz of the way hdb flats are build. There is a small hollow space for pipes and cables in the concrete between u and your neighbour and it is common to have fine cracks there especially if the flat above has changed their flooring(very common in hdb flats). Therefore a small piece of rock dropping and hitting a pipe can also result in the marble sound. as for those furniture shifting sounds in the middle of the night, all i can say is that not everyone does sleep early. A simple drag of the chair in the dead of the night will also sound loud to the person below. So thats my logical reasoning to those 'ghostly' sounds in the middle of the night. Remember, whenever u feel that u have encountered with a ghost it is mostly just your mind at work.
