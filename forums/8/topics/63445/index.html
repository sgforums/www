RAFFLES HOTEL

  • tattoogirl
    6 Dec 03, 02:43
    Hi all! If you have children, or nieces, nephews, cousins who are kids, or any friend who has kids, here's something to share with you:

    Raffles Hotel has set up a Santa's Grove (#01-27) which features several exciting activities. From now till 24th Dec, Santa will be sharing exciting Christmas Tales from 5-530pm, and for those who want a snapshot with him, bring your cameras along to take a picture with him from 530-630pm. Children will receive delicious Raffles Hotel Christmas goodies. And it's all FREE! My cousin said the cookies are really delicious and they're giving them away free to kids.

    Also, a spectacular miniature train set ordered all the way from Germany is displayed and will keep children enthralled as it chugs along a festive scenic display.

    One more fun thing... a skilful balloon sculptor will be delighting all with his creative balloon sculptures of cartoon characters (my fav is Nemo!), animals and almost anything under the sun! Be there at 2-3pm to catch the BalloonMan in action!

    An extra for people who want to listen to the voices of the VJC choir:
    Christmas Carolling from 21st to 25th Dec in the evenings.

    The reason why I'm sending this info to all is because my cousin designed the entire promotion package and specially ordered all these special treats just for children. The traffic at other parts of town is usually very high and crowded like anything, so if you want to bring your children, nephews, nieces, cousins, etc to somewhere less crowded for an enjoyable festive time, try Santa's Grove at Raffles Hotel. Hope you like it!
