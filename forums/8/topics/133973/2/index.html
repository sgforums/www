    You see, many of these gym recruitment staff survive on sign-ups and renewals. Their basic is very low and a majority of their income comes from commissions. And I believe this is how it works.....(an example only):

    Basic S$800 per month (or something stupidly low).
    Target: S$40,000 worth of membership sales per month.

    If they hit S$39,999 worth of membership sales, they get zero commissions, so they have to survive with only S$800.
