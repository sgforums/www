  • Meat Bao
    23 Jul 09, 19:59

    Sentosa - especially catch the fountain show

    National Museum - if you have time, can add Asian Civilization Museum

    Zoo - the daytime zoo, it has more shows

    Esplanade - schedule your arrival to watch a few shows

    Big Wheel - enter it

    Singapore river/ clarke quay boat tour - nighttime is better, very scenic

    Orchard rd - sightseeing

    Discovery centre and science centre - always good for all ages, catch the 3D show

    Food centres - such as food courts inside malls, choose a place with wide selection and variety of local food. Tourists especially like this.

    Beaches/ Parks - sightseeing, sunset-watching, roller-blading, or cycling, strolling/ chit-chatting

    Downtown East - go to the themepark, the land one or the water one

    Kallang Entertainment Centre - ice skating, also have bowling and other things.

    Libraries - good collection of books

    Thats all from me, enjoy.

    * I add one more: underwater world.
