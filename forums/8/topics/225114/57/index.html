    "wonder what those passages are for hor ?" so she turned and looked at one of the passageways and ... kept quiet.

    i took her silence to be of typical feminine fear of the dark and unknown, so i shrugged it off and just strolled with her further to sundial plaza. which most people should know, is pretty close to the expressway and there's a small dirt path that leads to an awesome view of the CBD.

    * * * * *
    ok, that place was last seen to be the MAIN haunt of people during the fireworks displays at the esplanade area..
    * * * * *

    so we went there to look at the CBD view, took a few pictures and left. on the way back, past the sundial plaza, she gripped my hand.

    "let's go."

    i was about to open up my mouth and ask her why when i realized it...
