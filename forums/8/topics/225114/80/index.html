I finally forced myself to go to bed telling myself that I should sleep already since I have to be awake quite early the next day. So I off the lights and closed my eyes but I could not sleep. So I closed my eyes and thought of something to make me feel sleepy such that I could sleep. After some time, i felt myself getting sleepier and sleepier, so I tried to relax and hoped my head would get me off to sleep. But instead of going off to sleep, I had this jerks of half asleep half awake. Just like when you are about to sleep you suddenly wake up and when you suddenly wake up, you feel like sleeping again sort of thing. So I decided that its getting irritating, I might as well wake up and use the washroom before I go back to sleep.

    But I found out that I could not move at all. I couldnt even to open my eyes. I found myself not breathing. I was awake at that point of time due to shock but I couldnt move. And I was quite relieved when I managed to force myself to start breathing again. My body was easier to control as I forced my eyes open.

I saw white shadows of glowing balls of white mist (like the pictures you took but more clear) which (to my opinion) if it were to materialise will look like small human/witch-like characters but they look horrible. (Its not cute or funny ok?) Those things were floating in alignment to my furniture very close to me (Like they were really travelling on my furniture) and they were travelling in from the window and surrounding me. I was like struggling from my confinement in this kind of situation. Suddenly, my parents open the door and came in quarrelling which aroused me out of my predicament. I suddenly able to move and they were really shocked when I just jumped out of my bed. This is not the only time I experienced this kind of thing...
    I have alot of ghost laying (some say it's physiological, you mind is awake but your nerves haven't turn on yet) on me experiences during young, almost everyday since sec.3 to sec.4, that why I don't sleep during the night, but after I moved house, all these occurences stopped, it only happened again during one hungry ghost festival.
