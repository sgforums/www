    Sharing my REAL encounter in a hotel room... (no pepper & salt)

    I've heard many ghost stories about Genting Highland, rumors about suicides & murders there. As a 'mature' adult, these stories doesn't affect me when I went there few years ago. Frankly I'm quite skeptical about most ghost stories.

    However, I will always recall one incident whenever I read about Genting Highland's haunted hotel stories. On the 2nd night, as usual after casino games, my friend & I return to the hotel room, the type with two single beds and a small bathroom. I was sleeping on the one closer to the window.
    Around 3am to 4am I was suddenly woken by a strong feeling of fear, in a semi conscious/ semi dream state I was not able to move my body, within seconds I started 'hearing' echoed chants or mumbling of some kind of language. I managed to open my eyes and 'forced' myself awake (fully conscious)... my body temperature was a mixture of warm & cold... a weird sensation I never had b4. The most freaky part was when I sense something(s) moving fast around the quiet room, especially near the window area (window was locked). The 'movement' of the wind was like someone running pass again & again in different directions... this last nearly for spooky 10 mins.

    I've been to Genting many times & that was my first or 'worse' encounter. I travel quite offen & I know my health. I am sure what I encountered
    was not something scientific or psychology related.
