    rem one....
    went to NPCC camp long time ago,at Ubin, with a few schools. so around 40 of us.

    Near to campsite, there was an abandoned toilet. and it was just near to my tent.

    the night before we left, I being a fast sleeper, was knocked out.
    Suddenly, at around 2am, I woke up, and saw my schools' people all crowded near the tent and toilet.

    As I just woke, I was abit cong cong. One of my good friend came over and shook me quite violently and asked me whether I am okie. I said "yes lah, and ask what's happening?".

    They told me, some guards patrolling ( we got some people patrolling the campsite), heard crying inside the toilet.

    I was saying, "what liao, just 5m from where I am sleeping leh".

    Then one friend of us, W, try to go inside and see.
    He walked half-way, and then stopped.

    He told us that while he walking in, he felt a gush of wind coming out, and stopped him.

    Normally, in a tent, can sleep 5, but with this, all from my school decided not to sleep and stay together since our tents are all near the toilet.

    At day-break, my friend, W, went in. And he said he saw writings on the wall..

    Wow liao.. no wonder when we want to do our business, the CIs asked us to go to jetty, some 20m to do.

    For me, luckily, I slept through what happened.
