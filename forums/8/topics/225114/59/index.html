    POSSESSIVE GIRLFRIEND

    This story should not be read at night. Especially for those who have gone Changi for those SAF stand-alone single-storey bungalow chalet before. The particular chalet in mention is not those along the coast.

    * * * * *

    A group of my friends and me went to the chalet for a good 3 day, 2 night stay during our polytechnic vacation. There were about 4 girls and 5 guys staying, with a few other friends coming during the day to BBQ and play games.

    Nothing much happened during our first day. We had our BBQ with our friends and just basically chilled out. At least until the guys decided to disturb some girls who were washing the utensils in the toilet sink. So we decided to use a few black T-shirts to throw past the toilet window to act as a passing shadow.

    The girls did scream when they saw it, but they figured out it was as and came out screaming at us with skewers in hand. (Point to note: It is ALWAYS dangerous to disturb women with weapons ) So after our little escapade, we just concentrated on the BBQ food.

    Then, one of the girls came out of the toilet and slapped me on the back of my head.

    "OI ! Dun disturb la ! Scary lei !"

    I looked at her and told her I was busy eating, no time to disturb her. She didn't believe and proceeded to form a defensive wall with the other girls to scold me. Of course, my ex gf defended me and we just put the incident to rest as over active imagination.

    At least until my ex's parents came and join us for the BBQ. They were the ones who booked the chalet for us. We were all busy enjoying ourselves and played games until those who couldn't stay overnight left. My ex's parents then pulled her aside for awhile before they went back.

    All was quiet until the time came for us to shower. The girls decide to bathe first because they were scared. So they took turns, with the rest of them guarding outside just to make sure we don't disturb them. This time, we really didn't do anything. And the only thing happening was... The tap couldn't be closed tightly. It just kept on dripping.

    My ex became more and more uncomfortable as the night wore on while we continued playing games. Me being the idiot, I didn't notice. Then when the guys decided to rest for the night in the master bedroom, the first few scary incidents happened...

    The tap in the main toilet of the chalet started to drip. Non-stop. Even after we closed the tap tightly. Drip.. drip.. drip.. The guys didn't bother. But the girls were scared stiff everytime they went toilet. So they decided to use the toilet in the master bedroom.

    Then the tap in the main toilet burst open by itself. We could hear the gushing of the water from the master bedroom which was around 10m away. So one of the guys went to close it tight. The tap suddenly stopped when he reached for the doorknob. Shrugging it off, he came back to the bedroom where we were just talking nonsense.

    And it gushed again. So the 4 of us guys (including me) decided to go this time. We ran for it this time, but again the gushing stopped just before we reached it. As we looked at each other, the gushing started again.

    Accompanied with screams..

    And this time it was coming from the master bedroom, with only 4 girls and a guy left inside.. Or rather, they were supposed to be inside, until they started running out..
