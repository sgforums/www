    Anyway, the hospital where my great grandmother (Ggrandmother) was staying in, have a lot of dogs around the building. That night when my grandmother woke up, a lot of dogs started howling into the night, it sounded as though they were frightened or crying.

    A few days later, my Ggrandmother called my ahma to her bedside and told her. She said that during the days when she was unconscious, she saw her husband (passed away already). She was walking in a kind of hole or passage way where there is a light at the end. On the way, her husband came up to her and called her. He ask her to go back to where she belong and that her time is not up yet. He than led her to another passage where there is no light and is darker and scarier. He than let go of her and said that he cant continue or he will cross the border. My Ggrandfather than told her to keep walking and not to look back.

    On the way she walk back, she heard a lot of howlings and screamings. She was very frightened but determined to get back. Finally, after walking for a long time, she reached the back door of the hospital which she recognised. Over there, she saw the dogs gritting their teeths at her and barking at her very loudly making her tremble.

    Finally, she saw the nurses and doctors in her ward, soon, she woke up and, thereafter, my ahma was told of this story,

    But exactly 1 year later, on the same date she woke, my Ggrandmother passed away.
