    ok....it happened in my secondary school...I was from CCHS(M) before it was expanded and redeveloped to what it is today.

    During my time, the old buildings were all 2-storey max, except for the Technical Block and the Auditorium.

    There was a pond, bigger than what it is today.

    The campus itself was rumoured to be a Kempeitai's station and where they stored the corpses before they disposed of it.

    This is the place where it all happened....

    The general workers of the school will always finished their work, locked the gate and went home before dark fall, especially one malay uncle, who would tell us spooky tales that took place.

    The gate at the auditorium will always be locked. But pple will always claimed to have seen a lady in white inside the auditorium. The main hall of the auditorium is a hall that has got a big stage, seats (downstair and upstair), much like the theater, where u would ahve circus seats.

    And behind the stage, there would be numerous changing rooms, which have been converted to storeroom and locked away for a very very long time. IF u are in the luck, u will be able to open oen of them..and if u are daring enough, u are always "invited" to explore the room at ur own risk.

    it happened during a camp...

    it was during the march holiday...which seemed weird cos camps were normal held in june holiday.

    i was one among the senior who went back to help out in the camp.

    something din quite feel right that time, but no one could tell.

    we had treasure hunt on the very first day, late afternoon...but it stretched on to evening...8 plus...and of a sudden...we heard a loud scream...and a few boys were running from the auditorium...

    they were from one team who went in search of the treasure..and went cross the boundary we have set for every camper...NEVER CROSS OR GO NEAR THE AUDITORIUM AFTER DARK...that rule has been observed for a very very long time...but a few boys broke it

    they ran back and told us that one guy fainted infront of the gate to auditorium...

    the camp organisers and a few of us ran immediately and got the fainted chap back to one of the classrooms.

    they were a group of 3...one cheeky guy, a talkative guy and a fat guy...the cheeky guy went pale...the talkative guy was not talkative anymore during that camp...and the fat guy, who fainted, kept saying " we din go there in purpose...she stared at me....i dun want to die...her face got no feature.."

    we were speechless....

    that night, instead of getting the students to go sentry...we senior did instead...it was a full moon night......the moon was usually round and the coloor was dirty yellow.......

    we were sitting by the pond side, watching over the sleepy campers...and suddenly...i saw a light...coming from inside the auditorium....

    tis like a candle light...shining from inside...moving from one place to another...

    we were shocked...but none of us dared to go over and investigate...

    4am...we took turn to sleep...and my replacement had come to relief me of my duty...i went to a classroom and laid on the table...determined not to fall asleep...so i could react to any emergency immediately...

    lying down on the table, arranged in a long shape...watching the ceiling fan...i slowly dozed off....and was deeply asleep..without switching off the fan and the lights...

    suddenly i was sweating..i woke up, looked at my watch, 5am..damned..they switched off the fans and the light..hmmm...TG was sleeping over at the row of table perpendicular to where i sleep, maybe it was too sleep..i only saw shadow...a dark silhouette..i dozed off into sleep again

    i was woken up by the camper's shouting...they were doing PT...i looked around..TG has gone..oh well...i went to the toilet and wash up...

    after washing up, i went to look for the other guys, and found them all sleeping in the st john room, on beds....tamade...good brothers they called themselves...got bed...never bother to wake me up...

    i was furious....
