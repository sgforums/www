    This incident is not a considered a ghost sighting, but it may be considered as either coincidence or supernatural. It happened during my grandma's funeral.

    1st incident (I was still in army camp, recounted from my mum):
    As usual, the rites were being done. As everyone was kneeling down while the priest chants, a black butterfly was seen fluttering/flying around and over everyone's head.

    2nd incident (I was there):
    The rites were being done for the last time; the next morning is to move the coffin to Lim Chu Kang for burial. This time, as everyone was kneeling down, a white butterfly appeared. The butterfly suddenly flew around everyone's head as the priest is chanting, and continue flying until the chanting ends. Right after that, the butterfly flew out of the HDB void deck and up, in the direction towards the unit where my grandma once stayed.
