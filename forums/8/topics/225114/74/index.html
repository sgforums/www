    I just had an experience.

    Me and a group of friends were exploring an old tunnel complex (secret place). We reached around 11pm. Was completely dark, we had to use torchlight, bash into the forest. So around 11:30pm we reached the tunnels, and we went into the first room, then some guy commented he felt 'very dangerous' and we turned back to explore the area. When we were exiting, a friend said he heard something from one of the other rooms. While going into the forest, someone remarked he smelled a fragrance, after a while I smelled it too. A very faint fragrance, on and off.

    After a few minutes we went back into the tunnel, and went down to explore with bright torches. At first was nothing, then we went back to the first room to rest. I heard something from outside the room, like someone talking a few words, or might just be an animal.

    We made a second trip down, and did something crazy. We split into groups of 2 people, each group at a part of the tunnel. 2 people were a few metres apart. Then we switched off all lights and sat in the darkness for 10 minutes. Then there was this "BONG" sound from the end of the tunnel. Sounded metallic, without an echo or resonating sound. This really startled me. Then the "bongs" became more frequent and louder. Finally the 10 minutes were up and we left. As I was climbing out, I heard a knocking noise from the same end of the tunnel, this time a different sound.

    Then I ventured in for the third time into the tunnel and repeated the experiment, this time I sat near the end of the tunnel. Lights off again. After about 3 minutes, the "bong" noises sounded again, this time much louder, and louder as each sound came, and as frequent, as fast as 12 seconds per sound. Then I felt uneasy, a little freaky and signaled to my friend to move, we moved about a metre away from the end, and turned our backs to the end of the tunnel.

    It was at this position that I heard soft footsteps behind me, someone stepping on the wet sand (there was sand inside the tunnel). And I felt as if someone was walking behind me, my back felt a tingling sensation. I was only about 50cm from the wall. It was only around 3 minutes later the next "bong" came, this time much softer. I also heard very faint music at a point in time.

    Finally 10 minutes was over and we went back to the first room and discussed our stories. My friend who was in front of me said he hear deep breathing behind him (in direction of the tunnel end). Another friend down the tunnel (near the entrance) said he felt as if someone walked pass him. About the music, because we had a torch with radio, but I don't know if the torch was inside the tunnel at the time.
