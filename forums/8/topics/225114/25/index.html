    After they reclaimed the sea, this part of the beach was turned into a giant lagoon. It's now closed to public because there have been apparently alot of strange drownings near the shore

    Apparently they victims were near the beach and they all drowned because they got sucked in by a whirlpool (accordingly to some people who claim to be eye witnesses)

    The thing is that the water isn't very deep. Maybe about 1.5metres.

    The people from the village (when this happened the gov had already relocated them into nice houses near the beach, but the culture is still very kampung) said that it's caused by water spirits because they are angry that there are no more fishermen going out to see to catch fish and giving them offerings at the end of the day.

    No one knows for sure but that stretch of beach is now closed to public and that was like many years ago.
