    Man Choose Wife

    A man is in love with three lovely women but can't choose between them. So he decides to conduct a test to see which one should be his wife.

    He gives each of them $5,000 and tells them to do whatever they want with the money and then come back and report on what they did.

    The first woman takes her $5,000 and spends it on perfumes, trips to a spa and hairdresser, and lots of new clothes and shoes. She tells him she did this because she loves him so much and wants to look the best she can for him because that makes him happy.

    The second woman takes her $5,000 and spends it on golf clubs, tennis rackets, a stereo system and lots of other "boy toys" and gives it all to him. She tells him this is because she loves him so much and wants to do whatever it is that will make him happy.

    The third woman takes her $5,000 and buys mutual funds, stocks, and bonds with it. She more than doubles the basic fund and gives the original $5,000 investment back to him. She tells him that this is because she loves him so much and wants to him to know that knowing she is financially independent will make him happy.

    The man thinks over the action of each woman and is impressed by how each one expresses love. It is a hard decision to make but he'll only ask one to marry him. So after careful thought he decides-----
