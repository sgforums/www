    For starters, I'm not a person that watches serials like a cult. Further, my chinese stinks so bad it would put a bucket of prawns in the hot sun to shame.

    But one day i went home and my housemates were watching the above title and i sat down and watched it for a while. The first thing that caught my attention was that the girl resembled my first gf! *gosh* oh well.. anyway they were on disc 15 so i took the first 10 and started watching. That's one way to improve my chinese, I figured.

    I thought I might start a thread and give my comments as i watch thru the show. So far i'm halfway thru disc 3.

    Not many comments so far, except that in those days, i realised they didn't have mobile phones. they would plan to meet up at a certain date and they would be there for their "date". I contrasted that to today's society where we are not only able to update each other instantly on our whereabouts and plan activities, but that we almost "expect" our partners to "report" our whereabouts and activities should we not be with them. I found it very heartening and romantic that they would plan to be at a place when the snow first falls and both parties were true to their promise.

    I guess the problem is that society now isn't as simple as it was then. we can't afford to plan to meet when the snow first falls (there's no damn snow in singapore anyway!) becos of our hectic schedules here in sg. But wouldn't it be nice to make such sweet covenants with our loved ones and add that bit of spice, take time and effort at our inconvenience to commit certain things to each other? I believe it can still be done in our society, AND without snow.

    That's it for now. I will try my best not to include any spoilers to those that haven't but want to watch it. And may i request that no one else does ok? Thanxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
