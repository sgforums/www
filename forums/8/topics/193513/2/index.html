    As with previous upgrades in bandwidth, this upgrade will come at no additional charges.     Said Mr Thomas Ee, Senior Vice President of IP Services, StarHub: "As always, StarHub will continue to seek ways to proactively add value on our products and services, so as to serve our customers better. For example, we provide free Digital Voice subscription to all post-paid MaxOnline subscribers, allowing them to save at least S$10 on their residential fixed-line bill monthly. Similarly, we believe many MaxOnline users will be delighted with this upgrade. With this and more, we give customers more reasons to choose StarHub as their preferred service provider."

    To benefit from StarHub's upcoming value offering, the MaxOnline customer simply need to reset his cable modem by turning the electrical power source off and on again from the effective date to enjoy the enhanced online experience instantly.
