    To all those guys be it fit or scrawny, Poly or JC, the time has finally come for u people to step into the life of National Service. How u may dread the day but alas...the truth is that each guy born as a Singaporean is a liability of 2 or 2 and a half years to the government. I'll always remember the day i was enlisted, incidentally it was during France 98, the morning when England was playing Romania and i just wanted to see the last live soccer match before i "went to jail". I could still feel the sadness i felt when i bade farewell to my family and especially my girlfriend....it was heartwrenching. But life had to go on, time flies when u're in there and the first three months is always the worst, when u were treated like a dog....the amount of welfare u get depends on your performance especially smoking breaks. But after BMT life is alot more easier, well actually it depends on where u r posted to. I underwent BMT in a mono intake unit and i was one of those "Lucky" few to stay and rest got posted out as either drivers or storemen. So i toiled and went throughed the motion, the weekends being the foremost thing i look forward to everyday and time passed by quickly....1st year and then 2nd year, so here i am now, just had my ORD function few weeks back and looking ever so forward to my ORD date. My advice to all u enlistees is to just hang in there and always look forward to the weekends so as to pass time quickly. Always do things right and samrt way. In the Army u can do anything u like.... but dun get caught. Dun chao keng too much or u wont find any True buddies. Lastly....always powder your privates or u can start growing Cauliflowers. ORD LOH!!!!!!!! June 22 2000
