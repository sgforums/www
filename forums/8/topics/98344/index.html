    Angus Steak house (I think it is the name but angus anyways ) on the top level of takashimaya department store. (go through the escalators at the department store - it's above the sports wear dept) Lovely candlelite dinners. The whole restuarant lights are dimmed to a romantic level and occasionally they have someone playing piano . Go for the window seats - at night the lights and people outside quite nice to look at.

    I used to go there for lunch and once for dinner when I was in singapore and I recommended it to my parents. My parents use it for anniversary and birthdays now.

    Abit pricey for the dinner though but worth it. Comes with appetizer, soup, salad , main course , dessert. Choose the beef - it is good for beef. I think set dinner about 40 dollars per person. They serve one at a time - not like jack's place which piles everything straight on the table. Probably set you back about 80 dollars but I can tell you it is way worth it
