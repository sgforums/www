  • morningglory
    26 Nov 06, 13:14
    depending on your makeup (light or thick?), you can try cleansing oil(eg Fancl) which removes everything including waterproof mascaras well. but it may be drying for the skin because well, it removes everything on your skin including the good oils produced by the skin. i heard that cleansing oil can cause breakouts for acne-prone skin for some people.

    cleansing milk is more gentle for the skin but and good for light makeup. if you have thick makeup, you may have to use more to clean off the makeup. it doesnt remove waterproof mascaras well so you have to use a separate eye makeup remover.

    hope this helps.
