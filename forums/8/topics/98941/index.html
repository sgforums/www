  • Qitai
    21 Oct 04, 11:25
    Prefer the North-East and East Coast too (i.e. Hua Lian, Ta Lu Ke). More natural views there. Recommend if you like good views and natural landscapes.

    If restricted to Taipei, then night market loh. You can see night market almost everywhere there. If interested, you can go 101 building too. If want to just move out a bit, go Tan Shui - can take MRT there, so this one is easy. Can see street market there and cross the river to eat a bit of seafood as well as walk along the river. Quite nicely done up now.

    For Hot Spring, you can go Yang Ming Shan. Got volcanic activity there too. Can go see Xiao You Keng.
