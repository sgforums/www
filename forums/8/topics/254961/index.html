    What are your favourite movies and why? Here're mine:

    Pulp Fiction - Excellent dialogue; Tarantino at his best
    Memento - One of those movies where you're like wtf is going on the whole time until the very end when everything makes sense and huge plot twist. Masterfully done
    Kill Bill vol. 1 - Excellent cinematography and stylised fight scenes. Especially love the anime bit. Another masterpiece by Tarantino
    Reservoir Dogs - A Tarantino movie. need I say more?
    Harold and Kumar Go to White Castle - Probably one of the funniest movies I've ever seen.
    The Godfather Trilogy - Excellent storyline and screenplay.
    Tenacious D in the Pick of Destiny - One of the funniest movies I've seen combined with my love of rock music makes this another of my favourites. Plus it stars one of my favourite bands too: TENACIOUS D. And of course the cameo appearance by Ronnie James Dio. I mean any movie with RJD has to be good.
    The Departed - Great plot, twists. A classic Scorcese masterpiece
    Goodfellas - Another great classic by Scorcese
    Shaft - Classic blaxploitation film
    Superfly - Same as above
    The Nightmare Before Christmas - One f*ckin trippy movie. Try watching this when high, it's the best.
    Jackass 1 and 2 - Real crazy stunts done by a bunch of idiots = comic genius.
