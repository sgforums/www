    I guess when you look at the sun rise over a windy, chilly morning and you realise that millions do not share the same privilege as you do, then that's peace and contentment in your heart.

    The silence of a few minutes as you stand at a busy junction in town, as all the cars move off, and there is nothing left, that is peace too.

    The simple pleasure of taking a bus home from work, all your fellow riders are asleep or reading, that's peace too.

    A moment's silence when you make a lame joke and the guys just freeze, before bursting out in laughter, that's embarassing peace.

    The night scene along Tras Street, the votive candles on the bartops, the slow chatter of people, and the hum of jazz and funk, that's hip peace.

    Standing on a beach in OZ's Sunshine Coast, watching the sunset over the vast Pacific, knowing that another human standing thousands of km away in San Diego or Santiago, is gazing at the same body of water, that's peace and serendipity.

    I love peace.Do you?
