    Originally posted by SBS9845X:
    I am 15 this year and in another 3 years time i will be enlisted in the army. Can anyone please tell me the entire procedure with full details from the day of registration in CMPB to the finishing of NS? Also please tell me in what instances people are exempted from NS? I also want to know how can i do admin work in the army instead of artillery. Thanks a million.
    Fine... I assume that you will be a poly student and a PES B enlistee without getting downgraded/OOC in the future...

    When you are 17.5 years old you will be told to register for NS and you will register NS in that period but don't forget to send your deferment letter from the poly to CMPB. Then in the next 2 years plus SAF will not be 'harnessing' you except that they will send lots of brouchures to you asking you want to sign up.

    Then in the final year of your Poly (i.e 6-9 months before enlisting) you will be told to go CMPB to do medical checkup but the checkup is useless one as CMPB's MO are horrible.

    Also you will required to do NAPFA test in your campus or TPY stadium after the check ups. Details will be reveled by your poly.

    Then 1-2 months before enlistment you will receive the enlistment letter.

    NS starts with BMT... You will take 10 or 16 weeks of BMT (depends on whether you pass your NAPFA or not). After BMT, you will post to the vocation you are suitable with. Be it services (clerk, storeman, mechanics), combat (MP, commandos, guardsmen) or commanders (SISPEC).

    After 2 years of NSF you ORD but NS doesn't stop here. You will be required to take 13 cycles of in camp training until you are 40 years old. Also mobilisation and IPPT tests are also required after you ORD.

    Peoples who exempts from NS are: Physically/Mentally disabled and people with very sick illness like AIDS, Cancers etc.

    For admin work, most are for those PES C and E people where there have medical problems that prevent them from chiong suah but there are also PES A and B people doing clerks too. All depends on your fitness, educational level, aptitute, working experience etc etc.
