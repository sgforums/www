    wait a minute... now that i read again, do you mean you have to write about the plant??
    If that is the case, outline looks something like this:

    1) Intro - definition
    - e.g. scientific name of cotton plant

    - where does it normally grow or where is it found

    2) Body
    - when was it discovered and by whom
    - cotton plant growth cycle
    - description of its appearance and various parts
    - function of each part
    - current issues e.g. growth and production of cotton. Type of technology used. Any scarcity issues as countries get industrialised?

    3) Conclusion
    - future research on cotton (what else can it be used for? current research being done in terms of agriculture sciences etc.)
    - future production of cotton - is it a dying trade?
    (may want to add own point of view on the industry.)
