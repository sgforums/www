    If you guys are looking for any fun outdoor adventure, at the same time want to make new friends, do check out this 14th Basic Outdoor Adventure Training Course (B.O.A.T). Just to share more info abt the club which I belong :

    At Toa Payoh Adventure Club, we organize outdoor sports activities. In the last 20 years, more than 400 participants have gone through this program, run by a volunteer committee of experienced outdoor enthusiasts. B.O.A.T course runs every two years.

     

    Over a period of 3 months, you will get hands on experience in activities such as hiking, kayaking, abseiling, map-reading, campcraft and outdoor cooking as well as a local camp and 2 overseas hiking expeditions in Malaysia.

    We welcome beginners or novices to join us! Our age range is quite wide... youngest is 15.. oldest is a retiree at 50.

    There will be a course preview and slideshow presentation on the 06th February 2009 (Friday). You may also like to take the opportunity to have all your queries and concerns addressed during this session or sign up for the course.
