    Most people who claim that NUS and NTU are 'lousy' gave reasons like poor administrative support, lecturer etc....But actually these are actually just the tip of the iceberg. What makes a university a world class university is mainly its students. In singapore, everybody wants to get into the university regardless of whether they like studying because they have this thinking that only with a proper paper qualification will they have a 'future'. Thus the uni here is filled with people who study for the sake of making a better living. This is'nt extremely difficult...just plain hard work without passion is enough to see a person through uni. Some even manage to get honours.

    In other countries, people who enter uni usually have passion in the subjects they are undertaking. As such, they produces world class scientist and engineers. This is something singapore is incapable of producing with the current education system where academic qualification is the only path towards success. That's why there's this influx of 'foreign talent'......
