    can always do that yourself..

    i have done it..

    what you will need is

    ice cream of ur fav favour..
    popian skin / spring roll skin
    flour
    egg

    use 2pc of spring roll skin,
    wrap the ice cream in it..
    egg white to seal the skin

    egg and batter to coat the skin and roll breadcrumbs.
    repeat afew times..

    Put the finish wrap in the freezer

    heat up the oil to tip top hot.. untill the the smoke get in ur eyes..
    and remove the wrap frm the freezer and fry.. asap..

    Made sure ur move is fast enough as nt to melt the ice cream..
