    Hi,

    Many anti-virus and firewall programmes nowadays come in packages (they usually call them 'suites') which include anti-spyware progs. Of course, these are not free.

    If you want it for free, the choices are limited. A good choice would be 'Ad-aware SE' by a company called Lavasoft (www.lavasoft.com).

    From what I understand (and what I've seen), there is no fool-proof anti-spyware/anti-adware prog. Spies are always building new ways to get around defences. Defences are always extending themselves to prevent spies. Its a challenge of skills and timing between the two. And they all put us in fear and make us spend a lot of money.....hey, maybe the creators of spyware and anti-spyware, viruses and anti-viruses are the same people!!

    And there are custom-made spywares by professional hackers (some people say sometimes from professional corporations!! eg. ISPs, authorities, etc.) which cannot be identified/stopped too. These are the most serious cases where the hacker specifically targets your PC (as opposed to a general commercial-type spyware just spread over the net).

    Using a good firewall will limit unauthorised information going in and out of your PC. Its a good first line of defense.

    Gentlerock

    What the PC geeks recommend..... use both AdAware SE and SpyBot Search & Destroy. These are the two most trustworthy anti-spyware programs. Best of all, they're free.

    You can download both at www.download.com if I'm not wrong.

    Run SpyBot first before running AdAware.... Spybot can wipe out spyware, whereas AdAware can only quarantine them. AdAware's scanning function is more thorough though.... that's what I experienced. Conduct full scans once a week.... you'll always be able to sniff out some tracking cookies, that's for sure.

    At all costs, DO NOT use those tools advertised on pop-up ads..... Think about it. If it's anti-spyware and anti-adware, why would they use adware to advertise?
