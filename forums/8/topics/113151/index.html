    Do I regret my poor Mandarin skills? Sure, I do. I'm desparately trying to catch up with my Mandarin now for three reasons:

    - So that I can understand when people are being cynical/sarcastic with me.
    - So that I can handle my major markets better (China & Taiwan).
    - So that I can more confidently call myself, well, a Chinese. After all, to understand a culture, its often necessary to speak its language first. And vice versa.
