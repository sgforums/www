    TS,

    i think so long the doctor/hospital you go is reputable, i don't see any wrong going.

    But please consider the following issues...

    1. if there happen to be malpractice, do you think you can pursue justice in a foreign country?

    2. even if you want and can, do you want to go thru the trouble?

    3. you might not encounter malpractice, but you may encounter a practice that is perfectly legal in another country just that you are not used to it. you will also feel "cheated".

    4. you also need to consider, should anything happen to you in future, do you rather go back to that country where your records are. or you go to a local doctor that don't have your history? In asia, medical reports are not that easy to transfer.
