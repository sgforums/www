    http://www.safra.sg/cohesion.aspx?sectionid=14
    Cohesion Building Programme
    The SAFRA Cohesion Building Programme (CBP) was developed in 1985 to help foster esprit de corps among NSmen within their own units, especially at the platoon level.

    CBP provides a subsidy scheme that helps to defray the cost of organising cohesion activities for unit's NSmen as well as a waiver of rental fees for SAFRA-owned facilities at its five clubs.

    The amount of subsidy granted is based on the participating number of NSmen (per head basis) and the type of cohesion activity.

    Application for subsidy can be made via a simple application form and this can be submitted to SAFRA (CBP Administration Office), 2 Telok Blangah Way, Singapore 098803 via fax at 6377 9140. For enquiries, please contact us at 6377 9141.
