    JAE Mass Appeal Exercise(NGEE ANN POLY)

    03 March 06

    The appeal exercise for JAE applicants will be held on 6 - 7 March from 9am - 6pm at Ngee Ann Polytechnic, Blk 1, Atrium. Appellants must be eligible for the course that they would like to appeal for.

    JAE Appeals Exercise (SINGAPORE POLY)
    This Appeals Exercise is for applicants who had applied under the JAE and now wish to appeal for a change in course or were not successful in gaining a place in an institution (not posted to any course).
    Date: 06 March - 07 March (Monday and Tuesday)
    Venue: Concourse (T1A)
    Time: 8.30 am to 4.30pm

    JAE applicants who wish to make an appeal should go to the institution that they are appealing to, to submit their Appeal Form.

    The outcome of the appeal will available at the end of March 2006

    JAE Appeals Exercise (TEMASEK POLY)
    Location
    Students Services & Admissions
    Admin Blk 9, Level 2, Lobby C
    Temasek Polytechnic
    21 Tampines Avenue1
    Singapore 529757

    When to Submit an Appeal

    6 & 7 Mar 2006 (8.30 am to 5.00 pm)

    What to bring along

    Original and photocopy of Form A or GCE 'O' Level results & CCA grade.

    Appeal Outcome

    The expected outcome of appeal will be released tentatively 3 weeks from the date of submission.

    Successful candidates will receive an enrolment package or notification by post, latest by mid April 2006. Other candidates will be notified by email, if an email address is provided.
