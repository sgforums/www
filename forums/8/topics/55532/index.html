    A Tale of Two Sisters (Korean)

    Two sisters, Su-mi and Su-yeon, return home after recovering an illness, but Su-mi, the elder sister, intentionally avoids her stepmother, Eun-joo, while Su-yeon, the younger sister, is fearful of her stepmother.

    On their first night, Su-yeon sneaks in Su-mi's room saying there is someone in her room and Su-mi sees a ghost of her late mother who hung herself in Su-yeon's closet. Strange things begin to happen from then on: a ghost haunts the house and stepmother's birds are poisoned to death. Assuming Su-yeon is behind the inauspicious incidents, Eun-joo locks her in the closet, and when Su-mi learns about the cruel punishment, the conflict between Eun-joo and the sisters get more bitter than ever.

    One day, the sisters' father, Mu-hyun, leaves for town and Su-mi finds there is no sign of Su-yeon. Su-mi sports Eun-joo dragging a big bag through the dark corridor, leaving bloody stains behind her. Su-mi follows the trail of blood and confronts Eun-joo. They start fighting vehemently and Su-mi falls down after being struck by Eun-joo. A while later, Mu-hyun returns and finds Su-mi lying on the floor and exclaims that Su-yeon is already dead.....
