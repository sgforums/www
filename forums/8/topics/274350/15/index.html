    Thinking about it properly, What are Odex's products that they want to claim damages from?

    The anime? No. If you look porperly, Odex is a SUB-LICENSE owner of the anime. THat means, Odex have no rights on the anime itself. What Odex has rights over is the stuff they sell. The CDs, the box cover and their crappy subtitles.

    So what did any of the "illegal downloaders" steal from Odex to create damages? People download fansubs which consists of the anime and subtitles buy the fansubs. Did the downloaders copy the Odex's box cover? Did the downloaders copy Odex's CD design? Did the downloaders copy Odex's crappy subtitles?

    If the answers for the 3 above question is "NO", then Odex have no rights to sue people in the 1st place.

    And since Odex has no rights to sue the downloaders in the 1st place, sending any threats for money sounds something like something from the Penal code.

    Extortion (Penal Code, Cap 224, Sec 383)

    Illustrations
