    every segment has its own rehearsals. for e.g. parade segment combined started at nee soon camp parade square for 3 weekends before going to kallang. first segment, the parade+flypast+mobile column is handled by RSAF HQ and ADSD.

    for show segment, other associations may start as early as march with costume-testing, chereography.

    of cos, the EXCO for next year is already up. next yr will be spearheaded by engineering formation for a change. it'll be at marina south.

    for this year, combined rehearsals started 3rd Jun. therefore:

    3rd Jun (CR1)
    10th Jun (CR2)
    17th Jun (CR3)
    24th Jun (CR4)
    2nd Jul (CR5)
    8th Jul (CR6 - Family Day)
    15th Jul (NE)
    22nd Jul (CR7)
    29th Jul (PRE)
    5th Aug (CR8)
    9th Aug (NDP)

    total is 10 weekends and 1 public holiday burnt at National Stadium, not counting SAF Day on 1st Jul.
