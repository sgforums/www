     Mudhalvan (1999)

    Mudhalvan (1999) is a Tamil political film directed by S.Shankar, later re-made in Hindi as Nayak: The Real Hero. The film stars Arjun, Manisha Koirala and Sushmita Sen. The film's score and soundtrack are composed by AR. Rahman. . The lead actor of Mudhalvan, Arjun has appeared a second time for Shankar in this movie after their debut vehicle Gentleman in 1993 was a runaway success. The story of an ordinary reporter who happens to interview a state minister one fine day agrees to a challenge to act as the Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu , and makes such a success of it that soon he is embroiled in political intriguey. The hit song Shakalakka Baby by A.R.Rahman went on to gain international acclaim appearing in Ander Loyd Webber's Bombay Dreams and kelly Poon's Soundtracks. Former Miss Universe Sushmita Sen also makes a cameo appearance for the song. This film was a tremendous success at the box office and continued Shankar's dream run. It won the Filmfare Best Tamil Film Award in the year of its release
