    www.redbullartofcan.sg

    OVERVIEW

    The Red Bull Art of Can is a nationwide hunt for creativity and is open to everyone, from fulltime artists to simply one with a creative flair. Built, sculpt, weld, glue, hammer, bend, fold, print, tape and paint, whatever!

    Be bold! Send a message! Make a statement. Your primary material must be a blue and silver Red Bull can, but you will need to add plenty of imagination and creativity. Make a sculpture, a picture, a 3D model, a mobile, a video or a piece of modern art, but make it beautiful, colourful, clever, amusing or outrageous.

    The five-month long campaign will culminate in a premium art exhibition from 2-11 July, where the selected pieces will be displayed and the top three pieces will win attractive prizes. The judges will select the top winners just prior to the final exhibition, with the winner to be announced on opening night, 2 July, Friday.

    Registration is now open and all submissions must reach us by 15 May, 2010.
