    It's the December holidays. A SGForums outing was organized at Pulau Ubin. 10 members turned first to set things up. They're lexelle, NaiveGirl, floral_gal, Hello_Kitty, NgKaiHoe, DaKaR, FoReVeR, y2care, FnU and of course, The Great One.

    It was 1pm, just taken our lunch, and now waiting for the next boat to go to Pulau Ubin. The boat finally came, we're on!

    Exactly when the last person boarded the boat, the sky slowly turned dark and started to rain half-way the journey to the island.

    After a "swing-here-swing-there" boat ride, we finally reached our destination. We ran to the nearest shelter to wait for the rain to stop.

    It rained for almost 2 hours. We packed up, found a clear area in the forest and start pitching the tents. There are 10 tents to pitch, one tent for 3 persons, the tents were pitched in a semi-circular manner, the space in front for the campfire.

    The rest of the members will arrive tommorow...

    I was rudely awakened by the sharp screaming of NaiveGirl , "What the hell did you scream so loud?"

    NaiveGirl: "Look...over...there..."

    The rest of us have already woken up by that time, thanks to NaiveGirl, and we found that the last tent was ripped apart and the tent was soaked in blood.

    Everyone was gathered around and went for lunch. Then we went fishing, played soccer, skateboard, rollerblade, etc..., until 5pm.

    We went back to the campsite to set up the campfire, unload the firewoods etc...

    Night fall. We were singing and dancing when suddenly we heard a loud scream. (heez!)

    Then we saw pixie_gal running out of the bushes, (What was she doing behind the bushes?) screaming, "Gui ah! (Ghosts!)"

    A pair of green eyes are looking at us from behind pixie_gal. Then with frightening suddenness, a 2 metres tall T-Rex-like beast jumped out of the bushes.

    The girls shriek, barbecue sticks flying here and there, total chaos.

    Then someone shouted, "Pick up the barbecue sticks, we can stab this bastard to death if we work together!"

    All the girls went to hide the tent liaoz. (What's the damn use man?)

    The beast stood there motionless. We didn't move too. The situation now is very tense. The beast only moved its green glowing eyes to look at us. We stood together, on a count of 3, we charged!

    The beast gave a loud roar and charged at us too. It gave a powerful swipe with its claws, someone was hit and he flew 5 metres away. (Dunno who so sway.)

    We clinched on the beast like rats clinching on a cat, and we started stabbing.

    "Kan ni na lah, poke the head, not the backside!"
    "Shut up lah!"

    The beast now is totally overwhelmed. Kena stabbed so many times, hit by punches and flying kicks, then all at once, everyone gave the beast a stab in the head.

    The beast didn't make any sound. We retreated. It stood there motionless for 5 seconds before falling onto the ground.

    "Phew!"
    "What the damn hell was that thing?"
    Someone replies, "Your momma."

    All of us sat down for a rest, the girls came out, still shaking from the shock. Then someone said, "What's that?"

    To our horror, far into the forest, in front of us, was a huge patch of glowing green!
