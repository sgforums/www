    Your MP3 Decoder may broken. First, Go to Start --> Run --> Type regsvr32.exe l3codeca.acm, then hit enter.

    Go to C:\Windows\System32\l3codeca.acm and rename it to l3codeca.bak. Then open WMP and go to Help --> Check for Updates. This will re-download the decoder and place a new copy on your system. It should be registered and ready to go at this point, but you can also run regsvr32.exe l3codeca.acm again just be sure.
