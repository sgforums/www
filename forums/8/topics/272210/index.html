    5'11" is equal to 5 feet 11 inches.
    *note there is no such thing as 5'13", which is equivalent to 6'1"

    (') denotes feet
    (") denotes inches

    it'd be easier if you convert it to inches rather than inputing 5.92 feet into the convert. in this case, 5'11" would be 60" + 11" = 70".

    1 feet is = to 12 inches
    1' = 12"

    more accurate units of the inches are as follows:
    e.g
    5-1/2" = 5.5"
    5-1/4" = 5.25"
    5-1/8" = 5.125"
    5-1/16" = 5.0625"
    and so on... the denominator gets multiplied by 2 everytime a smaller measurement is required.
    for e.g, there is no such thing as 5-1/15". and 5-2/16" is incorrect either as it is equal to 5-1/8"

    i'm surprised alot of people nowadays still don't know how to convert inches to mm. 1" = 25.4mm.

    personally, i think inches is a better system. why? because it's easier for humans to imagine a length of feet and inches, rather than meter and milimeters. the SI standard was established for the purpose of standardization...

    oh btw, 3 feet is = 1 yard. that's about a stride length of the average adult. and 1 feet, as the name suggests, measures about the sole of an average adult. so you see, measurements with the imperial actually makes life easier.
