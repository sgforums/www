    Just a few tips that I thought I'd offer:

    1) Study hard; it contributes to your wealth-building efforts. Don't listen to the losers out there who lick ass at their studies and dissuade you from succeeding academically. This is Singapore -- an education offers you way more than just employment opportunities.

    2) Cut the crap out and grow up. Got a PSP? Throw that shit away, you're better off using your efforts learning or interacting with people. Like to play WoW? Cut that shit out too. It never did anything for anybody; if you want to succeed, you have to behave like a disciplined person. And stop all that partying crap that drains your wealth and makes you feel stupid. If you want sex, which is basically why guys go to clubs anyway, get a girlfriend. I can assure you none of the idiots who play DotA and WoW is going to make it in life as long as they keep that shit up. Girls, no more Desperate Housewives, XOXO Gilmore Girls and America's Next Top Model. Get your life in order, come on.

    3) Learn how to invest and stop spending money like it's a constant. Saving is a prerequisite to investing. Also, run a part-time business; be creative. Many self-made millionaire entrepreneurs made it online running their business with no employees (plentyoffish.com, craigslist.org, 4chan.org, etc.). Learn from people like them. It's no secret that stocks, real estate and small business are the key engines to wealth generation.

    4) Find a physical activity that you enjoy and do it once or twice weekly. For example, I enjoy gymnastics so I join a cheerleading squad that lets me do my thing and keeps me stuck to a regular schedule. Stop eating all that junk that makes you feel like shit, like instant noodles, crackers, biscuits, etc. Sleep well and drink a lot of water. Never neglect your health no matter what, because your health is a prerequisite to achieving anything.

    5) Keep your social circle wide. Mingle around with people, network and get to know both guys and girls alike. Build up your social skills. With an education, wealth, health and self-confidence, the girlfriend will come naturally. Don't sweat over this aspect of your life; get the other aspects in order first.
