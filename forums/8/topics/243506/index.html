    It says:

    AC Adapter
    Input: 100-240V ~ 0.2A 50-60Hz LPS
    Output: 5V === 1A
    That adaptor can be used for the purpose you want. The current does not matter at all.

    In essence, what's stated in your AC adapter (using the principles of a transformer) basically tells you that adaptor converts an AC input VOLTAGE source of anything between 100V and 240V to a DC output VOLTAGE source of 5V(which is exactly what you want).

    The stipulated current there does not affect the performance of your source (it'd be quite monotonous if I explained it to you in technical/engineering terms). Just bear in mind it's the VOLTAGE that powers your mp3 player, not the CURRENT (I'm sure you know batteries come in various voltages for a reason).
