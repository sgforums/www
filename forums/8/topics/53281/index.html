    Step 1. Worsen the situation

    Tell your audience, times are terrible, in fact worst than it actually is. Tell them the company might cease operation when it has only been making a lost 1st time in 20 years. Or tell them the sky is falling when its only drizzling.

    Expected reaction. Your audience should feel armagaddon is near. An air of fear and confusion should surround and your audience must be at a lost.

    Step 2. Re-iterate good times.

    Remind them that times had been better. They were once enjoying steady salary raises. Cite examples of that one staff who raised from delivery man to director of logistics in 20 years. And who provided that room for growth? YOU OF COURSE! Its you and you the speaker alone who over the years had sweat and tolled and the audience had milked your efforts.

    Expected reaction. Nostalgy, remembering good times, smiles and dreamy reactions are expected.

    Step 3. Blame them, blame external factors, blame everyone else.

    Say its their fault. High cost of operations is their fault. Lost of profits is due to external factors. Lost of customers is due to competition. Keep pushing until it sounded like you have absolutely no responsibility to bear.

    Expected reaction. Guilt. The audience must feel that they are to be blamed mostly, that they ought to be axed, have their pay cut and they deserve it.

    Step 4. Motivational speech.

    "Together we can make a difference boys and girls". Tell them that. Tell them they were once producing results too. If you don't have any other examples to cite, then talk about your delivery boy turned director again. Remember, if he can do it, so could you! It helps if you would crack a few jokes here and there now to loosen the crowd.

    Expected reaction. Mental orgasm. Slow head nods, tears and signs of determination are what you should see. You have just conditioned your audience for the next big thing. To do... WHAT? Read on.

    Step 5. Drop the bomb.

    You are going to retrench them all. You are going to cut their pay. You are forcing them on no pay leaves. You are closing the company. Go ahead and drop the bomb. If you have done the 4 previous steps right, like submissive sheeps they will do your biddings.
