    Taking pictures of building don't necessarily need good optical zoom. 3x should also be enough, unless you want to be a professional. What's most important is looking for a camera where it's sensor is sensitive and colours look vibrant and there's as little noise as possible.

    Optical zoom means it zooms by adjusting it's lenses. While digital zoom means the camera's brain zooms in digitally without any movement from the lenses. Digital zoom will degrade picture quality very badly.
