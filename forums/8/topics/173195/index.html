    Q2:

    Let t be the increase/decrease in toll charge needed in cents.

    Let S be the toll charge received daily.

    Hence, the equation is:

    S = (36,000 - 300t)(t + 100) (since when t = 0, toll charge will be $1 or 100 cents)

    Hence simplify,

    S = 6,000t - 300t^2 + 3,600,000

    dS/dt = 6,000 - 600t

    When dS/dt = 0 t = 10

    Check, d^2S/dt^2 = - 600 S will be at a maximum.

    Hence, required toll charge = 100 + 10 cents = $1.10.
