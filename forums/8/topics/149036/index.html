    Mont Blanc wallets are currently being produced by this German luxury leather company called Seeger. In the 90s. Seeger was a luxury leather brand manfacturing quality leather products, till Richemont Holdings decided to purchase it.

    Therefore purchasing a Mont Blanc wallet will bear Seeger's motto of "Cashmere in leather"

    Personally i don't really fancy a Mont Blanc wallet.Especially with their metal logo on it.Looks kinda of off. I am currently using a Porsche Design wallet.Their wallets are also made by specialist leather makers in Germany. Its leather is superbly tanned and treated. Very nice feel to it.

    You might want to check out Porsche Design range of products at The Pinnicale Collection. Their main botique is opposite Orchard Parade Hotel. or they have a booth at Takashimaya 3rd floor mens department.

    With regards to wallets without coin compartments, they are usually meant for indiviuals who don't carry coins and usually charging to their cards and hardly spending cash. But i usually buy my wallets without the compartment as it doesn't even out my wallet and i prefer to carry my coins in a small coin pouch
