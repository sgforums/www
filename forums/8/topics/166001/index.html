    Go for flash-based or microdrives 'cos they are not affected by shaking.

    Besides, you don't really need 10 gig of space on a MP3 player, you'll never be able to finish listening to even 1 gig within a day. Of course, some people will argue that if you have 10 gig you can store everything on it and you don't have to change songs. I thought that way before, but now I don't think so anymore.

    Pros of HDD-based players with 10gig and above capacity:
    - Cheaper by capacity/$ ratio
    - Can use as backup harddisk or store large amounts of anime etc.

    Cons:
    - More susceptible to damage by dropping
    - Large amount of space might be under utilised
