    Hi,
    Sorry to inform you that such conversion is not allowed. The usage of CPF accounts are:
    Ordinary accounts:
    - house purchase
    - children edu
    - transfer to your Retirement Account to buy annuity which gives up lifetime retirement income when u reach 55 years old.
    - investing in funds

    SA:
    - transfer to your Retirement Account to buy annuity which gives up lifetime retirement income when u reach 55 years old.
    - investing in funds

    Medisave:
    - purchase hospitalization insurance
