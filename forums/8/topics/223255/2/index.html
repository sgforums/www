    generally if u wanna create the volume effect on your hair:

    scoop up a considerable amount of wax/mud (slightly less than the area of your thumbnail)

    rub your hands together to spread the wax/mud evenly

    apply the wax/mud onto your hair by messing up your hair (not too much, just lightly)

    proceed to style your hair to your desired style

    ______________________________________________________________

    for the "spiky" effect:

    repeat the same as above

    twist and pull up areas of your hair which you wish to spike
