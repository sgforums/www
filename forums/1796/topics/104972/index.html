    while it seems a kind act to feed strays, i would recommend not to do it. If you really love cats, bring them home and keep them at home. Once you start feeding strays, they get accustomed to being fed and that reduces their ability to find and forage food on their own. It also makes them more trusting to humans and makes them susceptible to possible abusers.

    I strongly suggest that if you love cats, bring them home and keep them as a pet. It pains my heart to see dead kittens and cats every now and then because they are too trusting and abusers get their hands on them.
