'Cats of the World' Photo Exhibition

  • QX179R

    Cat lovers are in for a treat this month. The 'Cats of the World' Photo Exhibition is back for a fourth year running. On display will be a selection of some 50 snapshots of cats captured in their element - graceful, lazy, sneaky, aloof, loving.

    The shots by professional photographers from all walks of life were captured in unique settings and backdrops around the world, including Singapore, Thailand, Canada, Jordan, Japan and India.

    Photos from this year and past year collections will be on sale. Proceeds raised will go to the Cat Welfare Society.

    The exhibition at The Arts House is on from 10am to 10pm and runs till June 29. Admission is free.
