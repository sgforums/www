    Cat Adoption Drive at Pet Safari VivoCity on Sunday 4 Sep

    Submitted by veron on Mon, 08/29/2011 - 09:15
    Time: 
    09/04/2011 - 15:00

    You are my whole life
    Please take care of me for life...

    Cat Welfare Society and Pet Lovers Centre Foundation are jointly holding a Cat Adoption Drive for the first time in Pet Safari stores!

    There will be around 10 - 15 adorable cats and kittens seeking homes at the fornightly adoption drive from Sep 4 to Dec 11 2011. Some of them are from our Tanjong Pagar Railway Station project where cats and kittens were rescued after being found abandoned when owners moved away. These cats deserve a forever home!

    Date: Alternate Sundays from Sep 4 to Dec 11
    Time: 3 - 8pm
    Where we will be:
    Sep 4 VivoCity
    Sep 18 Eastpoint
    Oct 2 Nex
    Oct 16 VivoCity
    Oct 30 Eastpoint
    Nov 13 Nex
    Nov 27 VivoCity
    Dec 11 Eastpoint

    Potential adopters will be screened for suitability and commitment. An adoption fee of $40 for every successful adoption applies to ensure adoptions are genuine. Successful adopters will each receive a Pet Lovers Centre “Adopter’s Starter Kit” goodie bag, including cat food, shampoo and litter worth $30!

    In September, we also have $100 Tiger Airways vouchers and other Pet Lovers Centres' prizes to give away when you participate in our in-store games!
