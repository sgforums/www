    30 Mar 10, 13:22

    Long hair cats need to be washed at least once every month.

    Short hair cats, actually never need to be washed at all. Wash only when they are dirty. Or if they have white furs, and they are looking dirty.

    The most important thing is to calm them down, with plenty of coaxing. Prepare your towels, hair dryer, and shampoo before hand. Use warm water, cold water is shocking to them. Their body is small and they lose body heat fast and will start shivering. They could easily get sick.

    Wet, shampoo, rinse, quickly, and then towel dry. Then blow dry with warm air. All them time you must, be calming the cat down with your voice.
