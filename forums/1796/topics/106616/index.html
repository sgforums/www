    heres my 1st contribiution :

    I am a cat, thats what I am,
    I grew up in bliss,with balls of string
    butterflies and birds of spring
    with long naps and loving pats
    I am now grown and grown

    I rule the roost with velvet paws
    I bow down to none but mom alone
    She has loved me with heart and soul
    and i love her to the bone

    She comes in late and all alone,
    and I purr a song to her soul.
    I see her eyes and lift her up
    with a nuzzle and a touch.
    kneading her tummy and biting her toes
    I bring a smile and remove the woes.

    I protect her and lie by her door till first light
    hunting and killing many a cockroach with a bite!
    My life is one of so much bliss!

    I am a cat, thats what I am.
    I would be no other cos i love my mother!

    I am what I am and would be no other!
