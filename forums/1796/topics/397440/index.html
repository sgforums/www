    Feeding Stray Cats isn't illegal but littering is! So please clean up after feeding.

    Tips on feeding:

    1. Don't place the food directly on the floor. Use paper plate, bowl or newspaper.
    2. Always clean up and leftover and do not leave the mess.
    3. Feed on places with low human traffic and in open spaces
    4. Alway feed them downstair. Never feed them in the corridor or on stairs landing as the cats may be attached to the area and lottier in these area causing inconvience to your neighbours.
    5. Never throw out the food from your windows.

    BE RESPONISBLE WHEN FEEDING THE STRAYS!!!
