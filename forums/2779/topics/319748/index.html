    Nanostray 2 is a scrolling shooter video game for the Nintendo DS and sequel to the original Nanostray.

    Addressing the complaints many had with tacked-on touchscreen features, Nanostray 2 has three control schemes -- classic control, left-handed touch control, and right-handed touch control. Like the previous game, gameplay focuses more on graphic quality rather than touch-screen control. Customization remains a key part of the experience: at the start of a level, players have the ability to adjust which special weapons they'll take into the fight, and also alter the angle of their side-mounted guns as they get mounted on the front, sides and rear of the ship during the action. Besides the main single-player mode, Nanostray 2 also has a Challenge mode, where players can try to get a set number of points, collect a set number of coins, or survive for a specific time limit.
