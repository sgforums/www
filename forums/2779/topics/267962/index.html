    Resident Evil DS rom is squished down to a portable format that preserves most of what made the original so great. Resident Evil: Deadly Silence is basically a straight port of the 1996 PlayStation classic, though it adds a few neat twists made possible by the Nintendo DS roms. While the graphical presentation isn't as impressive as you'd expect from Resident Evil, it's still impressive to have the whole game playable in the palm of your hand. More importantly, Resident Evil DS rom still packs some good freaky fun, and it's a cool way for the series' newer fans to experience the game that coined the phrase "survival horror."
