Chelsea may have to deal with Ronaldo

  • Ben_Ng69
    17 Mar 11, 02:35

    Chelsea may have to deal with Ronaldo if they want to sign Santos star Neymar.

    Former Real Madrid and Inter Milan star Ronaldo, who has just hung up his boots, wants to become a player agent and has identified Chelsea target Neymar as one of his first potential clients.

    "We are experiencing a large demand from the best athletes in Brazil. At the start we are not going to be able to attend to everyone and we will have to select a few. Neymar and Lucas interest us, because they will be the future of Brazilian football and should be playing in the 2014 World Cup," Ronaldo told Radio Bandeirante.

    Neymar is currently represented by Brazilian super agent Wagner Ribeiro.
