    Real Madrid are ready to take a massive hit on selling Kaka back to AC Milan.

    El Mundo Deportivo says Real could be willing to sell Kaka back to Milan for less than €25 million - having spent €58 million to sign the Brazilian two years ago.

    Real president Florentino Perez and Milan vice-president Adriano Galliani are now in talks over a fee for the former World Player of the Year.

    Despite rival interest from Manchester City and Chelsea, it is Milan that are in the driving seat to land Kaka this summer.
