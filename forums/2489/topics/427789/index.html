Evra: I would love Juventus keeper Buffon at Man Utd

  • Ben_Ng69
    14 Apr 11, 13:09

    Manchester United fullback Patrice Evra would love to see Gigi Buffon at Old Trafford.

    The Juventus goalkeeper has been linked with United as a replacement for the retiring Edwin van der Sar.

    Evra said: "Buffon? It would be nice to play with him.

    "But we hope that Van der Sar will remain with us for another year. I say that to him every day."
