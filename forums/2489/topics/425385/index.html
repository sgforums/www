Messi rules out ever leaving Barcelona

  • Ben_Ng69
    17 Mar 11, 17:48

     

    Barcelona star Lionel Messi has ruled out leaving the Nou Camp.

    The Argentina international has been linked with Manchester City and Inter Milan this season.

    But he insists he can only see his future at Barca.

    "My future is here in Barcelona, I would like to finish my career at the club," said Messi.
