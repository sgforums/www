    this is the recipe for my cookies .. i think they're butter cookies .. this is taken from a recipe book (:



    Ingredients

    125g butter
    1/2 cup caster sugar
    1 egg
    1/4 teaspoon vanilla essence
    1 cup plain flour
    1 cup self raising flour

    Baking Procedure
    1. Beat butter and sugar till creamy .
    2. Add egg & vanilla essence & beat well .
    3. Add both types of flour .
    4. Sandwich the dough between 2 pieces of baking paper and roll to 5mm thick .
    5. Cut out dough with cookie cutters .
    6. Bake at 160 degrees C for 10-15 mins or until slightly golden .
