    the water bath for the creme brulee is called bain marie.

    i try to see if i can remember my recipe for the double choc muffins, cos i am not at home now.

    100g plain flour
    1 tsp baking powder
    1 tbsp cocoa powder
    50g choc chips
    25g butter, melted
    half teacup of milk (i bought low-fat milk instead)
    25g caster sugar
    1 small egg, beaten

    1) sift flour, baking powder and cocoa powder together.
    2) mix melted butter, milk and egg together in a separate bowl.
    3) make a well in the centre of the flour mixture.
    4) pour in the liquid mixture into the centre
    5) add in the choc chips and sugar and mix. (i noticed the recipe didnt mention when to add the sugar, so i added in at this stage)
    6) spoon the mixture into cake cases in muffin tray.
    7) bake in pre-heated oven at 210C for 20min(is it too long?)

    Ingredients
    Nai Bai or whatever veggie you like
    Gingko Nuts
    Fresh mushroom of your choice
    Minced Garlic
    Chicken Stock
    Oyster Extract / Sauce
    Corn Flour

    Method
    Cook Gingko Nuts & Mushrooms in the chicken stock
    Add in Oyster Extract / Sauce for additional flavor and taste
    Add in Corn Flour to thicken it
    Set aside
