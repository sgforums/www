    "I took this photo in the summer of 1984 in my living room. It's of a painting I did on a piece of smoked glass of the band KISS. I would always take photos of paintings and/or drawings i did, but what came out of this one caught me by surprise.

    If you look to the center and to the right you can see what appears to be the shape of a SKULL (close-up below). This I swear I did not paint into the picture, no photo enhancements either. This photo was taken using 35mm film with flash on. I still have the original photo but can't find the negatives."
