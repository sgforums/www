    "A FORMER Royal Marine is planning to climb Snowdon - with a fridge strapped to his back. Paul McKelvey will scale Wales' highest peak as the challenging climax of a bizarre sponsored walk.

    He set out from Liverpool on Wednesday to walk the 120 miles to Snowdon, a tough enough task without the extra burden of the 40-kilo fridge.

    Paul, 35, was asked to take on the marathon challenge in aid of Zoe's Place, a children's hospice in West Derby, Liverpool. He hopes to raise Â£5,000.

    In the past, he has carried a fridge to London in aid of the Weston Spirit.

    His current walk has taken him through Flintshire and Denbighshire to the A5, and last night he was stopping traffic as he headed along the road towards Capel Curig.

    For the final trek up Snowdon tomorrow morning, he will be joined by relatives and friends, and representatives of the charity.

    His brother-in-law Paul Preston is driving a support car and collecting donations.

    "The support along the way has been great, with lots of people asking what is going on," he said."
