The tree spirit...

  • PaJeRoMiNi Ver 1.0

    "On July 8, 2003 at about 5:00 a.m. I was awakened by a violent storm. After the storm passed, I opened the window of my home-office and noticed the window was full of TREE!!! I didn't hear the tree fall or impact the house (first photos below) during the storm, but there it was leaning on the house, extended to the peek of the roof.

    I was surprised for a second time after these photos were developed. The photos below show an object on the house [other than the tree]... an object that should not exist... that was not visible when I took the photographs. I call this object The Tree Spirit.

    Below, you will see two cropped [not enlarged] photos of The Tree Spirit. The first shows The Tree Spirit Vertical, and looks to me to take the form of a woman [maybe an angel] with a rectangular object upon her chest; perhaps a shield. There was no real damage to the house.

    Further below is the same photo has been rotated 90 degrees clockwise to show what may be a nativity or crucifixion scene. Here's how see the nativity scene: Notice near the bottom of the object, what could be the image of a baby facing you with it's arms spread. To the left of the baby a person, perhaps holding something over the baby. Above the baby a spot that might be a star. Another object frames the baby from your right, but I can't tell what this is.

    It looks a bit like a crucifixion scene too... if you see the baby [with arms extended] as an adult upon the cross.

    Also notice a bit of distortion in the roof [to the right of the bigger object]. I don't see how this lends to the image, but it doesn't belong there. I admit, you have to use your imagination to see anything; however, the object is unquestionably present, and it shouldn't be."
