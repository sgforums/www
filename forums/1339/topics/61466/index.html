We were walking around outside, because everyone else there was older than us, and talking about when they were young. We found it boring, so decided to go for a walk outside. After a few minutes, we came to the old barn. The moon was almost full, and shining very brightly. We could see right through the barn. I saw a shadow moving within the barn, and though my eyes were playing ticks on me, but then my sister screamed. The shadow looked like a girl with a long dress and a bonnet, hanging from one of the beams in the barn by a rope around her neck. It was slowly swinging back and forth. We were both scared, so we started running back to the house. When I looked back, I could no longer see the figure in the barn. After getting back in the house, we talked about it, and had both seen the same thing.

    A few years later, we were back at my aunts, and exploring the basement. Since the house is so old, the basement is set up strange, with several rooms. The floor is concrete throughout the whole basement, except for in the furthest corner from the door to the basement. In that particular corner, there is a patch of dirt that is about 3x5 1/2 feet. It seemed weird to us, and we asked my aunt about it. She freaked out that we had even gone in the basement, and told us to never go in there again. A few weeks later, when they weren't home, we went back there, and brought with us the ouija board (listed in this auction). We went to the spot in the basement that had the dirt floor, and tried to see if the ouija board would work. We were sitting there for a few minutes, and nothing. We were about ready to give up when the planchette started moving. I thought my sister was moving it, but then I noticed that the way both of our hands were on it, it would be to difficult to move without the planchette flipping over. It was a long time ago, so I don't really rmemeber exactly what it spelled out, but I do remember one thing. When we asked who they were, it spelled out "Elizabeth". Up to that point in time, I had never gotten the ouija board to even work. It certainly never did anything strange. It just sat in a box in my room.

    After the "Elizabeth" incident, when I got back home, I stuck the box with the ouija board on my desk. It was touching my lava lamp, and within 30 seconds, the light bulb in the lava lamp burned out. I just assumed it was a coincidence, but then other things started happening. When the ouija board was in my room, and I was listening to the radio, or even a cd, it would have a static sound. Not enough to bother me, but enough to notice it. If I stuck the board in another room, the radio would sound normal again. The same with the tv. It was just weird. I put the ouija board in storage until a few years later, after I was married, and we bought a new house.

    I had forgotten I even had the ouija board, and was sort of happy to see it again. I brought it up to my bedroom, and then as luck would have it, the light bulb in my lamp on the dresser burned out. That scared me, so I stuck it in the basement. We went to sleep, and when I woke up in the morning, the ouija board and planchette were on my dresser, even though I had stuck them in the basement, and the box was missing. I searched the whole house for the box, and I still can find it to this day. After that night, the board stayed in the basement, and seemed to remain dormant for a while. A year or so after we moved in to the house, I was in the basement doing laundry when I heard a noise like fingernails on a chalk board, only less shrill. I figured it was our cat, getting into something, so I started moving things around to try to find him. Thats when I noticed on a shelf in the corner, the ouija board was sitting there, and the planchette was moving by itself. I was so freaked out I immediatly left the basement. Later, I wondered what it was spelling out, so I went back down there, WITH my husband (less scary that way!!!) and went to see it. It WAS still moving, and it was spelling "why" over and over. I took the planchette, left the board where it was. I put the planchette in a drawer in my dresser upstairs.

    After this, other strange things started happening. My 2 year old started pointing out the windows, and crying, and hiding. When I asked her what was wrong, she told me "Ghosts look at me!!!!" That scared the hell out of me. This happens on a regular basis now. Other things that happen include the tv turns on by itself, and the refrigerator turns itself up to as cold as it can get, even if we put tape in the dial. Sometimes the water in the bathtub will run by itself. Nothing dangerous, but it still scares me. I figured it had something to do with the ouija board....but then I found something out from one of my neighbors. Back in the 1800's when my town was first founded, the lot that my house is on, plus 3 others, used to be a cemetery!!!!!! After researching it, it said that there were originally 200 people buried there, and there are only records of some of the graves being moved. No one knows what happened to the rest.
