  • PaJeRoMiNi Ver 1.0
    1 Dec 04, 19:56
    Just follow the road straight from Jalan Tebrau (The main road that runs thru JB, parallel to the train station), you'll pass by a small town at the edge of JB,Ulu Tiram, into an ulu road that leads to Mersing.

    Take note that you'll have to turn left at a point into a road that leads to Kota Tinggi. Just follow the road signs.

    You'll have to pass thru the town of Kota Tinggi (after crossing a bridge, you'll reach the town) before you'll reach the waterfall.

    One word of advice, DON'T eat the Roti John from the food stall at the foot of the waterfall. It's more effective than Eno, if you catch my drift.

    And beware of fishes at the top part of the waterfall. They bite.
