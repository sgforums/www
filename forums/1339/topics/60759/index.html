    "My wife and I hosted our annual Halloween party on Saturday October 25th (2003). This was the first picture I took of the evening and was taken at 10:10 MST. The setting on the camera was wrong and the flash did not go off. I was taking a photo of Batman and the man to my left of him holding a bottle (below). The "figure" to my right of Batman (close-up above) was not there when I took the photo. The only person at the party who was as short as the "figure" was a woman dressed as a turtle and she was sitting on a couch in front of Batman when the photo was taken.

    We often feel a presence in our living room. The chair I usually sit in is normally where the man holding the bottle is standing. Often when I sit there I get glimpses of something to my right and behind me. It happens all the time and it always startles me. We have lived in this house for three years and are the fourth owners since it was built in 1950. We don't know any history of who lived here or what has happened over the years.

    This is an absolutely unretouched digital photograph. This is my first digital camera and I have taken less than 100 photos. I don't have a clue how to manipulate images and have just figured out how to attach it to an e-mail."

    --Preston L.
    Fort Collins, Colorado
