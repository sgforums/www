    This building is the icon of Taman Jurong back in the early days as it was the tallest building in Taman Jurong.

    From what I heard (rumors):-
    1.) The building is being plan to be redevelop into a commerical building or upgrade (cause it is a 1-rm flat)
    2.) The lease to the NTUC, clinic, shops at 1st and 2nd floor has not expire yet

    However, I do notice:-
    1.) There is a recent bank renovation (early this year or late last year), I am not sure is it OUB or UOB or OCBC, the one just outside NTUC entrance.
    2.) There are also constant mainteance work on the building itself.
    So there might be plans to this building afterall.

    If you want to have spooky stuff, this place might have something as this place is also a well-known place for suicide in the past (at least from what I heard from my Father). The most recent case is which a woman was throw off this building and the lover hid in the water tanks on the top floor during the police hunt.

    For me, there are so many changes in Taman Jurong in the past 10 years and this building brings my childhood memories instead of any spooky stuff.
