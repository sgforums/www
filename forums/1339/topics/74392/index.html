    I remember being quite affected by a series of suicides about a week apart.. oh.. ok.. not series but only 2 suicides. Two people had jumped off in my building and the other person at the opposite block. There's a carpark between us but I can see that building really clearly (no obstractions. Anyway, i nearly saw him jumping off that day.. but i had made a detour around the market.

    Anyway, a few weeks.. or maybe months later. It was about 6.15am, i was ready to go to the bus stop to get to school. Yes.. in those days.. it was JC for me. Well.. i saw someone's shadow. It was completely black. Just a black figure sitting on the ledge of the common corridor. Ya know.. just on top and swinging his legs.. ! I thought someone else wanted to jump off. I even stare at it for another few seconds while i was screaming for my mum to come to the window.
    Well i turned my back for about another 1/2 minute. When i turned back it was gone.

    Did i see him??
