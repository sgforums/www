    Often, taxi drivers doing the midnight shifts, would see a lady in
    white or in pale colours flagging for their taxis near to Kranji
    MRT station. If they had the guts to stop, they are mostly ordered
    to go to a cemetery. The payment would mostly be hell notes. Some
    unlucky drivers would be treated to a view of the ghost's real
    appearance. That is why the drivers will not go to Woodland,
    Sembawang and Khatib. The road was quite quiet and dim.

    There have been many reports of people traveling on the roads
    of Singapore late at night meeting strange looking people asking
    for directions. Mostly the strange looking people would be dressed
    in hoods. Upon closer examinations, one will discover that there
    is no face under the cover of the hoods. Other says that the blood
    on the scary face are seen along the road.

    Tampines Rd was once a long and winding road between Serangoon Rd and Upper Changi Rd North, but the building of Tampines and Pasir Ris Towns cut the road into two, resulting in 2 Tampines Rd in 2 locations.
