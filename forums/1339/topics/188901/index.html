    The Girl in Red
    "The Girl in Red" was a home video taken by a Taiwanese family who were hiking at the countryside of Taichung. The video was taken in March 1998 at in the afternoon.

    After the video was recorded, the family did not touch the tape and left it inside the cam. After a short while (a few days, months? it was not stated), one of the family members passed away.

    It was then that someone suggested that the family watch the video to bring back some good memories of the person that passed away.

    It was then that they realised that in the video, the person who passed away, while coming down a slope, had smiled for the camera and there was evidently a pair of fangs in his mouth!

    They also realised that in another shot, there was a little girl in red following them.

    Here's the creepy part.

    None of the family members remembered seeing this girl during the whole trip. And she wasn't part of the family either. there was no way we could have missed seeing her there, says one family member.

    The whole place was one big forested area, with no villages or houses within a large radius (impossible to walk into the area). Anyone walking past or following them, the family would have clearly noticed them.
