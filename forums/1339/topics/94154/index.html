    SIMEI: NAMED AFTER A MURDER CASE?

    I've heard stories from the old-pokes of Simei that it got it's name from a case happened in the old days. If I'm not mistaken, told by them, the case was involving girls whom was murdered and their bodies was found in an underpass around Simei. Friends of mine says that this underpass was well-known to be one of THE haunted spot in Simei. I've yet to see with my two eyes towards this underpass, but one day... I will.

    Just for your information 'SI' mean four in one of Chinese dialects and MEI, well I assumed it mean female/girl.

    But remember, this is what I've heard, told by the old-pokes of Simei. Though, I find it hard to believe.
