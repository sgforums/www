    It was previously called Kangkar (foot of the harbour). Renamed Sengkang (prosperous harbour) in 1995. The name was taken from a road called Lorong Sengkang (which no longer exists), where Blk 232 now stands.

    If you stand at the junction of Punggol Rd and Sengkang East Way 10 years ago, you will be standing at the junction of Punggol Rd, Lorong Buangkok (towards Compassvale) and Jalan Seranggong Kechil (towards Rivervale).
