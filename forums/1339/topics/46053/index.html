    Why certain rooms in Changi barrack got completely sealed off by walls?

    In Changi barrack, there are some rooms that just got completely sealed off by walls. The believed answer is that these hidden chambers have no entrance, no exit and no windows; they just got tightly enclosed. Rumors from some NS guys said that the walls on these rooms would bleed mysteriously. No matter how many times and how many layers of paints were used to cover up, the bleeding stain would slowly reveal out. This horrible sign sometimes will manifest into a Satan's face. The army could not explain it. So they just totally seal off these rooms.

    Anyone formerly from Changi Barracks or have heard of this urban legend? Can anyone vouch for it's authencity?
