    The Urban Infiltrators is a collective of activists, radicals, punks and explorer youths keen in the search of excitement behind the mundane drone of urban life in this concrete jungle.
    We are truly pleased to find like-minded people like you here and surprised to find Urban Explorers (Singapore).
    Unlike other so-called 'exploring clans' who are more into lame ghost hunting and attracting pointless problems with the phenomenal, we are not interested in any ghostly encounters or whatever horror stories you might be oh-so-excited to tell us about.



    The U.I, unlike the other said clans (that shall remain unamed because you might have all heard about them anyway) infiltrate abandoned buildings and sites in search of history and significance. Our activities and exploits are purely done out of informational, aesthetic, artistic and amusement reasons. We conduct thorough research and we do not organize groups of annoying people for 'tours' or whatever nonsense in the hopes of encountering something unexplained.

    So far we have infiltrated 3 sites (though I am still quite unsatisfied with the missions) and we have run up an extensive list of abandoned buildings/hospitals/hotels to explore.

    We are not interested in ghosts although sometimes unexplainable s**t happens that isn't expected and require us to abandon our mission and get the f**k out of wherever we happen to be at.
    We do not break in.
    We do not trespass. (This bit is slightly debatable)
    We do not eat children.
    We are not terrorists.


    Me looking up towards the roof

    I cannot divulge information on our activites and exploits - yet. Due to our tight security protocol on the organization's explorations, research, documents and whereabout as well as its members, we intend to keep the organization as underground as possible and limit the information flow to the mainstream society in order to protect our secrecy.
    However, if you are interested in joining us in our explorations, do leave comments here and maybe, if there is keen interest, some infomation shall be voluntarily leaked.

    There is much to explore, much to discover here. There are still buildings, abandoned and vacated, untouched by the reclamations/redevelopment department of the Housing Development Board. We must get to them before they perish.
