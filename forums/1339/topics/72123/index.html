    Face of Ghost in Cat

    "The photo below was taken in the mid 80s, and it depicts my cat Theresa (long since passed away) sleeping curled up in a ball with her paw over her face. There wasn't anything unusual about her, my dad just took the picture because she looked cute.

    However, upon receiving the photo back, my father pointed out something that caught his eye immediately, and then I saw it too. The face that comes from the cat is that of a profile of an old man with a receding hairline, sunken eyes and a white mustache. The face is unmistakable and eerie, as the image seems to be produced from the way in which she is laying.

    Most people I show this to are in awe and find it very creepy, and many say they can no longer make out the cat, they only see the old man. I have also submitted the face with an outline to aid those who have trouble seeing it. I don't know if this is a ghost or not, but it is amazing either way."
