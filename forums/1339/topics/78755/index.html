An encounter in the toilet in camp...

  • kopiosatu
    9 May 04, 00:10
    My friend's account, a regular infantryman in Bedok camp.

    It was late at night and he was planning to take a shower. He was situated at the top floor where 9 other section commanders were.

    He went into the toilet, the last cubicle was serviceable, the second last one isn't. He noticed that some whispering was coming from the unserviceable cubicle (maybe a guy using a handphone?). He peeped below to see if he could see any feet... but there wasn't anything... both cubicle doors were closed.

    He went to the bunk and pulled one guy to listen for something...

    My friend whispers "Eh how... you hear someone whispering?"

    The guy nods, my friend asks "who do you think it is? all of us are in the bunk"

    The guy shrugs, shakes his head, says "i dunno" and quickly walks out.

    My friend proceeds to shower. In the camp, there are windows for ventilation along the walls, and the angle of the glass enables my friend to have a direct view of the 2 closed cubicle doors behind him.

    He hears a flush...

    He thinks "hmmm coming out, see who it is..."

    The looks at the glass... the 2 doors are closed... and suddenly...

    The 2 doors open at the same time, and halfway.

    My friend turns around and opens the two doors... he told us "At that time, i didn't feel afraid... more curious..."

    He quickly finished his shower and carried on with his own stuff...
