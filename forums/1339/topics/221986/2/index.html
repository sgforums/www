    No problem.

    The Labrador tunnels are well-lighted so torches are not necessary for this part of the tour. As for Fort Berlayer, it can get pretty dark so torches are recommended for this part of the night tour.

    The guides are the core members from the Singapore Urban Explorers team and they are pretty knowledgable. They'll tell you about the place's history and some interesting facts as you go through the the fort with them.

    Ushers and safety tape will be placed around the fort to cordon off potential danger areas and prevent visitors from straying into high-risk accident areas.

    As for registration, you will just need to purchase the tickets at the carnival itself when it starts at 6pm and you will be inserted into the tour group.
