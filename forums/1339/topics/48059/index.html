    Anyone had paranormal experiences there?

    My fren, who was living in Ang Mo Kio some time ago, went for jogging in the park at about 6:30 in the morning.As he was waiting to be enlisted into BMT, he felt that jogging could help him build up his stamina for the upcoming training he was going to face.

    As it was a weekday, there were not many ppl around. Having jogged here many times, he treated it just like any normal day.

    While jogging along one of the ponds, he noticed an old man fishing at the pond. The old man's back was facing my fren. My fren, being the kapoh that he was, jogged over to the old man and asked:"Ah Pek, you diao dao yu mah?"(Any fish, ah pek?)

    The old man turned around and my fren got the shock of his life!
    The old man had no face, only two nostrils! My fren just ran like crazy out of Bishan Park.

    Ater that incident, my fren never went jogging again. No wonder when he jogs in camp now, he always asks for a companion.
