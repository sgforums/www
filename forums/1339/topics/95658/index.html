Fishy Photo at Night Safari

  • jolin80
    29 Sep 04, 11:42


    Whenever I take a look at this photo, it will always give me a chilly feeling. The photo was taken in August 2002 at the Night Safari. My friends, Pauline, Melvin, Kelvin and I went there on a Saturday night at around 11pm to have fun as we had nothing to do.

    When I was driving towards the Night Safari, we came to this road which was very small and dimmed and all of the sudden I felt creepy. However, I just cast it aside thinking it was because of the darkness. Once we reached there, we had a lot of fun and took many photos. Everyone of us would take turns to be photographed at the same place.

    One week later, Melvin had developed the film and we looked at the photos. But there was this particular photo that looked eerie and it was the one that I took it alone. There was this white shadow in front of me and it looked like a fish. The rest of my friends who also pictured at the same spot didnt have the shadow at all. I was really freaked out when I saw it. It was so unbelievable till now I am still thinking whether it is the supernatural or is because of my friend's smoke as he was smoking at that time.
