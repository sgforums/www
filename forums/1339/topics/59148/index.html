    An 'off the shelf' wooden bed from Ducal (slightly modified and steel reinforced!) has been grafted onto a severely shortened (over 1.2m) chassis. An unleaded van engine drives the rear wheels through a 4 speed manual gearbox.



    Front disc brakes, rear view mirrored ceiling and washable drapes all come as standard, though air conditioning is definitely not optional!

    Hidden under the duvet are conventional controls, however, a fixed seat position required an adjustable pedal assembly. The fuel tank is replenished via a hot water bottle at the foot of the bed.Four point harnesses and reclined bucket seats prevent the driver and two very close friends falling out during the night!



    Base Vehicle: Volkwagen Variant
    Engine: 1800cc (Air cooled)
    Power: 75bhp
    0-60: 40 winks
    Top Speed: 69mph
    Weight: 1400kg
    Length: 2580mm
    Width: 1960mm
    Height: 2460mm
    Seating: 3 in a bed!
    Colour: Purple n Pine
