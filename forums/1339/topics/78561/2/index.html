  • hunter
    19 Jun 04, 21:52
    would like to share with you abt my encounter with the spooky kind during my ICT which hd justed ended.

    During the last phase of our deployment exercise in sarimun avenue (spelling correct?) which is somewhere near LCK, our unit was depolyed at abt 9pm in this isolated deployment site. There is an abandoned housing estate opp our deployment side and diagonally to our right are the foreign labourers quarters. It was late at nite with hardly any traffic on the road except for some foreign labourers wandering abt like zombies.

    Sentries were deployed at the gate to prevent any unauthorised entry to the deployment site and these foreign labourers stared at us as though we hv invaded their privacy. Anyhow, it was the fourth day of the field exercise and we are totally exhausted and all waiting for bde orders to cut the exercise around 2359.

    I finished my sentry duty at abt 11pm and returned to the hq tonner to rest and found that most of the guys were not around and my buddy upon returning went away as well to smoke in other side of the deployment site. The hq tentage next to us (abt 5 meters away) looks quite deserted. I realised I was all alone in my area. I was surpised that I did not expressed any sense of fear or loneliness, perhaps due to my exhaustion. Suddenly, I hv this urge to piss and I walked towards the bushes. As I pissed, I was chilled by this sudden strong cold breeze send cold shivers down my spine and I was absolutely terrified. My hair was stand on end. I quickly walked back towards the hq tonner. I climbed onto it and put down all my sbo and rifle and sat down trying to blank out. For no particular reason, I turned towards the bushes where i pissed and stared into it. To my horror, I saw a black human shape slowly being formed and in no time, more and more black human shapes appeared in the bushes and float/walk towards me. I almost wet my pants and told myself those black human shapes floating/walking towards me are probably the controllers and or 30 sce guys whose gonna attack us but I knew better that the nite attack was over an hour ago and it can't be them again!!!

    The thought of these black human shapes coming for me and pull me into the bushes horrified me and it was then I regained my senses and quickly picked up my sbo and rifle and jumped out of tonner and ran towards the hq tentage. However, there is strange urge inside me to go towards the bushes and I was kind of like struggling internally to pull back.

    To my relieved, upon reaching the hq tentage, I found the signaller inside and stay put. I was trembling. A good fifteen mins or so passed and the other guys came back; I joined them and walked back towards the hq tonner and I stared into the bushes again and this time round, there were no black human shapes.

    I kept thinking of this incident over and over again. Did I really saw something or was it that I had a haullucination due to exhaustion.
    I wished it was the latter.
