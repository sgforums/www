    Woodlands coffeeshop offers free food and drink to attract crowds

    SINGAPORE : A newly opened coffeeshop in Woodlands has served up thousands of plates of free food to draw customers.
    It has been quite a delicate balancing act for owners at the coffeeshop in Block 345A, Woodlands Ave 3 who had to cope with crowds of up to 6,000 customers during the day.

    But the customers are not complaining, after all, these $2 mega meal deals are free of charge.

    Hungry diners started flocking in at 10'o clock in the morning and were each given a $2 coupon which they could redeem for their favourite food.

    There was a constant stream of customers and owners say about 4,000 coupons were given out during the morning alone

    Because of its relatively secluded location, about $20,000 was set aside for this one-day promotional campaign which owners hope will whet residents' appetites and keep them coming back for more. - CNA
