    "Back in August of this year I picked up my brother and his family from the Kansas City airport and then we drove to Atchison, Kansas. As we drove around the historic streets of Atchison, I told them about how this is the most haunted town in Kansas, and that one of these houses lives "Sally" a girl ghost from the civil war era.

    Back in the mid 1990's the TV show "Sightings"* did a feature story on "Sally" the scratching ghost. She would only scratch the man of the house. Some of the scratches on the mans arm and chest would even bleed.

    So about 40 minutes after driving around, telling my brother and his family about this story, we were swimming and my mother asked me what are those scratches on my neck? I didn't think much of it until I took a shower and looked in the mirror! I had four, two inch long red scratches on the right hand side of my neck! The skin was never broken, I never felt a thing, and they lasted for three days before fading away. I have attached a picture (below) of the scratches, taken about 30 min. after I discovered them."

    --Erik N.
    Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri
