    "These pictures (below) are of a strange angel statue my friend found. She was driving home one evening and saw a large terra cotta flower pot sitting out in the garbage in front of a home. She stopped and picked it up. After she got it home she noticed that it had a statue of an angel inside it.

    This angel is very strange, its head is broken off and when she looked down inside the body she saw another statue inside it. The inner statue is of a man and woman they are only visible to mid chest because they are immersed in fiberglass inside and cannot be removed. They appear to be made of a different material than the outer part of the angel, which is fiberglass. There are no names or manufacturing marks on it anywhere.

    Adding to the strangeness, when my friend first got the statue home, it had tons of black ants crawling out of the shell of the head. So many that you could not easily pick it up or hold on to it. There was nothing in the head that would have attracted them, no food. Now, it has been several days and the ants are still coming out of the body. Lots of them.

    I believe I once had heard a caller on the show say that ants are an indicator of magic spells. We cannot figure out if this is a majickal item or not. What do you think? We are open to suggestions. Strange Angel though, isn't it? "
