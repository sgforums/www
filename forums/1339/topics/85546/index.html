    2) Then during a camping for the softball team, we were then allowed to use the home economic classroom which is at level 3 for our cooking. Then when everybody was crowding in the room, i was out alone at the corridoor....then i saw her. she was a girl around my age, but strange her uniform were very dirty and torn. then through her eyes, i saw nothing but sadness. the next moment she was at the level 1....calling out to me asking me to come. Then the next thing i knew, my classmate slapped me. Then she told me she saw me crossing over the metal bar and about to jump.

    soon after the school reopens which is in 2 weeks time, another gal from the express stream started screaming and crying in the home economic class. Then she fainted.

    Soon, on a teacher day performance, a teacher performed a play based on the true story of the dead girl. it was as per wat the gal told me. she was raped by a construction worker when our school was partial in construction. i was the 3rd batch. she cut her wrist in the toilet at tat level. but she loved cooking so she in the home economic class.

    3) there also another ghost in the technical block, he has his hand cut off in due to a mistake while doing his project.

    4) Then during one of the NPCC camp that we have when in secondary 3, 3 of the gals including me were trying to sleep on the desks tat we placed together, and we slept with out lying on our torso meaning heads down. then i could hear a voice asking me to get up. i was scare and did not listen.
