    Dear fellow forumites of SgForums,

    It has been a fruitful and eventful journey for the Singapore Urban Explorers in the past 3 years.

    For the next 2 months, the team will be taking a break from the maintenance of the website and concentrate on the re-vamping of the new site layout. Expect more exploration journals and articles when the site re-opens in end 2006.

    A lot of feedback has been received on the old site's layout, navigation and also some issues on registeration by both SgForums members and the website registered members.

    As such, I would like to let everyone know about our new updates on the re-vamped site.

    - Visitors will no longer need to register to read journals, view photos or read threads in the site forum. Registration is only required for people who would like to post their own exploration photos and journals in our site. (p.s Registration is free)

    - A new section is opened for registered members to post their own exploration photos and journals. Fancy showing us your foray into Old Changi Hospital? Or did you have some weird experience while walking through the Red House?

    - Site & forum is scaled down for easier navigation.

    - Many more journals on our own exploits and combined outings with our affiliates: Asia Paranormal Investigators (API) and Light Of Atlantis (LOA)

    Watch out for the revolution, come 2007!
