    "I work at a job where I am there during odd hours... really late, really early. I've heard the typical rumours of any job where people say that people have died on the job. I do know of at least one confimred case of someone dying while working this job for my employer. Well, many times working late into the night, I feel as if someone is there...as if someone is watching me as I work.

    One particular night in March 04, as I was putting some finishing touches on a project, I heard movement down a small hall, and smelled something that smelled like singed hair. I looked down the hall to check it out and noticed the light in the room was on when I know for a fact it wasn't on earlier. That creeped me out and tried to take my mind off of it, blaming it on me just being forgetful or a co-worker from earlier in the day leaving it on.

    Well, I put the finishing touches on my assignement and then decided to do a walk around of the place, and when I got to that room, my stomach dropped to my knees... I saw these goddawful footprints. The first thing that popped in my head is that the devil himself was watching me and then turned to leave. Was it a dead employee who lived an impure life? Was it a demon trying to catch me? I don't know, make your own judgments. I have more pictures and can always take more, as the footprints are still there."

    Brad J.
    Beauty, KY
