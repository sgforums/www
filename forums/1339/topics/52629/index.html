    Grave Smile
    from Tonya K. Mooneyham

    "My grandmother died in 2000 and was buried alongside of the son she lost at 5 months old in 1940. This is the first time we took my daughter to the grave site. When I got the pictures developed, I saw this blue spot on the picture. Questions went through my mind as to what it was. It is not a flaw on the film or the picture itself. Staring at it for a moment a face started to appear to me. You can plainly see the eyes, nose, and mouth (smiling). This was the first picture that was taken. It does not appear in the second picture taken. "
