    If you are brave enough, you can go to an isolated and forgotten cemetery in Johor. It is situated about 2 km off track in a secondary jungle. It was believed that most of those buried were victims of the Japanese onslaught just days before they took Singapore during the second world war. Over there was a haunted house about the size of an HDB 2-roomer. Nearby are surrounded by wild banana plant. All you need to do is to tie a red string to the head of a nail that is nailed into some banana plant and with the other end leading to the hut where you are suppose to camp overnight and remain vigil alone. Yes, you should be alone to witness whatever incident that may follow. Carry a camera if you like. Any brave man here to undertake this challenge?
