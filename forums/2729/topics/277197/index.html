    To some extend, what you leaned in Sec 1 and 2 will be in the O level syllabus.

    For Science subject, Secondary 2 syllabus just covers the general information for the 3 area of Science - Chemistry, Biology and Physic.

    Not much use because at O level, you'll be level more indepth.

    For Math subject, Secondary 2 syllabus offers a basic foundation for Algebraic manipulation and Graphs Technique. These two are probably the most useful stuff taught in lower secondary which have direct beneficiary to your upper sec syllabus.

    I am not too sure for Languages and humanities. Hopefully someone can shed some light on this.
