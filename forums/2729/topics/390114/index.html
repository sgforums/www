    The most important things are that whether you have the confidence that you will improve, and the commitment to work extremely hard to ensure that your grades will improve. Lacking either of these two will likely result in similar grades once again ( Touchwood ). 

    I have no idea whether it will be easy to transfer out from RP or not, but I highly doubt that you are the only one with the idea of transferring from one course in a poly to another course in another poly. Thus, if you really wish to enter your desired course, it will be better for you to retake your "O"s. 

    However, if you ended up choosing to enter the diploma course in RP, yet failed to transfer out ( Touchwood again ), I will advice that you work hard and also read up about the routes which you can take from there on. 

    All the best to whichever choice you make.
