    Hi Coldweeliang,

    Please kindly pardon me for saying so, but you have been relaxing a bit too much...

    Having goals is a good thing, but if you are not going to put in ample effort, it is unlikely that your results will improve.

    As for your queries, whether you will have time to study in the army or not depends a lot on your vocation and your unit. It is almost certain that you will enter a mono-intake unit unless you get selected for SCDF or SPF, thus I will say that it is unlikely that you will have enough time to study.

    If you are to retake your "O"-level examinations in the army, you will have to take leave/off while hoping that the examinations do not fall on dates where major exercises are being conducted which involve your unit. Whether your superior will approve your leave/off is another question too.

    As for the Syllabus, I do not think there will be any major changes to it. However, it is best to seek the help of a junior, and perhaps even your secondary school teachers, if possible.

    Cheers.
