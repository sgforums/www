    Maths and Chem is important, especially for accountancy and medicine.

    To go law, good GP skills is required.

    Econs is important also if you want to go into finance and economics.

    Physics is a must if you want to do engineering.

    Actually PCME is a nice option. Opens up a lot of options for you in uni. BCME is more suited for medicine/biochemistry/pharmacology.

    Actually H2 physics is not THAT difficult, but you need plenty of practice. It is much easier than H2 biology for example.

    If you really want to drop physics to H1, I suggest you take up another H3. Chemistry and Economics may be good H3 choices.

    In any case, good luck for your promos! Grit your teeth and it will be over. J2 will come soon enough.
