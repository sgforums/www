    Hi Veit,

    It is possible to get into these JCs, though you will have to get more than two bonus points for two of the JCs.

    The cut-off points for the JCs are:

    3 points for both Science Stream and Arts Stream of HCI (2008)

    3 points for both Science Stream and Arts Stream of RJC (2008)

    4 points for Science Stream and 5 points for Arts Stream of VJC (2009)

    Cheers.
