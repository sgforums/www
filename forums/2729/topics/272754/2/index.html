    Tips for picture conversation, hope this is not too late

    Setting
    - What is going on here(happening)?
    - Who are this people?
    - Where is this place?
    + Time of Day
    + What is the atmosphere like

    People
    - What are they doing?
    - What are the people thinking?
    - How are the people feeling?

    Before/After event
    - What happened before taking the pic
    - What happened after taking the pic

    Significance
    - Social Issues, NE Message etc....

    Note: Some segments need to have X-ref and support by giving your own personal experience and also on one's general knowledge.

    This was given by my EL teacher

    All the best
