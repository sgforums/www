6,000 employees of Certis CISCO Security eligible for union

  • Funnyjunny

    More than 6,000 employees of Certis CISCO Security will be eligible for union membership, with immediate effect.

    This follows a memorandum of understanding between the security firm and the Union of Security Employees (USE).

    The eligible employees represent 60 per cent of Certis' workforce.

    As part of the MOU, a union-management selected committee will be set up to promote the welfare and well-being of Certis CISCO staff who become members of the union.

    This will complement the existing process for handling grievances.

    The committee will be supported by an Industrial Relations Officer of USE.
