    (Right) The factory where the incident took place. (Left) An example of an industrial flour mixer. (For illustration purposes only)

    A bizarre death occurred yesterday evening at a factory that makes food products.

    At around 10.57pm on 18 Feb, a 25-year-old Chinese national worker died after his leg got caught in a large flour mixing machine in the factory located at Defu Lane 3.

    It is unknown how the man's leg got caught but it resulted in him being physically pulled into the machine.

    The man was dead by the time he was successfully pulled out of the machine at least 45 minutes later.

    A spokesperson from the factory declined to comment on the incident.

    Source: Lianhe Wanbao, 19 February 2012. Click here for the Chinese report.
