    • From MyFox National Reports

    A young boy was rescued after getting his head stuck in a storm drain Thursday in Lakeside, Calif. The 7-year-old may have been trying to retrieve a lost toy. Rescue teams used the Jaws of Life to pry the drain apart in order to free the boy.

    The whole ordeal took about 40 minutes. The boy was not injured, however officials had paramedics standing by to examine him.

    The El Cajon, Calif., fire department released the photo to the left of the rescue.
