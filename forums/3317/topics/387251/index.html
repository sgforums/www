    Two 17-year old boys pleaded guilty in a district court today to voluntarily causing hurt to an off-duty policeman. 

    Sanjay Arvind Hoondal and Mosadiq Agustino Musa, both unemployed, had ganged up with two other 17-year old boys to beat up Senior Staff Sergeant Lim Lye Hock. 

    The incident took place at the void deck of Block 418, Tampines Street 12 on the 27th of June last year. 

    Sanjay and Mosadiq will be sentenced on the 27th of this month. 

    Another co-accused, Marcus Jasveer Sandhu pleaded guilty late last month and will be sentenced in two weeks’ time. 

    The fourth member of the group, Muhammad Fazly Shadub will be dealt with separately. 

    If convicted of voluntarily causing hurt, the teenagers could be jailed a maximum of two years and fined a maximum of $5,000.
