    Mark Chow jailed

    FORMER TV actor and model agency owner Mark Chow Yee Sze was jailed for four months and two weeks on Friday for molesting a woman and criminal intimidation.

    The 30-year-old had pleaded guilty earlier to caressing the breast of a then 24-year-old aspiring model inside a unisex toilet of a bar at Gallery Hotel near Robertson Quay on Aug 16, 2006.

    He also admitted to threatening to shoot at three staff members of the Institute of Mental Health while pointing a submachine gun-like object towards them at Buangkok View on Nov 26 last year.

    About five minutes later, he approached a Certis Cisco officer with two pistol-like objects with intent to cause him alarm.

    Four other charges of molestation and one criminal intimidation were taken into consideration during his sentencing.

    Chow, who once managed MC Models International, is now unemployed.

    He was fined $6,000 earlier on April 1 for molesting a former pub operations manager at the office of a downtown pub in January 2007 after a trial.
