    Swedish football fans of fierce rivals Djurgården and AIK had a nasty surprise awaiting them when their teams played in Stockholm on Thursday.

    The game was followed by clashes between fans in the train station, leading to some amazing tactics from the Swedish police.

    In the video below (dug up by 101 Great Goals), you can see the police slowly push shouting fans onto the down escalator, and then turn it up to comically high speeds. By the end, only police are left at the top of the escalator.
