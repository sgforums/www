    6 months' jail for bomb hoax

    A MAN who triggered a state of high alert among police and security forces with a bomb hoax was yesterday jailed for six months.

    Chu Seng Chou, a 37-year-old port operations executive, had, on Feb 26 last year sent an e-mail to the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) with the line 'Train Car and Station have a bomb'.

    No train station was specified.

    An MHA assistant executive, upon seeing the e-mail, alerted the police.

    Sixty police officers were mobilised, and patrols near all train and railway stations and bus companies stepped up.

    The court heard that Chu pulled off the hoax to vent his anger over a delay in the train service the evening before.
