    A FORMER taxi driver was charged in court on Monday with two counts of molesting a teenager.

    Leow Hock Soon, 61, is accused of touching the 18-year-old female inappropriately twice within 10 minutes in the cab along a road of Woodlands Industrial Park E9 on Sep 27 last year.

    Leow, who is out on police bail, will appear in court again on July 18.

    His passport has been impounded.

    If convicted, he faces a jail term of up to two years and/or fine.
