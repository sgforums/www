  • QX179R

    A man, believed to be a 23-year-old Chinese National, was stabbed at Pioneer Mall on Monday (May 16). STOMPer Rel0adingData, who was at Pioneer Mall to buy food, recalls seeing blood stains trailing from McDonald's to a laundry mat.

    According to a news report, the victim was chased by someone holding a knife. Apparently, seven or eight other youths were also involved in the chase. Eyewitnesses describes the youths as an all-male gang, ranging from 15 to 22 years of age, who "looked like gangsters."

    The fight is believed to have started at Block 638, Jurong West Street 61.

    The STOMPer tells us:

    "I was going to Pioneer Mall to get some food when I saw blood stains trailing from McDonald's to the laundry-mat.

    "Two police cars were present, and after asking them what happened, I got some answers.

    "Apparently, someone was stabbed/slashed, and he collapsed right in front of the laundry-mat.

    "From the details I got, it seems like the victim may have been a China National."
