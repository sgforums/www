    KUALA LUMPUR, Malaysia – A Malaysian Cabinet minister has denied allegations that he raped his Indonesian maid four years ago.

    Information Minister Rais Yatim said the allegations circulating on the Internet are part of a campaign to discredit the government ahead of general elections.

    The allegations were made by an Indonesian labor activist group in an internal report that made its way into blog postings.

    The 68-year-old Rais had remained silent but on Wednesday issued a strong denial released through the state news agency, Bernama.

    Without naming anyone, he blamed bloggers and opposition parties for the "ugly and wicked lies."

    -- AP
