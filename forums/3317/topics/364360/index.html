Pump prices up

  • QX179R
    16 Jun 09, 14:36

    Pump prices have gone up for two categories of petrol at Caltex.

    These are the Premium 95 with Techron and Platinum 98 with Techron.

    At noon today, Caltex increased the retail prices of these two grades by 4 cents.

    A litre of 95 grade will now cost $1.679 after discount at Caltex.

    Over at Shell, pump prices increased by 4 cents since yesterday afternoon for their 95 and 98 petrol grades.

    Shell is also selling one litre of its 95 grade at S$1.679 after discount.

    A check with S-P-C found no increase in prices for their range of petrol.

    According to figures on S-P-C's website, one liter of 95 grade will set you back $1.727.
