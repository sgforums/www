    A STOMPer is upset that a teenager and his friends had made rude comments on a suicide case in Ang Mo Kio. According to the STOMPer, the teenager also happens to be a friend of the suicide victim.

    Said the STOMPer:

    "After reading about the A*STAR employee that posted a rude comment on Facebook about the motorcyclist, it seems that some teens are following in his footsteps.

    "This 17-years-old teen posted something rude about a suicide case that happened early morning on 28th April, Wednesday, in Ang Mo Kio.

    "Furthermore, his friend also joined in commenting that it is 'nice' and that it is a 'once in a lifetime opportunity'.

    "I'm a friend of the victim and I hope that by exposing such acts, even by teenagers, it will prevent other teenagers from doing the same thing in future.

    "This is a very disrespectful thing to say.

    "Especially since they are friends (the teenage boy and the victim)."
