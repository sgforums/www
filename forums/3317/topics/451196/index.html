Search on for missing crew member near Marina South Pier

  • QX179R
    24 Apr 12, 11:58

    SINGAPORE: A search and rescue (SAR) operation has been mounted near Marina South Pier for a crew member from a harbour craft who was reported missing at sea.

    The Maritime and Port Authority (MPA) said it received a call about the incident at about 8.30am on Tuesday.

    Two MPA and one Police Coast Guard craft, as well as divers, are involved in the search.

    The MPA has issued navigational broadcasts to vessels in the vicinity to keep clear of the SAR area and to report any sighting of the missing person.

    Operations at Marina South Pier are not affected.
