    "This happened today when I was on board bus number 179 heading to Boon lay Interchange. I was seated at a distance away from them but they were speaking very loudly that I could hear them from five seats away.

    "Although they were conversing in English, I think they are from China judging by their accents and the constant mentions about the system there.

    "These two professors, which they claimed to be while conversing, were criticizing Singaporean students as 'stupid' and slow learners, causing them to be unable teaching the syllabus on time.

    "They were also complaining about the administrative system in NTU, saying that it takes a lot of time to get things done while comparing a similar but faster system in China with that of NTU.

    "It pains me that NTU have such professors who demean the university so openly."
