    KUALA TERENGGANU, MALAYSIA: A Year Two pupil has complained that a stranger bit her private parts after offering her candy and showing her pornographic clips while she was waiting for her mother after school yesterday.

    The eight-year-old girl related the incident to her mother, who lodged a police report at the Cabang Tiga station.

    State CID chief Asst Comm Mohd Fauzi Abduri said the pupil, who attended an evening religious class, told her mother that one 'abang' approached her and asked her to watch a lewd clip that was stored in his cellphone.

    She was then given a candy and lured to an empty classroom where the man took off her underwear and began to caress and bite her private parts, ACP Mohd Fauzi said.

    The girl shrieked before the stranger let her off, he said. A few minutes later, her mother arrived at the school.

    ACP Mohd Fauzi said police believed the perpetrator was in his early 20s.

    He said the victim was sent to Hospital Sultanah Nur Zahirah for a medical check-up.
