900 MRT passengers affected by train breakdown

  • QX179R
    18 Jun 09, 00:05

    SINGAPORE: Some 900 passengers were affected Wednesday afternoon when northbound train services between Woodlands and Marsiling MRT stations were delayed by 20 minutes.

    According to SMRT, this was due to a train fault at about 2.25pm.

    As a result, passengers had to alight from the defective train at Marsiling and the subsequent train behind it at Woodlands.

    Passengers on the affected trains and stations were kept informed through announcements.
