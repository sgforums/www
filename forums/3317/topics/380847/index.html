    Newlywed groom found dead at hotel driveway

    SINGAPORE: A newlywed groom was believed to have fallen to his death just hours after his wedding reception at Hilton Hotel.

    Police said they received a call at about 3.25am on Wednesday that a man was lying motionless outside a hotel in Orchard Road.

    The Chinese man, who was in his early thirties, was clad in T-shirt and shorts. His body was found at the hotel driveway.

    Paramedics pronounced him dead at about 3.35am. He was believed to have fallen from the 24th floor near the hotel pool area.

    Police are investigating the incident.
