    It was a wedding that ended with the police arriving as unexpected special guests.

    They were called in to adjudicate after a couple who held their nuptials at the Hotel Novotel at Clarke Quay on Friday refused to pay the $8,000 balance on their bill.

    Their beef? Smelly fish and fish that was not cooked properly were served to their guests. The couple also claimed poor service from the hotel staff, complaining there were not even enough wine glasses provided for guests to toast the couple.

    According to a Shin Min Daily News report on Saturday, the couple said they tried to negotiate with the hotel's banquet department for a reduced rate, but were unsuccessful. This eventually led to the hotel calling the police to the scene.
