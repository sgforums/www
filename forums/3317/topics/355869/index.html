Accident along Selegie Road

  • QX179R
    14 Apr 09, 13:49

    Police say, nearly 30 people were injured in a traffic accident that took place at the junction of Selegie and Mackenzie Roads this morning.

    The accident involved a tour coach and an SBS bus, service number 56.

    Andrew, who called the news hotline said, the coach was carrying Chinese passengers.

    "There was an SBS bus, and then this tour bus hit the SBS bus from behind. This tour bus has a lot of tourists. And the tourists were on the roadside. A lot of them are injured. Then there are two ambulances. But it is very bad, because all the screws and nuts, everything is on the floor. There are so many helpers, you've got the Civil Defence Force, you've got the police."

    The injured passengers were sent to Alexandra, the Singapore General and Tan Tock Seng Hospitals

    Police say, they received a call about the accident shortly after 10 this morning.

    They are investigating the case.
