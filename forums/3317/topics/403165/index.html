Poly holidays not family-friendly

  • wmx6296102
    22 Jun 10, 15:51

    I AM a secondary school teacher and a mother of two teens, both studying in different polytechnics.

    The term vacations of polytechnics vary and often do not coincide with those of primary and secondary schools as well as junior colleges. This makes it difficult to plan a family vacation together.

    For instance, my daughter's next long vacation includes a week of tests running right through the same September week-long break as secondary schools (Sept 4 to 12). So there is no way we can plan a holiday in that week. Similarly, my son has semestral examinations beginning Sept 6 for the next two weeks.

    I am sure there are other families who face the same problem. Even though my kids are not young, we treasure our family vacations.

    Much has been said about the importance of families spending time together. Can the polytechnics work together and plan some of the term vacations to coincide with those of primary and secondary schools as well as JCs?

    Cindy Soon (Mrs)
