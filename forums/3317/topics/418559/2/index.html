    Aliff, you've hit 'all-time low'

    REACTIONS of straitstimes.com readers to jailed footballer Aliff Shafaein's call for fans to help him settle $10,500 worth of traffic fines came fast and furious.

    And they echoed the sentiments of S-League fans when the story broke on Wednesday.

    'What an attitude', wrote Tan Yong. He added: 'He should wake up from his 'soccer star' dreams and face reality. He made his own bed, and he should rightfully lie in it.'

    Some even suggested Aliff resign and try a different profession. Vincent Ong asked: 'Aliff, time to hang up your boots for good? Try your speeding skills on the F1 pro circuit.'

    Bennie Tay chided: 'Shame on you for even considering getting others to pay for your crime! Spend the extra 53 days inside and do some soul searching.'

    But Ridzuan Osman had the final say, arguing that Aliff's request was too much after what he had done.

    'I thought he was a nice guy and probably deserved another chance, but this is definitely an all-time low. Time to go and hang up your boots.'

    -- ST
