KTM Train Derailed At Bukit Timah

  • ^Acid^ aka s|aO^eH~

    Sauce: Stomp

    A KTM passenger train leaving for Malaysia from the Bukit Timah station was derailed at about 8am this morning (Nov 9). No one was injured.

    The train was derailed 200m away from the Bukit Timah KTM station on its way to Malaysia. Passengers were reported as merely feeling a 'bump' and no one was injured.

    The KTM technical staff are on-site and the train is being towed to the Tanjong Pagar station.
