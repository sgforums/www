Tycoon's wife to get $41m

  • QX179R
    2 May 10, 10:26

    HE was just a shipping clerk who became a self-made millionaire.

    She was his homemaker wife through their 49 years of marriage. They have four grown-up children.

    But Mr Yeo Chong Lin, 73, and Madam Nancy Tay, 71, split in July 2005 on grounds of his adultery.

    Now, a High Court judge has ordered that Madam Tay should get $40.79 million, or 35 per cent, of Mr Yeo's assets. Justice Judith Prakash estimated his worth to be more than $115 million. The judgment also took into account her assets of $860,000.

    Her judgment was released last Monday, and Mr Yeo must pay his ex-wife within six months.

    When contacted last night, Mr Yeo said he will appeal, and expressed concern that the six-month deadline might require him to liquidate assets to come up with the cash.
