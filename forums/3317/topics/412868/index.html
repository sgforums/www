    A STOMPer said a female Chinese national, who was apparently walking around naked in her HDB flat, locked a boy up in her flat after accusing him of peeping at her.

    Said the STOMPer in an email to STOMP today (Oct 11):

    "A 12-year-old boy was detained by a naked PRC lady in her HDB apartment for hours, only to be released upon the arrival of the police.

    "The incident took place at around noon yesterday (Oct 10) at Block 898A, Woodlands Drive 50.

    "According to a witness to the incident, the lady was walking around the house naked with the main door left open.

    "The 12 year-old boy was running along the corridor playing with two of his friends in front of her unit when the naked lady came out of the house and grabbed the boy’s arm, accusing him of peeping at her.

    "The young boy tried to break loose from her grip in vain and was forcibly dragged into her apartment, as she slammed the main door behind her. She was heard hurling expletives at the boy behind closed doors.

    "Neighbours who arrived at the unit after hearing the commotion tried to pacify the lady and urged her to let the boy go but to no avail.

    "The lady insisted that the boy had broken the law by peeping at her naked body and therefore should be punished.

    "One of the neighbours who was at the unit warned her that it is against the law of Singapore to hold the boy against his will but she insisted that she had done nothing wrong. She refused to open the door and shouted at the neighbours to go away and that she would deal with him her own way.

    "The police was called in subsequently and the lady released the boy after much persuasion from the police officer. The police officer then took her statement and left the scene.

    "The parent of the boy was not at the scene throughout the whole incident.

    "It is not known if Police will be taking any action against the lady for her indecency or her wrongful confinement of the young boy."
