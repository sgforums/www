    A shop at Raffles City Shopping Centre has lost two bottles of wine worth a total of $320 to a thief.

    The shop's supervisor had been informed by his superior yesterday that the two bottles were missing.

    CCTV footage showed a man acting suspiciously in the outlet the day before at about 9.30am.

    The man was wearing a long sleeved white shirt with a dark coloured tie and dark pants.

    He was carrying a black bag and also a shopping bag, into which he placed two wine bottles.

    The footage shows him leaving the shop later carrying only his black bag.

    He had not made any payment before leaving.

    Police are investigating the case.
