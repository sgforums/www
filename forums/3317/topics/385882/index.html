    Charged with spiking coffee

    AN UNEMPLOYED woman was charged on Thursday with spiking the coffee of a man so as to steal from him.

    Vicky Herman Quek Bee Lan, 44, allegedly put sleeping pills into a cup of coffee which was consumed by Mr Lim Yang Hua, 61, a retiree, at the void deck of Block 2C Upper Boon Keng Road on Monday.

    She is believed to be involved in similar cases and was remanded for a week for further investigation.

    She will be back in court on Dec 31.

    If convicted, she faces a jail term of up to 10 years and a fine.
