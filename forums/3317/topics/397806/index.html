    TWO teenage girls assaulted and molested a 15-year-old girl after accusing her of saying bad things about them, a court heard.

    Siti Aidah Shaharudin, 19, admitted in a Community Court on Wednesday to punching, slapping and kicking the victim together with her accomplice, then 15, at a flat in Hougang on Jan 31, 2007.

    Community Court judge Ng Peng Hong postponed sentencing pending probation and reformative training reports on May 19.
