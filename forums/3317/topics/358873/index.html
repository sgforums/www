COE prices rise across the board

  • QX179R
    6 May 09, 19:18

    SINGAPORE : COE prices were higher in all categories in the latest bidding exercise which ended on Wednesday.

    The biggest rise was seen in the COE price for Category A (below 1600cc) - up by S$900 to S$8,489.

    COE price for cars above 1600cc was up a mere S$62 to S$7,552, while those in the Open Category jumped S$712 to S$8,501.

    In the Goods Vehicles and Bus Category, the COE price was up S$490 to S$7,100.

    Motorcycle COE rose S$13 to S$902.
