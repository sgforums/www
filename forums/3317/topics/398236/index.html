Marina Bay Sands welcomes Sinkapore.com

  • Magyar
    3 May 10, 14:59

     

    Marina Bay Sands welcomes the launch of – Sinkapore.com – Share and Discover what is Happening in Singapore

    What is Sinkapore.com?

    Sinkapore.com is a blog owned by Amos Atlantis Group and Alliance Atlantis Media.

    Sinkapore.com will publish topics ranging from citizen journalism to what’s new in Singapore. These topics consists of citizen journalism (content that is provided by people like you), Singapore’s latest attractions and icons, hot babes and hunks, fashion shows, politics, interesting happenings and news, hot debate and discussions, what’s with Singapore youths, what’s going on in trains and buses and many more…

    Alliance Atlantis Media will be setting up a new office at Marina Bay Financial Centre in the third quarter of 2010 when it’s completed.

    We intend to do more than what STOMP.com.sg is currently doing. We’ll bring 10 professional photographers from Canada to Singapore. They’ll be going around every corner of Singapore to take photos of funny and interesting happenings and sights that will attract the attention of tons of readers, for example – Taxi driver strips naked in the middle of Orchard Road

    Within this few years, starting from the new decade of 2010, we’ll take citizen journalism to a whole new level. We’ll recruit potential citizen journalists like you through social media etc. Together, we can make Singapore a nation of citizen journalists.

    Citizen journalism can help create awareness and help to promote businesses through means of publicity and social media. Starting from this very moment, if you have anything you think that would attract readers attention, do not hesitate to contact us. We’ll publish your content right here on Sinkapore.com for Singapore and the world to enjoy.

    You don’t have to be a Singaporean or live in Singapore to participate in this exciting adventure and revolution of the internet. As long as you’re a human being living on planet Earth, you’re utmost welcomed!

    Let’s encourage all Singaporeans and the world to be citizen journalists. Together, we can make a difference and spice up the internet!
