    Man murdered at Toa Payoh Lorong 1

    http://sg.news.yahoo.com/blogs/singaporescene/man-murdered-toa-payoh-lorong-1-144420034.html

    A 47-year-old Chinese man was murdered at block 128 Toa Payoh Lorong 1 on Wednesday evening.

    Police told Yahoo! Singapore that they were informed of the incident at about 7.54pm and deployed assistance to the location.

    They found the man lying injured at the void deck of the block. He was sent to Tan Tock Seng Hospital but died on Thursday morning.

    Police immediately mounted operations to establish the identity of the assailant and swiftly arrested a 50-year-old male Singaporean in connection with the case.

    Investigations are ongoing.
