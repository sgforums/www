  • XD2229P

    STOMPer Lim strongly believes while there is a difference between Singlish and bad English, the usage of both should be banned here.

    She recounted two separate incidents to illustrate her point.

    “This is indeed true and sad to find that there are still many people out there who can’t distinguish ‘Singlish’ and ‘English’. 

    “Many people got it wrong about Singlish when they thought that it is the accent that makes it Singlish. 

    “It is actually the grammar and sentences structure.  Many adult takes like lightly and not knowing that this would hurt their kids in composition essays. 

    “Hence, I urge all adults to be more mindful and alert in this area and stop imparting them to the next generation.

     
    “I was at a Clinic last night and there is a woman who said this to her son:”Ask your dad to run fast, our number is going to be reached.”  Do you call this English or Singlish? 

    “That is the first time I hear this sentence.  I learned from the Americans to express in a more direct manner.  I would say: ‘Get your daddy here now, it’s our turn’. 

    “I thought the issue is not about running to the door or running slowly or faster or the number going to be reached, it is our turn.

     
    “After my turn was over, I went to the hawker stalls and there was a family speaking Singlish saying: ‘What you want to call?’ to his children. 

    “Why not say: ‘Go order your food’ if the kids are ordering themselves, or  ‘What is your pick tonight?’ if the adult is ordering for them.


    “Both incidents showed that many educated adults were speaking and teaching Singlish to their children. 

    “They have difficulties in compositing sentence that make good senses.

    “This could also be a sign that their working environment is filled with people speaking Singlish as well. 

    “If you were in an environment where people speak English, you would have felt inferior and mend it and maintained your language competency and also bring it home.

    “The use of Singlish should be banned."
