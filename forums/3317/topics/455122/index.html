The offences occurred from February to June after Wong got to know the 15-year-old girl through a church choir. They chatted regularly online; she would address him as "daddy", while he would call her "daughter". The offences were discovered in August last year after the girl's teacher noticed her poor school performance and called her father. The girl then told her father what happened and a police report was made. Wong, who pleaded guilty on June 25, apologised on Wednesday in Court for what he had done.
