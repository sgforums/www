    A half-naked woman was spotted at Mohamed Sultan Road as she chased after four men and then got beaten up by them. She had apparently had her modesty outraged by the group and her belongings stolen.

    The woman, who looked to be in her 20s, was wearing only a top and a bra that was pulled down, exposing her breasts. She was holding her pants and panties as she chased after four men. At least one other woman was running behind the half-naked woman.

    According to reports in The Straits Times and The New Paper, it started with an argument between a group of three women and four men who approached them.

    It is believed that during the exchange, the four men outraged the modesty of at least two of the women in the group, before making off with a mobile phone and a wallet that belonged to the victims.

    An eyewitness, who snapped a photo of the incident, said the half-naked woman then gave chase but was attacked by the group of men, who pushed her to the ground, punched and kicked her. The fight was only broken up after the eyewitness, a 16-year-old, shouted that the police were in the vicinity.

    Police said they received a call at 5.25am on Monday (May 7) requesting assistance at Mohamed Sultan Road. Four men were arrested in connection with the incident -- two of them 23, and the other two 22 and 35 years old.
