    Young Singaporean girls still do not seem to care about the repercussions of displaying their open behaviour online, as another case has surfaced, about a girl from a polytechnic who offers 'freelance services' and has a preference for 'sugardaddies' below 40 years old.

    According to the online ad, the girl provides 'topless sensual massage' and other 'special services'.

    She says she used to work as a model and is providing the services to pay for her studies and to pay for her mother's medical treatment.

    STOMPer Modestyeest, who said he received the link to the online advertisement via email, said:

    "I was shocked to see that some of our young polytechnic girls (19 to 22) are openly soliciting on the Internet without any qualms or an uneasy conscience.

    "I am not sure if their parents are aware of what they are doing on the quiet.

    "They even pose semi nude in their websites and they declare to all and sundry that their families are in financial difficulties.

    "If this trend is on the rise then the poly authorities should look into this very serious problem of their young students."

    This latest report came just after STOMP published the case of another polytechnic girl exposing her chest with her boyfriend and posting the pictures on her blog.

    These two cases are just some of the numerous reports STOMP has received in the past few months, highlighting the worrying trend of youths openly displaying their misbehaviour online.

    Other similar cases reported to STOMP by concerned netizens include underageds teens smoking, shoplifting and even describing their sex life in detail for all to read.
