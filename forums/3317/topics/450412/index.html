    A teacher, 47, has accused a KTV hostess of cheating her husband of over $100,000 and sending him lewd text messages. She is now left with only $300 to support her two children.

    The KTV hostess allegedly sent the teacher's husband lewd SMSes, asking to meet at the 'old place'.

    According to the teacher, who was only identified as Madam Tan, her husband knew the woman in August 2011 and started borrowing heavily from the banks and had incurred credit card debts of more than $100,000 after that.

    The man also stopped giving Madam Tan a monthly allowance of $500, and even started borrowing money from her. The affair was only discovered when Madam Tan chanced upon the SMSes sent by the KTV hostess.

    Madam Tan told reporters she is now left with $300 in her bank account. She discovered this when she went to withdraw money after one of the banks applied a Court Order to freeze the family's assets unless Madam Tan's husband pays up $700.

    Madam Tan has decided not to file a divorce with her husband for the sake of their two children. She was also beaten up by him when she confronted him about it, said the report.
