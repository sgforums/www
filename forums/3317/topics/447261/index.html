    Two junior college boys shared a quick peck on the lips in the school canteen as a dare after being egged on by a schoolmate. Pictures and even a video was taken, and on the same day, the students involved were hauled off to the principal's office on Feb 8.

    According to a report in The New Paper today, the student who had promised to shave his head bald if his schoolmates did it was handed a letter for his parents to sign, acknowledging his withdrawal from the school. The boys who had shared the kiss were handed suspensions.

    STOMPer Gary, who read about the incident was surprised but not shocked. He said:

    "I think the school was too harsh on its students. It's just a prank, for goodness sake. They weren't committing murder or theft.

    "It was just a simple peck on the lips that only lasted for a few seconds -- but a fleeting moment.

    "Actually a similar incident happened last time in my junior college. So I'm not surprised by this news."

    STOMPer Sally also shared her views:

    "I'm glad they were punished. We cannot encourage this kind of behaviour in a place for learning. It's a school, not your home!

    "If students are so daring and they take this kind of thing so lightly, they should be educated -- hence punished -- so that they will realise.

    "What they did was just stupid. It's not funny at all. Who knows what they will be capable of later in life?

    "And why do kids nowadays love to record everything, be it on video or pictures? They are just incriminating themselves, even if they don't post it online.

    "I'm glad to read in the news report that the student who egged the boys on is repentant for what he did."
