    Sewage leak at ION Orchard affects jewellery stores on B2

    SINGAPORE: A sewage leak at ION Orchard Tuesday left the mall's basement two's annex area soiled.

    The annex area consists of a row of nine jewellery shops - called Jewellery Street - which was shut off to shoppers.

    The mall's management has traced the leak to a clogged pipe in their internal drainage system.

    The leak apparently occurred at about 5pm and the area is being cleaned up.
