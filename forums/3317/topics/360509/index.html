Four people died in early morning accident

  • QX179R
    18 May 09, 12:40

    An accident involving a lorry and a trailer along Gul Road early this morning left four foreign workers dead.

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force, SCDF said as a result of the collision, the front passenger’s cabin of the lorry was crushed.

    SCDF used hydraulic spreader and cutter and 2 winches to free the front seat passenger from the wreckage.

    The man, who was already unconscious, was later pronounced dead by paramedics.

    Two passengers at the rear of the lorry were also pronounced dead at the scene.

    Another died later at the National University Hospital.

    Six other workers on the lorry were injured and sent to NUH.
