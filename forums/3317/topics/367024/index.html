    EZ-Link introduces season pass for public transport users

    Frequent users of public transport may want to grab hold of the new EZ-Link season pass.

    The pass which went on sale today allows them unlimited bus and train rides at a fixed price.

    The price ranges from $45 a week for 4 train rides and unlimited bus travel daily to $190 monthly for unlimited bus and train trips.

    Until 31st October however, EZ-Link is offering a promotional discount on the Basic Season Pass.

    The EZ-Link Season Pass is an extension of the new-generation Ez-link card which is CEPAS compliant that went on sale in December.

    Like any other regular adult Ez-link card, the Season Pass can be used for other micro payments.

    EZ-Link has also tied up with an insurer to provide insurance coverage on the first time loss or theft of the Season Pass.
