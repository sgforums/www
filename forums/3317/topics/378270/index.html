    Total government land sale revenue falls 41% on year to S$7.3 billion in FY2008/09

    The Singapore Land Authority or SLA says the total land sale revenue for its 2008-2009 fiscal year stood at 7.3 billion dollars. 

    That's a 41 percent decrease from the 12.4 billion dollars it earned over the previous year. 

    Releasing its annual report today, SLA says the top seller was the Atrium@Orchard grade A office building. 

    The property garnered 840 million dollars for the government. 

    As the recession set in and the government withdrew land sales, SLA's operating income also saw an 8 percent decline to 92.5 million dollars. 

    But the utilisation rate of state land is at a 4-year high of 79 percent, up from last year's 77.8 percent.
