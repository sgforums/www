    S'porean involved in accident in Phuket still in critical condition

    SINGAPORE: A 20-year-old Singaporean who was involved in a jet ski accident last Friday while on holiday in Phuket, Thailand, is still in critical condition.

    It's believed Mr Loh Ying Jie was riding a jet ski off Patong Beach when he hit by one of his friends on another jet ski.

    After undergoing an operation to remove a blood clot, Mr Loh was flown back to Singapore and admitted into the Singapore General Hospital on Sunday night.

    An SGH spokesperson says Mr Loh is still on ventilation support.
