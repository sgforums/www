Bogus cop molester charged

  • QX179R
    28 Nov 10, 12:50

    A 21-year-old man has been charged with making a 12-year-old girl perform a sexual act on him.

    Jafny Mohamed Sunny allegedly committed the crime at a lift landing at a block of flats in the east at around 1pm on 8 October. 

    If convicted, he can be jailed up to 20 years and receive at least 12 strokes of the cane. 

    It’s also believed Jafny had been molesting several other women in the neighbourhood since late September by posing as a Police officer. 

    He allegedly pretended to check his victims for items such as drugs before molesting them. 

    Jafny is also believed to have flashed fake passes at the women to convince them that he was a police officer. 

    The slim, tanned young man, who’s unrepresented, will be remanded at the Central Police Division. 

    He will be back in court next Friday.
