    SINGAPORE : A molest victim followed her alleged attacker around, leading to his arrest on Saturday.

    The police on Sunday said they arrested a 22-year-old illegal immigrant who is believed to have molested at least two women at Holland Drive.

    They said that at about 9am on Saturday, a 32-year-old woman spotted a man at the wet market there whom she recognised as the one who had molested her, about a week ago, at the wet market.

    She called the police as she trailed him but lost him near Block 44 Holland Drive.

    Officers from Clementi Police Division arrived and arrested him near Block 2 Holland Avenue at about 4pm.

    He will be charged in court on Monday with outrage of modesty and can be jailed up to 10 years and be caned.

    He will also be charged under the Immigration Act, and can be jailed up to six months and be given at least three strokes of the cane.
