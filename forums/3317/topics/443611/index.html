Staff charged with stealing duty-free items on SIA plane

  • QX179R
    15 Dec 11, 17:56

    SINGAPORE: Ground equipment staff were charged Thursday with five counts of stealing duty-free items onboard a Singapore Airlines plane.

    Glenn Mejia Santos, 35, along with Rene Boy Catipon Frani, 30, committed the offences on five occasions over two months from October 17 this year.

    They allegedly stole watches, pens, jewellery, and perfume worth more than S$8,000 in all.

    Both will be back in court on December 22.

    If convicted for theft, they could each face up to three years' jail and a fine on each charge.
