Singapore's "Father of Counselling" Anthony Yeo dies

  • QX179R
    21 Jun 09, 22:06

    SINGAPORE : Singapore's "Father of Counselling" Anthony Yeo died on Saturday from complications as a result of his leukaemia.

    The 60-year-old veteran counsellor appeared on Channel NewsAsia's "Talking Point" in February to talk about mental health during a recession.

    In May, Mr Yeo was diagnosed with Burkitt's lymphoma, cancer of a type of white blood cell that fights off body infection.

    Even though it went into remission after chemotherapy, he developed complications, including a lung infection and pneumonia.

    Mr Yeo will be cremated on Monday.

    A memorial service will be held next month.
