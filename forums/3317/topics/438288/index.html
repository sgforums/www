    A man who hid in a store's fitting room to film a woman changing in the next cubicle was sentenced to three weeks' jail on Tuesday.

    Ong Woon Yong, 23, admitted to insulting the modesty of the 30-year-old woman at Cotton On outlet at Ang Mo Kio Hub on Oct 18, 2010.

    A court heard that the supervisor of the outlet became suspicious when Ong stayed in the fitting room for a long period.

    She went to the cubicle beside Ong's and peeked from under the partition. She saw him using his mobile phone to film the victim who was in the fitting room on the other side. She detained Ong and called the police.
