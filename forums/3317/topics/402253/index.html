    KUALA LUMPUR: A couple, who had just arrived at their condominium after work, was brutally shot dead by two men.

    It was learnt that the couple, who died at the scene due to severe head injuries, worked at a night club in the city.

    The unidentified 32-year-old female driver had just parked in the basement car park of Palm Villa condominium in Taman Maluri, Cheras.

    It was learnt that she had just turned off the engine when two men approached them at 3.30am yesterday.

    The suspects then opened fire from close range before fleeing with their jewellery and belongings.

    The male victim, identified as KC Wong, 52, was found in the passenger’s seat.

    Residents alerted the police when they heard shots being fired.

    City deputy CID chief Asst Comm Khairi Arasa said when police arrived at the scene, they found the two dead in a pool of blood in their Honda CRV.

    He said police also found 13 bullets at the scene, adding that the victims had five gunshot wounds.

    Closed-circuit television footage at the condominium showed the men lingering around the condominium, apparently waiting for the victims.

    “The footage also showed that the suspects escaped by climbing over the fence of the condominium,” he said at the scene.

    Police are investigating if the motive was robbery or revenge.

    The bodies have been sent to the Hospital Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia for a post-mortem - Malaysia The Star 12/06/2010
