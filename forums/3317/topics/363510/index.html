Murder at Blk 89 Redhill Close

  • QX179R
    10 Jun 09, 13:50

    A 46-year-old Indian man was killed in a murder case which took place at Redhill Close last night.

    At about 9.40pm yesterday, police were informed that there was an injured person at the open carpark of Blk 89.

    Upon their arrival, the Indian man was found with stab wounds to his upper body.

    He's believed to have been involved in a fight earlier on with two Chinese men.

    The victim was conveyed to the Singapore General Hospital where he eventually succumbed to his injuries at around 11pm.

    Police have since established the identities of the two Chinese men who were involved in the fight with the deceased.

    Efforts are ongoing to track down the two suspects.

    If you have any information on this case, do call the Police Hotline at 1800-255-0000.
