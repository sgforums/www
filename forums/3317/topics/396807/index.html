    A STOMPer was shocked when some people suggested Holly Ho become a prostitute in Geylang on her Facebook page. In addition, some of them used obscenities on each other.

    Singaporean Holly Ho first rose to notoriety among the internet community when she asked people to give 10 cents each, so that she could be a millionaire by the time she was 20. Read the whole story here.

    In an email today (Apr 17) the upset STOMPer wrote:

    "Users were advising Holly Ho on what she could do to make a living. To our surprise, there are actually brats and kids commenting that she should go be a prostitute.

    "I know they were only joking but I still commented and argued for them to respect women.

    "There was also a JC graduate who worked in a real estate company. I was quite proud of his achievements until he showed off all his vulgarities.

    "I warned him not to post any more vulgarities as it might affect his reputation and reflects his morality, but he still scolded another user and myself with rough vulgarities.

    "I know everyone gets fed up sometimes and turns angry, but he used vulgarities on users who tried to tell him off. I did not even scold him as much as he did other users.

    "I just laugh at his logic and want to tell him that having a certificate doesn't mean that you necessarily earn more money.


    "I have consulted the police and they advised me to lay a Magistrate's complaint. Moreover, I advised one of the witness's father to write to his school and am still waiting for the school's reply on this ex student of theirs.

    "It seems some educated JC graduates lack moral education."
