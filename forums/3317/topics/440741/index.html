    SINGAPORE: A man has been arrested for trespassing into a female toilet and taking a photograph of a woman from outside a cubicle.

    The 36-year-old suspect was detained at Block 55 Ayer Rajah Crescent on Saturday with the help of two members of the public.

    Police said the victim, a 31-year-old woman, was in a cubicle inside the female restroom when she saw a flash light coming from outside the cubicle.

    After leaving the toilet, she informed her colleagues who spotted the suspect behaving suspiciously near the female toilet.

    When confronted, the suspect fled. Two passers-by managed to chase the suspect and detained him.

    The suspect was handed over to the Police and placed under arrest.
