    Spotted: Transport Minister taking the train

    AsiaOne
    Mon, May 30, 2011

    TRANSPORT Minister Lui Tuck Yew was spotted taking the train during the morning rush hour on Monday, according to photos sent in by a contributor to citizen-journalism website Stomp.

    This is the second time he was spotted on public transport. On May 25, Mr Lui was photographed in a public bus, sparking questions as to whether he was testing the transport system, or if it was staged.

    The reader who contributed the photos to Stomp said: "I snapped pictures of him on the train this morning at 7.45am, on the North South line heading towards Jurong East at Bukit Gombak. 

    "Welcome to the crowd!"

    Transport Minister spotted on public transport
    Click on thumbnail to view (Photos: Stomp)

    From the photos sent in, Minister Lui had to stand as the train appeared somewhat crowded.

    Judging from the position of the hand rails, he was quite close to the MRT train doors.

    The Minister was also seen holding a copy of a newspaper (My Paper), he was photographed not reading it and it looked like he was more intent on observing the surroudings and train stops.
