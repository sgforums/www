    Lower gas tariffs for Singapore households from Feb to Apr

    SINGAPORE: The gas tariff for households will decrease by 3.8 per cent or 0.67 cent per kilowatt hour (kWh), from Feb 1 to April 30, City Gas announced on Thursday (Jan 28). This means the tariff will be 16.80 cents per kWh.

     

    The reduction is due to a 16 per cent drop in fuel costs compared to the previous quarter.

     

    City Gas reviews the gas tariffs based on guidelines set by the Energy Market Authority (EMA) and the new rates have been approved by the gas industry regulator.
