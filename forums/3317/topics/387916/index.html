I did not start smoking after that, i though i can control the influence form others and not smoke. A few years later, when i was in poly i slowly become a social smoker, a few of my friends smoke and when we go pub and club, i often take a stick from them. However, it was before i turn 18, i decided to buy alcohol and cig before being legal for the last time, that was the time i buy my 1st packet of cig. This 1st pack kick off a start and turn me into a smoker, at 1st it was itchy hand and itchy mouth, then it turns to smoking finish the pack before it turns bad. So this is how i began the youth smoking journey.
