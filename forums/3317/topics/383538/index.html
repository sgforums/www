    5-year-old girl killed in car accident at Punggol

    SINGAPORE: A five-year-old girl died in an accident at Punggol on Tuesday evening.

    The fatal accident took place at Edge Field Plains opposite Block 126A.

    Police said they received a call at around 8.20pm about an accident involving a car and a pedestrian.

    Paramedics pronounced the girl dead at 8.35pm.

    The car's 27-year-old driver has been arrested for allegedly causing the girl's death.

    Witnesses can call the police hotline at 1800-547-1818.
