More than 800,000 households to get utilities rebates

  • QX179R
    1 Jan 16, 22:46

    SINGAPORE: More than 800,000 Singaporean HDB households will receive the next instalment of utilities rebates from this month, the Finance Ministry said in a statement issued on Friday (Jan 1). 

     

    Depending on their flat size, eligible Singapore households will each receive a GST Voucher – Utilities-Save (U-Save) of between S$45 and S$65. The GST Voucher – U-Save is distributed every four months, with the next instalment to be paid out in April. 

     

    In its statement, the Finance Ministry said: “Annually, those living in 1- and 2-room HDB flats will be able to offset about 3 to 4 months of utilities bills on average with their regular GST Voucher – U-Save. Those living in 3- to 4-room HDB flats can offset about 1 to 2 months of utilities bills on average.”

     

    Introduced by the Government in Budget 2012 to help lower- and middle-income households with their expenses, the GST Voucher – U-Save provides HDB households with a rebate to offset their utilities bills, and is paid every January, April, July and October.
