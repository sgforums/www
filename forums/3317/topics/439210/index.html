Anita Mui's jewellery fetches US$200,000 at HK auction

  • dragg

    HONG KONG: An auction of watches and jewellery from the estate of the late Hong Kong singer-actress Anita Mui fetched US$873,000 - double than what was expected.

    One of the highlights of the collection is a pearl and diamond necklace with matching earrings, which drew the highest bid of some US$200,000 at the Sotheby's auction.

    Carved with gold feathers and encrusted with 16-carats worth of rose-cut diamonds, the singer had worn it on-stage during her final concert appearance in 2003.

    The Cantopop diva died in 2003 after a battle with cervical cancer.

    The proceeds of auction will be used to fund monthly payments to Mui's mother and for management and maintenance of her estate.
