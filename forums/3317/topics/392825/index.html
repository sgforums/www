    Two cases of men dressed as women in public places have made STOMPers stop and stare. One was sporting a moustache and a dress, and the other a bright pink figure-hugging leotard.

    STOMPer Amie was taken aback by the sight of this man dressed in a tight pink dress strutting his stuff at City Square Mall. To top it off, the man appeared to have forgotten to shave off his moustache.

    Amie describes the sight:

    "On Friday (Mar 5) at around 7:00 pm, my boyfriend and I went to the new City Square Mall for dinner.

    "We spotted an old man in his 60s dressing like a woman. He was standing outside a shop selling ladies shoes on the second floor for quite some time. Then he approached the sales staff and started chatting happily with them.

    "We believe he must be a regular customer there. He was wearing a very tight short pink dress with black stockings and high heels. However, he was not wearing any wig to cover his short grey hair, there was also no make-up on his face and his tummy was bulging out.

    "The best part is that he don't even bother to shave off his moustache!

    "He seemed to enjoy the attention he got from everyone, including the security guards.

    "We lost our appetite and left the mall without having our dinner."
