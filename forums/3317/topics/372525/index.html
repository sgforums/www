    A 41-year old Indian national was found dead in the Singapore River in the wee hours of this morning.

    Police say, they received a call about a man falling into the water near Eu Tong Sen Street at around 12.45am.

    Officers from the Singapore Civil Defence Force conducted a search of the river.

    The body was recovered near the Swissotel Merchant Court Hotel about two hours later.

    It was found at a depth of about five metres, some four metres away from the river bank.

    The man was pronounced dead at 2.42am.

    Police have classified the case as an unnatural death.
