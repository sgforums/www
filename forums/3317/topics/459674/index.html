    SINGAPORE: Non-constituency Member of Parliament (NCMP) Lina Chiam plans to ask about the MP's role in approving the use of community spaces like void decks, at the upcoming Parliament sitting.

    A post on the Singapore People's Party Facebook page said Mrs Chiam has tabled a question for the parliamentary sitting on 16 October.

    Mrs Chiam's move follows Potong Pasir MP Sitoh Yih Pin's announcement on Tuesday that the void deck of Block 108 on Potong Pasir Avenue 1 will soon be occupied by a voluntary welfare organisation.

    The void deck was used by former Potong Pasir MP Chiam See Tong for his Meet-the-People's sessions.
