Boys climb on top of sports car & post video on Facebook

  • XD2229P
    22 Feb 10, 11:26

    A STOMPer came across a video on Facebook, apparently taken by two boys who filmed themselves climbing on top of a sports car at a carpark.

    The STOMPer said the boys are from the Singapore Sports School, based on their Facebook profiles and the photos they posted.

    The sports car in the video seems to be a Porsche.

    Said the STOMPer:

    "I don't have any idea when did this incident took place or if they did anything else at the carpark.

    "But perhaps sports car owners at the carpark might unknowingly have these kids' footprints on their cars."

    According to the comments on the Facebook account, the boys pulled the daring stunt after a friend's birthday party.

    Friends of the two boys had also commented on their daring stunt.

    While some called it 'stupid', 'brainless' and 'uncivilised', most seemed to give the thumbs up.

    One friend even thought that the stunt was not a big deal and that different 'people have fun differently'.

    One of the culprits seemed smug about the whole incident, even bragging that there will be a 'next time'.
