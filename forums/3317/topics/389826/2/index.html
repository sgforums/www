    Hearse of the late Ng Teng Fong makes its way past Far East Plaza

    SINGAPORE: The hearse of the Singapore's richest man, the late Ng Teng Fong was driven past Far East Plaza on Saturday afternoon.

    Mr Ng died on Tuesday afternoon after suffering a brain haemorrhage on January 23.

    The procession which started from his home at Watten Estate made its way to the Orchard Road area.

    Staff from Far East Organisation gathered outside Far East Plaza to pay their last respects.

    The late Mr Ng had set up Far East Organization in 1960 and by the 1980s, he was known as the "King of Orchard Road".

    Over the years, the property giant developed over 700 malls, condominiums and hotels in Singapore and Hong Kong. - CNA/vm
