Baby boy abandoned at AMK bus stop

  • QX179R
    4 Jul 10, 14:10

    A four-month-old baby boy was found abandoned at a bus stop yesterday afternoon.

    The baby was left in his stroller with a red blanket and some pieces of cloth. There was also a green purse containing money and a passport-size photograph.

    A woman passer-by spotted him and watched the little one for over an hour, before raising the alarm.

    The police said they were alerted at about 3.45pm.

    An hour later, a woman, believed to be the baby's mother, a 34-year-old, was arrested two bus stops away on the other side of the road.

    Police are investigating.

    Child abandonment carries a jail term of up to seven years and/or a fine.

    A spokesman for the Ministry of Community Development, Youth and Sports told The New Paper that 10 babies were abandoned from 2007 to September last year.

    -- TNP
