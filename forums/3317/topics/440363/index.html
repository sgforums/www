MOH investigates TB cases in Bedok Town Secondary School

  • QX179R
    25 Oct 11, 19:16

    SINGAPORE: The Ministry of Health is advising residents living near Bedok Town Secondary School to see a doctor if they have a cough or are feeling unwell.

    The reminder comes after the latest case of tuberculosis was discovered in a student of Bedok Town Secondary School.

    The patient is the third case from the school this year.

    MOH says tuberculosis can spread in a community and it is hard to trace.

    It says early detection is essential in fighting the infection

    The ministry's Tuberculosis Control Unit is screening students and teachers who have had close contact with the girl, and is also investigating if the three cases are linked.
