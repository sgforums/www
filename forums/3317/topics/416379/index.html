    It read: "Under the Rapid Transit Systems Act (Chapter 263A, Section 42):

    "Section 15b - No one shall place any litter upon the railway premises, except into receptacles expressly provided for that purpose. (Maximum fine of $500)

    "Section 17 - Passenger not to cause nuisance - No one shall conduct himself on any train or in any part of the railway premises so as to cause nuisance or annoyance to other passengers. (Maximum fine of $500)"
