    Couple found dead at condominium in Novena

    SINGAPORE: Police are investigating two unnatural deaths - believed to be that of a husband and wife - at a condominium in Novena.

    The Singapore Police Force (SPF) said it was alerted to the case at 200 Moulmein Road - the address of Novena Suites, a condominium near Velocity - at around 7pm on Saturday (Oct 22). 

    The 46-year-old man, identified as Wang Chan Foo, was found motionless at the foot of the condominium and was pronounced dead by paramedics at the scene. The 44-year-old woman, identified as Ng Soo San, was found dead in a unit in the same block. 

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force said the man fell to his death from the balcony while responders were setting up a safety life air pack. Subsequently, the authorities forced entry into the 13th floor condominium unit. 

    Channel NewsAsia understands that Ms Ng was found in one of the bedrooms with multiple stab wounds on her upper body.

    The couple is believed to have been in the process of separation and has two children, a 13-year-old daughter and a seven-year-old son who were attending tuition nearby when the incident occurred. The condominium unit was rented by Mr Wang, while Ms Ng is said to have been living at the couple's matrimonial home in Toa Payoh.

    Police said no suspects were arrested in this case.
