  • QX179R
    11 Nov 10, 13:10

    A group of students from St Joseph's Institution (SJI) have given an ultimatum to a contributor to the SJI Facebook page who calls himself SJI Patriot.

    It all started a few months ago when SJI Patriot claimed all he wanted was a "civil and healthy" debate on Facebook, about the present state of the school which he's disappointed in.

    But over the past few months, he has called SJI students "sissies", "snobs" and "nerds", who cannot play football.

    He also laments that the school isn't made up of enough church-going Catholics, and claims that SJI's days as a "superior and formidable school" are over.

    Three students from the school have banded together and issued SJI Patriot an ultimatum.

    He has until this Sunday to upload a video on the SJI Facebook page, admit to his mistakes, and apologise for what he has done. It could not be determined if all three are current students.

    -- TNP
