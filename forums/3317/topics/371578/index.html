Shell's National Day promotion attracts long queues

  • QX179R
    9 Aug 09, 23:47

    Long queues were seen forming outside Shell Petrol stations islandwide this afternoon.

    All thanks to a National Day promotion by the company which saw discounts of 44 cents per litre of fuel between 4 and 5pm.

    At the petrol station along Lornie Road, for instance, more than 30 cars were spotted in the queue.

    Car owners were notified of the deal via SMS.

    "It's a very good offer. Because now the petrol price is so expensive. I think they should do it more frequent. I saved at least eight to ten dollars."
    "I think it's good, but they probably should have it longer, so you don't get a long queue out there."
