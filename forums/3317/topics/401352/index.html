    SINGAPORE : Singapore is having additional security arrangements at Changi Airport for ministers and delegates arriving for the Shangri-La Dialogue which starts on Friday.

    From Thursday till Saturday, the Singapore Armed Forces (SAF) will be deployed alongside the police at the airport and surrounding premises to protect people and property against any terrorist attack.

    The government's online Gazette Notice said the defence minister is concerned about any attack during or before the Shangri-La Dialogue.

    As such, the SAF is required to be deployed to areas where delegates would be arriving.

    One such occasion when SAF soldiers were deployed to supplement police officers at Changi Airport was in the aftermath of the September 11 attack in 2001.

    MM Lee, DPM Teo hold bilateral meetings at Shangri-La Dialogue

    SINGAPORE : Singapore Minister Mentor Lee Kuan Yew, who attended the opening dinner for the Shangri-La Dialogue, called on visiting South Korean leader Lee Myung-bak for discussions on Friday evening.

    President Lee is here for a working visit, where he delivered the keynote speech at the Shangri-La Dialogue.

    President Lee will also witness the signing of two Memoranda of Understanding between Singapore and South Korea at the Istana on Saturday.

    Singapore's Deputy Prime Minister and Defence Minister, Teo Chee Hean, also held a series of bilateral meetings with ministers and delegation leaders attending the Shangri-La Dialogue.

    Among them is Russia's Deputy Prime Minister Sergei Ivanov, who will be speaking at the plenary session on counter-insurgency and strengthening governance.

    Mr Teo also held talks with Indonesia's Defence Minister, Purnomo Yusgiantoro.

    The Indonesian minister will speak during the final plenary session on Sunday on the subject of strengthening the regional security architecture.

    Mr Purnomo was in Singapore recently with Indonesian President Susilo Bambang Yudhoyono for the retreat between the leaders of Singapore and Indonesia.
