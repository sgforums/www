    Man gets 7 years' jail & cane for rape

    SINGAPORE : A man has been sentenced to seven years' jail and eight strokes of the cane for raping a 22-year-old woman he had met at a club.

    28-year-old Ong Mingwee, who manages a provision shop, was arrested on February 12, 2009.

    Ong, who met the victim at Zouk, claimed that she had seduced him when they were dancing and made him feel embarrassed.

    When the club closed at 4am, both left in a taxi for his home at Block 203 Toa Payoh North.

    There, the victim started to cry and said she was scared and wanted to go home.

    Ong maintained that the woman was not drunk when they left the nightspot together.

    However, the prosecution told the court that Ong had lied and the victim was highly intoxicated at that time.

    Deputy Public Prosecutor Leong Wing Tuck said that the distress of the victim was clearly evident by the calls she had made to her mother and friend.

    He said the victim was crying and shouting over the phone and asked to be picked up from Ong's place.

    She had also informed her mother, over the phone, that Ong would not let her go unless she had sex with him.

    Later, Ong asked the woman if he could have sex with her. He claimed that she had given her consent by nodding her head.

    DPP Leong said that Ong had taken advantage of the victim in her drunken state.

    District Judge Hamidah Ibrahim also said that she had "serious reservations" about Ong's defence.

    She said she found it "very incredible" that the victim gave her consent in her upset and agitated state.

    Ong remained emotionless when his sentence was read out. He is appealing against his sentence.

    - CNA/al
