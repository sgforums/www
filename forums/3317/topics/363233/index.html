CAAS wins IATA Eagle Award

  • QX179R
    8 Jun 09, 14:42

    The Civil Aviation Authority of Singapore, CAAS, has won the International Air Transport Association Eagle Award for the ‘Best Air Navigation Service Provider’ for 2009.

    The award ceremony is being held today at the 65th IATA Annual General Meeting in Kuala Lumpur.

    The IATA Eagle Awards are given to leading airport operators and air navigation service providers who are committed to providing value for money service, by taking positive steps to control infrastructure costs and related charges.

    The award was awarded to CAAS at the recommendations of an independent Eagle Award panel.

    With this award, CAAS has won a total of five Eagle Awards to date.
