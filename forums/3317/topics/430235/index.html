    A 29-YEAR-OLD woman is believed to be the youngest most prolific mother in Taiwan.

    Married for 12 years, Lai Zhen Ni has already given birth to two girls and six boys, and is currently pregnant with her ninth child. She is expecting the new arrival by the end of the year

    'Maybe I can beat my mother,' she joked, adding her mother - now 46 - delivered her first baby at the age of only 17. 'My mother had 11 children and the age difference between the eldest and the youngest is 24 years. My youngest brother is three years younger than my eldest son.'

    When the headmistress of Meihua Elementary School heard that Mrs Lai was pregnant again, she asked the eldest son: 'Is it true?' The boy, who has to take care of his siblings, answered: 'Yes, she's four months pregnant now... my parents keep on having kids, I'm quite tired already.'

    Mrs Lai's husband Peng Ke Yong said doctors and nurses have advised him to undergo birth control measures.

    'But children are gifts from God. Let nature take its course,' said the odd-job worker and farmer. -- THE STAR/ANN
