Christopher Lee, Fann Wong to register marriage on 16 May

    SINGAPORE: The date to the long awaited wedding has finally been fixed. Christopher Lee and Fann Wong will be registering their marriage on 16 May this year.

    Their wedding ceremony will take place later this year on 29 September at the Shangri-la Hotel in Singapore.

    About 700 guests are expected.

    Another wedding dinner will be held in Malacca for Christopher's Malaysian relatives and friends on October 11.

    Christopher Lee's father died recently.

    According to Chinese customs, the couple must register their marriage within 100 days in order to proceed with the wedding.

    Preparations for the wedding are now half complete and the groom gave us an idea of what the wedding will be like.

    He said: “The wedding should be romantic. It should be relaxing for our guests to celebrate this special occasion with us.”
