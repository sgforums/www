    SINGAPORE: A teenager took turns with a 40-year-old neighbour to have sex with an underage girl in a lorry.

    If convicted he could be jailed 10 years, fined or given both.

    The case against the 40-year-old neighbour, Hang Tuah Jumaat, will come up for mention in January next year.

    The 17-year-old youth is also the ex-boyfriend of the 13-year-old girl.

    The teenager and Hang Tuah Jumaat invited the girl for drinks and they were all driven to Kranji Camp between 21 and 22 April 2010 by Hang Tuah Jumaat.

    They had drinks together and the girl became intoxicated and drowsy.

    Hang Tuah Jumaat then asked the teen to have sex with the girl, but he declined and went to sleep.

    When he woke up, he saw Hang Tuah Jumaat having sex with the girl.

    The teen was again told by Hang Tuah Jumaat to have sex with the girl and this time he did.

    The court heard that the girl's eyes were closed and she appeared intoxicated throughout.

    Hang Tuah Jumaat drove the teen and the girl back, but left the girl sleeping in the lorry when she complained that she could not get up.

    The girl later went to school where she was referred to a counsellor, and a police report was made.

    On the same day, the teenager was detained in school where he admitted to the offence.

    He claimed he was high on heroin at the time of the incident and had acted out of fear towards Hang Tuah Jumaat, court documents revealed.
