    PM Lee visits Belgium

    Official visit ties in with Asem summit

    By Zakir Hussain

     

    BRUSSELS: Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong arrives here today for a four-day official visit to Belgium, his first as Singapore's head of government.

    His visit ties in with the eighth biennial summit of the Asia-Europe Meeting (Asem), which brings leaders from both continents together for talks on Monday and Tuesday.

    He will also visit PSA Corp's operations at the Port of Antwerp, which is PSA's largest investment outside Singapore.

    While here, Mr Lee will have an audience with King Albert II of Belgium, said a statement from Singapore's Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

    He will meet Belgium's Prime Minister Yves Leterme, who will host him to an official dinner.

    He will also meet Mr Andre Flahaut, the president of the Belgian Chamber of Representatives, which is the lower house of Belgium's federal parliament.
