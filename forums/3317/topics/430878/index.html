    Teen faces more molestation charges

    A POLYTECHNIC student charged a week ago with molesting a teen was slapped with five more charges of outrage of modesty on Friday.

    Lim Yang Tee, 16, is said to have molested five girls aged 11 to 13 in lifts at various Housing Board blocks in Jurong West between October 2010 and March this year.

    He was arrested last Thursday by police over the latest incident that day for allegedly molesting a 16-year-old girl outside a lift lobby at a block of flats in Jurong West.

    Lim, represented by Mr Anthony Lim, has been remanded for psychiatric assessment.

    He will be back in court on June 10.

    If convicted, he faces a jail term of up to two years or fine or caning or any combination of punishments.
