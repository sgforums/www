    SINGAPORE: A woman was found dead in a flat in Jurong West on Saturday morning.

    Police said they received a call at 6.44am about the incident in a flat on the 14th floor of Block 633, Jurong West Street 65.

    They said the victim is a 25-year-old Singaporean.

    Police have detained a 38-year-old man, who's believed to be her boyfriend.

    He will be charged in court on April 1.

    If convicted of murder, he will face the death penalty.

    Neighbours told Channel NewsAsia the flat was shared by several people and they heard a couple quarrelling on Saturday morning.
