    SINGAPORE: The cable car system linking Sentosa and Mount Faber will reopen to the public on Wednesday after a more than nine-month makeover.

    The upgraded cable cars boast bigger cabins and are wheelchair friendly.

    Mount Faber Leisure Group said they also feature a touch-screen infotainment system.

    It said there will also be what has been touted as a world's first, a seven-star Jewelled Cabin.

    It's adorned with Swarovski Crystals and leather upholstered chairs.

    The cabins will also be available for rent for occasions like weddings, anniversaries and company events.
