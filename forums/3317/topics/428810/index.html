    A man was nabbed at Lucky Plaza for allegedly molesting girls this afternoon (Apr 24), said STOMPer zi liang. The man was first pinned down by four men, before police arrived at the scene.

    Said zi liang in his contribution:

    "While walking past the underpass between Ngee Ann City and Lucky Plaza around 4pm today, I happened to see this incident.

    "I heard that this guy went around molesting girls and he was nabbed.

    "Before police arrived, a crowd was gathered at the area.

    "People were shouting that a man had molested girls, and four people were pinning a man to the ground.

    "Police arrived about 15 minutes later."

    STOMP contacted the police and a spokesperson said:

    "Police confirmed that a call was received today around 5pm informing of the incident at the shopping mall along Orchard Road.

    "Upon police's arrival, a man in his mid 20s was placed under arrest for the alleged outrage of modesty.

    "Police investigations are ongoing."
