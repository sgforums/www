    Police investigate derogatory remarks posted in online forum

    SINGAPORE: A Chinese national is assisting Police in investigations into a case where derogatory remarks about a Malay man were allegedly posted online in a forum last week.

    Police said on June 17 that the man is in his 20s and confirmed that investigations are on-going.

    News reports said at least two police reports were filed against the man for allegedly posting the remarks in the Chinese-language forum Discussions in the Lion City about a Malay man with whom he had fought.

    Netizens responded angrily to his seven-sentence post in the forum for new citizens.

    The post was later taken down by the author known only by his online name, Nai He Re Chen Ai.

    He later apologised for his remarks and claimed he had exaggerated the incident.

    - AFP/fa
