Lift drags woman to her death

  • deathmaster
    16 Dec 11, 18:58

    An advertising executive who stepped into a lift at her Madison Avenue office building got caught as the doors were closing and was dragged along as it shot upward, crushing her to death between floors.

    Two other people who had entered just before the doors closed looked on in horror as Ms Suzanne Hart, 41, was killed.

    They were rescued from the lift, which jammed between the first and second floors, and were taken to a hospital to be evaluated for psychological trauma. Neither appeared to be physically injured, police and fire department officials said.

    Public safety and law enforcement officials said Ms Hart was stepping into the lift when her foot got caught in the gap between the lift car and the lobby floor. The car then rose abruptly with its doors still open, pulling her along, they said.

    The accident happened on Wednesday morning in a 26-storey midtown Manhattan office tower near Grand Central Terminal.

    Investigators with the city's buildings department were trying to determine what went wrong. Safety mechanisms are supposed to prevent lifts from moving while their doors are open. AP
