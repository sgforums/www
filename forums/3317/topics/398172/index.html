    Ex-foreign worker's bill sent to STOMPer due to registered billing address

    StarHub says that STOMPer received an ex-employee's reminder letters for payment as his place of residence was registered as the official billing address.

    This was in response to the STOMPer's complaint that he was receiving letters from StarHub which warned him that legal action will be taken if payment was not made.

    In an email to STOMP (5 May), StarHub said:

    "We refer to the STOMPer's feedback.

    "We terminated the mobile broadband service subscribed by Mr Chen when the STOMPer notified us of Mr Chen’s departure from the country earlier.

    "However, there was an outstanding amount in Mr Chen’s account. As a result, system-generated reminders addressed to Mr Chen were being sent to his registered billing address, which is where the STOMPer and his parents live.

    "We have since contacted the STOMPer, apologised for any inconvenience caused and resolved the matter with him."
