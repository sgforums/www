Man bribes police with S$50 in exchange for release

  • QX179R

    SINGAPORE: A 49-year-old man has been arrested for trying to bribe a policeman.

    Police said on Monday that the suspect wanted the officer to release him after he was nabbed for possessing a flick knife.

    This happened at about 6.45am on Saturday, after the suspect entered Singapore through Marina South Pier while travelling to the Philippines.

    A flick knife with a blade of 7cm was found in his baggage during a x-ray screening at the customs checkpoint.

    The suspect was referred to duty officer, Corporal Muzaffar Mohd Hasan.

    During the interview, the suspect offered cash of S$50 to Corporal Muzaffar in return for his release.

    Police said Corporal Muzaffar warned the suspect, rejected the bribe without hesitation and placed him under arrest.
