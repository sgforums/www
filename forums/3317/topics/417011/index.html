    SINGAPORE : Some women in Singapore gave up their bras on Saturday, all in the name of charity.

    Organised by lifestyle website CozyCot, the event encourages women to donate bras to their less fortunate sisters in Southeast Asia.

    Nicole Yee, Founder of CozyCot said: "There are many disadvantaged teens from countries like Bali and Cambodia, they live in very poor conditions and are badly in need of basic necessities which we, in Singapore, often take for granted. Giving a bra costs us nothing, but means a world of difference to another woman."

    Some 200 bras were collected on Saturday. Donors have till the end of the month to donate at CozyCot at Orchard Central.

    The company is hoping to collect at least 1,000 bras.

    One donor said: "I actually dump all of it away. I just put them in the trash but with this (campaign), at least I can help to donate more bras in the future and recycle them."

    Another donor said: "I think it's great because donating to other girls in other countries is a good cause."
