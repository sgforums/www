    SINGAPORE: Police arrested 40 persons in an operation on Friday night targeted at a public entertainment outlet in Geylang.

    A team of police officers from Bedok Police Division conducted enforcement checks at a public entertainment outlet located along Geylang Lorong 13.

    Police said among the 40 suspects, 37 were arrested for unlawful employment offences; two foreigners were arrested for immigration offences; and one local was arrested for disorderly behaviour.

    Summons were also taken against the operator of the outlet for running an unlicensed public entertainment outlet and selling liquor without a valid licence.
