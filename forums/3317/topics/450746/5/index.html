    2 men charged with paid sex with underage girl back in court

    SINGAPORE: Two men facing charges of having paid sex with an underage girl were back in court on Monday for a date to be fixed for a discovery conference.

    Choong Sin Jian, 27, and Lee Vin Sen, 35, will be returning to court on June 11 for their lawyers to meet the prosecution to exchange information on their cases.

    They are among 48 men charged with having commercial sex with an underage girl.

    Choong allegedly paid S$600 for the girl's sexual services on two occasions at Strand Hotel in Bencoolen Street in January 2011.

    He is represented by Mr Peter Fernando.

    He will be claiming trial to both charges.

    Lee allegedly paid the girl S$450 for sex at the Marina Bay Sands Hotel in September 2010.

    Lee, who was previously represented by Mr Sng Kheng Huat, will now be represented by Ms Lim Kim Hong.

    If convicted, they could be jailed up to seven years and fined.
