    3 men charged with paid sex with underage girl back in court

    SINGAPORE: Three men who were charged in court on April 16 with having paid sex with an underage girl were back in court on Monday to fix a date for a discovery conference.

    Koh is believed to have paid S$600 for the girl's sexual services at Strand Hotel at Bencoolen Street in December 2010.

    He is represented by Mr Peter Ong.

    Peck, whose lawyer is Mr Anand Nalachandran, allegedly paid her S$450 for sex at Hotel 81 at Becoolen Street in October 2010.

    Pyi, who will be represented by Mr James Sebastian, faced two charges of paying the girl S$650 on two occasions at Strand Hotel between December 2010 and January 2011.

    The Attorney-General's Chambers also tendered amended charges which now reflect the name of the underage girl, which was initially omitted to protect her identity.

    If convicted, they could be jailed up to seven years and fined.
