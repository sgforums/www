    Traffic police vehicle involved in collision at Haig Road

    SINGAPORE: A traffic police patrol vehicle was involved in a collision along Haig Road, near the Geylang Serai Hari Raya Bazaar, on Sunday (Jun 26).
     

    Photos posted by Facebook user Angeline Xie at 9.10pm show the scene of the accident, with the hood of the traffic police vehicle smashed in.
     

    A Channel NewsAsia viewer who declined to be named said he heard a siren at about 8.50pm, and saw the patrol vehicle chasing a car. Seconds later, he heard a loud bang and saw the patrol vehicle colliding with another BMW car. The collision took place in front of a crowded bus stop, and the patrol vehicle's airbag was activated, he said.
     

    While the eyewitness said he was unsure if anyone was injured, an ambulance arrived soon after. The car the patrol vehicle appeared to have fled the scene, he added.


    Channel NewsAsia has reached out to the police and the Singapore Civil Defence Force for more details.

    Collision near Haig Road: 1 injured, 28-year-old man arrested

    SINGAPORE: A 28-year-old man has been arrested following a collision involving a traffic police patrol vehicle on Sunday (Jun 26), in which one person was injured.

     

    The Singapore Civil Defence Force said it was alerted to an incident requiring ambulance assistance at the junction of Geylang Road and Engku Aman Road at 8.56pm. One male driver in his 30s was conveyed to Changi General Hospital as a result of the accident, the SCDF said. 

     

    Police said investigations into the accident are ongoing. 

     

    Photos posted by Facebook user Angeline Xie at 9.10pm show the scene of the accident near Haig Road and the Geylang Serai Hari Raya Bazaar, with the hood of the traffic police vehicle appearing to have been smashed in.

    A Channel NewsAsia viewer who declined to be named said he heard a siren at about 8.50pm, and saw the patrol vehicle chasing a car. Seconds later, he heard a loud bang and saw that the patrol vehicle had collided with another BMW car. The collision, which took place in front of a crowded bus stop, caused the patrol vehicle's airbag to be activated, he said.
