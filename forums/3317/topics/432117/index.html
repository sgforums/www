    She was heading down to the first floor to get glasses at 11.45pm on Jan 22. The court heard he had been drinking alcohol and immediately after committing the offence, he went to hide under the spiral staircase and squatted there.

    The waitress, also a Chinese national, demanded he come out and when he did, he apologised to her and walked away. She informed a bouncer of the pub, who detained Lin and called the police.
