    Car overturns along Thomson Road, driver and passenger escape unhurt

    SINGAPORE: A car turned turtle along Thomson Road at about 3.25pm on Monday, but no one was injured.

    Police said the vehicle was travelling along Thomson Road towards Upper Thomson Road.

    It is unclear how the car had overturned.

    Despite the damage to the vehicle, both the 21-year-old driver and his passenger escaped unhurt.
