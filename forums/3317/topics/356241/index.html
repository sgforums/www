CNB seizes $207,000 worth of drugs

  • QX179R
    17 Apr 09, 10:08

    The Central Narcotics Bureau or CNB has seized more than $200,000 worth of drugs in a series of raids.

    The haul includes 1.12 kilograms of heroin and more than 1,000 Erimin-5 tablest.

    CNB received information that a suspected drug trafficker had received a consignment of heroin from a courier from overseas.
    Its officers raided yesterday a hideout at Blk 133 Lorong Ah Soo where the 31-year-old trafficker was arrested.

    His associate - a 35-year-old unemployed female and a suspected abuser - were also arrested.

    The next day, CNB officers arrested the 26-year-old drug courier and his friend in a Malaysian registered vehicle.

    They will be investigated for the offence of importation of a controlled drug.
