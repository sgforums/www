    SINGAPORE: A man has been charged with ill-treating a child.

    The 24-year-old man is the boyfriend of the victim's aunt.

    He allegedly abused his position while the two-year-old child was under his care.

    The man, who cannot be named, is accused of hitting the toddler with a clothes hanger and scalding his foot with a heated iron some time on 7 January 2011.

    The incident occurred at a flat along Teck Whye Lane.

    If convicted, the man can be fined up to S$4,000 or serve up to four years in jail, or both.
