    STOMPer Old Horse has been receiving an advertisement via email, bugging him to date a beautiful young Malaysian girl. He wonders what his chances of success are, as he is an old taxi driver who studied only up to Primary 6.

    Said the STOMPer:

    "Of late I have been receiving a spate of emails from this dating agency in Kuala Lumpur asking me to submit my 'application' to make friends with a Malaysian girl of 20 whose parents are Korean and Chinese.

    "How could I make friend with a girl who is half my age and furthermore I have only a Primary 6 education.

    "I work part time as a taxi driver and by local standards I am a man of very limited means and assets.

    "I am wondering if a beautiful girl in her prime of life would ever be interested in a man with only a modest income and a body which is a far cry from that of David Beckham.

    What are my chances of success?"
