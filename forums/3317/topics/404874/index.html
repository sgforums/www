    Student who whacked classmate with broomstick counselled

    The student who was caught on video attacking his classmate with a broomstick has been counselled. The principal of the students confirmed this in her reply to STOMPer Rachel, who wrote to her after watching the video on STOMP.

    The video showed the student hitting his classmate on the head repeatedly with a broomstick, in front of the teacher in the classroom.

    After writing in to the principal of the school for comments, STOMPer Rachel then forwarded the principal's reply to STOMP.

    In her reply, the Principal said:

    "The school takes a serious view of the matter.

    "Disciplinary action is being taken on the students involved according to the school rules.

    "The students have also been counselled, and have apologised for their behaviour.

    "The parents of both students have been informed of the incident and are working closely with the school.

    "The Form Teacher of the students involved is following up with the class and helping them learn from this incident, using this as a learning moment to teach them respect and on the potential danger of such acts."
