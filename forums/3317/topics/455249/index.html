Be part of Spore's community life, PM Lee tells new citizens

  • QX179R
    7 Jul 12, 13:08

    SINGAPORE: Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong has urged new citizens to join in the country's community life, as the best way to integrate into Singapore society.

    He also urged Singaporeans to reach out to new citizens on a personal level, both at home and at work.

    He said Singapore is proud to consider itself an open, multi-ethnic society.

    Mr Lee was speaking at a ceremony that welcomed 200 new citizens as they received their identification certificates in Ang Mo Kio on Saturday morning.

    Another 150 new citizens in the constituency will also receive their certificates in the afternoon.

    The legal status of citizenship is just one aspect of citizenship, Mr Lee said.

    "Much more important are the intangible factors that make citizenship meaningful - shared values, loyalty, and an emotional attachment to the people and places in the country," he said.

    -CNA/ac
