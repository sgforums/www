Impact of Honda, Toyota recalls minimal in Singapore

    SINGAPORE: The impact of the global recall of Japanese-made Honda and Toyota cars appears to be minimal in Singapore.

    Honda recalled more than 430,000 vehicles worldwide, mostly in North America, to fix defective airbags that could blow up and injure or kill its drivers.

    Here in Singapore, only 39 Honda Accord cars are affected by it. Distributor Kah Motors said the cars were made in Japan between 2001 and 2002.

    As for the recall of Toyota Prius cars, 194 have been affected in Singapore so far.

    Parallel importers, which make up 25 per cent of Singapore's market share, said they are recalling 124 Prius cars.

    Meanwhile, official distributor Borneo Motors is asking 70 car owners to send theirs for a software upgrade. 26 out of the 70 have already done so.
