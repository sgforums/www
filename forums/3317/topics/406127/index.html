Watching these Ris Low YOG cheers would demoralise you

  • XD2229P
    23 Jul 10, 18:03

    A cheer is suppposed to 'cheer' you up, but STOMPer Buay Tahan felt demoralised after watching Ris Low's new cheers for YOG.

    Ris Low, 20, first shot to infamy when she displayed her poor command of English in a RazorTV interview, mispronouncing words like red ('rad'), bikini ('bigini'), and prints ('preens').

    She was crowned Miss Singapore World 2009 in September last year, but she was stripped of her title when a news report revealed that she had been convicted of credit card fraud.

    STOMPer Buay Tahan, who came across Ris Low's YOG cheer online, said:

    "Ris Low has come up with a cheer for YOG.

    "I guess she is better off being a model than a motivator.

    "I somehow feel demoralised after seeing this video."

    Ris Low's video was contributed as part of The New Paper's Youth Olympic Games cheer contest. The caption on her video reads 'Ris Low put on her thinking cap and made us two cheers for our Youth Olympic Games cheer competition.'
