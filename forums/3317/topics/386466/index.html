    JAKARTA (AFP) – Former Indonesian president Abdurrahman Wahid died in hospital on Wednesday aged 69, after a long battle with illness, a party official said.

    Wahid, popularly known as Gus Dur, was practically blind, diabetic and had suffered strokes.

    "Gus Dur just passed away," said Lukman Edy from Wahid's National Awakening Party.
