Khaw on the social media difference

  • Melissaloanfirm
    1 May 11, 23:48

    MOMENTS before Health Minister Khaw Boon Wan could knock on the door of a flat in Woodlands, it opened.

    The resident, a voter in Sembawang GRC, had technology to thank for her apparent prescience. She had been tracking him in real time on Kampung Sembawang's Facebook page.

    A few minutes later, a photograph of her with the minister went live on the constituency's Facebook site.

    Mr Khaw, 58, who is gunning for a third term, said on Sunday that the presence of social media made the difference between this and all previous polls.

    'How you skilfully integrate new-media campaigning with old-media campaigning - that is key,' he said.

    He added that if one were not tech-savvy and lived where people were, one would lose out; in areas with more old folk, not being comfortable with technology was less of a disadvantage.

    -- ST
