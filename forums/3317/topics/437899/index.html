    More employers are wiring up their homes with closed-circuit television systems (CCTVs) to monitor their maids, but at least two maids have raised questions about the invasion of privacy.

    Last month, a maid known as Chona De Guzman posted a comment on the Ministry of Manpower's (MOM) Facebook page asking if it was right for her employer to install a CCTV camera in her room.

    It prompted a reply from another maid, Ms Joan Asequia Gonzales, who said she faced the same situation at her employer's home.

    'I don't have privacy,' she wrote in the post.
