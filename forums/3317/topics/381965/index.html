    Search ongoing for missing 18-year-old at East Coast

    The search is still on going for an 18-year-old boy who is believed to have drowned off East Coast Park yesterday. 

    The Police Coast Guard, the Naval Diving Unit as well as the Maritime Port Authority were called in for the search from around 6.30pm yesterday. 

    The 18-year-old Malay boy was seen drowning about 25 metres from the shoreline near the MacDonalds restaurant in East Coast.

    Body of 18-year-old teen found at East Coast shore

    SINGAPORE: The body of the 18-year-old teen who drowned at East Coast on Sunday has been found.

    According to the Police, the body washed ashore at around the same location where he last went missing.

    He was pronounced dead at about 2.10am on Tuesday.

    On Sunday, the emergency services had received a call at around 6.20pm about the boy, who was seen struggling in the water. The incident happened about 25 metres from the shoreline near the McDonalds restaurant.
