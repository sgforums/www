Case trust suspends 16 private education organisations

  • QX179R
    4 Nov 09, 00:54

    16 private education organisations were found without a student protection scheme or SPS facility and were suspended by case trust. 

    The SPS serves to safeguard the student's tuition fees. 

    Marketing Institue of singapore and the turning point academy were among those in the list. 

    Case trust began monitoring several accredited private education insitutions after NTUC income withdrew as an insurance provider from Nov 1. 

    The suspension was issued today after numerous calls and reminder letters for the private education organisations to put in place another valid SPS after NTUC's withdrawal. 

    During the period of suspension, the 16 education organisations cannot enroll new international students.
