    "This picture was taken showing volunteers (in red) 'chasing' people away from the smoking zone just because they said that MP will be coming.

    STOMPer Ong wrote:


    "On the morning of 7 Apr, Sunday, at around 9.30am to 11.20am the food court at Block 511 Bishan was booked by an MP and volunteers.
