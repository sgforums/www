    A 39-YEAR-OLD auxiliary police officer was found dead with a gunshot wound to his head in the toilet of a building in Jurong on Monday afternoon.

    Lee Kong Beng, a police constable with Aetos, did not show any sign of distress during the course of work, said a spokesman from the security company.

    Police are investigating the death and do not suspect foul play.
