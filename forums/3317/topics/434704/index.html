    STOMPer MoreDull is shocked to read about a 23-year-old woman who punched, kicked and blamed her mother for her failure to make it big in the modelling industry.

    According to the news report in Lianhe Wanbao today (Jul 19), it seems the aspiring model left home in October 2010, taking with her a notebook computer and three pieces of gold jewellery worth $3,500.

    Mrs Liu, 45, told Lianhe Wanbao that her daughter had joined a modelling agency with the dream of becoming a famous model. After leaving home, her daughter then lived in the modelling agency's office.

    When Mrs Liu tried to look up her daughter, she was instead greeted with punches and kicks and was chased away. Her daughter blamed Mrs Liu for giving her a 'wrong' name and giving birth to her on the 'wrong' day, which affected her shot at fame.

    It seems her daughter also cut ties with her close friends, telling them she had no time to chat as she had to work every day and not to disturb her anymore.
