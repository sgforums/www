    STOMPers were shocked when they read about a girl in China who was willing to trade her virginity for a white iPhone 4. What is with youths' mentality nowadays, they ask.

    Apparently, the girl is born in the 1990s and is from the Maoming city of south China's Guangdong province. She posted on the Chinese version of Twitter, Weibo, that she was willing to exchange her 'valuable first night' for a white iPhone 4 which retails from RMB 4,999 in China.

    According to the online news source, the girl, whose surname is Wen, posted a picture of herself together with her QQ number and encouraged young men to contact her if they were serious about the deal. It seems a man contacted her and they started chatting online, however nothing happened and her virginity is still intact.

    Said STOMPer Wu Yia Boh in his email:

    "I was shocked to come across news of a woman who said that she'll give up her virginity for an iPhone 4 in an online news report.

    "Previously, a boy sold his kidney for an iPad 2 and an iPhone.

    "What has happened to youths' mentality nowadays?"
