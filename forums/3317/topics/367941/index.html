    MOE surpasses recruitment target of 3,000 teachers

    SINGAPORE: At half time in June, the Education Ministry has already surpassed its annual target of recruiting 3,000 teachers this year.

    Speaking at the National Institute of Education's teachers' investiture on Monday, Education Minister Ng Eng Hen assured that although the ministry has stepped up hiring, it has also continued to be rigorous in selecting teachers and educators.

    He said this is to ensure that only those with passion, aptitude and commitment towards teaching are selected.

    Besides recruiting good teachers, Dr Ng added that the ministry also needs to continually develop its existing pool of educators, so they are kept abreast of the latest in education practices.

    The Education Ministry has also offered post-graduate scholarships to 51 education officers to pursue their Masters degrees or PhDs here, and abroad.

    In all, 1,893 newly qualified teachers will receive their diplomas over the next three days.

    - CNA/yt
