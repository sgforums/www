  • QX179R

    Several STOMPers have expressed anger over this insensitive comment made on Twitter by a nurse. She had called a family 'orang kaya baru' or 'newly rich' after they asked for donations to fund their baby's overseas operation, only to have her pass away this morning.

    Baby Nur had been born with a rare heart condition, and her family had requested for donations for her to undergo a heart transplant overseas. She died suddenly this morning however, prompting the woman's remark about the family becoming 'orang kaya baru', a Malay term meaning 'newly rich people'.
