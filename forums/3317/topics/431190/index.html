Teenager arrested for killer litter

    A 14-YEAR-OLD boy was arrested on Tuesday for allegedly throwing a glass bottle from thr sixth floor of a block of flats in Punggol Central.

    Officers called to the scene spotted glass fragments on the ground floor.

    After questioning a group of students on the roof garden of the block, one of them was arrested.

    Investigations against him are ongoing.

    The commander of the Ang Mo Kio Police Division, Deputy Assistant Commissioner Hse Sin Yun, said the law takes a serious view of irresponsible acts that endanger the lives of others.

    An individual found guilty of a rash act can be jailed for six months and fined $2,500.

    (Taken from The Straits Times, Thursday, May 26 2011, Page B11)
