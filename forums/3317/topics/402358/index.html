    STOMPer Frankie is upset that the workers refused to give them their window panels back after they had removed them. They were also told that if they want to keep the window panels, they will have to tear the panels down themselves.

    The STOMPer wrote:

    "The workers working on the windows at Block 456 all the way to Block 460 in Ang Mo Kio have told the residents that if they want their window panels back, they would have to tear them down themselves.

    "They claimed that the window panels belong to them and they will not return even when asked.

    "We've checked with the relevant authorities on this issue and they said that the window panels actually belong to the residents and should be given back to them upon request.

    "However, it seems that the workers would not follow instructions, even after we informed them on what the authorities had said.

    "Every morning, without fail, I will see residents climbing onto their windows, trying to tear down the panels without any safety gear.

    "The workers will look at them at the side.

    "This is so dangerous, what if the residents fall?

    "After the residents have removed the parts they wanted, they asked the workers to tear down the remaining parts of the panels but they declined to do so, saying that it is not their job.

    "Aren't the workers supposed to maintain work safety and prevent residents from entering their work zone?"
