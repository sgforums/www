    Case of school principal accused of bullying, abuse & embezzlement being reviewed by MOE

    SINGAPORE: The Education Ministry is currently reviewing accusations against the principal of a secondary school in Pasir Ris.

    Channel NewsAsia understands from a source that the principal has been accused of bullying, abuse and embezzlement.

    When asked about these accusations, an MOE spokesperson told Channel NewsAsia that the case is currently under review.

    She added that the principal has been given a new assignment at the MOE headquarters in the interim.
