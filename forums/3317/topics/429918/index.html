Roof at CHJIMES collapses after car rams into pillar

  • QX179R
    8 May 11, 14:02

    STOMPers have sent in photographs that show that a part of the roof at CHJIMES has caved in after a car crashed into a supporting pillar.

    STOMPer Weiyang relates:

    "Roof collapsed after a car rammed into a pillar at CHIJMES."

    STOMPer tan lay khim also sent in a photograph showing that the Police have arrived at the scene.

    According to the Police, a taxi and a car were involved in a collision that resulted in the car crashing into a pillar at CHJIMES. A portion of the roof collapsed and the area has been cordoned off for safety.

    The Police report that no passers-by were injured by the collapsed roof, but the taxi driver involved in the car accident was conveyed to a hospital.
