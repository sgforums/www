  • parn
    19 Jun 03, 23:03
    Hello pple, when your initial d card expire and during the car evaluation, you will need to choose again the tune that you want for you car.
    Say tuning is referring to the parts/modifications that you get from 0 pts to 120k pts. And internal modifications is referring to the upgrades after 120k pts. Part change belongs to the tuning group.
    Internal modifications examples are ecu, engine oil, braking fluid, spark plug...etc changes.
    So if you are already in tune A, and you want to continue in tune A with all your part change, you will not lose your tuning.
    But if you want to change to other tune, then you will lose your tuning with all your part change.
    By the way, no matter how you change tune type (A-D), you will not lose your internal modifications...but I think the tachnometer will be change if you change from a tune A trueno to other tune. Somebody pls confirm this alright?
    Your internal modifications will continue as long as you reached the pts required for the modifications.

    Hope it is clear enough.
