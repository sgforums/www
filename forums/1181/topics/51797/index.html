    Myogi Wet vs Cappucino

    For the 2 hairpin turns, look to your left. For the first one, the railings will change to a different type once it's near the hairpin. Downshift and release your accelerator pedal once you spoted different railings near the hairpin. Try to clear this hairpin below 155 to prevent knocking on the walls. As long as you don't hit the walls, you can clear it as low as 140 and you will still be able to regain back your speed after the hairpin.

    For the second hairpion, downshift when you reach the last 2 sign board. Those 2 are white signboards which is placed quite close together. Downshift straight away you saw the first white signboard, your speed will drop to 155 - 165. No matter what, do not touch any of the walls, and also not touching any of the walls in the map.
