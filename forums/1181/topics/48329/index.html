    The different level of Auras explained!

    Level 0 - 10
    At level 0 - 10, there's no aura for your car. If you win, your level increases if you win a player battle.

    Level 11 - 20
    At these levels, your aura will be Blue. It will grow bigger as your level increases. And if you lose a battle, your level might decrease.



    Level 21 - 30
    At level 21 - 30, your aura starts to change colour. The colour changes accordingly to different levels.
    Green (Lvl 21,22)
    Violet (Lvl23,24)
    Orange (Lvl25,26)
    Red (Lvl27)
    Yellow (Lvl28[/i])
    Purple (Lvl29)
    Cyan (Lvl30)



    Level 31 - 99
    And at level 31 - 99, you will be able to get the rainbow Aura if you win 10 games consecutively. Your original aura at these levels is white.
