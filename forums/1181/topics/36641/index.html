How do i purchase the Initial D Arcade memory card? (Part 1)

  • kohanson
    27 Feb 03, 23:55
    So you want to buy the card that most youngsters have right? You have come to the right place.. I'll teach you how to purchase the card here. Your name, your Car Model , and the Stages you have completed will be printed on your card.

    Ok, now follow the instructions below..

    1. you insert the correct amount of tokens which is usually $1.50 to start the game going. And you will come to this screen as soon as the correct amount of tokens have been inserted. Choose the left option if you do not want anyone sitting beside you to challenge you.



    2. Insert a memory card if you have one. If not, press the Start Button to continue.



    3. At this screen, it is time to purchase your card with the correct amount of tokens indicated on the screen. The card will usually be $2.00. Your card will be issued to you after you finish your game.



    4. This screen shows that your card has been purchased.



    5. This is the Car Selection Screen, you have a wide varieties of cars to choose from. You might want to take a look here to see which cars are available for this game. There is no turning back, so if you stepped on the accelerator, that means you cannot undo your action.



    6. At this screen, you are able to change the colour of the car by shifting the gear knob up or down. Click on the Start Button or depress the Accelerator to continue.
