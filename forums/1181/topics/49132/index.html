  • parn
    20 Jun 03, 10:11
    If you use the bronze rims in wet weather, the car will be very difficult to flow the ideal cornering line during drifting. Like what Ryousuke_FC3S has mentioned, it will go out of control.

    The overall good wheels for FD is definitely the wheels for Tune A, the black wheel with the silver cone in the centre. Its handling in wet weather is no difference with dry weather. With the exception that it doesn't accelerate as fast as it is in dry weather. But you will be able to drive your FD the same way as in dry weather. Its cornering ability is only average, but you just need to brake early and turn early into a corner to obtain the ideal corner line that you desire.
    Cornering speed is a little bit lower compared to the bronze rims, about 3-5km/h lower.

    Good luck.
