Converting your Card from Version 1 to version 2.

  • kohanson
    26 Apr 03, 23:54
    This page will show you the procedure on how to transfer your data from your Old Initial D Arcade Version 1 card to the latest Version 2 Card.

    1. After inserting your usual credits and inserting your version 1 card, the machine will come to this screen. Telling you that you are using the version 1 card and needs you to convert to the latest version 2 card inorder to continue playing.



    2. At this screen, Insert the correct amount of credits to continue the transfering of datas into your new Initial D version 2 card. You need to play all the stages again, your points and your car's modification will still be intact.



    3. After you have inserted the correct amount of credits, your version 1 card will be ejected from the machine.



    4. Your new version 2 card is created! Enjoy.
