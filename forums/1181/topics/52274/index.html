    For the strength and weakness

    AE86 and Cappocuino try not to "kiss" any walls, if not the RPM pick up will be very slow. Also the cappocuino is a very drifty car so must control it very well if not you will be spending alot of time trying to counter steer back to position. Other than that, this 2 cars have a better top speed compared to the S2000 and STI.

    S2000 for me is quite an average car with 6 gears. Nothing too fancy about this car. Tends to oversteer abit as well.

    As for the STI, you will find that the handling will be quite different from the others as it's a 4WD car. You will be amazed by the pickup speed of this car.

    My car of choice is the Impreza WRX Type R. I have tried the AE86 and Capp but I just can't seem to control these 2 cars well. I guess I played too long with the WRX.
