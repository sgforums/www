    86 Trueno
    1. Cup of water - don't spill out all the water when driving Akina uphill for 2CP approx.
    2. Beat Keisuke - just beat him by bring ahead 40+m
    3. Scare Iketani - just drive normally and Iketani will faint when his bar at the top is all filled up.
    4. Beat Keisuke - you start on half-way through Akina DH and just neat him.
    5. Scare Iketani - just drive normally and Iketani will faint when his bar at the top is all filled up.
    6. Get to the top of Akina - just drive akina UH and complete it within the alotted time.
    7. Beat Nazakato - Just beat him on Akina Downhill
    8. Beat Shingo - This is a Gum-tape death match. You will have a very hard time steering while steeping the gas pedal.
    9. Beat Ryosuke - just beat him Downhill Akina.
    10. Beat Mako and Sayuki - Beat them on REAL USUI Downhill. NOTE: This track is VERY hard to drive and VERY long
    11. Beat Kenta - Beat Kenta in REAL MYOGI Downhill Rain by 200+m
    12. Beat the Evo4 - Just beat that guy in the Evo4 on Akina Downhill
    13. Scare Natsuki - You are driving a RED 180SX vs. a Black S13 on Akagi Downhill and all you have to do it scare her?? Until the bar on the top is full
    14. vs. Evo3 - You will face Evo3 on Akagi Downhill and you cannot win here because your Engine will blow
    15. Race down Akina DH - Just race Akina Downhill with Wataru beside you thinking why your engine is so slow. You will race with the A Group Engine but with the OLD Tachometer. So yea, even if your needle is point to 8000RPM, you can still accelerate.
    16. vs. Wataru - I don't know how exactly this race works but there are 3 rounds on Wataru's home course. I just beat him all 3 times. Shomuu pass
    17. vs. Evo3 - Beat the Evo3 guy on Iro downhill, you will start first
    18. vs. MR2 - Beat the MR2 guy on Iro downhill.
    19. Drive Natsuki on Akina downhill within 3'15

    You will start Akagi Story mode here using Keisuke's FD
    20. Beat Nakazato with FD on Akina Uphill
    21. Beat Nazakato on Real Myogi Uphill. Dry for CP1-CP3 and Wet starting from CP4. Only pass him ONLY on CP4 or else you lose.
    22. FD vs. Evo4 on Akagi Uphill.
    23. Use FC to beat Evo3 on Akagi Downhill.
    24. Fd vs. 86 Turbo started on Akagi downhill already, just take the lead all the way and you win.

    Project D
    25. 86 Mecha vs. Eunos on unknown track downhill, dust him and you win.
    26. Beat R34 with FD on same track Uphill.
    27. 86 Mecha vs. EK9 (crappier one) 86 Mecha starts with lead but can't go over 9000RPM. Pretty hard. Emma pass Downhill.
    28. Same opponent and same car on Emma pass Downhill but EK9 has the lead and there's a grip bar on the top. Just be a bit careful and the EK9 is fast!
    29. FD vs. DC2 on Emma pass Uphill. DC2 super rocket luanch at start and it's very fast throughout the course.
    30. Happougahara course. 86 Mecha vs. EK9 (Master) There's Inbound and Outbound. Start with Inbound and if EK9 has 100+m lead of you, it's CLEAR. Then Outbound, you need to beat him. Your car lights will automatically shut off and on if the advantage meter is less than 30- and on if advantage is more than 30- or leading. NOTE: This opponent is super fast!!!
    31. Nightmare WRX. Cars are rolling and you start with 150km/h before the first hairpin with 86 Mecha. Becareful, this is Akina Downhill and Bunta's WRX is SUPER FAST!! Corners and straights. DO NOT MAKE ANY MISTAKE and take the turns at MAXIMIN speed or else you lose.
