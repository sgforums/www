    Break Down of Happougahara




    Happougahara - 5th Stage.

    Happougahara Strategy. Uphill. Blue Arrow
    This map is full of turns, so for most of the turns, i release my accelerator to gain control of my own car.

    Turn 2 - I use 3rd gear to make all the hairpin turns. The speed will be around 90km/h.

    Turn 3 - This is the most difficult hairpin turn, you need to get down to 85 - 95km/h in order not to hit the wall.

    Tips: Remember to use the Out-In-Out Technique for the turns.. so that you can turn at a higher speed and save you a few milli seconds.
