    Originally posted by -Kevin-:
    wats with the advantage metre?

    The advantage meter means that you will know how far your opponent is away from you in meters. If the meter is showing red, that means your opponent is infront of you, while white means that you are infront of your opponent. During the night, if you are trailing behind your opponent and you switch off your light, your opponent will not be able to see the distance in meters away from you.
