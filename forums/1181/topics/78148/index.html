    Initial D 4th Stage ep. 1 & 2 was aired on April 17th in Japan, channel 183. Bad news is that it's going to take a long time for all the episodes to be aired.. (ep. 3 & 4 will be on air on June 19th; ep. 5 & 6 will be on air on August 21st .. etc.) There are total of 14 episodes in 4th Stage. Initial D 4th Stage is going to be viewed on a Pay-Per-View basis. Each episode is around 25 minutes in length. Brief summary of ep. 1 & 2: Takumi joined the team Project D that Ryousuke formed. Project D's goal is to beat the best racer and set a record time in opponent's home course. (Both uphill and downhill) And it's ultimate goal is to conquer the whole of Japan. In episode 1 & 2, Takumi will face a tough opponent from the team S.S.R - Tohru Suetsugu, who drives a Mazda Miata (MX-5 Roadster) and in episode 2, Takahashi Keisuke with S.S.R - Atsuro Kawai, who drives a FR GT Turbo (Nissan Skyline), and a EK9 (Honda Civic) shows up, it's next challenger will be...
