  • kohanson
    1 May 03, 22:44
    Lap 1, Turn 1: At this time your car should be around the speed of 160 . This one you can go in by just releasing your accelerator, but the point is - start your turn early. If you release the accelerator abit latee then likely, you are going for the wall.

    Lap 1, Turn 2: At this time your car might be travelling at around 180. Start to release your accelerator when you see the second or third signboard on the left, then when you are getting closer to the curve, start your turn and step on brakes at the same time, but once you are halfway through the curve step back on your accelerator (you should be at about 160km/h at this time). Control your car back into its straight position and continue driving.

    Lap 2, Turn 1: Technique is more or less as Lap 1 Turn 1 but may need to step on the brakes. Should go in at max 165 and out min 155.

    Lap 2, Turn 2: Same as Lap 1 Turn 2.

    One word of advice, if you find that your speed drops until 130km/h, then pray hard that the Capuccino car doesn't catch up.
