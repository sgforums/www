    tried the game for the 1st time on my copyversion on PS2..

    my observations from a first-time player of the game:

      dunno what's going on coz it's japanese...

      beat the computer challenger easily from an anyhow choose car...

      the gameplay is really boring... prefer Gran Turismo...

      the "song" is irritating at best, but mostly annoying...

      gamepad play is not recommended but i couldn't be stuffed to buy a steering wheel, even for GT3

      smooth graphics.. very much like the arcade version which seems just as smooth

      very unrealistic physics... much like GTAdvanced where you can make hairpins in a street vehicle at 120 km/h (you WILL turn turtle no matter who says what when the tyres bite)


    personally, i think it's overhyped... trying to be too many things... it's trying to be simulator but the physics is all wrong.. it's trying to be an arcade game but does not have the over-the-top stuff like in Crazy Taxi... it's trying to be like a movie with a plot, but no one knows what's going on coz it's in Japanese... it's trying to be "bad" with unsavory characters around... it's trying to win over the "wholesome crowd" with a lack of mayhem...

    so it ends up being a very trying game...

    i'd rather play Def Jam Vendetta or Gran Turismo
