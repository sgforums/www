    Originally posted by Dark Conviction:
    The FD can hit very respectable times on Akina. You cannot turn using the FF method or a DC2 method since the FD Rx7 is FR and has a different turning radius.

    I do a 2:56 using the FD Spirit and the method i use to 'conquer' the turns is by 'rocking' the car towards the outside of a turn and then using the rebound of grip to throw the car into the normal cornering direction.

    Eg: Approaching a right turn
    1) I keep extreme right
    2) About 3-4 car lengths i make a dive to the left
    3) Shift down, slight brake, release accl
    4) Once i am 3/4 into the left, i then turn right attacking the right corner. (i turn slightly right , then when i get my car pointing in the apex of the corner, i make a full right into the turn).

    Not easy putting down in words but i hope this helps.
