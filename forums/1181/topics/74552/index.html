    MR2 Parts List

    Muffler:
    Tune A: Initial D (Kai's)* great startup and steady
    Tune B: Tom's
    Tune C,D: TRD
    Tune N: Toyota good acceleration, faster 4th/5th gear

    Tires:
    Tune A,D: TRD* grippy+drifty, nice balance, good start
    Tune C: TRD (Gold Rim)
    Tune B: Volk (IMO it slides a tad bit "too" much)

    Rear Spoiler:
    Tune A,N: Toyota
    Tune B: Tom's
    Tune C: C-One
    Tune D: TRD* very stable and good down force
    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
    non-changeable parts
    Tune B: Tom's front spoiler, side skirts, rear bumper
    Tune C: C-One front spoiler, side skirts, rear bumper* light weight
    Tune D: Aero Set.

    Notes:

    So the suggestion could be C-Tune with TRD Wheels, A-Tune Muffler, D-Tune Spoiler. Or if you believe in N-Tune then just change to C and alter parts after you reached full tune.
