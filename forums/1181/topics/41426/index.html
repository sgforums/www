    to change your settings, move the gear shift down or up to move between the options. To change a value, step on your Accelerator Pedal and turn your steering wheel.

    1. This is the BGM - Back Ground Music. On / Off / ??

    2. This is the Map Settings. Map On / Map Off / Paper Cup

    3. This is the Force Feedback of your Stering Wheel. 1 weak, 9 strong. The weaker your steering wheel, the easier it is you take to turn. This does not affect the turning radius.

    4. Meter Settings. Normal / Simple

    5. Not sure about the 5th Settings.
