    I have seen some other people do that for AE86. I think the reason is to get more power to the rear wheels during the turn so as to push the rear end of the car away from the side and round the turn.

    Quite a dangerous thing to do since at the point of downshifting, speed is turned into power and if your car is not aligned properly, this method is more likely to push your car into the side rather than out of the turn.
