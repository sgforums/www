    A Bug Card is a card where the points on it has become maximum. The maximum point allowable on the card is 999,999,999. Yes it is 999 million just 1 point short of 1 billion. Bug cards are easily identifiable since they have this many points. Sometimes the points actually go over the limit and you'll see something like this: "000,014,958". The display can only show 9 digits and so the 10th digit is chopped off.

    But there is nothing super about the bug card. Just that since it has all the points, the tunes for the car will be finished faster. The last tune is at 990,000. This can be reached by normal playing though it may take some time.
