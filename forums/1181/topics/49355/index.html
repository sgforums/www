    Message from Zone X:
    Zone X is organising a nationwide arcade competition as part of NTUC club's Up & 'Bout Singapore.

    The Ultimate Arcade Challenge wil lbe held along with the XBox Challenge at DownTown East, as a two-day event this weekend.
    Participants will pit their skills against each other in Initial D Version 2, Time Crisis 3 and Soul Calibur II.

    So far, over 1,000 have signed up, paying $10 each towards the Courage Fund.

    Zone X's assistant general manager Ang Gim Seng said he believes this is the largest local arcade competition ever.

    The top players can win trips to Japan to attend the Japan Amusement Machinery Manufacturers Association (Jamma) show, the Tokyo Games show and Sega Joypolis (not open to the public).

    They will also get $500 spending money for their trip there.
