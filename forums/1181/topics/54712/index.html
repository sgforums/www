    This 15k upgrade is your car tunes. Basically it's supposed to enhance your car. All cars will have this. The only difference is the course tune when your car has 0 - 120k points. A previous post did list out what the tunes are. I think it's one of the very early ones, so good luck looking for it.

    The part change is the game's recommendation for improving your performance. The game bases this on the race you just finished. It may not be accurate since you may be just having a lousy day. As for whether the part is effective, I think it should be although you'll need to know what it does.

    As for the parts change, the price is listed below:
    Parts Change
    5,000 : Exhaust Pipe
    10,000: Side Mirror
    40,000: Bonnet
    60,000: Spoiler
