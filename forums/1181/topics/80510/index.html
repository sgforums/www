    Today (Wednesday, 26 May 2004) at around 7.40 p.m. , I entered a Pasir-Ris bound MRT at the Aljunied MRT Station. In the train there was this girl with a pink shirt holding on to a grab pole. A weirdo who was wearing a blue long-sleeved shirt, a fake Valentino belt and black pants immediately went forward to clean the grab pole with disinfectant (germ killer) solution. This weirdo was aged around 15 to 17, according to my estimate. He was also wearing spectacles and carried a beige haversack.

    The girl's polyphonic ringtone at that time was "Night of Fire". When her phone rang as the train approached Tanah Merah MRT Station, the weirdo shouted at the girl "RINGTONE THIEF, YOU COOP MY FILES! INITIAL D SUCKS!".

    Another guy who was sitting beside me on the MRT told me that this weirdo had done such a similar verbal attack on two other people with I-D ringtones before, also in MRT trains. This guy himself was so scared that he immediately changed his ringtone, for his ringtone is "Burning Desire".

    Anyone with a similar experience should share it here. We want to get the weirdo and possibly try to check if he is an escaped mental patient.
