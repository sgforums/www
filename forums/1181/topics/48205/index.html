    Hello, the 7 stars on ur card does not indicate that u are level 7. For the first time u complete the story mode, u will get 6 stars on ur card. Subsequent completion of the story mode will give u 1 star. There is also the challenge level whereby the more victories u won over another player, the higher the level u will get. However, for the challenge level, it doesnt really matter how high ur level is. Cos i was at level 3 last nite and is able to win a level 10 player more than once. Most important is the skill that counts. Hope all this information helps.

    1. Complete Story Mode once (Max 6 Stars)
    2. Play Bunta Mode (win 1 game in each of the 4 tracks - 1 star, max 10 stars)
    3. Win 5 games in a Battle (1 star, max 10 stars)
    4. Complete Story Mode 2nd time. (Max 6 Stars)
