    Not Takumi in Akina (this race is not really difficult), it's Takumi in the new map you mentioned with the tunnel. He's the last black box with the question mark. Can't get past him. After winning Takumi in this race, then Bunta in Akina is unlocked.

    Nothing to pai seh about since you drive GC8V in this map. I think the RX will rule in this map. Tried my friend's new RX8 on this map and somehow it seems to fly as compared to GC8V.

    One thing you have to know is that for this map and the other new one is that you cannot knock at all. Or at least knock as few times as possible. I know it's not easy but try not to keep too close to his car to try to overtake him (I also guilty of this, too frustrated already ) since you cannot see the road and you're most likely to knock. Brake a little or slow down to let him go first if you cannot beat him at the start of the race. Against EVO and RX in this map, I don't think you can.

    There are a number of spots on the track that suddenly widen a bit and then narrow again, take note of these. It is these spots that you have the greatest chances of overtaking. Also try overtaking at those big turns since they are also wider. Once you do manage to get in front, don't screw up! Or he'll force his way through by banging into you. Don't worry even if he's only 10 meters behind you, if you do the turns properly, there's no way he can overtake you, just like when you're trying to overtake him.

    There is 1 turn with oil on the road. If you can, use it to get round the turn faster. Don't use it to crash into the side.
