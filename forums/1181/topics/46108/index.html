    You can only get a star when challenging people if only you don't take out your card even though you lose and continue to challenge them until you get 5 wins. It doesn't need to be 5 straight wins though. Say if you battle with somebody and you win and lose some rounds, as long as if you lose and you continue to challenge them until you collected 5 wins from them, you will get the star and level up. However if you collect 3 straight wins, the arcade machine will force you to take out your card and you collect 9000 pts for your 3rd win. Not sure if there is any other way to collect stars for battling with other player, but this is what I know.

    yes correct, for every 5 battle races you won, you gain a star. The maximum amount of star you can reach is 10 star. good luck!

    1. Complete Story Mode once (Max 6 Stars)
    2. Play Bunta Mode (win 1 game in each of the 4 tracks - 1 star, max 10 stars)
    3. Win 5 games in a Battle (1 star, max 10 stars)
    4. Complete Story Mode 2nd time. (Max 6 Stars)
