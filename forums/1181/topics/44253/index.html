    ok another news..

    The June issue of the UK car magazine EVO has an exclusive 5 page interview with Kazunori Yamauchi.. they caught up with him at the recent UK Millbrook testing event. In the article there are 8 pictures which appear to be from GT4.. most appear to be renders but two look very interesting.. the first shows what appears to be replay action on the Tsukuba circuit, the second shows a reply view of an EVO VIII Tommi Makinen rally car crossing the finishing line of the Grand Canyon - Tuweep rally course!

    Some photos from Millbrook showed the following cars.. TVRs (as previously reported), Jaguar (maybe Type R?), Noble M12 GTO, Aston Vantage convertible, Rolls Royce/Bentley, Leading Edge RT240, Caterham Fireblade, Triumph TR6? and a selection of vintage cars that I didn't recognise.

    Other snippets from the interview:

    "GT4 is due out by Xmas 2003" this could be a UK release, that means English version for us, singaporeans!

    "over 1000 cars will be included in GT4"
