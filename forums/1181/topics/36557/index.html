    Usui - 2nd Stage.

    Usui Dry Left Strategy. Red Arrow

    Turn 1 - Keep the speed at around 175 - 180km/h and try not to go below that.

    Turn 2 - In the movie Initial D, this corner is well known as the C121. If you are an experienced player, you will be able to drift pass this turn without knocking the curb. If you are not confident enough, release the accelerator pedal to gain traction.

    Turn 3 - Keep your speed at 198kmh. But i guess most cars will only be able to reach till around 205km/h.

    Turn 4 - Slow down to 165 - 170km/h for this wide hairpin turn. Reduce speed by downshifiting and mild braking.

    Usui Wet Left Strategy. Red Arrow

    Turn 1 - Keep the speed at around 170 - 175km/h and try not to go below that. You can reduce speed by downshifiting and mild braking.

    Turn 4 - Try to pass this corner at around 160 - 165km/h. You can reduce speed by downshifiting and mild braking.

    Tips: Remember to do the out-in-out rule for the turns.. so that you can turn at a higher speed and save you a few milli seconds.

    Myogi Wet vs Cappucino

    For the 2 hairpin turns, look to your left. For the first one, the railings will changeto a different type once it's near the hairpin. Downshift and release your accelerator pedal once you spoted different railings near the hairpin. Try to clear this hairpin below 155 to prevent knocking on the walls.

    For the second hairpion, downshift when you reach the last 2 sign board. Those 2 are white signboards which is placed quite close together. Downshift straight away you saw the first white signboard, your speed will drop to 155 - 165.
