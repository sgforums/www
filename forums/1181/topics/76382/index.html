    There are a few reasons that can explain the difference you feel.

    First of all, it is the car GC8V itself. At start (0 points), this car is already equipped with all the mods up till 120k points. Basically, it's not really a stock car as compared to the other cars at start. Therefore you would have found it easier using GC8V than using the other cars, up until 120k points. Oh and you should know, you can't have part changes for GC8V.

    Second of all, it may be that AWD is just your style since you did play with some other cars and found them not so good. Actually, it's not that the cars are lousy, it's just your preference/style. Also I think AWD is also easier to drive than the others.

    And finally, it's because subconsciously you memorized part of the tracks you have played on and so when you play them again, you're better than when you first played them. Basically you got more experience so your skills have improved.

    Don't be worried to use the brakes a lot when just starting to play this game. You need to figure out for yourself how you should handle each turn at which speed. If you constantly go in real fast and scrape the walls, you'll never find the proper way to handle the turns. Go in slow and then come out fast and not the other way around, fast in slow out. Try to do all your braking before the turn and accelerate out all through the turn. Doesn't matter if you brake too much, you can slowly adjust after you play more.
