    Taiwanese pop singer Jay Chau will be starring in a new movie called Initial D, a movie based on a Japanese manga comic strip. Playing other leading rols will be Edison Chen Koon-hei, Stephen Fung Tak-lun and a yet to be chosen Korean actress.
