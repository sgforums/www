    Originally posted by bismarck:
    what tunes for each car ?
    ermm tunes make little difference...different tunes make little difference to times, but each tune suits different driving styles.for the FD, Tune A is supposedly the 'best' tune to start with. if you choose tune B, it will have a big single turbo instead of the twin-turbo which the other tunes have. It is better for high-speed tracks. Also, choosing N-tune for the FD will be a good choice at inspection as it will make the car lighter. Just fit the necessary parts after that. For the R34, Tune C is the mid-high rpm tune, it is the best tune. Get the Mines and Nismo parts on it after you full-tune it. N-Tune for the Skyline is poor. For the Evo IV, go for tune A and then swtich to N-tune at inspection.
