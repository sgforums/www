    ER34, very nice car to use. Especially my Tune B one (got it because the Nismo bodykit looks good), it's grippy as heck, making it a fun TA car. The only change i made is the Initial D muffler which seems to give it a more powerful 4th and 5th gear.

    Battle-wise it isnt that amazing. its slow startup will get it killed by alot of cars on quite a number of places, but a savvy ER34 driver should be able to handle it.

    PS: someone once told me that the ER34 and RX-8 have quite similar characteristics in IDAS games. So actually if u're looking for a all-rounder that works everywhere, i recommend the RX-8

    er34

    tune b = rain
