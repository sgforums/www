    It's mentioned in kohanson's earlier post "Secret Moves and Tips of Initial D Arcade revealed". Just push the gear knob forward.

    The GC8 actually has a good acceleration though it won't reach top speed as fast as some of the other cars. The engine sounds funny, I agree but it is also quite loud. Change gear whenever you hear that the engine is dragging so you don't have to keep looking at the meter. Basically it just sound very high pitch. It will also be cool if you can perfect the tires screeching at starting.

    Since the GC8 is a 4WD car, all 4 wheels grip the road and you can achieve a better acceleration during a turn. But if you use this advantage wrongly, you're going to hit the barriers. This advantage also helps in tracks that have uneven road surfaces.
