    For the 2 hairpin turns, look to your left. For the first one, the railings will changeto a different type once it's near the hairpin. Downshift and release your accelerator pedal once you spoted different railings near the hairpin. Try to clear this hairpin below 155 to prevent knocking on the walls.

    For the second hairpion, downshift when you reach the last 2 sign board. Those 2 are white signboards which is placed quite close together. Downshift straight away you saw the first white signboard, your speed will drop to 155 - 165.

    I find a pretty good method to control the vehicle for the 2 turns is to play with the accelerator. When approaching the turn at about 200 meters, release your accelerator to about 1/2. The vehicle should still be able to maintain its speed but the accelerating force is gone, it may even decelerate slightly. Use the out-in-out method (pump the brakes a little if too fast) and you should be able to take the turn much easier as your vehicle will sort of slide through. This may vary slightly depending on the vehicle mods and driving wheels.
