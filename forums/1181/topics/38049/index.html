    The Point Scoring System

    As we all know Initial D arcade is characterized by allowing the player to accumulate points for tuning up their cars. But new players are unaware that after they accumulate the 120k points, they have not come close to the full tuning limit of their cars. A fully tuned car can mean different things, generally all your main car modifications are finished at 120,000 points. After that comes the maintenance work. This basically goes to 990,000. When your car is fully tuned, your car's handling, top speed, and acceleration will be increased. Below you will find a list of the maintanene schedule point intervals.

    note: This list is taken from Version 1. It might be around the same as Version 2.

    Tunings
    75000 : Advanced motor oil exchange
    90000 : Advanced differential fluid exchange
    105000: Advanced transmission fluid exchange
