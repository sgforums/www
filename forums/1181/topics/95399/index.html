    Well, the name.. quite obvious..

    the KEY simply identify your card. I am sure 2 person can have the same KEY but i doubt they will have the same name and same car. So if you ever lost your card, that will help u identify.

    The car type.. thats obvious too..

    The maps you completed. Once you defeated every opponent in that specify map once you will get a stamp of the map name on your card.

    Stars are simply your level of play. They are gain by completing maps, battle lvl and bunta challenge.

    A card for V3 can only play for 51 times. After that you will get into Car Inspection which will change your card. But your pts, stars, bunta challenge, battle lvl all remain. You can choose AT or MT and your Tune.
