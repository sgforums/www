    AT has no problems with the first 4 gears (for 5-gear cars) and 5 gears (for 6 gear cars). The problem comes only when the AT shifts to the highest gear, and that is shifting a bit too early.

    AT is slower than MT because you need to brake from a slightly further distance. Whereas if you use a MT car, you can downshift and brake at the same time, shortening the braking distance.
    Why not manually shift down the AT and brake since the game allows drivers to hit one gear down? If you do so, the AT has a tendency to go even one more gear lower as you corner, resulting in drastic loss of speed. These scenarios can be easily seen and experienced in Irohazaka.
