    Ok, as for the U-turns, there's a few of methods you can use to negotiate the various u turns. Like those turns in Iroha, you can change down to 3 to negotiate the first few turns while some of them, you can use gear 4 to go through. And if you don't want to brake, you can down gear earlier and use engine brake. Your Speed will drop faster than just releasing the pedal. And for some of the U turns, like in Akina, you can just use gear 4, and release the pedal to gain traction..
