    Irohazaka - 6th Stage.

    Irohazaka Strategy. Downhill

    For the first hairpin turn, depress the brake pedal and drop from 5th or 6th gear to 3rd gear for the turn. The recommended speed for all hairpin turns are 90 - 105km/h. As for me, I will usually drop down to 3rd gear 1sec before approaching the hairpin, and accelerate all the way when my car has passed 3/4 of the curve.

    At 1 part of the hairpin turns, you could drive into the bushes and cut down your time by a few seconds. There's 4 of them together, and you need to be at 3rd gear to negotiate the sharp turns ..

    Tips: Remember to do the out-in-out rule for the turns.. so that you can turn at a higher speed and save you a few milli seconds.
