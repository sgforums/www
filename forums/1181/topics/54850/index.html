    Most of my friends who use FD chooses tune A and some others choose D.

    There really arent that much difference to the tunes. Even the top class players have their own theories as to which tune is best. Ultimately, it goes down to which tune you like and find it suitable to your driving style.

    As for Irrou, the best car is no doubt DC2. But the FD is highly competitive too on Irrou if you learn not to hit the walls or guard rails. The pick up is faster and if you can block and maintain that lead, you should be able to beat most DC2 users unless they are top class.

    FD would dominate the 1st 3 courses (over DC2) and are literally the king of Akagi. Dry or wet, it doesnt matter.

    Hope this helps.
