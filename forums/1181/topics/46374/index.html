    This is how you get stars:
    Complete Story Mode first time: 6 stars
    Complete Story Mode second time: 1 star
    Complete Story Mode third time: 1 star
    Defeat Bunta one time in all 4 tracks: 1 star (max 10 stars)
    The remaining 12 stars come from your challenger level. You get 1 star at the following challenger level:
    Level 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, 15, 18, 21, 24, 27, 30
