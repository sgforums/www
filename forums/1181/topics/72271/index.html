    Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution IV RS (CN9A)

    Summary of tunes
    N tune - best in everything but may drift a bit

    A tune - best accel

    B tune - best speed

    C tune - cornering

    D tune - stable

    Engine/internals
    A tune - should have 300PS
    Other tunes unknown

    Wheels
    A tune - stock - average grip, average in rain, average start - all course
    B tune - Enkei white 5-spoke - excellent grip, average in rain, good start
    C tune - Enkei grey multispoke - good grip, good in rain, good start
    D tune - Valdi Sport gold 6-spoke - avg grip, excellent in rain, bad start
    no tune - Enkei white 6-spoke - excellent grip, good in rain, excellent start - all courses

    Muffler
    N tune - Mitsubishi single - avg accel, bad steer, good start, 1-2 good 3-5 avg - all courses
    A tune - Initial D Hawk/Seiji type single pipe muffler - avg accel, avg steer, excellent start, 1-3 excellent 4 avg 5 bad - last 3 courses
    B tune - HKS - good accel, good steer, good start, 1-4 good 5 avg - all courses
    C tune - Fujitsubo - good accel, excellent steer, avg start, 1-3 avg 4 bad 5 good - first 3 courses
    D tune - Bozz Speed dual muffler - excellent accel, excellent steer, good start, 1-3 good 4 excellent 5 avg - last 3 courses

    Rear spoiler/wing
    N tune - Mitsubishi - avg accel, avg steer, good in rain
    A tune - Initial D lightened Evo IV spoiler - good accel, good steer, avg in rain
    B tune - HKS GT wing - excellent accel, excellent steer, bad in rain, unstable at high speed, AVOID RAIN, great steering
    C tune - Valdi Sport Evo V-type spoiler - good accel, good steer, excellent in rain, great in the rain, stable at high speed, good steering
    D tune - Bozz Speed - excellent accel, good steer, avg in rain, not as light as B. very stable at high speed, OK in the rain

    Front bumper
    N tune - Mitsubishi - good accel, avg steer, stable
    A tune - Mitsubishi - same as N
    B tune - HKS - excellent accel, excellent steer, very unstable, super lightweight, get's "odd" at high speed, crap in rain
    C tune - Valdi Sport - excellent accel, good steer, unstable, lightweight, sometimes gets strange at high speed, OK for rain
    D tune - Bozz Speed (Evo IV bumper + additional spoiler lip) - avg accel, good steer, very stable, Great at high speed due to the extra down force, great in rain

    Stickers
    A tune - Monster Sport sticker - +1.5% accel
    A tune - Initial D Emperor sticker - +1.5% accel
