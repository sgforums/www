    Well here's an 86 parts list..you cant change parts on bunta's car..

    Summary of tunes

    A tune - the fastest but drift, very drift in rain, used by Takumi in Initial D

    B tune - more controllable, some people said the exterior looks like the Trueno used by the drift king of Japan, more control, OK in rain

    C tune - grip with the GT wing behind it and the funny bumper, but slower because of weight

    D tune - more or less the same like B tune, but little slower because of the hood

    Engine/internals
    all 86 Trueno tunes (N to D) will get 11000 rpm AE101 engine (about 240PS)

    Wheels
    N tune - Toyota wheels - easy to feint drift, have grip when needed (predictable handling)
    A tune - RS Watanabe black 8-spokes - very fast, drift, bad in rain
    B tune - Volk Racing TE37 gold - average
    C tune - RS Watanabe black 4-spokes - more grip, stable but understeer
    D tune - RS Watanabe silver 8-spokes - like A tune
    no tune - Volk Racing TE37 white - more or less like the gold version

    Muffler
    A tune - Initial D Takumi/Tak type muffler - fastest start, accelerates fast, acceleration tapers off at about 11500 rpm, powerful 3rd and 4th gear
    B tune - Techno Pro Spirit muffler - easier to push to 12000 rpm, more balanced power distribution
    C tune - Techno Pro Spirit muffler - same like above
    D tune - Ukiya Shokai muffler

    Rear spoiler/wing
    A tune - none
    B tune - TRD spoiler - more control
    C tune - TRD-type spoiler + GT wing - stability
    D tune - Ukiya Shokai spoiler that resembles TRD's - more control

    Front bumpres
    A tune - standard bumpers with Cibie foglights
    B tune - TRD bumper
    C tune - funny looking Techno Pro Spirit bumper
    D tune - Ukiya Shokai bumper with no. plate on the left

    Sideskirts
    A tune - none
    B tune - TRD
    C tune - Techno Pro Spirit sideskirts + wheelarches
    D tune - Ukiya Shokai

    Engine hood/bonnet
    A tune - Initial D Takumi/Tak type carbon fibre bonnet - lightweight
    B tune - TRD carbon fibre bonnet - lightweight
    C tune - Techno Pro Spirit GT-type carbon fibre bonnet with air vents - lightweight
    D tune - Ukiya Shokai GT-type bonnet with air vents

    Stickers
    A tune - Initial D Fujiwara Tofu Shop sticker - +2% accel
    not available for other tunes
