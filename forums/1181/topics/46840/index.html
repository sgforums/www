    To unlock this car, please follow the instructions.
    This car can be unlock when the first time you buy a car.

    1) When you are at the Car Factory Selection Screen where all the models are shown

    2) Shift your gear Down , Down , Up , Up , Down , Up , Down , Up

    3) depress the Brake, at the same time, push the View Change button

    4) Steer your steering wheel to select Subaru

    5) The Subaru WRX GC8V will now be selectable. It is Takumi Fujiwara's father, Bunta's Car..




    Good luck and have fun!
