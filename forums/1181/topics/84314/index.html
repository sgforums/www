  • HIRA
    2 Jul 04, 00:03
    For the 86 Trueno,

    A, B and C tunes get the AE101 racing engine at 120k. This engine gives great accel in the upper gears.

    D Tune gets turbo at 120k. This gives good accel in the lower gears, giving better start-up but possibly lower top-end.

    N Tune gets the normal engine.

    For N-Tune, yes you get internal upgrades exactly the same way as you would the other tunes.

    Turbo cars usually have a faster start-up, so you can say they have an effect.

    The RX8 is a 6 gear car.

    I hope that answers all your queries..
