    Originally posted by *ShiN*:
    wow... WRX.. how did you manage to do that? the fastest i did for uphill was like 197-198....
    Yeah this is quite tough to get. Not always successful myself either. First you need to enter the hairpin from the outer lane. You need to tap the brake very gently and the small point in time that you tap the brake is also the time you have to steer the wheel. Do not fully lock it, just a little bit before full lock. And this is the tricky part, you need to counter steer slowly when you are about halfway in the hairpin. Also after steering, lightly step on the accelerator and maintain it while in the turn. Once you're nearly out, floor it.

    This works for a 4WD though. I saw one guy using an RX and what he did was a double tap on the brake and then steer. He got 200-202.
