That's because a raunchy new survey by an online adult retailer has revealed that estate agents have more sex than any other profession. The Daily Mirror reported that property agents have found a way to relieve the stress of the credit crunch with those working in the housing business having sex four times a week - double the national average. Cabin crew came in second, rocking the headboard three times a week, followed closely by lawyers, secretaries and call-centre workers . Lorry drivers At the other end of the spectrum, lorry drivers were found to be the worst when it comes to sex - they only manage to get under the covers twice a week. Librarians, nurses, teachers and customer services representatives followed lorry drivers as the worst jobs to have when it comes to sex. A spokesman for www.onjoy.com, which carried out the poll of 3,000 adults, said: 'Despite the declining property market, estate agents are turning their cheeky chat and negotiating skills to other areas. 'With twice as much action between the sheets as other Brits, they're not letting the pressures affect their sex lives.' Nearly a third of those polled also confessed to bedding a colleague, with 28 per cent getting up to no good on work premises. While lorry drivers may have topped the list as the worst job for sex, they still did better than the average Singaporean. According to the 2007/2008 Durex sexual well-being survey, the average Singaporean has sex about 62 times a year, or roughly once a week.
