    Thinking of a quicky at Geylang? Think again!

    October 29th, 2010 | Author: Editorial


    If you are a married or attached man thinking of getting a quicky at Geylang Red Light District behind your woman’s back, think again!

    A website dedicated to “checking cheating Singaporean men in Geylang” has been setup to catch unfaithful men “in the act” and posting their photos online.

    Purportedly helmed by a Singaporean female, it professes to be “a dedicated team of private investigators whom do up this as a FREE service to the ladies out there in Singapore”.

    An introduction on the website claims the team conducts “…hourly patrol of geylang streets to take down pictures of your boyfriends, hubby, male relatives , friends or even male colleagues whom walk along the secret streets of geylang.”

    It further claims that “a list of car plate numbers will also be filmed down for you to validate your hubby ’s claim of doing overtime.”

    Man "caught" leaving a brothel

    The website also contains information and photos on the favourite parking places for cheating men who drives.

    Visitors to the website are also invited to contribute photos of “cheating” men by emailing the webmaster.

    At the time of this article, this writer counted at least 50 odd photos posted on the website supposedly of “cheating” men and women walking along various alleys in Geylang. There were no explicit photos of “cheating parties” caught in the act.

    So male readers who are thinking of visiting Geylang for a quicky, do not be surprised if sometime, somewhere, someone walks up to you and say: “Smile, you are on candid camera!”
