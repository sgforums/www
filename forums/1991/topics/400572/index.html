    Sexual dysfunction is the main reason for the unsatisfactory sexual life. Sexual dysfunction is defined as the inability in the men to get the erection or sustain the erection for the sufficient time during the love making session. The problem of sexual dysfunction has destroyed the sexual life of men. Relationship in the men and women is suffering a lot due to the sexual dysfunction. Married men are not able to satisfy their partners and are ending with the divorce. Sexual dysfunction has really become a bad nightmare for the men. Sexual dysfunction is noted among the 70% of the men in the world, either temporarily or permanently.
    According to research scientists who are trying to find out the causes of sexual dysfunction, the main reasons for Sexual dysfunction are physical factors, organic factors, or psychological factors. Among 90% of men suffering from Sexual dysfunction, physical factors or organic factors are found be the cause. Psychological factors were found to be the reason for sexual dysfunction in nearly 10% of men. Even the tiredness, anxiety, temporary decrease in libido, and lack of compatibility with the sexual partner can lead to the sexual dysfunction. Some times even the mental disorders like depression, less self confidence can cause sexual dysfunction.
