    Interview with Roger Ng, property sales agent.

    1) Do you put leaflets into HDB's mailboxes/doors as a means of advertising?

    A: Yes.

    2) Where do you put the pamphlets? in the doors, letterboxes or do you pay Singpost to do it?

    A: Both doors and letterboxes.

    3) How frequently do you do this? How much does it cost?

    A: As frequent as weekly, and it may cost between $400-$500.

    4) How effective has this advertising strategy been for you?

    A: It is effective especially when the frequencies increased and the vastness of areas expands. We want to extend distribution of leaflets as far as possible to attract the maximum number of customers.

    5) How do you hire people to distribute these leaflets?

    A: Through companies, where they provide such services.

    6) If you do not give out leaflets are there other alternative means of advertising that you employ?

    A: Through internet, newspapers etc.

    7) Do you think this is the best way to promote yourself? Why?

    A: There is no best way; every way has equal opportunities for business.

    8) Do you think people are indifferent or annoyed by these leaflets?

    A: There will always be a small group of people who are indifferent and/or annoyed. While, people who need to sell their houses now or near future will find this very helpful.

    9a) Would you be willing to switch to paying money to put adverts on notice boards at the void deck?

    A: Certainly. It seems more cost effective than employing runners.

    9b) How much would you be willing to pay to advertise this way?

    A: $300-$400

    10) Would you be willing to place pamphlets in plastic slots placed beside the lifts instead of putting it in mailboxes and doors?

    A: Sure do.

    11) How would you respond to a law imposed that bans advertisements in doorways?

    A: I always believe that there are many ways to reach the customers even if not putting flyers on doorways.
