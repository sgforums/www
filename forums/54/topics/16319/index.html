SAFRA IT Gaming Competition

  • red_beret
    3 May 01, 00:56
    SAFRA Bukit Merah is holding an IT Gaming Tournament from the 30 May - 3 Jun 2001. It will be a tournament with a difference! A total of 6 games will be used during the tournament. The games are Starcraft, Quake3, Red Alert2, Tribes2, Fifa2001 and Serious Sam.

    The Tournament will be played in teams of 4. In the Qualifying round, 3 games will be used. The top 32 teams from the Qualifying round will then be formed into 8 groups of 4 teams for the second stage. And the top 2 teams from each group will fight out in single elimination format using the other 3 games. The tournament rules & regulations will be out by 5 May.

    Entry fee for tournament is $10 for SAFRA member and $15 for guest. All participants get a goodies bag worth $30. Prizes will be given for early birds and best team name. Prizes worth over $10000 are up for grab for top 3 teams and best individual gamers for each game.
    For individuals who register alone, we will form a team for you.

    Beside the tournament, there is also a 24hr LAN Party where gamers bring their own PC minus the monitor and play overnight from 2 Jun to 3 Jun.

    24 hrs LAN Party - entry fee $10 just bring your PC monitor will be provided. Participants will get a chance to win in the lucky draw.
    CYBERWARRIORs, hesitate no more and log on to http://www.geocities.com/cyberextreme2001 for rules & regulations and application form. You can also email to Karen at tkaren@safra.org.sg or call Alan / Karen at 3773 672/671.
