    At Temasek Poly, the campus don't seem like a campus. The round entrance reminds you of star trek. I think their concept of 'resort' school is working out. The people inside are also the most interesting bunch of people compared to other schools. Read more about the Editor's Coments.

    Editors Comments:
    Temasek Polytechnic is a Polytechnic that tries to appeal to the young and trendy. All their Adverts and Marketing campaign are aim to make TP look Hip. Their Campus looks good actually, on par with NYP. Their Best Course should be Information Technology, Design and also Applied Science. When I visited TP, there are always lots of colorful hair, interesting clothes and characters to look at. I seriously wonder if its a school or a shopping center. But hey, isn't that what students want? A study place which is not a study place at the same time? If you like that kind of atmosphere, TP is the place for you. I've heard remarks a higher number of 'dropouts' in the school, it might be due to the 'resort school' concept or the Shopping centers and reservoirs nearby? But i seriously say that results are up to you, not really the school. TP is a 'concept' school. And because of that, with their reputable courses. I gave it 5 Stars for its achievements of building a school which is really FOR the students.
