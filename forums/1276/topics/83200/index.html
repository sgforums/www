    SEIZE THE DAY, JOIN THE RACE

    - THE STREETWISE RUN

    Keeping Young Lives On Track.

    Since 2001, Beyond Social Services has been organising the Streetwise Run on the 1st Sunday of July, Singapore's National Youth Day Weekend. This event draws public attention to youth issues & also provides an opportunity for youths from all walks of life to celebrate the vitality and promise of youth.

    Celebrate Youth Day with Beyond Social Services this 4th July at the Streetwise Run 2004. Challenge yourself and run for charity. They have in store for you a great race and a concert by the beach with your favourite MTV VJs - Denise Keller and Nadya Hutagalung.

    Run, Sing or Dance your way to attactive prizes and stand to win a pair of Return air-tickets to Bangkok, as well as Sony Ericsson and Samsung handphones.

    WHEN & WHERE is it?

    Sunday, 4 July 2004. The event starts at 12pm with the 1st flag off at 3.30pm, at East Coast Parkway, Carkpark F2 near Bedok Jetty.

    Registration forms and Full details of the Streetwise Run are avaliable at the website: www.beyond.org.sg or call 6375 2940.

    Please note that registration for all competitive categories closes on Wednesday, 30 June 2004, 7.30pm.
