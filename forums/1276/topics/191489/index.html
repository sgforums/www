    go to the provision shop or minimart to get some ant-poison..

    they are little blister-packs of this beige granules...

    put the stuff near the trails.. and if the ants do not take them, sweep away, take another lot from the blister pack, add a few grains of sugar, drip a few drops of water to get the sugar to coat the poison... then put near the trails...
