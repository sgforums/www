    ok guys I am back.

    I stayed at the Radius International which is average and quite convenient. Its just 10 mins walk from two major shopping centres; Times Square and Sungei Wang. Two great places to shop.

    Went to Petaling Street (Chee Chiong Gai in Cantonese). All the chiong branded stuff there. But disappointing as the stores are repetitive and they always quote 3 to 4 times their selling prices when they know you are not local. The prices not as cheap as those in Thailand.

    My friend was pissed off as some wanted to sleep at 10 plus in the night. He said that we did not go overseas to sleep. I apologise to him that I di dnot join him, as I was not sure if it was safe to walk around after midnight. The constant rain really put us off.

    I bought the thing I need and I am quite sure I did regret not buying anything. May go there again soon for short breaks.
