    For those who like sport & water activities. Shopping at Phuket town is a good deal too.

    Everyday suntanning and seeing topless babes at the beaches, esp Patong Beach. Can't take a photo of the beach w/o getting a topless babe in it.

    Every evening eating seafood. The seafood is very fresh and everyday we are better at bargaining for a lower price. The first day, We contacted a Singaporean tour quide and the bastard brought us to an expensive stall. Somemore we treat him . After that we move on our own bcoz we know the place liao.

    Every night drink Heniken in our Hotel till we drop. Buy from the mini marts or 7-11. Big bottle the most expensive is selling at most S$2.80 (70 baht).

    6 days is a good period to stay. No rush.

    Do
    1) Ask around for the price of everything (services, transport & shopping). Find out the range so that you will not be fleeced.

    2) More around on your own and try to arrange your own programme. The tour or hotel package is expensive. If you walk-in into the shops running the activities is a lot cheaper.

    3) Bring a calculator to convert the price. Your calculator is your communication tool on money saving.

    Do Not
    1) Do Not Buy from the Indian and middle east ppl running sovenior stalls. They will not hestitate to be rude to Asians and quote you up to 6 times. The locals ladies are a lot better as they respect tourist.

    2) Do not rush to buy anything. A better deal pops up when you search at ease. They usually quote 3 to 4 times the price when there is price tags. Bargain.

    3) Do not drive or ride on your own. When you are a foreigner, right or wrong during accident, you are wrong. Tons of money is needed to settle the accidents. Only car insurance exist and they cover the physical vehicle only only. Move around on their tuk tuk is quite reliable.

    I stayed at Kamala Bay Garden Resort. Very relaxing place. Room has pantry so we saved a lot by cooking our own makan, esp breakfast.
    Found a taxi driver called Arron Pattanee, who runs is own taxi services just outside the hotel. He is a reliable guide who speaks English. Brought us around at a good price. We feel safe with him. He got a lot of lobang for the activities at Phuket.

    Will go back there again
