The 'expert' at the shop told us we have missed the correct hours to burn 'items', and told us to do it the next day before 11 am. On that day I was wondering if the dream was just a meaningless one.

    I told my father and my brothers about this dream. Sad that they told me since my mum passed away, they have NEVER dream of her at all.

    I am wondering if I missed my mother so much that it caused me to have dreams of her. Or did she 'approach' me due to some factors?
