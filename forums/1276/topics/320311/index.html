    Buona Vista constituency launches community garden at Holland Close

    SINGAPORE: It’s harvest time at the Buona Vista constituency, courtesy of the newly launched Buona Vista community garden at Block 3A, Holland Close.

    Minister in the Prime Minister’s Office Lim Swee Say, also joined residents to reap the fruits of their labour.

    The community garden was set up to engage retirees and the elderly. It also helps residents of all ages to bond with one another.

    The community garden is open to all residents daily from 8am to 9am and 5pm to 6pm. — CNA/vm
