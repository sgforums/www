    Max Brenner's Chocolate Bar
    #01-06 Esplanade Mall
    Tel: 6235-9556
    Price: $18

    The fondue here comes in separate little pots, one each for white, milk and dark chocolate.

    The pots bear a striking resemblance to regular aromatherapy burners, but a water bath between the tealight and the chocolate prevents the latter from getting burnt.

    The side serving of fluffy marshmallows, fresh fruit and sweet bread disappears quickly, but you can order more.

    Haagen Dazs
    #02-01/03 Esplanade Mall Tel: 6336-4022
    #01-34 Anchorpoint Mall Tel: 6479-6495
    Price: $15.90 (serves one), $24.90 (serves two)

    Seasonal fruit and - get this - bite-sized cubes of Haagen Dazs ice-cream, are served with the fondue here. The ice-cream is deeply frozen so it will not melt instantly when swirled around in the luscious pool of warm Belgian chocolate.

    Once covered with chocolate, twirl the sticky sweet delight in a bowl of finely-chopped nuts and let it slide slowly down your throat.

    Union Bar/Restaurant
    81 Club Street
    Tel: 6327-4990
    Price: $14+++

    Expect something different from the Callebaut chocolate fondue each time you dine at Union Bar. While the premium quality of the Belgium chocolate served remains the same, the fresh fruit varies according to season. For an extra $5, the waiter will gleefully tip some whiskey or rum into your chocolatey pool.

    My Humble House
    #02-27 Esplanade Mall
    Tel: 6423-1881
    Price: $12

    The sturdy bowl of melted chocolate gets outshone by its elegant sidekick - a plate of juicy red strawberries sitting daintily on a bed of crushed ice. As if that is not divine enough, you also get two curls of lavender chestnut ice-cream to complete the heady experience.
