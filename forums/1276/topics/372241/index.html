    I was approched by a young boy and a young girl (teenagers) at the foot of my block just now.

    The girl called out to me and said that she and the boy are sellng ice-cream to raise money for their mother who is very ill in hospital. On hearing that I would presume that the boy is her brother. The girl said they have only one box of ice-cream left. She said she is selling at $16 and they can earn $4. They hope to sell the last box and they want to rush down to the hospital to visit their mother. They show me their styrofoam box and it was the last box. They sound convincing and desperate.

    I seldom buy or eat ice-cream. But out of pity, I agree to buy from them. I only have $15 and the boy sold the box of ice-cream to me. When I reach home, my wife saw the box of 'Magnolia' ice-cream I bought and told me it only cost abt $6.

    A while later I found the boy outside my flat corridor selling another box to my neighbour. After that he was using his handphone to call his friends to meet him at the corridor. Then the same girl earlier and two more other teenager turned up at the corridor with styrofoam box. I then realise they are a group. They were happily pooling in their money. I then knew their story is a fraud.

    I went up to them and asked the boy how many of them is selling ice-cream. I asked him why he told me its the last box and why he lied to me. He just said "we are really leaving". I hand the boy the ice-cream he sold me and demanded for my money back. He gave me back my money and said sorry. After I went into my flat, I heard one of them telling the boy as they were going into the lift, 'You should have sold him at $10, you still earn."

    Just to let you guys know what I encountered. Sympathy being abused.
