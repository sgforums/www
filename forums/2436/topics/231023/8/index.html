    The Vineyard

    Simply put, the best wine comes from the best grapes. Using our years of knowledge and unprecedented experience, Amphorae experts sought to find the perfect vineyards to produce our wine.
    The vast amount of our cabernet Sauvignon comes from selected vineyards in the Golan Heights and the Upper Galilee -- relatively cool climate regions yet differering in their teroir. From the volcanic soil of the Golan Heights to the Terra Rosa of the upper Galilee, each of the vineyards gives the wine its own unique input.
    At Amphorae Vineyard we do our own planting to ensure high quality in the future. We picked the perfect spot in the Upper Galilee at 650 meters above sea level. We planted 10 hectare with Cabernet Sauvignon, Shiraz, Chardonnay and Vigonier. The staff at Amphorae vineyard have devoted their expertise and passion into making these grapes worth waiting for.
    Part of our vineyard program includes also organic grapes to make organic wine. Fortunately the winery is located at the heart of Makura ranch, which is an exclusively organic ranch. We planted Merlot in the ranch in a unique spot, where cool winds from the sea gently flow through the vines. We anticipate that within three years we will have a world-class organic Merlot.
