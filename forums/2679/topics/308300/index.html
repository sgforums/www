    My coffee shop auntie know I like Marlboro Ice mint, so she recommended me the cheaper Next Chill Menthol.. I bought it just awhile ago nia..

    'Coolness' is as though the same as Ice Mint.

    Those Ice mint regulars who tried Chill menthol will notice some difference I noticed it too.

    1) Next Chill menthol's smoke is less dense...
    2) Next Chill menthol's got a 'cheap' taste.. unlike the 'premier' Marlboro Ice Mint
    3) You finger will smell less bad with Chill Menthol than Ice Mint
    4) The Chill menthol's smoke lingers on you a lot shorter time than Ice Mint's.
    5) Chill menthol's Plastics wrapping as compared to Ice Mint's metallic wrapping


    Anyway, Chill menthol's is a substitution/replaced for Ice mint in term of Quality and price.

    Something I dislike about Chill menthol is that, you can smell that the menthol is added in excess to give the same coolness as Ice Mint.

    Tar and nicotine level the same, at 8mg and .6mg respectively.
