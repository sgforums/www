Whisky Galore - Compton MacKenzie

    The story is set in the middle of the Second World War, in Scotland.



    Everything is in short supply and rationing is the order of the day. When food runs low, the people take it on the chin.

    However, when whisky starts to run out, the town starts to panic.

    Morale is at rock bottom and they've broken out the pickaxes.

    There is no more joy, no more conversation, no more fun.

    Then a miracle happens, as a ship loaded with whisky runs aground near the town.

    Read what happens when the whole town is united in conspiracy. Hilarious!

    3 1/2 out of 5 for alcoholism.
