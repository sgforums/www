    No Reservations - Anthony Bourdain



    This book is the chronicle of the television series of the same title, but instead of mirroring what happened in the series, the crew has taken pictures and there are thoughts, feelings and incidences which did not make the series.

    There are also little touches and pictures like the food eaten, the dishes tried, the alcohol imbibed and where to find them which adds a personal touch.

    Highly recommended if you are a fan of the series. 4 1/2 out of 5
