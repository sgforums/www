    However, they cannot see past the "smut peddler" part, even after he has fought more than almost everyone to protect the freedoms that the US are proud of.

    .. and he's paid out...

    If a man can easily find such facts, anyone can. In fact, he laments the dumbing down of the US society.

    Then it hit me: we may be going the same way.

    Read the book.. and be horrified. Hopefully into action.

    Highly recommended! 4 out of 5
