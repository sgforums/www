    Kenji is a "personal tour guide" whose job is in fact, a sex tour guide.

    Things start to become strange and scary when Frank, an American tourist becomes his latest client.



    Kenji suspects his client is a serial killer as he looks into the eyes of Frank and finds something that causes him to fear for his very soul.

    Kenji's moral paralysis and complicity in the activities of his client reveals the rotten interior of his society.

    Ultimately, it is his girlfriend who saves him, leaving him to wonder if his society is lost in their rush for instant gratification and pleasure.

    Recommended (but an acquired taste) 3 1/2 out of 5
