    Anyway, good to great is a good book for anyone interested in Business Literature written in a less academic tone. It's definitely more readable vis-a-vis harvard business revew, etc.

    In short this book sets put to ask "Can a good company become a great company and if so, how?" Jim Collins and his team of researcher thus sets out to look at a myriad of the largest corporations in America in search of the answer.
