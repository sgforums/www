    The Wind-up Bird Chronicle by Haruki Murakami



    The story starts out when Okada's car goes missing. This unsettles his wife and lots of strange things start to happen to Okada. His wife grows distant, he gets strange phone calls and strange people move in and out of his life.

    His wife leaves him and his life falls into a strange spiral of weirdness where the strange people all have strange stories to share with him.

    Ultimately, everything is intertwined in a surreal and fascinating tale of desperation, despair, hope and redemption.

    A very recommended read, 4 1/2 out of 5.
