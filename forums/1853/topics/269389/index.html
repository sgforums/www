    Blind Willow, Sleeping Woman - Haruki Murakami



    A collection of Murakami short stories. It is a glimpse into his imagination, where he weaves tales of the mundane to the surreal. He tells of tales with themes of loss, sexuality, wonder, love and more.

    Some themes are reused in his novels, some strangely familiar, some daringly original.

    A wonderful read. 3 3/4 out of 5.
