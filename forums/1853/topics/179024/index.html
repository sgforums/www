He Died with a Felafel in his Hand - John Birmingham

  • the Bear
    19 Feb 06, 14:03
    For all who have had the horror of sharing house, thinking of sharing house or are sharing house with people.



    The book cover says "Hilarious true stories of House-Sharing Hell"

    Personally, I've been through some of these things written inside. Stories that I find myself actually nodding my head to.

    This book details with brutality, yet sometimes with surprising tenderness of the different sordid stories of roommates, housemates and all in between. Of Goths, junkies, loners, psychos and potheads.

    I read it all in a day. And occasionally, I still do read this book. Just to keep my feet on the ground, and remind me where I have been.

    Recommended (needs strong stomach and dark sense of humour): 4 out of 5
