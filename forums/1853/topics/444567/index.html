    Extract of Jef Yen :

    How to buy e-books from the Amazon US store

    • Again, do NOT buy e-books (even if the books are free) using the Kindle's Whispernet/3G/WiFi.
    • Ensure that you have sufficient funds in your account. If not, buy more gift cards and apply them to your account.
    • Exit your Internet browser.
    • Switch on your VPN, open your Internet browser and go Amazon. Find your book and click 'buy now with 1-click', 'deliver to your Kindle'. If you choose to wirelessly send it to your Kindle, you should receive the book in less than a minute. You can also choose to download the file and transfer it to your Kindle using USB. If asked for the billing address, use any US address. Note that this address will not be used because funds will originate from your gift card balance.
    • Switch off the VPN.
