BooksActually Is Actually Giving Away Free Reads

  • FireIce
    26 Jun 15, 18:06

    Christmas has come early for book lovers!

    In a novel interpretation of their biannual sale, BooksActually – in the spirit of sharing the joy of reading – is giving away free reads. Like any good book, this BooksActually sale with longtime collaborator Kinetic Singapore, comes with a good ol’ twist. While 25% off is offered for the books in-store (which is as good as giving away a quarter of each book for free), the same goes for online reads as well! This is effectively means you get to also choose from a range of diverse titles online and enjoy a free read of a quarter of each book.

    Image Credit: thebookquarter.com

    We on the same page? Hurry on over to the aptly titled The Book Quarterwebsite to read the generous selection of lovely books because the ‘pop-up’ site’s chapter ends when the sale does too!
