    He was a percussionist of the band and I'm very sure he would love to meet the band members again. It would be really nice if all of the members can play the instruments tgt again and reminisce the glory days.
    Please let me know if there are any meet ups or anything I can do to help gather all the alumni, it would be a pleasant surprise for my dad:)
