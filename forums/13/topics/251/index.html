    Hi people...

    Jus wanted to share my experience... my first crash!!

    If you think you are a careful rider and never expect to get into any accidents... well.. think again. I don't mean to scare anybody.. jus wanna make sure u guys are always prepared and properly geared up to protect yourselves.

    I recently skidded off a bent and crashed into a grass verge (thank goodness). Tho it was only a minor accident I fell heavily on the right side of my body and was badly bruised and scratched. As most riders the initial thot was "doh! my bike" but then i soon realised the extend of damage on me rather then the bike.

    As I "thought" that I was only going kopi with my friend I was wearing jus my t-shirt... besides the weather can get hot. I ususlly wear my jeans and boots when i ride.

    When i picked myself up from the fall I soon noticed that my t-shirt had been ripped up pretty bad with holes in many areas. I was scratched and cut on my right arm. chest, stomach and shoulder. But becoz of my jeans and boots.. my legs and toes were saved from injuries that would most likely have occured coz i noticed the marks on them. It also pays to get a good helmut coz i hit my head pretty hard against the ground but I was spared coz i was using my arai on that day.

    To cut a long story short... u guys be careful out there especially during the wet weather.

    1. Always wear proper gear while riding. Jeans, proper shoes, jacket, gloves if u want.

    2. Use a proper approved helmut coz that is the most important thing that protects your fragile little head from the floor

    3. Ride save and don't do anything stupid.

    Jus my 2 cents worth... better someone else experience a crash and live to tell others about it... so hopefully u guys take these little advise.

    Happy riding and take care out there

    Transcendence

The guys said that the, un-kept and long, grass broke my fall.
