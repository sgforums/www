    Whatever you wanna do with your insurance, you must first ask the bike shop. Some bike shops dont allow you to do the insurance yourself....like mine.

    I'm riding an SP too and I just renewed my insurance. I checked out NTUC's prices(not the online one) and they were more expensive than Mitsui. I changed from 1st party to 3rd party.

    All you gotta do is to go down to your bikeshop and check it out, shouldn't be too difficult.
