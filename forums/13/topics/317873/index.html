    running in, usually should be around 3000-5000km i think.

    and the fastest you should go is at around 30% of its top speed.

    so let's say last time i had a S4. i could go up to 180, 20% is around 80-90.

    but frankly, u should read the manual. because for my current bike, the run in is totally different from what i used to do for my previous bikes... and its stated in the manual how to run in my bike.


    what kind of accesories are you looking for? i usually head to Ah Boy (Kelantan Lane) or Motoworld (Ubi Industrial Park), good service workshop... u gotta ask around because i usually send back to the guy who sold it to me. Anyway when i service its just normally oil change, so no matter where you go... how different can an oil change be?

    yes, i wash my bike... when i have the time.

    pointers? most important parts of your bike u have to note constantly are your BRAKES and TYRES.

    you can feel these 2 wear off as u ride, so always be sensitive to these 2 areas.

    most important pointer for yourself is to ride safely and get a proper helmet.
