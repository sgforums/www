    Just go to any bikeshop to check the chain tension (normally free if u patronise the shop, worst cut throat also ard $5).

    Remember that if you do change the chain, the sprockets will have to change too. Different bikes have different chain size and prices. For your bike shld be ard $100++ depending on o ring or x ring.

    Buy chain lube and spray on the rubber seals on the chain. (any bikeshop will sell) Take diesel or kerosene with a toothbrush to clean the chain , brush off all the grease and sand particles so you can relubricate the chain.
