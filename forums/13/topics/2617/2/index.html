    regarding your bike choice, best way to avoid depreciation, is to get a used bike. when you get a new bike, even when you sell like in 1 or 2 months, it knocks off at least $500 off the value. reasoning behind this is that you gotta entice the buyer to buy your bike, over paying the small difference and getting a new one. certain bikes depreciate faster, like continental bikes. so, get a used one, and one with good resale, like erm.. honda or yamaha bikes. i was told this lar.. a new cagiva planet costs about $11k, 2 years later, it sells for about $6k at the dealer.. so deduct the markup that the dealer gives on this bike, you can figure out how much the owner loses...
