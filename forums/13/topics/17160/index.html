    Me riding my friend's Millenium version TZM(under sub-rider's name) while he's in BMT, taking care for him. Heard from Miss Siti over at Heong Leong that TZM now got year 2001 version, and in different colors.

    Generally, TZM is a good performance bike. Seat is larger than most of the 2B bikes and top speed is highest(not considering mod KRR) and if mod, can go even faster. Parts are also cheap but fuel consumption I believe, would be a bit more than SP. However, the good thing is TZM don't give out much smoke due to the 2T "regulator".

    Read on the manual that the "regulator" only injects sufficient 2T to lubricate the piston, so can save a tiny bit on 2T lah. No need to top up so often. Me using Castrol TTS, results not bad.
