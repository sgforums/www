    10pm she called and asked me to go JB, my brain tells me is not so sfe on my 1983 bike but my heart says must go.

    11pm got off from work and went to add water to the radiator incase bike overheat, still wondering why my temp meter not working.

    12am meet up with her and went to bank in some cash , not safe to carry too much when traveling

    1am reach JB and do the most important things, pump super cost effective petrol and buy cheapo and nice HOON KI

    2am wanted to go sentosa to makan but still cannot find the way

    3am made a u-turn when i found myself 11+ km from JB onwards to KL ....opps there is a bunch of malay Kup Zai riders... felt unsafe , better ride slowly, 80km.

    4am finally reached Eden side the nice and Ex kopi tiam and made my order of cuttlefish kangkong, BBQ fish, deep fried squrriel, stir fried bamboo clams,... ... .. not to 4get the smelly fried beancurd. ermm the bill came up to be abt 50 ringgit. Think Kulai much better place to makan =)

    5am reach tampines and saw a unconcious TZM rider and his bike at a turn fron Eden to custom. luckily i am a safety rider =) hope the guy is alright. looked at my meter and found that the disturn from JB to tampines is 30 km.

    Hope to go again soon , by the way the suqirrel meat taste good, i wonder if it is rat or something. luckily i did not tell her my suspicion
