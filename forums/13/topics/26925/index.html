    There are few way the engine oil can get into the combusion chamber, usually is the piston rings broken. Shoud not happen to a new bike, unless you really push it to the limit. (If the piston ring broke, the engine will be noisy).

    If the 4T oil get into the piston, you will see black smoke.
    Try smell the exhaust gas abit, if the 4T leak in to piston, you should smell some oil in the exhaust gas. Normally 4strokes engine should be quite clean.

    Dont adjust the carburetor if you are not sure about it. Pay a bit to a good mechanic for some tuning.

    4 strokes bike should sure quite lasting, no problem to ride for 10years if you really taking care.
