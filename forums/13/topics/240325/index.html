    1) Just look at the spinning rubber belt at the left foot side...your slipper can easily get caught in it and crush your toes!

    2) The long stretch to the handle bar means in an emergency braking, you will not be able to have the necessary support. The oil tank is not there to stop your crouch from sliding forward. Your ape hanging hands will not be in an ideal position to support your body forward momentum.

    3) The turning angle is atrocious, you cannot do U-turn safely.

    4) Most of them got NO rear suspension, some only got the 'ah-peh' kind of spring seat you find on bicycles. You backside will split in no time.

    5) Do you think you will feel secure parking you bike in any shopping center or HDB carpark? Ok, you may live in a private house or condo, but you would have to park somewhere while you are out riding right? In good case, at most you get gressy fingerprints on your chrome parts.
