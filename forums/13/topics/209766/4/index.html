    before moving is not classified as a "blind spot check", its a "check back"

    so EVERYTIME before u move, do a check back
    while u'r approaching the turn (about one bike distance from the turn), do a "blind spot check".

    u must know which one is checking back, which one is blindspot check. so u won't feel like you're doing the blindspot check twice.
