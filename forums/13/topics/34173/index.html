    chim Question u got here:

    basically i classify servicing into 2 types: minor and major

    minor servicing includes the washing of carb, air filter, changing of gear oil, spark plug

    major servicing includes the washing of engine block, piston head, piston rings, power valve, changing of coolant

    for my case, i do minor servicing quarterly and do major servicing annually...

    hope tis helps
