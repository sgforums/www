    Originally posted by j007:
    u mean i have a worn out clutch cable??

    just checked with bike shop, told me to go down for some fine tuning...
    fine tune wat? clutch freeplay?

    u see to my understanding wat happened was that my clutch cable worn out such that i need to adjust my clutch until the biting point is far away. the tension in the cable was worn out such that at fully clutch in it still did not clear the biting point ie still at biting point with fully clutch in. dats why at 1st gear cannot kick to neutral, but solved when switched off coz engine not running will be able to engage.

    i solved it by adjusting biting point very far away. no probs but i wasnt comfortable with the far biting point so i changed the cable. like i mentioned, u can try checking by adjusting such that ur biting point is further away then usual. if it completely solve ur problem then its the cable. u can oso check by clutching in fully, at 1st gear, rev; if u can feel the bike wanting to move forward even at fully clutch in then u HAVE to adjust ur freeplay.
