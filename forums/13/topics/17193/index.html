    The bigger the tyre, the more contact and grip between the tyre and surface. Thus, increased stability..but with more contact and grip..it would be slower. Thus, less contact and grip, the faster the bike will enter and exit a corner. About the acceleration part, gear ratio can be adjusted to suit oneself's capabilities. Changing a sprocket size to a bigger one would improve acceleration speed but decrease the top end. Good example is scrambler's sprocket. Notice how big it is?
