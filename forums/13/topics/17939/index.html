    transfer fee = the fee you pay for transfer of ownershop of bike.
    if i remember correctly, is either $30 or $65 or $75 at LTA.

    road tax and COE is issued to the bike, not the owner. doesn't have to be transferred. road tax has to be renewed, either annually or every 6 months.

    insurance.... the headache part...
    some insurance plans are non-transferable, meaning when you buy the bike, you have to get a new insurance.

    you can get a new insurance either by going down to the comnpany yourself to buy the policy, or buy through the motorshop. it's cheaper to buy thru the company, becoz... "aiyo u oso know, we open motorshop, oso must earn money one mah..."

    but if you buy a new bike, then lan lan gotta get from chia pang. only buy from private owner, or renew insurance then can buy straight from the insurance companies.
