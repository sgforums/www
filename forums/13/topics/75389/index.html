    Well, if you change yer current fireblade to a sportster, first thing first, there are a few models of sportsters..Which model?? The model will define yer foot control.. E.g A 2003 Sportster Sport has mid-foot control for a more sporty posture and a 2003 Sportster Custom has forward-foot controls for a cruising down the boulevard look..... The torque for the sportster is scary i would say...but I think the haya would beat the sportster flat... But of course you can't compare a sports-tourer to a cruiser...

    Cons: Only this year 2004, sportster starts to have the engine rubber mounted to reduce vibrations... so most 2nd hand sportster should be heck of a "shake"... Don't expect your harley sportster to be loud if you are not willing to spend money to mod the bike e.g Slip-ons, Screamin' Eagle Air Filter.....

    Adv: Sportster is the cheapest and lightest harleys and have similar handling to its big brothers, the Dynas and Softails

    I can only give you some advice on Harleys as I know little on sports bike

    My 2 cents...
