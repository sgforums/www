    Not saying impossible, but it is quite difficult to have digital speedo which is accurate (unless the speedo is come with the original motorcycle).

    Let's said .. the speedo is came from bicycle which tyre has xx cm in diameters... now you put on motorcycle which may be larger or smaller than xx cm.. the digital speedo will read more wrongly than your bike original analog speedo... I am not sure .. unless there is a tool to set up the speedo to use the appropriate tyre.. then, it is possible. But how? The tuning must be done by test machine box perhaps.
