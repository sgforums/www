    WR200 - There's no new wr. The latest would be FQ plate. So far, this is the best dirt bike for 200(my own opinion). It is cheaper to maintan and the spare parts are also cheaper. FQ and FP plate would cost u abt $7K and $5.5K - $6K respectively. It's heavier than KTM and actualli also heavier than nsr150 (i tried my frenz repsol) but due to the high center of gravity, they tend to feel veri light when going at high speed. A finely tuned engine can give u an excellent km per litre ratio. My wr travels ard 23-25km per litre of petrol. Full tank is ard 8-9 litre.

    KTM200 - Gives the most power but also veri expensive to maintain. It drinks petrol and 2T like water. 15km per litre and full tank is at 10 litre. A new bike cost abt $10-$11K(price at 2 months ago).

    GasGas - Quite powerful. Rare to find oso. Veri expensive to maintain. Not much info....

    KDX - Old bike. Quite problematic i heard. Not much info....

    XR200 - You forgot this bike. 4 stroke engine. Not veri fast but excellent for road travel.

    DR200 - Quite similar to xr. Heard this baby can give 35km per litre. I'm not sure if this is true.

    If u do some light trailing.... it would be better to get a wr. If u are into heavy trailing.... its best to get a ktm(for trailing only)and another bike fo road travel.

    Surprisingly, WR and KTM are quite easy to control even if u are travelling at 90km/h. It's unlike what people say. However, u cannot play corner lah... rainy day oso must be careful (but which rider shouldn't???).

    Hope u find wat u r looking for....cheers!!!
