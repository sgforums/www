    KTm200 is a 2 stroke bike. White smoke is very common in 2 stroke bike. Most likely due to too much 2T. Due to the heavy wear and tear of this particular bike, the 2T input is quite high and unburnt excess 2T turn out as white smoke.

    Bike not idling properly maybe due to too low idling rate, improper settings of carburettor esp pilot jet and the pilot screw.

    Gear problems maybe due to gear oil which have lost it's properties or worn out clutch plates..Other factors may contribute too.

    My advise is pay a trip down to Paul Chow to have it checked.Trail Culture Engineering 282 MacPherson road. 65474454

    Mechanic

    scrams have lighter flywheels, which means that dying from idling even when warmed up is not uncommon... so... make it a habit to play throttle yeah?

    plug-fouling occurs frequently for scrams... sad to say... KTM is notorious for that. buy a spare plug and keep with you la.. if your bike is very very hard to start again, change the plug.

    but please do as mechanica suggested. it's after all a second-hand bike, so you've no idea what's wrong with it. best bring it for a thorough check to solve all hidden problems before they surface one-by-one. better to do everything one shot, or keep going back every other day??
