    used to pillion on Super 4.. quite comfy.. it's true that pillions will slide down towards rider but i think if the pillion not too heavy, shld not be a prob to the rider..

    as for pressing down on tank, i guess it's a natural reaction coz by pressing against the tank, u wld think that u won't be pressing against the rider (causing him discomfort).. BUT it can be extremely tiring.. plus if the rider suddenly breaks, the pressure on the pillion's wrists can be nerve-wrecking (literally).

    thus my suggestion wld be to juz relax and hold on to the rider. coz if u tense urself up, can get body cramps.. now, u wldn't want that, wld u?
