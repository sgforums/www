    Originally posted by yinjie:
    hello anyone know why my sp spit watery black oil from the maffler???
    Most likely your 2T oil consumption is too high. Have you ever measured your 2T consumption? Do you see a lot of white smoke coming out your exhaust, even when the engine has reached normal running temperature?

    For SP, 1 litre of 2T for every 40-50 litres of petrol is normal. The factory setting is even lower, about 1 litre for every 70 litres. Try to adjust your pump, then see if there's still black oil coming out from your muffler.

    improper tunning / modification .. leading to a too rich mixture that may not be combust fully
