    Tong ur pipe, means cut open ur pipe, remove all the blocking chambers inside. These chamber are actually to reduce the air flow from your engine, so that its power will be reduced, also known as (powercut).

    So when u tong ur pipe, gradually you can get the maximum air pumped out from your engine, thus increase the acceleration of your bike.
