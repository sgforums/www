    for TA150

    cruising at 6th gear:
    -80kmh at 4000rpm (most comfortable speed, engine sound very quiet)

    -90kmh at 5000rpm (getting abit stressed at mid range, but enough for overtaking vehicles on the left lane)

    -100km/h at 6000rpm (the engine sounds slightly different from wat u hear at 80km/h, gd for cruising on the expressway, speed limit 90km/h is actually 100km/h on ur analog meter)

    -110km/h at 7000rpm (valve opens, different engine sound, can feel the acceleration if u throttle more)
