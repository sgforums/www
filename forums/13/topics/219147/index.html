    To get out of the mini-circuit, you have to show the instructors that you are generally comfortable on the bike and know the basics :

    - look far ahead when turning; look well _past_ the turn. You may have to learn to lean the bike a little. Actually, leaning the bike gives it more traction on the ground, so don't be afraid of that.
    - throttle a little more when turning; don't press your front brake esply.
    - don't jam your brakes when stopping
    - you got to look and feel relaxed.
