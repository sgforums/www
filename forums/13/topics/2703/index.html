    Dear friends,

    I was travelling across from west to East twice yesterday. Then halfway , i kept thinking if i buang which way should i fall over/fling myself towards ......

    I have never fallen before or buang before, even during pract. Then was thinking oh shit, what if i fall , do i know how to react?
    My thoughts are
    1.) If got road shoulder,Fling yourself towards it.
    2.) must Skid , try not to skid into another lane, or lagi worse oncoming lane.
    Fall onto your own lane at least car behind can see.
    And avoid being followed by Long vehicles or buses which have difficulty stopping in time.

    Then sway sway , today i got my first experience. Luckily , occurred in this lucky place. I was going from basement carpark in my hostel, puddle of oil/water , dem slippery. My bike was literally stationery/at 5km/hr. i took note slippery and travel super slowly. but bo pian the floor too slippery, so i skidded , literally a stationery bike fall from 90degs to 0 degs.

    Had minor cut on hand. And a post cruciate ligament tear. Siau liao, so slow and no danger , oso can get lig tear. I put knee brace and took some anti-inflammatory, must rest for 2-3 weeks can liao.

    But imagine if you are on the expressway with a big truck behind you!!! I sweat at that thought. I rem i chopped off a young 19-year-old man's leg after he skidded to oncoming lane and was ran over by cement mixer. Bike chui nvm , leg oso chui.

    So pls , DON"T ride when rain or after rain!!! And i think it is wise to stay left and go slowly. I go slow at 70-80kmh at leftmost lane like old ah peh on scooters wan. I always wear protective jacket then sway sway just now din't wear bcos thot short journey to library onli, then kenna cut and skin abrasion

    Protection really impt! REALLY ! Gloves and riding jacket.
