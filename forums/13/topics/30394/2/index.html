    Servicing mainly consists of:
    1) Washing of carb =$8 - $15
    2) Washing of air filter =usually free if wash carb
    3) Change gear oil = 1 bottle can choose wat u like
    4) Change spark plug = $3 - $18
    5) Top up/Change coolant water = 1 bottle can choose wat u like
