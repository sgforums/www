    1. use a tuned exhaust pipe (unobstructed, tuned expansion chamber, straight-through stinger),
    2. "racing" carburettor (can be large or small depending on whether you're talking about maximum airflow or air velocity in the venturi), + a proper rejet,
    3. high-flow air filter (or none at all),
    4. sprockets optimized for top-speed (something super big in front and super small at the back, e.g. 16 front, 39 rear, even if it takes me forever to reach top-speed),
