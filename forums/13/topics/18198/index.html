    I don't think its realistic to believe that one can pay a low initial downpayment & still pay a low monthly installment.

    Imagine a bike @ S$8k. Usual minimum DP would be abt 20% (ie $1600) w/interest @ abt 4%. Take that u choose to take 4yrs to repay the loan sum @ $6400, interest sum would stand @ $256 x 4yrs = $1024. Total sum @ $7424. Mthly installment @ $154 x 48mths.

    Would $154 be considered too much or ok? Hopefully its manageable for u. Gotta be realistic. :-)

    As for items like insurance, rd tax etc. if the price of the bike is a OTR(On-the-Road) quote, all charges that's needed to get the bike on the road should hv been included in the selling price. Jus make sure what u've been quoted is indeed OTR. Cheers!
