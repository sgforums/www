Killing the transient voltage across the battery

  • X9_panel
    11 Dec 06, 19:59
    Recently, found that a few of model motorcycles were happening the transient voltage across the battery. The owner changed the IU more than 2 times without knowing the reason. The bike shop was using the analog or digital volt-meter to measure, found nothing wrong due to out of measurement range.

    Therefore, I measured with oscilloscope as displayed 78Vac spark noise.

    So I design and build the special device to bypass the transient voltage to serve the local market at low cost.

    Model: TransDrop-15
    Specification:
    Provide high overvoltage protection by clamping action with bi-directional at 15Vdc +0.8 / -0.7v
    Prevent over voltage charging
    Clamping transient over voltage
    Peak Pulse Power = 1500W;Power = 5W
    Size: 25mm x 12.5mm x 12.5 mm (subject to part available)
    Lead cables (150mm x 2) are included.

    Advantage: to protect ECU, CDI, IU, MP3 and other electronics devices

    Simple installation method:
    Red wire connected to Battery positive.
    Black wire connected to Battery negative.

    This device can be used on DC12V system for all types of motorcycles or cars.

    Remark: This device is not doing the same thing as voltage stabilizer.
