    If you liked bikes and would like to meet different folks with difference experiences of bikes. You are more likely willing to meet those old men bikers at LC foodcourt Katong.

    They are usually really old moto siao and still rides nowadays.

    You see a mix mesh of big and small bikes there usually on Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday & saturday nite.

    These old men has gold wings, St11, triumph rockets, harleys to cubkia, sonic & fjr to interceptor vfr, r1, r6 scramblers s4 & even sp among themselves, their children, their spouse & mistresses or girlfriends.

    Do drop by and say hi and ask questions...they always welcome young folks that ask them politely and join them for drinks or makan at the table. In return, maybe you can help them with their high tech handphones (which they dont always know how to use.)

    And there is young folks there, below the ages of 30 so you might just find yourself in a comfortable group for touring or track with the older folks giving experiences and advise that you might not know you need till you really needed it.
