    The pipes carry exhaust gases out. Shorter pipe meant that it takes shorter time for the gases to get out....making it less 'choke'. Less 'choke' = piston not restricted = more power. Perhaps different pipes are much more suitable for different bikes as they provide the most ideal back pressure ratio.
