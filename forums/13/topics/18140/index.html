    chain noisy means 2 things
    1. chain loose.
    chk for free play. meaning u pull the chain off the end of the sproket and see how much slack there is.
    donch knoe the amt for sp. mebbe other sp riders would advise u ?

    2. chk ur sprokets.
    is the teeth too sharp ?
    of it is, means its going to go soon.

    advice: change chain and sproket in a set.
    eg. u change chain and use back old sproket.
    like that would cause the chain to wear off faster also.

    also, do clean ur chain regularly. i posted something sometime back. if ure keen on how to do so, pls do a search.

    it proven that regular chain maintainence does help increase the lifespan of the chain significantly.

    as for the other problem....
    a few causes.

    wheel bearing gone?
    this wan i dunno how to chk. others pls advise

    steering cone?
    a set of bearings that control ur steering. when one of it wears off, one side would be harder to turn than the other. can feel it
