    did think about this bike before, if not for the prohibitive price tag, i just might have gotten it.. paul chow of trail culture owns one, which is nicely tricked-up with accessories, both original and after-market. can take a look at it at his shop at macpherson. it looks very good with a taller windscreen. 17 litre fuel tank... i think it really depends on your needs, plus the length of time you expect to own the bike... cos at 9k (base price), and 15bhp (SP produces ard 37, TZM ard 39 etc etc), it might be underpowered if you're into some fun. but it should be a great commuter. reviews from local riders say it holds 100km/h with a pillion, vibration free. do think about it if:

    you've some cash to spare, you intend to ride quite a while, you don't really wish to speed or out-accelerate cars, if you're about 1.75m or so. (saddle height is about 800mm or slightly less if i don't remember wrongly).
