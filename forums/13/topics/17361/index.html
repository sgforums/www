    Ah ... another new baby ... heee .. hee ...
    Basically, to learn riding and get the license, you need to enrol yourself with one of the Driving Centre.

    1. BBDC (Bukit Batok Driving Centre)
    2. SSDC (
    3.

    BBDC has its own website on the Internet. You can browse at this address.
    www.bbdc.com.sg

    For enrolment, you need your IC, and 2 photos, plus around $100 for the registration and membership.

    There will be 5 Theory lessons followed by an evaluation test, and then the final test called the RTT (Riding Theory Test).

    While you are taking your 5 Theory lessons, you can also go for your 4 practical lessons concurrently. Then comes your circuit evaluation test.

    After your practical lessons and passing your theory test (RTT), you can go and book for your PDL (Provisional Driving License). You need this PDL because you will be practicing on the road.

    That's basically for starters. I am sure you will get to know more once you enrol and get the lessons going.

    Before you plunge straight into enjoying the ride, I hope you are already mentally prepared that a rider is at more risk than any other motorist. That said, enjoy the experience, and if you have further questions, there are always people here tohelp you. Cheers !

    Dear Apple_Pie, As per Noah's reply, at BBDC, It's 8 pratical divided into 13 subjects.U'll have to complete 5 theory lessons, take an evaluation test.

    Upon passing the Evaluation test, then can u book for your Riding Theory Test(RTT). Then go for ur practical lessons while waiting for ur RTT test date. U can only go up to subject 10 or practical 5, that's all.

    Only after passing ur RTT can u apply for ur Provisional Driving License(PDL). This license allows u to be on the road to complete ur subject 11-13 or practical 6-8.

    After clearing ur subject 13, u can go book ur Traffic Police (TP) test date. U will need to pass this last test(TP) to obtain ur 2B license....Total cost for the whole course is abt $500-$700/-...depending on how good the instructors think ur riding skills are.

    Hope this informal piece of information help! WELCOME TO THE WORLD OF EXTREME FREEDOM!
