    only the "convoy" is postponed till october. the event itself was still held over the weekend.

    this is my day 2 review.

    for those of you who didnt attend. you didnt miss anything. only bikes on display were a few yamahas, triumphs and the regular harleys. nothing you cant view at the agents mah pte n harley.

    there was only a handfull of vendors for motorcycle stuff. also nothing special. seat wraps, riding gear. henna tattos, stickers.... yawnz.....

    i couldnt find a single hotdog stand wen i was hungry. $6 for a slightly cold beer in a plastic cup is really too expensive. thank god hooters was open.

    the gals who joined the biker babe contest were so dolled up that they all lookd liked clones. cant really tell them apart. organisers. this is not miss universe. one of the things biking sybolises is freedom. let the gals wear what they want & doll themselves up. instead u made them look like a bunch of bananas. cnt tell one from the other. then again. they seem like most of them come from the same agent...

    the MC for the events is also hopeless. at times he talks so fast that you cant even understand what he is trying to say.

    now for the best bike competition. word of advice for all you guys who ever thought of winning one. no matter how much $ you pour into your bike and how great it looks. make sure your bike is the same brand as the main sponsor or else dont waste your time...

    overall there was no atmosphere. nothing surprising or fun. it was a flop. good thing i didnt have to pay any entrance fees.

    only thing i agree upon is kudos to the road marshalls for leading me smoothly to a parking lot and also keeping a lookout for the safety of pedestrians as some show offs were zipping around on their bikes like they had an urgent meeting with the toilet bowl.

    to the organisers of singapore big bike fest... next year get someone to head your team that really has been to big bike festivals overseas and knows the meaning of the word biker. else just stick to family days n d&ds...
