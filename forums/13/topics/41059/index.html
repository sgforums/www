    brake oil will usually not run dry. if its does tt mite means there is a leak somewhr in the pump system or the hoses. Brake fluid is normally clear in color and wil turn darker over time. once the brake fluid is too dark to look thru, its time to change the oil.

    sometimes if u look into the master pump window and c the fluid level drop, its also a sign tt the brake pads are worn out. as the pump cylinder move out of the brake to compensate for the worn pads the level of oil will drop. but once u get the pads replaced and the cylinders are puched back intot he pump. the oil leverl will rise once again.
