    Now, riding a bike is dangerous like most say. I'm not too bothered by those comments at all because I love riding bikes. Unlike the previous poster who has had a 'bike phase' and grown out of it, there are people who love riding bikes even if they can afford 5 cars.

    But unlike those people who can afford so many cars and yet they still purchase a bike to ride, you on the other hand have serious budget constraints.

    Get a Class 3 first would be a better choice for uyr case, from what i see.

    1stly, you are going to have a car in the family, imagine on days wehn the car is available for ur use , but you are without a license , wouldn't u be more "dui" ?
    Hehe..

    2ndly, getting a license early is good when u can afford to buy a car for urself in the future. Insurance companies will take into account of how long you have been holding the license , when deciding the premium to load you. Furthermore, if you dun drive or ride, for 3 years, that also means you dun kenna any demerit points. You will then be eligible for the certificate of merit from the TP. That is another 5% savings on top of ur insurace NCDs. This is especially helpful, for people who buy cars like integras and mx5. The insurance for a 25 yr old will cost from $4k onwards per year without NCD, and with a class 3 license that is 1-2 yrs old.

    In a sense, it pays to get a Class 3 license, even if you dun forsee urself driving one or much in the near future.
