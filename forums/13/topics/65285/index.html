    Go slow man.. if you book multiple sessions at once.. you might be wasting your time and money if you pass the subject in one try.

    Anyway, the route to a licence is always not about speed, but gaining the skill and experience to better control the bike.

    You can choose to go through the subjects fast and keep failing the TP Test later (which will take a longer time ultimately) or you can choose to go through the subjects slow and steadily and pass the TP Test with lots more confidence..

    For myself, I had problems with some of the skill courses (plank & slope). Also, prepare to take each road subject twice or more.. unless you have public road experience.. for me, I was a greenhorn on the public road and trust me.. the circuit is a much more predictable and safer place.. hehe. Although it may look cluttered with vehicles sometimes.
