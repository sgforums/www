    Sometimes it's not just the strength. Of course the more power you have, the easier for you to handle the bike. IMHO, your technique in handling the bike is more important. If you want to take class 2, you have to lower a class 2 bike (CB750 at BBDC) until it lays flat on the ground, and lift it up again. And I've seen some petite ladies riding class 2 bikes. Surely they've passed the test, haven't they?
    About your height, I have a relative whose body is not normal, due to a childhood accident. His height is just slightly over 4". But he can ride a cub with almost no difficulty at all. Of course cannot expect him to ride a Hayabusa lah, because of his abnormality. So again, it's your riding technique that really counts
