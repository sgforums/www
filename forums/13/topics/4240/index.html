    if u like a heavy bike, get the intruder. it weighs a lot for a 125cc bike because of the engine components. a heavy bike is good because it provides more stability. the finish tends to be better cos it is from japan.

    the phantom 200 is the latest model from honda. they take the cbx 200 strada (road bike)engine and put it in a chopper chassis.

    i would suggest you go for the 200cc bike since it has more torque and power than the intruder.

    however note that the phantom's paintwork and chrome tends to be of average quality. if cosmectics are of importance to you, try not to ride it in the rain.
