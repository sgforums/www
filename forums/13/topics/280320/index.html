  • mancha
    24 Sep 07, 21:26
    You are so used to the light weight of the bicycle, that you forgot that the motorcycle is heavier. When the bike leans over slightly to the right of 90 degrees, it will pull you over.
    For the time being always remember to lean your bike slightly to the left, to where you are standing. Do this even when you are pushing the bike. Later you would be able to hold it upright 90 degrees naturaly. But still, lean slightly left is the best. Don't be shy of leaning the bike against your body.

    Later when you decide to take up Class 2A or Class 2, you will again face the same weight problem. Its just a matter of getting accustom to the weight.
