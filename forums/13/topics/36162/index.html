    don't know if this would help, but propeller planes have about the same concept as your engine.

    basically the choking could mean that you have a "detonation" in the cylinders and that usually caused by foul spark plugs, there unable to burn the mixture of fuel and air properly.

    you will also notice black smoke because when theres too much air and little fuel mixing, you'll get black smoke and vice-versa to get blue smoke. when the engine travels too fast and the spark plugs can't detonate fast enough, the sequence will than be wrong and end up with a stall.

    have your engine go for an inspection or have the spark plugs changed. by the way what i mentioned above is based on airplane's engine concept.
