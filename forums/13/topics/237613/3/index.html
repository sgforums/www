    Haha, 2B tp test...its practically a PAIN. Those Tp old men, well lets they don't see very well.

    There are tips and tricks in this test which is much stricter than Class 3 test.
    As told to me by BBDC instructor as follows:

    1: Dress appropiately, this means no dyed hair, proper long sleeves and pants. Good impression always makes the 1st point.

    2: When riding, always look straight but when you need to look down, do it with your eyes, they can't see your eyes anyway ( thats how they penalized me 5 times for blind spot and 2 times for not checking side roads. )

    3: For narrow plank, don't think too much bout time, they only require 5 sec on plank.

    4: For Pylon Slalom, do it as fast as possible but remember the proper way, NEVER knock any cones ( I did it on my 1st test )

    5: S course, do it as normal, they rarely time you.

    6: Blindspots (Argh how I hate those but in time to come they are actually very important ) needed to be check at every turn and neccessary spots, always TURN your head.

    7: Sideroads same as blindspots

    8: Lastly, your confidence, don't be nervous and go through it, all will be fine

    9: God bless and good luck.

    Thanks to my instructor, I got only 8 points for my 2nd test. As for my first...38 points, 30 points came from blindspots and I never know how they see wobbling...
