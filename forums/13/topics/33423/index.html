    not a yandao here, but take a look at your log card, it says that "PARF Benefit: $0", which means you won't get a cent from the remaining value of the vehicle, based on OMV. what you will get however, is the pro-rated remaining life of your COE. the COE's value will be written on the log card. suppose i bought a bike with a 10 year COE of $1000, i'll get $800 when i scrap after 2 years exactly (that's assuming straight-line depreciation). should be la... why not give LTA a call and see what they have to say?
