    Asian SuperCross, in brief, consists of a series of competitions for motorcyclists in participating countries (Macau, Japan, Indonesia, Malaysia and Singapore). The riders accumulate their points through a series of competition to claim the overall SuperCross Championship title. The series of competitions require the riders to flaunt their best jumps and techniques while crossing the obstacles on the sand-filled, man-made terrain.
