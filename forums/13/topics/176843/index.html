    The Phantom is a hardy bike.
    Just need to change engine oil, every 2000km
    Change brake pad, when necessary.
    Oil the chain.
    It is a low maintenance bike.
    You must remember to loosen the sparkplug every year or it will get stuck.
    Mine fires every time without fail for 3+ years on the original spark plug.
    Brake oil still there. Although its high time to change it too.

    Problems: Engine don't sound good when not properly warmed up.
    No fuel gauge. Therefore fill tank every approx 250km.
    Full tank milage 300km.
    Loose cables rattles under the tank.
