  • Obsessionz
    12 Jan 06, 12:07
    I'm a vespa px excel owner and hav a great passion for vintage vespas too.

    to my opinion, u need only abt $5 for full tank and regular servicing to keep ur vespa in a good shape. of cos, the important thing is to check ur brakes and tires too. its a very convenient machine, and unique too. u can ride in style with own colours or designs. vintage and chic.

    on the expressway, always be wary of the lorries especially cos sometimes they can't see u. and if it rains, do ride safely. it might get slippery as the tires are smaller than other bikes. abt speed, I've rode up to 100km/h. keep a safe distance and be in control of the vespa.

    if u wish to purchase a vintage bike, its advisable to bring someone who's experienced or owns a vespa. sometimes cos its vintage, there's a need to change the parts or battery. the fully restored ones are not bad I guess.

    hope this helps u in deciding which bike to get. fyi, this is just my opinion and u should think carefully and be responsible for ur own actions. gd luck for ur TP in future.
