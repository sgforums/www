    firstly congras on getting a phantom. now comes the tedious part. maintaining. to really maintain ur phantom, wash once a week, wax once a month. chrome parts use water and then wipe over hard with dry cloth. for sports rim, spray with jif and use a brush to clean. as for performance, wash carb once in three months, change 4t at that time also. also lubricate chain once a week and after rain. check regularly for the chain tension. also ur brake pads and brake fluid level.
