    you can view the market prices of second hand bikes @

    Bukit Merah Queensway,
    Ubi Industrial Park (Near TP HQ),
    Jalan Besar.
    www.singaporebikes.com/forums (have a experienced rider or someone who knows about bike to view it with you)
    the problem with forum is that some sellers like to dilly dally, for fast sale its best to approach shop.

    depending on how much your d/p is, interest is about 0.07 p/a. transfer fee $100, agreement fee $200.

    so payment is like

    (Machine Price + Insurance + transfer fee + agreement fee ) - downpayment * 1.07 for 1 year / 12 months = XXX.XX the amount you pay

    second hand bike like a wave will probably costs you around $3k + including insurance depending on year made, your age and etc.

    wave is very low maintenance bike, probably around 45 - 55 km per litre?

    and the full tank size is probably about 4 or 4.5 litres.

    other bikes like wave are, Yamaha X1, Yamaha Sparks 110, Yamaha Sparks 135 (T135), Yamaha 125Z (2 Strokes), Yamaha X1-R(new bike OTR, 2 double disc brakes back and front), Suzuki Raider, Yamaha Nouvo etc.
