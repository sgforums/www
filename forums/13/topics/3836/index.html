    I heard from a couple of guys that Henry and his friend were seriously injured in the track session on Saturday. Injuries are kinda common on track. But it is minimised greatly compared if you fall at the same rate on the public roads where you smash your bones onto the railings, entangle your intestines on the street lamps as you get gutted when you slashes your abdomen onto something sharp etc... (a bit too much)

    But I still wanna tell you. Don't feel disheartened. Many pple also crashed there and even worse before. When you recover, you can stil pickyourself up and return to the battle field once more.

    Yesterday I went to the track also with 3 other friends. Two of them crashed out and I must say pretty serious. But it was a great day except for the crashes.

    I had a great time playing with the SBR guys. Had a taste of how it is like to be surrounded by a majority of wind whizzing angmohs. Getting chiet by 4 bikes in one lap (2 DUCATI996, 1 GSX-R750SRAD & 1 GSX-R1000) mostly angmohs. It was a friendly match or I should rather say FREE TRACK LESSONS. (I think someone mistook it that I was disturbed by SBR. I want to clarify -> I was not disturbed by the SBR. We're all willing parties on the track. One of our basic duties is to try and keep ahead of each other. So no such things as disturb or not. They were just doing what they were supposed to do.) I could hardly catch up with Joe's members but learnt a lot about my mistakes. Their almost flawless lining & aggressive strategies+plannings = ruthless formidable 100% sucess in overtaking at any part of the track.

    I'd sincerely encourage all riders out there to enjoy a day of wild riding not on the road but on the track. Simply purchase a leather jacket (can share) at abt RM120 ($60) and use SAF leather combat boots, full face helmet and a pair of leather gloves (can share) at $60 + hard jeans. RM35/hr on weekdays and RM50/hr on Holidays and weekends. All tickets transferrable as long as its still not expired. And the ticket also can be shared among a few guys if you all don't mind sharing the ticket for an hour. If you're not confident enough you can opt for a weekday where few people will go. So very likely can book the whole track with only you and your mates. For those interested, can gimme a call or find Dom.

    P.S. eh... don't see Dom blur blur very kind Mr Nice guy on the road leh... He's actually very scary if he wanna trash anybody on the track... rite DOM? hee...
