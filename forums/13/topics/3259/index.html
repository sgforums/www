    anyway i have a really bad experience wif the tp once... and wonder if anyone here had the same?

    i was riding wif 2 other bikes from orchard meridien down to mr bean oppo paradiz ctr...
    and everyone knows that on a sat nite the tp always patrol the old corners that started from plaza singapura to cathay.. so my frens and i took extra precaution and rode at a nice safe pace of 40kmh on the left most lane keeping dead ctr of the lane itself.
    in the corners that followed we were about 2 cars length in front of each other going at about 35kmh? knowing that this is a TP hotspot so extra safe.. after which we turned towards mr bean all the time i was the third bike and i was watching the rear from my mirrors... no bikes behind us..

    when we passed by mr bean we stopped at the following red traffic light. about a min passed and 3 task force tp riders rode up to that same traffic light, stopped about 2 lanes away and just looked at us. after about 5secs of looking they rode directly in front of us and shouted for us to get off. asked for the usual license and road tax etc.. and told us they were going to charge us wif DANGEROUS RIDING which warrents 24demerit pts... talk about being shocked...

    the case dragged for a year.. and we got a lawyer to write letters here and there... and in the end each of us paid s$800 each for the lawyer fee, 4months suspension all classes, and s$2000 fine.

    moral of the story.. the tp that day abused their authority and charged 3 innocent riders. it has been 3years now.. but the memory still lingers on..

    anyone have something to share??
