    For a SAF driver ... you will need to click 7000 KM during your NS life to get a civilian license ... During this 7000KM you cannot get into any accidents .., or any offences on road while driving a SAF vehicles ...

    When you are posted to stts ... it is a school that provides training for those who have just been posted to DRIVER course ... You as a driver will attend and practice all different stages as in a pte school ... but you will learn more then what a driving centre is teaching ...

    Upon finishing your course ... you will be posted to a camp ... (mostly 1st transport) to serve the rest of your NS life ...

    When you are going to ORD ... you will be posted back to stts to attend a SAF driver class A course ... (with this class A course then you can get your civilian license) ... after this class A course you will be posted back to your original camp ...

    When the time you get your pink IC ... those clerks at your camp(MANPOWER Branch) will issue you a letter for your SAF license to convert into a civilian license at SSDC (if i am not wrong ... not very sure bout which driving centre also)

    Hope this helps
