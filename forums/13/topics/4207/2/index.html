    yah yah, i heard someone calling my name? hehe aprilia si bo? hehehe, aiyah, aprilia u wanna mod ah? But u must prepare for somethings lor...

    1) Eat petrol like FUCK!! Full tank tahan 1 day, 2 days at most...
    2) Must tahan to siam TP (if u going for pipe)
    3) Got $$.

    So mod is standard one lah. Basics are
    1) Fuel Jet
    2) Reed Valve
    3) Sprockets
    4) Pipe (Arrow, RS, chamber)
    5) Settings
    6) drill some holes on your air filter to maximise a little air intake for better pickup.

    If u still have $$,
    7) Change to Mikuni carbuerator
    Change to single ring piston
    9) Port your block
    10) Splitfire cables and plugs

    If u still have $$$$$ dono where to spent,

    10) change to carbon fibre fairings/rims.
    11) customise your frame by cutting off thos "extra" metal parts, drill holes on your swing arm
    12) Change to hydrolic suspensions.
    13) Take out meter/lights.
    14) Put racing damper.
    15) Use steel brake hose for better braking, (ur bike so fast must use better brakings)
    16) One seater
    17) Maybe get a Pilot Race slick tyres.
    1 I think the radiator oso can modify. (4get how liao)

    Recommended to pump jet fuel if u have $$, in SG i think Shell XO can liao. 2T can use Castrol A747 premix and Motul 800 premix. Engine oil for aprilia recommended Rock Oil GRO geor oil. SO far only these stuffs i know...

    But last advice is... don mod lah, no point. Not racer, mod for what? hahaha original original can tong pang gals can liao..

    juz to add on to wat TZR have said mod bike are quite standard 4 the things to change but some things muz take note like
    1)be sure that the plug used is suitable in the sense plug usually need to upgrade to colder plug after mod
    2)if u increase the input u muz increase the out put or else mod is useless (exhaust).
    3)bike is more easy to damage after mod
    4)for input u muz make sure the oxygen and fuel muz be optimise (incomplete combustion if too much fuel which can coz faster carbon build up and waste fuel if u can increase the intake manifold it will give greater output)this is something u muz make sure the mechanic do and not simply tell u to change everything but not make sure the amt of fuel to amt of oxygen
    5)timing chain,cam shaft,valve,etc muz b in perfect condition and of this not in good condition will result in incorrect timing oof valve opening closing,example timing chain become longer elongation due to tension ,cam on cam shaft wear between cam and follower due to poor lubrication,valve may crack due to heat and the spring closing the valve may not function properly.
    and by the way changing triple clutch plate oso help alittle in mod ur bike
