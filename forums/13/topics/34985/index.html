  • skeujin
    11 Feb 03, 17:19
    b4 entering a corner, drop to gear below wat u actually need.
    After u enter the corner, throttle the bike, nvr play the entire corner w a closed throttle!
    Control the radius of the turn by lightly playing w yr front brake w the throttle open. This is also how u do a power slide. Practise slowly first. when u get used to this and take a big corner, u mite hav to up a gear somewhr after the apex.
    If u r flying solo, when u take a deep corner, do the following. corner left, out body weight on right leg. corner right put body weight on left leg. This will help u aviod the bike slipping away from under u.
    Always look far into the corner and stop staring at the road infront of your tyre. Guage the apex and play accordingly.
    When taking a right corner, start taking the bend from the left most and sweep inwards. when it's time for u to throttle u should be at the innermost point of the apex and start throttling out and start straightening out till u r upright back on the leftmost area again. Always check your rearview mirrors for other vehicles comming up from behind.
