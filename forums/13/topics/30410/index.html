    Proarms r supposedly more rigid than normal swingarms as it only has a single armed attachment point (tyre mount). Normal swingarms with double arms are said to be able to 'twist' at the attachment points (by mere fractions). Normal arms are also harder to align after a tyre change or chain adjustment (two point adjustment).
