    Dear fellow car lovers,

    Thanks to all your supports, Dreamcars Asia 2004 was held successfully last year. This year, the show is back with more cars, babes, highlights and programmes!

    The Dreamcars Asia Motorshow 2005 will be held from 30th June to 3rd July at the Singapore Expo Halls 1 & 2. The opening hours are from 10am to 10pm and 10am to 8pm on the last day.

    Some of the highlights / Programmes:

    - Dreamcars Sound Challenge (First time ever to be held indoors)

    - Wheels Asia Race Queen Seach (saturday & sunday)

    - Dress Up Cars Showdown (Co-organized by D.U.K.E.)

    - Japanese Exhibitors with their souped up race cars

    - Japanese Race Queens

    - More souped up cars

    - Tamiya Asia Cup (Singapore) Qualifying Championship Finale 2005

    - Dreamcars SuperSpecial Rally Round 3 & 4 (saturday & sunday at carpark J behind Expo)

    - Stand a chance to win attractive prizes and a brand new Fiat Punto in the lucky draw (draw on Sunday)

    There will be a 10% discount for bulk purchase of minimum 20 tickets and maximum 100 tickets. Each forum will be entitled to 1 multi entry Moderator Pass. Name of the Moderator and Forum will be required to be submitted together with a passport size photograph by 20th June 2005 to our office (Email also accepted).

    For more details, please visit our website at www.dreamcarsasia.com or call us at 64474588.

    See you there!
