THE SINGAPORE BIKE SHOW 2016

  • FireIce
    17 Aug 16, 23:19

     

    The inaugural show will be take place in Singapore, 20-21 August 2016, at Singapore EXPO Hall 4. It will comprehensively showcase the latest models and designs of motorcycles, scooters, bicycles and e-bikes. It will coincide with the launch of Singapore Ride Safe 2016 to actively promote safe riding and cycling. The show serves as a key platform for motor associations, manufacturers, distributors, training schools and delivery companies in Asia to converge, network and develop business opportunities.
