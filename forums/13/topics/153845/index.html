    For Fig.8 you need to mentally go with the flow.
    Tilting your head, leaning or throwing yourself in the direction of the bend. You need courage and confidence in yourself for this.

    Entering the Fig.8 perfectly is important. Shoot into the Fig.8 swiftly, not too fast, not too slow and immediately kick to 2nd gear. You will get the momentum, then go with the flow.
    Courage, my dear fellow, courage.

    Crank Course is more technique.
    Move off, and change to 2nd only when inside the starting straight path. Turn before reaching the bends. This is important. You are actually making a rounded turn at the first bend and also at the second bend. The path you travel is something like this ~ . Time is no problem if you go through the crank course smoothly.

    In all the courses, your eye line is most important.
