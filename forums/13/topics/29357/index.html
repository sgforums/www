    Ways to improve physical appearance of Phantom:

    -Chrome linings around front mud guard, tank n rear mud guard
    -Increase the height n/or design of front signal lights
    -Change to a bigger headlight/ Headlight cap
    -Add 2 more small headlights
    -Change handlebar
    -Install windshield
    -Change throttle grips, balancers
    -Add leather grips n/or leather strips
    -Change color of speedometer panel bulbs
    -Install a tank bra
    -Install a crashbar
    -Install floorboard footrest for both rider n pillion
    -Install sideboxes/ leather saddlebags
    -Install mainbox
    -Change the rear signal lights
    -Change the front n rear No. plates
    -Attach leather mud guard under rear No. plate

    Ways to improve performance of Phantom:

    -Add fuel/ engine oil additives
    -Denso Iridium Power Plugs (IW24)
    -Change chain to Oring or Xring
    -Change rear sprocket size ( big or small up to ur preference )
    -Use Shell Vpower
    -Use fully synthetic 2T n 4T
    -Use better quality brake pads
    -Change to a maintenance-free battery
    -Change to better quality tires

    etc.....etc......etc

    just my 2 cents worth

    22 Nov 02, 01:19
    Hi there,

    It's true that 4 stroke engines don't use 2T oil in its combustion process. The oil consumption that might occur in a 4 stroke engine is usually the result of a leaky piston ring. This means the engine oil is leaking pass the piston ring & into the combustion chamber. The 4T oil ends up being burnt up. This results in a drop of engine oil level. Always good to check on the oil level at least once in a week. If the amount of engine oil being burnt up is quite large, its best you check the piston/cylinder. The leaky piston ring might bring on bigger/more expensive damages in time to come!

    Hope this helps. Cheers!
