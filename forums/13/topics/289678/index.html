    word of warning, i work dispatch and have ridden vespas, they are fun and have a nice seating position but they are twitchy in conering because of small tyres and short wheelbase in my opinion.

    and be very careful in the wet, they can skid easily, i know some people who have skidded on vespas. especially on the slippery stripes when the road markings are wet.

    and my friend who owns one says when there is a pillon the you have to adjsut your riding style drastically.

    but then again my experience on vespas so far is pretty bad conditioned ones and older models.

    all in all its a fun commuter bike, but just be careful in corners.
