    there are basically 2 types of wheelies..1)throw clutch kind which is very bad for your clutch(obviously)and 2)using the bounce of your front suspension..let's say you're in 2nd gear..ride ard 30-40kmph..and quickly close throttle w/o clutchin in..the deceleration of your bike will shift the weight to the front and compress your front forks..and at the point of maximum compression..open throttle..the combination of the return extension of the front forks and the forward acceleration will result in a sweet wheelie..be careful when u open throttle,diff bikes will react diffly..motogp bikes wheelie everytime they change gears.. in mal,my fren saw hondacup wheelie from traffic lite...if u're bored wif wheelie ,then u can try stoppie..the opposite of wheelie..moving forward wif rear wheel in the air..like tom cruise in mission impossible..some pple call it endo..btw, the writer of this article will not be responsible for any consequences of your actions..tha above info is for educational purposes only..
