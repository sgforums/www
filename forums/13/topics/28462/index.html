    The pair were travelling along Bedok South Road towards Chai Chee when they crashed into the taxi which was making a right turn into Bedok South Avenue 1.

    A van was turning right into the expressway when it hit an oncoming car.

    Four passengers of the car were sent to the hospital. One of them was bleeding from the forehead while another complained of chest pains.

    Police are investigating both cases and are appealing for witnesses to call the Traffic Police at 1800-547-1818.
