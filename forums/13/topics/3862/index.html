    B]
    Don't be discouraged . Here is tips from a person who heng heng passed.I took very few lessons and din't go for a single revision except one while waiting for the test.

    1.) Arrg ga liao with the instructors there. If they arrange to give you an instructor bike, there is a huge difference, my practice bike all problematic, but i got a instructor bike for my test. It purred.

    2.) Try to be the last few 75-80 onwards. Higher chance of passing. Apparently for the 80th candidate , TP already start to pack up oreadi. So I was the 80th candidate!
    Everyone there told me , i close one eye oso can pass wan.

    3.) Be relaxed, try very hard to psycho yourself to fail/fail oso nv mind. Usually you pass instead.

    4.) Plank , grab tight , look far ahead and never look down.
    5.) Figure 8 , swing with the bike ,it tilt you tilt. And cheat abit by starting across the line , start off fast and kick up to second gear very fast, fast release , then speed up whenever possible, but slow just before coming out.
    6.) E-brake, keep practicing the motions. straigten arms, release throttle earlier , look far, pull in front brake hard, rear brake abit, then 5 seconds later, you clutch in.

    7.) Exaggerate your motion abit, check back , check blindspot .

    8.) on road, look far ahead and plan your positioning. Go slow near hazards or near too many cars.( trust me TP look alot into this) KEEP SAFE FOLLOWING DISTANCE. Go slower better than faster. I calculated that you lose less points going slow then not having enuf following distance and safety.

    9.) Start off fast , but check blindspot slowly and exaggeratedly. Change down to lower gears far ahead before you reach the junction for the right turn,

    10.) U-turn , don't use rear brake. look far and aim where you go , maintain throttle AT A CONSTANT VELOCITY. START OFF doing the U-turn further away from the kerb. about 1.5 m-2m. Most likely you are always too near.

    11.) Those above are mainly my mistakes. so the most impt lesson i have to tell you is:

    Look at your practice tests. See where your mistakes are , memorise them and make a conscious effort not to repeat these mistake. SERIOUS , for class III or Class 2b.

    like that you will be able to pass first time and save money.

    But first time pass doesn't mean you are a good rider. usu , these are the ones who get into accidents.

    Good Luck , Joel
