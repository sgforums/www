    Not really the same as buying a new insurance as you're just adding your name to the main rider's policy.

    Yes, it is shared between the two persons.

    Nope, will not be cheaper as the insurance company will use the premium of the less experienced rider. So if you're adding yourself (less experienced) to a main rider, you'll have to top up the additional premium.

    Hope it helps.
