    A transport bike might be the one u are looking for rite .... maybe a NSR125 will be just nice for you ... not to forget a TZR ... TZRR ... RXZ ... NSR150 ... TZM150 ... KRR150 ... and alot more
    The last three that i have mention is more to sports bike ... but i myself think that all are good bikes ...

    My advise buy the bike that u really like and dun care what others said bout that bike ... Get a bike u truely love

    For question 2 ... u will need a class 2B which allow to ride up to 200cc bike ... if you are staying in the west ... bukit batok driving centre will be the best choice for you ... u can look up at their website at ... www.bbdc.com.sg ... all the informations is stated clearly in the website ...

    As for the last question ... it all depends on yourself ...

    Average cost = how much u willing to spend on the bike ...

    COE = there is no fix COE ...every month there is a different price (Apply to new bike ONLY) ... for Second hand bike most bike shop will include COE in their selling price

    Fuel/Petrol = This depends on how much u ride on road ... meaning the more u ride the bike the more visits you pay to the petrol station

    Insurance =This depends on what bike you are getting ... Each bike have a different rate ... and there is also a different rate for those under 21 and above 21

    Maintainace = The more you ride the more you need to service your bike ... There are ppls who service their bike every 5000 KM ... there are ppls who service their bike at 10000 KM ... it all up to the RIDER on how they are going to maintain their bike

    Hope this helps
