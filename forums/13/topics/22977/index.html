  • ammo
    27 Jun 02, 08:32
    The road at Old Upper Thomson Road is what we call Pierce track.
    It used to be a section of an official racetrack in 1980s Singapore mainly for automobile racing. This might be an indication to those of you who have not gone there before as to how technical the track gets.

    First-timers who are interested in having a look at the road are encouraged to go in a group and not alone. DO NOT go at more than 50 km/h for the first time round for the entire road no matter how good your skills are, there are a lot of blind corners and unpredicted radius turns.
    (There have been countless number of fatalities ever since the road was used for race purposes, and watch out esp. for the section at lamp-post num 77)

    All in all it's a very nice series of bends.
