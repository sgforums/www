    Hi there,

    I used Castrol TTS (fully synthetic) on my TZM before but since my riding style is rather sedate, it resulted in a lot of oily residue at my exhaust pipe. I gathered that it is only worth to use if you are always riding at higher RPMs, say 8000 and above. Also, there is a lot of white smoke at start ups.

    I am now using Yamalube 2S which is semi-synthetic and I am pretty happy with it. Doesn't seem to have any detrimental effects nor did I notice much performance difference between it and the TTS.

    As for gearbox oil, I am using Shell 4T as it is the recommended oil to use by Yamaha Japan for Yamaha bikes.

    Hi there,

    I think if you are talking about 2 stroke bikes, then engine oil is the same as gearbox oil in this context as I notice a lot of people using the terms this way.

    Best way is to follow what your manual defines and stick with it. In my manual, engine oil = 2T oil while transmission oil = gearbox oil.
