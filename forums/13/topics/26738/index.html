    24 Sep 02, 09:08
    1) bring ur NRIC
    2) get ready to pay $72.10 for registration

    $72.10 consists of: Basic Theory Booklet, Riding Booklet, course fees for 4 theory lessons and evaluation, lastly the admin fees

    3) u may want to pay $20-23 for ur 1st prac lesson, which can start even b4 u had finished ur theory lessons.

    4) let's say u can clear all the 4 theory lessons and pass the evaluation in 1 day, u can book the Riding Theory Test at the counter for $6.

    5) let's say u wanna go prac, get ready another $1 to buy the gloves there

    Therefore must prepare about $100 on the day of ur registration

    Cheers
