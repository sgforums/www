    Dear Jim Bean, the "sliding" effect of a car when taking a bend/corner is called "Drifting". It is VERY popular in the Japanese racing circles. They have really funny competitions to see who can drift the best. It is so extreme that the drivers would do anything to score. I read in one article that some hold the door open while performing the drift. Japan's "Drift King" is Keiichi Tsuchiya. By the way, drifting can only be performed on Rear-Wheel drive performance cars.
