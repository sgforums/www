    anyone know pgm4 .... got any indication for 2T low liao ?

    Yes, there is, on your LCD digital display, when your 2-t is low, it will have "OIL" on the LCD and a red led light "ON" just beside your "Orange" sidestand light.

    When the "Red" light 2-t oil lights up but do not blink, it means you still have around 40 km before the 2-t runs out, provided that you do not activate the RC valves. If you are unsure when will the 2-T will finally run out, make a visual check at the 2-T Box located inside your right fairing's biggest air-intake.

    Hope this helps....
