    Yup, me too have this problem before, is your bike front lightbulbs the original one?If they are not, then, it might be drawing more current from the battery! If one day, you slot in your card and the LCD meter display keeps on flicking on and off, remove one of your headlight's socket and try slot in the card again, you will be able to start your bike.

    I think it's the bulbs which are causing all these problems, change it back to the original ones-Stanley bulbs, it will work fine then. Stanley Bulbs can be purchased from Eversucess along Desker Road.
