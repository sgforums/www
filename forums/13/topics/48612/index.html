    why not ask yourself, 'what kinda cruiser do i want' first?
    do u want
    performance? (ta150 2 stoke) cannot tour too much due to 2-stroke
    elegance? (Shadow 200 or is it 125?) personal opinion
    economical? (TA200) touring!!!
    dirt cheap? (Dalim but dun cry when smth's spoilt)

    for me, i chose a TA200.
    top speed: 145km/hr
    pick up speed: pretty good
    corners: cant go really low but got sense of achievement from scrapping your footpegs.
    petrol consumption: 300+km before hitting reserves. (after run in)
    looks classy, one of the best performing cruisers in class 2b.
    but remember to change the darn stock IRC tyres to metzler ones and brake pads to SBS street ones.

    just my 2 cents worth
