    I myself ride an Aprilia RS125 Replica and would just like to put forward something that my local garage told me upon purchasing the Aprila.

    When I asked them which bike had the highest top end they said that they both do. The Aprilia has the highest speed as a standard 33bhp (factory settings) bike however the Mito can be tuned much more than the RS giving it the overall speed advantage. However, the difference when fully tuned is so small that all in all it's down to personal preference and appearance. I prefer the RS because of the looks and maybe it's just me but I like the sound (it's not as tinny as the Mito)
