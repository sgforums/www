Singapore's first automatic 'bike spa' opens in Bukit Merah

  • FireIce
    25 Oct 17, 17:32

    For owners of motorcycles and scooters looking for somewhere to get their bikes cleaned, try visiting this “bike spa” at Caltex in Jalan Bukit Merah.

    This automated bike wash system made its debut in Singapore on Sun (22 Oct), and is the first “bike spa” in Asia to be located at a service station.

    The bike spa supposedly cleans in “mere minutes” and is “suitable for bikes of all shapes and sizes’.

    According to a photo posted by Bike Spa Singapore on their Facebook page, prices for the bike spa range from $6 to $10.

    Price list for Bike Spa Singapore (Photo: Bike Spa Singapore | Facebook)
    More

    There is no news on whether there will be more of such “spas” opening in other parts of Singapore yet, but Bike Spa Singapore already warns of “long queues” from 11am to 1pm and 5pm to 7pm.
