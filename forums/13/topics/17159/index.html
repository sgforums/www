    You can try going to SSDC'd traffic police counter or Ubi Ave 3 Traffic Police department to enquire about this matter. I think if you're not going to stay in S'pore for more than 12 mths, you don't need to convert your license. However, if you intend to ride for more than a year, you would need to take the basic theory test to convert your license. A good place to do that would be SSDC, they have a package for license conversion, go check it out at their information counter.
