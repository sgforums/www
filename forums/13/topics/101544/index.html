    Two injured in Kallang Bahru accident

    SINGAPORE : A motorcyclist and his pillion rider were injured after their motorcycle was involved in a collision with a taxi at the junction of Kallang Bahru and Boon Keng Road.
    The motorcylist sustained deep cuts on his left ankle, while his companion had a fracture and some cuts on his left leg.

    Both riders, in their 20s, were treated at the Tan Tock Seng Hospital.

    The taxi driver and his passenger escaped injuries. - CNA
