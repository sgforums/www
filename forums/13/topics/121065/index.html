    depends on where you take your lessons..

    all have 8 practicals - 5 in circuit and 3 on road.

    if you're from BBDC, you would notice that for first 5 pracs, its broken into 2 "subjects" , so you need 2 prac lessons at least to pass 1 practical level. so "actual" total = 13 practicals.

    other centres , normal 8 practicals. (but maybe more difficult to pass 1 prac with 1 lessons.)

    average lessson to complete ~ 20, more or less depends on your coordination skills.

    average TP, maybe 1 - 2 or better skilled people, 3 to 4 for not-so-skilled, and more than 4, hmmm ...try harder. maybe driving is easier for those people.

    as for how long to complete this course, depends on how frequent you go for lessons.

    all these info are found in your handbook when you first register. be more proactive!

    cheers.
