  • LoudnessFZ
    28 Jun 07, 21:20
    2 strokes and 4 strokes are the technical specs of the engine.

    if u understand what strokes are,2 strokes are simply a compressed 4 stroke engine.

    its made to make it more faster and..i donno what else.well certainly 4 strokes
    are more efficient and long lasting.

    that's that.

    2 strokes need 2t oil to lubricate the engine,without these,ur piston will jam
    and overheat and wham ur bike need overhaul or scrap.u need to refill them whenever they're used up,which depends on how often u ride.

    4 strokes no need 2t oil,just simply engine oil,change it every month(i think) or every 3 months or every 5000km of riding.

    these 2 strokes technology are used most on lower cc bikes particularly the
    125,150 and 250ccs.

    some 2 strokes bikes are---Rxz,tzr,tzm,krr,sp,125z,phantom150 and many more.

    4 strokes are used most on larger cc bikes.

    if u wanna know how to differentiate 2 or 4 stroke bike,look at the exhaust
    and hear the sound

    as u already know i guess,2 stroke bike make very high pitch sound
    and give out white smoke(2t oil)

    and 4 strokes are those like phantom200,deep low bassy sound.

    theres more but ull learn bout it step by step.
