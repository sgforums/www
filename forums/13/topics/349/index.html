    Would love to go but alas work demands. Just a few gentle reminders.

    1) Don't speed. The group should maintain about the same speed.

    2) Check engine oil, spark plug, chain (if applicable), battery prior to trip

    3) Get experienced riders to be point (first) vehicle and last (safety) vechicle.

    If anything happens in Malaysia, it could get rather troublesome.

    And by the way do beware once you've exit the Highway. One occasion I got stopped because I didn't spot the Traffic Police on the shoulder. He then "summon" me for speeding, reckless driving etc. Wanted SGD$200!!! Holiday ruined.
