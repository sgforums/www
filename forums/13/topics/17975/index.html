    <Aprilia RS250>
    90deg V-Twin 2 stroke Engine. Made in Italy. Top speed can hit 210kph(stock condition on a RS250 meter), 70 brake horsepower, Maintenance can kill unless you're rich. I spent abt $200-$300 monthly (inc. installments) to change damaged/worn parts. Full tank can travel 200km if you keep to speed limit.
    - averagely Nimble and agile (but not as agile and nimble as NSR250),
    - more stable than NSR250 in corners(less likely to lose your front or rear),
    -aggressive seating posture which aids alot in cornering,

    <Honda NSR250>
    90deg V-Twin 2 stroke Engine. Made in Japan. Top speed can hit 180kph(stock condition on a NSR250SE meter), 45 brake horsepower (power cut; full power is also 70bHP), Maintenance can also kill unless you're rich.

    - Very Nimble and agile; lightning response
    - less stable than RS250 in corners(more likely to lose your front or rear in corners if not ridden properly),
    -less aggressive seating posture which hinders some people in terms of cornering,

    But it is up to the individual. Some pple suitable for RS250 some suitable for NSR250. Cannot say which is a better bike. Must say you're suitable for which type of handling.
