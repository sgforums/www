    Just came back from bike lesson ..... These are the few 'I think is interesting' things I heard in the lesson ....

    1. While on an expressway, the instructor saw a rider who was riding and messaging SMS on his handphone.

    2. A passenger was waiting for a taxi outside the school (T-junction) when a taxi on the 2nd lane, is heading in his direction. However, on the opposite direction, another taxi caught sight of the waiting passenger and the oncoming taxi. Both taxi drivers wanted to get the passenger; so the taxi on the opposite side of the road did a U-turn, while the oncoming taxi increased speed. Both collided. A 3rd taxi came by and picked up the passenger.

    3. The instructor showed us some videotapes on defensive riding. In one of the scene, a motorcyclist was travelling without his head light .... a TP came along and stopped him. The TP was smiling and signed to the rider to switch on his headlight. Then he smiled again and moved off. Everyone began to laff ... "Real or not ? So friendly ah ?"
