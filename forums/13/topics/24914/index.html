Just a simple reminder

  • ShunLi
    15 Aug 02, 01:09
    I dunno whether you all take extra care on this(read below) while riding on the road. But i just wanna remind you all not to ride too near to the side of trailers carrying stuffs. Or even a lorry carrying goods.

    This afternoon, I was riding beside a long trailer carrying big wooden planks when suddenly one of the planks just fell out from the trailer right beside me Missed me by half a metre cos i stay more to the center of the lane while the trailer occupied another lane on my left. It would be disastrous if it was to fall onto me.

    Also, ride carefully in tunnels. On the road, we skidded, we rolled all over. In the tunnel, you fall, you crash hard onto the concrete walls. Witnessed an accident in the tunnel entrance to CTE/AYE from boatquay towards JURONG.(On the first day of the Hungry Ghost Festival) The fall was TERRIFYING Thank God the rider, who is my fren, escaped death.

    Take extra care on this, fellow riders. Afterall, we're riding a bike...not driving a car.
