    It's not an alarm.
    There's a chip in your key which is like a lock. You use the original HISS key to start your engine.

    But if you try to duplicate the key with the normal key maker, when you use it, the engine will stall and hence you can't ride your bike. It's to prevent people from stealing your bike by just duplicate it.

    You can only duplicate the HISS key with the Honda agent or ask your motordiam to get it for you. Can't get it anywhere else.
