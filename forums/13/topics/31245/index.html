    Please share yours, this serves as a warning to those young and newbie riders to take care of themselves on the road.
    My latest buang, is last week or is it the previous week....nevermind...here goes....

    I was traveling along AYE in the morning, heading towards the city, I was riding on the first lane, overtaking, I wasn't paying attention, then a vehicle infront of me brake. Shit, I e-brake, but siao liao, cannot e-brake in time, so to make the best outcome in a bad situation, I tried to siam to the left. Too late, Bang! I crash into the vehicle. scratch the car's left side bumper and crash left side lights(brake,reverse,etc lights) and I fell onto the 2nd lane.

    I was lucky that day, the car on the 2nd lane stop just in time, so I wasn't run over. I just got bruise and scratches on my right leg. And the shocking thing is my bike no problem!!!! That fella's car got smash and my bike no problem.

    But I thank the heaven that I'm in one piece, just think, if the 2nd lane car didn't stop, I wouldn't be here writing this. It was my fault anyway, I wasn't paying attention on the road, just have to live with my mistake and pay the damages.

    My advice to newbies : Pay attention on the road. Accidents happen in a split second!

    buang 1:

    raining, road wet. PIE jalan bahar exit, left filter than turn right, ahead lights red, from far i gradually kick lower and lower gear, from second to first kick too soon, engine brake very sudden, suay suay front tire happen to be on white arrow, coupled with sudden engine brake my front tire skid. so sudden, my ta150 so heavy before i realized i skidded i already kissed the road. heng car behind swerve into other lane. kind malay taxi driver helped me up. i ride home to mechanic, bike go straight is with handlebar turned all the way to the right. leg bleeding from cut, arm bruised, jeans tear big hole.

    moral: careful when braking on wet road, especially where got white arrow. oso, for phantom crash bar is good idea to protect handlebar when buang.

    buang 2:

    just finish raining, road still wet. road got bend, i bank wif second gear. never see oil patch, front tire skidded, again i kissed the floor before i realized i skidded.

    moral: wat can i say? you suay means you suay.

    buang 3:

    my hdb multi story carpark. outside heavy rain, floor extremely wet. the slope go next floor u all know almost ninety degree turn. bank my bike a bit, immediately front tire skid i kissed the floor again.

    moral: multi story car parks the floor damn bloody slippery when wet. once, i braked, tires stopped but bike still moving forward handlebar start dancing almost skidded. so, dat situation try onli first gear half clutch move, go veri slowly, turning use handlebar NEVER bank.

    overall moral: GET RID THOSE BLOODY IRC TIRES!!!
