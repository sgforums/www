    if you read the hire purchase act carefully, the wordings did not state that you should go for the comprehensive type.

    as long as the goods are insured, the bike shop cannot take legal action against you, although this will sour your relationship with them.

    you can do the insurance at ntuc but you need to keep the shop informed. you must also give them a photocopy of the insurance cert.

    hope this can clear your doubts.
