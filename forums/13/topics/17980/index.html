    28 Feb 02, 13:41
    To the best of my limited knowledge, Castrol products are now blended in Malaysia not Singapore any more. As such, Castrol is compelled to use base oil from Petronas, the Malaysian oil company. Base oil is a major component in all lubricant product. If the base oil is lousy, the end product will also be affected. That explains the inconsistency of Castrol TTS in the recent year or so.
