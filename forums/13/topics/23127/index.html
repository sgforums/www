    greetings fella east landers

    pls beware of the bend exiting from TPE from ECP , particulary exit 3C, which is to elias road,

    on 30/6 , i was heading home from work travelling on TPE, as mention i went about that particular exit. which is a long bend, it was my favourite corner which i play everynite,

    entering the exit ,i smell strong petrol, geez, i was wondering how could my bike be leaking petrol while cornering, anyway i tot it was the petrol on the floor, but i jus dismissed it and continue playing my bend, while exiting, i skidded, alongside wif my bike, helpless to the middle lane of the main road, (the exit of the bend is a filter)

    it was quite a bad skid as i remeber at one point fo time, my foot peg is few inches from my face,
    i found my shoulders, tummmy(opps) and elbow wif multiple abrasions

    anyway, here comes the best part, the car behind stopped, and moved off!!!! wow, its a big merc, i was reeling wif pain on the floor, but nobody stopped, onli see show, even oncoming car jus slow down , stare n went off, biangz. until abt half a min later, one kind hearted gentleman on the a blue corrolla ACROSS the road came to help me, i push my bike to the side, i felt like fainting,

    the gentleman look bery bery concern, even i was shock, after assuring him tat i m ok, he move off

    i inspected my bike, gear shifter gone, lights working but cannot start, chain drop, halfway, SCCCCCCCCCRRRRRRRREEEEEEECHHHHHHH, boom, piangz

    someone had an accident on the same bend, but his is in the middle part, at that point of time, i cannot take it anymore, collapsed on the floor,

    after recovering abit, i walked down the bend, to find another aprilia, whom also suffer the same fate as me, he looked shock at me, coz nobody would be walking there, after telling him my story, we two have a gd laugh, knn,

    really suei corner

    at this time, another ah beng, on a hexoagon came playing the same corner, saw us , and bend even lower, he skidded, but he mange to pull back in time, heng for him, suei for us

    after inspecting the whole bend, duno which bastard leak petrol, the whole road is fill wif petrolrite from the beginnnin to the end

    lesson learned..

    1)understand ur corner b4 playing, no petrol today doesnt mean no petrol tomolo

    2)dun take warnings for granted, look for black patches on the floor, petrol smell

    3)pls stop to help accident victims, they really do need help

    4)pls dun play tat exit (exit 3c to elias road on tpe heading towards woodlands)

    5)if ur bike/car/helicopter/plane/truck,trailer,tank,jeep,tonner
    is leakin 2t, 4t, coolant, petrol in huge quantities, pls do something about it

    lastly, pls guys, wear jeans, n get a gd helmet, it really helped in the even of an accident, now gotta clean my wound (ouch!)
