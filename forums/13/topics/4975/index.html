    Season parking at normal open air HDB carpark is $13. $15 is for multi-storey carpark. You have to buy dbl lot ($13*2) if your bike width exceeds one lot. Parking fines for parking in a parking lot without valid parking coupon, the penalty is $8. Parking in a carpark but not in a parking lot is $25, even though it is one the pavement of a carpark.

    When kena in HDB area, the summon will be issued by the HDB, when you are in the CBD area, the summon will be issued by the URA.
