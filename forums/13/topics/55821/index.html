    From my point of view, as long as you don't make any immediate failures and have a smooth ride. More or less will pass for the test. For courses like figure-8, crankcourse and slalom pylon, i apply light rear braking throughout the course as to minimise jerking as the power of the 2a bikes are greater. Hope this tip can make you look more "zai" instead going through courses jerking and wobbling.
