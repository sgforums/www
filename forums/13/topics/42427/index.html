    Originally posted by nismoS132:
    just wondering, how much did you guys get the rennsport for?

    any comments on that tyre?
    thinking of getting it.
    got it at just below $500...RS2
    these tyres are good for people who like tyres similar to the BT010...it's got a neutral feel and corners with the slightest lean...at times, it feels like it wants to corner all on its own...
    this does feel weird at times especially on the highways when you are trying to go staright... the tyre profile makes you wanna ride at an angle to the road, cos there is where the tyre performs its best.

    the RS2 tyre is super sticky, but not extremely great for everyday road rides cos it doesn't heat as much as it should (as compared to to on the track) so it tends to pick up more crap than a standard road compound tyre.... in wet weather (like what we are having recently), it works surprisingly well for a cut slick, but DUN push or lean too hard into a turn.... the slick edges tend to aquaplane .. i had a nice front end slide across one lane at 40km/h during the rain... reminded me of my mortality....

    at the tracks... the tyres inspire you to push your limits in the corner... making you take corners at higher lean angles and higher corner speeds....yet without the fear of losing the front end.... rear tyre slides pretty predicatably when pushed hard....

    really good at soaking up bumps along the track.... but tends to want to stand-up when you are on the brakes in the corner.... great flickability through turns...

    tyre wear is pretty good...ridden about 2000-3000km (inclusive of 2hours of track) so far... tyre is still good for another hard track day....but i guess the tyre wear would depend on your won riding style...

    it's the 2nd race compound tyre i've used and in my opinion, possibly the best tyre so far.... next up for me to try would be the pirelli supercorsa... should be the same as the rennsport... but i wanna know for myself instead of relying too much on other people's info....

    if money is no option, why not try the Michellin Pilot Race? read about it in mags.....
