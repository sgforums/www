    Originally posted by vti_79:
    1.is sp insurance higher than rxz..by how much?how about things like rd tax and hdb parking and scrap value?
    Not sure about road tax and scrap value, but HDB parking fee should be the same for all bikes, isn't it?

    2.petrol wise,how far can u go with $10 on sp and on rxz?
    Based on my experience...
    RXZ can travel 27-28 kms per litre of petrol.
    SP varies between 20 to slightly more than 25 kms per litre of petrol, depends on your riding style.

    3.does rxz need 2T?any 2b bike no need 2T?
    Of course, RXZ is a 2-stroke bike. All 2-strokers need 2T. All 4-strokers don't need 2T, and there's quite a lot of 2B 4-strokers in the market, like Phantom 200.

    5.For rear brake,disc or drum better?cos sp is disc and rxz is drum...
    Theoritically, disc is better than drum. All sport bikes nowadays use disc rear brake. But my SP's rear brake is a bit lousy
    Dunno if other SP riders also have the same problem...

    6.Heard rxz is a very problem free bike with high resale value..is that true?if no,what are the bad points of rxz?
    Problem free... true
    High resale... quite true
    Bad point... not as good looking as SP
