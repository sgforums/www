    honda calls their variable valve timing technology VTEC. suzuki bandits are equipped with something similar, called VV or VVT, i'm not really sure, but such bandits can be identified by the red cylinder block cover.

    bandits have fully adjustable suspensions, not sure about the S4s. certain ver S S4s and 50th anniversary ver S have brembo front brakes, and i think that's really cool.

    if your friend is into naked streetfighters, has he considered the yamaha XJR400? ohlins rear shocks, brembo pumps and R1 (dunno what brand) calipers. there's also the ducati monster 400 as well.

    if he's willing to sacrifice a little top end for better fuel economy, a hornet 250 would do nicely as well. i guess he can't go wrong buying a honda...
