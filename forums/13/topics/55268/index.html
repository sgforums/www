    Hi, for your questions.. I'll try to answer them as detailed as I can.

    1) Yes, there is no private learning for Class 2B. However, you may book for extra self-practice lessons, which are cheaper than normal lessons or revisions.

    2) Yes, the riding theory test is not the basic theory test.. it has been modified so that it caters to motorcyclists.. but it is quite similar.

    3) It is a requirement now that all trainees go through the 5 theory lessons before you go for the evaluation conducted by the driving school, after you pass the evaluation, you may book for your RTT.

    4) You are correct. After getting 2B, you have to wait one year before you can proceed to learn Class 2A.
