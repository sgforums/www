    In motocycling trainee terms, it means the test day where retired police officers were re-employed at invigilators on that test date to qualify or fail the testee on their management of a bike and road safety on that day.

    Trainees are trained by driving centres instructors in the circuit and on certain roads to pass that test of showing their abilities in managing the bike and the road condition prior to that TP date.
