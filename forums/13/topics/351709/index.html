    With more and more HDB car parks turning to EPS, are they being fair to motorcyclist who have to pay 65 cents per entry? 

    Currently with the coupon system, we tear a 65 cents coupon for whole day parking at any HDB/URA car parks.

    Now if you have to go about, in and out of the car park, you pay 65 cents each time. This is a far cry from the coupon system.

    The EPS is beneficial for car owners, who are charged by the minute, but penalise the humble motorcycle rider.

    Decision makers and planners are most likely car owners, and therefore they just couldn't care about the bikers.
