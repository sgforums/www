    2B sports bike are categorised as below

    -NSR150SP (low budget, low Overall performance, taxi bike, high reliability, high power)

    -TZM150 (high budget, low overall performance, moderate reliability, high power)

    -KRR150 (Low budget, low overall performance, moderate reliability, high power)

    -RS125 (High budget, high overall performance, low reliability, moderate power)

    -Mito125 (Very high budget, Very high overall performance esp on track, low reliability, moderate power)

    -TZRR125 (Low budget, Moderate Overall performance, Rare, Moderate Reliability, Low power)
