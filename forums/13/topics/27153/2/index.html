    The Hayabusa is a very fast bike... but depends on your definition of fast, fast in a straight line, yes it is, bike shootouts are usually done at unused airport runways... don't see too many roads like that in Singapore or anyweher else though, the Zx12r is also another monster, but all that power must be able to be controlled by the rider.

    I feel the Zx12r has better handling than the Hayabusa, due to weight distribution and the shorter wheelbase i.e. it WILL corner better, and the bike is more nimble.

    Therefore straight speed fast, I agree would be the busa, but overall fast, in my opinion would be the ZX.
