    On 12 Aug 02 at about 1700hrs, while I was busy fixing some hydraulic pipings on the Almond vehicle, I received a message from my friend from Sungei Gedong Camp. He told me Gan died on Saturday in JB after he skidded during a corner and hit his neck on the kerb or railing. He died en route to hospital.

    Gan has been a very gutsy but reckless rider on the road. I've encouraged him to go to track but he didn't seemed interested. Advices always sound so harsh and offensive yet they helps.

    A reminder to all riders reading this post. Even though JB is out of SG TP power, we're still subjected to perils on the road. And it's more hazardous in the dimly lit JB with many unkwown potholes on the ground. So please ride safely. If you want to play, I strongly encourage you to join the tracksters and have an enjoyable day on the track. It proved track worths the trip.
