    wenever i start mine in the morn, i switch on choke, start then leave the choke on (let the engine run a little faster), after bout 15~20 seconds the engine will lower a little, then i switch off the choke to let engine run at usual.
    next i will just play with the throttle. how i tell is (when still neutral of coz) i open throttle slightly and i see how it sounds. i always experience either one of two distinctively different sounds: one is the weak rev which i believe wat you are describing. if like dat i keep blowing throttle. after a while when i test again by opening the throttle slightly again, i should experience the second sound, which is stronger or like normal when the engine no longer cold. this has been my morning ritual ever since dat time just come out of my carpark at the junction, light turn green, i open throttle skali weak weak like you describe and it almost stalled. i quickly clutch in, blow harder and released clutch. of coz then the bike shot out like a rocket.
