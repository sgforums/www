    Hi there,i've been riding the TA200 for 8 mths and this is what i feel:

    Thumbs up:1)low fuel consumption
    2)low maintenance
    3)powerful and steady engine
    (for a 2B bike);
    130km/h with pillion no problem!
    Fxxk up : 1)lousy chrome.i spotted specks of
    rust just two weeks on the road!Even though i clean diligently there's still rust formation.
    2)Slow pickup.get used to smelling the exhaust of all those sp,tzr or even vespa when the lights turn green.
    3)Needs long warming up for the engine to really get going.
    4)Tyres that are prone to skid.

    Overall however if you are the type of safety rider who dont mind all the super4s n sp zooming pass u its a good road bike,cheap mah.($5500+ first hand.)
    Your girl wil also appreciate the comfortable sit and the convienence of the box.
