    mine schedule:

    2T top up every 3-5 weeks
    chain lube every 500km/1week
    every 4000km/2months, change gear oil
    every 6000km/3months, change spark plug

    every 10000km/6months, wash carb, air filter,
    every 20000km/12months, wash block, piston, pistonring, valve, change coolant
