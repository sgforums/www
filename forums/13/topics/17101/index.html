    There are pros and cons of both:

    Bike
    ====
    (+)Cheaper, less maintenance cost, faster to commute (especially during traffic congestions), less parking charges, less toll (or ERP), nippy, fun.
    (-)Man on machine rather than man in machine.
    Tropical weather tends to spoil the fun.
    Cannot carry much.. maximum one passenger or some goods.

    Car
    ===
    (+) Safer, stable, no need to worry of rainy days, spacious, comfort (stereo, aircond, cushion seats, VCD player), large boot space
    (-) Expensive, high maintenance cost, fuel hungry, high parking and toll charges, high insurance and road tax.
