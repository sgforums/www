    18 Dec 01, 18:58
    donch take mez z1 tyres. they are only good for straight roads.

    rs125 use Dunlop GPR70, pirelli Dragon or batalax BT 010 or 090.

    110 for front and 140/50 for rear. profile 60

    speaking from personal exp.
    mez tyres no good to play corner. go certain angle will slide. but good for long straight roads.

    GPR70 track tyre. good for corner but no good for wet weather. summore these few months wet days ahead. most ex of the lot

    Batalax very sticky. so sticky until end of the day still can see the leaves on the tyres.
    recomended by fren. i no try b4 so no comments.

    dragon tyres. road/track no problem. compound very hard so takes a while for the tyres to be seasoned. im using them. good for me.

    theres no one right tyres for all. see ur riding pattern then decide.

    if u trust ah boy then go get his batalax.
    200/pair. f**king cheap.

    ar bo can get at any other shop. ET not bad. do chk out the other thread for his addy.
