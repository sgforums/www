    If that's the case, of course it's normal lah, nothing to worry about
    When the clutch handle is released, the gears are engaged, and the rear wheel will rotate, becos there's no friction with the ground.

    4. Can anyon tell me if there's any tap to shut off so there's no fuel coming out ?
    Usually it's below the tank, not far from the carb. For SP and RXZ, it's on the left hand side.
