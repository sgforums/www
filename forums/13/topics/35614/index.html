    which part of the bike are you refering to? autosol removes rust, but rust prevention is minimal... if it's a non-wear area (decorative, low contact, like the chrome covers on the sides), you could use clear lacquer to seal in the nice finish after you polish. it's available at most hobby or woodcraft shops in spray form. lacquer is not indestructible, can get worn off, but a good application of 5 thin coats (allowing each coat to dry in between sprays) can last you a good while if the part is not frequently touched or worn. plus if you take the time to buff and polish the surface... whooooo... nice... seen gibson guitars before??
