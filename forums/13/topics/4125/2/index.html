    Vibration is a bad thing in mechanics.

    And especially in engine, if an engine vibrates too much, it tells that something is not veri good abt it. It may be underused, overused or just not veri efficient.

    An engine that vibrates too much may give u extra power but it may not last veri long, hence the lower reliability.

    I can give u a few examples but i tink 1 is enuff. Take the gears inside ur engine. When you have excessive vibration, you may accidentally widen the backlash. You might end up wif a grind gear(toothless gear).

    Personally, tw is a cool bike but it is not so reliable when you campare wif other bikes. I believe you have got my opinion on the relativity of vibration and reliability of a bike but its only mine...
