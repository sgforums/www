  • david1881
    18 Aug 02, 01:22
    I am very satisfied with my Phantom 200.

    Very fuel effiecient bike, better than my previous GN125. I could get about 350km per full tank. Pick up wise is not as good as Phantom 150 because this is a 4 stroke bike, but it has good torque and less gear changes are necessary.

    Maximum speed I ever achieved was 130km/h but the meter has error reading of about 10%. I verified this by installing a Cateye bicycle speedometer. Cateye reading + 10% = Phantom reading.

    Long distance comfort is also a lot better than all sports bike because I rode this babe to KL and back on National Day without any back ache.

    I strongly recommend this bike for cheap and reliable transport. But for more safety, please change the IRC tyres to better grip rubber because it's terrible in the wet and during emergency braking.

    Just my opinion.
