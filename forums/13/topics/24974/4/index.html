    In 2 strokes bike, 2T oil is used to lubricate the piston and combustion champer. 4T oil is the engine oil use to lubricate the gear, clutch etc.

    4 strokes engine need only engine oil.

    (You can refer to my earlier post about how 2 strokes engine work).

    Run in ?
    Riding not exceeding 60KM/h for the first 500KM, change engine oil.
    Then not exceeding 80KM/h for the next 1000KM. change engine oil again at 1500KM, after that, you ride at any speed as long no exceeding the law permitted.
