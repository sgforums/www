    Went for this on thurs, heres the summary (for those who are curious)

    - starts with theory (very sian ah talk about bike accidents...etc.. but make u aware that no point cheong on the road..)
    - next practical (ride in the small circuit) use FQ CB125T bike, in first gear ride around pylons, initially quite difficult then one hand riding!! wah siong! then 2 hand riding , suddenly so easy to celar the pylon, then stand up while riding round the pylon, SIONG!!!)
    - then riding simulator (quite sian lah)

    - then, do rear skidding and how to counter skid!
    this one look damm cool lahm, like in the movies where the bike skid!

    - then 12-1 (big circuit no car)
    - start warm up at the plank and then go to the bumpy course (that only 2A do) and ride on it.. wah piang! not easy bust half stand then can clear it.. and then again after the e-brake,
    - then do the zig-zag plan! siong.. hard to clear.... then

    then the instructor say,
    "now i want u to try your best to catch me and over take me ...if you can lah, if u cant just try to follow behind ok"

    wah after that he go round the s course, crank course, car's s course and crank course and back to s-course.. all the way so many conner he want us to celar it as fast as possible!
    fun man!!! conner like no body business!! i conner until the foot peg scrap the floor ah.. but i still cannot do it so fast.. but very fun lah feel like a racer!!.. this is something u cant do on the road..well legally anyway...

    -then next...on narrow road do uturn... many uturn on narrow road.. do until headache.. this one quite difficult.. not cant do it..
    - and then riding in s-course where front wheel up the kerb, rear wheel below the kerb!! this damm thing siong!!!

    all in all..on lah the course, ok lah not bad.. learn some new thing, get to play conner....
