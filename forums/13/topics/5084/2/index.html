    metalic silver or chrome silver? Chrome silver must be very hard.. I think u should use air brush not gas spray... Spray one is too strong, so the painting won't smooth. If not smooth, then chrome silver is ugly cos the dropling will be very obvious. I suggest u to ask motortiam to do it for you.

    If metalic silver should be ok one (like those on KRR). But, carefull when doing coating... must be aware from that dropling which drop from spraying hole. Must often wipe it with tissue and make sure that the hole doesn't have excess liquid or not blocked with dried paint residue. So, before each spraying, must try first on something else to be sure. Every spraying, don't press to long... the liquid will surely drop one.. maybe every 10 secs then wipe the hole, then continue again...
