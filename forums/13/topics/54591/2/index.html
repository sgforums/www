    simple piece of advice: "Ride like everyone on the road is trying to kill u." This piece of advice was given to me by a long time bike rider. In other words, ride defensively and ride slow. No point speeding and cutting thru traffic just for a few minutes and no point getting the fastest 2b bike either.
    I've been in two accidents despite taking all these precautions (both times knocked down by blind and idiotic drivers), but i still love riding. I have gotten my class 2, but i still mutter a prayer everytime i put on my helmet. Unfortunately, since there's only that many precautions u can take, and the rest is up to other road users. there are drivers out there who are arrogant, idiotic, or just plain blur.
    I am not going to try to convince u riding is safe. It isn't, due to the fact that there are factors that are not in your control. Still, many of us love riding because it is part of this that makes it exhilirating. So, before u decide to take up this form of transport, consider this carefully, and keep this in mind. Equally important, keep in mind your loved ones too.
    Good luck.
