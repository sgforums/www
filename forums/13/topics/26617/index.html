    F50:

    A gasket is a material e.g cork, reinforced cardboard, copper etc.
    that is used between 2 pieces of metal. for example, the block and the head, head and cam cover, sump plug and sump.
    a gasket is used because the two metalic surfaces don't seal tight and therefore to seal 2 pieces of metal.
