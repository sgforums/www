    This is not written by me, but lifted off "The Motorcycle Rider" Delphi forum located at http://www.delphi.com/motorbiker/messages

    Some things to take note of when purchasing a 2nd hand bike :-

    1) Check Brake Disc for warp and chipped
    areas.
    2) Steering Cone.
    3) Check for leaking fork seals.
    4) Check sprocket and chain wear.
    5) Look out for contaminated brake oil in
    the reservoir.
    6) Condition and wear of tyres.
    7) Wheels Bearings, shake wheels they should
    not budge at all.
    Check for rust in fuel tank.
    9) Check suspension for too soft or too
    hard.
    10)Check all switches and lights.
    11)Check for oily patches on engine and
    visually inspect gaskets.
    12)Check oil filter to see there is any
    damage around the sides.
    13)Coolant rubber tubings to check for leaks
    14)Check frame to see if there are cracks or
    re-welded.
    15)Blow engine after warm up, no smoke
    allowed for 4-strokers. Smoky no good.
    16)Check engine block to see got putty or
    silicone. Ask why is it there.
    17)Single carbs no need to worry too much,
    in-line-4s check for spark plug firing
    and snyc.

    Hope this helps.
