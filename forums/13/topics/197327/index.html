    1. What are good sports bike for beginners (No i dont need any modded bikes with nitro n all). Just plain good looking beginners bike?

    - A beginner doesn't start riding a superbike in SG. A beginner starts off riding a bike with a maximum of 200cc. So far I haven't seen anyone in Singapore turbocharging their bikes. If you want a sportsbike looking model, you can consider the SP or Kips

    2. Same Question for the cruiser?

    - Small cruiser? the most common one would be the Honda Phantom. There are other models too.

    3. What kind of license will i need? I hear there are classes or something.. Sorry but i didnt quite ge it from the pieces i read in various threads.

    - Class 2b = up to 200cc, Class 2a = 201 - 400cc, Class 2 = 401cc onwards
    Licence will start at 2b, you can only get 2a one year after you've passed. Same goes to the class 2, one year after you've passed 2a.

    4. Is it possible to ride from SG to ML and TL??

    - Yes, in fact many riders in SG do that. But it is not recommended to go on a 2b bike, though i've seen photos of a phantom at the thai border.
