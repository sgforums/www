    to cut things short, an overly rich mixture can cause plug fouling, throttle bogging at low revs, poor fuel consumption.. can't think of any advantages of having a rich mixture. a richer mixture does help your bike run cooler though.

    a lean mixture will feel weak at low rpm, run much hotter, possibly cause engine damage due to overheating. you'll feel a power gain at higher revs, but it's not good for your engine...
