    Originally posted by eric_jugonz:
    Phantom 200 comes with tubeless IRC tyres.. not much difference though.. Slippery also..
    My advice to all fellow Phantom riders: The IRC tires are made specially for new bikes, using more percentage of syntethic rubber than natural rubber. You see, manufacturer will tender to supply tyres to assembler. They have to quote the cheapest in order to win the tender. So the enduser is the end-loser. This is a common phenomenon too with cars. That is why, it is no uncommon to find a new owner trading in his set of tyres when new.

    Don't compromise your own safety with sub-standard tyres. If you can't afford, ride over to Malaysia and have it changed at any motorbike shops. The price is half that of Singapore's and you get good-gripping tyres.
