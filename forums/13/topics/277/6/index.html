    You may want to try the following that helped me.

    1) Change spark plug to Denso Iridium
    2) Change engine oil to synthetic oil
    3) Change to Shell V-Power
    4) Use Fuel Additive (to clear carbon deposits in carb)
    5) Tune up/clean your carb (for 1/2 throttle to 3/4 position)

    There should be immediate improvements if you try out some or all of the above.
