    20 Aug 02, 13:04
    I run-in my bike with the following parameters:

    max speed 80km/h
    distance to run-in: 800km
    1st oil change at: 800km
    2nd oil change at: 2300km
    use full synthetic oil only plus some additive to protect the engine.

    Now i average of riding at 100km/h to 110km/h on the Xpress way after 800km.

    But very important. Warm up the bike every start-up for 2-4mins b4 riding. this is to ensure the oil sump circulates within the engine.
