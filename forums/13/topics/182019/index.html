    Erm hi there! Congrats on your 2B! Buy bike is like buy fish in market. Here let me explain.

    Go to any bikeshop, bring your license and IC and $$ for downpayment, maybe $1000? Look around and see got what good bikes you like in mind. If you buy new bikes, its much the same, but if 2nd hand, u gotta be careful not to anyhow buy, coz it might just be a problematic bike. Suggest you get new bikes.

    Season parking 1st time must bring log card and IC to your neighbourhood's HDB office to buy. $16/month for multi story.

    Petrol just go to any petrol kiosk, as long as petrol will do. If u dono how you can consult the uncle pump attendents, they will guide you. DON'T ever pump diesel, those are for lorries and trucks...
