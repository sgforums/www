    Hi, I'm new here and happen to get from my search on BT090.

    I just bought a front BT090 because of a very bad puncture and had no time to wait for a BT010. Wished I had wait longer coz I really regretted the choice the moment I left the shop.

    The compound of the tire is damn hard. Absolutely no grip even on a hot sunny day in Singapore. You may think its the wax, but by using my fingers to feel, the centre area of the tire which the wax had worn of is more slippery than the sides which had some sticky coating which conned me into buying it.

    My bike feels damn loose and oversteers alot, bike feels like its dropping. I guess I have to lower the suspension height.

    When I corner in, Its so hard to recover back.

    Maybe my judgement is wrong, maybe its really racing tires like you guys said, but I can't find this model in their website.

    Whatever it is, don't try it, it'll make you hate riding.
