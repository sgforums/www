    TS,

    Yes you are allowed to own bikes in Singapore. Even if you do not own a licence or have converted your licence.

    It might be prudent to note that you should try to have the right class of licence for the class of bike that you are intending to buy.

    There have been cases of folks who bought a bike, but his converted Singapore licence only allows him a Class 2b. So he has no choice, either to leave the bike at his home or to sell the bike.
