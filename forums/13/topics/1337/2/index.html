    er.. some useful info about the "thickness" lar.. "120/60 ZR15" is a typical tyre rating. btw i got this from the singaporebikes website.

    the "120" refers to the width in mm, the "60" is the "aspect ratio", which is the thickness of the tyre expressed as a percentage of the width. so in the case above, a "120/60" tyre would have a side profile of 72mm.

    the "ZR" is a speed rating, which means the tyre is suited for travelling at speeds greater than 240km/h, "Q" is up to 160km/h, "T" is up to 190km/h and so on...

    the last number is your rim diameter in inches lar... hope this helps..
