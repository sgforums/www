    Originally posted by sakaratulz:
    er...how do u all derestrict the pipe?
    issit the muffler part?do it ureself or mechanic do wan?thx

    Destrict the pipe normally at the stomach of the pipe. Derestrict at motor shop. For SP, they will open up the stomach by welding it. Then using the same welding tool, they will remove something in the pipe that is restricting the power of the bike. When the pipe is cleared, they will weld back the pipe.
    I ever tried to destrict at the silencer part too. The mechanic simply remove the middle part of the silencer and drill more holes to allow more power output. But then the pipe then be slightly noiser and sharper.
    To counter this, just insert a special type of cotton wool. This cotton will soften the sharpness of the pipe after derestricting. But you have to change this cotton regularly cos it will get dirty and therefore lsing the effect. This special type of cotton is also found in the filter of some air-con.
