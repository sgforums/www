    This kind of thread did appear before but I think quite some time ago liao.

    For your benefit.

    Annual Stuff
    1.) Road tax (depending if wat on the cc, and age of the bike can vary. $70 bucks and above) Can pay pay half-yearly also.
    2.) Inspection.
    3.) Insurance (depending on bike and age of rider. Of course, p-plate on 2B sport bike will be expensive as a guide think in hundreds of dollars)

    Monthly Stuff
    1.) Parking. $15 bucks for open air/ 17 for multi-storey
    2.) Depending on ur school premies. U may incurred additional parking cost.
    3.) Bike installment, depends on the bike shop arrangements (ususally 200 and above)

    Weekly Stuff
    1.) Petrol. Depends on ur bike (drink petrol or eat petrol). Depends on traveling distance. If u travel from east to west, everyday, on a sport bike, ur weekly petrol is confirm above $10 per week.
    2.) Depending on if u ride 2 strokers, u may need to top up 2T oil on a weekly or monthly basis.

    Maintenance Stuff
    1.) Depending on ur riding behavior, some dudes like me go for servicing every 5000km, this translate to 6 mths (for me only, some can be earlier or later).
    This includes changing of engine oil 4T, oil filter.
    10000km, wash carb, change brake pads, spark plugs, if tyre bald liao needs to change
    For me I usually spend $50 and above (but mine is a 400cc)
    (Disclaimer here: Ur bike condition plays a big part, 2nd hand bike ususally needs more attention and money, as they have already been on the road for a certain time, wear and tear on other parts comes into play like sprocket, chain, pistons, etc, this will cost more than regular servicing)

    Cosmetic stuff
    1.) Depends on individual, if u thinking of riding sports bike, than confirm u will hand itchy, butt itchy go and add this, modify that. All this translate to $$$$.

    Haywire conditions
    1.) Bike suddenly suka suka breaks down. Depending on where and the damage, u might need to call tow (=$$) and repairs (=more $$$)

    This is so far I can remember, if u guys can add on, please add on
