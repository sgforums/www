    type: speed trap

    Description: its a back lane leading into the back gate of bedok camp, on many early mornings, a police van will be at the lane wif a speed camera inside, u cannot see him, but he can see u, sometimes i see BMWs hiding inside late at nites, u can onli see them within a 1 km range, but up till then, they might have snap a picture of u speeding, if u r not paying attention, u might not know whether its there at all.

    3) Location: duno wad road, running beside paya lebar base one, leading to hougang from exit of TPE

    type: speed trap

    description: after a bend, somewhere oppistie shell,near the fire station of paya lebar air base (tango alpha whisky gate) a police van is there wif a camera also, speed limit is 70.

    4) Location, end of TPE and entrance into ECP or airport boulevard

    type:camper

    description: 2 left lanes will be leading to airport, 1 right lane into ECP, and a long long continuos double white line will be seperating them, in mornings, the entrance is usaully packed, those who cut queue by crossing the double white lines, will be booked, I WAS BOOKED THERE AND GOT 4 DEMERIT POINTS. 2 TPs n 1 ROV was there n i never see them until i went pass them.

    5) Location: sim lim square

    type:camper

    description: those who try to siam ERP by going on pavement n riding anti traffic will be booked, i think some of u all might know.

    6)location: Kelanton Lane (i-love-lim-ah-boy road)

    type: camper

    description :as our dear friend honda castrol has said, there got TP!!, i myself saw on TP scooter on the back lane, who knoes wheres the fella hiding ?

    Location: Lim Chu Kang emergency runway
    Type: Camper 80% / Sniper 20%

    I used to work in Sungei Gedong Armour Camp. Pass by LCK almost everyday for almost 3 years. Speed limit = 60kph. Seen TP or normal police hiding in the sliproads near the cemetry or at the sliproads near LCK camp/Murai camp. When our BX, SM1 and Ultra were on exercises in the night, they can even see police camping at the roads near Ama Keng or even as inside as Neo Tew. Might be armed with speed trap devices.

    Location: Sungei Tengah Road
    Type: Camper 80% / Sniper 20%

    Some of the times I'd choose to go by PIE BKE KJE then exit at Sungei Tengah Road which can bring me to LCK runway. I've seen police hiding outside Tengah Air base waiting for anyone to speed pass. Luckily never speed on several occasions. But since these two places are immediately linked to each other, it is inevitable to have police covering these 2 areas at the same time. Better be careful.
