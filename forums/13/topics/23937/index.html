    a 4-stroke engine has "engine oil" (4T) located in the crankcase, where it lubricates the pistons as well.. this dedicated form of lubrication is sufficient for the engine, as opposed to a 2 stroker, where the crankcase is empty. lubrication is provided by the 2T mixed with the fuel... the "engine oil"/4T for a 2 stroker lies in the gearbox only...
