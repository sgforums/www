    i had a friend's friend who was working with police planning. you know all those red light cams? well some are empty. they rotate the cameras around every month or so. to where i dunno. so some cameras are a deterrence only.

    as for the speed thing, it's more technical, so bear with me. they use radar waves, cos IR is found in white light, and might confuse the signal. the speed cam shoots out waves in intervals. it's actually not how fast the beam comes back, because all electromagnetic waves travel at same speed. IR waves and radar waves travel at same speed. so during one beam shoot, the beam bounce off your vehicle and bounce back, the computer notes the distance. the next beam does the same thing. after 2 shots, the camera would know how fast you're going, because it knows how much you travel in that short interval of time. if you're too fast then you kenna already. hope this helps.
