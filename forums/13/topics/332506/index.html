    5 Oct 08, 21:01

    Go down to the driving centres and ask the instructors. They are always willing to explain to you. They will give you the true picture.

    Booking practical lessons is no hassel. If you are early you can always get a slot in the available days, in the week or the next. Its best to book immediately after you have finished a practical lesson, because you need to know first if you have to repeat a practical lesson or progress to the next stage.

    But for the TP test, you can only book for the test date after you pass your centre's assessment test.

    If you want you 2B, just go register at any of the centres and start your practical. How soon you get your licence depend on how fast you develop your riding skills. You havn't start your riding lessons yet, and you are griping about test dates.
