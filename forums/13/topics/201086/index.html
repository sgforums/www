    I just did scraping of my bike 2 months back.

    The procedure is this:

    1) Go down to LTA @ Sin Ming bringing along your vehicle's documents - LOG CARD (impt) and your IC. You can authorize someone else to act on your behalf by passing all documents to him/her together with your letter of consent. That person must bring along his/her IC too.

    2) Approach the Service Counter which you can see immediately when you enter LTA, ask for a Quene Number for Counter 13 or 14.

    3) When your number is called, tell the LTA staff that you want to scrape your vehicle.

    From that onwards, the staff will guide you. Remember to obtain from him/her a Derage Form aka Deregistration Form.

    You can either bring your bike to authorized scrapeyards (locations ask LTA - 1800 - 225 5582) or engage a towing service which they will charge you for doing it for you.

    By scraping bike, you won't be able to get back any liquid sum of your bike. You are only entitled to the remaining rebate of your COE which you can use it for your next purchase of a vehicle or you can transfer it to anyone else for a $10 transferring fee; alas, selling it to others.

    Hope my infos help.
