    Recently, while paying for my road tax and insurance for my motorcycle, I was told of this Personal Accident Insurance by NTUC Income.

    Medical Expenses: $1,000.
    You need to pay more if you want more or higher benefits.
    Premium burn if there if there are no claims.
    Its a cheap and simple cover, so I just throw $50 there.
