  • roger
    19 Oct 01, 09:25
    some words of caution for you

    despite being restricted to 12hp in singapore, the aprilia is still a very fast bike.

    most 2b riders are not able to handle the power and lightness of the aprilia. as a result, aprilia riders are very prone to accidents.

    the brakes are extremely strong for a 125 cc bike. take care not to brake too hard. the grip of the tyres and frame design are very good, which means that you can corner to the extreme.

    however 2 up riding needs extreme care. a pillion will greatly change the centre of gravity of the bike. try to ride for a few months without pillions to get used to the bike.

    servicing will be expensive compared to SP or KRR.

    all in all, if you can afford it, get one.
    but handle it with care.
