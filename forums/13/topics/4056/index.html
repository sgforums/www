    suntec roundabout: DO NOT PARK THERE. that area is mostly for despatch riders who are there only for a short while. i have seen ppl kena summon there many times on weekends. same as the place under the bridge near convention center. park in the carpark lah!!! ITS FREE LAH.

    bugis: beware the high humps in the carpark. on weekend nights, the parking lots inside are super crowded. there is a dark alley behind the opposite coffeeshop which is free parking after 5pm, perfectly legal. but is very dark, park at own risk. i park there many times but nothing happen lah...too secluded until even thieves dunno the place.

    simlim: park on the pavement beside taxi stand. BEWARE the coupon parking lots around simlim though, always got ppl come check very very very VERY often. infamous place. got once i left my bike for 5 mins came back got summon liao.

    orchard area: behind hmv, free but beware helmet thieves. Meridien, free parking. plaza sing. if i not wrong taka is also free parking... always see bikes outside the old sparks at 7th floor.
