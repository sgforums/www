    Ok here is how I feel after riding the X9, 180cc.

    The digital gauges are very nice and very useful, it will prompt for its servicing, Oil change and belt change; so u do not have to remmber and keep track of the mileage. There is a charger outlet in the underseat compartment, wif internal limit switch lighting, for u to charge ur HP or plug in any ting u like. The Piaggio dealer r friendly and helpful.

    Here r some munis pt... At 1st I get headach when riding it cos the ht of the wind shield is just right to cover ur body cosing the drag wind to directly hit ur head cosing the headach, but now use to it liao... Nxt the under seat compartment is not as big as i thought, only can put 1 normal helmet and 1 half cap, i thoughht it can hold 2 normal helmet. Then its the fuel comsumsion its pretty high considing that it only 180cc, $18, 98 petrol, can only go to abt 300km, almost same as my R1. Lastly maybe only to me its Slow... no pick up like skipper cos of it 4stroke engine... and when i went servicing I was told that the run in speed is 70km/h they were shock when the saw my top speed at the bike digital gauge, the gauge display time, date, top speed, mean speed..etc, my was already 115..
