    Originally posted by eric_jugonz:
    how to check the tyre pressure?
    Just go to the air pump at the petrol kiosk, set to the desired level and pump. If it's too low, the pump will have a "ting ting ting" sound (not sure about the new digital ones; I think it's a beep or something) until the pressure has been raised to the preset level.

    Alternatively, you could buy a pressure gauge which I don't really think is necessary.
