    when they calculate your insurance premium, they assign you points for every detail.
    the higher the points, the higher the premium.

    this are how points are assigned.

    types of insurance
    1st party --> 61
    TPFT (3rd party, fire and theft) --> 36
    3rd party --> 25

    age of insurer
    below 21 --> 20
    21-25 -> 16
    26-27 -> 10
    28-30 -> 4
    30+ and own car --> 0

    driving experience
    0-1yr --> 8
    1-2yr --> 4
    over 2yrs --> 0

    claims experience
    <5k --> 0
    5k-10k --> 5
    10k - 15k --> 10
    15k-20k --> 15
    20k+ --> *special*

    this are the ones i have on paper.

    vehicle model
    aprilia --> 22
    daewoo --> 18

    100-299cc --> 8
