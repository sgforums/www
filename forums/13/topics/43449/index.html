    Share with some experience from my bigger bike friends, so here is my advice.

    Class 2 bike is usually for "show-off" use one. Go for one only if you really got the extra money to spare, but don't know where to use. Usually those from armed force ride big bike - signed on personal usually got a lot of money but no time to spent. Anyway, it good to aim big - got target in life better than none.

    Class 2A bike got better performance than 2B, but at a higher cost. 2A bikes onwards usually lead the traffic, which is a very big advantage. Some people think that having a 2A bike is enough liao, but from what my friend say - when you have power, you will ask for more power. That's why some people advise to change for a class 2 bike directly rather than going an extra step to 2A first. Do it if you can endure it.

    Class 2B everyone knows the advantages. The only thing I can say about 2B bikes is - not shiok enough. My advice is - don't invest on a new 2B bike, not worth it (money wise), if you are planning for an upgrade in the future.

    In summary, get a 2 bike if you REALLY got the money. Get a 2A bike when you are fed up with 2B. Remain with a 2B if upgrading is a problem. About bike license, go for as high as possible, just for fun or whatever reason.
