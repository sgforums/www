    Hi. i got my class 2B and 2A license at CDC, located at UBI. So i'm only able to tell you roughly how much each practical cost. For SSDC, located at Yio chu kang, it should be around the same but at BBDC, located at Bukit Batok, they go by subjects.

    At CDC, you have to pass 8 practicals and riding theory test before you can book for the real test by the traffic police testers.

    For Prac 1 to 5, its only in the circuit, cheaper at $22++. Prac 5 is an evaluation of the whole circuit's obstacle course.

    For Prac 6 to 8, they are on the road, at $32++. You will need to have a provisional driving license which you can get for $25 upon passing the riding theory test($6). Prac 8 is an evaluation of circuit's obstacles and on the road.

    Circuit revision available upon completion of prac 4, road revision available upon completion of prac 7. $10 and $20 respectively i think..............

    The enrolment fee cost $72++. Let's say you need two trys to pass a prac, that will be $[(20 x 5) x 2]+[(30 x 3) x 2]= $380

    Enrolment fee $72
    R.t.t $6
    Pdl $25
    Pracs $380 Haven't add the amount of revision.
    Test $35

    7 Sep 03, 23:01
    It used to be very fast last time..... but now may take a slower period. You will bring $100 and two ic colour photos. $72++ for the enrolment and straightaway book your prac 1. Total $90++. At CDC, there's a ATM. And if you are going to CDC, you will have to deposit money with them( this they will explain to you ).

    Let me share with you the fastest way to your license. When you enrol, bk prac 1. And check out when do they have lessons, you will need completion of lesson 1 to 4 to bk for your R.t.t. It's best to go on dates that has two lesson in one day. Upon completion of prac 1, you should have booked your R.t.t. The waiting time is around one and half months, during this time, you can try to complete your circuit's obstacle evaluation. R.t.t. results will come in 14 days, with it, you can get your pdl in order to proceed onto prac 6 to 8 and finally your test. Gd luck, take care when riding.
