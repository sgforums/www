    FEB 5, 2002 TUE (ST)

    THREE men suffered minor injuries when a police patrol car collided with a motorcycle yesterday morning at the junction of Woodlands Avenue 1 and Woodlands Street 32.

    The motorcycle became lodged underneath the front of the patrol car in the crash, which happened shortly after 9 am. A traffic light was also knocked over.

    The men on the motorcycle and two male police officers were taken to Tan Tock Seng Hospital for examination.

    One officer suffered a minor injury on his forehead. The two motorcycle riders, though showing no visible signs of injury, complained of chest pains.

    A police spokesman said the officers were responding to a case when the accident happened.
