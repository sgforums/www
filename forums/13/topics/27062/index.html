  • WhitePhantom
    3 Oct 02, 15:25
    i m not a sports biker
    but here's my personal opinion on all the 2B sports series
    no flame pls!

    NSR-SP150------ Most Common, therefore all parts n accessories r readily available in the markets, easy to trade information on maintenance n modifications.......

    KRR-Kips150------Another brand replica of SP, looks quite the same in style n size, but in a cheaper production. Must take care cos parts harder to find than SP. Performance on par or else abit faster top end....

    TZM150-------Expensive n rare for its series, modernised TZR of the older days. Well acknowledged by many sports enthusiasts as the fastest stock bike compared to SP n KRR

    RS125 Replica/Extrema------- Built for the racing faithful, very big for its class, (might look a 2A bike for 1st timers). Def good at corners n stability cos if its built, but too bad its a 125

    Mito125------- Another italian classic, performance wise wil not lose to RS125, but take care of it. It will fail u if neglected

    NSR125Hornet------- the old NSR that is, the 125. Strangely bigger in size than its bro, the SP. Performance on par with the other 125s.....

    TZR-R125------ 'big brother' of the legendary TZR, another big 125 on the rd.....
