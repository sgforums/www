    hi there jOhO.. getting your class 2b license wil include going for some theory
    1.enrol urself in one of the driving schools in sg. enrolment fee ard $70++
    2. go for theory lessons conducted by the sch, sit for a RTT (riding theory test), apply for PDL.
    3. attend practicals at driving centre, you wil nid the PDL before u can go on the road for practicals.
    (step 2 and 3 can be done concurrently)
    4. complete and pass all practicals den wait for ur TP test date lor.
    5. Buy bike( max 200cc), insurance coverage etc.

    a yr after u passed ur class 2b, u may enrol for ur 2a if u intend. and likewise for class 2.

    or u may wan to check out the below webby.
    www.ecitizen.gov.sg
    click "transport"
    click "Learn to Drive/Ride"
    click "procedure"

    hope the above info wil be of help.
