    Hi there,

    Choices available are Shoei, Nolan, Arai, HJC and AGV. Shoei and Nolan can be found at Chong Aik, Arai at Chiap Lee, HJC at Motoworld. I am not too sure about AGV. Other brands are Suomy, Bell, Bieffe but I am not sure whether they are available in Singapore.

    I am using an Arai at the moment and it is very comfortable, but noisy. Many people overseas have good things to say about HJC helmets. Most important while choosing helmets is that they have to fit your head snugly and should be Snell and DOT approved.
