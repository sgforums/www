    To run a registered Club actually requires quite alot of hardwork.

    - There must be an official committe that handles all the legal & operation of the Club.
    - The Club will be carefully screened by the CID, who issues the Club license.
    - A formal AGM proceedings must be organised & the Club's formation successfully passed as the Club's 1st motion.
    - The discussion of benefits & discounts will have to be headed by a good sales negotiator to have any chance of success.
    - All Club activities must be archived & may be summoned by the Authorities when requested.
    - Major official Club gatherings & activities must be notified to the Authorities & permission/license approved for the event to take place.
    - etc.

    Do consider the amount of work & determination needed to make a Motorbike Club a possiblity. If taken up, do see it thru & don't give up. All the best!
