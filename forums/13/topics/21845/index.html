    francis:

    1. is your battery getting old already? don't think it's anything serious, if rectifier fails your bike would have died long ago liao... if it's a wet cell, try top up your battery water or get it replaced lor...

    2. most likely it's a failing brake light switch. i had the same problem for my front brake, sometimes have, sometimes don't have. it's a small electric component near the brake lever/pedal. try spray some contact cleaner first. if it still doesn't help, check the connections, if it's those clip-on types for you, perhaps it's loose. use pliers to crimp the connector, and put it back on. wanna change the whole thing also can.. for my planet, the switch costs $20, for your jap beauty, prolly less...
