    maintenance wise is the same for all bikes

    healthy bike=
    once a mth

    spark plug 10 bux
    gear oil 20 bux
    valve=DIY if u know
    if not around 10-20 bucks

    now on the sensitive part, no offense to all

    wif the milo kong, it is confirm a loser bike
    coz firstly the milo kong has got 3 restrictor plates blocking the air flow at the end can

    one more at the stomach, plus the cone ,

    but once u mod liao by removing all the restrictive elements inside the bloody milo

    once the valve open its really amazing wif the acceleration, some friends on my bike commented that they almost got thrown backwards

    this is becoz of the different setup of the rave valve and the other YPVS,KIPS and RC valves

    the other valves system on jap bikes opens according to the rev, but the rave valve opens fully once at a certain RPM

    but then no matter wad wif the milo kong, modded or not modded, ur top speed still will lose to all other jap bike,

    but i mus say the rave valve on aprilia is bery bery hard to maintain

    u must decarbonise the valve every mth or so
    and it is bery hard to remove when it is jam becoz the power valve is made of bery bery weak material coz its light

    but for japanese bike, the valve u can dun wash for 1-2 yrs also neber mind

    friend, i suggest u dun get an aprilia unless ur interest and money is there

    if ur the type who give up easiy or wanting to have a cheap to maintain bike, then get a jap bike coz italian parts r bery expensive
    and they dun last long as they r meant for tracks and not for road

    if given a choice, i think i will get a jap bike becoz i am a poor guy.....
