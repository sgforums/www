    it will not directly affect your fuel consumption but u will realise u seem to be getting less km's with your full tank on a bigger sprocket. reason is dat u will tend to be travelling at a higher RPM while cruising compared to small sprocket.

    for eg: small sprocket at 100km/h 6th gear RPM is 6000

    big sprocket at 100km/h 6th gear RPM is 7000.

    higher RPM = more revolutions on the engine components which take in the same amount of fuel whether small or big sprocket. therefore in the above example...theoretically speaking... u would only travel 6/7 of the normal full tank distance den empty liao.
