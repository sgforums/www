    The "WD" in WD-40 means Water Dispersant.

    Good to spray on parts that you want to prevent from rusting. However, WD-40 attracts dirt very easily. I use it on my radiator fins to prevent rusting but prefer to clean my chain using a rag soaked in kerosene(as most bike manuals state) and then oiling it with some motor oil or a chain lube.

    Some bikers say it is not recommended to spray WD-40 on O-ring chains because it will eat into the rubber seals. Whether this is true or not, I do not know as there is some dichotomy to the tests conducted so far.

    An alternative is to install the Scottoiler automatic chain lube system (http://www.scottoiler.com).
