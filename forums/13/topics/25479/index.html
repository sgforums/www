    liews.. the signal relay everyone is talking about controls the blinking of the signal lights. if your signal lights can blink, it's working. if the signal INDICATOR light on your instrumentation panel is not working, change the bulb first la... dey...

    but it does sound like a weak batt... get a new one, charge it up, ride high rev for a while. if it still dies... ooops.. other stuff... rectifier most likely. if your bike is sparking normally, it's a sign that the alternator is working. most likely your rectifier that's screwed and not providing the right charging current to your batt...
