    1st u wana keep ur 2B bike or sell it away? If sell, find a buyer for ur present bike 1st. (1st problem solved)

    2nd u have have ample downpayment, just go bikeshop choose the bike u wan then they will settle the rest for u. But if u're buying from private owner in full cash terms, bring u and him down to LTA and do the transfer.

    Make sure he brings:
    1.) IC
    2.) Log card
    3.) Insurance
    4.) Driving Licence

    U bring:
    1.) IC
    2.) Driving Licence

    U have to buy ur own insurance b4 u can ride the bike. Just walk in to any NTUC income outlets and check out the price for ur insurance and buy it on the spot. Not sure just ask around, its all over SG.
