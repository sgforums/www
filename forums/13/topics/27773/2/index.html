    Partly la. Bike no power is 1 thing la. But also must see the pillion weight and how the pillion sit all that. U can try pillion someone heavy, tell him to sit near u, then a bit futher from u. Cos if the pillion weight too behind of the back wheel, the rear wheel act as a pivot making the bike unbalance.

    I pillion Ah Tee, he nv use his knee to grip my hips. He sit near to me still quite ok. If he sit more behind, can feel the difference 1, my front wheel like lighter and difficult to control.

    I pillion Henry, he know how to sit, use his knee to grip my hips tightly. So the bike easier to control, except no power and longer braking distance due to the extra load la.
