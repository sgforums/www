    Wad i learnt is...

    1) On Ignition
    2) Kick to either 4th or 5th gear
    3) Clutch In
    4) Push the bike and run about 10 steps
    5) Throw Clutch
    6) Throttle a bit
    7) If engine starts, clutch in again...
