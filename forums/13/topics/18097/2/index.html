    Originally posted by roger:
    jackets are good during the night, when the weather is much cooler.

    during the day, it can be really uncomfortable. currently are there any comfortable jackets that protect as well as cool the body?
    dpds on how much you're willing to spend. there's a synthetic fabric called "Gore-Tex/Gortex" (not to be confused with female sanitary products), in the same league as "Kevlar". what Gortex does is that it allows ventilation in ONE direction only, from inside to out. heat dissipation and sweat from your body can easily be dispersed to the exterior environment, while rain etc cannot enter. it's like wearing a raincoat that's cooling and not stuffy. costs a bomb though, probably around the price range of $300 to $500. not sure where to get, but most shops that carry touring products/wear should have them. full suits are available. however, i'm not sure how strong the fabric is, or how well it protects against abrasion in a crash.
