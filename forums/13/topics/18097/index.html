    Err... actually, Nolan and AGV are not that expensive...
    These 2 Italian brands, although not very popular in Spore, are actually quite good. Maybe not as good as Shoei or Arai, but some models are worn by MotoGP riders. You can see the replicas at Nolan and AGV websites.

    I've owned 3 Nolan helmets so far, becos couldn't afford Shoei or Arai
    The 1st one saved my life a couple of times. In an accident, my face hit the side door of a van, got a nosebleed from the impact. Then at home, when I examined my helmet, I realized that there was a long stretch of car paint on my visor, and it didn't even crack! Phew... had I worn a cheap cheap helmet, my nose would have been flattened liao...

    Bought my last one, Nolan N81, at Looi's for $220. Dunno whether it's too expensive or not. They don't have many models there.
    Then found out that the authorized dealer in Spore is Chong Aik (Onan Road). They should have more selections, and some models should cost you less than $200.
