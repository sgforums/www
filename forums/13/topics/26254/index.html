    Basic maintence:
    1) If you wanna take care of your paintwork, wash weekly and wax it. Cover it with canvas if possible whenever you park the bike.
    2) Change engine oil every 5000 kils
    3) Clean your spark plug every 1000 kils, change every 3 months
    4) Warm your bike everytime you start it in the morning and try not to rely on the choke coz it will get dependent on it and it's not a good thing in future.

    This is wat i do with my bike lah, different pple got different way of handling.
