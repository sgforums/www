    thread starter problem was
    when about to wear the helmet

    sometimes we specky when wearing the helmet with spec on, the spec tends to misalign

    to counter this problem what i did was
    hold the straps and the d-ring
    use my thumbs to hold the strap and d ring to the lower part of the cushion
    use the rest of my fingers to hold the outer part of the helmet
    this is the position to prevent the straps and d-ring from moving about

    use the bottom back part of the cushion helmet and place it on your forehead just before your hair
    slide it in

    when it is nearing your ear,
    use a bit force from the your hands to expand the helmet

    OR

    when nearing your ear
    pull either one to make it expand
    follow by the other side

    once the ear part have been clear
    just pull down the helmet and strap it

    I use to encounter this problem. You just have to find the right helmet/specs.

    Generally, I hold on to the sides of the helmet and apply some outward pressure by pulling on the straps before sliding the helmet over my head.

    Seems to work well but really, there are many variables such as the shaope of your head, the helmet, your specs, etc.

    If you are wearing a full face helmet, then you just have to slide your specs in after you have worn your helmet. After a while you will find it almost 2nd nature and it would not bother you. This method might work for open-faced helmets too. So try it.

    Of course, the simplest way to avoid these difficulties is to wear contact lens.
