    How to tell mivec with body kit from original:

    1. evo has no sunroof. mivec has.
    2. evo has a rear window wiper. mivec doesn't.
    3. evo has protruding body around the tyres that are part of the body. mivec can be fitted but you can see the seam lines.
    4. evo has recaro seats. mivec doesn't.
    5. evo has a custom Momo steering wheel that is made only for evo. mivec steering is the mitsubishi one.

    I guess these are enough to indentify!
