    2T is a lubricant, has to be topped up about once every 2 months (depending on ur usage..this is for my case)
    What you must change is the engine oil, normally change at 1000km,3000km,6000km,9000km then after that change every 6000km or when u got too much $$
    Don't save on 2T and engine oil, buy better quality ones; ur engine will be in a better condition.
    Also, at abt 22000km should wash valve..
    Quite a lot of minor stuff here and there, you'll learn along the way; When you go motor shop, talk to the mechanics, they'll teach you some stuff as well.
