    Hey guys wif Phatom 150/200, maybe we can exchange some pointers here. I am really interested to boost the performance and stablity of it.

    Original components used on the bike itself is not that good. Want to know what parts to change for better performance, while maintaining stability and of coz, must be able to pass inspection.

    Here is what I found out (Phantom 200):

    Using pure V-Power, the pick-up and high speed performance are better, esp. at around 110km/h and above...

    Using pure Shell Advance Ultra 4, performance drop slightly at low speed, however, at high speed, it is much smoother in acceleration. Using a portion mix of Shell Advance Ultra 4 1.2l and GearUp (3 cap full), transmission is smoother, low speed acceleration is better.

    Out from factory, the choke seems to have problem, causing jerky acceleration and deceleration. Idling has to tune up, after adjusting the choke (air-intake) too.

    Anyone has a good workshop wif good mechanics who really know how to tune bike, no matter how low-class Phantom is? My workshop mechanics not that good, can oni do normal service. They noe nuts on real racing and fine-tuning bikes.
