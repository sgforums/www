    took mine at Ubi/CDC.

    First, enrol 1 yr membership around $30.
    On the spot u can book first lesson. In case u donno, riding lesson can onli book one at a time, eg, take lesson 1, fail, book lesson 1 again and take again an so on till pass then can book lesson 2 and so on and so on. Lessons inside circuit cost $20++.
    In the meantime very advisable go riding theory lessons. About 4 lessons all free. RIDING theory, so all must take including with other class licences. After u mark attendance for the 4 lessons go book riding theory test, probably will get few weeks after booking. Reason why must take theory concurrently is u can apply PDL to go road lessons if and only if you complete all circuit lessons AND pass riding theory. Means if u happily take riding lessons in circuit but bochap theory lessons u say 'aiyah take later lah' u will find when u complete circuit lessons u will be stuck coz cannot advance to road lessons, worse still if u fail theory test then book again few weeks later, so for weeks u cannot ride waste time.
    Once complete all road lessons can book tp. at ubi, standard wait 2 months. i took around end of my ns, went lessons twice a week took me me 6 months before first time i book tp. i passed only at third attempt 4 1/2 months later, total took me 10++ months. With respect to number of lessons taken and number of attempts at tp, i say i'm average. if u ask me whether can get 2b in 4 months, i say depend in the 4 months how often u plan to go for lessons and most important how many times u take tp. if u take lessons like mad and pass first time tp i say can

    joho ...

    1st : you have to enrol yourself to UBI driving centre which is nearest to you

    2nd : you will have to go for the theory lesson conducted by the driving centre before you are allow to book for the RIDING THEORY TEST (RTT)

    3rd : after you have finish all the theory lesson then you can book your RTT ...

    *note : before you have finish the theory lessons you can take the practical lessons which were conducted in the circuit of the driving centre ...

    4th : only when you pass your RTT ... then you can apply for your Provisional Driving License (PDL)

    *PDL = a license for you to drive/ride on PUBLIC road without a Qualified Driving License(QDL)

    5th : in a driving centre you will have to follow the practicals subject by subject till you finish all subjects

    *subject = E-brake is a subject ... Plank course is a subject ... etc etc

    6th : after all lessons taken ... then you will have to take a test conducted by the driving centre (Final Evaluation Test)

    7th : upon passing the FET then you can book for your Traffic Police Test ...

    I do not know how many lesson does ubi have to take ... cos i took my license at BBDC ... sorry i cant help you on that ...

    Hope what i said above do you some help ...
