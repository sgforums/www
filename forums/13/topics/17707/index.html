    That depends on the age of the bike. If less than 5 years old cos the accident statistics is suppose to increase with age.

    But do consider. First party is very ex.
    How ex your insurance is depends on the following:

    1.) Age: >21 years cheaper
    2.) Riding experience/ Driving experience
    3.) Any demerit pts/ court conviction in the pas THREE years.( not one year as I originally thought)
    4.) insurance company.
    5.) First/2nd /3rd party
    6.) Any rider clause
    7.) any sub riders
    8.) Any other usage of bike ( e.g attach side-cars) , for goods transport etc.)

    I believe only NTUC has first party. Correct me if i am wrong.
