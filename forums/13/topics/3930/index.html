    All you need to do is to look up the yellow pages for the numbers of whichever branch that is closest to you, then call them and ask if there are any vacancies.
    That's what i did. I just started oso.

    It's true that there are alot of Malays. But most of them are actually quite friendly. Once you get to know them, it'll be alright.

    One thing you gotta know is that...besides sending orders, you oso need to do anything under the sun. More like "Bangala". Also, the bikes suck big time...need to get used to and control.

    The pay is like this: $4.20/hr. $0.60 per order during wkdays, $1 during wkends. Not alot, for me noe choice, since can't find other jobs.

    No NSF allowed if I'm not wrong. They do not accept your smart card.

    In conclusion, yellow pages is your answer.

    hihi.. u can go to the following outlets to ask for vancancies for delievery rider. i am from pizzahut of coz i will strongly recommond you to the outlets which the management there is better. if you noe those road area at bedok, siglap, tanjong katong and tampines new town, you can go to bedok north blk 21 for interview. siglap is located oppsite siglap shopping centre. tanjong katong is located beside a Bp petrol station near to tanjong katong Girl sch. as for tampines new town, its located a blk 139 tampines st ll near the market. you can even try goin to hougang heartmall or hougang mall for an interview.. they seldom advertise in the paper. normally people juz walk in and ask..

    per hr is $4.20-$4.50, each order you go for weekdae btw 11am-2pm is $1 per trip and after 2pm for weekdae is $0.60 per trip. weekends and public holidaes $1 per order from 11am-11pm. your total pay will then deduct 20% of the CPF. as for public holidaes, you will be pay 1.5 hrs extra if you work more than 8hrs a day and tat 1.5 hrs is consider your ot hrs.. so you can actuali earn up to $600-800 a mth if you work ot every sat and sun..

    gd luck.......
