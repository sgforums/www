    Hi Joel, ok i am also no $$ person, so i use cheap stuff for my bike. U must think lor, we TZR is 10 year old bike, semi synthetic stuffs are good anough lah.

    Engine oil i normally use normal one i can find lor, i change it only when i think my changing of gears are very very rough... which is about 2-3 months.

    Plugs i use those $2.50 can oredi. NGK one.

    2T i use Esso Phase Green one, even cheaper than your Silver bottle one, hehe

    Your carb please wash it every 6 months. Coz TZR fuel tank, it will enter water on rainy days. That time i never service carb whole year, that day cannot start, carb and fuel tank stucked with too much rain water...

    Always bring an extra spark plug, some tools, alan keys, small spanner and screwdriver, just in case lah, u never know when it burns out lor.

    For petrol wise, ours is old bike, so using Octane 92 petrol will be better. Also can use 95 and above fuel, just depends on what u like lor.

    I ITE time everyday use Shell XO. But now i everyday use Shell Fushion 92. hehe

    And also pls check your radiator everyday. Open up and see if inside got water. TZR have a common problem, gets overheated easily.

    hmm rest i oso dono, if u have problem just gimme a call.
