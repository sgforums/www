    just to clear it up.

    motor up is NOT an engine oil or a fuel additive.

    it is poured into the engine oil compartment where it will bond to the metal surface of the internal engine components, coating them with rather lasting layer of film dat reduces friction between surfaces, thus it runs smoother and less noise is produced, also prolonging engine life.

    yes it does work. but gradually the film is worn out a few months later. also it doesn work as well on 2 strokes bcos the 2 stroke piston is not lub by the engine oil but rather the 2t.
