    When shifting down gears, let's say from 5 to 3, in order to prevent the bike from jerking and to engage the lower gear smoothly, before letting out the clutch to engage 3rd gear ( the bike is in neutral ), open the throttle a little to increase the engine rpms to match the bike speed to that of the lower gear, and then smoothly release the clutch.

    I use this method whenever I shift down even if I shift down one gear at a time and it works for me.
