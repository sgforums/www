  • Money &Power
    22 Dec 99, 01:07
    No doubt, for the amount of money you pay for an M5, it is an engineering masterpiece.

    But I have to agree with Dakar and alex that the maintenance and repair costs are just too excessive. for an M5. Unless you have tons of money to throw around, I, personally, would not recommend an M5. Cars of this type is very fussy when it comes to maintenance and repair.

    Moreover, it is very unlikely that you will speed up to 250km/h in an M5 on any part of the world roads with exception of the Autobahn.

    If you are a BMW lover, I would strongly suggest a 323i or 323Ci. It gives the best value for money. It runs great and it defnitely would not dissapoint you in terms of performance, ride, handling, practicality and safety. Although its maintenence cost is still higher than most ordinary cars, it still cost much much less than an M5 to run.

    Regards!
