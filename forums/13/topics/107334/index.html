Full faced versus open faced helmets.

  • Old Bird
    14 Dec 04, 23:42
    I have been riding motorcycles for many many years now back even in those days when full face helmets were banned.

    I have seen many bike accidents and also had my fair share of them too. I had friends who lost all their front teeth in accidents or had their chin smashed and deformed thereafter when they hit the road face down in an accident. Nobody rides a motorcycle facing backwards.

    I have even been fined by the TP for wearing a full face helmet during those days when it was banned. And I kept on wearing it coz I value my life more than the stupid fine.

    What amazes me is why today I still see people wearing open faced helmets when full face helmets are easily available and totally legal!! I shiver at the thought of what will happen to their pretty face and teeth in the event of an accident.

    Like I said, I have been involved in 2 major accidents before and thanks to the full face helmet I wore, I can still smile at people today. If you can see the cracked and scraped chin guard fo the 2 helmets... you will understand why I wear full face helmets all the time.

    Never never think that an accident won't happen to you. You may super safe in the way you ride but there are alot of dangerous people driving on the roads especially taxi drivers. All it takes is for them to make a mistake and you pay for it.

    We as bikers, should be attired in a manner that would afford the best protection in the unfortunate event of an accident.

    Some people think it is cool to wear an open faced helmet, especially those Harley wannabe Phantom riders. Just think how cool you look with your teeth all smashed in and mouth and lips torn and all bloodied up.

    Ride smart. Ride safe.
