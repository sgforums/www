    Yup, i used own recipe. Here it is, based on 2 pax serving. Enjoy


    Ingredients:
    1/2 tsp chopped garlic & chopped shallots
    1 tsp ground chilipadi paste (can use the normal cilip paste but i prefer cilipaidi cos its HOT)
    c-o-c-k-l-e-s / fishcake(on pref)
    1 egg
    1 tbsp oyster sauce ,1 tbsp dark sauce, 1 tbsp light soy sauce
    Chives , cut roughly
    2 bunches of Kway Teow (fresh)

    ____
    Heat about 5 tbsp oil, then when oil is very hot, add garlic, shallots, cilipaste.

    Make sure the chili is really cooked, before putting in the fishcake, chives, noodles. Stir.

    Add the oyster, dark, light sauces. Stir, taste and adjust.

    Lastly, add the egg to the mix.

    ENJOY!
