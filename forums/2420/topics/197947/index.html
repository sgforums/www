    i've tried peter pan, skippy, goober, peanut butter and co., snickers, milkyway. all of them were tried in usa, and i have to say, that skippy has the nicest peanut butter of all in terms of just plain peanut butter.

    after doing alot of research on nutrition and brands, skippy has turned out to be the one that is most healthy in terms of caloric density and nutrients provided. peanut oil is actually considered a healthy oil, in fact oil from nuts are considered the healthy oil together with fish, flaxseed, coconut, olive etc..

    skippy is considered the higher end in the peanut butter industry, and it certainly does show in its pricings at stores.

    one thing that skippy does not offer is certainly the variety, while they have reduced fat by 30% type of peanut butter, they do not provide peanut butter with honey (peter pan), or peanut butter with jelly (goober), peanut butter with chocolate syrup (snickers, milkyway, goober).

    the one that wins in variety and has unique ingredients in its peanut butter.. would be the ones from peanut butter and co. they provide peanut butter with cinnamon and raisins, peanut butter in dark chocolate, infused peanut butter with white chocolate chips.
