    Dear Sir/Madam,

    CRIME ALERT – POLICE NOTICE OF MAS SELAMAT BIN KASTARI

    You would probably have known by now that Singapore’s Jemaah Islamiyah (JI) leader, Mas Selamat bin Kastari, has escaped from Whitley Road detention centre on 27 Feb 2008 and is currently at large.

    2 A massive manhunt involving the local authorities such as police, military and Special Forces is currently underway to track him down.

    3. Mas Selamat is not known to have escaped with the help of any accomplice. While on the run, he is likely to attempt to conceal his identity and could resort to stealing items like clothes, food, and even vehicles such as delivery trucks, motorcycles, cars or bicycles in his desperation to leave the country. For more suspicious indicators, please refer to Annex A. In the event that you discover missing items, as listed in Annex A, during this period, please call 999 immediately.

    4. While Police is keeping close tabs on the development of the situation, it is pertinent that the public at large remain vigilant and keep an active lookout for the fugitive. We also strongly urge you to secure and lock your vehicles when you go about your daily activities, even if it is a short period of time.

    5 Enclosed is a poster of the fugitive for your reference. If you have any information on the fugitive or wish to report any suspicious persons fitting the poster descriptions, please call 999 immediately.

    Yours sincerely

    EVON NG

    ASSISTANT DIRECTOR

    COMMUNITY INVOLVEMENT DIV

    OPERATIONS DEPARTMENT

    SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE

    CRIME PREVENTION – A SHARED RESPONSIBILITY

     

     

    Annex A

    How SgForums members can help the Police

    Call 999 if you see any of these suspicious behavior or activities:

    What to look out for

    Mas Selamat may alter his looks to avoid detection

    Other than the photos already released to the press, keep a lookout for these signs:

    a) Ill fitting clothes

    b) Avoids eye contact

    d) Observable limp on left leg when running or walking briskly

    e) Attempts to conceal or disguise appearance, such as wearing of cap, head-dress or dark glasses.

    Mas Selamat needs food when hiding, and may resort to stealing money and food. He may also change his appearance by stealing clothes.

    He may steal transportation means to keep moving to avoid detection or escape from the country.

    Keep a lookout for these theft cases:

    a) Theft of food

    b) Theft of money

    c) Theft of clothing, including from foreign workers dormitory or unattended laundry lines

    d) Theft of transportation means

    · Bicycle

    · Motor-cycle

    · Motor-car

    · Boats

    (MSK is a trained vehicle mechanic and knows how to operate motorized boats.)

    Police believes Mas Selamat is likely to be more active at night.

    If you have more information to share with the Police, you can call 999 or email us at spf_police_information@spf.gov.sg. Your identities will be kept strictly confidential.
