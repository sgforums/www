"Supreme Court" Phone Scam

  • TehJarVu
    11 Apr 08, 11:21

    I got a call this morning, it's a recorded message that kept saying,

    "Important announcement from the Supreme Court, Important announcement from the Supreme Court, for more information, dial 109.

    Important announcement from the Supreme Court, Important announcement from the Supreme Court, for more information, dial 109...."

    so i dialed 109, and a woman who was obviously not a Singaporean answered and said that it is the Supreme Court  and she asked me,

    "WHAT IS YOUR NAME"

    alert alert
    you call me and don't know my name?

    She said when she has my name then she can check the computer system what it is about

    So I told her I am going to call the police.

    I did and the police confirmed that it is a scam, don't be alarmed and avoid tallking to these people and asked me to lodge a report at any police station.

    So do be careful and tell the old folks in the family who are alone at home about this in case they got scammed.
