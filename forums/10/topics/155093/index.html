    Concepts, assumptions and presumptuousness have marked our leadership system for a long time.

    When leaders started over-hyping the risk of welfarism they already plnated seed of a high-cost over-charged system of government as department after departmentment started to over-charge to full market values. They even charge citizens full land costs when the lands belong to them being paid by their taxes. So assumptions can be dangerous and cause many problems - problems of going to the extreme and neglecting other aspects of corporate governance.

    Every policy has its pluses and minuses. about welfarism already caused us the extremely distressing high-cost problem.

    Assumptions and presumptuousness about the goodness of foreign investments and foreign talents have their munuses. The push to go for foreign investments have already caused us a weaken domestic sector of the economy.

    Our SMEs which depend on services are crushed by all the foreigners competing against them for the same small pie. Mediacorps is a clear example of what can happen when there are too small pie to share among too many players. Yet for the SMEs, the government does not care whether they survive or not. They not only open the doors fully to the foreigners to compete against them the government also itself competes agsinst them.

    So don't let all the assumptions about our being a top-class country, fool us the citizens. We are not world class. We are going to lose out in real competitiveness and practical technology start-ups because of neglect in other areas in failing to build real capabilities.

    Let our leaders learn real capabilities not all the policy assumptions. The past is full of assumptions which did not work. Our loss of economic competitiveness is due to brain drains of real talents by the thousands while government is busy promoting elitism or talents of the academic kind. It is time to get real and go for real entrepreneurship and abilities.

    Stop blowing the trumpet, and let citizens who are trying hard to start business survive and only then will we have more capabilities and talents of the practical kind. By persisting in arrogance, we are going to suffer in many other ways.
