    A man worked hard, found a good wife, and had 2 sons. After many years of sheer hardwork and a lot of sacrifices, he grew successful and rich, and could provide security and wealth for his family.

    His son Tom soon grew up, educated and well taken care of.

    One day he saw sports car, heard his friends raved about it, and he wanted one as well. He went to his father and ask for it, as he was still studying and could never afford one on his own.

    His father, who loved his child, and feared for his safety, being only 18years old, refused. Tom turned petulant and rebellious. He accused his father for being a miser, not loving him, and plenty of other hurtful things. His father, hurt, but turned to stone and continued to refuse such wilful belligrancy even after explaining his reasons. The friction grew, and Tom never did reconcile with his father.

    The second son Micheal, was much responsible. When he turned 18, he too wanted a sports car. But he knew and understood his father's concerns for a son's safety - years of grooming and hard work, surely he doesn't want the son to be injured or incapicitated in an accident.

    So he did his best to prove to his father that he is a responsible 18 year old. He took his driving licence, clean his father's car, and undertook to drive his dad's car for short nearby errands at the Mamashop to buy bread, sent his mother for medical check-ups. In time, the father grew to trust him, and Micheal himself learn about responsibilities as well.

    Within a year, his father bought him a better european sports model on his own initiative, to the delight of a by then 19 year old NS man.

     

    There is more to politics than one can possibly gained from books or exported models. Singapore politics are very much different and unique. For the opposition and the politically bitter ones who chosed to live in rose-tinted delusions, they must wake up to reality. To get a credible opposition party into the political foreground, one must understand MM Lee himself.

    MM Lee sacrificed much and fought titanic battles to gain our independence, which was a mistake served on a plate. Even though our previous generation played a huge part in its eventual success of a nation of today, no one can deny it was policies and enforcements 'forced down' by MM Lee and his team on a largely uneducated population that do not understand what 'freedom' really entails, even as some present generation do no equally understand.

    He is no dictator, nor seek to have pedestal made after him, which infact, he banned such proposals. All he wanted, like a father, is the safety and well being of the nation in good hands.

    Thus, if one wants a credible opposition in government, then the opposition must show they are better than the ones MM Lee had groomed, equally as responsible, capable, and one whom will not take the populist path that will ultimately lead to doom. Someone who is caring and not afraid to to make hard and hated decisions that may hurt some, but will ensure the survivability of million others.

    Behave like a responsible son, and not the wilful child, and change may be on the way. The best way to prove it is to manage the town council well, with surpluses and smiling constituents.

    As usual, only my 2 cents worth.
