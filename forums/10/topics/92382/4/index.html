    To:Gregorsamsa,

    What are the rationale for these 7 points of true democracy?

    Why 1 man 1 vote? Why not 1 man two votes for some and 1 man 1 vote for the rest? Why give a vote to those who may be inept to vote correctly or not so well informed?

    Why protect citizens of basic rights of freedom of speech or association?

    Why protect citizens from prosecutions on account of his speaking up or participating in political or democratic processes?

    Why set standards for government or executives as they are elected by the people to do their works of governance?

    Why take part in governance when the elected representatives are supposed to be given the powers by the people to carry out these duties ?

    These are all good questions that must be answered if the 7 points of true democracy are to be adopted by Singapore to provide a basis or guidelines to answer to our particular political system - presently essentially a mixure of socialism, meritocracy, and leaderships by the ablest or presumed ablest.

    I shall now try to answer these queries as follows:-

    (1) 1 MAN 1 VOTE

    There was some suggestions in the past by SM Lee to grant 1 man 2 votes to the elderly citizens who presumably are more mature and could vote more responsibly to prevent a freak election result by the presumably less mature younger voters.

    This suggestion was not pursued for obvious reasons. One reason could be that what is mature or less mature is not determined by age or demography. It is too subjective. Why tinker with 1 man 1 vote for something less reliable. At least 1 man 1 vote does not need explanations as to who is more responsible or not responsible in voting. Will young people agree that they are less responsible.

    So you see, Singapore should not try to tinker with established democratic system that has evolved over the ages based on 1 man 1 vote unless there are compelling reason to do so.

    It is similarly difficult or problematic to define aptness or what is more informed or less informed.
