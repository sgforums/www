    When a Singaporean reaches 40 years old, he should realise that his shelf life in the workforce is only about 10 years, upon discovering that he is considered "obsolete" at that age Â— even after having acquired a Masters' degree at 30.

    The biggest issue may be the refusal to acknowledge that the problems exist Â— let alone working on solving them.
