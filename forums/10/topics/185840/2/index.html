    We come into the structure of any normal companies (discarded those non conformed) .
    Assume 85 % will be likely to be formed by non executives
    Assume 10 % lower and middle management
    Assume 5% top management.

    I think will be very close, anyway this is only an practicable example.

    The non executives and few lower management will be likely to be the ground peoples doing most of the daily work. (can be long hours, say hotel will be 8 - 12 hours depending). Hence, if you see a hotel employee with face "black black". that does not say that she is non performer, maybe she just overworked and not enough rest.

    While a top management that smile to you is a performer, maybe his job is just to smile. (who knows)

    Middle management will likely to be involved in planning with top management running the company.

    Look at my earlier posting, you will noticed that I never included the top management inside this performance bonus scheme.

    Saying a company with this kind of system (typical 6 month bonus type)

    Outstanding - 5% - 6 months
    Very Good - 15% - 4 months
    Good - 50% - 2 months
    Satisfactory - 30% - 1 month

    The executive and non executive can be separated. (Look fair to all, also can mix) Remember to exclude the top management.

    The middle exec to the lowest of non executives can be accessed by their supervisors and appraised accordingly (hopefully fairness)..

    The top management will just simply have to follow the lowest 30% of the system. Instead of the current practise of giving themselves high performance bonus themselve.

    Why, it's no the fault of those that falled inside the satisfactory performance level. It can be stated that the top management had failed to guide their staff's performance to improve only!! If their lowest performance staff had improved together with company's financial performance, the amount payable can be adjusted up for them. Wouldn't you think this is a better and fairer system for the employee..
