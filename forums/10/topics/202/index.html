    To enter OCS there are a couple of criteria;
    1) education - either poly, A level (minimum)or degree holder as a prerequisite. Anything *lower*, sorry.

    2) fitness - PES A and B only. anything else *sorry* and not just passing your IPPT. you need to score well.

    3) peer appraisal - if your peers think you're shit, then the officers will be very hesitent about recommending you to OCS; cos chances are that if you can't even get along with your peers - you'd be a shit leader.

    4) PSC Scholar - unfair as it may sound these b*stards worm their way into OCS purely cos they have some O(^*%*( scholarship. Many of these people are lousy soldiers, but hey, this is S'pore. Got paper qualifications can already. MINDEFwill close both eyes.

    5) If your father is bigshot and plays golf with LKY or GCT - duh.

    6) BMT performance - usually the platoon best 5-6 will get it. Or if you go to SISPEC, don't fret, if you do well, and come int within the top 10% (i could be wrong) you also get recommended to join OCS

    Hope that helps
