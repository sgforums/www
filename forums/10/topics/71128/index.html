To get the license for your Windows, you gotta pay $40, which going by Sg standards will soon have to be paid only via EZ-link. Even for the contractors, which on normal occasions, would be happily laughing their way to the bank as this guarantees them fixed income, is curling their mole hair and wondering, walan eh, the taiji skills of HDB is really quite solid ... they cover their ass, than who cover MY ass.

Worse, this SPs must be licensed and paid for yearly and that these SPs are licensed by another business entity, Netscape, so that if your Windows crash, its Netscape's fault. The business logic here is kooky to say the fucking least.
