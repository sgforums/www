    In short and in SG context, all ministers and office bearers 'resign' from office for the duration of elections. Even the PM is technically NOT running the country during this period.

    Should any of their constituents be contested and lost to opposition parties, that person can no longer hold office.

    In short, if you're not an MP, you cannot qualify as a minister.
