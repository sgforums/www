Suggested agenda for future Policy-Alternative Forum

  • robertteh
    25 Apr 08, 09:44

    If PM Lee really wants to get Singaporeans to cooperate with the government, it is suggested he implement the following:-

    (1) Get rid of the selfishness of the ruling party e.g. If government does not hand out do not over-tax or over-charge the citizens.
    (2) Stop all the calculative scheming and hypocrisy e.g. GRC and GLC are not helping the country or people.
    (3) Be humble and serve the people and regard leaders as people given the opportunities to serve the people not as idols to be worshipped as great talents.
    (4) Practise openness, accountability and transparency e.g. Temasek and GIC investing people's monies overseas to pass the test of creating jobs and helping retirements or lowering of costs of living and not strategic glory seeking acquisitions on gut feel of any one in charge or Ms. Ho Ching whose decisions were seldom right any way thus far as seen in Shin Corp etc.
    (5) Stop all the profiteering double charges on lands or passing of more costs down to citizens through scheming of GLCs.
    (6) Tell the truths about hidden profits and surpluses in annual budgets on land sale and GLCs etc before passing more burdens or taxes or GST.
    (7) Stop governing the country by means of legalistic wrangling e.g. passing of laws to get its way against objections of negative impacts on people ignoring real concerns and issues like increasing their own salaries and bonuses without accountability or with falsified benchmarking
