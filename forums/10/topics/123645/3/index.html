    The issue of whether Taiwan is part of China is one of sovereignty and territorial integrity. No government will easily relinquish their basic national interest, when push comes to shove on this matter. Historically, there is little argument that Taiwan did belong to China over a long period of time and most nations have accepted that Taiwan is part of China.

    For the people of PRC, they believe Taiwan belongs to them. However, for some Taiwanese, (especially those from the earlier generation from the mainland) they believe that PRC belongs to them! If over a period of time when Taiwan evolves its own way of life, then the argument based on historical sovereignty weakens. That is one of the reason why it is in PRC's interest to integrate the economy and culture of Taiwan as closely as possible with theirs in order to forestall eventual separation.

    The issue of reunification or otherwise is a separate matter which IMO should be decided by both the peoples of PRC and Taiwan and should not be forced in order to avoid bloodshed and widespread disruption. The best scenario to emerge would be peaceful reunification or separation by mutual consent.

    Reunification could be brought about when the economic, cultural and political way of life of both the PRC and Taiwan becomes more similar. For their economy and culture to become more similar is perhaps just a matter of time as more people travel and live between the two lands. It is much more difficult for their political systems to become more similar. This can only happen if and when PRC makes deep reforms and democratise their political systems. Will this happen and if so, when? Probably yes. As more and more of their citizens improves themselves economically and become more vocal about their rights, it will be necessary for their government to take account of their views. When that happens, reunification might then make good sense.

    From the pov of the US, Japan and S Korea, reunification might not be in their national interest, but that is a separate story.
