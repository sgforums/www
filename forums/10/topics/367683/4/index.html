    How does a politician stay in power for 50 years ?

    With the idealistic and lofty objective of a - PAP self-renewal plan, it enabled LKY to stay in power, while his colleagues in the Old Guard were retired, or simply made irrelevant and moved out of office by re-drawing the electoral boundary lines that erased the constituencies. 

    Many in the PAP old guard had resented being moved out so early in their political career and made redundant in retirement.

    Some of the old guards like Dr Goh Keng Swee was allowed to act as a consultant adviser to the Communist PRC Government, while Lim Kim San was elevated to be a President Council advisor.

    To prevent Toh Chin Chye from becoming an independent candidate, he was made a Deputy Prime Minister before being eased out of office.

    These old guards were the only PAP members who were prepared to face down the ideas of LKY, and have a good debate on policies before these were tabled for parliamentary debates.

    With the passing of the Old Guards, and after more then 20 years since Goh Chok Tong's taking over from the political reins, it seems that LKY still have ambitions to continue to be in the Cabinet in one way or another - to continue "guiding" the reins.
