    I feel that there are 2 types of gangsters. The smart ones, and the stupid ones. But unfortunately, gangsters here seems to fall predominantly in the latter group.

    the smart gangsters create illegal gambling syndicates, legal investments, legal and illegal moneylending.

    but the dumb ones just only know how to take orders from the smart ones, without thinking of the consequences. e.g. the back alley gang fights, pimps, drug trafficker, small-time loanshark. These pawns are often the ones getting caught, and the ones who paid for their crimes, while their smart gang chiefs live rich off these disposable pawns.

    and often, these pawns are lowly educated people, or people who are lazy to make a honest living, or worse, a combination of both.

    often, you can read of school-dropouts being arrested for working as runners for loansharks, or people who want to make easy money. $50 to pour paint at a debtor's house, as oppose to working 8hrs distributing flyers (also $50).

    this group of people are the scourge of society, not contributing, and yet making life hard for others.

    to cut down on the appeal of gangsterism, it is necessary to impose very harsh penalties, to disuade the potential group of people from even having the thought of joining a gang.

    one case study we can use is the negligible number of singaporean drug mules.

    Since singapore imposed a mandatory death sentence for those caught of trafficking drugs, we rarely see singaporean (even drug addicts), risking their life to bring in drugs. Most of the traffickers caught are foreigners.

    following this train of thought, if we impose very harsh penalties on gang-joining, people who reconsider the risk, and we would see a decrease in gang-related activities, since the number of people involved in the gang circle would be smaller.
