    Most people do not share in the rich of life because they live in constant fear of this or that or something else. This is a strange fact but it is true. Most people don't intentionally want to fail yet we fail to suceed when we permit a positive mind to become negated through fear thoughts, and thoughts of doubt and worry.

    It is impossible to achieve success in life while harboring in the mind thoughts of failure. To become successful we must think success thoughts. To achieve success we cannot permit a positive mind to be pinched, narrowed and hampered by any thoughts of fear and worry.

    Why do people fear? If people only knew the root-source of this tragic & often fatal eveil, the would forever keep the door to it closed. They would shut it out of their lives forever. To fear is sheer, utter nonsense. To fear is animalistic. Fear is am expression of a faithless soul.

    The root-source of fear is faithlessness. The strangers on the shores of life who know not GOD, who know not self, are the fearful ones.

    To say we believe is not enough. We must become the part or else we are but actors upon the stage of life. Most people do not believe what they profess. Consequently, they are literally starving on the very shores of the stream of life which carries infinite supply.
