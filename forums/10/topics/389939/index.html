"GOVERNMENT TAIJI"

  • oic
    3 Feb 10, 15:07

    My dear pure blooded Sporeans, do you notice in the past few days how the government have emphasized foreign workers as if they are the ones that are causing the unhappiness of foreigners in Spore. Nothing has been said abt the 533,000 PRs who have got into this beautiful island of ours so easily.  Some of them have even had the audacity to go back home to their home countries to put on their malitia uniforms and take part in their National Day parade, carrying, of cos their machine guns. And after that, proclaimed how much they love their motherland and promising not to come back to Spore.

    So, my dear Govt, I think you got the whole point of foreigners in Spore wrong.  Its not  the work permit holders that get us upset, it is the PERMANENT RESIDENTS and NEW CITIZENS tat you so blatantly allow to come into Spore that make us upset.

    Another example of Government taiji - the current trial of the Rojak seller in Geylang Serai Market. Do you notice, that the pathetic and lowly rojak seller is being punished by those, while those in the NEA, from the Minister to the health inspectors that was suppose to do the check and inspections regularly, have not been called to account for themselves.

    I suppose all "these people" are blameless .
