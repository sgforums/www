    SINGAPORE: Some lower income families were in for a treat on Saturday. About 450 members from lower income families from the Pasir Ris-Punggol area received gift bags containing essential items worth S$40.

    Giving the bags out at the Singapore Flyer were Deputy Prime Minister and Defence Minister Teo Chee Hean and grassroots leaders.

    The Singapore Association of the Visually Handicapped gave massages and Institute of Technical Education (or ITE) students provided manicure services at the event.

    Organised by the People's Association Youth Movement, the event was designed to allow youth volunteers to connect with the community. - CNA/vm
