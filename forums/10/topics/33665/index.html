    i personally feels that more poly student can be accepted into the local uni... provided that their results in poly as well as in their O levels are good lah...

    But then the problem lies in the absence of a standardized entrance examinations....

    its is difficult if not unjust to set a percentage for poly student that will be permitted to join local U... each individuals should be given an opportunity to go to the local U on their own merits against others.... under a meritocracy system...

    BUT therein lies a problem... the courses taken by A level student is different from poly student... and because of this bi-track system... its difficult to set a generally acceptable standdardized entrance examinations... that would satisfy both groups...

    My suggestion is that... ALL poly student should be given an opportunity to take an entrance examination at the local U, in their respective faculty of studies of choice......

    if they make the grade.. they should be permitted to enter local U...and the local U should expand a particular faculty if need to meet the demand...

    this way... there is no complaint on the existance of preferences for A level student or poly student.. each entered university on their own merit... they pass.. they enter.. they dont pass... dont bother blaming others...

    alternatively... we can remove the bi-track system and go for a mono track version...

    all O-level student can only proceed to A level... no more poly...

    all poly campus are combined and reformed to become singapore's number 4 technology univerisity handing out degrees and doctorates.... instead of diploma.... all current diploma holders can convert the diploma to degree after passing a A level equivalent examination...

    Foreign uni... some good ... some bad.. some okay okay... but the problem is worst... how to measure their credibility??

    BUT i personally feels that... as long as you went oversea and study full time in a respectable university of global standing.. your degree should be just as acceptable to a local Uni degree....

    BUT if you take those correspondence, part time, fast track, buy over the counter degree.... i think... no need for me to say... you also paiseh to call yourself a degree holder....
