    If we were to use Temasek Holdings as a benchmark, the PAP- controlled town councils have in fact put in a credible performance!

    For Temasek Holdings, the amount of losses- unrealised- in absolute terms is in billions! While the amount of losses- unrealised- in absolute terms is more than 1%!

    The combined losses of the PAP- controlled town councils is only enough for 16 firework shows- assuming S$1m per show, while the losses- unrealised- by Temasek Holdings will be enough for quite a few years!

    When Temasek Holdings lose such a big amount of money, Singaporeans didn't 'cry father, cry mother' like hell, but when the PAP- controlled town councils lose S$16m, Singaporeans instantly 'cry father, cry mother' like hell!

    It is as if there are a lot of vacancies for 'filial sons' and 'filial daughters'!

    If we were to use the opposition- controlled town councils as a benchmark, then obviously, we are betting on John McCain- instead of Barack Obama- becoming the US president!
