    SOA is the Armour version is SISPEC/OCS. It's basically only a school.
    It's where you go for the Armour conversion course to earn the black beret.

    Graduates of SOA with be posted to an Armor Unit(SARs)

    An Armor Unit consist of two types of Armour formation:
    The Armor Infantry AI (not IA) and the Tanks.

    AI uses APCs like M113/Bionix. It's bascally infantry (7 man section) with a APC, driver and Vehicle Commander. They use armor assaults...just like infantry except that they've got more fire power and the APC to back them up.

    Tank are AMX-13/SM1 tanks. It acts as fire support for AI. Consist of the driver, gunner and Vehicle Comm.

    In SOA, you either become an AI of tankee trainee. So after you post out to your unit, you will be either AI or tankee in that unit depending on which vocation you'll be trained on.

    Outstanding trainee might stay back in SOA as instructors.

    SARs are active/NS units. They are the one that will fight in the war.

    All this, SOA, SARs are all part of a bigger organization call the ARMOR FORMATION.

    Depending whether you are a OCT or Specialist, SOA is hell......not to scared you but it's real HELL......worst than suspec/OCS.....but it's all to train the guys to be alert....armor is no joke and are quite accident prone...But it's only after you get to your SAR then you can kiss your trainee days goodbye!

    Once Armor, Always Armor........Welcome to the family!
