    I was reading with amusement today's article in the STOnline. What would happen if PAP went bad. Thats a bit of a thought. Well in response, that is the reason for a multi party government. By vesting control in a single dominant party, there is the danger of nepotism happening. Although there might be percieved efficiencies to be gained from a single party rule, those efficiencies do not last. They degrade over time. ALthough credit has to be given for the ruling party's effort in raising the economic wealth of the state, it is time that they understand that change is a neccessarily aspect of that process.

    I am neither for or against the ruling party but am more interested in the development of the singaporean society. I am interested in how the country can participate as a member of the global society cyet still operating under its present governance system.
