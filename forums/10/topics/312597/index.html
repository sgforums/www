    I notice that the term "chauvinism" is being misused here sometimes.
    To my understanding, the meaning of chauvinism is being blindly and aggressively proud of own group of identity be it country, race or gender.

    For example, country A fighting with country B. Citizens of country A support A even if A is wrong.
    This although is chauvinism but I am less resistance to it because a portion of this chauvinism is due to patriotism.
    It is like a son who side his mother even when his mother is at fault. He is wrong but at least he is filial enough.

    There is another type which is worse than chauvinism. Imagine a son so scared that people might call him chauvinist, so he purposely oppose whatever his mother do, be it right or wrong, he just want to attack blindly. To make it worst, he can even accuse his siblings of being chauvinist without the least sense of guilty when his siblings help his mother.
    This kind of person is usually more dishonest, timid and superficial.

    If everyone take the middle path instead of overdoing it, our society will be free of unnecessary disputes & accusation.
    Just my 2 cents, comments are welcome.
