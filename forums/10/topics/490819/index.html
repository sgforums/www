    PM Lee Hsien Loong on leave from Dec 12 to 25; DPM Teo Chee Hean is Acting Prime Minister

     

    SINGAPORE - Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong will be on leave from Saturday (Dec 12) to December 25, the Prime Minister's Office said in a statement on Friday.

    During his absence, Deputy Prime Minister Teo Chee Hean, who is also Coordinating Minister for National Security, will be Acting Prime Minister.

    The notice was also published on the online portal of the Government Gazette on Friday.
