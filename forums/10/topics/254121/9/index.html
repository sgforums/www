    Feb 5, 2005
    Boycott establishments that favour Caucasians
    DURING the colonial era, Caucasians were served first in any establishment, public or private. They enjoyed the best of everything. Singaporeans were subservient and solicitous towards them. As they were our masters, we had no choice in the matter.

    But, alas, four decades after independence, nothing has changed. We still hold Caucasians in higher esteem than we do our fellow citizens, and dance attendance on them at the expense of locals. We see this servile attitude enacted every day. It is endemic.
