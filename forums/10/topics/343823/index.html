    Two men were hurt in an incident on Sunday morning at Yio Chu Kang Community Centre, after a man reportedly threw a bottle filled with kerosene.

    This is according to a hotline caller. Channel NewsAsia understands one of the men hurt is Yio Chu Kang Member of Parliament Seng Han Thong while the other is a temple chairman.

    Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) officials said they received a call around noon saying two men had received face and neck burn injuries at the community centre.

    The two men have been taken to the Singapore General Hospital.

    A hospital spokesperson confirmed that two men, with more than 10 per cent burns, were admitted to the Burns Unit shortly after 1pm.

    Police are investigating.
