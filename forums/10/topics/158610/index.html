    Originally posted by stellazio:
    how to perform in he outwide world without the basic knowledge acquired through theory work?
    Theory or concepts are important but their testing through applications in class for validity is even more important and the best way to incorporate such practical learning is through applications.

    That means for every topic or subject, students should be able to come out with an application, designing a complete products or service able to meet marketing criteria. This latter application aspect of learning will lead to creativity among student. It is suggested that such education system should be run in pallelel to examination and class works.

    Those who apply their knowledge even for conventional learning like in IR or cooking will go further than students who merely accomplish class works. The practical broad-based education will produce more innovations and at the end entrepreneurship among students so that when they graduate they are likely to start a business.
