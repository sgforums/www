    If a government openly is seen to be unable to practise fairness and equity in nation building e.g. buying of votes with free monies and promises of estate upgrading to its own supporters in electioneering, will it still possess the moral authority to tell their lawyers to do likewise and stay honest and not to cheat clients?

    Leaders have been telling people to make the economy competitive by practising flexible or variable wage. Have they themselves shown willingness to practise variable wages.

    Have they set high moral and ethical standards ? Will it work if the leaders keep preaching people to be honest and they themselves are unwilling to be honest in election campaigning or accept variable wage system. Can they then expect the lawyers to be honest with clients' monies?

    Before they expect the lawyers, architects, engineers, auditors, accountants and professionals to be honest, should they themslves be prepared to be honest.

    They say variable wage is good. If they believe it to be good can they set the minister's 1st $20,000 of monthly salaries to be the fixed component and the remaining $120,000 to be set as variable component which will be payable on good performance and witheld like in the NKF case or where the MOM fail to achieve the job creation target as announced.

    Citizens' wages have been dropping since 1970s while ministers' and civil lords' salaries kept increasing bucking the trend. They even get bonuses when most of the private sector employees did not even even one month as bonus for the past many years.

    So how can they expect all the professionals to practise honesty and integrity if the leaders can legalistically take any amount of salaries or bonuses just by going to parliament to pass any motion they like to get all such rewards.

    If they cannot practise the above-stated leadership by example, will they succeed in persuading the professionals to do so. Are the leaders sincere in wanting to build a honest administration or are they merely wishy washy and full of hot-airs. I think this is what the professionals are thinking now about ministers' asking citizens to make their low wages even lower by making them flexible while the ministers are enjoying their own highest salaries and highest bonuses for efforts put in by citizens' taxes.

    All the professionals must now be asking after recent election buying of votes and estate upgrading tiff with PP and HG: "If they can do what they like with their own clients' monies why can't we do the same with our clients' monies...catch us if you can."
