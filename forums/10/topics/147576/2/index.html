    Perhaps the more pertinent question is: why arent we doing anything about it?

China, for example, faces the same problem, no matter how nationalistic the PRCs may be.     However, if a country has to come up tops in this aspect, it must surely be Singapore - presidential walkovers, MPs who've never fought an election, can attest to this. It is not enough to heap blame solely on the government, though they might seem contradictory at times when encouraging a civil society; the people in Singapore must bear responsibility.
