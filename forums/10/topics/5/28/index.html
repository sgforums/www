With luck or skill, maybe the lighter vehicle. Speed advantage in such a condition would be bull, cos there's hardly a chance to miss, and if their fire control is good enough, the light tanks WILL take hits. 45 ton vs 60 ton, 15 tons difference, how much speed difference, and acceleration difference would there be? Is it enough to dodge a mach 3 APFSDS round?

    Honestly, do you guys think that our LSVs can possibly race around firing ATGMs and kill tanks with 1 hit? Modern explosive reactive armor can almost ensure that a tank can take 2 hits in the same position. Sure, some say we can hit around the flanks or the rear, but I say if someone's got heavy armor taking the point, they'll cover its flank with everything they have.

    IMO, if we persist in believing that there is NO need for heavy armor, we might find ourselves in the same position as allied tankers in WW2. Them Sherman 75mm guns couldn barely scrach the German Tigers. While the Tigers simply picked the inadequately armored shermans off with their 88mm guns.

    I remember this guy called Shinseki in the US ARMY, proposing to replace all the heavy armor with light "air mobile" armor, that could move quicker, highly manueverable, and pack the equivalent firepower of a tank. He proposed to replace M1A2s, and all heavy armor with a version of LAVs, armed with 105mm guns. He foresaw a future war, where speed, and maneuverability, where the LAVs would be more effective than the M1s. Urban enviroments, jungle warfare.

    Myth: Wheeled armored vehicle can move faster in jungle, and muds.

    Myth: Lighter and faster vehicles are more survivable in jungle and urban warzones.

    The Australians learnt it the hard way when they sent LAVs to east timor. Their WHEELED LAVs virtually crawled in the MUD. Soon, the LAVs became more of a burden than help, when many simply toppled over to their sides when navigating rough terrain.

    Lighter vehicles tend to get killed quicker too. Simply cos their armor is not enough to stop an RPG. The LAVs armor cant stop a 5.56 carbide tip round from penetrating its armor under 10 meters. .50 cal weapons will turn it into swiss cheese. Anti tank missiles, man portable or not, will almost guarantee a kill. You may be fast enough to dodge one rocket, lucky enough, maybe two. What about 3, 4, 5?

    Fact is, tanks are not suited for FIBUA. Its dangerous for the tank and its crew. If its a fact that MBTs can't fight well in a urban area, why should a lighter armored tank be able to survive when a single hit can definitely kill it?

    60 ton roadblock? On the defensive, yes, I WOULD want a 60 ton roadblock that actually shoots back at the enemy.
