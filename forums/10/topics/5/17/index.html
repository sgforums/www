  • rsafpage
    22 Aug 01, 21:42
    Combat radius does not equal range divided by 2. You have to factor in stuff like warload, mission profile(hi-lo-hi, lo-lo-lo etc) and stuff like power settings over target, loiter time over target etc. Range specs are often taken from manufacturers data for aircraft in clean condition(no warloads), max internal fuel, and most econ power setting(ie cruise). IIRC to estimate combat radius its best ot use a figure of around 30% max range.
