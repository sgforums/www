    The IVIS is an inter-vehicular interface system. Basically, inside the M1A2, the TC cockpit has a computer, displaying a minimap. On it, it shows where all his friendly units are. Designated using Symbols. When encounters the enemy, he punches in the grid coordinate of the enemy, and it is updated throughout the system. The system is also linked to the Company FIST, so when fire support is needed, the FIST would have already have the coordinates locked in and sent to their MLRS and guns. Because it displays all the friendlies, it prevents fatricide as the commanders would know where their friendlies are.

    Basically, every tank is equipped with GPS to facilitate automatic update of friendly unit positions.
