    You guys know how sniper works? They camouflage themselves, and interdict targets @ long range. Its pretty much how our SM1s work.

    Armored warfare these days is a mess. If you think that armored warfare is like the Ardennes, huge clusters of tanks firing and rushing, wrong.

    The main objective of an armored force now, is simply to deny, or gain control of advantageous terrain. The SM1s compensate armor for range. Which means it will complish its mission via direct long range fire from its maingun. But admit it, the guns on the SM1 ain't exactly "long ranged" either. It has probably improved range, but not exactly long range. APFSDS rounds slow down over distance, reducing penetration ability. Heat rounds have reduced penetration ability BUT that stays constant regardless of range. Its really not easy. But thats not gonna work when enemy tanks come knocking and looking for full contact. Thats when armor counts.

    About reactive armor. Reactive armor is a kind of armor technology bent to foil shaped charge warheads. Shaped charged warheads travel to its target, then explodes, sometimes shooting out a penetrator. Shaped charge warheads includes, anti-tank missiles, HEAT rounds. They have a constant penetration regardless of range as its the explosion that drives fragments to penetrate the armor. *IMAGINE A SHOTGUN flying at u, wearing a kevlar vest, then firing it into your kevlar as the gun muzzle presses against it* OWww.

    What is reactive armor? Its actually explosives placed on the external side of the tank, exploding outwards. How does it counter shaped charge warheads? Remember that shaped charged warheads uses an explosive force to propell its fragments or penetrator thru the armor. The reactive armor explodes as the shaped charge warhead comes into contact with it, generating a counter explosive force in the opposite direction of the shaped charged warhead. It will effectively prevent penetration of the armor. Of course, its Murphy's world out there, shit does happen.

    After a section of the reactive armor explodes, it leaves behind a blank patch, where a second hit in that area would generally Kill the tank. Which is why mobility is a very important asset in today's armored war.

    Oh yeah, Reactive armor does NOT work well against Long rod penetrators like APFSDS.
