    The government should consist of the ruling party MPs and opposition MPs and NMPs and the whole public service hierachy. There should be no artful split of the government into two camps as the ruling party MPs and one opposition MPs once election is over even though opposition MPs may speak up against ruling party's policies.

    Leaders should diffentiate themselves from their party once they form the government in parliament and focus from that day onwards on achieving their collective needs and aspirations of the people. It should be made an offence or contempt of parliament for PM or ministers or anyone to try to split the parliament into ruling party MPs and opposition MPs thereby dividing them into two different camps.

Leaders must serve with dedication and distinction to people's cause for upgrading themselves socially and economically.
