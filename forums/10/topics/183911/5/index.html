    The key issue with election-linked HDB upgrading is rule of law. Nitty gritty issues like whether the opposition can better help in upgrading their areas are technical details.

    The main point here is if the government starts to link upgrading to votes, it is actually buying votes with upgrading monies even if it is using people's monies.

    It is an illegal act in any country to buy votes whether with the party's own monies or people's monies. In Malaysia certain UMNO minister has to step down to take the rap for vote buying which was declared illegal.

    The issuing of economic restructuring shares or topping up of CPF medisave is also an illegal act if done prior to or near election time.

    When the government starts to buy votes it is telling people not to obey laws and consequently people can be excused for future breaking or underming of the rule of law.

    So when will our government stop gerrymandering election processes like using the GRC or giving monies to the people in any way during or prior to election in order to uphold the rule of law.

    Will the new chief justice rule on this in the larger interest of all Singaporeans so that he will have authority to dispense justice over the country with rule of law.
