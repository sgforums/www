Electricity tariffs up 4.98% from next quarter

  • sunnytv
    25 Jun 08, 21:09

    SINGAPORE : Electricity tariffs will go up by 4.98 per cent (4.98%) or by 1.19 cents per kilo watt per hour (kWh) for all households from the next quarter (July 1 to September 30) beginning July 1.

    Singapore Power said the hike is due to higher electricity costs arising from higher fuel prices. The forward fuel oil price is 11.03 per cent higher than that of US$74.40 per barrel for the current quarter.

    The electricity tariff is reviewed every quarter. — CNA
