    SINGAPORE : One of the three victims from the Yishun triple murder was cremated at the Mandai Crematorium on Thursday.

    36-year-old Yang Jie, a Chinese national, was found dead after falling from a sixth storey public housing flat about two weeks ago.

    The other two victims were found dead in the Yishun flat.

    Yang Jie's 15-year-old daughter, who survived the brutal attack, was at the funeral.

    The teenager was barely able to walk, but she managed to leave the Tan Tock Seng Hospital to attend her mother's funeral on a wheelchair.

    The victim's family members, including her mother, 62-year-old Chang Wei, were also present at the funeral.

    Madam Chang had flown in to Singapore from Shenyang a few days ago. She has been allowed to stay in Singapore until her family's matters have been settled.

    42-year-old Chinese national Wang Zhi Jian has been charged with the murder of one of the three women, Zhang Meng.
