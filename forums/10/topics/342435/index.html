    Progressing backwards
    Thursday, 18 December 2008

    It was reported today that the Government is building more two- and three-room flats because they are presently in demand. Two-room flats are those with a living room and one bedroom. A few years ago the Government was saying that it would phase out the smaller flats because Singaporeans were moving into 4- and 5-room flats as they got richer. What happened?
