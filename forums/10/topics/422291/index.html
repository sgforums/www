    Work in Singapore for 2 years and get citizenship

    February 7th, 2011 | Author: Editorial



    Singapore citizenship (or PR) are given out like candies, if the claims in an advertisement that appears in one of China’s largest blog is anything to go by.

    According to an advertisement which appears on SINA portal, applicants with a basic degree and above are invited to work in singapore for 2 years after which they will be eligible to apply for immigration to Singapore.

    The advertisement listed Singapore’s official ICA website as the source of the eligibility claims and touted their “Special Employment & Immigration Plan” as offering various vacancies  for candidates with “basic understanding of english”, promising starting pay of S$2000 and above.

    The advertisement is reproduced below in verbatim, with a translated version following the chinese version.

    Work in Singapore – Special Plan (working abroad + immigration)

    Plan Description: After you have successfully completed working in Singapore for 2 years, you will be eligible to apply for immigration to Singapore (please refer to the Singapore immigration website: www.ica.gov.sg).

    Application requirements:

    Gender: Male or Female

    Age: 22-38 year-old

    Education: Bachelor degree or above (with certificate)

    Work experience: more than 1 year

    Language requirement: good spoken English

    Job categories are as follows (subject to changes):

    1. Logistic Company – Executive Officer: basic S$2500, with logistic working experience, accounting experience, fluent in English.

    2. Trading Company – Administrative Executive: basic S$2200, more than 2 years of relevant work experience, able to communicate in English, use computer and office equipment and answer phone calls in English.

    3. Construction and interior design company hiring Draftsman: basic S$2000-2500, more than 3 years experience in civil engineering or architectural drawing / familiar with the steel structure design, AutoCAD, StruCAD software / able to communicate in simple English.

    4. Project Manager: S$2000-2500, experience in hardware, furniture or signage design / with AutoCAD design experience / somewhat fluent in English.

    5. Electrical Engineer: S$2500-2800, requires at least three years experience in PLC design, good understanding of electrical lines and power design technology, good English.

    6. PHP Application Developer: S$2500, familiar with PHP application development, 2-3 years of experience is preferred / able to communicate in simple English.

    7. Project Site Supervisor: S$2500, manage and coordinate the whole engineering project, supervise the budget, schedule, quality and safety of the entire project.

    8. Accounting and Human Resource Executive: basic S$2,500, with accounting experience, fluent in English.

    Tianjin Compass Overseas Employment Services Co., Ltd.

    Address: 105 Jianshe Road Heping District, Tianjin Binjiang International Hotel, #1107

    Tel: 400-611-6868

    Contact: Jia, Zheng, Zhang and Wang
    .

    Source: This recruitment ad appears in China SINA portal on 16th Jan 2011 (http://blog.sina.com.cn/s/blog_729091b70100ocus.html).
