    Go see http://www.channelnewsasia.com/stories/singaporelocalnews/view/271473/1/.html and Saturday's 21/4 Straits Times page 3's reports on ex-MP Chng Hee Kok being charged in court.

    I read and re-read the articles, and I still found something missing. Then I realised what it was. Both articles FAILED to mention that Chng was an ex-PAP MP. The words People's Action Party do not appear anywhere in these report.

    Surely such a detail, that the current ruling PAP has an ex-MP falling from grace, is important to the readers. But the fact that the Straits Times and channelnewsasia.com failed to mention this detail in their reports is hardly surprising. The omission is subtle, but it says volume about the close links between our mass media providers and the PAP.
