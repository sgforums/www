    Dr Goh Keng Swee is certainly the true architect of Singapore and its Economy - as he was one of the very few from Asia with a PhD in Economics during the early 1950s.

    Amongst the Founding Fathers - Dr Toh Chin Chye, Late Rajaratname, Lim Kim San, Othman Wok, Lee Kuan Yew - he was the quietest and most modest of the lot.

    Dr Goh was the key organiser - with Dr Toh Chin Chye - in bringing Singapore out of Malaysia, and bring stability to the national psychic during those uncertain days immediately after separation.

    A thinker and a visionary, Dr Goh held the helm at the Finance Ministry and made Singapore a financial center comparable to London, New York, Chicago, Luxembourg, Frankfurt and Tokyo.

    The Economic Development Board was his brainchild, and was established to develop Jurong Industrial Estate and to attract manufacturers from across the globe.

    Singapore's land reclamation works was also the brainchild of Dr Goh Keng Swee and resulted in extensive works to reclaim swampland for the future Jurong Town Industrial Estate.

    The Development Bank of Singapore was also formed to take over the financial funding of enterprises from the Economic Development Board.

    When Dr Goh became the Minister of Defense, the prowess of his mind and his clear vision into the future helped to lay the foundation of the SAF from its early beginning. The Peoples' Defense Force was also established by him, to allow volunteers interested in the Military Defense of Singapore.

    After retiring from Singapore Politics - for personal reasons - he was engaged to be a Consultant to help China open up, and merge into the Economy of the World.

    His contribution to China's progress will not be known, but looking at the phenomenal growth of China in just a short span of twenty years, and comparing to what Singapore has similarly achieved in the same time frame, one can guess that his contribution has not been insignificant.
