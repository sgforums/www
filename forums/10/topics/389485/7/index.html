    Anyway, the new PM of Australia was sworn-in without much fuss by their Governor-General providing the sumpah service for the nation at a much cheaper rate.  

     

    Australia Governor-General

    wiki --

    Their Constitution does not set a term of office, so a Governor-General may continue to hold office for any agreed length of time, however a typical term of office is five years. At the end of this period, a commission is occasionally extended by a short period.

    The salary of the Governor-General is regulated by the Constitution, which fixed an annual amount of 10,000 pounds, unless the parliament decides otherwise. The Constitution states that the salary of the Governor-General may not be increased during his or her term of office. Under the Governor-General Act of 1974 each new commission has resulted in a pay increase. Today, the law ensures the salary is higher than that for the Chief Justice of the High Court, over a five year period. The annual salary during Michael Jeffery's term was $365,000. Quentin Bryce's salary is $394,000.[4] Until 2001, Governors-General did not pay income tax on their salary; this was changed after the Queen agreed to pay tax.[4]
