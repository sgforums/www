    This is the Year of Election - for many countries in Asia as well as the USA.

    Here is a question for you to ponder over. Please read carefully and give an honest answer. Election is a damn serious business so please convince yourself before giving your answer.

    THREE potential candidates are vying for a top post in a certain country. Here are their credentials.


    Candidate A - He associates with crooked politicians and consults with astrologists. He's had two mistresses and spews foul languages. He also chain smokes and drinks 8 to 10 martinis a day.

    Candidate B - He was kicked out of office twice during his adult working life, doped opium in college, frequently sleeps till noon time and drinks a quart of whiskey every evening.

    Candidate C - He is a decorated war hero having risen from the rank and file. He's a vegetarian, doesn't smoke, drinks an occasional beer and never cheated on his wife.

    Which one of these candidates would be your FIRST choice?
