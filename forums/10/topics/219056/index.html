    The father was featured in the Channel NewsAsia report, and confirmed my suspicion of inattentive parent(s) - who were obviously taking in the sights and sounds of a shopping mall, and OBLIVIOUS to the ever present dangers of various situation to the innocent ones.

    He proudly claimed that he had his young son on one side, and the little daughter on the other side, leading both of them by their hands; and would never allow any one of them to fool around on the escalator.

    Unfortunately, he failed to notice the sharp jagged teeth that meshed together when each "moving step" folds and disappear into the escalator frame work.

    The accident was waiting to happen with the type of soft shoes worn by that little toddler, which can so easily get "clawed" by the jagged teeth of the moving escalator step that meshed into each step, as it folds inwards.

    The warning for escalator users is for everyone to stand in the middle of the yellow painted rectangle painted on every piece of the moving floor board.

    It is clear that the parent was OBLIVIOUS to the warning instruction to the moving dangers and allowed both his children to stand on both side of his big framed body, where the danger is MOST PRONOUNCED at the side of the YELLOW PAINTED BOX.

    For this accident, it is clearly the responsibility lay with the parent, who IGNORED BASIC COMMONSENSE to be on the lookout for dangers to his own children.

    Every moving piece of machine or man made equipment can be a help to human kind, but it can also cause serious injury when not carefully used.

    Closing the door of a car is a natural instinct.

    How many of us actually will take care and observe if anyone is holding on to the door frame while attempting to balance oneself while getting out and up from a very comfortable ride ?
