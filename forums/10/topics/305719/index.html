    Most likely Micropolis or Shin Corp will be treated as long-term investment and remain closeted as paper losses to avoid queries.

    One way out perhaps is for them to wait to sell certain counters for gains in order to eventually cover up or offset the heavy losses in counters like Micropolis or Shin Corp or wait longer to find some talents to turn around such companies.

    In other words, he will have to use creative accounting called codes of practice to delay realizing losses from Micropolis or Shin Corp or Global Crossing as the board can then give some excuses like long-term investments to delay counting such losses until some other counters make monies to cover up or liquidate such irretrievable losses.
