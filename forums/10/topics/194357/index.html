How to benefit from IR?

  • robertteh
    6 Jun 06, 21:02
    I am sure our leaders are not that stupid and should know too well that IR was not what they are after. What they are after is to use IR to leverage the whole economy and create whole new generation of economic spin-offs.

    To leverage on the IR, we need first-class Finance or Economic Minister and a First-Class Cabinet who understand how to use IR to create such spin-offs.

    IR can have direct spin-off on tourism but that is not enough. It is necessary to understand how tourists especially the cash-rich tycoons can be made to park their fund here by opening up account here and be given benefits of certain kind. Better still they can start businesses here and be given more benefits. This requres whole new approaches to traditional investment incentives.

    So it is essential to set up a think tank to come up with such type of incentives since business people will also come here not just the normal travellers.

    Businesses that will benefit from such IR arrivals will have to be identified. Processes have to be set up to integrate IR visitations to certain industries like banking, Swiss-Banking, Financial Hubbing, HQ status for leisure and tourism and Venture-capital hubs and many more.

    We will need a holistic economic master plan to integrate IR visitations to economic spin-offs. We can implement them consistently, so that we do not do the IR or IR sake but for something bigger and at the end will make ours an economic IR.

    It will be sinful to do the IR for IR sake or to only create marginal benefits of immediate tourism industries and not the rest of the economy. If we are short-sighted and do not know how to use IR to create economic spin-offs, we will end up not benefiting from IR but only creating a decadent immoral society.

Is the government able to rise above the fray and not allow the society to sink further into social degradation. Is the government committed to building a socially upright and vibrant society so that even with Marina IR, people will hold their head high and not be swayed by these vices ?
