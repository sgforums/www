    To the Church in Laodicea

    Rev 3:15-19

    "I know your deeds, that you are neither cold nor hot. I wish you were either one or the other.16 So, because you are lukewarm - neither hot nor cold - I am about to spew you out of my mouth." 17 You say "I am rich, I have acquired wealth & do not need a thing." But you do not realize that you are wretched, pitiful, poor, blind and naked. 18 I counsel you to buy from me gold refined in the fire, so you can become rich; & white clothes to wear, so you can cover your shameful nakedness; and salve to put on your eyes, so you can see. 19 Those whom I love I rebuke & discipline. So be earnest & repent.

The cycle of judgment is like the wet snowball that becomes larger & sweeps people in its path until God has done a thorough work.

     

    In the US, the cycle of judgment has been in existence for 13 years. Some are still having difficulty accepting that there is a cycle of judgment. But there have been tornados that destroyed 2 to 3 cities, earthquakes, hurricanes that destroyed billions of $ of properties, drought, Sep 11. In 1990, the Lord showed Nita that the destruction of Pentagon is judgment from God.

     

    God's judgment has come to the church leaders, states, nations. US has suffered much. Canada has moved to a degree of cycle of judgment. Now the judgment has come to S'pore.

    But we have taken the blessings of God into ourselves in a wrong way & have become lifted up with pride. If left unchecked, we believe that we are invincible & that whatever we do, God is with us.

     

    A very large % of the churches are full of pride. God is patient with us for a while. Because He is longsuffering, we think He is approving. When we least expect it, we find that we are swept by the cycle of judgment. God is watching over the S'pore church and checking out.

     

    We must weep for the sins that we see in the S'pore churches. We must weep with pain, grief & repentance that God will purge His church. When leaders go astray, we have to pray that God will have mercy & bring them out of deception. Judgment is swift and hard to bear. If we believe that we have been blessed, than the blessings must go where it needs to go. e.g. to bless the poor & needy. We should not just acquire wealth & increase in wealth.

     

    Nita has seen mega churches been judged and became almost empty within one day. It is not wrong to build big churches to support big congregation but extravagance is wrong.

     

    We must realize our need for kingdom power and authority. When we honestly examine our heart conditions, we are not rich in spirit. May God shake His church & shake us awake. We need to hunger for Christ & see His kingdom power, beauty.

     

    We need to be broken before God in prayer. We must pray for revival & outpouring of His Holy Spirit, His righteousness. If we do not pray, we will not see revival. Pray for restoration.

     

    Prayer + tears = Revival

     

    Nita was given a vision of the scene of a stormy sea where there was a large ship that looked like a tall freight. A Trojan horse was seen walking in the water towards the ship. The Trojan horse continued to walk and stood next to the ship. The ship did nothing. It was not concerned. The horse found the neck of the ship, took a huge bite & the ship sank.     If the church is not walking in holiness, humility, righteousness, walking in its knees in prayers, any nation is subjected to the forces of the enemies. We must live a life of holiness, brokenness & on our knees. We cannot be up to the standard if we are not praying.
