Mahathir: End of an era....

  • BillyBong
    1 Nov 03, 17:30
    The stepping down of Mahathir heralds the end of 22 years at Malaysia's helm and the coming of new and perhaps uncertain times.

    While his one-time successor Anwar Ibrahim was touted as a new generation of educated and capable leaders, it would appear that the new PM Datuk Abdullah Ahmad Badawi is a more unassuming and passive leader, bringing a completely different style of leadership to the ruling caste.

    He is no doubt a devoted muslim and his assension to the position of prime minister has definitely ruffled more than a few feathers with the PAS opposition because he poses a serious threat to their plans for UMNO-controlled Kedah.

    For now i am pleased to sit back knowing that at lest till after the next elections, we will have predictable cross-strait relations.
