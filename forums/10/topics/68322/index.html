    Govt has been quite concerned about citizens not having enough children. Yes, the population has been aging fast and the birth rate, esp among the Chinese, has been declining (though it picked up a little around year 1990). However, people have more than enough reasons not to support this campaign.

    Personally, like most Singaporeans these days, I have more reasons not to have children. Living expenses are getting higher every year. GST increase by 1%, bus fare increased over the last few years, etc sound insignificant to some. But to those who have a house/flat/apartment to maintain, a slight inflation to the power supply bill can be impactful. If we put all the expenses together, it can actually rip off a big portion of a person's salary.

    To make things worse, Govt encouraged wage cut during bad times and even company restructuring (which is a nicer word for retrenchment). Some media keep reporting that the economy and job market are forecasted to pick up this year. But many employees are still not sure if they can keep the job the next day, if they do not lose the job today.

    Having children is a big investment of a lifetime. In situation like this, nobody will want to make this kind of commitment. It's like making a huge investment, knowing that it's going to fail.

    What if the parents lose their jobs tomorrow? What's going to happen to the children? Get help from community chest? Will the govt do anything if a child has no pocket money for school? Govt has suggested having child care centres for younger children (age less than 2 years old). Currently all the child care centres only accept children above 2 years old. This, no doubt, does help the working mothers. But still, Govt has not addressed the most essential part of bringing up children - MONEY. Baby bonus is just a one time thing. The money may seem attractive. But raising up a child costs a lot more than that.

    And nobody has so far asked this question: Who benefits from citizens having more children? The parents or the govt?
