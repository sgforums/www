    Principal = $120,000
    Period = 20 years
    Payment = $8,400 a year ($700 a month)

    Punch it into my financial calculator:
    The implied interest rate = 3.44...% p.a.

    Use the implied interest rate to find the period where both annuities and perpetuities (life annuities) will be equivalent.

    Interest = 3.44...% p.a.
    Principal = $120,000
    Payment = $6,000 a year ($500 a month)

    Punch it into my financial calculator:
    Period = 34.5 years.

    This is just a rough calculation, but given the figures by the Minister, a person would have to live another 34.5 years after 62 years (when minimum sum withdrawal is possible) in order to breakeven from the perpetuity (life annuity).
