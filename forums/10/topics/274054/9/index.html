    Scenario 1
    IF our cpf is invested in private insurance companies' annuities products and there is no state guarantees for these products, then effectively a significant portion of our retirement funds is privatized which means our retirement funds are subjected to higher risk of the private sector than before and we are given no choice for this risk.
