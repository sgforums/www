    By S Ramesh, Channel NewsAsia | Posted: 09 June 2007 1745 hrs

    SINGAPORE: Two thieves have struck in broad daylight and stolen hand railings along a pavement on Friday.

    Police say a 20 year old woman called them after witnessing two men removing the orange-coloured railings at about 4pm near Block 121 Bukit Batok Central.

    The men placed the railings in a lorry with a green canvas before walking towards Bukit Batok Central.

    The lorry driver then drove off.

    The two men were said to be fair complexioned and dressed in blue shirt and pants - while the lorry driver was said to be elderly, fair complexioned, plump and dressed in a blue shirt and black shorts.
