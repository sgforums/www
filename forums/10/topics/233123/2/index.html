    I still remember a photo where the South Korean side has smooth paved road and North Korean side has crackly road.

    Why?? Actually they are one nation. They are one nation but with two different governments. Different management.

    So, when I compare Singapore to JB and Batam, it is to illustrate the strength of Singapore management.

    Although you think Singapore demographics is different from JB or Batam, but actually, not so much different. It's not so much a question of demographics, but management.
