    SINGAPORE : The NKF Board and its CEO Mr TT Durai have offered to resign in order to give the Health Minister a free hand on restoring public confidence.

    Pending completion of these reviews, the Health Ministry says the NKF will suspend all active fundraising activities.

    Earlier, the Chairman and members of the board, together with its CEO, had approached Mr Khaw Boon Wan for advise on how to respond to the public reactions to the ongoing court case between the NKF and the Singapore Press Holdings.

    Mr Khaw had stressed two immediate priorities.

    These are that caring for NKF dialysis patients should not be disrupted in any way and secondly the shattered public confidence must be restored.

    Both the NKF board members and the CEO shared this view. - CNA/de
