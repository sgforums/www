    The Current Constitution has failed to provide for the basic rights of citizens or established the role of government and its component branches.

    Without such provisions, we cannot evolve a system of government which will respect the rights of citizens and protect our citizens from being unjustly persecuted by the government leading to instability of the country.

    It should define larger aspects of nation building, it is essential to provide appropriate safeguard to prevent abuse and motivate all citizens to participate in social and cultural and all aspects of nationhood.

    A forward-looking constitution will motivate citizens to participate in all aspects of nation building.

    As it is because of the lack of commitments towards protecting the citizens' rights against abuse, people are treated as digits and number with hardly any respect and all sorts of laws are being passed which increase their hardship and burden.

    There are also a great deal of complexities and uncertainties in areas of administration of justice because of lack of such basic guarantee of citizens' rights leading to conflicts and instability.

    There should be a proper balance between the need for law and order and fulfilling of citizens' needs for protections against abuse of power against the state and the citizens.

    The lack of such provisions has led to over-controls, over-regulations and over-legalistic passing of laws and enforcement resulting in autocracy and stifling of creativity and enterprises of society.

    In the long-term interest of the state or the people, the constitution will need to be upgraded to one which provides not only law and order but protections of citizens and ensuring the greatest good for the greatest number.

    To begin with we need to approach the constitutional upgrading to cover the following major areas of governments:-

    (1) Systematic decentralized institutionalized administration,
    (2) Independence of the judiciary and administration of justice,
    (3) Upholding the democratic one-man-one-vote election process.
    (4) implementation processes to ensure all component branches of government are permitted to exercise their respective roles and funcations with accountability and responsibility so as to uphold citizens' rights, freedoms to participate in nation building from greater social, cultural and economic opportunity without discrimination through class, elitism, creed of any kind.

    Specifically the constitution would establish the component branches of government so that each will be able to exercise its role effectively without any domination or influence by the executive and its supervisory civil service in the interest of all citizens.

    When such a constitution is established, it will then be possible to rally behind the executive and support all the government policies and decisions as such an executive will be working to safeguard the interests and wellbeing of the people.

    The existing constitution is too general leaving the executive too much power to work on its own agenda without accountability and transparency and many policies, appointments of government etc are not working in the interest of the people e.g. "20 major policy errors of governemnt" as posted to the feedback unit.

    It is not sufficient for the government to work on conceptual leadership-centric system as it will lead to abuse window dressing, creative accounting and look-good justifications of policies and presentations.

    There is a need to recognize people needs and aspirations through the constitution as a compact between the government and the people in the long-term interest and any persistent legalistic wrangling of the current constitution will lead ultimately to instability.

    It is essential if the government wants to engage the people in understanding its policies and decisions for the government to revise the inadequacies and shortcomings in the current constitution and upgrade it to one which will motivate the citizens' collective talents, to work together with the government with coordination, knowledge applications and innovation in every field, the key to the people's vibrancy and competitiveness.
