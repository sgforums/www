The Little Red Dot

  • robertteh
    8 Apr 07, 01:27
    In the east was a little red dot,

    Once a one-man-one-vote democracy,

    Where good men were elected by citizens

    To protect them against injustices and inequity,

    To serve the people with humility,

    Alas these men were trusted and given opportunity,

    Which they claim as their worth and mandate,

    To tweak the laws to give them power,

    And became haughty and cockey,

    With power they give themselves the highest salaries,

    Selectively benchmarked against captains of industry,

    Not satisfied with the world's highest salaries,

    That surpass the president of United States,

    They say highest is still not enough,

    They give themselves highest bonuses and life-long fees,

    And demanded ever-higher pays,

    They tax, recover all costs and kept taxes as surplus,

    Or reserve which they boast as their success,

    Not satisfied with one-man-one-vote freak election,

    They tinker with democratic voting process,

    And tweak it into a group representation or GRC

    In name it was to provide racial harmony,

    In fact it is to block any best from election in single wards,

    To give them monopoly and power to flex muscle,

    To sue anyone for speaking the truth in election process,

    Till bankruptcy with no one standing on election day,

    Election day is day for erection of banners,

    To declare walk over due to no contest in GRCs,

    Some rebates may be given on conservancy,

    To pacify peasants and potential rebel,

    Once election is over, GST will be increased

    From selling public housing built on people's lands,

    Charged at market value at a little discount called subsidy,

    Forever and ever there will be more taxes,

    To build more surpluses to pay world's highest salaries,

    Otherwise they say there will be corruption or loss of talents,

    Alas surpluses became lost in souzhou or shin corp,

    But these were no mistakes but long term investments,

    Election goodies will take care of unhappy memories,

    Integrated resort will drown misery,

    Never mind dropping middle class wages,

    Never mind there are many issues like the rising costs,

    For they will say which country has no rising costs,

    For they will say people must trust leaders,

    For they will say every one makes mistakes,

    For they will push away all mistakes,

    By saying that People are the grousers,

    They could not guarantee performance due to globalization,

    They could not guarantee value-add economic restructuring,

    They claim that little red dot has no resources,

    People must remain forever grateful to their little red dot.
