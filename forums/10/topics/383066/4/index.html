    Angel might not know that minimum wage is floor price, so just to explain to her the meaning of minimum wage as a floor price.

    There are 2 forms of price control in economics ie floor price (ie minimum price) or price ceiling (ie maximum price).

    A floor price is used when the equilibrium or market price is too low and a price ceiling is used when the equilibrium or market price is too high.
