    MM Lee finally admitted as reported on CNA tonight that what he felt that his ministers' projection of a 6.5 million population may not be sustainable.

    Many people have given their view on this and yet he has refused to accept such feedbacks.

    He now said that he felt that the comfortable or optimum population for Singapore should be between 5.0 to 5.5 millions. Apparently it is the first time he has to slap his own minister Mah Bow Tan's face for such a stupid forecast.

    He also said what he was most afraid of is not Singapore not being able to attract foreign talents from outside but its own citizen talents being attracted away from outside. As if for years he has not received people's many feedbacks of his anti-people policies causing such brain drain of our own talents to other countries.

    Apparently it is only after many years that he was finally convinced that the citizens' concern of such brain drains was right.

    In one policy after another it took many years after great deal of harm was done then he realized the damage he has caused due to his own stubbornness.

    What makes him finally change? Perhaps he was now really hurt for the first time because even his own grand children have decided too that Singapore is not the country to return to as home sweet home come back after education abroad.

    It is proven once again that for many years citizens complaints against his narrow and self-centred anti-people policies were not wrong after all.

    What is wrong is his own obstinacy. It took him more than a decade to change his streaming elitist policy and it also took him decade to recognize his own people are talents who were driven away because of his not bothering with promoting utilization of such practical talents.

    The problem is he has been the one who obstructs the younger ministers and others to change by over-emphasising and talking too much of his old policies which were anti-people and anti-progress most of the time.

    The only time Singapore can move with the time is for him to step down and leave well alone. There are many talents out there who could reason out things and do a lot better how to keep up with the larger world outside.
