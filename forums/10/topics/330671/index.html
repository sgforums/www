    With all the furor about foreigners and their place in society, in addition to Singapore's low fertility rate, let me present some fresh new ideas for solving these 2 problems and ensuring Singapore's prosperity into the indefinite future.

    First, all Singaporean males must be streamed. The smartest 1% will be groomed to be the future leaders, although this proportion may disappear once all sons of elite Singaporeans are counted. Of the remainder, the strongest and healthiest 20% will be forcibly drafted to the SAF to protect the interests of elites and FTs. The remaining 80% shall be indentured labor, eliminating the need for foreign workers.

    The same applies to Singaporean females. The best-looking 1% will be reserved as the wives for the future leaders, while the rest will be free to date and marry talented foreign men. None shall be allowed to breed with the inferior Singaporean male stock. Given the dislike for local men by Singaporean girls, this will not be too hard to achieve.

    Finally, foreign talent must be well-treated. Voting rights shall be reversed, with elites and foreigners holding the power of the ballot while local Singaporeans are denied it. This is to prevent uppity locals from disrupting the peaceful lives of FTs and elites. Freedoms are also to be similarly regulated, with foreigners having absolute freedom of speech and movement and Singaporeans having to have an ERP chip implant that monitors their movement via gantries, and immediate decapitation for anything said against the government and FTs. Also, it shall no longer be a crime to kill a Singaporean, but when a foreigner is killed, 9 generations of the family of the Singaporean responsible will be shot.



    I hope my ideas are of use in deciding Singapore's future. We live in a constantly changing world, and old ideas such as the superiority of Singaporeans over foreigners must be discarded.
