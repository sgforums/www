    Letter from Michael Chua Kheng Hwee

    IT APPEARS it's not just women who risk getting the boot from their work place when they go on maternity leave. There are men who defer their reservist in-camp training (ICT) for fear of being replaced.

    I have a friend who is working in an IT position based on a one-year renewable contract.

    The Ministry of Defence notified him that he was scheduled to go for his ICT this month. But he deferred his training for fear of losing his job to some foreign talent.
