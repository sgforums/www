According to a recent BBC report, some T-shirt manufacturers prefer Italian workers to Romanian workers even though Italians are far more expensive. The reason is that not only can Italian workers make the product, but when the machines malfunction or misalign the seams as they do from time to time, Italian workers can spot the problem, repair the machine and rectify the problem. Romanian workers cannot go the extra step and in the end the costs are higher in Romanian factories due to higher error rates.
