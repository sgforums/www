    The time of political awakening has come upon us. But what must the citizens do to take advantage of such awakening?

    Here are three steps they could take:

    (1) Time - Time is now because of internet age. Circumstances and rules of the game are changing. Government now realizes that the young not turning to the news in the internet for news and facts instead of the newspapers which are generally perceived by people as not telling the truths, telling half-truths and disabled to report on real problems.

    Government now wants to counter cyberspace criticisms because of this new circumstance.

    The government now cannot instruct or direct the people what to do and believe. People will not take what they say as truths. For example, newspapers are reporting everyday that property market is doing well, but people could find out whether it is. Watch for what the cyberspace says.

    Why Minister Mah Bow Tan continues to air his own arrogant opinion about how affordable is public housing, people read about great discontent being expressed in the cyberspace and about the profiteering going on in HDB to make double profits on the lands people already owned and paid for by their taxes.

    Minister Khaw will be seen as not fully addressing the issues of concern if he continues to impose the presumptuous organ robbery opt-out scheme forcibly taking away organ from person in a coma situation against human right to save the dying person.

    (2) Political Awakening - More people have come to understand their right to air their views on how the country should be run especially involving wrong doings like the NKF or the lack of accountability in making investments of people's hard-earned monies in Shin Corp.

    People are now asking for change to past government policies like the taxing and cost-recovery system, which drives up costs of living and wasteful spending of their hard-earned monies on golden taps.

    They are also disagreeing with use of taxpayers' monies for estate upgrading with priority given to the loyal pap supporting precincts.

    They are also telling the government not to engage in money politics with giving of monies to buy votes prior to election.

    They are also questioning whether the accounts presented to parliament based on current revenues versus current expenditure with silence on capital gains and revenues from windfalls in land sales are truthful.

    They are also asking to change the constitution to prevent using of GRC for maintaining racial equilibrium in voting when there are more practical method like proportional representation or reserved seats which will not result in incumbent party undue advantages in contesting election.

    Citizens must post their views and opinions objectively without being anti-anyone. Be consistent and focused like in the NKF views.

    (3) Implementation - For a start, People must at least go to the internet an take an active part in political renewal in whatever form. They must put up proposals and get fellow citizens to support the political parties and candidates to vote for including oppositions of good quality or even certain incumbent. They are not against the government but are taking an active part in deciding the political renewal they want.
