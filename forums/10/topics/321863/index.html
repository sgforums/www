    Many had choosed to express their anger against the govt here. It is an outlet to voice their frustrations, and nothing wrong with it. The only thing wrong is when it turns to slander. More so when political opportunists take such disenchantment to sow discord and strife amongst us citizens, using us all as tools for their despicable political ambitions.

    If you noticed, these slimy opportunists offers one no real solutions to help, but to stoke up the flames of discontent by adding the volitile fuel of falsehoods and half truths to burn the dismay faced by many in these current rise in inflationary pressures faced by many, not a problem created by our govt, but by events beyond our control.

    Rather than to go into a senseless debate with them, for they will never admit to the truth or comprehend logic, they will only insult  and show their further stupidity, helping no one. Thus this post, from a citizen to another, to share ideas on how to cope with the current times.

    First of all, we must understand our problems, face up to facts and only then can we confront it to seek solutions. There is no free lunch in this world. Our society, while will never abandon the weak, neither will we help those who do not wish to help themselves but to sit around all day to moan and bitch.

    The only way to fight inflation is to get better jobs or rise in salaries. Fighting with bosses will only ensure capital investments leave our shores, or forcing the govt for handouts will only bankrupt our next generation. Each year we get to welcome est.more than 50,000 new baby citizens, and we must ensure they have a future and are protected.

     

    Education

    ------------

    It is only through a good education that you may be able to get a better job. You will have to ensure that what you study is relevant to the industries that needs workers. Eg;-Financial Wealth management is the current hot job, CGI is another.

    For those who already left for work,  do go back for nite studies. There is no choice if one seeks more money. The govt will offer grants to those who wants to help themselves. As long as you qualify for a job and have the personality to match it, you will be hired. Even if you can't find a job here, your certificate will help you overseas, as a wealth manager in New York or air con mechanic in Australia.

    Grocery

    -----------

    The govt had done their best to scout for the cheapest sources, and to cut down on the  middlemen by buying direct from such sources. While our 3.5million population cannot fight against the international corporate shopkeepers who get better bulk discounts for billions of people such as Walmart, attempts had been made on inter-govt talks for better rates. Establishment of business ties with Russia, Vietnam, and Libya are such attempts.

    As long as you buy what you need, and buy house brands, you will never starve in Singapore. Furthermore, if one is truly desperate and in need of food, CDC agencies will assist to issue food vouchers. If you do know of some family who is starving, please let them know our society will help them. They only need to approach our CDC centres and show that they are incapable of helping themselves, such as handicapped, or out of job, and help will be offered.

    With all these help offered, it is hoped that the food bought is not wasted, and children must be taught not to waste their food.

    Employment

    --------------

    In these times, attitudes are very important. While it is not necessary to beg or grovel before the bosses, there is also no need to take on a confrontational role with them. Without them, we will all have no jobs.

    Reason and logic will move minds. Even if it doesnt, so long as we are proffesional in our duties and our work output is relevant and helps him in his profitability, there is no reason to get fired.

    If one is retrenched, under our laws, he is protected and entitled to retrenchment benefits. With such monies, it would be wise to upgrade oneself mentally for other jobs that are avaliable in the market.

    While the pay may be low in some firms, but if one shows his capability, he will be paid more in time. We are living in an era where capability is rewarded.

    Entreprenuership

    -------------------

    After spending years on the job, and learning from it, one may consider taking the entreprenuership approach. In our country, there are no barriers to entry, saved your own desire and mental strength. Our govt offers grants to startup in critical and relevant industries, thus you will not stand alone. The only requirement is that you must have a sound plan as well as a capable team to help build up your company.

    Recreation

    --------------

    Recreation costs had gone up. Thus there is a need to seek cheaper alternatives. All work and no play is not what our society wants. The beach, gardens and parks are still free.

    Cinema tickets are cheaper during the workdays. Furthermore, there are cheap DVDs for rental as well.

    Pubs are fun, but so can the neighbourhood coffee stalls as well. And it is much cheaper. $15 a mug of beer in pub vs $5.50 for a bottle in the heartlands.

    Homes

    ---------

    Prices of home had gone up. in 1970s, the first SIT flats costs only around $15,000 for a 3 room flat. But in those days, salaries were only around $800 a month and a cup of coffee costs only 30cts.

    The world had changed, and costs of materials, which are largely imported, had gone up as well. And it will continue to spiral even for the next few generations to come.

    Thus the rise in prices. But still, a HDB 3rm flat is much more cheaper than a similar sized 99 yr leased condo.

     

    Times are hard, as it had been for our previous generations. While we have a better standard of living than some other nations, we can improve it for ourselves and the next generations, not by fighting one another over senseless issues, but critical ones that will elevate our lives further, as what our govt had been focussing on, and sadly, that which the opposition parties had been disconnected from.

    If you have further ideas or solution that can help our fellow citizens, you are welcome to contribute to this thread. But if your intention is to whine and bitch, please open up your own thread.

    First of all, we must understand our problems, face up to facts and only then can we confront it to seek solutions. There is no free lunch in this world. Our society, while will never abandon the weak, neither will we help those who do not wish to help themselves but to sit around all day to moan and bitch.

    The only way to fight inflation is to get better jobs or rise in salaries. Fighting with bosses will only ensure capital investments leave our shores, or forcing the govt for handouts will only bankrupt our next generation. Each year we get to welcome est.more than 50,000 new baby citizens, and we must ensure they have a future and are protected.

     

    Education

    ------------

    It is only through a good education that you may be able to get a better job. You will have to ensure that what you study is relevant to the industries that needs workers. Eg;-Financial Wealth management is the current hot job, CGI is another.

    For those who already left for work,  do go back for nite studies. There is no choice if one seeks more money. The govt will offer grants to those who wants to help themselves. As long as you qualify for a job and have the personality to match it, you will be hired. Even if you can't find a job here, your certificate will help you overseas, as a wealth manager in New York or air con mechanic in Australia.

    Grocery

    -----------

    The govt had done their best to scout for the cheapest sources, and to cut down on the  middlemen by buying direct from such sources. While our 3.5million population cannot fight against the international corporate shopkeepers who get better bulk discounts for billions of people such as Walmart, attempts had been made on inter-govt talks for better rates. Establishment of business ties with Russia, Vietnam, and Libya are such attempts.

    As long as you buy what you need, and buy house brands, you will never starve in Singapore. Furthermore, if one is truly desperate and in need of food, CDC agencies will assist to issue food vouchers. If you do know of some family who is starving, please let them know our society will help them. They only need to approach our CDC centres and show that they are incapable of helping themselves, such as handicapped, or out of job, and help will be offered.

    With all these help offered, it is hoped that the food bought is not wasted, and children must be taught not to waste their food.

    Employment

    --------------

    In these times, attitudes are very important. While it is not necessary to beg or grovel before the bosses, there is also no need to take on a confrontational role with them. Without them, we will all have no jobs.

    Reason and logic will move minds. Even if it doesnt, so long as we are proffesional in our duties and our work output is relevant and helps him in his profitability, there is no reason to get fired.

    If one is retrenched, under our laws, he is protected and entitled to retrenchment benefits. With such monies, it would be wise to upgrade oneself mentally for other jobs that are avaliable in the market.

    While the pay may be low in some firms, but if one shows his capability, he will be paid more in time. We are living in an era where capability is rewarded.

    Entreprenuership

    -------------------

    After spending years on the job, and learning from it, one may consider taking the entreprenuership approach. In our country, there are no barriers to entry, saved your own desire and mental strength. Our govt offers grants to startup in critical and relevant industries, thus you will not stand alone. The only requirement is that you must have a sound plan as well as a capable team to help build up your company.

    Recreation

    --------------

    Recreation costs had gone up. Thus there is a need to seek cheaper alternatives. All work and no play is not what our society wants. The beach, gardens and parks are still free.

    Cinema tickets are cheaper during the workdays. Furthermore, there are cheap DVDs for rental as well.

    Pubs are fun, but so can the neighbourhood coffee stalls as well. And it is much cheaper. $15 a mug of beer in pub vs $5.50 for a bottle in the heartlands.

    Homes

    ---------

    Prices of home had gone up. in 1970s, the first SIT flats costs only around $15,000 for a 3 room flat. But in those days, salaries were only around $800 a month and a cup of coffee costs only 30cts.

    The world had changed, and costs of materials, which are largely imported, had gone up as well. And it will continue to spiral even for the next few generations to come.

    Thus the rise in prices. But still, a HDB 3rm flat is much more cheaper than a similar sized 99 yr leased condo.

     

    Times are hard, as it had been for our previous generations. While we have a better standard of living than some other nations, we can improve it for ourselves and the next generations, not by fighting one another over senseless issues, but critical ones that will elevate our lives further, as what our govt had been focussing on, and sadly, that which the opposition parties had been disconnected from.

    If you have further ideas or solution that can help our fellow citizens, you are welcome to contribute to this thread. But if your intention is to whine and bitch, please open up your own thread.
