    I bought this book for $10 in centrepoint a few years ago.
    I remember I spotted J.B Jeyaratnam with his helper trying to get attention from the after office hour crowd to buy his book. As the word "trying" has suggested, I felt that JBJ should not have this type of fate lah... So, I approached him.

    I : "Errrr... can I have one of those? How much is one of those?"
    JBJ : "10 dollars"

    omg... at tat time, I was . I always have the image tat JBJ is a fighter who speaks aggressively and fight against the strongest man in Singapore. But from his reply, it sound so soft and weak... Just like a reply from my grandfather... I hand him my $10...

    JBJ : " Do u need me to sign?"
    My head was in the blank... huh??? I never thought of u signing my book leh... hahaha... I do not noe how to answer at that time... So, I just replied "No need"

    Although it is a short encounter, but indeed it induced many feelings in me.
    1) He sounds very feeble
    2) But yet, he has the strength and drive to get his message across
    3) When he is selling his book, u can feel the salesman's humble.
