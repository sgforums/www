    heres a secret: your bmt platoon commander does not have a lot of influence in posting you to OCS even though it really looks like he's the man to 'carry' for all those who want to go OCS. the best way to try for OCS is to perform really really well during this thing called Sittest field camp in your BMT. its where you are split up into teams with other recruits you dont know and for three days each recruit will be given a chance to be leader and a mission to complete. the three days are deliberately made tough they're not looking for mere leaders but leaders who can think under pressure. believe me, for recruits, its tough.
    and if you think bmt was tough let me assure you sispec and ocs is hell a lot tougher. not that i want to say you, but a lot of ppl enter bmt thinking "wah i want to enter ocs then very glamour cannot be that difficult right?"
    i've seen a lot of those kind. from my experience, recruits who just enter or about to enter bmt who openly tell the whole world they want to enter ocs only think of the glamour and will start asking for slack appointments by the end of their bmt.
    my advise: relax brother. ask why you want to enter OCS? glamour? prestige? or the challenge of going thru hell?
    i'm serious.
