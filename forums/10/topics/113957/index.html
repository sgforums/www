    24 Jan 05, 02:07
    paintball = messy! [they have a website but its a bit useless]
    airsoft = clean!

    just some things i picked up on:
    get up to the site around 6.30ish for a start around 7pm, games played until 10.30
    fps at all firefight sites is 328fps with a 0.2 BB - random chronoing is carried out.
    you can rent face protection (at a nominal charge) but you will need to call firefight ( 0870 8554188 ) to pre-book them (they dont generally bring any kit with them unless specifically needed/requested)

    [airsoft at electrowerkz is run by an airsoft skirmishing co called 'firefight']

    so phone them in advance if you want to go down, and need to hire kit.
    i think some of the electrowerkz/slimelight staff [usually 2 or 3 guys/girls] are always there so its worth actually asking them about membership of the club while you're there.
    Also, youll probably find leaflets/flyers that i think would get you into slimilight too.

    hth

    grenades and pyros are not allowed as theyre too noisy for area.
    claymores ok

    oh and because the paintball happens alot, if you do go for airsoft, take spare clothes perhaps - the paint from paintball can sometimes still be left around even after clean-up. it gets very smelly and gets all over your clothes. you also slip up all over the place hence 'slimelight'..hehe
