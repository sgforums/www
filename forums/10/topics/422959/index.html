    Zainul Abidin Rasheed

    Netizens have setup a FaceBook group named “Vote Zainul Abidin Rasheed Out” to appeal to residents of Aljunied GRC to vote agsinst Mr Zainul Abidin Rasheed.

    Explaining the reason why Zainul Abidin Rasheed should be voted out,  the group said:

    Beyond handshakes, smiles and attending social functions at Aljunied GRC, what has Zainul Abidin Rasheed done for the constituents of Aljunied GRC?

    What has Zainul Abidin Rasheed beyond launching a boat from Oman to Singapore done for the country or the people?

    What has Zainul Abidin Rasheed, despite riding on the popularity of Malay community, done for the Malay community in terms of closing the education, income, employment and housing gap between the Malay community and other communities?

    Mr Zainul Abidin Rasheed is a PAP Member of Parliament for Aljunied GRC and Senior Minister of State, Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
