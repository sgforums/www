    My thinking is that the Opp parties should just leave CST and LTH to hold their respective forts and not be bothered too much by GCT. In terms of tactics, PAP's move is designed to exert pressure on the Opps --- they could have just quietly sent in GCT during the elections instead of making a big diversion beforehand.

    --- Even without doing this, the opp are facing the threat of elimination.
    --- Singaporeans might just vote to save the opposition and preserve an alternative voice.
    --- It raises the elections to a national level (it already is if AMK is contested) since it will decide the political landscape of Singapore's future generation.
    --- It will force Singaporeans to made a decision (aka seek people's mandate) whether they want an alternative voice and stop passing the buck, hoping someone somewhere else will vote for the opp.
    --- If Singaporeans really do not want an alternative voice, all the opp can close shop, unhappy voters can migrate and the rest can live happily ever after.
