    Can the claim from Malaysia be valid, when Indonesia has as much right to a similar claim ?

    The White Stone Island sits squarely in the middle of the International Water Ways - that separate the territorial waters of Indonesia and Malaysia.

    Neither Indonesia nor Malaysia disputed Singapore's 150 year administrative rule over this piece of rock; and with the most recent 1965 political agreement between Malaysia and Singapore - that had Singapore evicted from Malaysia - the Malaysian Government did not dispute the territory being ceded as part of Singapore's territory.
