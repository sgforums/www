    Read some interesting stuffs about Singapore UAV and thought of sharing it with you folks...

    --------------
    Aeronautics Unmanned Systems is one of a number of small Israeli UAV companies. Another, Emit Aviation Consultancy (Petach Tikva, Israel), has assisted Singapore Technologies Dynamics (ST) in the development of the Blue Horizon UAV. ST Dynamics has exported the 330-pound, tail-first Blue Horizon to the Philippines.

    Blue Horizon is not the only UAV under development for Singapore 's secretive military. Firefly is reportedly a joint ST/Israeli project for a high- altitude, high-speed reconnaissance UAV that could be converted into a cruise missile.

    Last year, Singapore 's MoD disclosed the existence of Lalee (low altitude, long-enduring endurance), a large multi-purpose maritime and littoral UAV. According to the MoD, US aircraft designer Burt Rutan (creator of the round-the-world Voyager manned aircraft) has participated in a three- year project to define the UAV. A twin-boom vehicle with a 26-foot-long payload pod, Lalee is designed to support a task group operating in littoral waters with both radar and optical surveillance and communications-relay functions. The term "long-enduring endurance" indicates that Singapore is looking for not only a long flight endurance but also a long service life. The next step would involve the testing of a proof-of-concept prototype.

    The existence of ambitious programs such as Lalee is important even if they do not all see fruition. It indicates that, after a long and difficult adolescence, UAVs are beginning to reach maturity.
    -----------
