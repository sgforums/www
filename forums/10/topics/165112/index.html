    The first principle in any human progress is without doubt openness, discussions and deliberations as it is only when majority of citizens are interested to contribute to the governance that there could be the best solutions and choices that lead to progress and competitiveness.

    Singaporeans have been denied of many opportunities to train the people to excel in applying practical knowledge applications and innovations.
