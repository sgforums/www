    A concept simply explained for easier understanding.

     

    This dirty bank idea was mooted by the US. Dump all toxic assets into this 'bank' and get aid for its pre-crisis value.


    Toxic assets are not without value, eg - a house. In this crisis, that house is worth very low if sold. In future, when stability returns, such assets will rise in value and be transferrable for better cash.


    Majority of nations have treasuries. While such funds are meant for social spending, a large significant proportion of it is left untouched, used only for emergencies. Such funds would be use to fund the dirty bank.


    When existing banks become solvent and better regulated, confidence and stability will return and jobs re-created.


    Meantime, the dirty bank will only operate to find ways to secure better prices for its assets. They will not be subjected to any govt tax regulations, such as property tax or stamp duties.

     

    Maintenance costs will be borned by the govt and where possible, the assets such as properties be rented out to earn income and refund the treasury.
