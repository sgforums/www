    Like most other MBTs, the role of the Tempests would probably be to slug it out in direct frontal combat with enemy armor. Since the Tempests are not very mobile compared to our other armored assets, especially since our combat engineers are not that well equipped for MLC60 vs MLC30, they will likely not be used in the attack. I think that they would be used to defend against attacking armor columns by engaging them head on. Therefore, they will be used as a rearguard or vanguard force, to prevent any enemy armored thrust from dividing our forces or penetrating to our rear areas. I believe that they were originally purchased in case potential aggressors decided to match our armored capability, which they have so far failed to do. MBTs are primarily anti-armor weapons, and until potential aggressors have significant armor capability (which may be very soon), they will not be very useful.
