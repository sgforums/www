    My experience of my NS life is,

    1. A rite of passage
    2. A involuntary rite of passage
    3. Being paid peanuts
    4. Acting like a monkey when OILs are around the corner
    5. Getting injured and downgrading
    6. Thanks injury for a cushy post at HQMES, doing projects.
    7. Leaves cushy post for OETI, damn the daily muster parades.
    8. Finding out that life is a big irony, Jaguar Coy's 3 OOC personnel become 3SGs
    while the rest are men.
    9. Finding out that if you do not pull rank on others, others will do so to you for
    your failure to do so.
    10.Feeding one stray dog some rations while doing Guard Comd duty.
    11.Being able to wake up at 0900 on a weekday is succinct pleasure.
    12.Never though I would miss a piece of pink plastic so much
