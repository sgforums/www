    Minister Mentor Lee Kuan Yew has hit out at the Workers' Party candidate James Gomez.

    Speaking at his first rally speech of this election at Nee Soon East on Tuesday evening, he said Gomez is a liar and a bad egg in the Workers' Party.

    And he feels party chief Mr Low Thia Khiang has lower standards of integrity compared to Mr Chiam See Tong.

    Mr Lee said: "Gomez is a liar. When cornered, he says, "I apologise". But he refuses to say what he has apologised for. He will not answer the questions asked by the Minister for Home Affairs. So the minister has come out with a statement setting the full facts to show that Gomez has lied and done so deliberately. He planned to damage the Elections Department and the government, by pretending that we fixed him by not giving him a minority candidate certificate and so prevented him from taking part in a GRC where they needed him as an Indian minority candidate."

    Mr Lee noted that blame would be on the Elections Department and the minister, Mr Wong Kan Seng, in whose portfolio the Elections Department comes under.

    MM Lee said: "Mr Wong gave him the last three days to come clean, repeatedly asking to answer simple questions. Gomez chose to say nothing. Low Thia Khiang said he had asked Gomez for the details. Low chose to defend him but refused to clarify his position on Gomez's deliberate trick to put the blame on the Elections Department."

    "Mr Low decided to back Gomez. I thought Mr Low was politically smarter than Mr Chiam See Tong. When Chiam found Dr Chee Soon Juan was a bad egg, he criticised Chee and distanced himself from him. Chiam lost his own party. Mr Low appears to have lower standards of integrity compared to Chiam."

    Mr Lee also reminded voters to consider carefully the candidates who could look after their lives, jobs and homes.

    Said Mr Lee, "During elections, the opposition makes promises which they know they cannot fulfil, because they will not be the government. Many people go to their election rallies to enjoy the noise and excitement. But when you go home, consider carefully which candidate or the group of candidates in the GRC can look after your lives, your jobs, homes, your children's education, health, schools, transportation, playgrounds and parks.

    "The PAP has improved your lives continuously over 40 years. In the last five years, together we overcame problems. Had the government been incompetent, they would have brought Singapore down -- Asian financial crisis, SARS, terrorism, Iraq war. Now there is the danger of an oil crisis, the price could spike from US$70 to over US$100 per barrel, because of the problem of Iran's nuclear technology. If that happens, there will be a world recession." - CNA /ch/ct
