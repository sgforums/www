    Whenever people talk about pension scheme, the basic principles would always be: to compensate the government employees for long-term loyalty, below-market salaries or some other criteria.

    The important issues no matter what other talent the MPs or ministers may have is to use commonsense and to debate in front of the whole population when taking this kind of monies like millions of annuities for life of retired MPs and Ministers whether they are prepared to be honest to want to follow such basic principles.

    If these basic principles cannot be followed then what principles they now want to change and add to the traditional pension scheme should be put forward for all to accept and not just by their own MPs to avoid conflicts of interest.     If there is no explanations again then this kind of taking is daylight robbery.
