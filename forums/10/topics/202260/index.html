    Raymond Lim raised the concern about teens planning to leave Spore during the 16th Temasek Seminar. According to SPH's survey on 2,548 teens from India, China, Malaysia, Japan & Spore, 53% Sporeans thought of migrating. India is 39% & Msia is 28%.
