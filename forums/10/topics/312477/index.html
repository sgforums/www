  • Ponders
    27 Mar 08, 08:08

    I think why Singapore has so many MPs because MPs are based on MP to Population Ratio, not MP to Country Size Ratio.

    Singapore's 84 MPs based on 4million ppl is actually quite normal compared to similar Westminster style countries.

    As for the Mayor post. Wasn't this created like around the turn of the century?

    It wasn't really clarified what Mayor can or cannot do and what Council they lead. Also, Singapore's 2 Mayors are controlling areas with boundaries the typical Singaporean won't know.

    In a typical City, if there was a municipal problem or issue. The resident will lodge a complaint to the municipal office. The Municipal office will discuss the problem with the municipal committee, Commitee includes people like, Police Chief, Waste & Sewage Chief, Roads and Traffic Chief, Finance Chief, Education Chief, Licenses Chief, etc.

    It is spelt out clearly that there is NO ONE else you can approach if you encounter problems. So their duties are clearly defined.

    But in Singapore, Policing is done by SPF, Waste and Sewage by NEA (or Onyx), Roads & traffic by LTA, Finance (town council), Education (MOE), Licenses (SPF or related agency)

    They do not represent themselves in Town Council meetings.

    This leave me to wonder what a Mayor in Singapore actually does other than sending newsletter every quarter.
