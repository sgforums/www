    Is your surname similar to anyone in Government, that will reflect any person of importance in Government ?

    If you want to get a faster response from the DPP, try to make a public nuisance of yourself, and you will get immediate attention within 24 hours.

    Look what had happened to members of the Opposition, who had stepped on the wrong side of the politics; and they got themselves immediate attention.

    From my own experience, with the help of my lawyer, I had to play the role of a detective to track down my own assailant in an assault case - which the Police refused to accept as a criminal assault and insist that it is a Civil Case that they have no jurisdiction over.

    After 3 months of footwork and stakeout, I got all that I need for the Police to act; and to my great surprise, the Investigating Officer was continuously on course (from Driving Class to Skill Development), and my case file remain at the bottom of a pile of other cases.

    His colleagues informed me to report my findings to the Police Post that is nearest to the address of my assailant. It was to my own good fortune that there was a Neighborhood Police Post at the void deck of my assailant's block; and I managed to convince the Station Sergeant to take the particulars of my assailant.

    One would have thought that the Investigating Officer's Department would have the initiative to act on the information from me that the assailant has been positively identified by myself, and that his particulars are with a specific NPP station. To my surprise, I was advised by the colleague of the Investigating Section to inform the NPP Sergeant to contact the Investigating Section.

    This is the problem with what an average Singaporean can encounter, when one has no influence, connection, or fame; and this is despite the fact that I am educated and know my own rights under the law.

    What will the treatment be to an average housewife, or anyone from the lower income group, who are largely lesser educated ?
