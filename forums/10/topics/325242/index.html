  • Jingojingjing

    Hello ,

     

    2 lapses were found when I was just simply browsing through yesterday's and today's (friday) newspapers :

    1.  Thurday ,  F.Y.I section, pg23, Global Brand Forum advertisement (quarter page ad) :  

    ERROR : "Joanne Ooi Marketing and Creative Director of .... as she shares his  insights on maverick approaches to brand building at the world's most anticipated brand forum"    <-- how can this be a logically sound statement?

     

     

    2.  Friday ,  Life! section, pg 3, article titled  'True teen models' :

    ERROR :  "She is learning ballet at a top Australian school and he has takan  part in the Harvard University Model UN.  Both are poised to make their mark"   <--- what does 'takan' mean?

     

    I am dismayed by the lapses. These basic and fundamental typo errors should have been detected and corrected before sending for mass printing. 

    I hope such mistakes will not become a habit.

    What to do?

    Its happened.

    Lets close ranks

    and  move on ?

     

    yup, I think I shall move on... to the next section of the newspaper.
