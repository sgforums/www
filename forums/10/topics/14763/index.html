    1) One who is able to motivate
    2) One who make people work for him willing, without complain.

    5) One who get the work done (same as point 2 but does not need the subordinates to work for them willing. They can use whips or anything that I care to obtain their objectives)

    To me, most of the officers are just slave drivers (and thus fall into supervisor category). They dun motivate (some dun even bother to know your name. To them, we are just a tool to help them promote.)
