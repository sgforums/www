    The US is by no means perfect. Fundamental flaws exisit in their society such as lack of gun control, no universal access to medicare, a large population of poor and disenfranchised. Sure there is lobbying and representation by interest groups -- but frequently there are still two sides lobbying every major issue.

    The one thing the U.S. has going for it is patriotism. Rich or poor, black or white, educated or ignorant -- almost every American believes his/her country is the greatest country on earth, and is prepared to defend America and its interests to the death if required. Every American truly believes that if someone really wants to make something of themselves that that can and will be achieved. Every American knows that you can fight city hall if your case is just, and generally believes that justice will be served when called upon -- and if it isn't it will the second time or the third time. Patriotism isn't just born in you -- it's instilled and when it's as instilled as it is in Americans its because there is a good reason.

    Almost none of that is true in many parts of the world, and little of that is true in Singapore where you can never be sure of justice if it isn't in the government's interest. So before you slag the US, take a look around you. Imperfect as America is, if you have the deep conviction that you are as prepared to defend your country and beliefs as almost every American is -- then you can walk the walk without just talking the talk.
