    It is good to be known as a democratic country to get a better reputation and international community respect and recognition.

    Whether we are a democratic country can be assessed from the way we conduct the election, control freedoms of media reporting, accountability for ministers' failures or mistakes in implementing government policies etc and liberal use of defamation laws to sue the political opponents for speeches made during election, or control of internet and blogs to curb freedoms of expressions of opinions and views by the citizens (last freedom left since the press and public media are controlled and prohibited from so-called journalistic crusading).
