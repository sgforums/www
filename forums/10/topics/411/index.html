    Okay, its our home made assault rifle vs the German one. I checked through the available tech. specifications and got the following comparisons.

    G-36 Cyclic rate is 750RPM, vs SAR-21's 650 RPM max.

    In terms of Length, the Vanilla SAR-21 w/o stands about 805mm.

    G-36 = 998mm
    G-36k = 860mm
    G-36C = 720mm

    SAR-21 weight, w/o addons is 3.82kg.

    Vs G-36 = 3.6kg, G-36k = 3.3kg, G-36C = 2.8kg.

    The G-36 series seem much lighter, and equally ass capable as the SAR-21. Personally, I'd sure like to be running around with a 2.8kg weapon. =) A whole KG lighter than the SAR-21.

Again, while I would think it would slow down *slightly* the shooter compared to the ease spring, it would make it easier for lefties. To those who haven't tried it, let me tell you, I can really see how having both eyes open while shooting could make a difference when using the weapon

Food for thought?
