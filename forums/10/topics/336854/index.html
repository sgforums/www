    Lesson learnt from Ah Bian : Absolute Power comes with Absolute Responsibilities. Integrity of a person  determines how 'perfect' any system can be.

    But who can ensure a person will hold himself accountable?

    I hope the Apathetic people of the world think about this and not remain naive any longer.

    TW island, unlike some places, has a strong opposition presence and culture and history. Also, the accused is an Ex-Presidente.There is a lot of rights and privileges bestowed on an ex. Also, the alleged crimes involved are many many and complex involving many top officials. Financial meltdown also could be a factor.

    This espisode proved beyond any doubt that TW democracy has progressed some more. Even an ex-president, having so much privilege can still be locked up given hardcore evidence. Its easy to criticise anything, anyone. The fact remains, TW boleh! TW has locked up an ex-President.
