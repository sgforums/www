    Originally posted by tranquilice:
    Is there any penalty for not casting a vote? (ie. Fail to turn up to vote)
    Yes, but it is not a big deal. The penalty is that your name will be removed from the voters' register and you will not be able to vote in the future. You will also not be able to stand as a candidate in subsequent parliamentary and presidential elections.

    You can apply to have your name reinstated. However, unless you can show that you had a valid reason for not voting, you will need to pay $5.

    Source: 'Voting is Compulsory', Elections Department of Singapore Website.
