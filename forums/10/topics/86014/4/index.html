The huge radar above the hull is probably another fire control radar? The phased array normally consists of 4 panels mounted in the radar tower.

Aegis is powerful because the phased array radars can track multiple targets simultaneously, something that most fire control radars are limited by the speed of the servo-motor systems and the number of radars itself.
