    He is really too obsessed with his self-glory. It is as if only he alone has all the answers to all the problems and issues facing this little red dot.

    With his intelligence and learning he should well have realised that greatness of leadersship for centuries of human history is never achieved through his style of autocracy, arrogance, obsessiveness of his power, selfishness and shenanigan or fixing people or shutting out of dissenting views or oppositions.

    What is wrong with alternatives like empowering people, through unleashing their potential, letting oppositions taking a part in nation building and being accountable to people in all areas of services to the people.

    What will be ruined if oppositions are voted in to check on such obsessiveness and stop all his policies aimed at selfishly preserving his power like taxing, hoarding and profiteering at the expense of the people.

    What is wrong with letting his other younger 2nd generation and 3rd generation leaders to grow and create a more motivational system of running the country with full participation of all and with full accountability to the people in governing the country.

    What is wrong with oppositions asking for greater openness and democracy and get the people to vote more righteous and people-centred government and eventually passing back the initiative and stake back to the people which is the only sure way of inspiring the people to work together to build a more united country? A house divided is a house in doom.

    Has he not done enough harm already for the past 20 years of interferring with the growth of pro-people collective leadership?

    By refusing to endorse and fully implement such open and accountable and collective leadership to inspire his hardworking people to strive for greater height his ill repute will be deeply interred in his bone after death despite his initial contributions.

    28 Jun 08, 09:44

    (Myhope for LKY)

    LKY is the single obstacle to our ongoing progress. We could have arrived at being a greater country if we had put our resources to work smartly for the past 20 years without his top-down selfish alienation of the people dividing them into elites and non-elites rich and poor. He is attempting to convert the whole of middle class and lower middle class citizens into more useless goons by his type of policies. That is the reason now many citizens can no longer start a small business ending up as useless goons waiting to drive a taxi or clean the table.

    By his constant housing tax on low-cost housing and double- and triple- vehicle tax alone without solutions to problems he has been driving up the costs of doing business by taxing and hoarding and profiteering way of running this country that no one or very few can start a small business to keep the economy running or competitive.

    By pushing up the costs of doing business, we are now a down-grade moribund economy drifting to find a small window of survival and easily driven into recessions in the slightest world downturn.

    What can we do to make the economy competitive or stay in business?

    Two solutions: (1) For big businesses like GLCs - increase fees and charges with excuse like "no free lunch

    (2) Ministers stop all the LKY-style of taxing and hoarding monies in running the country.
    Ministers are appointed from people with experiences in running the whole business structuring and revamping total business strategies of calibre like Dr. Winsemius Goh Keng Swee or Ooi Hong Leong or Stanley Shih not those who only carry balls or bags or favors to charge higher costs like maboro tan.

    Find a more capable group of ministers who have the overall knowledge and command and ability and get them to be working furiously on restructuring the economy to a value-adding economy as Goh Keng Swee has done. Targets: created the latest IT inventions to tap into the global super-personal-communications business, mastering the micro-chip or wafer fab new technology applications and converting the entire energy system to solar-based and wind-based system for street lighting and lesser-powered practical technology.

    He should stop hiking property and selling lands to make profits for HDB or URA at the expense of citizens as such property play finally ends up impoverishing the ordinary people benefiting only a few rich and the powerful elites.

    Get real and start to work the economy based on motivating all citizens and not just the rich and the elites.

    This is my hope for LKY if he is still a good leader and not trying to cover up his mistakes with more suing of people.
