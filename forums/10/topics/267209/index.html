    If people do not vote for them where is the government?

    Government is merely a group of citizens employed by the people to look after certain affairs of state. Government when elected is only the employee or servants of the people. At the most, it is only the trustee where funds and assets belonging to the people need to be looked after as part of their duties and responsibilities.

    So if ever you are employed by the government you are actually employed by the people and the government merely uses the people's monies to employ you the citizen.

    They use citizens' fund to employ citizens who will have to work if so employed not for government sake but for the people. If government does anything wrongful, employees of government has the right to tell them off and do what is right for the people in their own perception.
