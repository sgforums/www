    The Annuity for Others (and Maybe, just Maybe Me) Scheme

    It is like having several credit cards, and having your purchase points spread out over all of them. You have points, but not enough in anyone of them to redeem anything.

    Pension should kick in at the 'official' retirement age, 62, 65 or 67.
    We should not be discussing whether age 85 or 80, at all.

    Medical insurance and annuities are to be bought privately, at own option.
    Its your own responsibility.

    Those rich or lazy who do work and therefore do not have CPF accounts, they are on their own.
