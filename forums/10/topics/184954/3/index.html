    dear alien2

    The new Enlistment Act which was passed on 3rd April 2006 requires all male Singapore citizens and Permanent Residences of age 13 and above to apply for an Exit Permit from Mindef if they are planning to go overseas for 3 months or longer.

    Therefore that boy who is 14 will be required to apply for an exit permit if he is planning to stay overseas for 3 months or longer. If his application is successful, then everything will be fine. However, if he does not apply for the exit permit and continues to stay overseas for more than 3 months; then he and his parents have commited an offence in which both parents would be fined to a maximum of S$2000.

    Regarding him being arrested at Changi, I do not think that will happen. But what I am sure is that his parents would be fined.

    Hope this is helpful.

    Cheers
