As for people unless they are enemy agents, do not use ISA against them or to threaten them.

    If our leaders can learn to be more broadminded a lot of all these grouses and complaints in the NKF will disappear but because they insisted on being righteous or legalistic without they themselves showing willingness to be righteous and following rule of laws in the GRC and defamation suit, they are getting a lot of back lash.

    Change. They will win the respect of all citizens if they can do their job with broadmindedness and less sensitivities. "Da Tong and Xiao Kan" (Nation First Self Last) applies to politicians as well as ordinary citizens.
