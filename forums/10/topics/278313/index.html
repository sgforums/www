Are the regulars of SC whiners?

  • Rock^Star
    7 Sep 07, 19:13
    Debates over the past years with forumites have led me to think: "Are we whiners or are we just sharing our views out of love for the country?

    Many have called us whiners. Well, you all know who.

    A whiner in my opinion is someone who has suffered injustice AND rants for the sake of ranting.

    Many regulars are here to contribute by sharing the truth. We keep ourselves updated about the political and socio-economic changes in Singapore. We post excerpts, articles and links to meaningful political blogs.

    So do we rant for the sake of ranting? I beg to differ. We "rant" for a cause because we do not want this country where we were brought up to be ruined by a narcissistic party. AND we back it up. We are here to spread the word.

    Many also think we are losers but are we? I work out of Singapore and I am not affected by GST hikes. Yet, it makes my blood boil to see every solution to every problem as a price increase. Many of us here are also professionals in our own right.

    You judge.
