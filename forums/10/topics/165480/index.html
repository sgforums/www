    I presume that there might be an election coming soon.

    We should seriously consider voting for the opposition parties.

    It's not that PAP is really that bad. They have done well in some situations but badly in others. The main reason is that there isn't enough "alternate" voices in the parliment to speak up for the general public.

    PAP, without strong opposition parties, has grown too proud and conceited for the good of S'pore.

    Most of us just say there isn't enough good opposition parties around for them to consider. I think it is not entirely true. We should try attending the various meetings held by the opposition parties as well as check out their websites to know exactly what they are doing. There are really some good and serious candidates in opposition parties!

    Over-throwing PAP is definitely not a good idea as they have very good experience in governing S'pore. But to have more people checking on the ruling party is what we desperately need!

    Give them a chance so that we in turn will have a better future in S'pore.
