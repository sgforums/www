    From Collins Dictionary of Economics:

    per capita income see INCOME PER HEAD

    income per head or per capita income

    the GROSS NATIONAL PRODUCT (GNP) of a country divided by its POPULATION. Income per head gives a more general indication of a country's monetary STANDARD OF LIVING than its absolute level of GNP as Fig. 87, overleaf, shows, although the USA has a higher absolute level of GNP than Japan, its income per head is much lower. However, an income per head measure of people's standard of living is itself also flawed as an indicator of typical living standards because, in practice, the distribution of income is not equal and non-monetary elements of lifestyle quality differ as between different people.
