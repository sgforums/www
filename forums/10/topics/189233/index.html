    Well....

    This is it.

    It has been a nice dream....the past 9 days.
    We hear the speeches, we saw the cross-fire, we were inspired by the idealism and courage, and we heard the things we want to hear someone tell the Govt being told.....all the things that doesn't happen often in SG.

    For a moment there, it almost felt that a real change is actually happening in Singapore. it almost felt like this time, it's not going to be the same, that this time it's gonna make a difference.

    Almost.



    Tmrw is polling day. And tomorrow night, the results are going to be out.
    I feel like i am holding a lottery ticket and waiting for the results.
    In some ways, i wish the results will never be known so that I can continue to dream and hope, of all the things that's going to be different should I win the lottery.
    I wish the results will never be out because i also know that chances are, it's just another false hope.

    Chances are, the results are not going to be that big a surprise from previous elections.

    Well, it was fun while it lasted.

    Let's look forward to the next mid-spring festival 5 years later. Hope i still get a chance to vote then.
