    Whilst in BMT, I took up the Figher Pilots Program, which consist of Medical, Coordination test and 2 interviews.

    Started off with 32 candidates. before medical screening starts, 2 had been kicked out. After Medical, 9 more were booted. After 5 hrs of computer test at CMPB, further 15 got kicked out. I managed to hold out to the remaining 6 where during the interview where I sat upon a panel of 3 Interviewees in which 1 of them is Liet Col., a Capt, psychologist.

    Didn't quite make it when they asked whether I prefer Commercial or Air force. I said I prefer commercial but don't mind airforce. It was only then I realise how dumb I was. By the way. You only have 1 chance. If you fail the Fighter Pilots Program, you cannot retake ever in your life.

    Good luck
