Writings were all over the wall along the corridor, indicating the loansharkÂ’s contact number. As the police are all along aware that the debts came from our previous owners, I was greatly disappointed that till now, nothing effective was carried out and no assistance was given to us. Every time we lodged a police report on the loansharkÂ’s harassment, we were told that investigations will be carried out and that requires time. But on our side, every single day spells danger and we are constantly living in fear. It seems like all the investigations led to nowhere as history repeats itself once again.

    We do understand the large volume of similar cases the CID are handling, but what we are facing now are syndicates who fears nothing of the law because till date, it is not an offence in Singapore to borrow money from illegal lenders. We can never get to the root of the problem if nothing is changed about punishing borrowers for their act. As the victims, we felt the frustration and anger because we have to bear the consequences of things we did not do, and even the police are unable to give us a solution.
