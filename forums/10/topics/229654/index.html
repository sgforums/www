    Statements 1) and 2) are your personal opinions of the food, and so ought to be okay. I've seen far more scathing food reviews than that, though I think you might want to leave out that part about the c*ckroach powder.

    Statement 3) may be construed as defamatory by some more sensitive people. Maybe saying that you don't like his attitude is fine, but saying that he's an 'ass-face' definitely isn't.

    Bottom line, if you ain't comfortable with it, then you may not want to post it.

    Defamation more likely than libel as a cause of action. In order to prove that what was said above is not defamatory, there are some available defences. Its unlikely that a blog is able to rely on a defence of privileged publication so the only defence is that the words used were true in substance and in fact.
