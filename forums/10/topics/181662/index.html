    THE PERKS TO BEING A SUBSCRIBER OF ST INTERACTIVE

    ST Interactive has been a free-access site since 1995. From Mar 20, It will become a subscription website. Subscription rates will depend on whether the subscriber is already buying the print edition of the newspaper. Below we compare what readers are getting before with what subscribers will get after March 20:

    BEFORE The site offered only a three-day archive of its webpages. This means that on Feb 1, 2005, a Tuesday, you would have been able to search only as far back as Jan 29, a Saturday.
    AFTER MARCH 20

    The archive will become a seven-day one. This means that on April 1, 2005, a Friday, you'll be able to search back as far as March 25, the previous Friday.

    BEFORE All reports in the Money section were accessible only after 6pm Singapore time.
    AFTER MARCH 20
    All reports in the Money section will be live on the site from 6am
    Singapore time, along with the rest of the material on the site.

    BEFORE Only three reports from Life! and Sunday Life were available.
    AFTER MARCH 20
    All reports from Life! and Sunday Life Â– your source of info on showbiz and lifestyle Â– will be available online.

    BEFORE The tech weekly magazine Digital Life was available online Â– but only from 2pm on Wednesday, a day and a half after its print cousin was distributed with the newspaper.
    AFTER MARCH 20
    Digital Life goes online from 6am on Tuesday, the same day its print
    edition is distributed with the newspaper.

    BEFORE The health weekly magazine Mind Your Body and the fashion weekly magazine Urban were not available online.
    AFTER MARCH 20
    Both these magazines will be available online to STI subscribers from 6am on the days they are distributed with the newspaper, that is, Wednesdays for Mind Your Body and Thursdays for Urban. (Urban will go online on Thursdays later this year.)
