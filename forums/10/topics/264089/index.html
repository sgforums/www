    Jack Sparrow was bewildered. "Who the hell are you? What do you care where I'm moving to?"

    "Malaysian people love you, Abang Jack. They've sent me to invite you to stay in Malaysia. Best place to live in the world!" Loud Mouth explained enthusiastically. He couldn't help but think how many votes he is going to get if he manages to bring back Jack Sparrow.

    Jack Sparrow frowned. "Malaysia? Heard little of it. Tell me how it's better than Singapore, and I shall consider."

    "Okay, first, everything is so transparent in Singapore. Feel so naked la. In Malaysia, you could do so many things, and there are many ways for cover-ups."

    "Cover-ups, eh?" Jack Sparrow's eyes brighten, "Okay, go on."

    "In Malaysia, nobody dare to criticize me...err.. us, because they are so scared! So we could basically get away with doing anything we like!"

    " Something like that. Oh yeah, and..."Loud Mouth looked around to check if there's anyone eavesdropping, "We'll be spending millions sending some astronaut to the space, but if you're interested, we could replace him with you!"

    "Pirates in the Outer Space eh? Hmm...interesting.... But I know nothing about doing scientific experiments for you guys, mate."

    "Who says it's really about science? Just carry our flag, take a picture, make the people feel "boleh", and the whole nation will idolize you lah. Got it?"

    "All right, you convinced me!" Jack Sparrow jumped at the opportunity, and whipped out a cellphone, "Let me call Kuan Yew to tell him I'm not coming anymore. Looks like Malaysia's more an interesting place than the casinos in Singapore, eh?"
