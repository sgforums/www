    Democrats invite opposition parties for forum on election reform
    15 Nov 07

    15 November 2007

    The Hon Mr Chiam See Tong MP
    Chairman
    Singapore People's Party

    Mr J B Jeyaretnam
    18A Smith Street
    Singapore

    The Hon Mr Low Thia Kiang MP
    Secretary-General
    Workers' Party

    Mr Mohd Ali Aman
    Secretary-General
    PKMS

    Mr Sebastian Teo
    President
    National Solidarity Party

    Dear Sirs,

    I hope this letter finds you well.

    I would like to invite you and other opposition party and civil society leaders to speak at a public forum. Entitled "Reform of the Election System in Singapore" we hope to use the occasion to discuss what can be done to remedy the deficiencies of our electoral process.

    As you know, a genuinely free and fair election system benefits not just the opposition but also, and more important, the people whom we serve.

    I would like to propose 13 January 2007, Sunday, for the forum. If you find the date inconvenient, please suggest an alternative and I will try to coordinate with the other invitees to find a mutually convenient date for all.

    In the meantime we will book a venue for the meeting and will inform you of the details as soon as they become available.

    I hope that you will respond favourably to this invitation and look forward to hearing from you.

    Sincerely,

    Chee Soon Juan
    Secretary-General
    Singapore Democratic Party
