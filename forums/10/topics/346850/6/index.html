    I agree. I am not into PAP bashing, or criticise the ruling party just for the sake of it. But I believe in the saying that "absolute power corrupts absolutely". History has proven this correct. However, well intentioned the original aim was, over time human nature takes a selfish road because there is an intrinsic self-preservation mode built into human pysche.

    Look at the communists - the original intention was to get rid of abuse and unfairness by the rich, the royal and the landed. But in the end, what happens? After the so-called class struggle - there are still the rich, the royal and the landed (the new communist ruling elite).  But they are so entrenched,  no one dared questioned them for decades!

    Remember the book "Animal Farm" by George Orwell? In the book, the animals revolted against the farmers but in the end, among them, the pigs brainwashed the rest of the animals so that the animals give up their will, rights and liberty. 

    So while PAP has done much for Singapore in the past, and had generally been an efficient government, that does not mean that they will remain so. The future belongs to us - not a single political party. We must have alternative voices to keep not just PAP but any future ruling party in check - because absolutely power corrupts absolutely.

    Multi-party systems in other countries may be more messy, but we have to live with that for liberty. Quoting a Christian scripture (which is also a value shared by all other religions) - "man does not live by bread alone." Thus, material wealth aka a strong economy while important, is on its own not enough for a good life. It is not a good enough justification to agree to chain down your human spirit, your creative mind, your independant thoughts, your integrity. Man is meant to be free.
