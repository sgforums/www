Free supply of tea or coffee.  I can say that during my 2 yrs there, all my course mate really put in 101% in the lessons. But of course, sure have a few rotten apples. But not like what TS mention above-----skip class etc etc so serious.

     

    After the 1st year of our course, one third of the class fail their exams and lan lan kena kick out. But they still can received their NTC-3 ( which is one 1 yr course ). NTC-2 is a 2yrs course and only take in those with min GCE-O lvls. Those with N lvls can only enrol in NTC-3 then pass the one yr course before enrol in the 2nd yr with us. I can say even the weakest students also put in much effort to pass whatever test/exams which are throw to us. For what reasons?????--------> Fighting for merit ( which is a direct enrol to Poly ). Only awarded to the top 2 students in the class. No need to take any entrance test. Too bad I only missed that mark by a few points.
