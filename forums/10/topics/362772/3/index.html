    I am in the Civil service Line. It is really unfair that i don't get my deserved bonus. I work in Singapore Sports Council. I serve the public who are not always polite and civil.     Then there are folks who get angry at us for the Badmiton court bookings, they call us to book courts, then show up late without making payment  expecting their courts to be there despite us telling them that they have to pay up  latest within 15 mins before their playing time or else their courts will be given out to whoever requests it over the counter.

     

    Ours is not a 8 to 5.30 job. I start work at 6.45 am and finish at 10.15 pm on certain days without Lunch or dinner breaks, when we don't have enough manpower. The management is more worried about Public complaining there is noone in the counter is service them then let their staff go for Lunch or dinner breaks.

     

    We work 6 days a week, including sats, Sun and Public Holiday when many Government places close, we are still open. I did not even take MC for one yr, only to get this kind of news.

     

    We are not getting our Bonus despite Singapore is getting revenue in Sports, like Asian Youth games, Youth Olympics and F1 Night race. Anyone reading this, i request humbly, not to verbally abuse us  for doing our jobs. We are who are also humans. We do our jobs based on our guidelines. I came across many places where i got poor service but will not lash out cause it creates only bad energy and the next person waiting to be served would not get top class service
