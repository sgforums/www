    I've been to some friends and relatives HDB homes where they have a live-in maid.

    Most of the maids live in very sad conditions as most do not have a room to sleep in and end up sleeping in the kitchen or something.

    Most of the maids we now get in Singapore are not even Filippinos as they are too expensive. Most of the Filippino maids are lively, clever (some highly educated) and most importantly, quite modern.

    Most people now have maids from the poorest parts of Sri Lanka, Indonesia and I don't know what else. Most of them probably do not even have running water back home. There's nothing wrong with these people except that they do not belong with a modern, fastidious Singaporean family living in a HDB flat!

    Of course most Singaporeans would find these maids unhygienic, underperforming or "stupid" etc simply becos they haven't much clue about the modern world. And most keep their maids working long hours without any contact with their own friends or relatives, so most of the maids just slowly go bonkers as everyday they feel isolated and treated less and less human.

    So they start to rebel in small ways like disobedience, breaking things etc and this causes the irate SG women to push even harder and eventually, the abuse starts.

    I've lived in HK where a U-grad Filippino maid comes to clean my flat once a week for SGD 40 for 3 hours' work.

    In the six years I ived there, there were many incidences of husbands murdering wives and cutting up family members etc but almost no sadistic abuse of maids. Why? HK is an even more high pressure society.

    There are of course, the occasional rape or molest - but these are perpetuated by men for very different reasons. What I did not come across was the instances of slow, methodical torture and dehumanisation of live-in maids carrried out by HK women.

    There are many speculative reasons but I think the main reason is the Filippinos themselves. Most are well-educated, all are irrepressibly lively and all have a very strong mutual support network. Simply-put, you would think twice to abuse a maid in HK.

    I really hope people with maids will remember to be human. I treat my cats better than some people treat their maids.
