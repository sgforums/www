  • robertteh
    9 Dec 05, 21:40
    US$150 billions of reserve and that is not enough. More taxes, double charging on state lands and triple charging on vehicle ownership and more fare increases. Our tax-and-recover system of government will just keep charging the people and build its own reserve.

    But will the US$150 billions of reserve be put to good use to lessen people's medical care or lower costs of living or doing business? Or will it be put to build more of the 7 wonders or grandiose marine platform which cannot create jobs or increase our economic competitiveness? Or will it be used to pay more salaries and bonuses based on claims of success in collecting revenues from people like happening in NKF.

    If US$150 billions of reserve are divided into two portions and one portion is set up as endowments to generate interests which is channeled to lessen costs of living or avoid further utility or medical hikes, why should the people perpetually suffer more fee increases and why should there be all the double charging on lands and triple charging on vehicles? Without defining the purpose of reserve, it is absurd to keep building up reserves. That is the reason why NKF is incurring so much anger. Only the numbskulled and thick-skinned and the self-centred cannot see such anger.
