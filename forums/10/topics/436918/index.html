Your vote is not secret

  • Punggol meegoreng
    27 Aug 11, 14:57

    Casted my vote just then.

    You have to mark attendance 1st just before you are handed the voting slip.

    The attendance list have the serial number (same s/no as in the card mailed to you) and your IC number. The voting slip have your serial number ! The ballot box (eg. A1) are the area or block you belong to. Anybody notice that ?

    Some years back, a close colleague of mine, one batch my junior, told me his batch boy had joined another department. His batch boy called him up and asked him who he voted for and why. Tried to get information and so forth. At the end of the call, he was told not to tell anybody about this. That close collegue go to tell everybody he could. Confidential ?

     It is such arrangement that irk many people.
