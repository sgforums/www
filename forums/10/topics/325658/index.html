    Why Chee's sister denied Stanford visit
    I REFER to last Saturday's article, ''No' to Stanford trip for Chee's sister'. It contains a number of serious errors. It is regrettable that The Straits Times did not seek to verify the facts with us, unlike Lianhe Zaobao which did verify, and therefore published a more accurate article.
