Is Minister Lui's cyber counter-insurgency successful?

  • robertteh
    15 Apr 08, 23:00

    PM Lee again repeated that he would debunk the many lies told by people in many blogs, forums or podcasts. So about two years ago he sent his ministers like Lui Tuck Yew and Ng Eng Hen to counter the lies told in the internet in many sites including youngpap, sgforums, sammyboy etc.

    Were the armies of counter-insurgency agents successful in debunking the netizens' views about NKF, Shin Corp, Merryl Lynch and the paying of another round of Ministers' super-salary increments soon the GST was increased in the name of helping the poor last year.

    Go and read the posts of cyber-agents with nicks like salman, bigcannonking, wy47, om or lately singlemalcollector (forumsoneasia) and you would no doubt appreciate the beauty of some of the most uncouth languages used in slamming people as whiners, crybabies or ungrateful quitters.

    Of course PM Lee after these cyber-countering disasters could not say that he has lost debates on government policies to these netizens as that would be bad for face.

    With such loss of face he ought to change tune by not talking about debunking them as he had proudly claim he could but is now trying to say something different like it is a reality to live with or govt could not really rebut all of them in the net.

    Such cyber-counter-insurgency attempt will certainly fail because the netizens are not the simplistic lot easily deceived by some ****-and-bull stories like "no free lunch" or "no one will be left behind" or "foreign talents will create more jobs for citizens" craps often told often in the past by their scheming masters out to deceive simple folks in showing ceremonies and campaigns.

    Spending of part of ministers' or civil servants' time who are actually paid by people from the public payroll is certainly illegal use of people funds.

    Surely the citizens have a case for asking refund of salaries from such people as such people being paid out of public payroll should not spend time away from their jobs in their respective ministries and waste time in countering their people's political evolution pr participation to upgrade to a long-term sustainable democracy to prevent the constant abuse of power as posted.

    If anything agents like singlemaltcollector has only aroused immediate resentments and revulsions for their oft-bombastic boast of language ability, life style or intellect with outright slamming of netizens' poems or writings as childish or naive.

    These agents are only antagonising netizens as whiners or crybabies or ungrateful or quitters wannabes like they way the leader belittled Chiam See Tong as lacking enough of "A"s in his "O" level results.

    Beware too of technique like pretending to be sympathetic or sharing of their thought on alternative policies or solutions hoping to catch your support to walk the middle path.

    With the States Times, the brainwashing process might consist of putting up ghost writers to publish pro-establishment news or analysis but with the internet the indoctrination process is going to end up hurting the ruling elites the next time round.

    So friends do stay the course and fear no knock at the door in the middle of the night as long as you are serving as active citizens or people's ultimate angel warrior to protect the sanctified interest.
