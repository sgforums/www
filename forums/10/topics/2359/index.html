    Ever ask why the US marines and US army fights with different tactics in mind??

    The US marines goes around rushing and attacks relentlessly with the objective of penetrating as far as they can go.

    While the US army fights and hold the ground... clear any remnants left behind by the marines.

    I consider both strategy as complementary...

    the US marines tactic enables rapid strike, depriving the enemy of rest, unbalance the enemy's plan and defence strategy by hitting the enemy and constantly changing the situation. this complicates enemy planning especially if the enemy has a rigid command system where all orders come from the top.

    The marines objective is simple, KEEP MOVING... KEEP HITTING and KEEP up the pressure on the enemy until either the enemy crack or the marines went out of steam.

    But this strategy alone by the marines tax the limits of the marines logistics and manpower... in short... this strategy cannot apply for long without rest. eventually, the marines will also get tired and they will start making mistakes and if the enemy has a huge reserve military units, they could wait until the marines got tired and then move in.. with their fresh backup force..

    So... any ordinary general could see how to beat the marines and this strategy obviously cannot work alone.

    Enter the US army with its complementary strategy to save the marines butt. the US army move into area cleared by the marines and set up defences and ensure that the marines logistic lines remains safe and free-moving. The army will follow where the marines went and continue to fortify defences all along the way.

    When the marines gets tired or face an insurmountable force.. they pull back and let the US army deal with the enemy hot on their heals.. since the US army didnt fight most of the time and has ample time to prep their defences.. fresh and well-supplied.. the US army will withered any enemy units trying to attack the US marines (which will now be sleeping behind the US army lines).

    Once the US army significantly weakens any enemy attacks and pile up huge losses for the enemy... in comes the reckless marines again.

    So... the 2 forces of the US armed forces is liken to the sword and the shield. the marines are the sword while the army is the shield, spiked shield if u like. US army special force like SEAL and DELTA force plus para units are like the flying daggers.

    In SAF... the guards are the sword, the infantry and armour is the shield. the commando and the special force are the flying daggers.

    So... the armor must fight in conjuction with other units like infantry, engineers, scouts, arty and other units. the armor are not expected to lead an assault, neither are they expected to fight on their own.

    3. the infantry is guarding the armor's flank, so no no to sneak attack at bottleneck.

    4. the engineers are on hand to convenietly remove the traps, mine field or whatever along the way before the armor arrives.
