    Wanted: Your views on annual wage guidelines

     

    The National Wages Council, which will come up with wage guidelines for the year ahead, is looking for feedback from the public.

    The council, comprising a tripartite committee of officials, unionists and employers, will meet this month and next month.

       In drawing up the annual guidelines, it will consider factors such as Singapore's economic performance and the domestic and global economic outlook.

       The country's economic competitiveness, labour market conditions, inflation and productivity growth will also be taken into account.

       As part of the council's suite of wage recommendations last year, it had called on companies to give a minimum monthly wage hike of $60 to employees drawing a basic monthly salary of $1,000 or less.

       Those wishing to share their views may e-mail nwc_secretariat@mom.gov.sg by April 25.
