    Response from MOM pertaining to the supervisor slapping his co-worker incidence. [Alert] The Ministry of Manpower (MOM) has been notified via email today (20 May 2013) of an online video depicting someone who appeared to be physically abused by another. The person who recorded the video has contacted MOM and we have alerted the Police. As updated by the Police, the victim has lodged a police report. Employers are expected to treat their employees fairly and with respect. In situations where employees feel that they are unfairly treated or threatened, they should report to their supervisor/HR, or if unionised, they should seek advice and assistance from their union. If the threat involves physical harm, the employees should report this to the Police immediately. https://www.facebook.com/sgministryofmanpower
