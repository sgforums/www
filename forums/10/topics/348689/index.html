    Calling for volunteers to prepare for the coming General Elections

    Dear readers,

    As some of you may already know, Mediacorp made a Freudian slip yesterday by showing a page with the title - “General Elections 2009″, while the content still remains on 2006 election.  (The page has since been removed).

    We reckon that there is a likelihood that the elections will be held during the March or June holidays.

    The Wayang Party Club will be setting up a special General Elections blog to house all articles, podcasts and video recordings of opposition rallies.

    Our aim is only ONE: to help the opposition, regardless of which party, to gun down a GRC. Therefore we will be hedging our bets on the “A” team.

    We will pour in all our resources to flood the GRC with flyers and VCDs everyday from nomination day onwards to counter the mainstream media propaganda.

    Do not worry about the cost for we have more than enough money to fund the entire campaign on our own without the need to solicit for external contributions.

    To enable us to maximize our impact, we seriously need the following manpower:

    1. Video personnel with their own camrecorders and video editors to record the rally speeches and upload to youtube on the spot.

    2. Photographers.

    3. Writers.

    4. Volunteers on the ground to distribute flyers and VCDs.

    We cannot afford to wait till the date of election is announced to start the preparations for it will be too late by then. Our only hope lies in prising one GRC away from the PAP.

    Once a GRC falls, more Singaporeans will be willing to join the opposition to create a domino effect to topple their despotic rule eventually.

    Our dear comrades, let us not wait for another 5 years to teach these suckers a lesson.

    Let’s do it RIGHT NOW !

    Please email us at wayangparty@hotmail.com if you are keen to join our team of citizen journalists and grassroots volunteers for the coming general election.

    THE EDITORIAL TEAM
