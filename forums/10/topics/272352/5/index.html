    The company must satisfy all of the following conditions in order to qualify for a claim for a DTR:
    a. The company is resident in Singapore for the relevant basis year;
    b. Tax has been paid or is payable on the same income in the foreign country in accordance with the provisions of the DTA; and
    c. The income must be remitted or deemed to be remitted into Singapore and is subject to tax in Singapore.
