PM and MM Lee glared at him, while President Nathan tucked into his meal happily. When President Nathan was halfway eating his food, he stood up, still with food in his mouth, and started singing "thats the way a ha a ha I like it a ha a ha, thats the way a ha a ha I like it a ha a ha..." He was singing and dancing, and PM Lee told him to "stop it" while MM Lee told him to "shut up." President Nathan showed a black face but they didnt bother, because his face was always black.
