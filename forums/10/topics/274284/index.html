    the 'address sticker' that you talk about is actually a controlled form, an official document. it has serial numbers, and one can trace the particulars of the one who requested the address change simply by looking at the serial number of the sticker. if it really has been done, then tracing the person who made the address change is as easy as ABC.

    not anyone can gain access to these forms. only ICA and Police Stations stock these forms, and they are not to be taken out of such places.

    anyway, registering a false address is an offence punishable by a maximum fine of $5000 and prison sentence of 5 years. and this is just one charge of many that the perpetrator can face.
