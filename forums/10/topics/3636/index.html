    Hi!

    I was from a manpower dept in airforce, so I know how the ranking is done.

    Basically, there is a very systematic way of ranking personnels for promotion. It is based on performance ranking and of course recommendations from officers (sorry, don't think I can give more details. supposed to be confidential). Because everybody wants to be promoted, they have to choose some over others, and yes, sometimes if the officer fights hard enough, the man will get the promotion. Sometimes, it is because some guy has been in his rank for 'quite some time', and he gets promoted over another guy who has the same ranking but younger in age.

    I guess the bottomline is that the system tries to be fair, but it relies too much on arbitration and judgement of officers...
