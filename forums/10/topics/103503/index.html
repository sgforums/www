Referendum for Casino

  • goh meng seng
    20 Nov 04, 00:46
    One of my helpers have suggested for us to press for a referendum for the issue of Casino.

    No matter how we look at it, this is about trading social well being for economic gains. Whether this is acceptable to us will affect us in the years to come.

    The impact on the society would vary but it is important for Singaporeans at large to decide whether they would agree to have casino or not, with the potential threats on social order and well being.

    Thus, it is time for us, the citizens to reclaim our power and right to decide on issues that might have wide impact on us as a Nation and a people.

    I suggest we hold the referendum on the casino issue in the coming general elections, to save cost. This is also good in the sense that at least for those constituencies that do not have the chance to vote, they could still exercise their rights of ownership of this place we call Singapore.

    Goh Meng Seng

    Disclaimer: This is not Workers' Party's Official stand yet, I am just opening up this issue for everybody to discuss.
