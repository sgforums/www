    Originally posted by qlqq9:
    Just have a guide like part timers will have to work a maximum of such and such hours to be entitle bonus. The Bonus should be just one amount.
    If you do that, you then risk disgruntlement amongst the part time staff. People management is always tricky. Say the minimum for part-timers to qualify is 1000 Hours a year. 1 of 2 things can happen. The staff will stop working when he hits 1k hours, or work for 2 companies with 1k hr requirement to get bonus.

    Or staff a works 1100 hours that year, another staff works 2200 hours that year. You pay the same amount, you risk having dissatisfaction within the ranks.

    Many other scenarios also. Like logistics parttimers who are paid per delivery made etc.. which essentially in same cases, are private sub-contractors of the logistics firm. that again is another can of worms.

    Not saying it can't be done tho. Just that is going to take considerable effort on the part of the paying employer. Especially companies with many different types of part-timers.
