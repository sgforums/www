    Here are a few debating tips :

    1 Think ahead of your opponent.
    Expect what response you will get from your posting and start organising your defence early. Do a feint, show a weakness and draw them close for a knock out punch.

    2 Control the pace of the debate.
    Do not rush into firing a reply before you are sure of what to say. Take your time to reply.

    3 Vary the pace of the debate
    That will disrupt your opponent's train of thought and expose their weakness. Turn up the heat if necessary but maintain your cool at all times.

    4 Give credit when your opponent raise a good point.
    Nobody is right all the time. Be gracious and kind.

    5 Ignore the opponent when they are raving madly.
    Stick to your own points, give more examples and reiterate your stand, reinforce your position. Let them shoot themselves in the foot.

    6 Keep a second window open for search.
    There are tons of info online. It only takes a few seconds to do a simple search. You need facts to back you up.

    7 Read widely.
    Keep abreast of current affairs, read international mags like newsweek and time. Read works by contemporary authors. Read widely in different fields such as law and economics.

    Hope the above points help.
