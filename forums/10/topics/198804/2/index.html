    Actually I think there is a simple reason why the press in Singapore can never be "free of government influence". The main reason it remains controlled is because Singaporeans do not bother that it has been controlled. How often does one read the newspapers and wonder "am I getting the whole story?". Only when Singaporeans truly realise this, and the importance of correcting this, then we will start heading towards the direction of having free press in Singapore.
