    BRIDGES COMPONENTS
    The project also consists of the construction of road and railway bridges to replace the Causeway. The removal ofthe Causeway will be followed by the dredging of a navigation channel to allow passage of vessels.

    RELOCATION OF WATER MAINS
    Existing Water Mains, which are routed along Jalan Bukit Chagar, have been relocated to facilitate the construction of the CIQ Complex, while the Water Mains along Jalan Jim Quee have been relocated to facilitate the construction of JB Sentral and internal roads. In addition to the diversion of the major Water Mains, other SAJ Water Mains serving the surrounding areas have also been diverted and upgraded to serve the additional needs of the Integrated CIQ Complex and ancillary buildings.

    The Water Mains running along the Causeway will be replaced with submarine pipes, and where necessary, the pipes will be protected by armour protection units. Cathodic protection will be applied to the submarine pipes to prevent corrosion.
