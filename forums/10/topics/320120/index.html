40 plus years of governing by big-banner bigotry assumptions

  • robertteh
    7 Jun 08, 09:30

    Our system is one where a bigot has decided to use big-banner assumptions to run the whole country irrespective of right or wrong or consequences such big-banner assumptions of dubious effect might produce just to get by.

    No one for the past 40 plus years have quite succeeded in questioning or nailing down the biases of all these big-banner assumptions, like talents, leadership, meritocracy, pragmatism, rule-of-law, foreign-talents-create-jobs which were all looking good as big banner but on closer examination produce more problems for the citizens.

    How to choose a talent? Is he a talent because he befits the criteria of the selector. What are in those list of criteria. Loyalty, conformity, education, relevance, independence of thought, creativity, entrepreneurship, morality, sense of duty, etc etc.

    Meritocracy has been used as a way to allow leaders too to select people of their own choice without reference to certain objective criteria acceptable to citizens in both determination of criteria and manner of implementation.

    Foreign talent sounds good as the name suggests. But when it comes to implementation, the cheapest unskilled worker of certain undefined values or objective measured against government's social and economic and moral duty to citizens are completely excluded in implementation for expediency or pragmatism another big banner without meaning in relation to goodness of government to original principle.

    Yet sad to say despite the good-looking big banner assumptions of goodness of policies many of the policies adopted for years have been misunded to suit the convenience of leaders and government to serve their own narrow purposes of handing up a self-glorifying report card for themselves without deep-down benefits to citizens.

    Will such leaders last long and get away with all claims of success? So far none including Dr. Catherine Lim, Ngiam Tong Dow, JBJ, CSJ, LTK, Gopalan have been able to challenge of validity or assumed rightness of such big banner assumptions. Only time will tell perhaps.
