    multilevel marketing.

    You do know what that is right?

    Basically you buy items to join the scheme then you introduce others. They buy items and you get a commission of what they buy. They introduce others and you get a commission of what they buy too.

    You basically earn money by continuing to introduce people to the scheme to buy items and you get commissions from what they buy.

    If you're lucky, you're also able to sell the item you buy. More likely you can't. You earn money off how many people you can cheat into joining the scheme and you cheat them of their money and cheat the people they introduce of their money too and so on and so forth.

    It avoids illegality because there is a limit, unlike pyramid schemes but its basically a con game.
