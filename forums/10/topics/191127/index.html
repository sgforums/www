  • Ponders

    I just feel that without a strong opposition, PAP can just do what they deem fit without much obstructions or challenge.

    While I say so far they have not done anything detrimental to Singapore overall, but they have done things that is quite unpopular. Let me state some.

    1. Casino Issue.
    In the long run, the casino is for the better of Singapore to prevent a stagnantion in the economy. However, the approach in which they tried to sell the idea to Singapore was less than "democratic"

    Public consultation were limited to certain audience beacuse they claim only ppl who understand the dynamics understand the entire issue.

    When Public consulation was opened to bigger audience, people who are against the idea are readily shot down.

    It just seems that public consulation is for the sake of it and the entire Casino decision has be pre-determined.

    2. Melvin Tan issue.
    When Melvin Tan's case become a big uproar in Singapore. Mindef were quick to amend the laws unopposed.

    People like Melvin Tan are relatively fewer in numbers, there are many others who "fled" the country. There are even more who migrated after NS.

    As a result of Melvin Tan's issue, Melvin Tan only have a financial setback but current Singapoeans suffer.

    13 year olds are now more restricted in travelling. Further studies before NS is not allowed now. Suddenly more people suffer because of one guy.

    Why, in the first place Mindef allowed the entire Melvin Tan issue to happen?

    3. GST.
    It increased unopposed.

    4. Transport.
    Ditto. Just 2 weeks after election, the entire hike issue is back again. Now PTC say it might increase. And what did they say before election? They say, now is not time for review.

    They claimed it is unrelated to election and PTC is independant commission.

    But let me ask this. is the ruling party capable and able to overrule PTC's decisions?
