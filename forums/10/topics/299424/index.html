    TRAIN SERVICE DISRUPTIONS ON 25 DECEMBER 2007

    1. On 25 December 2007, southbound train service between Yew Tee and
    Woodlands MRT stations was disrupted at 9.43 am when someone jumped onto the southbound track at Kranji MRT Station. Police and SCDF were on site to investigate the incident but no one was found.

    2. However, at 11.12 am, northbound train service between Woodlands and Kranji MRT stations was disrupted when a man was hit by a train at Marsiling MRT Station.

    Incident at Kranji MRT station
    Maintaining train service between Yew Tee and Woodlands MRT station

    3. To maintain service on the unaffected stretches of the North-South line during the incident, southbound trains were turned around at Yew Tee Station and some northbound trains were turned around at Woodlands Station. Normal train service resumed at 10.53 am.

    4. Bus bridging services were activated at 10.08 am to ferry passengers between Yew Tee and Woodlands MRT stations during the disruption. About 3,500 passengers travelling between Yew Tee and Woodlands stations were affected during the incident.

    Incident at Marsiling MRT station

    Maintaining train service between Kranji and Woodlands MRT stations

    5. To maintain service on the unaffected stretches of the North-South line during the incident, northbound trains were turned around at Woodlands Station and southbound trains were turned around at Kranji Station. Normal train service resumed at 11.48 am.

    6. Bus bridging services were activated at 11.12 am to ferry passengers between Kranji and Woodlands MRT stations during the disruption. About 1,900 passengers travelling between Kranji and Woodlands stations were affected by the incident.
