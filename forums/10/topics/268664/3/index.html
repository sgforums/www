    Manner real etiquette
    Singaporeans behave in a civilized manner, always embodies respect for others. They sit, the correct rules, they will not be separated from his legs, if cross-feet. just one-legged knee stack directly in the other legs of his knees. They stand, body upright, without putting hands on the buttocks, because it is angry performance.
    Between the etiquette in social occasions, with the guests whom Singaporeans, generally Shi handshake ceremony. Between women and men can shake hands, but to a man, the more appropriate way is first women to reach out to shake hands again. Malaysian is the first contact between using their hands and then put their hands to recover the chest.
    Food etiquette Singaporeans are the staple food rice, sometimes Chifuzi etc., but not Preference buns. Malaysian meal general hand grasping food, they wash their hands before meals habit of dining at the use of his right hand. Tea is the universal love of the local people, guests, they often entertain tea, the Chinese prefer to drink tea dumplings, agreed to have prospered.
    Business etiquette in Singapore in business activities is the best month of March to October, to avoid the Christmas and Chinese New Year.
    Local business people speak English, met to exchange business cards, business cards can be printed in English. During the talks, as far as possible not to smoke. Singaporeans do not like spending extravagantly, entertained the other side will not be too ostentatious, especially in commercial activities, appreciation banquet hosted owner should not exceed the level to avoid each other have other ideas.
    Singapore Tourism etiquette of public transport is more developed, and tourism during the bus ride is very convenient, Each station has marked significant mark. Singapore is a multi-ethnic country, the distribution shops have national characteristics. As the eastern region is the "Malay market," mainly Malay clothing, food sale.
    Singapore government tries to prevent tipping, even for the additional services attendant pay tips, and the other possible rejection. Singapore, the main taboos of the people of all nationalities of faith : Most Chinese Buddhist or Taoist beliefs; Malaysian basic beliefs of Islam; Indian Hindu; Pakistanis have mostly Islam; Westerners generally Christianity. Singaporeans D'seven characters, not like the turtle. Fang prohibited fireworks.
