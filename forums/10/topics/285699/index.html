    UP to 4,000 top lawyers and judges will gather here from all over the world for the International Bar Association's 2007 Annual Conference, the most prestigious event on the calendar for the legal profession.

    Singapore was selected as the destination of its Annual Conference 2007. Singapore is a unique and dynamic city, filled with culture and brimming with energy and finesse. It is where urban meets traditional offering the modern and cosmopolitan whilst retaining its local flavour. Voted 5th best business meeting city, it offers the perfect opportunity for both business and pleasure.

    Minister Mentor Lee Kuan Yew was there to officially announce the opening ceremony today.

    MM Lee delivered the keynote speech at the five-day conference which is also the 60th anniversary of the IBA which was formed in New York in 1947.

    Today, hundreds of western suit angmors flock about premises around and within Suntec City. They were later invited to Raffles Hotel for a Welcome Party.

    IBA Conference Singapore lasts form 14-19 October, Suntec City.
