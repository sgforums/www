HDB loophole

  • 4GlassWall
    15 Mar 08, 21:30

    I found out the following HDB loophole for those who wanna get a flat.

    As most of us know, to get a HDB flat before the age of 35 you must register under 2 names, either siblings or as ROMed couple. Recently I got to know a few unROMed couples who manage to get a HDB flat without showing their ROM certifcates. As the proccess goes, after collecting the keys to your flat, you must produce a ROM cert to the HDB within 6 months. Many people do not do this but are left in peace to live their lives in the new flat. I have know couples who broke up after applying for a flat but they still get the unit and one of the family are living there. Then there are those who did not register their marriage and still live there. These people have been living there for years and HDB do not even know that they are living there without documentation.

    This is a loophole which i believe many exploit and HDB are ignoring it or not doing their job. These people causes many people who really needs housing to wait for many years to get one. HDB should start checking on all the documents and get these people to produce their documents or else its not fair to others.
