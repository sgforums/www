    This inaugural category aims to create a platform for contestants to showcase a different talent out of sing/dance/compose, bet it be it beat-boxing, juggling, yoyo-tricks, playing different instruments or even magic.

    Brief Introduction of Impresario

    Impresario is an annual nationwide talent search competition organized by Nanyang Technological University Cultural Activities Club. In its 18th year running, Impresario has become one of the main channels of talent emergence in Singapore. The objective is to promote the local arts scene by giving music and dance enthusiasts a platform to showcase their talents. This year, Impresario 2010 has taken a step further and is inviting submission of any talent showcase through The Open Platform.


    To participate, just follow these simple steps:

    Step 1: Think of your special talent

    Step 2: Film it down on video(s) and upload either on YouTube or digital delivery service website (Eg. yousendit.com)

    Step 3: Complete the registration form and send it to     theopenplatform@cacimpresario.com!

    If you know you've got the talent and you wish to share it, do take part in The Open Platform. What's more, registration is entirely free of charge!

    For more details, visit http://www.cacimpresario.com/The-Open-Platform/
