    The GRC system is a farce. It is just a safe and secure method to bloster the ranks of a ruling party intent on maintaining power.

    Reverting to SMCs for general elections mean individual candidates regardless of party have no choice but to come out of their tortoise shells and campaign vigorously to win GENUINE support. Not only do they have to reward the constituents who vote them into office, they have to make good on any PROMISES made, ensuring a mutual trust with the residents.

    This is something our ruling party is clearly unwilling to do. Such an election will mean zero protection for the nameless neophytes hiding behind prominent ministers and just 'SHOWING FACE' once every 5 years. Just take a look at the situation now: the ruling party claims that their politically aligned grassroots and town councils are helping the unemployed get jobs, assisting the needy obtain grants and apply for endowment funds.

    Yet, it is the people who have to COME TO YOU at meet the people's sessions to seek help and to obtain information how to qualify and apply for monetary grants. And even after all their grandiose claims, there are still ST reports of bursaries and endowment funds which are not being used simply because the application and qualification are so COMPLICATED or NOT PUBLICISED TO THE RIGHT CROWD.

    And after all this, a 5/6 Member GRC dilutes the substance of each candidate, with a slanted preference to focus on their key anchorman, usually a heavyweight minister, to ride the wave to victory. How can we possibly know the true credentials of any one new face if he/she is not given the opportunity to present their abilities?

    Conversely, how then can we conclude with such simplicity that an opposition candidate is not on equal if not better terms? He is just a person without the vast financial clout that deludes the ruling party that 'money makes mare go'.
