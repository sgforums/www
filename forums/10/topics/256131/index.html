    The greatest respect anyone can get even from their opponents is from his own performance and conduct and graciousness with which he performs his duties.

    So is his job is not well done and people are suffering from rising costs of living as against their wages and he is still claiming success or unqualified success like being the world's best condemning others as mediocre then his performance would be too doubtful or even shameful to earn true respect.

    As for now government is the party being elected and paid by the people to do a job to be judged and not the opposition. It must earn the respect of the opposition and not the other way round. It must earn the people's or opposition's respect but it does not admit problems and would deny them time and time again with hypocrisy.

    There is something seriously wrong with the ruling party's motivation and sense of self-worth and if it does not change this mindset then it will continue to be confrontational and suffer loss of respect.

    To earn opposition and people's true respect they must perform their duties with diligence and heart for with good government and not by suing or shouting down opponents or forcing people to accept their errors with hypocrisy and justification it will not gain any true respect from anyone.
