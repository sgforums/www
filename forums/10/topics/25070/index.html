    I see a GBU-hanging off that bug. GBU-22?

    Thats cause of the detonation radius of those missiles. Real pain in the ass.

    AMRAAMs launched at Max range are easier to dodge. Turn the opposite direction, fly away while dropping chaff, and hit the burners. Chances are, the AMRAAM won't catch up in time to kill you.

    Medium AMRAAMs are a headache, btween 15-20miles, they are almost impossible to dodge. Best bet is to beam the missile continously, and wait for your opportunity to cut into the missile. Chances of survival is about... say 30%?

    Close AMRAAMs are slightly easier if you have the KE and within corner airspeed. Know where its coming from, and sharp turn towards it. And keep turning towards it until it self-destructs. They aren't that fabulous at close range, but still deadly. About 50% survival.

    Missiles you can't never avoid are, AA-11 Archers and Python4s. They miss you the first time, they can still come back for you again.

    Superpak3 is a Mod of falcon4 rather. It fixes a lot of bugs, and currently I have not experienced any crashes after installing it.

    It modifies the F-16 in falcon4 to actual Block52 standard. Without the mod, the falcon felt more like a Block 30 machine. They added new weapons such as the JSOW and of course, fixed the AMRAAM bug.

    AI actually goes all out to avoid getting shot now, and most goes all out to kill you. With SuperPak3, u get 2 Multi-color MFDs, yes no more green only. Added an LRS to A2A radar mode. (was wondering what happened to that). They reskinned a lot of the planes, so they look better... (esp. the F-4Es) and the added a few more planes, eg. EF2000, TornadoADV, Su-27+ family (including uncles n aunties n grandsons),

    And to if you choose to power up you F-16 off the ramp instead of just taking off. Well, read the manual. You actually have to power up all the various sub-systems, calibrate your INS, Altitude warning, n finally starting up your engines. Its the closest you get to the real thing.
