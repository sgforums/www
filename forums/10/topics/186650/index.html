Silly it is about the economy.

  • robertteh
    17 Apr 06, 23:04
    TRA LA LA the old taperecorder plays on.

    Sweet music to the young, the idealistic or ungrateful.

    Who have much to learn from life's lessons.

    They need to understand plenty and be tolerant.

    Of higher fees and fares because nothing is for free.

    Small and vulnerable is our island without any oil or treasure.

    So do not utter problems but ask not the government but the people for lack of training.

    Ask not perplexing questions about the competitiveness or costs of living.

    It is all about the external world circumstances.

    Cost of living, bus fares or tuition fees will go up according to economy and market forces.

    May be others have better solution. But let them stand for GRC election.

    Election is the festive story time and every body should be happy.

    Never mind about GRC or walkover.

    Messy chaos and dropping currencies are sweeping many countries.

    Tell them to the young they will then understand.

    It is silly to ask for lower fees but not lower minister's wages as the latter are different.

    Everything is one answer one size fits all.

    It is the economy market forces, demand versus supply, overheads, transportation or distribution costs, manhours spent etc.

    But er one should not under-pay the peanut millions to root out corruption.

    So the young must learn to be tolerant of costs and policy matters.

    If they have the guts they should stand and form the next government.

    Never mind his deposit monies but probably he has to tell where the fund comes from.

    Problems there will be; but there will be goodies and all will be forgotten.

    No major issues really come what may.

    More stories about danger of welfare; chaos in other country

    Don't forget to remind the youth of some distant past glory in such a country.

    Play and play will be the old recorder about the past, life, costs and wages.

    What goes down must come up again not a matter of talent or worth of the million.

    True democracy comes with a price. People must pay and pay with no exception.

    Elites will it again and again and everything will be taken care of.

    People who do not understand are just whiners.

    Silly are all the questions; everything has an explanation.

    If IR it has to be then over the dead body.

    So let it be as it is all about the economy.
