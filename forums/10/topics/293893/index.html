    Truly capable and good leaders are those who said "We must industrialise the country and upgrade the economy" and within 5 years without any excuses the whole New Jurong Industrial Park was completed offering 100,000 jobs to the citizens.

    Since Dr. Goh Keng Swee left, there were many slogans like "we want to upgrade the economy, to value-adding technology-driven one" or "we want to remake Singapore" but today we are still waiting and waiting and citizens are seeing dropping wages and being told it is globalization this or that.

    We still have not yet come across a GKS-like minister who could promise and honor the words with great feat.

    LKY talked about policies like meritocracy or leadership (his autocracy mostly) most of the time but could not deliver.

    He has a lot of reasons why certain things happened without holding himself responsible for lack of results.

    That ultimately is the difference between man and boy, scholars and real performers.
