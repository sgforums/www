    Caning is also a punishment meted out for vandalism and molest. As regards the latter, we must bear in mind that molest is a charge that usually hinges on one person's word against the other, and the Singapore Courts have been known to favour the woman's testimony despite the principle that a person must be proven to be guilty beyond a reasonable doubt. Let's not be blind to the notion of miscarriage of justice, particularly in our country where the emphasis is on "efficiency".

    Bottom line is that a State should not be sanctioned to inflict barbaric punishment, no matter the offence. Caning is a medieval measure for medieval times. The death penalty is barbaric too and should be abolished as well. Deterrent sentencing is just the easy way out and a reflection of unprogressive thinking.
