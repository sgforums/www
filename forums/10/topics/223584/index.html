    In a fair gamble, like the toss of a coin, if you buy $1 heads, the payout should be $2, which means if you buy both heads and tails to win at $2, the toss of the coin resulting in heads would mean they pay you $2, resulting in neither gain or loss.

    In a game of 4D, say you buy all the possible number of combinations from 0000 to 9999 (10,000 combinations all Big bets), which cost like $10,000. When you strike all the numbers, you will only get like

    1st Prize----: $2,000
    2nd Prize---: $1,000
    3rd Prize-----: $ 490
    Starter-------: $2,500 (10 prize of $250)
    Consolation-: $ 600 (10 prize of $60)
    ==================
    Total Prize : $6,590
    ==================

    You pay $10,000 for all the bets, but only get back $6,590.

    This is like playing a game of coin toss, where you pay $1 for a bet, when you win they will only pay you $1.318 instead of $2.

    Only get back 65.9% instead of getting back 100%!!!

    It's a negative sum game!!!
