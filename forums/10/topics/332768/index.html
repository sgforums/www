    Hi,

    I tried to post this in existing threads. Many such were expired threads. so, here I create a new thread.

    I have a dear friend who suffered a dengue attack not long ago. Fortunately, he survived the ordeal.

    I like to urge people to post pictures or videos of potential mossie breeding sites to alert the public and authorities responsible to take action and do the needful and get rid of obvious breeding sites.

    The following are pics I like to share with all about a potential breeding site for mossies in Ang Mo Kio Hub carpark basement 3. Not sure if all the carparks there at all levels are the same. Its quite disturbing. There are stagnant pools of water there appear to be there for  a long time and thus I am concerned why it is so.

    AMK Hub has many many visitors. The carpark are dark places ideal for mossies unless something is done about it that we are not aware.

    Please help to inform your friends about this .  The pics can be found at :
