    8 Aug 04, 23:54
    Personally, I find that the cost of living in Singapore is quite reasonable given the standard of living here except for a few items.

    Things that I find reasonably priced in Singapore

    (1) Food - $2-4 dollars a meal is reasonable I think
    (2) General Education - Never remember paying much for school until University.
    (3) Clothing - Can be as cheap as $3 a piece if you really don't mind brand/quality.

    Things I find unreasonable
    (1) Housing - I am fine with Condo price subjected to market forces. But it seems ridiculous for me to have direct HDB flat purchase to be subjected to market forces. It should be based on cost.

    Things which I am unsure
    (1) Healthcare - expensive I think, but am not too sure since I try not to see doctors in general
    (2) Transport - Taxi is cheap, Car is expensive, Train/Bus is slightly towards the expensive side I think.
    (3) University education is expensive. Am unsure if I agree with government arguement that they can charge more since graduates gets to earn it back eventually.

    Above is just my personal perception.
