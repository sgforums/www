  • deemweet
    25 Apr 06, 01:20
    The widening income gap is a consequence of changes to the tax structure, namely, lowering of income tax and (a few years ago) raising of sales tax. Income taxation is progressive whereas sales taxation is regressive. As such, the gap can easily be closed in the future should a need arise by further modifying these two taxes. Thus, the widening of the income gap is not a real physical problem, but an artefact of tax structures that can be overcome, if and should such a need arise.
