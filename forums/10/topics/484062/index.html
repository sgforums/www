SingTel CEO Snags Million-Dollar Salary even as Company Perf

  • Cowboyjohnnylah

    Reposted from Redwire Times Singapore

     

    SingTel’s net profit dipped by over 12 percent. But SingTel Ceo Chua Sock Koong bagged $4.6 million last year, a drop from the $4.9 million which she earned in 2012. This doesn’t include the returns on the value of her shares.

     

    Clocking a 13 percent increase in net profits, Singapore Airlines rewarded its CEO Goh ChoonPhong with a 50 percent pay raise, bringing his salary to $2.6 million.OCBC subsidiary Great Eastern profits dropped 43 per cent from $1.2 billion to $675 million. But that didn’t stop the company CEO Christopher Wei from increasing his wages to $4.3 million – a 4 percent jump.

     

    These findings were culled by the Straits Times.
