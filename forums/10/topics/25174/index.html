    Study in aussie will cost you roughly around 30k per year. (Course fee + Living expenses + Air Tickets). Depending on the degree you pursuing, it will take one to two years.

    The best way is still to take up the "P.M. scholarship"...err..I mean the Papa Mama Scholarship if available. You have long repayment periods, and can negoitate with your parents if you really tight and need to skip a payment to them.

    The other solution is to borrow from various sources, and have few loans. But the payment of the loan can kill you. And you might not find a job that fast when you return.

    Distance learning is another option, but you must check if it is recognised by your employer. It allows you to work, yet study. But it's tiring and take quite a lot of willpower.

    The final option is to work for a number of years, save up before you pursue your degree. The door of education is always open if you have the determination. I had course mates who are in the 60s.

    Just my two cents, hope this helps a little.
