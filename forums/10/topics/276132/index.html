    I think in singapore, there is absolutely no shortage of talents to join political parties. Afterall, how many parties do we need or talking about? right?

    So, there is enough talents, in my view to form a few parties.

    PAP is saying it wants to recruit more. This means they believe there are more talents.

    The real problem is not talent.

    The only thing we as voters should be concerned about is how to improve our Democracy. How to mature it.

    Good political parties should be fair.
    Good political parties should understand and respect the meaning of Democracy.
