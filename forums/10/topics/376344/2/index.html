    Dear Lionoasis,

    Singapore is not sinking but leaking badly.

    While the PAP government is trying to maintain an even keel, it is plugging holes at one end of the vessel, but causing new leaks elsewhere.

    The resilient and creative Singaporeans will know how to cope with the situation, and some have found new opportunities to survive the precarious situation.

    Those who are more resourceful have found themselves a dinghy, or a luxury yatch, even a private jet - that serves as a getaway for leisure and as an insurance.

    The remaining Singaporeans - who are less able to fend for themselves for a variety of reasons - will remain at the mercy of circumstances, with a Government that will not give any direct assistance in any significant ways that can alleviate this group from their desperate situation; but will place more demands on the entire population in the various communal self-help programs as the Government remain aloof to the situation that they have created.

    This will require more Singaporeans to be educated and informed of the present injustices and abuses.

    It will require more Singaporeans to be aware of the responsibility of each individual that the Rights of the Community must be protected from being hijacked by the Government for the most insignificant reasons.

    It will require the Community to be aware that the Individual, who is protecting the Community, will also need the support from the Community to protect the Individual.

    It is through this understanding that Singapore is not the exclusive project of any single political party.

    This Singapore project - which require as much support from Singaporeans - must also result in the interests of Singaporeans being protected first and foremost, and not the interests of any single Political Party, whether in power or waiting at the side-lines.
