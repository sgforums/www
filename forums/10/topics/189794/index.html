    This Ge indeed opened up my eyes and mind...

    I learn that Economy progress is not a guage of a Nation's success.
    Quality of Citizen's life is.

    Singaporeans are people without souls, without sparkle in their eyes...
    possibly because of our political, society system.
    Our society make people too self-centred, too materialistic and
    too little compassion.

    The more they try to control, the more votes they will lose...

    Also, I now do not support the banning of various websites
    (eg political, gay websites..)
    i used be against gay websites in sg....(not that im against gays)
    now i dun support it anymore...
    but maybe this one, i think our society still not ready for it yet...

    Ok maybe i already know these,
    but these 9 days solidifies the importance of these...
