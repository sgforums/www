    Precariously perched on the edge of a brittle cliff of a lifestyle.

    The true rich, are rarely seen in public. They aren't the ones you see on Sunday in carparks, polishing their Jags, Merc., and Volvos. They are the ones, you see passing by on a small little motorised bicycle on a Sunday enjoying the morning air, and later going home to their humble 3 room flat. Tomorrow, they go to work in the BMW roadster parked downstairs, and walks into the office and into room marked Chairman/President.

    I saw so many rich, who don't dress rich. They looked like they might even ask you for a loan!

    These are people, with heart, and sound mentality. They know all types of VALUES in life. Why? Because they slogged like a dog, got looked down upon, criticised and stepped on, when they were struggling up.

    Ironically, they are not vengeful. Humility builds character, and clears the clouds from one's eyes.
