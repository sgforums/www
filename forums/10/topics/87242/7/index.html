    Not to side with anyone but here is a copy of Brief Taiwan history.

    Brief Taiwan history.

    1544 - Taiwan first discovered by Portugese

    1662 - Koxinga (Zheng Cheng Gong), the last general of Ming, claimed Taiwan when Ming falls to Qing

    1683 - Taiwan Annexed to Qing Empire, defeated by ex-Koxinga's general Shi Liang

    1895 - China Ceded Taiwan to Japanese as part of the peace treaty with Japan (Yes, Taiwan was part of Japan for 50 years)

    1945 - WWII ended. Taiwan back to China

    1949 - KMT lost to CCP. Fled to Taiwan and rule it since.

    I leave it to the reader to decide how to intepret the above information and if it is relevant at all to China's claim of Taiwan.
