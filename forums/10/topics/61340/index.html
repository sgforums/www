    PROS

    1. Gov can make tough decision if it is a one party nation such as GST rise and reducing CPF contribution. One party rule means more control and less time to make a decision in tis rapidly changing world

    2. Singapore is a small nation, weak in power, land and influence. Competition has stiffened among countries recently and Singapore is caught in a bad shape due to regional problems and terrorist threats. Our primary concerns should be on survival and not higher level of needs such as beliefs in freedom etc

    3. Though Gov related companies r controlled by Gov, they r at least big enough to go global like Singtel etc. Most private companies in Singapore r too small to be of any significant.

    4.

    CONS

    1. If Gov make bad decision, tat is it. No other control mechanism to audit Gov action. Mistakes will be covered up and make unknown to public. Single party Gov listen lesser to alternatives view

    2. Feeling of being suppressed by single party Gov. Action toward opposition make citizen fearful of repercussion of speaking against Gov freely.

    3. If 2 political parties present, more intellectual conflict and exchange could take place. A strong and positive development may result from such action
