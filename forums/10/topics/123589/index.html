    OH!! UNIQUELY SINGAPORE
    (Just for laugh)

    Unabashed is the little red dot,

    Facade that shines with

    Leadership and Talents,

    Policies and ideas stricken men

    And women clamouring five Cs

    Not a word nor a breather

    Except in speaker corner,

    Lest be smitten,

    And outlawed with OB,

    Muffled by cries of inner souls,

    Sullen men old and young

    Accepted as fate

    To pay a-double,

    For Lands acquired with tax,

    At market price deemed subsidised

    To pay a-triple,

    For vehicles in COE, ERP after excise.

    In surplus for rains that never come.

    Election brimming with goodies,

    Swiss standard of living a-promise,

    All reocrds and worldly bests,

    Dream high for soccer world cups.

    Alas dreams stay dreams as crises

    Came in quick succession,

    True grit made of sterner stuff,

    Growth Triangles fumbled,

    Scholarly leaders fell in recessions,

    Entrepreneurs and businesses went abegging

    Assets deflated and grow no more

    Burdened by costs skyhigh

    Richly adorn scholars shrunk in comfort zone,

    Unending calls more sacrifice,

    Remaking and change to no end,

    Only Fortunes knowth own plans,

    Entrepreneurs and true talents,

    Forsaken left high and dry

    Soon replaced by Foreign hands,

    Gladly received as talents,

    Nay to dreams and misty dreams

    Nay to 5 Cs and asset enhancement

    Left the mortgages and weariness.

    Good ideas and change for better days.

    The rule of the day are glorious past,

    And Self-admirations Good or feign

    Mistakes well a part of life,

    See no evil hear no evil

    All beyond reproach or blame.

    What a mighty show on earth

    Of the unique red dot.
