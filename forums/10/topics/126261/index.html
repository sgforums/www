    Maximise their budget allocation for the financial year...

    I was pondering this topic after taking part in a recent event organized by the Ministry of Environment and Water Resources at Suntec Convention Centre..

    The event took place just over the Friday and Saturday 8th and 9th April..

    The event took up one exhibition hall.. over a Friday and Saturday.. peak periods i would suppose for the rentals

    As a participating exhibitor, i was shocked to know that we did not need to pay a single cent for the booth.. so this means that MEWR, NEA and PUB must have footed this bill on their own.

    There was a lot of wasted space.. not the entire hall was filled with booths compared to the normal high turnout exhibitions..

    Being from the private sector, i wont think it helped in any bit cos there wasn any pre-event information telling people abt this.. so the private sector who participated hardly gained anything cos the public turned up and really i felt its a lost cause..

    Public moneys... spent like this.. is a real terrible way.. I am speculating that its probably to spend the remaining cent they have for the financial year..

    I wonder if they did it to spend the remaining cents of the last financial year in order to get a bigger amt this year for the ministry..

    Wonder how many more examples are there?
