    KUALA LUMPUR: Jemaah Islamiyah militant Mas Selamat bin Kastari, who was captured in Malaysia last year after escaping a Singapore detention centre, has been handed back to Singaporean authorities, an official said Friday.

    "We can say that Mas Selamat has been handed over to Singapore authorities," Jamilah Taib, a home ministry spokeswoman, told AFP.

    "We don't know when the handover took place and will release details as we have them," she added.
