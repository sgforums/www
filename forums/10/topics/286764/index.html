  • Melvin Tan
    20 Oct 07, 14:32
    Based on the Order Paper of the Parliament sitting this Monday (2 days' time), Myanmar-related issues will dominate the session, with nearly 1/3 of the questions on it alone (12 out of 38 ).

    More than half (7 out of 12) are from non-PAP MPs. WP NCMP Sylvia Lim filed 3 questions and NMPs Siew Kum Hong and Eunice Olsen filed 2 each. The other 5 questions are from PAP MPs Irene Ng (2), Lee Bee Wah, Lily Neo and Charles Chong.

    All MPs, ruling party MPs or otherwise, have covered basically all areas that were discussed in online forums, from Asean's stand on action against to trade with Myanmar to Myanmar-related protests in Singapore.

    For those following the Myanmar issue, it could be a sitting to watch. I recommend to read the questions and ministers' replies in the Hansard for the most accurate contents, rather than rely on the printing press.
