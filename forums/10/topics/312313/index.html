    On Wednesday , 12 March 2008 Edition of this paper , there was a front page news report on how a China student studying in Singapore had suffered from leukamemia and the students of the school even set up an online appeal to ask Singaporeans to donate and help this cancer - stricken boy in whatever they can .

    But to their shock and anger , instead of receiving support for their donation drive to help the boy , some Singaporeans refused to help and even pour scorn to their efforts and even said that they would rather offer their help only if he is a fellow Singaporean ! 80% out of the 120 people who took part in an online poll set up on this topic said that they WOULD NOT offer help to this boy , just because he is of China Nationality !

    We are living in a muti-racial society and Singapore is also a unique country as it is not only a multi-racial society but also for it's multi- nationalism too !

    We are known to be the FIRST to offer help to other countries when they are in need of food , clothing when the country had just experienced a natural disaster or to war torn areas . We are so willing to donate and offer help in whatever we can , so why do u think some Singaporeans attitude towards this China boy who needs our help too so different ?
