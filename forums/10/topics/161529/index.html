    The government can make the whole economy vibrant and then will be more jobs by doing the following:-

    (1) Stop GLCs from competing against domestic businesses.

    (2) Do not tax and recover all costs and do not double charge on land for essential government services so that there will be liquidity to create economic activities and vibrancy. Then the private sector will grow and create more jobs with more businesses, contracts. Since the 1980s when government went into business to compete against the people for business, the domestic sector has been languishing but there is no admission of this fact.

    (3) Use more of surpluses to stimulate growth by investing such monies in growths and technology start-ups.

    Once the government takes care not to hurt the economy then everything will be vibrant again. Then there is no need to give excuses or talk about more foreigners leaving and property becoming vacant. When domestic businesses are good there will be no such problems.
