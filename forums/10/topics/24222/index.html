    To whom it may concern,

    I have read your article.

    Prior to committing the criminal act (in the case, selling of pirated vcd), he had a choice - to sell (face the music) or against selling it (life goes on as before).

    Once he had committed this crime, he will have to bear the consequences. Ignorant about what it leads to is no defence.

    He was lucky enough to have been given a warning and been given a new start. Under the penal code,once a criminal act is committed, he can be prosecuted to court for a proceeding regardless when. There is no case (guilty as charged and convicted) There is no expiration for criminal act. 3 yrs later, he still had a choice, either pay the fine or face the jail sentence, not many prosecuted could say that they had such luck.

    He could have been convicted with a minimum of few months in jail, in addition with a fine.

    Blessing in disguise, in my humble opinon.
