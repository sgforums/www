    It really depends what you view as necessity. 

     

    If we dont mind Changi airport to be as congested as London Heathrow, i think T3 should be enough. 

     

    How if we want Changi Airport to remain as one the most efficient airport for both passengers and transit airlines, then we will have to consider T4 because when the IRs, F1 and YOG are all running on full cylnders, changi airport will be very congested.

     

    And it will be a disaster for Singapore and SIA, if airlines decide to use KLIA or Bangkok instead of Singapore as their transit hub.
