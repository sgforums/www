    (2) To provide a more suitable alternative of ensuring equitable representation of minority racial candidates by providing for 20% of seats of parliaments to be reserved for contest among minority racial candidates.

    (3) To prohibit use of block- or group-representation voting system to avoid shackling of the one-man-one-vote universal electoral system.
