    Concepts and assumptions are concepts and assumptions. Usually they are unproven or untruths. It will be worse it such assumptions are just excuses for government to shift their responsibility to look after its own citizens.

    It is certainly not good corporate governance to govern a country based on some wild assumptions which run contrary to our basic social fabric like loyalty of government to citizens begets loyality of citizens to the nation.

    If we need a few Foreign talents here and there for certain specialist areas of works and professions, just confine the permits and privileges specifically for such categories but not across the board or let employers choose freely among the cheapest labor force in the world.

    People stick out the neck for their government doing NS and paying certain high fees and taxes and chipping in in bad time to forego certain CPF to employers. So why should people's interest be taken so lightly on some unproven assumption that all foreign talents will be talents and allow employers to take them as they like across the board.

    Due to lack of accountability to the people over the past 40 years many policies were formulated based on such unproven assumption resulting in high costs of living which cause our loss of economic competitiveness and many problems as posted to the feedback unit.

    Today, there is a tendency to govern the whole nation by assumptions of all kind, including lately the assumption that pro-PAP ward will object to upgrading for Potong Pasir or Hougang wards according to objective schedule.

    Assumptions about contribution of foreign talents is too general and sweeping. It is right for citizens to stand their ground and desist from being over-whelmed by such highly risky and fallacious assumptions which tend to excuse the government of problems and inefficiencies.

    Leaders should be held accountable to all such assumptions as otherwise we may just one day lose all the jobs and opportunities to more foreigners without even foreigners contributing anything to the economy or job creations as assumed.

    We might end up foreigners taking us for a ride and leaving us at the next downturn for their own country of origin.

    Why work on assumptions over such matter as citizens loyalty and government's duty to preserve jobs and opportunities for their own citizens.
