    GNP = C + G + I + NX +NFP

    NFP = payment to foreigners for inputs used in producing goods and services, less money the economy receives for selling the same factors of production.

    The only difference in the formula is NX + NFP, which means ( Net exports + Net factor payments), this diffence in formula only deals with values of production less the selling price, not unemployment rate.

    Saying GDP is roughly tied to unemployment rates is not wrong.
