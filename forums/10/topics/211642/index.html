How great does LKY want to be - Suggestions ?

  • robertteh
    23 Sep 06, 07:00
    LKY lately again has spoken up on race and provoked the Malaysian leaders into lambasting Singapore as a tiny country or interfering neighbour.

    How great can someone be in this world by talking about his own race other than saviour of men who truly wants to lessen fellow men's suffering and depressions.

    If someone hope to tell the world how great his leadership or statemanship by talking about race or racial superiority, will he achieve his greatness?

    He should spend time to come up with a bigger plan - common ASEAN market and people-to-people relation-projects. This is the better way to cooperate and win over more neighbouring countries.

    Insteed of denying problems as if they never existed, he should be more open and objective in studying other suggestions and spend time to change in the way he governs the country by embracing bigger objective - good corporate governance involving and allowing everyone to have a part to play in political development of a country.

    So it is better he looks into how to provide truly balanced and good corporate governance to balance up the current approach is only based on promoting elitism and government's own surpluses causing high costs of living and inequity and problems.

    It is time he recognizes that his past 41 years' of government is too narrow and too one-tracked and did not benefit majority of citizens.

    By balancing and allowing monies to be returned to people he will succeed in creating more vibrancy, jobs, much better job than now and over the past 41 years.
