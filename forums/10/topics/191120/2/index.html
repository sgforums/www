    About contractual breach. Online auction sites like ebay and yahoo are not considered 'traditional auctioneer' by the law. Thus, these sites functions like an auction, but are not bounded by the laws regarding traditional auctions where the drop of the hammer will bound a contract. Thus, as long as no money has changed hand, no one is bounded by any contract or sort to buy or sell any items, even if someone 'bidded' successfully.
