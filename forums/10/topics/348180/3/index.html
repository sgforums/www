    Indian (1996)

    Indian is a 1996 Tamil film directed by S. Shankar. The film stars Kamal Haasan, Manisha Koirala, Urmila Matondkar,. The film's score and soundtrack are composed by A. R. Rahman. Indian was nominated by India as its entry for the Best Foreign Language Film for the Academy Awards in 1996.  The Corruption Theme was later used in Mudhalvan, Nayak, Anniyan and Sivaji. The movie celebrates the 45th anniversary of india's Independence .The story follows the a series of murders commited by a 70 year old ex freedom fighter from World War 2 to serve as a deterrent to corrupt government officials. The film is the highest grossing movie in the indian film industry in the 1990s and had won various state and national awards.

    (Mild Language, Strong violence, Graphic scenes)

     

    Mudhalvan (1999) 

    Mudhalvan (1999) is a Tamil political film directed by S.Shankar, later re-made in Hindi as Nayak: The Real Hero. The film stars Arjun, Manisha Koirala and Sushmita Sen. The film's score and soundtrack are composed by AR. Rahman. . The lead actor of Mudhalvan, Arjun has appeared a second time for Shankar in this movie after their debut vehicle Gentleman in 1993 was a runaway success. The story of an ordinary reporter who happens to interview a state minister one fine day agrees to a challenge to act as the Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu , and makes such a success of it that soon he is embroiled in political intriguey. The hit song Shakalakka Baby by A.R.Rahman went on to gain international acclaim appearing in Ander Loyd Webber's Bombay Dreams and kelly Poon's Soundtracks. Former Miss Universe Sushmita Sen also makes a cameo appearance for the song. This film was a tremendous success at the box office and continued Shankar's dream run. It won the Filmfare Best Tamil Film Award in the year of its release

    (Mild Language, Mild Violence)

    Anniyan(2005)

    Anniyan  is a 2005 Tamil action thriller directed by S. Shankar. The film stars Vikram and Sada. The film's score and soundtrack are composed by Harris Jayaraj. The story of a timid lawyer suffering from Multiple Personality disorder turns into a vicious vigilante and undertaker of superhuman abilities by night commits a series of gruesome murders inspired by an ancient text called 'The Book Of Hell" which dictates punishment for created sins in one's waking life.His idea is that such a murder will reverberate with shock and awe in the society. His victims are corrupt government officials who were negligent in their duty to the public. His condition comes to light after his troubled past is revealed, the death of his sister due to the negligence of government officials. The film won the best south indian film award in that year and a number of awards for best new visuals, sounds and editing.

    (Strong Language, Very Strong violence,Graphic scenes, M+18)

     

    Sivaji the Boss (2007)

    Sivaji the Boss is a 2007 tamil film directed by S.Shankar. The film stars Rajinikanth and Shreya with music by AR Rahman The movie revolves around a well-established Software System Architect  who returns home to India after finishing work in America. On his return, he dreams of giving back to society with free medical treatment and education. However his plans face a roadblock in the form of the highly affluent and influential businessman and politician,. When corruption also arises, Sivaji is left with no option but to fight the system in his own way and to establish a one man private government. The film won awards for best screenplay, story , production and music in 2007. its also the first 4k digital movie in Asia.The film,  opened to a record breaking box-office reception across the globe despite a few controversies, becoming the highest ever grossing Tamil film of the Chennai film industry

    (Mild language, Moderate violence)

    Endhiran (Upcoming in 2009)

    Endhiran ( the Robot) is an upcoming tamil feature film directed by S.Shankar. The film stars Rajinikanth and  Aishwarya Rai with music by AR Rahman. Special effects for this film is supervised by Stan Winston from Hollywood and the stunts are done by Yuen Woo Ping From Hong Kong. The story revolves around a scientist and his creation , a cyborg. The film is a project titled "Robo"  that was to be released in 2002 by shankar due to high production costs, it was shelved. The original screenplay written in 2001 had Kamal Hassan And Preity Zinta as the lead cast. The film is touted to be S.Shankar's Best project to date.

    (Not yet Rated)
