    there are 2 "normal" movies directed by shankar if u wan to check them out. Does not have spectacular storylines like Indian, Mudhalvan or Anniyan.

    Jeans (1998) 

    Jeans is a 1998 romantic tamil language film directed by S.Shankar. The film stars the former Miss world Aishwarya Rai and the 90s indian heartrob Prashant in lead roles The music for the film is composed by AR Rahman. The story follows  the story of a girl who pretends to be two different people to woo a pair of twins  with disastrous consequences. The 1st indian film with scenes shot with the 7 wonders of the world as backdrops. The film was a box office failure in India  but well known for its soundtrack which  won the national award for best music in that year. The film was nominated by India as its entry for the Best Foreign Language Film for the Academy Awards in 1998

     

    Boys (2003)

     Boys is a 2003 teenage comedy  film directed by S.Shankar. The music is composed by AR Rahman.The film stars Siddarth, Genelia, Bharath, Vivek, Nakul and Senthil . Boys' is the story of five boys and a girl from different families They are youthful, vibrant, careless violating all conventions and In the process, they discover their hidden talents. The film was a box office failure in India but did reasobably well overseas.
