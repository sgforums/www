    Thailand's goverment last night seized Shin Corp's ITV. The company had failed to pay US2.9 billion (S$4.4billion) in back fees and fines for violations of it original concession agreement.

    ITV shares had been suspended from trading on the Stock Exchange of Thailand since last tuesday. The stock fell 91 percent since Temasek acquired ITV's parent in January last year. The station's 1010 employees now faces an uncertain future. Employees are expected to be laid off.

    The Thai government is investigating Temasek and the Shin Corp. group of companies to see whether their mobile-phone and satellite concessions violated Thai law.
