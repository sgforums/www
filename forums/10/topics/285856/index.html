    So if students are not allowed to develop naturally will they know how to argue and discuss critically to come up with what is good system for Singapore.

    Why our cannot ask good searching questions of his government? Standard solutions to textbook problems?

    MM Lee should know why both the undergrads and journalists are unable to develop their intellectual abilities? Only it will be poor public relation for him to concede the real cause.
