    Hi,

    According to my memory of official records, Chee joined the SDP and rose quickly to become Chiam's assistant SG. He was sacked from NUS for using research funds for his wife's thesis and wanted to protest against this (and not that he wrote a paper or article). Chiam offered him a place in Potong Pasir but he wanted to do it on NUS grounds. Chiam proposed that the SDP CEC censure and stop him but the CEC voted against the proposal. Chiam then resigned as SG, which also excludes him from the CEC. At the next CEC meeting, the CEC appointed Chee as acting SG. Later, Chiam spoke negatively against the party and was expelled from SDP. He fought the decision in court and won and was reinstated as a member.

    For the WP, according to official records, JBJ had wanted WP to raise funds to clear his bankruptcy. Low and the WP CEC then disagreed as raising funds in such a manner was illegal. They also did not agree to using the existing party funds to clear him as they felt it was for the party purpose and not for an individual, whether leader or member. At the following CEC conference / election, JBJ did not attend, meaning that the SG post would be vacated. Low then ran for it unopposed (and not that he ousted or contested against JBJ and won). Later, JBJ resigned from the party altogether citing differences (as opposed to Chiam's resigning only as a leader while remaining as a member).

    The above is subjected to corrections.
