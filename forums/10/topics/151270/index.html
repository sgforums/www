    Singapore has 3 branches of gov:
    1. Legislature (Parliament + President)
    2. Executive (President + Prime Mister + Cabinet Minister)
    3. The Judiciary (Supreme Court and other courts)

    This 1-2-3 separation of powers is supposed to be a safeguard against tyranny.

    The Chief Justice is appointed by the President with the advice of the Prime Minister.
