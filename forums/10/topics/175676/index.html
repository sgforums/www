    One such corporatised government service e.g. MediaCorp/Mediawork ran into heavy losses due to unsustainable fees due to internal inefficiencies in 2002 within 2 years of corporatisation proving the fact that in the real market environment (not monopoly), government departments trying to increase fees in one-sided manner like HDB will lose monies and have to be forced by market to close shops..like what Mediacorp has discovered in 2003.
