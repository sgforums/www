    http://www.wp.org.sg/yac/youths2.htm#C2

    A businessman, Meng Seng's entrepreneurial spirit led him to start his own business immediately after obtaining his Bachelor Degree (with Honours), Major in Economics, from the National University of Singapore (NUS) in 1993.

    Meng Seng subscribes strongly to democratic socialism. His active engagement in politics started during his undergraduate days in NUS. He posted his comments and views on socio-political issues on "primitive" bulletin boards and soon progressed to posting his views and engaging in discussions on newsgroups such as soc.culture.singapore in the mid-90s.

    Meng Seng engaged in the comfort of such "sideline" politics until the 2001 general election when it was imminent that opposition politics would be crushed unless more people were willing to come forward and do their part.

    As a firm believer of the need for check and balance in every system, it dawned on him that there was a real danger and possibility that Singapore might lose its only two opposition MPs in Parliament. Said Meng Seng, "It was a time of reckoning and I had to make a choice to relinquish my comfortable position as an armchair critic. All the discussions and talks would be meaningless when the time comes for genuine action and we do not rise to the occasion."

    Meng Seng believes strongly in a forward-looking opposition that goes beyond opposing and confrontational politics. After meeting with Mr Low Thia Khiang, it became clear to him that the direction of The Workers' Party of Singapore (WPS) resonated with his ideals of pro-Singapore politics. This was his fundamental reason for joining the WPS.

    He explained, "I was glad to learn that the CEC members share similar views and stance as I do. We are not afraid to look at ourselves critically and most importantly, we believe in putting national and peoples' interest above the party's interests."

    Meng Seng hopes to contribute to the WPS and Singapore with his knowledge in Economics, and helps the party in its various activities.
