    Hi, attended a talk given by Sylvia Lim, Chairwomen of WP in NTU yesterday. Just want to share my personal opinion of her based on it.

    I think among the oppositions speech that I have attended so far, she is one of the more practical and witty one. However, I do feel that she is a bit of a slow warmer, as in the first ten minutes or so of her talk, she appears to be uneasy and is even a bit repelling in terms of her body language. But once the mingle starts and the setting becomes more informal, she becomes a lot more friendly and charismatic. I think this might let her down in election campaign because I feel that she is still unable to handle large formal crowds. And also, being a slow warmer, she might lose the precious first impression when she steps onto a stage. She might do better if she engage one of those image consultants that specialise in packaging politicians and teach them the "correct" body languages. Not sure if we have those in Singapore though.
