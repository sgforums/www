    We spoke to 12 of the 25 residents in the home. We interviewed them separately outside the home. Eleven claimed they had been abused and a 12th boy corroborated their accounts.

    After three weeks of investigation, we alerted the police and the Ministry of Community Development, Youth and Sports.

    An official probe is underway. Meanwhile, here are the painful stories related by the tortured teenage boys.

    TORTURE BY URINE
    The welfare home, set up to care for and educate kids of hard-up parents or guardians, is not being identified to protect the identities of the innocent children. All the names have been changed.

    Listening to the kids' tales of violent and harsh physical punishment over the last two years, one boy's story stands out.

    Adam, 12, claims that he suffered like no other.

    He once had urine poured on his head as punishment in November 2005.

    First, he was made to strip down to his underwear, squat over a toilet, and tuck his hand behind his knees.

    His childcare supervisor, Ja (not his real name), then poured urine over the then-10-year-old's head.

    It was Adam's punishment for a playful prank in the toilet - he had peed on his friend while they were in the toilet together.

    Adam had queued up with other boys to enter the 2nd-floor staff toilet to wash themselves in the evening. Three boys went in together each time.

    When it was Adam's turn, he decided to 'shoot' some urine onto a younger boy standing beside him.

    The younger boy cried and immediately complained to their supervisor, Ja.
    Adam said: 'He asked me, 'You like urine so much, right?' '

    Then, the supervisor yelled to the other boys waiting outside, asking for volunteers to urinate into a blue drinking cup.

    Two boys entered the toilet and each urinated into the cup, filling it to the brim, and handed it to Ja.

    Ja summoned Adam back into the toilet and closed the door.

    Adam claimed he was told to remove his clothes but to leave his underwear on.

    Recalling the incident, the wide-eyed Primary 6 boy said he obeyed for fear of a beating.

    Urine was then slowly poured over his head as he crouched, shivering, above a squat toilet.

    Despite the boy's pleas, Ja allegedly continued pouring it till the cup was empty.

    'I said 'sorry, sorry'. I said 'please don't do like this' but he did not listen,' claimed Adam.

    By this time, more boys had gathered, causing a commotion.

    Said Adam: 'Everybody was outside... I could hear other boys laughing.'

    A 13-year-old boy who spoke separately to The New Paper on Sunday verified the incident.

    'He (the supervisor) asked us to urinate into the cup. Then I saw him pouring the urine over Adam.'

    When the ordeal was over, Ja opened the door slightly and asked the 13-year-old boy to fetch new clothes for Adam.

    Adam's brother, Sam, 14, a Sec2 student, ran down from his third-storey dormitory after hearing the commotion.

    He said: 'My brother was crying and crying. I ran to hug him.

    'Everybody thought that was the end of the punishment.'

    Ja then aimed a hose at Adam, despite the boy's plea to let him 'bathe himself.'

    Adam said: 'I felt it was disgusting. It's filthy.'

    The New Paper on Sunday spoke to 10 boys on different occasions - each confirmed the incident and the identity of the victim.

    A more brutal action of the same supervisor was related by Nazri, 12.

    The boy was almost choked to death when he was grabbed by the neck and lifted off the ground in at least two incidents about a year ago.

    'HE STRANGLED ME'
    The skinny, 1.3m tall, Pri6 student said: 'Ja strangled me.'

    Nazri said he was late for an outing one day. He did not have shoes so he wore his slippers instead.

    This provoked the fury of Ja, who said 'cannot, cannot' and dragged him back to his dormitory.

    Then came Nazri's punishment.

    He claimed: 'He (Ja) strangled my neck, then he pulled me up. He pushed me to the cupboard.'

    According to Nazri, Ja had used both his 'giant' hands to choke him, the force of which pushed him backwards.

    He added: 'When Ja strangled me, I pushed his hand. I said 'go away, it's my business if I want to wear slippers.'

    He often cries when he's alone, but says he doesn't know why.
