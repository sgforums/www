    Donating your organs while you are alive to anyone (loved one or not) is prehaps the most unnatural to do, going by your own thesis of "nature".     In fact, donating your organs after you are dead, by all reasoning is certainly more natural given that your ability to carry on your genes has now terminated and at the very least, the most rationalistic gamble to help the human race in survival is to have one last shot in boostering some other person's gene fitness in the survival of the fittest.
