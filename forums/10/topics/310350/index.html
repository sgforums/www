    We the people would have thought a leader of such intelligence like MM Lee should well know that people can forgive leaders for mistakes if mistakes are honest ones as to err is human to forgive divine.

    But what has MM Lee to deserve such understanding from its people? He has been arrogant all his life towards other people. He is always comparing his leadership with other countries' leaders and are condescending all the time to his own people.

    When such a leader makes a mistake and wants his people to believe him will he get his people to understand his problems e.g. complacency of his government.

    He has yet to show his care and concern for citizens or morality in governing the country e.g. rising costs of living or citizens' many problems with their living and working.

    Such caring for his citizens for their problems will he be able to get his citizens to understand it is human to make mistakes on the part of his million dollar ministers or other superscale prison chief in shabby administration on the part of ISD or prison.

    It is all a matter of people's reactions to his own arrogance, talk-down and condescending attitude that his leadership is world's best and infallible.

    You reap what you sow. This general unhappiness with arrogance of government has already happened in reactions over NKF, Shin Corp and UBS.

    It is not that people cannot understand mistakes can happen. It is so simple the governing philosophy that Mas Selamat's escape may be unexpected as mistakes can happen.

    There is a stronger underlying reason - the basically lack of humility and the current high-handedness and arrogance of the government are the main reason why people are unhappy whenever something like this happen.

    It is time the PM looks into his way of governing the country which is too arrogant. If he wanted to quote MM Lee as saying we are not infallible, he must make sure his government change its present arrogance and high-handedness in running this country.
