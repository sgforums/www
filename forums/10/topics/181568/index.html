    Error in numbering of polling districts

    There's been a slip-up in the numbering of 22 polling districts.

    These districts come under the electoral divisions of Bukit Panjang, Holland-Bukit Timah and Tanjong Pagar.

    The errors, however, do not change the boundaries of these three electoral divisions.

    Voters will also not be affected in any way.

    According to the Head of the Elections Department Lee Seng Lup, the error occurred during data transmission.

    Corrections have since been made.

    If you have purchased the registers of electors and electoral maps, you may return them to the Elections Department in exchange for corrected copies
