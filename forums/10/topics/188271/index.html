    He perceives people as fishes in the ocean and deal with them according to the degree of threats. If anyone simply talks and give ideas, no matter how true or how good is an idea it is of no value to him. Unless the person concerned is a bigger fish, he would not listen to him. So this ocean threat parable indeed is the very realistic way of looking at how he runs the country.

    People talk often about good ideas and how to solve certain problems and suggested to him many time to be more proactive and caring. Will he care about such problems? Look at how he replied to people's complaint about high costs of living being created by his high-wage push over the 1970s-1990s. But to him : government is a big fish and people and oppositions are all small fishes so why should he listen to them?

    So would he give way to opposition politicians and would carry on with defamation suits to check them for any fishy stories so it is a matter of who is a bigger or smaller fish in the ocean.

    So big fishes like Port Palepas and USA and China souzhou local authority will make him change not all the smaller fishes swimming in the ocean. If the smaller fishes talk about high utility and high land charges and these are the problems alright but the fishes are too small and would be ignored.

    So what if the CEOs of NKF, PSA or Comfort Taxi, essentially superannuated ex-government officers are overpaid he would not bother with the small fishes. He must see some big fishes in the ocean e.g. Maersk Lines or port palepas or Air Asia then he would make some changes to his old policies.

    Problem is : people are not yet politically matured or sophistication to become big fishes in the ocean so there will be no change to old policies.

    If Singaporeans understand the importance of their votes and not give in on all the GRCs and gerrymandering they would not have to suffer the dropping wages and rising costs while the government is denying their complaints.

    It is the real ocean we are living in and people must understand enough about the fish parable before there can be a change.
