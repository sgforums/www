    It is very simple. There is no chicken or egg condition attached when people went to the poll to elect leaders to govern the country. Even if these leaders were elected without pays or with low pays they must still do their jobs well done and not give any excuses for any failure to try to explain them away or deny them with a chicken and egg poser.

    In the election the leaders do not tell the electorate they agree to stand for election only on certain conditions like they are not answerable for any failures beyond their control.

    They must show results and people are the final judge of any results or lack of results and people will remain their master and not become their servile slaves to let them do as they like due to any propaganda.

    Leadership should include ability to win over the people of all shape and sizes personality traits or dispositions. If they have no patience or personal EQ to win over the people in general and opponents as well they are not fit to be leaders period.

    Leaders must remain calm and act professionally and above board at all times no matter how trying the circumstances may be. If they cannot accept or tolerate criticisms right or wrong and try to fix them with underhand like sending cover agents to undermine civil views and opinions they are not good leaders.

    They must pass high standards of test such as the above-mentioned in order to lead the people with justice and equity, logic, rationale setting personal example with endurance of sacrifice as necessary like Nelson Mendela or Gandhi has done.

    Good leaders must answer to problems and issues as disclosed in NKF and not sue critics or oppositions for trying to question them over such problems.

    It is simply not right to put up all kinds of propaganda to make people look bad in order to make themselves too better especially in overseas visits to Middle East recently.

    This kind of government can be described simply as "Chih Li Bah Wai" or loosely translated "Biting the fingers that feed food" or "Getting filled at home while crawling out to serve others"

    It is wrong to use power to get their way like legalistic wrangling or passing all sorts of laws to get their way or to increase their own pays and rewards or undermine fair and free election like the GRC

    From the above review of our governing system it should be quite clear that our governing system has left much to be desired.

    This type of government is parochial and not sustainable especially in the internet age.

    Many loyal citizens here have put up their views on this subject with logic and rationale. There is an urgent need to overhaul the whole governing system for the better.

    Otherwise the government will continue to face criticisms and unhappiness as seen all over the island.

    Moreover our leaders are being paid handsomely at the world's highest salary for iron ricebowl bill collector without accountablity for major mistakes or errors as seen in Mas Selamat.

    He who pays the piper calls the tune. So it is wrong to keep denying or pushing away any problem by blaming on the people for wnatever reason.
