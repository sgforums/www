There is some... office politics going on currently, so bewarned, anyone doing the same thing as yourself is tikaming 'send like no send'. Sometimes back, most NOT all of these papers are diligently passed to respect depts, but that's because the admin were kind enough, not because it's their duty. How I know? Not important. Once the kind/good people all gone, of course all other papers other than cheques, or application of credit a/c, will have to tikam heavily.

    Besides, all I can say is there is currently some other projects going on that relates to various depts processing details.

Any organisation is roughly the same, we either have good people to run and stablise the show, or we have wars running.     Just as a (resigned) old colleague commented when we met last month, '...something big is going to happen...'.

    Go quickly to any OCBC centre and properly do your updates, and email a letter to the customer service stating the issue to cover yourself. Don't be a sacrifice of others' error.
