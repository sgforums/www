    Such lack of talents in economic restructuring has been the main factor for our last two deep recessions. Perhaps after Dr. Goh Keng Swee has left there was no heavy weight minister including LKY himself who are equally able and capable to create whole new technology start-ups or new industries to create value-adding jobs for our new graduates or citizens. Up to now LHL who was supposed to oversee the entire economic restructuring exercise during the last two recessions have yet to produce the desired final results.

    LKY probably is only relying on property and location play to get by with the help of world recovery to say he was successful without any real solid success to sustain the frail economy.

    However at the first sign of world recovery of which Singapore was among the last of the Asian dragons to recover from about 2004 once the property began to move due to economic cycle the ministers and MM Lee quickly claimed for credit talking about leadership and many theories like all foreigners will create jobs for citizens when actually the real intention was to suppress wages of citizens to be competitive. It looks like other than cutting CPF and citizens' wages and open the door to cheap foreign labor on the excuse of talent there is no other solutions to restructuring.

    Intrinsically our own domestic sector of the economy is still very frail and weak thriving mainly on some retailing and services and we have not created in-depth strategic manufacturing facilities as smaller countries like Finland or Switzerland have done.

    During any recessions these foreign companies would be more attracted to lower-cost countries as seen during past recessions leaving us very much in a lurch.

    Through years of one-tracked cutting of citizens' wages and promoting foreign investments at the expense of own SMEs and domestic sector and strategic skill training and technology-start-ups, the whole economy has been downgraded to third world falling behind Korea, Taiwan and even Hong Kong in terms of creating major world-beating computer, or manufacturing world-needed products or services.

    The ministers are only keen in throwing monies at foreign investors but not create more incentives for our own domestic businesses which have to struggle against all kinds of taxes and rising business costs in utility and transportation etc.

    Surely if our ministers have the talents they would not have neglected promoting our own local businesses first before promoting the foreigners. There has been a total lack of understanding of the need to promote hands-on practical knowledge applications and incentivise manufacturing of our own technology and knowledge-based products.

    A first-world country is one which should excel in terms of practical knowledge applications, innovation and abilities to produce world-needed products by being able to motivate our own citizens and just keep throwing money incentives at foreign companies or engaging in property play IR or being at the mercy of world economy.

    This kind of wage-cutting pro-foreigner government could only be good in putting up showy look-good projects like various world-class hubbing or biotech or biomedcal which have yet to make any major breakthrough.

    In any world slow down or recession we will again see the true color of this type of look-good pretentious talent-talk boasting. It will be another repeat of the last two recessions with no particular areas of excellence. People will once again lose their jobs by thousands and foreigners will again be leaving us to go elsewhere to cream another golden pots and take us for fools.

    So judge for yourself : Is LKY that world-best as he has claimed?
