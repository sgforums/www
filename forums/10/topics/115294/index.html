    I can give you a summary of what's like to stay in Victoria , Australia.
    You need at least 18K -20K for your rent , food, bills , books on top of your tuition fees. That is a bare minimum estimate.

    Depending on your student visa to survive playing a russian roulette. You can work at most 20 hrs/week during a school semester. Most chinese restuarants will employ waiters/waitresses but they pay bare minimum and sometimes illegally low ( 4-6 dollars per week).

    If you are a nurse, the hospitals will employ you on a casual basis because they need nurses. Otherwise tough luck getting jobs.

    You can opt to study distance in Singapore and complete your final year in australia.
