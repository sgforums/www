    Annoyingly, the papers have again stated that Singaporeans are shying away from cleaning jobs. But as I see it, how could ANYONE survive on a $1,000.00 salary apart from the status of being a cleaner? Where's the logic?

    A local person who has children can't even get by decently on a $1,500.00 salary, what on earth can $700 to $800 or $1,000 do??

    Personally, even if they offer $3,000 and above, I'd still not want to take up such a job - I'd be wasting the money I spent "upgrading" myself.

    When is the media and our government going to see that it isn't the image of such jobs, but survivability of such low salaries!!??
