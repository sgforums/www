    Saw many threads here critising the PAP government mainly for the foreign talent and gst increase.

    While I am not a great supporter of PAP, I will like to offer some perspective on these 2 issues.

    GST increase

    - I think increasing GST is really no need for us to have such a big hoo ha reaction towards it. Afterall, prices including GST will increase. Moreover, the government will be giving us money too from the funds generated from increase in gst

    Foreign talent

    - Low wage foreigner workers doing jobs which singaporeans do not want such as construction worker etc.

    I think there is no need for us to be jealous of them as they are doing jobs which we singaporeans do not want.

    -High wage workers doing professional jobs such as Accountants etc.

    While we maybe angry that they are here to snatch jobs from us, why not think about it in this way.

    When these people come to singapore to work, being high wage earners, they will spend alot of money on food,education, rental or some may want to buy houses, leisure etc. When they spend money, these will generate the economy of Singapore which will in turn create more jobs for us.

    Moreover, these foreigners maybe earning alot of money but after deducting all the expenses, they do not really have that much money left.

    So why be jealous of them?

     

    The only thing that I am not happy with these foreign talents is the government sponsoring their education fees in polytechnics and university which is alot of money
