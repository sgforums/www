    Yet, with the NE, NS, and EW MRT lines intersecting each other at City Hall and Raffles Place - (and with the lines running through older buildings along Robinson Road-Cecil Street, North Bridge Road-South Bridge Road, Serangoon-Upper Serangoon Road, Victoria Street-Sims Avenue-Eunos) - those old buildings along these roads seems to be unaffected.
