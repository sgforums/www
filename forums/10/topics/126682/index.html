    Hello Farmer Brown,

    Whether there will be jobs for returning overseas graduates or not depends on how entrepreneurial and creative now is the government.

    Many past policies of government are too restrictive, or only concentrating on controls and not facilitation. Such narrow policies benefitting only government's own efficiencies and report cards do not lead to more jobs.

    There must be big changes to all these narrow and restrictive policies like the self-promoting or self-benefitting tax-and-recover charging of all fees. There should be facilitation of citizens and businesses to create a more pro-business and pro-people to grow future business.

    Many of us in our own different ways are posting here to make government understand that a knowledge-based new world needs every citizen as elite and talent to contribute to all areas of excellence and competitiveness and not just a few connected to their limited network.

    But people must understand and support good corporate governance too as without people showing an interest sadly nothing will change.
