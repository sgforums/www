    Although small in size, Singapore has made major contribution with the talents of Singaporeans like:

    Dr Tommy Koh
    Dr Kishore Mahbubani
    Dr Chan Heng Chee

    all of whom had served as Singapore's Ambassador to the UN.

    Dr Tommy Koh had chaired the Law of the Sea Conference, guiding the many conflicting self-interests of so many member countries, to finally achieve an elaborate, sensible final agreement that was internationally accepted by all nations - big and small - in the conduct of relations with each other.

    This important piece of International Law gave as much protection over the economic and political interests of large nations, as well as that of smaller nations, over the wealth in the large expanse of open sea, as well as to the rights of passage through territorial waters that are narrow and enclosed.

    The United Nations also sponsors or support the following bodies in which Singapore is also an active member of:-

    SECURITY COUNCIL
    DISARMAMENT AND INTERNATIONAL SECURITY
    WORLD HEALTH ORGANIZATION
    ASIAN DEVELOPMENT BANK
    INTERNATIONAL MONETARY FUND
    NON-ALIGNED MOVEMENT

    for more information, you may wish to visit the following site:

    http://www.sda.sk/MUN/MUN%20particip.htm
