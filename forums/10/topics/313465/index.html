    Westminster Framework of government has already provided the more effective model which will prevent abuse and hypocrisy from taking over of power by a few to serve their own private agenda.

    What were copied from Westminster was merely the few facets lile election, parliament and some semblance of legitimacy in law making.

    If Westminster is wholesomely implemented we will not have a MM who insisted on everyone following his will to do his bidding as is happening up to now and the present parliament.

    From the 1970s we have been having much difficulty to change from power that be system to a more open and pro-people and pro-motivation system as Hong Kong and South Korea and Scandinavian countries are and will probably be enjoying for many years.

    All the tweaking of laws to control the people to suit the ruling party should be disbanded together with GRC to revert to the original democratic Westminster model if we want to grow further from here as a people.
