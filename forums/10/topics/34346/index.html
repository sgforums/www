    Based on the continuous broadcast by CNN and BBC World News, it seems that when the Space Shuttle took off about 2 weeks ago, some debris from the fuel tank had fallen off and hit the "insulation tiles" below the Space Shuttle.

    The "insulation tiles" below the Space Shuttle were lost during the take off, and these "insulation tiles" were critically needed to form a heat shield below the Spare Shuttle during its re-entry into earth.

    With a few pieces of "insulation tiles" lost at the critical areas on the wings of the Space Shuttle, the heat built-up at temperatures of over 3000 degrees C, affected the Space Shuttle that was built in aluminium, which then disintegrated at a height of over 40 miles above Texas, USA.
