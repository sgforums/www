    I am in favour of legislation, although I feel the one proposed by Ms Eunice Olsen in Parliament is too restrictive against employers.

    When the Monthly Variable Component (MVC) was introduced, the tripartite partners tried to promote it but was not for legislation. At a meeting with representatives of SNEF (in May 2001), I proposed legislation which without will not be welcomed by employers who may find they need to pay more. They did not comment, perhaps considering, to give them benefit of doubt.

    6 years on, MVC has not been heard in the local newspaper. If it was successful, I am sure it would. Economic downturns are a cyclical trend and we may dread the next one will once again see a cut in our CPF employer contributions.

    Similarly, I believe the problem of employing older workers will not be solved without some form of legislation.
