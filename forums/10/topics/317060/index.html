Proposal to amend GRC voting system

  • robertteh
    10 May 08, 10:22

    There is little justification for GRC in the context of advancement to a more mature democracy which will motivate every citizen to work together with a more accountable governing party - through participation of every citizen in selecting the best individuals to form the most accountable government for the best of the country.

    If GRC must be maintained, for racial harmony in recognizing the above-mentioned democratic principle, which is universally recognized today as the most effective system of government then GRC if adopted should be symbolic and not used as an excuse to tweak the voting system to favor the incumbent through bending the constitution or electoral boundaries or group entrenchment devices and it should be balanced and not be island-wide.

    Cut down GRC drastically to four zones to represent our culture to accommodate minority races to have representatives in parliament. In all other areas let the minority candidates fight on their own merit which they need to have if they want to represent their minority races as majority race candidates do not have the privilege to be elected if they just enter the ticket of election except too by their own personal qualities and appeal to voters.

    Let us grow up and let people have a fair chance to elect the kind of leaders they want openly without being controlled or constrained by political tweaking of the constitutional right to elect their best individual candidates of any race in any area.

    If a minority race is elected by the majority race like Obama in US that will show we have naturally matured politically as a society. Cooking of GRC is narrow-minded and selfish and should be disbanded or at most tempered to prevent the whole country's being controlled by any incumbent single party.
