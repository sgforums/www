    There... It's Chinese New Year's Eve and one of my young cousins, aged about 10, was playing firecrackers and fireworks on his street.
    But unlucky enough, one of his crackers flew to a front of a Malaysian police patrol car...

    To solve this problem, my cousin gave the policeman a RM10 Ang Bao and the policeman open one eye and shut the other...

    So much FUN, I was having with the firecrackers and fireworks though it was illegal...

    Scene 2:

    I saw this WITH MY OWN EYES!

    It was CNY and was a Malaysian public holiday for all. But there were "hardworking" people in Malaysia. One of them were the Malaysian Trash Collectors, without them trash would have accumulate at home during CNY.

    There... It was CNY's first day. Happily all my relatives and I was busy having fun. Then... A smell came... and flies came... Then we saw a garbage truck at out doorstep...

    To avoid giving Ang Bao to them, the garbage men, we send our kid to give them garbage only... But then, the garbage men did not leave but was compressing garbage with a little garbage water *Urg * Then we quickly gave them a Ang Bao and ask them to leave.

    The Ang Bao giving was very much, almost traditional every CNY, my relatives say.

    Though nothing really much to fuss about, since it was a happy CNY. But this really "helps" in the reputation of the garbage men... (Well, they already did not have a good image...)
