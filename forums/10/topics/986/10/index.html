    Theres no permanent USAF aircraft based at Singapore. There is however a training unit, the 497th Combat Training Sqn, which is part of the USAF's Pacific Air Forces, PACAF stationed at PLA. The Commando Sling agreement with the USAF allows up 6 deployments of 6-8 USAF aircraft to Singapore for training with the RSAF or RSAF and RAAF, called Ex Commando Sling(USAF + RSAF) and Ex Slingaroo (USAF RAAF and RSAF).

    The exchanges have tapered off somewhat in the past few years, but I remember the best years were from 95-98, where the deployments were all year round and I got to see USAF units from all over the world in Singapore including USAF F-15s and F-16s from units from Japan and Korea(most common) and even from places like Utah Alsaka Arizona Florida and Air National Guard units from South Dakota and Louisiana.

    Check out the 497 CTS homepage http://www.sing.af.mil/
