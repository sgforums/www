    Aljunied GRC MP Cynthia Phua asks PAP leadership about ward line-up
    By Asha Popatlal, Channel NewsAsia

    A change in the People's Action Party line-up of faces for Aljunied GRC may take place in the next General Election.

    The recent electoral boundaries report showed that Mrs Lim Hwee Hua's Serangoon ward has moved from Marine Parade GRC to Aljunied GRC.

    Considering the change, Madam Cynthia Phua was asked if she would continue as MP in the 5-member Aljunied GRC.

    Madam Phua said she had spoken to the party leadership about it.

    "Two days ago, I discussed this issue with the leadership. Among the six of us, one person must go. We must be mentally prepared. I hope to stay here but I leave it to the party leadership to decide," she told reporters at a skills training event to teach hawkers about good service.

    Madam Phua said Mrs Lim would be welcomed if she was fielded in Aljunied, as her performance is good.

    But Madam Phua added that it was also possible someone new could be fielded.

    As for the opposition, the Workers' Party, team led by its Chairman Sylvia Lim, is expected to contest in Aljunied GRC. - CNA/ir
