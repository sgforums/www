    1. Parents are never always correct, if the child disagree with the parents, the child may voice their disagreement, but should do it with respect. Freedom of speech with love.
    2. If I am proven wrong, I will apologise. I expect the same from them.
    3. Respect each other's space and property. Ask permission when entering each others private zone eg cupboards and drawers. I ask permission when opening my sons' drawers and school bags.
    4. Objective and realistic goals are set. When in doubt, please clarify. This is important when we look at our children and decide what they can do. Many times, such goals are unrealistics (eg getting band 1 ALL the time).
