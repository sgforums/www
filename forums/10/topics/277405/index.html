    Young PAP to hold monthly info sessions to reach out to Singaporeans

    Channel NewsAsia - Saturday, September 1 SINGAPORE

    The Young PAP kicked off its drive to reach out to more Singaporeans with a large-scale information session on Saturday.

    The auditorium was packed with those interested in joining the Young PAP fold and they came prepared with questions for Members of Parliament Teo Ser Luck and Liang Eng Hwa.

    Mr Teo says: "When I look at the questions that they ask, I find that they have a keen interest to know more, they have a keen interest to want to participate in the activities. Most of them were more concerned about their time and commitment that they need to commit in order to join those activities on the ground."

    This is the first of many more dialogue sessions the Young PAP will be organising monthly to reach out to young Singaporeans.

    About 200 of them attended the session at the PAP headquarters on Saturday and at least half of them are first timers.

    Each of these dialogue sessions will be helmed by an MP. - CNA/ch
