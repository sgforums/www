    I have learnt today, to my demise, that the very institution (BANKS) that makes money its business, are charging a service fee for exchange of coins to notes. So, depositing $2 worth of 1 cents coins will set me back by 50%, and depositing $200 worth of $1 coints will set be back by 0.5% !!! It is abosutely hard to understand the rational. I wonder if this is the banking industry practise outside Singapore. Or, if this is Uniquely Singapore (mind the STB punt) where our local banks are really stretching the entrepreneurship spirit to the limit.
