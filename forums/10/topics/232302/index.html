    Customers are willing to buy seats coz the stall owner claim that the seat is heavily subsidized. You see a seat is always a hot item on busy days, thus by the creative calculations of the stall owner, worth alot of $$$. If you own a seat, you will always have a seat even on busy days. Imagine, you don't need to stand around to wait on busy days. For that convenience, you pay a subsidized price because you are a loyal customer.

    Why would anyone want to go to another chicken rice stall owner when there is such an efficient and creative one?
