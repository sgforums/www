    SINGAPORE: Some of Singapore's outstanding architectural works are getting noticed in Europe.

    They are now featured in an exhibition at the prestigious architectural and urban design Aedes Gallery in Berlin, Germany.

    The gallery is the first private architecture gallery in Europe and is renowned for recognising and promoting leading architecture around the world.

    The Urban Redevelopment Authority (URA) said the architectural works featured are a testament to Singapore's growing reputation as a well-designed city and a talent base for Asia and beyond.

    The showcase by URA exhibits the work of 20 Singapore architects under the age of 45 - from trailblazing residential projects to commercial developments in Dubai.

    The exhibition panels are on display at the Aedes Gallery from 28 March-12 May this year.
