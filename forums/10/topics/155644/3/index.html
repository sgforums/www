    That's a fair argument. Yes, Singaporeans have the right to choose the society that they live in. However, they also need to realize that some of (and at least part of) their opinions are not shared by others. what puts me off is that the reasons for their opposition are often trivial and fail to get to the point. Perhaps they leave the real details (all the 2nd hand smoking stuff) to the experts, but to write such inconsequential letters to the forum reflects something about their mindset.
