Public Rubbish bins are getting extinct

  • MobyDog
    18 Jan 06, 19:12
    Gees.. have anyone noticed that Public rubbish bins are getting scarce by the year ? I mean I have been notice the reduction since those contracts have been privatised.

    Carparks

    There used to be a rubbish bin attached to every other lamp post at the carparks and a main bin at every 50 metres. Then reduced to every two other lamp post and 2 to 4 bins at every big carparks and 1 or 2 at medium size carparks.

    Now, my carpark (Medium/large)has only 2 bins and all bins attched to lamp post are gone.

    Viod decks

    Used to have 4 to 6 bins, now only one at lift landing and one at the sitting area. of course there will be one next to RC (for obvious reasons).

    Streets

    Along most roads with pavement, It seems almost extinct.

    Only at bus stops, it seems.

    Now, not that this is a big issue, but sometimes I seemed to have urge just to dump my rubbish by the road side. I mean I looked around and don't seem to be able to see any bins. Where there are, they are out of my way and is 100m away. Look at Orchard rd, you noticed tat the Bins are far in between.

    Gosh.. I don't wished to be caught in the future and serve community service. But I'm afraid I have a weak will. All the past government anti-littering campaign seems to be getting away from me. Sigh>
