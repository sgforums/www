    It seems that you don't know what a migrant is. For the sake of simplicity, take it as equivalent to PR. You can therefore be an Australian migrant and a Singaporean citizen at the same time. Once you have held migrant status for two years, you are eligible to apply for citizenship - the only difference between the two after that point is that you get voting rights as a citizen.
