    Does it mean that we must suck up to every superpower? Even though they might have a stronger and better economy than us, do we Singaporeans have to lower ourselves to this standard? What makes you think that the PRC Chinese will accept us? I have been studying in the university for 3 full years and I can clearly see that the a large majority of the PRCs and other foreign students tend to stick to their own group, rather than spend time interacting with us locals. Let me put it this way, if they are a minority in Singapore, they will try to interact with us so that they can better survive here. However, now they are existing at such large number that they don't need to do so. They are now creating their own community and not integrating into our society! Now this is the real problem.

    For those who have been emphasizing on the migrant nature of Singapore, let me remind you of this fact. We spend 40+ years to build up our own identity, taking form of common "improper" Singlish as our form of communication, common love of food from various other cultures etc. I am proud to say that even when I go overseas, all I need is to listen to someone talking on his or her phone to know if they are fellow Singaporeans. Now, do we really want to demolish all these foundations that we have built? To go back to the times where Raffles divided the different races into different areas? This is the reason why Singapore has so many racial riots and tension in the early years.

    I am alright in facing and competing with them in University. But I felt disadvantaged instead and this is the main problem. First, we have to serve NS, and right after that, we entered University. After two years of brain inactivity. Now, we are faced with scholars that are brought over from different countries to fight with us. I am still alright with that. But, while we are given one year free stay in hostel (I am living 2 hours drive from the University), they are given 2 years so as to adjust to a "foreign land". However, it means that while we locals are fighting hard to balance study and CCAs, they are free to concentrate on their study in one entire year! Now that is one of the explanation  why their result are better than us. I am aware of this as the foreign students went flloding to elect for office bearers position for different societies and clubs in their second year, and some got elected with the "help" from their fellow friends.

    Secondly, the Government always said that our study is subsidized. But do you know that the foreign students are also entitled to the same subsidy? They do have an additional condition, that is to work in Sinagpore for three years. So do we locals get extra subsidy if we work in Singapore for three years? Lastly, we have to keep our fitness at peak as we got to report for IPPT every year, while the foreign students are all welcome to spend their weekends off to wander and enjoy their life in Singapore. Furthermore, for those who failed (I am the lucky one, lol), they had to go for RT after their lessons.

    There are many who said that it is our duty to serve the country, which I agreed too. But the problem is why the country is shortchanging us while we are doing our best for her? Why are the foreigners given so much advantages over us?
