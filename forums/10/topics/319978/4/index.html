    To come back to the topic....is there a better gov to run Singapore..

    I personally cannot envision such a scenario...

    Someone pointed out before, Potong Pasir is a poor area, and Orchard, district 10 always PAP.

    This makes sense, and also a natural phenomenon.

    In a working and fine country, the ones who will become the opposition are the disgruntled people. Disenfranchized people. They form these breakaway groups apart from the center group.

    This is not to badmouth or insult them, this is just a discussion, no disrespect.

    So.....I find it really hard to imagine there is another PAP clone kind of opposition....maybe Singapore size is too small.....or PAP is very good in recruiting the potential ones...whatever the reason, fact is, the opposition now is in another class. And they are serving a certain niche or ideology.

    If we see, for instance Workers Party.

    Have u ever thought how come the name is Workers Party?  and then the logo, there is hammer actually....it is quite similar to Soviet style sickle.

    So my thinking is....this is another reason why opposition will never win....not only because of lack of talents, it is also because they serve a certain niche ideology, not in the center. It will attract some people, but not enough people.

    Judging from what they persue, I rather have PAP continue to govern Singapore. PAP is the macro-managers, center-positioned, and very much a technocrats party. They will persue whatever policy is good for the overall future of Singapore.

    WP, and other opposition, I tell you something. Their brand of vision will be bad for Singapore, overall. Why..? Because these are disgruntled people with niche ideology. It will be bad to have them rule over the majority.

    One example, easy one.......in election campaigns, a theme getting repeated in recent years is the issue of so-called Foreign Talents (FT).

    You will see the oppositions saying all sorts of things against FT.....

    But is that good for overall health of Singapore?

    This brand of politics is what will derail Singapore, fake populism and pandering.

These 2 phenomenons play to the interest of disgruntled peoples of their societies but actually harm their long term objective as a successful country.

    That is why I dont like to see WP, SDP, winning. In my opinion it is harmful to Singapore's interest.
