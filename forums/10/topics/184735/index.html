The longest holdout of the last 82 year Old

  • Atobe
    2 Apr 06, 14:34


    With the Chief Justice YPH stepping down at the age of 80, and Tony Tan finally leaving politics at the YOUNG age of 70plus, and more MPs leaving politics at even YOUNGER age of 50s and 60s, when will the 82 year old MM LKY leave the political scene ?

    What possibly can be the reason for him to hang on to the GIC Chair, and to Tanjong Pagar Constituency - (which he hardly appear to perform his MP duties, which are handled by a proxy aka a stand-in) ?

    Are there skeletons in the cupboard that need to be hidden for generations to come, which explains for his insistence in staying on in politics, and holding on to the strings to political power ?

    Could the reason for a strong ONE PARTY DOMINANCE is to prevent any Opposition Party from coming into Power and discover the vast amount of abuses and losses that have never seen daylight ?

    It took the overthrow of Sadam Hussein to discover the abuses of his decades in power in Iraq, and it took the overthrow of Ferdinand Marcos to discover the 2000 pairs of shoes in the cupboard that Imelda Marcos bought with Public Funds besides the other shocking abuse of power in the Philippines by the First Family locking the nation in Martial Law for decades.

    What will we find in our Singapore Political Cabinet if ever a new Political Party take over the reins of Government ?
