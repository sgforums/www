    Many people knows that water from the well could taste very different, sometimes, even "sweet".

    There are research done in the world on how water affects the health of human being. In certain places where water quality is good, people live longer.

    There is one group of thought that says contaminations to our water supply is the major cause of an alarming rise in cancer rate. Thus, they argue, that we should only drink distilled water. However some argue that distill water lacks minerals, thus, not healthy at all.

    There is currently a world wide research on "healthy water"; It is bascially due to the fact that the human body is make up of 70% water, that makes it critical for human health. The latest findings sum up one critical point, it is not distilled water that we need, but "magnetized" water that we need for our health.

    Through various research (including Chinese university, Chin Hua University), it is found that what makes the well water "sweet" is the natural magnetism of the Earth's magnetic forces. Artificial water treatment has made water become "hard", loosing the natural magnetism. It is a matter of "hard" or "soft" water that affects human health. Most of the time, chlorinated or flourided water treatment had made the water "hard". The molecular structure of the treated water become much denser, heavier. These research argues that if these water are being "magnetized", it could make it "soft", loosen up the molecular structure. It makes the water taste a bit "sweet" like well water. This "magnetized" water are said to help the digestive system to stay healthy. Depending on how the water is being magnetized, it could provide different functions. (IT could be North Pole, South Pole or Double Pole magnetized).

    Whatever it is, Singapore's New Water is not going to be a good source. Neither is distilled water, which is basically "hard" water. Boiling distilled water may make it harder.

    Thus, as it says, if you could get your water source "magnetized", you could get better quality water. The so call "energy" water you see in the market are basically "magnetized" water. It could be proven a cheap and easier way to keep a healthy body afterall.
