    What is leadership? What are leaders supposed to accmplish ? Are they supposed to be forceful, pragmatic, or full of good presentations, rhetorics, concepts and assumptions ?

    In this topic, we will examine the unique qualities, abilities or attributes that leaders should possess in order to solve diverse problems, set direction and unite people into taking collective actions to strive for maximum good of the maximum number.

    In my personal opinion, the following are some qualities or criteria required of the leaders:-

    (1) Passion,
    (2) Ability to Motivate followers,
    (3) Ability to get people to work with coordination and apply knowledge.
    (4) Ability to Translate goals and objectives into implementation processes to accomplish goals e.g. bring about competitiveness, technology start-ups and jobs etc.
    (5) Invest monies and perform tasks with Accountability and Transparency
    (6) Collective leadership by core team to make major decisions,
    (7) Sense of integrity to avoid self-centred assumptions or controls that stifle mass participation in knowledge application.

    In whatever actions he takes, a leader must serve the followers with reliability, or sense of justice, empathy with the poor or concern with social and economic wellbeing of all.

    A leader should be honourable, possess the highest integrity and not engage in artful arguments or fallacies or rhetorics or empty promises.

    He should understand that the need to strive for social and cultural progress to sustain conditions for economic vibrancy and competitiveness of people in their respective fields and would unleash their skills and talents.

    He should practise broad-based practical education for his people which will lead to widespread application of technology and knowledge.

    In the fast-paced technological age of internet, people need empowerments of participative processes and facilitation to compete together as a team. Only when a government is collective and empowering the people will it lead to development of creative, entrepreneurial and proactive and practical applications of knowledge by all in every field.

    Leaders who have spent to much of energy in controls and regulations, or taxing and recovering of costs to the full market values will cause hardship.

    Today's leaders should recognise people are the true partners of business or enterprise so that the masses could be totally involved in decision making and working towards results together as a team.

    In this way they can then leverage the many resources at work and mobilise the people to realize technologicial start-ups and value-add economic competitiveness which currently could only be talked about.

    Today's leaders should be collective open and consultative possessing the total functional abililites and total scope and radar range to cover the whole spheres of social culture and economic exchanges and leveling up of competitiveness. They should level up to objective management to avoid self-interests and obsessive controls which are not consonent with the majoirity's vibrancy and growth.

    Process management lays out duties and responsibilities of the whole team to enable and empower the team to sustain efficiency and productivity and entrepreneurship with coordination and application of knowledge at all level to attain results to attain the movement's objectives.
