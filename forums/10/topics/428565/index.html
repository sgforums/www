    Speaking at a press conference to introduce the second batch of WP candidates, Mr Low said that Mr Mah’s attacks on the WP were an attempt to distract voters from the issue of flat prices.


    “I would advise (Mr Mah) not sidetrack from the issue of public housing that the WP has raised in its manifesto,” said the Hougang MP.


    “The WP is saying that the current flat prices are too high and would be a financial burden for younger generations of Singaporeans. What is the government’s response to that?”


    Mr Low also defended the WP’s proposals for the government to lower the price of state land for HDB blocks, and denied that this was tantamount to “raiding the reserves”.


    He said that, based on such logic, the PAP has been guilty of taking from the reserves for years.


    “The PAP’s election budget and the Grow and Share Package to please the voters also mean that we will have less contributions to the reserves. This is also raiding the reserves!!” he said.
