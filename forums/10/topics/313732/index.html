    The government has over the years been using propaganda to paint a picture as if without the authoritarian leadership of LKY everything will fall into chaos.

    Nothing can be further from the truth. Today people have matured with education and travel etc and they do know what are rights and wrongs although they do not generally express them in public.

    Just talk to a group of reasonably educated people and you will be surprised they have quite good ideas what were right and wrong with our current government policies and what would be good for the nation going forward.

    What are need now is active ongoing political evolution to a model which will capture the good and reject the bad. It will take some years before people will fully realize the goodness of democracy. It is only with democracy all citizens can play a greater part in nation building and we could again feel the confidence and buzz in every aspect of our national life.

    Active citizens play an important role here. As a young nation we have yet to be given the chance to mature and benefit from the full goodness of democracy such as that of the Westminster model.

    We should stop blindly listening to all the past and current propaganda of the government that western democracy is no good which will cause instability. Such propaganda has been driven into our national consciousness so much over the past 40 years that many have believed in such propaganda.

    Look at the democracy being followed by Scandinavian countries, Australia, New Zealand, South Korea, Taiwan or Hong Kong. They have achieved much progress without causing so much problems to their ordinary citizens because their citizens are motivated to play a greater role in contributing to nation building which in our case is totally claimed to be the achievement of the power that be.

    system of government people are more motivated and more civic minded and contributing to greater stability and progress to their nations.

    On the other hand more and more tyrannical and authoritarian regimes everywhere are causing oppressions and great suffering and even genocide.

    I am afraid our authoritarian rule is too deteriorating by the days with the press media timidly reporting good news while active citizens are giving up playing their positive role as seen in the more advanced democracies which our leaders carefully avoided comparison with.

    Our brand of authoritarian governing system disguised as Westminster democracy is bringing about a deeply divided and fractured society and people are frustrated with all the power play and arrogance and callousness of the whole system relying only on power to get its way.

    If unchecked it will lead to instability, explosions or implosions. At least 10 citizens who have the means are migrating every day as they see little or no future in such an authoritarian rule.

    Even foreigners who took up PR or citizenship here may not stay cohesive or loyal being interested in coming here for their jobs as they eventually will be just as unhappy with oppressions seen every day in the way government is running this place for its own selfish interests and agenda.

    If our leaders sincerely want to avoid chaos they should ease off their suppressions and oppressions and let citizens be free to play their role in upholding justice and equity or freedoms of choice in electing their government.

    What solution do we have as a people? I have suggested in other thread we should go back to the original institutional Westminster model which will work better balancing the need for leadership with accountability and responsbility.

    People will stay as master not the servants in such a system. They will be empowered to exercise their role again to vote good MPs of their free choice but be forced by GRCs into all the walkovers and from such people power we will proceed to evolve our own best political system most suitable for us.

    Let us active citizens ponder over the full goodness of institutional Westminster Model which contains all the proper check and balance which is the only way to motivate all the work together to build our own good model eventually.

    Abolish the GRC. Expose all the selfish or self-centred propaganda against democracy. Open up and let all citizens have equal opportunity to participate in election or be freely permitted to stand election without all the impediment from the GRC.
