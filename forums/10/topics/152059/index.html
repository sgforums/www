    I beg to differ.
    Where did Singaporeans get the idea that they were "whiners", "fussy employees" and "quitters"?

    These were thrown up by the media and the labels stayed stuck to the minds of Singaporeans, because everytime someone wishes to express a view, he gets labelled as a "whiner". It is a psychological trick of the media because a pre-conceived negative image forms even before the message is heard.

    Personally, i have been to many forums (like Sammyboy) and while there are many who are whining (due to frustration or other reasons), there are also many who offer highly reasonable views on a variety of subjects. Indeed, i was able to bounce off quite a few ideas from their replies. Anyway, expressing a view is hardly whining --- on internet forums, it gives a guage on how the Internet community views the problem.
