Suggested New Manifestos to the ministers

  • robertteh
    22 Jan 06, 23:20

    The ongoing debates over the new manifestos unveiled by the Workers' Party perhaps has opened up a certain pandora box of sorts - are the existing manifestos of political parties including those of the incumbent party in power adequate to address today's needs and aspirations of the citizens.

    For example, do they provide guidance and inspirations to the parties to strive to fulful fresh challenges in the new age. In my personal opinion, the new party Manifestos of today should cover the following basic needs and aspirations of the people:-

    (1) Identify new challenges, set new directions and create social and cultural aohesiveness among the people to bring about a vibrant and caring classless society free from elitism and discriminations.

    (2) Set high standards of morality among leaders e.g. leading by personal examples so as to more effectively mobilize the people to accept new challenges and sacrifices, acceptance of long-term pains for the sake of preserving competitiveness and survival, fiscal prudence through creating annual budget surpluses and reserves and charging of full market costs through corporations of essential medical, housing and transportation services.

    (3) Regular review of policies that may cause problems and hardship among the less endowed section of the population e.g. meritocracy, granting of benefits like scholarships, employment or investment opportunities to foreigners etc.

    In the light of the problems uncovered in the NKF saga and the white elephant fructus, it would appear necessary to re-examine basic policies and solve diverse problems, set direction so as to find new common grounds to better unite people into accepting discomforts of certain good but painful policies for the collective good of all. It is only through the new challenges that all citizens can be expected to strive for maximum good of the maximum number.

    LEADERSHIP QUALITIES

    Leaders of the new age in particular should be selected and groomed to lead people with personal examples and good corporate governance. Leaders in particular should be expected to possess certain basic qualities :-

    (1) Passion,
    (2) Ability to Motivate followers,
    (3) Ability to get people to work with coordination and apply knowledge.
    (4) Ability to translate complex goals and ideas into implementation processes to bring about competitiveness, technology start-ups and jobs etc.
    (5) Invest monies and perform tasks with Accountability and Transparency
    (6) Collective leadership by core team to make major decisions,
    (7) Sense of integrity free from theoretical assumptions or controls that stifle mass participation in knowledge application.

    PATIENCE AND DETERMINATION

    Many a times, past policies failed not because of lack of intelligence or academic abilities but because of lack in other areas e.g. patience and determination to motivate and persuade people to common platforms for the collective good of the nation. Such beliefs should be practicable and attainable and of benefit to the people not just in the long term but short and medium term and should be free from conceptual stuffs which might cause wastage of time and resources and hardship.

    HUMILITY AND RELIABILITY

    :eaders should be selected from people who are in possession of visible humility of servant, reliability, sense of justice, empathy to attain social and economic wellbeing of all.

    HONOUR AND INTEGRITY

    He should be honourable, possess integrity and not engage in money, coflicts of personal or interest in contracts or self-reward or self-promotion or assumptions based on fallacies or rhetorics or empty promises.

    SOCIAL AND CULTURAL VIBRANCY

    Beside the above-stated basic qualities, leader should cultivate in-depth understanding of the intimate relationship between social, cultural and economic characteristics of the people so as the people could be made to nurture and unleash their skills and talents of the people through interactions to reach ultimeate vibrancy and excellence.

    BROAD-BASED EDUCATION AND KNOWLEDGE APPLICATION

    Leaders should strive to be practical and result-oriented not just on account of personal efficiency but the overall efficiency of the whole team. They should constantly equip them with skills and knowledge application abilities through broad-based practical education.

    USUAL MISTAKES - JUSTIFICATIONS AND DENIALS

    Finally, leaders should avoid resort to justifications or excuses or denials of problems thereby blurring the accountability or transparency in implementation of correct and equitable major policy or performance mistakes.

    IMPLEMENTATION ABILITY THE KEY

    Above all, leaders should be concerned with efficiency of mplementation and results of whatever policies and beliefs through carrying them out in tangible work processes to enable all to participate in innovation and knowledge application and compete together as a team.

    Leaders shoudd not just be concerned with taxing and collections of revenues or accumulating them as surpluses but the wise use of such surpluses to benefit job creation or lowering of costs of basic services to his people.

    Leaders shoudl practise people-friendly policies and avoid using petty controls and regulations, or artful creative accounting, double taxing and recovering of costs to avoid causing hardship to the people.

    Leaders should treat people as true partners so that all will have a part to play as a team and all will be totally involved in decision making and effective implementation working towards results.

    Above all leaders should work towards maximizing utilization of available resources e.g. empty properties, vacant lands, idle capitals to create jobs and realize innovations, entrepreneurship, application of knowledge and value-add competitiveness.

    When leaders have identified all challenges, such challenges should be laid down in objective and accountable tangible work processes to empower staff so that the whole team will work towards attaining the objectives and goals without being hindered by any forms of self-centred or intuitive policies or assumptions.

    Finally when over time, leaders should be prepared to "let go" and let his team manage tasks from overall perspectives and committed processes to produce team efficiency and productivity to attain larger results and objectives.
