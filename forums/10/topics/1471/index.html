    They have no locus standi to sue us. No court in the World will recognise an individual's claim (even if it is valid one, which in this case is not) against a soverign state. The UN charter as well as the Charter for the ICJ will not entertain claims if one of the parties to the action refuses to appear before it.

    Then these monkeys, even assuming they prove they have the standing to sue Singapore, the question is: sue who?
