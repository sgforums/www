    If the situation is such that our public transport system is crap...for eg: bus always arrives late, bus always arrives in pairs, driver is rude, accident statistics are high etc etc, then we have every reason to complain over such frequent fare hikes.

    Nobody like an increase in prices but let's face it. Improvements in technology, cost of living and capital purchases are combining to make public transportation a seriously expensive industry. Coupled with the increase of oil prices, you have rising costs battering down your turnovers.

    Upping the prices is merely the next logical step. While we can do away with items like TV mobile (i consider it a complete failure as the end product does not justify the previous mark-up), we should not ignore the new 3G buses with added safety features to protect passengers and (possibly) more spacious leg room, larger capacity, smoother ride etc.

    2.4% increase when computed with 60 cents works out to a 1.44 cent increase. Take that factor and compare to various prices will show abt a 2 cent mark up.

    For the price we pay and the improvements SBS transit and SMRT are lobbying, it is a reasonable proposal.
