    Internship in the US
    by Speedwing Training

    You may think that finding a job/internship thousands of miles away is a difficult task. However, job-hunting techniques for the USA are the same as for any country: the more professional the job, the more effort that is required to obtain it. Be resourceful, active, persistent, and patient, and you could soon be working in the USA. Thanks to the Internet, finding internship opportunities has become much easier in recent years.

    Here are a few suggestions:

    U.S. firms' website
    Check out the websites of the U.S firms you're interested in. Go to the career section and look for any listed internship opportunities.Most US companies do provide regular internship programs, especially in the summer.

    If a website doesn't have any internship information, e-mail the company to explain your situation and ask if it has opportunities for someone like you.

    Surfing the Net
    Surf the net for US-focus employment websites is the other best alternative.
