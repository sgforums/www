    Education is a must in today's high powered society that is hunger for paper qualification. But of course, this paper qualificaiton requires one to be flexible as well. The holder of this qualification have to posess other non-academic qualities, such as interpersonal relationship, well spoken english (at least fluent) and the list goes on.

    Put it this way, without education, i will loose out to those who have. But even if i have a paper qualification, i may not be at a great advantage bcos there are a lot more others who have the same, if not better and higher qualificaion than me.

    Hence, i would say that paper qualificaiton is just a passport to the real world in order for us to have a "respectable" job. After we are secured of a job, the rest depends on how the individual is able to cope with. Remember that studying and working are 2 very different kind of "jobs". Some might be able to excel in one, but not the other.

    Although there is a very strong correlation between education and income, it is not always the case. In the past ppl with low education still managed to be high income earners. Take Bill Gates for example, he dropped out from Havard, but still is the world's richest man.

    Personally i feel that besides education, opportunity and luck still played a great role in determining whhether one will succeed in future.

    Hence, the importance of education is still a very open-ended question subjected to debate. We shall see what the others got to say... ...
