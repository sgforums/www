    ASIA'S 25 MOST POWERFUL

    Rank: 24
    Ho Ching
    Company: Temasek Holdings, Singapore

    Ho is half of Singapore's most powerful couple. Her husband, Lee Hsien Loong, is Prime Minister, with responsibility for running the nation's economy. She is CEO of Singapore's secretive state-owned investment house, whose $100 billion portfolio includes controlling stakes in many of the country's leading companies, including Singapore Airlines, SingTel, and defense contractor SingTech. An electrical engineer by training, the 50-year-old Ho is under increasing pressure to ease the government's grip on the city-state's business community. But aside from the recent sale of the Raffles Hotel group for $860 million, neither Ho nor her husband give any hint of letting go.
