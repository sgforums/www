    This decision can be better made through a total understanding of fundamentals of society.

    Fundamentals of society up to now despite the advancement of civilizations remain to be :

    social and culture integrity,
    justice and equity,
    goodness over evil, (belief in accountability to our ultimate creator)
    mutual respect and harmony,
    community bonding,
    empathy for the weak, deprived and oppressed,
    law and order,
    values, creativity, entrepreneurship etc.

    In short, in such a society, it is such fundamentals like positive values which will bring about peace, progress and prosperity and social cohesion essential to creating a greater and more vibrant for survival in an increasingly uncertain world.

    For such similar reasons though different motives, leaders have been talking about promoting values like family togetherness, social cohesions and even for want of something better, confucianism.

    Will we do better if we see our government giving up such fundamentals and begin to promote gambling, compromise on open prostitutions and lately encouraging more and more outlandish traits among people with inborn yearning due to imperfection of creations to make such traits the norm of society.

    At the moment, gay people are not deprived or really prohibited from taking up jobs or being accepted for particular professions and vocations. This is enough and there is no need to want to be more pragmatic like making more monies from gambling and other negatives. What could be the more noble and pragmatic reason to want to openly declare homosexuality as a normal part of open culture and social order. Why the sudden urge to want to promote gays be more openly accepted as acceptable norms of society.

    Let the goodness triumph over evil, social cohesions over frictions and not let the deviants rise over the other positive values held as fundamentals of society for the sake of peace, progress and orderly growth of society in which all could bring up their families without being subjected to undue evil influences of all sorts associated with deviant conduct and wayward behaviour even if such wayward conduct and behaviour are not deliberate acts.

    Let the mainstream society continue to growth from strength to strength with people practising fundamental values and not the deviants around them.
