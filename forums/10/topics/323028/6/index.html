    Many individuals consider several factors when they get a property analysis. :

    1. The location of the land is very important. Is the land in area that is appreciating?
    2. Are there other business property buildings around this place?
    3. Also, the price of the asset is very important.
    4. Are the taxes expensive?
    5. Are there any local government and zoning laws?
    6. Finally you should see if the investment property is a source of potential rental income.
