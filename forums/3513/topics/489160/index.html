    MUMBAI, India /PRNewswire/ --

    Seek. Read. Write. Repeat With Ivyclique 

    With a significant growth in the consumption of online content over the last decade, the Internet generation will now be able to create their own shareable 'content playlists' on the first user-driven content sharing and publishing platform Ivyclique. Launched in March 2015, Ivyclique combines beautifully curated content from across the web, with uncompromisingly original pieces from its resident subject matter experts and content generated by users across 80 categories, in a fun and easy-to-share manner.

    (Logo: http://photos.prnewswire.com/prnh/20150707/753203)

    At the core of Ivyclique, is a powerful technology backbone that curates the best pieces from across the web, making sure there is high fidelity content to match everyone's tastes.

    Ivyclique is ready to revolutionize the way online users engage and share virtually with its unique Content Playlists and #Cliques  features. It is the only platform that allows users to create and share customized playlists, a collection of related content which can include an array of documents, existing content on Ivyclique, videos, pictures, URLs and presentations. The #Cliques is a market-disruptive feature that allows corporations to capture live conversations around their brands, in 'curated chat-rooms'. Brand managers can upload their brand's 'playlist' and engage with interested users and target customers, leading to top quality primary market research information that commands a premium. Corporates can also outsource creation of high quality content for their blogs to Ivyclique.

    Targeting the global youth between ages of 16-35, Ivyclique is a great tool for educational institutions, corporates, bloggers, writers and young professionals. It can also be accessed through its dynamic mobile app on the Android platform that allows users tremendous flexibility in content creation and dissemination. The platform will soon be available to Apple users on iOS as well.

    The CEO and Founder of Ivyclique, Anjan Purandare has always been passionate creating and sharing high quality content and wanted to provide the Internet users an access to such content in an easy and personalized format on Ivyclique.in. Within just two months of its launch, Ivyclique has had over 1,00,000 unique visitors. It has a rapidly expanding global fan-base across USA, UK, Middle East, India and SE Asia.
