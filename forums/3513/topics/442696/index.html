    Winners of 2011 South East Asia Property Awards Unveil in Singapore 

    SINGAPORE, Dec. 1, 2011 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Winners of the inaugural South East Asia Property Awards 2011 were announced last night (November 30, 2011), at a Gala Dinner attended by over 300 industry leaders at Singapore's Hotel Fort Canning. 

    Winners of the 2011 South East Asia Property Awards are: 

    Best Developer (SE Asia) 
    Far East Organization 

    Best Villa Development (SE Asia) 
    Song Saa Private Island, Brocon Co. 

    Best Condo Development (SE Asia) 
    Nassim Park Residences, UOL Group Ltd / Russville Pte. Ltd. / Orix Investment & Management 

    Best Architectural Design (SE Asia) 
    Sandy Island, YTL Singapore Pte 
    Architecture - Claudio Silvestrin Architects 

    Best Interior Design (SE Asia) 
    The Residences at St. Regis, Bankok, The Minor Group 
    Interior - Brennan Beer Gorman Architects, New York; Plan Architect Co. 

    Best Shared Ownership Development (South East Asia) 
    The Village Coconut Island, The Village Management Co. 

    Green Development Award (South East Asia) 
    Ocean Financial Centre, Keppel Land International Limited 

    Real Estate Personality of the Year (SE Asia) 
    Ho Kwon Ping, Executive Chairman, Banyan Tree Holdings 

    Best Developer (Singapore) 
    Far East Organization 

    Best Housing Development (Singapore) 
    Sandy Island, YTL Singapore Pte 

    Best Condo Development (Singapore) 
    Nassim Park Residences, UOL Group / Russville Pte. Ltd. / Orix Investment And Management Private Limited 

    Best Developer (Thailand) 
    Laguna Resorts & Hotels Public Company Limited 

    Best Villa Development (Thailand) 
    Malaiwana, Malaiwana Park Development Co. 

    Best Condo Development (Thailand) 
    The Residences at St. Regis, Bankok, Minor Group 

    Best Developer (Indonesia) 
    PT Lippo Karawaci Tbk 

    Best Villa Development (Indonesia) 
    Montigo Resorts, Nongsa, KOP Properties Pte 

    Best Condo Development (Indonesia) 
    St.Moritz Penthouses & Residences, PT. Mandiri Cipta Gemilang / PT Lippo Karawaci Tbk 

    Best Developer (Malaysia) 
    YTL Land & Development Berhad 

    Best Villa Development (Malaysia) 
    Martinique Villas By-the-Sea, Eastern & Oriental Berhad 
    (joint-venture CIMB-Mapletree & Al Salam Bank) 

    Best Condo Development (Malaysia) 
    Sunway Vivaldi, Sunway D'Mont Kiara Sdn Bhd 

    Best Developer (Philippines) 
    Ayala Land 

    Best Villa Development (Philippines) 
    Alphaland Balesin Island Club, Alphaland Corporation 

    Best Condo Development (Philippines) 
    Anchor Skysuites, Achor Land Holdings 

    Best Developer (Vietnam) 
    Indochina Land 

    Best Villa Development (Vietnam) 
    Hyatt Regency Danang, Marble Mountain Resort JSC / Indochina Land 

    Best Condo Development (Vietnam) 
    The Estella, Keppel Land (Vietnam) / Estella Joint Venture Co. 

    Best Developer (Cambodia) 
    Brocon Co. 

    Best Development (Cambodia) 
    Song Saa Private Island, Brocon Co.
