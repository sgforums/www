    Get in Control with the PSA Lite for Microsoft Dynamics CRM

    NAARDEN, the Netherlands /PRNewswire/ --

    Assistance Software is excited to announce the launch of PSA Lite for Microsoft Dynamics CRM. PSA Lite is specifically designed for the needs of the Professional Services Industry and will transform the standard Microsoft CRM solution; creating an Advanced Opportunity and Project Management Solution including Time Entry. The official launch date for PSA Lite for Microsoft Dynamics CRM is July 1st 2015.

    Assistance Software believes PSA Lite for Microsoft Dynamics CRM will give the user more control on their Forecast and Projects. Key features include:

    Users can now Forecast and Budget their Opportunities.
    Convert closed Opportunities to a Project with team members and your Budget in hours and money.
    PSA Lite is free to download and easy to upgrade if users require the full PSA Suite with more functionality.
    PSA Lite for Microsoft Dynamics CRM will be presented at the Microsoft Worldwide Partner conference in Orlando (July 12th until July 16th ). For more information, to register for Online Webinars or Download the solution go to: http://www.assistancesoftware.com/psalite

    About Assistance Software 

    Assistance was founded in 1990. In 2011, Assistance released its new version, Assistance PSA Suite, built on and released in conjunction with Microsoft Dynamics CRM. The Company has subsequently been inducted into the Microsoft President's Club, and was a finalist for the Worldwide Business Solutions Award.

    Assistance Software Group BV is the parent group of four wholly-owned subsidiaries held by three shareholders. The Company provides PSA solutions to 25.000+ users worldwide and is headquartered in the Greater Amsterdam area with a North American marketing office in Tampa, Florida.
