    CH2M HILL Singapore Pte Ltd Honoured at Workplace Safety and Health Awards

    SINGAPORE, July 18, 2012 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- CH2M HILL (www.ch2mhill.com), a global full-service consulting, design, construction, and operations firm, was awarded Silver Awards for Workplace Safety and Health (WSH) Performance Awards 2012. Major industry players such as Baker Hughes, Pfizer, Stone & Webster, Celanese, Foster Wheeler and Invista were invited to the awards ceremony at the Swissotel Hotel on July 17.

    Presented by the WSH Council and supported by the Ministry of Manpower, the awards recognize organizations and individuals who have contributed to achieving excellence in workplace safety and health. The Performance Award gives recognition to companies or organizations that have performed well in safety and health through the implementation of sound safety and health management systems. This is the second health and safety recognition for CH2M HILLSingapore following receiving the Gold Award for RoSPA Occupational Health and Safety Awards on May 2012.

    Guest of Honor, Tan Chuan-Jin, Minister of State for Manpower congratulated CH2M HILL Singapore Pte Ltd on its success and encouraged them to maintain efforts to achieve continued excellence in WSH performance and practices.

    Gordon Fraser, CH2M HILL I&AT Business Group Southern Asia Managing Director stated, "Health, Safety and Environmental Protection are CH2M HILL's core values and we are committed to providing a safe, secure and environmentally responsible workplace for all our employees. We foster a 'Target Zero' culture across the company that is focused on continual improvement of our HSE programs worldwide. This award I believe reflects CH2M HILL's commitment in this area."

    CH2M HILL was also awarded a Certificate of Commendation on application for Safety and Health Award Recognition for Projects (SHARP) for International Flavour and Fragrance project they served. The SHARP Award recognizes projects or worksites that mobilize significant manpower and have shown their ability to manage good safety and health performance on a project-level.

    CH2M HILL opened its office in Singapore in 1996 and employs nearly 250 people. Projects delivered by CH2M HILL include the Deep Tunnel Sewerage System (DTSS), Changi Water Reclamation Plant (CWRP), ABC Waters Programme, and Seagate Disk Drive Facility.

    Headquartered near Denver, Colorado, USA, employee-owned CH2M HILL is a global leader in consulting, design, design-build, operations, and program management for government, civil, industrial and energy clients. The firm's work is concentrated in the areas of water, transportation, environmental, energy, facilities and resources. With US$6.4 billion in revenue and 30,000 employees, CH2M HILL is an industry-leading program management, construction management and design firm, as ranked by Engineering News-Record and named a leader in sustainable engineering by Verdantix.
