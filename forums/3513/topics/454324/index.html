Marc C. Brugger Appointed Managing Director of Rosewood Beij

  • Helen.zhang
    20 Jun 12, 11:46

    DALLAS, June 19, 2012 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Marc C. Brugger has been appointed managing director of Rosewood Beijing, scheduled to open Summer 2013 as the first China hotel for Rosewood Hotels & Resorts(R). Mr. Brugger brings 20 years luxury hotel experience to his new role. 


    (Photo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2012/06/13/20120613180359475400.html ) 

    Most recently, Mr. Brugger was general manager of Crown Towers Hotel & Casino Macau. Other positions he has held in the destination's upper echelon properties included vice president VIP gaming services for City of Dreams Casino Macau and vice president hotel and F&B for Altira Hotel & Casino Macau. 

    Previously, Mr. Brugger was director of food and beverage for Four Seasons Hotel Dublin, the largest food and beverage operations of Four Seasons in Europe. His former nine years with Hyatt International included food and beverage management positions at Park Hyatt Goa and Grand Hyatt Hong Kong, with additional postings in Mexico and Malaysia. 

    A Swiss native, Mr. Brugger speaks five languages. He recently received his executive MBA from Ivey School of Business and previously graduated with a bachelor of science degree in international hospitality management from Ecole Hoteliere de Lausanne in Switzerland. 

    Founded in 1979 and headquartered in Dallas, Texas, Rosewood Hotels & Resorts(R) manages one-of-a-kind luxury properties in the world's most desired destinations. Rosewood properties embrace the company's A Sense of Place(R) philosophy, reflecting the history, architecture, scale and sensibilities of the destination. The Rosewood collection includes some of the most legendary hotels and resorts in the world, including Rosewood Mansion on Turtle Creek(R) in Dallas, The Carlyle in New York, Rosewood Little Dix Bay on Virgin Gorda, as well as contemporary classics such as Rosewood Sand Hill(R) in California, Rosewood San Miguel de Allende(R) in Mexico, and Rosewood Tucker's Point in Bermuda. Rosewood also offers Sense(R) spa -- featuring treatments reflective of the location's culture and natural environment -- available exclusively at select properties. For more information, please visit www.rosewoodhotels.com.
