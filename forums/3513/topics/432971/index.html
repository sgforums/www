    Hughes Executive to Speak at CASBAA Forum and CommunicAsia 2011 in Singapore

     

    2011-06-17 07:00 

     

    SINGAPORE, June 17, 2011 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Hughes Network Systems, LLC (Hughes), the global leader in broadband satellite solutions and services, today announced that Ramesh Ramaswamy, assistant vice president of marketing, International Division at Hughes, will be speaking at the Cable and Satellite Broadcasting Association of Asia (CASBAA) Singapore Satellite Industry Forum and CommunicAsia 2011. Mr. Ramaswamy will be taking part in three panel discussions, one at the CASBAA Forum, and two at CommunicAsia 2011.

    WHO:  Ramesh Ramaswamy, Assistant Vice President of Marketing, International  
       
    WHAT:  CASBAA Singapore Satellite Industry Forum 
      "Satellite Broadband/Ka-Band and Higher?"– Panel Discussion
    WHEN:  Monday, 20 June 2011, 12 noon
    WHERE:  Shangri-La Hotel, Singapore
       
    WHAT:  CommunicAsia 2011 
      Satellite Communications, Broadband-Driven Trends Track
      "Satellite Broadband" - Panel Discussion
    WHEN: Tuesday, 21 June 2011, 11:40 am
    WHERE:  Marina Bay Sands, Singapore
       
    WHAT:  CommunicAsia 2011 
      Satellite Communications, Broadband-Driven Trends Track
      "Mobility" - Panel Discussion
    WHEN:  Tuesday, 21 June 2011, 1:50 pm
    WHERE:  Marina Bay Sands, Singapore

    At the CASBAA Singapore Satellite Industry Forum, Mr. Ramaswamy will be taking part in a discussion on the increased uptake of Ka-band technology in North America and Europe as a means of providing lower cost satellite broadband service, and explore possible future options, including higher frequency bands.

    At CommunicAsia 2011, he will be sharing his views in two panel discussions: one focusing on broadband satellite solutions in Asia, and the other on the role of satellite technology and services in bringing the mobile market to its true potential.
