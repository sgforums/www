Cartus Wins Gold at the Singapore HEALTH Awards

  • Helen.zhang
    23 Jul 15, 11:10

    DANBURY, Conn. /PRNewswire/ -- Cartus Corporation, a leading provider of global relocation services that is celebrating its 60th anniversary this year, announced today that its Singapore office has won the Gold Award at the biennial Singapore HEALTH (Helping Employees Achieve Life-Time Health) Awards.

    Logo - http://photos.prnewswire.com/prnh/20140311/MM81238LOGO

    Presented by the Health Promotion Board, the Singapore HEALTH Award gives national recognition to organizations with commendable Workplace Health Promotion (WHP) programs.

    This is the fourth time that Cartus has been recognized in the biennial award program, having previously won the Gold Award in 2012; a Silver Award in 2010; and a Bronze Award in 2008.

    Kenneth Kwek, senior vice president and general manager of Cartus Asia Pacific operations, commented, "We are pleased to receive this Gold award. Employee health and welfare has always been one of our top priorities. Over the years, we have made good progress in promoting health awareness in our Singapore office, increasing the number of employees who live a healthy lifestyle and include exercise as part of their daily regime. This award serves to motivate us to continue our pursuit of advocating a healthy lifestyle among our employees."

    Some of the WHP programs offered by Cartus for employees in Singapore include complimentary health screenings, regular health communications, health and wellness mini bazaars, health workshops, a weekly fruit day, and yoga, Pilates, and zumba classes as well as fitness and recreation activities such as badminton, futsal, and jogging.
