    Hong Kong Social Media Week 2012 Success

    HONG KONG, Feb. 23, 2012 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Social Media Week is a multi-city global conference connecting people, content and conversations around emerging trends in social and mobile media. Hong Kong is one of 12 cities participating in this global platform, including Hamburg, London, Miami, New York, Paris, San Francisco, Sao Paulo, Singapore, Tokyo, Toronto and Washington DC.

    (Logo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2009/09/09/20090909984310.jpg )
    (Logo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2012/02/06/20120206114508732510-l.jpg )

    Social Media Week took place in Hong Kong from 13th - 17th February 2012. Social Media Week 2012 marks the second year the week-long event has been held in the city. This year, Social Media Week brought together over 3,000 attendees across 40 events with online conversation generating over 400,000 tweets.

    SMWHK is organised and led by City Partner Cohn&Wolfe-impactasia together with a team of 22 Advisory Board members who have helped to curate the overall event content through the support of a diverse set of events partners across the city. As one of the advisory board members, Harbour City hosted 4 different events during Social Media Week 2012, with the topics of Food & Beverage, Travel, Fashion & Beauty as well as Technology.

    With hundreds of tenants and friends from the industry's support, 4 events were sold out quickly. In total, Harbour City had invited over 20 speakers to share their social media journeys including:

    About Social Media Week

    Social Media Week (http://socialmediaweek.org) is one of the world's most unique conferences, providing global perspectives on emerging trends in social and mobile media. In 2011, the conference was hosted in eleven cities worldwide and attracted more than 18,000 attendees with hundreds of thousands connecting online and through mobile media. Currently city partners and their local advisory boards are creating programs which collectively will consist of more than 500 free to attend events worldwide this February 2012. Social Media Week is owned and operated by Crowdcentric Media LLC.
