Executive Search Dynamo Begins Asia Expansion

  • Helen.zhang
    24 Jul 15, 11:30
    -Kingsley Gate Partners is open for business in Singapore and Mumbai

    DALLAS /PRNewswire/ -- One of Asia's leading search professionals, Manisha Deva has been driving expansion into new markets in the region for two of the largest search firms in the world for over fifteen years. She now joins Kingsley Gate Partners as an anchor Partner there to guide the rapid expansion of the firm in that part of the world. Manisha has placed members of the boards of directors, CEOs, CXOs and other senior management positions in Asia with extensive coverage of growth markets in South East Asia including India. Specializing in cross-border assignments, Manisha has also lent her expertise to strategic assignments in private equity, hedge fund, private banking, wealth management, asset management as well as corporate, retail, and investment banking organizations. Based on her global relationships, Manisha has helped her Asian clients get the finest talent from across the world.

    Nancy Albertini, member of the Office of the CEO at Kingsley Gate Partners pointed out that the firm's clients have been asking to be supported in Asia for a long time. "We were looking for a leader who was sector agnostic and had deep geographical penetration of the region," she said. "During our due diligence of partners to fill this key leadership role, Manisha's name kept popping up from various CEOs in the region. It was a no-brainer. She is the ideal person to lead our growth strategy in Asia and we intend to give her all the support she needs to speed up this global expansion."

    "As I travelled around the country with Nancy meeting the existing clients of Kingsley Gate Partners as part of my onboarding process, I was amazed at the loyalty that I witnessed," said Manisha Deva. "This is the kind of quality search firm that clients in Asia seek. They want a firm that will stick with them in good times and in bad, not focusing on the fee but on a long term relationship. Having a deep knowledge of the psyche of Asian companies, I know that Kingsley Gate Partners will quickly become an integral part of the Asian Corporate landscape and I am thrilled to be the one leading the charge there."
