Doxee Expands its International Partner Network Through Agre

  • Helen.zhang
    30 Jul 15, 18:17
    -India's leading Information Management and Delivery Company to sell and support the Doxee CCM offering

    SAN JOSE, Calif. and MODENA, Italy  /PRNewswire/ -- Doxee, a leader in cloud based enterprise-class Customer Communication Management and Document Output Production solutions and services, announced today the continued expansion of its International Partner business through an agreement withFriends Color Images Pvt. Ltd. Friends is managed services organization that specializes in customer communication management Solutions headquartered in New Delhi, India. The company has the largest services based infrastructure in the Indian Sub-Continent, spread across four strategically important locations in India.

    (Logo: http://photos.prnewswire.com/prnh/20150325/736010-a )

    "We have chosen to adopt the Doxee Platform to enhance our Customer Communications Management offerings, especially in the areas of cloud CCM and interactive communications where we see tremendous growth and new business opportunities. We are excited to work with the management, sales and technical support teams at Doxee who bring proven experience in cloud CCM managed services to our organization," said Vineet Sawhney, Director, Friends Color Images Pvt. Ltd. "The selection of Doxee's Platform-as-a-Service aligns with our dedication to deliver best-in-class technologies and solutions that ensure our customers succeed in their respective markets."

    "This is a significant agreement for us and demonstrates our commitment to developing a global partner-centric business," said Ron Friedman, chief operating officer of Doxee. "We are enthusiastic about this new partnership with Friends. Their company epitomizes Doxee's commitment to align with partners who have the reputation and resources to dominate their market with our disruptive technology."  

    Friends will provide all market and customer-facing services for the Doxee platform to its customers under a Managed Services business model. Doxee will provide its cloud CCM PaaS and complete support of the IaaS offering in India.

    Doxee 

    Doxee is a leading global technology and services provider of enterprise-class, cloud-based CCM. Enterprises worldwide use Doxee products and services to deliver highly personalized and interactive customer communications to their clients.

    Friends Color Images Pvt. Ltd. 

    Friends has been serving Indian enterprise clients in a wide range of sectors such as Telecommunications, Utilities Insurance, Banks and other financial institutions. The company has an extensive expertise in customer communication management solutions for the industries that it works in.
