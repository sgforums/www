Interakt, a Full Service Digital Communications Company...

  • Helen.zhang
    4 Sep 15, 16:46

    SINGAPORE  /PRNewswire/ -- Interakt Digital Group, a full service digital communications agency , established in 2004, is celebrating its 11th year in business this year, continuing its growth as one of the few completely independent agencies in the region.

    Speaking on this occasion, Redickaa Subrammanian, the CEO, summarized, "The last decade and more has been both challenging and satisfying, especially as a totally self-funded entity. Those in the market know the significance of this status and the resilience that it translates to. We've been creative enough to bag some really great brands as our customers, diverse enough to ride on the varying market forces across regions but above all lucky enough to have a wonderful, close-knit team that has stood united over this long journey."

    Interakt's core practices of digital marketing, engaged learning, technology and user experience have seen it servicing over 200 clients across 15 verticals and 50 geographies. Its engagements - regional and global - have seen the delivery of over 750 digital campaigns across web, social and mobile as well as training for over 400,000 engaged learners.

    A testament to its brand contributions are the words of Mr. Biswajit Dash, Senior Manager and COE Specialist at RIL, a Fortune 100 company, who said, "It's overwhelming to see Interakt's team work, global skill set, design and development capabilities, and above all the zeal to deliver.They make the impossible seem meaningless with faith and hard work."

    Mr. Narasimha M. Rao, Vice President, RD & E at Ecolab, observed, "I really appreciated Interakt's ability to understand what we were trying to accomplish as a business, and proactively suggest improvements to our original ideas, working as a true partner to provide that 'value added' touch to everything."

    Following the turn of a decade, Interakt is now set to leverage this tremendous experience in marketing communications with a foray into the development of marketing automation solutions and related services, as a natural extension to its capabilities.

    To learn more about Interakt, visit https://interaktco.com/.

    About Interakt: 

    Interakt is a full-service digital communications company that specializes in Communications Inside Out. It offers a wide array of services across its four key practices - digital marketing, engaged learning, user experience and technology. Spanning across all four continents, the company is headquartered in Singapore with offices in New York, San Francisco, London, Dubai, Chennai and Bengaluru.
