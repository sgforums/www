    Air China to Resume Shanghai to Paris Direct Service Next Year 

    BEIJING, Dec. 1, 2011 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Air China will resume its direct rotations between Shanghai and Paris starting March 10, 2012, and the existing Shanghai-Beijing-Paris services (CA933/4) will be rescheduled to become direct rotations between Beijing and Paris operated with B777-300ER. 

    (Logo: http://www.prnasia.com/xprn/sa/2008/12/26/200812261146.jpg ) 

    Details: 

    -- CA833/4, Shanghai-Paris-Shanghai, on Monday, Wednesday, 
    Friday, Saturday and Sunday, departing at 00:15 from 
    Shanghai Pudong and at 12:30 from Paris 
    -- CA933/4, Beijing-Paris-Beijing, daily, departing at 13:30 
    from Beijing and at 19:30 from Paris 

    These arrangements will not only offer more flight options to passengers, but also make it easier for passengers to make transfers in Shanghai as a gateway and Beijing as a hub.
