    Air Ticket + DB Railway Package Now Available on Air China's German Website 

    BEIJING, Nov. 30, 2011 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Air China continues to develop global websites that offer a wide range of international travel support products to meet the demands of passengers and provide them with more convenience. In November 2011, Air China's German website began offering Rail and Fly bookings in both German and English. Now, passengers will be able to purchase air tickets and DB railway tickets at the same time for more complete travel planning and convenient service. 

    (Logo: http://www.prnasia.com/xprn/sa/2008/12/26/200812261146.jpg ) 

    Effective immediately, passengers visiting Air China's German website can use Rail & Fly to easily check schedules and purchase tickets online for both Air China and Deutsche Bahn, the German National Railway without leaving home. They can depart from any train station in Germany where DB railway service is available and travel to Frankfurt, Munich and Dusseldorf airports for their connecting Air China flights to China and around the world. This web service offers passengers the ease of an advance ticket purchase and flexible booking options. Using the online system, passengers can select the most convenient transit airport and flight time to meet their travel requirements and book train tickets to the airport at the same time. Take the DB Rail & Fly Pick number shown in the confirmation email to the railway station to board the corresponding train to the airport where the flight departs. 

    For more information, please visit Air China's German website (www.airchina.de) and select the Rail & Fly option.
