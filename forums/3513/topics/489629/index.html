WSO2 and Industrie IT Partner to Provide Digital Services an

    SYDNEY  /PRNewswire/ -- Australian owned technology company, Industrie IT has announced its continued partnership with WSO2 to deliver integration solutions and services across Asia Pacific.

    Industrie IT recognises that the API movement to integration is key to the ever-changing business environment. APIs enable organisations to rapidly connect different services to create compelling customer experiences. Industrie IT has been delivering cost-effective, high performance integration solutions for their clients leveraging the WSO2 Carbon enterprise middleware platform for the last three years.

    "There's an enormous opportunity for businesses to implement innovative and cost effective solutions via WSO2," said Con Zeritis, CEO of Industrie IT. "For years, our partnership has been delivering outstanding results acrossAsia Pacific and we are honoured to continue on this journey."

    WSO2 delivers the industry's most comprehensive open source platform for building, integrating, managing, securing and analyzing companies' APIs, applications, and Web services -- on-premises, in the cloud, on mobile devices, and across the Internet of Things.
