HubSpot Expands Footprint in Asia Pacific, Plans to Open....

  • Helen.zhang
    16 Jun 15, 12:07

    CAMBRIDGE, Mass. /PRNewswire/ -- Today, leading inbound marketing and sales software company, HubSpot, announced it will open an office in Singapore during Q4 of 2015. The Singapore office will support HubSpot's growth across Asia Pacific and increase its ability to help businesses in the region transform the way they attract, engage, and delight customers.

    Logo - http://photos.prnewswire.com/prnh/20110817/NE53515LOGO

    The Singapore office will join HubSpot's Sydney, Australia office as the second in the region and will act as the company's APAC headquarters. It will be the company's fifth office globally. HubSpot's global headquarters are located in Cambridge, MA with its European headquarters in Dublin, Ireland and a satellite office in Portsmouth, NH.

    "The launch of our Singapore office signals HubSpot's growing global reach and the international appeal of the inbound marketing movement," said JD Sherman, HubSpot's COO. "Since opening our first regional office inSydney in 2014, we've seen more mid-market companies in APAC looking to deliver personalized, data-driven customer experiences. Our presence in Singapore will allow HubSpot to deliver further on that demand."

    HubSpot's Singapore office will be staffed with sales, marketing, services and support teams and will be focused on growing relationships with customers and agency partners across Asia Pacific.   

    About HubSpot
