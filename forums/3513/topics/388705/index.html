    After 15 years of In-building experience, 4 generations of networks integration and over 3,000 deployments worldwide, CONSISTEL harness all the experience, expertise and knowledge into the development of ATRIUM - The total solution that manages every process within the In-building wireless life-cycle.

    SINGAPORE, Jan. 20 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- CONSISTEL, a leading independent service provider for wireless and mobile network solutions today announced ATRIUM, a highly anticipated In-building wireless enterprise software suite that manages every process within the In-building wireless network lifecycle.

    (Logo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2009/10/16/20091016179029.jpg )

    Announcing the new software suite, Masoud Bassiri, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, CONSISTEL Pte Ltd said, "The release of ATRIUM represents a significant leap forward to the fulfillment of our vision to become the leading In-building solutions provider. After 15years of In-building experience in integrating thousands of In-building wireless systems worldwide, we recognize the need for a comprehensive solution that enables a high level of automation in every process within the In-building wireless life-cycle. Hence, CONSISTEL paves its way into the development of ATRIUM - A breakthrough solution that revolutionizes the way In-building networks are designed and deployed, providing and monitoring real-time information on all projects. We're the first company with a complete strategy to help network operators, system integrators and equipment manufacturers to transform their business processes in a fully automated way."

    "By benchmarking ATRIUM application software to our experience, we have discovered that the use of ATRIUM will help reduce services and labor costs by over 65% and full integration costs by over 30%. This is a significant cost saving for the customer in addition to the substantial time saving due to faster and more efficient design and implementation," added Masoud.

    ATRIUM is a powerful enterprise software suite that enables communication and collaboration across organizations, providing and monitoring real-time information on all projects through accurate analytics and intelligent information processing. With a unique design and methodology, ATRIUM encompasses multiple capabilities and features integrated into one enterprise platform. The fully integrated modules can be combined to automate every process within the In-building wireless value-chain or customized to integrate only those modules required today, with the option to add more modules and applications as the needs expand tomorrow.

    ATRIUM will also be launched via a global webcast in conjunction with GSMA Mobile World Congress, CeBIT 2010 and CTIA Wireless 2010.
