Atlaspost.com Launches New Website in Southeast Asia: The Su

  • Helen.zhang
    17 May 10, 12:12


    TAIPEI, Taiwan, May 17 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- The DEMO 08 People's choice award winner Atlaspost.com, the geo-based social networking website, having almost one million registered users world wide, is now the largest map-based social network service provider and a top 20 website in Taiwan.

    After the landmark victory, and taking a firm stand in the Taiwan market during the first quarter of this year, Atlaspost.com has officially launched the new website to replicate its successful social network services experience throughout the Southeast Asia market.

    Taiwan's largest geo-based social networking website, Atlaspost.com, combines a social network with its innovative geo-neighborhoods idea, letting users locate their posts and photos on map and share with their friends, neighbors and communities in a way that draws attention at its first launch.

    Following the release of POI, geo-route services offer extensive geo locating services for tagging landmarks and travel destinations, together seizing the principle of location-based services to deliver a comprehensive service package.

    "Apart from the idea itself, the predominance of Atlaspost.com relies on the cohesive interactions between users, which creates a socialized virtual community that addicts people to the site. Connecting virtual and real society results in constant growth on the website," said Jerry Kuo, CEO of Atlaspost.com.

    Having a successful business model, he is pleased to announce the expansion of the overseas market, and to introduce the geo-based social networking idea in Southeast Asia. The new launch expects to bring an increase of 100,000 overseas visits per month by the end of 2010.
