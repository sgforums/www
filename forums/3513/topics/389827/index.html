Cytech Global and Ecliptek Signed Distribution Agreement

    Cytech Global Pte Ltd and Ecliptek Corporation Signed Distribution Agreement for ASEAN Countries and India

    2010-02-01 11:00

        SINGAPORE, Feb. 1 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Cytech Global Pte Ltd, a wholly owned subsidiary of Cytech Technology Ltd who is one of the leading technical distributors of semiconductors in Asia, has announced today that it has signed a distribution agreement with Ecliptek Corporation, a leading manufacturer of crystals and oscillators headquartered in the United States, for ASEAN countries and India. Cytech Global is now an authorized Ecliptek distributor in Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Philippines, Indonesia, Vietnam and India. This expanded agreement complements Ecliptek's existing distribution network in the South Asia Pacific region.

        "We are honored to have the opportunity to distribute Ecliptek products in ASEAN countries and India," said YM Gui, President of Cytech Global. "With this new partnership, we look forward to expanding our complimentary product line card, by providing a comprehensive offering of crystals and oscillators to our valued customers." 

        "Cytech Global is strategically advantageous to Ecliptek, based on its demand creation focus and professional sales force. We are pleased to join forces with them to extend our reach to customers' within the ASEAN countries and India," said Robert Zarrow, Vice President Of Sales, Ecliptek Corporation.

        About Cytech Global Pte Ltd

        Cytech Global is a wholly owned subsidiary of the Cytech Technology Ltd. Cytech Technology  is one of Pan-Asia's leading technology oriented value-added distributors. Established since 1998 with headquarter in Hong Kong, it has a workforce of more than 200 employees spanning over 16 offices. For the financial year ended 31 March 2008, Cytech Technology became a member of Macnica group which achieved a consolidated revenue of approximately US$1.3 billion.

        About Ecliptek Corporation

        Founded in 1987, Ecliptek is a leading supplier of innovative frequency control products to the electronics industry. Headquartered in Costa Mesa, CA, Ecliptek provides complete engineering support and unparalleled customer service to OEM customers and distributors worldwide.
