Luminator Technology Group Acquires Gorba/Invertag AG

  • Helen.zhang
    6 Aug 15, 18:18

    PLANO, Texas /PRNewswire/ -- Luminator Technology Group (LTG) is pleased to announce the acquisition of Gorba/Invertag AG.

    Logo: http://photos.prnewswire.com/prnh/20150803/254278LOGO

    Gorba AG was founded in 1982 and is a globally active supplier of complete passenger information systems for public transportation. Based in Switzerland, Gorba AG has representatives throughout Europe, Asia and theMiddle East. Invertag AG, founded in 1964, develops and produces high-quality display and input systems for the public transportation, industry and digital signage market. The mutual strengths in hard and software technologies and a deep understanding of the standards in public transport in the focus of the transaction will create new generations of products meeting the increasing demands and needs in public transportation.

    "The acquisition of Gorba will enhance our mass transit portfolio and augment our offering in the passenger information and communication systems," said Avi Zisman, President and CEO of LTG.

    LTG is a leading manufacturer and integrator of display and lighting technology, providing products and systems to a wide range of customers in the Bus, Rail and Aircraft transportation segments.

    LTG branded products, marketed under the Luminator, Lawo, Axion, Focon, Mobitec, Twin Vision, VSN, Gorba and lnvertag brand names, provide the best-in-class signage and passenger information systems. It is our objective with the addition of Gorba/lnvertag AG to maintain these products and brand names independently to ensure continued support to our customers and maintain our cutting edge technology position in the market place.

    Through our individual brands and by combining our skills, experience and resources we provide the best and most comprehensive support to our operations and customers worldwide.

    For more information on LTG, please visit www.luminatortechnologygroup.com.
