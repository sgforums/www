    Egyptian Minister of Investment Leads Delegation to Singapore to Boost Investor Relations

    Wednesday, February 10, 2010

    SINGAPORE, Feb. 10 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- An Egyptian delegation led by HE Dr Mahmoud Mohieldin, Minister of Investment, Arab Republic of Egypt, will be coming to Singapore on March 04, 2010 to showcase Egypt as a destination for institutional and direct investment in a half-day conference organized by NextVIEW Pte Ltd ("NextVIEW"), a Singapore-based leading provider of real time trading tool solutions, investor education and financial conferences in Asia Pacific. This event is also co-hosted by DBS Bank and the Singapore Business Federation.

    An event spearheaded by CI Capital, a leading investment bank in Egypt and a wholly-owned subsidiary of Commercial International Bank which holds the largest market capital in the Egyptian banking sector, the 2nd Egypt Day Investor Conference in Singapore will feature four Egyptian listed companies, all involved in four different sectors of the economy - financial services, cables manufacturer, oil & gas, and carpets manufacturer.

    Out of the four companies, three are constituents of the Egyptian Exchange’s EGX 30 Index, namely Maridive Oil & Services, El Sewedy Cables and Commercial International Bank.

    Among other notable speakers at the conference are Mr Stephen Ho, Managing Director of DBS Bank, Mr Maged Shawky, Chairman of the Egyptian Stock Exchange and Mr Osama Saleh, Chairman of the General Authority for Investment and Free Zones of Egypt.

    The conference aims to tighten investor links between Egypt and Singapore and other ASEAN countries through a range of issues such as the current state and trends of the Egyptian investment climate and economic plans, business framework and environment, direct investment and joint opportunities in Egypt and many more.

    Attendance to the Egypt Day is free but by registration only. Interested institutional or corporate investors who would like to have a one-to-one meeting with any of the delegates can also register their interests on-line so that necessary arrangements can be made. More information of the event and registration form can be found on http://ir.nextview.com/egyptday
