Options Group Announces the Launch of Its New Smarter, Faste

  • Helen.zhang
    5 Aug 15, 16:26

    NEW YORK /PRNewswire/ -- Options Group announces the launch of our new website to better represent our core services: exclusive rapid access to top talent, primary research intelligence, and strategic organizational analysis. It illustrates how we want to be viewed and valued as we continue to take a market leadership position in changing the face of financial services recruitment, research, and consulting around the globe.

    Smarter, faster executive search will now be accessible to more financial and nonfinancial organizations worldwide thanks to our new optimized website. The new branding reflects this key advantage symbolically with unique imagery to convey the qualities of Options Group. Our recruiting practice continues to increase product and geographic coverage, and our commitment to help clients take the uncertainty out of the decision-making process is achieved by our "enterprise approach," allowing us to provide the best solutions for their talent needs.

    OGiQ, our market intelligence engine, supports our expanded strategic services in offering deeper insight and elevated intelligence. Our research products combine market data with proprietary information and serve to empower clients to make the right business decisions with confidence. 

    The new website is designed to be simple to navigate for both users and search engines, responsive to adapt to any device. The key tenets of our success are conveyed throughout the site and reflect our culture: teamwork, integrity, accountability, and loyalty.

    With an exciting new image, this is an important inflection point for Options Group. This is just one of our many initiatives, as we expect to roll out with additional announcements throughout the year to further demonstrate our commitment to provide innovative and world-class products and services to our investors, clients, candidates, vendors, and future employees.

    Please explore our new website at www.optionsgroup.com.

    ABOUT OPTIONS GROUP
    Founded in 1992, Options Group is a leading global executive search and strategic consulting firm for the financial services industry.  Since 2000, we have maintained a local presence on five continents and have placed thousands of mid- to senior-level professionals in all areas of the financial services industry at a range of institutions.  With over 140 consultants and market intelligence professionals worldwide, Options Group has a thorough knowledge of key competencies in the financial industry and is at the cutting edge of global hiring and compensation trends.
