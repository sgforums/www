PublicAffairsAsia and News Corp to Honor Excellence in Diplo

  • Helen.zhang

    HONG KONG, July 26 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- PublicAffairsAsia, in association with News Corporation, is set to honor the work done by Diplomatic Missions in the commercial and public affairs arena with a new Gold Standard Award. The Gold Standard Award for Excellence in Diplomatic Engagement will identify achievement by an ambassador or embassy in their work with business, governments and non-governmental organizations (NGOs).

    The award, which will formally be launched on August 2, is open to ambassadors and embassies operating in Asia as well as to ambassadors and embassies from countries in Asia operating globally.

    Nominations are sought from businesses, parent foreign ministries and directly from the ambassadors and embassies themselves and will be judged by a senior panel of leaders from business, the political arena and the professional public affairs industry. The award is a centerpiece of The Gold Standard Awards, which is the only program celebrating excellence in engagement amongst business, government and NGOs in Asia.
