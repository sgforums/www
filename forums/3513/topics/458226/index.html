    The Hearing Solution Company Pte Ltd Completes Asset Purchase Deal of Coherent Instruments Pte Ltd & Resonic Pte Ltd in Singapore

    SINGAPORE, Sept. 5, 2012 /PRNewswire/ -- The Hearing Solution Company Pte Ltd (THSC), the largest hearing care retail chain in Southeast Asia with operations in South Korea, is pleased to announce the completion of an asset purchase deal to acquire Coherent Instruments Pte Ltd & Resonic Pte Ltd with effect on 1st September 2012, at an undisclosed amount.

    Coherent & Resonic has a twelve-year history operating a retail and distribution business for hearing instruments inSingapore. This represents a substantial expansion of its Singapore business unit's capabilities.

    This acquisition takes the number of THSC outlets in Singapore to seven, making it the largest hearing retail chain inSingapore by permanent hearing centres.

    About The Hearing Solution Company Pte Ltd (THSC)

    Established in Asia in 2003 to cater to the needs of the hearing-impaired community, The Hearing Solution Group today is the biggest Hearing Health Care provider in SEA with more than 70 centres in 4 countries. It has its headquarters and flagship centre in Singapore and centres in Malaysia, Indonesia and South Korea.
