Corporate Fitness App Creator Rewardz Woos Group Insurance..

  • Helen.zhang
    16 Jul 15, 15:12
    -HR tech developer to enhance group insurance packages with its Flabuless workplace fitness lifestyle platform

    SINGAPORE /PRNewswire/ -- Local corporate benefits technology startup Rewardz has signed with a slew of insurance providers to create a healthcare technology platform targeted at group insurance end-users.

    The developers of HR benefits platform EMPerks and corporate fitness platform Flabuless looks to influence corporate health and wellness in a big way, by offering its technology through its group insurance partners to enhance preventive healthcare initiatives that ultimately reduce medical costs and reduces staff downtime due to illness and ailments.

    "The group insurance industry presents an excellent opportunity for us to roll out a unique, content-rich and technologically advanced corporate wellness platform," said Rewardz CEO Sudhanshu Tewari.

    The mobile-ready Flabuless platform has drawn keen interest from the group insurance industry such as Tokio Marine Life Insurance Singapore Ltd., who is working on an exclusive arrangement with Rewardz to offer this platform to their selected customer segments; Lockton Companies (Singapore) Pte Ltd; as well as Mercer (Singapore) Pte Ltd, who has added Flabuless into its Mercer Marsh Benefits corporate health benefits solution.

    Mercer's Product Development Leader, Foong Foong Cheong adds, "With Flabuless, employees are motivated to adopt health-conscious behaviors which in the long run are contributing factors to reducing lifestyle-related risks of heart disease, hypertension, diabetes, stroke, and cancer." 

    Rewardz has taken on more than 30 clients over the last two years, including AETOS Holdings Pte Ltd, Schenker Singapore Pte Ltd, Crocs Singapore Pte Ltd, and National University Hospital Pte Ltd with their flagship EMPerks corporate perks platform. Its Flabuless mobile app has also been adopted by corporations such as Fuji Xerox Asia Pacific Pte Ltd and Lendlease.

    Rewardz is also looking to expand its footprint through regional and global partnerships, and its Flabuless platform is on track to be available in Australia, Hong Kong, Indonesia, Dubai and Peru by the end of this year.

    About Rewardz

    Rewardz is a corporate perks service based in Singapore that complements human resource (HR) efforts for medium to large organisations. The mobile and web-based platform helps organisations acquire company benefits from third-party vendors, as well as organise company events, handle corporate bookings and deliver customised push notifications for the organisation's HR activities.

    In just under two years, Rewardz has garnered more than 30 clients, including National University Healthcare System (NUHS). Courts, Crocs, Fuji Xerox, Aetos, SPRING Singapore, and the Public Utilities Board (PUB). Rewardz has also received recognition as "Best in Benefit Technology" during the Benefits Asia 2013 Conference.

    In April 2015, the Rewardz application was rebranded as EMPerks, making Rewardz an umbrella brand for both its current product lines -- EMPerks and Flabuless.

    By July 2015, the Rewardz platform is also expected to have more than 500 rewards from its growing vendor pool.

    About EMPerks

    Created in 2012, EMPerks is Rewardz's flagship online and mobile app that provides employees with corporate privileges in areas such as health, food and beverage, and lifestyle. EMPerks makes corporate rate information more accessible via web and mobile, and simplifies perks redemption for employees whose organisations are signed up with the service.

    About Flabuless

    Launched as a full-fledged mobile application in April 2015, Flabuless encourages employees to keep fit by rewarding them with both cash and non-cash incentives by completing individual and team fitness challenges, as well as compete with one another with online fitness tracking records and leaderboard comparisons. Flabuless is also programmed to seamlessly sync with popular fitness devices such as RunKeeper and Fitbit for a better experience.
