    CompareClinic.com -- First Review-driven Healthcare Community in Asia Pacific -- Launched During FDI World Congress 2012 
    2012/08/29 08:15

    Dedicated to promoting quality healthcare, wherever you are 

    HONG KONG and SINGAPORE, Aug. 29, 2012 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Compare Clinic (www.compareclinic.com) -- the first review-drive healthcare community in Asia Pacific -- was introduced during 100th FDI World Congress 2012 (www.fdicongress.org), the leading global congress with participants all over the world which is currently held at Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre. 

    (Photo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2012/08/28/20120828153116206696.html ) 

    Compare Clinic helps people connect with trusted medical specialists, both local and overseas, and provides the public with updated medical information from three different sources, i.e. advanced treatment technology from medical manufacturers, clinical education information from dental and medical surgeons, and genuine feedback from patients. 

    Engaging Web 2.0 communications among patients and healthcare professionals, and healthcare manufacturers 

    "In the past, patients relied on one-way communication -- either looking up medical directory or asking friends and family for advice -- to find the right doctors. Using Web 2.0 technology, Compare Clinic facilitates three-way communication among patients and between healthcare manufacturers, medical doctors and dentists. We help people make informed decisions as to what dental and medical treatment they should receive and where they can go for that," commented Henrik Petersen, a founder of Compare Clinic. 

    "Our ultimate goal is to connect patients with trusted healthcare professionals. In turn, healthcare service providers can reach out to more patients and build reputation. Together, these help optimize healthcare resources and capacity in society at large. " 

    Patients Peer Review 
    Find and connect with clinics with advanced treatment options that fit their needs more easily. 
    Gain relevant, quality information from healthcare service providers and third-party feedback from peers' reviews 
    Provide feedback and advocate for quality treatment 

    Medical/Dental Clinic Profile 
    Provide complete clinic profile and treatment options for patients globally, take patient enquiries and make appointment booking. 
    Share clinical and education information targeted to the right patient groups. 
    Effective patients' advocate and referral via CompareClinic.com 

    Manufacturers - Clinic Finder 
    Inform patients of advanced treatment concept and technology. 
    Connect clinics who adopt this technology with the right patients 
    Learn feedback from patients' peer review. 

    A whole new way to connect patients to clinics anywhere, anytime. 

    "The positive healthcare market outlook and growing healthcare demand in APAC arises from the expanding middle class and the aging population. Asia's population is expected to grow to 4.5 billion or 60% of the world's population, by 2050. Technology adopted by Compare Clinic will revolutionize how healthcare services will be offered and marketed," commented Virginia Chan, Director of Compare Clinic. 

    The rise of medical tourism and mobility of people 

    Medical tourism is becoming a major push factor for economic development in APAC. Frost & Sullivan reported that the Asian medical tourism market is estimated to reach US$4.4 billion in 2012 with a projected CAGR of 15% for the next 5 years. Increasingly, the market sees a clientele shift of medical tourists from westerners to affluent Asians. Thus, advanced medical centres thrive in India, South Korea, Singapore, Thailand and the Philippines etc. People's increased mobility also adds demand for information on quality dental / medical treatment outside their home country. 

    About Compare Clinic 

    Compare Clinic is a review-driven community that helps people connect with trusted medical specialists, both local and overseas. It helps dental and medical clinics reach out to more patients and build trustworthy reputations and provide updated information to the public from three different sources, i.e. advanced treatment technology from healthcare manufacturers, clinical education information and clinic profile from medical doctors and dentists, and genuine feedback from patients. Founding team of the company brings in over 30 years of international healthcare management and IT experience, aiming at promoting quality healthcare to the public.
