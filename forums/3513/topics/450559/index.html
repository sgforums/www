TripAdvisor Unveils 2012 Travellers' Choice Hotels for Famil

  • Helen.zhang
    12 Apr 12, 16:45

    Awards honour family-friendly properties worldwide, according to reviews and opinions from millions of travellers 

    SINGAPORE, April 11, 2012 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- TripAdvisor( http://www.tripadvisor.com.sg )(TM), the world's largest travel site*, today announced its 2012 Travellers' Choice(R) award-winning Hotels for Families. This year, 504 top family-friendly properties were recognised in 25 markets around the globe including Asia, Australia, the South Pacific, Africa, Caribbean, Europe and the U.S.

    In Asia, Club Med Sahoro - Karikachi Kogen( http://www.tripadvisor.com/Hotel_Review-g1025638-d601595-Reviews-Club_Med_Sahoro_Karikachi_Kogen-Shintoku_cho_Hokkaido.html ) in Shintoku-cho, Japan came in first while Club Mahindra Kodagu Valley(http://www.tripadvisor.com/Hotel_Review-g641714-d1156207-Reviews-Club_Mahindra_Kodagu_Valley-Madikeri_Kodagu_Karnataka.html ) in Madikeri, India took the second place. 

    In Thailand, Phuket laid claim to 6 out of the 10 top hotels in Thailand while Hua Hin, Bangkok, Koh Samui and Mae Ai had 1 winner each. The Chava Resort( http://www.tripadvisor.com/Hotel_Review-g297937-d1138064-Reviews-The_Chava_Resort-Thalang_Phuket.html ) in Phuket took the first place in Thailand and sixth place in Asia. 

    In Indonesia, Club Med Ria Bintan( http://www.tripadvisor.com/Hotel_Review-g297718-d605509-Reviews-Club_Med_Ria_Bintan-Bintan_Riau_Archipelago_Riau_Islands_Province_Sumatra.html ) was crowned the top hotel. Bali secured 6 out of the 10 top winning hotels, Bintan gained 2 while Bandung and Pulau Sugi had a winner each. 

    In Malaysia, Casa del Rio Melaka( http://www.tripadvisor.com/Hotel_Review-g306997-d1859159-Reviews-Casa_del_Rio_Melaka-Melaka_Central_Melaka_District_Melaka_State.html ) made it to the top spot followed by Somerset Ampang Kuala Lumpur( http://www.tripadvisor.com/Hotel_Review-g298570-d1929970-Reviews-Somerset_Ampang_Kuala_Lumpur-Kuala_Lumpur_Wilayah_Persekutuan.html ) and Jonker Boutique Hotel( http://www.tripadvisor.com/Hotel_Review-g306997-d1749499-Reviews-Jonker_Boutique_Hotel-Melaka_Central_Melaka_District_Melaka_State.html ) in Melaka. 

    Award winners were determined based on the hotels most highly rated by those who travelled with family. Unlike any other honours, TripAdvisor Travellers' Choice winners are based on millions of valuable reviews and opinions from travellers around the world. 

    "Travellers looking to plan their family trips need not look any further with these highly-rated family-friendly properties. They are great vacation and accommodation ideas by those who really matter -- travellers themselves," said Jean Ow-Yeong, TripAdvisor spokesperson.
