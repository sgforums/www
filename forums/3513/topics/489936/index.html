Grey Group agrees to acquire nudeJEH

  • Helen.zhang
    4 Sep 15, 16:24

    SINGAPORE  /PRNewswire/ -- Grey Group today announced its acquisition of nudeJEH, a leading and award-winning boutique advertising and digital agency in Thailand. Following the acquisition, nudeJEH will join Grey Group Thailand and be known as GREYnJ United.

    Founded in 2011 through the merger of Nude Communication and JEH United, nudeJEH provides creative, branding, strategy consultation, web design and production services as well as having its own digital arm, Nine Dotz.

    The agency has a headcount of more than 60 full-time staff and an impressive roster of clients including BIO Consumer (Fineline, Tros, D-nee and Vivite ), Cutepress, Bar-B-Q plaza, Ananda Development, Bangkok Airways, Bangkok Dusit Medical Services and Tesco Lotus.

    "We are delighted to welcome nudeJEH to Grey Group. The agency's reputation for excellence fits with our 'Famously Effective' culture," said James R. Heekin, Chairman and CEO of Grey Group. "It will add to our growth in this important economy."

    The acquisition will bring immediate benefits to the clients of nudeJEH, including access to Grey Group's offerings such as its in-house broadcast production facility, GreyWorks, its full service digital and social medial agency, Grey Digital and its Hong Kong based shopper-marketing agency, Grey DPI. Similarly, Grey Group's clients from across the globe will benefit from the creativity and local expertise of nudeJEH.

    "This acquisition demonstrates our commitment to constantly deliver more value services for our clients," saidNirvik Singh, Chairman and CEO of Grey Group Asia Pacific. "Grey Group is rapidly establishing a strong leadership position in the Thai advertising industry and the partnership with nudeJEH is an excellent strategic fit to lead the industry with ground-breaking creative work."

    "This is such an exciting moment for us. We constantly seek innovative business solutions for the clients. Working with Grey Group, we can leverage each other's complementary strengths and create compelling strategies and outstanding creativity to address the clients' business needs," said Thor Santisiri, Chairman of nudeJEH.

    "We are looking forward to this opportunity to extend our operations in the Thai advertising industry. Grey Group has always been a trailblazer in producing innovative work, and with our shared vision, we are looking forward to creating the next generation of famously effective work," said Jureeporn Thaidumrong, Creative Chairwoman of nudeJEH.

    "We are excited and enthusiastic about this new journey with Grey. By combining the talent and technology, we will definitely see growth acceleration and creativity at its best," said Kanaporn Hutcheson, Managing Director of nudeJEH.

    About Grey Group 
    Grey Group ranks among the largest global communications companies. Its parent company is WPP (NASDAQ:WPPGY). Under the banner of "Grey Famously Effective Since 1917," the agency serves a blue-chip client roster of many of the world's best known companies: Procter & Gamble, GlaxoSmithKline, Diageo, Pfizer, Canon, 3M and Boehringer Ingelheim.     Synopsis 
    Mother's Day in Thailand is celebrated on August 12, and is a public holiday. In the Thai culture, it is common to share a meal and spend some time with our mom on that occasion. Unfortunately not everyone has a chance to be with their mom on that day, especially those working in the hospitality industry, and those who have left their hometown to work in the city in order to support their family. Bar-B-Q Plaza is one of the largest restaurant chains in the South East Asia region, with over 100 branches in Thailand, Malaysia and Indonesia. Our brand belief is to 'make the most of now', and on Mother's Day we want everyone to make the most of a meal with their mom, including our staff. But because it is one of the busiest days of the year for all restaurants around the country, our staff will be working hard while others can afford to spend time with their mom. As a solution, Bar-B-Q Plaza had arranged a little surprise for their most hardworking employees, especially those living away from home and family. A month before Mother's Day, we gave them the chance to share a meal and spend a moment with their moms. But to make that bonding moment truly special, we first asked our staff a few simple questions. A few simple questions that we can all ask ourselves too.
