Fortune Fountain Forum Held in Singapore

  • Helen.zhang
    15 Jun 15, 16:35

    SINGAPORE /PRNewswire/ -- The Fortune Fountain Forum – "Seeking Fortune and One Belt, One Road" Singapore forum was hosted by Fortune Fountain (Beijing) Holding Group Co. Ltd and Beijing Gainfull Wealth Group and co-hosted by Smart Fortune (Beijing) Culture and Media Co., Ltd at the Shangri-La Hotel in Singapore on May 18, 2015. With wealth creation as the key topic, the event held debates on how to best create wealth and on the Chinese government's "One Belt, One Road" initiative.

    Two of Fortune Fountain (Beijing) Holding Group's projects, the "D color" naked eye 3D flat panel handset – a world first – and the Chinese green agriculture-based "plant factory", drew the attention of the participants during the Made in China , Created in China, Chinese Trade and Chinese Service investment and project collaboration sessions. In addition to a presentation of its services, Swiss bank UBS led an in-depth discussion with Fortune Fountain (Beijing) Holding Group's Beijing Gainfull Wealth Group.

    Based on China's "One Belt, One Road" strategy and the 2015 Retrace the Maritime Silk Road initiative with the aim of promoting Chinese culture and thought, Fortune Fountain (Beijing) Holding Group kicked off a series of economic and trade forums with the goal of seeking new opportunities in overseas markets for China-made products. The initiative also asks Chinese entrepreneurs and investors who are already doing business with the firm to promote the connection between China and the rest of the world in terms of both monetary capital and intellectual capital, in a move to help fulfil the "One Belt, One Road" vision on a global level.

    Established in 2011, Fortune Fountain (Beijing) Holding Group Co. Ltd invests chiefly in businesses and financial instruments. The business investments are mostly in heavy industry, geriatric healthcare and assisted living facilities, agriculture and forestry, R&D and production of new energy sources and new materials, R&D for mobile telecommunications and the Big Dipper satellite navigation and positioning system, while the financial investments are focused on portfolio management, whole fund management, wealth management and asset management.

    Source: Fortune Fountain (Beijing) Holding Group Co. Ltd
