i.Tech Launches Power Solution Product Line

  • Helen.zhang
    21 Jul 10, 13:44

    Super-compact chargers provide power for iPhone, iPod and other mobile communication and entertainment devices

    HONG KONG, July 21 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- i.Tech Dynamic today introduced its new Power Solution product line. The i.Tech PowerApp and i.Tech Power X are portable, compact, external batteries designed to provide long, extended life for mobile communications and entertainment devices.

    (Photo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2010/07/15/20100715433806.html )
    (Photo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2010/07/15/20100715691225.html )
    (Logo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2009/09/02/200909021619.jpg )

    i.Tech PowerApp, a Made for iPhone-certified product, is one of the slimmest iPhone external batteries on the market. It is designed to provide four hours of additional talk time or Internet use for the iPhone 3G and 3GS. The charger also functions as an anti-slip protective case; it fits snugly around the body of the iPhone, leaving just the device's touchscreen exposed.

    The rechargeable i.Tech PowerApp features a built-in LED that functions as a camera flash for iPhone photography. This lets users take photos in low-light situations, such as restaurants, and it doubles as a handy flashlight for emergency use. An intelligent charging setting enables the device, while plugged in to a power source, to charge the iPhone first before recharging the PowerApp; and an LED indicator on the back of the unit show's the device's battery level while charging. Users can liven up the look of their iPhones with one of PowerApp's three available bold colors-matte black, metallic silver and metallic red.

    The i.Tech Power X is a universal charger that's incredibly compact-about the shape and size of a roll of pennies but just over half an ounce in weight. It comes with 10 adaptor tips that fit a wide range of mobile phones and entertainment devices, including the iPhone and PSP, and a compact case that keeps the Power X and adaptors organized and makes it convenient for users to charge their devices anywhere.

    The i.Tech Power X charges via USB connection and is available in five stylish colors-grey, aqua blue, pink, navy blue and red-each with an anodized aluminum finish.

    "PowerApp and Power X are highly portable and incredibly convenient back-up chargers, fully functioned and user-friendly," said Wendy Cho, managing director, i.Tech Dynamic Ltd. "i.Tech is well known for its outstanding mobile phone accessories and Bluetooth headsets. The Power Solution series provides even more convenience, more freedom and more entertainment for consumers. And the products are perfect for users on the go."
