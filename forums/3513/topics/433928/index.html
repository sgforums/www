(ISC)2 Announces Honorees for Asia-Pacific Information Secur

  • Helen.zhang
    8 Jul 11, 10:49

    Celebrates ISLA fifth anniversary with honorees at Gala Dinner on July 26 in Jakarta, Indonesia

    PRNewswire-Asia/ -- (ISC)2(R) ("ISC-squared"), the world's largest not-for-profit information security professional body and administrators of the CISSP(R), today announced the honorees for its fifth annual Asia-Pacific Information Security Leadership Achievements (ISLA) Program. (ISC)2 will announce four distinguished workforce initiatives led by the most outstanding honorees and a Community Service Star recipient at the Gala Dinner on July 26 in Jakarta, Indonesia.

    Guest of Honor, Dr. Ir. Ashwin Sasongko, M.Sc., director general for Informatics Applications, Ministry of Communication and Information Technology, Republic of Indonesia, together with (ISC)2 executives, will celebrate with the accomplishments of the talented information security leaders in Asia-Pacific.

    Through ISLA, (ISC)2 publicly recognizes the ongoing commitment by information security leaders throughout Asia-Pacific to build a highly qualified and ethical workforce. Part of (ISC)2's Global Awards Program, ISLA aims to recognize the achievements of outstanding information security professionals who have significantly contributed to the enhancement of the workforce by demonstrating a leadership role in an information security workforce improvement initiative, program or project. Professionals were nominated in three distinct categories: Managerial Professional for Information Security Project(s), Senior Information Security Professional, and Information Security Practitioner.     "ISLA has come a long way to gain such prestigious recognition from the regional industry since its inception five years ago. We are determined to continue to honor professionals who dedicate their time to protecting the vital components of information security infrastructure, and who strive for improvement and innovation every day. The growth of this program is evident in the continuously increasing number of nominations each year, and the caliber of the professionals nominated. (ISC)2 is grateful for the support from the Indonesian government as well as our Guest of Honor, Dr. Ir. Ashwin Sasongko. We look forward to celebrating with honorees and other distinguished guests in Jakarta," said W. Hord Tipton, CISSP-ISSEP, CAP, CISA, executive director of (ISC)2.

    (ISC)2 officials will recognize the ISLA honorees, showcased honorees, and Community Service Star at the ISLA Gala Dinner on July 26, 2011 at the Shangri-la Hotel in Jakarta, Indonesia. Concurrently, the (ISC)2 Security Leadership Event - SecureAsia@Jakarta Exhibition and Conference - will be held July 26-27 at the Shangri-la Hotel in Jakarta.
