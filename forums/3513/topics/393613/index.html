    CoreNet Global Welcomes its 2010 Singapore Summit Sponsors

     

    SINGAPORE, Mar. 15 /PRNewswire-Asia-AsiaNet/ --

     

     Colliers International, Haworth, Jones Lang LaSalle, Marina Bay Financial 

    Centre and One Raffles Quay confirm as headline sponsors

     

        Leading corporate real estate association, CoreNet Global, is pleased to 

    welcome the title sponsors for its highly anticipated Singapore Summit which 

    entitled, "Paradox: Balancing Contradictions in an Uncertain World," will be 

    held at the Shangri-la Hotel on March 23-25, 2010.    

     

        The conference - now in its eighth year - today serves as the main event 

    for corporate real estate professionals across the region to network and learn 

    about industry trends and opportunities affecting the commercial property 

    sector in Asia.  

     

        For 2010, more than 350 delegates and over 20 sponsors have already 

    committed their support to the event which through a series of general 

    assemblies, educational tracks and break-out sessions will discuss and debate 

    the implications that the uncertain economic and business environment has had 

    on the industry.

     

        In addition, the event will be officiated by Mr. Manohar Khiatani, Chief 

    Executive Officer of JTC Corporation, who will deliver opening remarks at the 

    commencement of the Summit's first general assembly scheduled to take place at 

    9.00a.m. on the morning of Wednesday 24th March.

     

        In terms of sponsorship, Ms. Melanie Hill, CoreNet Global's EMEA and Asia 

    Pacific Vice President of Operations commented, "We are both delighted and 

    appreciative of the tremendous support that we have received from all of our 

    sponsors for this year's Singapore Summit. Our event sponsors include some of 

    the industry's best known and most respected names, and we are proud to have 

    their participation." 

     

        Ms. Hill expressed that Platinum Sponsors Colliers International, Haworth, 

    Jones Lang LaSalle, Marina Bay Financial Centre and One Raffles Quay, will all 

    feature prominently throughout the Summit programme and participate in sessions 

    that include: Creating Outstanding Real Estate Leaders and Organizations; 

    Strategic Partnerships - Ingredients for Success; as well as Landlords and 

    Tenants - The Fight Club?
