    Marsh Named 'Broker of the Year' for Fourth Consecutive Year in Asian Insurance Industry Awards

     

    2010-10-19 11:10 

     


    SINGAPORE, Oct. 19 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Marsh, the world's leading insurance broker and risk advisor, has been named "Broker of the Year" at the 14th Annual Asia Insurance Industry Awards.  This is the fourth year in a row and fifth time in six years Marsh has taken home this accolade.

    The awards were the result of an extensive review process by a panel of over 20 international judges comprised of leading insurance industry professionals. The award was judged according to the following criteria:

    • In-depth knowledge and understanding of the market through research
    • Responsiveness to customer needs
    • Innovation
    • Industry leadership
    • Business retention and growth
    • Sound financial management
    • Outstanding customer service resulting in business referrals
    • Professional standards

    Alan Cheah, CEO of Marsh in Asia, said: "We are both honoured and humbled by this achievement. Marsh is focused on being a driving force in the industry's development, helping businesses across the region optimize their approach to risk management.  I would like personally to thank both our clients and our 1,600-plus people across the region, without whom this award would not be possible."

    Alex Moczarski, President of Marsh's International Division, said: "These awards are yet further testimony to Marsh's ability to deliver exceptional risk and insurance solutions for clients around the world -- especially in emerging markets.  Asia represents a significant growth engine for Marsh, and we remain committed to helping our clients become risk confident in this dynamic region.

    The awards are organised by the Asia Insurance Review and The Review, both leading industry publications, covering the insurance, reinsurance, broking and risk management industries across the region.

    About Marsh
