iProperty Awards to Showcase the People's Choice of the Year

  • Helen.zhang
    7 Dec 12, 15:47
    iProperty Awards to Showcase the People's Choice of the Year's Real Estate Developments Voters stand to win prizes including a SGD $20,000 living-room makeover SINGAPORE, Dec. 4, 2012 /PRNewswire/ -- Fancy an elegant living room makeover in the New Year? Leading property portal iProperty.com.sg today opens the online voting for Singapore's first People's Choice Awards of 2012. Participants stand to win a range of prizes, including a brand new and stylish designer living room from Lourve Design. Voters who wish to participate in the People's Choice Awards can do so by nominating their standout property across each category, to enter the prize draw. The grand prize is that swanky living room makeover from Lourve worth SGD $20,000. The second prize is three SGD $3,000 vouchers from ToTT, the largest kitchenware and tableware retail store in Asia. The third prize is two SGD $2,500 vouchers from Philips Lighting Singapore, and the fourth prize is a voucher from Accentuate Home worth SGD $2,500. The People's Choice Awards span ten award categories: Staycation Award, Take My Breath Award, Swinging Single Pad Award, Object of Desire Award, Property I Would Even Bet My Granny's Savings Award, Supersize Me Award, Saving Gaia Award, Foodie Paradise Award, Life's a Beach Award, and City Slickers Award. "We are very proud to host the People's Choice Awards, which is the first of its kind in Singapore. It will let us gauge consumer sentiment as the public votes for their choice of newly-launched properties across the 10 categories, and give developers insights into consumer preferences. The voting results will also provide us with insights to the property sector from a consumers' point of view," said Sean Tan, General Manager of iProperty.com Singapore. Commenting on their partnership with the People's Choice Awards, Mervyn Toh, Director from Lourve Design said: "We are very excited to be supporting iProperty and the People's Choice Awards. This is a great opportunity for us to engage with the growing number of digitally-savvy consumers and give us exposure to this particular segment of the market. We are especially looking forward to transforming the living room of the lucky winner!" Grace Tan, Marketing Manager for ToTT, said, "We are delighted to be a partner in the inaugural iProperty People's Choice Awards and share our passion for cooking, baking and hosting special events. When it comes to memorable dining experiences, nothing beats the ones we have in the comfort of our homes." For more information and to vote for The People's Choice Awards, please visit iproperty.com.sg/awards/. Closing date for voting is January 6, 2013.
