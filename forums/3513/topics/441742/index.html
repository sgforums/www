Wynn Resorts Named Five-Star Hotels and Spa by Forbes Travel

  • Helen.zhang
    17 Nov 11, 14:26

     

    (Wynn Macau) -- Wynn Macau and The Spa at Wynn Macau;
    (Wynn Las Vegas) -- Tower Suites at Wynn, Tower Suites at Encore, The Spa at Wynn, and
    The Spa at Encore are named Five-Star Hotels and Spa;
    All Winners Showcased on Newly Launched Forbes Travel Guide Website Startle.com

    MACAU, Nov. 16, 2011 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Forbes Travel Guide, formerly Mobil Travel Guide unveiled its 54th annual listing of Five-Star hospitality establishments worldwide, and named Wynn Macau and The Spa at Wynn Macau as part of this list. The Wynn Las Vegas Tower Suites, the Tower Suites at Encore and the spas at Wynn and Encore also received the esteemed award. The resorts are showcased with all of the 2012 award winners on Startle.com( http://www.startle.com ), the new interactive website of Forbes Travel Guide.

    Wynn Macau and The Spa at Wynn Macau are the only resort and spa to receive the Forbes Five-Star award for four consecutive years in Macau.

    Wynn Macau is one of just 57 hotels to achieve the Forbes Five-Star rating, and the only hotel in Macau to be recognized with this award for four consecutive years. This recognition reinforces the resort's leadership role in transforming Macau into an international travel and leisure destination.

    The Spa at Wynn Macau is the only spa in Macau to receive the Forbes Five-Star Award for four consecutive years, and is one of only 30 hotel and resort spas to receive this honor in 2012.

    Ian Michael Coughlan, President of Wynn Macau, said: "We are honored to receive these Forbes Five-Star Awards for the fourth consecutive year and to be recognized among the best Hotels and Spas in the world. These awards are a tribute to the ongoing commitment made by Wynn Macau team members to delivering exceptional experiences for our guests."

    The Tower Suites at Wynn Las Vegas has earned a Forbes Five-Star rating for six consecutive years, and the Tower Suites at Encore has received the Forbes Five-Star award in its third consecutive year of operation.

    The Spa at Wynn and The Spa at Encore in Las Vegas are among only four spas in Las Vegas to be recognized with the Forbes Five-Star award. Previously, The Spa at Wynn Las Vegas garnered acclaim from the Forbes Travel Guide with three consecutive Four-Star award wins in 2006, 2007 and 2008, and Five-Star awards in 2009, 2010 and 2011. The Spa at Encore also received a Forbes Five-Star award in its first two years of operation in 2010 and 2011.

    Collectively, Wynn Resorts has received more Forbes Five-Star awards than any other casino resort in the world.

    Wynn Las Vegas, Encore Las Vegas and Wynn Macau are the latest installments to the travel ratings system that has been the gold standard in the industry since 1958.

    "Our annual ratings represent the best of the best in hotels, restaurants and spas. They serve as guideposts for consumers who are looking for exceptional travel experiences," said Shane O'Flaherty, President, Inspections & Ratings, Forbes Travel Guide. "This year, we are thrilled to be able to provide rich new content for each of our Five-Star and Four-Star honorees on our just-launched travel planning platform, Startle. It's a whole new way to explore which of these select properties is right for you."
