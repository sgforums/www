Amazing Mid-Year Getaways with Carlson Rezidor's Hot Deals

  • Helen.zhang

    SINGAPORE, July 6, 2012 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Enjoy a mid-year break with the return of Hot Deals, Carlson Rezidor Hotel Group's mid-year promotion exclusively for its Asia Pacific hotels. Book any hotel in Asia Pacific, across all brands - Radisson®, Park Plaza®, Park Inn by Radisson and Country Inns & Suites By Carlson(SM) - and save up to 30% off the best room rates. Simply make a reservation from now until September 9, 2012 for a minimum two-night stay between July 1 and November 30, 2012 to take advantage of the hottest hotel deals in Asia Pacific.

    To put that extra sizzle into a Hot Deals hotel stay, all guests will be rewarded with 10,000 Bonus Gold Points® for Club Carlson(SM) loyalty program for stays between July 17 and September 30, 2012. They simply need to sign up as Club Carlson members and register for the "Two Nights. 10,000 Points." promotion via www.clubcarlson.com/10000points to qualify for their bonus points. In addition, new Club Carlson members will also receive a welcome reward of 1,000 Bonus Gold Points. Club Carlson Gold Points can be used to redeem free room nights at Carlson Rezidor hotels worldwide, making these Hot Deals the perfect way for guests to reward themselves further by trying out new hotels in exciting destinations.

    "Hot Deals make business travel and vacations extra special with fabulous savings and bonus loyalty points. It is our way of thanking and rewarding our guests. Following the overwhelmingly positive response that we received from our previous Hot Deals promotions, we are confident that this season it will be equally as popular with our guests across Asia Pacific. Hot Deals enables business and leisure travelers to maximize their savings and enjoy planning more trips, the smartest way to travel right now," said Lucinda Semark, executive vice president, Revenue Generation, Carlson Hotels, Asia Pacific.

    Hot Deals is Carlson Rezidor's multi-brand promotion, which happens twice a year, across Asia Pacific. Hot Deals rewards loyal guests with special perks such as Club Carlson bonus points, on top of huge savings on the best hotel rates at all Carlson Rezidor hotels in the Asia Pacific region, to offer guests the opportunity to extend their holidays, book more trips and try out new hotels in exciting new destinations.

    About Carlson Rezidor Hotel Group

    Carlson Rezidor Hotel Group -- born in early 2012 -- is one of the world's largest and most dynamic hotel groups. The portfolio of the Carlson Rezidor Hotel Group includes more than 1,300 hotels, a global footprint spanning 81 countries and territories, a powerful set of global brands (Radisson Blu, Radisson®, Park Plaza®, Park Inn by Radisson, Country Inns & Suites By Carlson(SM) and Hotel Missoni). In most of the group's hotels, guests can benefit from the loyalty program Club Carlson(SM), one of the most rewarding loyalty programs in the world. The Carlson Rezidor Hotel Group and its brands employ more than 80,000 people.

    Carlson Rezidor Hotel Group is headquartered in Minneapolis, Minn, and Brussels, Belgium.
