    MSIG Insurance Partners Underwriting Agency, DUAL to Provide Financial Lines Capacity in the Singapore Market SINGAPORE, Jan. 21, 2013 /PRNewswire/ -- MSIG Insurance (Singapore) Pte. Ltd. ("MSIG Singapore"), a member of the MS&AD Insurance Group, is pleased to announce the launch of its financial lines capability. MSIG Singapore will partner global underwriting agency, DUAL, to provide specialised insurance solutions for Financial Lines products including Directors and Officers (D&O), Professional Indemnity, Investment Managers, Management Liability and Association Liability covers. These financial lines products will allow MSIG Singapore to bring together a flexible and comprehensive insurance package that will address the growing needs of its private and corporate networks. Paul Faulkner, CEO of MSIG Singapore, said: "With this latest line of covers, our clients will now have a wider range of insurance solutions to choose from. These financial lines products are specifically designed with local laws and regulations in mind. We will continue to invest and grow this new capability and we look forward to working with DUAL to deliver the high quality solutions that MSIG is well known for." Damien Coates, CEO of DUAL International, commented: "This is an exciting extension of our excellent partnership with MSIG. We have a growing presence in Asia, with plans for further expansion in line with the needs of our clients. The combination of MSIG's competitive strength and DUAL's specialist product expertise, gives us a fantastic platform for that growth and expansion."
