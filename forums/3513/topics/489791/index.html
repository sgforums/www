    INDIANAPOLIS  /PRNewswire/ -- AOTMP recently announced the dates for Telecom Management training Boot Camps, specially designed to bring tangible and quantifiable benefits to business professionals leading complex telecom environments, at its authorized training center, Volare Training, in Singapore. Students will learn how to achieve telecom spend visibility driven through KPI-driven analytics, adopt best practices to identify spend leakages and optimize their telecom expenditures, and how to streamline their existing business processes to achieve operational efficiencies:

    Telecom Management Fundamentals for Business | Oct 7 – 9: Principles of the Efficiency First® Framework and key terms and concepts that define the telecom management industry. Telecom management fundamentals taught in an interactive setting where participants can apply learned principles to gain clear understanding of business performance in a telecom environment. Ideal for senior level leadership (IT or Finance Director, VP IS/IT, CIO).

    Fixed and Mobile Telecom Auditing | Nov 16 – 20: Establishes foundation practices for performing telecom audits, resolving billing disputes, optimizing services and addressing the root cause of many common exceptions identified in a telecom audit. Audit practices that adhere to principles of the Efficiency First® Framework will be shared with instructor-led exercises. Ideal for telecom, IT, finance professionals seeking to learn and master fixed and mobile telecom auditing techniques.

    Telecom Vendor Sourcing and Procurement | Dec 7 – 9: Principles to identify and select telecom vendors suited to meet and exceed expectations. Ideal for IT/technology/telecom sourcing and procurement professionals.
