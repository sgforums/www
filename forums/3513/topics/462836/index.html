    Asian Asset Managers to Tackle Performance Analysis and Risk Management Challenges at IPARM Asia Summit SINGAPORE, Dec. 12, 2012 /PRNewswire/ -- Market volatility, increasing scrutiny from the investors and regulators, coupled with the increased complexity of investment products, have led to the role of risk and performance becoming exceptionally important. The 6th Annual IPARM Asia Summit, taking place 30th - 31st January in Hong Kong sets the perfect platform to discuss and share strategies to tackle performance analysis and risk management. Speakers from some of Asia's leading asset managers, including: BlackRock, Franklin Templeton, BNP Paribas Securities Services, Aberdeen and AmInvestment will come together to share their ways of staying ahead of the curve to secure investors' and shareholders' trust. "I am looking forward to the insightful and critical panel discussions at the 6th Annual IPARM Asia 2013," commented Andrew Kophamel, Head of Performance, Asia Pacific at Aberdeen Asset Management. Andrew will join a panel discussion shedding perspectives on outsourcing risk and performance management, the pros and cons, evaluation criteria and ultimately delivering optimal results for clients. BI-SAM and Eagle Investment Systems, both recognised leaders in providing risk and performance software to asset managers, have come on board as sponsors. "Eagle is honoured to once again be sponsoring IPARM. IPARM gives performance and risk experts the opportunity to network and share their ideas on how to address common industry challenges. Eagle is seeing increased demand for performance and risk solution across the APAC region," commented John Legrand, Managing Director for Eagle Investment Systems. Meanwhile, Global Marketing & Communications Manager Benedicte Godet from BI-SAM said, "We are very pleased to be sponsoring IPARM Asia 2013. Each year, the conference provides a great opportunity to gain insight on Asian performance and risk management practice. We also appreciate the opportunity to participate in a panel discussion on one of our area of expertise: Fixed Income Attribution." In conjunction with the summit, Finance IQ recently had an exclusive interview with BNP Paribas Security Services' Head of Investment Reporting and Performance - Asia Pacific, Mr. Madhu Gayer. He gave a full 360-degree view on the ways to enhance the current performance measurement framework and the impact. The interview can be downloaded at the 6th Annual IPARM Asia 2013 Resource Centre.
