    HONG KONG, Nov. 8, 2011 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- A brand new sourcing trade fair, FUJIAN COMMODITIES EXPO-SINGAPORE & ASIA EXPO-SINGAPORE, will take place on 23-25 November, 2011, offering a new sourcing location for South-East Asian trade buyers.

    The Fujian Provincial People's Government and Kenfair Exhibition (Hong Kong) Limited will co-organise these two business events in the Sands Expo and Convention Centre, at the new Marina Bay complex in Singapore for regional importers, distributors and wholesalers.

    Many China exporters are now placing increased focus on the developing markets in this region and the rapidly- growing ASEAN countries to expand their business networks. Singapore, as the geographical centre and key trading entrepot of South-East Asia, is the logical location for this new consumer retail products expo.

    FUJIAN COMMODITIES EXPO-SINGAPORE & ASIA EXPO-SINGAPORE is set to deliver 600 Asian and China based manufacturers, all presenting a variety of quality-led and competitively-priced merchandise. The expo will combine two distinct sections -- FUJIAN COMMODITIES EXPO-SINGAPORE will line up over 300 quality Mainland China enterprises from Fujian Province in China; ASIA EXPO-SINGAPORE will showcase a wide spectrum of over 200 exhibitors from MAINLAND CHINA, HONG KONG, INDONESIA, SOUTH KOREA, THE PHILIPPINES, TAIWAN, THAILAND and VIETNAM but presented under one roof for the convenience of the Asian and regional buyers who are sourcing and identifying new business opportunities.

    On show will be new ranges of innovative Giftware & Premiums, Housewares, Toys & Baby Products, Consumer Electronics, Gadgets, Food & Native Products, Garments & Apparel, Textiles & Fabrics, Footwear & Fashion Accessories, Hardware & Building Materials and Machinery & Auto Parts.

    The expo coincides with the final year-end sourcing cycle for most regional buyers and will feature a well-edited and diverse merchandise mix for buyers to wrap-up their autumn sourcing in South-East Asia.

    ORGANISERS

    Fujian Provincial People's Government

    The Fujian Provincial People's Government has been very active in launching exhibitions and initiatives to boost the export strength and market reach of its provincial enterprises.

    Kenfair Exhibition (Hong Kong) Limited

    Kenfair is a leading Hong Kong-based trade fair management company with rich experience in hosting and managing large-scale international trade exhibitions. Apart from its annual flagship MEGA SHOW every October in Hong Kong, Kenfair has run trade shows in Macau, London, Las Vegas, Warsaw, Shanghai and Ningbo.
