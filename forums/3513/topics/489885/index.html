    SINGAPORE /PRNewswire/ --Atos, an international leader in digital services, today announced that as the appointed Official IT Integration Partner, it delivered its integration expertise on an extraordinary scale at the 28th South East Asian Games Singapore 2015 (SEA Games 2015).

    Its 270 member strong, multi-national project team from China, Spain and Singapore helped define new standards for future SEA Games and Major Events across the world. 

    Amidst critical deadlines, requirement changes and sheer scale of event, Atos achieved its own Gold standard in the race against time, resource and cost. The dedicated team of business technologists implemented an improved Games Management System across 31 venues and 402 events. This ensured an efficient flow of IT processes and support for the Games preventing any outage throughout the duration of the Games.

    Additionally a dedicated mobile application was launched, a first for the SEA Games, bringing the latest games results to the public enhancing public engagement and accessibility to the Games itself.

    "The South East Asian Games could not have been staged without the right technology behind them. With our vast experience, we take advantage of the latest advances in technology. We have helped to transform the IT of these Games, improving the experience for athletes, spectators and viewers. Atos embraces digital transformation and see step changes in how disruptive technologies address emerging challenges and make a major impact," saysMarta Sanfeliu, General Manager Olympics, Major Events at Atos.

    "Our personal conviction to deliver a flawless performance through cost effective measures, robust and secure platforms thereby developing new documentations and tools has set a new benchmark in the Major Events industry. I am proud of our achievements. More so of our team that has once again proven their mettle," saysHerbie Leung, APAC CEO, Atos.
