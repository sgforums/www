    2012 WCG World Finals on Stage at the End of Nov. in Kunshan KUNSHAN, China, Nov. 12, 2012 /PRNewswire/ -- The 2012 World Cyber Games (WCG 2012) will once again be held in Kunshan, China. This year's World Finals will be held in Kunshan International Exhibition Centre from Nov 29 to Dec 2. More than 500 contestants from over 40 countries and regions are expected to participate. Established in 2000, WCG aims at creating healthy and harmonious Internet Culture. Tempered over ten years, it has become a global electronic competition of the greatest scale with participants and viewers worldwide. Cyber Game masters from around the globe will gather in different cities and share the joy and passion of the Cyber Games with other Cyber fans.
