China Mobile Group Terminal Decides in Favor of the Rohde &

  • Helen.zhang

    MUNICH, Germany, Feb. 20, 2012 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- China Mobile Group Terminal Co., Ltd. will use the R&S CMW500 wideband radio communication tester from Rohde & Schwarz to perform protocol conformance tests on time-division long-term evolution (TD-LTE) terminal equipment. The T&M manufacturer prevailed against the competitors in a call for bids.

    The R&S CMW500 is configured as a protocol tester for the TD-LTE conformance tests performed by China Mobile Group Terminal. The company was founded in April 2011 by network operator China Mobile Limited to promote the development of third- and fourth-generation mobile radio terminals, focusing on the 3G and 4G TD versions, i.e. the two Chinese standards TD-SCDMA and TD-LTE.

    Anton Messmer, Director of the Mobile Radio Testers Subdivision at Rohde & Schwarz, commenting on the latest order: "China Mobile plays a leading role in TD-LTE. We are pleased that China Mobile Group Terminal has chosen the R&S CMW500 from Rohde & Schwarz for its protocol conformance tests."

    The multistandard platform can do much more, however. For example, the R&S CMW500 can also cover RF or application tests. It is therefore the ideal solution for all phases in the development and production of mobile terminals.
