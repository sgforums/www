Appier Introduces First Programmatic TV Solution in Asia....

  • Helen.zhang
    25 Jun 15, 10:36

    TAIPEI /PRNewswire/ -- Appier, the leading Artificial Intelligence (AI) empowered cross-screen technology company, today announced its introduction of the first programmatic TV solution (Appier Programmatic TV) in Asia, aiming to bring advertisers in the region an effective approach to reach the substantial but fragmented TV-watching populations by adding intelligence to traditional TV buying to drive increase in response rates. The new offering is a valuable complement to complete Appier's true cross-screen advertising portfolio that harness its proprietary artificial intelligence (AI) technologies to enable all advertisers to identify the same users across all screens, spanning from mobile phones, tablets, PCs to TVs.

    "It's exciting to bring Appier Programmatic TV to advertisers and marketers when we are striving to challenging conventional thinking of TV buying," said Chih-Han Yu, Co-founder and CEO of Appier. "Appier Programmatic TV is an unconventional solution, uniquely defining how we use AI technology to effectively help advertisers analyze consumers' fast-changing lifestyles in the multi-screen era while it can dramatically improve TV buying with intelligence through data analysis correlated to other mobile and PC campaigns to increase response rates."

    When programmatic TV is considered as the next frontier of digital revolution for advertisers and marketers, Appier's automated data-driven channel recommendations are crucial to them whom want to accurately capture TV audience' profiles to achieve business goals with optimized advertising budgets. With Appier programmatic TV, advertisers now can plan TV campaigns based on specific targeted audience profiles by associating TV buying strategies with user behaviors on all other screens.

    Appier has made the revolutionary programmatic TV solution initially available in Singapore through a partnership with a well-known Singapore-based telecom company while the offering will be projected to more markets soon. Appier's channel recommendation system can also incorporate insights from advertisers' existing analytics capabilities, enabling brands to leverage data-enabled technologies to identify the most relevant channels.

    With addition of programmatic TV solution to Appier's strong product portfolio, the company can now enable advertisers an extensive reach to a broad spectrum of audience across mobile devices, PCs and TVs, and more importantly, allowing them to take advantage of Appier's AI powered cross-screen solutions to make the most intelligent media buying decisions holistically.
