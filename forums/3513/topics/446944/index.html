Perfect World Officially Launches "Chi Bi" in Indonesia

  • Helen.zhang
    9 Feb 12, 14:27

    BEIJING, Feb. 1, 2012 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Beijing Perfect World Network Technology Co., Ltd. ("Perfect World"), a leading online game developer and operator based in China, formally announced today that it has launched "Chi Bi" inIndonesia through PT. Buana Online Internasional ("Buana"), a subsidiary of its partner PT. Prodigy Infinitech, in January 2012.

     

    (Logo:http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2009/04/16/200904161852.jpg )

     

    "Chi Bi" is the company's first 3D MMORPG based on the true history of the Three Kingdoms. The game was officially launched in 2008 in mainland China. The title contains a compelling story with grand and magnificent effects as well as a true and factually correct reflection of the war scenes during the Three Kingdoms period. "Chi Bi", with its strong Chinese elements, was enthusiastically embraced by a number of players as soon as it was launched, and has already proven its popularity in all of the markets worldwide in which it has been launched.

     

    As for the official launch of the game in Indonesia, Budiman Tiandy, Buana's Chief Operating Officer, said: "Chi Bi is an outstanding online game based on traditional Chinese culture. In addition to sophisticated game content, its background based on authentic historical fact also makes it one of the favorite online games among players in different countries. We believe Chi Bi will become an immediate hit in Indonesiathrough our close cooperation with Perfect World."

     

    Zhu Qi, President of Perfect World Interactive Entertainment Co., Ltd., added: "We are delighted with the successful launch of Chi Bi in Indonesia. As an online game based on Perfect World's strategy of delivering best-in-class products,Chi Bi has gained popularity among global players and stimulated much interest in traditional Chinese culture among players worldwide. We are confident that through our cooperation with Buana, Chi Bi will demonstrate increased market value and add to the attraction that players around the world have for Chinese online games."
