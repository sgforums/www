PT.NISCONI Moves Forward with AVEVA

  • Helen.zhang
    30 Jul 10, 15:30

    2010-07-29 09:00

     

        JAKARTA, Indonesia, July 29 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- AVEVA Group plc (LSE:AVV),  one of the world's leading providers of engineering design and information management solutions to the plant, power and marine industries, announced that PT. Nippon Steel Construction Indonesia (NISCONI) has increased their investment in AVEVA technology.  A long standing AVEVA customer, PT. NISCONI recently committed to a significant ongoing investment of AVEVA software and services for their Indonesian business operations.

     

        As one of the largest onshore and offshore engineering and construction service providers in Indonesia, PT. NISCONI retains technology excellence through its use of AVEVA Plant solutions and has also announced a significant increase in its investment with AVEVA. 

     

        Established in 1972, PT. NISCONI came on board with AVEVA in 1995 and has been using AVEVA PDMS extensively in their onshore and offshore engineering and construction services. PT. NISCONI is a joint venture company between Perusahaan Pertambangan Minyak dan Gas Bumi Negara (PERTAMINA) and Nippon Steel Corporation, one of the world's largest steel producers.
