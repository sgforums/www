    Crystal CG Sponsors the U.S. Pavilion of the Expo 2010 Shanghai

    2010-04-09 20:22

     

     

    Leading Enterprise Shows Chinese Visualization Charm

     

        SHANGHAI, April 9 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Following the launch of the renowned Expo Shanghai Online ( http://www.expo.cn ), and previously won contracts with the Italy Pavilion and the United Nations Pavilion to build their online pavilions for Expo 2010 Shanghai, Crystal Digital Technology Co., Ltd. (Crystal CG) today announced a cooperation deal with the U.S. Pavilion organizers to develop the U.S. online pavilion.  The new deal highlights the Company’s commitment to the Expo as the Designated Multimedia Design Service Provider of Expo 2010 Shanghai China.  With many multinational corporations, such as Microsoft and GE, being sponsors of the U.S. Pavilion, Crystal CG is the first Chinese enterprise to become a sponsor of the U.S. Pavilion.

     

        After the signing and unveiling the 3D promotional movie trailer for the U.S. Pavilion, Crystal CG will soon focus on the development, building, integration and communication issues of the U.S. online pavilion.  Crystal CG will incorporate its advanced, self-developed N-city technology, a cross-web rich media production, and launch a platform designed to create a "multi-dimensional" online display tool by realizing a 3D simulative and interactive software system, through a series of standard production procedures.  This promising technology is widely used in a range of business fields as an effective way of marketing.

     

        Since its establishment in 1995, Crystal CG has grown into the biggest digital visualization service provider in Asia, with over 10 branches worldwide, and is dedicated to providing world-class digital visualization services on the back of its advanced digital 3D technologies.

     

        The cooperation with the U.S. Pavilion is viewed as another achievement for Crystal CG while symbolizing its move into the international market. As a global visualization specialist in the multimedia age, Crystal CG leverages its advantages in communication and marketing and helps its clients to realize their ideas by applying 3D visualization technology to creative solutions.  After the big success of its project as Beijing 2008 Games Graphic Design Services Supplier, Crystal CG is now dedicating its efforts on the Expo 2010 Shanghai China and as the Official Digital Imaging Services Supplier of the London 2012 Olympic Games, striving to write a miraculous chapter on the world stage for the visualization industry of China.
