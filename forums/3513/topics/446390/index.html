    InterCall Announces Strategic Partnership with Singapore Exchange (SGX)

    SINGAPORE, Jan. 30, 2012 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- InterCall Asia Pacific, the largest conferencing and collaboration service provider in the world, announced today its strategic partnership with Singapore Exchange (SGX), the Asian gateway connecting investors with corporate issuers, to deliver InterCall's new Virtual Environment technology to SGX-listed companies. 

    Virtual Environments enable businesses to easily build and customise virtual marketing, recruiting and training environments. Attendees are greeted by a virtual host and can view sessions, ask questions, chat, network and view supporting material. The attendee interface is designed for ease-of-use and a high level of attendee engagement, which enables high levels of interactivity and accessibility among participants. 

    This solution will be added to SGX's Corporate Services portfolio, and both companies are excited to deliver this value-added service to SGX's listed partners. 

    "InterCall's virtual platform provides SGX-listed companies with another avenue to raise their company profiles with investors and customers. It adds to SGX's expanding suite of corporate services to promote investor outreach and board excellence," said Mita Natarajan, Head of Corporate Services at SGX. 

    InterCall is excited to be able to partner with SGX, Asia's most internationalised exchange, to provide a branded and interactive virtual environment platform. The solution is engaging, intuitive and easy to navigate. "Together, we can leverage each other's strengths to provide state-of-the-art, virtual solutions to a range of customers listed on SGX in Singapore, and throughout Asia," said Jon Bratsis, director of Unified Communications for InterCall Asia Pacific. 

    About InterCall 

    InterCall, a subsidiary of West Corporation, is the largest conference and collaboration service provider in the world and a leading provider of global cloud-based unified communications services. Founded in 1991, InterCall offers telephony, messaging, conferencing and collaboration tools for businesses of all sizes, from large global enterprises to small or regional companies. With a global footprint and broad service capabilities, InterCall's flexible models for hosted, managed and on-premises communications services help companies get the most out of their business processes. 

    InterCall's strong U.S. presence, including four call centers and 26 sales offices, is bolstered by operations in Canada, Mexico, Latin America, the Caribbean, the United Kingdom, Ireland, France, Germany, Australia, mainland China, India, Hong Kong, Singapore, Malaysia, Japan and New Zealand.
