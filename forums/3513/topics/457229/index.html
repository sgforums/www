    The Supreme Court of Singapore Comes Onboard at Legal IQ's eDiscovery Asia 2012 
    2012/08/16 14:11

    SINGAPORE, Aug. 16, 2012 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- The Supreme Court of Singapore and Heads of Legal across Asia come together to discuss how to make eDiscovery more accessible at Legal IQ's eDiscovery Asia 2012 conference this November at the Amara Hotel, Singapore. 

    As part of the global series, the inaugural eDiscovery Asia 2012 will bring together leading legal professionals and regulatory bodies to discuss how to implement eDiscovery; utilise the e-litigation process through information governance platforms in order to improve e-litigation and enhance data management and security. 

    Aiming to be the driving force for eDiscovery technological innovation in Asia, eDiscovery Asia 2012 will provide the perfect platform for legal experts in the industry to come together and shape the future development of eDiscovery policies, strategies and initiatives. 

    Mr Wong Siew Hong, Partner at Eden Law LLP and a speaker at the conference commented, "eDiscovery Asia 2012 -- Simple enough for a beginner, complex enough for experience eDiscovery users. Brilliant!" 

    The rise in the volume of stored data across Asia means that corporate and legal departments need to re-look at the discovery part of the litigation process in order to successfully collect, analyse and dispose their data. Proactive eDiscovery solutions & strategies are becoming more critical to legal departments, yet the solutions for costs, implementation, and management are still widely unknown. 

    By attending the conference, organisations can establish how to implement successful information governance policies and frameworks as part of their core business process, to enhance data accessibility and reduce inconsistencies. Mr Tay Yong Seng, also a speaker at the conference and Partner at Allen & Gledhill LLP, further added, "IQPC eDiscovery Asia provides good discussions and comments from all representatives and speakers across industries." 

    The full programme agenda can be downloaded from the website. For more event details, visit www.ediscoveryasiapacific.com.
