    Janus Corporate Solutions Adds New Office Space to Accommodate Rapid Growth

    Janus Corporate Solutions has doubled its office space as more businesses look to incorporate inSingapore.

    SINGAPORE, Sept. 4, 2012 /PRNewswire/ -- Janus Corporate Solutions, a leading Singaporecompany registration services firm( http://www.guidemesingapore.com ) in Singapore, has acquired a large new office space to accommodate its expanding team and service its growing client base.

    The new space is located within Hong Leong Building, the current site of Janus' operations. It will effectively double the company's total office space, while remaining in the same strategic location in the heart of Singapore's central business district. Renovations began last month and are projected to be completed in the next couple of weeks.

    The expansion ensures that Janus will be able to maintain its high quality of service as it continues to widen its operations. Rapid growth in both clients and services, along with strategic hiring, has led to the company's third office expansion in the last three years.

    "It is truly an exciting time for all of us right now, not just at Janus but in Singapore as a whole," said Ms. Jacqueline Low, Chief Operating Officer of Janus Corporate Solutions. "Our growth reflects the confidence our clients have placed in Janus and the quality of service our talented staff provides."

    "Over the past few years, our client base has grown significantly, partially due to the fact that the world is finally seeing Singapore for what it truly is - a very suitable global business hub. Entrepreneurs and businesses all over the world are recognizing that Singapore is an attractive place to do business because of factors such as its pro-enterprise policies, stable economy, attractive tax rates( http://www.guidemesingapore.com/taxation ), highly skilled workforce, and sensible immigration visa schemes( http://www.guidemesingapore.com/relocation ). The location is also strategic for doing business in Asia, and on top of everything else it is a great place to live," she explained.

    "To keep up with the growing demand while ensuring that we continue to provide the great service that has earned us the trust of our existing clients, we have doubled our space to complement our existing office," continued Ms Low. "We believe that bright times continue to be ahead for the region, and we look forward to helping more businesses set up their operations in Singapore(http://www.guidemesingapore.com/incorporation ) as they pursue opportunities for success inAsia."
