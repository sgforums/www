Dip Into Cool Deals with Carlson Rezidor's Summer Promotion

  • Helen.zhang
    16 Jul 15, 15:10

    SINGAPORE  /PRNewswire/ -- Carlson Rezidor Hotel Group is offering holidaymakers the "Cool Deals, Dip In!" promotion across its hotel brands in Asia Pacific this summer season. From July 15, 2015 to September 30, 2015, travelers who book and stay two consecutive nights or more at participating Radisson Blu®, Radisson®, Park Plaza®, Park Inn® by Radisson and Country Inns & Suites By Carlson hotels will enjoy 15% off the Best Available Rates. They will also earn up to Quadruple Gold Points® in Carlson Rezidor's Club Carlson global rewards program, as well as dining privileges for children, with three complimentary meals a day.

    "There is nothing like a cool dip in summer and kicking back by the poolside with a refreshing drink that has an umbrella in it. So, round up the whole family for Carlson Rezidor's "Cool Deals, Dip In!" promotion now and enjoy a great summer vacation together at leading resort destinations like Phuket, Davao, Goa and Fiji, while earning extra loyalty points. These are the things that happy memories are made of," said Sandy Russell, vice president, Commercial Operations, Carlson Rezidor Hotel Group, Asia Pacific.

    Club Carlson members who book "Cool Deals, Dip In!" and stay two or three nights will earn Double Club Carlson points. For stays of four nights and longer, Club Carlson members will be rewarded with Quadruple Club Carlson points. No registration is required and members can earn up to a maximum of 100,000 points for every stay. Several participating hotels are also offering a Kids Eat Free privilege. Children who are 12 years and below can enjoy three complimentary meals a day during every qualifying stay.

    "Cool Deals, Dip In!" bookings can be made through www.clubcarlson.com/cool-deals, as well as the brand websites, call centers and directly with the hotels.
