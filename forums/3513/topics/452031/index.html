    bizhub PRO 951 Brings 5 Improved Solutions to Production Printing

    New entry level monochrome production printer from Konica Minolta provides better image quality, paper handling, reliability & durability, registration and operability.

     

    SINGAPORE, May 7, 2012 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Konica Minolta Business Solutions Asia Pte Ltd.is pleased to announce the release of bizhub PRO 951 in Singapore. Designed for the light production business market, the bizhub PRO 951 succeeds the popular bizhub PRO 950, and is scheduled to ship in 2012.

     

    Improvements include 1200dpi x 1200dpi print resolution, wider range of paper handling capability ranging from 40gsm to 350gsm, increased paper size to handle 324mm x 483mm, maximum monthly duty of 1.5 million prints and advance front and back registration resulting in output that is more professional. Customers will get more mileage as a result of better performance, ease of use and handling. In addition, the bizhub PRO 951 is perfectly positioned to complement existing Konica Minolta MIFs.

     

    "We are excited to bring the bizhub PRO 951 to Singapore," said Mr. Daisuke Mori, Sales and Marketing Director of Konica Minolta Business Solutions Asia. "This is our new entry level monochrome production printer and is perfect for Central Reprographics Departments. The bizhub PRO 951's enriched solutions will enable a wider range of customers to achieve a higher standard of professionalism even in the low-end market."

     

    The bizhub 950 was introduced in March 2009 and is scheduled to be retired with the introduction of its successor. In accordance with Konica Minolta's retirement policy, support for bizhub Pro 950 will be available for the next 7 years. Customers should consider upgrading to the bizhub 951 for increased efficiency and productivity.
