CloudFX Sees International Growth

  • Helen.zhang
    20 Mar 13, 09:52

    Renowned Cloud computing and Virtualization firm opens Australian offices

    SINGAPORE, March 18, 2013 /PRNewswire/ -- Asia Pacific's leading Cloud technology advisory firm, CloudFX, has opened Australian offices on the back of strong demand from leading businesses within the region.

    The firm opened offices in Melbourne and Sydney earlier this month, following detailed research into the region and discussions with potential new partners.

    CloudFX President & CEO Damian Crotty says the move is well-timed for a market that is showing promise and maturity in adopting Virtualization and Cloud strategies.

    "We are truly excited about the potential to bring value to the Enterprise and Public Cloud provider market in Australia, which is widely regarded as one of the most mature Virtualization markets. However, when seen in the context of leveraging highly automated, rapidly provisioned, seamlessly orchestrated IT services to enable businesses to achieve their objectives, Australian Enterprises are really at the start of the journey," he says.

    According to Crotty, it is apparent that organizations in Australia are aware of the need to move forward and adopt a streamlined approach to Cloud solutions.

    "While we see well known Australian Telco and IT brands offering Public Cloud services, these organizations - by their own admission - would agree a significant amount of work needs to be done to position themselves as a strategic alternative sourcing model for IT services," he says.

    The expansion into Australia is a step further in long term plans for CloudFX's global growth agenda. It leads further growth efforts in North Asia with markets such as Japan showing significant opportunity and need for Cloud computing solutions.

    The Singapore-headquartered firm is the only one of its kind in Asia-Pacific and has seen significant growth in the past four years. Since its inception in 2009, CloudFX has expanded rapidly within the region, with offices in Malaysia and Indonesia established in 2011. The company has helped transform businesses across Asia-Pacific to the Cloud, including many of the leading finance and telecom companies in the region.

    "CloudFX specializes in areas where customers need real world expertise like Virtualization and Cloud Management, Service Orchestration, Billing - Metering methodologies, IT Service Catalogue creation and integration and finally, service delivery frameworks that deliver rapid value added services to an organization," says Crotty.
