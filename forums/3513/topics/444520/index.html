    Getting To Know China with The "Happy Chinese New Year" Application

    BEIJING, Dec. 29, 2011 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- On 29 December, 2011, the fun and free "Happy Chinese New Year" interactive application for iPad was released online at the Apple Store. 

    This application introduces overseas "Happy Chinese New Year" festivities of China's Ministry of Culture and other organizations. The App offers information about Chinese performing arts troupes entertaining across the world during the first half of 2012, and introduces Chinese traditions and customs for celebrating the Spring Festival. Each interface can toggle instantly between Chinese and English languages. Sharing the App can allow more people to get to know China's wonderful traditional culture. You can extend New Year's wishes to your good friends and relatives with our Chinese New Year electronic greeting card, which has rich accents of the special characteristics of Chinese culture. 

    Since 2010, China has held "Happy Chinese New Year" festivities in Thailand, Singapore, Japan, Korea, and other countries as well as in Hong Kong and Macau regions, which have been welcomed with great enthusiasm. If you would like to know more, please check out the "Happy Chinese New Year" App. 

    "Happy Chinese New Year" is developed by CAV Television Production Co., Ltd.
