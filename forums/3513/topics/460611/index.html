    Hearing & Wellness Day, 27th Oct 2012 Singapore: The Hearing Solution Group

    SINGAPORE, Oct. 24, 2012 /PRNewswire/ -- The Hearing Solution Group's Hearing & Wellness will be held on the 27th of October from 10am to 4pm at the Singapore Management University (SMU) Administration building, level 4. This event features the latest hearing technologies for hearing impaired people as well as showcasing the current wellness trends in Singapore.

    The event is open to the public and will feature:

    • Distinguished Guest Speaker, Dr Barrie Tan, Deputy Director of the Centre for Hearing & Implants in the Singapore General Hospital.
    • Guest Speaker, Ms Alleya Cheng, Audiologist from Siemens
    • Open to Public (Attending talks will need advanced telephone bookings as seats are limited)
    • A Health Carnival featuring not just hearing related issues, but also Chiropractic screening, FOBT test for Colorectal Cancer and Photo booth.
    • A Lucky Draw for attendees
    • And special promotional prices for hearing aids up to 15% off!

    Event Details:

    • 11am - 12pm - Talk by Dr Barrie Tan
    • 12pm - 3pm - Lunch, Games and Lucky Draw 1
    • 3pm - 4pm - Talk by Ms Alleya Cheng
    • 4pm  - Lucky draw 2
    • End of event

    About The Hearing Solution Company Pte Ltd (THSC)

    Established in Asia in 2003 to cater to the needs of the hearing-impaired community. The Hearing Solution Group today is the biggest Hearing Health Care provider in SEA with more than 70 centres in 4 countries. It has its headquarters and flagship centre in Singapore and centres in Malaysia, Indonesia and South Korea.
