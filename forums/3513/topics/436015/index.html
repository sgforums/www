    All in Print China 2011 Show Preview Conference Held in Shanghai

    SHANGHAI, Aug. 11, 2011 /PRNewswire-Asia-AsiaNet/

    The 4th International Exhibition for All China Printing Technology & Equipment 
              (All in Print China 2011), organized by Printing Technology Association of 
              China, China Academy of Printing Technology and Messe Dusseldorf (Shanghai) 
              Co., Ltd., will be held from November 14 to 17, 2011 in Shanghai New 
              International Expo Center. The encouraging news came when, by June 30th, all 
              the booths for All in Print China 2011 had been booked up. 
              
              To enable the industry players to have a clear and better understanding of the 
              highlights of All in Print China 2011, the organizers held a show preview 
              conference in Kerry Hotel, Pudong, Shanghai on August 3rd to unveil the 
              spotlighting details of the exhibition and the exhibitors with their latest 
              exhibits in the conference. The conference attracted over 130 attendees, 
              including representatives of renowned companies in the industry, leaders of 
              printing and packaging associations as well as trade media from the globe.
              
              Industry Summit for Brilliant Printing Equipments and Technologies 
              
              During the conference, representatives of main exhibitors released their latest 
              printing equipment and technologies to be exhibited in All in Print China 2011. 
              In digital and prepress sector, renowned companies such as Kodak, Fujifilm, 
              Fuji Xerox, Konica Minolta, EPSON, EFI, Founder, BASCH, Cron and Konita will 
              participate. Kodak will exhibit inkjet printing systems such as PROSPER 1000, 
              NexpressSX, Prosper S10 and DS5300 intended for solutions such as publishing on 
              demand, digital label, personalized commercial printing and electronic 
              supervision code. As an active response to the theme of Green Printing, 
              Fujifilm will exhibit traditional, digital and environment-friendly solutions. 
              Fuji Xerox will also show their strength in digital printing equipment such as 
              Nuvera288, C1000P, DC8002, C550/560P and DC1450G. Founder will display EagleJet 
              P5000 inkjet printing system, a digital publishing and commercial printing 
              solution worth people's attention. Konica Minolta will participate in All in 
              Print China with its new "bizhub PRESS" digital printing series in 2011. 
              
              In printing equipment sector, global printing giant Komori will exhibit 
              four-color offset press Lithrone-G440+H-UV, innovative UV system "H-UV system" 
              and "offset publishing on demand" solution, as well as next generation tone & 
              alignment management device "PDC-SX". A lot of domestic printing machinery 
              manufacturing companies, such as Shanghai Electric Printing & Packaging 
              Machinery Group, SYNNEX China and Jiangsu Changsheng Group Co., Ltd. will also 
              take part in the fair and promote numerous new products. Shanghai Electric 
              Printing & Packaging Machinery Group will present roll-fed offset printing 
              press, sheet-fed offset press, digital press, publication binding automatic 
              line, automatic hot stamping die-cutter, automatic folder gluer and automatic 
              cutting production line etc; SYNNEX China will bring various new products from 
              its seven subsidiaries to the fair; Beijing Jia He Ding Xin Technology & 
              Business Co., Ltd. will bring offset printing and digital printing equipment 
              such as RYOBI 520GE/GX sexto four-color press, DINGA C6000/C7000 digital color 
              press; Jiangsu Changsheng Group Co., Ltd. will exhibit its new products such as 
              CSS102-4 large four-color offset perfector, CS102-5UL five-color UV polishing 
              offset press and CSS1040B1 large offset perfector to demonstrate the strength 
              of domestic products.
              
              In addition, packaging and post-press company Masterwork Machinery Co., Ltd. 
              will display their multi-position hot stamping solution, automation solution 
              for waste-removing die cutter, digital solution for digital quality testing 
              machine and high speed folding solution for high-speed folder gluer etc. Label 
              and bill printing company Taiyo Kikai will exhibit combined label printing 
              machine and total solution for label printing; EskoArtwork will demonstrate 
              their intelligent packaging process and label processing solution.
              
              AIP 2011 to Present You a Printing Industry Summit in Autumn 
              
              Since its debut in 2003, All in Print China has kept a stable growth for eight 
              years. With organizers' principle of pursuing quality and focusing on service, 
              the trade fair has gained substantial support and great recognition from 
              exhibitors in previous fairs and become one of the best known comprehensive 
              printing technology and machinery exhibition in China. The total exhibition 
              area of All in Print China 2011 is expected to be about 71,000 m2, and the fair 
              will welcome over 600 exhibitors from all over the world and over 100,000 trade 
              visitors. Since its launching, All in Print China 2011 has attracted a lot of 
              applications for exhibition, and the booth area of over 80% of large exhibitors 
              has increased substantially compared with the previous fair. 
              
              Meanwhile, there will be over a dozen of concurrent events and forums, such as 
              China Printing Forum 2011 and "Digital Printing in China" Technology Summit 
              Forum; the organizers will also join efforts with relevant industry 
              organizations to hold various concurrent seminars and events, and offer great 
              support for exhibitors to hold various customer events and press conferences. 
              Apart from continuing set-up of customized special zones covering digital and 
              prepress, CTP, ink, direct mailing and media, in 2011, All in Print China has 
              specially established green printing special zone, education special zone as 
              well as label and bill special zone to offer more featuring spotlights for 
              visitors.
              
              For more information about the trade fair, please visit www.allinprint.com
