    MFG.com Takes Valued Buyer Members on a Manufacturing Tour of Singapore

    SINGAPORE, Dec. 9, 2011 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- MFG.com, the largest online marketplace for the manufacturingindustry is traveling to Singapore with overseas buy-side product manufacturers with intentions of sourcing projects totaling in the hundreds of millions of dollars. The tour is launching today and will entail an exploration into the Asian manufacturing marketplace. This tour will help buy-side product manufacturers discover the many advantages Singapore suppliers provide from a global perspective. Additionally, it will help further the development of the Singapore manufacturers by presenting them face-to-face with European and American buyers who are bringing with them many opportunities from overseas.

    The MFG.com series of Asian sourcing tours have left an unmistakable footprint on the land of Delhi, Mumbai and Bangalore in India as well as Shanghai, Suzhou, Ningbo, Dongguan, Taipei, Taichung, and Kaohsiung in China, connecting buyers with an extensive group of manufactures located in the main Asian industrial markets, and facilitating the perfect match between business partners. MFG.com as the largest online sourcing marketplace for manufacturing has an extensive amount of buy-side product manufacturers for made-to-order parts who are searching for corporations specializing in machining, fabrication, forging, plastic injection molding, and stamping etc. These activities have been very well received by buyer members around the world.

    James Jin, President of MFG.com Asia Pacific as well as the initiator of the Asian manufacturing sourcing tours said, "It is MFG.com's unwavering goal to both rapidly and effectively match buyers with the right suppliers, with the purpose of providing buyers with the best value as well as bringing business opportunities to Asian suppliers. More and more international buyers are starting to pay attention to the Asian manufacturing industry, which is undergoing a rapid escalation and a new economic growth of this emerging and promising market, and Singapore is an integral part."

    Valentin Balter, international sourcing professional for Nextech, which is a world-leading solar energy panel manufacturer, remarked, "By participating in the meetings between buyers and suppliers, I not only have found many surprisingly good and potential suppliers which match perfectly with our machining, fabrication, stamping, and die casting requirements, but they also provided me with a very good venue where I can, as an international sourcing professional, share experiences with and learn from my peers. MFG.com is a fantastic sourcing platform."

    Since the launch of the online sourcing marketplace in 2000, MFG.com has been uniquely focused on the sourcing of made-to-order parts and manufacturing components. It offers an integrated solution to the global manufacturing industry by enabling companies to intelligently connect, source and collaborate on a global basis with transparency and intellectual property protection. This "one-stop" platform offers a win-win situation to both buyers and suppliers by accelerating the sourcing time, improving sourcing quality and saving on costs across the board.
