    -Fast Increasing Tissue Paper Market Activity and Trends in Southeast Asia

    HONG KONG /PRNewswire/ -- The future trends of Southeast Asia are crucial for the global tissue paper industry: where next for a market that has experienced such rapid and aggressive growth?

     
    ASEAN


    With a 2 days conference, 15 exhibiting sponsors and social functions, Tissue World during 28 - 29 October 2015expands its reach to Southeast Asia!

    Access the preliminary agenda at: www.tissueworld.com/twj-preliminary-agenda-day-1/ 

    Amidst the environmental issues plaguing Indonesian tissue manufacturers and the implementation of ASEAN Economic Community 2015, the regional tissue sector has been posed with an opportunity to further expand its reach in Asia and beyond. Hence, anticipating a growing demand for tissue and hygiene products in the region and also for export, the industry is looking up to policy makers for a more beneficial business climate.

    The newly launched Tissue Word Jakarta Conference will bring together industry leaders to discuss crucial environmental and economic factors and how this vastly important and dynamic region will continue to impact the world's tissue industry. The conference will highlight through case studies and panels the importance for regional and global players to commit to forest conservation to remain competitive in the ASEAN. It will also offer participants insight on state-of-the-art technologies that is drawn upon from global leaders to optimize production output whilst remaining energy efficient.

    Registrants will receive a copy of the new series "TW FOCUS: Southeast Asia", a report on the region's tissue industry. To register visit www.tissueworld.com/Jakarta.

    Join the discourse to tackle the biggest topics impacting the region, including:

    • Regional Market Fundamentals: Get the latest analyses, trends and economic indicators for Southeast Asia
    • New Development Strategies and the future for Pulp and Paper industry in Indonesia following the environmental issues it faces
    • Developing Sophisticated Marketing Plans to cultivate green consumption habits among end users
