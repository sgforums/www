CITIC Telecom CPC Reaffirms Industry Leadership with World V

  • Helen.zhang
    15 Jun 11, 12:05

    Award adds to a stellar year in which the company prepares to launch two new SmartCLOUD series solutions and continues expansion in Asia Pacific

    PRNewswire-Asia/ -- CITIC Telecom International CPC Limited ("CITIC Telecom CPC"), a wholly-owned subsidiary of CITIC Telecom International Holdings Limited (SEHK: 1883), reaffirmed its industry leadership by winning the prestigious World Vendor Awards 2011 - Best Hosted Solution. The recognition comes on top of a successful year for the company, which has consolidated its reputation for offering high quality and innovative services in the information and communications technology (ICT) industry.

    The World Vendor Awards, which is organized by Total Telecom, focus on telecommunications industry vendors and use a panel of independent and impartial industry experts to judge entries. The awards give recognition to the true excellence among the companies supplying the hardware, software and services for the telecommunications industry worldwide.

    The Best Hosted Solution award was given to CITIC Telecom CPC's Managed Video Conferencing Service -- VC² (VC-square). The service provides users with flexibility and mobility to access crystal clear video images by enabling HD Personal Telepresence with 3G Broadband Wireless connection. VC² stands out for its high quality, scalable, flexible and efficient HD Managed video conferencing solution that acts as a communication tool and enables business continuity at all times. Very importantly, customers get to enjoy the mentioned benefits at an affordable rate with the managed service model that eliminates the usual high CAPEX required for setting up a video conferencing system, as well as the regular maintenance costs that come with it.

    VC² was first launched in 2009 as the first service of its kind in Asia to adopt H.264/SVC (Scalable Video Coding) video conferencing technology for converged IP networks to provide IP-VPN HD-based Managed Video Conferencing Services. The Best Hosted Solution award further demonstrates the company's proactive approach in harnessing the latest technologies to develop high quality services that deliver efficient, yet affordable and easy-to-manage solutions. With its two flagship services -- TrueCONNECT(TM) MPLS VPN and TrustCSI(TM) Information Security Services, and a series of complementary managed services, CITIC Telecom CPC keeps to its commitment in offering customers with high-quality, seamless ICT solutions to facilitate secure and efficient communications with business partners and customers, at anytime and anywhere.

    The award comes at a strategic time as the company recently announced a name change from CPCNet to CITIC Telecom CPC and is preparing to launch two new solutions in its third flagship series -- SmartCLOUD. Hitting the market in the near future are SmartCLOUD Compute, a revolutionary virtual server solution that goes beyond ordinary offerings to deliver best-in-class high-performance and high-availability with unprecedented value and management ease; and SmartCLOUD Mail, a suite of secure business e-mail management tools with strong mobility.

    "CITIC Telecom CPC has strived to provide its customers with the best in quality and innovation by always anticipating and meeting their changing business needs," said Stephen Ho, CEO of CITIC Telecom CPC. "This has enabled us to keep ahead of market trends -- for example, our SmartCLOUD series will offer our customers a whole new level of flexibility and stability. We are very glad to win the World Vendor Awards and it will encourage us to continue innovating and raising the bar as we widen our service coverage to the Asia Pacific region and beyond."
