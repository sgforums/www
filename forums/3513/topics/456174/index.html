UBM India: Prime Source Forum - Focus on India

  • Helen.zhang
    25 Jul 12, 11:18

    GURGAON, India, July 24, 2012 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- UBM has been organising Prime Source Forum (PSF) in Hong Kong every year since 2006, bringing together more than 400 senior executives from 20+ countries to discuss the challenges and opportunities that manufacturers, suppliers and retailers are facing in the supply chain of the apparel industry. This year, PSF is coming to Gurgaon, India, from 5-7 September, bringing in senior decision makers of sourcing and supply chain from apparel brands, retails and manufacturers to discuss the most relevant challenges of the downstream apparel industry. 

    Aimed at providing the latest updates and solutions to the apparel industry, this conference will offer in-depth discussions on the most critical industry issues including sourcing parameters, quality versus price, creation of agile and responsive supply chains, technical innovations for maintaining quality, packaging and bar-coding, regulatory updates, financial and environmental sustainability in present economic turmoil's, developing human resource and much more. 

    With over 100+ leading decision makers in attendance including Atul Ujakar, Country Director, Nike Sourcing, Luke Banks, B&M Head, Marks & Spencer, Bob Kirke, Executive Director, Canadian Apparel Federation and many more, this conference will provide you with latest and most efficient solutions to enhance your business sustainability whilst meeting the regulatory and safety guidelines. 

    Visit http://www.primesourceforum-india.com/pr to view the complete programme. 

    UBM India is a subsidiary of UBM plc, which is the second largest independent exhibition organiser in the world. It is the largest trade exhibition organiser in India, responsible for 26 exhibitions in different locations across the country. The company is also involved in the organisation of conference programmes throughout India and in the publications of trade journals and magazines. For further details please visit the UBM India website, http://www.ubmindia.in.
