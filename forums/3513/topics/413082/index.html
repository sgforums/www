    Mobil 1 Launches WILL YOU BE THE ONE Contest Offering a Formula Racecar Driving Experience of a Lifetime

     

    2010-10-11 09:00 

     

    Mobil 1 will send 24 winners from across Asia to a Formula racecar driving school for a ride of their lives

        SHANGHAI, Oct. 11 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Mobil 1 raises the bar again when it launches an online contest in Asia to offer motor enthusiasts a thrilling Formula Racecar driving experience. Fans can sign up online athttp://www.mobil1.com.sg to enter the Mobil 1 WILL YOU BE THE ONE contest for a chance to win an opportunity to learn to drive like a professional racer. Twenty-four winners from across Asia will be sent to the Global Racing Schools in Zhuhai, China for a ride of their lives. 

        As the world's leading synthetic engine oil driven by winners, Mobil 1 brings fans closer to the action by allowing them to participate in first-hand training required for exceptional high performance on the racetrack. "Through the Mobil 1 WILL YOU BE THE ONE contest, we are excited to bond together a group of Formula racecar fanatics and enable them to experience professional training for advanced motorsports skills," says Mr. Eric Carmichael, Asia Pacific Marketing Manager, ExxonMobil Lubricants and Specialties Company.

        24 potential winners stand a chance for the opportunity of a lifetime to train with Formula racecar professionals and share their passion with fellow motor enthusiasts from across Asia. As a team, they will receive professional training for motor-sports skills such as advance defensive skills, prevision driving skills, circuit driving and drifting. Contest winners will also hit the tracks to experience adrenalin pumping racecar performance with 0 to 100km/hour in under 5 seconds and reach top speeds of 235km/h in Formula Racecars.

        As the world's leading synthetic engine oil, Mobil 1 is the choice of champions and provides all-round winning performance on track and on road. Just ask Jenson Button and Lewis Hamilton (FIA Formula One(TM) World Champions), our leading ambassadors who constantly rely on Mobil 1 to keep them and their cars in a winning position.

        Contest winners will have the chance of a lifetime to experience Mobil 1's synthetic engine oil technology on the tracks like world champions. They will also have the extraordinary chance to bond and share their experience with their own team of motor enthusiast.

        The chance of a lifetime starts September 15, 2010 at 12:00 am (GMT) and closes on December 31, 2010 at 11:59 pm (GMT). Once registered, contestants earn points by referring friends and participating on weekly online quizzes to register online at http://www.mobil1.com.sg . Mobil 1 will be sending 24 winners with the most points for this 3-day action packed adventure in Manila.
