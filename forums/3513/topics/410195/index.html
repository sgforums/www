Asia's Best Lifestyle Design Event in Bangkok,Thailand

  • Helen.zhang
    6 Sep 10, 14:28


    BANGKOK, Sept. 2 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- The Bangkok International Gift Fair and Bangkok International Houseware Fair (BIG+BIH), the region's favorite duo design event, will be held for the 30th time at the design hub of Asia, under the theme Soul and Style of the Orient, which will highlight Oriental essence with a twist.

    Organized by the Department of Export Promotion, in conjunction with Thai Lifestyle Product Federation, BIG+BIH fairs have become the main stage to showcase the niches of the Thai lifestyle product industry, which are design, quality, and business integrity, to the world. The fairs are known as the milestone marker of the industry.

    The consistent success of BIG+BIH is the result of the cooperation between both private and public sectors with the aim to boost the export of lifestyle products from Thailand. The event in October is expected to generate over US$2.6 billion revenue for the industry.

    This October event will stage the contemporary impression of Eastern characters, delicate craftsmanship, exotic materials, and lasting quality that meets international standards and is environmentally friendly. Highlighted products are gifts, premiums, home decorative, artificial flowers and plants, handicrafts, household items, spa products, home textiles, toys & games, stationery, and other design objects.

    In addition to experiencing the unique products and fresh ideas at BIG+BIH fairs, the multinational visionary buyers are invited to enjoy the exclusive privileges from the moment they land at the Bangkok international airport. Offered and sponsored by the supporters of the BIG+BIH fairs, buyers can enjoy a long list of perks on accommodation, dining, and recreation facilities.

    BIG+BIH October 2010 will be held at the Bangkok International Trade and Exhibition Center (BITEC), which is situated in a short driving distance from Suvarnabhumi airport. Traders are welcome to the event for business negotiations during 19-22 October between 10am and 6pm. The event is open to general public on 23-24 October between 10am and 9pm. Admission is free. Trade visitors are encouraged to pre-register at www.bigandbih.com.
