    The 7th Annual Event Private Equity Southeast Asia Congress Returns 

    SINGAPORE, Dec. 5, 2011 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Following the hugely successful event held this year, the leaders in Southeast Asia's private equity sector can look forward to the region's primary networking conference in Singapore from 6-7 March 2012. The event will be a major opportunity for Limited Partners and General Partners looking to make private equity, venture capital and angel investments. 

    Eugene Lai, a Partner at Southern Capital Group, reveals that relationships are very important in Southeast Asia and strong relationships will give a firm a competitive advantage. ". It is not difficult to get to know people including intermediaries, but it takes a long time to develop strong relationships," he said. 

    Join Eugene and over 150 of the region's leading CEO's, chairmen and business owners focused on buyouts, growth capital, distressed asset and venture capital investment at Private Equity Southeast Asia. The conference will highlight to you the top opportunities in Indonesia, Vietnam, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand and the rest of Southeast Asia and feature over 40 speakers. 

    The conference is dedicated to providing the Southeast Asia Private Equity sector with the definitive forum to network and learn. It will enable attendees to understand how they can capitalise on the buoyant PE market in South East Asia and establish local connections within Southeast Asia's growing markets. 

    Private Equity Southeast Asia will facilitate entry and growth into the regions hottest sectors and industries looking for investors. Delegates will meet institutional LPs waiting to capitalise on rapidly emerging private equity investment markets in the region, and develop a deeper understanding of Southeast Asia's often complex GP-LP and GP-investee company relationships to warrant greater success in deal sourcing, structuring and fundraising.
