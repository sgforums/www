    NEW YORK, March 5, 2012 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom LLP issues the following statement in connection with an accidental 8-K filing made on behalf of client Wynn Resorts Ltd:

    "We learned earlier today that a clerk in our filing department inadvertently made an unauthorized filing with respect to Wynn Resorts Ltd. We apologize that this mistake occurred. We have taken steps to rectify the situation as quickly as possible and are reviewing what occurred to ensure that it cannot happen in the future."
