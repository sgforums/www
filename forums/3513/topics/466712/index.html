DoctorPage Strengthens Market Leader Position in Singapore:

  • Helen.zhang
    7 Mar 13, 12:59
    DoctorPage Strengthens Market Leader Position in Singapore: 1,500 Bookable Doctors and Dentists Online SINGAPORE, March 6, 2013 /PRNewswire/ -- DoctorPage, http://doctorpage.sg, the healthcare portal that facilitates online medical appointment bookings has just announced the onboarding of 1,500 doctors and dentists onto its Singapore-based platform, firmly entrenching its position as the largest and most popular healthcare community in Singapore. Barely five months after its launch, DoctorPage is already holding a database of more than 12,500 healthcare professionals on the portal. With the onboarding of 1,500 bookable doctors and dentists onto its platform, DoctorPage now offers users a wider selection of medical providers to choose and book appointments from. The team at DoctorPage utilizes its online marketing and technology expertise to help doctors and dentists greatly improve their online visibility. By being part of Singapore's largest healthcare community, DoctorPage provides a personalized web profile for every healthcare professional to detail his/her qualifications, professional affiliations and professionally taken photographs. In addition, DoctorPage also provides doctors and dentists an opportunity to gain access to a database of more than 300,000 engaged users facilitated through DoctorPage's cross-platform marketing services. "We are pleased to announce that DoctorPage now offers 1,500 bookable doctors and dentists, making DoctorPage the preferred healthcare community for healthcare professionals in Singapore," said Mr. Scheichenost, CEO at DoctorPage. "This milestone is a result of the management's strong focus towards execution, coupled with a long term strategy of continuous product and service improvements for doctors." With a firm foothold as the market leader in Singapore, DoctorPage looks set to bring its innovative service to doctors and dentists in the Asia-pacific region. About DoctorPage DoctorPage was founded in June 2012 in Singapore by Max-F. Scheichenost, Chief Executive Officer (CEO); Dr Dawn Soo, Co-Founder and Chief Information Officer (CIO); and Daniel Endres, Chief Operating Officer (COO). It aims to improve healthcare access across Asia by providing a user-friendly platform that enables patients to search and book appointments with their preferred healthcare providers in a few simple clicks. A complementary customer relationship management system (CRM) is provided to the healthcare professionals to help optimize patient flow and clinic efficiency. DoctorPage recently released a new version of its iOS and iPad app which is available for download on iTunes and is currently working on its Android app. Mr. Scheichenost was the co-founder of DailyDeal (acquired by Google Inc. in September 2011), and Founder and Managing Partner of Alps Ventures, an Austrian-based investment company. Dr Soo is a medical physician by training with clinical experience in both the public and private healthcare sectors in Singapore. In addition she holds an MBA from top-tier business school, INSEAD. Mr. Endres was the Entrepreneur-in-Residence at DailyDeal and Operations Manager at home24 in Berlin.
