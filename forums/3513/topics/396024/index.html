    SEOUL, April 7 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- MASTERIMAGE 3D (http://www.masterimage3D.com , CEO: Younghoon Lee), a 3D technology specialist, announced that it will participate in C3D World (China international Forum & Exhibition) from April 8th to 11th in China.

    (Photo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2010/04/07/20100407564977.html )

    In the exhibition, MASTERIMAGE 3D will put on display its Digital 3D Cinema System (MI-2100), already in use in about 1,500 movie theatres in 30 countries around the world, and a Reusable 3D Eyewear (MI-G200).

    The exhibition will also have Autostereoscopic 3D LCD, a Glasses Free 3D image display panel, and Hitachi's 3D Phone Wooo H0001 Phone, the first ever mass-produced product (3D phone) using this panel.

    A representative of MASTERIMAGE 3D said, "for the last years, MasterImage3D are growing at remarkable speed in 3D display market, with our MI-2100 and Autostereoscopic 3D LCD. Not only in C3D World, we will also participate in NAB (National Association of Broadcasters) Show, the world's largest electronic media show in Las Vegas from April 10th in order to let the worldwide market know our technical superiority."

    You can experience various products from MASTERIMAGE 3D at C3D World '3D Interactive Zone' Booth# 2D003 (http://www.c3dworld.org ).
