Countdown to Tissue World's Sao Paulo Launch Next Week

  • Helen.zhang
    15 May 15, 14:44

    HONG KONG /PRNewswire/ -- The Tissue World team is making the final touches to welcome the industry to our debut launch in Latin America!

     

     

     
    Sao Paulo

    The first edition of Tissue World Sao Paulo -- the gateway to the tissue industry in Brazil and South America -- will get underway at the Transamerica Expo Center in São Paulo from 20-22 May.

    The tissue industry will gather to meet and mingle with key decision makers to get the latest on this vibrant and dynamic region.

    Visitors will meet the whole supply chain, from pulp production in which Brazil is particularly strong, to tissue makers, suppliers and distributors.

    We are proud to announce the many high profile companies that have registered to attend, including CMPC Celulosa, Perini, Eldorado Brasil Celulose, Kimberly-Clark Brasil, Valmet, Fibria Celulose, and many more!

    To view the list of attendees so far go to: http://www.tissueworld.com/twsp-selection-of-attendees/

    In addition to the exhibition hall, visitors can keep pace with pressures from customers, regulators and advocacy groups by joining the discussion on the hot topics debated at the conference.

    The final conference programme includes talks from the Bank of America Merrill Lynch's Thiago Lofiego, Klabin'sFrancisco Razzolini, Huntsfield Brasil Consultancy's Geraldo Ferreira, Euromonitor's Leonardo Freitas, CMPC Tissue Puente Alto's Armando Vera, WWF Chile's Cecilia Alcoreza, and more.

    Topics include: Global pulp market and supply/demand trends; Brazil's tissue market opportunity; engaging in the world production chain; key trends and opportunities in the expanding Brazilian tissue and hygiene market.

    For the final conference programme go to: http://www.tissueworld.com/tissue-world-sao-paulo-2015-conference/

    To register as a visitor is free of charge, go to: http://www.tissueworld.com/tissue-world-sao-paulo-conference-registration-landing-page/

    For more information please visit www.tissueworld.com/SaoPaulo

    Tissue World is the leading global event series serving the tissue industry worldwide since 1993. An integrated and intertwined platform consisting of exhibitions, conferences and a magazine providing an unmatched offline and online meeting place to do business, exchange ideas and learn, all year round.
