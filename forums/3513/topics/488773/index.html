    HONG KONG and SINGAPORE /PRNewswire/ -- S&P Capital IQ®, a business unit of McGraw Hill Financial (NYSE:MHFI), today announced the launch of IRFacts, an integrated data, analytics and market research platform, created in partnership with the Singapore Exchange (SGX), for IR professionals.

    Developed specifically for companies listed on the SGX, IRFacts offers an integrated approach to information aggregation and offers IR professionals the ability to monitor market trends, the most recent consensus and detailed estimates, news and key developments, and target investors on one platform.

    "IRFacts is a user-friendly platform built for the Investor Relations officers and management reporting," said Jose Ordonez, Managing Director, S&P Capital IQ. "Our market data and aftermarket research assists IROs in presenting their pitch in a clear, transparent manner to broader regional and global investor audiences, as well as offering tools to simplify benchmarking against their peers."

    "We continuously work to set standards that help to improve the quality and liquidity of shares listed on the Singapore Exchange," said Mita Natarajan, Senior Vice President, Head of Corporate Services, Listings, Singapore Exchange. "IRFacts is an incredible tool which will help companies improve and sustain higher levels of transparency among a global investor audience, which in turn will improve their liquidity in the long term."

    Providing market data and aftermarket research across an array of companies, industries and market, IRFacts is a fully customizable platform that covers 18,000 active companies in over 100 countries and covers over 5,700 indices.

    To find out more about IRFacts, contact Listing Corporate Services at tel: +65-6713-7257 or email:listings@sgx.com.
