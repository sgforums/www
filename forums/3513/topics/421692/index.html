    Host Fun Meetings at Langham Place, Beijing Capital Airport and Win an iPad


    BEIJING, Jan. 31, 2011 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Who says people can't mix meetings with pleasure? Langham Place, Beijing Capital Airport proves people can!  As one of Beijing's top MICE masters, the hotel brings the best out of a team by combining bright, stylish and thoughtfully-equipped venues with a series of innovative and fun activities.

    What's more, Langham Place, Beijing Capital Airport is offering meeting planners an opportunity to win a 32G Wi-Fi Apple iPad when booking events.

    COOK LIKE A LOCAL

    It takes real technique and skill to handle the heat in the kitchen and create dishes with the wow factor. That's why Langham Place's chef can build a team into an army of gourmet stars! From selecting the freshest seasonal ingredients at a Beijing food market to putting the finishing touches to a beautifully-presented dish, their chef's brigade will teach the team what it takes to be masters of local flavour, as well as the importance of working together to create perfection.

    This half-day team-building cooking class program is ideal for groups of between 10 to 40 persons, and is charged at RMB300 per person.

    THE AMAZING RACE LANGHAM PLACE

    Race around Langham Place, Beijing Capital Airport and experience the thrill of the hit reality game show The Amazing Race!  The Amazing Race Langham Place will put teamwork to the test and will surely bring out the competitive spirit in all, not to mention some funny memories.

    MEET ME OUTDOORS

    The gorgeous lakeside park at the hotel's doorstep provides a unique venue for meetings in the refreshing outdoors. Whether for welcoming parties, team-building or afternoon picnics, Langham Place, Beijing Capital Airport will put participants in the scenic picture to take full advantage of Mother Nature.

    HIGH-POWERED TAKE OFF

    Meeting planners are spoilt for choice with 22 meeting rooms including the Capital Ballroom offering over 2,700 sq metres of flexible technologically-advanced function space filled with natural light.

    Full-day fun-filled-meeting packages are priced at RMB470 per person while half days is just RMB390 per person.

    GIVE ME AN IPAD

    Meanwhile, until 28 February 2011, meeting planners booking several events and meetings will have a chance to win a 32G Wi-Fi Apple iPad!
