ChinaScope Announces Additional Investment from Moody's Corp

  • Helen.zhang
    8 May 12, 17:25

    SHANGHAI, May 7, 2012 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- ChinaScope Financial ( 

    www.chinascopefinancial.com), an integrated provider of China-focused analytics and business intelligence, today announced that Moody's Corporation has increased its investment in ChinaScope, giving it a 25% stake in the company. 

    The additional investment follows Moody's acquisition of a 16.7% ownership stake in ChinaScope in September 2011 and underscores the commitment of both companies to produce and deliver high-quality China-focused data and analytics to a global client base. 

    About ChinaScope Financial 

    Founded in 2009, ChinaScope is the financial data and analytics platform for global financial professionals with critical quantitative and qualitative information on China. ChinaScope's platform covers raw and processed data and information on companies, industries and the macro economy. ChinaScope helps clients gather and analyze information on China so they can work better, smarter and faster.
