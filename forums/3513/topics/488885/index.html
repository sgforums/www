Little India Dentist Dental Seeks to Provide Good and....

    SINGAPORE /PRNewswire/ -- A family dental clinic conveniently located on Serangoon Road, and easily accessible from Farrer Park, Dhoby Ghaut and Bugis, among other places, the Little India Dentist Dental clinic, staffed by dentists who are registered with the Singapore Dental Council, has been in operation for the past 10 months. The clinic seeks to broaden its outreach in an effort to serve more Singaporeans with good dental care.

    Situated in the heart of Little India, the clinic is equipped with the latest technology and equipment available in dentistry. As a family oriented dental clinic, the Little India Dentist offers a wide range of services, including cosmetic dentistry (which includes teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, orthodontics and implants, among others), kids dentistry, all-on-4 implants, wisdom tooth extraction, dental implants, teeth whitening, teeth straightening through Invisalign & CFast (Cosmetic Tooth Alignment process), porcelain veneers and dental crowns, and root canals. In particular, Little India Dentist is one of two dental clinics in Singapore that offer CFast, a new and advanced dental technique. CFast is not only a faster procedure than the more traditional Invisalign, but is also a more economical alternative. Little India Dentist also offers Zoom Teeth Whitening - one of the fastest options for teeth whitening on the market.

    The dentists who work at Little India Dentist are Dr. Nivedita Seerpi, Dr. Mansoor Walipoor, Dr. Yushmee Ramburrun, and Dr. Katy Kennedy. The dentists at the Little India Dentist Dental clinic learnt their trade in many different parts of the world, and every dentist is registered with the Singapore Dental Council.

    Providing affordable dental care is a goal of Little India Dentist. It is a CHAS clinic, and offers additional subsidies to Pioneer Generation-eligible residents. For younger patients, Little India Dentist is also a Baby Bonus Approved Institution. Little India Dentist will provide subsidized basic dental care for the holders of these cards. Little India Dentist is also Medisave Accredited for day surgery procedures, including procedures such as as wisdom tooth extraction and dental implants.

    Little India Dentist is open every day of the week. Same day appointments are available in cases of dental emergencies.
