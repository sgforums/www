    The Medical City Improves Patient-Centric Care Through Partnership with Orion Health

    SINGAPORE /PRNewswire/ -- Orion Health -- an independently owned eHealth software company delivering interoperable solutions for healthcare facilities, organisations and regions -- has partnered with The Medical City (TMC) to improve patient-centric care through the implementation of Orion's solutions amongst the TMC enterprise.

    Supporting the TMC launch across the Philippines, the strongly integrated Orion-TMC Go Live support team proactively provided on-site support for hospital staff with flexible technology infrastructure that provides powerful information sharing capabilities across multiple healthcare facilities. In partnership with TMC, Orion provides its Hospital Integration System (HIS) and Clinical Portal solution to hospital staff for production use across multiple TMC sites.

    Accomplished in less than six months, Orion's rapid implementation of technology enabled the roll-out to take place at ground-breaking speed. As of today, TMC Clark and TMC Iloilo have successfully integrated Orion's solutions into their systems and have found great success with many patient visits processed and improvements evident in clinical efficiency.

    "Every year, 400,000 patients pass through TMC seeking treatment and rehabilitation. The care that patients receive is our highest priority, and with such a partnership with Orion, we are now better equipped to deliver top-notch care for them. As of today, numerous patients have been a beneficiary of our collaboration with Orion, and we look forward to more opportunities to improve our existing infrastructure for their benefit", said Dr. Alfredo Bengzon, President and Chief Executive Officer of TMC.

    Testament to their multi-site functionality, Orion has capabilities to support multiple organisation units of a single entity, enabling rapid and reliable sharing of information between disparate health software systems and organisations.

    "Orion has expertise in delivering interoperable solutions for the healthcare industry, and is committed to establishing a presence in the region for the sharing of best practices to make a difference in the people that matter the most – our patients. Large hospital systems like The Medical City reach numerous patients every year, and Orion is honoured to be their partner to fill the gaps and bring advanced healthcare solutions to people all over the Philippines," said Ian McCrae, Chief Executive Officer of Orion Health.

    The Orion-TMC Go Live project not only sees impact in numerous patients' lives, but also in the day-to-day jobs for healthcare workers. Connecting doctors, nurses, and administrators across the network through access to a single system will result in a more streamlined patient journey and will ultimately enable better quality health services. Since June 2014, Orion has fostered strong ties amongst their Professional Services Group (PSG) and Client Support Services teams, enabling cross-functional, and cross-geography work integration in the Philippines. PSG teams in the region have lent their expertise when leading an intensive knowledge transfer program while working very closely with the team in seeing through TMC Go Live. Orion's regional presence is also evident as their Bangkok and New Zealand teams provide support in planning and deploying solutions in record-breaking time.
