    SINGAPORE, Sept. 10, 2012 /PRNewswire/ -- Montigo Resorts Nongsa plots the perfect escapade for adventure travelers and holiday makers with the Montigo Escape Package. Guests may rekindle romance or create lasting memories with friends and family as they sail on a sunset cruise or indulge in a spa treatment.

    Specially designed as a quick but elaborate way to escape daily routine and rejuvenate the body and mind, the Montigo Escape Package is priced at $333 SGD per person per night with the following privileges:

    -- Exclusive sunset dinner cruise with a dedicated chef for four guests

    -- 60-minute pampering Montigo massage session per guest

    -- Complimentary upgrade to Hilltop Pool villa, subject to availability

    -- Daily American breakfast set

    -- Complimentary mini-bar selections and snacks replenished once daily

    -- Return yacht transfers and return land transfers at Nongsa

    The Montigo Escape package is valid from now till 30 November 2012 with a minimum stay of two consecutive nights for four persons. For more information, please call Montigo Resorts Nongsa at +65-6533-7337 or log-on to montigoresorts.com.

    About Montigo Resorts Nongsa

    Montigo Resorts Nongsa is a sprawling 12-hectare property located on the exclusive north-east shore of Batam Island, Indonesia. Just a 30-minute boat ride from Singapore, the resort boasts 88 contemporary villas, each with two bedrooms, a plunge pool, private dining area, a sky lounge and spectacular views of the South China Sea. The award-winning* resort will be the first under the Montigo Resorts Brand. Opening rates begin at $350 SGD, subject to service charge and prevailing government taxes. Montigo Resorts is operated by KOP Hotels & Resorts, a member of KOP Group. For more information, please visit www.montigoresorts.com.
