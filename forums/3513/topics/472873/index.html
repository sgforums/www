    A team of marketing and design professionals are announcing the launch of a new marketing and visual communications firm in Singapore. Vision Media Marketing is the brainchild of Kelvin Ling, an entrepreneur with a strong background in marketing.

    “When I just started pursuing my entrepreneur dreams, I realised there were too many obstacles in setting up. Specifically, the pricing charged by design firms for logo, name card and marketing collaterals designs were exorbitant. Vision Media Marketing was set up to solve these pain points for both new and existing businesses. We eliminate all cost which do not provide value, and channel the savings back to our customers,” said Vision Media Marketing Principal Consultant, Mr Kelvin Ling.

    Vision Media Marketing was formed to provide innovative ideas and designs at a very low cost. Terming themselves as Singapore’s lowest costing marketing & visual communications firm, the company aims to allow all businesses which require design work a cost effective option, without sacrificing any design quality. The team consist of fiercely passionate creative designers with over 60 years of experiences combined.

    “I have had many friends & associates from various industries who have told how difficult it is to find talented graphic designers to work at their companies. For those who do not require a full time designer, they have lamented at how expensive freelancers are changing them,” said Miss Angie Gan, Deputy Operations Director of Vision Media Marketing.  

    With Vision Media Marketing, businesses can now utilize the firm’s expertise in creating print advertisement, signboards, banners, corporate identification, flyers & various other marketing collaterals. How low is the pricing exactly? The typical corporate logo will cost about $500 at most design firms. At Vision Media Marketing, it will only cost $105.

     

    Interested businesses and individuals can get in touch with Vision Media via http://visionmediamkt.wix.com/ideas .
