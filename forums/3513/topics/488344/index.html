ONE15 Marina Club Launches All-New Ethnic Wedding Packages..

  • Helen.zhang
    15 May 15, 12:01

    SINGAPORE /PRNewswire/ -- On 17 May 2015 (Sunday), 1pm - 5pm, ONE15 Marina Club will be premiering its Malay Wedding Show titled Mahligai Idaman di ONE15, or Your Dream Wedding at ONE15. Designed to be glamorous, inspiring and specially tailored for the Malay ethnic group, the event will feature the launch of the Club's all-new Ethnic Wedding Packages, a Bridal Fashion Show by Chantique the Bridal Gallery, Yacht Charter Talk by ONE15 Luxury Yachting, a special Tea Reception and a Lucky Draw where couples-to-be can stand to win attractive prizes. Admission is priced at SGD$28 nett per couple.

    In a bid to offer more comprehensive wedding packages for its guest, Asia's finest marina club, ONE15 Marina Club, extended their wedding services to include a series of ethnic wedding packages, which consists of culturally-inspired decorations and delicacies from a variety of ethnic cuisines amongst other privileges. Providing the consumer with more choices, this move was made right after the Club achieved an official stamp of endorsement by MUIS, certifying one of its kitchens to prepare and serve Halal food.

    "Our presence in the Mega Wedding & Lifestyle and Exotica Indonesia exhibition, a wedding show focusing on the Malay ethnic group, earlier this year as well as our feature on Berita Harian, has increased Malay couples-to-bes' interests in using our Club as their wedding venue. Additionally, the Club is always striving to provide the most impeccable and personable service; thus, we are proud to be able to finally offer certified-halal wedding packages to our Muslim guests," said Mr Brian E. Werner, General Manager of ONE15 Marina Club, "On top of that, we will also be offering halal dining options for events and meetings held at the Club."

    Apart from Malay Wedding Packages, ONE15 Marina Club also provides an array of choices for other wedding couples including -- Indian, Chinese, Western, and International Wedding Packages; all of which inclusive of unlimited serving of soft drinks, tea and coffee throughout the banquet, selection of wedding favours for confirmed guests and a complimentary one-night stay at the Club's bridal room amongst other perks. Each package is also specially crafted to suit the needs of the different ethnic groups and to provide a truly unforgettable wedding for the couple and their guests. The Club also has a comprehensive range of solemnisation packages.

    Mahligai Idaman di ONE15, in which the ethnic wedding packages will be launched, is set to take place at the Club's Constellation Ballroom; a 360-seater pillar-less grand ballroom featuring state-of-the-art sound and lighting systems. Presenting Show guests with a truly congenial and immersive experience, couples-to-be will be treated with complimentary shuttle services from HarbourFront, a visual feast with the bridal fashion showcase by Chantique the Bridal Gallery, a gastronomical experience with the Tea Reception, valuable advice by the Club's Wedding Specialists and a tour of the Club's wedding and solemnization venues on request. For couples seeking a more unique and contemporary affair, the talk by ONE15 Luxury Yachting aims to provide couples with yacht charter options to exchange their vows with the open sea as their backdrop.

    "We believe that every wedding deserves an extraordinary setting to create memories to last a lifetime which is why we have created an event that specialises in providing couples with the options to do just that. Couples planning to tie the knot soon can also take this opportunity to check out our onsite wedding promotions," shared Ms Sandy Sim, Director of Catering Sales at ONE15 Marina Club.

    Interested couples can register at +65-6305-6990 or email weddings@one15marina.com.

    About ONE15 Marina Club

    Since its inauguration in 2007, ONE15 Marina Club is the winner of several international accolades including the Outstanding Hospitality Award at the Helm Magazine Singapore Yacht Show Awards 2013, Asian Marina of the Year consecutively from 2012 - 2014, Best Asian Marina/Best Yachting Club 2009 and the prestigious FIVE Gold Anchor Award. The Club also has the honour of hosting upscale international yachting events including the Clipper 2013 - 14 Round the World Yacht Race, the Volvo Ocean Race 2008/09, Asia Superyacht Conference 2009 - 2011, Asia Pacific Superyacht Conference 2012 and 2013, the Asia Pacific Yachting Conference 2014 -2015 and the Singapore Yacht Show 2011 - 2015. Located in Sentosa Cove, the prime leisure district ofSingapore, ONE15 Marina Club features a beautiful marina of 270 berths with facilities to accommodate megayachts of up to 200 feet. The well-appointed clubhouse encompasses world-class facilities including fine restaurants and bars, luxurious suite rooms, a modern spa, a well-equipped gymnasium, members' and kids' lounges as well as conference and meeting rooms. Evoking the glamour and elegance of Monte Carlo, ONE15Marina Club is Asia's most desired waterfront leisure destination.
