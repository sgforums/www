Doxee Adds GEOCOM to its Growing List of Business Partners i

  • Helen.zhang
    26 Aug 15, 17:08
    -GEOCOM to complement its IT services portfolio with Doxee Platform CCM offering

    SAN JOSE, Calif. and MONTEVIDEO, Uruguay  /PRNewswire/ -- Doxee, leader in cloud based enterprise-class Customer Communication Management (CCM), announced today a new partnership withGEOCOM Uruguay to sell and support the Doxee platform to enterprise accounts in Uruguay.

    (Logo: http://photos.prnewswire.com/prnh/20150325/736010-a )

    GEOCOM is the Uruguayan leader in information technology solutions and cloud services and the NAP (Network Access Point) for the country. GEOCOM manages the infrastructure of private and public enterprises and many large customers host their data in GEOCOM's data centers.

    "GEOCOM is a strong IT service provider with cloud expertise and a well-established sales and support organization in their local country," said Doxee CEO Sergio Muratori Casali. "We expect this partnership to increase GEOCOM's revenue from existing and new customers in the region."

    The new alliance will expand their current CCM offering in the Uruguayan market from printed communications to interactive services. GEOCOM will complement their present cloud services with eDox Interactive Communications and Personalized Video. The company plans to promote the Doxee service offering to its many public sector customers, and expand to utilities, banking and insurance market segments.

    "Doxee is the perfect complement to our current CCM service offering for large enterprises," said Jorge Baptista, VP. "Many of our enterprise customers are moving to electronic communication. By adding Doxee's cloud-based platform with eDox Interactive Communications and Personalized Video solutions to our managed service portfolio, we can meet our customers' need for less expensive, faster and more effective communications. Commensurate with our world-class IT capabilities and data centers where high quality is our differentiator, the Doxee platform clearly positions us as the leader in CCM services in Uruguay."

    About Doxee 

    Doxee is a leading global technology and services provider of enterprise-class, cloud-based CCM. Enterprises worldwide use Doxee products and services to deliver highly personalized and interactive customer communications to their clients.

    About GEOCOM 

    GEOCOM Uruguay SA provides global solutions, with certified quality, applying state of the art IT technologies that are best suited to the demands and needs of its customers with the ongoing goal of exceeding their expectations. GEOCOM has local presence in Uruguay, Chile, Colombia and Peru.
