    Mavenir Offers CSFB Solution Helping Global Operators Launch New Apple and LTE Devices

    Enables Voice and SMS services to 4G LTE smartphones, such as iPhone 5, without impacting MSC core

    RICHARDSON, Texas, Sept. 25, 2012 /PRNewswire/ -- Mavenir Systems, a leading innovator of mobile infrastructure solutions for 4G Long Term Evolution (LTE) operators, enables the rapid introduction of Voice and SMS services with its Circuit Switch Fallback (CSFB) solution that helps operators launch CSFB independent of their MSC.  Mavenir already has several live CSFB deployments with tier 1 operators in multiple countries. Several more are expected to go live in 2012.

    Circuit Switch Fallback (CSFB) enables delivery of circuit-switched voice services to LTE handsets by triggering "fall back" to the 3G network to complete voice calls. With CSFB, operators can immediately support new LTE-enabled smartphones, such as the newly announced iPhone 5, for voice and SMS in advance of IMS-based VoLTE solutions that won't be widely available until 2014.

    While CSFB is key to enabling the introduction of 4G LTE smartphones, the technology itself has the drawback of perpetuating investments in legacy 3G core infrastructure. Mavenir's unique solution overlays the legacy infrastructure and allows operators to focus their CAPEX investments on next generation 4G technology with a CSFB solution that can be launched within 60 days.

    "Our value proposition is combining proven CSFB technology with our speed to market and the ease of integrating into 4G LTE networks." said Pardeep Kohli, president and chief executive officer of Mavenir Systems. "Our IWF CSFB function helps the network economics of introducing new LTE services quickly and cost effectively."

    Mavenir is focused on helping operators around the world with their network transformation to all IP and their introduction of new advanced services, quickly and efficiently.

    Added Kohli, "This is the first step for many operators, but they can easily leverage this Mavenir CSFB investment along the path to SRVCC and VoLTE."

    For information on how Mavenir can deliver CSFB in 60 days, contact us.   

    About Mavenir Systems:

    Mavenir delivers Converged Voice, Video and Messaging solutions, based on the mOne® Convergence Platform, to wireless operators globally.  Mavenir's value-added IMS and cloud-based solutions enable the transformation of legacy core networks by offering compelling new services such as VoLTE and RCS, and by providing uniquely innovative solutions to address the challenge of migrating subscribers and services to 4G.
