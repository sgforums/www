Hong Kong Social Media Week 2012 -- Harbour City Hosts Panel

  • Helen.zhang
    6 Feb 12, 16:43

    HONG KONG, Feb. 6, 2012 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Social Media Week is a multi-city global conference connecting people, content and conversations around emerging trends in social and mobile media. Hong Kong is one of the 12 cities participating in this global platform, including Hamburg, London, Miami, New York, Paris, San Francisco, Sao Paulo, Singapore, Tokyo, Toronto and Washington DC.

    (Logo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2009/09/09/20090909984310.jpg )
    (Logo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2012/02/06/20120206114508732510-l.jpg )

    The mission of Social Media Week Hong Kong is to open up conversation about social media in Asia Pacific, with a focus on Hong Kong and mainland China, through a series of creative events that connect brands, marketers, media, and the public unique venues across the city. Social Media Week 2012 marks the second year the week-long event that has been held in the city, which last year brought together 2,300 attendees across 38 events with online conversation generating over 400,000 tweets.

    Social Media Week will be taking place in Hong Kong from 13th - 17th February 2012. It is hosted by City Partner Cohn&Wolfe - impactasia alongside a team of advisors. As the largest and most social media active shopping mall in Hong Kong to date with the most diverse offerings, Harbour City is one of the official partners and advisory board members who is hosting 4 different events during Social Media Week 2012, with the topics of Food & Beverage, Travel, Fashion & Beauty as well as Technology.

    Social Media Week Hong Kong (SMWHK) is now open for online registration. For more information, please visit
    http://socialmediaweek.org/hongkong.

    About Social Media Week

    Social Media Week (http://socialmediaweek.org) is one of the world's most unique conferences, providing global perspectives on emerging trends in social and mobile media. In 2011, the conference was hosted in eleven cities worldwide and attracted more than 18,000 attendees with hundreds of thousands connecting online and through mobile media. Currently city partners and their local advisory boards are creating programs which collectively will consist of more than 500 free to attend events worldwide this February 2012. Social Media Week is owned and operated by Crowdcentric Media LLC.
