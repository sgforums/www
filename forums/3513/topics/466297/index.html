Torchlight II's Asian Version to Be Launched in Asia Pacific

  • Helen.zhang
    26 Feb 13, 12:21
    BEIJING, Feb. 25, 2013 /PRNewswire/ -- The Asian version of Torchlight II, researched and developed by Perfect World's subsidiary Runic Games, will soon be launched. (Logo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2009/04/16/200904161852.jpg ) (Photo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2013/02/25/201302251255031070.html ) Torchlight II is a pay-to-install action PRG game developed by Perfect World's subsidiary Runic Games. As a part of the Torchlight game series, Torchlight II not only maintains the same unique style and all of the exciting action-packed features of its predecessor, but also expands the global footprint of the game while adding new feature content, including multi-player cooperation through the web or a LAN. Torchlight II's creative designs of monsters and treasure troves, the opportunity to apply a substantially wider range of skill sets, plus the novel experience of the random elements, vastly extend the universe of player groups. Torchlight II has been enthusiastically received by players across North America since its launch in the region. Shortly after launch, Torchlight II was selected as the Role-playing Game of the Year in the 2012 annual best PC games ranking conducted by U.S. media authority IGN. Perfect World and Runic Games will officially launch Torchlight II Asia version, including versions in traditional Chinese, simplified Chinese and Japanese, in the near future, further extending the universe of players who will have the opportunity to enjoy the thrill of the game. These versions will be released by Perfect World's subsidiaries and partners across Asia. Meanwhile, Torchlight II Asia version will include new and intriguing content as part of the collector's pack. Once the game is officially online, the global limited collector's pack is certain to provide yet more thrills for players. The new legions of players that are expected to be brought on board with the Asian version will add to the popularity of the game. We believe that Torchlight II will not fail us in demonstrating its outstanding market value. SOURCE Beijing Perfect World Network Technology Co., Ltd.
