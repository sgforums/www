Be Happy" and Connect with Trusted Medical Specialists First-of-its-kind online healthcare community in Asia Pacific allows for smarter healthcare decisions through medical experts and peer review Longaevitas Cancer Wellness forum by Dr David Tan in collaboration with bioDYNE Labs(R) now open for public registrations SINGAPORE, March 12, 2013 /PRNewswire/ -- Holistic healthcare is just a click away now for consumers in Singapore, thanks to Compare Clinic( http://www.compareclinic.com ) (www.compareclinic.com), a new online healthcare community that helps them easily find and connect with trusted medical specialists that best fit their needs, both locally and overseas. (Photo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2013/03/11/20130311174123424617.html ) Visitors to Compare Clinic will get free access to clinic profiles as well as genuine customer feedback for each medical facility or clinic. Additionally, the website also provides users with health tips and information on the latest treatment options from certified healthcare professionals to help them make more informed decisions about the medical treatment they are considering. They can then connect with these medical specialists through the website's online appointment booking system, Users can also write reviews of clinics and the treatments they have received, and share their experience online. "A fulfilled life is only possible with good health and Compare Clinic aims to help people lead happier lives by being a stepping stone to better health," said Virginia Chan, Director of Compare Clinic. "Our focus is on educating people in order to help them make the right medical decisions for holistic wellbeing. We believe in the balance of body, mind and soul and hope to help users achieve this through the information we provide in collaboration with medical experts." Compare Clinic also benefits medical and dental service providers by helping them effectively reach out to a wider pool of patients. Any clinic or medical facility can sign up for a free basic profile on the site, with the option to upgrade to a paid premium account that allows them to better showcase their services and further engage with users by publishing their own content. "With rising healthcare costs and an ageing population, the nation's healthcare resources will also inevitably face a strain. The feedback provided by our online community, coupled with our appointment booking system can help alleviate this through better matching of patients to doctors," added Ms Chan. "Our ultimate goal is to help patients get quality healthcare anywhere, anytime. Singapore is one of Asia's medical tourism hubs and with consumer spending on healthcare in Asia Pacific expected to grow to US$1 trillion by 2016 (Note 1), there is a real business opportunity for medical service providers here that Compare Clinic is aligned with to expand their patient database." Compare Clinic currently offers information and customer feedback for clinics in more than 20 categories from east and west, including acupuncture, cardiology, dental services, fertility, holistic health, eye care, physiotherapy, chiropractic, orthopaedics and aesthetics. For more information or to register for a free account, users can visit www.compareclinic.com and stand a chance to win some healthy gifts. Dr. Tan will also talk about the latest results of his research work in cancer and provide some lifestyle management tips for cancer prevention.
