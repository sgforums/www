Meltwater Group Expands Global Footprint with New Malaysia O

  • Helen.zhang
    11 Feb 11, 15:05

     

    Worldwide growth continues with opening of office for online media monitoring tool, Meltwater News, in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

    KUALA LUMPUR, Malaysia, Feb. 11, 2011 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Meltwater Group( http://www.meltwater.com ), one of the world's leading Software as a Service (SaaS) companies, today announced further international expansion with the opening of an office in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.ï¹›Dedicated to Meltwater News( http://www.meltwater.com/products/meltwater-news ), its online media monitoring tool, the office will strengthen its global market leadership while enabling the company to break into a new market in Malaysia.

    With this latest addition, there are now 55 Meltwater News offices worldwide. Meltwater News Malaysia was launched out of the regional HQ, Meltwater News Hong Kong, and joins its sister office Meltwater News Singapore to serve a rapidly growing number of clients across South East Asia.

    Launched in 2001, Meltwater News is a real-time, online media monitoring tool, which has now grown to be one of the largest online media monitoring providers, with more than 18,000 customers worldwide.

    Furthermore, the Kuala Lumpur office has already secured such new customers as Astro( http://www.astro.com.my ), Panasonic( http://www.panasonic.com.my ), Petronas( http://www.petronas.com ), M&C Saatchi( http://www.mcsaatchi.com ) and KPJ Healthcare( http://www.kpjhealth.com.my ).

    "With the Kuala Lumpur office we are fortifying our strategy of continuing to lead the market, both in existing and new territories," said Jorn Lyseggen, CEO and founder of Meltwater Group.ï¹›"We continue to invest in growth in spite of the challenging global financial climate. There is an enormous growth potential in the fast evolving media landscape. Through rapid product innovation and by the strength of our global distribution, Meltwater is uniquely positioned to capitalize on these emerging opportunities."

    Meltwater News breaks the mould of traditional media monitoring services by delivering the business critical information that executives in organizations worldwide require to gain, and maintain, their competitive edge.

    With Meltwater News' expansive international coverage and robust search capabilities, users can find the information they need, when they need it, via an easy-to-use, Web-based interface.ï¹›Meltwater News' interactive analytical capabilities enable users to get even more value from their media mentions by evaluating trends, mapping press activity, identifying target markets and measuring the ROI of marketing and public relations campaigns.

    About Meltwater

    The Meltwater Group is a privately held software company founded in Norway in 2001, serving more than 18,000 clients through 55 offices located across North America, South America, Europe, Middle East, Africa, Asia and Australia.
