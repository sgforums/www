i.Tech Introduces MusicBAND 807 Bluetooth Headphones

  • Helen.zhang
    9 Nov 10, 09:55

    Lightweight, wireless, stereo headset lets music lovers bring their own music everywhere they go-without missing phone calls

    HONG KONG, Nov. 8, 2010 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- i.Tech Dynamic today introduced the MusicBAND 807, a sleek, lightweight, stereo headset that offers high-quality performance for listening to music. Users never have to miss a call when enjoying their favorite songs -- a button on the right earpiece lets users seamlessly answer phone calls from a Bluetooth-enabled phone or mobile device.

    (Photo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2010/11/05/20101105609254.jpg )
    (Logo: http://www.prnasia.com/sa/2009/09/02/200909021619.jpg )

    The wireless MusicBAND 807 is a portable Bluetooth stereo headset in a comfortable, stylish headband design. i.Tech's audio engineering provides high-quality music performance and a rich, in-depth stereo sound. In addition, the headset features Bluetooth's Advanced Audio Distribution Profile, or A2DP, which lets users stream songs from A2DP-compatible Bluetooth mobile phones or music devices. Once paired with the headset, the phone or music device can be used to control song play. With AVRCP (Audio/Video Remote Control Profile), users can remotely control music playback modes, such as next and previous track selection. When a call comes in, the music is automatically suspended while the user answers or rejects the call through the headset, then smoothly returns to the previous audio setting after the call.

    Easy-to-use controls let the wearer adjust volume, select audio tracks from a paired music device, and pick up or end phone calls. MusicBAND 807 uses Clear Voice Capture (CVC) technology for noise and echo cancellation, and it incorporates Fast Stream technology, which reduces audio latency, ensuring that booming sounds from video sources are heard by the user without any delay.

    "The MusicBAND 807 was engineered to truly let customers bring their own music with them," said Wendy Cho, managing director, i.Tech Dynamic Ltd. "Stylish, lightweight and with a fashionable carrying case, this i.Tech headset gives music lovers high-quality audio that they'll want to keep with them everywhere they go."

    Boasting outstanding battery life, MusicBAND 807 offers 10 hours of talk time, seven hours of music play and 350 hours of standby time, and it fully charges in less than three hours.

    MusicBAND 807 will be available in black and white metallic finishes, and will come with a fashionable cloth carrying case.

    MusicBAND 807 Features

    -- 10 hours talk time
    -- 7 hours music play time
    -- 350 hours standby time
    -- Multifunction button controls power, pairing, picking up and
    ending calls, and last-number redial
    -- Separate buttons for volume up/next song and volume down/previous
    song
    -- Music play/pause button
    -- Status indicator shows pairing status and mode of operation
    -- Voice dialing
    -- Call reject
    -- Call transfer

    MusicBAND 807 Specifications

    -- Bluetooth Version 2.1 + EDR Class 2
    -- Bluetooth profile: HSP, HFP, A2DP and AVRCP
    -- Weight: 0.83 ounce / 23.5 grams
