    The Executive Centre Expands to Singapore's Marina Bay Financial Centre

    -Establishing the fourth Singaporean centre in the city's fastest growing district

    SINGAPORE, May 6, 2015 /PRNewswire/ -- The Executive Centre, Asia Pacific's leading premium serviced office provider, is pleased to announce the grand opening of its latest business centre at Marina Bay Financial Centre Tower 1 on May 7, 2015. This expansion project establishes The Executive Centre's fourth location in Singapore.

     
    The Executive Centre establishes the fourth location in Singapore at Marina Bay Financial Centre Tower One.

    Located at the heart of Singapore's fastest growing central business district, the Marina Bay Financial Centre is set to become a landmark property in Singapore's emerging financial market. This iconic addition to the rapidly developing Marina Bay area marks the new district's first fully-integrated development. Complete with three commercial towers, two luxurious residential towers, and a shopping mall, the Marina Bay Financial Centre is certain to contribute remarkably to the growth of this new hub.

    Situated on the eleventh floor, The Executive Centre at Marina Bay Financial Centre Tower 1 will offer premium serviced office suites with high-quality interior design and spectacular views of Marina Bay, coupled with contemporary conference facilities, integrated IT and communications systems and extensive business support services. The promising nature of Marina Bay's premium corporate environment will create numerous opportunities for The Executive Centre to work alongside other motivated market players and expand its business. Additionally, this new centre comes as an addition to The Executive Centre's existing Singaporean locations at Ocean Financial Centre, One Raffles Quay, and Six Battery Road.

    Uniquely tailored for the professional services sector, Marina Bay Financial Centre Tower 1 offers large, open-floor plans and state-of-the-art amenities. The entire development remains well-connected to key transportation nodes through the Raffles Place Interchange and Downtown MRT (Mass Rapid Transit) stations. Marina Bay Financial Centre is also in close proximity to the Marina Bay Sands integrated resort, the Singapore Flyer, The Esplanade -- Theatres on the Bay, the Gardens by the Bay, and many other exciting lifestyle and entertainment locales. This area, adjacent to Singapore's Downtown Core, has drawn in many global investors, all dedicated to transforming Marina Bay into the city's pivot for its growing financial industry.

    Iky Ng, City Head -- Singapore of The Executive Centre has shown confidence in the success of The Executive Centre's forth serviced office location in Singapore, "The development of Marina Bay will play a significant role in doubling the size of the existing central business district and create a financial hub more than twice the size ofLondon's renowned Canary Wharf. Marina Bay's committed investors and prospective tenants will continue to seek innovative, premium office solutions in the area and spur Singapore's exciting economic growth."

    Paul Salnikow, Chairman, and CEO of The Executive Centre has stressed that the new flagship centre will play an important role "as we enter the next phase of our company's growth." Salnikow added that, in addition to the Marina Bay Financial Centre opening, The Executive Centre looks forward to continuing its "expansion in the premium serviced office market in other emerging economies in Asia."
