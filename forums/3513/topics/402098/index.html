    Draeger Unveils Latest Innovations in Non-invasive Ventilation at ICEM 2010

     

    2010-06-09 18:11

     

     

    Expert to Share Knowledge and Best Practices on Non-invasive Ventilation  

     

        SINGAPORE, June 9 /PRNewswire-Asia/ -- Draeger, a Germany-based company with 120 years of innovation in ventilation, will be participating in ICEM 2010, the International Conference on Emergency Medicine in Singapore on 9 -- 12 June at the Suntec International Convention & Exhibition Centre Singapore. 

     

        To support medical professionals enlarging their knowledge on NIV, Draeger will share practical experiences in emergency care ventilation at ICEM.  Mr Michael Rodda, a leading Draeger expert in the field of ventilation in emergency medicine and a former Registered Nurse with more than 20 years of experience in both clinical practice and equipment expertise, will share insights on how NIV can benefit patients as well as practical ideas to improve success rate with NIV during his talk on 10 June. 

     

        Mr Andreas Frahm, President, Draeger Medical Region Asia Pacific, said, "Technology has always played a key role in emergency medicine to ensure accurate diagnosis and adequate stabilization of a patient prior to definitive treatment. In respiratory emergencies, non-invasive ventilation (NIV) can help to prevent or delay intubation, therefore minimizing complications associated with invasive ventilation. Patients not only experience enhanced therapy and better care, but also can be treated safely during transportation. The result is improved outcomes and a more confident team of healthcare professionals. With this talk on non-invasive ventilation, Draeger hopes to contribute to the development of emergency medicine in collaboration with the participants at ICEM 2010."

     

        Latest NIV equipment on display at ICEM

     

        Draeger will showcase its latest NIV equipment that are being used in emergency departments all over the world, including the Oxylog(R) 3000 plus, the latest model of the Oxylog(R) series, which is designed to be used in both hospital and outdoor environments. Its robustness and transportability, in combination with the wide range of ventilation modes and monitoring options, supports patient care with improved outcomes. 

     

        Manufacturer of the Oxylog(R) ventilator series is Draeger Medical AG & Co. KG, Germany.

     

        Draeger at ICEM 2010

     

        Draeger will be at booths 14 and 15, at ICEM from 9 to 12 June 2010 at Suntec International Convention & Exhibition Centre Singapore. 

     

        The talk on Non-Invasive Ventilation in the Emergency Department will be on 10 June 2010, Room 303 and 304, from 12.15pm to 12.45pm.
