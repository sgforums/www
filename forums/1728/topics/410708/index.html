    Don't always go into the past... Whenever you have spare time (and even in the midst of busy-ness), learn to be present, submerge yourself (let go of yourself) into the everyday mundane and ordinary experience of seeing, hearing, smelling, and you'll realize how wonderful and blissful and extraordinary that ordinary aliveness is. If you are able to realize and experience pure consciousness, you'll be freed from identification with a mental story, you will be willing to drop all that 'me-stories' and live in Actuality which is absent of a separate self.

    I can say I am not a perfect person, but I choose never to live in attachments and guilt, but in Actuality. I do not identify myself as my past or future or notion of present, but as Pure Presence/Consciousness, which is all there is - even a memory of past or projection of future are mere thoughts arising in and as Presence/Consciousness.
