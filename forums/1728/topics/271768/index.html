    Hi,
    before Buddha statues were made, the relics (stupas) were the objects of veneration, and since the Buddha was no longer around, the "closest" to his physical body were his relics or ashes. And they were so important that right after Buddha's Passing, countries wanted to wage wars against each other to have possesion of the relics but in the end they were divided into eight portions.
    During Asoka's reign the relics were further divided and that's how we get the relics in various countries.
