    Venerable Thubten Chodron has conducted many of such retreats in USA and many have benefited from her teachings.

    In this workshop, she will guide you through the following topics, combining dharma teachings with meditational techniques :

    - What is karma and how does it work?

    - The four opponent powers for purification

    - Letting go of guilt

    - Forgiving and apologizing

    - Doing the Vajrasattva visualization and mantra recitation together.

    Its not common that a good dharma teacher comes along. Why wait? Limited seats only. Register now! For online registration/enquiry: apmc@awarenessplace.com

    Venue: Ven Hong Choon Memorial Hall (3rd Floor)

    Tel: 6336 5067, 6849 5300 (Registration Required)
    Closing Date: 9th April 06
    Course Offering: $50(Inclusive of lunch & refreshments)

    i'd like to acknowledge neutral_onliner to thank you for posting this thread in the forum for the benefit of many (those who through this website able to come to know and benefit from these 2days retreats with venerable thubten chodron.)

    i attended the workshop and were very meaningful, beneficial, precious and purifying. in the workshop, we received teachings from venerable thubten chodron, had meditation practice on vajrasattva visualization, reciting vajrasattva mantra, contemplations, group discussions and saying the great prayers of samantabhadra bodhisattva. this dharma workshop has benefitted me to understand the practice of bodhisattva's path abit more profoundly. and, i am extremely delighted that i was able to take part in the ceremony held by venerable thubten chodron on taking refuge in the tripple gem.

    dedication
    due to this merit may we soon
    attain the enlightenment state of vajrasattva
    that we may be able to liberate
    all sentient beings from their sufferings.

    may the precious dodhi mind
    not yet born arise and grow.
    may that born have no decline,
    but increase forever more.
