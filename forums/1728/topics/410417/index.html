    The Teachers
     
    Most Venerable Drikung Ontrul Rinpoche is a senior lineage-holder of the Drikung Kagyu tradition. Ontrul Rinpoche is Himself a terton and has manifested signs of numerous spiritual accomplishments. He is recognized by the late 16th Gyalwa Karmapa and the Drikung Kyabgon as the reincarnation of the Elder Brother of Terton Nuden Dorje.Ontul Rinpoche's son, Ratna Rinpoche, will be the ritual assistant and is recognized by HH the Dalai Lama and the Drikung Kyabgon as an emanation of Terton Nuden Dorje.
