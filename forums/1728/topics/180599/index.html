    After the success of the Camp Ehi-Passiko 2005 The Sequel with a record 107 campers, i would like to on behalf of Singapore Buddhist Mission (Youth Group) annouce the date for the upcoming Camp Ehi-Passiko 2006 The Prequel.
    Date is finalised from 29th May to 1st June.
    Venue at Camp Christine.
    As our poster is still in the midst of production, please enjoy the pictures from last camp instead.
    2006 Prequel would be SBMY's 13th Camp and we hope it would be another landmark for the group with a target participants of 120.
    Youth aged 12 to 21 are welcomed to join.
    Interested participants or for more information on our camp or organisation, please call Samadi at 97599802 or email at samadhi_atomic@hotmail.com.

    As the dates get closer, I will update here on more information on the camp. Do do do support us as we strive to bring the Dhamma to the youths!
