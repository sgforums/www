    Kundalini and Sex
    (kundalini yoga and sex)

The raising of kundalini is not all of a sudden! Kundalini awakening is a very slow process... needs practicing patience, persistence and perseverance at its level best!

On sighting a maiden woman taking bath in forlorn circumstances in deep dense jungles (forests) fell from grace... he had to repeat practice of celibacy all over again!

    The full awakening of kundalini is beset with certain preconditions... foremost of which is practice of absolute celibacy! In absence of practice of absolute celibacy... nothing much in the field of kundalini rising is achieved! A practice of 12 years of Brahmacharya Vrata (the physical part of celibacy) is necessitated for full activation of kundalini.
