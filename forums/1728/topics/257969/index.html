    Dear Dharma Brothers and Sisters,

    We are very fortunate to have His Holiness Phakchok Rinpoche in
    Singapore this coming June. His Holiness is the eldest son His Eminence
    Tsikey Chokling Rinpoche, who just recently visited us.

    His Holiness has kindly agreed to give two public talks during His visit
    to Singapore. Attached is a leaflet containing the details of the two
    talks as well as the Phakchok Rinpoche's biography.

    Please do join us for His Holiness Phakchok Rinpoche's talks which are
    well-known to be direct, accessible and always fresh, opening up our
    minds in a playful and inspiring way.

    Tashi delek!

    Adelene
    o/b of Chairman,Mr Loh Mun Hon
    Oddiyana Ka-Nying Buddhist Centre

    Ps: Venue for both talks will be held at Ean Keng Temple ( 48 Frankel
    Ave)
