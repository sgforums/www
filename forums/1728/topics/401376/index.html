    During the winter retreat 2010, about 120 people gathered from all Europe to practice a Dorje Drölo Drubchö composed by Düdjom Rinpoche. They had the great fortune to enjoy the presence of Chokling Jigme Palden Rinpoche during the whole week.

    On the early morning, practices of the Düdjom Tersar Ngöndro, Riwo Sangchö and Chöd of Tröma Nagmo were performed. Then Drubchö was perfomed all day long and the Zor Torma was sent into the fire, burning away all the obstacles of the Community.In the evening, Chokling Jigme Palden Rinpoche and Namkha
    Rinpoche gave their precious teachings on Ngöndro and Dzogchen
