    During the time of the Buddha, there was a mother who carried her dead child to everyone asking for a elixir or medicine to restore the child back to life, someone pitied her and told her to consult the Buddha.

    When she told the Buddha her sad story, he told her to go to any household and get mustard seeds, but the household must not have anyone who have had passed away. every househould she went and enquired all have someone near and dear to them passed away.

    she enquired from many households, true to form, she realises death comes to all and no one can escape from death. she understood what the Buddha was conveying the message to her, she buried her child and came back to the Buddha, asking for ordination. her name was kisa gotami i think.
