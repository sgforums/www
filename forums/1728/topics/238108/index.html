    Today ,according to Buddhist Calendar of the Mahayana Buddhism

    marks the birthday of Bodhisattva Maitreya

    the Next-in-Line Buddha of to come and turn the Dharma wheel

    Bodhisattva Maitreya ,embodiment of Loving Kindness

    was the Founder of Yogacara Buddhism .He transmitted the Teachings of
    Yogacara to Asanga and Vasubandhu Which Flourish the new 2nd age of Mahayana Buddhism in central India the Emptiness and Mind-only teachings

    Which breaks all brahmanical views of the mind as eternal ,and further exploration on the Mahayana point of view in Mind teaching ,as compared to the Southern School of Abidhmma teachings

    Actually there is not real birthday for Bodhisattva Maitreya.It is decided as an interesting monk who was plump and carries a cloth bag who never seems to exhausted on helping the needy during the Sung dynasty .the Monk said
    "Maitreya is Maitreya ,thus is who i am " and set himself aflame and end his life in Saha world

    thats the coming about of the Fat Maitreya in Buddhist temple
    for the round tummy symbolise his virtue in magnamious to ppl who despise him
