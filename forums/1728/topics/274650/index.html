    Preserving the Noble Bodhisattva way of life

    In Southeast Asia especially in Singapore ,we see a rise of new trend of Buddhist ,bilingual in education and middle-class

    in the 1980s Buddhist temple of Mahayana tradition held dharma class and puja in chinese , appealing to the chinese population .

    in the 1990s till the 7 years past the millenium ,a upgrowing trend of suiting to the bilingual group and even the mainly english-speaking population at large

    With major buddhist temple organisation like Phor Kark See and Shuanglin Monastery ,Buddhism is reaching the masses as it simplicity appeal attracts many

    At the Same time ,with the exchange of Vajrayana tradition to singapore
    Singapore is indeed blessed with the array of lineage not found in other
    parts of the world

    In order for Mahayana Buddhism to continue "Passing on the Lamp" to the next generation,certain issue have to readdressed and resolved

    1.adopt democratic approach to temple management committee
    which inherently been around since the time of Buddha

    2.Engage more discussion on Buddha teaching ,particularly on how to apply
    into the Daily way of life

    3.Continue to engage as support group to people in need

    4.Restructure Dharma classes so as to appeal to youngster , like field trips
    organising outdoor activities like rock climbing ,ice skating and so on

    the list is non-exhaustive as when the youngster draw to the religions , will give new impetus to the development of both spiritual and personal development in the Mahayana Buddhist community
