Medicine Buddha and Pureland

    May all sentient beings have happiness and the causes of happiness.

    May they be free from suffering and the causes of suffering.

    May they not be separated from the Supreme Bliss which is free from all suffering.

    May they live in the Great Equanimity which is free from all attachment and aversion.

    In the land encircled by snow mountains

    You are the source of all happiness and good;

    All-powerful Chenresig, Tenzin Gyatso,

    Please remain until samsara ends.
