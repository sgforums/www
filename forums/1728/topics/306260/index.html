    Originally posted by Canihelpyou02:

    If that is the case,why Buddhist is still praying to Buddha and Boddhisttvah asking for help.

    I for one don't often pray for help. Buddhism is not a praying religion, and many of those people who "pray" aren't Buddhists to begin with. So we must not mistake Buddhism with the common Chinese folk religion that prays to all Buddhist and non-Buddhist figures, and deities.

    By the way, Buddhists don't pray to deities, unlike folk religions. Deities are samsaric beings, still in the cycle of rebirth. Buddha and Bodhisattvas are above samsara, they are enlightened beings. Even then we do not 'pray' to them, we only pay respect and veneration.

    Through their teachings, we aim to practice the Boddhisattva path, attain Buddhahood ourselves. We do not 'worship' them. Everyone's Buddha Nature is complete, just that we have not realised it.
