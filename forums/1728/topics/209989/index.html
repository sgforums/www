    Oneday, after Ch'an Master BaiZhang finished His talk, an old man did not left. Ch'an master ask why. The old man replied that he's not human and that he's a fox spirit transform as a human, due to a long time practice in BaiZhang mountain. During then, a student asked, " Do a great cultivator still create karma?" He said "Do not leave karma." Hence he was reborn into the animal realms for 500 years as a fox. "Knowing that Master is a great cultivator, please enlightened me. Do great cultivator still create karma?"
    Ch'an master replied, "Do not conceal karma. "
    The old man was enlightened and no longer attached to his old concept that a great cultivator do not leave karma. We can have karma on our form body but our inner mind is not affected, then karma become natural. He later when back to the cave and passed on (ie liberated).
