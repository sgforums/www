    Becoming a monk is to walk the path of the deathlessness. Besides accruing a great deal of merit, that is not the point. The point is to obtain liberation from the samsara, either as arahant or bodhisattva. Bodhisattva will still remain in this saha world practising the paramitas and learning until he is ready to be a buddha to save all sentient beings.

    Even if this life you do not attain arahanthood, you will one day attain arahanthood in the future.
