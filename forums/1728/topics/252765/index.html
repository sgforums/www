    vows help one initiate the relevant Yin Yuan (karma) to help us realise our goals in the future

    but the problem with commoners like us is that our thoughts are constantly changing and varies countlessly, the second after we vow, we may not feel as "true" to the moment as before (a crude analogy will be how you feel before and after you do your partner)

    thus vows need to be practised day-in-day-out, so that it actually "seeps" into your 8th consciousness, then slowly all your actions and decisions will start to align behind these vows, thereby attaining them in due time

    side note: because your actions and decisions start to align, you actually are changing your original karma (bad reference but easy to understand), this is also how certain people are able to single-handedly turn their life around
