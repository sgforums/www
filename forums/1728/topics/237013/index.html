    Save Our Buddhists CAMPAIGN
    A Free Publication Campaign in Poor Buddhist Countries

    The Buddhist Maha Vihara has launched the Save our Buddhists Campaign to educate our fellow Buddhists in poor countries about their noble religion - Buddhism. Our fellow Buddhists in countries such as Cambodia, Sri Lanka and India are facing an evangelist onslaught, which without our help, would be converted to other religions simply because they do not know practical Buddhism in their daily lives and Buddhist apologetics.

    Sadly, Buddhist in these countries are suffering from a lack of resources, mainly free Dhamma publications. We have identified trustworthy groups to work with to periodically publish and post these free Dhamma publications to the various adopted Buddhist temples and centers to be distributed to devotees who visit the temples. By printing in these countries, we yield significant cost savings in terms of economies of scale, lower printing cost and lower domestic postage.

    We strongly appeal to you come forward to donate to this noble cause. We also plan to expand this program to several other Buddhist countries gradually, funds permitting. We have to date published and distributed about 120,000 free publications in India and Sri Lanka in the last 3 years under this program. We plan to double our commitment to print about 240,000 free Dhamma booklets overseas for this year with your support.

    If you are interested to participate in this meritorious deed, please contact Sis. Phang at the Vihara office, 603-22741141 (telephone), 603-22732570 (fax). All donations will be receipted under the Buddhist Maha Vihara.
