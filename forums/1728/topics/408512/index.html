    We wish to inform you that the annual 3-day Krodikali Summer Retreat
    in Ipoh, Malaysia will begin on 5 September till 7 September. All
    participants are required to be in Ipoh Dudjom Dharma House for an
    orientation and briefing on Friday (4 September 2010) at 10:00am.
    During this session, participants will receive the retreat schedule
    and the rules and regulations of the retreat. At the same time,
    Rinpoche will confer us The Supreme Krodikali Initiation at the start
    of the retreat. This initiation is strictly for DISCIPLES ONLY.

    For your information, this retreat is not open to public but it is
    compulsory for all Garab Rinpoche's disciples, strictly ONLY for
    Rinpoche's disciples and Dudjom lineage followers. Should any
    non-disciple of Garab Rinpoche wishes to participate in this retreat,
    kindly email Rinpoche's personal assistant, Koncho Nyenda at
    garab_dorje@garabdorje.com to seek permission.
