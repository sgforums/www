    Hello,

    I would like to seek your opinions on something. I am a Pure Land practitioner. A few years ago, I was doing some contemplation on impermanence and then suddenly this tranquil feeling enveloped me. I felt very peaceful and in that state I felt free from desires. It was almost like time stopped, but not quite. However, that state lasted only about 30 seconds and I have never experienced it again after that. Is that what people call "access concentration" or is that "emptiness"? Sometimes I still think back on it and wonder what it is as that was the first and only time I ever experienced it.

    How do I experience that state again? I tried contemplation on impermanance after that but I didn't get that feeling again..
